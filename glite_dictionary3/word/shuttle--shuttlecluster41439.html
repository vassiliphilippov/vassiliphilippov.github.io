shuttle

noun
transport that runs back and forth between two points
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtl̩ / (BrE)
a bus, train, or aircraft that travels frequently between two places, usually making repeated trips back and forth

Examples

shuttle bus service
A shuttle service runs between the city center and the train station.
The airport shuttle leaves every 30 minutes from the arrivals terminal.
We took the hotel shuttle to the convention center.

Related Terms

  • synonym – commuter train: train used for regular journeys between home and work
  • synonym – ferry: boat or ship transporting people or vehicles across water regularly
