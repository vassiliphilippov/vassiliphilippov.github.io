to regenerate

verb
restore strength or energy
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdʒɛnəˌreɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒɛnəˌreɪt /; / rɪˈdʒɛnəreɪt / (BrE)
to restore or regain strength, energy, or vitality; to revitalize physically or mentally

Examples

The spa promised to regenerate both body and mind through relaxation treatments.

Regular exercise gives me the energy to regenerate and stay healthy.
