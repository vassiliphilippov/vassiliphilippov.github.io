to marinade

verb
soak food in a marinade before cooking
Pronunciation: / ˌmærəˈneɪd / (AmE), / ˌmærɪˈneɪd / (BrE)
to soak food, especially meat or fish, in a marinade before cooking to add flavor or tenderize it

Examples

to marinade meat
It's best to marinade the chicken overnight to let the flavors develop.
Many recipes recommend you marinade fish before baking to keep it moist.
The chef decided to marinade the beef in red wine and herbs before grilling it.

  • synonym – marinate: to soak food in a seasoned liquid before cooking
