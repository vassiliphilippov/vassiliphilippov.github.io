epigastrium

noun
upper central abdominal region
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈɡæstriəm / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈɡæstriəm / (BrE)
the upper central region of the abdomen, located just below the sternum and over the stomach

Examples

pain in the epigastrium
He complained of discomfort in his epigastrium after the meal.
Pain in the epigastrium can indicate stomach or pancreatic problems.
The doctor pressed on the epigastrium to check for tenderness above the stomach.

Related Terms

  • antonym – hypogastrium: the region of the abdomen below the umbilicus
