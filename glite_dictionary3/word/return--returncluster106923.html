return

noun
tax document reporting income and liabilities
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtɜrn / (AmE), / rɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
an official document that a person or company submits to the tax authorities, stating income and other details for tax assessment

Examples

return for taxes
Be sure to submit your return before the tax deadline to avoid penalties.
She realized she'd made a mistake on her return after it had already been filed.
The accountant helped me fill out my return for last year's taxes.
