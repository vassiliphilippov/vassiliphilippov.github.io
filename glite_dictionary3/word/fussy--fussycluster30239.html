fussy

adjective
overcrowded or overly detailed in design
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌsi / (AmE), / ˈfʌsi / (BrE)
containing too many small details, making it seem overcrowded or overly elaborate

Examples

fussy design details
She preferred a clean layout over a fussy design full of decorations.
The invitation looked fussy and cluttered, making it hard to read the important details.
The wallpaper was too fussy for my taste, with an overwhelming number of patterns.
