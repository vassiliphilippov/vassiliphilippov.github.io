to miniaturise

verb
make something much smaller in size
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪniə.tʃəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪn.i.ə.tjʊə.raɪz / (BrE)
to design or construct something on a much smaller scale than usual

Examples

to miniaturise a device
Engineers managed to miniaturise the device so it could fit inside a watch.
Many technological advances focus on the ability to miniaturise electronic components.
Thanks to modern science, we can now miniaturise complex machines for medical use.
