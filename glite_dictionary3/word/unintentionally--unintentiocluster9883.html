unintentionally

adverb
not deliberately or on purpose
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈtɛnʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈtɛnʃənəli / (BrE)
Without intention; in a way that is not deliberate or planned.

Examples

unintentionally hurt feelings
He unintentionally offended his friend with a careless remark.
I unintentionally left my phone at home this morning.
She broke the vase unintentionally while cleaning the shelf.
