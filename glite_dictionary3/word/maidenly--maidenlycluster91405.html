maidenly

adjective
owning qualities of a maiden; modest or demure
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdənli / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdnlɪ / (BrE)
Characteristic of or appropriate for a maiden, especially in being modest, demure, or innocent.

Examples

maidenly blush

The novel described her maidenly innocence in poetic detail.
