doe

noun
female deer, rabbit, or kangaroo
Pronunciation: / doʊ / (AmE), / dəʊ / (BrE)
A mature female of certain mammals, such as deer, rabbit, or kangaroo, especially when the male is called a 'buck'.

Examples

female doe deer
A doe and her fawn grazed quietly at the edge of the forest.
During the spring, it's common to spot a doe with her young in the meadow.
The hunter saw several does but chose not to take a shot.
