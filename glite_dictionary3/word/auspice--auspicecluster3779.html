auspice

noun
a favorable omen or sign
Pronunciation: / ˈɔː.spɪs / (AmE), / ˈɔː.spɪs / (BrE)
A sign or omen believed to indicate future success or a favorable outcome.

Examples

good auspice
The event opened under the auspice of a rising sun, which many took as a sign of good luck.
The new project began under favorable auspices, with support from both the government and the community.
They interpreted the strange bird's presence as an auspice for the coming harvest.
