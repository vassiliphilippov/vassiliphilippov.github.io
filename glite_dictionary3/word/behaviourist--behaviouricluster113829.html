behaviourist

adjective
relating to behaviorism theory
Pronunciation: / bɪˈheɪvjɚrɪst / (AmE), / bɪˈheɪvjərɪst / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of behaviorism, a theory in psychology that focuses on observable behaviors rather than internal mental processes.

Examples

behaviourist theory
Critics argue that behaviourist models overlook important mental processes.
Her research is grounded in behaviourist principles and rejects introspection.
The new therapy program takes a strictly behaviourist approach to treating phobias.
