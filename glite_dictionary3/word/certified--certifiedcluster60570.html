certified

adjective
officially recognized as qualified
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.təˌfaɪd /; / ˈsɝː.t̬ə.faɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.tɪ.faɪd / (BrE)
officially recognized as having met specific requirements, standards, or qualifications, usually by a certificate or authority

Examples

certified teacher
He became a certified translator after passing a rigorous exam.
Only certified electricians are allowed to perform this type of work.
She is a certified yoga instructor with years of experience.
After passing several exams, he became a certified lifeguard.
Only certified electricians are allowed to install the wiring in this building.
She hired a certified accountant to handle her finances.
