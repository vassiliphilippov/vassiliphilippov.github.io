ninth

noun
one of nine equal parts
Pronunciation: / naɪnθ / (AmE), / naɪnθ / (BrE)
Each of nine equal parts into which something is divided; one part out of nine.

Examples

a ninth share
A ninth of the entire budget was allocated to research.
If you have nine students and one leaves, that's one ninth of the group.
She divided the cake into nine pieces and took one ninth for herself.
