climacteric

noun
period marking end of menstruation in women
Pronunciation: / klaɪˈmæk.tə.rɪk / (AmE), / klaɪˈmæk.tər.ɪk / (BrE)
the period in a woman's life when menstruation ceases, typically occurring in middle age; menopause

Examples

the climacteric in women
Doctors can provide support for women going through the climacteric.
Some women experience emotional and physical changes during the climacteric.
The climacteric usually occurs between the ages of 45 and 55.

Related Terms

  • synonym – menopause: the time in a woman's life (typically between 45 and 55 years old) when menstruation ends
