to azure

verb
make or become sky-blue
Pronunciation: / ˈæʒər / (AmE), / ˈæʒə / (BrE)
(literary) to make or become blue, especially sky-blue in color.

Examples

to azure the canvas
As she painted the canvas, she skillfully azured the background to capture the calm serenity of the sea.
Spring skies azure the mountain peaks with a delicate hue.
The early dawn slowly azured the eastern sky.
