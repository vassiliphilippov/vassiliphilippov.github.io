to appeal

verb
formally challenge a decision, especially legally
Pronunciation: / əˈpil / (AmE), / əˈpiːl / (BrE)
to formally ask a higher court or authority to change a decision

Examples

If you disagree with the decision, you can appeal within 30 days.
