to operate

verb
function; work as intended
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.pəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒp.ə.reɪt / (BrE)
to function or perform as intended or expected

Examples

operate correctly
After years of use, his old laptop still operates smoothly.
If the generator doesn't operate during storms, the hospital could lose power.
The new coffee maker will not operate unless it is plugged in properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – function: work or operate in the proper or expected way
  • antonym – malfunction: fail to work properly
  • synonym – work: perform as expected or intended (machines, devices)
