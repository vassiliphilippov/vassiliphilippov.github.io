isothermal

adjective
constant-temperature (esp. scientific)
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪ.səˈθɜr.məl / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.səʊˈθɜː.məl / (BrE)
(of a process, change, or condition) occurring at a constant temperature.

Examples

isothermal process
During an isothermal expansion, the temperature of the gas remains unchanged.
In isothermal processes, heat energy flows to keep the temperature constant.
The chemist maintained isothermal conditions throughout the experiment.
