mouflon

noun
wild mountain sheep of Corsica and Sardinia
Pronunciation: / ˈmuː.flɑn / (AmE), / ˈmuː.flɒn / (BrE)
a wild sheep of mountainous regions, native to Corsica and Sardinia, with short, curved horns and a brown coat

Examples

wild mouflon sheep
A solitary mouflon was spotted grazing in the early morning light.
Researchers tracked a herd of mouflons across the rocky slopes.
The mouflon is considered an ancestor of modern domestic sheep.
