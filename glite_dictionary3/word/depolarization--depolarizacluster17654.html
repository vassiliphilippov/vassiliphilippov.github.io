depolarization

noun
reduction of political polarization

The process of reducing political or ideological polarization within a society, group, or public debate; efforts or outcomes that decrease sharp partisan division.

Examples

political depolarization
The mayor launched a campaign for depolarization by hosting community forums with opposing groups.
Political scientists praised the new education program aimed at long-term depolarization among young voters.
International mediators worked toward depolarization of the talks to help both sides reach agreement.
