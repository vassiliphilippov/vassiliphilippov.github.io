probe

noun
official investigation or inquiry
Pronunciation: / proʊb / (AmE), / prəʊb / (BrE)
an official or careful investigation to find out information about something

Examples

official probe
After the data breach, a formal probe was conducted to determine its cause.
The government launched a probe into allegations of corruption among the top officials.
The newspaper uncovered new evidence during its probe of the financial scandal.
After the data breach, the company announced a probe to determine how it happened.
Scientists sent a robotic probe to study the surface of the distant planet.
The research team launched a probe into the origins of the unusual signal.
