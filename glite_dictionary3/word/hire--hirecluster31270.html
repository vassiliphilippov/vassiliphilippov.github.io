to hire

verb
obtain use by paying rent
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪə / (BrE)
to obtain the temporary use of goods or services by paying for them under a lease or rental agreement

Examples

to hire a car
It is common to hire extra equipment for big events rather than buying it.
We decided to hire a car for our holiday in Spain.
You can hire bicycles from the shop next to the station.

Related Terms

  • synonym – rent: to pay for the use of something (especially property or equipment)
