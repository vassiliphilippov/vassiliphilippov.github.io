boarder

noun
student living at school
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdə / (BrE)
a student who lives and sleeps at school during term time rather than traveling home each day

Examples

school boarder
As a boarder, he often stayed on campus over the weekends.
She was a boarder at a prestigious private school for six years.
The school provides separate dormitories for boarders and day pupils.

Related Terms

  • antonym – day student: a student who attends school but does not live there
