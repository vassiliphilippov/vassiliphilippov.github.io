picaresque

adjective
fiction involving clever adventurers or rogues
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪkəˈrɛsk / (AmE), / ˌpɪkəˈrɛsk / (BrE)
relating to a type of fiction that features clever rogues or adventurers as protagonists, often recounting their escapades in a humorous or satirical way

Examples

picaresque novel adventure
Her latest novel is a picaresque tale filled with colorful characters and daring escapades.
Many picaresque stories follow the misadventures of a charming but unscrupulous protagonist.
The film's picaresque tone set it apart from traditional hero-centric epics.
