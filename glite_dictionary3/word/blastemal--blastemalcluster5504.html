blastemal

adjective
relating to a blastema (cell mass)
Pronunciation: / blæsˈtiːməl / (AmE), / blæsˈtiːməl / (BrE)
of or relating to a blastema, the mass of undifferentiated cells from which an organ or tissue develops, especially in embryonic or regenerative contexts.

Examples

blastemal cells
Certain fish have remarkable blastemal regeneration abilities when their fins are damaged.
Research focuses on the molecular signals that drive blastemal tissue formation during healing.
The tumor exhibited a predominance of blastemal cells under microscopic examination.
