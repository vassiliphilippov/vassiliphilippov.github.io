stewed

adjective
cooked slowly in liquid
Pronunciation: / stuːd / (AmE), / stjuːd / (BrE)
(of food) cooked slowly in liquid, especially water, usually together with vegetables and seasonings.

Examples

stewed vegetables
After being stewed for hours, the vegetables nearly melted in your mouth.
She served stewed apples with cinnamon for dessert.
The stewed beef was incredibly tender and flavorful.

Related Terms

  • synonym – braised: food cooked slowly in a small amount of liquid
  • antonym – roasted: (of food) cooked by dry heat, especially in an oven
