izar

noun
long loose cotton garment for women
Pronunciation: / ɪˈzɑr / (AmE), / ɪˈzɑː / (BrE)
a long, loose cotton outer garment, usually white, traditionally worn by Muslim women in North Africa and the Middle East, covering the entire body
