mixer

noun
cryptocurrency transaction anonymizer
Pronunciation: / c8m6aks59r / (AmE), / c8m6aks59 / (BrE)
a (usually online) service that pools, shuffles, or otherwise mixes cryptocurrency transactions to hide the links between senders and recipients, often used to increase privacy or to launder funds.

Examples

crypto mixer service
Investigators traced funds, but the suspect used a mixer to anonymize the crypto transactions.
Some exchanges refuse deposits that have passed through a known mixer because of money-laundering risks.
Privacy advocates argue a mixer can protect legitimate users from surveillance while critics warn of abuse.

Related Terms

  • synonym – coinjoin: a privacy technique or protocol for combining multiple Bitcoin payments into a single transaction to increase anonymity.
  • synonym – tumbler: a service that mixes cryptocurrencies to obscure transaction trails (synonymous in crypto contexts).
