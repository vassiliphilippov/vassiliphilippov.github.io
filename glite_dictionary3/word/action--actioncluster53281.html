to action

verb
begin legal proceedings against
Pronunciation: / ˈækʃən / (AmE), / ˈækʃən / (BrE)
(law) To institute legal proceedings against a person or organization; to file a lawsuit.

Examples

to action a company
A group of residents actioned the developer for damage to their property.
She decided to action her former employer over unfair dismissal.
The company was actioned for breach of contract by several employees.
