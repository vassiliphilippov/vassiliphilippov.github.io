Felidae

noun
the cat family of mammals
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːlɪˌdeɪ /; / ˈfi·lɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪdiː /; / ˈfiː.lɪ.diː / (BrE)
the biological family of mammals that includes all cats, such as lions, tigers, leopards, cheetahs, and domestic cats
