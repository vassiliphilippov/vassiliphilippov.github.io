program

noun
coherent course of study at a school or university
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
a set of courses and requirements forming a coherent course of study at a school, college, or university

Examples

university program

She transferred to a different program because her interests changed over time.
The university offers a highly competitive MBA program for business professionals.
