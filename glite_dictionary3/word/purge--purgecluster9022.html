to purge

verb
to vomit; to eject stomach contents
Pronunciation: / pɝdʒ / (AmE), / pɜːdʒ / (BrE)
to cause someone to vomit or evacuate their bowels, or to cleanse the body of impurities, especially as a medical treatment
