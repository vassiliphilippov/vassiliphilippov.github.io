Wavell

proper noun
British field marshal in WWII North Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪvəl / (AmE), / ˈweɪvəl / (BrE)
(1883–1950) British field marshal who commanded Allied forces in North Africa during World War II, defeating the Italians before being overcome by German forces.
