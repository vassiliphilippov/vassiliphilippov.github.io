Sabaoth

noun
biblical term for hosts or armies
Pronunciation: / ˈsæbiˌɔθ, ˈseɪbaʊˌɒθ / (AmE), / ˈsabɪˌɒθ, səˈbeɪɒθ / (BrE)
(in the Bible) Hosts or armies, especially as a title for God meaning 'the Lord of hosts.'
