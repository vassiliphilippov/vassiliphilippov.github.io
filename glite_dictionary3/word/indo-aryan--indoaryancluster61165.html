Indo-Aryan

adjective
relating to Indo-Aryan peoples or languages
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndoʊˈɛriən / (AmE), / ˌɪndəʊˈɛəriən / (BrE)
of or relating to the Indo-Aryan branch of the Indo-European peoples, languages, or cultures, especially those originating in South Asia.

Examples

Indo-Aryan culture
Ancient texts were written in early Indo-Aryan dialects.
Scholars study Indo-Aryan migration patterns to understand the region's history.
The Indo-Aryan languages are spoken by millions across South Asia.
