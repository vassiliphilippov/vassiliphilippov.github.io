fulfilment

noun
process of handling customer orders (business)
Pronunciation: / fʊlˈfɪlmənt / (AmE), / fʊlˈfɪlmənt / (BrE)
(in business and logistics) the process of receiving, processing, packing, and delivering customer orders to complete a sale.

Examples

order fulfilment
The retailer outsourced fulfilment to a third-party company to speed up delivery times.
Our new warehouse uses automated systems to improve fulfilment accuracy and reduce order errors.
During the holiday rush, their fulfilment team worked overnight to meet customer demand.

Related Terms

  • synonym – logistics: the planning and movement of goods from supplier to customer
  • synonym – order processing: the steps a company follows to receive and prepare customer orders
