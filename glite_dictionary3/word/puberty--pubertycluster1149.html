puberty

noun
stage of reproductive maturity development
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuːbɚti / (AmE), / ˈpjuːbəti / (BrE)
the period during which adolescents reach sexual maturity and become capable of reproduction

Examples

puberty changes
Doctors can help answer questions that arise during puberty.
Most girls begin puberty between the ages of 10 and 14.
Voice changes are a common sign of puberty in boys.
