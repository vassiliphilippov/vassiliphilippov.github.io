wasted

adjective
serving no useful purpose
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪstɪd / (AmE), / ˈweɪstɪd / (BrE)
used or done with no useful result; pointless or not effective

Examples

wasted effort
All their efforts turned out to be wasted when the plan was cancelled.
He hated giving wasted advice that nobody ever followed.
They spent hours in wasted meetings that achieved nothing.
All the time and energy put into the project ended up being wasted.
It was a wasted opportunity that they later regretted.
She felt her talents had been wasted in her previous job.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fruitless: failing to achieve the desired result
  • synonym – pointless: having no purpose; serving no useful purpose
  • antonym – productive: producing good or useful results
  • synonym – squandered: used wastefully or extravagantly without gain
  • antonym – useful: able to be used for a practical purpose or in several ways
