to freewheel

verb
coast in a vehicle without pedaling or power
Pronunciation: / ˈfriˌwil / (AmE), / ˈfriːwiːl / (BrE)
to coast in a vehicle, especially a bicycle, without using power from the pedals or engine, often relying on a freewheel mechanism

Examples

to freewheel down a hill
He let the bicycle freewheel down the hill, enjoying the smooth ride.
She loves freewheeling along country roads without pedaling.
When the engine failed, the car freewheeled quietly to a stop.

  • synonym – coast: move easily without using power
