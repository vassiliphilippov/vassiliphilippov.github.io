signorina

noun
Italian title for an unmarried woman
Pronunciation: / ˌsiːnjɔːˈriːnə / (AmE), / ˌsiːnjɔːˈriːnə / (BrE)
An Italian courtesy title for an unmarried woman, equivalent to 'Miss', used either alone or before a name.

Examples

signorina Rossi
'Buongiorno, signorina Rossi,' the teacher greeted her with a smile.
As a young signorina, she spent her summers in Florence.
The waiter politely addressed her as signorina when he brought the menu.
