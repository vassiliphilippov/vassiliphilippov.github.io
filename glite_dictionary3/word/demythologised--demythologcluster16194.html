demythologised

adjective
with mythical elements removed
Pronunciation: / diːˈmɪθəˌlɑdʒaɪzd / (AmE), / diːˈmɪθ.ə.lə.dʒaɪzd / (BrE)
Having had mythical elements or qualities removed, especially in reference to accounts, explanations, or retellings formerly steeped in myth.

Examples

demythologised history
Much of modern science is based on demythologised understandings of natural phenomena.
The demythologised account sought to separate historical fact from religious lore.
The author presented a demythologised version of the ancient legend.
