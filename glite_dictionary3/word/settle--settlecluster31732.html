to settle

verb
take up residence and become established
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛt̬.əl /; / ˈsɛt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈsɛt.əl /; / ˈsɛtl̩ / (BrE)
to make a place your home or become established in a new place
