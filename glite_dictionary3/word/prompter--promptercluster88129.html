prompter

noun
device displaying words for reading aloud
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːmp.tər / (AmE), / ˈprɒmp.tə / (BrE)
a device that displays words for people to read, especially during a performance or broadcast

Examples

TV prompter screen
During the play, the actors occasionally glanced at the prompter when they forgot their lines.
Television presenters often use a prompter to help them read lengthy scripts.
The news anchor relied on the prompter to deliver her lines smoothly.
