unbroken

adjective
not interrupted; continuous
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbroʊkən / (AmE), / ʌnˈbrəʊkən / (BrE)
Not interrupted; continuous in space, time, or sequence.

Examples

unbroken silence
She enjoyed the unbroken silence of the early morning hours.
The hiking path stretched for miles as an unbroken line through the forest.
The team maintained an unbroken winning streak throughout the season.
