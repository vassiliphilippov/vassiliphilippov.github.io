common

adjective
of low or inferior quality
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən /; / ˈkɑm.ən / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən /; / ˈkɒm.ən / (BrE)
of low or inferior quality, lacking refinement or good manners; typical of ordinary people and sometimes considered crude or unsophisticated
