suspect

adjective
regarded with suspicion or doubt
Pronunciation: / səˈspɛkt / (AmE), / səˈspɛkt / (BrE)
regarded with suspicion or doubt; not to be trusted or considered as possibly dishonest or false

Examples

The police found the business arrangement suspect after reviewing the details.
