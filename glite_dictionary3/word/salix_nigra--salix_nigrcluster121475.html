Salix nigra

noun
species of willow tree (black willow)
Pronunciation: / ˈsælɪks ˈnaɪɡrə / (AmE), / ˈsælɪks ˈnaɪɡrə / (BrE)
A species of willow native to North America, also known as black willow, with dark bark and narrow leaves, commonly found near streams and lakes.
