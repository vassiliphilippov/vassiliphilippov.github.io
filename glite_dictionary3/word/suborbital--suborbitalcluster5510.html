suborbital

adjective
trajectory not completing a full orbit
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈɔrbɪtl̩ / (AmE), / sʌbˈɔːbɪtəl / (BrE)
(of a trajectory, flight, or vehicle) reaching space but not completing one full orbit around a celestial body.

Examples

suborbital space flight
A suborbital rocket briefly entered space before returning to Earth.
The company conducted its first suborbital test flight last year.
This suborbital mission will gather data for future orbital launches.
