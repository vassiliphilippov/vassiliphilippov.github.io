to demineralise

verb
remove minerals or salts
Pronunciation: / diˈmɪnəˌraɪz / (AmE), / diˈmɪnərəlaɪz / (BrE)
to remove minerals or salts from a substance, especially water.

Examples

to demineralise water
Certain medical treatments require completely demineralised water for patient safety.
The company uses advanced filters to demineralise the water before bottling it.
You should demineralise your water supply to prevent scale buildup in the equipment.

Related Terms

  • antonym – mineralise: add minerals or salts to a substance
  • synonym – purify: remove contaminants from something
