monarda

noun
aromatic herb of the genus Monarda
Pronunciation: / məˈnɑrdə / (AmE), / məˈnɑːdə / (BrE)
Any of a genus (Monarda) of aromatic, often showy herbs of the mint family, native to North America, commonly known as bee balm.

Examples

monarda flowers bloom
She planted several monarda varieties to add color and fragrance to her herb bed.
The vibrant flowers of monarda attract bees, butterflies, and hummingbirds to the garden.
Traditional herbalists often used monarda as a remedy for colds and sore throats.
