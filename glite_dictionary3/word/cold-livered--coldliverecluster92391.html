cold-livered

adjective
emotionally detached; unfeeling
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊld ˌlɪvɚd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊld ˌlɪvəd / (BrE)
lacking warmth of feeling; emotionally detached or unresponsive, sometimes to the point of cruelty

Examples

cold-livered person
His cold-livered attitude made it hard to connect.
She was often called cold-livered for her lack of empathy.
The cold-livered judge showed no mercy in his rulings.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cold-hearted: lacking compassion or kindness; unfeeling
  • antonym – warm-hearted: kind and sympathetic; showing affection or compassion
