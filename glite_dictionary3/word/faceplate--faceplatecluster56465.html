faceplate

noun
protective covering on front of device
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪsˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈfeɪsˌpleɪt / (BrE)
A protective covering or panel fitted to the front of a device, machine, or component to shield or conceal it.
