fohn

noun
warm dry Alpine wind
Pronunciation: / foʊn / (AmE), / fəʊn / (BrE)
a warm, dry wind blowing down the northern slopes of the Alps, often causing sudden temperature increases

Examples

strong fohn wind
Ski resorts sometimes close certain slopes when a fohn is expected due to increased avalanche risk.

The arrival of the fohn brought unseasonably warm temperatures to the mountain villages.
