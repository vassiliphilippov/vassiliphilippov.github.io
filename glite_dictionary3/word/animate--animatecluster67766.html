to animate

verb
give lifelike qualities to
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈænɪmeɪt / (BrE)
to give lifelike qualities or movement to something, especially in art or film

Examples

to animate a character
New technology allows filmmakers to animate objects with incredible realism.
She used her voice to animate the puppet during the performance.
The artists animate the characters to make them appear alive.

Related Terms

  • synonym – enliven: to make something more lively or interesting
  • antonym – inanimate: not alive, lacking the qualities of life
