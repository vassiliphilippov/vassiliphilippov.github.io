neurotropic

adjective
affecting the nervous system preferentially
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈtroʊpɪk, ˌnʊrəˈtrɑpɪk / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈtrɒpɪk / (BrE)
(of a virus, toxin, or chemical) tending to attack or affect the nervous system preferentially.
