shiftily

adverb
in a sly, suspicious manner
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪftɪli / (AmE), / ˈʃɪftɪli / (BrE)
In a way that shows someone seems dishonest or evasive.

Examples

look shiftily around
He glanced shiftily around the room before slipping out the door.
She smiled shiftily when asked about the missing money.
The suspect answered the questions shiftily, avoiding eye contact throughout the interview.
