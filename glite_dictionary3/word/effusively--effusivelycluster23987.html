effusively

adverb
in an overly emotional or enthusiastic way
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfjuːsɪvli / (AmE), / ɪˈfjuːsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that shows unrestrained or excessive emotion or enthusiasm.

Examples

effusively praise someone
He greeted his old friend effusively, hugging him tightly and smiling broadly.
She effusively praised his latest novel during the interview.
The guests thanked their hosts effusively for the wonderful dinner.

  • synonym – effusive: expressing feelings of gratitude, pleasure, or approval in an unrestrained or heartfelt manner
