rumble

noun
servant's seat or luggage space in carriage
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌmbəl / (AmE), / ˈrʌmbəl / (BrE)
a servant's seat or a luggage compartment at the rear of a carriage, especially in older horse-drawn vehicles
