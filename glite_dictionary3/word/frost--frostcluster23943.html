to frost

verb
to make a surface rough or translucent
Pronunciation: / frɑst / (AmE), / frɒst / (BrE)
to cover or treat (a surface) so that it becomes rough, translucent, or lightly speckled in appearance

Examples

to frost glass
She always has her hair frosted with highlights at the salon.
Some people frost glass doors to give them a textured, fancy look.
The decorator decided to frost the bathroom window for privacy.
