slope

noun
measure of inclination from horizontal
Pronunciation: / sloʊp / (AmE), / sləʊp / (BrE)
the degree to which a line or surface inclines from the horizontal

Examples

slope of a line
Engineers calculated the slope of the road to ensure proper drainage.
The slope of the hill made cycling up it quite challenging.
The graph shows a line with a constant slope throughout its length.
