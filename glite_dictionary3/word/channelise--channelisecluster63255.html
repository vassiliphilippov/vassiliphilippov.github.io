to channelise

verb
direct or guide along a course
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæn.ə.laɪz /; / ˈtʃænəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈtʃæn.ə.laɪz /; / ˈtʃænəlaɪz / (BrE)
