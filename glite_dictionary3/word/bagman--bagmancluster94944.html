bagman

noun
collector/distributor of criminal payoff money
Pronunciation: / ˈbæɡ.mæn / (AmE), / ˈbæɡ.mən / (BrE)
a man employed by a criminal organization to collect or distribute illegal payoff money

Examples

bagman collects cash
Authorities suspected that the bagman was laundering money for the entire operation.
He worked as a bagman for several years before getting caught.
The police arrested the bagman as he was delivering money to the mob boss.
