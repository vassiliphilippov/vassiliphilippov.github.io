to laden

verb
to place a heavy load on
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdən / (AmE), / ˈleɪdən / (BrE)
to put a heavy or full load on or into something

Examples

to laden a cart
After the harvest, the farmers laden the carts with fresh produce.
The moving crew ladened the truck until it was completely full.
The workers laden the ship with containers every morning.
