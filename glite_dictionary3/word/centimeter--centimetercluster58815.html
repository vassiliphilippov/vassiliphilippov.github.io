centimeter

noun
metric unit of length (1/100 meter)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntɪˌmitər / (AmE), / ˈsɛntɪˌmiːtə / (BrE)
a metric unit of length equal to one hundredth of a meter, commonly used to measure small distances

Examples

ten centimeters long
Her height increased by three centimeters over the summer.
Please use a ruler marked in centimeters when drawing your diagram.
The fabric measured exactly 50 centimeters wide.

  • degree higher – meter: the base unit of length in the metric system
