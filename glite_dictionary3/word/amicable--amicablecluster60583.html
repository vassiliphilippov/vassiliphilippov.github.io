amicable

adjective
friendly; without hostility
Pronunciation: / ˈæmɪkəbəl / (AmE), / ˈamɪkəb(ə)l / (BrE)
(of relations or agreements) characterized by friendliness and a lack of disagreement or hostility.

Examples

amicable relationship
Despite their breakup, their relationship remained amicable and respectful.
The negotiations ended with an amicable agreement that satisfied both parties.
They reached an amicable solution to their dispute without going to court.

Related Terms

  • synonym – friendly: pleasant and kind; like a friend
  • antonym – hostile: characterized by conflict or unfriendliness
