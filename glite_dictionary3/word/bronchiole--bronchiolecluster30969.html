bronchiole

noun
smallest air passages in the lungs
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɑːŋ.ki.oʊl / (AmE), / ˈbrɒŋ.kɪ.əʊl / (BrE)
any of the small branches into which a bronchus divides, ending in alveoli in the lungs.
