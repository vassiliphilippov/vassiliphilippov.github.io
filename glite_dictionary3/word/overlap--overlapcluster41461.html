to overlap

verb
extend over and cover a part
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈlæp / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈlæp / (BrE)
to extend over and partly cover a part of something else

Examples

to overlap tiles
Make sure the tiles overlap slightly to prevent leaks.
The edges of the curtains overlap to keep out the light.
Their schedules overlap by an hour every Wednesday.

Related Terms

  • antonym – separate: to cause to move or be apart
