dashboard

noun
vehicle instrument/control panel
Pronunciation: / ˈdæʃˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈdɑːʃ.bɔːd / (BrE)
the panel in a vehicle or aircraft containing instruments and controls for operation

Examples

car dashboard
A warning light flashed suddenly on the car's dashboard.
Sarah checked the fuel gauge on the dashboard before starting her trip.
The airplane's dashboard is filled with complex instruments and displays.
