to poke

verb
send a virtual nudge
Pronunciation: / po8ak / (AmE), / p598ak / (BrE)
to send a brief virtual nudge or greeting to someone on a social network, typically to get their attention or say hello.

Examples

to poke a friend online
She poked an old friend on the site to say hello and start a short conversation.
They poke new contacts as a light greeting when they reconnect online after years apart.
After seeing the update, he poked his colleague to get their attention quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – nudge: to give a small push or reminder, physically or figuratively
