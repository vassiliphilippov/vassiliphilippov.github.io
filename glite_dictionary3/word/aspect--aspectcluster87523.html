aspect

noun
grammatical category of verb action
Pronunciation: / ˈæspɛkt / (AmE), / ˈæspɛkt / (BrE)
(Grammar) A grammatical category expressing the beginning, duration, completion, or repetition of an action as indicated by a verb.

Examples

perfect aspect
Perfect aspect expresses a completed action relevant to another point in time.
Some languages have many more aspect distinctions than English does.
The progressive aspect is used to indicate an ongoing action in the present.
