egress

noun
reappearance after an eclipse (astronomy)
Pronunciation: / ˈiːˌɡrɛs / (AmE), / ˈiːɡrɛs /; / ˈiːˌɡrɛs / (BrE)
the act of going out, leaving, or emerging from a place; an exit or means of departure (also used in astronomy for the reappearance of a celestial body)
