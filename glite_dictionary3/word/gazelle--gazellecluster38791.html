gazelle

noun
small swift antelope of Africa and Asia
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈzɛl / (AmE), / ɡəˈzɛl / (BrE)
a small, swift antelope with slender legs and lustrous eyes, native to Africa and Asia, known for its graceful movements

Examples

graceful gazelle running
A herd of gazelle darted across the plain as the sun began to set.
At the wildlife park, visitors watched a gazelle leap gracefully over a fallen branch.
The cheetah is known for hunting the swift gazelle in the savannah.
