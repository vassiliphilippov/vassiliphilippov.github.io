Plataleidae

noun
family of birds (spoonbills)
Pronunciation: / pləˈtæliˌidaɪ / (AmE), / pləˈtæliːɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of large wading birds known as spoonbills, characterized by their distinctive long, flat, spoon-shaped bills.
