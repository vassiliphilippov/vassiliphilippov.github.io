1st Viscount Montgomery of Alamein

proper noun
British WWII field marshal and military leader
Pronunciation: / fɜrst ˈvɪskaʊnt mɒnˈtɡəməri əv ˌæləˈmeɪn / (AmE), / fɜːst ˈvɪskaʊnt mɒnˈtɡʌməri əv ˌæləˈmeɪn / (BrE)
(1887–1976) An English general who led British forces to victory in North Africa during World War II and later commanded British ground troops in the Normandy invasion.
