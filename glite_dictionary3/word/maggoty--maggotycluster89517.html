maggoty

adjective
infested with maggots
Pronunciation: / ˈmæɡədi / (AmE), / ˈmaɡəti / (BrE)
spoiled and infested with maggots, especially due to decomposition or exposure to flies

Examples

maggoty meat
The maggoty fruit in the basket had to be thrown away before it spread to the others.
The cheese was so old that it had become maggoty and inedible.
We found a maggoty piece of meat in the old refrigerator.
