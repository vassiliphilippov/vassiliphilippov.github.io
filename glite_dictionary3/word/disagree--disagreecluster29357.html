to disagree

verb
not be the same; differ
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsəˈɡri / (AmE), / ˌdɪsəˈɡriː / (BrE)
(of two or more things) to be different from each other; not match or correspond.

Examples

disagree in details
The maps disagree in their depiction of the boundary lines.
The two witnesses' stories disagree on several important details.
These results disagree with those presented in the previous study.

Related Terms

  • synonym – differ: to be unlike or distinct in nature, form, or characteristics
