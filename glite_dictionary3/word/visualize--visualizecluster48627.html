to visualize

verb
make visible; render perceptible
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒuəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒuəlaɪz /; / ˈvɪʒʊəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something visible, especially using technology or special methods (including in medicine)
