to socialize

verb
to participate in social activities
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəlaɪz / (BrE)
to participate in social activities and interact with other people

Examples

to socialize at parties
After moving to a new city, he made an effort to socialize more and meet his neighbors.
At the company picnic, everyone had a chance to socialize and enjoy the afternoon.
She finds it easy to socialize with people from different backgrounds.

Related Terms

  • antonym – isolate: to remain apart from others; to keep away from social contact
  • synonym – mingle: to move among and talk to other people at a social event
