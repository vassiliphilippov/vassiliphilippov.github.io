Anglo-French

noun
the French language used in medieval England
Pronunciation: / ˌæŋɡloʊ ˈfrɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˌæŋɡləʊ ˈfrɛntʃ / (BrE)
the French language as spoken and used in medieval England, especially by the ruling classes and in law and administration

Examples
