celluloid

noun
medium for photographic film or movies
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛl.jə.lɔɪd /; / ˈsɛljəˌlɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.jʊ.lɔɪd /; / ˈsɛljʊlɔɪd / (BrE)
a transparent, flammable material used in early photographic film and plastics, and by extension, a reference to movies or the film industry
