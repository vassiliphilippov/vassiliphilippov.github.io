simulation

noun
computerized imitation of real-world processes
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪmjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪmjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
an imitation or model of a real-world process, system, or situation, often using computers, for study, training, or testing

Examples

  • synonym – emulation: the process of imitating another system, especially in computing
  • synonym – model: a simplified representation used to explain or predict real-world processes
  • antonym – reality: the state of things as they actually exist
  • synonym – replica: an exact copy of something, often on a different scale
