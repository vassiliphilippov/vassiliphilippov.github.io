wizard

noun
an exceptionally skilled person
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪzɚd / (AmE), / ˈwɪzəd / (BrE)
a person who is dazzlingly skilled in a particular field or activity, often showing great ingenuity or expertise

Examples

wizard with computers

She is a wizard at solving complex mathematical problems.
The young programmer proved himself a real wizard with computer code.
