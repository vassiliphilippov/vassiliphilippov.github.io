nursling

noun
infant tended by a nurse
Pronunciation: / ˈnɝːs.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɜːs.lɪŋ / (BrE)
an infant or young child being cared for by a nurse or nursemaid, especially one being breastfed

Examples
