to step

verb
set a mast upright on a ship
Pronunciation: / stɛp / (AmE), / stɛp / (BrE)
(nautical) To set or fix (a mast) upright in its step on a ship.

Examples

to step the mast
After a storm, they had to step the mast again to make the ship seaworthy.
It took several sailors to properly step the replacement mast during repairs.
The crew worked together to step the main mast before the voyage began.
