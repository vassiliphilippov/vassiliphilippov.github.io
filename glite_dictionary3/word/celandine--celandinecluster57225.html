celandine

noun
a perennial herb with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlənˌdaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsiːləndiːn / (BrE)
A perennial herb with branched woody stems and bright yellow flowers, commonly found in Europe and Asia.
