Atakapa

noun
member of Indigenous people of Louisiana and Texas
Pronunciation: / ˌætəˈkɑːpə / (AmE), / ˌatəˈkɑːpə / (BrE)
a member of an Indigenous people who formerly lived along the Gulf Coast of Louisiana and Texas

Examples

the Atakapa people
A descendant of the Atakapa people spoke about preserving their culture.
Many artifacts belonging to the Atakapa have been found in Louisiana.
The Atakapa were known for their subsistence on fishing and hunting along the Gulf Coast.
