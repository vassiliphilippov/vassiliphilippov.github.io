corn

noun
painful thickened skin on toes
Pronunciation: / kɔrn / (AmE), / kɔːn / (BrE)
a small, painful area of thickened skin, usually on the toes, caused by pressure or friction, especially from ill-fitting shoes

Examples

painful corn on toe
He soaked his feet to relieve the pain caused by a corn between his toes.
If you wear tight shoes, you may develop a corn on your foot.
The doctor examined the corn on her toe and recommended better-fitting shoes.

Related Terms

  • synonym – callus: an area of thickened skin caused by pressure or friction
