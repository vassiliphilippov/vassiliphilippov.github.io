to range

verb
project over a certain distance
Pronunciation: / reɪndʒ / (AmE), / reɪndʒ / (BrE)
(of a gun or other device) to have a specified distance over which it can shoot or project something.

Examples

range a target
Older cannons rarely ranged more than a mile.
The new artillery can range targets up to 20 kilometers away.
The rifle ranges farther than most in its class.

Related Terms

  • synonym – reach: to extend as far as a specified point
