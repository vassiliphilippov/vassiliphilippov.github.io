Canna

noun
genus of tropical, lily-like herbs
Pronunciation: / ˈkænə / (AmE), / ˈkanə / (BrE)
A genus of perennial, lily-like herbs native to the tropical and subtropical Americas, known for their large leaves and brightly colored flowers; the sole genus of the family Cannaceae.
