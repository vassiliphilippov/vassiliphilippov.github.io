to beware

verb
to be cautious or on guard
Pronunciation: / bɪˈwɛr / (AmE), / bɪˈweə / (BrE)
to be cautious and alert to dangers or risks; to be on guard

Examples

to beware of danger
Beware of the dog sign on the gate warns visitors to be careful.
She told him to beware when walking alone at night.
Travelers are advised to beware of pickpockets in crowded tourist areas.
