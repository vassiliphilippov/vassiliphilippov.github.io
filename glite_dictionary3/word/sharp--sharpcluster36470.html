sharp

adjective
dangerously steep
Pronunciation: / ʃɑrp / (AmE), / ʃɑːp / (BrE)
(of a slope, drop, or angle) dangerously steep or sudden.

Examples

sharp turn on road
A sharp incline made cycling up the hill quite challenging.
Be careful—there is a sharp drop just beyond the edge of the trail.
The mountain path became so sharp that we had to use our hands to climb.

  • antonym – gentle: not steep or abrupt (of a slope)
