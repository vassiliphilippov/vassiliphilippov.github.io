glint

noun
a brief flash of light
Pronunciation: / ɡlɪnt / (AmE), / ɡlɪnt / (BrE)
a small, quick flash or gleam of light, often reflected from a surface

Examples

The diamond gave off a brilliant glint in the afternoon light.
