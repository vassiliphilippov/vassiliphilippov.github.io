m'lady

noun
sarcastic label for pretentious chivalrous person
Pronunciation: / məˈleɪdi / (AmE), / məˈleɪdi / (BrE)
Informal, sarcastic term (often online) for a person—typically a man—who affectingly adopts archaic, chivalrous manners or language; used to mock pretentious, performative chivalry.
