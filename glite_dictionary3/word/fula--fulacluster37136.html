Fula

noun
a West African language spoken by the Fulani people
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːlə / (AmE), / ˈfuːlə / (BrE)
a group of related languages spoken by the Fulani people across West Africa, widely used as a lingua franca from Senegal to Chad
