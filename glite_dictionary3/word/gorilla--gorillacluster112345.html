gorilla

noun
a large ape from central and western Africa
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈrɪlə / (AmE), / ɡəˈrɪlə / (BrE)
a very large, strong ape with black fur, native to the forests of central and western Africa, and the largest living primate

Examples

wild gorilla
Researchers spent weeks in the rainforest tracking a family of wild gorillas.
The gorilla sat quietly among the dense bamboo, watching the visitors with calm eyes.
Though powerful, a gorilla is typically gentle unless threatened.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chimpanzee: a smaller, intelligent ape from Africa
  • synonym – orangutan: a large, red-haired ape from Southeast Asia
