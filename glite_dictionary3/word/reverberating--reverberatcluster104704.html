reverberating

adjective
characterized by resonance or echoing sound
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɜrbəˌreɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːbəreɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a sound) continuing in a series of echoes; full of or characterized by resonance.

Examples

reverberating sound waves
Her voice was reverberating through the empty auditorium.
The reverberating sound of the church bells filled the entire valley.
They listened to the reverberating thunder after the storm began.

Related Terms

  • synonym – echoing: producing a sound that is repeated or reflected
  • synonym – resonant: (of sound) deep, clear, and continuing to sound or echo
