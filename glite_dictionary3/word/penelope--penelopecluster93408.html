Penelope

proper noun
faithful wife of Odysseus in Greek mythology
Pronunciation: / pəˈnɛləpi / (AmE), / pəˈnɛləpi / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, the wife of Odysseus, renowned for her faithfulness and devotion during his long absence after the Trojan War.
