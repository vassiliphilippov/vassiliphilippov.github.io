Issachar

proper noun
tribal ancestor in the Old Testament
Pronunciation: / ˈɪsəˌkɑr / (AmE), / ˈɪsəkɑː / (BrE)
(in the Bible) a son of Jacob and Leah, regarded as the ancestor of one of the twelve tribes of Israel.

Issachar son of Jacob
