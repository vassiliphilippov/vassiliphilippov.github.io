remit

noun
assigned area of responsibility or authority
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɪt / (AmE), / ˈriː.mɪt / (BrE)
the area of responsibility or authority that a person, committee, or organization is assigned to deal with

Examples

remit of a committee
Education reform is outside my official remit.
Environmental law does not fall within the remit of this committee.
The task force's remit included reviewing the safety procedures.
