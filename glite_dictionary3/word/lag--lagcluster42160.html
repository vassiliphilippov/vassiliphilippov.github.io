to lag

verb
throw at a mark (as with coins)
Pronunciation: / læɡ / (AmE), / læɡ / (BrE)
to throw or toss something, such as a coin, toward a mark, especially as part of a game

Examples

to lag a coin
He lagged his coin with impressive accuracy, winning the round.
In the old game, each child would lag a penny toward the wall.
Players take turns lagging coins to see who lands closest to the marker.
