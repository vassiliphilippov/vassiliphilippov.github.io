gourd

noun
hard-shelled inedible fruit
Pronunciation: / ɡɔrd / (AmE), / ɡɔːd / (BrE)
any of various inedible fruits with hard rinds, often used as utensils or for decoration

Examples

dried gourd decoration
Many traditional African instruments are made using dried gourd shells.
She displayed a collection of painted gourds on the mantelpiece.
The artist carved a beautiful design into the gourd to make a decorative bowl.
