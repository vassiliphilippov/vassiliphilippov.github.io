zep

noun
a type of large sandwich (US regional)
Pronunciation: / zɛp / (AmE), / zɛp / (BrE)
A large sandwich consisting of a long crusty roll split lengthwise and filled with meats, cheese, and often tomato, onion, lettuce, and condiments; known regionally in the United States.

Examples

a zep sandwich
I ordered a ham and cheese zep for lunch from the corner deli.
In Norristown, the zep is a local favorite that many people grow up eating.
She brought a huge zep to the picnic, packed with salami, provolone, onions, and tomatoes.
