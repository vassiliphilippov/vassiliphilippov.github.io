lengthening

noun
the act of making longer
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛŋθənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛŋθənɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of making something longer in space or time; the act of extending or prolonging something.

Examples

gradual lengthening of days
Doctors noticed a gradual lengthening of daylight hours as spring approached.
The lengthening of the skirt brought it back into fashion.
The sudden lengthening of the meeting frustrated several attendees.

Related Terms

  • synonym – prolongation: the action of making something last longer
  • antonym – shortening: the act of making something shorter
