nitrous

adjective
containing or relating to nitrogen
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtrəs / (AmE), / ˈnɪtrəs / (BrE)
(of a chemical substance) containing or relating to nitrogen, especially with a lower oxidation state.

Examples

nitrous compound
Certain fertilizers contain nitrous compounds to help promote plant growth.
Engineers are studying the environmental impact of nitrous emissions from vehicles.
The doctor used nitrous oxide as an anesthetic during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • antonym – nitric: containing or relating to nitrogen, especially with a higher oxidation state
