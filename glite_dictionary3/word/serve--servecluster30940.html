serve

noun
stroke that starts play in sports
Pronunciation: / sɝv / (AmE), / sɜːv / (BrE)
(in sports) a stroke that starts play by putting the ball into play, especially at the beginning of a game or after a point.

Examples

a strong serve
After a fault on his second serve, he lost the point.
Her first serve was an ace that landed just inside the line.
His powerful serve intimidated his opponent throughout the match.

  • synonym – service: the act of serving the ball in tennis and similar sports
