antiseptic

adjective
scrupulously honest; morally clean
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tɪˈsɛp.tɪk /; / ˌæn.tiˈsɛp.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈsɛp.tɪk /; / ˌæn.tɪˈsep.tɪk / (BrE)
(figurative) morally or figuratively clean, pure, or free from corruption, error, or anything offensive
