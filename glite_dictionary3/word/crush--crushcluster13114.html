crush

noun
a dense crowd of people
Pronunciation: / krʌʃ / (AmE), / krʌʃ / (BrE)
A large and densely packed crowd of people.

Examples

a crush of people

Rescue workers struggled to reach the injured through the crush of people in the corridor.
