ahuehuete

noun
Montezuma cypress, famed ancient Mexican tree
Pronunciation: / ɑːˌweɪ.wɛˈteɪ / (AmE), / ɑːˌweɪ.ˈweɪ.teɪ / (BrE)
A Montezuma cypress tree native to Mexico, especially referring to the renowned ancient specimen at Santa Maria del Tule, noted for its immense size and age.

Examples

ancient ahuehuete tree
Botanists study the ahuehuete to learn about resilience and longevity in trees.
Many consider the ahuehuete a national symbol of Mexico's natural heritage.
The ancient ahuehuete at Santa Maria del Tule attracts thousands of visitors each year.
