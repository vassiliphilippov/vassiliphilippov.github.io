recap

noun
a brief summary or review
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌkæp / (AmE), / ˈriːˌkæp / (BrE)
a brief summary or review of what has been discussed, often at the end of a meeting, broadcast, or report

Examples

meeting recap
After a long meeting, the manager provided a quick recap for those who joined late.
At the end of the presentation, she gave a recap of all the major points discussed.
The news program always ends with a recap of the day's top stories.

Related Terms

  • synonym – review: a formal assessment or summary of something
  • synonym – summary: a brief statement or account giving the main points
