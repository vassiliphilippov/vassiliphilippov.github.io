to commit

verb
entrust to someone's care or responsibility
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɪt / (AmE), / kəˈmɪt / (BrE)
to entrust someone or something to the care or protection of another; to place in charge or under responsibility

Examples

commit keys to care
Before leaving, I commit the keys to my neighbor for safekeeping.
In her will, she chose to commit her children to the care of her sister.
Many people commit their hopes and dreams to trusted friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym – delegate: to give a particular job, duty, right, etc. to someone else so that they do it for you
  • synonym – entrust: to assign the responsibility for doing something to someone
