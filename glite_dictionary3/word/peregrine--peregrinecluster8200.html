peregrine

noun
a species of falcon
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛr.ɪ.ɡrɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɛr.ɪ.ɡrɪn / (BrE)
a large, fast falcon (Falco peregrinus), widely distributed and noted for its speed, formerly used in falconry

Examples

peregrine falcon
Centuries ago, peregrine falcons were highly prized in the art of falconry.
Conservationists have helped restore peregrine populations in many urban areas.
The peregrine is renowned for its incredible diving speed when hunting prey.
