pseudoprostyle

adjective
with columns only across the portico opening
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdoʊˈproʊstaɪl / (AmE), / ˌsuːdəʊˈprəʊstaɪl / (BrE)
(of a building) having free-standing columns in a portico only along the front entrance, not along the sides.

Examples

pseudoprostyle temple facade
Architectural historians identified the building as pseudoprostyle due to the portico's distinct arrangement.
The ancient temple is pseudoprostyle, with columns only along its principal façade.
Unlike peripteral designs, a pseudoprostyle structure often has solid walls along the sides.

Related Terms

  • synonym – prostyle: having a row of columns only at the front of a building
