Oryx gazella

noun
large South African antelope (gemsbok)
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrɪks ɡəˈzɛlə / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪks ɡəˈzɛlə / (BrE)
a large South African antelope with long, straight horns and a broad black band along its flanks; also known as the gemsbok.
