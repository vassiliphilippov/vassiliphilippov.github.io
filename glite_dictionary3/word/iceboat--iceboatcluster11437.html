iceboat

noun
sailing vessel for ice travel
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈaɪsˌbəʊt / (BrE)
A sailing vessel equipped with runners and a cross-shaped frame, designed for traveling over ice.

Examples

iceboat glides on ice
After the storm, the iceboat needed repairs to its sails and runners.
He built a homemade iceboat to glide across the frozen lake.
The annual iceboat races attract enthusiasts from all over the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ice yacht: a larger, more elaborate iceboat used mainly for racing
