to wonder

verb
to feel amazement or admiration
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌndɚ / (AmE), / ˈwʌndə / (BrE)
to feel admiration, surprise, or amazement, often caused by something impressive or unexpected

Examples

to wonder at beauty
I couldn't help but wonder at the breathtaking view from the mountaintop.
She paused to wonder at the beautiful patterns in the night sky.
Tourists often wonder at the ancient architecture of the city.
