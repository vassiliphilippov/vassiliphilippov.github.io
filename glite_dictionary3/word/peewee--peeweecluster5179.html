peewee

noun
a very small person or thing
Pronunciation: / ˈpiˌwi / (AmE), / ˈpiːwiː / (BrE)
a person or thing that is very small, often used in a disparaging or informal way; a very small person.

Examples

a peewee kid
Because he was the peewee of the group, he always got the front seat in photos.
She may be a peewee, but she's stronger than most people twice her size.
The basketball team called their youngest member a peewee, but he was quick on his feet.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dwarf: a person of unusually short stature
  • antonym – giant: an extremely large person
