to brief

verb
give essential information
Pronunciation: / briːf / (AmE), / briːf / (BrE)
to give someone the essential information or instructions about a situation or task

Examples

to brief a team
She briefed me on what to expect during the interview.
Soldiers are thoroughly briefed before proceeding with their missions.
The manager briefed the team before the important client meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym – debrief: to question someone about a completed mission or task
  • synonym – inform: to give information to someone
