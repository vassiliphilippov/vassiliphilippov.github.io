indifferently

adverb
without interest or concern
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɪf.ər.ənt.li / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪf.ərənt.li / (BrE)
in a way that shows a lack of interest or concern

Examples

to watch indifferently
He indifferently watched the game, not caring who won or lost.
She answered the question indifferently, barely looking up from her book.
The manager indifferently dismissed the employee's concerns about working conditions.
