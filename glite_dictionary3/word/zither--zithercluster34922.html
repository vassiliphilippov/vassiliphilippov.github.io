zither

noun
stringed musical instrument played flat
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪðər / (AmE), / ˈzɪðə / (BrE)
a musical instrument consisting of a flat wooden soundboard with many strings stretched across it, played by plucking or striking the strings with the fingers or a plectrum while the instrument lies flat

Examples

play the zither
He carefully tuned his zither before the performance began.
She played a haunting melody on her zither at the folk music festival.
The zither adds a unique sound to traditional music in many Central European countries.
