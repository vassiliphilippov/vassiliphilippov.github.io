woodiness

noun
quality of being woody
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈwʊd.i.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being woody, especially regarding the coarse texture characteristic of wood or woody plant parts

Examples

woodiness in plant stems
Gardeners often select plants with less woodiness for softer foliage.
Overcooked green beans can develop an unpleasant woodiness in texture.
The asparagus had a noticeable woodiness near the ends.
