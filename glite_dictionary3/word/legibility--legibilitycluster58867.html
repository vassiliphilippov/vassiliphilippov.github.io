legibility

noun
ease of reading handwriting or print
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛdʒəˈbɪləti /; / ˌlɛdʒəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌlɛdʒɪˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being easy to read or understand, especially in writing or printing
