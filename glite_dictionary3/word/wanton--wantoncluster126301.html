to wanton

verb
to engage in amorous play (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑn.tən /; / ˈwɑːntən /; / ˈwɑntən / (AmE), / ˈwɒn.tən /; / ˈwɒntən / (BrE)
(archaic or literary) to behave in a playful, flirtatious, or extravagantly indulgent way, often with sensual pleasure or lack of restraint
