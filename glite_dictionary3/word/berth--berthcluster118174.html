berth

noun
a bed on a ship or train
Pronunciation: / bɝːθ / (AmE), / bɜːθ / (BrE)
a fixed bed or sleeping place on a ship or train, often arranged in tiers

Examples

berth on a train
Each passenger was assigned a berth on the overnight train to Paris.
She climbed into the upper berth and pulled the curtain closed for privacy.
The ship offers comfortable berths for travelers during long voyages.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bunk: a narrow bed stacked over or under another, especially on a ship or train
