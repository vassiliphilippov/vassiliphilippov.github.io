lithography

noun
printing process using a flat surface
Pronunciation: / lɪˈθɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / lɪˈθɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
a printing process that uses a flat surface (like stone or metal) to transfer ink only to specific areas, often for making posters or art prints
