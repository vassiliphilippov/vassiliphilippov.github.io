to take

verb
consume or regularly have (food, drink, medicine, etc.)
Pronunciation: / teɪk / (AmE), / teɪk / (BrE)
to consume or use a food, drink, or medicine regularly, or to have something added to one's food or drink

Examples

to take medicine
Do you take milk in your coffee?
I don't take sugar in my tea.
She always takes medicine with her breakfast.

Related Terms

  • synonym – consume: to eat, drink, or ingest
  • antonym – refuse: to indicate or show that one is not willing to accept or consume
