misappropriation

noun
wrongful use of entrusted property or funds
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪs.əˌproʊ.priˈeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪs.əˌprəʊ.priˈeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of dishonestly or wrongfully taking or using money, property, or ideas for one's own use
