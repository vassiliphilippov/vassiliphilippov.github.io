spreader

noun
device for scattering materials
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɛdər / (AmE), / ˈsprɛdə / (BrE)
a machine or device used to scatter seeds, fertilizer, sand, or similar materials evenly over an area.

Examples

spreader for fertilizer
After the snowfall, the city used a spreader to evenly distribute sand on the roads.
Make sure the spreader is set correctly before using it to apply fertilizer.
The lawn spreader helped her plant grass seed quickly and efficiently.
