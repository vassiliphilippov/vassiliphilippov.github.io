to signalize

verb
equip with traffic signals
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡnəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnəlaɪz / (BrE)
to equip (a road, intersection, etc.) with traffic signals

Examples

to signalize a junction
After several accidents, engineers decided to signalize the busy junction.
Many rural crossroads remain unsignalized, but urban planners hope to signalize them soon.
The city plans to signalize the intersection to improve safety for pedestrians.
