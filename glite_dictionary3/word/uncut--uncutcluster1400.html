uncut

adjective
not injured by cutting
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkʌt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌt / (BrE)
not injured or damaged by cutting; remaining intact and not having been cut

Examples

uncut hands
Despite the sharp glass all around her, her hands were uncut.
He was lucky to emerge from the accident uncut and unharmed.
The survivors were relieved to escape, their bodies uncut and safe.
