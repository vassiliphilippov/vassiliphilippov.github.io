differential

adjective
showing or based on a difference
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəl / (BrE)
relating to or dependent on differences or distinctions; showing a difference between things or people.

Examples

differential treatment
He argued that differential tuition fees could increase inequality.
Researchers observed a differential response to the drug among participants.
The new policy led to differential treatment of students from different backgrounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym – discriminatory: making or showing an unjust or prejudicial distinction
