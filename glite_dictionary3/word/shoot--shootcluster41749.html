to shoot

verb
measure altitude with a sextant
Pronunciation: / ʃut / (AmE), / ʃuːt / (BrE)
(of a navigator or astronomer) to measure the altitude of a celestial object using a sextant.

Examples

to shoot a star
He learned how to shoot a star as part of his maritime training.
