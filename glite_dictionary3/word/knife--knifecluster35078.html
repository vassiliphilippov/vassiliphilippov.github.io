knife

noun
sharp-edged cutting tool
Pronunciation: / naɪf / (AmE), / naɪf / (BrE)
A tool with a sharp, pointed blade and a handle, used for cutting.

Examples

use a knife to cut
He always carries a pocket knife when hiking in the woods.
She carefully cut the vegetables with a sharp knife.
The chef selected his favorite knife for slicing the fish.
