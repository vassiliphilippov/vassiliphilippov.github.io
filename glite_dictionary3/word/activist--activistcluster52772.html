activist

noun
person advocating social or political change
Pronunciation: / ˈæktɪvɪst / (AmE), / ˈæktɪvɪst / (BrE)
a person who campaigns vigorously for political or social change, often as part of a group

Examples

activist for climate action
Many activists have been arrested for demanding equal rights and justice.
She became an activist after witnessing injustices in her community.
The environmental activist organized a protest to raise awareness about climate change.

Related Terms

  • synonym – campaigner: a person who actively supports a cause
  • synonym – reformer: someone who works for change, especially social or political
