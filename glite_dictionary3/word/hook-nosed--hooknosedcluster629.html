hook-nosed

adjective
having a curved, prominent nose
Pronunciation: / hʊk ˈnoʊzd / (AmE), / hʊk ˈnəʊzd / (BrE)
having a prominent, curved nose resembling the shape of a hook or an eagle's beak (aquiline nose)

Examples

a hook-nosed man
He was a tall, hook-nosed man with sharp cheekbones and intense eyes.
Her hook-nosed profile was strikingly similar to that of her grandfather.
The character in the novel is described as hook-nosed and quick-tempered.

Related Terms

  • synonym – aquiline: having a curved or hooked shape, like an eagle's beak (especially of the nose)
