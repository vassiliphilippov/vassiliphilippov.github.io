to ride

verb
copulate, especially of animals (vulgar/slang)
Pronunciation: / raɪd / (AmE), / raɪd / (BrE)
(of an animal, especially a male) to copulate with another animal, usually a female; (vulgar/slang, sometimes used of humans, often offensive) to have sexual intercourse with.

Examples

a bull rides a cow
During the documentary, the bull was shown riding the cow on the farm.
Some people use the term 'ride' in a vulgar sense to mean having sex with someone.
The veterinarian observed the stallion ride the mare during breeding season.
