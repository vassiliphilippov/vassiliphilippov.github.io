hired

adjective
engaged for a fee
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪɚd / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəd / (BrE)
(of a person or service) engaged to work or perform for payment rather than voluntarily.

Examples

hired worker
She works as a hired assistant during tax season.
The company brought in several hired consultants to improve efficiency.
We got a hired car for our road trip through the countryside.
