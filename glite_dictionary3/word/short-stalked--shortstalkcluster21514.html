short-stalked

adjective
having a relatively short stalk (of plants)
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrt ˌstɔkt / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt ˌstɔːkt / (BrE)
(of a plant or plant part) having a stalk that is shorter than average or typical.

Examples

short-stalked leaves
Many herbs in this garden are short-stalked, making their leaves close to the ground.
The short-stalked species thrives in rocky soils and exposed environments.
The botanist compared short-stalked and long-stalked flowers to study their adaptability.
