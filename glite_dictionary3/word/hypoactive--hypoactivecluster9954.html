hypoactive

adjective
abnormally inactive
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌhɪpəʊˈæktɪv / (BrE)
Having less than normal activity; abnormally inactive.

Examples

hypoactive thyroid gland
A hypoactive gastrointestinal tract can lead to constipation and discomfort.
Children with a hypoactive response may seem withdrawn or less responsive in stimulating environments.
The patient's hypoactive thyroid was causing fatigue and weight gain.
