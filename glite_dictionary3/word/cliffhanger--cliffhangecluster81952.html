cliffhanger

noun
suspenseful contest or story ending
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪfˌhæŋər /; / ˈklɪfˌhæŋɚ / (AmE), / ˈklɪfˌhæŋə / (BrE)
an event or ending in a story, show, or competition that leaves the outcome uncertain and keeps the audience in suspense
