free-thinking

adjective
independent in thought, especially religious
Pronunciation: / ˈfriˌθɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌθɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
forming one's own opinions independently of authority or established beliefs, especially in matters of religion.

The philosopher was known for his free-thinking approach to religious issues.
