hearty

adjective
warm and enthusiastic; friendly
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrti /; / ˈhɑrti / (AmE), / ˈhɑːti / (BrE)
showing sincere, warm, and enthusiastic friendliness or strong, wholehearted feelings

Examples

hearty welcome
She extended a hearty handshake to welcome the newcomer.
The guests were greeted with a hearty smile at the front door.
Their hearty laughter filled the room during the celebration.
Her hearty congratulations made me feel truly appreciated.
The mayor gave a hearty welcome to all the delegates at the conference.
They offered hearty support for the new initiative, never wavering in their enthusiasm.
