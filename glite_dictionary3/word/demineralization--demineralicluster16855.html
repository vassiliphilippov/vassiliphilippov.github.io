demineralization

noun
removal of minerals from a liquid
Pronunciation: / diˌmɪnərəlɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / diːˌmɪnərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the removal of minerals and mineral salts, especially from water or other liquids

Examples

water demineralization
