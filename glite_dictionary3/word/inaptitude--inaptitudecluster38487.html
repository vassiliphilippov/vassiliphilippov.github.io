inaptitude

noun
lack of ability or skill
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈæptɪˌtjud / (AmE), / ɪnˈæptɪtjuːd / (BrE)
lack of ability or skill; unfitness for a particular activity or purpose.

Examples

inaptitude for work
Despite his enthusiasm, his inaptitude made him unsuitable for the position.
Her inaptitude for math became clear after several failed exams.
The manager was concerned about the team's general inaptitude for problem-solving.

Related Terms

  • antonym – aptitude: natural ability to do something
  • synonym – ineptitude: lack of skill or ability
