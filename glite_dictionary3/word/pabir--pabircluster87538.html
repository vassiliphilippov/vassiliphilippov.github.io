Pabir

noun
Chadic language of Nigeria
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːbɪr / (AmE), / ˈpæbɪə / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken south of Lake Chad, mainly in Borno State and parts of Adamawa State, Nigeria

The Pabir language is still spoken by thousands of people in northeastern Nigeria.
