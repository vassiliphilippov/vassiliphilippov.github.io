polyestrous

adjective
having multiple periods of estrus per year
Pronunciation: / pɑliˈɛstrəs / (AmE), / pɒliˈɛstrəs / (BrE)
(of an animal) having more than one period of sexual receptivity (estrus) in a year.

Examples

polyestrous animals breed
Cows are polyestrous animals, meaning they can breed several times throughout the year.
The reproductive cycle of polyestrous species allows for more opportunities to produce offspring each season.
Unlike dogs, which are typically monoestrous, cats are polyestrous and go into heat multiple times annually.

Related Terms

  • antonym – monoestrous: having only one period of estrus per year
