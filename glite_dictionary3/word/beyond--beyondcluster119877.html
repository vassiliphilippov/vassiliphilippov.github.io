beyond

adverb
further in space, time, or degree
Pronunciation: / biˈɑnd / (AmE), / bɪˈɒnd / (BrE)
At or to a point further in space, time, or degree than something or someone.

Examples

beyond midnight
After midnight, the city becomes quiet, but some restaurants stay open beyond.
Her achievements go beyond what anyone expected from her.
The project will continue into next year and beyond.
