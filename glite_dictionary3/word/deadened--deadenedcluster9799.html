deadened

adjective
lacking sensation; numb
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdənd / (AmE), / ˈdɛdənd / (BrE)
lacking physical sensation or responsiveness; numb.

Examples

deadened nerves
After the accident, he had a deadened feeling in his left leg.
Her emotions seemed deadened by years of hardship.
The dentist worked quickly on the deadened nerve in her tooth.
