unripened

adjective
not fully ripe or mature
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈraɪpənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈraɪpənd / (BrE)
(of fruit, vegetables, or other produce) not yet fully developed or mature; not ripe.

Examples

unripened fruit
Avoid eating unripened tomatoes as they can be hard to digest.
The green, unripened fruit was not suitable for the recipe.
The jam was too tart because it was made with unripened berries.

Related Terms

  • antonym – ripe: fully developed or matured, especially for food
  • synonym – unripe: not matured or not ready for use or consumption
