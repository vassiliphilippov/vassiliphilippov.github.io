gesture

noun
movement expressing an idea or feeling
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛstʃər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛstʃə / (BrE)
a movement of part of the body, especially the hand or head, to express an idea or meaning

Examples

hand gesture
His angry gesture left no doubt about how he felt.
She made a friendly gesture by waving her hand as we arrived.
The teacher used a simple gesture to quiet the noisy class.
He waved his hand in a friendly gesture as she approached.
Nodding is a common gesture of agreement in many cultures.
The referee made a gesture to indicate a foul during the match.

Related Terms

  • synonym – motion: the act of moving one part of the body
  • synonym – sign: a movement or action conveying information
  • synonym – signal: an action or gesture that serves as a message
  • antonym – speech: spoken expression of thoughts
