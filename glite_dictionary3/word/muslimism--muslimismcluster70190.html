Muslimism

noun
alternative term for Islam
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌz.lɪˌmɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈmʊz.lɪˌmɪz.əm / (BrE)
an alternative term for the religion of Islam, the monotheistic faith founded in Arabia in the 7th century based on the teachings of Muhammad and the Koran
