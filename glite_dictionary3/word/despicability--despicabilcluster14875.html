despicability

noun
quality of being despicable
Pronunciation: / dɪˌspɪkəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / dɪˌspɪkəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
The quality of being deserving of contempt or disgust due to a lack of moral or ethical values.

Examples

moral despicability
Many people were shocked by the despicability of the politician's actions.
She could not forgive the despicability that led to such a heartless crime.
The film explores the despicability of greed and betrayal among friends.
