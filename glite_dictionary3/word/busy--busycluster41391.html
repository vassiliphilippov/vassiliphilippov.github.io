busy

adjective
unavailable for use (of facility)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪzi / (AmE), / ˈbɪzi / (BrE)
(of a telephone line, lavatory, or similar facility) unavailable for use because it is already occupied or engaged.

Examples

busy phone line
Every time I call her, the line is busy.
Sorry, the conference room is busy with another meeting right now.
The bathroom is busy, so you'll have to wait your turn.

Related Terms

  • antonym – available: free for use; not occupied or busy
  • synonym – engaged: (British) used to describe a telephone line or toilet that is unavailable or occupied
