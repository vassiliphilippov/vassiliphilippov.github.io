unswayed

adjective
not influenced or affected
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsweɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsweɪd / (BrE)
not influenced or affected by something; remaining firm or unchanged in opinion or feeling.

Examples

remained unswayed by pressure
Despite strong arguments, the committee remained unswayed and made no changes to the proposal.
His opinion on the subject was completely unswayed by public pressure.
She listened to the criticisms but stood unswayed in her decision to pursue her dream.
