peeling

noun
loss or shedding of an outer layer
Pronunciation: / ˈpilɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of shedding or losing an outer layer, especially of skin or a surface, in thin layers or scales

Examples

skin peeling
After a sunburn, peeling of the skin can occur as new layers form underneath.
He applied lotion to reduce the peeling caused by dryness in winter.
The peeling on the old walls revealed layers of paint from previous decades.
