to blend

verb
combine or harmonize with something
Pronunciation: / blɛnd / (AmE), / blɛnd / (BrE)
to mix or combine things so that they become one or work well together

Examples

to blend colors
She wanted her makeup to blend naturally with her skin tone.
The blue curtains blend perfectly with the walls in the living room.
The new wing of the building blends well with the original architecture.
She tried to blend the ingredients into a smooth paste.
The artist blended red and yellow to create a vibrant orange.
Their voices blend beautifully in harmony.
Carefully blend the flour and sugar before adding the eggs.
She tried to blend her new ideas with the team's traditional approach.
The new paint color blends well with the existing décor.
