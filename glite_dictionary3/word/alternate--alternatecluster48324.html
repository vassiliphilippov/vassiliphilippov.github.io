to alternate

verb
go back and forth between two states
Pronunciation: / ˈɔl.tərˌneɪt /; / ˈɔl.tɚ.neɪt /; / ˈɔl.tɚˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈɔːl.təˌneɪt /; / ˈɔːl.tə.neɪt / (BrE)
to take turns, go back and forth, or cause people to take turns
