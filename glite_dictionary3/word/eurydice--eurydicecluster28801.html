Eurydice

proper noun
wife of Orpheus in Greek mythology
Pronunciation: / jʊˈrɪdɪsi / (AmE), / jʊəˈrɪdɪsi / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) the wife of Orpheus, who died shortly after their wedding and for whom Orpheus descended into the underworld.
