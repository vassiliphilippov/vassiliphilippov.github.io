smart

adjective
impertinent; disrespectfully bold
Pronunciation: / smɑrt / (AmE), / smɑːt / (BrE)
(of a person or remark) showing an impudent or disrespectfully bold attitude; cheeky or impertinent.

Examples

smart remark
Don’t get smart with your teacher or you might get detention.
If you keep making smart remarks, you'll get into trouble.
She gave the officer a smart answer and was asked to leave the building.
