to hail

verb
greet with enthusiasm
Pronunciation: / heɪl / (AmE), / heɪl / (BrE)
to greet, welcome, or praise someone enthusiastically or publicly

Examples

to hail a hero
Crowds lined the streets to hail the returning heroes after the long journey.
She was hailed with cheers and applause as she stepped onto the stage.
We all gathered at the airport to hail our champion's triumphant return.
He was hailed as a hero after rescuing the child from the river.
The movie was widely hailed by critics for its originality.
The press hailed her performance as a stunning success.
