steatocystoma

noun
skin cyst filled with fatty matter
Pronunciation: / stiˌætəˈsɪstoʊmə / (AmE), / stiˌatəˈsɪstəmə / (BrE)
A cyst of the skin that contains fatty matter secreted by a blocked sebaceous gland.

Examples

steatocystoma on skin
A steatocystoma may be mistaken for other common skin cysts during initial examination.
Multiple steatocystomas can develop in some individuals, forming a condition known as steatocystoma multiplex.
The dermatologist diagnosed the lump as a steatocystoma and recommended minor surgery for removal.
