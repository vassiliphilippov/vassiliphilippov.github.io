imperial

adjective
relating to an emperor or empress
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɪriəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪəriəl / (BrE)
relating to or suitable for an emperor, empress, or empire; regal, majestic, or associated with imperial authority
