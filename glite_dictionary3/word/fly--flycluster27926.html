fly

noun
tent flap for entrance
Pronunciation: / flaɪ / (AmE), / flaɪ / (BrE)
a flap consisting of a piece of canvas that can be drawn back to provide an entrance to a tent

Examples

tent fly
Make sure to secure the fly at night to keep out insects and rain.
She opened the fly to let some fresh air into the tent.
