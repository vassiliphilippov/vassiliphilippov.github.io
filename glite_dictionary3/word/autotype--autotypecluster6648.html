autotype

noun
permanent print process using carbon pigment
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːtoʊˌtaɪp / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊˌtaɪp / (BrE)
a process for producing permanent prints using a carbon pigment; also, a print produced by this process
