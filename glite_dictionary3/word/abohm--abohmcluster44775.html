abohm

noun
unit of electrical resistance (10⁻⁹ ohms)
Pronunciation: / ˈæb.oʊm / (AmE), / ˈab.əʊm / (BrE)
a unit of electrical resistance equal to one billionth of an ohm (10⁻⁹ ohms) in the centimeter-gram-second (CGS) electromagnetic system.

Examples

one abohm resistance
In theoretical calculations, the magnetic wire's resistance was measured in abohms.
One abohm is many orders of magnitude smaller than a typical resistor's value.
