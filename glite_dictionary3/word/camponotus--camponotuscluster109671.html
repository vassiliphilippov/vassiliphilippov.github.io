Camponotus

noun
genus of carpenter ants
Pronunciation: / ˌkæm.pəˈnoʊ.təs / (AmE), / ˌkæm.pəˈnəʊ.təs / (BrE)
A genus of large ants, known as carpenter ants, that nest in wood and can damage structures by excavating galleries.
