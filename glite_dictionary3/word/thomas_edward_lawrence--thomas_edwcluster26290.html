Thomas Edward Lawrence

proper noun
Welsh soldier and author, led Arab revolt (WWI)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑməs ˈɛdwərd ˈlɔrəns / (AmE), / ˈtɒməs ˈɛdwəd ˈlɒrəns / (BrE)
(1888–1935) A Welsh soldier, scholar, and writer known for organizing the Arab revolt against Ottoman rule during World War I and for his memoir, 'Seven Pillars of Wisdom'.
