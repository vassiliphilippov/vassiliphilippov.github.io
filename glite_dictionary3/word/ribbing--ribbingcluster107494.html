ribbing

noun
good-natured teasing or mockery
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪbɪŋ / (BrE)
good-natured teasing or mockery, often intended to amuse or provoke without causing offense

Examples

friendly ribbing

He took the constant ribbing from his friends in good humor.
The group engaged in some friendly ribbing before the meeting started.
