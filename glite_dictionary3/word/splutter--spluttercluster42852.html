splutter

noun
explosive, spattering noise
Pronunciation: / ˈsplʌtər / (AmE), / ˈsplʌtə / (BrE)
A series of short, explosive, spattering noises, typically made by something malfunctioning, choking, or struggling to work.

Examples

engine splutter
The engine gave a loud splutter before coming to a stop.
There was a splutter of laughter from the back of the room.
We heard the splutter of rain against the metal roof.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sputter: A series of soft explosive or spitting sounds (noun)
