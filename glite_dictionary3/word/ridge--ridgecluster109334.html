ridge

noun
long, narrow raised border (anatomy)
Pronunciation: / rɪdʒ / (AmE), / rɪdʒ / (BrE)
a long, narrow, raised border or margin, especially on a bone, tooth, or membrane

Examples

bone ridge
A dental ridge can be felt along the top of the jawbone.
The surgeon carefully avoided the ridge of tissue during the procedure.
The tibia has a sharp ridge along its anterior surface.

Related Terms

  • synonym – crest: a prominent ridge or raised part, especially on a bone
