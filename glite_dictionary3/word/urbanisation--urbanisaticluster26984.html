urbanisation

noun
the growth of cities and urban life
Pronunciation: / ˌɝː.bən.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌɝː.bə.nɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɜː.bən.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process or condition of an area or population becoming more urban, especially as cities and towns grow and more people live in them
