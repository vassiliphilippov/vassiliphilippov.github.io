Nelumbo

noun
genus of aquatic lotus plants
Pronunciation: / nəˈlʌm.boʊ /; / nəˈlʌmboʊ / (AmE), / nɪˈlʌm.bəʊ /; / nɪˈlʌmbəʊ / (BrE)
a genus of aquatic plants known as the true lotuses, including the sacred lotus and American lotus, sometimes placed in the water lily family (Nymphaeaceae)
