noviciate

noun
period of being a novice, especially religious
Pronunciation: / noʊˈvɪʃiˌeɪt / (AmE), / nəʊˈvɪsɪeɪt / (BrE)
the period or state of being a novice, especially in a religious order, before taking final vows

Examples

religious noviciate
During her noviciate, she learned the rules and spiritual practices of the convent.
Many found the strict schedule challenging during their noviciate year.
The noviciate is often a time of reflection and preparation for new members of religious orders.
