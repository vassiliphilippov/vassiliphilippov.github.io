EST

noun
Eastern Standard Time (U.S. time zone)
Pronunciation: / i ɛs ti / (AmE), / iː ɛs tiː / (BrE)
abbreviation for Eastern Standard Time, the standard time in the eastern part of the United States, five hours behind Greenwich Mean Time (GMT−5)

Examples

EST time zone
Please note that our customer service hours are 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. EST.
Television shows often list their times in EST to avoid confusion across time zones.
The conference call is scheduled for 2:00 p.m. EST on Friday.

Related Terms

  • antonym – EDT: Eastern Daylight Time; daylight saving time for the same zone
