to respond

verb
reply or answer verbally
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɑnd / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒnd / (BrE)
to reply or react to something, either by saying something or by taking action

Examples

to respond to a question
Please raise your hand if you wish to respond during the discussion.
She paused before she responded to the reporter's question.
The teacher asked a question, and several students responded with thoughtful answers.
How do you usually respond to criticism at work?
She didn't respond to my email until the next morning.
The immune system will respond to the presence of bacteria.

Related Terms

  • synonym – answer: to reply to a question or statement
  • antonym – ignore: to deliberately not respond to something
  • synonym – react: to behave in a particular way in response to something
  • synonym – reply: to answer, especially in speech or writing
