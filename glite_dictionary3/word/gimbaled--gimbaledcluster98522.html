gimbaled

adjective
supported on gimbals to stay level
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪm.bəld / (AmE), / ˈɡɪm.bəld / (BrE)
(of an object) supported on gimbals so as to remain steady or level even when its base moves or tips.

Examples

gimbaled camera mount
A gimbaled camera mount ensures smooth, stable footage during movement.
He designed a gimbaled platform to keep the instrument steady on uneven terrain.
The ship’s compass was gimbaled to remain level in rough seas.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gimbal: a device consisting of a pivoted ring that allows an object to remain level
