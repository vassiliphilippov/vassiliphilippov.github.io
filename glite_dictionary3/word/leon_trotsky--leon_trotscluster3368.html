Leon Trotsky

proper noun
Russian revolutionary and Communist leader (1879–1940)
Pronunciation: / liˈɑn ˈtrɑtski / (AmE), / liˈɒn ˈtrɒtski / (BrE)
(1879–1940) Russian revolutionary and Communist theorist who worked closely with Lenin, helped build up the Red Army, and was later expelled from the Communist Party and assassinated in Mexico.

Examples

Leon Trotsky led revolution
Leon Trotsky played a leading role in the Russian Revolution of 1917.
After his exile, Leon Trotsky continued to write and influence socialist movements from abroad.
Many historians have studied the rivalry between Stalin and Leon Trotsky.
