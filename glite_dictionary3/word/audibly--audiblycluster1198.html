audibly

adverb
in an audible manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɑdəbli / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪbli / (BrE)
In a way that can be heard; in an audible manner.

Examples

speak audibly
He read the instructions audibly to ensure the group understood.
She sighed audibly when she heard the disappointing news.
The teacher spoke audibly so that everyone in the room could hear.
