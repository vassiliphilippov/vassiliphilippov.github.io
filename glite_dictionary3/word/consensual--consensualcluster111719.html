consensual

adjective
based on mutual consent
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɛnʃuəl / (AmE), / kənˈsɛnʃuəl / (BrE)
existing or made based on the mutual consent or agreement of all parties involved

Examples

consensual agreement
All parties signed a consensual agreement before proceeding with the merger.
The judge confirmed that the contract was consensual, with both sides in full agreement.
They engaged in a consensual conversation about their future plans.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unilateral: done by or affecting only one party or side
