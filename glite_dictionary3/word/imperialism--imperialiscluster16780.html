imperialism

noun
dominance via digital platforms and data
Pronunciation: / 6amc8p6ari59ccl6az59m / (AmE), / 6amc8p6a59ri59l6az59m / (BrE)
(in digital contexts) the dominance or influence exercised over people, markets, or states through control of digital platforms, data flows, or internet infrastructure, typically by powerful corporations or governments.

Examples

digital imperialism online
Many scholars argue that big tech's control over global platforms amounts to digital imperialism.
Activists criticize the company's policies as a form of economic imperialism over user data and markets.
Debates on internet governance increasingly focus on preventing digital imperialism by powerful states and firms.

Related Terms

  • synonym – data colonialism: the extraction and appropriation of personal and social data by corporations or states for power and profit
  • synonym – digital colonialism: the process by which digital technologies and platforms reproduce colonial patterns of control and extraction
