intertribal

adjective
between or among tribes
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈtraɪbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈtraɪbəl / (BrE)
Occurring between or involving different tribes.

Examples

intertribal conflict
Dancers from different nations participated in the annual intertribal powwow.
Several treaties were signed to end intertribal conflicts in the region.
The museum displayed artifacts from intertribal trade routes spanning the continent.
