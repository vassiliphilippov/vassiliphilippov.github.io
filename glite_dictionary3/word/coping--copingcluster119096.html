coping

noun
brick or stone cap on the top of a wall
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊpɪŋ / (BrE)
A brick or stone laid sideways at the top of a wall to form a protective cap or finish.

Examples

coping stone wall
Rainwater runs off easily thanks to the sloped coping along the garden wall.
The mason carefully placed the final coping to protect the top of the wall from weather.
We chose a stone coping to add both beauty and durability to the new fence.
