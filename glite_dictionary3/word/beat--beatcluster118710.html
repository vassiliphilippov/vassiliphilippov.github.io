to beat

verb
to puzzle or bewilder someone
Pronunciation: / bit / (AmE), / biːt / (BrE)
to be too difficult for someone to understand or solve; to puzzle or bewilder someone

Examples

beats me why
It beats me why anyone would want to climb such a dangerous mountain.
Quantum physics beats most students when they first encounter it.
The complexity of the instructions beats me every time I try to follow them.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mystify: to utterly confuse or puzzle someone
  • synonym – perplex: to make someone feel completely baffled
