to chitchat

verb
talk casually about unimportant things
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪtˌtʃæt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪtˌtʃæt / (BrE)
to engage in light, informal conversation about unimportant topics

Examples

to chitchat with friends
After the meeting, the colleagues chitchatted about their weekend plans.
They enjoy chitchatting in the break room during lunch.
We sat and chitchatted over coffee for almost an hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chat: to talk in a friendly, informal way
  • degree higher – discuss: to talk about something in detail
  • degree higher – gossip: to talk about other people's private lives
