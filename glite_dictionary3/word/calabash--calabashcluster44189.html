calabash

noun
bottle made from a dried gourd shell
Pronunciation: / ˈkæləˌbæʃ /; / ˈkæl.əˌbæʃ / (AmE), / ˈkæləbæʃ /; / ˈkæl.ə.bæʃ / (BrE)
a round gourd or a container made from the dried shell of this gourd, often used for storing liquids, as a utensil, or as a musical instrument
