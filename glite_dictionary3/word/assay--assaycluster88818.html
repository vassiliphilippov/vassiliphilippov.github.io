to assay

verb
analyze chemically
Pronunciation: / əˈseɪ / (AmE), / əˈseɪ / (BrE)
to analyze or examine a substance, especially to determine its composition or quality, often by chemical means

Examples

to assay a mineral
Scientists assayed the mineral deposit to determine its gold content.
Technicians are assaying blood samples for signs of infection.
The lab will assay the water samples to check for trace metals.
