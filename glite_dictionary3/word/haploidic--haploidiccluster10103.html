haploidic

adjective
having a single set of chromosomes
Pronunciation: / hæpˈlɔɪdɪk / (AmE), / hæpˈlɔɪdɪk / (BrE)
(Biology) Having a single set of chromosomes in the cell, as in gametes of organisms.
