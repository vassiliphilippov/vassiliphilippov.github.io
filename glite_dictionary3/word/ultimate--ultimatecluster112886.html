ultimate

adjective
furthest/highest in degree or order
Pronunciation: / ˈʌltəmət / (AmE), / ˈʌltɪmət / (BrE)
being the furthest or highest in degree or order; utmost or extreme.

Examples

ultimate challenge
Climbing Mount Everest is considered the ultimate challenge for many mountaineers.
Their ultimate goal is to build a sustainable business that helps the community.
Winning the gold medal was the ultimate achievement of her career.

  • antonym – minimal: of the least possible amount or degree
  • synonym – supreme: highest in rank or authority
