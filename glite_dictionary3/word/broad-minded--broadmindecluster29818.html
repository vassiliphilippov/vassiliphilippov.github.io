broad-minded

adjective
open and accepting of different views
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔdˌmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdˌmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
Willing to accept and respect opinions or behaviors that are different from your own.

Examples

broad-minded attitude
He praised the leader's broad-minded approach to complex social issues.
Her broad-minded attitude helped create a welcoming environment for people from diverse backgrounds.
The company values broad-minded individuals who are open to new ideas and approaches.

Related Terms

  • antonym – narrow-minded: not willing to accept opinions or behaviors different from one's own
  • synonym – open-minded: willing to consider new ideas and perspectives
  • synonym – tolerant: willing to accept feelings, habits, or beliefs that are different from your own
