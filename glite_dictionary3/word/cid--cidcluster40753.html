CID

noun
caller identification service
Pronunciation: / si aɪ ˈdi / (AmE), / siː aɪ ˈdiː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for caller identification: a service or display that shows the telephone number and sometimes the name of an incoming caller on a device.

Examples

CID displays number
Before answering, she checked the phone's CID display to see who was calling.
Telecom regulations require networks to transmit accurate CID information with each outgoing call today.
An unknown number blocked my mother because her phone didn't show a CID for that caller.

Related Terms

  • synonym – CLI (calling line identification): a technical term for the transmission of the caller's number between telephone networks
  • synonym – caller identification: the service or feature that displays the number or name of an incoming caller
