glazier

noun
person who installs glass
Pronunciation: / ˈɡleɪziɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡleɪziə / (BrE)
a person whose job is cutting, fitting, and installing glass for windows and doors

Examples

glazier installs windows
After the storm, a glazier was called to replace several broken windows.
He apprenticed as a glazier before starting his own glass business.
The glazier carefully installed the new window pane in the storefront.
