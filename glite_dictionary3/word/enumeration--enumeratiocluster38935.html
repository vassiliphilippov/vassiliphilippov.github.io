enumeration

noun
the act of counting or listing
Pronunciation: / ɪˌnuːməˈreɪʃən /; / ɪˌnuməˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌnjuːməˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of counting or listing things, or the list that results from this process

Examples

enumeration of items
Her enumeration of the reasons for leaving sounded well rehearsed.
The census requires a careful enumeration of every resident in the city.
The teacher asked the class for an enumeration of all the prime numbers under 50.
Each enumeration in the appendix is accompanied by a brief description.
The enumeration of the contents made it easy to find each section.
The report included an enumeration of all the tasks that had been completed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – catalogue: an ordered list, usually in book or pamphlet form
  • synonym – counting: the action of finding the total number of something
  • synonym – list: a series of items written, printed, or spoken consecutively
  • synonym – listing: the action of listing things in order
