Tanacetum coccineum

noun
a garden perennial with pink or white flowers; source of pyrethrum
Pronunciation: / ˌtænəˈsiːtəm kɒkˈsɪniəm / (AmE), / ˌtanəˈsiːtəm kɒkˈsɪniəm / (BrE)
A perennial plant of Asiatic origin cultivated for its divided aromatic leaves and pink-purple to white daisy-like flowers; also used as a source of natural insecticide (pyrethrum).
