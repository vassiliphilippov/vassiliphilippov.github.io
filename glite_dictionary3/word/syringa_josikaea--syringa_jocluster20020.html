Syringa josikaea

noun
a central European lilac shrub
Pronunciation: / sɪˈrɪŋɡə joʊˈsɪkiˌiːə / (AmE), / sɪˈrɪŋɡə jɒˈsɪkiːə / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub native to central Europe, with elliptic leaves and upright clusters of lilac or deep violet flowers; also known as the Hungarian lilac.
