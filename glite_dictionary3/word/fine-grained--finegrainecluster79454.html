fine-grained

adjective
having very small particles or grains
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪn ˌɡreɪnd / (AmE), / ˈfaɪn ˌɡreɪnd / (BrE)
consisting of or having very small, closely packed particles or a fine texture (used for materials like sugar, wood, stone, etc.)
