whippoorwill

noun
North American nocturnal bird
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪpərˌwɪl / (AmE), / ˈwɪpəwɪl / (BrE)
A nocturnal North American bird (Caprimulgus vociferus) with a distinctive repeated call, characterized by grey-and-white plumage.

Examples

a whippoorwill calls at night
As dusk settled, the whippoorwill called from the woods near the river.
