enchantment

noun
great delight caused by something wonderful
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtʃæntmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈtʃɑːntmənt / (BrE)
a feeling of great delight or pleasure, often as if under a magical spell

The forest at dawn filled him with a sense of enchantment he could not describe.
