disproportional

adjective
out of proportion; unequal in size or degree
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪs.prəˈpɔr.ʃə.nəl / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.prəˈpɔː.ʃə.nəl / (BrE)
too large or too small in comparison with something else; out of proportion.

Examples

disproportional number of people
A disproportional number of resources were allocated to the smaller department.
The punishment seemed disproportional to the minor offense committed.
Their investment was disproportional to the potential benefits.
