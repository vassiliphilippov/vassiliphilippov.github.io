flowery

adjective
resembling or related to flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊ.ɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ər.i / (BrE)
having or suggesting the qualities, appearance, or fragrance of flowers.

Examples

flowery dress
