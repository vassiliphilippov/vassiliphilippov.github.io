to forecast

verb
predict future events, especially weather
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌkæst /; / ˈfɔːr.kæst / (AmE), / ˈfɔːkɑːst /; / ˈfɔː.kɑːst / (BrE)
to predict or estimate what is likely to happen in the future, especially using information or analysis
