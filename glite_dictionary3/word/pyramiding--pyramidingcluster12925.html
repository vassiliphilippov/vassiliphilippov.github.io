pyramiding

noun
fraudulent business practice resembling a pyramid scheme
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪr.əˌmaɪd.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪə.rəˌmaɪd.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of expanding a chain of distribution by adding more distributors who sell to one another, often at progressively higher prices, leading to artificially inflated retail prices; commonly associated with fraudulent pyramid schemes

Examples

pyramiding sales network
Her research exposed widespread pyramiding in certain multi-level marketing firms.
Regulators warned the public about the risks of getting involved in pyramiding operations.
The company was accused of pyramiding and defrauding thousands of investors.
