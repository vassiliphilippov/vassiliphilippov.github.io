squeezability

noun
quality of being easily compressed
Pronunciation: / skwiːzəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / skwiːzəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being able to be compressed or occupy less space when pressure is applied

Examples

squeezability of a bottle
Designers considered both squeezability and durability when choosing the packaging material.
The squeezability of the new plastic bottles makes them convenient for travel.
Toy manufacturers test for squeezability to ensure products are safe for children.

  • synonym – compressibility: the ability of a substance to be reduced in volume
