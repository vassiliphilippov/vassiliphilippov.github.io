avatar

noun
digital user image or character
Pronunciation: / \u02c8\u00e6v\u0259\u02cct\u0251r / (AmE), / \u02c8\u00e6v\u0259t\u0251\u02d0 / (BrE)
a graphical image, icon, or digital character that represents a user in online platforms, video games, forums, or virtual environments.

Examples

online avatar image
She customized her online avatar to resemble her in-game character.
His forum avatar shows his initials rather than a personal photograph for privacy.
In the virtual meeting, everyone chose a cartoon avatar to make the space feel friendlier.

Related Terms

  • synonym – digital persona: the online identity or character a person presents in digital environments
  • synonym – icon: a small graphical symbol or image used to represent something, often a user or application
  • synonym – profile picture: a digital image used to represent a user's identity on social media or online accounts
