to relate

verb
have or establish a connection
Pronunciation: / rɪˈleɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪt / (BrE)
to have or establish a connection or association with; to understand or identify with someone or something

Examples

relate to people
Her experiences in another country helped her relate to people from different backgrounds.
I often find it hard to relate to abstract art.
It can be difficult for parents to relate to their teenage children.

Related Terms

  • synonym – connect: to join or link together
  • synonym – identify: to regard oneself as sharing the same characteristics or experiences
  • antonym – separate: to set or keep apart; not connected
