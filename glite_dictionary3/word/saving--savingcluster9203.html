saving

noun
reduction in cost, expenditure, or resource use
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈseɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
an act or instance of reducing expenditure, cost, or resource use; the amount of money, time, or resources saved

Examples

saving money
By booking her flight early, Anna managed to get a saving of fifty dollars.
The new energy-efficient heating system led to a significant saving on their utility bills.
Walking to work every day resulted in a noticeable saving on transportation costs.
