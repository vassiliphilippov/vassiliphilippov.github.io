sportsmanlike

adjective
showing fairness and sportsmanship
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔrts.mənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts.mənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
showing or characterized by fairness, respect, and generous behavior in sports or competition; exhibiting sportsmanship.

Examples

sportsmanlike behavior
Her sportsmanlike attitude made her a role model for other athletes.
It was a sportsmanlike gesture to shake hands with the opponents at the end of the game.
The team was praised for their sportsmanlike behavior after losing the match.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sporting: showing fairness or being generous, especially in sports
  • antonym – unsportsmanlike: not showing fairness or sportsmanship
