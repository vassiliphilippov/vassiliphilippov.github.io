pitta

noun
brightly colored terrestrial bird genus
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtə / (AmE), / ˈpɪtə / (BrE)
A brightly colored, chiefly terrestrial bird of the genus Pitta, with short wings and tail and a stout bill, found in Asia, Australia, and Africa.
