rotogravure

noun
printed material or process using rotary intaglio printing
Pronunciation: / ˌroʊtəɡrəˈvjʊr / (AmE), / ˌrəʊtəɡrəˈvjʊə / (BrE)
a printing process that uses engraved cylinders to produce high-quality images, especially for magazines and packaging; also, printed material made by this process
