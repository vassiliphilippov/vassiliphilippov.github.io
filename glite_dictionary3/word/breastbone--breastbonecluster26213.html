breastbone

noun
the flat bone in the center of the chest
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛstˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈbrɛstbəʊn / (BrE)
the flat bone at the front of the chest to which the ribs and clavicles are attached; also called the sternum

Examples

breastbone pain
She was struck on the breastbone by the soccer ball, but was not badly hurt.
The breastbone protects vital organs such as the heart and lungs.
The doctor pressed gently on his breastbone during the examination.
