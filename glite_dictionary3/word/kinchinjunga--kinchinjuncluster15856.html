Kinchinjunga

proper noun
third highest mountain in the world
Pronunciation: / kɪn.tʃɪnˈdʒʌŋ.ɡə / (AmE), / kɪn.tʃɪnˈdʒʌŋ.ɡə / (BrE)
a mountain in the Himalayas, located on the border between Nepal and the Indian state of Sikkim, and the third highest mountain in the world (28,169 feet or 8,586 meters high)
