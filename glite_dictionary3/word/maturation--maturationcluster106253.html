maturation

noun
formation of pus in an abscess
Pronunciation: / ˌmætʃəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmatʃəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
(Medicine) The formation of pus in an abscess or vesicle, especially as part of the process leading to discharge.

Examples

maturation of pus
Antibiotics were prescribed while monitoring the maturation of the pus-filled vesicle.
Proper maturation of the infected area can be crucial for effective drainage.
