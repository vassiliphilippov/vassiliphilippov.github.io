biogenous

adjective
produced by living organisms
Pronunciation: / baɪˈɑːdʒənəs / (AmE), / baɪˈɒdʒ.ɪ.nəs / (BrE)
(of a substance or material) produced by or originating from living organisms.

Examples

biogenous sediment layer
Calcium carbonate in chalk is a biogenous sediment formed by the accumulation of shell fragments.
Many marine sediments are biogenous in origin, containing remains of organisms like algae and plankton.
Scientists study biogenous deposits to understand past life forms and environmental changes.
