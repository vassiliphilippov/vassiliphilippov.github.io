shirker

noun
person who avoids duty or work
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɝːkər / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who avoids work, duty, or responsibility, especially deliberately

Examples

lazy shirker
During the war, anyone suspected of being a shirker faced severe criticism from the public.
No one wanted to be labeled a shirker, so everyone volunteered to help.
The company quickly noticed the shirker who always found excuses to avoid difficult tasks.

Related Terms

  • synonym – slacker: a person who avoids work or effort
  • antonym – worker: a person who works, especially a person who does a particular kind of work
