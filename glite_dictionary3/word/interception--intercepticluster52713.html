interception

noun
the act of stopping or interrupting
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.tɚˈsɛp.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈsɛp.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of stopping or interrupting something before it reaches its intended destination

Examples

interception of a message
His quick interception of the ball changed the course of the game.
Police managed the interception of the suspect before he could cross the border.
The interception of the message prevented the secret from being revealed.
