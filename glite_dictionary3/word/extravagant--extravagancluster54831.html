extravagant

adjective
excessive; unrestrained, especially with feelings
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstrævəɡənt / (AmE), / ɪkˈstrævəɡənt / (BrE)
Unrestrained or excessive, especially in expression of feelings, actions, or praise.

Examples

extravagant praise
His extravagant gestures made his excitement clear to everyone.
She was known for her extravagant displays of affection.
The governor's extravagant praise for the mayor surprised the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym – excessive: Exceeding a normal, usual, or proper limit
  • antonym – restrained: Characterized by moderation or self-control
