solo

adjective
performed by a single voice or instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊləʊ / (BrE)
(of music or performance) composed or performed by a single voice or instrument, not accompanied by others.

Examples

solo singer
Each member of the orchestra had a chance to perform a solo passage.
She played a beautiful solo violin piece at the concert.
The solo performance showcased his incredible talent.

Related Terms

  • antonym – ensemble: music performed by a group of musicians
