infernal

adjective
used as an intensifying expletive
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɝːnl̩ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɜːnl / (BrE)
(informal, usually before a noun) Used for emphasis to express annoyance, anger, or irritation.
