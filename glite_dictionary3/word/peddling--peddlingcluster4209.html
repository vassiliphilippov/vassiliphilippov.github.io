peddling

noun
selling goods by traveling around
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛd.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɛd.lɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of selling goods, especially by going from place to place or door to door, often as a way of making a living

Examples

peddling goods door-to-door
She made a modest income through peddling handmade jewelry at the local market.
Street peddling was once a common way for immigrants to earn a living in big cities.
The city council is considering new regulations on peddling in public parks.
