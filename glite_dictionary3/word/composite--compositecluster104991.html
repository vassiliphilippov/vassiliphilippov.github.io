composite

adjective
relating to the daisy family of plants
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɑzɪt / (AmE), / kəmˈpɒzɪt / (BrE)
of, relating to, or belonging to the plant family Compositae (now commonly called Asteraceae), which includes daisies, sunflowers, and related plants.

Examples

composite flower family
Botanists often study the reproductive structures of composite species to understand plant evolution.
Sunflowers are classic examples of composite flowers, with many tiny florets arranged in a single head.
The composite family includes such familiar plants as daisies and asters.
