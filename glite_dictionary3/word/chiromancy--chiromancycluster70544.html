chiromancy

noun
fortune-telling by reading palms
Pronunciation: / ˈkaɪrəˌmænsi / (AmE), / ˈkʌɪrəmənsɪ / (BrE)
The practice of predicting a person's future by interpreting the lines and features of their palms.

Examples

chiromancy reading palms
Although chiromancy is often dismissed as a pseudoscience, it remains popular in some cultures.
He developed an interest in chiromancy after reading a book about palm reading.
Many people visit practitioners of chiromancy in hopes of discovering what their future holds.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – divination: the practice of seeking knowledge of the future by supernatural means
  • synonym – palmistry: the practice of interpreting a person's character or predicting their future by examining their palm
