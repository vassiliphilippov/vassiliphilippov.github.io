picturesque

adjective
vividly expressive; creating striking images
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪktʃərˈɛsk / (AmE), / ˌpɪktʃərˈɛsk / (BrE)
(of language or description) vividly expressive or striking in effect, often creating strong images in the mind.

Examples

picturesque language style
He gave a picturesque explanation of the city's lively streets, painting vivid scenes with his words.
Her picturesque account of the festival made us feel as though we were really there.
The novel is filled with picturesque dialogue that captures the charm of the setting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – vivid: producing powerful feelings or strong, clear images in the mind
