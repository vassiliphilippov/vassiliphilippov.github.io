observance

noun
the act of following a rule or custom
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɝː.vəns / (AmE), / əbˈzɜː.vəns / (BrE)
the act of following or adhering to a law, custom, or practice

Examples

observance of tradition
Many families have their own unique observance of holiday traditions.
Strict observance of the law ensured order in the community.
The observance of safety regulations is essential in the workplace.
