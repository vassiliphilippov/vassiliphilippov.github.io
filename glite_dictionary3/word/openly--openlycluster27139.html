openly

adverb
in a frank or public manner
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpənli / (AmE), / ˈəʊpənli / (BrE)
In a way that is not concealed or secret; in a frank or public manner.

Examples

openly admit mistakes
He openly admitted his mistake during the meeting.
She openly criticized the new policy in front of the entire team.
The couple openly discussed their relationship issues with friends.
