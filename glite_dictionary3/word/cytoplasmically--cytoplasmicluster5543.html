cytoplasmically

adverb
by means of the cytoplasm
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪtəˈplæzmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsaɪtəʊˈplæzmɪkli / (BrE)
By means of or through the cytoplasm; in a manner relating to the cytoplasm of a cell.

Examples
