shelvy

adjective
full of submerged reefs or shoals
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛl.vi / (AmE), / ˈʃɛl.vi / (BrE)
(of waters) containing or abounding with submerged reefs, sandbanks, or shoals; dangerous or shallow because of hidden underwater features.

Examples

shelvy coastline
Local fishermen warned us that the shelvy coastline could be treacherous for larger boats.
Many shipwrecks have occurred in the shelvy channels of this estuary.
The map marked the area as shelvy, indicating the presence of numerous hidden reefs.

  • synonym – shoaly: containing or full of shoals (shallow places)
