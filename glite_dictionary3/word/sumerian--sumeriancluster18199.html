Sumerian

noun
member of ancient Sumerian people
Pronunciation: / suːˈmɛriən / (AmE), / suːˈmɪəriən / (BrE)
a member of an ancient people who lived in Sumer, in southern Mesopotamia, one of the earliest known civilizations

Examples

ancient Sumerian people
A Sumerian might have participated in rituals at the ziggurats in Ur.
Archaeologists have discovered several Sumerian artifacts in the ruins of southern Mesopotamia.
The ancient Sumerian civilization developed one of the earliest writing systems in the world.
