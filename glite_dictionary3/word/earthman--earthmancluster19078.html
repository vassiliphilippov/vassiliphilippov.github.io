earthman

noun
male inhabitant of the Earth (esp. in sci-fi)
Pronunciation: / ˈɝːθˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈɜːθˌmæn / (BrE)
(in science fiction) a man who is an inhabitant of the Earth, especially as distinguished from beings of other planets.

Examples

alien meets an earthman
In many classic science fiction novels, an earthman ventures to distant worlds.
The alien ambassador studied the earthman with a mixture of curiosity and caution.
The proud earthman refused to bow before the galactic emperor.
