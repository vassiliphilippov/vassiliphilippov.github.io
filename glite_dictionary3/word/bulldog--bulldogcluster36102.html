to bulldog

verb
attack or pursue with fierce determination
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌdɔg / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌdɒg / (BrE)
to attack or pursue someone or something with determination and ferocity, like a bulldog

Examples

to bulldog a problem
Despite fierce competition, she bulldogged her way to the top of the industry.
Security guards bulldogged the intruder until he was safely restrained.
The press bulldogged the politician with relentless questions all evening.

  • synonym – attack: to take aggressive action against someone or something
