Saddam Hussein

proper noun
Iraqi former president and military leader
Pronunciation: / səˈdɑm huˈseɪn /; / səˈdɑːm huːˈseɪn / (AmE), / sæˈdæm hjuːˈseɪn /; / sæˈdæm hʊˈseɪn / (BrE)
(1937–2006) Iraqi political leader who was President of Iraq from 1979 to 2003, known for leading Iraq in wars against Iran and Kuwait and for his authoritarian rule
