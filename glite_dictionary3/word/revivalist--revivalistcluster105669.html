revivalist

noun
preacher who leads religious revivals
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvaɪvəlɪst / (AmE), / rɪˈvaɪvəlɪst / (BrE)
a preacher who promotes or conducts religious revivals, especially in the Christian faith

Examples

famous revivalist preacher
As a revivalist, she traveled across the country spreading the gospel.
Many were inspired by the revivalist and decided to join the church.
The revivalist preached passionately at the crowded tent meeting.
