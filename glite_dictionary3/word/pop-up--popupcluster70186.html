pop-up

noun
temporary retail store
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːp ˌʌp /; / pɑp ʌp / (AmE), / ˈpɒp ˌʌp /; / pɒp ʌp / (BrE)
a shop, store, or event space that operates temporarily in a location for a short period of time
