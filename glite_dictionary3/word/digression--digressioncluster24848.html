digression

noun
deviation from main path or route
Pronunciation: / daɪˈɡrɛʃ.ən / (AmE), / daɪˈɡrɛʃ.ən / (BrE)
a temporary departure from the main path or route during a journey

Examples

digression from the route
After a brief digression from the main trail, the hikers soon found their way back on course.
The tour included an intentional digression to visit a hidden waterfall off the main road.
We took a small digression through the woods to avoid the heavy traffic on the highway.

  • synonym – detour: an alternative route or deviation from a direct route
