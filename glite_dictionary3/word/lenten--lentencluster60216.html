lenten

adjective
relating to Lent (Christian period of fasting)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛntən / (AmE), / ˈlɛntən / (BrE)
relating to or appropriate for Lent, the period of fasting and penitence in the Christian calendar

Examples

lenten season
During the lenten season, many people abstain from eating meat.
She wore plain clothing as part of her lenten observance.
The family prepared a simple lenten meal of bread and soup.
