turbinate

adjective
relating to the nasal turbinate bones
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝː.bəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.bɪ.nət / (BrE)
relating to the scroll-shaped turbinate bones in the nasal passages, which help filter and humidify inhaled air
