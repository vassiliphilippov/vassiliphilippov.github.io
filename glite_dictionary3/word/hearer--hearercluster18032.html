hearer

noun
someone who listens
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪrər / (AmE), / ˈhɪərə / (BrE)
a person who listens, especially someone who pays attention to what is being said

Examples

attentive hearer
As a good hearer, he understood not just words but emotions.
She was a patient hearer who never interrupted the speaker.
Teachers value students who are thoughtful hearers in class discussions.
