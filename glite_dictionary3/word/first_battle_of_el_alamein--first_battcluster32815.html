First Battle of El Alamein

proper noun
World War II battle in Egypt, 1942
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝst ˌbætəl əv ˌɛl ˌæləˈmeɪn / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˌbætəl əv ˌɛl ˌæləˈmeɪn / (BrE)
a major World War II battle in 1942 in which British forces under Montgomery defeated German troops led by Rommel near El Alamein, Egypt
