retainer

noun
advance fee for professional services
Pronunciation: / rɪˈteɪnər / (AmE), / rɪˈteɪnə / (BrE)
a sum of money paid in advance to secure the services of a professional, especially a lawyer

Examples

pay a retainer fee
A large retainer was required to secure the services of the architect.
The lawyer asked for a retainer before starting any work on the case.
They paid a monthly retainer to keep the consultant available for advice.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deposit: sum of money paid as a guarantee or advance for services
  • synonym – fee: payment made for professional services
