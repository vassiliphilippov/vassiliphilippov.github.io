inauspiciously

adverb
in a way that is unpromising or unlucky
Pronunciation: / ɪn.ɔˈspɪʃ.əs.li / (AmE), / ɪn.ɔːˈspɪʃ.əs.li / (BrE)
In a manner that suggests a lack of success or good fortune; in an unpromising or unfavorable way.

Examples
