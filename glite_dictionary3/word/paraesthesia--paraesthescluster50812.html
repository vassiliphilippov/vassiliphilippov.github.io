paraesthesia

noun
abnormal skin sensation (tingling, prickling, burning)
Pronunciation: / ˌpær.iˈsiː.ʒə / (AmE), / ˌpær.iˈsiːs.θi.ə / (BrE)
An abnormal sensation of the skin, such as tingling, prickling, or burning, typically caused by nerve damage.

Examples

paraesthesia in the foot
After sitting with his legs crossed too long, he experienced paraesthesia in his foot.
People with diabetic neuropathy often complain of persistent paraesthesia in their hands and feet.
The patient described a burning paraesthesia that made it difficult to sleep at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – numbness: lack of physical sensation or feeling
  • synonym – tingling: slight prickling or stinging sensation, often in the skin
