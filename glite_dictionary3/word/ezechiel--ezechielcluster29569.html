Ezechiel

proper noun
6th-century BC Hebrew prophet
Pronunciation: / ɪˈziːkiəl / (AmE), / ɪˈziːkiəl / (BrE)
a Hebrew prophet of the 6th century BC, exiled to Babylon in 587 BC, whose prophecies are recorded in the Book of Ezekiel in the Bible

Examples

Ezechiel the prophet
Ezechiel was among the exiles taken to Babylon by Nebuchadnezzar.

The Book of Ezechiel contains vivid prophecies and symbolic imagery.
