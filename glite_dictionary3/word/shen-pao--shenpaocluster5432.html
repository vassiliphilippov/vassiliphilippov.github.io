Shen-pao

noun
deity of the Taoist Trinity
Pronunciation: / ʃɛn ˈpaʊ / (AmE), / ʃɛn ˈpaʊ / (BrE)
A member of the Taoist Trinity, identified with Lao-tse in Taoist tradition.
