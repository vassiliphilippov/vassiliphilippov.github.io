silky-haired

adjective
having soft, smooth, shiny hair
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlki hɛrd / (AmE), / ˈsɪlki hɛəd / (BrE)
Having hair that is soft, smooth, and shiny like silk.

Examples

silky-haired cat
She brushed her silky-haired cat every morning to keep its fur smooth.
The puppy was silky-haired and loved to be petted.
Visitors admired the silky-haired horse as it trotted in the field.
