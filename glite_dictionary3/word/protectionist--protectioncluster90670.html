protectionist

noun
advocate of trade protectionism
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛkʃənɪst / (AmE), / prəˈtɛkʃənɪst / (BrE)
A person who supports the policy of restricting imports from other countries to protect domestic industries.

Examples

protectionist trade policy
As a committed protectionist, she argued for higher tariffs on imported goods.
Many economists debate the impact protectionists have on global markets.
The new legislation was widely praised by leading protectionist groups.

  • antonym – free trader: a person who supports free trade policies
