to re-emerge

verb
appear or become visible again
Pronunciation: / ˌri ɪˈmɜrdʒ / (AmE), / ˌri ɪˈmɜːdʒ / (BrE)
to appear or become visible again after having disappeared or been in obscurity

Examples

to re-emerge after years
After several years of silence, the artist began to re-emerge with new work.
Old traditions often re-emerge during times of cultural revival.
The disease may re-emerge if vaccination rates decline.
