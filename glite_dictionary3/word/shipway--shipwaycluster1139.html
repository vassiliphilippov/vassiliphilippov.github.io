shipway

noun
canal for seagoing vessels
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪpˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪp.weɪ / (BrE)
a canal large enough to allow seagoing vessels to pass through

Examples

shipway for large vessels
Dredging the shipway ensured that deep-draft vessels could access the port.
Engineers designed the shipway to accommodate even the largest freighters.
The new shipway greatly improved transportation between the river and the sea.
