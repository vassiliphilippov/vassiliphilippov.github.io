planter

noun
decorative container for plants
Pronunciation: / ˈplæn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈplæn.tə / (BrE)
a decorative container in which a house plant or other small plant can be grown indoors or outdoors

Examples

planter with flowers
He bought a new planter to display his succulents in the living room.
She placed the fern in a ceramic planter by the window.
The balcony was filled with colorful flowers in hanging planters.

Related Terms

  • synonym – flowerpot: a container, usually round and made of clay or plastic, used for growing plants
