jejuneness

noun
naivety; lack of experience or sophistication
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈdʒuːn.nəs / (AmE), / dʒɪˈdʒuːn.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being naive, simplistic, or lacking maturity and experience of life

Examples

jejuneness of youth
He outgrew the jejuneness of his youth through years of travel and study.
Her jejuneness was evident when she trusted the stranger's unlikely story.
The essay's jejuneness made it unconvincing to more experienced readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – naivety: lack of experience, wisdom, or judgement
