knife

noun
pointed stabbing weapon
Pronunciation: / naɪf / (AmE), / naɪf / (BrE)
A weapon consisting of a handle and a blade with a sharp point, used for stabbing or thrusting.

Examples

draw a knife
He drew his knife in self-defense during the altercation.
The ancient warrior brandished a ceremonial knife at his enemies.
The suspect was found carrying a knife with a sharp, gleaming blade.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dagger: a short knife with a pointed and edged blade, used as a weapon
  • degree higher – sword: a long-bladed weapon used for cutting or thrusting
