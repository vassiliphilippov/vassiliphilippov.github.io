camera

noun
virtual viewpoint in 3D graphics
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmərə / (AmE), / ˈkæm(ə)rə / (BrE)
(in computer graphics and 3D modeling) the virtual viewpoint or position and orientation within a scene from which images are rendered or displayed.

Examples

3D camera position
In the 3D engine, you can move the camera to change the scene's perspective.
The animator adjusted the camera position to create a dramatic angle for the character.
When rendering, set the camera focal length and field of view for more realistic images.

Related Terms

  • synonym – viewpoint: the position or angle from which a scene or image is seen or represented
  • synonym – virtual camera: a software construct representing the viewpoint used to render a 3D scene
