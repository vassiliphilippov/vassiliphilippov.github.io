sanitarium

noun
hospital for recovery or chronic disease treatment
Pronunciation: / ˌsænɪˈtɛriəm / (AmE), / ˌsænɪˈtɛːrɪəm / (BrE)
a hospital where people go to recover or receive treatment for chronic diseases, especially one for long-term care

Examples

She was sent to a sanitarium to recover from her long illness.
The remote mountain sanitarium specialized in treating patients with tuberculosis.
