megaphone

noun
cone-shaped voice amplifier
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛɡəˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈmɛɡəfəʊn / (BrE)
A cone-shaped device held to the mouth to make the human voice louder and more direct.

Examples

use a megaphone at protest
During the protest, someone handed her a megaphone so she could address the crowd.
He improvised a megaphone with a rolled-up piece of paper.
The coach used a megaphone to shout instructions across the field.

  • synonym – bullhorn: an electronic loudspeaker used to amplify the voice
