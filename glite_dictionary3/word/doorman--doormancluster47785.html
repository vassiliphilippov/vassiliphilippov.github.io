doorman

noun
man guarding or attending a building entrance
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔrˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈdɔːmæn / (BrE)
A man whose job is to guard, oversee, or attend the entrance to a building, such as a hotel, apartment, or club.

Examples

hotel doorman
A uniformed doorman stood outside the exclusive nightclub, checking invitations.
The doorman greeted guests with a smile as they entered the hotel.
The building hired a new doorman to improve security at the main entrance.

Related Terms

  • synonym – porter: person employed to carry luggage and look after the entrance to a building
