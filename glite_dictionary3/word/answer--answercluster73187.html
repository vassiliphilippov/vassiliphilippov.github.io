to answer

verb
say or write in reply
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.sɚ /; / ˈænsɚ / (AmE), / ˈɑːn.sə /; / ˈɑːnsə / (BrE)
to reply or respond (spoken, written, or by action) to a question, request, accusation, or situation

Their lawyer answered all the points raised by the prosecution during the trial.
