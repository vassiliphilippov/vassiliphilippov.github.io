analogous

adjective
similar in some respects; comparable
Pronunciation: / əˈnæl.ə.ɡəs / (AmE), / əˈnæl.ə.ɡəs / (BrE)
similar or comparable in certain respects, typically in a way that makes clearer the nature of the things compared, though otherwise different.

Examples

analogous situation
His explanation used an analogous situation to help clarify the point.
Internet search engines are analogous to libraries in providing access to information.
The structure of a whale’s fin and a human hand are analogous in their bone arrangement.

Related Terms

  • synonym – comparable: similar in some aspects; able to be likened to another
  • antonym – different: not the same as another; dissimilar
