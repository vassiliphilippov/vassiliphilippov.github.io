untarnished

adjective
not spoiled or stained; pure
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɑrnɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɑːnɪʃt / (BrE)
(especially of reputation or record) not spoiled, stained, or made less good; remaining pure or unimpaired.

Examples

untarnished reputation
Despite years in politics, his untarnished reputation set him apart from his peers.
She valued her untarnished name above all else.
The silver remained untarnished after careful storage.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stainless: without stain or blemish, especially morally; spotless
  • antonym – tarnished: damaged or spoiled, especially in reputation
  • synonym – unsullied: not spoiled or made impure; pure
