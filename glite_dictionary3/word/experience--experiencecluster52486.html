experience

noun
overall user or customer impression
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
the overall impression, quality, and emotional response a person has when interacting with a product, service, or brand, especially in design, marketing, and customer-service contexts.

Examples

user experience
Companies now measure experience across digital and physical touchpoints to improve satisfaction.
The app's slow loading time ruined the onboarding experience for many new users.
As a designer, she examines every step to make the user experience feel effortless.

Related Terms

  • synonym – customer experience: the overall perception a customer has of a company's products and services across all touchpoints (often abbreviated CX).
  • synonym – user experience: the quality of a person's interactions with a digital product or service (often abbreviated UX).
