curator

noun
selector of online content
Pronunciation: / kj\u028a\u02c8re\u026at\u0259r / (AmE), / kj\u028a\u0259\u02c8re\u026at\u0259 / (BrE)
a person who selects, organizes, and presents online content (such as articles, videos, or posts) for a particular audience or platform.

Examples

curator of online content
The website employed a curator to choose articles that matched readers' interests each week.
As a social media curator, she organized videos into playlists for the brand's followers.
Independent curators often build audiences by selecting high-quality posts and explaining why.

Related Terms

  • synonym – content manager: someone responsible for planning, creating, and managing online content on a website or platform
  • synonym – editor: a person who selects, prepares, and often revises content for publication
