to register

verb
operate registers on an organ
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdʒɪstər / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstə / (BrE)
to manipulate the registers of an organ in order to produce different combinations of sounds

Examples

to register organ stops
An experienced organist knows how to register the instrument for a wide variety of musical effects.
It can be challenging to register properly during complex pieces that require frequent changes.
