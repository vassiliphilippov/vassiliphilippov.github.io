to redeem

verb
restore honor or worth
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdim / (AmE), / rɪˈdiːm / (BrE)

Examples

to redeem a reputation

She made a generous donation to redeem her earlier mistakes.
The team's victory helped redeem their disappointing season.
