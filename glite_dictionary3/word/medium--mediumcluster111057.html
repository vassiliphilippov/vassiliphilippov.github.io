medium

noun
person claiming to contact the spirits of the dead
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːdiəm / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiəm / (BrE)
a person who claims to communicate with the spirits of the dead and acts as an intermediary between them and the living

Examples

spirit medium
She has worked as a professional medium for over twenty years.
The documentary interviewed a famous medium about his experiences with séances.
The family invited a medium to help them communicate with their late grandmother.
