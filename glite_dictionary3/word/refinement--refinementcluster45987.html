refinement

noun
elegance or cultured quality
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfaɪnmənt / (AmE), / rɪˈfaɪnmənt / (BrE)
the quality of being cultured, elegant, polished, or showing good taste

Examples

refinement of manners
Her speech displayed a remarkable refinement that impressed the entire audience.
Many appreciate the refinement of classical music and its subtle complexities.
The refinement of his manners set him apart in social gatherings.
Her writing demonstrates a remarkable level of refinement and grace.
Over the years, the ballet dancer's technique achieved a state of near refinement.
The meal was prepared and presented with exquisite refinement.

Related Terms

  • antonym – coarseness: lack of refinement or delicacy
  • synonym – perfection: a state of flawlessness or being without fault
  • synonym – polish: the quality of being excellent and flawless in style or execution
  • synonym – sophistication: cultured knowledge or worldly experience; refinement of taste or manners
  • antonym – vulgarity: lack of good taste or refinement; coarseness
