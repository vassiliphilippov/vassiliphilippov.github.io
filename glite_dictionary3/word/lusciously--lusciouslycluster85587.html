lusciously

adverb
in a delightful, richly pleasant way
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌʃəsli / (AmE), / ˈlʌʃəsli / (BrE)
In a way that produces a rich, sweet, and pleasant taste or sensation.

Examples

lusciously sweet cake
He described the cake as lusciously rich and decadent.
The chocolate melted lusciously in my mouth, leaving a sweet aftertaste.
The peaches are lusciously juicy this season and perfect for dessert.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deliciously: in a very pleasant or enjoyable way (especially regarding taste)
