decrepit

adjective
lacking strength due to age or weakness
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkrɛpɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈkrɛpɪt / (BrE)
lacking physical strength or vitality, especially because of old age or infirmity; worn out or weakened

Examples

decrepit old man
After the long illness, he appeared decrepit and frail, barely able to walk across the room.
The decrepit old house looked as if it would collapse at any moment.
The shelter cared for many decrepit dogs abandoned by their owners.

Related Terms

  • synonym – feeble: lacking physical strength, especially due to age or illness
  • antonym – vigorous: strong, healthy, and full of energy
