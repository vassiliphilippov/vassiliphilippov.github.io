huffing

noun
forceful exhalation
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhʌfɪŋ / (BrE)
An act of exhaling air forcefully, often as a sign of annoyance, effort, or exertion.

Examples

loud huffing after exercise
After running up the hill, his huffing could be heard across the field.
She expressed her irritation with a loud huffing as she walked out the door.
The horse stood in the cold, huffing clouds of breath into the air.

  • synonym – panting: breathing quickly and heavily
