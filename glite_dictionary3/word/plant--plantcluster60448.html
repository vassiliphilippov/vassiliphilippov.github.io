to plant

verb
set or fix firmly in position
Pronunciation: / plænt / (AmE), / plɑːnt / (BrE)
to fix or set something securely and deeply in a place or position

Examples

to plant your feet
He planted his cane firmly in the soft earth and pushed himself up.
She planted her feet solidly on the ground, ready for the challenge.
The attacker planted a fist in his opponent's jaw during the match.
