metaphysical

adjective
relating to metaphysics or philosophical theory
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the branch of philosophy that deals with the fundamental nature of reality and existence; of or pertaining to metaphysics.

Examples

metaphysical philosophy
She has a keen interest in metaphysical questions about time and existence.
Some critics argue that poetry can address metaphysical ideas more effectively than prose.
The novel explores metaphysical themes through its dreamlike narrative.
