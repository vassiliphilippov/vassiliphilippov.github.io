full-fashioned

adjective
knitted to fit body shape
Pronunciation: / fʊl ˈfæʃənd / (AmE), / fʊl ˈfæʃənd / (BrE)
(of hosiery or other knitted garments) knitted to fit the shape of the body, often resulting in a better fit and finer appearance.

Examples

full-fashioned sweater
She prefers full-fashioned sweaters because they provide a better fit.
The company is known for its high-quality full-fashioned knitwear.
These full-fashioned stockings conform snugly to the leg and ankle.
