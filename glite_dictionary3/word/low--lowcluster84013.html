low

adjective
having a low pitch (of sound/voice)
Pronunciation: / loʊ / (AmE), / ləʊ / (BrE)
(of a sound or voice) having a pitch or frequency that is below average or below what is normal.

Examples

low pitch voice
His voice was unusually low for someone his age.
Please play a low note on the piano.
The thunder made a low rumbling sound in the distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deep: (of a sound) low in pitch
  • antonym – high: (of a sound) high in pitch or frequency
