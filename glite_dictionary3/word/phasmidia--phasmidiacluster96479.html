Phasmidia

noun
subgroup of nematode worms
Pronunciation: / fæzˈmɪdiə / (AmE), / fazˈmɪdɪə / (BrE)
a subgroup of nematode worms characterized by the presence of phasmids (special sensory organs) and certain sensory structures, used in some classification systems
