to atomize

verb
spray into fine mist
Pronunciation: / ˈæ.tə.maɪz / (AmE), / ˈæ.tə.maɪz / (BrE)
to spray or reduce a liquid into a fine mist or very small particles

Examples

to atomize a liquid
Modern perfumes are often atomized rather than dabbed onto the skin.
The chef used a special nozzle to atomize the oil over the salad.
The machine is designed to atomize the disinfectant for even coverage.
