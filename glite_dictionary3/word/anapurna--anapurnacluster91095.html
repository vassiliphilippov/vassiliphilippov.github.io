Anapurna

proper noun
Hindu goddess of plenty
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːnəˈpʊrnə / (AmE), / ˌænəˈpʊːnə / (BrE)
A Hindu goddess of plenty, regarded as a benevolent aspect of Devi and the wife of Siva.

Examples

Anapurna Hindu goddess
Pilgrims traveled to the temple to offer thanks to Anapurna for the season’s abundant harvest.
Stories of Anapurna are central to many Hindu festivals celebrating prosperity and sustenance.
The image of Anapurna is often depicted holding a bowl of food, symbolizing nourishment and generosity.
