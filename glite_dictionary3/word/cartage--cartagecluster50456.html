cartage

noun
transportation of goods by cart or truck
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrtɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːtɪdʒ / (BrE)
the act or cost of transporting goods by cart or truck, especially over a short distance

Examples

cartage charges
All cartage expenses must be approved before the goods are moved.
Arrangements for cartage were made following the demolition of the building.
The company charged a flat fee for cartage of the old furniture.
