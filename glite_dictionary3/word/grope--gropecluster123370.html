to grope

verb
search with hands blindly
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊp / (AmE), / ɡrəʊp / (BrE)
to search for or try to find something by feeling with your hands, especially in the dark or when you cannot see clearly

Examples

to grope in the dark
I watched him grope around the desk, searching for his keys.
In total darkness, we groped our way down the hallway.
She had to grope for the light switch when the power went out.
He had to grope in the dark for the light switch.
She groped for the right words to express her feelings.
Their minds groped for an explanation that made sense.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fumble: to use the hands clumsily while doing or handling something
