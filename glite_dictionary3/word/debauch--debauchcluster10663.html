to debauch

verb
corrupt morally or by sensual pleasure
Pronunciation: / dɪˈbɔtʃ / (AmE), / dɪˈbɔːtʃ / (BrE)
to corrupt someone morally or lead them into excessive indulgence in sensual pleasures

Examples

to debauch young minds
Critics claimed the film would debauch the morals of its viewers.
His wealth allowed him to debauch himself with luxury and excess.
The conquerors tried to debauch the population with promises of easy pleasure.

  • synonym – subvert: to undermine the morals or principles of someone
