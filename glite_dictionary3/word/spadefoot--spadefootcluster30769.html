spadefoot

noun
burrowing toad with spade-like hind feet
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪdˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈspeɪdfʊt / (BrE)
a burrowing toad of the Northern Hemisphere with a hard, spade-like projection on each hind foot, used for digging
