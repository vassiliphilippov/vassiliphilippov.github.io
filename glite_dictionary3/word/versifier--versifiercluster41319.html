versifier

noun
a writer of often inferior verses
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜːrsɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈvɜːsɪfaɪə / (BrE)
a person who writes verses, especially one who is considered a minor or inferior poet

Examples

talented versifier
His reputation as a versifier did little to earn him respect among serious poets.
The anthology included works by accomplished poets and the occasional versifier.
Though technically skilled, he was dismissed as merely a versifier by critics.
