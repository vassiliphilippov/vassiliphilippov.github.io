carcinoma

noun
malignant tumor of epithelial tissue
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːrsɪˈnoʊmə / (AmE), / ˌkɑːsɪˈnəʊmə / (BrE)
A malignant tumor that develops from epithelial cells and is one of the main types of cancer.
