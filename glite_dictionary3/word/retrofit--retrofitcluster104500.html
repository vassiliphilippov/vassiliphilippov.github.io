to retrofit

verb
add new parts to something old
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛtroʊˌfɪt / (AmE), / ˈrɛtrəʊˌfɪt / (BrE)
to add new or improved parts, equipment, or technology to an existing building, machine, or system to improve or modernize it
