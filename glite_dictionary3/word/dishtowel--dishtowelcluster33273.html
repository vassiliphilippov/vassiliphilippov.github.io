dishtowel

noun
towel for drying dishes
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪʃˌtaʊəl / (AmE), / ˈdɪʃˌtaʊəl / (BrE)
A towel used for drying dishes after they have been washed.

Examples

dry plates with a dishtowel
He hung the clean dishtowel on the handle of the oven.
She grabbed a dishtowel to dry the plates after dinner.
The dishtowel was damp from wiping so many glasses.
