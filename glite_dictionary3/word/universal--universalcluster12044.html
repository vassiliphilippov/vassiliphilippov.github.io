universal

noun
trait or pattern found universally among people
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪˈvɜrsəl / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsəl / (BrE)
a behavioral or cultural trait, value, or pattern found among all human beings or all members of a particular group

Examples

cultural universal
Some researchers believe that moral codes have certain universals across civilizations.
The anthropologist argued that language is a universal among human societies.
The study of emotions explores whether smiling is a universal or varies by culture.
