Usama bin Laden

proper noun
founder of al-Qaeda; Arab terrorist (1957–2011)
Pronunciation: / uˈsɑːmə bɪn lɑːdən / (AmE), / uˈsɑːmə bɪn ˈlɑːd(ə)n / (BrE)
(1957–2011) An Arab terrorist and the founder of al-Qaeda, responsible for organizing various major terrorist attacks, including those on September 11, 2001.
