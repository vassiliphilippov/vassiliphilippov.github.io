creakingly

adverb
in a creaky manner
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkriːkɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that produces or resembles creaking.

Examples

door opens creakingly
She opened the ancient wardrobe creakingly, wincing at the sound.
The stairs groaned creakingly with every step he took.
The wind made the loose sign sway creakingly all night.
