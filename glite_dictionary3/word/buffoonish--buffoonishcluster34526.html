buffoonish

adjective
like a clown; foolish or ridiculous
Pronunciation: / bəˈfuːnɪʃ / (AmE), / bəˈfuːnɪʃ / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a buffoon; foolish, clown-like, or ridiculous in manner or appearance.

Examples

buffoonish behavior
His buffoonish antics at the party made everyone laugh, even though they were rather silly.
She wore a buffoonish outfit for the comedy show, complete with mismatched shoes and a giant bow tie.
The actor gave a buffoonish performance, delighting the audience with exaggerated expressions and slapstick humor.
