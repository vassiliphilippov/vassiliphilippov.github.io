prowl

noun
an act of stealthy moving or searching
Pronunciation: / praʊl / (AmE), / praʊl / (BrE)
an act of moving about stealthily or searching for something, especially as if hunting prey

Examples

on a prowl for food
After hearing noises outside, he did a quick prowl around the house.
She went out for a midnight prowl around the city.
The tiger set off on a prowl through the dense jungle.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stalk: the act of stealthily pursuing prey
