regular

adjective
military: forming part of a country's official armed forces
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛɡjələr / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjələ /; / ˈrɛɡjʊlə / (BrE)
belonging to an official, full-time group or organization, such as the main army or permanent staff
