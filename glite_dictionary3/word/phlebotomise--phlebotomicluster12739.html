to phlebotomise

verb
draw blood medically
Pronunciation: / fləˈbɑːtəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / flɪˈbɒtəmaɪz / (BrE)

Examples

The nurse skillfully phlebotomised the patient to obtain a blood sample for testing.
