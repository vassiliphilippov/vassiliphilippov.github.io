upstanding

adjective
honest and respectable
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌpˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person) honest, respectable, and having strong moral principles; deserving of respect or esteem.

Examples

upstanding citizen
She is known as an upstanding citizen who volunteers in her spare time.
The judge praised her as an upstanding member of the community.
We can always count on Mark to make the right choice; he's truly upstanding.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dishonest: not honest; intending to deceive
  • synonym – honorable: deserving of honor or respect
  • synonym – respectable: regarded by society to be good or acceptable
