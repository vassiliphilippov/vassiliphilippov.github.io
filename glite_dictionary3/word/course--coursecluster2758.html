course

noun
area prepared for a sports event
Pronunciation: / kɔrs / (AmE), / kɔːs / (BrE)
an area of land or water laid out and prepared for a sports event, such as golf, racing, or cross-country running

Examples

golf course

She walked along the edge of the golf course, admiring the well-kept greens.
The championship was held on a challenging course with many sharp turns.
