fusillade

noun
simultaneous burst of gunfire
Pronunciation: / ˌfjuːsəˈleɪd / (AmE), / ˌfjuːsɪˈlɑːd / (BrE)
a rapid and simultaneous discharge of many firearms or shots

Examples

a fusillade of bullets
A sudden fusillade shattered the silence, scattering everyone in panic.
During the parade, a fusillade of blanks was fired into the air as a salute.
The soldiers responded with a fusillade that forced the enemy to retreat.

Related Terms

  • synonym – barrage: a concentrated artillery bombardment over a wide area
  • synonym – volley: a number of bullets, rocks, or other objects thrown or fired at once
