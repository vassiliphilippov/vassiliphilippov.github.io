unknowingly

adverb
without knowing; without intention
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈnoʊɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈnəʊɪŋli / (BrE)
Without being aware or conscious of something; without intention or knowledge.

Examples

unknowingly make a mistake
He unknowingly offended his colleague with a careless remark.
She unknowingly provided the wrong directions to the new restaurant.
The hikers unknowingly crossed into private property during their trek.

Related Terms

  • antonym – knowingly: with full awareness or intention
  • synonym – unwittingly: without being aware; unintentionally
