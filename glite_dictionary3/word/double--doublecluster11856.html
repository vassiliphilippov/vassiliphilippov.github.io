to double

verb
have two functions or roles
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl / (BrE)
to have a second job or function; to serve in two roles or capacities, often at the same time

Examples

to double as teacher
During the festival, the local gym doubles as a community center.
She often doubles as both nurse and cook in the household.
The family car doubles as a delivery vehicle for their small business.
