thrall

noun
state of complete control or domination
Pronunciation: / θrɔl / (AmE), / θrɑːl / (BrE)
the state of being completely controlled or dominated by another person or by a powerful influence

Examples

in thrall to power
Many people have fallen into the thrall of social media, unable to disconnect.
She escaped the thrall of her controlling partner after many years.
The novel explores a world in the thrall of a tyrant king.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bondage: the state of being a slave or under control of someone else
  • antonym – freedom: the state of being free, not under the control of another
