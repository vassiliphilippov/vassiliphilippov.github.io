vizor

noun
face-protecting part of a medieval helmet
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈvʌɪzə / (BrE)
a piece of armor with openings for the eyes, attached to a medieval helmet to protect the face

Examples

vizor on a knight's helmet
Her costume included a replica vizor modeled after those worn by medieval soldiers.
The knight lowered his vizor before entering the tournament arena.
Through the narrow eye slits of his vizor, he watched the battlefield unfold.

Related Terms

  • synonym – visor: face guard of a helmet, especially in medieval armor
