downhill

adjective
sloping downwards steeply
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌhɪl / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌhɪl / (BrE)
(of a slope or surface) sloping downwards, especially at a steep angle.

Examples

downhill slope
After climbing the hill, we enjoyed a quick downhill walk back to the village.
The downhill trail was much faster but required extra caution.
They built their house on a downhill street that flooded easily during rainstorms.

Related Terms

  • antonym – uphill: sloping upward, opposite of downhill
