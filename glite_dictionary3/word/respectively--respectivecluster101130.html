respectively

adverb
in the order given
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɛktɪvli / (AmE), / rɪˈspɛktɪvli / (BrE)
in the order given; relating or referring to each in the sequence mentioned

Examples

numbers respectively listed
The children are aged five, seven, and ten, respectively.
The committee members voted yes, no, and abstained, respectively.
