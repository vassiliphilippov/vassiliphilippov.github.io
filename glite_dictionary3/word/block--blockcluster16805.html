block

noun
obstruction in a pipe or tube
Pronunciation: / blɑk / (AmE), / blɒk / (BrE)
an obstruction that prevents the normal flow in a pipe or tube

Examples

pipe block
A build-up of hair can create a block in the shower drain over time.
The plumber removed a block from the kitchen sink drain.
There was a stubborn block in the bathroom pipe that caused flooding.
