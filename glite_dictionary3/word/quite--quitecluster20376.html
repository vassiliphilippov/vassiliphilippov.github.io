quite

adverb
to a remarkable or exceptional degree
Pronunciation: / kwaɪt / (AmE), / kwaɪt / (BrE)
to a significant, noticeable, or remarkable degree; fairly, rather, or really

Examples

quite a performance

That was quite an adventure you had on your trip to Peru.
The view from the mountaintop was quite something to behold.
It's quite late, so we should probably head home soon.
She recovered quite quickly after her illness.
The soup was quite tasty, with a subtle blend of spices.
