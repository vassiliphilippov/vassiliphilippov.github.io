Urdu

proper noun
the official language of Pakistan; related to Hindi
Pronunciation: / ˈɚrdu / (AmE), / ˈɜːduː / (BrE)
the official literary language of Pakistan, closely related to Hindi and written in Arabic script; widely used in India, especially by Muslims
