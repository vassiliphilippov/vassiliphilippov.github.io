purifying

adjective
removing impurities; making pure
Pronunciation: / ˈpjʊrəˌfaɪɪŋ /; / ˈpjʊr.əˌfaɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpjʊərɪˌfaɪɪŋ /; / ˈpjʊər.ɪ.faɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
