to commercialize

verb
make commercial; turn into a business
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɝːʃəˌlaɪz /; / kəˈmɝː.ʃə.l̩.aɪz / (AmE), / kəˈmɜːʃəlaɪz /; / kəˈmɜː.ʃəl.aɪz / (BrE)
to make something available for sale or operated mainly to make money, often changing its original character
