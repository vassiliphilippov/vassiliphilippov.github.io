province

noun
administrative region in a country
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.vɪns / (AmE), / ˈprɒv.ɪns / (BrE)
a principal administrative division of certain countries or empires, forming a territorial unit of government

Examples

Canadian province
Many Canadians identify with their home province as much as with the country itself.
She was born in a small province far from the capital city.
The country is divided into several provinces, each governed locally.
