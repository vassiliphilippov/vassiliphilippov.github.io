cackly

adjective
resembling the cackling of a hen
Pronunciation: / ˈkækli / (AmE), / ˈkækli / (BrE)
making a sound like the cackles or squawks of a hen, especially after laying an egg; having a harsh, shrill, or repetitive laugh or tone.

Examples

cackly laugh
He broke the silence with a cackly joke, drawing giggles from his friends.
Her cackly laughter filled the quiet room, startling everyone nearby.
The old radio had a cackly tone that reminded him of hens in the barnyard.
