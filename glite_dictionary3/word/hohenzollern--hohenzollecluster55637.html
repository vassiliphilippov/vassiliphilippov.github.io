Hohenzollern

proper noun
German ruling noble family
Pronunciation: / hoʊənˈzɔːlərn / (AmE), / həʊənˈzɒlən / (BrE)
a German noble family that ruled Brandenburg and Prussia, and later the German Empire

Examples

the Hohenzollern dynasty
