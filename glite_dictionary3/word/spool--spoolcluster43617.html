to spool

verb
wind onto a spool or reel
Pronunciation: / spuːl / (AmE), / spuːl / (BrE)
to wind thread, wire, tape, or similar material onto a spool or reel

Examples

to spool tape
Before playback, the audio tape must be spooled onto the reel.
He carefully spooled the fishing line to avoid tangles.
The machine automatically spools the thread onto the bobbin.
