chimneystack

noun
chimney part above roof
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪmniˌstæk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪmniˌstak / (BrE)
The part of a chimney that projects above the roof, often containing multiple flues.

Examples

tall chimneystack
A tall chimneystack rose above the old manor house, adding character to the skyline.
From the garden, you could see several pots on top of the chimneystack.
The chimneystack was rebuilt to comply with modern safety regulations.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chimney: a vertical channel that conducts smoke and gases up from a fireplace or furnace
