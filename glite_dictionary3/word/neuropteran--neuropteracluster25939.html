neuropteran

noun
insect of the order Neuroptera
Pronunciation: / nuˈrɑːptərən / (AmE), / njʊəˈrɒptərən / (BrE)
an insect of the order Neuroptera, characterized by biting mouthparts and four large membranous wings with a network of veins

Examples
