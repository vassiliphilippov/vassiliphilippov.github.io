unhealed

adjective
not healed or recovered
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhiːld / (AmE), / ʌnˈhiːld / (BrE)
(of a wound, injury, or emotional hurt) not fully healed or recovered.

Examples

unhealed wound
Pain surged from her still unhealed ankle after the accident.
Some wounds, whether physical or emotional, remain unhealed for many years.
The doctor examined the unhealed cut on his arm carefully.
