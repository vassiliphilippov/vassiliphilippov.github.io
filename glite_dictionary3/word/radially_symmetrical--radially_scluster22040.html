radially symmetrical

adjective
symmetrical around a central point
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdiəli sɪˈmɛtrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəli sɪˈmɛtrɪkəl / (BrE)
having a symmetrical arrangement of parts around a central point, so that sections taken at intervals around the center are identical

Examples

radially symmetrical starfish
A starfish is a classic example of a radially symmetrical animal.
Many sea creatures, such as jellyfish, are radially symmetrical in shape.
The flower's petals are arranged in a radially symmetrical pattern.
