riffraff

noun
disreputable or undesirable people
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪfˌræf / (AmE), / ˈrɪfˌræf / (BrE)
people regarded as disreputable or undesirable; the lower social classes, often spoken of with disdain.

Examples

local riffraff
He didn't want to associate with what he called the local riffraff.
Some believe the city is being overrun by riffraff from neighboring areas.
The manager refused to let the riffraff into the nightclub.

Related Terms

  • antonym – elite: the group of people considered to be the best in a society
  • synonym – rabble: an unruly crowd; the common people considered as a mob
