agar

noun
gelatinous substance from algae used in labs and food
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.ɡɑr /; / ˈæɡˌɑr / (AmE), / ˈɑː.ɡɑː /; / ˈɑːɡɑː / (BrE)
a gelatinous substance from seaweed or algae, used to grow microorganisms in labs and as a thickener in foods

Examples
