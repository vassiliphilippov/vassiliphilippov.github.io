giving

noun
the act of making voluntary donations or gifts
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving something (such as money, property, gifts, or help) to others, especially generously or charitably
