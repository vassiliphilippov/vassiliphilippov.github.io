tam-o'-shanter

noun
round Scottish woolen cap
Pronunciation: / ˌtæm.oʊˈʃæntər / (AmE), / ˌtam.əʊˈʃantə / (BrE)
a round woolen cap with a flat top and usually a pompom in the center, traditionally worn in Scotland
