topmast

noun
the mast above the lower mast (ships)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑpˌmæst / (AmE), / ˈtɒpˌmɑːst / (BrE)
The section of a ship's mast, located above the lower mast, and the highest mast in a fore-and-aft rig.

Examples

topmast lookout post
The lookout was posted at the topmast to spot incoming vessels.
The sailor climbed the topmast to secure the flag during the storm.
With the addition of a new topmast, the ship sailed more swiftly.
