curly-leaved

adjective
having curly or wavy leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜrli ˌlivd / (AmE), / ˈkɜːli ˌliːvd / (BrE)
(of a plant) having leaves that are curled or wavy rather than flat

Examples

Some varieties of kale are curly-leaved, while others have flatter leaves.
The curly-leaved parsley is preferred by chefs for its decorative appearance.
