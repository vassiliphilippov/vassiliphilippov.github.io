shaven

adjective
with hair closely shaved off
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪvən / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪvən / (BrE)
Having hair, especially a beard, removed closely to the skin by shaving.

Examples

cleanly shaven face
After a fresh shower, he felt much better and cleanly shaven.
He appeared at the meeting shaven and dressed in a crisp suit.
The shaven heads of the recruits made them look almost identical.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unshaven: not having shaved, especially when stubble is present
