chetah

noun
large, spotted, fast African/Asian wild cat
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːtə / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːtə / (BrE)
A large, long-legged wild cat of Africa and southwestern Asia with distinctive black spots and nonretractile claws, known as the fastest land animal.

Examples

wild chetah running
In ancient times, nobles sometimes kept a chetah for hunting expeditions.
The chetah is well known for its incredible speed when hunting prey.
Unlike most cats, a chetah has claws that do not retract.
