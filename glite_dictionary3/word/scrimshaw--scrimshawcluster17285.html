scrimshaw

noun
carved or engraved whalebone or ivory ornament
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɪm.ʃɔ / (AmE), / ˈskrɪm.ʃɔː / (BrE)
an ornament or carving made by engraving or carving on whalebone, whale ivory, walrus tusk, or similar materials, especially by sailors and whalers

Examples

antique scrimshaw carving
Collectors are often fascinated by the fine detail in antique scrimshaw.
