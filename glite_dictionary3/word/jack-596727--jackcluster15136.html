jack

noun
a large tropical fruit (jackfruit) with edible flesh and seeds
Pronunciation: / dʒæk / (AmE), / dʒak / (BrE)
a large tropical fruit from South and Southeast Asia, with a thick green rind and sweet yellow flesh, also called jackfruit; both the pulp and seeds are edible.

Examples

ripe jack fruit
Farmers harvested a huge jack that weighed more than 30 kilograms.
Roasting the seeds of the jack brings out a nutty flavor that many people love.
The jack was served fresh, its sweet flesh enjoyed by everyone at the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym – jackfruit: the large tropical fruit of the tree Artocarpus heterophyllus
