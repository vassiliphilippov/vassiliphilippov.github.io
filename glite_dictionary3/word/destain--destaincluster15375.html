to destain

verb
remove stain from a lab specimen
Pronunciation: / diˈsteɪn / (AmE), / diˈsteɪn / (BrE)
to remove stain from a laboratory specimen, especially in order to enhance contrast under a microscope

Examples

to destain a slide
After staining the gel, scientists often destain it to better visualize the bands.
The technician carefully destained the tissue sample before examining it under the microscope.
To improve contrast, the lab protocol includes a step to destain the specimen after initial staining.
