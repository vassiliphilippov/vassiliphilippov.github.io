propenal

noun
pungent unsaturated liquid aldehyde (acrolein)
Pronunciation: / proʊˈpɛnæl / (AmE), / prəʊˈpɛnæl / (BrE)
A pungent, colorless, unsaturated liquid aldehyde (chemical formula C3H4O) made from propene; also known as acrolein, used in organic synthesis and as a biocide.
