shall-flower

noun
plant with decorative white and pink flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæl ˌflaʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈʃæl ˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
A plant cultivated for its shiny oblong leaves and arching clusters of white flowers tinged with pink, crinkled yellow lips, and magenta-striped petals.
