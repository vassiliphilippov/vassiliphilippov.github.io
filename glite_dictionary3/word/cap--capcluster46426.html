cap

noun
a lid for a bottle or container
Pronunciation: / kæp / (AmE), / kap / (BrE)
a cover or lid that fits over the top or end of something, such as a bottle, pen, or tube, to protect or seal it

Examples

cap a bottle
Make sure to put the cap back on after using the glue.

The medicine comes in a small bottle with a childproof cap.
He replaced the cap on the pen so it wouldn't dry out.
Please make sure the cap is tightly closed before shaking the bottle.
The bottle cannot be opened without removing the cap.
