bitter

adjective
harsh or painfully sharp (esp. of cold)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtər / (AmE), / ˈbɪtə / (BrE)
(of a sensation, especially cold) causing a sharply painful, stinging, or harsh feeling.

Examples

bitter cold wind
A bitter wind made it almost impossible to walk along the coast.
The storm brought bitter weather that lasted all week.
We were caught in the bitter cold before sunrise.

Related Terms

  • synonym – biting: sharply painful, especially of wind or cold
