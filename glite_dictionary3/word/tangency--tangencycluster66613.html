tangency

noun
point of contact or junction
Pronunciation: / ˈtæn.dʒən.si / (AmE), / ˈtan.dʒən.si / (BrE)
a point or junction where two things, especially electrical conductors, touch or are in physical contact

Examples

tangency of wires
Any dirt or corrosion at the tangency could lead to a loss of signal in the circuit.
Technicians checked each tangency in the system before powering up the device.
The tangency between the two wires must be clean to ensure a proper electrical connection.

Related Terms

  • synonym – connection: a point where two or more things are joined
  • synonym – contact: the state of physical touching or meeting
