low

adverb
at or near ground level
Pronunciation: / loʊ / (AmE), / ləʊ / (BrE)
at or to a position close to the ground or below the usual height

Examples

bend low
He bent low to tie his shoelaces.
The plane was flying unusually low over the mountains.
The tree branches hung low after the storm.
