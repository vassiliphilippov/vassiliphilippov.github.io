editorial

noun
opinion article in a publication
Pronunciation: / ˌɛdəˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
An article in a newspaper or magazine that gives the opinions of the editors or publishers on a particular topic.

Examples

editorial in newspaper
Her editorial explained the reasons behind the company's recent changes.
Many people eagerly read the Sunday editorial for insight into the editor's views.
The newspaper published an editorial criticizing the government's new policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym – column: a regular feature or article by a particular writer, often expressing opinions
  • synonym – op-ed: an article expressing the opinion of a named author, typically not from the editorial staff
