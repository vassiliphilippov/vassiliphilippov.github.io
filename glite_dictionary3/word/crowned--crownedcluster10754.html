crowned

adjective
having a dental crown
Pronunciation: / kraʊnd / (AmE), / kraʊnd / (BrE)
having an artificial crown fitted to a tooth

Examples

crowned tooth
After his root canal treatment, the dentist recommended the tooth be crowned.
Getting a crowned tooth can restore its strength and appearance.
She has several crowned molars due to past cavities.

Related Terms

  • antonym – uncrowned: not provided with a crown; in this sense, a tooth not fitted with a dental crown
