plessimeter

noun
instrument for medical percussion
Pronunciation: / plɛˈsɪmɪtər / (AmE), / plɛˈsɪmɪtə / (BrE)
a small, thin metal plate placed against the body and tapped with a plexor during medical percussion to detect differences in underlying tissues
