blasphemy

noun
showing contempt for the sacred
Pronunciation: / ˈblæsfəmi /; / ˈblæs.fə.mi / (AmE), / ˈblasfəmi /; / ˈblɑːs.fə.mi / (BrE)
the act, speech, or offense of showing disrespect or lack of reverence for God or something considered sacred
