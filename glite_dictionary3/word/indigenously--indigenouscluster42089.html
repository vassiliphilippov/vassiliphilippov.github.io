indigenously

adverb
in a native or original manner
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɪdʒənəsli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪdʒənəsli / (BrE)
in a way that is native or original to a particular place or environment.

Examples

indigenously developed customs
Certain traditions have developed indigenously among these isolated communities.
The crops thrive indigenously in this region without human intervention.
These animals are found indigenously only on the island.
