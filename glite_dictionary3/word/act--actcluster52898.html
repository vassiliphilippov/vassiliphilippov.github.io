to act

verb
be suitable for stage performance
Pronunciation: / ækt / (AmE), / ækt / (BrE)
(of a play, scene, etc.) to be suitable for performance on stage, especially in terms of effectiveness or dramatic quality.

Examples

the play acts well
Certain monologues act better on stage than they do on paper.
Some scripts simply do not act because of clumsy dialogue.
The final scene acts well and leaves a strong impression on the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym – perform: to present a play, scene, etc. to an audience; to act a part
