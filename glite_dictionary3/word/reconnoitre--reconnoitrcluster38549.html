to reconnoitre

verb
make a preliminary survey or exploration
Pronunciation: / rəˈkɑː.nɔɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / rɪˈkɒn.ɔɪ.tə / (BrE)
to make a preliminary survey or exploration, especially to gather information about an area or enemy forces

Examples

to reconnoitre enemy positions
Before advancing, the patrol paused to reconnoitre the surrounding area for possible dangers.
Scientists were sent to reconnoitre the uninhabited island for potential research sites.
The general ordered his troops to reconnoitre the enemy camp before dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym – scout: to search an area or gather information, especially in military contexts
  • synonym – survey: to examine an area carefully, especially to gather information
