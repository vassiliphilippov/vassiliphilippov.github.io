ischium

noun
lower part of the hipbone
Pronunciation: / ˈɪskiəm / (AmE), / ˈɪskɪəm / (BrE)
the curved bone forming the lower and back part of each half of the pelvis

Examples

broken ischium bone
A fracture of the ischium can be very painful and may require several weeks to heal.
Damage to the ischium can result from severe falls or car accidents.
The pelvis consists of three main bones: the ilium, the ischium, and the pubis.
