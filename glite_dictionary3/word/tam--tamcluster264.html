tam

noun
a round Scottish woolen cap
Pronunciation: / tæm / (AmE), / tæm / (BrE)
A round, flat woolen cap of Scottish origin, often with a pompom in the center, traditionally worn as part of Scottish dress.

Examples

Scottish tam hat
He pulled his tam down over his ears to keep warm in the chilly wind.
She wore a bright red tam that matched her scarf perfectly.
The shop in Edinburgh sold traditional Scottish tams to tourists.
