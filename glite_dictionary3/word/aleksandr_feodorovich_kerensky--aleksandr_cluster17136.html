Aleksandr Feodorovich Kerensky

proper noun
Russian revolutionary and provisional leader, 1881–1970
Pronunciation: / əˈlɛksəndər fiˈoʊdərəvɪtʃ kəˈrɛn.ski / (AmE), / əˈlɛksəndə fiˈɔːdərəvɪtʃ kəˈrɛnski / (BrE)
(1881–1970) Russian revolutionary and politician who served as head of state after the abdication of Nicholas II, later overthrown by the Bolsheviks.
