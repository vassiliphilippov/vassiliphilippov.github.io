overwatch

noun
game or military mode for monitoring and responding to threats
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈwɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈwɒtʃ / (BrE)
a position or mode in video games or military operations where a unit monitors an area and automatically responds to enemy movement or actions

Examples

on overwatch duty
In the game, placing a unit on overwatch allows it to fire at any enemy that moves within its line of sight.
The soldier activated overwatch mode, ready to react to any threat.
The squad took up overwatch to cover the team's advance across the open ground.
