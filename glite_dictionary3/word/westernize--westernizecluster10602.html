to westernize

verb
adopt Western customs or practices
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstərˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈwɛstənaɪz / (BrE)
to adopt or cause to adopt the customs, values, or practices of Western countries, especially Europe or North America

Examples

to westernize a country
After the revolution, the government tried to westernize the education system.
Many societies have chosen to westernize certain aspects of their culture.
The city has rapidly westernized over the past few decades, with new businesses and fashions.

Related Terms

  • antonym – easternize: to make or become Eastern in character
  • synonym – modernize: to make modern or up to date
