stomach

noun
region between the chest and pelvis
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌmək / (AmE), / ˈstʌmək / (BrE)
the part of the body between the chest and the pelvis, especially considered as the location of the abdomen or belly

Examples

flat stomach
After overeating, my stomach felt painfully full.
He lay on his back, his stomach rising and falling as he breathed deeply.
She placed her hand on her stomach and laughed at the silly joke.

Related Terms

  • synonym – abdomen: the part of the body containing digestive organs; the belly
  • antonym – chest: the front surface of a person's body between the neck and the abdomen
  • antonym – pelvis: the lower part of the trunk of the body, between the abdomen and the thighs
