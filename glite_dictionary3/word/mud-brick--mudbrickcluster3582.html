mud-brick

adjective
made of bricks formed from mud
Pronunciation: / mʌd brɪk / (AmE), / mʌd brɪk / (BrE)
made of or incorporating bricks formed from mud and dried in the sun, rather than fired in a kiln.

Examples

mud-brick house
Archaeologists uncovered a mud-brick temple dating back thousands of years.
Many rural houses in the region are simple mud-brick structures with thatched roofs.
The ancient city was built with mud-brick walls that have lasted for centuries.
