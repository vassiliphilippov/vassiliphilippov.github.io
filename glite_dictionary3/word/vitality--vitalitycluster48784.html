vitality

noun
ability to survive and grow
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈtæləti / (AmE), / vaɪˈtælɪti / (BrE)
the power or ability of something to survive, grow, or continue to exist; the capacity for continued life and development.

Examples

vitality of a plant
Doctors were surprised by the patient's vitality after such a serious operation.
Seeds with higher vitality are more likely to sprout and develop into healthy plants.
The vitality of the young tree was obvious as it quickly adapted to its new environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym – vigour: physical strength and good health; energy and enthusiasm
