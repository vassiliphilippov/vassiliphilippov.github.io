to deliver

verb
bring goods or items to someone
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪvɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvə / (BrE)
to bring goods, letters, or other items to a particular place or person; to make a delivery

Examples

deliver a package
Every morning, the postman delivers letters to all the houses on our street.
The restaurant can deliver your order to your home within thirty minutes.
They delivered the new couch this afternoon, just as promised.
