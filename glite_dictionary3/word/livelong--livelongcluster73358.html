livelong

noun
perennial plant with purplish-white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪv.lɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪv.lɒŋ / (BrE)
A perennial northern temperate plant with toothed leaves and heads of small purplish-white flowers, commonly known as 'livelong.'
