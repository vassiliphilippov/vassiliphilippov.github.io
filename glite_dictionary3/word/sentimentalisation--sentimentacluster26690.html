sentimentalisation

noun
the act of making something sentimental
Pronunciation: / sɛnˌtɪmənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / sɛnˌtɪmɛntəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something sentimental, or indulging in excessive sentiment

Examples

sentimentalisation of history
Her sentimentalisation of the past made her overlook its many difficulties.
The sentimentalisation of childhood often ignores the hardships many children face.
The movie's sentimentalisation of war was criticized by reviewers.
