to celebrate

verb
honor or praise publicly
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛl.əˌbreɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.ɪˌbreɪt / (BrE)
to praise or honor someone publicly, assigning them high social importance

Examples

celebrate a hero
He was celebrated for his contributions to science around the world.
The community celebrated her achievements with a special award ceremony.
Throughout the city, artists who break new ground are often celebrated in the media.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lionize: to treat someone as a celebrity, to give a lot of public attention to
