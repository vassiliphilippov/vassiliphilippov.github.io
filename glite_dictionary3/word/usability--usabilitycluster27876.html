usability

noun
quality of being easy and efficient to use
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːzəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌjuːzəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being easy and efficient to use or operate, especially in providing good service to users

Examples

good usability design
Improving usability can significantly enhance customer satisfaction with our service.
The new website was praised for its excellent usability and convenient navigation.
We're conducting tests to assess the usability of our latest app before its release.
