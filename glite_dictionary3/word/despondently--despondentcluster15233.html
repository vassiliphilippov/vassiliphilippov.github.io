despondently

adverb
in a hopeless or dejected manner
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspɑndəntli / (AmE), / dɪˈspɒndəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a loss of hope or confidence; in a dejected or discouraged manner.

Examples

sigh despondently
After losing the match, the team walked off the field despondently.
He stared despondently at the test results, unable to hide his disappointment.
She sighed despondently when she realized her efforts had gone unnoticed.
