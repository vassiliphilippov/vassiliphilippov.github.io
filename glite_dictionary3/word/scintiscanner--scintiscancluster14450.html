scintiscanner

noun
apparatus for medical scintigraphy
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪn.tɪˌskæn.ər / (AmE), / ˈsɪn.tɪˌskæn.ə / (BrE)
an apparatus used in medical imaging to produce a scintigram by detecting and recording radiation from radioactive tracers in the body

Examples

medical scintiscanner
A scintiscanner helps doctors visualize organ function by detecting gamma radiation from tracers.
Technicians must be trained to properly operate the scintiscanner during patient imaging procedures.
The hospital recently acquired a new scintiscanner for the nuclear medicine department.

  • synonym – scintigram: the image produced by scintigraphy
  • synonym – scintigraphy: the process of producing images using radioactive tracers
