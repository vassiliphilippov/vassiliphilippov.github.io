entourage

noun
group accompanying an important person
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːn.tuːˈrɑːʒ / (AmE), / ˈɒn.tʊ.rɑːʒ / (BrE)
a group of people who travel with and support an important person, such as a celebrity or leader

Examples

entourage of bodyguards
A small entourage accompanied the diplomat to the international summit.
Her entourage made all the arrangements for her stay during the concert tour.
The actor arrived at the premiere surrounded by his entourage of assistants and bodyguards.

Related Terms

  • synonym – followers: people who support and accompany someone in a position of importance
  • synonym – retinue: a group of advisers or assistants accompanying an important person
