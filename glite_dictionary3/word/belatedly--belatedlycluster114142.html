belatedly

adverb
later than expected or usual
Pronunciation: / bɪˈleɪtɪdli / (AmE), / bɪˈleɪtɪdli / (BrE)
Later than expected or usual; after the appropriate or expected time.

Examples

belatedly apologized
He belatedly realized he had forgotten her birthday and sent an apology.
She belatedly finished her assignment after the deadline had passed.
The announcement was belatedly posted on the company's website.
