Laurus nobilis

noun
aromatic Mediterranean evergreen tree (bay laurel)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔːrəs noʊˈbaɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˈlɔːrəs nəʊˈbɪlɪs / (BrE)
A small evergreen tree of the Mediterranean region, with aromatic glossy leaves used as a culinary herb (bay leaf) and blackish berries; also used by ancient Greeks to crown victors.
