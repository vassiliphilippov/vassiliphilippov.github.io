crudely

adverb
in a rough or unrefined manner
Pronunciation: / ˈkrudli /; / ˈkruːdli / (AmE), / ˈkruːdli / (BrE)
in a rough, unrefined, or unsophisticated way; lacking tact, skill, or polish

Examples
