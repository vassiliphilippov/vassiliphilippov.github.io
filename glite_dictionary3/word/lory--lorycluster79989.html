lory

noun
small Australasian parrot
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔːri / (AmE), / ˈlɒri / (BrE)
A small, brightly colored parrot native to Australasia with a brush-tipped tongue for feeding on nectar and soft fruits.

Examples

colorful lory bird
A lory uses its unique tongue to feed on nectar and soft fruits in the wild.
Many zoos have exhibits featuring the stunning rainbow lory from Australia.
The lory perched gracefully on the branch, its vivid feathers shining in the sun.
