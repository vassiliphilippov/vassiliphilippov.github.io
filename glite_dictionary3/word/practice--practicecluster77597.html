practice

noun
customary behavior or method
Pronunciation: / ˈpræktɪs /; / ˈpræk.tɪs / (AmE), / ˈpræktɪs /; / ˈpræk.tɪs / (BrE)
a habitual or usual way of doing something, or the act of putting an idea, theory, or plan into effect
