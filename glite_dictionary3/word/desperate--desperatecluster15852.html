desperate

adjective
reckless or violent from urgency or despair
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛspɚɪt /; / ˈdɛspɚət /; / ˈdɛsprət / (AmE), / ˈdes.p(ə).rət /; / ˈdɛspərət / (BrE)
involving or showing risky, bold, or dangerous actions or situations because of extreme need or hopelessness
