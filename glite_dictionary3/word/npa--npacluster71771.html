NPA

proper noun
Philippine Maoist militant group
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.piˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛn.piˈeɪ / (BrE)
a Maoist insurgent group in the Philippines, serving as the armed wing of the Communist Party of the Philippines, known for opposing the government and United States military presence.
