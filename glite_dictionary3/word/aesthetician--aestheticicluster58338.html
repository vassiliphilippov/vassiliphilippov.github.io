aesthetician

noun
philosopher of aesthetics/beauty
Pronunciation: / ˌɛsˌθiˈtɪʃən, ˌæsˌθiˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌiːsˌθɛtˈɪʃən, ˌæsˌθɛtˈɪʃən / (BrE)
a philosopher who specializes in the study of the nature of beauty and aesthetic experience

aesthetician studies beauty
