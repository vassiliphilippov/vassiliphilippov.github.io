anonymous

adjective
lacking individuality or distinct features
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑːnɪməs / (AmE), / əˈnɒnɪməs / (BrE)
Lacking distinctive features or individuality; not easily identified or distinguished.

Examples

an anonymous building
He worked as an anonymous bureaucrat for decades, never standing out from his colleagues.
Rows of anonymous buildings lined the industrial estate.
The décor of the hotel lobby was deliberately anonymous to appeal to all tastes.

Related Terms

  • antonym – distinctive: having a quality or characteristic that makes something different and easily noticed
  • synonym – featureless: having no distinct features or characteristics
