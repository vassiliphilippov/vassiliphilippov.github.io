enrollment

noun
the group of registered people, e.g., students
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈroʊlmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈrəʊlmənt / (BrE)
The group of people registered or enrolled at a particular institution, especially at a school or college, at a given time.

Examples

enrollment numbers rise
Next semester's enrollment is expected to reach a new record.
The enrollment of international students has doubled over the past decade.
The university reported that its enrollment increased by five percent this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – class: a group of students who enroll or graduate together
  • synonym – registration: the act or process of enrolling or being enrolled
