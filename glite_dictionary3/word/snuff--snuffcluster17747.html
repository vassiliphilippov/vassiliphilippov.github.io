to snuff

verb
sniff or smell inquiringly
Pronunciation: / snʌf / (AmE), / snʌf / (BrE)
to sniff or smell something inquisitively or with curiosity

Examples

to snuff a scent
He snuffed the contents of the vial suspiciously before handing it back.
She paused to snuff the fragrant air of the garden.
The dog began to snuff at the ground, searching for hidden treats.
