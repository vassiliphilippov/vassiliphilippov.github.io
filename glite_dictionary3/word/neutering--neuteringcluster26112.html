neutering

noun
sterilization of an animal
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːtərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtərɪŋ / (BrE)
the surgical procedure of making an animal unable to reproduce, typically by removing reproductive organs

Examples

neutering a pet
After neutering, the dog's behavior became noticeably calmer.
Many pet owners choose neutering as a responsible way to prevent unwanted litters.
The veterinarian recommended neutering to help control the stray cat population.
