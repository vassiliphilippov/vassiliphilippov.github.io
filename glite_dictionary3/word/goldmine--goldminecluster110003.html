goldmine

noun
source of great value or abundance
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldˌmaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌmaɪn / (BrE)
A place, source, or thing that yields a plentiful supply of something valuable or desirable.

Examples

goldmine of ideas
His grandfather's attic proved to be a goldmine of old photographs and letters.
The library was a goldmine of information for her research project.
When the website went viral, it turned into a goldmine for its creators.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bonanza: a situation or event that creates a sudden increase in wealth or good fortune
  • synonym – treasure trove: a collection or source of valuable things
