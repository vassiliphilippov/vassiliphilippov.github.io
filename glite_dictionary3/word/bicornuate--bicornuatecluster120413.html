bicornuate

adjective
having two horns or horn-shaped parts
Pronunciation: / baɪˈkɔrn.ju.ɪt / (AmE), / baɪˈkɔː.njuː.eɪt / (BrE)
(of an organ or structure) having two horns or horn-shaped parts, especially applied to the uterus in some mammals.
