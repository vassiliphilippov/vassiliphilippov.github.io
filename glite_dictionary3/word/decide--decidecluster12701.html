to decide

verb
cause to make a decision
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsaɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈsaɪd / (BrE)
to cause (someone) to make a decision; to influence or determine someone's choice

Examples

decide her to act
Her friend's advice finally decided her to change careers.
It was the opportunity to travel that decided him to accept the job offer.
The poor weather decided us against going hiking this weekend.

  • synonym – determine: to influence or decide the outcome of something
