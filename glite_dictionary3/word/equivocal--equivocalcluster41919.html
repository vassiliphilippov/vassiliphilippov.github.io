equivocal

adjective
open to multiple interpretations; ambiguous
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkwɪvəkəl / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪvəkəl / (BrE)
open to more than one interpretation; ambiguous or uncertain, sometimes with the intention to mislead
