to asterisk

verb
to mark with an asterisk
Pronunciation: / ˈæstɚˌɪsk /; / ˈæstərɪsk / (AmE), / ˈæstərɪsk / (BrE)
to mark something with an asterisk (*) to show it is important or to call attention to it

Examples

to asterisk important words
In the appendix, the authors asterisked every dubious source.
Please asterisk any items that require further discussion at the meeting.
The editor decided to asterisk all corrected sections of the manuscript.
He asterisked all the key points in his presentation notes.
Please asterisk any items that require immediate action.
While reviewing the contract, she began asterisking sections she wanted to discuss further.
