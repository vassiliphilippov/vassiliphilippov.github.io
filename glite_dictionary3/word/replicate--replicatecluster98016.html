to replicate

verb
repeat or reproduce an action or result
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛplɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈrɛplɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to make or do something again in exactly the same way, especially to achieve the same result

Examples

to replicate a result
It is difficult to replicate such outstanding success year after year.
She couldn’t replicate the amazing cake she made for his birthday last time.
The scientists tried to replicate the experiment to confirm their results.
