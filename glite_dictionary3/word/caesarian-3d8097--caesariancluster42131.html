caesarian

adjective
relating to Caesarean (abdominal) delivery
Pronunciation: / sɪˈzɛriən / (AmE), / siˈzɛːrɪən / (BrE)
Relating to a Caesarean section, a surgical operation for delivering a baby through an incision in the abdomen and uterus.

Examples

caesarian birth
Many hospitals have seen an increase in caesarian births in recent years.
She was born by caesarian section after an unexpected medical emergency.
The doctor recommended a caesarian delivery due to complications during labor.
