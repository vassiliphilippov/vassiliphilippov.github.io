to tenant

verb
occupy as a tenant
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnənt / (AmE), / ˈtɛnənt / (BrE)
To occupy or live in (a property or land) as a tenant.

Examples

to tenant a house
Many historical buildings are no longer tenanted and have fallen into disrepair.
Several artists tenant the studios in that converted warehouse.
The old farmhouse was tenanted by a family for over fifty years.
