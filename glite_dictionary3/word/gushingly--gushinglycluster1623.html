gushingly

adverb
in an excessively enthusiastic manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡʌʃɪŋli / (BrE)
In an excessively enthusiastic, effusive, or unrestrained manner.

Examples

to speak gushingly
He greeted his old mentor gushingly, unable to hide his excitement and admiration.
She gushingly thanked her friends for their support after winning the contest.
The critic spoke gushingly about the director's latest film, praising every aspect of it.

Related Terms

  • synonym – effusively: in a way that shows unrestrained emotion or enthusiasm
  • antonym – restrainedly: in a controlled or reserved manner
