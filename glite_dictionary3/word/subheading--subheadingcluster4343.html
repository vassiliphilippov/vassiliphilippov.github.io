subheading

noun
heading for a section of text
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌhɛdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌhɛdɪŋ / (BrE)
A title or heading given to a subdivision of a piece of writing to indicate what the section is about.

Examples

subheading in a chapter
Each chapter is divided into smaller sections with a subheading for easy reference.
Please add a descriptive subheading beneath the main title to clarify the topic.
