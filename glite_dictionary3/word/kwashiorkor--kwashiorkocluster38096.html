kwashiorkor

noun
severe protein-deficiency malnutrition in children
Pronunciation: / kwɑːʃiˈɔrkər / (AmE), / kwɒʃɪˈɔːkɔː / (BrE)
A severe form of malnutrition in children caused by a diet overly high in carbohydrates and extremely low in protein.

Examples

kwashiorkor in children
A balanced diet is crucial to prevent kwashiorkor in vulnerable populations.
Children suffering from kwashiorkor often display swollen bellies and thin limbs.
The aid workers treated many cases of kwashiorkor in the famine-affected region.
