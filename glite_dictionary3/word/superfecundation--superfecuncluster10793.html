superfecundation

noun
fertilization of multiple eggs by sperm from separate acts
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˌfiːkʌnˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˌfiːkʌnˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
the fertilization of two or more ova released during the same menstrual cycle by sperm from separate acts of intercourse, especially from different males
