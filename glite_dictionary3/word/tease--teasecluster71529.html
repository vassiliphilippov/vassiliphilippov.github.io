tease

noun
person who mocks or provokes playfully
Pronunciation: / tiz / (AmE), / tiːz / (BrE)
a person who habitually mocks or annoys others, or who provokes curiosity or desire, often playfully

Examples

a playful tease
Don't be a tease; just tell us what happened already!
Everyone knows Kara is a real tease at parties, always making jokes and laughing at her friends.
He was such a tease, dropping hints about the surprise but never actually revealing it.

Related Terms

  • synonym – prankster: a person who plays practical jokes
  • synonym – tormentor: a person who deliberately annoys or hurts others
