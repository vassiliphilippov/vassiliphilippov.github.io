preoccupation

noun
the state of being absorbed in thought
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˌɑːkjuˈpeɪʃən /; / priˌɑːkjuˈpeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːˌɒkjʊˈpeɪʃən /; / priˌɒkjʊˈpeɪʃən / (BrE)
