to bracket

verb
enclose in brackets (verb)
Pronunciation: / ˈbræk.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈbræk.ɪt / (BrE)
to enclose (a word, phrase, or remark) in brackets, typically in written or printed text

Examples

to bracket extra words
Editors often bracket additional information for clarity in quoted text.
Please bracket any names that should be omitted from the list.
You must not bracket entire sentences unless necessary.
