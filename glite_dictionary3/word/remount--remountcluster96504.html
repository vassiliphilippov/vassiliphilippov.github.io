to remount

verb
mount or put back again
Pronunciation: / riˈmaʊnt / (AmE), / rɪˈmaʊnt / (BrE)
to mount or put back into position again, especially after being disassembled or removed

Examples

to remount a cover
After repairing the device, he carefully remounted the cover.
She had to remount the horse after falling during the lesson.
The engineer will remount the panel once the inspection is complete.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mount: to put in position or assemble for use
