concha

noun
shell-shaped anatomical structure
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.tʃə, ˈkɑŋ.kə / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.kə, ˈkɒŋ.kə / (BrE)
(Anatomy) A structure resembling a shell in shape, especially in the ear or nose.

Examples

ear concha
Swelling of the auricular concha can affect hearing.
The inferior concha is one of the three turbinates in the nasal cavity.
