to vlog

verb
to create and present a video blog
Pronunciation: / vlɑɡ / (AmE), / vlɒɡ / (BrE)
to create and present a video blog, usually by filming oneself and publishing the video online

Examples

to vlog daily
He spent hours editing before uploading the vlog to his channel.
Many students now vlog about their experiences studying abroad.
She decided to vlog her entire road trip from start to finish.
