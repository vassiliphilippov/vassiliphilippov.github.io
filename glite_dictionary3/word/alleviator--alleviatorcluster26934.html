alleviator

noun
person who makes suffering more bearable
Pronunciation: / əˈliːviˌeɪtər / (AmE), / əˈliːvɪeɪtə / (BrE)
a person, especially a therapist, who makes suffering more bearable or less severe

Examples

trusted alleviator of pain

Many see nurses as natural alleviators in times of crisis.
