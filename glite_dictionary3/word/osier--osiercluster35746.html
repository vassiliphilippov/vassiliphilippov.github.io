osier

noun
flexible willow twig used in weaving
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.ʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.zɪə / (BrE)
a type of willow tree or shrub with flexible twigs, or the twigs themselves, often used for weaving baskets and furniture
