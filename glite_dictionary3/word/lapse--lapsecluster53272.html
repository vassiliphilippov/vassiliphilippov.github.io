to lapse

verb
(of time) to pass
Pronunciation: / ˈlæps / (AmE), / ˈlæps / (BrE)
(of time) to pass or slip by.

Examples

time lapses quickly
As the afternoon lapsed, the crowd began to thin out.
Many months would lapse before she received a reply.
Several years have lapsed since they last met.
