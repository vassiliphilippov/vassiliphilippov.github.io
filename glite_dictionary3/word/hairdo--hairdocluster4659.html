hairdo

noun
arrangement or style of hair
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌdu / (AmE), / ˈheə.duː / (BrE)
A way in which a person's hair is cut and arranged, especially as a particular style.

Examples

fancy hairdo
He spent an hour at the salon getting his hairdo just right for the wedding.
She arrived at the party with a stunning hairdo that caught everyone's attention.
The actress changed her hairdo for every scene in the film.

Related Terms

  • synonym – haircut: the act of cutting the hair or the style in which it is cut
  • synonym – hairstyle: a particular way of cutting and arranging the hair
