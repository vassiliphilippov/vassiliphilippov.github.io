apparatus

noun
specialized equipment or device
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˈrætəs / (AmE), / ˌæpəˈreɪtəs / (BrE)
A complex device or set of equipment designed for a particular purpose.

Examples

firefighter apparatus
Firefighters checked that all their apparatus was functioning properly.
Modern medical apparatus can monitor a patient's vital signs continuously.
The laboratory apparatus was carefully sterilized before use.
