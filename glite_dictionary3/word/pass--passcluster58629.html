to pass

verb
give or relay information
Pronunciation: / pæs / (AmE), / pɑːs / (BrE)
to communicate or relay information, news, or a message to someone else

Examples

to pass a message
Could you pass this information on to the rest of the group?
I'll pass the news along to everyone at the meeting.
Please pass the message to your team as soon as possible.
