pick

noun
tool for plucking stringed instruments
Pronunciation: / pɪk / (AmE), / pɪk / (BrE)
A small, thin device made of metal, plastic, or other materials, used for plucking the strings of a musical instrument.

Examples

guitar pick tool
A lost pick can easily slip between the strings and disappear inside the guitar.
He always uses a pick when playing his electric guitar.
She bought a new pick made of tortoiseshell at the music store.

Related Terms

  • synonym – plectrum: a small flat tool used to pluck or strum the strings of some musical instruments
