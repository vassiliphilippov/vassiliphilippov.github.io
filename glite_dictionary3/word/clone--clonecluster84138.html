clone

noun
AI-generated digital replica of a person
Pronunciation: / kloʊn / (AmE), / kləʊn / (BrE)
A digital or AI-generated replica of a real person that simulates their voice, appearance, or behaviour for online interactions or media.

Examples

AI clone avatar
A company released a clone of the late founder to answer questions from fans.
Several startups now offer virtual clones that can handle customer service chats automatically.
Journalists warned the audience that the politician's online clone repeated prewritten lines during interviews.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deepfake: synthetic media in which a person's likeness or voice is artificially manipulated or generated
  • synonym – digital double: a digital representation of a person used in virtual environments or interactions
  • antonym – original: the real person from whom the digital replica is based; not artificially generated
