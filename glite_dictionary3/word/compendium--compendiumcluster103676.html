compendium

noun
concise, comprehensive summary or collection
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɛndiəm / (AmE), / kəmˈpɛndiəm /; / kəmˈpɛndɪəm / (BrE)
a book or document that collects and summarizes information, stories, or works for reference or study

Examples

compendium of stories
