Phaseolus coccineus

noun
runner bean plant; food bean with red flowers
Pronunciation: / fəˌziːoʊləs kɒkˈsɪniəs / (AmE), / fəˌziːələs kɒkˈsɪniəs / (BrE)
A tropical American perennial bean plant with red flowers and mottled black beans, commonly known as the runner bean, widely grown for food, especially in Britain.
