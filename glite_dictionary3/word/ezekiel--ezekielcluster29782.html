Ezekiel

proper noun
Old Testament book of prophecy
Pronunciation: / ɪˈziːkiəl / (AmE), / ɪˈziːkiəl / (BrE)
an Old Testament book containing prophecies attributed to the prophet Ezekiel, including predictions of the downfall and restoration of Jerusalem and Judah
