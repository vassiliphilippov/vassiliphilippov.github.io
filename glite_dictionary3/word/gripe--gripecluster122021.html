gripe

noun
an informal complaint or objection
Pronunciation: / ɡraɪp / (AmE), / ɡraɪp / (BrE)
An informal complaint or expression of dissatisfaction, especially about something relatively minor.

Examples

a gripe about work
Despite his gripe about the noise, he renewed his lease.
My only gripe about the hotel is the slow Wi-Fi.
She aired her gripe with management during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – complaint: an expression of dissatisfaction or annoyance
  • antonym – praise: expression of approval or admiration
