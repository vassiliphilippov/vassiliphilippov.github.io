4-hitter

noun
baseball: game with 4 hits allowed by pitcher
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔr ˌhɪtər / (AmE), / ˈfɔː ˌhɪtə / (BrE)
(in baseball) a game in which the pitcher allows the opposing team exactly four hits.

Examples

pitch a 4-hitter
She became the first woman in the league to pitch a 4-hitter against the champions.
The rookie pitcher threw a brilliant 4-hitter in his major league debut.
Yesterday's 4-hitter helped the team advance to the next round of the playoffs.
