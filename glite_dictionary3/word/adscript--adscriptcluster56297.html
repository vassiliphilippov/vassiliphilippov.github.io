adscript

adjective
bound to land; tied to an estate
Pronunciation: / ˈædˌskrɪpt / (AmE), / ˈadˌskrɪpt / (BrE)
(of a person) bound to a particular tract of land, with their service or labor transferable along with the property to new owners.
