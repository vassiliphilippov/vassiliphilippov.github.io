Galatians

proper noun
New Testament epistle by Saint Paul
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈleɪʃənz / (AmE), / ɡəˈleɪʃənz / (BrE)
A book of the New Testament containing the epistle from Saint Paul to the Christian community in Galatia.
