seminar

noun
small group academic discussion class
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmɪˌnɑr /; / ˈsem.əˌnɑr / (AmE), / ˈsɛmɪnɑː /; / ˈsem.ɪ.nɑː / (BrE)
a class or meeting where a small group discusses a subject, usually led by an expert or teacher
