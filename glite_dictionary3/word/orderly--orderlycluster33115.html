orderly

noun
hospital attendant with non-medical duties
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdɚli / (AmE), / ˈɔːdəli / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, employed in a hospital to help with general tasks that do not involve direct medical treatment of patients

Examples

hospital orderly
After his surgery, an orderly brought John fresh linens and water.
The orderly helped transfer the patient from his bed to the wheelchair.
The hospital staff called for an orderly to assist with moving equipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym – attendant: a person who assists or serves others as part of their job
