popinjay

noun
archaic term for a parrot
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑpɪnˌdʒeɪ /; / ˈpɑːpɪnˌdʒeɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɒpɪnˌdʒeɪ /; / ˈpɒpɪndʒeɪ / (BrE)
a person who is vain, showy, or talks too much (originally an old word for a parrot, now used as an insult)
