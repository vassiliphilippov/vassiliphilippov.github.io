resonance

noun
mutual understanding in a relationship
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛzəˌnəns / (AmE), / ˈrɛzənəns / (BrE)
A quality in a relationship characterized by mutual understanding, trust, and agreement between people.

Examples

resonance with ideas
During the meeting, I felt a strong resonance with his perspective.
The film achieved a deep resonance with audiences because of its universal themes.
There was an immediate resonance between the new colleagues, leading to a successful partnership.

Related Terms

  • synonym – rapport: a close and harmonious relationship where people understand each other's feelings and ideas
