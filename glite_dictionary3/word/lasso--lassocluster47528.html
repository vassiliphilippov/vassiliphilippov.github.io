lasso

noun
noosed rope for catching animals
Pronunciation: / ləˈsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈlasəʊ / (BrE)
a long rope with a sliding noose at one end, used especially for catching cattle or horses.

Examples

lasso a horse
She learned how to throw a lasso while working on the ranch.
The cowboy quickly swung his lasso and caught the runaway calf.
The performer demonstrated his impressive lasso skills at the rodeo.

Related Terms

  • synonym – noose: a loop formed in a rope, used for catching or holding something
