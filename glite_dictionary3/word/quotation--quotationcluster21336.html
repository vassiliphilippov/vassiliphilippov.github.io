quotation

noun
the practice of quoting from sources
Pronunciation: / kwoʊˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / kwəʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
words or passages taken from a book, speech, or other source, the act of quoting them, or a note showing where they came from
