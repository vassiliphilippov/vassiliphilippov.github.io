horizontal

adjective
parallel to the horizon
Pronunciation: / ˌhɔːrɪˈzɑːntl̩ / (AmE), / ˌhɒrɪˈzɒntl̩ / (BrE)
Parallel to the horizon or to a base line; at right angles to the vertical.

Examples

horizontal position

The table has a perfectly horizontal surface.
