inconsiderate

adjective
lacking concern for others
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.kənˈsɪd.ə.rət /; / ˌɪnkənˈsɪdərət / (AmE), / ˌɪn.kənˈsɪd.ər.ət /; / ˌɪnkənˈsɪdərət / (BrE)
not caring about the feelings, needs, or rights of others; acting without thinking about the consequences for others
