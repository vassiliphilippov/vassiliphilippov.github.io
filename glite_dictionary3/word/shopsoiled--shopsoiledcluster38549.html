shopsoiled

adjective
worn from shop display
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑpˌsɔɪld / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpˌsɔɪld / (BrE)
(of goods) having become slightly damaged, faded, or dirty from being displayed or handled in a shop, and therefore considered less valuable.
