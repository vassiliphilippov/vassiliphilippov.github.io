biweekly

noun
periodical published twice a week or every two weeks
Pronunciation: / baɪˈwiːkli / (AmE), / baɪˈwiːkli / (BrE)
A periodical that is published either twice a week or every two weeks.

Examples

biweekly magazine
She writes a column for a biweekly that focuses on science and technology.
The company launched a new biweekly to keep readers informed about industry trends.
Their biweekly is distributed to subscribers on alternate Thursdays.
