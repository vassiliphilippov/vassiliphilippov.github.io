Neomys anomalus

noun
a species of water shrew
Pronunciation: / ˌniː.oʊˈmɪs əˈnɒmələs / (AmE), / ˌniː.əˈmɪs əˈnɒmələs / (BrE)
A species of water shrew found in parts of Europe, known for its semi-aquatic habits and pointed snout.
