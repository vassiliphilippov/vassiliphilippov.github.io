endowment

noun
funds or property supporting an institution
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdaʊmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdaʊmənt / (BrE)
money or property given to an institution, person, or group to provide a permanent source of income (the fund itself or the act of giving it)
