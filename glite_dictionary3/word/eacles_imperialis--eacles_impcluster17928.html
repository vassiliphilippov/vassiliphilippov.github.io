Eacles imperialis

noun
a large North American moth species
Pronunciation: / ˈiːkliːz ˌɪm.pɪˈriː.ə.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈiːkliːz ˌɪm.pɪˈrɪə.lɪs / (BrE)
A large North American moth with yellow wings marked with purplish or brownish spots; its larvae feed on trees such as maple and pine.
