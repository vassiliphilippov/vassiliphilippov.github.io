teeterboard

noun
a balanced board ridden up and down
Pronunciation: / ˈtitərˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈtiːtəˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a long, narrow board balanced in the middle on a support, which is ridden up and down by children or acrobats at either end

Examples

teeterboard in a circus
After painting the old teeterboard, it looked as good as new.
The children giggled as they rode the teeterboard in the park.
The circus performer expertly flipped on the teeterboard during the act.
