to sanitise

verb
make clean or sterile
Pronunciation: / ˈsænətaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsænɪtʌɪz / (BrE)
to make something clean and free from bacteria or germs by cleaning or sterilizing

Examples

to sanitise kitchen surfaces
All surfaces should be sanitised before use in a commercial kitchen.
Please sanitise your hands before entering the ward.
The hospital staff sanitised the equipment thoroughly after each procedure.
