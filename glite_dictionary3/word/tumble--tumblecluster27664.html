tumble

noun
acrobatic rolling movement
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌmbəl / (AmE), / ˈtʌmbəl / (BrE)
an acrobatic movement involving rolling or turning over end to end, such as a somersault

Examples

a forward tumble
Children love practicing a simple tumble during their first gymnastics lessons.
He lost his balance and took a tumble down the grassy hill, laughing all the way.
The gymnast performed a perfect tumble on the mat during her routine.
