abstruse

adjective
difficult to understand
Pronunciation: / æbˈstruːs / (AmE), / əbˈstruːs / (BrE)
Difficult to understand; obscure and complex.

Examples

abstruse theory
Many readers found the novel overly abstruse and gave up halfway through.
She struggled to make sense of the abstruse mathematical theorem in the textbook.
The philosopher's explanation of time was so abstruse that few could follow it.
