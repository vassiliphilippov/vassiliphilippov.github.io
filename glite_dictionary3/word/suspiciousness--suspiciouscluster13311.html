suspiciousness

noun
the quality of being suspicious
Pronunciation: / səˈspɪʃ.əs.nəs / (AmE), / səˈspɪʃ.əs.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being suspicious, especially a tendency to mistrust or doubt others' motives.

Examples

suspiciousness towards people
Her suspiciousness made her question everyone's motives during the investigation.
His suspiciousness was evident whenever he talked about his coworkers' intentions.
Increasing levels of suspiciousness can harm relationships if not addressed honestly.

  • synonym – distrustfulness: a tendency to distrust others
  • antonym – trustfulness: a tendency to trust others
