ozaena

noun
chronic nasal disease with foul discharge
Pronunciation: / oʊˈziːnə / (AmE), / əʊˈziːnə / (BrE)
A chronic disease of the nose marked by a foul-smelling nasal discharge and atrophy of the nasal tissues.

Examples

chronic ozaena symptoms
Chronic ozaena can lead to significant discomfort and social embarrassment due to the persistent odor.
