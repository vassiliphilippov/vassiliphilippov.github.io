Hygrophorus tennesseensis

noun
species of fungus with sticky brown cap
Pronunciation: / haɪˌɡrɑːfəˌrʊs tɪˌnɛsiˈɛnsɪs / (AmE), / haɪˌɡrɒfəˌrʊs tɪˌnɛsiˈɛnsɪs / (BrE)
