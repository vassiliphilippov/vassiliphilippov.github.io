microcephalus

noun
person with abnormally small head (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.kroʊˈsɛf.ə.ləs / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.krəʊˈsɛf.ə.ləs / (BrE)
A person with an abnormally small head and an underdeveloped brain, usually as a result of a congenital condition.

Examples

child with microcephalus
Historically, people with microcephalus were often misunderstood and poorly treated.
She specializes in treating children diagnosed as microcephalus to provide early support and care.
