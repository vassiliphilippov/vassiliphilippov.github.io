trap

noun
device for catching animals or things
Pronunciation: / træp / (AmE), / træp / (BrE)
a device or mechanism for catching or holding animals or objects, or a U-shaped pipe that holds water to block sewer gases

Examples

animal trap
He checked his trap every morning hoping to find something inside.
Mice were caught in the trap placed behind the kitchen cabinet.

All bathroom fixtures must be fitted with a trap to meet building codes.
If the trap dries out, unpleasant odors may come up from the drain.
The plumber noticed that the trap under the sink was clogged with grease.
