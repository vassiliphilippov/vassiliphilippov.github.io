ingroup

noun
exclusive group with shared identity
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌɡruːp / (BrE)
a small, exclusive group of people who share a common interest or identity, often with a sense of belonging or favoritism toward each other
