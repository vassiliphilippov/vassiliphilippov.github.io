dotty

adjective
slightly crazy or eccentric
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːti / (AmE), / ˈdɒti / (BrE)
informal: slightly crazy or eccentric in behavior or thinking; mildly insane.

Examples

dotty old lady
He wore mismatched socks and hats, making him appear rather dotty to the neighbors.
My grandmother can be a bit dotty at times, but everyone loves her stories.
Some people thought the old inventor was dotty, but his ideas were ingenious.

Related Terms

  • synonym – crazy: mentally deranged; extremely eccentric or wild
  • antonym – sane: of sound mind; not mentally ill or eccentric
