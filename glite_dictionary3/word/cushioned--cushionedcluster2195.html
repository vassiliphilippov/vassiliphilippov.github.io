cushioned

adjective
padded for comfort or protection
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊʃənd / (AmE), / ˈkʊʃənd / (BrE)
having a layer of padding or soft material to make something more comfortable or to absorb impact.

Examples

cushioned shoes
She bought a pair of cushioned shoes to reduce strain on her feet.
The cushioned seats made the long journey much more comfortable.
We relaxed on the cushioned bench by the window.

Related Terms

  • synonym – padded: filled or covered with soft material for comfort or protection
  • antonym – unpadded: not cushioned or padded
