synizesis

noun
chromatin contraction in meiosis
Pronunciation: / sɪˈnɪzɪs / (AmE), / sɪˈnɪzɪs / (BrE)
the contraction of chromatin into a dense mass at one side of the nucleus during the prophase of meiosis
