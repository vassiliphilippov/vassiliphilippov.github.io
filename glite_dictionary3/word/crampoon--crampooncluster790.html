crampoon

noun
iron shoe spike for walking on ice
Pronunciation: / kræmˈpuːn / (AmE), / kræmˈpuːn / (BrE)
an iron spike attached to a shoe for preventing slipping on ice or during climbing

Examples

Crampoons are essential equipment for traversing icy slopes safely.
