hermaphrodite

adjective
having both male and female reproductive organs
Pronunciation: / hərˈmæf.rəˌdaɪt / (AmE), / hɜːˈmæf.rə.daɪt / (BrE)
(of an animal or plant) having both male and female reproductive organs.

Examples

hermaphrodite plants reproduce

The hermaphrodite flowers of the plant allow it to self-pollinate.
