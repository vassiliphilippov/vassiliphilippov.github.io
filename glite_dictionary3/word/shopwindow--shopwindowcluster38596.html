shopwindow

noun
shop's front display window
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑpˌwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpˌwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
A window at the front of a shop, facing the street, used for displaying goods for sale.

Examples

shopwindow display
She caught her reflection in a brightly lit shopwindow as she walked down the street.
The jewelry sparkled in the shopwindow and attracted passersby.
Tourists stopped to admire the dresses in the shopwindow on Fifth Avenue.

Related Terms

  • synonym – display window: window used to display goods in a store
  • synonym – shopfront: the front side of a shop facing onto a street
