to spruce

verb
make neat or stylish in appearance
Pronunciation: / sprus / (AmE), / spruːs / (BrE)
to make yourself or someone else look neat, smart, or stylish (often used as 'spruce up')

Examples

to spruce yourself up
Before the guests arrived, he spruced himself up in his favorite suit.
She decided to spruce up her office with some fresh flowers and new curtains.
The company is planning to spruce up its old logo for the new campaign.
He always tries to spruce himself up for job interviews.
She decided to spruce up before meeting her old college friends for dinner.
We should spruce up the house before our guests arrive.
