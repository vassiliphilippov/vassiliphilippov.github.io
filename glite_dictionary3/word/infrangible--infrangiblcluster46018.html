infrangible

adjective
impossible to violate or break
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfrændʒəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈfrændʒɪbəl / (BrE)
impossible to break or violate; not able to be infringed upon

Examples

infrangible rights
The constitution guarantees infrangible rights to all its citizens.
The treaty established infrangible boundaries between the two nations.
They believe the code of honor is infrangible and must be respected by everyone.
