para

noun
number of live births (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˈpæɹə / (AmE), / ˈpæɹə / (BrE)
(in obstetrics) a woman who has given birth to one or more liveborn children, or the number of liveborn children delivered by a woman.

Examples

para two children
Her medical chart recorded her as a para three and gravida four.
The patient is a para two, having given birth to two healthy children.
Understanding a woman's para status helps doctors assess her obstetric history.
