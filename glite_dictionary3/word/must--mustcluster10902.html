must

noun
stale or mouldy smell or taste
Pronunciation: / mʌst / (AmE), / mʌst / (BrE)
An unpleasant smell or taste that is stale, old, or mouldy.

Examples

musty smell
After years in the attic, the books gave off a strong must when opened.
He wrinkled his nose at the must of old bread left in the back of the cupboard.
The basement had an unmistakable must that lingered no matter how much she cleaned.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mustiness: the state of being musty; the quality of smelling or tasting old or stale
