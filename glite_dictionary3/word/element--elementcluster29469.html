element

noun
a straight line generating a cylinder or cone
Pronunciation: / ˈɛləmənt / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪmənt / (BrE)
A straight line that, when moved, generates the surface of a cylinder or a cone.

Examples

element of a cylinder
Each element of the cylinder runs parallel to the axis.
In geometry, the element of a cone helps define its shape.
The teacher drew the element to explain the construction of the surface.
