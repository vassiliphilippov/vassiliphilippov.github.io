pallet

noun
portable platform for goods
Pronunciation: / ˈpælət / (AmE), / ˈpælɪt / (BrE)
a flat, portable platform on which goods are stacked, stored, or moved, typically using a forklift

Examples

wooden pallet stack
A forklift operator moved the heavy pallet across the warehouse floor.
Each pallet can hold up to a thousand kilograms of produce.
The boxes of electronics were stacked neatly on a pallet ready for shipping.
