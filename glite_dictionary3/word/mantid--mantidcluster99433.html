mantid

noun
predatory insect (praying mantis)
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈmæn.tɪd / (BrE)
A predatory insect of warm regions with a long body and large eyes, known for holding up its forelimbs as if in prayer.
