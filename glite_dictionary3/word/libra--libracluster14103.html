Libra

noun
constellation between Virgo and Scorpius
Pronunciation: / ˈliːbrə / (AmE), / ˈliːbrə / (BrE)
A small, faint zodiacal constellation in the Southern Hemisphere, situated between Virgo and Scorpius.
