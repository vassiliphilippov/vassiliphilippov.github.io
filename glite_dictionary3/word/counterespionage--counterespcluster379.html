counterespionage

noun
detecting and preventing espionage
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərɪˈspɪɒnɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntərˈɛspɪɒnɪdʒ / (BrE)
The activity of detecting and preventing espionage by enemy agents; measures taken to spy on or thwart spies.
