undersized

adjective
smaller than normal size
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈsaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈsaɪzd / (BrE)
smaller than the usual or expected size for its type or kind.

Examples

an undersized athlete
Many athletes face challenges if they are considered undersized for their sport.
She wore an undersized coat that didn't reach her wrists.
The puppy was noticeably undersized compared to the rest of the litter.

Related Terms

  • antonym – oversized: larger than the usual or expected size
  • synonym – small: of a size that is less than normal or usual
