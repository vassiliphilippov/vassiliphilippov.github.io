closeness

noun
proximity; nearness in space
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊsnəs / (AmE), / ˈkləʊsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being near in space or position; proximity.

Examples

physical closeness
He was startled by the sudden closeness of the approaching train.
She valued the closeness of her office to the main entrance.
The closeness of the two buildings made it difficult for sunlight to enter.

Related Terms

  • antonym – distance: the amount of space between two points
  • synonym – proximity: the state of being near in space or relationship
