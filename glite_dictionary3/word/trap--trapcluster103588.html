trap

noun
informal word for the mouth
Pronunciation: / træp / (AmE), / træp / (BrE)
(informal) the mouth, especially when considered as a source of noise or talk.

Examples

shut your trap
He opened his trap and started complaining again.
If you don't have anything nice to say, keep your trap shut.
She told him to close his trap during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mouth: the opening in the face through which food is taken in, and which is used for speaking
