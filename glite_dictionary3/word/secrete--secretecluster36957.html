to secrete

verb
to hide or conceal something
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkrit / (AmE), / sɪˈkriːt / (BrE)
to hide something carefully; to place out of sight, often for safekeeping or concealment

Examples

to secrete valuables
He secreted the letter behind a loose brick in the wall.
She secretes her spare keys in a plant pot on the porch.
The documents were secreted in a locked drawer to prevent them from being discovered.

Related Terms

  • synonym – conceal: keep something from being seen or found; hide
  • antonym – reveal: make something known or visible
