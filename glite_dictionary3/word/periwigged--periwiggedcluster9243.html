periwigged

adjective
wearing a period-style wig
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛrɪˈwɪɡd / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪˈwɪɡd / (BrE)
Wearing a periwig, especially as was fashionable among men in the 17th and 18th centuries.
