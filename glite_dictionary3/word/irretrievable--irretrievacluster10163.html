irretrievable

adjective
impossible to recover or restore
Pronunciation: / ˌɪ.rɪˈtriv.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌɪ.rɪˈtriː.və.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to recover, regain, or repair; unable to be restored or remedied.

Examples

irretrievable loss
After the scandal, her reputation suffered irretrievable damage.
He committed an irretrievable mistake that changed the course of his career.
The documents were destroyed in a fire, making their loss completely irretrievable.
