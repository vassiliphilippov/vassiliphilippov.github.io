to approach

verb
begin to deal with
Pronunciation: / əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)

Examples

to approach a problem

It can be helpful to approach each challenge with an open mind.
We must approach this sensitive issue carefully to avoid misunderstandings.
