androecium

noun
male reproductive organs of a flower
Pronunciation: / ænˈdroʊ.i.əm / (AmE), / ænˈdrəʊ.si.əm / (BrE)
the collective male reproductive organs (stamens) of a flower

Examples

androecium of a flower
In botanical studies, the structure of the androecium is important for plant identification.
Some flowers have a very prominent androecium that makes up most of the blossom.
The androecium consists of several stamens arranged around the pistil.
