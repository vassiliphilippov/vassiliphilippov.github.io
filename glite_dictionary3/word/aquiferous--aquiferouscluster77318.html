aquiferous

adjective
relating to an aquifer; water-carrying
Pronunciation: / ˌækwəˈfɛrəs / (AmE), / ˌakwɪˈfɛːrəs / (BrE)
relating to or containing an aquifer; able to carry or transmit water through porous rock or sediment

Examples

aquiferous rock layer
An aquiferous layer can provide a reliable source of fresh water for the community.

The rocks in this region are aquiferous and supply water to the surrounding wells.
