Mangifera indica

noun
species of mango tree
Pronunciation: / ˌmæŋɪˈfɛrə ɪnˈdikə / (AmE), / ˌmaŋɪˈfɪərə ɪnˈdɪkə / (BrE)
A large evergreen tropical tree native to South Asia, widely cultivated for its large, sweet, oval fruit known as the mango.
