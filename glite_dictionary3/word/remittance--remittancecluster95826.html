remittance

noun
money sent to another place or person
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɪtəns / (AmE), / rɪˈmɪtəns / (BrE)
a sum of money sent as payment or as a gift, especially to someone in another place or country

Examples

send a remittance home
After moving abroad, he sent a monthly remittance to his family in India.
Many countries rely heavily on remittances from citizens working overseas.
The company accepts remittance via electronic transfer or check.
