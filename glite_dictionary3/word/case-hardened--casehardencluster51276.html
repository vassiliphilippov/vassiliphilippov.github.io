case-hardened

adjective
emotionally toughened or insensitive
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪs ˌhɑrdənd / (AmE), / ˈkeɪs ˌhɑːd(ə)nd / (BrE)
(of a person) emotionally toughened or made insensitive to feelings by experience or hardship.

Examples

case-hardened reporter
After years on the crime beat, the reporter had become case-hardened and rarely showed emotion at tragic events.
She was not as case-hardened as some of her colleagues and still felt sympathy for her clients.
The war produced many case-hardened veterans who found it difficult to talk about their experiences.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hardened: (of a person) emotionally toughened; not easily affected or upset
  • synonym – insensitive: showing or feeling no concern for others' feelings
  • antonym – sensitive: easily affected by or responsive to emotions
