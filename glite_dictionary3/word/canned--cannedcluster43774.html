canned

adjective
pre-recorded for broadcast
Pronunciation: / kænd / (AmE), / kænd / (BrE)
(of music, laughter, or other sound) recorded in advance for later use or broadcast, rather than being live.

Examples

canned laughter
Many television commercials use canned music rather than hiring musicians.
Some viewers find canned applause distracting during game shows.
The sitcom added canned laughter to make the jokes seem funnier.

Related Terms

  • antonym – live: (of sound or broadcast) not prerecorded; occurring in real time
