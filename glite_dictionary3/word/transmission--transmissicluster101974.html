transmission

noun
the passing on of a disease
Pronunciation: / trænˈsmɪʃən / (AmE), / trænzˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
an instance of a disease, infection, or condition being passed from one person, animal, or place to another

Examples

virus transmission risk
Close contact increases the risk of transmission among family members.
Strict hygiene measures slowed the transmission of the disease in the community.
The government is working to reduce the transmission of the virus in schools.

Related Terms

  • synonym – contagion: the transmission of a disease by direct or indirect contact
  • synonym – infection: the process of infecting or the state of being infected
