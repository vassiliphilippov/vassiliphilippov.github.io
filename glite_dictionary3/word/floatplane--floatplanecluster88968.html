floatplane

noun
aircraft with floats for water landings
Pronunciation: / ˈfloʊtˌpleɪn / (AmE), / ˈfləʊtˌpleɪn / (BrE)
an aircraft fitted with floats instead of wheels, enabling it to take off from and land on water

Examples

floatplane lands on water
Several curious birds circled above the floatplane as it prepared for takeoff.
The floatplane touched down smoothly on the calm lake.
They chartered a floatplane to reach the remote island.
