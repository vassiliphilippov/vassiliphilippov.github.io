gillie

noun
soft shoe with decorative lacing (dance)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡɪli / (BrE)
A light shoe without a tongue, featuring decorative lacing up the instep, traditionally worn in Scottish and Irish dancing.

Examples

gillie dance shoes
She laced up her gillies before the Irish dance competition began.
These gillies provide flexibility and support for intricate dance steps.
Traditional Scottish dancers often wear gillies for performances.
