basidiolichen

noun
lichen with a basidiomycete fungus
Pronunciation: / bəˌsɪdioʊˈlaɪkən / (AmE), / bəˌsɪdiəʊˈlaɪkən / (BrE)
A type of lichen whose fungal component belongs to the Basidiomycota group of fungi.

Examples

rare basidiolichen species
Researchers discovered a new species of basidiolichen in the tropical rainforest.
The unique characteristics of basidiolichens make them important subjects in fungal taxonomy studies.
Unlike most lichens, a basidiolichen contains a basidiomycete fungus instead of an ascomycete.
