to bastardise

verb
declare a child illegitimate
Pronunciation: / ˈbæstɚˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈbɑː.stə.daɪz / (BrE)
to declare (a child) to be illegitimate, especially formally or legally

Examples

to bastardise a child
Decades ago, certain laws could bastardise a child born outside of marriage.
He was unjustly bastardised because of his parents' circumstances.
The court moved to bastardise the child after the legitimacy was challenged.
