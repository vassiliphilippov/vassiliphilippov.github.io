rouser

noun
stirring performance or event
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊzər / (AmE), / ˈraʊzə / (BrE)
something (such as a speech, song, or performance) that excites, energizes, or strongly stirs an audience.

Examples

a real rouser performance
The keynote was a real rouser, leaving the audience buzzing with energy.
The band closed with a rouser that had everyone cheering and dancing in the aisles.
Her speech proved a rouser, rallying the volunteers to double their efforts before the deadline.
