squill

noun
medicinal Mediterranean plant with white flowers
Pronunciation: / skwɪl / (AmE), / skwɪl / (BrE)
a Mediterranean or Old World bulbous plant with spikes of small white, blue, or pink flowers, whose bulb is sometimes used in medicine
