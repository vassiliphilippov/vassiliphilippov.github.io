illegitimately

adverb
in a manner born to unmarried parents
Pronunciation: / ˌɪləˈdʒɪtəmətli / (AmE), / ˌɪlɪˈdʒɪtɪmətli / (BrE)
In a way that occurs to a child whose parents are not legally married to each other.

Examples

born illegitimately
Decades ago, children born illegitimately often faced discrimination in their communities.
He was born illegitimately at a time when society strongly disapproved of such circumstances.
The novel's protagonist struggles with the stigma of being illegitimately born.
