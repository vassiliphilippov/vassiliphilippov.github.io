to armour

verb
to equip with armor
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrmər / (AmE), / ˈɑːmə / (BrE)
to equip with armor or protective covering, especially for military purposes

Examples

to armour a vehicle
During the renovation, the castle walls were armoured with steel plates.
The engineers armoured the vehicles for maximum protection against explosives.
They plan to armour the ship before deploying it into hostile waters.
