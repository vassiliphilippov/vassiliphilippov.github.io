goldilocks

noun
European perennial plant with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊl.diˌlɑks / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊl.diˌlɒks / (BrE)
An early-flowering perennial plant of southern and southeastern Europe, with yellow flower heads resembling those of goldenrod.
