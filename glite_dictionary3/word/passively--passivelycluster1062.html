passively

adverb
in a passive manner
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪvli / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that shows acceptance of what happens without active response or resistance.

Examples

passively accept change
Patients may passively accept treatment without knowing all of their options.
She sat passively during the heated discussion, saying nothing.
The audience listened passively to the long lecture.

Related Terms

  • antonym – actively: in a manner involving action or participation
  • synonym – passive: accepting or allowing what happens without response
