Hieronymus Bosch

proper noun
Dutch painter, master of fantastical art
Pronunciation: / haɪˈrɑːnɪməs bɑʃ / (AmE), / hɪəˈrɒnɪməs bɒʃ / (BrE)
(c. 1450–1516) A Dutch painter known for his fantastical imagery and detailed landscapes, famous for works such as 'The Garden of Earthly Delights.'
