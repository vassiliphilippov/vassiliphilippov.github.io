to sandbag

verb
protect or reinforce with sandbags
Pronunciation: / ˈsænˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈsænˌbaɡ / (BrE)
to protect or reinforce an area using sandbags, especially to prevent flooding or damage

Examples

to sandbag a house
Residents formed a human chain to sandbag their homes before the storm hit.
The city workers rushed to sandbag the riverbank as the water levels kept rising.
Volunteers spent all afternoon sandbagging the roads to prevent floodwaters from entering the town.
