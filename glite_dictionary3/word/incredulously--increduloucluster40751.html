incredulously

adverb
in a manner showing disbelief
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkrɛdʒələsli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkrɛd.jʊ.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a manner that shows disbelief or skepticism.

Examples

laughed incredulously
He laughed incredulously when told he had won the lottery.
She stared incredulously at the news anchor, unable to believe the announcement.
The students listened incredulously as the professor described the impossible experiment.
