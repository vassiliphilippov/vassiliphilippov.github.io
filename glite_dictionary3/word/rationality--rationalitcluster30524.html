rationality

noun
quality of being reasonable or logical
Pronunciation: / ˌræʃəˈnæləti / (AmE), / ˌræʃəˈnæləti /; / ˌræʃəˈnælɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being reasonable, logical, and guided by reason rather than emotion

Examples
