Cupressus goveniana

noun
California cypress tree species
Pronunciation: / kuˈprɛsəs ˌgoʊvɪˈnaɪənə / (AmE), / kjuːˈprɛsəs ˌɡəʊvɪˈneɪənə / (BrE)
A small, sometimes shrubby cypress tree native to California, often cultivated as an ornamental plant. It includes forms such as pygmy cypress and Santa Cruz cypress in some classifications.
