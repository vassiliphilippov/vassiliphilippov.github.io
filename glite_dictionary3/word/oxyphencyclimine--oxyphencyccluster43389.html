oxyphencyclimine

noun
anticholinergic drug for peptic ulcers
Pronunciation: / ˌɑksiˌfɛnsɪˈklaɪmin / (AmE), / ˌɒksɪˌfɛnsɪˈklɪmiːn / (BrE)
An anticholinergic drug used to treat peptic ulcers by reducing stomach acid secretion.
