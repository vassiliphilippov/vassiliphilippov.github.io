Hell

noun
(Christianity) place of eternal punishment for sinners
Pronunciation: / hɛl / (AmE), / hɛl / (BrE)
(in Christianity) the place where Satan and evil spirits dwell, and where sinners are believed to suffer eternal punishment after death.

Examples

go to Hell after death
According to the story, those who do evil may be condemned to Hell for eternity.
Many artists have depicted Hell as a realm of suffering and chaos.
The preacher spoke of Hell as a place of fire and torment.
