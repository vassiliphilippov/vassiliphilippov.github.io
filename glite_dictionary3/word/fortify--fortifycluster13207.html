to fortify

verb
strengthen or build up defenses
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrtəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to make something stronger, especially by building defenses or by adding support or encouragement

Examples

to fortify the body
Many breakfast cereals are fortified with vitamins and minerals.
She drank a cup of tea to fortify herself before the meeting.
The army worked through the night to fortify the city walls before the attack.

  • antonym – weaken: to make or become less strong
