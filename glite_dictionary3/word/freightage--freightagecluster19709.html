freightage

noun
charge for transporting goods
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪ.t̬ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈfreɪ.tɪdʒ / (BrE)
the charge or payment for transporting goods by a carrier

Examples

freightage charges paid
Due to increased freightage costs, our shipping expenses doubled this year.
The company was responsible for paying the freightage on all imported materials.
The invoice lists the total freightage charged for transport by rail.

Related Terms

  • synonym – freight: goods transported in bulk by truck, train, ship, or aircraft
