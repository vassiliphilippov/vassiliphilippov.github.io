execration

noun
a curse or invocation of harm
Pronunciation: / ˌɛksɪˈkreɪʃən /; / ˌɛksəˈkreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛksɪˈkreɪʃən / (BrE)
a curse or the act of cursing or expressing strong hatred towards someone or something
