assurance

noun
a binding promise or guarantee
Pronunciation: / əˈʃʊrəns / (AmE), / əˈʃʊərəns / (BrE)
a firm promise or commitment to do something, give something, or refrain from an action

Examples

give an assurance
He gave his assurance that he would never disclose the confidential information.
She sought assurance from the manager that the issue would not happen again.
The company offered an assurance that repairs would be completed by Friday.

Related Terms

  • synonym – guarantee: a formal assurance or promise, especially in writing, that certain conditions will be fulfilled
  • synonym – pledge: a solemn promise or commitment to do or give something
  • synonym – promise: a declaration or assurance that one will do something or that a particular thing will happen
