to transition

verb
change from one state to another
Pronunciation: / trænˈzɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / trænˈzɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
to change or make something change from one state, condition, or place to another

Examples

to transition careers
After years of working in finance, she transitioned to a career in education.
As the seasons transition, many people notice changes in their energy levels.
The company plans to transition its servers to a new cloud platform next year.
Over the next year, the school will transition to a new curriculum.
She helped her team transition from traditional methods to a digital workflow.
The company needed to transition its workforce to adapt to new technologies.
