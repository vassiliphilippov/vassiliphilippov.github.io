debarkation

noun
disembarking from a ship or aircraft
Pronunciation: / diˌbɑːrˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / diˌbɑːˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of passengers and crew leaving a ship or aircraft

Examples

debarkation of passengers
After the cruise docked, we waited an hour for debarkation to begin.
Safety instructions were given before debarkation from the ferry.
The airline crew assisted elderly passengers during debarkation at the airport.
