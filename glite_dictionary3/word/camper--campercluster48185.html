camper

noun
vehicle equipped for camping
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmpɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæmpə / (BrE)
a motor vehicle or trailer equipped with living facilities for camping while traveling

Examples

family camper
Many families choose a camper for convenient and comfortable road trips.
The camper was fully stocked with a small kitchen and sleeping quarters.
We rented a camper and drove across the country during the summer break.
