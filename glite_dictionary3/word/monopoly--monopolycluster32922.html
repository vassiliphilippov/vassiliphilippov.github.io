Monopoly

proper noun
a board game about real estate
Pronunciation: / məˈnɑpəli / (AmE), / məˈnɒpəli / (BrE)
A proprietary board game in which players buy, trade, and develop properties to eventually drive other players into bankruptcy by collecting rent, aiming to obtain a monopoly of real estate holdings.

play Monopoly game
Every family gathering, someone insists on starting a game of Monopoly.
