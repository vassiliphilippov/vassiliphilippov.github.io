descender

noun
part of a letter extending below baseline
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɛndɚ /; / dɪˈsɛndər / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛndə / (BrE)
the part of a lowercase letter (like 'g', 'j', or 'y') that extends below the line of other letters, or a letter with such a part
