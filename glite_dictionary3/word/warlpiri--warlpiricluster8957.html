Warlpiri

noun
Australian Aboriginal language
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːrlˌpɪri / (AmE), / ˈwɑːlˌpiːri / (BrE)
A language spoken by the Warlpiri people, an Indigenous Australian group, primarily in Australia's Northern Territory.

Warlpiri language speakers
