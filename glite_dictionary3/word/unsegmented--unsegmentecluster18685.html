unsegmented

adjective
not divided into segments
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛɡmən̩tɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛɡmɛntɪd / (BrE)
(of an organism or object) not divided into segments.

Examples

unsegmented body
Earthworms are segmented, but tapeworms have an unsegmented body.
Some primitive animals, like flatworms, are unsegmented.
The fossil was identified by its simple, unsegmented structure.
