rising

noun
an organized rebellion or uprising
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
an organized attempt by a group of people to oppose or overthrow those in authority, especially by force; an uprising or rebellion

Historians still debate the causes of the failed rising in the capital.
The government quickly suppressed the rising before it could spread to other regions.
