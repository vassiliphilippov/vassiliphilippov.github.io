amortization

noun
gradual reduction of asset or debt value
Pronunciation: / əˌmɔrtəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌmɔːtəˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of gradually paying off a debt or reducing the value of an asset over a period of time through regular payments
