saucily

adverb
in an impudent or cheeky manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːsɪli / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsɪli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of respect or is cheekily bold or impertinent.

Examples

smile saucily
He tossed his head saucily, ignoring the warning in her voice.
She winked saucily at him before turning away.
The child saucily replied to the teacher's question, making the class laugh.
