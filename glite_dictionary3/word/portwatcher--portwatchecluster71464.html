portwatcher

noun
software/device that monitors network ports
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrtˌwɑtʃər / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtˌwɒtʃə / (BrE)
(in computing) a program or hardware device that monitors network ports for activity and reports or logs unusual connections or traffic.

Examples

portwatcher monitors traffic
The security appliance includes a portwatcher that alerts administrators to unexpected inbound connections.
Before deployment, they configured the portwatcher to ignore routine traffic and only flag anomalies.
During the incident, the portwatcher recorded multiple failed login attempts on critical service ports.

Related Terms

  • synonym – network monitor: software or hardware that observes and records network activity
  • synonym – port monitor: a tool that watches network ports for traffic and events
