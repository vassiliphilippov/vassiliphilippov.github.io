flintlock

noun
old firearm with flint ignition
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪnt.lɑk / (AmE), / ˈflɪnt.lɒk / (BrE)
an old type of firearm that uses a flint striking ignition mechanism, especially common before the 19th century

Examples

old flintlock pistol
He handed down his grandfather's old flintlock as a family heirloom.
Soldiers loaded gunpowder and a lead ball into their flintlock rifles before battle.
The museum displayed a beautifully engraved flintlock pistol from the 18th century.
