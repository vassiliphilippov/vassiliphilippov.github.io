cackler

noun
hen that squawks after laying an egg
Pronunciation: / ˈkæk.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæk.lə / (BrE)
a hen that has just laid an egg and makes a loud, shrill noise

Examples

noisy cackler hen
A loud cackler startled the children as they walked past the henhouse.
After laying her egg, the cackler strutted around the coop, squawking proudly.
The farmer could always hear the cackler in the morning after collecting eggs.
