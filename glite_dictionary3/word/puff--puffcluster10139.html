puff

noun
exaggerated promotional praise
Pronunciation: / pʌf / (AmE), / pʌf / (BrE)
an article or statement that gives exaggerated praise, especially for promotional purposes

Examples

puff piece article
He wrote a puff for her latest book, calling it a masterpiece.
The company's website is full of puff that doesn't match the reality of its services.
The magazine published a puff about the new restaurant that made it sound incredible.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blurb: a brief advertisement or promotional description, often on a book cover
