neuter

adjective
of neither masculine nor feminine grammatical gender
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːtər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtə / (BrE)
(of grammatical gender) belonging to a category that is neither masculine nor feminine.

Examples

neuter noun
In German, the word 'Mädchen' is classified as neuter rather than feminine.
Some languages lack a neuter gender and use only masculine and feminine.
The pronoun 'it' is considered a neuter form in English.
