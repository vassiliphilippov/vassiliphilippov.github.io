stomach

noun
inclination for difficulty or conflict
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌmək / (AmE), / ˈstʌmək / (BrE)
the desire or inclination for dealing with unpleasant, difficult, or confrontational situations

Examples

stomach for a fight
Despite the harsh criticism, she showed plenty of stomach for defending her work in public.
He found he had no stomach for office politics and avoided confrontations whenever possible.
The coach questioned whether the player had the stomach to endure a tough season.
