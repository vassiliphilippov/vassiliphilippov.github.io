malted

noun
milkshake with malt powder
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔltɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɔːltɪd / (BrE)
A milkshake made with milk, ice cream, and malt powder, often sweetened and flavored, popular especially in the United States.

Examples

a malted milkshake
She prefers a malted over a regular milkshake because of its rich, nutty flavor.

Whenever I go to the diner, I always order a chocolate malted with my burger.
