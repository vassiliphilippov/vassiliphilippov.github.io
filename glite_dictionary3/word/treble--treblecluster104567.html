to treble

verb
to increase threefold
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛbəl / (AmE), / ˈtrɛbəl / (BrE)
to increase something to three times its original amount or size

Examples

to treble profits
Cases of the disease trebled over the course of the last decade.
If you treble the recipe, you'll have enough for a large party.
The company's profits trebled in just five years thanks to the new strategy.
