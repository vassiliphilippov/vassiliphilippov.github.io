grille

noun
framework of metal bars
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪl / (AmE), / ɡrɪl / (BrE)
A framework of metal bars used as a partition, screen, or protective barrier, often in doors, windows, or vehicles.

Examples

window grille
He looked at her through the window's protective grille.
The car's front grille was damaged in the accident.
The ornate grille on the mansion’s front door was made of wrought iron.
