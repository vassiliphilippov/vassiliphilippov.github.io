micrometer

noun
one millionth of a meter
Pronunciation: / maɪˈkrɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / maɪˈkrɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a metric unit of length equal to one millionth of a meter (0.000001 m)

Examples

micrometer length unit
A bacterium typically measures about one micrometer in length.
Fibers with diameters less than one micrometer are used in specialized filters.
The tool can detect particles as small as a few micrometers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – micron: another name for a micrometer as a unit of length
  • degree higher – millimeter: a metric unit of length equal to one thousandth of a meter
