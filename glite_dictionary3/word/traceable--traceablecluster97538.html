traceable

adjective
able to be traced to a source or cause
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪsəbəl / (AmE), / ˈtreɪsəbl / (BrE)
able to be discovered or identified as originating from a particular source or cause, especially when followed by 'to'

Examples

traceable to a source
His accent was traceable to his years spent living in Australia.
Many health issues are traceable to environmental factors and lifestyle choices.
The error was traceable to a simple miscalculation in the data.

Related Terms

  • synonym – attributable: able to be ascribed or credited to a particular source
