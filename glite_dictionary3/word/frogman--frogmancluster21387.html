frogman

noun
underwater worker with special equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔɡˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈfrɒɡˌmæn / (BrE)
a person, typically a man, who works underwater using special equipment such as a diving suit and breathing apparatus, especially for military or rescue operations

Examples

frogman in diving suit
As a frogman, he specialized in underwater demolition and reconnaissance missions.
The Navy sent a highly trained frogman to inspect the ship’s hull for damage.
Tourists watched a frogman perform a dramatic rescue in the harbor.
