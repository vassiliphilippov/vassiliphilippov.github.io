caudex

noun
woody stem base of certain perennial plants
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːdɛks / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdɛks / (BrE)
the thickened, often woody stem base of certain plants (such as perennials, palms, or tree ferns), usually at or near ground level, from which new growth can emerge
