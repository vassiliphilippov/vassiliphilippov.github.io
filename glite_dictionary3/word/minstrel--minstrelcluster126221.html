to minstrel

verb
celebrate by singing as minstrels did
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnstrəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪnstrəl / (BrE)
(archaic) To celebrate or entertain by singing, in the manner of minstrels.

Examples

to minstrel songs
On festival nights, villagers minstreled together beneath the stars.
She minstreling her joys, filled the air with sweet melodies.
The bard minstrelled in the great hall, reviving tales of heroism and love.
