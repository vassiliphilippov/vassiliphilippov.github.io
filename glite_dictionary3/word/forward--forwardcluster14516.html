forward

adjective
of a gear that moves vehicle ahead
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrwərd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːwəd / (BrE)
(of a gear) designed to propel a vehicle in the normal direction of travel, as opposed to reverse.

Examples

forward gear
He accidentally shifted out of forward and stalled the engine.
Make sure the car is in forward gear before you start moving.
The truck has five forward gears and one reverse gear.
