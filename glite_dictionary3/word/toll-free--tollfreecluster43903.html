toll-free

adjective
not requiring payment of a toll or fee
Pronunciation: / toʊl fri / (AmE), / təʊl friː / (BrE)
(of a road, telephone number, etc.) not requiring the payment of a toll or fee for use.

Examples

toll-free phone number
A toll-free helpline is available for anyone needing assistance.
All calls to the toll-free customer service number are free of charge.
They took the new toll-free highway to avoid paying fees.
