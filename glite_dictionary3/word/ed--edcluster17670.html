ED

noun
erectile dysfunction (impotence)
Pronunciation: / iː diː / (AmE), / iː diː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for erectile dysfunction: the inability of a man to achieve or maintain an erection suitable for sexual intercourse.

Examples

ED treatment options

Many men experience ED at some point in their lives, especially as they age.
Treatments for ED have improved significantly in recent years, offering new hope to patients.
