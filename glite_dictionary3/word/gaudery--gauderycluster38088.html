gaudery

noun
showy or pretentious display
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɔː.də.ri / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːdəri / (BrE)
showy or pretentious display, often lacking in taste or value; ostentatious decoration or outward show.

Examples

flashy gaudery display
Critics dismissed the exhibition as empty gaudery lacking any real artistic value.
Her dress was a study in gaudery, glittering with tasteless sequins and bright colors.
The palace was adorned with such gaudery that it seemed more vulgar than grand.

Related Terms

  • antonym – modesty: lack of pretentious display
  • synonym – ostentation: showy display meant to attract notice
