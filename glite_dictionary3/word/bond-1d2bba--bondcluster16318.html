bond

noun
debt security issued by government or company
Pronunciation: / bɑnd / (AmE), / bɒnd / (BrE)
a certificate issued by a government or corporation promising to pay back borrowed money with interest at a specified time

Examples

government bond
Matured bonds are paid back to the investors with interest.
She invested a large sum in government bonds to ensure a steady income.
The company issued new bonds to raise capital for expansion.
