to mechanise

verb
introduce machines; make mechanical
Pronunciation: / ˈmek.əˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɛk.ə.nʌɪz / (BrE)
to introduce machines or automatic devices into (a process, industry, etc.) to replace manual labor; to make mechanical

Examples

to mechanise a factory
Many farms have mechanised to reduce the need for manual labor.
The company decided to mechanise its packaging process for greater efficiency.
The factory is mechanising its assembly line to boost production.

Related Terms

  • synonym – automate: to use automatic equipment instead of manual labor
