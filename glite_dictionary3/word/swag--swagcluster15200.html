swag

noun
promotional freebies
Pronunciation: / swæɡ / (AmE), / swæɡ / (BrE)
informal promotional merchandise or free branded items given away at events, conferences, or by companies.

Examples

free swag bag
The conference handed out free swag like pens and tote bags to attendees.
Volunteers filled hundreds of swag bags with samples and coupons before the event.
Startups often give branded swag away to attract visitors to their booths at trade shows.

Related Terms

  • synonym – freebie: an item given free, especially for promotion
  • synonym – promotional merchandise: branded items used to promote a company or event
