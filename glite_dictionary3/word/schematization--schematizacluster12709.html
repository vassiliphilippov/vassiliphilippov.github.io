schematization

noun
arrangement by scheme or formula
Pronunciation: / ˌskiːmətɪˈzeɪʃən /; / skiː.mə.taɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌskiːmətaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / skiː.mət.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of representing or arranging something in a simplified or diagrammatic way, often to make complex information easier to understand

Examples

Artistic schematization often emphasizes form over content.
