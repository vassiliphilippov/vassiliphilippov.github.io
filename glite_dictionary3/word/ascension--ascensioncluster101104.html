Ascension

noun
Christian feast marking Christ's ascent to heaven
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛnʃən / (AmE), / əˈsɛnʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
(in Christianity) the celebration of Christ's ascent into heaven, observed on the 40th day after Easter.
