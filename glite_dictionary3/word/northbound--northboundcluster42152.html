northbound

adjective
moving or heading toward the north
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrθˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈnɔːθˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
(of vehicles, travelers, etc.) moving or heading toward the north.

Examples

northbound traffic lanes
