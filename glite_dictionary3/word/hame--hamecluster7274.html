hame

noun
part of a draft horse's harness
Pronunciation: / heɪm / (AmE), / heɪm / (BrE)
either of a pair of curved metal or wooden supports attached to the collar of a draft horse, used to hold the traces for pulling a vehicle or plough

Examples

hame on a horse collar
Each hame was polished until it gleamed, ready for the parade.
He repaired the horse's collar and carefully reattached the hames before setting off.
