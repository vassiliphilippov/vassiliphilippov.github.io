rubber

noun
eraser for removing pencil marks
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbɚ / (AmE), / ˈrʌb.ə / (BrE)
A small piece of rubber or a synthetic material used for erasing pencil marks, often attached to the end of a pencil.
