rare

adjective
having low density
Pronunciation: / rɛr / (AmE), / reə / (BrE)
(of a substance or gas) having low density; not thick or tightly packed.

Examples

rare air conditions
At high altitudes, the atmosphere becomes increasingly rare and difficult to breathe.
Helium is a rare gas used in balloons.
The climbers struggled to breathe in the rare mountain air.
