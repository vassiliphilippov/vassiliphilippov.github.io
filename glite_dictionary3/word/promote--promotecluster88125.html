to promote

verb
raise to a higher job position
Pronunciation: / prəˈmoʊt / (AmE), / prəˈməʊt / (BrE)
to give someone a higher or more important position or rank within an organization

Examples

promote an employee
After years of dedication, she was finally promoted to senior manager.
He hopes to be promoted within the next year.
The company often promotes employees who show leadership potential.

Related Terms

  • synonym – advance: to move someone forward to a higher position
  • antonym – demote: to lower someone to a less important position
