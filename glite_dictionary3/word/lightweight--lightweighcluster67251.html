lightweight

noun
an unimportant or insignificant person
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌweɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtˌweɪt / (BrE)
a person who is considered unimportant, lacking in influence, or not very serious

Examples

business lightweight
Despite his big talk, he's considered a lightweight in the business world.
Don't underestimate her—she may seem like a lightweight, but she's quite determined.
Many critics dismissed the new senator as a political lightweight with little experience.
