sporting

adjective
showing sportsmanship or fair play
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔːrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)
showing fairness, sportsmanship, or respect for fair play, especially in competition or resolving disagreements

Examples

sporting attitude
He is known for his sporting attitude, even in defeat.
She made a sporting gesture by congratulating her opponent after the match.
They reached a sporting compromise that satisfied both parties.
