Eblis

proper noun
evil spirit in Islamic mythology
Pronunciation: / ˈiːblɪs / (AmE), / ˈiːblɪs / (BrE)
In Islamic mythology, the principal evil jinni who defied God and was cast from heaven; often regarded as the Islamic equivalent of Satan.

Examples

Eblis defies God
According to legend, Eblis refused to bow to Adam and was cast out from paradise.
