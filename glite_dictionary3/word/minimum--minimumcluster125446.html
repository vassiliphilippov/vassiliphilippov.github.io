minimum

adjective
least possible amount or degree (adj.)
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnɪməm / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪməm / (BrE)
the least or smallest amount, degree, or quantity possible or permitted.

Examples

minimum requirements met
Applicants must have the minimum qualifications to be considered for the job.
Each player must pay a minimum fee before joining the club.
There is a minimum age requirement to enter the competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym – maximum: the greatest possible amount or degree
  • synonym – minimal: of the least amount or degree
