screamer

noun
person who shouts loudly
Pronunciation: / ˈskriːmər / (AmE), / ˈskriːmə / (BrE)
a person who shouts or speaks in a very loud and high-pitched voice, often for emphasis or out of excitement or fear

Examples

loud screamer at games
My brother is a real screamer at football games, cheering for his team with all his might.
She became known as the office screamer because of her loud reactions to mistakes.
The baby was quite a screamer and could be heard from the other room.
