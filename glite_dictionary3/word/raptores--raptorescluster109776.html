Raptores

noun
obsolete group of birds of prey
Pronunciation: / ræpˈtɔːriˌiz / (AmE), / ræpˈtɔːriːz / (BrE)
A former taxonomic group comprising birds of prey, once including both falcons and owls, now considered obsolete in modern classification.
