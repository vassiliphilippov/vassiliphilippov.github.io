hostile

adjective
unfriendly; antagonistic
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑstəl /; / ˈhɑːstəl / (AmE), / ˈhɒstaɪl / (BrE)
showing strong dislike or antagonism; unfriendly or difficult to get along with

Examples

hostile attitude
He faced a hostile audience that booed throughout his speech.
Her voice grew hostile when she heard the accusations.
The two countries have maintained a hostile relationship for decades.
His hostile attitude made any compromise impossible.
Relations between the countries remained hostile despite international efforts to reconcile them.
The two hostile factions refused to negotiate a peace treaty.
