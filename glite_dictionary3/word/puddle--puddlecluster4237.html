to puddle

verb
work or manipulate a wet mixture
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌd.əl / (AmE), / ˈpʌd.əl / (BrE)
to work or manipulate a wet mixture, such as concrete or mud, especially to make it more compact or uniform

Examples

to puddle wet concrete
Before laying the bricks, the builders puddled the mud to ensure a good consistency.
He spent the afternoon puddling the clay to prepare it for pottery.
Workers must puddle the concrete thoroughly to prevent air pockets from forming.
