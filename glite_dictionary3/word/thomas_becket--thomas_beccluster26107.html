Thomas Becket

proper noun
Archbishop of Canterbury, murdered in 1170
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑməs ˈbɛkɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɒməs ˈbɛkɪt / (BrE)
(c. 1118–1170) The Archbishop of Canterbury from 1162 until his murder in 1170, known for his conflict with King Henry II over the rights and privileges of the Church.
