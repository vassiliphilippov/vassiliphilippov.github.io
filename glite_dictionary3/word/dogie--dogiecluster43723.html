dogie

noun
motherless range calf (US western)
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊ.ɡi / (AmE), / ˈdəʊ.ɡi / (BrE)
A motherless calf in a range herd of cattle, especially in the western United States.

Examples

orphan dogie calf
Ranchers often watch for dogies during calving season to ensure they get proper care.
She bottle-fed the orphaned dogie until it was strong enough to rejoin the cattle.
The cowboy roped a dogie that had wandered too far from the herd.
