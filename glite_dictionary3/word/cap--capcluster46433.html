to cap

verb
set a maximum number or amount
Pronunciation: / kæp / (AmE), / kap / (BrE)
to set an upper limit on the number or amount of something

Examples

to cap spending
Our school needs to cap the class size at thirty students.
The government decided to cap rent increases to protect tenants.
To preserve fish populations, authorities will cap the annual catch.
