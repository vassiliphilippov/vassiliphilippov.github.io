funereal

adjective
gloomy or solemn, like a funeral
Pronunciation: / fəˈnɪriəl / (AmE), / fjʊˈnɪəriəl / (BrE)
Suggestive of or appropriate to a funeral; having a mournful, solemn, or gloomy character.

Examples

funereal silence
His voice took on a funereal tone as he spoke of the tragedy.
The cloudy sky gave the city a funereal atmosphere.
The house was filled with a funereal silence after the bad news arrived.
