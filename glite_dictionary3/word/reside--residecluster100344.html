to reside

verb
to be present or inherent in something
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzaɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈzaɪd / (BrE)
(of a quality, power, etc.) to be present or inherent in something; to exist as an essential characteristic

Examples

reside in the power
Authority ultimately resides in the people according to the constitution.
The final decision must reside in the hands of the committee.
True leadership qualities often reside within those who least expect it.

  • synonym – inhere: to exist essentially or permanently in something
