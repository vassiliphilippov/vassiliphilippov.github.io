honor

noun
a woman's chastity or virtue (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːnər / (AmE), / ˈɒnə / (BrE)
(archaic) A woman's virtue or chastity, especially as regarded in terms of her reputation.

Examples

her honor lost
