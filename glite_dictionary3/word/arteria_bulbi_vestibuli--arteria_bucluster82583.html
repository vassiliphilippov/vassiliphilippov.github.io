arteria bulbi vestibuli

noun
artery supplying bulb of vestibule (female anatomy)
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪəriə ˈbʊlbaɪ vəˈstɪbjʊlaɪ / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə ˈbʊlbi vɛsˈtɪbjʊlaɪ / (BrE)
A branch of the internal pudendal artery in females that supplies blood to the bulb of the vestibule of the vulva.

Examples

arteria bulbi vestibuli supplies vulva
An obstruction in the arteria bulbi vestibuli could affect blood flow to the vulva.
During pelvic surgery, care must be taken not to damage the arteria bulbi vestibuli.
The arteria bulbi vestibuli is responsible for supplying blood to the vestibular bulbs in female anatomy.
