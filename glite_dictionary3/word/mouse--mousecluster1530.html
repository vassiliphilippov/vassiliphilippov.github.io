mouse

noun
quiet or timid person
Pronunciation: / maʊs / (AmE), / maʊs / (BrE)
a person who is quiet, timid, or shy and easily overlooked by others

Examples

quiet mouse
Although Jane is brilliant, she is a bit of a mouse in meetings and rarely speaks up.
Despite being called a mouse at work, she proved her strength during the crisis.
He used to be a mouse in the classroom, but now he's much more confident.

Related Terms

  • antonym – extrovert: a person who is outgoing and socially confident
  • synonym – shrinking violet: a person who is very shy or modest
  • synonym – wallflower: a person who is shy or unpopular and chooses to watch rather than participate
