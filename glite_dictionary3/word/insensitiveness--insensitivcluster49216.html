insensitiveness

noun
lack of emotional responsiveness
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɛnsɪtɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛnsɪtɪvnɪs / (BrE)
lack of awareness or concern for others' feelings, or inability to respond emotionally to situations or people.

Examples

his insensitiveness to feelings
He apologized for his insensitiveness after realizing how much his words had hurt his friend.
Her insensitiveness made it difficult for her to connect with her coworkers during stressful times.
The manager's insensitiveness to employee concerns led to low morale in the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym – insensitivity: lack of concern for others' feelings; unresponsiveness
  • antonym – sensitivity: ability to perceive and respond to the feelings of others
