fax

noun
electronic document-sending machine
Pronunciation: / fæks / (AmE), / fæks / (BrE)
a machine that sends and receives printed documents electronically over a telephone line or radio signal

Examples

fax machine
He sent the contract by fax to the main office.
Many businesses still use a fax for urgent document transmission.
The report arrived on the fax machine this morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – facsimile: an exact copy, especially of written or printed material
