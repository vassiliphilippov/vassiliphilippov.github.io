dreaming

noun
indulging in waking fantasies or reveries
Pronunciation: / ˈdriːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdriːmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of having imaginative thoughts or visions while awake; indulging in fantasies or reveries

Examples

dreaming of adventures
He was lost in dreaming about distant places and adventures during the meeting.
Her constant dreaming made it hard for her to concentrate on her schoolwork.
Sometimes, a little dreaming can lead to creative solutions to real problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym – daydreaming: having pleasant, imaginative thoughts while awake
  • antonym – reality: the state of things as they actually exist
  • synonym – reverie: a state of being pleasantly lost in one's thoughts
