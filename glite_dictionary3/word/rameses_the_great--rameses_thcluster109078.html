Rameses the Great

proper noun
Egyptian pharaoh, 1279–1213 BC
Pronunciation: / ˈræmɪˌsiz ðə ˈɡreɪt / (AmE), / ˈræmɪˌsiːz ðə ˈɡreɪt / (BrE)
