repetitive

adjective
involving frequent repetition
Pronunciation: / rəˈpɛt̬.ə.t̬ɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈpɛt.ɪ.tɪv / (BrE)
Involving or characterized by doing the same thing again and again.

Examples

repetitive tasks
Children often enjoy repetitive games because they know what to expect.
Some jobs require repetitive tasks to be performed all day.
The music had a repetitive rhythm that soon became boring.

Related Terms

  • synonym – repetitious: tending to repeat excessively
  • antonym – varied: showing variety; not repetitive
