thoughtfulness

noun
careful, deep consideration
Pronunciation: / ˈθɔt.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt.f(ʊ)l.nəs /; / ˈθɔːt.f(ə)l.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of thinking carefully or deeply about something before acting
