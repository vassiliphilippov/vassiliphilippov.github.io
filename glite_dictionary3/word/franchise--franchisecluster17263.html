franchise

noun
business authorized to distribute goods for a company
Pronunciation: / ˈfrænˌtʃaɪz / (AmE), / ˈfræn.tʃaɪz / (BrE)
a business or the official right to sell or distribute a company's goods or services in a particular area under an agreement
