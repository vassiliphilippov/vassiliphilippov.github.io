purposefulness

noun
quality of having a definite purpose
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜːrpəsfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpəsfəlnəs / (BrE)
The quality of having a clear aim or determination; the state of being purposeful.

Examples

strong purposefulness
Her purposefulness inspired the whole team to keep working toward their goals.
The purposefulness of the project gave everyone a sense of direction and motivation.
You can see the purposefulness in his actions as he plans each step carefully.
