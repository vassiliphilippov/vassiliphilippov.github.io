inglorious

adjective
shameful or bringing disgrace
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡlɔriəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡlɔːriəs / (BrE)
shameful or not bringing honor or glory; causing a loss of reputation or distinction

Examples
