manually

adverb
by hand; not automatically
Pronunciation: / ˈmænjuəli / (AmE), / ˈmanjʊəli / (BrE)
Using the hands or physical effort rather than machinery or automation; by hand.

Examples

manually operate a machine
She prefers to drive cars that can be shifted manually.
Some tasks must be done manually when the system is down.
The technician adjusted the machine manually to ensure accuracy.

Related Terms

  • antonym – automatically: by means of a machine or system, without human intervention
