snootiness

noun
arrogance; being snooty
Pronunciation: / ˈsnuːt̬i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsnuː.ti.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of behaving in a way that shows you think you are better than other people; arrogance or pretentiousness

Examples

her snootiness at parties
Many people disliked the club for its reputation of snootiness and exclusion.
She tried to hide her snootiness during the meeting, but it was obvious to everyone.
The snootiness of the staff at the restaurant made us feel unwelcome.

Related Terms

  • antonym – humility: the quality of having a modest view of one's importance
