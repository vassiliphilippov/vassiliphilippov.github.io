azederach

noun
tree with purple blossoms, inedible fruits
Pronunciation: / ˌæzəˈdɛræk / (AmE), / ˌazɪˈdɛræk / (BrE)
A tree native to northern India and China, known for its purple blossoms and small inedible yellow fruits, often grown in the southern United States as a shade tree.
