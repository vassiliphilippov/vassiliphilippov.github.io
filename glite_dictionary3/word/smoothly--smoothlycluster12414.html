smoothly

adverb
in a diplomatic or fluent manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsmuðli / (AmE), / ˈsmuːðli / (BrE)
in an even, effortless, or untroubled way; without problems, awkwardness, or interruption

Examples

to run smoothly

The meeting transitioned smoothly from one topic to another.
Her speech flowed smoothly from start to finish, impressing the audience.
If everything runs smoothly, the project should be completed ahead of schedule.
The transition between the two managers went smoothly and without conflict.
