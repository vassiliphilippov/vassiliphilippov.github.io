namely

adverb
that is to say; specifically
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪmli / (AmE), / ˈneɪmli / (BrE)
used to introduce more specific information or examples about something just mentioned; that is to say.

Examples

namely three countries
Several countries were represented at the conference, namely France, Germany, and Italy.
The company is looking to hire people with strong skills, namely engineers and designers.
There are three main issues to address, namely cost, timing, and quality.
