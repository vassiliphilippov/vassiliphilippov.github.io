scallopine

noun
thin sautéed cutlets, usually veal or poultry
Pronunciation: / ˌskæləˈpiːni / (AmE), / ˌskæləˈpiːni / (BrE)
Thin slices of veal or poultry, pounded, floured, and sautéed, often served with a sauce.
