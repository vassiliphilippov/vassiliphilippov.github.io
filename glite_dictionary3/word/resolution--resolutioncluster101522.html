resolution

noun
analysis into distinct components
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzəˈluʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛzəˈluːʃən / (BrE)
The process of analyzing or separating something into clear, distinct components; the act of breaking down a complex entity into simpler parts for examination.

Examples

resolution of an equation
Mathematical resolution of the equation revealed its hidden variables.
The scientific team performed a resolution of the mixture to study its individual compounds.
Through careful resolution, the philosopher divided the argument into basic propositions.
