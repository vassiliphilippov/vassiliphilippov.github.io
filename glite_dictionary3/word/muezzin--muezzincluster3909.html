muezzin

noun
Muslim official who calls to prayer
Pronunciation: / muˈɛzɪn / (AmE), / mjuˈezɪn / (BrE)
a Muslim official in a mosque who calls the faithful to prayer from a minaret five times a day

Examples

muezzin calls prayer
Each dawn, the muezzin's voice echoed across the city.
The muezzin climbed the stairs of the minaret to call the faithful to prayer.
Tourists listened quietly to the evening call of the muezzin from the mosque.
