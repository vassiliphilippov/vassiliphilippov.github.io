disarmer

noun
person who disables weapons/explosives
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɑrmər / (AmE), / dɪˈzɑːmə / (BrE)
a person (such as a bomb technician) who disables, removes, or renders safe weapons or explosive devices.

Examples

bomb disarmer
The bomb squad sent an experienced disarmer to carefully examine the suspicious package before it exploded.
After hours of delicate work, the military disarmer successfully removed the detonator from the roadside bomb.
Several civilian disarmers trained in EOD techniques responded quickly to neutralize the device.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bomb technician: a trained specialist who handles and renders safe explosive devices
  • synonym – explosive ordnance disposal: the military or police process of detecting, identifying, and rendering safe explosive threats
