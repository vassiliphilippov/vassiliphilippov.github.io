enteral

adjective
relating to the intestines or alimentary canal
Pronunciation: / ˈɛn.tə.rəl / (AmE), / ˈɛn.tə.rəl /; / ˈɛn.tər.əl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring within the intestines, especially as a way to give food or medicine through the digestive tract (not by injection)
