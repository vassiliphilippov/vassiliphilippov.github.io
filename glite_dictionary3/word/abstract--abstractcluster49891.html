to abstract

verb
consider theoretically; remove from specifics
Pronunciation: / æbˈstrækt /; / əbˈstrækt / (AmE), / æbˈstrækt /; / əbˈstrækt / (BrE)
to think about something in a general or theoretical way, without focusing on specific examples

Examples
