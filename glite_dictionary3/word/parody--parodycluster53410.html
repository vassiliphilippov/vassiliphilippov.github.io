to parody

verb
imitate humorously to mock or satirize
Pronunciation: / ˈpærədi /; / ˈpɛr.ə.di / (AmE), / ˈparədi /; / ˈpær.ə.di / (BrE)
to imitate the style or content of someone or something in a humorous or satirical way, often by exaggerating certain features
