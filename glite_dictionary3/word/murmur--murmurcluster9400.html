murmur

noun
a quiet complaint or protest
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝː.mɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɜː.mə / (BrE)
a low and indistinct expression of complaint or discontent

Examples

a murmur of protest
Despite the harsh decision, no more than a quiet murmur was heard from the students.
She heard a faint murmur of protest from the back of the room.
There was a murmur of discontent among the employees after the new policy was announced.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – complaint: an expression of dissatisfaction or annoyance
  • synonym – grumble: a muted complaint or mutter, especially about something minor
  • degree lower – whisper: speak very softly using one's breath without vocal cords
