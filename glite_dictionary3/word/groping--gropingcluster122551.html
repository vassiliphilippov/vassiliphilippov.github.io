groping

adjective
uncertain or hesitant action
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊpɪŋ / (BrE)
acting in a way that shows uncertainty, hesitance, or lack of confidence, as if searching blindly for the right way

Examples

groping reply
Her groping answer showed she wasn't sure how to respond.
In his groping attempts to explain the idea, he revealed his uncertainty.
The scientist made a groping effort to understand the complex phenomenon.

Related Terms

  • antonym – confident: showing certainty and assurance
  • synonym – tentative: showing uncertainty or hesitation
