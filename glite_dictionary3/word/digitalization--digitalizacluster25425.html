digitalization

noun
process of converting to digital form
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪdʒɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪdʒɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
The process of converting information, activities, or systems into digital form and using digital technologies to improve or automate them.

Examples

digitalization of records
The corporation's digitalization strategy aims to digitize records and automate workflows.
Small museums relied on digitalization of catalogs so people could search collections online easily.
During the pandemic, schools accelerated digitalization to deliver lessons and materials remotely to students.

Related Terms

  • synonym – digital transformation: broader organisational change that uses digital technologies to improve performance or reach
  • synonym – digitization: the act of converting information into digital format (often used interchangeably with digitalization)
