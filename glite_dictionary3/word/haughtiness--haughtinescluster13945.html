haughtiness

noun
arrogant superiority; disdainfulness
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈhɔːtɪnəs / (BrE)
The quality of having or showing an attitude of superiority and disdain for people or things perceived to be inferior.

Examples

cold haughtiness
Despite her obvious haughtiness, she was insecure about her own achievements.
He spoke to the staff with a haughtiness that many found deeply offensive.
Her haughtiness made it difficult for her to form genuine friendships with her colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym – arrogance: an attitude of superiority shown in an overbearing manner
  • antonym – humility: a modest or low view of one's own importance
