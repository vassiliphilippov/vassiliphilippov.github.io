Baal

proper noun
ancient Semitic fertility deity
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑl / (AmE), / ˈbɑːl / (BrE)
A title or name for various local fertility and nature deities worshipped by ancient Semitic peoples, considered a false god by the Hebrews.

Baal worship rituals
