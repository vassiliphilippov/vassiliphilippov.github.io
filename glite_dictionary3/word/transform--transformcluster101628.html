to transform

verb
change in form, appearance, or nature
Pronunciation: / trænˈsfɔrm /; / trænsˈfɔrm / (AmE), / trænsˈfɔːm / (BrE)
to change something or someone in a significant or dramatic way, especially in form, appearance, or character
