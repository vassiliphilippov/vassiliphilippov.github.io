shuffleboard

noun
game where disks are pushed to score
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌfəlˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈʃʌf.əl.bɔːd / (BrE)
a game in which players use long sticks to push weighted disks along a smooth surface toward a scoring area

Examples

play shuffleboard with friends
He quickly mastered the rules of shuffleboard and became the club champion.
The retirement community organizes weekly shuffleboard tournaments for residents.
They spent the afternoon playing shuffleboard on the deck of the cruise ship.
