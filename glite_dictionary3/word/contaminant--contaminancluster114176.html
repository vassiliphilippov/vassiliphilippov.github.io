contaminant

noun
substance causing impurity or pollution
Pronunciation: / kənˈtæm.ə.nənt / (AmE), / kənˈtæm.ɪ.nənt / (BrE)
A substance that makes something impure or harmful by contact or mixture.

Examples

contaminant in water
A water filter can remove harmful contaminants from your drinking supply.
Scientists are testing for any contaminant in the soil after the chemical spill.
The presence of even a small contaminant can affect experimental results.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pollutant: a substance that pollutes, especially in the environment
