absorption

noun
uptake of nutrients after digestion
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɔrpʃən /; / əbˈzɔr.pʃən /; / əbˈzɔrpʃən, æbˈzɔrpʃən / (AmE), / əbˈzɔːpʃən /; / əbˈzɔːp.ʃən /; / əbˈzɔːpʃən, æbˈzɔːpʃən / (BrE)
the process by which one thing takes in or soaks up another (such as the body taking in nutrients, a material taking in energy, or a substance being taken up by another)
