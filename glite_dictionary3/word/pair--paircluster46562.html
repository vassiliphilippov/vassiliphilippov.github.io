to pair

verb
(of animals) mate or copulate
Pronunciation: / pɛr / (AmE), / peə / (BrE)
(of animals) to come together to mate or engage in sexual intercourse.

Examples

to pair animals
During the breeding season, wolves will pair within their pack.
Many birds pair for life and return to the same nesting spot each year.
Some fish species pair only briefly to spawn before separating again.
