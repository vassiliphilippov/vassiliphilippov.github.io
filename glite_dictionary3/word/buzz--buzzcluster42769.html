to buzz

verb
make a low, vibrating sound
Pronunciation: / bʌz / (AmE), / bʌz / (BrE)
to make a continuous, low, vibrating sound, like that produced by insects such as bees

Examples

to buzz like bees
Cicadas often buzz in the trees during hot summer afternoons.
His phone began to buzz on the table, signaling an incoming call.
The bees buzzed loudly as I approached the garden.

  • synonym – hum: make a low, steady continuous sound
