differentially

adverb
in a manner showing differences
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəli / (AmE), / ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a difference or distinction between things; in a manner dependent on differences

Examples

differentially expressed genes
Some genes are expressed differentially in response to environmental stresses.
The medication works differentially on individuals based on their genetic backgrounds.
The policy changes affected various groups differentially depending on their economic status.
