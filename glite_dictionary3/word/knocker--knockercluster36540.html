knocker

noun
metal object for knocking on doors
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑkər / (AmE), / ˈnɒkə / (BrE)
a metal object fixed to a door and used to knock for attention by lifting and striking it against the door

Examples

door knocker ring
A brass knocker shaped like a lion's head adorned the entrance.
She lifted the heavy knocker and rapped loudly on the old wooden door.
The sound of the knocker echoed through the empty hallway.
