to remake

verb
make new or different
Pronunciation: / riˈmeɪk / (AmE), / riˈmeɪk / (BrE)
to make something again or differently, especially so as to improve or renew it

Examples

to remake your life
He is trying to remake his career after a long break.
She decided to remake her wardrobe after moving to a new city.
The director plans to remake the film for a modern audience.
