flatfoot

noun
a foot with a fallen arch
Pronunciation: / ˈflætˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈflætˌfʊt / (BrE)
a foot with a fallen arch, causing the sole to be abnormally flat and spread out

Examples

painful flatfoot
Children with flatfoot may experience pain or fatigue after walking long distances.
Special insoles can help people manage the discomfort caused by flatfoot.
The doctor diagnosed him with flatfoot after examining the shape of his feet.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fallen arch: the condition of the arch of the foot collapsing
