codification

noun
process of systematizing laws or rules
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌkoʊ.də.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌkəʊdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌkɒd.ɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act or result of arranging laws, rules, or information into a systematic and organized code or system
