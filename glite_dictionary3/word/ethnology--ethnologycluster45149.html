ethnology

noun
study of human cultures and ethnic groups
Pronunciation: / ɛθˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ɛθˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study of the characteristics, origins, and relationships of different human cultures and ethnic groups
