click

noun
sound: suction stop consonant
Pronunciation: / klɪk / (AmE), / klɪk / (BrE)
A stop consonant sound produced by suction of air into the mouth, found in some languages such as Bantu.

Examples

Xhosa click sound
Children are often fascinated by the unusual click sounds in Xhosa words.
In some southern African languages, the click is used as a regular speech sound.
The linguistics professor played a recording of a dental click for the class.
