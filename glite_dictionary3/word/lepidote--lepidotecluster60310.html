lepidote

adjective
covered with small scales; rough to the touch
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛpɪˌdoʊt / (AmE), / ˈlɛpɪdəʊt / (BrE)
(of a surface, especially a plant) rough to the touch because it is covered with small scales or scurf.
