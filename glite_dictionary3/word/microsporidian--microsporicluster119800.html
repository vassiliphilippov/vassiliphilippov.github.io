microsporidian

noun
microscopic parasite of arthropods and fishes
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊspɔːˈrɪdiən / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊspəˈrɪdiən / (BrE)
a microscopic, spore-forming parasite that infects and destroys host cells, particularly in arthropods and fishes
