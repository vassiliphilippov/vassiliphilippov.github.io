microphotometer

noun
device measuring small-area density
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.kroʊ.foʊˈtɑː.mɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.krəʊ.fəˈtɒ.mɪ.tə / (BrE)
An instrument used to measure density variations in very small or microscopic areas, typically in photographic or microscopic materials.

Examples

microphotometer measures film
A technician adjusted the settings of the microphotometer for optimal scanning of the photographic plate.
Accurate measurements from the microphotometer revealed subtle changes in density across the specimen.
The laboratory uses a microphotometer to analyze the fine details in stained tissue samples.
