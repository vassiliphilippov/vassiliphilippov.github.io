saute

noun
dish of quickly fried food
Pronunciation: / sɑːˈteɪ / (AmE), / sɔːˈteɪ / (BrE)
a dish made by quickly frying small pieces of food in a small amount of oil or butter over high heat

Examples

chicken saute dish
She ordered the chicken saute for her main course.
The restaurant's vegetable saute is a popular vegetarian option.
This seafood saute is cooked with garlic and fresh herbs.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stir-fry: a dish of food quickly fried in a wok with a small amount of oil, often with vegetables and meat
