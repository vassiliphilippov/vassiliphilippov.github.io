ovule

noun
structure in seed plants containing the female germ cell
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.vjul /; / ˈəv.juːl / (AmE), / ˈɒv.juːl / (BrE)
a small structure in plants (and sometimes animals) that contains the female cell and can develop into a seed or egg after fertilization
