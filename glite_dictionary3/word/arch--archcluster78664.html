arch

noun
curved structure in the body, especially foot
Pronunciation: / ɑrtʃ / (AmE), / ɑːtʃ / (BrE)
a curved structure in the body that supports or encloses organs, especially the inner side of the foot

Examples

foot arch
A flat arch can sometimes lead to problems with posture or walking.
He developed pain in his arch after running a marathon.
The arch of her foot was unusually high, making standard shoes uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym – instep: the arched part of the top of the foot
