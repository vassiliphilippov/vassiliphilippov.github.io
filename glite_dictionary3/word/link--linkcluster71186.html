link

noun
unit of length, 1/100 of a chain
Pronunciation: / lɪŋk / (AmE), / lɪŋk / (BrE)
a unit of length equal to 1/100 of a surveyor's chain, commonly used in land measurement, equal to 7.92 inches (20.12 centimeters)

Examples

measured in links
A traditional Gunter's chain contains 100 links for easier calculation.
Surveyors once used the link as a practical unit of length.
The old map measures each lot in links rather than meters.
