stealthiness

noun
quality of being stealthy
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛlθi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈstɛlθɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being quiet and secretive in order to avoid being noticed

Examples

cat's stealthiness
Her stealthiness made her an excellent detective who could gather information unnoticed.
The stealthiness of the cat allowed it to surprise the unsuspecting bird.
The soldier moved with such stealthiness that no one heard him approach.
