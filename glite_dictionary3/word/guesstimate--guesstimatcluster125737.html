to guesstimate

verb
estimate roughly by guessing and calculating
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɛstɪˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɛstɪˌmeɪt / (BrE)
to make an estimate based on a mixture of guesswork and calculation, usually when precise information is unavailable

Examples

to guesstimate the cost
Can you guesstimate how long the repairs will take?
She quickly guesstimated the cost of the project.
We had to guesstimate the number of guests attending the event.

Related Terms

  • synonym – estimate: to judge or calculate an approximate value
