ghetto

noun
segregated environment due to bias
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɛt.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡɛt.əʊ / (BrE)
a segregated environment or way of living or working, caused by social bias or stereotyping, which confines a group of people

Examples

social ghetto barrier
Community leaders worked hard to break free from the social ghetto created by stereotypes.
She felt trapped in a ghetto of low expectations at her workplace.
The art world can sometimes become a ghetto for non-traditional artists.
