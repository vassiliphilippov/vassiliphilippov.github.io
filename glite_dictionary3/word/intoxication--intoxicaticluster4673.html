intoxication

noun
extreme excitement or elation
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌtɑksɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˌtɒksɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
A state of heightened excitement or elation, often experienced beyond normal bounds of self-control or moderation.

Examples

intoxication of success
In his memoir, he describes the intoxication of newfound freedom after years of restrictions.
She felt an overwhelming intoxication as she stepped on stage to accept the award.
The intoxication of victory made the team forget their earlier doubts.

Related Terms

  • synonym – exhilaration: a feeling of lively excitement and happiness
  • antonym – sobriety: the state of being sober or moderate; absence of excitement or excess
