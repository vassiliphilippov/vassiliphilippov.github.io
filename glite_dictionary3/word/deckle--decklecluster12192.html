deckle

noun
rough edge of handmade or imitation paper
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛkəl / (AmE), / ˈdɛkəl / (BrE)
the rough, feathered edge left on handmade paper, or artificially produced on machine-made paper to imitate this effect

deckle edge paper
Collectors often value the deckle on limited edition books as a mark of authenticity.
