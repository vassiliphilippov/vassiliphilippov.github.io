pivot

noun
axis or shaft enabling rotation
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪvət / (AmE), / ˈpɪvət / (BrE)
a short shaft or pin on which something turns or oscillates, serving as an axis of rotation

Examples

pivot pin
He oiled the pivot to ensure the wheel would spin smoothly.
The clock's hands are attached to a small pivot in the center.
The entire mechanism rotates around a central pivot.
