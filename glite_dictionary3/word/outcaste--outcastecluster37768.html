outcaste

noun
person outside the caste system
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌkæst / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌkɑːst / (BrE)
A person who does not belong to any caste, especially one who has been excluded from the traditional caste system, often facing social ostracism.

Examples

an outcaste person
Being labeled an outcaste brought immense hardship to her and her family.
He was treated as an outcaste after breaking the village traditions.
Throughout history, outcastes have often been forced to live on the outskirts of society.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pariah: an outcast; someone despised or avoided by others
