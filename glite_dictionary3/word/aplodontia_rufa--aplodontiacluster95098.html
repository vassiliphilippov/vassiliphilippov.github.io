Aplodontia rufa

noun
a burrowing rodent of the Pacific coast (mountain beaver)
Pronunciation: / ˌæp.ləˈdɒn.ʃə ˈruː.fə / (AmE), / ˌæp.ləˈdɒn.tɪ.ə ˈruː.fə / (BrE)
a bulky, nocturnal, burrowing rodent found in upland areas along the Pacific coast of North America; the most primitive living rodent, also known as the mountain beaver
