erasable

adjective
able to be erased or removed
Pronunciation: / ɪˈreɪsəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪˈreɪsəbl̩ / (BrE)
Able to be erased or removed, especially from a surface or record.

Examples

erasable pen marks
Please write your answers in pencil so they are erasable.
The whiteboard is designed to be erasable for easy reuse.
This marker is not erasable, so avoid using it on the board.
