panfish

noun
small edible fish for recreational fishing
Pronunciation: / ˈpænˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpænˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
Any of various small edible fish, especially those caught by anglers and not commonly sold commercially.

Examples

Many anglers enjoy frying up fresh panfish for a simple meal.
The region's lakes are home to a variety of panfish, including bluegill and crappie.
