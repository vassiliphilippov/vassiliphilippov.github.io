Franz Kafka

proper noun
Czech novelist and writer of existential fiction
Pronunciation: / frænz ˈkɑfə / (AmE), / frants ˈkafkə / (BrE)
(1883–1924) A Czech novelist and short story writer who wrote in German, known for works depicting surreal, nightmarish situations and isolated individuals.
