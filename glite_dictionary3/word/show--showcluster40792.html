to show

verb
depict or represent visually
Pronunciation: / ʃoʊ / (AmE), / ʃəʊ / (BrE)
to depict or represent (someone or something) in a picture, photograph, or other visual medium

Examples

show a picture
In the photograph, the artist shows the mountain landscape bathed in morning light.
The mural shows historical scenes from the town's past.
The painting shows a group of villagers celebrating the harvest festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym – depict: to represent or show something in a picture or story
  • synonym – portray: to represent or describe in a painting, drawing, or other art form
