to apotheosise

verb
to glorify or deify someone or something
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑːθioʊˌsaɪz / (AmE), / əˈpɒθɪəsaɪz / (BrE)
To elevate someone or something to the status of a god; to glorify or idealize greatly.

Examples

to apotheosise a hero
Many fans apotheosise their favorite athletes, treating them as larger-than-life figures.
Some leaders are apotheosised in history books, despite their flaws.
The artist was apotheosised by critics after her groundbreaking exhibition.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deify: to make someone or something into a god; to treat as a god
  • antonym – demonize: to portray as wicked or evil, the opposite of making divine
