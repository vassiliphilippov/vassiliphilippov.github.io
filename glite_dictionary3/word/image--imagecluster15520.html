to image

verb
to produce a visual representation (e.g., with MRI)
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
to produce a visual representation of something, especially using medical or scientific imaging techniques such as MRI or X-ray.

Examples

to image the brain
Modern hospitals are equipped to image every part of the body noninvasively.
Technicians imaged the knee joint to determine the extent of the injury.
The doctor decided to image the patient's brain using MRI to check for abnormalities.

  • synonym – scan: to examine the body using a medical imaging device
