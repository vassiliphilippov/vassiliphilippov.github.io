dowerless

adjective
without a dowry
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊ.ɚ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈdaʊ.ə.ləs / (BrE)
(of a woman) having no dowry to bring to marriage.

Examples

dowerless bride
Because she was dowerless, she relied on her talents and wit to secure her future.
Her parents lamented that she was dowerless and unlikely to catch a wealthy suitor.
In many old novels, a dowerless girl faces limited marriage prospects.

  • synonym – dowry: property or money brought by a bride to her husband on marriage
