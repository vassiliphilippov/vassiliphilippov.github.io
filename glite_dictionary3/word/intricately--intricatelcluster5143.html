intricately

adverb
in a detailed or complex manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.trɪ.kət.li / (AmE), / ˈɪn.trɪ.kət.li / (BrE)

intricately decorated fabric

The movie's plot was intricately woven, requiring viewers to pay close attention.
