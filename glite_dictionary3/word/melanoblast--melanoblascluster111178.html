melanoblast

noun
precursor cell of a melanocyte
Pronunciation: / mɛˈlænəˌblæst / (AmE), / mɪˈlanəˌblɑːst / (BrE)
a type of cell in the developing embryo that gives rise to a melanocyte, which produces melanin pigment in the skin
