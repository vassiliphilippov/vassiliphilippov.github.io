Eragrostis tef

noun
an African cereal grass, source of teff grain
Pronunciation: / ɛrəˈɡrɑstɪs tɛf / (AmE), / ɛrəˈɡrɒstɪs tɛf / (BrE)
a species of grass native to Ethiopia, cultivated for its small edible seeds (teff), which are used as a cereal grain and for forage
