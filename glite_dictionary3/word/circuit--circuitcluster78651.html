circuit

noun
journey or route around a place
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.kɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.kɪt / (BrE)
a journey or route that goes all the way around a place and returns to the starting point; or one complete lap around a course

Examples

circuit of the island
On our vacation, we took a guided circuit of the island's historical sites.
The bike ride included a challenging circuit around the lake.
They completed a full circuit of the city before stopping for lunch.
Each runner completed three circuits of the track during the warm-up.
For this workout, do one circuit of the exercises without resting in between.
The final circuit of the Formula 1 race was the most exciting.

Related Terms

  • antonym – detour: a longer route that is taken to avoid a problem or to visit somewhere along the way
  • synonym – excursion: a short journey or trip, especially one taken for leisure
  • synonym – lap: a single trip around a race track or course
  • synonym – tour: a journey for pleasure in which several different places are visited
