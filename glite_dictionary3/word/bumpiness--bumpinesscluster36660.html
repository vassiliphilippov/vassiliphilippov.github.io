bumpiness

noun
roughness due to many bumps
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌmpi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈbʌm.pi.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality or condition of having a rough or uneven surface marked by many bumps.

Examples

bumpiness of the road
She ran her hand across the wall, noticing its bumpiness.
The bumpiness of the cake's surface showed it had not been properly smoothed.
The bumpiness of the road made the ride uncomfortable.

  • antonym – smoothness: the quality of having an even or flat surface
