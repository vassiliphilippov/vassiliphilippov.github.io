Fissipedia

noun
former suborder of Carnivora (zoology)
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪsɪˈpiːdiə / (AmE), / ˌfɪsɪˈpiːdɪə / (BrE)
a former suborder of Carnivora comprising carnivorous mammals with separated toes, such as cats, dogs, and bears

Examples

Fissipedia mammals
In older texts, otters were sometimes grouped under Fissipedia rather than Pinnipedia.
Some taxonomists distinguished between Fissipedia and Pinnipedia within the order Carnivora.
The classification Fissipedia is now considered outdated in modern zoology.
