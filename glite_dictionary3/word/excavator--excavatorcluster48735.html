excavator

noun
person who excavates earth or rock
Pronunciation: / ˈɛk.səˌveɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛk.səˌveɪ.tə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to dig out earth, rock, or other material, especially for foundations or quarrying.

Examples

excavator uncovers ruins
Each excavator must be trained to handle the massive machinery used at the quarry.
The excavator spent hours digging the foundation for the new building.
The site manager consulted the excavator about potential obstacles in the soil.
