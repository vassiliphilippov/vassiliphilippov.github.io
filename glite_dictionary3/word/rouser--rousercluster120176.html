rouser

noun
one who wakes or stirs others
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊzər / (AmE), / ˈraʊzə / (BrE)
a person who wakes others from sleep or stirs them to activity

Examples

rouser of sleepers
He acted as the unofficial rouser for his sleeping friends on the hiking trip.
My grandmother was always an early rouser, making sure we never missed the sunrise.
The camp designated one rouser each morning to wake everyone for breakfast.
