Aeolian

adjective
relating to the wind or Aeolus
Pronunciation: / iˈoʊliən / (AmE), / iˈəʊliən / (BrE)
relating to or caused by the wind; of or pertaining to Aeolus, the Greek god of the winds.

Examples

Aeolian sand dunes
Many deserts are dominated by Aeolian features created by persistent winds.

The Aeolian processes shaped the dunes over thousands of years.
