handle

noun
part of an object for holding
Pronunciation: / ˈhændl̩ / (AmE), / ˈhændl / (BrE)
the part of an object designed to be held by the hand so that the object can be used or moved easily

Examples

handle of a cup
Be careful not to break the handle on that old teapot.
She lifted the basket by its handle and carried it across the room.
The mug slipped out of his hand because the handle was wet.

Related Terms

  • synonym – grip: a part of an object to be grasped with the hand
