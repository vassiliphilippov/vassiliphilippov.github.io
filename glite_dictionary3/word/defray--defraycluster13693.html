to defray

verb
to pay for costs or expenses
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfreɪ / (AmE), / dɪˈfreɪ / (BrE)
to pay or provide the money for (costs, expenses, etc.)

Examples

to defray the costs
A scholarship helped defray the costs of her education.
The company agreed to defray all travel expenses for the conference.
The government will defray part of the repair bill for homeowners after the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cover: to provide for the cost of something
  • synonym – pay: to give money for goods or services
