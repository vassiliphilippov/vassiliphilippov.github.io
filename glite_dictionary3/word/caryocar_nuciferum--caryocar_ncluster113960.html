Caryocar nuciferum

noun
South American evergreen tree; edible seeds
Pronunciation: / ˌkɛr.i.oʊˌkɑr nuːˈsɪf.ərəm / (AmE), / ˌkæ.rɪ.əʊˌkɑː njuːˈsɪf.ərəm / (BrE)
A large evergreen tree native to South America, with trifoliate leaves and edible nutlike seeds used as food and for cooking oil.
