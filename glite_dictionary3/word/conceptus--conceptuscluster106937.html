conceptus

noun
organism in early embryonic development
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɛp.təs / (AmE), / kənˈsɛp.təs / (BrE)
an animal organism in the early stages of development, including the embryo and its associated membranes, prior to reaching the fetal stage or, in lower forms, before becoming a larva

Examples

developing conceptus
Abnormalities in the conceptus can sometimes lead to pregnancy complications.
Researchers study the conceptus to understand early stages of mammalian development.
The ultrasound revealed the presence of a single conceptus in the uterus.
