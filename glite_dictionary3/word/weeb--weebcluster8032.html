weeb

noun
non-Japanese obsessed with Japanese culture (slang)
Pronunciation: / wib / (AmE), / wiːb / (BrE)
(slang, often derogatory) A non-Japanese person, especially a white person, who is obsessed with Japanese culture, particularly anime and manga.

Examples

anime weeb
Although she enjoys manga, she doesn't consider herself a weeb.
He collects anime figures and watches Japanese shows all day; some call him a weeb.
Online forums are full of heated arguments about who is a real weeb and who isn't.
