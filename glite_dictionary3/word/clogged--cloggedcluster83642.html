clogged

adjective
thickened or stuck together in lumps
Pronunciation: / klɑgd / (AmE), / klɒgd / (BrE)
(of a substance) thickened or stuck together in soft, thick lumps; coalesced into a mass or clot.

Examples

clogged gravy
After standing for hours, the gravy was clogged and unappetizing.
The drain became clogged with wet leaves after the storm.
The printer jammed because the ink nozzles were clogged with dried pigment.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clotted: having formed into clots or lumps
