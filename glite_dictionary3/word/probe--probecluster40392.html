to probe

verb
to examine physically or investigate
Pronunciation: / proʊb / (AmE), / prəʊb / (BrE)
to examine or explore something physically, especially with an instrument or device, or in a thorough, investigative manner

Examples

to probe a wound
Scientists probed the ocean floor for signs of volcanic activity.
She probed into the mystery, searching for any hidden clues.
The doctor carefully probed the wound to assess its depth.

Related Terms

  • synonym – explore: to investigate or examine systematically
  • synonym – scan: to examine something closely or systematically
