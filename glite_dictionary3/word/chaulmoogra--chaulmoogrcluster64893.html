chaulmoogra

noun
East Indian tree, source of medicinal oil
Pronunciation: / tʃɔlˈmuːɡrə / (AmE), / tʃɔːlˈmuːɡrə / (BrE)
An East Indian tree (Hydnocarpus species) whose oily seeds produce chaulmoogra oil, historically used to treat leprosy and other skin diseases.
