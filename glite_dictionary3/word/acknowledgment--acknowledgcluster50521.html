acknowledgment

noun
statement of recognition or thanks
Pronunciation: / əkˈnɑlɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / əkˈnɒlɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
a statement or action that shows recognition of something or someone, especially by expressing thanks or appreciation

Examples

acknowledgment of help
His polite nod was a brief acknowledgment of her presence in the room.
She included a heartfelt acknowledgment to her mentors in the book's introduction.
The project would not have succeeded without an acknowledgment of everyone's hard work.

Related Terms

  • antonym – denial: refusal to acknowledge or recognize something
  • synonym – recognition: acceptance or acknowledgment of the existence or validity of something
