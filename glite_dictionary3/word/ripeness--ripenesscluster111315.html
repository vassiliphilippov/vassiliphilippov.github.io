ripeness

noun
state of being ripe or mature
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪpnəs / (AmE), / ˈraɪpnɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being ripe, especially fully mature or developed and ready for use, consumption, or harvest

Examples

ripeness of fruit
Chefs often judge fruit by its color and ripeness before preparing dishes.
Grapes reach their optimum ripeness in late summer under warm sunshine.
The ripeness of the berries determines the best time for harvesting.
