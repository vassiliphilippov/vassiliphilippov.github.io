bas bleu

noun
woman with literary or intellectual interests
Pronunciation: / bɑ bleɪ / (AmE), / bɑː bløː / (BrE)
A woman with literary or intellectual interests, especially one belonging to a literary circle; a bluestocking.

Examples

a bas bleu writer
In Victorian England, a bas bleu might be both admired and criticized for her passion for learning.
She was considered something of a bas bleu in her small town for her extensive reading and poetry.
The literary club, once mocked as a gathering of bas bleus, became highly respected over time.
