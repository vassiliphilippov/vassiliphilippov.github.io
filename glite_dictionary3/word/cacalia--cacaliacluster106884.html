Cacalia

noun
genus of tall mountain herbs
Pronunciation: / kəˈkeɪliə / (AmE), / kəˈkeɪlɪə / (BrE)
A genus of tall, smooth herbs found in forested mountainous regions of Europe and Asia Minor, sometimes including species usually classified in the genus Emilia.
