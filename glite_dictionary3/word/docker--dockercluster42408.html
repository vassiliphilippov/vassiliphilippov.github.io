docker

noun
port laborer loading/unloading ships
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːkər / (AmE), / ˈdɒkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to load and unload ships at a port

Examples

docker at the port
Many dockers went on strike to demand better working conditions at the harbor.
My grandfather worked as a docker in Liverpool for most of his life.
The docker finished unloading the cargo before the rain began to fall.

Related Terms

  • synonym – longshoreman: a person who works on the docks loading and unloading ships, especially in the US
  • synonym – stevedore: a person employed at a dock to load and unload cargo from ships
