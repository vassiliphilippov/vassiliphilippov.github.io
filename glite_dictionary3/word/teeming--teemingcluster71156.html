teeming

adjective
abundantly filled or swarming
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtiːmɪŋ / (BrE)
abundantly full or swarming, especially with living things such as people or animals.

Examples

teeming with life
After the rain, the garden was teeming with birds and insects.
The documentary showed the teeming life of the coral reef.
The narrow streets were teeming with vendors and shoppers during the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym – crowded: full of people or things, packed
  • antonym – empty: having nothing or no people inside
