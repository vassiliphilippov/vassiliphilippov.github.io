deliberate

adjective
carefully thought out and intentional
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪbərət / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪbərət / (BrE)
done consciously and intentionally; planned or considered in advance

Examples

She made a deliberate decision to confront her boss about the issue.
