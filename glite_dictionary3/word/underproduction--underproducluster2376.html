underproduction

noun
inadequate or insufficient production
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɚpɹəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌʌndəpɹəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
The production of less than is required or expected.

Examples

underproduction in factories
Persistent underproduction in agriculture can lead to higher food prices.
The company is addressing underproduction by upgrading its machinery and hiring more staff.
The factory suffered from underproduction due to a shortage of raw materials.
