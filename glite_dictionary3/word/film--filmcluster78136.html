film

noun
photographic material for making images
Pronunciation: / fɪlm / (AmE), / fɪlm / (BrE)
photographic material consisting of a thin strip coated with a light-sensitive emulsion, used for making photographs, negatives, or transparencies

Examples

roll of film
Digital photography has largely replaced traditional film in recent years.
I found an old roll of film in my grandfather's camera bag.
Make sure the film is loaded properly before you start taking pictures.
