to approach

verb
come close in quality or character
Pronunciation: / əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
to be similar to something or to come close in quality, character, or amount

Examples

to approach perfection
As technology advances, artificial intelligence approaches human-like performance in many areas.
Her writing style approaches that of classic novelists in its elegance and depth.
The new model approaches perfection but still has a few minor flaws.

Related Terms

  • antonym – differ: to be unlike or dissimilar
  • synonym – resemble: to be like or similar to something
