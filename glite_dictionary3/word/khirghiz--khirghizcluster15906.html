Khirghiz

noun
member of a Central Asian ethnic group
Pronunciation: / kɪrˈɡiːz / (AmE), / kɪəˈɡiːz / (BrE)
A member of a Turkic ethnic group traditionally inhabiting vast regions of Central Asia and Siberia, especially Kyrgyzstan.
