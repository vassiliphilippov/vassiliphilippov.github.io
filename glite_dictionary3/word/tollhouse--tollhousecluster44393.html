tollhouse

noun
booth for collecting tolls
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊlˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈtəʊlˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A small building or booth next to a tollgate where tolls are collected.

Examples

Restoration efforts have preserved the stone tollhouse beside the village gate.
The old tollhouse now serves as a museum about the history of the road.
