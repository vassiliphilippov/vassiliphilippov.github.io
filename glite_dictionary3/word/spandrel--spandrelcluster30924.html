spandrel

noun
triangular area between arches and the plane above
Pronunciation: / ˈspæn.drəl / (AmE), / ˈspæn.drəl / (BrE)
an approximately triangular space between the side curves of two adjoining arches and the horizontal area above them

Examples

spandrel between arches
Each spandrel was decorated with a different mosaic pattern depicting scenes from history.
Restorers carefully repaired the cracks in each spandrel during the renovation.
The ornate designs in the spandrel of the cathedral drew the eyes upward.
