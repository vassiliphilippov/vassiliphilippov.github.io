delinquent

adjective
overdue; unpaid debt or account
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪŋkwənt /; / dəˈlɪŋkwənt / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪŋkwənt / (BrE)
not paying debts or failing to do something you are responsible for

Examples

delinquent account payment

It was delinquent of her not to respond to important work emails.
