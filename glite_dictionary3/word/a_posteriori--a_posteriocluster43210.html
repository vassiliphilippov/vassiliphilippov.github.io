a posteriori

adjective, adverb
based on observed facts or experience
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ pɑːˌstɪəriˈɔːraɪ /; / ˌeɪ pəˌstɪriˈɔri / (AmE), / ˌeɪ pɒˌstɪəriˈɔːraɪ /; / ˌeɪ pɒˌstɪəriˈɔːri / (BrE)
based on or derived from actual observation or experience, rather than from theory or pure logic (used as an adjective or adverb)
