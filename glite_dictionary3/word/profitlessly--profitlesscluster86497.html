profitlessly

adverb
without profit or gain
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːfɪtlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈprɒfɪtlɪsli / (BrE)
In a way that brings no profit or gain; without yielding advantage or benefit.

Examples

search profitlessly
He searched profitlessly for any evidence to support his claim.
The company toiled profitlessly for years before finally turning a profit.
They invested profitlessly in several schemes that yielded no returns.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fruitlessly: in a way that produces no result or success
  • antonym – profitably: in a way that brings profit or gain
