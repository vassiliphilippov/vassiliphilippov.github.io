Sequoia gigantea

noun
towering Californian conifer; giant sequoia
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkwɔɪ.ə dʒaɪˈɡæn.ti.ə / (AmE), / sɪˈkwɔɪ.ə dʒɪˈɡæn.ti.ə / (BrE)
