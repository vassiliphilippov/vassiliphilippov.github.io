finger

noun
a unit based on a finger's width or length
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
a unit of linear measurement based on the width or length of a finger, used especially in older or literary contexts

Examples

finger width measure
He measured the depth of water by the simple method of using his finger as a guide.
The old carpenter estimated the gap to be about a finger wide.
The recipe calls for a finger of ginger, roughly the width of your own.
