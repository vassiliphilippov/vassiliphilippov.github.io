compatibly

adverb
in a compatible manner
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpæt̬.ə.bli / (AmE), / kəmˈpæt.ə.bli / (BrE)
In a way that is consistent or can exist or work together without conflict.

Examples

work compatibly together
Different departments must function compatibly to achieve common goals.
The new software runs compatibly with earlier versions of the operating system.
We must ensure that environmental policies operate compatibly with economic growth.

Related Terms

  • antonym – incompatibly: in a way that cannot exist or work together without conflict
