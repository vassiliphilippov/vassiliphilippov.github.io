manoeuvrable

adjective
able to be easily steered or moved
Pronunciation: / məˈnuːvərəbl̩ / (AmE), / məˈnjuːvərəbl̩ / (BrE)
capable of being easily steered or moved into different positions

Examples

highly manoeuvrable car
A smaller car tends to be more manoeuvrable on narrow city streets.
Despite its size, the yacht remained remarkably manoeuvrable in the crowded harbor.
The new drone is highly manoeuvrable, allowing it to avoid obstacles easily.
