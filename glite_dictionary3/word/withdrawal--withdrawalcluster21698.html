withdrawal

noun
method of birth control by withdrawing before ejaculation
Pronunciation: / wɪðˈdrɔəl / (AmE), / wɪðˈdrɔːəl / (BrE)
A method of birth control in which the penis is withdrawn from the vagina before ejaculation to prevent pregnancy.

Examples

withdrawal method
Although withdrawal is a widely used method, it is less effective than other birth control options.
Some couples rely on withdrawal as their primary form of contraception.
The doctor discussed the risks associated with using withdrawal to prevent unwanted pregnancies.
