heedlessness

noun
lack of attention or care
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːdləsnəs /; / ˈhidl̩snɪs /; / ˈhidləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈhiːdləsnəs /; / ˈhiːdləsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of not paying attention or not being careful, especially when it may cause problems or danger
