swashbuckling

noun
adventurous, flamboyant daring behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑʃˌbʌklɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈswɒʃˌbʌklɪŋ / (BrE)
bold, adventurous, and often recklessly daring behavior, especially in a flamboyant or showy manner.

Examples

her swashbuckling adventures

He admired the swashbuckling of old-time explorers who braved the unknown.
The film is filled with swashbuckling, sword fights, and daring sea escapes.
