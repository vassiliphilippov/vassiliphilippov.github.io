vorticella

noun
freshwater protozoan with a stalk
Pronunciation: / vɔr.tɪˈsɛl.ə / (AmE), / vɔː.tɪˈsel.ə / (BrE)
A single-celled protozoan organism with a transparent, goblet-shaped body and a retractile stalk, typically found in freshwater habitats.
