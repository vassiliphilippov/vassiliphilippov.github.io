MPEG

noun
video and audio compression standard
Pronunciation: / ɛm piː dʒiː / (AmE), / ɛm piː dʒiː / (BrE)
a set of standards for compressing digital video and audio data, or a digital file encoded according to those standards.

Examples

MPEG video file
Most video streaming services use MPEG standards for efficient delivery.
She downloaded a movie in MPEG format to ensure high quality.
The presentation explained how MPEG compression reduces file size without major quality loss.

Related Terms

  • synonym – MP3: a popular audio file format using MPEG-1 Layer 3 compression
