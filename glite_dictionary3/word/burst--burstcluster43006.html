burst

noun
sudden intense occurrence
Pronunciation: / bɝst / (AmE), / bɜːst / (BrE)
a sudden, brief, and intense occurrence of something (such as activity, emotion, or sound)

Examples

She spoke calmly at first, then in a burst of anger, shouted at the crowd.
