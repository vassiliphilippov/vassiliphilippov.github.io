tweedy

adjective
resembling country gentry; outdoorsy or traditional
Pronunciation: / ˈtwidi / (AmE), / ˈtwiːdi / (BrE)
Having characteristics associated with country gentry, such as being informal, traditional, clannish, or outdoorsy, often suggesting a fondness for tweed clothing.

Examples

tweedy country style
His tweedy style made him stand out among the urban crowd.
She always wore tweedy jackets when she visited her cottage in the countryside.
The university had a distinctly tweedy atmosphere, filled with old traditions and autumn walks.
