chalybeate

adjective
containing iron (esp. of water)
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪbiˌeɪt / (AmE), / kəˈlɪbɪət / (BrE)
(of water or minerals) containing or impregnated with iron, especially giving a taste of iron.

Examples

chalybeate spring water
Scientists tested the chalybeate water for its iron content.
The chalybeate taste of the mineral water was immediately noticeable.
The town is famous for its chalybeate springs, believed to have health benefits.
