Squillidae

noun
family of marine burrowing crustaceans
Pronunciation: / skwɪˈlɪdiː / (AmE), / skwɪˈlɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of marine crustaceans (mantid shrimps) that burrow in mud or under stones in shallow water along seashores.
