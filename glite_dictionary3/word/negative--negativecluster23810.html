negative

adjective
expressing denial or refusal
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛɡətɪv / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡətɪv / (BrE)
expressing or indicating denial, refusal, or the absence of something; consisting in or characterized by negation.

Examples

negative response given
She gave a negative answer to his invitation, declining politely.
Sometimes a negative response can be just as helpful as a positive one.
The report contained several negative remarks about the company's performance.

Related Terms

  • antonym – affirmative: expressing agreement or positive statement
  • antonym – positive: expressing approval or confirmation; opposite of negative
