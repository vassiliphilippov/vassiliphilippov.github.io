pedigree

noun
ancestry of a purebred animal
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛd.ɪˌɡri /; / ˈpɛdɪˌɡri / (AmE), / ˈpɛd.ɪˌɡriː /; / ˈpɛdɪɡriː / (BrE)
the recorded ancestry or lineage of a person or animal, especially as proof of being purebred or distinguished
