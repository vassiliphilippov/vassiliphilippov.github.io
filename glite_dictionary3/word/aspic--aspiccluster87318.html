aspic

noun
savory jelly for encasing food
Pronunciation: / ˈæspɪk / (AmE), / ˈæspɪk / (BrE)
A savory jelly made from meat or fish stock, used as a mold to encase meats, seafood, eggs, or vegetables.

Examples

aspic with vegetables
