uncolumned

adjective
without columns (architecture)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkʌləmnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌləmnd / (BrE)
(of a structure or space) lacking columns; without columns as architectural supports or decorative elements.

Examples

uncolumned hall
Designers admired the modern, uncolumned facade of the building.
The uncolumned space made the room feel airy and expansive.
The open, uncolumned hall offered an uninterrupted view of the stage.
