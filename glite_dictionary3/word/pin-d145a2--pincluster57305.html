pin

noun
slang: leg (usually plural)
Pronunciation: / pɪn / (AmE), / pɪn / (BrE)
(informal) a leg, especially a person's leg; usually used in the plural to refer to both legs.

Examples

sore pins after running
After running the marathon, my pins were sore for days.
He lost his balance because his pins gave out from exhaustion.
She showed off her long pins in a new summer dress.

  • synonym – leg: the limb used for standing or walking
