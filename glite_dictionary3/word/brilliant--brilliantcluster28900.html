brilliant

adjective
intensely bright or strong in color
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪljənt / (AmE), / ˈbrɪljənt / (BrE)
(of a color) very bright, strong, or striking in appearance.

Examples

brilliant color
She wore a brilliant blue scarf that brightened up the whole room.
Sunlight made the tulip's petals appear even more brilliant against the green leaves.
The artist chose a brilliant red for the centerpiece of the painting.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dull: lacking brightness or intensity of color
  • synonym – vivid: (of color) intensely deep or bright
