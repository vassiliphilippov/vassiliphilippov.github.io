to shade

verb
represent shadows or shading in art
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪd / (AmE), / ʃeɪd / (BrE)
to represent the effect of shade or shadow on something, especially in drawing or art

Examples

to shade a drawing
In the drawing class, we learned how to shade faces to create depth.
She used a soft pencil to shade the background of her sketch.
The artist carefully shaded the apple to make it appear three-dimensional.
