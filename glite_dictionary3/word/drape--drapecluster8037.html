to drape

verb
arrange cloth or fabric in loose folds
Pronunciation: / dreɪp / (AmE), / dreɪp / (BrE)
to hang or arrange cloth, fabric, or something similar loosely over or around something, often for decoration or covering (including metaphorical uses)
