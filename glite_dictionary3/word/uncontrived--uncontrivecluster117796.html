uncontrived

adjective
not artificial or forced
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.kənˈtraɪvd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kənˈtraɪvd / (BrE)

Examples

She has an uncontrived charm that makes everyone feel comfortable around her.
The documentary has an uncontrived feel, capturing real moments without staging.
The laughter at the party was uncontrived and infectious, spreading from group to group.
