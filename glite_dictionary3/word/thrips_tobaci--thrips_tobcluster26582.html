Thrips tobaci

noun
a species of injurious thrips
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɪps toʊˈbeɪsaɪ / (AmE), / ˈθrɪps təʊˈbɑːsʌɪ / (BrE)
A species of thrips that is harmful to onion plants and sometimes tobacco.

Examples

Thrips tobaci damages onions
Experts recommend using integrated pest management to control Thrips tobaci.
Farmers must monitor their fields regularly to prevent Thrips tobaci infestations in their onion crops.
The damage caused by Thrips tobaci can greatly reduce the yield of both onions and tobacco.
