Lowlands

proper noun
southern, non-mountainous region of Scotland
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊˌlændz / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌlændz / (BrE)
an area in southern Scotland that is not mountainous, traditionally contrasted with the Highland region

Examples

the Lowlands of Scotland
After touring the Highlands, they traveled south into the Lowlands for a different landscape.
Many people from the Lowlands speak with a distinct Scottish accent.
The Lowlands have historically been the center of Scotland's most populous cities.
