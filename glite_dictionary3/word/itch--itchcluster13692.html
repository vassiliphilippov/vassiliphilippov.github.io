itch

noun
restless desire or longing
Pronunciation: / ɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɪtʃ / (BrE)

Examples

an itch for adventure
After years in the city, she felt an itch to explore the countryside.
His itch for adventure grew stronger every day he stayed at home.
When spring arrived, many people had an itch to travel abroad.
