diffuser

noun
device that disperses essential oils
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfjuːzər / (AmE), / dɪˈfjuːzə / (BrE)
A device that disperses essential oils or fragrances into the air as a fine mist to scent a room or for aromatherapy.

Examples

diffuser for oils
She turned on the diffuser to fill the room with calming lavender scent while she read.
At the spa the therapist used a ceramic diffuser to distribute essential oils during the massage.
Modern USB diffusers often have timers and LED lights for mood lighting and safety.
