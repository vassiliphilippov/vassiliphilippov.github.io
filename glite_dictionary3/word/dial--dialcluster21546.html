dial

noun
control knob for tuning devices
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəl / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəl / (BrE)
a control on a radio, television, or other device that is used to select stations, adjust settings, or tune frequencies by turning it

Examples

volume dial
Adjust the dial slowly until the picture becomes clearer.
She turned the dial to find her favorite radio station.
The old television set had a dial instead of buttons.
