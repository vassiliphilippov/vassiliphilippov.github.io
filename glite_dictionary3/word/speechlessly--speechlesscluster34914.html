speechlessly

adverb
without the ability to speak
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːtʃləsli / (AmE), / ˈspiːtʃləsli / (BrE)
in a way that shows one is unable to speak, typically due to shock, emotion, or weakness

Examples

react speechlessly
After the shocking news, he walked away speechlessly from the meeting.
She stared speechlessly at the unexpected gift, unable to find any words.
They listened speechlessly as the final verdict was delivered.
