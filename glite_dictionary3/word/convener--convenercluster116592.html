convener

noun
person who organizes meetings
Pronunciation: / kənˈvinər / (AmE), / kənˈviːnə / (BrE)
the person responsible for calling people together for meetings, especially of a committee or group

Examples

convener of a meeting
As convener, it is your responsibility to send out the agenda before each meeting.
She served as the convener of the planning committee for several years.
The convener called everyone to order and began the discussion promptly at nine.

  • synonym – chairperson: person who presides over a meeting
