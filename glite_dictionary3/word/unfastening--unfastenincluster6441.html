unfastening

noun
the act of loosening or undoing something
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfæstənɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɑːsnɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of loosening or undoing something that has been fastened or secured.

Examples

unfastening a button
A gentle unfastening of the ribbon revealed the gift inside.
She struggled with the unfastening of the stubborn laces on her old boots.
The unfastening of his seatbelt was the first sign that the flight was about to land.
