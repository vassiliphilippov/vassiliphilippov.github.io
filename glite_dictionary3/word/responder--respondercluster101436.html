responder

noun
person who replies or reacts
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɑndər / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒndə / (BrE)
a person who replies or reacts to something, such as a question, situation, or event

Examples

emergency responder
Emergency responders worked tirelessly through the night to help those in need.
The first responder arrived at the scene within minutes of the accident.
We analyzed the answers of each responder to the survey.
