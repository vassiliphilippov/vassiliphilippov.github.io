stockcar

noun
rail car for transporting livestock
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːkˌkɑːr / (AmE), / ˈstɒkˌkɑː / (BrE)
A railway freight car with latticed sides, designed for transporting livestock.

Examples

During transport, the animals were kept cool and ventilated in the stockcar.
