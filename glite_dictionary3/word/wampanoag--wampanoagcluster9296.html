Wampanoag

noun
Indigenous people of Rhode Island and Massachusetts
Pronunciation: / ˌwɑːmpəˈnoʊ.æɡ / (AmE), / ˌwɒmpəˈnəʊ.aɡ / (BrE)
A member of an Indigenous people of Rhode Island and Massachusetts, historically known for greeting the Pilgrims in the 17th century.
