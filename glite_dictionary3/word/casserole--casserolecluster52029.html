casserole

noun
baked dish of mixed ingredients
Pronunciation: / ˈkæs.əˌroʊl / (AmE), / ˈkæs.ər.əʊl / (BrE)
A dish consisting of meat, vegetables, or other ingredients baked together and served in a deep dish.

Examples

casserole with chicken
Leftover vegetables can easily be turned into a tasty casserole.
She prepared a delicious casserole of chicken and rice for dinner.
The restaurant is known for its hearty beef casserole during the winter months.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stew: dish of meat and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid
