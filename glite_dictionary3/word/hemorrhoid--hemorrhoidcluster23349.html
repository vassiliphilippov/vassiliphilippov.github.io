hemorrhoid

noun
swollen vein near the anus
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛm.əˌrɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈhɛm.ər.ɔɪd / (BrE)
A swollen or inflamed vein in the region of the anus, which may cause discomfort or bleeding.
