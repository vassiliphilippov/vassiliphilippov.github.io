weeknight

noun
a night from Monday to Friday
Pronunciation: / ˈwikˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈwiːk.naɪt / (BrE)
any night of the week except Saturday or Sunday; a night on which people normally have to work or go to school the next day

Examples

busy weeknight evening
I prefer to stay home on a weeknight and relax after work.
She doesn't like to have guests over on a weeknight because she has to wake up early.
The restaurant is much quieter on a weeknight than on weekends.
