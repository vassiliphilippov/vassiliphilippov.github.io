yeoman

noun
officer in British royal bodyguard
Pronunciation: / ˈjoʊmən / (AmE), / ˈjəʊmən / (BrE)
an officer in the ceremonial bodyguard of the British monarch, traditionally responsible for the security and ceremonial duties at royal events

Examples

royal yeoman guard
A yeoman stood guard at the entrance to the royal residence during the ceremony.
Each yeoman is selected for their distinguished service in the military.
Tourists enjoyed taking photos with the yeomen dressed in their historic uniforms.
