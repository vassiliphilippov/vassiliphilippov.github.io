hopscotch

noun
children's hopping game
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑpˌskɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɒpˌskɒtʃ / (BrE)
a children's game where players toss a small object onto numbered spaces drawn on the ground and hop through them to retrieve it
