alga

noun
aquatic, chlorophyll-containing organism
Pronunciation: / ˈælɡə / (AmE), / ˈælɡə / (BrE)
A simple, mainly aquatic organism that contains chlorophyll but lacks true stems, roots, and leaves, typically found in water or moist environments.
