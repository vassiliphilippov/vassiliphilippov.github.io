tepidness

noun
lack of enthusiasm or passion
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛpɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈtɛpɪdnɪs / (BrE)
A lack of enthusiasm, passion, or intensity.

Examples

tepidness of response
His tepidness toward the project disappointed his colleagues.
Many critics noted the tepidness of her response to the proposal.
Public tepidness about the reforms made change slow and difficult.

Related Terms

  • synonym – apathy: lack of interest or concern
  • antonym – enthusiasm: strong excitement or eagerness
  • synonym – lukewarmness: the quality of being only moderately warm or mildly interested
