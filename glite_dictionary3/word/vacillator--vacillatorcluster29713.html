vacillator

noun
person who hesitates or wavers
Pronunciation: / ˈvæsɪˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈvæsɪleɪtə / (BrE)
a person who is indecisive or frequently changes their opinions or decisions, often hesitating to act

Examples

an uncertain vacillator
As a known vacillator, he struggled to make even the smallest decisions promptly.
Instead of being decisive, she became a vacillator during moments of crisis.
The committee grew frustrated with the vacillator who could never commit to a plan.

Related Terms

  • antonym – decider: a person who makes decisions firmly and confidently
  • synonym – vacillate: to waver between opinions or actions; be indecisive
