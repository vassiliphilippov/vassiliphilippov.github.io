worrying

adjective
causing worry or anxiety
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜriɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwʌriɪŋ / (BrE)
causing anxiety, distress, or concern; making someone feel worried.

Examples

worrying news
It is worrying that so few people are taking health precautions.
She received some worrying news about her job yesterday.
The rising number of thefts is a worrying trend in the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • antonym – comforting: making someone feel less worried or anxious
  • synonym – disturbing: causing anxiety or unease
