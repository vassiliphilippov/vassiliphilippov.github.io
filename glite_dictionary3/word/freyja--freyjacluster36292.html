Freyja

proper noun
Norse goddess of love and fertility
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪ.jə / (AmE), / ˈfreɪ.jə / (BrE)
In Norse mythology, the goddess of love, beauty, and fertility, daughter of Njord and sister of Freyr.

Examples

Norse goddess Freyja
According to legend, Freyja rides a chariot drawn by two cats.
Many ancient Norse poems tell stories of Freyja and her magical necklace.
