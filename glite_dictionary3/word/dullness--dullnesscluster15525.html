dullness

noun
lack of sharpness or clarity of edge
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌlnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʌlnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being not sharp or lacking a clear edge or point.

Examples

dullness of a blade
Over time, the dullness of the scissors became frustrating when wrapping gifts.
She noticed the dullness of the blade after months of frequent use.
The dullness of the knife made it hard to cut the vegetables.

  • antonym – sharpness: the quality of having a fine, clear edge or point
