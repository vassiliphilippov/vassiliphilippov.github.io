assignment

noun
appointing someone to a non-elective position
Pronunciation: / əˈsaɪnmənt / (AmE), / əˈsaɪnmənt / (BrE)
the act of appointing someone to a position or post, especially one that is not filled by election

Examples

assignment to a position
Her assignment to the project manager position surprised many in the company.
The assignment of key positions was handled by the board without a vote.
The committee oversaw the assignment of directors to their new roles.
