sinusitis

noun
inflammation of a paranasal sinus
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪ.nəˈsaɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˌsaɪ.njuˈsaɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of one or more of the paranasal sinuses, often causing pain or pressure in the face.

Examples

sinusitis with pain
Chronic sinusitis can significantly affect a person's quality of life.
Many people develop sinusitis after catching a cold or suffering from allergies.
She visited her doctor because her sinusitis caused severe headaches and facial pressure.
