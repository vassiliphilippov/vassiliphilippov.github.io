gutless

adjective
lacking courage or vitality
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌt.ləs / (AmE), / ˈɡʌt.ləs / (BrE)
Lacking courage or determination; cowardly or lacking vitality.

Examples

a gutless person
He was too gutless to stand up to the bullies at school.
Many saw the politician's refusal to act as a gutless move.
The film's hero refused to be gutless in the face of danger.
