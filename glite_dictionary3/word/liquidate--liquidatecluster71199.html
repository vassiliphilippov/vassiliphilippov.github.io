to liquidate

verb
kill to eliminate a threat
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwəˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪdeɪt / (BrE)
to get rid of someone, especially a threat, by killing them

Examples

to liquidate an enemy
He was assigned to liquidate a threatening enemy operative.
The government secretly liquidated several opposition leaders.
The mafia decided to liquidate the witness before the trial could begin.

Related Terms

  • synonym – eliminate: remove or get rid of, especially by killing
  • antonym – spare: to refrain from killing or injuring
