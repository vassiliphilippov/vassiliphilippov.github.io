sensibility

noun
mental responsiveness and awareness
Pronunciation: / sɛn.səˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / sɛn.sɪˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the ability to respond emotionally or intellectually to something; mental responsiveness and awareness.

Examples

artistic sensibility
Her refined artistic sensibility set her work apart from others in her field.
The novel demonstrates the author's remarkable sensibility to cultural differences.
Traveling broadened his sensibility and made him more open-minded.

Related Terms

  • antonym – insensitivity: lack of responsiveness to emotional or intellectual impressions
  • synonym – sensitivity: the quality of being sensitive to sensations or impressions; responsiveness
