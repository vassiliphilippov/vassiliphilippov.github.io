qualitatively

adverb
in terms of quality or nature
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑː.lɪˌteɪ.tɪv.li / (AmE), / ˈkwɒ.lɪˌteɪ.tɪv.li / (BrE)
In a manner that relates to the quality or nature of something, rather than its quantity.

Examples

qualitatively different results
Participants were asked to describe their experiences qualitatively rather than providing numerical ratings.
The results were analyzed qualitatively to understand the underlying patterns.
The two treatments differ qualitatively, not just in terms of degree but of fundamental nature.

Related Terms

  • antonym – quantitatively: in a quantitative manner; relating to quantity or amount
