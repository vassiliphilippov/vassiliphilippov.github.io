solitude

noun
a solitary or secluded place
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑː.lə.tud / (AmE), / ˈsɒl.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
A place where one is alone or isolated from others; a solitary or secluded spot.

Examples

solitude of the forest
He found peace in the solitude of the forest.
She escaped to a solitude far from the city to collect her thoughts.
The old monastery stands in complete solitude on the mountain ridge.

Related Terms

  • antonym – crowd: a large number of people together
  • synonym – seclusion: the state of being private and away from other people
