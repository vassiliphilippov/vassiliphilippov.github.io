Siegfried

proper noun
hero of the German legend Nibelungenlied
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːɡˌfriːd / (AmE), / ˈsiːɡfriːd / (BrE)
A legendary German warrior hero of the Nibelungenlied, known for slaying a dragon, claiming the Nibelungs' treasure, awakening Brynhild, and meeting a tragic end.
