halter

noun
headgear for leading a horse
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔltər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːltə / (BrE)
A rope or strap placed around the head of a horse or other animal, used for leading or tying it.

Examples

halter for horse
She struggled to put the halter on the restless foal.
The groom fitted the horse with a new halter before leading it out of the stable.
The old leather halter was worn but still sturdy enough to use.
