spread

noun
soft food mixture for bread, etc.
Pronunciation: / sprɛd / (AmE), / sprɛd / (BrE)
a soft mixture, such as butter, jam, or cheese, that is put on bread, crackers, or similar foods, or used as an ingredient in other dishes

Examples

spread for toast
Peanut butter is a popular spread for sandwiches.
She served several types of spread at the party, including hummus and olive tapenade.
This cream cheese spread goes perfectly with bagels for breakfast.
