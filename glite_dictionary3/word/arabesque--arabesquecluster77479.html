arabesque

noun
ballet position with one leg raised behind
Pronunciation: / ˌærəˈbɛsk / (AmE), / ˌærəˈbɛsk / (BrE)
a ballet position in which one leg is extended horizontally backward while the arms are outstretched, creating a conventional pose

Examples

ballet arabesque pose
Many ballet students struggle to achieve a balanced arabesque on pointe.
She moved gracefully into an arabesque at the end of her solo.
The teacher corrected his arabesque, insisting he lift his leg higher.
