diffusor

noun
device that diffuses sound waves
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfjuːsər /; / dɪˈfjuzər / (AmE), / dɪˈfjuːsə /; / dɪˈfjuːzə / (BrE)
a device that spreads sound or light evenly in a space to improve quality

Examples

diffusor for sound
Architects used a wooden diffusor to create a balanced sound environment in the auditorium.
