decal

noun
a design for transfer onto another surface
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːˌkæl / (AmE), / ˈdiːkæl / (BrE)
A design prepared on special paper for transfer to another surface, such as glass, porcelain, or metal.

Examples

decal on a window
After cleaning the window, he carefully transferred the decal to the glass.
She placed a decal of a butterfly on her laptop for decoration.
The model car kit came with several decals to apply for extra detail.
