legging

noun
close-fitting garment for the leg
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡɪŋ / (BrE)
a close-fitting garment covering the leg, usually extending from the knee to the ankle

Examples

legging for protection
He wore a single legging to protect his injured leg during the hike.
The knight strapped on a leather legging before riding into battle.
When the weather turned cold, she added a thick woolen legging over her stockings.

Related Terms

  • synonym – leggings: tight-fitting stretch pants covering the legs, usually worn by women
