escort

noun
act of accompanying for protection
Pronunciation: / ˈɛsˌkɔrt /; / ˈɛskɔrt / (AmE), / ˈɛskɔːt / (BrE)
a person or group who goes with someone to protect, assist, or guide them, or the act of doing this
