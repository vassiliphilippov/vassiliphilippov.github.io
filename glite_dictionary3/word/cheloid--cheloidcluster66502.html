cheloid

noun
raised scar tissue from excessive healing
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːlɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈkiːlɔɪd / (BrE)
a raised and thickened area of pinkish scar tissue that forms at the site of an injury due to excessive tissue repair
