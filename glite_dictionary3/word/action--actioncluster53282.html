to action

verb
implement; put into effect
Pronunciation: / ˈækʃən / (AmE), / ˈækʃən / (BrE)
to put into effect; to carry out or implement (a decision, plan, etc.)

Examples

to action a plan
All recommendations from the meeting will be actioned by next week.
Please ensure that these changes are actioned without delay.
The manager actioned the requests as soon as he received them.

Related Terms

  • synonym – execute: to carry out or perform a plan, order, or course of action
  • synonym – implement: to put a plan or decision into effect
