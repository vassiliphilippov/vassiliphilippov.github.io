to arc

verb
form a curve or arch
Pronunciation: / ɑrk / (AmE), / ɑːk / (BrE)
to move or form in a curved shape or path; to make or become arched

Examples

to arc over water
Lightning sometimes arcs across the sky during fierce storms.
She watched the basketball arc toward the hoop and drop in.
The bird's wings arc gracefully as it glides through the air.

Related Terms

  • synonym – arch: to form or cause to form an arch, to curve
  • antonym – straighten: to make or become straight, not curved
