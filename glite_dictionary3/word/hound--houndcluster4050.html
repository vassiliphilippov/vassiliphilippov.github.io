to hound

verb
to pursue relentlessly
Pronunciation: / haʊnd / (AmE), / haʊnd / (BrE)
to pursue or harass someone persistently and relentlessly

Examples

to hound a person
She felt hounded by debt collectors day and night.
The paparazzi continued to hound the actor despite requests for privacy.
The police were accused of hounding the innocent man for years.

Related Terms

  • synonym – harass: to subject someone to repeated or persistent attacks or pressure
  • antonym – leave alone: to stop bothering or pursuing someone
  • synonym – persecute: to treat someone cruelly and unfairly, especially because of their beliefs
