sortie

noun
military aircraft mission
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔrti / (AmE), / ˈsɔːti / (BrE)
(military) A single operational flight made by one aircraft during a mission or military operation.

Examples

combat sortie
During the conflict, each pilot averaged two sorties per day.
The fighter jet completed its sortie over enemy territory without encountering resistance.
Weather conditions forced the cancellation of several scheduled sorties yesterday.
