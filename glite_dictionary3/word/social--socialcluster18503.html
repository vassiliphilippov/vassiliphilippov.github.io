social

adjective
involving companionship with others
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.ʃəl /; / ˈsoʊʃəl / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃəl /; / ˈsəʊʃəl / (BrE)
(of activities, events, or places) involving friendly gatherings or interaction with others

Examples

social gathering

The hotel offers several social spaces where guests can mingle and relax.
They renovated the old church into a social hall for community events.
