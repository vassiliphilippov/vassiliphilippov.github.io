intermediator

noun
mediator or link between parties
Pronunciation: / ɪn.tɚˈmiː.di.eɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ɪn.təˈmiː.di.eɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person who negotiates or acts as a link between different parties to help them reach an agreement

Examples

intermediator in negotiations
During the talks, an experienced intermediator helped bridge the gap between the two sides.
In international disputes, an intermediator often plays a crucial role in achieving peace.
The company hired an intermediator to handle negotiations with the union.

Related Terms

  • synonym – intermediary: a person acting as a link between people in order to bring about an agreement
  • synonym – mediator: a person who attempts to make people involved in a conflict come to an agreement
