pyelography

noun
X-ray imaging of kidneys and ureters
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪəˈlɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˌpaɪɪˈlɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
an X-ray examination of the kidneys and ureters using an injected radiopaque dye to make the structures visible

Examples

pyelography procedure
Because of her allergy to iodine, she could not undergo intravenous pyelography.
Contrast dye is injected before pyelography to help visualize the kidneys clearly on X-rays.
The doctor recommended pyelography to assess any obstructions in her urinary tract.

Related Terms

  • synonym – urography: X-ray imaging of any part of the urinary tract
