backdown

noun
withdrawal from a position
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈbæk.daʊn / (BrE)
an act of withdrawing from a stated position or commitment

Examples

government backdown

Her refusal to issue a backdown made negotiations difficult.
The company’s sudden backdown surprised industry analysts.
