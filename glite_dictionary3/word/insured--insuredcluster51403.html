insured

noun
person protected by insurance
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈʃʊrd / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃɔːd / (BrE)
A person or entity whose interests are protected by an insurance policy.

Examples

insured person claims
All insured parties are entitled to compensation as stated in the policy.
The insured must file a claim within 30 days of the incident.
The company offers benefits to the insured in case of accidental injury.
