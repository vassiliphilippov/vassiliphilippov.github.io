borsch

noun
beet-based Eastern European soup
Pronunciation: / bɔrʃ / (AmE), / bɔːʃ / (BrE)
a soup of Eastern European origin made principally with beetroot, often including cabbage, potatoes, and meat

Examples

hot borsch soup
In the winter, my grandmother would make steaming bowls of borsch to warm the whole family.
She learned how to cook borsch from a recipe passed down through generations.
The restaurant is famous for its traditional Ukrainian borsch served with sour cream and bread.
