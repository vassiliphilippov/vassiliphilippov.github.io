problematical

adjective
open to doubt or debate
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑːbləˈmætɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌprɒblɪˈmætɪkəl / (BrE)
Open to doubt or debate; not certain or settled.

Examples

problematical explanation
His explanation for the error was highly problematical and failed to convince the jury.
The effectiveness of the new policy remains problematical until more data are collected.
Whether the company can recover from its losses is problematical at this point.

  • synonym – problematic: open to question or doubt; uncertain
