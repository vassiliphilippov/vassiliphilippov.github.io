buccula

noun
fold of fatty tissue under the chin
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌk.jʊ.lə / (AmE), / ˈbʌk.jʊ.lə / (BrE)
A fold of fatty tissue located under the chin, often referred to as a double chin.

Examples

visible buccula under chin
As she looked in the mirror, she noticed a small buccula forming beneath her jawline.
In anatomical texts, the buccula is often illustrated as part of the submental region.
Some cosmetic procedures are designed to reduce the appearance of a prominent buccula.
