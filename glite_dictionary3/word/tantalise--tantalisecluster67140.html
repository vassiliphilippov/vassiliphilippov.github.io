to tantalise

verb
persistently tease or criticise
Pronunciation: / ˈtæn.tə.laɪz / (AmE), / ˈtan.tə.laɪz / (BrE)
to annoy or harass someone with persistent criticism or teasing

Examples

to tantalise a classmate
His classmates would tantalise him for his unusual accent every day.
She found it exhausting when her friends tantalised her about her new haircut.
The reporters continued to tantalise the politician with questions about the scandal.

Related Terms

  • synonym – razz: to make fun of or deride
  • synonym – tease: to make fun of or attempt to provoke in a playful way
