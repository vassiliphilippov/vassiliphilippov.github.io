whir

noun
soft continuous sound of rapid motion
Pronunciation: / wɝː / (AmE), / wɜː / (BrE)
a continuous soft, rapid sound made by something moving quickly or rotating

Examples

the whir of a fan
A sudden whir erupted as the old computer started up.
He listened to the whir of insect wings outside the window.
The only sound in the room was the gentle whir of the ceiling fan.
