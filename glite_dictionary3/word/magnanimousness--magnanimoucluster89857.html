magnanimousness

noun
quality of being magnanimous; generosity
Pronunciation: / mæɡˈnænəməsˌnəs / (AmE), / mægˈnænɪməsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being magnanimous; generosity and nobility of spirit, especially towards someone less powerful.
