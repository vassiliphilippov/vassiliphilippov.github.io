graduation

noun
academic ceremony conferring degrees/diplomas
Pronunciation: / ˌɡrædʒuˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɡradjuˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a ceremony at which academic degrees or diplomas are formally conferred on students who have completed their studies

Examples

graduation ceremony event
After graduation, he moved to a new city to start his job.
Her parents attended her graduation ceremony at the university.
The students tossed their caps in the air to celebrate graduation.
