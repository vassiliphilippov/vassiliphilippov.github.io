materiality

noun
physical existence or substance
Pronunciation: / məˌtɪriˈæləti / (AmE), / məˌtɪəriˈæləti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being physical or composed of matter; physical existence.

Examples

physical materiality
Modern art often challenges our understanding of materiality and form.
Philosophers have long debated the materiality of the human soul.
The materiality of the sculpture makes it strikingly real and tangible.
