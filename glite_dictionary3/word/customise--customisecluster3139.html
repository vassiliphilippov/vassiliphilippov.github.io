to customise

verb
modify to suit requirements
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəmaɪz / (BrE)
to change or make something to suit a particular person's needs or preferences

Examples

to customise a product
She decided to customise her laptop with unique stickers and decals.
The chef is happy to customise any dish to meet dietary restrictions.
You can customise your phone with different themes and ringtones.
The car was customised with special wheels and a new paint job.
They decided to customise their wedding invitations with a unique design.
You can customise your computer settings to suit your needs.
