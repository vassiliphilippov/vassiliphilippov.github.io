emptor

noun
a buyer or purchaser
Pronunciation: / ˈɛmptɔr / (AmE), / ˈɛmptɔː / (BrE)
a person who buys something; a buyer, especially in legal or commercial contexts

Examples

emptor of goods
In legal documents, the emptor is often contrasted with the vendor.
The emptor must examine goods carefully before purchase, as the principle of caveat emptor applies.
The rights and responsibilities of the emptor are stated in the contract.
