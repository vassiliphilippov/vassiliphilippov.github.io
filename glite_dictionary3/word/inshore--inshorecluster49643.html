inshore

adverb
toward the shore
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌʃɔr / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌʃɔː / (BrE)
toward the shore, especially from the sea or a body of water.

Examples

move inshore quickly
Fishermen often move inshore during stormy weather.
The boat drifted slowly inshore as the tide changed.
