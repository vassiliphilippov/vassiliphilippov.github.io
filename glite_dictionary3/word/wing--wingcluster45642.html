wing

noun
section added to a building
Pronunciation: / wɪŋ / (AmE), / wɪŋ / (BrE)
a part of a building that projects from or is connected to the main structure, often serving a specific function

Examples

hospital wing
The hospital's new wing was completed last year and includes additional patient rooms.
The west wing of the house was used as a library and study area.
They added a wing to the museum for special exhibitions.
