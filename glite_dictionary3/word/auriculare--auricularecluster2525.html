auriculare

noun
cranial point at ear canal opening
Pronunciation: / ɔːˌrɪkjəˈlɛri / (AmE), / ɔːˌrɪkjʊˈleəri / (BrE)
The craniometric point located at the center of the opening of the external acoustic meatus (ear canal).

Examples

auriculare point on skull
During the skull measurement, the technician carefully located the auriculare on each side.
Researchers often use the auriculare as a reference point in anthropological studies.
The distance between the left auriculare and the right mastoid was noted.
