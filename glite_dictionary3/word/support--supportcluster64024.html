support

noun
military assistance or reinforcement
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
assistance provided, especially in a military context, to help strengthen a force or aid in the success of an operation, often by supplying additional personnel or equipment.

Examples

military support
Naval support was crucial for the success of the landing operation.
The army advanced under heavy support from airstrikes and artillery.
They radioed headquarters for immediate support after losing several vehicles.

Related Terms

  • synonym – reinforcement: additional military forces or resources sent to strengthen an existing force
