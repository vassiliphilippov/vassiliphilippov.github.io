womanliness

noun
quality of being womanly
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊmənlinəs / (AmE), / ˈwʊmənlinəs / (BrE)
The quality of possessing characteristics traditionally associated with women or adult females, such as gentleness or nurturing.

Examples

her womanliness shines
He admired her womanliness, especially her kindness and compassion toward others.
Her womanliness was evident in both her gentle manner and her strength during difficult times.
The character's womanliness is celebrated throughout the novel as a source of power and grace.
