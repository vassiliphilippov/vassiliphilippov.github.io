uniform

adjective
not varying; consistent; not differentiated
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːnəˌfɔrm /; / ˈjunəˌfɔrm /; / ˈjuːnɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪfɔːm /; / ˈjuːnɪˌfɔːm / (BrE)
not varying; the same in all parts or at all times; showing consistency or sameness
