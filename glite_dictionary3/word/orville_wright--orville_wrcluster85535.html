Orville Wright

proper noun
American aviation pioneer, co-inventor of the airplane
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrvɪl ˈraɪt / (AmE), / ˈɔːvɪl ˈraɪt / (BrE)
(1871–1948) An American aviation pioneer who, with his brother Wilbur Wright, invented and built the first successful airplane.
