untimbered

adjective
not having timbers
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɪmbɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɪmbəd / (BrE)
lacking timbers; not made with or reinforced by wooden beams or supports.

Examples

untimbered mine shaft
Engineers inspected the untimbered shaft for structural safety concerns.
The barn was completely untimbered, relying entirely on steel supports.
They launched an untimbered canoe onto the calm lake at dawn.

Related Terms

  • antonym – timbered: having timbers; made or reinforced with wooden beams
