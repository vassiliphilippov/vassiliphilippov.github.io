heart

noun
symbolic shape used on cards and for love
Pronunciation: / hɑrt / (AmE), / hɑːt / (BrE)
A shape with two rounded sides curving inward at the top and meeting at a point at the bottom, often used as a symbol of love and on playing cards.

Examples

red heart shape
He sent her a card with a big red heart on the front.
She doodled a heart in her notebook during class.
The Queen of hearts is my favorite playing card.

  • synonym – diamond: a red, diamond-shaped symbol on playing cards
  • synonym – spade: a black symbol on playing cards shaped like an upside-down heart with a stalk
