medic

noun
military medical practitioner
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪk / (BrE)
a person trained to provide medical care, especially a member of the armed forces who treats wounded soldiers in the field

Examples

army medic
After training, she was assigned as a medic with the infantry unit.
The wounded soldier was quickly attended to by the medic on the battlefield.
When the alarm sounded, the medic rushed to help the injured.
