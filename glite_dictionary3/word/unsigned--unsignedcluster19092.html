unsigned

adjective
not bearing a signature
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsaɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsaɪnd / (BrE)
not having a signature; not signed by an individual or official source.

Examples

unsigned letter
She found an unsigned letter on her desk, making her wonder who sent it.
The artist's work remained unsigned, leaving its creator a mystery.
The contract was unsigned, so it was not legally binding.
