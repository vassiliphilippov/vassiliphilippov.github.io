octet

noun
a group or set of eight
Pronunciation: / ɑkˈtɛt / (AmE), / ɒkˈtɛt / (BrE)
A group or set of eight people or things considered as a unit; especially, a group of eight musicians or a musical composition for eight performers.

Examples

octet of bytes
In computer networks, each octet represents eight bits of data.

The jazz ensemble performed as an octet for the special concert.
