to dicker

verb
negotiate or bargain terms
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪkər / (AmE), / ˈdɪkə / (BrE)
to negotiate or bargain the terms of a deal, especially over a price

Examples

to dicker over price
She spent nearly an hour dickering with the car dealer before making a decision.
The two merchants began to dicker over the price of the antiques.
We didn't have time to dicker about the details, so we accepted the initial offer.
