Watusi

noun
member of a central African ethnic group
Pronunciation: / wɑˈtusi / (AmE), / wɑːˈtuːsi / (BrE)
A member of a Bantu-speaking ethnic group living chiefly in Rwanda and Burundi, also known as Tutsi.
