BC

adverb
before COVID-19 (informal)
Pronunciation: / bi si / (AmE), / biː siː / (BrE)
(informal) Used to mark time as occurring before the COVID-19 pandemic; 'Before COVID-19'.

Examples

pre-pandemic BC
Many people say life felt easier BC, before lockdowns and social distancing became normal.
Companies compare sales figures from BC years to see how the pandemic changed consumer behavior.
People jokingly refer to pre-pandemic travel as the BC era when flights were easier.

Related Terms

  • antonym – post-pandemic: the period after the main COVID-19 pandemic disruptions
  • synonym – pre-pandemic: the period of time before the COVID-19 pandemic
