to gore

verb
to wound with a sharp object
Pronunciation: / ɡɔr / (AmE), / ɡɔː / (BrE)
to wound or pierce with a sharp or penetrating object, especially the horn or tusk of an animal

Examples

to gore with horns
An antelope's sharp horns can gore its predators in self-defense.
Several people were injured when the wild boar gored them during the festival.
The bull charged at the matador and gored him in the leg.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pierce: to penetrate or make a hole through something using a sharp object
  • synonym – stab: to thrust a pointed weapon into someone or something
