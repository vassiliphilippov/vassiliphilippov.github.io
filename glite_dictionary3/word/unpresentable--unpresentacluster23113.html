unpresentable

adjective
not suitable to be seen or shown in public
Pronunciation: / ˌənprɪˈzɛntəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnprɪˈzɛntəbl / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) not fit or suitable to be shown or presented in public because of poor appearance, condition, or behavior.

Examples

look unpresentable in public
After working all night, she felt too unpresentable to meet her friends for breakfast.
He apologized for his unpresentable clothing at the formal dinner.
The old sofa was so unpresentable that we had to throw a blanket over it before guests arrived.

Related Terms

  • antonym – presentable: fit to be seen, neat or suitable in appearance
