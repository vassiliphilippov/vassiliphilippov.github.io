absorbent

adjective
able to absorb liquid or substances
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɔrbənt, æbˈzɔrbənt / (AmE), / əbˈzɔːbənt, æbˈzɔːbənt / (BrE)
able to take in and hold liquid or other substances easily; capable of absorbing.

Examples

absorbent towel
Cotton is an absorbent material often used in clothing and bandages.
These sponges are so absorbent that they soak up water instantly.
This paper towel is highly absorbent and perfect for cleaning up spills.

Related Terms

  • antonym – nonabsorbent: not able to absorb liquids or substances
  • synonym – porous: having minute spaces or holes through which liquid or air may pass
