reliever

noun
person who calms or lessens anxiety
Pronunciation: / rɪˈliːvər / (AmE), / rɪˈliːvə / (BrE)
a person who lessens the severity of something, especially by calming fears or anxiety

Examples

reliever of stress
As a reliever of fear, the counselor reassured everyone during the emergency.
She has always been a reliever in stressful situations, helping her friends stay calm.
The therapist proved to be a true reliever of the patient's overwhelming anxiety.

Related Terms

  • synonym – allayer: someone who calms or lessens strong feelings or fears
  • synonym – alleviator: a person who makes something less severe
