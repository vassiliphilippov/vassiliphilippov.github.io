inorganically

adverb
not involving carbon compounds
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌɔrˈɡænɪkli / (AmE), / ɪnˌɔːˈɡænɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner not involving carbon compounds; in a way relating to substances not derived from living organisms.

Examples

inorganically formed minerals
Many minerals are formed inorganically deep within the Earth’s crust.
Some compounds can be synthesized inorganically in a laboratory setting.
These nutrients occur naturally inorganically and are essential for plant growth.
