abnormality

noun
below-normal intelligence condition
Pronunciation: / ˌæb.nɔːrˈmæ.lə.ti / (AmE), / ˌæb.nɔːˈmæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
A condition in which intelligence is significantly below the normal range, originally referring to intellectual retardation or impairment.

Examples

intellectual abnormality
Certain abnormalities of intelligence may require specialized educational support.
Early intervention can help children with mental abnormality reach their potential.
The doctor noted an abnormality in the child's cognitive development.
