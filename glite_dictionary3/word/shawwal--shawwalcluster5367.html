Shawwal

noun
tenth month of Islamic calendar
Pronunciation: / ʃɑːˈwɑl / (AmE), / ʃaʊˈwɑːl / (BrE)
The tenth month of the Islamic calendar, following Ramadan and preceding Dhu al-Qi'dah.

Examples

Shawwal after Ramadan
Eid al-Fitr is celebrated on the first day of Shawwal, marking the end of Ramadan.
Many Muslims begin voluntary fasting in Shawwal after completing Ramadan.
The month of Shawwal is significant in Islamic traditions and commemorations.
