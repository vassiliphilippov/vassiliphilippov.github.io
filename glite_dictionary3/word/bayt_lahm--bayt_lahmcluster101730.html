Bayt Lahm

proper noun
town near Jerusalem, also Bethlehem
Pronunciation: / baɪt ˈlɑm / (AmE), / baɪt ˈlɑːm / (BrE)
A small town near Jerusalem in the West Bank, traditionally regarded as the birthplace of Jesus and the early home of David; also known as Bethlehem.
