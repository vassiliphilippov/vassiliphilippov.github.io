retail

noun
the sale of goods to the public
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈriːteɪl / (BrE)
the sale of goods to the public in relatively small quantities for use or consumption rather than for resale

Examples

retail business
Online retail has dramatically changed the way people shop for goods.
She works in retail and enjoys interacting with customers every day.
The store specializes in both retail and wholesale sales.
