square

noun
open public area at street intersection
Pronunciation: / skwɛr / (AmE), / skweə / (BrE)
an open area in a town or city where two or more streets meet, often used for public gatherings or events

Examples

town square
Cafés and shops line the historic square in the center of town.
The city organized a concert in the main square last summer.
Tourists gathered in the crowded square to watch the street performers.
