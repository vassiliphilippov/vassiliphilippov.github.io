Reptilia

noun
class of reptiles
Pronunciation: / rɛpˈtɪliə / (AmE), / rɛpˈtɪlɪə / (BrE)
a class of cold-blooded, air-breathing vertebrate animals with dry skin covered with scales or horny plates; includes snakes, lizards, turtles, and crocodiles
