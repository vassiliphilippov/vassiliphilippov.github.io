monitor

noun
device that displays images or data on a screen
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnɪtər /; / ˈmɑː.nɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɒnɪtə /; / ˈmɒn.ɪ.tə / (BrE)
an electronic device that displays information or checks the quality or operation of something (such as a computer screen or a device for checking audio, video, or systems)
