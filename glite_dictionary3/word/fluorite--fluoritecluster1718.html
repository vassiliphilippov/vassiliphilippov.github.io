fluorite

noun
a mineral; source of fluorine
Pronunciation: / ˈflʊrˌaɪt / (AmE), / ˈflɔːr.aɪt / (BrE)
a soft mineral consisting of calcium fluoride, often occurring in cubic crystals and fluorescent under ultraviolet light; a chief source of fluorine
