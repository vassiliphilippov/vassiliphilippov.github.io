Kodiak

proper noun
island off southern Alaska, historic Russian settlement
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊdiˌæk / (AmE), / ˈkəʊdiːˌæk / (BrE)
an island off southern Alaska in the Gulf of Alaska, notable as the site of the first European settlement in the area, founded by Russians in 1784.

Examples

Kodiak Island Alaska
Early Russian settlers established a colony on Kodiak in the 18th century.
