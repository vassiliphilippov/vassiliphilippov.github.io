independence

noun
freedom from control or influence
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndəˈpɛndəns /; / ˌɪndɪˈpɛndəns / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈpɛndəns / (BrE)
the state of being free from the control or influence of others, especially when a country becomes free from another country's rule
