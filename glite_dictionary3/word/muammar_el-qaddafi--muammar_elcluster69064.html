Muammar el-Qaddafi

proper noun
Libyan political leader (ruled 1969–2011)
Pronunciation: / muːˈɑːmɑːr ɛl kəˈdɑːfi / (AmE), / mʊˈɑːmɑː ɛl kæˈdæfi / (BrE)
(1942–2011) The Libyan political and military leader who ruled Libya from 1969, after coming to power in a coup, until his death in 2011.

Examples

Muammar el-Qaddafi ruled Libya
In 1969, Muammar el-Qaddafi led a coup that overthrew the Libyan monarchy.
