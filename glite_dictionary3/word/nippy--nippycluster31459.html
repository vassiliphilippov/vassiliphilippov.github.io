nippy

adjective
having a sharp or biting taste
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪpi / (AmE), / ˈnɪpi / (BrE)
(of food or drink) having a sharp or biting taste; tangy or pungent.

Examples

nippy cheese flavor
She prefers mild cheeses over nippy varieties that sting the palate.
The sauce was too nippy for my taste, overpowering the other ingredients.
This nippy cheese adds a bold flavor to the sandwich.
