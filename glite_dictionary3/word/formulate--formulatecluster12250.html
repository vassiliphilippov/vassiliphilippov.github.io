to formulate

verb
prepare according to a formula
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrmjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to prepare or create according to a set formula or method

Examples

to formulate a solution
She was tasked to formulate the new cleaning solution in the laboratory.
The scientists will formulate a new chemical compound based on established procedures.
To ensure consistency, pharmacists formulate the medicine exactly as prescribed by the standard recipe.

Related Terms

  • synonym – devise: to invent or plan something carefully
  • antonym – improvise: to create spontaneously without preparation
