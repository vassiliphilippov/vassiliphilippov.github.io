impressed

adjective
affected or influenced; admiring
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈprɛst / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛst / (BrE)
feeling admiration or respect for someone or something, or being deeply affected by something's quality or skill

impressed by his talent

I was really impressed with how smoothly the event was organized.
She was impressed by the artist's unique technique and creativity.
