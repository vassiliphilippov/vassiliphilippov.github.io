stay

noun
supporting rope or cable on a ship
Pronunciation: / steɪ / (AmE), / steɪ / (BrE)
A strong rope or wire cable on a ship, used to support a mast or spar.

Examples

mast stay
He repaired the stay using a length of new wire.
If a stay breaks in a storm, the mast can be damaged.
The forward stay must be checked before raising the sail.

Related Terms

  • synonym – shroud: a rope that supports a ship's mast from side to side
