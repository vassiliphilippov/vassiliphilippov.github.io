gently

adverb
in a soft or light manner
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛntli / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntli / (BrE)
in a soft, light, or kind way; not harsh or forceful

Examples

She gently closed the door so as not to wake the baby.
