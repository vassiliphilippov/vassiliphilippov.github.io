Senecan

adjective
of or relating to Seneca (Roman philosopher)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnɪkən / (AmE), / ˈsɛnɪkən / (BrE)
Relating to, characteristic of, or in the manner of the Roman philosopher Seneca, especially his style or thought.

Examples

Senecan philosophy style
Her Senecan attitude toward adversity reflected a deep appreciation for stoic philosophy.

The tragedy employed Senecan themes of revenge and fate throughout its five acts.
