to sensibilise

verb
make aware or sensitive
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsɪbəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsɪbɪˌlaɪz / (BrE)
to make someone or something sensitive or aware of something

Examples

to sensibilise the public
The campaign aims to sensibilise the public to environmental issues.
The training sessions are designed to sensibilise staff to cultural differences in the workplace.
Through her work, she hopes to sensibilise people to the challenges faced by the disabled community.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sensitize: make sensitive or aware; standard spelling in American English
