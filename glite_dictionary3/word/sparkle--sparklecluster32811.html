to sparkle

verb
emit or produce sparks
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑr.kəl /; / ˈspɑrkəl / (AmE), / ˈspɑː.kəl /; / ˈspɑːkəl /; / ˈspɑːkl / (BrE)
