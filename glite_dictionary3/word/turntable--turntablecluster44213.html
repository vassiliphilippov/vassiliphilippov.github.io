turntable

noun
revolving tray for dining table
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrnˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnˌteɪbəl / (BrE)
a circular, rotating tray placed on a dining table to make food or condiments easily accessible to everyone

Examples

dining table turntable
A glass turntable in the center of the table made sharing dishes easy.
Please place the salad on the turntable so everyone can reach it.
The guests took turns spinning the turntable to serve themselves.
