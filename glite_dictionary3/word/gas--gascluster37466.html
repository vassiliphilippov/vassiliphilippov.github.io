gas

noun
gaseous fuel for cooking or heating
Pronunciation: / ɡæs / (AmE), / ɡas / (BrE)
A substance in the gaseous state used as a fuel, especially for cooking or heating in homes.

Examples

gas for cooking
After the pipeline was repaired, the supply of gas was restored to the building.
The kitchen stove runs on gas instead of electricity.
We use gas to heat our water during the winter months.

Related Terms

  • antonym – electricity: form of energy used as an alternative to gas for cooking and heating
  • synonym – liquefied petroleum gas: fuel gas stored under pressure as a liquid
  • synonym – natural gas: flammable gas consisting mainly of methane used as fuel
