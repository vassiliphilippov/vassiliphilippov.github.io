easterly

adverb
from the east (direction)
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstɚli / (AmE), / ˈiːstəli / (BrE)
(of wind or current) coming from the east.

Examples

wind blows easterly
During autumn, the prevailing breezes tend to come easterly over the hills.
Strong winds blew easterly across the coast, making sailing difficult.
The weather forecast predicts that showers will move easterly overnight.
