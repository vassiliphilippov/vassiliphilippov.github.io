to flag

verb
mark or identify with a flag
Pronunciation: / flæɡ / (AmE), / flæɡ / (BrE)
to mark, highlight, or draw attention to something, especially for review or action

Examples

to flag an email

If you notice any discrepancies, please flag them for review.

We use a special icon to flag important messages in the system.
