Juniperus virginiana

noun
a North American juniper tree (eastern red cedar)
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒuːnəˈpɪərəs vɜːrdʒɪˈneɪniə / (AmE), / ˌdʒuːnɪˈpɛərəs vɜːdʒɪˈneɪnɪə / (BrE)
A small evergreen coniferous tree, native to eastern North America, with a conical crown, reddish-brown bark that peels in shreds, and small, sharp needles; also known as eastern red cedar.
