ovary

noun
female reproductive organ producing ova
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvəˌri / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəri / (BrE)
Either of a pair of female reproductive organs in which ova are produced and which secrete estrogen and progesterone.

Examples

human ovary
Damage to the ovary can affect fertility in women.
Hormones produced by the ovary play a crucial role in regulating the menstrual cycle.
The surgeon explained that the ovary was healthy and functioning normally.

  • synonym – egg: the female reproductive cell; ovum
