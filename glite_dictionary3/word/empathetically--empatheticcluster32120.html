empathetically

adverb
in an empathetic manner
Pronunciation: / ˌɛmpəˈθɛtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɛmpəˈθɛtɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows empathy; with an understanding of and sensitivity to another person's feelings.

Examples

empathetically listen to problems
He listened empathetically to the stories of the refugees.
She nodded empathetically as her friend described her difficult situation.
The teacher responded empathetically to the student's concerns about the exam.
