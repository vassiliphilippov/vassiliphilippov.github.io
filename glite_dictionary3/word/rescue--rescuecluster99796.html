rescue

noun
the act of saving from danger
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛs.kju / (AmE), / ˈrɛs.kjuː / (BrE)
The act of saving someone or something from a dangerous or distressing situation.

Examples

a daring rescue
Firefighters performed a daring rescue during the night-time blaze.
Thanks to the quick rescue efforts, everyone made it out safely.
The rescue of the stranded hikers was broadcast live on television.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deliverance: the act of being rescued or set free
  • antonym – endangerment: the act of putting someone or something in danger
  • synonym – save: to keep someone or something safe from harm or danger
