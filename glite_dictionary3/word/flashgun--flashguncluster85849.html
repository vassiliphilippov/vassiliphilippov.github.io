flashgun

noun
device for producing a camera flash
Pronunciation: / ˈflæʃˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈflæʃˌɡʌn / (BrE)
a device that produces a brief flash of light for use in photography

Examples

flashgun for cameras
He forgot to bring his flashgun, so the pictures came out too dark.
Older cameras often require a separate flashgun to provide enough light indoors.
The photographer attached a flashgun to his camera before the shoot.
