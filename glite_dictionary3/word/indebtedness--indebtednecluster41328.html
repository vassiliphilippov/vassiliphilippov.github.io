indebtedness

noun
state of being obligated to someone
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɛtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛtɪdnəs / (BrE)
the state of being under an obligation to someone for a service or favor received

Examples

deep indebtedness
He expressed his indebtedness by writing a heartfelt thank you letter.
His indebtedness to his friends was clear after they helped him through tough times.
She felt a deep sense of indebtedness to her mentor, who had guided her career.
