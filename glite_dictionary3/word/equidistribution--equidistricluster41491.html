equidistribution

noun
equal-frequency distribution
Pronunciation: / iːkwɪˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən / (AmE), / ˌiːkwɪdɪs.trɪˈbjuː.ʃən / (BrE)
a statistical or mathematical distribution in which all values or outcomes are equally likely to occur

Examples

equidistribution of numbers
If each outcome occurs an equal number of times, the set shows equidistribution.
Random number generators are often tested for equidistribution to ensure fairness.
The concept of equidistribution is essential in probability theory and statistics.

  • synonym – uniform distribution: a probability distribution where all outcomes are equally likely
