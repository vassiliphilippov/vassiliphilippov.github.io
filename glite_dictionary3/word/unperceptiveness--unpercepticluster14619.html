unperceptiveness

noun
lack of insight or understanding
Pronunciation: / ʌn.pərˈsɛp.tɪv.nəs / (AmE), / ʌn.pəˈsɛp.tɪv.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of lacking insight or sympathetic understanding

Examples

emotional unperceptiveness
His unperceptiveness made it difficult for him to notice when others needed help.
She was frustrated by her manager's unperceptiveness to the team's concerns.
The critic's unperceptiveness was evident in her failure to grasp the story's deeper meaning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – insensitivity: lack of sensitivity or awareness to others
  • antonym – perceptiveness: the quality of having insight and understanding
