to stink

verb
have a bad smell
Pronunciation: / stɪŋk / (AmE), / stɪŋk / (BrE)

Examples

to stink badly
Her shoes stank after she wore them in the rain all day.
If you leave the trash outside, it will soon stink in the summer heat.
The locker room stinks of sweat and old gym clothes.
