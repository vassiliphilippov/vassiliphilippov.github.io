gametocyte

noun
precursor cell developing into a gamete
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈmiːtəˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ɡəˈmiːtəˌsaɪt / (BrE)
An immature cell in animals or plants that develops into a gamete through the process of meiosis.
