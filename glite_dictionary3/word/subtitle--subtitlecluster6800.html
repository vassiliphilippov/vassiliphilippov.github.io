to subtitle

verb
add subtitles to video or film
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌtaɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌtʌɪt(ə)l / (BrE)
to provide (a film, video, or broadcast) with subtitles, usually as a written translation of the dialogue

Examples

to subtitle a film
Many foreign films are subtitled in English for wider distribution.
She has experience subtitling movies for both television and streaming platforms.
They decided to subtitle the documentary so that international audiences could understand it.

  • synonym – caption: to provide a short title or explanation below an image or video
