assignable

adjective
able to be allocated or assigned
Pronunciation: / əˈsaɪnəbəl / (AmE), / əˈsaɪnəbəl / (BrE)
Capable of being allocated or given to a person, role, or resource; able to be assigned (especially of tasks, issues, or responsibilities). Does not imply legal transfer of ownership.

Examples

assignable tasks list
The project backlog lists several assignable tasks for a junior developer this sprint.
Make sure each bug report is assignable to a team member with the right expertise.
Only issues marked assignable will appear in the scheduler for next week's sprint planning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – allocatable: able to be allocated or distributed to someone or something
  • antonym – transferable: able to be transferred to another owner or party (legal transfer)
