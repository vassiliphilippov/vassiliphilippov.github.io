half-witted

adjective
very stupid or foolish
Pronunciation: / ˌhæfˈwɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌhɑːfˈwɪtɪd / (BrE)
Very stupid or foolish; lacking intelligence or sense.

Examples

a half-witted idea
He came up with a half-witted idea that was quickly rejected by the team.
It was half-witted of her to leave the keys in the car with the engine running.
The movie's plot is a half-witted attempt at comedy that fails to deliver any real laughs.
