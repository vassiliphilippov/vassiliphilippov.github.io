Mulwi

noun
Chadic language spoken in Cameroon
Pronunciation: / ˈmʊlwi / (AmE), / ˈmʊlwi / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken south of Lake Chad, mainly in the Far North Region of Cameroon, in the Mayo-Danay division, Maga subdivision.
