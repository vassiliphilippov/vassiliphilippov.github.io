Cronartium ribicola

noun
fungus causing white pine blister rust
Pronunciation: / kroʊˈnɑːrtiəm raɪˈbɪkələ / (AmE), / krəʊˈnɑːtɪəm rɪˈbɪkələ / (BrE)
a species of fungus that causes white pine blister rust and requires plants of the genus Ribes as an alternate host
