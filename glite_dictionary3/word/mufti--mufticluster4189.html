mufti

noun
civilian dress for uniformed personnel
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌf.ti / (AmE), / ˈmʌf.ti / (BrE)
civilian clothes worn by someone who normally wears a military or other official uniform

Examples

in mufti at work
He arrived at the meeting in mufti, which surprised his colleagues who were used to seeing him in uniform.
On weekends, the soldiers were allowed to wear mufti instead of their usual uniforms.
The school organizes a mufti day where students can wear casual clothes for charity.
