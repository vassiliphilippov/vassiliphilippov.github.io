bogus

adjective
fake, fraudulent, or not genuine
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊɡəs / (AmE), / ˈbəʊɡəs / (BrE)
not genuine; fake or fraudulent, usually intended to deceive

Examples

bogus ID card
He tried to enter the club with a bogus ID card but was stopped at the door.
The company lost thousands of dollars after paying invoices for bogus services.
The police warned people about a bogus charity collecting money in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fake: not genuine; a thing that is not what it purports to be
  • antonym – genuine: truly what something is said to be; authentic
