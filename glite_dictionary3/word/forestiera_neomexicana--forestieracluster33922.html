Forestiera neomexicana

noun
A spiny shrub native to the southwestern US
Pronunciation: / fɔːrˈɛstiːrə niːoʊmɛkˈsɪkənə / (AmE), / fɒrˈɛstiərə niːəʊmɛkˈsɪkənə / (BrE)
A spiny, branching deciduous shrub of the southwestern United States, producing clusters of small yellow-white flowers before the leaves and followed by attractive black berry-like fruits.
