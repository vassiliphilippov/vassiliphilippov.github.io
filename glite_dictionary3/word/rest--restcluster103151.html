rest

noun
support for placing objects
Pronunciation: / rɛst / (AmE), / rɛst / (BrE)

Examples

rest for a rifle
He steadied his elbow on a rest attached to the workbench.
The musician placed her violin on a rest during the break.
The sniper used a sandbag as a rest for his rifle.
