Ornithogalum umbellatum

noun
small plant with star-shaped white flowers
Pronunciation: / ɔːrˌnɪθoʊˈɡeɪləm ʌmˌbɛləˈtɑːm / (AmE), / ɔːˌnɪθəʊˈɡɑːləm ʌmˌbɛləˈtɑːm / (BrE)
