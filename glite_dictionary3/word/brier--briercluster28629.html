brier

noun
prickly Eurasian wild rose
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪ.ər / (AmE), / ˈbraɪ.ə / (BrE)
A Eurasian wild rose with prickly stems, fragrant leaves, and bright pink flowers that produce scarlet hips.

Examples

wild brier rose
In the garden, a brier bloomed, its flowers bright against the green leaves.
She gathered some brier roses to decorate the old cottage window.
The path was overgrown with brier and bramble, making it difficult to walk.
