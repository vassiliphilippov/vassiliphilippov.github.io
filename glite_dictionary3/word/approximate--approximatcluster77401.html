to approximate

verb
be nearly the same as
Pronunciation: / əˈprɑksɪˌmeɪt / (AmE), / əˈprɒksɪmeɪt / (BrE)
to be nearly the same as something else; to be close in value, amount, or nature

Examples

to approximate a value
In reality, the numbers only approximate the true costs.
Our findings approximate the conclusions drawn by the previous researchers.
The new design approximates the original concept quite closely.

Related Terms

  • antonym – differ: to be unlike or dissimilar
  • synonym – resemble: to be similar to or look like
