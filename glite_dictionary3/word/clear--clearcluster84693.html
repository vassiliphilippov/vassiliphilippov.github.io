to clear

verb
pass by or over without touching
Pronunciation: / klɪr / (AmE), / klɪə / (BrE)
(of a moving object) to go past, over, or under something without touching it.

Examples

clear a hurdle
Make sure the car can clear the low bridge before driving underneath.
The athlete managed to clear the hurdle easily.
The plane just cleared the mountain peak by a few meters.
