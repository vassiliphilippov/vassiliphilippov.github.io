egomaniac

noun
obsessively self-centered person
Pronunciation: / ˌiːɡoʊˈmeɪniæk / (AmE), / ˌiːɡəʊˈmeɪniæk / (BrE)
A person who is obsessively self-centered and egotistical.

Examples

self-obsessed egomaniac
Calling him an egomaniac might be harsh, but he certainly likes to be in the spotlight.
Her friends grew frustrated with her egomaniac behavior and constant need for attention.
The company failed because its CEO was an egomaniac who refused to listen to advice.
