misbranded

adjective
falsely or illegally labeled
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈbrændɪd / (AmE), / mɪsˈbrændɪd / (BrE)
(of a product) labeled falsely or misleadingly, especially in violation of statutory regulations.

Examples

misbranded food products
Inspectors routinely check for misbranded products on store shelves to protect consumers.
