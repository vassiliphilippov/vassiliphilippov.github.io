slurred

adjective
spoken indistinctly or unclearly
Pronunciation: / slɝd / (AmE), / slɜːd / (BrE)
(of speech) spoken in an indistinct way, as if the speaker cannot articulate words clearly, often due to intoxication or illness.

Examples

slurred speech
After several drinks, his slurred speech made it hard to understand him.
Her words were so slurred from exhaustion that she could barely finish her sentence.
The actor delivered his lines in a deliberately slurred voice to portray drunkenness.

Related Terms

  • antonym – clear: easily heard and understood, opposite of 'slurred' speech
