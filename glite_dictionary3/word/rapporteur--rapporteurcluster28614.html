rapporteur

noun
person who prepares official reports
Pronunciation: / ˌræpɔːrˈtɜr / (AmE), / ˌræpɔːˈtɜː / (BrE)
a person appointed by a committee or organization to compile and present reports of its meetings or proceedings

Examples

rapporteur presents report
As the rapporteur, she presented the findings to the larger assembly.
The rapporteur's report provided a clear overview of the debate and its conclusions.
The committee appointed a rapporteur to summarize the discussions for the official record.
