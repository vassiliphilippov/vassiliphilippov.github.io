occupied

adjective
being held, filled, or in use
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːkjupaɪd /; / ˈɑːkjəˌpaɪd / (AmE), / ˈɒkjʊpaɪd /; / ˈɒkjʊˌpaɪd / (BrE)
(of a space, seat, room, building, or land) being used, filled, or lived in by someone; not available
