plume

noun
a feather or cluster of feathers
Pronunciation: / plum /; / pluːm / (AmE), / pljuːm /; / pluːm / (BrE)
a large or decorative feather, or a group of feathers, often used for decoration on hats, clothing, or as part of a bird's covering
