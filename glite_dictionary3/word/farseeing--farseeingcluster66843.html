farseeing

adjective
able to see far distances
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrˌsiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːˌsiːɪŋ / (BrE)
Able to see across a great distance; having exceptional vision for distant objects.

Examples

farseeing eyes
Scientists have studied animals with farseeing vision to improve surveillance technology.
The farseeing eagle soared above the mountains, spotting prey miles away.
With his farseeing abilities, the lookout warned the crew long before danger arrived.
