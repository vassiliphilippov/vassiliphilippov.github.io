to go

verb
operate or function (machines)
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ / (BrE)
to function, operate, or work (especially of machines or devices); or to stop working or fail

Examples

go wrong
Does this clock still go after so many years?

The old washing machine finally went last night and flooded the laundry room.

  • synonym – fail: to stop working or not succeed
  • synonym – run: to function or operate, often referring to engines or devices
