neurodermatitis

noun
chronic localized itchy skin condition
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊdɜːrˌməˈtaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊdɜːməˈtaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A chronic skin condition characterized by persistent itching and thickened skin in localized areas, often of unknown cause.

Examples

neurodermatitis causes itching
Many people with neurodermatitis experience stress-related flare-ups of intense itching.
Persistent scratching made his neurodermatitis worse, increasing the risk of infection.
The dermatologist diagnosed her with neurodermatitis after noticing thickened patches on her neck.
