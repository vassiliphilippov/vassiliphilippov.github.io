prominence

noun
importance; being well-known
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.mə.nəns / (AmE), / ˈprɒm.ɪ.nəns / (BrE)
the state of being important, famous, or easily noticed

Examples

gain prominence in society
He achieved prominence as a leading environmental activist.
Her sudden prominence in the industry surprised everyone.
The newspaper gave prominence to the story on its front page.
Despite his early prominence, he faded from public view after retirement.
Her prominence in the scientific community grew rapidly after she published her research.
The issue gained prominence during the election campaign.
