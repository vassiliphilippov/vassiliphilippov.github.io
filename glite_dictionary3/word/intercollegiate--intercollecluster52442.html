intercollegiate

adjective
between colleges or universities
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərkəˈlidʒɪt / (AmE), / ˌɪntəkəˈliːdʒɪət / (BrE)
Existing or taking place between or involving different colleges or universities, especially in sports or competitions.

Examples

intercollegiate sports competition
Many students enjoy attending intercollegiate basketball games throughout the season.
She participates in the intercollegiate debate team, traveling to compete against students from other universities.
The university is known for its strong intercollegiate athletics program.
