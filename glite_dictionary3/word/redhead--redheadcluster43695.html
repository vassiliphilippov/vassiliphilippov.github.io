redhead

noun
a North American duck with a reddish-brown head
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌhɛd / (BrE)
A North American diving duck (Aythya americana) with a grey-and-black body and a reddish-brown head, especially the male.

Examples

a redhead duck
During migration, thousands of redheads gather in large flocks on inland lakes.
The male redhead is easily recognized by its striking reddish head color.
Wildlife photographers often seek out redheads in marshes during the breeding season.
