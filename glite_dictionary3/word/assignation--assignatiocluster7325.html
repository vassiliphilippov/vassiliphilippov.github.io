assignation

noun
the act of distributing or allotting
Pronunciation: / əˌsaɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌsaɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of distributing something by allotting or apportioning, often according to a plan or system

Examples

assignation of tasks
After a complex assignation of seats, each delegate knew where to sit.
Budget assignation is carried out by an independent committee each year.
The assignation of responsibilities ensured that every team member had a clear role.

Related Terms

  • synonym – allocation: the process of giving out resources or duties
  • synonym – apportionment: the action of dividing and distributing according to a plan
