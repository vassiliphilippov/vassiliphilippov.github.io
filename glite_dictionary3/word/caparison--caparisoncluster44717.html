to caparison

verb
dress a horse with decorative covering
Pronunciation: / kəˈpærɪsən / (AmE), / kəˈpærɪsən / (BrE)
to dress or cover (a horse or other animal) with an ornamental or decorative covering

Examples

to caparison a horse
Grooms spend hours caparisoning each horse for the festival.
She caparisoned the horse in velvet and gold for the royal procession.
The knights caparison their steeds before the grand parade.
