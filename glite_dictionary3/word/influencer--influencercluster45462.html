influencer

noun
person with large social media following who promotes products or ideas
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnfluənsər / (AmE), / ˈɪn.flʊ.ənsə / (BrE)
a person with a large following on social media who is able to shape public opinion or promote products, often for payment

Examples

social media influencer
As a successful influencer, he regularly collaborates with lifestyle brands on social media.
Several companies pay the influencer to promote their new clothing line on Instagram.
The rising influencer used her platform to raise awareness about environmental issues.
