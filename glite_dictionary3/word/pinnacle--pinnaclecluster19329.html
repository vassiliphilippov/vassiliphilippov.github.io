pinnacle

noun
the highest point or level
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪn.ə.kəl / (AmE), / ˈpɪn.ə.kəl / (BrE)
the highest or most successful point or level; the peak of achievement or development

Examples

pinnacle of success
At the pinnacle of her fame, she was recognized around the world.
Their company reached its pinnacle before the market downturn.
Winning the Nobel Prize was considered the pinnacle of his scientific career.

Related Terms

  • antonym – nadir: the lowest point; the most unsuccessful or depressing stage
  • synonym – peak: the highest point; summit; especially in achievement
