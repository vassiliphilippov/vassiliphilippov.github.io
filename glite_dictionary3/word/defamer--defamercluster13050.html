defamer

noun
someone who slanders or libels others
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfeɪmər / (AmE), / dɪˈfeɪmə / (BrE)
a person who damages another's reputation by making false or malicious statements

Examples

online defamer
Online anonymity can make it easier for a defamer to escape consequences.
She confronted her defamer in court after her reputation was ruined.
The newspaper sued the defamer for spreading baseless rumors about its editor.

Related Terms

  • synonym – libeler: person who publishes a false written statement damaging to a person's reputation
  • synonym – slanderer: person who makes false spoken statements damaging to a person's reputation
