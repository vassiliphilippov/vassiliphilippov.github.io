harrier

noun
a type of hawk that hunts over open ground
Pronunciation: / ˈhæriər / (AmE), / ˈhæriə / (BrE)
a hawk of the genus Circus, which hunts over open land and marshes, preying on small animals.

Examples

harrier flying low
A harrier relies on its keen eyesight to spot small mammals hiding in the grass.
Several harriers were spotted circling over the flooded meadows at dawn.
The marsh harrier glided low above the reeds, searching for its next meal.
