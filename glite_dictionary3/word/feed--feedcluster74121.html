to feed

verb
(of water) to flow into something
Pronunciation: / fid / (AmE), / fiːd / (BrE)
(of a stream, river, or similar) to flow or empty into another body of water.

Examples

feed into a river
Several mountain streams feed into the main reservoir each spring.
The Amazon River feeds into the Atlantic Ocean.
The canal feeds directly into the harbor, providing fresh water.

Related Terms

  • synonym – flow: to move along in a steady, continuous stream
