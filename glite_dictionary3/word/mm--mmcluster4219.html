M.M.

noun
metronome mark in music
Pronunciation: / ɛm ɛm / (AmE), / ɛm ɛm / (BrE)
An abbreviation for 'Maelzel's Metronome,' indicating the tempo of music by specifying the number of beats per minute.

Examples

M.M. marking in music
Please set the metronome to match the M.M. marking in your sheet music.
The composer specified an M.M. of 100 to ensure the correct pace.
