dialogue

noun
conversation between people
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪ.ə.lɔɡ /; / ˈdaɪəˌlɔg / (AmE), / ˈdaɪ.ə.lɒɡ /; / ˈdaɪəlɒg / (BrE)
a conversation between two or more people, or the words spoken by characters in a book, play, or movie

Examples

The dialogue in the play was witty and engaging.
Writers often struggle to create realistic dialogue between their characters.
