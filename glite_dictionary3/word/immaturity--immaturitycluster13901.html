immaturity

noun
the state of not being mature
Pronunciation: / ˌɪməˈtʃʊrəti / (AmE), / ˌɪməˈtjʊərɪti / (BrE)
The state or quality of not being fully developed or grown; lack of maturity.

Examples

emotional immaturity
Emotional immaturity can affect one's ability to maintain healthy relationships.
His constant joking was seen as a sign of immaturity.
The team's immaturity led to many avoidable mistakes during the tournament.
