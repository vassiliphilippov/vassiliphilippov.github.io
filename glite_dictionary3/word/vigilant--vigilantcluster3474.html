vigilant

adjective
keeping careful watch; alert
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪdʒələnt / (AmE), / ˈvɪdʒɪlənt / (BrE)
keeping careful watch for possible danger or difficulties; alert and attentive.

Examples

vigilant guard
Parents must remain vigilant when young children are playing near water.
She is always vigilant about locking all the doors before going to bed.
The security guards were vigilant throughout the night to prevent any disturbances.

  • antonym – inattentive: not paying attention; failing to be attentive
