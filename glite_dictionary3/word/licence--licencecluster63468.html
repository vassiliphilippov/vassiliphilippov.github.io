licence

noun
official permission document
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪ.səns / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.səns / (BrE)
an official document that gives someone permission to do or own something

Examples

licence to drive
She applied for a fishing licence before the season started.
The restaurant lost its alcohol licence after breaking the law.
You need a licence to drive a car in the UK.

Related Terms

  • synonym – license: official document granting permission (American English spelling)
  • synonym – permit: document granting legal permission
