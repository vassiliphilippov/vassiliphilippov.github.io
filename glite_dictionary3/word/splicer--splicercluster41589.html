splicer

noun
device for joining film, tape, or paper
Pronunciation: / ˈsplaɪsər / (AmE), / ˈsplaɪsə / (BrE)
a mechanical device used to join two pieces of film, tape, or paper together

Examples

film splicer tool
Always clean the splicer after joining several strips of tape.
Modern studios often use a digital splicer instead of manual tools.
The film editor used a splicer to repair the damaged reel.

Related Terms

  • antonym – cutter: a device for making cuts in materials like tape or film
  • synonym – joiner: a device or person that joins things together
