showy

adjective
attracting attention by brilliance or conspicuousness
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊ.i / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.i / (BrE)
attracting attention by displaying brilliance, virtuosity, or conspicuousness, often in a way intended to impress others

Examples

showy performance
His showy skills on the basketball court drew everyone’s attention.
She wore a showy dress adorned with sequins that sparkled under the lights.
The pianist gave a truly showy performance that captivated the audience.
