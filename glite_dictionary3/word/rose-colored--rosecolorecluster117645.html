rose-colored

adjective
overly optimistic; unrealistically hopeful
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊz ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊz ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
Having an unduly optimistic or hopeful outlook; viewing things in an unrealistically positive way.

She tends to have a rose-colored view of every situation, even when things are difficult.
