Iguania

noun
a group of lizards including iguanas and chameleons
Pronunciation: / ɪˈɡweɪniə / (AmE), / ɪˈɡweɪniə / (BrE)
a large group of lizards, including iguanas, anoles, chameleons, and related species, primarily found in the Americas and Madagascar

Examples

A majority of lizards seen in Central and South America belong to the group known as Iguania.
