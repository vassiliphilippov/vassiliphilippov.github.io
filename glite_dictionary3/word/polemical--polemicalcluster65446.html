polemical

adjective
controversial or argumentative
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / pəˈlɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
involving strongly critical, controversial, or disputatious writing or speech

Examples

polemical article
Her polemical essay challenged the policies of the current administration.

The debate quickly turned polemical as both sides began accusing each other of spreading misinformation.
