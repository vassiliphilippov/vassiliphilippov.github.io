Betty Friedan

proper noun
American feminist and writer (1921–2006)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛti friˈdæn / (AmE), / ˈbɛti friˈdan / (BrE)
(1921–2006) An American feminist, writer, and activist, best known for her book 'The Feminine Mystique' and for founding the National Organization for Women.
