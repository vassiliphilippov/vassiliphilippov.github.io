to poetize

verb
write poetry or make poetic
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊ.əˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpəʊ.ɪ.taɪz / (BrE)
to compose poetry or to express something in a poetic style; to turn into verse

Examples

to poetize about love
Although he usually writes novels, he sometimes likes to poetize about nature and love.
She began to poetize her daily experiences, turning the mundane into verse.
The ancient legends were poetized by medieval bards into beautiful ballads.
