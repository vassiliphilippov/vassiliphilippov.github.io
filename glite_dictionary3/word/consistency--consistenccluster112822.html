consistency

noun
uniformity; agreement among things or parts
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɪstənsi / (AmE), / kənˈsɪstənsi / (BrE)
the quality of always behaving or performing in a similar way, or of things being in agreement or harmony with each other

Examples

consistency in behavior
Good writing often depends on the consistency of style and voice throughout the text.
The consistency of his work impressed his managers and colleagues alike.
There needs to be consistency between the company's policies and its practices.

Related Terms

  • antonym – inconsistency: the quality of being inconsistent or lacking harmony
