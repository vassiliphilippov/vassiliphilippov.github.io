to throw

verb
cast a die or dice in a game
Pronunciation: / θroʊ / (AmE), / θrəʊ / (BrE)
to cast a die or dice onto a flat surface, especially as part of a game of chance

Examples

to throw dice
He threw a six and won the game.
It's your turn to throw the dice.
She picked up the die and threw it onto the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym – roll: to cause a die or dice to turn over as it moves along a surface
