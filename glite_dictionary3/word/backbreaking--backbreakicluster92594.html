backbreaking

adjective
requiring exhausting physical effort
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌbreɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbækˌbreɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
Requiring extremely hard physical effort, often to the point of exhaustion.

Examples

backbreaking work
Cleaning out the barn was a backbreaking task that took all day.
She found the construction work to be both dangerous and backbreaking.
The workers spent hours doing backbreaking labor in the fields.
