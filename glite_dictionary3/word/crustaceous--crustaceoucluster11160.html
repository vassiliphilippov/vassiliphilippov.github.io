crustaceous

adjective
having a hard outer shell or crust
Pronunciation: / krʌˈsteɪʃəs / (AmE), / krʌˈsteɪʃəs / (BrE)
having or resembling a hard outer shell or crust, especially like that of a crustacean.

Examples

crustaceous layer forms
Some soils in arid regions develop a crustaceous layer after heavy rainfall.
The artist depicted the crab’s crustaceous armor in vivid detail.
The snail’s crustaceous covering protects it from predators.

  • synonym – shell-like: resembling or having the qualities of a shell
