first-string

adjective
regular or starting member of a team
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝst ˌstrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˌstrɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a player or team) being among the main or regular members who usually start the game, not substitutes
