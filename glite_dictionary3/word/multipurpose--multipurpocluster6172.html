multipurpose

adjective
having multiple uses; adaptable
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈpɜːrpəs / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈpɜːpəs / (BrE)
Designed or suitable for use in several different ways or for several purposes.

Examples

multipurpose cleaner
She bought a multipurpose cleaner that works on glass, tiles, and metal surfaces.
The community center is a multipurpose building used for meetings, sports, and local events.
This multipurpose gadget can open bottles, cut wires, and even has a mini flashlight.

Related Terms

  • antonym – single-purpose: designed for only one use or function
