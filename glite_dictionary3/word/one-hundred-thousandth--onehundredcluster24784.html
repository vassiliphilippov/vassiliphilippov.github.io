one-hundred-thousandth

noun
one part in a hundred thousand; ordinal number
Pronunciation: / wʌn hʌndrəd ˈθaʊzəndθ / (AmE), / wʌn hʌndrəd ˈθaʊzəndθ / (BrE)
Each of one hundred thousand equal parts into which something can be divided; also, the ordinal number corresponding to one hundred thousand in a sequence.
