grinder

noun
large sandwich with meats and cheese (U.S.)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪndər / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪndə / (BrE)
a large sandwich made from a long crusty roll filled with meats, cheese, and various toppings; known by different names in different regions of the United States
