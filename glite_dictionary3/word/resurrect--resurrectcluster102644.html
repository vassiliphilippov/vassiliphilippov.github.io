to resurrect

verb
bring back to life or revive
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzəˈrɛkt / (AmE), / ˌrɛzəˈrɛkt / (BrE)
to bring someone or something back to life, existence, or active use after death, extinction, or disappearance
