decor

noun
style and furnishings of an interior
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪˌkɔr / (AmE), / ˈdeɪkɔː / (BrE)
The style, layout, and furnishings used to decorate the interior of a room or building, especially in terms of their overall effect.

Examples

modern decor
She admired the vintage decor of the old-fashioned café.
The modern decor in the hotel lobby created a welcoming atmosphere for guests.
Their new apartment's decor reflects a minimalist, Scandinavian style.

Related Terms

  • synonym – decoration: the process of decorating or the thing used to decorate
  • synonym – furnishings: the furniture, fittings, and decorative objects in a room or building
