unthinkingly

adverb
without thinking or consideration
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a lack of careful thought or consideration; without thinking

Examples

to act unthinkingly
He unthinkingly repeated the rumor to his friends.
She unthinkingly agreed to the plan without considering the consequences.
They unthinkingly threw away the documents they actually needed.
He apologized for his unthinkingly rude remarks at the meeting.
She unthinkingly agreed to the proposal, not realizing its consequences.
The comment was made unthinkingly, without any intention to offend.
