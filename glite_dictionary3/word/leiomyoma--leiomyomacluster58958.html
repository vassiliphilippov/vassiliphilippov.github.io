leiomyoma

noun
benign tumor of smooth muscle
Pronunciation: / laɪ.oʊ.maɪˈoʊ.mə / (AmE), / laɪ.əʊ.maɪˈəʊ.mə / (BrE)
a benign tumor composed of smooth muscle cells, most commonly found in the uterus or digestive tract

Examples

leiomyoma in uterus
A large leiomyoma can sometimes cause abdominal discomfort or pain.

Treatment options for a leiomyoma depend on its size and location.
