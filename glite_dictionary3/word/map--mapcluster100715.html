to map

verb
represent as if using a map
Pronunciation: / mæp / (AmE), / mæp / (BrE)
to depict or represent something as if using a map, especially to show details, features, or emotions clearly

Examples

map emotions clearly
Her determination was clearly mapped across the lines of her expression.
Sorrow was mapped on his face after hearing the tragic news.
The novelist mapped each character's journey with intricate emotional detail.
