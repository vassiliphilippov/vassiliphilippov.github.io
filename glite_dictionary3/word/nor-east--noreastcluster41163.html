nor'-east

noun
compass point between north and east
Pronunciation: / nɔrˈist / (AmE), / nɔːˈiːst / (BrE)
the compass point lying halfway between north and east, at an angle of 45 degrees

Examples

nor'-east wind direction
A cold breeze blew steadily from the nor'-east all morning.
Sailors adjusted their course to head nor'-east toward the next port.
The storm was approaching from the nor'-east, bringing heavy winds.
