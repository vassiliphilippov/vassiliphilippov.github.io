Tyrannidae

noun
family of New World tyrant flycatchers
Pronunciation: / tɪˈrænɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / tɪˈrænɪdiː / (BrE)
A large family of New World birds known as tyrant flycatchers, most numerous in Central and South America, also found in the United States and Canada.
