deviant

noun
person whose behavior is abnormal or unacceptable
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːviənt / (AmE), / ˈdiːvɪənt / (BrE)
a person whose behavior or morals differ markedly from what is considered normal or acceptable, especially in a sexual context

Examples

a deviant person
She argued that not every deviant is harmful to society.
Some people labeled him a deviant simply because of his unconventional lifestyle.
The novel explores the mind of a social deviant ostracized by the community.

Related Terms

  • antonym – conformist: a person who behaves in accordance with accepted standards
  • synonym – pervert: a person whose sexual behavior is considered abnormal and unacceptable
