crisp

noun
thin fried potato snack (British)
Pronunciation: / krɪsp / (AmE), / krɪsp / (BrE)
(British) A thin slice of potato that is fried until crisp and eaten cold as a snack.

Examples

packet of crisps
At the party, there were bowls filled with crisps of every flavor.
Do you prefer salt and vinegar or cheese and onion crisps?
