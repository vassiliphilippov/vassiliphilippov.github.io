to apostrophize

verb
use an apostrophe in writing
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑstrəˌfaɪz / (AmE), / əˈpɒstrəˌfaɪz / (BrE)
To mark or indicate with an apostrophe; to use an apostrophe in writing.

Examples

to apostrophize a word
Many people mistakenly apostrophize plurals, which is incorrect.
She had to apostrophize the contraction to show the missing letters.
When writing possessives, remember to apostrophize correctly.
