Mobula hypostoma

noun
small species of manta ray
Pronunciation: / moʊˈbjuːlə haɪˈpɑstəmə / (AmE), / məʊˈbjuːlə hʌɪˈpɒstəmə / (BrE)
A small species of manta ray, reaching up to four feet in width, that travels in schools.

Examples

Mobula hypostoma swims in groups
Researchers observed a large group of Mobula hypostoma swimming near the coral reef.
The Mobula hypostoma is known for its acrobatic leaps above the ocean surface.
Unlike larger mantas, the Mobula hypostoma rarely exceeds four feet in width.
