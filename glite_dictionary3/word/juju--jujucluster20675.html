juju

noun
magical power or charm
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuˌdʒu /; / ˈdʒuːˌdʒuː / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːdʒuː /; / ˈdʒuːˌdʒuː / (BrE)
a magical power or charm, especially in West African cultures; also, an object believed to have such power or bring good or bad luck
