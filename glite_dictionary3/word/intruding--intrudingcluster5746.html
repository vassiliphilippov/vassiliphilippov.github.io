intruding

adjective
projecting inward
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtruːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈtruːdɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an object or part) projecting inward into a space or cavity.

Examples

intruding mass in lung
