nonreturnable

adjective
not able or permitted to be returned
Pronunciation: / nɑn.rɪˈtɝn.ə.bəl / (AmE), / nɒn.rɪˈtɜː.nə.bəl / (BrE)
(of an item) not able or permitted to be returned to the seller or manufacturer for a refund, replacement, or deposit.

Examples

nonreturnable item policy
All clearance items are nonreturnable and cannot be exchanged or refunded.
Customers should check if the ticket is nonreturnable before making a purchase.
The store switched to nonreturnable bottles to simplify its recycling system.
