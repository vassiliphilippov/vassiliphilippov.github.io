Ficus rubiginosa

noun
Australian ornamental fig tree
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪkəs ˌruːbɪdʒəˈnoʊsə / (AmE), / ˈfaɪkəs ˌruːbɪdʒɪˈnəʊsə / (BrE)
An Australian fig tree often cultivated as an ornamental plant, recognized for its resemblance to the banyan and sometimes used for brushwood.
