flow

noun
state of deep absorption and focus
Pronunciation: / floʊ / (AmE), / fləʊ / (BrE)
a mental state of complete absorption and focused enjoyment in an activity, in which a person loses self-consciousness and often performs at their best.

Examples

in a flow state
She entered a deep flow while writing and finished the article in an hour.
Coaches teach athletes how to reach flow by setting clear goals and removing distractions.
When painters are in flow, time seems to disappear and their strokes become effortless.

Related Terms

  • antonym – distraction: something that prevents someone from giving full attention to a task
  • synonym – focus: concentrated attention on a particular task or subject
  • synonym – immersion: a state of being deeply involved or absorbed in something
