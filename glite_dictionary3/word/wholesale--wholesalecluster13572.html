wholesale

adverb
at a wholesale price; in bulk for resale
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊlˌseɪl / (AmE), / ˈhəʊlseɪl / (BrE)
At a wholesale price; in a manner involving the sale of goods in large quantities to be retailed by others.

Examples

buy wholesale for less
If you buy these shirts wholesale, you'll get a much better price.
Many stores purchase products wholesale and then mark them up for retail sale.
The supplier offered to sell the goods wholesale to small businesses.

Related Terms

  • antonym – retail: at a retail price; in small quantities to consumers
