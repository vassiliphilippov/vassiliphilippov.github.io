to initiate

verb
to start or begin something
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɪʃiˌeɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈnɪʃieɪt / (BrE)
to start or cause something to begin, such as a process, action, event, or discussion

Examples

to initiate a process

The scientist initiated a major research project last year.
She helped initiate several community projects during her time in office.
Talks to initiate a peace agreement are underway between the two countries.
The company decided to initiate a new marketing campaign this quarter.
New safety procedures were initiated after the incident.
She decided to initiate the discussion by asking a series of thoughtful questions.
The company plans to initiate a major expansion project next year.
