univalve

noun
mollusk with a single-piece shell
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːnɪˌvælv / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪˌvɑːlv / (BrE)
A mollusk, especially a gastropod, with a shell consisting of a single piece.

Examples
