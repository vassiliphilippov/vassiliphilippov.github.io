Antichrist

noun
the adversary of Christ in Christian prophecy
Pronunciation: / ˈæntiˌkraɪst / (AmE), / ˈæntɪˌkraɪst / (BrE)
(in Christian belief) the great adversary of Christ who is prophesied to appear before the end of the world, opposing Christ and deceiving many until ultimately defeated.
