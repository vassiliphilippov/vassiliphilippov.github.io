dimly

adverb
lacking interest or vitality
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪmli / (AmE), / ˈdɪmli / (BrE)
In a way that lacks interest, energy, or vitality.

Examples

dimly attended event
The party was dimly attended and lacked any real excitement.
The speech was delivered dimly, leaving the audience unmoved.
Their enthusiasm for the project faded, and progress continued dimly.
