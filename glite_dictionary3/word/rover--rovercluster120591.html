rover

noun
person who wanders with no settled home
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊvər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊvə / (BrE)
a person who wanders from place to place and has no settled home

Examples

wandering rover
After years as a rover, she finally decided to settle down and build a life in one city.
As a rover, he never stayed in one place for long, preferring the open road to a fixed home.
In the old tales, the rover traveled across continents, living a life of constant change.

Related Terms

  • synonym – nomad: a member of a people who move from place to place rather than settling permanently
  • antonym – settler: a person who settles in a new area and establishes a home
  • synonym – wanderer: a person who travels aimlessly or without a fixed destination
