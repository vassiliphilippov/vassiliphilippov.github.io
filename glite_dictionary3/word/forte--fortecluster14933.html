forte

adjective
musical direction: loudly
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔr.teɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.teɪ / (BrE)
(in music) played loudly; used as a direction or indication that a section should be performed at a high volume.

Examples

forte marking
Look for the forte markings in your sheet music and remember to increase your volume there.
She enjoyed the dramatic shift from piano to forte in the chorus of the song.
The conductor signaled that the next movement should be played forte for greater impact.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – fortissimo: (in music) very loud; a musical direction for very high volume
  • degree lower – mezzo-forte: (in music) moderately loud; a musical direction for medium-high volume
  • antonym – piano: (in music) played softly; a musical direction for quiet volume
