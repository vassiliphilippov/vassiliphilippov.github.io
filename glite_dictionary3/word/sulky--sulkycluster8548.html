sulky

noun
two-wheeled horse-drawn racing vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌlki / (AmE), / ˈsʌlki / (BrE)
a light two-wheeled vehicle for one person, typically drawn by one horse, used especially in harness racing

Examples

racing sulky cart
Many harness races feature drivers seated in sulkies behind their horses.
She admired the lightweight design of the sulky as it sped around the track.
The jockey climbed into the sulky and prepared for the race.
