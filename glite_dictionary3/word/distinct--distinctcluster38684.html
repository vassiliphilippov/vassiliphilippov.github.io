distinct

adjective
clearly different; separate
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɪŋkt / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪŋkt / (BrE)
clearly different or separate from something else; not the same

Examples

distinct from others

There is a distinct difference between these two proposals.

The colors in the painting are intentionally kept distinct to create a dramatic effect.
The committee operates as three distinct units, each with its own responsibilities.
