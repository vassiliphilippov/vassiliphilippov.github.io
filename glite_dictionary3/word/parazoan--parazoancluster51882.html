parazoan

noun
primitive multicellular marine animal (e.g., sponge)
Pronunciation: / ˌpær.əˈzoʊ.ən / (AmE), / ˌpar.əˈzəʊ.ən / (BrE)
a primitive multicellular marine animal, such as a sponge, whose body is porous and supported by a fibrous skeletal framework, usually forming sessile colonies

Examples
