to desexualize

verb
suppress or redirect sexual urges or traits
Pronunciation: / diˈsɛkʃuəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / diːˈsɛksjʊəlaɪz / (BrE)
To redirect or suppress sexual desires or characteristics, removing or neutralizing sexual content or urges.

Examples

to desexualize media content
He tried to desexualize his thoughts through meditation and mindfulness exercises.
Some therapies seek to desexualize problematic behaviors by redirecting urges into nonsexual activities.
The media often desexualizes certain body types by omitting any sexual representation.
