adoptable

adjective
suitable for adoption
Pronunciation: / əˈdɑptəbəl / (AmE), / əˈdɒptəbl / (BrE)
suitable or eligible to be adopted, especially by law or under official standards.

adoptable child

There is a growing list of children classified as adoptable by social services.
