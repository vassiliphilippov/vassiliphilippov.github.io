punchball

noun
bag for boxing training
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌntʃˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈpʌntʃˌbɔːl / (BrE)
A ball or bag, often inflated and suspended, used in boxing training by being punched repeatedly to improve speed and coordination.

Examples

punchball for boxing practice
She hung a punchball in the garage for boxing practice after work.
The boxer spent hours each day hitting the punchball to improve his reflexes.
The gym's new punchball is adjustable for different heights and training routines.
