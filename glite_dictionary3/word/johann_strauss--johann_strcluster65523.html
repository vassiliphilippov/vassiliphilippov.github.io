Johann Strauss

proper noun
Austrian composer of waltzes (1804–1849)
Pronunciation: / joʊˈhɑːn ˈstraʊs / (AmE), / jəʊˈhan ˈstraʊs / (BrE)
(1804–1849) an Austrian composer best known for his waltzes, often referred to as the 'father of the waltz'.
