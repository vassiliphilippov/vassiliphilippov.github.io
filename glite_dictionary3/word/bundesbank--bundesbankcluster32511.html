Bundesbank

proper noun
Germany's central bank
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊndəsˌbæŋk / (AmE), / ˈbʊndəsˌbaŋk / (BrE)
the central bank of Germany, responsible for managing the country's currency and monetary policy

the Bundesbank sets policy
