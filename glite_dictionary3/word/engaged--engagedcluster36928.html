engaged

adjective
of gears, interlocked and operating
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒd / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒd / (BrE)
(of toothed parts or gears) interlocked and actively working together.

Examples

engaged gears
Before starting the machine, make sure all the gears are engaged.
If the gears are not engaged properly, the engine could be damaged.
The technician confirmed that the transmission was engaged before proceeding with the test.

Related Terms

  • synonym – meshed: of gears, interlocked with another gear
