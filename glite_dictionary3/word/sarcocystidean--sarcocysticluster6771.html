sarcocystidean

noun
muscle parasite of vertebrates (protozoan)
Pronunciation: / ˌsɑrkoʊˌsɪsˈtɪdiən / (AmE), / ˌsɑːkəʊˌsɪsˈtɪdiən / (BrE)
