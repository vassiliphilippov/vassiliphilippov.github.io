dropper

noun
gaming device that drops players
Pronunciation: / c8dr51p5a / (AmE), / c8dr52p59 / (BrE)
In video games and level design, a device or mechanism that releases or drops players or in-game objects from a height, often to create a challenge, obstacle, or transition between areas.

Examples

game dropper device
The level designer placed a dropper at the top to send players into the challenge below.
Many Minecraft maps include steep droppers that test a player's timing and landing skills.
If you miss the moving platform, the dropper releases the player into a pit below.
