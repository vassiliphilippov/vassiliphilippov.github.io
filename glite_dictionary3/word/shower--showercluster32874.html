to shower

verb
spray or sprinkle with something
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊ.ɚ /; / ˈʃaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊ.ə /; / ˈʃaʊə / (BrE)
to spray or fall in large quantities, like rain or confetti

Examples

to shower the crowd
During the parade, children showered the streets with colorful streamers.
Fans showered the singer with gifts and flowers at the concert.
The bride and groom were showered with confetti as they left the church.
Confetti showered the crowd after the team won the championship.
During the meteor storm, hundreds of shooting stars showered across the night sky.
Leaves showered down from the trees with every gust of wind.
