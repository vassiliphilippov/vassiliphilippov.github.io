staunch

adjective
firm and loyal; dependable supporter
Pronunciation: / stɑntʃ / (AmE), / stɔːntʃ / (BrE)
firm and dependable, especially in supporting someone or something; loyal and strong in commitment.

Examples

staunch supporter
As a staunch friend, he always stood by me in difficult times.
Despite criticism, she remained a staunch supporter of the cause.
The senator is known as a staunch defender of civil liberties.

Related Terms

  • synonym – loyal: showing firm and constant support
  • synonym – steadfast: very firm and unwavering in support or belief
  • antonym – unreliable: not dependable or trustworthy
