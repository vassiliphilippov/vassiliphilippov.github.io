associate

noun
person involved jointly in an activity
Pronunciation: / əˈsoʊsiˌeɪt /; / əˈsoʊʃiˌeɪt / (AmE), / əˈsəʊsiˌeɪt / (BrE)
a person who works with or is connected to another person, such as a colleague, partner, or companion

Examples

business associate
Each associate has specific responsibilities within the joint project.
He discussed the proposal with his associate before making a decision.
Maya and her business associate opened a new restaurant downtown.
He introduced me to his longtime associate at the conference.
She considers him more than a colleague; he's a trusted associate.
The bar was filled with old friends and associates sharing stories.

Related Terms

  • synonym – colleague: a person with whom one works in a profession or business
  • synonym – companion: a person who accompanies or associates with another
  • synonym – friend: a person with whom one has a bond of mutual affection
  • synonym – partner: a person who takes part in an undertaking with another, especially in a business or company
  • antonym – rival: a person competing with another for the same objective
  • antonym – stranger: a person whom one does not know
