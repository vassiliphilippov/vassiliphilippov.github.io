bombardment

noun
exposure to high-energy particles
Pronunciation: / bɑmˈbɑrdmənt / (AmE), / bɒmˈbɑːdmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of subjecting a material or substance to the impact of high-energy particles, such as electrons or alpha rays, especially in scientific experiments.

Examples

bombardment by particles
After prolonged bombardment by neutrons, the isotope began to decay rapidly.
Scientists studied the effects of gamma ray bombardment on crystal structures.
The bombardment of the metal surface with alpha particles led to a new discovery.
