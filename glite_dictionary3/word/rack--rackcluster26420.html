to rack

verb
arrange or place items in a rack
Pronunciation: / ræk / (AmE), / ræk / (BrE)
to place or arrange items, especially balls in a game such as pool, in or on a rack

Examples

to rack pool balls
Before the game, he carefully racked all the balls on the pool table.
Please rack your weights after you finish exercising at the gym.
She racked the cues neatly at the end of the night.
