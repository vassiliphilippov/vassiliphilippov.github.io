to miter

verb
confer a miter on a bishop
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈmaɪtə / (BrE)
to confer a miter on (a bishop), especially at the time of consecration; to make someone a bishop.

Examples

to miter a bishop
It is a great honor to be mitered as a bishop in the cathedral.
The archbishop will miter the newly appointed clergyman during Sunday's service.
The ceremony to miter the candidate was attended by bishops from across the country.
