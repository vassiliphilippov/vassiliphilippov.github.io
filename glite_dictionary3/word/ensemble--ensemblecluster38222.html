ensemble

noun
whole formed by assembled parts
Pronunciation: / ɑnˈsɑmbəl / (AmE), / ɒnˈsɒmbəl / (BrE)
An assemblage of parts or details, especially in a work of art, regarded as forming a unified whole.

Examples

ensemble of details
Each detail in the sculpture contributes to the powerful ensemble seen by visitors.
The architect designed each room so that the ensemble creates a sense of harmony.
The painter’s attention to every element resulted in an ensemble that was both balanced and captivating.
