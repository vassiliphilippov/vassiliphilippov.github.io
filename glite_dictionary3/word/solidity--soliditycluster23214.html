solidity

noun
quality of being strong or reliable
Pronunciation: / səˈlɪdəti / (AmE), / səˈlɪdəti / (BrE)
the quality of being strong, reliable, or firmly established, especially in financial, factual, or moral terms

Examples

financial solidity
Her solidity in times of crisis is deeply reassuring to those around her.
Investors are attracted to the company's financial solidity.
The solidity of the evidence was enough to convince the jury of his innocence.
