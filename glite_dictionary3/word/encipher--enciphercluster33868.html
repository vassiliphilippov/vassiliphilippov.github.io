to encipher

verb
convert text into code
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsaɪfər / (AmE), / ɪnˈsʌɪfə / (BrE)
to convert (a message, text, or information) into a coded form so that it cannot be easily understood without the key

Examples

to encipher a message
During the war, soldiers had to encipher sensitive messages to keep them secret.
She used a complex algorithm to encipher the document, making it unreadable to unauthorized users.
The software will encipher your personal data before storing it on the server.

Related Terms

  • antonym – decipher: convert coded text back into ordinary language
  • synonym – encode: convert information into a particular form, often for secrecy
  • synonym – encrypt: secure data by converting it to a coded format
