disconnection

noun
lack of understanding or connection
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskəˈnɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪskəˈnɛkʃən / (BrE)
a lack of understanding or communication between people, groups, or ideas, often resulting in a sense of separation

Examples

disconnection between people
A disconnection between theory and practice can hinder real-world progress.
The film explores the disconnection people feel in modern urban life.
There was a profound disconnection between management and staff after the policy changes.

  • synonym – disconnect: a lack of connection or agreement, especially between ideas or people
