sou'-west

noun
compass point between south and west
Pronunciation: / saʊ ˈwɛst / (AmE), / saʊ ˈwɛst / (BrE)
The compass point lying halfway between south and west, corresponding to 225 degrees.

Examples

sou'-west direction
He set a course toward the sou'-west, hoping to reach the coast before nightfall.
The storm was blowing in from the sou'-west with increasing intensity.
The wind shifted to the sou'-west, bringing warmer air into the region.
