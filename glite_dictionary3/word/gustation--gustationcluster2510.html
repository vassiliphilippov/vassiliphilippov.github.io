gustation

noun
the sense of taste
Pronunciation: / ɡʌˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ɡʌsˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the sense or faculty of tasting, especially the ability to distinguish different flavors such as sweet, sour, bitter, and salty

Examples

gustation of flavors
Certain medications can affect gustation, making food taste bland or unusual.
Research into gustation has revealed much about how we perceive flavors.
Unlike smell, gustation usually requires direct contact with the substance.

  • synonym – taste: the sense that enables a person or animal to perceive the flavor of substances
