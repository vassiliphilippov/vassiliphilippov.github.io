acinar

adjective
relating to segments of aggregate fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈæsɪˌnɑr / (AmE), / ˈæsɪnɑː / (BrE)
Relating to the small segments or individual parts that together form an aggregate fruit, such as those found in a blackberry.
