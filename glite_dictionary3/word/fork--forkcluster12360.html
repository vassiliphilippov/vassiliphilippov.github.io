to fork

verb
attack two pieces at once (chess)
Pronunciation: / fɔrk / (AmE), / fɔːk / (BrE)
(in chess) to attack two or more enemy pieces at the same time with one move.

Examples

to fork two pieces
He managed to fork both my bishop and queen with a single move.
If you aren't careful, your opponent might fork your two major pieces.
The knight can fork the king and the rook on its next move.
