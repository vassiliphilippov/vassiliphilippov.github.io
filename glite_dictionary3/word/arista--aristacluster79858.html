arista

noun
bristle on insect antenna
Pronunciation: / əˈrɪstə / (AmE), / əˈrɪstə / (BrE)
a stiff, bristle-like structure at or near the tip of the antenna of certain flies and other insects

Examples

arista on a fly
Entomologists often examine the arista to help identify different species of flies.
The arista can vary in length and thickness among various fly families.
The housefly's arista is easily visible as a fine bristle at the tip of its antenna.
