unneighborly

adjective
not friendly or kind as a neighbor
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈneɪbɚli / (AmE), / ʌnˈneɪbəli / (BrE)
not friendly or considerate as a neighbor; lacking the helpfulness or goodwill expected from neighbors

Examples

unneighborly behavior
Her unneighborly attitude discouraged others from reaching out.
His unneighborly remarks made everyone at the meeting uncomfortable.
Leaving trash in the hallway is a very unneighborly thing to do.

Related Terms

  • antonym – neighborly: friendly and helpful as a neighbor
