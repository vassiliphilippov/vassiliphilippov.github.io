immodestly

adverb
in an immodest or boastful manner
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈmɑdəstli / (AmE), / ɪmˈmɒdɪstli / (BrE)
In a way that lacks modesty; in an immodest or boastful manner.

Examples

to speak immodestly
He immodestly titled his autobiography 'The Genius of Our Age'.
She spoke immodestly about her achievements, drawing critical glances from the audience.
The artist immodestly claimed his new painting would change the course of modern art.
