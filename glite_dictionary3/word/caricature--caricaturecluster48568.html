caricature

noun
exaggerated comic representation of a person
Pronunciation: / ˈkærɪkəˌtʃʊr / (AmE), / ˈkærɪkətjʊə / (BrE)
A drawing or description of someone that exaggerates certain features or characteristics in order to create a comic or grotesque effect.

Examples

caricature of a politician
She laughed at the caricature drawn by the street artist on her holiday in Paris.
The film's villain was more of a caricature than a realistic character.
The newspaper published a caricature of the mayor, highlighting his prominent chin and bushy eyebrows.

Related Terms

  • synonym – parody: an imitation with deliberate exaggeration for comic effect
  • antonym – portrait: a painting, drawing, or photograph of a person
  • synonym – satire: the use of humor or exaggeration to criticize
