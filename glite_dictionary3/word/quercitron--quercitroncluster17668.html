quercitron

noun
North American tree with dye-producing bark
Pronunciation: / kwɜːrˈsɪtrɑn / (AmE), / kwəːˈsɪtrɒn / (BrE)
a medium to large deciduous tree native to eastern North America, with dark outer bark and yellow inner bark used for dyeing and tanning
