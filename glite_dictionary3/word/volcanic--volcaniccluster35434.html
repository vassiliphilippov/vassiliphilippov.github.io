volcanic

adjective
prone to explosive outbursts (fig.)
Pronunciation: / vɑlˈkænɪk / (AmE), / vɒlˈkænɪk / (BrE)
(of a person or feeling) liable to erupt suddenly or violently; explosively unstable.

Examples

volcanic anger
Even small annoyances could trigger his volcanic outbursts.
His volcanic personality often made social gatherings unpredictable.
She had a volcanic temper that frightened everyone in the office.
