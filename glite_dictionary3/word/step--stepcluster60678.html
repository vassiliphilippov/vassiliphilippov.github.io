step

noun
block in a ship holding the mast's base
Pronunciation: / stɛp / (AmE), / stɛp / (BrE)
A solid block fixed to a ship's beams, in which the heel of a mast or capstan is set.

Examples

mast step on ship
A worn step can cause instability in the mast during strong winds.
The shipwright checked the step before raising the new mast.
They repaired the step to ensure the capstan was firmly mounted.
