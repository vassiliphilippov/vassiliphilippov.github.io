cold

adjective
completely unconscious
Pronunciation: / koʊld / (AmE), / kəʊld / (BrE)
completely unconscious, especially as a result of a blow, shock, or intoxication.

Examples

knocked cold
After the accident, he was knocked cold and didn’t wake up for several minutes.
She drank so much that she passed out cold on the couch.
The fighter was hit so hard that he went cold before hitting the mat.
