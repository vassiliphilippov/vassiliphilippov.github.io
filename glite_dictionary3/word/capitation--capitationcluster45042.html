capitation

noun
fixed per-person tax or payment
Pronunciation: / ˌkæpɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkæpɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a tax or payment of a fixed amount per person, regardless of income or resources

Examples

capitation tax payment
A capitation fee was imposed, making everyone contribute equally to the fund.
Some healthcare providers receive payment on a capitation basis rather than for each service provided.
The government introduced a capitation tax that required every adult to pay the same amount annually.
