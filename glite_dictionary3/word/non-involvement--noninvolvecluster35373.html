non-involvement

noun
the state of not participating
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪnˈvɑlvmənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪnˈvɒlvmənt / (BrE)
the state or fact of not taking part in or being connected with something, especially the activities of a group

Examples

non-involvement in a conflict
He explained his non-involvement in the controversy during the interview.
Her non-involvement in the project surprised her colleagues.
The company maintained strict non-involvement in political affairs.

Related Terms

  • synonym – detachment: the state of being objective or aloof
  • antonym – participation: the action of taking part in something
