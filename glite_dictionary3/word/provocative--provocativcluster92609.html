provocative

adjective
causing strong reaction or stimulating discussion
Pronunciation: / prəˈvɑkətɪv / (AmE), / prəˈvɒkətɪv / (BrE)
Causing annoyance, anger, or another strong reaction; stimulating thought, discussion, or excitement.

Examples

provocative question
He gave her a provocative smile that made her blush.
Her provocative comments sparked a heated debate among the panelists.
The artist's provocative work challenged traditional ideas about beauty.
