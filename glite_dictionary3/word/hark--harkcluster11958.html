to hark

verb
listen carefully (imperative)
Pronunciation: / hɑrk / (AmE), / hɑːk / (BrE)
listen carefully; used to draw someone's attention to a sound, especially in commands or exclamations

Examples

hark to a sound
Hark to the thunder rolling over the hills tonight.
Hark! The distant bells are ringing in the valley.
She told her children to hark to the sounds of nature on their walk.

  • synonym – listen: give one's attention to a sound
