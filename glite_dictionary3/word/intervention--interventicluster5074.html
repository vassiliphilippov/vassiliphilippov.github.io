intervention

noun
action taken to help a situation, especially medically
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.tɚˈvɛn.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈvɛn.ʃən / (BrE)
the action of intervening, especially treatment or actions taken to improve a situation or relieve illness or injury

Examples

medical intervention
Early intervention can significantly improve outcomes for children with developmental delays.
The committee discussed possible interventions to address the rising number of infections.
The doctor recommended a surgical intervention to treat the patient's condition.
