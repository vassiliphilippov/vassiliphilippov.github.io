crucifix

noun
cross with figure of Jesus
Pronunciation: / ˈkruː.səˌfɪks / (AmE), / ˈkruː.sɪ.fɪks / (BrE)
A representation of a cross with a figure of Jesus Christ on it, used especially as a symbol in Christian churches and devotion.

Examples

wooden crucifix on wall
A large crucifix hung on the wall above the altar in the old church.
He wore a silver crucifix around his neck as a sign of his faith.
The artist carefully carved a wooden crucifix for the local parish.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cross: an upright post with a transverse bar, used especially as a Christian symbol
  • synonym – crucifixion: the execution of a person by nailing or binding them to a cross
