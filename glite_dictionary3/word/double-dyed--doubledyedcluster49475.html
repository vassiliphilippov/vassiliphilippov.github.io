double-dyed

adjective
fabric dyed twice or in two colors
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌb.əl.daɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʌb.əl.daɪd / (BrE)
(of fabric) dyed twice to achieve greater depth of color or dyed using two different colors.

Examples

double-dyed fabric
The artisan uses a double-dyed method to create unique fabric designs.
This shirt is double-dyed to ensure the color lasts even after many washes.
You can notice the difference in tone between regular and double-dyed materials.
