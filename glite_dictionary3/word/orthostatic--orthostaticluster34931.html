orthostatic

adjective
relating to upright posture
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrθəˈstætɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəˈstætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or caused by an upright standing posture, especially in reference to physiological effects or medical conditions.

Examples

orthostatic hypotension
He was diagnosed with orthostatic hypotension after repeatedly feeling lightheaded upon standing.
Some people experience orthostatic dizziness when they stand up quickly from a seated position.
The nurse checked for orthostatic changes in blood pressure after the patient complained of faintness.
