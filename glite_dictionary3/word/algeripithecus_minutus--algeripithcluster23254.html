Algeripithecus minutus

noun
extinct primate species from Algeria
Pronunciation: / ælˌdʒɛrəˌpɪˈθikəs maɪˈnuːtəs / (AmE), / ælˌdʒɛrɪpɪˈθiːkəs mɪˈnjuːtəs / (BrE)
a tiny extinct primate species that lived about 46 to 50 million years ago in what is now Algeria, considered by some as a possible earliest anthropoid
