flight

noun
unit in air force smaller than a squadron
Pronunciation: / flaɪt / (AmE), / flaɪt / (BrE)
a unit in an air force that is smaller than a squadron, typically consisting of several aircraft and personnel

Examples

air force flight
Each flight consists of several aircraft as well as ground crew members.
He was promoted to lead the flight during his second year in the air force.
The flight was tasked with conducting patrols along the eastern border.
