to bop

verb
to hit or strike hard
Pronunciation: / bɑp / (AmE), / bɒp / (BrE)

Examples

to bop someone playfully

If you don't pay attention, the ball might bop you right in the nose.
She bopped the punching bag with surprising strength during her workout.
