heelbone

noun
bone forming the heel (calcaneus)
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːlˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈhiːlˌbəʊn / (BrE)
the largest bone in the tarsus, forming the prominence of the human heel; also known as the calcaneus.

Examples

broken heelbone
After her fall, she limped heavily because her heelbone was bruised.
An X-ray showed a small spur on his heelbone, which was causing him discomfort.
The doctor confirmed that the fracture was in the heelbone and recommended a cast for six weeks.
