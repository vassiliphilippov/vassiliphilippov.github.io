blowback

noun
backward escape of gases from a gun
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈbləʊˌbæk / (BrE)
the backward escape of gases and unburned gunpowder from a firearm after it is discharged.

Examples

blowback from a gun
After pulling the trigger, the shooter noticed some blowback from the side vent.
Engineers redesigned the chamber to reduce blowback and improve safety.
The old rifle suffered from excessive blowback whenever it was fired.
