tutorial

noun
short online instructional guide or video
Pronunciation: / tuˈtɔriəl, tjuˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / tjuːˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
a short instructional video or written guide (especially online) that explains how to do a particular task or use a piece of software.

Examples

online tutorial video
I followed an online tutorial to assemble the bookshelf for my living room.
The app includes a short tutorial that shows how to edit photos step by step.
He watched several YouTube tutorials before trying to replace the laptop battery himself.

Related Terms

  • synonym – guide: a book, film, or online page that explains how to do something
  • synonym – how-to: a short guide explaining how to do something
