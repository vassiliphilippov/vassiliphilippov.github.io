shakable

adjective
able to be weakened or disturbed
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪkəbl / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪkəbl / (BrE)
Able to be weakened or disturbed; not stable or secure.

Examples

shakable confidence
Her once firm beliefs turned out to be shakable under pressure.
The manager's authority proved surprisingly shakable during the heated debate.
Their trust in the new system was clearly shakable after the recent failures.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fragile: easily broken or damaged
  • antonym – unshakable: impossible to weaken or disturb
