to convert

verb
score extra points after a touchdown (in football)
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɝt / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːt / (BrE)
(in American and Canadian football) to score an extra point or points after a touchdown by kicking the ball through the uprights or advancing the ball into the end zone.
