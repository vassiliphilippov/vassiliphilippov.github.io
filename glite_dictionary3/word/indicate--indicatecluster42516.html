to indicate

verb
state or express briefly
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to state or express something briefly or clearly

Examples

indicate your choice

She indicated her approval with a quick nod.
The instructions clearly indicate what steps to take next.
