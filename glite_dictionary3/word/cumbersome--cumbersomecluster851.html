cumbersome

adjective
hard to handle due to size or weight
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmbərsəm / (AmE), / ˈkʌmbəsəm / (BrE)
large or heavy and therefore difficult to carry, handle, or use; unwieldy.

Examples

cumbersome suitcase
She found the suitcases cumbersome to carry through the crowded airport.
The old printer was too cumbersome to fit on his small desk.
The procedure was so cumbersome that they decided to update their entire system.

Related Terms

  • synonym – unwieldy: difficult to move or use because of size, shape, or weight
