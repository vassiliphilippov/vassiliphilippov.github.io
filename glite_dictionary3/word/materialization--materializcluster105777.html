materialization

noun
appearance of something in bodily form
Pronunciation: / məˌtɪriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / məˌtɪəriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process by which something, especially a spirit, appears in bodily or tangible form

Examples

materialization of a ghost
The materialization of the apparition was accompanied by a sudden drop in temperature.
There are many stories of the materialization of ghosts during séances.
Witnesses claimed that the materialization of the spirit startled everyone in the room.
