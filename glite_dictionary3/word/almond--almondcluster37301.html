almond

noun
tree that produces edible nuts
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlmənd / (AmE), / ˈɑːmənd / (BrE)
a small deciduous tree native to Asia and North Africa, cultivated for its pink blossoms and edible nuts enclosed in a hard green hull

Examples
