being

noun
a living or sentient creature
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbiːɪŋ / (BrE)
a living creature; a sentient or existing entity, especially one capable of independent action or thought.

Examples

a living being
Some people believe there may be intelligent beings elsewhere in the universe.
The ancient legend spoke of a mysterious being that protected the forest from harm.
The scientist studied the behavior of every living being in the ecosystem.

Related Terms

  • synonym – creature: a living animal, especially an animal as distinct from a human being
  • synonym – entity: something that exists as a distinct thing
  • antonym – object: a thing that can be seen or touched; often contrasted with 'being' as lifeless rather than living
