principal

noun
main party in financial transaction
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪnsəpəl / (AmE), / ˈprɪnsəpəl /; / ˈprɪnsɪpəl / (BrE)
a person who has the highest authority or plays the main role in a place, activity, or situation (such as a school, a crime, a business deal, or a performance)
