remise

noun
fencing: second thrust on same lunge
Pronunciation: / rəˈmiz / (AmE), / rɪˈmiːz / (BrE)
In fencing, a second thrust made on the same lunge, typically when an opponent fails to riposte.

Examples

fencing remise attack
A well-placed remise can catch an inattentive opponent off guard.
After parrying his attack, she quickly executed a remise when he failed to defend himself.
The coach praised his perfect timing on the remise during the final bout.
