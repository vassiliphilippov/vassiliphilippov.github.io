pushing

noun
the act of exerting force to move something
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of applying force to move something away from oneself or in a particular direction

Examples

pushing a heavy cart
Pushing a heavy cart up the hill took all of their strength.
After some pushing, the car finally rolled out of the mud.
Regular pushing and pulling are part of her workout routine.

Related Terms

  • antonym – pulling: the act of applying force to move something closer
