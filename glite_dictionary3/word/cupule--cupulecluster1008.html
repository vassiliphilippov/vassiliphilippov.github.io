cupule

noun
cup-shaped structure at the base of an acorn
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuː.pjuːl / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.pjuːl / (BrE)
A cup-shaped structure of hardened bracts at the base of an acorn or similar fruit, enclosing or supporting it.

Examples

cupule of acorn
Botanists examined the cupule structure to identify the species of oak.
Each acorn is held in place by a woody cupule at its base.
The cupule protects the developing acorn until it is mature.
