paneled

adjective
fitted with panels or wainscoting
Pronunciation: / ˈpænəld / (AmE), / ˈpænəld / (BrE)
fitted or decorated with panels, especially of wood; having panels or wainscoting as part of its design.

Examples

paneled wooden walls

The grand library featured paneled walls and a marble fireplace.
They restored the old house, keeping its beautifully paneled doors intact.
