sixth

noun
the position number six in a sequence
Pronunciation: / sɪksθ / (AmE), / sɪksθ / (BrE)
The position in a sequence that comes after the fifth and before the seventh; one of six equal parts.

Examples

sixth place

One sixth of the pie was left after the party.
