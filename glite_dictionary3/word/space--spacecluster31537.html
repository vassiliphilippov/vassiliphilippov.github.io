to space

verb
arrange things at intervals
Pronunciation: / speɪs / (AmE), / speɪs / (BrE)
to arrange or set things apart from each other at intervals

Examples

to space chairs evenly
Please space the chairs evenly around the table.
She spaced the events throughout the day to avoid feeling rushed.
Trees should be spaced at least six feet apart for optimal growth.
