wellbeing

noun
state of being healthy and happy
Pronunciation: / wɛlˈbiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌwɛlˈbiːɪŋ / (BrE)
A state of being comfortable, healthy, or happy.

Examples

mental wellbeing
Meditation can greatly improve a person's sense of wellbeing.
Physical exercise is essential for your overall wellbeing.
The government is responsible for the social wellbeing of its citizens.
