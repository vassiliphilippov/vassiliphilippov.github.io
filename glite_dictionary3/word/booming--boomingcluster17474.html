booming

adjective
deep, resonant, and loud (voice or sound)
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbuːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a voice or sound) deep and resonant; loud and echoing.

Examples

booming voice
A booming sound echoed from the distant cliffs as thunder rumbled overhead.
His booming voice could be heard across the crowded hall.
The booming laughter filled the empty auditorium.

Related Terms

  • antonym – muffled: (of a sound) not loud because of being obstructed in some way
  • synonym – resonant: (of a sound) deep, clear, and continuing to sound or ring
