to roll

verb
emit a deep, prolonged sound
Pronunciation: / roʊl / (AmE), / rəʊl / (BrE)
to emit or produce a deep, prolonged, reverberating sound, as thunder or drums do

Examples

to roll like thunder
A low, ominous sound rolled through the valley.
Drums rolled as the parade approached the square.
The distant thunder began to roll across the mountains.
