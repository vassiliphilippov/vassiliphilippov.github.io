Secotiaceae

noun
family of fungi with stalk, cap, and wrinkled gleba
Pronunciation: / sɛˌkoʊʃiˈeɪsiː /; / sɛˌkoʊtiˈeɪsiː / (AmE), / sɛˌkəʊtɪˈeɪsiː /; / sɛˌkəʊtɪˈeɪsɪiː / (BrE)
a family of fungi with a stalk and cap, and a wrinkled mass of tissue (gleba) where spores are produced; often considered deformed or misshapen forms of other fungi
