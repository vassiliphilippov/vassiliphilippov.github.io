upgrade

noun
an improved reservation or service
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈʌpɡreɪd / (BrE)
an improvement or the act of raising something to a higher standard, quality, or status (such as a better seat, service, or condition)

Examples

upgrade to business class
After arriving at the airport, I was offered an upgrade to business class.
She was surprised to find her hotel upgrade included a suite with an ocean view.
Thanks to her frequent flyer status, he often receives a complimentary upgrade.
After the upgrade, the train's engines were much more efficient and powerful.
Many customers complained until the company finally offered a free upgrade to the product.
The latest software upgrade fixed several bugs and added new features.

Related Terms

  • antonym – downgrade: a reservation or status that has been lowered in quality or importance
  • synonym – improvement: the process of making something better
