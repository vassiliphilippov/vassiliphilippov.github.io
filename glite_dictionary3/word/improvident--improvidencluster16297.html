improvident

adjective
lacking foresight; not providing for the future
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈprɑvədənt /; / ɪmˈprɑːvɪdənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɒvɪdənt / (BrE)
not planning or caring for the future; spending or acting without thinking about future needs

Examples

improvident spending habits
