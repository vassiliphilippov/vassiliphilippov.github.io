tyre

noun
rubber covering on a wheel
Pronunciation: / taɪər / (AmE), / taɪə / (BrE)
A thick rubber covering, typically filled with air, that fits around the outer edge of a wheel on a vehicle to provide traction and cushion the ride.

Examples

tyre pressure
It's important to check your car's tyre pressure regularly for safe driving.
