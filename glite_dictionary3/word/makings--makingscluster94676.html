makings

noun
qualities indicating potential
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪkɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈmeɪkɪŋz / (BrE)
the qualities or attributes that are likely to enable someone to become or achieve something; potential

Examples

makings of a leader
He showed all the makings of a successful entrepreneur even as a teenager.
The coach said that Sarah has the makings of an Olympic champion.
With her leadership skills, she has the makings of a great manager.
