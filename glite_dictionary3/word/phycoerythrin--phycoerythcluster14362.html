phycoerythrin

noun
red pigment in red algae
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪkoʊˈɛrɪθrɪn / (AmE), / ˌfʌɪkəʊˈɛrɪθrɪn / (BrE)
a red protein pigment found in red algae, involved in photosynthesis by capturing light energy

Examples

phycoerythrin in red algae
Researchers are studying how phycoerythrin helps red algae absorb light at different ocean depths.

The extraction of phycoerythrin from algae is important for scientific and industrial applications.
