ludo

noun
board game with dice and counters
Pronunciation: / ˈluːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈluːdəʊ / (BrE)
a simple board game, played on a square board, in which players race their counters around the board based on rolls of a die, aiming to get all their pieces home before the others

Examples

play ludo with friends
Every family gathering, someone insists on bringing out ludo for a game.
She taught her younger brother how to play ludo last weekend.
We played ludo with the children on a rainy afternoon.
