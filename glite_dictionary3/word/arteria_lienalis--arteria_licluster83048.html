arteria lienalis

noun
artery supplying blood to the spleen
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪr.i.ə liːˈæn.əlɪs / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪə.rɪə liːˈeɪ.nə.lɪs / (BrE)
the artery that branches from the celiac trunk and supplies blood to the spleen; also called the splenic artery
