stent

noun
mesh tube to keep a vessel open
Pronunciation: / stɛnt / (AmE), / stɛnt / (BrE)
a small mesh tube inserted into a body passage, such as a blood vessel, to keep it open.

Examples

insert a stent
After receiving a stent, the patient was able to walk without chest pain.
Drug-eluting stents are commonly used to help prevent arteries from closing again.
The surgeon used a stent to open the patient's blocked artery.
