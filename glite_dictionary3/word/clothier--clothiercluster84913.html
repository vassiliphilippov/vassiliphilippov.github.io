clothier

noun
seller of clothing; men's clothing merchant
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊðiər / (AmE), / ˈkləʊðɪə / (BrE)
a person or merchant who sells clothing, especially men's clothing

Examples

clothier sells suits
He apprenticed with a prominent clothier in the city before opening his own shop.
Many wealthy clients depended on their trusted clothier for the latest fashions.
The clothier offered a fine selection of suits and dress shirts.
