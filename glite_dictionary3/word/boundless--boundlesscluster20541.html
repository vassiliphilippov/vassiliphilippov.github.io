boundless

adjective
without limits or boundaries
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊndlɪs / (AmE), / ˈbaʊndlɪs / (BrE)
seeming to have no boundaries; unlimited in amount, number, degree, or extent.

Examples

boundless energy
Her boundless curiosity led her to explore every corner of the library.
The desert stretched before us in boundless silence.
They were filled with boundless optimism about the future.
