to present

verb
to salute or take a prescribed position (military)
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzɛnt / (AmE), / prɪˈzɛnt / (BrE)
(of a soldier) to make a salute or adopt a prescribed position in accordance with military regulations.

Examples

to present arms
Guards are trained to present with precision during state ceremonies.
The soldiers present their arms as the general enters the parade ground.
Upon the command, every cadet must present himself at attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym – salute: to make a formal gesture of respect, especially in the military
  • synonym – stand at attention: to assume a prescribed upright posture for military courtesy
