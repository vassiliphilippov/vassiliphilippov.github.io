l

noun
abbreviation for litre (metric unit of capacity)
Pronunciation: / ɛl / (AmE), / ɛl / (BrE)
abbreviation for litre or liter: a metric unit of capacity equal to 1,000 cubic centimeters or approximately 1.75 pints.

Examples

2 l of water
She drank more than two l of water after the run.
The bottle was marked with a 1 l capacity label.
The car's fuel tank holds 50 l of petrol.
