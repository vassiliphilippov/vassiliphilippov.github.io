pouring

adjective
flowing rapidly and in large quantities
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a liquid) flowing rapidly and in large quantities; gushing.

Examples

pouring rain
A pouring stream rushed through the canyon after the storm.
The pouring rain made it impossible to see the road ahead.
Water was pouring from the broken pipe, flooding the basement.
