iron

noun
type of golf club with a metal head
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪɚn / (AmE), / ˈaɪən / (BrE)
A type of golf club with a relatively narrow, flat metal head, used for making shots from the fairway or rough.

Examples

golf iron
He chose a seven iron to hit the ball onto the green.
Many beginner golfers find it easier to use an iron rather than a wood.
She kept a full set of irons in her golf bag for different shots.

Related Terms

  • antonym – wood: a type of golf club with a large rounded wooden or metal head, used for longer shots
