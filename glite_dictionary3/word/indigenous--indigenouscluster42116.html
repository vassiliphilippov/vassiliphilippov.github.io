indigenous

adjective
native to a particular place
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɪdʒənəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪdʒɪnəs / (BrE)
originating or occurring naturally in a particular place; native

Examples

indigenous plants
Many traditional foods are made with plants indigenous to the region.
The koala is indigenous to Australia and is not found living in the wild elsewhere.
These customs remain important to the indigenous communities who have lived here for centuries.

Related Terms

  • antonym – nonindigenous: not originating in the place where found; foreign
