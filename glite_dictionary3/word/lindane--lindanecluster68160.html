lindane

noun
insecticide and medicinal compound
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪnˌdeɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɪndeɪn / (BrE)
a white crystalline compound used as an agricultural insecticide and in some medicinal preparations to treat lice and scabies

Examples
