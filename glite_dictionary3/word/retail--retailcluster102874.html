retail

adverb
at a retail price or by retail
Pronunciation: / ˈriː.teɪl / (AmE), / ˈriː.teɪl / (BrE)
at a retail price or by retail; in the manner of selling to individual customers rather than wholesale.

Examples

sold retail only

Many items are available both wholesale and retail.
We can supply the product retail to individual customers.
