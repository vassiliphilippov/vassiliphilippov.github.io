irascibility

noun
tendency to become easily angry
Pronunciation: / ɪˌræsəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ɪˌrasɪˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being easily angered or prone to outbursts of temper

Examples

his irascibility showed
Despite his irascibility, he was respected for his honesty and directness.
Her irascibility made it difficult for colleagues to approach her with feedback.
Moments of irascibility were rare, but when they happened, everyone noticed.

Related Terms

  • antonym – calmness: the quality of being calm and not easily angered
  • synonym – irritability: the quality of being easily irritated or annoyed
