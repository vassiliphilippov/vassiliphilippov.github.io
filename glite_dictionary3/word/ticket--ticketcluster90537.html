ticket

noun
label attached to show details or ownership
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
a small label made of paper, cardboard, or plastic, attached to an object to indicate its owner, nature, price, or other information

Examples

price ticket label
A small ticket on the vase indicated it was fragile.
Every piece of luggage was marked with a ticket for identification.
The shirt still had its ticket attached, showing the size and price.
