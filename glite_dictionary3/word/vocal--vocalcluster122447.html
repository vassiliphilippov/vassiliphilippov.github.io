vocal

adjective
outspoken; openly expressive
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈvəʊ.kəl / (BrE)
expressing opinions or feelings freely and insistently, especially in public; outspoken

Examples

vocal critic

The committee grew more vocal in its demands for better working conditions.
