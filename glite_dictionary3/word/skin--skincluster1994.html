skin

member of skinhead subculture (slang)
Pronunciation: / skɪn / (AmE), / skɪn / (BrE)
(slang) A member of a subculture, primarily in Britain and the US, characterized by a shaved head, often associated with skinheads; sometimes linked to racist or violent behavior, though not all members share these views.
