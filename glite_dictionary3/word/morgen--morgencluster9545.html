morgen

noun
South African land area unit (~2 acres)
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrɡən / (AmE), / ˈmɔːɡən / (BrE)
a unit of land measurement used in South Africa, equal to approximately two acres (0.8 hectares)

Examples

a morgen of land
He purchased an additional ten morgens to expand his vineyard.
The farm covers 120 morgens of fertile land.
The surveyor measured the property at just over five morgen.
