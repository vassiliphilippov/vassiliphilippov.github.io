Pediculus corporis

noun
parasitic body louse species
Pronunciation: / pəˈdɪkjələs ˈkɔrpərɪs / (AmE), / pɛˈdɪkjʊləs ˈkɔːpərɪs / (BrE)
A species of parasitic louse that infests the human body, commonly known as the body louse.
