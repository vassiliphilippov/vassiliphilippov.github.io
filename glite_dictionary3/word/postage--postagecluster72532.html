postage

noun
money charged for mailing
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstɪdʒ / (BrE)
the amount of money required to send a letter or package by mail

Examples

postage cost
Make sure you put enough postage on the envelope before mailing it.
She forgot to pay the postage, so her letter was returned.
The postage for international packages is much higher than for domestic mail.
