protection

noun
support, endorsement, or guidance
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛk.ʃən /; / prəˈtɛkʃən / (AmE), / prəˈtɛk.ʃən /; / prəˈtɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act or state of keeping someone or something safe from harm, danger, or loss; support, covering, or financial security that prevents damage or injury
