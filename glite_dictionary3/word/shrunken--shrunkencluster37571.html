shrunken

adjective
reduced in size or intensity
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrʌŋ.kən / (AmE), / ˈʃrʌŋ.kən / (BrE)
Made or having become reduced in size, strength, effect, or intensity.

Examples

shrunken profits
Consumers felt the impact of the shrunken value of the dollar.
She looked at the shrunken profits in disappointment.
The region's shrunken economy led to higher unemployment rates.
