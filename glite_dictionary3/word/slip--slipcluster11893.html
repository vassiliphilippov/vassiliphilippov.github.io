to slip

verb
move sideways or slide unintentionally
Pronunciation: / slɪp / (AmE), / slɪp / (BrE)
to slide or move sideways, often unintentionally or out of control

Examples

to slip on a slope
He lost his balance, causing his foot to slip sideways down the muddy slope.
If you walk too fast on the wet floor, you might slip and fall.
The tires began to slip on the icy road as we turned the corner.
