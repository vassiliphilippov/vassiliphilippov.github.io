unrecognisable

adjective
not able to be recognized
Pronunciation: / ʌnˌrɛkəɡˈnaɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˌrɛkəɡˈnaɪzəbəl / (BrE)
Not able to be recognized, often because of significant damage, change, or alteration.

Examples

almost unrecognisable after accident
After her dramatic makeover, she was almost unrecognisable from her previous self.
The city skyline became unrecognisable after years of rapid development.
The old factory was so badly burned it was completely unrecognisable to former workers.
