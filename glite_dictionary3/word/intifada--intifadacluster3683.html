intifada

noun
Palestinian uprising against Israeli occupation
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɪˈfɑdə / (AmE), / ˌɪntɪˈfɑːdə / (BrE)
an uprising or rebellion, especially the Palestinian uprisings against Israeli occupation in the Gaza Strip and West Bank beginning in the late 1980s and in 2000

Examples

the intifada began
His book provides firsthand accounts from those who participated in the intifada.
Many historians analyze the causes and effects of the intifada in their studies of the Middle East.
The first intifada lasted from 1987 to 1993 and had a profound impact on the region.
