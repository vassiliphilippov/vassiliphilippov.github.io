germane

adjective
relevant and appropriate
Pronunciation: / dʒɚˈmeɪn / (AmE), / dʒəˈmeɪn / (BrE)
relevant to a subject under consideration; appropriate to the matter at hand.

Examples

germane to the topic
Her comments were not germane to the topic we were discussing.
Only evidence that is germane to the case will be considered by the judge.
The committee welcomed suggestions that were truly germane to their mission.

Related Terms

  • antonym – irrelevant: not connected with or relevant to something
  • synonym – pertinent: relevant or applicable to a particular matter
