sport

noun
a person who shows good nature under challenge
Pronunciation: / spɔrt / (AmE), / spɔːt / (BrE)
a person who behaves in a cheerful, tolerant, or good-natured way when teased, defeated, or facing difficulties

Examples

good sport
Even though he lost the game, Tom was a real sport about it.
She's a good sport and never gets upset when jokes are played on her.
Thanks for being such a sport and going along with our surprise.
