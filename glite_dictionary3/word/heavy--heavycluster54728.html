heavy

adjective
compact, fine-grained (of soil)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvi / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi / (BrE)
having great weight, density, or thickness; difficult to lift, move, or work with (used for objects, substances, people, fabric, soil, etc.)
