degradation

noun
decline to a lower, less respected state
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛɡ.rəˈdeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛɡ.rəˈdeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process or state of being reduced to a lower, less respected, or less desirable condition

Examples

environmental degradation
Environmental degradation threatens the health and livelihoods of many communities.
He felt a sense of degradation after losing his job and status.
The degradation of the city's infrastructure became apparent after years of neglect.

Related Terms

  • synonym – debasement: the act of lowering in status, value, or quality
  • synonym – humiliation: the act of making someone feel ashamed or lose respect
  • antonym – improvement: the process of becoming better or more desirable
