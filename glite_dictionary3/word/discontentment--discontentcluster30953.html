discontentment

noun
state of dissatisfaction or longing
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskənˈtɛntmənt / (AmE), / ˌdɪskənˈtɛntmənt / (BrE)
A feeling of dissatisfaction or unhappiness with the current situation; a longing for something better.

Examples

deep discontentment
He tried to hide his discontentment, but his unhappiness was obvious to everyone.
Her constant discontentment with her job eventually led her to look for new opportunities.
There was a general air of discontentment among the students over the new policies.

Related Terms

  • antonym – contentment: a state of happiness and satisfaction
  • synonym – dissatisfaction: lack of satisfaction with something
