vegetative

adjective
passive or monotonous (activity/lifestyle)
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛdʒ.ə.teɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒ.ɪ.tə.tɪv / (BrE)
(of an activity or lifestyle) passive, monotonous, and lacking in energy or excitement.

Examples

vegetative lifestyle routine
After his retirement, he slipped into a vegetative routine of watching television all day.
She worried that her son's summer had become too vegetative and unproductive.
Trapped in a vegetative cycle, he felt his days lacked challenge or purpose.
