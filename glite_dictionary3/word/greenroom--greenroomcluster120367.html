greenroom

noun
performers' backstage waiting room
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrinˌrum / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnˌruːm / (BrE)
a room in a theater or studio where performers can relax when they are not on stage or awaiting their performance

Examples

the greenroom backstage
After his performance, he invited friends to join him in the greenroom.
Nervous but excited, she waited in the greenroom for her cue to go on stage.
The actors gathered in the greenroom to rehearse their lines before the show.
