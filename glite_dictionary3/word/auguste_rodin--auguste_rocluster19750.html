Auguste Rodin

proper noun
French sculptor, created The Thinker
Pronunciation: / oʊˈɡust roʊˈdæn / (AmE), / əʊˈɡuːst ˈrəʊ.dæ̃ / (BrE)
(1840–1917) A French sculptor renowned for his expressive renderings of the human form, best known for works such as The Thinker and The Kiss.
