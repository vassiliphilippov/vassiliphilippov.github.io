opaqueness

noun
degree of being opaque
Pronunciation: / oʊˈpeɪknəs / (AmE), / əʊˈpeɪknɪs / (BrE)
the quality or degree of being opaque and not allowing light to pass through

Examples

opaqueness of glass
Scientists measured the opaqueness of the material to assess its suitability for laboratory use.
She adjusted the curtains to increase their opaqueness and block the morning sunlight.
The opaqueness of the frosted glass prevented anyone from seeing inside the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym – opacity: the quality of being opaque, also used in technical contexts
  • antonym – transparency: the quality of being transparent or clear
