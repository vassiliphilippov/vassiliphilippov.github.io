self-interest

noun
focus on one's own advantage or welfare
Pronunciation: / sɛlfˈɪntrəst /; / sɛlf ˈɪntrəst / (AmE), / sɛlfˈɪntrɪst /; / sɛlf ˈɪntrɪst / (BrE)
the act or fact of thinking only about what is best for yourself, without caring about others

Examples

personal self-interest

In politics, accusations of self-interest often damage a candidate’s reputation.
