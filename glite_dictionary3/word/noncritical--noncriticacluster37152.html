noncritical

adjective
not complying with critical standards
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈkrɪdɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈkrɪtɪkəl / (BrE)
Not involving or adhering to established standards of criticism or evaluation; not critical.

Examples

noncritical review comments
Her review was considered noncritical, as it lacked any substantive feedback.
Many noncritical readers accept information without questioning its accuracy.
The report was rejected for its noncritical analysis of the data.

Related Terms

  • antonym – critical: involving careful judgment or evaluation
