shamisen

noun
Japanese three-stringed musical instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑː.mɪ.sɛn / (AmE), / ˈʃæmɪsɛn / (BrE)
a traditional Japanese stringed instrument with a long neck, three strings, a rectangular soundbox, and a fretless fingerboard, played with a plectrum

Examples

play the shamisen
She learned to tune a shamisen during her trip to Kyoto.
The delicate sounds of the shamisen added atmosphere to the traditional Japanese performance.
The musician captivated the audience by playing the shamisen with remarkable skill.
