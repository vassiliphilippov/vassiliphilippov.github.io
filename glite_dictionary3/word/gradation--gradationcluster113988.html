gradation

noun
arrangement in stages or degrees
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɡrəˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of arranging or classifying things in stages or degrees according to a scale or series

Examples

gradation of skill
