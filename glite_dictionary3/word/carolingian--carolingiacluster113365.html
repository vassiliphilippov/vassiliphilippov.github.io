Carolingian

adjective
of the Frankish Carolingian dynasty or era
Pronunciation: / ˌkɛrəˈlɪndʒiən / (AmE), / ˌkarəˈlɪndʒiən / (BrE)
of or relating to the Frankish dynasty founded by Charlemagne's father, or to its period of rule (about 751–987 AD).

Examples

Carolingian art style
A Carolingian manuscript was discovered in the monastery's ancient library.
Scholars have studied Carolingian art for its unique blend of classical and Germanic influences.
The Carolingian Empire played a crucial role in shaping medieval Europe.
