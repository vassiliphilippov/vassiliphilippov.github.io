slowworm

noun
a small legless European lizard
Pronunciation: / ˈsloʊˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈsləʊˌwɜːm / (BrE)
A small, legless European lizard (Anguis fragilis) with smooth skin and tiny eyes, often mistaken for a snake.
