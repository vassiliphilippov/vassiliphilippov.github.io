Poecilocapsus lineatus

noun
a yellow or orange leaf bug with four black stripes
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊsɪloʊˈkæpsəs laɪˈneɪətəs / (AmE), / ˌpəʊsɪləkˈæpsəs lɪˈneɪətəs / (BrE)
A species of yellow or orange leaf bug with four black stripes on its back, commonly found in central and eastern North America.
