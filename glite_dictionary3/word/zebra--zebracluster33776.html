zebra

noun
African animal with black-and-white stripes
Pronunciation: / ˈziː.brə / (AmE), / ˈziː.brə / (BrE)
A large African wild animal of the horse family with black-and-white stripes covering its body.

Examples

zebra crossing the savanna
Each zebra has a unique pattern of stripes, much like a human fingerprint.
The zebra ran swiftly across the grasslands, evading its predator.
Visitors to the safari park were excited to see a herd of zebras near the waterhole.
