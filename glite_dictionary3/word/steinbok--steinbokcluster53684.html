steinbok

noun
small African antelope
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪnˌbɑk / (AmE), / ˈstaɪnˌbɒk / (BrE)
a small, slender antelope (Raphicerus campestris) native to the plains of southeastern Africa, characterized by short, straight horns and reddish-brown fur.

Examples

a wild steinbok
During our safari, we spotted a solitary steinbok nibbling on the dry shrubs.
The steinbok darted across the grassland, blending in perfectly with its surroundings.
Unlike larger antelopes, the steinbok tends to live alone or in pairs.
