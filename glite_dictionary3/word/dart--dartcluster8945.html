dart

noun
seam or fold for shaping fabric
Pronunciation: / dɑrt / (AmE), / dɑːt / (BrE)
A narrow, tapered fold sewn into fabric to shape a garment, typically around the bust, waist, or hip.

Examples

sew a dart in fabric
Careful marking is essential before sewing a dart to ensure a smooth finish.
The tailor added a dart at the waist to give the dress a more fitted look.
You can use a dart to shape the bodice when making blouses.
