behaviour

noun
observable actions or responses
Pronunciation: / bəˈheɪvjər /; / bɪˈheɪvjər / (AmE), / bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
the way a person or animal acts, responds, or conducts themselves, especially as seen by others

Examples

child's behaviour at school
