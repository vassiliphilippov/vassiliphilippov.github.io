uneducated

adjective
lacking formal education
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɛdʒəˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɛdjuːkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
lacking a good or formal education; not having been taught or trained, especially at school or a higher institution.

Examples

uneducated person
He felt insecure among his colleagues because he thought they saw him as uneducated.
It's wrong to assume someone is uneducated just because they speak differently.
Many adults in rural areas are still uneducated due to the lack of schools.
