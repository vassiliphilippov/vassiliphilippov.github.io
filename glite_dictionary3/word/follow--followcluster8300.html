to follow

verb
subscribe to updates on social media
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːloʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɒləʊ / (BrE)
to subscribe to updates from someone or something on social media, so you see their posts or activity

Examples

to follow a celebrity online

She decided to follow all the local restaurants on Instagram for menu updates.
I just followed your page so I can stay updated with your posts.
If you follow her on Instagram, you'll see her latest travel photos.
Many celebrities have millions of people who follow them on Twitter.
