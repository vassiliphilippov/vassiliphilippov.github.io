lamppost

noun
pole supporting a streetlight
Pronunciation: / ˈlæmpˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈlampˌpəʊst / (BrE)
A tall, usually metal post supporting an outdoor lamp, typically used for lighting streets or public areas.

Examples

street lamppost
A bird sat on top of the old lamppost outside my house.
He leaned against the lamppost while waiting for the bus.
The city installed new lampposts to improve street lighting.
