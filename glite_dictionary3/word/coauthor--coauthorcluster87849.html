coauthor

noun
person who collaborates in writing
Pronunciation: / koʊˈɔːθər / (AmE), / kəʊˈɔːθə / (BrE)
A person who collaborates with one or more others in writing a book, article, or other work.

Examples

coauthor a book
He was proud to be a coauthor of the award-winning textbook.
She is listed as a coauthor on the research paper.
The novel was written by two coauthors working closely together.

Related Terms

  • synonym – author: the writer of a book, article, or report
