solicitude

noun
excessive concern or care
Pronunciation: / səˈlɪs.ə.tud / (AmE), / səˈlɪs.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
an attitude or feeling of excessive care or concern for someone or something

Examples

solicitude for a patient
He thanked the staff for their solicitude during his stay at the hospital.
Her mother's solicitude for her health was sometimes overwhelming.
With great solicitude, she checked every detail to make sure nothing went wrong.
