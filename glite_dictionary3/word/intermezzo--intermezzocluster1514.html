intermezzo

noun
short musical movement between main sections
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈmɛtsoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmɛtsəʊ / (BrE)
a short piece of music or a brief performance played between the main parts of a longer musical work, play, or opera
