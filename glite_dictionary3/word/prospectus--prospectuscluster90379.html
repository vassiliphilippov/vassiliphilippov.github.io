prospectus

noun
formal document offering securities for sale
Pronunciation: / prəˈspɛktəs / (AmE), / prəˈspɛktəs / (BrE)
a formal written document offering securities for sale, providing details about a proposed business enterprise for investors

Examples

prospectus for investors
According to the prospectus, the funds raised will be used to expand operations and research new products.
Every investor should read the prospectus carefully before committing any money to the fund.
The company issued a prospectus outlining the terms of its upcoming stock offering.
