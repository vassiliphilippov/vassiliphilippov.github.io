semaphore

noun
flag-based signaling system
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛm.əˌfɔr / (AmE), / ˈsɛm.ə.fɔː / (BrE)
a system of sending signals by using two handheld flags, rods, or arms, where the position of the flags indicates different letters or messages

Examples

semaphore flag system
She learned how to use semaphore during summer camp.
The sailors used semaphore to communicate between the ships.
The scouts practiced messages with semaphore flags on the hill.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Morse code: a system of sending messages using sequences of dots and dashes
  • synonym – signal: to transmit information or instructions using gestures or devices
