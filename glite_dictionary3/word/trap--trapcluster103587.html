trap

noun
light two-wheeled carriage
Pronunciation: / træp / (AmE), / træp / (BrE)
a light, usually two-wheeled carriage, often drawn by one horse and designed for carrying one or two people

Examples

horse-drawn trap
He arrived at the estate in a smart trap drawn by a chestnut horse.
In the 19th century, a trap was a common means of private transportation.
The old photograph showed a trap waiting by the gate for passengers.
