cracker

noun
a paper party favor that pops when pulled
Pronunciation: / ˈkrækər / (AmE), / ˈkrækə / (BrE)
a decorated paper tube that makes a sharp noise when pulled apart and typically contains a small toy or gift, used as a party favor, especially at Christmas
