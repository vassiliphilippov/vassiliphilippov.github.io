reservation

noun
the act of keeping something back
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzərˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛzəˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of keeping something back or setting it aside for future use or a particular purpose

Examples

reservation of funds
A large reservation of the final decision was put in place until the full report was reviewed.
The company made a reservation of funds for emergency situations.
The old library included a reservation of certain books for special collections.
