burr

noun
rotary file for smoothing edges
Pronunciation: / bɝː / (AmE), / bɜː / (BrE)
A small rotary cutting tool, typically with a roughened surface, used for smoothing or shaping rough edges left on metal, wood, or other materials.

Examples

rotary burr tool
After cutting the pipe, he removed the rough area with a rotary burr.
Dentists often rely on a burr when shaping a patient's tooth during procedures.
The machinist used a burr to smooth the sharp edges on the metal part.

  • synonym – grinder: Machine or device for grinding or smoothing surfaces
