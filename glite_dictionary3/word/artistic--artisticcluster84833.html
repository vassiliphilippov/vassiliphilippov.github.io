artistic

adjective
relating to art or artists
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪstɪk / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to art or artists, or showing imagination and skill in art.

Examples

artistic talent
He developed an artistic style that set him apart from his peers.
Her artistic talent was evident in every painting she created.
The gallery displayed a range of artistic photographs from local artists.
