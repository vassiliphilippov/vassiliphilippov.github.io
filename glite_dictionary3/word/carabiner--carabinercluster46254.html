carabiner

noun
metal loop with a spring gate, used in climbing
Pronunciation: / ˌkærəˈbiːnər / (AmE), / ˌkærəˈbiːnə / (BrE)
a strong, oblong metal ring with a spring-loaded gate, used to quickly and reversibly connect components, especially in climbing and mountaineering
