heavy

adjective
dense, hard-to-digest (food)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvi / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi / (BrE)
(of food) dense or inadequately leavened and likely to be difficult to digest.

Examples

heavy bread
After eating a heavy meal, he felt sluggish all afternoon.
Her scones were too heavy and sat in my stomach like a rock.
The cake turned out heavy because I forgot to add enough baking powder.

Related Terms

  • antonym – light: (of food) easily digestible or well-leavened
