guiltily

adverb
in a way that shows guilt
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪltɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡɪltɪli / (BrE)
In a way that shows or suggests a feeling of guilt.

Examples

look guiltily around
He looked around guiltily after breaking the vase.
She guiltily admitted that she had forgotten his birthday.
The child hid his hands behind his back and smiled guiltily.

Related Terms

  • synonym – shamefacedly: in a way that shows embarrassment or shame
