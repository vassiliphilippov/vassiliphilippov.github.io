earmark

noun
identification mark on an animal's ear
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈɪəˌmɑːk / (BrE)
a mark or tag placed on the ear of a domestic animal for identification purposes

Examples

earmark on a sheep
Each sheep received a small earmark to distinguish it from others in the flock.
Loss of a clear earmark can make tracing cattle ownership difficult.
The farmer checked the earmark before returning the cow to its pen.
