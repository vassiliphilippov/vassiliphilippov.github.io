Euproctis phaeorrhoea

noun
species of European moth; pest of shade and fruit trees
Pronunciation: / juːˈprɑktɪs ˌfiːəˈroʊiə / (AmE), / juːˈprɒktɪs ˌfiːəˈrəʊɪə / (BrE)
A small brown and white European moth, now present in eastern United States, known as a pest of various shade and fruit trees.
