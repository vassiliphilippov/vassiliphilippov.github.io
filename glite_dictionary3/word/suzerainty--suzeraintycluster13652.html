suzerainty

noun
the control of one state over another
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːzərənti /; / ˈsuːzərənti, ˈsjuːzərənti / (AmE), / ˈsjuːzərənti / (BrE)
the right or position of a powerful state or ruler to control the foreign affairs of another state, while allowing it to govern itself internally
