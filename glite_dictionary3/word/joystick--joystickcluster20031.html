joystick

noun
manual control device; input lever
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪ.stɪk / (BrE)
a lever-like control device that you move with your hand to operate a machine, computer, or aircraft
