to spoonfeed

verb
feed with a spoon
Pronunciation: / ˈspuːnˌfiːd / (AmE), / ˈspuːnˌfiːd / (BrE)
to feed someone, especially a baby or invalid, with a spoon

Examples

to spoonfeed a baby
It usually takes months before a toddler stops needing to be spoonfed.
She patiently spoonfed her baby, making sure not to spill a drop.
The nurse had to spoonfeed the patient because he was too weak to eat by himself.
