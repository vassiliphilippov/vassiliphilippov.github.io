esplanade

noun
open walkway by the sea
Pronunciation: / ˌɛspləˈneɪd / (AmE), / ˌɛspləˈnɑːd / (BrE)
a long, open, level area, usually beside the sea, designed for walking or strolling

Examples

The city renovated the esplanade to attract more visitors to the waterfront.
