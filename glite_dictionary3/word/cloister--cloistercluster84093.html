cloister

noun
religious residence in seclusion
Pronunciation: / ˈklɔɪstɚ / (AmE), / ˈklɔɪstə / (BrE)
a place, often a monastery or convent, where members of a religious community live in seclusion from the outside world

Examples

a cloister for monks
After years in the cloister, he found city life overwhelming.
She sought guidance from the nuns living in the secluded cloister.
The ancient cloister provided a tranquil haven for the monks.

Related Terms

  • synonym – convent: a residence for a community of nuns
  • synonym – monastery: a building or complex where monks live and work
