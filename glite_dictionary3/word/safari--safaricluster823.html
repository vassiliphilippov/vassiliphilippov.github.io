safari

noun
overland wildlife expedition
Pronunciation: / səˈfɑri / (AmE), / səˈfɑːri / (BrE)
an overland expedition, especially for hunting or observing wildlife, traditionally in Africa

Examples

go on a safari
During the 1920s, wealthy adventurers often went on safari for big-game hunting.
The safari lasted two weeks and covered hundreds of miles across the savanna.
They planned a safari through Kenya to see lions and elephants in the wild.
