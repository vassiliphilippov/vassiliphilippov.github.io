Lenape

noun
a Native American people of the Northeastern US
Pronunciation: / ləˈnɑːpi / (AmE), / ləˈnɑːpi / (BrE)
a member of an Indigenous Algonquian people originally inhabiting areas of New Jersey, New York, Delaware, and Pennsylvania

Examples

Lenape people
Many Lenape traditions are still practiced by their descendants today.
She discovered her ancestors were part of the Lenape tribe.
The Lenape were among the first Indigenous peoples to encounter European settlers in the region.
