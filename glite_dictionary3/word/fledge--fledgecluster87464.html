to fledge

verb
rear young birds for flight
Pronunciation: / flɛdʒ / (AmE), / flɛdʒ / (BrE)
to care for and rear (young birds) until they are ready to fly

Examples

to fledge young birds
Birdwatchers observed the robins as they fledged their young in the spring.
Many species fledge their offspring with impressive devotion and patience.
The parents will fledge their chicks over the next few weeks before they leave the nest.
