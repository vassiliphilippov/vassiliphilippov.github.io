headcount

noun
number of people in a group
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
the number of people present in a particular group, organization, or event

Examples

headcount of employees
Budget cuts forced the company to reduce its headcount by 10%.
The manager did a quick headcount before the meeting started.
We need an accurate headcount to order enough food for the event.

Related Terms

  • synonym – head: a person considered as one individual when counting a group
