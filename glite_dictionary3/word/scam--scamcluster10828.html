scam

noun
fraudulent scheme; swindle
Pronunciation: / skæm / (AmE), / skæm / (BrE)
a dishonest or illegal plan, especially one for making money by deceiving people

Examples

online scam
He lost all his savings in a cryptocurrency scam.
The online store turned out to be a scam that never delivered any products.
The police issued a warning about a new phone scam targeting elderly residents.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fraud: wrongful or criminal deception intended to result in financial or personal gain
  • antonym – legitimate business: a lawful and honest commercial activity
  • synonym – swindle: a fraudulent scheme or action for personal gain
