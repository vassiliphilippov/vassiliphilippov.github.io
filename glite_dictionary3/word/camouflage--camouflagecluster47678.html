camouflage

noun
device or method for concealment or disguise
Pronunciation: / ˈkæməˌflɑʒ / (AmE), / ˈkaməˌflɑːʒ /; / ˈkæməˌflɑːʒ /; / ˈkæməflɑːʒ / (BrE)
the act or means of hiding or disguising something so it is not easily seen or recognized (can be literal or metaphorical)
