favourite

adjective
preferred above all others
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪvərɪt /; / ˈfeɪvərət / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərɪt / (BrE)
preferred or liked more than others; especially appealing or popular

Examples

favourite food
Among all the desserts, chocolate cake is my favourite because my grandmother always makes it for me.
He was the manager's favourite player, often chosen for the most important matches.
She is clearly the favourite student in the class, always getting extra attention from the teacher.
She wore her favourite dress to the party, knowing it always draws compliments.
The show quickly became a favourite with television audiences worldwide.
This is a favourite restaurant among local families and visitors alike.
