equable

adjective
not varying; steady and uniform
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkwəb(ə)l, ˈiːkwəb(ə)l / (AmE), / ˈɛkwəbl̩, ˈiːkwəbl̩ / (BrE)
not changing or varying greatly; steady or uniform in nature, character, or temperature

Examples

equable climate
His equable temperament made him well-suited to leadership positions.
The region is famous for its equable climate, attracting visitors all year round.
The temperature in these coastal waters remains relatively equable throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • antonym – variable: liable to change or vary; not constant
