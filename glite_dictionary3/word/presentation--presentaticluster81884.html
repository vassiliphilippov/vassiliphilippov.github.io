presentation

noun
formal introduction of a person
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌprɛzn̩ˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the formal act of introducing a person to another person or to the public, often in a ceremonial way

Examples

presentation to guests

His presentation as the new CEO was carefully organized to impress stakeholders.
The ambassador's official presentation to the queen was followed by a grand ceremony.
