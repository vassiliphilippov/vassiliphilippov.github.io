match

noun
a formal contest or competition
Pronunciation: / mætʃ / (AmE), / matʃ / (BrE)
a formal contest in which two or more persons or teams compete against each other

Examples

a football match
She trained hard for the tennis match and hoped to win the championship.
The boxing match lasted twelve rounds before a winner was declared.
Tomorrow's soccer match between the rival schools has attracted a lot of attention.
