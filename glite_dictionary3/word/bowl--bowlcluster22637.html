bowl

noun
part of a pipe holding tobacco
Pronunciation: / boʊl / (AmE), / bəʊl / (BrE)
The rounded part of a smoking pipe, open at the top, used to hold tobacco.

Examples

A cracked bowl can make a pipe difficult to smoke.
