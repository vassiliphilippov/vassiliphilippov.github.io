vermifuge

noun
medicine for expelling intestinal worms
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrməˌfjudʒ / (AmE), / ˈvɜːmɪfjuːdʒ / (BrE)
a medicine or substance used to expel parasitic worms from the body, especially from the intestines

Examples

herbal vermifuge remedy
Before modern medicine, people often relied on herbal vermifuge preparations for deworming.
Many traditional remedies used plant-based substances as natural vermifuges.
The doctor prescribed a vermifuge to treat the child's intestinal parasite infection.
