AI

noun
artificial intelligence in computers
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪˈaɪ / (AmE), / ˌeɪˈaɪ / (BrE)
the study and development of computer systems that can perform tasks normally requiring human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving

Examples

AI technology advances
AI has made significant advances in areas such as speech recognition and image classification.
Many experts believe that AI will transform industries from healthcare to transportation.
Some people worry about the ethical implications of powerful AI systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym – machine learning: a field of study that gives computers the ability to learn without being explicitly programmed
  • antonym – natural intelligence: the ability to think, learn, and solve problems as present in humans and animals
