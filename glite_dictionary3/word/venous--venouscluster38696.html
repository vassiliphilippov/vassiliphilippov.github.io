venous

adjective
relating to veins
Pronunciation: / ˈviːnəs / (AmE), / ˈviːnəs / (BrE)
relating to, containing, or performing the functions of veins in the circulatory system

Examples

venous blood sample
A blood sample was taken from a venous source rather than an artery.
He is suffering from venous insufficiency in his legs.
