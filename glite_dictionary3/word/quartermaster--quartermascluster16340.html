quartermaster

noun
military officer in charge of supplies
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔːrdərˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtəməstə / (BrE)
an officer in the armed forces responsible for the supply of food, equipment, and uniforms to troops

Examples

quartermaster supplies equipment
After reporting to the quartermaster, we received new boots and tents.
The quartermaster ensured every soldier had adequate clothing and rations for the winter.
The quartermaster's careful inventory kept the battalion well supplied throughout the deployment.
