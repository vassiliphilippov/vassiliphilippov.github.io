to mingle

verb
become involved with others or with something
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪŋɡəl / (BrE)
to become involved with or take part in an activity, group, or situation, especially in a way that mixes one's presence or participation with others

Examples

to mingle with guests
He was hesitant to mingle in the heated debate at the meeting.
Politicians often mingle in public events to connect with their constituents.
She decided to mingle with the local community to better understand their traditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mix: to combine or join with others
  • synonym – participate: to take part in something
