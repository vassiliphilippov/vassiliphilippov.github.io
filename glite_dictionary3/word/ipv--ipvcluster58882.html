IPV

noun
intimate partner violence (abuse)
Pronunciation: / aɪ pi vi / (AmE), / aɪ piː viː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for intimate partner violence: physical, sexual, psychological, or emotional abuse committed by a current or former intimate partner.

Examples

IPV domestic abuse
Public health campaigns increasingly define IPV as domestic abuse across communities.
She sought counseling after years of emotional and occasional physical IPV from her partner.
Researchers measure rates of IPV using surveys that ask about controlling, violent, or coercive behaviors.

Related Terms
