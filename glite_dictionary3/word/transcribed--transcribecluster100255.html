transcribed

adjective
recorded for broadcast
Pronunciation: / trænˈskraɪbd / (AmE), / trænˈskraɪbd / (BrE)
(of a broadcast or announcement) previously recorded and not performed live.

Examples

transcribed message played
A transcribed message played over the loudspeakers as soon as we arrived.
The news bulletin was a transcribed segment prepared earlier in the day.
We heard a transcribed announcement welcoming passengers aboard the train.

Related Terms

  • antonym – live: (of a broadcast) transmitted as it happens, not recorded
