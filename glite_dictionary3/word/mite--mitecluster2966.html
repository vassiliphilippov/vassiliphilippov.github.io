mite

adverb
a little; slightly (informal use)
Pronunciation: / maɪt / (AmE), / maɪt / (BrE)
slightly; by a small amount (used informally to mean 'a little').

Examples

a mite taller
He’s a mite taller than his brother.
I was feeling a mite nervous before the exam.
The soup is a mite too salty for my taste.
