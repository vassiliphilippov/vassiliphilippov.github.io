to billet

verb
assign soldiers lodging
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪlɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɪlɪt / (BrE)
to assign lodging, especially for soldiers, typically in civilian houses

Examples

to billet soldiers
During the war, local families were asked to billet soldiers in their homes.
The officer decided to billet the entire regiment in nearby cottages for the winter.
Villagers objected to having troops billeted on them without any compensation.
