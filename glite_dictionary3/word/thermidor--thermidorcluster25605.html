Thermidor

noun
eleventh month of the French Revolutionary calendar
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜrmɪˌdɔr / (AmE), / ˈθɜːmɪdɔː / (BrE)
the eleventh month of the French Revolutionary calendar, corresponding roughly to July and August, known as the month of heat

Examples

Thermidor summer month
According to the Revolutionary calendar, Thermidor falls during the sweltering summer months.
Historians often refer to the events of Thermidor when discussing Robespierre's downfall.
The coup d'état of Thermidor marked a turning point in the French Revolution.
