acceptability

noun
quality of being satisfactory or acceptable
Pronunciation: / əkˌsɛptəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / əkˌsɛptəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being accepted as satisfactory or meeting approved standards.

Examples

acceptability of a proposal
Her idea gained acceptability after several experts endorsed it.
The acceptability of the new policy will depend on public opinion.
We need to test the acceptability of the product before launching it.

Related Terms

  • synonym – acceptance: the act of accepting something; approval
  • antonym – unacceptability: the quality of not being satisfactory or not meeting standards
