Albuginaceae

noun
a family of blister-producing fungi
Pronunciation: / ælˌbjudʒɪˈneɪsiː /; / ælˌbjuːdʒɪˈneɪsiː / (AmE), / ælˌbjuːdʒɪˈneɪsiː /; / ælˌbjuːdʒɪˈniːsiː / (BrE)
