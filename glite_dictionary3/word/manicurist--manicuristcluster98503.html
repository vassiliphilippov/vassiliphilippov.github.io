manicurist

noun
nail care professional
Pronunciation: / ˈmænəkjʊrɪst / (AmE), / ˈmanɪkjʊərɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to clean, trim, and polish fingernails, usually as a beauty treatment

Examples

A skilled manicurist can make even the roughest hands look elegant.

The manicurist carefully shaped and polished each of my nails.
