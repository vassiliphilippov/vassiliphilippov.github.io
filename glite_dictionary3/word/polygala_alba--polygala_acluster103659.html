Polygala alba

noun
a North American perennial herb with white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːlɪˈɡeɪlə ˈælbə / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈɡeɪlə ˈalbə / (BrE)
A perennial bushy herb native to central and southern United States, with white flowers that have green centers and often a purple crest, related to Seneca snakeroot.
