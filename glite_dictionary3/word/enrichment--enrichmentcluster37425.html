enrichment

noun
gift that increases someone's wealth
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈrɪtʃmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈrɪtʃmənt / (BrE)
a gift or donation that substantially increases the wealth of the recipient

Examples

enrichment by inheritance
Legal disputes often arise when families argue over the enrichment of one member through generous gifts.
She viewed the inheritance as an unexpected enrichment that changed her life.
The sudden enrichment of the charity allowed it to expand its programs nationwide.
