gastrectomy

noun
surgical removal of the stomach
Pronunciation: / ɡæsˈtrɛktəmi / (AmE), / ɡæsˈtrɛktəmi / (BrE)
a surgical operation to remove all or part of the stomach

Examples

gastrectomy for stomach cancer
After being diagnosed with stomach cancer, the patient underwent a gastrectomy.
Recovery from a gastrectomy typically involves a modified diet and close medical supervision.
The surgeon explained the risks associated with a partial gastrectomy to the family.
