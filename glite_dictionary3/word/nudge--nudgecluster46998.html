nudge

noun
a slight push to attract attention
Pronunciation: / nʌdʒ / (AmE), / nʌdʒ / (BrE)
a slight push, usually with the elbow or hand, to attract attention or prompt action

Examples

a gentle nudge
She gave him a gentle nudge to wake him up.
The cat demanded attention with a small nudge against my leg.
With a nudge of his elbow, he pointed out the mistake.

  • synonym – poke: a quick push with a finger or a sharp object
