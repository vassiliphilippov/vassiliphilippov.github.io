padlock

noun
detachable lock with a hinged shackle
Pronunciation: / ˈpædˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈpædˌlɒk / (BrE)
a detachable lock with a hinged shackle, used for securing gates, doors, or containers and which can be fastened by snapping it shut

Examples

padlock on the gate
He used a padlock to secure his bicycle to the fence.
She forgot the combination for the padlock on her locker.
The storage unit was protected by a heavy padlock on the door.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lock: mechanical device used to fasten something securely
