pinch

noun
a small amount held between fingers
Pronunciation: / pɪntʃ / (AmE), / pɪntʃ / (BrE)
a small amount of something, especially a powder or spice, that you can hold between your finger and thumb

Examples

pinch of salt
Add a pinch of salt to the soup before serving.
She sprinkled a pinch of sugar over the fruit.
The recipe only calls for a pinch of cinnamon.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dash: a small quantity of an ingredient
  • synonym – touch: a slight amount
