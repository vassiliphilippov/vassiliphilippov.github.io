to upgrade

verb
make better or more modern
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌɡreɪd /; / ˈʌpɡreɪd / (BrE)
to improve something by making it newer, better, or more up-to-date; to raise something to a higher standard

Examples

to upgrade a phone
After several years, I finally upgraded my phone to a newer model.

We are upgrading our website to provide a better user experience.
After the review, her job title was upgraded to senior manager.
The airline decided to upgrade all frequent flyers to business class for the flight.
We need to upgrade our computer systems to stay competitive in the market.
