rheumatism

noun
painful joint or muscle disorder
Pronunciation: / ˈruːməˌtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈruːmətɪzəm / (BrE)
a medical condition causing pain and inflammation in the joints, muscles, or connective tissues, often with stiffness

Examples

The doctor prescribed medication to ease his rheumatism.

  • synonym – lumbago: pain in the lower back, often classed as a type of rheumatism
