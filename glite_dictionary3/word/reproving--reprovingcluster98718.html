reproving

adjective
expressing disapproval or correction
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpruvɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈpruːvɪŋ / (BrE)
expressing or conveying disapproval or censure, especially with the intention of correcting someone

reproving look

With a reproving tone, she reminded him to say thank you.
