clamber

noun
an awkward or laborious climb
Pronunciation: / ˈklæmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈklæmbə / (BrE)
an awkward and laborious climb, especially one made using both hands and feet

Examples

a difficult clamber
After a difficult clamber over the rocks, they reached the summit.
Getting into the attic required an awkward clamber up the narrow ladder.
The hikers faced a steep clamber up the muddy hillside.
