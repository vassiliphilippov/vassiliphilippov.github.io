convention

noun
large formal meeting or assembly
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɛn.ʃən /; / kənˈvɛnʃən / (AmE), / kənˈvɛn.ʃən /; / kənˈvɛnʃən / (BrE)
a large meeting or formal assembly, especially for a particular group, profession, or activity

Examples

annual convention
Most major comic artists will attend the annual convention in San Diego this summer.

The political parties held their convention to nominate their presidential candidates.

Proper procedures are followed during the convention of an international conference.
The convention of the committee took place every year in January.
