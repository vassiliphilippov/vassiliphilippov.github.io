to curtain

verb
to provide with curtains
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝː.tn̩ / (AmE), / ˈkɜː.tən / (BrE)
to provide with or enclose by a curtain or drapery

Examples

to curtain a window
After moving in, they decided to curtain all the front windows for privacy.
She quickly curtained the open shelves to hide the clutter.
The stagehands will curtain the theater before the performance begins.
