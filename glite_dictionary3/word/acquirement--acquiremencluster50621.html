acquirement

noun
skill gained by training
Pronunciation: / əˈkwaɪərmənt / (AmE), / əˈkwaɪəmənt / (BrE)
a skill or ability that has been obtained through training or learning

Examples

valuable acquirement
Her greatest acquirement was the ability to communicate fluently in three languages.
Reading every day is an acquirement that has benefited him in many aspects of life.
Years of practice led to the acquirement of expert violin skills.
