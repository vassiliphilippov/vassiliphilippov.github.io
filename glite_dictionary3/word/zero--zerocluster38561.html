zero

noun
point of origin on a scale; no quantity
Pronunciation: / ˈziːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈzɪərəʊ / (BrE)
the point on a numerical scale from which positive or negative values are measured; the value denoting nothing or the absence of quantity

Examples

zero on the scale
Set the scale so that it reads zero before you weigh the object.
The temperature dropped to zero during the night.
Water freezes at zero degrees Celsius.
