to overlap

verb
coincide partly or wholly
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈlæp / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈlæp / (BrE)
(of two things) to partly or completely cover the same area, time, or elements as each other; to coincide in part or in whole.

Examples

to overlap interests
Her interests overlap with mine in several areas, especially art and music.
Our meetings often overlap, making it hard to attend both.
The two shifts overlap by an hour to ensure a smooth transition.

Related Terms

  • synonym – coincide: to occur at or during the same time
  • antonym – conflict: to be in opposition or not align
