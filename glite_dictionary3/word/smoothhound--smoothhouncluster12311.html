smoothhound

noun
a small shark of European coastal waters
Pronunciation: / ˈsmuðˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈsmuːðhaʊnd / (BrE)
A small, slender shark of the genus Mustelus, found in European coastal waters and known for its smooth skin and dog-like snout.
