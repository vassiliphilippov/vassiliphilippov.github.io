shredded

adjective
very muscular (slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃrɛdɪd / (BrE)
informal (slang) having very well-defined muscles; extremely lean and muscular.

Examples

shredded abs
After months of training, the athlete looked shredded and ready for competition.
Her new gym routine left her visibly shredded, with clear muscle definition.
Many fitness influencers use supplements claiming to help you get shredded before summer.

Related Terms

  • antonym – flabby: having soft, loose flesh and lacking muscle tone; opposite of 'shredded'
  • synonym – ripped: having clearly visible, well-defined muscles; very fit
