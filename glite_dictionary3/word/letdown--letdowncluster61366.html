letdown

noun
disappointment when expectations are not met
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛtˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈlɛtdaʊn / (BrE)
a feeling of disappointment or dissatisfaction when something does not meet your expectations

Examples

a big letdown
Despite the hype, the concert turned out to be quite a letdown.
He felt a huge letdown when his team lost the finals.
The ending of the movie was a real letdown after so much build-up.

  • synonym – disappointment: a feeling of sadness because something did not happen as expected
