laminate

noun
a multi-layered bonded material
Pronunciation: / ˈlæm.əˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈlæm.ɪ.neɪt / (BrE)
a sheet of material made by bonding together two or more layers, often to improve strength, appearance, or durability

Examples

wood laminate panel
Engineers tested the strength of the new wood laminate under extreme pressure.
He replaced all the old tiles with a modern, gray laminate flooring.
The countertop was finished with a durable laminate that resisted stains and scratches.
