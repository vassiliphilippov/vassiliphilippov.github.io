Dhaulagiri

proper noun
mountain in the Himalayas, Nepal
Pronunciation: / daʊ.ləˈɡɪri / (AmE), / daʊ.ləˈɡɪəri / (BrE)
A mountain in the Himalayas in Nepal, 8,167 meters (26,795 feet) high, the seventh highest mountain in the world.

climb Dhaulagiri
Dhaulagiri was first successfully climbed in 1960 by a Swiss-Austrian-Nepali expedition.

The view of Dhaulagiri at sunrise is breathtaking from the nearby village.
