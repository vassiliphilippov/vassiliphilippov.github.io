psychic

adjective
of or relating to the mind
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪ.kɪk / (AmE), / ˈsʌɪ.kɪk / (BrE)
relating to or influenced by the human mind or psyche, especially as distinct from the physical body

Examples

psychic energy release
Many argue that dreams are a form of psychic energy release.
She suffered a deep psychic trauma after the accident, affecting her mental health for years.
The novel explores the psychic wounds caused by war and loss.
