to cluck

verb
make a clucking sound (as a hen)
Pronunciation: / klʌk / (AmE), / klʌk / (BrE)
(of a hen) to make a series of short, low sounds; to utter a clucking sound.

Examples

to cluck like a chicken
At dawn, I could hear chickens clucking in the backyard.
She clucked her tongue disapprovingly at the mess in the kitchen.
The mother hen began to cluck loudly when a stranger approached her chicks.
