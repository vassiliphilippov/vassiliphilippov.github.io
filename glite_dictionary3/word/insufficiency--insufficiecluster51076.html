insufficiency

noun
organ or body part not functioning properly
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnsəˈfɪʃənsi / (AmE), / ˌɪnsəˈfɪʃənsi / (BrE)
(medical) Inability of a bodily organ or part to function normally or adequately.

Examples

insufficiency of the heart
Cardiac insufficiency can lead to decreased blood flow throughout the body.
Renal insufficiency often requires close monitoring and medical treatment.
The patient was diagnosed with adrenal insufficiency after showing symptoms of fatigue and weight loss.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deficiency: Lack or shortage of something necessary in the body
  • synonym – failure: Collapse or cessation of normal function, especially in organs
