to caponise

verb
castrate a cock to make a capon
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkæpənaɪz / (BrE)
to convert a male chicken (cock) into a capon by castration

Examples

to caponise a rooster
It was common practice in some regions to caponise cockerels before raising them for the table.
The farmer decided to caponise several of his young roosters to improve the quality of the meat.
Veterinarians sometimes caponise poultry on request for small-scale producers.
