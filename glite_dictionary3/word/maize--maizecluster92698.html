maize

noun
a cereal plant; corn
Pronunciation: / meɪz / (AmE), / meɪz / (BrE)
A tall annual cereal plant (Zea mays) native to the Americas, widely cultivated for its yellow grain, used as food, livestock feed, and in industry.

Examples

maize field
Farmers in this region grow maize as their main crop.
Many Mexican dishes use maize as a primary ingredient.
The government is investing in research to improve maize yields.

  • synonym – corn: another common term for maize, especially in American English
