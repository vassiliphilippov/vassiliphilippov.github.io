annexation

noun
the act of joining or adding territory
Pronunciation: / ˌænəkˈseɪʃən /; / ˌænɪkˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌænɪkˈseɪʃən /; / ˌanɪkˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of adding or taking over territory or property, especially when a country or organization makes it officially part of itself
