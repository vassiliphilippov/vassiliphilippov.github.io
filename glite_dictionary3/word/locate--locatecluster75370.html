to locate

verb
assign or place in a particular location
Pronunciation: / loʊˈkeɪt / (AmE), / ləʊˈkeɪt / (BrE)
to place or establish someone or something in a particular place; to assign a location to

Examples

to locate a new office
Government agencies often locate offices near major transport links.
Many artists locate their studios in vibrant neighborhoods.
The company decided to locate its headquarters in San Francisco.

Related Terms

  • antonym – disperse: to distribute or spread over a wide area (sometimes contrasted with centralizing or assigning to a point)
  • synonym – station: to assign to a specified place or position
