cottar

noun
Scottish Highland peasant farmer
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.tər / (AmE), / ˈkɒt.ə / (BrE)
A peasant farmer, especially one in the Scottish Highlands who holds a small cottage with a plot of land.

Examples

cottar family farm
Many cottars faced hardship during the Highland Clearances.
The Highland cottar worked his small plot of land to support his family.
