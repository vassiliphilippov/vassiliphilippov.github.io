to stamp

verb
walk with heavy steps
Pronunciation: / stæmp / (AmE), / stæmp / (BrE)
to walk with heavy, forceful steps, often making a loud noise

Examples

to stamp your feet
He angrily stamped across the room after hearing the bad news.
She stamped down the hallway, clearly upset about something.
The children stamped their feet to stay warm in the cold playground.
