family Aplysiidae

noun
family of sea hares (marine mollusks)
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli æˌplɪsiˈaɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli aplɪsɪˈiːdiː / (BrE)
A family of large marine gastropod mollusks commonly known as sea hares, characterized by a soft, often slug-like body and two rhinophores on their head.
