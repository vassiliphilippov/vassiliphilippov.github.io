Scipio the Elder

proper noun
Roman general; defeated Hannibal at Zama
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪpioʊ ði ˈɛldɚ / (AmE), / ˈskɪpɪəʊ ði ˈɛldə / (BrE)
(circa 237–183 BC) Roman general who led the invasion of Carthage during the Second Punic War and defeated Hannibal at Zama.
