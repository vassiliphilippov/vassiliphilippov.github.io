Utopia

noun
imaginary perfect place or state
Pronunciation: / juˈtoʊpiə / (AmE), / juːˈtəʊpiə / (BrE)
an imagined place or state of things in which everything is perfect

Examples

perfect utopia society
Many philosophers have tried to describe a Utopia where everyone lives in harmony.
She dismissed his vision as unrealistic, calling it a modern Utopia that could never exist.
The new city plan was inspired by the concept of a Utopia, with green spaces and no pollution.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dystopia: an imagined state or society where there is great suffering or injustice, typically a totalitarian or environmentally degraded one
