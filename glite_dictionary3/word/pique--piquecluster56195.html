to pique

verb
to cause resentment or indignation
Pronunciation: / piːk / (AmE), / piːk / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel resentment or indignation; to offend or irritate someone's feelings

Examples

to pique his pride
He was piqued by their lack of interest in his proposal.
Her comments piqued his curiosity and his irritation at the same time.
It piques me when people ignore my advice.

Related Terms

  • synonym – annoy: to make someone slightly angry or irritated
  • synonym – offend: to cause to feel hurt or upset, often through words or actions
