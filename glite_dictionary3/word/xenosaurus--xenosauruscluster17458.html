Xenosaurus

noun
genus of Mexican lizards (Xenosauridae)
Pronunciation: / ziːnəˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / ziːnəˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
A genus of slender-bodied Mexican lizards, the only genus of the family Xenosauridae, characterized by an upper surface covered with tiny granules and tubercles.
