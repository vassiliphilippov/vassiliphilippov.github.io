PU

noun
polyurethane polymer used in various products
Pronunciation: / piː juː / (AmE), / pjuː / (BrE)
a type of synthetic polymer, polyurethane, used in making adhesives, foams, plastics, paints, and rubber-like materials

Examples

PU leather jacket
Many synthetic leathers use PU as a more affordable alternative to real leather.
The manufacturer switched to PU foam to improve the comfort of their chairs.
The suitcase is made from high-quality PU for durability and water resistance.
