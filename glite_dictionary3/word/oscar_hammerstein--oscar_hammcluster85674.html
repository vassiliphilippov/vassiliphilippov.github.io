Oscar Hammerstein

proper noun
American lyricist; partner of Richard Rodgers
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːskər ˈhæmərstaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɒskə ˈhæmərstaɪn / (BrE)
(1895–1960) American lyricist best known for his collaborations with composer Richard Rodgers on musicals such as 'Oklahoma!' and 'The Sound of Music'.
