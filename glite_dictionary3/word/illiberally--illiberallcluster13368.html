illiberally

adverb
in a narrow-minded or intolerant way
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɪbərəli / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪbərəli / (BrE)
In a manner that is not broad-minded or tolerant; in a narrow or intolerant way.

Examples

to judge illiberally
Decisions made illiberally often stifle creativity and progress.
He interpreted the rules illiberally and refused any exceptions.
The policy was enforced illiberally, leaving little room for individual circumstances.
