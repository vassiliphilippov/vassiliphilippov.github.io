polo

noun
a sport played on horseback using mallets
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊ.ləʊ / (BrE)
A team sport played on horseback, in which players score goals using long-handled mallets to hit a wooden ball.

Examples

polo match on horses
He has played polo professionally for over a decade.
Learning to ride is a prerequisite for participating in polo.
The annual polo tournament attracts players from around the world.
