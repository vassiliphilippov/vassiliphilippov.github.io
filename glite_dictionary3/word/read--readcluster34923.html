to read

verb
study a subject at university
Pronunciation: / ɹiːd / (AmE), / ɹiːd / (BrE)
(especially British English) to study a particular subject at a university or for an examination.

Examples

to read law at university
He read medicine at Cambridge before becoming a doctor.
Many students read philosophy as part of their degree.
She is reading law at Oxford University.
