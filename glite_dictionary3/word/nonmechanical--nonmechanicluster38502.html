nonmechanical

adjective
not operated by machinery
Pronunciation: / nɑn.məˈkæn.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / nɒn.mɪˈkæn.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
Not operated by or involving machinery or mechanical means; not mechanical.

Examples

nonmechanical method
He developed a nonmechanical method for measuring the temperature.
Many traditional watches are nonmechanical and use digital technology instead.
The artist prefers nonmechanical tools like pencils and brushes for her work.
