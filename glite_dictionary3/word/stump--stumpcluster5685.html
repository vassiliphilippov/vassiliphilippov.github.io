stump

noun
upright post forming wicket in cricket
Pronunciation: / stʌmp / (AmE), / stʌmp / (BrE)
(in cricket) any of the three upright wooden posts which together with the bails form the wicket.

Examples

cricket stump
After hitting the stump, the bails fell, signaling the batsman was bowled.
He set up the stumps before the start of the cricket match.
The bowler aimed directly at the stump to try and get the batsman out.
