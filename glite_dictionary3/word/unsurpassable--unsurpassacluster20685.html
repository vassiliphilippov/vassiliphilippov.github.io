unsurpassable

adjective
impossible to be exceeded
Pronunciation: / ʌn.sɚˈpæs.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌn.səˈpɑː.sə.bəl / (BrE)

The team's record for most wins in a season remains unsurpassable to this day.
