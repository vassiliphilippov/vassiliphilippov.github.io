psychoactive

adjective
affecting the mind or mental processes
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊˈæktɪv / (BrE)
(of a drug or substance) affecting mental activity, behavior, or perception.

Examples

psychoactive drug effects
Many antidepressants are considered psychoactive medications with mood-altering effects.
Scientists studied the psychoactive properties of the plant extract to understand its impact on anxiety.
Some countries have strict regulations on psychoactive substances to protect public health.
