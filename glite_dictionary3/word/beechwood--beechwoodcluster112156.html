beechwood

noun
wood from beech trees, used in construction or furniture
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtʃˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃˌwʊd / (BrE)
The hard, pale wood of any of several beech trees, used in making flooring, containers, plywood, and tool handles.

Examples

beechwood furniture
A strong beechwood handle makes this tool both comfortable and durable.
The furniture was made from high-quality beechwood, giving the room a warm appearance.
They installed beechwood flooring throughout the house for a natural look.
