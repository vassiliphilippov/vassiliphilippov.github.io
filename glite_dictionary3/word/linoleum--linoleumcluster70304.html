linoleum

noun
durable floor covering material
Pronunciation: / lɪˈnoʊliəm / (AmE), / lɪˈnəʊliəm / (BrE)
A type of durable, washable floor covering made from solidified linseed oil and other materials, often used in kitchens and bathrooms.

Examples

linoleum floor tiles
After the renovation, the hallway gleamed with new linoleum tiles.
The kitchen floor was covered in linoleum that had a retro checkerboard design.
They decided to replace the old carpet with linoleum for easier cleaning.
