glowworm

noun
insect that emits visible light
Pronunciation: / ˈɡloʊˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊˌwɜːm / (BrE)
an insect larva or wingless adult female, especially of a firefly, that emits a soft, visible light in the dark

Examples

glowworm lights up
A single glowworm illuminated the inside of the old log.
At night, the forest path was dotted with the faint green light of a glowworm.
Children were fascinated by the glowworms shining in the garden after dark.
