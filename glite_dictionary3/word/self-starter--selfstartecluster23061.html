self-starter

noun
a proactive, independent person
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈstɑrtɚ / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈstɑːtə / (BrE)
a person who is energetic and able to start things independently without needing prompting from others

Examples

motivated self-starter
As a self-starter, he quickly identified problems and proposed effective solutions.
Companies value employees who are self-starters and can manage their own tasks.
Jessica is a real self-starter who never waits for instructions before getting to work.
