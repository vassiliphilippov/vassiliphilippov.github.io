to deodorize

verb
remove unpleasant odors
Pronunciation: / diˈoʊdəˌraɪz / (AmE), / diːˈəʊdəraɪz / (BrE)
to eliminate or neutralize unpleasant odors from something

Examples

to deodorize a room
Please deodorize the garbage can before the guests arrive.
Some air purifiers can effectively deodorize rooms that have been smoked in.
The company claims its new laundry detergent can deodorize even the strongest smells.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deodorant: a substance applied to the body to prevent or mask body odor
  • synonym – freshen: to make something smell cleaner or newer
  • synonym – sanitize: to make clean and free from bacteria or unpleasant odors
