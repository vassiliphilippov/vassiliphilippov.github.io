smolder

noun
a fire burning with smoke but no flame
Pronunciation: / ˈsmoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈsməʊldə / (BrE)
A fire that burns slowly with smoke but without flame.

Examples

a smolder of smoke
A thick smolder drifted from the ruins after the fire was put out.
The campfire was reduced to a smolder after hours of burning.
They watched the faint smolder in the darkness, waiting for it to die.

  • antonym – blaze: a strong, bright flame or fire
