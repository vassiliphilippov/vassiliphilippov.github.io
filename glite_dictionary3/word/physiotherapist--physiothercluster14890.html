physiotherapist

noun
healthcare professional specializing in physical therapy
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪzioʊˈθɛrəpɪst / (AmE), / ˌfɪziəʊˈθɛrəpɪst / (BrE)
a healthcare professional who treats physical injuries, disabilities, or disorders through exercise, manual therapy, and other physical methods

Examples

physiotherapist treats injuries
After her surgery, a physiotherapist helped her regain strength and mobility in her leg.
Many athletes work with a physiotherapist to prevent injuries and improve their performance.
The hospital hired a new physiotherapist to expand their rehabilitation services.
