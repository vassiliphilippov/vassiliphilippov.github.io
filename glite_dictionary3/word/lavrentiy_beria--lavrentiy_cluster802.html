Lavrentiy Beria

proper noun
Soviet chief of secret police under Stalin
Pronunciation: / lɑːvˈrɛn.ti bəˈri.ə / (AmE), / lʌvˈrɛnti bəˈriːə / (BrE)
(1899–1953) Soviet chief of secret police under Joseph Stalin, known for overseeing the security apparatus and involved in political purges; executed after Stalin's death.
