nihilistic

adjective
relating to a belief that life is meaningless
Pronunciation: / ˌnaɪəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɪhɪˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
Believing that life is meaningless or rejecting all religious and moral principles, often associated with nihilism.

Examples

nihilistic worldview
Critics describe the novel's ending as darkly nihilistic and unsettling.
His nihilistic view of the world left him feeling empty and detached.
The film explores a nihilistic philosophy, rejecting traditional values and beliefs.

Related Terms

  • synonym – nihilism: the rejection of all religious and moral principles, often in the belief that life is meaningless
  • antonym – optimistic: expecting the best possible outcome; hopeful about the future
