roaring

noun
deep, loud, prolonged noise
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
a loud, deep, and prolonged noise, often like an animal's roar or a powerful force

Examples

loud roaring noise
A sudden roaring filled the stadium when the home team scored.
He awoke to the roaring of thunder during the night.
The roaring of the waterfall could be heard from miles away.
A sudden roaring from the jungle startled the campers during the night.
The arena was filled with the roaring of the crowd as the team entered.
They heard a terrible roaring outside, as if a wild creature was approaching.
