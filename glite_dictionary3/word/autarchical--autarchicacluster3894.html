autarchical

adjective
relating to autarchy or absolute rule
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈtɑːrkɪkəl / (AmE), / ɔːˈtɑːkɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by autarchy; ruled by absolute authority or self-sufficiency.

Examples

autarchical government
An autarchical government does not tolerate dissent or shared decision-making.
During the crisis, they tried to create an autarchical economy, aiming for complete self-sufficiency.
The country's autarchical policies led to economic stagnation and international isolation.

  • synonym – autocratic: relating to ruling with unlimited power or authority
  • antonym – democratic: relating to or supporting democracy or its principles
