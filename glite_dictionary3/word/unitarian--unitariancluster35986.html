Unitarian

adjective
relating to Unitarianism or its followers
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪˈtɛriən / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Unitarianism or its followers, who believe in the oneness of God rather than the Trinity

Examples

Unitarian beliefs
Her beliefs reflected a Unitarian approach, emphasizing reason and individual conscience.
Several towns in New England have historic Unitarian congregations dating back to the 19th century.
The Unitarian church welcomed people of all backgrounds to its Sunday services.
