shrunken

adjective
lean and wrinkled from age or illness
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrʌŋkən / (AmE), / ˈʃrʌŋkən / (BrE)
(of a person or part of the body) lean and wrinkled as a result of age, illness, or shrinkage.

Examples

shrunken face
After months of illness, his face appeared shrunken and his clothes hung loosely on his body.
The fruit had become shrunken and unappetizing after being left out in the sun.
They found her sitting by the fire, a shrunken figure wrapped in blankets.

Related Terms

  • synonym – shriveled: wrinkled and contracted, especially due to loss of moisture or age
  • synonym – withered: dried out and shriveled, especially due to age or disease
  • synonym – wizened: shrunken and wrinkled, often with age
