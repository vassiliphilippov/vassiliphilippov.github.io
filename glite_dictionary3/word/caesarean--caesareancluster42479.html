caesarean

noun
surgical delivery of a baby
Pronunciation: / siˈzɛəriən / (AmE), / siˈzɛəriən / (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which a baby is delivered through an incision made in the mother's abdominal wall and uterus

Examples

have a caesarean operation
After a long labor, the doctors recommended a caesarean to ensure the safety of mother and child.
Her first child was born by caesarean, but her second was a natural delivery.
The hospital has a specialized team for performing caesarean deliveries.
