turner

noun
flat kitchen utensil for turning food
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːnər / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnə / (BrE)
A cooking utensil with a flat, flexible blade and a long handle, used to turn or serve food.

Examples

flip eggs with turner
After cooking the fish, he carefully lifted it from the pan with a turner.
She used a turner to flip the pancakes on the hot griddle.
The silicone turner won't scratch your nonstick pans.
