propriety

noun
correct or appropriate behavior
Pronunciation: / prəˈpraɪəti / (AmE), / prəˈprʌɪəti / (BrE)
the state or quality of conforming to accepted standards of behavior or morals; appropriateness

Examples

social propriety
In matters of business, propriety is highly valued.
She questioned the propriety of his comments during the meeting.
They debated the propriety of wearing jeans to the formal ceremony.

Related Terms

  • antonym – impropriety: failure to observe standards of behavior
