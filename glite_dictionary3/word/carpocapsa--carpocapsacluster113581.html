Carpocapsa

noun
former genus name for codling moths
Pronunciation: / kɑːrpəˈkæpsə /; / ˌkɑːr.pəˈkæp.sə / (AmE), / kɑːpəˈkæpsə /; / ˌkɑː.pəˈkæp.sə / (BrE)
a genus of moths (including the codling moth) whose larvae are pests of apples and other fruit; now usually called Cydia
