organismic

adjective
relating to an organism as a whole
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrɡəˈnɪzmɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡəˈnɪzmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of an organism considered as a whole, especially in contrast to its individual parts or processes

Examples

organismic perspective
His theory takes an organismic perspective, looking at society as a unified entity.
Many biologists argue for an organismic view of ecological communities, treating them as integrated wholes.
The organismic approach in psychology emphasizes studying the whole person rather than isolated parts.
