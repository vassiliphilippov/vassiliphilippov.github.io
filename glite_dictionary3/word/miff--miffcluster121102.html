to miff

verb
to annoy or offend slightly
Pronunciation: / mɪf / (AmE), / mɪf / (BrE)
to annoy or offend someone slightly; to cause to be annoyed

Examples

to miff a friend
He tried not to show that he was miffed by the criticism.
Her sarcastic remarks miffed him more than she expected.
It really miffed me when they arrived late without calling.

Related Terms

  • synonym – annoy: to make someone slightly angry
  • antonym – please: to make someone happy or satisfied
