rummy

noun
a card game based on sets and sequences
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌmi / (AmE), / ˈrʌmi / (BrE)
A card game in which the objective is to form sets or sequences, with the winner being the first to meld all their cards.

Examples

play rummy cards
After dinner, they relaxed with a few rounds of rummy at the kitchen table.
Every Sunday evening, we play rummy with the family.
He taught his friends the rules of rummy during their camping trip.
