delinquent

adjective
guilty of a misdeed or offense
Pronunciation: / dəˈlɪŋkwənt / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪŋkwənt / (BrE)
(of a person, especially a young one) showing or characterized by a tendency to commit crime or engage in antisocial behaviour.

Examples

delinquent teenager behavior
School counselors work with students who are considered at risk of becoming delinquent.
Special programs aim to rehabilitate delinquent youths in the community.
The judge decided to give the delinquent teenager a second chance.

Related Terms

  • synonym – criminal: a person who has committed a crime
  • antonym – law-abiding: obeying the law and not committing crimes
