pyrograph

noun
a design made by burning onto a surface
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪ.roʊˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈpʌɪ.rəˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
a decorative design or image produced by burning a design onto a surface, typically wood or leather, using a heated instrument
