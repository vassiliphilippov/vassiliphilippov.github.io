minor

adjective
not of legal age
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə / (BrE)
(of a person) not having reached the legal age of adulthood; underage.

Examples

minor child
Alcohol cannot be sold to minors under any circumstances.
As a minor, she was not allowed to sign the contract herself.
The club does not allow minors inside after 9 p.m.

  • antonym – adult: a person who has reached the legal age of adulthood
