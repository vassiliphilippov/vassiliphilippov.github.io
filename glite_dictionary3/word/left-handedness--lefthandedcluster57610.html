left-handedness

noun
preference for using the left hand
Pronunciation: / lɛft ˈhændɪdnəs / (AmE), / lɛft ˈhændɪdnəs / (BrE)
The habit or preference of using the left hand for tasks such as writing and eating, instead of the right hand.

Examples

left-handedness in writing
His left-handedness was obvious when he picked up the pen to sign his name.
Many tools are designed for right-handed users, making life more challenging for those with left-handedness.
Studies have explored the connection between left-handedness and creativity.
