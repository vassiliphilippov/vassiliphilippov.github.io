Schadenfreude

noun
pleasure at another's misfortune
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːdənˌfrɔɪdə / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːdənˌfrɔɪdə / (BrE)
Pleasure derived from another person's misfortune or pain.

Examples

Schadenfreude at failure
It was hard to hide her Schadenfreude when her rival failed the exam.
Some people can't help but feel a bit of Schadenfreude when celebrities make mistakes in public.
The internet is often a platform for collective Schadenfreude when viral mishaps occur.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gloating: malicious satisfaction at another's misfortune
