babygram

noun
X-ray of an infant's whole body
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.biˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.biˌɡræm / (BrE)
a radiographic image showing the entire body of an infant, usually taken to diagnose medical conditions

Examples

babygram X-ray image
A babygram may reveal congenital conditions that would not be seen in a standard X-ray.
Pediatricians use a babygram when they need a full-body image for diagnosis.
The doctor ordered a babygram to get a comprehensive view of the newborn's skeleton.
