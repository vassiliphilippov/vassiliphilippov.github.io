George VI

proper noun
King of the United Kingdom, 1936–1952
Pronunciation: / dʒɔrdʒ sɪksθ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒ ðə sɪksθ / (BrE)
(1895–1952) King of the United Kingdom and the Dominions of the British Commonwealth from 1936 to 1952, and Emperor of India until 1947; succeeded Edward VIII.
