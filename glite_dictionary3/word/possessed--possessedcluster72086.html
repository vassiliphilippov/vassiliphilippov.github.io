possessed

adjective
controlled by a strong force or emotion
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɛst / (AmE), / pəˈzɛst / (BrE)
(of a person) controlled or strongly influenced by a powerful force, such as an emotion, idea, or external power.

Examples

possessed by passion
During the performance, he acted as if possessed by the spirit of the character.
She was possessed by an overwhelming desire to succeed, letting nothing stand in her way.
The artist painted with a possessed intensity, lost in a world of his own emotions.

  • synonym – obsessed: preoccupied or dominated by a particular thought or feeling
