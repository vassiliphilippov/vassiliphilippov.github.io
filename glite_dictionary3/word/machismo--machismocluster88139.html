machismo

noun
exaggerated masculinity; macho behavior
Pronunciation: / məˈtʃizmoʊ / (AmE), / məˈtʃiːzməʊ / (BrE)
strong or exaggerated sense or display of masculinity, often characterized by assertiveness, dominance, or aggression.

Examples

to show machismo
His machismo was evident in everything from his walk to the way he spoke.
She found his constant displays of machismo exhausting and outdated.
The film criticizes the culture of machismo that dominates the sports world.

  • synonym – masculinity: qualities or attributes regarded as characteristic of men
