supercharged

adjective
having increased engine power
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈtʃɑrdʒd / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈtʃɑːdʒd / (BrE)
(of an engine or vehicle) having increased power from being fitted with a supercharger.

Examples

supercharged engine
After installing a supercharged system, the boat's performance improved dramatically.
Many muscle cars from the 1960s were factory supercharged for extra power.
The new sports car has a supercharged engine capable of incredible acceleration.
