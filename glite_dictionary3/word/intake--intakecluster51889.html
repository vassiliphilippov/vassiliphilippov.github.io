intake

noun
the act of breathing in
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌteɪk / (AmE), / ˈɪn.teɪk / (BrE)
the act of breathing in; the drawing in of air or other gases, especially into the lungs

Examples

intake of breath
His rapid intake of breath signaled his surprise.
Regular exercise can increase your lung intake during physical activity.
The doctor measured the patient's oxygen intake to assess lung function.

Related Terms

  • synonym – inhalation: the action of inhaling or breathing in
