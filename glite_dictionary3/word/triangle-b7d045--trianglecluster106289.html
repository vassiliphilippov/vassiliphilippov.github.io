triangle

noun
triangular drawing instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪˌæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ.æŋ.ɡl̩ / (BrE)
a flat, typically plastic or metal, triangular tool used in technical drawing to draw straight lines at specific angles, usually 30°, 45°, or 60°.

drawing triangle
