flinders

noun
small fragments; splinters
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪndɚz / (AmE), / ˈflɪndəz / (BrE)
Small pieces, fragments, or splinters, especially as a result of something being smashed or broken.

The vase fell from the shelf and shattered into flinders all over the floor.
