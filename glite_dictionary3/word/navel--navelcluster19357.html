navel

noun
scar on the abdomen; bellybutton
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪ.vəl / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.vəl / (BrE)
The small, central scar on the abdomen marking the spot where the umbilical cord was attached in the womb; bellybutton.

Examples
