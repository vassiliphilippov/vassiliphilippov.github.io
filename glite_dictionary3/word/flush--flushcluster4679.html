flush

adjective
level or even with another surface
Pronunciation: / flʌʃ / (AmE), / flʌʃ / (BrE)
(of a surface) exactly level with or even to an adjoining surface; forming the same plane.

Examples

flush with the wall
Be sure the lid is flush before securing it with screws.
The new countertop sits flush with the kitchen cabinets, creating a seamless look.
The picture frame was mounted flush against the wall so there were no gaps.

Related Terms

  • synonym – level: having a flat and even surface, not sloping or uneven
