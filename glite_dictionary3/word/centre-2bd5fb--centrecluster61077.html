centre

noun
main or most important part
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntər /; / ˈsɛn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛntə /; / ˈsɛn.tə / (BrE)
the main focus or most important part of something, or the person/thing that attracts most attention

Examples

the centre of attention
At the centre of the controversy is the question of personal freedom.

All eyes were on her, as she became the centre of the conversation.
His funny stories always make him the centre of the party.
The new museum was quickly established as the centre of cultural life in the city.
