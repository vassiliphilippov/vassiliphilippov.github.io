spectrograph

noun
photographic record of a spectrum
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛktɹəˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈspɛktrəɡrɑːf / (BrE)
A photograph or other visual record produced by a spectroscope, showing the spectrum of light or other radiation from a source.

A clear spectrograph can reveal the unique spectral lines emitted by a star.
