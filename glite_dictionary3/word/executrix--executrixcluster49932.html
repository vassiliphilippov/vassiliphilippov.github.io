executrix

noun
female executor of an estate
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˌzɛk.jəˈtrɪks / (AmE), / ɪɡˌzɛk.jʊˈtrɪks / (BrE)
A female executor, especially one appointed to administer the estate of a deceased person.

Examples

executrix of a will
As executrix, she was responsible for settling all outstanding debts.
The court confirmed her appointment as executrix after the hearing.
The will named her as the executrix of her husband's estate.
