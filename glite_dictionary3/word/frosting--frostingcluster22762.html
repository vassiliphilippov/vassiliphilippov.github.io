frosting

noun
sweet topping for cakes
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfrɒstɪŋ / (BrE)
A sweet, flavored mixture, usually made from sugar and other ingredients, used to cover or decorate cakes and other baked goods.
