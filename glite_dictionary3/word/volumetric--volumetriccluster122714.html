volumetric

adjective
relating to measurement by volume
Pronunciation: / ˌvoʊ.ljuˈmɛ.trɪk / (AmE), / ˌvɒl.juˈmɛ.trɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of volume or involving the quantity of space that a substance or object occupies.

Examples

volumetric analysis method
The chemist performed a volumetric analysis to determine the concentration of the solution.
The engineer measured the volumetric flow rate of water through the pipe.
