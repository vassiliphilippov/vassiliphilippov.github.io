ENE

noun
compass point between east and northeast
Pronunciation: / iː en iː / (AmE), / iː en iː / (BrE)
the compass point that is halfway between east and northeast, corresponding to 67.5° on the compass.

Examples

ENE direction on compass
A storm was approaching from the ENE, so the sailors prepared the ship.
The wind shifted from the ENE just before dawn, bringing cooler air.
We traveled in an ENE direction for several miles.
