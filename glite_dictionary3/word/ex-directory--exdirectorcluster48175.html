ex-directory

adjective
not listed in the telephone directory
Pronunciation: / ɛks dɪˈrɛktəri / (AmE), / ɛks dɪˈrɛktəri / (BrE)
(of a telephone number) not listed in the public telephone directory, usually at the subscriber's request.

Examples

ex-directory phone number
He gave me his ex-directory contact information after the interview.
Many celebrities choose ex-directory numbers to avoid unwanted calls.
She prefers to have her number ex-directory to maintain her privacy.
