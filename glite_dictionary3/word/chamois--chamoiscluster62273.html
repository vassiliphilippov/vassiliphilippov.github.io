chamois

noun
mountain-dwelling antelope of Eurasia
Pronunciation: / ʃæˈmwɑː / (AmE), / ˈʃæmwɑː / (BrE)
A small agile mountain antelope of Eurasia with short hooked horns, found in the Alps and other mountain ranges.

Examples

wild chamois herd
A single chamois stood motionless, blending into the rocky mountain landscape.
Sightings of chamois are common in the European Alps during the early morning.
The herd of chamois gracefully navigated the rocky cliffs.
