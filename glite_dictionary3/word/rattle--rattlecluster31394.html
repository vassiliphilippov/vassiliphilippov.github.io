rattle

noun
a baby's toy that makes noise when shaken
Pronunciation: / ˈrætl̩ / (AmE), / ˈrætl̩ / (BrE)
A baby's toy that makes a shaking or rattling sound, usually by containing small loose objects inside.

Examples

baby rattle toy
He bought a soft rattle as a gift for his newborn niece.
She shook the rattle to calm her crying baby.
The brightly colored rattle quickly became her favorite toy.
