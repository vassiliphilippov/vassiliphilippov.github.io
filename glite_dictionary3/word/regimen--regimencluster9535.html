regimen

noun
systematic medical or health plan
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdʒ.ə.mən / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒ.ɪ.mən / (BrE)
(medicine) a systematic plan, especially for treatment, diet, or exercise, designed to improve or maintain health.

Examples

strict regimen
His new fitness regimen requires him to work out for an hour every morning.
Many people follow a daily regimen of vitamins, diet, and exercise to stay healthy.
The doctor prescribed a strict regimen of medication and physical therapy after her surgery.
