tailorbird

noun
tropical Asian warbler known for sewing nests
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪlərˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈteɪləˌbɜːd / (BrE)
A small tropical Asian warbler known for stitching leaves together to make and conceal its nest.

Examples

tailorbird stitching leaves
Several species of tailorbird can be found in gardens throughout South and Southeast Asia.
