Dasypus

proper noun
genus of long-nosed armadillos
Pronunciation: / ˈdæ.sɪ.pəs / (AmE), / ˈdæsɪpəs / (BrE)
A genus of armadillos, commonly known as the long-nosed armadillos, and the type genus of the family Dasypodidae.
