unlaureled

adjective
not having received acclaim or reward
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɔr.əld / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɔː.rəld / (BrE)
not having been crowned with laurel or given acclaim; unrewarded or uncelebrated.

Examples

unlaureled artist
Artists sometimes endure years of unlaureled struggle before their work is recognized.
Her unlaureled dedication to her patients went largely unnoticed outside the hospital walls.
Many of history's greatest contributors remain unlaureled by the societies they served.
