gale

noun
a very strong wind
Pronunciation: / ɡeɪl / (AmE), / ɡeɪl / (BrE)
A very strong wind, typically with speeds between 45 and 90 knots, classified as force 7 to 10 on the Beaufort scale.

Examples

a strong gale
Last night, a fierce gale brought down several trees in our neighborhood.
The ship struggled to stay on course during the gale that swept across the sea.
We delayed our hike because a strong gale was forecast for the afternoon.
