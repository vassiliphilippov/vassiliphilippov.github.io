abulia

noun
loss of willpower or initiative
Pronunciation: / əˈbuːliə / (AmE), / əˈbjuːlɪə / (BrE)
A loss or impairment of the ability to make decisions or act voluntarily; an abnormal lack of willpower or initiative, often seen in certain mental illnesses.
