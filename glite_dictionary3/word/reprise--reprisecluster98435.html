to reprise

verb
repeat a musical theme
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpraɪz / (AmE), / rɪˈpriːz / (BrE)
to repeat or return to an earlier theme or section in a musical composition.

Examples

to reprise a song
During the encore, the performers reprised their earlier hit, delighting the audience.
The band reprised the chorus at the end of the song for dramatic effect.
The symphony reprises its opening melody in the final movement.
