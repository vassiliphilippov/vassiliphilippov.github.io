kola

noun
West African tree producing caffeinated nuts
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊlə / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlə / (BrE)
A West African tree that produces large brown nuts containing caffeine, which are used to make cola extract.

Examples

kola tree nuts
The kola tree is widely cultivated in Nigeria for its valuable nuts.
They chewed a kola nut to help stay awake during the long ceremony.
Traditional soft drinks originally used extracts from the kola tree.
