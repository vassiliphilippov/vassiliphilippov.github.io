hemoglobin

noun
oxygen-carrying blood protein
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːməˈɡloʊbɪn / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˈɡləʊbɪn / (BrE)
A protein in red blood cells that carries oxygen from the lungs to tissues throughout the body.
