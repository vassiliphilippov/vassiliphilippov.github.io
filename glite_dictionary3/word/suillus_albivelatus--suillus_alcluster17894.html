Suillus albivelatus

noun
an edible mushroom with reddish-brown cap and white stalk
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːɪləs ˌælbɪˈveɪlətəs / (AmE), / ˈsjuːɪləs ˌal.bɪˈveɪ.lə.təs / (BrE)
A species of edible mushroom with a short, squat form, a reddish-brown cap, and a white stalk, typically found under pines in the spring.
