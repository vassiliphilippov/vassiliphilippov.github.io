apostolical

adjective
relating to a pope or the papacy
Pronunciation: / əˌpɑːstəˈlɪkəl / (AmE), / əˌpɒstəˈlɪkəl / (BrE)
of, relating to, or proceeding from a pope or the papacy as regarded as the successor of the Apostles; papal.

Examples

apostolical authority
He claimed apostolical succession as the basis for his office.
The council debated measures within their apostolical powers.
The new decree was issued under apostolical authority.
