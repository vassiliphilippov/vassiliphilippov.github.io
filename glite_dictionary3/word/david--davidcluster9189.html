David

proper noun
patron saint of Wales
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪvɪd / (AmE), / ˈdeɪvɪd / (BrE)
A Christian saint who is the patron saint of Wales, c. 520–600, known for his devout lifestyle and founding of religious communities.

Examples

Saint David of Wales
Every year, Welsh people celebrate David on Saint David's Day.
Pilgrims visit St. David's Cathedral in Wales to honor the saint.
