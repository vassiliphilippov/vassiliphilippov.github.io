hunger

noun
strong desire or craving
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌŋɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈhʌŋɡə / (BrE)
a strong desire or craving for something other than food or drink

Examples

hunger for knowledge
As a child, she had a hunger for knowledge that kept her reading constantly.
His hunger for success drove him to work harder than anyone else in the company.
Many artists are inspired by a deep hunger for recognition and expression.

Related Terms

  • antonym – aversion: a strong dislike or disinclination
  • synonym – craving: an intense desire for something
  • synonym – thirst: a strong desire for something (not liquid)
