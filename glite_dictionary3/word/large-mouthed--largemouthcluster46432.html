large-mouthed

adjective
having a relatively large mouth
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑrdʒ ˌmaʊθt / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdʒ ˌmaʊθt / (BrE)
Having a mouth that is relatively large in proportion to the rest of the body or face.
