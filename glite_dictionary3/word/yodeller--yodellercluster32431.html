yodeller

noun
singer who performs yodels
Pronunciation: / ˈjoʊdələr / (AmE), / ˈjəʊdələ / (BrE)
a singer who rapidly alternates between chest and head voice, especially in Swiss or Alpine folk music.

Examples

famous yodeller sings
Her dream was to become a professional yodeller and perform around the world.
The Swiss yodeller entertained the crowd with traditional mountain songs.
The festival featured a talented yodeller who impressed everyone with his vocal skill.
