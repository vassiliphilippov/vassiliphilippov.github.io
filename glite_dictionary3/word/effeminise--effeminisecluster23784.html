to effeminise

verb
to make more feminine in appearance or quality
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛm.əˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛm.ɪ.naɪz / (BrE)
to make someone or something have more feminine or womanly qualities or appearance, especially in a way that makes them seem less masculine

Examples

to effeminise a man
He felt his mannerisms were being unfairly effeminised by his peers.
Some critics argued that the movie effeminised the hero's character.
The new fashion trend seemed to effeminise traditional male attire.

Related Terms

  • synonym – feminize: to make more feminine in appearance or character
  • antonym – masculinize: to make more masculine in appearance or quality
