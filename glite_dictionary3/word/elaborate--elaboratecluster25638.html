to elaborate

verb
develop or produce something complex
Pronunciation: / əˈlæbəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈlæbəreɪt / (BrE)
to produce something from basic elements or sources; to develop or refine into a more complex product

Examples

to elaborate a molecule
Certain enzymes elaborate specific molecules within the cell.
Honeybees elaborate wax from the sugars they consume.
The plant cells elaborate complex compounds from simple nutrients.

  • synonym – synthesize: to combine elements to form a more complex product
