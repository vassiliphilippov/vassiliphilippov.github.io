riposte

noun
fencing counterattack after a parry
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpoʊst / (AmE), / rɪˈpɒst / (BrE)
(in fencing) a quick counterattack made immediately after parrying an opponent's lunge.

Examples

swift riposte in fencing
After a perfect parry, he launched a swift riposte to score the point.
She executed a flawless riposte after blocking his attack.
The fencer's quick riposte caught her opponent off guard.

Related Terms

  • synonym – parry: to ward off or deflect an attack, especially in fencing
