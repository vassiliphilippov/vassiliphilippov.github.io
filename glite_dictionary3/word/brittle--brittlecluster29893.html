brittle

noun
hard caramelized sugar candy
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪɾl̩ / (AmE), / ˈbrɪt.əl / (BrE)
A hard, thin sheet of caramelized sugar, often with nuts, eaten as a sweet.

Examples

peanut brittle
