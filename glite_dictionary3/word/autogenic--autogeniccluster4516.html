autogenic

adjective
originating within the body
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
(especially of physiological processes) originating within the body or arising from internal causes.

Examples

autogenic training exercises
Muscle relaxation can occur through autogenic training, which relies on self-induced suggestions.
The patient's recovery was aided by autogenic healing processes within the tissue.
The therapist taught him autogenic techniques to reduce stress and control his anxiety.

Related Terms

  • antonym – exogenic: originating from outside the body
