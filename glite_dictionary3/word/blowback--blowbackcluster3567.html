blowback

noun
auto-loading firearm operating system
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈbləʊˌbak / (BrE)
A system of operation in self-loading firearms where energy from the cartridge case being pushed backward by expanding gases is used to cycle the action.

Examples

blowback operation system
Many submachine guns rely on blowback operation due to its cost-effectiveness and simplicity.
The designer chose blowback operation to reduce the number of moving parts in the weapon.
The pistol uses a simple blowback mechanism, making it easy to maintain and reliable.
