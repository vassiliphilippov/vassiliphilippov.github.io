module

noun
cognitive faculty of the mind
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːdʒuːl / (AmE), / ˈmɒd.juːl / (BrE)
a distinct cognitive or perceptual faculty of the mind, responsible for specific mental processes

Examples

brain module
Researchers debate whether the visual system operates as an independent module or is integrated with other mental functions.
Some theorists propose that the language ability is governed by a specialized module in the brain.
The concept of a cognitive module helps explain how we process information rapidly and unconsciously.
