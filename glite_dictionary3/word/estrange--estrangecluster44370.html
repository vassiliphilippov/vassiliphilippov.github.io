to estrange

verb
make someone feel alienated or distant
Pronunciation: / ɛˈstreɪndʒ /; / ɪˈstreɪndʒ / (AmE), / ɪˈstreɪndʒ / (BrE)
to make someone feel distant, unfriendly, or separated from another person, group, or environment
