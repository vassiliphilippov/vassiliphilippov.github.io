pinnate-leafed

adjective
having pinnate leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪneɪt ˌliːft / (AmE), / ˈpɪneɪt ˌliːft / (BrE)
Having leaves that are pinnate in form, with leaflets arranged on either side of a common axis.

Examples

pinnate-leafed fern
Many ferns are pinnate-leafed and can be identified by the arrangement of their leaflets.
The pinnate-leafed tree stood out among the other broadleaf species in the garden.
The botanist collected samples from several pinnate-leafed plants for her research.
