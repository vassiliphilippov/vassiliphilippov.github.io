Fuji-san

proper noun
the highest mountain in Japan
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːdʒi ˌsɑːn / (AmE), / ˈfuːdʒi ˌsæn / (BrE)
an extinct volcano in south-central Honshu, Japan; the country's highest peak, famous for its symmetrical snow-capped summit and significance in Japanese culture and religion
