rhymeless

adjective
lacking rhyme (in poetry)
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪm.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈraɪm.lɪs / (BrE)
(of verse or poetry) not having rhyme; lacking rhyming lines or sound patterns.

Examples
