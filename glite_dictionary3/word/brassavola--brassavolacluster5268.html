Brassavola

noun
genus of tropical American orchids
Pronunciation: / brəˈsævələ / (AmE), / brəˈsavələ / (BrE)
A genus of tropical American orchids that grow on trees (epiphytic) or rocks (lithophytic), characterized by rhizomatous growth and showy flowers.
