Malay

adjective
relating to Malaysia or its people/language
Pronunciation: / məˈleɪ / (AmE), / məˈleɪ / (BrE)
of or relating to the people, language, or culture of Malaysia, the northern Malay Peninsula, or the western Malay Archipelago

Examples

Malay culture
Many traditional Malay dishes use coconut milk as a key ingredient.
She is studying Malay history at university.
The Malay language is spoken in several Southeast Asian countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Malaysian: relating to Malaysia or its people; broader than Malay, as Malaysia is multiethnic
