mogul

noun
a bump on a ski slope
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊɡəl / (AmE), / ˈməʊɡəl / (BrE)
a small mound or bump of hard snow on a ski slope, formed by repeated turns of skiers

Examples

ski mogul
Advanced skiers enjoy navigating the field of moguls at the mountain resort.
She lost her balance after hitting a large mogul halfway down the slope.
The steep run was covered in moguls, making it challenging for beginners.
