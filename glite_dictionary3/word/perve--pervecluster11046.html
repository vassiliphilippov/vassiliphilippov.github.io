to perve

verb
look at someone lecherously (slang)
Pronunciation: / pɜrv / (AmE), / pɜːv / (BrE)
(slang) To look at someone with amorous or lecherous intentions, often in a way that is inappropriate or makes them uncomfortable.

She felt uncomfortable when she realized someone was perving on her from across the bar.
Stop perving on people at the gym—it's really creepy.
