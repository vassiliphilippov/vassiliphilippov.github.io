vaccinator

noun
person who administers vaccines
Pronunciation: / ˈvæk.sɪ.neɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈvæk.sɪ.neɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person, especially a medical practitioner, who administers vaccines to protect people against diseases

Examples

vaccinator gives shots
During the campaign, teams of vaccinators visited remote villages to provide immunizations.
She trained as a vaccinator to help fight the spread of infectious diseases in her community.
The vaccinator carefully prepared the syringe before administering the shot to the child.

Related Terms

  • synonym – immunizer: a person who immunizes someone, especially by vaccination
