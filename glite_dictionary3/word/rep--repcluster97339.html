rep

noun
informal abbreviation for 'representative'
Pronunciation: / rɛp / (AmE), / rɛp / (BrE)
An informal abbreviation of 'representative,' used especially for a person who represents a company, organization, or group.

Examples

company rep visits
If you have any issues with your order, please contact your sales rep.
She works as a rep for a pharmaceutical company.
The company's rep visited our office yesterday to discuss the new products.
