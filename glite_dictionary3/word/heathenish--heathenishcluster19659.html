heathenish

adjective
not acknowledging the God of major monotheistic religions
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːðənɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈhiːðənɪʃ / (BrE)
Relating to people or practices that do not acknowledge the God of Christianity, Judaism, or Islam, often seen as irreligious or pagan.

Examples

heathenish customs
His neighbors disapproved of his heathenish customs and rituals.
Missionaries sought to convert those they considered heathenish to their faith.
The village still celebrated a few heathenish festivals passed down from ancient times.

Related Terms

  • synonym – heathen: A person who does not belong to a widely held religion, especially Christianity, Judaism, or Islam
  • synonym – pagan: A person holding religious beliefs other than those of the main world religions
