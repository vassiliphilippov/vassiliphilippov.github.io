hanky

noun
informal handkerchief
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋki / (AmE), / ˈhaŋki / (BrE)
An informal term for a handkerchief, a square piece of cloth used for wiping the eyes or nose, or worn as a costume accessory.

Examples

a clean hanky
He always carries a clean hanky in his pocket.
She reached into her purse for a hanky to wipe her tears.
The magician pulled a colorful hanky out of his sleeve during the show.
