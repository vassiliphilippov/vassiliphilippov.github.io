crackle

adjective
decorated with fine surface cracks
Pronunciation: / ˈkrækəl / (AmE), / ˈkrækəl / (BrE)
(of a surface) having a network of fine cracks, especially as in glazes on pottery or ceramics.

Examples

crackle finish on porcelain
She admired the crackle finish on the old porcelain plate.
The antique vase has a beautiful crackle glaze that adds to its charm.
The artist used a special technique to create a crackle effect on the surface of the bowl.
