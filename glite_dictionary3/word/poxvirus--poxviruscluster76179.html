poxvirus

noun
virus causing pox diseases
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑksˌvaɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈpɒksˌvaɪərəs / (BrE)
any of a group of viruses that can cause pox diseases, such as smallpox or cowpox, in vertebrates.
