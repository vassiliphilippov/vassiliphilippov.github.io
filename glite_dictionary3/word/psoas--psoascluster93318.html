psoas

noun
a muscle in the lower abdomen/pelvis
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.əs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.as / (BrE)
either of two large muscles in the lower abdomen and pelvis that flex the trunk and rotate the thigh

Examples

psoas muscle injury
Surgeons must be careful not to damage the psoas during abdominal procedures.
The psoas muscle plays a key role in lifting your knees toward your chest.
Tightness in the psoas can cause lower back pain in athletes.
