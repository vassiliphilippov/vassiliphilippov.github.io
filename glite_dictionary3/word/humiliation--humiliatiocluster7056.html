humiliation

noun
state of shame or loss of dignity
Pronunciation: / hjuˌmɪliˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / hjuˌmɪlɪˈeɪʃən /; / hjuːˌmɪlɪˈeɪʃən /; / hjuˌmɪliˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a feeling of shame or loss of self-respect, or an event that causes this feeling, often from being made to look foolish or inferior
