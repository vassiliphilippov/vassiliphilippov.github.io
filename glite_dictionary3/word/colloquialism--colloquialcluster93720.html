colloquialism

noun
an informal word or phrase
Pronunciation: / kəˈloʊkwiəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / kəˈləʊkwiəˌlɪzəm / (BrE)
a word or phrase that is used in informal language or conversation, rather than in formal speech or writing

Examples

common colloquialism
The phrase 'gonna' is a common colloquialism in spoken English.
The teacher asked us to avoid colloquialism in our formal essays.
Writers sometimes use colloquialisms to make dialogue sound more natural.
