to chivvy

verb
harass or nag persistently
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪvi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪvi / (BrE)
to repeatedly tell someone to do something, or to harass or nag persistently

Examples

to chivvy children along
She had to chivvy her children along so they wouldn't be late for school.
Stop trying to chivvy me into finishing the project; I'll do it in my own time.
The manager was always chivvying his team to meet deadlines.

Related Terms

  • synonym – harass: to subject to aggressive pressure or intimidation
  • synonym – nag: to annoy someone by constantly urging them to do something
