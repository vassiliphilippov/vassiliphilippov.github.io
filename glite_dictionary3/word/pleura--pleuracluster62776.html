pleura

noun
membrane lining lungs and chest cavity
Pronunciation: / ˈplʊr.ə / (AmE), / ˈpljʊə.rə / (BrE)
the thin membrane that lines the chest cavity and covers the lungs

Examples

pleura covers lungs
Each lung is enclosed by a double layer of pleura.
Injury to the pleura may interfere with normal breathing.
The pleura can become inflamed and cause severe chest pain.
