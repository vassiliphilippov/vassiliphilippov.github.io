dispensation

noun
the act of giving out or distributing
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪspənˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪspɛnˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of distributing or supplying something, especially in portions or according to a system

Examples

dispensation of medicine
Government regulations strictly control the dispensation of certain drugs to ensure safety.
Her job involved the dispensation of medicines to patients at the clinic.
The dispensation of food supplies was organized by local volunteers during the emergency.
