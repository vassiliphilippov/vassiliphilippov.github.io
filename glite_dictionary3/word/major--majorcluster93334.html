major

noun
military rank above captain
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdʒə / (BrE)
a commissioned officer in the army, air force, or marines, ranking above captain and below lieutenant colonel

army major
After ten years of dedicated service, she was promoted to major in the Air Force.

The major signed the documents authorizing the new recruits' training.
