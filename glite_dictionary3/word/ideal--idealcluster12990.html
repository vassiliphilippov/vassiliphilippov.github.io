ideal

adjective
existing only as a concept or idea
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdiəl / (AmE), / aɪˈdɪəl / (BrE)
existing only in the mind or as a concept, not in reality; forming a mental image rather than a real example

Examples

ideal world scenario
Her essay contrasted the ideal city described in literature with the flaws of actual urban life.
Some philosophers argue that justice, as discussed in theory, is often only ideal and rarely achieved in practice.
The artist created an ideal landscape that could never exist in the real world.
