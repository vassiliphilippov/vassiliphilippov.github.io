quarantined

adjective
isolated digital item for security

(in computing and security) isolated from a system or network and rendered inaccessible or harmless to prevent the spread of malware or other threats.

Examples

quarantined computer files
The antivirus program quarantined the suspicious attachment to stop it from infecting other files.
After detecting unusual behavior, the system automatically quarantined the affected process for further analysis.
The security team reviewed the logs and found that several emails had been quarantined by mistake.
