auricle

noun
heart atrium's small pouch
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈɔːrɪkəl / (BrE)
a small conical pouch projecting from the upper anterior part of each atrium of the heart

Examples

left auricle of heart
During surgery, the surgeon noted an abnormality in the left auricle.
The auricles of the heart help increase atrial volume.
The right auricle receives oxygen-poor blood from the body.
