smoothness

noun
suave or ingratiating manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsmuð.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsmuːð.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being charming, ingratiating, or suavely polite in manner, often to gain favor or advantage

Examples

smoothness in conversation
His smoothness in conversation made him a favorite among the guests.
She won everyone over with her remarkable smoothness during the negotiation.
Some people saw his smoothness as genuine charm, while others found it insincere.
