to crook

verb
to bend or cause to bend
Pronunciation: / krʊk / (AmE), / krʊk / (BrE)
to bend or cause something, especially a part of the body, to bend

Examples

to crook your finger
He quickly crooked his leg as he dodged the obstacle in the path.
She crooked her arm around his shoulders to comfort him.
The old man crooked his finger to gesture them closer.
