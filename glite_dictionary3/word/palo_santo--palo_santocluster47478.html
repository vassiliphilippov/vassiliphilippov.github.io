palo santo

noun
fragrant South American tree used for incense
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːloʊ ˈsɑːn.toʊ / (AmE), / ˌpɑːləʊ ˈsan.təʊ / (BrE)
A South American tree of dry regions, especially in Argentina and Paraguay, with fragrant resinous wood used for incense and essential oils.

Examples

palo santo incense stick
Artisans in South America sometimes carve palo santo wood into decorative objects.
