fibroadenoma

noun
benign breast tumor
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪbroʊˌædəˈnoʊmə / (AmE), / ˌfaɪbrəʊˌadɪˈnəʊmə / (BrE)
a benign, movable, firm, and non-tender tumor of the breast, commonly found in young women
