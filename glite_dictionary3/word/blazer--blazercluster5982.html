blazer

noun
lightweight, single-breasted jacket
Pronunciation: / ˈbleɪzɚ / (AmE), / ˈbleɪzə / (BrE)
A lightweight, single-breasted jacket, often with a distinct color or stripe, commonly worn as part of a uniform or for smart-casual occasions.

Examples

blazer and tie
He wore a navy blazer and grey trousers to the interview.
Members of the club are required to wear a red blazer at official events.
The school's uniform included a striped blazer with the crest on the pocket.
