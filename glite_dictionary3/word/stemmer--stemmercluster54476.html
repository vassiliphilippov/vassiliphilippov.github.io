stemmer

noun
person who strips stems from tobacco leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛmər / (AmE), / ˈstɛmə / (BrE)
A worker who removes the stems from moistened tobacco leaves and bundles the leaves together for processing.

Examples

tobacco stemmer worker
A skilled stemmer could process hundreds of leaves in a single day.
Many stemmers were employed in the factory during the harvest season.
The stemmer worked quickly to prepare the tobacco leaves for drying.
