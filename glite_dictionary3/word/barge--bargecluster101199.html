to barge

verb
transport by barge
Pronunciation: / bɑrdʒ / (AmE), / bɑːdʒ / (BrE)
to transport goods or people by barge on a body of water

Examples

to barge goods
During the flood, emergency supplies were barged to the isolated town.
The company barges coal down the river to the power plant.
They barged heavy machinery across the canal to the construction site.
