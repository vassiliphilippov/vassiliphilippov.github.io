demeaning

adjective
making someone feel less respected or valued
Pronunciation: / dɪˈminɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈmiːnɪŋ / (BrE)
causing someone to lose their dignity or respect; making someone feel less important or valued.

Examples

demeaning comment
He apologized for making a demeaning remark during the meeting.
She found his comments extremely demeaning and refused to respond.
Some jobs can feel demeaning if you are not respected by your employer.

  • synonym – humbling: causing someone to feel less proud or less important
  • synonym – humiliating: causing someone to feel ashamed and foolish
  • antonym – uplifting: making someone feel more cheerful or inspiring dignity
