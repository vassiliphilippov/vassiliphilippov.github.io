to solarize

verb
convert to solar power
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.ləˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɒl.ə.raɪz / (BrE)
to equip a building, vehicle, system, or site with solar panels or other solar-energy technology so that it runs on electricity generated from sunlight.

Examples

to solarize a building
The city aims to solarize its public buildings within five years.
They solarized the factory roof last summer to reduce energy costs and carbon emissions.
Homeowners are solarizing older houses by adding panels and batteries for backup power.

Related Terms

  • synonym – install (solar panels): to set up solar panels and associated equipment so a property can use solar energy.
  • synonym – retrofit: to add new technology or features to older buildings or systems (often used of installing solar equipment).
