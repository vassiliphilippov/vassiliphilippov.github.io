mudcat

noun
edible catfish of the southern US
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌdˌkæt / (AmE), / ˈmʌdˌkat / (BrE)
the flesh of a scaleless food fish, often farmed, found especially in the southern United States and commonly used as food; another name for certain types of catfish
