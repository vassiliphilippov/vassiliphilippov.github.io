ford

noun
shallow crossing place in river
Pronunciation: / fɔrd / (AmE), / fɔːd / (BrE)
A shallow place in a river or stream where people or vehicles can cross.

Examples

ford in the river
During the dry season, the stream's ford is safe for cattle to cross.
The path led down to a ford where the water was only knee-deep.
We had to wade through the ford to reach the other side of the river.
