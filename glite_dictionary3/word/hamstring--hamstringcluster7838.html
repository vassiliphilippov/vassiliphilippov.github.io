hamstring

noun
tendon/muscle at back of knee or thigh
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmˌstrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈham.strɪŋ / (BrE)
one of the tendons at the back of the knee, or the group of muscles at the back of the thigh that bends the knee and straightens the hip

Examples

pulled hamstring muscle
After tearing his hamstring, he was unable to finish the race.
Stretching before exercise helps prevent hamstring strains.
The athlete spent months in rehab for a hamstring injury.

Related Terms

  • antonym – quadriceps: the large muscle group at the front of the thigh
