to stipple

verb
engrave or draw using dots or strokes
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪpəl /; / ˈstɪp.əl / (AmE), / ˈstɪp(ə)l /; / ˈstɪp.əl /; / ˈstɪpəl / (BrE)
to make a picture, pattern, or texture by applying many small dots or short strokes, especially in drawing, painting, or engraving
