miscue

noun
faulty billiards/snooker shot
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsˌkju / (AmE), / ˈmɪs.kjuː / (BrE)
a faulty shot in billiards or snooker in which the cue slips off the ball

Examples

miscue on the shot
He groaned after his miscue cost him the winning shot in the game.
Her opponent capitalized on a critical miscue during the third frame.
The match was tense, but one unexpected miscue decided the outcome.
