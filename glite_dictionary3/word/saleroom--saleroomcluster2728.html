saleroom

noun
room where goods are displayed for sale
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈseɪlruːm / (BrE)
a room or area where goods are displayed for sale, especially at an auction

saleroom auction event
Paintings were carefully arranged in the saleroom for prospective buyers to view.
