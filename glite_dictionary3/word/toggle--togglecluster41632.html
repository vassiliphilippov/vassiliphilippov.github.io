to toggle

verb
operate by toggle switch
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑɡəl / (AmE), / ˈtɒɡəl / (BrE)
to release, activate, or operate by means of a toggle switch

Examples

to toggle the power
The pilot toggled the landing gear to deploy as the plane approached the runway.
To test the alarm, you should toggle the switch and listen for the beep.
With one quick motion, she toggled the emergency power system on.
