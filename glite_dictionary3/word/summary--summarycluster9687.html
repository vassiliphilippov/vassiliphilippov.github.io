summary

noun
concise account of main points
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌməˌri / (AmE), / ˈsʌməri / (BrE)
a brief statement or account giving the main points of something, without details

Examples

summary of the report
After the meeting, he sent everyone a summary of the key decisions made.
She provided a summary of the research findings at the end of her presentation.
The back cover of the book gives a brief summary of the plot.

Related Terms

  • synonym – abstract: a summary of the contents of a book, article, or formal speech
  • synonym – outline: a general description or plan that gives the main ideas
