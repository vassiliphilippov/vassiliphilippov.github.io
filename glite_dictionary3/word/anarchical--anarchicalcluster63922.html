anarchical

adjective
without order or control
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑrkɪkəl / (AmE), / əˈnɑːkɪkəl / (BrE)
Without order, law, or control; characterized by a lack of authority or government.

Examples

an anarchical society
Some critics argue that the internet is inherently anarchical due to its lack of central governance.
The classroom became anarchical when the teacher left the students unsupervised.
The sudden collapse of the government led to an anarchical state where crime increased rapidly.
