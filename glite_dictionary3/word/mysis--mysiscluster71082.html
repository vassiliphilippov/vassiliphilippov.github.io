Mysis

noun
genus of small crustaceans (Mysidae)
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪsɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɪsɪs / (BrE)
A genus of small, shrimp-like crustaceans, type genus of the family Mysidae, found in marine and freshwater environments.
