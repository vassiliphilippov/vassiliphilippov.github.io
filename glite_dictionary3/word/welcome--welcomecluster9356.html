welcome

adjective
received with pleasure or satisfaction
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛl.kəm / (AmE), / ˈwɛl.kəm / (BrE)
received with pleasure or approval; giving pleasure or satisfaction; freely accepted or gladly permitted.

Examples

welcome news
After a long day in the cold, the hot soup was a welcome treat.
He made all his guests feel truly welcome in his home.
She received the welcome news that she had passed her exams.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unwelcome: not received with pleasure or approval; not welcome
