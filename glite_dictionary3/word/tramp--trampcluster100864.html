tramp

noun
sound of heavy footsteps
Pronunciation: / træmp / (AmE), / tramp / (BrE)
the sound or act of heavy, regular footsteps, often made by people walking together

Examples

the tramp of boots
She recognized the familiar tramp of his boots on the wooden floor.
The tramp of feet echoed down the empty corridor.
We listened to the tramp of soldiers marching past the town square.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clomp: a heavy, dull sound of footsteps
  • synonym – tread: the sound of someone walking
