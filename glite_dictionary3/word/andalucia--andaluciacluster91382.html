Andalucia

proper noun
region in southern Spain
Pronunciation: / ˌændəˈluʃə / (AmE), / ˌændəˈluːθiə / (BrE)
a region in southern Spain, known for its rich Moorish history and cultural heritage, bordering both the Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea.
