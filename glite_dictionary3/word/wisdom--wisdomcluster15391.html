Wisdom

noun
biblical/apocryphal book on wisdom
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪzdəm / (AmE), / ˈwɪzdəm / (BrE)
an Apocryphal book of the Bible, chiefly a meditation on wisdom, ascribed to Solomon but likely written in the first century BC
