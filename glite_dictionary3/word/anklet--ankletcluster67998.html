anklet

noun
electronic ankle monitor
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋklət / (AmE), / ˈæŋklət / (BrE)
an electronic device, worn around the ankle, that monitors a person's location or enforces compliance with court-ordered restrictions such as probation, parole, or house arrest.

Examples

electronic anklet monitor
The court ordered him to wear an anklet that transmits his location twenty-four hours a day.
Officers checked the defendant's anklet and confirmed the GPS signal was active before release.
During house arrest, she forgot the charger for her anklet, causing alerts to the monitoring center.

Related Terms

  • synonym – GPS tracker: a device that uses satellite signals to determine and report its location; often a component of ankle monitoring systems
  • synonym – ankle monitor: an alternative name for an electronic device worn at the ankle to track location for legal supervision
  • synonym – electronic tag: a device attached to a person that electronically records presence or location as part of surveillance
