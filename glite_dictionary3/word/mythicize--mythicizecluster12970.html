to mythicize

verb
make into a myth
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪθɪˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪθɪsʌɪz / (BrE)
to make something into a myth or treat it as a myth; to give something mythical qualities or interpret it in terms of mythology

Examples

to mythicize a hero
Over time, cultures often mythicize their national heroes.
Some writers mythicize historical events to capture the imagination of readers.
The media tend to mythicize celebrities and their personal stories.
If we mythicize the founding of the nation, we may ignore important facts.
Many historians have tried to mythicize the lives of ancient heroes.
The film tends to mythicize the struggles of its main character, turning reality into legend.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mythologize: to create or promote a myth about something
