mignonette

noun
a fragrant Mediterranean annual plant
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪnjəˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˌmɪnjəˈnɛt / (BrE)
a Mediterranean annual plant widely cultivated for its dense, spikelike clusters of greenish or yellowish-white flowers with a strong, spicy fragrance.
