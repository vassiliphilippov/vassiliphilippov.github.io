poke

noun
a sharp push or prod
Pronunciation: / poʊk / (AmE), / pəʊk / (BrE)
a quick, sharp push or prod with a finger or a pointed object

Examples

a quick poke
I felt a sudden poke on my shoulder and turned around.
She gave her brother a playful poke in the side to wake him up.
The child responded to the noise with a curious poke of his finger.
