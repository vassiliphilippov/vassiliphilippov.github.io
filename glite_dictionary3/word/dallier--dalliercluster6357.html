dallier

noun
person who wastes time
Pronunciation: / ˈdæliər / (AmE), / ˈdæliə / (BrE)
a person who wastes time or avoids making decisions or taking action

Examples

lazy dallier
As a notorious dallier, he never finishes his assignments on time.
Instead of acting decisively, she was accused of being a dallier and losing valuable opportunities.
The manager grew frustrated with the dallier in the team who kept slowing the project down.
