draw

noun
football play involving handoff after fake pass
Pronunciation: / drɔ / (AmE), / drɔː / (BrE)
(American football) a play in which the quarterback pretends to pass before handing the ball to the running back, who runs toward the line of scrimmage.

Examples

a draw play
A well-executed draw can result in significant yardage if the defense bites on the fake.
Coach called a draw play, hoping the defense would expect a pass.
The team ran a draw to catch the defense off guard on third down.
