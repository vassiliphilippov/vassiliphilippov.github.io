slight

adjective
delicately thin or slender (of build)
Pronunciation: / slaɪt / (AmE), / slaɪt / (BrE)
(of a person or their build) delicately thin or slender in appearance; not strongly built.

Examples

slight build
A slight girl stood silently at the door, hardly making a sound.
She was known for her slight frame and graceful movements.
The coat hung loosely on his slight shoulders.

Related Terms

  • synonym – slim: thin in an attractive way (of a person's figure)
