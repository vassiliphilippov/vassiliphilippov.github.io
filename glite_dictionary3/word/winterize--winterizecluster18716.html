to winterize

verb
prepare for winter conditions
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.tə.raɪz / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə.raɪz / (BrE)
to prepare (a building, vehicle, or system) for use or protection during the winter season, especially by making adaptations to withstand cold weather

Examples

to winterize a car
Before the first snowfall, mechanics recommend you winterize your car to prevent engine damage.
It's important to winterize your home to save on heating costs during the cold months.
We spent the weekend winterizing the boat so it would be protected until spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym – insulate: to protect or cover with material that prevents heat loss or intrusion
