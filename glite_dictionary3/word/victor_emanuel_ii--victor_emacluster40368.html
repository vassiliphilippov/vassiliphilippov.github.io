Victor Emanuel II

proper noun
king of Italy who unified the country
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪktər ɪˈmænjuːəl səˈkənd / (AmE), / ˈvɪktə ɪˈmænjuːəl səˈkənd / (BrE)
(1820–1878) The king of Italy who completed the unification of Italy by acquiring Venice and Rome.
