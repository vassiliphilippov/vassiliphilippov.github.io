Hydnocarpus laurifolia

noun
tree of western India; source of hydnocarpus oil
Pronunciation: / haɪdˈnoʊ.kɑːr.pəs ˌlɔːr.ɪˈfoʊ.li.ə / (AmE), / hɪdˈnɒ.kə.pəs ˌlɔː.rɪˈfəʊ.lɪ.ə / (BrE)
