severe

adjective
strict and unsparing; harsh in discipline
Pronunciation: / səˈvɪr / (AmE), / sɪˈvɪə / (BrE)
unsparing and uncompromising, especially in discipline or judgment; strict and harsh

Examples

severe punishment
Her father could be quite severe when someone broke the household rules.
The headmaster was known for his severe approach to discipline, allowing no exceptions to the rules.
The judge's severe criticism of the defendant shocked everyone in the courtroom.
