quintette

noun
group of five
Pronunciation: / kwɪnˈtɛt / (AmE), / kwɪnˈtɛt / (BrE)
a group or set of five similar people or things considered as a unit

Examples

quintette of paintings
A quintette of paintings by the same artist hung in the gallery.
The award was given to a quintette of talented young poets.
The project team formed a quintette to tackle the new assignment.
