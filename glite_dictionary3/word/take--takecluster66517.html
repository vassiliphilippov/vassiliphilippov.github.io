to take

verb
interpret or understand (words, messages, actions)
Pronunciation: / teɪk / (AmE), / teɪk / (BrE)
to interpret or understand something in a particular way; to perceive or construe the meaning or intention of words, actions, or situations

Examples

to take a comment seriously
How did you take her sudden resignation? Was it a surprise to you?
Please don't take my comments as criticism; I only want to help.
Some people take his jokes literally, but they are meant to be ironic.

Related Terms

  • synonym – interpret: to explain or understand the meaning of something
  • antonym – misinterpret: to interpret incorrectly
