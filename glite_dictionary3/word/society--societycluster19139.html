society

noun
the fashionable social elite
Pronunciation: / səˈsaɪəti / (AmE), / səˈsaɪəti / (BrE)
the group of people in a community who are considered fashionable, wealthy, and influential, often seen as a social elite

Examples

high society
He always dreamed of being accepted into fashionable society.
Many novels describe the strict rules of society in Victorian England.
She was introduced to society at her debutante ball.

Related Terms

  • synonym – aristocracy: the highest class in certain societies, especially those holding hereditary titles or offices
  • synonym – the elite: a select group that is superior in terms of ability or qualities to the rest of a group or society
  • antonym – the masses: the ordinary people in society who are not part of the elite
