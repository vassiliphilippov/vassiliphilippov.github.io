siren

noun
a dangerously seductive woman
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪrən / (AmE), / ˈsaɪərən / (BrE)
a woman who is considered to be very attractive and likely to lead men into dangerous or compromising situations; a dangerously seductive woman

Examples

seductive siren woman
He warned his friend not to fall for the siren who had broken many hearts before.
The film's protagonist is ensnared by a siren who manipulates him for her own gain.
Throughout history, stories have been told of the siren who uses her beauty to bewitch men.

Related Terms

  • synonym – femme fatale: an attractive and mysterious woman who leads men into dangerous situations
  • synonym – temptress: a woman who tempts someone to do something, especially sexually
