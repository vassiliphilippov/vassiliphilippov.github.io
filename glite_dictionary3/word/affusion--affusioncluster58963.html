affusion

noun
baptism by pouring water on head
Pronunciation: / əˈfjuʒən / (AmE), / əˈfjuːʒən / (BrE)
the act of baptizing someone by pouring water on their head

Examples

affusion baptism ceremony

Some Christian denominations prefer affusion over immersion for baptism ceremonies.
The priest performed the affusion by gently pouring water on the baby's head.
