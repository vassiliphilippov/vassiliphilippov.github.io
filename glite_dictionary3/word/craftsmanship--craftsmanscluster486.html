craftsmanship

noun
skill in a craft or trade
Pronunciation: / ˈkræft.smənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈkrɑːft.smən.ʃɪp / (BrE)
Skill and artistry in a particular craft, occupation, or trade, especially as evident in the quality of work produced.

Examples

fine craftsmanship
The exquisite craftsmanship of the wooden table impressed everyone who saw it.
This pottery shows remarkable craftsmanship for such a young artist.
Traditional Japanese craftsmanship is highly valued around the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym – amateurishness: lack of skill or refinement in a craft or occupation
  • synonym – artisanship: skill characteristic of an artisan or craftsman
