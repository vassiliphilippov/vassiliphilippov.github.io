to spellbind

verb
hold the attention of; fascinate
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛlˌbaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈspɛlˌbaɪnd / (BrE)
to hold someone's complete attention as if by magic; to fascinate or captivate intensely
