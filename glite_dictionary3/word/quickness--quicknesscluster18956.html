quickness

noun
ability to think and respond rapidly
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈkwɪknɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being able to think and respond rapidly, especially showing intelligence or mental agility

Examples

quickness of mind
Her quickness of mind impressed everyone during the debate.
Interviewers value quickness in candidates who must solve problems in real time.
The comedian's quickness allows him to make witty remarks on the spot.
