absentmindedness

noun
state of being inattentive or distracted
Pronunciation: / ˌæbsəntˈmaɪndɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌabsəntˈmaɪndɪdnəs / (BrE)
the state of being so preoccupied or distracted that one fails to pay attention to their immediate surroundings or duties

Examples

his absentmindedness at work
Because of his absentmindedness, he often missed important details in meetings.
Her absentmindedness caused her to forget where she left her keys.
The professor's legendary absentmindedness led to many amusing stories among his students.

Related Terms

  • antonym – attentiveness: the quality of being attentive; paying close attention
  • synonym – distraction: the state of being distracted; lack of attention
