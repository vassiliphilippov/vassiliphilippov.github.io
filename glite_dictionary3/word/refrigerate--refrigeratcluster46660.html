to refrigerate

verb
keep something cold to preserve it
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfrɪdʒəˌreɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɪdʒəreɪt / (BrE)
to keep something cold, especially to preserve it or prevent it from spoiling

Examples
