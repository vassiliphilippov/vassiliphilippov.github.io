uncrowned

adjective
(of a tooth) without an artificial crown
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkraʊnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkraʊnd / (BrE)
(of a tooth) not fitted with an artificial crown, especially referring to molars and bicuspids.

Examples

uncrowned tooth
Her uncrowned bicuspid was sensitive to cold foods.
Many patients have several uncrowned teeth that require restorative work.
The dentist examined the uncrowned molar to assess the extent of decay.

Related Terms

  • antonym – crowned: (of a tooth) fitted with an artificial crown
