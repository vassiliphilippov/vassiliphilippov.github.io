Alan Shepard

proper noun
first American astronaut in space
Pronunciation: / ˈælən ˈʃɛpərd / (AmE), / ˈælən ˈʃɛpəd / (BrE)
(1923–1998) American astronaut, naval officer, and test pilot who became the first American in space with his suborbital flight in 1961.
