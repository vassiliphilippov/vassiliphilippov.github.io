Bart

noun
abbreviation for baronet (British title)
Pronunciation: / bɑrt / (AmE), / bɑːt / (BrE)
A title abbreviation used after a man's name to indicate that he is a baronet, a member of the British order of honor ranking below a baron and above a knight.

Examples

Sir Bart.
He was introduced as Sir Malcolm Reynolds, Bart., at the formal dinner.
Official documents listed his name as Sir Anthony Forth, Bart.
The invitation was addressed to Sir Julian Taylor, Bart., and Lady Taylor.
