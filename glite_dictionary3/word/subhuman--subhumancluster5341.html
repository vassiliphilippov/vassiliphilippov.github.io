subhuman

adjective
unfit for human beings; below human standards
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈhjumən / (AmE), / sʌbˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
(of conditions, treatment, etc.) below the level considered acceptable for human beings; unfit for human life.

Examples

subhuman living conditions
Access to clean water and sanitation put an end to the subhuman circumstances in the neighborhood.
The factory workers protested against the subhuman environment in which they were forced to work.
The refugees were living in subhuman conditions with little food or shelter.

Related Terms

  • synonym – inhuman: lacking human qualities of compassion and mercy; cruel or barbaric
