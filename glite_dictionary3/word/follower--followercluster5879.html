follower

noun
person who accepts leadership or guidance
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːloʊər / (AmE), / ˈfɒləʊə / (BrE)
a person who supports and accepts the guidance or leadership of someone else

Examples

loyal follower
He was a loyal follower of the king, always ready to serve.
Many young people became devoted followers of the famous musician.
The political candidate has thousands of followers on social media.
