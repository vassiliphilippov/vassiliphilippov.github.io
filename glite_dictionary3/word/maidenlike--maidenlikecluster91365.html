maidenlike

adjective
like or characteristic of a maiden
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Having the qualities or appearance traditionally associated with a young, unmarried woman; modest or demure.

Examples

maidenlike innocence
Her maidenlike innocence charmed everyone she met.
She lowered her eyes with a maidenlike modesty.
The painting depicted a young woman with a maidenlike grace and poise.
