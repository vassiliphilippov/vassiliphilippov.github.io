hypotonus

noun
state of reduced muscle tone
Pronunciation: / haɪˈpoʊ.tə.nəs / (AmE), / haɪˈpəʊ.tə.nəs / (BrE)
A condition in which muscular tissue has diminished tone or tension; the state of being hypotonic.

Examples

hypotonus in muscles
