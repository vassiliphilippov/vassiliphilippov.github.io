Ayrshire

noun
breed of dairy cattle from Scotland
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌʃɪr / (AmE), / ˈɛəʃə / (BrE)
a hardy breed of dairy cattle originating from Ayr, Scotland, known for high milk production and adaptability
