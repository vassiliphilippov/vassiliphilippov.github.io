plexiglass

noun
weather-resistant transparent plastic material
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛksiˌɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈplɛksɪˌɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a lightweight, transparent, and weather-resistant thermoplastic material used as a glass substitute, especially in windows, displays, and barriers

Examples

plexiglass window panel
After the storm, the broken plexiglass panel was quickly replaced.
Because plexiglass is both durable and transparent, it's often used for aquarium windows.
The store installed plexiglass shields at the checkout counters to protect employees and customers.
