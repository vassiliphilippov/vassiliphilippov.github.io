main

noun
player's primary game character
Pronunciation: / meɪn / (AmE), / meɪn / (BrE)
(in gaming) a player's primary in-game character or character class, the one they habitually use and identify with.

Examples

game main
In this game, my main is a stealthy archer built for quick strikes.
She switched her main from a mage to a tank to help in raids.
Pro players often master several mains but usually have one they prefer in tournaments.

Related Terms

  • antonym – alt: an alternate character used less often than a player's main
  • synonym – main character: the primary character a player uses most often in a game
