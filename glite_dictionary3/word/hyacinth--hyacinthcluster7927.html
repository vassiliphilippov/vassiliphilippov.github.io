hyacinth

noun
spring-flowering bulbous plant
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪəsɪnθ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəsɪnθ / (BrE)
a bulbous plant of the genus Hyacinthus, cultivated for its fragrant, typically blue, purple, or white flowers that appear in spring.

Examples

hyacinth spring flowers
Gardeners often plant hyacinth bulbs in autumn for vibrant flowers in April.
She arranged a vase of hyacinths on the dining table for a touch of spring color.
The hyacinth bloomed early this year, filling the garden with its sweet scent.
