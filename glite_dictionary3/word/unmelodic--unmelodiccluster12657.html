unmelodic

adjective
lacking melody or musical quality
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.məˈlɑː.dɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌn.məˈlɒ.dɪk / (BrE)
Not having a melody or pleasant tune; lacking a musical quality.

Examples

harsh unmelodic voice
Her singing sounded harsh and unmelodic, lacking the usual charm of her performances.
The composer deliberately used an unmelodic structure to create a sense of unease.
The critics described the band's latest album as oddly unmelodic compared to their previous work.
