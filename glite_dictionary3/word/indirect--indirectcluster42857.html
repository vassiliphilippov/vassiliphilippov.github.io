indirect

adjective
not as a direct effect or result
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.dəˈrɛkt, ˌɪn.daɪˈrɛkt /; / ˌɪn.dəˈrɛkt, ɪnˈdaɪ.rɛkt / (AmE), / ˌɪn.dɪˈrɛkt, ˌɪn.daɪˈrɛkt /; / ˌɪn.dɪˈrɛkt, ˌɪndaɪˈrɛkt / (BrE)
not happening or caused directly, but through an intermediate step, person, or thing

Examples
