Hypoxis

noun
genus of star grass plants
Pronunciation: / haɪˈpɑksɪs / (AmE), / hɪˈpɒksɪs / (BrE)
a genus of small perennial plants, often called star grass, with grass-like leaves and star-shaped yellow flowers that grow from corms and have leafless flowering stalks
