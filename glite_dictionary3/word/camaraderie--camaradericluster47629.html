camaraderie

noun
mutual trust and friendship
Pronunciation: / ˌkæməˈrɑdəri / (AmE), / ˌkæməˈrɑːdəri / (BrE)
mutual trust and friendly relationships among people who spend a lot of time together

Examples

workplace camaraderie
She missed the camaraderie of her colleagues after she retired from the company.
The camaraderie among the teammates was evident after they won the championship.
Volunteering at the shelter created a sense of camaraderie among the workers.
