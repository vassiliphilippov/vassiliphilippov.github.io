particulate

adjective
composed of separate fine particles
Pronunciation: / pɑrˈtɪkjələt / (AmE), / pɑːˈtɪkjʊlət / (BrE)
consisting of or composed of minute, separate particles, especially as distinct from a continuous substance

Examples

particulate filter system
A filter is required to remove particulate contaminants from drinking water.
Regulations limit the amount of particulate emissions that factories can release into the atmosphere.
The scientists analyzed the particulate matter in the air samples to assess pollution levels.

Related Terms

  • antonym – continuous: forming an unbroken whole; without interruption
  • synonym – granular: consisting of small grains or granules
