Kangchenjunga

proper noun
mountain in the Himalayas; third highest in the world
Pronunciation: / kæn.tʃɛnˈdʒʊŋ.gə / (AmE), / kæn.tʃɛnˈdʒʌŋ.gə / (BrE)
a mountain in the Himalayas, on the border between Nepal and the Indian state of Sikkim, the third highest peak in the world at 28,208 feet (8,598 meters)
