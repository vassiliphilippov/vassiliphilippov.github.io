extrospective

adjective
observing the external world, not oneself
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəˈspɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəʊˈspɛktɪv / (BrE)
Focused on observing or examining the external world rather than one’s own thoughts or feelings; not introspective.

Examples

extrospective attitude
Her extrospective approach made her very aware of her environment.
The seminar encouraged students to become more extrospective and attentive to others.
Unlike his extrospective friends, Mark often lost himself in thought.

Related Terms

  • antonym – introspective: examining one's own thoughts and feelings
