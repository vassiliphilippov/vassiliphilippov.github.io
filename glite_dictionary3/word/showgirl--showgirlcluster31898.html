showgirl

noun
female stage dancer or entertainer
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊˌɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
A woman who performs as a dancer or entertainer in a chorus line or stage show, often in elaborate costumes.

Examples

showgirl in costume
As a young showgirl, she rehearsed for hours before each evening's performance.
She dreamed of becoming a showgirl in Las Vegas one day.
The musical featured a shimmering line of showgirls in glittering costumes.
