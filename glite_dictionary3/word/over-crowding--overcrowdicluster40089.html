over-crowding

noun
excessive crowding in a space
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈkraʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkraʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
The situation in which there are too many people or things in one place, making it uncomfortable or difficult to function.

Examples

over-crowding in trains
Over-crowding in the subway during rush hour makes commuting stressful for many people.
Due to over-crowding in the shelter, some residents had to sleep on the floor.
The committee discussed measures to reduce over-crowding in city schools.
