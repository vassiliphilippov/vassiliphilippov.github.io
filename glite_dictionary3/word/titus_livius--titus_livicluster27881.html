Titus Livius

proper noun
Roman historian (59 BC–AD 17)
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪ.təs lɪˈvaɪ.əs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪ.təs lɪˈvɪ.i.əs / (BrE)
(59 BC–AD 17) A Roman historian best known for his multi-volume work 'Ab Urbe Condita,' chronicling the history of Rome from its foundation.
