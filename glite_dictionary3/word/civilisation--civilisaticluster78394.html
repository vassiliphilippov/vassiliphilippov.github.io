civilisation

noun
particular society at a given time/place
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪvələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪvəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌsɪvɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an advanced and organized society, especially one from history with its own culture and institutions
