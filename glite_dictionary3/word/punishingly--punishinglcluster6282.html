punishingly

adverb
in a harsh or severe manner
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌnɪʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈpʌnɪʃɪŋli / (BrE)

The marathon runners pushed through the punishingly hot weather to reach the finish line.
