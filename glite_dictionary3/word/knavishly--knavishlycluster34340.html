knavishly

adverb
in a cunning or deceitful way
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪvɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈneɪvɪʃli / (BrE)
In a cunning, deceitful, or dishonest manner.

Examples

to act knavishly in business

She smiled knavishly, knowing she had outwitted her opponents.
They knavishly misled everyone to achieve their own goals.
