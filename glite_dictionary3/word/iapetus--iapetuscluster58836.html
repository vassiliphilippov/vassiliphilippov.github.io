Iapetus

proper noun
Titan in Greek mythology, father of Atlas
Pronunciation: / aɪˈæpətəs / (AmE), / aɪˈæpɪtəs / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, a Titan who was the father of Atlas, Epimetheus, and Prometheus.
