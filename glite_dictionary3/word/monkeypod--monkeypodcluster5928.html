monkeypod

noun
a tropical American tree with showy flowers and seed pods
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋkiˌpɑd / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋkiˌpɒd / (BrE)
A large tropical American tree (Samanea saman) with bipinnate leaves, clusters of flowers with crimson stamens, and seed pods often eaten by cattle; also called the rain tree.
