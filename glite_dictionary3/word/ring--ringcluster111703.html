to ring

verb
to surround or encircle something
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪŋ / (BrE)
to form a circle around; to surround or enclose on all sides

Examples

ring the city
A crowd rings the stage during the concert, eager to get a closer look.
Police ringed the building to prevent anyone from entering or leaving.
The mountains ring the small village, creating a natural barrier.
