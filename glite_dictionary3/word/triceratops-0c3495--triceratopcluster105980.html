triceratops

noun
three-horned dinosaur with neck frill
Pronunciation: / traɪˈsɛrəˌtɑps / (AmE), / traɪˈsɛrətɒps / (BrE)
a large herbivorous dinosaur of the late Cretaceous period, having three horns on its head and a large bony frill at the neck

Examples

a triceratops skeleton
