to anesthetize

verb
administer an anesthetic
Pronunciation: / əˈnɛsθəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / əˈniːsθətaɪz / (BrE)
to administer an anesthetic to (a person or animal) so as to induce loss of sensation or consciousness

Examples

to anesthetize a patient
Dentists often anesthetize the area around a tooth before performing an extraction.
It took only minutes to anesthetize the injured animal before surgery.
The surgeon will anesthetize the patient before starting the operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sedate: to calm or induce sleep by administering a sedative
  • antonym – wake: to cause (someone) to stop sleeping or become conscious
