avocational

adjective
relating to a hobby or secondary activity
Pronunciation: / ˌævəˈkeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌævəˈkeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or engaged in an avocation; not connected with one's main job or profession

Examples

avocational interests
He became known for his avocational interest in classical music, even though he worked as an engineer.
Her avocational pursuits include painting and photography, which she enjoys outside her professional commitments.
The organization coordinates various avocational workshops for those seeking personal growth and enrichment.
