contagiously

adverb
in a contagious way
Pronunciation: / kənˈteɪdʒəsli / (AmE), / kənˈteɪdʒəsli / (BrE)
In a way that is easily spread from one person to another, either literally (as with disease) or figuratively (as with mood or behavior).

Examples

laugh contagiously
His laughter spread contagiously through the room until everyone was chuckling.
Joy seemed to radiate from her contagiously, brightening everyone's mood.
The teacher's enthusiasm for science was contagiously passed on to her students.

Related Terms

  • synonym – infectiously: in a way that spreads easily between people; synonym for 'contagiously'
