recluse

noun
person who lives in solitude
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkluːs / (AmE), / rɪˈkluːs / (BrE)
a person who lives alone and avoids other people; someone who withdraws from society

Examples

a recluse lives alone
After losing his family, the author became a recluse and rarely spoke to anyone.
She admired the peaceful lifestyle of a recluse, far from the noise of society.
The recluse lived on the edge of town, surrounded by woods and wildlife.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hermit: a person living in seclusion, often for religious reasons
