positive

adjective
affirmative or approving in attitude or outlook
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːzɪtɪv /; / ˈpɑːzətɪv / (AmE), / ˈpɒzɪtɪv /; / ˈpɒzətɪv / (BrE)
showing a constructive, approving, or beneficial attitude; producing good results

Examples

positive attitude
All the feedback we received about the project was overwhelmingly positive.
His response was positive, assuring us that everything would be fine.
She always tries to maintain a positive outlook, even during difficult times.
Exercise can have many positive benefits for both body and mind.
The new policy is having a positive effect on student performance.
We should focus on the positive aspects of change rather than the negative ones.
