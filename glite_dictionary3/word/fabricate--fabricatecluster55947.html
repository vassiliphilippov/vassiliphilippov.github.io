to fabricate

verb
manufacture or construct from components
Pronunciation: / ˈfæbrɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈfæbrɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to construct or manufacture something, typically from prepared components

Examples

to fabricate a machine
He fabricated the device using materials from the hardware store.
Modern factories fabricate thousands of components every day.
The company fabricates metal frames for industrial use.
