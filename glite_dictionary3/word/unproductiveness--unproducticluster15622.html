unproductiveness

noun
lack of productivity or results
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprəˈdʌktɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnprəˈdʌktɪvnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of not producing or achieving results; lack of productivity

Examples

chronic unproductiveness
Chronic unproductiveness can harm both morale and profits in a company.

The meeting was marked by remarkable unproductiveness and little was accomplished.
