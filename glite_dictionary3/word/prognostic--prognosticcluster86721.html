prognostic

noun
sign or omen of something to come
Pronunciation: / prɑːɡˈnɑːstɪk / (AmE), / prɒɡˈnɒstɪk / (BrE)
A sign or indication of a future event; an omen or predictive symptom.

Examples

prognostic sign
Many considered the dark clouds a prognostic of the approaching storm.
Some physicians regard certain early symptoms as a prognostic for the development of the disease.
The sudden drop in stock prices was seen as a prognostic of economic recession.

  • synonym – omen: a sign that is believed to predict the future
