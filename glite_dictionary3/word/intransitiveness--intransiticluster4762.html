intransitiveness

noun
quality of being intransitive (of verbs)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtrænzətɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈtranzɪtɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being intransitive; the condition of a verb that does not require or cannot take a direct object
