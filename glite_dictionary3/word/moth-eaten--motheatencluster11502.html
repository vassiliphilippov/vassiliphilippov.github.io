moth-eaten

adjective
old and worn; shabby or damaged
Pronunciation: / mɔθ ˌitən /; / ˈmɔθ ˌitn̩ /; / ˈmɔθˌitn̩ / (AmE), / mɒθ ˌiːtən /; / ˈmɒθ ˌiːtn̩ /; / ˈmɒθˌiːtn̩ / (BrE)
old, worn, or damaged in appearance, especially like something eaten by moths; shabby or tattered
