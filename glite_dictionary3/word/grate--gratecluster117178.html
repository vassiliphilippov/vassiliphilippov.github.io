grate

noun
harsh scraping noise
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt / (AmE), / ɡreɪt / (BrE)
A harsh, grating, or scraping sound, typically produced by friction or abrasion.

Examples

the grate of metal
I woke to the grate of metal dragging over concrete outside my window.
The grate of chalk on the blackboard made everyone cringe.
The grate of her voice was unmistakable after a night of shouting.
