lamivudine

noun
antiviral drug for HIV and AIDS
Pronunciation: / ləˈmɪv.juːˌdin / (AmE), / ləˈmɪv.juːˌdiːn / (BrE)
a nucleoside reverse transcriptase inhibitor used in combination with other drugs to treat HIV and AIDS
