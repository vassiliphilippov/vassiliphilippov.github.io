transformation

noun
mathematical function altering axes
Pronunciation: / ˌtræns.fərˈmeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌtrænz.fəˈmeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
(mathematics) A function that changes the position or direction of the axes of a coordinate system.

Examples

geometric transformation
A reflection is a type of geometric transformation that flips a figure over a line.
Linear algebra studies how a transformation can change a vector's position in space.
The transformation rotated the entire graph by 90 degrees.
