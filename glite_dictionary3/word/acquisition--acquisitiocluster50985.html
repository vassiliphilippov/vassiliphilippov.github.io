acquisition

noun
ability gained by training or study
Pronunciation: / ˌækwəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌækwɪˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
a skill or ability that has been gained by learning or practice

Examples

Proper acquisition of driving abilities requires both lessons and practice.
Singing was an acquisition developed through years of training.
