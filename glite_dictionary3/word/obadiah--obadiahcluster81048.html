Obadiah

proper noun
book of prophecy in the Old Testament
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊbəˈdaɪə / (AmE), / ˌəʊbəˈdaɪə / (BrE)
A book of the Old Testament containing the prophecies of Obadiah; the shortest book in the Christian Bible.

Examples

In Bible study groups, Obadiah is often read alongside Amos and other minor prophets.
Many scholars debate the historical context behind Obadiah and his message to Edom.
