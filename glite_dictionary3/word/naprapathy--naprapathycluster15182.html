naprapathy

noun
system of drugless therapy for connective tissues
Pronunciation: / nəˈpræpəθi / (AmE), / nəˈprapəθi / (BrE)
A system of treatment that does not use drugs, based on the belief that symptoms of disease are caused by disorders of ligaments and connective tissues.
