interpreter

noun
person who translates spoken language
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɝːprətɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːprɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to translate spoken words from one language into another, typically in real time

Examples

interpreter translates speech
She works as an interpreter for visiting foreign delegations.
The UN meeting included an interpreter to ensure everyone understood the discussions.
Without an experienced interpreter, communication would have been impossible.
