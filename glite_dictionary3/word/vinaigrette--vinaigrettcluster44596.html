vinaigrette

noun
oil-and-vinegar salad dressing
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪnəˈɡrɛt / (AmE), / ˌvɪnɪˈɡrɛt / (BrE)
A salad dressing made from oil and vinegar, typically seasoned with mustard, garlic, and other ingredients.

Examples

vinaigrette salad dressing
My favorite salad is tossed with a lemon-garlic vinaigrette and topped with grilled chicken.
She drizzled vinaigrette over the fresh greens for a light, tangy flavor.
The chef whipped up a homemade vinaigrette using olive oil, red wine vinegar, and Dijon mustard.
