complaint

noun
formal legal accusation; initial pleading in a case
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpleɪnt / (AmE), / kəmˈpleɪnt / (BrE)
(Law) a formal statement made to a court or authority that someone has committed an offense or is responsible for a problem
