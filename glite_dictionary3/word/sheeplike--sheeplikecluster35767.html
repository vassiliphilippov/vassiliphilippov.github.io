sheeplike

adjective
docile or timid like a sheep
Pronunciation: / ˈʃipˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈʃiːp.laɪk / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of a sheep, especially in being docile, timid, or easily led.

Examples

sheeplike obedience
His sheeplike obedience made it easy for others to take advantage of him.
She gave a sheeplike smile and followed the group without question.
The students waited in sheeplike silence for the teacher's instructions.

Related Terms

  • antonym – assertive: having or showing a confident and forceful personality
  • synonym – docile: willing to be taught; submissive
  • synonym – timid: showing a lack of courage or confidence; easily frightened
