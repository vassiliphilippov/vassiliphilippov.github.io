Pinus aristata

noun
a long-lived pine tree of the Rocky Mountains
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪnəs əˈrɪstətə / (AmE), / ˈpɪnʊs ˌarɪˈsteɪtə / (BrE)
a small, slow-growing pine tree native to the Rocky Mountains of the western United States, known for its longevity and dense branches, with short needles in bundles of five and cones with thorn-tipped scales
