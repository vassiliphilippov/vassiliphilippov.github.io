bounty

noun
abundant amount; generosity
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊnti / (AmE), / ˈbaʊnti / (BrE)
a generous amount or abundance of something, especially when given as a gift or provided by nature

Examples

nature's bounty
The garden gave us a bounty that lasted all summer.
The orchard produced a bounty of apples after the rainy season.
We were grateful for the bounty of food on the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym – abundance: a very large quantity of something
  • synonym – profusion: an abundance or large quantity of something
  • antonym – scarcity: the state of being in short supply
