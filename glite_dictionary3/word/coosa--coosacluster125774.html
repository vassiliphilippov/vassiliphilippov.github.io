Coosa

proper noun
river in Georgia and Alabama, USA
Pronunciation: / ˈkusə / (AmE), / ˈkuːsə / (BrE)
A river in the southeastern United States, rising in northwestern Georgia and flowing southwest through eastern Alabama to join the Tallapoosa River near Montgomery, forming the Alabama River.

the Coosa River
Many cities and towns developed along the Coosa because of its importance for transportation and trade.
