stripped

adjective
having contents or covering removed
Pronunciation: / strɪpt / (AmE), / strɪpt / (BrE)
having had everything extraneous or contents removed; left bare or empty

Examples

stripped of furniture
After the move, the once cozy living room was completely stripped of furniture.
The abandoned storefront had stripped walls, revealing patches of old paint.
When the house was repossessed, it was left stripped and empty.
