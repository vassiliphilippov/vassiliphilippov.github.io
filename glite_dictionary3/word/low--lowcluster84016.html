low

adjective
less than usual in degree or amount
Pronunciation: / loʊ / (AmE), / ləʊ / (BrE)
not enough in amount or quantity; below the usual or expected level; insufficient

Examples

low energy level
Our stocks of food are running low after the long winter.
She spoke in a low voice so as not to wake the baby.
The water level in the river is very low this summer.
After several days without rain, the river's water level became dangerously low.
Her energy was low after the long hike in the mountains.
We're running low on printer paper, so we need to order more soon.
