sprinkling

noun
a light, patchy rain shower
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋklɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋklɪŋ / (BrE)
a light, brief fall of rain that only affects some spots, not widespread

Examples

a light sprinkling of rain
A light sprinkling fell as we walked to the station, but the sun was shining a block away.
Farmers hoped for more than a sprinkling, as their fields were still too dry.
There was just a sprinkling of rain last night, enough to wet the ground but not much more.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – downpour: a very heavy, sudden rain shower
  • degree higher – drizzle: very light rain, finer than a sprinkling
