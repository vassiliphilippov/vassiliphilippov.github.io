accordance

noun
agreement; conformity
Pronunciation: / əˈkɔrdəns / (AmE), / əˈkɔːdəns / (BrE)
agreement or conformity with a rule, opinion, or request; concurrence or harmony.

Examples

accordance with instructions
He completed the task in accordance with the manager's instructions.
The policy was changed in accordance with recent government regulations.
The teams acted in full accordance to ensure a successful outcome.

Related Terms

  • synonym – agreement: a situation in which people have the same opinion; harmony
  • antonym – disagreement: a lack of consensus or harmony; difference of opinion
