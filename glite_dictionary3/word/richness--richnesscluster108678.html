richness

noun
quality of being fertile or productive
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃ.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of producing abundant and vigorous growth, especially in soil or land

Examples

richness of soil
After years of careful composting, the richness of his garden beds increased noticeably.
Farmers value the richness of the land for its ability to yield excellent crops.
The richness of the rainforest soil supports an incredible variety of plants.
