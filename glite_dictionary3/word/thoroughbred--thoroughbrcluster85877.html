thoroughbred

noun
a purebred animal, especially a horse
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜːroʊˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈθʌr.ə.bred / (BrE)
An animal, especially a horse, of pure breed with a recorded pedigree.

Examples

a pure thoroughbred animal
He paid a high price for a thoroughbred with an impressive pedigree.
The Kentucky Derby features only thoroughbred horses from around the world.
The farm specializes in breeding thoroughbred racehorses.

Related Terms

  • synonym – purebred: an animal bred from parents of the same breed
