cadaster

noun
register of land ownership and value
Pronunciation: / kəˈdæstər / (AmE), / kəˈdɑːstə / (BrE)
An official public register detailing the ownership, boundaries, and value of land for purposes such as taxation.

Examples

cadaster of land parcels
Accurate information in the cadaster is essential for resolving land disputes.
Local taxes were assessed based on data collected in the national cadaster.
The government updated the cadaster to reflect new property lines after the survey was completed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cadastral: relating to a cadaster or to land surveying
  • synonym – land register: an official record of land ownership
