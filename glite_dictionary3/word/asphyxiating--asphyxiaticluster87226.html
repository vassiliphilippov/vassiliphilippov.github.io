asphyxiating

adjective
causing suffocation by lack of oxygen
Pronunciation: / əsˈfɪksiˌeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / əsˈfɪksɪeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing or likely to cause asphyxia by depriving someone or something of oxygen.

Examples

asphyxiating gas leak
He reported an asphyxiating sensation as the air supply was cut off.
The chamber was filled with asphyxiating gas during the test.
Workers evacuated the building when asphyxiating fumes began to spread.
