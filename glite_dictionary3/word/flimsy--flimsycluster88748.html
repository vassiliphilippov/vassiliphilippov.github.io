flimsy

noun
thin lightweight paper for carbon copies
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪmzi / (AmE), / ˈflɪmzi / (BrE)
A thin, strong, lightweight, translucent paper used especially for making carbon copies.

Examples

flimsy carbon paper
He wrote the letter on a flimsy so a carbon copy could be made.
She needed a flimsy to create an extra copy for her records.
The old files are filled with yellowed flimsies from decades ago.
