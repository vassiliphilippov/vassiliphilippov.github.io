veneering

noun
process of applying a veneer layer
Pronunciation: / vəˈnɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / vɪˈnɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of applying a thin layer of material, such as fine wood, to the surface of an object to improve its appearance or finish
