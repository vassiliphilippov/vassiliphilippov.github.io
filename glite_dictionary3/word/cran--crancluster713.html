cran

noun
unit for measuring herring
Pronunciation: / kræn / (AmE), / kræn / (BrE)
a unit of measure, especially for fresh herring, equal to about 37.5 imperial gallons (170 liters)

Examples

cran of herring
He calculated the total shipment by multiplying the number of crans by the average weight per vessel.
Records show that a typical boat could carry several crans per catch during the season.
The fishermen brought in ten crans of herring at the end of the day.
