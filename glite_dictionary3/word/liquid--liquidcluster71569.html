liquid

adjective
(of eyes) full of tears; tearful
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd / (BrE)
(of eyes or a gaze) appearing to be full of tears, giving a smooth or shining quality suggestive of being wet or tearful.

Examples

liquid eyes
He looked at her with liquid eyes, emotions barely concealed.
Her liquid eyes glistened as she held back tears at the movie's ending.
The portrait captured a child’s liquid gaze, as if she were about to cry.
