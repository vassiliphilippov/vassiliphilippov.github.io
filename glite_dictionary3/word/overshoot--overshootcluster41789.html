to overshoot

verb
to go beyond a target or point
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈʃuːt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈʃuːt / (BrE)
to go past or beyond a target, mark, or intended point

Examples

If you overshoot the target, adjust your aim downward next time.
