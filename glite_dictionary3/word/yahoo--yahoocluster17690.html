Yahoo

noun
brutish creature from 'Gulliver's Travels'
Pronunciation: / ˈjæhu / (AmE), / ˈjɑːhuː / (BrE)
A member of a race of brutish and uncivilized creatures, resembling humans, that are subjugated by the Houyhnhnms in Jonathan Swift's novel 'Gulliver's Travels'; often used to describe a crude or uncouth person.

Examples

a Yahoo in society
He behaved like a complete Yahoo at the dinner party, shocking all the guests.
In literary criticism, the term Yahoo is used to describe individuals who are crude and uncultured.
The Yahoos in Swift's novel are portrayed as savage and filthy, contrasting with the wise Houyhnhnms.
