stirrer

noun
person who causes trouble or spreads rumors
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪrɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɜːrə / (BrE)
a person who deliberately spreads rumors or causes trouble to provoke others

Examples

office stirrer
Don't be such a stirrer—there's no need to create drama where none exists.
Every group has its stirrer, always trying to provoke arguments among friends.
She gained a reputation as a stirrer for constantly spreading gossip at work.
