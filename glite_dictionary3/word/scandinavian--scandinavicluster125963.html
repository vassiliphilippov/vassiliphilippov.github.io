Scandinavian

noun
person from Scandinavia
Pronunciation: / ˌskændəˈneɪviən / (AmE), / ˌskændɪˈneɪviən / (BrE)
a person who is from or lives in Scandinavia, typically Denmark, Norway, or Sweden

Examples
