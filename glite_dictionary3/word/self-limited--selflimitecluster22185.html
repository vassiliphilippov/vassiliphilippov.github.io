self-limited

adjective
disease resolving without treatment
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈlɪmɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈlɪmɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a disease or condition) tending to resolve or run its course without requiring medical treatment.

Examples

self-limited illness
Although uncomfortable, the rash is typically self-limited and disappears without intervention.
Many cases of viral gastroenteritis are self-limited and do not require antibiotics.
The doctor explained that the infection was self-limited and would likely resolve in a few days.

Related Terms

  • synonym – acute: disease or condition with rapid onset and short duration
