to damascene

verb
to inlay metal with gold or silver
Pronunciation: / ˈdæməˌsin / (AmE), / ˈdaməsiːn / (BrE)
to inlay metal with gold or silver to produce intricate patterns or designs

Examples

to damascene a sword
Ancient craftsmen often damascened decorative patterns onto armor and weaponry.
Modern jewelers sometimes damascene silver objects to enhance their visual appeal.
