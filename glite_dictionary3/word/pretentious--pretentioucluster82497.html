pretentious

adjective
trying to seem more important or impressive
Pronunciation: / prɪˈtɛn(t)ʃəs /; / prɪˈtɛn.ʃəs /; / prɪˈtɛnʃəs / (AmE), / prɪˈtɛnʃəs /; / prɪˈtɛn.tʃəs / (BrE)
trying to impress others by pretending to be more important, cultured, or knowledgeable than you really are; showy or ostentatious
