bandelet

noun
narrow ring-shaped molding on columns
Pronunciation: / ˈbændəˌlɛt / (AmE), / ˈbændəlɛt / (BrE)
A narrow molding in the form of a ring, especially one located at the top of a column.

Examples

bandelet on a column
In ancient Greek temples, the bandelet was often ornately carved.
The architect added a bandelet to emphasize the graceful transition at the top of each column.
The restoration team carefully repaired the damaged bandelet alongside the Corinthian capitals.
