to bug

verb
to annoy persistently
Pronunciation: / bʌɡ / (AmE), / bʌɡ / (BrE)
to annoy or bother someone persistently, especially in a way that is irritating

Examples

to bug a friend
It really bugs me when people are late for meetings.
Stop bugging your sister while she's trying to study.
The noise from the construction site has been bugging the neighbors all week.

Related Terms

  • antonym – calm: to make someone feel less annoyed or irritated
  • synonym – harass: to trouble or annoy someone repeatedly or persistently
  • synonym – pester: to annoy someone repeatedly
