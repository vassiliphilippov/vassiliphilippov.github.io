pessimistically

adverb
in a pessimistic way
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛsɪˈmɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌpɛsɪˈmɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of hope or expects the worst to happen.

Examples

view pessimistically results
He looked at the bleak job market pessimistically and decided not to apply.
She pessimistically assumed that her proposal would be rejected.
Some analysts pessimistically predict an economic downturn next year.

Related Terms

  • antonym – optimistically: in an optimistic way; expecting a favorable outcome
