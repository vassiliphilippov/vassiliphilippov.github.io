contact

noun
a lens worn on the eye to correct vision
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.tækt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tækt / (BrE)
A thin, curved glass or plastic lens worn directly on the eye to correct vision or administer medication.

Examples

contact lens
I lost a contact while swimming in the pool.
Many people find contacts more comfortable than glasses.
She put in her contact before leaving for work.
