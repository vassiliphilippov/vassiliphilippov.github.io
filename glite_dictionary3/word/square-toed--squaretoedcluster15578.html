square-toed

adjective
exaggeratedly proper or conventional
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɛrˌtoʊd / (AmE), / ˈskweə təʊd / (BrE)
(of a person or attitude) extremely conventional, old-fashioned, or proper, often to an exaggerated degree.

Examples

square-toed attitude
Despite her young age, Maria had a square-toed view of social behavior.
His square-toed opinions were often at odds with his creative colleagues.
The square-toed teacher insisted on strict dress codes and formal language in class.

Related Terms

  • synonym – conventional: following accepted customs and proprieties
  • antonym – liberal: open to new behavior or opinions; willing to discard traditional values
  • synonym – straitlaced: excessively strict in manners or morals
