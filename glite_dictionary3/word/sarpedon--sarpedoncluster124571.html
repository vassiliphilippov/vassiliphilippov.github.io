Sarpedon

proper noun
son of Zeus in Greek mythology
Pronunciation: / sɑːrˈpiːdən / (AmE), / sɑːˈpiːdən / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) a son of Zeus who became king of Lycia and fought for the Trojans during the Trojan War, killed by Patroclus.

Sarpedon in the Iliad
In the Iliad, Sarpedon is depicted as a noble and courageous warrior.
