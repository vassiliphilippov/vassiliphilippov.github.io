wolfishly

adverb
in a wolf-like manner
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊlfɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈwʊlfɪʃli / (BrE)
In a manner characteristic of a wolf, especially suggesting greed, hunger, or ferocity.

Examples

look wolfishly at food
He stared at the cake wolfishly, unable to hide his hunger.
She grinned wolfishly before taking the last slice of pizza.
The dog eyed the steak wolfishly, drooling in anticipation.
