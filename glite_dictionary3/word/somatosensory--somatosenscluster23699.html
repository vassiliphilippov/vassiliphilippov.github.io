somatosensory

adjective
relating to bodily sensations
Pronunciation: / səˌmæt̬oʊˈsɛnsəri / (AmE), / səˌmatəʊˈsɛnsəri / (BrE)
relating to sensations of the body, such as touch, pain, temperature, and proprioception, processed by the nervous system

Examples

somatosensory system function
A somatosensory test assesses a person's response to physical stimuli like pressure and vibration.
Patients with somatosensory deficits may not feel temperature or pressure on their skin.
The somatosensory cortex plays a vital role in processing touch and pain.
