fillet

noun
boneless slice or side of a fish
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪlɪt / (BrE)
a boneless piece or slice of meat or fish, usually cut lengthwise

Examples

fillet of salmon
A fresh cod fillet is perfect for making fish tacos.
He ordered a grilled salmon fillet for dinner at the restaurant.
The chef showed us how to remove the bones from the fillet.
I ordered a beef fillet because it is the most tender cut available.
She prepared a delicious sauce to accompany the pan-seared fillet.
The restaurant specializes in grilled fillet served with seasonal vegetables.

Related Terms

  • synonym – loin: the part of a fish or animal cut from the back, often boneless
  • degree lower – sirloin: another cut of beef, generally less tender than fillet
  • degree higher – steak: a slice of meat, especially beef, cut across the grain
  • degree higher – tenderloin: the whole tenderloin portion of beef from which fillets are cut
