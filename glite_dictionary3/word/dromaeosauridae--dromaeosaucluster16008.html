Dromaeosauridae

noun
family of bipedal predatory dinosaurs
Pronunciation: / droʊˌmiːoʊˈsɔːrɪdiː /; / droʊˌmiː.oʊˈsɔːrɪˌdiː / (AmE), / drəʊˌmɪəʊˈsɔːrɪdiː /; / drəˌmiː.əʊˈsɔːrɪˌdiː / (BrE)
