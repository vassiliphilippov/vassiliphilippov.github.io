relay

noun
fresh team of animals to relieve a tired one
Pronunciation: / ˈriː.leɪ /; / ˈriːleɪ /; / ˈriˌleɪ / (AmE), / ˈriː.leɪ /; / ˈriːleɪ / (BrE)
a team or group (of animals, workers, or athletes) that takes over from another to continue a task or race
