byproduct

noun
secondary product from manufacturing
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌprɑːdʌkt / (AmE), / ˈbaɪˌprɒdʌkt / (BrE)
a secondary product that is produced during the manufacture or processing of something else

Examples

byproduct of production
Glycerin is obtained as a byproduct in soap manufacturing.
Pollution is often an unwanted byproduct of industrial growth.
The plant produces chemicals as its main output and salt as a byproduct.

Related Terms

  • synonym – co-product: a product produced jointly with another in a process
  • antonym – main product: the principal product in a manufacturing process
