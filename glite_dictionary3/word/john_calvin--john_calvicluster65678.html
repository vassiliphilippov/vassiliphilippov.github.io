John Calvin

proper noun
French-born Swiss theologian and Reformation leader
Pronunciation: / dʒɑn ˈkælvɪn / (AmE), / dʒɒn ˈkælvɪn / (BrE)
(1509–1564) A French-born Swiss theologian whose teachings, especially on predestination and justification by faith, greatly influenced the development of Presbyterianism and the Protestant Reformation.
