polite

adjective
having good manners; respectful
Pronunciation: / pəˈlaɪt / (AmE), / pəˈlaɪt / (BrE)
having or showing good manners and respect for other people; not rude

Examples

polite greeting
He thanked the waiter in a polite manner for his service.
It's important to be polite when meeting new people.
She always speaks in a polite and respectful tone.
Although he disagreed, he remained polite throughout the conversation.
It's polite to say thank you when someone helps you.
The receptionist was very polite when she greeted the customers.
