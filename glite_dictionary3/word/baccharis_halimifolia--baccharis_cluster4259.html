Baccharis halimifolia

noun
shrub of salt marshes; white, plumelike fruit tufts
Pronunciation: / ˈbækərɪs ˌhælɪmɪˈfoʊliə / (AmE), / ˈbakərɪs ˌhalɪmɪˈfəʊlɪə / (BrE)
