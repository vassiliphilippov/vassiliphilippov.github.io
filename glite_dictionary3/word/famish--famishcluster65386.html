to famish

verb
deprive of food; cause starvation
Pronunciation: / ˈfæmɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪʃ / (BrE)
to deprive someone of food; to cause to suffer from extreme hunger

Examples

to famish a prisoner
Cruel leaders would sometimes famish prisoners as a means of control.
If you famish a plant of light and water, it will wither and die.
The drought threatened to famish the livestock in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym – starve: to suffer or die from lack of food
