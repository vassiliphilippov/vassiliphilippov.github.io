biennially

adverb
every two years
Pronunciation: / baɪˈɛn.i.əli / (AmE), / baɪˈen.i.əli / (BrE)
Every two years; happening once in each period of two years.

Examples

meets biennially for review
The organization meets biennially to review its progress and set new goals.
They publish a major report biennially on global climate trends.
This art exhibition is held biennially, attracting artists from all over the world.
