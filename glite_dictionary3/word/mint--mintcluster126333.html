to mint

verb
create tokens or NFTs on a blockchain
Pronunciation: / m6ant / (AmE), / m6ant / (BrE)
(in blockchain and cryptocurrency) to create new digital tokens or non-fungible tokens (NFTs) by recording them on a blockchain, often assigning ownership and metadata.

Examples

to mint an NFT
The platform will mint a limited series of NFTs and list them for auction next week.
She minted her artwork as an NFT to ensure provenance and transfer digital ownership.
After confirmation, the network started minting new tokens and assigned them to contributors' addresses.

Related Terms

  • synonym – issue: to formally create and release coins, tokens, or securities
  • synonym – tokenize: to represent an asset or item as a token on a blockchain
