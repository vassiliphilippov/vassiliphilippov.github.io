indisputable

adjective
impossible to question
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈspjutəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈspjuːtəbəl /; / ˌɪndɪˈspjuːtəb(ə)l / (BrE)
impossible to doubt or question; certain and not open to dispute

Examples
