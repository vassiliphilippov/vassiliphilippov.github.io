studying

noun
the act of learning or reading carefully
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌdiɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstʌdiɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of carefully reading, learning, and practicing in order to gain knowledge or skills, especially by focusing on memorization and understanding.

Examples

studying for exams
Effective studying often requires finding a quiet space with minimal distractions.
My grades improved significantly once I developed a regular studying routine.
She spent all night studying for her biology exam and felt prepared in the morning.
