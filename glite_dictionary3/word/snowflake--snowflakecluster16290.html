snowflake

noun
a crystal of snow
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌfleɪk / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌfleɪk / (BrE)
A single small, delicate crystal of snow, often appearing in unique, intricate shapes as it falls from the sky.

Examples

a snowflake falls
A single snowflake landed on her glove and melted quickly.
Children tried to catch a snowflake on their tongues as they played outside.
Each snowflake is unique, with its own beautiful pattern.
