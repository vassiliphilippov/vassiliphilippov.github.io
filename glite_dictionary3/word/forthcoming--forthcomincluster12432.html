forthcoming

adjective
willing to talk or share information
Pronunciation: / fɔrθˈkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / fɔːθˈkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
willing to talk or give information to others; open and communicative.

Examples

forthcoming with details
Although he seemed shy at first, Mark was quite forthcoming about his career once we started talking.
She was not very forthcoming when asked about the project's delays.
The spokesperson was unusually forthcoming with details during the press conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym – communicative: willing to impart information; talkative
  • antonym – reticent: not revealing one's thoughts or feelings readily
