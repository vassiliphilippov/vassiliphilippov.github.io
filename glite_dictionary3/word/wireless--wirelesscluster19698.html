wireless

noun
radio receiver (especially old-fashioned term)
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪr.ləs / (AmE), / ˈwaɪə.ləs / (BrE)
a radio receiver for detecting and amplifying transmitted signals, especially used in reference to early radio sets

Examples

old wireless radio
He spent the afternoon listening to music on the wireless.
In the 1930s, families would gather around the wireless for the evening news.
She bought an antique wireless as a conversation piece for her living room.
