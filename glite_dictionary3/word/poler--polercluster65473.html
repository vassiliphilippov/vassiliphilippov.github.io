poler

noun
horse harnessed beside a carriage pole
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊlər / (AmE), / ˈpəʊlə / (BrE)
a draft horse harnessed alongside the shaft or pole of a vehicle, especially in team harness arrangements

Examples

poler horse team
A sturdy poler was essential for pulling the heavy wagon up steep hills.
The experienced coachman carefully guided the poler into position beside the shaft.
The team consisted of a pair of wheelers and a single poler hitched to the carriage.
