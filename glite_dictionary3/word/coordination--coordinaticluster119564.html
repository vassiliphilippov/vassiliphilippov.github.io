coordination

noun
state of equal importance or rank
Pronunciation: / koʊˌɔːrdnˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / kəʊˌɔːdɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
The state of being of equal importance, rank, or degree.

Examples

coordination of resources
Effective management relies on the coordination of resources with similar levels of importance.
The new policy establishes coordination among all departments, ensuring they have equivalent authority.
There must be coordination between the teams, each having equal responsibility in the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym – equality: the state of being equal, especially in status, rights, or opportunities
  • antonym – inferiority: the state of being lower in importance, rank, or degree
