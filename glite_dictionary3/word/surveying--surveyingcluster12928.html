surveying

noun
measuring land for maps or construction
Pronunciation: / sɚˈveɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / səˈveɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the science or practice of determining the positions, distances, and angles between points on the ground, especially for the purpose of mapping or construction

Examples

surveying land boundaries
After graduation, he found a job in surveying with an engineering firm.
Modern surveying uses advanced technology like GPS and drones.
The bridge project required extensive surveying before construction could begin.
