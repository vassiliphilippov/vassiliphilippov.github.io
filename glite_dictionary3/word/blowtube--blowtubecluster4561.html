blowtube

noun
tube for directing air into a flame
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊˌtuːb / (AmE), / ˈbləʊˌtjuːb / (BrE)
a tube used to direct air or gas into a flame to intensify and concentrate heat

Examples

blowtube for heating flame
In metalworking, a blowtube can help increase the flame's temperature for soldering.
She carefully inserted the blowtube into the torch to concentrate the heat on a small area.
The glassblower used a blowtube to shape the molten glass.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blowpipe: a tube used to direct a stream of air (or other gas) into a flame
