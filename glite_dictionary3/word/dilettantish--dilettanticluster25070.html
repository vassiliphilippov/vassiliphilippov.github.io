dilettantish

adjective
showing superficial or amateurish interest
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪləˈtæntɪʃ / (AmE), / ˌdɪlɪˈtæntɪʃ / (BrE)
showing a superficial interest or knowledge in an art or field of study; amateurish.

Examples

dilettantish work
Despite his dilettantish attempts at writing, he never published a novel.
Her dilettantish approach to photography meant she rarely took the craft seriously.
The museum curator dismissed the painting as the work of a dilettantish hobbyist.

Related Terms

  • synonym – amateurish: showing lack of professional skill or knowledge
