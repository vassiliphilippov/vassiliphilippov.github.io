advisory

noun
official warning or advice announcement
Pronunciation: / ədˈvaɪzəri / (AmE), / ədˈvaɪzəri / (BrE)
an official announcement that warns or gives advice to the public, especially about possible danger or difficult conditions

Examples

weather advisory
A travel advisory was released following the civil unrest in the region.
Motorists should pay attention to the fog advisory before planning their routes.
The National Weather Service issued a advisory about the approaching storm.

  • synonym – warning: a statement telling people about possible danger or problems
