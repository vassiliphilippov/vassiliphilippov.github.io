to dash

verb
add a small, altering or enlivening element
Pronunciation: / dæʃ / (AmE), / dæʃ / (BrE)
to add a small amount of something to enliven, alter, or give flavor to something else

Examples

to dash food with salt
He likes to dash his coffee with cinnamon to give it a special taste.
She decided to dash her salad with a little lemon juice for extra flavor.
The chef dashed some chili flakes onto the pasta to make it spicier.
