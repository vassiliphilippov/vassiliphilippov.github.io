to vulcanize

verb
treat rubber with heat and chemicals
Pronunciation: / ˈvʌlkəˌnaɪz /; / ˈvʌl.kəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈvʌlkənaɪz /; / ˈvʌl.kə.naɪz / (BrE)
