roof

noun
maximum permitted limit; upper cap
Pronunciation: / ruf / (AmE), / ruːf / (BrE)
an upper limit or maximum allowed for something, such as spending or salaries

Examples

roof on spending
A roof on salaries was imposed to reduce costs across the company.
The new law establishes a roof for political campaign donations.
They set a roof on the monthly expenses to control the budget.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cap: an upper limit or restriction
  • synonym – ceiling: an upper limit, especially an imposed one
