Zovirax

noun
trademarked antiviral drug for herpes viruses
Pronunciation: / ˈzoʊvəˌræks / (AmE), / ˈzəʊvɪˌræks / (BrE)
A trademark for an oral antiviral drug (acyclovir) used to treat infections caused by herpes viruses, including genital herpes; it relieves symptoms but does not cure the infection.
