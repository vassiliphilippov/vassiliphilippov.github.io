pilose

adjective
covered with fine, soft hairs
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪloʊs / (AmE), / ˈpɪləʊs / (BrE)
(of a surface, especially of a plant or animal) covered with fine, soft hairs.

Examples

pilose leaf surface
Some caterpillars are recognized by their pilose bodies, which are covered with fine hairs.
The leaves of the plant are pilose, giving them a soft, velvety texture to the touch.
Under the microscope, the pilose surface of the stem was clearly visible.
