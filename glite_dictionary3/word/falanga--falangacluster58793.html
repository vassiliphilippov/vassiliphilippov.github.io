falanga

noun
beating soles of feet as torture
Pronunciation: / fəˈlɑːŋɡə / (AmE), / fəˈlæŋɡə / (BrE)
A form of torture in which the soles of the feet are beaten with whips, sticks, or cudgels.

Examples
