barouche

noun
type of horse-drawn carriage
Pronunciation: / bəˈruːʃ / (AmE), / bəˈruːʃ / (BrE)
a four-wheeled horse-drawn carriage with a folding top, with inside seats for two couples facing each other, and an outside seat for the driver

Examples

ride in a barouche
During their tour of the city, they rode through the streets in a vintage barouche.
The elegant family arrived at the party in a gleaming barouche drawn by two fine horses.
The museum had a restored barouche on display, once used by 19th-century nobility.
