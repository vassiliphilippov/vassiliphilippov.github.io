to fatigue

verb
to tire or exhaust greatly
Pronunciation: / fəˈtiɡ / (AmE), / fəˈtiːɡ / (BrE)
to exhaust or make tired through overuse, great strain, or stress

Examples

to fatigue the body
Climbing the steep mountain quickly fatigued the entire group.
Continuous noise can fatigue workers and reduce their concentration.
The long hours of study began to fatigue even the most dedicated students.

Related Terms

  • synonym – exhaust: to make someone extremely tired
  • antonym – invigorate: to give strength or energy to someone
