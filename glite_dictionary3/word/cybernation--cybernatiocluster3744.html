cybernation

noun
automatic computer control of processes
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪbərˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsaɪbəˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the automatic control of industrial or other processes by computers rather than by humans

Examples

cybernation of factories
Concerns about unemployment have arisen with the rise of cybernation in industry.
Many factories now rely on cybernation to increase efficiency and reduce labor costs.
The shift towards cybernation has transformed traditional manufacturing processes.
