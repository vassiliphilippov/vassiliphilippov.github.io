thwartwise

adverb
in a crosswise direction; at right angles
Pronunciation: / ˈθwɔrtˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈθwɔːtˌwaɪz / (BrE)
Extending or lying across; in a crosswise direction; at right angles to the long axis.
