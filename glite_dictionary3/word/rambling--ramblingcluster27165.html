rambling

adjective
winding or meandering (especially of a path or stream)
Pronunciation: / ˈræmblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈræmblɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a path, road, or stream) following a winding or indirect course; meandering.

Examples

rambling country road
A rambling country road led us past fields and old barns.
The rambling stream made its way lazily through the meadow.
We walked along a rambling trail that twisted through the dense forest.

  • synonym – winding: having twists and turns; not straight
