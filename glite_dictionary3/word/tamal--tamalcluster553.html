tamal

noun
Latin American steamed stuffed dough dish
Pronunciation: / təˈmɑl / (AmE), / təˈmɑːl / (BrE)
A traditional Latin American food made of cornmeal dough stuffed with a meat or other filling, usually wrapped in corn husks and steamed.

Examples

a tamal with chicken
He ordered a spicy tamal filled with chicken for lunch.
The street vendor sells delicious homemade tamales every morning.
Wrapping each tamal in a corn husk is a family tradition during the holidays.

  • synonym – tamale: alternative form of 'tamal', common in US English
