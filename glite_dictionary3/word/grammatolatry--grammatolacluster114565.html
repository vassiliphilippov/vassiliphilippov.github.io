grammatolatry

noun
worship of words or language
Pronunciation: / græməˈtɑlətri / (AmE), / grəˈmatələtri / (BrE)
excessive veneration or worship of words or of language itself

Examples

obsessive grammatolatry of language
His devotion to precise grammar and vocabulary bordered on grammatolatry.
Some critics have accused certain language purists of grammatolatry, placing form above meaning.
The author's grammatolatry was evident in her meticulous attention to word choice and phrasing.

Related Terms

  • synonym – logolatry: worship or excessive reverence for words
