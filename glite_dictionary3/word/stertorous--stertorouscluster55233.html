stertorous

adjective
making a harsh snoring sound (breathing)
Pronunciation: / ˈstɜr.tɚ.əs / (AmE), / ˈstɜː.tər.əs / (BrE)
(of breathing) making a harsh or heavy snoring or gasping sound, typically indicating obstruction of the air passages.
