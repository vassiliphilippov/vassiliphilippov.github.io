remove

noun
degree of separation or distance (formal)
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmuːv / (AmE), / rɪˈmuːv / (BrE)
a degree of separation or distance, especially figurative or genealogical; a step or stage away from something

Examples

a remove from tradition
