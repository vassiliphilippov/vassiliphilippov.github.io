to militarize

verb
to make military in character or use
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪz / (AmE), / mɪˈlɪtəraɪz /; / ˈmɪlɪtəraɪz / (BrE)
to equip, prepare, or adapt (a country, organization, or area) for military use or to give it a military character
