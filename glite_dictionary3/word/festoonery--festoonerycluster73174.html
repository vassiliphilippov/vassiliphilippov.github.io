festoonery

noun
decorative garlands or chains hung in curves
Pronunciation: / fɛˈstunəri / (AmE), / fɛˈstuːnəri / (BrE)
A decorative arrangement of flower chains, garlands, or ribbons hung in curves between points.

Examples

colorful festoonery decorations
Holiday festoonery adorned the windows and archways, giving the house a festive charm.
The ballroom was breathtaking, filled with festoonery of flowers and greenery draped from the ceiling.
They decorated the stage with colorful festoonery for the spring festival.
