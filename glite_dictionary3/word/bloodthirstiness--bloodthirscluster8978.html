bloodthirstiness

noun
tendency to seek or enjoy violence
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdˌθɜrstinɪs / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌθɜːstinɪs / (BrE)
A strong desire to kill or injure; a tendency to seek or enjoy violence and bloodshed.

Examples

extreme bloodthirstiness
Few were prepared for the sheer bloodthirstiness of the ancient gladiatorial games.
His bloodthirstiness became apparent as the battle dragged on and he showed no mercy.
The villain's bloodthirstiness struck fear into everyone who crossed his path.
