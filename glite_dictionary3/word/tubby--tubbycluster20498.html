tubby

adjective
short and plump (person or animal)
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌbi / (AmE), / ˈtʌbi / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) short and plump; chubby or slightly fat, especially in a rounded, sometimes comical way.

Examples

tubby little cat
After months of homemade treats, my cat has become quite tubby.
People teased him about being tubby, but he always responded with a smile.
The tubby little boy laughed as he chased the puppy around the yard.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chubby: plump or slightly overweight
  • antonym – thin: having little fat on the body
