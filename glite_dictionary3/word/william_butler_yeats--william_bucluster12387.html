William Butler Yeats

proper noun
Irish poet and dramatist (1865–1939)
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪljəm ˈbʌtlɚ ˈjeɪts / (AmE), / ˈwɪljəm ˈbʌtlə ˈjeɪts / (BrE)
