to slack

verb
to treat lime with water (slake)
Pronunciation: / slæk / (AmE), / slæk / (BrE)
(of lime) to cause to heat and crumble by adding water; to slake.

Examples

to slack lime
The process to slack lime must be done carefully to avoid accidents.
To produce mortar, builders often slack quicklime by adding water.
When you slack the lime, it generates a great deal of heat.

Related Terms

  • synonym – slake: to cool or refresh something, especially by adding water to lime
