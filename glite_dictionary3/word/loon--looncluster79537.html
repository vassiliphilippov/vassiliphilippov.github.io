loon

noun
large diving bird of the Northern Hemisphere
Pronunciation: / lun / (AmE), / luːn / (BrE)
A large fish-eating diving bird of the Northern Hemisphere, with webbed feet placed far back on the body, related to the grebes.

Examples

wild loon call
A loon called across the quiet lake at dawn.
Many birdwatchers travel north to see the striking black-and-white loon.
We spotted a loon diving for fish near the shore.
