poutingly

adverb
in a pouting manner
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈpaʊtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a pout or expresses displeasure or sulking.

Examples

speak poutingly
He answered poutingly, making it clear he was not pleased with the outcome.
She crossed her arms and looked poutingly at her parents when denied dessert.
The child sat poutingly in the corner after being scolded.
