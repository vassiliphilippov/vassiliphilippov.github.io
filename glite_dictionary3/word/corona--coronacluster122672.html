corona

noun
viral disease (COVID-19 pandemic)
Pronunciation: / kəˈroʊnə / (AmE), / kəˈrəʊnə / (BrE)
A viral disease caused by SARS-CoV-2, responsible for the global pandemic that began in 2020; also called COVID-19.

Examples

corona virus pandemic
Many people started working from home during the corona pandemic.
The spread of the corona virus led to lockdowns around the world in early 2020.
Wearing masks became common as a precaution against corona.

  • synonym – COVID-19: the official name for the disease caused by SARS-CoV-2
