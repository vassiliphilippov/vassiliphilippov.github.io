buskin

noun
knee-high boot, often historical or theatrical
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈbʌskɪn / (BrE)
a boot that covers the foot and lower leg, typically reaching halfway up to the knee, often associated with ancient Greek and Roman actors or traditional costume.
