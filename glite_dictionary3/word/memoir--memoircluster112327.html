memoir

noun
written account of one's own life
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛmwɑr / (AmE), / ˈmɛm.wɑː / (BrE)
A written account in which someone describes experiences from their own life.

Examples

memoir of childhood
He spent years writing his memoir about his travels across Asia.
Her best-selling memoir gives insight into her childhood struggles.
The author reveals personal secrets in her latest memoir.

Related Terms

  • synonym – autobiography: an account of a person's life written by that person, usually more comprehensive than a memoir
