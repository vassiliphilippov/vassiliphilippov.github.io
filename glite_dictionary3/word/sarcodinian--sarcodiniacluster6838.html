sarcodinian

noun
type of protozoan with pseudopods
Pronunciation: / sɑrˈkoʊdɪniən / (AmE), / sɑːˈkəʊdɪniən / (BrE)
A protozoan belonging to a group characterized by movement and feeding by means of pseudopods.
