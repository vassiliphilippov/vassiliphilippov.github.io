custom-made

noun
item made to customer's specifications
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstəm meɪd / (AmE), / ˌkʌstəm ˈmeɪd / (BrE)
an item that has been made according to a customer's individual requirements

Examples

a custom-made suit
He ordered a custom-made suit for his wedding.
The jeweler crafted a custom-made ring based on her design.
We specialize in custom-made furniture for unique spaces.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bespoke: made to order for a particular customer
  • antonym – off-the-shelf: ready-made and available for sale immediately
