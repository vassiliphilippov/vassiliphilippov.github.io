tonsure

noun
religious shaving of the head
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑnʃər /; / ˈtɑn.ʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɒnʃə /; / ˈtɒn.ʃə / (BrE)
the act or practice of shaving part or all of the crown of the head, especially by members of a religious order as a sign of humility or devotion
