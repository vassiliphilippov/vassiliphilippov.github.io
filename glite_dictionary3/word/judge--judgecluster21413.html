to judge

verb
form an opinion after careful consideration
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌdʒ /; / dʒʌdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌdʒ /; / dʒʌdʒ / (BrE)
to form an opinion or conclusion about something or someone after considering the facts
