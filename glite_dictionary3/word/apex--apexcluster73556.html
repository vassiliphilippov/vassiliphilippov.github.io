apex

noun
the highest point of something
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.pɛks / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.pɛks / (BrE)
The highest point or tip of something, especially one forming an angle or a peak.

Examples

apex of a mountain
A pyramid's apex is directly above its base's center.
At the apex of the mountain, the air was thin and cold.
The company reached the apex of its success in the early 2000s.

Related Terms

  • antonym – nadir: the lowest point
  • synonym – summit: the highest point of a hill or mountain
