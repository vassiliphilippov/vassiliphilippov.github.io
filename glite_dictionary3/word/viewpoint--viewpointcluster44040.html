viewpoint

noun
place offering a view
Pronunciation: / ˈvjuːˌpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈvjuːˌpɔɪnt / (BrE)
a place from which something can be viewed, often offering a good overall perspective

Examples

scenic viewpoint
From this viewpoint, you can see the city skyline stretching out before you.
The trail leads up to a viewpoint overlooking the lake and the surrounding valleys.
We stopped at a viewpoint along the highway to take photos of the mountains.
