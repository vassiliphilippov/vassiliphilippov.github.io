raise

noun
increase in a bet (poker)
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪz / (AmE), / ˈreɪz / (BrE)
an act of increasing the size of a bet in gambling, especially in poker

Examples

a raise in poker
After her raise, most of the other players folded.
He made a raise of fifty dollars, surprising everyone at the table.
The raise changed the momentum of the poker game.

Related Terms

  • antonym – call: to match the current bet in poker without increasing it
  • antonym – fold: to withdraw from the round in gambling, especially poker
