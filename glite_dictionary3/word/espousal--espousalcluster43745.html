espousal

noun
the act of adopting or supporting something
Pronunciation: / ɪˈspaʊzəl / (AmE), / ɪˈspaʊzəl / (BrE)
the act of adopting, supporting, or accepting a cause, idea, or proposal with approval

Examples

espousal of change
Her public espousal of the reform agenda surprised many of her colleagues.
His espousal of environmental causes won him widespread respect.
The company's espousal of new technologies set it apart from its competitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym – adoption: the act of taking up or accepting something
  • antonym – rejection: the act of refusing to accept, use, or believe something
