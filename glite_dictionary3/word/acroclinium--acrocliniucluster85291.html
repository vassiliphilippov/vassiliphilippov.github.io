Acroclinium

noun
genus of everlasting flowering herbs or shrubs
Pronunciation: / ˌæk.roʊˈklɪn.i.əm /; / ˌækroʊˈklɪniəm / (AmE), / ˌakrəʊˈklɪnɪəm /; / ˌækrəʊˈklɪnɪəm / (BrE)
