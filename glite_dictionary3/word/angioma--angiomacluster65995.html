angioma

noun
benign tumor of blood or lymphatic vessels
Pronunciation: / ˌændʒiˈoʊmə / (AmE), / ˌandʒiˈəʊmə / (BrE)
A benign tumor consisting of a mass of blood vessels or lymphatic vessels.

Examples

angioma on skin
An angioma can sometimes require treatment if it causes discomfort or grows rapidly.
Most angiomas are harmless and do not need to be removed.
The doctor diagnosed the red mark on her skin as an angioma.
