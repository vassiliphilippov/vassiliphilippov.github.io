compact

noun
small, economical car
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpækt / (AmE), / ˈkɒmpækt / (BrE)
a small, economical car, typically with seating for four or five passengers and designed for efficient fuel use.

Examples

compact car model
He traded in his old SUV for a compact to save money on gas.
Parking in the city is much easier with a compact than with a large car.
They rented a compact for their road trip to keep costs low.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – mid-size: a larger car compared to a compact but smaller than a full-size car
  • synonym – sedan: a car with a separate compartment for luggage
  • degree lower – subcompact: an even smaller and more economical car than a compact
