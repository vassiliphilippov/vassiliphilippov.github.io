palmate

adjective
(of a leaf) leaflets radiating from a point
Pronunciation: / ˈpæl.meɪt / (AmE), / ˈpæl.meɪt / (BrE)
(of a leaf) having lobes or leaflets that spread out from a single point, like the fingers of a hand.
