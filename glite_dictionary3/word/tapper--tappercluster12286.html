tapper

noun
person who taps a surface repeatedly
Pronunciation: / ˈtæpər / (AmE), / ˈtæpə / (BrE)
a person who repeatedly strikes a surface lightly, often with their fingers or an object

Examples

nervous tapper with fingers
She noticed a tapper in the audience, nervously drumming his fingers on his chair.
Teachers often ask habitual tappers to stop during exams.
The constant noise from the tapper at the next desk was a source of distraction.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drummer: a person who plays a drum, often striking repetitively
