to check

verb
put opponent's king in check (chess)
Pronunciation: / tʃɛk / (AmE), / tʃɛk / (BrE)
(in chess) to put an opponent's king under direct attack.

Examples

to check the king
He checked my king, forcing me to block with a bishop.
If you check the king, your opponent must respond immediately.
She managed to check her opponent's king with a clever move.
