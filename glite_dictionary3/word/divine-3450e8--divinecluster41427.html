divine

adjective
extremely good or delightful
Pronunciation: / dəˈvaɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪn / (BrE)
(of something) extremely good, beautiful, or delightful, as if inspired by the gods.

Examples

divine beauty
Her performance in the lead role was simply divine and left the audience speechless.
The chocolate cake was divine, with a rich, velvety texture and deep flavor.
They described their trip to the coast as a divine experience, filled with beauty and joy.

Related Terms

  • synonym – heavenly: extremely pleasing or enjoyable
  • antonym – mundane: lacking interest or excitement; dull
  • synonym – sublime: of such excellence or beauty as to inspire great admiration
