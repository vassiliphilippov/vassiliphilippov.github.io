spine-tipped

adjective
tipped with a spine (botanical)
Pronunciation: / spaɪn tɪpt / (AmE), / spaɪn tɪpt / (BrE)
(of a leaf or plant part) having a tip that ends in a sharp, stiff point or spine.

Examples

spine-tipped leaf
Some holly leaves are spine-tipped, making them difficult for animals to eat.
The botanist noted the spine-tipped characteristics of the rare desert shrub.
The cactus has several spine-tipped branches that help protect it from herbivores.
