oatcake

noun
thin, flat cake made from oatmeal
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊtˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈəʊtˌkeɪk / (BrE)
a thin, flat, unleavened cake made from baked oatmeal, often eaten as a snack or with meals

Examples

crispy oatcake snack
He spread butter on a freshly baked oatcake and ate it for breakfast.
She served a plate of oatcakes with cheese for afternoon tea.
Traditional Scottish oatcakes are often enjoyed with soup or stew.
