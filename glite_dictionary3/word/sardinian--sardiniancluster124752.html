Sardinian

noun
Romance language of Sardinia
Pronunciation: / sɑrˈdɪniən / (AmE), / sɑːˈdɪniən / (BrE)
the Romance language spoken in Sardinia, sometimes considered a distinct language from Italian, with many Spanish loanwords

Examples

Sardinian language
Many people in central Sardinia still speak Sardinian as their first language.
Some linguists argue that Sardinian should be classified as a separate language rather than as an Italian dialect.
The influence of Spanish can be heard in many words of modern Sardinian.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Italian: the Romance language of Italy, related to Sardinian
  • synonym – Spanish: the Romance language of Spain, a source of loanwords in Sardinian
