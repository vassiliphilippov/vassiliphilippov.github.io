Abel Janszoon Tasman

proper noun
Dutch navigator; explorer of Tasmania and New Zealand
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪbəl ˈjænzun ˈtæzmən / (AmE), / ˈeɪbəl ˈjænzən ˈtæzmən / (BrE)
(1603–1659) Dutch explorer and navigator, the first European known to reach Tasmania and New Zealand.

Abel Janszoon Tasman discovered Tasmania
