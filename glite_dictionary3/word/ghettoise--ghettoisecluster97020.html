to ghettoise

verb
confine or isolate like a ghetto
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɛtoʊˌaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡɛtəʊˌaɪz / (BrE)
to put or confine (a group or community) in a ghetto or to isolate and marginalize them as if in a ghetto

Examples

to ghettoise a minority group
Critics argue that certain laws have served to ghettoise minority communities.
Educational reforms must ensure not to ghettoise students from disadvantaged backgrounds.
The policy threatened to ghettoise immigrants in isolated neighborhoods.
