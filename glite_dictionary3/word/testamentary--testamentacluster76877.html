testamentary

adjective
relating to a will or testament
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛstəˈmɛntəri / (AmE), / ˌtɛstəˈmɛntri / (BrE)
relating to or set out in a will or testament; bequeathed by or pertaining to a written will.

Examples

testamentary will
A testamentary gift of property requires clear evidence of intent.
The lawyer explained the testamentary provisions of her late uncle's will.
They challenged the testamentary capacity of the elderly testator in court.
