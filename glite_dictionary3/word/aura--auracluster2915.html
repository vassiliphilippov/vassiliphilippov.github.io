aura

noun
preliminary sensation before migraine or seizure
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrə / (AmE), / ˈɔːrə / (BrE)
A sensation, such as a cold breeze or bright light, experienced before the onset of certain disorders, especially migraines or epileptic seizures.

Examples

migraine aura appears
Doctors advise patients to rest immediately if they notice their aura.
She recognized the tingling aura as a warning of an approaching seizure.
Some people experience a visual aura before the headache of a migraine begins.
