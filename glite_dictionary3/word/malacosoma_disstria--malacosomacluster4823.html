Malacosoma disstria

noun
a North American moth species (forest tent caterpillar)
Pronunciation: / ˌmæləkəˈsoʊmə dɪˈstraɪə /; / ˌmæləkəˌsoʊmə dɪsˈtraɪə / (AmE), / ˌmaləkəˈsəʊmə dɪˈstraɪə /; / ˌmæləkəˌsəʊmə dɪsˈtraɪə / (BrE)
a North American moth species whose larvae, called forest tent caterpillars, live in groups, spin silk mats in trees, and can damage forests by eating the leaves
