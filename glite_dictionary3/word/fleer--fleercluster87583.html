to fleer

verb
smirk or laugh contemptuously
Pronunciation: / flɪr / (AmE), / flɪə / (BrE)
to smile or laugh in a contemptuous or derisive manner

Examples

to fleer at mistakes
He would fleer at every mistake his rival made.
She could not help but fleer at the foolish proposal.
The enemy soldiers fleered from the safety of their walls.
