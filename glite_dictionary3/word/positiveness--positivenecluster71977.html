positiveness

noun
encouraging or optimistic quality
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑzətɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˈpɒzətɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being encouraging, optimistic, or likely to lead to success

Examples

his positiveness inspires
Even in challenging times, his positiveness kept everyone motivated and hopeful.
Her positiveness inspired the whole team to believe they could win the championship.
The positiveness of the feedback encouraged us to continue with the project.
