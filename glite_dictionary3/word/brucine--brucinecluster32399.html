brucine

noun
a bitter, poisonous alkaloid compound
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːsiːn / (AmE), / ˈbruːsiːn / (BrE)
a bitter, poisonous alkaloid compound similar to strychnine, extracted from the seeds of nux vomica and related plants
