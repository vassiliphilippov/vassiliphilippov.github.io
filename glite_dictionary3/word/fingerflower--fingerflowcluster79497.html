fingerflower

noun
plant with tubular purple flowers; source of digitalis
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡɚˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A tall European biennial or perennial plant with clusters of large tubular pink-purple flowers; its leaves produce digitalis and are poisonous to livestock.

Examples

fingerflower plant blooms
Caution is needed, as fingerflower leaves can be toxic to animals if ingested.
