hurdy-gurdy

noun
mechanical stringed musical instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈhɝːdi ˌɡɝːdi /; / ˌhɝːdiˈɡɝːdi /; / ˌhɜːrdi ˈɡɜːrdi / (AmE), / ˈhɜːdi ˌɡɜːdi /; / ˌhɜːdiˈɡɜːdi /; / ˌhɜːdi ˈɡɜːdi / (BrE)
a stringed musical instrument that makes sound by turning a crank, with a wheel rubbing against the strings and keys or a keyboard to play melodies
