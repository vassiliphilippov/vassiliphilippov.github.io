stand-alone

adjective
able to function independently
Pronunciation: / ˈstænd əˌloʊn / (AmE), / ˈstænd əˌləʊn / (BrE)
(of a device, system, or program) able to operate independently without being connected to or relying on other equipment, systems, or components.

Examples

stand-alone device
The application can be used as a stand-alone program or as part of a larger suite.
They bought a stand-alone generator for emergencies.
This printer is a stand-alone device that doesn’t need to connect to a computer.

  • synonym – independent: not depending on others; self-sufficient
