barrier

noun
obstacle to progress or achievement
Pronunciation: / ˈbæriɚ / (AmE), / ˈbæriə / (BrE)
A circumstance or condition that prevents progress or makes it difficult to achieve something.

Examples

overcome a barrier
He is determined to overcome every barrier standing in his way.
Language differences can be a barrier to effective communication.
Poverty remains a significant barrier to education for many children worldwide.

Related Terms

  • antonym – aid: something that helps or assists progress
  • synonym – hindrance: a thing that provides resistance or delay to something or someone
  • synonym – obstacle: something that blocks progress or prevents movement or action
