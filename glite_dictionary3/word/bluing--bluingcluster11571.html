bluing

noun
blue substance for whitening laundry or hair
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbluːɪŋ / (BrE)
a blue substance added to laundry or hair to whiten it or give it a bluish tinge, especially to counteract yellowing

Examples

bluing laundry water
My grandmother always added a little bluing to the wash to keep the sheets looking bright.
Some old-fashioned laundries still rely on bluing to maintain crisp, white linens.
The hairdresser used bluing to help neutralize the yellow tones in her client's hair.
