telfer

noun
car in an aerial cable transport system
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlfər / (AmE), / ˈtɛlfə / (BrE)
A car or carrier used as a conveyance in a telpherage (a system of overhead cable transport).

Examples

telfer cable car
A single telfer can carry hundreds of kilograms along the cableway.
Maintenance on each telfer is performed regularly to ensure safety.
The workers loaded the materials into the telfer for transport across the construction site.
