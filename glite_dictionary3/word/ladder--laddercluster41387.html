ladder

noun
run in fabric (esp. stockings)
Pronunciation: / ˈlædɚ / (AmE), / ˈladə / (BrE)
a run or row of unravelled stitches in a knitted fabric, especially in stockings or tights

Examples

stocking ladder
A small hole in the sock soon became a ladder that ran all the way down.
Carefully pulling on her stockings, she tried not to create another ladder.
She noticed a ladder in her tights just before leaving for work.
