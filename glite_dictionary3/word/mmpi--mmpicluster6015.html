MMPI

noun
personality assessment test
Pronunciation: / ɛm ɛm pi aɪ / (AmE), / ɛm ɛm pi aɪ / (BrE)
A standardized self-report questionnaire used to assess personality traits and psychological disorders.

Examples

MMPI personality test
Researchers often use the MMPI to evaluate psychological traits in clinical settings.
She answered hundreds of questions on the MMPI to help understand her personality profile.
The psychologist decided to administer the MMPI as part of the diagnostic process.
