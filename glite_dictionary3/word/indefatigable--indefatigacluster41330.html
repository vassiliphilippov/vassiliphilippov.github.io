indefatigable

adjective
persisting tirelessly; never giving up
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈfætɪɡəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈfætɪɡəbəl / (BrE)
(of a person or their efforts) persisting tirelessly and with unflagging energy.

Examples

indefatigable worker
Despite the setbacks, he remained indefatigable in his pursuit of justice.
Her indefatigable spirit inspired the entire team to keep pushing forward.
The volunteers were indefatigable, working day and night to help those in need.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fatigued: tired or exhausted
  • synonym – tireless: showing great effort or energy without getting tired
  • synonym – unflagging: not declining in strength or vigor
