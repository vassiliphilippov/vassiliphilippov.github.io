plot

noun
main story in literature or film
Pronunciation: / plɑt / (AmE), / plɒt / (BrE)
the sequence of events that make up the main story of a novel, play, film, or similar work

Examples

plot of a story
After reading the first few chapters, she still couldn’t figure out the plot of the story.
Many readers enjoy novels for their complex plots and well-developed characters.
The film’s plot was full of unexpected twists and kept the audience guessing.

Related Terms

  • synonym – storyline: the narrative path or main theme of a story
  • degree lower – subplot: a secondary or subordinate plot in a story
