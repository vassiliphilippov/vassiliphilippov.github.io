to enrol

verb
register formally as a member or participant
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈroʊl / (AmE), / ɪnˈrəʊl / (BrE)
to register or sign up formally as a participant, member, or student

Examples

to enrol in a course
I plan to enrol in a cooking class next semester.
New students must enrol before attending orientation.
She decided to enrol her son in the local swimming club.

Related Terms

  • synonym – register: to put one's name on an official list or record
  • antonym – withdraw: to remove oneself formally from membership or participation
