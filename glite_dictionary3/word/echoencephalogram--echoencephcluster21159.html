echoencephalogram

noun
ultrasound image of the brain
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkoʊɪnˌsɛfəˈlɵɡræm / (AmE), / ˌɛkəʊɛnˌsɛfəˈlɒɡram / (BrE)
A graphical image of the brain produced by using ultrasound waves, typically obtained by an echoencephalograph.

Examples

an echoencephalogram scan
An echoencephalogram is a non-invasive technique used in neurological diagnostics.
She underwent an echoencephalogram after experiencing persistent headaches.
The doctor reviewed the echoencephalogram to look for any signs of brain injury.
