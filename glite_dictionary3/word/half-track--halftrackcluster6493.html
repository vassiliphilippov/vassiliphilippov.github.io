half-track

noun
military vehicle with wheels and tracks
Pronunciation: / ˈhæf ˌtræk / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf ˌtræk / (BrE)
a military vehicle with wheels at the front for steering and continuous tracks at the back for propulsion, used for traveling over rough ground

Examples

military half-track vehicle
He restored an old half-track from World War II as a hobby project.
The army convoy moved steadily forward, each half-track loaded with supplies.
The museum's collection includes a rare German half-track used on the Eastern Front.
