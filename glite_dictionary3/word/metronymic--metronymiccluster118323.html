metronymic

noun
name from mother's name
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtroʊˈnɪmɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɛtrəˈnɪmɪk / (BrE)
a name derived from the name of one's mother or a maternal ancestor

Examples

metronymic surname origin
Her surname is a metronymic passed down from her maternal grandmother.
In some cultures, children receive a metronymic rather than a patronymic surname.
The study of metronymics reveals patterns of matrilineal descent in naming traditions.
