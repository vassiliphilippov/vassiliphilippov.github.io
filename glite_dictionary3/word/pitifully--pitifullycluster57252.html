pitifully

adverb
in a way that arouses pity
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪt̬əfli / (AmE), / ˈpɪtɪfəli / (BrE)
to an extent or degree that arouses pity or compassion; in a way that deserves pity.

Examples

pitifully thin
She looked pitifully thin after the long illness.
The child's torn shoes were pitifully inadequate for the freezing weather.
The injured bird was pitifully trying to fly with its broken wing.
