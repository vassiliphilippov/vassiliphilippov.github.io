starch

noun
substance for stiffening clothes in laundry
Pronunciation: / stɑrtʃ / (AmE), / stɑːtʃ / (BrE)
a substance in powder or liquid form, made from plants and used to stiffen fabric in laundering

Examples

spray starch on clothes
After washing his shirts, he always sprayed them with starch before ironing.
She added extra starch to the laundry to make the collars crisp.
The maid used liquid starch to give the tablecloth a firm finish.
