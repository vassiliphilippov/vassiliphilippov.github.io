syrinx

noun
primitive wind instrument of bound pipes
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪr.ɪŋks / (AmE), / ˈsɪr.ɪŋks / (BrE)
A primitive wind instrument consisting of several parallel pipes of varying length bound together, also known as panpipes.
