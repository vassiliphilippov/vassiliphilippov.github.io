.22-caliber

adjective
describing a 0.22-inch bore in guns/ammo
Pronunciation: / ˌtwɛnti ˈtuː ˈkælɪbər / (AmE), / ˌtwɛnti ˈtjuː ˈkælɪbə / (BrE)
(of a gun or its ammunition) having a bore diameter of twenty-two hundredths of an inch (0.22 inch).

Examples

.22-caliber rifle
He bought a .22-caliber rifle for target practice.
