cannily

adverb
in a clever or shrewd manner
Pronunciation: / ˈkænɪli / (AmE), / ˈkanɪli / (BrE)
in a shrewd or clever manner; with foresight or prudence.

Examples

to plan cannily
He invested his money cannily, choosing stocks that steadily grew over time.
She handled the negotiations cannily, securing the best deal for her company.
The thief cannily avoided all the cameras as he moved through the gallery.
