Marxist

adjective
relating to the theories of Marx and Engels
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrksɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɑːksɪst / (BrE)
of, relating to, or supporting the political and economic theories of Karl Marx and Friedrich Engels, especially regarding class struggle and socialism.

Examples

Marxist theory
Her analysis reflects a distinct Marxist perspective on history.
Many Marxist thinkers have contributed to social theory in the 20th century.
The group organized a Marxist study circle for discussing political philosophy.

  • antonym – capitalist: supporting or relating to capitalism
  • synonym – communist: relating to or supporting communism
