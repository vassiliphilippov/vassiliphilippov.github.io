blueness

noun
the quality of being blue
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːnəs / (AmE), / ˈbluːnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being blue; the characteristic of resembling the color of the clear sky in the daytime

Examples

deep blueness
He admired the deep blueness of her eyes, which seemed almost luminous in the sunlight.
Her painting captures the blueness of the ocean at sunrise with remarkable precision.
The blueness of the sky was striking against the white clouds above the valley.
