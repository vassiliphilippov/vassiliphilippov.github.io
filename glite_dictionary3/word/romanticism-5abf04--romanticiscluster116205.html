romanticism

noun
exciting and mysterious quality
Pronunciation: / roʊˈmæntɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / rəʊˈmantɪsɪzəm / (BrE)
the quality of being exciting, adventurous, and full of mystery, especially as associated with heroic times or adventures

Examples

the romanticism of adventure
Many people are attracted to the romanticism of exploring uncharted territories.
The romanticism of old pirate legends still fascinates readers today.
There was a certain romanticism attached to the life of traveling artists.

Related Terms

  • synonym – adventure: an unusual and exciting or daring experience
  • synonym – mystique: a fascinating aura of mystery, power, or allure
  • antonym – prosaicism: the quality of being prosaic; lacking poetic beauty or excitement
