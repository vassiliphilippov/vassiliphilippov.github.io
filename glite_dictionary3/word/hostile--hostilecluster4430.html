hostile

adjective
unfavourable to life or growth
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑː.stəl / (AmE), / ˈhɒs.taɪl / (BrE)
(of an environment or condition) very unfavourable to life, growth, or success.

Examples

hostile environment
Few plants can survive in such a hostile climate.
Scientists are studying bacteria that thrive in hostile environments like deep-sea vents.
The desert's hostile conditions make it difficult for most animals to live there.
