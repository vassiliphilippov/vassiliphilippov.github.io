to compere

verb
act as a master of ceremonies
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɛr / (AmE), / kɒmˈpɪə / (BrE)
to act as a master of ceremonies at a public event, introducing performers or speakers

Examples

to compere a show
He has compered several television shows over the years.
Next week, I'll be compering a charity fashion event.
She was invited to compere the awards ceremony last night.
