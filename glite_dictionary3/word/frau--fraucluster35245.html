Frau

noun
German courtesy title for a woman
Pronunciation: / fraʊ / (AmE), / fraʊ / (BrE)
A German courtesy title or form of address used for an adult woman, especially before a surname or full name.

Examples

Frau Schmidt
In his letter, he referred to her as Frau Meyer, following the local custom.
The children respectfully addressed her as Frau Schmidt during their visit to Berlin.
The tour guide introduced their host as Frau Müller, a prominent community leader.
