lithomancer

noun
practitioner of divination by stones
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪθ.oʊˌmæn.sər / (AmE), / ˈlɪθ.əʊˌmæn.sə / (BrE)
A person who practices lithomancy, the art of divination by interpreting stones or gems.
