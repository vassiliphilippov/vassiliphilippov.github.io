suit

noun
set of similar playing cards
Pronunciation: / suːt / (AmE), / sjuːt / (BrE)
one of the four sets into which a deck of playing cards is divided, each set having the same symbol and color

Examples

suit of hearts
Clubs are my least favorite suit when I play bridge.
He was dealt five cards, all in the same suit.
Remember to follow suit if you can.
