to knit

verb
make fabric by interlinking yarn with needles
Pronunciation: / nɪt / (AmE), / nɪt / (BrE)
to make fabric or garments by interlinking yarn with needles or similar tools.

Examples

to knit a sweater
He learned how to knit a scarf from an online tutorial.
Many people find it relaxing to knit while listening to music.
She loves to knit warm sweaters for her grandchildren during the winter.
