authority

noun
the power to command or decide
Pronunciation: / əˈθɔrəti / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɒrəti / (BrE)
the power or right to give orders, make decisions, or enforce obedience

Examples

authority to decide
After the storm, local authority imposed a curfew to keep residents safe.
Parents sometimes struggle when children question their authority.
The manager has the authority to approve all budget requests in her department.

Related Terms

  • synonym – power: the ability to direct or influence behavior or events
  • antonym – subordination: the condition of being under the authority or control of another
