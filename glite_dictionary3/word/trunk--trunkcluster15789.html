trunk

noun
elephant's long, flexible nose
Pronunciation: / trʌŋk / (AmE), / trʌŋk / (BrE)
the long, flexible, tubular nose of an elephant, used for breathing, smelling, drinking, and grasping objects

Examples

elephant trunk
An elephant's trunk contains thousands of muscles and is incredibly strong and sensitive.
The baby elephant wrapped its trunk around its mother's tail as they walked.
The elephant lifted water with its trunk and sprayed it over its back.
