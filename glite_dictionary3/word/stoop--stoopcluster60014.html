stoop

noun
a bent-forward posture
Pronunciation: / stuːp / (AmE), / stuːp / (BrE)
a posture in which the top half of the body bends forward and downward, often as a result of habit, age, or physical condition

Examples

a slight stoop
After months at the computer, she developed a slight stoop in her posture.
He tried to straighten his stoop when posing for the family photograph.
The old man walked with a noticeable stoop, evidence of years of hard labor.

Related Terms

  • synonym – slouch: a lazy, drooping posture or movement
  • antonym – upright: a vertical or straight posture
