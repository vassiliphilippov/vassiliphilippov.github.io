voucher

noun
certificate for goods, services, or discounts
Pronunciation: / ˈvaʊtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈvaʊtʃə / (BrE)
A printed certificate or document that can be exchanged for goods, services, or a discount.

Examples

voucher for free meal
She handed the cashier a voucher worth twenty dollars off her purchase.
The company issued vouchers as compensation for delayed flights.
You can use this voucher to get a free coffee at the café.

Related Terms

  • synonym – coupon: a ticket or document that can be exchanged for a financial discount or rebate
