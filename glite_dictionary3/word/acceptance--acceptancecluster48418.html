acceptance

noun
the act of receiving something offered
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛptəns / (AmE), / əkˈsɛptəns / (BrE)
the act of agreeing to receive or take something that is offered

Examples

acceptance of an offer
He was surprised by their quick acceptance of his proposal.
Her acceptance of the invitation made the host very happy.
The acceptance of the award was a proud moment for her.
