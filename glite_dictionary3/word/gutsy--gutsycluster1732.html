gutsy

adjective
showing bravery; spirited
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌtsi / (AmE), / ˈɡʌtsi / (BrE)
Showing courage, determination, or spirit, especially in difficult situations; robust or uninhibited.

Examples

gutsy move
It was a gutsy decision to start her own business during a recession.
She gave a gutsy speech that challenged the status quo.
The gutsy performance of the underdog team inspired the crowd.
