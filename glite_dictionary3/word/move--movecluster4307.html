to move

verb
formally propose in a debate or meeting
Pronunciation: / muːv / (AmE), / muːv / (BrE)
to formally propose something, such as a motion, during a debate or in a parliamentary meeting

Examples

to move a motion
I would like to move that we postpone the meeting until next week.
She moved for a vote on the new regulations after the debate.
The council member moved an amendment to the proposal during the session.
