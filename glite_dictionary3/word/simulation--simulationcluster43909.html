simulation

noun
act of pretending; false appearance
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪmjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪmjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of pretending or giving a false appearance, especially in order to deceive

Examples

false simulation
His politeness was only a simulation to win her trust.
She detected the simulation of sadness in his voice.
The magician's trick relied on careful simulation of impossible feats.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deception: the act of deceiving someone
  • antonym – honesty: the quality of being truthful or genuine
  • synonym – pretense: the act of making something appear true or real when it is not
