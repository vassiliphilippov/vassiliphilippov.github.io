Hypodermatidae

noun
family of parasitic warble flies
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊˌdɜːrməˈtɪdiː /; / haɪpoʊˌdɜrməˈtɪdiː / (AmE), / ˌhɪpəʊˌdɜːməˈtɪdiː /; / hʌɪˌpə(ʊ)dɜːməˈtɪdɪiː / (BrE)
