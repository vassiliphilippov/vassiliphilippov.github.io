skimming

noun
brushing lightly in passing
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of lightly brushing against something while passing by

Examples

skimming a surface
He avoided a full collision with the chair, escaping with only a slight skimming against its edge.
The bird's skimming of the water created gentle ripples across the pond.
With a quick skimming of her fingertips along the wall, she moved quietly through the dark hallway.
