suitably

adverb
in an appropriate or fitting manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːtəbli / (AmE), / ˈsuːtəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is appropriate or right for a particular purpose or situation

Examples

suitably dressed
He was suitably impressed by her presentation skills.
She dressed suitably for the cold weather.
The hotel was suitably decorated for the festive season.

Related Terms

  • synonym – appropriately: in a way that is suitable or proper in the circumstances
  • antonym – unsuitably: in a way that is not suitable or not right for the circumstances
