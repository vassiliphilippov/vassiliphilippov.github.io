immoderateness

noun
quality of being excessive
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɑdɚrət.nəs / (AmE), / ɪˈmɒdərət.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being excessive and lacking in moderation.

Examples

extreme immoderateness
Critics have pointed out the immoderateness of her remarks during the debate.
Living with immoderateness often results in unhealthy habits and stress.
The immoderateness of his spending eventually led to financial trouble.

Related Terms

  • synonym – excessiveness: quality of exceeding the limits of what is normal or desirable
  • antonym – moderation: the quality of being moderate; restraint or avoidance of excess
