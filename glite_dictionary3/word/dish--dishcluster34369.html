to dish

verb
to serve food
Pronunciation: / dɪʃ / (AmE), / dɪʃ / (BrE)
to serve or distribute food to people, often using 'dish out'

Examples

to dish out food
After the race, he dished up a hearty breakfast for the team.
At the shelter, volunteers dished hot stew to everyone in line.
She dished out generous servings of pasta to her guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym – distribute: to give out things to several people
  • synonym – serve: to offer food or drink to someone at a meal
