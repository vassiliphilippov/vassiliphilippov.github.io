terminal

noun
station for passengers or goods
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜːrmɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈtɜːmɪnəl / (BrE)
a building or area at a station, airport, or port where passengers or goods begin or end their journey

Examples

airport terminal
After landing, the passengers walked to the main terminal to collect their luggage.
Cargo trucks lined up at the shipping terminal waiting to be unloaded.
The new terminal at the airport features dozens of shops and restaurants.

Related Terms

  • synonym – depot: a place for storing large quantities of goods or for passengers to board vehicles
  • synonym – station: a place where passengers or goods are loaded or unloaded
