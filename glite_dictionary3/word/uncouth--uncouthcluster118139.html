uncouth

adjective
lacking refinement or good manners
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkuθ / (AmE), / ʌnˈkuːθ / (BrE)
lacking good manners, refinement, or grace; uncultured or coarse in behavior or appearance

Examples

uncouth behavior
Despite his brilliance, some found his manners a bit uncouth.
His uncouth behavior at dinner shocked the other guests.
The uncouth language used in the play was criticized by reviewers.
