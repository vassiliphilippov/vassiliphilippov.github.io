to uncurl

verb
straighten from a curled position
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɜrl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɜːl / (BrE)
to move or cause to move out of a curled or coiled position

Examples

to uncurl a leaf
As the fern leaves uncurl, they reveal a beautiful pattern.
She slowly uncurled her fingers to show the hidden coin.
The cat began to uncurl after its nap in the sun.
