Brahminism

noun
orthodox Hindu religious and social system
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɑːmɪˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈbræmɪˌnɪzəm / (BrE)
the religious and social system of orthodox Hinduism, based on Vedic teachings and rituals, emphasizing the authority and role of the Brahmin (priestly) caste
