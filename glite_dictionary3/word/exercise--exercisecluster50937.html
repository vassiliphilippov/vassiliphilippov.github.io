exercise

noun
an action regarded as a type or example of something
Pronunciation: / ˈɛksərˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛksəsaɪz / (BrE)
an activity or action regarded mainly as an example of something, often with a particular quality (typically negative, such as futility or pointlessness)

Examples

exercise in futility
The meeting turned out to be nothing more than an exercise in bureaucracy.
The whole debate became an exercise in avoiding the real issues.
Trying to convince him was an exercise in futility.
