cognisance

noun
awareness or knowledge of something
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːɡnɪzəns / (AmE), / ˈkɒɡnɪzəns / (BrE)
knowledge or awareness of something; the state of being aware or informed.

Examples

cognisance of danger
She spoke up as soon as she became fully cognisance of the risks involved.
The court will take cognisance of the new evidence submitted by the defense.
The teacher took cognisance of his students' unique learning styles when planning her lessons.

Related Terms

  • synonym – awareness: the state of being conscious of something
  • antonym – ignorance: lack of knowledge or awareness
