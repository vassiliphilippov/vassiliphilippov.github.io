diplopia

noun
double vision
Pronunciation: / dɪˈploʊpiə / (AmE), / dɪˈpləʊpiə / (BrE)
a condition in which a single object is perceived as two images; double vision

Examples

diplopia symptoms appear
Covering one eye may temporarily relieve symptoms of diplopia.

Sometimes diplopia occurs as a side effect of certain medications.
