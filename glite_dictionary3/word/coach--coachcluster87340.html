coach

noun
person who trains athletes or a team
Pronunciation: / koʊtʃ / (AmE), / kəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a person who trains or teaches others, especially in sports or skills

Examples

coach a sports team
As a coach, she motivates her players both on and off the field.
He became a coach after retiring from professional basketball.
The soccer team hired a new coach to improve their performance next season.
A language coach assisted the cast with their accents for the play.
She hired a coach to help improve her vocal technique.
The young actor worked with an experienced coach before his big audition.

Related Terms

  • antonym – athlete: a person trained to compete in sports
  • synonym – instructor: a person who teaches or trains something
  • antonym – student: a person who is taught by a coach
  • synonym – trainer: person who teaches or prepares others, especially in sports
  • synonym – tutor: a private teacher, especially one who teaches a single student or a very small group
