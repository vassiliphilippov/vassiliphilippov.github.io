diagnostics

noun
tests for computer systems
Pronunciation: / daɪ.əɡˈnɑstɪks / (AmE), / daɪ.əɡˈnɒstɪks / (BrE)
a set of tests, programs, or tools used to check the condition, performance, or problems of computer systems, devices, or software.

Examples

computer diagnostics
Before shipping the laptop, the technician ran diagnostics to ensure all components were functioning.
The car's onboard system showed error codes after running diagnostics during the factory inspection.
If your phone is slow, try the built-in app that performs diagnostics and reports hardware problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym – diagnostic test: a test used to identify faults or problems in hardware or software
  • synonym – troubleshooting: the process of finding and fixing problems in systems or devices
