Hsuan Chiao

noun
Chinese philosophical and religious system (Taoism)
Pronunciation: / ˈʃwɑːn ˈtʃiːaʊ / (AmE), / ˈʃwæn ˈtʃaɪ.əʊ / (BrE)
A popular Chinese philosophical and religious system based on the teachings of Lao-tzu, characterized by belief in many gods, alchemy, divination, and magic.
