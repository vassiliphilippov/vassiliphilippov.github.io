knowingly

adverb
deliberately; with full awareness or intent
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈnəʊɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows full knowledge, awareness, or intent; deliberately.

Examples

knowingly break the rules
He knowingly withheld information from the committee.
She knowingly broke the rule, accepting the consequences that might follow.
The company knowingly sold products that did not meet safety standards.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deliberately: in a way that is intentional or planned
  • synonym – intentionally: with intention or on purpose
  • antonym – unwittingly: without being aware; unintentionally
