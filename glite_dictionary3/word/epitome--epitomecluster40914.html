epitome

noun
a perfect example; embodiment
Pronunciation: / ɪˈpɪtəmi / (AmE), / ɪˈpɪtəmi / (BrE)
a person or thing that is a perfect example of a particular quality or type

Examples

epitome of kindness
His actions were the epitome of generosity and kindness.
She is the epitome of elegance and grace.
The quiet village is often described as the epitome of rural charm.
