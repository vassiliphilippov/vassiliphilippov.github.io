peck

noun
a bird's quick bite or strike
Pronunciation: / pɛk / (AmE), / pɛk / (BrE)
a quick, sharp bite or strike made by a bird with its beak

Examples

peck from a bird
After a warning peck from the crow, the cat kept its distance.
She flinched as the chicken delivered a quick peck at her hand.
The goose gave him a sharp peck on the arm when he tried to feed it.
