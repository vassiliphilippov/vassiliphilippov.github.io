to care

verb
wish or desire to do something (polite)
Pronunciation: / kɛr / (AmE), / keə / (BrE)
to wish, desire, or prefer to have or do something, often used in polite offers or invitations

Examples

to care to join
I don't care to discuss the details at this moment.
If you care to try, you might find it enjoyable.
Would you care to join me for dinner this evening?
