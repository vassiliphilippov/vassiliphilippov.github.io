misinterpretation

noun
incorrect interpretation; misunderstanding
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪs.ɪnˌtɝː.prəˈteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪs.ɪnˌtɜː.prɪˈteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
An incorrect understanding or explanation of something; the act of interpreting something wrongly.

Examples

misinterpretation of facts
A misinterpretation of the data could result in flawed conclusions.
Her repeated misinterpretation of the rules led to unnecessary confusion during the meeting.
The controversy was fueled by a misinterpretation of his original statement.
