geisha

noun
Japanese female entertainer and hostess
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪ.ʃə / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪ.ʃə / (BrE)
A Japanese woman trained to entertain men by performing traditional music, dance, and engaging in conversation.

Examples

traditional geisha performance
Many tourists are fascinated by the art and elegance of a geisha.
She studied for years to become a skilled geisha in Kyoto.
The geisha entertained the guests with traditional music and graceful dance.
