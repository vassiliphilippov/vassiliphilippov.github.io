mussitation

noun
low indistinct murmuring sound
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌsɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmʌsɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
A low, continuous, indistinct sound resulting from murmuring, often with lip movements but without producing clear speech.
