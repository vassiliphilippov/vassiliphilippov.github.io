to dictate

verb
say aloud for someone to write down
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪk.teɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɪk.teɪt / (BrE)
to say words aloud so that they may be written down or recorded by someone else

Examples

to dictate a letter
She prefers to dictate her letters instead of typing them herself.
The manager asked his assistant to dictate the meeting minutes.
The professor would dictate notes while the students wrote them down.

Related Terms

  • synonym – recite: to repeat aloud from memory
  • synonym – transcribe: to put spoken words into written form
