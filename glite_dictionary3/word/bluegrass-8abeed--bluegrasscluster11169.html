bluegrass

noun
fast-tempo country music genre
Pronunciation: / ˈbluˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A style of country music played at a fast tempo, typically featuring banjos, guitars, and other stringed instruments.

Examples

bluegrass music festival
Many musicians travel to Kentucky to experience authentic bluegrass performances.
She learned to play the banjo by practicing classic bluegrass songs.
The festival featured several top bluegrass bands from around the country.
