floater

noun
wandering person without a fixed home
Pronunciation: / ˈfloʊtər / (AmE), / ˈfləʊtə / (BrE)
a person who moves from place to place without a permanent home or regular means of support

Examples

wandering floater person
After losing his job, he became a floater, drifting from town to town in search of work.
Her family was worried when she started living like a floater, with no address or steady income.
The city tried to provide shelters for floaters during the harsh winter months.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drifter: a person who moves from place to place without a permanent home or job
  • synonym – vagrant: a person without a settled home or regular work, wandering from place to place
