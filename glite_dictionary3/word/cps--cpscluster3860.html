cps

noun
clicks per second (gaming measure)
Pronunciation: / si pi ɛs / (AmE), / siː piː ɛs / (BrE)
Informal abbreviation for 'clicks per second', a measure of how many times a mouse or other input is clicked in one second, used especially in gaming to gauge clicking speed.

Examples

cps in gaming
Top players can reach over 20 cps during intense duels.
He practiced clicking to improve his cps before the online tournament next week.
The coach measured each player's sustained cps with a simple test to compare speeds.
