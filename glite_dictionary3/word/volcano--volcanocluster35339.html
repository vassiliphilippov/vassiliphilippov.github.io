volcano

noun
mountain through which lava erupts
Pronunciation: / voʊlˈkeɪnoʊ / (AmE), / vɒlˈkeɪnəʊ / (BrE)
a mountain or hill with an opening (crater or vent) through which lava, ash, and gases erupt from the earth's crust
