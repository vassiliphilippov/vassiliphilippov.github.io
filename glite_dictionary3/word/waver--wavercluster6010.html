to waver

verb
to pause in uncertainty or be indecisive
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪvɚ /; / ˈweɪvər / (AmE), / ˈweɪvə / (BrE)
to be uncertain or hesitant, especially when making a decision or maintaining a belief
