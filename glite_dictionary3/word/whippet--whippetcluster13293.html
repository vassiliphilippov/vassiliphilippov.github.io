whippet

noun
a slender, fast breed of dog
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪpɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɪpɪt / (BrE)
A small, slender breed of dog similar to a greyhound, originally developed in England for racing and hunting.

Examples

whippet dog breed
Many families choose the whippet because of its affectionate nature and elegant appearance.
She adopted a gentle whippet from the local animal shelter.
The whippet raced across the field at remarkable speed.
