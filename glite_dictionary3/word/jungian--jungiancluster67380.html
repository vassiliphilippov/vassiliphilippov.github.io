Jungian

noun
follower of Jung's theories
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋiən / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋiən / (BrE)
A follower or advocate of the theories of Carl Jung, the Swiss psychiatrist and psychoanalyst.

Examples

a Jungian therapist
As a practicing Jungian, she often interpreted dreams using archetypes and symbolism.
He became a Jungian after reading extensively about psychoanalysis in his twenties.
Many Jungians believe that the unconscious mind is the key to personal growth.
