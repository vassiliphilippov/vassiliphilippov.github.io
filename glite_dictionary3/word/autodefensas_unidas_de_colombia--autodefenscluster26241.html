Autodefensas Unidas de Colombia

proper noun
Colombian paramilitary and terrorist group
Pronunciation: / aʊtoʊdeɪˈfɛnsɑs ˈunidɑs deɪ koʊˈlʌmbiə / (AmE), / ˌaʊtəʊdeɪˈfɛnsæs ˈjuːnɪdæs deɪ kəˈlʌmbiə / (BrE)
