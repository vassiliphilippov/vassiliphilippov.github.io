Phthirus

noun
genus of parasitic crab lice
Pronunciation: / ˈθaɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈθaɪrəs / (BrE)
A genus of parasitic lice, including the crab louse that infests humans.

Examples

Phthirus pubis species
Phthirus pubis is the species of louse responsible for crab louse infestations in humans.
The discovery of Phthirus in ancient remains shows its long association with humans.
Treatment for Phthirus infestations typically involves topical insecticides.
