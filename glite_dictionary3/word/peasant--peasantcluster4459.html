peasant

noun
an uncouth, unrefined person
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛzənt / (AmE), / ˈpɛznt / (BrE)
an uncouth or uncultured person; someone regarded as lacking refinement or manners (often used derogatorily)

Examples

rude peasant
Don't be such a peasant; use your napkin properly.
He was called a peasant by his colleagues because of his rude behavior at dinner.
Some critics dismissed the artist as a peasant who lacked sophistication.
