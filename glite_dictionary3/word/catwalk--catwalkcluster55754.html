catwalk

noun
platform models walk on at fashion shows
Pronunciation: / ˈkætˌwɔk / (AmE), / ˈkæt.wɔːk / (BrE)
a narrow, usually raised platform that extends into an auditorium, used by models at fashion shows or by performers during presentations.

Examples

fashion catwalk
She dreamed of one day walking the catwalk in Paris.
The models strutted confidently along the catwalk during the fashion week finale.
The new designer's collection was the talk of the catwalk this season.

  • synonym – runway: a platform on which models walk to display clothes
