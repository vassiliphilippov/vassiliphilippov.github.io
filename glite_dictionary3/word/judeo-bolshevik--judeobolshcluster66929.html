Judeo-Bolshevik

adjective
derogatory term linking Jews to Bolshevism
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒuːdioʊ boʊlˈʃɛvɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʒuːdɪəʊ bɒlˈʃɛvɪk / (BrE)
A derogatory term describing alleged connections between Jews and Bolshevism, used especially in anti-Semitic conspiracy theories and propaganda.

Examples

Judeo-Bolshevik propaganda
Many historians have documented the use of the term 'Judeo-Bolshevik' in anti-Jewish propaganda.
References to a 'Judeo-Bolshevik conspiracy' appear in numerous extremist publications from the 20th century.
The "Judeo-Bolshevik" myth was widely propagated in Nazi Germany to justify anti-Semitic policies.
