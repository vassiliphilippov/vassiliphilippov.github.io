fibroid

noun
benign tumor of fibrous tissue, esp. uterus
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪ.brɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈfʌɪ.brɔɪd / (BrE)
a benign tumor composed mainly of fibrous or muscular tissue, especially one occurring in the uterus

Examples

uterine fibroid
After the surgery to remove the fibroid, her symptoms greatly improved.
Many women experience pain or heavy bleeding as a result of fibroids.
The doctor explained that the fibroid in her uterus was noncancerous but would require monitoring.
