Kalahari

proper noun
desert in southwestern Africa
Pronunciation: / ˌkæl.əˈhɑː.ri / (AmE), / ˌkæ.ləˈhɑː.ri / (BrE)
A large desert region in southwestern Africa, mainly covering Botswana and parts of Namibia and South Africa.

Examples

the Kalahari Desert
Scientists have studied the unique plant life adapted to survive in the harsh Kalahari environment.
The vast Kalahari is home to a diverse range of wildlife, including meerkats and antelopes.
We crossed the edge of the Kalahari Desert during our trip through Botswana.
