shortness

noun
being shorter than average height
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrt.nəs / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt.nəs / (BrE)
the condition or quality of being shorter than average in stature or height.

Examples

shortness of height
Because of her shortness, she was always placed at the front in group photos.
He was sensitive about his shortness compared to his taller classmates.
His shortness made it difficult for him to reach the top shelf.

Related Terms

  • antonym – tallness: the condition of being tall or of above-average height
