bellyache

noun
stomach or abdominal pain
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛliˌeɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɛliˌeɪk / (BrE)
A pain or ache in the stomach or abdominal area.

Examples

bellyache after eating
After eating too much candy, she complained of a bellyache all evening.
Children often get a bellyache from running around after lunch.
He skipped dinner because he had a mild bellyache from lunch.
