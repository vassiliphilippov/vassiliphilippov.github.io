Achilles

proper noun
Greek mythological hero of the Iliad
Pronunciation: / əˈkɪliz / (AmE), / əˈkɪliːz / (BrE)
A hero of Greek mythology, the greatest Greek warrior in the Iliad, whose only vulnerable spot was his heel.
