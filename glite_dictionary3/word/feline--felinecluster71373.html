feline

noun
a mammal of the cat family
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈfiːlaɪn / (BrE)
a mammal belonging to the cat family (Felidae), typically with a slender body, round head, and often sharp retractile claws; includes domestic cats, lions, tigers, and related animals

Examples

wild feline
Lions, leopards, and house cats all belong to the feline family.
She adopted several felines from the animal shelter, including both big and small cats.
The tiger, being a large feline, stalks its prey with incredible stealth.
