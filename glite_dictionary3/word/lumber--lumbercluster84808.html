lumber

noun
wood prepared for building
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈlʌmbə / (BrE)
wood that has been cut and prepared for use in building or carpentry

Examples

lumber for building
He stacked piles of lumber in the shed to use for future home repairs.
Prices for lumber have increased due to high demand and limited supply.
The new house was constructed from high-quality lumber shipped from the Pacific Northwest.
