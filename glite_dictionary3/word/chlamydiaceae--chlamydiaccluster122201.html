Chlamydiaceae

noun
family of Gram-negative parasitic bacteria
Pronunciation: / klæmˌɪdiəˈsiːi / (AmE), / klamˌɪdɪəˈsiːiː / (BrE)
A family of Gram-negative bacteria that are obligate parasites in warm-blooded vertebrates, including humans.
