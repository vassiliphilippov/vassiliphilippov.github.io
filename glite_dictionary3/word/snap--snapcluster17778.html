snap

noun
ability to spring back; elasticity
Pronunciation: / snæp / (AmE), / snæp / (BrE)
the ability of an object or material to quickly return to its original shape after being stretched or compressed; elasticity or resilience

Examples

good snap in fabric
After years of use, the couch cushions had lost their snap and felt flat.
The elastic band still has plenty of snap and stretches easily without breaking.
The material is chosen for its durability and snap in sportswear.
