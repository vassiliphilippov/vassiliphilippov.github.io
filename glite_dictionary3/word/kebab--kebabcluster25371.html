kebab

noun
marinated meat cooked on a skewer
Pronunciation: / kəˈbɑb / (AmE), / kɪˈbæb / (BrE)
A dish of cubes of meat, often marinated, cooked and served on a skewer, usually with vegetables.

Examples

grilled kebab skewer
At the street market, vendors sold lamb kebabs wrapped in warm flatbread.
Each kebab was carefully marinated and cooked over an open flame.
We ordered chicken kebabs with grilled peppers and onions for dinner last night.

  • synonym – shish kebab: skewered and grilled cubes of meat, usually with vegetables
