Pliny

proper noun
Roman author and naturalist (23–79 AD)
Pronunciation: / ˈplɪni / (AmE), / ˈplɪni / (BrE)
(proper noun) A Roman author and naturalist (23–79 AD), known for his encyclopedic work 'Natural History' and for dying during the eruption of Mount Vesuvius.
