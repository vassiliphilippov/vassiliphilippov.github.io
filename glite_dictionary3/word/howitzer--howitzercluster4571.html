howitzer

noun
short-barreled artillery gun
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊ.ɪt.sɚ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊ.ɪt.sə / (BrE)
a type of large, short-barreled artillery gun designed to fire shells at high angles for relatively short ranges

Examples

a howitzer fires shells
During the demonstration, the soldiers fired the old howitzer as part of the ceremony.
Modern howitzers can quickly be moved and set up by just a few people.
The army positioned a howitzer on the hill to provide artillery support.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cannon: a large, heavy type of artillery gun
  • synonym – mortar: a short, portable, high-angle artillery piece
