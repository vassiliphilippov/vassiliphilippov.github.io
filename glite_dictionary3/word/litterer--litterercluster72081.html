litterer

noun
person who drops litter in public
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪtərər / (AmE), / ˈlɪtərə / (BrE)
a person who discards rubbish or waste in public places, especially where it is forbidden or inappropriate

Examples

litterer drops trash
Signs warn that any litterer will be fined $100.
The park was full of rubbish because of one careless litterer.
We must educate people so that no one becomes a habitual litterer.
