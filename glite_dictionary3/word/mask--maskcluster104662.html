mask

noun
covering for disguising the face
Pronunciation: / mæsk / (AmE), / mɑːsk / (BrE)
a covering worn on the face to disguise, conceal, or protect it, or for ceremonial purposes

Examples

wear a mask
At the costume party, everyone wore a mask to hide their identity.
He put on a surgical mask before entering the lab.
The actor removed his mask at the end of the performance.
During the festival, everyone put on a colorful mask and joined the parade.
He wore a mask to protect himself from the dust while cleaning the attic.
The doctor asked the patient to wear a mask before entering the examination room.
