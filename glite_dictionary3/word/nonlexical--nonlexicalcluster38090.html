nonlexical

adjective
not relating to words or vocabulary
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈlɛksɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈlɛksɪkəl / (BrE)
not relating to words or the vocabulary of a language; not consisting of or expressed as words.

Examples

nonlexical features
Certain grammatical features in language are nonlexical and cannot be found in the dictionary.
Phonological processes are often considered nonlexical components of language.
The researcher distinguished between lexical and nonlexical items within the data set.
