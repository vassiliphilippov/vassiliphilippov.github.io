mandioc

noun
tropical plant; cassava; edible starchy root
Pronunciation: / ˈmændiˌɑk / (AmE), / ˈmændɪɒk / (BrE)
a tropical plant with long tuberous edible roots and brittle stems, cultivated for its starchy roots used as food and to make tapioca

Examples

mandioc root vegetable
After harvesting, she peeled the mandioc roots and prepared them for cooking.
Tapioca is made from the starchy extract of mandioc roots.
The farmers grew mandioc as a staple crop to feed the entire village.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cassava: another term for the tropical plant with starchy edible roots (Manihot esculenta)
  • synonym – manioc: alternative spelling for the same plant, especially in some English dialects
