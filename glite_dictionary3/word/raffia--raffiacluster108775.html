Raffia

noun
genus of feather palms used commercially
Pronunciation: / ˈræfiə / (AmE), / ˈræfiə / (BrE)
A genus of feather palms native to tropical Africa, Madagascar, and the Americas, widely grown for commercial use, especially for their fibrous leaves.
