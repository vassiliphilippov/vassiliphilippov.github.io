defalcation

noun
sum of money misappropriated
Pronunciation: / ˌdiːfælˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdiːfælˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
an amount of money that has been misappropriated or embezzled, especially by someone trusted with its charge
