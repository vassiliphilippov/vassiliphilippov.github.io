shirt

noun
upper-body garment
Pronunciation: / ʃɝt / (AmE), / ʃɜːt / (BrE)
A garment for the upper part of the body, typically having a collar, sleeves, and a front opening.

Examples

white shirt
He wore a crisp white shirt to the job interview.
In summer, I prefer to wear a light cotton shirt to stay cool.
She spilled coffee on her favorite shirt this morning.
