Kalashnikov

noun
Russian automatic rifle, especially AK-47
Pronunciation: / kəˈlæʃnɪˌkɑv / (AmE), / kəˈlæʃnɪˌkɒf / (BrE)
A type of automatic rifle, especially the AK-47, originally developed in the Soviet Union by Mikhail Kalashnikov.
