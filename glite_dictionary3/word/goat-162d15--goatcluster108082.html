goat

noun
greatest of all time (slang)
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊt / (AmE), / ɡəʊt / (BrE)
informal slang. a person who is widely regarded as the greatest of all time in a particular field or activity.

Examples

the goat of tennis
Many fans argue that Serena Williams is the goat of women's tennis because of her records.
After his tenth title, commentators debated whether Messi deserved the goat label in football history.
When fans argue online, someone will claim their favorite player is the goat of all time.

Related Terms

  • synonym – greatest: the best or most outstanding in a particular category
  • synonym – legend: a person celebrated for notable achievements and long-lasting fame
