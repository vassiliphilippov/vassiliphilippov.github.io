unironed

adjective
not ironed (of clothes or linens)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈaɪərnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈaɪənd / (BrE)
(of clothes or linens) not ironed; having creases or wrinkles left unremoved by ironing.

Examples

unironed shirt sleeves
Despite her busy schedule, her unironed blouse looked presentable.
He grabbed a shirt from the pile of unironed laundry before heading out.
The wedding dress arrived slightly unironed after being shipped in a small box.

Related Terms

  • antonym – ironed: (of clothes or linens) pressed with an iron to remove wrinkles
