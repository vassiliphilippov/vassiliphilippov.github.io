Ernst Mach

proper noun
Austrian physicist and philosopher
Pronunciation: / ɜrnst mɑk / (AmE), / ɜːnst mɑːk / (BrE)
(1838–1916) Austrian physicist and philosopher noted for his work on the Mach number in physics and for contributions to logical positivism.

Examples

Ernst Mach number theory
Ernst Mach made significant contributions to both physics and philosophy in the late 19th and early 20th century.
Many philosophers consider Ernst Mach's work foundational to the development of logical positivism.
The concept of the Mach number is named after Ernst Mach.
