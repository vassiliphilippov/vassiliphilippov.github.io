lithomancy

noun
divination using stones or talismans
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪθ.əˌmæn.si / (AmE), / ˈlɪθ.əˌmæn.si / (BrE)
divination by interpreting the patterns formed by stones or stone talismans.

Examples

lithomancy reading stones
A book on lithomancy described how to cast stones and interpret the patterns.
Many ancient cultures practiced lithomancy to predict important events.
The fortune teller offered readings in tarot, palmistry, and lithomancy.
