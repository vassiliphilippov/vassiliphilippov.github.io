Teng Hsiao-ping

proper noun
Chinese communist leader, 1970s–1990s
Pronunciation: / tʌŋ ˌʃaʊˈpɪŋ / (AmE), / tɛŋ ˌʃaʊˈpɪŋ / (BrE)
(1904–1997) Chinese communist leader who was the paramount figure in China from the late 1970s to the early 1990s, introducing major economic reforms.
