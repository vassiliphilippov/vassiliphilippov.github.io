bawling

noun
loud cries while weeping
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)
loud, unrestrained cries made while weeping or expressing strong emotion

Examples

loud bawling sounds
After hearing the bad news, his bawling echoed down the corridor.
She was too upset to speak, lost in her bawling and tears.
The bawling of the baby could be heard throughout the house.

Related Terms

  • synonym – wailing: long, high-pitched cries of grief or pain
  • degree lower – whimpering: quiet, broken cries; sobbing softly
