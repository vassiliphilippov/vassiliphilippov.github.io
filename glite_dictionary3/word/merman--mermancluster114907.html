merman

noun
mythical male half-man, half-fish
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝː.mæn / (AmE), / ˈmɜː.mæn / (BrE)
A mythical male creature with the upper body of a man and the tail of a fish, believed to live in the sea.

Examples

a merman in the sea
In stories, a merman often falls in love with a human woman.
Legends tell of a merman who could summon storms with a wave of his hand.
The villagers claimed to have seen a merman swimming near the rocky shores.
