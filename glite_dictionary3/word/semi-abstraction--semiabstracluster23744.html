semi-abstraction

noun
partly abstract art style
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmi ˌæbˈstrækʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛmi ˌabˈstrakʃən / (BrE)
A style of art in which some elements are recognizable but are rendered in a way that is not fully realistic.
