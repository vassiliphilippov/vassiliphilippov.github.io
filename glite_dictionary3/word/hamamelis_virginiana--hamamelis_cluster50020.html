Hamamelis virginiana

noun
North American shrub, also called witch hazel
Pronunciation: / ˌhæməˈmilɪs vɜrdʒɪˈneɪnə / (AmE), / ˌhaməˈmiːlɪs vɜːdʒɪˈneɪnɪə / (BrE)
A shrub native to eastern North America, known for its small yellow flowers that appear after the leaves have fallen; commonly called witch hazel.
