Gastromycetes

noun
group of fungi with enclosed spore layer
Pronunciation: / ˌɡæstroʊmaɪˈsiːtiːz / (AmE), / ˌɡæstrəʊmɪˈsiːtiːz /; / ˌɡastrəʊmʌɪˈsiːtiːz / (BrE)
a former class of fungi in which the spore-producing layer is enclosed until the spores mature, including puffballs, earth stars, and stinkhorns
