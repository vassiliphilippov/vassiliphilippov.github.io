cellulitis

noun
skin infection causing swelling and redness
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛl.jəˈlaɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˌsɛl.jʊˈlaɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
A bacterial infection causing inflammation of the skin and underlying tissues, often accompanied by redness, swelling, warmth, and pain.
