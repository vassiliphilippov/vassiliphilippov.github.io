grip

noun
a flat wire hairpin
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪp / (AmE), / ɡrɪp / (BrE)
(British) A flat wire hairpin whose prongs press tightly together, used to hold hair in place.

Examples

hair grip
Do you have a spare grip for my bun?
In Britain, a bobby pin is often called a grip.
