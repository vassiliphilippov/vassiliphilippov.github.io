borrowing

noun
act of obtaining money or resources from a lender
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːroʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɒrəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of obtaining money or something else of value from someone with the intention of returning it, often with interest

Examples

borrowing money
Banks have tightened their borrowing criteria in response to financial instability.
Households often rely on borrowing to finance major purchases such as cars or homes.
The government's excessive borrowing contributed to the rise in national debt.
