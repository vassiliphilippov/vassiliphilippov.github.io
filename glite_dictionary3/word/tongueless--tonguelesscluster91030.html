tongueless

adjective
unspoken; silent; without speech
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌŋ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈtʌŋ.lɪs / (BrE)
(literary) Expressed or existing without spoken words; silent or unspoken.

Examples

tongueless silence
She gave him a tongueless look of sympathy across the crowded room.
Some grief is too deep for words, remaining tongueless and hidden.
The artist's tongueless plea for peace resonated with many viewers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – silent: not making or accompanied by any sound
  • antonym – spoken: expressed in speech; uttered aloud
  • synonym – wordless: not expressed in words; unspoken
