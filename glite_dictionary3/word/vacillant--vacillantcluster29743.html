vacillant

adjective
uncertain or indecisive
Pronunciation: / ˈvæsɪlənt / (AmE), / ˈvasɪlənt / (BrE)
Uncertain or wavering in purpose or action; indecisive.

Examples

vacillant decision making
Her vacillant attitude cost her the opportunity she had hoped for.
In moments of crisis, leaders cannot afford to appear vacillant or unsteady.
The committee remained vacillant in its decision, unable to commit to a clear course of action.

Related Terms

  • antonym – decisive: able to make decisions quickly and confidently
  • synonym – uncertain: not sure or confident
