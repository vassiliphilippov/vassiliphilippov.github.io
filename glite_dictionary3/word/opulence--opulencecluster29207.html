opulence

noun
great wealth or luxuriousness
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.pjə.ləns / (AmE), / ˈɒp.jʊ.ləns / (BrE)
great wealth or luxuriousness; the state of having or displaying immense riches, often in a lavish or extravagant manner

Examples

show of opulence
Despite his opulence, he lived a surprisingly modest life among the wealthy elite.
Her wedding dress embodied opulence, adorned with precious jewels and intricate lacework.
The royal palace was a symbol of opulence with its gilded halls and marble staircases.
