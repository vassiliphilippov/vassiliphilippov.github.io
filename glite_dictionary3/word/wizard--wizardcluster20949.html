wizard

noun
person with magical powers
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪzərd / (AmE), / ˈwɪzəd / (BrE)
a person who is believed to have magical powers and practice sorcery

Examples

powerful wizard
As a child, she loved reading stories about a wise old wizard who helped heroes with his magic.
Dressed in a long robe and pointed hat, the wizard amazed the crowd with his mysterious tricks.
The wizard cast a powerful spell over the kingdom, causing everyone to fall asleep for a hundred years.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sorcerer: a person who claims or is believed to have magic powers; a wizard
  • synonym – witch: a woman thought to have magic powers, especially evil ones
