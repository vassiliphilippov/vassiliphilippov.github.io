phaneromania

noun
compulsive picking at body parts
Pronunciation: / fænəroʊˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / fanərəʊˈmeɪnɪə / (BrE)
an irresistible urge or compulsion to pick at superficial body parts, such as nails or skin

Examples

phaneromania nail picking
His chronic phaneromania led to constant irritation and injury to his fingertips.
Psychologists often encounter phaneromania in patients dealing with anxiety and stress.
Treatment for phaneromania may involve behavioral therapy and stress management techniques.
