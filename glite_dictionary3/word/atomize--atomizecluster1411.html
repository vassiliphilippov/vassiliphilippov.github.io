to atomize

verb
to destroy with powerful weapons
Pronunciation: / ˈætəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈætəmaɪz / (BrE)
to destroy or hit with powerful weapons, such as bombs, especially reducing something to small fragments or particles; to annihilate through force

Examples

to atomize a city
The air force planned to atomize the enemy bunkers with a series of precision strikes.
The movie depicted a city being atomized by a catastrophic explosion.
They threatened to atomize the entire complex if peace talks failed.

  • synonym – obliterate: to wipe out; destroy utterly
