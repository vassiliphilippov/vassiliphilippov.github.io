Armillaria ponderosa

noun
a large edible mushroom species
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˌmɪlɛəriə pɒn.dəˈroʊsə / (AmE), / ɑːˌmɪl.ə.rɪə pɒn.dəˈrəʊ.sə / (BrE)
a large species of edible white mushroom, developing brown stains as it ages, with white gills and a spicy aroma, collected commercially for cooking, especially in the Pacific Northwest
