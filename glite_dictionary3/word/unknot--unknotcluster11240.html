to unknot

verb
to untie or undo a knot
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈnɑt / (AmE), / ʌnˈnɒt / (BrE)
to undo or untangle a knot, or to make something that is knotted become separate or straight

Examples

to unknot a necklace
Can you help me unknot this necklace before it breaks?
It took several minutes to unknot the tangled rope.
She carefully began to unknot the threads of the old sweater.
