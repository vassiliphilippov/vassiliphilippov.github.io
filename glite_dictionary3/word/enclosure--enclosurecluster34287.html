enclosure

noun
item included with a letter or package
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkloʊʒɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈkləʊʒə / (BrE)
a document or item placed inside an envelope along with a covering letter, usually as additional information or support

Examples

enclosure with letter
Please find the enclosure with the relevant documents attached to this letter.
Remember to mention any enclosure at the bottom of your cover letter.
The hiring manager reviewed each enclosure submitted by the candidates.
