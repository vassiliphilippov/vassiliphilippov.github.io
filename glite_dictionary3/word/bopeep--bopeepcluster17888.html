bopeep

noun
children's game of hiding and revealing face
Pronunciation: / boʊˈpip / (AmE), / bəʊˈpiːp / (BrE)
A children's game in which one hides one's face and then suddenly reveals it, usually saying 'boo!'

Examples

play bopeep with a child
He remembered his grandmother playing bopeep with him when he was small.
In the nursery, the children were giggling as they enjoyed a game of bopeep.
The mother played bopeep with her baby to make her laugh.
