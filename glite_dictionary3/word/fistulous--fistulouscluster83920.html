fistulous

adjective
hollow and tube-shaped; reed-like
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪstʃu·ləs /; / ˈfɪs.tʃə.ləs / (AmE), / ˈfɪstjʊləs /; / ˈfɪs.tjʊ.ləs / (BrE)
hollow and tube-shaped, like a reed; often used to describe passages or structures in medical or botanical contexts, especially relating to a fistula
