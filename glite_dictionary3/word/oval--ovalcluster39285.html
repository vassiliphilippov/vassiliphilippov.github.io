oval

noun
an elongated round shape; ellipse-like curve
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.vəl / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.vəl / (BrE)
a closed curve on a plane, shaped like an elongated circle or ellipse, having no sharp corners and a single continuous edge
