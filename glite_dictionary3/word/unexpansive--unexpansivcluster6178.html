unexpansive

adjective
not tending to expand
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.ɪkˈspæn.sɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪkˈspæn.sɪv / (BrE)

unexpansive material
Because the material is unexpansive, it is ideal for applications requiring stability under temperature changes.
