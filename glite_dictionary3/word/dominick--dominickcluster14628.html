Dominick

noun
American chicken breed with barred plumage
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑməˌnɪk / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪnɪk / (BrE)
An American breed of chicken with barred grey feathers, raised for meat and brown eggs.

Examples

Dominick chicken breed
Many heritage breeds, including the Dominick, have regained popularity with backyard poultry keepers.
