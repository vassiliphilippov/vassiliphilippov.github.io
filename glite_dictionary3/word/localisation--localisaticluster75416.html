localisation

noun
determining the location of something
Pronunciation: / ˌloʊkəlɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌləʊkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
The process of determining or identifying the exact place or position of something.

Examples

localisation of a tumor
Accurate localisation of the tumor is critical for successful surgery.
The localisation of the sound allowed the rescue team to find the trapped miners.
Using GPS, the localisation of the lost hiker was achieved within minutes.
