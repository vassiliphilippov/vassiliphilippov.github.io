Asteridae

noun
major group of flowering plants in botany
Pronunciation: / æs.təˈraɪ.di.i /; / ˌæstɚˈaɪdiː / (AmE), / ˌastəˈraɪdiːiː /; / ˌæstəˈraɪdiː / (BrE)
a large group or subclass of flowering plants with fused petals and usually two fused carpels, including many familiar families such as Solanaceae and Compositae
