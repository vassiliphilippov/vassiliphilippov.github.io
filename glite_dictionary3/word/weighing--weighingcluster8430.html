weighing

noun
careful consideration or deliberation
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈweɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
Careful and deliberate consideration or assessment of something before making a decision.

Examples

weighing of options
After much weighing of the pros and cons, she accepted the new job offer.
His decision was not impulsive, but the result of careful weighing of all factors.
The committee's weighing of the evidence took several days before they reached a verdict.
