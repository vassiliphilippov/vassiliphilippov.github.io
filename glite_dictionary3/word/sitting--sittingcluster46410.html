sitting

noun
spiritualist meeting to contact spirits
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a meeting at which spiritualists attempt to communicate with spirits, typically led by a medium

Examples

spiritualist sitting ceremony
At last night's sitting, several participants claimed to have witnessed strange phenomena.
The family attended a sitting with the renowned medium in hopes of contacting their ancestor.
The group scheduled a second sitting after the medium declared she sensed another presence in the room.
