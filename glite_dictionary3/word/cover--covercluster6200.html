cover

noun
fixed charge in restaurants/nightclubs
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌv.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌv.ə / (BrE)
A fixed charge added by a restaurant or nightclub, in addition to the cost of food and drinks.

Examples

cover charge
Some cafés in Italy automatically include a cover charge on your receipt.
The restaurant adds a cover to every bill, regardless of what you order.
There was a $20 cover just to enter the club on Saturday night.
