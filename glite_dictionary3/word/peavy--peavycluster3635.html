peavy

noun
lever with spike for handling logs
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːvi / (AmE), / ˈpiːvi / (BrE)
a stout wooden or metal lever with a sharp spike and often a side hook, used for handling and turning logs

Examples

peavy log tool
A peavy is essential equipment when working in traditional logging camps.
He gripped the peavy tightly, maneuvering the logs down the riverbank.
The lumberjack used his peavy to roll the heavy log into position.
