to anthropomorphize

verb
attribute human traits to non-humans
Pronunciation: / ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfaɪz / (AmE), / ˌænθrəˈpɔːfəʊmaɪz / (BrE)
to attribute human characteristics or behavior to an animal, object, or non-human entity

Examples

to anthropomorphize animals
Children often anthropomorphize their toys, giving them names and personalities.
It is common to anthropomorphize animals in animated movies to make them more relatable.
Some people anthropomorphize the weather, saying that the sun is 'smiling down' on them.

Related Terms

  • synonym – personify: to represent or think of as having human qualities
