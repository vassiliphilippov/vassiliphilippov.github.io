unwritten

adjective
not planned or written in advance
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɪtən / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪtən / (BrE)
(of remarks, actions, etc.) not planned or written in advance; spontaneous or improvised.

Examples

unwritten speech
His unwritten speech impressed the audience with its sincerity and spontaneity.
Much of the conversation at the meeting was unwritten and developed naturally.
The actor gave an unwritten response to the unexpected question on stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym – improvised: said or done without previous preparation
  • antonym – planned: prepared or arranged in advance
