nozzle

noun
tube or spout for discharging fluid
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːzəl / (AmE), / ˈnɒzl̩ / (BrE)
A short tube or spout through which a liquid or gas is discharged, often controlling the direction or flow.

Examples

nozzle of a hose
He cleaned the fuel nozzle to ensure proper engine performance.
She attached a new nozzle to the bottle to make watering the plants easier.
The garden hose nozzle can be adjusted to produce a fine spray or a strong jet.
