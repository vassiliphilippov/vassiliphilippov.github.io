eiderdown

noun
soft feathers of the eider duck
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪdərˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈaɪdədaʊn / (BrE)
The soft feathers or down of the eider duck, valued for use in bedding, such as quilts and pillows.

Examples

eiderdown feathers
Her grandmother cherished an old eiderdown that kept her warm through cold winters.
Many bedding companies advertise the warmth and softness of eiderdown products.
The luxurious quilt was filled with eiderdown collected from northern shores.
