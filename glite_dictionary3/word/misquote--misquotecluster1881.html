to misquote

verb
repeat someone's words incorrectly
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈkwoʊt / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈkwəʊt / (BrE)
to repeat or cite someone's words incorrectly; to quote inaccurately

Examples

to misquote a politician
He claimed his words were misquoted by the newspaper.
Please make sure you do not misquote the instructions in your explanation.
The journalist was accused of misquoting the senator during the interview.

Related Terms

  • synonym – misreport: to report information inaccurately
  • antonym – quote: to repeat someone's exact words
