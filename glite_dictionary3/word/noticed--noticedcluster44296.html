noticed

adjective
perceived or observed
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊtɪst / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtɪst / (BrE)
(of something) having been perceived or observed; having attracted attention.

Examples

noticed by others
His contributions to the project were quickly noticed by the management team.
She hoped her efforts would not go noticed by her supervisor.
The change in the company's policy was immediately noticed by the employees.
