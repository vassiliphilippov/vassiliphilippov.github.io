plush

noun
soft, thick, luxurious fabric
Pronunciation: / plʌʃ / (AmE), / plʌʃ / (BrE)
a soft, rich fabric with a thick, long pile, softer and longer than velvet

Examples

plush fabric
She wore a jacket lined with gray plush to keep her warm.
The sofa was covered in a deep red plush that felt incredibly soft.
Toys made from plush are especially popular with young children.
