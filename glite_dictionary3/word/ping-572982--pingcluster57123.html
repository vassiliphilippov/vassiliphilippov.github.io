ping

noun
network round-trip time / latency
Pronunciation: / pɪŋ / (AmE), / pɪŋ / (BrE)
the measured round-trip time for a data packet to travel from one device to another and back, usually expressed in milliseconds; used to indicate network responsiveness or latency.

Examples

low ping time
In online gaming, his ping stays under 20 ms most of the time.
Video calls drop quality when the participant's ping rises above one hundred milliseconds.
Run a network test and check the server's average ping to diagnose connectivity problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym – latency: the delay between a request and the start of a response in a system or network
  • synonym – round-trip time: the time it takes for a signal to go to a destination and return, often abbreviated RTT
