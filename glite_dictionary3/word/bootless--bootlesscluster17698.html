bootless

adjective
futile; unsuccessful; without result
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːtləs / (AmE), / ˈbuːtlɪs / (BrE)
(archaic or literary) Unproductive of success; futile; having no practical result or effect.

Examples

bootless effort
All her pleading was bootless against his firm decision.
Despite their efforts, their bootless attempts to contact him ended in silence.
The knight's quest turned out to be bootless, as he returned home empty-handed.

Related Terms

  • antonym – effective: successful in producing a desired or intended result
  • synonym – fruitless: failing to achieve the desired result
  • synonym – futile: incapable of producing any useful result; pointless
  • synonym – vain: producing no result; useless
