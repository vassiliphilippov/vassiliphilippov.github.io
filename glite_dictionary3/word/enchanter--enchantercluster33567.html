enchanter

noun
a person who uses magic or casts spells
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtʃæn.tər / (AmE), / ɪnˈtʃɑːn.tə / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, who uses magic or casts spells; a sorcerer or magician

Examples

evil enchanter
She read tales of a mysterious enchanter who transformed people into animals.
The legendary enchanter cast a spell to protect the village from harm.
The young prince sought advice from a wise enchanter hidden deep in the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym – magician: a person with magical powers
  • antonym – muggle: a person without magical powers (fantasy, especially Harry Potter)
  • synonym – sorcerer: a person who claims or is believed to have magic powers
  • synonym – witch: a woman thought to have magic powers
