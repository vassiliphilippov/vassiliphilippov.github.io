scurry

noun
hurried, confused rush or movement
Pronunciation: / ˈskɜri / (AmE), / ˈskʌri / (BrE)
a hurried and often confused movement or rush, typically made by people or small animals

Examples

a sudden scurry
The unexpected announcement caused a sudden scurry in the newsroom.
There was a scurry of activity as the team prepared for the meeting.
We heard a scurry in the bushes and saw a rabbit dart away.

Related Terms

  • synonym – rush: a sudden movement forward; hurry
  • synonym – scramble: hurried and often disorganized movement
