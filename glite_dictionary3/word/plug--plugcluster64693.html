plug

noun
object that blocks a hole or opening
Pronunciation: / plʌɡ / (AmE), / plʌɡ / (BrE)
an object designed to fit tightly and block an opening or hole, especially to stop the flow of liquid or gas

Examples

plug a hole
A cork plug was used to seal the wine bottle tightly.
After he pulled out the plug, water started draining from the bathtub.
She inserted a rubber plug into the leaking pipe to stop the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym – seal: a device used to close something tightly to prevent passage of air, liquid, etc.
  • synonym – stopper: a device used to close or seal a hole or container
