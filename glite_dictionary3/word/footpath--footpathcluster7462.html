footpath

noun
narrow path for pedestrians
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌpæθ / (AmE), / ˈfʊt.pɑːθ / (BrE)
A narrow path for people to walk on, typically away from a road.

Examples

walk along the footpath
Please keep to the footpath to avoid disturbing the local wildlife.
There is a footpath running along the edge of the meadow.
We followed the footpath through the woods until we reached the river.
