misfortunate

adjective
deserving pity due to bad luck
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈfɔɹ.tʃə.nət / (AmE), / mɪsˈfɔː.tjʊ.nət / (BrE)
deserving or arousing pity, especially due to bad luck or unfortunate circumstances; pitiable.

Examples

the misfortunate victims
Many felt compelled to help the misfortunate victims of the flood.
Stories of misfortunate characters often evoke deep empathy from readers.
The misfortunate child was left alone after the disaster struck the village.
