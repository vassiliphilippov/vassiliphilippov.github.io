to stream

verb
broadcast media online in real time
Pronunciation: / striːm / (AmE), / striːm / (BrE)
to transmit or receive (audio or video content) over the Internet in real time, without downloading the entire file beforehand

Examples

to stream a video
Many universities stream their lectures to make them accessible to students who are off campus.
She decided to stream the event for viewers around the world.
We will stream the conference so remote participants can join online.

Related Terms

  • synonym – broadcast: to transmit a program or signal by radio or television
