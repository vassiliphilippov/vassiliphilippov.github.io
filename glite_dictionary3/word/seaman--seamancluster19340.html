seaman

noun
a male sailor
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːmən / (AmE), / ˈsiːmən / (BrE)
a man who works as a sailor, especially a member of a ship's crew below the rank of officer

Examples

seaman on a ship
As a young seaman, he traveled to ports all around the world.
The experienced seaman climbed the rigging to adjust the sails during the storm.
The navy promoted him from ordinary seaman to a higher rank after two years of service.
