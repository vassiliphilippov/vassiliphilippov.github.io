hyena

noun
large carnivorous mammal of Africa/Asia
Pronunciation: / haɪˈinə / (AmE), / haɪˈiːnə / (BrE)
A large, doglike carnivorous mammal native to Africa and southern Asia, best known for its scavenging habits and distinctive vocalizations.

Examples

laughing hyena
Several hyenas gathered together under the moonlight in the African savannah.
The hyena cackled loudly as it circled the remains of the carcass.
Unlike lions, a hyena will often scavenge for its food rather than hunt.

  • synonym – jackal: a wild doglike animal native to Africa and Asia
