storm

noun
violent assault on a fortress
Pronunciation: / stɔrm / (AmE), / stɔːm / (BrE)
a direct and violent attack against a fortified place or stronghold, typically by military forces

Examples

storm on the castle
After a heavy artillery barrage, they prepared for the storm on the castle.
The army launched a storm on the enemy fortress at dawn.
The final storm broke through the city walls, ending the siege.
