polarity

noun
sentiment orientation of text
Pronunciation: / poʊˈlærəti / (AmE), / pəˈlarəti / (BrE)
(in computing and linguistics) the orientation of sentiment expressed in a text or utterance, indicating whether it is positive, negative, or neutral.

Examples

polarity of a sentence
The sentiment analysis tool flagged the post's polarity as positive.
Researchers measured the polarity of thousands of tweets during the election to track public mood.
Customer service uses comments to assess product polarity, helping prioritize issues for the support team.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sentiment: an attitude or feeling expressed in language, typically positive, negative, or neutral
  • synonym – tone: the general character or attitude of a piece of writing or speech, often related to sentiment
