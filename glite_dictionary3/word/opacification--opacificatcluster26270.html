opacification

noun
process of becoming opaque or cloudy
Pronunciation: / oʊˌpæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / əʊˌpæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of becoming or making something cloudy or opaque, especially in medical or scientific contexts

opacification of the lens
