thwarting

noun
act of hindering or preventing
Pronunciation: / ˈθwɔɹɾɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθwɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of preventing someone from carrying out their plans or efforts

Examples

Despite many thwarting of her plans, she remained determined to succeed.
The security guard's quick action led to the thwarting of the attempted robbery.
Their persistent efforts faced constant thwarting from unexpected obstacles.
