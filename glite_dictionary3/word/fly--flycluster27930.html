to fly

verb
hit a high ball in baseball
Pronunciation: / flaɪ / (AmE), / flaɪ / (BrE)
(in baseball) to hit a ball high into the air but not far, typically so that it can be caught before it hits the ground.

Examples

to fly a ball
He flied the ball deep to left, but the outfielder made the catch.
She flies to right field almost every time she's up to bat.
The batter managed to fly out to center field in the seventh inning.
