arteria perinealis

noun
branch of the internal pudendal artery in the perineum
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪəriə ˌpɛrəˈniːəlɪs / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə ˌpɛrəˈniːɑːlɪs / (BrE)
A branch of the internal pudendal artery that supplies blood to the superficial structures of the perineum.

Examples

arteria perinealis injury
An injury to the arteria perinealis can result in significant bleeding in the area.
During the surgery, care was taken not to damage the arteria perinealis.
The arteria perinealis plays a crucial role in supplying blood to the perineal region.
