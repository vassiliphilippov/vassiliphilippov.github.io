thunderclap

noun
sudden shocking event or news
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌndɚˌklæp / (AmE), / ˈθʌndəˌklæp / (BrE)
a sudden and startling event or announcement; a shocking surprise

Examples

a sudden thunderclap event

His sudden confession was a thunderclap that shocked everyone present.
The resignation of the CEO came as a thunderclap to the entire industry.
