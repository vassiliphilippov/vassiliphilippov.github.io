to force

verb
seize or take by force
Pronunciation: / fɔrs / (AmE), / fɔːs / (BrE)
to take something by force, especially in a military, violent, or confrontational manner

to force a city

The army planned to force the city at dawn despite heavy resistance.
The rebels forced the gates open and took control of the palace.
