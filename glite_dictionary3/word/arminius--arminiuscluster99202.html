Arminius

proper noun
German hero, leader vs Romans in AD 9
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈmɪniəs / (AmE), / ɑːˈmɪniəs / (BrE)
A German chieftain who led an alliance of Germanic tribes to victory against the Romans at the Battle of the Teutoburg Forest in AD 9.
