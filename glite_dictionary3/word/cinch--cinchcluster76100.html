to cinch

verb
secure a saddle on a horse
Pronunciation: / sɪntʃ / (AmE), / sɪntʃ / (BrE)
to fasten a saddle on a horse by tightening the cinch (a strap that goes around its belly)

Examples

to cinch a saddle
Before the long ride, the groom cinched each of the horses securely.
He carefully cinched the saddle before mounting the horse.
Make sure to cinch the strap tightly so the saddle doesn't slip.

Related Terms

  • synonym – girth: to secure with a girth (a broad strap around a horse)
