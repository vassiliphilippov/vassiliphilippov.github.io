muzzle

noun
restraint over animal's snout
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌzəl / (AmE), / ˈmʌzəl / (BrE)
a device placed over the mouth of an animal or person to prevent biting, eating, or speaking

Examples

muzzle for a dog
Before grooming, the owner placed a muzzle over her pet's snout for safety.
Some dog breeds are required by law to wear a muzzle in public places.
The veterinarian fitted a muzzle on the dog before the examination.
During his captivity, the prisoner was forced to wear a muzzle to prevent him from calling for help.
Protestors argued that using a muzzle on detainees was an inhumane form of restraint.
The notorious guard placed a muzzle in the mouths of those who dared to shout.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gag: a device placed in or over the mouth to stop a person from speaking
  • synonym – restraint: a device or means of limiting movement or action
