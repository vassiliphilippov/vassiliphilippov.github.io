humanist

noun
scholar of humanities or liberal arts
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuːmənɪst / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmənɪst / (BrE)
A scholar of the humanities, especially of classical languages, literature, and culture; a student of the liberal arts.

Examples

humanist scholar
As a humanist, she translated several Latin texts into English.
Many humanists contributed to the revival of classical learning in Europe.
The Renaissance humanist dedicated his life to studying ancient Greek philosophy.
