typhoon

noun
a tropical cyclone in the Pacific/Indian oceans
Pronunciation: / taɪˈfuːn / (AmE), / taɪˈfuːn / (BrE)
A tropical cyclone with strong winds and heavy rain that forms in the western Pacific or Indian oceans.

Each year, several typhoons strike Southeast Asia, bringing torrential rain and gale-force winds.
