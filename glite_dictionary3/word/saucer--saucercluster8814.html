saucer

noun
parabolic antenna for satellite or radio signals
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔsɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsə / (BrE)
a directional antenna consisting of a parabolic reflector designed to focus microwave or radio frequency signals, often used for satellite communication

Examples

satellite saucer antenna
Many homes have a saucer in their backyard for satellite TV.
Technicians adjusted the angle of the saucer to improve reception.
The television signal was received by a large saucer on the roof.
