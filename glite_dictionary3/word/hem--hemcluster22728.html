hem

noun
throat-clearing sound to signal hesitation or attract attention
Pronunciation: / hɛm / (AmE), / hɛm / (BrE)
a sound like clearing the throat, used to attract attention, show hesitation, fill a pause, or express mild embarrassment

Examples

a quiet hem sound
He filled the silence with a quiet hem, unsure how to respond to the question.
She gave a discreet hem before beginning her speech, signaling she was nervous.
There was an awkward hem from the back of the room as he tried to get the teacher's attention.
