hosiery

noun
stockings, socks, and similar legwear
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊʒəri / (AmE), / ˈhəʊzəri / (BrE)
Stockings, socks, tights, and similar knitted or woven legwear, collectively; in British usage, may also include underwear.

Examples

hosiery store

The department store has a wide selection of hosiery in various colors and styles.
