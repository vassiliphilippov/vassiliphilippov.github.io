teddy

noun
sleeveless one-piece women's undergarment
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛdi / (AmE), / ˈtɛdi / (BrE)
A woman's sleeveless, one-piece undergarment that combines a camisole and panties, usually made of light fabric and often worn as lingerie or sleepwear.

Examples

lace teddy
Her red lace teddy was both elegant and comfortable.
She bought a silk teddy to wear on her wedding night.
The store has a variety of teddies in different colors and styles.

Related Terms

  • synonym – camisole: a woman's sleeveless undergarment for the upper body
  • synonym – chemise: a woman's loose-fitting undergarment or dress
  • antonym – corset: a tightly fitting undergarment extending from below the chest to the hips
