skate

noun
sports shoe with wheels or blade for gliding
Pronunciation: / skeɪt / (AmE), / skeɪt / (BrE)
A boot, shoe, or frame with a set of wheels or a blade attached, worn on the foot to glide smoothly over ice or a hard surface for sport or recreation.

Examples

skate with wheels
Don't forget to bring your skates to the rink tomorrow.
He strapped on his skates and headed onto the ice.
Her new roller skates made her the fastest on the block.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ice skate: shoe with a metal blade for skating on ice
  • synonym – roller skate: shoe with small wheels for skating on hard surfaces
