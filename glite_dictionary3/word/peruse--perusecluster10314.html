to peruse

verb
read or examine carefully
Pronunciation: / pəˈɹuz / (AmE), / pəˈɹuːz / (BrE)
to examine or read something carefully and in detail

Examples

to peruse a document
Each applicant should peruse the guidelines thoroughly before submitting their documents.
He likes to peruse old newspapers whenever he visits the library.
She took a few moments to peruse the menu before placing her order.

Related Terms

  • antonym – scan: to look at quickly, often for a particular purpose
