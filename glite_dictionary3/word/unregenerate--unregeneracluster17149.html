unregenerate

adjective
not morally or spiritually reformed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈdʒɛn.ɚ.ɪt /; / ˌʌnrɪˈdʒɛnərət /; / ˌʌn.rɪˈdʒɛn.ə.rət / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈdʒɛn.ər.ət /; / ˌʌnrɪˈdʒɛnərət / (BrE)
stubbornly refusing to change beliefs, behavior, or character; not feeling sorry or willing to improve
