to surmount

verb
to be placed on top of something
Pronunciation: / sərˈmaʊnt / (AmE), / səˈmaʊnt / (BrE)
to be set on top of something; to be placed above as a covering or ornament

Examples

surmounts the roof
A golden eagle statue surmounts the pedestal in the city square.
A tall spire surmounts the church tower, creating a striking silhouette against the sky.
The family crest surmounted the ornate gate at the entrance to the estate.

Related Terms

  • synonym – top: to cover or be placed on the highest part of something
