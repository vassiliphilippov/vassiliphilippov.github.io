provision

noun
a stated condition in a contract
Pronunciation: / prəˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / prəˈvɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
a condition or requirement that is stated in a legal document or agreement

Examples

contract provision
According to the new provision, all employees must complete safety training annually.
The contract contains a provision that allows either party to terminate with notice.
They added a provision to the agreement to cover unexpected expenses.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clause: a distinct provision or section in a legal document
  • synonym – stipulation: a specific demand or condition in a contract or agreement
