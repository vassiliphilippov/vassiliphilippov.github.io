dictated

adjective
imposed by authority
Pronunciation: / dɪkˈteɪtɪd / (AmE), / dɪkˈteɪtɪd / (BrE)
determined or imposed by someone in authority, rather than by choice or chance

Examples

He resented the dictated schedule, preferring more flexibility in his work.
The company had to accept the dictated terms of the contract.
