gas-tight

adjective
impermeable to gas; airtight
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæs.taɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡæs.taɪt / (BrE)
not allowing air or gas to pass in or out; completely sealed against the passage of gas

Examples

gas-tight container
Make sure the door is gas-tight before starting the experiment.
The engineers designed a gas-tight seal for the new pipeline.
The lab stored chemicals in gas-tight containers to prevent leaks.
