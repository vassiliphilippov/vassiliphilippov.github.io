sgraffito

noun
decoration made by scratching to reveal a layer
Pronunciation: / sgrəˈfiːtoʊ / (AmE), / sgrəˈfiːtəʊ / (BrE)
A technique of decorating ceramics or walls by scratching through a surface layer to reveal a different color beneath.
