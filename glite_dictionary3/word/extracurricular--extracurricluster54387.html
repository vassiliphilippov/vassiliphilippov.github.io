extracurricular

adjective
outside regular duties or curriculum
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəkəˈrɪkjələr / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəkəˈrɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
not part of the main or required activities of a school, job, or course; outside the usual curriculum or responsibilities
