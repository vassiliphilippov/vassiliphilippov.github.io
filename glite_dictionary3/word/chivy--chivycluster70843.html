to chivy

verb
to annoy or pester repeatedly
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪvi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪvi / (BrE)
to annoy or pester someone continually; to harass or nag repeatedly

Examples

to chivy a coworker
He felt chivied by constant demands from his supervisor.
If you chivy her too much, she'll just quit the project.
She wouldn't stop chivying him about his messy room.

Related Terms

  • synonym – harass: to disturb or trouble someone persistently
  • synonym – nag: to annoy or criticize someone repeatedly
  • synonym – pester: to annoy someone with repeated requests or interruptions
