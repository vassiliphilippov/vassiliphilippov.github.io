Marian Anderson

proper noun
American contralto and civil rights figure
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛriən ˈændɚsən / (AmE), / ˈmɛːrɪən ˈandəsən / (BrE)
(1902–1993) an American contralto and renowned singer known for her performances of spirituals and classical music, and a prominent figure in the civil rights movement.
