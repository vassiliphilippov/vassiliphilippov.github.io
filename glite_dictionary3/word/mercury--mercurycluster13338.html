Mercury

proper noun
innermost planet in the Solar System
Pronunciation: / ˈmɜrkjəri / (AmE), / ˈmɜːkjʊri / (BrE)
the smallest planet in the Solar System and the one closest to the Sun

Examples

Mercury orbits Sun
Mercury completes an orbit around the Sun in just 88 Earth days.
Because Mercury has almost no atmosphere, its surface temperatures vary greatly between day and night.
Through a telescope, Mercury appears as a small, bright point close to the horizon at sunrise or sunset.
