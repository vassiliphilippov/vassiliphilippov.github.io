Vladimir Ilich Ulyanov

proper noun
Russian revolutionary and first head of the USSR
Pronunciation: / vləˈdɪmɪr ˈiːlɪtʃ juːlˈjænɒf / (AmE), / ˈvlædɪmɪə ˈiːlɪtʃ uːlˈjænɒf / (BrE)
(1870–1924) The Russian revolutionary known as Lenin, founder of the Bolshevik Party, leader of the Russian Revolution, and the first head of the USSR.
