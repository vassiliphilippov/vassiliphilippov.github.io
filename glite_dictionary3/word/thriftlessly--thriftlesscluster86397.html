thriftlessly

adverb
in a wasteful or unthrifty manner
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɪftlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈθrɪftlɪsli / (BrE)
In a manner that lacks thrift or careful management of resources; wastefully or without regard for saving.

Examples

spend thriftlessly on clothes
He lived thriftlessly, never saving a penny for the future.
In her youth, she spent her inheritance thriftlessly on fleeting pleasures.
They managed their finances thriftlessly, quickly falling into debt.

Related Terms

  • antonym – thriftily: in a manner showing thrift or economy
  • synonym – wastefully: in a way that uses resources carelessly
