to mince

verb
walk with short, dainty steps
Pronunciation: / mɪns / (AmE), / mɪns / (BrE)
to walk with short, dainty steps, often in an affected or delicate manner

Examples

to mince across stage
He minced through the puddles, trying not to get his shoes wet.
She minced down the street in her new heels, careful not to scuff them.
The actor minced across the stage to exaggerate the character's nervousness.
