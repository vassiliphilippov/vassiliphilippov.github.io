eryngo

noun
spiny-leaved plant of the genus Eryngium
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrɪŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ɪˈrɪŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
Any plant of the genus Eryngium, typically having spiny leaves and often blue or purple flower heads.
