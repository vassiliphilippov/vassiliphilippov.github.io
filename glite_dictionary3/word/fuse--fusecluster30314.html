to fuse

verb
equip with a fuse
Pronunciation: / fjuːz / (AmE), / fjuːz / (BrE)
to equip or provide (something) with a fuse, especially for safety or detonation purposes

Examples

to fuse a bomb
Before transporting the fireworks, they carefully fused each shell.
Modern electrical appliances come fused to prevent damage from surges.
The technicians will fuse the new devices for added safety.
