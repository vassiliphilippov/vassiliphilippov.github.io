competitiveness

noun
desire or drive to compete
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɛt̬.ə.tɪv.nəs / (AmE), / kəmˈpɛt.ɪ.tɪv.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having a strong desire to compete and succeed against others

Examples

strong competitiveness in business
A healthy sense of competitiveness can motivate employees to perform their best.
Her competitiveness was evident during every match she played.
Too much competitiveness sometimes leads to unnecessary conflict in a team.
