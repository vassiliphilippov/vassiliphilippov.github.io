elision

noun
deliberate omission
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɪʒən / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪʒən / (BrE)
the deliberate omission of something, especially in speech, writing, or a process

Examples

The report's elision of key facts sparked public debate.
