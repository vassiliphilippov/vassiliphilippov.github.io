sprinter

noun
short-distance runner
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪntər / (AmE), / ˈsprɪntə / (BrE)
an athlete who runs short distances at very high speed, especially in races of 100, 200, or 400 meters

Examples

Olympic sprinter
After years as a competitive sprinter, she decided to coach young athletes.
The sprinter exploded off the starting blocks with incredible speed.
Usain Bolt is the world's fastest sprinter over 100 meters.
