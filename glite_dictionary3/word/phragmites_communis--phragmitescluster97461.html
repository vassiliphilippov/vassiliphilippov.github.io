Phragmites communis

noun
tall reed plant with feathery plumes
Pronunciation: / fræɡˈmaɪtiːz kəˈmjunɪs / (AmE), / fræɡˈmaɪtiːz kəˈmjuːnɪs / (BrE)
A tall perennial reed with broad leaves and large, feathery flower clusters, found widely in moist areas of North America and used for making mats, screens, and arrow shafts.
