to glass

verb
scan with binoculars
Pronunciation: / ɡlæs / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
to scan an area with binoculars or a similar optical instrument, especially in outdoor settings such as hunting or surveillance

Examples

to glass the hills
After lunch, they glassed the edge of the forest, hoping to spot elk.
She spent the morning glassing the distant hillside for movement.
The hunter climbed the ridge to glass the valley for signs of deer.

Related Terms

  • synonym – scan: to look over something carefully or systematically
