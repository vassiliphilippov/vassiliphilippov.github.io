carrageen

noun
edible Atlantic seaweed
Pronunciation: / ˈkærəˌɡin / (AmE), / ˈkærəˌɡiːn / (BrE)
a dark purple edible seaweed found on the Atlantic coasts of Europe and North America, commonly used as a thickener in foods
