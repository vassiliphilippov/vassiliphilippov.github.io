bluebottle

noun
a flowering plant with bright blue (or other colored) heads
Pronunciation: / ˈbluˌbɑt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌbɒtl̩ / (BrE)
an annual Eurasian plant (Centaurea cyanus) with showy heads of blue, purple, pink, or white flowers, often cultivated in gardens

Examples

wild bluebottle flower
She picked a delicate bluebottle and tucked it behind her ear.
The bluebottle added a splash of vivid color to her summer garden.
Wild bluebottles were seen blooming along the edges of the wheat fields.
