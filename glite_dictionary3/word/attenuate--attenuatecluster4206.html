to attenuate

verb
become weaker or less intense
Pronunciation: / əˈtɛnjuˌeɪt / (AmE), / əˈtɛnjʊeɪt / (BrE)
to become weaker or to make something weaker in strength, value, intensity, or thickness

Examples

to attenuate a signal

The acid was attenuated to reduce its corrosive properties.
