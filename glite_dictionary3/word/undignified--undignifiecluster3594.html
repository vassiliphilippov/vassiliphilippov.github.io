undignified

adjective
lacking dignity or respectfulness
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdɪɡnɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdɪɡnɪfaɪd / (BrE)
lacking dignity; appearing or behaving in a way that causes loss of respect or esteem

Examples

an undignified act
Crawling on the floor in front of everyone felt undignified.
He found it undignified to argue loudly in public.
The politician's undignified behavior shocked many of his supporters.
