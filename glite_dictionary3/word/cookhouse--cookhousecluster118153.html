cookhouse

noun
kitchen area on a ship or camp
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊkˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈkʊk.haʊs /; / ˈkʊkˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a building or area, often separate from the main structure, where food is prepared and cooked for a group of people, especially in camps, ships, or worksites
