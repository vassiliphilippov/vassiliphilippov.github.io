unwearied

adjective
not tired; energetic
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɪr.id / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɪə.rid / (BrE)
not tired or fatigued; showing no signs of weariness or loss of energy.

Examples

unwearied effort
Despite the long hours, her unwearied enthusiasm inspired the whole team.
He worked with unwearied patience to solve the complex problem.
The unwearied efforts of the volunteers made the event a great success.
