biocatalytic

adjective
relating to biocatalysts or their action
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˌkæt.əˈlɪt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊˌkæt.əˈlɪt.ɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or involving biocatalysts, especially the use of natural catalysts, such as enzymes, to accelerate chemical reactions.
