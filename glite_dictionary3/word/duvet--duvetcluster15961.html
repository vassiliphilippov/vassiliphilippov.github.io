duvet

noun
soft quilted bed covering
Pronunciation: / duːˈveɪ / (AmE), / ˈdjuː.veɪ / (BrE)
A soft, often quilted blanket filled with down, feathers, or a synthetic alternative, used as a covering on a bed.

Examples

duvet cover set
After washing the duvet, it felt fresh and fluffy again.
She pulled the duvet up over her head to keep warm during the cold night.
We bought a new duvet for the guest bedroom before our friends arrived.

Related Terms
