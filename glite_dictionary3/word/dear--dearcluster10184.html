dear

adverb
at great cost or sacrifice (adv)
Pronunciation: / dɪr / (AmE), / dɪə / (BrE)
(adverb, chiefly literary) At a great cost or sacrifice; with severe loss, suffering, or penalty.

Examples

paid dear price
He learned the importance of honesty and paid dear for his mistake.
His pride cost him dear in the end, leaving him alone and regretful.
The rebels fought for their freedom, but their victory came dear.
