toll

noun
fee for using a road or bridge
Pronunciation: / toʊl / (AmE), / təʊl / (BrE)
a charge paid to use a particular road, bridge, or other public facility

Examples

toll road fee
Many drivers avoid the route because the toll is considered too expensive.
The highway charges a toll for each vehicle that passes through.
We stopped to pay the toll at the bridge before continuing our drive.
