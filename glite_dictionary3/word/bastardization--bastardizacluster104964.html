bastardization

noun
act of debasing or corrupting
Pronunciation: / ˌbæstərdɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌbɑːstədʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of lowering the quality, value, or purity of something by introducing inferior elements or by corrupting its original form

Examples

bastardization of culture
He accused the developers of the bastardization of the historic neighborhood.
Many linguists lament the bastardization of certain words due to modern slang.
The bastardization of the classic recipe led to criticism from traditionalists.

Related Terms

  • synonym – corruption: the act or process of making something morally depraved or debased
  • antonym – purification: the process of making something pure
