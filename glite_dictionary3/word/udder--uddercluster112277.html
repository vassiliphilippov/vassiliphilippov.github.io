udder

noun
mammary gland of cows, sheep, etc.
Pronunciation: / ˈʌdɚ / (AmE), / ˈʌdə / (BrE)
The large, bag-shaped mammary gland of female cows, sheep, goats, and other bovids, which produces and stores milk.

Examples

cow's udder
Goats have a smaller udder compared to cows.
She explained how an udder can become infected if not properly cared for.
The farmer carefully cleaned the cow's udder before milking.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mammary gland: the gland in mammals that produces milk
  • synonym – teat: the projection from an udder or breast through which milk is drawn
