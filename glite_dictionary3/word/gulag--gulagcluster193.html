gulag

noun
Russian prison camp system in the Soviet era
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuˌlɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡuːlæɡ / (BrE)
A system of Russian prison camps for political prisoners, especially as operated in the former Soviet Union.

Examples

Soviet gulag camps
Books and films have described the harsh conditions of the Soviet gulag system.
Her grandfather spent ten years in a Siberian gulag during the 1940s.
Many dissidents were sent to the gulag for criticizing the government.
