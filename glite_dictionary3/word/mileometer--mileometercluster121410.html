mileometer

noun
instrument recording miles traveled
Pronunciation: / maɪˈlɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / maɪˈlɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument in a vehicle that records the number of miles traveled

Examples

mileometer reading miles
He checked the mileometer before setting off on his journey.
The car’s mileometer showed that we had traveled over 30,000 miles since we bought it.
The mechanic recorded the reading from the mileometer during the annual service.

Related Terms

  • synonym – odometer: an instrument that measures the distance traveled by a vehicle
