hypodermic

adjective
under the skin; relating to injections
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpəˈdɜrmɪk / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəˈdɜːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to the region immediately beneath the skin; or intended for injection beneath the skin.

Examples

hypodermic injection
A hypodermic injection delivers medication directly beneath the skin.
He carried a hypodermic syringe in his medical kit for emergencies.
The nurse used a hypodermic needle to administer the vaccine.
