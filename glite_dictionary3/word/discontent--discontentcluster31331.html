discontent

noun
dissatisfaction; longing for improvement
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskənˈtɛnt / (AmE), / ˌdɪskənˈtɛnt / (BrE)
a feeling of dissatisfaction with the present situation; a longing for something better

Examples

public discontent
Political discontent can lead to widespread protests and calls for change.
She voiced her discontent with the service at the restaurant.
There was growing discontent among the workers over the new company policies.

Related Terms

  • antonym – contentment: a state of happiness and satisfaction
  • synonym – dissatisfaction: lack of satisfaction with something
