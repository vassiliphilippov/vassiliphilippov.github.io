Sezession

noun
Austrian art movement like Art Nouveau
Pronunciation: / zəˈzɛs.i.ˌɔn / (AmE), / zɛˈzɛs.i.ˌɔn / (BrE)
an Austrian movement in art and architecture around the 1890s, parallel to Art Nouveau, emphasizing decorative and modern design
