absentee

noun
someone who is absent
Pronunciation: / ˌæb.sənˈti / (AmE), / ˌæb.sənˈtiː / (BrE)
a person who is not present at a place where they are expected or required to be

Examples

absentee worker
Repeated absentee workers may face disciplinary action under the new policy.
