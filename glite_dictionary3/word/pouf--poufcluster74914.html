pouf

noun
thick cushion used as a seat or footstool
Pronunciation: / puːf / (AmE), / puːf / (BrE)
A thick, low cushion that is used as a seat or footstool.

Examples

sit on a pouf

She purchased a colorful pouf for the corner of her living room.
The children sat comfortably on the large pouf while playing games.
