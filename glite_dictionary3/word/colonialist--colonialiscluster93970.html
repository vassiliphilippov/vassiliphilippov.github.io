colonialist

noun
supporter or participant in colonialism
Pronunciation: / kəˈloʊniəlɪst / (AmE), / kəˈləʊniəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports, advocates, or participates in colonialism, especially in establishing colonies or controlling other countries

Examples

British colonialist leader
He was criticized as a colonialist for advocating overseas expansion.
Many countries fought long struggles to rid themselves of colonialist rule.
The policies of the colonialist powers reshaped many regions of the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym – anti-colonialist: a person who opposes colonialism
  • synonym – imperialist: a person who supports or practices imperialism
