asymptomatic

adjective
showing no symptoms of illness
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪsɪmpˌtæmˈætɪk / (AmE), / ˌeɪsɪmptəˈmatɪk / (BrE)
Not showing any symptoms of disease or illness.

Examples

asymptomatic COVID patient
Although she tested positive, she was completely asymptomatic throughout her quarantine period.
Many people with the virus remain asymptomatic and never realize they were infected.
Routine screening can help identify asymptomatic carriers of infectious diseases.
