shrinkage

noun
loss of goods from a store due to theft
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrɪŋkɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈʃrɪŋkɪdʒ / (BrE)
the loss of goods from a store, especially as a result of theft by customers or employees

Examples

retail shrinkage
Employee theft is one of the major contributors to retail shrinkage.
Several stores reported an increase in shrinkage during the busy holiday season.
The manager implemented new security measures to reduce shrinkage and protect inventory.

Related Terms

  • synonym – shoplifting: the act of stealing goods from a store while pretending to shop
  • synonym – theft: the act of stealing something
