parous

adjective
having given birth to offspring
Pronunciation: / ˈperəs / (AmE), / ˈpɑːrəs / (BrE)
(of a woman or female animal) having given birth to one or more viable offspring.

Examples

parous female animal
After her first child, she was considered parous by her obstetrician.
Certain risk factors increase for parous women compared to those who have never given birth.
The study focused on both nulliparous and parous women to measure health outcomes.

Related Terms

  • antonym – nulliparous: (of a woman or female animal) having never given birth
