dillenia

noun
a genus of evergreen trees or shrubs with edible fruit
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɛniə / (AmE), / dɪˈliːniə / (BrE)
Any of several evergreen trees or shrubs of the genus Dillenia, grown for their attractive foliage and nodding magnolia-like flowers, often followed by edible fruits used in curries, jellies, and preserves.
