lighterage

noun
fee for shipping goods in lighters
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtərɪdʒ / (BrE)
the process or cost of transporting goods by lighter (a flat-bottomed barge)
