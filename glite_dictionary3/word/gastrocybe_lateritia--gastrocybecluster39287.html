Gastrocybe lateritia

noun
a species of conic-capped fungus
Pronunciation: / ˌɡæstroʊˈsaɪbi ˌlætəˈrɪʃə / (AmE), / ˌɡæstrəʊˈsɪbi ˌlætəˈrɪtia / (BrE)
A species of fungus in the genus Gastrocybe, characterized by a conic cap, thin upright-to-drooping stalk, and a gelatinous cap that disperses spores as the stalk collapses.
