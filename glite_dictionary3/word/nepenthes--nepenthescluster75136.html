Nepenthes

noun
genus of tropical pitcher plants
Pronunciation: / nɪˈpɛnθiːz / (AmE), / nɪˈpɛnθiːz / (BrE)
A genus of tropical pitcher plants known for their distinctive pitfall traps used to capture and digest insects.
