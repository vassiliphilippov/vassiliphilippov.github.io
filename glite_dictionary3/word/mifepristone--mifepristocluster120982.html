mifepristone

noun
abortion-inducing medication
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪfəˈprɪstoʊn / (AmE), / ˌmɪfəˈprɪstəʊn / (BrE)
A synthetic steroid drug used to induce abortion, especially in the early stages of pregnancy, by blocking the action of progesterone.

mifepristone abortion pill
Mifepristone is most effective when taken within the first weeks of pregnancy.
