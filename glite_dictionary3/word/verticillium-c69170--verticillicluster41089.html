verticillium

noun
genus of plant-pathogenic fungi
Pronunciation: / vərˌtɪsɪˈliəm / (AmE), / vɜːˌtɪsɪˈliːəm / (BrE)
A genus of fungi, many species of which are plant pathogens causing diseases such as wilts in a variety of crops.

Examples

verticillium wilt disease
Farmers are concerned about verticillium wilt affecting their tomato crops.
Researchers identified verticillium as the cause of the yellowing leaves in the potato fields.
The presence of verticillium in the soil can severely impact the yield of susceptible plants.
