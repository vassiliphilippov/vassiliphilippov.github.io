Sphenisciformes

noun
the order of birds including penguins
Pronunciation: / sfɛˌnɪsɪˈfɔrmiːz / (AmE), / sfɛˌnɪsɪˈfɔːmiːz / (BrE)
an order of flightless aquatic birds comprising the penguins, found mainly in the Southern Hemisphere

Examples

Sphenisciformes live in Antarctica
