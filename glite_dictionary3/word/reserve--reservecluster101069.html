reserve

noun
area set aside for a specific purpose
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɝv / (AmE), / rɪˈzɜːv / (BrE)
an area of land set aside for a specific purpose, such as wildlife protection, resource management, or habitation by a particular group

Examples

wildlife reserve
Hunters are not allowed to enter the reserve without special permission.
Many indigenous communities live on a reserve specially designated for them.
The government established a reserve to protect endangered species in the region.

  • synonym – preserve: area where wildlife or natural resources are protected
