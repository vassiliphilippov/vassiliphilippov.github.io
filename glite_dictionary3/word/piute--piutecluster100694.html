Piute

noun
member of a Native American people of the southwestern US
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪ.uːt / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ.uːt / (BrE)
A member of either of two related Shoshonean peoples (the Northern Paiute and Southern Paiute) living in the southwestern United States.

Examples

a Piute tribe member
Anthropologists have studied the migration patterns of the Piute for many years.

The Piute have a rich tradition of storytelling and crafts.
