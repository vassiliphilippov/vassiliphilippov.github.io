to fork

verb
divide into branches
Pronunciation: / fɔrk / (AmE), / fɔːk / (BrE)
to divide or split into two or more branches or parts, or to shape something in a way that resembles a fork

Examples

to fork into two roads
At the crossroads, the main road forks to the left and right.
The hiking trail forks just before the summit, so pay attention to the signs.
The river suddenly forks into two smaller streams near the old bridge.
As lightning struck the tree, the branch forked into two jagged parts.
She forked her fingers to form the peace sign.
The road forks just ahead, so choose your path carefully.

Related Terms

  • synonym – branch: (of a road, path, etc.) to divide into a branch or branches
