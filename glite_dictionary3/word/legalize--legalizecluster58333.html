to legalize

verb
make legal by law or authority
Pronunciation: / ˈliːɡəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəlaɪz / (BrE)
To make something legal by official or legislative action.

Examples

to legalize same-sex marriage
Many countries have chosen to legalize same-sex marriage in recent years.
The government decided to legalize the use of electric scooters in city centers.
There are ongoing debates about whether to legalize certain recreational drugs.
