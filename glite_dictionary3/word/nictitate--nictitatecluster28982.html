to nictitate

verb
to blink or close the eyes briefly
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪktɪˌteɪt / (AmE), / ˈnɪktɪteɪt / (BrE)
to blink or briefly close the eyes, especially as a natural or automatic movement

Examples

to nictitate quickly
As the harsh light flickered on, she instinctively nictitated to protect her eyes.
He tried not to nictitate during the staring contest.
Some birds and reptiles can nictitate using a special transparent eyelid.
