jacquard

noun
intricately patterned fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈʒækɑrd / (AmE), / ˈʒækɑːd / (BrE)
A fabric with an intricate, often raised pattern, woven on a Jacquard loom.

Examples

jacquard fabric pattern
He admired the intricate designs woven into the jacquard upholstery.
The dress was made from a luxurious jacquard that shimmered in the light.
They imported several bolts of jacquard for the new furniture line.
