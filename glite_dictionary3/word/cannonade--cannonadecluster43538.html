to cannonade

verb
attack with heavy artillery
Pronunciation: / ˌkænəˈneɪd / (AmE), / ˌkanəˈneɪd / (BrE)
to attack or bombard with heavy artillery or cannons over a period of time

Examples

to cannonade a city
Enemy forces cannonaded the city for days, causing widespread destruction.
The rebels attempted to cannonade the fortress throughout the night.
They began cannonading the hilltop at dawn to weaken the enemy defenses.

  • synonym – bombard: to attack with bombs or artillery
