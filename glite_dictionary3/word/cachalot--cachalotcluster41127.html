cachalot

noun
large whale; sperm whale
Pronunciation: / ˈkæ.tʃəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈkaʃ.əˌlɒt / (BrE)
a large whale, also known as the sperm whale, with a distinctive large cavity in its head containing spermaceti and oil, and a source of ambergris

Examples

giant cachalot whale
A gigantic cachalot surfaced near the research vessel, thrilling everyone on board.
Ambergris, a valuable substance, is produced in the digestive system of the cachalot.
The cachalot is renowned for its deep dives in search of squid.
