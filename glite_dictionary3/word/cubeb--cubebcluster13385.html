cubeb

noun
tropical vine with spicy fruits
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuː.bɛb / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bɛb / (BrE)
A tropical Southeast Asian shrubby vine (Piper cubeba) of the pepper family, cultivated for its spicy, berrylike fruits used as a spice and in medicine.
