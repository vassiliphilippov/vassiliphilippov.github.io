hearing

noun
official opportunity to present one's case
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪrɪŋ /; / ˈhɪr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɪərɪŋ /; / ˈhɪə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
an official meeting, often in a court or before a committee, where evidence or viewpoints are presented and considered

Examples

hearing in court

She gave her testimony during the hearing at the courthouse.
