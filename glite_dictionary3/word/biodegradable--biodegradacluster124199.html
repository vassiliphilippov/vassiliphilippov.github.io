biodegradable

adjective
can be naturally decomposed by living organisms
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊ.dɪˈɡreɪ.də.bəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊ.dɪˈɡreɪ.də.bəl / (BrE)
able to be broken down naturally by bacteria or other living organisms, especially in a way that is not harmful to the environment

Examples

biodegradable packaging material
Many companies are switching to biodegradable products for sustainability reasons.
That cleaning solution is certified as biodegradable and safe for marine life.
The packaging is made from biodegradable materials to reduce environmental impact.

Related Terms

  • synonym – compostable: able to break down into compost under suitable conditions
  • antonym – non-biodegradable: not able to be decomposed naturally
