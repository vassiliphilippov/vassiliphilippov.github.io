deliverer

noun
person who transfers money or goods
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪvɚɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvərə / (BrE)
a person who gives up or transfers money, goods, or property to another

Examples

deliverer of money
As the deliverer of the funds, she made sure every payment was documented.
Each deliverer must receive a receipt after handing over the goods.
The deliverer handed the package to the receptionist at the front desk.

Related Terms

  • synonym – donor: a person who donates or gives something to another
  • antonym – recipient: a person who receives money, goods, or property from another
