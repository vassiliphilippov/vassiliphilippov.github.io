sandspur

noun
prickly grass with spiny burs
Pronunciation: / ˈsændˌspɝ / (AmE), / ˈsændˌspɜː / (BrE)
a grass native to the eastern United States and tropical America, known for its spikelets surrounded by prickly burs that can attach to skin or clothing
