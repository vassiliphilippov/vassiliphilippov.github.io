cosplayer

noun
person who dresses as fictional character
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊsˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈkɒsˌpleɪə / (BrE)
A person who dresses up as a character from a comic book, movie, video game, or similar media, especially as a hobby or at conventions.

Examples

dedicated cosplayer
Being a dedicated cosplayer means spending weeks perfecting your outfit and makeup.
She is an award-winning cosplayer known for her detailed superhero costumes.
Thousands of cosplayers gathered at the convention to show off their elaborate costumes.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cosplay: the activity of dressing up as a character from a fictional work
