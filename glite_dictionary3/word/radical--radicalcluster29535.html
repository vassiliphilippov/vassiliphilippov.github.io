radical

noun
component conveying meaning in written characters
Pronunciation: / ˈrædɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈrædɪkəl / (BrE)
a graphical component of a logogram, especially in Chinese or Japanese writing, that conveys the basic lexical meaning and is used to classify characters

Examples

Chinese radical
Most Chinese dictionaries classify characters according to their radical.
Students learn to identify the radical in each kanji to understand its basic meaning.
The radical for 'water' appears in many characters related to liquids.
