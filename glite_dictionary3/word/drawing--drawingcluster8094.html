drawing

noun
the act of pulling a load
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɔɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of moving a load by pulling it, typically with physical force or a vehicle

Examples

drawing of carts
Early railways depended on the drawing of carriages by steam engines.
The drawing of heavy carts required strong horses in the past.
The drawing of logs from the forest was done by oxen.
