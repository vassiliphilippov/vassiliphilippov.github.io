form

noun
current ability or performance level
Pronunciation: / fɔrm / (AmE), / fɔːm / (BrE)
a person's or team's current ability to perform well, especially in sports or competitions

Examples

in good form
After his injury, he struggled to regain his best form.
Critics say the tennis star has lost his form since the tournament.
The singer was in top form during last night's performance.
