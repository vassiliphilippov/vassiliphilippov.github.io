extrovert

adjective
outgoing, socially confident person or trait
Pronunciation: / ˈɛk.strə.vɝt / (AmE), / ˈɛk.strə.vɜːt / (BrE)
(of a person) outgoing and socially confident; oriented toward external things or the social environment.

Examples

extrovert personality
As a child, he was quiet, but as an adult, he became much more extrovert.
Rebecca is such an extrovert that she loves meeting new people and thrives at parties.
The company's culture appeals more to extrovert personalities who enjoy teamwork and open communication.

Related Terms

  • antonym – introvert: a person who tends to be shy and prefers solitary activities
