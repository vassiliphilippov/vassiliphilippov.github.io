narrow

adjective
not wide; small in width
Pronunciation: / ˈnæroʊ / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ / (BrE)
having a small distance from one side to the other; not wide.

Examples

narrow street
She drew a narrow line with her pencil across the page.
The path through the forest was so narrow that only one person could walk at a time.
We crossed a narrow wooden bridge over the stream.

Related Terms

  • antonym – wide: having a great distance from side to side
