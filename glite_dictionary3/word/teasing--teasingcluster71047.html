teasing

noun
making fun of or provoking someone
Pronunciation: / ˈtizɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtiːzɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making fun of or provoking someone in a playful or sometimes hurtful way

Examples

friendly teasing
