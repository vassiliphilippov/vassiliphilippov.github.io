regular

noun
clothing size for average build
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛɡjələr / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊlə / (BrE)
a standard garment size for persons of average height and weight, as distinguished from 'petite', 'tall', or 'plus' sizes

Examples

regular size
Do you want the regular or the tall size for these trousers?
She usually wears a regular, but sometimes opts for petite in jackets.
The store was out of regular sizes, so he tried a large.
