Samoa

proper noun
South Pacific island group
Pronunciation: / səˈmoʊ.ə / (AmE), / səˈməʊ.ə / (BrE)
a group of volcanic islands in the South Pacific, known for its tropical climate, scenic landscapes, and Polynesian culture; includes the independent nation of Samoa and American Samoa.

Samoa attracts thousands of visitors each year with its unspoiled beaches and rich traditions.
