to snap

verb
take a photograph quickly
Pronunciation: / snæp / (AmE), / snæp / (BrE)
to take a photograph quickly, especially in an informal or spontaneous way

Examples

to snap a photo
He snapped a picture of the cat sleeping on the windowsill.
She quickly snapped a photo of the rainbow before it faded.
Tourists crowded around, all trying to snap the perfect shot of the monument.
