experiment

noun
controlled scientific test or procedure
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛrəmənt / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛrɪmənt / (BrE)
a scientific procedure undertaken to make a discovery, test a hypothesis, or demonstrate a known fact

Examples

a scientific experiment
Before publishing his findings, he repeated the experiment several times to ensure accuracy.
Students conducted a simple experiment to observe the reaction between vinegar and baking soda.
The results of the experiment supported the scientist's original hypothesis.

Related Terms

  • synonym – test: a procedure intended to establish the quality, performance, or reliability of something
  • synonym – trial: a test of performance, qualities, or suitability
