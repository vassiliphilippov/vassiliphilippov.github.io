to flex

verb
contract a muscle by tightening it
Pronunciation: / flɛks / (AmE), / flɛks / (BrE)
to contract (a muscle) by tightening it, often to show strength or for exercise

Examples

to flex your muscles
He likes to flex his biceps in front of the mirror.
She flexed her calf muscles during the warm-up routine.
Try to flex your stomach muscles and hold for five seconds.
