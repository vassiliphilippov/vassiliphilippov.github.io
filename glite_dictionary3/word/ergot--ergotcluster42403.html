ergot

noun
a fungus infecting cereals, source of alkaloids
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrɡət / (AmE), / ˈɜːɡət / (BrE)
a fungus that infects cereal plants, forming black masses instead of grains and causing a disease that can produce toxic compounds
