honour

noun
award or symbol of distinction
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːnər / (AmE), / ˈɒnə / (BrE)
a tangible award, medal, or other symbol given to show public approval or distinction for an achievement or quality

Examples

receive an honour
She received an honour for her outstanding scientific work.
The soldier was presented with a special honour at the ceremony.
Winning the literary prize was the highest honour of his career.

Related Terms

  • synonym – award: a prize or other mark of recognition given for achievement
  • antonym – dishonour: loss or lack of respect or esteem
