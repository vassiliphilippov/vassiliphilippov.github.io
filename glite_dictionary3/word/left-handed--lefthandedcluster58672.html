left-handed

adjective
rotating or turning to the left
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛftˈhændɪd / (AmE), / ˌlɛftˈhændɪd / (BrE)
(of a rotation or movement) turning or rotating to the left, especially in a counterclockwise direction.

Examples

left-handed screw thread
He replaced the part with a left-handed version to match the machinery's requirements.
In physics, certain molecules can have left-handed or right-handed rotation.
The screw has a left-handed thread, which means it tightens counterclockwise.

  • antonym – right-handed: rotating or turning to the right
