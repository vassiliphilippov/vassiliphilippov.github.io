eutectic

noun
mixture with a unique melting point
Pronunciation: / juːˈtɛktɪk / (AmE), / juːˈtɛktɪk / (BrE)
a mixture of substances that melts or solidifies at a single, lower temperature than any other proportion of the same substances

Examples

eutectic mixture melts
Scientists observed the eutectic mixture melting at a much lower temperature than its individual components.
Solder containing a eutectic blend is valued for its improved melting properties.
The alloy was formed by combining metals at their eutectic composition.
