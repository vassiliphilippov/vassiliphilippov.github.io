fidgetiness

noun
restlessness; tendency to fidget
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪdʒəṭinɪs / (AmE), / ˈfɪdʒɪtɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being unable to stay still due to restlessness or agitation

Examples

nervous fidgetiness
During the long meeting, her fidgetiness became obvious as she kept shifting in her chair.
He tried to hide his fidgetiness, but his tapping fingers gave him away.
The fidgetiness of the children made it difficult for the teacher to keep their attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym – calmness: the state of being free from agitation or excitement
  • synonym – restlessness: the state of being unable to rest or relax
