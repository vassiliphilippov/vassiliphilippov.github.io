to span

verb
extend across an area or period of time
Pronunciation: / spæn / (AmE), / spæn / (BrE)
to stretch, extend, or reach across (a space, an area, or a period of time)

Examples

to span many years
Her academic career spanned more than four decades.
The bridge spans the river, connecting the two towns.
The festival spans six days and features over fifty performances.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cover: to extend over or include an area, subject, or period of time
  • synonym – traverse: to travel or extend across or through something
