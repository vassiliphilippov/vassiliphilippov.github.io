haemophilic

adjective
relating to hemophilia
Pronunciation: / hiˌmoʊˈfɪlɪk / (AmE), / hɪˌməʊˈfɪlɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or having hemophilia, a medical condition in which the blood does not clot properly.

Examples

haemophilic patient
He was diagnosed as haemophilic and required regular treatment.
Research continues into better therapies for the haemophilic community.
Specialized medication is necessary for haemophilic patients undergoing surgery.
