little

adverb
to a small extent; not much
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl / (BrE)
to a small extent or degree; not very much.

Examples

to speak little
He spoke little during the meeting, preferring to listen to others' opinions.
She had little to say after hearing the unexpected news.
They slept little that night, worrying about their journey in the morning.
