dance

noun
rhythmical movements to music
Pronunciation: / dæns / (AmE), / dɑːns / (BrE)
a series of rhythmical steps and movements performed to music, often as a form of art or expression

Examples

dance performance
She learned a beautiful dance for the school talent show.
The dance lasted for hours and attracted a large crowd to the plaza.
Their wedding ended with a traditional folk dance that everyone joined in.
Ballet is a beautiful form of dance that requires years of training.
She devoted her life to the art of dance and became a celebrated choreographer.
The dance told a story without using any words.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ballet: a highly formalized artistic dance form
  • synonym – dancing: the activity or art of dancing
  • synonym – music: organized sound often accompanying dance
  • antonym – song: a musical composition with lyrics
