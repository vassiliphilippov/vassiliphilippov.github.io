scrupulous

adjective
extremely careful and thorough
Pronunciation: / ˈskruːpjələs / (AmE), / ˈskruːpjʊləs / (BrE)
Extremely careful to do things properly and with great attention to detail.

Examples

scrupulous attention to detail
It is important to be scrupulous when handling confidential information.
She was scrupulous about checking every fact in the report before submitting it.
The surgeon performed the operation with scrupulous attention to cleanliness.

Related Terms

  • antonym – careless: not giving attention or care; not thorough
  • synonym – meticulous: showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
