hao

noun
Vietnamese currency subunit
Pronunciation: / haʊ / (AmE), / haʊ / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Vietnam, equal to one tenth of a dong.

Examples

one hao coin
Although the hao is still a legal currency, it is rarely seen in circulation today.
Coins denominated in hao have become collectors’ items.
The value of ten hao equals one Vietnamese dong.
