reorganisation

noun
the process of organizing differently
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌɔrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of arranging or structuring something in a new or different way, often involving significant or extensive changes

Examples

major reorganisation plan
After the merger, the university underwent a complete reorganisation of its academic departments.
