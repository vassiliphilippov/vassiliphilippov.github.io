beyond

adverb
on the farther side; farther away
Pronunciation: / biˈɑnd / (AmE), / bɪˈɒnd / (BrE)
at or to the farther side of something from the point of view of the observer.

Examples

beyond the river
A small house stood beyond the river, barely visible in the mist.
From her window, she could see fields stretching far beyond the village.
We watched the sun set beyond the distant mountains.
