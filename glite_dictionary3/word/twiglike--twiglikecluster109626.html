twiglike

adjective
thin or slender like a twig
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪɡˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtwɪɡˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Thin or slender like a twig; resembling a twig in shape or size.

Examples

a twiglike branch
After weeks of illness, he appeared shockingly twiglike and frail.
Her twiglike arms could barely lift the grocery bags.
The artist drew twiglike lines to represent the delicate branches.
