tod

noun
unit of wool weight (c. 28 lbs)
Pronunciation: / tɑd / (AmE), / tɒd / (BrE)
a unit of weight for wool in the UK, equal to approximately 28 pounds (about 12.7 kilograms)

Examples

a tod of wool
In the 18th century, a tod was a common measure for fleeces.
Records showed that each tod weighed close to 28 pounds.
The farmer sold three tods of wool at the market.
