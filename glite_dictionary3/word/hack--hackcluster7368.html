to hack

verb
manage or deal with (informal)
Pronunciation: / hæk / (AmE), / hak / (BrE)
(informal) to be able to manage or handle a situation successfully, especially one that is difficult or challenging.

Examples

to hack college life
After years of late-night studying, she realized she couldn't hack college life anymore.
Do you really think you can hack such a demanding job?
Some people can't hack the pressure of living in a big city.
