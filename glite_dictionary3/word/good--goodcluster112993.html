good

noun
a commodity or tradable product
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd / (AmE), / ɡʊd / (BrE)
A raw material or product that is traded or sold, typically in large quantities, especially to be used in manufacturing or production processes.

Examples

export goods

Oil is one of the most important goods in the global economy.
