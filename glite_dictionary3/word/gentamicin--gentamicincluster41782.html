gentamicin

noun
an antibiotic for bacterial infections
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛntəˈmaɪsɪn / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛntəˈmʌɪsɪn / (BrE)
an antibiotic used to treat various bacterial infections, especially of the urinary tract, obtained from certain species of actinomycete bacteria
