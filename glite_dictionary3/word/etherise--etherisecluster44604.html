to etherise

verb
anaesthetize using ether
Pronunciation: / ˈiːθəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈiːθəˌraɪz / (BrE)
to administer ether to someone in order to induce anesthesia

Examples

to etherise a patient
In the 19th century, doctors would often etherise their patients for even minor procedures.
She was quickly etherised to ensure she felt no pain during the surgery.
The surgeons decided to etherise the patient before beginning the operation.
