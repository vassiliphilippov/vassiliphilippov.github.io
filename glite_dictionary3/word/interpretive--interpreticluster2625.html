interpretive

adjective
providing or relating to interpretation
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɜːrprətɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːprətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or providing an explanation or interpretation of something, especially artistic or cultural works.
