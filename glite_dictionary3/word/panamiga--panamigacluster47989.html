panamiga

noun
Central/South American stingless nettle plant
Pronunciation: / ˌpænəˈmiːɡə / (AmE), / ˌpanəˈmiːɡə / (BrE)
A low, stingless nettle native to Central and South America, characterized by velvety brownish-green toothed leaves and clusters of small green flowers.
