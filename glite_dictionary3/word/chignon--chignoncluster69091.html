chignon

noun
hair rolled or coiled at the neck
Pronunciation: / ʃiːˈnjɑn / (AmE), / ʃiːˈnjɒ̃ / (BrE)
a style in which long hair is twisted into a roll or knot and worn at the back of the neck

Examples

chignon hairstyle
Ballet dancers often wear their hair in a tight chignon during performances.
She styled her hair in a neat chignon for the wedding.
The actress swept her hair into a classic chignon for the red carpet event.
