residual

adjective
remaining; relating to a remainder
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɪdʒuəl / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪdjuəl / (BrE)
remaining after the main part or quantity has gone or been removed; relating to a remainder.

Examples

residual effect remains
After paying all the bills, there was only a residual amount of money left.
Doctors monitored the residual effects of the medication on his system.
The residual chemicals in the water made it unsafe to drink.

Related Terms

  • synonym – remaining: still existing, present, or left after other parts are gone
