ridge

noun
long narrow elevation on ocean floor
Pronunciation: / rɪdʒ / (AmE), / rɪdʒ / (BrE)
a long, narrow, raised area or line on a surface, such as land, the ocean floor, snow, or sand

Examples

mountain ridge
