crowing

adjective
boastful; showing self-importance
Pronunciation: / ˈkroʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person) boastful or showing self-importance, often in an annoying or exaggerated way.

Examples

crowing attitude
After winning the match, his crowing attitude irritated his teammates.
She couldn't stand his crowing about his promotion.
The crowing remarks from the manager made the staff uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym – boastful: showing excessive pride and self-satisfaction
  • antonym – humble: having or showing a modest opinion of one's own importance
