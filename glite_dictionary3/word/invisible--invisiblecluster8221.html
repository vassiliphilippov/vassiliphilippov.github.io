invisible

adjective
not able to be seen or noticed
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɪzəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɪzəbl / (BrE)
not able to be seen or not readily noticeable; not easily detected or prominent

Examples

invisible work contributions
His efforts often went invisible to the public, though they made a big difference.
She wore make-up that made her scars practically invisible.
The stain on the carpet was almost invisible in the dim light.
