lycanthrope

noun
person who can turn into a wolf
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪkənˌθroʊp / (AmE), / ˈlaɪkənˌθrəʊp / (BrE)
(in mythology and folklore) a person who can transform from a human into a wolf, typically during a full moon.

Examples

a lycanthrope transforms
According to legend, silver is the only thing that can harm a lycanthrope.
In many stories, a lycanthrope struggles between human and animal instincts.
The villagers believed a lycanthrope was responsible for the attacks during the full moon.
