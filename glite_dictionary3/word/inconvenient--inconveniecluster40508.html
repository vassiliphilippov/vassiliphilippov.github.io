inconvenient

adjective
causing trouble or difficulty
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.kənˈviː.ni.ənt /; / ˌɪnkənˈviːniənt / (AmE), / ˌɪn.kənˈviː.ni.ənt /; / ˌɪnkənˈviːniənt / (BrE)
causing trouble, difficulty, or discomfort; not suited to someone's needs, plans, or comfort

Examples
