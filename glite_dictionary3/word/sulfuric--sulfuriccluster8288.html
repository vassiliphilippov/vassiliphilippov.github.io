sulfuric

adjective
relating to sulfur or sulfur compounds
Pronunciation: / sʌlˈfjʊrɪk / (AmE), / sʌlˈfjʊərɪk / (BrE)
of, relating to, or containing sulfur, especially in compounds in which sulfur has a high oxidation state.
