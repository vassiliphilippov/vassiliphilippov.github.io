pestilence

noun
deadly epidemic disease
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛstɪləns /; / ˈpɛstələns / (AmE), / ˈpɛstɪləns / (BrE)
a deadly and widespread infectious disease, especially one that causes many deaths (such as the plague)
