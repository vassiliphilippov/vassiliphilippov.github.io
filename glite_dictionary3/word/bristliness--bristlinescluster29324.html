bristliness

noun
quality of being bristly or spiny
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪstl̩inəs / (AmE), / ˈbrɪstlɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being bristly; covered with short, stiff hairs or spines

Examples

bristliness of animal fur
He avoided petting the animal because of the bristliness of its fur.
The bristliness of cactus plants protects them from hungry animals.
The bristliness of the old brush made it uncomfortable to use.
