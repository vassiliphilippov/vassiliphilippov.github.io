sylvite

noun
a mineral; potassium chloride ore
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlˌvaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪlˌvaɪt / (BrE)
A mineral composed of native potassium chloride, commonly found in sedimentary deposits and used as an important source of potassium.
