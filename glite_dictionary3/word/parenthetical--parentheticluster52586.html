parenthetical

noun
expression in parentheses
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛrənˈθɛtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌparənˈθɛtɪkəl / (BrE)
an expression, remark, or word inserted within parentheses in a sentence

Examples

a parenthetical remark
His essay contained several parentheticals that distracted from the main argument.
The author used a funny parenthetical to share extra information with readers.
Too many parentheticals can make a passage harder to follow.

Related Terms

  • synonym – aside: a remark or passage that is not part of the main subject
