Virgo

proper noun
constellation of the zodiac between Leo and Libra
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝːɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈvɜːɡəʊ / (BrE)
a large constellation of the zodiac lying on the celestial equator, situated between Leo and Libra
