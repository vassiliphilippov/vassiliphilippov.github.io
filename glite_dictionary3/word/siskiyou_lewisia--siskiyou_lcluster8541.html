Siskiyou lewisia

noun
mountain perennial with rosette leaves and showy flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪskiˌjuː luːˈɪziə / (AmE), / ˌsɪskjuː luːˈɪsiə / (BrE)
an evergreen perennial plant, Lewisia cotyledon, native to the mountains of southwestern Oregon and northern California, with a rosette of long, spatula-shaped leaves and clusters of pink or white-and-red-striped flowers
