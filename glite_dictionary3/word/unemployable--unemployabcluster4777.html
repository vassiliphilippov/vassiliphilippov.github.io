unemployable

adjective
not able to be employed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.ɪmˈplɔɪ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪmˈplɔɪ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be employed, especially because of lack of skills, qualifications, or suitability

Examples

unemployable for most jobs
Some people fear that automation will make many workers unemployable in the future.
The long-term illness left her feeling isolated and unemployable.
Without any formal education or experience, he was considered unemployable by most companies.
