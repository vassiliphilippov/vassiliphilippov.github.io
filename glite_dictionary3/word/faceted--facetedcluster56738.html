faceted

adjective
having flat surfaces (facets), especially on gems
Pronunciation: / ˈfæsɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈfæsɪtɪd / (BrE)
having flat surfaces (facets) cut or formed on a surface, especially on a gem or crystal

Examples

faceted gemstone surface
Each faceted bead in the necklace sparkled under the sun.
Light danced off the faceted glass, creating a beautiful pattern on the wall.
The jeweler admired the faceted surface of the newly cut sapphire.
