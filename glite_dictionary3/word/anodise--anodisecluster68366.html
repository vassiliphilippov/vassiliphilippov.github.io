to anodise

verb
to electrochemically coat metal with oxide
Pronunciation: / ˈænəˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈan.ə.daɪz / (BrE)
to coat a metal, especially aluminium, with a protective or decorative oxide layer by using an electrochemical process

Examples

to anodise aluminium parts
Before painting the frame, make sure to anodise it for better adhesion.
The manufacturer decided to anodise the aluminium parts to prevent corrosion.
We can anodise the surface in different colours for aesthetic purposes.
