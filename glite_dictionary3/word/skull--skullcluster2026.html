skull

noun
gameplay modifier increasing difficulty
Pronunciation: / skʌl / (AmE), / skʌl / (BrE)
(in video games) a gameplay modifier, often shown as a skull icon, that changes game rules or increases difficulty.

Examples

skull modifier activated
Activating the skull before the match made enemies much tougher and more aggressive.
She picked up a skull that doubled experience but disabled checkpoints for the run.
Speedrunners often avoid turning on any skull to keep their runs consistent and fast.

Related Terms

  • synonym – difficulty modifier: a change that makes a game harder or easier
  • synonym – game modifier: a setting or item that changes how a game behaves or its difficulty
