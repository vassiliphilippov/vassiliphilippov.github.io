release

noun
termination of employment
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlis / (AmE), / rɪˈliːs / (BrE)
the act of ending a person's employment, making them free to leave their job or position

Examples

release from employment
After the company merger, several employees faced release due to overlapping roles.
His sudden release from the company surprised many of his colleagues.
The manager announced the release of two team members during the staff meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dismissal: the act of removing someone from employment
  • antonym – resignation: the act of voluntarily leaving one's employment
