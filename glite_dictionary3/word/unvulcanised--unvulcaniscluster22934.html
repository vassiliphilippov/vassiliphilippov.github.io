unvulcanised

adjective
not vulcanized (of rubber)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvʌlkəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈvʌlkənaɪzd / (BrE)
(Of rubber) not subjected to the process of vulcanization; in a raw or untreated state.

Examples

unvulcanised rubber sheets
Unvulcanised rubber is sticky and less durable than its treated counterpart.
Natural rubber is often transported in its unvulcanised form for processing.
The factory stores both unvulcanised and vulcanised rubber for different manufacturing needs.

Related Terms

  • antonym – vulcanised: (Of rubber) treated with sulfur under heat for durability
