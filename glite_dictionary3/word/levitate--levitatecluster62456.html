to levitate

verb
rise or float in the air
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvəˌteɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɛvɪteɪt /; / ˈlɛv.ɪ.teɪt / (BrE)
to rise or cause something to rise and float in the air, apparently defying gravity, usually by magic or tricks
