uttermost

adjective
greatest possible extent; most extreme
Pronunciation: / ˈʌt̬.ɚˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈʌt.ə.məʊst / (BrE)
of the greatest possible degree or extent; the most extreme or utmost.

Examples

The expedition reached the uttermost limits of the country.
