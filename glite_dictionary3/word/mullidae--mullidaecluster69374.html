Mullidae

noun
a family of marine fish (goatfishes, red mullets)
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌlɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˈmʌlɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of marine fish known as goatfishes or red mullets, characterized by their barbels and found chiefly in tropical and subtropical waters.
