Lech Walesa

proper noun
Polish labor leader and former president
Pronunciation: / lɛk vəˈwɛnsə / (AmE), / lɛk vaˈwɛnsə / (BrE)
(born 1943) Polish labor leader and statesman, co-founder of Solidarity, and President of Poland from 1990 to 1995.
