Rhizoctinia solani

noun
fungus causing black scurf on potatoes
Pronunciation: / raɪˌzɑkˈtɪniə soʊˈlɑni / (AmE), / raɪˌzɒkˈtɪniə səʊˈlɑːni / (BrE)
a species of fungus that causes black scurf disease on potatoes, resulting in black, scurfy spots on the tubers
