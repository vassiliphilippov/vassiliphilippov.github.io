monosyllabically

adverb
in a monosyllabic manner
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnəˌsɪˈlæbɪkli / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəˌsɪˈlæbɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner using or consisting of monosyllables; speaking or written using short, single-syllable words.

Examples

reply monosyllabically
He answered each question monosyllabically, barely making eye contact.
She spoke monosyllabically, her responses limited to 'yes' or 'no'.
The witness replied monosyllabically, clearly nervous about saying too much.
