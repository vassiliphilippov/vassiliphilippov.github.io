idiot

noun
a very unintelligent person (insult)
Pronunciation: / ˈɪdiət / (AmE), / ˈɪdiət / (BrE)
a person with very low intelligence or who acts in a very unintelligent way, often used as an insult

Examples

call someone an idiot
Despite being a brilliant scientist, she worried people might think she was an idiot because of her mistake.
He called me an idiot for forgetting my keys again.
Only an idiot would try to fix electrical wiring without turning off the power.
