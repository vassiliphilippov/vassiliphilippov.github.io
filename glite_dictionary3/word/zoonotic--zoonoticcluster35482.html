zoonotic

adjective
transmissible from animals to humans
Pronunciation: / zuː.əˈnɑː.tɪk / (AmE), / zəʊ.əˈnɒt.ɪk / (BrE)
(of a disease) able to be transmitted from animals to humans.

Examples

zoonotic disease outbreak
Mosquitos can carry zoonotic viruses such as West Nile and Zika.
Public health officials monitor zoonotic outbreaks to prevent epidemics.
Rabies is a zoonotic disease that can be spread by animal bites.

Related Terms

  • synonym – zoonosis: a disease that can be transmitted from animals to humans
