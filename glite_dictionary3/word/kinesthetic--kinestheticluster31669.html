kinesthetic

adjective
relating to bodily movement sensation
Pronunciation: / ˌkɪnəsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɪnɪsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the sensation of body movement and position (kinesthesis).

Examples

kinesthetic awareness
Dancers rely heavily on their kinesthetic awareness to coordinate complex movements.
Some students are kinesthetic learners who understand concepts better through physical activities.
The kinesthetic sense allows you to touch your nose even with your eyes closed.
