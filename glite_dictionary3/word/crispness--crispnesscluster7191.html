crispness

noun
pleasing firmness and freshness
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪsp.nəs /; / ˈkrɪspnəs / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsp.nəs /; / ˈkrɪspnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being firm, fresh, and slightly brittle, especially in food, materials, or fabrics

Examples

crispness of an apple

The crispness of the apple made it especially enjoyable to eat.

The crispness of the apple made it especially refreshing on a hot day.
