predictor

noun
something used to estimate the future
Pronunciation: / prɪˈdɪktər / (AmE), / prɪˈdɪktə / (BrE)
a factor or indicator that is used to estimate or forecast a future event or outcome

Examples

key predictor of success
Analysts look for the strongest predictor of economic growth in global markets.
