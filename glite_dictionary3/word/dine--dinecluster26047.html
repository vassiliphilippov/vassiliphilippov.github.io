to dine

verb
to eat dinner, often formally
Pronunciation: / daɪn / (AmE), / daɪn / (BrE)
to eat dinner or the main meal of the day, especially in a formal or social setting

Examples

to dine at a restaurant
She prefers to dine early in the evening before attending the theater.
