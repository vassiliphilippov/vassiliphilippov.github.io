to crust

verb
to form a hard outer layer
Pronunciation: / krʌst / (AmE), / krʌst / (BrE)
(of a substance) to form a hard outer layer or become covered with a crust.

Examples

to crust over wounds
As the wound healed, it began to crust over.
If you leave the soup out overnight, it may crust on top.
Mud can crust as it dries in the hot sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym – encrust: to cover or become covered with a crust or hard coating
