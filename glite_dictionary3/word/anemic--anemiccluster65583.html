anemic

adjective
lacking energy or vigor
Pronunciation: / əˈnimɪk /; / əˈniː.mɪk / (AmE), / əˈniːmɪk /; / əˈniː.mɪk / (BrE)
lacking in strength, energy, color, or spirit; weak or feeble (can also refer to someone suffering from anemia)

Examples

anemic performance
Her anemic response failed to convince the committee of her interest.
The company's sales growth was anemic compared to last year.
The team's anemic performance disappointed the fans.
After months of fatigue, the doctor diagnosed her as anemic.
Critics described the team's performance as anemic in the final game.
The patient's skin appeared pale and anemic during the examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pallid: pale, typically because of poor health
  • antonym – robust: strong and healthy; vigorous
