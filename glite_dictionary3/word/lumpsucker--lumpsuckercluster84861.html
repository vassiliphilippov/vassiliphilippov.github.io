lumpsucker

noun
a small marine fish with a sucking disc
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌmpˌsʌkər / (AmE), / ˈlʌmpˌsʌkə / (BrE)
A small marine fish, typically with a rounded body and modified pelvic fins forming a sucking disc, belonging to the family Cyclopteridae.

colorful lumpsucker fish
