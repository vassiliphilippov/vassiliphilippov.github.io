to measure

verb
have specific dimensions
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒə / (BrE)
(of an object) to have a particular size, length, width, or amount as stated.

Examples

The room measures twelve feet by fifteen feet.
The rug measured exactly the right width for our hallway.
This ancient oak tree measures over five meters in circumference.
