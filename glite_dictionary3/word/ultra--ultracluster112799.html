ultra

adjective
extremely far from the norm
Pronunciation: / ˈʌl.trə / (AmE), / ˈʌl.trə / (BrE)
(of views, actions, etc.) extremely or excessively far from what is considered normal or moderate; extreme.

Examples

ultra strict rules
His ultra strict diet excluded all forms of sugar and carbohydrates.
Many voters were alarmed by the candidate's ultra conservative stance on social issues.
The party distanced itself from its ultra radical members during the campaign.

Related Terms

  • synonym – extreme: very great in degree; far from what is usual
  • antonym – moderate: not extreme; within reasonable limits
