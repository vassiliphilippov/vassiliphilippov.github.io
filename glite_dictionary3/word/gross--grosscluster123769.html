gross

adjective
lacking detail; general
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊs / (AmE), / ɡrəʊs / (BrE)
(of a description or distinction) general; lacking in fine detail or precision.

Examples

gross estimate
At this stage, we only need a gross estimate of the total cost.
He made his decision based on a gross understanding of the problem, not its subtle details.
The gross features of the plan were acceptable, but the finer points needed work.

Related Terms

  • synonym – general: covering only main points, not detailed
  • antonym – specific: precise, with fine detail
