mastigophoran

noun
flagellated protozoan
Pronunciation: / ˌmæs.tɪˈɡɑː.fəˌræn / (AmE), / ˌmæs.tɪˈɡɒ.fər.ən / (BrE)
a mostly nonphotosynthetic, free-living protozoan that moves using one or more whiplike flagella; some species are pathogens of humans and animals
