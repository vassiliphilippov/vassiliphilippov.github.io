sombrely

adverb
in a serious or sad manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːmbɚli / (AmE), / ˈsɒmbəli / (BrE)
In a serious, sad, or grave manner; without cheerfulness or brightness.

Examples

to speak sombrely
He walked sombrely through the silent, empty streets.
She spoke sombrely about the challenges facing the company.
The ceremony was conducted sombrely, with everyone dressed in black.

Related Terms

  • antonym – cheerfully: in a happy, bright way
  • synonym – gloomily: in a way that is dark or depressed
