inoffensively

adverb
in a not offensive manner
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnəˈfɛnsɪvli / (AmE), / ˌɪnəˈfɛnsɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner that is not likely to offend or cause discomfort; not unpleasantly or hurtfully.

Examples

speak inoffensively
He voiced his concerns inoffensively, choosing his words carefully to avoid upsetting anyone.
She smiled inoffensively at the group, hoping to make a good impression.
The dog sniffed around inoffensively, not bothering anyone in the park.

Related Terms

  • antonym – offensively: in a way that is likely to offend or insult
