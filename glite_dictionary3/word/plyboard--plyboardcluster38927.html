plyboard

noun
laminated board of thin wood layers
Pronunciation: / ˈplaɪˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈplaɪˌbɔːd / (BrE)
A strong board made of thin layers of wood glued together, used in construction and furniture-making.

Examples

plyboard sheet
Contractors often choose plyboard because it is lightweight and strong.
The walls of the shed were built with plyboard for extra durability.
We used a sheet of plyboard to create the backing for our bookshelf project.
