Halicarnassus

proper noun
ancient Greek city in Asia Minor; site of famous mausoleum
Pronunciation: / ˌhælɪkɑːrˈnæsəs / (AmE), / ˌhælɪkɑːˈnæsəs / (BrE)
an ancient Greek city on the southwestern coast of Asia Minor, now in Turkey, famous as the site of the Mausoleum at Halicarnassus, one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.

Examples

ancient Halicarnassus city

Many historians visit Halicarnassus to study its ancient ruins and rich history.
The Mausoleum at Halicarnassus was renowned as one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.
