to allay

verb
to relieve or satisfy thirst or desire
Pronunciation: / əˈleɪ / (AmE), / əˈleɪ / (BrE)
to relieve or satisfy (a feeling such as thirst); to make (thirst, desire, etc.) less strong or easier to bear

Examples

to allay thirst
A glass of water helped to allay his terrible thirst after the long run.
She drank slowly, hoping to allay her thirst before the meeting.
The soup was so refreshing it allayed my thirst on the cold evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym – quench: to satisfy (thirst, desire, etc.), especially by drinking
