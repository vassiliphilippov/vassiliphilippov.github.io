stinkpot

noun
small musky North American turtle
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋkˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkˌpɒt / (BrE)
a small North American freshwater turtle (Sternotherus odoratus) known for releasing a strong musky odor when disturbed

Examples
