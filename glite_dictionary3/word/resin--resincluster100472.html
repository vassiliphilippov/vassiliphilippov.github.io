resin

noun
sticky plant substance or synthetic material
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛz.ən / (AmE), / ˈrɛz.ɪn / (BrE)
a solid or semisolid sticky substance obtained from certain plants or produced synthetically, often used in making plastics, varnishes, or adhesives
