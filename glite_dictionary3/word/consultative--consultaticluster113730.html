consultative

adjective
giving advice; advisory
Pronunciation: / kənˈsʌltətɪv / (AmE), / kənˈsʌltətɪv / (BrE)
giving advice or having the role of offering suggestions and recommendations rather than making decisions

Examples

consultative role
Her role is more consultative than executive, offering guidance rather than making decisions.
The committee serves a purely consultative function within the organization.
They formed a consultative panel to provide expert opinions on the new project.
