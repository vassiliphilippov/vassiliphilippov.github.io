omnipotence

noun
unlimited power or authority
Pronunciation: / ɑmˈnɪpətəns / (AmE), / ɒmˈnɪpətəns / (BrE)
the quality or state of having unlimited or very great power

Examples

omnipotence of a god
He felt a sense of omnipotence after winning the championship.
Many religions attribute omnipotence to their gods.
The story explores the dangers of absolute omnipotence in the hands of a single person.
