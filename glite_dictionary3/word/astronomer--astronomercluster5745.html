astronomer

noun
person who studies astronomy
Pronunciation: / əˈstrɑː.nə.mɚ / (AmE), / əˈstrɒn.ə.mə / (BrE)
a person whose job or hobby is the scientific study of stars, planets, and other objects in outer space

Examples

astronomer studies stars
As a child, she dreamed of becoming an astronomer and exploring the universe.
Caroline Herschel was a pioneering astronomer in the 18th century.
The astronomer observed a new comet through the powerful telescope.

Related Terms

  • degree lower – amateur: a person who engages in a pursuit as a pastime, not a profession
  • degree higher – astronaut: a person trained to travel and work in space
  • synonym – astrophysicist: a scientist who studies the physical properties of celestial bodies
