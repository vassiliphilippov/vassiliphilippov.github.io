poke

noun
a punch or blow with the fist
Pronunciation: / poʊk / (AmE), / pəʊk / (BrE)
(informal) A punch or blow delivered with the fist, especially in boxing or a fight.

Examples

a hard poke
After the argument, she threatened to give him a poke if he didn't leave.
He gave his opponent a swift poke to the jaw during the second round.
The boxer landed a solid poke, knocking his rival back a step.

Related Terms

  • synonym – punch: a forceful hit with the fist
  • antonym – slap: a blow with the open hand
