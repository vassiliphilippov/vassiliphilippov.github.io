forger

noun
person who operates a forge
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrdʒər / (AmE), / ˈfɔːdʒə / (BrE)
a person who operates a forge, typically working with metal by heating and shaping it; a blacksmith.

Examples

forger shapes metal
As a young apprentice, he learned from the village forger how to craft tools.
Before the advent of factories, every town relied on a skilled forger for its metalwork.
The forger skillfully shaped the red-hot iron into a new horseshoe.
