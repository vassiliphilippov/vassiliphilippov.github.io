waders

noun
waterproof boots for wading, esp. by anglers
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪdɚz / (AmE), / ˈweɪdəz / (BrE)
waterproof boots covering the legs and sometimes the chest, worn especially by anglers when wading in water

Examples

fishing in waders
A tear in his waders left him with soaked trousers after a morning of fishing.
He pulled on his waders before stepping into the icy river to fish.
The waders kept her legs dry as she crossed the marsh.
