soaking

noun
the act of making completely wet
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊkɪŋ /; / ˈsoʊ.kɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊkɪŋ /; / ˈsəʊ.kɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of making something or someone completely wet by immersing in liquid
