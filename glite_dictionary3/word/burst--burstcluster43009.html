burst

noun
a sudden explosion or eruption
Pronunciation: / bɝst / (AmE), / bɜːst / (BrE)
A sudden breaking open or eruption, especially as a result of pressure; an explosion or the act of bursting.

Examples

burst of fireworks
A burst of fireworks lit up the night sky during the festival.
The burst of the water pipe flooded the basement within minutes.
There was a sudden burst of laughter from the other room.
