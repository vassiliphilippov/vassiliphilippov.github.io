to grind

verb
work hard and persistently
Pronunciation: / ɡraɪnd / (AmE), / ɡraɪnd / (BrE)
to work hard and steadily, often on dull or repetitive tasks

Examples

to grind through work
After months of grinding, they finally completed the project.
Every night she would grind through her assignments until midnight.
He has been grinding away at his job to support his family.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drudge: to do hard, menial, or dull work
  • antonym – rest: to cease work or movement in order to relax
  • synonym – toil: work extremely hard or incessantly
