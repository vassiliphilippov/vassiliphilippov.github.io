Cyperus papyrus

noun
plant used to make ancient papyrus
Pronunciation: / saɪˈpɪrəs pəˈpaɪrəs / (AmE), / saɪˈpɪərəs pəˈpaɪərəs / (BrE)
a tall sedge native to the Nile valley, known for its stem used in ancient times to produce papyrus paper and other materials
