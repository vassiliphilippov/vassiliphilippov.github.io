check

noun
written bank payment order
Pronunciation: / tʃɛk / (AmE), / tʃɛk / (BrE)
a written order to a bank to pay a stated sum from the drawer's account to the person named

Examples

bank check
All purchases must be made by check or credit card.
Please sign the check before giving it to the cashier.
