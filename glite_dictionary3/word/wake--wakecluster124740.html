to wake

verb
make someone aware or alert
Pronunciation: / weɪk / (AmE), / weɪk / (BrE)
to make someone aware of or alert to something, especially something they had not noticed before

Examples

to wake someone to danger
Her experience abroad woke her to the realities of poverty.
His letter woke the community to the need for better education.
The documentary woke many viewers to the urgent problem of climate change.

  • synonym – alert: to warn or make someone aware of a dangerous or important situation
