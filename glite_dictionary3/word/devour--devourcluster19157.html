to devour

verb
eat eagerly in large amounts
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaʊɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈvaʊə / (BrE)
to eat or consume something eagerly or completely (including food or things like fire destroying something)

Examples

to devour a meal
After a long hike, she devoured her lunch hungrily.
He devours books as quickly as he can get them.
The children devoured the chocolate cake within minutes.
Flames quickly devoured the old wooden barn, leaving only ashes behind.
She devoured the novel in a single sitting, unable to put it down.
The hungry wolves devoured their prey within minutes.
She devoured her breakfast, eager to get to work on time.
The children devoured the pizza within minutes after coming home from school.
They were so hungry that they devoured every last crumb on their plates.
