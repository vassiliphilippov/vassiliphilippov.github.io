lost

adjective
doomed or irretrievably destroyed
Pronunciation: / lɔst / (AmE), / lɒst / (BrE)
Having no hope of being saved, recovered, or rescued; spiritually or physically doomed or destroyed.

Examples

lost soul
He felt like one of the lost after the collapse of his community.
The message was meant to give hope to the lost and those without direction.
There was a sense of despair among the lost survivors stranded at sea.
