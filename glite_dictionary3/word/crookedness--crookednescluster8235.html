crookedness

noun
dishonesty; deceitfulness
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʊkɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈkrʊkɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being deceitful, dishonest, or underhanded

Examples

The politician's crookedness was eventually exposed by the investigative journalists.
