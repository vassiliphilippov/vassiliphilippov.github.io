to hang

verb
to droop or let fall loosely
Pronunciation: / hæŋ / (AmE), / hæŋ / (BrE)
to fall or drape loosely, as with fabric, clothing, or hair

Examples

hang loosely

She began to hang her head when she was scolded.
The tired flowers hung limply over the edge of the vase.
Her hair hung down to her waist in silky waves.
The curtains hang beautifully from the tall windows.
This coat hangs better on a sturdy hanger.
