contestee

noun
one whose victory is challenged
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑn.tɛsˈti / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.tɛsˈti / (BrE)
a person whose victory in a competition, election, or race is formally challenged by another party

Examples

contestee in an election
As the contestee, she had to present evidence that the race was fair.
The election contestee was required to defend their win in court.
The losing candidate officially filed a complaint against the contestee after the results were announced.

  • synonym – contestor: a person who formally challenges a result
