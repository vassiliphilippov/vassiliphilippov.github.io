contributor

noun
person who adds content or code online
Pronunciation: / kənˈtrɪbjətə / (AmE), / kənˈtrɪbjətə / (BrE)
A person who adds content, code, edits, or other material to an online collaborative project (for example, an open-source repository or a wiki).

Examples

contributor to a project
She became a contributor to the open-source project, submitting code and documentation.
As a regular contributor, he improves articles and answers questions on the community wiki.
Several contributors reviewed the pull request before the maintainer merged it into master.

Related Terms

  • synonym – author: a person who writes books or other works
  • synonym – collaborator: a person who works together with others on a shared project
