shuttle

noun
badminton projectile; feathered object
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtl̩ / (BrE)
A light, feathered or synthetic object with a rounded base used as a projectile in the game of badminton.

Examples

shuttle in badminton
She served the shuttle expertly over the net.
The player accidentally crushed the shuttle during the match.
You need a new shuttle if the feathers get damaged.
