crank

adjective
(boat) unstable, easily tips under sail
Pronunciation: / kræŋk / (AmE), / kræŋk / (BrE)
(of a boat) inclined to tip or heel over easily under sail, lacking stability.

Examples

crank sailing boat
Because the dinghy was crank, it required careful maneuvering to avoid capsizing.
She found the skiff dangerously crank in rough weather.
The old wooden boat was so crank that it frightened inexperienced sailors.
