telferage

noun
cable transportation system with suspended cars
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlfərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtɛlfərɪdʒ / (BrE)
A transportation system in which cars (telphers) are suspended from cables and operated by electricity.

Examples

telferage cable system
A telferage was constructed to connect the two factories on opposite hillsides.
Historians examined the old telferage to understand early electric transit technology.
The mining company used a telferage system to transport ore across the valley.
