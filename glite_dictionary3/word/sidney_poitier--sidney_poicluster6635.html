Sidney Poitier

proper noun
Bahamian-American actor and director
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪdni pwɑːˈtjeɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪdni ˈpwɑːtieɪ / (BrE)
(1927–2022) A Bahamian-American film actor and director, notable as the first Black man to win the Academy Award for Best Actor and for breaking racial barriers in Hollywood.

Examples

Sidney Poitier won an Oscar
Sidney Poitier won an Academy Award for Best Actor in 1964.
I watched a documentary about the life and career of Sidney Poitier.
Many people credit Sidney Poitier with opening doors for Black actors in Hollywood.
