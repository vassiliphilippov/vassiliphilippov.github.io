to emend

verb
correct or improve a text
Pronunciation: / iˈmɛnd / (AmE), / ɪˈmɛnd / (BrE)
to correct or improve a text, especially by removing errors

Examples

to emend a manuscript
Scholars continue to emend the manuscript as new evidence emerges.
She carefully emended the typos in the original document before submission.
The editor was asked to emend several passages that were unclear.

Related Terms

  • synonym – amend: to make minor changes to a text or document
  • synonym – edit: to prepare written material for publication by correcting and modifying it
