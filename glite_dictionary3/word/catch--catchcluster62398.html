catch

noun
game of throwing and catching a ball
Pronunciation: / kætʃ / (AmE), / kætʃ / (BrE)
a children's game in which a ball is thrown back and forth between two or more people, usually for fun or practice

Examples

play catch outside
After dinner, they went outside to play catch in the yard.
He enjoys a simple game of catch with his father every evening.
