breastfeeding

noun
feeding a baby with mother's milk
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛstˌfidɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrɛstˌfiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of feeding a baby with milk directly from the mother's breast.

Examples

breastfeeding a baby
Breastfeeding is recommended for at least the first six months of a child's life.
Hospitals now offer support groups for new mothers struggling with breastfeeding.
Some mothers find breastfeeding challenging at first but become more comfortable with practice.
