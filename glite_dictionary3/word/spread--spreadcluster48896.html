spread

noun
difference between two numerical values
Pronunciation: / sprɛd / (AmE), / sprɛd / (BrE)
the difference or gap between two numbers, amounts, or values, especially in prices, rates, or statistics

Examples

the spread in prices
Banks profit from the spread between the interest rates they charge and pay.
The spread between wholesale and retail prices has increased this year.
There is a large spread in test scores between the two schools.
