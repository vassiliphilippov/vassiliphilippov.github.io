pillar

noun
key principle or supporting element
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlɚ /; / ˈpɪlər / (AmE), / ˈpɪlə / (BrE)
an important person, principle, or thing that supports or is central to a group, organization, or idea

Examples

pillar of society
Education is considered a pillar of a healthy society.
Freedom of speech stands as a pillar of democracy.
Integrity is one of the main pillars of their corporate culture.
Many regarded Dr. Khan as a pillar of the local medical community.
She was a pillar of strength for her family during difficult times.
Without its financial pillar, the organization would struggle to survive.
