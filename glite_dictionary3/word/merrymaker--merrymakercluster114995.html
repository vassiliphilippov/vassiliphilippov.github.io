merrymaker

noun
person taking part in celebrations
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛriˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈmɛrimeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in lively or noisy festivities

Examples

holiday merrymaker
As midnight approached, the streets were crowded with merrymakers shouting and laughing.
At the New Year's Eve party, every merrymaker wore a colorful hat.
The parade was filled with merrymakers dancing and waving flags.

Related Terms

  • synonym – partygoer: a person who attends parties
  • synonym – reveler: a person who is enjoying themselves in a lively and noisy way, especially with drinking and dancing
