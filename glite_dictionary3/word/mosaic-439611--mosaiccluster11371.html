mosaic

noun
pattern made of many small pieces
Pronunciation: / moʊˈzeɪ.ɪk /; / moʊˈzeɪɪk / (AmE), / məʊˈzeɪ.ɪk /; / məʊˈzeɪɪk / (BrE)
a picture, pattern, or image made by arranging many small, often colored, pieces together; also, any design or image made from many small elements
