stumblebum

noun
clumsy or incompetent person
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌm.bəlˌbʌm / (AmE), / ˈstʌm.bəl.bʌm / (BrE)
a person who is clumsy, awkward, or incompetent, especially in a foolish or bumbling way (sometimes used for a second-rate or clumsy boxer)
