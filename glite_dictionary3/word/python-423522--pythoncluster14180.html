python

noun
archaic: soothsaying spirit or oracle-giver
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪˌθɑn / (AmE), / ˈpʌɪθən / (BrE)
(Archaic) A soothsaying spirit or a person believed to be possessed by such a spirit, especially one who delivers oracles.
