hexapod

noun
six-legged robot
Pronunciation: / h5bks59 p51d / (AmE), / h5bks59p52d / (BrE)
(in robotics) a machine or robotic platform with six legs, designed to walk or provide stable locomotion on uneven terrain.

Examples

robotic hexapod
The research team tested a new hexapod prototype across rocky slopes to evaluate balance and endurance.
Engineers programmed the hexapod to climb stairs and maneuver through debris during search-and-rescue trials.
Many universities build open-source hexapod platforms so students can study legged locomotion algorithms.

Related Terms

  • synonym – robotic platform: a hardware base used to test robotic algorithms; can be legged or wheeled and used for research.
  • synonym – walking robot: a robot designed to move using legs rather than wheels; a hexapod is a six-legged example.
