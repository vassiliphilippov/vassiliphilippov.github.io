clypeus

noun
plate on insect's head
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪ.pi.əs / (AmE), / ˈklɪp.i.əs / (BrE)
a shield-like plate located on the front part of an insect's head, above the labrum

Examples

insect clypeus plate
Identification often depends on the shape and size of the clypeus.
The clypeus of the beetle was broad and slightly convex.
Under the microscope, the clypeus appeared as a distinct shield-shaped structure.
