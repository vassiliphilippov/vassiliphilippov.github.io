to grind

verb
shape or form by abrasion
Pronunciation: / ɡraɪnd / (AmE), / ɡraɪnd / (BrE)
to shape, form, or decorate something by rubbing it with a tool or abrasive

Examples

to grind a lens
He used a special tool to grind the lens to the correct shape.
Skilled workers grind metal parts to precise specifications in the factory.
You must grind the edges carefully to avoid cracks in the glass.
She enjoys grinding floral designs onto small glass bowls.
The artist expertly grinds intricate patterns into crystal glasses.
They ground the family crest onto the silver trophy.
