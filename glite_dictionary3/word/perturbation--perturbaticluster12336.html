perturbation

noun
state of being worried or upset
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜrtɚˈbeɪʃən /; / pɚˌtɝˈbeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɜːtəˈbeɪʃən /; / pəˌtɜːˈbeɪʃən / (BrE)
a state of being worried, anxious, or mentally disturbed
