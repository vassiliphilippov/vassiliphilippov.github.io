to engage

verb
to betroth or give in marriage (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
(archaic) To arrange the marriage of someone; to betroth or give in marriage.

Examples

to engage a daughter
In those times, parents might engage their sons and daughters at an early age.
The king would often engage his children for political alliances.
The nobleman chose to engage his daughter to a wealthy merchant.
