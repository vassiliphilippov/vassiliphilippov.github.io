Zionist

adjective
relating to the movement supporting a Jewish nation
Pronunciation: / ˈzaɪənɪst /; / ˈzaɪ.ən.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈzaɪənɪst /; / ˈzaɪ.ə.nɪst / (BrE)
relating to or supporting Zionism, especially the movement for the re-establishment and development of a Jewish nation in what is now Israel
