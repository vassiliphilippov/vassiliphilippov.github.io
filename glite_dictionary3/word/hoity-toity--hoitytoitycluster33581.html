hoity-toity

adjective
snobbish; pretentious; haughty
Pronunciation: / ˌhɔɪ.tiˈtɔɪ.ti / (AmE), / ˌhɔɪ.tiˈtɔɪ.ti / (BrE)
Behaving in a manner that is snobbish, pretentious, or overly self-important.

Examples

a hoity-toity attitude
Don't be so hoity-toity; everyone here is just trying to have a good time.
She looked down her nose at us with a very hoity-toity attitude.
The restaurant has a hoity-toity vibe, but the food is reasonably priced.

Related Terms

  • antonym – humble: having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's importance
  • synonym – pretentious: attempting to impress by affecting greater importance or merit than is actually possessed
