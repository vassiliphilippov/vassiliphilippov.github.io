coat

noun
outer garment with sleeves, worn outdoors
Pronunciation: / koʊt / (AmE), / kəʊt / (BrE)
an outer piece of clothing with sleeves, worn outdoors, that covers the upper body and sometimes the legs, typically for warmth or protection

Examples

winter coat
He put on his coat before going outside into the cold.
Please hang your coat on the rack by the door.
She bought a new winter coat with a fur-lined hood.

Related Terms

  • synonym – jacket: a short coat; a garment for the upper body, shorter than a coat
