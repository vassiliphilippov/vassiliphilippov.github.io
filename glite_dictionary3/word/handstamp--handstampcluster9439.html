to handstamp

verb
mark with a rubber stamp
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌstæmp / (AmE), / ˈhændˌstæmp / (BrE)
to mark (a document or item) by impressing a rubber stamp, often to show official approval.

Examples

to handstamp a document
Every outgoing parcel must be handstamped to confirm it has been checked.
She handstamped each ticket at the entrance to the event.
The clerk handstamped the document before returning it to me.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stamp: to impress a mark or seal on something with a tool or die
