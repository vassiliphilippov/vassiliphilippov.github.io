bar

noun
horizontal rod for gymnastic exercises
Pronunciation: / bɑr / (AmE), / bɑː / (BrE)
a long, rigid, straight piece of metal, wood, or similar material, used as a support, for exercise, as part of equipment, or as an obstruction
