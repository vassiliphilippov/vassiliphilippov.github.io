to beam

verb
broadcast a TV or radio signal
Pronunciation: / biːm / (AmE), / biːm / (BrE)
to send out (a radio or television signal); to broadcast over the airwaves

Examples

to beam a signal
Children's shows are regularly beamed directly into homes in rural areas.
The news program will be beamed live to millions of viewers around the world.
They beamed the concert across the country via satellite last night.
