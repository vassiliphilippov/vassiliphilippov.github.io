Elam

proper noun
ancient kingdom in southwestern Asia (now Iran)
Pronunciation: / ˈiːlɑm / (AmE), / ˈiːləm / (BrE)
An ancient country in southwestern Asia, located east of the Tigris River in what is now Iran, known for its warlike people and significant role in early Mesopotamian history.

ancient Elam civilization
Archaeologists have uncovered remarkable artifacts from Elam that date back more than four thousand years.
