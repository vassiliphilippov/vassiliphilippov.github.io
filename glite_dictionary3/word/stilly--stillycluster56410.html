stilly

adjective
quiet or calm (poetic)
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪli / (AmE), / ˈstɪli / (BrE)
(poetic) Quiet; still or calm, especially in a peaceful or hushed way.

Examples

stilly night air

The cottage stood in the stilly dusk, untouched by the distant celebrations.
