Frankliniella fusca

noun
species of thrips harmful to crops
Pronunciation: / fræŋkˌlɪniˈɛlə ˈfʌskə / (AmE), / fræŋkˌlɪniˈelə ˈfʌskə / (BrE)
A species of thrips, commonly known as the tobacco thrips, that is harmful to tobacco and peanut crops.
