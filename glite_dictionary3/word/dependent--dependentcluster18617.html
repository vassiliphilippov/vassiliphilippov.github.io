dependent

adjective
addicted to a drug
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
Addicted to a drug and unable to function normally without it.

Examples

dependent on drugs
After years of substance abuse, he became dependent on painkillers.
Patients who are dependent on opioids often require supervised withdrawal.
She realized she was dependent on sleeping pills to get through the night.
