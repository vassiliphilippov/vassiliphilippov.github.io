brotherhood

noun
solidarity; feeling of brotherly support
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌðɚˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈbrʌðəhʊd / (BrE)
a feeling of solidarity or support among people, as if they were brothers

Examples

global brotherhood
Efforts to promote brotherhood have reduced tensions between the communities.
