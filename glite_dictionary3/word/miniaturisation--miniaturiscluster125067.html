miniaturisation

noun
the process of making things very small
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪniətʃəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪniətʃəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something much smaller in size, especially by using advanced technology

Examples

miniaturisation of technology
Thanks to miniaturisation, entire computers can now fit in your pocket.
