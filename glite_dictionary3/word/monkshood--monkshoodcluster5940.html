monkshood

noun
a poisonous plant with blue-purple flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋksˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋksˌhʊd / (BrE)
a poisonous plant with hood-shaped blue or purple flowers, found in northern Europe and containing aconite

Examples
