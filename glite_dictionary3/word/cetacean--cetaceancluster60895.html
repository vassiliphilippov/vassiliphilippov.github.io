cetacean

noun
large aquatic mammal (whale, dolphin, etc.)
Pronunciation: / sɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / sɪˈteɪsiən /; / sɪˈteɪsɪən / (BrE)
a large aquatic mammal of the order Cetacea, such as a whale, dolphin, or porpoise
