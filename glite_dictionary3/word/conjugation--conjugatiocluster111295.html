conjugation

noun
pairing of sexes for reproduction
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn.dʒəˈɡeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.dʒʊˈɡeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process in biology where two organisms or cells join together, especially to exchange genetic material

Examples

The process of conjugation can ensure greater genetic diversity among offspring.
