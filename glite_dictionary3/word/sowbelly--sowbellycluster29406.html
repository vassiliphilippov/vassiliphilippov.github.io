sowbelly

noun
salt pork from a pig's belly
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊˌbɛli / (AmE), / ˈsəʊˌbɛli / (BrE)
Salt pork cut from the belly of a pig, often used as a staple food in traditional American cooking.
