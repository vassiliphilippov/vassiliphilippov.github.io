to strip

verb
draw the last milk from a cow
Pronunciation: / strɪp / (AmE), / strɪp / (BrE)
(of a cow) to draw out the last milk during milking, often by hand, after most of the milk has been withdrawn.

Examples

to strip a cow
It's important to strip the cow at the end of milking for hygiene.
She learned to strip the cows by hand on her family's dairy farm.
The farmer carefully stripped each cow to ensure no milk was left in the udder.
