to core

verb
remove the central part (esp. of fruit)
Pronunciation: / kɔr / (AmE), / kɔː / (BrE)
to remove the central part or core from something, especially a fruit

Examples

to core an apple
After coring the tomatoes, chop them for the salad.
He cored the pears quickly to prepare the dessert.
Use a sharp knife to core the apple before slicing it.
