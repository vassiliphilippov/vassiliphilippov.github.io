madrasa

noun
Muslim religious school, esp. in South Asia
Pronunciation: / məˈdrɑːsə / (AmE), / məˈdrɑːsə / (BrE)
A Muslim school, especially one in South Asia, where religious and sometimes secular subjects are taught.
