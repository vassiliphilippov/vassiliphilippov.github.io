Georgian

adjective
relating to British rule under Kings George I–IV
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔrdʒən / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒiən / (BrE)
relating to the period during which the Hanoverian kings named George ruled Britain (1714–1830), or to the style, culture, or events of that time.

Examples

Georgian style furniture
He was fascinated by Georgian literature and culture.
The elegant architecture of the Georgian era can still be seen in Bath.
The estate was restored to its original Georgian design.
