superposition

noun
placement of one thing over another
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərpəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəpəˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
the action of placing one thing on or above another, especially so that parts overlap; the state of being thus arranged
