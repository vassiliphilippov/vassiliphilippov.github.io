concealing

adjective
hiding or covering up
Pronunciation: / kənˈsilɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈsiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
keeping something from being seen, noticed, or known; hiding or covering up

Examples

concealing makeup
Heavy, concealing makeup was used for the role.
She wore a concealing dress that covered her scar.
The concealing clouds hid the moon from view.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hiding: keeping out of sight; similar to concealing
  • antonym – revealing: showing more than usual; the opposite of concealing, especially for clothing or information
