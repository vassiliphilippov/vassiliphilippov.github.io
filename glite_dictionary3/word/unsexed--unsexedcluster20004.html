unsexed

adjective
sex not yet determined (animals)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛkst / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛkst / (BrE)
(of animals, especially young poultry) not yet sexed; the sex of the animal has not been determined.

Examples

unsexed chicks shipped
The hatchery ships unsexed day-old chicks, so farmers separate males and females themselves.
They bought several unsexed quail, planning to determine their sexes as the birds matured.
When ordering chicks online, choose either sexed or unsexed options depending on your purpose.

Related Terms

  • antonym – sexed: having had the sex of an animal identified or separated by sex.
