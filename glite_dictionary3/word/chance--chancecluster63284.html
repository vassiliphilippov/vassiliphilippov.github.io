chance

adjective
happening by accident; not planned
Pronunciation: / tʃæns / (AmE), / tʃɑːns / (BrE)
happening or done without being planned or intended; occurring by accident.

Examples

chance discovery
A chance discovery in the lab led to a major scientific breakthrough.
It was a chance meeting that changed the course of their lives.
The team won thanks to a chance mistake by their opponents.

Related Terms

  • synonym – accidental: happening by accident, not planned
  • antonym – intentional: done on purpose, not by chance
