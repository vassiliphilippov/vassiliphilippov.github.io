nogging

noun
brick infill in timber frames
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɒɡɪŋ / (BrE)
Rough brickwork or masonry used to fill the spaces between timbers in a wooden frame.

Examples

nogging between timbers
Some restoration projects preserve the original nogging to maintain historical accuracy.
