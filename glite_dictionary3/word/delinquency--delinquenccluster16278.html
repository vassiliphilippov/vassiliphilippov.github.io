delinquency

noun
nonpayment of a debt when due
Pronunciation: / dəˈlɪŋkwənsi / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪŋkwənsi / (BrE)
failure to pay a debt or financial obligation by its due date.

Examples

delinquency in payments
Any prolonged delinquency on your loan may affect your credit score.
High rates of credit card delinquency can indicate economic difficulties.
The homeowner's mortgage delinquency resulted in a warning from the bank.

Related Terms

  • synonym – default: failure to fulfill a financial obligation, especially to repay a loan
  • antonym – repayment: the act of paying back money that is owed
