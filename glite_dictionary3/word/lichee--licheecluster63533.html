lichee

noun
small Chinese fruit with sweet pulp
Pronunciation: / ˈliː.tʃi / (AmE), / ˈliː.tʃi / (BrE)
A small round fruit with a thin, brittle red shell, sweet translucent pulp, and a single seed, native to China; also called lychee.

Examples

fresh lichee fruit
He bought a bag of dried lichees from the market.
I tasted a fresh lichee for the first time on my trip to China.
The dessert was topped with slices of juicy lichee.
