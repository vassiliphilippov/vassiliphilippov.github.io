Neosporin

noun
antibacterial ointment (brand name)
Pronunciation: / ˌniː.oʊˈspɔɹ.ɪn / (AmE), / ˌniː.əʊˈspɔː.rɪn / (BrE)
A brand name for an ointment containing several antibacterial agents, used to prevent infection in minor skin injuries and sometimes in eye drops for minor eye infections.

Examples

apply Neosporin ointment
Doctors sometimes recommend Neosporin for minor burns or scrapes.
I always keep a tube of Neosporin in my first-aid kit.
She applied Neosporin to the cut to prevent infection.
