to host

verb
receive or entertain guests
Pronunciation: / hoʊst / (AmE), / həʊst / (BrE)
to receive or entertain guests at one's home or event; to act as a host for others

Examples

to host a meeting
Last weekend, they hosted several out-of-town guests for the wedding.
She loves to host dinner parties for her friends and colleagues.
We decided to host the annual holiday party at our house this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – entertain: to receive guests and provide them with amusement or hospitality
