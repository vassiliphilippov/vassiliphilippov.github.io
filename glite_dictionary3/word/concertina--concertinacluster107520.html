to concertina

verb
collapse or fold up like a concertina
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑnsərˈtinə / (AmE), / ˌkɒnsəˈtiːnə / (BrE)
to collapse, fold, or compress into alternating ridges and grooves like the bellows of a concertina.
