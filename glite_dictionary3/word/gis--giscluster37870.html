GIS

noun
system for geographic data analysis
Pronunciation: / dʒi aɪ ɛs / (AmE), / dʒiː aɪ ɛs / (BrE)
An information system designed to capture, store, manipulate, analyze, manage, and present geographical or spatial data.

Examples

GIS mapping software
Learning how to use a GIS has become essential for careers in urban planning.
Many cities now rely on a GIS to plan transportation networks and manage resources.
