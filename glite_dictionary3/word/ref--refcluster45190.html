ref

noun
chief official in a sport (informal)
Pronunciation: / rɛf / (AmE), / rɛf / (BrE)
(informal) the chief official in a sporting event, responsible for enforcing the rules and ensuring fair play.

Examples

the ref blows whistle
After the match, the losing team blamed the ref for their defeat.
Every ref knows how hard it is to make the right call under pressure.
The players argued for a penalty, but the ref refused to stop the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym – referee: the chief official in sports, ensuring fair play
  • synonym – umpire: an official who watches a game to enforce the rules (mainly used in cricket, baseball, etc.)
