dishonorableness

noun
lack of honor or respect
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɑnərəbəlnəs / (AmE), / dɪsˈɒnərəbəlnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being unworthy of honor or respect.

Examples

dishonorableness of actions
His repeated lies only added to the dishonorableness of his actions.
Such dishonorableness cannot be tolerated in a position of leadership.
The court condemned the dishonorableness displayed by the defendant.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dishonor: loss or lack of honor or respect
  • antonym – honorableness: the quality of deserving honor or respect
