break

noun
abrupt change in voice tone
Pronunciation: / breɪk / (AmE), / breɪk / (BrE)
an abrupt change in the tone or pitch of the voice, especially during puberty or because of strong emotion

Examples

a break in his voice
As he spoke about his childhood, there was a break in his voice.
During puberty, many boys experience a sudden break in their speech.
She tried to hide her emotions, but a break in her voice gave her away.
