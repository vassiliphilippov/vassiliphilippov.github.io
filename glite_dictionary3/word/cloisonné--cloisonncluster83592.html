cloisonné

adjective
decorated metalwork with enamel compartments
Pronunciation: / klɔɪˈsɑːneɪ / (AmE), / klwɑː.zɒˈneɪ / (BrE)
(of metalwork) decorated with colored enamel separated by thin metal strips, which are fired to create compartments of color.

Examples

cloisonne vase design
She admired the intricate patterns on the cloisonné jewelry.
The artisan explained how cloisonné enamel is created by filling metal compartments with colored glass paste.
The museum displayed a beautiful cloisonné vase from the Ming dynasty.
