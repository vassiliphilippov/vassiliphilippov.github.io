springiness

noun
quality of being elastic or springy
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋi.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being elastic or able to return to an original shape after being stretched or compressed.

Examples

springiness of the floor
Gymnasts rely on the springiness of the floor to perform their routines safely.
Over time, repeated use reduces the springiness of rubber bands.
The springiness of the new mattress made it especially comfortable to sleep on.

Related Terms

  • synonym – elasticity: the ability of an object or material to resume its normal shape
  • antonym – rigidity: the quality of being stiff and not easily bent or changed
