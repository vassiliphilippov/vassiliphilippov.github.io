codicil

noun
legal amendment to a will
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊ.dɪ.sɪl / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.dɪ.sɪl / (BrE)
a legal document that adds to or modifies the terms of an existing will

Examples

codicil to a will
If you wish to change your will, you can do so by adding a codicil.
She added a codicil to her will, specifying her donation to charity.
The lawyer prepared a codicil to update the beneficiary list in the client's will.
