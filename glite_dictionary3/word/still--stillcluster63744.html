still

adjective
not sparkling (of a drink)
Pronunciation: / stɪl / (AmE), / stɪl / (BrE)
(of a drink) not sparkling; lacking bubbles or effervescence, especially in reference to wine or water.

Examples

still water bottle
I prefer still water to sparkling when I eat out.
Many people enjoy a glass of still wine with their meal.
Please specify if you would like your mineral water still or sparkling.
