good

adverb
completely; thoroughly (informal adverb)
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd / (AmE), / ɡʊd / (BrE)
(informal, dialect) completely, thoroughly, or to a high standard (used as an adverb)

Examples

warned good enough

She said her ankle felt good after running for the first time since her injury.

You did pretty good on your presentation for someone who was so nervous.
