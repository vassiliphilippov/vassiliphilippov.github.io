to sniffle

verb
cry or complain with sniffling
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɪf.əl /; / ˈsnɪfəl / (AmE), / ˈsnɪf.əl /; / ˈsnɪfəl / (BrE)
to breathe noisily and repeatedly through the nose, usually because of a cold or while crying or complaining
