chocolate

noun
hot cocoa-based drink
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːklət / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət / (BrE)
A hot drink made by mixing cocoa powder, milk, and sugar, usually served hot.

Examples

drink hot chocolate
After sledding, the children warmed up with delicious chocolate made on the stove.
On cold winter nights, I love to drink a cup of chocolate by the fire.
She ordered a steaming mug of chocolate with whipped cream at the café.
