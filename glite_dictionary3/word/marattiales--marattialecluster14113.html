Marattiales

noun
an order of lower ferns
Pronunciation: / məˌrætiˈeɪliˌz / (AmE), / məˌrætɪˈeɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of lower ferns, coextensive with the family Marattiaceae, distinguished by large, fleshy rhizomes and sporangia usually in clusters

Examples

Marattiales ferns group
Marattiales are among the most ancient lineages of living ferns.
Botanists often study Marattiales to understand the evolution of vascular plants.
Many species within Marattiales are found in tropical regions worldwide.
