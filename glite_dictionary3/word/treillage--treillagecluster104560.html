treillage

noun
latticework support for climbing plants
Pronunciation: / treɪˈjɑʒ / (AmE), / treɪˈjɑːʒ / (BrE)
a framework of latticework, often made of wood or metal, used to support climbing plants

Examples

treillage garden wall
A decorative treillage can transform a plain wall into an elegant focal point.
The roses looked beautiful climbing over the treillage at the entrance to the garden.
They installed a sturdy treillage to support their growing ivy along the fence.
