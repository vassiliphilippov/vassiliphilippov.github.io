maple

noun
wood from the maple tree
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.pəl / (BrE)
The hard, close-grained wood of the maple tree, especially the sugar maple, used particularly for furniture and flooring.

Examples

maple wood furniture
The chef preferred a maple cutting board for its durability.
They installed maple flooring throughout the living room for a warm look.
This table is made from solid maple and has a beautiful grain.

Related Terms

  • synonym – oak: hardwood from the oak tree, often used in furniture
  • antonym – pine: softwood from the pine tree
