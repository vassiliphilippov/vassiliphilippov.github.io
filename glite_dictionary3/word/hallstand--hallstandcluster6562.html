hallstand

noun
furniture for coats, hats, umbrellas in halls
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔlˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈhɔːlˌstænd / (BrE)
a piece of furniture, typically placed in a hall or entranceway, designed for storing coats, hats, umbrellas, and often featuring a mirror.
