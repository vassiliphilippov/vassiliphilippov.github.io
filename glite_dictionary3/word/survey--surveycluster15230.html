to survey

verb
measure and map land
Pronunciation: / sərˈveɪ / (AmE), / səˈveɪ / (BrE)
to measure and map the boundaries and features of an area of land

Examples

to survey land boundaries
As part of their training, students learn how to survey rough terrain accurately.
Before construction began, engineers had to survey the entire site.
The company was hired to survey the farmland and establish property lines.
