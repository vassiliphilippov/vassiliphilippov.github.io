security

noun
tradable financial asset (e.g., share, bond)
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊrəti / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərəti / (BrE)
(finance) A tradable financial asset such as a share, bond, or other instrument, giving the holder the right to receive interest, dividends, or other financial benefits.

security bond
He prefers low-risk securities with a steady stream of interest payments.
Investors often diversify their portfolios by purchasing different types of securities.
