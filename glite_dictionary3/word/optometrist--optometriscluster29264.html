optometrist

noun
eye specialist who tests vision
Pronunciation: / ɑpˈtɑmətrɪst / (AmE), / ɒpˈtɒmətrɪst / (BrE)
A person trained to examine the eyes for vision defects and to prescribe corrective lenses.

Examples

optometrist checks eyes
I have an appointment with the optometrist to check my eyesight next week.
If you notice changes in your vision, you should consult an optometrist promptly.
The optometrist prescribed new glasses after my vision test.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ophthalmologist: a medical doctor specializing in the treatment of eye diseases and surgery
  • synonym – optician: a person qualified to make and supply glasses and contact lenses
