to unhorse

verb
to dismount from a horse
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɔrs / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɔːs / (BrE)
to cause to dismount from a horse; to remove or come down from a horse

Examples

to unhorse a rider
After the long ride, she carefully unhorsed and stretched her legs.
The knight managed to unhorse his opponent with a swift move.
The trainer told us how to unhorse safely after a lesson.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dismount: to get off a horse, bicycle, or other thing one is riding
