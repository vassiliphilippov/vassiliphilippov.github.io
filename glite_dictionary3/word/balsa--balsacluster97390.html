balsa

noun
light, strong wood from the balsa tree
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːl.sə / (AmE), / ˈbɔːl.sə / (BrE)
A lightweight, strong wood from the balsa tree, often used for making model airplanes, floats, and insulation.

Examples

balsa wood model
Life jackets can contain balsa wood to provide buoyancy.
Model airplanes are often constructed from balsa because it is light and easy to shape.
The artist used blocks of balsa for carving small sculptures.
