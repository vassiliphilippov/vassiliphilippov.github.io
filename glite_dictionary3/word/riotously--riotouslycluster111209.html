riotously

adverb
in an exuberant or unrestrained way
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪ.ə.təs.li / (AmE), / ˈraɪ.ət.əs.li /; / ˈraɪ.ə.təs.li / (BrE)
in a wild, lively, or uncontrolled way; with great excitement or energy

Examples
