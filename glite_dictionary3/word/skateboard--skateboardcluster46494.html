to skateboard

verb
ride on a skateboard
Pronunciation: / ˈskeɪtˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈskeɪtˌbɔːd / (BrE)
to ride on a flat board with small wheels attached, known as a skateboard

Examples

to skateboard in parks
He loves to skateboard around the park after school.
Many teenagers skateboard on the city streets during the summer.
She learned to skateboard when she was eight years old.
