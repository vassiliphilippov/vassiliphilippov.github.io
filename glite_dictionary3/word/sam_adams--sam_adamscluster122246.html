Sam Adams

proper noun
American Revolutionary leader and patriot
Pronunciation: / sæm ˈædəmz / (AmE), / sæm ˈædəmz / (BrE)
(1722–1803) An American Revolutionary leader and patriot, organizer of the Boston Tea Party and signer of the Declaration of Independence.
