to condemn

verb
express strong disapproval
Pronunciation: / kənˈdɛm / (AmE), / kənˈdɛm / (BrE)
to express very strong disapproval of; to criticize or denounce, often publicly

Examples

to condemn an action
Environmental groups are condemning the government's decision to cut funding for renewable energy projects.
Many citizens publicly condemned the company's unethical practices.
The mayor did not hesitate to condemn the violence at the protest.

Related Terms

  • antonym – approve: to officially agree to or accept as satisfactory
  • synonym – denounce: to publicly declare something to be wrong or evil
