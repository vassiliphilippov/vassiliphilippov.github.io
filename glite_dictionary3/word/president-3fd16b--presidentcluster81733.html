president

noun
chief officer of a college or university
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛzəˌdɛnt / (AmE), / ˈprɛzɪdənt / (BrE)
the head administrative officer of a college or university, responsible for overall management and leadership

Examples

president of a university
After serving as president for ten years, she decided to retire and focus on research.
The president of the university announced a new scholarship program for students.
The faculty met with the college president to discuss changes to the academic calendar.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chancellor: the head of a university in some countries, often ceremonial in the UK
  • synonym – principal: the head of a school or college, especially in the UK
