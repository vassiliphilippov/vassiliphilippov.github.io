slouchy

adjective
loose or lacking stiffness (esp. clothing or posture)
Pronunciation: / ˈslaʊtʃi / (AmE), / ˈslaʊtʃi / (BrE)
(of a garment or posture) lacking stiffness or structure; loose, relaxed, or tending to slouch.

Examples

slouchy jeans
He prefers slouchy jeans because they feel more comfortable than fitted ones.
She wore a slouchy sweater that hung loosely around her shoulders.
The chair's slouchy design made it perfect for relaxing in the living room.

Related Terms

  • synonym – baggy: (of clothing) loose and hanging in folds
  • antonym – rigid: stiff, not flexible or loose
