jaunt

noun
pleasure trip or excursion
Pronunciation: / dʒɑnt / (AmE), / dʒɑːnt / (BrE)

Examples

jaunt to the beach
A spontaneous jaunt to the beach helped them relax after a busy week.
Our afternoon jaunt through the market was the highlight of the trip.
They decided to take a quick jaunt to the countryside over the weekend.
