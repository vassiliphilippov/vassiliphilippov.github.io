Shitler

noun
derogatory insult likening someone to Hitler using vulgar language
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪtlɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtlə / (BrE)
A highly offensive and vulgar insult for a leader, formed by blending 'shit' and 'Hitler,' intended to equate the person with both incompetence and tyranny.

Using the word Shitler in public discourse is guaranteed to provoke strong reactions and controversy.
