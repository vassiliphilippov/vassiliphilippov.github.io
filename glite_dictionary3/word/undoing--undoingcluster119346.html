undoing

noun
reversal or cancellation of an earlier action
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈduɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈduːɪŋ / (BrE)
an act of reversing or cancelling the effect of a previous action

Examples

the undoing of progress
Her apology was the undoing of all the progress they had made.
The court's decision amounted to the undoing of the previous verdict.
The new law was intended as the undoing of the reforms introduced last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – reversal: an act of changing to the opposite direction or position
