right-hander

noun
person who primarily uses right hand
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtˌhændər /; / ˌraɪtˈhændər / (AmE), / ˈraɪtˌhændə /; / ˌraɪtˈhændə / (BrE)
a person who uses their right hand more skillfully or frequently than their left
