to turn

verb
to make or earn (profit, etc.)
Pronunciation: / tɝn / (AmE), / tɜːn / (BrE)
(of a business or person) to make or earn (a profit or amount of money), especially through buying and selling.

Examples

turn a profit
After several failures, they finally turned a significant income from their investment.
She hopes the new venture will turn a much better profit next quarter.
The business managed to turn a small profit in its first year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – earn: to receive (money) in return for work or services
  • antonym – lose: to fail to keep or maintain (money, profit, etc.)
