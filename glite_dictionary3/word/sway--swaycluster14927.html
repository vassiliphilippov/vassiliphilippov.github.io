sway

noun
controlling influence or power
Pronunciation: / sweɪ / (AmE), / sweɪ / (BrE)
power or influence that controls or affects people, decisions, or events.

Examples

sway over people
After the election, the new president was said to hold considerable sway over public opinion.
For decades, the family maintained their sway in the region’s politics.
Religious leaders have lost much of their sway over the younger generation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – control: the power to direct or regulate something
  • synonym – influence: the capacity to have an effect on the character, development, or behaviour of someone or something
