parquet

noun
main floor seating area in a theater
Pronunciation: / pɑrˈkeɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkeɪ / (BrE)
the seating area on the main floor of a theater, located between the orchestra section and the parquet circle

Examples

Our seats in the parquet provided an excellent view of the stage.
She preferred the parquet to the balcony for its proximity to the actors.
The parquet was quickly filled as theatergoers arrived for the show.
