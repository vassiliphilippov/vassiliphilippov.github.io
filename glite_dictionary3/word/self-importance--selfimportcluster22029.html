self-importance

noun
exaggerated sense of one's importance
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf ɪmˈpɔrtəns /; / ˌsɛlf.ɪmˈpɔɹ.təns / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ɪmˈpɔːtəns /; / ˌsɛlf.ɪmˈpɔː.təns / (BrE)
an exaggerated sense of your own importance or value; thinking you are more important than you really are
