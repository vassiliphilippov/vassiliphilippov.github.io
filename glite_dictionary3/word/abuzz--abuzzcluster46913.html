abuzz

adjective
full of buzzing, excitement, or activity
Pronunciation: / əˈbʌz / (AmE), / əˈbʌz / (BrE)
filled with a continuous, excited, or busy noise, like the sound of buzzing bees; lively with activity or conversation.

Examples

abuzz with excitement
Social media became abuzz after the celebrity made the announcement.
The entire office was abuzz with news of the major merger.
The garden was abuzz with bees during the spring morning.
