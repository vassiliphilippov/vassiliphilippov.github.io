to thrash

verb
defeat decisively in a competition
Pronunciation: / θræʃ / (AmE), / θræʃ / (BrE)
to defeat someone completely and decisively, especially in a competition or fight

Examples

to thrash a rival team
Our football team thrashed the defending champions 5–0 last night.
The young chess prodigy thrashed all of his opponents in the tournament.
Their debate team has thrashed every rival this season.

Related Terms

  • synonym – beat: to defeat someone in a competition or fight
  • antonym – lose: to be defeated in a competition
  • synonym – trounce: to defeat very thoroughly, especially in a contest
