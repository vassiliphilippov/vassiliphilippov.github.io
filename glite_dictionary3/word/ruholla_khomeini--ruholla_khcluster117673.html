Ruholla Khomeini

proper noun
Iranian religious and political leader (1900–1989)
Pronunciation: / ruːˈhoʊlɑː koʊˈmeɪni / (AmE), / ruːˈhɒlə kəʊˈmeɪni / (BrE)
