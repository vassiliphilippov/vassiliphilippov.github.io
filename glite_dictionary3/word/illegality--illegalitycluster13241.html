illegality

noun
the state of being illegal
Pronunciation: / ɪləˈɡæləti / (AmE), / ɪlɪˈɡalɪti / (BrE)
The state or condition of being illegal; the quality of being contrary to law or in violation of a legal statute.

Examples

company illegality
Allegations of illegality surfaced during the company’s financial audit.
He was unaware of the illegality of downloading copyrighted material without permission.
The illegality of their actions led to a lengthy court case.

Related Terms

  • antonym – legality: the state or quality of being in accordance with the law
  • synonym – unlawfulness: the quality or state of not being lawful
