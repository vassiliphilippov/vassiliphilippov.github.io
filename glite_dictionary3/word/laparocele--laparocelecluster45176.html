laparocele

noun
abdominal wall hernia
Pronunciation: / ˈlæpəroʊˌsiːl / (AmE), / ˈlæpərəʊˌsiːl / (BrE)
A hernia in which abdominal contents protrude through the abdominal wall.

Examples

laparocele after surgery
A laparocele can sometimes develop at the site of an abdominal incision.
The surgeon diagnosed the pain as resulting from a laparocele after a previous operation.
Treatment for a laparocele usually involves surgical repair of the hernia.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hernia: the protrusion of an organ through the structure that normally contains it
