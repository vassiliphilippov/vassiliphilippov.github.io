correctness

noun
conformity to social expectations
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɛktnəs / (AmE), / kəˈrɛktnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of behaving or speaking in a way that conforms to accepted social conventions or expectations

Examples

correctness in behavior
He valued social correctness and always followed proper etiquette.
Her correctness at the formal dinner was noticed and praised by everyone.
The teacher emphasized the importance of correctness in both language and manners.

Related Terms

  • antonym – incorrectness: the state of not conforming to standards or expectations
  • synonym – propriety: the state of conforming to conventional standards of behavior or morals
