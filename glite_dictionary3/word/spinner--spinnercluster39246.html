spinner

noun
board game device with a spinning pointer
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnər / (AmE), / ˈspɪnə / (BrE)
a device used in board games, consisting of a dial and a pointer that is spun to determine a random outcome or the next move

Examples

game spinner
Players take turns spinning the spinner to see how many spaces to move.
Some board games use dice instead of a spinner to introduce chance.
The spinner landed on red, so she had to draw a card.
