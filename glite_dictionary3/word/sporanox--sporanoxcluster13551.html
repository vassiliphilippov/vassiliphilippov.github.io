Sporanox

noun
trademarked oral antifungal drug
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔːrəˌnɑks / (AmE), / ˈspɒrəˌnɒks / (BrE)
A trademarked oral antifungal medication used to treat fungal infections, especially of the nails.

Sporanox treats infections
