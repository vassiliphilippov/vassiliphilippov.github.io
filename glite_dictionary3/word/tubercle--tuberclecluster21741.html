tubercle

noun
small rounded protuberance on a plant
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːbərkəl / (AmE), / ˈtjuːbəkəl / (BrE)
a small, rounded, wartlike protuberance on a plant, especially one found on roots or stems

Examples

tubercle on a root
Several tubercles can be seen on the surface of the plant root.
The stem develops tubercles that help in nitrogen fixation.
You can identify this species by the small tubercle at each leaf base.
