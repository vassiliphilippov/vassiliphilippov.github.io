glyceryl trinitrate

noun
explosive chemical; used as a vasodilator
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɪsəriːl traɪˈnaɪtreɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪsərɪl traɪˈnaɪtreɪt / (BrE)
A heavy, yellow, poisonous, oily, explosive liquid made by nitrating glycerol, used in explosives and as a vasodilator in medicine.

Examples

glyceryl trinitrate tablets for angina
Doctors often prescribe glyceryl trinitrate in tablet or spray form for angina.
Factories that manufacture glyceryl trinitrate must follow strict safety precautions due to its explosive nature.
The patient was given glyceryl trinitrate to relieve his chest pain.
