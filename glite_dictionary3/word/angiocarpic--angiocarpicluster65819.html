angiocarpic

adjective
fruit enclosed in a shell or husk
Pronunciation: / ˌændʒioʊˈkɑːrpɪk / (AmE), / ˌandʒɪəʊˈkɑːpɪk / (BrE)
(of a fruit) having the seeds enclosed within a shell, husk, or pericarp.
