readability

noun
quality of being easy to read
Pronunciation: / ˌriːdəˈbɪlɪti /; / ˌriːdəˈbɪlət̬i / (AmE), / ˌriːdəˈbɪlɪti /; / ˌriːdəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of writing or print that is easy to read and understand
