groundsman

noun
person who maintains outdoor grounds
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraʊndzmæn / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊn(d)zmən / (BrE)
a person employed to take care of the grounds of a park, estate, or sports facility

Examples

groundsman maintains field
After the storm, the groundsman cleared fallen branches from the football field.
The groundsman arrived early to mow the grass and trim the hedges.
The park's groundsman takes great pride in keeping the gardens immaculate.

Related Terms

  • synonym – groundskeeper: person who maintains the grounds of a property (esp. in American English)
