suspiciously

adverb
in a suspicious manner
Pronunciation: / səˈspɪʃəsli / (AmE), / səˈspɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows distrust or doubt; with suspicion.

Examples

look suspiciously at him
If someone offers you a deal that seems too good to be true, look at it suspiciously.
She glanced suspiciously at the stranger lingering by the door.
The manager watched the transaction suspiciously, unsure of the customer's intent.
