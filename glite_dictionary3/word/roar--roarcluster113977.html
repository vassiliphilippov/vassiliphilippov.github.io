to roar

verb
laugh loudly and heartily
Pronunciation: / rɔr / (AmE), / rɔː / (BrE)
to laugh very loudly and uncontrollably, often with great enjoyment

Examples

to roar with laughter
Everyone roared at his unexpected impersonation during the party.
She roared when she heard the hilarious story.
The audience roared with laughter at the comedian's jokes.
