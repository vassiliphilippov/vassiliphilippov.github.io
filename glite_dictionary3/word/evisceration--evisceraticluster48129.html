evisceration

noun
removal of internal organs
Pronunciation: / ɪˌvɪsəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌvɪsəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the removal of internal organs or the contents of an organ from a body, especially in a medical or surgical context
