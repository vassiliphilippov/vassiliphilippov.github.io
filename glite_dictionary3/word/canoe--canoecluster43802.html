canoe

noun
narrow boat propelled by paddle
Pronunciation: / kəˈnu / (AmE), / kəˈnjuː / (BrE)
A narrow, lightweight boat with pointed ends, typically propelled by paddling.

Examples

paddle a canoe
During our vacation, we rented a canoe to explore the river.
He learned how to steer a canoe when he was a child.
They paddled the canoe across the calm lake at sunrise.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – boat: a vessel for traveling on water
  • synonym – kayak: a small, narrow watercraft typically with a covered deck, paddled with a double-bladed paddle
