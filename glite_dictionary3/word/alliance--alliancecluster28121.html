alliance

noun
connection by kinship, marriage, or common interest
Pronunciation: / əˈlaɪəns / (AmE), / əˈlaɪəns / (BrE)
a relationship or connection formed between people, groups, or nations, often by kinship, marriage, or shared interests

Examples

alliance by marriage
Business leaders sometimes create an alliance to pursue common interests.
Political parties may enter an alliance before an important election to increase their chances of success.
The two families formed an alliance through marriage to strengthen their social ties.
