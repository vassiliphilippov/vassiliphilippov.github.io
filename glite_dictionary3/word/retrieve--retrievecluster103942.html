to retrieve

verb
recall from memory
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtriv / (AmE), / rɪˈtriːv / (BrE)
to bring a memory, fact, or piece of information back into your mind; to recall something mentally

Examples

to retrieve a memory
Despite the stress, she managed to retrieve the details from her memory.
He could not retrieve the name of his first-grade teacher, no matter how hard he tried.
When asked about the incident, she tried to retrieve exactly what had happened.

Related Terms

  • antonym – forget: to fail to remember; to be unable to recall
  • synonym – remember: to bring something back to mind; to recall
