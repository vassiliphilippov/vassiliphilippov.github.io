Monteverdi

proper noun
Italian composer (1567–1643)
Pronunciation: / ˌmɒntiˈvɛrdi / (AmE), / ˌmɒntiˈvɛːdi / (BrE)
(1567–1643) Italian composer whose work marks the transition from the Renaissance to the Baroque era in music.
