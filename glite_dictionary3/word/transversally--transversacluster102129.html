transversally

adverb
in a transverse or crosswise manner
Pronunciation: / trænzˈvɝːsəli / (AmE), / trænzˈvɜːsəli / (BrE)
In a way that is across or at right angles to the main direction; in a transverse manner.

Examples

transversally across a surface
In anatomy, the CT scan image is taken transversally through the body.
The fibers were arranged transversally to provide extra strength.
The road cuts transversally across the valley, connecting the villages on either side.
