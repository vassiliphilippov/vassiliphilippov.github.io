barrenwort

noun
a creeping perennial used as ground cover
Pronunciation: / ˈbær.ənˌwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈbær.ənˌwɜːt / (BrE)
a slow-growing perennial plant with creeping rhizomes and semi-evergreen leaves on wiry stems, often used as ground cover in gardens

Examples

barrenwort ground cover
Gardeners often choose barrenwort for its ability to thrive in dry, shady spots.
In early spring, delicate flowers appear on the barrenwort before the leaves fully emerge.
The shady border was filled with barrenwort, creating a lush green carpet.
