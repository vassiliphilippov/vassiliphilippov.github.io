Felis serval

noun
African wildcat species (serval)
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːlɪs ˈsɜːrvəl / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪs ˈsɜːvəl / (BrE)
A slender, long-legged African wildcat with large ears and a tawny, black-spotted coat, also known as the serval.

Examples

Felis serval hunts birds
The Felis serval is known for its remarkable jumping ability when hunting birds in tall grass.
Unlike domestic cats, the Felis serval has much longer legs and larger ears.
Wildlife reserves in Africa often provide a sanctuary for the Felis serval.
