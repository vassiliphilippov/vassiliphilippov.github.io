fritter

noun
food fried in batter with filling
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈfrɪtə / (BrE)
A piece of food, such as fruit, meat, or vegetables, coated in batter and deep-fried.

Examples

apple fritter
She ordered an apple fritter and a cup of coffee for breakfast.
The menu featured several types of fritter, including ones filled with cheese and ham.
Vegetable fritters make a delicious appetizer or side dish.
