to greet

verb
to say hello or welcome someone
Pronunciation: / ɡrit / (AmE), / ɡriːt / (BrE)
to say hello or welcome someone when you meet or communicate with them

Examples

to greet a friend
He greeted me warmly after not seeing me for years.
She always greets her colleagues with a smile in the morning.
They stood at the door to greet their guests as they arrived.
As soon as I entered the room, she greeted me with a warm smile.
He always greets his neighbors when he sees them in the morning.
We were greeted at the door by a friendly receptionist.

Related Terms

  • antonym – farewell: an expression of good wishes when parting
  • synonym – welcome: to greet someone in a friendly way upon arrival
