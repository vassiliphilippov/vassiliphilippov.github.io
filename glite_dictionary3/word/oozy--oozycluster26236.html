oozy

adjective
leaking slowly; full of ooze
Pronunciation: / ˈuːzi / (AmE), / ˈuːzi / (BrE)
slowly leaking out or seeping, especially in regard to liquid or semi-liquid substances; resembling or containing ooze.

Examples

oozy mud
After the rain, we stepped in an oozy patch of ground that stuck to our boots.
The cake was deliciously moist, with an oozy layer of chocolate in the center.
The wound looked oozy, so the nurse cleaned it carefully before applying a bandage.
