vena lacrimalis

noun
vein draining the lacrimal gland
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪnə ləˌkraɪˈmeɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˈviːnə læˌkraɪˈmælɪs / (BrE)
A vein that drains blood from the lacrimal gland and empties into the superior ophthalmic vein.

Examples

Inflammation near the vena lacrimalis can lead to complications affecting the eye.
