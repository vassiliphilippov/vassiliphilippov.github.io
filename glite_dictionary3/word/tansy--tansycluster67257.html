tansy

noun
aromatic Eurasian herb with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈtænzi / (AmE), / ˈtanzi / (BrE)
a common perennial aromatic herb (Tanacetum vulgare), native to Eurasia, with buttonlike yellow flower heads and bitter-tasting leaves, sometimes used medicinally

Examples

tansy plant leaves
Clusters of tansy added a splash of yellow to the wildflower meadow.
