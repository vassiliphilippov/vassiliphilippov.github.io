thinkable

adjective
that can be imagined or considered
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪŋkəbəl / (AmE), / ˈθɪŋkəbl̩ / (BrE)
Able to be imagined, conceived, or considered as a possibility.

Examples

every thinkable possibility
Every thinkable possibility was discussed during the meeting.
It's hardly thinkable that such an event could happen in our quiet town.
The idea seemed barely thinkable to anyone at the time.

  • antonym – unthinkable: impossible to imagine or accept
