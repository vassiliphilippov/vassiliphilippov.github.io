ping

noun
short electronic alert or notification
Pronunciation: / pɪŋ / (AmE), / pɪŋ / (BrE)
a brief electronic message, alert, or notification sent to someone, especially to prompt a response or bring something to their attention

Examples

a quick ping notification
He missed the meeting because he didn’t see the ping on his phone.
I got a ping when Sarah replied to the group chat.
The app sends you a ping for upcoming events so you never miss anything important.

  • synonym – notification: an electronic message alerting someone to new information or events
