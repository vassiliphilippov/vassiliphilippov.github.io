honest

adjective
genuine; without pretension
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːnəst / (AmE), / ˈɒnɪst / (BrE)
Simple, genuine, and without pretension; not elaborate or showy.

Examples

honest meal
He preferred an honest job that did not require showing off.
The inn provided honest comfort rather than luxury.
We enjoyed a meal of honest home-cooked food.

Related Terms

  • synonym – genuine: truly what it is said to be; authentic
  • antonym – pretentious: attempting to impress by affecting greater importance or merit
