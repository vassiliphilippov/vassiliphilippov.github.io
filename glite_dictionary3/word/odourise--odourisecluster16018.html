to odourise

verb
add a smell to something
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊdəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈəʊdəˌraɪz / (BrE)
to cause something to have a smell, especially by adding a scent or odor

Examples

to odourise natural gas
Natural gas companies odourise gas so leaks can be detected easily.
Some chemicals are deliberately odourised for safety reasons.
The manufacturer decided to odourise the product with a fresh citrus scent.
