to simplify

verb
make simpler or easier
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪm.pləˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪm.plɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
to make something simpler, easier to understand, or less complicated

Examples

to simplify a process
He simplified his lifestyle by selling unnecessary possessions.
The teacher tried to simplify the complex math problems for her students.
They hope to simplify the registration process next year.

  • antonym – complicate: to make something more complex or difficult
