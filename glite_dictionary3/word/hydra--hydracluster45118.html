Hydra

noun
genus of small freshwater animals
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdrə / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪdrə / (BrE)
A genus of small, freshwater animals with tentacles, known for their regenerative ability, belonging to the phylum Cnidaria.
