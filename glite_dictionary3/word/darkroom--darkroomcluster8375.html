darkroom

noun
room for developing photographs
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑrkˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈdɑːkˌruːm / (BrE)
a room from which normal light is excluded, used for developing photographs or film

Examples

photo darkroom
He spent hours in the darkroom developing his latest batch of photographs.
Our school still has a fully equipped darkroom for the photography club.
The smell of chemicals filled the darkroom as the film was processed.
