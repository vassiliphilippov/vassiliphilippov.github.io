to skin

verb
scrape or injure the skin's surface
Pronunciation: / skɪn / (AmE), / skɪn / (BrE)
to injure or scrape the surface of the skin, especially by rubbing or falling

Examples

to skin your knee
Be careful not to skin your knuckles when using that tool.
She skinned her knee when she tripped on the pavement.
The child skinned his elbow while climbing the tree.

  • synonym – graze: to scrape and break the surface of the skin
