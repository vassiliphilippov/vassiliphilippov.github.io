riskless

adjective
free of risk or danger
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪs.kləs / (AmE), / ˈrɪs.kləs / (BrE)
Having no risk; free from the possibility of loss, harm, or danger.

Examples

riskless investment
She preferred the riskless route to success, avoiding any ventures that might lead to failure.
This government bond is regarded as a riskless investment by many financial experts.
While no business is truly riskless, some industries are considered more stable than others.
