kudu

noun
African spiral-horned antelope
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːdu / (AmE), / ˈkuːduː / (BrE)
either of two large African antelopes with twisted, spiraling horns and distinctive white body stripes, found in woodland and savannah regions

Examples

a kudu with horns
Local guides explained that the kudu is shy and prefers to browse in dense brush.
The male kudu is easily recognized by its long, spiraled horns and white stripes.
We spotted a kudu moving quietly among the acacia trees during our safari.
