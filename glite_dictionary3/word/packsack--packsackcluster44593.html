packsack

noun
bag carried on back or shoulder
Pronunciation: / ˈpækˌsæk / (AmE), / ˈpækˌsæk / (BrE)
a bag, often with shoulder straps, carried on the back or over the shoulder, used for carrying supplies

Examples

packsack with supplies
He slung his packsack over his shoulder before heading out for the day.
She stuffed the groceries into her old packsack before leaving the shop.
Travelers often use a durable packsack to carry their gear on hiking trips.
