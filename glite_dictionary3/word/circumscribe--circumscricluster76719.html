to circumscribe

verb
to draw a shape around another in geometry
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrkəmˌskraɪb /; / ˌsɜrkəmˈskraɪb / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkəmskraɪb /; / ˌsɜːkəmˈskraɪb / (BrE)
to draw a shape (especially a circle) around another shape so that it touches at all possible points, especially in geometry
