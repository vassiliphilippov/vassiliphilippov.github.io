to tincture

verb
fill or permeate with a quality
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋktʃər / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋktʃə / (BrE)
to fill or permeate something with a particular quality or substance, especially in a subtle or slight way

Examples

to tincture memories
A subtle melancholy tinctured his memories of childhood.
Her voice was tinctured with regret as she spoke of the past.
The evening sky was tinctured with shades of orange and pink.

Related Terms

  • synonym – imbue: to fill or permeate with a feeling or quality
  • synonym – permeate: to spread or diffuse through
