half

adjective
one of two equal parts
Pronunciation: / hæf / (AmE), / hɑːf / (BrE)
Being one of two equal parts into which something is divided; consisting of one of two equivalent parts in value or quantity.

Examples

half a sandwich
I only ate half the sandwich because I wasn't very hungry.
She asked for a half portion to save room for dessert.
We waited for half an hour before our names were called.

  • antonym – whole: entire; in one piece; not divided into parts
