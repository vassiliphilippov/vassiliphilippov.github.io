to present

verb
formally give or hand over
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzɛnt / (AmE), / prɪˈzɛnt / (BrE)
to give or hand over something formally, especially as a gift, honor, or in a ceremony

Examples

to present an award
Please present your ticket at the entrance for verification.
She was proud to present her findings to the board of directors.
The principal will present the award to the top student at the ceremony.
At the meeting, the chairman presented a commemorative plaque to the founder.
They decided to present her with a bouquet at the end of the ceremony.
We would like to present this scholarship to an outstanding student.
She is invited to present the trophy to the winning team.
The principal will present the diplomas at the graduation ceremony.
We were honored when the mayor presented us with the community service award.

Related Terms

  • synonym – award: to give something as a prize or honor
  • synonym – gift: something given willingly to someone without payment; a present
  • synonym – give: to hand over or offer something to someone
  • synonym – grant: to give or bestow officially
  • antonym – receive: to be given or presented with something
