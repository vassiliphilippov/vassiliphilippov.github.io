punkie

noun
small bloodsucking insect, biting midge
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈpʌŋki / (BrE)
A very small two-winged biting insect that feeds on the blood of mammals, birds, and sometimes other insects; also known as a biting midge or no-see-um.

Examples

biting punkie insect
She woke up covered in itchy bites from the tiny punkies that came through the screen.
The marsh near the lake was full of punkies in the summer.
We couldn't have a picnic outside because the punkies were biting everyone.
