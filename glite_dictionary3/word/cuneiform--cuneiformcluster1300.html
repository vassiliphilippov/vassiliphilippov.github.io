cuneiform

noun
ancient wedge-shaped writing system
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːni.əˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈkjuːnɪ.ɪfɔːm / (BrE)
an ancient writing system with wedge-shaped characters, used in Mesopotamia and Persia

Examples

cuneiform writing system
Archaeologists discovered tablets covered in cuneiform script at the excavation site.
Learning to decipher cuneiform opened a window into early Mesopotamian civilization.
The ancient Sumerians used cuneiform to record their laws and transactions.
