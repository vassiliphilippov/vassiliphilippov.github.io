to diphthongize

verb
change a vowel to a diphthong
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪfˌθɑŋˌaɪz / (AmE), / ˈdɪfθɒŋaɪz / (BrE)
(of a vowel) to change from a single, simple sound (monophthong) to a diphthong; to pronounce as a diphthong.

Examples

to diphthongize a vowel
Some English long vowels diphthongize over time, especially in certain dialects.
The linguist explained how the sound system of Old English began to diphthongize certain vowels.
When borrowed into English, this word’s original vowel diphthongized to fit native pronunciation patterns.
