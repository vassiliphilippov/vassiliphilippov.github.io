backbend

noun
acrobatic movement bending backward to the floor
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌbɛnd / (AmE), / ˈbækˌbɛnd / (BrE)
An acrobatic movement in which a person bends their trunk backward from a standing position until their hands touch the floor.
