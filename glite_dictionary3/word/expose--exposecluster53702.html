to expose

verb
allow light to affect film or sensor
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspoʊz / (AmE), / ɪkˈspəʊz / (BrE)
to allow light to reach photographic film or a camera sensor, usually in order to create an image

Examples

to expose film to light
Modern cameras automatically expose the sensor according to the lighting conditions.
She accidentally exposed the roll to sunlight, ruining all the photographs.
To properly capture the details, you must expose the film for the correct amount of time.

Related Terms

  • synonym – photograph: to capture an image with a camera by exposing film or a sensor to light
