harbour

noun
refuge or place of safety
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrbɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːbə / (BrE)
A place of refuge, comfort, or security, especially where someone feels protected or safe.

Examples

harbour of safety
For many, the church became a harbour from the storms of life.
He sought a harbour where he could rest and recover.
In times of trouble, her family home was her harbour.

Related Terms

  • antonym – danger: exposure or vulnerability to harm
  • synonym – refuge: a place providing shelter or protection from danger
  • synonym – sanctuary: a safe place, especially one of refuge or protection
