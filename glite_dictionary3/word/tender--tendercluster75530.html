tender

noun
small boat for ship-to-shore transport
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛndɚ /; / ˈtɛn.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɛndə /; / ˈten.də / (BrE)
a small boat, ship, or railroad car used to support, supply, or transport people or goods to a larger vessel or engine
