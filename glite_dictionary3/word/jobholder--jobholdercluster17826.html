jobholder

noun
person with a regular job
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑbˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒbˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person who holds a regular, paid job, especially as an employee in an organization

Examples

full-time jobholder
As a jobholder, she is entitled to paid vacation and health benefits.
The company's jobholders are required to attend the safety workshop every year.
The survey aimed to measure the satisfaction levels of jobholders in various industries.
