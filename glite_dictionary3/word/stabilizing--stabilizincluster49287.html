stabilizing

adjective
making something stable
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪbəˌlaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsteɪbɪlaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
causing something to become stable or less likely to change suddenly

Examples

stabilizing force
A good leader can be a stabilizing force during times of crisis.
Adding sandbags along the riverbank had a stabilizing influence on the eroding shoreline.
The government introduced new policies with a stabilizing effect on the economy.
