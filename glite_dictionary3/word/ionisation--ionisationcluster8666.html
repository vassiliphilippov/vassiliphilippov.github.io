ionisation

noun
process of forming ions
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪ.ə.nəˈzeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌaɪə.nəˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.ə.naɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process in which atoms or molecules become ions, usually by gaining or losing electrons due to energy such as heat, radiation, or electric fields
