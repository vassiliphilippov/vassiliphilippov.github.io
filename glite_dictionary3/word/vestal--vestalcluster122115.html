vestal

adjective
relating to Vesta (Roman goddess)
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛstəl / (AmE), / ˈvɛstəl / (BrE)
of or relating to the Roman goddess Vesta or her priestesses.

Examples

vestal priestess
In ancient Rome, the vestal rites were performed only by specially chosen priestesses.
Many temples included a vestal altar dedicated to household gods.
The painter depicted a vestal figure standing by a sacred fire.
