hopeful

noun
an aspiring person, candidate, or competitor
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊpfəl / (AmE), / ˈhəʊpfəl / (BrE)

young hopeful

There were more than fifty hopefuls auditioning for the lead role in the musical.
