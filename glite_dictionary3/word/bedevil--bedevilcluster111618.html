to bedevil

verb
to confuse or perplex persistently
Pronunciation: / bɪˈdɛvəl / (AmE), / bɪˈdɛvəl / (BrE)
to cause someone great confusion or difficulty; to perplex or bewilder persistently

Examples

to bedevil a project
Technical problems continued to bedevil the research team throughout the year.
The ambiguous instructions only served to bedevil the new employees.
Unanswered questions about the policy still bedevil the committee.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bewilder: to cause to become perplexed and confused
  • antonym – clarify: to make (an idea or statement) clear
  • synonym – confuse: cause (someone) to become bewildered or perplexed
