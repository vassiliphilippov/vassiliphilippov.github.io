curtsey

noun
a respectful gesture by women involving bending the knees
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜːrtsi / (AmE), / ˈkɜːtsi / (BrE)
a respectful gesture performed by bending the knees with one foot forward, traditionally made by women

Examples

a graceful curtsey
After the dance, all the performers ended with a curtsey and a smile.
She made a curtsey to the queen during the ceremony.
The young girl gave a graceful curtsey before leaving the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bow: a gesture of bending at the waist, typically by men as a sign of respect
