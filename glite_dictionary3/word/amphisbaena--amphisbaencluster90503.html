Amphisbaena

noun
genus of worm lizards
Pronunciation: / ˌæm.fɪsˈbiː.nə / (AmE), / ˌam.fɪsˈbiː.nə / (BrE)
A genus of worm lizards, the type genus of the family Amphisbaenidae, known for their elongated, limbless bodies.
