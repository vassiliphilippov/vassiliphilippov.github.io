to bejewel

verb
adorn with jewels or stones
Pronunciation: / bɪˈdʒuːəl / (AmE), / bɪˈdʒuːəl / (BrE)

The designer loved to bejewel evening gowns with sparkling crystals for added glamour.
They plan to bejewel the costume masks for the festival parade.
