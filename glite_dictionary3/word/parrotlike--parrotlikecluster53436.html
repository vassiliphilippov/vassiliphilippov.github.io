parrotlike

adjective
mechanically imitated or repeated
Pronunciation: / ˈpærətˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpærətlaɪk / (BrE)
(of speech or behavior) mechanically imitated or repeated without thought or understanding, like that of a parrot.

Examples

parrotlike repetition
He answered with a parrotlike repetition of what he'd heard on the news.
The children repeated the pledge in a parrotlike fashion every morning.
The student's parrotlike recitation of facts showed little real understanding.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mechanical: done without thought or spontaneity; automatic
  • synonym – rote: memorization or repetition without understanding
