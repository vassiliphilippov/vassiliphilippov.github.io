sheet

noun
piece of bed linen; cloth for bed
Pronunciation: / ʃit / (AmE), / ʃiːt / (BrE)
a large rectangular piece of cloth used on a bed, usually in pairs, to cover a mattress or to lie under while sleeping

Examples

sheet on a bed
He prefers to sleep under a cotton sheet in the summer.
She changed the sheet on the bed before her guests arrived.
The hotel provides fresh sheets every day.
