audit

noun
official inspection of financial records
Pronunciation: / ˈɔdɪt / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪt / (BrE)
an official inspection of an organization's financial accounts and records, typically by an independent body

Examples

financial audit report
A government audit revealed discrepancies in the public spending records.
Before applying for a loan, the business ordered an external audit of its accounts.
The company underwent an annual audit to ensure its financial statements were accurate.
