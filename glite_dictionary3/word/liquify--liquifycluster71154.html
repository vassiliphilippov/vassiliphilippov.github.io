to liquify

verb
become liquid (esp. when heated)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwəˌfaɪ /; / ˈlɪkwɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪfʌɪ /; / ˈlɪkwɪfaɪ / (BrE)

to liquify solid metal
