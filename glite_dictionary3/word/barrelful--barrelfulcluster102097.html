barrelful

noun
amount held by a barrel
Pronunciation: / ˈbærəlˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈbærəlˌfʊl / (BrE)
The amount that can be contained in a barrel of any size.

Examples

a barrelful of water
After the rainstorm, the cellar was flooded with a barrelful of water.
The brewery produced a barrelful of ale for the village festival.
They harvested a barrelful of apples from the orchard this year.
