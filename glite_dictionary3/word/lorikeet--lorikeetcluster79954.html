lorikeet

noun
small brightly colored parrot of Australasia
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔrɪˌkit / (AmE), / ˈlɒrɪˌkiːt / (BrE)
A small, brightly colored parrot native to Australasia, known for its slender tongue adapted for feeding on nectar and soft fruits.

Examples

colorful lorikeet feathers
A rainbow lorikeet perched on the branch, its vivid feathers shimmering in the sunlight.
As a pet, the lorikeet requires a special diet rich in fruit and nectar.
Many Australian gardens attract lorikeets with fruit and nectar-based feeders.
