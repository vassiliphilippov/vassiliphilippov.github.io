abandonment

noun
withdrawal of support or help
Pronunciation: / əˈbændənmənt / (AmE), / əˈbændənmənt / (BrE)
the act of withdrawing support or help from someone to whom one owes allegiance or responsibility

Examples

family abandonment
After his abandonment of the company during its crisis, his reputation suffered greatly.
Many animals suffer from abandonment when their owners move away.
The sudden abandonment of the project left everyone confused and frustrated.
