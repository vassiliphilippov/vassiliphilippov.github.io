Anne Robert Jacques Turgot

proper noun
French economist and reformer under Louis XVI
Pronunciation: / æn roʊˈbɛr ʒæk turˈgoʊ / (AmE), / æn rɒˈbɛː ʒak tʊəˈɡoʊ / (BrE)
(1727–1781) A French economist and statesman who served as Controller-General of Finances under Louis XVI and advocated for economic reforms, notably the abolition of feudal privileges.
