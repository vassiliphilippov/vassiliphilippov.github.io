to illuminate

verb
to shine light on; light up
Pronunciation: / ɪˈluːməˌneɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈljuːmɪneɪt / (BrE)
to light up or make something visible by shining light on it

Examples

The lanterns illuminated the garden with a soft, golden glow.
