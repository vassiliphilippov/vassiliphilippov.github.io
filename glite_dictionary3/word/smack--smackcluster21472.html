to smack

verb
hit with a sharp, forceful blow
Pronunciation: / smæk / (AmE), / smæk / (BrE)
to hit someone or something with a sharp, forceful blow, usually with the open hand

Examples

to smack the ball
He smacked the ball with all his strength during the game.
She smacked his hand away when he tried to take her notebook.
The frustrated parent smacked the table to get everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – punch: to hit with a closed fist
  • synonym – slap: to hit with the open hand, often lightly
