solo

noun
activity or performance by one person
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊləʊ / (BrE)
an activity or performance done by one person without help from others, especially in music or other arts

Examples

solo project
After many group projects, he finally gave his first public speaking solo.
She played a beautiful solo on the violin during the concert.
The guitarist performed an improvised solo that amazed the audience.
