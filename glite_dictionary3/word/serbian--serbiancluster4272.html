Serbian

noun
member of a Slavic people from Serbia
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜr.bi.ən / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.bi.ən / (BrE)
A member of a Slavic ethnic group originating from Serbia and neighboring regions, particularly those who settled there in the 6th and 7th centuries.

Examples

Serbian people
She is proud of her Serbian heritage and traditions.
The Serbian greeted us warmly during our visit to Belgrade.
There is a large community of Serbians living in the city.
