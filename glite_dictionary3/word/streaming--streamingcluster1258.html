streaming

adjective
exuding fluid in large amounts (body)
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstriːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a part of the body) giving off a fluid, such as tears, sweat, or mucus, in large or continuous amounts.

Examples

streaming eyes with tears
After running in the heat, his streaming face was covered with sweat.
She wiped her streaming eyes with a tissue after the sad movie.
The child came in from the cold, his nose streaming with mucus.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dry: not exuding fluid; lacking moisture
  • synonym – weeping: shedding tears; crying
