transitively

adverb
in a transitive manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtræn.zə.tɪv.li / (AmE), / ˈtræn.zɪ.tɪv.li / (BrE)
In a way that relates to a transitive verb or action; so as to take a direct object.

Examples

used transitively in grammar
In many languages, verbs can function transitively or intransitively depending on the context.
The verb 'address' can be used transitively in the sentence 'She addressed the audience.'
When you use 'enjoy' transitively, it must be followed by a direct object.
