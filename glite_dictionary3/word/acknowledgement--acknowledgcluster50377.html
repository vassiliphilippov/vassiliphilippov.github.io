acknowledgement

noun
recognition or acceptance
Pronunciation: / əkˈnɑlɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / əkˈnɒlɪdʒm(ə)nt /; / əkˈnɒlɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the act of recognizing, accepting, or showing thanks for something or someone
