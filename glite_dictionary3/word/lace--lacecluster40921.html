lace

noun
cord for fastening shoes or clothes
Pronunciation: / leɪs / (AmE), / leɪs / (BrE)
a cord or string passed through eyelets or hooks in shoes or garments to pull the edges together and fasten them

Examples

lace on a shoe
He bent down to tie the lace on his running shoe before the race.
My jacket has a broken lace and I need to replace it.
The child learned to thread the lace through the tiny eyelets of her new boots.
