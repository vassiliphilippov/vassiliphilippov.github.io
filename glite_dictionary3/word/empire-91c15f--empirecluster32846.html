empire

noun
large business organization or group
Pronunciation: / ˈɛm.paɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛm.paɪə / (BrE)
a group of diverse companies or organizations controlled and managed by a single owner or group

Examples

empire of companies
His media empire includes newspapers, television stations, and film companies.
She built a fashion empire starting from a single boutique shop.
The technology empire expanded rapidly by acquiring several start-up companies.
