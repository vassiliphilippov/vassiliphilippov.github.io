contrary

adjective
going in an opposing direction
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.trɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.trə.ri / (BrE)
(of movements or forces) going in an opposite or opposing direction.

Examples

contrary wind direction
