rusty

adjective
impaired in skill through neglect
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌsti / (AmE), / ˈrʌsti / (BrE)
Impaired in skill or ability due to lack of recent practice.

Examples

rusty language skills
Her Spanish was rusty after years of not using it.
It's been years since I played the piano, so I'm a bit rusty.
You might feel rusty at first, but your skills will come back quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – out of practice: not having practiced a skill for some time
