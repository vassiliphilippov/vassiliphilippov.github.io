district

noun
administrative area or region
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪstrɪkt / (AmE), / ˈdɪstrɪkt / (BrE)
an area of a country, city, or town that is marked off for administrative, governmental, or other official purposes

Examples

district school zone
Each district has its own local council and administrative offices.
The city was divided into several districts for the purpose of organizing emergency services.
The school is located in the financial district of the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym – region: a large area of land, usually without precise boundaries
  • synonym – zone: an area that is different from others in some way, often for particular use or administration
