to misuse

verb
use something wrongly or improperly
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈjus / (AmE), / mɪsˈjuːz / (BrE)
to use something in a wrong or improper way, or for the wrong purpose

Examples

to misuse a drug

Teachers worry that some students misuse digital resources by plagiarizing material.
