to conk

verb
to faint; lose consciousness suddenly
Pronunciation: / kɑŋk / (AmE), / kɒŋk / (BrE)
(informal) To suddenly lose consciousness; to faint.

Examples

to conk out suddenly
After standing in the sun too long, she suddenly conked out at the festival.
He was so exhausted after the marathon that he conked out on the sofa.
If I don't get some water soon, I might just conk out.

  • synonym – faint: to suddenly lose consciousness for a short time
