to splint

verb
to support with a splint
Pronunciation: / splɪnt / (AmE), / splɪnt / (BrE)
to support or immobilize (a body part, especially a broken bone) with a splint

Examples

to splint a leg
The nurse carefully splinted the patient's fractured wrist.
They splinted his finger using a small piece of wood and medical tape.
You should splint the injured leg before moving the person to prevent further damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym – immobilize: to prevent from moving, especially in a medical context
  • synonym – set: to put a broken bone into position for healing
