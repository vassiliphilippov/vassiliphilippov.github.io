unendingly

adverb
without end; endlessly
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɛndɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈɛndɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that does not stop; without an end or interruption.

Examples

talk unendingly
He talked unendingly about his travels, much to everyone's amusement.
She worked unendingly to achieve her dreams, never allowing setbacks to deter her.
The rain fell unendingly throughout the night, soaking the entire city.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ceaselessly: without stopping or pausing; continually
  • synonym – endlessly: in a way that has or seems to have no end
