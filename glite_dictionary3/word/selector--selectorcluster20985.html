selector

noun
device for choosing options/settings
Pronunciation: / səˈlɛktər / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛktə / (BrE)
a device or switch used to choose between different options or settings

Examples

selector switch position
The radio has a selector that lets you choose between AM and FM bands.
Turn the selector to the desired temperature setting before operating the oven.
Use the mode selector on the camera to switch between photo and video functions.
