unremunerative

adjective
not providing payment or profit
Pronunciation: / ʌn.rɪˈmjunəˌreɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / ʌn.rɪˈmjuː.nər.ə.tɪv / (BrE)
(of a job, work, or activity) not providing payment, profit, or reward.

Examples

unremunerative work
She left her unremunerative job to pursue a more profitable career.

Volunteering can be fulfilling even if the work is unremunerative.
