plastic

noun
credit or debit card for payment
Pronunciation: / ˈplæs.tɪk / (AmE), / ˈplæs.tɪk / (BrE)
(informal) a credit card or debit card used to make payments instead of cash.

Examples

pay with plastic
He prefers to pay with plastic instead of carrying cash.
Many stores today only accept plastic for purchases over $10.
Since I forgot my wallet, I couldn't use my plastic at the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • antonym – cash: money in coins or notes, as distinct from checks, credit, or debit cards
  • synonym – credit card: a card allowing the holder to purchase goods or services on credit
