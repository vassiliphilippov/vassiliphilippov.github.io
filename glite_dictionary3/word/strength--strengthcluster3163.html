strength

noun
particular asset, skill, or forte
Pronunciation: / strɛŋkθ / (AmE), / strɛŋ(k)θ / (BrE)
a particular quality or ability that gives someone an advantage or makes them especially good at something

Examples

personal strength
Communication has always been his greatest strength in the workplace.
Her ability to remain calm under pressure is a real strength.
One of the company's main strengths is its innovative product design.

Related Terms

  • synonym – forte: a thing at which someone excels
  • antonym – weakness: a disadvantage or an area where a person does not perform well
