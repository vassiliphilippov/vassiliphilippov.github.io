to canalize

verb
provide an area with canals
Pronunciation: / ˈkænəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / kəˈnælaɪz / (BrE)
To provide (a city or area) with canals for transportation or drainage.

Examples

to canalize a city
Efforts to canalize the area were met with opposition from local residents concerned about environmental impact.
In the 19th century, engineers worked to canalize several European cities for better trade routes.
The city plans to canalize the river to improve transportation and control flooding.
