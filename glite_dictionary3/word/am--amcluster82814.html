A.M.

noun
the time between midnight and noon
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ ˈɛm / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˈɛm / (BrE)
The period of time between midnight and noon; used to specify hours before midday in the 12-hour clock system.

Examples

early A.M. hours
Breakfast will be served until 10 A.M. in the dining room.
She usually wakes up around 6 A.M. to go jogging before work.
The train is scheduled to depart at 8:30 A.M. sharp.
