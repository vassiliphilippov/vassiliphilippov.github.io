to grumble

verb
make a low, continuous sound
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrʌmbəl / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌmbəl / (BrE)
(of a thing) to make a low, continuous, and often indistinct sound.

Examples

to grumble softly
An old refrigerator can sometimes grumble quietly in the night.
As the train approached, the tracks started to grumble beneath its weight.
The distant thunder began to grumble across the valley.

  • synonym – rumble: to make a deep, heavy, continuous sound
