impassable

adjective
impossible to cross or travel through
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpæsəbəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɑːsəbəl / (BrE)
(of a road, path, or area) impossible to travel across or through.

Examples

impassable mountain road
Because of flooding, the trail was completely impassable for hikers.
The mountain road became impassable after the heavy snowfall.
They had to turn back when they reached an impassable barrier of fallen trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym – impassible: incapable of being passed through (occasionally confused with 'impassable')
  • antonym – passable: able to be passed, crossed, or traveled through
