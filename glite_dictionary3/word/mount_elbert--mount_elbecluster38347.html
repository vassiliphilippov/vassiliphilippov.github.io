Mount Elbert

proper noun
highest peak in the Rocky Mountains
Pronunciation: / maʊnt ˈɛlbərt / (AmE), / maʊnt ˈɛlbət / (BrE)
The highest peak in the Rocky Mountains, located in central Colorado, rising to 14,431 feet (4,399 meters).
