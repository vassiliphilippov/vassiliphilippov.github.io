topknot

noun
decorative knot or bow in the hair
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑpˌnɑt / (AmE), / ˈtɒp.nɒt / (BrE)
A decorative ribbon, bow, or knot worn on top of the head or in the hair as an ornament.

Examples

topknot with ribbon
She tied a bright red topknot into her hair for the celebration.
The little girl wore a blue ribbon topknot that matched her dress.
