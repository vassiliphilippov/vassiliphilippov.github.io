to snip

verb
cut quickly, often with scissors
Pronunciation: / snɪp / (AmE), / snɪp / (BrE)
to cut something quickly and neatly, usually with scissors or a similar tool

Examples

to snip a coupon
He snipped a coupon from the newspaper before heading to the store.
She used small scissors to snip a loose thread from her shirt.
The gardener snipped the dead leaves off the plant with ease.
