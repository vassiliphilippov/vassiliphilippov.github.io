awakened

adjective
aroused or brought to awareness
Pronunciation: / əˈweɪkənd / (AmE), / əˈweɪkənd / (BrE)
(of feelings, interests, etc.) aroused or brought into a state of awareness or activity.

Examples

awakened interest
After her travels, she felt an awakened curiosity about the world.
The book led to an awakened sense of social responsibility in many readers.
The workshop created an awakened passion for painting in him.

Related Terms

  • synonym – activated: made active or operative
  • antonym – dormant: not active or having no activity
