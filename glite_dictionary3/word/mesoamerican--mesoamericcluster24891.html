Mesoamerican

noun
member of a Mesoamerican indigenous people
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛzoʊəˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / ˌmɛsəʊəˈmɛrɪkən / (BrE)
a member of one of the indigenous peoples of Mesoamerica, an area extending from central Mexico to northern Central America before the Spanish conquest.

Examples

ancient Mesoamerican people
Archaeologists continue to study the daily life of Mesoamericans before European contact.
Many ancient Mesoamericans built complex cities with intricate architectural designs.
Some Mesoamerican groups developed advanced calendars and writing systems.
