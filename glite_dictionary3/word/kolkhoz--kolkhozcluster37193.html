kolkhoz

noun
collective Soviet state farm
Pronunciation: / kɑlˈxɔz / (AmE), / kɒlˈxɒz / (BrE)
A collective farm in the former Soviet Union that was owned and run by the state.

Soviet kolkhoz farm
