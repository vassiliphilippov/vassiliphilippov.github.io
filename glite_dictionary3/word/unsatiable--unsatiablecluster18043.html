unsatiable

adjective
impossible to satisfy
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈseɪʃəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈseɪʃɪəbl̩ / (BrE)
(rare) Impossible to satisfy; always wanting more.

Examples

unsatiable curiosity
He felt an unsatiable curiosity about the mysteries of the universe.
Her ambition was unsatiable, driving her to seek ever greater challenges.
The crowd's unsatiable desire for entertainment kept the performers on stage late into the night.
