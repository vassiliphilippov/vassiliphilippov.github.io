aquiline

adjective
curved, eagle-like (esp. of nose)
Pronunciation: / ˈækwəˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈakwɪlʌɪn / (BrE)
(of a person's nose) curved or hooked like an eagle's beak.

Examples

aquiline nose shape
Her grandfather's aquiline features were passed down to several of his descendants.
Many believe his aquiline nose gives him a distinguished appearance.
The portrait features a woman with an aquiline profile and piercing eyes.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hooked: having a sharp curve or bend, often used of noses
  • antonym – straight: not curved or bent, often describing a nose shape
