salaciousness

noun
quality of being obscene or lewd
Pronunciation: / səˈleɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / səˈleɪʃəsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of behaving in a way that is obscene or intended to arouse sexual desire

Examples

salaciousness of gossip
Many tabloids thrive on the salaciousness of celebrity scandals to boost their sales.
She was uncomfortable with the salaciousness of the jokes being told at the party.
The novel was banned because critics believed its salaciousness was inappropriate for young readers.
