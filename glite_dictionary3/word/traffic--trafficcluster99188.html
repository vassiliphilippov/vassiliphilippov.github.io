traffic

noun
data activity over a network or system
Pronunciation: / ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
the amount of signals or data communicated over a network or communication system within a given period of time

Examples

internet traffic
Cell phone traffic increased dramatically during the emergency.
During peak hours, traffic on the website can slow page loading times.
The IT team monitors network traffic to prevent potential cyber attacks.

  • synonym – bandwidth: maximum rate of data transfer across a network
