inactivation

noun
process of making inactive
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnˌæktɪˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪnˌæktɪˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something inactive or rendering it unable to function

Examples

inactivation of an enzyme
Enzyme inactivation can occur at high temperatures during processing.
The inactivation of the virus was achieved by heating the sample.
They studied the inactivation mechanism of the gene under various laboratory conditions.
