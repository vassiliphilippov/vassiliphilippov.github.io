Psithyrus

noun
a parasitic genus of large bees
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪθɪrəs /; / ˈsɪθɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪθɪrəs / (BrE)
a genus of large bees, related to bumblebees, that do not collect pollen, have no worker caste, and are parasitic on other bees
