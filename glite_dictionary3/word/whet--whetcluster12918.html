to whet

verb
sharpen by rubbing
Pronunciation: / wɛt / (AmE), / wɛt / (BrE)
to sharpen (a blade or tool) by rubbing it, especially on a whetstone

Examples

to whet a knife
He used a stone to whet the edge of his knife before cooking.
She carefully whetted the blade until it gleamed in the firelight.
The blacksmith paused to whet his chisel between hammer blows.
