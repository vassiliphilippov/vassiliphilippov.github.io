to wink

verb
close and open one eye as a signal
Pronunciation: / wɪŋk / (AmE), / wɪŋk / (BrE)
to quickly close and open one eye, usually as a signal, greeting, or reflex

Examples

to wink at a friend
He winked at me to let me know everything was fine.
She caught his attention and winked playfully.
Whenever she tells a joke, she always winks at the end.
He couldn't help but wink at the amusing story.
She gave me a quick wink to show she was joking.
When the teacher looked away, the boy winked at his friend.
