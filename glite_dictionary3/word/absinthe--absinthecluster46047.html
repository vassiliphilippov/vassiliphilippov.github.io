absinthe

noun
strong green liqueur with wormwood and anise
Pronunciation: / ˈæb.sɪnθ / (AmE), / ˈæb.sɪnθ / (BrE)
a strong green alcoholic liqueur flavored with wormwood and anise, traditionally associated with France and Switzerland
