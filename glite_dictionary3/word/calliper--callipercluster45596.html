calliper

noun
instrument for measuring thickness or distance
Pronunciation: / ˈkælɪpər / (AmE), / ˈkælɪpə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure the distance between two opposite sides of an object, or the thickness or diameter of an object

Examples

calliper measurement tool
Digital callipers are now commonly used in laboratories for precise measurements.
He checked the diameter of the pipe with his calliper before starting the repair.
The engineer used a pair of callipers to measure the thickness of the metal sheet.
