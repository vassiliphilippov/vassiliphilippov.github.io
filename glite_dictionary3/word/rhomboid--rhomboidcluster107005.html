rhomboid

noun
type of parallelogram with oblique angles
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːm.bɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɒm.bɔɪd / (BrE)
A parallelogram with oblique angles and only opposite sides equal in length; a quadrilateral with adjacent sides of unequal length.

Examples

rhomboid shape
