lea

noun
unit of length for yarn or thread
Pronunciation: / liː / (AmE), / liː / (BrE)
a measure of length for yarn or thread, typically equivalent to about 80 or 120 yards, depending on the material and system used

Examples

lea of yarn
A lea is a traditional unit used in the textile industry.
Each lea of linen was carefully measured before being dyed.
The weaver requested another lea of silk to finish the tapestry.
