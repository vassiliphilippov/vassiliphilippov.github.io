expert

adjective
having special knowledge or skill
Pronunciation: / ˈɛk.spɚt / (AmE), / ˈɛk.spɜːt / (BrE)
Having or showing a high level of knowledge or skill in a particular area.

Examples

expert advice
His expert opinion was sought during the crucial stages of the investigation.
She handled the situation in an expert manner, impressing everyone in the room.
The chef prepared an expert presentation of the dish, with every element perfectly balanced.
