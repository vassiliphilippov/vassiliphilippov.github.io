Archaeopteryx

noun
extinct primitive Jurassic bird
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrkiˈɑːptərɪks /; / ˌɑr.kiˈɑp.tə.rɪks / (AmE), / ˌɑːkiˈɒptərɪks /; / ˌɑː.kiˈɒp.tər.ɪks / (BrE)
an extinct primitive bird from the Jurassic period, showing features of both dinosaurs and modern birds; considered a key transitional fossil
