guilloche

noun
ornamental pattern of interlaced wavy bands
Pronunciation: / ɡiˈloʊʃ / (AmE), / ɡiˈlɒʃ / (BrE)
An architectural ornament or decorative pattern consisting of two or more interlacing wavy bands.

Intricate guilloche designs are often found on banknotes and official documents as a security feature.
