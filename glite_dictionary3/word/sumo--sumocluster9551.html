sumo

noun
Japanese style of wrestling
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsuːməʊ / (BrE)
A Japanese form of wrestling in which competitors try to force each other out of a small ring or make any part of the opponent’s body (except the soles of the feet) touch the ground.

Examples

sumo wrestling match
He trained for years to become a professional sumo wrestler.
Many tourists enjoy watching sumo matches when they visit Japan.
The rules of sumo are strict about who wins and how the ring is used.
