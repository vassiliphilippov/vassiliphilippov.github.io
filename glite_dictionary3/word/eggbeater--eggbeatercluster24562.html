eggbeater

noun
informal term for a helicopter
Pronunciation: / ˈɛɡˌbiːtɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛɡˌbiːtə / (BrE)
(informal) A helicopter; an aircraft without wings that obtains lift from the rotation of overhead blades.

old eggbeater helicopter
During the war, the old eggbeater was used for vital rescue missions.
