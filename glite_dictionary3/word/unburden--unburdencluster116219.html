to unburden

verb
remove a burden or load
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɝːdən /; / ʌnˈbɝːdn̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɜːdn̩ / (BrE)
to free yourself or someone else from a burden, problem, or worry, often by talking about it
