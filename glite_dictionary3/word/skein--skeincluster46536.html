skein

noun
length of yarn wound in a coil
Pronunciation: / skeɪn / (AmE), / skeɪn / (BrE)
a length of yarn or thread wound in a loose, elongated coil, often for use in knitting or weaving

Examples

a skein of yarn
Please wind the skein into a ball before you start knitting.
She bought a skein of blue wool to knit a scarf.
The basket was filled with colorful skeins of yarn.
