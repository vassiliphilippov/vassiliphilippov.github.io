Dadaism

noun
early 20th-century avant-garde art movement
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːdəˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈdɑːdəˌɪzəm / (BrE)
an avant-garde art movement of the early 20th century, characterized by a rejection of traditional aesthetics and logic, often promoting irrationality and nonsense

Examples

Dadaism art movement
Many artists embraced Dadaism as a protest against the horrors of World War I.
