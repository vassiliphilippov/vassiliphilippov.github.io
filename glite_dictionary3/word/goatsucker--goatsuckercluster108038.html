goatsucker

noun
nocturnal insect-eating bird (Caprimulgidae)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊtˌsʌkər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊtˌsʌkə / (BrE)
A nocturnal or crepuscular bird of the family Caprimulgidae, with mottled plumage and large eyes, that feeds on insects.
