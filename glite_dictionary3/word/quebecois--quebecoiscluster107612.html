Quebecois

noun
French-speaking person from Quebec
Pronunciation: / keɪˌbɛkˈwɑ / (AmE), / kɛˌbɛkˈwɑ / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Quebec, especially one who speaks French

Examples

a Quebecois person
As a Quebecois, she is deeply passionate about preserving the French language in Canada.

The film features a cast of talented Quebecois actors speaking in their native French dialect.
