double-bogey

noun
golf: two strokes over par on a hole
Pronunciation: / ˌdʌbl ˈboʊɡi / (AmE), / ˌdʌbl ˈbəʊɡi / (BrE)
(in golf) a score of two strokes over par for a hole.

It's tough to recover from a double-bogey in a close tournament.
