swatter

noun
tool for killing insects
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːtər / (AmE), / ˈswɒtə / (BrE)
an implement with a flat surface and a handle, used for killing insects such as flies or mosquitoes

Examples

swatter kills flies
He missed the mosquito with the swatter and it escaped through the window.
She grabbed a swatter to chase the buzzing flies out of the kitchen.
The old swatter in the corner had holes in its mesh.
