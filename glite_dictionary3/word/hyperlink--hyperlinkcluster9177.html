hyperlink

noun
clickable link in a digital document
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪ.pɚ.lɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.pə.lɪŋk / (BrE)
A word, phrase, or image in a digital document that you can click on to go to another place or file.

Examples

click the hyperlink
Click the hyperlink to access the full article online.
Every hyperlink on the page is carefully labeled for easy navigation.
She added a hyperlink to her email so recipients could visit her website.
