starwort

noun
a low-growing plant with star-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːr.wɜrt / (AmE), / ˈstɑː.wɜːt / (BrE)
A low-growing plant of the Northern Hemisphere, bearing small white star-shaped flowers, especially of the genus Stellaria.
