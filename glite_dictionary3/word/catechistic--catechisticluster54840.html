catechistic

adjective
of or relating to a catechism
Pronunciation: / ˌkætɪˈkɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌkatɪˈkɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to, characteristic of, or resembling a catechism, especially in method or rigor

The teacher's catechistic questions helped the students memorize important facts.
