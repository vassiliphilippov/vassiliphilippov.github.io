jam

noun
a crowded situation of people
Pronunciation: / dʒæm / (AmE), / dʒam / (BrE)
a situation where a large number of people are packed closely together, making movement difficult

Examples

traffic jam
A jam formed on the bridge after the fireworks ended.
There was a jam at the subway entrance as commuters tried to get through.
We couldn't move forward because of the jam in front of the concert hall.

  • synonym – crowd: a large number of people gathered together
