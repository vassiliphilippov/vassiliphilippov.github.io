regular

adjective
having normal bowel movements
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛɡjələr / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjələ / (BrE)
(of a person's bowel movements) occurring normally or as expected, not constipated.

Examples

regular bowel movements

The doctor asked if he had been regular over the past week.
Yogurt is sometimes recommended to help people stay regular.
