earnestly

adverb
in a serious and sincere manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrnəstli / (AmE), / ˈɜːnɪstli / (BrE)
in a serious and sincere way; with deep and heartfelt intention

Examples

listen earnestly to advice
He listened earnestly, eager to understand every detail of the plan.
She spoke earnestly about the importance of helping others.
The volunteers worked earnestly to finish the project before the deadline.
