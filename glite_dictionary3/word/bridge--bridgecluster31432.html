bridge

noun
blockchain cross-chain transfer system
Pronunciation: / brɪdʒ / (AmE), / brɪdʒ / (BrE)
(in blockchain technology) a system or protocol that enables transfer of tokens, assets, or data between two separate blockchain networks.

Examples

blockchain bridge transfer
Developers deployed a new bridge to transfer tokens securely between Ethereum and Solana networks.
Security audits showed vulnerabilities in the cross-chain bridge that allowed attackers to drain funds.
Users locked assets on one chain while the bridge minted equivalent tokens on another blockchain.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blockchain bridge: another common term for a bridge used to transfer assets between chains
  • synonym – cross-chain bridge: a mechanism specifically designed to move value or data between different blockchains
