drenched

adjective
thoroughly covered or filled with something
Pronunciation: / drɛntʃt / (AmE), / drɛntʃt / (BrE)
thoroughly covered, soaked, or filled with something, often used figuratively or in combination (e.g. moon-drenched, sweat-drenched)

Examples

drenched in rain
After the storm, the hikers were completely drenched from head to toe.
His shirt was drenched with sweat after the intense workout.
She stood in the field, drenched in golden sunlight.
