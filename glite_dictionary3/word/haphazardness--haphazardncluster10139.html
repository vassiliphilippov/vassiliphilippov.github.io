haphazardness

noun
lack of order or planning
Pronunciation: / hæpˈhæzərdnəs / (AmE), / hæpˈhæzədnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being random, disorganized, or lacking any plan or predictable pattern.

Examples

urban haphazardness
City growth often follows a pattern of haphazardness rather than careful planning.
She was frustrated by the haphazardness of the files on her desk.
The haphazardness of his notes made it difficult to follow his argument.

  • antonym – orderliness: the quality of being methodical and organized
  • synonym – randomness: the quality of lacking pattern or predictability
