snowdrop

noun
North American anemone with white, pink-tinged flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌdrɑp / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌdrɒp / (BrE)
A common anemone of eastern North America with solitary white flowers often tinged with pink.
