Haganah

proper noun
Jewish paramilitary group in British Mandate Palestine
Pronunciation: / hɑːɡəˈnɑː / (AmE), / həˈɡɑːnə / (BrE)
the underground Jewish paramilitary organization in British Mandate Palestine from 1920 to 1948, which became the foundation of the Israeli Defense Forces
