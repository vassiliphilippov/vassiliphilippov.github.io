Achaea

proper noun
modern regional unit of Greece (capital Patras)
Pronunciation: / əˈkiə / (AmE), / əˈkiə / (BrE)
(in modern Greece) a regional unit of Western Greece, created in the 2011 administrative reform, with Patras as its largest city and administrative centre.

modern Achaea region
The port of Patras is the economic hub of Achaea and draws many tourists each summer.
