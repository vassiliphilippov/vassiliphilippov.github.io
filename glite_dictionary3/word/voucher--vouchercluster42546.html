voucher

noun
document confirming payment or expense
Pronunciation: / ˈvaʊtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈvaʊtʃə / (BrE)
a written document that confirms a payment, purchase, or expenditure, often used as proof for reimbursement or record-keeping

Examples

voucher for payment
A hotel voucher was provided to all conference attendees.
He kept every voucher from his business trip to ensure proper accounting.
Please attach the voucher to your expense report for reimbursement.

Related Terms

  • synonym – receipt: a written acknowledgment of having received money or goods
