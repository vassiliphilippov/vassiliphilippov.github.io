inharmoniousness

noun
lack of harmony; discord
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌhɑrˈmoʊniəsnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˌhɑːˈməʊniəsnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of lacking harmony, especially in sound, appearance, or relationships.

Examples

inharmoniousness of colors
She noticed the inharmoniousness between the colors in the new design.
The inharmoniousness of the music made it difficult for the audience to enjoy the performance.
Their constant arguments revealed an underlying inharmoniousness in their relationship.

Related Terms

  • synonym – discordance: the state of being in disagreement or conflict
  • antonym – harmony: the quality of forming a pleasing and consistent whole
