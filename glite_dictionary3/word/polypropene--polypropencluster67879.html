polypropene

noun
synthetic polymer from propylene
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈproʊpiːn / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈprəʊpiːn / (BrE)
a synthetic polymer made from propylene, used as a thermoplastic material for molding and manufacturing various products

Examples

polypropene plastic container
Most plastic containers are made from polypropene because of its durability and resistance to chemicals.
The automotive industry often uses polypropene for manufacturing interior parts.
