Hegelian

noun
follower of Hegel's philosophy
Pronunciation: / həˈɡeɪliən / (AmE), / hɛˈɡeɪliən / (BrE)
A person who supports or follows the philosophy of Georg Wilhelm Friedrich Hegel.

Examples

famous Hegelian thinker
As a Hegelian, he believed in the dialectical process of history.
She became a prominent Hegelian after studying German idealist philosophy at university.
