rectification

noun
purification by distillation (chemistry)
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛktəfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
(Chemistry) the process of refining or purifying a substance by repeated distillation.
