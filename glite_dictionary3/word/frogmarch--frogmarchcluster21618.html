to frogmarch

verb
to force someone to walk by pinning their arms or limbs
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔgˌmɑrtʃ /; / ˈfrɔɡˌmɑrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈfrɒgˌmɑːtʃ /; / ˈfrɒɡˌmɑːtʃ / (BrE)
to force someone to walk somewhere by holding and restraining their arms, usually against their will
