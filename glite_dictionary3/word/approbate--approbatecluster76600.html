to approbate

verb
officially approve or sanction
Pronunciation: / ˈæprəˌbeɪt /; / ˈæp.roʊˌbeɪt / (AmE), / ˈæprəˌbeɪt /; / ˈæp.rəʊˌbeɪt / (BrE)
