to harmonise

verb
bring into harmony or agreement
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrməˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈhɑːmənaɪz / (BrE)
to make different things, ideas, or actions agree or work well together; to make them compatible

Examples

to harmonise different rules

It's important to harmonise our policies with those of our partners to avoid confusion.

The rich red tones harmonise beautifully with the cream walls in the living room.
