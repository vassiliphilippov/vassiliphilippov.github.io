greedily

adverb
in a greedy or selfish manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːdɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡriːdɪli / (BrE)
In a way that shows an intense and selfish desire for something, especially food or wealth.

Examples

eat greedily
He greedily read every book he could find on the subject.
She greedily grabbed all the coins from the table before anyone else could react.
The children ate the cake greedily, not leaving a single crumb.
