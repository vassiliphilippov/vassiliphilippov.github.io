expediently

adverb
in a practical or convenient manner
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspiːdiəntli / (AmE), / ɪkˈspiːdiəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is convenient and practical, although possibly improper or immoral

Examples

to act expediently in business
Although not entirely honest, she completed the task expediently to meet the deadline.
He made the decision expediently, without considering the long-term consequences.
The company acted expediently to resolve the issue and avoid public embarrassment.

Related Terms

  • antonym – improperly: in a way that is not proper
  • synonym – practically: in a practical manner
