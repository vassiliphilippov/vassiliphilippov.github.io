predictor

noun
device for antiaircraft fire control
Pronunciation: / prɪˈdɪktər / (AmE), / prɪˈdɪktə / (BrE)
a device or computer controlling antiaircraft fire that calculates the predicted position of an aircraft at the moment a shell will arrive

Examples

antiaircraft predictor device
Advances in technology led to improved predictor systems for targeting fast-moving planes.
Technicians calibrated the predictor before the exercise to ensure accuracy.
The antiaircraft battery relied on its predictor to calculate where to aim.
