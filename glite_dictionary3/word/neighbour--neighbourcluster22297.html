neighbour

noun
nearby object or entity
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪbər / (AmE), / ˈneɪbə / (BrE)
an object or entity that is located near another of the same kind

Examples

closest neighbour
Saturn’s largest moon, Titan, is a neighbour to several smaller moons.
The museum is a neighbour of the city library in the downtown area.
Venus is considered Earth's closest neighbour in the solar system.
