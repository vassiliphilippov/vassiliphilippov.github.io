Mimosa pudica

noun
tropical plant with touch-sensitive leaves
Pronunciation: / mɪˈmoʊsə pjuːˈdɪkə / (AmE), / mɪˈməʊsə pjuːˈdɪkə / (BrE)
A tropical plant with sensitive leaves that fold and droop when touched or at night, native to the Americas and Australia, often known as the 'sensitive plant.'
