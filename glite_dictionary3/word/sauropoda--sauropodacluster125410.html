Sauropoda

noun
suborder of large long-necked dinosaurs
Pronunciation: / sɔːˈrɑːpədə / (AmE), / sɔːˈrɒpədə / (BrE)
a suborder of very large, plant-eating dinosaurs with long necks, long tails, and four thick legs, such as Brachiosaurus and Diplodocus
