footer

noun
note or line at bottom of a page
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊdɚ / (AmE), / ˈfʊtə / (BrE)
A printed note, line, or section placed at the bottom of each page, typically containing information such as page numbers, document title, or copyright details.

Examples

footer with page number
Each page of the report includes a footer displaying the page number and document title.
He added the date to the footer of the presentation slides for reference.
Please make sure your company's address is on the footer of every letterhead.
