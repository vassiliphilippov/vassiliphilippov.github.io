better

adverb
in a more excellent or suitable manner
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛt̬.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɛt.ə / (BrE)
In a more excellent, effective, or advantageous manner; to a greater degree or in a more suitable way.

Examples

speak better than before
He speaks French better than he speaks Spanish.
She recovered much better after the new treatment.
The team performed better in the second half of the game.
