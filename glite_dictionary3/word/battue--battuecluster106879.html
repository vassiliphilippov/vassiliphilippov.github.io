battue

noun
a hunt with beaters driving game to hunters
Pronunciation: / bæˈtu / (AmE), / baˈtyu / (BrE)
a hunt in which beaters drive game animals towards waiting hunters.

Examples

battue with beaters
During the annual battue, dozens of beaters moved through the forest to flush out pheasants.
The estate organized a formal battue for its distinguished guests.
Unlike a solitary hunt, a battue relies on teamwork between hunters and beaters.
