pungapung

noun
putrid-smelling Asian aroid plant
Pronunciation: / ˈpuŋˌɑːpuŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʌŋəˌpʌŋ / (BrE)
A putrid-smelling tropical aroid native to southeast Asia, especially the Philippines, grown for its edible tuber.
