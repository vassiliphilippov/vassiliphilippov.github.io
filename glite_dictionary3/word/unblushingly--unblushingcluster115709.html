unblushingly

adverb
without shame or embarrassment
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈblʌʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈblʌʃɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no shame or embarrassment; without blushing.

Examples

unblushingly tell a lie

She unblushingly asked for a raise, confident in her worth to the company.
The politician unblushingly denied any wrongdoing despite the evidence.
