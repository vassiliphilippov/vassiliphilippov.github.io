level

noun
a flat, horizontal surface
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈlɛvəl / (BrE)
a flat or even surface that is horizontal, especially one that is at a right angle to a plumb line

Examples

level surface
Always make sure the bookshelf sits firmly on the level.
If you park on the level, your car will be less likely to roll away.
The patio was built on the level for stability and safety.
