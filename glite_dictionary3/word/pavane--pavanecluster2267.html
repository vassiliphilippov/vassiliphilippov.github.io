pavane

noun
slow, stately Renaissance dance or its music
Pronunciation: / pəˈvɑːn /; / pəˈvɑn / (AmE), / pəˈvɑːn / (BrE)
a slow and stately dance popular in 16th- and 17th-century Europe, or the music composed for such a dance
