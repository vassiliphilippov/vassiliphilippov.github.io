mummer

noun
actor performing without speaking
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌmɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌmə / (BrE)
an actor who performs without speaking, using gesture and facial expression to convey meaning

Examples

mummer in a play
In medieval festivals, a mummer would tell stories without uttering a single word.
She trained for years to become a skilled mummer in the traditional style.
The mummer captivated the audience with his expressive movements and comic gestures.
