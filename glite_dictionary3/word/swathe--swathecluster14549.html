swathe

noun
enveloping bandage or strip of cloth
Pronunciation: / sweɪð / (AmE), / swɒð / (BrE)
a long strip of cloth used to wrap or bind something, especially as a bandage

Examples

swathe of bandage
He carefully removed the swathe to check the healing wound.
Her leg was encased in a thick swathe of white gauze after the operation.
The nurse wrapped a fresh swathe around his injured arm.
