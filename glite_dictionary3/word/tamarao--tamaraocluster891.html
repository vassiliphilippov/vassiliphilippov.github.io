tamarao

noun
small wild buffalo of Mindoro, Philippines
Pronunciation: / təˈmɑːraʊ / (AmE), / təˈmɑːraʊ / (BrE)
A small, endangered wild buffalo (Bubalus mindorensis) native to the island of Mindoro in the Philippines.

Examples

wild tamarao buffalo
Conservation efforts aim to protect the remaining tamaraos in their natural habitat.
