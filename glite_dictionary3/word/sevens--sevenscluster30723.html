sevens

noun
card game based on playing sevens in sequence
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛv.ənz / (AmE), / ˈsɛv.ənz / (BrE)
a card game in which players lay down sevens and then continue to play cards in sequence in the same suit; the winner is the first to play all their cards

Examples

play sevens cards
In sevens, it is important to keep track of every suit.
