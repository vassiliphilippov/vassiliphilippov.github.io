apart

adverb
excluding from consideration
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑrt / (AmE), / əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
Used to show that something is excluded from consideration; except for or aside from.

Examples

apart from the noise
Apart from the weather, we had a wonderful holiday.
All financial difficulties apart, the project was a huge success.
All jokes apart, you really should see a doctor.

  • synonym – aside: excluding from consideration; except for
