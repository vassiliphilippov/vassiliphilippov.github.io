extent

noun
degree or limit of something
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛnt / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnt / (BrE)
the degree or limit to which something happens or is true; the amount, size, or scope of something

Examples

to a large extent
The flood caused damage to a considerable extent.
To some extent, I agree with your assessment.
We still do not know the full extent of the problem.
