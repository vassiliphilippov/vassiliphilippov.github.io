hyrax

noun
small African or Asian mammal
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪˌræks / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪ.raks / (BrE)
A small, herbivorous mammal native to Africa and Asia, with rodent-like front teeth and hoof-like toes.
