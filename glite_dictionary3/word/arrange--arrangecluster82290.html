to arrange

verb
to organize or position things attractively
Pronunciation: / əˈreɪndʒ / (AmE), / əˈreɪndʒ / (BrE)
to put things, ideas, or information in a particular order or position, often neatly or for a specific purpose

We are arranging our schedule for next week's conference.
