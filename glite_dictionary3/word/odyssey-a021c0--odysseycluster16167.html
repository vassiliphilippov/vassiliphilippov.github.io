odyssey

noun
a long, adventurous journey
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːdəsi / (AmE), / ˈɒdɪsi / (BrE)
a long and adventurous journey, often marked by many changes of fortune

Examples

an odyssey across continents
After college, she embarked on an odyssey around the world, exploring dozens of countries and cultures.
His medical recovery was an odyssey filled with setbacks and triumph.
The novel chronicles the protagonist’s odyssey through war-torn regions in search of peace.
