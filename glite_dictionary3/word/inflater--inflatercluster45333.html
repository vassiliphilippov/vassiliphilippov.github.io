inflater

noun
hand pump for inflating items
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfleɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈfleɪtə / (BrE)
a hand-operated device for pumping air to inflate items such as tires or inflatable toys

Examples

bicycle tire inflater
He used a small inflater to pump air into his bicycle tire.
Make sure the inflater is working before your long road trip.
The emergency kit included a portable inflater for flat tires.

  • synonym – pump: a device for moving liquids or gases by pressure or suction
