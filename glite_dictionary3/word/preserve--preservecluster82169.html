preserve

noun
protected area for wildlife
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzɝv / (AmE), / prɪˈzɜːv / (BrE)
an area of land where animals and plants are protected, especially for conservation purposes; a wildlife refuge

Examples

wildlife preserve
Hunting is strictly forbidden in the animal preserve established by the government.
Rangers patrol the preserve to ensure the animals are kept safe from poachers.
The national preserve attracts tourists who want to see rare wildlife in their natural habitat.

Related Terms

  • synonym – reserve: a protected area of land for animals or plants
  • synonym – sanctuary: a safe place, especially one for animals or birds
