terrorization

noun
the act of causing fear or terror
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtɛrəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of causing someone to feel intense fear or terror, sometimes for political purposes

Examples

terrorization of people

International laws condemn the terrorization of civilians during armed conflict.
She spoke about her experiences of terrorization at the hands of extremist groups.
The terrorization of the local community led many to flee their homes.
