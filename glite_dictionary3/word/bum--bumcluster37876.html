to bum

verb
spend time idly or lazily
Pronunciation: / bʌm / (AmE), / bʌm / (BrE)
to spend time being lazy or doing nothing in particular, especially in an idle or aimless way

Examples

to bum around town
After graduation, she spent a year bumming around before starting her job.
He decided to bum around Europe for the summer with no set plans.
They were bumming on the beach all day, not doing much of anything.
