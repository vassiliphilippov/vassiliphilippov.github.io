to syncretize

verb
unite different beliefs or principles
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋkrəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋ.krɪ.taɪz /; / ˈsɪŋkrɪtʌɪz / (BrE)
to unite or combine different beliefs, systems, or practices into one, especially in religion, philosophy, or culture

Examples
