to nitrogenise

verb
treat with nitrogen
Pronunciation: / naɪˈtroʊdʒəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / naɪˈtrɒdʒənaɪz / (BrE)
to treat or combine something with nitrogen or a nitrogen compound, typically in a chemical or industrial process

Examples

Some fertilizers are produced by first nitrogenising basic feedstock chemicals.
