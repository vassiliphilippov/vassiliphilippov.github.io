to square

verb
raise to the power of two
Pronunciation: / skwɛr / (AmE), / skwɛə / (BrE)
to multiply a number by itself; to raise a number to the power of two

Examples

to square a value
If you square twelve, the result is one hundred forty-four.
To find the area of a square, you square the length of one side.
When you square a negative number, you always get a positive answer.
