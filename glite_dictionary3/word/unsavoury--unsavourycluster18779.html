unsavoury

adjective
morally offensive or unpleasant
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈseɪvəri / (AmE), / ʌnˈseɪvəri / (BrE)
Unpleasant or morally offensive, especially in relation to character or reputation.

Examples

unsavoury character
He was dismissed from his job due to an unsavoury incident in his past.
The company avoided any unsavoury dealings that could harm its reputation.
They were concerned about the unsavoury characters hanging around the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym – repugnant: extremely distasteful or unacceptable
  • antonym – respectable: regarded by society to be good, proper, or correct
