partition

noun
structure dividing spaces in an organism
Pronunciation: / pɑːrˈtɪʃən /; / pɑrˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / pɑːˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
a structure or object that divides one area or space from another, such as a wall, screen, or anatomical divider
