to marbleize

verb
make to look like marble
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrbəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɑːb(ə)lʌɪz / (BrE)
to make something look like marble, often by applying a finish or pattern that imitates marble's appearance

Examples

to marbleize a surface
She plans to marbleize the kitchen countertops to give them a luxurious appearance.
The artists managed to marbleize the columns so skilfully that they looked like real marble.
They hired a specialist to marbleize the old fireplace in the living room.
