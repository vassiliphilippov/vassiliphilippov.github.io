perceptivity

noun
keen or accurate perception
Pronunciation: / pərˌsɛpˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / pəˌsɛpˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
the ability to notice or understand things quickly and accurately; keenness of perception

Examples

keen perceptivity
Her perceptivity allowed her to sense tension in the room before anyone else noticed.
The critic's perceptivity makes his reviews especially insightful.
With great perceptivity, she understood the emotions behind his words.
