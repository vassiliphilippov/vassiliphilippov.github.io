foliage

noun
leaf-like architectural ornament
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊliɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊlɪɪdʒ / (BrE)
An ornament or decoration in architecture that resembles or is shaped like leaves.

Examples

decorative foliage
Medieval architects often used stylized foliage to embellish entryways and windows.
Stone foliage motifs decorate the capitals of the pillars in the old hall.
The cathedral's intricate foliage is carved along the arches and columns.
