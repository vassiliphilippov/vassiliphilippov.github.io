imitator

noun
software or AI that copies a person or style
Pronunciation: / c86am6accte6at59r / (AmE), / c86am6ate6at59 / (BrE)
a non-human agent or software system (for example, an AI model or voice-cloning program) that reproduces the voice, appearance, style, or behavior of a real person, brand, or creative work.

Examples

AI imitator of voices
The studio hired an AI imitator to reproduce the late singer's voice for the new duet.
A small start-up developed a imitator that copies brand tone across social media posts.
Security researchers detected a phone app acting as a imitator to trick users into sharing passwords.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deepfake: a synthetic media (often video or audio) in which a person's likeness is replaced or simulated by machine learning techniques
  • synonym – voice clone: a digital reproduction of a person's voice created by machine-learning models
