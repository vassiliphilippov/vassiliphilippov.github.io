flavourless

adjective
lacking flavor or taste
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪvərləs / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvələs / (BrE)
having little or no taste; lacking in flavor or distinctive quality.

Examples

flavourless soup

Many people complain that supermarket strawberries are flavourless compared to homegrown ones.
The soup was completely flavourless, so I added extra salt and pepper.
