Pythonidae

noun
family of nonvenomous snakes (pythons)
Pronunciation: / paɪˈθɑnɪˌdiː /; / paɪˈθɑːnɪˌdiː / (AmE), / paɪˈθɒnɪˌdiː /; / pʌɪˈθɒnɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of nonvenomous snakes, known as pythons, found mainly in the Old World and distinct from the Boidae family
