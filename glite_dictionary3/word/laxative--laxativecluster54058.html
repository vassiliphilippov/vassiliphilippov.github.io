laxative

adjective
promoting bowel movement
Pronunciation: / ˈlæk.sə.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈlæk.sət.ɪv / (BrE)
(of a substance) having the effect of stimulating or promoting the evacuation of feces from the body.

Examples

Certain fruits, such as prunes, are known for their laxative properties.
