to languish

verb
lose strength or health, often from grief
Pronunciation: / ˈlæŋɡwɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈlaŋɡwɪʃ /; / ˈlæŋɡwɪʃ / (BrE)
to become weak, lose strength, or decline, especially because of neglect, hardship, or emotional pain
