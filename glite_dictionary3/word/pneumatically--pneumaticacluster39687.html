pneumatically

adverb
by means of pressurized air or gas
Pronunciation: / nuːˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / njuːˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or operated by air or gas under pressure.

Examples

pneumatically powered tools
The cylinders are operated pneumatically rather than electrically for greater efficiency.
The doors open pneumatically with a gentle hiss as the bus arrives.
The tools can function pneumatically in environments where electrical sparks are dangerous.

Related Terms

  • antonym – hydraulically: in a manner operated by liquid under pressure
