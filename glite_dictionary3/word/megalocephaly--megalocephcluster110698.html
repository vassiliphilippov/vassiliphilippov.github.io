megalocephaly

noun
abnormally large head (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛɡəloʊˈsɛfəli / (AmE), / ˌmɛɡələ(ʊ)ˈsɛfəli / (BrE)
A medical condition in which the head is abnormally large, without increased intracranial pressure and with symmetrical overgrowth.
