Antonio Lopez de Santa Ana

proper noun
Mexican general and president, 1795–1876
Pronunciation: / ænˌtoʊnioʊ loʊˈpɛz deɪ ˌsæntə ˈænə / (AmE), / ænˌtəʊniəʊ ˈlɒpez deɪ ˌsæntə ˈænə / (BrE)
(1795–1876) Mexican general and president, known for attempting to suppress the Texas Revolution and for his leadership during the Mexican-American War.
