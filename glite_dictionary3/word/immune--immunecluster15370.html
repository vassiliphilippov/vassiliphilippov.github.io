immune

adjective
not affected by something
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmjun / (AmE), / ɪˈmjuːn / (BrE)
not affected or influenced by something, especially something that others are usually affected by; usually followed by 'to'.

Examples

immune to criticism
After years of disappointment, she felt almost immune to bad news.
He appeared completely immune to their attempts at persuasion.
Some people seem immune to criticism no matter how harsh it is.

Related Terms

  • antonym – susceptible: easily affected or influenced by something
