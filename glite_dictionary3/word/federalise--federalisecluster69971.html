to federalise

verb
bring under federal government control
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛdərəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈfɛdərəlaɪz / (BrE)
to bring (a state, organization, etc.) under the control or authority of a federal government

Examples

to federalise a system
Debates arose about whether to federalise the health care system across all provinces.
Some officials oppose efforts to federalise education, preferring local control.
The government plans to federalise the regional police forces to improve coordination.
