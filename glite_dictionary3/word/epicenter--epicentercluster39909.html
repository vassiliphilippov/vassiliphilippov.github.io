epicenter

noun
point above earthquake focus
Pronunciation: / ˈɛpɪˌsɛntər / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪˌsɛntə / (BrE)
the point on the Earth's surface directly above the focus of an earthquake

Examples

epicenter of an earthquake
Emergency crews rushed to the epicenter to assist those affected by the tremor.
The city lay near the epicenter of the earthquake, suffering the most damage.
The scientists used seismographs to determine the precise epicenter of the quake.
