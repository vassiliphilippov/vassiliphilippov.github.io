vena sternocleidomastoidea

noun
vein draining sternocleidomastoid muscle
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪ.nə stɜr.noʊˌklaɪ.dəʊˌmæs.tɔɪˈdiː.ə / (AmE), / ˈviː.nə stɜː.nəʊˌklaɪ.dəʊˌmæs.tɔɪˈdiː.ə / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the sternocleidomastoid muscle and empties into the internal jugular vein

Examples

vena sternocleidomastoidea drains muscle
Damage to the vena sternocleidomastoidea can lead to excessive bleeding during procedures involving the sternocleidomastoid muscle.
In some individuals, the vena sternocleidomastoidea is more prominent and easily observed during neck surgery.
The vena sternocleidomastoidea empties into the internal jugular vein, contributing to venous drainage of the neck.
