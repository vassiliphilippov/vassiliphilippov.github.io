Prilosec

noun
antacid medication (brand name)
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪloʊˌsɛk / (AmE), / ˈpraɪləsɛk / (BrE)
A trademarked antacid medication used to decrease the amount of acid produced in the stomach, commonly prescribed for acid reflux and ulcers.
