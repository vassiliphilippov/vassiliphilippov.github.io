horsehair

noun
hair from a horse's mane or tail
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsˌhɛr / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsˌheə / (BrE)
the hair taken from the mane or tail of a horse, often used for upholstery, brushes, or stringed instrument bows

Examples

horsehair brush
He bought a vintage brush made from genuine horsehair at the market.
The violin bow was restrung with horsehair for a better sound quality.
Upholsterers once commonly used horsehair as a stuffing material for furniture.
