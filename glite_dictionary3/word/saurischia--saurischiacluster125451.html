Saurischia

noun
order of dinosaurs with lizard-like hips
Pronunciation: / sɔːˈrɪskiə / (AmE), / sɔːˈrɪskɪə /; / sɔːˈrɪskiə / (BrE)
a major order of extinct dinosaurs, including theropods (meat-eaters) and sauropods (large, long-necked plant-eaters), with a lizard-like pelvic structure
