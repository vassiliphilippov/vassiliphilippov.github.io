periclase

noun
white mineral; magnesium source
Pronunciation: / ˈper.əˌkleɪs / (AmE), / ˈper.ɪ.kleɪz / (BrE)
A white crystalline mineral consisting of magnesium oxide, occurring naturally and used as a source of magnesium.
