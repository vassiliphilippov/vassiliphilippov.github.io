Georgia

proper noun
country in the Caucasus, Eurasia
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔr.dʒə / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔː.dʒə / (BrE)
A country in the Caucasus region of Eurasia, situated on the Black Sea and bordered by Russia, Turkey, Armenia, and Azerbaijan; formerly part of the Soviet Union, independent since 1991.
