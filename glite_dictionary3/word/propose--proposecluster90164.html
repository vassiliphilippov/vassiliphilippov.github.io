to propose

verb
intend or plan to do
Pronunciation: / prəˈpoʊz / (AmE), / prəˈpəʊz / (BrE)
to intend or plan to do something; to have as one's aim

Examples

to propose a project
He proposed to start his own business after graduation.
The committee proposes to examine the issue in greater detail.
We propose to visit Europe next summer if our finances allow.

Related Terms

  • synonym – intend: to have a plan or purpose in mind
  • synonym – plan: to decide on and arrange in advance
