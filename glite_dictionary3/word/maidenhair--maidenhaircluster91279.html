maidenhair

noun
a delicate fern of the genus Adiantum
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdnˌhɛr / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdnˌheə / (BrE)
A type of fern of the genus Adiantum, known for its delicate, finely divided fronds resembling hair.
