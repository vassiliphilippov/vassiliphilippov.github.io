to clamp

verb
fasten or hold with a clamp
Pronunciation: / klæmp / (AmE), / klɑːmp / (BrE)
to fasten or hold something tightly in place using a clamp

Examples

to clamp a photo frame
She clamped the photo frame to the table so it would not slip.
The mechanic carefully clamped the pipe to prevent it from moving during the repair.
You should clamp the two boards together while the glue dries.
