confirmation

noun
Christian sacrament, admission to full church membership
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn.fɚˈmeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.fəˈmeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a Christian sacrament in which a baptized person is formally admitted as a full member of the church and receives the strengthening of grace

Examples

Catholic confirmation ceremony
After her confirmation, she felt a deeper connection to the church community.
He chose his godfather to be his sponsor at his confirmation last year.
The bishop will conduct the confirmation ceremony for the new members this Sunday.

  • synonym – baptism: the Christian sacrament of initiation and purification with water
