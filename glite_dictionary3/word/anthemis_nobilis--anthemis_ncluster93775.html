Anthemis nobilis

noun
Eurasian medicinal plant; Roman chamomile
Pronunciation: / ænˈθɛmɪs ˈnoʊbɪlɪs / (AmE), / anˈθɛmɪs ˈnɒbɪlɪs / (BrE)
A Eurasian plant with apple-scented leaves and white-rayed flowers, used medicinally; also known as Roman chamomile.

Examples

Anthemis nobilis herbal tea
