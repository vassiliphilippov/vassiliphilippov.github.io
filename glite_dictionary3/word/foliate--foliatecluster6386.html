foliate

adjective
having or resembling leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊ.li.eɪt / (AmE), / ˈfəʊ.li.eɪt / (BrE)
having or resembling leaves, or having a specified kind or number of leaves (often used as a combining form).
