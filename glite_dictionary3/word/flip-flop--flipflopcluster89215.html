flip-flop

noun
a type of thong sandal
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪpˌflɑp / (AmE), / ˈflɪp.flɒp / (BrE)
A light, open sandal held on the foot by a thong that passes between the big toe and the second toe.

Examples

wear flip-flops in summer
During summer, I wear flip-flops almost every day.
He left his flip-flops outside the front door.
She slipped into her flip-flops before heading to the beach.
