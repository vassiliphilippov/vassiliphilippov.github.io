Tertry

proper noun
historical battle in France, 687
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrtri / (AmE), / ˈtɜːtri / (BrE)
A battle fought in France in 687 among the descendants of Clovis, significant in the history of the Frankish kingdoms.

the Battle of Tertry
