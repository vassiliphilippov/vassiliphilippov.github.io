wrong

adjective
not functioning properly
Pronunciation: / rɔŋ / (AmE), / rɒŋ / (BrE)
(of a machine, system, or process) not functioning properly or as it should; out of order.

Examples

wrong with the car
If your car sounds strange, something might be wrong with the engine.
The computer went wrong after the power outage last night.
There’s something wrong with this washing machine—it won’t spin.
