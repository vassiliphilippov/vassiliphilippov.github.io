advowson

noun
right to present a nominee for a church benefice
Pronunciation: / ædˈvaʊzən / (AmE), / ədˈvaʊzən / (BrE)
the right in English law to recommend or present a nominee for appointment to a vacant church benefice

Examples

advowson of a church
During the dispute, both parties claimed the advowson to the benefice.
