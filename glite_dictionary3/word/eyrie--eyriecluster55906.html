eyrie

noun
dwelling at a high altitude
Pronunciation: / ˈɪri, ˈɛri / (AmE), / ˈɪəri, ˈɛəri / (BrE)
A building, room, or dwelling situated at a very high altitude, often with a commanding view.

Examples

mountain eyrie
He built his mountain retreat as a luxurious eyrie overlooking the valley below.
The monastery's eyrie location made it perfect for contemplation and solitude.
The radio host broadcasted from his eyrie high above the city skyline.

Related Terms

  • synonym – aerie: a large nest of a bird of prey, especially an eagle; also a high dwelling
