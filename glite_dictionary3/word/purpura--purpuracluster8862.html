purpura

noun
subcutaneous bleeding causing purple skin spots
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːpjərə / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpjʊərə / (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by purple spots on the skin or mucous membranes caused by subcutaneous bleeding as a result of various blood diseases.
