Doolittle

proper noun
American general and aviation pioneer
Pronunciation: / ˈduˌlɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈduːlɪtl / (BrE)
(James Harold Doolittle, 1896–1993) United States Air Force general and aviation pioneer who led the famous 1942 Doolittle Raid on Tokyo during World War II.
