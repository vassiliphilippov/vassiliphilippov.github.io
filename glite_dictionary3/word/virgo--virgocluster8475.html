Virgo

noun
person born under Virgo sign
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝːɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈvɜːɡəʊ / (BrE)
A person born between August 23 and September 22, when the sun is in the Virgo astrological sign.

Examples

a Virgo person
As a Virgo, she is said to be practical and analytical.
Many Virgos are thought to be detail-oriented and organized.
My sister is a Virgo and believes in astrology.
