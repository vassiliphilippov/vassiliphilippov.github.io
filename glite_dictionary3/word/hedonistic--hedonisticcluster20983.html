hedonistic

adjective
devoted to pleasure
Pronunciation: / ˌhidəˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌhiːdəˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
devoted to the pursuit of pleasure; characterized by a belief that pleasure or happiness is the highest good.

Examples

hedonistic lifestyle
Critics argue that the novel portrays a dangerously hedonistic philosophy, ignoring responsibility.
She adopted a hedonistic lifestyle after moving to the coast, seeking constant pleasure and excitement.
The city's nightlife is famous for its hedonistic atmosphere, attracting people from around the world.
