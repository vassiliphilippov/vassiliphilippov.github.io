kinglet

noun
a small songbird with a bright crown
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋlət / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋlɪt / (BrE)
a very small songbird found in the Northern Hemisphere, resembling a warbler and known for its bright crown.

Examples

tiny kinglet bird
During migration, kinglets often visit gardens as they search for insects.
The distinctive yellow crown of the kinglet makes it easy to identify in the wild.
We spotted a kinglet flitting among the branches in the early morning light.
