grip

noun
film/TV worker who handles cameras and equipment
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪp / (AmE), / ɡrɪp / (BrE)
a worker responsible for setting up, maintaining, and moving equipment such as cameras and lighting on a film or television set

Examples

film grip
After years as a grip, he learned the ins and outs of every piece of filmmaking equipment.
During the shoot, the director thanked the grip for solving a tricky lighting problem.
The grip worked quickly to reset the camera position between scenes.
