squawky

adjective
making a harsh, loud sound
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɔki / (AmE), / ˈskwɔːki / (BrE)
(of a sound) harsh, loud, and unpleasant, like the squawking of a bird.

Examples

squawky bird sounds
Children giggled at the squawky noises coming from the barn's chicken coop.
Her old portable radio had a squawky sound that made music hard to enjoy.
The parrot's squawky call echoed through the house, startling everyone at breakfast.

Related Terms

  • synonym – harsh: unpleasantly rough or jarring to the senses
  • antonym – mellow: pleasantly smooth or soft; not harsh
