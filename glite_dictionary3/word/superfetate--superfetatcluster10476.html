to superfetate

verb
conceive when already pregnant
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈfiːteɪt / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈfiːteɪt / (BrE)
(of a female mammal) to conceive one or more embryos while a previous pregnancy is ongoing.

Examples

to superfetate during pregnancy
In rare cases, mammals may superfetate due to hormonal irregularities.
Scientists documented a rabbit that appeared to superfetate while already pregnant.
The mare was found to superfetate, carrying embryos at different stages of development.
