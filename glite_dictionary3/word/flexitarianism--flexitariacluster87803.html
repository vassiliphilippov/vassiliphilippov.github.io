flexitarianism

noun
mostly vegetarian but flexible diet
Pronunciation: / ˌflɛksɪˈtɛriəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌflɛksɪˈteəriənɪzəm / (BrE)
The practice of eating a mostly vegetarian diet while occasionally including meat or fish.

Examples

flexitarianism diet plan
Flexitarianism is gaining popularity among people who want to reduce their meat consumption without cutting it out entirely.
Her commitment to flexitarianism means she occasionally enjoys seafood, though her meals are mostly plant-based.
Many restaurants offer menus that cater to flexitarianism as well as vegetarian and vegan diets.
