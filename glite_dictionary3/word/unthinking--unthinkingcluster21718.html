unthinking

adjective
mentally sluggish; not thoughtful
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or their behavior) characterized by mental sluggishness or a lack of active thought; inattentive or slow to use reason.

Examples

unthinking behavior shows
His unthinking response revealed how little attention he was paying to the details.
She blamed her unthinking actions on exhaustion and a lack of sleep.
The unthinking crowd followed the leader without question or hesitation.
