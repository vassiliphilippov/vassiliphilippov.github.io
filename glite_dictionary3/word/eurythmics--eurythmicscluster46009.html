eurythmics

noun
teaching music through movement
Pronunciation: / jʊˈrɪðmɪks / (AmE), / jʊəˈrɪðmɪks / (BrE)
a method of teaching musical understanding through harmonious bodily movement in response to musical rhythms

Examples

eurythmics class activity
Many schools incorporate eurythmics into their early music education programs to engage students physically and musically.
The music teacher used eurythmics to help children feel and understand the rhythm of the piece.
Through eurythmics, students learned to interpret musical phrases using coordinated body movements.
