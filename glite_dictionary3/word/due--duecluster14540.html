due

adverb
directly or exactly in a direction
Pronunciation: / duː / (AmE), / djuː / (BrE)
(adverb) Directly or exactly in a particular direction, especially on a compass; straight.

Examples

due north
Point the telescope due south to observe the star.
The explorers traveled due east to reach the coast.
We sailed due north for several days without changing course.
