sulfanilamide

noun
antibacterial sulfa drug compound
Pronunciation: / sʌlˌfænɪˈlæmaɪd / (AmE), / sʌlˌfænɪˈlæmaɪd / (BrE)
a white, odorless crystalline compound used as an antibacterial sulfa drug and as the parent compound of most sulfa drugs
