turnaround

noun
area for vehicles to turn around
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrnəˌraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnəraʊnd / (BrE)
an area of sufficient space for a vehicle to turn and go in the opposite direction

Examples

turnaround area
A gravel turnaround was provided at the end of the driveway for delivery trucks.
Please do not block the turnaround, as it is needed for emergency vehicles.
