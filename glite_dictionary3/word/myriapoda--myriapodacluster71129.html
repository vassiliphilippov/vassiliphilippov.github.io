Myriapoda

noun
a group of segmented arthropods, e.g. millipedes
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪriˈæpədə / (AmE), / ˌmɪrɪˈapədə / (BrE)
a group of arthropods with long, segmented bodies and many legs, especially including millipedes and similar creatures
