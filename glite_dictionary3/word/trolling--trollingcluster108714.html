trolling

noun
deliberate online provocation
Pronunciation: / ˈtroʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
informal behaviour on the internet of posting provocative, deceptive, or off-topic messages to provoke emotional reactions or disrupt discussion.

Examples

online trolling behavior
Online forums are often disrupted by trolling that aims to start angry debates among users.
Her posts were ignored because the moderators suspected she was engaging in trolling for attention.
He admitted his trolling had been intended as a joke but it upset several people.

Related Terms

  • synonym – baiting: deliberately provoking someone by saying things designed to annoy them
  • synonym – flaming: sending angry or insulting messages online, often to provoke a hostile response
