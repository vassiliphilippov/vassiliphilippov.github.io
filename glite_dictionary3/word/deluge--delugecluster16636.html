deluge

noun
overwhelming quantity or flood
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛludʒ / (AmE), / ˈdɛljuːdʒ / (BrE)
A large quantity of something that arrives at the same time, overwhelming in number or amount.

Examples

a deluge of messages
A sudden deluge of criticism forced the politician to reconsider the proposal.
During the holiday sale, the website experienced a deluge of orders within hours.
The customer service team was faced with a deluge of emails after the software crash.

Related Terms

  • synonym – flood: a large amount of something that comes at once
  • synonym – torrent: a sudden, large amount of something (figurative)
