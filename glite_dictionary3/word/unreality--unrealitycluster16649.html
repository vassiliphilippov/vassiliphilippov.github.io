unreality

noun
difficulty dealing with reality
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.riˈæ.lə.ti / (AmE), / ˌʌn.riˈæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
a lack of practical engagement or ineptitude in dealing with real situations; a state in which a person is detached from reality

Examples

unreality in daily life
His sense of unreality made it difficult for him to respond to the emergency.
She accused him of unreality when he ignored the obvious problems at work.
The manager's unreality about the company's finances led to poor decisions.
