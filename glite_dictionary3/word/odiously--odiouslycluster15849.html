odiously

adverb
in a hateful, offensive manner
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊdiəsli / (AmE), / ˈəʊdiəsli / (BrE)
In a way that arouses or deserves hatred or strong dislike; in an offensive and hateful manner.

Examples

odiously rude
He was odiously smug, always belittling those around him without any shame.
She treated her colleagues odiously, making everyone feel uncomfortable and unwelcome.
The dictator ruled odiously, showing no regard for human rights or justice.

Related Terms

  • antonym – pleasantly: in a pleasing or agreeable manner
