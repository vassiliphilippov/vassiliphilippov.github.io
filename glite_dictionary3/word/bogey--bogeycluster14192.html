bogey

noun
golf score of one over par
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊ.ɡi / (AmE), / ˈbəʊ.ɡi / (BrE)
(in golf) a score of one stroke over par on a hole.

Examples

a bogey on a hole
After two birdies and a bogey, she finished the front nine just over par.
He made a bogey on the final hole, missing par by just one stroke.
If you play safe on this hole, you’ll probably end up with a bogey.
