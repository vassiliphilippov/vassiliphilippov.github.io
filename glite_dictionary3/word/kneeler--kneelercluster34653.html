kneeler

noun
person who is kneeling
Pronunciation: / ˈniːlər / (AmE), / ˈniːlə / (BrE)
a person who is in a kneeling position, especially while praying or performing a task

Examples

praying kneeler
A kneeler on the grass watched the game from the sidelines.
Each kneeler at the ceremony bowed their head in silence.
The church was quiet except for the single kneeler praying by the altar.
