springer

noun
a large spaniel breed of dog
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋə / (BrE)
a breed of large spaniel with a wavy, silky coat, usually black or liver and white, originally bred for flushing game

Examples

springer spaniel dog
She has owned a springer since she was a child, and they went on long walks together.
The springer bounded across the field in pursuit of a pheasant.
Their springer loves swimming and retrieving sticks from the lake.
