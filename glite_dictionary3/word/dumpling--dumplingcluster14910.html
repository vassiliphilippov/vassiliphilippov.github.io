dumpling

noun
small ball or strip of boiled/steamed dough
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmplɪŋ /; / ˈdʌm.plɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʌmplɪŋ /; / ˈdʌm.plɪŋ / (BrE)
a small ball or strip of dough, plain or filled, boiled, steamed, or sometimes fried, eaten as food

Examples

chicken soup with dumplings

She carefully rolled out the dough to make dumplings for the stew.
The restaurant serves delicious pork dumplings with spicy dipping sauce.
She made a batch of dumplings for the family dinner.
Some Asian cuisines use rice flour to make their dumplings extra delicate.
The chicken stew was served with fluffy dumplings on top.
