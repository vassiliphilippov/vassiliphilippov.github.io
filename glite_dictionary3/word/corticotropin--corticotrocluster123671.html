corticotropin

noun
pituitary hormone that stimulates adrenal cortex
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔrtɪkoʊˈtroʊpɪn / (AmE), / ˌkɔːtɪkəʊˈtrəʊpɪn / (BrE)
