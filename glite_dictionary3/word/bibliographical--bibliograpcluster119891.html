bibliographical

adjective
relating to bibliography or book lists
Pronunciation: / ˌbɪbliəˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌbɪbliəˈɡræfɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or dealing with bibliography, especially the listing, cataloging, or systematic description of books and written works

Examples
