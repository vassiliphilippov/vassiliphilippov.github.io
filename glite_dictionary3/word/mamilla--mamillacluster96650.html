mamilla

noun
nipple; small breast projection
Pronunciation: / məˈmɪlə / (AmE), / məˈmɪlə / (BrE)
A small rounded projection, especially the nipple of a mammary gland.

Examples

mamilla of breast
Reconstruction of the mamilla is often part of breast surgery after mastectomy.
The infant instinctively turned toward the mother's mamilla to nurse.
The surgeon examined the patient's mamilla for any signs of abnormality.
