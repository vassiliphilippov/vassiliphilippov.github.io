momot

noun
tropical American bird with green and blue plumage
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊˌmɑt / (AmE), / ˈməʊˌmɒt / (BrE)
A tropical American bird related to the motmot, characterized by green and blue plumage and a resemblance to a blue jay.
