instruction

noun
the act or process of teaching
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstrʌkʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈstrʌkʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of teaching, especially in a formal setting; the activities involved in imparting knowledge or skill to others

Examples

instruction session
All new employees must attend instruction on workplace safety during their first week.
His success in mathematics was due to excellent instruction from dedicated teachers.
The school offers specialized instruction for children with learning disabilities.
