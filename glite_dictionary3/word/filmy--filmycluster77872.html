filmy

adjective
very thin and almost transparent
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlmi / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmi / (BrE)
very thin and light, often almost transparent or able to transmit light; resembling a film.

Examples

filmy veil
Mist drifted over the lake in a filmy veil.
She wore a filmy scarf that shimmered in the morning sun.
The butterfly's filmy wings looked almost invisible against the sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym – diaphanous: light, delicate, and translucent
  • antonym – opaque: not able to be seen through; not transparent
  • synonym – sheer: very thin, almost transparent
