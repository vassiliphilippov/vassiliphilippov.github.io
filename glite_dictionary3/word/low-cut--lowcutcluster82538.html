low-cut

adjective
having a low neckline (of clothes)
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ ˌkʌt /; / ˌloʊˈkʌt / (AmE), / ˈləʊ ˌkʌt /; / ˌləʊˈkʌt / (BrE)
(of clothing) having a neckline or edge cut lower than usual, so that more skin is exposed
