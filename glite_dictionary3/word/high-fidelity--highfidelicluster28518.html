high-fidelity

adjective
minimal distortion in sound reproduction
Pronunciation: / haɪ fəˈdɛlɪti / (AmE), / haɪ fɪˈdɛlɪti / (BrE)
(of audio equipment or recording) producing sound with very little distortion, closely resembling the original.

Examples

high-fidelity sound system
Audiophiles often invest in high-fidelity speakers for the best listening experience.
Our new studio is outfitted for high-fidelity sound production.
The concert was recorded with high-fidelity equipment to capture every musical detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hi-fi: audio equipment with high-fidelity sound quality
