spendthrift

noun
person who spends money extravagantly
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛndˌθrɪft / (AmE), / ˈspɛndˌθrɪft / (BrE)
a person who spends money in a careless or extravagant way

Examples

a spendthrift friend
His spendthrift ways left him with little savings.
Sarah is a notorious spendthrift who buys expensive clothes she doesn't need.
The company suffered under the leadership of a spendthrift CEO.

Related Terms

  • antonym – miser: a person who hoards wealth and spends as little money as possible
  • synonym – prodigal: a person who spends money wastefully
