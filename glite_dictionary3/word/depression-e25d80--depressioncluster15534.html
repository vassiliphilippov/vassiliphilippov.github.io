depression

noun
intense sadness or despondency
Pronunciation: / dɪˈprɛʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈprɛʃən / (BrE)
a mental state of persistent sadness, loss of interest, or a serious medical condition involving these feelings

Examples

suffer from depression
After losing his job, he fell into a deep depression that lasted for months.
Many people experience feelings of depression during difficult times in their lives.
She sought therapy to help her cope with her ongoing depression.
During episodes of depression, even simple tasks can feel overwhelming.
Many people with depression find it hard to enjoy activities they once loved.
She has been struggling with depression since losing her job last year.
After months of persistent sadness and fatigue, she was diagnosed with clinical depression.
Individuals diagnosed with depression often require support from mental health professionals.
Treatment options for major depression may include therapy and medication.
