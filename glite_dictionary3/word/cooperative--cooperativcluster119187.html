cooperative

adjective
working together with others
Pronunciation: / koʊˈɑːpəˌreɪtɪv /; / koʊˈɑːpərətɪv, koʊˈɑːprətɪv /; / koʊˈɑːpərətɪv / (AmE), / kəʊˈɒpərətɪv /; / kəʊˈɒpərətɪv, kəʊˈɒprətɪv / (BrE)
willing to work with others or involving people working together for a common goal
