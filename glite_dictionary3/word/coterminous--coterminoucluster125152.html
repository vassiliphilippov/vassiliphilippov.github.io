coterminous

adjective
having the same boundaries or extent
Pronunciation: / koʊˈtɜːrmɪnəs / (AmE), / kəʊˈtɜːmɪnəs / (BrE)
having the same boundaries or extent in space, time, or meaning as something else; sharing equal scope or duration

Examples

coterminous with the contract
His tenure as president was coterminous with a period of economic growth.
The company's fiscal year is coterminous with the calendar year.
The two countries' borders are coterminous along the river for several hundred miles.

Related Terms

  • synonym – conterminous: having a common boundary or bordering; nearly identical in meaning and use to 'coterminous'
