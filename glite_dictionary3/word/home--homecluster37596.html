home

noun
institution for people needing care
Pronunciation: / hoʊm / (AmE), / həʊm / (BrE)
an institution for people who need care, such as the elderly or children, who cannot live independently

Examples

care home
After his stroke, he moved into a home to receive round-the-clock care.
Her grandmother has lived in the home for several years.
Many children in the home were waiting to be adopted.

Related Terms

  • synonym – care home: a home providing accommodation and care for people who are unable to look after themselves
  • synonym – nursing home: an institution that provides nursing care for the elderly or disabled
