sweat

noun
nervous agitation or worry
Pronunciation: / swɛt / (AmE), / swɛt / (BrE)
a state of nervous agitation or anxiety caused by worry about something

Examples

in a sweat of worry
Don't get in such a sweat over the small stuff.
He was in a real sweat before his interview.
She broke out in a sweat waiting for the results.

  • antonym – calm: a state of being relaxed and not worried
  • degree higher – panic: sudden uncontrollable fear or anxiety
