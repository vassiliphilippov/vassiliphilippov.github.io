vena ileocolica

noun
a vein draining parts of the lower intestine
Pronunciation: / ˌviː.nə ˌaɪ.li.oʊˈkoʊ.lɪ.kə / (AmE), / ˌviː.nə ˌaɪ.li.əʊˈkəʊ.lɪ.kə / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the end of the ileum, the appendix, the cecum, and the lower part of the ascending colon

Examples

vena ileocolica drains intestine
Damage to the vena ileocolica can lead to significant bleeding during intestinal surgery.
The vena ileocolica drains venous blood from the lower right quadrant of the abdomen.
The surgeon carefully identified the vena ileocolica before proceeding with the operation.
