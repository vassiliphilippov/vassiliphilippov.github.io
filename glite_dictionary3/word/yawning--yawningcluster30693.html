yawning

adjective
showing lack of attention or boredom
Pronunciation: / ˈjɔnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈjɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or group) characterized by lack of attention or boredom, often shown through yawning.

Examples

yawning silence
A yawning silence filled the room during the dull presentation.
The speaker tried to energize the yawning audience with a joke.
The teacher noticed a yawning class and decided to change her strategy.

Related Terms

  • antonym – attentive: paying close attention
  • synonym – bored: feeling weary and uninterested
