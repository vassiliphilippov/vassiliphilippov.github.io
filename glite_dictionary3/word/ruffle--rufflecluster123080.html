to ruffle

verb
pleat or gather fabric decoratively
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌf.əl / (AmE), / ˈrʌf.əl / (BrE)
to pleat or gather fabric so that it forms a decorative frill or ruffle

Examples

to ruffle fabric edges
Before sewing, you need to ruffle the curtain fabric to achieve the desired effect.
She decided to ruffle the edges of the tablecloth for a fancy look.
The seamstress expertly ruffled the hem of the dress.
