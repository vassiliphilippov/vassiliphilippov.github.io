payslip

noun
pay document showing earnings and deductions
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪ.slɪp / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ.slɪp / (BrE)
a document given to an employee that shows the amount of money they have earned and the deductions made, such as tax and insurance

Examples

payslip with salary details
Her payslip showed several deductions for tax and pension contributions.
When he noticed an error on his payslip, he contacted HR immediately.
You should keep your payslip in case you need to prove your income.
