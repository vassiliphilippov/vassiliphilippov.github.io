thumbnail

noun
small preview image
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌm.neɪl / (AmE), / ˈθʌm.neɪl / (BrE)
a small, reduced-size image used to represent or preview a larger image, video, or file, typically used on websites, in galleries, or file browsers.

Examples

thumbnail image preview
The webpage displayed a thumbnail of each photo to speed up loading.
She clicked the thumbnail to view the high-resolution version of the landscape photograph.
The video editor automatically generated thumbnails to help users find the right clip quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – icon: a small graphical representation used to represent a file, application, or function
  • synonym – preview: a short or small view of something meant to show or sample the full content
