to incarnadine

verb
color or make flesh-colored
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑrnəˌdin / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɑːnədiːn / (BrE)
to make or color something flesh-colored, especially with a pinkish or reddish hue

Examples

to incarnadine the sky
A warm light seemed to incarnadine the clouds at sunset.
Her memories of those days incarnadined her thoughts with nostalgia.
The artist incarnadined the cherub's cheeks to give them a lifelike glow.
