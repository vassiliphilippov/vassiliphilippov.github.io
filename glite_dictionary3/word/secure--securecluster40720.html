secure

adjective
free from fear or doubt
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
feeling confident and free from fear or doubt; not worried or anxious

Examples

feel secure at work
After talking to her manager, she felt much more secure in her job.
Children need to feel secure and loved at home.
He is secure in the knowledge that he did his best.
