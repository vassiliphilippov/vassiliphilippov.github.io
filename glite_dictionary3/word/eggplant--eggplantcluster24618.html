eggplant

noun
vegetable with shiny purple skin
Pronunciation: / ˈɛɡˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈɛɡˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
a vegetable with shiny purple (sometimes white or yellow) skin and soft flesh, or the plant that produces it, used in cooking

Examples

grilled eggplant slices

The recipe calls for one large eggplant, diced into cubes.
If you roast eggplant, its flesh becomes soft and flavorful.
