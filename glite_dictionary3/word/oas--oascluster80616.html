OAS

noun
Organization of American States (international body)
Pronunciation: / oʊ eɪ ɛs / (AmE), / əʊ eɪ ɛs / (BrE)
an international organization of most countries in the Americas, founded in 1948 to promote economic, military, social, and cultural cooperation

Examples

OAS meeting
Diplomats from the OAS worked together to resolve a regional conflict.
