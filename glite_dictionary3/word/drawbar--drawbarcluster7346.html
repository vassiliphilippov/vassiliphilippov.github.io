drawbar

noun
metal bar for coupling vehicles or equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɔˌbɑr / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːbɑː / (BrE)
a strong metal bar with a hook or coupling used to attach one vehicle or piece of equipment to another for pulling

Examples

drawbar of a tractor
Always check the condition of the drawbar before attaching any equipment.
The engineer repaired a damaged drawbar on the freight car.
The tractor's drawbar was used to tow the heavy trailer across the field.
