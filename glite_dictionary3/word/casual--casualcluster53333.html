casual

adjective
suitable for ordinary or everyday occasions
Pronunciation: / ˈkæʒuəl / (AmE), / ˈkæʒjʊəl / (BrE)
designed or suitable for everyday use, rather than for formal occasions

Examples

casual clothes
Athletes often change into casual attire after leaving the field.
He prefers to wear casual clothes on weekends instead of a suit and tie.
The invitation said the dress code was casual, so jeans and a T-shirt were perfectly acceptable.
