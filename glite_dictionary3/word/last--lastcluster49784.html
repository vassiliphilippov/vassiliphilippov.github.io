last

adjective
not able to be changed; final
Pronunciation: / læst / (AmE), / lɑːst / (BrE)
(of a decision, statement, or action) not to be changed or reversed; final or definitive.

Examples

last decision
After months of debate, the committee's decision is last and cannot be appealed.
The manager's choice will be the last word on the matter.
The supreme court has the last authority in legal disputes.
