vaporous

adjective
almost transparent; thin and light
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪ.pɚ.əs / (AmE), / ˈveɪ.pər.əs / (BrE)
Extremely thin or light to the point of being almost transparent or able to transmit light.

Examples

vaporous scarf fabric
She wore a vaporous scarf that fluttered in the breeze.
The artist painted vaporous clouds drifting across a pale blue sky.
Through the vaporous curtains, sunlight softly filled the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym – diaphanous: light, delicate, and translucent, typically of fabric
  • antonym – opaque: not able to be seen through; not transparent or translucent
  • synonym – sheer: very thin; almost transparent, as of fabric
