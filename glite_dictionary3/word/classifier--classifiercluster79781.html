classifier

noun
linguistic word class indicator
Pronunciation: / ˈklæsɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
A word or morpheme used in some languages, especially in counting, to indicate the semantic class or category of the noun being referred to.

Examples

Chinese classifier word
A classifier can specify whether something is flat, round, or long in shape.
In Mandarin Chinese, the classifier 'ge' is used for many common objects.
Languages like Thai and Japanese also use classifier words in counting nouns.
