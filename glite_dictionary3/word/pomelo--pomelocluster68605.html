pomelo

noun
large citrus fruit similar to grapefruit
Pronunciation: / pəˈmɛloʊ / (AmE), / pəˈmiːləʊ / (BrE)
a large citrus fruit with a thick rind and sweet or juicy flesh, similar to a grapefruit but usually larger, or the evergreen tree that bears this fruit
