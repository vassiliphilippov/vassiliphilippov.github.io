felucca

noun
Mediterranean sailing boat
Pronunciation: / fəˈlʌkə / (AmE), / fəˈlʌkə / (BrE)
A fast, narrow sailing boat of the Mediterranean, traditionally rigged with one or more lateen sails.

sail a felucca boat
