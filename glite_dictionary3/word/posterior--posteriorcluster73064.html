posterior

noun
a back tooth; molar or premolar
Pronunciation: / pɑːˈstɪriɚ / (AmE), / pɒˈstɪəriə / (BrE)
a tooth located at the back of the mouth, such as a molar or premolar.

Examples

posterior tooth pain
Proper brushing should include attention to each posterior near the back of the jaw.
Removal of an impacted posterior can be more complicated than other teeth.
The dentist examined the patient's posterior for signs of decay.
