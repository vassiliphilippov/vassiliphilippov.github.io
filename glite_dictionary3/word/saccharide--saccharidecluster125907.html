saccharide

noun
type of carbohydrate (sugar, starch)
Pronunciation: / ˈsæk.əˌraɪd / (AmE), / ˈsak.ə.rʌɪd / (BrE)
any of a class of carbohydrates, including sugars and starches, that serve as structural components in living cells and as a source of energy
