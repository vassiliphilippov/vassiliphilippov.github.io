prissy

adjective
excessively fastidious; easily shocked or disgusted
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪsi / (AmE), / ˈprɪsi / (BrE)
excessively fussy, proper, or easily shocked, especially about cleanliness, neatness, or behaving correctly

Examples
