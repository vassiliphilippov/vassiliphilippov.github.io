unobtrusive

adjective
not attracting attention; inconspicuous
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəbˈtruːsɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnəbˈtruːsɪv / (BrE)
Not attracting attention or standing out in an undesirable way; discreet or inconspicuous.

Examples

unobtrusive presence
His unobtrusive manner made him well-liked by colleagues who valued privacy.
She preferred to be unobtrusive at social gatherings, blending into the background.
The security cameras were placed in unobtrusive locations throughout the building.
