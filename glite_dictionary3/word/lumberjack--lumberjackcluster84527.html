lumberjack

noun
a short, warm outer jacket
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌmbɚˌdʒæk / (AmE), / ˈlʌmbəˌdʒak / (BrE)
A short, warm outer jacket, typically with a checkered pattern and associated with outdoor work, especially logging.
