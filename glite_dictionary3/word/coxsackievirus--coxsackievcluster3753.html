coxsackievirus

noun
group of enteroviruses causing disease
Pronunciation: / kɑkˈsækiˌvaɪrəs / (AmE), / kɒkˈsækiˌvʌɪərəs / (BrE)
a group of enteroviruses that cause various diseases, sometimes resembling poliomyelitis but typically without paralysis

Examples

coxsackievirus infection
