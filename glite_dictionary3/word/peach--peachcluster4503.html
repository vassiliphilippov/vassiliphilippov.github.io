peach

noun
attractive woman (informal)
Pronunciation: / pitʃ / (AmE), / piːtʃ / (BrE)
(informal, sometimes offensive) An attractive or seductive woman.

Examples

she is a peach

He called her a peach, but she rolled her eyes at the old-fashioned compliment.
In the 1960s, movie magazines often referred to glamorous actresses as "Hollywood peaches."
