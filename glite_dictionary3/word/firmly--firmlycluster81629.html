firmly

adverb
with strong determination
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝːmli / (AmE), / ˈfɜːmli / (BrE)
in a way that shows strong determination or resolve

Examples

firmly believe something
He firmly believes that hard work will pay off in the end.
She firmly refused to change her decision despite the pressure.
You need to speak firmly if you want to be taken seriously.

Related Terms
