perfunctory

adjective
done with little effort or as a formality
Pronunciation: / pərˈfəŋktəri /; / pərˈfʌŋktəri / (AmE), / pəˈfʌŋktəri / (BrE)
(of an action or gesture) done with little effort, interest, or enthusiasm; as a formality, without real attention
