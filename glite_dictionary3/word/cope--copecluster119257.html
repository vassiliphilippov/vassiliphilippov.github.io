cope

noun
brick or stone capping at a wall's top
Pronunciation: / koʊp / (AmE), / kəʊp / (BrE)
A brick or stone that is placed sideways at the top of a wall to form a protective cap or finish.

Examples

stone cope cap
After years of weather, the cope needed to be replaced to prevent leaks.
The mason carefully positioned the cope on top of the new garden wall.
They chose a decorative stone cope for the boundary wall to enhance its appearance.
