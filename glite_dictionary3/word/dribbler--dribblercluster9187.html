dribbler

noun
person who dribbles a ball or saliva
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪb.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈdrɪb.lə / (BrE)
a person, especially a player, who dribbles; someone who moves a ball by repeatedly bouncing or controlling it, or a person (such as a baby) who lets saliva or liquid drip from their mouth

Examples

messy dribbler baby
Among the basketball team, Jordan is the best dribbler with the ball.
Her baby is quite a dribbler and always needs to wear a bib.
The young soccer player was an excellent dribbler and could get past any defender.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dribble: to move or let fall in drops, or to control the ball repeatedly in sports
