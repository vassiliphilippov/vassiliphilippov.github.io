Cygnus

proper noun
ISS cargo spacecraft
Pronunciation: / c8s6agn59s / (AmE), / c8s6agn59s / (BrE)
A robotic cargo spacecraft (developed by Orbital ATK and launched by Northrop Grumman) used to carry supplies and experiments to the International Space Station.
