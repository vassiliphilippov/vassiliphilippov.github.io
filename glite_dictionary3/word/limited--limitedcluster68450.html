limited

adjective
restricted in amount or scope
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪtɪd / (BrE)
restricted in amount, size, quality, or scope; not large, not great, or not impressive

Examples

limited resources
Access to the database is limited to authorized users only.
His ability to help was limited by the resources available.

His skills as a pianist are rather limited compared to others in the competition.
The team's limited performance last season disappointed many fans.
