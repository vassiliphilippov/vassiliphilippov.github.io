Columba

noun
genus of typical pigeons
Pronunciation: / koʊˈlʌmbə / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Columbidae, comprising the typical pigeons, including the rock pigeon and related species.

Examples

Columba species list
Many domestic pigeons are descended from wild birds in the Columba genus.
Ornithologists study several species within the genus Columba to understand their migration patterns.
The rock pigeon, or Columba livia, is commonly found in cities worldwide.
