to naturalise

verb
adapt a wild plant or land to an environment
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃrəˌlaɪz /; / ˈnætʃərəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈnatʃərəlaɪz /; / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəlʌɪz / (BrE)
