drinkable

adjective
safe or suitable for drinking
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪŋkəbəl / (AmE), / ˈdrɪŋkəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of a liquid) safe or pleasant to drink; suitable for drinking.

Examples

drinkable water
Some wines are not drinkable until they have aged for several years.
The tap water in this village is barely drinkable.
They found a spring with fresh, drinkable water in the mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym – potable: safe to drink (formal)
  • antonym – undrinkable: not suitable for drinking
