underlying

adjective
present but not immediately obvious
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɚˈlaɪɪŋ /; / ˈʌndɚˌlaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈlaɪɪŋ /; / ˈʌndəˌlaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
fundamental or basic but not immediately obvious; forming the real basis of something
