polished

adjective
shiny, smooth, or refined
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑlɪʃt /; / ˈpɑːlɪʃt / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪʃt / (BrE)
shiny and smooth from being rubbed, cleaned, or treated; refined or accomplished in appearance or surface
