to jibe

verb
be in agreement, match
Pronunciation: / dʒaɪb / (AmE), / dʒaɪb / (BrE)
to be in agreement, to match, or to be compatible with something

Examples

jibe with facts
Her account of the story didn't jibe with the facts uncovered in the investigation.
His experience doesn't jibe with my understanding of how the process works.
The numbers in your report don't jibe with those in the official records.

Related Terms

  • synonym – agree: to have the same opinion or to be consistent
  • synonym – coincide: to happen at the same time or correspond in nature
  • antonym – conflict: to be incompatible or at odds
