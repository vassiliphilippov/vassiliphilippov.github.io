undemocratically

adverb
in a non-democratic way
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndəˌmɛkrəˈtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˌmɛkrəˈtɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that does not follow democratic principles or procedures.

Examples

undemocratically change laws
Changes to the constitution were made undemocratically, with no public discussion allowed.
He was criticized for acting undemocratically in selecting the team leader.
The new rules were imposed undemocratically, without consulting the members.
