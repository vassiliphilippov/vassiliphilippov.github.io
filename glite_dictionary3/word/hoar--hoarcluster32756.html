hoar

noun
white frost of ice crystals on surfaces
Pronunciation: / hɔr / (AmE), / hɔː / (BrE)
A white frost consisting of ice crystals that form on surfaces, especially outdoors, in cold conditions.

Examples

hoar frost crystals
A delicate layer of hoar covered the branches, turning the forest silver.
In the early morning, the grass sparkled with hoar created by the cold night air.
The old fence posts were edged with hoar after the sudden drop in temperature.

Related Terms

  • synonym – frost: a deposit of small white ice crystals formed on the ground or other surfaces
  • synonym – rime: frost formed on cold objects by the rapid freezing of water vapor
