to obtund

verb
to dull or blunt, especially medically
Pronunciation: / əbˈtʌnd / (AmE), / əbˈtʌnd / (BrE)
to make less sharp, intense, or sensitive; to dull or blunt the edge of something, often used in medical or technical contexts

Examples

to obtund pain
Doctors observed that the sedative had obtunded her reflexes significantly.
Repeated exposure to the noise seemed to obtund his hearing over time.
The medication can obtund the sensation of pain in patients undergoing surgery.
