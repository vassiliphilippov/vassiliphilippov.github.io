stilbesterol

noun
synthetic nonsteroidal estrogen hormone
Pronunciation: / stɪlˈbɛstəˌrɔl / (AmE), / stɪlˈbɛstərɒl / (BrE)
A synthetic nonsteroidal estrogen, once used to treat menstrual disorders, but later found to cause cancer in daughters of treated women.
