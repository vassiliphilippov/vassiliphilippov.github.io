potter

noun
a person who makes pottery
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːtər / (AmE), / ˈpɒtə / (BrE)
a person who makes pottery by shaping clay and firing it in a kiln

Examples

skilled potter
A master potter always knows the right temperature to fire the clay in the kiln.
She has been a skilled potter for over twenty years, selling her work at local markets.
