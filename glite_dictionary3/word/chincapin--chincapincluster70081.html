chincapin

noun
edible nut from small US chestnut trees
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪŋkəˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪŋkəpɪn / (BrE)
A small edible nut from either of two species of small chestnut trees native to the southern United States, resembling a hazelnut.
