digestible

adjective
able to be digested by the body
Pronunciation: / daɪˈdʒɛstəbl̩ / (AmE), / dɪˈdʒɛstɪbl̩ / (BrE)
(of food or substances) able to be broken down and absorbed by the body through digestion.

Examples

easily digestible food
Fish is generally considered a highly digestible source of protein.
Many people find that cooked vegetables are more digestible than raw ones.
The tablets are coated to make them easily digestible for children.

Related Terms

  • antonym – indigestible: not able to be digested
