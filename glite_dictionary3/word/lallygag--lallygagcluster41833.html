to lallygag

verb
linger or idle aimlessly
Pronunciation: / ˈlæliˌɡæɡ / (AmE), / ˈlalɪˌɡaɡ / (BrE)
to spend time aimlessly or to be idle, often by lingering in a place with no apparent purpose
