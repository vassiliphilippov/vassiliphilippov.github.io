portrayal

noun
representation or likeness of a person
Pronunciation: / pɔrˈtreɪ.əl / (AmE), / pɔːˈtreɪ.əl / (BrE)
a representation or likeness of a person or thing in art, such as a painting, sculpture, photograph, or portrait
