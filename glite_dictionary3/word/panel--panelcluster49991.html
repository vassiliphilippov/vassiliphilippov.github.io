to panel

verb
cover with panels
Pronunciation: / ˈpænəl / (AmE), / ˈpanəl / (BrE)
to cover or decorate a surface, especially a wall or ceiling, with panels

Examples

to panel a wall
After the renovation, the team paneled the hallways with decorative metal sheets.
The contractors will panel the conference room to improve its acoustics and appearance.
They decided to panel the study with rich mahogany for a classic look.
