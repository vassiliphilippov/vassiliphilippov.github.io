expense

noun
money spent, often for business purposes
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛns / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛns / (BrE)
an amount of money spent on something; a cost or charge (especially for business or personal purposes)

Examples

expense report
Please submit your expenses from the business trip by Friday.
She carefully tracked every expense for her departmental budget.
Travel expenses are reimbursed if you provide the original receipts.
The company keeps a record of every expense for tax purposes.
Their monthly expenses included rent, utilities, and groceries.
Travel expenses can often be reimbursed by your employer if you keep receipts.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cost: the amount of money required to buy, do, or make something
  • synonym – expenditure: the action of spending funds; an amount spent
  • antonym – income: money received, especially on a regular basis, for work or through investments (contrast)
