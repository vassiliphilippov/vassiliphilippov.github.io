buffet

noun
sideboard with shelves and drawers
Pronunciation: / bəˈfeɪ / (AmE), / ˈbʌf.ɪt / (BrE)
A piece of furniture, typically placed along the wall of a dining room, with shelves and drawers for storing dishes, silverware, and table linens.

Examples

buffet with drawers
A large mirror hung above the buffet, reflecting the soft light from the chandelier.
The antique buffet in the dining room was inherited from my grandmother.
We arranged the plates and cutlery neatly inside the drawers of the buffet.
