amelia

noun
congenital absence of limbs
Pronunciation: / əˈmiːliə / (AmE), / əˈmiːliə / (BrE)
A congenital condition in which one or more limbs (arms or legs) are completely absent at birth.

Examples

born with amelia
Because amelia was detected early, the doctors planned a comprehensive rehabilitation program.
Cases of amelia are often associated with genetic mutations or prenatal environmental factors.
