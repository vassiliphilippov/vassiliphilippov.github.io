Santa Anna

proper noun
Mexican general and political leader (1795–1876)
Pronunciation: / ˌsæn.tə ˈæn.ə / (AmE), / ˌsæn.tə ˈæn.ə / (BrE)
(1795–1876) Mexican general and political leader; president of Mexico multiple times, known for attempting to suppress the Texas Revolution and for his involvement in the Mexican-American War.

Santa Anna was a pivotal figure during the Texas Revolution and the Mexican-American War.
