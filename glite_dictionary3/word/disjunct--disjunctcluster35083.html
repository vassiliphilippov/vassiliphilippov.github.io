disjunct

adjective
having head, thorax, and abdomen distinctly separated
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈdʒʌŋkt / (AmE), / dɪsˈdʒʌŋkt / (BrE)
(of an organism, especially an insect) having deep constrictions separating the head, thorax, and abdomen.

Examples

disjunct body segments
Certain wasps are easily identified by their disjunct body segments.
