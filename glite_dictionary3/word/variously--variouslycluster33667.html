variously

adverb
in different ways
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛriəsli / (AmE), / ˈvɛːrɪəsli / (BrE)
in several different ways; differently at different times or by different people

Examples

variously described by people
Critics have variously interpreted the artist’s intention behind the painting.
The collection has been variously described as charming, eccentric, or confusing.
The plan was variously supported and opposed by members of the committee.
