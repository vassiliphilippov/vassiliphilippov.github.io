apposition

noun
the grammatical relationship of two adjacent nouns
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌapəˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
the grammatical relationship between two noun phrases or elements, where the second explains or identifies the first, typically appearing side by side.
