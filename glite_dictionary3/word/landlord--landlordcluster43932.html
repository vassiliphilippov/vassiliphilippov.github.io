landlord

noun
person who rents out property
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌlɔrd / (AmE), / ˈlænd.lɔːd / (BrE)
a person or organization that owns property and rents it out to tenants

Examples

landlord collects rent
After years of renting, she finally met her new landlord in person.
Tenants must contact their landlord if they have any maintenance issues.
The landlord increased the rent after renovating the apartments.
