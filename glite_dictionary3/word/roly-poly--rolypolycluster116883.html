roly-poly

noun
a plump or rotund person
Pronunciation: / ˌroʊli ˈpoʊli / (AmE), / ˌrəʊli ˈpəʊli / (BrE)
a person who is round and plump in appearance or build; a rotund individual

Examples

roly-poly little kid
As a child, I was a roly-poly who adored sweets and pastries.
Everyone loved Uncle Benny, a cheerful roly-poly with a booming laugh.
The toddlers in the park were a group of roly-poly little ones running after bubbles.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chubby: slightly fat in a way that is endearing
  • antonym – skinny: very thin, especially in an unattractive or unhealthy way
