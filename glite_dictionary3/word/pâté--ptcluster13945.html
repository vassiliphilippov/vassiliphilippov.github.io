pâté

noun
rich savory meat paste or spread
Pronunciation: / pɑːˈteɪ / (AmE), / pæˈteɪ / (BrE)
a rich, savory paste made of finely minced or ground liver, meat, or poultry, usually seasoned and served as a spread or filling

Examples

pate on bread
Duck liver pâté is a popular appetizer in French cuisine.
He impressed his guests by making homemade chicken pâté for the party.
She spread a thick layer of pâté on a slice of toasted baguette.
