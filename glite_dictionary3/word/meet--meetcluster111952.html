to meet

verb
be present to greet on arrival
Pronunciation: / mit / (AmE), / miːt / (BrE)
to be present at a place in order to greet or receive someone upon their arrival

Examples

to meet the guests
Could you meet the guests at the front desk this afternoon?
Emma was happy to meet her friend at the train station after years apart.
I will meet you at the airport when your flight arrives.
