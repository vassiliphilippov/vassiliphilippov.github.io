fitting

noun
furnishings or equipment (often plural)
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
an item of furniture or equipment, especially as part of the fixed furnishings in a room, ship, or hotel; usually used in the plural.

Examples

fitting in the kitchen
All the kitchen fittings will be replaced during the renovation.
The luxurious hotel is known for its elegant fittings and decor.
The ship's wooden fittings have been restored to their original state.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fixture: an item of equipment that is fixed in position in a building
