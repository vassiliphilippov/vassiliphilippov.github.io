Burhinus oedicnemus

noun
species of nocturnal shorebird (Eurasian stone-curlew)
Pronunciation: / bɝːˈhaɪnəs ˌiːdɪkˈniːməs / (AmE), / bɜːˈhɪnəs ˌiːdɪkˈniːməs / (BrE)
A species of large-headed, large-eyed shorebird, active at dawn and dusk or at night, found in the Old World and parts of tropical America, notable for its thickened knee joint; also known as the Eurasian stone-curlew.
