to curtsey

verb
bend knees in respectful greeting
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜːrt.si / (AmE), / ˈkɜːt.si / (BrE)
(of a woman or girl) to lower the body briefly by bending the knees as a gesture of respect, typically in greeting or before someone of higher rank.

Examples

to curtsey before royalty
At the end of the performance, the ballerina curtseyed gracefully to the audience.
The little girls spent the afternoon practicing how to curtsey properly in dance class.
Whenever she entered the room, she would curtsey before the queen.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bow: bend the upper part of the body forward as a gesture of respect or greeting
