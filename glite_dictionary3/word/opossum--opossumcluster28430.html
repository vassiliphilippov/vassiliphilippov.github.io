opossum

noun
nocturnal American marsupial with a prehensile tail
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑsəm / (AmE), / əˈpɒsəm / (BrE)
A small to medium-sized nocturnal marsupial found in the Americas, with a hairless prehensile tail and an ability to climb trees.

Examples

American opossum
An opossum was spotted rummaging through the garbage cans last night.
During the camping trip, we saw a mother opossum carrying her babies on her back.
The opossum curled its tail around the tree branch as it climbed higher.

Related Terms

  • synonym – marsupial: animal of a group that carries and nurses young in a pouch
  • antonym – possum: marsupial found in Australia and New Guinea
