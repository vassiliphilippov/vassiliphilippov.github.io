mechanical

adjective
relating to or operated by machines or mechanisms
Pronunciation: / məˈkænɪkəl / (AmE), / mɪˈkænɪkəl / (BrE)
operated by or relating to machines or tools; resembling the movement or behavior of a machine rather than a living thing

Examples

mechanical voice response
Her response sounded mechanical, as if she were reciting a script.
The old clock is powered by a mechanical winding mechanism.
We were fascinated by the mechanical dinosaur at the science museum.

Related Terms

  • synonym – automatic: working by itself with little or no direct human control
