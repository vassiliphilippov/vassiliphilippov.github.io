Dionaea muscipula

noun
carnivorous plant, Venus flytrap
Pronunciation: / daɪ.oʊˈniː.ə mʌsˈsɪpjʊlə / (AmE), / dʌɪ.əˈnɪə mʌsˈsɪpjʊlə / (BrE)
A carnivorous plant native to the coastal plains of the Carolinas, known for its hinged, bristled leaf blades that close to trap insects; commonly called the Venus flytrap.
