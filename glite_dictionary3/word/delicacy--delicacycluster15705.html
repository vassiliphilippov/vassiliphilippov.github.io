delicacy

noun
the quality of being delicate and beautiful
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛlɪkəsi / (AmE), / ˈdɛlɪkəsi / (BrE)
the quality of being delicate and beautiful in appearance

Examples

delicacy of design
He admired the delicacy of the glass sculpture, afraid that even a touch might break it.
The delicacy of her features made her portrait especially captivating.
There was a certain delicacy in the way she arranged the flowers in the vase.
