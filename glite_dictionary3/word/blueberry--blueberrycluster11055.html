blueberry

noun
small, edible dark-blue berry
Pronunciation: / ˈbluˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌbɛri / (BrE)
A small, sweet, edible dark-blue berry that grows on various shrubs, often used in cooking and baking.

Examples

fresh blueberries in yogurt
She added fresh blueberries to her morning cereal for extra flavor.
The blueberry pie was a hit at the family picnic.
Wild blueberries are smaller and have a more intense taste than cultivated ones.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bilberry: a similar small edible blue-black berry from a different shrub
