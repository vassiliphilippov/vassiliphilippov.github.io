unproven

adjective
not proved or demonstrated
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈproʊvən / (AmE), / ʌnˈpruːvən / (BrE)
not having been demonstrated to be true or valid; not proved.

Examples

unproven allegations
She was accused based on unproven allegations.
The effectiveness of the treatment is still unproven.
The theory remains unproven despite years of research.

Related Terms

  • antonym – proven: shown by evidence or argument to be true or existent
  • synonym – unsubstantiated: not supported or proven by evidence
  • synonym – unverified: not confirmed as true or accurate
