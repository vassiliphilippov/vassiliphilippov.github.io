beef-witted

adjective
slow-witted or foolish (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːf ˌwɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈbiːf ˌwɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a person) slow-witted; lacking intelligence or quickness of mind, foolish.

Examples

a beef-witted fool
His beef-witted remarks revealed a lack of understanding of the issue.
In old comedies, the beef-witted character often became the butt of jokes.
She called him beef-witted after he made another obvious mistake.

Related Terms

  • synonym – foolish: lacking good sense or judgment
