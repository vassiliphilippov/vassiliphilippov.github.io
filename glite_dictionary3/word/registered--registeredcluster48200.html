registered

adjective
officially listed as qualified
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdʒɪstɚd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstəd / (BrE)
officially listed or recorded as having the necessary qualifications, especially to practice a profession or perform a specific function

Examples

registered nurse
Only registered electricians are allowed to sign off on the safety of this building.
She visited a registered dietitian for advice on her meal plans.
To work as a registered nurse in this country, you must pass specific exams.

Related Terms

  • synonym – licensed: having official permission to do something, especially professionally
  • antonym – unregistered: not officially recorded or enlisted
