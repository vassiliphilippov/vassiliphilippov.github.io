Mohammedan

noun
old term for Muslim; follower of Mohammed
Pronunciation: / moʊˈhæmədən / (AmE), / məˈhæmɪdən / (BrE)
A follower of Mohammed; an old term for a Muslim, now considered outdated and sometimes offensive.

Examples

a Mohammedan believer
He wrote a book about the laws and customs of Mohammedan societies in the Middle Ages.
The old documents referred to the population as Mohammedan rather than Muslim.
Use of the term Mohammedan is discouraged today because it is considered outdated and inaccurate.
