amphiuma

noun
an aquatic, eel-shaped salamander
Pronunciation: / æmˈfjuːmə / (AmE), / æmˈfjuːmə / (BrE)
a large, aquatic salamander of the genus Amphiuma, found in the southern United States, characterized by its eel-like body and very small limbs
