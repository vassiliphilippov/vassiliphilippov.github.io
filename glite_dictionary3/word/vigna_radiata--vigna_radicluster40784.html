Vigna radiata

noun
plant species, also called mung bean
Pronunciation: / ˌvaɪɡnə ɹædˈiːətə / (AmE), / ˌvɪɡnə rædˈɪətə / (BrE)
a bushy annual plant widely cultivated in Asia and other warm regions for its edible seeds, commonly known as mung beans; the main source of bean sprouts in Chinese cooking.
