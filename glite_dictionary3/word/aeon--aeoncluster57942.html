aeon

noun
an immeasurably long period of time
Pronunciation: / ˈiː.ɑn / (AmE), / ˈiː.ɒn / (BrE)
An immeasurably or indefinitely long period of time; an age.

Examples

an aeon passes
It seemed like an aeon had passed before the results were announced.
Some believe the universe has existed for countless aeons.
The ancient ruins have stood silent for aeons, shrouded in mystery.
