punter

noun
football player who punts the ball
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌnɚ / (AmE), / ˈpʌntə / (BrE)
a player in football who drops the ball from their hands and kicks it before it touches the ground, usually to clear it downfield

Examples

football punter
As a rookie punter, he quickly earned the coach's trust with his consistent performance.
The punter lined up and booted the ball deep into the opponent's territory.
The team's punter had an amazing game, averaging over fifty yards per kick.
