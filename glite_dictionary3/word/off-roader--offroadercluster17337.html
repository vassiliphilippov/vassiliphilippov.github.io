off-roader

noun
bicycle for rough terrain
Pronunciation: / ˌɔfˈroʊdər / (AmE), / ˌɒfˈrəʊdə / (BrE)
a bicycle with a strong frame and wide tires, designed for use on rough or mountainous terrain rather than paved roads

Examples

off-roader mountain bike
He bought a new off-roader to tackle the rugged mountain trails.
Her old off-roader handled the forest route much better than a standard road bike.
Many cyclists prefer an off-roader when riding on dirt paths or rocky landscapes.
