sirenian

noun
large herbivorous aquatic mammal (order Sirenia)
Pronunciation: / saɪˈriːniən / (AmE), / saɪˈriːniən / (BrE)
a large herbivorous aquatic mammal of the order Sirenia, such as a manatee or dugong, characterized by paddle-shaped tails and flipper-like forelimbs
