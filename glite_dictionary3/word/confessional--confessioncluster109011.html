confessional

noun
booth for hearing confessions (church)
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɛʃənl̩ / (AmE), / kənˈfɛʃənəl / (BrE)
a small enclosed booth or room in a church where a priest hears confessions from members of the congregation

Examples

The confessional was decorated with ornate wood carvings.
