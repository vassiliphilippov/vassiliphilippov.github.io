auspex

noun
Roman omen-interpreting priest
Pronunciation: / ˈɔspɛks / (AmE), / ˈɔːspɛks / (BrE)
(in ancient Rome) a religious official who interpreted omens, especially from the behavior of birds, to guide public decisions or policy.
