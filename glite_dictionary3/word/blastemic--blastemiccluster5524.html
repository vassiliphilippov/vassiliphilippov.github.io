blastemic

adjective
relating to blastema (cell mass)
Pronunciation: / blæˈstiːmɪk / (AmE), / blɑːˈstiːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a blastema, an undifferentiated mass of cells from which new tissue develops; especially in embryology or tissue regeneration.

Examples

blastemic tissue growth
Researchers observed blastemic changes during the early stages of limb regeneration.
Some cancers show a predominance of blastemic cells under the microscope.
The presence of blastemic tissue is a key feature in certain pediatric tumors.
