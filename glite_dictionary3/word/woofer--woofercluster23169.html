woofer

noun
loudspeaker for low-frequency sounds
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊfɚ / (AmE), / ˈwʊfə / (BrE)
a loudspeaker designed to reproduce low-frequency sounds, typically bass.

Examples

woofer speaker system
He replaced the old woofer in his speaker system to improve sound quality.
Many home theaters include a dedicated woofer for richer movie audio.
The subwoofer and woofer together created deep, powerful bass throughout the room.
