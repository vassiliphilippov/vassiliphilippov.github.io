worshiper

noun
person who admires excessively
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜːrʃɪpər / (AmE), / ˈwɜːʃɪpə / (BrE)
a person who admires someone or something so much that they fail to recognize any faults

Examples

worshiper of celebrities
Don't be a blind worshiper; everyone has flaws.
He is such a worshiper of his boss that he ignores all her mistakes.
The biography was written by a worshiper who overlooked critical aspects of the subject's life.

Related Terms

  • synonym – admirer: person who regards someone with respect or warm approval
  • antonym – critic: person who expresses an unfavorable opinion
