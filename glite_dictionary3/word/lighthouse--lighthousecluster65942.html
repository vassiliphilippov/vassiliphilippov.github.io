lighthouse

noun
tower with a warning light for ships
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈlaɪthaʊs / (BrE)
a tall structure built on or near the coast, equipped with a powerful light to warn or guide ships at sea
