significative

adjective
serving to indicate or signify
Pronunciation: / sɪɡˈnɪf.ɪ.kə.tɪv / (AmE), / sɪɡˈnɪf.ɪ.kə.tɪv / (BrE)
(formal) serving to indicate, signify, or point out something; showing clearly.

Examples

significative gesture
Archaeologists consider these artifacts significative of early human settlement.
Her silence was more significative than any words she could have spoken.
The gesture was highly significative of his true feelings.
