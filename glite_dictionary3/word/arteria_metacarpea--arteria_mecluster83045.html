arteria metacarpea

noun
artery along metacarpal bones in the hand
Pronunciation: / ɑrˌtɪriə mɛtəˈkɑrpiə / (AmE), / ɑːˌtɪəriə mɛtəˈkɑːpiə / (BrE)
Any of the arteries in the hand that run along the metacarpal bones, supplying blood to the dorsal or palmar regions.

Examples

A blockage in the arteria metacarpea can restrict blood flow to the hand.
