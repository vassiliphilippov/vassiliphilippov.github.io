caliber

noun
diameter of a tube or gun barrel
Pronunciation: / ˈkæləbɚ / (AmE), / ˈkælɪbə / (BrE)
The internal diameter of a tube, especially the barrel of a firearm, or of a bullet or shell.

Examples

caliber of a bullet
Because of its small caliber, the gun is lightweight and easy to handle.
Police recovered a handgun with a .45 caliber barrel at the scene.
The rifle was designed to use ammunition of a specific caliber.
