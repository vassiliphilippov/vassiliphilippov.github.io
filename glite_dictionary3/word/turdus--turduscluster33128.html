Turdus

noun
a genus of thrushes (birds)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝː.dəs / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.dəs / (BrE)
A genus of birds that includes the typical thrushes, such as the blackbird and the song thrush, in the family Turdidae.
