vibist

noun
person who creates a vibe
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪbɪst / (AmE), / ˈvaɪbɪst / (BrE)
informal (slang) a person who creates, curates, or projects a particular atmosphere, feeling, or mood (a 'vibe'), especially at events or online.

Examples

party vibist
The festival's headliner was accompanied by a known vibist who curated the crowd's energy all night.
She's a real vibist, always rearranging lights and music to create the perfect party mood.
On social media, the influencer built a following by being a relaxed vibist in every video.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mood-setter: a person who establishes or controls the mood or atmosphere in a place or event
