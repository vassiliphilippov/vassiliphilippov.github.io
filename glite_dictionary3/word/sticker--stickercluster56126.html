sticker

noun
digital image used in chat
Pronunciation: / c8st6ak59r / (AmE), / c8st6ak59 / (BrE)
a small digital image or graphic used in messaging apps or on social media to express emotion, reaction, or identity; often sent like a message.

Examples

send a digital sticker
She sent a funny digital sticker in the chat to express mood.

Teachers sometimes ban animated stickers in class group chats to reduce distractions.
