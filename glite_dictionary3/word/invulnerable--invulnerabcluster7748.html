invulnerable

adjective
impossible to harm or damage
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvʌlnɚəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈvʌlnərəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to harm or damage; not able to be wounded or overcome by attack.

Examples

invulnerable to attack
Despite several attempts, the fortress remained invulnerable to enemy attacks.
His self-confidence made him seem almost invulnerable to criticism.
The superhero was portrayed as completely invulnerable in the movie.

Related Terms

  • synonym – impregnable: unable to be captured or broken into
  • antonym – vulnerable: able to be easily physically or emotionally hurt
