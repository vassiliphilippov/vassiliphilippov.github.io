correspondent

noun
journalist reporting for media organizations
Pronunciation: / kəˌspɑn.dənt / (AmE), / kɒˌrɒs.pɒn.dənt / (BrE)
a journalist who is employed to report news for newspapers, magazines, radio, or television, often from a particular place or on a particular subject

Examples

foreign correspondent
A sports correspondent covered the Olympic Games live from Tokyo.
She became the chief political correspondent for the national newspaper last year.
The BBC's foreign correspondent filed a report from the war zone.

Related Terms

  • antonym – editor: a person who is in charge of and determines the final content of a publication
  • synonym – reporter: a person who reports news stories; journalist
