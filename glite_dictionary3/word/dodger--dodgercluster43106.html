dodger

noun
small southern corn bread cake
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑdʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈdɒdʒə / (BrE)
A small, oval-shaped cake of corn bread, usually baked or fried, especially in Southern U.S. cuisine.

Examples
