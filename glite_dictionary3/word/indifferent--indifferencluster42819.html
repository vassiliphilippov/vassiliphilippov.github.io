indifferent

adjective
not important; having no effect
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɪfrənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪfərənt / (BrE)
not important; having no particular significance or effect

Examples

indifferent result
He felt that the outcome was indifferent to his plans and didn't worry about it.
To her, the specific date was indifferent as long as the meeting happened.
Whether they win or lose is indifferent to most of the spectators.

Related Terms
