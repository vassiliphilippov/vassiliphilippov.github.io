bigeneric

adjective
cross between different genera
Pronunciation: / baɪ.dʒəˈnɛr.ɪk / (AmE), / baɪ.dʒɪˈnɛr.ɪk / (BrE)
(of a hybrid) resulting from a cross between species belonging to different genera.
