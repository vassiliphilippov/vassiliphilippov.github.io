trompe l'oeil

noun
visual art creating an illusion of three dimensions
Pronunciation: / tɹoʊmp lɔɪ / (AmE), / tɾɒ̃p lœj / (BrE)
a style of painting or visual art that creates the optical illusion that depicted objects exist in three dimensions

Examples

trompe l'oeil painting
She specializes in trompe l'oeil murals that turn blank walls into scenes of open landscapes.
The artist used trompe l'oeil techniques to make the painted window look astonishingly real.
Visitors admired the ceiling's elaborate trompe l'oeil, mistaking painted moldings for actual carvings.
