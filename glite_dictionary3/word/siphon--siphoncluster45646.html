siphon

noun
tube for transferring liquid via gravity
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪ.fən / (AmE), / ˈsaɪ.fən / (BrE)
a bent tube used to move liquid from one container to another at a lower level by means of atmospheric pressure and gravity

Examples

water siphon tube
A simple siphon can be used to move water from a barrel into a watering can.
He used a siphon to transfer the gasoline from one tank to another.
The laboratory technician carefully set up the siphon to drain the chemical solution.

  • synonym – tube: hollow cylinder used for conveying fluids
