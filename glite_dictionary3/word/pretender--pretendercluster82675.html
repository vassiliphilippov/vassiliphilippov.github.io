pretender

noun
person who feigns or makes false claims
Pronunciation: / prɪˈtɛndər / (AmE), / prɪˈtɛndə / (BrE)
a person who falsely claims or feigns to be something, especially to deceive others

Examples

fake pretender
He was nothing but a pretender who tricked everyone into believing he was a doctor.
Many saw the self-proclaimed leader as a pretender with no real authority.
The company exposed the pretender and warned customers about his scams.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fraud: a person who deceives others by pretending to be something they are not
  • synonym – impostor: a person who pretends to be someone else, often for deceitful purposes
