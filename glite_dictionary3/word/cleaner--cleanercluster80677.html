cleaner

noun
software that removes junk files
Pronunciation: / ˈkliːnər / (AmE), / ˈkliːnə / (BrE)
a software program or app that removes unnecessary files, caches, duplicates, or other digital traces to free storage and often improve system performance.

Examples

computer cleaner
I ran the cleaner app to clear cache and reclaim space.
The IT department recommended a system cleaner to remove old logs and free disk space.
Before installing the update she ran several cleaners to delete duplicate files.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cache cleaner: a tool that removes cached files to free space or resolve stale data issues
  • synonym – disk cleaner: a program that deletes temporary and unnecessary files to recover disk space
