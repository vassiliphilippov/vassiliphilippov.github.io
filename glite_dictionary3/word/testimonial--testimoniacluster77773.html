testimonial

noun
a tribute or statement of esteem
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛstəˈmoʊniəl / (AmE), / ˌtɛstəˈməʊniəl /; / ˌtɛstɪˈməʊniəl / (BrE)
a statement or act that shows appreciation, praise, recommendation, or provides evidence of someone's character, ability, or the quality of something
