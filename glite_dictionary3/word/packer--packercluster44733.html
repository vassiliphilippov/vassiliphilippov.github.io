packer

noun
meat industry wholesaler or company
Pronunciation: / ˈpækər / (AmE), / ˈpakə / (BrE)
a person or company that prepares and packages meat for sale, especially as a wholesaler in the meat industry.

Examples

meat packer
Labor negotiations at the local packer have caused meat shortages in the region.
Many ranchers sell their cattle directly to a packer for processing and distribution.
The large packer supplies beef to supermarkets nationwide.
