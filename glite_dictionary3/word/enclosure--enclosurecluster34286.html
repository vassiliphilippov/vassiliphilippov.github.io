enclosure

noun
the act of enclosing something
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkloʊʒər / (AmE), / ɪnˈkləʊʒə / (BrE)
the act of surrounding or closing off something within something else

Examples

the enclosure process

Strict enclosure of confidential documents is required by company policy.
The enclosure of the animals in the pen kept them safe from predators.
