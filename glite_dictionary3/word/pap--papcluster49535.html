pap

noun
small projection of a mammary gland
Pronunciation: / pæp / (AmE), / pæp / (BrE)
a small projection of a mammary gland, such as a nipple or teat, especially in animals or archaic use in humans

Examples

animal pap
In some mammals, the pap is barely visible except when nursing.
Old texts often refer to the human nipple as the pap.
The sow fed her piglets from each pap in turn.

Related Terms

  • synonym – nipple: the small projection on the breast through which milk is drawn
  • synonym – teat: the projection on a mammary gland from which the milk is sucked
