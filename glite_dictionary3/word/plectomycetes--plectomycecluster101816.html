Plectomycetes

noun
class of fungi with closed fruiting bodies
Pronunciation: / plɛk.toʊ.maɪˈsiːtiːz /; / plɛk-toʊ-maɪˈsiːtiːz / (AmE), / plɛk.təʊ.maɪˈsiːtiːz /; / plɛk-təʊ-maɪˈsiːtiːz / (BrE)
