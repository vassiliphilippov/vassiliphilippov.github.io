archesporium

noun
primitive reproductive cell in plants or fungi
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrkəˈspɔːriəm / (AmE), / ˌɑːtʃɪˈspɔːrɪəm / (BrE)
a primitive cell or group of cells in plants or fungi from which a mother cell develops, eventually leading to spore formation
