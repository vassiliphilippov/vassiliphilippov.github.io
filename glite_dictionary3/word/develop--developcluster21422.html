to develop

verb
produce photographic images using chemicals
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɛləp / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləp / (BrE)
to make (an image) visible on photographic film or paper by treating it with chemical solutions

Examples

to develop a photo
It takes a few minutes to develop the images from the negatives.
The photographer asked the lab to develop her latest roll of film.
You can still develop black-and-white photos in a darkroom at home.
