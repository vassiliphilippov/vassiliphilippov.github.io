behaviour

noun
conduct or response of a machine or substance
Pronunciation: / bɪˈheɪvjər / (AmE), / bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
the way in which a machine, substance, or system acts or responds under particular conditions

Examples

behaviour of water
Researchers are studying the behaviour of certain chemicals at high temperatures.
