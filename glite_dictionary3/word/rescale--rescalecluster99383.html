to rescale

verb
adjust to a new scale
Pronunciation: / riˈskeɪl / (AmE), / riˈskeɪl / (BrE)
to adjust or establish (something) according to a new scale or proportion

Examples

to rescale a chart
After changing the units, the software automatically rescales the measurements.
We decided to rescale the graph to make the data easier to interpret.
You may need to rescale the image so it fits on the page properly.
