cardholder

noun
person possessing a credit or debit card
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrdˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person who has and is authorized to use a credit card or debit card

Examples

cardholder credit card
Each cardholder must sign the back of their credit card as soon as they receive it.
Every cardholder is advised to keep their PIN confidential.
The bank will contact the cardholder if any suspicious activity is detected.
