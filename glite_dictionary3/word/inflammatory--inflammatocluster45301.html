inflammatory

adjective
caused by or relating to inflammation
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈflæməˌtɔri / (AmE), / ɪnˈflaməˌtəri / (BrE)
characterized by or causing inflammation, especially in a part of the body

Examples

inflammatory disease
Doctors prescribed medication to reduce the inflammatory process in her joints.
Some skin conditions are considered inflammatory diseases and require special treatment.
The patient showed signs of an inflammatory response after the surgery.

  • antonym – noninflammatory: not involving or caused by inflammation
