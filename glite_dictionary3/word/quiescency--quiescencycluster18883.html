quiescency

noun
state of quiet inaction
Pronunciation: / kwiˈɛsənsi /; / kwaɪˈɛsənsi / (AmE), / kwɪˈɛsənsi / (BrE)
a state of quietness or inactivity; a period of rest or dormancy
