Katsuwonus pelamis

noun
species of tuna used in Japanese cooking
Pronunciation: / kætsuːˈwoʊnəs pəˈlamɪs / (AmE), / katsjuːˈwəʊnəs pɪˈleɪmɪs / (BrE)
a species of tuna, also known as skipjack tuna, whose flesh is often dried and flaked for use in Japanese cookery
