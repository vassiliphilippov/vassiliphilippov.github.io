Marquise de Montespan

proper noun
French noblewoman, mistress of Louis XIV
Pronunciation: / mɑrˈkiz də mɑnˈtɛspæn / (AmE), / mɑːˈkiːz də mɒnˈtɛspæn / (BrE)
(1641–1707) A French noblewoman who was the official mistress of King Louis XIV of France until his attentions shifted to Madame de Maintenon.
