curtain

noun
a figurative barrier to communication or vision
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈkɜːtn̩ / (BrE)
something that acts as a barrier to communication or vision, especially in a figurative or metaphorical sense

Examples

curtain of smoke
A dense curtain of fog made driving difficult that morning.
Negotiations were hampered by a curtain of silence from the other side.
The country maintained a curtain of secrecy around its military operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym – barrier: something that blocks movement, access, or communication
  • synonym – screen: something that shelters, protects, or hides; a similar figurative barrier
  • synonym – veil: something that conceals, disguises, or obscures
