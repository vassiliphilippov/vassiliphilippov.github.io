noctiluca

noun
bioluminescent marine protozoan
Pronunciation: / nɑk.tɪˈluː.kə / (AmE), / nɒk.tɪˈluː.kə / (BrE)
a large single-celled marine protozoan, especially Noctiluca scintillans, that produces bioluminescence and is commonly found in the surface waters of coastal seas.

Examples

noctiluca glowing water
A bloom of noctiluca turned the sea a glowing blue along the coast.
On warm summer nights, the waves sparkled owing to countless noctiluca in the water.
Scientists collected samples of noctiluca to study their glowing properties.
