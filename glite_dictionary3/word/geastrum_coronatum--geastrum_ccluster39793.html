Geastrum coronatum

noun
a species of earthstar fungus
Pronunciation: / dʒiˈæstrəm ˌkɔrəˈneɪtəm / (AmE), / dʒiˈæstrəm ˌkɒrəˈneɪtəm / (BrE)
A species of earthstar fungus with a bluish spore sac and purplish brown gleba; its outer layer splits into rays that bend backward to raise the spore sac at maturity.
