to ticket

verb
issue a fine or penalty notice
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪk.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɪk.ɪt / (BrE)
(of a police officer or authority) to issue an official notice of a fine or penalty for a legal or parking violation.

Examples

to ticket a car
Parking officers regularly ticket vehicles that block fire hydrants.
She was ticketed for speeding on the highway last month.
The police will ticket any car left in the no-parking zone overnight.
