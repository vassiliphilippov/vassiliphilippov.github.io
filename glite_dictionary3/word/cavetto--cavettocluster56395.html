cavetto

noun
concave quarter-circle molding
Pronunciation: / kəˈvɛtoʊ / (AmE), / kəˈvɛtəʊ / (BrE)
A concave molding with a cross section shaped like a quarter circle, commonly used in architecture and decorative design.
