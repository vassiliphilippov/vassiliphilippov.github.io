lipemia

noun
excess lipids in the blood
Pronunciation: / laɪˈpiːmiə / (AmE), / lɪˈpiːmɪə / (BrE)
the presence of an abnormally high concentration of lipids in the blood

Examples

lipemia in blood test
Severe lipemia can interfere with certain blood tests, making diagnosis more challenging.
The laboratory results revealed significant lipemia, indicating a possible metabolic disorder.
Treatment is required when lipemia leads to complications such as pancreatitis.
