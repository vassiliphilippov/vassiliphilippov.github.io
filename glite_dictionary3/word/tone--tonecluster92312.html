tone

noun
steady sound with no overtones
Pronunciation: / toʊn / (AmE), / təʊn / (BrE)
a steady musical sound of a single frequency without overtones

Examples

pure tone test
Each tone used in the test was carefully isolated to avoid any overtones.
She heard a clear tone when the tuning fork was struck.
The audiologist played a tone at varying frequencies to check for any hearing loss.

Related Terms

  • antonym – noise: a sound, especially one that is loud or unpleasant or that causes disturbance
  • synonym – note: a musical sound of a particular pitch and duration
