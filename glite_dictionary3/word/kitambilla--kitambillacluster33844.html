kitambilla

noun
Indian maroon-purple gooseberry-like fruit
Pronunciation: / kɪˈtæmbɪlə / (AmE), / kɪˈtæmbɪlə / (BrE)
A maroon-purple fruit native to India, similar to a gooseberry, with tart-sweet purple pulp often used in preserves.

Examples

ripe kitambilla fruit
Dried kitambillas are a popular ingredient in some regional Indian desserts.
She tasted the tart-sweet pulp of the kitambilla and decided to use it in her preserves.
The market had a fresh batch of kitambilla for making traditional Indian jams.
