squirmer

noun
person who can't stay still
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɝːmɚ / (AmE), / ˈskwɜːmə / (BrE)
a person, especially a child, who cannot stay still and is always fidgeting or wriggling

Examples

restless squirmer
On long car rides, my little brother is always a squirmer and can’t settle down.
She tried to calm the squirmer on her lap, but he just kept wiggling.
The teacher asked the squirmer in the back row to try to sit still during the story.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fidget: a person who is always moving nervously or restlessly
