to juggle

verb
manage multiple tasks at once
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌɡəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌɡl / (BrE)
to try to manage or deal with several activities, tasks, or responsibilities at the same time

Examples

to juggle work tasks
As a student, I learned to juggle classes, assignments, and part-time jobs.
He often has to juggle several projects at the same time at work.
She found it challenging to juggle her career, family, and social life.
