vamp

noun
seductive woman who exploits men
Pronunciation: / væmp / (AmE), / væmp / (BrE)
a sexually attractive woman who uses her charm to seduce and exploit men, often for personal gain

Examples

a vamp in movies
In the classic film, she played a vamp who manipulated every man she met.
Rumors spread quickly that the new actress was a real-life vamp, always at the center of scandal.
Writers often portrayed strong, independent women as vamps in early Hollywood movies.
