hajji

noun
Muslim who made pilgrimage to Mecca
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːdʒi / (AmE), / ˈhædʒi / (BrE)
A Muslim who has completed the pilgrimage to Mecca; used as a term of respect.

Examples

hajji after pilgrimage
He was honored as a hajji after returning from Mecca.
Many people in the community addressed her as hajji in recognition of her pilgrimage.
The title hajji is highly respected in Islamic cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pilgrim: a person who makes a journey to a holy place for religious reasons
