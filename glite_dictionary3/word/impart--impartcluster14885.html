to impart

verb
give a quality to something
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɑrt / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɑːt / (BrE)
to give a particular quality, feeling, or characteristic to something or someone

Examples

impart a special quality
A sense of elegance is imparted by the antique furniture in the living room.
His thoughtful remarks imparted a deeper meaning to the discussion.
The spices impart a unique flavor to the dish.
