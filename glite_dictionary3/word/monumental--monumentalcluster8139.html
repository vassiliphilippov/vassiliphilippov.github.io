monumental

adjective
of great significance or importance
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːn.jəˈmen.t̬l̩ /; / ˌmɑː.njuˈmen.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˌmɒn.jʊˈmen.təl /; / ˌmɒn.juˈmen.tl̩ / (BrE)
extremely important, impressive, or massive in size, scope, or achievement

Examples

monumental achievement
Her novel is considered a monumental work in modern literature.

We stood in awe before the monumental cathedral that towered over the city.
