decoding

noun
converting code to plain text
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkoʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈkəʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of converting coded or encrypted information into ordinary language or readable form

Examples

decoding a secret message
Accurate decoding of the signal is crucial for clear communication.
She is skilled in the decoding of ancient scripts.
The decoding of the message required the use of a special cipher key.
