Moloch

noun
force demanding sacrifice or obedience
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊ.lɑk /; / ˈmoʊ.lɑːk / (AmE), / ˈmɒl.ək /; / ˈmɒl.ɒk / (BrE)
a powerful force, system, or thing that demands extreme sacrifice or obedience, often at great human cost (originally from a mythological deity)
