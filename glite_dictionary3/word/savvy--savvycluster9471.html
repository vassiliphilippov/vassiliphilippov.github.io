savvy

noun
practical understanding; shrewdness
Pronunciation: / ˈsævi / (AmE), / ˈsævi / (BrE)
Practical understanding or shrewdness; the ability to make good judgments.

Examples

business savvy
Despite his technical skills, he lacks the savvy to manage a team effectively.
Her business savvy impressed the investors during the pitch.
You need a lot of political savvy to navigate city hall.

  • antonym – ignorance: lack of knowledge or awareness
