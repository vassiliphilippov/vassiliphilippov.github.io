drop-off

noun
designated unloading place
Pronunciation: / drɑp ˈɔf /; / \u02c8dr\u0251p \u02cc\u0254f / (AmE), / drɒp ˈɒf /; / \u02c8dr\u0252p \u0252f / (BrE)
a place or area where people or things are left, delivered, or picked up

Related Terms

  • synonym – drop-off zone: a marked area (often at airports, schools, or stores) for leaving passengers or deliveries.
