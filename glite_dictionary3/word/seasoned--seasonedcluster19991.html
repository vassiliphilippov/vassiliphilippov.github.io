seasoned

adjective
experienced and competent
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːzənd / (AmE), / ˈsiːzənd / (BrE)
having a lot of experience of doing something and therefore knowing how to do it well; rendered competent through trial and experience

Examples

seasoned professional
After many years in the army, he became a seasoned officer.
She is a seasoned journalist who has covered stories around the world.
The company hired a seasoned manager to turn things around.
