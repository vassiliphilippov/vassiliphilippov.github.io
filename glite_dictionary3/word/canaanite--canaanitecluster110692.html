Canaanite

noun
extinct Semitic language of ancient Canaan
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪnəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.nə.naɪt / (BrE)
an extinct Semitic language formerly spoken by the people who occupied Canaan prior to the Israelite conquest
