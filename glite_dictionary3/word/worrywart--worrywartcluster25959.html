worrywart

noun
person who worries excessively
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːiˌwɑrt / (AmE), / ˈwʌriˌwɑːt / (BrE)
a person who constantly worries, especially about things that may never happen

Examples

Although he's usually calm, he turns into a worrywart when exam results are about to be released.
Don’t be such a worrywart; everything is going to work out fine.
My grandmother is a total worrywart who always assumes the worst will happen.
