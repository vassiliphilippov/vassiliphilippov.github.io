cross-cultural

adjective
involving more than one culture
Pronunciation: / krɒsˈkʌltʃərəl / (AmE), / krɒsˈkʌltʃərəl / (BrE)
involving or comparing two or more different cultures

Examples

cross-cultural communication
Her cross-cultural experiences made her sensitive to cultural differences.
The study offered a cross-cultural comparison of parenting styles in Asia and Europe.
We attended a cross-cultural workshop to learn about business etiquette overseas.
