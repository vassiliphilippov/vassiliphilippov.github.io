key

noun
rectangular area on a basketball court
Pronunciation: / kiː / (AmE), / kiː / (BrE)
In basketball, the rectangular area, including the foul line, in front of each basket, usually marked in a contrasting color from the rest of the court.

Examples

basketball key
Defenders crowded the key to block any easy layups.
She took a free throw from the top of the key after being fouled.
The player dribbled past his defender and drove straight into the key.

Related Terms

  • synonym – paint: the area near the basket bounded by the key, often painted a different color
