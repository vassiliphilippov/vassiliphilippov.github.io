venthole

noun
hole for escape of gas or air
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛntˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈvɛnthəʊl / (BrE)
A hole allowing gas or air to escape from an enclosed space.

Examples

venthole in a furnace
Each furnace was equipped with a venthole to allow excess gases to escape.
Steam escaped rapidly through the venthole on top of the chamber.
The technician drilled a venthole in the tank to prevent pressure from building up.

  • synonym – vent: an opening that allows air, gas, or liquid to pass out of or into a confined space
