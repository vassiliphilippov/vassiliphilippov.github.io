mA

noun
unit: milliampere (1/1000 ampere)
Pronunciation: / ˌɛm ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛm ˈeɪ / (BrE)
A unit of electric current equal to one thousandth of an ampere (milliampere).

Examples

mA reading
My multimeter can measure up to 2000 mA in the current mode.
The LED only requires 20 mA to operate efficiently.
The circuit draws about 50 mA of current when active.
