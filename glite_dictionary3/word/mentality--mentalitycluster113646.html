mentality

noun
way of thinking; mental attitude
Pronunciation: / mɛnˈtæləti / (AmE), / menˈtalɪti / (BrE)
A particular way of thinking; a habitual or characteristic mental attitude that determines how a person interprets and responds to situations.

Examples

winning mentality
He approached every challenge with a scientific mentality, analyzing problems before acting.
Her competitive mentality helped her to succeed in a demanding career.
To change the team's fortunes, we need to shift their mentality from defeatist to optimistic.

Related Terms

  • synonym – attitude: a settled way of thinking or feeling about something
  • synonym – mindset: a person's way of thinking and their opinions
