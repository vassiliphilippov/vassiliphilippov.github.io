blending

noun
subtle gradation or transition
Pronunciation: / ˈblɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
a gradual transition or gradation between two or more things, with small or imperceptible differences between adjacent stages

Examples

smooth blending of colors
In design, smooth blending ensures there are no harsh lines between different shades.
The blending from light to dark was so gradual it was almost impossible to see where one ended and the other began.
The artist achieved a realistic effect through the careful blending of colors on the canvas.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gradation: a scale or series of successive changes, stages, or degrees
