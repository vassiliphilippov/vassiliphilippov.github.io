contributory

adjective
partly responsible; helping cause
Pronunciation: / kənˈtrɪb.jəˌtɔr.i / (AmE), / kənˈtrɪb.jʊ.tər.i / (BrE)
Helping to cause or bring about something; being partly responsible for an outcome or result.

Examples

contributory factor
Poor nutrition was a contributory factor in the development of the disease.
The report highlights several contributory causes of the accident.
The witnesses agreed that her oversight was contributory to the overall failure.

Related Terms

  • synonym – causal: acting as a cause; relating to cause and effect
  • antonym – noncontributory: not helping to cause an effect; not partly responsible
