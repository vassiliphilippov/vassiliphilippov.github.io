fatigue

noun
boredom from overexposure
Pronunciation: / fəˈtiɡ / (AmE), / fəˈtiːɡ / (BrE)
boredom or apathy caused by overexposure to something, especially when used with a modifier.

Examples

fatigue from ads
After months of campaigns, voters showed clear signs of election fatigue.
Many viewers are experiencing news fatigue and are tuning out of current events.
She complained about 'diet fatigue' after trying so many different meal plans.

Related Terms

  • synonym – apathy: a lack of interest or enthusiasm
  • synonym – boredom: the state of being bored
