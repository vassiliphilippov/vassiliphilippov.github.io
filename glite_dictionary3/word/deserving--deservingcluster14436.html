deserving

adjective
worthy of a particular treatment
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzɝvɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːvɪŋ / (BrE)
worthy of being given help, praise, punishment, or another treatment; having qualities that merit a particular response, often used to describe those seen as entitled to aid or recognition.

Examples

deserving poor
Many charities focus on helping the deserving poor in the community.
