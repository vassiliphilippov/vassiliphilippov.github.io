gristle

noun
tough cartilage tissue in meat
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪsəl / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪsəl / (BrE)
Tough, elastic cartilage found in meat, which is chewed with difficulty and is mostly converted to bone in adults.

Examples

gristle in meat
I had to spit out the gristle because it was too tough to chew.
She trimmed away the gristle before cooking the chicken.
The steak was delicious, except for a piece of gristle near the center.

  • synonym – cartilage: firm, elastic tissue; main constituent of gristle
