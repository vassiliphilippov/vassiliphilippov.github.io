to consent

verb
agree or give permission
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɛnt / (AmE), / kənˈsɛnt / (BrE)
to give permission or agree to do something

Examples

to consent to a request
After much discussion, she finally consented to the proposal.
He refused at first, but later consented to help with the arrangements.
The chairman will not act unless all members consent.

Related Terms

  • synonym – agree: have the same opinion or give approval
  • antonym – refuse: to indicate unwillingness or reject
