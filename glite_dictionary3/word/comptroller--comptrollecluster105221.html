comptroller

noun
official who audits and manages accounts
Pronunciation: / kəmˈtroʊlɚ / (AmE), / kəmˈtrəʊlə / (BrE)
an official who maintains and audits the financial accounts of an organization, especially in government or large companies

Examples

city comptroller
As city comptroller, his role is to ensure accurate public financial reporting.
The annual audit was supervised by the new comptroller in the finance department.
The company hired a comptroller to oversee all financial operations.

  • synonym – controller: an official managing financial records and audits
