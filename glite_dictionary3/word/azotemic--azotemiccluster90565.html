azotemic

adjective
having excess nitrogenous waste in the blood
Pronunciation: / ˌæz.oʊˈtiː.mɪk / (AmE), / ˌaz.əʊˈtiː.mɪk / (BrE)
relating to or having an excess of nitrogenous waste products in the blood, usually as a result of kidney dysfunction

Examples
