to basify

verb
make or become more basic (chemistry)
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsɪˌfaɪ / (BrE)
to make or become more basic, less acidic; to convert into a base.

Examples

to basify a solution
Adding sodium hydroxide will basify the solution, raising its pH.
Some treatments basify acidic soil to make it suitable for planting.
The chemist needed to basify the mixture before continuing the experiment.

Related Terms

  • antonym – acidify: to make or become more acidic
  • synonym – alkalize: to make more alkaline or basic
