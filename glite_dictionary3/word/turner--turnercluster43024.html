turner

noun
one who swings ropes in jump rope
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːnər / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnə / (BrE)
a person who swings the ends of the ropes in the game of jump rope while others jump

Examples

turner in jump rope
During recess, Ella was the turner while her friends took turns jumping rope.
He started as a turner, but soon joined the jumpers when he got the rhythm.
The game requires two skilled turners to keep the ropes moving smoothly.
