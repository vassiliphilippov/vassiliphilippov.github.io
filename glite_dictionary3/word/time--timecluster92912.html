time

noun
a period available for something
Pronunciation: / taɪm / (AmE), / taɪm / (BrE)
The period during which something is available or can be used or managed as a resource to do something.

Examples

enough time to finish
I didn't have enough time to finish my assignment before the deadline.
Please make time for a quick meeting this afternoon.
She likes to take time out of her busy schedule to relax.
