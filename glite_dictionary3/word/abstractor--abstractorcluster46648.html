abstractor

noun
automated summarizer (software/AI)
Pronunciation: / əbˈstræk.tɚ / (AmE), / əbˈstræk.tə / (BrE)
a software tool or artificial intelligence system that automatically creates concise summaries of documents, articles, or datasets.

Examples

AI abstractor tool
The company uses an abstractor to summarize thousands of medical articles.
We trained an abstractor to condense long reports into one-paragraph summaries for clinicians.
Several hospitals adopted abstractors to speed literature review across multiple departments.

Related Terms

  • synonym – summarize: to give a brief statement of the main points of something
  • synonym – summarizer: a person or system that produces a brief statement of the main points of a text
