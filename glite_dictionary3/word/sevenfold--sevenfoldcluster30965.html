sevenfold

adverb
seven times as much or many
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛvənˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈsɛv.ən.fəʊld / (BrE)
By seven times; to a degree or amount seven times as great.

Examples

rose sevenfold
Donations to the charity have risen sevenfold since last year.
In the past decade, the city’s population grew sevenfold.
The company’s profits increased sevenfold after the new product launched.
