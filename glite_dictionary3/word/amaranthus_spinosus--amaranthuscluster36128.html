Amaranthus spinosus

noun
spiny annual plant of Asia and Africa
Pronunciation: / ˌæməˈrænθəs spaɪˈnoʊsəs / (AmE), / ˌaməˈranθəs spaɪˈnəʊsəs / (BrE)
an erect annual plant native to tropical central Asia and Africa, characterized by a pair of divergent spines at most leaf nodes
