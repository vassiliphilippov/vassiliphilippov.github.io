draggingly

adverb
in a slow or tedious manner
Pronunciation: / ˈdræɡɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈdræɡɪŋli / (BrE)
In a slow, laborious, or tedious manner; as if dragging.

Examples

to speak draggingly
He answered the questions draggingly, as though he were too tired to care.
She walked draggingly across the field, her feet barely leaving the ground.
The meeting proceeded draggingly, with every topic taking twice as long as necessary.
