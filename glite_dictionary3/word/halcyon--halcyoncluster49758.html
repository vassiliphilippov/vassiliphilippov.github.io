Halcyon

proper noun
mythical woman changed into a kingfisher
Pronunciation: / ˈhæl.si.ən / (AmE), / ˈhals.i.ən / (BrE)
(In Greek mythology) a woman, originally named Alcyone, who was transformed into a kingfisher bird.

Examples

Halcyon in Greek myth
According to Greek legend, Halcyon calmed the seas during her nesting period.
In the ancient story, Halcyon was changed into a bird after losing her husband at sea.
Many poets refer to Halcyon in describing periods of peace and calm.
