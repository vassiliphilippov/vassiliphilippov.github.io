tear

noun
an act of tearing
Pronunciation: / tɛr / (AmE), / tɪə / (BrE)
an act of ripping or splitting something, or the result (a hole or split made by tearing)

Examples

a tear in fabric
He gave the envelope a quick tear to open it.
The dress suffered a tear when it caught on a nail.
There was a large tear down the side of the paper.
Can you fix the tear in my jeans before the party tonight?
She noticed a small tear in the sleeve of her jacket after brushing against the fence.
The curtains had a tear near the bottom where the cat scratched them.

Related Terms

  • antonym – mend: to repair a tear or hole in fabric
  • synonym – rip: an act of ripping; a tear or split made by pulling something apart
