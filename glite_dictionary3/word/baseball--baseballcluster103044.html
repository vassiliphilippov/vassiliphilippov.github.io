baseball

noun
a team game played with bat and ball
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsˌbɔːl / (BrE)
A game played between two teams of nine players who take turns batting and fielding, using a bat and ball, with the goal of scoring runs.

Examples

play baseball game
He has played baseball since he was a child.
Many families watch baseball together on summer evenings.
The baseball team won the championship last season.
