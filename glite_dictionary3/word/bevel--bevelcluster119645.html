bevel

noun
angled edge or surface
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈbɛvəl / (BrE)
An edge or surface that is sloped at an angle other than 90 degrees, especially where two surfaces meet.

Examples

bevel edge
Adjust the saw to achieve the correct bevel for this joint.
He cut a bevel on the wood so the pieces would fit together smoothly.
The glass top has a decorative bevel along the edge.
