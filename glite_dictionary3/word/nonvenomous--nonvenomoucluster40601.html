nonvenomous

adjective
not producing or containing venom
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈvɛnəməs / (AmE), / nɒnˈvɛnəməs / (BrE)
Not producing or containing venom; not capable of injecting venom.

Examples

nonvenomous snake species
Garter snakes are generally considered nonvenomous and pose no threat to humans.
It is important to distinguish nonvenomous from venomous species when encountering snakes in the wild.
Unlike cobras, pythons are nonvenomous and kill their prey by constriction.
