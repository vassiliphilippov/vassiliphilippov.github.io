to pacify

verb
bring peace by ending violence
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈpæsɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to bring peace to a place by ending conflict or violence, often through intervention or force

Examples

to pacify a troubled area
Efforts to pacify the city included peace talks and humanitarian aid.
International organizations often step in to pacify war-torn areas and help rebuild communities.
The government sent troops to pacify the troubled region after months of unrest.

Related Terms

  • antonym – agitate: to stir up or provoke (opposite of pacify)
  • synonym – calm: to make calm or tranquil
