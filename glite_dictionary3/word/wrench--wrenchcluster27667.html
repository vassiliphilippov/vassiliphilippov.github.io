wrench

noun
tool for turning nuts or bolts
Pronunciation: / rɛntʃ / (AmE), / rɛntʃ / (BrE)
a hand tool with a shaped opening or jaws used for gripping, holding, or turning nuts, bolts, and similar objects

Examples

use a wrench on bolts
He grabbed a wrench to tighten the loose bolt on the pipe.
The mechanic reached for a wrench from his toolbox.
You'll need a wrench if you want to assemble that furniture.

Related Terms

  • synonym – spanner: British term for wrench; hand tool for turning bolts and nuts
