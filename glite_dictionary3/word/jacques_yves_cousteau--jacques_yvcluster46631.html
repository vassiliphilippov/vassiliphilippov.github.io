Jacques Yves Cousteau

proper noun
French underwater explorer and filmmaker
Pronunciation: / ʒæk iv kuˈstoʊ / (AmE), / ʒak iv kusˈtoʊ / (BrE)
(1910–1997) A French underwater explorer, filmmaker, and researcher known for pioneering marine conservation and inventing the Aqua-Lung.

Examples

Jacques Yves Cousteau explored oceans
Jacques Yves Cousteau revolutionized scuba diving with his invention of the Aqua-Lung.
