baby

noun
youngest member of a group
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.bi / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi / (BrE)
the youngest or most junior member of a group, especially a family, regardless of actual age

Examples

the baby of the family
As the baby of the team, she often gets the easiest tasks.
Even though he's thirty, he's still the baby of the family.
Everyone spoiled her because she was the baby of the group.
