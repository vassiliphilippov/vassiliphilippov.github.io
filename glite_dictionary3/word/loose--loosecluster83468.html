loose

adjective
not tight or closely fitted
Pronunciation: / lus /; / luːs / (AmE), / luːs / (BrE)
not tight, secure, or firmly fixed; not fitting closely

Examples

loose clothing
Make sure your dog's collar isn't too loose or it might slip off.
She prefers to wear loose dresses during hot weather for extra comfort.
The screw was so loose that the shelf nearly fell down.
His shirt hung loose around his thin frame.
Make sure the rope is not loose before you start climbing.
She tied her hair back, but a few strands were still loose.
Her shoelaces were loose, so she stopped to tie them.
Make sure the bolts aren't loose before driving the car.
The sticker became loose and fell off the notebook.
