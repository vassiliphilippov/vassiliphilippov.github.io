to scrunch

verb
crush or wrinkle by squeezing
Pronunciation: / skrʌntʃ / (AmE), / skrʌntʃ / (BrE)
to crush or squeeze something so that it becomes wrinkled, folded, or creased

Examples

to scrunch paper
After sleeping on it, my shirt was badly scrunched and needed ironing.
She scrunched the letter into a ball and tossed it away.
The child loved to scrunch up pieces of paper during art class.

Related Terms

  • synonym – crumple: to crush something so that it becomes creased or wrinkled
  • antonym – smooth: to make something even or without wrinkles
