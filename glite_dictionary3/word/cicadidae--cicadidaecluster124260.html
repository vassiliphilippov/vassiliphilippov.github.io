Cicadidae

noun
the family of cicadas (insects)
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkædəˌdiː / (AmE), / sɪˈkædəˌdiː / (BrE)
The family of large insects known as cicadas, characterized by transparent wings and loud, distinctive mating calls, found in warm climates worldwide.
