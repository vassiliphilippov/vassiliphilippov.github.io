Roccellaceae

noun
family of lichen-forming fungi
Pronunciation: / roʊˌsɛləˈsiː.iː /; / roʊˌsɛləˈsiːiː / (AmE), / rɒˌsɛlɪˈsiː.iː /; / rɒˌsɛləˈsiːiː / (BrE)
a family of fungi in the division Lichenes, most of which form lichens with green algae; some species grow on other lichens
