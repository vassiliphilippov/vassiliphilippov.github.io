to familiarize

verb
make familiar; become acquainted with
Pronunciation: / fəˈmɪljəˌraɪz / (AmE), / fəˈmɪliəraɪz / (BrE)
to make (someone or oneself) aware of or knowledgeable about something; to cause to become familiar with.

Examples

to familiarize yourself with rules
She took time to familiarize herself with the scientific terms before the meeting.
Teachers often familiarize students with exam formats in advance.
The company offers orientation sessions to help new employees familiarize themselves with workplace procedures.

Related Terms

  • synonym – acquaint: to make someone aware of or familiar with
  • antonym – alienate: to make (someone) unfriendly or unfamiliar with something
