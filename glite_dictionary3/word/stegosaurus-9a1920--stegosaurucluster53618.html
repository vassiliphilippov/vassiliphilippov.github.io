stegosaurus

noun
herbivorous dinosaur with back plates and spiked tail
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛgəˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / ˌstɛgəˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
a large herbivorous dinosaur of the Jurassic period, characterized by a row of upright bony plates along its back and a spiked tail

A life-sized model of a stegosaurus is the centerpiece of the museum's Jurassic exhibit.
Paleontologists believe the tail spikes of a stegosaurus were used for defense against predators.
The stegosaurus had distinctive plates running along its back, making it one of the most recognizable dinosaurs.
