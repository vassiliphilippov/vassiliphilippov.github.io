popper

noun
container for inhalant stimulant drug
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑpər / (AmE), / ˈpɒpə / (BrE)
a small container of amyl nitrate or butyl nitrite, used as a recreational stimulant and inhaled for its euphoric effects

Examples

to inhale popper
He bought a popper from the pharmacy before the party.
Some club-goers use popper to enhance their experience on the dance floor.
The effects of inhaling a popper are usually short-lived but intense.
