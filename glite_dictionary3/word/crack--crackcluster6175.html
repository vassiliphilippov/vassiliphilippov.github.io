to crack

verb
to break down mentally under pressure
Pronunciation: / kræk / (AmE), / kræk / (BrE)
(of a person) to break down emotionally or mentally under pressure; to suffer a nervous breakdown.

Examples

to crack under pressure
After months of relentless stress, he finally cracked during the meeting.
She tried to handle the workload, but eventually she cracked.
Some people crack under pressure while others remain calm.
