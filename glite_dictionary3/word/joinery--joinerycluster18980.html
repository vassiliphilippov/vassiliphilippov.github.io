joinery

noun
fine woodworking by a joiner
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪnəri / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪnəri / (BrE)
the craft or skill of making wooden structures or fittings, such as doors, stairs, and window frames, by joining pieces of wood together
