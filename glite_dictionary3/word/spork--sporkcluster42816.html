spork

noun
eating utensil combining spoon and fork
Pronunciation: / spɔrk / (AmE), / spɔːk / (BrE)
A eating utensil with a shallow bowl like a spoon, but with tines at the tip, combining features of a spoon and a fork.

Examples

plastic spork
He laughed when he first saw the spork, but soon found it surprisingly useful.
She brought a spork for her lunch because it worked as both a spoon and a fork.
The camping kit included a lightweight plastic spork for eating all kinds of food.
