de Sade

proper noun
French writer associated with sadism
Pronunciation: / də ˈsɑd / (AmE), / də ˈsɑːd / (BrE)
The Marquis de Sade (1740–1814), French soldier and writer noted for works describing sexual cruelty, from whose name the term 'sadism' is derived.
