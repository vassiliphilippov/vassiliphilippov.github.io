directivity

noun
quality of being directional or focused in a direction
Pronunciation: / dɪˌrɛkˈtɪvəti /; / ˌdɪrɛkˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / dɪˌrɛkˈtɪvɪti /; / ˌdɪrɛkˈtɪvəti / (BrE)
the property of a device (like an antenna or microphone) to send or receive energy or signals more effectively in certain directions
