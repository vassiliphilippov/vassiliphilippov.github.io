pineapple

noun
a tropical fruit with stiff leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪnˌæpəl / (AmE), / ˈpaɪnˌæpəl / (BrE)
a large tropical fruit with sweet, juicy, yellow flesh and a tough, spiky skin, or the plant that produces it
