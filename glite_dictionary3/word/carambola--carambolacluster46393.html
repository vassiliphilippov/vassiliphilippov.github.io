carambola

noun
tropical fruit also called starfruit
Pronunciation: / ˌkærəmˈboʊlə / (AmE), / ˌkærəmˈbəʊlə / (BrE)
A deeply ridged, yellow-brown tropical fruit, used raw in salads or as a dessert when ripe, also known as starfruit.

Examples

fresh carambola slices
Farmers in tropical regions cultivate carambolas for export to other countries.
The carambola is often sliced to reveal its star-shaped cross-section in fruit salads.
You can eat a ripe carambola on its own or use it as a garnish for drinks.
