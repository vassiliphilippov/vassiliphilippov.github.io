to energise

verb
make energetic or give energy to
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnərˌdʒaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛnəˌdʒaɪz / (BrE)
to make someone feel full of energy or more alert and active; to give energy to something

Examples

to energise a person
A brisk morning walk always energises me for the day ahead.
Drinking fresh orange juice in the morning energises many people.
The coach's inspiring speech energised the whole team before the match.

Related Terms

  • synonym – invigorate: to give strength or energy to; to refresh
  • synonym – stimulate: to encourage something to become more active; to make more alert
  • antonym – weaken: to make less strong or less energetic
