to search

verb
look for information
Pronunciation: / sɝtʃ / (AmE), / sɜːtʃ / (BrE)
to try to find information about something or someone by looking carefully or investigating

Examples

to search online information
Before writing her paper, Maya spent hours searching for relevant articles online.
Detectives searched for clues that could help solve the mystery.
The journalist had to search through old archives to verify the story.
