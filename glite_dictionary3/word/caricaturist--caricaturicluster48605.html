caricaturist

noun
person who draws exaggerated portraits
Pronunciation: / ˌkær.ɪ.kəˈtʃʊr.ɪst / (AmE), / ˌkær.ɪ.kəˈtjʊər.ɪst / (BrE)
A person who draws or creates caricatures, exaggerating certain features for comic or satirical effect.

Examples

caricaturist draws portraits
At the fair, a skilled caricaturist quickly sketched humorous portraits of visitors.
She hired a caricaturist to entertain guests at the wedding with funny illustrations.
The famous caricaturist exaggerated the politician's nose in his latest drawing.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cartoonist: an artist who draws cartoons, often for newspapers or magazines
  • synonym – satirist: a writer or artist who uses humor, irony, or exaggeration to criticize people or society
