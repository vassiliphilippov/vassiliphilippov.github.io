unbeknownst

adjective, adverb
without someone's knowledge
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnbɪˈnoʊnst / (AmE), / ˌʌnbɪˈnəʊnst / (BrE)
happening or existing without someone knowing; unknown to someone (usually used with 'to')

Examples

Unbeknownst to the manager, the team had already finished the project ahead of schedule.
