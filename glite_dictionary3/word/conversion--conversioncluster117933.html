conversion

noun
change of religion or beliefs
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɜrʒən / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of changing from one religion, belief system, or way of life to another, often involving a spiritual transformation

Examples

conversion to a religion

His conversion to Islam occurred during his university years.
The community celebrated his conversion from atheism to Christianity.
After his conversion to Buddhism, he dedicated his life to helping others.
Her sudden conversion was a turning point in her personal journey.
Many people find inner peace after a spiritual conversion.
