stopped

adjective
blocked (of a nose)
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑpt / (AmE), / ˈstɒpt / (BrE)
(of a nose) blocked, so that air cannot easily pass through it.

Examples

Allergy season always gives her a stopped nose in the mornings.
If your nose feels stopped, try using a saline spray to help you breathe.
