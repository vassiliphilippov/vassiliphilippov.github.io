to stagger

verb
to shock or astonish greatly
Pronunciation: / ˈstæɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈstæɡə / (BrE)
to shock or greatly surprise someone; to overwhelm with amazement or disbelief

Examples

to stagger your friends
I was completely staggered by the amount of money he had spent.
It will stagger you to learn how easy the solution really is.
The news of her sudden resignation staggered everyone in the office.
