to tusk

verb
remove tusks from an animal
Pronunciation: / tʌsk / (AmE), / tʌsk / (BrE)
to remove the tusks from an animal, especially an elephant or walrus.

Examples

to tusk an elephant
Efforts to tusk walruses for research purposes are carefully regulated.
Illicit poachers often tusk elephants for the illegal ivory trade.
Veterinarians may tusk wild boars to prevent them from injuring each other.
