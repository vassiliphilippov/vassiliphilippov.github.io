yodel

noun
songlike cry alternating chest and falsetto voice
Pronunciation: / ˈjoʊ.dəl / (AmE), / ˈjəʊ.dəl / (BrE)
a songlike call or cry in which the voice quickly switches between the normal register and falsetto, typical in certain styles of music, especially in the Swiss Alps

Examples

Alpine yodel performance
During the festival, several performers impressed the crowd with a traditional Swiss yodel.
She practiced her yodel until it was clear and melodic.
The echo of a distant yodel could be heard across the mountain valley.

Related Terms

  • synonym – yodeling: the act or process of producing yodel calls
