notice

noun
request for payment of a debt or bill
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊtɪs / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtɪs / (BrE)
an official letter or message demanding payment of a debt or overdue bill

Examples

receive a notice bill
If you ignore the notice, additional penalties may apply.
She received a notice from the utility company for the outstanding balance.
The landlord sent a notice demanding payment of the overdue rent.
