to quarter-ass

verb
do with minimal effort (slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔɚɾɚˌæs / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtəˌas / (BrE)
to do something with very little effort or in a half-hearted way

Examples

to quarter-ass a project
He tends to quarter-ass things he doesn't care about.
The team was warned not to quarter-ass the final presentation.
You can't just quarter-ass your assignments and expect to get good grades.
