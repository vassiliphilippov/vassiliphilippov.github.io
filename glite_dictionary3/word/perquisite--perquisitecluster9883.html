perquisite

noun
exclusive right or privilege
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrkwəzɪt /; / ˈpɜːr.kwɪ.zɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːkwɪzɪt /; / ˈpɜː.kwɪ.zɪt / (BrE)
a special benefit or privilege given to someone because of their job or position, in addition to regular pay
