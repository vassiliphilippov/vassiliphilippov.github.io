caramel

noun
brown, sweet burnt sugar
Pronunciation: / ˈkærəmɛl, ˈkɑːrməl / (AmE), / ˈkærəməl, ˈkɑːrəmɛl / (BrE)
A brown, sweet substance made by heating sugar until it melts and is used to color and flavor foods.

Examples

caramel sauce topping
She added a spoonful of caramel to her coffee for extra sweetness.
The apples were dipped in melted caramel at the fall festival.
The dessert was topped with a layer of smooth, golden caramel.
