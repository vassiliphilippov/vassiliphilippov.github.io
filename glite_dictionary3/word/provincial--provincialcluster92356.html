provincial

noun
official in a Catholic religious order
Pronunciation: / prəˈvɪnʃəl / (AmE), / prəˈvɪnʃəl / (BrE)
an official in a Roman Catholic religious order who is responsible for an ecclesiastical province, acting under the superior general

Examples

provincial of order
After ten years as provincial, she was appointed to a higher office within the order.
Each provincial submits regular reports to the superior general in Rome.
The provincial called a meeting of all the superiors in the province.
