schilling

noun
former Austrian currency unit
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Austria before the adoption of the euro, used until 2002

Examples

Austrian schilling coin
