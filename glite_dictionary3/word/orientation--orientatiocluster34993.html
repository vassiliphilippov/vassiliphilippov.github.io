orientation

noun
screen or display direction (portrait/landscape)

(computing) the direction in which a device or its display shows content, typically portrait or landscape, as used by apps and operating systems.

Examples

screen orientation
The app automatically adjusts to your orientation and switches to landscape when you turn the tablet.
Please lock the screen orientation during presentations to avoid unexpected rotations.
Some older games do not support both orientations, forcing players to use portrait only.
