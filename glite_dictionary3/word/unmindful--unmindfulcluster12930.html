unmindful

adjective
not aware or attentive
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmaɪndfəl /; / ʌnˈmaɪn.fəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈmaɪndf(ə)l /; / ʌnˈmaɪn.fəl / (BrE)
not noticing or not paying attention to something; not aware
