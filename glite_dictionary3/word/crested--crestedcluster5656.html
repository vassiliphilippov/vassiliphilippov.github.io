crested

adjective
decorated with a plume (helmet)
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˈkrɛstɪd / (BrE)
(of a knight's helmet) having a decorative plume or feathered ornament on top.

Examples

crested knight helmet
Only nobles could afford to have crested helmets specially designed for tournaments.
The knight rode into battle wearing a crested helmet that gleamed in the sunlight.
The museum displayed a crested helmet from the medieval period, complete with its original plume.

Related Terms

  • synonym – plumed: adorned with a feather or feathers, often as decoration
