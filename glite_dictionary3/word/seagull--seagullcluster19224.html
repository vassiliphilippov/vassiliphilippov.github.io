seagull

noun
aquatic coastal bird with long wings
Pronunciation: / ˈsiˌɡʌl / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌɡʌl / (BrE)
A mostly white aquatic bird with long pointed wings and short legs, commonly found near coasts and inland waters.

Examples

seagull flies over beach
A seagull swooped down and snatched a piece of bread from the sand.
The sound of seagulls reminds me of childhood trips to the beach.
We watched the seagulls soaring above the crashing waves.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gull: any of several long-winged aquatic birds of the family Laridae
