sheikh

noun
leader of an Arab village or family
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪk / (AmE), / ʃeɪk / (BrE)
the leader of an Arab village, family, or tribe, especially a man respected for age or authority

Examples

sheikh of the tribe
After his father died, Ahmed became the new sheikh of the clan.
During the negotiations, the sheikh spoke on behalf of the entire family.
The sheikh called a meeting of all the elders in the village.
