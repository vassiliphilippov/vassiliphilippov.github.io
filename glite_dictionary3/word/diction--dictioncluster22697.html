diction

noun
choice or style of words in expression
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪkʃən / (AmE), / ˈdɪkʃən / (BrE)
the choice and use of words in speech or writing; the manner in which something is expressed verbally or in text

Examples

formal diction
A formal diction is often used in academic essays to convey seriousness and precision.
She was praised for her clear diction during the debate, which helped her arguments stand out.
The poet's diction is both simple and powerful, making the emotions easy to feel.
