hieroglyphic

noun
writing system using picture symbols
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk / (BrE)
A writing system that uses picture symbols to represent words and sounds, especially as used in ancient Egypt.

Examples

ancient hieroglyphic writing
Each hieroglyphic represented a specific sound or idea.
The Rosetta Stone was key to deciphering ancient Egyptian hieroglyphics.
Visitors admired the hieroglyphic inscriptions carved on the walls of the temple.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pictograph: A symbol representing a word or idea in a pictorial form.
