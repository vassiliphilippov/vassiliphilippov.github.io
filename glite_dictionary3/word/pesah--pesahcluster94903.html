Pesah

noun
Jewish festival of Passover
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪ.sɑ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ.sɑː / (BrE)
The Jewish festival of Passover, traditionally lasting eight days from the 15th of Nissan, commemorating the exodus of the Israelites from Egypt.
