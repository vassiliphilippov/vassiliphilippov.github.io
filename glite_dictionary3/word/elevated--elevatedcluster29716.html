elevated

adjective
higher than usual in degree or amount
Pronunciation: / ˈɛləˌveɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪveɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of amount or degree) higher than normal or usual; increased.

Examples

elevated blood pressure
After his surgery, the doctor noticed an elevated white blood cell count, which indicated a possible infection.
Pollution levels remain elevated in major cities during the summer months.
She was diagnosed with elevated cholesterol and advised to change her diet.
