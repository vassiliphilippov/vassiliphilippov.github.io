climate

noun
average weather conditions of a region
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪ.mət / (AmE), / ˈklaɪ.mət / (BrE)
the typical weather conditions of a region, averaged over a long period of time

Examples

tropical climate
Scientists are concerned about changes in global climate.
The Mediterranean climate is ideal for growing olives and grapes.
The harsh climate of the desert makes life there challenging.
