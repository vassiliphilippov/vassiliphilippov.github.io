cockup

noun
serious mistake; blunder
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːk.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.ʌp / (BrE)
a serious mistake or blunder, especially one resulting from confusion or incompetence

Examples

major cockup
The event turned into a real cockup after the main speaker canceled at the last minute.
There was a massive cockup with the train schedules, causing hours of delay.
We had to restart the project because of a cockup in the initial design phase.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blunder: a careless or stupid mistake
  • synonym – fiasco: a complete failure, especially in a ludicrous or humiliating way
  • antonym – success: the accomplishment of an aim or purpose (opposite of a mistake)
