fanatic

adjective
extremely enthusiastic or devoted
Pronunciation: / fəˈnætɪk / (AmE), / fəˈnætɪk / (BrE)
having or showing excessive enthusiasm and intense devotion to a cause or idea; extremely zealous or passionate

Examples

fanatic support for a cause
Her fanatic devotion to animal rights inspired others to join the cause.
Online communities can sometimes attract fanatic followers who defend their beliefs relentlessly.
The coach was a fanatic supporter of his team, attending every game with unmatched enthusiasm.

Related Terms

  • antonym – moderate: showing restraint or within reasonable limits
  • synonym – zealous: full of zeal or enthusiastic devotion
