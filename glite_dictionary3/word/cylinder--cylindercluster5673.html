cylinder

noun
container for compressed gases
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪl.ɪn.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɪl.ɪn.də / (BrE)
a metal container in the shape of a cylinder, used especially to hold gases such as oxygen or compressed air under pressure

Examples

an oxygen cylinder
Always check that the cylinder is full before taking it on a diving trip.
Compressed air cylinders must be stored upright and secured to prevent accidents.
The hospital replaced the empty oxygen cylinder in the emergency ward.
