ephemeral

noun
a short-lived organism or thing
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛmərəl / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛmərəl / (BrE)
an organism or thing that lasts for a very short time, especially an insect that lives only briefly in its adult form

Examples

ephemeral insect
The mayfly is a classic example of an ephemeral in the insect world.
These ephemerals burst into bloom for just a few days each spring.
Unlike most desert plants, ephemerals complete their life cycle quickly after rain.
