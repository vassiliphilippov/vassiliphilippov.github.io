sunglasses

noun
glasses to protect eyes from sunlight
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌɡlæsɪz / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌɡlɑːsɪz / (BrE)
Glasses with darkened or polarized lenses worn to protect the eyes from sunlight.

Examples
