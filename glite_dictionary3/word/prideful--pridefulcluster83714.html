prideful

adjective
arrogantly superior; disdainful
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪd.fəl / (AmE), / ˈpraɪd.fəl / (BrE)
having or showing an arrogant sense of superiority or disdain towards others; excessively proud

Examples

a prideful person
Despite criticism, he remained prideful and refused to admit he was wrong.
Her prideful attitude made it difficult for her to accept help from anyone.
The prideful glance he gave the others revealed he considered himself superior.

Related Terms

  • synonym – arrogant: having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities
  • antonym – humble: having or showing a modest or low view of one's importance
