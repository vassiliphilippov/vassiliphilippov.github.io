Ryukyuan

adjective
relating to the Ryukyu Islands
Pronunciation: / raɪˈjuːkjuən / (AmE), / raɪˈjuːkjʊən / (BrE)
Relating to the Ryukyu Islands, their people, language, culture, or artifacts.

Examples

Ryukyuan culture traditions
The museum exhibit highlighted Ryukyuan artifacts and traditional performances from Okinawa.
She enjoyed sampling Ryukyuan dishes, especially the local seaweed salad and sweet potato sweets.
The festival featured a troupe performing Ryukyuan dances accompanied by sanshin and drums.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Okinawan: relating to Okinawa, the largest of the Ryukyu Islands; often used interchangeably in contexts about Okinawan culture
