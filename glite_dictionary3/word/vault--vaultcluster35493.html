vault

noun
act of jumping over something
Pronunciation: / vɔlt / (AmE), / vɔːlt / (BrE)
an act of leaping or jumping over something, especially using the hands or a pole for support

Examples

gymnastics vault
He cleared the fence with a single vault during the escape.
Her record-breaking vault secured her first place in the competition.
The gymnast's vault over the horse was flawless and impressive.
