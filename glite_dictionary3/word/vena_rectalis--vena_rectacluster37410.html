vena rectalis

noun
veins of the anal canal and rectum
Pronunciation: / ˈviːnə rɛkˈteɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˈviːnə rɛkˈtɑːlɪs / (BrE)
Any of several veins that drain blood from the walls of the anal canal and rectum.

Examples

vena rectalis drains rectum
Damage to a vena rectalis may lead to internal rectal bleeding.
The vena rectalis plays a crucial role in the venous drainage of the rectum.
The surgeon carefully identified each vena rectalis during the procedure.
