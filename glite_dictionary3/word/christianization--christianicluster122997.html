Christianization

noun
conversion to Christianity
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪstʃənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkrɪstʃənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of converting individuals or groups to Christianity, or the adoption of Christianity as a religion by a people or region

Examples

Christianization of a country
Early missionaries played a key role in the Christianization of remote regions.
Many ancient customs disappeared during the Christianization of the local population.
The Christianization of Europe had a profound impact on its culture and institutions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – conversion: the act or process of changing religion or belief
