to enunciate

verb
to state clearly
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnʌnsiˌeɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈnʌnsieɪt / (BrE)
to express or state (an idea, thought, or opinion) clearly and distinctly

Examples

to enunciate your ideas
It is important to enunciate your arguments clearly in a debate.
She always enunciates her intentions to avoid misunderstandings.
The professor made sure to enunciate his points so everyone could understand.

Related Terms

  • synonym – articulate: to express an idea clearly
  • antonym – mumble: to say something indistinctly or unclearly
