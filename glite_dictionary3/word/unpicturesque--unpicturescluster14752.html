unpicturesque

adjective
not visually attractive or charming
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌpɪk.tʃərˈɛsk / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌpɪk.tʃərˈɛsk / (BrE)
lacking beauty, visual appeal, or charm; not picturesque

Examples

an unpicturesque town
Despite its reputation, the town felt rather unpicturesque during the rainy season.
The city outskirts were disappointingly unpicturesque compared to the historic center.
We stayed in an unpicturesque motel beside a busy highway.
