Allium ascalonicum

noun
species of onion producing mild, clustered bulbs
Pronunciation: / əˈliːəm ˌæskəˈlɒnɪkəm / (AmE), / əˈliːəm ˌaskəˈlɒnɪkəm / (BrE)
