brooding

noun
incubation of eggs by a bird
Pronunciation: / ˈbruː.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbruː.dɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of a bird sitting on eggs to incubate and hatch them by the warmth of its body

Examples

brooding hens
Proper brooding conditions are essential for the health of young poultry.
Scientists observed the brooding behavior of penguins in the wild.
The mother hen's brooding ensured that all the chicks hatched safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym – incubation: the process of keeping eggs warm so they develop and hatch
