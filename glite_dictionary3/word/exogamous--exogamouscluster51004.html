exogamous

adjective
marrying outside one's group
Pronunciation: / ɛkˈsɑː.ɡə.məs / (AmE), / ɛkˈsɒ.ɡə.məs / (BrE)
Relating to or involving the custom of marrying outside one's own social group, clan, or tribe.
