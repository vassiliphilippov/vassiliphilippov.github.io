mechanistically

adverb
in a mechanistic manner
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛkəˈnɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌmɛkəˈnɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or resembles mechanical processes or systems; in a manner that treats something as if it operates like a machine.

Examples

to analyze mechanistically
Some critics argue that humans should not be analyzed mechanistically without regard for emotions or consciousness.
The behavior of the system was explained mechanistically, focusing solely on physical laws.
The cells responded mechanistically to the stimulus, as predicted by the theory.

  • synonym – mechanistic: relating to or resembling a mechanism or mechanical processes
