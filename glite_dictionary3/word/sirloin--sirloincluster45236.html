sirloin

noun
prime cut of beef from the loin
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrˌlɔɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɜːˌlɔɪn / (BrE)
the portion of the loin of beef just in front of the rump, often considered a prime cut of meat

Examples

sirloin steak
At the butcher's, I bought a large sirloin for our weekend barbecue.
He ordered a juicy sirloin steak for dinner.
The chef expertly grilled the sirloin to medium-rare.
