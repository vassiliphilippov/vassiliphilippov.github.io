designation

noun
the act of identifying or naming
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛzɪɡˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛzɪɡˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of officially assigning a name, title, status, or job to someone or something

Examples

designation as manager
