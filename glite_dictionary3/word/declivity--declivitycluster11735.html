declivity

noun
a downward slope or bend
Pronunciation: / dɪˈklɪvəti / (AmE), / dɪˈklɪvɪti / (BrE)
A downward slope, especially of a hill or surface.

Examples

steep declivity
After the heavy rain, water rushed quickly down the declivity.
He lost his footing on the slippery declivity and slid several meters.
The trail led down a steep declivity towards the river below.
