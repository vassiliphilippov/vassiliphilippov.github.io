choky

adjective
uncomfortably tight around the neck
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃoʊki / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊki / (BrE)
(of clothing or other objects) uncomfortably tight around the neck or throat, tending to cause a feeling of choking.
