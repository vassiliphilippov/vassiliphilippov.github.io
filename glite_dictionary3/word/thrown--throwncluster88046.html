thrown

adjective
twisted, spun filaments (textiles)
Pronunciation: / θroʊn / (AmE), / θrəʊn / (BrE)
(of filaments) twisted together, especially spun into thread or yarn.

Examples

thrown silk threads
He inspected the thrown filaments before weaving them into the fabric.
In traditional weaving, only thrown yarn is used for the warp threads.
The finest scarves are made from thrown silk, giving them unmatched smoothness.
