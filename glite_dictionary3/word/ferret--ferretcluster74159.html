to ferret

verb
to hunt using ferrets
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrət / (AmE), / ˈfɛrət / (BrE)
to hunt rabbits or other small animals by using ferrets to drive them from their burrows

Examples

to ferret rabbits
He learned how to ferret with his grandfather on weekends.
In the old days, hunters would often ferret for rabbits in rural England.
They used trained dogs and a ferret to flush out rabbits from the field.
