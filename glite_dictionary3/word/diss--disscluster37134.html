to diss

verb
to insult or show disrespect
Pronunciation: / dɪs / (AmE), / dɪs / (BrE)
to treat someone with disrespect; to insult or speak rudely to someone, especially in a public or pointed way

Examples

to diss a friend
If you diss your boss, you might get into serious trouble at work.
She felt hurt after her friend dissed her in front of everyone.
The rapper dissed his rival in a new song that quickly went viral.
