Normodyne

noun
trade name for an antihypertensive drug (labetalol)
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrməˌdaɪn / (AmE), / ˈnɔːməˌdaɪn / (BrE)
A trade name for labetalol, an antihypertensive drug that blocks both alpha and beta-adrenergic receptors to lower blood pressure.
