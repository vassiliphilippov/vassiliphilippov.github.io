promoter

noun
person who organizes or publicizes events
Pronunciation: / prəˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / prəˈməʊtə / (BrE)
a person or company that organizes and publicizes public events, especially entertainment such as concerts, sports, or shows

Examples

concert promoter
As a promoter, she is responsible for booking venues and advertising concerts.
Many young artists rely on a promoter to gain exposure and attract fans.
The promoter secured a famous band to headline the summer music festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym – organizer: a person who arranges or coordinates events or activities
  • synonym – sponsor: a person or organization that supports an event financially or through resources
