to pole

verb
support with poles (esp. plants)
Pronunciation: / poʊl / (AmE), / pəʊl / (BrE)
to support or hold up (plants or similar objects) by means of poles

Examples

to pole beans
Farmers often pole beans to keep the vines off the ground.
She carefully poled each sapling in the garden to protect them from the wind.
We need to pole the young tomato plants to help them grow straight.
