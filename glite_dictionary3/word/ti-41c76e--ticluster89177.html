ti

noun
tropical shrub with elongated leaves, used for thatching and food
Pronunciation: / ti / (AmE), / ti / (BrE)
a tropical shrub (Cordyline fruticosa) with tufted elongated leaves, used for thatching, clothing, and food in southeastern Asia, Australia, and Hawaii

Examples

ti plant leaves
In Hawaii, the ti plant is often grown in gardens for its beautiful foliage.
Leaves from the ti plant can be woven into skirts and traditional garments.
The roots of the ti are sometimes baked and eaten as a sweet treat.
