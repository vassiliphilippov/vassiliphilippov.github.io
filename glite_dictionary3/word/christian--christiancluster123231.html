Christian

noun
follower of Christianity
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪs.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.tʃən / (BrE)

Early Christians often faced persecution for their faith.
He became a Christian after studying the teachings of Jesus.
She was raised as a Christian and goes to church every Sunday.
