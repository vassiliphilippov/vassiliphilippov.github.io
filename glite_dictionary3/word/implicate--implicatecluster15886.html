to implicate

verb
to show involvement in a crime
Pronunciation: / ˈɪm.plɪ.keɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪm.plɪ.keɪt / (BrE)
to show or suggest that someone is involved in a crime or wrongful act

Examples

to implicate a suspect
Several senior officials were implicated in the financial scandal that rocked the company.
She refused to answer questions that might implicate her friends.
The evidence clearly implicates him in the robbery last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – involve: to include someone or something in a situation or activity
