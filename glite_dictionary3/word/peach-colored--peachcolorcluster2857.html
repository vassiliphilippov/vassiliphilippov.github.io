peach-colored

adjective
pale pinkish-orange color
Pronunciation: / ˈpitʃ ˌkʌlərd / (AmE), / ˈpiːtʃ ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
Having the pale pinkish-orange color of a ripe peach.

Examples

peach-colored roses
He handed her a bouquet of peach-colored roses for their anniversary.
She wore a beautiful peach-colored dress to the summer party.
The bedroom walls were painted in a soft, peach-colored hue that brightened the space.
