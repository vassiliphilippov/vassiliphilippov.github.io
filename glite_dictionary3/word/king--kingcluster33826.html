king

noun
most important chess piece
Pronunciation: / kɪŋ / (AmE), / kɪŋ / (BrE)
In chess, the most important piece; although the weakest in power, the game is lost if it is put in checkmate.

Examples

chess king in check
A check occurs when the king is threatened by an opposing piece.
He moved his king into the corner to avoid checkmate.
In chess, protecting your king should always be your top priority.
