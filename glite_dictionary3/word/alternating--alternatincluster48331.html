alternating

adjective
occurring by turns; in succession
Pronunciation: / ˈɔl.tɚ.neɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɔːl.tə.neɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
(of things or actions) occurring by turns; happening first one, then the other, in succession.

Examples

alternating days
Rain and sunshine came in alternating periods throughout the afternoon.
She had alternating shifts at work: one week in the day, the next at night.
The music was played at alternating intervals of loud and soft passages.

Related Terms

  • synonym – alternate: to occur or cause to occur by turns
