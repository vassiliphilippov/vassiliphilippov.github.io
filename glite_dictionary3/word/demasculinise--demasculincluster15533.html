to demasculinise

verb
remove testicles of a male animal
Pronunciation: / diˈmæskjəˌlaɪnˌaɪz / (AmE), / diːˈmæskjʊlɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to remove the testicles of a male animal, thereby depriving it of male characteristics; castrate.

Examples

to demasculinise a male animal
After the surgery, the animal was effectively demasculinised and became more docile.
Farmers routinely demasculinise young bulls to manage herd behavior.
Veterinarians often demasculinise male livestock to control breeding.
