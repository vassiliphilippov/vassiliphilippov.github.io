rakishly

adverb
in a jaunty or stylish manner
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪkɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈreɪkɪʃli / (BrE)

Examples

smile rakishly

The actor smiled rakishly as he entered the room, completely at ease.
