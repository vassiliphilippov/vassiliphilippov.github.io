to memorialise

verb
mark an online profile as a memorial
Pronunciation: / m59c8m54d0ri59ccla6az / (AmE), / m6ac8m54d0r6a59la6az / (BrE)
(in digital or social-media contexts) to designate or convert an online profile, page, or account so that it serves as a memorial for a deceased person, often restricting normal access and allowing tributes.

Examples

to memorialise an account
The family asked the network to memorialise his profile so friends could leave messages.
After the accident, the platform memorialised her account and displayed a tribute banner.
Volunteers spend time memorialising former members by adding photos and short stories.
