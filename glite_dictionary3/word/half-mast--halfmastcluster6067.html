half-mast

noun
flag position below the top as a sign of mourning
Pronunciation: / ˌhæfˈmæst /; / ˌhæf ˈmæst / (AmE), / ˌhɑːfˈmɑːst /; / ˌhɑːf ˈmɑːst / (BrE)
the position of a flag on a pole, lowered from the top as a sign of mourning or respect

Examples
