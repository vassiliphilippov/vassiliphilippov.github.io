mixable

adjective
capable of being mixed
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪksəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈmɪksəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of substances) capable of being mixed together without separation; able to form a homogeneous mixture.

Examples

mixable liquids
Oil and water are not mixable under normal conditions.
The resin and hardener must be mixable to produce a strong adhesive.
These two liquids are mixable and will not separate after stirring.

Related Terms

  • synonym – miscible: able to be mixed to form a homogeneous solution (used mainly in chemistry)
