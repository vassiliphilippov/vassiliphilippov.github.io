Russula

noun
a genus of fungi with brittle, colorful caps
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌs.jʊ.lə /; / ruˈsuːlə / (AmE), / ˈrʌs.jʊ.lə /; / rʌˈsjuːlə / (BrE)
a large group (genus) of mushrooms with stout stems, brittle caps of various colors, and white spores, lacking a ring and a volva
