waxy

adjective
having the quality of wax
Pronunciation: / ˈwæksi / (AmE), / ˈwæksi / (BrE)
having the appearance, texture, or quality of wax; made of or covered with wax.

Examples

waxy surface
After polishing, the table had a waxy shine that made it look almost new.
Be careful—the hallway floor is still waxy and could be slippery.
Her hands felt waxy after handling the candles all afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym – waxen: made of or covered with wax; wax-like in appearance or texture
