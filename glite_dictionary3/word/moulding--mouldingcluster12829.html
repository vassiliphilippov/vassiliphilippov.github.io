moulding

noun
A sculpture made by molding
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈməʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
a sculpture or object produced by shaping material in a mold, including preliminary models made in wax or clay

Examples

This moulding will serve as the base for the finished marble sculpture.
