unconventionally

adverb
in an unconventional manner
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈvɛnʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈvɛnʃənəli / (BrE)
In a way that is not based on or conforming to what is generally done or believed; in an unconventional manner.

Examples
