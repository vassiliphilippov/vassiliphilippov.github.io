institutional

adjective
relating to an institution or organization
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.stɪˈtuː.ʃə.nəl /; / ˌɪn.stɪˈtuː.ʃən.əl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.stɪˈtjuː.ʃə.nəl /; / ˌɪn.stɪˈtjuː.ʃən.əl / (BrE)
relating to or involving an institution, especially a large organization such as a university, church, or bank
