critic

noun
person who frequently finds fault
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˈkrɪtɪk / (BrE)
a person who habitually finds fault or makes harsh and often unfair judgments

Examples

harsh critic
Instead of offering advice, he became a harsh critic of every decision.
She's her own worst critic and rarely appreciates her achievements.
The constant critic in the meeting made progress difficult for everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym – faultfinder: a person who finds excessive faults
  • antonym – praiser: a person who expresses approval or admiration
