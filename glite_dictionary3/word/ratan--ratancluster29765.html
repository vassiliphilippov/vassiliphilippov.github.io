ratan

noun
cane or switch from rattan palm
Pronunciation: / rəˈtæn / (AmE), / rəˈtan / (BrE)
A thin switch or cane made from the stems of rattan palms, often used for whipping or as a walking stick.

Examples

a ratan cane
A thin ratan switch was left by the door as a walking aid.
He fashioned a simple basket out of ratan he found in the jungle.
The teacher used a ratan to maintain discipline in the classroom.
