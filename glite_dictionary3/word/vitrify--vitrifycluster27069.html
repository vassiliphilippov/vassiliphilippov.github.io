to vitrify

verb
become glassy; undergo vitrification
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪtrəˌfaɪ /; / ˈvɪtrɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈvɪtrɪfʌɪ / (BrE)
to change into glass or a glass-like substance, usually by heating or fusion
