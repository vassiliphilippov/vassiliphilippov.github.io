res gestae

noun
legal facts surrounding an act
Pronunciation: / reɪz ˈʤɛstaɪ /; / reɪs ˈdʒɛsti, reɪz ˈdʒɛstaɪ / (AmE), / reɪz ˈʤɛstaɪ /; / reɪs ˈdʒɛstiː, rɛz ˈdʒɛstaɪ / (BrE)
(in law) facts, circumstances, or statements so closely connected to an event that they are considered part of the event and can be used as evidence in court
