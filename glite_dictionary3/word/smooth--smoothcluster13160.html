to smooth

verb
make easier by removing obstacles
Pronunciation: / smuð / (AmE), / smuːð / (BrE)
to make (something, such as a process or situation) easier by removing obstacles or difficulties

Examples

to smooth the process
Additional funding could smooth the implementation of the project.
Diplomatic efforts helped smooth the transition to democratic rule.
The new regulations are intended to smooth the approval process for small businesses.

  • synonym – facilitate: to make an action or process easier
