plainsman

noun
man from the plains region
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪnzmən / (AmE), / ˈpleɪnzmən / (BrE)
a man who lives in or comes from a plains region, especially the Great Plains of North America

Examples

experienced plainsman
As a seasoned plainsman, he knew how to survive harsh winters and summer droughts.

The old plainsman shared stories of buffalo hunts under the endless sky.
