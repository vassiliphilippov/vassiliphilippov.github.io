integration

noun
combining into a whole
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntəˈɡreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of combining two or more things so that they work together or form a whole

Examples

integration of systems
Successful cultural integration can enrich a society and promote harmony.
Technological integration is essential for modern businesses to stay competitive.
The integration of the two departments improved efficiency and communication.

Related Terms

  • synonym – consolidation: the action or process of making something stronger or more solid, or of combining things into a single more effective or coherent whole
  • antonym – disintegration: the process of losing cohesion or strength; breaking into parts
