cloyingly

adverb
in an excessively sweet or sentimental way
Pronunciation: / ˈklɔɪ.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈklɔɪ.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that is excessively sweet, sentimental, or rich to the point of causing discomfort.

Examples

cloyingly sweet flavor
He spoke to his dog in a cloyingly affectionate tone that made everyone smile.
Her cloyingly sweet voice made the compliment sound insincere.
The dessert was cloyingly rich, and I couldn't finish my slice.
