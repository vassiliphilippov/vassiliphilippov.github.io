to mature

verb
cause a sore/wound to ripen and discharge pus
Pronunciation: / məˈtʃʊr / (AmE), / məˈtjʊə / (BrE)
(of a wound or sore) to cause to ripen and discharge pus.

Examples

to mature a boil
