kelp

noun
large brown seaweed
Pronunciation: / kɛlp / (AmE), / kɛlp / (BrE)
a large brown seaweed with broad, tough fronds, found in underwater forests in coastal areas

Examples

kelp forest
Divers explored the underwater forests of kelp along the rocky coast.
Harvested kelp is used in food products and cosmetics.
Many species of fish rely on kelp beds for shelter and food.
