band

noun
identifier ring for a bird's leg
Pronunciation: / bænd / (AmE), / bænd / (BrE)
a strip of material fastened around a bird's leg for identification or tracking, especially in ornithological studies

Examples

bird band tag
Each band carries a unique code to help researchers track bird movements.
Several rare birds were found with a band indicating they had migrated from another region.
The ornithologist placed a band on the sparrow's leg before releasing it into the wild.
