parley

noun
negotiation between enemies
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrˌli / (AmE), / ˈpɑːli / (BrE)
a discussion or conference held to negotiate terms, especially between enemies or opposing sides during a conflict

Examples

parley between enemies
After hours of fierce fighting, both sides called for a parley to avoid further bloodshed.
The pirates held a parley with the merchant crew before deciding their fate.
The two commanders agreed to a parley to discuss the terms of a possible truce.
