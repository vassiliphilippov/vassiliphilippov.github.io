et alibi

adverb
and elsewhere (in references)
Pronunciation: / ɛt ˈæ.laɪ.bi / (AmE), / ɛt ˈal.ɪ.baɪ / (BrE)
and elsewhere; used in references to indicate other locations or occurrences in a text or document not listed in detail.
