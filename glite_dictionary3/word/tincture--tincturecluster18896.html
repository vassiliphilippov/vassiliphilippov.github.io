tincture

noun
slight trace or hint
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋktʃər / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋktʃə / (BrE)
a slight trace or hint of a quality or feeling

Examples

a tincture of sadness
Her voice held a tincture of regret that was hard to miss.
The atmosphere was calm, but with a tincture of tension beneath the surface.
There was a tincture of irony in his remarks during the meeting.
