easy

adjective
not strict or severe
Pronunciation: / ˈizi / (AmE), / ˈiːzi / (BrE)
not strict or severe; allowing freedom and not enforcing rigid rules or standards

Examples

easy on rules
He thought the team's coach was too easy on the players during practice.
Her professor is known for being an easy grader who rarely marks students down.
The school adopted more easy rules to reduce student stress.
