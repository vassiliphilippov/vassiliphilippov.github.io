to pulverize

verb
become powder or dust
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌlvəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈpʌlvəraɪz / (BrE)
to crush or grind something into a fine powder or dust; to break down into small particles

Examples

to pulverize rocks
If allowed to age long enough, the bread will eventually pulverize into crumbs.
In extremely dry conditions, rocks can pulverize naturally over centuries.

Chefs use a blender to pulverize the vegetables for the soup.
The machine can easily pulverize rocks into fine dust.
