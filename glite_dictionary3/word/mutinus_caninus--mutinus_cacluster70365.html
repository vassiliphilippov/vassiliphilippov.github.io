Mutinus caninus

noun
species of stinkhorn fungus
Pronunciation: / mjuːˈtaɪnəs kəˈnaɪnəs / (AmE), / mjuːˈtiːnəs kəˈnaɪnəs / (BrE)
A type of stinkhorn fungus with a slender, phallic-shaped stalk covered in slimy spore mass near the tip, often attracting insects.
