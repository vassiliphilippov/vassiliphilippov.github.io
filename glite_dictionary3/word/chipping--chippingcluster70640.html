chipping

noun
implanting an identification microchip
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of implanting a small identification microchip in an animal or object, typically to enable permanent identification and registration.

Examples

chipping a pet
The veterinary clinic offers chipping services to help owners identify lost pets quickly.
After the adoption, they paid for chipping and registered the dog's details with the national database.
Wildlife researchers use chipping to track animal movements and study migration patterns over years.

Related Terms

  • synonym – implantation: the act of placing or fixing a device (such as a microchip) into the body
  • synonym – microchipping: the process of inserting a microchip into an animal for identification
