echoing

adjective
having reflected, repeating sounds
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛkəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a sound or place) characterized by sounds that are reflected and repeated.

Examples

echoing hallway
Her voice seemed louder in the echoing hallways of the old school.
The footsteps sounded strange in the echoing corridor.
We heard laughter coming from the echoing chambers of the cave.

Related Terms

  • synonym – resonant: (of a sound) deep, clear, and continuing to sound or ring
