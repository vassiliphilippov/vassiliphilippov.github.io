unlisted

adjective
not publicly visible online; accessible by link
Pronunciation: / ənˈlɪstɪd / (AmE), / ənˈlɪstɪd / (BrE)
(on digital platforms) not made publicly visible or included in search results or public listings; accessible only to people with a direct link or with specific permission.

Examples

unlisted YouTube video
She uploaded an unlisted video that only friends with the link could watch.
The teacher shared an unlisted album of the field trip so parents could view photos.
Change the setting to unlisted if you want the post hidden from searches but accessible by URL.

Related Terms

  • antonym – listed: included in a public list, directory, or searchable index
  • antonym – public: visible and accessible to all users; appearing in search results or listings
