tack

noun
short nail with a broad head
Pronunciation: / tæk / (AmE), / tæk / (BrE)
a small nail with a sharp point and a broad, flat head, used to fasten materials together

Examples

tack on a board
He used a tack to pin the notice to the bulletin board.
Mind the tack on the floor so you don’t step on it.
The upholstery was held in place with a single tack in each corner.
