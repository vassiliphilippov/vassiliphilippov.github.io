stifler

noun
person who suppresses or restrains
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪf.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈstaɪf.lə / (BrE)
a person who suppresses, restrains, or inhibits something, especially speech, actions, or feelings

Examples

stifler of ideas
Don't let fear be a stifler of your ambitions and dreams.
Every workplace has at least one stifler who insists on sticking to traditional methods.
She became a stifler of creativity by always dismissing new ideas in meetings.
