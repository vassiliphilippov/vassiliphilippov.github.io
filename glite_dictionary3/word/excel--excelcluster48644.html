to excel

verb
be exceptionally good at something
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsɛl / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛl / (BrE)
to be exceptionally good at an activity or subject; to surpass others in a particular area

Examples

to excel at sports
He excelled at chess from a very young age.
Many athletes excel in high-pressure situations.
She has always tried to excel in everything she does.
