fibromyositis

noun
inflammation of muscle and connective tissue
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪbroʊˌmaɪəˈsaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌfɪbrəʊˌmaɪəˈsaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A medical condition involving localized inflammation of muscle and connective tissue, often causing pain and stiffness.

Examples
