looseness

noun
frequent and watery bowel movements
Pronunciation: / ˈluːsənəs / (AmE), / ˈluːsənɪs / (BrE)
a condition characterized by frequent and watery bowel movements, often as a symptom of infection, food poisoning, or other gastrointestinal illnesses

Examples

looseness of bowels
Doctors often ask about any recent looseness when diagnosing stomach problems.
The patient reported experiencing looseness after eating contaminated food.
Travelers sometimes suffer from looseness due to changes in diet or water.
