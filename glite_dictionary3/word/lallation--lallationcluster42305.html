lallation

noun
defective pronunciation of l or r sounds
Pronunciation: / læˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ləˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a speech defect in which the sound /l/ is incorrectly articulated or the phoneme /r/ is pronounced as /l/

Examples

speech lallation problem
An untreated lallation can affect a person's confidence when speaking in public.
Lallation is often noticed when young children are learning to speak and struggle with certain sounds.
The speech therapist worked with the child to correct his lallation.
