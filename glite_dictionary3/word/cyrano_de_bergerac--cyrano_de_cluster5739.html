Cyrano de Bergerac

proper noun
French soldier; hero of Rostand's play
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪrəˌnoʊ də bərˈʒɛræk / (AmE), / ˌsɪərəˌnəʊ də bɛəˈʒɛəræk / (BrE)
a French soldier and writer (1619–1655), primarily remembered as the larger-than-life hero with a prominent nose in Edmond Rostand's 1897 play of the same name.
