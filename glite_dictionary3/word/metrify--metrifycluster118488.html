to metrify

verb
convert to the metric system
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtrəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to convert something from a non-metric system to the metric system.

Examples

to metrify road signs
After joining the European Union, the country decided to metrify all its road signs and measurements.
Engineers worked to metrify the machinery specifications for international compatibility.
The school curriculum was updated to metrify science experiments from imperial to metric units.

Related Terms

  • synonym – metricate: to change to the metric system
