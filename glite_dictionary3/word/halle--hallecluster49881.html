Halle

proper noun
city in eastern Germany
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑː.lə / (AmE), / ˈha.lə / (BrE)
A city in the Saxony-Anhalt region of Germany, situated on the Saale River, historically a member of the Hanseatic League in the 13th and 14th centuries.
