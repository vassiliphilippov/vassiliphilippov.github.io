arteria laryngea

noun
artery supplying blood to the larynx
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪriə ləˈrɪndʒiə / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə ləˈrɪndʒiə / (BrE)
Either of two arteries that supply blood to the larynx.

Examples

arteria laryngea supplies larynx
An injury to the arteria laryngea can cause significant bleeding in the neck.
During surgery, care must be taken not to damage the arteria laryngea.
The arteria laryngea is crucial for supplying blood to the vocal cords.
