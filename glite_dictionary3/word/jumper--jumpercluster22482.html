jumper

noun
knitted garment for the upper body
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmpɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpə / (BrE)
a crocheted or knitted garment worn on the upper part of the body, typically with long sleeves; a sweater (especially in British English)

Examples

warm jumper
He put on his favorite jumper before heading out into the chilly evening.
She wore a bright blue jumper with jeans to the party.
You should knit yourself a warm jumper for the winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sweater: a warm knitted piece of clothing covering the upper part of the body
