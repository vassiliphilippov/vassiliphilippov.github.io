occupation

noun
act of occupying a building
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːkjəˈpeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɒkjʊˈpeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of occupying, taking control, or taking possession of a building, especially for use or habitation

Examples

occupation of a building
Following the storm, the occupation of the damaged school was prohibited for safety reasons.
The activists staged an occupation of the old factory to protest its demolition.
The unauthorized occupation of empty houses has become a pressing issue in some cities.
