to agenise

verb
bleach flour with Agene
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪdʒəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈeɪdʒənaɪz / (BrE)
to age or bleach flour using Agene (nitrogen trichloride)

Examples

to agenise flour
Early in the twentieth century, many mills would agenise flour to improve its appearance.
Regulations soon prohibited companies from continuing to agenise flour due to health risks.
The process used to agenise flour has been largely abandoned in modern baking.
