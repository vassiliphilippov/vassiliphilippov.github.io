Canis anthus

noun
African golden wolf (canid species)
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪnɪs ˈænθəs / (AmE), / ˈkeɪnɪs ˈænθəs / (BrE)
the scientific name for the African golden wolf, a canid native to the deserts and savannas of Northern Africa, closely related to the golden jackal and the grey wolf
