munificence

noun
great generosity; liberal giving
Pronunciation: / mjuˈnɪfəsəns / (AmE), / mjuːˈnɪfɪsns / (BrE)
great generosity or liberal giving, especially of gifts or money

Examples

munificence of a donor
She thanked her uncle for his munificence in funding her education.
The benefactor's munificence allowed the hospital to build a new wing.
The festival was made possible by the munificence of local sponsors.
