to ride

verb
to depend on something (figurative)
Pronunciation: / raɪd / (AmE), / raɪd / (BrE)
(of an outcome or situation) to depend on or be determined by something.

Examples

ride on success
Much of our success in the market will ride on how customers react to the new product.
The entire season rode on the final match against their rivals.
Their hopes for promotion ride on the outcome of this meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – depend: to be determined by or reliant on something
