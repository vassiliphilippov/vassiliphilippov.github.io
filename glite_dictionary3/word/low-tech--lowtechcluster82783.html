low-tech

adjective
involving simple or basic technology
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ tɛk / (AmE), / ˌləʊ ˈtɛk / (BrE)
Using or involving only simple, basic technology rather than advanced or sophisticated methods.

Examples

low-tech solution

The school uses low-tech teaching methods that don’t require computers or tablets.
