lethargically

adverb
in a sluggish or unenergetic way
Pronunciation: / lɪˈθɑrdʒɪkli / (AmE), / lɪˈθɑːdʒɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that lacks energy or enthusiasm; sluggishly.

Examples

move lethargically around
After his illness, he moved lethargically around the house all morning.
He responded lethargically to the coach's instructions, barely making an effort.
The students listened lethargically to the long, monotonous lecture.

Related Terms

  • antonym – energetically: in an energetic way, with lots of energy
  • synonym – lazily: in a lazy, unenergetic manner
