Nageia

proper noun
genus of Asian evergreen trees
Pronunciation: / nəˈdʒiːə / (AmE), / nəˈdʒiːə / (BrE)
A small genus of Asian evergreen trees with columnar crowns, distinguished by leaves that lack a midrib; native to eastern Asia, including India, the Philippines, and New Guinea.
