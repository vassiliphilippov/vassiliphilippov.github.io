bordelaise

noun
sauce with red wine and marrow
Pronunciation: / ˌbɔrdəˈleɪz / (AmE), / ˌbɔːdəˈleɪz / (BrE)
A rich brown sauce made with red wine and beef marrow, often served with beef dishes.

Examples

bordelaise sauce for steak
For a touch of elegance, pour bordelaise over the roasted beef before serving.
