praiseworthily

adverb
in an admirable way
Pronunciation: / ˌpreɪzˈwɝːðɪli / (AmE), / ˌpreɪzˈwɜːðɪli / (BrE)
In a manner that deserves praise or admiration; admirably.

Examples

to act praiseworthily
He acted praiseworthily when he returned the lost wallet to its rightful owner.
She handled the difficult situation praiseworthily, earning the respect of all her colleagues.
The volunteers praiseworthily devoted their time to rebuilding the community center.
