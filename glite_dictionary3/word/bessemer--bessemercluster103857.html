Bessemer

proper noun
British inventor and metallurgist
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛsəmər / (AmE), / ˈbɛsɪmə / (BrE)
(1813–1898) British inventor and metallurgist best known for developing the Bessemer process for the mass production of steel.
