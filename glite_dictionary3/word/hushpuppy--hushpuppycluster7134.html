hushpuppy

noun
deep-fried cornmeal ball
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌʃˌpʌpi / (AmE), / ˈhʌʃˌpʌpi / (BrE)
A small ball of cornmeal batter that is deep-fried, often served as a side dish in Southern United States cuisine.

Examples

crispy hushpuppy balls
