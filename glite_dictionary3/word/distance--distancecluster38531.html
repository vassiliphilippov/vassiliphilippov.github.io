distance

noun
emotional detachment or lack of closeness
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪstəns / (AmE), / ˈdɪstəns / (BrE)
a state of emotional detachment or lack of closeness, often caused by personal withdrawal or indifference

Examples

emotional distance
A certain distance developed between the two friends over the years.
She maintained a professional distance from her colleagues to avoid conflicts.
Their sudden distance made it difficult to discuss personal issues openly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – aloofness: the quality of being distant or reserved
  • antonym – closeness: a feeling of emotional nearness or intimacy
  • synonym – indifference: lack of interest, concern, or sympathy
