gipsywort

noun
a Eurasian herbaceous plant with white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪpsiˌwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪpsiˌwɜːt / (BrE)
a hairy Eurasian herb (Lycopus europaeus) with two-lipped white flowers, typically found in damp places along riverbanks and ditches.

Examples

wild gipsywort plant
Botanists identified the mysterious plant as gipsywort during their field survey.
Many herbalists value gipsywort for its historical uses in traditional remedies.
