listlessly

adverb
without energy or enthusiasm
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪstlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈlɪstlɪsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows little or no energy, interest, or enthusiasm.

Examples

move listlessly around
After a long day, he spoke listlessly to his friends.
She stared listlessly out the window, barely noticing the rain.
The students moved listlessly through the crowded hallway.
