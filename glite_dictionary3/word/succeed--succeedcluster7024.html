to succeed

verb
achieve a goal or be successful
Pronunciation: / səkˈsiːd / (AmE), / səkˈsiːd / (BrE)

Examples

to succeed in business
After years of hard work, she finally succeeded in publishing her first novel.
If we succeed with this plan, the company will expand internationally.
They succeeded despite facing numerous obstacles along the way.
