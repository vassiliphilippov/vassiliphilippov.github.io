apomictic

adjective
reproducing without fusion of gametes
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˈmɪktɪk / (AmE), / ˌapəˈmɪktɪk / (BrE)
(of reproduction, especially in plants) not involving sexual fusion; able to produce offspring without fertilization
