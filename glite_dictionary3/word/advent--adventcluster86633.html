Advent

noun
the four-week period before Christmas
Pronunciation: / ˈæd.vɛnt / (AmE), / ˈæd.vɛnt / (BrE)
The period including the four Sundays before Christmas, observed by many Christians as a time of waiting and preparation for the celebration of the birth of Christ.

Examples

Advent season begins
Many churches hold special services during Advent to prepare for Christmas.
She enjoys the traditions and reflection that come with Advent each year.
The children opened a new door on their Advent calendar each day leading up to Christmas.
