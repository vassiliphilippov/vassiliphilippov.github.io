elderberry

noun
small dark berry from the elder tree
Pronunciation: / ˈɛldɚˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈɛldəˌbɛri / (BrE)
A small, dark purple fruit from the elder tree, often used to make wines, jellies, and syrups.
