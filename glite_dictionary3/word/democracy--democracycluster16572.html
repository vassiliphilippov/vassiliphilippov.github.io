democracy

noun
system of government by the people
Pronunciation: / dəˈmɑːkrəsi /; / dɪˈmɑkrəsi /; / dɪˈmɑːkrəsi / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒkrəsi / (BrE)
a system of government in which people have the power to make decisions, usually by electing representatives; also, the belief in or support for such a system
