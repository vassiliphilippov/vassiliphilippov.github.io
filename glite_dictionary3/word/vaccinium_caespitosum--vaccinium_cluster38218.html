Vaccinium caespitosum

noun
a low-growing North American shrub with blue berries
Pronunciation: / vækˈsɪniəm sɛsˈpɪtəsəm / (AmE), / vækˈsɪniəm siːˈspɪtəsəm / (BrE)
A low-growing, tufted deciduous shrub native to northern and alpine regions of North America, with pink to coral-red flowers and sweet blue berries.
