pairing

noun
the act of matching a male and female for reproduction
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of bringing a male and female together for reproduction, especially in animals or plants

Examples

animal pairing
Seasonal changes often trigger pairing behavior in many species.
Selective pairing of plants can produce offspring with desirable traits.
