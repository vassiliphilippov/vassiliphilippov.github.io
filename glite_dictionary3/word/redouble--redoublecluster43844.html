to redouble

verb
to double again; increase greatly
Pronunciation: / riˈdoʊbəl /; / riˈdʌbəl /; / riˈdʌb.əl / (AmE), / riˈdʌbəl /; / riˈdʌb.əl / (BrE)
to make something much greater, stronger, or more intense, especially after it has already increased
