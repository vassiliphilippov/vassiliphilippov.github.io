wages

noun
recompense or retribution for actions
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪdʒɪz / (AmE), / ˈweɪdʒɪz / (BrE)
Consequences or return received, especially as reward for virtuous acts or punishment for wrongdoings.

Examples

wages of sin
Her good deeds brought her the wages of a clear conscience.
In literature, the wages of betrayal are often depicted as loneliness and regret.
The preacher warned that the wages of sin is death.

Related Terms

  • antonym – punishment: the infliction of a penalty as retribution for an offense
  • synonym – reward: something given in recognition of service, effort, or achievement
