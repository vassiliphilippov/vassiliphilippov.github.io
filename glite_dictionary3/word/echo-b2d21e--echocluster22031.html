to echo

verb
(of sound) to be repeated by reflection
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɛkəʊ / (BrE)
(of a sound) to be repeated or to resound because it is reflected off a surface.

Examples

to echo through caves
As he shouted, his voice echoed off the distant mountains.
Her laughter echoed through the empty corridor, making the silence feel even deeper.
The boom of the fireworks echoed across the valley for several seconds.

Related Terms

  • synonym – resound: (of a sound) to fill a place with sound; to echo loudly
