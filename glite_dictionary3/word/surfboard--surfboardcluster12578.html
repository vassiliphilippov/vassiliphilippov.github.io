to surfboard

verb
ride waves on a surfboard
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːf.bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈsɜːf.bɔːd / (BrE)
to ride on waves in the sea while standing or lying on a surfboard

Examples

to surfboard on waves
She loves to surfboard every morning when the waves are high.
They surfboarded along the coast until sunset.
We watched the athletes surfboarding during the competition.
