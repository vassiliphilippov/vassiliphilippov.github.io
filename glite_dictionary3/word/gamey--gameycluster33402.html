gamey

adjective
sexually suggestive, slightly indecent
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪmi / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmi / (BrE)
(of language, stories, etc.) slightly shocking, often because of sexual content or suggestiveness.

Examples

gamey jokes
Her stories always have a gamey sense of humor that some people might find inappropriate.
Some of the jokes in his routine were a bit gamey for the audience.
The novel was considered too gamey for publication in the 1950s.

Related Terms

  • antonym – innocent: not intended to be sexually suggestive or indecent
  • synonym – racy: entertaining and slightly shocking, often due to sexual content
  • synonym – risqué: slightly indecent or liable to shock, especially by being sexually suggestive
