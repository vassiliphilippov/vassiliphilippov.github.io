to reckon

verb
take into account; consider (as a factor)
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛk.ən / (AmE), / ˈrɛk.ən / (BrE)
to consider or take into account something, especially as a factor to be dealt with; to regard as necessary to address or handle

Examples

to reckon with problems
Politicians often have to reckon with opposition from various groups.
The company failed to reckon with public opinion when launching the new product.
We must reckon with the unpredictable effects of climate change.

Related Terms

  • synonym – consider: to think about carefully, especially to make a decision
  • antonym – ignore: to deliberately pay no attention to
