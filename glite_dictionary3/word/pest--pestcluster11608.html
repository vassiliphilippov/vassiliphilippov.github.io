pest

noun
epidemic disease with high death rate
Pronunciation: / pɛst / (AmE), / pɛst / (BrE)
any epidemic disease characterized by a high death rate, especially historically significant contagious illnesses such as the plague

Examples

deadly pest outbreak
During the Middle Ages, many communities were afflicted by a deadly pest.
Some historians believe the sudden decline was caused by an unknown pest.
The village was devastated by a pest that claimed hundreds of lives.

Related Terms

  • synonym – epidemic: widespread occurrence of an infectious disease in a community at a particular time
  • synonym – plague: a serious infectious disease that spreads quickly and kills many people
