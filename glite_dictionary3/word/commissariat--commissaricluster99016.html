commissariat

noun
food supply, especially for military
Pronunciation: / kəˌmɪsəriˈæt / (AmE), / kəˌmɪsəriˈæt / (BrE)
a stock or supply of food, especially for a military force or expedition

Examples

army commissariat
A shortage in the commissariat forced the troops to go on half rations.
During the expedition, the explorers relied heavily on the central commissariat for their daily needs.
The army's commissariat was well stocked to last through the harsh winter.
