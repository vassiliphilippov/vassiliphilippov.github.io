know-how

noun
practical knowledge and skill
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊˌhaʊ / (AmE), / ˈnəʊˌhaʊ / (BrE)
practical knowledge and skill to do something effectively, especially of a technical nature

technical know-how

The company's success relies on the technical know-how of its engineers.
