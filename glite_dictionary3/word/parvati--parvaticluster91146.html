Parvati

proper noun
Hindu goddess, wife of Shiva
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrvəti / (AmE), / ˈpɑːvəti / (BrE)
(in Hinduism) the wife of Shiva and a benevolent aspect of the goddess Devi, regarded as the Hindu goddess of plenty.

Examples

Parvati goddess of plenty
In Hindu mythology, Parvati is known for her devotion to her husband Shiva.
Many devotees worship Parvati for blessings of prosperity and family harmony.
The temple festival was dedicated to Parvati, honoring her role as the mother goddess.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Devi: the great goddess in Hinduism, of whom Parvati is a manifestation
  • synonym – Shiva: principal Hindu deity, husband of Parvati
