reproduction

noun
copy of an original work or object
Pronunciation: / ˌriːprəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːprəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
a copy of an original work of art, document, or object; something made to closely resemble the original

Examples

reproduction painting
She bought a reproduction of the antique vase for her living room.
The museum displayed a reproduction of the famous painting for the public.
This book contains several high-quality reproductions of ancient manuscripts.

Related Terms

  • antonym – original: the first form or version from which copies are made
  • synonym – replica: an exact copy or model of something, especially one on a smaller scale
