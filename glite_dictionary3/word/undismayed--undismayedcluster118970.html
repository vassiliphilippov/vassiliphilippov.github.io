undismayed

adjective
not discouraged or unshaken in purpose
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.dɪsˈmeɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪsˈmeɪd / (BrE)

The hikers, though tired, were undismayed by the rough terrain ahead.
