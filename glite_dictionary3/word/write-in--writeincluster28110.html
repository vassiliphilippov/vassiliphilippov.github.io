write-in

noun
a vote for an unlisted candidate
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪt ɪn / (AmE), / ˈraɪt ɪn / (BrE)
a vote, candidate, or option in an election where a person's name is written on the ballot by the voter because it is not among the printed choices
