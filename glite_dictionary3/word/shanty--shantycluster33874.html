shanty

noun
sailors' rhythmic work song
Pronunciation: / ˈʃænti / (AmE), / ˈʃænti / (BrE)
a rhythmic work song sung by sailors, usually to accompany manual labor on board ship

Examples

sing a shanty
A famous shanty like 'Drunken Sailor' can still be heard at maritime festivals today.
He learned an old shanty from his grandfather, who was once a merchant seaman.
The sailors raised their voices in a shanty as they hauled in the heavy rope.
