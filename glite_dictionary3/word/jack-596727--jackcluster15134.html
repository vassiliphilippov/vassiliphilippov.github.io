jack

noun
small game piece used in jacks
Pronunciation: / dʒæk / (AmE), / dʒak / (BrE)
a small six-pointed metal or plastic piece used as part of the game of jacks, which players pick up while bouncing a ball

Examples

jack in game
Each player tossed a jack into the circle before starting the game.
She collected all the jacks from the floor after they finished playing.
The shiny metal jack rolled under the sofa and was hard to reach.
