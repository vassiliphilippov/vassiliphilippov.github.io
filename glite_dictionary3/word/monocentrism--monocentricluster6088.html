monocentrism

noun
having a single center
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑː.nəˈsɛn.trɪ.zəm / (AmE), / ˌmɒ.nəˈsɛn.trɪ.zəm / (BrE)
the condition or quality of having or being organized around a single center or central point.
