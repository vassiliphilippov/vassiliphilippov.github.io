to anagrammatize

verb
form or interpret anagrams
Pronunciation: / əˈnæɡrəˌmætaɪz / (AmE), / əˈnæɡ.rə.mə.taɪz / (BrE)
To arrange the letters of a word or phrase so as to form an anagram, or to read them out of order to discover a hidden meaning.

Examples

to anagrammatize a word
During the puzzle competition, she managed to anagrammatize each clue in under a minute.
He loves to anagrammatize famous names to create clever pseudonyms.
The author anagrammatized the villain's name to hint at his true identity.
