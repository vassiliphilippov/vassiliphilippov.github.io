taqiyya

noun
concealing faith to avoid persecution
Pronunciation: / tɑːˈkiːjə / (AmE), / tæˈkiːjə / (BrE)
the practice of concealing one's true religious beliefs to avoid persecution, especially in the context of Islamic jurisprudence

Examples

practice taqiyya secretly
He practiced taqiyya during the conflict to protect his religious identity.
In history, some minority groups practiced taqiyya when openly expressing their faith was dangerous.
Muslims living under threat sometimes practiced taqiyya to preserve their safety.
