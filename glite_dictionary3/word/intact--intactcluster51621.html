intact

adjective
hymen unbroken (medical/archaic sense)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtækt / (AmE), / ɪnˈtækt / (BrE)
(of a woman) having the hymen unbroken; not having experienced sexual intercourse.

Examples

intact before marriage
In certain cultures, a woman was expected to be intact before marriage.
Medical examinations were conducted to determine if the patient was still intact.
The narrative described her as pure and intact, untouched by any man.
