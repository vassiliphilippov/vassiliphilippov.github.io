lordliness

noun
overbearing pride; superior manner
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔrdlinəs /; / ˈlɔrdli.nəs / (AmE), / ˈlɔːdlɪnəs /; / ˈlɔːdli.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of behaving in a proud, commanding, or dignified way
