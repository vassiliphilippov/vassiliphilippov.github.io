gas

noun
excess gas in the digestive tract
Pronunciation: / ɡæs / (AmE), / ɡæs / (BrE)
Excessive accumulation of air or other gases in the digestive tract, often causing discomfort or bloating.

Examples

stomach gas pain
After eating beans, she suffered from gas and abdominal pain.
Certain foods can cause gas and bloating in some people.
Over-the-counter remedies are available to relieve gas discomfort.

  • synonym – flatulence: the accumulation of gas in the alimentary canal
