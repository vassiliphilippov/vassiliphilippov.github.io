asexuality

noun
lack of evident sex or sex organs
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪˌsɛkʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌeɪˌsɛkʃuˈælɪti / (BrE)
the state of having no evident sex or sex organs, especially in certain animals, plants, or organisms

Examples

plant asexuality
Because of its asexuality, the species does not display typical mating behaviors.
Some simple organisms exhibit asexuality, lacking any visible differences between sexes.
The asexuality of certain plants allows them to reproduce without pollination.
