bowlegged

adjective
having outwardly curved legs at the knees
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊˌlɛɡɪd / (AmE), / ˈbəʊˌlɛɡɪd / (BrE)
(of a person) having legs that curve outward at the knees, causing the knees to be spaced apart when standing with feet together.

Examples

bowlegged child
As a child, she was slightly bowlegged, but her legs straightened with time.
Doctors often recommend special exercises for bowlegged children to improve leg alignment.
The bowlegged cowboy sauntered into the saloon after a long ride.

Related Terms

  • antonym – knock-kneed: having knees that bend inward so they touch or nearly touch while standing
