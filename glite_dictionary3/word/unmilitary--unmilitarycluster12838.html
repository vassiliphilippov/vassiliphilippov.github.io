unmilitary

adjective
not connected to the military
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɪlɪˌtɛri / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɪlɪtəri / (BrE)
Not related to, characteristic of, or suitable for soldiers or the military.

Examples

unmilitary attitude
Her manner was distinctly unmilitary, preferring cooperation to command.
The ceremony took place in an unmilitary setting, emphasizing peace and unity.
The new policy was criticized for its unmilitary approach to national security.

Related Terms

  • antonym – military: related to soldiers, the armed forces, or warfare
  • synonym – nonmilitary: not relating to or involved with the military
