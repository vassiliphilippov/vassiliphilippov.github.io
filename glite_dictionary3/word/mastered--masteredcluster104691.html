mastered

adjective
fully understood or learned
Pronunciation: / ˈmæstɚd / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstəd / (BrE)
Fully understood or learned so as to be able to use or explain it without difficulty.

Examples

He has all the necessary skills mastered for the new job.
The basics of chess are easily mastered by most children.
