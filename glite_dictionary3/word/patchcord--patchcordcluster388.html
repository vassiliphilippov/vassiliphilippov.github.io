patchcord

noun
cable with plugs for electronic connections
Pronunciation: / ˈpætʃˌkɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpætʃˌkɔːd / (BrE)
a length of cable with a plug at each end, used to connect components in electronic or audio equipment

Examples

patchcord for speakers

Without a patchcord, you won't be able to reroute the audio signal.
