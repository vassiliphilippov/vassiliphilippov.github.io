unavailable

adjective
not accessible or obtainable
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈveɪ.lə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈveɪ.lə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be used, obtained, or reached; not accessible or at hand.

Examples

unavailable for comment
I'm sorry, but that report is currently unavailable to the public.
She tried to call him, but his phone was unavailable all afternoon.
Tickets for the concert became unavailable within minutes of going on sale.
