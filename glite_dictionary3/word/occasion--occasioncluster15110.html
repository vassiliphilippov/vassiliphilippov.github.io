to occasion

verb
to cause or bring about (verb)
Pronunciation: / əˈkeɪʒən / (AmE), / əˈkeɪʒ(ə)n / (BrE)
to cause something to happen; to give rise to or bring about (an event or situation)

Examples

to occasion problems
His careless remark occasioned an awkward silence in the room.
The delay was occasioned by heavy rainfall in the area.
The new policy occasioned much debate among community leaders.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cause: to make something happen, especially something bad
  • synonym – provoke: to cause a particular reaction or feeling, often undesired
