BA

noun
Business Analyst (occupation)
Pronunciation: / \u02ccbi\u02d0 \u02c8e\u026a / (AmE), / \u02ccbi\u02d0 \u02c8\u0251\u02d0 / (BrE)
informal abbreviation for Business Analyst: a professional who analyses business processes and requirements and translates them into specifications for IT or organisational projects.

Examples

BA job
The project team hired a BA to document the client's requirements.
Our new BA interviewed stakeholders to map current processes and identify gaps for improvement.
As a BA, she translated user needs into clear technical specifications for developers.
