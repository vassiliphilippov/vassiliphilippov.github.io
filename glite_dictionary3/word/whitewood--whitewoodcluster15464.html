whitewood

noun
light wood of the tulip tree used for furniture
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪt.wʊd / (BrE)
the light, easily worked wood of the tulip tree, often used for making furniture and veneer

Examples

whitewood furniture
Due to its affordability, whitewood is a common choice for mass-produced furniture.
Many kitchen cabinets use whitewood because it is easy to shape and stain.
The table was crafted from whitewood, giving it a smooth and pale finish.
