barren

adjective
unable to produce offspring
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrən / (AmE), / ˈbærən / (BrE)
(of a woman or female animal) unable to produce offspring; sterile.

Examples

barren woman
After years of trying, the couple discovered that the wife was barren.
Old tales sometimes blame a witch's curse if a field or woman is barren.
Veterinarians determined the mare was barren and could not be bred.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fertile: able to produce offspring; capable of reproducing
  • synonym – sterile: incapable of producing offspring or fruit
