defeasible

adjective
capable of being annulled or terminated
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfiːsəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈfiːsɪbəl / (BrE)
(of a right, interest, or claim) capable of being annulled, voided, or terminated under certain conditions.
