chador

noun
cloth covering worn by some Muslim/Hindu women
Pronunciation: / tʃəˈdɔr / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːdɔː / (BrE)
A large piece of cloth worn by some Muslim and Hindu women to cover the head, and often the body, leaving only the face exposed.

Examples

wear a chador
Her mother helped her choose a new chador for the celebration.
Many women in the city wear a black chador as part of their daily attire.
She gracefully adjusted her chador before stepping outside.
