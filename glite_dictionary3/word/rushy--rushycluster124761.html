rushy

adjective
abounding in rushes (plants)
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌʃi / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃi / (BrE)
(of land or an area) covered with or abounding in rushes (grasslike plants).

Examples

rushy wetland
A dense, rushy wetland bordered the remote lake.
The cattle grazed near the rushy banks of the pond.
We walked across the rushy fields at sunset.
