aspersion

noun
act of sprinkling water in baptism (rare)
Pronunciation: / əˈspɝːʒən / (AmE), / əˈspɜːʃən / (BrE)
the act of sprinkling water, especially as part of a baptismal ceremony in some Christian traditions (rare)

Examples
