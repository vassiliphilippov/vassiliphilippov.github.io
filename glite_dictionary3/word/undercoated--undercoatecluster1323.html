undercoated

adjective
with rustproof coating on vehicle underside
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɚˈkoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈkəʊtɪd / (BrE)
(of a motor vehicle) having a coating of tar or another rustproof material applied to the underside to prevent rust or corrosion.

Examples

undercoated car underside
His new car was undercoated at the dealership before delivery.
Older vehicles are often not undercoated unless requested by the owner.
We decided to buy the undercoated truck to protect it from the harsh winter road conditions.
