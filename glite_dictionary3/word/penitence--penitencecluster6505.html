penitence

noun
remorse for past wrongdoing
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnɪtəns / (AmE), / ˈpɛnɪtəns / (BrE)
the feeling of regret or sorrow for having done something wrong; remorse for one's past actions or conduct

Examples

The thief showed no penitence, even after being convicted in court.
