bumbler

noun
an inept or clumsy person
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌmb.lər / (AmE), / ˈbʌm.blə / (BrE)
A person who frequently makes mistakes or acts awkwardly due to lack of skill or competence.

Examples

clumsy bumbler
Despite his good intentions, Tom was a real bumbler in the kitchen, dropping and burning everything.
She worried her bumbler of a coworker would ruin the project with another careless mistake.
The detective was portrayed as a lovable bumbler, always stumbling upon clues by accident.
