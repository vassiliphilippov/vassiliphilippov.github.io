becoming

adjective
fitting; appropriate; proper
Pronunciation: / bɪˈkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of behavior or appearance) appropriate or suitable; fitting or proper according to custom or propriety.

Examples

becoming behavior
He displayed becoming manners while meeting his partner's parents for the first time.
Her becoming modesty was admired by everyone at the event.
It is not becoming for a guest to arrive excessively late to a dinner party.

Related Terms

  • synonym – proper: suitable or appropriate; following rules or customs
  • synonym – seemly: conforming to accepted notions of propriety or good taste
  • antonym – unbecoming: not in keeping with standards of what is proper or suitable
