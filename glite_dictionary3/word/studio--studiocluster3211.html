studio

noun
workplace for artists or art instruction
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːdioʊ / (AmE), / ˈstjuːdiəʊ / (BrE)
a room or building where artists, dancers, musicians, or photographers practice or teach their art

Examples

artist studio space
Many photography students rent a studio to practice lighting techniques.
She teaches ballet classes every afternoon in her studio.
The artist spent hours alone in his studio preparing his latest paintings.
