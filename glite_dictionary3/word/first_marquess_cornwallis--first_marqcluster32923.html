First Marquess Cornwallis

proper noun
British commander in American War of Independence
Pronunciation: / fɜrst mɑrˈkwɛs kɔrnˈwɑlɪs / (AmE), / fɜːst mɑːˈkwɪs kɔːnˈwɒlɪs / (BrE)
