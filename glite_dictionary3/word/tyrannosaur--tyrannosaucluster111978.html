tyrannosaur

noun
large predatory Cretaceous dinosaur
Pronunciation: / tɪˈrænəˌsɔr / (AmE), / tɪˈranəsɔː / (BrE)
A large carnivorous bipedal dinosaur with massive, serrated teeth, that lived in North America during the later Cretaceous period.
