yellowcake

noun
impure uranium oxide concentrate
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊˌkeɪk / (BrE)
an impure concentrated mixture of uranium oxides produced during the processing of uranium ore, used as an intermediate in the manufacture of nuclear fuel

Examples

uranium yellowcake production
After mining uranium ore, the next step is to convert it into yellowcake.
International agencies closely monitor the movement of yellowcake to prevent nuclear proliferation.
The shipment of yellowcake was safely transported to the nuclear facility.
