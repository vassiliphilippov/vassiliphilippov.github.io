repertory

noun
theatrical company presenting a repertoire of plays
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛp.ərˌtɔr.i / (AmE), / ˈrep.ə.tri / (BrE)
a theatrical company that regularly presents plays from a set repertoire; also, a system of presenting such plays.

Examples

repertory theater group
Joining the repertory gave her the chance to perform in many classic dramas and comedies.
Our local theater operates as a repertory, staging different plays throughout the season.
The repertory enjoys a loyal audience, thanks to its rotating schedule of productions.
