ascoma

noun
fruiting body of certain fungi
Pronunciation: / æsˈkoʊmə / (AmE), / æsˈkəʊmə / (BrE)
A fruiting body of certain fungi (Ascomycetes) in which the spore-bearing layer (hymenium) is present on a broad disklike structure.

Examples

ascoma of a fungus
Scientists collected ascoma samples to study spore formation under different conditions.
The ascoma of this fungus can be seen as a small, cup-shaped structure on decaying wood.
The distinctive shape of the ascoma helps mycologists identify the species.
