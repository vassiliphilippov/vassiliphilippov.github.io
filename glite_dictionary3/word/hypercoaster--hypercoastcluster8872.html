hypercoaster

noun
large, high-speed roller coaster
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪpɚˌkoʊstɚ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪpəkəʊstə / (BrE)
a type of roller coaster that is at least 200 feet (61 meters) tall and often features high speeds and steep drops

Examples

ride a hypercoaster
After waiting in line for an hour, we finally rode the tallest hypercoaster in the country.
Many thrill-seekers travel across the world to experience every major hypercoaster ever built.
The new hypercoaster at the amusement park reaches speeds of 70 miles per hour.
