Citlaltepetl

proper noun
highest peak and volcano in Mexico
Pronunciation: / sit.lalˈte.pɛtəl / (AmE), / sit.lalˈtɛ.pɛtl / (BrE)
an extinct volcano in southern Mexico, also known as Pico de Orizaba; the highest peak in Mexico, standing at 18,695 feet (5,636 meters)
