uncorroborated

adjective
not supported by evidence
Pronunciation: / ʌn.kəˈrɑː.bəˌreɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ʌn.kəˈrɒb.əˌreɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
(of a statement or evidence) not supported or confirmed by additional information or proof.

Examples

uncorroborated report
Because the source was uncorroborated, the editor refused to publish the article.
He dismissed the report as uncorroborated gossip.
The allegation remained uncorroborated after months of investigation.
