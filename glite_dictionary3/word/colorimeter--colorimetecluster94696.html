colorimeter

noun
device or app for calibrating display colors
Pronunciation: / \u02cck\u028cl.\u0259\u02c8r\u026am.\u0259.t\u025a / (AmE), / \u02cck\u028cl.\u0259\u02c8r\u026am.\u026a.t\u0259 / (BrE)
a device or application that measures the color characteristics of displays or images (for example chromaticity and luminance) and is used to calibrate screens for accurate color reproduction.

Examples

screen colorimeter
Before editing photos, she used a colorimeter on her monitor to ensure accurate colors.
The photographer calibrated his laptop with a colorimeter before sending images to the magazine.
He installed a smartphone colorimeter app to check the shop's display brightness and color.

Related Terms

  • synonym – calibrator: a device or tool used to adjust equipment to a known standard, often for accurate measurements or display output
  • synonym – spectrophotometer: an instrument that measures the intensity of light at different wavelengths; used for more detailed color measurement in professional settings
