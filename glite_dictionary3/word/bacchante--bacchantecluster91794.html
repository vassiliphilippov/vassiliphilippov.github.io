bacchante

noun
female devotee of Bacchus (mythology)
Pronunciation: / bækˈænti, bəˈkæn.ti / (AmE), / bakˈkanteɪ, bəˈkænti / (BrE)
(in classical mythology) a female priestess or devotee of Bacchus, the Roman god of wine; a maenad.
