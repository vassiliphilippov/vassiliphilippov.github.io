nonconformism

noun
refusal to conform to orthodox beliefs
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.kənˈfɔrˌmɪz.əm /; / nɑn.kənˈfɔrˌmɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.kənˈfɔː.mɪz.əm /; / nɒn.kənˈfɔː.mɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice or principle of not following established customs, attitudes, or norms, especially in religion, culture, or society
