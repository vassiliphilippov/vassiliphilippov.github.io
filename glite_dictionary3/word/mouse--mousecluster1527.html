mouse

noun
small rodent with pointed snout and tail
Pronunciation: / maʊs / (AmE), / maʊs / (BrE)
a small rodent with a pointed snout, small ears, a slender hairless tail, and an elongated body, often found in a variety of environments

Examples

pet mouse
A mouse can squeeze through surprisingly small holes in the wall.
She bought a cage for her pet mouse and filled it with bedding.
The mouse darted under the cupboard as soon as the lights were turned on.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – rat: a larger rodent similar in appearance to a mouse
  • synonym – shrew: a small, mouse-like mammal with a long pointed nose
  • synonym – vole: a small rodent similar to a mouse but with a stouter body and shorter tail
