Adelgidae

noun
family of conifer-infesting aphids
Pronunciation: / əˈdɛl.dʒɪˌdi /; / əˈdɛl.dʒɪ.di / (AmE), / əˈdɛl.dʒɪˌdiː /; / əˈdɛl.dʒɪ.diː / (BrE)
