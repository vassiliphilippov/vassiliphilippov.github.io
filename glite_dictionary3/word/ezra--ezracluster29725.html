Ezra

proper noun
Old Testament book about Ezra's reforms
Pronunciation: / ˈɛzrə / (AmE), / ˈɛzrə / (BrE)
A book of the Old Testament describing the efforts of the priest Ezra to restore Jewish law and worship in Jerusalem after the Babylonian Exile.

Ezra contains accounts of the rebuilding of the Temple in Jerusalem.
