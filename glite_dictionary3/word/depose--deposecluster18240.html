to depose

verb
remove from office by force
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpoʊz / (AmE), / dɪˈpəʊz / (BrE)
to remove someone forcibly from office or a position of power

Examples

to depose a leader
After months of protests, the president was deposed from office.
Historians studied the factors that led to the king being deposed by his rivals.
The military sought to depose the corrupt leader and establish a new government.

  • synonym – dethrone: to remove from a throne or position of authority
  • synonym – dismiss: to remove someone from their job or position
