nonessential

adjective
not absolutely necessary
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.ɪˈsɛn.ʃəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.ɪˈsɛn.ʃəl / (BrE)
Not absolutely necessary; not of vital or central importance.

Examples

nonessential item for work
Access to the internet is nonessential for this job, but it can be helpful.
Attendance at the meeting is nonessential, so feel free to skip it if you are busy.
Many nonessential expenses were cut during the company's restructuring.
