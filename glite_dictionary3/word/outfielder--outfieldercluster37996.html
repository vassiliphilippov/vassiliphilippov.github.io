outfielder

noun
baseball player in the outfield
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌfiːldɚ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌfiːldə / (BrE)
(Baseball) a player positioned in the outfield, responsible for fielding balls hit beyond the infield.

Examples

baseball outfielder
During practice, the coach focused on improving the outfielders' throwing accuracy.
She has played as an outfielder since joining the baseball team.
