Victoria

proper noun
Queen of Great Britain and Ireland, 1837–1901
Pronunciation: / vɪkˈtɔriə / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːriə / (BrE)
Queen of Great Britain and Ireland and Empress of India from 1837 to 1901; the last Hanoverian monarch of England.
