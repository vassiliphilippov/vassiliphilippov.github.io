bilingualism

noun
ability to speak two languages
Pronunciation: / baɪˈlɪŋɡwəlɪzəm / (AmE), / baɪˈlɪŋɡwɪlɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
The ability to speak and understand two languages fluently.

Examples

childhood bilingualism

Research has shown that bilingualism can benefit cognitive development.
