tolerantly

adverb
in a tolerant manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːlərəntli / (AmE), / ˈtɒlərəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows willingness to accept behavior or opinions that are different from your own.

Examples

listen tolerantly to others
Despite the criticism, he responded tolerantly and explained his point of view calmly.
She listened tolerantly to the children arguing over the smallest details.
They behave tolerantly towards people with different beliefs.

Related Terms

  • antonym – intolerantly: in an intolerant manner
  • synonym – tolerant: willing to accept different opinions or behaviors
