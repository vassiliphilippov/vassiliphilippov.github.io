apotheosis

noun
the perfect example; pinnacle of perfection
Pronunciation: / əˌpoʊθiˈoʊsɪs /; / əˌpɑːθiˈoʊsɪs / (AmE), / əˌpɒθiˈəʊsɪs /; / əˌpɒθɪˈəʊsɪs / (BrE)
the perfect or highest example of something; the highest point or culmination; (historically) the elevation of someone to divine status
