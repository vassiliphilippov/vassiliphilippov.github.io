cultivation

noun
preparing land for growing crops
Pronunciation: / ˌkʌl.tɪˈveɪ.ʃən /; / ˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkʌl.tɪˈveɪ.ʃən /; / ˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
