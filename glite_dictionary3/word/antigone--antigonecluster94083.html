Antigone

proper noun
daughter of Oedipus in Greek mythology
Pronunciation: / ænˈtɪɡəni / (AmE), / ænˈtɪɡəni / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, the daughter of King Oedipus who defied authority by burying her brother and was ultimately condemned to death.
