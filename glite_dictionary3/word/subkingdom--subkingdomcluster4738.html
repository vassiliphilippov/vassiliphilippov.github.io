subkingdom

noun
taxonomic rank below kingdom
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈkɪŋdəm / (AmE), / sʌbˈkɪŋdəm / (BrE)
(biology) a taxonomic group ranking below kingdom and above phylum in biological classification.

Examples

biological subkingdom
In biology class, we learned how each subkingdom contains various phyla.
Plants and animals belong to different subkingdoms within their respective kingdoms.
The classification was revised to include a new subkingdom for certain microorganisms.
