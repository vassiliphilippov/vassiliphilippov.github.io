to paraphrase

verb
express in different words
Pronunciation: / ˈpærəˌfreɪz / (AmE), / ˈpærəfreɪz / (BrE)
to express the same meaning of something spoken or written using different words, especially to make it clearer

Examples

to paraphrase a sentence
Can you paraphrase this sentence to make it easier to understand?
She paraphrased the professor's comments during the discussion.
When writing essays, students often need to paraphrase information from sources.

Related Terms

  • antonym – quote: to repeat or copy out a phrase or passage exactly
  • synonym – rephrase: to say or write something in different words
