tensile

adjective
capable of being stretched or drawn out
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛn.saɪl / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.saɪl /; / ˈtɛn.sʌɪl / (BrE)
able to be stretched or relating to tension (especially describing materials that can be stretched without breaking)
