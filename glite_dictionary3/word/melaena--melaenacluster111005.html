melaena

noun
dark, bloody feces (medical)
Pronunciation: / məˈliːnə / (AmE), / məˈliːnə / (BrE)
Abnormally dark, tarry feces containing blood, typically resulting from bleeding in the gastrointestinal tract.

Examples

melaena in stool
An episode of melaena may indicate a serious medical condition requiring prompt attention.
The doctor suspected gastrointestinal bleeding when the patient presented with melaena.
The nurse noted that the patient's melaena had a distinct, foul odor and sticky texture.
