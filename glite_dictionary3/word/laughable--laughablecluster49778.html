laughable

adjective
provoking laughter; absurd or funny
Pronunciation: / ˈlæfəbəl / (AmE), / ˈlɑːfəbəl /; / ˈlɑːfəbl̩ / (BrE)
so absurd, funny, or unreasonable that it causes laughter or deserves ridicule; ridiculous
