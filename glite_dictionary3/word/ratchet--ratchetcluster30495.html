ratchet

noun
mechanical device allowing one-way motion
Pronunciation: / ˈrætʃɪt / (AmE), / ˈrætʃɪt /; / ˈratʃɪt / (BrE)
a mechanical device with a toothed wheel or bar and a pawl that allows movement in only one direction
