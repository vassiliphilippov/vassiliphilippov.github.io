illumination

noun
amount of light; visibility
Pronunciation: / ɪˌluːməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌljuːmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the amount of light in a place or the light that falls on a surface

Examples

street illumination lighting
Nighttime illumination on the street makes pedestrians feel safer.
The illumination in the room was too dim for reading.
The sudden illumination from the headlights revealed the deer in the road.
Proper illumination is crucial in photography to achieve a clear image.
The laboratory was designed to provide uniform illumination for all workstations.
The technician measured the illumination levels in each corner of the room.
