slam-bang

adjective
violent, sudden, and noisy
Pronunciation: / ˈslæmˌbæŋ / (AmE), / ˈslæmˌbæŋ / (BrE)
Violent, sudden, and noisy; characterized by forceful or energetic action, often accompanied by loud sounds.

Examples

slam-bang action scenes
The movie was filled with slam-bang action sequences that kept the audience on the edge of their seats.
The novel opens with a slam-bang car chase through the crowded city streets.
Their project was completed in a slam-bang rush just minutes before the deadline.
