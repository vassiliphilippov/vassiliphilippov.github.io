Spirula peronii

noun
a small tropical cephalopod with a spiral shell
Pronunciation: / spɪˈruːlə pɛˈroʊni / (AmE), / spɪˈruːlə pɛˈrəʊniː / (BrE)
A small species of tropical cephalopod known for its prominent eyes, short arms, and a many-chambered shell coiled in a flat spiral.
