corridor

noun
enclosed passageway in a building
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːr.ə.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒr.ɪ.dɔː / (BrE)
a long, narrow enclosed passage in a building, with doors leading to rooms on either side or at the ends

Examples

school corridor
She walked down the corridor, glancing into each classroom as she passed.
The hotel corridor was lined with paintings and elegant lights.
Voices echoed in the empty corridor late at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hallway: an interior passage in a building that leads to rooms
  • synonym – passage: a narrow way through something, especially inside a building
