uneager

adjective
not eager or enthusiastic
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈiɡər / (AmE), / ʌnˈiːɡə / (BrE)
not showing eagerness, enthusiasm, or interest; lacking keenness or willingness.

Examples

uneager response
His uneager handshake suggested he was not interested in the proposal.
She gave an uneager response to the job offer, clearly hesitant about accepting.
The students looked uneager to start the exam on a Monday morning.
