isomerization

noun
conversion of a compound into an isomer
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪ.sə.mə.rəˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.sə.mə.rɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process by which a compound is converted into an isomer with a different arrangement of atoms but the same molecular formula
