to clap

verb
strike with flat of hand in greeting or encouragement
Pronunciation: / klæp / (AmE), / klap / (BrE)
to strike someone or something with the flat of the hand, usually in a friendly, encouraging, or greeting manner

Examples

to clap someone on shoulder
He clapped his friend warmly on the arm when they met at the station.
She clapped him on the back as a sign of congratulations.
The coach clapped her hand on my shoulder before the big game.
