harp

noun
large stringed musical instrument
Pronunciation: / hɑrp / (AmE), / hɑːp / (BrE)
a large musical instrument with a triangular frame and strings that are plucked with the fingers, producing a gentle, resonant sound

Examples

play the harp
Learning to play the harp requires patience and dedication.
She played the harp beautifully during the wedding ceremony.
The orchestra featured a soloist on the harp for the concerto.
