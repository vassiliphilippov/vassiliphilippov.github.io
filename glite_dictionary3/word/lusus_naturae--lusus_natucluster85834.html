lusus naturae

noun
a freak or anomaly of nature
Pronunciation: / ˈluːsəs nəˈtʃʊəri, nəˈtʃʊri / (AmE), / ˈluːsʊs næˈtjʊəriː / (BrE)
a person or animal that is markedly unusual or deformed, as if a 'freak of nature'

Examples

In Victorian times, people with rare conditions were often labeled a lusus naturae.
