papilliform

adjective
shaped like a papilla
Pronunciation: / pəˈpɪlɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / pəˈpɪlɪfɔːm / (BrE)
Having the shape of a papilla; shaped like a small, rounded projection or nipple.

Examples

papilliform growths
Certain types of tumors can exhibit papilliform structures.
The scientist noted the papilliform projections on the tissue sample.
The surface of the leaf was described as papilliform under the microscope.
