Athyrium thelypteroides

noun
a fern species with silvery outgrowths
Pronunciation: / əˈθɪriəm ˌθɛlɪptəˈrɔɪdiːz / (AmE), / əˈθɪriəm ˌθɛlɪptəˈrɔɪdiːz / (BrE)
