opposition

noun
the relation between opposing things
Pronunciation: / ˌɑː.pəˈzɪʃ.ən /; / ˌɑːpəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɒp.əˈzɪʃ.ən /; / ˌɒpəˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or state of opposing, resisting, or being in conflict with someone or something
