laxness

noun
lack of strictness or care
Pronunciation: / ˈlæks.nəs / (AmE), / ˈlæks.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being lacking in strictness, care, or attention; negligence or slackness.

Examples

laxness in discipline
His laxness in meeting deadlines caused frequent problems at work.
The laxness of the security measures made the museum vulnerable to theft.
The judge criticized the laxness of the company's safety protocols.

Related Terms

  • synonym – negligence: failure to take proper care, neglect
  • antonym – strictness: quality of being strict or exacting
