blend

noun
word formed from parts of others
Pronunciation: / blɛnd / (AmE), / blɛnd / (BrE)
A word formed by merging parts of two or more other words and combining their meanings, such as 'brunch' from 'breakfast' and 'lunch'.

Examples

blend word example
'Motel' is an example of a blend combining 'motor' and 'hotel'.
Linguists study how blends are created and used in daily language.
The word 'smog' is a blend of 'smoke' and 'fog'.
