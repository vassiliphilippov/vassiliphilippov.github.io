comic

noun
stand-up comedian or performer of jokes
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.mɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ɪk / (BrE)
a professional performer whose job is to tell jokes and entertain people, often by performing comical acts

Examples

stand-up comic
Last night, a famous comic performed at the local comedy club.
Many comics rely on personal experiences as material for their acts.
The comic entertained the audience with a hilarious routine about daily life.
