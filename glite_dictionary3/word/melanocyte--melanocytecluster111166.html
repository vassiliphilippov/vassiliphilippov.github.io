melanocyte

noun
skin cell producing melanin
Pronunciation: / məˈlænəˌsaɪt / (AmE), / mɪˈlanəˌsaɪt / (BrE)
a cell in the skin's basal layer that produces the pigment melanin, primarily responsible for skin color
