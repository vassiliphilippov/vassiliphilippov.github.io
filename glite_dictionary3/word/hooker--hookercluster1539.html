hooker

noun
golfer whose shots curve left
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊkər / (AmE), / ˈhʊkə / (BrE)
A golfer whose shots typically curve sharply to the left (for right-handed golfers).

Examples

lefty hooker golfer
As a hooker, he often found himself in trouble on the left side of the fairway.
Even professional golfers can become a hooker if they lose control of their grip.
His coach helped him correct the swing that made him a notorious hooker among his friends.
