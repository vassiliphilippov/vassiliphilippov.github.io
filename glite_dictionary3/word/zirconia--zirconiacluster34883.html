zirconia

noun
white oxide of zirconium used in ceramics and industry
Pronunciation: / zɚˈkoʊniə / (AmE), / zɜːˈkəʊniə / (BrE)
a white crystalline oxide of zirconium, used in making ceramics, refractories, insulation, abrasives, enamels, and glazes

Examples

Cubic zirconia is widely used as a diamond substitute in jewelry.
