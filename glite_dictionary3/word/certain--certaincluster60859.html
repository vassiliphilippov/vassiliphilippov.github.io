certain

adjective
sure to happen; inevitable
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːtən /; / ˈsɝːtn̩ /; / ˈsɜːrtən / (AmE), / ˈsɜːtən /; / ˈsɜːtn̩ /; / ˈsɜːtn / (BrE)
sure or established beyond doubt; inevitable or bound to happen

Examples
