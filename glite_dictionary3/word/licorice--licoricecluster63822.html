licorice

noun
a chewy black candy
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkərɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈlɪkərɪs / (BrE)
A chewy, black candy flavored with the extract from the root of the licorice plant.

Examples

chewy licorice candy
He offered her a piece of licorice on the train ride.
Many people dislike the distinctive flavor of black licorice.
My grandmother always kept a bag of licorice in her kitchen for a sweet treat.
