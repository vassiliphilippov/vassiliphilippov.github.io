pleximeter

noun
instrument used in medical percussion
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛksɪˌmitɚ / (AmE), / ˈplɛksɪˌmiːtə / (BrE)
a small, thin metal or ivory plate placed against the body and struck with a plexor during medical percussion to aid diagnosis

Examples

pleximeter in medical exam
A pleximeter is an essential tool for physicians practicing classical percussion examination.
Older models of the pleximeter were often made of ivory rather than metal.
The doctor placed a pleximeter on the patient's chest before tapping it with a plexor.
