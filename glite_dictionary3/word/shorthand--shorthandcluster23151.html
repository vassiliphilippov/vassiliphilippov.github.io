shorthand

adjective
written in abbreviated, symbolic form
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔːrtˌhænd / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːthænd / (BrE)
written or expressed in an abbreviated or symbolic form; using a system of rapid writing or symbols to represent words and phrases quickly.

Examples

shorthand notes style
Many journalists use shorthand systems to keep up with fast-paced discussions.
She took shorthand notes during the meeting for efficiency.
The court reporter's shorthand transcript was later typed up in full.
