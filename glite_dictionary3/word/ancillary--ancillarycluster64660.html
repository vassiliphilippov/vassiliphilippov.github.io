ancillary

adjective
providing additional support; supplementary
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.səˌleri / (AmE), / ˈæn.sɪ.ləri / (BrE)
providing additional help or support to the main activities or operation of something; supplementary.

Examples

ancillary services support
Doctors often rely on ancillary services such as lab tests and x-rays to make diagnoses.
The company hired additional ancillary staff to support its growing operations.
The main exhibit was impressive, but several ancillary displays provided useful background information.
