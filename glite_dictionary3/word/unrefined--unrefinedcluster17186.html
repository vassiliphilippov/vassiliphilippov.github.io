unrefined

adjective
lacking manners or sophistication
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈfaɪnd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈfaɪnd / (BrE)
(of a person or their behavior) lacking refinement, good manners, or sophistication; uncouth.

Examples

unrefined table manners
Despite his wealth, his manners remained unrefined and abrasive.
Her unrefined comments made others uncomfortable at the formal dinner.
The movie portrays the hero as an unrefined but good-hearted man.

Related Terms

  • antonym – refined: having or showing good taste, manners, or sophistication
  • synonym – uncouth: lacking good manners or refinement
