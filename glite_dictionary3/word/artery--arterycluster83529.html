artery

noun
major route or thoroughfare
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrtəri / (AmE), / ˈɑːtəri / (BrE)
A main road, route, or channel that carries significant traffic, serving as a principal means of transportation or communication.

Examples

main artery road
After the accident, the artery was closed, causing major traffic delays.
Riverside Drive serves as an important artery for daily commuters.
This road is the main artery connecting the city center to the airport.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bypass: a road passing around a town or its center to provide an alternative route
  • synonym – highway: a main road, especially one connecting major towns or cities
