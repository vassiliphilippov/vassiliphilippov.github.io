cadre

noun
core group in a larger movement or organization
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.dɚ /; / ˈkɑːˌdreɪ / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.drə /; / ˈkɑːdri / (BrE)
a small group of trained people who form the core of a larger organization, especially in political or military contexts
