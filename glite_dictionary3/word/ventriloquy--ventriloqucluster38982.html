ventriloquy

noun
art of projecting one's voice to seem from elsewhere
Pronunciation: / vɛnˈtrɪləkwi / (AmE), / vɛnˈtrɪləkwi / (BrE)
the art or practice of speaking so that the voice appears to come from another source, especially as a form of entertainment

Examples

ventriloquy on stage
He mastered the skill of ventriloquy while performing at local theaters.
Her talent for ventriloquy amazed both children and adults alike.
Successful ventriloquy requires precise control of one’s vocal cords and mouth movements.
