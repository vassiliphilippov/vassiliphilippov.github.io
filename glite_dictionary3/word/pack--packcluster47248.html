to pack

verb
fill to capacity
Pronunciation: / pæk / (AmE), / pæk / (BrE)
to fill a place so that there is no room for more people or things

Examples

to pack the stadium
Fans began to pack the stadium hours before the game started.
Spectators packed into the court to hear the verdict.
The small restaurant was packed on Friday night, with no tables left.
