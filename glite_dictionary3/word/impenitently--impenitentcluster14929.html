impenitently

adverb
without regret or remorse
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɛnətəntli / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɛnɪtəntli / (BrE)
In a manner showing no regret, remorse, or repentance for one's actions or behavior.

Examples

impenitently refuse apology
He impenitently defended his controversial statements, ignoring criticism from others.
She impenitently refused to apologize after the argument.
The official impenitently repeated his earlier remarks during the press conference.
