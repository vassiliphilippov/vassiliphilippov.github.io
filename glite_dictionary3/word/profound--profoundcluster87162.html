profound

adjective
very deep and undisturbed (of sleep)
Pronunciation: / prəˈfaʊnd / (AmE), / prəˈfaʊnd / (BrE)
(of sleep) very deep and undisturbed; complete and intense.

Examples

profound sleep
After three sleepless nights, he finally enjoyed several hours of profound rest.
Despite the noise outside, she fell into a profound sleep within minutes.
The medication left her in such a profound state of sleep that she did not wake until noon.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deep: extending far down from the top or surface; (of sleep) very thorough and uninterrupted
