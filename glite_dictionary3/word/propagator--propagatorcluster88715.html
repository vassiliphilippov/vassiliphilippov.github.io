propagator

noun
person who propagates plants
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːpəˌɡeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈprɒpəˌɡeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who propagates plants, especially by encouraging their growth and reproduction, often under controlled conditions such as in a greenhouse.

Examples

propagator of plants
As a skilled propagator, she developed new varieties of ornamental plants for the nursery.
Many garden centers employ at least one propagator to manage their plant production.
The experienced propagator carefully tended the young seedlings in the greenhouse.

Related Terms

  • synonym – horticulturist: a person who grows and studies garden plants
