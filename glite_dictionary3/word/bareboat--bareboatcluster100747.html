bareboat

noun
vessel chartered without crew or provisions
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈbeə.bəʊt / (BrE)
a vessel, especially a yacht, that is available for charter without a captain, crew, or provisions included

Examples

bareboat charter rental
A bareboat charter requires the renter to have sufficient sailing experience.
Unlike a crewed yacht, a bareboat comes without staff or provisions.
We decided to charter a bareboat for our sailing trip, so we could captain it ourselves.
