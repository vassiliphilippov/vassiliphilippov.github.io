Komi

noun
Finno-Ugric ethnic group of Russia
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊ.mi / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.mi / (BrE)
A member of an indigenous Finno-Ugric people living in the northwestern Urals region of Russia.
