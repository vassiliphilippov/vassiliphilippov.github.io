Fyodor Dostoyevsky

proper noun
Russian novelist known for psychological insight
Pronunciation: / ˈfjɔdər ˌdɔstoʊˈjɛfski / (AmE), / ˈfjɔːdɔː ˌdɒstɔɪˈjɛfski / (BrE)
