empyrean

noun
the highest heaven; celestial sphere
Pronunciation: / ˌɛm.pɪˈri.ən / (AmE), / ˌɛm.pɪˈriː.ən / (BrE)
the highest part of heaven or the sky, especially conceived as a sphere or vault in which celestial bodies move or are fixed

Examples

the empyrean sky
Legends often speak of gods residing in the empyrean far beyond mortal reach.
Stargazers contemplated the vastness of the empyrean on a clear night.
The poet gazed upward, imagining angels dwelling in the empyrean above.
