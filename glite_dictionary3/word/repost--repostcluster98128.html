repost

noun
content posted again on social media
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈpoʊst / (AmE), / ˌriːˈpəʊst / (BrE)
A message, image, or other content that has been published again on social media after being previously posted.

Examples

repost of a meme
He was annoyed when he saw the meme was just a repost from last year.
Many accounts on Instagram fill their feeds with reposts from other users.
She shared a repost of the video because she thought it was important.

Related Terms

  • synonym – post: an item published on social media or an online platform
