epididymis

noun
tube storing and transporting sperm in testes
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈdɪdəmɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈdɪdəmɪs / (BrE)
A highly coiled tube situated on the back of each testis that stores and transports sperm from the testes to the vas deferens.

Examples

the epididymis stores sperm
Damage to the epididymis can affect a man's fertility.
Doctors examined the patient's epididymis for signs of infection after he reported pain in his groin.
The epididymis is responsible for storing and maturing sperm cells produced in the testis.
