unaccountable

adjective
impossible to explain
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈkaʊntəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈkaʊntəbl̩ / (BrE)
Impossible to explain or understand; not able to be accounted for.

Examples

unaccountable events
For some unaccountable reason, the radio stopped working overnight.
She felt an unaccountable sense of sadness that she couldn't describe.
There was an unaccountable delay in the delivery of our package.

Related Terms

  • antonym – accountable: required or expected to justify actions or decisions; responsible
  • synonym – inexplicable: impossible to explain or understand
