impotence

noun
inability to copulate (in men)
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmpətəns / (AmE), / ˈɪmpətəns / (BrE)
The inability, especially in men, to achieve or maintain an erection or to copulate.

Examples

male impotence
Impotence can have psychological as well as physical causes.
Medical treatments have been developed for men suffering from impotence.
The stigma associated with impotence can make it difficult for men to seek help.

Related Terms

  • synonym – erectile dysfunction: difficulty achieving or maintaining an erection
  • antonym – fertility: ability to reproduce or conceive offspring
