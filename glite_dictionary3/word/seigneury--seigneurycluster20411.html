seigneury

noun
authority or domain of a feudal lord
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪn.jə.ri / (AmE), / ˈseɪn.jʊəri /; / ˈseɪn.jəri / (BrE)
the land, estate, or domain and the authority of a feudal lord, especially in the feudal system of France or French Canada
