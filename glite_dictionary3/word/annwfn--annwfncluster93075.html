Annwfn

proper noun
Welsh mythical otherworld
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.uːv.n̩ / (AmE), / ˈan.ʊvn / (BrE)
In Welsh mythology, the otherworld; a paradisiacal land inhabited by fairies or supernatural beings.

Examples

journey to Annwfn
In medieval Welsh tales, heroes often journey to Annwfn in search of adventure or wisdom.
Some stories equate Annwfn with other Celtic otherworlds such as Avalon.
The legends describe Annwfn as a realm of abundance, beauty, and eternal youth.
