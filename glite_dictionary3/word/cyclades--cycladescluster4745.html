Cyclades

proper noun
group of Greek islands in the southern Aegean
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪkləˌdiːz / (AmE), / ˈsɪkləˌdiːz / (BrE)
a group of over 200 islands in the southern Aegean Sea, southeast of mainland Greece, known for their historical and scenic significance

the Cyclades islands
