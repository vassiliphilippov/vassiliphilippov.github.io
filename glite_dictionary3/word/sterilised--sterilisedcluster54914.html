sterilised

adjective
made infertile by procedure
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛrəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈstɛr.ɪ.laɪzd / (BrE)
Made infertile by surgical or chemical means; prevented from reproducing.

Examples

sterilised cat after surgery
After the surgery, the cat was sterilised to prevent unwanted litters.
Some plants have sterilised seeds that cannot germinate.
The government provided free clinics where stray dogs were sterilised.
