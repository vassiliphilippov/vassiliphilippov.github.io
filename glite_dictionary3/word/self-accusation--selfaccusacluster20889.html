self-accusation

noun
the act of blaming oneself
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf ˌækjəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ˌækjʊˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)

Examples

The therapist encouraged him to replace self-accusation with self-compassion.
