carboniferous

adjective
containing or yielding carbon
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈbɑnəˌfɛrəs / (AmE), / kɑːˈbɒnɪfərəs / (BrE)
relating to or containing carbon, or capable of producing or yielding carbon, especially as in coal-forming processes.

Examples

carboniferous material sample
Scientists analyzed carboniferous rocks to study ancient life forms.
The carboniferous content of the material makes it valuable for energy production.
The region is carboniferous and known for its rich coal deposits.
