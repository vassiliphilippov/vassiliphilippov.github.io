ecumenism

noun
movement for unity among Christian churches
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkjuːməˌnɪzəm /; / ɪˈkjuməˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / iːˈkjuːmənɪz(ə)m /; / ɪˈkjuːmənɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
the principle or aim of promoting unity and cooperation among Christian churches or, more generally, among different religious groups
