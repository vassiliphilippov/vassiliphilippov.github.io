compass

noun
navigational direction-finding instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmpəs / (AmE), / ˈkʌmpəs / (BrE)
an instrument for finding direction, typically consisting of a magnetic needle that points north

Examples

magnetic compass

Sailors have used a compass to navigate the seas for centuries.
