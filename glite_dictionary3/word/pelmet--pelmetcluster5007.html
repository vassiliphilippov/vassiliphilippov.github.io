pelmet

noun
decorative board concealing curtain rails
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛlmət / (AmE), / ˈpɛlmɪt / (BrE)
a narrow decorative board or framework fitted across the top of a window to conceal the curtain fittings

Examples

pelmet above window
Cleaning the pelmet was part of her routine spring cleaning every year.
She chose a simple pelmet to hide the curtain rod and complement the drapes.
The elegant pelmet above the window matched the room's classic décor.
