glacial

adjective
extremely cold or unfriendly (of manner)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡleɪ.ʃəl / (AmE), / ˈɡleɪ.si.əl / (BrE)
(of a person's manner or expression) extremely cold, unfriendly, or showing disdain.

Examples

glacial attitude

She gave him a glacial look that ended the conversation immediately.
The board greeted the proposal with a glacial silence, making their disapproval clear.
