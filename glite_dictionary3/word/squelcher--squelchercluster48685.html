squelcher

noun
crushing or silencing remark
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɛltʃər / (AmE), / ˈskwɛltʃə / (BrE)
a remark or response that completely silences or overwhelms someone, especially by being sharp or conclusive

Examples

a sharp squelcher
He delivered a squelcher that left his opponent speechless during the debate.
Her comeback was such a squelcher that nobody dared to reply.
Sometimes a well-timed squelcher is more effective than a lengthy argument.

Related Terms

  • synonym – put-down: an insulting or dismissive remark
  • synonym – retort: a sharp, quick, or witty reply
