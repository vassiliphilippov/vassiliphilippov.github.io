Saint Irenaeus

proper noun
early Christian theologian and bishop of Lyons
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ɪrəˈneɪəs / (AmE), / seɪnt ɪrəˈneɪəs / (BrE)
A Greek theologian and bishop of Lyons, noted for his antiheretical writings and recognized as a saint and Doctor of the Church (circa 130–200).
