anniversary

noun
the yearly date of a past event
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˈvɝːsəri / (AmE), / ˌænɪˈvɜːsəri / (BrE)
the date each year on which an event took place in a previous year, or the celebration of this date

Examples

anniversary celebration
Our company marks its founding anniversary every October.
They celebrated their twentieth anniversary with a trip to Paris.
Tomorrow is the anniversary of their first meeting.
