monochromasy

noun
complete color blindness
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑː.nəˈkroʊ.mə.si / (AmE), / ˌmɒn.əˈkrəʊ.mə.si / (BrE)
A condition of complete color blindness in which colors can only be distinguished based on brightness, not hue.
