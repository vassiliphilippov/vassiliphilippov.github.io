plump

noun
sound of a heavy fall
Pronunciation: / plʌmp / (AmE), / plʌmp / (BrE)
A sound produced by something falling heavily or suddenly.

Examples

a loud plump
She heard a plump from the next room and rushed to investigate.
The apple hit the ground with a soft plump.
There was a dull plump as the book landed on the carpet.
