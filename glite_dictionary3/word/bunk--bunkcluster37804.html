to bunk

verb
evade paying, especially fare
Pronunciation: / bʌŋk / (AmE), / bʌŋk / (BrE)
to avoid paying, especially by sneaking past a payment checkpoint, such as on public transport; to evade fare.

Examples

to bunk the train
He was fined after being caught trying to bunk the train.
She decided to bunk the subway fare when she realized she was running late.
Some students regularly bunk the bus without getting caught by ticket inspectors.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dodge: to avoid (something) by moving quickly or cleverly
  • antonym – pay: to give money for goods or services
