smoking

noun
the act of inhaling smoke from tobacco, etc.
Pronunciation: / ˈsmoʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of inhaling and exhaling the smoke of tobacco or other substances

Examples

smoking a cigarette
Smoking inside public buildings is prohibited by law.
He decided to quit smoking for his health.
Many people are concerned about the effects of secondhand smoking on children.

Related Terms

  • antonym – abstention: the act of refraining from something, esp. smoking or drinking
  • synonym – smoke: the act of inhaling smoke from burning substances
