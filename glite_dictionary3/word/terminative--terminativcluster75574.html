terminative

adjective
serving to mark an ending
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrmɪnəˌtɪv / (AmE), / tɜːˈmɪnətɪv / (BrE)
marking or relating to an end; bringing something to a conclusion or termination

Examples

terminative clause
Many legal agreements include a terminative clause that specifies their end.
The contract was terminative with the completion of the project.
The medication was given a terminative stop date to prevent long-term side effects.
