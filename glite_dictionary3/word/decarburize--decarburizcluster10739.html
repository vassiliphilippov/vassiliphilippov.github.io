to decarburize

verb
remove carbon from a metal or engine
Pronunciation: / diˈkɑrbjəˌraɪz / (AmE), / diˈkɑːbjəraɪz / (BrE)
to remove carbon from a metal or engine, especially as part of a chemical or cleaning process

Examples

to decarburize steel parts
Before reassembling the machinery, ensure you have fully decarburized all affected parts.
Technicians need to decarburize the engine periodically for optimal performance.
The process to decarburize steel reduces its hardness and makes it more ductile.
