abstractionism

noun
art based on non-representational forms
Pronunciation: / əbˈstrækʃənˌɪzəm / (AmE), / əbˈstrækʃənɪzəm /; / əbˈstrakʃənɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
an art style or method that represents ideas or concepts without showing real objects, focusing on form, color, and composition instead of realistic images
