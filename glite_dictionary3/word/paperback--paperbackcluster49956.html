paperback

noun
book with a paper cover
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpərˌbæk /; / ˈpeɪ.pɚˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəbæk /; / ˈpeɪ.pəˌbæk / (BrE)
a book with a flexible paper cover, not a hard cover

Examples

buy a paperback novel
A new paperback version of the classic was released last month.
Most students bought the paperback edition because it was more affordable.
She picked up a paperback novel to read on the train.
I prefer a paperback to a hardcover when traveling because it fits better in my bag.
She loves reading paperbacks because they are lightweight and easy to carry around.
The latest bestseller is now available in paperback at all major bookstores.
