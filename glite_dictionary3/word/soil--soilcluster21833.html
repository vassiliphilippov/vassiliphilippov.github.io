soil

noun
stain or impurity
Pronunciation: / sɔɪl / (AmE), / sɔɪl / (BrE)
a stain or taint that makes something impure, dirty, or unclean

Examples

soil of suspicion
No soil of suspicion ever attached itself to her honesty.
She felt that any lie would bring a soil to her good name.
The scandal left a soil on his otherwise unblemished reputation.
