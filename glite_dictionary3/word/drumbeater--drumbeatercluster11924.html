drumbeater

noun
enthusiastic or militant promoter
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌmˌbiːtər / (AmE), / ˈdrʌmˌbiːtə / (BrE)
A person who actively and enthusiastically promotes or supports a cause, idea, or campaign, sometimes in an aggressive manner.

Examples

political drumbeater
Many drumbeaters for the new policy attended the city council meeting.
She became a passionate drumbeater for better healthcare in her community.
The senator has always been a prominent drumbeater for environmental reform.

  • antonym – opponent: someone who opposes or resists a cause or proposal
