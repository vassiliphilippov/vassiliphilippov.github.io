white

adjective
of summer nights when sun barely sets
Pronunciation: / waɪt / (AmE), / waɪt / (BrE)
(of summer nights in northern latitudes) characterized by a sky that stays bright because the sun barely sets.

Examples

white nights
During June in St. Petersburg, you can experience the famous white nights with almost no darkness.
Reading under the soft light of a white night feels magical up north.
The phenomenon of white nights attracts tourists to northern cities every summer.
