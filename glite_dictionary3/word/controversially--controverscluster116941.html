controversially

adverb
in a way that causes controversy
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːntrəˈvɜːrʃəli / (AmE), / ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃəli / (BrE)
In a way that gives rise to public disagreement or is likely to cause controversy.

Examples

to act controversially
He acted controversially when he overturned the popular decision of the board.
The film's ending was controversially interpreted by critics and audiences alike.
The politician spoke controversially about a sensitive social issue on live television.
