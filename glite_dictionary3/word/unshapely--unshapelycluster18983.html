unshapely

adjective
not well-proportioned or pleasing in shape
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃeɪpli / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃeɪpli / (BrE)
not having a well-proportioned or pleasing form or shape; unattractive in appearance due to poor outline or proportions

Examples

unshapely figure
After the accident, the once beautiful vase became unshapely and lost its appeal.
He wore a heavy coat that made his figure appear bulky and unshapely.
The statue looked oddly unshapely when viewed from the side.

Related Terms

  • synonym – misshapen: not having the normal or natural shape
  • antonym – shapely: having an attractive and well-proportioned shape
