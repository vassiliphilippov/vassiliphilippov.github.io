to globalize

verb
make worldwide in scope or operation
Pronunciation: / ˈɡloʊbəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊbəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something affect or involve the whole world; to cause to operate at an international scale

Examples

to globalize a business
Efforts to globalize education have led to more international student exchanges.
Many companies are seeking to globalize their operations in order to reach new markets.
The internet has helped to rapidly globalize information access around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym – internationalize: to make something international in character or operation
  • antonym – localize: to restrict something to a particular place
