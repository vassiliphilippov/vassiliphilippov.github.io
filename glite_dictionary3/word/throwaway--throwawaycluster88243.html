throwaway

noun
abandoned homeless boy (sometimes offensive)
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊəˌweɪ / (BrE)
a term, sometimes considered offensive, for a homeless boy who has been abandoned and lives on the streets

Examples

a throwaway child
Many organizations are dedicated to supporting throwaways and preventing youth homelessness.
Social workers try to help throwaway children find shelter and support.
The novel tells the story of a throwaway trying to survive in the city.
