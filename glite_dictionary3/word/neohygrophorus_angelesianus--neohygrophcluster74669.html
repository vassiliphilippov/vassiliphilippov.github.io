Neohygrophorus angelesianus

noun
a species of fungus found in western North America
Pronunciation: / ˌniː.oʊ.haɪˌɡrɑːˈfɔːrəs ˌæn.dʒəˌliːˈzeɪ.ən.əs / (AmE), / ˌniː.əʊ.haɪˌɡrɒˈfɔː.rəs ˌæn.dʒəˌliːˈzeɪ.ən.əs / (BrE)
A species of fungus characterized by a small brown convex cap with a depressed center, waxy wine-colored gills, and a brown stalk; typically found fruiting in or near melting snow banks in the western mountains of North America.
