uppishness

noun
arrogant or snobbish behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈʌpɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of behaving in a snobbish, arrogant, or self-important way, especially above one's social position or status

Examples

uppishness at work
He mistook her confidence for uppishness, but she was simply self-assured.
Her sudden uppishness after the promotion made her colleagues uncomfortable.
There's no room for uppishness in our team, as everyone is treated equally.

Related Terms

  • synonym – arrogance: exaggerated sense of one's own importance; conceit
  • antonym – modesty: the quality of not being proud or not believing you are important
