roan

noun
horse with brownish, white-mixed coat
Pronunciation: / roʊn / (AmE), / rəʊn / (BrE)
a horse with a brownish coat thickly interspersed with white or gray hairs, giving a speckled appearance

Examples

roan riding horse
Judges admired the roan for its distinctive and attractive coloring.
She rode her roan into the ring, its speckled coat shimmering under the sunlight.
The rancher owned a beautiful roan that stood out from the rest of the herd.
