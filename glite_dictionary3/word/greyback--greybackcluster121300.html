greyback

noun
Confederate soldier (historical US)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪˌbak / (BrE)
(historical, US) A nickname for a Confederate soldier during the American Civil War, derived from their grey uniforms.
