to range

verb
to extend or spread over an area
Pronunciation: / reɪndʒ / (AmE), / reɪndʒ / (BrE)
to extend, spread, or vary over an area, region, or between limits

Examples

range from A to B
The company's influence ranges across several continents.
The mountain lions range from the foothills to the upper valleys.
The network of roads ranges throughout the city, connecting distant neighborhoods.
Hotel prices in the city range from $80 to $300 per night, depending on the season.
Temperatures in this region range between 10°C and 25°C throughout the year.
The participants’ ages ranged widely, from teenagers to people in their seventies.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cover: to spread over or occupy a surface or area
  • synonym – extend: to cover a certain area; to reach over a space
  • synonym – vary: to change or differ within a range or limits
