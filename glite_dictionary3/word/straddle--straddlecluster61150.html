to straddle

verb
to extend across an area or boundary
Pronunciation: / ˈstræd.əl / (AmE), / ˈstræd.əl / (BrE)
to extend across or over a particular area or range, often covering it partially or entirely

Examples

straddle two countries
A major highway straddles the town, dividing it into two parts.
Her property straddles two different counties, making taxation complicated.
The mountain range straddles the border between France and Spain.
