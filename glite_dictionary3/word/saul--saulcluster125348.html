Saul

proper noun
first king of Israel in the Bible
Pronunciation: / sɔl / (AmE), / sɔːl / (BrE)
(in the Bible) the first king of Israel, known for defending Israel against its enemies, notably the Philistines.

Examples

King Saul of Israel
According to tradition, Saul was chosen by God to be the first king of Israel.
Many paintings depict Saul in scenes of battle against the Philistines.
The story of Saul is told in the First Book of Samuel in the Bible.
