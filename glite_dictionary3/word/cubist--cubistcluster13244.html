cubist

adjective
relating to Cubism (art style)
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːbɪst / (AmE), / ˈkjuːbɪst / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Cubism, an early 20th-century art movement that emphasized abstract, geometric forms.

Examples

cubist painting style
He adopted a cubist approach to architecture, emphasizing geometric simplicity.
Her paintings feature a distinct cubist style, with fragmented shapes and bold lines.
The exhibit showcases several cubist works by Picasso and Braque.

  • synonym – cubism: an art movement focusing on geometric forms and fragmentations
