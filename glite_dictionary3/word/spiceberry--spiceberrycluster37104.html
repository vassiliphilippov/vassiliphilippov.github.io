spiceberry

noun
spicy red berrylike fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪsˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈspaɪsˌbɛri / (BrE)
A spicy, red, berrylike fruit that is a source of wintergreen oil.

Examples

red spiceberry fruit
In the wild, birds are attracted to the bright red spiceberries growing on the shrubs.
Many people value the oil extracted from the spiceberry for its refreshing scent.
The spiceberry is often used to add a unique flavor to herbal teas.
