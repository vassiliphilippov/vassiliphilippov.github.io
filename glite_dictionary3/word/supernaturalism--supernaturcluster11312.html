supernaturalism

noun
belief in supernatural forces
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈnætʃrəˌlɪzəm /; / suː.pɚˈnætʃ.rə.lɪ.zəm / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈnætʃrəlɪzəm /; / ˌsuː.pəˈnætʃ.rə.lɪ.zəm / (BrE)
the belief in or attribution of events to supernatural forces, beings, or influences beyond scientific or natural explanation
