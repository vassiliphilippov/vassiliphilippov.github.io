signaler

noun
person who communicates by signals
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡnələr / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnələ / (BrE)
a person who communicates or sends messages using signals rather than spoken or written language

Examples

army signaler
A skilled signaler is essential in situations where radios cannot be used.
During the exercise, the signaler maintained communication between the teams using hand gestures.
The signaler used flags to convey urgent information across the field.
