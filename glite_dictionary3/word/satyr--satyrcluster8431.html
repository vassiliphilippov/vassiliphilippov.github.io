satyr

noun
man with strong sexual desires
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈseɪtə / (BrE)
a man who is regarded as having strong or uncontrollable sexual desires, often in a way that is considered lecherous or lascivious

Examples

a satyr with women
He was often described as a satyr because of his constant pursuit of women.
In literature, a satyr is sometimes portrayed as a symbol of uncontrollable desire.
The old man’s behavior at the party was that of a satyr, making many guests uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lecher: a man given to excessive sexual indulgence
  • antonym – prude: a person excessively proper in conduct, especially regarding sexual matters (opposite)
