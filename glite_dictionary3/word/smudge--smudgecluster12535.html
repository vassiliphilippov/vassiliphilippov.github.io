smudge

noun
dirty mark or stain
Pronunciation: / smʌdʒ / (AmE), / smʌdʒ / (BrE)
a dirty mark or stain that makes a surface look untidy

Examples

a smudge on glass
She wiped the smudge of chocolate from the boy's face.
The white shirt had a noticeable smudge on the sleeve.
There was a dark smudge on the page where the ink had spread.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blotch: an irregular patch or mark, typically on skin or material
  • synonym – stain: a mark that is difficult to remove, caused by a substance
