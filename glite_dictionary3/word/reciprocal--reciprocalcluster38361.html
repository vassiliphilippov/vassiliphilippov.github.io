reciprocal

noun
multiplicative inverse in mathematics
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɪprəkəl /; / rəˈsɪprəkəl / (AmE), / rɪˈsɪprəkəl /; / rɪˈsɪprəkl / (BrE)
Mathematics: a number, expression, or concept that is the multiplicative inverse of another; when multiplied together, the result is 1
