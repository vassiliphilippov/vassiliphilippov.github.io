sledding

noun
riding on a sled for sport or fun
Pronunciation: / ˈslɛdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈslɛdɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of riding on a sled or sleigh, especially down a snow-covered hill

Examples

sledding down a hill
After the snowfall, the children spent all afternoon sledding down the hill.
He remembers sledding with his cousins every year as a favorite childhood memory.
Many families visit the park for sledding during the winter holidays.
