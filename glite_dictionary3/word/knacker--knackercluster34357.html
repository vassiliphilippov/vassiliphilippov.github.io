knacker

noun
person who salvages materials from old structures
Pronunciation: / ˈnækər / (AmE), / ˈnakə / (BrE)
a person who buys old buildings or ships to dismantle them and recover usable materials

Examples

knacker dismantles ships
After the ship ran aground, a knacker was hired to break it up for parts.
Several knackers made their living dismantling derelict structures in the city.
The knacker arrived to survey the old warehouse before starting the demolition.

Related Terms

  • synonym – salvager: a person who salvages things, especially from wrecks
