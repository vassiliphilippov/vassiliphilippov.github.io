picture

noun
visible part of a TV broadcast
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪktʃər / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
the visible part of a television broadcast as received on a screen, especially as opposed to the sound

Examples

TV picture quality
