entrepot

noun
duty-free trading port or city
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːntrəˌpoʊ /; / ˌɑːntrəˈpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɒntrəˌpəʊ /; / ˈɒntrəpəʊ / (BrE)
