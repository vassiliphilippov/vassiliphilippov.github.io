dog

noun
mechanical catch for moving/preventing motion
Pronunciation: / dɔg / (AmE), / dɒg / (BrE)
A hinged catch that fits into a notch of a ratchet to move a wheel forward or prevent it from moving backward.

Examples

ratchet dog
A broken dog can cause the ratchet wheel to slip, leading to mechanical failure.
Replacement of the worn dog restored full control over the rotation of the gear.
The engineer inspected the dog on the ratchet mechanism to ensure it was functioning properly.
