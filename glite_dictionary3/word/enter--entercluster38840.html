to enter

verb
formally begin duties or office
Pronunciation: / ˈɛn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛn.tə / (BrE)
to assume an official position or begin duties in an office or role, especially a formal one

Examples

to enter office
After years of preparation, she will enter the office of prime minister next week.
The prince is set to enter the throne following his father's abdication.
Upon election, he will enter into the responsibilities of his new role.

Related Terms

  • synonym – accede: to take up a position, especially a throne or office
  • antonym – leave: to go out of or resign from a position or office
