to spin-dry

verb
dry by spinning to remove water
Pronunciation: / spɪnˈdraɪ / (AmE), / spɪnˈdraɪ / (BrE)
to dry clothes or other fabrics by rapidly spinning them to remove excess water, using centrifugal force, often in a washing machine or dryer

Examples

to spin-dry wet clothes
I usually spin-dry my shirts before hanging them out to finish drying.
She spin-dried the towels to save time before heading out.
The machine will spin-dry the clothes after the wash cycle ends.
