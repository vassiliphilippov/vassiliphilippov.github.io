topical

adjective
of current interest; relevant now
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːpɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈtɒpɪkəl / (BrE)
(of a subject, idea, or event) relating to current affairs or of immediate relevance or interest; concerned with topics of present or recent interest.

Examples

topical news story
Many newspapers feature columns that discuss topical events and trends.
She gave a presentation on the topical subject of renewable energy sources.
The debate was highly engaging because it addressed several topical issues facing society today.
