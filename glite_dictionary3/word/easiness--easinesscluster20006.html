easiness

noun
state of calmness; lack of worry
Pronunciation: / ˈiːzinəs / (AmE), / ˈiːzɪnɪs / (BrE)
a state or feeling of calmness and a lack of tension or worry; tranquility.

Examples

easiness of mind
After the exam was over, a sense of easiness settled over the classroom.
He valued the easiness he felt during quiet walks in the park.
The soft music brought a feeling of easiness to the guests at dinner.
