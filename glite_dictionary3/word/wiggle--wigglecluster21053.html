wiggle

noun
a small, quick movement
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈwɪɡl / (BrE)
a small, quick movement back and forth or from side to side; the act of wiggling

Examples

a quick wiggle
After a quick wiggle, the fish slipped off the hook and swam away.
He got rid of the stubborn tooth with just a gentle wiggle.
The puppy gave a little wiggle of excitement when it saw its owner.
