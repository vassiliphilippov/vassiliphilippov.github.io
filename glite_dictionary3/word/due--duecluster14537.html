due

adjective
expected to arrive at a set time
Pronunciation: / duː / (AmE), / djuː / (BrE)
expected or scheduled to arrive or happen at a particular time.

Examples

due to arrive
Her flight is due to land any minute now.
The next bus is due at 7:30 p.m. sharp, so don't be late.
The report is due tomorrow, so we need to finish it today.

Related Terms

  • synonym – expected: regarded as likely to happen or arrive
  • antonym – overdue: not having arrived or happened by the expected time
