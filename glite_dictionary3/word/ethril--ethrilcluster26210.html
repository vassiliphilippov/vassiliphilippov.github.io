Ethril

noun
brand name for antibiotic erythromycin
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθrɪl / (AmE), / ˈɛθrɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of the antibiotic erythromycin, used to treat infections caused by various bacteria.
