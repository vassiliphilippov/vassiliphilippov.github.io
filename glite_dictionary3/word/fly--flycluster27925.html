fly

noun
baseball hit high in the air
Pronunciation: / flaɪ / (AmE), / flaɪ / (BrE)
(in baseball) a ball that is hit high into the air.

Examples

a fly ball
He caught the fly near the warning track.
The batter hit a long fly to center field.
With two outs, a deep fly ended the inning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fly ball: (baseball) a ball hit high into the air; alternative term for 'fly' in this sense
  • antonym – grounder: (baseball) a ball that is hit along the ground; contrast with 'fly'
