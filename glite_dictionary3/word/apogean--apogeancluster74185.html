apogean

adjective
relating to an apogee
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˈdʒiːən / (AmE), / ˌapəˈdʒiːən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of an apogee, particularly the point in the orbit of the moon or a satellite at which it is farthest from the earth.

Examples

apogean distance
At apogean distances, the satellite moves slower in its orbit around the planet.
Scientists study apogean positions to understand variations in satellite orbits.
The most significant apogean tides are observed when the moon is furthest from the Earth.

Related Terms

  • antonym – perigean: relating to the perigee, the nearest point to earth in an orbit
