decay

noun
rotting or decomposition of matter
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkeɪ / (AmE), / dɪˈkeɪ / (BrE)
the process by which organic matter breaks down and rots over time

Examples

tooth decay
Dental decay can be prevented with regular brushing and flossing.

The scent of decay filled the old, abandoned house.
