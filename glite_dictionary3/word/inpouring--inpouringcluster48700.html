inpouring

noun
an inflow or influx
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌpɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌpɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
a flowing in or influx, especially of something abstract such as feeling or ideas

Examples

inpouring of emotion
She felt an inpouring of gratitude for her friends' support.
The city saw an inpouring of refugees during the conflict.
There was an inpouring of donations after the disaster struck.

Related Terms

  • synonym – influx: a flowing in, especially of a large number of people or things
  • antonym – outflow: a flowing out; efflux
