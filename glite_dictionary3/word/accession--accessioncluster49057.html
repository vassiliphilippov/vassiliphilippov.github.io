accession

noun
the right to enter or join something
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛʃən / (AmE), / əkˈsɛʃən / (BrE)
the right or opportunity to enter or join a place, institution, or organization

Examples

accession to the group
After lengthy negotiations, the company's accession to the trade association was approved.
His accession to the exclusive club was finally granted after months of waiting.
The treaty outlines the terms for a country's accession to the European Union.
