fantasy

noun
false belief or misconception
Pronunciation: / ˈfæn.tə.si / (AmE), / ˈfæn.tə.si / (BrE)
a belief or idea that many people hold but that is not true; an illusion or misconception

Examples

childhood fantasy
It's a common fantasy that life will be perfect after retirement.
Many people have the fantasy that money will bring them happiness.
The idea that fame guarantees success is just a fantasy.
