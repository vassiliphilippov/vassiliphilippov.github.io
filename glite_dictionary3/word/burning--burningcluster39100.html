burning

noun
torture by burning the skin
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
A form of torture in which the victim's skin is deliberately burned with cigarettes, cigars, or other hot objects.

Examples

cigarette burning marks
Evidence of burning was found on the suspect's arms.
Prisoners reported instances of burning during interrogations.
The victim suffered burning at the hands of his captors.
