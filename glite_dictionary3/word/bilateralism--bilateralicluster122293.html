bilateralism

noun
the state of being symmetrical on both sides
Pronunciation: / baɪˈlæt̬.ɚ.əˌlɪz.əm / (AmE), / bʌɪˈlat.ər.əl.ɪz.əm / (BrE)
the state or property of being symmetrical with respect to a vertical plane; especially having identical right and left sides

Examples

bilateralism in design
Architects sometimes design buildings with bilateralism to achieve a sense of balance and harmony.
Many animals in the animal kingdom exhibit bilateralism in their body plan.
The bilateralism of the butterfly's wings is a classic example of natural symmetry.
