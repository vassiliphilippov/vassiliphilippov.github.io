Stalin

proper noun
Soviet leader, 1879–1953
Pronunciation: / stɑːˈlɪn / (AmE), / ˈstɑːlɪn / (BrE)
(1879–1953) Soviet leader who succeeded Lenin and ruled the Soviet Union with a totalitarian regime, noted for purging opposition and centralizing power.

Examples

Stalin's regime
During Stalin's regime, millions of people were subjected to political purges.
