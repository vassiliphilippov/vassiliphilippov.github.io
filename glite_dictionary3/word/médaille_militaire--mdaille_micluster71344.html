Médaille Militaire

noun
French military decoration for bravery or service
Pronunciation: / meɪˌdaɪ məˈlɪtəri / (AmE), / meɪˌdaɪ məˈlɪtəri / (BrE)
a French military decoration awarded for acts of bravery or long service, especially to non-commissioned officers and enlisted personnel
