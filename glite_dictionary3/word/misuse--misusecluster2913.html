misuse

noun
improper or excessive use
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈjus / (AmE), / mɪsˈjuːz / (BrE)
the act of using something in an improper or excessive way

Examples

misuse of power
Concerns were raised about the misuse of taxpayer funds in the project.
His misuse of company resources led to a formal warning from his supervisor.
The misuse of prescription drugs can have serious health consequences.

Related Terms

  • synonym – abuse: improper or wrongful use, especially in a way that is harmful
  • antonym – proper use: using something in the correct or intended way
