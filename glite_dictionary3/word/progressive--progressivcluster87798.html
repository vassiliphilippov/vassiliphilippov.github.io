progressive

noun
a verb tense indicating ongoing action
Pronunciation: / prəˈɡrɛsɪv / (AmE), / prəˈɡrɛsɪv / (BrE)
A verb tense used to indicate an action that is continuing or in progress at a certain time.

Examples

progressive verb tense
In English, the progressive is formed with the verb 'be' and the present participle.
Students often confuse the simple past with the progressive.
The sentence 'She is reading' is in the progressive tense.
