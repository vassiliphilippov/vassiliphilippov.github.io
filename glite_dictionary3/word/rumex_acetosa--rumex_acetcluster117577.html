Rumex acetosa

noun
a species of sorrel with acidic edible leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈruːmɛks əˈsiːtəsə / (AmE), / ˈruːmɛks əˈsiːtəʊsə / (BrE)
A species of sorrel native to Europe and Asia, with large, slightly acidic leaves used in salads and as a spring green.
