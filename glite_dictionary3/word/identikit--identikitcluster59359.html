Identikit

noun
composite face image for police identification
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdɛntɪˌkɪt / (AmE), / aɪˈdɛntɪˌkɪt / (BrE)
a picture of a person's face, made by combining images of typical facial features, based on descriptions given to the police, to help identify suspects

Examples

Identikit photo of suspect
An Identikit was distributed to all officers in the area during the investigation.
The police released an Identikit image of the suspect to the press.
Witnesses helped the artist create an Identikit based on their memory of the attacker.
