knightly

adjective
chivalrous or courteous towards women
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtli / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtli / (BrE)
(of a man or his behavior) displaying chivalrous or courteous attention towards women, as an ideal knight would.

Examples

knightly behavior
He acted in a truly knightly manner, holding the door open for her.
His knightly gestures made everyone around him smile with admiration.
In medieval tales, heroes are often praised for their knightly devotion to ladies.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chivalrous: showing polite and honorable behavior, especially toward women
