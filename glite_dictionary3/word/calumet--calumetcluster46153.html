calumet

noun
ceremonial Native American pipe
Pronunciation: / ˈkæl.juˌmɛt / (AmE), / ˈkæl.juː.mɛt / (BrE)
A highly decorated ceremonial pipe used by some Native American peoples, especially in ritual contexts or as a symbol of peace.
