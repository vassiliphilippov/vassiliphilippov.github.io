self-fertilisation

noun
fertilization within the same individual
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˌfɜrtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˌfəːtɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which an organism's own male and female gametes unite to produce offspring
