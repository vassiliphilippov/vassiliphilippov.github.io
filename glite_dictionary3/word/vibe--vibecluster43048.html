to vibe

verb
to feel a mutual emotional connection
Pronunciation: / vaɪb / (AmE), / vaɪb / (BrE)
to have a mutual understanding, connection, or positive feeling with someone or something; to resonate emotionally, often used informally

Examples

to vibe with music
Are you vibing with this music, or should we change it?

They really vibed at the party and ended up talking all night.
