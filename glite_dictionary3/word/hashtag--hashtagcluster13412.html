to hashtag

verb
add a hashtag to a post
Pronunciation: / ˈhæʃtæɡ / (AmE), / ˈhæʃ.tæɡ / (BrE)
To add a hashtag (a word or phrase preceded by #) to a social media post or message to label, categorize, or promote it.

Examples

to hashtag a photo
Influencers often hashtag their posts to reach new audiences and join trending conversations.
Before posting the photo, she hashtagged it with at least five tags to attract attention.
The marketing team is hashtagging every campaign post to improve tracking and engagement metrics.

Related Terms

  • synonym – tag: to attach a label or keyword to online content to categorize or find it
