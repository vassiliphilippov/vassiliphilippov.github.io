lightly

adverb
to a small extent or quantity
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtli / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtli / (BrE)
in a small amount or with moderation; not much or not heavily

Examples

lightly dust with sugar
She dusted the cake lightly with powdered sugar before serving.
Spread the butter lightly over the toast for a healthier option.
The sauce should be applied lightly so the flavor isn't overwhelming.
Doctors recommend eating lightly before intense exercise.
During the holidays, he tried to drink lightly despite the many parties.
She prefers to eat lightly in the evenings to avoid feeling too full.
