home-style

adjective
made in a traditional, homemade style
Pronunciation: / hoʊm-staɪl / (AmE), / həʊm-staɪl / (BrE)
(especially of food) prepared or made in a simple, traditional way, as if done at home.

Examples

home-style cooking
He enjoys the home-style atmosphere and comfort food at his grandmother's house.
Many people prefer home-style meals that remind them of childhood.
The restaurant is famous for its home-style fried chicken and mashed potatoes.
