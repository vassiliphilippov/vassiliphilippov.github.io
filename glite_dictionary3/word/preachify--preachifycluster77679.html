to preachify

verb
speak in a moralizing or sermon-like way
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːtʃɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈpriːtʃɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to speak in a way that is overly moralizing or like delivering a sermon, often in a tedious or self-righteous manner

Examples

to preachify at friends
He preachified to the students about the importance of honesty.
Please stop preachifying; everyone knows how you feel about recycling.
She tends to preachify when discussing politics, which annoys her friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sermonize: to give moral advice in a tedious or self-righteous way
