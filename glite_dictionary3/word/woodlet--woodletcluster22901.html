woodlet

noun
small cultivated wood or grove
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdlət / (AmE), / ˈwʊdlɪt / (BrE)
A small cultivated wood, typically a garden area with trees but little or no undergrowth.

Examples

shady woodlet
A narrow path led us through the woodlet to the main garden.
She often found inspiration walking in the quiet woodlet behind her cottage.
The estate's woodlet was carefully maintained to encourage birdlife while preventing thick undergrowth.
