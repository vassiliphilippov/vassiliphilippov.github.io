poky

adjective
moving or working slowly; sluggish
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊki / (AmE), / ˈpəʊki / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) slow or moving at a sluggish pace, especially so as to waste time.

Examples

a poky worker
She was so poky getting ready that we missed the train.
The servers were a bit poky tonight, so dinner took much longer than usual.
This poky computer makes even simple tasks take forever.

Related Terms

  • synonym – slow: moving or acting at a low speed
  • synonym – sluggish: lacking energy; moving slowly
  • antonym – speedy: moving or happening quickly
