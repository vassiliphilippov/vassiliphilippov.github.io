show

noun
public performance or entertainment event
Pronunciation: / ʃoʊ / (AmE), / ʃəʊ / (BrE)
an event or performance presented to entertain an audience, such as a play, concert, or exhibition.

Examples

a show on stage
After the show, the cast came out for a final bow.
The art show featured works from local and international artists.
We bought tickets to a Broadway show for her birthday.

Related Terms

  • synonym – concert: a musical performance given in public
  • synonym – exhibition: an event at which objects such as works of art are put on display
  • synonym – performance: an act of presenting a play, concert, or other form of entertainment
