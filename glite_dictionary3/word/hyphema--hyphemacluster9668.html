hyphema

noun
bleeding in the front chamber of the eye
Pronunciation: / haɪˈfiːmə / (AmE), / hɪˈfiːmə / (BrE)
bleeding into the anterior chamber of the eye, typically caused by trauma or certain medical conditions

Examples

hyphema after eye injury
A severe hyphema can threaten vision and requires prompt medical attention.
After the accident, the doctor diagnosed her with a hyphema in her left eye.
Wearing protective eyewear can help prevent hyphema during contact sports.
