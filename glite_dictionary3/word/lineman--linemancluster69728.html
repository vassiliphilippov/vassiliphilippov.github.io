lineman

noun
person who repairs power or phone lines
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪnˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnmən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to install or repair electrical power lines or telephone lines, typically working outdoors and at heights

Examples

electric lineman
A team of linemen worked overnight to restore telephone service to the area.
It takes special training to become a skilled lineman for the electrical company.
The lineman climbed the pole to fix the downed power lines after the storm.
