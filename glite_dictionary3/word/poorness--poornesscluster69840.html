poorness

noun
meagerness or insufficiency
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊrnəs /; / ˈpɔrnəs / (AmE), / ˈpɔːnəs /; / ˈpɔːnɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being insufficient, of low standard, or not good enough in amount or quality
