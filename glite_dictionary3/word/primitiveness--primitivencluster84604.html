primitiveness

noun
the condition of being primitive or unrefined
Pronunciation: / prɪˈmɪt̬ɪvnəs / (AmE), / prɪˈmɪtɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being primitive, especially being wild, simple, or undeveloped

Examples

the primitiveness of culture
Many artists are fascinated by the primitiveness of ancient cave paintings.
Some critics admired the primitiveness in her writing style, considering it raw and honest.
The primitiveness of their tools made survival a daily struggle.
