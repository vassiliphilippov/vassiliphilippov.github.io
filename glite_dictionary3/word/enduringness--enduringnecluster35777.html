enduringness

noun
the quality of being durable or long-lasting
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʊrɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdjʊərɪŋnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of lasting for a long time or resisting stress, wear, or force; durability.

Examples

remarkable enduringness
Her enduringness in the face of adversity inspired everyone around her.
Scientists are studying the enduringness of new materials under extreme conditions.
The enduringness of the bridge is a testament to its excellent construction.
