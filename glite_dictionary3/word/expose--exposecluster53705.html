to expose

verb
remove clothing to reveal body
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspoʊz / (AmE), / ɪkˈspəʊz / (BrE)
to remove all or part of one's clothing to reveal one's body, especially in a way that may be considered inappropriate or indecent

Examples

to expose yourself in public
It is illegal to expose yourself in public.
She did not want to expose her arms to the sun without sunscreen.
The actor accidentally exposed his chest during the performance.
