to sharpen

verb
become sharp or sharper
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑr.pən /; / ˈʃɑːrpən /; / ˈʃɑːr.pən / (AmE), / ˈʃɑː.pən /; / ˈʃɑːpən / (BrE)
to make something clearer, more defined, or more precise (such as an image, sound, argument, or sense)

Examples

As the discussion progressed, opinions began to sharpen on both sides.

You can sharpen the photo with the editing software to improve its clarity.
