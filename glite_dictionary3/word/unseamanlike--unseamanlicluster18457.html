unseamanlike

adjective
not characteristic of a good seaman
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsiː.mənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ʌnˈsiː.mənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Not characteristic of a good seaman; lacking the qualities, skills, or behavior expected of a competent sailor.

Examples

unseamanlike behavior
His unseamanlike manner made it clear he was new to life at sea.
Leaving the ropes untied was considered highly unseamanlike behavior by the experienced sailors.
The captain criticized the crew for their unseamanlike conduct during the storm.
