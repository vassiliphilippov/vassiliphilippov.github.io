bungling

adjective
clumsy or incompetent; lacking skill
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌŋɡlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋɡlɪŋ / (BrE)
clumsy or incompetent; doing things in a way that shows a lack of skill or ability

Examples

bungling mistake
A bungling thief accidentally triggered the alarm and was quickly caught.
Despite their bungling attempts, the team failed to solve the puzzle.

Her bungling attempt to fix the computer only made things worse.
The bungling waiter spilled soup all over the customer's lap.
