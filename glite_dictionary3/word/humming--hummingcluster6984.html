humming

noun
continuous, low, steady sound
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
A continuous, low, steady sound, like that made by bees, machinery, or traffic.

Examples

engine humming
He could hear a soft humming coming from the engine.
The humming of the refrigerator filled the quiet kitchen.
The gentle humming outside her window lulled her to sleep.
