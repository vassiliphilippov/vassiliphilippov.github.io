mercy

noun
a fortunate event to be thankful for
Pronunciation: / ˈmɜr.si / (AmE), / ˈmɜː.si / (BrE)
a fortunate event or situation that causes relief and is something to be thankful for

Examples

a mercy in trouble
By some mercy, the lost keys were found right before we had to leave.
Getting a seat on the crowded train this morning was a real mercy.
It was a mercy that the storm passed without causing any damage.

  • antonym – misfortune: an unfortunate condition or event
