to engrave

verb
impress deeply in memory or mind (figurative)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡreɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡreɪv / (BrE)
(figurative) To impress or fix deeply in the mind or memory.

The loss of his childhood home engraved a lasting sorrow in his heart.
