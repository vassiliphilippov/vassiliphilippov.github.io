casteless

adjective
having no caste or status in a caste system
Pronunciation: / ˈkæs.tləs / (AmE), / ˈkɑːs.tləs / (BrE)
not belonging to any caste, especially in societies organized by a caste system; having no place or official status in such a social hierarchy

Examples

casteless person
After being expelled, he lived as a casteless wanderer, shunned by all communities.
In the rigid social structure, casteless individuals were often marginalized and denied basic rights.
The movement aimed to empower the casteless members of society.

Related Terms

  • synonym – outcaste: a person who has been expelled from or does not belong to a caste
