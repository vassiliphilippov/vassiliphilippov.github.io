lithophytic

adjective
relating to rock-growing plants or organisms
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪθəˈfɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌlɪθəˈfɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of plants or organisms that grow on rocks

Examples

lithophytic plants on rocks
Cacti can be lithophytic, adapted to surviving with minimal soil.
Many orchids have lithophytic species that thrive on rocky surfaces.
The lithophytic mosses were found growing in crevices of the limestone cliff.
