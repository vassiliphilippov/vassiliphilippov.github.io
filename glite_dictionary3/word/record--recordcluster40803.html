to record

verb
to make a permanent account
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɔrd / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːd / (BrE)
to make a written, audio, or visual account of something so it can be used or referred to later; to store sounds, images, or information electronically

They recorded several takes before choosing the best version.
