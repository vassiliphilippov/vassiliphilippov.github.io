to twist

verb
deliberately distort meaning
Pronunciation: / twɪst / (AmE), / twɪst / (BrE)
to deliberately change the meaning of something that someone has said or written, often to mislead or deceive

Examples

to twist the truth
He often twists what people say to support his own arguments.
Please don’t twist my words to mean something I didn’t say.
She accused the reporter of trying to twist the facts of the story.

Related Terms

  • antonym – clarify: to make something less confused and more comprehensible
  • synonym – distort: to change the meaning or facts of something to suit yourself
  • synonym – misrepresent: to describe falsely or unfairly
