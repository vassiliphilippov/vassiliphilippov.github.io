callout

noun
public accusation or shaming (online)
Pronunciation: / \u02c8k\u0254l\u02cca\u028at / (AmE), / \u02c8k\u0254\u02d0l.a\u028at / (BrE)
a public accusation or criticism of someone, especially on social media, intended to expose wrongdoing, shame them, or demand accountability.

Examples

social media callout
After the exposé, the celebrity faced a fierce callout on several social platforms.
She tried to ignore the initial callout but later posted a formal public apology.
The online callout forced the company to launch an internal investigation the same week.

Related Terms

  • synonym – accusation: a charge or claim that someone has done something wrong; often the central act in a callout
  • antonym – shoutout: a public praise or acknowledgment, typically positive; contrasts with an accusatory callout
