tale

noun
a trivial or harmless lie
Pronunciation: / teɪl / (AmE), / teɪl / (BrE)
a lie, especially a trivial or harmless one, often told by a child

Examples

make up a tale
As a child, she often made up tales to avoid getting into trouble.
Don’t believe every tale he tells you – he likes to exaggerate.
He was caught telling tales about why he hadn’t done his homework.
