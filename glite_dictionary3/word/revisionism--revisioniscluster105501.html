revisionism

noun
moderate or evolutionary form of Marxism
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɪʒ.əˌnɪz.əm /; / rɪˈvɪʒənɪzəm / (AmE), / rɪˈvɪʒ.ən.ɪz.əm /; / rɪˈvɪʒənɪzəm / (BrE)
