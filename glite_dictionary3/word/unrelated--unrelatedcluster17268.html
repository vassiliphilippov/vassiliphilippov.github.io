unrelated

adjective
not related by kinship
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈleɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈleɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
not connected by kinship; not belonging to the same family by birth or marriage.

Examples

unrelated children
Although the two girls share a last name, they are completely unrelated.
He was raised by people who were unrelated to him by blood or marriage.
Many believe that adoption brings together unrelated people to form a family.
