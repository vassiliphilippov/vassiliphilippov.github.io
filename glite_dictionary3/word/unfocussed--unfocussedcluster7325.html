unfocussed

adjective
not concentrated or lacking clear focus
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfoʊkəst / (AmE), / ʌnˈfəʊkəst / (BrE)
not concentrated or directed at a single point, objective, or subject; lacking focus or clear direction.

Examples

unfocussed mind
His unfocussed energy made it difficult for him to finish any of his projects.
The photograph was unfocussed, so the details were blurry and indistinct.
The team's strategy was unfocussed, leading to confusion among the players.

Related Terms

  • antonym – focused: concentrated or directed at a single point
  • synonym – scattered: spread out in different directions, lacking order or unity
