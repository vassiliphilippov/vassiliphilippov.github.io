warm-toned

adjective
having a rich, mellow sound
Pronunciation: / wɔrm toʊnd / (AmE), / wɔːm təʊnd / (BrE)
Describing music or sound that has a rich, mellow, and pleasant quality, lacking harshness or coldness.

Examples

warm-toned music
His new album features a distinctly warm-toned production that sets it apart from earlier works.
Many listeners prefer warm-toned recordings for their inviting, natural character.
The violin section played with a warm-toned elegance that filled the concert hall.
