dashed

adjective
having gaps or spaces (as in lines)
Pronunciation: / ˈdæʃt / (AmE), / ˈdaʃt / (BrE)
marked by a series of short lines with gaps or spaces between them; composed of dashes.

Examples

dashed line
Please sign your name on the dashed line at the bottom.
She traced the dashed outline before cutting the paper.
The dashed line on the road indicates where you may pass other vehicles.
