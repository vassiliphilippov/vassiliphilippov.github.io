to re-explain

verb
explain again or differently
Pronunciation: / ˌriː ɪkˈspleɪn / (AmE), / ˌriː ɪkˈspleɪn / (BrE)
to explain something again, especially in a different or clearer way or from a new perspective

Examples

to re-explain the idea

Sometimes teachers have to re-explain difficult topics to ensure all students are following.
