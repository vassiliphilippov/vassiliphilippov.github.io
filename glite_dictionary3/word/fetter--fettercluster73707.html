fetter

noun
ankle shackle; chain for feet
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɛtə / (BrE)
a chain or shackle placed on the ankles or feet, typically to restrain movement.

Examples

iron fetters
He felt as if invisible fetters were holding him back from freedom.
Iron fetters were used to restrain slaves during transport.
The prisoner struggled against the heavy fetters locked around his ankles.

Related Terms

  • synonym – manacle: a metal band for fastening someone's arms or hands
  • synonym – shackle: a device for restraining movement by locking around the wrists or ankles
