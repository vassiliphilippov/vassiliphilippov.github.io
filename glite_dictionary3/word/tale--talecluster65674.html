tale

noun
narrative; story of events
Pronunciation: / teɪl / (AmE), / teɪl / (BrE)
a narrative or story describing a series of events, often imaginative or fictional, presented in written, spoken, or other media

Examples

tell a tale
He told a fascinating tale about his adventures in South America.
She wrote a tale of mystery and suspense that captivated her readers.
The movie is based on a classic fairy tale.

Related Terms

  • synonym – narrative: a spoken or written account of connected events; a story
  • synonym – story: an account of imaginary or real people and events told for entertainment
