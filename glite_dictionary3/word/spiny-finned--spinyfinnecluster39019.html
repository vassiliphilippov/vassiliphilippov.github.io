spiny-finned

adjective
having or relating to spiny fins (of fish)
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪni fɪnd / (AmE), / ˈspaɪni fɪnd / (BrE)
(of a fish) having fins that are supported by spines rather than soft rays; relating to or characteristic of spiny-finned fish.

Examples

spiny-finned fish species
Many spiny-finned species inhabit coral reefs around the world.
The spiny-finned fish are easily recognized by the sharp rays supporting their fins.
The perch is a typical example of a spiny-finned fish.
