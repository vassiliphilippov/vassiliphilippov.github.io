Remus

proper noun
twin brother of Romulus in Roman myth
Pronunciation: / ˈriːməs / (AmE), / ˈriːməs / (BrE)
(in Roman mythology) the twin brother of Romulus, said to be one of the founders of Rome.

Remus and Romulus
