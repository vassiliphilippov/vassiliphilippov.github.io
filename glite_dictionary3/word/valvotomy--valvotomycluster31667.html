valvotomy

noun
surgical incision of a heart valve
Pronunciation: / vælˈvɑtəmi / (AmE), / valˈvɒtəmi / (BrE)
a surgical incision into a narrowed heart valve to relieve an obstruction

Examples

valvotomy for heart valve
Advances in cardiac surgery have made valvotomy a safer option for treating valve stenosis in children.
Recovery from a valvotomy usually requires careful monitoring for complications such as infection or bleeding.
The patient underwent a valvotomy to improve blood flow through the narrowed mitral valve.
