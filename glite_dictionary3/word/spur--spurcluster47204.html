spur

noun
encouraging remark or stimulus
Pronunciation: / spɝː / (AmE), / spɜː / (BrE)
a remark or comment that encourages someone to try or do something, or motivates action

Examples

a spur to action
Her praise acted as a spur to his efforts in the project.
Sometimes a gentle spur is all someone needs to take the first step.
The threat of failure was the main spur for him to study harder.

Related Terms

  • antonym – deterrent: something that discourages or inhibits action
  • synonym – incentive: something that encourages a person to do something
