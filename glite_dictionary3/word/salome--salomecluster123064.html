Salome

proper noun
biblical figure; Herodias's daughter
Pronunciation: / səˈloʊmi / (AmE), / səˈləʊmiː / (BrE)
(in the Bible) the daughter of Herodias whose dancing so pleased Herod Antipas that he granted her request for the head of John the Baptist.
