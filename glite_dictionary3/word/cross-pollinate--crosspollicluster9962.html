to cross-pollinate

verb
to mix ideas from different fields
Pronunciation: / krɔs ˈpɑləˌneɪt /; / ˌkrɔs ˈpɑl.əˌneɪt / (AmE), / krɒs ˈpɒləˌneɪt /; / ˌkrɒs ˈpɒl.ɪ.neɪt / (BrE)
to combine or exchange ideas, methods, or influences from different fields or groups to create something new or improved
