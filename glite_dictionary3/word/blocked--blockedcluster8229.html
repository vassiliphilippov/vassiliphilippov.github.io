blocked

adjective
prevented from contacting/viewing by another user
Pronunciation: / blɑkt / (AmE), / blɒkt / (BrE)
(on social media or messaging platforms) prevented by another user or the platform from viewing, contacting, or interacting with a particular account.

Examples

blocked account online
I'm blocked from messaging him on the social app, so I can't reach him.
After the argument she discovered she had been blocked and couldn't see his profile posts anymore.
Customer support told me my account is blocked by the other user, so comments are hidden.

Related Terms

  • synonym – banned: formally prevented by a platform from accessing or using a service; similar in effect though usually applied by the platform rather than an individual user.
  • antonym – unblocked: not prevented from viewing or contacting an account; the opposite of being blocked on a platform.
