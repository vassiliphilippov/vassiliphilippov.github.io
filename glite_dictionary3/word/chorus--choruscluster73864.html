chorus

noun
repeated part of a song sung by a group
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːrəs / (AmE), / ˈkɔːrəs / (BrE)
the part of a song that is repeated and sung by a group of singers, often following each verse and joined by the soloist

Examples

chorus of a song
After each verse, the soloist motioned for the audience to join in on the chorus.
The chorus of the song was so catchy that everyone started singing along.
The band's new single has a powerful chorus that repeats several times.

  • synonym – refrain: the repeated line or group of lines in a song or poem
