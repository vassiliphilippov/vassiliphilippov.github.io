Tsuga caroliniana

noun
species of hemlock tree native to southeastern U.S.
Pronunciation: / ˈtsuːɡə ˌkæroʊlɪˈaːnə / (AmE), / ˈtsuːɡə ˌkærəlɪˈɑːnɪə / (BrE)
