haloalkane

noun
organic compound with halogen-substituted alkane
Pronunciation: / ˌheɪloʊˈælkeɪn / (AmE), / ˌheɪləʊˈælkeɪn / (BrE)
an organic compound in which one or more hydrogen atoms of an alkane have been replaced by halogen atoms
