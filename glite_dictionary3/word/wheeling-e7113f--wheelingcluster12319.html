wheeling

noun
propelling on wheels
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of moving or propelling something on wheels

Examples

wheeling a cart
Her job involved the wheeling of heavy trolleys from the storeroom to the kitchen.
The noise from the wheeling of luggage across the tiles echoed through the hall.
There was a steady wheeling of carts along the village lanes each morning.
