discontentedness

noun
the state of being dissatisfied
Pronunciation: / dɪs.kənˈtɛn.tɪd.nəs / (AmE), / dɪs.kənˈtɛn.tɪd.nəs / (BrE)
The state of being dissatisfied or uneasy with the current situation; a feeling of longing for something better.

Examples

growing discontentedness
Her discontentedness with the results was evident in her frustrated expression.
Periods of discontentedness can sometimes motivate positive change in one's life.
The growing discontentedness among employees led to a call for workplace reforms.

Related Terms

  • antonym – contentment: a state of happiness and satisfaction
  • synonym – discontent: a feeling of dissatisfaction with one's circumstances
