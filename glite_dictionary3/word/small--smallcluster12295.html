small

adjective
written in lowercase (letter)
Pronunciation: / smɔl / (AmE), / smɔːl / (BrE)
(of a letter) written in lowercase rather than uppercase; minuscule.

Examples

This font makes it difficult to distinguish between small 'l' and capital 'I'.
