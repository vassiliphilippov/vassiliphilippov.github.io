maraschino

noun
cherry preserved in liqueur or syrup
Pronunciation: / ˌmærəˈskiːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˌmærəˈskiːnəʊ / (BrE)
a cherry preserved in maraschino liqueur or a similar sweet syrup, often used as a garnish in drinks or desserts

Examples

maraschino cherry garnish
A single maraschino added a touch of color to her cocktail.
He placed a bright maraschino on top of the whipped cream.
The recipe called for two maraschino cherries as a garnish.
