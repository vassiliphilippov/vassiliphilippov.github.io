chamber-work

noun
music for a small group of instruments
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪmbər wɜrk / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪmbə wɜːk / (BrE)
a musical composition or performance intended for a small group of instruments, typically one player per part, rather than a full orchestra
