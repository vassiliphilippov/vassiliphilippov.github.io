to fortify

verb
add alcohol to a drink
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːr.t̬ə.faɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.tɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
to add alcohol to (a drink or beverage) in order to increase its alcoholic strength

Examples

to fortify wine
She noticed that her dessert wine had been fortified during the production process.
Some wines are fortified with brandy to enhance their alcohol content.
They decided to fortify the eggnog with a splash of rum for the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym – spike: to add alcohol or a stimulant to a drink (usually secretly)
