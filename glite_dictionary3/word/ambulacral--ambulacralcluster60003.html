ambulacral

adjective
relating to echinoderm ambulacra
Pronunciation: / ˌæmbjʊˈleɪkrəl / (AmE), / ˌæmbjʊˈleɪkrəl / (BrE)
relating to the ambulacra, or the areas bearing tube feet, of radial echinoderms such as starfish and sea urchins

Examples

ambulacral groove
Damage to the ambulacral areas can impede the movement of sea urchins.
The biologist studied the development of the ambulacral system in juvenile echinoderms.
The starfish's ambulacral grooves are lined with tube feet used for locomotion and feeding.
