turtler

noun
player using a defensive 'turtle' strategy
Pronunciation: / c8t5dd0t59l59r / (AmE), / c8t5cd0tl59 / (BrE)
A player (especially in video games) who adopts an extremely defensive 'turtle' strategy, avoiding risk and staying protected rather than attacking.

Examples

defensive turtler
During the final round, the turtler refused to push forward and waited for opponents' mistakes.
Commentators criticized the turtler for stalling the game instead of trying to win aggressively.
In ranked play, many players accuse a turtler of ruining the match by never leaving cover.

Related Terms

  • synonym – camper: a player who stays in one place (often hidden) to gain an advantage; often used negatively
  • antonym – rusher: a player who uses aggressive, fast attacks instead of defensive play
  • synonym – turtle (verb): to play very defensively in a game, focusing on protection and delay rather than offense
