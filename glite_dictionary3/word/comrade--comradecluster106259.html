comrade

noun
a companion or close friend
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmˌræd / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.reɪd / (BrE)
a companion or friend, especially one who shares activities, interests, or experiences with another person

Examples

trusted comrade
After years of working together, they became close comrades and shared many memories.
He regarded his fellow soldiers as trusted comrades both on and off the battlefield.
She greeted her old college comrade with a warm embrace at the reunion.
