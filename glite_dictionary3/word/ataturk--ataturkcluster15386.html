Ataturk

proper noun
founder and first President of modern Turkey
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːtəˌtɜrk / (AmE), / ˈætətɜːk / (BrE)
the surname adopted by Mustafa Kemal (1881–1938), founder and first President of modern Turkey, who established a secular and progressive republic.

Examples

Ataturk founded Turkey
Ataturk implemented sweeping reforms that transformed Turkey into a secular republic.
The modern city of Ankara features many monuments dedicated to Ataturk.
Turkish students study the legacy of Ataturk in their history classes.
