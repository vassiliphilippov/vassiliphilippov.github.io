spinner

noun
revolving fishing lure
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈspɪnə / (BrE)
a type of fishing lure that spins or revolves as it is pulled through the water, designed to attract fish

Examples

fishing spinner
Many fishermen believe that a brightly colored spinner works best in murky water.
She caught her first fish using a silver spinner she bought at the local tackle shop.
The angler tied a new spinner to his line to attract trout in the river.
