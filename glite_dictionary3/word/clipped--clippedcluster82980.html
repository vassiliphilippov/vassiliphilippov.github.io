clipped

adjective
having quick, short, precise sounds (of speech)
Pronunciation: / klɪpt / (AmE), / klɪpt / (BrE)
(of speech or pronunciation) characterized by quick, short, and precise sounds, often sounding abrupt or concise.

Examples

clipped speech
His instructions were clipped and to the point, leaving no room for confusion.
She was recognized immediately by her clipped British accent.
The professor spoke in a clipped tone that demanded attention.
