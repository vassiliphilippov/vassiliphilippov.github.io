terminus

noun
statue or bust atop a pillar (arch.); Roman boundary marker
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝː.mə.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.mɪ.nəs / (BrE)
(architecture) A statue, human bust, or animal figure carved from the top of a square pillar, originally used as a boundary marker in ancient Rome.
