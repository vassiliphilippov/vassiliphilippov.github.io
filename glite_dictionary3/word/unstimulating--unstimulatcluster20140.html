unstimulating

adjective
not stimulating or interesting
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈstɪmjəˌleɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈstɪmjʊleɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not interesting, exciting, or energizing; lacking stimulation or interest

Examples

an unstimulating job
An unstimulating book can quickly lose a reader's attention.
He found the work environment unstimulating and started looking for a new job.
The lecture was so unstimulating that several students dozed off in the middle.

Related Terms
