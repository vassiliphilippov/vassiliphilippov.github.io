to size

verb
treat with size to stiffen or seal
Pronunciation: / saɪz / (AmE), / saɪz / (BrE)
to treat or coat a porous material with size (a glutinous substance) in order to stiffen, glaze, or seal it

Examples

to size the canvas
Before painting, we need to size the canvas to ensure the paint adheres properly.
She spent the morning sizing the walls with a special adhesive.
The craftsman sized the paper to make it less absorbent.
