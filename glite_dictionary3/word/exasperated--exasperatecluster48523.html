exasperated

adjective
greatly annoyed or out of patience
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzæspəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɑːspəreɪtɪd / (BrE)

Examples

The teacher had an exasperated expression after explaining the instructions for the third time.
