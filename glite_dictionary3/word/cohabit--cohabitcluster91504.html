to cohabit

verb
exist or occur together
Pronunciation: / koʊˈhæbɪt / (AmE), / kəʊˈhæbɪt / (BrE)
to exist or be present together in the same place or time, especially in reference to different things or groups

Examples

to cohabit with species
In this garden, exotic and native plants cohabit harmoniously.
Several rare animal species cohabit peacefully within the protected forest.
The two faiths have managed to cohabit in the same region for centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym – coexist: to exist at the same time or in the same place
