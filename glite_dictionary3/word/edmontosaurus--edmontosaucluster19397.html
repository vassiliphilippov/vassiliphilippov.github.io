Edmontosaurus

noun
duck-billed dinosaur genus
Pronunciation: / ɛdˌmɑntəˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / ɛdˌmɒntəˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
A genus of large, herbivorous duck-billed dinosaurs that lived in what is now Canada and the United States during the late Cretaceous period.
