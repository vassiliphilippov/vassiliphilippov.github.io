stentorian

adjective
loud, powerful (of voice/sound)
Pronunciation: / stɛnˈtɔriən / (AmE), / stɛnˈtɔːrɪən / (BrE)
(of a voice or sound) loud and powerful; deep and resonant.

Examples

stentorian voice
Despite her small frame, her stentorian commands silenced the noisy crowd.
He delivered the news in a stentorian tone that caught everyone's attention.
The teacher's stentorian voice filled the entire auditorium.
