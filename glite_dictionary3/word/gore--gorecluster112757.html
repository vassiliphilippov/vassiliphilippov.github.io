to gore

verb
cut fabric into gores (dressmaking)
Pronunciation: / ɡɔɹ / (AmE), / ɡɔː / (BrE)
to cut (fabric) into triangular pieces called gores, especially to make it fit a particular shape, as in dressmaking

Examples

to gore fabric pieces
For a flattering fit, she recommends goring the panels of the dress.
He gored the fabric carefully to achieve smooth seams.
The dressmaker decided to gore the skirt for a fuller shape.
