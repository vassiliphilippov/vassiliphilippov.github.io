accuser

noun
person who makes an accusation
Pronunciation: / əˈkjuːzər / (AmE), / əˈkjuːzə / (BrE)
a person who claims that someone has committed a wrongdoing or crime; a person who accuses.

Examples

the accuser in court
He listened carefully to what his accuser had to say before responding.
The accuser presented evidence to support her claims in court.
The media interviewed the accuser after the trial ended.
