Alexandrian

noun
native or resident of Alexandria, Egypt
Pronunciation: / ˌælɪɡˈzændriən / (AmE), / ˌalɪɡˈzandriən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Alexandria, especially the city in Egypt

Examples

an Alexandrian resident
Many Alexandrians still celebrate the city's ancient traditions.
She is proud to be an Alexandrian, with a deep connection to the city's rich history.
