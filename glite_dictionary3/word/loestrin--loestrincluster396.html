Loestrin

noun
oral contraceptive brand name
Pronunciation: / loʊˈɛstrɪn / (AmE), / ləʊˈɛstrɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for an oral contraceptive pill containing estradiol and norethindrone.
