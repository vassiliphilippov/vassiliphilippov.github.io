shako

noun
tall military ceremonial hat
Pronunciation: / ˈʃækoʊ / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːkəʊ / (BrE)
a tall, usually cylindrical military hat with a visor, often adorned with a plume, worn especially on ceremonial occasions by members of certain military units
