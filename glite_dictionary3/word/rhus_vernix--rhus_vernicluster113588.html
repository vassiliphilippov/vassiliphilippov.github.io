Rhus vernix

noun
North American shrub known as poison sumac
Pronunciation: / ɹʌs vɜrˈnɪks / (AmE), / ɹʌs ˈvɜːnɪks / (BrE)
A North American swamp shrub, also known as poison sumac, with pinnate leaves, greenish flowers, and greenish-white berries, which produces an irritating oil that can cause skin rashes.
