pertinently

adverb
in a relevant or appropriate way
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrtənəntli / (AmE), / ˈpɜːtɪnəntli / (BrE)
In a way that is relevant or appropriate to the subject being considered.

Examples

pertinently answer a question
His comments were always pertinently focused on the main topic of discussion.
She responded pertinently to the committee's questions, addressing each issue with clarity.
The lawyer argued pertinently that the evidence supported her client's case.

Related Terms

  • antonym – irrelevantly: in an irrelevant manner
  • synonym – relevantly: in a relevant manner
