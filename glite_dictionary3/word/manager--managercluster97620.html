manager

noun
person who trains and directs a sports team or athlete
Pronunciation: / ˈmænədʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒə / (BrE)
A person responsible for supervising and training an athlete or a sports team.

Examples

manager of a team
Her manager helped her improve her performance and win several tournaments.
The manager made a bold decision to change the team's tactics before the final game.
The football club appointed a new manager at the start of the season.
