seaworthy

adjective
fit for sailing at sea
Pronunciation: / ˈsiˌwɝːði / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌwɜːði / (BrE)
(of a ship or boat) in a condition suitable for a sea voyage; fit for sailing safely at sea.

Examples

seaworthy ship
After extensive repairs, the vessel is now considered seaworthy by the authorities.
The ship was thoroughly inspected and declared seaworthy before her maiden voyage.
They refused to sail until a seaworthy boat was provided.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unseaworthy: not fit for sailing at sea
