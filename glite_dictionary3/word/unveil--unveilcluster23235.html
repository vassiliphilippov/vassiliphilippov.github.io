to unveil

verb
reveal or make visible for the first time
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈveɪl / (AmE), / ʌnˈveɪl / (BrE)
to make something known or visible, especially for the first time; to reveal something that was hidden or secret

Examples

to unveil a new product
She decided to unveil her latest painting at the art exhibition.
The company will unveil its new product next week at the conference.
The investigation aims to unveil the truth behind the mysterious disappearance.

Related Terms

  • antonym – conceal: to hide or keep something secret
  • synonym – disclose: to make secret or new information known
  • synonym – reveal: to make something known that was previously secret or hidden
