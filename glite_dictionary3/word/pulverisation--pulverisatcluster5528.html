pulverisation

noun
powder; substance in fine particles
Pronunciation: / ˌpʌlvɚɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpʌlvəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
A solid substance broken down into fine, loose particles through the process of pulverizing.

Examples

pulverisation residue
The pulverisation of the ore made it easier to extract valuable minerals.
