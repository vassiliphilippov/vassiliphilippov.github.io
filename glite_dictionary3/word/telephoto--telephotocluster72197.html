telephoto

noun
photo taken with a telephoto lens
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛləˈfoʊtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈfəʊtəʊ / (BrE)
A photograph taken with a telephoto lens, which allows distant objects to appear closer and larger.

Examples

telephoto of wildlife
He took a stunning telephoto of the eagle perched on a distant branch.
The magazine featured several beautiful telephotos from the safari trip.
Using a telephoto lets photographers capture wildlife without disturbing the animals.
