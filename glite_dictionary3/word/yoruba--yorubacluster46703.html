Yoruba

noun
West African language
Pronunciation: / ˈjɔːrəˌbə, joʊˈruːbə / (AmE), / ˈjɒrʊbə, jəʊˈruːbə / (BrE)
A Niger-Congo language of the Kwa branch spoken by the Yoruba people chiefly in southwestern Nigeria and neighboring regions.
