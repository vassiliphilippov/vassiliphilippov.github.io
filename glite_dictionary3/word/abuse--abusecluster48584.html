to abuse

verb
speak insultingly or offensively
Pronunciation: / əˈbjuːz / (AmE), / əˈbjuːz / (BrE)
to speak to someone in a rude or insulting way, especially using offensive language

Examples

to abuse someone verbally
During the heated argument, she abused her colleague with harsh words.
It is unacceptable to abuse others, even when you are angry or frustrated.
Spectators in the stands abused the referee for his controversial decision.
