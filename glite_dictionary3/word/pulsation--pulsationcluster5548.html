pulsation

noun
rhythmic increase and decrease
Pronunciation: / pʌlˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / pʌlˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
a regularly recurring increase and decrease in the strength or quantity of something, such as sound, light, or pressure

Examples

regular pulsation of sound
Astronomers detected a regular pulsation in the radio signals from the star.
The pulsation of blood in her veins was steady and strong.
The pulsation of the music could be felt through the floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pulse: a single beat or throb, especially of the heart or arteries
