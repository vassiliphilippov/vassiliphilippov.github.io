acridity

noun
bitter or pungent quality
Pronunciation: / əˈkrɪdəti / (AmE), / əˈkrɪdəti / (BrE)
the quality of being bitter or pungent, especially in taste or smell

Examples

acridity of smoke

The acridity of the smoke made it almost impossible to breathe in the room.
You could taste the acridity of the lemon peel in the dessert.
