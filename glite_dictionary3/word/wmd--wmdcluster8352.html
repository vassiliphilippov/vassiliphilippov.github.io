W.M.D.

noun
weapon causing mass casualties (nuclear, chemical, biological)
Pronunciation: / ˌdʌbəl.juː ˌɛm ˈdiː / (AmE), / ˌdʌbəl.juː ˌɛm ˈdiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for weapon of mass destruction; a weapon capable of causing widespread and significant harm or death to large numbers of people, including both civilians and military personnel, especially nuclear, chemical, or biological weapons
