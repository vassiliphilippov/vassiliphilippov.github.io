camo

noun
fabric with camouflage pattern
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkæməʊ / (BrE)
A fabric printed with a camouflage pattern, typically in green, brown, black, and tan, designed to make the wearer less visible in natural surroundings.

Examples

camo jacket
He wore a camo jacket during the hunting trip to blend in with the trees.
The soldiers' uniforms were made of camo to help conceal them in the field.
The store sells backpacks and hats in different camo patterns.
