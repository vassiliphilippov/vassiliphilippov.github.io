planthopper

noun
jumping plant-feeding insect
Pronunciation: / ˈplænthɑpər / (AmE), / ˈplænthɒpə / (BrE)
An insect of various families related to leafhoppers and spittlebugs, typically found on plants and known for their jumping ability, but rarely damaging to cultivated plants.

Examples

planthopper insect species
She studied the planthopper population as part of her entomology research.
The rice fields were teeming with planthoppers after the rainy season.
Unlike some pests, the planthopper seldom causes serious crop damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym – leafhopper: a small jumping insect that feeds on the sap of plants
  • synonym – spittlebug: a small insect known for producing frothy spittle masses on plants
