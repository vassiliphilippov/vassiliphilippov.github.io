dm

noun
private social-media message
Pronunciation: / diː ˈɛm / (AmE), / diː ˈɛm / (BrE)
Informal abbreviation of 'direct message': a private written message sent directly to another user on a social media platform or messaging app.

Examples

send a dm
She sent me a dm with a link to the article I asked for.
If you want the details, please drop them in my dm after the meeting.
The celebrity answered fans' questions during the Q&A through a dm.

Related Terms

  • synonym – direct message: a private message sent directly to another user on a social platform
  • synonym – private message: a message intended for only one recipient rather than a public post
