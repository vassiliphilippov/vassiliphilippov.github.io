intimate

noun
close friend; confidant
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.tɪ.mət / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tɪ.mət / (BrE)

Examples

trusted intimate
He has only a few intimates who truly understand him.

Unlike acquaintances, an intimate can be relied upon in difficult times.
