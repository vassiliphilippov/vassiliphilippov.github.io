marquetry

noun
decorative inlaid work on furniture
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkətri / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪtri / (BrE)
the art or technique of applying pieces of inlaid wood, ivory, or other materials to a surface to form decorative patterns or pictures, especially on furniture

Examples

marquetry furniture design
He specializes in creating custom marquetry for luxury furniture.
The antique cabinet was decorated with exquisite marquetry depicting floral patterns.
The museum's collection includes rare examples of eighteenth-century marquetry.
