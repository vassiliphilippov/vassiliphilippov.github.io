built up

adjective
accumulated or increased over time
Pronunciation: / bɪlt ʌp / (AmE), / bɪlt ʌp / (BrE)
Accumulated or increased gradually over a period of time.

Examples

built up over time
After many years, dust had built up on the old shelves.
Plaque that has built up in your arteries can lead to heart problems.
Stress can become dangerous if it is built up without relief.
