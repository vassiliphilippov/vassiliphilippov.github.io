regularity

noun
consistency; fixed pattern
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛɡjəˈlærəti / (AmE), / ˌrɛɡjʊˈlarɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being consistent, predictable, or occurring at fixed intervals or according to a pattern

Examples

daily regularity
Despite the storm, there was a regularity in the ocean waves that was calming to watch.
She admired the regularity of the train schedule, which made commuting easy.
The regularity of his daily walks contributed to his good health.

Related Terms

  • antonym – irregularity: the quality of lacking consistency, predictability, or a fixed pattern
