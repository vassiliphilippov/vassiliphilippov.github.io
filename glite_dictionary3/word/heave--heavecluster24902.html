heave

noun
ineffectual vomiting spasm
Pronunciation: / hiv / (AmE), / hiːv / (BrE)
an involuntary spasm of retching, often producing little or no vomit

Examples

a dry heave
Another round of heaves hit him, but nothing came up.
She felt a heave in her stomach after spinning around so quickly.
The smell of the garbage gave him a sudden heave before he could get away.
