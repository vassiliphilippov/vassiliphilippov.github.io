sensed

adjective
perceived by instinct or inference
Pronunciation: / sɛnst / (AmE), / sɛnst / (BrE)
perceived by instinct, intuition, or inference rather than by direct observation or obvious external signs

Examples

sensed danger nearby
A sensed presence behind him caused him to turn around suddenly.
She had a sensed feeling that something was wrong, even though there was no clear evidence.
The sensed tension in the room made everyone uncomfortable, though no one spoke about it.

Related Terms

  • synonym – intuitive: understood instinctively, without explicit reasoning
  • synonym – perceived: recognized or understood through the senses or the mind
