extravagancy

noun
quality of being excessive or beyond limits
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstrævəɡənsi / (AmE), / ɪkˈstrævəɡənsi / (BrE)
the quality or state of exceeding reasonable limits, especially in decorum, probability, or truth

Examples

unusual extravagancy
Critics noted the extravagancy of the design, calling it unnecessarily ornate.
Her extravagancy in storytelling often amused her friends.
The extravagancy of his claims made them hard to believe.

Related Terms

  • synonym – extravagance: the quality of being excessive or lacking restraint
  • antonym – moderation: the avoidance of excess or extremes
