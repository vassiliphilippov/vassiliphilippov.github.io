overemotional

adjective
excessively emotional
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚɪˈmoʊʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪˈməʊʃənəl / (BrE)
displaying emotion to an excessive or abnormal degree; too emotional.

Some people say she's overemotional, but I think she's just honest about her feelings.
The movie's script made the characters seem overemotional in even the simplest situations.
