connivance

noun
secret cooperation in wrongdoing
Pronunciation: / kəˈnaɪ.vəns /; / kəˈnaɪvəns / (AmE), / kəˈnaɪ.vəns /; / kəˈnʌɪvəns / (BrE)
secretly helping or allowing something wrong or illegal to happen
