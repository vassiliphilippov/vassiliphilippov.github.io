ventilation

noun
open discussion or debate
Pronunciation: / ˌvɛn.tɪˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌvɛn.tɪˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of discussing or debating a matter thoroughly and openly, especially one of public interest

Examples

public ventilation of issues
A detailed ventilation of the proposal was necessary before a final vote could be taken.
Public ventilation of the issue allowed everyone to express their viewpoints openly.
The ventilation of these controversial policies helped the community reach a consensus.

  • synonym – discussion: the act of talking about something with others
