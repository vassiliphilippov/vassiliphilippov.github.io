sloppy

adjective
soil: soft, wet, and watery
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑpi /; / ˈslɑːpi / (AmE), / ˈslɒpi / (BrE)
soft, wet, or smeared with liquid, often making something messy or difficult to use

Examples

sloppy ground
