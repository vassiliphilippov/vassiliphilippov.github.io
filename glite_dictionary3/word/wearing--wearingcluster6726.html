wearing

noun
the act of wearing clothing or adornment
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɛə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of having something on your body as clothing or an accessory

Examples

wearing a jacket
Daily wearing of sunscreen can help protect your skin from damage.
Her wearing of bright colors made her stand out in the crowd.
The wearing of uniforms is required in many private schools.
