drawee

noun
person or bank to pay a cheque or draft
Pronunciation: / drɔˈi / (AmE), / drɔˈi / (BrE)
the person or bank required to pay a cheque or draft when it is presented for payment

Examples

drawee bank
In this transaction, the bank acted as the drawee for the customer's check.
The check was returned because the drawee refused payment due to insufficient funds.
When presenting a draft, the drawee must verify the authenticity before releasing the money.

Related Terms

  • antonym – drawer: the person who writes a cheque or draft
  • synonym – payee: the person to whom a cheque is made payable
