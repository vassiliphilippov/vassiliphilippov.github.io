Ganges

proper noun
major river in India, sacred in Hinduism
Pronunciation: / ˈɡænˌdʒiːz / (AmE), / ˈɡændʒiːz / (BrE)
A major river in northern India and Bangladesh, considered sacred by Hindus, flowing from the Himalayas to the Bay of Bengal.
