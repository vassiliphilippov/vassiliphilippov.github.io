burning

noun
pain that feels like heat or fire
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
A sensation of pain that feels hot and as if it were on fire.

Examples

burning pain sensation
He described a constant burning in his lower back after the accident.
Many people with sunburn experience an intense burning that lasts for hours.
Patients often report a burning in the eyes when exposed to smoke.
