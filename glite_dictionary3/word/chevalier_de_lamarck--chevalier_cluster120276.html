Chevalier de Lamarck

proper noun
French naturalist; inheritance of acquired traits theory
Pronunciation: / ʃəˌvaljeɪ də ləˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ʃəˌvalɪeɪ də ləˈmɑːk / (BrE)
(1744–1829) A French naturalist who proposed that evolutionary changes result from the inheritance of acquired characteristics.

Examples

Chevalier de Lamarck theory of evolution
Chevalier de Lamarck was among the earliest scientists to propose a theory of evolution.
