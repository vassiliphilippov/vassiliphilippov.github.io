vengefulness

noun
desire for revenge
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛndʒfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈvɛndʒfəlnəs / (BrE)
a strong and persistent desire to seek revenge or retaliate for a perceived wrong

Examples

deep vengefulness
Her vengefulness knew no bounds after being betrayed by her closest friend.
Instead of vengefulness, she chose to forgive and move forward with her life.
There was a cold vengefulness in his eyes whenever he spoke of his rivals.
