precious

adverb
used to emphasize 'very' or 'extremely'
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛʃəs / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃ.əs / (BrE)
(informal) Used to emphasize that there is very little of something or that something is very true; very, extremely.

Examples

precious little time
He made precious little progress with his homework over the weekend.
There was precious little hope of success after the announcement.
We have precious few options left at this point.

Related Terms

  • synonym – scarcely: barely; only just
  • synonym – very: to a high degree; extremely
