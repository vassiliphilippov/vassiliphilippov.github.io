taximeter

noun
device calculating taxi fares
Pronunciation: / ˈtæksɪˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈtæksɪˌmiːtə / (BrE)
a device installed in a taxi that calculates and displays the fare according to the distance traveled or time spent on a journey

Examples

taximeter shows fare
After an hour-long trip, the taximeter showed a surprisingly high fare.
Make sure the taximeter is running to avoid disputes about the fare.
The taximeter started as soon as the driver pulled away from the curb.
