Mark Twain

proper noun
American writer and humorist (1835–1910)
Pronunciation: / mɑrk ˈtweɪn / (AmE), / mɑːk ˈtweɪn / (BrE)
The pen name of Samuel Langhorne Clemens (1835–1910), a celebrated American writer and humorist known for works such as 'The Adventures of Tom Sawyer' and 'Adventures of Huckleberry Finn'.
