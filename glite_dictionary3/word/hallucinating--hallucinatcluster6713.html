hallucinating

adjective
experiencing hallucinations
Pronunciation: / həˈluːsɪˌneɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / həˈluːsɪneɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
experiencing hallucinations or delirium, often seeing or hearing things that are not present

Examples

hallucinating strange things
After three sleepless nights, he began hallucinating strange figures in the darkness.
She sounded hallucinating when describing the imaginary voices she heard.
The patient was hallucinating and reported seeing vivid colors on the walls.

Related Terms

  • synonym – delirious: in a disturbed state of mind, often with confused thinking
  • antonym – lucid: clear-minded; able to think clearly
