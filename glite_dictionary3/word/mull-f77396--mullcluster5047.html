to mull

verb
heat a drink with sugar and spices
Pronunciation: / mʌl / (AmE), / mʌl / (BrE)
to heat (a drink, especially wine or cider) with sugar and spices to make it hot and flavorful

Examples

to mull hot wine
During the winter, many people mull cider as a comforting evening treat.
She decided to mull some red wine with cloves and cinnamon for the holiday party.
The chef mulled the beverage to bring out a warm, fragrant flavor.
