microscopy

noun
use of microscopes for scientific study
Pronunciation: / maɪˈkrɑːskəpi / (AmE), / mɪˈkrɒskəpi / (BrE)
the scientific study or technique of using microscopes to observe objects too small to be seen by the naked eye
