to embroider

verb
decorate with needlework
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbrɔɪdər / (AmE), / ɪmˈbrɔɪdə / (BrE)
to decorate cloth or fabric with patterns or images using needlework

Examples

to embroider a shirt
