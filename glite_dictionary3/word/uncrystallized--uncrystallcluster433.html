uncrystallized

adjective
not fully formed or definite
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrɪstəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrɪstəlaɪzd / (BrE)
not fully formed or definite; lacking a clear or final structure or form.

Examples

uncrystallized ideas
Her plans for the future are still uncrystallized and open to change.
His thoughts on the subject remained uncrystallized despite days of consideration.
The committee discussed several uncrystallized proposals at the meeting.
