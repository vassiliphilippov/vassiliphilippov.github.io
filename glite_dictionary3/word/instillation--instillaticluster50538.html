instillation

noun
drop-by-drop introduction of liquid
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.stəˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.stɪˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of introducing a liquid drop by drop, especially into a body part or cavity

Examples

instillation of eye drops
Regular instillation of medication was necessary to treat the patient's ear infection.
The instillation of eye drops should be done carefully to avoid contamination.
The nurse explained the instillation procedure before beginning the treatment.

Related Terms

  • synonym – injection: the act of forcing liquid into something, usually with a syringe
