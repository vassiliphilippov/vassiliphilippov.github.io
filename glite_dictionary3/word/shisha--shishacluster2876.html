shisha

noun
water pipe for smoking tobacco
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːʃə / (AmE), / ˈʃiːʃə / (BrE)
An oriental water pipe with a long flexible tube for smoking, where the smoke is passed through a water vessel before inhalation.

Examples

shisha pipe
He learned how to prepare the shisha before lighting the coals.
The lounge offered a wide selection of flavored shisha.
We enjoyed smoking shisha at the café after dinner.
