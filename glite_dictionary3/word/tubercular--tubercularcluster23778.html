tubercular

adjective
relating to a tubercle or tuberosity
Pronunciation: / tuˈbɜrkjələr / (AmE), / tjuːˈbɜːkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or having the form or nature of a tubercle or tuberosity, especially in anatomy.

Examples

tubercular bone structure
An injury to the tubercular region can affect muscle movement.
The bone has a tubercular surface for the attachment of tendons.
The radiologist noted a tubercular prominence on the X-ray image.
