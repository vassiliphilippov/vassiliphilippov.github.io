pictured

adjective
seen in the mind as an image
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪktʃərd / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃəd / (BrE)
seen or imagined in the mind; formed as a mental image.

Examples

he pictured victory
He often pictured himself winning the gold medal.
She described the city exactly as she had pictured it in her dreams.
The scene was more beautiful than I had ever pictured in my mind.
