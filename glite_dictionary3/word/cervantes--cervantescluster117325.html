Cervantes

proper noun
Spanish novelist and author of 'Don Quixote'
Pronunciation: / sərˈvɑːntiz / (AmE), / səˈvɑːnteɪz / (BrE)
(1547–1616) Spanish writer famous for 'Don Quixote', which satirizes chivalry and influenced the development of the modern novel.
