charity

noun
kindly and lenient attitude toward people
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛrəti / (AmE), / ˈtʃærəti / (BrE)
a kindly and forgiving attitude toward others, showing generosity of spirit and tolerance

Examples

show charity to others
He spoke with remarkable charity about his opponent, praising his good intentions.
In moments of frustration, it is easy to forget the importance of charity toward others.
She treated her critics with charity, never holding a grudge against them.

  • synonym – leniency: the quality of being more merciful or tolerant than expected
