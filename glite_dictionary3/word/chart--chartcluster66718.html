chart

noun
music ranking list
Pronunciation: / tʃɑrt / (AmE), / tʃɑːt / (BrE)
a listing, usually published weekly, of the most popular or best-selling recorded music

Examples

music chart
Making it into the charts is a significant milestone for any debut artist.
That album stayed at the top of the charts for five consecutive weeks.
The singer's new single quickly climbed the charts after its release.
