queen

noun
face card in a deck, picture of a queen
Pronunciation: / kwin / (AmE), / kwiːn / (BrE)
one of four playing cards in a standard deck, each bearing the image of a queen and ranked between jack and king.

Examples

queen of hearts
If you play the queen now, you might win the trick.
She drew the queen of hearts from the deck and smiled.
The queen is ranked between the king and the jack in most card games.

Related Terms

  • degree lower – jack: face card showing a young man or servant, ranked below queen
  • degree higher – king: face card showing a king, ranked highest in its suit
