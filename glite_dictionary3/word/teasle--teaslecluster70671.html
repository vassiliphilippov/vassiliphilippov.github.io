teasle

noun
spiny-flowered plant of the Dipsacus genus
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːzəl / (AmE), / ˈtiːz(ə)l / (BrE)
any of several tall herbs of the genus Dipsacus, native to the Old World, with dense, spiny flower heads often used historically in textile processing
