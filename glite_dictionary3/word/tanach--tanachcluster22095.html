Tanach

proper noun
the Jewish scriptures; Hebrew Bible
Pronunciation: / təˈnɑx / (AmE), / təˈnax / (BrE)
the canonical collection of Jewish scriptures consisting of the Torah, the Prophets (Nevi'im), and the Writings (Ketuvim)
