chip

noun
thin, crisp slice of fried potato
Pronunciation: / tʃɪp / (AmE), / tʃɪp / (BrE)
A thin, crisp slice of potato that is fried in deep fat and eaten as a snack or side dish.

Examples

chip with dip

She ordered fish and chips at the seaside café.
Would you like some chips with your sandwich?
