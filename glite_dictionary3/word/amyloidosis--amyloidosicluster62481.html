amyloidosis

noun
disease: abnormal amyloid protein deposits
Pronunciation: / ˌæm.ə.lɔɪˈdoʊ.sɪs / (AmE), / ˌam.ɪ.lɔɪˈdəʊ.sɪs / (BrE)
a rare medical condition in which abnormal protein (amyloid) deposits build up in organs or tissues, disrupting their normal function
