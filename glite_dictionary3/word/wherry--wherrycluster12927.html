wherry

noun
a type of light rowboat
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛri / (AmE), / ˈwɛri / (BrE)
a light, narrow rowboat used for racing or for carrying goods and passengers, especially in rivers, harbors, and inland waters

Examples

racing wherry
During the regatta, each team raced a sleek wherry across the lake.
The fishermen loaded their catch into the wherry and rowed back to the harbor.
Tourists enjoyed a leisurely ride down the river in a traditional wherry.
