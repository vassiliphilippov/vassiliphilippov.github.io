sharpness

noun
strong, biting taste or smell
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːrpnəs / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːpnəs / (BrE)
the quality in a taste or smell that is strong, biting, or pungent

Examples

sharpness of flavor
She couldn't stand the sharpness of the blue cheese aroma.
The sharpness of the ginger lingered on his palate.
The vinegar's sharpness added depth to the salad dressing.
