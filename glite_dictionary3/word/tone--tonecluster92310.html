tone

noun
musical notation for pitch and duration
Pronunciation: / toʊn / (AmE), / təʊn / (BrE)
A symbol or mark in musical notation that indicates the pitch and duration of a sound to be played or sung.

Examples

tone symbol music
Each tone on the sheet music corresponds to a specific sound on the instrument.
He struggled to read the tone at the beginning of the passage.
The composer carefully wrote every tone to match the lyrics.
