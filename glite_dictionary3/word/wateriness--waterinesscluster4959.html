wateriness

noun
quality of being watery or thin
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔːtɚinəs /; / ˈwɔt̬ɚi nɪs /; / ˈwɔtəri.nəs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtərɪnɪs /; / ˈwɔːtəri nɪs /; / ˈwɔːtəri.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being watery, thin, or lacking substance, either literally (like food, soil, or liquids) or figuratively (like arguments or ideas)
