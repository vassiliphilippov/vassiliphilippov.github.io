bureaucratically

adverb
in a bureaucratic manner
Pronunciation: / bjʊˌroʊkrəˈtɪkli / (AmE), / bjʊəˌrəʊkrəˈtɪkli /; / bjʊəˌrɒkrəˈtɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that follows bureaucratic methods, rules, or procedures, often involving excessive paperwork or rigid processes

Examples

The process was handled bureaucratically, causing months of unnecessary delay.
