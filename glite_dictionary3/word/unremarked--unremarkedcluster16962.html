unremarked

adjective
not noticed or commented on
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈmɑrkt / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈmɑːkt / (BrE)
(of something) not noticed or commented on; passing without attention.

Examples

unremarked by others
Her small acts of kindness often passed unremarked by those around her.
His quiet departure went unremarked by anyone at the party.
Several key issues remained unremarked throughout the lengthy meeting.

  • synonym – unnoticed: not seen or observed; overlooked
