endocrinology

noun
study of the endocrine system and hormones
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.doʊ.krəˈnɑ.lə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌɛn.dəʊ.krɪˈnɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
The branch of medicine and biology concerned with the endocrine glands, their secretions (hormones), and related disorders.
