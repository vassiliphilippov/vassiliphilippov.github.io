spin

noun
a rapid turning or whirling movement
Pronunciation: / spɪn / (AmE), / spɪn / (BrE)
a rapid turning or whirling movement; the act of turning around quickly (including a rapid, spiraling descent of an aircraft)

Examples

rapid spin
