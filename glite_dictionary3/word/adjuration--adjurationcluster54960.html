adjuration

noun
solemn, earnest appeal or urging
Pronunciation: / ˌædʒəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌadʒəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)

Examples

The knight made an adjuration to his companions, urging them not to lose hope.
