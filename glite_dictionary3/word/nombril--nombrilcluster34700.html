nombril

noun
heraldic center point on a shield
Pronunciation: / nɑmˈbriːl / (AmE), / ˈnɒm.brɪl / (BrE)
In heraldry, the point on a shield located midway between the base and the center.

Examples

nombril point on shield
A red circle was placed on the nombril to symbolize the heart of the family.
In traditional heraldry, the nombril is distinct from the exact center of the shield.
The knight’s coat of arms featured a star at the nombril of the shield.
