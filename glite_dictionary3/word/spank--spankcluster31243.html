to spank

verb
hit on the bottom as punishment
Pronunciation: / spæŋk / (AmE), / spæŋk / (BrE)
to hit someone, usually a child, on their bottom with an open hand as a punishment or to discipline them

Examples

to spank a child
He spanked his son lightly for running into the street.
Some parents still choose to spank their children as a form of discipline.
The teacher threatened to spank the mischievous student if he didn't behave.

  • synonym – slap: to hit someone or something with the flat part of the hand or an object
