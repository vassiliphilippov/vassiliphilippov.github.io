squatting

noun
crouching with knees bent near heels
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɑːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskwɒtɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of assuming or maintaining a crouching position with the knees bent and the buttocks close to the heels.

Examples

deep squatting position
Extended squatting can be difficult if you are not used to it.
Farmers use squatting while working in the fields to stay close to the crops.
He injured his knees from too much squatting during weightlifting practice.

  • synonym – crouching: the act of bending your knees and bringing your body close to the ground
