naturally

adverb
in a natural or normal manner
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃ.ɚ.li / (AmE), / ˈnætʃ.ə.li / (BrE)
In a natural or normal way, without artificiality or effort.

Examples

naturally react to news
He responded naturally to the surprising news, showing genuine excitement.
She moved so naturally on stage that the audience forgot she was acting.
The children naturally picked up the language after a few months of living abroad.

Related Terms

  • antonym – artificially: in a way that is not natural; with effort or contrivance
