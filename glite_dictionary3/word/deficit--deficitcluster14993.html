deficit

noun
the shortfall when liabilities exceed assets
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛfɪsɪt /; / ˈdɛfəsɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɛfɪsɪt / (BrE)
an amount by which something is too small or less than it should be, especially money, resources, or time
