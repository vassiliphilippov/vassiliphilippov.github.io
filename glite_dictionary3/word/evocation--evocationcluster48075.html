evocation

noun
summoning supernatural forces by spells
Pronunciation: / ˌiː.voʊˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌiː.vəˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of calling forth supernatural forces or spirits, often by means of spells or incantations

Examples

evocation of spirits
During the ritual, the group chanted in unison in hopes of a successful evocation.
Tales of witches often involve the evocation of demons or other supernatural beings.
The priest performed an evocation to summon the ancient spirits of the forest.
