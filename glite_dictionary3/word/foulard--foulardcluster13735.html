foulard

noun
light silk or silk-like fabric
Pronunciation: / fuːˈlɑrd / (AmE), / fuːˈlɑːd / (BrE)
A light, soft fabric with a plain or twill weave, typically made of silk or a silk-like material, often printed with a pattern.

Examples

silk foulard scarf
Delicate foulards are often used for making scarves and ties.
She wore a bright foulard around her neck to complement her dress.
The dress was made from a printed silk foulard that felt cool against the skin.
