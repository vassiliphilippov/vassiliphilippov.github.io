to manifest

verb
to list officially (cargo/passengers)
Pronunciation: / ˈmænəˌfɛst / (AmE), / ˈmænɪfɛst / (BrE)
to record or list (cargo, passengers, etc.) in an official manifest, especially for a ship or aircraft

Examples

to manifest cargo
Cargo that isn't manifested cannot legally be transported overseas.
Every pallet must be manifested before loading onto the ship.
The crew carefully manifested each passenger and their belongings.

Related Terms

  • synonym – register: to record formally or officially on a list
