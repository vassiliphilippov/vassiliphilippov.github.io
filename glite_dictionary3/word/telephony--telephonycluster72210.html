telephony

noun
transmission of voice over distance
Pronunciation: / təˈlɛfəni / (AmE), / tɪˈlɛfəni / (BrE)
The technology or system of transmitting voice or spoken communication over a distance, especially by telephone.

Examples

modern telephony system
Advancements in telephony have made international calls more accessible and affordable.
Digital telephony systems offer clearer sound quality compared to older analog technologies.
The company specializes in cloud-based telephony solutions for small businesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym – telephone: a device for transmitting speech over distance
