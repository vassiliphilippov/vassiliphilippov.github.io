anteater

noun
mammal that eats ants and termites
Pronunciation: / ˈænˌtitər /; / ˈænˌtiːtər / (AmE), / ˈænˌtiːtə / (BrE)
a mammal with a long snout and tongue, specialized for eating ants and termites, found in Central and South America; includes the giant anteater and similar species
