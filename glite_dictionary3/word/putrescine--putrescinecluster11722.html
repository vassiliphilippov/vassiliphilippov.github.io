putrescine

noun
foul-smelling compound from decaying tissue
Pronunciation: / pjuˈtrɛˌsin / (AmE), / pjuˈtrɛsiːn / (BrE)
a colorless, crystalline compound with a foul odor, produced during the decomposition of animal tissue

Examples

putrescine in decaying meat
During decomposition, putrescine is one of the first compounds to be produced from amino acids.
