neediness

noun
excessive need for attention or affection
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdinəs / (AmE), / ˈniːdɪnɪs / (BrE)
the state of requiring excessive attention, affection, or reassurance from others

Examples

emotional neediness
His neediness often made friends feel overwhelmed and exhausted.
She worked hard to overcome her neediness and become more independent.
Some people are put off by excessive neediness in relationships.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dependency: excessive reliance on others for emotional support
  • antonym – independence: freedom from the need for support or help from others
