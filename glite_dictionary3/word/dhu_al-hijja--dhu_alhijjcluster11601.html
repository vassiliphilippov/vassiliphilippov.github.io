Dhu al-Hijja

noun
twelfth month of the Islamic calendar
Pronunciation: / ˌduː əlˈhɪdʒə / (AmE), / ˌduː əlˈhɪdʒə / (BrE)
the twelfth month of the Islamic calendar, during which the pilgrimage to Mecca (hajj) is performed; has an extra day in leap years

Examples

Dhu al-Hijja pilgrimage month
In some years, Dhu al-Hijja has one extra day to adjust for the lunar calendar.
Muslims travel to Mecca during Dhu al-Hijja to perform their annual pilgrimage.
The start of Dhu al-Hijja marks a significant period in the Islamic calendar.
