cithern

noun
old pear-shaped stringed instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪðɚn / (AmE), / ˈsɪθən / (BrE)
a 16th-century stringed musical instrument resembling a guitar, with a pear-shaped soundbox and wire strings
