misestimation

noun
an incorrect calculation or judgment
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˌɛstəˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˌɛstɪˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
An incorrect calculation or assessment; a mistake made when estimating or judging.

Examples

serious misestimation
A single misestimation can have far-reaching consequences in scientific research.
His misestimation of the risks led to serious financial losses for the company.
The project's delay was largely due to a misestimation of the resources required.
