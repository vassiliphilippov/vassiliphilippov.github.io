to bespot

verb
mark with spots
Pronunciation: / bɪˈspɑt / (AmE), / bɪˈspɒt / (BrE)
to mark with spots or as if with spots

Examples

to bespot a surface
Over time, mildew and moss bespot the old stone wall.
Raindrops bespotted the windshield as the storm began.
The painter accidentally bespotted the canvas with drops of red paint.
