to eclipse

verb
surpass in importance or prominence
Pronunciation: / ɪˈklɪps / (AmE), / ɪˈklɪps / (BrE)
to make someone or something seem less important or less impressive by comparison

Examples

eclipse a famous person
Her achievements quickly eclipsed those of her colleagues.
His generosity eclipses anything I've seen before.
The band's fame eventually eclipsed that of their mentors.

Related Terms

  • synonym – outshine: to be much more impressive or important than someone or something
  • synonym – overshadow: to make someone or something seem less important by being more successful or impressive
