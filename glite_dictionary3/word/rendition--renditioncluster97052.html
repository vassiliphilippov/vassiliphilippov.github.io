rendition

noun
digital rendering output
Pronunciation: / rɛnˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / rɛnˈdɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a digital image, video, or audio file produced by a computer rendering process, especially in 3D graphics, architecture, animation, or visual effects.

Examples

3D rendition image
The architecture firm shared a high-quality rendition of the proposed building after the rendering upgrade.
After hours of tweaking materials, the animator produced a photorealistic rendition of the creature.
Students submitted three different renditions of the 3D model for the design course critique.

Related Terms

  • synonym – rendering: the process of producing an image or video from a model by means of computer software
  • synonym – visualization: the process or result of representing designs or data visually, often using computer graphics
