deficit

noun
the margin by which one is behind in a game
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛfəsɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɛfɪsɪt / (BrE)
(sports) the amount by which a team or individual is trailing in the score during a game or competition.

Examples

overcome a deficit
Despite the early deficit, she remained confident and fought back to tie the match.
The team overcame a ten-point deficit in the second half to win the game.
They couldn't recover from the goal deficit before the final whistle blew.

  • antonym – lead: the score by which a team is ahead in a game
