askance

adverb
with suspicion or disapproval
Pronunciation: / əˈskæns / (AmE), / əˈskɑːns / (BrE)
with a look or attitude of suspicion, disapproval, or doubt, often sideways

Examples

look askance at news

She viewed the newcomer's odd behavior askance.
The manager glanced askance at my request, clearly not convinced.

The teacher regarded his explanation askance, clearly doubting its truth.
Whenever he mentioned his grand plans, his friends glanced askance at each other.

Whenever he hears gossip, he tends to glance askance at those spreading the rumors.
