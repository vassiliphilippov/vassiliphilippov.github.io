intervening

adjective
occurring between events or times
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈviniŋ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈviːnɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a period or event) occurring or situated between other events or points in time.

Examples

intervening years
During the intervening years, the city grew rapidly.
Little progress was made in the intervening period before negotiations resumed.
Many changes took place in the intervening months between the two meetings.
