tichodrome

noun
crimson-and-grey cliff-dwelling songbird
Pronunciation: / ˌtɪkəˈdroʊm / (AmE), / ˌtɪkəˈdrəʊm / (BrE)
a small crimson-and-grey songbird (Tichodroma muraria) that lives on cliffs and town walls in southern Eurasia and northern Africa
