Bahai

noun
a follower of the Bahá'í faith
Pronunciation: / bəˈhaɪ / (AmE), / bəˈhɑːi / (BrE)
a follower or adherent of the Bahá'í faith, a monotheistic religion emphasizing the spiritual unity of humankind
