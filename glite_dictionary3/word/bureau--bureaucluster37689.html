bureau

noun
government department or agency
Pronunciation: / ˈbjʊr.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈbjʊə.rəʊ / (BrE)
a government department or agency responsible for a particular area of administration

Examples

bureau of statistics
Each bureau reports its findings to the central authority on a monthly basis.
She works as an analyst for the Federal Investigation Bureau.
The Environmental Protection Bureau issued new regulations last week.
