dacryocyst

noun
dilated end of the lacrimal (tear) duct
Pronunciation: / ˈdækri.oʊˌsɪst / (AmE), / ˈdakrɪəˌsɪst / (BrE)
the dilated end of the lacrimal duct at the nasal corner of the eye that collects tears from the lacrimal glands
