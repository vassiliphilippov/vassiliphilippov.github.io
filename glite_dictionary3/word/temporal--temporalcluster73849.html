temporal

adjective
of or relating to the temples (skull)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpərəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərəl / (BrE)
Relating to the temples, the sides of the head behind the eyes.

Examples

temporal bone
He suffered a fracture to the temporal bone after the accident.
The temporal arteries can be palpated just in front of the ears.
There was bruising over the temporal region of her head.
