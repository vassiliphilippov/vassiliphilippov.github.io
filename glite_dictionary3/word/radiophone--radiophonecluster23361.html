radiophone

noun
telephone using radio waves
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdiˌoʊfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊfəʊn / (BrE)
A telephone that transmits voice or data using radio waves instead of wires or cables.
