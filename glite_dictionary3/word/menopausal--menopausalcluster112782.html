menopausal

adjective
relating to menopause
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛnəˈpɔzəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛnəˈpɔːzəl / (BrE)
Relating to or affected by the menopause—the time in a woman's life when menstruation ceases permanently.

Examples

menopausal symptoms
Many women experience menopausal symptoms such as hot flashes and night sweats.
She joined a support group for menopausal women to share experiences and advice.
The doctor recommended regular exercise to help manage menopausal changes.
