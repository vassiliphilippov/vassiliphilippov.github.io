childlike

adjective
having qualities of a child (innocence/trust)
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪldˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪldˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the good qualities of a child, such as innocence, trust, or simplicity (especially when describing an adult)
