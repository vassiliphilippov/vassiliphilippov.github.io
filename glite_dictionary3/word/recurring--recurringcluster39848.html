recurring

adjective
happening again or repeatedly
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɜrɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈkɜːrɪŋ / (BrE)
happening or appearing again, especially repeatedly or periodically over time.

Examples

recurring nightmares
He suffers from recurring nightmares that disrupt his sleep several times a week.
Seasonal allergies are a recurring problem for many people each year.
The team meets on a recurring basis to discuss ongoing projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym – periodic: occurring at regular intervals
  • antonym – sporadic: occurring at irregular intervals, not consistent or steady
