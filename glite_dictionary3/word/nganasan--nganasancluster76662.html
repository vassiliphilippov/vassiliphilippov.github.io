Nganasan

noun
member of an indigenous Siberian people
Pronunciation: / ˌŋɑːnəˈsɑːn / (AmE), / ˌŋanəˈsɑːn / (BrE)
A member of a Samoyedic people living on the Taimyr Peninsula in northern Siberia.
