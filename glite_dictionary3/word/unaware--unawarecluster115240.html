unaware

adjective
not aware; not knowing something
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈwɛr / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈweə / (BrE)
not knowing or noticing something; not having knowledge or awareness of something, often used with 'of'.

Examples

unaware of danger
He remained unaware of the criticism directed at his recent statements.
Many people are unaware of the environmental impact of their daily choices.
She walked into the room, completely unaware that everyone was waiting to surprise her.

Related Terms

  • antonym – aware: having knowledge or perception of a situation or fact
  • synonym – ignorant: lacking knowledge or awareness in general or about something
