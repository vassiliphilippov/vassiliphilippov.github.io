multicolour

adjective
having multiple colors
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈmʌltɪˌkʌlə / (BrE)
Having sections, patches, or patterns of different colors, especially bright or varied ones.

Examples

multicolour scarf
She wore a multicolour scarf that brightened up her entire outfit.
The children's toys were arranged in a cheerful, multicolour display on the shelves.
They painted the wall in a multicolour pattern to create a lively, playful atmosphere.
