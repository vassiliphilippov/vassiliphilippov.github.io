Illecebrum verticillatum

noun
a European annual plant with white flower clusters
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛsəˌbrʌm ˌvɜːrtɪsɪˈleɪtəm / (AmE), / ɪˌliːsɪˌbrʊm ˌvɜːtɪsɪˈleɪtəm / (BrE)
