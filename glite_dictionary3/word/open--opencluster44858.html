open

adjective
not defended; vulnerable to attack
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.pən /; / ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.pən /; / ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
not protected, shielded, or defended; exposed or vulnerable to harm or attack

Examples

open to attack
After the army retreated, the borders were left open to invasion.
The commander declared the city open to avoid unnecessary destruction.
With its defenses down, the fortress became open to enemy forces.
An open wound should be cleaned immediately to prevent infection.
She left the window open during the storm, letting rain into the room.
The campsite was completely open to the weather, so we needed extra blankets.
