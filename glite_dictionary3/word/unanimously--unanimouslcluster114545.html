unanimously

adverb
with complete agreement
Pronunciation: / juˈnænəməsli / (AmE), / juˈnænɪməsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows complete agreement by everyone involved; without dissent.

Examples

unanimously approved the plan
All the members unanimously agreed to postpone the meeting.
The board voted unanimously to implement the new policy.
The resolution was unanimously adopted by the committee.
