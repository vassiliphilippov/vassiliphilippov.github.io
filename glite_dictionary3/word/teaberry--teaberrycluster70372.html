teaberry

noun
spicy red berry; source of wintergreen oil
Pronunciation: / ˈtiˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈtiːˌbɛri / (BrE)
a low-growing North American shrub with spicy red berries and aromatic leaves, or its small red fruit, both used as a source of wintergreen oil
