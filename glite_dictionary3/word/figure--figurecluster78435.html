to figure

verb
to think or believe as likely
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡjə / (BrE)
To think or believe that something is likely or probable; to conclude or assume based on reasoning.

Examples

to figure the answer
I figured you would want to know about the meeting.
She figures it will take about an hour to finish the report.
We figure they are arriving around seven based on the schedule.
