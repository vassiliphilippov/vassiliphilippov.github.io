to diworsify

verb
make worse by poor diversification
Pronunciation: / daɪˈwɜːrsɪfaɪ / (AmE), / daɪˈwɔːsɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to make something worse by diversifying, especially by adding investments or projects that reduce overall quality or performance
