Arctostaphylos

noun
genus of shrubs (bearberry, manzanita)
Pronunciation: / ɑrktoʊˈstæfɪloʊs / (AmE), / ɑːktəʊˈstæfɪlɒs / (BrE)
A genus of shrubs in the heath family commonly known as bearberry and manzanita, native to North America.
