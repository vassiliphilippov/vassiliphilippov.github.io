perspicacity

noun
keen insight or perceptiveness
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜrspɪˈkæsəti / (AmE), / ˌpɜːspɪˈkæsəti /; / ˌpɜːspɪˈkæsɪti / (BrE)
the ability to notice, understand, or judge things quickly and accurately; keen insight
