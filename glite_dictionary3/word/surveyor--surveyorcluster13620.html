surveyor

noun
person who measures land boundaries and features
Pronunciation: / sərˈveɪ.ər / (AmE), / səˈveɪ.ə / (BrE)
an engineer or professional who measures and determines the boundaries, features, and elevations of land or structures

Examples

surveyor measures land
A team of surveyors was hired to determine the elevation of the new road site.
The surveyor carefully mapped out the property boundaries before construction began.
The landowner consulted a surveyor to resolve a dispute with his neighbor.
