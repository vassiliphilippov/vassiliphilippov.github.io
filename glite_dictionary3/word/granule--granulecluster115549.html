granule

noun
tiny grain or particle
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræn.juːl / (AmE), / ˈɡran.juːl / (BrE)
a small particle or grain, especially one of a substance such as sand, sugar, or salt

Examples

sugar granule
The medicine comes in granule form and should be mixed with water before taking.
The sugar dissolved quickly because it was made up of fine granules.
You could see tiny granules of sand stuck to her feet after walking on the beach.
