to fluoridize

verb
add fluoride to (water or teeth)
Pronunciation: / ˈflʊr.ɪˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈflʊər.ɪˌdaɪz / (BrE)
to add fluoride to something, especially to water or teeth, to help prevent dental decay

Examples

to fluoridize drinking water
Experts debate whether it is necessary to fluoridize all public drinking water.
Many communities fluoridize their water supplies to combat tooth decay.
The dentist recommended we fluoridize our children's teeth during routine check-ups.
