to spearhead

verb
to lead or initiate something
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪərˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈspɪəˌhɛd / (BrE)
to lead or initiate an activity, movement, or attack

Examples

to spearhead a project
A passionate team is spearheading the development of renewable energy technologies at the university.
Dr. Lin will spearhead the campaign to improve community health services in the area.
The city mayor spearheaded several initiatives to promote environmental sustainability last year.

Related Terms

  • antonym – follow: to go or come after a leader or guide
  • synonym – lead: to go before or guide in direction, course, action, opinion, etc.
