fielding

noun
handling the ball while defending (sports)
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːl.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfiːl.dɪŋ / (BrE)
The action of playing in the field and handling the ball, especially in baseball or cricket, in order to prevent the opposing team from scoring.

Examples

excellent fielding skills
Good fielding can make all the difference in a close baseball game.
Her exceptional fielding saved several runs for the team that day.
The coach praised the squad’s fielding skills after their flawless performance.
