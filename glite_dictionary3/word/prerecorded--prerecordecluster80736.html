prerecorded

adjective
recorded in advance
Pronunciation: / ˌpriː.rɪˈkɔːr.dɪd / (AmE), / ˌpriː.rɪˈkɔː.dɪd / (BrE)
(of sound or video) recorded in advance, especially for later broadcast or presentation.

Examples

prerecorded message
A prerecorded message will play after the tone.
The show included several prerecorded interviews with experts.
They chose a prerecorded soundtrack instead of a live orchestra.
