feature

noun
special newspaper or magazine article
Pronunciation: / ˈfitʃɚ /; / ˈfiːtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈfiːtʃə / (BrE)
a special article in a newspaper or magazine, or a product or item that is highlighted or given special attention

Examples

special feature article
Our newspaper is planning a Sunday feature exploring climate change solutions.
She was interviewed for a feature about women in science.
The magazine published a feature on local artists in its April issue.
Retailers often select one or two items as a feature to attract customers' attention.
The supermarket placed the new cereal as a feature near the front entrance.
This week's feature is a buy-one-get-one-free deal on laundry detergent.
