hotspur

noun
rash or impetuous person
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtˌspɝ / (AmE), / ˈhɒt.spɜː / (BrE)
A person who is rash, impetuous, or eager, especially one quick to act without thinking.

Examples

reckless hotspur
He was a real hotspur, always rushing into things without thinking them through.
In the debate, she played the hotspur, never hesitating to challenge the opposition.
The young knight earned the nickname hotspur for his impulsive actions on the battlefield.

Related Terms

  • synonym – firebrand: a person who stirs up trouble or ignites passions
  • synonym – impulsive: acting or done without forethought
  • degree lower – reckless: without regard to danger or consequences
