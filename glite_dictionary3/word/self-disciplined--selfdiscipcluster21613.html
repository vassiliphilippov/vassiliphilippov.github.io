self-disciplined

adjective
having self-control and restraint
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈdɪs.əˌplɪnd / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈdɪs.ɪˌplɪnd / (BrE)
able to control one's own feelings and overcome one's weaknesses; showing restraint and the ability to follow one's own rules or standards.

Examples

self-disciplined student
A self-disciplined person can stick to a diet even when tempted by sweets.
Becoming self-disciplined helped him resist distractions and focus on his studies.
She is remarkably self-disciplined, waking up early every day to exercise before work.

Related Terms

  • antonym – impulsive: acting or done without forethought
  • synonym – self-controlled: having the ability to control one's emotions and actions
