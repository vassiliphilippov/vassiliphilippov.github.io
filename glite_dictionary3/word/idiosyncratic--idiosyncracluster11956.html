idiosyncratic

adjective
peculiar or distinctive to an individual
Pronunciation: / ˌɪdi.oʊsɪnˈkrætɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪdi.əʊsɪnˈkrætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a particular individual; highly distinctive or peculiar to one person or thing
