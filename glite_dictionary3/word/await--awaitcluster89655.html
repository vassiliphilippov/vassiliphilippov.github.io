to await

verb
wait for; look forward to
Pronunciation: / əˈweɪt / (AmE), / əˈweɪt / (BrE)
to wait for or look forward to the occurrence of something, especially with anticipation

Examples

to await good news
A warm welcome awaits all visitors to the museum.
She awaited news from the hospital with growing anxiety.
We eagerly await the release of the new book next month.

Related Terms

  • synonym – anticipate: to expect or look ahead to something with pleasure
  • synonym – expect: regard something as likely to happen
