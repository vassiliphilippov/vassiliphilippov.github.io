malamute

noun
breed of large Alaskan sled dog
Pronunciation: / ˈmæləˌmjut / (AmE), / ˈmaləˌmjuːt / (BrE)
a large breed of strong sled dog, developed in Alaska and known for its stamina and thick coat

Examples

Alaskan malamute dog
Alaskan malamutes have a reputation for being both powerful and friendly.
She adopted a rescue malamute from the animal shelter last winter.
The malamute is well suited for hauling heavy loads across snowy terrain.
