Mark Anthony

proper noun
Roman general and lover of Cleopatra
Pronunciation: / mɑrk ænˈθəni / (AmE), / mɑːk ænˈθəni / (BrE)
(c. 83–30 BC) Roman general and politician; ally of Julius Caesar and lover of Cleopatra, defeated by Octavian at the Battle of Actium.
