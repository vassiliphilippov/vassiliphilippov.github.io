unasked

adjective
not requested or invited
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈæst / (AmE), / ʌnˈɑːskt / (BrE)
Not requested, invited, or solicited; occurring or given without being asked for.

Examples

unasked advice
A swift image of the accident sprang unasked into his mind.
He felt grateful for the unasked acts of kindness shown by his friends.
She offered unasked advice during the meeting, surprising everyone.
