press

noun
weightlifting exercise lifting bar overhead
Pronunciation: / prɛs / (AmE), / prɛs / (BrE)
a weightlifting exercise in which a barbell is lifted from shoulder height to a position overhead in a smooth motion

Examples

a press exercise
After injuring his shoulder, Tom could no longer do the press.
He performed a flawless press with the barbell at the competition.
The press is an essential part of many weightlifting routines.
