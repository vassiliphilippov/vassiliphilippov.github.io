drayhorse

noun
horse used for pulling carts
Pronunciation: / ˈdreɪˌhɔrs / (AmE), / ˈdreɪˌhɔːs / (BrE)
A heavy, powerful horse bred and used for pulling carts, wagons, or loads.

Examples

drayhorse pulls cart
In the 19th century, the drayhorse was essential for transporting goods around the city.
She admired the strength and calm nature of the great drayhorse pulling the cart down the lane.
The brewery still uses a drayhorse to deliver their beer barrels to local pubs.
