perisher

noun
mischievous or troublesome person
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛrɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈpɛrɪʃə / (BrE)
(informal, British) A mischievous or troublesome person, especially a child; sometimes used to mean a disagreeable person.
