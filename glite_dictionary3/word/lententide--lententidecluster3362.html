Lententide

noun
the period of Lent in the Christian calendar
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛn.tənˌtaɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɛn.tən.taɪd / (BrE)
the period of forty weekdays from Ash Wednesday to Holy Saturday, observed by Christians as Lent
