terabyte

noun
unit of digital information (about 1 trillion bytes)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrəˌbaɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɛərəˌbaɪt / (BrE)
a unit of digital information storage equal to about one trillion bytes (1,000 or 1,024 gigabytes)
