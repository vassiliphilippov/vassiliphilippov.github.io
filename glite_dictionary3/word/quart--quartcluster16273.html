quart

noun
unit of liquid capacity (US, 32 fl oz)
Pronunciation: / kwɔrt / (AmE), / kwɔːt / (BrE)
a unit of volume (for liquid or dry substances) equal to a quarter of a gallon or two pints, used in the US and UK measurement systems
