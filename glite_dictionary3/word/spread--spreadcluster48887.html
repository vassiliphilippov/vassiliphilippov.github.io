to spread

verb
to cover a surface with a layer
Pronunciation: / sprɛd /; / spɹɛd / (AmE), / sprɛd /; / spɹɛd / (BrE)
to cover, apply, distribute, or extend something over an area or surface (physically or metaphorically)
