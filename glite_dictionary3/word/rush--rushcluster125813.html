rush

noun
sudden strong feeling or stimulation
Pronunciation: / rʌʃ / (AmE), / rʌʃ / (BrE)
a sudden strong feeling of excitement, pleasure, or stimulation, often caused by drugs, adrenaline, or emotional events

Examples

a rush of excitement
He described feeling a powerful rush when he jumped from the plane.
The drug gave her an intense rush that lasted only a few minutes.
Winning the award gave him a huge rush of excitement.
