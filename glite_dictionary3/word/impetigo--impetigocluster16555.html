impetigo

noun
a contagious skin infection with sores and blisters
Pronunciation: / ˌɪmpəˈtaɪgoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɪmpɪˈtiːgəʊ / (BrE)
a highly contagious skin infection, especially in children, that causes red sores and blisters which burst and form a yellowish crust

Examples

impetigo infection in children
After a few days of treatment, the symptoms of impetigo began to clear up.
Good hygiene can help prevent the spread of impetigo among young children at school.
The doctor diagnosed the child with impetigo after noticing the yellowish crust on her lips.
