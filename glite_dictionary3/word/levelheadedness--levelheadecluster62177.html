levelheadedness

noun
the quality of having sound judgment
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛvəlˈhɛdɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌlɛvəlˈhɛdɪdnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being calm, sensible, and able to make good decisions, especially in difficult situations.

Examples

her levelheadedness under pressure
Despite the stressful environment, his levelheadedness inspired confidence in everyone around him.
Her levelheadedness helped the team navigate the crisis without panic.
The manager's levelheadedness was crucial during the negotiations with the new clients.
