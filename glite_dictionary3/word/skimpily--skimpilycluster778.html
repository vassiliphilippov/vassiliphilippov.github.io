skimpily

adverb
in a skimpy or revealing way
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪmpəli / (AmE), / ˈskɪmpɪli / (BrE)
In a way that reveals a lot of the body; in a manner that covers only a small amount, especially of clothing.

Examples

skimpily dressed
Many partygoers arrived skimpily clad despite the chilly weather.
She danced skimpily in the brightly lit club.
The model was skimpily dressed for the summer fashion shoot.

Related Terms

  • synonym – scantily: in a scanty or insufficient manner, especially with clothing
