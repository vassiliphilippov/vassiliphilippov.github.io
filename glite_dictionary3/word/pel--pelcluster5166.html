pel

noun
smallest element in an image display
Pronunciation: / pɛl / (AmE), / pɛl / (BrE)
(computing) the smallest discrete element of an image or display, typically a single colored dot, especially on a CRT screen; also known as a pixel.

Examples

color pel
Each pel can be manipulated to display a different color, forming a complete image.
Early monitors had fewer pels, resulting in visible blocky images.
Increasing the number of pels on the screen improves the image clarity.

  • synonym – pixel: the smallest unit of a digital image or display
