bitterness

noun
sharp, pungent taste (coffee, quinine)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtərnəs /; / ˈbɪtɚnəs / (AmE), / ˈbɪtənəs / (BrE)
a sharp, unpleasant taste, as found in some foods or drinks

Examples

bitterness of coffee

The bitterness of the medicine made it hard to swallow.
