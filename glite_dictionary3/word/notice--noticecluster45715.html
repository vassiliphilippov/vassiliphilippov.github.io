notice

noun
official statement to end an agreement
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ.tɪs / (BrE)
an official or formal written statement announcing the intention to end an arrangement, contract, or tenancy

Examples

give notice to quit
She handed in her notice yesterday and will finish work in two weeks.
Tenants are required to give one month's notice before leaving the apartment.
The company sent a formal notice to terminate the lease.
