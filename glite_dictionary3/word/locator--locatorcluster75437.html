locator

noun
device or app that finds location
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊkeɪtər / (AmE), / ləʊˈkeɪtə / (BrE)
a device or software application that identifies, shows, or transmits the location of a person, object, or place, often using GPS, Bluetooth, or mapping data.

Examples

GPS locator device
The hiker activated a locator to send her coordinates to rescuers.
Airport staff used a locator to track the missing luggage across terminals.
He bought a Bluetooth locator to help find his keys when they go missing.

Related Terms

  • synonym – beacon: a transmitting device that helps determine or broadcast the location of something nearby
  • synonym – tracker: a device or application that records or shows the position or movements of a person or object
