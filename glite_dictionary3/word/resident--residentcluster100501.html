resident

noun
physician in hospital-based training
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛzɪdənt / (AmE), / ˈrɛzɪdənt / (BrE)
a physician in training who lives at and works in a hospital, caring for patients under the supervision of senior staff

Examples

hospital resident
After graduating from medical school, she became a resident at a large urban hospital.
He completed his training as a surgical resident before moving on to a fellowship.
The resident was called in the middle of the night to assess a new patient.

Related Terms

  • synonym – intern: a student or recent graduate undergoing supervised practical training, especially in medicine
