aliquot

noun
exact divisor of a number
Pronunciation: / ˈæ.lɪˌkwɑt / (AmE), / ˈæl.ɪ.kwɒt / (BrE)
a number that divides another number exactly, without leaving a remainder; an exact divisor

Examples

aliquot of 20
Since 5 is an aliquot of 20, dividing 20 by 5 leaves no remainder.
The teacher explained how to find every aliquot of a given integer.
Twelve has several aliquots, including 1, 2, 3, 4, and 6.
