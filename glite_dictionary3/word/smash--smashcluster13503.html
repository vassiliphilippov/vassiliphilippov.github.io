smash

noun
hard overhead shot in tennis
Pronunciation: / smæʃ / (AmE), / smæʃ / (BrE)
(in tennis and similar sports) a forceful shot hit above the head, typically downwards into the opponent's court.

Examples

tennis smash
His signature move is an aggressive smash at the net.
She won the point with a powerful smash that her opponent couldn't return.
The coach taught her how to execute a perfect smash during the match.
