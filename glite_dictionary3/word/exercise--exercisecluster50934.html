exercise

noun
physical activity for fitness
Pronunciation: / ˈɛk.sɚ.saɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛk.sə.saɪz / (BrE)
physical activity that is done to improve or maintain health and fitness, usually involving repeated movement of muscles or the body

Examples

do exercise daily
Doctors recommend getting some exercise every day to stay healthy.
I went for a run in the park to get some morning exercise.
Swimming is my favorite type of exercise because it works all the major muscle groups.

Related Terms

  • antonym – rest: a period of relaxing and not exercising or being active
  • synonym – workout: a session of vigorous physical exercise, often in a gym
