wanton

noun
sexually unrestrained woman (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːntən / (AmE), / ˈwɒntən / (BrE)
(archaic) A woman who behaves in a sexually unrestrained or lascivious manner.

Examples

a wanton woman
He accused her of being a wanton, though such language is rarely used today.
In the tragic play, she was reviled as a wanton by the townspeople.
The old tale warned of the dangers of becoming a wanton in a conservative society.
