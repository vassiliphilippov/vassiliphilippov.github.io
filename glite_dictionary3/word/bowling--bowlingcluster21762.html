bowling

noun
delivering the ball to the batsman (cricket)
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
(in cricket) the act or action of delivering the ball to the batsman by the bowler.

Examples

fast bowling in cricket
His fast bowling intimidated even the best batsmen during the match.
She has been practicing her bowling technique every weekend at the cricket club.
Spin bowling requires great skill and accuracy.
