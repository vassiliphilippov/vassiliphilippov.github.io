natural

adjective
music: neither sharp nor flat
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃɚəl / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl / (BrE)
(of a musical note) neither sharp nor flat; being played or sung at its ordinary pitch.

Examples

natural note
The C major scale consists entirely of natural notes.
The teacher asked if the note was a B natural or a B flat.
When you play the piece, make sure that every F is natural, not sharp.
