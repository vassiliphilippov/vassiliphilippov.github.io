Ostrya carpinifolia

noun
a southern European hop hornbeam tree
Pronunciation: / ˈɑstriə ˌkɑrpɪnəˈfoʊliə / (AmE), / ˈɒstriə ˌkɑːpɪnɪˈfəʊliə / (BrE)
