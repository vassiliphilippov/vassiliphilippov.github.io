dossier

noun
collection of documents with detailed information
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːs.i.eɪ / (AmE), / ˈdɒs.i.eɪ / (BrE)
a collection of documents or papers containing detailed information about a particular person or subject, often used for official or investigative purposes

Examples

dossier of documents
Her application included a thick dossier of references and achievements.
Police compiled a dossier detailing the suspect’s activities over the past year.
The journalist reviewed the dossier on the politician before writing her article.

Related Terms

  • synonym – file: a collection of documents relating to the same subject or person
