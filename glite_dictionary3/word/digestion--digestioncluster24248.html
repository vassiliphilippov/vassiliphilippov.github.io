digestion

noun
breaking down food for absorption
Pronunciation: / daɪˈdʒɛstʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈdʒɛstʃən / (BrE)
the process by which the body breaks down food into substances that can be absorbed and used

Examples

good digestion
A balanced diet can improve your digestion and overall health.
Doctors recommend walking after meals to aid digestion.
Stress is known to interfere with normal digestion.
