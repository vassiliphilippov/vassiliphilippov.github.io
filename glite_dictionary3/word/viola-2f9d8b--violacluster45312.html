viola

noun
bowed string instrument, deeper than violin
Pronunciation: / viˈoʊlə / (AmE), / vaɪˈəʊlə / (BrE)
A bowed stringed instrument of the violin family, slightly larger than a violin and tuned a fifth lower, producing a deeper sound.

Examples

play the viola
During the quartet, the viola part added warmth to the overall sound.
She prefers playing the viola to the violin because of its richer tone.
The orchestra needed a new viola player for the upcoming concert.
