guest

noun
person invited and shown hospitality
Pronunciation: / ɡɛst / (AmE), / ɡɛst / (BrE)
a person who is invited or receives hospitality, such as in a home, hotel, restaurant, or at an event

Examples

guest at a party
As a hotel guest, you are entitled to complimentary breakfast.
The guest arrived with a bottle of wine as a thank you for the invitation.
We invited several guests to our anniversary party.
As a guest at the restaurant, she was treated to excellent service.
The guest checked out early after enjoying his stay.
The hotel welcomed every guest with a complimentary drink at check-in.

  • synonym – visitor: someone who visits a person or place
