schematically

adverb
in a schematic or structured way
Pronunciation: / skɪˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / skiˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that involves or uses a schematic or diagram; in a simplified or structured form.

Examples

schematically show a process
Instructions are often schematically presented to highlight the main steps.
The author schematically outlined the stages of development in the article.
The process was schematically represented in the diagram for easy understanding.
