decline

noun
downward slope or bend
Pronunciation: / dɪˈklaɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈklaɪn / (BrE)
A downward slope or bend, especially in the ground or a surface.

Examples

steep decline
Rainwater flowed quickly down the decline behind the house.
The hikers struggled up the steep decline after reaching the peak.
The path made a sharp decline toward the river.
