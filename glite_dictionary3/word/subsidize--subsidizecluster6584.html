to subsidize

verb
to support financially with a subsidy
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌb.sɪˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsʌb.sɪ.daɪz / (BrE)
to give financial support to a person, group, or activity, usually by a government or authority

Examples
