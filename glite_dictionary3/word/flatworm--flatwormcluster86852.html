flatworm

noun
worm with a flat, soft body
Pronunciation: / ˈflætˌwɝm / (AmE), / ˈflætˌwɜːm / (BrE)
a worm with a soft, flat body, some of which are parasitic and others free-living, belonging to the phylum Platyhelminthes

Examples

a flatworm under a microscope
Scientists studied the regeneration abilities of the flatworm in their laboratory.
Some flatworms are parasites that live inside the intestines of other animals.
You can find the colorful flatworm gliding across the ocean floor.
