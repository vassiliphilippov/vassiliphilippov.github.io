pagination

noun
numbering of pages in documents
Pronunciation: / ˌpædʒ.əˈneɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌpædʒ.ɪˈneɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the system of numbering the pages in a book, document, or publication

Examples

pagination in a book
Careful pagination ensures that references and appendices in a book align correctly.
The pagination in this report was off, making it hard to follow the charts.
We need to fix the pagination before sending the document to be printed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – page numbering: the act or process of numbering the pages of a written work
