Pseudaletia

noun
genus of moths (armyworm larvae)
Pronunciation: / suːˌdæˈliːʃiə / (AmE), / sjuːˌdæˈliːtɪə / (BrE)
A genus of moths whose larvae are known as armyworms, some of which are significant agricultural pests.
