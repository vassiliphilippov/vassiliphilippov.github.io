pertinence

noun
relevance; applicability
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝː.tn̩.əns / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.tɪ.nəns / (BrE)
the quality of being relevant or directly related to the matter at hand

Examples

pertinence to the topic
Before including any data, consider its pertinence to your main argument.
She questioned the pertinence of the new policy to the actual problems faced by employees.
The pertinence of his comments was appreciated during the heated debate.

Related Terms

  • antonym – irrelevance: the quality of not being connected with the subject at issue
  • synonym – relevance: the quality of being connected with the subject at issue
