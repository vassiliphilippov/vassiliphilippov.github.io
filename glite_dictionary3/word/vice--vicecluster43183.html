vice

noun
tool for holding objects tightly
Pronunciation: / vaɪs / (AmE), / vaɪs / (BrE)
a metal tool with two jaws, attached to a workbench, used to hold an object firmly while work is done on it

Examples

vice grip
A small vice is useful for holding jewelry pieces during repair.
Be careful not to damage the metal when securing it in the vice.
The carpenter tightened the vice to hold the wooden plank steady.
