béarnaise

noun
a sauce with tarragon and shallots
Pronunciation: / beɪrˈneɪz / (AmE), / beəˈnɛːz / (BrE)
a rich sauce similar to hollandaise, made with clarified butter, egg yolks, white wine, vinegar, shallots, and tarragon

Examples

bearnaise sauce for steak
He ordered his steak with béarnaise on the side for a touch of luxury.
The chef whisked the eggs carefully to make a silky béarnaise sauce.
We enjoyed asparagus served with a generous topping of béarnaise.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hollandaise: a sauce made with egg yolks, melted butter, and lemon juice
