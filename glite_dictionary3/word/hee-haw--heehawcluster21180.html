to hee-haw

verb
donkey's braying sound
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːˌhɔ / (AmE), / ˈhiːˌhɔː / (BrE)
(of a donkey) to make a loud, harsh braying sound.

Examples

to hee-haw loudly
As the children approached, the old mule hee-hawed in greeting.
The donkey began to hee-haw loudly at dawn, waking everyone nearby.
We heard the animal hee-haw from the other side of the field.
