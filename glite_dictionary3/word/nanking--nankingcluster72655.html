Nanking

proper noun
former capital city of China; Yangtze River
Pronunciation: / nænˈkɪŋ / (AmE), / nænˈkɪŋ / (BrE)
a city in eastern China on the Yangtze River, formerly the capital of China and the site of the 1937 Nanjing Massacre.
