to legitimate

verb
make legal or official
Pronunciation: / lɪˈdʒɪtɪˌmeɪt / (AmE), / lɪˈdʒɪtɪmeɪt / (BrE)
to make something legal, lawful, or officially accepted; to justify or show something is valid or reasonable

She sought to legitimate her claim by providing official documents.
The committee was asked to legitimate the new policy changes before implementation.
