Trapa bicornis

noun
species of water chestnut with two-pronged fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪpə baɪˈkɔrnɪs / (AmE), / ˈtreɪpə bɪˈkɔːnɪs / (BrE)
An Asian aquatic plant species known as water chestnut, whose fruit is spiny with two prongs rather than four.
