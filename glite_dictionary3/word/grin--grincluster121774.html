grin

noun
broad, often joyful smile
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪn / (AmE), / ɡrɪn / (BrE)
A broad smile in which the corners of the mouth are turned up, usually showing pleasure or amusement.

Examples

a big grin
A nervous grin spread across his lips as he stepped onto the stage.
He greeted everyone with a wide grin on his face.
Her mischievous grin made everyone laugh.

Related Terms

  • antonym – frown: a facial expression showing displeasure or worry
  • synonym – smile: a pleasant facial expression with the corners of the mouth turned up
