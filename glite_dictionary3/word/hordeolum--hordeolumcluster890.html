hordeolum

noun
infection of eyelid gland (stye)
Pronunciation: / hɔrˈdiːoʊləm / (AmE), / hɔːˈdiːələm / (BrE)
a localized infection of the sebaceous gland of the eyelid, commonly known as a stye

Examples

hordeolum on eyelid
A hordeolum can cause pain, swelling, and redness near the edge of the eyelid.
Proper hygiene can help prevent the recurrence of a hordeolum.
The patient was diagnosed with a hordeolum affecting his left eyelid.
