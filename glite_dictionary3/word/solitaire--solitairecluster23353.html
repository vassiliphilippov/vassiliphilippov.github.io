solitaire

noun
single gemstone set alone
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑlɪˌtɛr / (AmE), / ˌsɒlɪˈteə / (BrE)
A single gemstone, especially a diamond, set alone in a piece of jewelry, such as a ring or pendant.

Examples

solitaire diamond ring
For their anniversary, he bought her a gold ring with a single solitaire.
She proudly showed off the beautiful solitaire on her engagement ring.
The necklace featured a sparkling diamond solitaire as its centerpiece.
