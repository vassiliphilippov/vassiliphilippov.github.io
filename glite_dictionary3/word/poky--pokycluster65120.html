poky

adjective
small and unimportant (of a place)
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊ.ki / (AmE), / ˈpəʊ.ki / (BrE)
(of a place) small, remote, and insignificant; lacking importance or interest.

Examples

a poky town
He felt stuck in his poky hometown, longing for a more exciting life.
The town she grew up in was a poky little village in the countryside.
We stopped at a poky café that hardly had any customers.

Related Terms

  • antonym – important: of great significance or value
  • synonym – insignificant: not important or lacking significance
