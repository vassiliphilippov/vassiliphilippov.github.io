epaulette

noun
ornamental shoulder piece on a uniform
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpəˈlɛt / (AmE), / ˈɛpəlɛt / (BrE)
an ornamental shoulder piece or decoration, often made of cloth and sometimes with fringes, worn as part of a uniform, especially in the military

Examples

military epaulette
He adjusted his epaulette before the ceremony commenced.
Many formal military jackets feature ornate epaulettes on the shoulders.
The general's uniform was adorned with gold epaulettes that gleamed in the sunlight.
