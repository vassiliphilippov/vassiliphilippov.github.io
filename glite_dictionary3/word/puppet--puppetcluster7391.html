puppet

noun
doll worn on hand and manipulated by fingers
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌp.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈpʌp.ɪt / (BrE)
a doll or small figure of a person or animal that is moved by hand or by strings, especially for entertainment

Examples

puppet show performance
During the show, the puppet danced and sang under the bright lights.
He crafted a colorful puppet out of felt and buttons for the school play.

Each puppet was skillfully crafted with colorful costumes and expressive faces.
She created a puppet theater in her living room to entertain her friends.
The children watched in fascination as the puppet danced across the stage.
