Otis

proper noun
genus of European bustards (birds)
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊtɪs / (AmE), / ˈəʊtɪs / (BrE)
A genus of large terrestrial birds (bustards) in the family Otididae, including the great bustard, found in Europe and adjacent regions.
