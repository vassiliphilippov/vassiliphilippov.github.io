tenpin

noun
bottle-shaped pin used in bowling
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.pɪn / (BrE)
one of the ten bottle-shaped pins used in tenpin bowling, knocked down by the ball
