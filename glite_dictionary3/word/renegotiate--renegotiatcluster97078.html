to renegotiate

verb
discuss and change terms again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːnəˈɡoʊʃieɪt /; / ˌriːnɪˈɡoʊʃieɪt /; / ˌriː.niˈɡoʊ.ʃi.eɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːnɪˈɡəʊʃieɪt /; / ˌriː.nɪˈɡəʊ.ʃi.eɪt / (BrE)
to discuss and change the terms of an agreement, contract, or arrangement, usually to reach a new or improved deal
