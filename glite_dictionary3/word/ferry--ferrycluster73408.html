ferry

noun
boat carrying people/vehicles across water
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛri / (AmE), / ˈfɛri / (BrE)
a boat or ship for carrying people, vehicles, or goods across a body of water on a regular schedule

Examples

ferry boat crossing
Passengers waited in line to board the ferry with their cars.
The last ferry leaves the port at midnight every day.
We took the ferry across the bay to reach the island.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bridge: a structure for crossing over water, antonym as it replaces a ferry's function
  • synonym – ferryboat: a boat used as a ferry
