tinderbox

noun
potentially explosive situation or place
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪndɚˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈtɪndəbɒks / (BrE)
a situation or place that is likely to become suddenly violent or dangerous, especially because of existing tensions

Examples

political tinderbox situation
During the riots, reporters described the atmosphere downtown as a tinderbox.
Political tension in the region turned the city into a tinderbox overnight.
With so many grievances left unresolved, the workplace was a tinderbox waiting for a spark.

Related Terms

  • synonym – powder keg: a situation or place likely to erupt in conflict or violence
