galvanising

adjective
exciting or electrifying
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæl.vəˌnaɪ.zɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡal.vəˌnaɪ.zɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing someone to feel a sudden strong emotion or excitement; thrilling or electrifying.

Examples

galvanising speech
Her performance had a galvanising effect on the audience.
The coach made a galvanising speech that inspired the team to victory.
The new legislation acted as a galvanising force for social change.
