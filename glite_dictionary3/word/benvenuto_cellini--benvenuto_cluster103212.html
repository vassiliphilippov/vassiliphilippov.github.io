Benvenuto Cellini

proper noun
Italian sculptor and goldsmith (1500–1571)
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛn.vəˈnu.toʊ tʃɛˈli.ni / (AmE), / ˌbɛn.veˈnuː.tə tʃɛˈliː.ni / (BrE)
(1500–1571) An Italian sculptor and goldsmith of the Renaissance, famous for his technical skill and artistic works, including the sculpture Perseus with the Head of Medusa.
