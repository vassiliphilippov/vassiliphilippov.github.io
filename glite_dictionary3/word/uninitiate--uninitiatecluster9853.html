uninitiate

noun
person lacking initiation or experience
Pronunciation: / ʌn.ɪˈnɪʃ.i.ət / (AmE), / ʌn.ɪˈnɪʃ.i.ət / (BrE)
a person who has not been introduced to or is inexperienced in a particular field or activity.

Examples

the uninitiate in chess
Guides are available to help the uninitiate understand the basics of the game.
The technical jargon can easily confuse the uninitiate.
To the uninitiate, the ritual seemed strange and complicated.

Related Terms

  • antonym – initiate: a person who has been introduced to the mysteries of a field or activity
  • synonym – novice: a person new to a field or activity
