to hash

verb
chop food into small pieces
Pronunciation: / hæʃ / (AmE), / hæʃ / (BrE)
to cut (food, especially meat or potatoes) into small pieces, usually in preparation for cooking

Examples

to hash vegetables
Many recipes call for you to hash leftover turkey and vegetables for a quick meal.
She used a sharp knife to hash the onions before adding them to the stew.
The chef hashed the potatoes to make a classic breakfast hash.
