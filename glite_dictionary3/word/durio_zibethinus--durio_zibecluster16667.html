Durio zibethinus

noun
tropical Asian tree; source of durian fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈdʊrioʊ zɪˈbɛθɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈdjʊərɪəʊ zɪˈbɛθɪnəs / (BrE)
A tree native to southeastern Asia whose large, spiny, oval fruit is widely eaten and known as durian.
