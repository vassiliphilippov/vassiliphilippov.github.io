ventricle

noun
heart chamber that pumps blood to arteries
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛn.trɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈvɛn.trɪ.kəl / (BrE)
a chamber of the heart that receives blood from an atrium and pumps it into the arteries

Examples

heart ventricle
Damage to the right ventricle can lead to serious health problems.
Doctors detected a defect in one of the heart's ventricles during the scan.
The left ventricle is responsible for pumping oxygenated blood to the body.
