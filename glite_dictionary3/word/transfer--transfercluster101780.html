transfer

noun
the act of transferring ownership
Pronunciation: / ˈtrænsfɜr / (AmE), / ˈtrɑːnsfɜː / (BrE)
the act of officially moving ownership, rights, or property from one person or entity to another

Examples

transfer of ownership
A written contract is often required for the transfer of land.
He authorized the transfer of ownership to his business partner.
The transfer of property must be recorded with the local authorities.

Related Terms

  • antonym – acquisition: the act of gaining possession or control of something
  • synonym – conveyance: the legal process of transferring property from one owner to another
