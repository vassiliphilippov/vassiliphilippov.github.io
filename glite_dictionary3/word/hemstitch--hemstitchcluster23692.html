to hemstitch

verb
sew with decorative openwork stitches
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛmˌstɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɛmˌstɪtʃ / (BrE)
to sew (the border of a piece of fabric) using a decorative stitch that draws out threads along the edge, creating a delicate openwork pattern

Examples

to hemstitch a handkerchief
He learned to hemstitch a handkerchief as part of a sewing class.
She carefully hemstitched the edges of the tablecloth for a refined look.
The artisan hemstitches decorative borders on linen napkins for her clients.
