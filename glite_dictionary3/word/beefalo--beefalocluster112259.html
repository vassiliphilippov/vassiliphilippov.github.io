beefalo

noun
hybrid of cattle and buffalo
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːfəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbiːfəˌləʊ / (BrE)
A hardy breed of cattle created by crossing domestic cattle with the American buffalo, valued for its leaner beef.
