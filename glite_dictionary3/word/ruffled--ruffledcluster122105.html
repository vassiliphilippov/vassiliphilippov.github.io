ruffled

adjective
having decorative ruffles or frills
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌfəld / (AmE), / ˈrʌfəld / (BrE)
(of fabric or clothing) having decorative ruffles or frills, typically along the edges.

Examples

ruffled dress sleeves

She wore a beautiful dress with ruffled sleeves to the party.
The curtains were made of ruffled white linen that added elegance to the room.
