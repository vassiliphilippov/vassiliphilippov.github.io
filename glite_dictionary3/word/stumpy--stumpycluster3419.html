stumpy

adjective
short and thick in shape
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌmpi / (AmE), / ˈstʌmpi / (BrE)
short and thick in appearance, often with short, thick legs or parts; resembling a stump

Examples

stumpy legs
A stumpy tree trunk was all that remained after the storm.
His fingers were short and stumpy after years of manual labor.
The dog had stumpy legs that made it look adorable.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chunky: thick, solid, and often short in build or appearance
  • antonym – spindly: long or tall and thin, lacking thickness
