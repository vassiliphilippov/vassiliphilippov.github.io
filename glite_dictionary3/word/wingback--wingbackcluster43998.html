wingback

noun
offensive football position
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋˌbæk / (BrE)
(American football) an offensive back who lines up just behind or outside the end.

Examples

offensive wingback
He played as a wingback during his college football career.
The wingback motioned to the left before the snap.
The coach decided to use a wingback for more options on the outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fullback: an offensive back positioned deep behind the line; usually a lead blocker
  • synonym – halfback: an offensive back who usually lines up behind the quarterback
