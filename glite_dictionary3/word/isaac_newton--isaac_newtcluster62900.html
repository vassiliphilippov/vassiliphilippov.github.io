Isaac Newton

proper noun
English mathematician and physicist
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪzək ˈnuːtən / (AmE), / ˈaɪzək ˈnjuːtən / (BrE)
(1642–1726) An English mathematician, physicist, and astronomer, best known for formulating the law of gravitation and the three laws of motion.
