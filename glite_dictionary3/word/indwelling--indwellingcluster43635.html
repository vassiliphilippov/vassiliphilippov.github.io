indwelling

adjective
existing as an inner force or spirit
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdwɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈdwɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a quality, force, or spirit) existing or residing within something as an inner principle or presence.
