miserable

adjective
marked by physical discomfort or suffering
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪz.ərə.bəl /; / ˈmɪz.ɚ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪz.ər.ə.bəl / (BrE)
very unhappy, uncomfortable, or suffering; experiencing great distress or hardship

Examples
