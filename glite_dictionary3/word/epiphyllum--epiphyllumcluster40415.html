epiphyllum

noun
a tropical cactus with flat stems and showy flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈfɪləm / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈfɪləm / (BrE)
a tropical cactus of the genus Epiphyllum, characterized by flattened, branching stems and large, showy tubular flowers, often grown as a houseplant
