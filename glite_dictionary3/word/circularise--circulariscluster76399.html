to circularise

verb
distribute circulars/notices
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːr.kjə.ləˌraɪz /; / ˈsɝː.kjə.ləˌraɪz /; / ˈsɜrkjələˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.kjʊ.lə.raɪz /; / ˈsɜːkjʊləraɪz / (BrE)
to send out information, notices, or letters to a group of people so that many people know about it
