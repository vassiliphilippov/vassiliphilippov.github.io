Lyrurus tetrix

noun
a large black European grouse
Pronunciation: / laɪˈrʊərʌs ˈtɛtrɪks / (AmE), / lɪˈrʊərʌs ˈtɛtrɪks / (BrE)
A large northern European grouse species, the male of which is mostly black with a distinctive lyre-shaped tail, commonly known as the black grouse.
