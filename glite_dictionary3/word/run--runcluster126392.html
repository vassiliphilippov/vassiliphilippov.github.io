to run

verb
to pursue animals in hunting
Pronunciation: / rʌn / (AmE), / rʌn / (BrE)
(of dogs or hunters) to pursue or chase (wild animals) as part of hunting for food or sport.

Examples

dogs run deer
During the hunt, the Duke’s men ran wild boars with skilful precision.
For generations, the villagers would run deer across the open fields.
The hounds were trained to run foxes through the dense woods.
