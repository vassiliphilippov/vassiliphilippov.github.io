unintrusive

adjective
not interfering or intrusive
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.ɪnˈtru.sɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈtruː.sɪv / (BrE)
Not interfering or attracting unwanted attention; not intrusive.

Examples

unintrusive manner
Her unintrusive manner helped put everyone at ease during the meeting.
The security cameras are designed to be as unintrusive as possible so guests feel comfortable.
They chose a lighting system that was effective yet unintrusive in the historic building.
