daddy longlegs

noun
spiderlike arachnid with long thin legs
Pronunciation: / ˈdædi ˌlɔŋˌlɛgz / (AmE), / ˈdædi ˌlɒŋˌlɛɡz / (BrE)
an arachnid with a small rounded body and very long thin legs, commonly found in gardens and houses

Examples
