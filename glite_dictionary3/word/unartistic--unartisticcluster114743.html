unartistic

adjective
lacking artistic sensibility
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɑrˈtɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnɑːˈtɪstɪk / (BrE)
lacking an appreciation for or sense of beauty and the arts; not artistic.

Examples

an unartistic painting
Despite his passion for music, his paintings remained distinctly unartistic.
He admitted that his arrangement of the room was rather unartistic.
Her handwriting was functional but completely unartistic.
