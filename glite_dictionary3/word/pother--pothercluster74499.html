to pother

verb
make a fuss; be agitated
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌðər / (AmE), / ˈpɒðə / (BrE)
to make a fuss or become agitated, especially about something trivial

Examples

to pother over details
She pothered all afternoon about the seating arrangements for the dinner.
Stop pothering and just get started on the work.
There's no need to pother over such a small mistake.
