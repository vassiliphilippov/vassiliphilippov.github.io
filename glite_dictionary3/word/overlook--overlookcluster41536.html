overlook

noun
a viewpoint or scenic high place
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvɚˌlʊk / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəlʊk / (BrE)
a high place, often by a road or trail, from which you can see a wide area below

Examples

scenic overlook
There's a popular overlook just off the main highway that offers spectacular photo opportunities.
Tourists gathered at the overlook to watch the sunrise over the mountains.
We stopped at the overlook to take in the breathtaking view of the valley below.
