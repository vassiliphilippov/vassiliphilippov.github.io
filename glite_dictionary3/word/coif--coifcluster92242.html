to coif

verb
to cover with a close-fitting cap
Pronunciation: / kɔɪf / (AmE), / kɔɪf / (BrE)
to cover or dress the head with a close-fitting cap, especially a coif; to put a coif on.

Examples

to coif the head
Every initiate was coifed as part of the ceremony.
She coifed her daughter for the medieval reenactment.
The judge was coifed in traditional headgear before entering the courtroom.
