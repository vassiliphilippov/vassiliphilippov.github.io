honeyed

adjective
smooth, pleasing sound
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌnid / (AmE), / ˈhʌnɪd / (BrE)
(of sound or speech) pleasantly smooth and sweet to hear; pleasing to the ear.

Examples

honeyed voice
He spoke in honeyed tones that were impossible to resist.
The advertisement featured a honeyed jingle that stayed in my mind all day.
The singer's honeyed voice captivated everyone in the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dulcet: (especially of sound) sweet and soothing
  • antonym – harsh: unpleasantly rough or jarring to the senses
