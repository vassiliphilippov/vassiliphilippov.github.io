uninterestingly

adverb
in a dull or uninteresting manner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɪntrəstɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈɪntrɪstɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that lacks interest or excitement; in a dull or uninteresting way.

Examples

to speak uninterestingly
She spoke uninterestingly during the presentation, causing many in the audience to lose focus.
The comedian told his jokes uninterestingly, and the crowd barely reacted.
The novel was uninterestingly written, so I struggled to finish it.
