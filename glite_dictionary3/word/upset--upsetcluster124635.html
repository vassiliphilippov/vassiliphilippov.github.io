upset

noun
state of emotional distress or worry
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈʌpsɛt / (BrE)
A state of being unhappy, anxious, or disturbed mentally; emotional disturbance or distress.

Examples

emotional upset
After the argument, he was in a state of emotional upset for the rest of the day.
The news of her sudden departure caused a great upset in the office.
Their divorce was a major upset for the whole family.

Related Terms

  • antonym – calm: a peaceful or tranquil state, free from disturbance
  • synonym – distress: great pain, anxiety, or sorrow; acute physical or mental suffering
