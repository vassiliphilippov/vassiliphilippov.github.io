ilmenite

noun
a mineral and source of titanium
Pronunciation: / ˈɪlməˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪlmɪnaɪt / (BrE)
a black, weakly magnetic mineral composed of iron titanium oxide, found in metamorphic and igneous rocks and used as a major source of titanium
