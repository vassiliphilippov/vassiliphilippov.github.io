invoice

noun
itemized bill for goods or services
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌvɔɪs / (AmE), / ˈɪnvɔɪs / (BrE)
a detailed statement listing goods supplied or services rendered, with the amount due for payment

Examples

pay an invoice
Please make sure to pay the invoice by the end of the month.
The company sent an invoice to the client for the completed project.
We received an invoice yesterday for the office supplies we ordered.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bill: a statement of money owed for goods or services
  • antonym – receipt: a written acknowledgment of having received goods, money, etc.
