silhouette

noun
binary mask of an object's shape (computer vision)
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪl.əˈwɛt / (AmE), / ˌsɪl.əˈwɛt / (BrE)
(in computer vision and image processing) a binary mask or representation that shows the shape or outline of an object in an image, typically used for segmentation, pose estimation, or shape analysis.

Examples

silhouette mask image
The algorithm outputs a clean silhouette of the person for pose estimation.
During evaluation, the model produced accurate silhouettes even in noisy, low-light images.
Annotators traced the subject's silhouette to create ground-truth segmentation masks for training.

Related Terms

  • synonym – outline: the outer edge or shape of an object
