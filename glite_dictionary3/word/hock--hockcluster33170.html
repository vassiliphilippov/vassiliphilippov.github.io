hock

noun
joint on the hind leg of hoofed animals
Pronunciation: / hɑk / (AmE), / hɒk / (BrE)
the joint on the hind leg of a four-legged animal, especially a horse, that corresponds to the human ankle

Examples

horse's hock joint
Tenderness in the hock can be a sign of joint problems in cattle.
The horse injured its hock while running in the field.
The veterinarian examined the swollen hock for signs of infection.
