keyhole

noun
hole in a lock for a key
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈkiːhəʊl / (BrE)
a hole in a lock into which a key is inserted to operate it

Examples

keyhole in a door
He inserted the key into the keyhole and turned it slowly.
She peeked through the keyhole to see who was inside the room.
The old door had a large, ornate keyhole.
