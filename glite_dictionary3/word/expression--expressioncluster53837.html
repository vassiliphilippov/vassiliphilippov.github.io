expression

noun
communication of ideas through speech or writing
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛʃ.ən /; / ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛʃ.ən /; / ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
the act, style, or manner of communicating thoughts, feelings, or ideas, especially in speech or writing
