robber

noun
person who steals using or threatening violence
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑbɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɒbə / (BrE)
a person who steals from someone by using or threatening violence

Examples

armed robber
Police arrested the robber just a block away from the bank.
The robber threatened the cashier with a knife and demanded all the money from the register.
The robber wore a mask to hide his identity during the heist.

Related Terms

  • synonym – burglar: a person who illegally enters buildings to steal
  • synonym – thief: a person who steals, without specifying use of violence
  • antonym – victim: a person who suffers from a crime or attack
