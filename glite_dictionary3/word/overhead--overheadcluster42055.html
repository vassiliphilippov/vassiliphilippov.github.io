overhead

noun
administrative resource use in computing
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌhɛd /; / ˈəʊvəhɛd / (BrE)
the extra time, space, or resources needed by a computer system for administration or processing, not for the main task
