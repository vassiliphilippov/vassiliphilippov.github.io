Narcissus

proper noun
a youth from Greek mythology who loved his own reflection
Pronunciation: / nɑrˈsɪsəs / (AmE), / nɑːˈsɪsəs / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) a beautiful young man who fell in love with his own reflection, ultimately leading to his downfall.
