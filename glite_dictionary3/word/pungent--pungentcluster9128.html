pungent

adjective
having a strong, sharp taste or smell
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌn.dʒənt / (AmE), / ˈpʌn.dʒənt / (BrE)
having a strong, sharp taste or smell that is often so intensive as to be unpleasant or stimulating

Examples

pungent smell
She wrinkled her nose at the pungent fumes coming from the cleaner.
The pungent aroma of garlic immediately filled the kitchen.
Wasabi has a pungent flavor that can make your eyes water.

Related Terms

  • synonym – acrid: having a very strong, bitter, and unpleasant taste or smell
  • antonym – bland: lacking strong features or flavor
