to repeat

verb
to occur again
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpit / (AmE), / rɪˈpiːt / (BrE)
to happen or occur again

Examples

events repeat over time
History sometimes repeats itself in unexpected ways.
If the error repeats, please contact technical support.
The cycle repeated every few months, causing frustration among the staff.

Related Terms

  • antonym – cease: to stop happening or to come to an end
  • synonym – recur: to happen again, especially at regular intervals
