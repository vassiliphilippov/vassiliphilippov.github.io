stabile

noun
stationary sculpture with fixed parts
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪˌbiːl / (AmE), / ˈsteɪ.bɪl / (BrE)
a sculpture consisting of fixed parts, usually made of sheet metal, supported on a stationary base

Examples

modern stabile sculpture
A brightly colored stabile stands near the building's entrance, catching the eye of visitors.
The museum displayed a large stabile in the sculpture garden.
This artist is famous for designing both mobiles and stabiles.
