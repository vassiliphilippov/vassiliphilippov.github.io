eruptive

adjective
producing or involving eruptions
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrʌptɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈrʌptɪv / (BrE)
producing or characterized by eruptions, especially of a sudden or explosive nature.

Examples

eruptive skin disease
Some skin conditions, such as measles, are described as eruptive diseases.
The geologist gave a lecture about eruptive rocks formed by volcanic activity.
The region has several eruptive volcanoes that pose a risk to nearby communities.
