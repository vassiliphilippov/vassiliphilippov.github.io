contained

adjective
kept under control; not spreading
Pronunciation: / kənˈteɪnd / (AmE), / kənˈteɪnd / (BrE)
(of a situation or substance) prevented from spreading or becoming worse; kept under control.

Examples

contained fire
Firefighters worked through the night until the blaze was finally contained.
Thanks to quick action, the virus outbreak was largely contained before it spread to other regions.
The chemical leak was contained within a secured area and posed no risk to nearby residents.
