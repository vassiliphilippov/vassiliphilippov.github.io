caplin

noun
small northern forage fish
Pronunciation: / ˈkæplɪn / (AmE), / ˈkaplɪn / (BrE)
a small northern fish (Mallotus villosus) found in Arctic and sub-Arctic waters, serving as food for seabirds, marine mammals, and other fish
