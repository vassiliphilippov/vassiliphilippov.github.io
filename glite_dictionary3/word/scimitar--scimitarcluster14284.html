scimitar

noun
curved sword from the Middle East or Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmɪˌtɑr / (AmE), / ˈsɪmɪtə / (BrE)
A sword with a curved blade, originating from the Middle East or Asia, with the cutting edge on the convex side.

Examples

scimitar sword blade
He drew his scimitar in one smooth motion, its blade gleaming in the sunlight.
Stories of ancient warriors often mention the use of the scimitar in battle.
The museum displayed a beautiful scimitar with an ornate handle and jeweled sheath.
