drawer

noun
sliding panel in a software interface
Pronunciation: / \u02c8dr\u0254r / (AmE), / \u02c8dr\u0254\u02d0.\u0259 / (BrE)
(in computing, especially mobile and web interfaces) a sliding panel or hidden area that appears from the side, top, or bottom of the screen to show navigation options, controls, or notifications.

Examples

app drawer menu
Open the left drawer to view the app's navigation menu.
She swiped from the screen edge and the notification drawer slid down instantly.
In settings, press and hold the drawer to rearrange the navigation items.

Related Terms

  • synonym – panel: a distinct area of an interface that holds controls or information
  • synonym – sidebar: a vertical panel in an interface showing navigation or related content
