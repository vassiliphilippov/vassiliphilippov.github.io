head

noun
pressure exerted by a fluid
Pronunciation: / hɛd / (AmE), / hɛd / (BrE)
the height or pressure of a liquid or gas, especially used to measure its force in a system

Examples

head of steam
A greater head of steam increases the locomotive's speed.
The engineer calculated the head required to power the water turbine.
We monitored the pressure gauge to ensure the head in the pipe was adequate.
