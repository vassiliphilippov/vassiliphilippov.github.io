requisite

noun
something necessary or indispensable
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkwəzɪt / (AmE), / ˈrɛkwɪzɪt / (BrE)
a thing that is necessary or indispensable for achieving a particular purpose

Examples

requisite for entry
A passport is a requisite for international travel.
Clean water is a basic requisite for healthy living.
The project could not succeed without the proper requisites.
