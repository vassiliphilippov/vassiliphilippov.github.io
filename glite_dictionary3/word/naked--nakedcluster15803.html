naked

adjective
unaided by instruments (eye, ear, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪkəd / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪd / (BrE)
(of the eye, ear, etc.) not using any aid or device; unaided by instruments.

Examples

naked eye
The bacteria were invisible to the naked eye and required a microscope for observation.
This sound is too faint to be detected by the naked ear.
With the naked eye, you can see the stars, but not the planets in detail.
