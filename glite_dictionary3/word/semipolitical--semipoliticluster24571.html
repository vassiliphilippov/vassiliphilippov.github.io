semipolitical

adjective
partly political; somewhat political
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˌpəˈlɪtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˌpəˈlɪtɪkəl / (BrE)
Partly or somewhat political; having political aspects but not entirely political.

Examples

semipolitical group activity
