to flourish

verb
wave or swing dramatically
Pronunciation: / ˈflɝː.ɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈflʌr.ɪʃ / (BrE)
to wave or swing something in a dramatic or showy way, especially to attract attention

Examples

to flourish a flag
She flourished the invitation proudly in front of her friends.
The knight flourished his sword in a show of bravado.
The magician flourished his wand before pulling a rabbit from the hat.
