brahmin

noun
member of highest Hindu caste
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɑːmɪn / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːmɪn / (BrE)
A member of the highest Hindu caste, traditionally priests or scholars, regarded as the priestly class in the caste system.

Examples

Hindu brahmin priest
As a brahmin, she was expected to study sacred texts and perform rituals.
He was born into a brahmin family known for their scholarly traditions.
Many brahmins became prominent figures in Indian religious and cultural life.
