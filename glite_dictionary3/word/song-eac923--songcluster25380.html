song

noun
musical sounds made by birds
Pronunciation: / sɔŋ / (AmE), / sɒŋ / (BrE)
the musical sounds produced by birds, especially as a way of communicating or attracting mates

Examples

bird song
Some birds learn their song by listening to adults of their species.
The nightingale's song can be heard throughout the forest in early spring.
The robin greeted the morning with a cheerful song from the garden fence.
