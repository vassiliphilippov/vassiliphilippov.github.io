to comment

verb
add explanations about words or text
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːˌmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɛnt / (BrE)
to write or make explanatory notes about words or phrases in a text

Examples

to comment on a text
Editors often comment in the margins to clarify difficult passages for readers.
She was asked to comment on the old manuscript by adding definitions for archaic words.
The teacher will comment directly on students’ essays with helpful explanations.
