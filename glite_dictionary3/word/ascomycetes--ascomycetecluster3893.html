Ascomycetes

noun
class of sac fungi
Pronunciation: / ˌæskəˈmaɪsitiːz /; / ˌæskoʊˈmaɪsitiːz / (AmE), / ˌæskəʊˈmaɪsiːtiːz / (BrE)
