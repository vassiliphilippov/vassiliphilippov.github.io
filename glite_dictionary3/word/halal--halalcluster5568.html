halal

noun
meat prepared according to Islamic law
Pronunciation: / hɑːˈlɑl / (AmE), / həˈlɑːl / (BrE)
Meat from animals that have been slaughtered in the way prescribed by Islamic law (shariah).

Examples

halal meat sold
Many supermarkets now stock halal meat to cater to Muslim customers.
She asked the butcher if the chicken was halal before buying it.
The restaurant serves only halal dishes, ensuring everything meets Islamic dietary requirements.

Related Terms

  • synonym – kosher: meat or food prepared according to Jewish dietary laws
