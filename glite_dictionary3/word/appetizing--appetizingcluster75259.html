appetizing

adjective
stimulating the appetite; appealing in smell or appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈæpəˌtaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈapɪtʌɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
having an appearance or smell that makes you want to eat

Examples

appetizing smell
The aroma of grilled chicken is extremely appetizing when you're hungry.
The chef decorated the dish in an appetizing way with colorful vegetables.
The freshly baked bread looked so appetizing that I couldn't resist having a slice.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unappetizing: not appealing or stimulating to the appetite
