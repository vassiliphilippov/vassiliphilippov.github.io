thorn

noun
a sharp-pointed projection on a plant
Pronunciation: / θɔrn / (AmE), / θɔːn / (BrE)
a small sharp-pointed projection on the stem or leaf of a plant, often capable of causing injury if touched

Examples

thorn on a rose
Many desert plants have long, sharp thorns to protect themselves from animals.
She pricked her finger on a thorn while picking roses in the garden.
The bush was covered in thorns that made it difficult to approach.

Related Terms

  • synonym – prickle: a small, sharp, needle-like structure on plants
  • synonym – spine: a stiff, pointed structure on certain plants or animals
