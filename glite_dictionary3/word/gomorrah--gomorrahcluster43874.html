Gomorrah

proper noun
biblical city destroyed for wickedness
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈmɔɹə / (AmE), / ɡəˈmɒɹə / (BrE)
(In the Bible) an ancient city near the Dead Sea, traditionally destroyed by God for the wickedness of its inhabitants.
