frothily

adverb
in a frothy manner
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɑːθɪli / (AmE), / ˈfrɒθɪli / (BrE)
In a manner that is full of or covered with froth; with a frothy appearance or consistency.

Examples

bubbles rise frothily
Laughter erupted frothily in the room, bubbling over with excitement.
The milk steamed frothily in the cup as the barista prepared the cappuccino.
The waves crashed frothily against the shoreline, leaving a trail of white foam.

Related Terms

  • synonym – frothy: full of or covered with small bubbles; foamy
