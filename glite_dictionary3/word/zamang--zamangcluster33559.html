zamang

noun
tropical American tree, Samanea saman
Pronunciation: / zəˈmɑːŋ / (AmE), / zəˈmaŋ / (BrE)
a large ornamental tropical American tree (Samanea saman) with bipinnate leaves and rounded clusters of flowers with crimson stamens; its seed pods are eaten by cattle

Examples

a zamang tree
Farmers often let cattle graze around the zamang because its seed pods are nutritious.
The zamang is admired for its striking globes of crimson flowers.
The sprawling branches of the zamang provide shade in many tropical parks.
