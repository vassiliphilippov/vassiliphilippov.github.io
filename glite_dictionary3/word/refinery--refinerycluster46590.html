refinery

noun
plant for purifying crude substances
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfaɪnəri / (AmE), / rɪˈfaɪnəri / (BrE)
An industrial plant where a crude substance, such as oil or sugar, is purified and processed.

Examples

After the sugarcane harvest, the stalks are sent to a refinery for purification.
