gelechiid

noun
a moth of the Gelechiidae family
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈliːkiɪd / (AmE), / dʒɪˈliːkɪɪd / (BrE)
A small, slender-winged moth of the family Gelechiidae, many of whose larvae are agricultural pests.

Examples

a gelechiid moth
A gelechiid was identified as the source of the infestation.
Scientists are studying the behavior of gelechiids to improve pest control methods.
