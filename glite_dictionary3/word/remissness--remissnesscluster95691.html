remissness

noun
negligence; carelessness
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɪsnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈmɪsnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being careless or negligent in performing one's duties or responsibilities.

Examples

remissness at work
His remissness in completing the project led to a stern warning from his supervisor.
Repeated remissness in performing her duties eventually cost her the job.
The remissness of the security staff was blamed for the incident.
