to follow

verb
imitate the actions of someone
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːloʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɒləʊ / (BrE)
to imitate the actions or behavior of someone; to use someone as a model

Examples

to follow a role model
Many people follow celebrities on social media and try to dress like them.
She tried to follow her older brother by joining the same sports teams he did.
Young artists often follow the techniques of famous painters to develop their own style.

Related Terms

  • synonym – imitate: to copy someone's actions, appearance, or manner
  • antonym – lead: to show others how to do something by being a model
