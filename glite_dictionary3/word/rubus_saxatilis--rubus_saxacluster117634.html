Rubus saxatilis

noun
European bramble with red berries
Pronunciation: / ˈruːbəs sækˈsætɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˈruːbəs sækˈsætɪlɪs / (BrE)
A species of European trailing bramble bearing small red, berrylike fruits, commonly known as stone bramble.

Examples

Botanists studied the spreading habit of the Rubus saxatilis in its native habitat.

The berries of Rubus saxatilis are edible but rather tart compared to those of common raspberries.
