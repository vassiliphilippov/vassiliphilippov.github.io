wildness

noun
state of being wild or untamed
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪld.nəs / (AmE), / ˈwaɪld.nɪs /; / ˈwaɪl(d)nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being unrestrained, unpredictable, or behaving in an unruly or impulsive way

Examples

wildness in behavior
Her wildness and unpredictability made her the most exciting member of the team.
The wildness of the sea during the hurricane was both terrifying and beautiful.
The landscape's wildness attracted adventurers from around the globe.
Despite her parents' strict rules, an irrepressible wildness often showed in her actions.
He struggled to balance his natural wildness with society's expectations of good behavior.
The wildness in his eyes made everyone nervous during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym – calmness: the state of being calm and free from disturbance
  • synonym – ferocity: the state of being fierce and violent
  • antonym – orderliness: the quality of behaving in a controlled and predictable way
  • synonym – unruliness: the quality of being difficult to control or discipline
