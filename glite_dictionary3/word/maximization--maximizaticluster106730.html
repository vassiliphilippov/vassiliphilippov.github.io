maximization

noun
making something as great as possible
Pronunciation: / ˌmæksɪməˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmaksɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something as large or great as possible

Examples

maximization of profits
Environmental policies should focus on the maximization of resource efficiency.
Her research is dedicated to the maximization of learning outcomes in the classroom.
The company's goal is the maximization of profits each quarter.

Related Terms

  • antonym – minimization: the act of making something as small as possible
