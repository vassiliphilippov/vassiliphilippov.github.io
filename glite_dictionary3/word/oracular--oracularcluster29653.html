oracular

adjective
relating to an oracle
Pronunciation: / ɔˈrækjələr / (AmE), / əˈrækjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to, resembling, or characteristic of an oracle or prophecy; giving wise or authoritative decisions or opinions

Examples

oracular advice
Ancient Greek leaders often sought oracular guidance before making important decisions.
His advice was so oracular that no one could tell what he really meant.
The priestess gave an oracular response that was difficult to interpret.
