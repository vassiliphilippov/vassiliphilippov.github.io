to roll

verb
pronounce 'r' with a trill
Pronunciation: / roʊl / (AmE), / rəʊl / (BrE)
to pronounce the letter 'r' with a trill or vibration, typically by rapidly vibrating the tongue against the roof of the mouth

Examples

to roll your r's
She practiced every day so she could roll her r's like a native Italian.
