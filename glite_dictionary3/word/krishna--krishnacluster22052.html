Krishna

proper noun
major Hindu deity, Vishnu's eighth avatar
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪʃ.nə / (AmE), / ˈkrɪʃ.nə / (BrE)
In Hinduism, the eighth and most important avatar of Vishnu, often depicted as a handsome young man playing a flute, worshipped as a major deity.

Examples

Krishna plays the flute
Many Hindus worship Krishna as the supreme deity and celebrate his birthday with great devotion.
Stories about Krishna playing the flute are popular in Indian art and literature.
The teachings of Krishna in the Bhagavad Gita are central to Hindu philosophy.
