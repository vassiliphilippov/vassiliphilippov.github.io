pericardium

noun
membrane surrounding the heart
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛrɪˈkɑrdiəm / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪˈkɑːdiəm / (BrE)
The membrane consisting of two layers that encloses and protects the heart.
