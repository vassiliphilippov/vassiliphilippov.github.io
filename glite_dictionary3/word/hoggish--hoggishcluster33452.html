hoggish

adjective
greedy or gluttonous; like a hog
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑɡɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɒɡɪʃ / (BrE)
Resembling a hog; greedily or coarsely gluttonous or selfish.

Examples

hoggish appetite
His hoggish appetite shocked everyone at the dinner table.
It's rude to act so hoggish when food is being shared among friends.
She made a hoggish grab for the last piece of cake, not caring who was watching.

Related Terms

  • synonym – piggish: resembling a pig; greedy or gluttonous
  • antonym – selfless: concerned more with the needs of others than oneself
  • synonym – swinish: like a swine; brutishly gluttonous or coarse
