crab

noun
a habitually grouchy or irritable person
Pronunciation: / kræb / (AmE), / kræb / (BrE)
a person who is habitually irritable or grouchy, especially someone who complains or quarrels frequently

Examples

a grumpy crab
After a long day at work, he turns into a real crab.
Don't be such a crab—just enjoy the party for once.
Her neighbors called her a crab because she always complained about the noise.

Related Terms

  • antonym – cheerful person: a person who is happy and pleasant
  • synonym – grouch: a person who complains often and is habitually grumpy
  • synonym – grump: a person who is easily annoyed or constantly complains
