sacral

adjective
relating to the sacrum (base of the spine)
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪ.krəl / (AmE), / ˈsæ.krəl / (BrE)
relating to or situated near the sacrum, the large triangular bone at the base of the spine.

Examples

sacral vertebrae
He was diagnosed with a fracture of the sacral vertebrae.
Physical therapy focused on reducing sacral pain after his injury.
The sacral nerves play a vital role in bladder control.
