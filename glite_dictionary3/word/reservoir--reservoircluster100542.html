reservoir

noun
large supply or reserve
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛzərˌvwɑr / (AmE), / ˈrɛzəvwɑː / (BrE)
a large supply of something that is available for use as needed; a store or reserve

Examples

reservoir of energy
After months of stress, he found his inner reservoir of patience running low.
She drew from her deep reservoir of experience to solve the problem.
The country still has a reservoir of goodwill among its allies.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stockpile: a large accumulated reserve of goods or materials
  • synonym – store: a supply or stock of something kept for future use
