Basse-Normandie

proper noun
former region of northwestern France
Pronunciation: / bɑs nɔr.mɑ̃.di / (AmE), / bas nɔːˈmɑ̃.di / (BrE)
a former administrative region in northwestern France, forming the western part of historic Normandy, now part of Normandy region since 2016

The cuisine of Basse-Normandie is famous for its cheeses and apple-based dishes.
