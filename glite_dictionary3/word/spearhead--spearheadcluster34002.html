spearhead

noun
leading unit or element in an attack
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈspɪəˌhɛd / (BrE)
The leading or forefront part of an attacking military force or a movement.

Examples

spearhead of an attack
As the spearhead broke through the defenses, the rest of the troops followed closely behind.
The 3rd Cavalry served as the spearhead of the advance into enemy territory.
Their unit was chosen to be the spearhead of the operation, moving in ahead of the main force.
