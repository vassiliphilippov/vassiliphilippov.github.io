musculus transversalis abdominis

noun
abdominal muscle with transverse fibers
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌs.kjə.ləs trænˌsvɜrˈsælɪs æbˈdɑː.mə.nɪs / (AmE), / ˌmʌs.kjʊ.ləs trænˌsvɜːˈsælɪs æbˈdɒ.mɪ.nɪs / (BrE)
a flat abdominal muscle with transverse fibers that forms part of the anterior and lateral walls of the abdominal cavity
