round

adjective
full and rich in sound
Pronunciation: / raʊnd / (AmE), / raʊnd / (BrE)
(of sounds or tones) full, rich, and smooth in quality.

Examples

round voice
A good opera singer strives for a round sound, especially in the lower register.
Her voice had a round tone that filled the concert hall beautifully.
The cello produced a round, resonant note that lingered in the air.

Related Terms

  • synonym – orotund: (of voice or speech) full, round, and imposing
