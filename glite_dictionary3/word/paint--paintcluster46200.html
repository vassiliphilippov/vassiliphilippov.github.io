paint

noun
the rectangular area near the basket on a basketball court
Pronunciation: / peɪnt / (AmE), / peɪnt / (BrE)
(basketball) the rectangular area near the basket, including the foul lane, usually painted a different color than the rest of the court.

Examples

in the paint area
Defenders tried to protect the paint by closing off the lane.
He grabbed the rebound and immediately kicked it out of the paint.
Their center is unstoppable when he drives into the paint.
