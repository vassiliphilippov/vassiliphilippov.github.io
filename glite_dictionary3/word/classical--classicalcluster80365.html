classical

adjective
relating to European traditional music
Pronunciation: / ˈklæsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈklæsɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to music in the European tradition, especially from the late 18th to early 19th centuries, such as symphonies and operas

Examples

classical music concert
Many people find classical music relaxing after a stressful day.
