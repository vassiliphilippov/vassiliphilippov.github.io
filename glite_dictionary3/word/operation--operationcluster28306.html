operation

noun
process or series of actions
Pronunciation: / ˌɑpəˈreɪʃən /; / ˌɑː.pəˈreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən /; / ˌɒp.əˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a series of actions or an organized activity to achieve a particular result, especially when carefully planned or coordinated

Examples

construction operation
Building a bridge requires several complex operations to be completed in a specific order.

Running the restaurant is quite an operation, requiring careful coordination between the staff.
The rescue operation involved hundreds of volunteers and several helicopters.
