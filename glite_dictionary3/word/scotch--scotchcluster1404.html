Scotch

noun
whisky made in Scotland
Pronunciation: / skɑtʃ / (AmE), / skɒtʃ / (BrE)
A type of whisky distilled in Scotland, especially whisky made from malted barley in a pot still.

Examples

glass of Scotch
He poured himself a glass of Scotch after a long day at work.
Many collectors seek rare bottles of aged Scotch.
Would you prefer Scotch or bourbon with your meal?
