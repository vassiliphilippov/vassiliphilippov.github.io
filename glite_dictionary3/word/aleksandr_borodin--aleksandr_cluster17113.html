Aleksandr Borodin

proper noun
Russian composer and chemist
Pronunciation: / əˌlɛkˈsɑndr ˌbɔrəˈdin / (AmE), / alɪkˈsandə ˌbɒrəˈdiːn / (BrE)
(1833–1887) A Russian composer and chemist, noted for his symphonies, operas, and chamber music, and as a leading member of the group known as The Five.
