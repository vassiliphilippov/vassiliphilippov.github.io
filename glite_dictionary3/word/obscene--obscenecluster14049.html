obscene

adjective
shockingly excessive or extreme (amount/degree)
Pronunciation: / əbˈsin / (AmE), / əbˈsiːn / (BrE)
(of an amount, quality, or degree) shockingly or unreasonably excessive.

Examples

obscene profits
Critics called the corporation's profits obscene during economic downturns.
Some luxury homes are listed for obscene amounts that only the super-rich can afford.
The CEO received an obscene bonus last year, far surpassing what most employees made.

Related Terms

  • synonym – excessive: more than is necessary, normal, or desirable
  • antonym – moderate: kept within reasonable limits; not excessive or extreme
