scallopini

noun
thin, sautéed veal or poultry cutlets
Pronunciation: / skəˈloʊpiːni / (AmE), / skæˈlɒpɪni / (BrE)
Thin slices of veal or poultry that have been pounded, coated with flour, and sautéed, often served with various sauces.

Examples

chicken scallopini dish
I learned how to make scallopini by watching a cooking show.
She prepared delicious chicken scallopini for the family dinner.
The restaurant is famous for its veal scallopini served with a lemon butter sauce.
