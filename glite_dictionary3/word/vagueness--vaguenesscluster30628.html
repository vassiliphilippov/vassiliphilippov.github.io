vagueness

noun
unclearness or lack of precision
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪɡ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈveɪɡ.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being unclear, imprecise, or lacking definite form or meaning

Examples

vagueness of statement
Critics complained about the vagueness of the official statement on policy changes.
