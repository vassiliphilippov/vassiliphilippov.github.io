agrobiology

noun
study of plant nutrition and crop yield
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡroʊbaɪˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌæɡrəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The scientific study of the nutrition, growth, and yield of crops, particularly in relation to improving agricultural productivity.
