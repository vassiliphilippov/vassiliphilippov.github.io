focussed

adjective
alternative spelling of focused; clearly directed
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊ.kəst / (AmE), / ˈfəʊ.kəst / (BrE)
(alternative spelling of focused) being in focus; clearly directed or concentrated.

Examples

focussed attention
She kept her mind focussed despite the many distractions in the room.
The speaker remained focussed throughout her presentation, answering every question with confidence.
With his attention focussed on the task, he quickly found a solution.
