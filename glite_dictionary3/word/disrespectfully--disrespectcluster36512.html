disrespectfully

adverb
in a disrespectful way
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛktfəli / (AmE), / ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛktfəli / (BrE)

Examples

to speak disrespectfully
He spoke disrespectfully to his manager, which cost him his job.
She rolled her eyes disrespectfully when her mother gave her advice.
The student disrespectfully interrupted the teacher during her lecture.
