protest

noun
formal declaration of objection
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊ.tɛst / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.tɛst / (BrE)
a formal statement declaring strong objection or disapproval, especially made officially or publicly

Examples

formal protest letter
She gave her protest in writing to the management.
The contract was signed under protest after lengthy discussions.
The organization filed a formal protest against the proposed changes.

Related Terms

  • antonym – approval: agreement or consent to a decision or request
  • synonym – objection: an expression of opposition or disapproval
