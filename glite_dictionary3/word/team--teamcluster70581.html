team

noun
group working together (often in sports)
Pronunciation: / tim / (AmE), / tiːm / (BrE)
a group of people who work together to achieve a common goal, especially in sports or at work

Examples

team wins a match
A strong team spirit can make a big difference in achieving success.
She joined a project team at her new job to help develop a software update.
The basketball team practiced every evening to prepare for the championship.

Related Terms

  • synonym – group: a number of people or things that are together or in the same place
  • antonym – individual: a single person, considered separately from a group
