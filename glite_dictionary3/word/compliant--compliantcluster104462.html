compliant

adjective
willing to comply or obey
Pronunciation: / kəmˈplaɪənt / (AmE), / kəmˈplaɪənt / (BrE)
willing to obey rules or requests or to do what other people want; inclined to comply.

Examples

a compliant child
Although he was usually compliant, today he voiced his own opinion.
Her compliant attitude made team projects much easier to manage.
The students were compliant with all the classroom rules and instructions.

Related Terms

  • antonym – defiant: openly resisting or refusing to obey
  • synonym – obedient: willing to do what one is told to do
