to waul

verb
make loud, plaintive, high-pitched cries
Pronunciation: / wɔl / (AmE), / wɔːl / (BrE)
(of a cat or similar animal) to make high-pitched, whining, or plaintive cries.

Examples

to waul like a cat
Every night the stray cats would waul outside my window, making it hard to sleep.
She heard a kitten wauling under the porch for its mother.
The tomcats waul loudly during mating season, disturbing the quiet of the neighborhood.
