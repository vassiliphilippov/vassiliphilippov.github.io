pathology

noun
study of disease or abnormal condition
Pronunciation: / pəˈθɑlədʒi /; / pəˈθɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / pəˈθɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of diseases and the branch of medicine that deals with their causes, development, and effects; also, any disease or abnormal condition
