to tumble

verb
dry clothes by rotating them in hot air
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌmbəl / (AmE), / ˈtʌmbəl / (BrE)
to dry or process clothes by rotating them in a barrel or drum with heated air, as in a tumble dryer

Examples
