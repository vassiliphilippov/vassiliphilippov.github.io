George Sandism

noun
philosophy or style reflecting George Sand's unconventional views
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɔrdʒ ˈsændˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌdʒɔːdʒ ˈsandˌɪzəm / (BrE)
advocacy or embodiment of views or behaviors associated with George Sand, particularly unconventional or feminist attitudes that challenge societal norms
