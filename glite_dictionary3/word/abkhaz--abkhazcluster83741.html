Abkhaz

noun
disputed region in the Caucasus
Pronunciation: / æbˈkɑz / (AmE), / abˈkɑːz / (BrE)
a region with limited international recognition, located on the eastern coast of the Black Sea in the Caucasus, considered by most countries as part of Georgia

Examples

Abkhaz region Caucasus
Few countries officially recognize Abkhaz as an independent state.
