fondu

noun
meat/seafood cooked in oil and dipped in sauces
Pronunciation: / fɑnˈduː / (AmE), / fɒnˈdjuː / (BrE)
A dish consisting of small cubes of meat or seafood cooked in hot oil and then dipped in a variety of sauces.

Examples

fondu with meat cubes
Each guest cooked their own meat in the fondu and tried different dipping sauces.
For her birthday party, she served a fondu with chicken, beef, and shrimp.
We enjoyed a seafood fondu at the fancy restaurant last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fondue: A Swiss dish of melted cheese or chocolate in which pieces of food are dipped
