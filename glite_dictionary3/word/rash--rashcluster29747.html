rash

noun
sudden series of unpleasant events
Pronunciation: / ræʃ / (AmE), / ræʃ / (BrE)
a large number of unpleasant or undesirable events that happen suddenly within a short period

Examples

rash of problems
A sudden rash of complaints overwhelmed the customer service department.
The company faced a rash of lawsuits after the product recall.
There has been a rash of break-ins in the neighborhood this month.
