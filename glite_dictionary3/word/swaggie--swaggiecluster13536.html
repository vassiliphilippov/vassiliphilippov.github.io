swaggie

noun
Australian itinerant laborer or traveler
Pronunciation: / ˈswæɡi / (AmE), / ˈswæɡi / (BrE)
(Australian) An itinerant worker or traveler who carries personal belongings in a bundle while searching for work, especially in rural areas.

Examples

old swaggie walking
In old Australian songs, the life of a swaggie is often romanticized.
The swaggie walked down the dusty road with all his belongings in a bundle.
We met a friendly swaggie by the river who shared stories of his travels.
