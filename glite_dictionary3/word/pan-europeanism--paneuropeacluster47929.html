pan-Europeanism

noun
belief in European unity or identity
Pronunciation: / ˌpæn jʊˌrɔːˈpiːənˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌpan jʊˌrəʊˈpɪːənɪzəm / (BrE)
the idea or advocacy of political, cultural, or economic unity among European countries or the belief in a shared European identity

Examples

pan-Europeanism promotes unity
Discussions about pan-Europeanism often focus on shared cultural heritage and political cooperation.
Some criticize pan-Europeanism as ignoring important national differences within Europe.
The movement for pan-Europeanism gained momentum after World War II.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Europeanism: support for European integration or values
  • antonym – nationalism: advocacy of the interests or culture of a particular nation
