collar

noun
rim of a shoe or boot
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑlər /; / ˈkɑːlər /; / ˈkɑlɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒlə / (BrE)
a band or ring of material worn or placed around the neck (of a person, animal, or as decoration or marking)
