pose

noun
model's posture for art or photos
Pronunciation: / poʊz / (AmE), / pəʊz / (BrE)
a particular way of standing or sitting, especially one adopted for artistic or photographic purposes

Examples

striking a pose
He struck a dramatic pose in front of the mirror.
The artist directed the model to change her pose frequently during the session.
The photographer asked her to hold a pose for several minutes.
