capriccio

noun
free-form lively musical composition
Pronunciation: / kəˈpriːtʃioʊ / (AmE), / kəˈprɪtʃiəʊ / (BrE)
an instrumental musical composition, typically lively and free in form, exhibiting improvisational style and not adhering to rules of any specific musical form
