volute

noun
spiral or scroll-shaped ornament
Pronunciation: / vəˈluːt / (AmE), / vəˈluːt / (BrE)
an ornamental spiral or scroll-shaped design, especially commonly found in the capitals of Ionic columns and in decorative arts

Examples

volute column capital
She admired the elegant volute design on the vase in the antique shop.
The Ionic column is easily recognized by its distinctive volute at the capital.
The craftsman carved an elaborate wooden volute to decorate the stairway banister.

Related Terms

  • synonym – spiral: curve that winds around a central point and moves progressively farther away
