bore

noun
person who causes boredom
Pronunciation: / bɔr / (AmE), / bɔː / (BrE)
A person who talks too much about dull or uninteresting topics, causing others to feel bored.

Examples

a bore at parties
I hope the new manager isn't a bore during meetings.
Nobody wanted to sit next to the bore at dinner because his stories put everyone to sleep.
Tim is such a bore; he always talks about his stamp collection for hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drag: a dull or tiresome person or thing
  • antonym – entertainer: a person who amuses or holds interest
