independent

noun
a freelance writer or artist
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndəˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
a writer or artist who works for different employers without a long-term contract; a freelancer

Examples

an independent writer
After years of working at magazines, she became an independent and now submits articles to various publications.
As an independent, he decides which projects to take on and when to work.
Many artists choose to be independents so they can have more creative freedom.
