nutmeg

noun
aromatic seed used as spice
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ /; / ˈnʌtmɛɡ / (BrE)
the hard, aromatic seed of a tropical tree, used as a spice in cooking and baking; also, the tree that produces this seed
