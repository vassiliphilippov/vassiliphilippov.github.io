washed-out

adjective
faded or pale in color
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃt aʊt / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃt aʊt / (BrE)
(of a color or object) having lost brightness or vividness; faded or pale in appearance.

Examples

washed-out colors
After many summers in the sun, the curtains looked completely washed-out.
She wore a washed-out blue shirt that had lost its original color.
The photograph had a washed-out quality, making the scene look pale and faded.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bright: vivid or intense in color
  • synonym – faded: having lost color or brightness
  • degree lower – pale: light in color, not vivid
