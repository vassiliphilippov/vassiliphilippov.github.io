to take

verb
archaic: have sexual intercourse with
Pronunciation: / teɪk / (AmE), / teɪk / (BrE)
(Archaic) To have sexual intercourse with (a person).

Examples

to take a lover
In many old novels, the word take is used to imply seduction or conquest.
She feared he would take her as the previous lord had his maid.
The villain threatened that he would take the princess by force.
