to develop

verb
make land or resources usable
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɛləp / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləp / (BrE)
to make land, resources, or an area available for use or suitable for a particular purpose, often by building or improving infrastructure

Examples

to develop land
Investors hope to develop the region's mineral resources for export.
She wants to develop her family's farmland to attract new businesses.
The city plans to develop the waterfront into a vibrant commercial area.
