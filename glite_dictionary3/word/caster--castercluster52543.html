caster

noun
shaker for sprinkling powdered sugar
Pronunciation: / ˈkæstər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːstə / (BrE)
a small container with a perforated top, used for sprinkling powdered sugar or other fine substances

Examples

sugar caster
A silver caster sat in the center of the tea table.
She reached for the caster to sprinkle some sugar on her strawberries.
The baker dusted the cake with icing sugar from a small caster.

Related Terms

  • synonym – shaker: a container with a perforated top for sprinkling salt, pepper, etc.
