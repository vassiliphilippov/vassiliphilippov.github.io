to capitalize

verb
write with a capital letter
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpɪtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkapɪtəlaɪz / (BrE)
to write or print (a letter or word) using a capital letter or all capital letters

Examples

to capitalize a word
Names of countries should always be capitalized in formal writing.
Please capitalize the first letter of each sentence.
Remember to capitalize proper nouns on your application form.

Related Terms

  • antonym – lowercase: to write or print using small letters; not capitals
