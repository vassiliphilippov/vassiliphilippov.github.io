Dasyurinae

noun
a marsupial subfamily (carnivorous species)
Pronunciation: / ˌdæziˈjʊərɪˌniː / (AmE), / ˌdæsjʊˈrɪːniː / (BrE)
A subfamily of small to medium-sized carnivorous marsupials native to Australia and New Guinea, including dasyures, native cats, pouched mice, banded anteaters, and Tasmanian devils.
