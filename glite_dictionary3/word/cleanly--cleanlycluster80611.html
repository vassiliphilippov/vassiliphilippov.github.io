cleanly

adverb
in a tidy, uncontaminated manner
Pronunciation: / ˈklin.li / (AmE), / ˈkliːn.li / (BrE)
in a way that causes little or no dirt or pollution; in a tidy or uncontaminated manner.

Examples

to cut cleanly
Please cut the cake cleanly so that each piece looks neat.
Solar panels generate electricity cleanly without releasing pollutants.
The new engine runs cleanly and produces fewer emissions.
