mealworm

noun
larva of a darkling beetle
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːlˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈmiːlˌwɜːm / (BrE)
The larva of a darkling beetle, especially of the family Tenebrionidae, often used as animal feed or in scientific research.

Examples

mealworm as animal feed
Farmers sometimes use mealworms to feed chickens and reptiles.
Many birds enjoy eating mealworms as a protein-rich treat.
Some people now raise mealworms for use in sustainable human food products.
