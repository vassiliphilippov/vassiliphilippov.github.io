blowzy

adjective
untidy or slovenly (esp. of a woman)
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊzi / (AmE), / ˈbləʊzi / (BrE)
(of a woman) having an untidy, unkempt appearance, often regarded as slovenly or coarse.

Examples

blowzy hair
Her blowzy hair and stained dress gave her a careless, wild look.
The novelist described the landlady as a blowzy woman with a harsh voice.
Though once beautiful, she had become rather blowzy in her later years.

Related Terms

  • antonym – neat: tidy and orderly in appearance or habits
  • synonym – slovenly: messy and careless in appearance or habits
