applesauce

noun
sweet purée of cooked apples
Pronunciation: / ˈæp.əlˌsɔs / (AmE), / ˈap.əl.sɔːs / (BrE)
A sweetened purée made from cooked apples, often flavored with sugar and spices.

Examples
