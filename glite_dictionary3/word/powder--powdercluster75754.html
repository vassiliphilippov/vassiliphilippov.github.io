powder

noun
dry, loose particles, often crushed solid
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdər / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə / (BrE)
a dry substance consisting of very small, loose particles, typically produced by crushing or grinding a solid

Examples

fine powder particles
After the hike, his clothes were covered in fine white powder.
She sprinkled powder over the cake before serving it.
The pharmacist measured the powder carefully before mixing it into the solution.
