bonnet

noun
hat tied under the chin
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑn.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɒn.ɪt / (BrE)
a woman's or child's hat that is tied under the chin and often covers the ears and the back of the head

Examples

wear a bonnet
In period dramas, young girls are often seen in lace-trimmed bonnets.
She wore a flowery bonnet to protect her head from the sun.
The baby's bonnet was tied securely under her chin.
