omeprazole

noun
medicine that suppresses stomach acid
Pronunciation: / oʊˈmɛprəˌzoʊl / (AmE), / əʊˈmiːprəzəʊl / (BrE)
A drug that reduces stomach acid production, used to treat conditions like heartburn, ulcers, and gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD).

Examples

omeprazole tablets daily
Omeprazole is available both by prescription and over the counter.
My doctor prescribed omeprazole for my acid reflux.
Taking omeprazole daily helps to relieve her frequent heartburn.
