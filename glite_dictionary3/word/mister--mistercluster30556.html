Mister

noun
title for a man
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɪstə / (BrE)
a title or form of address used for a man, usually placed before the surname or full name

Examples

Mister Smith
Mister Smith will see you now in his office.
Please address all correspondence to Mister John Davies.
When I was young, we were expected to call every man Mister rather than use their first name.

Related Terms

  • antonym – Miss: title or form of address for an unmarried woman
  • antonym – Mrs: title or form of address for a married woman
  • antonym – Ms: title or form of address for a woman, regardless of marital status
  • synonym – Sir: polite title of respect for a man, especially in formal or respectful address
