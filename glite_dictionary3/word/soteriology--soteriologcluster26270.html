soteriology

noun
theology of salvation
Pronunciation: / soʊˌtɪriˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / səʊˌtɪəriˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of Christian theology that deals with the study of salvation, particularly as achieved through divine agency.

Christian soteriology studies
