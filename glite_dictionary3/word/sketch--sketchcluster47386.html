to sketch

verb
describe or summarize briefly
Pronunciation: / skɛtʃ / (AmE), / skɛtʃ / (BrE)
to give a rough or brief description or summary of something without going into full detail

Examples

to sketch the plan
Can you sketch your plan for the team before the meeting?
He sketched the plot of his novel in just a few sentences.
The report only sketches the main points, leaving out many details.

Related Terms

  • antonym – elaborate: explain or describe in more detail
  • synonym – outline: give a general description or summary of
