cesarian

noun
delivery of a baby by surgical incision
Pronunciation: / sɪˈzɛəriən / (AmE), / sɪˈzɛəriən / (BrE)
a surgical procedure to deliver a baby by cutting through the abdominal wall and uterus

Examples

cesarian delivery
After complications during labor, the doctors decided to perform a cesarian.
Her first child was born naturally, but the second required a cesarian.
Some women opt for a planned cesarian for medical or personal reasons.
