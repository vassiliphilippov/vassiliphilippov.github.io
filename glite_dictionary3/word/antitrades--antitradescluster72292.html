antitrades

noun
winds opposite to the trade winds
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.tiˌtreɪdz / (AmE), / ˈantɪˌtreɪdz / (BrE)
winds in the upper atmosphere that blow above but in the opposite direction to the trade winds
