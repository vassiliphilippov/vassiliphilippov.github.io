miserableness

noun
state of being miserable
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪzərəblnəs / (AmE), / ˈmɪzərəblnɪs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being miserable, especially due to suffering, affliction, or misfortune.

Examples

deep miserableness
His constant complaints revealed a deep miserableness that worried his friends.
She couldn't hide her miserableness after so many disappointments in a row.
The miserableness of their surroundings was evident in the crumbling buildings and unpaved streets.
