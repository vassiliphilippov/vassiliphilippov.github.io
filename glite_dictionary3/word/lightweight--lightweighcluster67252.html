lightweight

adjective
not important or influential
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌweɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtˌweɪt / (BrE)
having little importance, influence, or ability; not considered serious or significant

Examples

lightweight politician
Despite his charm, he was regarded as a political lightweight.
Some critics dismissed her as a lightweight in the academic world.
The candidate was too much of a lightweight to be considered for the top position.
