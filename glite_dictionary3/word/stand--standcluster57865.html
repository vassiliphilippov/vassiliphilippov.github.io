stand

noun
group of trees or plants in one place
Pronunciation: / stænd / (AmE), / stænd / (BrE)
a group of trees or plants of the same kind growing together in a particular area

Examples

stand of trees
A large stand of pine trees bordered the northern edge of the property.
The forest service protected an old-growth stand from logging operations.
They discovered a rare stand of wildflowers during their hike.
