to glue

verb
fix or attach as if with glue
Pronunciation: / ɡluː / (AmE), / ɡluː / (BrE)
to fasten or fix firmly, as if with glue; to cause to be immovably focused or attached

Examples

glued to the screen
Her attention was glued on the speaker throughout the presentation.
I was so interested in the novel that I glued myself to the couch and finished it in one sitting.
The children were glued to the television during the cartoon show.

Related Terms

  • synonym – adhere: to stick or remain fixed to a surface
  • antonym – detach: to remove or separate; to become unfastened
