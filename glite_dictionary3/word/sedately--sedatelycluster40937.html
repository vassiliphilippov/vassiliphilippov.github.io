sedately

adverb
in a calm and dignified manner
Pronunciation: / sɪˈdeɪtli / (AmE), / sɪˈdeɪtli / (BrE)
In a calm, dignified, and unhurried manner.

Examples

to reply sedately
She sedately replied to all the questions, never losing her composure.
The elderly man walked sedately down the garden path, enjoying the peaceful afternoon.
The parade moved sedately through the town, accompanied by soft music.
