superficiality

noun
lack of depth or substance
Pronunciation: / ˌsuː.pɚˌfɪʃ.iˈæ.lə.t̬i / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˌfɪʃ.iˈæl.ə.ti /; / ˌsuː.pəˌfɪʃ.iˈæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
