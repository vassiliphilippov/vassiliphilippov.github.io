anaerobiotic

adjective
living or functioning without oxygen
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.ɛr.oʊ.baɪˈɒ.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌan.ɪə.rəʊ.baɪˈɒ.tɪk / (BrE)
living or active in the absence of free oxygen

Examples

anaerobiotic bacteria
Certain bacteria are anaerobiotic and thrive in environments where oxygen is absent.
Deep in the soil, anaerobiotic processes break down organic material without using oxygen.
Some types of decomposition are driven by anaerobiotic organisms.

Related Terms

  • antonym – aerobiotic: living or active only in the presence of oxygen
  • synonym – anaerobic: living or occurring without oxygen
