Sphenodon

noun
genus of reptiles: tuatara
Pronunciation: / sfɪˈnoʊdɑn / (AmE), / sfɪˈnəʊdɒn / (BrE)
A genus of reptiles comprising the tuatara, native to New Zealand and the only living representative of the order Rhynchocephalia.
