superstrate

noun
layer placed above another layer
Pronunciation: / ˈsuː.pɚˌstreɪt / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pə.streɪt / (BrE)
a stratum or layer that is superimposed on or above another; especially, a language or material layer that overlies another.

Examples

superstrate layer above
In linguistics, a superstrate language influences the vocabulary of a community's original language.
Researchers studied the effect of applying a new superstrate to improve durability.
The glass panel acts as a superstrate over the underlying metal sheet.
