to imbibe

verb
to drink; to take in liquids
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbaɪb / (AmE), / ɪmˈbaɪb / (BrE)
to drink, especially alcohol; to take in liquids by drinking

Examples

to imbibe wine
Guests were invited to imbibe a special punch created for the event.
On a hot day, hikers should imbibe plenty of water to stay hydrated.
The team gathered at the pub to imbibe and celebrate their victory.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drink: to take liquid into the mouth and swallow
  • degree higher – guzzle: to drink greedily or rapidly
