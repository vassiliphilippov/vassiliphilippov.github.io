bodily

adverb
in physical form, as a whole body
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːdɪli / (AmE), / ˈbɒdɪli / (BrE)
in physical form; as a whole body rather than in spirit or part

Examples

bodily lifted
He lifted the small child bodily and placed her on his shoulders.
The legend says the saint ascended bodily into the sky.
The statue was carried bodily out of the building during the renovations.
