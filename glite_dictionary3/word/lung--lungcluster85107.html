lung

noun
respiratory organ in the chest
Pronunciation: / lʌŋ / (AmE), / lʌŋ / (BrE)
either of the two saclike organs in the chest that are responsible for breathing by removing carbon dioxide from and adding oxygen to the blood in vertebrates
