glassy

adjective
having a shiny, nonporous ceramic surface
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsi / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsi / (BrE)
smooth, shiny, or slick like glass; having a glass-like appearance or surface (including ceramics, roads, etc.)
