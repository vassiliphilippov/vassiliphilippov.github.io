to fall

verb
begin doing something vigorously
Pronunciation: / fɔl / (AmE), / fɔːl / (BrE)
(when used with 'to' and a noun or gerund) to begin doing something energetically and with determination.

Seeing the messy kitchen, she fell to cleaning with impressive speed.
The students fell to writing their essays as soon as the test began.
