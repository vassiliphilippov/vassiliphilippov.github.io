venting

noun
the act of expressing or releasing something
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɛn.tɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of expressing one's thoughts or feelings, especially anger or frustration, or the process of releasing air, gas, or liquid.

Examples

venting feelings to a friend
He found venting his frustrations to a friend helped him feel better.
Sometimes, venting is necessary to cope with stressful situations.
The system requires regular venting to prevent a dangerous buildup of gas.
