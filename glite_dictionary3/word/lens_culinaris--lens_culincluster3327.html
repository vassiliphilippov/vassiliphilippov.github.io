Lens culinaris

noun
scientific name for the lentil plant
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛnz kʊˈlɪnərɪs / (AmE), / ˈlɛnz kjuːˈlɪnərɪs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the lentil plant, an annual herb native to Eurasia widely cultivated for its edible seeds and leafy stalks used as animal fodder.
