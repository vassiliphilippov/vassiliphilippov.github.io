voluntarily

adverb
by one's own free will
Pronunciation: / ˌvɑːlənˈtɛrəli / (AmE), / ˌvɒl.ənˈteə.rɪ.li / (BrE)
In a way that is done by one's own free will, without being forced or coerced.

Examples

voluntarily join a group
He voluntarily submitted to the fingerprinting process as part of the investigation.
She voluntarily donated her time to help the community center.
The company CEO voluntarily reduced his salary during the financial crisis.

Related Terms

  • antonym – involuntarily: in a manner done without one's will; not by choice
  • synonym – willingly: in a willing manner, without reluctance
