capsizing

noun
the overturning of a boat or ship in water
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp.saɪ.zɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkap.saɪ.zɪŋ / (BrE)
(nautical) the event of a boat or ship accidentally turning over in the water.
