bisection

noun
division into two equal parts
Pronunciation: / baɪˈsɛkʃən / (AmE), / baɪˈsɛkʃən / (BrE)
The act of dividing something into two equal parts.

Examples

bisection of an angle
A perfect bisection of the angle is required in this geometry problem.
The bisection of the cake ensured everyone got the same amount.
To construct a perpendicular, first perform a bisection of the line segment.

  • synonym – division: the action of separating something into parts
  • degree higher – trisection: division into three equal parts
