examination

noun
detailed self-review, esp. religious
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˌzæməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪɡˌzæmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
an act of closely and reflectively reviewing one's thoughts, actions, or conscience, especially as a daily religious practice

Examples

daily examination of conscience
