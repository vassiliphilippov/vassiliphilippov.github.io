annunciator

noun
device showing active electrical circuits
Pronunciation: / əˈnʌn.siˌeɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / əˈnʌn.si.eɪ.tə / (BrE)
a device that indicates and announces which electrical circuit has been activated, often used in control panels or switchboards
