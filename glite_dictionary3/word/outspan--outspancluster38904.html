to outspan

verb
remove yoke or harness from animals
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌspæn / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.spæn / (BrE)
to remove the yoke or harness from draft animals, especially to allow them to rest

Examples

to outspan the oxen
The travelers outspanned their mules beside the river for a brief rest.
They stopped at noon to outspan the oxen and eat lunch under the trees.
You must always outspan the horses after a long journey to prevent fatigue.
