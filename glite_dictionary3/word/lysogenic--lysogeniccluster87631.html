lysogenic

adjective
capable of producing or undergoing lysis
Pronunciation: / ˌlaɪsəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌlaɪsəˈdʒɛnɪk /; / ˌlɪsəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or capable of undergoing lysogeny, where a virus integrates its genetic material into a host cell, which may later lead to cell destruction
