to pride

verb
take pride in; be proud of
Pronunciation: / praɪd / (AmE), / praɪd / (BrE)
to take great pride or satisfaction in something; to be especially proud of a particular quality, skill, or achievement

Examples

to pride oneself
He prided himself on never missing a day of practice throughout his career.
She prides herself on her punctuality and reliability at work.
The chef prides himself on using only the freshest local ingredients.

Related Terms

  • synonym – boast: talk with excessive pride and self-satisfaction
  • antonym – humble: feeling or showing modesty rather than pride
