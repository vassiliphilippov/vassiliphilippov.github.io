photolithograph

noun
print made by photographic lithography
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˈlɪθəˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˈlɪθəˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
a print made by using a photographic process to prepare the plate for lithography

Examples

photolithograph of a building
For the exhibition, he created a photolithograph capturing the city's historic architecture.

The museum's collection includes a rare photolithograph of the original city map.
