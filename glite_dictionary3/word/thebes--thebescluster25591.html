Thebes

proper noun
ancient city in Egypt on the Nile River
Pronunciation: / θibz / (AmE), / θiːbz / (BrE)
an ancient Egyptian city located on the Nile, known for its splendid temples and tombs; flourished between the 22nd and 18th centuries BC

Examples

ancient Egyptian Thebes
Archaeologists have uncovered new artifacts in the ruins of Thebes.
In ancient times, Thebes was a major center of political and religious power in Egypt.
Many tourists travel to Thebes to see its famous temples and the Valley of the Kings.
