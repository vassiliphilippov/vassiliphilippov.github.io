lost

adjective
not gained or won; missed
Pronunciation: / lɔst / (AmE), / lɒst / (BrE)
(of a contest, opportunity, or object) not gained, won, or obtained; missed or forfeited.

Examples

lost chance
After a lost opportunity like that, it's hard to stay positive.
It was a lost cause from the very beginning.
The team felt disappointed after their lost game.
