foretop

noun
lock of horse's mane between ears
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌtɒp / (BrE)
A lock of a horse's mane that grows forward between the ears, often hanging down over the forehead.

Examples

horse's foretop
Mud clung to the foretop as the mare galloped through the field.
She smoothed the pony's foretop before placing the bridle on.
The horse's foretop was neatly braided for the show.
