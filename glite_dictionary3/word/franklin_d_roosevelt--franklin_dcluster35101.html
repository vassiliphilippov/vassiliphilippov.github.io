Franklin D. Roosevelt

proper noun
32nd President of the United States
Pronunciation: / ˈfræŋklɪn di ˈroʊzəˌvɛlt / (AmE), / ˈfræŋklɪn di ˈrəʊzəˌvɛlt / (BrE)
(1882–1945) The 32nd President of the United States, serving from 1933 to 1945; known for leading the country through the Great Depression and World War II, and for implementing the New Deal.
