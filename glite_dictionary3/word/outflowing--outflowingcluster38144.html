outflowing

adjective
flowing outward
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌfloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.fləʊ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
flowing outward from a source or place; moving away in a continuous stream.

Examples

outflowing gas
Astronomers detected outflowing gas from the star, indicating strong stellar winds.
The outflowing water from the dam created a powerful current downstream.
The river has an outflowing channel that leads to the sea.
