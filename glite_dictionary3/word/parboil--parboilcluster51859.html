to parboil

verb
boil food briefly before further cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrˌbɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈpɑːbɔɪl / (BrE)
to partially cook food, especially vegetables, by boiling it briefly

Examples

to parboil vegetables
It is best to parboil potatoes before roasting them to get a crisp texture.
Many recipes require you to parboil vegetables before adding them to stews.
She always parboils her carrots to make them cook faster in the oven.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blanch: briefly boil food, then plunge into cold water
