unjustly

adverb
in an unfair or wrongful manner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdʒʌstli / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʒʌstli / (BrE)
In a way that is not fair or just; in an unfair or wrongful manner.

Examples

treat people unjustly
It is wrong to treat people unjustly based on their background.
Several protesters claimed they were unjustly arrested during the demonstration.
The employee felt he was unjustly blamed for the project's failure.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fairly: in a just or fair manner
  • antonym – justly: in a manner that is morally right or fair
  • synonym – wrongly: in a way that is not correct, just, or fair
