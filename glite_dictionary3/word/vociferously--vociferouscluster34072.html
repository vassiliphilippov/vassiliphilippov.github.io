vociferously

adverb
in a loud and forceful manner
Pronunciation: / voʊˈsɪfərəsli / (AmE), / vəʊˈsɪfərəsli / (BrE)
In a loud and forceful manner, often expressing opinions or complaints with great intensity.

Examples

to argue vociferously
He protested vociferously against the new policy at the town meeting.
She vociferously denied any involvement in the incident.
The fans cheered vociferously as their team scored the winning goal.
