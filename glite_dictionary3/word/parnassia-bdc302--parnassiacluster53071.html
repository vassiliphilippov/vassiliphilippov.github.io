parnassia

noun
genus of evergreen bog plants with pale flowers
Pronunciation: / pɑrˈnæsiə / (AmE), / pɑːˈnasɪə / (BrE)
A genus of bog plants, usually evergreen, with broad basal leaves and a single pale flower that resembles a buttercup.
