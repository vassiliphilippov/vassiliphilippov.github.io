describable

adjective
able to be described
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskraɪbəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈskraɪbəbəl / (BrE)
able to be described or explained in words.

Examples

describable in words
His relief after hearing the good news was perfectly describable as pure joy.
Some emotions are not easily describable with simple language.
The sculpture was so unique that it was barely describable in words.
