locust

noun
hardwood from the locust tree
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ.kəst / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəst / (BrE)
The hard, durable wood obtained from any of various locust trees, especially used in construction and carpentry.

Examples

locust wood furniture
Carpenters prize locust for its strength and durability in outdoor furniture.
He split the locust logs for firewood, noting how dense the wood was.
The fence posts were made from sturdy locust to resist rot and insects.
