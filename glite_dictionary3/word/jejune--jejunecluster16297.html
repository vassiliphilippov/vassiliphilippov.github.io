jejune

adjective
lacking interest or significance
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈdʒun / (AmE), / dʒɪˈdʒuːn / (BrE)
lacking interest, significance, or impact; dull or uninspiring.

Examples

jejune story
Although the novel received publicity, readers found the story rather jejune and uninspired.
His presentation was surprisingly jejune, failing to engage the audience at all.
Many critics agreed that the movie's plot was jejune and lacked emotional depth.

Related Terms

  • synonym – insipid: lacking flavor, excitement, or interest
  • antonym – interesting: arousing curiosity or attention; engaging
