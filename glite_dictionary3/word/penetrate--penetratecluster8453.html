to penetrate

verb
to insert the penis during sex
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnəˌtreɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɛnɪˌtreɪt / (BrE)
(of a man) to insert the penis into the vagina or anus of someone.

Examples

to penetrate the body
For pregnancy to occur, the sperm must be released inside after the penis penetrates the vagina.
