to ravel

verb
untangle or disentangle
Pronunciation: / ˈrævəl / (AmE), / ˈrævəl / (BrE)
to untangle or separate the threads or elements of something; to disentangle

Examples

to ravel a knot
It took hours to ravel the cords that had been left in a heap.
She carefully tried to ravel the threads of the old sweater.
The detective managed to ravel the truth from a tangle of lies.

Related Terms

  • synonym – unravel: to undo or separate the threads; to make clear or disentangle
