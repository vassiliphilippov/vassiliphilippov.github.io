prognosis

noun
prediction of future outcome
Pronunciation: / prɑgˈnoʊsɪs /; / prɑːɡˈnoʊsɪs / (AmE), / prɒgˈnəʊsɪs /; / prɒɡˈnəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a prediction about how a situation or disease will develop, especially the likely outcome in medicine
