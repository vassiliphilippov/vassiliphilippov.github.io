to zero

verb
adjust a firearm's aim for accuracy
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪroʊ / (AmE), / ˈzɪərəʊ / (BrE)
to adjust the sight of a firearm so that it is accurate at a particular distance

Examples

to zero a rifle
Before the competition, she carefully zeroed her rifle on the range.
The soldiers zeroed their weapons at 300 yards to ensure accuracy.
You should always zero your scope after changing ammunition.
