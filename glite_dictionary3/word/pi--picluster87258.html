PI

noun
lead scientist of a research project
Pronunciation: / piː aɪ / (AmE), / piː aɪ / (BrE)
the person who is primarily responsible for the design, conduct, and management of a research project, especially in academic or scientific settings

Examples

PI leads research
As the PI, Dr. Williams supervised the entire research team.
She was promoted to PI after securing her own grant for the study.
The PI is responsible for submitting the final report to the funding agency.

Related Terms

  • synonym – principal investigator: the main scientist or researcher in charge of a research project
