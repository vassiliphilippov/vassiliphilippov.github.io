waxing

noun
application of wax to a surface
Pronunciation: / ˈwæksɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwæksɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of applying wax to a surface for protection, shine, or hair removal.

Examples

waxing a car
Car waxing helps protect the paint from environmental damage.
Regular waxing of the floor keeps it looking shiny and new.
She booked an appointment for a leg waxing before her holiday.
