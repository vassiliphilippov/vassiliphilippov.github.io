to boldface

verb
make text bold in print or on screen
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊldˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ˈbəʊldˌfeɪs / (BrE)
to print or write (text) in a bold typeface; to make text bold.

Examples

to boldface a word
He boldfaced the date so it would stand out on the invitation.
If you boldface headings, the document becomes much easier to scan.
Please boldface the key terms in your report for greater emphasis.

Related Terms

  • synonym – italicize: to print or write in italics
  • synonym – underline: to draw a line under text for emphasis
