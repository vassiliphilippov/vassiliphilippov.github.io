boat-shaped

adjective
concave like a boat
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊt ˌʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈbəʊt ˌʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a concave form or outline resembling the shape of a boat.

Examples

boat-shaped leaves
Some leaves are naturally boat-shaped to help channel water toward their roots.
The ancient bowl was boat-shaped, with gently curved sides and pointed ends.
