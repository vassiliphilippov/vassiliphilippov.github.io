fattiness

noun
quality of containing fat
Pronunciation: / ˈfæti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfæti.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of containing a high amount of fat.

Examples

fattiness of food
Nutritionists warn that the fattiness of some processed foods may increase health risks.
The fattiness of the steak made it very juicy and flavorful.
The chef balanced the fattiness of the dish with fresh citrus to brighten the flavor.

  • antonym – leanness: the quality of containing little fat
