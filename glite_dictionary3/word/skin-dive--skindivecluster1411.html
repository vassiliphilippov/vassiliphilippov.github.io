to skin-dive

verb
swim underwater using a snorkel
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪnˌdaɪv / (AmE), / ˈskɪnˌdaɪv / (BrE)
to swim or dive underwater using only a snorkel and no scuba equipment

Examples

to skin-dive with snorkel
A group of tourists decided to skin-dive in the shallow bay.
She learned to skin-dive before attempting scuba diving.
They love to skin-dive along the coral reefs during their vacation.

Related Terms

  • antonym – scuba dive: to swim underwater using a self-contained underwater breathing apparatus (scuba)
