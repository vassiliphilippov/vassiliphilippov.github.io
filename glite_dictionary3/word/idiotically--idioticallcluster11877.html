idiotically

adverb
in a foolish or senseless manner
Pronunciation: / ˌɪdiˈɑtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɪdɪˈɒtɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of intelligence or good judgment; foolishly or senselessly.

Examples

acted idiotically in public
He idiotically ignored the warning signs and proceeded anyway.
The manager responded idiotically to the clear evidence presented by his team.
They acted idiotically during the meeting, making everyone uncomfortable.
