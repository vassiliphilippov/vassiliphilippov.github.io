harmless

adjective
not able to cause harm; safe
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrmləs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːmləs / (BrE)
not causing or capable of causing harm or injury; safe.

Examples

harmless to people
After testing, the chemical was found to be harmless to humans.
She assured us that the prank was meant to be harmless fun.
The snake was completely harmless despite its frightening appearance.
