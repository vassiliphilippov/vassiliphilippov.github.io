curtsy

noun
gesture by bending knees (women)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːtsi / (AmE), / ˈkɜːtsi / (BrE)
a respectful gesture made by bending the knees and lowering the body, performed especially by women as a greeting or sign of respect

Examples

a graceful curtsy
At the end of her dance, she gave a deep curtsy to the audience.
In some traditional ceremonies, a curtsy is still expected as a mark of respect.
The young girl performed a perfect curtsy before the queen.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bow: a gesture of bending the upper body forward, usually by men, to show respect
