to tick

verb
mark with a check mark
Pronunciation: / tɪk / (AmE), / tɪk / (BrE)
to mark (an item, box, etc.) with a check mark to show that it has been selected, completed, or verified

Examples

to tick the answer
I tick the correct answers on my quiz so I can review my mistakes easily.
Please remember to tick the box if you agree to the terms and conditions.
She ticked each item off her shopping list as she put it in the cart.

Related Terms

  • synonym – check: to mark with a check mark; chiefly American usage
