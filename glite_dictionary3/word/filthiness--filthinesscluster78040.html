filthiness

noun
moral corruption or impurity
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlθinəs / (AmE), / ˈfɪlθinɪs / (BrE)
the state of being morally corrupt or polluted; moral impurity

Examples

moral filthiness
Many ancient texts caution against the filthiness of the soul.
She was troubled by the moral filthiness depicted in the novel.
The preacher warned of the filthiness that can result from a life of dishonesty.
