Alfred Binet

proper noun
French psychologist; IQ test pioneer
Pronunciation: / ælˌfred bɪˈneɪ / (AmE), / ˈælfred ˈbɪneɪ / (BrE)
(1857–1911) French psychologist known for pioneering work on intelligence testing and the development of the first practical IQ test.
