Areca catechu

noun
Southeast Asian palm tree; betel nut palm
Pronunciation: / əˈriːkə kəˈtiːku / (AmE), / əˈriːkə kəˈtiːkjuː / (BrE)
A species of palm tree native to Southeast Asia, whose orange or scarlet fruit, known as betel nut, is chewed in many cultures.
