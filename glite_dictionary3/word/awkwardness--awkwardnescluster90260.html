awkwardness

noun
lack of grace or ease in movement
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːkwərdnəs /; / ˈɔːkwərd.nəs /; / ˈɔkwərdnəs / (AmE), / ˈɔːkwədnəs /; / ˈɔːkwəd.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being clumsy, unskillful, or lacking grace in movement or actions
