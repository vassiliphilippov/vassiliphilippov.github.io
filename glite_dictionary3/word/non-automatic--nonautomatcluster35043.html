non-automatic

adjective
requiring manual operation, not automatic
Pronunciation: / nɑn ˌɔtəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / nɒn ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
(of a device or process) not operating automatically; requiring manual operation or intervention.

Examples

non-automatic coffee maker
He prefers non-automatic coffee makers because he enjoys controlling the brewing process himself.
Many older cars have a non-automatic transmission that requires the driver to shift gears manually.
The security door uses a non-automatic locking system that must be engaged by hand.

Related Terms

  • antonym – automatic: operated by itself without human intervention
