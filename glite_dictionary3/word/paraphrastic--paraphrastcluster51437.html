paraphrastic

adjective
expressed by paraphrasing
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈfræstɪk / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈfrastɪk / (BrE)
expressed or altered by paraphrasing; using different words to convey the same meaning

Examples

paraphrastic translation
Her paraphrastic translation made the poem easier to understand.
The article provides a paraphrastic summary of the original speech.
The teacher preferred a direct quotation over a paraphrastic version.

Related Terms

  • antonym – literal: using the exact words without paraphrasing
  • synonym – paraphrase: to express the meaning using different words
