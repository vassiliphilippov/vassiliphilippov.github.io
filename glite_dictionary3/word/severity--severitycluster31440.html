severity

noun
degree of unpleasantness or harshness
Pronunciation: / sɪˈvɛrəti /; / səˈvɛrəti / (AmE), / sɪˈvɛrəti /; / sɪˈvɛrɪti / (BrE)
the degree or extent of something bad or difficult, such as pain, injury, or weather
