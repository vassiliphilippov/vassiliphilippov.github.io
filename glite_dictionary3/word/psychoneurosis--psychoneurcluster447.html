psychoneurosis

noun
mild mental disorder (not organic)
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkənoʊˈroʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkənjʊəˈrəʊsɪs / (BrE)
A mild mental disorder involving chronic distress but not resulting from any known neurological or organic dysfunction.

Examples

diagnosed with psychoneurosis
After months of overwhelming stress, he was diagnosed with psychoneurosis by his therapist.
Early intervention can help manage and treat psychoneurosis effectively.
The symptoms of psychoneurosis often include anxiety, depression, or obsessive behavior.

Related Terms

  • synonym – neurosis: mild psychiatric disorder without loss of touch with reality
  • antonym – psychosis: severe mental disorder involving loss of contact with reality
