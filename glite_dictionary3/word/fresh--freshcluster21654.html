fresh

adjective
not yet used or soiled
Pronunciation: / frɛʃ / (AmE), / frɛʃ / (BrE)
(of an object) not yet used, worn, or soiled; in a clean or new condition.

Examples

fresh shirt
I put on a fresh shirt before going out to dinner.
She handed me a fresh sheet of paper to write my notes.
The guests were given fresh towels every morning at the hotel.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clean: free from dirt, marks, or stains
  • antonym – dirty: covered or marked with an unclean substance
  • synonym – new: recently created, not previously used
  • antonym – used: having already been used; not new or fresh
