nullifier

noun
person who invalidates something
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌlɪˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈnʌlɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
an official or person who invalidates or nullifies something, especially a legal or official document

Examples

official nullifier role
As the official nullifier, she had the authority to cancel the results of unfair elections.
He asked to meet the nullifier responsible for annulling his license.
The court appointed a nullifier to review and void suspicious contracts.
