intuition

noun
instinctive understanding or knowing
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.tuˈɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.tjuˈɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the ability to know or understand something immediately, without needing to think or reason

Examples
