diabatic

adjective
involving transfer of heat
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪ.əˈbæt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌdaɪ.əˈbæt.ɪk / (BrE)
(of a process) involving or accompanied by a transfer of heat to or from a system.

Examples

diabatic heat transfer
Cloud formation is often the result of diabatic temperature changes involving heat exchange with the environment.
Engineers must consider diabatic effects when designing systems exposed to varying heat flows.
The scientist explained that a diabatic process cannot conserve energy within the system.
