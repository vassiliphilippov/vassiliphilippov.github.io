lichen

noun
eruptive skin disease with thick lesions
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪkən / (AmE), / ˈlɪkən / (BrE)
Any of several skin diseases marked by hard, thick, clustered lesions resembling the plant lichen.

Examples

lichen skin disease
Chronic lichen can cause discomfort and itching for those affected by the skin condition.
The patient was diagnosed with lichen after a dermatologist observed the thick, grouped lesions.
Topical treatments are often prescribed to manage the symptoms of lichen on the skin.
