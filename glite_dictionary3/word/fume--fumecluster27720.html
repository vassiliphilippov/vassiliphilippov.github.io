to fume

verb
treat with fumes (disinfecting/pest control)
Pronunciation: / fjuːm / (AmE), / fjuːm / (BrE)
to treat or expose something to fumes, especially to disinfect or eliminate pests

Examples

to fume stored items
Some museums fume ancient artifacts to protect them from insect damage.
They fumed the storage area overnight to eradicate all pests.
To control the infestation, workers will fume the greenhouse with a special gas.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fumigate: to disinfect or purify by exposure to smoke or gas
