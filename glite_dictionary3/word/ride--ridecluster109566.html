to ride

verb
describe how a vehicle feels when driven
Pronunciation: / raɪd / (AmE), / raɪd / (BrE)
(of a vehicle) to have or provide a particular type of feeling or quality when being driven or ridden in.

Examples

the car rides smoothly
His luxury sedan rides very quietly, even on bumpy streets.
The old bicycle rides rough over gravel roads.
This SUV rides more comfortably than my previous car.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drive: to operate and control the direction and speed of a motor vehicle
