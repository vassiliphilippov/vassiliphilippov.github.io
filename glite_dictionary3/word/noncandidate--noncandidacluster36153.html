noncandidate

noun
person who is not running for office
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈkændəˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈkændɪdeɪt / (BrE)
a person, especially a politician, who has publicly stated that they are not running for a particular political office

Examples

noncandidate for president
After weeks of speculation, the senator confirmed he was a noncandidate for the presidency.
Despite being a noncandidate, she continued to influence the political debate.
The press conference was held to clarify her status as a noncandidate in the upcoming election.
