LGBTQ

noun
the community of non-heterosexual and/or non-cisgender people
Pronunciation: / ˌɛl dʒiː biː tiː ˈkjuː / (AmE), / ˌɛl dʒiː biː tiː ˈkjuː / (BrE)
the community of people whose sexual orientation or gender identity is not heterosexual or cisgender, including but not limited to lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and queer or questioning individuals
