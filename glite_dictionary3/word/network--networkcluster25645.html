to network

verb
make professional or social contacts
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛtˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈnɛtˌwɜːk / (BrE)
to interact with others to exchange information and develop professional or social contacts, especially for career or business purposes

Examples

It's important to network at industry conferences to build valuable connections.
Many people find jobs through networking rather than by applying online.
