bimbo

noun
an attractive but unintelligent young woman
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪmboʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɪmbəʊ / (BrE)
A young woman, typically considered attractive but regarded as unintelligent and often kept or indulged by wealthy or powerful older men.

Examples

a bimbo at a party
Many people unfairly label her a bimbo because of her looks and the company she keeps.
She proved she's not just another bimbo by launching a successful business.
The tabloids often feature stories pairing the millionaire with a glamorous bimbo.
