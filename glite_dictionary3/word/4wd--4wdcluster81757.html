4WD

noun
four-wheel-drive vehicle or system
Pronunciation: / fɔr ˌwiː diː / (AmE), / fɔː ˌwiː diː / (BrE)
a transmission system that powers all four wheels of a vehicle simultaneously, used especially for off-road driving

Examples

4WD system
He switched his truck into 4WD to navigate the snowy road safely.
Many SUVs come with 4WD for better traction on rough terrain.
Some drivers prefer 4WD vehicles for outdoor adventures and off-roading.
