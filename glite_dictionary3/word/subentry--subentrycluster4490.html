subentry

noun
entry within a main entry
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌɛntri / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌɛntri / (BrE)
An entry that is included within a main entry, such as in a dictionary or an account book.

Examples

subentry in a dictionary
Cross-references are often given in a subentry beneath the primary heading.
Each subentry in the dictionary provides additional information about a specific usage of the main word.
The accountant listed the expenses as a subentry under the main project budget.

Related Terms

  • synonym – entry: an item that is written or printed in a list, dictionary, account, or similar record
