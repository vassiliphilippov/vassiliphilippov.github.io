extraneousness

noun
the state of being irrelevant
Pronunciation: / ɛkˈstreɪniəsnɪs /; / ɪkˈstreɪniəsnəs / (AmE), / ɛkˈstreɪniəsnɪs /; / ɪkˈstreɪnɪəsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being irrelevant, unrelated, or not essential to the subject or situation
