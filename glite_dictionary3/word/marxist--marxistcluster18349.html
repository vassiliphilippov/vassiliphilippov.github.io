Marxist

noun
supporter of Marxist political theory
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrksɪst /; / ˈmɑːrksɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɑːksɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or believes in the political and economic theories of Karl Marx, especially advocating for socialism and a classless society
