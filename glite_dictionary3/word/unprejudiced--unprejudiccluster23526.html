unprejudiced

adjective
not influenced by bias; impartial
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈprɛdʒəˌdɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈprɛdʒədɪst / (BrE)
Not influenced by personal feelings, bias, or preconceived opinions; impartial or fair.

Examples

unprejudiced opinion
His unprejudiced approach helped resolve the dispute quickly.
She gave an unprejudiced opinion about the proposal after reviewing all the facts.
The committee seeks unprejudiced members who can judge each case fairly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – impartial: treating all rivals or disputants equally; fair and just
  • antonym – prejudiced: having or showing a dislike or distrust that is derived from prejudice; biased
