flying

adjective
brief and hurried
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a visit, glance, etc.) done quickly and without staying for long; brief and hurried.

Examples

flying visit
He cast a flying glance at the report before the meeting began.
She paid a flying visit to her grandparents on her way to the airport.
They made a flying inspection of the site, finishing in under fifteen minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym – brief: short in duration
  • antonym – lengthy: very long; lasting for a long time
