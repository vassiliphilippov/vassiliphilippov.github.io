glazed

adjective
having a shiny or smooth coating
Pronunciation: / ɡleɪzd / (AmE), / ɡleɪzd / (BrE)
having a shiny or smooth surface or coating, often as a result of being covered with a glaze

Examples

glazed pottery finish

The bakery sells delicious glazed doughnuts every morning.
The table had a glazed finish that reflected the light.
