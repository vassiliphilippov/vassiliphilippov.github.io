mint

noun
place where coins are made
Pronunciation: / mɪnt / (AmE), / mɪnt / (BrE)
an official place where coins are made by authority of the government

Examples

government mint building
Security is extremely tight at the mint to prevent counterfeiting and theft.

The government announced plans to build a new mint to produce coins more efficiently.
