incision

noun
surgical cut in body tissue
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɪʒən / (BrE)
a surgical cut made in body tissue or an organ, especially during an operation

Examples

surgical incision
After the incision healed, she was able to walk comfortably again.
