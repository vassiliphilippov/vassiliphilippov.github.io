pigmy

noun
a very short-statured person from certain ethnic groups
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡmi / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡmi / (BrE)
a member of any of various peoples, especially in Africa and Asia, whose average adult height is less than five feet

Examples

pigmy tribe member
Anthropologists studied the way of life among the pigmy peoples of Central Africa.
Legends about pigmy people appear in several ancient cultures.
The forest-dwelling pigmy tribes have unique cultural traditions.
