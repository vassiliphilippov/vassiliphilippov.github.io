tepid

adjective
showing little enthusiasm or interest
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛpɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɛpɪd / (BrE)
showing little enthusiasm or interest; unenthusiastic or lukewarm

Examples

tepid response
He offered tepid support to the new project, barely expressing any interest.
Her tepid response to the proposal made everyone uncertain about her commitment.
The audience gave only tepid applause after the long speech.
