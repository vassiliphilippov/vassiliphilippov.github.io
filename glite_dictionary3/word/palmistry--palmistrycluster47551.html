palmistry

noun
fortune-telling by reading palms
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑlməstri / (AmE), / ˈpɑːmɪstri / (BrE)
the practice of telling people's fortunes by interpreting the lines and features on the palm of the hand

Examples

palmistry reading hands
Many believe that palmistry can reveal hidden aspects of a person's character.
She became interested in palmistry after attending a workshop on fortune-telling.
The old woman offered palmistry readings at the summer festival.
