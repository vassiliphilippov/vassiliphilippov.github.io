uncompetitive

adjective
not inclined to compete
Pronunciation: / ʌn.kəmˈpɛ.t̬ɪ.vɪv / (AmE), / ʌn.kəmˈpɛ.tɪ.vɪv / (BrE)
not inclined to compete or lacking a desire to compete with others.

Examples

an uncompetitive child
Children who are uncompetitive might prefer cooperative games over sports that focus on winning.
His uncompetitive nature made him uninterested in joining the debating club.
The company's uncompetitive attitude means it rarely strives to outperform its rivals.
