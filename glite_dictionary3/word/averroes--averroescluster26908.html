Averroes

proper noun
Arabian philosopher and commentator on Aristotle
Pronunciation: / əˈvɛroʊiːz / (AmE), / əˈvɛrəʊiːz / (BrE)
(1126–1198) An Arabian philosopher and physician born in Spain, known for his influential commentaries on Aristotle and his impact on medieval European thought.

Examples

Averroes philosopher of Spain
Averroes made significant contributions to philosophy, especially through his commentaries on Aristotle.
Many medieval scholars in Europe studied the works of Averroes.
The teachings of Averroes had a lasting impact on both Islamic and Western philosophy.
