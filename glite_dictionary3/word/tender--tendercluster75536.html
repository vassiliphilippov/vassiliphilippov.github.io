tender

adjective
easily hurt or sensitive (body, flesh)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛndɚ /; / ˈtɛn.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɛndə /; / ˈten.də / (BrE)
easily hurt, sore, sensitive to pain or pressure, or easy to cut or chew

Examples

tender meat
