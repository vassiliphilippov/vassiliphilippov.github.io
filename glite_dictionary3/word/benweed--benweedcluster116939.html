benweed

noun
European weed with yellow daisylike flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛnˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈbɛnˌwiːd / (BrE)
A widespread European weed with yellow, daisylike flowers, known to be toxic to cattle if eaten in large amounts.
