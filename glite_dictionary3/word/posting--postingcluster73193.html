posting

noun
public notice or advertisement displayed
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstɪŋ / (BrE)
a sign, notice, or advertisement displayed in a public place, especially to provide information or promote an event, product, or service

Examples

posting on a wall
A new posting on the bulletin board announced the upcoming seminar.
She stopped to read a posting about job opportunities.
The concert was promoted through a large posting downtown.
