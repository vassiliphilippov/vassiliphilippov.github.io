dud

noun
explosive device that fails to go off
Pronunciation: / dʌd / (AmE), / dʌd / (BrE)
an explosive device, such as a shell or firework, that fails to detonate or function as intended

Examples

dud firework fails
Everyone held their breath, but the grenade was just a dud.
The firework was a complete dud, failing to light up the night sky as expected.
The old bomb turned out to be a dud and posed no threat to the workers.

  • antonym – success: device or event that works as intended
