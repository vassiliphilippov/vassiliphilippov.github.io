manfulness

noun
the quality of being manly or courageous
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmæn.f(ə)l.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of showing the traditional characteristics or virtues associated with an adult man, such as courage, strength, or resolve

Examples

show manfulness in danger
His manfulness was evident in the way he handled the crisis with calm authority.
In many stories, manfulness is associated with acts of bravery and honor.
She admired the manfulness he displayed despite the difficult circumstances.
