humification

noun
process of forming humus from plant remains
Pronunciation: / ˌhjuːməfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌhjuːmɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which plant material decomposes to form humus in soil

Examples

humification of plant material
Forests rely on humification to convert fallen leaves into nutrient-rich humus.
Humification is a crucial step in the build-up of fertile soil.
Rapid humification can improve soil structure and fertility.
