Isuridae

noun
former name for shark family Lamnidae
Pronunciation: / aɪˈsjʊrɪˌdiː /; / aɪˈsjʊrəˌdiː / (AmE), / aɪˈsjʊə.rɪ.diː /; / aɪˈsjʊərɪdiː / (BrE)
a scientific name for a family of sharks, now usually considered the same as Lamnidae (the family that includes mako and great white sharks)
