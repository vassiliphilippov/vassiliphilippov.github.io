exception

noun
grounds for criticism or objection
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛpʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
grounds for criticism or objection; a reason to disapprove or challenge something.

Examples

exception to his honesty
His honesty is above exception and cannot be questioned.
She lives a life that is beyond exception in every way.
The manager's decision was accepted without exception from the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym – criticism: the expression of disapproval of someone or something
  • synonym – objection: an expression or feeling of disapproval or opposition
