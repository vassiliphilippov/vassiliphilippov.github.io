edgewise

adverb
with difficulty in a conversation
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdʒˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛdʒ.wʌɪz / (BrE)
(Especially in the phrase 'get a word in edgewise') if you get a word in edgewise, you manage to say something in a conversation dominated by others.

Examples

get a word in edgewise
I couldn't get a word in edgewise during the heated debate.
It's hard to get your opinion in edgewise when everyone is interrupting.
She talked so much that nobody else could speak edgewise.

Related Terms

  • synonym – edgeways: variant form with same meaning: as if by an edge; barely
