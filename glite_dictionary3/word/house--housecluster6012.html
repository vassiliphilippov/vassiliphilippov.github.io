house

noun
legislative assembly or body
Pronunciation: / haʊs / (AmE), / haʊs / (BrE)
an official assembly or body that has legislative powers, especially as part of a parliament or legislature

Examples

the House of Commons
Debates in the house often last for hours as members discuss key legislation.
Many countries have an upper and a lower house in their parliamentary systems.
