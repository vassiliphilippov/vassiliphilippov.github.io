moneron

noun
single-celled organism of former kingdom Monera
Pronunciation: / məˈnɪər.ɒn / (AmE), / məˈnɪə.rɒn / (BrE)
A single-celled organism without a distinct nucleus, once classified in the kingdom Monera, that typically reproduces by asexual budding or fission and obtains food by absorption, photosynthesis, or chemosynthesis.
