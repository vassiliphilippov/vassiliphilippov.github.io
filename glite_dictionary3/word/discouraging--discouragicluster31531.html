discouraging

adjective
causing loss of confidence or hope
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskɝːɪdʒɪŋ /; / dɪsˈkɝː.rɪ.dʒɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪsˈkʌrɪdʒɪŋ /; / dɪsˈkʌr.ɪ.dʒɪŋ / (BrE)
making someone less confident, hopeful, or willing to do something; deterring or dissuading
