recorder

noun
person who keeps official records
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɔrdər / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːdə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to keep official records or accounts in an organization or institution.

Examples

recorder of records
A trained recorder ensures that important information is accurately preserved for the future.
As city recorder, she is responsible for maintaining all municipal records.
The recorder of the meeting carefully documented every decision that was made.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clerk: a person employed to keep records and accounts or perform office work
  • synonym – secretary: an official who is responsible for keeping records, minutes, and correspondence
