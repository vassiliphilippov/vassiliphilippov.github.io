outcropping

noun
visible part of a rock formation
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌkrɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌkrɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
a part of a rock formation that is visible above the surface of the surrounding land

Examples

rock outcropping

Fossils were discovered embedded in a limestone outcropping near the village.
