infection

noun
spread of emotion or attitude
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
the spreading of an emotion, attitude, or enthusiasm from one person to others; contagious influence

Examples

emotional infection
Her infection of optimism brightened everyone in the office.
The coach’s infection of team spirit made the players believe they could win.
There was an infection of laughter that quickly spread throughout the crowd.
