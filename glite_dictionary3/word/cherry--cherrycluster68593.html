cherry

noun
small red fruit with a hard stone
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
A small, round, typically red or black fruit with a single hard stone at its center, produced by the cherry tree.

Examples

fresh cherry fruit
Fresh cherries are in season during the summer months.
She picked a ripe cherry from the tree and ate it immediately.
The cake was decorated with a bright red cherry on top.
