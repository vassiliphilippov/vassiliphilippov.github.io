restively

adverb
in a restless, fidgety manner
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛstɪvli / (AmE), / ˈrɛstɪvli / (BrE)
In a restless or fidgety manner, showing impatience or uneasiness.

Examples

shift restively in seat
During the long meeting, he shifted restively in his seat.
She tapped her fingers restively on the table while waiting for news.
The audience waited restively as the performance was delayed further.

Related Terms

  • antonym – calmly: in a peaceful or untroubled manner
  • synonym – restless: unable to relax or be still; constantly moving
