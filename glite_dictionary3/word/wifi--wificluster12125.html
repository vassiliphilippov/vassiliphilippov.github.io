WiFi

noun
wireless local area network technology
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈwʌɪˌfʌɪ / (BrE)
A system for connecting devices to a local area network wirelessly using high frequency radio signals, typically within a limited range.

Examples

connect to WiFi
Before streaming the movie, make sure your device is connected to the WiFi.
I couldn't join the meeting because the WiFi signal was too weak in my room.
Many coffee shops offer free WiFi to their customers for internet access.

Related Terms

  • antonym – ethernet: a wired networking technology used in LANs
  • synonym – wireless network: a network in which devices are connected without physical cables
