to thumb

verb
handle or feel with the thumb or fingers
Pronunciation: / θʌm / (AmE), / θʌm / (BrE)
to move, touch, or handle with the thumb or with the fingers, often in an exploratory or repeated way

Examples

to thumb the edge
He absentmindedly thumbed the edge of the envelope as he thought.
She thumbed the pages of the magazine while waiting for her appointment.
The detective thumbed through the old photos looking for clues.
