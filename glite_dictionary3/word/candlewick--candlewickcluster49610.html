candlewick

noun
soft tufted yarn or fabric pattern
Pronunciation: / ˈkændlˌwɪk / (AmE), / ˈkændlˌwɪk / (BrE)
A soft tufted cord or yarn, often forming patterns on fabric, especially used in bedspreads or textiles.

Examples

candlewick bedspread
Old-fashioned candlewick was popular in linens during the early twentieth century.
