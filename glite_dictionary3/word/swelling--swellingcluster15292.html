swelling

noun
increase in volume of a substance when heated
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈswɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
an increase in the volume of a substance, typically when heated, often accompanied by the release of water

Examples

swelling of dough
