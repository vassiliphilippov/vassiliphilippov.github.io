to pan-fry

verb
cook in a pan with a little oil or fat
Pronunciation: / ˈpænˌfraɪ / (AmE), / ˈpænˌfraɪ / (BrE)
to cook food in a small amount of oil or fat in a pan over direct heat

Examples

to pan-fry chicken pieces
I usually pan-fry chicken breasts for a quick and healthy meal.
It's best to pan-fry the vegetables until they are just tender.
The fish was pan-fried in olive oil and served with lemon slices.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – deep-fry: to cook food by immersing it fully in hot oil
  • synonym – fry: to cook in hot fat or oil, typically in a shallow pan
  • synonym – sauté: to quickly fry food in a small amount of fat
