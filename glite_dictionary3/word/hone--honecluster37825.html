to hone

verb
sharpen with a fine stone
Pronunciation: / hoʊn / (AmE), / həʊn / (BrE)
to sharpen (a blade or tool) with a fine stone or similar implement; to make sharper or more effective

Examples

to hone a blade
It takes practice to hone a blade to perfect sharpness.
She honed her woodworking tools to ensure clean cuts.
The chef carefully honed his knife before preparing the vegetables.
