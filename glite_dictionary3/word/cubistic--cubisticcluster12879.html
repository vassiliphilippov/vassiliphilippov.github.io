cubistic

adjective
relating to cubism (art style)
Pronunciation: / kjuˈbɪstɪk / (AmE), / kjuːˈbɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of cubism, an early 20th-century style of art that uses geometric shapes and multiple perspectives.

Examples

cubistic painting style
Many critics admire Picasso's cubistic period for its bold experimentation.
The cubistic style reshaped the way people perceived art in the early 1900s.
The artist's most recent paintings adopt a distinctly cubistic approach to form and space.
