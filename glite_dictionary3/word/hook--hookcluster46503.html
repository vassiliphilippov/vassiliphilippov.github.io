to hook

verb
seize with the foot (sport)
Pronunciation: / hʊk / (AmE), / hʊk / (BrE)
to use the foot to seize or secure something, especially in sports such as football or rugby

Examples

to hook the ball
In rugby, he tried to hook the ball out of the scrum using his boot.
She expertly hooked the ball with her foot to change its direction.
The defender managed to hook the ball away from the striker's feet.
