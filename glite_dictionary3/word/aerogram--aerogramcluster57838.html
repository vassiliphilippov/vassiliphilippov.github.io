aerogram

noun
letter sent by airmail
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈɛːrəˌɡram / (BrE)
A letter specially designed for sending by airmail, usually a lightweight sheet that folds into its own envelope.
