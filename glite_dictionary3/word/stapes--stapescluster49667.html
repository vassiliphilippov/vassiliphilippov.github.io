stapes

noun
bone in the middle ear transferring sound
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪpiːz / (AmE), / ˈsteɪpiːz / (BrE)
the stirrup-shaped bone in the middle ear that transmits sound vibrations from the incus to the cochlea

Examples

the stapes bone
After the surgery, her stapes function was significantly improved.
Damage to the stapes can result in hearing loss.
The stapes is the smallest bone in the human body.
