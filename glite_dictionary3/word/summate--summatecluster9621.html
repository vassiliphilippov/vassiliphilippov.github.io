to summate

verb
to add up; to total
Pronunciation: / səˈmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌmeɪt / (BrE)
to find or determine the total sum of a set of numbers, amounts, or items

Examples

to summate numbers
She carefully summated the expenses to ensure nothing was left out.
The results from each experiment were summated to calculate the overall effect.
When the points were summated, our team won by a narrow margin.

Related Terms

  • synonym – add: to combine numbers or quantities to get a total
  • antonym – subtract: to take a number or amount away from another
