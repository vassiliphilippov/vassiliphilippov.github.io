copybook

noun
book for practicing handwriting
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑpiˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈkɒpiˌbʊk / (BrE)
a book containing examples of handwriting for students to imitate, used for teaching penmanship

Examples

copybook handwriting practice
