bouillon

noun
clear seasoned broth
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːjɑn / (AmE), / ˈbuːjɒ̃ / (BrE)
a clear seasoned broth made by simmering meat, fish, or vegetables in water

Examples

hot bouillon soup

The chef prepared a delicate chicken bouillon as the first course.
Vegetable bouillon is often used as a base for soups and sauces.
