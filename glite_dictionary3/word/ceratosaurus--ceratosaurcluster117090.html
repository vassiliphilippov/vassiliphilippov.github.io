Ceratosaurus

noun
genus of horned carnivorous dinosaurs
Pronunciation: / sɛˌrætəˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / sɛˌrætəˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
A genus of primitive, carnivorous saurischian dinosaurs characterized by a prominent horn on the nose, known from the Jurassic period.

Fossils of Ceratosaurus have been found in North America and Africa.
