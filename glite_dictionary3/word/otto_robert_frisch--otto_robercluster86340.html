Otto Robert Frisch

proper noun
Austrian-British physicist; co-discovered nuclear fission
Pronunciation: / ˈɑtoʊ ˈroʊbərt ˈfrɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɒtəʊ ˈrəʊbət ˈfrɪʃ / (BrE)
(1904–1979) British physicist, born in Austria, who with Lise Meitner identified nuclear fission and described the potential of a nuclear chain reaction.

Otto Robert Frisch worked with Lise Meitner to explain the process of nuclear fission.
