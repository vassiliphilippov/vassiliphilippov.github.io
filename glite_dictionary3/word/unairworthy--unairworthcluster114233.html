unairworthy

adjective
not fit or safe for flight
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɛrˌwɜrði / (AmE), / ʌnˈeəˌwɜːði / (BrE)
not safe or fit for flight; not meeting the required standards for an aircraft to operate safely.

Examples

unairworthy aircraft
After the inspection, the mechanic declared the plane unairworthy and grounded it for repairs.
Flying an unairworthy helicopter poses serious risks to both crew and passengers.
The regulations prohibit airlines from transporting passengers in unairworthy aircraft.
