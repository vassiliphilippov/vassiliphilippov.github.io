stone

noun
British unit of weight (14 pounds)
Pronunciation: / stoʊn / (AmE), / stəʊn / (BrE)
a unit of weight equal to 14 pounds (about 6.35 kilograms), commonly used in Britain and Ireland to measure human body weight

Examples

weighs ten stone
He lost two stone after changing his diet and exercise routine.
In Britain, people often refer to their weight in stone rather than in kilograms.
The baby's weight was recorded as seven stone, which surprised everyone.
