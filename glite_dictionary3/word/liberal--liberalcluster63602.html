liberal

noun
person favoring reform and civil liberties
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪbərəl / (AmE), / ˈlɪbərəl / (BrE)
a person who supports or promotes political and social progress, reform, and the protection of individual rights and civil liberties

Examples

liberal activist
As a liberal, she advocated for free speech and equal rights.
Many liberals support environmental reform and social justice policies.
The debate featured a liberal and a conservative discussing education reform.

  • synonym – progressive: person advocating social reform and new ideas
