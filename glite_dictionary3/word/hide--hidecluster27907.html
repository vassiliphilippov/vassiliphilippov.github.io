to hide

verb
stay out of sight for safety
Pronunciation: / haɪd / (AmE), / haɪd / (BrE)
to go or stay somewhere secretly so that no one can see or find you, especially for protection or safety

Examples

to hide under the bed
After hearing the intruders, she quickly hid under the bed to avoid being seen.
He was hiding in the basement until the storm passed.
The children hid behind the bushes during their game of hide-and-seek.

Related Terms

  • synonym – conceal: to prevent something from being seen or known about
  • antonym – reveal: to make something known that was secret or hidden
