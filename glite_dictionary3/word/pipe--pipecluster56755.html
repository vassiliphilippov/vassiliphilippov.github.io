to pipe

verb
transport by pipeline
Pronunciation: / paɪp / (AmE), / paɪp / (BrE)
to transport a substance, such as oil, gas, or water, by means of a pipe or pipeline

Examples

to pipe oil
Engineers are piping oil across hundreds of miles through underground pipelines.
Fresh water is piped into remote villages in the mountains.
The company plans to pipe natural gas from Russia to Europe.
