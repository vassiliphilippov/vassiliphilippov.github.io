unregenerated

adjective
not morally or spiritually reformed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈdʒɛn.ɚˌeɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈdʒen.ər.eɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
not having been reformed or changed, especially in a moral or spiritual sense; remaining unchanged or unreformed.

Examples

an unregenerated criminal
Despite years in prison, he remained an unregenerated criminal with no sign of remorse.
Some traditions still hold unregenerated views that resist modern reforms.
The preacher spoke of the necessity to help the unregenerated find spiritual renewal.
