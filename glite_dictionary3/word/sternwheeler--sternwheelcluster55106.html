sternwheeler

noun
paddle steamer with rear wheel
Pronunciation: / ˈstɝːnˌwiːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɜːnˌwiːlə / (BrE)
a paddle steamer with its paddle wheel located at the stern (rear) of the vessel

Examples

historic sternwheeler boat
The sternwheeler glided gracefully down the Mississippi River under a cloudless sky.
The museum features a restored sternwheeler that once carried cargo and passengers along the Columbia River.
Tourists boarded the historic sternwheeler for a sunset cruise.
