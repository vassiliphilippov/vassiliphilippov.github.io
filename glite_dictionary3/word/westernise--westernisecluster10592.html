to westernise

verb
make more Western in character
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstərnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈwɛstənaɪz / (BrE)
to make more like the customs, culture, or characteristics of Western countries, especially Europe or North America

Examples

to westernise a country
After decades of isolation, the nation began to westernise rapidly, adopting new fashions and technologies.
Many countries have attempted to westernise their education systems to compete internationally.
The government tried to westernise the economy by encouraging foreign investment and privatization.
