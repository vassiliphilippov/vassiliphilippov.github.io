to dim

verb
lower car headlights' beam
Pronunciation: / dɪm / (AmE), / dɪm / (BrE)
to switch (a car's headlights) from a higher to a lower beam

Examples

to dim headlights
It's safer to dim your high beams in populated areas.
Please dim your headlights when another car approaches.
