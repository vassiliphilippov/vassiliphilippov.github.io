chalk

noun
stick for writing or drawing on blackboards
Pronunciation: / tʃɔk /; / tʃɑk / (AmE), / tʃɔːk / (BrE)
a soft, white or colored substance (or stick made from it) used for writing or drawing, or the natural white rock it comes from
