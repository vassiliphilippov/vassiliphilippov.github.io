weeaboo

noun
person obsessed with Japanese culture (slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːəˌbuː / (AmE), / ˈwiːəˌbuː / (BrE)
(slang, often pejorative) A non-Japanese person, especially a white person, who is obsessed with Japanese culture, particularly anime and manga, often to the point of adopting mannerisms perceived as Japanese.

Examples

anime weeaboo
Despite being called a weeaboo, she genuinely appreciates Japanese language and culture.
On the internet, being labeled a weeaboo can carry negative connotations.
Some people call Jake a weeaboo because he only watches Japanese anime and uses random Japanese words.

Related Terms

  • synonym – otaku: (in English) a person with obsessive interests, especially in anime and manga
