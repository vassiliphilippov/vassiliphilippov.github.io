Foeniculum vulgare dulce

noun
cultivated fennel with edible bulbous stem base
Pronunciation: / foʊˌɛnɪkjuːləm vʌlˈɡɛər i ˈdʊl tʃeɪ / (AmE), / fəʊˌɛnɪkjuːləm vʌlˈɡɑːrɪ ˈdʊl tʃeɪ / (BrE)
A cultivated variety of fennel grown particularly for its edible, aromatic, bulbous stem base, commonly known as Florence fennel or finocchio.
