distraction

noun
amusement or diversion from worries
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstrækʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈstrækʃən / (BrE)
an activity or entertainment that amuses or diverts attention, especially from worries or unpleasant thoughts

Examples

fun distraction
Reading a novel is a welcome distraction from the stress of daily life.
She used music as a distraction to help her relax after a difficult day.
The children enjoyed the magician's show as a lively distraction during the long afternoon.

  • synonym – amusement: something that entertains or amuses
  • synonym – entertainment: an event, performance, or activity designed to entertain
  • antonym – worry: a feeling of anxiety or concern
