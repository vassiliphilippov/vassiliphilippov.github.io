puma

noun
large wild cat of the Americas
Pronunciation: / ˈpuː.mə / (AmE), / ˈpjuː.mə / (BrE)
A large, slender, wild cat native to the Americas, with a tawny coat and no spots or stripes, also known as the cougar or mountain lion.

Examples

wild puma
A puma was spotted roaming near the edge of the forest last night.
Many ranchers in South America have learned how to protect livestock from pumas.
The puma is an agile predator, capable of leaping great distances to catch its prey.
