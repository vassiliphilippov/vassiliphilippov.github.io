pianissimo

adverb
very softly (music direction)
Pronunciation: / piːəˈnɪsɪˌmoʊ / (AmE), / piːəˈnɪsɪməʊ / (BrE)
(in music) played or to be played very softly.

Examples

to play pianissimo passages
In the score, the passage marked pianissimo demands great control from the performer.
Please play the final bars pianissimo for a gentle ending.
The piece begins pianissimo, creating a delicate and intimate atmosphere.

  • degree higher – piano: a direction in music; to be played softly
