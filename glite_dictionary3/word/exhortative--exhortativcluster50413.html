exhortative

adjective
giving strong encouragement
Pronunciation: / ɛɡˈzɔːr.tə.tɪv / (AmE), / ɛɡˈzɔː.tə.tɪv / (BrE)

Examples

exhortative speech
His tone was exhortative, pushing the team to strive for success despite the difficulties.
She wrote an exhortative letter urging her friend not to give up on her studies.
The leader delivered an exhortative speech to inspire the volunteers before their work began.
