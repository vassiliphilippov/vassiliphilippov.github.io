prefecture

noun
the office or area of a prefect
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌfɛktʃər / (AmE), / ˈpriːfɛktʃə / (BrE)
an administrative district or area governed by a prefect, especially in countries like France, Japan, or ancient Rome
