gewgaw

noun
cheap, showy ornament or jewelry
Pronunciation: / ˈɡyuːˌɡɔ / (AmE), / ˈɡjuːˌɡɔː / (BrE)
a small, cheap, showy ornament or piece of jewelry, often considered gaudy or of little value

Examples

The necklace looked pretty, but it was just another gewgaw bought from a tourist shop.
