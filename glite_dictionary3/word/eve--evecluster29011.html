Eve

proper noun
the first woman in the Bible
Pronunciation: / iv / (AmE), / iːv / (BrE)
In the Bible, the first woman, created by God as the wife of Adam and mother of all human beings.

Examples

Adam and Eve
According to the Book of Genesis, Eve was created from one of Adam’s ribs.
In Western art, Eve is often depicted holding an apple in the Garden of Eden.
Many theological discussions center on the actions of Eve and Adam in the story of original sin.
