nonresident

noun
person not living in a specified place
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈrɛzɪdənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈrɛzɪdənt / (BrE)
a person who does not live in a particular place, especially one who is not a permanent resident of a country, state, or city

Examples

international nonresident
As a nonresident, she had to pay higher tuition fees for the university.
Many nonresidents visit the city during the festival season.
Taxes for nonresident workers are calculated differently from those of residents.
