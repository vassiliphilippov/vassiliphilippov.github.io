to card

verb
separate fibers with a carding tool
Pronunciation: / kɑrd / (AmE), / kɑːd / (BrE)
to separate and straighten the fibers of wool, cotton, or other material using a carding tool or machine

Examples

to card wool
Before spinning the wool, you must card it thoroughly to remove any tangles.
Cotton is often carded before it is dyed and spun into thread.
She learned how to card fleece in her textile arts class.
