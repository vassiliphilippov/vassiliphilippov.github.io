untellable

adjective
too great to be described
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɛləbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɛləbəl / (BrE)
too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words; inexpressible.

Examples

untellable sadness
She felt an untellable sadness that words could not express.
The painting was of untellable beauty and left everyone speechless.
The survivors endured untellable hardships during their journey.

  • synonym – indescribable: impossible to describe; too extraordinary for words

  • synonym – unspeakable: so bad or extreme that it cannot be expressed in words
