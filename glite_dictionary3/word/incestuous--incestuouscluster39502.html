incestuous

adjective
excessively close or exclusive relationships
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɛstʃuəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛstjuːəs / (BrE)
(of relationships or activities) excessively close or exclusive, resembling or suggestive of incest, especially by involving only a small group with limited outside contact.

Examples

incestuous business group
His incestuous association with the same business partners raised eyebrows among competitors.
Some countries have an incestuous media environment where only a few voices are ever heard.
The committee's incestuous relationships prevented new ideas from coming in.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cliquish: tending to form exclusive groups
  • synonym – exclusive: not admitting others to join; closed group
