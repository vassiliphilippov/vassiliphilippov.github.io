plunge

noun
brief swim or immersion in water
Pronunciation: / plʌndʒ / (AmE), / plʌndʒ / (BrE)
a brief swim or immersion in water, especially for refreshment or as a sudden action

Examples

a refreshing plunge
After a long hike, she enjoyed a refreshing plunge in the lake.
He took a quick plunge into the ocean before breakfast.
The cold weather didn't stop them from having a morning plunge.
