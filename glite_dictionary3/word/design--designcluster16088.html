design

noun
a plan or intention guiding actions
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈzʌɪn / (BrE)
a plan or purpose that guides actions; an intention, often with a specific aim or, occasionally, a secret motive

Examples

secret design
He acted friendly, but his real design was to undermine their trust.
Her designs on the property were well known to everyone in the village.
Many suspected that he had a design to seize power.

Related Terms

  • synonym – aim: a purpose or intention; a desired outcome
  • synonym – intention: something that you intend or plan to do
  • synonym – purpose: the reason for which something is done or created
