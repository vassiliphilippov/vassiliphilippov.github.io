gabled

adjective
having a triangular end (gable), as in architecture
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪbəld / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪbəld / (BrE)
(of a building or roof) having one or more gables, typically relating to a roof with a pitched triangular end.

Examples

gabled roof design
Many traditional houses in the village feature gabled ends facing the street.
The cottage had a charming gabled roof with intricate woodwork along the edges.
We admired the old gabled barn standing at the edge of the field.
