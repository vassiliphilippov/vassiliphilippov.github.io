verbaliser

noun
person who expresses ideas in words
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜːrbəlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈvɜːbəlaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who expresses ideas in words, especially by speaking, or who tends to speak a lot
