unbelted

adjective
not having a belt (especially a garment)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɛltɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɛltɪd / (BrE)
(of a garment) not having a belt, or not fastened with a belt.

Examples

unbelted coat
His unbelted raincoat flapped open in the wind.
She wore an unbelted dress that flowed loosely around her waist.
The new collection features unbelted coats for a more relaxed look.
