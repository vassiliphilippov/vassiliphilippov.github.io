round-arm

adjective, adverb
with a horizontal or outward arm swing
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊnd ɑrm / (AmE), / ˈraʊnd ɑːm / (BrE)
using or describing a throwing or hitting action where the arm swings outward at shoulder height, not over the top or underarm (especially in sports like cricket or baseball)
