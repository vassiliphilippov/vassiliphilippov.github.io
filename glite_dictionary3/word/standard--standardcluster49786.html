standard

noun
basis of comparison; accepted level
Pronunciation: / ˈstændɚd / (AmE), / ˈstændəd / (BrE)
an accepted level of quality, rule, or example used as a basis for comparison or judgment

Examples

high standard

We expect employees to maintain the highest standards of honesty.
