Arminian

adjective
relating to Arminianism (Christian theology)
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈmɪniən / (AmE), / ɑːˈmɪniən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Arminianism, a Christian theological doctrine emphasizing free will in salvation.

Examples

Arminian beliefs
Her theological writings reflect a strong Arminian influence.
Many Methodist churches hold to Arminian doctrines regarding salvation.
The Arminian perspective on predestination differs from the Calvinist view.
