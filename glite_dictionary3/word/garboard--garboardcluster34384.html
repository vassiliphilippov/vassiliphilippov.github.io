garboard

noun
plank next to the keel of a ship
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɔːd / (BrE)
the first plank or wale laid next to the keel of a wooden ship, forming the lowest part of the hull
