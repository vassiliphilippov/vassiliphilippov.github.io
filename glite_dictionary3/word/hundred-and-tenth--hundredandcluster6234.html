hundred-and-tenth

adjective
ordinal number for 110th
Pronunciation: / ˌhʌndrəd ən ˈtɛnθ / (AmE), / ˌhʌndrɪd ən ˈtɛnθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number corresponding to one hundred and ten, coming after one hundred and ninth in a sequence.
