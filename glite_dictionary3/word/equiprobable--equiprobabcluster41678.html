equiprobable

adjective
equally likely to occur
Pronunciation: / ˌiːkwəˈproʊbəbəl / (AmE), / ˌiːkwɪˈprɒbəbl / (BrE)
having an equal likelihood of occurring; equally probable.

Examples

equiprobable outcomes
If each team has an equiprobable chance of winning, the game is considered fair.
In a fair coin toss, heads and tails are equiprobable outcomes.
The model assumes that all possible results are equiprobable.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – certain: sure to happen; inevitable
  • antonym – improbable: not likely to happen or be true
  • degree lower – probable: likely to happen or be the case
