misalignment

noun
incorrect positioning or alignment
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪs.əˈlaɪn.mənt / (AmE), / ˌmɪs.əˈlaɪn.mənt / (BrE)
the state or condition of things not being properly aligned or in the correct relative position

Examples

misalignment of wheels
A misalignment of the car's wheels may lead to uneven tire wear.
Eye strain can result from a misalignment between your monitor and your natural line of sight.
The engineer discovered a misalignment in the gears that caused the machine to malfunction.

Related Terms

  • antonym – alignment: the arrangement in a straight line or in correct position
