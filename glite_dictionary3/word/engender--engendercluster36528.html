to engender

verb
produce offspring; reproduce
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈdʒɛndɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɛndə / (BrE)
to produce or bring into existence (offspring) through reproduction

Examples

to engender new life
Ancient myths describe gods who could engender entire races with a single thought.
In certain animals, both male and female contribute genetically to engender their young.
The queen bee engenders thousands of offspring each season.

Related Terms

  • synonym – beget: to father or produce offspring
  • synonym – father: to be the male parent of a child
