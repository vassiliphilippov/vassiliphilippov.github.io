weapon

noun
means of gaining advantage, especially figurative
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛpən / (AmE), / ˈwɛpən / (BrE)
A thing used to gain an advantage in an argument or competition, especially in a figurative sense.

Examples

a weapon in debate
He brought out every conversational weapon he had to win the argument.
Humor proved to be his most effective weapon in the debate.
She saw charm as a valuable weapon in business negotiations.
