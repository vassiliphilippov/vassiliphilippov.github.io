canopy

noun
a roof-like covering for shelter
Pronunciation: / ˈkænəpi / (AmE), / ˈkænəpi / (BrE)
a covering, typically of fabric, that is used as a roof or shelter to protect an area from the sun or rain

Examples

canopy bed
As they walked, thick vines formed a natural canopy overhead, sheltering them from the sun.
The outdoor market had a colorful canopy over each stall to protect vendors from rain.
We set up a canopy in the garden to provide shade during the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym – awning: a sheet of canvas or other material stretched on a frame and used to keep the sun or rain off a storefront, window, doorway, or deck
  • synonym – roof: the structure forming the upper covering of a building or vehicle
