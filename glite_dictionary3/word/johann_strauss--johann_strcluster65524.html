Johann Strauss

proper noun
Austrian composer of famous waltzes
Pronunciation: / joʊˌhɑn ˈstraʊs / (AmE), / jəʊˌhan ˈstraʊs / (BrE)
(1825–1899) An Austrian composer famous for his waltzes, known as the ‘waltz king’, and the son of Johann Strauss the Elder.
