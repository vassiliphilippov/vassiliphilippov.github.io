rapacious

adjective
aggressively greedy
Pronunciation: / rəˈpeɪʃəs / (AmE), / rəˈpeɪʃəs / (BrE)
Aggressively greedy or grasping for wealth or possessions.

Examples

rapacious business practices
Environmental groups criticized the corporation for its rapacious exploitation of natural resources.
Her rapacious appetite for fame led her to betray her closest friends.
The rapacious landlord constantly raised the rent to extract more money from his tenants.
