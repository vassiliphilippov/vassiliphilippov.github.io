press-up

noun
exercise of raising and lowering the body using the arms
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛs ʌp / (AmE), / ˈprɛs ʌp / (BrE)
an exercise in which you lie facing down and raise and lower your body using your arms, supporting your weight on your hands and toes

Examples

do press-ups daily
He collapsed to the floor after completing the last set of press-ups.
She does fifty press-ups every morning as part of her workout.
The coach told the team to drop and give him twenty press-ups.

Related Terms

  • synonym – push-up: an arm exercise performed lying face down and pushing the body up and down with the arms (American English)
