Monocotyledones

noun
group of flowering plants with a single seed leaf
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnoʊˌkɑːtɪlɪˈdoʊniːz / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəʊˌkɒtɪlɪˈdəʊniːz /; / ˌmɒnəkɒtɪlɪˈdəʊniːz / (BrE)
a major group of flowering plants whose seeds have a single seed leaf (cotyledon), usually with parallel-veined leaves; includes grasses, lilies, palms, and orchids
