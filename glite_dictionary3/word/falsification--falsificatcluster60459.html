falsification

noun
deliberate misrepresentation of facts
Pronunciation: / ˌfælsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌfɔlsəfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfɔːlsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of deliberately altering, distorting, or misrepresenting facts or documents to make them false
