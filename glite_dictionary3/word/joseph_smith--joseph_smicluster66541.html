Joseph Smith

proper noun
founder of the Mormon Church
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒoʊzəf smɪθ / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊzɪf smɪθ / (BrE)
(1805–1844) The American religious leader who founded the Mormon Church (The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints) in 1830.

Joseph Smith claimed to have translated the Book of Mormon through divine guidance.
