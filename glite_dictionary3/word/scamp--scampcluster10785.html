scamp

noun
mischievous or cheeky person
Pronunciation: / skæmp / (AmE), / skamp / (BrE)
A person, especially a child, who is playfully mischievous or cheeky.

Examples

cheeky scamp
Everyone laughed as the scamp pulled a funny prank during the party.
That little scamp hid my keys again this morning.
Though he tried to look innocent, the scamp had mischief written all over his face.
