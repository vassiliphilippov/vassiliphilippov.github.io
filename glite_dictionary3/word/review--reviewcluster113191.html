review

noun
critical evaluation article or essay
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvju / (AmE), / rɪˈvjuː / (BrE)
an essay, article, or spoken judgment that evaluates and gives an opinion about a book, play, film, or other work

Examples

review of a movie
Before seeing the play, I always read at least one review.
She wrote a detailed review of the new novel for the newspaper.
The film received an excellent review from several critics.
After the disappointing results, a full review of the strategy was conducted.
She requested a review of her performance evaluation by a different manager.
The committee ordered a review of the company’s safety practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym – assessment: the act of making a judgment about something
  • synonym – critique: a detailed analysis and assessment of something, especially a literary, philosophical, or political theory
  • synonym – evaluation: the process of judging or calculating the value of something
  • antonym – ignore: to pay no attention to
  • antonym – recommendation: an endorsement or positive opinion about something, in contrast to a neutral or negative evaluation
