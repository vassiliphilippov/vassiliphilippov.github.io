frontal

noun
forehead adornment
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌn.tl̩ /; / ˈfrʌn.təl / (AmE), / ˈfrʌn.təl / (BrE)
an ornamental or decorative covering for the front, such as a band worn on the forehead or a cloth covering the front of an altar

Examples

frontal altar cloth
A silver frontal adorned the dancer's forehead, catching the light as she moved.
Ancient priests often included a golden frontal as part of their sacred vestments.

The church committee ordered a new frontal to match the recently restored altar.
The richly embroidered frontal was changed for each season of the church year.
