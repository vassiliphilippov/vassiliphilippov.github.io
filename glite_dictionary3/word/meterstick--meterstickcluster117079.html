meterstick

noun
measuring stick one meter long
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːtərˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈmiːtəˌstɪk / (BrE)
a measuring stick one meter long, usually marked with centimeters and millimeters

Examples

meterstick for measuring length
A meterstick is essential for measuring boards and other long objects in the workshop.
He measured the length of the table with a meterstick to ensure it fit in the space.
The teacher used a meterstick to draw a straight line on the chalkboard.
