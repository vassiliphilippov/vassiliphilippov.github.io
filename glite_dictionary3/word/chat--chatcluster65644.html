to chat

verb
talk informally
Pronunciation: / tʃæt / (AmE), / tʃæt / (BrE)
to talk in a friendly and informal way about unimportant matters

Examples

to chat with friends
She enjoys chatting with her neighbors during her evening walk.
We sat by the fire and chatted about our childhood memories.
While waiting for the train, they chatted about the weather and their weekend plans.

Related Terms

  • synonym – converse: to talk with others, usually in a formal way
  • degree higher – gossip: to talk about personal or sensational topics, sometimes about others
