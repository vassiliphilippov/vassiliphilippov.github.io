tantalising

adjective
arousing longing for the unobtainable
Pronunciation: / ˈtæn.təˌlaɪ.zɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtæn.tə.laɪ.zɪŋ /; / ˈtan.tə.laɪ.zɪŋ / (BrE)
arousing desire or expectation for something that is attractive but just out of reach or unobtainable

Examples

tantalising smell of food

She felt a tantalising sense of adventure after reading his mysterious letter.
The aroma of fresh bread was tantalising to those waiting outside the bakery.

The tantalising smell of freshly baked bread filled the kitchen.
The prospect of a holiday by the sea was tantalising after months of hard work.
