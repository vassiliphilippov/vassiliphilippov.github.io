prospective

adjective
relating to the future; expected
Pronunciation: / prəˈspɛktɪv / (AmE), / prəˈspɛktɪv / (BrE)
relating to or likely to happen in the future; expected to become something or someone.

Examples

prospective student
All prospective students must submit their applications by July 31.
She is a prospective candidate for the job opening next month.
The company is reviewing the prospective benefits of the new project.
