measure

noun
process of assigning numerical values
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒə / (BrE)
the act or process of assigning a value, usually numerical, to a quantity, object, or phenomenon according to a defined standard or rule

Examples

measure of temperature
Accurate measure of temperature is essential in scientific experiments.
Each measure was repeated three times to ensure consistency.
The researchers developed a new measure to assess cognitive performance.
