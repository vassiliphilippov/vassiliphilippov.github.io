gelatinous

adjective
thick and jelly-like
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈlætənəs / (AmE), / dʒəˈlætɪnəs / (BrE)
Thick and sticky like gelatin; having a jelly-like consistency.

Examples

gelatinous texture
After cooling, the broth became gelatinous and wobbly.
He found the gelatinous mass of the dessert unappealing.
The sauce had a gelatinous texture that coated the vegetables evenly.
