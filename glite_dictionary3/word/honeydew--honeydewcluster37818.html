honeydew

noun
a type of sweet, pale green melon
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌniˌdu / (AmE), / ˈhʌnɪdjuː / (BrE)
a large, round, smooth-skinned melon with pale green flesh and a greenish-white rind, popular as a sweet fruit
