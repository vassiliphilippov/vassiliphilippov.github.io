to vascularise

verb
form or develop blood vessels
Pronunciation: / ˈvæskjəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈvæskjʊləraɪz /; / ˈvaskjʊləraɪz / (BrE)
to develop or supply blood vessels in an organ or tissue

Examples
