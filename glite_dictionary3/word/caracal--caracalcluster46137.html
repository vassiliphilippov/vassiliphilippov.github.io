caracal

noun
a medium-sized wild cat with tufted ears
Pronunciation: / ˈkærəkæl / (AmE), / ˈkarəkal / (BrE)
a medium-sized wild cat with long tufted ears, native to deserts and dry regions of northern Africa and southern Asia
