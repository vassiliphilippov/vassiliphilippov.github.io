sportscaster

noun
person who reports on sports events
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔrtsˌkæstər / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtskɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who reports on or narrates sports events for television, radio, or other media

Examples

famous sportscaster on TV
Aspiring to become a sportscaster, she practiced announcing local sports events.
During the game, the sportscaster provided lively play-by-play commentary.
The famous sportscaster covered the Super Bowl for more than twenty years.
