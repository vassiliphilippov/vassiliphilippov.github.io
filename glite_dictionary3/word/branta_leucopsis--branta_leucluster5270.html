Branta leucopsis

noun
species of European goose (barnacle goose)
Pronunciation: / ˈbræntə luːˈkɑpsɪs / (AmE), / ˈbræntə ljuːˈkɒpsɪs / (BrE)
A European species of goose, smaller than the brant, which breeds in the far north; commonly known as the barnacle goose.
