housemother

noun
leader/mentor of a drag or ballroom house
Pronunciation: / \u02c8ha\u028as\u02ccm\u028c\u00f0\u025a / (AmE), / \u02c8ha\u028as\u02ccm\u028c\u00f0\u0259 / (BrE)
A senior female member who leads, mentors, and cares for members of a drag or ballroom 'house', guiding performances and offering emotional and practical support.

Examples

housemother at a drag house
The housemother of the local drag house gave each newcomer advice before their first performance.
At the ball, the housemother announced the lineup and coached contestants between walks.
When the youngest queen struggled, the housemother arranged practice sessions and introduced her to promoters.
