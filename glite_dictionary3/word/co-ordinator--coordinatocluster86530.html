co-ordinator

noun
a person who organizes people or activities
Pronunciation: / koʊˈɔrdəˌneɪtər / (AmE), / kəʊˈɔː.dɪ.neɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to organize people or activities so that they work together effectively and harmoniously

Examples

co-ordinator of a project
As project co-ordinator, she ensures that every team member knows their tasks.
The event co-ordinator handled last-minute changes with remarkable calm.
The school hired a new co-ordinator to manage all after-school programs.
