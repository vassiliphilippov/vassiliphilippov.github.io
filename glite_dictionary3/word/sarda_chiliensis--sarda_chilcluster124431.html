Sarda chiliensis

noun
Pacific bonito species with oily flesh
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑrdə kɪliˈɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɑːdə kɪlɪˈɛnsɪs / (BrE)
a species of bonito found along the Pacific coast of the Americas, valued for its dark oily flesh, which is suitable for canning
