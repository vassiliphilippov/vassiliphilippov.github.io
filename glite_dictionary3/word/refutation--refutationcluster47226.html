refutation

noun
the act of proving something false
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛfjuˈteɪʃən /; / ˌrɛfjəˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛfjʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of proving that a statement, theory, or accusation is wrong or false, or the speech, writing, or evidence that does this
