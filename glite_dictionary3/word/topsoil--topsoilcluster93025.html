topsoil

noun
upper layer of soil
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑpˌsɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈtɒpˌsɔɪl / (BrE)
the uppermost layer of soil, usually rich in organic matter and nutrients, in which most plants have their roots

Examples

topsoil layer
Gardeners often add new topsoil to improve the quality of their plants' growth.
