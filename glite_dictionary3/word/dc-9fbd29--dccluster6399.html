DC

noun
abbreviation for data center
Pronunciation: / diː siː / (AmE), / diː siː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for data center: a facility that houses computer servers, storage systems, networking equipment, and supporting infrastructure used to store, process, and distribute data.

Examples

DC server room
The company expanded its network by opening a new DC in the Midwest to reduce latency.
During maintenance, engineers took the primary DC offline and shifted traffic to backups.
Startups often colocate servers in a shared DC to avoid high capital expenses.

Related Terms

  • synonym – data center: a building or space that contains computer servers, storage, and networking equipment for large-scale data processing and storage
  • synonym – server farm: a group of computer servers maintained together in a single location to provide services or store data
