tramp

noun
vagrant or homeless wanderer
Pronunciation: / træmp / (AmE), / træmp / (BrE)
a person who has no home or job and travels from place to place, usually living by begging or doing casual work

Examples

a homeless tramp begging
She gave the tired-looking tramp a sandwich and a cup of tea.
The city shelter provides meals and beds for tramps and the homeless.
The old tramp wandered the streets, carrying all his belongings in a battered suitcase.

Related Terms

  • synonym – beggar: a person, typically homeless, who lives by asking for money or food
  • synonym – drifter: a person who moves from place to place without a fixed home or job
  • synonym – vagrant: a person without a settled home or regular work, who wanders from place to place
