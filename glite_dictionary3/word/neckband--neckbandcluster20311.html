neckband

noun
wearable band with speakers/earbuds
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛkˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈnɛkˌbænd / (BrE)
a wearable audio device consisting of a flexible band worn around the neck that holds speakers, earphones, and the device's electronics (often wireless).

Examples

wireless neckband headphones
I recently bought a pair of neckband headphones for my workouts.
She draped the Bluetooth neckband around her shoulders and pressed play.
During the flight, he used a soft silicone neckband to listen to podcasts without earbuds.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Bluetooth headset: a wireless audio device that connects by Bluetooth, often worn around the head or neck
  • synonym – headphones: electronic devices worn over the ears or in the ear to listen to sound
