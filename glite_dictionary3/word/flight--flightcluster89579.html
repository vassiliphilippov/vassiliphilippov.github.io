flight

noun
group of flying birds
Pronunciation: / flaɪt / (AmE), / flaɪt / (BrE)
A group of birds flying together in the air.

Examples

flight of birds
A flight of geese passed overhead at sunset, forming a distinct V-shaped pattern.
The hunters watched in awe as a flight of ducks took off from the marsh.
We saw a flight of swallows darting over the lake in search of insects.
