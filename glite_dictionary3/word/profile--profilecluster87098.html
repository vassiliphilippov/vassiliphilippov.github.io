to profile

verb
depict side view or outline of a face
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊ.faɪl / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.faɪl / (BrE)
to represent or depict the outline or side view of a person's face, especially by drawing or painting

Examples

to profile a face
In her latest sketch, she profiles her subject with a delicate, expressive line.
The artist carefully profiled each member of the cast for the commemorative painting.
The mural profiles several historical figures in a striking side view.
