invalidated

adjective
declared legally null or void
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvælɪˌdeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈvalɪdeɪtɪd / (BrE)

Examples

invalidated contract

The results of the election were invalidated due to widespread voting irregularities.
