Spermophilus

noun
a genus of typical ground squirrels
Pronunciation: / spərˈmɑːfɪləs / (AmE), / spəːˈmɒfɪləs / (BrE)
a genus of ground squirrels commonly referred to as typical ground squirrels, found mostly in Eurasia and North America
