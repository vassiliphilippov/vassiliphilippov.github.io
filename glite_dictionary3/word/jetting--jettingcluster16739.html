jetting

adjective
propelled in a narrow stream
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛt.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛt.ɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a liquid or substance) being propelled violently in a usually narrow stream.

Examples

jetting stream of water
A jetting stream of water shot from the fire hose, knocking over the debris in seconds.
Molten metal was jetting from the foundry's furnace, illuminating the dark workshop.
They stared at the jetting oil pouring out of the damaged pipeline.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gushing: pouring out quickly and in large amounts
  • synonym – spraying: dispersing liquid in tiny drops
