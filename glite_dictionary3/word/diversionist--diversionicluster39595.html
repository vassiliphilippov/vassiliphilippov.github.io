diversionist

noun
person who commits sabotage or disruption
Pronunciation: / dəˈvɝːʒənɪst / (AmE), / dɪˈvɜːʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who commits sabotage or deliberately causes disruption, especially to divert attention from an intended target

Examples

During wartime, a diversionist might be sent behind enemy lines to disrupt communications.
