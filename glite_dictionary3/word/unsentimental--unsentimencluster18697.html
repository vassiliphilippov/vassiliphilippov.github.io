unsentimental

adjective
not influenced by emotion; realistic
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsɛn.təˈmɛn.t̬əl / (AmE), / ˌʌnsen.tɪˈmen.təl / (BrE)
Not influenced by emotion or sentiment; dealing with facts or situations realistically and with determination.

Examples

unsentimental approach
His unsentimental attitude allowed him to make tough decisions without hesitation.
She offered an unsentimental account of their financial troubles, focusing solely on the facts.
The documentary gives an unsentimental look at life in the city, revealing both its beauty and hardships.
