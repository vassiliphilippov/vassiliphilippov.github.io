to translate

verb
result in an equivalent effect or outcome
Pronunciation: / trænzˈleɪt / (AmE), / trænsˈleɪt / (BrE)
(of one thing) to lead to or result in an equivalent or corresponding effect, outcome, or form.

Examples

translate into results
A higher score on the test does not always translate into better job performance.
The new policy will translate into lower energy costs for consumers.
Their support for the project could translate into increased funding from donors.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lead to: to have something as a result
  • synonym – result in: to cause a particular situation or outcome
