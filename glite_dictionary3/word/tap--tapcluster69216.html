tap

noun
tool for cutting internal screw threads
Pronunciation: / tæp / (AmE), / tæp / (BrE)
a tool used for cutting female (internal) screw threads in a hole

Examples

thread-cutting tap
A broken tap can be difficult to remove from the hole without special tools.
He bought a new tap set for his workshop to repair damaged threads.
To create a threaded hole, you first drill the hole and then use a tap to cut the threads.
