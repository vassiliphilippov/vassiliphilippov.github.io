watercolour

noun
paint with water-soluble pigments
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔːtərˌkʌlɚ /; / ˈwɔt̬ərˌkʌlɚ /; / ˈwɑtərˌkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌkʌlə /; / ˈwɔːtəkʌlə / (BrE)
a paint made with pigments suspended in water, used by artists for translucent and delicate effects
