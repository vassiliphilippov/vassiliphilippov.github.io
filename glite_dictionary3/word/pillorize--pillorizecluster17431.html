to pillorize

verb
criticize or ridicule publicly
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪləˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈpɪləˌraɪz / (BrE)
to criticize or ridicule someone publicly, often in a harsh or humiliating manner

Examples

to pillorize a public figure
It is unfair to pillorize someone for a single mistake without considering their intentions.
Social media can sometimes pillorize public figures, making their errors go viral.
The press was quick to pillorize the politician after the scandal emerged.

Related Terms

  • synonym – criticize: to express disapproval of someone or something
  • synonym – ridicule: to mock or make fun of someone
