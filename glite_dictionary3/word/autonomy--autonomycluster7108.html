autonomy

noun
self-government; political independence
Pronunciation: / əˈtɔnəmi / (AmE), / ɔːˈtɒnəmi / (BrE)
the right or condition of self-government, especially in a political context; freedom from external control or influence

Examples

political autonomy
Cultural autonomy is important for preserving minority languages.
Many former colonies fought for autonomy in the twentieth century.
The region demanded greater autonomy from the central government.

Related Terms

  • synonym – independence: the fact or state of being independent
  • synonym – sovereignty: supreme power or authority
  • antonym – subjugation: the act of bringing someone or something under domination or control
