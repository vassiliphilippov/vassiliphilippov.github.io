leguminous

adjective
relating to legumes (plants like beans and peas)
Pronunciation: / lɪˈɡjuːmɪnəs / (AmE), / lɪˈɡjuːmɪnəs / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of plants of the legume family, such as beans, peas, and lentils.

Examples
