landau

noun
a type of four-wheeled carriage with a divided, folding roof
Pronunciation: / ˈlænˌdaʊ / (AmE), / ˈlændau / (BrE)
a four-wheeled carriage with a roof divided into two sections that can be lowered or removed separately at the front and back

Examples

horse-drawn landau carriage
Each summer, tourists enjoy rides through the park in a horse-drawn landau.
The royal couple arrived at the ceremony in an elegant landau drawn by two white horses.
Vintage car enthusiasts admired the restored landau on display at the museum.
