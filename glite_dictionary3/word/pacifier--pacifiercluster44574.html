pacifier

noun
soothing device for babies to suck
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈpæsɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
A rubber or plastic device for an infant to suck or bite on, used to calm or soothe.

Examples

baby pacifier
He always keeps a spare pacifier in the diaper bag.
Please sterilize the pacifier before handing it to the infant.
The baby stopped crying as soon as she was given her pacifier.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dummy: British term for a baby's pacifier
  • synonym – soother: another term for a pacifier (chiefly British)
