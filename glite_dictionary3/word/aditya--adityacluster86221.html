Aditya

proper noun
Hindu solar deity; son of Aditi
Pronunciation: / əˈdiːtjə / (AmE), / əˈdiːtjə / (BrE)
In Hindu mythology, one of a group of solar deities who are sons of Aditi, often associated with celestial light.

Examples

Aditya in Hindu mythology
In Vedic scriptures, Aditya is revered as one of the protectors of the world.
Many Hindus offer prayers to an Aditya at sunrise for health and prosperity.
The Rigveda mentions each Aditya by name, highlighting their roles and attributes.
