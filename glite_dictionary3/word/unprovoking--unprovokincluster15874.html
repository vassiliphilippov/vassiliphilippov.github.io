unprovoking

adjective
not provoking or stimulating
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprəˈvoʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnprəˈvəʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
Not likely to arouse a strong reaction or provoke a response.

Examples

unprovoking tone
He maintained an unprovoking tone throughout the discussion, keeping the atmosphere calm.
Her remarks were deliberately unprovoking to avoid escalating the argument.
The movie’s content was so unprovoking that most viewers lost interest halfway through.

Related Terms

  • antonym – provoking: causing a reaction, especially anger or interest
