stinging

noun
sharp, sudden pain or sensation
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
a sharp, sudden pain or sensation similar to being stung

Examples

a stinging pain

The stinging from the paper cut lasted for several minutes.
