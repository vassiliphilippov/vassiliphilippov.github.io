to lard

verb
prepare or cook with lard
Pronunciation: / lɑrd / (AmE), / lɑːd / (BrE)
to prepare or cook (food) with lard, especially by adding lard as a fat or using it as a cooking medium.

Examples

to lard the meat
Before baking, you should lard the meat to keep it moist during cooking.
The chef decided to lard the roast with strips of bacon for added flavor.
Traditionally, they would lard the pie crust to make it flaky and rich.
