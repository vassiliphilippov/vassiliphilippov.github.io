logic

noun
reasoned or sound judgment
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːdʒɪk /; / ˈlɑː.dʒɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɒdʒɪk /; / ˈlɒdʒ.ɪk / (BrE)
the quality of being reasonable or the set of principles that guide correct reasoning or argumentation (including as a field of study)

Examples

sound logic
