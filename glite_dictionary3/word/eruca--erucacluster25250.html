Eruca

noun
genus of Mediterranean herbs (arugula)
Pronunciation: / ɪˈruːkə / (AmE), / ɪˈruːkə / (BrE)
A genus of herbs in the family Brassicaceae native to the Mediterranean, including the commonly cultivated arugula (rocket).
