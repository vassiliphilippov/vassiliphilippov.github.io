to flay

verb
to strip the skin off
Pronunciation: / fleɪ / (AmE), / fleɪ / (BrE)
to remove the skin from a person or animal, especially as a punishment or when preparing meat

Examples

to flay an animal
In medieval times, traitors were sometimes flayed as a gruesome punishment.
