to bean

verb
hit on the head (esp. with a baseball)
Pronunciation: / bin / (AmE), / biːn / (BrE)
to hit someone on the head with a hard object, especially a pitched baseball

Examples

to bean a batter
If you’re not careful, you might get beaned by a foul ball.
She beaned him with her notebook for telling another bad joke.
The pitcher accidentally beaned the batter in the fifth inning.
