barbital

noun
barbiturate sedative and hypnotic drug
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrbɪˌtɔl / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbɪtəl / (BrE)
A barbiturate drug used as a sedative and hypnotic to induce sleep.

Examples

barbital tablets for sleep

Research has shown that barbital has mood-altering effects as well as sedative properties.
The doctor prescribed barbital to help the patient sleep through the night.
