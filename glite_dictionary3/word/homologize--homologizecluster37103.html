to homologize

verb
make or demonstrate as homologous
Pronunciation: / hoʊˈmɑːləˌdʒaɪz /; / həˈmɑːləˌdʒaɪz / (AmE), / həˈmɒl.ə.dʒaɪz /; / həˈmɒlədʒaɪz / (BrE)
to show or establish that two or more things have the same origin, structure, or function, especially in science or biology
