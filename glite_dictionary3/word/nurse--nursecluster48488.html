nurse

noun
woman caring for children in a household
Pronunciation: / nɝs / (AmE), / nɜːs / (BrE)
a woman, traditionally employed in a household, who cares for and tends to children

Examples

childhood nurse
In Victorian novels, it was common for a nurse to accompany children on walks.
The nurse comforted the child when he woke from a nightmare.
The wealthy family hired a nurse to look after their young children.
