Phalangium opilio

noun
species of harvestman arachnid
Pronunciation: / fəˌlændʒiəm oʊˈpɪlioʊ / (AmE), / fəˌlændʒɪəm əʊˈpɪlɪəʊ / (BrE)
A species of harvestman, a spiderlike arachnid with a small rounded body and very long thin legs, commonly known as the daddy longlegs.
