to martyrise

verb
torture or torment like a martyr
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrtəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtɪraɪz / (BrE)
to torture or torment someone as a martyr, or to subject to prolonged suffering

Examples

to martyrise a victim
Do not martyrise yourself for a cause that will not appreciate your sacrifice.
In the novel, the antagonist martyrises the hero, pushing him to the brink of despair.
The regime would martyrise its prisoners to make an example of them.
