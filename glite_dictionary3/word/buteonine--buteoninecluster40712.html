buteonine

noun
hawk of the genus Buteo
Pronunciation: / bjuːˈtiːəˌnaɪn / (AmE), / bjuːˈtiːəˌnaɪn / (BrE)
Any hawk of the genus Buteo, characterized by broad wings and a soaring flight.

Examples

buteonine hawk
Researchers observed several buteonine species nesting in the canyon.
The red-tailed hawk is a common buteonine found throughout North America.
Unlike falcons, buteonine hawks typically soar with their wings held in a shallow 'V'.
