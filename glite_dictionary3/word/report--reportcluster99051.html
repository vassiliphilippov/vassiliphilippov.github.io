report

noun
sharp explosive sound (esp. gun)
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɔrt / (AmE), / rɪˈpɔːt / (BrE)
a sudden loud noise, such as the bang produced by a gun or explosion

Examples

gun report
A single report echoed through the valley as the gun was fired.
The hunters waited quietly after the report of the rifle shattered the dawn.
We heard the report and immediately knew something serious had happened.
