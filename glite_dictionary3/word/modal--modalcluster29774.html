modal

adjective
most frequent in a set or distribution
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊ.dl̩ / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.dl̩ / (BrE)
(of a value or category) relating to or constituting the most frequent value in a set or distribution.

Examples

modal value frequency
In this town, the modal household size is four people.
Researchers identified the modal response as the most common answer on the questionnaire.
The modal class in the survey was the group aged 21–30 years.
