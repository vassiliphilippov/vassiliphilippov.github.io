androgenous

adjective
relating to androgenesis (male-only reproduction)
Pronunciation: / ænˈdroʊdʒənəs / (AmE), / ænˈdrɒdʒənəs / (BrE)
Relating to or resulting from androgenesis, a type of reproduction involving only male genetic material.
