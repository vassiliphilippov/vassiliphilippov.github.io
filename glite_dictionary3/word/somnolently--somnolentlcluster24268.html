somnolently

adverb
in a drowsy or sleepy manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːmnələntli / (AmE), / ˈsɒmnələntli / (BrE)
In a drowsy or sleepy manner; appearing to be nearly asleep.

Examples

speak somnolently
After a long day of travel, she answered the phone somnolently and barely understood the message.
He listened somnolently to the lecture, struggling to keep his eyes open.
The cat blinked somnolently in the afternoon sun, refusing to move from the windowsill.
