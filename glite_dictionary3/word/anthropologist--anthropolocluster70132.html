anthropologist

noun
person who studies anthropology
Pronunciation: / ˌænθrəˈpɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌænθrəˈpɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies or is an expert in anthropology, especially the study of human societies and cultures and their development
