circularity

noun
the quality of being circular
Pronunciation: / sɝːkjəˈlærɪti / (AmE), / sɜːkjʊˈlærɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being circular or round, especially in a 2-dimensional figure

Examples

circularity of a shape
Architects admired the circularity of the new auditorium's floor plan.
The artist struggled to achieve circularity in the design of the mosaic.
The perfect circularity of the pond made it easy to measure its diameter.

Related Terms

  • synonym – roundness: the quality of being round or curved
