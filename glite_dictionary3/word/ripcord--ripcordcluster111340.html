ripcord

noun
cord for releasing a parachute
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪpˌkɔrd /; / ˈrɪpkɔrd / (AmE), / ˈrɪpˌkɔːd /; / ˈrɪpkɔːd / (BrE)
a cord that you pull to release a parachute or to let gas out of a balloon so it can descend
