Arauca

proper noun
river in Colombia and Venezuela
Pronunciation: / əˈraʊkə / (AmE), / əˈraʊkə / (BrE)
A river in northern South America that rises in Colombia and flows eastward into the Orinoco River in central Venezuela.

Examples

the Arauca River
Heavy rains caused the Arauca to overflow its banks.
The Arauca is an important river for transportation and agriculture in the region.
The border between Colombia and Venezuela is partly defined by the Arauca river.
