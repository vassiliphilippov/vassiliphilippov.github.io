to unwrap

verb
remove the covering from
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈræp / (AmE), / ʌnˈrap / (BrE)
to remove the covering or wrapping from something

Examples

to unwrap a present
Carefully, he unwrapped the fragile vase from its many layers of paper.
She watched eagerly as he began to unwrap the present.
The children couldn't wait to unwrap their Christmas gifts.
