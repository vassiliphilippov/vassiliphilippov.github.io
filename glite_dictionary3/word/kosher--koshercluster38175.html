kosher

adjective
legitimate or acceptable
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊʃər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊʃə / (BrE)
Considered proper, legitimate, or in accordance with accepted standards; genuine or trustworthy.

Examples

kosher business deal
Are you sure this operation is kosher? We can't afford to get into trouble.
Everything was above board and completely kosher according to company policy.
The deal didn’t look kosher to the investigators, so they decided to look deeper.

Related Terms

  • antonym – illegitimate: not authorized by the law; not in accordance with accepted standards
  • synonym – legitimate: conforming to rules or laws; justifiable
