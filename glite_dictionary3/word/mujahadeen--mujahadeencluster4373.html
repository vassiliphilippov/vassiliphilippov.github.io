mujahadeen

noun
Muslim guerrilla fighters in jihad
Pronunciation: / muːˌʒɑːhəˈdiːn / (AmE), / muːˌdʒɑːhəˈdiːn / (BrE)
A group of Muslim guerrilla fighters, especially those engaged in military or religious struggle (jihad) against non-Muslim forces.
