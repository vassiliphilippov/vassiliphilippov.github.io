conjuration

noun
act of creating illusions or tricks
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑndʒəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒndʒəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
an act or display that appears magical or mysterious, especially one that deceives or amazes spectators by seeming to defy ordinary explanation
