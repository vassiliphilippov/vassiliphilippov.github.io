sideboard

noun
dining room furniture with shelves and drawers
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdbɔːd / (BrE)
A piece of furniture, typically placed in a dining room, with shelves and drawers used for storing tableware and serving food.
