to tan

verb
convert hides into leather
Pronunciation: / tæn / (AmE), / tan / (BrE)
to treat animal skins and hides with tannic acid or other chemicals so as to convert them into leather

Examples

to tan animal hides
Before machinery, workers manually tanned skins in large pits.
The company tans thousands of animal skins every year for the leather industry.
Traditional craftsmen still tan hides using natural plant extracts.
