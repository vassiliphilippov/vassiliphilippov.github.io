self-aggrandising

adjective
seeking to appear more important
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf əˈɡræn.daɪz.ɪŋ /; / ˌsɛlf əˈɡræn.dəˌzaɪ.zɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf əˈɡræn.daɪz.ɪŋ /; / ˌself əˈɡræn.dɪ.zaɪ.zɪŋ / (BrE)
behaving in a way that tries to make yourself seem more important or powerful than you really are
