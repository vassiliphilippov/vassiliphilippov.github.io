cool

adjective
calm and self-controlled under pressure
Pronunciation: / kul / (AmE), / kuːl / (BrE)
remaining calm and self-controlled, especially in difficult or stressful situations; not displaying strong emotion.

Examples

cool under pressure
Despite the chaos, she remained cool and handled the emergency with confidence.
He tried to act cool during the interview, but you could tell he was nervous.
It's important to stay cool when negotiations get tense.

Related Terms

  • synonym – collected: calm and composed; not distracted by emotion
  • antonym – nervous: easily agitated or anxious, showing lack of calm
  • synonym – unflappable: not easily upset or agitated; remaining calm
