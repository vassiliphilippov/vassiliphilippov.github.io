insatiate

adjective
impossible to satisfy
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈseɪʃiət / (AmE), / ɪnˈseɪʃɪət / (BrE)
never satisfied; impossible to appease or fulfill

Examples

insatiate desire
Despite years of success, she had an insatiate ambition for recognition.
His insatiate curiosity drove him to travel the world in search of knowledge.
The company's insatiate desire for growth led it to expand into new markets every year.
