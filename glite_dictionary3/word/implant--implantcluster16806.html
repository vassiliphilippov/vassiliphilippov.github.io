to implant

verb
set something securely or deeply
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈplænt / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɑːnt / (BrE)
to fix or set securely or deeply, especially into a body or other surface

Examples

to implant a device
Technicians carefully implanted sensors into the concrete foundation for monitoring.
The archaeologist implanted a tracking device in the sculpture for security purposes.
The surgeon will implant a pacemaker beneath the patient's skin.
