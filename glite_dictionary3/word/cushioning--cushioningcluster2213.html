cushioning

noun
material for protection or comfort
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊʃənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʊʃənɪŋ / (BrE)
Material used to fill, shape, protect, or provide comfort, especially by absorbing shock or impact.

Examples

cushioning material
She added cushioning to the package to prevent the vase from breaking.
The running shoes have extra cushioning to reduce impact on your joints.
The seat's cushioning made our long car ride much more comfortable.
