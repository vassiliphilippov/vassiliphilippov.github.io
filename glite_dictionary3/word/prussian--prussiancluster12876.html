Prussian

adjective
relating to Prussia or its people
Pronunciation: / ˈprʌʃən / (AmE), / ˈprʌʃ.ən / (BrE)
of or relating to Prussia or its inhabitants, especially in terms of history, culture, or characteristics associated with the former kingdom of Prussia.

Examples

Prussian discipline
His leadership style was often described as Prussian in its discipline and rigor.
The museum features a collection of Prussian military uniforms from the nineteenth century.
They admired the efficiency of the Prussian education system.
