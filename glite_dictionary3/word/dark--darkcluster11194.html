dark

adjective
gloomy, depressing
Pronunciation: / dɑrk / (AmE), / dɑːk / (BrE)
showing a gloomy, depressing, or sullen mood; making you feel unhappy or without hope

Examples

dark mood
After receiving the terrible news, she fell into a dark mood for several days.
He avoided watching dark movies because they always left him feeling sad and restless.
The novel is set during the dark years of the country’s crisis, when hope was scarce.
He gave a dark look that discouraged further conversation.
Her dark mood made everyone around her uneasy.
There was a dark silence in the room after the argument.
