delineation

noun
representation by drawing or painting
Pronunciation: / dəˌlɪniˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌlɪniˈeɪʃən /; / dɪˌlɪnɪˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or result of showing something by drawing, outlining, or painting
