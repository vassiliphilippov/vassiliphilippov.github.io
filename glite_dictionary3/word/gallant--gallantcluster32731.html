gallant

noun
attentive or escorting man
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlænt / (AmE), / ˈɡæl.ənt / (BrE)
a man who is attentive and courteous to women, often acting as their escort

Examples

gallant escort at party
As a true gallant, he offered her his arm as they walked.
Every lady at the party seemed to have a gallant by her side.
He was her gallant for the evening, escorting her to the ball.
