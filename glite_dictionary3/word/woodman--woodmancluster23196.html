woodman

noun
man who works with wood
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdmən / (AmE), / ˈwʊdmən / (BrE)
a man whose job is making things out of wood, especially by shaping or carving it

Examples

woodman carves wood
In old fairy tales, a brave woodman often helps the main character in the forest.
My grandfather was a skilled woodman who made furniture for the whole village.
The woodman carefully carved a new table from oak he had harvested himself.

Related Terms

  • synonym – carpenter: a person who makes and repairs wooden objects and structures
  • synonym – joiner: a skilled worker who joins pieces of wood, especially as a job
