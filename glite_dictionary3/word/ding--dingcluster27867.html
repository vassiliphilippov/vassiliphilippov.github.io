to ding

verb
gain a level (gaming)
Pronunciation: / dɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪŋ / (BrE)
(informal, gaming) to reach a higher character level in a video game after earning enough experience points.

Examples

to ding a new level
After defeating the raid boss, her character dinged and unlocked a new skill.
We spent the afternoon dinging on easy quests to reach the next tier.
When he dings, everyone in the party sends congratulations in the chat.

Related Terms

  • synonym – advance: to move forward to a higher stage or position
  • synonym – level up: to increase a player's level in a game; gain ranks or abilities by earning experience
