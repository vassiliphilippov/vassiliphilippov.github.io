supertax

noun
additional tax on already taxed income
Pronunciation: / ˈsuː.pər.tæks / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pə.tæks / (BrE)
an additional tax levied on certain kinds of income that have already been subjected to taxation; especially, a supplementary income tax imposed above a certain threshold

Examples

supertax on high income
After paying the regular income tax, he was surprised to receive a bill for the supertax.
Controversy grew when politicians proposed introducing a new supertax on luxury goods.
The government decided to impose a supertax on high earners to increase public funds.

Related Terms

  • synonym – surtax: an additional tax on something already taxed, especially income above a certain level
