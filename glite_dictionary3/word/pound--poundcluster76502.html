to pound

verb
partition (water) into compartments
Pronunciation: / paʊnd / (AmE), / paʊnd / (BrE)
To confine or partition off (water) into compartments, such as in a canal using locks.

Examples

to pound water
After the storm, workers pounded the floodwaters into separate containment areas.
Engineers pounded portions of the river to control water flow for navigation.
The canal locks are designed to pound water between different levels.
