Rorippa nasturtium-aquaticum

noun
species of watercress plant
Pronunciation: / rəˈrɪpə næˌstɜr.ʃəm əˈkwætɪkəm / (AmE), / rəˈrɪpə næˌstɜːʃəm ɔːˈkwætɪkəm / (BrE)
A perennial Eurasian plant, commonly known as watercress, that grows in running water and has fleshy, pungent leaves used in salads, as a potherb, or as a garnish.
