moonlight

noun
light from the Moon
Pronunciation: / ˈmunˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌlaɪt / (BrE)
the light that comes from the Moon, especially at night when it is visible in the sky

Examples

bright moonlight
She walked home by moonlight, enjoying the peaceful night air.
The garden looked magical in the soft moonlight.
You can see the outline of the mountains clearly in the bright moonlight.
