moonstone

noun
a gemstone with a pearly luster
Pronunciation: / ˈmunˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌstəʊn / (BrE)
a semi-precious gemstone that is transparent or translucent with a pearly luster, typically a variety of feldspar

Examples

shiny moonstone ring
Collectors prize high-quality moonstones for their distinctive sheen and clarity.
He admired the antique pendant, which was set with a large moonstone.
The moonstone in her ring glowed with a soft, pale light.
