ageism

noun
discrimination based on age
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪdʒɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈeɪdʒɪzəm / (BrE)
unfair treatment of people because of their age, especially the elderly or the young

Examples

ageism in hiring
Ageism can prevent older employees from being considered for promotions at work.
Challenging ageism is essential to create a fairer and more inclusive society.
The company was accused of ageism after asking job applicants to list their year of birth.
