to connect

verb
make a logical or causal link
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛkt / (AmE), / kəˈnɛkt / (BrE)
to establish a logical or causal relationship between things, ideas, or events

Examples

to connect two ideas
Can you connect these ideas in a way that makes sense?
I tried to connect his rude behavior with something that might have upset him.
It's hard to connect the rise in crime to any single factor.
