censer

noun
container for burning incense
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsər / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsə / (BrE)
a container in which incense is burned, especially during religious ceremonies, often swung on a chain

Examples

swing a censer
An ornate censer hung from the ceiling above the altar.
During the ceremony, incense was placed in the censer and lit.
The priest swung the censer, filling the chapel with fragrant smoke.
