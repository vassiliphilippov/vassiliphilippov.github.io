fluorescent

adjective
brightly glowing (of color)
Pronunciation: / fluˈɒr.ə.sənt / (AmE), / flʊəˈrɛs.ənt / (BrE)
(of a color) very bright and seeming to glow, even in daylight.

Examples

fluorescent yellow jacket
Cyclists often choose fluorescent gear for safety on the road.
She wore a fluorescent yellow jacket so she could be seen at night.
The artist used fluorescent paints to make the poster stand out.

  • synonym – neon: (of a color) extremely bright or vivid
