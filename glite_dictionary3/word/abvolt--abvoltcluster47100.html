abvolt

noun
unit of electric potential (CGS system)
Pronunciation: / ˈæbˌvoʊlt / (AmE), / ˈabˌvəʊlt / (BrE)
a unit of electric potential equal to one hundred millionth (10⁻⁸) of a volt, used in the CGS system of units

Examples

one abvolt unit
Electromagnetic equations in the CGS system often reference the abvolt as the unit of potential difference.
One abvolt is a very small unit compared to the standard volt used today.
The old laboratory equipment measured voltage in abvolts rather than volts.
