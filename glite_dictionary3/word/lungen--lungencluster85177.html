lungen

noun
tropical tree with sweet edible fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌn.dʒən / (AmE), / ˈlʌn.dʒən / (BrE)
A tree of southeastern Asia to Australia, grown mainly for its sweet, edible fruit resembling litchi nuts; sometimes classified under the genera Euphorbia or Nephelium.
