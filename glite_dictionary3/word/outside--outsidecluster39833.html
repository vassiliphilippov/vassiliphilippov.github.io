outside

adjective
unlikely to occur; improbable
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊt.saɪd / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.saɪd / (BrE)
(of a possibility or chance) very unlikely to happen or be the case.

Examples

outside chance exists
Scientists admit there is an outside possibility of life on other planets.
She applied for the scholarship, although she knew her chances were strictly outside.
There is only an outside chance that the team will win the championship this year.

Related Terms

  • antonym – likely: having a high probability of occurring or being true
  • synonym – remote: (of a chance or possibility) very unlikely to happen
