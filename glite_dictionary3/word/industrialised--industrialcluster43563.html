industrialised

adjective
developed with large-scale industry
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʌstriəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzd / (BrE)
(of a country, area, or society) having developed a large amount of industry; converted to an industrial economy or way of life.

Examples

industrialised country
Many industrialised nations have strict environmental regulations.
The gap between industrialised and developing countries has widened over time.
Urbanization is a common feature of industrialised societies.
