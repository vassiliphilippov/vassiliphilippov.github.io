enosis

noun
union of Greece and Cyprus (political movement)
Pronunciation: / ɛˈnoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ɛˈnəʊsɪs / (BrE)
The political movement advocating the union of Greece and Cyprus, particularly as sought by some Greek Cypriots.

enosis with Greece
