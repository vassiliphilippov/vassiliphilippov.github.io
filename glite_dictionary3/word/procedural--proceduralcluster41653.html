procedural

adjective
relating to procedure or process
Pronunciation: / prəˈsidʒɚəl / (AmE), / prəˈsiːdʒərəl / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with a procedure, especially an established or official way of doing something.

Examples

procedural steps
The committee was accused of a procedural error during the vote.
The company is updating its procedural guidelines to improve efficiency.
The court dismissed the case due to a procedural technicality.
