Sellotape

noun
transparent adhesive tape (brand)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛloʊˌteɪp / (AmE), / ˈsɛləˌteɪp / (BrE)
A type of transparent or semi-transparent adhesive tape, used for sealing, attaching, or mending, especially known as a brand in British English.

Examples

Sellotape a poster
Could you pass me the Sellotape, please?
He wrapped the package and sealed it with Sellotape.
She used Sellotape to stick the torn page back into the book.

Related Terms

  • synonym – adhesive tape: tape coated with adhesive for sticking things together
