laniard

noun
cord for carrying a small object
Pronunciation: / ˈlæniərd / (AmE), / ˈlæniəd / (BrE)
A cord worn around the neck, shoulder, or wrist to carry an object such as a knife, whistle, or badge.

Examples

laniard for a badge
Conference attendees received a badge on a blue laniard at registration.
He looped the laniard around his neck to keep his knife secure while climbing.
Police officers often wear their whistles attached to a laniard for easy access.
