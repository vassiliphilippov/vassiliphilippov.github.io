curvaceousness

noun
quality of having a shapely, curved body
Pronunciation: / kɝˈveɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / kɜːˈveɪʃəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having an attractively rounded and shapely body, especially referring to a woman's figure

Examples

her curvaceousness admired
Her curvaceousness was accentuated by the fitted dress she wore.
Many fashion designers celebrate the curvaceousness of the female form in their collections.
The model was admired for her curvaceousness on the runway.

Related Terms

  • synonym – curviness: the quality of having curves; another term for curvaceousness
  • antonym – slenderness: the quality of being attractively thin and graceful
