succinctly

adverb
in a concise, clear manner
Pronunciation: / səkˈsɪŋktli / (AmE), / səkˈsɪŋktli / (BrE)

Examples

She summarized the report succinctly in just a few sentences.
The instructions were explained succinctly for easy understanding.
