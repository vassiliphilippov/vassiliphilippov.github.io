unscalable

adjective
impossible to climb
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskeɪləbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈskeɪləbl / (BrE)
impossible to climb or ascend; unable to be scaled

Examples

unscalable wall
For centuries, the fortress was protected by its unscalable walls.
The cliff appeared unscalable, discouraging any attempt to reach the summit.
The sheer rock face was unscalable without special climbing equipment.
