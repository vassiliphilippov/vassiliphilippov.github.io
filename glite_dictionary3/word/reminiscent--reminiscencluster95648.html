reminiscent

adjective
bringing to mind something
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛmɪˈnɪsənt / (AmE), / ˌrɛmɪˈnɪsənt / (BrE)
tending to remind one of something; serving to bring to mind a particular person, event, or thing.

Examples

reminiscent of childhood days
His storytelling style is reminiscent of classic adventure novels.
The old building's architecture is reminiscent of the Victorian era.
The scent of the ocean air was reminiscent of her childhood summers.
