retreat

noun
period/place for prayer or meditation
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtrit / (AmE), / rɪˈtriːt / (BrE)
a period or place of withdrawal from normal activity, often for prayer, study, or meditation, especially in a religious context

Examples

a meditation retreat
After a stressful year, he attended a meditation retreat to find peace and clarity.
She spent a weekend at a spiritual retreat in the countryside, reflecting in silence.
The monastery offers a week-long retreat focused on prayer, study, and self-reflection.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pilgrimage: a journey to a sacred place for religious reasons
  • synonym – sabbatical: a period of paid leave for study or travel, often from work or duty
