equipage

noun
horse-drawn carriage or vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkwəpɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɛkwɪpɪdʒ / (BrE)
A carriage or vehicle with wheels, especially one drawn by horses, often accompanied by attendants and used in historical contexts.

Examples

royal equipage procession
Crowds gathered to watch the procession of equipages through the cobblestone streets.
In the 18th century, only the wealthy could afford an ornate equipage.
The royal family arrived in a grand equipage pulled by four white horses.

Related Terms

  • synonym – carriage: a wheeled vehicle for people, usually horse-drawn
