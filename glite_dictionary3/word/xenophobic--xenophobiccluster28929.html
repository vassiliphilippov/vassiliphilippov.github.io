xenophobic

adjective
having a dislike or fear of foreigners
Pronunciation: / ˌzɛnəˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / ˌzɛənəˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
having or showing a dislike or prejudice against people from other countries or cultures; fearful or hateful of foreigners or strangers

Examples

xenophobic attitudes
Fear of outsiders can sometimes lead to xenophobic attitudes in society.
Some voters expressed xenophobic views during the debate on immigration policies.
The party was criticized for its xenophobic rhetoric in the campaign.

Related Terms

  • antonym – tolerant: showing willingness to accept people from different backgrounds
  • synonym – xenophobia: dislike or fear of people from other countries
