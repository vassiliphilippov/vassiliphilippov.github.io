magazine

noun
firearms: container for cartridges
Pronunciation: / ˈmæɡəˌzin / (AmE), / ˈmæɡəziːn / (BrE)
a metal container or frame that holds cartridges and can be inserted into a firearm to supply ammunition automatically

Examples

magazine for bullets
Always check that the magazine is emptied before cleaning your gun.
The pistol jammed because the magazine was not properly seated.
The soldier reloaded his rifle by inserting a fresh magazine.
