transmitter

noun
organism or agent that spreads disease
Pronunciation: / trænˈsmɪtər / (AmE), / trænzˈmɪtə / (BrE)
an animal, person, or microorganism that carries and spreads a disease from one individual or organism to another

Examples

mosquito transmitter
Controlling insect populations can reduce the risk of transmitter-borne diseases.
In many tropical regions, mosquitoes are the primary transmitters of malaria to humans.
The study identified rats as potential transmitters of the new virus strain.

Related Terms

  • synonym – vector: an organism, typically an insect, that transmits a pathogen
