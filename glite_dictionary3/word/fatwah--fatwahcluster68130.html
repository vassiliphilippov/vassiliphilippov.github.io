fatwah

noun
Islamic legal ruling or opinion
Pronunciation: / ˈfæt.wɑ / (AmE), / ˈfæt.wɑː / (BrE)
An official ruling or legal opinion issued by an Islamic scholar on a point of Islamic law.

Examples
