dog

noun
domesticated mammal, pet or working animal
Pronunciation: / dɑɡ / (AmE), / dɒɡ / (BrE)
A domesticated carnivorous mammal (Canis familiaris) that has been bred in many varieties; commonly kept as a pet or for work such as hunting or guarding.

Examples

pet dog
A trained dog can help people with disabilities.
She adopted a rescue dog from the local animal shelter.
The dog barked all night outside the neighbor's house.

Related Terms

  • synonym – canine: relating to or resembling a dog
  • antonym – cat: a common domesticated pet, often compared with a dog
  • degree lower – puppy: a young dog
  • degree higher – wolf: wild carnivorous mammal related to the dog
