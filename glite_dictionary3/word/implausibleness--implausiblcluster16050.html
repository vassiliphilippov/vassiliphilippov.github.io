implausibleness

noun
quality of being unlikely or unbelievable
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈplɔzəbəlnəs / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔːzəbəlnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being difficult to believe; lack of plausibility.

Examples

the implausibleness of a story
Critics pointed to the implausibleness of the film's plot as its main weakness.
Her explanation had a tone of implausibleness that made listeners skeptical.
The sheer implausibleness of his story led everyone to doubt his innocence.
