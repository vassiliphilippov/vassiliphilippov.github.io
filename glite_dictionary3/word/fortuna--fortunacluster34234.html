Fortuna

proper noun
Roman goddess of fortune and luck
Pronunciation: / fɔrˈtuːnə / (AmE), / fɔːˈtjuːnə / (BrE)
In Roman mythology, the goddess of fortune and good luck; the counterpart of the Greek goddess Tyche.

Fortuna goddess worship
In ancient Rome, citizens often prayed to Fortuna for prosperity and blessings.
Statues of Fortuna were commonly erected in temples and marketplaces.
