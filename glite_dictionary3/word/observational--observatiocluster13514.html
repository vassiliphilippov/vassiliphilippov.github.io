observational

adjective
based on observation
Pronunciation: / ˌɑbzərˈveɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɒbzəˈveɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to, based on, or involving observation rather than theory or pure logic.

Examples

observational study
Her observational skills made her an excellent detective.
Scientists conducted an observational experiment rather than manipulating variables directly.
The study used observational data collected over several years.
