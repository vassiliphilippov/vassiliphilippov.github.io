to apotheosize

verb
elevate to divine status; glorify
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑːθiəˌsaɪz / (AmE), / əˈpɒθiəsaɪz / (BrE)
to elevate someone or something to the status of a god; to glorify or idealize greatly

Examples

to apotheosize a hero
In her speech, she seemed to apotheosize the achievements of the organization.
Many fans apotheosize their favorite musicians, treating them as if they can do no wrong.
The leader was apotheosized in the press after the victory, with articles praising his every decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deify: to make a god of; to glorify as supreme
  • synonym – idolize: to admire or revere excessively
