asexuality

noun
lack of sexual attraction (sexual orientation)
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪˌsɛkʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌeɪˌsɛkʃuˈæləti / (BrE)
a sexual orientation marked by little or no sexual attraction to other people; individuals may still form romantic relationships or experience romantic attraction.

Examples

identify with asexuality
Many young people today openly identify with asexuality and join supportive online communities.
Her doctor listened carefully when she explained her asexuality and how it affected relationships.
Universities now include asexuality in diversity training to raise awareness and understanding.
