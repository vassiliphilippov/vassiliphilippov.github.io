diet

noun
usual food and drink consumed
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
the usual food and drink that a person, animal, or group regularly eats and drinks

Examples

diet of pandas
A panda’s diet consists mostly of bamboo.
Her diet is rich in fruit and vegetables.
The bird’s natural diet includes insects and seeds.
