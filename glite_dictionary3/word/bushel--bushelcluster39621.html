bushel

noun
US dry measure (4 pecks)
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊʃəl / (AmE), / ˈbʊʃl̩ /; / ˈbʊʃəl / (BrE)
a unit of measurement for dry goods (such as grain or produce), equal to about 35 liters; the exact amount varies in the US and UK
