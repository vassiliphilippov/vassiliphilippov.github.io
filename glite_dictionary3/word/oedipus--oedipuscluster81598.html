Oedipus

proper noun
mythological king of Thebes
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdɪpəs, ˈidɪpəs / (AmE), / ˈiːdɪpəs / (BrE)
(In Greek mythology) a tragic king of Thebes who, unknowingly, killed his father and married his mother; the subject of Sophocles' tragedy 'Oedipus Rex'.
