telescopic

adjective
visible only with a telescope
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛləˈskɑpɪk /; / tɛləˈskɑpɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈskɒpɪk /; / tɛlɪˈskɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to a telescope or to seeing distant things (only visible with a telescope, or able to see far away as if with a telescope)
