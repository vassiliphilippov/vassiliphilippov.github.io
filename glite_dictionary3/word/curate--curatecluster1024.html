to curate

verb
select and organize content
Pronunciation: / kjʊˈreɪt / (AmE), / kjʊˈreɪt / (BrE)
to select, organize, and present items or content (such as artworks, articles, or playlists) as a carefully arranged collection for an audience.

Examples

to curate an art show
She curates a weekly playlist of indie tracks on Spotify.
The museum director plans to curate an exhibition of local artists next summer.
He has curated a selection of articles for the course reading list this term.

Related Terms

  • synonym – organize: to arrange or put in order for a particular purpose or use
  • synonym – select: to choose carefully from a number of alternatives
