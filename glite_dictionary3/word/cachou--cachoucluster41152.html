cachou

noun
scented breath-freshening lozenge
Pronunciation: / kæˈʃu / (AmE), / kaˈʃuː / (BrE)
A small, scented lozenge used to freshen the breath, especially to mask odors such as tobacco.

Examples

cachou breath freshener
He discreetly popped a cachou into his mouth after smoking his cigar.
She always carried cachous in her purse to keep her breath fresh.
The old-fashioned tin of cachous was passed around the table.
