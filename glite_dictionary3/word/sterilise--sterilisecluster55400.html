to sterilise

verb
make free from bacteria or microorganisms
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛrəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈstɛrəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make free from bacteria or other living microorganisms, usually by applying heat or chemicals

Examples

to sterilise medical equipment
Hospitals often use autoclaves to sterilise instruments quickly and thoroughly.
Medical equipment must be sterilised before surgery to prevent infection.
She sterilised the baby's bottles by boiling them in water.

Related Terms

  • synonym – disinfect: to clean in order to destroy bacteria
