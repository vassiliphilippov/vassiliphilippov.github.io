archetypical

adjective
characteristic of an original model
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrkɪˈtɪpɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɑːkɪˈtɪpɪkəl / (BrE)
very typical of a certain kind of person or thing; representing an original model or type after which others are patterned.

Examples

archetypical hero
He played the archetypical hero, brave and selfless throughout the novel.
Many fairy tales feature archetypical characters like the wicked stepmother.
The story contains archetypical themes of good versus evil.
