symbolisation

noun
use of symbols to convey meaning
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪmbələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪmbəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌsɪmbəlaɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the act or process of using symbols, objects, or images to represent ideas, qualities, or meanings
