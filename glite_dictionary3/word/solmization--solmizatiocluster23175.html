solmization

noun
naming musical notes with syllables
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊlməˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌsoʊlmɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɒlmɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the system or act of assigning syllables (such as do, re, mi) to the notes of a musical scale, especially for teaching or singing
