bag

noun
udder of a cow, sheep, or goat
Pronunciation: / bæɡ / (AmE), / bæɡ / (BrE)
(of cows, sheep, and goats) the udder; the mammary gland of certain animals that produces milk.

Examples

goat's bag
Goat breeders pay close attention to the health of the bag to ensure good milk production.
If a sheep has an infected bag, it may not be able to feed its lambs properly.
The farmer checked the cow's bag before milking her early in the morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – udder: the mammary gland of female cattle, sheep, goats, horses, and related animals
