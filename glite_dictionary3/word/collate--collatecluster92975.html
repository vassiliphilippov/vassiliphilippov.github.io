to collate

verb
assemble items in proper sequence
Pronunciation: / kəˈleɪt / (AmE), / kəˈleɪt / (BrE)
to collect and arrange items, especially documents or pieces of information, in the correct order

Examples

to collate printed pages
After printing, make sure to collate the pages to avoid any confusion.
Please collate the reports by date before submitting them to the manager.
The assistant was asked to collate the handouts for the meeting in the correct order.

Related Terms

  • antonym – disarrange: to put out of order
  • synonym – sort: put things in a particular order or category
