to jam

verb
to crush or bruise a body part
Pronunciation: / dʒæm / (AmE), / dʒam / (BrE)
to injure part of the body, especially a finger or toe, by crushing or hitting it hard, often causing bruising or pain without breaking the skin

Examples

to jam your finger
Be careful not to jam your toe against the door.
He jammed his thumb in the car door and it turned purple.
She jammed her finger while catching the basketball.

Related Terms

  • antonym – break: to fracture or snap a bone or hard tissue
  • synonym – bruise: to cause a dark mark on the skin from hitting or crushing it
