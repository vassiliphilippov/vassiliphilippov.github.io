profanatory

adjective
showing disrespect for the sacred
Pronunciation: / proʊˈfænəˌtɔri / (AmE), / prəˈfænətəri / (BrE)
Showing disrespect or irreverence toward something sacred; tending to desecrate.

Examples

profanatory language
His profanatory remarks offended many in the congregation.
Such profanatory behavior is condemned in most religious traditions.
The vandalism of the tomb was considered a profanatory act by the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sacrilegious: violating or showing disrespect toward something sacred
