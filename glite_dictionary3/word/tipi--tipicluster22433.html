tipi

noun
cone-shaped Native American tent
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːpi / (AmE), / ˈtiːpi / (BrE)
A cone-shaped tent traditionally used by some North American Indigenous peoples, especially the Plains tribes.

Examples

a tipi tent
A tipi can be quickly assembled and taken down for easy travel.
