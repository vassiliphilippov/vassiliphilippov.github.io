seta

noun
stalk of a moss capsule
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːtə / (AmE), / ˈsiːtə / (BrE)
A slender stalk supporting the capsule in mosses and certain other lower plants.

Examples

seta of a moss
Scientists measured the length of each seta to study its growth patterns.
The seta of the moss is delicate and easily damaged.
