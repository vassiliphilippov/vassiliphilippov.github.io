eruptive

adjective
actively spewing out lava or ash
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrʌptɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈrʌptɪv / (BrE)
actively spewing out or capable of spewing out lava, ash, or other material (especially of a volcano or geological feature)

Examples

eruptive volcano activity
