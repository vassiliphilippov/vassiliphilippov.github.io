to tempt

verb
make someone want to do/have something bad
Pronunciation: / tɛmpt / (AmE), / tɛmpt / (BrE)
to try to persuade or attract someone to do something, especially something wrong or unwise, by making it seem attractive

Examples

to tempt someone with chocolate

Related Terms

  • antonym – deter: to discourage someone from doing something
  • antonym – dissuade: to persuade someone not to do something
  • synonym – encourage: to give support or confidence to someone to do something
  • synonym – entice: to lure someone by offering hope or reward
  • synonym – persuade: induce someone to do something through reasoning or argument
  • synonym – seduce: to persuade someone to have sex or to do something they would not usually do
