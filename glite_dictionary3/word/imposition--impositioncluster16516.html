imposition

noun
an unwelcome burden or demand
Pronunciation: / ˌɪm.pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˌɪm.pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
an unwelcome demand or burden that is unfairly placed on someone

Examples

an imposition on time
He considered the request an unreasonable imposition on his time.
I hope it's not an imposition for you to stay late and help us.
She apologized for the imposition of asking for another favor.

Related Terms

  • synonym – burden: a heavy load, especially one that's difficult to carry; a figurative obligation
  • antonym – privilege: a special right or advantage not enjoyed by others; the opposite of being imposed upon
