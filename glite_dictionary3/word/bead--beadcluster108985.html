bead

noun
small round object or drop
Pronunciation: / bid / (AmE), / biːd / (BrE)
a small, usually round object, such as a piece used in jewelry or a drop of liquid

Examples

bead of sweat
Beads of sweat trickled down his face during the workout.
She wore a necklace strung with colorful beads.
Tiny beads of rain formed on the windowpane.
Children often enjoy making friendship bracelets using colorful beads.
She threaded a blue bead onto the necklace as she worked.
The curtain was made entirely of wooden beads hanging from top to bottom.
