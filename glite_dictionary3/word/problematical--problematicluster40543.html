problematical

adjective
difficult or complicated; posing problems
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑː.bləˈmæt.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌprɒb.ləˈmæt.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
difficult to understand, solve, or deal with; posing problems or complications.
