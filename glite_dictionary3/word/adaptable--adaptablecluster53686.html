adaptable

adjective
able to adjust to new conditions or uses
Pronunciation: / əˈdæptəbəl / (AmE), / əˈdaptəbl / (BrE)
able to change or be changed in order to suit different conditions or uses.

Examples

highly adaptable person
Children are generally more adaptable to change than adults.
This software is highly adaptable for various business needs.
To succeed in a fast-changing environment, you must be adaptable.

Related Terms

  • synonym – flexible: able to bend or change easily without breaking or losing shape
  • antonym – rigid: unable or unwilling to change or adapt
