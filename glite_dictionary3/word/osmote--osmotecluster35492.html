to osmote

verb
to pass through a membrane by osmosis
Pronunciation: / ɑzˈmoʊt / (AmE), / ɒzˈməʊt / (BrE)
(of a substance) to pass through a semipermeable membrane by osmosis.

Examples

to osmote through a membrane
Certain ions can osmote through the cell membrane, depending on the concentration gradient.
The experiment demonstrates how selectively permeable membranes allow only specific substances to osmote through.
Water molecules osmote through the plant roots during the process of absorption.

Related Terms

  • synonym – diffuse: to spread or cause to spread over a wide area or among a large number of people
  • synonym – osmosis: the process by which molecules pass through a semipermeable membrane
