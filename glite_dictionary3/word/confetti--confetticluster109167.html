confetti

noun
digital animated celebration effect
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɛti / (AmE), / kənˈfɛti / (BrE)
(in computing and digital design) an on-screen animated graphic of falling pieces, sparkles, or other small elements used to celebrate or reward a user action.

Examples

digital confetti animation
After completing the tutorial, the app celebrated my achievement with falling confetti across the screen.
The team cheered when the project tracker showed the final milestone and a burst of confetti.
Designers often add subtle confetti animations to reward users for completing small tasks.

Related Terms

  • synonym – animation: a sequence of images or visual changes that create the appearance of movement on a screen
