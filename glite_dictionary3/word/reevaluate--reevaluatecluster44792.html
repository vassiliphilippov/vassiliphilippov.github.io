to reevaluate

verb
assess again to reconsider judgment
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪˈvæljueɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪˈvæljueɪt / (BrE)
to consider or assess something again, especially in order to change a previous judgment or decision

Examples

to reevaluate a decision
After receiving new information, the committee decided to reevaluate the proposed plan.
It's wise to reevaluate your investments regularly to ensure they meet your goals.
You may need to reevaluate your priorities if you find yourself frequently stressed.
