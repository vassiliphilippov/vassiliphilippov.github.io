stiff

noun
ordinary person; working-class individual
Pronunciation: / stɪf / (AmE), / stɪf / (BrE)
(informal) An ordinary person, especially a working person or one who is not prominent or wealthy.

Examples

working stiff job
He was just another stiff in a long line of job applicants.
Most stiffs like me can't afford to eat out every night.
