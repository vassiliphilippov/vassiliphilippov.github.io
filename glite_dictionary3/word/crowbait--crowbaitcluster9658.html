crowbait

noun
very thin or weak horse near death
Pronunciation: / ˈkroʊˌbeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊˌbeɪt / (BrE)
An extremely thin or weak horse considered to be near death and of little value.

Examples

old crowbait horse
After years of hard work, the poor animal had become nothing but a crowbait.
Some buyers at the market were only interested in crowbait to butcher for cheap meat.
