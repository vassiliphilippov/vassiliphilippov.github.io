Xhosa

noun
a major South African Bantu language
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊsə, ˈzoʊsə / (AmE), / ˈkəʊsə, ˈzɒsə / (BrE)
A Bantu language spoken primarily in South Africa, closely related to Zulu and the country's second most common home language.
