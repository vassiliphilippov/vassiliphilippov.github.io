to airmail

verb
send by air postal service
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌmeɪl / (AmE), / ˈeə.meɪl / (BrE)
to send or transport (a letter or package) by air, especially using a postal service for faster international delivery.

Examples

to airmail a package
I need to airmail this package to Canada to ensure it arrives quickly.
She airmailed the signed documents to her lawyer overseas.
We are airmailing the invitations to our friends abroad.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mail: to send (a letter or package) by post
