caption

noun
text accompanying an image
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈkæp.ʃən / (BrE)
A brief explanation or description accompanying an illustration, photo, diagram, or similar image.

Examples

caption under picture
Each illustration in the book included a witty caption underneath.
He forgot to add a caption to the chart, making it unclear what the data represented.
The photo's caption provided the date and location of the event.

Related Terms

  • synonym – legend: text accompanying a map or diagram explaining symbols
  • synonym – subtitle: secondary title or explanatory text below the main title or image
