filling

noun
yarn woven across the warp in weaving
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the yarn or thread that is woven across the warp yarns to create fabric in weaving

Examples

cotton filling in fabric
Cotton filling is often used in making lightweight and breathable textiles.
In traditional weaving, the filling is inserted over and under the warp threads.
The artisan carefully aligned the filling threads with the warp to create a smooth fabric.
