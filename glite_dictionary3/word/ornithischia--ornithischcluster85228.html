Ornithischia

noun
group of bird-hipped extinct dinosaurs
Pronunciation: / ɔrˌnɪˈθɪskiə /; / ɔːrnɪˈθɪskiə / (AmE), / ɔːˌnɪˈθɪskɪə /; / ɔːnɪˈθɪskɪə / (BrE)
