to credit

verb
add money to an account
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛdɪt / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪt / (BrE)
to add a sum of money to an account; to record an amount as a benefit or payment received

Examples

to credit an account
Every month, the company credits my account with a small bonus for using their services.
She noticed that her paycheck had been credited directly to her savings account.
The bank will credit your account with the refund within five business days.

Related Terms

  • antonym – debit: to remove or deduct an amount from an account
