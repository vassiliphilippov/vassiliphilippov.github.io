southwest

adverb
toward or in the southwest
Pronunciation: / ˌsaʊθˈwɛst / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈwɛst / (BrE)
to, toward, or in the direction of the southwest; in a southwesterly direction.

Examples

drive southwest
Drive southwest for about 50 miles until you reach the canyon.
Storm clouds were moving rapidly southwest across the open plain.
The birds migrated southwest for the winter, seeking warmer climates.
