gunny

noun
coarse jute or hemp fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌni / (AmE), / ˈɡʌni / (BrE)
A coarse, heavy fabric made from jute or hemp, used especially for making sacks or bags.

Examples

gunny sack
He stitched the torn gunny sack to carry potatoes from the field.
Rice was transported in large gunny bags across the port.
The old barn was filled with rolls of unused gunny fabric.
