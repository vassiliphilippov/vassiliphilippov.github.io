hush-hush

adjective
secret; confidential
Pronunciation: / ˌhʌʃˈhʌʃ / (AmE), / ˌhʌʃˈhʌʃ / (BrE)
Kept secret or confidential, especially to avoid drawing attention.

Examples

hush-hush project
Details of the merger remained hush-hush until the official announcement.
The company is working on a hush-hush project that it refuses to discuss publicly.
There was a hush-hush meeting held late at night to avoid the press.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clandestine: kept secret or done secretively
  • antonym – public: openly done or known; not secret
  • synonym – secret: not known or meant to be known by others
