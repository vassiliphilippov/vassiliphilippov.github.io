dimout

noun
partial reduction of lights (wartime blackout)
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪmˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈdɪm.aʊt / (BrE)
a partial reduction of lights in a city or area, especially to make it less visible to enemy aircraft during wartime
