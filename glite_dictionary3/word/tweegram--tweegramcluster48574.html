tweegram

noun
Instagram post with only text
Pronunciation: / ˈtwiˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈtwiːˌɡræm / (BrE)
An Instagram post consisting solely of text, often created with a special image background to display the words.

Examples

tweegram with a quote
Instead of a photo, he posted a tweegram with his favorite motivational quote.
Many users use tweegrams to communicate messages that are too long for a regular caption.
She shared a tweegram expressing her gratitude to everyone who supported her new business.
