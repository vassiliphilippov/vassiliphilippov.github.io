fritillary

noun
butterfly with brown-and-silver wings
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪtəˌlɛri / (AmE), / frɪˈtɪləri / (BrE)
A butterfly of the family Nymphalidae, typically having brownish wings marked with black and silver spots.
