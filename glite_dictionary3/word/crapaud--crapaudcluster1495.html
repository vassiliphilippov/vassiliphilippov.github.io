crapaud

noun
large South/Central American frog
Pronunciation: / kræˈpoʊ / (AmE), / kræˈpəʊ / (BrE)
A large, toothed frog of South and Central America resembling the bullfrog.

Examples

a crapaud croaking
At night, the loud croaks of the crapaud echo through the rainforest.
Local hunters in Guyana are familiar with the crapaud that inhabits swampy areas.
The crapaud is often mistaken for the bullfrog because of its large size.
