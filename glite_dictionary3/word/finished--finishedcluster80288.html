finished

adjective
having a surface coating or treatment applied
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪnɪʃt / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃt / (BrE)
(of an object) having a surface coating or treatment applied, such as polishing, painting, or varnishing, as the final stage of production.

Examples

finished surface
He prefers to buy finished products instead of assembling them himself.
The finished table gleamed under the lights of the showroom.
The carpenter delivered the finished cabinets on schedule.
