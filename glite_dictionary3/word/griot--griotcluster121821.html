griot

noun
West African oral storyteller and musician
Pronunciation: / ˈɡri.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡri.əʊ / (BrE)
a storyteller, poet, or musician in West Africa who preserves and passes on the oral history, traditions, and genealogy of a family or community

Examples

griot preserves history
In many West African cultures, a griot is entrusted with preserving oral history.
She invited a famous griot to perform traditional songs at the festival.
The griot captivated the audience with tales of the village's ancestors.
