fullback

noun
offensive football position; blocking running back
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊlˌbæk /; / ˈfʊl.bæk / (AmE), / ˈfʊlbæk /; / ˈfʊl.bæk / (BrE)
(in American football) a player on the offensive team who lines up behind the quarterback, mainly blocks for the running back, and may also run with the ball or catch passes
