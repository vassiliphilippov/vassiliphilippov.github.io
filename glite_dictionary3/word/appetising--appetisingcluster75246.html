appetising

adjective
appealing to the appetite
Pronunciation: / ˈæpəˌtaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈapɪtaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
having an appearance or smell that makes you want to eat it; appealing to the appetite.

Examples

appetising food display
Everything on the buffet looked so appetising that I couldn't decide what to try first.
She arranged the fruit platter in a very appetising way.
The aroma of freshly baked bread was truly appetising.

Related Terms

  • synonym – appetizing: appealing to or stimulating the appetite (American spelling)
  • antonym – unappetising: not appealing to the appetite
