zaatar

noun
Middle Eastern spice blend
Pronunciation: / zɑːˈtɑːr / (AmE), / zɑːˈtɑː / (BrE)
a Middle Eastern seasoning blend made from dried hyssop or thyme, sesame seeds, sumac, salt, and sometimes other spices

Examples

zaatar seasoning mix
A small dish of olive oil and zaatar makes a delicious appetizer.
