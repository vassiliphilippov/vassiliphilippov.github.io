monoploid

adjective
having a single set of chromosomes
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑn.əˌplɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɒn.əˌplɔɪd / (BrE)
(of a cell or organism) having a single set of chromosomes, as opposed to the usual two sets in diploid organisms.
