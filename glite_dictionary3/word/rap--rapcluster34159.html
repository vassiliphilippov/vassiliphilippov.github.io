rap

noun
style of music with rhythmic spoken lyrics
Pronunciation: / ræp / (AmE), / ræp / (BrE)
A style of popular music characterized by spoken or chanted rhyming lyrics set to a strong rhythmic accompaniment, originating within African-American communities in the 1980s and 1990s.

Examples

rap music
Rap has become one of the most influential genres in modern music.
He prefers old-school rap to contemporary pop songs.
Many artists began their careers performing rap in small local clubs.
