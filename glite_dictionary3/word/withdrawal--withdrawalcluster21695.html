withdrawal

noun
emotional detachment or avoidance
Pronunciation: / wɪðˈdrɔəl / (AmE), / wɪðˈdrɔːəl / (BrE)
the act or process of avoiding emotional involvement or becoming detached from others

Examples

emotional withdrawal
After the breakup, he showed clear withdrawal from his friends and family.
Her sudden withdrawal made it hard for her colleagues to support her.
Periods of social withdrawal can be a sign of emotional distress.

Related Terms

  • synonym – detachment: the state of being objective or aloof; emotional separation
  • antonym – engagement: emotional involvement or participation
