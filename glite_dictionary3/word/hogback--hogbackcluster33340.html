hogback

noun
narrow ridge of hills
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔɡˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈhɒɡˌbæk / (BrE)
a long, narrow ridge with steep sides, especially one that resembles the back of a hog.

Examples

rocky hogback ridge
Erosion gradually carved a distinct hogback in the rocky terrain.
From a distance, the hogback looked like the spine of a giant creature.
The hikers followed the narrow hogback to reach the summit of the mountain.
