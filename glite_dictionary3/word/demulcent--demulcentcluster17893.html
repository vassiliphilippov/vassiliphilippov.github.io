demulcent

noun
soothing medication for irritated tissue
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmʌlsənt / (AmE), / dɪˈmʌlsənt / (BrE)
A substance, especially a medication, that soothes or protects irritated or inflamed tissue, particularly of the skin or mucous membranes.

Examples

herbal demulcent
Applying a demulcent can help protect damaged skin from further irritation.
Many cough syrups contain a demulcent to soothe the lining of the throat.
The pharmacist recommended a demulcent to relieve her sore throat.
