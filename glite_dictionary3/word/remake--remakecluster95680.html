remake

noun
new version of a film, song, etc.
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌmeɪk / (AmE), / ˈriːˌmeɪk / (BrE)
a new version of something, especially a film or song, that has been created again based on an earlier version

Examples

remake of a movie
Fans compared the original soundtrack to its recent remake.
Many critics felt the remake failed to capture the magic of the first film.
The studio released a remake of the classic 1950s sci-fi movie.

Related Terms

  • synonym – adaptation: a film or other work created from a previous source, often a book
