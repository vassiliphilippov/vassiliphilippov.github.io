to shave

verb
reduce an amount or price slightly
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪv / (AmE), / ʃeɪv / (BrE)
to reduce the amount or price of something, usually by a small amount

Examples

to shave the price
In an effort to attract more customers, the airline decided to shave its ticket prices.
The store agreed to shave ten percent off the price of the TV.
They managed to shave a few dollars from their monthly expenses.
