to starve

verb
deprive of something necessary
Pronunciation: / stɑːrv / (AmE), / stɑːv / (BrE)
to deprive someone or something of something necessary, causing suffering or malfunction

Examples

to starve the body
If you starve your body of essential vitamins, you can develop serious health problems.
The old computer was starved of updates, making it vulnerable to viruses.
The plants were starved of sunlight and slowly withered away.
