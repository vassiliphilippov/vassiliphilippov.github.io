halfway

adjective, adverb
in the middle between two points
Pronunciation: / ˈhæfˌweɪ /; / ˌhæfˈweɪ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf.weɪ /; / ˌhɑːfˈweɪ / (BrE)
in the middle between two points, places, or times; at an equal distance from two ends
