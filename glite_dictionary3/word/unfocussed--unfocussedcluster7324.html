unfocussed

adjective
not in clear focus; blurry
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfoʊkəst / (AmE), / ʌnˈfəʊkəst / (BrE)
(of an image, view, or gaze) not brought into clear focus; blurry or indistinct.

Examples

unfocussed image
After waking up suddenly, her vision remained unfocussed for a few moments.
His eyes grew unfocussed as he stared into the distance.
The photograph was too unfocussed to see any details clearly.

Related Terms

  • antonym – focussed: (of an image) brought into sharp focus
  • synonym – unfocused: variant spelling meaning not brought into clear focus
