clothed

adjective
covered with clothes or something like a covering
Pronunciation: / kloʊðd / (AmE), / kləʊðd / (BrE)
covered with clothes, a wrap, or as if with a layer or garment.

Examples

clothed in snow
She admired the mountains, clothed in winter snow.
The trees were clothed with new green leaves in spring.
The valley was clothed in a thick layer of morning mist.
