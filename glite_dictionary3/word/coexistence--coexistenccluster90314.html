coexistence

noun
state of existing together
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊ.ɪɡˈzɪs.təns / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.ɪɡˈzɪs.təns / (BrE)
the state of living or existing together, often peacefully, especially despite differences

Examples

peaceful coexistence
Achieving peaceful coexistence among competing nations remains a challenge.
The region's history is a testament to the coexistence of different religions and cultures.
Wildlife reserves are designed to encourage coexistence between humans and animals.

Related Terms

  • antonym – conflict: struggle or serious disagreement, often involving opposing groups
  • synonym – harmony: the state of living in agreement or peace
