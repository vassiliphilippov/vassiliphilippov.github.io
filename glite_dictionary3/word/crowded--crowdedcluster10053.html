crowded

adjective
filled beyond comfortable capacity
Pronunciation: / ˈkraʊdɪd / (AmE), / ˈkraʊdɪd / (BrE)
containing more people or things than is comfortable or possible; filled to excess capacity.

Examples

crowded street
His schedule was crowded with meetings and appointments all week.
She prefers to avoid crowded places because she feels anxious in large groups.
The subway was so crowded during rush hour that I could barely move.
