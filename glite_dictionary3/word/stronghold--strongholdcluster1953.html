stronghold

noun
fortified defensive structure
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɔŋˌhoʊld / (AmE), / ˈstrɒŋˌhəʊld / (BrE)
A place that has been fortified to protect against attack; a secure defensive structure.

Examples

mountain stronghold
Rebels retreated to their mountain stronghold as government forces advanced.
The castle served as a stronghold during the war, protecting the villagers from invaders.
The city was considered a stronghold of traditional values for centuries.
