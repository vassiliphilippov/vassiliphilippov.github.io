voiced

adjective
expressed in words or speech
Pronunciation: / vɔɪst / (AmE), / vɔɪst / (BrE)
expressed or communicated in spoken or written words; stated aloud or made known verbally.

Her concerns were voiced during the meeting, ensuring everyone understood her position.
It is important that every idea is voiced in a group discussion.
