waist

noun
narrow part of the body between ribs and hips
Pronunciation: / weɪst / (AmE), / weɪst / (BrE)
the part of the human body below the ribs and above the hips, often narrower than the parts above and below it

Examples

waist size
Her sweater was too tight around the waist, making it uncomfortable to wear.
She wore a belt around her waist, accentuating her slim figure.
The tailor measured his waist before altering the trousers.
