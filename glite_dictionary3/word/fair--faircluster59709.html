fair

adjective
manuscript with few corrections
Pronunciation: / fɛr / (AmE), / feə / (BrE)
(of a manuscript or document) having few or no corrections or alterations; prepared as a final, clean copy.

Examples

fair copy
After making several drafts, she wrote out a fair version of her novel.
Please submit a fair copy of your essay for evaluation.
The fair manuscript was easy to read, with barely any corrections.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clean: free from marks or corrections, as in a 'clean copy' of a document
