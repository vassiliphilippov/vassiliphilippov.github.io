to low

verb
make the sound of a cow; moo
Pronunciation: / loʊ / (AmE), / ləʊ / (BrE)
(of a cow or similar animal) to make a deep, long sound; to moo.

Examples

cows low softly
I heard a cow low softly in the early morning mist.
The cattle began to low as the farmer approached with food.
The distant lowing of the herd echoed across the valley.
