broiler

noun
oven or stove part for broiling
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈbrɔɪlə / (BrE)
An oven or a compartment in a stove used for cooking food with direct radiant heat from above.

Examples

oven broiler
After preheating the broiler, he cooked the steak for five minutes.
She placed the salmon under the broiler for a quick, crispy finish.
The recipe says to use the broiler to melt the cheese evenly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – grill: a device or part of a stove for cooking by direct heat, usually from below
