Mauritanie

proper noun
French name for Mauritania (African country)
Pronunciation: / moʊˌriːtəˈniː / (AmE), / məʊˌriːtəˈniː / (BrE)
The French name for Mauritania, a country in northwestern Africa bordering the Atlantic Ocean, mostly covered by the Sahara Desert.
