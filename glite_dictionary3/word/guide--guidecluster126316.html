to guide

verb
to lead or show the way
Pronunciation: / ɡaɪd / (AmE), / ɡaɪd / (BrE)
to show someone the way or lead them to a place, or to direct the course of someone or something

Examples

to guide a group
A local resident guided the lost tourists back to their hotel.
She guides visitors from the entrance to the exhibition hall.
The park ranger guided us through the forest trail safely.
A series of posts guided the hikers along the winding trail.
She used her instincts to guide us out of the dense forest.
The pilot skillfully guided the plane through the mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym – conduct: to guide or escort someone to a place
  • synonym – lead: to go with or in front of someone to show them the way
