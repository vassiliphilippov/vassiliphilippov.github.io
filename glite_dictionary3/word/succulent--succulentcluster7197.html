succulent

adjective
tender and juicy
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌk.jə.lənt / (AmE), / ˈsʌk.jʊ.lənt / (BrE)
tender, juicy, and full of moisture, especially when referring to food or plants

Examples

succulent steak
Desert environments often feature succulent plants that store water in their thick leaves.
She served a succulent roast chicken with herb stuffing for dinner.
The peaches were so succulent that the juice dripped down our fingers as we ate them.

Related Terms

  • synonym – juicy: full of juice; succulent, especially when describing food
