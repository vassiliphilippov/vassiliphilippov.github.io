to offset

verb
transfer ink to another surface (printing)
Pronunciation: / ˈɑfˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈɒfˌsɛt / (BrE)
(of printed matter) to transfer or smear ink onto another surface unintentionally.

Examples

to offset ink marks
Be careful when stacking these brochures, as the images might offset and leave marks.
If wet ink is not allowed to dry properly, it can offset onto the next page.
The printer used special drying techniques to prevent the text from offsetting onto other sheets.
