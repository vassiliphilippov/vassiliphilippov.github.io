aestivation

noun
arrangement of floral parts in a bud
Pronunciation: / ˌɛstəˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌiːstɪˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
(Botany) The arrangement of sepals and petals in a flower bud before it opens.
