barren

adjective
completely lacking; devoid
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrən / (AmE), / ˈbar.ən / (BrE)
completely lacking of a specified quality; devoid.

Examples

barren apology

His speech was barren of original ideas and inspiration.
The investigation's report was barren of useful conclusions.
