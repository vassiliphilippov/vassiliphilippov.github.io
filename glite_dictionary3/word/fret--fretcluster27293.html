to fret

verb
decorate with ornamental interlaced design
Pronunciation: / frɛt / (AmE), / frɛt / (BrE)
to decorate with an interlaced or ornamental pattern, especially with repeated geometric designs, or to decorate or ornament with a carved pattern, especially in wood or stone
