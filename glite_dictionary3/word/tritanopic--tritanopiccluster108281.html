tritanopic

adjective
unable to see blue or distinguish blue from yellow
Pronunciation: / ˌtraɪtəˈnoʊpɪk / (AmE), / ˌtrɪtəˈnɒpɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or affected by tritanopia, a form of color blindness in which a person cannot see blue or distinguish blue from yellow.

Examples

tritanopic vision test
A tritanopic test was used to examine his color perception.
People with tritanopic vision have difficulty distinguishing between blue and yellow objects.
The tritanopic condition is rarer than other forms of color blindness.
