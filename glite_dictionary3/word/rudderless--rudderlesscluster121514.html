rudderless

adjective
lacking direction or purpose
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌdər.ləs / (AmE), / ˈrʌdə.ləs / (BrE)
Lacking a clear sense of direction or purpose; without guidance or leadership.

Examples

rudderless company
After the CEO resigned, the company seemed rudderless and uncertain about its future.
He felt completely rudderless after graduating, unsure of which career path to take.
Without her guidance, the team became rudderless and struggled to make decisions.
