wiry

adjective
lean and sinewy in build
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪɹi / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəɹi / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) lean, strong, and having little fat, with muscles that are tough and sinewy.

Examples

wiry build
Despite his age, the old man was wiry and incredibly strong.
Her wiry frame made her an excellent long-distance runner.
The wiry dog darted through the underbrush with remarkable agility.
