disturber

noun
person causing disturbance
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɝːbər / (AmE), / dɪˈstɜːbə / (BrE)
a person who interrupts peace or causes disorder and commotion

Examples

noisy disturber
No one wanted to be known as a disturber during the ceremony.
She complained to security about the disturber at the library.
The loud music made him a disturber of the neighborhood's tranquility.

Related Terms
