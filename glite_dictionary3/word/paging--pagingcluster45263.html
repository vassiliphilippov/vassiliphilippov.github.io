paging

noun
calling someone's name over a loudspeaker
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of calling out the name of a person, especially using a loudspeaker system, to summon their attention

Examples

paging a customer
Frequent paging over the intercom kept the staff informed about arrivals.
Please refrain from unnecessary paging unless it is an emergency.
The nurse used paging to call the doctor to the emergency room.
