isophene

noun
line marking simultaneous biological events
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsəˌfiːn / (AmE), / ˈaɪsəˌfiːn / (BrE)
A line on a map connecting places where a specific biological event, such as plant flowering, occurs at the same time.
