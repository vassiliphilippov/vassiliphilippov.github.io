cowboy

noun
male performer skilled in rodeo riding and roping
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a man who performs in exhibitions of riding, roping, and similar skills, especially in rodeos or wild west shows

Examples

rodeo cowboy performer
As a professional cowboy, he traveled the country competing in rodeos.
He dreamed of becoming a famous cowboy after watching his idol perform at the arena.
The audience cheered as the cowboy expertly roped the charging steer.
