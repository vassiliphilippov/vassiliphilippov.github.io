mould

noun
container for shaping materials
Pronunciation: / moʊld / (AmE), / məʊld / (BrE)
a hollow container into which liquid or soft material is poured so that it takes a particular shape when it becomes solid

Examples

plastic mould container
Plastic parts are shaped by injecting material into a mould.
She used a jelly mould to make a festive dessert.
The chocolate was poured into a mould and left to set.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cast: to shape material by pouring it into a mould
  • synonym – template: a pattern used as a guide for shaping something
