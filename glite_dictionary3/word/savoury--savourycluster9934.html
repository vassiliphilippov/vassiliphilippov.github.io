savoury

noun
aromatic herb used for seasoning
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvəri / (AmE), / ˈseɪvəri / (BrE)
Either of two aromatic herbs of the mint family, used as a culinary seasoning: summer savoury (Satureja hortensis) or winter savoury (Satureja montana).

Examples

dried savoury herb
Dried savoury is often added to bean dishes for extra aroma.
She grows both summer and winter savoury in her herb garden.
The recipe called for a pinch of savoury to enhance the flavor of the stew.
