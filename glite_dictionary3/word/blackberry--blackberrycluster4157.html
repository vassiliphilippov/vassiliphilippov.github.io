blackberry

noun
sweet dark purple or black berry; the bramble plant
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈblækˌbəri / (BrE)
a wild bush with prickly stems that produces small, sweet, edible black or dark purple berries

Examples

wild blackberry bush
She added a handful of blackberries to her morning cereal.
The blackberry bush behind the shed is full of ripe fruit every summer.
We went to the woods to pick fresh blackberries for the pie.
