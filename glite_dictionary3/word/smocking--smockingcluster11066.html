smocking

noun
decorative gathered embroidery on fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsmɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
Decorative embroidery made by gathering fabric in tight stitches, typically to create elasticity and ornamental patterns on garments.

Examples

smocking on a dress
She learned the technique of smocking to add detail to children's clothing.
The dress featured delicate smocking around the collar and cuffs.
Vintage blouses with hand-stitched smocking are highly prized by collectors.
