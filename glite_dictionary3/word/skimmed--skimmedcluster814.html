skimmed

adjective
milk with cream removed
Pronunciation: / skɪmd / (AmE), / skɪmd / (BrE)
(of milk or a milk product) having had the cream removed; containing very little fat.

Examples

skimmed milk
Her dietician recommended switching to skimmed dairy products.
I always buy skimmed milk to reduce my fat intake.
The café offers both whole and skimmed milk for your coffee.

Related Terms

  • degree lower – semi-skimmed: milk from which part of the cream has been removed
  • synonym – skim milk: milk with almost all the cream removed
  • antonym – whole milk: milk as it is produced, with its natural fat content
