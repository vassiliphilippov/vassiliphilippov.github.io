to whoosh

verb
move quickly with a rushing sound
Pronunciation: / wuʃ /; / wuːʃ / (AmE), / wuːʃ / (BrE)
to move very quickly, often making a rushing or whistling sound (including gushing or squirting out rapidly)

Examples

The train whooshed past the platform, startling the passengers.
