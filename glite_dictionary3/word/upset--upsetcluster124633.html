upset

adjective
feeling troubled or distressed
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈsɛt / (AmE), / ʌpˈsɛt / (BrE)
feeling troubled, anxious, or distressed, especially because of something unpleasant or worrying

Examples

upset by news

It was clear from her expression that she was deeply upset by what happened.
She was so upset after the argument that she couldn't sleep.
