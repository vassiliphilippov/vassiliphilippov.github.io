to offer

verb
to put on (a play, show, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈɑfər / (AmE), / ˈɒfə / (BrE)
to present (a play, show, etc.) for public viewing; to produce or introduce on the stage

Examples

offer a play
Our drama club is offering a series of one-act plays this spring.
The local theater will offer a new comedy next season.
They offered an exciting production of 'Hamlet' last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – present: to put on a play or performance for an audience
  • synonym – stage: to produce and perform a play or show
