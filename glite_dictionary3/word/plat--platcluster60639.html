plat

noun
map of land divisions or lots
Pronunciation: / plæt / (AmE), / plæt / (BrE)
a map, drawn to scale, showing the divisions, streets, and lots of a piece of land, especially for survey or legal purposes

Examples

plat of land
According to the plat, the lot extends all the way to the riverbank.
Before construction can begin, a detailed plat must be filed with the county office.
The property developer submitted a plat to the city for approval of the new subdivision.
