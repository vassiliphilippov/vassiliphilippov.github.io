bare-knuckled

adjective
uncompromising; disregarding rules
Pronunciation: / bɛr ˈnʌkəld / (AmE), / beə ˈnʌkəld / (BrE)
(of an action or activity) performed in a direct and uncompromising way, often disregarding rules or conventions.

Examples

bare-knuckled business tactics
He pursued a bare-knuckled approach to business, cutting costs wherever possible.
The debate turned bare-knuckled as both candidates refused to hold back their criticisms.
The negotiations became bare-knuckled, with neither side willing to compromise.
