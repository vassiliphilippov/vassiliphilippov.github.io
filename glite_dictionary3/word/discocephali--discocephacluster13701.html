Discocephali

noun
order of fishes including remoras
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskoʊˈsɛfəli / (AmE), / ˌdɪskəʊˈsɛfəli / (BrE)
a small order of fishes (remoras) with a disk- or sucker-like structure on their heads used to attach to larger marine animals
