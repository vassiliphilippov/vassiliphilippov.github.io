communization

noun
conversion to state ownership
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːmjəˌnaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / kəˌmjunəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒmjʊnaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / kəˌmjuːnaɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the act or process of transferring property or business from private individuals to the state or community

Examples
