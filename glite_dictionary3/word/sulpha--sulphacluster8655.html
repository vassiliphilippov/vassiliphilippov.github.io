sulpha

noun
synthetic antibacterial drug
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌlfə / (AmE), / ˈsʌlfə / (BrE)
any of several synthetic antibacterial drugs that inhibit the growth of bacteria by interfering with the use of PABA (para-aminobenzoic acid)
