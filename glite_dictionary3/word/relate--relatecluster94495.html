to relate

verb
narrate; recount events
Pronunciation: / rɪˈleɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪt / (BrE)
to give an account of something; to narrate or recount

Examples

relate a story
During the interview, the witness related the events in clear detail.
She related her adventures in South America to her excited friends.
The author relates how the tragedy unfolded over several days.
