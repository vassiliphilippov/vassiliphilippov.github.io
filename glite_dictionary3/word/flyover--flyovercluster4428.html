flyover

noun
3D aerial tour in map apps
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈflaɪəʊvə / (BrE)
a feature in digital mapping applications that provides an interactive, photorealistic 3D aerial tour of a place, simulating the experience of flying over it.

Examples

flyover mode map
She explored Tokyo in flyover mode on her map app.
The travel site uses a photorealistic flyover to show users how neighborhoods look from above.
During the presentation, the designer played a short flyover of the proposed riverside development.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bird's-eye view: an elevated view of an area from above, often used for maps or images
  • synonym – satellite view: a map or image composed from satellite photographs showing an area from above
