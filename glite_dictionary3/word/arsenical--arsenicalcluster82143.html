arsenical

noun
a substance containing arsenic, used as pesticide or drug
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈsɛnɪkəl / (AmE), / ɑːˈsɛnɪkəl / (BrE)
a pesticide, drug, or other substance that contains arsenic, especially one used for controlling pests or as a medical treatment

Examples

an arsenical pesticide
