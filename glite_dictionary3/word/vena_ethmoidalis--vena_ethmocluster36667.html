vena ethmoidalis

noun
vein draining the ethmoidal sinuses
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪnə ˌɛθmɔɪˈdeɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˈviːnə ˌɛθmɔɪˈdeɪlɪs / (BrE)
Either of the veins that drain the ethmoidal sinuses, typically emptying into the superior ophthalmic vein.

Examples

vena ethmoidalis drains sinuses
An understanding of the vena ethmoidalis is important for surgeons operating near the orbit.
Damage to the vena ethmoidalis can result in complications during sinus surgery.
The vena ethmoidalis are responsible for draining blood from the ethmoidal sinuses.
