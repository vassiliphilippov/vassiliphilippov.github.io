perfecta

noun
bet on exact first and second finishers
Pronunciation: / pərˈfɛktə / (AmE), / pəˈfɛktə / (BrE)
a type of bet, especially in horse racing, in which the bettor must predict the first and second place finishers in the exact order

Examples

win a perfecta bet
Betting a perfecta is riskier than a quinella because the order matters.
He studied the racing form carefully before placing a perfecta on the race.
She won big after her perfecta bet on the top two horses paid off.
