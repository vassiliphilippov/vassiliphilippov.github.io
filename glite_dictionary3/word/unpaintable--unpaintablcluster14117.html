unpaintable

adjective
not able to be painted
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpeɪntəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpeɪntəbl / (BrE)
not able to be painted, especially not suitable for artistic depiction on canvas

Examples

unpaintable landscape
Some landscapes seem so surreal they feel almost unpaintable by even the most skilled artist.
The concept was so abstract that it was considered unpaintable in any traditional artistic medium.
The emotion he described was utterly unpaintable in words or with a brush.
