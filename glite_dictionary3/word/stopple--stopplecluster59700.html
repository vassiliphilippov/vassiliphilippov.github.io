to stopple

verb
close with a stopper
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːpəl / (AmE), / ˈstɒpəl / (BrE)
to close or seal something with or as if with a stopper

Examples

to stopple a jug
He carefully stoppled the jug to preserve the scent of the wine.
The pharmacist stoppled the vial before handing it to the nurse.
To avoid spillage, make sure to stopple every bottle tightly after use.
