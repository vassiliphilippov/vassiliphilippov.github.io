exiguity

noun
meagerness; scantiness
Pronunciation: / ɛɡˈzɪɡjuəti / (AmE), / ɛɡˈzɪɡjuːɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being scanty or meager; insufficiency or scantiness

Examples

exiguity of resources
Due to the exiguity of evidence, the case remained unsolved.
The exiguity of his resources made the task much more difficult.
The artist was forced to work with an exiguity of materials.
