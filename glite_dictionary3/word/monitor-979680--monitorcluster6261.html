to monitor

verb
to observe or check; track by device
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnɪtɚ /; / ˈmɑːnɪtər / (AmE), / ˈmɒnɪtə / (BrE)
to observe, check, or keep track of something over time, especially using electronic equipment or for a special purpose
