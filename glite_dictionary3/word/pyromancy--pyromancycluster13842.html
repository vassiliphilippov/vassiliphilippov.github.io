pyromancy

noun
divination by fire or flames
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪrəˌmænsi / (AmE), / ˈpɪərəˌmænsi / (BrE)
divination by interpreting fire or flames, especially their shapes and movements

Examples

pyromancy for answers
In some cultures, pyromancy was considered an important tool for making decisions.
Legends describe how oracles used pyromancy by reading the shapes in dancing flames.
The ancient priest practiced pyromancy to predict the outcome of the harvest.
