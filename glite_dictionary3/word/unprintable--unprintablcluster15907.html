unprintable

adjective
not suitable to be printed, offensive
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈprɪntəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈprɪntəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of language or material) unsuitable to be printed, especially because it is offensive, obscene, or not in good taste.

Examples

unprintable language
Many of the emails were so unprintable that they had to be censored before release.
The comedian was known for making unprintable jokes that shocked the audience.
The editor refused to publish the article because it contained several unprintable remarks.

Related Terms

  • synonym – obscene: offensive or disgusting to morality or decency
  • antonym – printable: suitable for printing; not offensive
