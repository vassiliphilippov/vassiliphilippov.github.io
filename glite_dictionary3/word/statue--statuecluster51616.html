statue

noun
sculpture of a person or animal
Pronunciation: / ˈstætʃu / (AmE), / ˈstætʃuː / (BrE)
A carved or cast figure of a person or animal, especially one that is life-size or larger, made from materials such as stone, metal, or plaster.

Examples

marble statue
Children played near the marble statue in the park.
The artist spent months sculpting a statue of a galloping horse.
The city square is famous for its bronze statue of a historic hero.

  • synonym – sculpture: the art of making three-dimensional figures or the object itself
