single-spaced

adjective
having no blank space between lines
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋ.ɡəl speɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋ.ɡl̩ speɪst / (BrE)
(of typed or printed text) having no blank spaces between lines.

Examples

single-spaced text
Her handwritten notes were always single-spaced for efficiency.
Please submit your essay as a single-spaced document.
The instructions specified that the report should be single-spaced using a standard font.

Related Terms

  • antonym – double-spaced: having a blank space between each line of text
