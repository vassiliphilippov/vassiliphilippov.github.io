to rendezvous

verb
meet at a prearranged place/time
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːndɪˌvuː / (AmE), / ˈrɒndɪvuː / (BrE)
to meet at a prearranged place and time; to come together at an agreed location

Examples

to rendezvous at camp
The expedition will rendezvous at base camp before continuing their journey.
The ships rendezvoused just off the coast before launching the joint operation.
We agreed to rendezvous at the café at noon.

  • synonym – meet: to come together with; to encounter (as persons or things)
