pliability

noun
adaptability or flexibility of mind
Pronunciation: / ˌplaɪəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌplaɪəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being easily influenced or adaptable in mind or character.

Examples

mental pliability
Companies often seek employees with pliability to respond to changing demands.
He admired the pliability of her opinions during difficult negotiations.
Her pliability allowed her to adjust quickly to new roles within the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym – flexibility: the quality of being able to change or adapt easily
  • antonym – rigidity: the quality of being unwilling to change or adapt
