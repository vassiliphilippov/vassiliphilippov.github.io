down

adjective, adverb
not functioning (esp. of systems or machines)
Pronunciation: / daʊn / (AmE), / daʊn / (BrE)
(of a machine, system, or service) not working or inoperative, usually temporarily

Examples

down for repairs
Our phones are currently down due to a power outage in the area.
The network will be down for maintenance tonight from midnight to 4 a.m.
When the website was down, customers were unable to place any orders.
Our phone lines went down during the storm, causing communication delays.
The website is down for maintenance, so you can't access it right now.
We couldn't process orders because the payment system was down all afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym – offline: not connected to or not operating on a network
  • antonym – up: working or functioning (of a computer, service, etc.)
