gutturally

adverb
in a harsh, throaty manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌt̬ɚəli / (AmE), / ˈɡʌt.jʊ.rə.li / (BrE)
In a guttural manner; using a harsh, throaty sound typically produced in the throat.

Examples

speak gutturally
He laughed gutturally, the sound rumbling deep in his throat.
She greeted us gutturally, her accent thick and throaty.
The actor spoke gutturally to convey the villain's menace.
