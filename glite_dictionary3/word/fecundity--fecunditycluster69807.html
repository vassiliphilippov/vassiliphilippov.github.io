fecundity

noun
ability to produce abundant growth or offspring
Pronunciation: / fɪˈkʌndəti / (AmE), / fɪˈkʌndɪti / (BrE)
the ability to produce many offspring, new growth, or to be very productive (especially in a biological or general sense); fertility
