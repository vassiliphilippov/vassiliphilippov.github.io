plainclothesman

noun
male detective or officer in civilian clothing
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪnˌkloʊðzˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈpleɪnˌkləʊðzˌmæn / (BrE)
A male police officer or detective who works in ordinary civilian clothes rather than a uniform, especially while on duty.
