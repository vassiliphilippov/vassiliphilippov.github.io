bravura

noun
brilliant technical skill (especially in the arts)
Pronunciation: / brəˈvʊr.ə / (AmE), / brəˈvjʊə.rə / (BrE)
a display of brilliant and showy technical skill, especially in artistic performance such as music or dance

Examples

bravura performance
Her solo was marked by extraordinary bravura and expressive depth.
The finale required considerable bravura from the orchestra's lead violinist.
The pianist's performance ended with a breathtaking bravura that left the audience in awe.
