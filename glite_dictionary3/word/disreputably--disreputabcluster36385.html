disreputably

adverb
in a disreputable manner
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪs.rɪˈpjuː.təb.li / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.rɪˈpjuː.təb.li / (BrE)

She disreputably avoided paying her taxes for several years.
The bar was known for being frequented by disreputably dressed patrons and shady characters.
