Eucalyptus coriacea

noun
Australian tree with smooth, shedding bark
Pronunciation: / juːˌkʌlɪptəs kɔːriˈeɪʃiə / (AmE), / juːˌkælɪptəs kɒriˈeɪsɪə / (BrE)
A small to medium-sized tree native to Australia and Tasmania, known for its smooth white to light-grey bark that sheds in patches or strips.
