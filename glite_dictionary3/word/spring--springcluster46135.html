spring

noun
elasticity; ability to return to shape
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ / (AmE), / sprɪŋ / (BrE)
a device (usually a metal coil) or the ability of something to return to its original shape after being stretched or compressed

Examples

spring in a mattress
Athletes often test the spring of a surface before jumping.
The spring in this fabric allows it to stretch without losing its shape.
The old mattress lost its spring after years of use.
He fixed the clock by replacing the broken spring inside.
The mattress contains a spring that makes it more comfortable to sleep on.
When you press the button, a spring pops it back up again.

Related Terms

  • synonym – coil: a looped or spiral shape, often as in a spring
  • synonym – elasticity: the quality of being elastic; ability to resume original shape
