kitambilla

noun
tropical fruit tree of Sri Lanka and India
Pronunciation: / kɪˌtɑːmˈbɪlə / (AmE), / kɪˌtæmˈbɪlə / (BrE)
A small shrubby spiny tree native to Sri Lanka and India, cultivated for its maroon-purple fruit with sweet, gooseberry-like pulp.

kitambilla tree leaves
