to fold

verb
confine animals in a pen
Pronunciation: / foʊld / (AmE), / fəʊld / (BrE)
to confine animals, especially sheep, in a fold (an enclosure or pen)

Examples

to fold sheep
After grazing all day, the animals were folded in the pen near the barn.
Every evening, the farmer folds his flock to keep them safe.
