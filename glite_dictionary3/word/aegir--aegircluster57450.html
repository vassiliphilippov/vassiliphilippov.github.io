aegir

noun
dangerous tidal wave caused by tides
Pronunciation: / ˈiːdʒər / (AmE), / ˈiːdʒə / (BrE)
a high, often dangerous tidal wave caused by the flow of tides, especially in narrow estuaries or where tidal currents collide

a powerful aegir wave
Fishermen watched as the massive aegir surged up the estuary, flooding the banks.
