to jockey

verb
ride a racehorse professionally
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑːki / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒki / (BrE)
to ride a racehorse as a professional jockey, especially in a competitive event

Examples

to jockey a horse
He has jockeyed hundreds of winning horses throughout his career.
Many young riders dream of one day jockeying on the biggest international tracks.
