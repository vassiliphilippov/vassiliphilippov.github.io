powderpuff

noun
soft pad for applying powder
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdərˌpʌf / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdəˌpʌf / (BrE)
a small, soft, rounded pad made of fluffy material, used for applying powder to the skin, especially on the face

Examples

powderpuff makeup pad
After washing her face, she dusted on some powder with her favorite powderpuff.
She used a powderpuff to gently apply the makeup to her cheeks.
The soft texture of the powderpuff made it ideal for sensitive skin.
