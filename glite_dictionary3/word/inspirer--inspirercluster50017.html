inspirer

noun
one who inspires others to act
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈspaɪɹɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈspaɪərə / (BrE)
a person who motivates or stimulates others to take action, often through encouragement or enthusiasm

Many consider Martin Luther King Jr. to be a great inspirer of positive change.
The company was looking for an inspirer who could lead by example and energize the staff.
