to factorize

verb
reduce a polynomial to its factors
Pronunciation: / ˈfæk.təˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈfæk.tə.raɪz / (BrE)
to resolve a mathematical expression, especially a polynomial, into a product of simpler expressions or factors

Examples

to factorize a polynomial
It is not always possible to factorize a quadratic equation with integer coefficients.
Students learned how to factorize polynomials completely in their algebra class.
The computer program can quickly factorize large numbers and polynomials.
