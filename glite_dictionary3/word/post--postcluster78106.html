to post

verb
mark with a stake or post
Pronunciation: / poʊst / (AmE), / pəʊst / (BrE)
to mark or indicate the position of something by placing stakes or posts

Examples

to post boundaries
Surveyors were hired to post the property boundaries before building began.
The construction crew will post the planned route for the new road today.
They decided to post a line along the edge of the field with wooden stakes.
