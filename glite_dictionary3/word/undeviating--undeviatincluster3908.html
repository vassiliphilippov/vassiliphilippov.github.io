undeviating

adjective
steady and constant; unchanging
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈdiːvieɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈdiːvieɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Not changing or varying; steady and consistent, especially in values or principles.

Examples

undeviating commitment to truth

She followed the plan with undeviating precision, never straying from the guidelines.
The soldier swore undeviating loyalty to his country.
