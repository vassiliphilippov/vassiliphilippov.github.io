to broider

verb
decorate with needlework
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔɪdər / (AmE), / ˈbrɔɪdə / (BrE)
to decorate (a fabric or item) with needlework, especially by embroidering elaborate designs

Examples

to broider a dress
He learned to broider delicate flowers along the edges of the cloth.
She would patiently broider intricate designs onto the fine silk.
The old tapestries had been broidered with scenes of the countryside.
