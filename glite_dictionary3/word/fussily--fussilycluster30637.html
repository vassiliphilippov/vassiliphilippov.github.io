fussily

adverb
in an excessively particular or fussy manner
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌsɪli / (AmE), / ˈfʌsɪli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows excessive concern for details or small things; in an overly particular or fastidious way.

Examples

fussily arranged table
He fussily explained the rules, not missing a single minor detail.
She checked the table setting fussily, adjusting every fork and spoon several times.
The assistant arranged the documents fussily, insisting they be perfectly aligned.
