terrorist

noun
person who uses violence for political aims
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrərɪst / (AmE), / ˈtɛrərɪst / (BrE)
a person who uses unlawful violence and intimidation, especially against civilians, in the pursuit of political aims

Examples

terrorist attack
Many countries have strict laws against supporting terrorist organizations.
Security forces arrested two terrorists near the border last night.
The attack was carried out by a terrorist group with a political agenda.

Related Terms

  • antonym – civilian: a person not in the armed services or the police force
  • synonym – freedom fighter: a person who takes part in a violent struggle to achieve a political goal, especially to overthrow a government
