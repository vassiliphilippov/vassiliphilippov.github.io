normally

adverb
in the usual way; under normal conditions
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔːrməli / (AmE), / ˈnɔːməli / (BrE)
in the usual or expected way; under normal conditions

Examples

normally happens

The trains normally arrive on time, even in bad weather.
We normally eat dinner at seven o'clock.
