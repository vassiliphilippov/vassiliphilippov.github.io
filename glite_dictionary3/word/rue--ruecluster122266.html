to rue

verb
to regret or feel sorry for
Pronunciation: / ruː / (AmE), / ruː / (BrE)
to feel regret or remorse for something; to be sorry about an action or event

Examples

to rue a mistake
He will surely rue the day he made that decision.
Many people rue their mistakes long after they are made.
She rued not taking the opportunity when it was offered.

Related Terms

  • synonym – regret: to feel sad, repentant, or disappointed over something that has happened
  • antonym – rejoice: to feel or show great joy or delight
