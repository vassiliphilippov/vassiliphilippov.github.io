vibrio

noun
curved rod-shaped bacterium
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪbrioʊ / (AmE), / ˈvɪbrɪəʊ / (BrE)
a genus of motile, curved rod-shaped bacteria, some species of which can cause diseases in humans and animals
