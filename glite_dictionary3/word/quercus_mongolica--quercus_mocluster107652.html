Quercus mongolica

noun
East Asian oak tree species
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɜrkəs mɒnˈɡɑlɪkə / (AmE), / ˈkwɜːkəs mɒŋˈɡɒlɪkə / (BrE)
A species of oak tree native to East Asia, known for its moderately light, fine-grained wood, especially found in Japan, China, Korea, and Russia.
