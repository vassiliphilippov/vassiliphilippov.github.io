blistery

adjective
extremely hot, as if raising blisters
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪstəri / (AmE), / ˈblɪstəri / (BrE)
(of weather or a surface) very hot, as if capable of causing blisters.

Examples

blistery summer day
On that blistery day, the sand burned our feet through our shoes.
The desert's blistery winds made travel almost unbearable.
We walked for miles under the blistery afternoon sun.
