dissertation

noun
long academic research paper for a degree
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsərˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɜːˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a long piece of writing on a particular subject, especially one that is written for a university degree or diploma, presenting the author's research and findings

Examples

write a dissertation
Before earning her doctorate, Maria had to defend her dissertation in front of a panel of experts.
She spent months writing her dissertation on the effects of climate change.
The graduate committee praised the originality of his dissertation.
