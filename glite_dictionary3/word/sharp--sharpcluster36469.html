sharp

adjective
clearly defined (sight or sound)
Pronunciation: / ʃɑrp / (AmE), / ʃɑːp / (BrE)
(of a sight or sound) clearly defined, distinct, or easy to perceive; not blurred or muffled.

Examples

sharp image on screen
His voice had a sharp clarity that cut through the noise of the crowd.
The mountain's outline appeared sharp against the morning sky.
The photograph was so sharp that you could see every detail of the subject's face.

Related Terms

  • antonym – blurred: not clear or distinct; lacking definition
  • synonym – clear: easy to perceive or understand; not blurred or obscured
  • synonym – crisp: clear, distinct in appearance or sound
