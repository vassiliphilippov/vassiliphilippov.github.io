natural

adjective
related by blood (biological)
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃrəl / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl / (BrE)
(of a parent or child) related by blood rather than adoption; biological.

Examples

natural parent
Courts often consider the rights of a natural parent in custody cases.
He recently discovered his natural father after years of searching.
She is his natural mother, while he was raised by an adoptive family.

Related Terms

  • antonym – adoptive: a parent or child related by legal adoption, not by blood
  • synonym – biological: related by biology or genetics, not adoption
