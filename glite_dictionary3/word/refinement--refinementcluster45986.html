refinement

noun
an improvement or enhancement
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfaɪnmənt / (AmE), / rɪˈfaɪnmənt / (BrE)
an improvement or small change made to something to make it better, more precise, or more sophisticated

Examples

refinement to design
After several refinements, the software became much more user-friendly.
The scientist introduced a refinement to the existing method, increasing its accuracy.
Their new recipe is a refinement of the traditional dish, making it lighter and healthier.

Related Terms

  • synonym – improvement: the act or process of making something better
  • synonym – modification: a change made to something, usually minor
