plow

noun
farm tool for turning soil
Pronunciation: / plaʊ / (AmE), / plaʊ / (BrE)
A large farming implement with one or more heavy blades, used for turning over the soil to prepare it for planting.

Examples

old plow machine
After years of use, the old plow was finally replaced with a new one.
Horses used to pull the plow before tractors became common.
The farmer used a plow to prepare the field before planting the seeds.
