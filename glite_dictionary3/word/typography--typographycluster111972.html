typography

noun
arrangement and design of printed type
Pronunciation: / taɪˈpɑɡrəfi /; / taɪˈpɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / taɪˈpɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the art and technique of arranging type to make written language visually appealing and easy to read, especially in printing or graphic design
