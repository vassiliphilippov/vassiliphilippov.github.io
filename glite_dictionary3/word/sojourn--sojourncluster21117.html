to sojourn

verb
stay temporarily
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊˌdʒɝn / (AmE), / ˈsɒdʒən / (BrE)
to stay in a place temporarily; to reside for a short period.

Examples

to sojourn in Paris
During their European tour, the artists sojourned in Paris for several weeks.
He will sojourn at his aunt's cottage while searching for a new apartment.
Travelers often sojourn in remote villages to experience local traditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym – reside: to live in a place permanently or for a long time
  • synonym – stay: to remain in a place for a period of time
