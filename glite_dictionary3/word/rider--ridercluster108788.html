rider

noun
person who rides an animal
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪdər / (AmE), / ˈraɪdə / (BrE)
a person who travels on an animal, especially a horse or camel, by sitting on its back and directing its movement

Examples

rider on a horse
A young rider fell off his pony during the show but was not hurt.
Each rider in the caravan wore protective gear to cross the desert on camels.
The experienced rider guided her horse skillfully through the narrow path.

Related Terms

  • synonym – equestrian: a person who rides horses, especially as a sport
