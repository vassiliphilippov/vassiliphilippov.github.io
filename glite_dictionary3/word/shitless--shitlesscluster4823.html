shitless

adjective
to the utmost degree; extremely
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtləs / (BrE)
Used after certain verbs to mean 'to the utmost degree'; completely or extremely.

Examples

scared shitless
He was scared shitless before his first skydiving jump.
She laughed shitless at his ridiculous joke.
The children were bored shitless during the long car ride.
