monoestrous

adjective
having a single annual estrous cycle
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑnoʊˈɛstrəs / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəʊˈɛstrəs / (BrE)
Having one estrous cycle in a year.

Examples

monoestrous animal species
Most wild foxes are monoestrous, breeding only once during the year.
The monoestrous reproductive pattern is common among large mammals such as bears and deer.
Unlike dogs, which are polyestrous, wolves are monoestrous and mate only in late winter.
