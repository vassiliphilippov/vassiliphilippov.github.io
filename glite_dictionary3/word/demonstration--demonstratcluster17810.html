demonstration

noun
public protest or political gathering
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
an event where a group of people gather publicly to express their opinions or protest, especially about political issues

Examples

public demonstration protest
Over 10,000 people attended the demonstration against new education laws in the city center.
Police were present to keep order during the peaceful demonstration on Sunday afternoon.
Yesterday’s demonstration attracted national media attention and led to several streets being closed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – protest: a public expression of objection or disapproval, often by a group
  • synonym – rally: a large public meeting, especially for support or protest
  • degree higher – riot: a violent public disturbance by a crowd
