aftereffect

noun
a delayed effect following an event or treatment
Pronunciation: / ˈæf.tɚ.ɪˌfɛkt /; / ˈæftərɪˌfɛkt / (AmE), / ˈɑːf.tər.ɪˌfɛkt /; / ˈɑːftərɪˌfɛkt / (BrE)
a delayed result or consequence that happens after an event, action, or treatment
