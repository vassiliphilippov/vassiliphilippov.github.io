load

noun
quantity of goods carried by a vehicle
Pronunciation: / loʊd / (AmE), / ləʊd / (BrE)
A quantity of goods or cargo carried by a vehicle, especially a large one such as a truck or ship.

Examples

truck load of goods
A full load of produce arrived at the market early in the morning.
After a long journey, the driver finally delivered the load to the warehouse.
The truck was carrying a heavy load of building materials.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cargo: goods carried by a ship, aircraft, or other large vehicle
  • synonym – freight: goods transported in bulk by truck, train, ship, or aircraft
