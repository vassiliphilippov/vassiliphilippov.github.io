to rebroadcast

verb
to broadcast again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbrɔdˌkæst / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbrɔːdˌkɑːst / (BrE)
to broadcast (a program, film, etc.) again after its original broadcast

Examples

to rebroadcast a program
The documentary was rebroadcast last night for viewers who missed it the first time.
The station plans to rebroadcast the interview due to popular demand.
They rebroadcast classic films every Sunday evening.
