Thallophyta

noun
former classification of seedless, flowerless organisms
Pronunciation: / θəˈlɑːləˌfaɪtə / (AmE), / θəˈlæləˌfaɪtə / (BrE)
(in former classifications) A major group of flowerless and seedless organisms, such as algae, fungi, and lichens, now considered a heterogeneous assemblage.

Examples

Thallophyta group of algae
