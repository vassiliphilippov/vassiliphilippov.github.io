to lipstick

verb
to apply or mark with lipstick
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪp.stɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɪp.stɪk / (BrE)
to apply or mark with lipstick, especially by coloring or tracing a shape or area

Examples

to lipstick a shape
During the play, the actors lipsticked large circles on their cheeks for a comic effect.
She lipsticked her signature onto the bathroom mirror before leaving.
The artist lipsticked a bright red heart on the canvas as a statement piece.
