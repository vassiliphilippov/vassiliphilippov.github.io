attender

noun
person who attends to another's needs
Pronunciation: / əˈtɛndər / (AmE), / əˈtɛndə / (BrE)
a person who waits on, tends to, or attends to the needs of another

Examples

attender to guests
As an attender, he ensured all the guests had everything they needed.
Each elderly resident is assigned an attender to help with daily activities.
The patient was grateful for the constant care of her attender during her recovery.
