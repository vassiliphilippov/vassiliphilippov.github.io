Prunus domestica insititia

noun
cultivated subspecies of plum tree
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːnəs doʊˈmɛstɪkə ɪnˌsɪtiːʃə / (AmE), / ˈpruːnəs dəˈmɛstɪkə ɪnˌsɪtɪtiːə / (BrE)
a subspecies of plum tree that has long been cultivated for its edible fruit, including greengages and damsons.
