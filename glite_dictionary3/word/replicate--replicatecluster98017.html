to replicate

verb
to make an exact copy
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛplɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈrɛplɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to make an exact copy of something; to reproduce or duplicate, especially in a scientific or technical context

Examples

to replicate a cell

This genetic material can replicate itself with high precision.
When exposed to the virus, the cells rapidly replicate and spread the infection.
