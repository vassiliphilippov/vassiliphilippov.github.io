brigade

noun
army unit smaller than a division
Pronunciation: / brɪˈɡeɪd / (AmE), / brɪˈɡeɪd / (BrE)
an army unit that is smaller than a division and typically consists of several battalions

Examples

army brigade
After the ceremony, the brigade marched proudly past the reviewing stand.
Each brigade is made up of several battalions commanded by a brigadier.
The 5th Infantry brigade was deployed to the border in response to the crisis.
