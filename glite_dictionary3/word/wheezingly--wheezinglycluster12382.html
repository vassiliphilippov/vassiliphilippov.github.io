wheezingly

adverb
in a wheezing manner
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːzɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈwiːzɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner characterized by wheezing; with a wheeze.

Examples

breathe wheezingly
He breathed wheezingly after running up the stairs.
She laughed wheezingly, her breath rattling in her chest.
The old man spoke wheezingly, struggling to catch his breath between words.
