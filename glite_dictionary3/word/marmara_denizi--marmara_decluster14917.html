Marmara Denizi

proper noun
inland sea in northwestern Turkey
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːrməˌrɑː dəˈniːzi / (AmE), / ˌmɑːmərə dəˈniːzi / (BrE)
an inland sea in northwestern Turkey, connecting the Black Sea to the Aegean Sea through the Bosporus and the Dardanelles
