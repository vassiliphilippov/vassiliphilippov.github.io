Kansa

noun
Siouan language of the Kansa people
Pronunciation: / ˈkænzə / (AmE), / ˈkænzə / (BrE)
the Siouan language variety spoken by the Kansa people, closely related to other Dhegiha dialects.

Examples

to speak Kansa language
He is learning to speak Kansa in an effort to help revitalize the language.
Linguists have documented many words from Kansa and related Dhegiha dialects.
The Kansa language was historically spoken along the Kansas River.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Kaw: the same language, also called 'Kaw' in reference to the Kansa tribe
  • synonym – Osage: the language of the Osage people, closely related Dhegiha Siouan dialect
