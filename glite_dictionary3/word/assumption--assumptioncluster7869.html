Assumption

noun
Virgin Mary's ascent into heaven (Christianity)
Pronunciation: / əˈsʌmpʃən / (AmE), / əˈsʌmpʃən / (BrE)
(in Christianity) the taking up into heaven of the body and soul of the Virgin Mary at the end of her earthly life.

Examples

the Assumption of Mary
Many famous works of art depict the Assumption of the Virgin Mary.
Pilgrims gathered to honor the Assumption at the cathedral.
The Feast of the Assumption is celebrated by Catholics every August 15th.
