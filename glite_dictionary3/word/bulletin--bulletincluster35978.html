bulletin

noun
short official report or statement
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlətɪn / (AmE), / ˈbʊlɪtɪn / (BrE)
a short official statement or report, especially one issued for immediate publication or broadcast

Examples

news bulletin
A weather bulletin interrupted the evening television program with news of the approaching storm.
The company released a bulletin announcing its new product line to the press.
The president issued a bulletin to update the nation on the emergency situation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – announcement: a public or formal notice or statement
  • synonym – communiqué: an official announcement or statement, especially to the media
