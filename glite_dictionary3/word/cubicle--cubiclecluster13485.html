cubicle

noun
small enclosed space or compartment
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːbɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈkjuːbɪkəl /; / ˈkjuːbɪkl̩ /; / ˈkjuːbɪk(ə)l / (BrE)
a small enclosed or partitioned space within a larger room, usually for one person to live, work, or study

Examples

She enjoyed the solitude of her cubicle after a long day of prayer.

I changed into my swimsuit in the changing cubicle at the pool.

He reserved a cubicle to study for his exams in peace.
