militarism

noun
policy of maintaining strong military power
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪtəˌrɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəˌrɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief or policy that a country should maintain a strong military capability and be ready to use it to defend or promote national interests

Examples
