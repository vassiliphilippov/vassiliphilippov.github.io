fitfully

adverb
irregularly; not continuously
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪtfəli / (AmE), / ˈfɪtfəli / (BrE)
In an irregular or interrupted manner; not steadily or continuously.

Examples

sleep fitfully
He slept fitfully, waking up every hour throughout the night.
Her interest in painting returned fitfully over the years, never lasting for long.
The wind blew fitfully through the narrow alleyways, making the lamps flicker.

Related Terms

  • synonym – intermittently: in a manner that stops and starts at intervals
  • antonym – steadily: in a regular, continuous manner
