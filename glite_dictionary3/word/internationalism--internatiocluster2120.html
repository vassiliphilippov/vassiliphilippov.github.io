internationalism

noun
the quality of being international in scope
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɚˈnæʃənəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəlɪz(ə)m /; / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the principle or belief that countries should cooperate and have a global outlook, rather than focusing only on their own interests

Examples

  • synonym – globalism: the operation or planning of economic and foreign policy on a global basis
  • antonym – nationalism: advocacy of the interests of one’s own nation, often to the exclusion of others
