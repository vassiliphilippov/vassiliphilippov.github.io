chancre

noun
painless sore (esp. in syphilis)
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæŋkɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃæŋkə / (BrE)
A painless, firm sore or ulcer that typically appears at the site of infection in the early stage of syphilis or certain other diseases.

chancre on genitals
