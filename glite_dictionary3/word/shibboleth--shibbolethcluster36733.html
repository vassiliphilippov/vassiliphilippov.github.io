shibboleth

noun
distinctive word or custom of a group
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪbəˌlɛθ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪbəˌlɛθ /; / ˈʃɪbəlɪθ / (BrE)
a word, phrase, or custom that shows someone belongs to a particular group, or a slogan or idea that is used to identify a group (sometimes seen as old-fashioned or unoriginal)
