unrefreshed

adjective
not rested or restored
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈfrɛʃt / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈfrɛʃt / (BrE)
not feeling rested or restored in energy, especially after sleep or a break

Examples

wake up unrefreshed

Despite eight hours of sleep, she woke up feeling unrefreshed and tired.
Some people report feeling unrefreshed even after a full night's rest.
