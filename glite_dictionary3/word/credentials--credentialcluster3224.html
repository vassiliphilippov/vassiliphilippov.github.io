credentials

noun
documents proving identity or qualifications
Pronunciation: / krəˈdɛnʃəlz / (AmE), / krɪˈdɛnʃəlz / (BrE)
documents or certificates that prove a person’s identity, qualifications, or status

Examples

credentials for a job

Her academic credentials include degrees from two prestigious universities.
