mamo

noun
extinct Hawaiian honeycreeper bird
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːməʊ / (BrE)
an extinct species of Hawaiian honeycreeper with black plumage and yellow feathers around the tail, once native to Hawaii
