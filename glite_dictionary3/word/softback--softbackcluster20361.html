softback

noun
book with a paper cover
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑftˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈsɒftˌbæk / (BrE)
A book with a flexible paper cover, as opposed to a hardcover book.

Examples

a softback book
All the textbooks for the course were published as softbacks to keep costs low.
She prefers reading novels in softback because they are lighter to carry.
The new edition is now available in both hardback and softback formats.

Related Terms

  • antonym – hardback: a book with a rigid cover, opposite of 'softback'
  • synonym – paperback: a book with a paper cover; another common term for 'softback'
