Grey

Lady Jane Grey, brief Queen of England
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪ / (AmE), / ɡreɪ / (BrE)
Lady Jane Grey (1536–1554), often referred to as the 'Nine Days' Queen,' was Queen of England for a brief period in 1553 before being deposed and executed for treason.
