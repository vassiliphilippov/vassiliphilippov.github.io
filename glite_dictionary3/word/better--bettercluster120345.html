better

adjective
more advisable, wiser, more advantageous
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛtər / (AmE), / ˈbɛtə / (BrE)
Wiser, more advisable, or more advantageous; used to indicate that something is preferable or recommended in a particular situation.

Examples

better choice to wait
It would be better to wait until morning before making a decision.
It's always better to ask for help if you're unsure.
You know better than to leave your keys in the car.
