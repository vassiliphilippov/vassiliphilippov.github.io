unrenewable

adjective
not able to be renewed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈnuː.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈnjuː.ə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be renewed or made new or fresh again

Examples

unrenewable visa
If your visa is unrenewable, you will have to leave the country when it expires.
Library books placed in the special collection are unrenewable and must be returned on time.
Many natural resources, such as oil, are considered unrenewable due to their limited supply.

Related Terms

  • antonym – renewable: able to be renewed or restored
