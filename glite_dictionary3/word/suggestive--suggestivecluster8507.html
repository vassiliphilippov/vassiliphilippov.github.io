suggestive

adjective
implying something improper or indecent
Pronunciation: / səgˈdʒɛstɪv / (AmE), / səgˈdʒɛstɪv / (BrE)
making you think of something improper or indecent without stating it directly.

Examples

suggestive wink
She gave him a suggestive wink across the crowded room.
The comedian's jokes were too suggestive for a family audience.
The film was criticized for its suggestive scenes.

Related Terms

  • antonym – explicit: stated clearly and in detail, leaving no room for doubt or implication, often about sexual content
