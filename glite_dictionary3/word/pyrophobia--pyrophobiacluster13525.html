pyrophobia

noun
morbid fear of fire
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪrəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌpʌɪrəˈfəʊbɪə / (BrE)
An intense or abnormal fear of fire.

Examples

severe pyrophobia after fire
After seeing the devastating forest fire, her pyrophobia became much worse.
His severe pyrophobia made it impossible for him to attend the family barbecue.
People with pyrophobia may avoid camping or lighting candles.
