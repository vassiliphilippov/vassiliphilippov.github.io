eyes

noun
person's or group's judgment or opinion
Pronunciation: / aɪz / (AmE), / aɪz / (BrE)
used to refer to a particular person's or group's opinion or judgment about someone or something, often in phrases such as 'in the eyes of'.

Examples

in the eyes of critics
In the eyes of many critics, the film was a masterpiece.
She tried to behave perfectly in her parents' eyes.
The new policy is unfair in the eyes of local residents.
