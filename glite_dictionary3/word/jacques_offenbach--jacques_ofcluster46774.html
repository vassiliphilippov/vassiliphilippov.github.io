Jacques Offenbach

proper noun
French composer of operettas and opera
Pronunciation: / ʒɑk ˈɔːfənˌbɑk / (AmE), / ʒak ˈɒfənbæk / (BrE)
(1819–1880) a French composer known for his operettas and for the opera 'The Tales of Hoffmann'.
