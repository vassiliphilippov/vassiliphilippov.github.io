Marengo

noun
site of Napoleon's 1800 victory over Austria
Pronunciation: / məˈrɛŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / məˈrɛŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
the site of a battle in 1800 where Napoleon Bonaparte's French forces defeated the Austrians, marking a significant victory during the Napoleonic Wars
