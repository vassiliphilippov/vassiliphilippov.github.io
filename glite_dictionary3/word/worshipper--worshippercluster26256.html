worshipper

noun
person who participates in religious worship
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrʃɪpər / (AmE), / ˈwɜːʃɪpə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in religious worship or shows reverence for a deity or divine being

Examples

devout worshipper at church
As a lifelong worshipper, she attends services and observes all important ceremonies.
Many worshippers gather here every Sunday to pray together.
The temple was filled with worshippers from early morning until late at night.
