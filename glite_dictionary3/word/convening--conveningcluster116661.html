convening

noun
the act of assembling or gathering
Pronunciation: / kənˈviːnɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈviːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of bringing people together for a meeting or assembly

Examples

convening a meeting
Due to the pandemic, the convening of large gatherings was postponed indefinitely.
The convening of the scientific symposium attracted experts from around the world.
The mayor called for the convening of a special council to address the crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym – assembly: a group of people gathered together in one place for a common purpose
  • antonym – dispersal: the act of people moving apart or leaving a gathering
