spar

noun
pole supporting rigging on a ship
Pronunciation: / spɑr / (AmE), / spɑː / (BrE)
a strong, thick pole made of wood or metal, used to support rigging and sails on a ship

Examples

spar on a ship
A new spar was installed to replace the one damaged in the storm.
Many ropes were attached to the main spar to control the sails.
The sailors lashed the broken spar to the deck to prevent it from rolling.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mast: a tall vertical pole on a ship that supports sails and rigging
