postulation

noun
formal submission to an authority
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstʃəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɒstjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a formal statement or request presented to an authority, especially in writing, to ask for something or state a position

Examples

postulation to the board
Her postulation to the board outlined the urgent need for new equipment.
Historical records show frequent postulation by citizens appealing to the city council.
The committee received a postulation from the local residents demanding improved road safety measures.

Related Terms

  • synonym – petition: a formal written request, usually signed by many people
