arak

noun
distilled liquor from palm sap or molasses
Pronunciation: / əˈrɑk / (AmE), / əˈræk / (BrE)
A strong distilled liquor made from the fermented sap of toddy palms or from fermented molasses, common in parts of South and Southeast Asia and the Middle East.

Examples

glass of arak
He sipped the local arak and enjoyed the warm evening breeze.
