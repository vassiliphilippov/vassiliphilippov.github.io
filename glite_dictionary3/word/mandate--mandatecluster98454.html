mandate

noun
investment instructions for a fund manager
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.deɪt / (AmE), / ˈmæn.deɪt / (BrE)
A formal set of investment objectives, constraints, and instructions given to a fund manager or advisor that guides how assets should be invested.

Examples

investment mandate
The client granted an investment mandate to the fund manager specifying risk tolerance and targets.
Our firm follows the client's mandate when choosing suitable bonds and equities for the portfolio.
The board revised its ethical investment mandate to avoid companies with poor labor practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym – brief: a set of instructions or information given to someone to carry out a task
  • synonym – investment policy: a document outlining objectives, risk tolerance, and rules for managing a portfolio
