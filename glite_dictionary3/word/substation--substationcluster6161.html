substation

noun
facility for transforming electrical voltage
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌsteɪʃən / (BrE)
a subsidiary station where electrical voltage is transformed for local distribution.

Examples

electric substation
A failure at the main substation disrupted electricity to thousands of homes.
Engineers inspected the substation after the storm caused widespread damage.
The new substation will help reduce power outages in the area.
