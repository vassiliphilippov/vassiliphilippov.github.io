clash

noun
conflict or disharmony between colors
Pronunciation: / klæʃ / (AmE), / klæʃ / (BrE)
an unpleasant combination or conflict, especially between colors that do not look good together

Examples

a clash of colors
Her outfit featured a severe clash of pink and orange that drew everyone's attention.
The artist intentionally created a clash of colors to make the painting more vibrant.
There was a striking clash between the bright red curtains and the green carpet.

Related Terms

  • antonym – harmony: a pleasing or congruent arrangement of colors
