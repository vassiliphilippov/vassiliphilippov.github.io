politico

noun
person involved in party politics
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪtɪˌkoʊ / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəʊ / (BrE)
a person who is actively involved in party politics, especially in a way that is regarded as self-serving or opportunistic

Examples

career politico
Many voters are distrustful of any politico who puts personal ambition before public service.
She became a prominent politico after leading several successful grassroots organizations.
The local politico was known for his fiery speeches and relentless campaigning.
