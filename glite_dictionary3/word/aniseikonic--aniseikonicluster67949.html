aniseikonic

adjective
relating to aniseikonia (visual defect)
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.ɪ.saɪˈkɑː.nɪk / (AmE), / ˌæn.ɪ.saɪˈkɒn.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of aniseikonia, a condition in which the images seen by the two eyes differ in size or shape.
