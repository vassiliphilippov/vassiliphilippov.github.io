convenience

noun
the quality of being useful or easy
Pronunciation: / kənˈvinjəns / (AmE), / kənˈviːniəns / (BrE)
the quality of being useful, easy, or suitable for a particular purpose

Examples

for your convenience
For your convenience, the bank is open late on Fridays.
Online shopping offers great convenience for busy people.
They added a bathroom on the first floor for added convenience.
He likes to have everything within easy convenience of his desk.
Please arrange the meeting at your earliest convenience.
The hotel was chosen for its convenience to the conference center.
