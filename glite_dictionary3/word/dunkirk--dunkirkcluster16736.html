Dunkirk

proper noun
French seaport; site of WWII evacuation
Pronunciation: / ˌdʌnˈkɜrk / (AmE), / ˌdʌnˈkɜːk / (BrE)
A seaport in northern France on the North Sea, known as the site of the evacuation of British forces in 1940 during World War II.
