albino

adjective
having albinism; lacking pigmentation
Pronunciation: / ælˈbaɪnoʊ / (AmE), / ælˈbiːnəʊ / (BrE)
having albinism; lacking normal pigmentation in the skin, hair, and eyes due to a genetic condition

Examples

albino snake
She adopted an albino snake, which had strikingly white scales.
Some plants, if albino, cannot produce enough chlorophyll to survive.
The albino rabbit was easily spotted in the green grass.

Related Terms

  • antonym – pigmented: having normal skin, hair, and eye coloration
