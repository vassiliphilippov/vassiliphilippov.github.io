Chemakuan

noun
a group of indigenous languages of Washington
Pronunciation: / tʃəˈmæk.ju.ən / (AmE), / tʃəˈmæk.ju.ən / (BrE)
a group of Native American languages formerly spoken in the Pacific Northwest region of Washington State, belonging to the Mosan phylum.
