chartaceous

adjective
having a papery texture
Pronunciation: / tʃɑrˈteɪʃəs / (AmE), / tʃɑːˈteɪʃəs / (BrE)
having a texture or consistency like paper; papery.

Examples

chartaceous leaves
Botanists often describe sepals as chartaceous when they resemble thin paper.
The chartaceous bracts separated easily from the stem.
The dried leaf had a distinctly chartaceous feel when touched.
