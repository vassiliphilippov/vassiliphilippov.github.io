yowl

noun
loud, wailing animal-like cry
Pronunciation: / jaʊl / (AmE), / jaʊl / (BrE)
a loud, wailing cry or utterance, especially one resembling the sound made by an animal

Examples

a loud yowl
Her yowl of pain could be heard throughout the neighborhood.
Suddenly, a yowl echoed through the dark alley, startling everyone.
The cat let out a piercing yowl when it saw the dog.

Related Terms

  • synonym – howl: a long, loud, doleful cry uttered by an animal or person
  • antonym – whisper: a soft or low utterance made with the breath, not the vocal cords
