Eurotiales

noun
order of fungi with scattered asci
Pronunciation: / jʊəˌroʊtiˈeɪliz /; / ˌjʊəroʊˈtaɪəliːz / (AmE), / jʊəˌrɒtɪˈɑːliːz /; / ˌjʊərəʊˈtaɪəliːz / (BrE)
