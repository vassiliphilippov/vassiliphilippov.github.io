to miss

verb
fail to reach or get to
Pronunciation: / mɪs / (AmE), / mɪs / (BrE)
to fail to hit, reach, catch, or make contact with something aimed at

Examples

to miss the bus

She threw the ball but missed her friend completely.

You should aim carefully so you don’t miss the target.
