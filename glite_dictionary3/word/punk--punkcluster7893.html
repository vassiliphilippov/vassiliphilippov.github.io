to punk

verb
prank or deceive someone
Pronunciation: / pʌŋk / (AmE), / pʌŋk / (BrE)
to trick, prank, or humiliate someone, typically by deceiving them or making them the target of a joke; informal.

Examples

to punk a friend
They planned to punk their teacher by hiding a realistic fake spider in his desk.
After the show he realized he'd been punked when the cameras revealed the hidden crew.
Stop punking your sister; she'll be really upset if you humiliate her in front of friends.
