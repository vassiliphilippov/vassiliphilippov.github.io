stance

noun
attitude or position on an issue
Pronunciation: / stæns / (AmE), / stɑːns / (BrE)
a way of thinking about something; a mental attitude or position taken on an issue

Examples

firm stance on issues
He adopted a firm stance against corruption in the company.
Her political stance on climate change is well known.
The government's stance on education has changed in recent years.
