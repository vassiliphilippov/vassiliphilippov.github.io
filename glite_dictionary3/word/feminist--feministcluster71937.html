feminist

noun
supporter of feminism
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛmənɪst / (AmE), / ˈfɛmɪnɪst / (BrE)
A person who supports feminism and advocates for equal rights for women.

Examples

feminist activist
As a proud feminist, she has long advocated for equal pay in the workplace.
Many young people identify as feminists and support gender equality initiatives.
The author’s latest book explores what it means to be a feminist today.

Related Terms

  • synonym – feminism: the advocacy of women's rights on the ground of the equality of the sexes
  • antonym – misogynist: a person who dislikes, despises, or is strongly prejudiced against women
