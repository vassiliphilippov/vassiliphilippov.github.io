rainbow

noun
arc of colors in the sky after rain
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪnˌboʊ / (AmE), / ˈreɪnbəʊ / (BrE)
An arc of different colors visible in the sky, caused by the refraction and dispersion of sunlight by rain.

Examples

rainbow in the sky
After the storm, a beautiful rainbow appeared across the sky.
Children ran outside to admire the bright rainbow after the rain stopped.
The legend says that there is a pot of gold at the end of every rainbow.
