mercurial

adjective
with qualities of the god Mercury
Pronunciation: / mɚˈkjʊriəl / (AmE), / məˈkjʊəriəl / (BrE)
Having characteristics such as eloquence, shrewdness, swiftness, or thievishness attributed to the god Mercury.
