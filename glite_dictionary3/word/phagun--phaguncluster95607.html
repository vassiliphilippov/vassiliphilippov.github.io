Phagun

noun
twelfth month of the Hindu calendar
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːɡʊn / (AmE), / ˈfɑːɡʊn / (BrE)
The twelfth month of the Hindu calendar, typically falling in February or March of the Gregorian calendar.

Examples

Phagun festival season
According to the Hindu calendar, spring begins in the month of Phagun.
Farmers look forward to the end of Phagun for the start of the new harvest.
The festival of Holi is usually celebrated during Phagun.
