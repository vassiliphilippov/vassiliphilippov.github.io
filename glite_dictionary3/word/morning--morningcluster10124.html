morning

noun
greeting or farewell used in the morning
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
A conventional expression of greeting or farewell used to wish someone a good morning.

Examples

greet with 'morning'
He waved and called out 'morning' to his neighbor on his way to work.
People greeted each other with a cheerful 'morning' at the breakfast table.
She smiled and said, "morning!" as she walked into the office.
