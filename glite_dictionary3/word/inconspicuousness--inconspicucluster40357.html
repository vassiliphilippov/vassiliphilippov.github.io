inconspicuousness

noun
the state of not being easily seen
Pronunciation: / ɪn.kənˈspɪk.ju.əs.nəs / (AmE), / ɪn.kənˈspɪk.ju.ə.snəs / (BrE)
the quality of not attracting attention or being easily noticed

Examples

inconspicuousness in public
His skill lies in the inconspicuousness with which he moves through the crowd.
She valued the inconspicuousness of their meeting place, as it attracted no attention.
The animal's inconspicuousness in its natural habitat helps it avoid predators.
