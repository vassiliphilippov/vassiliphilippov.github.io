flat

noun
railway freight car with no sides or roof
Pronunciation: / flæt / (AmE), / flæt / (BrE)
a freight car with a flat floor and no permanent sides or roof, used for transporting heavy or awkward loads

Examples

flat car
After the cargo was secured, the flat was attached to the rest of the train.
Oversized machinery is often shipped on a flat to accommodate its dimensions.
The workers loaded the pipes onto the flat for transport to the construction site.
