blistering

noun
formation of blisters
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪstərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblɪstərɪŋ / (BrE)
The process of forming blisters, especially in or beneath the skin.

Examples

skin blistering
Prolonged exposure to the chemical resulted in severe blistering on the technician's hands.
Some plant diseases cause blistering of the leaves, which can harm crop yields.
The sunburn was so bad that blistering occurred on his shoulders.
