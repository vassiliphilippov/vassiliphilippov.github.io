giveaway

noun
unintentional disclosure or revealing clue
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪvəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪvəˌweɪ / (BrE)
Something that inadvertently reveals information that was meant to be kept secret.

Examples

obvious giveaway sign
Her nervous laughter was a giveaway that she was hiding something.
The muddy footprints were the giveaway that someone had sneaked into the house.
The poker player's glance at his chips was a clear giveaway.

  • antonym – secret: something kept hidden or unknown from others
