herbivorous

adjective
eating only plants
Pronunciation: / hɜrˈbɪvərəs / (AmE), / hɜːˈbɪvərəs / (BrE)
(of an animal) feeding only on plants; not eating meat.

Examples

herbivorous animal
Cows are classic examples of herbivorous animals found on farms worldwide.
Many dinosaurs were herbivorous, surviving on a diet of leaves and plants.
The zoo separates herbivorous species from carnivorous ones for safety reasons.

Related Terms

  • antonym – carnivorous: feeding on other animals
  • antonym – omnivorous: feeding on both plants and animals
