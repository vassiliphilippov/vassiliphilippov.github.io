dupe

noun
person who is tricked or deceived
Pronunciation: / duːp / (AmE), / djuːp / (BrE)
a person who is easily deceived or tricked, especially into doing something foolish or unlawful

Examples

an easy dupe
Con artists often search for an easy dupe in crowded tourist spots.
Don't be a dupe; always check the facts before believing wild rumors.
He became an unwitting dupe in their elaborate scam.

Related Terms

  • antonym – swindler: person who cheats or tricks others
  • synonym – victim: person harmed or tricked by someone
