garlic

noun
pungent bulb used as seasoning
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrlɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːlɪk / (BrE)
a plant of the onion family with a strong smell and taste, whose bulb is used in cooking and sometimes medicine
