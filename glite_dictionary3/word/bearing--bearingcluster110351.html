bearing

noun
relevance or influence; connection
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɛərɪŋ / (BrE)
the relevance or influence that something has on a situation or decision

Examples

bearing on a decision
His comments had no bearing on the outcome of the meeting.
Personal feelings should not have any bearing on professional decisions.
The judge decided that new evidence might have a significant bearing on the case.

Related Terms

  • antonym – irrelevance: the quality of not being related or connected to something
  • synonym – relevance: the quality or state of being closely connected or appropriate
