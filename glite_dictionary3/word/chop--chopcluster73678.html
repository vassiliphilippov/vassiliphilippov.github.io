chop

noun
a small rib-in piece of meat
Pronunciation: / tʃɑp / (AmE), / tʃɒp / (BrE)
a small piece of meat, usually including part of a rib, cut from lamb, pork, or other meat animals for cooking or serving

Examples

lamb chop dinner
Each chop was seasoned with herbs before being placed on the barbecue.
He ordered a grilled lamb chop with a side of vegetables for dinner.
The butcher recommended the pork chops for a quick and tasty meal.
