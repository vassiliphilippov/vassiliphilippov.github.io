non-negotiable

adjective
not able to be bought or sold
Pronunciation: / nɑːn nəˈɡoʊʃəbl̩ / (AmE), / nɒn nɪˈɡəʊʃəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of a document, asset, or right) not able to be bought, sold, transferred, or exchanged for money or other value.

Examples

non-negotiable ticket
Certain tickets are marked as non-negotiable, so resale is not permitted.
The artist retained the copyright as a non-negotiable right.
The gift certificate is non-negotiable and cannot be exchanged for cash.

Related Terms

  • antonym – negotiable: able to be bought, sold, or transferred
