scarf

noun
piece of fabric worn around neck/head
Pronunciation: / skɑːrf / (AmE), / skɑːf / (BrE)
A long piece of fabric worn around the head, neck, or shoulders for warmth, protection, or decoration.

Examples

wear a scarf
At the party, his silk scarf added a dash of elegance to his outfit.
During winter, children often lose their scarves while playing outside.
She wrapped a red scarf around her neck to keep warm.
