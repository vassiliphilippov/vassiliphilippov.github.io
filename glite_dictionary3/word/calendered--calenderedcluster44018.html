calendered

adjective
smoothed by rolling, especially paper or fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈkæləndɚd / (AmE), / ˈkæləndəd / (BrE)
(of paper, fabric, or leather) having a smooth and shiny surface produced by pressing between rollers.

Examples

calendered paper sheets
Some dress fabrics are calendered to enhance their sheen and smoothness.
The calendered leather used for the cover has a distinctive glossy finish.
The calendered paper gave the magazine a sleek, professional look.
