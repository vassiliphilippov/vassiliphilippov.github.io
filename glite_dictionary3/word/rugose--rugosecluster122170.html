rugose

adjective
wrinkled or ridged (esp. of leaves)
Pronunciation: / ruˈɡoʊs / (AmE), / ruːˈɡəʊs / (BrE)
(of leaves or surfaces) having a wrinkled or ridged texture.

Examples

rugose leaf surface
Botanists often describe a plant as rugose when its foliage appears deeply wrinkled.
Some succulents have rugose leaves that help conserve water.
The rugose surface of the leaf made it easy to identify the species.

Related Terms
