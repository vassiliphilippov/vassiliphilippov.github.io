height

noun
highest stage or level
Pronunciation: / haɪt / (AmE), / haɪt / (BrE)
the point or period at which something is at its maximum level, strength, or development

Examples

height of power
During the height of the storm, the winds reached sixty miles per hour.
She rose to the height of her profession through years of hard work.
The orchestra was at the height of its popularity in the early 1990s.

  • synonym – summit: the highest attainable level of something
