scuff

noun
act of scraping or dragging the feet
Pronunciation: / skʌf / (AmE), / skʌf / (BrE)
an act of scraping, dragging, or rubbing the feet, especially along the ground, often resulting in a mark or abrupt noise

Examples

loud scuff sound
He heard a sudden scuff behind him and turned around quickly.
The hallway was quiet except for the scuff of her shoes on the tiled floor.
Years of children's scuffs had left marks all along the gymnasium floor.
