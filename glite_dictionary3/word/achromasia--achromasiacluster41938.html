achromasia

noun
abnormal lack of color in the skin
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ.kroʊˈmeɪ.zi.ə / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.krəʊˈmeɪ.zi.ə / (BrE)
a condition marked by an abnormal or unnatural lack of color in the skin, often resulting from bruising, illness, or emotional distress
