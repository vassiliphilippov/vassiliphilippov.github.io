second

noun
official attendant in a duel or boxing match
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛk.ənd / (AmE), / ˈsɛk.ənd / (BrE)
an official person who assists and attends to a contestant in a duel or boxing match, often handling arrangements and offering support during the event

Examples

duelist's second assists
Each duelist was accompanied by a trusted second to oversee the proceedings.
He acted as a second during the heated match, providing water and advice to the boxer.
In boxing, the second is responsible for tending to the fighter between rounds.
