godfather

noun
male baptismal sponsor
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑdˌfɑðɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌfɑːðə / (BrE)
a man who sponsors a child at baptism and undertakes to help guide their religious upbringing

Examples

godfather at baptism
At the christening, her godfather made a heartfelt promise to support her throughout her life.
Michael was proud to be asked to serve as godfather to his niece.
They invited their oldest friend to be the baby's godfather.
