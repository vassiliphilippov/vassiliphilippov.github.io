quintet

noun
musical composition for five performers
Pronunciation: / kwɪnˈtɛt / (AmE), / kwɪnˈtɛt / (BrE)
A musical composition written for five performers or instruments.

Examples

a quintet by Mozart
Our ensemble will perform a new quintet at the festival next month.
She composed a jazz quintet for saxophone, trumpet, piano, bass, and drums.
The string quintet by Mozart is one of his most celebrated chamber works.
