heartbroken

adjective
overwhelmed with sorrow or grief
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrtˌbroʊkən / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtˌbrəʊkən / (BrE)
Overwhelmed with great sorrow or grief, especially due to loss or disappointment.

Examples

feel heartbroken after loss

He felt heartbroken when his team lost the championship game.
She was heartbroken after hearing the news of her friend's passing.
