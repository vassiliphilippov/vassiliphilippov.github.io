alert

noun
automatic warning signal
Pronunciation: / əˈlɝt / (AmE), / əˈlɜːt / (BrE)
a warning or signal, often automatic, that alerts people to danger or a problem

Examples

emergency alert
A weather alert was issued for severe thunderstorms in the area.

The fire alert sounded throughout the building, prompting everyone to evacuate.
A security alert was triggered when the door was forced open.
Sign up for our text alerts to stay informed about emergencies.
The city issued an alert about severe weather approaching the area.
