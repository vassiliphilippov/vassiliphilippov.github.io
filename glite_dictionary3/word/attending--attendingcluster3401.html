attending

adjective
giving practical help or service
Pronunciation: / əˈtɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈtɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
actively giving practical help or services, especially as part of one's professional duties

Examples

attending physician
At the conference, the attending physician was responsible for assessing all the new arrivals.
Only attending staff members are permitted in the operating theatre during surgery.
The attending nurse checked on her patient every hour throughout the night.
