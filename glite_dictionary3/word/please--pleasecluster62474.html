to please

verb
to make someone happy or satisfied
Pronunciation: / pliz /; / pliːz / (AmE), / pliːz / (BrE)
to make someone happy or satisfied; to give pleasure to someone

Examples

to please a friend
It pleases me to see you so happy.
The new design will please most of our customers.
We hope these changes please everyone involved.
Her thoughtful gift truly pleased him on his birthday.
It is difficult to please everyone in a large group.
The chef always tries to please his customers by preparing their favorite dishes.

  • synonym – delight: to give great pleasure or satisfaction
  • antonym – displease: to cause someone to feel unhappy or annoyed
