ideography

noun
system using ideograms in writing
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪdiˈɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˌaɪdiˈɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the use of ideograms in writing; a system of writing in which ideas are represented by symbols rather than words or sounds
