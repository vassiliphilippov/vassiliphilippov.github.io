indiscriminate

adjective
without careful selection or distinction
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnət / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnət / (BrE)
done without careful selection, judgment, or consideration of differences

Examples
