ding

noun
small dent or impression
Pronunciation: / dɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪŋ / (BrE)
A small dent or impression in a surface caused by a blow or impact.

Examples

ding in the car door
She noticed a small ding on her phone after dropping it.
The suitcase had several dings from rough handling during the trip.
There was a ding in the car door after the hailstorm.
