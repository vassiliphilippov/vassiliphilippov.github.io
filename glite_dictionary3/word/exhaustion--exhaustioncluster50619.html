exhaustion

noun
extreme tiredness or fatigue
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɔstʃən /; / ɪɡˈzɑstʃən / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːs.tʃən / (BrE)
a state of extreme physical or mental tiredness; complete fatigue
