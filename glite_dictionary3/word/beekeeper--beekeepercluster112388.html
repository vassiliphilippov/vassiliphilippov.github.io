beekeeper

noun
person who keeps bees for honey
Pronunciation: / ˈbiˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈbiːˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person who owns or manages hives of bees, especially for producing honey

Examples

beekeeper collects honey
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a beekeeper and harvesting her own honey.
The beekeeper carefully inspected the hives to ensure the bees were healthy.
The local beekeeper gave a talk at the school about pollination and the importance of bees.

Related Terms

  • synonym – apiculturist: a person who studies or keeps bees, especially for commercial purposes
