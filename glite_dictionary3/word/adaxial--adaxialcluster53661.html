adaxial

adjective
facing toward the axis (botany)
Pronunciation: / ædˈæksiəl / (AmE), / adˈaksɪəl / (BrE)
(of a plant organ or part) facing toward or nearest to the axis of the organism.

Examples

adaxial leaf surface
Researchers measured the thickness of the adaxial and abaxial epidermis in several plant species.
Stomata are typically less numerous on the adaxial surface compared to the lower surface of leaves.
The adaxial surface of the leaf usually receives more sunlight than the underside.
