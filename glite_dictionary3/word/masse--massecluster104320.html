masse

noun
a curved cue shot in billiards or pool
Pronunciation: / mæˈseɪ / (AmE), / mæˈseɪ / (BrE)
a shot in billiards or pool in which the cue ball is struck with the cue held almost vertically, causing it to curve or spin sharply around another ball
