untravelled

adjective
not having traveled much
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtrævəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈtrævəld / (BrE)
not having traveled much, especially to foreign lands; lacking experience gained by travel

Examples

untravelled person
His opinions were shaped by an untravelled life in a small, familiar community.
She remained largely untravelled, never venturing outside her hometown.
The remote village lay on an untravelled road, far from the main highways.

Related Terms

  • antonym – well-travelled: having traveled to many places, especially to foreign lands
