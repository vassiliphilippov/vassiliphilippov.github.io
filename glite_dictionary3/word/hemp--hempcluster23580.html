hemp

noun
fiber from the hemp plant
Pronunciation: / hɛmp / (AmE), / hɛmp / (BrE)
A strong, coarse fiber obtained from the stems of the hemp plant, used to make rope, fabric, and other products.

Examples

hemp rope
Clothing made of hemp is durable and eco-friendly.
Farmers grow hemp for use in textiles and construction materials.
The ropes were made from strong hemp fibers.
