versatile

adjective
changeable; inconstant
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrsətl / (AmE), / ˈvɜːsətaɪl / (BrE)
(of a person, mood, or thing) liable to change quickly; changeable or inconstant.

Examples

versatile mood swings
Her versatile moods made it difficult to predict how she would react each day.
His versatile temperament made lasting relationships a challenge.
The weather in this region is so versatile that you need to be prepared for anything.
