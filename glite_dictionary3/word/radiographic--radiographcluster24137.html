radiographic

adjective
relating to or produced by radiography
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdiəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəˈɡrafɪk / (BrE)
relating to or produced by radiography, especially using X-rays to view the internal structure of something

Examples

Routine dental check-ups often include radiographic examinations to detect cavities.
