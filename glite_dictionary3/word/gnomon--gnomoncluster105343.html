gnomon

noun
part of a sundial showing the time
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊ.mɑn / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ.mɒn / (BrE)
the part of a sundial that casts a shadow, indicating the time of day.

Examples

gnomon on a sundial
During restoration, the broken gnomon was replaced to ensure the sundial functioned again.
The gnomon of the ancient sundial is carefully aligned with the Earth's axis.
When the sun shone, the gnomon cast a clear shadow onto the marble face.
