increasing

adjective
growing in tempo or volume (music)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkriːsɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈkriːsɪŋ / (BrE)
(music) Becoming greater in tempo and/or volume.

Examples

increasing tempo
She listened intently, noticing the increasing dynamics in the final section of the symphony.
The conductor signaled for the orchestra to play with increasing tempo until the end of the movement.
The music grew more intense, increasing both in speed and volume as the climax approached.
