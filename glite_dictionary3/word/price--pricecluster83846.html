price

noun
cost or sacrifice to obtain something
Pronunciation: / praɪs / (AmE), / praɪs / (BrE)
the value, cost, or sacrifice that must be given, done, or endured to obtain something

Examples

price of success
He was willing to pay any price for true happiness, even if it meant sacrifice.
She realized the price of her ambition was spending less time with family.
The price of freedom is eternal vigilance, as the saying goes.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cost: the amount paid or required for something; what is given up to achieve something
  • antonym – reward: something given in recognition of effort or achievement
