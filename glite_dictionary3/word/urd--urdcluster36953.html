Urd

proper noun
Norse goddess of fate, represents the past
Pronunciation: / ʊrd / (AmE), / ʊəd / (BrE)
In Norse mythology, a goddess of fate who personifies the past, and one of the three Norns.

Examples

Urd the Norn
According to the myth, Urd stands by the well under Yggdrasil, shaping destiny.
Some scholars believe that Urd represents not only the past but also the unchangeable nature of fate.
The tales of Urd, along with her sisters, have influenced many modern fantasy stories.
