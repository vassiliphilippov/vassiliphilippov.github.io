to pattern

verb
form or arrange in a pattern
Pronunciation: / ˈpæt.ɚn /; / ˈpætərn / (AmE), / ˈpæt.ən /; / ˈpætən / (BrE)
to arrange, design, or model something after a particular pattern or example

Examples

to pattern behavior
