duckpin

noun
short, squat bowling pin
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌkˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʌkˌpɪn / (BrE)
a type of small, squat bowling pin used in duckpin bowling, shorter and wider than a standard tenpin

Examples

duckpin bowling game
The alley had both tenpins and duckpins for different styles of bowling.
The bowler knocked over every duckpin with a single throw.
Unlike traditional bowling, duckpins are smaller and harder to knock down.
