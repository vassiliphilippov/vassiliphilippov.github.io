Arbutus unedo

noun
European evergreen shrub/tree with edible red berries
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈbjuːtəs juːˈniːdoʊ / (AmE), / ɑːˈbjuːtəs juːˈniːdəʊ / (BrE)
A small evergreen European shrub or tree, also known as the strawberry tree, bearing many-seeded scarlet berries that are edible but bland; native to Ireland, southern Europe, and Asia Minor.
