covering

noun
thing that covers, protects, or conceals
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌvərɪŋ /; / ˈkʌv.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌvərɪŋ /; / ˈkʌv.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
something that lies over or surrounds something else, especially for protection, shelter, or concealment

Examples
