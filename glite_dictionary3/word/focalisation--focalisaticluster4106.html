focalisation

noun
the act of bringing into focus
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊkələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfəʊkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of bringing something into focus or making something concentrated on a particular point

Examples

focalisation of viewpoint
In narrative theory, focalisation determines through whose perspective the story is presented.
The focalisation of the camera lens made the image much clearer.
The experiment required the focalisation of the laser beam on a very small target.
