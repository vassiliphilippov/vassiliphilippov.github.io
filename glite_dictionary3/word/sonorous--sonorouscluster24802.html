sonorous

adjective
full, deep, and loud (of sound)
Pronunciation: / səˈnɔrəs / (AmE), / səˈnɔːrəs / (BrE)
(of a sound) having a full, deep, and loud quality.

Examples

sonorous voice
She admired the sonorous quality of his deep laughter.
The sonorous voice of the announcer filled the auditorium.
The bells produced a sonorous and majestic tone that echoed across the valley.
