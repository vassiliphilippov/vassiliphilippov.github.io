Anacardium occidentale

noun
tropical evergreen tree; the cashew tree
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.əˈkɑːr.di.əm ˌɑk.sɪˈden.t̬əl / (AmE), / ˌæn.əˈkɑː.dɪ.əm ˌɒk.sɪˈdɛn.teɪl / (BrE)
