affixed

adjective
firmly attached or fastened
Pronunciation: / əˈfɪkst / (AmE), / əˈfɪkst / (BrE)
firmly attached to something; fastened or fixed in place.

Examples

affixed to the envelope
An official seal was affixed to the envelope to ensure its authenticity.
Each package had a barcode label affixed to the top.
The certificate was affixed to the wall beside the entrance.
