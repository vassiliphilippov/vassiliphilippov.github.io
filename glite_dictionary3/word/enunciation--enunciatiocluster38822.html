enunciation

noun
clear and distinct speech articulation
Pronunciation: / ɪˌnʌn.siˈeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌnʌn.siˈeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the clear and distinct articulation of speech, especially in a way that makes it easy for an audience to understand

Examples

clear enunciation of words
Clear enunciation is essential for good public speaking.
His enunciation was so precise that everyone in the back row could hear every word.
She worked with a coach to improve her enunciation before the debate.
