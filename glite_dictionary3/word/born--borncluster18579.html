born

adjective
naturally talented from birth
Pronunciation: / bɔrn / (AmE), / bɔːn / (BrE)
Having a natural ability or talent for something from birth.

Examples

born leader
He proved to be a born leader, guiding the team effortlessly.
She is a born storyteller, captivating everyone with her vivid tales.
Some believe that true artists are born, not made.
