neuroepithelium

noun
sensory epithelium in sense organs
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˌɛpəˈθiliəm / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˌɛpɪˈθiːlɪəm / (BrE)
A type of epithelium found in special sense organs that contains sensory nerve endings.
