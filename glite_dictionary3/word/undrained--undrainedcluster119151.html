undrained

adjective
not drained of liquid
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdreɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdreɪnd / (BrE)
not having had water or other liquid removed; not drained.

Examples

undrained soil
The engineer examined the risks associated with building on undrained clay soils.
The field remained undrained after days of heavy rain, making it difficult to use.
Wetlands should be left undrained to support the diverse ecosystem.
