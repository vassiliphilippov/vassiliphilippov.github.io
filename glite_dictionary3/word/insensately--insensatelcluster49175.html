insensately

adverb
without sensation or rational feeling
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɛnˌseɪtli / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛnsətli / (BrE)
In a way that lacks physical sensation, consciousness, or rational feeling; without reason or sensitivity.

Examples

to act insensately reckless
He drove insensately, ignoring all caution and rules.
She lashed out insensately, unaware of the pain she caused.
The mobs acted insensately, destroying everything in their path.

Related Terms

  • antonym – sensibly: in a sensible or rational manner
