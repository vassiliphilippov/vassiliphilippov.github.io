emoticon

noun
keyboard symbol representing an emotion
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmoʊtɪˌkɑn / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊtɪˌkɒn / (BrE)
A representation of a facial expression, such as a smile or frown, created using keyboard characters in electronic communication.

Examples

emoticon smiley face
I used a emoticon at the end of my message to show I was joking.
Many people include a smiley emoticon like :-) in their emails.
Some readers misinterpreted his sarcasm because he forgot to add a emoticon.

Related Terms

  • synonym – emoji: a digital image or icon used to express emotion or idea in electronic messages
