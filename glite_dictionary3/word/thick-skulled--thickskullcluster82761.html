thick-skulled

adjective
unintelligent; slow to understand
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪk ˌskʌld / (AmE), / ˈθɪk ˌskʌld / (BrE)
(informal) Unintelligent; slow to understand or lacking intelligence.

Examples

thick-skulled student
Despite her thick-skulled approach to learning, she eventually understood the concept.
He's so thick-skulled that he didn't notice the joke was on him.
Only a thick-skulled person would ignore such clear instructions.
