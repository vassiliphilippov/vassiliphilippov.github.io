jock

noun
athlete, especially in school or college
Pronunciation: / dʒɑk / (AmE), / dʒɒk / (BrE)
an athlete, especially one who is enthusiastic about sports or regularly competes in sports, often used informally for male athletes in schools or colleges

Examples

school jock
Although he's known as a jock, he excels in his studies too.
He was a typical jock in high school, playing football and basketball every season.
The jocks and the chess club rarely mixed at our school.
