strawman

noun
person used as a front for questionable activities
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɔˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈstrɔːˌman / (BrE)
a person who is used as a front or cover for some questionable or illicit activity, often to conceal the identity of those actually responsible

Examples

strawman owner
He acted as a strawman for the real owners of the company.
Investigators revealed that the buyer was merely a strawman for a secretive group.
The politician used a strawman to hide his financial interests.

Related Terms

  • synonym – front man: a person who serves as a cover for others in business or crime
