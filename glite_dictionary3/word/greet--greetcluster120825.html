to greet

verb
to be first perceived on arriving
Pronunciation: / ɡrit / (AmE), / ɡriːt / (BrE)
(of a thing or sensation) to be the first thing noticed or experienced on arriving somewhere.

Examples

a view greeted us
A stunning view greeted them at the top of the mountain.
A wave of cold air greeted us as we stepped into the room.
The smell of fresh bread greeted her when she opened the door.

Related Terms

  • synonym – welcome: to receive in a friendly or glad manner
