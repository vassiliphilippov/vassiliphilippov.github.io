treasury

noun
digital fund pool for a crypto project
Pronunciation: / c8tr5b9259ri / (AmE), / c8tr5b9259ri / (BrE)
(in blockchain and cryptocurrency contexts) a pool of digital funds or tokens held by a project, organization, or DAO and used to finance development, grants, operations, or community proposals; often controlled by smart contracts, multisignature wallets, or governance voting.

Examples

crypto treasury fund
The DAO transferred tokens from its treasury to fund the developer grant program this month.
Community members voted to allocate half of the treasury toward ecosystem incentives and marketing campaigns.
After the exploit, the project's multisig froze the treasury while auditors assessed recovery options.
