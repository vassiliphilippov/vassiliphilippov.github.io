supposed

adjective
expected or required to do
Pronunciation: / səˈpoʊzd / (AmE), / səˈpəʊzd / (BrE)
expected or required to do something, according to rules, instructions, or agreement; under orders.

Examples

supposed to follow rules
I'm supposed to call my boss before noon.
The students are supposed to hand in their assignments by Friday.
You're not supposed to park here after 6 p.m.

Related Terms

  • antonym – allowed: permitted, not forbidden
  • synonym – obliged: required by law, duty, or circumstance
