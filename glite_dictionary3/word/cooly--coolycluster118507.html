cooly

noun
offensive term for Asian laborer (slur)
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːli / (AmE), / ˈkuːli / (BrE)
(offensive) An ethnic slur for an unskilled Asian laborer, especially of Chinese or Indian descent, historically used in a derogatory manner.

Examples

a cooly laborer
Historical records often refer to Asian immigrant workers as coolies during the 19th century.
The term cooly is now widely recognized as an offensive slur and should not be used.
Using the word cooly in conversation is considered highly inappropriate and disrespectful.
