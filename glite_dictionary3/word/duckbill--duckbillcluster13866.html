duckbill

adjective
having a duck-like beak or snout
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌkˌbɪl / (AmE), / ˈdʌkˌbɪl / (BrE)
(of an animal) having a beak or snout shaped like that of a duck.

Examples

duckbill dinosaur
Scientists discovered a new species of duckbill dinosaur in North America.
The duckbill platypus uses its sensitive beak to detect prey underwater.
The mask's front is shaped like a duckbill to allow easier breathing.
