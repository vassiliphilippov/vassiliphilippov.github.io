spectator

noun
a person watching an event
Pronunciation: / spɛkˈteɪtɚ / (AmE), / spɛkˈteɪtə / (BrE)
a person who watches an event, show, or activity, usually as part of an audience but not taking part

Examples

spectator at a match
As a spectator, she enjoyed the concert without feeling nervous about performing.
Hundreds of spectators watched the fireworks display from the park.
The crowd of spectators cheered as the runners crossed the finish line.

Related Terms

  • synonym – audience: a group of people assembled to watch or listen to something
  • antonym – participant: a person who takes part in something
