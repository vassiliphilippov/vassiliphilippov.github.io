room

noun
enclosed space in a building
Pronunciation: / ruːm / (AmE), / ruːm / (BrE)
an area within a building that is enclosed by walls, a floor, and a ceiling, and forms part of a house or other structure

Examples

a room in a house
Her room was filled with sunlight each morning.
The hotel offers a room overlooking the ocean.
We painted every room in the house last summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chamber: a large room, especially a bedroom or a room used for a particular purpose
  • antonym – hallway: a corridor or passage in a building
