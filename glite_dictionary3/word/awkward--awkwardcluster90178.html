awkward

adjective
causing inconvenience or difficulty
Pronunciation: / ˈɔk.wɚd /; / ˈɑːkwərd / (AmE), / ˈɔː.kwəd /; / ˈɔːkwəd /; / ˈɔːk.wəd / (BrE)
causing difficulty, inconvenience, or being hard to deal with (including things that are physically difficult to handle or situations that are uncomfortable or embarrassing)
