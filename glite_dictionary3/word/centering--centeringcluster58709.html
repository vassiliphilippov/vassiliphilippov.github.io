centering

noun
putting football in play by snapping
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɛntərɪŋ / (BrE)
In American football, the act of putting the ball into play by snapping it between the center's legs to a back, usually the quarterback.

Examples

football centering snap
A bad centering can disrupt the entire offensive play in football.
He was penalized for an illegal centering during the first quarter.
The team's new center practiced centering every afternoon to ensure accurate snaps.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hiking: another term for centering, passing the football to a back
  • synonym – snap: the act of quickly passing the football from the center to the quarterback
