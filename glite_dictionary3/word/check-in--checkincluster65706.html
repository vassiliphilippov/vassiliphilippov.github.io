check-in

noun
registration of arrival (e.g., at a hotel or airport)
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛkˌɪn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛk.ɪn / (BrE)
the act of registering one’s arrival at a hotel, airport, or event by reporting your presence

Examples

check-in at the hotel
Online check-in is now available for all flights departing tomorrow.
Please have your passport ready at check-in.
There was a long line at the hotel check-in desk when we arrived.

Related Terms

  • antonym – check-out: the act of leaving and formally paying or announcing departure from a hotel, etc.
