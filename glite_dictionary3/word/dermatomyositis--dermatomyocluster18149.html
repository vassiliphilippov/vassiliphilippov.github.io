dermatomyositis

noun
inflammatory disease with muscle weakness and skin rash
Pronunciation: / ˌdɜːrməˌtoʊmaɪoʊˈsaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌdɜːmətəʊmaɪəˈsaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A rare inflammatory disease characterized by muscle weakness and skin rash, often affecting the neck, limbs, and upper body.

Examples

dermatomyositis with skin rash
Children and adults can both develop dermatomyositis, although the symptoms may vary depending on age.
The patient was diagnosed with dermatomyositis after presenting with severe muscle weakness and a reddish rash on her face.
Treatments for dermatomyositis often include corticosteroids and other immunosuppressive drugs.
