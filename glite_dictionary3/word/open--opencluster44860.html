open

adjective
having gaps or openings in texture
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
(of a texture or weave) having many small openings or spaces between the threads or fibers.

Examples

open weave
An open mesh net allows water to drain quickly.
The sweater has an open weave that lets the air circulate easily.
You can see through the curtain's open texture.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dense: (of a fabric or texture) packed closely together without gaps
