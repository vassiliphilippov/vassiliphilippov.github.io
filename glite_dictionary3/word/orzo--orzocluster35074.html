orzo

noun
a rice-shaped pasta
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrzoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɔːtsəʊ / (BrE)
A type of pasta shaped like grains of barley, commonly used in soups and salads, especially in Mediterranean cuisine.

Examples

orzo pasta salad
