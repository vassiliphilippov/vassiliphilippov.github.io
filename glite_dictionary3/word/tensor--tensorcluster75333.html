tensor

noun
mathematical object generalizing vectors and matrices
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛn.sɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.sə / (BrE)
a mathematical object that generalizes the concepts of scalars, vectors, and matrices, and is used to represent relationships between sets of geometric or physical quantities

Examples

stress tensor calculation
In physics, the stress tensor describes the internal forces within a solid body.
Machine learning models often rely on manipulating large data stored as tensors.
The curvature of spacetime in general relativity is represented by a complex mathematical tensor.
