lookdown

noun
a silvery, deep-bodied marine fish
Pronunciation: / ˈlʊkˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈlʊkˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a silvery, deep-bodied marine fish (Selene vomer) of the western Atlantic Ocean, with a steeply sloped forehead and eyes set high on its head.

Examples

a lookdown fish
