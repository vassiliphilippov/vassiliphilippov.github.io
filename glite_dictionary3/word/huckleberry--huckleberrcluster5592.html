huckleberry

noun
North American berry-bearing shrub
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌkəlˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈhʌk.əlˌbɛr.i / (BrE)
A North American shrub of the genus Gaylussacia that bears small, dark, edible berries similar to blueberries.

Examples

huckleberry bush
Huckleberry bushes thrive best in acidic soil and cooler climates.
The pie was made with fresh huckleberries gathered from the mountain trails.
We picked a bucket of huckleberries while hiking through the forest.
