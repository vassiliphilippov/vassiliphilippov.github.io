organizer

noun
person who recruits union members
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡənaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who arranges, coordinates, or recruits people for an event, group, or cause

Examples

organizer of an event
As a skilled organizer, she persuaded many employees to join the union.
Management was concerned about the influence of the organizer among the staff.
The union organizer visited the factory to talk to the workers about membership.
As the organizer of the concert, she managed every detail personally.
He was appointed organizer for the annual charity run.
The organizer made sure all the volunteers were assigned to their tasks.

Related Terms

  • synonym – coordinator: person who organizes people or groups so that they work together efficiently
  • antonym – disorganizer: a person who causes confusion or disorder
  • synonym – recruiter: person who enlists people for a group or activity
