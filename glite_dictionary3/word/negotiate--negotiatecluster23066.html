to negotiate

verb
succeed in passing through or over something
Pronunciation: / nɪˈɡoʊʃiˌeɪt /; / nəˈɡoʊʃiˌeɪt /; / nəˈɡoʊʃieɪt / (AmE), / nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪt / (BrE)
