nightfall

noun
the time when night begins
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtˌfɔl / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtˌfɔːl / (BrE)
the time in the evening when it becomes dark and night begins, just after sunset

Examples

at nightfall darkness
By nightfall, the temperature had dropped and the city lights were twinkling.
They set up their tents quickly as nightfall approached in the mountains.
We should try to finish the hike before nightfall so we don't get lost in the dark.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dusk: the time just before it becomes completely dark in the evening
  • degree lower – sunset: the time in the evening when the sun goes below the horizon
  • synonym – twilight: the period of soft light from the sky when the sun is below the horizon, before it gets completely dark
