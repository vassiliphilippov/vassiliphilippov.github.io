to colonise

verb
establish a colony or settle in a new area
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.ləˌnaɪz /; / ˈkɑː.lə.naɪz / (AmE), / ˈkɒl.ə.naɪz / (BrE)
to take control of an area and settle there, often by a foreign power, usually displacing or ruling the local people
