translatable

adjective
able to be transformed into another form
Pronunciation: / trænzˈleɪtəbl̩ / (AmE), / trænsˈleɪtəbl̩ / (BrE)
Able to be changed or transformed into another form or substance, especially in a way that resembles an alchemical or substantial conversion.

Examples

translatable into results
His vision was translatable into tangible results through hard work and collaboration.
Some chemical elements are not translatable into other substances by ordinary means.
The plan is only useful if the ideas are translatable into practical solutions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – convertible: able to be changed into a different form or use
  • antonym – immutable: unable to be changed; unchangeable
