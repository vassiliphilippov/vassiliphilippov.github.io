alert

noun
state of heightened readiness
Pronunciation: / əˈlɝt / (AmE), / əˈlɜːt / (BrE)
a state of heightened readiness or watchfulness, especially in preparation for possible danger or action

Examples

on high alert
Flood warnings kept the entire region on high alert for several days.
Following the earthquake, the city was put on alert for potential aftershocks.
The army remained on alert throughout the night in case of attack.

Related Terms

  • synonym – alarm: a sudden fear or concern about a danger or problem
  • antonym – relaxation: a state of being free from tension or anxiety
