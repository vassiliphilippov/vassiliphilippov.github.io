loafing

noun
idleness; avoiding work
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈləʊfɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of spending time idly or being unwilling to work; idleness or avoidance of employment

Examples

loafing around the house
His constant loafing around the house annoyed his parents.
Instead of finding a job, he spent his days loafing in the park.
The manager warned the workers that loafing during office hours would not be tolerated.

Related Terms

  • synonym – idleness: the state of being inactive or not working
  • antonym – working: engaged in employment or work
