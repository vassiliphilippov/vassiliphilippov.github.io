like a man

adverb
with maturity or honor
Pronunciation: / laɪk ə mæn / (AmE), / laɪk ə mæn / (BrE)
in a way that shows maturity, courage, or honor, especially by taking responsibility or facing difficulties bravely

Examples

face problems like a man
After hearing the criticism, he accepted it like a man and promised to do better.
He owned up to his mistake like a man and apologized sincerely.
She encouraged him to face the challenge like a man instead of running away.

Related Terms
