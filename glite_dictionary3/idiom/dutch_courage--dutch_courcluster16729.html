Dutch courage

noun
bravery from drinking alcohol
Pronunciation: / dʌtʃ ˈkɝːrɪdʒ / (AmE), / dʌtʃ ˈkʌrɪdʒ / (BrE)
confidence or bravery inspired by alcohol, especially when someone acts more boldly after drinking.
