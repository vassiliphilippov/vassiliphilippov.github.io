to take courage in both hands

verb
summon one's courage to act
Pronunciation: / teɪk ˈkɝː.ɪdʒ ɪn boʊθ hændz / (AmE), / teɪk ˈkʌr.ɪdʒ ɪn bəʊθ hændz / (BrE)
