to shoot first and ask questions later

verb
to act impulsively without understanding a situation
Pronunciation: / ʃuːt fɝst ənd æsk ˈkwɛs.tʃənz ˈleɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ʃuːt fɜːst ənd ɑːsk ˈkwɛs.tʃənz ˈleɪ.tə / (BrE)
to act quickly and impulsively without waiting to understand a situation fully, often leading to rash or uninformed decisions
