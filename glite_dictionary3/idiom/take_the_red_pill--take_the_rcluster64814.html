to take the red pill

verb
choose to face an uncomfortable truth
Pronunciation: / teɪk ðə rɛd pɪl / (AmE), / teɪk ðə rɛd pɪl / (BrE)
to choose to learn or accept an uncomfortable or difficult truth, especially when it challenges one's previous beliefs or realities
