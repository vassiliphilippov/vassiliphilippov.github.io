flight of fancy

noun
imaginative but unrealistic idea
Pronunciation: / flaɪt əv ˈfænsi / (AmE), / flaɪt əv ˈfænsi / (BrE)
An idea, story, or notion that is imaginative but unrealistic or unlikely to be true or practical.

Examples

wild flight of fancy
Her latest business idea was dismissed as a flight of fancy by her skeptical partners.
Sometimes, a simple flight of fancy can inspire groundbreaking innovation.
The novel is filled with flights of fancy that take the reader to imaginary worlds.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fantasy: An idea with no basis in reality
  • synonym – pipe dream: An unattainable or fanciful hope
  • antonym – reality: The state of things as they actually exist
