anything you can think of
Pronunciation: / juː ˈneɪm ɪt / (AmE), / juː ˈneɪm ɪt / (BrE)
used to indicate that anything you can think of is included or available.

Examples

you name it in a list
Her expertise covers painting, sculpting, pottery—you name it.
The store sells books, toys, clothes, electronics—you name it.
We have a huge selection of fruits—apples, oranges, bananas, you name it.
