soaked to the skin

adjective
completely wet through clothing
Pronunciation: / soʊkt tə ðə skɪn / (AmE), / səʊkt tə ðə skɪn / (BrE)
completely wet; having water soaked through one's clothing to the skin

Examples

soaked to the skin after rain
After walking home in the downpour, I was soaked to the skin and needed dry clothes.
She forgot her umbrella and ended up soaked to the skin on her way to work.
They went swimming in their clothes and got soaked to the skin for fun.
