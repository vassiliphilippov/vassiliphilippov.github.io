to blow a chance

verb
waste an opportunity
Pronunciation: / bloʊ ə tʃæns / (AmE), / bləʊ ə tʃɑːns / (BrE)
to waste an opportunity; to fail to take advantage of a favorable situation

Examples

to blow a chance at success
Don’t blow a chance to impress the manager by being unprepared.

If you arrive late to the interview, you might blow a chance at the job.
