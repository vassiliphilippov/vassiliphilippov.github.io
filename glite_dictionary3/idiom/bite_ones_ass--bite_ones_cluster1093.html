to bite one's ass

verb
respond rudely or defiantly (vulgar)
Pronunciation: / baɪt wʌnz æs / (AmE), / baɪt wʌnz ɑːs / (BrE)
(vulgar, informal) To respond to someone with defiance, irritation, or rudeness, often as a retort to criticism or authority.
