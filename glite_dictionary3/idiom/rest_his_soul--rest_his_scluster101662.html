rest his soul

x
used to show respect for a deceased man
Pronunciation: / rɛst hɪz soʊl / (AmE), / rɛst hɪz səʊl / (BrE)
(expressing a wish) used to show respect for a man who has died, often as a parenthetical remark after mentioning him.

Examples

rest his soul after his death
He was a kind man, rest his soul, and is still missed by many.
My father, rest his soul, taught me everything I know about gardening.
She would always mention her late husband, rest his soul, when telling family stories.
