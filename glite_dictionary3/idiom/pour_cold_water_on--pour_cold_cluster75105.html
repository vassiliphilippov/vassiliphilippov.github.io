to pour cold water on

verb
discourage or express disapproval
Pronunciation: / pɔr koʊld ˈwɔtɚ ɑn /; / pɔr koʊld ˈwɔt̬ɚ ɑn / (AmE), / pɔː kəʊld ˈwɔːtə ɒn / (BrE)
to discourage or criticize an idea, plan, or enthusiasm, making people feel less positive about it
