within reach

adjective
achievable or attainable
Pronunciation: / wɪˈðɪn ˈritʃ / (AmE), / wɪˈðɪn ˈriːtʃ / (BrE)
Achievable or attainable; not too difficult to obtain or accomplish.

Examples

within reach of success
Success seemed impossible at first, but now it feels within reach.
The medicine was placed just within reach of the patient so she could take it herself.
With a little more effort, the championship could be within reach for our team.

Related Terms

  • antonym – out of reach: not achievable or attainable; too difficult to obtain
