to hand someone their hat

verb
dismiss or fire someone (informal idiom)
Pronunciation: / ˈhændəd sʌmˌwʌn ðɛr hæt / (AmE), / ˈhændɪd sʌmwʌn ðeə hæt / (BrE)
(informal) To dismiss someone from a job or position, often signaling that it is time for them to leave.
