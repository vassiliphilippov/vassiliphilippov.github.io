to the death

adverb
with unwavering or ultimate commitment
Pronunciation: / tu ðə dɛθ / (AmE), / tə ðə dɛθ / (BrE)
With absolute and unwavering commitment, determination, or willingness to continue until the very end, even if it means facing death.

Examples

They vowed to fight to the death for their land and freedom.
