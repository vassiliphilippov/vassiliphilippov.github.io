to give a damn

verb
to care at all; to be concerned (usu. negative)
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv ə dæm / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə dam /; / ɡɪv ə dæm / (BrE)
to care at all about something or someone (usually used in the negative to show indifference or lack of concern)

to give a damn about problems
