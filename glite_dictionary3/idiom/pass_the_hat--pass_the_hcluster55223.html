to pass the hat

verb
collect money from a group of people
Pronunciation: / pæs ðə hæt / (AmE), / pɑːs ðə hæt / (BrE)
to ask a group of people to contribute money, typically for a specific cause or person

Examples

to pass the hat for donations

Whenever someone retires, we pass the hat and buy them a gift from everyone.
