to force someone's hand

verb
compel someone to act or decide
Pronunciation: / fɔrs sʌmwʌnz hænd / (AmE), / fɔːs sʌmwʌnz hænd / (BrE)
to make someone do something or make a decision sooner than they intended or before they are ready

Examples
