to jump the queue

verb
skip ahead in a line or queue
Pronunciation: / dʒʌmp ðə kju / (AmE), / dʒʌmp ðə kjuː / (BrE)
to move ahead of others waiting in line, especially by unfairly skipping one's turn

Examples

to jump the queue at a bus stop
If you try to jump the queue, people will likely call you out for being rude.
Please don't jump the queue, everyone has been waiting their turn.
Tourists sometimes jump the queue without realizing how offensive it is in the UK.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cut in line: to move ahead of others in a queue without waiting your turn
  • antonym – wait in line: to stand and wait for your turn in an orderly queue
