to shoot from the hip

verb
act or speak impulsively
Pronunciation: / ʃuːt frəm ðə hɪp / (AmE), / ʃuːt frəm ðə hɪp / (BrE)
to act or speak quickly and without careful thought or planning

Examples

to shoot from the hip in meetings

It's risky to shoot from the hip during important business negotiations.
When challenged, she tends to shoot from the hip rather than provide a measured response.
