to raise a toast

verb
make a short speech in honor of someone
Pronunciation: / ɹeɪz ə toʊst / (AmE), / ɹeɪz ə təʊst / (BrE)
to make a short speech and drink in honor of someone or something

Examples

raise a toast to friends
At the wedding, the best man raised a toast to the happy couple.
Before dinner, our host raised a toast to friendship and good health.
We all stood and raised a toast to our retiring colleague's achievements.

  • synonym – propose a toast: to formally suggest drinking in honor of someone
