not as black as one is painted

adjective
not as bad as claimed
Pronunciation: / nɑt æz blæk æz wʌn ɪz ˈpeɪntɪd / (AmE), / nɒt æz blæk æz wʌn ɪz ˈpeɪntɪd / (BrE)
not as bad or unpleasant as people say or believe; less dire than commonly perceived or described.

Examples

not as black as one is painted by others
Don't worry, the storm's aftermath is not as black as one is painted by the news.
The neighborhood is not as black as one is painted; it's actually quite friendly.
When you get to know him, you realize he's not as black as one is painted.
