on autopilot

adverb
acting without conscious thought
Pronunciation: / ɑn ˈɔːtoʊˌpaɪlət / (AmE), / ɒn ˈɔːtəʊˌpaɪlət / (BrE)
acting or functioning automatically, without deliberate thought or conscious awareness, especially out of habit or routine.

Examples

on autopilot at work
After years in the job, she completed her daily tasks on autopilot.
He drove the entire way home on autopilot, barely remembering the route he took.
Sometimes I find myself answering emails on autopilot before I have my morning coffee.

Related Terms

  • synonym – habitually: done by habit or routine
  • antonym – mindfully: done with conscious awareness
