to catch no ball

verb
fail to understand anything
Pronunciation: / kætʃ noʊ bɔl / (AmE), / kætʃ nəʊ bɔːl / (BrE)
(informal) To fail to understand anything; to comprehend nothing about what is being said or happening.

Examples

catch no ball in class
He speaks so fast that I catch no ball during his explanation.
I tried to follow the lecture, but I catch no ball at all.
Whenever they talk about physics, I just catch no ball.
