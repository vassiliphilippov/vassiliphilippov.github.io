the star of the show

noun
most important or impressive person or thing
Pronunciation: / ðə stɑr əv ðə ʃoʊ / (AmE), / ðə stɑːr əv ðə ʃəʊ / (BrE)
the person or thing that is the most important, impressive, or prominent among a group, especially in a performance or event

Examples

the star of the show on stage
After her stunning solo, Maria was clearly the star of the show.
Despite being a minor character, his performance made him the star of the show.
The adorable puppy became the star of the show during the parade.

  • synonym – scene-stealer: a person who draws more attention than others in a performance or situation
