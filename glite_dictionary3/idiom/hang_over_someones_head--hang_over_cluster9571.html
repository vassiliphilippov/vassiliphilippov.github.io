to hang over someone's head

verb
cause persistent anxiety or worry
Pronunciation: / hæŋ ˈoʊvɚ ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / hæŋ ˈəʊvə ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel continuous worry or anxiety because of something unpleasant or threatening that may happen

Examples

hang over someone's head like a threat
The possibility of failing the exam hung over her head the entire semester.
The threat of losing his job continued to hang over his head for months.
Unpaid bills can hang over your head and cause stress.
