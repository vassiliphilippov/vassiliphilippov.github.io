to push it to the limit

verb
go as far as possible; maximize effort or risk
Pronunciation: / pʊʃ ɪt tə ðə ˈlɪmɪt / (AmE), / pʊʃ ɪt tə ðə ˈlɪmɪt / (BrE)
to use all your energy or resources, or to take risks as far as possible, often to test your abilities or endurance
