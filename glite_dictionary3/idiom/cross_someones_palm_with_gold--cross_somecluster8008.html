to cross someone's palm with gold

verb
pay money for a service, esp. fortune-telling
Pronunciation: / krɔs ˈsʌmwʌnz pɑm wɪð goʊld / (AmE), / krɒs ˈsʌmwʌnz pɑːm wɪð gəʊld / (BrE)
to pay money to someone for a service, especially one involving fortune-telling or supernatural advice

Examples

to cross her palm with gold for fortune-telling
Before she read my tarot cards, the mysterious woman asked me to cross her palm with gold.
If you want to learn your fate, you must cross my palm with gold first, the fortune-teller said.
They would only reveal the secret after I crossed their palm with gold.
