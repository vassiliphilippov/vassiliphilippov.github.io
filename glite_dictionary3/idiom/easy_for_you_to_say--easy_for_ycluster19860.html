phrase showing advice is easier to give than follow
Pronunciation: / ˈizi fər ju tə seɪ / (AmE), / ˈiːzi fə juː tə seɪ / (BrE)
used to indicate that advice or comments are simple to give when one is not facing the difficulty oneself
