sick as a parrot

adjective
deeply disappointed or upset (Brit. idiom)
Pronunciation: / sɪk əz ə ˈpæɹət / (AmE), / sɪk əz ə ˈpærət / (BrE)
Extremely disappointed or upset, often as a result of an unfavorable outcome.

She felt sick as a parrot when she didn't get the promotion she worked so hard for.
