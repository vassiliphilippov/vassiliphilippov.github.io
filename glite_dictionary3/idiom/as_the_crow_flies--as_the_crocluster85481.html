as the crow flies

adverb
in a straight line (distance)
Pronunciation: / æz ðə kroʊ flaɪz / (AmE), / az ðə krəʊ flaɪz / (BrE)
measured in a straight line between two points, rather than by following roads or paths

Examples

distance as the crow flies
It's about five kilometers as the crow flies, but much further by road.
The city center is ten blocks as the crow flies from here.
The village is only two miles away as the crow flies.
