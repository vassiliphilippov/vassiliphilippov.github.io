for the sake of

conjunction
for the purpose or benefit of
Pronunciation: / fər ðə seɪk ʌv / (AmE), / fə ðə seɪk ɒv / (BrE)
done with the purpose or benefit of someone or something; for the purpose of achieving or preserving.

Examples

for the sake of peace
Let’s keep noise to a minimum for the sake of the neighbors.
She moved to the countryside for the sake of her children's health.
They stayed together for the sake of their family.

Related Terms

  • synonym – for the purpose of: with the aim or intention of
