in part

adverb
partly; to some extent
Pronunciation: / ɪn pɑrt / (AmE), / ɪn pɑːt / (BrE)
to some extent; partly but not completely.

Examples

in part because
He succeeded in part because of his determination.
My decision was made in part out of necessity.
The economic slowdown was caused in part by higher oil prices.
