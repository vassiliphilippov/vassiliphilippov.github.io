under the influence

adjective
affected by alcohol or drugs
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚ ði ɪnˈfluəns / (AmE), / ˈʌn.də ði ɪnˈflu.əns / (BrE)
in a state of being impaired or affected by alcohol or drugs, especially when it impairs one's ability to function normally or legally

Examples

under the influence of alcohol
He apologized for his rude behavior, saying he had been under the influence at the time.
It is illegal to operate heavy machinery while under the influence.
The police suspected she was under the influence after swerving on the road.
