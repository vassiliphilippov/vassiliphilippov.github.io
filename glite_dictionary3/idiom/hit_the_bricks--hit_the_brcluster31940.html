to hit the bricks

verb
to leave or go away, often for work
Pronunciation: / hɪt ðə brɪks / (AmE), / hɪt ðə brɪks / (BrE)
to leave or go away, especially to start looking for work or to begin traveling from place to place

Examples

to hit the bricks looking for work
After losing his job, he had no choice but to hit the bricks and look for new opportunities.
