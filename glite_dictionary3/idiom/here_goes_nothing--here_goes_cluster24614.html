here goes nothing

x
expression before attempting something risky or uncertain
Pronunciation: / hɪr ɡoʊz ˈnʌθɪŋ / (AmE), / hɪə ɡəʊz ˈnʌθɪŋ / (BrE)
used to indicate that one is about to try something risky or uncertain, often without high expectations of success

Examples

here goes nothing before trying
'Here goes nothing,' she said as she pressed the send button on her application.
Here goes nothing, I guess I'll try to fix the computer myself.
He stepped onto the stage and whispered, 'here goes nothing.'
