to do one's best

verb
make the greatest possible effort
Pronunciation: / du wʌnz bɛst / (AmE), / də wʌnz best / (BrE)
to try as hard as possible to achieve something or perform well, even if the outcome is uncertain

Examples

to do one's best in a competition
All the athletes did their best to win the race.
Even though she was nervous, she tried to do her best during the interview.
No matter how difficult the task, he promises to always do his best.

Related Terms

  • antonym – give up: to stop trying to do something; to quit
  • synonym – try: to make an attempt or effort to do something
