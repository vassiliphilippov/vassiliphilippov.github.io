no shit, Sherlock

interjection
sarcastic response to an obvious statement
Pronunciation: / noʊ ʃɪt ˈʃɜːrlɑːk / (AmE), / nəʊ ʃɪt ˈʃɜːlɒk / (BrE)
used sarcastically to indicate that someone has stated something obvious or self-evident

Examples

no shit, Sherlock after obvious comment
"I think you should save money." "No shit, Sherlock, that's what I'm trying to do."
"We need more rain for the crops to grow." "No shit, Sherlock."
He said the test was hard—no shit, Sherlock, we all struggled with it.

Related Terms

  • synonym – no kidding: used to express disbelief or sarcasm about an obvious remark
  • synonym – obviously: used to mean 'in a way that is easy to see or understand'
  • synonym – thank you, Captain Obvious: sarcastic idiom for pointing out the obvious
