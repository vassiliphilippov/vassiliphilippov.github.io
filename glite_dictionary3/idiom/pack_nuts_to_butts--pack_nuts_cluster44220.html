to pack nuts to butts

verb
crowd people together very tightly (vulgar slang)
Pronunciation: / pæk nʌts tə bʌts / (AmE), / pæk nʌts tə bʌts / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) To crowd people tightly together so that there is almost no space between them.

Examples

to pack nuts to butts in a subway
During rush hour, the subway cars were packed nuts to butts with commuters.
The festival was so popular that fans ended up packed nuts to butts near the stage.
We were packing nuts to butts in the elevator after the fire drill.

  • synonym – jam: to crowd or pack tightly
  • antonym – space out: to arrange with space between; opposite of crowding together
