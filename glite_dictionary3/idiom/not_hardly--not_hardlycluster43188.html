not hardly

adverb
emphatic way to say 'not at all'
Pronunciation: / nɑt ˈhɑrdli / (AmE), / nɒt ˈhɑːdli / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is not true at all; absolutely not.

Examples

not hardly believe it
She asked if I believed him—not hardly!
They promised to help, but did they show up? Not hardly.
Would I trust him after what he did? Not hardly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hardly: almost not; only a very small amount or degree
