to reach a nadir

verb
experience the lowest point
Pronunciation: / ritʃ ə nəˈdɪr / (AmE), / riːtʃ ə ˈneɪdɪə / (BrE)
to experience the lowest or most unsuccessful point in a situation or one's life.

Examples

reach a nadir in life
After months of financial trouble, the company finally reached a nadir in profits last quarter.
Her confidence reached a nadir following the harsh criticism of her performance.
Many say the team's morale reached a nadir after consecutive defeats.

Related Terms

  • antonym – peak: the highest point or level
  • synonym – rock bottom: the lowest possible level or point
