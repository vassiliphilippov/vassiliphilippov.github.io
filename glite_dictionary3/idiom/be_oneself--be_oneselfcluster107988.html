to be oneself

verb
act naturally, true to oneself
Pronunciation: / bi wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / bi wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to act in a natural and genuine way, without pretending or trying to fit someone else's expectations

Examples
