to pour fuel on the fire

verb
make a bad situation worse
Pronunciation: / pɔr fjuːəl ɑn ðə ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / pɔː fjʊəl ɒn ðə ˈfaɪə / (BrE)

Examples
