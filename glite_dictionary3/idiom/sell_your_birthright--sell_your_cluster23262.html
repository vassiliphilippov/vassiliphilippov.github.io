to sell your birthright

verb
give up something valuable for little
Pronunciation: / sɛl jʊr ˈbɜrθˌraɪt / (AmE), / sɛl jɔː ˈbɜːθˌraɪt / (BrE)
to give up something very valuable or fundamental, especially for something of little worth

Examples

to sell your birthright for money
He warned his friend not to sell your birthright for temporary pleasure.
In the pursuit of fame, she almost sold her birthright of privacy and peace.
Some fear that, by accepting such a low offer, we might sell your birthright.
