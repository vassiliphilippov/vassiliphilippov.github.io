in the know

adjective
having inside or privileged information
Pronunciation: / ɪn ðə noʊ / (AmE), / ɪn ðə nəʊ / (BrE)
having access to special or insider information that most people do not have; being aware of secret or recent developments

Examples

If you want to be in the know, you should subscribe to this newsletter.
Only a few staff members are in the know about the merger plans.
