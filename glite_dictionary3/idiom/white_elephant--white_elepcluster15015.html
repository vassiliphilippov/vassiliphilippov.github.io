white elephant

noun
costly, troublesome possession
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈɛl.ə.fənt /; / waɪt ˈɛlɪfənt / (AmE), / waɪt ˈɛl.ɪ.fənt /; / waɪt ˈɛlɪfənt / (BrE)
a possession that is expensive or difficult to maintain and is more trouble than it is worth
