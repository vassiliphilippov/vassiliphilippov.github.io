up at night

adverb
unable to sleep due to worry
Pronunciation: / ʌp ət naɪt / (AmE), / ʌp ət naɪt / (BrE)
unable to sleep during the night, especially because of worry or anxiety

Examples

keeps me up at night
His job stress often keeps him up at night.
She lay up at night, worried about her upcoming exams.
Thinking about the future sometimes keeps me up at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – insomnia: the condition of being unable to sleep
  • antonym – sleep soundly: to sleep deeply and without interruption
