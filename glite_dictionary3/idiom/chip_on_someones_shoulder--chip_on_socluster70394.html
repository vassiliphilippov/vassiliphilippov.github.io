chip on someone's shoulder

noun
a tendency to be easily offended or resentful
Pronunciation: / tʃɪp ɑn ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈʃoʊldər / (AmE), / tʃɪp ɒn ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈʃəʊldə / (BrE)
a tendency to be easily offended or to feel resentment, often as a result of past slights or grievances

Examples

carry a chip on your shoulder
Don’t walk around with a chip on your shoulder; let go of the past.
Ever since he was overlooked for the award, he’s had a chip on his shoulder.
She tends to carry a chip on her shoulder about being the youngest in the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym – grudge: persistent feeling of resentment
  • synonym – resentment: bitter indignation at being treated unfairly
