up to scratch

adjective
meeting the required standard
Pronunciation: / ʌp tə skrætʃ / (AmE), / ʌp tə skrætʃ / (BrE)
of a standard good enough to meet the required level or expectations; satisfactory or acceptable in quality.

Examples

up to scratch performance
After weeks of revision, her thesis was finally up to scratch.
If your computer isn't up to scratch, you might need to upgrade it.
We hired a new chef to ensure our food was up to scratch.

Related Terms

  • synonym – adequate: sufficient to meet a need or requirement
  • antonym – subpar: below the expected standard
