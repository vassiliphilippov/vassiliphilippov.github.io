on the fritz

adjective
malfunctioning, not working properly (of a device)
Pronunciation: / ɑn ðə frɪts / (AmE), / ɒn ðə frɪts / (BrE)
(of a machine or device) not working or operating as it should; malfunctioning.

Examples

on the fritz again
My washing machine is on the fritz and won't drain any water.
Sorry, the phone is on the fritz right now, so I can't call you back.
Their air conditioning was on the fritz all summer during the heat wave.
