to spike the punch

verb
add alcohol to punch (usually secretly)
Pronunciation: / spaɪk ðə pʌntʃ / (AmE), / spaɪk ðə pʌntʃ / (BrE)
to add alcohol to a non-alcoholic punch, especially secretly or unexpectedly at a social gathering

Examples

spike the punch with alcohol
He confessed that he had spiked the punch with rum during the party last night.
If you spike the punch, make sure you tell the guests so everyone is aware.
Someone tried to spike the punch at the school dance, but the chaperones stopped them.

Related Terms

  • synonym – spike: to add alcohol (or something else) to a drink or food, often secretly
