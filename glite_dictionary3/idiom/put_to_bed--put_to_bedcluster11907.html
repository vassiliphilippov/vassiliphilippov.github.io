to put to bed

verb
finalize or resolve something
Pronunciation: / pʊt tə bɛd / (AmE), / pʊt tə bɛd / (BrE)
to complete, resolve, or finalize something, especially after a period of work or discussion

Examples

put to bed a project
After editing for hours, the journalists finally put the story to bed.
Let's put this issue to bed so we can move on to new projects.
We need to put to bed all outstanding questions before launching the product.

Related Terms

  • synonym – finalize: to complete something so that it is finished or settled
  • synonym – resolve: to find a solution or settle something conclusively
