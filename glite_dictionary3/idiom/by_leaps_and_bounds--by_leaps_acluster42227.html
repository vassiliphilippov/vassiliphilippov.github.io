by leaps and bounds

adverb
very quickly; rapidly and greatly
Pronunciation: / baɪ lips ənd baʊndz / (AmE), / baɪ liːps ənd baʊndz / (BrE)
extremely quickly and by large amounts; with rapid and significant progress

Examples

improve by leaps and bounds
Her confidence has grown by leaps and bounds since she started her new job.
His language skills improved by leaps and bounds after moving abroad.
The company's technology is advancing by leaps and bounds every year.
