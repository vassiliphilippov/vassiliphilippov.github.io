to pay through the nose

verb
pay excessively; overpay
Pronunciation: / peɪ θru ðə noʊz / (AmE), / peɪ θruː ðə nəʊz / (BrE)
to pay an amount of money that is much higher than what is reasonable or expected

Examples

to pay through the nose for tickets
Many families are paying through the nose for childcare these days.
She refuses to pay through the nose for designer clothes when there are cheaper options available.
They had to pay through the nose for tickets to the concert because they booked too late.

Related Terms

  • synonym – get ripped off: to be cheated out of money or overcharged (informal phrase)
  • synonym – overpay: to pay more than the fair or expected amount
