and similar things
Pronunciation: / ænd sʌtʃ / (AmE), / ænd sʌtʃ / (BrE)
used to refer to things that are similar to or of the same type as those already mentioned

Examples

hiking, biking, and such
He enjoys outdoor activities—hiking, biking, and such.
The course covers topics like algebra, geometry, and such.
You can bring snacks, drinks, and such for the picnic.

Related Terms

  • synonym – and so on: used to indicate a continuation of a list in a similar vein
  • synonym – et cetera: and other similar things; and so forth
