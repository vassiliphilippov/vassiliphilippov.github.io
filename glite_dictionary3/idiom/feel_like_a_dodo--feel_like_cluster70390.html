to feel like a dodo

verb
to feel foolish or silly
Pronunciation: / fil laɪk ə ˈdoʊdoʊ / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈdəʊdəʊ / (BrE)
to feel foolish or silly, especially due to one's own actions or mistakes

Examples

to feel like a dodo after a mistake

I felt like a dodo when I realized I had left my keys in the car.
Whenever I forget someone's name, I just feel like a dodo.
