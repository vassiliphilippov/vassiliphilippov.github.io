to snatch defeat from the jaws of victory

verb
unexpectedly lose when about to win
Pronunciation: / snætʃ dɪˈfit frəm ðə dʒɔz əv ˈvɪktəri / (AmE), / snætʃ dɪˈfiːt frəm ðə dʒɔːz əv ˈvɪktəri / (BrE)
to lose a seemingly certain victory, often because of an unexpected mistake or reversal
