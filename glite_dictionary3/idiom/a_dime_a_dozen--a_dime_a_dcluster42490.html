a dime a dozen

adjective
very common; easy to find
Pronunciation: / ə daɪm ə dəˈzʌzən / (AmE), / ə daɪm ə dəˈzʌzən / (BrE)

Examples

Talented programmers may seem a dime a dozen, but truly exceptional ones are rare.
