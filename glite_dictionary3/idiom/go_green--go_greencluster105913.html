to go green

verb
adopt environmentally friendly practices
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ɡriːn / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ɡriːn / (BrE)
to begin making decisions and adopting practices that are environmentally friendly and reduce harm to the environment

Examples

to go green at work
It's important for manufacturers to go green to help combat climate change.
Many households have decided to go green by installing solar panels on their roofs.
The government is offering incentives for farmers who go green and use sustainable methods.
