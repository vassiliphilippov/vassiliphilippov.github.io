full of oneself

adjective
arrogant or self-important (idiom)
Pronunciation: / fʊl əv wʌnˌsɛlf / (AmE), / fʊl əv wʌnˌsɛlf / (BrE)
Having an excessively high opinion of one's own abilities or importance; arrogant or self-important.

Examples

seems full of oneself at work

She tends to get full of oneself whenever she receives compliments.
Try not to be full of oneself, even when you’re doing well.
