close at hand

adjective
nearby; within reach
Pronunciation: / kloʊs æt hænd / (AmE), / kləʊs æt hand / (BrE)
near in space; easily accessible or within reach.

Examples

close at hand for help
Help was close at hand when the hikers got lost.
I always keep a notebook close at hand to jot down my ideas.
The museum is conveniently close at hand if you want to visit after lunch.

Related Terms

  • antonym – distant: far away in space or time
  • synonym – nearby: close in distance; not far away
