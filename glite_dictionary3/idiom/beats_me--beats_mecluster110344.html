beats me

interjection
I don't know; I have no idea
Pronunciation: / bits mi / (AmE), / biːts miː / (BrE)
used to say that you do not know or have no idea about something

Examples

beats me when asked
If you’re asking why the bus is late, beats me.
It beats me how anyone can solve this puzzle so quickly.
Where Jane went after the party? Beats me!
