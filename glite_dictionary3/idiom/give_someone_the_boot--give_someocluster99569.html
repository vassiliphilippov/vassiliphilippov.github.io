to give (someone) the boot

verb
dismiss or fire someone
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv ðə buːt / (AmE), / ɡɪv ðə buːt / (BrE)
to dismiss or remove someone from a job or position; to fire someone

Examples

If you don't meet your targets this quarter, management might give you the boot.
They finally gave him the boot after numerous warnings about his behavior.
When he arrived late for the third time, his boss decided to give him the boot.
