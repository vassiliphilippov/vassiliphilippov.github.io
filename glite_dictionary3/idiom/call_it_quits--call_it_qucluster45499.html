to call it quits

verb
stop an activity; quit
Pronunciation: / kɔl ɪt kwɪts /; / kɑl ɪt kwɪts / (AmE), / kɔːl ɪt kwɪts / (BrE)
to stop doing something, usually because you have done enough or cannot continue

Examples

call it quits for today
After working late into the night, she finally decided to call it quits and go to bed.
Let's call it quits for today and finish the rest tomorrow.
They agreed to call it quits after hours of negotiation without progress.
After three hours of arguing, they decided to call it quits and go home.
If you feel tired, it's perfectly fine to call it quits for the day.
They finally called it quits after years of trying to fix their relationship.
