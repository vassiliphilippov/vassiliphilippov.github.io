stick in the mud

noun
a person who resists change or excitement
Pronunciation: / stɪk ɪn ðə mʌd / (AmE), / stɪk ɪn ðə mʌd / (BrE)
a person who is dull, unadventurous, or resistant to change or new ideas

Examples

old stick in the mud
Don't be such a stick in the mud; come and dance with us.
He's always been a stick in the mud when it comes to trying new foods.
My friends called me a stick in the mud because I didn't want to go skydiving.

Related Terms
