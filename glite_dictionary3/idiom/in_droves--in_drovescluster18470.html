in droves

adverb
in large numbers
Pronunciation: / ɪn droʊvz / (AmE), / ɪn drəʊvz / (BrE)
in very large numbers; in large crowds or groups

Examples

arrive in droves
After the announcement, customers started arriving in droves to buy the new product.
Students left the auditorium in droves when the speech ended.
Wild animals migrate in droves during the change of seasons.
