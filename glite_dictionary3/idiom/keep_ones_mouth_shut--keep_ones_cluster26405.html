to keep one's mouth shut

verb
remain silent; not reveal information
Pronunciation: / kip wʌnz maʊθ ʃʌt / (AmE), / kiːp wʌnz maʊθ ʃʌt / (BrE)
to not speak or not reveal information, especially to keep something secret or avoid trouble
