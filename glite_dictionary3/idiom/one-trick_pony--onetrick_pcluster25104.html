one-trick pony

noun
person/thing with only one skill
Pronunciation: / ˌwʌn trɪk ˈpoʊni / (AmE), / ˌwʌn trɪk ˈpəʊni / (BrE)
a person or thing with only one talent, skill, or area of expertise

Examples

a one-trick pony at work
If you want to advance in your career, try not to become a one-trick pony.
Many critics dismissed the band as a one-trick pony after their second album sounded just like their first.
People often call him a one-trick pony, but he takes pride in perfecting his one specialty.

Related Terms

  • antonym – jack of all trades: a person who can do many different types of work but is not necessarily very competent at any of them
