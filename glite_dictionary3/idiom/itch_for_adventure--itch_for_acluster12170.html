to itch for adventure

verb
strong desire for excitement or novelty
Pronunciation: / ɪtʃ fər ədˈvɛntʃər / (AmE), / ɪtʃ fə rəˈvɛntʃə / (BrE)
to have a strong and restless desire for excitement or new experiences

Examples

to itch for adventure abroad

Though he enjoyed his stable life, there was always an itch for adventure he couldn't quite satisfy.
