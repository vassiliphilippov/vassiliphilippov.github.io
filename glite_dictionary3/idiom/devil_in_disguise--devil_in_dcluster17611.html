devil in disguise

noun
person or thing that seems good but is actually evil
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛv(ə)l ɪn dɪsˈɡaɪz / (AmE), / ˈdɛv(ə)l ɪn dɪsˈɡaɪz / (BrE)
A person or thing that appears friendly or harmless but is actually dangerous or evil.
