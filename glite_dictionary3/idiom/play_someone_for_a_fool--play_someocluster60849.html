to play (someone) for a fool

verb
attempt to deceive someone as if they are foolish
Pronunciation: / pleɪ (ˈsʌmwʌn) fɚ ə ful / (AmE), / pleɪ (ˈsʌmwʌn) fə ə fuːl / (BrE)
to try to deceive or take advantage of someone by assuming they are naive or unintelligent
