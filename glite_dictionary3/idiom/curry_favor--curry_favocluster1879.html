to curry favor

verb
seek approval by flattery
Pronunciation: / ˌkɜri ˈfeɪvər /; / ˈkɝː.i ˈfeɪ.vɚ / (AmE), / ˌkʌri ˈfeɪvə /; / ˈkʌr.i ˈfeɪ.və / (BrE)
to try to gain someone's approval or advantage by flattery or insincere behavior

Examples

to curry favor with the boss
