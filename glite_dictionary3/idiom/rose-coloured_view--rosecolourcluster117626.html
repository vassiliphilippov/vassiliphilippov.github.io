rose-coloured view

noun
overly positive perspective
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊz ˌkʌlɚd vju / (AmE), / ˈrəʊz ˌkʌləd vjuː / (BrE)
an optimistic or unrealistically positive perspective, often ignoring potential problems or difficulties

Examples

a rose-coloured view of life
Even when things go wrong, she tends to have a rose-coloured view of the situation.
His rose-coloured view of the company's future made him ignore clear warning signs.
It's important to be realistic and not always see the world through a rose-coloured view.
