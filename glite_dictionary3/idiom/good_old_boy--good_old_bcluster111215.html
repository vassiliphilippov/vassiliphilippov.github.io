good old boy

noun
conservative, convivial Southern US white male
Pronunciation: / ˌɡʊd oʊld ˈbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˌɡʊd əʊld ˈbɔɪ / (BrE)
(in the southern US) a white man, especially from a rural area, with an unpretentious, friendly manner and conservative or intolerant views, who shows strong loyalty to his social group.

Examples

local good old boy
He grew up among the good old boys who controlled local politics for decades.
She criticized the good old boy network for excluding women from leadership positions.
The club felt like a gathering of good old boys, resistant to any change from outsiders.
