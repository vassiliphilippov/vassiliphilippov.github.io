to be a bomb

verb
to fail completely; be unsuccessful
Pronunciation: / bi ə bɑm / (AmE), / bi ə bɒm / (BrE)
to fail completely and be unsuccessful, especially in a noticeable or embarrassing way

Examples

be a bomb at the box office
Despite high expectations, the product launch was a bomb and sales were disappointing.
He realized his joke was a bomb when nobody laughed.
The new play was a bomb and closed after just one week.
