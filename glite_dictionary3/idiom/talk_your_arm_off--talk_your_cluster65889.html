to talk your arm off

verb
talk at excessive length
Pronunciation: / tɑk jɚ ɑrm ɑf / (AmE), / tɔːk jɔː ɑːm ɒf / (BrE)
to talk to someone for a very long time, often excessively or to the point of annoyance

Examples

talk your arm off about stories
If you let him, he'll talk your arm off about his travels.
My neighbor will talk your arm off if you ask her about her cats.
She can talk your arm off when she gets excited about a new project.

Related Terms

  • synonym – talk someone's ear off: to talk to someone for a long time, often excessively
