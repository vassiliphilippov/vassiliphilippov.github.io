to sleep with the fishes

verb
to be killed and disposed of in water
Pronunciation: / sliːp wɪð ðə ˈfɪʃɪz / (AmE), / sliːp wɪð ðə ˈfɪʃɪz / (BrE)
to be killed and disposed of in water, often used humorously to suggest someone's death at the hands of criminals
