to count sheep

verb
mental technique to fall asleep
Pronunciation: / kaʊnt ʃip / (AmE), / kaʊnt ʃiːp / (BrE)
to try to fall asleep by mentally counting imaginary sheep jumping over a fence or similar scene

Examples

to count sheep to sleep
As a child, he was told to count sheep whenever he had trouble drifting off.
She tried to count sheep, but it didn't help her fall asleep that night.
When I can't sleep, I usually count sheep to relax my mind.
