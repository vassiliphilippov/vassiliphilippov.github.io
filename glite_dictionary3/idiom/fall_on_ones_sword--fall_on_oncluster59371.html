to fall on one's sword

verb
take responsibility for a failure
Pronunciation: / fɔl ɑn wʌnz sɔrd / (AmE), / fɔːl ɒn wʌnz sɔːd / (BrE)
to take responsibility for a failure, especially by accepting blame or resigning from a position

Examples

fall on his sword and resign
After the scandal, the minister chose to fall on his sword and resigned from office.
She was praised for being willing to fall on her sword to protect her team from criticism.
Sometimes a leader must fall on their sword to maintain the organization's reputation.

Related Terms

  • antonym – pass the buck: shift responsibility to someone else
  • synonym – take the blame: accept responsibility for a mistake or failure
