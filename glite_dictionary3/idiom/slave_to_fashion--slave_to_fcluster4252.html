slave to fashion

noun
person obsessed with following fashion trends
Pronunciation: / sleɪv tə ˈfæʃən / (AmE), / sleɪv tə ˈfæʃən / (BrE)
A person who is excessively devoted to following the latest fashion trends, often at the expense of personal comfort or individuality.

Examples

slave to fashion follows trends
Being a slave to fashion means constantly updating your wardrobe, no matter the cost.
He doesn't care about brand names and refuses to be a slave to fashion.
Julia has become a slave to fashion, never wearing the same outfit twice.
