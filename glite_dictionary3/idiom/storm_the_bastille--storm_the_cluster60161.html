to storm the Bastille

verb
attack or challenge authority forcefully
Pronunciation: / stɔrm ðə bəˈstil / (AmE), / stɔːm ðə bæsˈtiːl / (BrE)
to attack or challenge a powerful institution or authority, especially in a decisive or forceful way

Examples

to storm the Bastille of power
In the debate, she urged her supporters to storm the Bastille of bureaucratic resistance.
Protesters threatened to storm the Bastille if their demands were not met.
The film depicts young activists planning to storm the Bastille of corporate power.
