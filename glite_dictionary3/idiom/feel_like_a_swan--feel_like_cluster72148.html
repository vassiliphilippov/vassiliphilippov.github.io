to feel like a swan

verb
to feel graceful and beautiful
Pronunciation: / fil laɪk ə swɑn / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə swɒn / (BrE)
to feel graceful and beautiful, often as a result of one's appearance or poise.

When she performed the ballet, she said she felt like a swan gliding across the stage.
