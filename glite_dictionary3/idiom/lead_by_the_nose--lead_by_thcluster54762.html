to lead by the nose

verb
manipulate or control (someone) by deceit
Pronunciation: / lid baɪ ðə noʊz / (AmE), / liːd baɪ ðə nəʊz / (BrE)
to control or manipulate someone completely, often without them realizing it

Examples

lead by the nose with lies
Politicians often try to lead the public by the nose with false promises.
She was so trusting that her colleagues could easily lead her by the nose.
The clever salesman managed to lead by the nose everyone in the meeting.
She was so infatuated that he could lead her by the nose without resistance.
The politician managed to lead by the nose the entire board into supporting his plan.
They claimed the CEO was led by the nose by his advisors on every important decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bamboozle: to deceive or trick someone
  • synonym – dominate: to have control or power over someone
  • antonym – liberate: to set someone free from control or influence
  • synonym – manipulate: to control or influence someone cleverly or unscrupulously
