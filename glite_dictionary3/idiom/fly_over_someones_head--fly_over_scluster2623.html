to fly over someone's head

verb
to not be understood by someone
Pronunciation: / flaɪ ˈoʊvɚ ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / flaɪ ˈəʊvə ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (BrE)
(of an idea, remark, etc.) to not be understood by someone, typically because it is too complicated or advanced.
