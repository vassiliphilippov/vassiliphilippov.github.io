if the shoe fits, wear it

x
accept criticism if applicable
Pronunciation: / ɪf ðə ʃu fɪts wɔr ɪt / (AmE), / ɪf ðə ʃuː fɪts weər ɪt / (BrE)
used to suggest that someone should accept a criticism or remark if it applies to them, even if it is unflattering

Examples

if the shoe fits, wear it after criticism
He always denies the accusations, but if the shoe fits, wear it.
She laughed at the comment but said, 'if the shoe fits, wear it.'
You seem upset by the feedback—if the shoe fits, wear it.

Related Terms

  • synonym – if the cap fits, wear it: British variant of the same idiom
