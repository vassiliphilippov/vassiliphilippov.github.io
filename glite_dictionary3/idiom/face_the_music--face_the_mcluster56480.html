to face the music

verb
accept consequences for actions
Pronunciation: / feɪs ðə ˈmjuːzɪk /; / ˈfeɪs ðə ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / feɪs ðə ˈmjuːzɪk /; / ˈfeɪs ðə ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
