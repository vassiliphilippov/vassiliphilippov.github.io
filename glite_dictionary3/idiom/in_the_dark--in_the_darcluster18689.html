in the dark

adjective
unaware or uninformed
Pronunciation: / ɪn ðə dɑrk / (AmE), / ɪn ðə dɑːk / (BrE)
not knowing about something because you have not been told or kept uninformed about something important

Examples

in the dark about news
I was completely in the dark about the changes to the schedule.
She hates being in the dark and always asks questions until she understands.
The management kept the employees in the dark regarding the company's future plans.

Related Terms

  • antonym – informed: having knowledge of a situation or fact
  • synonym – uninformed: lacking knowledge or awareness
