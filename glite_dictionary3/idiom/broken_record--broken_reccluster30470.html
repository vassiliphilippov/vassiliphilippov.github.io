broken record

noun
someone who repeats the same thing persistently
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊkən ˈrɛkərd / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkən ˈrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
a person who keeps repeating the same thing over and over, especially to the point of being annoying

Examples

I feel like a broken record reminding you to turn off the lights every day.
She sounded like a broken record with her constant reminders about deadlines.
