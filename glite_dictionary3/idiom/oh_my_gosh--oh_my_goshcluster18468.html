oh my gosh

interjection
exclamation of surprise or disbelief
Pronunciation: / oʊ maɪ ɡɑʃ / (AmE), / əʊ maɪ ɡɒʃ / (BrE)
used to express surprise, shock, or disbelief, often as a milder alternative to 'oh my God'

Examples

oh my gosh what happened
Oh my gosh, that dress looks amazing on you.
Oh my gosh, you scared me when you walked in so quietly!
I can't believe this is happening—oh my gosh!
