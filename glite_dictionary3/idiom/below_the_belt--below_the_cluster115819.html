below the belt

adjective, adverb
unfairly or hurtfully
Pronunciation: / bəˈloʊ ðə bɛlt /; / bɪˈloʊ ðə bɛlt / (AmE), / bɪˈləʊ ðə bɛlt /; / bɪˈləʊ ðə belt / (BrE)
unfair or cruel, especially in a way that is personal or breaks accepted rules of behavior
