to grease someone's palm

verb
to bribe someone for a favor
Pronunciation: / ɡriːs ˈsʌmwʌnz pɑːm / (AmE), / ɡriːs ˈsʌmwʌnz pɑːm / (BrE)
to secretly give money to someone, especially an official, in order to persuade them to do something for you; to bribe
