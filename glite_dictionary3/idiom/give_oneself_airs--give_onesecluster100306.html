to give oneself airs

verb
act superior or pretentious
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv wʌnˈsɛlf ɛrz / (AmE), / ɡɪv wʌnˈsɛlf ɛəz / (BrE)
to behave in a way that shows you think you are more important or superior to others.

Examples

to give oneself airs at work

She tends to give herself airs whenever she's among new people.
You shouldn't give yourself airs just because of one small achievement.
