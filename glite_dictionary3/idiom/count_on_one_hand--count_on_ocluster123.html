to count on one hand

verb
have a very small number
Pronunciation: / kaʊnt ɑn wʌn hænd / (AmE), / kaʊnt ɒn wʌn hænd / (BrE)
to have a very small number of something; used to emphasize that the total is very low

Examples

count on one hand true friends
He has so few close friends that he can count on one hand the people he truly trusts.
I can count on one hand the number of times I've seen him this year.
You could count on one hand the mistakes she made during her entire career.

Related Terms

  • synonym – few: a small number; not many
  • antonym – numerous: existing in large numbers
