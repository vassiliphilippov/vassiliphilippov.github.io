afraid of one's own shadow

adjective
extremely timid or easily frightened
Pronunciation: / əˈfreɪd əv wʌnz oʊn ˈʃædoʊ / (AmE), / əˈfreɪd əv wʌnz əʊn ˈʃædəʊ / (BrE)
