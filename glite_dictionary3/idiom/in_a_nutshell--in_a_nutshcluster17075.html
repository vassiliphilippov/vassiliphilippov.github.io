in a nutshell

adverb
briefly; in summary
Pronunciation: / ɪn ə ˈnʌtʃˌɛl / (AmE), / ɪn ə ˈnʌtʃɛl / (BrE)
used to introduce a short and clear summary of something

Examples

in a nutshell summary
