shock to the system

noun
a sudden and disruptive surprise or change
Pronunciation: / ʃɑk tə ðə ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ʃɒk tə ðə ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
an event or experience that is very surprising, unpleasant, or disruptive, especially because it is sudden and unexpected

Examples

a sudden shock to the system
For many students, the transition to university life is a significant shock to the system.
His abrupt resignation was a real shock to the system for the entire company.
Returning to work after a long vacation can be a shock to the system.
