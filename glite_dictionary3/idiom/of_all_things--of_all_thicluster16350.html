of all things

x
expression of surprise or disbelief
Pronunciation: / əv ɔl θɪŋz / (AmE), / əv ɔːl θɪŋz / (BrE)
Used to express surprise or disbelief that something is unusual or unexpected among possible options.

Examples

of all things he chose
He decided, of all things, to join the circus after college.
She chose, of all things, to bring her parrot to the meeting.
We ran out of, of all things, sugar while baking the cake.
