a closed mouth gathers no feet

x
it is safer to remain silent than to risk saying something foolish
Pronunciation: / ə kloʊzd maʊθ ˈɡæðərz noʊ fiːt / (AmE), / ə kləʊzd maʊθ ˈɡæðəz nəʊ fiːt / (BrE)
remaining silent avoids making mistakes or getting into trouble; one cannot say something foolish if one does not speak
