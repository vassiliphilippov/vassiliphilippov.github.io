lost in thought

adjective
deeply absorbed in thoughts
Pronunciation: / lɔst ɪn θɔt / (AmE), / lɒst ɪn θɔːt / (BrE)
deeply absorbed or preoccupied in one's own thoughts, often to the point of being unaware of one's surroundings.

Examples

lost in thought staring out
He sat by the window, lost in thought as the rain fell outside.
She was so lost in thought that she didn't hear her phone ring.
Whenever I'm lost in thought, I lose track of time.
