king of the hill

noun
person in a dominant or top position
Pronunciation: / kɪŋ əv ðə hɪl / (AmE), / kɪŋ əv ðə hɪl / (BrE)
a person, group, or organization that is in a dominant, leading, or superior position compared to others, especially after competition
