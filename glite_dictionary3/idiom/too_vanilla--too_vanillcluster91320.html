too vanilla

adjective
too plain or ordinary
Pronunciation: / tu vəˈnɪlə / (AmE), / tuː vəˈnɪlə / (BrE)
Unimaginative, unadventurous, or lacking in excitement; too plain or ordinary.

Examples

too vanilla for me
He turned down the job offer because the work environment seemed too vanilla for his dynamic personality.
If your vacation plans are too vanilla, you might get bored quickly.
The film's plot was too vanilla to impress most movie critics.

Related Terms

  • synonym – boring: Not interesting; tedious
  • antonym – exciting: Causing great enthusiasm and eagerness
  • synonym – plain: Simple or lacking adornment; ordinary
