to light up like a Christmas tree

verb
become brightly illuminated
Pronunciation: / laɪt ʌp laɪk ə ˈkrɪs.məs ˈtri / (AmE), / laɪt ʌp laɪk ə ˈkrɪs.məs ˈtriː / (BrE)
