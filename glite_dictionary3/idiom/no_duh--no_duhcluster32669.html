no duh

interjection
sarcastic way of saying something is obvious
Pronunciation: / noʊ dʌ / (AmE), / nəʊ dʌ / (BrE)
used sarcastically to indicate that something is obvious or should have been easily understood

Examples

no duh sarcastic reply
"If you don't water the plants, they'll die?" No duh, everyone knows that.
"You have to pay for the tickets upfront?" No duh, it's standard policy here.
"You're tired because you stayed up all night?" No duh, that's what happens when you don't sleep.
