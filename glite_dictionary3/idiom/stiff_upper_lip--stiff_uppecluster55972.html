stiff upper lip

noun
emotional self-restraint in adversity
Pronunciation: / stɪf ˈʌpɚ lɪp / (AmE), / stɪf ˈʌpə lɪp / (BrE)
the ability to remain calm and not show emotions such as fear or grief in difficult situations

Examples

keep a stiff upper lip
Despite receiving the bad news, he kept a stiff upper lip and didn't let his feelings show.
She admired his ability to maintain a stiff upper lip even when things got tough.
The team's stiff upper lip impressed their coach during the tense final moments of the game.
