for shits and giggles

adverb
just for fun; for amusement
Pronunciation: / fər ʃɪts ənd ˈɡɪɡəlz / (AmE), / fə ʃɪts ənd ˈɡɪɡlz / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is done purely for fun or amusement, without any serious purpose

Examples

Sometimes, she likes to try out new recipes for shits and giggles.
We went to the amusement park for shits and giggles on a rainy afternoon.
