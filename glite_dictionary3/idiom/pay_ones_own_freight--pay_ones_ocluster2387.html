to pay one's own freight

verb
cover one's own expenses
Pronunciation: / peɪ wʌnz oʊn freɪt / (AmE), / peɪ wʌnz əʊn freɪt / (BrE)
to take responsibility for one's own expenses or costs, without relying on others

Examples

to pay one's own freight at college
After leaving home, he was determined to pay his own freight through university.
In this club, everyone is expected to pay their own freight for events and meals.
