to wake up and smell the coffee

verb
become aware of reality
Pronunciation: / weɪk ʌp ænd smɛl ðə ˈkɑːfi / (AmE), / weɪk ʌp ənd smɛl ðə ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
to become aware of an unpleasant reality or to face the facts of a situation

Examples

wake up and smell the coffee about problems

She told him to wake up and smell the coffee about his reckless spending habits.
