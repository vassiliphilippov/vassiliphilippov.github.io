nail in the coffin

noun
event leading to failure or end
Pronunciation: / neɪl ɪn ðə ˈkɑːfɪn / (AmE), / neɪl ɪn ðə ˈkɒfɪn / (BrE)
an event or action that contributes finally or decisively to the failure or end of something

Examples

the nail in the coffin for a project
Rising interest rates proved to be the nail in the coffin for the struggling housing market.
The company's huge loss this quarter was the nail in the coffin for its CEO.
When the team lost its star player, it was a nail in the coffin for their championship hopes.

  • synonym – final blow: an event or action causing the complete failure or end of something
