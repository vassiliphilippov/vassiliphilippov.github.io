on the prowl

adverb
actively searching or looking for something
Pronunciation: / ɑn ðə praʊl / (AmE), / ɒn ðə praʊl / (BrE)
actively searching or looking for something, especially with keen attention or for prey or opportunities

Examples

He has been on the prowl for interesting antiques at yard sales all summer.
Late at night, the fox was on the prowl near the chicken coop.
