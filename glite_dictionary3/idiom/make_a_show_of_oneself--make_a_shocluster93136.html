to make a show of oneself

verb
attract attention by behaving conspicuously
Pronunciation: / meɪk ə ʃoʊ əv wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / meɪk ə ʃəʊ əv wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to behave in a way that attracts attention, often causing embarrassment or disapproval

Examples
