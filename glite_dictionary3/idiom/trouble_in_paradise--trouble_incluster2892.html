trouble in paradise

noun
conflict in a seemingly happy situation
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌbəl ɪn ˈpærəˌdaɪs / (AmE), / ˈtrʌbəl ɪn ˈparədaɪs / (BrE)
An unexpected problem or conflict in an apparently perfect or happy situation, especially in a romantic relationship.

Examples

trouble in paradise after argument
Everything seemed perfect, but a recent argument suggested there was trouble in paradise.
His evasive answers hinted at trouble in paradise within their business partnership.
Rumors of trouble in paradise began after the celebrity couple stopped appearing together in public.
