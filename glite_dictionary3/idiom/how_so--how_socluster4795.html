how so

x
asking for explanation or clarification
Pronunciation: / haʊ soʊ / (AmE), / haʊ səʊ / (BrE)
used to ask someone to explain or clarify what they have just said, especially asking in what way or for what reason something is true

Examples

how so after a statement
"It's getting late," she said. "How so?" he replied, glancing at his watch.
She said the results were surprising, so I immediately asked, "How so?"
You think this plan will work? How so?
