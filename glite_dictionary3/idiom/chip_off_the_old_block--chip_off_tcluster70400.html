chip off the old block

noun
person similar to a parent
Pronunciation: / tʃɪp ɑf ði oʊld blɑk / (AmE), / tʃɪp ɒf ði əʊld blɒk / (BrE)
a person who closely resembles one of their parents in appearance, character, or abilities
