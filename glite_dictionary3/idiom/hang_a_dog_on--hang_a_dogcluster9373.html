to hang a dog on

verb
insufficient reason or evidence
Pronunciation: / hæŋ ə dɔɡ ɑn / (AmE), / hæŋ ə dɒɡ ɒn / (BrE)
(of evidence or reasons) not sufficient to justify an action or judgment; not credible or convincing.

Examples

hang a dog on weak evidence
His weak arguments were hardly enough to hang a dog on during the debate.
None of the rumors is enough to hang a dog on in this situation.
The accusations were not enough to hang a dog on, so the case was dropped.
