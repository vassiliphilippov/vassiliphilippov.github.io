as best one can

adverb
to the best of one's ability
Pronunciation: / æz bɛst wʌn kæn / (AmE), / æz bɛst wʌn kan / (BrE)
to the utmost extent of one's ability or skill; as well as one is able

Examples

as best one can manage
During the interview, he answered the questions as best one can without preparation.
She managed the difficult situation as best one can, given the circumstances.
We must often adapt to changes as best one can in an unpredictable world.

Related Terms

  • synonym – as well as one can: to the best of one's ability; as well as possible
