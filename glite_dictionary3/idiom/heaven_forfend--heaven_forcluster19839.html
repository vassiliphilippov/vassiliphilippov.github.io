to heaven forfend

verb
used to wish that something bad does not happen
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvən fərˈfɛnd / (AmE), / ˈhɛv.ən fəˈfend / (BrE)
May heaven prevent (something undesirable) from happening; used to express a wish that something bad will not occur.

Examples

heaven forfend disaster
Heaven forfend that anything should happen to the children while they are away.
Heaven forfend we ever see such a disaster again in our town.
If, heaven forfend, the project fails, we'll have to start from scratch.

Related Terms

  • synonym – God forbid: used to express a strong wish that something unpleasant will not happen
