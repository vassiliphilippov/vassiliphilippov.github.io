to powder one's nose

verb
to discreetly visit the restroom (often for grooming)
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdɚ wʌnz ˈnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə wʌnz ˈnəʊz / (BrE)
(of a woman) to go to the restroom, especially to attend to one's makeup or personal grooming, often used as a discreet or euphemistic expression.
