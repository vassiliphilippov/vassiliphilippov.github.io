waiting to happen

adjective
inevitable or imminent event
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪtɪŋ tə ˈhæpən / (AmE), / ˈweɪtɪŋ tə ˈhapən / (BrE)
very likely or certain to occur soon, often used to describe something regarded as inevitable or impending, especially a problem or event.

Examples

an accident waiting to happen
His reckless driving is an accident waiting to happen.
The financial collapse was a disaster waiting to happen.
With all those unresolved safety issues, a crisis is waiting to happen.
