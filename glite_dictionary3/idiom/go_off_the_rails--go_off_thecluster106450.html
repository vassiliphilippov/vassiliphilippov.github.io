to go off the rails

verb
become chaotic or uncontrolled
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ɑf ðə reɪlz / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ɒf ðə reɪlz / (BrE)
(of a person, project, or situation) to become chaotic or uncontrolled; to deviate from the expected or correct course.

Examples
