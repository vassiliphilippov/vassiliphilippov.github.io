full speed ahead

adverb
at maximum effort or speed; without hesitation
Pronunciation: / fʊl spiːd əˈhɛd / (AmE), / fʊl spiːd əˈhɛd / (BrE)
used to indicate proceeding with maximum effort, speed, or determination, especially after a decision has been made or an obstacle removed

Examples

go full speed ahead
Despite the challenges, the team moved full speed ahead to meet the deadline.
Once the funds were secured, the company went full speed ahead with the development of the new app.
We're going full speed ahead on this renovation project now that we have the permits.
