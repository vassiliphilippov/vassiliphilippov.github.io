on pins and needles

adverb
very nervous or anxious
Pronunciation: / ɑn pɪnz ənd ˈnidlz / (AmE), / ɒn pɪnz ənd ˈniːdlz / (BrE)
very nervous or anxious while waiting for something to happen

Examples

on pins and needles waiting
I felt on pins and needles before the interview results were announced.
She was on pins and needles waiting for her turn to speak.
They spent the whole afternoon on pins and needles until the phone finally rang.
