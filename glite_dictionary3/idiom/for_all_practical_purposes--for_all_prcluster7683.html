for all practical purposes

adverb
effectively; in most practical respects
Pronunciation: / fɔr ɔl ˈpræktɪkəl ˈpɝpəsɪz / (AmE), / fə ɔːl ˈpræktɪkl ˈpɜːpəsɪz / (BrE)
in most practical situations; effectively or virtually (even though not strictly or officially so)

Examples

for all practical purposes finished
For all practical purposes, the problem has already been solved.
His explanation was, for all practical purposes, a confession.
The roads were, for all practical purposes, impassable after the storm.
