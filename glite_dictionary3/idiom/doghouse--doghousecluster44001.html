doghouse

noun
small outdoor shelter for a dog
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɡˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A small shelter or structure built outdoors to provide protection for a dog.

Examples

a doghouse outside
After chewing the shoes, the dog was sent outside to his doghouse.
Rain started pouring, so the puppy ran into its doghouse to stay dry.
The family built a new doghouse for their pet in the backyard.
