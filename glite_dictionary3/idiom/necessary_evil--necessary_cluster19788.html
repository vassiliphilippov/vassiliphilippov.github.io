necessary evil

noun
something undesirable but unavoidable
Pronunciation: / ˌnɛsəˌsɛri ˈivəl / (AmE), / ˌnesəsəri ˈiːvəl / (BrE)
an unpleasant or undesirable thing that must be accepted or done in order to achieve a better result or avoid a worse outcome

Examples

a necessary evil in life
For many people, commuting to work is a necessary evil they cannot avoid.
Some regulations are a necessary evil to ensure public safety.
Wearing a suit to job interviews is often seen as a necessary evil by young professionals.
