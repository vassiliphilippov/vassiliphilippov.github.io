on for size

adverb
test suitability or fit
Pronunciation: / ɑn fɔr saɪz / (AmE), / ɒn fə saɪz / (BrE)
used to suggest trying or testing something to see if it is suitable or if it works well

Examples

try on for size
He applied the new strategy on for size before rolling it out company-wide.
She handed me the new gloves and told me to try them on for size.
Why not propose your idea to the group and see how it fits on for size?
