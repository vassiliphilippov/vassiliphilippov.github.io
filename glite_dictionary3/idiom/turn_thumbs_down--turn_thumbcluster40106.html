to turn thumbs down

verb
vote against; express disapproval
Pronunciation: / tɝn θʌmz daʊn / (AmE), / tɜːn θʌmz daʊn / (BrE)
to vote against or express disapproval of something or someone

Examples

to turn thumbs down on a plan
The board members turned thumbs down on the merger after reviewing the financial reports.
The committee turned thumbs down on the proposed budget increase.
When asked about extending the deadline, the supervisor turned thumbs down without hesitation.
