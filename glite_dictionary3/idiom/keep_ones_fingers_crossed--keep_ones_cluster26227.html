to keep one's fingers crossed

verb
hope for good luck or a favorable result
Pronunciation: / kip wʌnz ˈfɪŋɡɚz krɔst / (AmE), / kiːp wʌnz ˈfɪŋɡəz krɒst / (BrE)
to hope that things will happen in the way you want them to; to wish for good luck or a favorable outcome

Examples

keep one's fingers crossed for luck
I kept my fingers crossed during the whole exam, hoping I would pass.
She always keeps her fingers crossed before a big job interview.
We're waiting for the results and keeping our fingers crossed that everything goes well.
