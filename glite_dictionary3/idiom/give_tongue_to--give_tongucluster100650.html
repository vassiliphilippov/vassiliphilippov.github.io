to give tongue to

verb
express or articulate (something)
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv tʌŋ tu / (AmE), / ɡɪv tʌŋ tʊ / (BrE)
to express or articulate something, either verbally or by making a sound

Examples

to give tongue to feelings
During the meeting, he gave tongue to his concerns about the project.
Many poets have given tongue to the beauty of nature in their work.
She could no longer remain silent and finally gave tongue to her frustrations.

Related Terms

  • antonym – suppress: to hold back or refrain from expressing (a feeling or thought)
  • synonym – voice: to express something in words; to utter
