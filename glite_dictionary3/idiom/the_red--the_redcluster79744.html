the red

noun
financial loss, debts exceeding income
Pronunciation: / ðə rɛd / (AmE), / ðə rɛd / (BrE)
a situation in which a business or person is operating at a financial loss; the state of having debts or expenditures exceeding income.

Examples

The firm's new product finally pulled it out of the red and into profitability.
