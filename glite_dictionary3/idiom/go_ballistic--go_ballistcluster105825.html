to go ballistic

verb
increase very rapidly
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ bəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ɡəʊ bəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)

Celebrity coverage made interest in the charity go ballistic, with donations pouring in overnight.
