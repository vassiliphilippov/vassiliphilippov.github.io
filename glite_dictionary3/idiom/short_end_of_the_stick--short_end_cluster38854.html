short end of the stick

noun
to receive the unfair or worse outcome
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt ɛnd əv ðə stɪk / (AmE), / ʃɔːt end əv ðə stɪk / (BrE)
to receive the worse outcome in a situation or to be treated unfairly compared to others

Examples
