to throw cold water on

verb
discourage; dampen enthusiasm
Pronunciation: / θroʊ koʊld ˈwɔtɚ ɑn /; / θroʊ koʊld ˈwɔt̬ɚ ɑn / (AmE), / θrəʊ kəʊld ˈwɔːtə ɒn / (BrE)
to criticize or discourage something, making people less excited or enthusiastic about it
