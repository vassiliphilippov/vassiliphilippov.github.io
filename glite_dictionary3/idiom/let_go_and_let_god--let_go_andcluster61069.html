to let go and let God

verb
relinquish control and trust in God
Pronunciation: / lɛt ɡoʊ ənd lɛt ɡɑd / (AmE), / lɛt ɡəʊ ənd lɛt ɡɒd / (BrE)
to relinquish control of a situation and place trust in a higher power, especially during difficult times

Examples

let go and let God during trouble
After months of worry, she finally decided to let go and let God.
During the recovery meeting, members encouraged one another to let go and let God.
When life feels overwhelming, some people choose to let go and let God.
