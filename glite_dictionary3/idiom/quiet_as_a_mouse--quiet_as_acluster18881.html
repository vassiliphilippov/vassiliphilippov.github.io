quiet as a mouse

adjective
extremely quiet or silent
Pronunciation: / ˈkwaɪət əz ə maʊs / (AmE), / ˈkwaɪət əz ə maʊs / (BrE)
extremely quiet or silent, especially so as not to be noticed

Examples
