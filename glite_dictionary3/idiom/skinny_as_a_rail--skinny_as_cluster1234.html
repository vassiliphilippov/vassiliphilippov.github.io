skinny as a rail

adjective
very thin (idiom)
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪni əz ə reɪl / (AmE), / ˈskɪni əz ə reɪl / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) extremely thin.

Examples

skinny as a rail after illness
After being ill for weeks, she looked skinny as a rail when she returned to school.
He used to be chubby as a child, but now he's skinny as a rail.
The marathon runner was skinny as a rail but impressively strong.

Related Terms

  • antonym – chubby: plump or overweight, especially in a pleasant way
  • synonym – skinny: very thin, especially in an unattractive way
  • synonym – thin: having little body fat; not thick
