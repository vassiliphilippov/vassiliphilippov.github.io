to have her tubes tied

verb
undergo surgery for female sterilization
Pronunciation: / hæv hɚ tuːbz taɪd / (AmE), / hæv hə tjuːbz taɪd / (BrE)
(of a woman) to undergo a surgical procedure to become permanently sterile by having the fallopian tubes closed or blocked.
