to make the grade

verb
reach the required standard
Pronunciation: / meɪk ðə ɡreɪd / (AmE), / meɪk ðə ɡreɪd / (BrE)
to reach the necessary standard; to be good enough to succeed, especially in a particular activity or field

Examples

to make the grade in school
Despite his efforts, Tom didn’t make the grade in the final round of interviews.
If your proposal doesn’t make the grade, it will be rejected by the committee.
It takes years of training to make the grade as a professional athlete.
