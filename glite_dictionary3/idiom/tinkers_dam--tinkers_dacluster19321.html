tinker's dam

noun
something of no value
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋkərz dæm / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋkəz dam / (BrE)
A thing regarded as worthless; something of no value.

His explanation isn't worth a tinker's dam to anyone who knows the facts.
