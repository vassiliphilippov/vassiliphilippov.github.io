to hit the hay

verb
go to bed, prepare for sleep
Pronunciation: / hɪt ðə heɪ / (AmE), / hɪt ðə heɪ / (BrE)
to go to bed or prepare to sleep, especially after a long day

Examples

to hit the hay after work
After a long day at work, I'm ready to hit the hay early tonight.
If you keep studying this late, you really should hit the hay soon!
She yawned and told everyone she was going to hit the hay.

  • synonym – go to bed: to get into bed with the intention of sleeping
  • synonym – turn in: to go to bed, often at a particular time
