on the fence

adjective
undecided; not taking sides
Pronunciation: / ɑn ðə fɛns / (AmE), / ɒn ðə fɛns / (BrE)
undecided or not committed to a particular course of action or side in a dispute

Examples

on the fence about a decision
After hours of discussion, she was still on the fence about whether to accept the job offer.
I was on the fence until I saw the final presentation.
Many voters remain on the fence, waiting for more information before making a decision.
