in a way

adverb
partly; to some extent
Pronunciation: / ɪn ə weɪ / (AmE), / ɪn ə weɪ / (BrE)
to some extent; from a certain perspective or in some respects (used to express that a statement is only partly or conditionally true)

Examples

in a way possible
In a way, her decision made sense, even if it surprised everyone.
In a way, losing the job was a blessing in disguise.
He was, in a way, responsible for the situation.
