sure, story, bud

interjection
sarcastic expression of disbelief
Pronunciation: / ʃʊr ˈstɔri bʌd / (AmE), / ʃɔː ˈstɔːri bʌd / (BrE)
A sarcastic phrase used to indicate disbelief or to dismiss someone's excuse or story as unconvincing.

Examples

sure, story, bud after an excuse
After listening to his wild tale, everyone at the table laughed and said, 'sure, story, bud' in unison.
She explained the broken vase was the cat's fault, and I couldn't help but reply, 'sure, story, bud'.
When he claimed he'd forgotten his homework again, I just said, 'sure, story, bud.'
