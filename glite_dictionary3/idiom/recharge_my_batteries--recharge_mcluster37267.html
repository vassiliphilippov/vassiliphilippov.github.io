to recharge my batteries

verb
to regain energy or enthusiasm
Pronunciation: / riˈtʃɑrdʒ maɪ ˈbæt̬ɚiz / (AmE), / riːˈtʃɑːdʒ maɪ ˈbætəriz / (BrE)
to rest or do something enjoyable in order to regain energy or enthusiasm after a period of work or stress

Examples

to recharge my batteries after work
After a long week at work, I just want to recharge my batteries over the weekend.
She took a solo trip to the countryside to recharge her batteries and clear her mind.
Taking a break from social media really helped me recharge my batteries.

Related Terms

  • antonym – exhausted: very tired, lacking energy
  • synonym – rest: to cease work in order to relax or recover strength
  • synonym – unwind: to relax after stress or effort
