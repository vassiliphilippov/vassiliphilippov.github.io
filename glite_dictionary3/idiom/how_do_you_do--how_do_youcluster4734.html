how do you do

interjection
formal greeting upon first meeting
Pronunciation: / haʊ də ju du / (AmE), / haʊ djuː juː duː / (BrE)
A formal greeting used when meeting someone for the first time, not requiring a literal answer.

Examples

how do you do at introductions
In formal business meetings, people often exchange how do you do on their first encounter.
She greeted her host politely with how do you do at the party.
When introduced to the new director, he smiled and said, how do you do?
