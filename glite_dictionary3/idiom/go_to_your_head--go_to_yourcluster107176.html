to go to your head

verb
to make someone conceited or arrogant
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ tə jʊər hɛd / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə jɔː hɛd / (BrE)
(of praise, success, or power) to make someone too proud or conceited.
