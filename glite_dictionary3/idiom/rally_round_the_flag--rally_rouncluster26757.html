to rally round the flag

verb
unite in support, especially in crisis
Pronunciation: / ˈræli raʊnd ðə flæɡ / (AmE), / ˈræli raʊnd ðə flaɡ / (BrE)
to come together and show united support for a cause, leader, or country, especially in times of crisis or danger

Examples

Whenever the country faces external threats, citizens tend to rally round the flag regardless of their political views.
