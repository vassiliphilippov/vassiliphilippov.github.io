to crack a skull

verb
suffer a severe head injury
Pronunciation: / kræk ə skʌl / (AmE), / kræk ə skʌl / (BrE)
to suffer a severe injury to the head, especially one that results in a break or fracture of the skull bones

Examples

to crack a skull badly
He fell off the ladder and cracked a skull on the pavement.
If you aren’t careful while skateboarding, you might crack a skull.
The doctor said she was lucky not to crack a skull in the accident.
