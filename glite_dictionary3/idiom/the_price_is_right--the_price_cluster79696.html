the price is right

x
if the cost is acceptable or fair
Pronunciation: / ðə praɪs ɪz raɪt / (AmE), / ðə praɪs ɪz raɪt / (BrE)
used to say that something will be bought, accepted, or agreed to if the cost is acceptable or fair

Examples

the price is right for a deal
If the price is right, we can close the deal today.
I’ll consider selling the house if the price is right.
She might agree to work for us, as long as the price is right.

Related Terms

  • antonym – overpriced: costing more than it is worth
  • synonym – reasonable: fair and sensible in cost or amount
