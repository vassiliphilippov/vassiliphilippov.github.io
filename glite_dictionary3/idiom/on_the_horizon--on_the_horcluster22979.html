on the horizon

adverb
likely to happen soon; at the visible boundary
Pronunciation: / ɑn ðə həˈraɪzən / (AmE), / ɒn ðə həˈraɪzn̩ / (BrE)
likely to happen or become apparent soon; at or near the visible boundary of the sky and earth or sea.

Examples

on the horizon for change
A major breakthrough in medical research may be on the horizon.
As we walked along the beach, a tiny island appeared on the horizon.
With the end of the semester on the horizon, students are preparing for final exams.
