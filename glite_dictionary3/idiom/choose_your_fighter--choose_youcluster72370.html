to choose your fighter

verb
select a representative or competitor
Pronunciation: / tʃuz jʊr ˈfaɪtɚ / (AmE), / tʃuːz jɔː ˈfaɪtə / (BrE)
to select a person or thing, often from a list or set of options, as one's representative or competitor, especially in a competitive or comparative context
