fever swamp

noun
chaotic, confusing, misinformation-filled situation
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːvər swɑmp / (AmE), / ˈfiːvə swɒmp / (BrE)
A situation marked by confusion, hysteria, or rampant misinformation, often making it difficult to discern the truth.

Examples

political fever swamp
Her comments only added to the fever swamp surrounding the scandal.
The political climate descended into a fever swamp of rumors and conspiracy theories.
Trying to navigate the fever swamp of online misinformation can be overwhelming for many.
