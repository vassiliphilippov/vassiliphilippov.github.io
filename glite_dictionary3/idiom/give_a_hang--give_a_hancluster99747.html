to give a hang

verb
to care at all (always used negatively)
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv ə hæŋ / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə hæŋ / (BrE)
(always in the negative) To care at all; to show any concern or interest (used to emphasize indifference).

Examples

not give a hang for rules
He acts as if he doesn't give a hang about his reputation.
I don't give a hang what they say about me.
She never gave a hang about following the latest trends.
