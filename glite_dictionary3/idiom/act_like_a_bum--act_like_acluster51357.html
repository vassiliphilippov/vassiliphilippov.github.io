to act like a bum

verb
behave lazily or irresponsibly
Pronunciation: / ækt laɪk ə bʌm / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə bʌm / (BrE)
to behave in a lazy or irresponsible manner, similar to the stereotype of a bum

Examples

to act like a bum at home
His parents told him not to act like a bum and to find a job.
If you act like a bum all summer, you'll never finish your project.
She was tired of seeing her brother act like a bum around the house every day.

Related Terms

  • synonym – laze around: to be lazy and do very little
  • antonym – work hard: to put in a lot of effort or energy into something
