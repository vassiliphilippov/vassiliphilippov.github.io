to bang one's head against a brick wall

verb
make futile or frustrating efforts
Pronunciation: / bæŋ wʌnz hɛd əˈɡɛnst ə brɪk wɔl / (AmE), / bæŋ wʌnz hɛd əˈɡɛnst ə brɪk wɔːl / (BrE)
to make repeated efforts to achieve something but have no success or feel frustrated by the lack of progress

Examples

to bang one's head against a brick wall for change
After months of trying to get support, she felt like she was banging her head against a brick wall.
Explaining the new process to the team was like banging my head against a brick wall.
Whenever he tried to change company policy, it was as if he was banging his head against a brick wall.
