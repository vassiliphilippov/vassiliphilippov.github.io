x
including everything/everyone mentioned; and everything else
Pronunciation: / ənd ɔl / (AmE), / ənd ɔːl / (BrE)
used after mentioning people or things to mean 'including everyone or everything mentioned' or 'and everything else as well'

Examples

dignity and all
After telling his story, he left the room, dignity and all.
She brought her siblings, parents, and all to the picnic.
The power went out in the whole neighborhood, streetlights and all.
