prime of life

noun
peak period of one's life
Pronunciation: / praɪm əv laɪf / (AmE), / praɪm əv laɪf / (BrE)
the period in a person's life when they are at their peak in terms of strength, energy, and ability

Examples

prime of life physically strong

She felt that she was in the prime of life when she started her own company at thirty.
