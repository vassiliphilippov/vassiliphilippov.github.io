to offer a carrot

verb
provide an incentive or reward
Pronunciation: / ˈɔfər ə ˈkærət / (AmE), / ˈɒfə ə ˈkærət / (BrE)
to provide an incentive or reward to encourage someone to do something

Examples

to offer a carrot as reward
She tried to offer a carrot to her children by promising extra screen time for finishing homework.
The government may offer a carrot to businesses that hire more apprentices.
To encourage productivity, the company decided to offer a carrot in the form of a bonus.
