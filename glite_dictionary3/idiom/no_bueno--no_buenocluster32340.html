no bueno

adjective
not good; undesirable (informal)
Pronunciation: / noʊ ˈbweɪnoʊ / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈbweɪnəʊ / (BrE)
used informally to indicate that something is not good, undesirable, or unacceptable.

Examples

no bueno at all
After tasting the soup, he just shook his head and said, 'no bueno.'
I checked the weather and it's supposed to rain all weekend—no bueno for our camping trip.
The internet going down during my online exam was definitely no bueno.
