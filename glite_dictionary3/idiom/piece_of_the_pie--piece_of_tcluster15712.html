piece of the pie

noun
share of profits or benefits
Pronunciation: / pis əv ðə paɪ / (AmE), / piːs əv ðə paɪ / (BrE)
a share or portion of something valuable, especially profits or benefits that are being distributed

Examples

get a piece of the pie
After the company went public, many employees hoped for a piece of the pie.
He worked hard so that he could claim his piece of the pie when the profits were divided.
Negotiators demanded a fair piece of the pie for each community involved.
