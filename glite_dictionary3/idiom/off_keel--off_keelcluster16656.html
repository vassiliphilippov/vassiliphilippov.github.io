off keel

adjective
not stable or balanced
Pronunciation: / ɑf kil / (AmE), / ɒf kiːl / (BrE)
not stable or balanced, either emotionally or physically; unsteady or unsettled.

Examples

feel off keel after travel
After the long flight, I felt completely off keel for hours.
The old boat was listing badly and seemed off keel on the water.
The unexpected news left him emotionally off keel.
