in hell

adverb
in a state of extreme discomfort or suffering
Pronunciation: / ɪn hɛl / (AmE), / ɪn hɛl / (BrE)
experiencing extreme discomfort, distress, or suffering, often used figuratively.

Examples

She described her recovery from the illness as being in hell for weeks.
With deadlines piling up and no help, he said he was in hell at work.
