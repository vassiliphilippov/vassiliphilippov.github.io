to blow chunks

verb
to vomit forcefully (slang)
Pronunciation: / bloʊ tʃʌŋks / (AmE), / bləʊ tʃʌŋks / (BrE)
(informal, slang) To vomit with force, often as a result of nausea or illness.

Examples

to blow chunks after eating
After seeing the gruesome movie scene, he had to blow chunks in the bathroom.
If you eat twelve hot dogs in five minutes, you might end up blowing chunks.
She felt so seasick on the boat that she nearly blew chunks.
