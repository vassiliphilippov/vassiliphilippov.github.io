trip to the woodshed

noun
punishment or strong reprimand
Pronunciation: / trɪp tə ðə ˈwʊdʃɛd / (AmE), / trɪp tə ðə ˈwʊdʃɛd / (BrE)
an occasion when someone is punished or strongly reprimanded, often privately

Examples

trip to the woodshed for mistakes

Her careless mistake led to an unexpected trip to the woodshed from her supervisor.
When the team lost the game, the coach gave them a real trip to the woodshed in the locker room.
