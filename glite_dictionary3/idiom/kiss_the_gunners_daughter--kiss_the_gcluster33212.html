to kiss the gunner's daughter

verb
naval euphemism for punishment by flogging
Pronunciation: / kɪs ðə ˈɡʌnɚz ˈdɔtər / (AmE), / kɪs ðə ˈɡʌnəz ˈdɔːtə / (BrE)
(historical) To undergo corporal punishment, especially in the navy, by being bent over a cannon and flogged.
