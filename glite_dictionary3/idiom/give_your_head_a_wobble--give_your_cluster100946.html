to give your head a wobble

verb
reconsider one's thoughts or actions (British, informal idiom)
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv jɔr hɛd ə ˈwɑbəl / (AmE), / ɡɪv jɔː hɛd ə ˈwɒbəl / (BrE)
used to tell someone to reconsider or rethink their ideas, often because they are being unreasonable or illogical

Examples

give your head a wobble after a silly idea
Before making such a ridiculous accusation, maybe give your head a wobble.
I told her to give her head a wobble and stop overreacting.
If you believe that story, you need to give your head a wobble and think again.
