to feel free

verb
used to permit or encourage without hesitation
Pronunciation: / fil fri / (AmE), / fiːl friː / (BrE)
used to tell someone that they can do something without needing to ask permission or without worrying

Examples

feel free to ask questions
Guests should feel free to use the facilities during their stay.
If you have any questions, feel free to ask me at any time.
Please feel free to take another brochure if you'd like.

Related Terms

  • synonym – go ahead: to proceed or do something, often after permission is given
