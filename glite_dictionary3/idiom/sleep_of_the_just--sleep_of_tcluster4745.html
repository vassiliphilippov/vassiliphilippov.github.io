sleep of the just

noun
peaceful, undisturbed sleep (deserved by the just)
Pronunciation: / sliːp əv ðə dʒʌst / (AmE), / sliːp əv ðə dʒʌst / (BrE)
a peaceful and undisturbed sleep, especially that which is thought to be deserved by a person who has lived a good life
