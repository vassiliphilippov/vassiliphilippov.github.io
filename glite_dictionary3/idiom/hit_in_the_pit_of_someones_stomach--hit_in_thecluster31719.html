hit in the pit of someone's stomach

x
sudden shock or anxiety felt physically
Pronunciation: / hɪt ɪn ðə pɪt əv ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈstʌmək / (AmE), / hɪt ɪn ðə pɪt əv ˈsʌmwənz ˈstʌmək / (BrE)
(of news or an event) to cause a sudden strong feeling of fear, anxiety, or shock, felt physically in one's stomach.

Examples
