to say no more

verb
stop speaking, add nothing more
Pronunciation: / seɪ noʊ mɔr / (AmE), / seɪ nəʊ mɔː / (BrE)
to stop talking, especially because further explanation is unnecessary or would not be appropriate

Examples

say no more during conversation
Sensing the awkwardness, she said no more and quietly left the room.
She realized her mistake and said no more during the meeting.
When the secret was revealed, he simply shrugged and said no more.

Related Terms

  • antonym – continue talking: keep speaking or elaborating
  • synonym – fall silent: suddenly stop speaking
