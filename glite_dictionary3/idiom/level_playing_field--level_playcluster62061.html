level playing field

noun
fair and equal conditions for competition
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvəl ˈpleɪɪŋ fiːld / (AmE), / ˈlɛv(ə)l ˈpleɪɪŋ fiːld / (BrE)
a situation in which everyone has the same fair chance to succeed, especially in competition

Examples

a level playing field in business
Government subsidies help create a level playing field for local businesses competing with international firms.
The new regulations will ensure a level playing field for small and large companies alike.
We must work together to build a level playing field in the job market.

Related Terms

  • synonym – equal opportunity: the principle that all people should have the same chances
  • antonym – unfair advantage: a situation where one competitor has an advantage over others
