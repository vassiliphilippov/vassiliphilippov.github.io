for chrissake

interjection
expression of frustration or urgency
Pronunciation: / fər krɪˈseɪk / (AmE), / fə krɪˈseɪk / (BrE)
used to express irritation, frustration, or urgency, often for emphasis

Examples

for chrissake, hurry up
For chrissake, just answer the question already!
Could you close the window, for chrissake, it's freezing in here.
I'm late, for chrissake, so please hurry up.
