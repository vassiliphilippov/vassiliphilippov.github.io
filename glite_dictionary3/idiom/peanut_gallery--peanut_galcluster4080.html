peanut gallery

noun
cheapest seats in a theater, upper balcony
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːnʌt ˌɡæləri / (AmE), / ˈpiːnʌt ˌɡæləri / (BrE)
the cheapest and usually most distant seats in a theater, located in the upper section of the balcony

Examples

from the peanut gallery seats
During the play, noisy comments came from the peanut gallery high above the stage.
Seats in the peanut gallery were the only ones we could afford for the concert.
The comedian joked about hecklers in the peanut gallery disrupting his performance.
