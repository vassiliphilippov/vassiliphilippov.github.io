tip of the hat

noun
gesture of respect or acknowledgment
Pronunciation: / tɪp əv ðə hæt / (AmE), / tɪp əv ðə hæt / (BrE)
an act or expression of respect or acknowledgment, originally referring to the gesture of touching or lifting one's hat

Examples

tip of the hat gesture
As a tip of the hat, the company honored its longest-serving employees at the annual dinner.
His kind words were a tip of the hat to the team's collective effort.
Let me give a tip of the hat to everyone who helped make this project a success.

Related Terms

  • synonym – doff one's hat: to remove or raise one's hat as a sign of respect
  • synonym – nod: a brief downward movement of the head, used as a greeting or sign of acknowledgment
