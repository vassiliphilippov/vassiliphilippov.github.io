to get stuffed

verb
rude way to tell someone to go away
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt stʌft / (AmE), / ɡɛt stʌft / (BrE)
(British, informal) Used to rudely tell someone to go away or reject their request, often in response to annoyance or rudeness.

Examples

get stuffed angrily
If he expects me to work late again, he can just get stuffed.
She rolled her eyes and told her ex-boyfriend to get stuffed after he tried to apologize.
When Tom insulted her, she snapped, "Get stuffed!" and walked away.
