without batting an eye

adverb
without surprise or hesitation
Pronunciation: / wɪðˈaʊt ˈbætɪŋ ən aɪ / (AmE), / wɪðˈaʊt ˈbatɪŋ ən aɪ / (BrE)
Without showing any surprise, concern, or hesitation.

Examples

without batting an eye at bad news
He delivered the bad news without batting an eye and remained completely calm.
She offered to help, without batting an eye, even though it was a big inconvenience.
The lawyer handled the shocking evidence without batting an eye during the trial.
