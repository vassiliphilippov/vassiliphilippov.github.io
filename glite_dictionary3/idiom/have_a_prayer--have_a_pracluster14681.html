to have a prayer

verb
have any chance of success
Pronunciation: / hæv ə prɛr / (AmE), / hav ə prɛə / (BrE)
to have any possibility or chance of success, especially when the odds are very low

Examples

have a prayer of winning
Despite his experience, he didn't have a prayer of winning the competition.
If the weather turns bad, the hikers won't have a prayer of reaching the summit.
Without a good lawyer, she doesn't have a prayer in court.

  • synonym – stand a chance: to have any likelihood of success
