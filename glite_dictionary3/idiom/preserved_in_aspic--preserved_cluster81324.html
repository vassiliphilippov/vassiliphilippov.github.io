preserved in aspic

adjective
unchanged or stagnant; frozen in time
Pronunciation: / pɚˈzɝvd ɪn ˈæspɪk / (AmE), / pəˈzɜːvd ɪn ˈæspɪk / (BrE)
(of something) kept unchanged or stagnant, often with a sense of being frozen in time or resistant to change.

Examples

preserved in aspic traditions
In her writing, Victorian manners are preserved in aspic for modern readers to observe.
Many rural customs seem preserved in aspic, hardly altered over the decades.
The company's policies are so old-fashioned, they're practically preserved in aspic.
