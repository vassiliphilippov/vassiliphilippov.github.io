like assholes

x
everyone has one; commonality idiom (vulgar)
Pronunciation: / laɪk ˈæshoʊlz / (AmE), / laɪk ˈæshəʊlz / (BrE)
Used in similes, typically in the phrase "opinions are like assholes; everyone has one", to highlight that something is common to all people, often implying that the things referred to (e.g., opinions) may be unwelcome or not unique.

Examples

opinions are like assholes
Don't be offended—she was just quoting that old line about how advice is like assholes; everyone has one.
He always says, 'Excuses are like assholes; everyone has one.'
The saying goes that opinions are like assholes, meaning we all have them and they're not always welcome.
