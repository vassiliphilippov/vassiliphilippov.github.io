to maintain the high ground

verb
keep a position of advantage or superiority
Pronunciation: / meɪnˈteɪn ðə haɪ ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / meɪnˈteɪn ðə haɪ ɡraʊnd / (BrE)

Examples

It can be difficult to maintain the high ground when others are being rude.
