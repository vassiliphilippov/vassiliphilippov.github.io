to write home about

verb
to be noteworthy or remarkable (often used negatively)
Pronunciation: / raɪt hoʊm əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / raɪt həʊm əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to be particularly noteworthy or remarkable; usually used in negative constructions to mean something is unremarkable or not impressive
