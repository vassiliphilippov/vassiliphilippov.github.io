fly in the ointment

noun
minor flaw that mars something good
Pronunciation: / flaɪ ɪn ði ˈɔɪntmənt / (AmE), / flaɪ ɪn ði ˈɔɪntmənt / (BrE)
a small problem or flaw that spoils or reduces the enjoyment of an otherwise good situation
