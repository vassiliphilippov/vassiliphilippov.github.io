to fly the flag

verb
show support or pride (often by displaying a flag)
Pronunciation: / flaɪ ðə flæɡ / (AmE), / flaɪ ðə flæɡ / (BrE)
to show support or pride for a country, group, or cause, sometimes by displaying its flag

Examples

fly the flag for your country
Every year, the town flies the flag for local artists by hosting a festival.
Fans began flying the flag after their team qualified for the finals.
She made sure to fly the flag for her country during the international conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym – show the flag: to make a public display of support, especially officially or ceremoniously
