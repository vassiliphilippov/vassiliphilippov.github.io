by the moment

adverb
increasing or changing rapidly with time
Pronunciation: / baɪ ðə ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / baɪ ðə ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
used to describe something that is increasing or changing rapidly and continuously as time passes

Examples

by the moment increasing
Her excitement grew by the moment as the concert approached.
The snow was piling up by the moment, covering the streets faster than expected.
Their worries seemed to fade by the moment as good news kept arriving.
