to crack a nut

verb
use excessive means for something simple
Pronunciation: / kræk ə nʌt / (AmE), / kræk ə nʌt / (BrE)
to use disproportionately powerful means to accomplish a simple or minor task

Examples

crack a nut with a hammer
Bringing in a global consultancy just to fix the website is really cracking a nut.
Building such a massive fence to keep out rabbits is like cracking a nut.
Hiring a lawyer over a minor disagreement seems like cracking a nut.
