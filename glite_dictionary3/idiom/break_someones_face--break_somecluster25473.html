to break someone's face

verb
threaten to physically injure someone's face
Pronunciation: / breɪk sʌmwʌnz feɪs / (AmE), / breɪk sʌmwʌnz feɪs / (BrE)
(informal) to threaten to physically attack and injure someone's face, often as an expression of anger or intimidation.

Examples

to break his face in anger
If you ever lie to me again, I swear I'll break your face.
She shouted at the intruder, warning she would break his face if he took another step.
The bully threatened to break his face if he didn't leave immediately.
