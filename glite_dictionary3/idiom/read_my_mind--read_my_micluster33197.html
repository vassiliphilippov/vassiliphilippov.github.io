to read my mind

verb
intuitively know someone's thoughts
Pronunciation: / ɹid maɪ maɪnd / (AmE), / ɹiːd maɪ maɪnd / (BrE)
to intuitively know or accurately guess what someone is thinking or feeling, often without being told

Examples

It's amazing how you can read my mind and know exactly what I want for my birthday.
Sometimes, I feel like my best friend can read my mind because she always understands how I feel.
