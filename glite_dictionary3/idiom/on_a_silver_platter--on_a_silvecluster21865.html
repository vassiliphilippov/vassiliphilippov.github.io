Pronunciation: / ɑn ə ˈsɪlvɚ ˈplætɚ / (AmE), / ɒn ə ˈsɪlvə ˈplætə / (BrE)
If something is given to you on a silver platter, you receive it very easily or without having to make any effort.

Examples

opportunity on a silver platter
She didn't want opportunities served on a silver platter; she preferred to earn them through hard work.
The inheritance was delivered to him on a silver platter without any struggle.
They expected the answers to be handed on a silver platter instead of researching themselves.
