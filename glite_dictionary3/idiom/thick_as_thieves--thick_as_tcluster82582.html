thick as thieves

adjective
very close or secretive friends
Pronunciation: / θɪk əz θivz / (AmE), / θɪk əz θiːvz / (BrE)
(of people) very close or friendly with each other, often sharing secrets or collaborating closely.

Examples

thick as thieves with her
Despite their differences, the sisters are thick as thieves and tell each other everything.
The old teammates remain thick as thieves long after their playing days ended.
Those two have been thick as thieves ever since they met at college.
