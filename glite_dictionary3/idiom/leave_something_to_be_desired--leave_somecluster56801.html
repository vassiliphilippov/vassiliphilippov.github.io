to leave something to be desired

verb
to be unsatisfactory or inadequate
Pronunciation: / liv ˈsʌmθɪŋ tə bi dɪˈzaɪrd / (AmE), / liːv ˈsʌmθɪŋ tə biː dɪˈzaɪəd / (BrE)
to be not as good as it should be; to be unsatisfactory or inadequate in some way

Examples

leave something to be desired in quality
Although the design is creative, the product's durability left something to be desired.
His performance in the interview leaves something to be desired, so he may need more practice.
The food at the banquet left something to be desired, as many guests complained about its taste.

Related Terms

  • antonym – adequate: good enough in quality, quantity, or degree
  • synonym – unsatisfactory: not good enough, not meeting expectations
