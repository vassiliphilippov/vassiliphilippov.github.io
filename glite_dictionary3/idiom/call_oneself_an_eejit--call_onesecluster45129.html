to call oneself an eejit

verb
to call oneself foolish; Irish slang expression
Pronunciation: / kɔl wʌnˈsɛlf ən ˈiːdʒɪt / (AmE), / kɔːl wʌnˈsɛlf ən ˈiːdʒɪt / (BrE)
to admit or describe oneself as being foolish or stupid, especially in a self-deprecating or humorous way (chiefly Irish slang)

Examples

to call oneself an eejit jokingly
After locking his keys in the car, he called himself an eejit and laughed it off.
She called herself an eejit for trusting the obvious email scam.
They called themselves eejits for forgetting to double-check the tickets before leaving.
