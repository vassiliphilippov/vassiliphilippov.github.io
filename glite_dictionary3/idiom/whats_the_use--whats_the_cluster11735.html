what's the use

x
expression of futility or lack of purpose
Pronunciation: / wʌts ðə jus / (AmE), / wɒts ðə juːs / (BrE)
used to express the belief that there is no point or benefit in doing something, often implying it is futile or hopeless

Examples

what's the use of trying
What's the use of arguing if no one is willing to listen?
He wondered what's the use of working so hard if he never gets ahead.
She sighed and said, 'what's the use?' as she gave up trying to fix the machine.
