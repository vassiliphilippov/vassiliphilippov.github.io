for the road

noun
a drink before leaving
Pronunciation: / fər ðə roʊd / (AmE), / fə ðə rəʊd / (BrE)
a final alcoholic drink taken before departing, especially at the end of a social gathering

Examples

a drink for the road
A quick gin and tonic for the road was just what she needed before the journey.
He poured himself a whiskey for the road after saying goodbye to everyone.
We decided to have one for the road before heading home.
