pardon me

interjection
polite expression for interrupting or apologizing
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrdn ˌmi / (AmE), / ˈpɑːdn ˌmiː / (BrE)
Used as a polite way of attracting someone's attention, or apologizing for interrupting or for a minor offense.

Examples

pardon me for interrupting
Pardon me, I didn't catch what you just said.
Pardon me, could you tell me what time it is?
She said, "pardon me" before asking her question.
