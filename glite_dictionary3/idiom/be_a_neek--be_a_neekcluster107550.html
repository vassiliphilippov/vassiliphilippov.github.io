to be a neek

verb
to act socially awkward or uncool
Pronunciation: / bi ə nik / (AmE), / biː ə niːk / (BrE)
to behave in a way that is socially awkward or uncool, especially by being overly studious or oblivious to popular trends

Examples

to be a neek at school
He didn't want to be a neek, so he went to the party.
If you don't relax a little, people will say you be a neek.
Stop acting like you be a neek and just join in the game!

Related Terms

  • antonym – cool: fashionable or socially accepted in a relaxed way
  • synonym – geek: a person who is very knowledgeable and enthusiastic about a subject, often seen as socially awkward
  • synonym – nerd: a person who is very interested in academic subjects, often seen as socially awkward
