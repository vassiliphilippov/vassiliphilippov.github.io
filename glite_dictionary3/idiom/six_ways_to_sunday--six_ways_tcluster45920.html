six ways to Sunday

adverb
in every possible way
Pronunciation: / sɪks weɪz tu ˈsʌn.deɪ / (AmE), / sɪks weɪz tʊ ˈsʌn.deɪ / (BrE)
in every possible way; by all conceivable methods or means.

Examples

checked six ways to Sunday
His plans were scrutinized six ways to Sunday by the review committee.
She checked the data six ways to Sunday before presenting her findings to the board.
The lawyer had prepared her argument six ways to Sunday to anticipate every possible objection.
