stuck on oneself

adjective
conceited or self-absorbed
Pronunciation: / stʌk ɑn wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / stʌk ɒn wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
Having an excessively high opinion of oneself; being conceited or self-absorbed.

Examples

always stuck on oneself
He was so stuck on oneself that he barely noticed anyone else in the room.
It's hard to work with someone who is always stuck on oneself.
People who are stuck on oneself often have difficulty accepting criticism.
