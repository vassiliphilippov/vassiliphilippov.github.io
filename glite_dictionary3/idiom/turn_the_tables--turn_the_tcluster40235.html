to turn the tables

verb
reverse a situation to gain advantage
Pronunciation: / tɝn ðə ˈteɪ.bəlz / (AmE), / tɜːn ðə ˈteɪ.bəlz / (BrE)
to reverse a situation so that you gain the advantage over someone who previously had the advantage
