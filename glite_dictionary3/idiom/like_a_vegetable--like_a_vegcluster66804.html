like a vegetable

adverb
unresponsive or inactive state
Pronunciation: / laɪk ə ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of a person) inactive, unresponsive, or showing little mental or physical activity, often as if unable to think or act.

Examples

sit like a vegetable
After his illness, he just sat like a vegetable in his chair all day.
Don't spend the whole weekend like a vegetable when you could be outside.
She felt drained and barely responsive, lying on the couch like a vegetable.
