to sting like a bee

verb
hurt or criticize sharply and intensely
Pronunciation: / stɪŋ laɪk ə bi / (AmE), / stɪŋ laɪk ə biː / (BrE)
to hurt, attack, or criticize sharply and intensely, often in a sudden or forceful way

Examples

to sting like a bee with words
Her clever remarks can really sting like a bee if you're not careful.
His criticism stings like a bee, making people think twice before speaking out.
The comedian's jokes stung like a bee and left the audience speechless.
