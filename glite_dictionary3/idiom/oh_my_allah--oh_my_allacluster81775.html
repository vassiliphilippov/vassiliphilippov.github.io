Oh my Allah

interjection
expression of surprise or disbelief
Pronunciation: / oʊ maɪ əˈlɑː / (AmE), / əʊ maɪ əˈlɑː / (BrE)
used to express surprise, shock, or disbelief, especially by speakers who prefer to refer to Allah instead of God

Examples

Oh my Allah what happened
Oh my Allah, I can't believe how much you've grown since I last saw you!
Oh my Allah, did you see the way he just disappeared around the corner?
When she won the award, she exclaimed, "Oh my Allah, this is such an honor!"
