to make it snappy

verb
do something quickly
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪk ɪt ˈsnæpi / (AmE), / ˈmeɪk ɪt ˈsnæpi / (BrE)
used to tell someone to do something quickly, without delay

Examples

The coach shouted, 'Make it snappy!' as the players got ready to leave.
When ordering at the counter, she told the server to make it snappy as she was in a hurry.
