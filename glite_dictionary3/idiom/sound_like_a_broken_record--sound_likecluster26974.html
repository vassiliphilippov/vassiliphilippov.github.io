to sound like a broken record

verb
repeat oneself annoyingly
Pronunciation: / saʊnd laɪk ə ˈbroʊkən ˈrɛkərd / (AmE), / saʊnd laɪk ə ˈbrəʊkən ˈrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
to keep repeating the same thing, especially a complaint or opinion, to the point of annoyance

Examples

to sound like a broken record complaining
I don't want to sound like a broken record, but could you please turn off the lights when you leave?
If I sound like a broken record, it's only because this is important.
She sounded like a broken record complaining about her job every day.
