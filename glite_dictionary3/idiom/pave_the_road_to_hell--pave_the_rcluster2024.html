to pave the road to hell

verb
cause negative outcomes through misguided actions
Pronunciation: / peɪv ðə roʊd tuː hɛl / (AmE), / peɪv ðə rəʊd tuː hɛl / (BrE)
to take actions that inadvertently lead to negative or disastrous outcomes, often as a result of negligence or misguided intentions

Examples

to pave the road to hell with good intentions
Ignoring environmental concerns now may pave the road to hell for future generations.
Some argue that strict regulations can pave the road to hell by creating even bigger problems.
Trying to cover up the mistake will only pave the road to hell for the company.
