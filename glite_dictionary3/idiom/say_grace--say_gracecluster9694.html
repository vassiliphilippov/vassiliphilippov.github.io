to say grace

verb
offer a prayer before eating
Pronunciation: / seɪ ɡreɪs / (AmE), / seɪ ɡreɪs / (BrE)
to recite a short prayer of thanks before a meal

Examples

It is their tradition to say grace before every meal, no matter where they are.
