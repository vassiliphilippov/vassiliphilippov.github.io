to whip up a storm

verb
prepare food quickly or cause excitement/commotion
Pronunciation: / wɪp ʌp ə stɔrm / (AmE), / wɪp ʌp ə stɔːm / (BrE)
to prepare something, especially food, quickly and skillfully, or to cause excitement, commotion, or strong feeling in a situation
