to act like a gadgie

verb
behave in a boastful or annoying way
Pronunciation: / ækt laɪk ə ˈɡædʒi / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈɡædʒi / (BrE)
to behave in a boastful or annoying manner, especially in a way associated with the stereotype of a 'gadgie'

Examples

to act like a gadgie loudly
He was acting like a gadgie at the party, bragging about his new car.
Please don't act like a gadgie in front of my friends; it's embarrassing.
She accused him of acting like a gadgie whenever he gets a little attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym – boast: to talk with excessive pride about one's achievements
  • antonym – down to earth: practical and unpretentious in manner
  • synonym – show off: to behave in a way that is intended to attract attention or admiration, often annoyingly
