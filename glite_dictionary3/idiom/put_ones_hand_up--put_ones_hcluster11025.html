to put one's hand up

verb
volunteer or signal by raising hand
Pronunciation: / pʊt wan(z) hænd ʌp / (AmE), / pʊt wʌnz hænd ʌp / (BrE)
to raise one's hand to volunteer or indicate willingness to participate, especially in a group setting

Examples
