to live large

verb
enjoy life extravagantly or luxuriously
Pronunciation: / lɪv lɑrdʒ / (AmE), / lɪv lɑːdʒ / (BrE)
to enjoy life in an extravagant, luxurious, or flamboyant manner; to spend freely and indulge in comforts or pleasures

Examples

to live large in luxury
After retiring, he decided to live large and travel the world in style.
Many celebrities live large with luxury cars and expensive homes.
She won the jackpot and immediately began living large in Las Vegas.

Related Terms

  • synonym – live it up: enjoy oneself in a lively or extravagant way
  • antonym – live modestly: to live with restraint and without extravagance
