to scream new money

verb
display wealth very conspicuously
Pronunciation: / skrim nuː ˈmʌni / (AmE), / skriːm njuː ˈmʌni / (BrE)
to display wealth in a way that is very obvious and flashy, often associated with people who have recently acquired wealth

Examples

scream new money with style
Everything about this lavish mansion screams new money rather than understated elegance.
Her designer clothes and oversized jewelry absolutely scream new money at every event she attends.
The gold-plated sports car outside his house really screams new money.

Related Terms

  • synonym – nouveau riche: recently acquired wealth; a person who has recently become rich, often perceived as lacking refinement
