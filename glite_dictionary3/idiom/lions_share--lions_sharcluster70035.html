lion's share

noun
largest part or portion
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪənz ʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈlaɪənz ʃɛː / (BrE)
the largest part or portion of something, especially when distributed among several people or groups

The company secured the lion's share of the government contract.
