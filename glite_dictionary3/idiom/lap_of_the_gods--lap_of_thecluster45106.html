lap of the gods

noun
outside human control; up to fate
Pronunciation: / læp əv ðə ɡɑdz / (AmE), / læp əv ðə ɡɒdz / (BrE)
used to say that something is beyond human control or depends on fate or chance.

Examples

The outcome of the match was left in the lap of the gods as neither team could score.
Whether their investment pays off is now in the lap of the gods.
