to go belly up

verb
fail or go bankrupt (idiom)
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ˌbɛli ˈʌp / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˌbɛli ˈʌp / (BrE)
(of a business or project) to fail completely or go bankrupt.

Examples

go belly up financially

Many startups go belly up within the first five years.
The company went belly up after several bad investments.
