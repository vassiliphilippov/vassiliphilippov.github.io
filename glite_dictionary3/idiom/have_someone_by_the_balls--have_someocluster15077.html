to have someone by the balls

verb
have complete power over someone
Pronunciation: / hæv ˈsʌmwʌn baɪ ðə bɔlz / (AmE), / hæv ˈsʌmwən baɪ ðə bɔːlz / (BrE)
to have complete power over someone, especially by controlling something they need or fear

Examples

have him by the balls completely
After the audit, the tax authorities had him by the balls and he had to comply with all their demands.
Once she discovered his secret, she really had him by the balls in negotiations.
The company has its competitors by the balls thanks to its exclusive patent.

Related Terms

  • synonym – have someone over a barrel: to have someone in a helpless or powerless position
