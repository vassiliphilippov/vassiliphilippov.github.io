say no more

x
expression meaning 'no further explanation needed'
Pronunciation: / seɪ noʊ mɔr / (AmE), / seɪ nəʊ mɔː / (BrE)
used to indicate that no further explanation or details are necessary, often because the listener understands or agrees

Examples

say no more to show understanding
If you're tired, say no more and I'll drive you home.
When she mentioned the deadline, he nodded and replied, "Say no more."
You want to leave early tonight? Say no more—I'll cover for you.

Related Terms

  • synonym – enough said: expression meaning that everything necessary has been stated
  • synonym – say less: informal expression used to indicate understanding without need for more details
