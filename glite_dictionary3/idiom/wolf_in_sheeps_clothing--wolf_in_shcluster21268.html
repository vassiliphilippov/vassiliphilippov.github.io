wolf in sheep's clothing

noun
deceptive person hiding harmful intent
Pronunciation: / wʊlf ɪn ʃips ˈkloʊðɪŋ / (AmE), / wʊlf ɪn ʃiːps ˈkləʊðɪŋ / (BrE)
a person who pretends to be harmless or friendly but is actually dangerous or deceitful

Examples

a wolf in sheep's clothing hides danger
He appeared trustworthy, but he was really a wolf in sheep's clothing all along.
The committee warned citizens to beware of any wolf in sheep's clothing trying to take advantage of them.
You need to be cautious in business; sometimes, a competitor can be a wolf in sheep's clothing.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deceiver: a person who causes others to believe something that is not true
  • antonym – friend: a person whom one knows and with whom one has a bond of mutual affection, typically contrasted with a wolf in sheep's clothing
  • synonym – impostor: person pretending to be someone else to deceive
