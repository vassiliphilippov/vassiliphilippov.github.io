dismissive phrase about self-justification
Pronunciation: / wəˈtɛvɚ hɛlps ju slip æt naɪt / (AmE), / wəˈtɛvə hɛlps ju sliːp ət naɪt / (BrE)
used to indicate that someone can justify their actions however they want, even if others do not agree or believe them, often in a dismissive or skeptical way
