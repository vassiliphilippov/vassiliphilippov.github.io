to mark your calendar

verb
make a note of a date
Pronunciation: / mɑrk jʊr ˈkæl.ən.dɚ / (AmE), / mɑːk jɔː ˈkæl.ən.də / (BrE)
to make a note of a date, especially to remember an event or appointment in the future

Examples

mark your calendar for an event
Don't forget to mark your calendar for the annual company picnic next Friday.
She reminded everyone to mark your calendar for the upcoming holiday sale.
You should mark your calendar so you don't miss the start of the new semester.
