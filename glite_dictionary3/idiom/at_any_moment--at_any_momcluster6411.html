at any moment

adverb
imminently; at any time
Pronunciation: / ət ˈɛni ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ət ˈɛni ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
at a time that could be very soon; at any time, especially unpredictably

Examples

at any moment danger arrives
A storm could develop at any moment, so we should be prepared to leave.
He was nervous, knowing the boss might call at any moment.
The train could arrive at any moment, so please wait on the platform.
