to sell like hot cakes

verb
be sold very quickly
Pronunciation: / sɛl laɪk hɑt keɪks / (AmE), / sɛl laɪk hɒt keɪks / (BrE)
to be bought quickly and in large quantities; to be extremely popular or in high demand

Examples

Homemade jam from that shop tends to sell like hot cakes during the holidays.
The new smartphone is expected to sell like hot cakes once it’s released.
Tickets for the concert sold like hot cakes and were gone within an hour.
