to grab life by the balls

verb
act boldly and take control of life
Pronunciation: / ɡræb laɪf baɪ ðə bɔlz / (AmE), / ɡræb laɪf baɪ ðə bɔːlz / (BrE)
to act decisively and boldly in dealing with challenges or opportunities, taking control of one's life and embracing risks

Examples

grab life by the balls fearlessly
After years of hesitation, he finally chose to grab life by the balls and move abroad.
If you want to make a real change, you have to grab life by the balls and go for it.
She decided it was time to grab life by the balls and start her own business.
