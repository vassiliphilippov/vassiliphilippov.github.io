to learn one's lesson

verb
gain wisdom from a mistake
Pronunciation: / lɝn wʌnz ˈlɛsən /; / lɝːn wʌnz ˈlɛs.ən / (AmE), / lɜːn wʌnz ˈlɛsən /; / lɜːn wʌnz ˈlɛs.ən / (BrE)
to understand from a bad experience or mistake and change your behavior to avoid repeating it

Examples

to learn one's lesson after a mistake
