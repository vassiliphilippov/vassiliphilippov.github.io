sacred cow

noun
person or thing immune to criticism
Pronunciation: / ˌseɪ.krɪd ˈkaʊ /; / ˈseɪkrəd kaʊ / (AmE), / ˌseɪ.krɪd ˈkaʊ /; / ˈseɪkrɪd kaʊ / (BrE)
a person, thing, belief, or practice that is considered too important to be criticized or questioned

Examples
