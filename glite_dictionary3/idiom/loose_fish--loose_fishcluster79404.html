loose fish

noun
person who doesn't fit in
Pronunciation: / lus fɪʃ / (AmE), / luːs fɪʃ / (BrE)
a person who feels out of place or does not conform to the norms of a group or environment

Examples

a loose fish in a crowd
After moving to the new city, Jenna often felt like a loose fish at social gatherings.
He remained a loose fish throughout high school, never quite blending in with any clique.
Sometimes being a loose fish allows you to see things differently from the rest of the crowd.

Related Terms

  • antonym – conformist: a person who conforms to accepted behavior or established practices
  • synonym – misfit: a person who does not fit in with others
  • synonym – outsider: a person who does not belong to a particular group
