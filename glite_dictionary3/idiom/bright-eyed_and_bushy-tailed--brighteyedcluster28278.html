bright-eyed and bushy-tailed

adjective
energetic and enthusiastic (idiom)
Pronunciation: / ˌbraɪt ˌaɪd ənd ˈbʊʃi teɪld / (AmE), / ˌbraɪt ˌaɪd ənd ˈbʊʃi teɪld / (BrE)
Full of energy and enthusiasm, especially in the morning or when starting something new.

Examples

bright-eyed and bushy-tailed at breakfast
After a restful night, the children were bright-eyed and bushy-tailed at breakfast.
Despite the early hour, he looked bright-eyed and bushy-tailed before the meeting.
She arrived bright-eyed and bushy-tailed for her first day at work.
