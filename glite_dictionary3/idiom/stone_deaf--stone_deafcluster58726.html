stone deaf

adjective
completely unwilling to listen or unable to hear
Pronunciation: / stoʊn dɛf / (AmE), / stəʊn dɛf / (BrE)
completely unwilling to listen to advice or unable to hear at all

Examples

stone deaf to advice
Despite repeated advice from her friends, she remained stone deaf to their concerns.
He is stone deaf when it comes to constructive feedback.
The company was stone deaf to criticism about its new policy.
