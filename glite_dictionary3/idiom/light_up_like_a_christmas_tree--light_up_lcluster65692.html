to light up like a Christmas tree

verb
to become visibly excited or happy
Pronunciation: / laɪt ʌp laɪk ə ˈkrɪs.məs ˌtri / (AmE), / laɪt ʌp laɪk ə ˈkrɪs.məs ˌtriː / (BrE)
(of a person or their expression) to appear suddenly very excited, happy, or animated, often with a visible change in facial expression or demeanor.
