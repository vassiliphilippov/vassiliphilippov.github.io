to fall at the last hurdle

verb
fail at the final stage
Pronunciation: / fɔl ət ðə læst ˈhɝdəl / (AmE), / fɔːl ət ðə lɑːst ˈhɜːdl̩ / (BrE)
to fail or be unsuccessful at the final or most important stage of something, especially after previous success

Examples

fall at the last hurdle in a competition
After months of careful preparation, the team fell at the last hurdle and lost the final match.
Many startups fall at the last hurdle when it comes to securing crucial investment.
She had passed every exam, only to fall at the last hurdle during the interview stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fall short: to fail to reach a goal or standard
  • antonym – succeed: to achieve a desired aim or result
