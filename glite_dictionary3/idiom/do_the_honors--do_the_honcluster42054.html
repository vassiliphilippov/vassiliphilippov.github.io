to do the honors

verb
act as host or perform a ceremonial task
Pronunciation: / du ði ˈɑnɚz /; / du ðə ˈɑnɚz / (AmE), / duː ði ˈɒnəz /; / duː ðə ˈɒnəz / (BrE)
to perform a social or ceremonial duty, such as introducing guests, starting an event, or serving others on a special occasion

Examples

Would you please do the honors and introduce our speaker for the evening?

Would you like to do the honors and cut the cake?
