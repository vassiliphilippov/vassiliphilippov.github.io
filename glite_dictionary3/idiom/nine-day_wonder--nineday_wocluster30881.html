nine-day wonder

noun
something briefly popular, soon forgotten
Pronunciation: / ˌnaɪn deɪ ˈwʌndər / (AmE), / ˌnaɪn deɪ ˈwʌndə / (BrE)
Something that is briefly popular or attracts great attention but is soon forgotten.

Examples

The invention was a nine-day wonder and was soon forgotten by the public.
