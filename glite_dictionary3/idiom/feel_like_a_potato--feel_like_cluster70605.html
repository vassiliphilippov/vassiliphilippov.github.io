to feel like a potato

verb
to feel lazy or unproductive
Pronunciation: / fil laɪk ə pəˈteɪtoʊ / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə pəˈteɪtəʊ / (BrE)
to feel extremely lazy or unproductive, as if lacking energy or motivation to do anything

When it rains all weekend, I just feel like a potato and don’t get anything done.
