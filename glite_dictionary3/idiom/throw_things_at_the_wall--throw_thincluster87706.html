to throw things at the wall

verb
try different ideas to see what works
Pronunciation: / θroʊ θɪŋz æt ðə wɔl / (AmE), / θrəʊ θɪŋz æt ðə wɔːl / (BrE)
to try out a number of ideas or methods to see which one is successful or effective; to experiment without a clear plan in the hope that something will work

During brainstorming sessions, we often throw things at the wall to encourage creativity.
