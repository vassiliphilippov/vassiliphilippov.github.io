light-bulb moment

noun
sudden moment of realization
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪt bʌlb ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt bʌlb ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
A sudden realization, insight, or understanding; a moment when something becomes clear.

The teacher smiled as her student had a light-bulb moment while solving the math problem.
