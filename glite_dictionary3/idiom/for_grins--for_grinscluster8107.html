for grins

adverb
for fun or enjoyment
Pronunciation: / fər ɡrɪnz / (AmE), / fə ɡrɪnz / (BrE)
Used to indicate that something is done for fun or enjoyment, not for any serious reason.

Examples

just for grins
She joined the dance class for grins, not to compete.
Sometimes I wear mismatched socks for grins.
They went to the amusement park just for grins.
