close at hand

adjective
imminent or about to happen
Pronunciation: / kloʊs ət hænd / (AmE), / kləʊs ət hand / (BrE)
(of an event or time) likely to happen very soon; imminent or approaching.

Examples

close at hand for victory
She sensed that victory was close at hand after months of preparation.
The deadline for the project is close at hand, so we all need to work quickly.
With winter close at hand, many animals begin to gather food and seek shelter.

Related Terms

  • antonym – distant: far away in time; not soon to happen
  • synonym – imminent: about to happen very soon
  • synonym – impending: likely to occur soon; forthcoming
