interjection
expression to emphasize a result or completion
Pronunciation: / ðɛr ju ˈhæv ɪt / (AmE), / ðeə ju ˈhæv ɪt / (BrE)
used to indicate that something has been completed or explained, or to emphasize a result or conclusion
