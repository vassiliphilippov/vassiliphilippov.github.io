sarcastically typical of white people or white privilege
Pronunciation: / saʊndz əˈbaʊt waɪt / (AmE), / saʊndz əˈbaʊt waɪt / (BrE)
(especially in US informal contexts) said to sarcastically suggest that something is typical of the behavior, attitudes, or excuses associated with white people or white privilege.
