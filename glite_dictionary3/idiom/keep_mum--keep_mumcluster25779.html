to keep mum

verb
stay silent; not reveal a secret
Pronunciation: / kip mʌm / (AmE), / kiːp mʌm / (BrE)
to remain silent or not reveal a secret or information

Examples

keep mum about news
He decided to keep mum about the deal until everything was finalized.
It's best to keep mum if you don't want to get into trouble.
The children managed to keep mum when questioned by their parents.

Related Terms

  • synonym – keep quiet: to not speak or reveal information
  • antonym – spill the beans: to reveal secret information
