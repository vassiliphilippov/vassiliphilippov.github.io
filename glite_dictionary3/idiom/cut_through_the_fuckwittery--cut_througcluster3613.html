to cut through the fuckwittery

verb
ignore nonsense and focus on what matters
Pronunciation: / kʌt θru ðə fʌkˈwɪtəri / (AmE), / kʌt θruː ðə fʌkˈwɪtəri / (BrE)
(vulgar, informal) To disregard pointless nonsense or stupidity and focus on the main issue.
