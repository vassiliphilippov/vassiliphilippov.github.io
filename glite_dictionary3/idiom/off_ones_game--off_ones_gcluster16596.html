off (one's) game

adjective
not performing at one's best
Pronunciation: / ɑf ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ɒf ɡeɪm / (BrE)
Not performing as well as usual; not at one's usual level of skill or effectiveness.

Examples

feel off my game
After a long flight, I felt off my game during the interview.
He apologized for being off his game and promised to improve next time.
The team appeared off their game and struggled to keep up with the opposition.
