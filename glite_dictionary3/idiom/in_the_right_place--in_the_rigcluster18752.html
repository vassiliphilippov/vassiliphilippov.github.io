in the right place

adjective
having sincere or good intentions
Pronunciation: / ɪn ðə raɪt pleɪs / (AmE), / ɪn ðə raɪt pleɪs / (BrE)
(of someone's feelings or intentions) well-meant or sincere, though perhaps not entirely correct or effective.

Examples

in the right place at heart
He sometimes gets confused, but his intentions are always in the right place.
Her advice wasn't very helpful, but her heart was in the right place.
Though she makes mistakes, everyone knows her heart is in the right place.
