to be one's own man

verb
be independent; self-sufficient
Pronunciation: / bi wʌnz oʊn mæn / (AmE), / bi wʌnz əʊn mæn / (BrE)
to be independent and able to make your own decisions, not influenced by others

Examples

to be one's own man in life
After years of taking advice from others, he finally decided to be his own man and start his own business.
It's important for a leader to be her own man and not simply follow the crowd.
She encourages her son to be his own man and make choices independently.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stand on one's own two feet: to be independent and not need help from others
