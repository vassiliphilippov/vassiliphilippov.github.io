to freeze like a deer in the headlights

verb
become paralyzed by fear or shock
Pronunciation: / friz laɪk ə dɪr ɪn ðə ˈhɛdlaɪts / (AmE), / friːz laɪk ə dɪə ɪn ðə ˈhɛdlaɪts / (BrE)
