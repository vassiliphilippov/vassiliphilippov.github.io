to catch a whiff

verb
notice a slight smell
Pronunciation: / kætʃ ə wɪf / (AmE), / kætʃ ə wɪf / (BrE)
to notice or briefly sense a slight smell of something

Examples

catch a whiff of coffee
As I walked into the kitchen, I caught a whiff of coffee brewing.
She caught a whiff of perfume as he passed by.
The children caught a whiff of cookies from the bakery down the street.
