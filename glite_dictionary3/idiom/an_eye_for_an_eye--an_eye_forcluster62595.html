an eye for an eye

noun
equal retaliation; tit-for-tat justice
Pronunciation: / ən aɪ fər ən aɪ / (AmE), / ən aɪ fə ən aɪ / (BrE)
the idea that retaliation or punishment should be equal in kind and degree to the original offense

Examples

an eye for an eye justice
He took an eye for an eye approach to settling disputes.
Many people argue that an eye for an eye only leads to more conflict.
Some laws are based on the principle of an eye for an eye.
