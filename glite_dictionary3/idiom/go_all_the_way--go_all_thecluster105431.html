to go all the way

verb
fully commit or finish something
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ɔl ðə weɪ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ɔːl ðə weɪ / (BrE)

Examples

to go all the way to success

She decided to go all the way and finish what she started.
They knew it would be hard, but they were determined to go all the way to success.
