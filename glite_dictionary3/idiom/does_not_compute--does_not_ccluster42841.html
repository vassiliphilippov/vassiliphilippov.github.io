to does not compute

verb
illogical; makes no sense
Pronunciation: / dəz nɑt kəmˈpjut / (AmE), / dəz nɒt kəmˈpjuːt / (BrE)
Used to indicate that something is illogical, makes no sense, or cannot be understood.

Examples

does not compute in my mind
Her explanation does not compute—she said she was home, but there are photos of her at the event.
The numbers you gave me do not compute with the total you claim.
When I heard his theory, it simply did not compute in my mind.
