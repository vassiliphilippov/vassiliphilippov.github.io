to go in the toilet

verb
to fail or be ruined (informal idiom)
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ɪn ðə ˈtɔɪlət / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ɪn ðə ˈtɔɪlət / (BrE)
(of plans or efforts) to fail completely or be ruined.

Examples

go in the toilet after failure
After the funding was cut, the project went in the toilet.
All our hard work went in the toilet when the computer crashed.
His dreams of promotion went in the toilet after he missed the deadline.

Related Terms

  • synonym – go down the drain: to fail or be wasted
