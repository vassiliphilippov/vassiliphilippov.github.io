Heaven forbid

interjection
express hope something bad will not occur
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvən fərˈbɪd / (AmE), / ˈhɛv.ən fəˈbɪd / (BrE)
Used to express the hope that something undesirable will not happen.

Examples

Heaven forbid an accident
Heaven forbid anything happens to the children while we're gone.
He said, 'Heaven forbid that anyone should lose their job over this mistake.'
I hope we have enough food—Heaven forbid we run out during the party.
