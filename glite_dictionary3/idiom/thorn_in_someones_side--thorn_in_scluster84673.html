thorn in someone's side

noun
persistent source of annoyance
Pronunciation: / θɔrn ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz saɪd / (AmE), / θɔːn ɪn ˈsʌmwənz saɪd / (BrE)
a person or thing that repeatedly causes trouble or annoyance for someone

Examples

constant thorn in someone's side
Her persistent questioning was a thorn in his side throughout the entire meeting.
Repeated technical failures proved to be a thorn in our side during the project.
The new regulations have become a thorn in the company's side since their introduction.
