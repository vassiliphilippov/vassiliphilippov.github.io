by nature

adverb
as a result of inherent qualities
Pronunciation: / baɪ ˈneɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / baɪ ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
because of one's inherent character or innate qualities; in a way that is natural to someone or something

Examples

kind by nature
He is generous by nature, always willing to lend a hand.
She is cautious by nature and rarely takes risks.
Some animals are aggressive by nature and require careful handling.
