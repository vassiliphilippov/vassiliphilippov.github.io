to feel like an island

verb
feel isolated or alone
Pronunciation: / fil laɪk ən ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ən ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
to experience a sense of being alone or isolated, especially in the midst of others

Examples

to feel like an island among people
After moving to a new country, Marta felt like an island without any close acquaintances.
Despite the lively atmosphere at the party, Anna couldn't help but feel like an island.
Even surrounded by friends, he sometimes feels like an island during difficult times.

  • synonym – feel isolated: to feel alone or separated from others
