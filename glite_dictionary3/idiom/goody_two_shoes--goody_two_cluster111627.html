goody two shoes

noun
person who is overly virtuous or well-behaved
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʊdi tu ˌʃuz / (AmE), / ˈɡʊdi tuː ˌʃuːz / (BrE)
a person who behaves in an extremely virtuous or well-behaved manner, often regarded as annoying or smug by others
