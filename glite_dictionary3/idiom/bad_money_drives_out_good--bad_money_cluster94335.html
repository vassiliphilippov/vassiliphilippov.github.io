economic principle about currency quality
Pronunciation: / bæd ˈmʌni draɪvz aʊt ɡʊd / (AmE), / bæd ˈmʌni draɪvz aʊt ɡʊd / (BrE)
The principle that inferior or debased currency tends to circulate more widely than better-quality currency, causing the latter to disappear from circulation.

Examples
