running on empty

adjective
exhausted or depleted of energy
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɪŋ ɑn ˈɛmpti / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ ɒn ˈɛmpti / (BrE)
having no energy, resources, or fuel left; extremely tired or depleted

Examples

running on empty after work
After another late night at work, she was running on empty by Friday afternoon.
By the time we reached the gas station, our car was really running on empty.
The team kept pushing through the project, even though they were running on empty.
