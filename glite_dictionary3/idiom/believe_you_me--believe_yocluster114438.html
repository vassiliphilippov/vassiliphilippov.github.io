believe you me

interjection
used for emphasis, meaning 'trust me'
Pronunciation: / bəˈliv ju ˈmi / (AmE), / bɪˈliːv juː ˈmiː / (BrE)
Used to emphasize that what you are saying is true, especially when you know from your own experience.

Examples

Believe you me, there’s no better way to solve this problem.
