to work like a dog

verb
to work very hard
Pronunciation: / wɝk laɪk ə dɔɡ / (AmE), / wɜːk laɪk ə dɒɡ / (BrE)
to work very hard and tirelessly, often with little rest or reward

Examples

to work like a dog all day
Even after years of working like a dog, he barely made enough to support his family.
If you work like a dog all your life, you deserve a comfortable retirement.
She had to work like a dog to finish the project before the deadline.
