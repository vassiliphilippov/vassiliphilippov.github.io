hand in glove

adverb
in close cooperation (idiom)
Pronunciation: / hænd ɪn ɡlʌv / (AmE), / hænd ɪn ɡlʌv / (BrE)
working very closely together, especially in a way that is considered secretive or suspicious

Examples

hand in glove with criminals
Investigators suspect the officials acted hand in glove with the criminal organization.
The politicians were working hand in glove with major corporations to influence policy.
The two companies operated hand in glove to corner the market.

Related Terms
