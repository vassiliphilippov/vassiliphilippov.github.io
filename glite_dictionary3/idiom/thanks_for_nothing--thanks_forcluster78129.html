thanks for nothing

interjection
sarcastic expression of disappointment
Pronunciation: / ˌθæŋks fər ˈnʌθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌθæŋks fə ˈnʌθɪŋ / (BrE)
used sarcastically to express disappointment or annoyance when someone's actions are unhelpful or do not meet expectations.

Examples

thanks for nothing sarcastically
After waiting hours for a reply, all he could say was thanks for nothing.
He promised to help, but in the end did nothing—thanks for nothing.
When the report turned out to be empty, she said, 'thanks for nothing, guys.'
