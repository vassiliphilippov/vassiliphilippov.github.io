stuck in the mud

adjective
unable to progress or move forward
Pronunciation: / stʌk ɪn ðə mʌd / (AmE), / stʌk ɪn ðə mʌd / (BrE)
unable to make progress or move forward, either literally or figuratively; trapped or hindered by obstacles

Examples

stuck in the mud after rain
After the car skidded off the road, it was stuck in the mud until help arrived.
She realized her career was stuck in the mud and decided to make a change.
We felt stuck in the mud when our project hit repeated setbacks.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bogged down: unable to progress because of being overwhelmed or impeded
