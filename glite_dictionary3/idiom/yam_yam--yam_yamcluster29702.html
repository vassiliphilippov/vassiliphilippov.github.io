yam yam

interjection
expression of food enjoyment
Pronunciation: / jæm jæm / (AmE), / jæm jæm / (BrE)
An exclamation expressing enjoyment or approval of the taste of food.

Examples

yam yam after tasting food
As soon as he tasted the fresh bread, he exclaimed, "yam yam!"
She texted a picture of her dinner with the caption, "yam yam—so delicious!"
The children giggled and shouted yam yam after trying the chocolate cake.
