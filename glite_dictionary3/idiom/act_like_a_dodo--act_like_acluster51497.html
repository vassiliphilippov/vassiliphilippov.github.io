to act like a dodo

verb
behave foolishly or cluelessly
Pronunciation: / ˈækt laɪk ə ˈdoʊdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈækt laɪk ə ˈdəʊdəʊ / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or clueless manner; to act as if lacking common sense

Examples
