to scratch one's head

verb
show confusion or puzzlement
Pronunciation: / skrætʃ wʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / skrætʃ wʌnz hɛd / (BrE)
to show confusion or uncertainty by touching or rubbing your head, often when you do not understand something

Examples

scratch his head in confusion
I'm still scratching my head over how they solved the problem so quickly.
She scratched her head when asked an unexpected question during the interview.
The new regulations made everyone scratch their head in bewilderment.

Related Terms

  • synonym – be baffled: to be totally unable to understand or explain something
  • synonym – be puzzled: to feel confused because something is hard to understand
  • antonym – understand: to grasp the meaning of something
