to throw one's cap over the windmill

verb
make an irrevocable commitment
Pronunciation: / θroʊ wʌnz kæp ˈoʊvɚ ðə ˈwɪndˌmɪl / (AmE), / θrəʊ wʌnz kæp ˈəʊvə ðə ˈwɪndmɪl / (BrE)
to make a decisive and irreversible commitment to a course of action, often one involving risk or boldness
