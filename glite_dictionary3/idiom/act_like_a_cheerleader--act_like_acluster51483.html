to act like a cheerleader

verb
show energetic support
Pronunciation: / ækt laɪk ə ˈtʃɪrˌlidɚ / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈtʃɪəˌliːdə / (BrE)
to display overt and enthusiastic support for someone or something, often in a conspicuous or vocal manner

Examples

to act like a cheerleader for a team
During the meeting, she acted like a cheerleader, encouraging everyone to stay positive.
He always acts like a cheerleader when our team is behind, clapping and shouting words of encouragement.
Instead of criticizing, Jake chose to act like a cheerleader and motivate his classmates before the exam.
