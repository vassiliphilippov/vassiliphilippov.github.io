the answer to my prayers

noun
exactly what is needed or wished for
Pronunciation: / ði ˈænsɚ tuː maɪ prɛrz / (AmE), / ði ˈɑːnsə tuː maɪ preəz / (BrE)
a thing or person that provides exactly what is needed or desired, especially as a solution to a major problem or difficulty

Examples

Winning the scholarship was the answer to my prayers after months of financial worry.
