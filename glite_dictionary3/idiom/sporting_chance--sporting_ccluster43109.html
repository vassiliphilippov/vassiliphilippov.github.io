sporting chance

noun
a reasonable probability of success
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔrtɪŋ tʃæns / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtɪŋ tʃɑːns / (BrE)
a fair or reasonable likelihood of success; a good opportunity to succeed but by no means a certainty

Examples

a sporting chance of winning
He knew his proposal was risky, but he believed he had a sporting chance.
The underdog team still has a sporting chance of winning the championship.
With enough preparation, you’ll give yourself a sporting chance at passing the exam.
