to bubble over with excitement

verb
express excitement openly
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌb.əl ˈoʊ.vɚ wɪð ɪkˈsaɪt.mənt / (AmE), / ˈbʌb.əl ˈəʊ.və wɪð ɪkˈsaɪt.mənt / (BrE)
to openly show one's excitement and lively enthusiasm; to be so excited that it is difficult to contain it

Examples

to bubble over with excitement at good news
He couldn't help but bubble over with excitement as he shared his news.
The children began to bubble over with excitement when they saw their birthday presents.
When her name was announced as the winner, she bubbled over with excitement.
