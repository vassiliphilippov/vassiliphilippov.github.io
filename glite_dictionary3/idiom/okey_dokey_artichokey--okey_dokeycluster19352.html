okey dokey, artichokey

interjection
playful way to say okay
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊki ˈdoʊki ˌɑrtɪˈtʃoʊki / (AmE), / ˌəʊki ˈdəʊki ˌɑːtɪˈtʃəʊki / (BrE)
(informal) Used playfully to express agreement or acceptance, similar to 'okay'.

Examples

okey dokey, artichokey with a smile
"Can you finish that by noon?" "Okey dokey, artichokey—I'll get it done!"
"Time to go?" "Okey dokey, artichokey," she replied with a smile.
When the teacher asked if everyone was ready to begin, someone shouted okey dokey, artichokey from the back of the room.
