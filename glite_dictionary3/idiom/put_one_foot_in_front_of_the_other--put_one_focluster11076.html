to put one foot in front of the other

verb
to keep moving forward, step by step
Pronunciation: / pʊt wʌn fʊt ɪn frʌnt ʌv ði ˈʌðɚ / (AmE), / pʊt wʌn fʊt ɪn frʌnt əv ði ˈʌðə / (BrE)
