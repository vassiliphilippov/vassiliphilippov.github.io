eye of the beer holder

noun
humorous twist on 'eye of the beholder'
Pronunciation: / aɪ əv ðə bɪr ˈhoʊldər / (AmE), / aɪ əv ðə bɪə ˈhəʊldə / (BrE)
a humorous adaptation of 'eye of the beholder,' suggesting one's perception of beauty is influenced by the amount of beer one has drunk
