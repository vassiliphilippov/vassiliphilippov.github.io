sometimes silence is better than speaking
Pronunciation: / spiːtʃ ɪz ˈsɪlvɚ ˈsaɪləns ɪz ˈɡoʊldən / (AmE), / spiːtʃ ɪz ˈsɪlvə ˈsaɪləns ɪz ˈɡəʊldən / (BrE)
used to say that it is often better to remain silent than to speak unnecessarily, as silence is more valuable than unnecessary words

Examples

speech is silver, silence is golden advice
At the board meeting, she wisely followed the rule that speech is silver, silence is golden.
