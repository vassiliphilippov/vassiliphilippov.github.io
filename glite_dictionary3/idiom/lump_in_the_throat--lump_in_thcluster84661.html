lump in the throat

noun
tight feeling in throat from emotion
Pronunciation: / lʌmp ɪn ðə θroʊt / (AmE), / lʌmp ɪn ðə θrəʊt / (BrE)
a tight or uncomfortable feeling in the throat caused by strong emotion, especially sadness or nervousness, often accompanied by the urge to cry

Examples

a lump in the throat from sadness
She spoke bravely, but you could hear the lump in her throat.
The news gave everyone in the room a lump in the throat.
The story was so touching that it brought a lump in my throat.
