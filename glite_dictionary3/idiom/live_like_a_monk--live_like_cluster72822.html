to live like a monk

verb
live simply and with discipline
Pronunciation: / lɪv laɪk ə mʌŋk / (AmE), / lɪv laɪk ə mʌŋk / (BrE)
to live in a very simple and disciplined way, usually avoiding luxuries and comforts

Examples

to live like a monk simply
After losing his job, Tom decided to live like a monk to save money and focus on his goals.
During exam season, she prefers to live like a monk, avoiding social events and distractions.
He spent several years living like a monk in the countryside, dedicating himself to meditation and study.
