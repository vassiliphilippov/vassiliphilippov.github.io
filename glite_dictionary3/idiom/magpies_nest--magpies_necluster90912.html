magpie's nest

noun
a messy or cluttered collection
Pronunciation: / ˈmæɡˌpaɪz nɛst / (AmE), / ˈmaɡ.pʌɪz nɛst / (BrE)
a place or collection that is untidy or cluttered, resembling the way a magpie gathers and hoards various objects

Examples

messy magpie's nest
After a week without cleaning, the living room looked like a magpie's nest.
Her handbag is such a magpie's nest that she can never find anything in it.
The garage has become a magpie's nest of tools, boxes, and forgotten projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clutter: an untidy collection of things
  • antonym – order: neat and organized arrangement
