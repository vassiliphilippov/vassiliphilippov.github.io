to hang tight

verb
wait patiently
Pronunciation: / hæŋ taɪt / (AmE), / hæŋ taɪt / (BrE)
to wait patiently and calmly, often during a delay or period of uncertainty

Examples

to hang tight during delays
If you hang tight, the next train should be here any minute.
Just hang tight while I find out what's happening.
The doctor told her to hang tight until the test results came in.
