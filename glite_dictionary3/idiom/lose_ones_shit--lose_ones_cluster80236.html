to lose one's shit

verb
become very angry or upset (informal, vulgar)
Pronunciation: / luːz wʌnz ʃɪt / (AmE), / luːz wʌnz ʃɪt / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) to suddenly become extremely angry, upset, or emotionally overwhelmed in a way that is hard to control
