to bring to justice

verb
hold accountable or punish for wrongdoing
Pronunciation: / brɪŋ tə ˈdʒʌstɪs / (AmE), / brɪŋ tʊ ˈdʒʌstɪs /; / brɪŋ tə ˈdʒʌstɪs / (BrE)
