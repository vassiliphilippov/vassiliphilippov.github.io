to be at pains

verb
make a special effort
Pronunciation: / bi æt peɪnz / (AmE), / bi ət peɪnz / (BrE)
to make a great effort to do something, often showing care or thoroughness

Examples

be at pains to explain
He was at pains to reassure everyone that the situation was under control.
She was at pains to explain every detail clearly to the committee.
The manager is at pains to ensure that his staff feel valued at work.
