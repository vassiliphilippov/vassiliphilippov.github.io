to keep one's nose to the grindstone

verb
work persistently and hard
Pronunciation: / kip wʌnz noʊz tə ðə ˈɡraɪndˌstoʊn / (AmE), / kiːp wʌnz nəʊz tə ðə ˈɡraɪndstəʊn / (BrE)
