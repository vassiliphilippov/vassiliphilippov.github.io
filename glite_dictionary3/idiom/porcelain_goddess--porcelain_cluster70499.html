porcelain goddess

noun
toilet (euphemism); very beautiful woman
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrs(ə)lɪn ˈɡɑdəs / (AmE), / ˈpɔːsəlɪn ˈɡɒdəs / (BrE)
a humorous or euphemistic term for a toilet, especially when referring to vomiting into it; sometimes also used to describe a woman who is exceptionally beautiful and delicate
