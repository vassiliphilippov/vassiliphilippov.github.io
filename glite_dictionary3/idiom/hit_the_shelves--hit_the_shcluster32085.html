to hit the shelves

verb
become available for sale
Pronunciation: / hɪt ðə ʃɛlvz / (AmE), / hɪt ðə ʃɛlvz / (BrE)
(of a product) to become available for sale in shops or stores.

Examples

hit the shelves in stores
After months of anticipation, the designer's latest collection finally hit the shelves today.
The company plans to hit the shelves with its innovative kitchen gadget early next year.
The new video game will hit the shelves just in time for the holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym – go on sale: become available for purchase
  • antonym – sell out: become sold completely, no longer available
