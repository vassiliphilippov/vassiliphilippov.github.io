to suit her book

verb
match one's plans or interests
Pronunciation: / sut hɚ bʊk / (AmE), / suːt hə bʊk / (BrE)
To match or be convenient for one's own plans or interests.

Examples

Find an arrangement that will suit your book and let me know.
