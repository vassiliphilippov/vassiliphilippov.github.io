to strike home

verb
have a strong or relevant effect
Pronunciation: / straɪk hoʊm / (AmE), / straɪk həʊm / (BrE)
(of a remark, comment, or explanation) to have a strong effect or to be especially relevant or meaningful, often causing realization or understanding.

Examples

strike home with a message
Her words about teamwork really struck home with the entire class.
The dangers of smoking finally struck home when his uncle was diagnosed with cancer.
When I saw the abandoned puppy, the need for animal shelters struck home for me.
