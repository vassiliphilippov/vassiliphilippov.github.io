to split the difference

verb
compromise by choosing a middle value
Pronunciation: / splɪt ðə ˈdɪf.ɚ.əns / (AmE), / splɪt ðə ˈdɪf.ərəns / (BrE)
to compromise by accepting a middle value or position, especially in negotiations when two parties disagree on an amount

Examples

If you want $100 and I want $50, let's split the difference and settle for $75.
