to leave someone hanging

verb
fail to support or respond to someone
Pronunciation: / liv sʌmˌwʌn ˈhæŋɪŋ / (AmE), / liːv sʌmˌwʌn ˈhæŋɪŋ / (BrE)
to fail to give someone the help, information, or support they need, especially at a crucial moment

Examples

leave someone hanging during a project
Don't leave your teammates hanging during the project.
I waited all night for an answer, but he just left me hanging.
She hates being left hanging when she needs a decision from her boss.
