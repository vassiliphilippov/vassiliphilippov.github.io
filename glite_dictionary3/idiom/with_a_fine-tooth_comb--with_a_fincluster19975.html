with a fine-tooth comb

x
to examine extremely carefully
Pronunciation: / wɪð ə faɪn tuθ koʊm / (AmE), / wɪð ə faɪn tuːθ kəʊm / (BrE)
If you go through something with a fine-tooth comb, you examine it very carefully and thoroughly.

Examples

examine with a fine-tooth comb
Detectives went over the crime scene with a fine-tooth comb to find any evidence.
She examined the documents with a fine-tooth comb before signing them.
The editor checked every line of the manuscript with a fine-tooth comb.
