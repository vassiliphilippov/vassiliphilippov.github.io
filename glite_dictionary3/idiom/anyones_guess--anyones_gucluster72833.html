anyone's guess

noun
something very uncertain or unpredictable
Pronunciation: / ˈɛniˌwʌnz ˌɡɛs / (AmE), / ˈeniwʌnz ˌɡɛs / (BrE)
used to say that something is very uncertain or impossible to predict.

Examples

anyone's guess at this point
How the election will turn out is anyone's guess at this stage.
The reason for his sudden resignation remains anyone's guess.
With so many delays, when the road will reopen is anyone's guess.
