to open doors

verb
create opportunities for progress or success
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpən dɔrz / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən dɔːz / (BrE)
to provide or create new opportunities, especially to enable progress or success

Examples

to open doors for careers
Her volunteer work helped open doors in the field of international relations.
Learning to code can open doors to many exciting careers.
Moving to a new city opened doors for him both personally and professionally.
