best of both worlds

noun
combining two advantages or benefits
Pronunciation: / bɛst əv boʊθ wɜrldz / (AmE), / bɛst əv bəʊθ wɜːldz / (BrE)
a situation in which you can enjoy the advantages of two very different things at the same time

Examples

enjoy the best of both worlds
Living in the suburbs but working in the city gives him the best of both worlds.
Our new hybrid car provides the best of both worlds: efficiency and power.
With her flexible job, Karen truly has the best of both worlds—a career and time with her kids.
