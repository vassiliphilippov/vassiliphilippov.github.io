against the clock

adverb
in a race to meet a deadline
Pronunciation: / əˈɡɛnst ðə ˈklɑk / (AmE), / əˈɡɛnst ðə ˈklɒk / (BrE)
in a race to finish something as quickly as possible before an allotted time runs out

Examples

work against the clock
She was racing against the clock to submit her application before the deadline.
The engineers worked against the clock to repair the power outage before sunrise.
We have to finish packing against the clock if we want to catch our flight.
