to raise a glass

verb
toast or celebrate with a drink
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪz ə ɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈreɪz ə ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
to celebrate or toast someone or something by holding up a drink, usually as part of a group

Examples

raise a glass to someone
At the retirement party, everyone stood to raise a glass in his honor.
Before dinner, she invited the guests to raise a glass to their gracious host.
Let's raise a glass to the new year and new beginnings.

Related Terms

  • synonym – toast: to propose or drink to the health or success of someone or something
