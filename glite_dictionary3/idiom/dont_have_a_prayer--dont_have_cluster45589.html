to don't have a prayer

verb
no chance of success
Pronunciation: / doʊnt hæv ə prɛr / (AmE), / dəʊnt hav ə preə / (BrE)
to have no chance of success at all

Examples

don't have a prayer of winning
If we try to compete with such a big company, we don't have a prayer.
She knew she didn't have a prayer of finishing the race first, but she still tried her best.
With no experience in politics, he doesn't have a prayer of winning the election.

Related Terms

  • antonym – have a shot: to have a chance (to succeed or win)
  • synonym – not stand a chance: to have no possibility of succeeding
