to give a flying shit

verb
(vulgar, informal) not care at all
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv ə ˈflaɪɪŋ ʃɪt / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə ˈflaɪɪŋ ʃɪt / (BrE)
(vulgar, informal) To care at all; to have any concern or interest (usually used in negative sentences to express complete indifference).

Examples

not give a flying shit
Do you really think he gives a flying shit whether we're late or not?
I don't give a flying shit what they think about my decisions.
She acts as if she doesn't give a flying shit about her grades this semester.
