off the cuff

adverb
without preparation; impromptu
Pronunciation: / ɑf ðə kʌf / (AmE), / ɒf ðə kʌf / (BrE)
spoken or done without previous preparation or thought; impromptu.

Examples

off the cuff remark
He gave an off the cuff speech at the meeting when the main speaker was delayed.
She answered the difficult question off the cuff, surprising everyone with her confidence.
Sometimes the funniest comments are those made off the cuff.
