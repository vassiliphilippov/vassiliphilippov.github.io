to stand up and be counted

verb
declare support publicly; show courage
Pronunciation: / stænd ʌp ənd bi ˈkaʊntɪd / (AmE), / stænd ʌp ənd bi ˈkaʊntɪd / (BrE)
to publicly show your support for something, especially when it involves taking a risk or showing courage

Examples

to stand up and be counted for change
It's important for citizens to stand up and be counted in times of crisis.
