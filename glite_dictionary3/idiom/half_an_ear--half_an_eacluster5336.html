half an ear

noun
to listen inattentively
Pronunciation: / hæf ən ɪr / (AmE), / hɑːf ən ɪə / (BrE)
to listen in an inattentive or partially engaged way; not giving full attention.

Examples

with half an ear listening
During the lecture, I caught the main points with half an ear.
He was only listening with half an ear while reading his emails.
She often listens with half an ear when she's distracted by her phone.

Related Terms

  • antonym – pay attention: to listen carefully and fully
