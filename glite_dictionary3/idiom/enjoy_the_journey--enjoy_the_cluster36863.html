to enjoy the journey

verb
appreciate the process of life or work
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒɔɪ ðə ˈdʒɜrn.i / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɔɪ ðə ˈdʒɜː.ni / (BrE)
to take pleasure in and appreciate the process or experience of life or a particular task, rather than focusing only on the end result

Examples

enjoy the journey of life
