far cry

noun
a long distance away
Pronunciation: / fɑr kraɪ / (AmE), / fɑː kraɪ / (BrE)
a long distance away, originally referring to the distance over which a shout or cry can be heard

Examples

far cry from town
Her cottage is a far cry from the city center.
It's a far cry from here to the next gas station.
The village is a far cry from the nearest town.
