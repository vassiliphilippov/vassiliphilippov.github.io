to sell one's soul

verb
compromise integrity for gain
Pronunciation: / sɛl wʌnz soʊl / (AmE), / sɛl wʌnz səʊl / (BrE)
to betray your principles or morals in exchange for personal gain, such as money, fame, or power

Examples
