to fall over backwards

1. VERB

/ fɔl ˈoʊvɚ ˈbækwɚdz / (AmE), / fɔːl ˈəʊvə ˈbækwədz / (BrE)
to make a great effort to help or please someone, often more than is necessary
try very hard to please someone

Examples

The company will fall over backwards to keep its best employees happy.
She always falls over backwards to help her friends with their problems.
He fell over backwards trying to impress his boss during the meeting.

Synset Examples

She falls over backwards when she sees her mother-in-law

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to bend over backwards - make every possible effort to please or help someone
