to saddle the horse

1. VERB

/ ˈsædəl ðə hɔrs / (AmE), / ˈsæd(ə)l ðə hɔːs / (BrE)
to put a saddle on a horse in preparation for riding
put a saddle on a horse

Examples

Every morning, he would saddle the horse before setting out on his ride.
Make sure you saddle the horse securely so the equipment doesn't slip.
She managed to saddle the horse quickly even though she was new to riding.

Synset Examples

She learned to saddle the horse with confidence.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to unsaddle - to remove the saddle from a horse
  • Synonym: bridle - to put a bridle on a horse
