to believe in

1. VERB

/ bəˈliv ɪn / (AmE), / bɪˈliːv ɪn / (BrE)
to have confidence in the existence, value, or goodness of someone or something
have confidence in something's worth or existence

Examples

She truly believes in the power of education to change lives.
Many people believe in working hard to achieve their dreams.
He believed in his team, even when others doubted them.

Synset Examples

John believes in oat bran

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trust - to have confidence in the reliability or truth of someone or something
  • Antonym: doubt - to feel uncertain about something or lack belief in it
