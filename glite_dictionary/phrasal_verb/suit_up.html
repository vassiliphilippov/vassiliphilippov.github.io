to suit up

1. VERB

/ sut ʌp / (AmE), / sjuːt ʌp / (BrE)
to put on formal or appropriate clothing, especially for a particular purpose or event
put on formal or appropriate clothing

Examples

Everyone needs to suit up before entering the boardroom.
He suited up in his finest attire for the awards ceremony.
The team suits up in matching uniforms before each game.

Synset Examples

She decided to suit up for the big presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to dress up - to put on formal or special clothes
