to face ostracism

1. VERB

/ feɪs ˈɑːstrəˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / feɪs ˈɒstrəˌsɪzəm / (BrE)
to be subjected to social exclusion or shunning by a group
to experience social exclusion

Examples

After coming forward as a whistleblower, she began to face ostracism from her colleagues.
Many people face ostracism in their communities because of their beliefs.
He feared he would face ostracism if he admitted his mistake.

Synset Examples

He faced ostracism for speaking out against bullying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be ostracized - to be excluded from a group or society
