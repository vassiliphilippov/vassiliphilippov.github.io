to make someone gyppy

1. VERB

/ meɪk ˈsʌmwʌn ˈdʒɪpi / (AmE), / meɪk ˈsʌmwən ˈdʒɪpi / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel ill or nauseous, especially after eating or drinking something
cause someone to feel ill or nauseous

Spicy food sometimes makes him gyppy, so he avoids it.
