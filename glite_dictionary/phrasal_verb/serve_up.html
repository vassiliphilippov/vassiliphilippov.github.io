to serve up

1. VERB

/ sɝv ʌp / (AmE), / sɜːv ʌp / (BrE)
to provide or present something, especially food, for people to eat or experience
present or provide, especially food

Examples

The chef will serve up a delicious three-course meal for the guests tonight.
The band served up a mix of classic hits and new songs during the concert.
She enjoys serving up homemade soup to her family on cold evenings.

Synset Examples

We serve meals for the homeless
She dished out the soup at 8 P.M.
The entertainers served up a lively show

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to dish out - to distribute or serve food or something else to people
