to take on the challenge

1. VERB

/ teɪk ɑn ðə ˈtʃælɪndʒ / (AmE), / teɪk ɒn ðə ˈtʃælɪndʒ / (BrE)
to accept or agree to face a difficult or demanding task
accept a difficult task

Examples

After much consideration, she decided to take on the challenge of running her own business.
The team was eager to take on the challenge and prove their abilities.
If you’re willing to take on the challenge, you could learn a lot from this experience.

Synset Examples

Our megateam is ready to take on the challenge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accept the challenge - to agree to do something difficult
