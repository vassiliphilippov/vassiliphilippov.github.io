to stay cozy

1. VERB

/ steɪ ˈkoʊzi / (AmE), / steɪ ˈkəʊzi / (BrE)
to remain warm and comfortable, especially indoors or in a pleasant environment
remain warm and comfortable

Examples

We decided to stay cozy by the fire all evening.
It's easy to stay cozy under a thick blanket on chilly nights.
During the storm, everyone tried to stay cozy in the cabin.

Synset Examples

We stayed cozy inside during the blizzard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to keep warm - to prevent oneself from becoming cold
  • Antonym: freeze - to feel very cold or to become cold
