to while away

1. VERB

/ waɪl əˈweɪ / (AmE), / waɪl əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to spend time in a leisurely, pleasant, or aimless way, especially to make a period of time pass more quickly or enjoyably.
spend time pleasantly or idly

Examples

We whiled away the afternoon playing cards on the porch.
She likes to while away the hours reading mystery novels.
They often while away rainy days by watching old movies together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pass the time - to do something to make time seem to go by more quickly

Etymology

From the noun 'while' (a period of time) used as a verb + 'away', meaning to pass time.
