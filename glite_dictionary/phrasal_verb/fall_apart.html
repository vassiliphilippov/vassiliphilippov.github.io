to fall apart

1. VERB

/ fɔl əˈpɑrt / (AmE), / fɔːl əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
to lose emotional or mental control, especially because of stress, grief, or trauma
lose emotional or mental control

Examples

She completely fell apart after hearing the tragic news about her friend.
When the pressure became too much, he started to fall apart at work.
Despite her strong appearance, she sometimes falls apart in private.

Synset Examples

She fell apart when her only child died

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to break down - to lose control of one’s emotions and start to cry
