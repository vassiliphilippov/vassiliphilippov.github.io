to raise up

1. VERB

/ ˈreɪz ʌp / (AmE), / ˈreɪz ʌp / (BrE)
to move or lift something or someone to a higher position or level
lift or move to a higher position

Examples

She used her arms to raise up the heavy box onto the shelf.
The workers raised up the platform so everyone could see the speaker.
He raised up his head to look at the sky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to lift up - to move something to a higher position
  • Antonym: lower - to move something to a lower position
