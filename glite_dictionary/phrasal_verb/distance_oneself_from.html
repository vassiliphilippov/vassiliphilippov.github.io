to distance oneself from

1. VERB

/ ˈdɪstəns wʌnˈsɛlf frʌm / (AmE), / ˈdɪstəns wʌnˈsɛlf frɒm / (BrE)
to deliberately separate oneself from someone or something emotionally, socially, or in terms of association
deliberately separate oneself

Examples

After the scandal, the senator made every effort to distance herself from the disgraced organization.
He began to distance himself from old friends to focus on his studies.
The company tried to distance itself from the controversial statements made by its former CEO.

Synset Examples

She decided to distance herself from the freeloader.
She distanced herself from the group of cokeheads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dissociate oneself from - to separate oneself from association or involvement with something or someone
