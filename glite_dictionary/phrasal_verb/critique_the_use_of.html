to critique the use of

1. VERB

/ krɪˈtik ði jus ʌv / (AmE), / krɪˈtiːk ðə juːs ɒv / (BrE)
to analyze and evaluate the manner or context in which something is used, particularly regarding language or concepts
analyze and evaluate how something is used

Examples

The professor often critiques the use of metaphors in students’ essays.
Several linguists have critiqued the use of jargon in medical communication.
She is critiquing the use of gendered language in modern literature.

Synset Examples

In her blog, she critiques the use of the word femoid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to analyze - to examine in detail for purposes of explanation and interpretation
  • Synonym: to criticize - to indicate the faults of someone or something in a disapproving way
