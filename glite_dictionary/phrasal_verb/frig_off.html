to frig off

1. VERB

/ frɪɡ ɑf / (AmE), / frɪɡ ɒf / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) Used to rudely or angrily tell someone to go away or leave you alone.
tell someone to go away rudely (slang)
slang vulgar

Examples

If you don't like my opinion, you can just frig off.
Fed up with the interruptions, I told everyone to frig off and let me work.
The comment was so rude that she told him to frig off immediately.

Synset Examples

When he started complaining, I told him to frig off.
If you can't support me, just frig off.
She was so annoyed that she told him to frig off.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fuck off - used to rudely order someone to leave

Etymology

Euphemistic alteration of 'fuck off,' with 'frig' used as a milder substitute for 'fuck.'
