to snivel over

1. VERB

/ ˈsnɪvəl ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈsnɪvəl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to complain or whine in a self-pitying way about something, often seen as minor or unimportant
complain or whine about (something)

Examples

Stop sniveling over every little setback and try to move forward.
She couldn't help but snivel over the criticism she had received.
There's no point in sniveling over what can't be changed.

Synset Examples

He began to snivel over his lost toy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whine about - complain in a complaining or petulant way
  • Synonym: complain about - express dissatisfaction or annoyance about something

Etymology

From 'snivel' (Middle English, from Old English *snyflan*) + 'over'; 'snivel' originally referred to nasal mucus, later to whimpering or whining.
