to have a go at

1. VERB

/ hæv ə ɡoʊ æt / (AmE), / hæv ə ɡəʊ æt / (BrE)
to try to do something, especially something you have not done before
attempt something

Examples

After seeing the skateboarders, I wanted to have a go at it myself.
If you think you can fix it, why not have a go at repairing the old radio?
She's nervous about singing in public but decided to have a go at karaoke anyway.

Synset Examples

Why not have a go at learning a new language?
I decided to have a go at baking today.
You should have a go at painting this weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: try - make an attempt or effort to do something
