to noodle over

1. VERB

/ ˈnuːdəl ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈnuːdl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to think about or consider something carefully, especially in a casual or informal way
think about; reflect on
informal american

Examples

We need to noodle over the budget proposal before submitting it to the team.
She likes to noodle over new designs during her morning walks.
He noodled over the offer for days before finally saying yes.

Synset Examples

Let's noodle over this idea before making a decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ponder - to think about something carefully, especially before making a decision
  • Synonym: to consider - to think about carefully
  • Antonym: to dismiss - to decide not to think about something any longer

Etymology

From American slang 'noodle' (head, brain) + 'over'; 'noodle' probably from German 'Nudel' (noodle), used playfully for 'head'.
