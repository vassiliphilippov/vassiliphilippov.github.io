to move it over

1. VERB

/ ˈmuːv ɪt ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈmuːv ɪt ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to shift something slightly to one side
shift something sideways slightly

Examples

Could you move it over a little so I have more space?
I had to move it over to fit the new painting on the wall.
Please move it over if it's blocking the doorway.

Synset Examples

Can you move it over a bit, please?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to move over - to shift position to one side
