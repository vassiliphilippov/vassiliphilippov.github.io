to slam shut

1. VERB

/ slæm ʃʌt / (AmE), / slæm ʃʌt / (BrE)
to close something violently or with great force, usually making a loud noise
close violently or forcefully

Examples

She accidentally let the window slam shut in the wind.
Make sure the gate doesn’t slam shut behind you.
The heavy bookcase door slammed shut with a bang.

Synset Examples

The door slammed shut with a loud wham.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slam - to close something forcefully, especially making a noise
  • Synonym: shut - to close something so that it is no longer open
