to open up

1. VERB

/ ˈoʊpən ʌp / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən ʌp / (BrE)
to make something available or possible, especially something new or previously unavailable
make available or possible

Examples

The new highway will open up remote areas to tourism and trade.
Her research helped open up new avenues in the field of medicine.
Digital platforms open up opportunities for businesses to reach international markets.

Synset Examples

  • Synonym: to unlock - to open or make something accessible
