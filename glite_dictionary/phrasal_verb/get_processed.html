to get processed

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈprɑː.sɛst / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈprəʊ.sɛst / (BrE)
to have something go through a formal procedure or review, typically by an authority or organization.
undergo a formal procedure or review

Examples

You will need to wait a few days for your application to get processed.
All the documents must get processed before the deadline.
Once your payment gets processed, we will ship your order.

Synset Examples

She was relieved to get her refund processed quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: process - to subject something to a formal procedure or system
