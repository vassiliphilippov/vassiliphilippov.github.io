to turn a trick

1. VERB

/ tɝn ə trɪk / (AmE), / tɜːn ə trɪk / (BrE)
(of a prostitute) to have sex with a customer for money.
(of prostitute) have a customer for sex
informal

Examples

She admitted to the police that she would sometimes turn a trick to make ends meet.
The character in the film was forced to turn a trick to pay her rent.
He was arrested in the alley while negotiating to turn a trick.
