to refresh memory

1. VERB

/ rɪˈfrɛʃ ˈmɛməri / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɛʃ ˈmɛməri / (BrE)
to help someone remember information or details that they have previously learned but may have forgotten
help someone remember something

Examples

Could you refresh my memory about what we discussed last week?
The lawyer asked a few questions to refresh the witness's memory.
Reading his notes helped refresh her memory before the exam.

Synset Examples

He showed me the old photos to refresh my memory.
Let me refresh your memory about the meeting details.
This article should refresh your memory of the topic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to remind - to cause someone to remember something
