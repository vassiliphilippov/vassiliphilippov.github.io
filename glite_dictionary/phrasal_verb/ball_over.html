to ball over

1. VERB

/ bɔl ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / bɔːl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to surprise or astonish someone greatly; to shock or amaze completely
to astonish or amaze greatly

Examples

The news of her sudden resignation really balled over the entire team.
I was completely balled over by the generosity of strangers during the crisis.
The unexpected plot twist in the movie balls over most audiences.

Synset Examples

I was floored when I heard that I was promoted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to amaze - to surprise greatly; to fill with astonishment
  • Synonym: knock someone's socks off - to impress or surprise someone very much
  • Antonym: bore - to make someone feel uninterested or unmoved
