to show colour

1. VERB

/ ʃoʊ ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / ʃəʊ ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
to display vibrant colors or to express one's personality or style visibly
display vibrant colors or express style

Examples

The tulips in the garden will show colour in early April.
She likes to show colour through her bold fashion choices.
As the festival began, the entire city started to show colour and vibrancy.

Synset Examples

The flowers will show colour as spring approaches.
He decided to show colour by painting his walls bright yellow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: express oneself - to communicate one's ideas or personality
  • Synonym: to show one's true colors - to reveal one's true character or intentions
