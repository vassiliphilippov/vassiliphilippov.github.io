to remind of

1. VERB

/ rɪˈmaɪnd əv / (AmE), / rɪˈmaɪnd ɒv / (BrE)
to cause someone to think of someone or something else because of similarities or associations
cause to recall someone or something by similarity

Examples

The actor's expressions remind of his father, who was also famous.
This music reminds of summer holidays at the beach.
Her handwriting reminds of the notes my grandmother used to write.

Synset Examples

Can you remind me about the deadline?
He reminded me of Kramer with his wild hair.
Her performance reminded everyone of Yehudi Menuhin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recall - to bring a memory of something into one's mind
  • Synonym: to evoke - to bring to mind or recall a feeling, memory, or image
