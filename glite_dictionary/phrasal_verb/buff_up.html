to buff up

1. VERB

/ bʌf ʌp / (AmE), / bʌf ʌp / (BrE)
to improve or enhance something, especially in appearance or quality
to improve or enhance

Examples

She worked hard to buff up her presentation before the big meeting.
Many students buff up their résumés with volunteer experience.
