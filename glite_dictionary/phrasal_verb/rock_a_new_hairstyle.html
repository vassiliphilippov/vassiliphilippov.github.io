to rock a new hairstyle

1. VERB

/ rɑk ə nu ˈhaɪrˌstaɪl / (AmE), / rɒk ə njuː ˈheəˌstaɪl / (BrE)
to confidently wear or display a new hairdo, often with pride or flair
confidently wear a new hairstyle
informal

Examples

Everyone complimented her when she decided to rock a new hairstyle at the reunion.
He's not afraid to rock a new hairstyle whenever he feels like changing his look.
After the breakup, she wanted a fresh start and chose to rock a new hairstyle at work.

Synset Examples

He decided to rock a new hairstyle for the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sport a new hairstyle - to wear a new hairstyle and show it off
