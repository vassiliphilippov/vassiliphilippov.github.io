to skeez around

1. VERB

/ skiz əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / skiːz əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to move about in a sneaky or awkward manner, especially in a way that draws negative attention
move in a sneaky or awkward way
informal

Examples

Every time there’s trouble, he seems to skeez around looking for gossip.
She caught the cat skeezing around the kitchen, obviously up to no good.
Stop skeezing around and just tell us what you want!

Synset Examples

I can't stand how he skeezes around the office.
