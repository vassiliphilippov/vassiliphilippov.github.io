to impregnate the soil

1. VERB

/ ɪmˈprɛɡ.neɪt ðə sɔɪl / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛɡ.neɪt ðə sɔɪl / (BrE)
to enrich soil by adding nutrients or chemicals that improve its fertility
add nutrients to soil

Examples

To restore the field, the farmers decided to impregnate the soil with composted organic matter.
Gardeners often impregnate the soil before planting new crops each season.
Regularly impregnating the soil can help ensure healthy plant growth.
