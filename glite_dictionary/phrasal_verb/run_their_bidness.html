to run their bidness

1. VERB

/ rʌn ðɛr ˈbɪdnɪs / (AmE), / rʌn ðɛə ˈbɪdnɪs / (BrE)
to operate or manage their business, typically used in informal or dialectal speech as a nonstandard form of 'run their business'
informal: manage their business
informal slang

Examples

They always run their bidness with a strong sense of community.
Even during hard times, she managed to run their bidness efficiently.
Some people judge how you run their bidness, but integrity matters most.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: run their business - to operate their business

Etymology

'Bidness' is a nonstandard pronunciation and spelling of 'business,' reflecting some regional and informal varieties of English.
