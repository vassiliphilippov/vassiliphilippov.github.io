to arrive late

1. VERB

/ əˈraɪv leɪt / (AmE), / əˈraɪv leɪt / (BrE)
to reach a place or event after the scheduled or expected time
reach after expected time

Examples

He tends to arrive late for work every Monday.
Despite the bad weather, she managed not to arrive late for the concert.
If you arrive late, you may not be allowed to enter the theater.

Synset Examples

Rupert always arrives late to the meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be late - to not be on time for something
