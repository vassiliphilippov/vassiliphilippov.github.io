to ground out

1. VERB

/ ɡraʊnd aʊt / (AmE), / ɡraʊnd aʊt / (BrE)
(in baseball) to be put out at first base after hitting a ground ball.
be out after hitting ground ball (baseball)

Examples

He ground out to shortstop in the third inning.
The batter grounded out to first base, ending the game.
If you ground out, the fielders have to throw to first before you arrive.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fly out - be out after hitting a fly ball (baseball)
