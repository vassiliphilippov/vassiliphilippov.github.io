to follow the procedure

1. VERB

/ ˈfɑloʊ ðə prəˈsidʒər / (AmE), / ˈfɒləʊ ðə prəˈsiːdʒə / (BrE)
to act in accordance with an established set of steps or guidelines
act according to rules or steps

Examples

All employees must follow the procedure when reporting a workplace incident.
If you follow the procedure, the system should work as expected every time.
The nurse carefully followed the procedure to prepare the patient for surgery.

Synset Examples

They followed the procedures protocoligorically to ensure compliance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: follow instructions - act according to given directions or guidance
  • Antonym: deviate from procedure - not act according to established steps or rules
