to measure out

1. VERB

/ ˈmɛʒər aʊt / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒə aʊt / (BrE)
to determine or allocate a quantity or amount by measurement, especially using a tool or standard unit
determine or allocate a measured amount

Examples

She carefully measured out exactly 200 milliliters of milk for the recipe.
The teacher asked the students to measure out the length of the table using a ruler.
Before adding sugar to the bowl, he measured out half a cup.

Synset Examples

Measure the length of the wall

Related Terms

  • Synonym: measure - to find the size, amount, or degree of something using a standard unit
