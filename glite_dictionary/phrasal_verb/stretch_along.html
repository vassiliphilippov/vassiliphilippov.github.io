to stretch along

1. VERB

/ strɛtʃ əˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / strɛtʃ əˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
to extend or occupy a long, narrow area or space, especially over a considerable distance
extend over a long area

Examples

A row of shops stretches along the high street, attracting many tourists.
The river stretched along the valley floor as far as they could see.
A thick forest stretches along the border between the two countries.

Synset Examples

The park stretched beneath the train line

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to extend - to spread or reach over a distance or area
  • Synonym: span - to stretch across a space or period
