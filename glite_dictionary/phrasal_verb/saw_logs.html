to saw logs

1. VERB

/ sɔ lɔɡz / (AmE), / sɔː lɒɡz / (BrE)
(informal) To snore loudly or noisily while sleeping.
snore loudly when asleep (informal)
informal

Examples

He was sawing logs so loudly last night that I couldn't fall asleep.
Once my dad starts to saw logs, you can hear him from the next room.
She admitted she saws logs whenever she's really tired.

Synset Examples

she complained that her husband snores

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snore - To breathe noisily during sleep

Etymology

From the imagery of the repetitive, grating sound of sawing wood, likened to the noise of loud snoring.
