to give suck

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv sʌk / (AmE), / ɡɪv sʌk / (BrE)
(of a woman or female animal) to breastfeed or nurse a baby or young animal.
to breastfeed or nurse
archaic

Examples

In the old farmhouse, the mother would give suck to her newborn every morning.
The ewe gave suck to her lambs despite the harsh winter conditions.
In some cultures, women give suck in the presence of family and friends without hesitation.

Synset Examples

The wetnurse suckled the infant
You cannot nurse your baby in public in some places

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to suckle - to nurse at the breast; to breastfeed
  • Synonym: nurse - to feed a baby at the breast
