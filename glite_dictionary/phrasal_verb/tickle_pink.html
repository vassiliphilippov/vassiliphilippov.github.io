to tickle pink

1. VERB

/ ˈtɪkəl pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈtɪkəl pɪŋk / (BrE)
to delight or amuse someone greatly; to make someone extremely pleased or entertained
delight or amuse greatly
informal

Examples

She was tickled pink by the thoughtful birthday surprise her friends arranged.
The news that his painting won first prize tickled pink the young artist.
His grandmother was tickled pink when she received a letter from her old friend.

Synset Examples

The children were thrilled at the prospect of going to the movies
He was inebriated by his phenomenal success

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrill - to cause someone to feel strong excitement or pleasure
  • Synonym: delight - to give someone great pleasure or satisfaction
