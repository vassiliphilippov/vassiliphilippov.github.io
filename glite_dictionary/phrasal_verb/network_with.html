to network with

1. VERB

/ ˈnɛtˌwɝk wɪð / (AmE), / ˈnɛtˌwɜːk wɪð / (BrE)
to interact with others so as to exchange information or develop professional contacts, especially for career or business purposes
interact to exchange information or contacts

Examples

After the seminar, she stayed to network with other professionals in her field.
Young entrepreneurs often attend conferences to network with potential investors and collaborators.
During the lunch break, he made an effort to network with people from different departments.

Synset Examples

He joined WUBA to network with industry leaders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: network - to interact and build connections for professional or social reasons
