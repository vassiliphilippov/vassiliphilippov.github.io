to see to

1. VERB

/ si tuː / (AmE), / siː tuː / (BrE)
to deal with, take responsibility for, or make arrangements for something; to attend to something.
take care of; deal with

Examples

He promised to see to the repairs before winter arrived.
Could you see to the guests while I finish cooking?
She always sees to every detail for our events.

Synset Examples

I'll see to the groceries after work.
Can you see to the arrangements for the meeting?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to attend to - to deal with or take responsibility for something
  • Antonym: neglect - to fail to care for something
