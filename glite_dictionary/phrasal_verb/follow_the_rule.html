to follow the rule

1. VERB

/ ˈfɑː.loʊ ðə ruːl / (AmE), / ˈfɒl.əʊ ðə ruːl / (BrE)
to strictly observe or comply with rules or regulations
comply with regulations

Examples

She always follows the rule when organizing company finances.
We must follow the rule about wearing safety gear in the lab.
If you follow the rule, you'll avoid unnecessary problems.

Synset Examples

They follow the rules religiously to ensure safety.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: break the rule - to not adhere to rules or regulations
  • Synonym: to obey - to do what you are told or what the law, a rule, etc. says you must do
