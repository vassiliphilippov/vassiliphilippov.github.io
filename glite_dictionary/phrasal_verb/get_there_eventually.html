to get there eventually

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ðɛr ɪˈvɛntʃuəli / (AmE), / ɡɛt ðeə ɪˈvɛntʃuəli / (BrE)
to succeed in reaching a goal or completing something after some time or effort
reach a goal after some time

Examples

If we keep working hard, we'll get there eventually.
Despite many setbacks, he managed to get there eventually.
It may take time, but you'll get there eventually if you stay focused.

Synset Examples

She believes that with persistence, she'll get there eventually.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to achieve - to successfully reach a desired objective or result
  • Antonym: to fail - to not reach a goal or objective
