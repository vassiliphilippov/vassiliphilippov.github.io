to grab a coffee

1. VERB

/ ɡræb ə ˈkɑːfi / (AmE), / ɡræb ə ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
to quickly buy or get a cup of coffee, often for a short break or on the go
quickly buy or get a coffee
informal

Examples

Do you want to grab a coffee after class?
She usually grabs a coffee on her way to work every morning.
Let's grab a coffee and catch up this afternoon.

Synset Examples

I’m gonna grab a coffee before the meeting.
Let's grab a cup of coffee after work.
I’ll grab coffee on the way to work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get a coffee - to obtain or buy a cup of coffee
