to cut back on

1. VERB

/ kʌt bæk ɑn / (AmE), / kʌt bæk ɒn / (BrE)
to reduce or decrease the amount or use of something, especially to save money or improve health
reduce amount or use of something

Examples

Many people are trying to cut back on sugar to lead healthier lives.
The company had to cut back on spending due to declining profits.
After seeing his credit card bill, he decided to cut back on unnecessary purchases.

Synset Examples

They decided to cut back on luxuries to pay the bills.
She decided to cut down on her expenses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to reduce - to make something smaller or less in amount, degree, or size
  • Synonym: cut down on - to reduce the size, amount, or number of something
