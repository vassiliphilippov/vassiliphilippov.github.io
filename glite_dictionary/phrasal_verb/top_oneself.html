to top oneself

1. VERB

/ tɑp wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / tɒp wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to achieve something even better than one's previous accomplishment; to surpass one's own earlier efforts.
surpass one's previous achievement

Examples

Each time she releases a new novel, she manages to top herself in terms of creativity.
The director tried to top himself with his latest film, but expectations were high.
After last year's success, he wasn't sure if he could top himself this season.

Synset Examples

Every year, he finds ways to top himself creatively.
He managed to top himself with an even better performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outdo oneself - to do better than one's previous best performance
