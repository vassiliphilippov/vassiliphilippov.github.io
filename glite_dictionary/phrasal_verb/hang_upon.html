to hang upon

1. VERB

/ hæŋ əˈpɑn / (AmE), / hæŋ əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to depend on or be determined by something
depend on; be determined by

Examples

The outcome of the negotiations will hang upon the approval of both parties.
Her hopes for promotion hang upon the results of her performance review.
The court's final decision may hang upon new evidence presented at the last minute.

Synset Examples

Everything seems to hang upon her approval.
The success of the project will hang upon funding.
Their future plans hang upon his decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to depend on - to be determined by or rely on
  • Synonym: to hinge on - to depend entirely on
