to recommend a book

1. VERB

/ ˌrɛkəˈmɛnd ə bʊk / (AmE), / ˌrɛkəˈmɛnd ə bʊk / (BrE)
to suggest a book that someone might enjoy or find useful
suggest a book to someone

Examples

Can you recommend a book that would help me learn more about gardening?
She often recommends a book to her friends after reading something inspiring.
If you liked that novel, I can recommend a book with a similar style.

Synset Examples

Can you recommend a good book for me?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suggest a book - offer a book as a possibility
