to put one over

1. VERB

/ pʊt wʌn ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / pʊt wʌn ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to deceive or trick someone; to successfully fool someone
deceive or trick someone
informal

Examples

He tried to put one over on his coworkers by pretending to be sick.
Don't let them put one over on you with that scam.
She realized he'd put one over and quickly confronted him.

Synset Examples

The immigrant was duped because he trusted everyone
You can't fool me!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - to trick or deceive someone
  • Synonym: dupe - to deliberately mislead or trick someone
  • Synonym: hoax - to trick someone into believing something false
