to expose oneself to

1. VERB

/ ɪkˈspoʊz wʌnˈsɛlf tu / (AmE), / ɪkˈspəʊz wʌnˈself tuː / (BrE)
to deliberately make oneself experience or be affected by something, especially something new or possibly challenging
subject oneself to (an experience or influence)

Examples

Traveling abroad is a great way to expose oneself to different cultures and ideas.
She wanted to expose herself to new perspectives by attending international conferences.
Artists often expose themselves to criticism to improve their work.

Synset Examples

He decided to expose himself to new experiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: subject oneself to - to allow oneself to experience or undergo something
