to overlook

1. VERB

/ ˌoʊvərˈlʊk / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈlʊk / (BrE)
to fail to notice or consider someone or something; to ignore, either unintentionally or by neglect
fail to notice or consider

Examples

Despite his hard work, the team overlooked him when giving out awards.
It hurts to be overlooked for a promotion you deserved.
She often felt overlooked in group conversations.

Synset Examples

People often overlooked him because he was just a wino.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ignore - to pay no attention to
  • Antonym: notice - to become aware of; to pay attention to
