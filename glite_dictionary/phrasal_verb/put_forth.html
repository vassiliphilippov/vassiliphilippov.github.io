to put forth

1. VERB

/ pʊt fɔrθ / (AmE), / pʊt fɔːθ / (BrE)
to make an effort or attempt; to try hard in order to achieve something
make an effort or attempt

Examples

The team put forth a tremendous effort to win the championship.
She always puts forth her best in every situation.
Despite their exhaustion, the volunteers put forth one final push to complete the task.

Synset Examples

We put forth our level best during the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: try - to make an attempt or effort to do something
  • Antonym: to give up - to stop making an effort or cease trying
