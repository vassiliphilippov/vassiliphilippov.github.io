to squash that rumor

1. VERB

/ skwɑʃ ðæt ˈruːmɚ / (AmE), / skwɒʃ ðət ˈruːmə / (BrE)
to decisively stop or suppress a rumor, preventing it from spreading further
to stop or suppress a rumor

Examples

We need to squash that rumor before it causes any more confusion at work.
The company quickly acted to squash that rumor about impending layoffs.
It’s important to squash that rumor before it damages someone’s reputation.

Synset Examples

Let's squash that rumor before it spreads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quash a rumor - to officially put an end to a rumor
