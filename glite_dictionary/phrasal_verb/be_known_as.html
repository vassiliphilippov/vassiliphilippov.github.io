to be known as

1. VERB

/ bi ˈnoʊn æz / (AmE), / bi ˈnəʊn æz / (BrE)
to be recognized, regarded, or referred to by a particular name, title, or reputation.
to be called by a name or reputation

Examples

He is known as a fair and honest judge in his community.
The city was known as the cultural capital during the 19th century.
She is known as the fastest runner on the team.

Synset Examples

she looked on this affair as a joke
He thinks of himself as a brilliant musician
He is reputed to be intelligent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be called - to be referred to by a name
  • Synonym: be referred to as - to be mentioned by a particular name or description
