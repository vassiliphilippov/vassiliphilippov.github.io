to award points to

1. VERB

/ əˈwɔrd pɔɪnts tu / (AmE), / əˈwɔːd pɔɪnts tʊ / (BrE)
to give a score or marks to someone or something in recognition of their achievement or performance
give a score or marks as recognition

Examples

The judges decided to award points to the team for their creativity.
Teachers can award points to students who participate actively in class.
The referee refused to award points to the player after the disputed move.

Synset Examples

He awarded ten points to Gryffindor for bravery.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: deduct points from - to remove points from someone's score
