to throw a party

1. VERB

/ θroʊ ə ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / θrəʊ ə ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
to organize and host a social gathering or celebration, typically for enjoyment
organize and host a social gathering

Examples

We're planning to throw a party for her birthday next month.
They decided to throw a party after winning the championship.
Do you want to throw a party at your house this weekend?

Synset Examples
