to stick on

1. VERB

/ stɪk ɑn / (AmE), / stɪk ɒn / (BrE)
to apply a thick or heavy layer of something such as paint or makeup
apply a thick layer (e.g., paint, makeup)

Examples

She likes to stick on plenty of sunscreen before going to the beach.
They stuck on multiple coats of paint until the wall looked perfect.
He tends to stick on too much cologne before important meetings.

2. VERB

/ stɪk ɑn / (AmE), / stɪk ɒn / (BrE)
to attach or fasten something onto the surface of something else, usually so that it stays in place
attach or fasten onto a surface

Examples

Please stick on a stamp before sending the letter.
She stuck on the label to make sure her bag was identified.
We can stick on these decals to decorate the wall.

Synset Examples

affix the seal here

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affix - to attach or fasten to something
  • Antonym: remove - to take something off a surface
