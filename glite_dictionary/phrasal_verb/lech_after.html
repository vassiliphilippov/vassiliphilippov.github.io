to lech after

1. VERB

/ lɛtʃ ˈæf.tɚ / (AmE), / lɛtʃ ˈɑːf.tə / (BrE)
(of a person) to have a strong or excessive sexual desire for someone, often in a way considered inappropriate or unwanted.
have strong sexual desire for
informal

Examples

He was caught leching after his coworker at the office party.
Some men can't help leching after every attractive woman they see.
She felt uncomfortable because he kept leching after her throughout the evening.

Synset Examples

he is lusting after his secretary

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to lust after - to have a strong sexual desire for someone or something

Etymology

From 'lech' (verb, to behave in a lecherous way), itself from 'lecher' and Middle English origins.
