to file a lawsuit

1. VERB

/ faɪl ə ˈlɔː.suːt / (AmE), / faɪl ə ˈlɔː.sjuːt / (BrE)
to officially begin legal action by presenting a complaint to a court of law
initiate legal action in court
legal

Examples

She decided to file a lawsuit after her contract was unfairly terminated.
The company filed a lawsuit against its former employee for breach of confidentiality.
Before you file a lawsuit, it's wise to consider the legal costs involved.

Synset Examples

They filed a lawsuit challenging the recent gerrymander.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sue - to start legal proceedings against someone
