to remassage the dough

1. VERB

/ ˌriːməˈsɑːʒ ðə doʊ / (AmE), / ˌriːməˈsɑːʒ ðə dəʊ / (BrE)
to knead dough again, typically to improve its texture or consistency
knead dough again

Examples

Don't forget to remassage the dough if it starts to lose its elasticity.
