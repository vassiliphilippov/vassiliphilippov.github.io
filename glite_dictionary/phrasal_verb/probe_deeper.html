to probe deeper

1. VERB

/ proʊb ˈdiːpər / (AmE), / prəʊb ˈdiːpə / (BrE)
to investigate or examine something more thoroughly or in greater detail
investigate more thoroughly

Examples

The committee decided to probe deeper into the company's financial records.
Detectives needed to probe deeper to find the evidence that would solve the case.
We must probe deeper if we want to fully understand the causes of the problem.

Synset Examples

The journalist decided to probe deeper into the scandal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to investigate - carry out a systematic or formal inquiry to discover facts
  • Synonym: to explore - examine or discuss in detail
