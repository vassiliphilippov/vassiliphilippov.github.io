to act like a Karen

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈkɛrən / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈkærən / (BrE)
to behave in a demanding, entitled, or unreasonable manner, especially in a way perceived as excessive or inappropriate when interacting with others, often in a customer service context
behave in an entitled or demanding way
informal slang

Examples

Please don't act like a Karen when talking to the staff at the restaurant.
She was acting like a Karen when she demanded to speak to the manager over a minor issue.
Whenever he doesn't get his way, he tends to act like a Karen and make a big fuss.

Synset Examples

She acted like a Karen during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Karen - a stereotype of an entitled, demanding woman, especially in North America
  • Synonym: be entitled - to have or show an excessive sense of entitlement

Etymology

From the given name Karen, popularized as a stereotype for entitled or demanding individuals in American culture around 2017.
