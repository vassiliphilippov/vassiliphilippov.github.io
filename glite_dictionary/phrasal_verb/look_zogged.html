to look zogged

1. VERB

/ lʊk zɑgd / (AmE), / lʊk zɒgd / (BrE)
to appear extremely tired or exhausted, especially after strenuous activity or lack of sleep
appear extremely tired or exhausted
informal

Examples

After pulling an all-nighter to finish his project, he looked zogged at the morning meeting.
You always look zogged after those overnight shifts at the hospital.
She looked zogged when she got off the ten-hour train ride.

Synset Examples

He looked zogged after the long flight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausted - very tired; drained of energy
  • Antonym: refreshed - feeling rested and energetic again
