to raise the issue

1. VERB

/ ˈreɪz ði ˈɪʃuː / (AmE), / ˈreɪz ði ˈɪsjuː / (BrE)
to bring up a topic, question, or problem for discussion or consideration
bring up a topic for discussion

Examples

If no one else will raise the issue, I suppose I must.
During the interview, she decided to raise the issue of pay equity.
He was hesitant about raising the issue in front of the entire team.

Synset Examples

The teacher will raise the issue at the next meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to bring up - to mention or introduce a topic
