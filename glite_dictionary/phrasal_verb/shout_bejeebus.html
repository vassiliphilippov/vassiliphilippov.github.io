to shout bejeebus

1. VERB

/ ʃaʊt bəˈdʒiːbəs / (AmE), / ʃaʊt bəˈdʒiːbəs / (BrE)
to exclaim "bejeebus" in surprise or frustration, usually in response to something unexpected or shocking
exclaim "bejeebus" in surprise or frustration
informal

Examples

When the dog jumped out from behind the door, I couldn't help but shout bejeebus!
She nearly dropped her coffee when she shouted bejeebus at the shocking news.
If you ever see the bill, you might just shout bejeebus too!

Synset Examples

When I saw the price, it made me shout bejeebus!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shout - utter a loud call or cry

Etymology

"Bejeebus" is a humorous alteration of "bejesus," an exclamation of surprise or emphasis.
