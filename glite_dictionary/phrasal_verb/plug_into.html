to plug into

1. VERB

/ plʌɡ ˈɪntu / (AmE), / plʌɡ ˈɪntə / (BrE)
to connect an electrical device to a power outlet or supply by inserting its plug
connect a device to power

Examples

Before using the blender, make sure to plug into a suitable power outlet.
You need to plug into the socket in order to charge your laptop.
The technician will plug into the main supply during the installation.

Synset Examples

Please plug in the toaster!
Connect the TV so we can watch the football game tonight

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to unplug - to disconnect from a power outlet
