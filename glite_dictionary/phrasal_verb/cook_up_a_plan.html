to cook up a plan

1. VERB

/ kʊk ʌp ə plæn / (AmE), / kʊk ʌp ə plæn / (BrE)
to invent or devise a strategy, often in a clever or secretive way
devise a strategy or plan

Examples

After hearing about the problem, she decided to cook up a plan to solve it.
The marketing team managed to cook up a plan that impressed the executives.
He quickly cooked up a plan for a weekend getaway.

Synset Examples

She decided to cook up a plan for the surprise party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devise - to plan or invent by careful thought
  • Synonym: conceive a plan - to form or create a plan in the mind
