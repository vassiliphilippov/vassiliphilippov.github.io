to hop off

1. VERB

/ hɑp ɔf / (AmE), / hɒp ɒf / (BrE)
to leave or get off a vehicle or place quickly and often lightly
leave or get off quickly

Examples

I need to hop off the bus at the next stop.
She hopped off her bike and rushed inside.
Let me hop off the call; I'll talk to you later.

Synset Examples

He decided to hop off early to avoid traffic.
I need to hop off the bus at the next stop.
Make sure to hop off the train before it leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to get off - to leave a form of transport
  • Synonym: alight - to get off a train, bus, or other vehicle
