to stay informed

1. VERB

/ steɪ ɪnˈfɔrmd / (AmE), / steɪ ɪnˈfɔːmd / (BrE)
to continue to keep oneself up-to-date with the latest information or developments
keep oneself updated or current

Examples

It's important to stay informed about changes in public health guidelines.
She subscribes to several newsletters to stay informed about her industry.
We rely on social media to stay informed during emergencies.

Synset Examples

He reads the news daily to stay informed.
We need to stay informed about issues in Worst Korea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keep informed - to ensure someone has the latest information
