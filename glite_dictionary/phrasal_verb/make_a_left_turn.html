to make a left turn

1. VERB

/ meɪk ə lɛft tɝn / (AmE), / meɪk ə lɛft tɜːn / (BrE)
to turn left, especially while driving, typically at an intersection or junction
to turn left while driving

Examples

At the stop sign, she decided to make a left turn instead of going straight.
Drivers are required to signal before they make a left turn in most countries.
You can't make a left turn at that intersection during rush hour.

Synset Examples

Make a left turn at the next intersection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turn left - to move or cause to move in a leftward direction
  • Antonym: make a right turn - to turn right at an intersection
