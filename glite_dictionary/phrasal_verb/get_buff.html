to get buff

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt bʌf / (AmE), / ɡɛt bʌf / (BrE)
to become muscular and physically fit, often through exercise or bodybuilding
become muscular or fit
informal

Examples

He spent months at the gym to get buff for the summer.
Many people start lifting weights because they want to get buff.
She challenged herself to get buff before her next birthday.

Synset Examples

He wants to get buff before his wedding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulk up - to increase muscle mass and size through exercise
  • Antonym: to slim down - to lose weight and become thinner
