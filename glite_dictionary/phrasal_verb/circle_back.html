to circle back

1. VERB

/ ˈsɝːkəl bæk / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkəl bæk / (BrE)
to return to a topic, issue, or task later after having temporarily moved away from it
to return to a topic later

Examples

Let me circle back to your earlier question at the end of the meeting.
If we don't have a solution now, we'll circle back next week with an update.
She promised to circle back after collecting all the necessary information.

Synset Examples

Let's circle back to this topic later.
We'll circle back after we gather more data.
Can we circle back tomorrow for an update?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: return - to come or go back to a place or topic
