to straighten out

1. VERB

/ ˈstreɪtn aʊt / (AmE), / ˈstreɪtn aʊt / (BrE)
to make something straight or level, removing bends or curves
make straight; remove bends or curves

Examples

She tried to straighten out the bent wire with her hands.
The gardener straightened out the hose before watering the plants.
Could you straighten out that rug? It looks crooked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to straighten - make or become straight
