to place upright

1. VERB

/ pleɪs ˈʌp.raɪt / (AmE), / pleɪs ˈʌp.raɪt / (BrE)
to put something in a vertical position, standing up rather than lying down or flat.
put in a vertical position

Examples

She carefully placed upright the framed photograph on the table.
After unpacking the TV, we need to place upright the screen before turning it on.
Please place upright all the chairs before leaving the room.

Synset Examples

Can you stand the bookshelf up?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stand up - to move from a sitting or lying position to a vertical position (object or person)
