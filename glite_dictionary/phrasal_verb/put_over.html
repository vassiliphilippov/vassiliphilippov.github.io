to put over

1. VERB

/ pʊt ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / pʊt ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to communicate an idea or message successfully so that it is understood
communicate an idea successfully

Examples

She managed to put over her point clearly during the meeting.
The teacher struggled to put over the concept to his students.
His enthusiasm helped him put over complex ideas to the audience.

Synset Examples

I couldn't get across the message
He put over the idea very well

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to get across - to successfully communicate an idea
  • Synonym: to convey - to make (an idea, impression, or feeling) known or understandable
