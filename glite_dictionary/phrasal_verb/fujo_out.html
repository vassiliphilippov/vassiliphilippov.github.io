to fujo out

1. VERB

/ ˈfuːdʒoʊ aʊt / (AmE), / ˈfuːdʒəʊ aʊt / (BrE)
to engage enthusiastically in fandom activities or discussions related to fujoshi culture, especially about male-male romantic pairings in anime or manga
enthusiastically take part in fujoshi fandom
informal slang

Examples

Whenever a new episode drops, they love to fujo out in the group chat for hours.
She didn't hesitate to fujo out when someone mentioned her favorite BL ship at the panel.
At the sleepover, everyone started to fujo out over the latest manga release.

Synset Examples

They love to fujo out and discuss their favorite characters.
He decided to fujo out at the anime convention.
She wasn’t afraid to fujo out in front of her friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to geek out - to enthusiastically discuss or pursue a hobby or interest

Etymology

From Japanese 'fujoshi' (a female fan of yaoi/BL manga), combined with English 'out' as in 'geek out'.
