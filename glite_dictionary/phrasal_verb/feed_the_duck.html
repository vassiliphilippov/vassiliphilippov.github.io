to feed the duck

1. VERB

/ fid ðə dʌk / (AmE), / fiːd ðə dʌk / (BrE)
to give food to a duck, often as a leisure activity at ponds or parks
give food to a duck

Examples

Every Saturday, the children love to feed the duck at the local park.
Please don't feed the duck bread, as it can be harmful to them.
We brought some seeds so we could feed the duck by the river.

Synset Examples

They went to the pond to feed the duck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feed - to give food to a person or animal
