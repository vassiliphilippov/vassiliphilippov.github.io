to move forward irregardless

1. VERB

/ muːv ˈfɔrwərd ɪˈreɡərdləs / (AmE), / muːv ˈfɔːwəd ɪˈreɡɑːdləs / (BrE)
to proceed or continue ahead despite difficulties, obstacles, or concerns, typically using the nonstandard word 'irregardless' for emphasis or in error for 'regardless'
proceed despite difficulties (nonstandard)
informal

Examples

Despite the objections, they chose to move forward irregardless of the consequences.
The company will move forward irregardless of the economic downturn.
She decided to move forward irregardless of what others thought.

Synset Examples

They decided to move forward irregardless of the risks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: move forward regardless - to continue despite possible difficulties or opposition
