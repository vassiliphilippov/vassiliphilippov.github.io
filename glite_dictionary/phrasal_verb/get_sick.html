to get sick

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt sɪk / (AmE), / ɡɛt sɪk / (BrE)
to become ill or unwell; to develop symptoms of illness.
to become ill

Examples

If you get sick, you should see a doctor as soon as possible.
Many people get sick after traveling to new countries.
He was afraid he might get sick from eating the street food.

Synset Examples

She tends to get sick when the seasons change.
Try not to get sick during the winter months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: become ill - to start to be in a state of sickness or disease
  • Antonym: to recover - to become healthy again after being sick
