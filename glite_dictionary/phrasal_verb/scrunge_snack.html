to scrunge snack

1. VERB

/ skrʌndʒ snæk / (AmE), / skrʌndʒ snæk / (BrE)
to obtain snacks by asking or taking them from others, often informally or without offering payment
obtain snacks from others informally
informal

Examples

They try to scrunge snack from their classmates during lunchtime.
It's become a habit for Tim to scrunge snack off his friends at every party.
Jenna didn't pack lunch, so she had to scrunge snack from coworkers throughout the day.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
