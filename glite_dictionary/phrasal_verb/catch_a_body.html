to catch a body

1. VERB

/ kætʃ ə ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / kæʧ ə ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
to find a dead person, especially in the context of a murder investigation; sometimes used as slang for being involved in or committing a killing
find a dead body in a crime context
slang

Examples

The detective was determined to catch a body before the suspect could flee the city.
Rumors spread quickly after someone claimed to have caught a body in the alley last night.
In many crime novels, the story begins when police catch a body under mysterious circumstances.

Synset Examples

The detective was determined to catch a body this week.
In the movie, they needed to catch a body to solve the mystery.
He was arrested after trying to catch a body last night.
