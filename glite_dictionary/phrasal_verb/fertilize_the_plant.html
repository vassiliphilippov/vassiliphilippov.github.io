to fertilize the plant

1. VERB

/ ˈfɝːt̬əˌlaɪz ðə plænt / (AmE), / ˈfəːtɪˌlaɪz ðə plɑːnt / (BrE)
to add nutrients or fertilizer to a plant to promote its growth
add nutrients to a plant

Examples

Be sure to fertilize the plant every few weeks during the growing season.
Gardeners often fertilize the plant with organic compost for better results.
You should avoid fertilizing the plant when the soil is already rich in nutrients.

Synset Examples

We need to fertilize the plants regularly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feed the plant - to give food or nutrients to a plant
