to slip (someone) a fiver

1. VERB

/ slɪp mi ə ˈfaɪvɚ / (AmE), / slɪp miː ə ˈfaɪvə / (BrE)
to give someone five pounds (or dollars) secretly or discreetly
give five pounds/dollars discreetly
informal british

Examples

Could you slip me a fiver to cover the taxi fare?
When I forgot my wallet, my brother slipped me a fiver without saying anything.
She slipped me a fiver so I could get a drink at the bar.

Synset Examples

He slipped me a fiver to help with my gas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: give (someone) a fiver - to hand someone five pounds or dollars
  • Degree Higher: slip (someone) a tenner - to give someone ten pounds/dollars discreetly
