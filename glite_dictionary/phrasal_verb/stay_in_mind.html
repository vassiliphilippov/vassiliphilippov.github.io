to stay in mind

1. VERB

/ steɪ ɪn maɪnd / (AmE), / steɪ ɪn maɪnd / (BrE)
to remain in one's memory; to be remembered or cherished over time
remain in memory; be remembered

Examples

Her words will always stay in mind whenever I face difficulties.
The lesson learned from his mistake stays in mind even after many years.
This special moment is sure to stay in mind for a lifetime.

Synset Examples

This beautiful sunset will stay in my mind forever.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stick in one's mind - be easily remembered
  • Antonym: to forget - fail to remember
