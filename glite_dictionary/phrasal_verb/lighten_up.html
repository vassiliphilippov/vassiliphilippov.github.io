to lighten up

1. VERB

/ ˈlaɪtn̩ ʌp / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtn̩ ʌp / (BrE)
to become more cheerful or less serious, often by becoming more relaxed or optimistic
become more cheerful or relaxed
informal

Examples

You should lighten up and enjoy the party instead of worrying so much.
After hearing the good news, she finally lightened up and smiled.
It's time to lighten up—not everything has to be so serious.

Synset Examples

after a glass of wine, he lightened up a bit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cheer up - to become happier or make someone happier
