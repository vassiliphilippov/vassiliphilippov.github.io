to graduate from college

1. VERB

/ ˈɡrædʒuˌeɪt frəm ˈkɑlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɡradʒuːeɪt frəm ˈkɒlɪdʒ / (BrE)
to complete a degree program and receive a diploma from a college or university
complete a college degree program

Examples

After four years of hard work, she finally graduated from college with honors.
Many students hope to graduate from college and find a good job.
He will be the first person in his family to graduate from college.

Synset Examples

My son just graduated from college this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graduate - to complete a course of study and receive a diploma
  • Antonym: drop out of college - to leave college before completing a degree
