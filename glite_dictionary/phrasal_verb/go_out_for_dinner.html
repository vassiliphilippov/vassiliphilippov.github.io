to go out for dinner

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ aʊt fɔr ˈdɪnər / (AmE), / ɡəʊ aʊt fə ˈdɪnə / (BrE)
to leave one's home to eat dinner at a restaurant or similar establishment; to dine out
to eat dinner at a restaurant

Examples

We decided to go out for dinner to celebrate our anniversary.
Sometimes, after a long week at work, I just want to go out for dinner.
Do you want to go out for dinner or should we cook at home tonight?

Synset Examples

Are you ready to go out for dinner tonight?
Let’s go out for dinner tonight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to dine out - to eat a meal away from home, especially at a restaurant
