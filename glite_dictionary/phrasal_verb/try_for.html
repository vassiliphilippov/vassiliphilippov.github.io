to try for

1. VERB

/ ˈtraɪ fɔr / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ fə / (BrE)
to attempt to obtain or achieve something, especially by making a determined effort
attempt to obtain or achieve

Examples

After years of training, she decided to try for a place in the national team.
Many students try for scholarships to help pay their tuition fees.
He will try for a promotion at work next year.

Synset Examples

She tried for the Olympics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attempt - to make an effort to achieve or complete something
  • Synonym: to go for - to attempt to achieve or win something
