to network effectively

1. VERB

/ ˈnɛtˌwɝk ɪˈfɛktɪvli / (AmE), / ˈnɛtˌwɜːk ɪˈfɛktɪvli / (BrE)
to build and maintain professional relationships in a purposeful and skillful way, often to advance one's career or business goals
build professional relationships skillfully

Examples

She learned to network effectively at conferences, which opened new career opportunities for her.
To succeed in international business, you must network effectively across cultures and industries.
New graduates often struggle to network effectively until they gain more experience.

Synset Examples

As a business girl, she knows how to network effectively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: network - to interact with others to exchange information and develop contacts
  • Synonym: schmooze - to chat or converse informally, especially in order to gain an advantage or make social or professional connections
