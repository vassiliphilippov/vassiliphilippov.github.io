to feel poopless

1. VERB

/ fil ˈpuːpləs / (AmE), / fiːl ˈpuːpləs / (BrE)
To feel extremely exhausted or lacking in energy.
to feel very tired or drained
informal

Examples

After running the marathon, he said he felt poopless and just wanted to lie down.
By the end of finals week, most of us feel poopless from all the studying.
She was so busy with her project that she was feeling poopless by Friday evening.

Synset Examples

She felt poopless after a long day at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausted - extremely tired; lacking energy
  • Antonym: rejuvenated - feeling refreshed or full of energy
