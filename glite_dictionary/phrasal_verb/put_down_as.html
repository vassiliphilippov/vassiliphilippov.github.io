to put down as

1. VERB

/ pʊt daʊn æz / (AmE), / pʊt daʊn æz / (BrE)
to register someone for something or assign someone a particular role or classification
register or assign a role to someone

Examples

You can put down as a sponsor if you'd like to donate.
The teacher put her down as the group leader for the project.
They put me down as a possible replacement on the waiting list.

Synset Examples

You can put me down as a volunteer for the charity.
He was put down as the team leader for the project.
She was put down as the main speaker for the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: list - to write or mention the names of people or things in a list
  • Synonym: to assign - to give someone a particular job or responsibility
