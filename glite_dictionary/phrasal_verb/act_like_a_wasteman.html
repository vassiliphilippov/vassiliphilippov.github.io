to act like a wasteman

1. VERB

/ ˈækt laɪk ə ˈweɪstˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈækt laɪk ə ˈweɪstmən / (BrE)
to behave in an irresponsible, foolish, or worthless manner, as described by the term 'wasteman'; often used in British slang
behave irresponsibly or foolishly (British slang)
slang british

Examples

Stop acting like a wasteman and start taking responsibility for your actions.
She told him to quit acting like a wasteman after he missed another important meeting.
If you keep acting like a wasteman, people won't take you seriously.

Synset Examples

Stop acting like a wasteman and get your life together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wasteman - a worthless or foolish person (British slang)

Etymology

Slang usage combining 'act' with 'wasteman', a British slang term meaning a worthless or foolish person.
