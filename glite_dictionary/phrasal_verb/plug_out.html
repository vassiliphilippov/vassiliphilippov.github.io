to plug out

1. VERB

/ plʌɡ aʊt / (AmE), / plʌɡ aʊt / (BrE)
to disconnect an electrical device from the power supply by removing its plug from a socket
disconnect by removing a plug

Examples

Please plug out your laptop before you leave the office.
She always remembers to plug out her phone charger at night.
He quickly plugged out the television during the thunderstorm.

Synset Examples

He forgot to plug out the toaster after breakfast.
Please plug out the device before cleaning it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to unplug - to disconnect an electrical device by removing its plug from the socket
