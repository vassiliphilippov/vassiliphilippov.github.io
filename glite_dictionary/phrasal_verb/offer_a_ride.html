to offer a ride

1. VERB

/ ˈɔfər ə raɪd / (AmE), / ˈɒfə ə raɪd / (BrE)
to provide transportation to someone in your vehicle, usually as a favor or gesture of kindness
to give someone a lift in your vehicle

Examples

She offered a ride to her coworker when she saw him waiting at the bus stop.
It was raining, so Mark kindly offered a ride to his friends after the movie.
Don't hesitate to offer a ride if you see someone stranded on the roadside.

Synset Examples

He offered me a ride home after the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: give a lift - to give someone a ride in your car
