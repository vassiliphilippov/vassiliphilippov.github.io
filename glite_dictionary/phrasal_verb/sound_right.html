to sound right

1. VERB

/ saʊnd raɪt / (AmE), / saʊnd raɪt / (BrE)
to seem correct, reasonable, or acceptable when considered or heard
seem correct or reasonable

Examples

That explanation doesn't sound right to me, so I think we should double-check the facts.
If this date doesn't sound right, we can try to find the correct one in our records.
Your suggestion really sounds right, and I think we should go with it.

Synset Examples

I don't know about that; it doesn't sound right.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to seem - to give the impression of being something
  • Synonym: to appear - to give the impression of being something
  • Antonym: sound wrong - to seem incorrect or inappropriate
