to spin out

1. VERB

/ spɪn aʊt / (AmE), / spɪn aʊt / (BrE)
to make something last longer than necessary; to prolong or extend the duration of something
prolong or extend (time, activity)

Examples

The speaker tried to spin out his presentation to fill more time.
She managed to spin out her vacation for another week.
The author spins out the story with unnecessary details.

Synset Examples

spin out a visit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to prolong - make something last longer
  • Synonym: to extend - cause something to continue for a longer period
  • Antonym: to shorten - make something shorter in duration
