to be due

1. VERB

/ bi du / (AmE), / biː djuː / (BrE)
to be caused by or the result of something
be caused by something

Examples

The delay was due to heavy traffic.
Her illness is due to a bacterial infection.
The success of the project is due in large part to teamwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: result from - to happen or exist as a result of something
  • Synonym: be caused by - to have something as a cause
