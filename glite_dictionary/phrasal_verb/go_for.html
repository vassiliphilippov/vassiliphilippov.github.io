to go for

1. VERB

/ ˈɡoʊ fər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ fə / (BrE)
to attempt to achieve or obtain something; to try for something earnestly
attempt to achieve something

Examples

If you want the promotion, you should go for it and show your interest.
Despite the difficulty, she decided to go for first place in the competition.
Many people dream of starting their own business, but only a few ever go for it.

Synset Examples

She tried for the Olympics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to try for - make an attempt to achieve something
