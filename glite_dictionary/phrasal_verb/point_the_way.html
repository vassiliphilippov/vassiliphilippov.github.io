to point the way

1. VERB

/ pɔɪnt ðə weɪ / (AmE), / pɔɪnt ðə weɪ / (BrE)
to indicate or show the correct path, direction, or approach to follow
show the correct direction or method

Examples

The street signs point the way for drivers unfamiliar with the area.
Her research may point the way to a new cure for the disease.
The guide raised his lantern to point the way through the dark tunnel.

Synset Examples

The sign pointed the way to London

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guide - to show or lead the way
  • Antonym: to mislead - to give the wrong direction or guidance
