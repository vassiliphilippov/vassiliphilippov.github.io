to act squirrelly

1. VERB

/ ækt ˈskwɜːrli / (AmE), / ækt ˈskwɪəli / (BrE)
to behave in a strange, nervous, or unpredictable manner
behave in a strange or nervous way
informal american

Examples

Whenever you ask Tom about his job, he tends to act squirrelly and change the subject.
The children started to act squirrelly after drinking too much soda at the party.
If your dog starts to act squirrelly around strangers, keep a close eye on him.

Synset Examples

She acted squirrelly when asked about her weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: act strangely - to behave in a strange way
  • Antonym: act normally - to behave in a normal or usual way

Etymology

From 'squirrelly', an American informal term likening nervous or odd behavior to that of a squirrel.
