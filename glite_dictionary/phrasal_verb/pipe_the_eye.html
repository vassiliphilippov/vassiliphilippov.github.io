to pipe the eye

1. VERB

/ paɪp ði aɪ / (AmE), / paɪp ðiː aɪ / (BrE)
to apply icing or decoration to a cake or pastry in a way that creates an eye-shaped design
apply eye-shaped icing decoration

Examples

She showed me how to pipe the eye without mistakes.
In the bakery class, we learned to pipe the eye on cupcakes for decoration.
If you want your cookies to look professional, try to pipe the eye carefully using a steady hand.
