to passed away

1. VERB

/ pæst əˈweɪ / (AmE), / pɑːst əˈweɪ / (BrE)
(of a person) to die, especially used as a polite or gentle way to refer to death.
die (euphemism)

Examples

His great-uncle passed away in his sleep at the age of 92.
We were saddened to hear that their dog had passed away last week.
She often thinks about her friend who passed away years ago.

Synset Examples

They gathered to mourn those who passed away.
Her grandmother passed away peacefully last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: die - to cease living
