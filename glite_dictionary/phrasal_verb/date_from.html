to date from

1. VERB

/ deɪt frʌm / (AmE), / deɪt frɒm / (BrE)
to have existed since or been made in a particular time in the past
originate in a specific past time

Examples

This building dates from the 18th century.
Some of the manuscripts date from the medieval period.
The tradition dates from ancient times.

Synset Examples

This story dates back 200 years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to date back - to have existed since a particular time in the past
