to come to hand

1. VERB

/ kʌm tə hænd / (AmE), / kʌm tə hænd / (BrE)
to become known, revealed, or discovered; to come into someone's possession or awareness
become known or revealed

Examples

New evidence has come to hand that could change the outcome of the trial.
Several important documents have come to hand since the start of the investigation.
Details about the missing funds only came to hand after a thorough audit was conducted.

Synset Examples

The truth finally came to light

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to come to light - to become known or revealed
