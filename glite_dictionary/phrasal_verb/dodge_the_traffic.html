to dodge the traffic

1. VERB

/ dɑdʒ ðə ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / dɒdʒ ðə ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
to avoid areas of heavy or congested traffic, especially by taking alternate routes
avoid areas of traffic congestion

Examples

We left early to dodge the traffic on our way to the airport.
She always checks her GPS to dodge the traffic during rush hour.
He managed to dodge the traffic by taking a shortcut.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avoid the traffic - to deliberately stay away from busy roads
