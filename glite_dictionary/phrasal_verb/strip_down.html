to strip down

1. VERB

/ strɪp daʊn / (AmE), / strɪp daʊn / (BrE)
to remove your clothes, especially all or most of them
remove clothing; get undressed

Examples

The children were told to strip down to their swimsuits before entering the pool.
He had to strip down for the medical examination.
Before the surgery, the patient was asked to strip down to his underwear.

Synset Examples

please don't undress in front of everybody!
She strips in front of strangers every night for a living

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undress - to take off your clothes
  • Antonym: dress - to put on clothes
