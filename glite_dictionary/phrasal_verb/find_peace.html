to find peace

1. VERB

/ faɪnd pis / (AmE), / faɪnd piːs / (BrE)
to reach a state of tranquility, acceptance, or inner calm, especially after difficulty or disturbance
achieve tranquility or inner calm

Examples

After years of turmoil, she finally found peace within herself.
Many people turn to nature to find peace and quiet away from the city.
He hopes to find peace by forgiving those who hurt him.

Synset Examples

She found peace in meditation every morning.
The artist found quietus in painting by the sea.
She found repose in the quiet of the library.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: find quiet - to achieve a state of quietness or calm
  • Synonym: to find solace - to find comfort or consolation, especially in grief or misfortune
