to dart across

1. VERB

/ dɑrt əˈkrɔs / (AmE), / dɑːt əˈkrɒs / (BrE)
to move suddenly and quickly across a space
move quickly across a space

Examples

When the light turned green, the children darted across the playground to reach the swings first.
The squirrel darted across the road, narrowly avoiding the approaching car.
She saw an opportunity and darted across the crowded hallway to greet her friend.

Synset Examples

He decided to dart across the street to catch the bus.
The skinny cat darted across the street quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dash across - to run or move quickly across something
  • Antonym: crawl across - to move slowly across something
