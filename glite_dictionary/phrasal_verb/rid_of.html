to rid of

1. VERB

/ rɪd əv / (AmE), / rɪd ɒv / (BrE)
To remove or free someone or something from a troublesome, unwanted, or harmful person, thing, or situation.
remove or free from something unwanted

Examples

We need to rid of these old clothes to make space in the closet.
He hopes to rid of his bad habits this year.
The medicine helps you rid of the infection quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to get rid of - eliminate or dispose of something unwanted
