to combat genderism

1. VERB

/ kəmˈbæt ˈdʒɛndərˌɪzəm / (AmE), / kəmˈbæt ˈdʒɛndərˌɪzəm / (BrE)
to actively oppose or fight against discrimination or unfair treatment based on gender
to oppose gender discrimination

Examples

Many organizations have implemented policies to combat genderism in the hiring process.
Teachers play a crucial role in helping to combat genderism from an early age.
The government launched an initiative to combat genderism across all public institutions.

Synset Examples

The campaign aimed to combat genderism in the workplace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fight sexism - to oppose discrimination based on sex or gender
