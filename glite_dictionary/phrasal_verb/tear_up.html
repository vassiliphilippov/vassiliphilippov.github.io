to tear up

1. VERB

/ tɛr ʌp / (AmE), / teə ʌp / (BrE)
to tear something into small pieces or shreds
rip into shreds

Examples

She angrily tore up the letter and threw it in the trash.
The children tore up their old notebooks at the end of the school year.
After reading the document, he tore up the contract into tiny pieces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to rip up - to tear something into pieces
