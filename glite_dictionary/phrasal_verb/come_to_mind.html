to come to mind

1. VERB

/ kʌm tə maɪnd / (AmE), / kʌm tə maɪnd / (BrE)
(of an idea, thought, or memory) to occur in one's thoughts; to be remembered or thought of suddenly.
to be thought of or remembered suddenly

Examples

Whenever I smell fresh bread, childhood memories come to mind.
Her name immediately came to mind when I heard about the job opening.
A few possible solutions come to mind if we think this through carefully.

Synset Examples

His name comes to mind when you mention the strike

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occur to - (of an idea or thought) to enter one's mind
  • Antonym: to forget - to be unable to remember something
