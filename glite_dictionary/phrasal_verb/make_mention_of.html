to make mention of

1. VERB

/ meɪk ˈmɛnʃən əv / (AmE), / meɪk ˈmɛnʃən ɒv / (BrE)
to refer to or briefly speak about something or someone, especially in passing
refer to or briefly mention

Examples

During the meeting, she made mention of the upcoming changes to the project.
The article doesn't make mention of the recent developments in the case.
If you speak to him, please make mention of my invitation to the party.

Synset Examples

Did she make mention of her plans for the weekend?
Please make mention of the deadline in your report.
He forgot to make mention of the important details.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mention - to refer to something briefly; to speak about something in passing
