to close your eyes

1. VERB

/ kloʊz jər aɪz / (AmE), / kləʊz jɔː aɪz / (BrE)
to shut one's eyelids; also, to deliberately ignore something or take a risk without knowing the outcome
shut your eyelids; ignore or risk

Examples

She told everyone to close your eyes before bringing out the birthday cake.
Sometimes you just have to close your eyes and take a leap of faith.
He chose to close your eyes to the obvious problems in the plan.

Synset Examples

Please close your eyes for a surprise.
Sometimes you need to shut your eyes and trust.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shut your eyes - to close your eyelids
  • Antonym: open your eyes - to open your eyelids; to become aware
