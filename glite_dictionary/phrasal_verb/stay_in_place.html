to stay in place

1. VERB

/ steɪ ɪn pleɪs / (AmE), / steɪ ɪn pleɪs / (BrE)
to remain stationary; to not move from a particular position or location
remain stationary; not move

Examples

The sign must stay in place during the entire event for safety reasons.
Please make sure the bandage stays in place while your wound heals.
The statues have stayed in place for centuries, untouched by time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to remain - to continue to exist or stay in the same place
  • Antonym: move - to change place or position
