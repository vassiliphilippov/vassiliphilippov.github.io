to fitted out

1. VERB

/ ˌfɪtɪd ˈaʊt / (AmE), / ˌfɪtɪd ˈaʊt / (BrE)
supplied with the necessary equipment, clothing, or furnishings for a particular purpose
equipped; provided with necessary items

Examples

The expedition was fitted out with the latest survival gear.
She had her new apartment fitted out with modern furnishings.
The ship is being fitted out before its maiden voyage.

Synset Examples

equipped for service in the Arctic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equipped - provided with the necessary supplies or equipment
  • Antonym: bare - without the usual covering, contents, or equipment
