to make shit of

1. VERB

/ meɪk ʃɪt ʌv / (AmE), / meɪk ʃɪt ɒv / (BrE)
(vulgar, informal) To treat something with contempt, belittle, or undermine its worth or significance.
disparage or undermine (vulgar, informal)
informal vulgar

Examples

Please don’t make shit of my ideas before you’ve even heard them out.
He always tries to make shit of other people’s achievements at work.
Stop making shit of everything—I worked hard on this project.

Synset Examples

Don't make shit of my hard work.
He tends to make shit of everything I say.
Stop making shit of my efforts and appreciate them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to belittle - to make someone or something seem unimportant
  • Synonym: to undermine - to weaken or damage (especially confidence, authority, or power)
