to get back to work

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈbæk tə wɜrk / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈbæk tə wɜːk / (BrE)
to return to one’s job or tasks after a pause or interruption
return to one’s job or tasks

Examples

After my lunch break, I have to get back to work and finish the report.
She plans to get back to work as soon as her sick leave ends.
Let’s get back to work before the boss notices we’re gone.

Synset Examples

I need to get back to work soon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resume - to begin doing something again after a pause
  • Antonym: to take a break - to pause work or activity temporarily
