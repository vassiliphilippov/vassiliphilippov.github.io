to give forth

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv fɔrθ / (AmE), / ɡɪv fɔːθ / (BrE)
to emit or send out (something such as a sound, light, smell, or breath)
emit; send out (sound, smell, etc.)

Examples

The old pine tree gave forth a pleasant resinous scent in the heat.
Her singing gave forth a sweet, clear tone that filled the hall.
As night fell, the lantern gave forth a gentle, flickering light.

Synset Examples

The chimney exhales a thick smoke

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to emit - to send out (something such as a sound, light, or gas)
  • Synonym: to exhale - to breathe out air or vapor
