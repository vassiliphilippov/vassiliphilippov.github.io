to piss around

1. VERB

/ pɪs əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / pɪs əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to waste time or behave in an unproductive or unserious way, especially instead of doing something important
waste time; be unserious
informal slang vulgar british

Examples

Stop pissing around and start working on your assignment.
He spent the afternoon pissing around with his friends instead of studying.
The manager was annoyed that everyone was pissing around instead of focusing on the project.

Synset Examples

Stop pissing around and help me finish this task.
It's time to stop pissing around and get serious.
They were just pissing around instead of making progress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to mess around - to behave in a silly or aimless way instead of working
  • Antonym: get down to business - to begin to do something seriously
