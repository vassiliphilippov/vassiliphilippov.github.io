to prepare to stop

1. VERB

/ prɪˈpɛr tə stɑp / (AmE), / prɪˈpɛə tə stɒp / (BrE)
to get ready to halt or cease movement, especially in response to a warning or signal
get ready to halt movement

Examples

Drivers should prepare to stop when they see the yellow traffic light.
The attendant signaled for the train to prepare to stop as it approached the station.
You must always prepare to stop when approaching a pedestrian crossing.

Synset Examples

The yellow light warned them to prepare to stop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stop - to cease movement; come to a halt
