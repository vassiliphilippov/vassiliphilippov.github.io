to stay together

1. VERB

/ steɪ təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / steɪ təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
(of two or more people) to remain united and loyal to each other, especially in difficult circumstances.
remain united and loyal to each other

Examples

During the crisis, the team decided to stay together rather than split up.
Good friends stay together even when things get tough.
Despite the challenges, they promised to stay together no matter what.

Synset Examples

The two families stuck together throughout the war

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stick together - remain united, especially during difficulties
