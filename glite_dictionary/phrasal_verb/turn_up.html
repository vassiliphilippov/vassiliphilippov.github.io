to turn up

1. VERB

/ tɝn ʌp / (AmE), / tɜːn ʌp / (BrE)
to find or discover something by digging in the ground
find by digging in the ground

Examples

They managed to turn up a rusty key while planting flowers in the yard.
During the excavation, the archaeologists turned up several ancient coins.
Sometimes, you might turn up unexpected items when digging in old gardens.

Synset Examples

I dug up an old box in the garden

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to dig up - to discover something by digging
