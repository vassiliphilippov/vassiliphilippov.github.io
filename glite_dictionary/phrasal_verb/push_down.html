to push down

1. VERB

/ pʊʃ daʊn / (AmE), / pʊʃ daʊn / (BrE)
to cause someone or something to move downward by applying force
force downward movement

Examples

The demonstrators tried to push down the barricade blocking the street.
She gently pushed down the lid on the box to close it.
The wrestler pushed down his opponent to pin him to the mat.

Synset Examples

The policeman downed the heavily armed suspect
The mugger knocked down the old lady after she refused to hand over her wallet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to knock down - to cause someone or something to fall to the ground by hitting or pushing
