to travel along

1. VERB

/ ˈtræv.əl əˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈtræv.əl əˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
to move or go along a particular route, path, or course
move along a route or course

Examples

They decided to travel along the coast to see all the seaside villages.
You can travel along this road for fifty miles without hitting any major towns.
The hikers travelled along the winding forest path until they reached the lake.

Synset Examples

follow the road
follow the trail

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to follow - to go or come after (a route, path, etc.)
  • Antonym: deviate - to depart from a route or course
