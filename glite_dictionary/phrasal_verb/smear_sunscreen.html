to smear sunscreen

1. VERB

/ smɪr ˈsʌnˌskrin / (AmE), / smɪə ˈsʌnskriːn / (BrE)
to spread sunscreen lotion thickly or unevenly over the skin, usually with the hands
spread sunscreen lotion on skin

Examples

Don't forget to smear sunscreen on your nose and shoulders before you go hiking.
She carefully smeared sunscreen over her children's faces before they played outside.
He hurriedly smeared sunscreen on his arms, worried about getting sunburned.

Synset Examples

He decided to smear sunscreen before heading to the beach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apply sunscreen - to put sunscreen on the skin
  • Synonym: rub in sunscreen - to massage sunscreen until it is absorbed
