to eat in

1. VERB

/ it ɪn / (AmE), / iːt ɪn / (BrE)
to have a meal at home rather than at a restaurant or elsewhere
have a meal at home

Examples

We decided to eat in tonight because it was raining heavily outside.
She usually eats in during the week and saves eating out for weekends.
Since they were on a tight budget, they preferred eating in rather than going to restaurants.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to eat out - to have a meal in a restaurant
