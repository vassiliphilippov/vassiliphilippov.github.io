to skeeze one's way into

1. VERB

/ skiz wʌnz weɪ ˈɪntu / (AmE), / skiːz wʌnz weɪ ˈɪntuː / (BrE)
to gain access to a place or situation by using manipulative, insincere, or sleazy charm
enter by manipulative or sleazy charm
informal slang

Examples

He always manages to skeeze his way into exclusive clubs, even without an invitation.
She tried to skeeze her way into the backstage area by flattering the security guard.
They skeeze their way into conversations by pretending to know everyone.

Synset Examples

He always manages to skeeze his way into the best parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worm one's way into - to gain entry or favor by cunning or devious means
