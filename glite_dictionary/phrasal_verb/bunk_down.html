to bunk down

1. VERB

/ bʌŋk daʊn / (AmE), / bʌŋk daʊn / (BrE)
to go to bed, especially in a temporary or makeshift place
go to bed, often temporarily

Examples

After the campfire, everyone decided to bunk down under the stars.
We usually bunk down in the living room during family reunions.
She bunked down on the floor with just a blanket and a pillow.

Synset Examples

We bedded down at midnight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to bed down - to settle down to sleep, often in a temporary place
