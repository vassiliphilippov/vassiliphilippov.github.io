to fall open

1. VERB

/ fɔl ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / fɔːl ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
(of an object or body part) to open suddenly or involuntarily, especially as a reaction to surprise or due to gravity.
to open involuntarily or suddenly

Examples

As the news was announced, his mouth fell open in astonishment.
The heavy book slipped from her hands and fell open to the first page.
When the magician revealed his secret, our jaws fell open in disbelief.

Synset Examples

His mouth dropped open
her jaw dropped open

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to drop open - to open suddenly or involuntarily, especially of the mouth or jaw
