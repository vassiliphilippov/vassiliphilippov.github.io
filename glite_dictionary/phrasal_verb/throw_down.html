to throw down

1. VERB

/ θroʊ ˈdaʊn / (AmE), / θrəʊ ˈdaʊn / (BrE)
to engage in a fight or competition, often in a bold or confrontational way
engage in a fight or competition
informal

Examples

Both teams are ready to throw down in tonight's final match.
When challenged, he never hesitates to throw down.
Rap battles are where artists truly throw down and show their skills.

Synset Examples

She’s not afraid to throw down when it matters.
The athletes are ready to throw down at the championship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fight - to engage in a physical combat or battle
  • Synonym: to compete - to strive against others in a contest
