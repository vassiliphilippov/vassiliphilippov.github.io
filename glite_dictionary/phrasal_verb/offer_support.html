to offer support

1. VERB

/ ˈɔfər səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈɒfə səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
to give help or assistance to someone, especially during a difficult time
provide help or assistance

Examples

The company was quick to offer support to employees affected by the closure.
Friends and neighbors offered support during her recovery after surgery.
She is always willing to offer support when someone is in need.

Synset Examples

The bishop visited the community to offer support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: support - help or assistance provided to someone
