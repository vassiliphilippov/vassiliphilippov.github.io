to save up

1. VERB

/ seɪv ʌp / (AmE), / seɪv ʌp / (BrE)
to gradually collect or put aside money for a specific purpose or future use
set money aside for future use

Examples

She decided to save up for a new computer over several months.
He is saving up to buy a ticket for the concert next summer.
They finally saved up enough money to go on vacation.

Synset Examples

He saves half his salary

Related Terms

  • Synonym: save - to keep money instead of spending it
