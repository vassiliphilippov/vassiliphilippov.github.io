to average up

1. VERB

/ ˈæv.ɚ.ɪdʒ ʌp / (AmE), / ˈæv.ər.ɪdʒ ʌp / (BrE)
to calculate the mean value of a set of amounts or scores
calculate the mean of numbers

Examples

At the end of the semester, I will average up all your quiz scores.
You need to average up last year's monthly sales to get an annual figure.
After collecting the survey responses, the team decided to average up the ratings for each product.

Synset Examples

It's time to average up your expenses for the month.
You should average up your scores to see overall progress.
Let's average up these numbers for a better estimate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: average - to calculate the mean value of numbers
