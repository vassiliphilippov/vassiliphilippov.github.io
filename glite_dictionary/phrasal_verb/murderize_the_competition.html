to murderize the competition

1. VERB

/ ˈmɝːdɚˌaɪz ðə kəmˈpɛt̬ɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdəˌaɪz ðə kəmˈpɛtɪʃn̩ / (BrE)
to defeat one's rivals decisively; to win overwhelmingly against competitors
defeat rivals decisively
informal slang

Examples

If their team plays like this again, they'll murderize the competition in the finals.
She training hard to murderize the competition at the national championship next month.
Last year, their company murderized the competition with an innovative new product.

Synset Examples

He promised to murderize the competition in the tournament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crush - to defeat someone completely
  • Synonym: thrash - to beat someone decisively

Etymology

Slang formation from 'murder' + '-ize', meaning to 'kill off' rivals metaphorically.
