to give it up

1. VERB

/ ˌɡɪv ɪt ˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌɡɪv ɪt ˈʌp / (BrE)
to stop doing something or to quit an activity, habit, or pursuit
stop doing something; quit

Examples

After years of smoking, he finally decided to give it up for good.
If you can't win the game, sometimes it's best to give it up and move on.
She found it hard to give it up even though everyone advised her to quit.

Synset Examples

You should give it up if it makes you unhappy.
She was told to give it up for her health.
He finally decided to give it up after years of trying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to quit - to stop doing something, especially permanently
  • Antonym: to continue - to keep doing something; to persist
