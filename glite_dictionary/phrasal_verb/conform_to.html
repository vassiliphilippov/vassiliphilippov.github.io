to conform to

1. VERB

/ kənˈfɔːrm tu / (AmE), / kənˈfɔːm tuː / (BrE)
to act in accordance with a rule, law, or standard; to comply with or follow something
act according to rules or standards

Examples

All employees must conform to the company's code of conduct.
New buildings have to conform to strict safety regulations.
He always tries to conform to his parents' expectations.

Synset Examples

conform to the rules

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comply with - to act in accordance with a wish or command
  • Antonym: to violate - to break or fail to comply with (a rule or law)

Etymology

From Latin 'conformare' (to shape, form), with 'to' marking the object of conformity.
