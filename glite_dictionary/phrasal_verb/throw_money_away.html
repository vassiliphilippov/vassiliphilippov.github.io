to throw money away

1. VERB

/ θroʊ ˈmʌni əˈweɪ / (AmE), / θrəʊ ˈmʌni əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to spend money wastefully or on something unnecessary or worthless
spend money wastefully

Examples

Many people throw money away on designer labels they don't really need.
If you subscribe to channels you never watch, you're just throwing money away.
She regrets throwing money away on expensive fads that didn't last.

Synset Examples

Don't throw money away on cheap knockoffs.
He realized he was just throwing money away on unnecessary subscriptions.
She felt guilty for throwing money away on impulse buys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waste - to use something carelessly or inefficiently
  • Antonym: save - to avoid spending money unnecessarily or to keep money safe
