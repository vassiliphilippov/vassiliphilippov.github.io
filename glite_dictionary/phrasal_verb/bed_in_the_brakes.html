to bed in the brakes

1. VERB

/ bɛd ɪn ðə breɪks / (AmE), / bɛd ɪn ðə breɪks / (BrE)
to condition or run-in new brake components so they function optimally and safely
condition new brakes for optimal use

Examples

It's important to bed in the brakes after installation to ensure maximum stopping power.
The mechanic advised me to bed in the brakes gradually over the first few days.
If you don't bed in the brakes correctly, you may experience reduced braking performance.

Synset Examples

Make sure to bed in the brakes properly for safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: run in - to operate a new machine carefully until it works smoothly
