to crop out

1. VERB

/ krɑp aʊt / (AmE), / krɒp aʊt / (BrE)
(of rock, mineral, etc.) to appear or become exposed at the surface of the ground.
become exposed at the surface

Examples

The underlying limestone crops out along the valley walls.
Several gold veins cropped out after the hillside was eroded.
You can see where the coal seam crops out near the river.

Synset Examples

A seam of coal bassets

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outcrop - a visible exposure of bedrock or ancient superficial deposits on the surface of the Earth
