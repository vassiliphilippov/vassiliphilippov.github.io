to ask out

1. VERB

/ æsk aʊt / (AmE), / ɑːsk aʊt / (BrE)
to invite someone to go on a date, especially in a romantic context
invite someone on a date

Examples

Tom finally got the courage to ask out his coworker for dinner.
You should ask out Julia if you like her that much.
She was surprised when Mike asked out her best friend instead.

Synset Examples

has he asked you out yet?
he asked me to a dance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invite - to ask someone to join you for an event or activity
