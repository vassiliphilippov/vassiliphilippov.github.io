to cap it all off

1. VERB

/ kæp ɪt ɔl ɔf / (AmE), / kap ɪt ɔːl ɒf / (BrE)
to finish or bring something to an impressive or notable conclusion
end impressively; finish notably

Examples

The fireworks display capped it all off at the end of the festival.
To cap it all off, she received a surprise promotion at work.
Our trip to Paris was amazing, and a boat ride along the Seine capped it all off.

Synset Examples

A stunning sunset capped it all off perfectly.
They had a lovely dinner to cap it all off.
To cap it all off, they won the championship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finish - to bring to an end; to complete something
  • Synonym: to culminate - to reach a climax or point of highest development
