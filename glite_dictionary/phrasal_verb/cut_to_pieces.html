to cut to pieces

1. VERB

/ kʌt tə ˈpisɪz / (AmE), / kʌt tə ˈpiːsɪz / (BrE)
to shred, tear, or break something into small parts; also, to cause something or someone to experience great emotional or physical damage.
to destroy or tear into small parts

Examples

After the hurricane, the old barn was cut to pieces by the wind and flying debris.
Her harsh criticism cut him to pieces, leaving him feeling completely defeated.
For the salad, the chef quickly cut the vegetables to pieces with expert precision.

Synset Examples

She cut the paper to pieces for the art project.
The storm cut the old tree to pieces.
She was so upset, she felt cut to pieces.
The chef cut the vegetables to pieces for the stir-fry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shred - to cut or tear into small strips; destroy
  • Synonym: to tear apart - to rip into pieces; to destroy emotionally or physically
