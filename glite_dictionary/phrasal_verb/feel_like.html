to feel like

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk / (BrE)
to want or have an inclination to do or have something
want or have an inclination to do something

Examples

I feel like going for a walk after dinner.
Do you feel like watching a movie tonight?
She said she didn't feel like eating anything this morning.

Synset Examples

I feel like staying in bed all day
I feel like a cold beer now

Related Terms

  • Synonym: want - to have a desire for something
