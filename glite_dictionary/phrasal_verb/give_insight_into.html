to give insight into

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ˈɪnˌsaɪt ˈɪntu / (AmE), / ɡɪv ˈɪnsaɪt ˈɪntuː / (BrE)
to provide a clear understanding or revealing information about someone or something
provide understanding or revealing information

Examples

The new report gives insight into the challenges facing the company.
Her diary gives insight into her daily struggles and triumphs.
This documentary gives insight into the lives of people living in remote areas.

Synset Examples

Studying NatSoc gives insight into that era's politics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to shed light on - to clarify or provide new information about something
  • Antonym: obscure - to make something less clear or more difficult to understand
