to tidy up

1. VERB

/ ˈtaɪdi ʌp / (AmE), / ˈtaɪdi ʌp / (BrE)
to make a place or a collection of things neat and orderly by putting things in their proper place
put things or places in order

Examples

I always tidy up my desk before guests arrive.
Could you tidy up the living room before dinner?
After the party, we all tidied up the garden together.

Synset Examples

Tidy up your room!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to clean up - to make something clean and tidy
