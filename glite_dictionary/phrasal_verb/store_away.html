to store away

1. VERB

/ stɔr əˈweɪ / (AmE), / stɔːr əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to put something in a place where it can be kept safely and used in the future
put aside for future use

Examples

After we finish using the camping equipment, let's store away everything neatly in the garage.
Grandma likes to store away homemade jams for the winter months.
Once you've read those documents, please store away the files in the cabinet.

Synset Examples

Make sure to store away the winter clothes soon.
It's time to store away the summer gear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to put away - to place something out of sight or in its usual place
