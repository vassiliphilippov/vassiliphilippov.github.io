to word it

1. VERB

/ wɝd ɪt / (AmE), / wɜːd ɪt / (BrE)
to express or phrase something in a particular way using words
phrase or express in words

Examples

She took a moment to consider how best to word it in her resignation letter.
If you word it carefully, your message will sound diplomatic rather than harsh.
I'm not sure how to word it without offending anyone.
