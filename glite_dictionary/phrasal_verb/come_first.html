to come first

1. VERB

/ kʌm ˈfɜrst / (AmE), / kʌm ˈfɜːst / (BrE)
to be considered the most important or to have the highest priority
to be the most important

Examples

In our company, customer satisfaction will always come first.
For many parents, their childrens needs come first before anything else.
When making decisions, safety should come first every time.

Synset Examples

In this competition, safety must come first.
For her, happiness will always come first.
Family should always come first in your priorities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: take precedence - to have greater importance or priority
