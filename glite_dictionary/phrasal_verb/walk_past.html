to walk past

1. VERB

/ wɔk pæst / (AmE), / wɔːk pɑːst / (BrE)
to move by something or someone on foot without stopping
move by on foot without stopping

Examples

She didn't even look at me as she walked past the door.
Every morning, I walk past the bakery on my way to work.
They were chatting as they walked past the old museum.

Synset Examples

The lamppost flickered as we walked past it at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pass by - to go past without stopping or noticing
