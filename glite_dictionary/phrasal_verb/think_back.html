to think back

1. VERB

/ θɪŋk bæk / (AmE), / θɪŋk bæk / (BrE)
to remember and consider something that happened in the past
remember past events

Examples

When I think back to my childhood, I remember playing in the garden with my cousins.
She often thought back to her college days during stressful times at work.
As we think back over the past year, we realize how much we have achieved.

Synset Examples

he remembered how he used to pick flowers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recall - to remember something from the past
  • Antonym: to forget - to fail to remember something
