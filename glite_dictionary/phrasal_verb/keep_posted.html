to keep posted

1. VERB

/ kip ˈpoʊstɪd / (AmE), / kiːp ˈpəʊstɪd / (BrE)
to inform someone regularly about new developments or updates in a situation
regularly inform about updates

Examples

I'll keep posted if there are any changes during your absence.
Please keep me posted about your arrival time so I can prepare.
The manager promised to keep us posted as soon as she heard back from the client.

Synset Examples

Make sure to keep her posted about the party details.
I will keep you posted on any updates regarding the project.
Please keep me posted about the meeting changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: update - to bring someone or something up to date with new information
