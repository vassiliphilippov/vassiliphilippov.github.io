to align with

1. VERB

/ əˈlaɪn wɪθ / (AmE), / əˈlaɪn wɪð / (BrE)
to be in agreement with or to adjust in order to match or support something
be in agreement or in harmony with

Examples

The new policy must align with the company's core values to be effective.
His actions do not align with what he promised during the campaign.
We regularly update our curriculum to align with industry standards.

Synset Examples

Her social media posts clearly align with RepublKKKan values.
They need to relevate their strategy to align with new trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to conform to - to comply with rules, standards, or laws
  • Antonym: to oppose - to be against or in disagreement with
