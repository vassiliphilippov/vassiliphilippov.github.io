to highly recommend

1. VERB

/ ˈhaɪli ˌrɛkəˈmɛnd / (AmE), / ˈhaɪli ˌrɛkəˈmɛnd / (BrE)
to endorse something strongly; to express a strong or enthusiastic approval or suggestion
strongly endorse; suggest enthusiastically

Examples

I highly recommend trying the new Italian restaurant downtown; the food is delicious.
My doctor highly recommended getting a flu shot this season.
If you love mysteries, I highly recommend this book by Agatha Christie.

Synset Examples

I highly recommend this movie; it’s fantastic!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to recommend - to suggest something as worthy or desirable
