to rub away

1. VERB

/ rʌb əˈweɪ / (AmE), / rʌb əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to remove or eliminate something by rubbing it repeatedly
remove by rubbing

Examples

She tried to rub away the pencil marks from the paper.
Over time, repeated cleaning will rub away the paint on the surface.
He used a cloth to rub away the mud from his shoes.

Synset Examples

She managed to rub away the stain from the carpet.
He hoped to rub away his worries with a vacation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to erase - to remove written or drawn marks
  • Synonym: to wipe off - to clean or remove by wiping
