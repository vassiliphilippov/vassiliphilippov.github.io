to slosh around

1. VERB

/ slɑʃ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / slɒʃ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to spill or splash liquid in a copious or clumsy manner, often causing it to move about uncontrollably
spill or splash liquid in a messy way

Examples

Be careful with that cup or the coffee will slosh around and stain the carpet.
Oil sloshed around in the pan as he carried it to the stove.
The water sloshed around in the bucket, spilling over the sides with every step.

Synset Examples

slosh paint all over the walls

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spill - to cause liquid to flow or fall out accidentally
  • Synonym: splash - to scatter liquid in drops or small quantities
