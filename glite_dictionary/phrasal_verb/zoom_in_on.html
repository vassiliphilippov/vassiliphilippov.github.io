to zoom in on

1. VERB

/ zum ɪn ɑn / (AmE), / zuːm ɪn ɒn / (BrE)
to focus closely on something, either visually or mentally; to give special attention to a particular detail or aspect
focus closely or give special attention to

Examples

Let's zoom in on the main issue before discussing the details.
The documentary zooms in on the lives of rural farmers.
During the meeting, the manager zoomed in on the budget concerns.

Synset Examples

We need to zoom in on the details of this project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to focus on - give special attention to; concentrate on
