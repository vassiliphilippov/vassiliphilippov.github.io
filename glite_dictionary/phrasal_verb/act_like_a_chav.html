to act like a chav

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə tʃæv / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə tʃæv / (BrE)
to behave in a way that is stereotypically associated with working-class youth, often characterized by flashy or brash behavior and speech, especially in British society
behave in a stereotypically chav manner
informal offensive british

Examples

He started to act like a chav after winning the lottery, flashing his money everywhere.
Whenever they go clubbing, some of the group act like a chav just for laughs.
She doesn't usually act like a chav, but tonight was an exception.

Synset Examples

Her friends joked that she was acting like a chav tonight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chav - a young person of a type characterized by aggressive behavior and flashy clothing (British slang)

Etymology

From 'act' + 'chav', with 'chav' originating in British slang for a young person from a low-income background, associated with certain fashion and behavior.
