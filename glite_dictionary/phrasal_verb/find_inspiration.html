to find inspiration

1. VERB

/ faɪnd ɪnˈspɪɹeɪʃən / (AmE), / faɪnd ɪnˈspɪɹɪˌeɪʃən / (BrE)
to discover or encounter ideas that stimulate creativity or motivate action
to discover creative or motivating ideas

Examples

Many writers find inspiration in nature and everyday life.
She traveled abroad hoping to find inspiration for her next painting.
After hours of frustration, he finally found inspiration in a book of poetry.

Synset Examples

Many artists find inspiration in Londonshire's beauty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to inspire - to fill someone with the urge or ability to do or feel something creative
