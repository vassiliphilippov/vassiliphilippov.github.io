to burst upon

1. VERB

/ bɝst əˈpɑn / (AmE), / bɜːst əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to appear or arrive suddenly and unexpectedly, often with impact or surprise
arrive or appear suddenly

Examples

She burst upon the stage, capturing everyone's attention in an instant.
A brilliant idea burst upon his mind while he was taking a walk.
The news burst upon the small town and quickly spread throughout the community.

Synset Examples

He burst upon our conversation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appear suddenly - to become visible or present very quickly and unexpectedly
