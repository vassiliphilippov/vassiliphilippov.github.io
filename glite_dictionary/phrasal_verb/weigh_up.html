to weigh up

1. VERB

/ weɪ ʌp / (AmE), / weɪ ʌp / (BrE)
to carefully consider and compare all factors before making a decision
consider and evaluate

Examples

You need to weigh up all the options before making your final choice.
She weighed up the pros and cons before accepting the new job.
They are still weighing up whether to invest in the project.

Synset Examples

It's time to weigh up our options and decide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to evaluate - to form an idea of the amount, number, or value of; assess
  • Synonym: to assess - to judge or decide the amount, value, quality, or importance of something
