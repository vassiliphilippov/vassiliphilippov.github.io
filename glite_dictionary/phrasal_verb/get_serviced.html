to get serviced

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈsɜrvɪst / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈsɜːvɪst / (BrE)
(of a vehicle or machine) to have maintenance or essential repairs carried out by a professional.
have a vehicle or machine maintained

Examples

I always get serviced at the same garage because they do a thorough job.
You should get serviced every 10,000 miles to keep your car running smoothly.
After hearing some strange noises, we decided to get serviced as soon as possible.

Synset Examples

I need to get my car serviced next week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: service - to perform routine maintenance on a vehicle or machine
