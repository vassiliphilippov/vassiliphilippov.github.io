to mention it

1. VERB

/ ˈmɛn.tʃən ɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.tʃən ɪt / (BrE)
to bring up or refer to something in conversation or writing
bring up in conversation

Examples

Did you mention it to your manager during the meeting?
Now that you mention it, I do remember seeing that movie.
She didn't mention it when we spoke yesterday.

Synset Examples

Now you mention it, I do remember that event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to bring up - to introduce a topic in conversation
  • Synonym: raise - to bring forward for discussion
