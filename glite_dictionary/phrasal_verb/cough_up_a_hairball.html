to cough up a hairball

1. VERB

/ kɑf ʌp ə ˈhɛrˌbɔl / (AmE), / kɒf ʌp ə ˈheəˌbɔːl / (BrE)
(of a cat or other animal) to expel a hairball from the mouth after coughing or retching.
expel a hairball (animal)

Examples

Every few weeks, my cat will cough up a hairball on the living room rug.
I knew something was wrong when the kitten tried to cough up a hairball but couldn't.
Some cats cough up a hairball more frequently during shedding season.

Synset Examples

The cat coughed up a hairball on the carpet.
The cat just coughed up a furball on the carpet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vomit - to eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth
