to pair off

1. VERB

/ pɛr ɔf / (AmE), / peə ɒf / (BrE)
(of people or things) to form pairs or to join together in pairs, especially for a particular purpose.
to form or join into pairs

Examples

At the end of the dance, everyone had to pair off with a partner.
The teacher asked the students to pair off for the science experiment.
Soon after the meeting started, members began to pair off for group discussions.

Synset Examples

The two old friends paired off

Related Terms

  • Synonym: match up - to pair or connect things or people appropriately
