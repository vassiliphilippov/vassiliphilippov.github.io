to make noise

1. VERB

/ meɪk nɔɪz / (AmE), / meɪk nɔɪz / (BrE)
to produce or emit a sound, especially a loud or noticeable one
to emit a sound

Examples

The children continued to make noise long after bedtime.
Please try not to make noise while others are working.
If you make noise in the library, you may be asked to leave.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make a racket - to make a lot of loud, unpleasant noise
