to frig it

1. VERB

/ frɪg ɪt / (AmE), / frɪg ɪt / (BrE)
(informal) To disregard something or decide not to care about it; to let it go or not worry about it.
disregard or not care about something (informal)
informal

Examples

When nothing was working, I finally said, 'frig it' and took a break.
You can't please everyone, so sometimes you just have to frig it and do what you want.
She was stressing over the details but decided to frig it and leave early.

Synset Examples

He told me to frig it and go have fun.
I thought about fixing the fence, but I decided to frig it.
When things get tough, I sometimes just frig it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forget it - used to indicate one should not worry about something
  • Synonym: to let it go - to stop caring or being concerned about something

Etymology

Probably a euphemistic alteration of 'fuck it', with 'frig' used as a milder substitute.
