to mercy kill

1. VERB

/ ˈmɝsi kɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɜːsi kɪl / (BrE)
to intentionally cause the painless death of a person or animal, especially to relieve suffering
cause painless death to relieve suffering

Examples

The veterinarian decided to mercy kill the horse to end its suffering.
Some animal shelters are forced to mercy kill severely ill pets.
He made the difficult decision to mercy kill his dog after it was diagnosed with a terminal illness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to euthanize - to put (an animal) to death humanely
  • Degree Lower: kill - to cause the death of a living thing
