to land up

1. VERB

/ lænd ʌp / (AmE), / lænd ʌp / (BrE)
to finally find oneself in a particular situation or doing something, especially unexpectedly or by chance
end up in a situation (often unexpectedly)

Examples

After missing the last train, we landed up spending the night at the station.
If you don’t study hard, you might land up with a low grade.
He took a wrong turn and landed up in a completely different town.

Synset Examples

He ended up marrying his high school sweetheart
he wound up being unemployed and living at home again

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to end up - to finally be in a particular place or situation
  • Synonym: to wind up - to finish up or come to be in a certain place or situation
