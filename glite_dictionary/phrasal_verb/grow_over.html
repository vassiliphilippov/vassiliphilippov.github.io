to grow over

1. VERB

/ ɡroʊ ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ɡrəʊ ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
(of a plant) to cover or extend across something by growing beyond its original area.
(of plants) to cover or spread across

Examples

Vines began to grow over the old wooden shed by midsummer.
After years of neglect, moss started to grow over the stone path.
If you leave the garden unattended, weeds will quickly grow over the flowerbeds.

Synset Examples

The ivy overgrew the patio

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to overgrow - to grow over and cover
