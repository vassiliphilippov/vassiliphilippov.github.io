to take a picture

1. VERB

/ teɪk ə ˈpɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / teɪk ə ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
to capture an image using a camera.
capture an image with a camera

Examples

Could you take a picture of us in front of the monument?
I forgot to take a picture at the party last night.
She asked her friend to take a picture with her new camera.

Synset Examples

Can you take a picture of us?
He loves to take a picture whenever he travels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photograph - record an image with a camera
