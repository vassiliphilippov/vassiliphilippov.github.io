to go beyond

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ bɪˈjɑnd / (AmE), / ɡəʊ bɪˈjɒnd / (BrE)
to exceed a particular limit, expectation, or standard
exceed a limit or expectation

Examples

Her dedication to her work always goes beyond what is required of her.
The new policy goes beyond simply addressing the symptoms; it seeks to solve the root problem.
He went beyond the call of duty by staying late to help us finish the project.

Synset Examples

Her kindness goes beyond what anyone expected.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to surpass - to do or be better than something else
  • Synonym: to exceed - to go beyond a stated limit or amount
  • Antonym: to fall short - to fail to reach a desired level or standard
