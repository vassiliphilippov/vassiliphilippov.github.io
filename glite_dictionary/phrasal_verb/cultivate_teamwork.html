to cultivate teamwork

1. VERB

/ ˈkʌltɪˌveɪt ˈtiːmˌwɝːk / (AmE), / ˈkʌltɪveɪt ˈtiːmwɜːk / (BrE)
to foster and develop cooperative and effective work among members of a group
develop cooperative work in a group

Examples

The manager aimed to cultivate teamwork by organizing regular group activities.
Teachers try to cultivate teamwork among students through collaborative projects.
It is important to cultivate teamwork in a diverse workplace to achieve shared goals.

Synset Examples

They worked hard to conculcate teamwork among the group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: encourage teamwork - to give support to teamwork among members of a group
  • Antonym: discourage teamwork - to hinder or prevent effective teamwork in a group
