to swing around

1. VERB

/ swɪŋ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / swɪŋ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to turn abruptly and face the opposite direction, either physically or in a figurative sense
turn abruptly to face the opposite way

Examples

She heard a noise behind her and swung around to see what it was.
The car swung around at the intersection and headed back.
He swung around when his name was called from across the room.

Synset Examples

He turned around to face his opponent
My conscience told me to turn around before I made a mistake

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to turn around - to move so as to face in the opposite direction
  • Synonym: face - to turn one's face or front in a particular direction
