to bob around

1. VERB

/ bɑb əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / bɒb əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to move up and down repeatedly in a random or gentle fashion, often while floating on water or in the air
to move up and down without direction

Examples

Children watched the small boats bob around on the gentle waves.
Leaves bobbing around in the pond caught the sunlight.
A colorful balloon bobbed around in the clear sky during the festival.

Synset Examples

the cork bobbed around in the pool

Related Terms

  • Synonym: float - to remain on the surface of a liquid without sinking
  • Antonym: sink - to go down below the surface of a liquid
