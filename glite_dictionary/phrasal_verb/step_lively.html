to step lively

1. VERB

/ stɛp ˈlaɪvli / (AmE), / stɛp ˈlaɪvli / (BrE)
to move quickly or with energy, especially in response to an instruction or when time is short
move quickly or energetically

Examples

The teacher told everyone to step lively so they wouldn't miss the bus.
We must step lively if we're going to finish before the deadline.
Security guards instructed the crowd to step lively and clear the area quickly.

Synset Examples
