to get it on

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ɪt ɑn / (AmE), / ɡɛt ɪt ɒn / (BrE)
to have sexual intercourse with someone; to engage in sexual activity
to have sexual intercourse
informal

Examples

They decided to get it on after the party ended.
Rumors spread that the two celebrities were getting it on backstage.
He bragged to his friends about how he finally got it on with his longtime crush.

/ ɡɛt ɪt ɑn / (AmE), / ɡɛt ɪt ɒn / (BrE)
to start or become actively involved in an activity, especially with energy or enthusiasm
start or engage in an activity
informal

Examples

Everyone was waiting for the band to get it on and play the first song.
It's time to get it on with our group discussion.
She encouraged us to get it on and make the most of the afternoon.
