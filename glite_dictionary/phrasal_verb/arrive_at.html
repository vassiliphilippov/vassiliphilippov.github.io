to arrive at

1. VERB

/ əˈraɪv æt / (AmE), / əˈraɪv æt / (BrE)
to reach a particular place, destination, or conclusion, especially after a journey or deliberation
reach a place or a conclusion

Examples

The train will arrive at the station in ten minutes.
After much discussion, they finally arrived at an agreement.
We arrived at our destination just as the sun was setting.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
