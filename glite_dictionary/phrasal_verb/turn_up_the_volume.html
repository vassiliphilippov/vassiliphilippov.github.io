to turn up the volume

1. VERB

/ tɝn ʌp ðə ˈvɑljum / (AmE), / tɜːn ʌp ðə ˈvɒljuːm / (BrE)
to increase the loudness of sound from a device such as a radio, television, or speaker
increase loudness of a device

Examples

Could you please turn up the volume? I can't hear what they're saying.
He reached for the remote and turned up the volume during his favorite song.
Whenever there is a party, someone always wants to turn up the volume on the music.

Synset Examples

She turned up the volume on her favorite podcast.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: turn down the volume - to decrease the loudness of a device
  • Synonym: increase the volume - to make the sound louder
