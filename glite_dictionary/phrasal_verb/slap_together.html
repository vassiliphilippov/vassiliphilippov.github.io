to slap together

1. VERB

/ slæp təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / slæp təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to make or assemble something quickly and carelessly, without attention to detail
assemble hastily and carelessly

Examples

They managed to slap together a basic website the night before the launch.
She slapped together a sandwich from whatever was left in the fridge.
We can slap together a quick report if you need it by this afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cobble together - make or assemble hastily and roughly
  • Synonym: to throw together - put things together quickly with little preparation
