to catch oneself

1. VERB

/ kætʃ wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / katʃ wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to stop oneself quickly, especially when about to say or do something one might regret
stop oneself abruptly

Examples

I had to catch myself before commenting on his mistake.
She caught herself just in time, realizing her words were too harsh.
He managed to catch himself before making an inappropriate joke.

Synset Examples

She managed to catch herself before telling her boss what was on her mind

Related Terms

  • Synonym: check oneself - to stop or restrain one's own actions or words
