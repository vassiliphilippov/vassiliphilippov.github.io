to write down

1. VERB

/ raɪt daʊn / (AmE), / raɪt daʊn / (BrE)
To record something by putting it in written form, especially so it will not be forgotten.
record in writing

Examples

Please write down your email address on this form.
I wrote down everything the teacher said during the lecture.
She always writes down her ideas in a notebook so she won't forget them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: record - to set down in writing or some other permanent form
  • Synonym: note - to write something briefly to remember it later

2. VERB

/ raɪt daʊn / (AmE), / raɪt daʊn / (BrE)
to record something by writing it on paper or another surface, especially to remember it later
record in writing

Examples

Please write down your name and contact details on the form.
She quickly wrote down the address before she forgot it.
While listening to the lecture, he began writing down important points.

Synset Examples
