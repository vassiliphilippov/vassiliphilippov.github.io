to release the handbrake

1. VERB

/ rɪˈlis ðə ˈhændˌbreɪk / (AmE), / rɪˈliːs ðə ˈhændbreɪk / (BrE)
to disengage the handbrake of a vehicle, allowing it to move
disengage a vehicle's handbrake

Examples

Before starting the car, remember to release the handbrake to avoid damaging the brakes.
She forgot to release the handbrake and wondered why the car would not move.
After parking on a slope, make sure to release the handbrake gently to prevent rolling.

Synset Examples

He forgot to release the handbrake, causing the car to stall.
Always check to release the handbrake in cold weather.
Make sure to release the handbrake before driving off.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: engage the handbrake - to apply the handbrake of a vehicle
