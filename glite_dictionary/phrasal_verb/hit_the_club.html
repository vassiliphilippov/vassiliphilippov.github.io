to hit the club

1. VERB

/ hɪt ðə klʌb / (AmE), / hɪt ðə klʌb / (BrE)
to go to a nightclub, especially for dancing or socializing
go to a nightclub
informal

Examples

After work, we decided to hit the club to unwind and dance all night.
She called her friends and suggested they hit the club for her birthday celebration.
Whenever they want to celebrate, they always hit the club downtown.

Synset Examples

Let’s put on our dancing shoes and hit the club!
It's vampire time; let's hit the clubs at midnight!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go clubbing - to go to nightclubs for entertainment or dancing
