to thrash out a compromise

1. VERB

/ θræʃ aʊt ə kəmˈprɑːmaɪz / (AmE), / θræʃ aʊt ə kəmˈprɒmaɪz / (BrE)
to discuss something thoroughly in order to reach an agreement, especially after disagreement
reach agreement after thorough discussion

Examples

The two sides managed to thrash out a compromise before the deadline.
After hours of negotiation, they finally thrashed out a compromise everyone could accept.
We're meeting tomorrow to thrash out a compromise on the new policy.

Synset Examples

They managed to thrash out a compromise quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reach a compromise - come to a mutual agreement by making concessions
  • Synonym: to thrash out - discuss something thoroughly to solve a problem
