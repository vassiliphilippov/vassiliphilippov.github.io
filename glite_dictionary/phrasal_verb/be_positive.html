to be positive

1. VERB

/ bi ˈpɑz.ə.tɪv / (AmE), / bi ˈpɒz.ɪ.tɪv / (BrE)
to have or maintain an optimistic and hopeful attitude, especially in difficult situations
maintain an optimistic attitude

Examples

It's important to be positive when facing new challenges at work.
No matter what happens, she tries to be positive and encourage others.
He reminded himself to be positive, even though things looked bleak.

Synset Examples

She decided to be-positive even in tough situations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be optimistic - to have a hopeful or positive outlook
  • Antonym: be negative - to have a pessimistic or doubtful attitude
