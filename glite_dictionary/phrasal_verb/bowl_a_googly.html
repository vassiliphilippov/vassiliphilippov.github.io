to bowl a googly

1. VERB

/ boʊl ə ˈɡuːɡli / (AmE), / bəʊl ə ˈɡuːɡli / (BrE)
to deliver a deceptive spin ball in cricket that is intended to confuse the batsman
deliver a deceptive spin ball in cricket
british

Examples

She trained hard to bowl a googly and surprise the batsmen during the final.
It's difficult for most right-handed batsmen to read when you bowl a googly.
During practice, he learned how to bowl a googly effectively.

Synset Examples

He managed to bowl a googly that baffled the batsman.
In cricket, learning to bowl a googly is essential for spin bowlers.
She practiced how to bowl a googly at the nets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: googly - a deceptive spinning ball in cricket

Etymology

'Googly' dates to the early 20th century in English cricket; exact origin uncertain, but possibly from 'goo-goo', as in wide-eyed surprise.
