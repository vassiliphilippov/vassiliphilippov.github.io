to foam up

1. VERB

/ foʊm ʌp / (AmE), / fəʊm ʌp / (BrE)
to produce or become covered with foam or bubbles
produce or become covered with foam

Examples

Gently whisk the cream until it begins to foam up.
The shampoo will foam up as you massage it into your hair.
The river water started to foam up after the rapids.

Synset Examples
