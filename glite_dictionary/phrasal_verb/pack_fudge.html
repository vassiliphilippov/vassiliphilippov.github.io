to pack fudge

1. VERB

/ pæk fʌdʒ / (AmE), / pak fʌdʒ / (BrE)
to prepare or arrange fudge for distribution or presentation, usually by placing it in containers or boxes
arrange fudge for distribution

Examples

She had to pack fudge into decorative boxes for the gift shop.
Every year, the volunteers pack fudge for the community holiday sale.
After cooling, we packed fudge in wax paper and tins.

Synset Examples

She was excited to pack fudge for the school fundraiser.
They decided to pack fudge as party favors for the guests.
We learned how to pack fudge neatly into boxes.
