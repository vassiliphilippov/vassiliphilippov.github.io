to give grief

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ɡriːf / (AmE), / ɡɪv ɡriːf / (BrE)
to cause someone distress, trouble, or annoyance
cause distress or annoyance
informal

Examples

His constant lateness is really giving me grief at work.
The faulty printer has been giving us grief all week.
She was upset because her friends kept giving her grief about her decision.

Synset Examples

His constant complaints are really giving me grief.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cause trouble - to create difficulties or problems for someone
  • Synonym: to annoy - to disturb or irritate someone
  • Antonym: to relieve - to ease someone's pain or distress
