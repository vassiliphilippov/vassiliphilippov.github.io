to request a refund

1. VERB

/ rɪˈkwɛst ə rɪˈfʌnd / (AmE), / rɪˈkwɛst ə rɪˈfʌnd / (BrE)
to formally ask for the return of money paid for a product or service, usually because it was unsatisfactory or faulty
ask for money back after a purchase

Examples

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can request a refund within 30 days.
After the show was cancelled, everyone rushed to request a refund from the ticket office.
She requested a refund because the shoes arrived damaged.

Synset Examples

I requested a refund for my faulty headphones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refund - an amount of money returned, usually after a complaint or return
