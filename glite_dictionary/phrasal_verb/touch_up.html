to touch up

1. VERB

/ tʌtʃ ʌp / (AmE), / tʌtʃ ʌp / (BrE)
to make small changes or improvements, especially to improve appearance or correct flaws
make small improvements to appearance

Examples

She needs to touch up her makeup before the event starts.
The artist will touch up the mural to restore its original colors.
Before the photoshoot, the stylist quickly touched up his hair.

Synset Examples

This photograph has been retouched!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to retouch - to improve by making slight changes, especially to a photo or painting
