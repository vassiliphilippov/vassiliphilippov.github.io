to disagree with

1. VERB

/ ˌdɪsəˈɡri wɪð / (AmE), / ˌdɪsəˈɡriː wɪð / (BrE)
(of food) to cause discomfort or digestive problems for someone.
(of food) cause digestive discomfort

Examples

Spicy food often disagrees with me, so I try to avoid it.
Milk sometimes disagrees with people who are lactose intolerant.
That rich dessert really disagreed with him and upset his stomach.

Synset Examples

Spicy food disagrees with some people

Related Terms

  • Antonym: agree with - (of food) to suit or not cause discomfort
