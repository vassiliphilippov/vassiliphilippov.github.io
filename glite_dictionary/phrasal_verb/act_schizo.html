to act schizo

1. VERB

/ ækt ˈskɪtsoʊ / (AmE), / ækt ˈskɪtsəʊ / (BrE)
to behave in an unpredictable or irrational way, often in a manner that seems erratic or unstable (offensive and informal)
behave unpredictably or irrationally (offensive, slang)
offensive slang informal

Examples

He tends to act schizo when things don’t go as planned.
She started to act schizo after hearing the shocking news.
Sometimes kids act schizo when they're under a lot of pressure.

Synset Examples

He acted schizo when faced with tough decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freak out - to react or behave in a wild or irrational way, usually from shock or fear
  • Antonym: act normal - to behave in a usual, expected, or sane way

Etymology

From 'schizo', a slang shortening of 'schizophrenic'.
