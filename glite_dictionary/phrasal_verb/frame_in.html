to frame in

1. VERB

/ freɪm ɪn / (AmE), / freɪm ɪn / (BrE)
to enclose something in or as if in a frame
enclose in a frame

Examples

The artist decided to frame in her painting with a beautiful wooden border.
Tall hedges were used to frame in the garden, creating a sense of privacy.
They wanted to frame in the porch to protect it from the wind.

Synset Examples

frame a picture
