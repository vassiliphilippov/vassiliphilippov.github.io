to get well

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt wɛl / (AmE), / ɡɛt wɛl / (BrE)
to recover good health after being ill or injured
to recover one’s health

Examples

She was very sick last week but has finally got well enough to return to work.
Doctors were surprised by how quickly he managed to get well after surgery.
Eat plenty of fruits and vegetables to help you get well faster.

Synset Examples

He got well fast

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to recover - to return to a normal state of health, mind, or strength

2. VERB

/ ɡɛt wɛl / (AmE), / ɡɛt wɛl / (BrE)
to recover from an illness or injury; to return to a healthy state
recover from illness

Examples

After a week in bed, he began to get well and feel stronger.
Doctors expect her to get well after completing the treatment.
It took him several months to fully get well after the accident.
