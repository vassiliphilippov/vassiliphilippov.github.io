to bomb up

1. VERB

/ bɑm ʌp / (AmE), / bɒm ʌp / (BrE)
to load an aircraft with bombs, especially in preparation for a combat mission
load aircraft with bombs

Examples

The crew prepared to bomb up the aircraft before the mission.
All the planes had been bombed up and were ready for takeoff at dawn.
Ground staff bombed up the bomber quickly to meet the urgent deadline.
