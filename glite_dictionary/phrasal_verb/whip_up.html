to whip up

1. VERB

/ wɪp ʌp / (AmE), / wɪp ʌp / (BrE)
to prepare or cook a meal quickly and with little effort
prepare or cook quickly

Examples

She managed to whip up a delicious dinner in just thirty minutes.
If you’re hungry, I can whip up some scrambled eggs for you.
He loves to whip up pancakes for breakfast on weekends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to rustle up - to prepare or provide something quickly, especially a meal
