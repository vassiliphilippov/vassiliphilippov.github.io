to give up

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ʌp / (AmE), / ɡɪv ʌp / (BrE)
to stop having, doing, or claiming something, often permanently and with no intention of returning to it or reclaiming it.
permanently relinquish or surrender

Examples

After years of trying, she finally gave up her search for her birth parents.
The company decided to give up control of its overseas operations.
When the hikers realized they were lost, they did not give up hope of being rescued.

Synset Examples

We gave the drowning victim up for dead

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abandon - to leave behind or give up completely
  • Synonym: surrender - to give something up to another
  • Antonym: keep - to retain possession or control of something
