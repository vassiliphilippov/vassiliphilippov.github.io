to wank around

1. VERB

/ wæŋk əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / wæŋk əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
(informal, mildly vulgar, chiefly British) to waste time or avoid doing something productive; to not take something seriously.
waste time; not be serious (British, informal)
informal vulgar british

Examples

Stop wanking around and get on with your homework!
They spent the whole afternoon wanking around in the park instead of working.
If you wank around too much, you'll miss your deadline.

Synset Examples

Don't wank around, we need to finish this project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to mess around - to behave in a silly or aimless way; to waste time
  • Synonym: to fool around - to spend time doing nothing useful; to behave unseriously
  • Antonym: work hard - to put in serious and productive effort

Etymology

From British slang "wank" (to masturbate) plus "around".
