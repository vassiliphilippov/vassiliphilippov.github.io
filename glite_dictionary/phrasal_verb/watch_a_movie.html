to watch a movie

1. VERB

/ wɑtʃ ə ˈmuːvi / (AmE), / wɒtʃ ə ˈmuːvi / (BrE)
to view a film, usually for entertainment
to view a film

Examples

Every Friday, we like to watch a movie together as a family.
She invited her friends over to watch a movie and eat popcorn.
I'm too tired to go out tonight; let's just watch a movie at home.

Synset Examples

Let's watch a movie tonight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watch - to look at something for a period of time
  • Synonym: see a movie - to go to or view a film (slightly broader in use)
