to get about

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ɡɛt əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to move from place to place; to travel or circulate within an area
move around; circulate

Examples

After her surgery, she found it difficult to get about the house.
The old man still manages to get about quite well despite his age.
He used a wheelchair to get about after the accident.

Synset Examples

How does she get around without a car?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to get around - to move from place to place, especially with ease
