to call someone a jerk

1. VERB

/ kɔl ˈsʌmwʌn ə dʒɝk / (AmE), / kɔːl ˈsʌmwʌn ə dʒɜːk / (BrE)
to insult someone by referring to them as a jerk; to say that someone is unpleasant or mean
insult someone by calling them a jerk
informal

Examples

If you call someone a jerk in front of others, it can really hurt their feelings.
He was so angry that he called his brother a jerk during the argument.
You shouldn't call someone a jerk just because you disagree with them.

Synset Examples

She called him a jerk after he made that comment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: call someone names - to insult or tease someone by using offensive names
  • Antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration for someone
