to catch paraflu

1. VERB

/ kætʃ ˈpærəfluː / (AmE), / katʃ ˈparəfluː / (BrE)
to become infected with paraflu, a mild illness resembling influenza
become infected with paraflu
medical

Examples

After a weekend outdoors, he caught paraflu and had to cancel his plans.
Last year, several students caught paraflu during final exams week.
She worries her son might catch paraflu from his classmates.

Synset Examples

I caught paraflu and had to stay home from work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catch flu - to become infected with influenza
  • Degree Lower: to catch cold - to contract a common cold

Etymology

Formed from 'catch' (become infected) and 'paraflu' (from 'para-' meaning resembling or related to, and 'flu' short for influenza).
