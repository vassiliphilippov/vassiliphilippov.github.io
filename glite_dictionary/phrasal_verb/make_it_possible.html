to make it possible

1. VERB

/ meɪk ɪt ˈpɑːsəbl̩ / (AmE), / meɪk ɪt ˈpɒsəbl̩ / (BrE)
to enable or allow something to happen; to make something feasible or achievable
enable something to happen

Examples

Her generosity made it possible for several students to go to college.
Technological advances have made it possible to communicate instantly around the globe.
Their support made it possible for the project to succeed on time.

Synset Examples

She made it possible for us to attend the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to enable - to make able; to provide the means or ability
  • Antonym: to prevent - to stop something from happening
