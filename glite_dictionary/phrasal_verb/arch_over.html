to arch over

1. VERB

/ ɑrtʃ ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ɑːtʃ ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to form or extend in the shape of an arch above or across something
form an arch above something

Examples

Tall tree branches arch over the narrow road, creating a tunnel effect.
The bridge's stone supports arch over the river below.
Clouds seemed to arch over the city as the storm approached.

Synset Examples

Big rocks overarch the stream

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to overarch - form an arch over
