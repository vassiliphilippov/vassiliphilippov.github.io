to keep away

1. VERB

/ kip əˈweɪ / (AmE), / kiːp əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to prevent someone or something from coming near or approaching
prevent from approaching

Examples

Please keep away from the edge of the platform for your own safety.
The fence is there to keep away wild animals from the garden.
He tried to keep away his little sister from the kitchen while cooking.

Synset Examples

I tried to keep the child away from the pool

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to keep out - to stop someone or something from entering a place
