to head to

1. VERB

/ hɛd tu / (AmE), / hɛd tuː / (BrE)
to go in the direction of a particular place; to make one's way towards a destination.
go towards a place

Examples

After breakfast, we decided to head to the museum for the new exhibit.
Are you heading to the meeting now or waiting until later?
She usually heads to the gym before work every morning.

Synset Examples

We decided to head to the local rink for skating.
Let’s head to D-Mart for groceries.
Every weekend, we head to Tarzhay for groceries.
We're heading to Cali for vacation next month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to go to - to move or proceed toward a place
