to stand by

1. VERB

/ stænd baɪ / (AmE), / stænd baɪ / (BrE)
to remain loyal to or support someone, especially in difficult times
support or remain loyal to someone

Examples

No matter what happened, she always stood by her sister.
True friends stand by you when things get tough.
He promised to stand by his teammates during the controversy.

Synset Examples

She stood by her husband in times of trouble
The friends stuck together through the war

Related Terms

  • Synonym: support - give help or encouragement to someone
  • Antonym: to betray - to be disloyal to someone
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02644714-v
Synset POS: v

2. VERB

/ stænd baɪ / (AmE), / stænd baɪ / (BrE)
to do nothing or take no action, especially when something bad is happening and intervention is expected.
do nothing in a situation

Examples

I couldn't just stand by and watch him being bullied.
She refused to stand by while her friend was in trouble.
Many people stood by as the incident unfolded, unwilling to help.

Synset Examples

He just stood by when the police beat up the demonstrators

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to intervene - become involved to influence what happens
  • Antonym: to step in - take action to become involved
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02732260-v
Synset POS: v

3. VERB

/ stænd baɪ / (AmE), / stænd baɪ / (BrE)
to be ready and available to provide help or perform a service if needed
be ready to help or act if needed

Examples

Medical staff will stand by in case of an emergency during the event.
Please stand by for further instructions from the crew.
The rescue team stood by all night, ready to assist if needed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wait - to stay in a place or remain inactive until something happens
  • Antonym: to ignore - to pay no attention to something or someone
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02644528-v
Synset POS: v

4. VERB

/ stænd baɪ / (AmE), / stænd baɪ / (BrE)
to wait and be ready for further instructions or action; to be in a state of readiness.
wait and be ready

Examples

Please stand by for further announcements from the captain.
The emergency crew was told to stand by until they were needed.
She had to stand by while her computer rebooted.

Synset Examples

She stood by the lamppost waiting for her friend.
Please stand by while I fetch the report.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wait - stay where one is or delay action until a particular time or event
