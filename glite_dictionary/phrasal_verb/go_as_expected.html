to go as expected

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ æz ɪkˈspɛktɪd / (AmE), / ɡəʊ æz ɪkˈspɛktɪd / (BrE)
to happen or proceed in the way that was planned or anticipated
proceed as planned or anticipated

Examples

Everything at the event went as expected, with no major problems.
The project didn't go as expected and had to be delayed.
It's wise to have a backup plan in case things don't go as expected.

Synset Examples

Despite the apetempt, the plan didn't go as expected.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to go according to plan - to proceed as planned or intended
  • Antonym: to go wrong - to fail to happen as intended
