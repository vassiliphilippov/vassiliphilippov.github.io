to call attention

1. VERB

/ kɔl əˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / kɔːl əˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
to make people notice something; to point out something so it is clearly seen or considered
draw notice to something

Examples

The speaker called attention to the need for better public transport in the city.
I would like to call attention to the errors in the last report.
She called attention to the beautiful mural on the school wall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to draw attention - cause people to notice something
  • Antonym: to ignore - fail or refuse to notice
