to break wind

1. VERB

/ breɪk wɪnd / (AmE), / breɪk wɪnd / (BrE)
to expel gas from the intestines through the anus; to fart, usually in a polite or less direct way
expel intestinal gas (polite term)

Examples

My grandfather tried to be discreet when he broke wind during dinner.
It's considered rude to break wind in public, but sometimes it's unavoidable.
Whenever someone breaks wind in the elevator, everyone tries not to laugh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fart - to expel intestinal gas through the anus (informal)
