to look up to

1. VERB

/ lʊk ʌp tu / (AmE), / lʊk ʌp tʊ / (BrE)
to admire and respect (someone)
admire or respect someone

Examples

Many young athletes look up to Serena Williams as a role model.
I have always looked up to my older brother for his kindness and strength.
She hopes her students will look up to her as a positive influence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to admire - to regard with respect or warm approval
  • Antonym: to look down on - to regard (someone) with a feeling of superiority
