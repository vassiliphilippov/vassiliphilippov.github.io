to piss and moan

1. VERB

/ pɪs ənd moʊn / (AmE), / pɪs ənd məʊn / (BrE)
to complain or grumble persistently and in an annoying way, especially about trivial matters; to whine repeatedly
to complain persistently or whine
informal vulgar

Examples

All he did during the meeting was piss and moan about the changes.
Instead of fixing the issue, she just pisses and moans every day.
They spent the trip pissing and moaning about the hotel service.

Synset Examples

She loves to piss and moan about her job.
He constantly pisses and moans about the weather.
Stop pissing and moaning; it's time to act.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to complain - to express dissatisfaction or annoyance about something
  • Synonym: whine - to complain in an annoying or persistent manner
  • Antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration; the opposite of complain
