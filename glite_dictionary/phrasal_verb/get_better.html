to get better

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈbɛt̬.ɚ / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈbɛt.ə / (BrE)
to improve in condition, quality, or situation; to become better
improve in condition or situation

Examples

After taking the medicine, she began to get better within a few days.
Economic conditions are expected to get better in the coming year.
If you practice regularly, your skills will gradually get better.

Synset Examples

He assured her that things would get better.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to improve - to become or make something better
  • Antonym: to deteriorate - to become worse
