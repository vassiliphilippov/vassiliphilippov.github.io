to ride horseback

1. VERB

/ raɪd ˈhɔrsˌbæk / (AmE), / raɪd ˈhɔːsbæk / (BrE)
to ride on a horse; to travel or move while sitting on and directing a horse
to ride on a horse

Examples

She learned to ride horseback at a young age on her family's farm.
Many tourists want to ride horseback along the scenic mountain trails.
He prefers to ride horseback instead of walking when checking the cattle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ride a horse - to sit on and control a horse as it moves
