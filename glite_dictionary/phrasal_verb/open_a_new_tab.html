to open a new tab

1. VERB

/ ˈoʊpən ə nuː tæb / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən ə njuː tæb / (BrE)
to create a new tab in a web browser, allowing additional web pages to be viewed without leaving the current page
create a new browser tab

Examples

Whenever I want to look up something quickly, I just open a new tab.
You can open a new tab to keep several websites active at once.
She prefers to open a new tab rather than navigate away from her current page.

Synset Examples

He opened a new tab to continue browsing.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: close a tab - to close or remove a browser tab
