to brush one's teeth

1. VERB

/ brʌʃ wʌnz tiθ / (AmE), / brʌʃ wʌnz tiːθ / (BrE)
to clean one's teeth using a toothbrush, typically with toothpaste
clean teeth with a toothbrush

Examples

Every morning I brush my teeth before breakfast.
Don’t forget to brush your teeth before going to the dentist.
She teaches her children to brush their teeth twice a day.

Synset Examples

He brushed his tooth carefully before bed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clean one's teeth - to make one's teeth clean
