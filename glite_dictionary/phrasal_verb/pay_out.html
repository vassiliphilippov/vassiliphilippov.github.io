to pay out

1. VERB

/ peɪ aʊt / (AmE), / peɪ aʊt / (BrE)
to spend or distribute money, especially from a fund or account
spend or distribute money

Examples

The company was forced to pay out millions in compensation after losing the lawsuit.
We have to pay out a large sum every year to maintain our equipment.
The insurance policy will only pay out if there is proof of damage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to disburse - to pay out money, especially from a fund
  • Antonym: to withhold - to refuse to give something, especially money
