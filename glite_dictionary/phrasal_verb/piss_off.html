to piss off

1. VERB

/ pɪs ɔf / (AmE), / pɪs ɒf / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) To annoy or anger someone; to make someone irritated or angry.
to annoy or anger someone (vulgar)
informal vulgar

Examples

Don't piss off your friend by being late again.
He seemed determined to piss off everyone in the room.
Loud noises always piss off the neighbors in this building.
