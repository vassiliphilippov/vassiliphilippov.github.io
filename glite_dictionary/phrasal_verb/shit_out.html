to shit out

1. VERB

/ ʃɪt aʊt / (AmE), / ʃɪt aʊt / (BrE)
To produce or express something quickly and carelessly, often with little effort or attention to quality.
produce something quickly or carelessly (vulgar, slang)
vulgar slang

Examples

He tends to shit out quick reports the night before every deadline.
The band just shat out another album without any real effort.
She shits out ideas all day, but rarely follows through with them.

Synset Examples

He always seems to shit out the same ideas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to dash off - write or produce something quickly
  • Synonym: to crank out - produce something in large quantities, typically quickly or mechanically
