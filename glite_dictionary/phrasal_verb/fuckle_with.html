to fuckle with

1. VERB

/ ˈfʌk.əl wɪð / (AmE), / ˈfʌk.əl wɪð / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) To mess around with or tinker with something, usually in a casual or careless way.
slang: mess with, tinker with
slang vulgar

Examples

He spent the whole afternoon trying to fuckle with the remote control to get it working.
Please don't fuckle with my computer settings without asking me first.
Every time the engine makes a noise, he feels the need to fuckle with it.

Synset Examples

Don't make me fuckle with the settings again.
I'll fuckle with it tomorrow if it still doesn't work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mess with - to interfere or tinker with something, sometimes unwisely
  • Synonym: tinker with - to make small changes to something, often experimentally or aimlessly

Etymology

Blend of 'fuck' (slang, vulgar) and 'tinker' or 'fiddle.'
