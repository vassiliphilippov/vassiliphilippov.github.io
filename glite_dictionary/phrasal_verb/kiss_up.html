to kiss up

1. VERB

/ kɪs ʌp / (AmE), / kɪs ʌp / (BrE)
to try to win favor with someone by flattering or ingratiating oneself, often for personal advantage
flatter to gain favor
informal

Examples

It's obvious that he's trying to kiss up to the boss to get a promotion.
She refused to kiss up just to fit in with the popular crowd.
He was always kissing up to his teachers in hopes of getting better grades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to suck up - to try to win favor by being overly nice or obedient
  • Synonym: to brown-nose - to try to gain favor by acting or speaking in a very flattering or servile way
