to chat about

1. VERB

/ tʃæt əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / tʃæt əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to talk in a casual or informal way about a particular subject
talk informally about a subject

Examples

We often chat about our favorite movies after class.
Whenever they meet, they chat about the latest news and gossip.
She likes to chat about her travels with anyone who will listen.

Synset Examples

He loves to chat about sports with his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to discuss - talk about something in detail
  • Degree Lower: chat - talk in a friendly, informal way
