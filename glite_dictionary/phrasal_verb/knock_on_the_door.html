to knock on the door

1. VERB

/ nɑk ɑn ðə dɔr / (AmE), / nɒk ɒn ðə dɔː / (BrE)
to hit a door with your hand or an object to attract the attention of those inside
hit a door to get attention

Examples

She quietly knocked on the door before entering the office.
I was surprised when someone knocked on the door late at night.
The children ran to knock on the door and sing carols.

Synset Examples

He knocked loudly on the door with the knocker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knock - to strike a surface noisily to attract attention
