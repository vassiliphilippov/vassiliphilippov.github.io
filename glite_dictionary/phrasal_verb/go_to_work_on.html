to go to work on

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ tə wɜrk ɑn / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə wɜːk ɒn / (BrE)
to begin working seriously on a task or project.
begin working on a task

Examples

We should go to work on fixing the car before it gets dark.
The team quickly went to work on the proposal after receiving feedback from management.
She plans to go to work on her assignment as soon as she gets home.

Synset Examples

They need to go to work on the project soon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: start - to begin doing something; synonyms as to start work on
  • Antonym: finish - to bring a task or activity to an end; antonym in task contexts
