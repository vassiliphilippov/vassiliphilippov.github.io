to keep entertained

1. VERB

/ kip ˌɛn.tɚˈteɪnd / (AmE), / kiːp ˌɛn.təˈteɪnd / (BrE)
to maintain someone's interest or amusement over a period of time
maintain someone's interest or amusement

Examples

The magician did his best to keep entertained the children during the party.
She brought games and books to keep entertained on the long journey.
He always knows how to keep entertained his guests at dinner.

Synset Examples

Teague's jokes kept the team entertained throughout the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to amuse - to make someone laugh or smile; to entertain
  • Antonym: bore - to make someone feel uninterested or tired by being dull
