to sew together

1. VERB

/ soʊ təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / səʊ təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to join two or more pieces of fabric or material by sewing them with a needle and thread
join pieces by sewing

Examples

She carefully sewed together the two pieces of cloth to make a pillow cover.
The tailor sewed together the torn seams on my jacket.
After the repairs, the patches were sewn together so neatly that they were barely visible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stitch - to make, mend, or join with stitches
  • Synonym: mend - to repair (something that is broken or damaged)
