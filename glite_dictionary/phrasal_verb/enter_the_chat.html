to enter the chat

1. VERB

/ ˈɛntər ðə tʃæt / (AmE), / ˈɛntə ðə tʃæt / (BrE)
to join an online conversation or discussion group, typically via a chat room or digital messaging platform
join an online conversation
informal

Examples

Please enter the chat by clicking the link sent to your email.
Several new participants entered the chat during the webinar.
After a few minutes of troubleshooting, I was finally able to enter the chat.

Synset Examples

Make sure to enter the chat before the meeting starts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: join the chat - to participate in an online chat or group discussion
  • Antonym: leave the chat - to exit an online chat or group discussion
