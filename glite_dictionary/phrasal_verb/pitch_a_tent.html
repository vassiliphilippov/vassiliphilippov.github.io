to pitch a tent

1. VERB

/ pɪtʃ ə tɛnt / (AmE), / pɪtʃ ə tɛnt / (BrE)
to set up or erect a tent, usually for camping or temporary shelter
set up a tent

Examples

We pitched a tent near the lake before sunset.
It started to rain just as they finished pitching a tent.
She pitched a tent on the hillside and admired the view.

Synset Examples

He pitched a tent in the backyard for the kids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: erect a tent - to put up a tent
  • Antonym: take down a tent - to disassemble or remove a tent

Etymology

From the verb 'pitch' (to set up or fix in place) combined with 'tent.'
