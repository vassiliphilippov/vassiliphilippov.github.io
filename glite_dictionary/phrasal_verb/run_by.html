to run by

1. VERB

/ rʌn baɪ / (AmE), / rʌn baɪ / (BrE)
to pass close to someone or something while moving quickly on foot
pass by while running

Examples

The kids would run by my house every morning on their way to school.
We watched the athletes run by as the marathon passed through our street.
A dog suddenly ran by while we were sitting in the park.

Synset Examples

We watched children were running by

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to run past - to move quickly past someone or something on foot
