to spin down

1. VERB

/ spɪn daʊn / (AmE), / spɪn daʊn / (BrE)
to gradually reduce activity or energy and begin to relax, especially after a period of busyness
gradually relax after activity

Examples

After a stressful meeting, I usually spin down by listening to music.
It's important to give yourself time to spin down before going to sleep.
She likes to spin down with a cup of tea in the evening.

Synset Examples

You need to spin down your activities before bed.
I like to spin down with a good book.
After a busy day, it's time to spin down and relax.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to wind down - to gradually reduce activity before stopping
