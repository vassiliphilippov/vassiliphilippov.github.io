to lie down

1. VERB

/ laɪ daʊn / (AmE), / laɪ daʊn / (BrE)
to put your body in a flat or horizontal position, typically in order to rest or sleep
recline; put body flat to rest

Examples

I'm going to lie down for a few minutes before dinner.
After feeling dizzy, she decided to lie down on the sofa.
You should lie down if you feel unwell.

Synset Examples

lie down on the bed until you feel better

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to recline - to lean or lie back in a relaxed position
  • Antonym: to stand up - to rise from a seated or lying position
  • Antonym: to sit down - to assume a seated position
