to pack on

1. VERB

/ pæk ɑn / (AmE), / pæk ɒn / (BrE)
to gain (weight), especially in noticeable or significant amounts
gain weight, often noticeably
informal

Examples

He started to pack on weight after switching to a desk job.
People often pack on a few extra pounds during the holidays.
She realized she had packed on ten kilos over the past year.

Synset Examples

He packed on two pounds over the summer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to put on - to gain weight
