to burn time

1. VERB

/ bɝn taɪm / (AmE), / bɜːn taɪm / (BrE)
to spend time idly or doing something unproductive, often to fill a period of waiting
spend time idly or unproductively
informal

Examples

We had an hour to wait, so we decided to burn time at the café.
She usually burns time scrolling through her phone before bed.
It’s easy to burn time watching videos online when you’re bored.

Synset Examples

We can burn time by grabbing a coffee.
Let’s burn some time before our meeting starts.
He likes to burn time by browsing social media.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kill time - to do something to make time pass more quickly while waiting
