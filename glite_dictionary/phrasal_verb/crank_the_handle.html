to crank the handle

1. VERB

/ kræŋk ðə ˈhændl̩ / (AmE), / kræŋk ðə ˈhændl / (BrE)
to turn a handle in order to operate or start a machine or mechanism
turn a handle to operate a machine

Examples

To rewind the tape, you need to crank the handle until it stops.
As a child, she used to crank the handle of the ice cream maker every summer.
Remember to crank the handle clockwise to open the old-fashioned car window.

Synset Examples

You need to crank the handle to start the old engine.
Make sure to crank the handle gently to avoid breaking it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turn the handle - to rotate a handle to make something happen
