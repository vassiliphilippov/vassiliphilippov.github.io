to churn ice cream

1. VERB

/ tʃɜrn aɪs kriːm / (AmE), / tʃɜːn aɪs kriːm / (BrE)
to mix and freeze a sweet creamy mixture by stirring continuously to make ice cream
mix and freeze to make ice cream

Examples

It takes about thirty minutes to churn ice cream by hand using a crank freezer.
We used fresh strawberries when we churned ice cream for the summer picnic.
She enjoys churning ice cream with her children on hot afternoons.

Synset Examples

He watched the machine churn the mixture into ice cream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make ice cream - to prepare ice cream by combining and freezing ingredients
