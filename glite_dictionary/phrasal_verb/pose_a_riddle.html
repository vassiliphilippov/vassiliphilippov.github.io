to pose a riddle

1. VERB

/ poʊz ə ˈrɪdəl / (AmE), / pəʊz ə ˈrɪdəl / (BrE)
to present a puzzling question or problem for others to solve
present a puzzling question

Examples

At the start of the meeting, the manager posed a riddle to spark creative thinking.
During the party, she enjoyed posing riddles to her friends.
Every morning, the radio show host poses a riddle for listeners to solve.

Synset Examples

The teacher posed a riddle to engage the students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ask a question - to pose or put forward a query
  • Antonym: solve a riddle - to find the answer to a puzzling question
