to crunch numbers

1. VERB

/ krʌntʃ ˈnʌmbɚz / (AmE), / krʌntʃ ˈnʌmbəz / (BrE)
to perform mathematical calculations or analyze numerical data, especially quickly or efficiently
perform calculations or numerical analysis

Examples

The accountants had to crunch numbers all night to prepare the final report.
Data analysts often crunch numbers to find patterns in large datasets.
She likes to crunch numbers before making any major purchases.

Synset Examples

She loves to crunch numbers for her budget.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to calculate - compute mathematically
