to give vent

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv vɛnt / (AmE), / ɡɪv vent / (BrE)
to express or release one's feelings, especially emotions such as anger or excitement
express or release feelings

Examples

She needed to give vent to her frustrations after the long meeting.
Many people give vent to their emotions through music or art.
He finally gave vent to his feelings in a passionate speech.

Synset Examples

She vented her anger
The graduates gave vent to cheers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: express - to show or make known a feeling or thought
