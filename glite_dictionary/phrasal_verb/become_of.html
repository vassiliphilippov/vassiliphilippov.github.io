to become of

1. VERB

/ bɪˈkʌm əv / (AmE), / bɪˈkʌm əv / (BrE)
to happen to someone or something; to be the outcome or fate of someone or something
happen to; be the fate of

Examples

No one knows what will become of the missing documents after the investigation.
She often wondered what had become of her childhood friends over the years.
If the government cuts the funding, what will become of the scientific research?

Synset Examples

I wonder what will become of their plans.
It's sad to see what has become of the neighborhood.
What will become of the old library?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to befall - (of something bad) happen to someone
