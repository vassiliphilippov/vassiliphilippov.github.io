to shop at

1. VERB

/ ʃɑp æt / (AmE), / ʃɒp æt / (BrE)
to buy goods from or be a customer at a particular store or business
buy goods from a store

Examples

Many people prefer to shop at local markets to support small businesses.
She always shops at the same grocery store because of their fresh vegetables.
I used to shop at that boutique before it closed last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shop - to visit shops to buy things
