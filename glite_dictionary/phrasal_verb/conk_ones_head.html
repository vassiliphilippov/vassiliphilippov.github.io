to conk one's head

1. VERB

/ kɑŋk wʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / kɒŋk wʌnz hɛd / (BrE)
to accidentally hit or bump one's head against something
hit or bump one's head by accident
informal

Examples

He managed to conk his head on the low doorway as he was leaving the room.
When she slipped on the ice, she conked her head against the ground.
Watch your step, or you might conk your head on that shelf!

Synset Examples

The loud noise made him conk his head on the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bump one's head - to knock or hit one's head accidentally

Etymology

Probably from slang 'conk,' meaning to hit or strike, origin uncertain but attested since the early 20th century.
