to burst forth

1. VERB

/ bɝst fɔrθ / (AmE), / bɜːst fɔːθ / (BrE)
to suddenly and forcefully emerge or appear, especially with noise or energy
emerge suddenly and forcefully

Examples

A rainbow suddenly burst forth after the storm clouds parted.
Cheers burst forth from the crowd as the team scored the winning goal.
The flowers burst forth in full bloom at the first sign of spring.

Synset Examples

His anger exploded

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to erupt - to break out suddenly and violently
  • Antonym: to subside - to become less violent or intense; to settle down

2. VERB

/ bɝst fɔrθ / (AmE), / bɜːst fɔːθ / (BrE)
to suddenly jump out from a hiding place to surprise someone
suddenly jump out to surprise

Examples

As soon as the door opened, the children burst forth from their hiding spot, laughing loudly.
Several animals burst forth from the bushes, startling the hikers on the trail.
Suddenly, masked actors burst forth onto the stage, surprising the audience.

Synset Examples

The attackers leapt out from the bushes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to leap out - to jump out suddenly from a concealed place
  • Synonym: spring out - to jump or move out suddenly and quickly

3. VERB

/ bɝst ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / bɜːst ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)
to appear or develop suddenly and vigorously, especially in the way flowers bloom; to emerge or blossom suddenly
to appear or blossom suddenly

Examples

After the rain, the wildflowers burst forth in a riot of colors across the meadow.
Cheers burst forth from the crowd as the fireworks began.
New ideas burst forth in her mind as she listened to the inspiring talk.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to erupt - to burst out suddenly and violently
  • Antonym: to wither - to dry up or fade away (especially of plants)

4. VERB

/ bɝst fɔrθ / (AmE), / bɜːst fɔːθ / (BrE)
to suddenly appear, emerge, or become visible or audible with force or intensity
suddenly emerge or appear

Examples

After hours of silence, he burst forth with a passionate speech.
In spring, new leaves burst forth from the bare branches.

Synset Examples
