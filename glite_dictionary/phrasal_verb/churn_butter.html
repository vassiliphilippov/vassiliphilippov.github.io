to churn butter

1. VERB

/ tʃɝn ˈbʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / tʃɜːn ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
to make butter by agitating cream until it separates into butter and buttermilk, typically using a churn
make butter by agitating cream

Examples

In the old days, families would churn butter by hand in a wooden churn.
She watched her grandmother churn butter as part of the farm's daily routine.
They invite visitors to help churn butter during the festival.

Synset Examples

They churn butter every summer for family gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butter - a pale yellow edible fatty substance made by churning cream
