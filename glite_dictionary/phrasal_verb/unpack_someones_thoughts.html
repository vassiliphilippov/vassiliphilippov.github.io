to unpack someone's thoughts

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈpæk ˈsʌmwʌnz θɔts / (AmE), / ʌnˈpak ˈsʌmwʌnz θɔːts / (BrE)
to examine, explain, or clarify one's ideas or reasoning in detail
analyze or clarify one's ideas
informal

Examples

After the heated argument, she needed time to unpack her thoughts before responding.
The teacher helped the student unpack his thoughts during the discussion.
I tried to unpack my thoughts in my journal last night.

Synset Examples

He took a moment to unpack his thoughts after the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to analyze - examine in detail to understand meaning or structure
  • Synonym: to clarify - make something clearer by explaining it more thoroughly
