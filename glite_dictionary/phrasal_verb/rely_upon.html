to rely upon

1. VERB

/ rɪˈlaɪ əˈpɑn / (AmE), / rɪˈlaɪ əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to depend on or trust someone or something with confidence
depend or trust with confidence

Examples

I know I can rely upon her honesty in all circumstances.
Many small businesses rely upon local customers to survive.
You shouldn't rely upon good weather for the outdoor event.

Synset Examples

she is someone you can really rely on when times get rough
you can rely on his discretion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to rely on - depend on with confidence; alternate preposition for rely
