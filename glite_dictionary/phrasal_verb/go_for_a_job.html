to go for a job

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ fər ə dʒɑb / (AmE), / ɡəʊ fə ə dʒɒb / (BrE)
to apply or actively pursue a position of employment
apply or pursue a job

Examples

After college, he decided to go for a job in marketing instead of continuing his studies.
Many graduates go for a job as soon as they finish university.
It's important to update your resume before you go for a job interview.

Synset Examples

She decided to go for a job that challenged her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apply for a job - to formally request employment at a company
