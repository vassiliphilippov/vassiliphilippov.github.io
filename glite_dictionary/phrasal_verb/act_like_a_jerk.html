to act like a jerk

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə dʒɝk / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə dʒɜːk / (BrE)
to behave in a rude, insensitive, or obnoxious manner
behave rudely or insensitively
informal

Examples

Please don't act like a jerk when we visit my parents.
After losing the game, he started acting like a jerk to everyone around him.
She realized she had acted like a jerk and apologized to her coworkers.

Synset Examples

He acted like a jerk during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be rude - to behave impolitely or disrespectfully
  • Antonym: behave oneself - to act in a polite and correct way
