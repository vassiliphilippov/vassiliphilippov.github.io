to ask around

1. VERB

/ æsk əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ɑːsk əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to try to obtain information by inquiring with several people, usually informally
inquire with several people

Examples

She decided to ask around to find a good mechanic in the area.
I'll ask around and see if anyone knows a reliable babysitter.
Before buying the car, he wanted to ask around for other people's experiences.

Synset Examples

I’ll ask around to see if anyone has seen him.
Let me ask around for some advice on that project.
You should ask around for recommendations on restaurants.
I’ll ask round to see who’s available.
I need to ask round about the meeting time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to inquire - to ask for information
  • Synonym: to ask - to put a question to someone
