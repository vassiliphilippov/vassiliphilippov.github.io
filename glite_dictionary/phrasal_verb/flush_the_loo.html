to flush the loo

1. VERB

/ flʌʃ ðə luː / (AmE), / flʌʃ ðə luː / (BrE)
to clean the toilet by operating the flush mechanism, especially in British English
flush the toilet (British)
british

Examples

Please remember to flush the loo before leaving the bathroom.
He always forgets to flush the loo after using it, which is quite annoying.
Children should be taught to flush the loo as soon as they're old enough.

Synset Examples

Make sure to flush the loo after use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flush the toilet - to clean or empty the toilet by flushing

Etymology

'Loo' is British slang for toilet, possibly from French 'lieux d'aisances' (places of ease).
