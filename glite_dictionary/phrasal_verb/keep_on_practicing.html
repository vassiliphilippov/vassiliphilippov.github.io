to keep on practicing

1. VERB

/ kip ɑn ˈpræk.tɪ.sɪŋ / (AmE), / kiːp ɒn ˈpræk.tɪ.sɪŋ / (BrE)
to continue to practice something, especially to improve your skills or maintain proficiency
continue to practice

Examples

If you want to get better at the piano, you have to keep on practicing.
Even when he felt discouraged, she told him to keep on practicing.
They kept on practicing every day until the routine was perfect.

Synset Examples

You should keep on practicing if you want to improve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: practice - to perform an activity repeatedly or regularly to improve
  • Antonym: to give up - to stop doing something, especially something continuous
