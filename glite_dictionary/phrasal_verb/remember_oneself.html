to remember oneself

1. VERB

/ rɪˈmɛmbɚ wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / rɪˈmɛmbə wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to regain one's self-control and return to proper or polite behavior after a lapse
regain self-control or propriety

Examples

After the heated argument, she paused to remember herself and apologized for her words.
You must remember yourself when speaking to your elders, even if you are upset.
He lost his temper briefly, but quickly remembered himself and continued calmly.

Synset Examples

Please remember yourself, Charles!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compose oneself - to calm oneself and regain control of one's feelings or actions
