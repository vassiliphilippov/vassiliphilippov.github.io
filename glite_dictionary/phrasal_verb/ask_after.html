to ask after

1. VERB

/ æsk ˈæftɚ / (AmE), / ɑːsk ˈɑːftə / (BrE)
to inquire about the health, well-being, or circumstances of someone
inquire about someone's well-being

Examples

Whenever I see your mother, she always asks after you.
At the reunion, many old friends asked after my father.
Please ask after Jane next time you visit her office.

Synset Examples

He made sure to ask after her health during lunch.
I will ask after the meeting to see how it went.
She always asks after your family when we meet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ask for - to request something
