to go together

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / ɡəʊ təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to match, fit well, or be compatible with each other, especially in terms of appearance or suitability
to match or be compatible

Examples

These two colors go together beautifully in the living room.
Do you think this top and these jeans go together?
Certain flavors, like lemon and mint, really go together in desserts.

Synset Examples

These shoes and dress go together perfectly.
Peanut butter and jelly really go together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: match - to be similar or suitable when compared
  • Antonym: clash - to not match or be harmonious
