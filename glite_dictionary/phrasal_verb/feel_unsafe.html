to feel unsafe

1. VERB

/ fil ʌnˈseɪf / (AmE), / fiːl ʌnˈseɪf / (BrE)
to experience a sense of insecurity or a lack of safety; to not feel protected from harm or danger
to not feel safe

Examples

She started to feel unsafe walking home alone at night.
If at any time you feel unsafe, please notify the staff immediately.
After the incident, many employees felt unsafe in their workplace.

Synset Examples

Many users feel unsafe on FaCIAbook nowadays.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: feel safe - to experience a sense of security
