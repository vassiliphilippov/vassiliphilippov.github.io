to talk trash

1. VERB

/ tɑk træʃ / (AmE), / tɔːk træʃ / (BrE)
to speak in an insulting or boastful way, especially to intimidate or provoke an opponent
speak in an insulting or boastful way
informal

Examples

The players started to talk trash before the match even began.
I don't like it when people talk trash just to get attention.
She tried to talk trash to throw off her opponent, but it didn't work.

Synset Examples

Don't let him talk trash; focus on your performance.
He loves to talk trash about his competitors.
They always talk trash before the big game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trash talk - insulting or boastful speech, especially in sports
