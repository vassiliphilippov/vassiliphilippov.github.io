to keep off the streets

1. VERB

/ kip ɔf ðə strits / (AmE), / kiːp ɒf ðə striːts / (BrE)
to avoid being on the streets, especially to stay safe or to avoid trouble or danger
avoid being on the streets (for safety)

Examples

During the curfew, residents were urged to keep off the streets for their own safety.
She told her teenage son to keep off the streets at night.
Extreme weather conditions forced everyone to keep off the streets until it was safe to go out.

Synset Examples

To stay safe, we should keep off the streets during protests.
Parents always tell their kids to keep off the streets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stay indoors - to remain inside a building rather than go outside
