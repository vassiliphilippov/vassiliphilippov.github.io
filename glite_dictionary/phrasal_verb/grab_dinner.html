to grab dinner

1. VERB

/ ɡræb ˈdɪnər / (AmE), / ɡræb ˈdɪnə / (BrE)
to eat dinner quickly or casually, often without formal planning
eat dinner casually or informally
informal

Examples

We decided to grab dinner after the movie ended.
Do you want to grab dinner at that new Italian place tonight?
She usually grabs dinner on her way home from work.

Synset Examples

Let's grab dinner together, what do you say?

Related Terms
