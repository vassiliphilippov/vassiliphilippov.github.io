to cross one's leg

1. VERB

/ krɔs wʌnz lɛɡ / (AmE), / krɒs wʌnz leɡ / (BrE)
to place one leg over the other while sitting
sit with one leg over the other

Examples

She habitually crosses her leg during meetings, finding it more comfortable.
He paused to cross his leg before starting his story.
Many people instinctively cross their leg when sitting for a long time.

Synset Examples

He crossed his leg while sitting on the couch.
