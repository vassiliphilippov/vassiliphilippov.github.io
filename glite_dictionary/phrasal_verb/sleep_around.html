to sleep around

1. VERB

/ sliːp əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / sliːp əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to have casual sexual relationships with multiple partners; not to be in a committed sexual relationship
have casual sexual relationships
informal

Examples

Rumors spread quickly when people heard he was sleeping around with his coworkers.
She regrets sleeping around during her college years.
After ending her long-term relationship, she started sleeping around in search of new experiences.

Synset Examples

She decided to stop sleeping around and focus on one relationship.
They broke up because he couldn't stop sleeping around.
He has a reputation for sleeping around at parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be promiscuous - to have many sexual partners
