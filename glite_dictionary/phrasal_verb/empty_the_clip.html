to empty the clip

1. VERB

/ ˈɛmpti ðə klɪp / (AmE), / ˈɛmpti ðə klɪp / (BrE)
to fire all the bullets from a firearm's magazine until none remain
fire all bullets from a magazine

Examples

During the simulation, the recruits were instructed to empty the clip when they encountered the target.
It can be dangerous to empty the clip without being fully aware of your surroundings.
In action movies, protagonists often empty the clip in dramatic firefights.

Synset Examples

He decided to empty the clip during practice to improve accuracy.
The soldier was ordered to empty the clip on command.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to reload - to refill a firearm with ammunition
