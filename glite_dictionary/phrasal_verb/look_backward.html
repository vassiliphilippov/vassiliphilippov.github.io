to look backward

1. VERB

/ lʊk ˈbækˌwɚd / (AmE), / lʊk ˈbæk.wəd / (BrE)
to turn your head or eyes to see what is behind you
turn to see behind oneself

Examples

She paused on the trail to look backward and check if anyone was following her.
Drivers are advised not to look backward when merging onto a busy highway.
Before leaving the room, he couldn't resist turning to look backward one last time.

Synset Examples

don't look back while you walk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to look back - to turn and look behind oneself; also to think about the past
