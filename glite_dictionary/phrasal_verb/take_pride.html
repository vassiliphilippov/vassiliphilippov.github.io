to take pride

1. VERB

/ teɪk praɪd / (AmE), / teɪk praɪd / (BrE)
to feel proud of something or someone, especially as a result of achievement or quality
feel proud of something or someone

Examples

She always takes pride in her work, ensuring every detail is perfect.
The chef took pride in using only the freshest ingredients in his dishes.
They take pride in helping their community through volunteer work.

Synset Examples

She took great pride in her sons

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be proud of - to feel deep pleasure or satisfaction as a result of a quality or achievement
