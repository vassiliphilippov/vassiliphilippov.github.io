to browse the internet

1. VERB

/ braʊz ði ˈɪntərˌnɛt / (AmE), / braʊz ði ˈɪntəˌnɛt / (BrE)
to look through or search information and content available on the internet, usually in a casual or exploratory manner
search or look through online content

Examples

Many people like to browse the internet in the evenings to relax after work.
She prefers to browse the internet on her tablet while traveling.
He spent an hour browsing the internet for information about his upcoming trip.

Synset Examples

She spends hours browsing the internet for new music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surf the web - to move from site to site on the internet for interest or information
