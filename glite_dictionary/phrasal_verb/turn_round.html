to turn round

1. VERB

/ tɝn raʊnd / (AmE), / tɜːn raʊnd / (BrE)
to move your body so that you are facing in the opposite direction
to turn one's body to face another direction
british

Examples

She suddenly turned round when she heard her name.
Please turn round so I can take your photo.
He turned round to greet the person behind him.

Synset Examples

She turned round to see who was calling her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to turn around - to move one's body to face the opposite direction (American English)
