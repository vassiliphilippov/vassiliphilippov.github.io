to knock over

1. VERB

/ nɑk ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / nɒk ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to cause something to fall or turn over, usually from an upright position, by striking or colliding with it
cause to fall over by striking

Examples

Be careful not to knock over the lamp when you walk by.
The strong wind knocked over several trash cans on the street last night.
She accidentally knocked over her water bottle during the meeting.

Synset Examples

The cat knocked over the flower vase
the clumsy customer turned over the vase
he tumped over his beer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overturn - turn something upside down or on its side
  • Antonym: upright - in a vertical or erect position (opposite state)
