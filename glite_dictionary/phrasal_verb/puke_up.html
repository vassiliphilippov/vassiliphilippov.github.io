to puke up

1. VERB

/ pjuk ʌp / (AmE), / pjuːk ʌp / (BrE)
to vomit food that has been eaten; to regurgitate something after ingesting it
vomit food that was eaten
informal

Examples

After drinking spoiled milk, he had to puke up his dinner.
The medicine made her so sick she puked up everything she had eaten.
He was embarrassed when he started to puke up at the party.

Synset Examples

She felt sick and had to puke up the pizza.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vomit - to eject matter from the stomach through the mouth
