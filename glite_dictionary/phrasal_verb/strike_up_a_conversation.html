to strike up a conversation

1. VERB

/ straɪk ʌp ə ˌkɑːn.vɚˈseɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / straɪk ʌp ə ˌkɒn.vəˈseɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
to begin talking with someone, especially someone you do not know well
start talking with someone

Examples

It can be nerve-wracking to strike up a conversation with someone new at a party.
During her flight, he managed to strike up a conversation with the person sitting next to him.
Sometimes, the best friendships begin when you strike up a conversation with a stranger.

Synset Examples

She decided to strike up a conversation with her neighbor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: start a conversation - to begin speaking with someone
  • Synonym: break the ice - to do or say something to relieve tension and get conversation started
