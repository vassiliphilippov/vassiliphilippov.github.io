to pound a beat

1. VERB

/ paʊnd ə bit / (AmE), / paʊnd ə biːt / (BrE)
to play a rhythm or beat with force or emphasis, especially in music
play a rhythm forcefully (music)

Examples

The drummer can pound a beat that energizes the whole crowd.
She loves to pound a beat on her drum during jam sessions.
With every song, the band would pound a beat that made everyone dance.

Synset Examples
