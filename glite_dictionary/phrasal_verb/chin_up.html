to chin up

1. VERB

/ tʃɪn ʌp / (AmE), / tʃɪn ʌp / (BrE)
to pull oneself upward while hanging from a bar until the chin is level with or above the bar, typically as a strength exercise
pull up on a bar as exercise

Examples

He can chin up ten times in a row without resting.
The trainer asked us to chin up as many times as possible for the test.
She struggled to chin up, but eventually built enough strength to do it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pull-up - an upper body exercise similar to a chin up, usually with palms facing away
