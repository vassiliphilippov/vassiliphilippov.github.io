to create from raw material

1. VERB

/ kriˈeɪt frəm rɔː məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / kriˈeɪt frəm rɔː məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
to make or produce something by starting with basic, unprocessed substances rather than using pre-made components
make using basic materials, not from kits

Examples

The artist prefers to create from raw material, crafting each sculpture entirely by hand.
Some chefs insist on creating from raw material to ensure the best flavors in their dishes.
The company aims to create from raw material rather than rely on imported parts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make from scratch - to make something starting from the most basic materials, not using a prepared product
