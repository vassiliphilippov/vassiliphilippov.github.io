to seek out

1. VERB

/ siːk aʊt / (AmE), / siːk aʊt / (BrE)
to look for and find a specific person or thing, often with effort or determination
look for and find something or someone

Examples

She made an effort to seek out talented artists for the new exhibition.
It took him several months to seek out the perfect apartment in the city.
Volunteers are encouraged to seek out those in need within the community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: search for - to try to find something by looking or seeking
  • Synonym: to look for - to try to find something or someone
