to act like a dumbo

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈdʌmboʊ / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈdʌmbəʊ / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or stupid way
behave foolishly or stupidly
informal slang

Examples

If you keep acting like a dumbo, nobody will take you seriously at work.
She realized she had been acting like a dumbo and quickly apologized for her mistake.
Stop acting like a dumbo—just read the instructions carefully before you begin.

Synset Examples

Quit acting like a dumbo and use your brain!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: act foolish - behave in a silly or unwise way
  • Antonym: act sensibly - to behave in a wise or sensible way

Etymology

From the noun 'dumbo' (slang for a foolish person), itself derived from the name of the Disney elephant character 'Dumbo' (1941).
