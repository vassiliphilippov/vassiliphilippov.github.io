to occupy oneself

1. VERB

/ ˈɑːkjəˌpaɪ wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / ˈɒkjʊˌpaɪ wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to engage in or keep oneself busy with an activity
to keep oneself busy

Examples

During the lockdown, he tried to occupy himself by learning to cook new dishes.
She would often occupy herself with puzzles to pass the time.
Children need to occupy themselves during long car journeys.

Synset Examples

She decided to occupy herself with gardening during the summer.
To avoid boredom, she found ways to occupy herself with new hobbies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: busy oneself - to keep oneself busy with an activity
