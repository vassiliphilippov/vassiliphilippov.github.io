to bathe in

1. VERB

/ beɪð ɪn / (AmE), / beɪð ɪn / (BrE)
to immerse oneself in water for the purpose of cleansing or relaxation.
immerse in water to cleanse or relax

Examples

After a long day, she likes to bathe in a hot tub to unwind.
Many animals instinctively bathe in rivers or lakes to clean themselves.
People sometimes bathe in mineral springs for the health benefits.

Synset Examples

I prefer to bathe in the evening for relaxation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: shower - to wash oneself under a spray of water
  • Synonym: soak - to sit or lie in liquid, especially for relaxation or cleaning
