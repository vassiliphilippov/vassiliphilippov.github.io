to prevail over

1. VERB

/ prɪˈveɪl oʊvər / (AmE), / prɪˈveɪl əʊvə / (BrE)
to prove more powerful or influential than someone or something else; to triumph against opposition
be more powerful or influential than

Examples

Justice will eventually prevail over corruption in the long run.
Despite difficult odds, her determination prevailed over adversity.
It is important that rational thinking prevails over impulsive reactions.

Synset Examples

In this race of truth, honesty prevails over deceit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triumph over - to defeat or overcome successfully
  • Antonym: to succumb to - to give in to a stronger force or influence
