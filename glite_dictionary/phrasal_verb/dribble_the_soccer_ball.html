to dribble the soccer ball

1. VERB

/ ˈdrɪbəl ðə ˈsɑkər bɔl / (AmE), / ˈdrɪbəl ðə ˈsɒkə bɔːl / (BrE)
to control and move a soccer ball along the ground using repeated light touches with the feet
move soccer ball with feet

Examples

He expertly dribbled the soccer ball past two defenders before taking a shot on goal.
It's important to keep your head up while you dribble the soccer ball down the field.
During practice, she worked on learning how to quickly dribble the soccer ball under pressure.

Synset Examples

The kids love to dribble the soccer ball around the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dribble - to move a ball along by repeated small kicks or bounces
