to go for a promotion

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ fər ə prəˈmoʊʃən / (AmE), / ɡəʊ fə ə prəˈməʊʃən / (BrE)
to actively seek or attempt to obtain a higher position at work or in an organization
seek a higher job position

Examples

After five years in the same role, she decided to go for a promotion.
If you believe you are ready, you should definitely go for a promotion.
He was nervous about whether to go for a promotion within his department.

Synset Examples

He plans to go for a promotion this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apply for a promotion - to formally request consideration for a higher position at work
