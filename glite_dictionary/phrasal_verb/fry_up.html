to fry up

1. VERB

/ fraɪ ʌp / (AmE), / fraɪ ʌp / (BrE)
to cook something by frying it in hot oil or fat
cook food by frying

Examples

I'll fry up some bacon and eggs for breakfast.
She quickly fried up the vegetables for a late-night snack.
Could you fry up the potatoes while I prepare the salad?

Synset Examples

I'll fry up some eggs for breakfast.
She decided to fry up some fish for dinner.
Can you fry up the chicken while I set the table?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fry - to cook food in hot oil or fat
