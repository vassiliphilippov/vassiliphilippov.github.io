to recommend that

1. VERB

/ ˌrɛk.əˈmɛnd ðæt / (AmE), / ˌrɛk.əˈmɛnd ðat / (BrE)
to suggest that someone do something; to advise a particular course of action
suggest or advise an action

Examples

I always recommend that you check the weather before planning a hike.
The teacher recommended that we read the entire book over the weekend.
Doctors often recommend that patients exercise regularly for better health.

Synset Examples

I would recommend that restaurant; it's fantastic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to advise - to recommend a course of action to someone
