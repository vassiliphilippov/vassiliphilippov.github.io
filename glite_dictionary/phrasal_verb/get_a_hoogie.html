to get a hoogie

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ə ˈhuːɡi / (AmE), / ɡɛt ə ˈhuːɡi / (BrE)
to buy or receive a hoogie (a type of sandwich), especially in a casual or regional context
buy or receive a hoogie sandwich
informal

Examples

We decided to get a hoogie for lunch because the local deli is famous for them.
Every time I visit this neighborhood, I make sure to get a hoogie from my favorite shop.
She's been craving something salty, so she wants to get a hoogie after work.

Synset Examples

She always gets a hoogie when she visits the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoagie - a large sandwich made on a long roll or baguette

Etymology

Variant of 'hoagie,' likely from Philadelphia English, denoting a submarine sandwich.
