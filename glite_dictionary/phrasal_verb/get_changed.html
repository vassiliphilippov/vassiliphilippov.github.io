to get changed

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈtʃeɪndʒd / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈtʃeɪndʒd / (BrE)
to change one's clothes, especially to put on different clothes for a particular purpose
change clothes

Examples

After work, I like to get changed into something more comfortable.
You should get changed before dinner since your clothes are dirty.
They all got changed quickly after swimming.

Synset Examples

I need to get changed before the party.
He'll get changed once he gets home.
Please get changed for the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: change clothes - to remove one set of clothing and put on another
