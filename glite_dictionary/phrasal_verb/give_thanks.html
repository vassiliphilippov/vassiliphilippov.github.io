to give thanks

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv θæŋks / (AmE), / ɡɪv θæŋks / (BrE)
to express gratitude or show appreciation, especially in words or prayer
express gratitude; show appreciation

Examples

Every year, families across the country give thanks during Thanksgiving dinner.
She paused to give thanks for all the support she had received.
Before the meal, everyone joined hands to give thanks for their good fortune.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to thank - to express gratitude to someone
  • Synonym: show appreciation - demonstrate gratitude or recognition
  • Antonym: to complain - to express dissatisfaction or annoyance
