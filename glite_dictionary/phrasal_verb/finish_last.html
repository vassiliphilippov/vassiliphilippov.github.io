to finish last

1. VERB

/ ˈfɪnɪʃ læst / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃ lɑːst / (BrE)
to come in last place or to be the least successful, especially in a competition or comparative situation
come in last place; be least successful

Examples

Even though he tried his best, Tom still finished last in the race.
You don't always have to finish last just because you play fair.
Our team finished last in the tournament this year, but we'll train harder next time.

Synset Examples

In the dating world, nice guys often finish last.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to lose - to be unsuccessful in a contest or competition
  • Antonym: win - to be successful in a contest or competition
