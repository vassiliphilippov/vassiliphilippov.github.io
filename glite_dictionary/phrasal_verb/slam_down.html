to slam down

1. VERB

/ slæm daʊn / (AmE), / slæm daʊn / (BrE)
To put something down forcefully and noisily; to consume food or drink quickly and energetically.
put down forcefully; drink/eat quickly

Examples

She was so angry that she slammed down her phone and stormed out of the room.
He slammed down a glass of water before heading back into the sun.
Please don't slam down your laptop on the desk; it might break.

Synset Examples

She slammed down her drink after hearing the news.
Don't slam down your things on the table!
He slammed down the book in frustration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slam - to shut or hit something with a lot of force, making a loud noise
