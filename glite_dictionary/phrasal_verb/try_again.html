to try again

1. VERB

/ traɪ əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / traɪ əˈɡɛn / (BrE)
to attempt to do something once more after a previous attempt has failed or not succeeded
make another attempt

Examples

If you don’t succeed on your first attempt, just try again.
Despite the setback, the researchers decided to try again with a different approach.
She failed to connect the call, but after a pause, she resolved to try again.

Synset Examples

Oh well, I guess we’ll try again tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to retry - to attempt something again
