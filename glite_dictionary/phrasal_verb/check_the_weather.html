to check the weather

1. VERB

/ tʃɛk ðə ˈwɛðər / (AmE), / tʃɛk ðə ˈwɛðə / (BrE)
to find out the current or forecast weather conditions, usually by consulting a source such as a report, website, or app
verify weather conditions

Examples

Before going on our hike, I always check the weather to avoid surprises.
She forgot to check the weather and got caught in a heavy rainstorm.
You can easily check the weather using your phone or a computer.

Synset Examples

It was wise of her to check the weather before leaving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: look up the weather - to search for information about the weather
