to blacken out

1. VERB

/ ˈblæk.ən aʊt / (AmE), / ˈblæk.ən aʊt / (BrE)
to make something completely dark so that no light is visible
make completely dark

Examples

A sudden power failure blackened out the entire building.
They used heavy curtains to blacken out the windows during the storm.
The old film reel blackened out just before the final scene.

Synset Examples

The dining room blackened out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to darken - make or become dark or darker
