to bump up the volume

1. VERB

/ bʌmp ʌp ðə ˈvɑljum / (AmE), / bʌmp ʌp ðə ˈvɒljuːm / (BrE)
to increase the loudness of sound, especially by adjusting a volume control
increase the loudness of sound
informal

Examples

I had to bump up the volume to hear the lyrics clearly.
He signaled to the DJ to bump up the volume for the next song.
During the party, someone asked to bump up the volume because the music was too soft.

Synset Examples

Let's bump up the volume on the speakers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to turn up the volume - increase the loudness of audio equipment
