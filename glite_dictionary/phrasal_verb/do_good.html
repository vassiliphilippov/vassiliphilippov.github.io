to do good

1. VERB

/ du ˈɡʊd / (AmE), / duː ˈɡʊd / (BrE)
to be beneficial to someone; to have a positive effect on a person's health or well-being
be beneficial for someone

Examples

A brisk walk in the morning will do good for your health.
Sometimes a change of scenery can really do good for your mind.
Getting some rest will do good after such a long journey.

Synset Examples

This will do you good

Related Terms

  • Synonym: benefit - to be of help or advantage
  • Antonym: harm - to be detrimental; to damage someone
