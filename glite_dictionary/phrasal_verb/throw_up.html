to throw up

1. VERB

/ θroʊ ʌp / (AmE), / θrəʊ ʌp / (BrE)
to eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth; to vomit
to vomit; eject stomach contents
informal

Examples

He felt sick and had to throw up after eating the spoiled food.
The child threw up on the bus ride to school.
She was so nervous before the performance that she almost threw up.

Synset Examples

After drinking too much, the students vomited
He purged continuously
The patient regurgitated the food we gave him last night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vomit - to eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth
  • Synonym: to regurgitate - to bring swallowed food back up into the mouth
