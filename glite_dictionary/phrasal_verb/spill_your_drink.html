to spill your drink

1. VERB

/ spɪl jər drɪŋk / (AmE), / spɪl jɔː drɪŋk / (BrE)
to accidentally cause the liquid in your drink to flow out, usually by knocking over the container
accidentally cause a drink to flow out

Examples

Be careful not to spill your drink while walking across the room.
If you spill your drink, you should clean it up right away.
She laughed so hard she almost spilled her drink.

Synset Examples

Don't spill your drink on the new carpet!
Watch it, or you'll spill your drink!
That dorkwad just spilled his drink everywhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spill - to cause liquid to flow out accidentally
