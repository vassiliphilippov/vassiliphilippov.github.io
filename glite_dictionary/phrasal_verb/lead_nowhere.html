to lead nowhere

1. VERB

/ lid ˈnoʊwɛr / (AmE), / liːd ˈnəʊweə / (BrE)
to have no result or not lead to any useful outcome or progress
result in no progress or outcome

Examples

After weeks of searching, our investigation led nowhere and we found no new clues.
All the plans he made led nowhere, leaving him feeling discouraged.
They argued for hours, but the discussion led nowhere and nothing was resolved.

Synset Examples

The negotiations went on for months but led nowhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to go nowhere - to make no progress or produce no result
