to get over

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to recover from an illness or medical condition; to return to a normal state of health
recover from illness

Examples

It took her several weeks to get over the flu completely.
I hope you get over your cold soon.
After the surgery, he managed to get over the infection quickly.

Synset Examples

He got well fast

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to recover - to become healthy again after being ill

/ ɡɛt ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
