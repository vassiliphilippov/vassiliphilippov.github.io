to fuck off

1. VERB

/ fʌk ɑf / (AmE), / fʌk ɒf / (BrE)
(vulgar slang) Used to tell someone to go away immediately, often angrily or rudely.
rudely tell someone to leave
vulgar slang

Examples

If you don't like it here, you can just fuck off.
She got angry and shouted at him to fuck off.
