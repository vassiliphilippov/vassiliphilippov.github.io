to check temperature

1. VERB

/ tʃɛk ˈtɛmpɚˌtʃɚ / (AmE), / tʃɛk ˈtɛmpərətʃə / (BrE)
to measure someone's body temperature to determine if they are ill or have a fever
measure someone's body temperature

Examples

The nurse came in to check temperature every hour during the night.
Parents should check temperature if their child feels unwell or has chills.
You can check temperature quickly with a digital thermometer.

Synset Examples

She checked his temperature for any signs of fever.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: take (someone's) temperature - to measure someone's body temperature
