to aim at

1. VERB

/ eɪm æt / (AmE), / eɪm æt / (BrE)
to intend to achieve something or focus effort on achieving a particular goal
intend or focus effort on achieving

Examples

The company is aiming at becoming the market leader within five years.
This training program aims at improving employees' communication skills.
We aimed at reducing costs without sacrificing quality.

Synset Examples

We aim at completing the project by next month.
They aim at improving community relations this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strive for - to make determined efforts to achieve something
  • Antonym: to ignore - to deliberately pay no attention to or avoid something (contrasting goal-directed action)
