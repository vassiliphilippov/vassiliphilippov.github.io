to play pranks

1. VERB

/ pleɪ præŋks / (AmE), / pleɪ præŋks / (BrE)
to carry out tricks or practical jokes intended to surprise or amuse others
carry out tricks or jokes on others

Examples

The students decided to play pranks on their teachers during the last week of school.
He often plays pranks on his little sister, like hiding her toys.
Some people find it annoying when coworkers play pranks in the office.

Synset Examples

He loves to play pranks; he's such a finook.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to play tricks - to do tricks or acts of deception for fun
  • Synonym: prank - a mischievous act or joke
