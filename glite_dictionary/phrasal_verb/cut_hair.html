to cut hair

1. VERB

/ kʌt hɛr / (AmE), / kʌt heə / (BrE)
to shorten the length of someone's hair by using scissors or another cutting instrument
shorten the length of hair

Examples

She decided to cut hair short for the summer.
He learned how to cut hair at a cosmetology school.
Many people prefer to cut hair at home instead of going to a salon.

Synset Examples

She decided to cut her hair short for summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trim - to make something neater by cutting small amounts, especially hair
