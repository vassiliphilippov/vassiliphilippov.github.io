to zap the music

1. VERB

/ zæp ðə ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / zæp ðə ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
to turn off or lower the volume of music, especially quickly or with a remote control
quickly turn off or lower music
informal

Examples

Can you zap the music? I'm trying to concentrate on my work.
She quickly zapped the music when the phone started ringing.
Before heading to bed, he always zaps the music so the house is quiet.

Synset Examples

He decided to zap the music while studying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mute - to turn off the sound on a device
  • Synonym: turn down the music - to lower the volume of music
  • Antonym: turn up the music - to raise the volume of music

Etymology

From 'zap' (originally meaning to strike or destroy suddenly, popularized in the context of operating remote controls in the 1980s).
