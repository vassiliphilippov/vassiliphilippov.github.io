to let deadlines slide

1. VERB

/ lɛt ˈdɛdˌlaɪnz slaɪd / (AmE), / lɛt ˈdɛdˌlaɪnz slaɪd / (BrE)
to fail to meet deadlines or intentionally ignore them
fail to meet deadlines

Examples

It's important not to let deadlines slide if you want to keep your clients happy.
Sometimes employees let deadlines slide when there are too many projects at once.
He promised not to let deadlines slide during the next quarter.

Synset Examples

He tends to let deadlines slide when he's busy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to miss a deadline - to not finish something by the required time
