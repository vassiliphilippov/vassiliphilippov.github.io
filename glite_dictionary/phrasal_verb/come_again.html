to come again

1. VERB

/ kʌm əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / kʌm əˈɡɛn / (BrE)
to return to a place or situation, often after a short time away
return to a place or situation

Examples

If you come again next week, we'll have the information ready for you.
She promised to come again after her vacation to help finish the project.
Customers are encouraged to come again if they need further assistance.

Synset Examples

Could you come again tomorrow for the details?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: return - to go or come back to a place
