to drop the course

1. VERB

/ drɑp ðə kɔrs / (AmE), / drɒp ðə kɔːs / (BrE)
to officially withdraw from or stop attending a class before completing it, especially in an educational institution
withdraw from a class

Examples

After realizing the workload was too much, she decided to drop the course.
You must fill out a form if you want to drop the course before the deadline.
He dropped the course halfway through the semester to focus on his job.

Synset Examples

He dropped the weeder course after the first week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to withdraw - to remove yourself from participation in a course or activity
