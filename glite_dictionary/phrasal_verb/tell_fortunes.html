to tell fortunes

1. VERB

/ tɛl ˈfɔrtʃənz / (AmE), / tɛl ˈfɔːtʃənz / (BrE)
to claim to predict the future, especially for others, often using cards, palms, or similar methods
predict the future for others

Examples

My grandmother used to tell fortunes with tea leaves for her friends.
Many people visit the fair to have someone tell fortunes for them.
He claimed he could tell fortunes just by looking at your palm.

Synset Examples

She loves to tell fortunes at the local fair.
She learned how to tell fortunes using tarot cards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to foretell - to predict a future event
  • Synonym: divine - to discover or predict by supernatural means
