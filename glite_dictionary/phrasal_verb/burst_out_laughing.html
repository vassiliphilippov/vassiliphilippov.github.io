to burst out laughing

1. VERB

/ bɝst aʊt ˈlæfɪŋ / (AmE), / bɜːst aʊt ˈlɑːfɪŋ / (BrE)
to suddenly start laughing loudly and uncontrollably
suddenly start laughing loudly

Examples

Everyone burst out laughing when she told the joke about the cat and the toaster.
I was trying to be serious, but I burst out laughing at his impression of the boss.
The comedian's final punchline made the whole audience burst out laughing.

Synset Examples

She couldn't help but burst out laughing at his silly dance.
When he tripped over the rug, we all burst out laughing.
The joke was so funny that everyone burst out laughing.
His joke was so funny, everyone burst out laughing.
His goofy ahh dance made everyone burst out laughing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to crack up - to suddenly start laughing very hard
  • Antonym: to burst into tears - to suddenly start crying loudly
