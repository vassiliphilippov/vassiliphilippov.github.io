to talk to oneself

1. VERB

/ tɑk tə wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / tɔːk tʊ wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to speak aloud when alone or not addressing others, often as a way of processing thoughts or feelings
speak aloud when alone

Examples

She often talks to herself while working through a complex problem.
Many people talk to themselves to organize their thoughts before a big presentation.
After hearing the strange noise, he found himself talking to himself as he searched the house.

Synset Examples

It's normal to talk to oneself when deep in thought.
He realized he was talking to himself during the long drive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mutter - to speak in a low or barely audible voice, especially in dissatisfaction or irritation
  • Synonym: to think aloud - to vocalize one's thoughts while thinking
