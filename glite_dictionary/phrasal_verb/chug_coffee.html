to chug coffee

1. VERB

/ tʃʌɡ ˈkɔfi / (AmE), / tʃʌɡ ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
to drink coffee quickly or in large gulps, often in a hurry or eagerly
drink coffee quickly or greedily
informal

Examples

He likes to chug coffee before the meeting to wake himself up.
Some students chug coffee late at night to stay awake for exams.
I watched her chug coffee as if her life depended on it.

Synset Examples

He likes to chug his coffee before the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gulp - to swallow quickly or in large mouthfuls
  • Antonym: sip - to drink in small quantities

Etymology

From 'chug' (imitative verb for drinking quickly), applied to 'coffee'.
