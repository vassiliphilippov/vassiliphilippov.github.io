to ponder over

1. VERB

/ ˈpɑndər ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈpɒndə ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to think about something carefully and for a long time
think carefully about

Examples

He would often ponder over his decisions late into the night.
Many philosophers ponder over questions of existence and purpose.
She sat by the window, quietly pondering over the letter she had just received.

Synset Examples

She loves to ponder over a good riddle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to contemplate - think deeply or consider carefully
  • Synonym: to reflect on - think about deeply or carefully
