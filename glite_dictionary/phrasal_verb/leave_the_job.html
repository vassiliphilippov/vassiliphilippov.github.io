to leave the job

1. VERB

/ liv ðə dʒɑb / (AmE), / liːv ðə dʒɒb / (BrE)
to resign or quit one's employment; to stop working for an employer, usually by choice
to quit or resign from employment

Examples

After five years at the company, he decided to leave the job and travel the world.
She plans to leave the job once she finds a new opportunity.
When you leave the job, make sure to give proper notice to your employer.

Synset Examples

She managed to leave the job without tears.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to resign - to formally leave a job or position
  • Synonym: to quit - to leave a job or stop doing something
