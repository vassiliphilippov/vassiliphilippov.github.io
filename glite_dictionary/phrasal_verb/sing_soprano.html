to sing soprano

1. VERB

/ sɪŋ səˈproʊnoʊ / (AmE), / sɪŋ səˈprəʊnəʊ / (BrE)
to perform the soprano vocal part, singing in the highest voice range in a choir or musical ensemble
perform in the highest singing voice

Examples

Every year, she volunteers to sing soprano in the holiday concert.
Although he usually sings tenor, he can also sing soprano when needed.
They needed someone to sing soprano for the new choral arrangement.

Synset Examples

She was asked to sing soprano in the choir.
To impress the judges, she will sing soprano.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sing alto - to perform in the alto vocal range, just below soprano
