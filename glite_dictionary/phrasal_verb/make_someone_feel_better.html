to make (someone) feel better

1. VERB

/ meɪk ˈsʌmwʌn fiːl ˈbɛtər / (AmE), / meɪk ˈsʌmwən fiːl ˈbɛtə / (BrE)
to improve someone's mood or help someone recover emotionally or physically
to improve someone's mood or well-being

Examples

A kind word from a friend can make me feel better when I'm having a bad day.
Listening to music always helps to make me feel better after a tough workout.
Her encouragement really made me feel better about my chances in the competition.

Synset Examples

Ma always knows how to make me feel better.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cheer up - to make someone feel happier
  • Synonym: comfort - to ease someone's distress or sadness
