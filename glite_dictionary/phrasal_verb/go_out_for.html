to go out for

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ aʊt fər / (AmE), / ɡəʊ aʊt fə / (BrE)
to leave one's home to take part in a social activity, such as eating at a restaurant, watching a film, etc.
leave home for social activity

Examples

We often go out for dinner on Fridays to try new restaurants.
Are you planning to go out for a walk after work today?
They decided to go out for ice cream to celebrate their anniversary.

Synset Examples

We decided to go out for a movie this weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to go out - to leave one's home for social or recreational purposes
