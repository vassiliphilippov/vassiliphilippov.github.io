to stop by

1. VERB

/ stɑp baɪ / (AmE), / stɒp baɪ / (BrE)
to make a short visit to a place or person, usually briefly or casually
make a brief visit

Examples

Would you like to stop by my office after lunch for a quick chat?
She decided to stop by her friend's house on the way home from work.
If you have time, please stop by the store and pick up some bread.

Synset Examples

Let's stop by Wawa for coffee before the trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to drop in - to visit someone informally and without arrangement
  • Synonym: to call in - to pay a short visit, usually unexpectedly
