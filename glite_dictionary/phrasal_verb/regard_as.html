to regard as

1. VERB

/ rɪˈɡɑrd æz / (AmE), / rɪˈɡɑːd æz / (BrE)
to consider or judge someone or something to be a particular thing
to consider to be

Examples

Many people regard as the Mona Lisa the most famous painting in the world.
She was widely regarded as the best player on the team.
Some scientists regard as climate change the biggest challenge of our time.

Synset Examples

she looked on this affair as a joke
He thinks of himself as a brilliant musician
He is reputed to be intelligent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to consider - to think about something or someone as having a particular quality
  • Synonym: to deem - to judge or consider in a particular way
