to earn a living

1. VERB

/ ɝn ə ˈlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ɜːn ə ˈlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
to make money in order to support oneself financially, usually through work or employment
make money to support oneself

Examples

She works two jobs to earn a living and support her family.
After losing his job, it became difficult for him to earn a living in the city.
Many artists struggle to earn a living solely from their creative work.

Synset Examples

He earns a living through manual labor every day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make a living - to earn money to support oneself; synonym for earn a living
