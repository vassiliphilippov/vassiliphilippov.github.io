to chop the wood

1. VERB

/ tʃɑp ðə wʊd / (AmE), / tʃɒp ðə wʊd / (BrE)
to cut wood into pieces, typically for use as firewood
cut wood into pieces for burning

Examples

Every morning, he would chop the wood to keep the fireplace burning through winter.
She asked her brother to chop the wood before it started raining.
They took turns chopping the wood to make enough kindling for the night.

Synset Examples

He will chop the wood for the fire tonight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: split wood - to separate wood into parts, especially for firewood
