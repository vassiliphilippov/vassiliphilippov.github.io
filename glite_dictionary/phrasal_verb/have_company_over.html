to have company over

1. VERB

/ hæv ˈkʌmpəni ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / hæv ˈkʌmpəni ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to host guests at one's home, especially for a social visit or gathering
host guests at home

Examples

We can't leave right now because we have company over for dinner.
She loves to have company over on weekends and cook for everyone.
Whenever they have company over, they make sure the house is spotless.

Synset Examples

I can't talk now, we have company over.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invite - ask someone to come to your home or event
  • Synonym: to entertain - receive guests and provide hospitality
