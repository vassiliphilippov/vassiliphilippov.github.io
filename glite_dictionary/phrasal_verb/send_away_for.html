to send away for

1. VERB

/ sɛnd əˈweɪ fɔr / (AmE), / sɛnd əˈweɪ fə / (BrE)
to request something to be sent to you by mail or online, usually by filling out a form or making a formal request
request by mail or online

Examples

I decided to send away for a catalog from my favorite store.
You can send away for a free sample on their website.
She sent away for her birth certificate last week.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: order - to request something to be supplied, especially goods
  • Synonym: send off for - to request something by mail or online (alternative phrasing)
