to pour down

1. VERB

/ pɔr daʊn / (AmE), / pɔː daʊn / (BrE)
to drink a liquid in its entirety, especially quickly or in one go
drink something quickly and completely
informal

Examples

He bragged that he could pour down an entire pint in seconds.
She poured down her coffee before rushing out the door.
After the long hike, they poured down bottles of water to rehydrate.

Synset Examples

He downed three martinis before dinner
She killed a bottle of brandy that night
They popped a few beers after work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: down - to drink something quickly and completely
  • Synonym: knock back - to quickly drink a beverage, especially alcohol
