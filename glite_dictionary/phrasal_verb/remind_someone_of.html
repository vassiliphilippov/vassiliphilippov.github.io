to remind someone of

1. VERB

/ rɪˈmaɪnd ˈsʌmwʌn ʌv / (AmE), / rɪˈmaɪnd ˈsʌmwən ɒv / (BrE)
to make someone think of or remember something or someone from the past
cause to remember (something/someone)

Examples

That painting really reminds me of my grandmother's house.
Her accent reminded him of his childhood in Ireland.
The song reminds people of summer road trips.

Synset Examples

The unique sound on the goose reminded me of childhood.
She wore a lovely scent that reminded me of spring.
That song always reminds me of Parklife.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to evoke - to bring a feeling, memory, or picture into the mind
