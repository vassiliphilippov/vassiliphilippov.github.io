to diagnosed with

1. VERB

/ ˈdaɪəɡnoʊzd wɪð / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəɡnəʊzd wɪð / (BrE)
identified as having a particular disease or medical condition through examination by a healthcare professional.
identified as having a disease or condition

Examples

After several tests, he was finally diagnosed with diabetes.
She was diagnosed with cancer at the age of thirty-five.
Many people are diagnosed with allergies in the spring.

Synset Examples

The doctor diagnosed him with jaundice after the tests.
The doctor diagnosed her with a rare disease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to diagnose - to identify a disease or problem by examination
