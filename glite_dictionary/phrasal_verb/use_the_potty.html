to use the potty

1. VERB

/ jus ðə ˈpɑti / (AmE), / juːz ðə ˈpɒti / (BrE)
(of a child) to defecate or urinate in a potty or use the bathroom, especially when learning toilet habits.
go to the bathroom (as a child)

Examples

Many parents celebrate when their child finally learns to use the potty.
If you need to use the potty, just let me know and I'll help you.
She asked her son if he needed to use the potty before leaving the house.

Synset Examples

The toddler proudly announced he needed to use the potty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to go to the bathroom - to urinate or defecate; to use the toilet
