to dump faulty product

1. VERB

/ dʌmp ˈfɔlti ˈprɑdʌkt / (AmE), / dʌmp ˈfɔːlti ˈprɒdʌkt / (BrE)
to discard or dispose of defective or unsatisfactory goods, especially in manufacturing or business contexts
discard defective goods

Examples

After discovering the contamination, the factory had to dump faulty product to ensure customer safety.
Companies are legally required to dump faulty product rather than sell it to consumers.
Whenever a batch fails quality checks, we must dump faulty product immediately.

Synset Examples

The company had to dump the faulty products immediately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to dispose of - to get rid of something unwanted
