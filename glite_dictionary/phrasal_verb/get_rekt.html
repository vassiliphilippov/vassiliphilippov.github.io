to get rekt

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt rɛkt / (AmE), / ɡɛt rɛkt / (BrE)
(informal, slang) To be defeated, ruined, or humiliated, often used in gaming or online contexts.
be defeated or humiliated (slang)
informal slang

Examples

If you don't watch out for snipers, you'll get rekt in this section of the game.
After talking trash all week, their team completely got rekt in the finals.
She was overconfident, but she got rekt in the first round.

Synset Examples

You can’t just play recklessly or you’ll get rekt.
He got rekt in the gaming tournament last night.
The team got rekt by their rivals in the finals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get owned - to be defeated or dominated (slang)
  • Antonym: triumph - achieve a great victory or success

Etymology

Slang alteration of "get wrecked"; "rekt" is a stylized spelling popularized in internet and gaming culture.
