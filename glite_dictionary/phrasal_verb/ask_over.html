to ask over

1. VERB

/ æsk ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ɑːsk ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to invite (someone) to come to your home, especially for a social occasion
invite someone to your home

Examples

We decided to ask over our neighbors for drinks on Friday night.
She loves to ask over her friends whenever she tries a new recipe.
Have you ever been asked over to their house for dinner?

Synset Examples

Can I invite you for dinner on Sunday night?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invite - to ask someone to attend or come to an event or place
