to curtain off

1. VERB

/ ˈkɝːtn̩ ɔf / (AmE), / ˈkɜːtn̩ ɒf / (BrE)
to separate or divide an area by using a curtain
separate using a curtain

Examples

We decided to curtain off part of the living room to create a private workspace.
The hospital staff will curtain off each bed to give patients some privacy.
They curtained off the stage before the performance began.
