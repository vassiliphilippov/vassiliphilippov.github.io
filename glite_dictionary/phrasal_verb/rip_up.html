to rip up

1. VERB

/ rɪp ʌp / (AmE), / rɪp ʌp / (BrE)
to tear something into small pieces or shreds
tear into small pieces

Examples

The child ripped up the letter and threw it in the trash.
She angrily ripped up the contract after reading its terms.
Before moving, he ripped up all his old photographs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to tear up - to tear into pieces
