to settle on

1. VERB

/ ˈsɛt̬l ɑn / (AmE), / ˈsɛt.əl ɒn / (BrE)
(of eyes, attention, etc.) to become fixed on something; to focus or concentrate on a particular thing or point.
focus or become fixed on something

Examples

Her eyes settled on the painting in the corner, and she stared at it in silence.
After scanning the menu, his gaze finally settled on the chocolate cake.
Once their attention settled on the speaker, the room became quiet.

Synset Examples

Her eyes fixated on a point on the horizon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to focus on - to direct attention towards something
