to cheat on

1. VERB

/ tʃit ɑn / (AmE), / tʃiːt ɒn / (BrE)
to be sexually unfaithful to one's partner in a romantic relationship or marriage
be sexually unfaithful to a partner

Examples

She was devastated when she discovered he had cheated on her.
Rumors spread that he was cheating on his long-term partner, though he denied it.
If you cheat on me, our trust will be broken forever.

Synset Examples

She cheats on her husband
Might her husband be wandering?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: be faithful to - remain loyal to one's romantic partner
