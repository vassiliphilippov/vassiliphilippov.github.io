divided up

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈvaɪdɪd ʌp / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪdɪd ʌp / (BrE)
distributed in portions, especially according to a plan or purpose; shared out among people
distributed in portions

Examples

The profits were divided up equally among the partners.
After dinner, the chores were divided up so everyone helped clean up.
Once the property was divided up, each sibling received their share.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shared out - distributed among several people
  • Synonym: allocated - assigned or distributed for a particular purpose
