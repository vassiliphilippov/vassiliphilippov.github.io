to fix the gap

1. VERB

/ fɪks ðə ɡæp / (AmE), / fɪks ðə ɡap / (BrE)
to repair or close an opening or space between two things
repair or close an opening

Examples

The carpenter used some wood filler to fix the gap between the floorboards.
We need to fix the gap around the frame so that cold air doesn't come in.
She called a handyman to fix the gap under her front door.

Synset Examples

They installed a shim to fix the gap under the window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seal - to close an opening or hole so that nothing can get through
  • Synonym: close - to move something so that an opening or gap is covered or blocked
