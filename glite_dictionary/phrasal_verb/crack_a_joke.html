to crack a joke

1. VERB

/ kræk ə dʒoʊk / (AmE), / kræk ə dʒəʊk / (BrE)
to tell a joke or make a humorous remark, usually to make people laugh
tell a joke or humorous remark

Examples

Whenever he feels nervous, he starts cracking jokes to lighten the mood.
She cracked a joke at the meeting, making everyone laugh.
They were cracking jokes all night at the party.

Synset Examples

He's such a funny boy, always cracking jokes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tell a joke - to say something funny to make people laugh
  • Synonym: wisecrack - a witty or sarcastic remark
