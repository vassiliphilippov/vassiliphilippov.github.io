to manage craving

1. VERB

/ ˈmænɪdʒ ˈkreɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒ ˈkreɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
to control or reduce strong desires for something, especially something unhealthy or addictive
control or reduce strong desires

Examples

Many people find it difficult to manage cravings when quitting smoking.
Therapists teach their clients how to manage cravings for unhealthy foods.
With the right strategies, you can manage cravings and make healthier choices.

Synset Examples

Nicotine patches helped her manage cravings effectively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resist temptation - to avoid giving in to a strong desire for something
