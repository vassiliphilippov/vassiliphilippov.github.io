to cry for

1. VERB

/ kraɪ fɔr / (AmE), / kraɪ fə / (BrE)
to need or require something very urgently; to be in great need of something
need or require urgently

Examples

The neglected garden clearly cries for some attention and care.
With sales falling, the company cries for new leadership and fresh ideas.
This complex problem cries for a thoughtful and urgent solution.

Synset Examples

This question cries out for an answer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demand - ask for something forcefully or urgently
  • Synonym: to require - to need something
