to catch a date

1. VERB

/ kætʃ ə deɪt / (AmE), / katʃ ə deɪt / (BrE)
to successfully arrange a romantic meeting or appointment with someone
arrange a romantic meeting

Examples

After weeks of trying, he finally managed to catch a date with his crush.
It's often hard to catch a date during busy exam periods.
She was thrilled when she managed to catch a date for Valentine's Day.

Synset Examples

She was excited to catch a date with someone new.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get a date - to succeed in securing a romantic appointment
  • Synonym: to ask out - to invite someone on a date
