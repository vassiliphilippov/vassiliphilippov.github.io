to brace up

1. VERB

/ breɪs ʌp / (AmE), / breɪs ʌp / (BrE)
to make (something) secure or supported from underneath, often by adding a structural support
make secure from underneath

Examples

They braced up the collapsing floor with extra beams.
The crew had to brace up the old bridge supports before starting repairs.
Engineers braced up the foundation to prevent the entire structure from sinking.

Synset Examples

undergird the ship

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to undergird - to support or strengthen from below
