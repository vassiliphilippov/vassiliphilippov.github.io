to brag excessively

1. VERB

/ bræɡ ɪkˈsɛsɪvli / (AmE), / bræɡ ɪkˈsɛsɪvli / (BrE)
to boast or talk with too much pride about oneself or one's achievements in an exaggerated way
boast in an exaggerated manner

Examples

He tended to brag excessively about his minor accomplishments at every opportunity.
People started to avoid her because she would brag excessively during conversations.
You don't need to brag excessively to prove your talent; let your work speak for itself.

Synset Examples

She called him an egofag for bragging excessively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boast - to talk with excessive pride and self-satisfaction
  • Antonym: to downplay - to make something appear less important or significant
