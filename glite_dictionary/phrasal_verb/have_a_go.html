to have a go

1. VERB

/ hæv ə ɡoʊ / (AmE), / hæv ə ɡəʊ / (BrE)
to make an attempt at something, especially something new or unfamiliar
attempt something; try
informal

Examples

Why don't you have a go at painting? You might be better than you think.
I've never tried skiing before, but I'm willing to have a go.
She was nervous about presenting, but decided to have a go anyway.

Synset Examples

I never sat on a horse before but I'll give it a go

Related Terms

  • Synonym: try - to make an attempt at doing something
  • Synonym: give it a go - to try something, especially for the first time
