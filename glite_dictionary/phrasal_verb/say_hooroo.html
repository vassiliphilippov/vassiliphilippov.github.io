to say hooroo

1. VERB

/ seɪ hʊˈru / (AmE), / seɪ hʊˈruː / (BrE)
to say goodbye or bid farewell, especially in Australian English
say goodbye (Australian)
informal british

Examples

It's almost midnight; I think it's time to say hooroo and head home.
She waved at the crowd and said hooroo after the party ended.
Before leaving for her trip, he stopped to say hooroo to his coworkers.

Synset Examples

It's time to say hooroo to our friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to say goodbye - to express farewell to someone

Etymology

From Australian English 'hooroo' (goodbye), origin uncertain, possibly from 'hooray'.
