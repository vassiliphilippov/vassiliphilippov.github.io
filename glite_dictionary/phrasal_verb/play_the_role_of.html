to play the role of

1. VERB

/ pleɪ ðə roʊl ʌv / (AmE), / pleɪ ðə rəʊl ɒv / (BrE)
to perform or assume the part of a particular character or function, especially in a play, film, or other performance
perform or assume a specific character

Examples

In last night's production, she played the role of Juliet with remarkable emotion.
He is often asked to play the role of the villain in action movies.
During the discussion, she decided to play the role of mediator to help resolve the conflict.
