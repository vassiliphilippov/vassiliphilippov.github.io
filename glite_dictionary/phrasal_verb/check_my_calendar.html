to check my calendar

1. VERB

/ tʃɛk maɪ ˈkæləndɚ / (AmE), / tʃɛk maɪ ˈkæl.əndə / (BrE)
to look at one's personal schedule to see if one is available at a particular time
verify one's schedule or availability

Examples

I'll need to check my calendar before confirming the meeting time.
Can you wait a moment while I check my calendar to see if I'm free?
She quickly checked her calendar and realized she had a doctor's appointment that day.

Synset Examples

Let me check my calendar, just a second.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: check my diary - look at one's diary to verify schedule (mainly UK English)
