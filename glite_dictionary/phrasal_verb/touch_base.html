to touch base

1. VERB

/ tʌtʃ beɪs / (AmE), / tʌtʃ beɪs / (BrE)
to make contact or establish communication with someone, often briefly or for an update
make brief contact or communicate
informal

Examples

Let me touch base with you next week to see how the project is going.
She touched base with her manager before making the final decision.
If you have any questions, just touch base by email or phone.

Synset Examples

did you finally connect with your long-lost cousin?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contact - to communicate with someone
