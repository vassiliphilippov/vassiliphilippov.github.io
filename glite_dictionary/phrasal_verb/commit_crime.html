to commit crime

1. VERB

/ kəˈmɪt kraɪm / (AmE), / kəˈmɪt kraɪm / (BrE)
to do something illegal; to carry out a criminal act
do something illegal

Examples

Anyone who commits crime must face the consequences under the legal system.
He was caught trying to commit crime late at night.
Some people commit crime out of desperation, while others do so for profit.

Synset Examples

They committed numerous crimes before getting caught.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: break the law - to disobey or act against legal rules
  • Antonym: obey the law - to act according to the law
