to ring up

1. VERB

/ rɪŋ ʌp / (AmE), / rɪŋ ʌp / (BrE)
to record the sale of goods on a cash register or electronic till
record a sale on a cash register

Examples

The cashier will ring up your items while you wait at the counter.
She quickly rang up all the groceries before the store closed.
I watched the clerk ring up several customers in just a few minutes.

Synset Examples

Sally rang up Eve's purchase of tomatoes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to check out - to pay for goods at a store's register
