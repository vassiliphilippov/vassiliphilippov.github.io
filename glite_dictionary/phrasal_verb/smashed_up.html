to smashed up

1. VERB

/ ˈsmæʃt ʌp / (AmE), / ˈsmæʃt ʌp / (BrE)
damaged or broken into pieces, especially as a result of forceful impact
damaged or broken into pieces

Examples

The old car was completely smashed up after the accident.
He smashed up his favorite mug while washing the dishes.
The storm smashed up several boats along the shoreline.

Synset Examples

He accidentally smashed up his phone after dropping it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broken - damaged and no longer in one piece or in working order
  • Antonym: intact - remaining whole or undamaged
