to draw a bead on

1. VERB

/ drɔ ə bid ɑn / (AmE), / drɔː ə biːd ɒn / (BrE)
to focus attention on or take aim at a particular target, goal, or objective
to aim at a target or goal

Examples

The young entrepreneur has drawn a bead on expanding her company into new markets.
During the debate, the candidate drew a bead on his opponent's economic policies.
He drew a bead on a promotion and worked tirelessly to achieve it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to aim at - to direct intention or weapon at a target
  • Synonym: set one's sights on - to decide to achieve something as a goal

Etymology

Originally from the shooting practice of aligning the bead sight of a gun with a target.
