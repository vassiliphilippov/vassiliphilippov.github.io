to shapeshift into

1. VERB

/ ˈʃeɪpˌʃɪft ˈɪntu / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪpʃɪft ˈɪntə / (BrE)
to transform oneself or be transformed into another form or being, especially by supernatural means
transform into another form

Examples

In many myths, a powerful wizard can shapeshift into an animal at will.
The villain shapeshifted into a shadow to escape detection.
Stories often describe gods who shapeshift into humans to walk among mortals.

Synset Examples

Some legends say witches can shapeshift into birds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transform into - to change into a different form

Etymology

Formed from 'shape' + 'shift', meaning to change shape; popularized in fantasy literature and media.
