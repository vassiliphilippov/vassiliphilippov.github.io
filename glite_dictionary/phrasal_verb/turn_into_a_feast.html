to turn into a feast

1. VERB

/ tɝn ˈɪntu ə fiːst / (AmE), / tɜːn ˈɪntuː ə fiːst / (BrE)
to make something simple become elaborate, especially by preparing or presenting it in a lavish way
make something simple elaborate or lavish

Examples

She can turn into a feast even the most basic ingredients in her kitchen.
A picnic in the park was turned into a feast with all the homemade dishes.
He loves to turn into a feast whatever is left in the fridge.

Synset Examples

She could turn two simple ingredients into a feast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elaborate - develop or present (something) in detail
  • Antonym: to simplify - make (something) simpler or easier to do or understand
