to think clearly

1. VERB

/ θɪŋk ˈklɪrli / (AmE), / θɪŋk ˈklɪəli / (BrE)
to be able to consider matters in a logical and rational manner, without confusion or distraction
have clear, rational thoughts

Examples

After staying up all night, he found it difficult to think clearly during the meeting.
She needed a moment to calm down so she could think clearly about her options.
When you're stressed or anxious, it's hard to think clearly and make good decisions.

Synset Examples

Fear can constrict your ability to think clearly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: think rationally - to think in a logical and reasonable way
