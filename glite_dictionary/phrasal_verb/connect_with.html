to connect with

1. VERB

/ kəˈnɛkt wɪð / (AmE), / kəˈnɛkt wɪð / (BrE)
to establish a relationship or emotional bond with someone
form a relationship or bond

Examples

It took some time for the new teacher to connect with her students.
He found it easy to connect with people during his travels.
Volunteering helped her connect with others who shared her passion.

Synset Examples

She learned Standarin to better connect with her clients.
She learned Esperanto to connect with international friends.
She is learning Hebrew to connect with her heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to bond with - to form a close relationship with someone
  • Synonym: relate to - to understand or empathize with someone
