to make peace

1. VERB

/ meɪk pis / (AmE), / meɪk piːs / (BrE)
to end a conflict or disagreement and establish harmonious relations; to reconcile
end conflict; reconcile

Examples

After years of rivalry, the two countries decided to make peace and sign a treaty.
I finally managed to make peace with my brother after our long argument.
Community leaders helped the two families make peace following months of dispute.

Synset Examples

The brothers who had been fighting over their inheritance finally made peace

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to reconcile - restore friendly relations; bring back harmony
  • Antonym: quarrel - an angry argument or disagreement
