to obstruct someone's plans

1. VERB

/ əbˈstrʌkt sʌmˈwʌnz plænz / (AmE), / əbˈstrʌkt sʌmˈwʌnz plænz / (BrE)
to deliberately prevent someone from carrying out or achieving what they have planned
to prevent someone's plans from succeeding

Examples

He tried to obstruct their plans by spreading rumors about their project.
It is unethical to obstruct someone's plans simply out of jealousy.
The sudden storm obstructed our plans for a picnic in the park.

Synset Examples

He had to obstruct their plans before they could proceed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thwart - to prevent someone from accomplishing something
  • Synonym: to frustrate - to prevent a plan or effort from succeeding
  • Antonym: aid someone's plans - to help someone achieve what they have planned
