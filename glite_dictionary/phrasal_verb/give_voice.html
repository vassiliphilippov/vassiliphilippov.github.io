to give voice

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv vɔɪs / (AmE), / ɡɪv vɔɪs / (BrE)
to express or articulate a thought, feeling, or opinion in words
to express in words

Examples

She was finally able to give voice to her frustrations after the meeting.
The novel gives voice to those often ignored by history.
He struggled to give voice to his emotions during the difficult conversation.

Synset Examples

He formulated his concerns to the board of trustees

Related Terms

  • Synonym: express - to put (thought) into words; to convey
  • Synonym: articulate - to express (an idea or feeling) clearly in words
