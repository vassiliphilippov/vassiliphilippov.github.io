to veg on the couch

1. VERB

/ vɛdʒ ɑn ðə kaʊtʃ / (AmE), / vɛdʒ ɒn ðə kaʊtʃ / (BrE)
to relax and do nothing, especially by sitting or lying on a couch
relax and do nothing on a couch
informal

Examples

After work, I just want to veg on the couch and watch TV.
Some weekends, she prefers to veg on the couch instead of going out.
He spent the entire afternoon vegging on the couch with his favorite snacks.

Synset Examples

I prefer to veg on the couch after a long day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to vegetate - to live or spend a period of time in a dull, inactive, or passive way

Etymology

Informal use of 'veg' as a shortening of 'vegetate', meaning to be inactive.
