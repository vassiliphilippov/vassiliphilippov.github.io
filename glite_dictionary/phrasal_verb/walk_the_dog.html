to walk the dog

1. VERB

/ wɑk ðə dɔɡ / (AmE), / wɔːk ðə dɒɡ / (BrE)
to take a dog outside for exercise, typically on a leash
take a dog out for exercise

Examples

Could you walk the dog before you leave for work?
Every evening, she enjoys walking the dog around the neighborhood.
He walked the dog despite the pouring rain.

Synset Examples

After work, he always takes time to walk the dog.
It’s her turn to walk the dog this evening.
I need to walk the dog before dinner.

Related Terms
