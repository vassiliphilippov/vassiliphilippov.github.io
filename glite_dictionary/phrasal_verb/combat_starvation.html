to combat starvation

1. VERB

/ kɑmˌbæt stɑrˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.bæt stɑːˈveɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
to take action to prevent or reduce severe hunger or famine
take action against hunger

Examples

Many aid organizations are working to combat starvation in drought-affected regions.
The government launched new programs to help combat starvation during the crisis.
Community volunteers join forces to combat starvation by distributing food supplies.

Synset Examples

They work hard to combat starvation in their community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fight hunger - take action to reduce hunger
