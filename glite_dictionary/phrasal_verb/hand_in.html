to hand in

1. VERB

/ hænd ɪn / (AmE), / hænd ɪn / (BrE)
to submit or deliver something, especially a document or piece of work, to someone in authority
submit or deliver something

Examples

Please make sure to hand in your assignment by Friday afternoon.
He forgot to hand in his application before the deadline.
All contest entries must be handed in at the front desk.

Synset Examples

Make sure to hand in your checks for reimbursement.
She had to hand in her checks at the end of the month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to submit - to present or give something to someone for consideration or judgment
