to try it on

1. VERB

/ ˈtraɪ ɪt ɑn / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ ɪt ɒn / (BrE)
to put on a piece of clothing or accessory to check its fit, appearance, or suitability before deciding to buy or use it
put on clothing to check fit or style

Examples

Whenever I go shopping, I always try it on to make sure it fits perfectly.
If you're not sure about the size, just try it on before buying.
She didn't hesitate to try it on when the shop assistant handed her the dress.

Synset Examples

She decided to try it on before making a purchase.
He told her to try it on and see how it fits.
Before buying, always try it on for the best fit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to try on - to put on clothing to see if it fits or suits; synonym (without pronoun)
