to nail a presentation

1. VERB

/ neɪl ə ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / neɪl ə ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
to perform exceptionally well or successfully when giving a presentation
perform a presentation very successfully
informal

Examples

She was nervous, but she absolutely nailed her presentation in front of the board.
With weeks of preparation, the team nailed the presentation and impressed the clients.
You'll do fine—just stay calm and remember all your points, and you'll nail your presentation.

Synset Examples

She fricking nailed her presentation today!
Hats off, he really nailed that presentation.
You guys really nailed that presentation!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ace - to perform extremely well at something
  • Antonym: blow a presentation - to do very poorly in a presentation

Etymology

From the slang sense of "nail" meaning to accomplish or succeed at something, originally from the verb "to nail" (to fix in place with a nail).
