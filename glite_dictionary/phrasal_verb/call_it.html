to call it

1. VERB

/ kɔl ɪt / (AmE), / kɔːl ɪt / (BrE)
to decide to stop doing something, especially when you think it is best to finish or give up at that point.
decide to stop or finish an activity

Examples

It's getting late, so let's call it and go home.
After hours of discussion, they agreed to call it for the day.
I'm tired of this argument; can we just call it and move on?

Synset Examples

She decided to call it after the third attempt.
I think we should call it before we lose more time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to give up - to stop trying to do something
  • Antonym: to continue - to keep doing something without stopping
