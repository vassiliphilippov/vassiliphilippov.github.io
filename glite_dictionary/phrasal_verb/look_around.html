to look around

1. VERB

/ lʊk əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / lʊk əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to examine or observe one's surroundings, typically by turning one's head or moving to see what is present
examine or observe one's surroundings

Examples

When you enter the museum, take a moment to look around and admire the architecture.
He paused to look around before deciding which path to take in the park.
As I looked around the room, I noticed several familiar faces.

Synset Examples

look around to see whether you can find the missing document

Related Terms

  • Synonym: look about - to examine one's surroundings; a synonym for 'look around'
