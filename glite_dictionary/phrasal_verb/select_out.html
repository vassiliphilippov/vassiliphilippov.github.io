to select out

1. VERB

/ səˈlɛkt aʊt / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛkt aʊt / (BrE)
to choose or identify specific items from a group, often by separating them from others
choose items from a group

Examples

The teacher asked the students to select out the most important facts from the passage.
During the inventory check, we need to select out all defective products for review.
Geneticists often select out organisms with desired traits for breeding experiments.

Synset Examples

Please select out the items you want to keep.
The software can help us select out the duplicates in the database.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: select - to choose from a number of options
