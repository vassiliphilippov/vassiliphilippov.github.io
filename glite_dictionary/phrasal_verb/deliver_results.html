to deliver results

1. VERB

/ dɪˈlɪvɚ rɪˈzʌlts / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvə rɪˈzʌlts / (BrE)
to achieve effective or successful outcomes, especially in a professional or business context
achieve successful outcomes

Examples

The new manager was hired because of her ability to deliver results consistently.
We expect every team member to deliver results on high-priority projects.
He failed to deliver results despite being given all the necessary resources.

Synset Examples

She always delivers results where it counts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to succeed - to achieve the desired aim or result
  • Antonym: to fail - to be unsuccessful in achieving a goal or result
