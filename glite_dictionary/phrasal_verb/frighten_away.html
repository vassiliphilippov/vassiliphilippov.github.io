to frighten away

1. VERB

/ ˈfraɪtən əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ˈfraɪtən əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to make someone or something go away by making them feel afraid
make go away by causing fear

Examples

The loud noises frightened away the birds from the garden.
Her harsh tone may frighten away potential clients who visit the office.
The security guard managed to frighten away the intruders before any damage was done.

Synset Examples

dashed by the refusal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to scare off - make someone or something go away by frightening them
