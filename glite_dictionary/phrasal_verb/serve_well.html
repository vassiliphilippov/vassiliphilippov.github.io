to serve well

1. VERB

/ sɜrv wɛl / (AmE), / sɜːv wɛl / (BrE)
to be useful, beneficial, or advantageous to someone or something; to help effectively
be of benefit; help effectively

Examples

His ability to adapt has served him well throughout his career.
A good education will serve you well in the future.
Her quick thinking served the team well during the crisis.

Synset Examples

Art serves commerce
Their interests are served
The lake serves recreation
The President's wisdom has served the country well

Related Terms

  • Synonym: benefit - to be of advantage or profit to
