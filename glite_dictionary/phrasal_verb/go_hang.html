to go hang

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ hæŋ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ hæŋ / (BrE)
to relax or spend time idly and informally, often without any particular purpose
spend time relaxing or idly
informal

Examples

Let's go hang at the park this afternoon and enjoy the weather.
After the meeting, the team decided to go hang at a nearby café.
Sometimes it's nice to just go hang by yourself and clear your mind.

Synset Examples

When he heard the news, he just wanted to go hang.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to hang out - to spend time relaxing or socializing
