to puddle up

1. VERB

/ ˈpʌd.əl ʌp / (AmE), / ˈpʌd.əl ʌp / (BrE)
(of people) to gather closely together or huddle in a group, often for fun or warmth.
to huddle or gather in a group

Examples

The children puddled up around the campfire to stay warm on the chilly night.
We puddled up near the window to watch the thunderstorm together.
As soon as it started to rain, everyone puddled up under the large umbrella.

Synset Examples

Let's puddle up and enjoy the rain.
They decided to puddle up and splash each other.
Don't forget to puddle up before heading outside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: huddle - to crowd together closely
