to waste time

1. VERB

/ weɪst taɪm / (AmE), / weɪst taɪm / (BrE)
to spend time doing things that are not useful or important, especially when you could be doing something more productive
spend time on unimportant things

Examples

Please don't waste time arguing about details that don't matter.
He realized he had wasted time watching television instead of studying for his exam.
We can't afford to waste time if we want to meet the deadline.

Synset Examples

Don't waste time on picayune matters during the meeting.
Don't wastetime on things that don't matter.
I can't believe he wasted his time on that pap.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to save time - to avoid unnecessary loss of time; to be efficient
