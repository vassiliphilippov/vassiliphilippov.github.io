to go to the beach

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ tu ðə bitʃ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə ðə biːtʃ / (BrE)
to visit the seaside, especially for leisure or recreation
visit the seaside for relaxation or fun

Examples

Every summer, we go to the beach to relax and swim in the ocean.
Let's go to the beach this weekend if the weather is nice.
They usually go to the beach early in the morning to avoid the crowds.

Synset Examples

We could go to the beach, or- perhaps the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go to the seaside - to visit the seashore for recreation
