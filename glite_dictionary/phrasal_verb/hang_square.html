to hang square

1. VERB

/ hæŋ skwɛr / (AmE), / hæŋ skweə / (BrE)
to be or remain perfectly aligned or straight when suspended, especially from a wall or ceiling
to be perfectly aligned or straight when suspended

Examples

I adjusted the clock so it would hang square above the fireplace.
Check the mirror from different angles to ensure it hangs square on the door.
The painting never hung square, no matter how many times we tried.

Synset Examples

Make sure the picture frames hang square on the wall.
