to drop a hint

1. VERB

/ drɑp ə hɪnt / (AmE), / drɒp ə hɪnt / (BrE)
to suggest or indicate something in a subtle or indirect way, especially to guide someone's actions or opinions
suggest something subtly

Examples

She tried to drop a hint about wanting to leave early, but no one noticed.
I often drop a hint when I think my friends have forgotten my birthday.
He dropped a hint about getting a promotion during the meeting.

Synset Examples

I always drop a hint when I need help.
She tried to drop a hint about her birthday.
He dropped a hint that he wanted a new bike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to suggest - to state or express indirectly
