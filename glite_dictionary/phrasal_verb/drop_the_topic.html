to drop the topic

1. VERB

/ drɑp ðə ˈtɑpɪk / (AmE), / drɒp ðə ˈtɒpɪk / (BrE)
to stop discussing a particular subject
stop discussing a subject

Examples

After he realized he was upsetting her, he decided to drop the topic altogether.
Can we please drop the topic and move on to something more pleasant?
She sensed the argument was going nowhere, so she chose to drop the topic.

Synset Examples

Let's drop the topic; it's making everyone uncomfortable.
We should drop the topic before it escalates.
She decided to drop the topic and change the conversation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: change the subject - to begin talking about something different
