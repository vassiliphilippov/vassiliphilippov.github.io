to get a discount

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ə dɪˈskaʊnt / (AmE), / ɡɛt ə dɪsˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
to receive a reduction in the usual price of something
receive a price reduction

Examples

If you show your student ID at the movie theater, you can get a discount.
We managed to get a discount because we bought the furniture in bulk.
She always tries to get a discount whenever she shops online.

Synset Examples

I got a discount on my new shoes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discount - a reduction in the usual cost of something
