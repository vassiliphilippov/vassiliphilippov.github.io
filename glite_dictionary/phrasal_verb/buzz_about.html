to buzz about

1. VERB

/ ˈbʌz əˌbaʊt / (AmE), / ˈbʌz əˌbaʊt / (BrE)
to talk excitedly or energetically about something, often spreading news or rumors
talk excitedly about something

Examples

The office was buzzing about the surprising changes in the company's leadership.
Fans are buzzing about the release date of the new album.
Social media was buzzing about the celebrity wedding all weekend.

Synset Examples

Investors were buzzing about the stonk after the announcement.
The town is buzzing about the Big Game.
Everyone was buzzing about it around the traps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gossip about - to talk about the personal lives or secrets of others
  • Synonym: whisper about - to talk quietly or secretly about something or someone
