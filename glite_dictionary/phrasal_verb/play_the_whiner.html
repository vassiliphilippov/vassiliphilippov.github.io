to play the whiner

1. VERB

/ pleɪ ðə ˈwaɪnər / (AmE), / pleɪ ðə ˈwaɪnə / (BrE)
to behave as if one is someone who complains excessively, especially to gain sympathy or avoid responsibility
act as someone who complains a lot

Examples

Whenever the meeting doesn't go his way, he always plays the whiner instead of offering solutions.
It's hard to respect someone who constantly plays the whiner at work.
She tried not to play the whiner, but the long hours really wore her down.

Synset Examples

He always plays the whiner when things get tough.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to complain - to express dissatisfaction or annoyance
  • Synonym: whine - to complain in a feeble or petulant way
