to spank it

1. VERB

/ spæŋk ɪt / (AmE), / spæŋk ɪt / (BrE)
to give a light slap to a malfunctioning device in hopes of making it work again
lightly slap a malfunctioning device
informal

Examples

Sometimes, when the old TV won't turn on, you just have to spank it.
He tried everything to fix the remote before resorting to spanking it.
If your printer jams again, don't be afraid to spank it gently.

Synset Examples

If the computer freezes, sometimes you just have to spank it.
When the car wouldn't start, he had to spank it a little.
