to clip the article

1. VERB

/ klɪp ði ˈɑrtɪkəl / (AmE), / klɪp ði ˈɑːtɪkl̩ / (BrE)
to cut out or save a newspaper or magazine article, usually for future reference
cut out or save an article

Examples

My mother used to clip the article every time my name was mentioned in the paper.
You should clip the article and keep it in your records for later use.
He clipped the article about the discovery and shared it with his colleagues.

Synset Examples

She decided to clip the article for future reference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cut out - to remove something by cutting, especially from printed material
