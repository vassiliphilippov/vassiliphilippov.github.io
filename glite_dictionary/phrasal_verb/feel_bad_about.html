to feel bad about

1. VERB

/ fil bæd əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / fiːl bæd əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to experience regret or guilt regarding something that has been done or not done
regret or feel guilty about

Examples

She still feels bad about forgetting his birthday last year.
You shouldn't feel bad about making a mistake; everyone does it sometimes.
He felt bad about letting his team down during the finals.

Synset Examples

Don't feel bad about asking for help.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regret - to feel sad or disappointed about something that has happened or been done
  • Synonym: feel guilty about - to experience a sense of guilt concerning something
