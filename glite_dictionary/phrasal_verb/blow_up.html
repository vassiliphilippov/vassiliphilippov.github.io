to blow up

1. VERB

/ bloʊ ʌp / (AmE), / bləʊ ʌp / (BrE)
to cause something to burst apart or explode with a violent release of energy
cause to explode violently

Examples

The engineers planned to blow up the old bridge early in the morning.
They threatened to blow up the building unless their demands were met.
Experts used dynamite to blow up the rocks blocking the road.

Synset Examples

We exploded the nuclear bomb

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to explode - to burst or cause to burst suddenly and violently
