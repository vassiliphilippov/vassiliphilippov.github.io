to climb on

1. VERB

/ klaɪm ɑn / (AmE), / klaɪm ɒn / (BrE)
to get up onto the back or surface of something, especially an animal or object
get up onto an animal or object

Examples

She quickly climbed on the horse and rode away.
The children were eager to climb on the playground equipment as soon as recess began.
He carefully climbed on the ladder to reach the roof.

Synset Examples

mount a horse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mount - to get on the back of (a horse, bicycle, etc.)
