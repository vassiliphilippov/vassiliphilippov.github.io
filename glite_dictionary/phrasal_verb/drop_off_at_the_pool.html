to drop off at the pool

1. VERB

/ drɑp ɑf æt ðə pul / (AmE), / drɒp ɒf æt ðə puːl / (BrE)
to leave someone at a swimming pool, usually after giving them a ride
take and leave someone at the pool

Examples

I usually drop off at the pool before going to work.
Can you drop me off at the pool on your way to town?
They asked their parents to drop them off at the pool after lunch.

Synset Examples

She'll drop the kids off at the pool and join us later.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to drop off - to deliver or leave someone or something at a particular place
