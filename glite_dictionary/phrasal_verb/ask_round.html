to ask round

1. VERB

/ æsk ˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ɑːsk ˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to invite someone to your home, especially for a social visit or meal
invite someone to your home

Examples

We decided to ask round all our friends for a barbecue this weekend.
If you're free Friday, I'll ask round a few colleagues for dinner.
They love to ask round neighbors whenever there's a holiday.

Synset Examples

Can I invite you for dinner on Sunday night?
