to try on

1. VERB

/ traɪ ɑn / (AmE), / traɪ ɒn / (BrE)
to put on a piece of clothing to check if it fits or looks good
put on clothing to check fit or appearance

Examples

She decided to try on the dress before buying it.
You should try on these shoes to see if they are comfortable.
He spent an hour trying on different jackets at the store.

Synset Examples

Try on this sweater to see how it looks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to try out - to test something to see if it works well
