to luck it

1. VERB

/ lʌk ɪt / (AmE), / lʌk ɪt / (BrE)
to do something by relying entirely on luck rather than planning or skill
act by relying on luck

Examples

He didn't study for the test and decided to luck it instead.
Sometimes, you just have to luck it and hope for the best.
She forgot her speech but managed to luck it on stage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wing it - to do something without preparation or planning
