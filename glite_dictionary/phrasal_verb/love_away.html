to love away

1. VERB

/ lʌv əˈweɪ / (AmE), / lʌv əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to diminish or remove difficulties, pain, or negative feelings through expressions of love or kindness
remove problems through love or kindness

Examples

She tried to love away his sadness by being there for him every day.
Sometimes a mother’s warmth can love away even the deepest fears in a child.
He hoped that their patience would eventually love away the resentment in the family.

Synset Examples

He tried to love away his troubles with kindness.
They believed laughter could love away their worries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wish away - to try to get rid of something by wishing it would go away
