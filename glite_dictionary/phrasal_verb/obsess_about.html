to obsess about

1. VERB

/ əbˈsɛs əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / əbˈsɛs əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to think about something too much or for an extended period of time, often in a way that is not helpful or healthy
think excessively about something

Examples

Try not to obsess about every small mistake you make at work.
She tends to obsess about her appearance before any big event.
It's unhealthy to obsess about things you can't change.

Synset Examples

She would obsess about her grades all semester.
Don't obsess over things you can't control.
He tends to obsess over minor details in his work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obsess over - to think about something excessively or constantly
