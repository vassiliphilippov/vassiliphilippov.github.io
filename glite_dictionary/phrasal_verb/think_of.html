to think of

1. VERB

/ θɪŋk ʌv / (AmE), / θɪŋk ɒv / (BrE)
to regard or consider someone or something in a particular way
consider; regard in a certain way

Examples

Many people think of Paris as the city of love.
He likes to think of himself as an expert on the subject.
I never thought of her as someone who would enjoy hiking.

Synset Examples

she looked on this affair as a joke

He is reputed to be intelligent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to regard as - to think of someone or something in a specified way
  • Synonym: look on as - to view or consider in a specific way
