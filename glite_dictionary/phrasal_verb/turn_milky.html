to turn milky

1. VERB

/ tɝn ˈmɪlki / (AmE), / tɜːn ˈmɪlki / (BrE)
(of a liquid or surface) to become cloudy or whitish in appearance, resembling milk.
become cloudy or whitish

Examples

After a few drops of acid, the solution turned milky within seconds.
As the fog rolled in, the road turned milky and visibility dropped.
When milk spoils, it often turns milky and opaque.

Synset Examples

The sky turned milky as the sun set.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cloudy - (of a liquid or sky) not clear, having a cloudlike appearance
  • Antonym: clear - free from cloudiness or impurities
