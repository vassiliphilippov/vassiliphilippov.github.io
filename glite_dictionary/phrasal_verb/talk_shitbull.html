to talk shitbull

1. VERB

/ tɔk ˈʃɪtbʊl / (AmE), / tɔːk ˈʃɪtbʊl / (BrE)
to speak nonsense or exaggerate, often to impress or deceive
speak nonsense or exaggerate (slang)
slang

Examples

He's always trying to talk shitbull when he feels insecure.
If you don't know the facts, don't talk shitbull at the meeting.
She accused him of talking shitbull just to look smart.

Synset Examples

He always talks shitbull when he's nervous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullshit - nonsense; false or deceptive talk
  • Synonym: talk nonsense - to speak in a way that is not sensible or true
  • Antonym: speak plainly - to speak honestly or in a straightforward way

Etymology

Blend of 'talk shit' and 'bullshit', both slang terms with meanings related to speaking dishonestly or nonsensically.
