to cast around

1. VERB

/ kæst əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / kɑːst əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to search anxiously or with difficulty for something, such as an idea, solution, or information
search anxiously or with difficulty

Examples

She cast around for something to say, but nothing came to mind.
The manager cast around for a solution to the sudden problem.
He was casting around in his mind for a suitable excuse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: search - to try to find something by looking or seeking
