to tickle someone's tummy

1. VERB

/ ˈtɪkəl ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈtʌmi / (AmE), / ˈtɪkəl ˈsʌmwənz ˈtʌmi / (BrE)
to touch someone's stomach lightly and repeatedly in a playful way to make them laugh
touch someone's stomach to cause laughter

Examples

The puppy rolled over, hoping I would tickle her tummy.
It's a tradition in our family to tickle someone's tummy when they're feeling sad.
He tried to tickle his sister's tummy to make her stop crying.

Synset Examples

The baby giggled as I tickled her tummy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tickle - to touch someone lightly to cause laughter
