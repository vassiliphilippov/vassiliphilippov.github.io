to ram home

1. VERB

/ ræm hoʊm / (AmE), / ræm həʊm / (BrE)
to make something clearly understood by emphasizing it forcefully
emphasize and make clear a point

Examples

The manager tried to ram home the importance of meeting the deadline.
She rammed home her argument with clear statistics and strong evidence.
He kept ramming home the idea that teamwork is essential for success.

Synset Examples

drive home a point or an argument
I'm trying to drive home these basic ideas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to drive home - to emphasize and make clear a point
