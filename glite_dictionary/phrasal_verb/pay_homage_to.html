to pay homage to

1. VERB

/ peɪ ˈhɑmɪdʒ tu / (AmE), / peɪ ˈhɒmɪdʒ tʊ / (BrE)
to show deep respect or honor to someone or something, often in a public or formal way
show respect or honor

Examples

The artist's latest painting pays homage to her childhood in the countryside.
During the ceremony, the leader paid homage to those who had served the country.
The film pays homage to classic horror movies from the 1970s.

Synset Examples

This sculpture pays homage to the city's rich history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honor - regard with great respect
  • Antonym: disregard - lack of consideration or respect for someone or something

Etymology

From Old French *hommage* (allegiance or respect), from Latin *hominaticum* (acknowledgment of allegiance by a vassal to a lord).
