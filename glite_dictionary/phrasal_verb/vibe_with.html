to vibe with

1. VERB

/ vaɪb wɪð / (AmE), / vaɪb wɪð / (BrE)
to feel a natural connection or sense of rapport with someone or something
feel rapport or connection with
informal

Examples

I immediately vibed with the new team when I joined the company.
It's hard for me to vibe with people who don't appreciate music.
They really vibed with the relaxed atmosphere at the café.

Synset Examples

I really vibe with her artistic vision.
They found they could vibe with each other's ideas.
It's easy to vibe with people who share your interests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to get along with - to have a friendly relationship with someone
  • Synonym: click with - to immediately feel a connection with someone

Etymology

From 'vibe' (short for 'vibration', used in the sense of an emotional atmosphere or feeling) + 'with'.
