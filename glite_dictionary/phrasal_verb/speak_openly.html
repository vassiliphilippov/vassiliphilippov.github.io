to speak openly

1. VERB

/ spiːk ˈoʊpənli / (AmE), / spiːk ˈəʊpənli / (BrE)
to talk in a candid and honest way, without hiding facts or feelings
talk frankly and honestly

Examples

During the meeting, she spoke openly about the challenges her team faced.
Politicians are often reluctant to speak openly about controversial issues.
He finally decided to speak openly with his parents about his plans to move abroad.

Synset Examples

She spoke openly about her experience with abortion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be frank - to speak honestly and directly
  • Synonym: be candid - to express oneself openly and honestly
