to go batshit

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ ˈbæt.ʃɪt / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈbæt.ʃɪt / (BrE)
to become extremely angry, irrational, excited, or crazy
become extremely angry or crazy
informal vulgar american

Examples

When the boss heard about the mistake, he went batshit and started yelling at everyone.
The crowd went batshit when the band appeared on stage.
If you try to mess with her stuff, she'll go batshit for sure.

Synset Examples

If he doesn't get his way, he tends to go batshit.
The team went batshit over the last-minute victory.
She went batshit when she discovered the surprise party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to lose it - to suddenly lose control of oneself (emotionally)
  • Synonym: freak out - to become suddenly very upset, angry, or frightened

Etymology

From 'batshit', an intensifier derived from 'batshit crazy', originally American slang.
