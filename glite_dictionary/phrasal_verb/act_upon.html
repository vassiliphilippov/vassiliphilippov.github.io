to act upon

1. VERB

/ ækt əˈpɑn / (AmE), / ækt əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to have an influence on something or someone, or to cause a change in them
influence or affect

Examples

The new law will act upon the entire community, shaping how people behave.
Certain chemicals in the medicine act upon the nerves to reduce pain.
Her words acted upon him deeply, changing his opinion about the matter.

Synset Examples

The artist's work influenced the young painter
She worked on her friends to support the political candidate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: influence - to have an effect on the character or behavior of someone or something
  • Synonym: affect - to produce a change in someone or something
