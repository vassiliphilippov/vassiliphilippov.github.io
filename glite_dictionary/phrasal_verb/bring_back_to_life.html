to bring back to life

1. VERB

/ brɪŋ bæk tə laɪf / (AmE), / brɪŋ bak tə laɪf / (BrE)
to cause someone or something to return to a living, functional, or active state; to revive or resurrect
restore to life or activity

Examples

Thanks to modern medicine, doctors were able to bring back to life the unconscious patient.
The artist's restoration work helped bring back to life faded masterpieces.
Scientists hope advances in cloning may one day bring back to life extinct animals.

Synset Examples

They hope to bring back to life the old traditions.
The new technology aims to bring back to life extinct species.
The paramedics worked to bring him back to life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to resuscitate - to revive someone from unconsciousness or apparent death
  • Synonym: to revive - to bring back to life, consciousness, or activity
  • Antonym: kill - to cause the death of someone or something
