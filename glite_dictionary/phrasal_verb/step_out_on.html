to step out on

1. VERB

/ stɛp aʊt ɑn / (AmE), / stɛp aʊt ɒn / (BrE)
(informal) to be unfaithful to one's romantic partner by having a sexual relationship with someone else.
cheat on a partner
informal

Examples

Rumors spread that he might step out on his wife during business trips.
She never expected him to step out on her after so many years together.
Some people believe that once you step out on someone, trust is hard to rebuild.

Synset Examples

He chose to step out on her with a coworker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cheat on - to be sexually unfaithful to a partner
