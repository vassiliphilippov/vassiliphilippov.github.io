to get down

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt daʊn / (AmE), / ɡɛt daʊn / (BrE)
to put something in writing; to record or write down text, music, ideas, etc.
record or write down

Examples

Let me get down your phone number before I forget it.
The scribe quickly got down every word the speaker said.
It took hours for the composer to get down the melody on paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to write down - to record something by writing it
