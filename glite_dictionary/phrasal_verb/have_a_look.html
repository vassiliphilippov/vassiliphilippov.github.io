to have a look

1. VERB

/ hæv ə lʊk / (AmE), / hæv ə lʊk / (BrE)
to look at something carefully or with attention
look at with attention

Examples

Could you have a look at my essay before I submit it?
She asked the mechanic to have a look at the engine.
Let's have a look at the results together.

Synset Examples

Have a look at this!
Get a load of this pretty woman!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to look at - to direct your eyes toward something
