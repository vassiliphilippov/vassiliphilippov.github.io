to range in

1. VERB

/ reɪndʒ ɪn / (AmE), / reɪndʒ ɪn / (BrE)
to direct or aim at a point or target, especially with the help of navigational aids or targeting systems
aim or direct at a target with navigational aids

Examples

The radar system automatically ranges in on incoming aircraft for precise targeting.
The artillery officer carefully ranged in the guns before giving the order to fire.
Modern missiles can range in on their targets with extreme accuracy using GPS guidance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aim - to point or direct toward a target
