to trick out

1. VERB

/ trɪk aʊt / (AmE), / trɪk aʊt / (BrE)
to dress or decorate in an elaborate or attractive way, especially by adding special clothes or adornments
decorate or dress up elaborately

Examples

The car was tricked out with colorful lights and custom wheels.
She tricked out her costume with feathers and glitter for the masquerade ball.
He loves to trick out his outfits with flashy accessories.

Synset Examples

She never dresses up, even when she goes to the opera
The young girls were all fancied up for the party

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to dress up - to wear formal or special clothes
  • Synonym: to adorn - to decorate someone or something with something
