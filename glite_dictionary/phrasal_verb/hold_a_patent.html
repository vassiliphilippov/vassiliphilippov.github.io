to hold a patent

1. VERB

/ hoʊld ə ˈpætənt / (AmE), / həʊld ə ˈpætənt / (BrE)
to possess the legal rights to an invention granted by a government authority.
possess legal rights to an invention
legal

Examples

The startup was able to attract investors because it holds a patent for its innovative battery technology.
Only one company holds a patent on this life-saving medication, limiting its availability.
After years of research, Dr. Carter finally held a patent for her groundbreaking medical device.

Synset Examples

The company holds a patent on that technology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patent - an official right to make or sell an invention
