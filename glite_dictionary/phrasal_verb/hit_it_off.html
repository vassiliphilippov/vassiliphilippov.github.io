to hit it off

1. VERB

/ hɪt ɪt ɔf / (AmE), / hɪt ɪt ɒf / (BrE)
to immediately have a good relationship with someone; to get along well from the start
quickly form a good relationship

Examples

I was surprised at how quickly they hit it off during their first meeting.
My roommate and I immediately hit it off when we moved in together.
The new colleagues hit it off and soon became close friends.

Synset Examples

My boss and I get along very well

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to get along - to have a harmonious or friendly relationship
  • Antonym: to fall out - to have a quarrel; stop being friends
