to feel lost

1. VERB

/ fil lɑst / (AmE), / fiːl lɒst / (BrE)
to experience confusion, uncertainty, or disorientation, often in unfamiliar situations or without usual support
experience confusion or disorientation

Examples

After moving to the new city, he began to feel lost without his friends.
Sometimes I feel lost during complicated meetings at work.
She started to feel lost when her plans suddenly changed.

Synset Examples

He felt lost in Bumfuck, Egypt during the detour.
She forgot her phone at home and felt lost.
She feels lost mitout her favorite book.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confused - unable to think clearly or to understand something
  • Synonym: disoriented - lacking a clear sense of direction or position
  • Antonym: to feel at home - to feel comfortable and relaxed in a place
