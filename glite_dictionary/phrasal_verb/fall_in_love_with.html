to fall in love with

1. VERB

/ fɔl ɪn lʌv wɪθ / (AmE), / fɔːl ɪn lʌv wɪð / (BrE)
to begin to feel strong romantic affection for someone or something
develop romantic feelings for

Examples

It is easy to fall in love with a city as vibrant as Barcelona.
She fell in love with him the moment they met.
Many people fall in love with music at a young age.

Synset Examples

She fell in love with Chicongo during her summer internship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fall for - to become strongly attracted to someone romantically
