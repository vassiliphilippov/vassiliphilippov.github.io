to buckle up

1. VERB

/ ˈbʌk.əl ʌp / (AmE), / ˈbʌk.əl ʌp / (BrE)
to fasten a seatbelt for safety, or to get ready for a difficult or exciting experience
fasten seatbelt; prepare for difficulty

Examples

Before we start the journey, please buckle up for your own safety.
The pilot told everyone to buckle up as turbulence was expected.
It's going to be a busy week at work, so get ready to buckle up.

Synset Examples

Always remember to buckle up for safety.
Make sure to buckle up before we drive.
It's time to buckle up for the rollercoaster ride.
Don't forget to buckle your seatbelt!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fasten - to close or secure something so that it won't open
  • Antonym: to unbuckle - to unfasten a seatbelt or belt
