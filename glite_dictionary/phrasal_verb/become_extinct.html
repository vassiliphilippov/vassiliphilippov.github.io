to become extinct

1. VERB

/ bɪˈkʌm ɪkˈstɪŋkt / (AmE), / bɪˈkʌm ɪkˈstɪŋkt / (BrE)
to cease to exist as a species, group, or phenomenon; to die out completely.
cease to exist; die out

Examples

Many animal species may become extinct if deforestation continues at the current rate.
Local dialects often become extinct when fewer people use them over generations.
Scientists study why certain plants became extinct thousands of years ago.

Synset Examples

The mastodon became extinct due to habitat changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to die out - to disappear completely; to become extinct
  • Antonym: to survive - to continue to live or exist
