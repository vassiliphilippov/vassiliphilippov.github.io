to made up of

1. VERB

/ meɪd ʌp ʌv / (AmE), / meɪd ʌp ɒv / (BrE)
consisting of the stated parts or people; composed of
composed of; consisting of

Examples

The cake is made up of several layers of chocolate and cream.
The committee is made up of teachers, parents, and students.
Our solar system is made up of the sun and the planets that orbit it.

Synset Examples

The team is made up of dedicated cagers from the community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: composed of - consisting of specific parts
  • Synonym: consist of - to be formed or made from certain things
