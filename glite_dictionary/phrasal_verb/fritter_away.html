to fritter away

1. VERB

/ ˈfrɪt̬ɚ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ˈfrɪtə əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to waste money, time, or resources on unimportant or foolish things
waste (money, time) on unimportant things

Examples

He managed to fritter away his entire fortune within a few years.
Don't fritter away your free time scrolling through your phone all day.
She realized she had frittered away countless opportunities by being indecisive.

Synset Examples

Fritter away one's inheritance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waste - use or expend carelessly or to no purpose
  • Antonym: conserve - protect from harm or destruction; prevent waste

Etymology

From the verb 'fritter' (to squander), which is possibly related to an obsolete word meaning 'to break into small fragments'.
