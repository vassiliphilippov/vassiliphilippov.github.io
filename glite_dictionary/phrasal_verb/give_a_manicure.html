to give a manicure

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ə ˈmænɪˌkjʊr / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə ˈmænɪˌkjɔː / (BrE)
to perform a cosmetic treatment on someone’s fingernails and hands, usually involving cleaning, shaping, and sometimes painting the nails
perform a manicure on someone

Examples

She loves to give a manicure to her friends during their spa nights.
The salon offers lessons on how to give a manicure professionally.
He learned to give a manicure as a special surprise for his mother.

Synset Examples

His girlfriend insisted on giving him a manicure for fun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manicure - a cosmetic treatment of the hands and fingernails
