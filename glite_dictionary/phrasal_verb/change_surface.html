to change surface

1. VERB

/ tʃeɪndʒ ˈsɜr.fɪs / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ ˈsɜː.fɪs / (BrE)
to undergo or cause to undergo an alteration in the surface of something, such as its texture, appearance, or composition
alter the surface of something

Examples

The new treatment can change surface properties to make the material more water-resistant.
Exposure to high temperatures may change surface color and texture.
Engineers change surface characteristics to improve the performance of machine parts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to modify - to make partial or minor changes to something
  • Antonym: to maintain - to keep something in its original condition without change
