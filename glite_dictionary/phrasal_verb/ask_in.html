to ask in

1. VERB

/ æsk ɪn / (AmE), / ɑːsk ɪn / (BrE)
to invite someone to enter a room or building, especially one's home
invite someone to enter

Examples

It was raining, so she decided to ask in the delivery driver while he waited for his ride.
After chatting at the door, he finally asked in his old friend to continue their conversation.
Don't be shy—if a guest arrives, it's polite to ask them in and offer a seat.

Synset Examples

We invited the neighbors in for a cup of coffee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invite in - to ask someone to come inside
