to stick by

1. VERB

/ stɪk baɪ / (AmE), / stɪk baɪ / (BrE)
to continue to support or be loyal to someone, especially in difficult situations
remain loyal to someone

Examples

Even when her team lost, she continued to stick by them.
True friends will always stick by you no matter what.
He promised to stick by his family during hard times.

Synset Examples

She stood by her husband in times of trouble
The friends stuck together through the war

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stand by - to remain loyal or supportive
