to scratch by

1. VERB

/ skrætʃ baɪ / (AmE), / skrætʃ baɪ / (BrE)
to manage to survive or get by with very limited resources or money
manage to survive with little
informal

Examples

After losing his job, he had to scratch by on his savings for months.
Many families scratch by on minimum wage incomes during tough financial times.
Even as a student, she managed to scratch by with careful budgeting.

Synset Examples

He had to scratch by with just enough money for food.
We can scratch by until the next paycheck arrives.
They managed to scratch by on their limited budget.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to get by - to manage to survive or cope, especially with difficulty
  • Synonym: to scrape by - to just manage to succeed, especially with very little money
  • Antonym: to thrive - to grow or develop successfully
