to clap on

1. VERB

/ klæp ɑn / (AmE), / klap ɒn / (BrE)
to apply something carelessly or hastily, often by spreading or slapping it onto a surface
apply carelessly or hastily

Examples

He clapped on some sunscreen before running outside.
The workers clapped on another coat of paint without much prep.
She clapped on a quick layer of makeup before the meeting.

Synset Examples

slap some paint onto the wall
