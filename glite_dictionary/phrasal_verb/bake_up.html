to bake up

1. VERB

/ beɪk ʌp / (AmE), / beɪk ʌp / (BrE)
to prepare or create baked food, often in a creative or spontaneous way
make baked food creatively or spontaneously

Examples

She decided to bake up some muffins with the blueberries she had left.
They love to bake up new recipes together on the weekends.
Last night, he baked up a batch of cookies for his friends.

Synset Examples

They managed to bake up a delicious surprise.
Let’s bake up some treats for the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to bake - to cook food with dry heat, especially in an oven
