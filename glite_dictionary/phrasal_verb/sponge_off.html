to sponge off

1. VERB

/ spʌndʒ ɑf / (AmE), / spʌndʒ ɒf / (BrE)
to clean a surface by rubbing it with a sponge and water
clean by rubbing with a sponge

Examples

She sponged off the table until it was perfectly clean.
After dinner, he sponged off the kitchen counter.
The janitor sponged off the whiteboard before the next class arrived.
