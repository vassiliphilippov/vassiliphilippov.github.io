to pop the lid back

1. VERB

/ pɑp ðə lɪd bæk / (AmE), / pɒp ðə lɪd bæk / (BrE)
to quickly replace the lid on a container or jar
replace a lid quickly

Examples

As soon as she finished scooping the sugar, she popped the lid back on the jar.
He popped the lid back quickly so the soup wouldn't spill.
After checking the contents, they popped the lid back and put the box away.

Synset Examples

Could you pop the lid back on the jar?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: put the lid back on - to place the lid back in its original position on a container
