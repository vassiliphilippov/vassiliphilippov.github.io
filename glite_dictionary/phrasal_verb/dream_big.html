to dream big

1. VERB

/ driːm bɪɡ / (AmE), / driːm bɪɡ / (BrE)
to have ambitious goals or aspirations; to set very high aims for oneself
set ambitious goals; aim high

Examples

Her parents always encouraged her to dream big and never settle for less than her best.
Many successful entrepreneurs will tell you to dream big if you want to change the world.
Even when he failed, he kept dreaming big and working toward his visions.

Synset Examples

Goodnight, sweet pea; sleep tight and dream big!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to aim high - to set one's goals at a high or ambitious level
  • Antonym: to play it safe - to avoid taking risks; to act cautiously
