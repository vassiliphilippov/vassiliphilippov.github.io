to streamline project management

1. VERB

/ ˈstrimˌlaɪn ˈprɑːdʒɛkt ˈmænɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈstriːmlaɪn ˈprɒdʒɛkt ˈmænɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of managing projects, often by simplifying processes or using better tools
make project management more efficient

Examples

We implemented new software to streamline project management across all departments.
Hiring a specialist has helped us streamline project management and meet deadlines more consistently.
The company aims to streamline project management by reducing unnecessary paperwork and improving communication.

Synset Examples

The team used vum to streamline their project management.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimize workflow - to make the sequence of work tasks as effective and efficient as possible
