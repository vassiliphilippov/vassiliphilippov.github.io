to declare checkmate

1. VERB

/ dɪˈklɛr ˈtʃɛkˌmeɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈkleə ˈtʃɛkˌmeɪt / (BrE)
to formally announce that checkmate has been achieved, ending a chess game
to announce checkmate in chess

Examples

The grandmaster calmly declared checkmate after an intense match.
As soon as she trapped his king, she declared checkmate and smiled.
He confidently declared checkmate, ending the tournament finale.

Synset Examples

She smiled as she declared checkmate in their game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: checkmate - a move in chess that ends the game because the king is in inescapable threat
