to outlaw child labor

1. VERB

/ ˈaʊtˌlɔ ˈtʃaɪld ˌleɪbɚ / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.lɔː ˈtʃaɪld ˌleɪbə / (BrE)
to make the employment of children illegal by law
to make child labor illegal

Examples

A global coalition is working to outlaw child labor in the textile industry.
Activists praised the parliament's decision to outlaw child labor nationwide.
