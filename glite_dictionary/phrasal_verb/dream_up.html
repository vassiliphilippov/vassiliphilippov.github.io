to dream up

1. VERB

/ drim ʌp / (AmE), / driːm ʌp / (BrE)
to invent or imagine something, often something unusual or creative
to invent or imagine something

Examples

He managed to dream up a creative excuse at the last minute.
The team worked all night to dream up a solution to the problem.
Who would have dreamed up such a bizarre idea for a movie?

Synset Examples

He thought up a plan to get rich quickly
no-one had ever thought of such a clever piece of software

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devise - to plan or invent by careful thought
  • Synonym: to imagine - to form a mental image or concept
