to heap up

1. VERB

/ hip ʌp / (AmE), / hiːp ʌp / (BrE)
to arrange things into piles or stacks
arrange into piles, stack up

Examples

After raking the leaves, they heaped up the piles at the curb.
She heaped up all of her laundry on the bed before sorting it.
The snow heaped up against the door after the storm.

Synset Examples

She piled up her books in my living room

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pile up - to accumulate or arrange in a pile or stack
