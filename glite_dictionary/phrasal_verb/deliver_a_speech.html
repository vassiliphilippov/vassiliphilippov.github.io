to deliver a speech

1. VERB

/ dɪˈlɪvɚ ə spiːtʃ / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvə ə spiːtʃ / (BrE)
to give a formal talk or address to an audience
give a formal talk to an audience

Examples

The president will deliver a speech at the opening ceremony.
She practiced for weeks before delivering a speech to the graduating class.
Many politicians are skilled at delivering a speech that inspires their audience.

Synset Examples

He began to tremble as he delivered his speech.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: give a speech - to make a formal spoken presentation to an audience
  • Synonym: to make a speech - to formally address people with a prepared talk
