to work it out

1. VERB

/ wɝk ɪt aʊt / (AmE), / wɜːk ɪt aʊt / (BrE)
to resolve or find a solution to a problem or difficulty
resolve a problem; find a solution

Examples

If you have a disagreement, try to work it out calmly and respectfully.
We couldn't remember the answer, but together we managed to work it out.
She hopes to work it out with her employer and keep her job.

Synset Examples

We can always work it out on the remix later.
Let's just work it out on the remix for a fresh sound.
The DJ said he could work it out on the remix easily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to solve - to find an answer to a problem or puzzle
  • Synonym: to figure out - to understand or find the answer to something
