to recommend to

1. VERB

/ ˌrɛkəˈmɛnd tu / (AmE), / ˌrɛkəˈmɛnd tʊ / (BrE)
to advise someone to see or consult a particular person for advice, help, or treatment
advise someone to see another person

Examples

The teacher decided to recommend to John that he visit the school counselor.
Whenever she felt uncertain, she would recommend to her friend to see a therapist.
I often recommend to patients that they consult a specialist for further advice.

Synset Examples

She always recommended her patients to a good doctor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refer to - to direct someone to another person or place for help
  • Antonym: discourage from - to advise someone not to do something
