to hit upon

1. VERB

/ hɪt əˈpɑn / (AmE), / hɪt əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to discover or think of something unexpectedly or by chance, especially after searching or considering
discover or realize by chance

Examples

After hours of calculation, Marie finally hit upon the answer she was seeking.
The scientists hit upon a new method by accident during their research.
While reorganizing the files, he hit upon some important missing documents.

Synset Examples

He hit upon the truth after much investigation.
They hit upon the solution after brainstorming for hours.
We hit upon a great idea during our meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to come across - to find or discover something by chance
  • Synonym: stumble upon - to discover something unexpectedly
