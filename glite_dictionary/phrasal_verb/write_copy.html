to write copy

1. VERB

/ raɪt ˈkɑpi / (AmE), / raɪt ˈkɒpi / (BrE)
to produce written material for advertisements, marketing, or other commercial publications
produce text for commercial publications

Examples

She was hired to write copy for the new campaign.
He spends his days writing copy for tech company websites.
They often ask the marketing team to write copy at short notice.

Synset Examples

She writes copy for Harper's Bazaar

Related Terms

  • Synonym: copywriter - a person who writes copy for advertisements or publicity material
