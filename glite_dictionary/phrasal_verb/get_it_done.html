to get it done

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ɪt dʌn / (AmE), / ɡɛt ɪt dʌn / (BrE)
to complete a task or action; to finish something that needs to be accomplished
complete a task or action

Examples

If you focus and work hard, you can get it done by the deadline.
Whenever a problem arises, she always finds a way to get it done.
We have a lot to do, so let's just get it done and go home.

Synset Examples

I’m tired of excuses, just get it done, no wucking furries.
Come on, let's get this done quickly!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finish - to bring something to an end or completion
  • Synonym: to accomplish - to achieve or complete successfully
