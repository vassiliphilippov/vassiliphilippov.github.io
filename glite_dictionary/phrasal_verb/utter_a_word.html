to utter a word

1. VERB

/ ˈʌt̬ɚ ə wɝːd / (AmE), / ˈʌtə ə wɜːd / (BrE)
to speak or say anything; typically used with a negative to mean to say nothing at all
say anything (often used with negatives)

Examples

She was so angry that she refused to utter a word during the whole meeting.
I didn't utter a word about our secret despite the pressure.
After the accident, he was too shocked to utter a word.

Synset Examples

He couldn't utter a word after the shocking news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: say a word - to speak; a similar phrase meaning to utter anything, often used in negative statements
