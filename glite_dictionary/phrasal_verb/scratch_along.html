to scratch along

1. VERB

/ ˈskrætʃ əˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈskrætʃ əˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
to manage to survive or get by with great difficulty, especially with limited resources
barely manage to survive or get by

Examples

After losing his job, he managed to scratch along on occasional part-time work.
Many families have to scratch along on very little during tough economic times.
She has been scratching along since her husband passed away, relying on small savings.

Synset Examples

I guess I can squeeze by on this lousy salary

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to get by - to manage with difficulty to make ends meet
  • Synonym: to squeeze by - to barely manage to cope, survive, or succeed
