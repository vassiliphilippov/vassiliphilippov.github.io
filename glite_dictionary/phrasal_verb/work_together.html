to work together

1. VERB

/ wɝːk təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / wɜːk təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to collaborate with one or more people in order to achieve a shared goal
collaborate for a shared goal

Examples

If we work together, we can finish this project much faster.
Teachers and parents need to work together to support children's learning.
The two countries agreed to work together on climate change solutions.

Synset Examples

They worked together to improve their community.
Let's work together for a better place to live.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cooperate - to act or work jointly toward the same end
  • Synonym: to collaborate - to work jointly on an activity or project
