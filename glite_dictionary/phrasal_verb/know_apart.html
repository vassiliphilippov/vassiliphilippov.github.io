to know apart

1. VERB

/ noʊ əˈpɑrt / (AmE), / nəʊ əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
to recognize or distinguish the difference between people or things
distinguish or recognize difference

Examples

Even their closest friends couldn't know apart the identical twins at first glance.
She has trained herself to know apart the authentic paintings from the forgeries.
It took me months before I could know apart the regional bird species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to tell apart - to recognize or distinguish the difference
  • Antonym: to confuse - to mistake one thing for another
