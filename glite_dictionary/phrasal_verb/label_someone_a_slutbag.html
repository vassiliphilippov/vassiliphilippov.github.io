to label someone a slutbag

1. VERB

/ ˈleɪbəl ˈsʌmwʌn ə ˈslʌtbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈleɪbəl ˈsʌmwən ə ˈslʌtbæɡ / (BrE)
to insult or stigmatize someone by calling them a 'slutbag', implying promiscuity or low moral character; highly offensive
insult someone as a 'slutbag' (offensive)
offensive vulgar

Examples

It's cruel and unfair to label someone a slutbag based on rumors.
She felt hurt after her classmates chose to label her a slutbag behind her back.
No one deserves to be labelled a slutbag for how they dress or behave.
