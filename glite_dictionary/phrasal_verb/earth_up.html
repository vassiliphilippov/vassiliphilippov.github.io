to earth up

1. VERB

/ ɝːθ ʌp / (AmE), / ɜːθ ʌp / (BrE)
to cover or block with earth or soil, especially as occurs after a landslide or for protective purposes
cover or block with earth

Examples

After the storm, debris and rocks earthed up the road leading to the village.
Heavy rains caused the hillside to collapse, earthing up several homes in its path.
They worked all day to clear the tunnel that had been earthed up after the landslide.
