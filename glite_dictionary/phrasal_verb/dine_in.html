to dine in

1. VERB

/ daɪn ɪn / (AmE), / daɪn ɪn / (BrE)
to eat a meal at home or at the place where it is prepared, rather than going to a restaurant
eat a meal at home

Examples

We decided to dine in tonight instead of going to a restaurant.
Because of the rain, they chose to dine in and cook something special together.
Many people prefer to dine in to save money and eat healthier food.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to eat out - to eat a meal away from home, especially at a restaurant
