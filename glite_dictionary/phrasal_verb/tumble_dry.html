to tumble dry

1. VERB

/ ˈtʌmbəl ˌdraɪ / (AmE), / ˈtʌmbəl ˌdraɪ / (BrE)
to dry (clothes or other items) by rotating them with hot air in a machine called a tumble dryer
dry in a machine using hot air

Examples

Please do not tumble dry this shirt as it may shrink.
The label says you can tumble dry these towels on a low setting.
After washing, I usually tumble dry my clothes for convenience.

Synset Examples

These fabrics are delicate and cannot be tumbled dry
