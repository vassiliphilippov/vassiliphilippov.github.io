to brighten up

1. VERB

/ ˈbraɪtn̩ ʌp / (AmE), / ˈbraɪtn̩ ʌp / (BrE)
to make or become more cheerful, lively, or attractive; to improve the mood or appearance of something or someone
make or become more cheerful or attractive

Examples

Fresh paint will brighten up the whole room and make it more inviting.
He tried to brighten up her day by bringing flowers.
As soon as the sun came out, the atmosphere seemed to brighten up instantly.

Synset Examples

Sandy always brightens up our gloomy days.
The flowers will brighten up any room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to brighten - to make or become more bright, cheerful, or lively
  • Synonym: to cheer up - to become or make someone happier
