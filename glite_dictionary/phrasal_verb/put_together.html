to put together

1. VERB

/ pʊt təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / pʊt təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to create or assemble something by combining different parts or elements
assemble or create from parts

Examples

We need to put together a plan before the meeting starts.
She put together the model airplane all by herself.
The committee was quickly put together to address the urgent issue.

Synset Examples

She pieced a quilt
He tacked together some verses
They set up a committee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to assemble - gather or fit together parts to make a whole
  • Synonym: construct - build or form by putting together parts
