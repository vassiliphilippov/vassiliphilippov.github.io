to bang down the door

1. VERB

/ bæŋ daʊn ðə dɔr / (AmE), / bæŋ daʊn ðə dɔː / (BrE)
to forcefully open a door by hitting or striking it, usually with great noise or violence
forcefully open a door by striking

Examples

The police had to bang down the door to reach the hostage.
When he heard the fire alarm, he rushed to bang down the door and rescue his neighbor.
She tried calling for help before deciding to bang down the door herself.

Synset Examples

He had to bang down the door to get inside.
In a panic, she banged down the door for help.
The police banged down the door during the raid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: break down the door - to force open a door, usually by breaking it; synonym for 'bang down the door'
