to clown around

1. VERB

/ klaʊn əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / klaʊn əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to behave in a silly or playful way, often making jokes or acting foolishly
behave in a silly or playful way

Examples

The students kept clowning around instead of getting to work.
Please stop clowning around and take this seriously.
Whenever he's with his friends, he tends to clown around a lot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fool around - to behave in a silly or playful manner
  • Synonym: to mess about - to behave in a silly or careless way (mainly British)
