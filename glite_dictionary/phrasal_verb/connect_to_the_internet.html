to connect to the internet

1. VERB

/ kəˈnɛkt tə ði ˈɪn.t̬ɚ.nɛt / (AmE), / kəˈnɛkt tə ði ˈɪn.tə.nɛt / (BrE)
to establish a link between a computer or device and the internet so that data can be sent or received
establish an online connection

Examples

Before you can download updates, you need to connect to the internet.
She couldn't connect to the internet because the Wi-Fi was down.
Many devices automatically connect to the internet when powered on.

Synset Examples

I remember using AOL to connect to the internet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go online - to start using the internet
  • Antonym: disconnect from the internet - to end an online connection
