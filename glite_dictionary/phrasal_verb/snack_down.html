to snack down

1. VERB

/ snæk daʊn / (AmE), / snæk daʊn / (BrE)
to eat snacks, especially in a quick or casual manner
eat snacks quickly or casually
informal

Examples

She likes to snack down on crackers while watching TV in the evening.
The kids snacked down on cookies as soon as they got home from school.
We were so hungry after the hike that we snacked down on everything in the bag.

Synset Examples

Let's snack down on some popcorn during the movie.
I love to snack down on fruit while working.
They decided to snack down on chips before dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to snack on - to eat a small portion of food, usually between meals
