to run along

1. VERB

/ rʌn əˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / rʌn əˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
to be in line with or to form a line alongside something
form a line along something

Examples

A fence runs along the border of the property, marking its limits.
Tall trees run along the riverbank, shading the water below.
Power lines run along the edge of the road for several miles.

Synset Examples

trees line the riverbank

Related Terms

  • Synonym: line - to form or mark a line along
