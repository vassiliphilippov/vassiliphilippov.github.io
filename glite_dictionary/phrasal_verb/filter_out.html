to filter out

1. VERB

/ ˈfɪl.tɚ aʊt / (AmE), / ˈfɪl.tər aʊt / (BrE)
to remove unwanted substances or elements from something by passing it through a filter
remove by filtering

Examples

The water treatment plant is designed to filter out harmful bacteria before the water reaches homes.
She used a fine mesh sieve to filter out the seeds from the juice.
New spam filters work to filter out unwanted emails automatically.

Synset Examples

filter out the impurities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filter - to pass a liquid, gas, light, or sound through a device to remove unwanted material
