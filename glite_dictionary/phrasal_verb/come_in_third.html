to come in third

1. VERB

/ kʌm ɪn θɜrd / (AmE), / kʌm ɪn θɜːd / (BrE)
to finish in third place in a competition, race, or ranking
finish in third place

Examples

She was thrilled to come in third in her first international marathon.
After a tough match, the team managed to come in third overall.
Despite his efforts, he only came in third in the spelling bee.

Synset Examples

He came in third, taking the number three spot in the race.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: come in first - to finish in first place
  • Degree Higher: come in second - to finish in second place
  • Degree Lower: come in fourth - to finish in fourth place
