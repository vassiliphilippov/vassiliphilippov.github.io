to play it safe

1. VERB

/ pleɪ ɪt seɪf / (AmE), / pleɪ ɪt seɪf / (BrE)
to avoid risks or potential danger by choosing the cautious or conventional option
avoid taking risks

Examples

Sometimes it's better to play it safe than to risk everything for a small reward.
She decided to play it safe and invest her money in government bonds.
If you don't know the answer, you can play it safe by choosing the most common option.

Synset Examples

No one likes a flattard who always plays it safe.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to take a chance - to do something adventurous or risky
