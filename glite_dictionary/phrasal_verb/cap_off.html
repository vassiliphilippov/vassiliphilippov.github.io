to cap off

1. VERB

/ kæp ɔf / (AmE), / kæp ɒf / (BrE)
to finish or complete something, especially with a notable or decisive action
finish or conclude decisively

Examples

The festival was capped off by a spectacular fireworks display.
He capped off his speech with a heartfelt thank you to his team.
We decided to cap off our trip with a visit to the famous museum.

Synset Examples

he capped off the meeting with a radical proposal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to conclude - to bring something to an end, especially formally
  • Synonym: end - to bring something to a finish
