to peel back

1. VERB

/ piːl bæk / (AmE), / piːl bæk / (BrE)
to remove a covering or layer by pulling it away, especially gradually or carefully
remove a covering by pulling

Examples

She carefully peeled back the label to read the instructions underneath.
When you peel back the skin of the banana, the fruit is ready to eat.
The mechanic peeled back the tape to expose the damaged wires.

Synset Examples

Make sure to peel back the tin tab before pouring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remove - to take something away or off
