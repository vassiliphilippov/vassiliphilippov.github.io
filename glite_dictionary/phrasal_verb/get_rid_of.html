to get rid of

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt rɪd əv / (AmE), / ɡɛt rɪd ɒv / (BrE)
to remove or eliminate something or someone unwanted or unnecessary

Examples

We are getting rid of unnecessary paperwork to streamline the office.
