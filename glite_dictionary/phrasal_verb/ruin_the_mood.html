to ruin the mood

1. VERB

/ ˈruːɪn ðə muːd / (AmE), / ˈruːɪn ðə muːd / (BrE)
to spoil or destroy the pleasant atmosphere or feeling in a situation
spoil the atmosphere or feeling

Examples

Her careless remark completely ruined the mood at the dinner party.
Don't play that depressing song; it'll just ruin the mood.
He apologized for ruining the mood with his negative comments.

Synset Examples

Negative Ned ruined the mood with his comments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to kill the mood - to spoil the atmosphere or feeling
