to pretty up

1. VERB

/ ˈprɛti ʌp / (AmE), / ˈprɛti ʌp / (BrE)
to make oneself or something look more attractive, especially by dressing up or applying makeup
make more attractive by grooming or styling

Examples

She likes to pretty up before going to a party.
They prettied up the room with fresh flowers and colorful pillows.
He spent an hour prettying up for the school dance.

Synset Examples

She dolled herself up for the night out with her friends

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to doll up - to dress or make oneself look smart or attractive
  • Synonym: to spruce up - to make something or someone look neater or tidier
