to go camping

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ ˈkæmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈkæmpɪŋ / (BrE)
to spend time outdoors, often overnight, sleeping in a tent or similar shelter for recreation
spend time outdoors sleeping in a tent

Examples

We plan to go camping in the mountains next weekend.
Every summer, my family likes to go camping by the lake.
She has never gone camping before, so she is very excited about the trip.

Synset Examples

They used to go camping every summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camp - to live temporarily in a tent or shelter outdoors
