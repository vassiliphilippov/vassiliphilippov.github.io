to poison your relationships

1. VERB

/ ˈpɔɪzən jɚ rɪˈleɪʃn̩ʃɪps / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪzən jɔː rɪˈleɪʃnʃɪps / (BrE)
to harm or damage your relationships, often gradually or by introducing negative feelings or behavior
harm or damage relationships

Examples

Gossip and mistrust can poison your relationships with friends and colleagues.
Holding grudges will only poison your relationships in the long run.
It's important to communicate openly, or you may unintentionally poison your relationships.

Synset Examples

Anger can poison your relationships over time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: damage your relationships - to cause harm to your relationships
  • Antonym: strengthen your relationships - to make your relationships stronger and healthier
