to blossom into

1. VERB

/ ˈblɑːsəm ˈɪntu / (AmE), / ˈblɒsəm ˈɪntu / (BrE)
to develop gradually into a particular quality, type, or state, especially something better or more mature
develop or mature into something

Examples

With dedication, his small business began to blossom into a thriving enterprise.
Over the summer, their casual meetings blossomed into a close relationship.
Many hope the reforms will blossom into lasting change for the community.

Synset Examples

With time, their friendship will blossom into something more.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: develop into - to become gradually or over time
