to add some visuals

1. VERB

/ æd səm ˈvɪʒuəlz / (AmE), / æd səm ˈvɪʒuəlz / (BrE)
to include images, graphics, or other visual elements to enhance or illustrate content
include images or graphics

Teachers often add some visuals to their lessons to help students understand the material.
