to slip into Danglish

1. VERB

/ slɪp ˈɪntu dæŋˌɡlɪʃ / (AmE), / slɪp ˈɪntjuː dæŋˌɡlɪʃ / (BrE)
to begin to unintentionally speak in a mixture of Danish and English
begin speaking mixed Danish-English

Examples

When speaking with her Danish friends, she sometimes slips into Danglish without realizing it.
It's easy to slip into Danglish if you're fluent in both languages.
During the meeting, he accidentally slipped into Danglish while explaining a technical concept.

Synset Examples

She often slips into Danglish when talking to her family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Danglish - a mixture of Danish and English languages

Etymology

Blend of 'Danish' and 'English', with 'slip into' meaning to start doing something unintentionally.
