to leave alone

1. VERB

/ liv əˈloʊn / (AmE), / liːv əˈləʊn / (BrE)
to not disturb, alter, or interfere with someone or something; to refrain from taking action affecting them or it.
not disturb or interfere with

Examples

If you see a wild animal in the park, it's best to leave alone and not approach it.
She decided to leave alone her old diaries, knowing some memories are best left untouched.
Sometimes it's wiser to leave alone issues that don't directly affect you.

Synset Examples

leave it as is
leave the young fawn alone
leave the flowers that you see in the park behind

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ignore - to pay no attention to
  • Antonym: to disturb - to interrupt or bother
