to kick it

1. VERB

/ ˈkɪk ɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɪk ɪt / (BrE)
(informal) To hang out or relax in a casual and informal way, often with friends.
relax or hang out informally
informal

Examples

On Friday night, we decided to just kick it at my place and watch movies.
After finals, everyone wanted to kick it and forget about school for a while.
Sometimes it's nice to kick it with good friends and not worry about anything.

Synset Examples

Let's just kick it and relax this weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to hang out - to spend time relaxing or socializing
  • Synonym: chill - to relax or take it easy
