to clothe oneself in layers

1. VERB

/ kloʊð wʌnˈsɛlf ɪn ˈleɪərz / (AmE), / kləʊð wʌnˈsɛlf ɪn ˈleɪəz / (BrE)
to dress by putting on multiple items of clothing, one over another, usually for warmth
put on several layers of clothing

Examples

He always clothes himself in layers before heading out for a winter hike.
It's smart to clothe oneself in layers during unpredictable spring weather.
She forgot to clothe herself in layers and shivered the whole evening.

Synset Examples

He decided to clothe himself in layers for the cold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dress in layers - to put on several garments, one over another, for warmth
