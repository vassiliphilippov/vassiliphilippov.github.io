to shout for help

1. VERB

/ ʃaʊt fər hɛlp / (AmE), / ʃaʊt fə hɛlp / (BrE)
to loudly call out requesting assistance, especially in an urgent situation
call out loudly for assistance

Examples

She had to shout for help because nobody was nearby to assist her.
I heard someone shouting for help in the alley last night.
He shouted for help when he realized he was trapped in the elevator.

Synset Examples

She decided to shout for help when she got lost.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cry for help - to call out loudly for assistance
