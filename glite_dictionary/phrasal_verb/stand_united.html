to stand united

1. VERB

/ stænd juˈnaɪtɪd / (AmE), / stænd juˈnaɪtɪd / (BrE)
to come together and act in solidarity, supporting a common cause or goal
act together in solidarity

Examples

The workers decided to stand united in demanding better wages from their employer.
In times of national crisis, citizens must stand united to overcome challenges.
If we stand united, we can achieve far more than by working alone.

Synset Examples

Many stood united against fascism in the protest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stand together - to act or remain united in support of each other
  • Antonym: stand divided - to be separated and lacking solidarity, often with conflicting interests
