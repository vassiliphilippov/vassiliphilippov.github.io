to pee off

1. VERB

/ piː ɑf / (AmE), / piː ɒf / (BrE)
(of a person, action, or thing) to irritate or annoy someone.
irritate or annoy someone
informal

Examples

His careless mistakes really pee off his coworkers.
Try not to pee off your boss by being late again.
She was completely peed off by the delays in service.

Synset Examples

His rude comments always seem to pee her off.
Don't let his attitude pee you off too much.
The constant noise from the construction site really peeves me off.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to annoy - to make someone a little angry
  • Degree Higher: to enrage - to make someone extremely angry
