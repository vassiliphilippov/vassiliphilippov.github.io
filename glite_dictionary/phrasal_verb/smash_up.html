to smash up

1. VERB

/ smæʃ ʌp / (AmE), / smæʃ ʌp / (BrE)
to damage or destroy something violently, especially by breaking it into pieces
damage or destroy violently

Examples

The vandals smashed up the old phone booth beyond repair.
She accidentally smashed up her favorite vase while cleaning.
The crash smashed up the front of the car badly.

Synset Examples

The teenager banged up the car of his mother

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wreck - to damage or destroy something completely
  • Synonym: to destroy - to damage something so badly that it no longer exists or cannot be repaired
