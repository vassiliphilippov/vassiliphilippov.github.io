to limit yourself to

1. VERB

/ ˈlɪmɪt jɚˌsɛlf tu / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪt jɔːˌsɛlf tʊ / (BrE)
to restrict your actions, choices, or experiences to a particular thing or set
restrict your actions or choices

Examples

Don't limit yourself to just the traditional solutions; try something creative.
When ordering at the buffet, he didn't limit himself to salad and chose a variety of dishes.
If you limit yourself to one perspective, you might miss important details.

Synset Examples

You should not limit yourself to just one option.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restrict yourself to - to confine your choices or activities to a set range
