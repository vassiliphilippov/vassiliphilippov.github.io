to be with it

1. VERB

/ bi ˈwɪð ɪt / (AmE), / bi ˈwɪð ɪt / (BrE)
to be aware of and able to understand modern ideas, styles, or situations; to be well-informed or up-to-date
be well-informed or up-to-date
informal

Examples

Grandpa tries hard to be with it and keep up with new technology.
If you want to succeed in marketing, you need to be with it and understand current trends.
I don't really be with it when it comes to social media apps.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: be out of it - not aware of current trends or information
  • Synonym: be in the know - to have special knowledge or information
