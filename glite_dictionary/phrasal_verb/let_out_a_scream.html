to let out a scream

1. VERB

/ lɛt aʊt ə skrim / (AmE), / lɛt aʊt ə skriːm / (BrE)
to emit a loud, sudden cry, typically because of fear, pain, or surprise
emit a sudden loud cry

Examples

She let out a scream when the door slammed shut behind her.
He let out a scream of excitement on winning the competition.
The audience let out a scream as the magician disappeared from the stage.

Synset Examples

She let out a scream when she saw the spider.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scream - a loud, high-pitched cry expressing extreme emotion
