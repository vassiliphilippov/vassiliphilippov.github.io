to get the job done

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ðə dʒɑb dʌn / (AmE), / ɡɛt ðə dʒɒb dʌn / (BrE)
to complete a task or achieve the desired result, especially effectively or efficiently
complete a task successfully

Examples

No matter how difficult the situation, she always gets the job done.
We don't need fancy equipment—as long as it gets the job done, it's fine.
The new manager made sure everyone knew their role so the team could get the job done.

Synset Examples

He’s a pro and always gets the job done right.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to accomplish - to achieve or complete successfully
  • Antonym: to fail - to not succeed in completing a task
