to siphon gas

1. VERB

/ ˈsaɪfən ɡæs / (AmE), / ˈsaɪf(ə)n ɡas / (BrE)
to transfer gasoline from one container or vehicle to another by means of a siphon tube, often by sucking it through a hose
transfer gasoline using a siphon

Examples

He used a plastic tube to siphon gas from the tank into a container.
After running out of fuel, they tried to siphon gas from a neighbor's car.
Thieves sometimes siphon gas from parked vehicles late at night.

Synset Examples

I had to siphon the gas from the old car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: syphon - the act of transferring a liquid through a tube using air pressure
