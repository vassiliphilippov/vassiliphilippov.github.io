to mark out

1. VERB

/ mɑrk aʊt / (AmE), / mɑːk aʊt / (BrE)
to set boundaries for or clearly delimit an area, object, or scope
set boundaries or delimit

Examples

The surveyors marked out the land before construction began.
She marks out spaces for the new garden beds with string.
The coach marked out the playing field using cones and chalk.

Synset Examples

mark out the territory

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to demarcate - to set the limits or boundaries of something
  • Synonym: to define - to mark the limits or outline of
  • Antonym: blur - to make the limits or boundaries of something less clear
