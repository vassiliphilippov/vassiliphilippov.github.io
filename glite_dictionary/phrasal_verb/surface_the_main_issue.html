to surface the main issue

1. VERB

/ ˈsɜːrfɪs ðə meɪn ˈɪʃuː / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfɪs ðə meɪn ˈɪʃuː / (BrE)
to draw attention to or highlight the most important problem or topic in a discussion
bring the most important issue to attention

Examples

It's time to surface the main issue so we can make real progress in the meeting.
The facilitator encouraged participants to surface the main issue early in the discussion.
By surfacing the main issue, we avoided wasting time on less important topics.

Synset Examples

We need to surface the main issues in this meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to bring up - to mention or introduce a topic for discussion
  • Synonym: highlight - to emphasize or make something stand out
