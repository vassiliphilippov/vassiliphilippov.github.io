to hit home

1. VERB

/ hɪt hoʊm / (AmE), / hɪt həʊm / (BrE)
to have a strong effect on someone because it is particularly relevant or familiar to them; to be fully understood or felt
to have a strong, relevant personal impact

Examples

Her words about the importance of health really hit home after my recent illness.
When he lost his job, the reality of the situation finally hit home.
It wasn't until she saw the empty room that it hit home he had left for good.

Synset Examples

I hope this message hits home!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to strike home - to be fully felt or understood
