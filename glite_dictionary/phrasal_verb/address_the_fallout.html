to address the fallout

1. VERB

/ əˈdrɛs ði ˈfaʊtˌlɔt / (AmE), / əˈdrɛs ði ˈfaʊtˌlɔːt / (BrE)
to respond to or manage the negative consequences or aftermath of an event or situation
manage consequences of an event

Examples

After the scandal, the company held a press conference to address the fallout.
The manager had to address the fallout of the decision with the staff.
It took months for the city officials to address the fallout from the budget cuts.

Synset Examples

Politicians scrambled to address the fallout from Manuelgate.
It's easier to zuke rather than deal with the fallout.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deal with the fallout - to handle or confront the consequences of an event
