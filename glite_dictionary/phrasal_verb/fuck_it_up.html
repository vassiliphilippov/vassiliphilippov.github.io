to fuck it up

1. VERB

/ fʌk ɪt ʌp / (AmE), / fʌk ɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to do something badly or ruin it completely, often by making a mistake
do something very badly; ruin it
vulgar informal

Examples

Be careful with that machine, or you might fuck it up irreparably.
I really fucked it up during my interview by saying the wrong thing.
She was afraid of fucking it up again after last time's disaster.

Synset Examples

If you don't follow the instructions, you might fuck it up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to mess up - to do something poorly or make a mistake that harms the result
  • Synonym: to screw up - to make a serious mistake or cause something to fail
