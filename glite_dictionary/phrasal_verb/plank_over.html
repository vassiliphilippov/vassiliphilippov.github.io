to plank over

1. VERB

/ plæŋk ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / plæŋk ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to cover (a surface) with planks of wood
cover with planks

Examples

After the flood, they had to plank over the damaged bridge for safe passage.
Workers began to plank over the muddy pathway to create a temporary sidewalk.
The old well was planked over to prevent accidents.

Synset Examples

The streets were planked

Related Terms

  • Synonym: board over - to cover with boards
