to renew your rego

1. VERB

/ rɪˈnuː jʊr ˈreɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / rɪˈnjuː jɔː ˈriːɡəʊ / (BrE)
to update or extend the official registration of a vehicle, typically required annually by law in Australia or New Zealand
update vehicle registration (Aus/NZ)
informal british

Examples

You must renew your rego each year to avoid fines.
She forgot to renew her rego and received a penalty notice.
It's easy to renew your rego online using the transport department's website.

Synset Examples

Don’t forget to renew your rego before it expires.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: register your car - formally record a vehicle with authorities
  • Synonym: renew your license - to update or extend the validity of a license

Etymology

Shortened form of 'registration'; 'rego' is Australian/New Zealand slang for 'registration'.
