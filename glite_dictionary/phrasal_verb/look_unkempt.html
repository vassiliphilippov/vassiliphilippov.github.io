to look unkempt

1. VERB

/ lʊk ʌnˈkɛmpt / (AmE), / lʊk ʌnˈkɛmpt / (BrE)
to appear messy, untidy, or disheveled in one's appearance
to appear messy or disheveled

Examples

After working all night, he began to look unkempt and exhausted.
If you don't iron your clothes, you'll look unkempt at the interview.
Her hair was tangled, making her look unkempt despite her best efforts.

Synset Examples

He looked unkempt in that wrinkled shirt and messy hair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appear disheveled - to seem messy or untidy
