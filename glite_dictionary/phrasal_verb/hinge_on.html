to hinge on

1. VERB

/ hɪndʒ ɑn / (AmE), / hɪndʒ ɒn / (BrE)
(of a plan, situation, or outcome) to depend entirely on or be determined by something else.
depend on; be contingent upon

Examples

The success of the project will hinge on securing enough funding.
Her decision hinges on whether she gets the job offer.
Our future plans hinged on the outcome of the negotiations.

Synset Examples

The outcomes rides on the results of the election
Your grade will depend on your homework

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to depend on - to rely on or be determined by something
  • Synonym: to rest on - (of a result or situation) to rely on something as a base
  • Antonym: be independent of - not to depend on something

Etymology

From the noun 'hinge', referring to something that allows movement or depends upon, + 'on'.
