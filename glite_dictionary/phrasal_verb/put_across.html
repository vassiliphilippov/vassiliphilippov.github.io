to put across

1. VERB

/ pʊt əˈkrɑs / (AmE), / pʊt əˈkrɒs / (BrE)
to communicate an idea, opinion, or message effectively so that it is understood by others
communicate effectively
informal

Examples

She managed to put across her proposal clearly during the meeting.
He struggled to put across his point in such a noisy environment.
The teacher used stories to put across complicated ideas to her students.

Synset Examples

Please communicate this message to all employees
pass along the good news

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to communicate - share or exchange information, news, or ideas
