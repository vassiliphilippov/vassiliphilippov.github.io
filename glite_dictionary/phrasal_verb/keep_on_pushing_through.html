to keep on pushing through

1. VERB

/ kip ɑn ˈpʊʃɪŋ θru / (AmE), / kiːp ɒn ˈpʊʃɪŋ θruː / (BrE)
to continue to persist or make an effort, especially in the face of difficulty or adversity
persist despite challenges

Examples

Despite exhaustion, she knew she had to keep on pushing through to finish the marathon.
When obstacles arise, I try to keep on pushing through instead of quitting.
He always keeps on pushing through no matter how tough things get.

Synset Examples

He decided to keep on pushing through the tough times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to persevere - to continue trying to do or achieve something despite difficulties
  • Antonym: to give up - to stop trying; to surrender
