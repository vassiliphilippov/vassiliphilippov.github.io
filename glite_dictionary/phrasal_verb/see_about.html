to see about

1. VERB

/ si əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / si əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to deal with, arrange, or make preparations for something; to take responsibility for handling a matter
attend to or arrange something

Examples

I'll see about getting us a table at the restaurant.
Could you see about fixing the broken window this afternoon?
Before we leave, I need to see about the travel arrangements.

Synset Examples

Could you see about lunch?
I must attend to this matter
She took care of this business

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to attend to - to deal with or handle something
  • Synonym: to take care of - to be responsible for something or someone
