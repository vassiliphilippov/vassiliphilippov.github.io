to whore oneself out

1. VERB

/ hɔɹ wʊnˈsɛlf aʊt / (AmE), / hɔː wʊnˈself aʊt / (BrE)
to compromise one's principles or dignity for personal gain, especially for money or publicity, in a way regarded as degrading
degrade oneself for gain
offensive vulgar

Examples

She refused to whore herself out just to get a promotion at work.
Some celebrities will whore themselves out for any endorsement deal they can find.
He admitted he'd whored himself out in his early career for a shot at fame.

Synset Examples

He’s not afraid to whore himself out for attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prostitute oneself - to offer or use one's talents or abilities in a way that is considered morally or ethically degrading, for personal gain
