to settle a disagreement

1. VERB

/ ˈsɛt̬l ə dɪsəˈɡriːmənt / (AmE), / ˈsɛtl ə dɪsəˈɡriːmənt / (BrE)
to resolve a dispute or come to an agreement after a conflict
resolve a dispute; come to agreement

Examples

The two companies were able to settle a disagreement through negotiation.
It can sometimes take time to settle a disagreement between friends.
A mediator was called in to help settle a disagreement among the staff.

Synset Examples

We need to settle this disagreement peacefully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resolve a dispute - to find a solution to a disagreement or argument
