to beat off the competition

1. VERB

/ biːt ɔf ðə kəmˈpɪt̬ʃən / (AmE), / biːt ɒf ðə kəmˈpɪtɪʃən / (BrE)
to succeed in defeating competitors or rivals in a contest or competition
defeat rivals in a competition

Examples

Last year, she managed to beat off the competition and claim first prize.
After months of preparation, the team finally beat off the competition to win the championship.
The company beat off the competition and secured the lucrative contract.

Synset Examples

He had to beat off the competition to win the contract.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outcompete - to surpass or defeat others in competition
  • Antonym: lose to - to be defeated by someone in a competition
