to overflowing with

1. VERB

/ ˌoʊvɚˈfloʊɪŋ wɪθ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈfləʊɪŋ wɪð / (BrE)
completely filled with or containing an excessive amount of something
containing an excessive amount

Examples

The garden was overflowing with colorful flowers in the spring.
Her mind was overflowing with new ideas after the conference.
The stadium was overflowing with excited fans before the concert began.

Synset Examples

The pizza table was overflowing with different toppings.
His inbox was overflowing with unread messages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brimming with - filled to the top with; full of
  • Antonym: devoid of - lacking or empty of
