to deffo check out

1. VERB

/ ˈdɛfoʊ tʃɛk aʊt / (AmE), / ˈdɛfəʊ tʃɛk aʊt / (BrE)
To definitely look at or visit something; to be sure to see or try something.
certainly visit or try
informal british

Examples

You should deffo check out that new café if you’re looking for great coffee.
I’ll deffo check out that movie you recommended.
Next time you’re in London, deffo check out Camden Market.

Synset Examples

You should deffo check out that new café.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to check out - to look at or examine something or someone

Etymology

"Deffo" is a British informal shortening of "definitely" (20th c.).
