to go to war

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ tə wɔr / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə wɔː / (BrE)
to begin fighting in a war; to commence hostilities between nations or groups
begin fighting in a war

Examples

Several countries threatened to go to war over the disputed territory.
If they attack our allies, we will have no choice but to go to war.
After years of rising tensions, the two nations finally went to war.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make war - to start or engage in warfare
  • Antonym: to make peace - to end hostilities or war
