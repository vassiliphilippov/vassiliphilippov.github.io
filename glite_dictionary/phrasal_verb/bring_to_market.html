to bring to market

1. VERB

/ brɪŋ tə ˈmɑrkɪt / (AmE), / brɪŋ tə ˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
to make a product available for sale to the public
make available for sale

Examples

The company plans to bring to market several new electric vehicles next year.
After years of research, the scientist was finally able to bring to market her innovative medical device.
It can take months or even years to bring to market a new pharmaceutical drug.

Synset Examples

He learned to bring his own hide to market if he wanted success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: launch - to introduce a new product or service to the market
  • Antonym: withdraw from market - to stop offering a product for sale
