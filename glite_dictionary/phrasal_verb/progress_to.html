to progress to

1. VERB

/ prəˈɡrɛs tu / (AmE), / prəˈɡrɛs tʊ / (BrE)
to achieve a particular level, stage, or goal after making advances or improvements
advance to a higher level or goal

Examples

After two years in secondary school, he progressed to university.
If you train regularly, you'll progress to the advanced class sooner.
Only the best candidates progress to the final round of interviews.

Synset Examples

make the first team
We made it!
She may not make the grade

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advance to - to move forward to a new level or stage
  • Antonym: to fall behind - to fail to keep up with progress
