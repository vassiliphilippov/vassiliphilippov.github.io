to get het up

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt hɛt ʌp / (AmE), / ɡɛt hɛt ʌp / (BrE)
to become overly excited, angry, or agitated, especially unnecessarily
become excited or angry

Examples

Please don't get het up over something so trivial.
She tends to get het up whenever there's a delay in the schedule.
It's easy to get het up about politics these days.

Synset Examples

There's no need to get het up about the small details.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get worked up - to become agitated or upset
  • Antonym: to calm down - to become less agitated or less angry

Etymology

From 'het', a dialect variant of 'heated', used in the sense of emotional agitation.
