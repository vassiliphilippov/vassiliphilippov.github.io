to argue over

1. VERB

/ ˈɑrɡju ˌoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡjuː ˌəʊvə / (BrE)
to have a disagreement or dispute about a particular subject or thing
dispute about something

Examples

The siblings often argue over what to watch on television.
They began to argue over who should pay the bill at dinner.
Politicians frequently argue over how to allocate public funds.

Synset Examples

They argued over potatoes and point all evening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quarrel - to have an angry argument or disagreement
  • Antonym: to agree - to have the same opinion or not disagree
