to reminisce about

1. VERB

/ ˌrɛmɪˈnɪs əˌbaʊt / (AmE), / ˌrɛmɪˈnɪs əˌbaʊt / (BrE)
to recall or talk about past experiences with pleasure or fondness
recall past experiences fondly

Examples

They often reminisce about the adventures they had during their college years.
After dinner, Sarah and her grandparents liked to reminisce about family holidays.
He would reminisce about his childhood every time he visited his old neighborhood.

Synset Examples

He often reminisced about his childhood in Amerika.
He reminisced about the stories his pappy told him.
He reminisced about his adventures in disna with friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to remember - to recall or bring to mind an experience or fact
  • Antonym: to forget - to fail to remember something

Etymology

From Latin *reminisci* (to remember), with 'about' added to specify the object of recollection.
