to shoot craps

1. VERB

/ ʃut kræps / (AmE), / ʃuːt kræps / (BrE)
to play the game of craps, a gambling game involving the throwing of dice
play the dice gambling game craps

Examples

Every Friday night, the group would gather in the basement to shoot craps for fun.
He lost all his cash after deciding to shoot craps at the casino table.
They were caught shooting craps in the alley behind the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: play craps - to participate in the game of craps
