to cut out

1. VERB

/ kʌt aʊt / (AmE), / kʌt aʊt / (BrE)
(of an engine, machine, etc.) to stop working suddenly.
stop operating suddenly (machine, engine)

Examples

The engine began to cut out as we drove up the hill.
If your radio suddenly cuts out, check the power cord first.
The air conditioner cut out during the hottest part of the day.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to break down - (of machinery) to stop working
