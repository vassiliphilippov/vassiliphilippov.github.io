to clock out

1. VERB

/ klɑk aʊt / (AmE), / klɒk aʊt / (BrE)
to record the time you leave work, especially by using a special machine
register leaving work

Examples

Please remember to clock out before you leave the building.
She accidentally forgot to clock out at the end of her shift yesterday.
After a long day, he was relieved to finally clock out and go home.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to clock in - to register the time you arrive at work, especially by using a machine
