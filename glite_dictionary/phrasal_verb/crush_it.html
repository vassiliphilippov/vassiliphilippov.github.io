to crush it

1. VERB

/ krʌʃ ɪt / (AmE), / krʌʃ ɪt / (BrE)
to perform extremely well, especially in a competitive or challenging situation
perform extremely well
informal slang

Examples

If we work together, I know we can crush it in the finals.
She always crushes it during her presentations at work.
They crushed it on the sales goals this year.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: blow it - to fail at something
