to whoop it up

1. VERB

/ wup ɪt ʌp / (AmE), / wuːp ɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to celebrate exuberantly and noisily, especially by shouting, drinking, or partying
celebrate noisily, party boisterously
informal

Examples

After the big win, fans gathered downtown to whoop it up late into the night.
When their exams were finally over, the students decided to whoop it up at their favorite bar.
Every New Year's Eve, people all over the world whoop it up to welcome the coming year.

Synset Examples

The members of the wedding party made merry all night
Let's whoop it up — the boss is gone!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to make merry - to celebrate and enjoy oneself
  • Synonym: to paint the town red - to go out and celebrate extravagantly

2. VERB

/ wup ɪt ʌp / (AmE), / wuːp ɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to celebrate enthusiastically or enjoy oneself in a loud and lively way
celebrate loudly; have a good time
informal

Examples

After the big win, the fans began to whoop it up in the streets.
Every Friday night, the bar crowd really whoops it up until closing time.
She invited everyone over so they could whoop it up on New Year's Eve.

Synset Examples

We’re going to whoop it up for her birthday.
They really know how to whoop it up at festivals.
Let’s whoop it up at the party tonight!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to celebrate - to observe a day or event with ceremonies or festivities
  • Synonym: party - to enjoy oneself at a gathering or event
