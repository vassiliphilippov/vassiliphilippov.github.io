to let it go

1. VERB

/ lɛt ɪt ɡoʊ / (AmE), / lɛt ɪt ɡəʊ / (BrE)
to choose not to react to something or not to take action, especially in response to a provocation or annoyance
choose not to react or act

Examples

She wanted to argue, but decided to let it go for the sake of peace.
When he insulted me, I just let it go and walked away.
It's better to let it go than to dwell on small mistakes.

Synset Examples

He thought of a reply but let it go

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ignore - to deliberately pay no attention to something
  • Antonym: to react - to respond to something with words or action
