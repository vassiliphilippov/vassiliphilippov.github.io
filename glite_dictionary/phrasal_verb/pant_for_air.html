to pant for air

1. VERB

/ pænt fər ɛr / (AmE), / pænt fə eə / (BrE)
to breathe quickly and heavily, usually after physical exertion or due to a lack of air
breathe heavily after exertion

Examples

She began to pant for air after climbing the steep hill so quickly.
He was panting for air after finishing the last lap of the pool.
As soon as the runners crossed the finish line, they stopped to pant for air.

Synset Examples

After the run, I had to pant for air.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gasp for breath - try to catch one's breath noisily, often after effort
