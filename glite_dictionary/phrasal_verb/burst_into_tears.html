to burst into tears

1. VERB

/ bɝst ˈɪntu tɪrz / (AmE), / bɜːst ˈɪntuː tɪəz / (BrE)
to suddenly begin crying, often as an emotional response
suddenly begin to cry

Examples

When she heard the bad news, she burst into tears right there in the office.
The little boy burst into tears after dropping his ice cream cone.
He managed to stay calm until the end, but then he burst into tears of relief.

Synset Examples

Upon receiving the gift, she burst into tears of joy.
She burst into tears at the sad movie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to break down - to lose control of one's emotions and start crying
