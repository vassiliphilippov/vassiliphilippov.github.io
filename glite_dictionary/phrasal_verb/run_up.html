to run up

1. VERB

/ rʌn ʌp / (AmE), / rʌn ʌp / (BrE)
to make (something, especially a garment) quickly by sewing
make quickly by sewing

Examples

She managed to run up a dress for the party in just a few hours.
The tailor will run up a pair of trousers while you wait.
Whenever she needs new curtains, she simply runs up some with her sewing machine.
