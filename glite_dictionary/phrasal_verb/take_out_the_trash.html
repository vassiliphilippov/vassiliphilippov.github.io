to take out the trash

1. VERB

/ teɪk aʊt ðə træʃ / (AmE), / teɪk aʊt ðə træʃ / (BrE)
to remove garbage from a place, typically to put it in an outside bin for collection
remove garbage from a place

Examples

She asked him to take out the trash before it started to smell.
Every Tuesday night, I take out the trash for collection early the next morning.
Who usually takes out the trash in your household?

Synset Examples

He forgot to take out the trash before the pickup.
Please remember to take out the trash tonight.
Don't forget to throw out the trash before you leave.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: throw out the trash - to dispose of garbage; synonymous with 'take out the trash'
