to color in

1. VERB

/ ˈkʌlər ɪn / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ɪn / (BrE)
to add color to a drawing or design, especially by filling in areas with crayons, markers, or paint
to fill a drawing with color

Examples

The children happily colored in the pictures in their activity books.
She asked him to color in the shapes using only red and blue pencils.
The teacher instructed the class to color in the map of Europe for homework.

Synset Examples

The child colored the drawings
Fall colored the trees
colorize black and white film

Related Terms

  • Synonym: color - to give something color; to paint or draw on
