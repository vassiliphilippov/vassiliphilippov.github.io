to say again

1. VERB

/ seɪ əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / seɪ əˈɡɛn / (BrE)
to repeat something that has just been said; to state again for clarification or emphasis.
to repeat what was just said

Examples

Could you say again what time the meeting starts?
I'm sorry, I didn't catch that—please say again.
The operator asked him to say again his name and phone number.

Synset Examples

Please say again your address for confirmation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repeat - to say or do something again
