to go shitnuts

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ ˈʃɪtnʌts / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈʃɪtnʌts / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) To become extremely angry or upset, often suddenly and without restraint.
become extremely angry or upset (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar

Examples

If you tell him the truth, he might go shitnuts and start yelling at everyone.
She went shitnuts when she saw the mess in the kitchen.
I'm warning you, don't go shitnuts over something so trivial.

Synset Examples

You can't go shitnuts over every little mistake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to lose it - to become very angry or emotional
  • Synonym: to lose one's temper - to suddenly become angry
  • Antonym: to keep one's cool - to remain calm in a difficult situation
