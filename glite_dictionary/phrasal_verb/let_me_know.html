to let me know

1. VERB

/ lɛt mi noʊ / (AmE), / lɛt mi nəʊ / (BrE)
to tell or inform someone about something, especially when new information becomes available or a situation changes
inform or update someone

Examples

Let me know if you need any help with your project.
Please let me know as soon as you get the results.
Can you let me know when you are ready to leave?

Synset Examples

Let me know when you finish with that book.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to inform - to tell someone about something
  • Synonym: to notify - to formally inform someone about something
