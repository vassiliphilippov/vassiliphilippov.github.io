to perk up

1. VERB

/ pɝk ʌp / (AmE), / pɜːk ʌp / (BrE)
to become or make more lively, cheerful, or energetic, especially after feeling tired or low
become more lively or energetic

Examples

A cup of coffee in the morning always helps me perk up.
You seemed tired earlier, but you really perked up after lunch.
The dog immediately perked up when he heard his owner's voice.

Synset Examples

I picked up after a nap

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to brighten up - to become more cheerful or lively
  • Synonym: to liven up - to become or make more lively
  • Antonym: to depress - to make someone feel less happy or energetic
Source: WordNet
