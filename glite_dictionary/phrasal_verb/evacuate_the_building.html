to evacuate the building

1. VERB

/ ɪˈvæk.ju.eɪt ðə bəˈbɪl.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈvæk.ju.eɪt ðə ˈbɪl.dɪŋ / (BrE)
to remove people from a building, especially for safety reasons during an emergency
remove people from a building for safety

Examples

After the alarm sounded, everyone was told to evacuate the building as quickly as possible.
Firefighters ordered the residents to evacuate the building due to the gas leak.
In an emergency, it is important to know the proper way to evacuate the building.

Synset Examples

They had to evacuate the building during the fire drill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to evacuate - to remove people from a place for safety
