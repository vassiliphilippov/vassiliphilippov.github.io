to act like an eshay

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk æn ˈɛʃeɪ / (AmE), / ækt laɪk æn ˈɛʃeɪ / (BrE)
to behave in a flashy, brash, or showy manner associated with the 'eshay' youth subculture, often involving displays of status or street attitude
behave flashily as an 'eshay'
slang

Examples

Everyone at the party noticed when Jake began to act like an eshay by flashing his designer sneakers.
Some teenagers act like an eshay to impress their friends at school.
He was acting like an eshay, speaking loudly and drawing attention to himself.

Synset Examples

He acted like an eshay, showing off his flashy phone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to show off - to behave in a way intended to attract attention or admiration; to display boastfully

Etymology

From Australian English slang 'eshay', referring to a subculture known for brash and ostentatious behavior.
