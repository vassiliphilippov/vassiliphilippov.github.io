to pelt with snowballs

1. VERB

/ pɛlt wɪð ˈsnoʊˌbɔlz / (AmE), / pɛlt wɪð ˈsnəʊˌbɔːlz / (BrE)
to throw snowballs at someone or something repeatedly and forcefully
throw snowballs at (repeatedly)

Examples

The children pelted with snowballs anyone who dared to walk through the playground.
After the first snowstorm, neighbors gathered to pelt with snowballs the statue in the park for fun.
Teenagers laughingly pelted with snowballs the windows of their friend's house during the winter holiday.

Synset Examples

They decided to pelt the intruder with snowballs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pelt - to attack by repeatedly throwing things
