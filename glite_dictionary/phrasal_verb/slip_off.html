to slip off

1. VERB

/ slɪp ɑf / (AmE), / slɪp ɒf / (BrE)
to remove (clothing or an object) quickly and easily
remove something quickly and easily

Examples

She slipped off her shoes as soon as she entered the room.
The actor slipped off his costume during the quick costume change backstage.
He quietly slipped off his watch before going into the pool.

Synset Examples

She slipped off her jacket

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to take off - to remove anything that is worn or carried, especially clothes
