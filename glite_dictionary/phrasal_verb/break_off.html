to break off

1. VERB

/ breɪk ɔf / (AmE), / breɪk ɒf / (BrE)
to remove or detach a small piece from something by breaking it
remove a small piece by breaking

Examples

She tried to break off a piece of chocolate without making a mess.
Be careful not to break off any pieces of the sculpture when moving it.
A corner of the tile will break off if you drop it.

Synset Examples

chip the glass
chip a tooth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chip - to break a small piece from something, especially from a hard material
