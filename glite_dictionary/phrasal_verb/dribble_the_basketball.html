to dribble the basketball

1. VERB

/ ˈdrɪbəl ðə ˈbæskətˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈdrɪbəl ðə ˈbɑːskɪtbɔːl / (BrE)
to repeatedly bounce a basketball on the floor while moving, as part of playing the game
bounce the basketball while moving

Examples

She practiced every day so she could dribble the basketball quickly down the court.
You need to dribble the basketball with control to avoid losing it to your opponent.
He learned how to dribble the basketball with both his left and right hand.

Synset Examples

He learned to dribble the basketball with both hands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dribble - to bounce a ball continuously with one hand while moving
