to stay off

1. VERB

/ steɪ ɔf / (AmE), / steɪ ɒf / (BrE)
to avoid entering or walking onto a particular place or surface
not enter or walk onto (a place)

Examples

Please stay off the construction site for your safety.
The teacher told the students to stay off the freshly painted floor.
If you stay off the wet grass, it will dry faster.

Synset Examples

keep off the grass
stay off the premises

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to keep off - to avoid walking or stepping onto something
