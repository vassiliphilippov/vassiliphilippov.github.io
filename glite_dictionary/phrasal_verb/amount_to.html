to amount to

1. VERB

/ əˈmaʊnt tu / (AmE), / əˈmaʊnt tʊ / (BrE)
to be equal to or the same as something, especially when describing a result or consequence
be the same as; be equivalent to

Examples

Failing to act now could amount to negligence on their part.
His words amounted to a threat, though he never said it directly.
Such behavior could amount to a breach of trust.

Synset Examples

Her action amounted to a rebellion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be equivalent to - to be the same in value, meaning, or effect
  • Antonym: be less than - to not reach the level or importance of
