to put through

1. VERB

/ pʊt θru / (AmE), / pʊt θruː / (BrE)
to connect a telephone caller to the person they wish to speak to
connect by telephone

Examples

Could you put through this call to Mr. Stevens, please?
The receptionist put through my call to the accounting department quickly.
I'll try to put through your call now; please hold the line.

Synset Examples

the operator put a call through to Rio

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to connect - to join two people by telephone; to link electronically

/ pʊt θru / (AmE), / pʊt θruː / (BrE)
