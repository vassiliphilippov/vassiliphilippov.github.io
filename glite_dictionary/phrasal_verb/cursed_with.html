to cursed with

1. VERB

/ ˈkɜrst wɪð / (AmE), / ˈkɜːst wɪð / (BrE)
having to suffer or be burdened with something negative or undesirable
burdened with something unpleasant

Examples

He always felt cursed with bad luck, no matter what he did.
Some people are cursed with a terrible sense of direction.
The actor joked about being cursed with too much success.

Synset Examples

stuck with the tab

Related Terms

  • Antonym: blessed with - having something positive or desirable
  • Synonym: burdened with - having to carry or deal with something difficult or troublesome
