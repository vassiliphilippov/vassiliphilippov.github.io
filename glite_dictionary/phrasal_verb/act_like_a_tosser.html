to act like a tosser

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈtɑsər / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈtɒsə / (BrE)
to behave foolishly or in an inappropriate manner, typically showing poor judgment or immaturity
behave foolishly or inappropriately
informal slang british vulgar

Examples

Stop acting like a tosser and listen to what I'm saying.
He really acted like a tosser when he started shouting at everyone.
If you act like a tosser at work, people won't take you seriously.

Synset Examples

He acted like a tosser at the party last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to make a fool of oneself - to behave in a way that makes one look silly or foolish
