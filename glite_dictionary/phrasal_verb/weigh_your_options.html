to weigh your options

1. VERB

/ weɪ jʊr ˈɑpʃənz / (AmE), / weɪ jɔː ˈɒpʃənz / (BrE)
to consider different possible choices carefully before making a decision
carefully consider choices

Examples

It's important to weigh your options before committing to a new job.
She took a moment to weigh her options before responding to the offer.
Make sure you weigh your options so you don't regret your decision later.

Synset Examples

You should weigh your options before making a decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to consider - think carefully about, especially before making a decision
  • Antonym: to decide - make a choice after thinking
