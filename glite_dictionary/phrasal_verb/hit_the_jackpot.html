to hit the jackpot

1. VERB

/ hɪt ðə ˈdʒækˌpɑt / (AmE), / hɪt ðə ˈdʒækˌpɒt / (BrE)
to have great or unexpected success, especially as the result of luck
have great success by luck

Examples

After years of trying, she finally hit the jackpot with her bestselling novel.
When he found that rare coin at the garage sale, he really hit the jackpot.
If we get this new contract, our company will hit the jackpot.

Synset Examples

I lucked out and found the last parking spot in the lot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get lucky - to have good luck; succeed by chance
  • Antonym: to miss out - to fail to benefit from something, often by bad luck

Etymology

From gambling, where hitting the jackpot means winning the largest prize.
