to leave office

1. VERB

/ liv ˈɑfɪs / (AmE), / liːv ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
to give up or retire from a position of authority or responsibility, especially in government or business
resign from a position

Examples

The prime minister will leave office at the end of the month.
After ten years as CEO, she finally decided to leave office.
Pressure mounted for the governor to leave office amid the corruption scandal.

Synset Examples

The Secretary of the Navy will leave office next month
The chairman resigned over the financial scandal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to resign - to formally leave a job or office
  • Antonym: to take office - to begin serving in an official position
