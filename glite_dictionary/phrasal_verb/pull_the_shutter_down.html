to pull the shutter down

1. VERB

/ pʊl ðə ˈʃʌtər daʊn / (AmE), / pʊl ðə ˈʃʌtə daʊn / (BrE)
to close or lower a shutter, often to secure a window or entrance
close or lower a shutter

Examples

When it started raining heavily, she quickly pulled the shutter down to keep the water out.
The shopkeeper always pulls the shutter down at closing time.
They pulled the shutter down before leaving for their vacation.

Synset Examples

After the storm, they had to pull the shutters down to prevent further damage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shut the window - to close a window
