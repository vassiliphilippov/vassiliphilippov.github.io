to lone it

1. VERB

/ loʊn ɪt / (AmE), / ləʊn ɪt / (BrE)
(informal) To do something alone, or to act independently, without company or assistance.
act alone; do independently
informal

Examples

Sometimes, you just have to lone it and trust your instincts.
He decided to lone it on the cross-country trip instead of traveling with friends.
She often lones it when she wants to clear her mind.

Synset Examples

During the hike, I decided to lone it and explore alone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to go it alone - to act or undertake something by oneself
