to clear away

1. VERB

/ klɪr əˈweɪ / (AmE), / klɪə əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to remove something from a place, especially in order to make it tidy or so that it is no longer visible
remove from a place or from sight

Examples

Please clear away the dishes after dinner so the table is tidy.
The workers quickly cleared away the debris after the storm.
Once everyone had left, she began clearing away the empty cups and plates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remove - to take something away from a place
  • Synonym: to tidy up - to make a place or thing neat by putting things in order
