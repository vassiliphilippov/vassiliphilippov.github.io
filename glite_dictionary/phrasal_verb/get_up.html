to get up

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ʌp / (AmE), / ɡɛt ʌp / (BrE)
to rise from bed after sleeping
rise from bed

Examples

I usually get up at six in the morning during the week.
She didn't want to get up because it was cold outside.
After his alarm rang three times, he finally managed to get up.

Synset Examples

I get up at 7 A.M. every day
They rose early
He uprose at night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to wake up - to stop sleeping and become awake
  • Antonym: to go to bed - to get into bed in order to sleep

/ ɡɛt ʌp / (AmE), / ɡɛt ʌp / (BrE)
