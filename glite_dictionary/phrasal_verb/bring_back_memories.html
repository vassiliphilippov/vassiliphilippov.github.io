to bring back memories

1. VERB

/ brɪŋ bæk ˈmɛməriz / (AmE), / brɪŋ bæk ˈmɛməriz / (BrE)
to make someone remember something from the past, often evoking emotions or nostalgia
make someone remember the past

Examples

Hearing our school anthem brings back memories of my childhood.
Flipping through old photographs always brings back memories for my grandmother.
The scent of fresh bread brought back memories of baking with his mother as a child.

Synset Examples

That old song always brings back fond memories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to remind - cause someone to remember something
