to speak volcanically

1. VERB

/ spiːk vɑlˈkænɪkli / (AmE), / spiːk vɒlˈkænɪkli / (BrE)
to speak in a way that is very passionate, forceful, or intense, as if driven by powerful emotion
to speak with intense passion or force

Examples

When he defends his beliefs, he often speak volcanically, impressing the audience with his fervor.
During the heated debate, she couldn't help but speak volcanically about the injustices she witnessed.
He tends to speak volcanically whenever the discussion touches on his deepest passions.

Synset Examples

She spoke volcanically, full of passion and energy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speak passionately - to speak with strong feeling or enthusiasm
  • Antonym: speak calmly - to speak in a calm, measured, or unemotional way
