to conduce to

1. VERB

/ kənˈdus tu / (AmE), / kənˈdjuːs tuː / (BrE)
to help to bring about a particular result; to contribute to something
help to bring about (result)

Examples

A positive attitude often conduces to personal success.
Regular exercise conduces to better mental health.
Clear policies conduce to a more efficient workplace.

Synset Examples

Healthy eating habits conduce to overall well-being.
Good communication can conduce to a better work environment.
Their teamwork will conduce to the project's success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contribute to - to help cause or bring about something

Etymology

From Latin conducere (to bring together).
