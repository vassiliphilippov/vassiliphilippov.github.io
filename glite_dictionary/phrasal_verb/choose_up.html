to choose up

1. VERB

/ tʃuz ʌp / (AmE), / tʃuːz ʌp / (BrE)
to divide into teams or sides, especially for a game or activity
to divide into teams for a game

Examples

After school, we would choose up sides for a game of baseball.
The kids quickly chose up before starting their soccer match.
Let’s choose up so we can get this basketball game going.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pick teams - to select players for each side; to form teams
