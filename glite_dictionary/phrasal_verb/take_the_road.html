to take the road

1. VERB

/ teɪk ðə roʊd / (AmE), / teɪk ðə rəʊd / (BrE)
(of a theatrical company or performers) to begin giving performances while traveling from place to place.
begin performing while touring

Examples

After months in the capital, the troupe decided to take the road and perform in rural towns.
The actors were exhausted but thrilled to take the road with their new play.
By the end of the year, the company had taken the road to audiences across the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tour - travel from place to place giving performances
