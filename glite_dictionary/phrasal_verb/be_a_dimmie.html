to be a dimmie

1. VERB

/ bi ə ˈdɪmi / (AmE), / biː ə ˈdɪmi / (BrE)
to act foolishly or show a lack of intelligence
act foolishly; lack intelligence
informal slang

Examples

If you be a dimmie during the test, you'll miss easy questions.
He told his brother not to be a dimmie and to check the facts.
You shouldn't be a dimmie when making important decisions.

Synset Examples

Don't be a dimmie; check the instructions first.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be a fool - to behave in a silly or unwise way
