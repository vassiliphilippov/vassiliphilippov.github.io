to bid for

1. VERB

/ bɪd fɔr / (AmE), / bɪd fə / (BrE)
to offer a particular amount of money for something, especially at an auction
offer a price at an auction

Examples

Several collectors bid for the antique vase at the auction.
He plans to bid for the house as soon as it goes on sale.
They were disappointed when someone else bid for the painting and won.

Synset Examples

She bid $500, Alex, for the rare painting at auction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: offer - to present or propose a price for something
