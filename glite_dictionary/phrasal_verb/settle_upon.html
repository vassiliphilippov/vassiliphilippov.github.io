to settle upon

1. VERB

/ ˈsɛt̬l əˈpɑn / (AmE), / ˈsɛtl əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to decide on something after considering options; to reach an agreement about something
make a final decision or choice

Examples

After much debate, the committee finally settled upon a new logo for the company.
We couldn't settle upon a vacation destination until the very last minute.
He quickly settled upon his choice and submitted the application.

Synset Examples

They finally settled upon a date for the wedding.
We settled upon a restaurant after hours of discussion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decide on - to choose from several alternatives
  • Synonym: agree on - to have the same opinion about a choice or decision
