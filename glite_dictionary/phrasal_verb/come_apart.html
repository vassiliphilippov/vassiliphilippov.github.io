to come apart

1. VERB

/ kʌm əˈpɑrt / (AmE), / kʌm əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
to break into pieces or fragments; to separate or fall apart
break into pieces or fragments

Examples

If you pull too hard, the toy might come apart in your hands.
Over the years, the old book started to come apart at the binding.
The cake was so soft that it began to come apart when I tried to slice it.

Synset Examples

The figurine broke
The freshly baked loaf fell apart

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fall apart - break into pieces or fragments
  • Synonym: break - separate into pieces due to force or impact
