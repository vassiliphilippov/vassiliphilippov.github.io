to crack a problem

1. VERB

/ kræk ə ˈprɑbləm / (AmE), / kræk ə ˈprɒbləm / (BrE)
to find a solution to a difficult or challenging problem
solve a difficult problem

Examples

The research team finally managed to crack a problem that had puzzled scientists for years.
After hours of brainstorming, she was delighted to crack a problem in the project design.
Sometimes it takes a fresh perspective to crack a problem others think is impossible.

Synset Examples

The team must crack this problem together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solve a problem - to find a solution to a problem
