to break up with

1. VERB

/ breɪk ʌp wɪð / (AmE), / breɪk ʌp wɪð / (BrE)
to end a romantic relationship with someone
end a romantic relationship

Examples

She finally broke up with her boyfriend after months of arguments.
He was devastated when Maria broke up with him out of the blue.
After several difficult conversations, Jenny decided to break up with Mark.

Synset Examples

She broke up with him over text? What the flying fuck!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: split up with - to separate from a romantic partner; end a relationship
  • Antonym: get back together with - to resume a romantic relationship after breaking up
