to cream the crop

1. VERB

/ kriːm ðə krɑp / (AmE), / kriːm ðə krɒp / (BrE)
to select the best individuals or items from a group
select the best from a group

Examples

Each year, universities cream the crop from thousands of applicants to build their incoming class.
The talent scout hoped to cream the crop at the youth tournament.
To succeed, companies often cream the crop during recruitment drives.

Synset Examples

They decided to cream the crop before the final selection.
We need to cream the crop to find the best candidates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pick the best - to choose the most excellent or suitable people or things
  • Synonym: cream of the crop - the very best or most desirable part of a group

Etymology

From the noun phrase 'cream of the crop', where 'cream' refers to the best part.
