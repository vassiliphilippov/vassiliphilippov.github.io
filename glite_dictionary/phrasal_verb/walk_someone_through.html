to walk someone through

1. VERB

/ wɑk ˈsʌmwʌn θru / (AmE), / wɔːk ˈsʌmwʌn θruː / (BrE)
to explain something to someone or help them complete a process by giving step-by-step instructions or guidance
guide someone step-by-step

Examples

Can you walk me through the application process step by step?
The technician will walk you through setting up your internet connection.
She patiently walked him through the entire procedure until he understood.

Synset Examples

I need someone to walk me through this software.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guide - to show someone the way or how to do something
