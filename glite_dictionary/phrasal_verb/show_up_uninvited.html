to show up uninvited

1. VERB

/ ʃoʊ ʌp ˌʌnɪnˈvaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʃəʊ ʌp ˌʌnɪnˈvaɪtɪd / (BrE)
to arrive at a place without having been invited
arrive without invitation

Examples

I can't believe you would just show up uninvited to someone's birthday party.
Please don't show up uninvited at my house; I prefer some notice.
He regrets showing up uninvited after he saw how surprised everyone was.

Synset Examples

It's not likely that he'll show up uninvited.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crash - to attend an event without an invitation, especially a party
