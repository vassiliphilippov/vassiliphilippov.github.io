to shot back

1. VERB

/ ʃɑt bæk / (AmE), / ʃɒt bæk / (BrE)
to reply quickly and sharply, often in a defensive or angry manner
reply quickly and sharply

Examples

When criticized by her colleague, she shot back with a sarcastic comment.
He shot back an answer before the question was even finished.
Instead of staying silent, she shot back at her accusers during the meeting.

Synset Examples

After he brushed me off, I shot back, 'Hello yourself!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retort - reply in a sharp, angry, or witty manner
  • Synonym: to snap back - reply quickly and sharply
