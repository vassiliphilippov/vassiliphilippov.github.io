to knock together

1. VERB

/ nɑk təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / nɒk təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to assemble or prepare something quickly, often in a makeshift way
assemble or prepare quickly

Examples

I can knock together some shelves for the garage if you need them.
She managed to knock together a costume just in time for the party.
We should knock together a quick lunch before the guests arrive.

Synset Examples

They managed to knock together a makeshift shelter.
We can knock together a quick dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to put together - to assemble components into a whole
