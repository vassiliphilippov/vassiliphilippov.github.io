to be allergic to

1. VERB

/ bi əˈlɝdʒɪk tu / (AmE), / bi əˈlɜːdʒɪk tuː / (BrE)
to have a hypersensitive reaction to a particular substance, food, or material; to suffer an allergic response when exposed to it
to have a hypersensitive reaction to something

Examples

I am allergic to shellfish, so I have to be careful when dining out.
She is allergic to cats, which makes it difficult for her to visit friends who have pets.
Many children are allergic to certain types of pollen during the spring.

Synset Examples

I'm allergic to peanuts, so I can't eat this.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: allergy - an abnormal reaction by the body to a substance
