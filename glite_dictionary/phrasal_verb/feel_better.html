to feel better

1. VERB

/ fil ˈbɛtər / (AmE), / fiːl ˈbɛtə / (BrE)
to experience an improvement in one's mood, emotions, or physical health
to improve in mood or health

Examples

After resting for a while, I started to feel better and went back to work.
Talking to her friends helped Emily feel better about the situation.
I hope you feel better soon after your illness.

Synset Examples

Mummy always knows how to make me feel better.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to recover - to return to a normal state of health or strength
  • Antonym: feel worse - to experience a decline in mood or health
