to piss on

1. VERB

/ pɪs ɑn / (AmE), / pɪs ɒn / (BrE)
to treat someone or their ideas with contempt or disrespect; to criticize or belittle harshly
treat with contempt; harshly criticize
informal vulgar

Examples

He always tries to piss on anyone else's achievements in team meetings.
I wish you would support me instead of just pissing on my suggestions.
Her manager pissed on her idea without giving it a fair chance.

Synset Examples

He loves to piss on my ideas before I can share them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to put down - to belittle or criticize someone in a way that makes them feel less important
  • Antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration
