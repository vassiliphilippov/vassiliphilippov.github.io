to pop a mint

1. VERB

/ pɑp ə mɪnt / (AmE), / pɒp ə mɪnt / (BrE)
to put a mint in one's mouth and consume it quickly, often for freshening breath
quickly consume a mint

Examples

Whenever she feels her breath isn't fresh, she will pop a mint before meetings.
He discreetly popped a mint after his coffee break.
People often pop a mint after lunch to freshen their breath.

Synset Examples

After lunch, he popped a breast mint for freshness.
