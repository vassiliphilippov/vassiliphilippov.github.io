to execute a U-turn

1. VERB

/ ˈɛksɪˌkjut ə ˈjuː tɜrn / (AmE), / ˈɛksɪˌkjuːt ə ˈjuː tɜːn / (BrE)
to make a complete U-shaped turn with a vehicle in order to travel in the opposite direction
make a U-shaped vehicle turn

Examples

After missing the exit, the taxi driver executed a U-turn to get back on track.
The police officer signaled for the car to execute a U-turn at the intersection.
It's illegal to execute a U-turn on this busy street during rush hour.

Synset Examples

The driver executed a U-turn to avoid the traffic jam ahead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make a U-turn - to perform a U-shaped turn with a vehicle
