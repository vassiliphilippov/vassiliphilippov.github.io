to garbage down

1. VERB

/ ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ daʊn / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ daʊn / (BrE)
to eat a large amount of food quickly and greedily
eat food quickly and greedily
informal

Examples

After skipping breakfast, he garbaged down three slices of pizza in minutes.
The kids garbaged down their dinner before running out to play.
I was so hungry after the hike that I garbaged down the entire sandwich.

Synset Examples

The children gobbled down most of the birthday cake

Related Terms
