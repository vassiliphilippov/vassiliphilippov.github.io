to head the ball

1. VERB

/ hɛd ðə bɔl / (AmE), / hɛd ðə bɔːl / (BrE)
to strike the ball using one's head, especially in soccer or football
strike the ball with one's head (in soccer)

Examples

During the match, he managed to head the ball into the goal.
Defenders are trained to head the ball away from their own net.
She leapt above the other players to head the ball clear of danger.

Synset Examples

You need to head-the-ball if you want to succeed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: header - an act of hitting the ball with the head in soccer
