to talk of

1. VERB

/ tɑk əv / (AmE), / tɔːk ɒv / (BrE)
to discuss or mention something in conversation, often briefly or as a topic of interest
mention or discuss (something)

Examples

At dinner, they talked of their childhood adventures and family traditions.
Historians often talk of the importance of this battle in shaping the nation's history.
People talk of economic recovery, but many are still struggling.

Synset Examples

they talked of many things

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to talk about - discuss or speak at length about something
