to cloud up

1. VERB

/ klaʊd ʌp / (AmE), / klaʊd ʌp / (BrE)
to become covered with clouds; to begin to appear cloudy in the sky
become covered with clouds

Examples

As soon as we got to the beach, the sky began to cloud up and it started to rain.
It will probably cloud up this afternoon, so bring an umbrella just in case.
The weather report says it will cloud up later, with a chance of showers in the evening.

Synset Examples

The sky clouded over

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cloud over - to become covered with clouds
