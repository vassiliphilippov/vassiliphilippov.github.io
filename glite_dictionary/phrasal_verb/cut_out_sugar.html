to cut out sugar

1. VERB

/ kʌt aʊt ˈʃʊɡɚ / (AmE), / kʌt aʊt ˈʃʊɡə / (BrE)
to stop consuming sugar, especially for health or dietary reasons.
eliminate sugar from diet

Examples

After seeing her blood test results, she decided to cut out sugar completely.
Many nutritionists recommend that people cut out sugar to improve their overall health.
He struggled at first when trying to cut out sugar from his daily diet.

Synset Examples

He had to cut out sugar from his diet for health reasons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to eliminate - to completely remove something
  • Synonym: to give up - to stop doing or having something, often a habit
