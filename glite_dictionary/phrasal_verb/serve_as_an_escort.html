to serve as an escort

1. VERB

/ sɜːrv æz ən ˈɛskɔrt / (AmE), / sɜːv æz ən ˈɛskɔːt / (BrE)
to accompany someone in a formal or official capacity, especially for protection or as a mark of respect
formally accompany someone

Examples

He was asked to serve as an escort for the visiting dignitary.
During the formal dinner, Mary served as an escort to the ambassador.
Officers often serve as an escort during high-profile events.

Synset Examples

He served as an escort during the gala.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: escort - a person who accompanies another for protection or as a companion
