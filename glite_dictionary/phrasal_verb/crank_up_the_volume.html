to crank up the volume

1. VERB

/ kræŋk ʌp ðə ˈvɑl.jum / (AmE), / kræŋk ʌp ðə ˈvɒl.juːm / (BrE)
to increase the sound level, especially by turning a knob or pressing a button on an audio device
raise the sound level

Examples

Could you crank up the volume a bit? I can't hear the dialogue clearly.
When her favorite song played, she quickly cranked up the volume and started dancing.
At the party, they cranked up the volume until the neighbors complained.

Synset Examples

He always cranks up the volume when his favorite song comes on.
He always turns up the volume on game nights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to turn up the volume - to increase the sound level
