to worry about

1. VERB

/ ˈwɜːri əˌbaʊt / (AmE), / ˈwʌri əˌbaʊt / (BrE)
to feel anxious or concerned about someone or something, often over what might happen
feel anxious or concerned about

Examples

You shouldn't worry about things you can't control.
She always worries about her children when they travel alone.
Try not to worry about your exams too much; just do your best.

Synset Examples

What's the good of worrying about things we can't change?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be anxious about - to feel uneasy or worried about something
  • Antonym: be unconcerned about - to not be worried or anxious about something
