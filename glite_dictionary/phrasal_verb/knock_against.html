to knock against

1. VERB

/ nɑk əˈɡɛnst / (AmE), / nɒk əˈɡɛn(st) / (BrE)
to hit or collide with something by accident, usually with some force
collide with something by accident

Examples

She accidentally knocked against the vase and it fell to the floor.
Be careful not to knock against the table when you walk by.
He knocked against a lamppost while looking at his phone.

Synset Examples

I ran into the telephone pole

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to bump into - to collide with something or someone accidentally
  • Synonym: crash into - to hit something violently while moving
