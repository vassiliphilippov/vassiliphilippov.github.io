to catch cold

1. VERB

/ kætʃ koʊld / (AmE), / kætʃ kəʊld / (BrE)
to become ill with a cold, usually as a result of exposure to cold air or infection
become ill with a cold

Examples

Make sure you wear a jacket, or you might catch cold in this weather.
She caught cold after getting soaked in the rain last week.
He is staying home because he caught cold and doesn't want to spread it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: come down with - to become ill with a particular illness
