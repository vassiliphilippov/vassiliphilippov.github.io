to live it up

1. VERB

/ lɪv ɪt ʌp / (AmE), / lɪv ɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to enjoy oneself in a lively or extravagant way, often by spending money or going out
enjoy oneself extravagantly
informal

Examples

After finishing their exams, the students decided to live it up at a beach party.
She likes to live it up when she goes on vacation, visiting fancy restaurants and clubs.
He plans to live it up on his birthday with his friends and family.

Synset Examples

it's your birthday, so let's live it up!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to celebrate - to acknowledge a significant or happy day or event with festivities
  • Antonym: to take it easy - to relax and not do anything strenuous or exciting
