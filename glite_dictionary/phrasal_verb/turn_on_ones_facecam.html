to turn on one's facecam

1. VERB

/ tɝn ɑn wʌnz feɪsˌkæm / (AmE), / tɜːn ɒn wʌnz feɪsˌkæm / (BrE)
to activate a camera that displays one's face, especially for livestreams or video calls
activate a camera showing one's face for streaming
informal

Examples

She was nervous to turn on her facecam during her first online class.
The streamer promised to turn on his facecam after reaching 1,000 subscribers.
If the interview is remote, please turn on your facecam so we can see you.

Synset Examples

He turned on his facecam for the live stream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to turn on - to activate a device or machine
