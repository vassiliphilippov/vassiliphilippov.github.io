to circle round

1. VERB

/ ˈsɜːrkəl raʊnd / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkəl raʊnd / (BrE)
to move in a circular motion around something or someone
move in a circular path around

Examples

The hawk began to circle round its prey high in the sky.
Children circle round the maypole every spring festival.
Police cars circling round the building added to the tension of the standoff.

Synset Examples

The Earth revolves around the Sun

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orbit - to move around something in a curved path, especially a planet or star
  • Synonym: circle - to move all the way around something in a circular path
