to keep safe

1. VERB

/ kip seɪf / (AmE), / kiːp seɪf / (BrE)
to protect someone or something from danger, harm, or loss; to ensure safety or security
protect from danger or harm

Examples

The security guard was hired to keep safe the valuable artwork in the gallery.
Parents do everything they can to keep safe their children.
Please keep safe these important documents until my return.

Synset Examples

She keeps the change safe behind the counter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to protect - to keep someone or something safe from harm or injury
  • Antonym: to endanger - to put someone or something at risk or in danger
