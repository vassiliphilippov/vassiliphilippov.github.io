to shake the plum tree

1. VERB

/ ʃeɪk ðə plʌm tri / (AmE), / ʃeɪk ðə plʌm triː / (BrE)
to cause ripe plums or other items to fall from a tree by shaking it; by extension, to forcefully remove items from their source
to shake a tree to harvest fruit

Examples

After the storm, we had to shake the plum tree to collect the last of the fruit.
They gathered beneath the branches and began to shake the plum tree for a bigger harvest.
Children love to shake the plum tree when the fruit is ripe in late summer.

Synset Examples

They had to shake the plum tree to get the ripe ones down.
Every summer, they shake the plum tree for a good harvest.
Let's shake the plum tree and gather the fruits.
