to go home

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ hoʊm / (AmE), / ɡəʊ həʊm / (BrE)
to return to one's home or place of residence
return to one's home

Examples

After the meeting, I decided to go home and rest.
She usually goes home by train every evening.
It's getting late; let's go home before it gets too dark.

Synset Examples

After the movie, we went home

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to come home - to arrive at one's home, often from a specific place
  • Antonym: to leave home - to depart from one's home
