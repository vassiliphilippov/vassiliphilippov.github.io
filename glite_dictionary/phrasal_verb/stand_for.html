to stand for

1. VERB

/ stænd fɔr / (AmE), / stænd fə / (BrE)
to represent, denote, or be a sign or symbol of something; to mean.
represent; denote; symbolize

Examples

The abbreviation 'UN' stands for 'United Nations'.
In chemical equations, 'Na' stands for sodium.
What does the symbol '$' stand for in this context?
