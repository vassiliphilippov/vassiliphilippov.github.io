to rule over

1. VERB

/ ruːl ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ruːl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to have control or authority over a person, group, or place
control or have authority over

Examples

For decades, the dictator ruled over the country with an iron fist.
The queen ruled over a vast empire during her reign.
After the revolution, a new government ruled over the region.

Synset Examples

The king ruled over his dominion with wisdom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to govern - to officially control and direct the affairs of a state, city, or organization
  • Synonym: to dominate - to have commanding influence on; exercise control over
  • Antonym: to submit - to accept or yield to the authority of another; antonym to 'rule over' in control relationship
