to buy at

1. VERB

/ baɪ æt / (AmE), / baɪ æt / (BrE)
to shop at a particular place as a customer or to do business with a specific vendor or store
shop at a particular place

Examples

Many customers prefer to buy at local markets rather than large supermarkets.
He always buys at that electronics store because of their excellent service.
We decided not to buy at that shop anymore after the prices went up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to shop at - to buy goods at a specific store or place
