to stow away

1. VERB

/ stoʊ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / stəʊ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to hide on a ship, aircraft, or vehicle in order to travel secretly and without paying
hide on a vehicle to travel secretly

Examples

The young boy managed to stow away on the cargo ship bound for Australia.
Several refugees attempted to stow away in trucks crossing the border.
He was caught trying to stow away on a flight to Paris.

Synset Examples

The illegal immigrants stowed away on board the freighter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stow - put something away or store it
