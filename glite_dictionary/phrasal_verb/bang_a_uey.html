to bang a uey

1. VERB

/ bæŋ ə ˈjuːi / (AmE), / bæŋ ə ˈjuːi / (BrE)
To make a U-turn, especially suddenly or abruptly, while driving.
make a U-turn (informal)
informal american

Examples

I missed the exit, so I had to bang a uey at the first intersection.
She realized she was headed the wrong way and quickly banged a uey.
The police officer signaled for him to bang a uey right there.

Synset Examples

The cop told him to bang a uey at the next light.
After missing the turn, she banged a uey.
He quickly banged a uey to avoid the traffic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make a U-turn - to turn a vehicle around in the opposite direction

Etymology

Probably from 'bang' (to move suddenly) and 'uey,' a playful shortening of 'U-turn.'
