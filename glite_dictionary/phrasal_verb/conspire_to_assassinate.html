to conspire to assassinate

1. VERB

/ kənˈspaɪɚ tu əˈsæsəˌneɪt / (AmE), / kənˈspaɪə tu əˈsæsɪneɪt / (BrE)
to secretly plan together to kill a prominent or important person
secretly plan to kill (an important person)

Examples

The generals conspired to assassinate the president after months of unrest.
Historical documents revealed that several nobles conspired to assassinate the monarch.
They were imprisoned when it was discovered they had conspired to assassinate a foreign leader.

Synset Examples

They conspired to assassinate the king.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plot to assassinate - secretly plan to kill someone
  • Synonym: conspire to murder - secretly plan with others to kill someone, without implication of political importance
