to flip the pancake

1. VERB

/ flɪp ðə ˈpænkeɪk / (AmE), / flɪp ðə ˈpænkeɪk / (BrE)
to turn a pancake over in the pan while cooking, usually to cook both sides evenly
to turn over a pancake while cooking

Examples

She managed to flip the pancake without spilling any batter on the stove.
It's tradition in our house to flip the pancake high into the air every Shrove Tuesday.
Be careful when you flip the pancake so it doesn't stick to the ceiling.

Synset Examples

She decided to flip the pancake to see if it was done.
He decided to flip the pancake in the air.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turn the pancake - to turn a pancake over during cooking
