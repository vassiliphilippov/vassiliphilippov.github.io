to sand the edge

1. VERB

/ sænd ði ɛdʒ / (AmE), / sænd ðiː ɛdʒ / (BrE)
to smooth the surface of an edge (especially of wood) by rubbing it with sandpaper or an abrasive tool
smooth the edge with an abrasive

Examples

Before applying the varnish, be sure to sand the edge of the table for a cleaner finish.
She carefully sanded the edge of each board to remove splinters.
After cutting the panels, I always sand the edge to ensure smoothness.

Synset Examples

Let's sand the edges before we paint the shelf.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smooth - to make a surface flat, even, or free from roughness
