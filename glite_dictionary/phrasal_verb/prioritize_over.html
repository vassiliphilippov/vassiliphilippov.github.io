to prioritize over

1. VERB

/ praɪˈɔːr.ə.taɪz ˈoʊ.vɚ / (AmE), / praɪˈɒr.ɪ.taɪz ˈəʊ.və / (BrE)
to treat one thing as more important than another
treat as more important

Examples

You should not prioritize over your health in order to finish work projects faster.
Many students prioritize over extracurricular activities when making their schedules.
Some people prioritize over their careers rather than spending time with family.

Synset Examples

As a spod, he always prioritized gaming over socializing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to prefer - to like one thing better than another
