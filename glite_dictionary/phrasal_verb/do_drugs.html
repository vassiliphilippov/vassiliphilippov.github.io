to do drugs

1. VERB

/ du ˈdrʌɡz / (AmE), / dəʊ ˈdrʌɡz / (BrE)
to use illegal or recreational drugs, especially habitually or as a form of recreation
use illegal or recreational drugs
informal

Examples

Many teenagers are pressured by their peers to do drugs, but not everyone gives in.
She stopped doing drugs after realizing how much it was affecting her life.
He admitted that he used to do drugs in college, but he quit years ago.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: take drugs - to consume drugs, especially for non-medical reasons
