to have a crush on

1. VERB

/ hæv ə krʌʃ ɑn / (AmE), / hæv ə krʌʃ ɒn / (BrE)
to be infatuated with someone, often in a romantic or admiring way, especially for a short time or without their knowledge
to be infatuated with someone
informal

Examples

I used to have a crush on my neighbor when I was in high school.
She didn't know that her best friend had a crush on her for years.
Many teenagers have a crush on celebrities at some point.

Synset Examples

She had a crush on her classmate all year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be infatuated with - to have a strong, foolish admiration or love for someone
