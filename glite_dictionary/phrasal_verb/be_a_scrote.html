to be a scrote

1. VERB

/ bi ə skroʊt / (AmE), / biː ə skrəʊt / (BrE)
(slang) To behave in a selfish or unhelpful way; to act like an unpleasant or inconsiderate person.
behave selfishly or unhelpfully (slang)
slang british

Examples

If you're just going to be a scrote, don't bother coming to the meeting.
He tends to be a scrote when people ask him for help.
Stop being a scrote and let your brother borrow your bike.

Synset Examples

Don't be a scrote and help your friend out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scrote - (slang) a contemptible or unpleasant person
  • Synonym: be a jerk - act in a selfish or inconsiderate way (informal)

Etymology

Slang form; 'scrote' is a shortened version of 'scrotum', used derogatorily in British English.
