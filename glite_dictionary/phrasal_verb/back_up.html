to back up

1. VERB

/ bæk ˈʌp / (AmE), / bæk ˈʌp / (BrE)
to provide support or evidence that shows something is true or valid
support with evidence or proof

Examples

You need to back up your argument with reliable sources.
The lawyer asked the witness to back up her statement.
Without data to back up his theory, his claim was dismissed.

Synset Examples
