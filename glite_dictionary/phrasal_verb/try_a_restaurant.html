to try a restaurant

1. VERB

/ traɪ ə ˈrɛs.təˌrɑnt / (AmE), / traɪ ə ˈrɛs.tərɒnt / (BrE)
to visit and eat at a restaurant, often to see what it is like or to experience the food or service
to visit and eat at a restaurant

Examples

We decided to try a restaurant we'd never visited before for our anniversary.
Whenever I travel, I like to try a restaurant recommended by locals.
They are always eager to try a restaurant that just opened downtown.

Synset Examples

Let’s try that new restaurant tonight for dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visit a restaurant - to go to a restaurant with the intent of eating there
