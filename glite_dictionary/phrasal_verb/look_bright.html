to look bright

1. VERB

/ lʊk braɪt / (AmE), / lʊk braɪt / (BrE)
to seem promising or full of hope for success or improvement
appear promising or optimistic

Examples

Despite the recent setbacks, the company's future looks bright thanks to new investments.
After graduation, her career prospects looked bright and full of opportunities.
As the sun rose over the city, everything suddenly looked bright and hopeful again.

Synset Examples

The future looks bright for the new team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: look promising - to appear likely to succeed or be positive
  • Antonym: look bleak - to appear unpromising or hopeless
