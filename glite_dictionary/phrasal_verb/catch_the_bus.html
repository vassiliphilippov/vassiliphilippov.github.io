to catch the bus

1. VERB

/ kætʃ ðə bʌs / (AmE), / katʃ ðə bʌs / (BrE)
to board a bus before it departs, especially by arriving in time for its scheduled departure
board a bus in time

Examples

I have to hurry if I want to catch the bus to work.
She missed breakfast so she could catch the bus on time.
He waved and ran to catch the bus as it was about to leave.

Synset Examples

I often run to catch the bus in the morning.
Make sure to catch the bus before it leaves.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to miss the bus - to fail to board a bus before it leaves
