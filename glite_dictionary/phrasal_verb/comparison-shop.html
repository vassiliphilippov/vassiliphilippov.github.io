to comparison-shop

1. VERB

/ kəmˈpɛrɪsən ʃɑp / (AmE), / kəmˈpærɪsən ʃɒp / (BrE)
to compare the prices of similar items at different stores before making a purchase
to compare prices before buying

Examples

Before making a big purchase, it's wise to comparison-shop to find the best deal.
She spent the afternoon comparison-shopping for a new laptop online.
He usually comparison-shops for groceries to save money each month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shop around - to look for the best price or quality by visiting different shops
