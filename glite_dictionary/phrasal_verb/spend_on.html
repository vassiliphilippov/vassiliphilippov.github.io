to spend on

1. VERB

/ spɛnd ɑn / (AmE), / spɛnd ɒn / (BrE)
to pay or use money in order to buy, do, or achieve something
use money for something

Examples

Last year, she spent on a new computer for her office.
Many families try to limit how much they spend on entertainment each month.
He regrets how much he has spent on unnecessary gadgets over the years.

Synset Examples

I can't believe how much I spent on that doney.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pay for - to give money in exchange for something
