to shake out the wrinkle

1. VERB

/ ʃeɪk aʊt ðə ˈrɪŋkəl / (AmE), / ʃeɪk aʊt ðə ˈrɪŋkl / (BrE)
to remove creases or folds by shaking a piece of fabric or clothing
remove wrinkles by shaking

Examples

She took off her jacket and shook out the wrinkle before hanging it up.
Try to shake out the wrinkle from the curtains after washing them.
Before ironing, you should shake out the wrinkle from the shirt to make it easier.

Synset Examples

He needs to shake out the wrinkles from his shirt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to smooth out - to make something smooth, especially by removing creases
