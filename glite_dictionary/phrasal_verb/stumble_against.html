to stumble against

1. VERB

/ ˈstʌmbəl əˈɡɛnst / (AmE), / ˈstʌmbəl əˈɡɛnst / (BrE)
to trip or bump into something by accident
trip or bump into accidentally

Examples

He wasn't looking where he was going and stumbled against the side table.
The child stumbled against the dog in the hallway and apologized.
She lost her balance and stumbled against the closed door.

Synset Examples

I stumbled against the curb while texting.
She stumbled against the chair and nearly fell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stumble - to trip or momentarily lose one's balance
