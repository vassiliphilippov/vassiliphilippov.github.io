to act like a wanksta

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈwæŋstə / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈwæŋstə / (BrE)
to behave as if one is tough or street-smart, especially when this is not genuine
pretend to be tough or streetwise
informal slang

Examples

He always acts like a wanksta at parties, but nobody takes him seriously.
Stop acting like a wanksta and just be yourself for once.
They thought she acted like a wanksta, but it was all for show.

Synset Examples

He acts like a wanksta, but we all know he's not tough.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pretend - to behave as if something is true when it is not
  • Antonym: authentic - genuine or real; not false or copied

Etymology

Formed from the slang 'wanksta' (blend of 'wannabe' and 'gangsta') plus the verb phrase 'act like'.
