to reflect on

1. VERB

/ rɪˈflɛkt ɑn / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛkt ɒn / (BrE)
to think carefully and deeply about something
think deeply about

Examples

After the meeting, she took some time to reflect on the feedback she had received.
Traveling allows people to step back and reflect on their lives from a new perspective.
He often reflects on his childhood when making important decisions.

Synset Examples

He often reflected on his mortality and life's purpose.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to contemplate - to think about something deeply and thoroughly
  • Synonym: to ponder - to think about something carefully, especially before making a decision
