to rub out

1. VERB

/ rʌb aʊt / (AmE), / rʌb aʊt / (BrE)
to remove or erase something by rubbing, especially a mark, drawing, or writing
remove or erase by rubbing

Examples

He used an eraser to rub out the pencil marks on the page.
Please rub out the incorrect answer and write the correct one.
The teacher asked her to rub out the chalk drawing from the board.

Synset Examples

Please erase the formula on the blackboard — it is wrong!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to erase - to remove written or drawn marks
