to heat the oven

1. VERB

/ hit ði ˈʌvən / (AmE), / hiːt ði ˈʌvən / (BrE)
to make an oven reach the desired temperature before placing food in it
preheat the oven for cooking

Examples

Before baking the cake, you should heat the oven to 180°C.
Remember to heat the oven so the bread bakes evenly.
She always forgets to heat the oven before starting her recipes.

Synset Examples

You need to heaten the oven to 350 degrees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to preheat - to heat something, especially an oven, beforehand
