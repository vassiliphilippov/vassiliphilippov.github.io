to use a dictionary

1. VERB

/ juz ə dɪkʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / juːz ə ˈdɪkʃən(ə)ri / (BrE)
to consult a dictionary to find out the meaning, spelling, or usage of a word
consult a dictionary for information

Examples

If you are unsure of a word's meaning, it's helpful to use a dictionary.
Students should learn how to use a dictionary effectively to expand their vocabulary.
She always tells her children to use a dictionary when they encounter unfamiliar terms.

Synset Examples

The teacher encouraged us to use a dictionary for vocabulary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consult a dictionary - to look in a dictionary for information
