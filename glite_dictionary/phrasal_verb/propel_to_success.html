to propel to success

1. VERB

/ prəˈpɛl tə səkˈsɛs / (AmE), / prəˈpɛl tə səkˈsɛs / (BrE)
to drive or push someone or something toward achieving success.
drive or push toward success

Examples

Her determination and hard work will propel to success in her chosen field.
Innovative ideas can propel to success even the smallest companies.
A supportive mentor may propel to success a struggling student.

Synset Examples

His ambition will propel him to success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drive to succeed - to strongly motivate or push toward achieving success
