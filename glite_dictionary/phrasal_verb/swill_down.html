to swill down

1. VERB

/ swɪl daʊn / (AmE), / swɪl daʊn / (BrE)
to drink something quickly or in large quantities, especially alcohol
drink quickly or in large amounts

Examples

He swilled down a pint of beer in less than a minute.
After the race, she swilled down a bottle of water to rehydrate.
They swilled down shots at the bar before heading out for the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to gulp down - drink quickly in large mouthfuls
  • Antonym: sip - drink slowly in small amounts
