to have something over with

1. VERB

/ hæv ˈsʌm.θɪŋ ˈoʊ.vɚ wɪð / (AmE), / hæv ˈsʌm.θɪŋ ˈəʊ.və wɪð / (BrE)
to complete or finish something unpleasant or stressful so that it is no longer a concern
finish something unpleasant or stressful
informal

Examples

I just want to have this meeting over with so I can relax.
She went to the dentist early to have it over with and stop worrying.
Let’s have the difficult conversation over with so we can move forward.

Synset Examples
