to fly on

1. VERB

/ flaɪ ɑn / (AmE), / flaɪ ɒn / (BrE)
to continue flying or to keep proceeding through the air
continue flying

Examples

Despite the storm, the pilot decided to fly on to the next airport.
After a brief rest on the branch, the swallow flew on towards its nest.
I watched the brightly colored butterfly fly on until it vanished from sight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to continue - to keep going without stopping
  • Synonym: fly - to move through the air using wings or a machine
