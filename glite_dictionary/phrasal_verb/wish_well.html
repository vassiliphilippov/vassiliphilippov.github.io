to wish well

1. VERB

/ wɪʃ wɛl / (AmE), / wɪʃ wɛl / (BrE)
to feel or express a hope that someone will have good fortune or success in the future
express hope for good fortune or success

Examples

Even though we are no longer together, I still wish well for him in his new job.
Before leaving the company, her colleagues gathered to wish well as she started her next adventure.
When you meet your former classmates, remember to wish well to those you haven’t seen in years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wish - to express a desire for something to happen
