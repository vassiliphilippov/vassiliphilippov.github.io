to go to

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ tu / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tuː / (BrE)
to be present at an event, place, or activity such as a meeting, service, or class; to attend.
attend an event or activity

Examples

She tries to go to every lecture during the semester.
Are you going to go to the conference next week?
He rarely goes to church on Sundays anymore.

Synset Examples

She attends class regularly
I rarely attend services at my church
did you go to the meeting?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to attend - to be present at (an event, place, or activity)
