to take delight in

1. VERB

/ teɪk dɪˈlaɪt ɪn / (AmE), / teɪk dɪˈlaɪt ɪn / (BrE)
to find great pleasure or satisfaction in (doing something)
find great pleasure in

Examples

She takes delight in watching the sun rise over the mountains every morning.
Many gardeners take delight in growing their own vegetables.
He took delight in surprising his friends with thoughtful gifts.

Synset Examples
