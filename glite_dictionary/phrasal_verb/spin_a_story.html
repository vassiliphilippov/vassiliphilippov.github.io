to spin a story

1. VERB

/ spɪn ə ˈstɔːri / (AmE), / spɪn ə ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
to invent or relate a story, often with imaginative or exaggerated details
invent or relate an imaginative or exaggerated story

Examples

She loves to spin a story for the children before bedtime, making every tale magical.
When questioned about his whereabouts, he tried to spin a story to avoid trouble.
He has a talent to spin a story that keeps everyone listening in suspense.

Synset Examples

The local illywhacker spun stories that entertained everyone.
The fictionmonger spun tales that captivated the crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to tell a story - to relate an account of events, either true or fictional
  • Synonym: make up a story - to invent a story, often on the spot
