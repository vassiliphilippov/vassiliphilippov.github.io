to feed into

1. VERB

/ fid ˈɪntu / (AmE), / fiːd ˈɪntuː / (BrE)
to contribute to or provide information, influence, or material that helps to shape or affect something else
contribute to or inform something

Examples

The survey responses will feed into our plans for next year.
Her ideas fed into the group discussion and shaped the outcome.
All the collected data is feeding into the national statistics report.

Synset Examples

These results feed into our understanding of climate change.
Her research will feed into the final report.
The data will feed into our new marketing strategy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contribute to - to help to cause something to happen
  • Synonym: to inform - to give knowledge or information to something
