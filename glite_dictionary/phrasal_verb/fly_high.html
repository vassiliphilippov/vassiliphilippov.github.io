to fly high

1. VERB

/ flaɪ haɪ / (AmE), / flaɪ haɪ / (BrE)
to feel very happy, excited, or confident; to be elated
feel very happy or elated

Examples

After winning the competition, she was flying high for weeks.
The team flew high after their impressive victory yesterday.
He always flies high when he hears good news about his family.

Synset Examples

He was flying high during the summer months

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to be on cloud nine - feel extremely happy or elated
  • Antonym: to feel down - feel sad or depressed
