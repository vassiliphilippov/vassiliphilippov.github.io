to log out

1. VERB

/ lɔɡ aʊt / (AmE), / lɒɡ aʊt / (BrE)
to exit a computer system or website so that others cannot access your account or information
exit a computer system or website

Examples

Please remember to log out of your account after using the computer.
She forgot to log out and someone else accessed her files.
You can log out from all devices for added security.

Synset Examples

Please log off before you go home

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to log off - to exit a computer system or website

Etymology

From 'log' (in the sense of making a record of use) + 'out', formed by analogy to 'log in'.
