to cuddle with

1. VERB

/ ˈkʌdəl wɪð / (AmE), / ˈkʌdəl wɪð / (BrE)
to hold someone or something close in a loving or affectionate way
hold close in a loving way

Examples

On cold winter nights, she loves to cuddle with her dog on the couch.
The couple cuddled with their baby, enjoying a quiet afternoon together.
He often cuddles with a warm blanket while reading before bed.

Synset Examples

The kids cuddled with Raggie during movie night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embrace - to hold someone closely in your arms, especially as a sign of affection
  • Synonym: snuggle with - to settle or move into a warm, comfortable position close to someone or something
