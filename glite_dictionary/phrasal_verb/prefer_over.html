to prefer over

1. VERB

/ prəˈfɜr ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / prɪˈfɜː ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to choose or like one option more than another; to show a preference for one thing instead of another
like one thing more than another

Examples

Most people prefer over paying in cash when they shop online.
He preferred over working from home rather than commuting every day.
Many diners preferred over the vegetarian option to the meat dish.

Synset Examples

She prefers brown over the usual black for shoes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to prefer - to like one thing or person better than another
  • Synonym: favor - to show preference for; to prefer
