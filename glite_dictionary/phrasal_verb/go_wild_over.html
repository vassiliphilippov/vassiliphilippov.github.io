to go wild over

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ waɪld ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ɡəʊ waɪld ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to become very excited, enthusiastic, or passionate about someone or something
become very excited about

Examples

The crowd went wild over the band's final performance.
Children go wild over ice cream on a hot day.
When she saw the puppies, she went wild over them and couldn't stop smiling.

Synset Examples

The kitten went wild over the catnip toy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go crazy over - to become very excited or overly enthusiastic about someone or something
  • Antonym: be indifferent to - to lack excitement or enthusiasm for something
