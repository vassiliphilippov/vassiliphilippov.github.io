to offer congratulations

1. VERB

/ ˈɔfɚ ˌkɑnɡrətʃəˈleɪʃənz / (AmE), / ˈɒfə ˌkɒnɡrætjʊˈleɪʃənz / (BrE)
to formally express joy or approval for someone's achievement or good fortune
formally express congratulations

Examples

Please offer congratulations to your sister for her graduation.
The principal took a moment to offer congratulations to the winning team.
Colleagues gathered to offer congratulations after she received her promotion.

Synset Examples

Everyone offered congradulations after the big win.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to congratulate - to express pleasure or acknowledgment for someone's success
