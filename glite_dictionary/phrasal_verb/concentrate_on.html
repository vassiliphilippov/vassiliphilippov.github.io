to concentrate on

1. VERB

/ ˈkɑn.sənˌtreɪt ɑn / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.sənˌtreɪt ɒn / (BrE)
to give all your attention or effort to a particular activity, subject, or problem
focus attention or effort on something

Examples

You need to concentrate on your studies if you want to pass the exam.
She tried to concentrate on her work despite the noise around her.
He found it hard to concentrate on reading with so many distractions.

Synset Examples

Her entire attention centered on her children
Our day revolved around our work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to focus on - direct attention or effort toward something
