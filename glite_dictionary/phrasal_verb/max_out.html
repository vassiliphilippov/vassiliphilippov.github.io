to max out

1. VERB

/ mæks aʊt / (AmE), / mæks aʊt / (BrE)
to reach the maximum limit or capacity of something
reach a maximum limit
informal

Examples

I managed to max out my gym membership benefits within the first few months.
She didn't want to max out her credit card before the holiday season.
The machine can max out at a speed of 200 miles per hour.

Synset Examples

I maxed out on all my credit cards

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to exceed - to go beyond a limit

Etymology

Shortened from 'maximum' plus 'out', meaning to reach the outer limit.
