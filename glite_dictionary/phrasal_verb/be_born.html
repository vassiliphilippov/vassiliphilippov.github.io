to be born

1. VERB

/ bi bɔrn / (AmE), / bi bɔːn / (BrE)
to come into existence at the moment of birth; to enter the world as a baby
come into existence by birth

Examples

She was excited to learn that her daughter would be born in the spring.
Many famous artists happened to be born in small towns rather than big cities.
When did you be born?

Synset Examples

She was born on a farm

Related Terms

  • Antonym: die - to cease to live; stop being alive
