to voice opinion

1. VERB

/ vɔɪs əˈpɪnjən / (AmE), / vɔɪs əˈpɪnjən / (BrE)
to express one's thoughts or views, especially aloud or publicly
express one's views aloud

Examples

During the meeting, she was not afraid to voice opinion on the proposed changes.
Citizens are encouraged to voice opinion during public forums.
He always makes sure to voice opinion when important issues are discussed.

Synset Examples

As a member of the committee, he voiced his opinions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: express opinion - to state or articulate one's viewpoint
  • Synonym: voice concerns - to express worries or doubts
