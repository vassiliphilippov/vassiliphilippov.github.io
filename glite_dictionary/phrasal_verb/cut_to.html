to cut to

1. VERB

/ kʌt tu / (AmE), / kʌt tʊ / (BrE)
(in film or television) to move suddenly from one scene to another.
move to another scene (film, TV)

Examples

As the villain entered the room, the movie cut to a scene of the terrified crowd outside.
Right after the car exploded, the episode cut to a commercial break.
The director decided to cut to the final showdown much earlier than expected.

Synset Examples

The camera cut to the sky

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fade in - gradual appearance of an image or scene (film/TV)
  • Antonym: to fade out - gradual disappearance of an image or scene (film/TV)
  • Synonym: jump cut - an abrupt transition between scenes or shots
