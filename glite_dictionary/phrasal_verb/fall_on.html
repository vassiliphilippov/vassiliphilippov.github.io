to fall on

1. VERB

/ fɔl ɑn / (AmE), / fɔːl ɒn / (BrE)
(of an event or date) to happen or occur on a particular day or date.
be scheduled on a specific date

Examples

Christmas falls on a Monday this year.
My next appointment falls on the 12th of March.
Their anniversary always falls on a weekend.

Synset Examples

Her birthday falls on the 15th of -th month.
