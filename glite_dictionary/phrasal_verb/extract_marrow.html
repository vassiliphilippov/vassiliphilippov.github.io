to extract marrow

1. VERB

/ ɪkˈstrækt ˈmæroʊ / (AmE), / ɪkˈstrækt ˈmærəʊ / (BrE)
to remove the soft substance found inside bones, especially for food or medicinal purposes
remove soft tissue from inside bones

Examples

The chef used a spoon to extract marrow from the roasted beef bones.
Some traditional recipes require cooks to extract marrow to create rich broths.
Archaeologists believe ancient people learned to extract marrow for its nutritional value.

Synset Examples

They cooked the bones to extract the marrow for the stew.
