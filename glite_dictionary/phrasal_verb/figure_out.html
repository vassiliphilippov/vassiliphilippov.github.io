to figure out

1. VERB

/ ˈfɪɡjɚ aʊt / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡə aʊt / (BrE)
to find an answer or solution to something by thinking; to understand the meaning of something
find a solution or understand

Examples

She couldn't figure out why her computer kept crashing.
It took me a while to figure out the answer to the puzzle.
After reading the instructions, he finally figured out how to assemble the desk.

Synset Examples

did you solve the problem?
Work out your problems with the boss
this unpleasant situation isn't going to work itself out
did you figure it out?
Did you puzzle out my meaning?
He could not work the math problem

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to work out - to solve or find a solution to a problem
  • Synonym: to puzzle out - to solve a problem or understand something with effort
  • Antonym: to misunderstand - to fail to understand correctly

Etymology

From 'figure' (to calculate or reason) plus 'out'; American usage since the 19th century.
