to say farewell

1. VERB

/ seɪ ˌfɛrˈwɛl / (AmE), / seɪ ˌfeəˈwɛl / (BrE)
to express good-bye or take leave of someone, often in a formal or emotional manner
to say good-bye; to bid farewell

Examples

She stopped at the door to say farewell to her friends before leaving.
He will say farewell at the retirement party next week.
We gathered at the airport to say farewell to our departing colleague.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bid farewell - to say good-bye, often formally
  • Synonym: say good-bye - to express good-bye
