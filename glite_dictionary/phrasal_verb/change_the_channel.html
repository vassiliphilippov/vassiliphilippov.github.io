to change the channel

1. VERB

/ ˈtʃeɪndʒ ðə ˈtʃænəl / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪndʒ ðə ˈtʃæn(ə)l / (BrE)
to switch from one television channel to another, typically using a remote control or by pressing a button on the television.
switch television channels

Examples

Could you please change the channel? This show is boring.
Every time a commercial comes on, she quickly changes the channel.
He forgot where the remote was and couldn't change the channel.

Synset Examples

He told me to change the channel to the news.
I always change the channel during commercials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: switch the channel - to switch from one television channel to another
