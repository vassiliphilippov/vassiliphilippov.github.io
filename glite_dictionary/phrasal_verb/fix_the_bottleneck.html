to fix the bottleneck

1. VERB

/ fɪks ðə ˈbɑtlˌnɛk / (AmE), / fɪks ðə ˈbɒtl.nɛk / (BrE)
to eliminate or resolve a point of congestion or delay, especially in a process or system
eliminate a point of congestion or delay

Examples

The engineers worked overnight to fix the bottleneck in the network system.
Management is under pressure to fix the bottleneck that's delaying product shipments.
Once we fix the bottleneck in the approval process, everything should run smoothly.

Synset Examples

We need to fix the bottleneck in our production line.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resolve the bottleneck - to find a solution for a point of congestion or slowdown
  • Antonym: create a bottleneck - to cause a new point of congestion or delay
