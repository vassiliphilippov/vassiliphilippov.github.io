to offer up

1. VERB

/ ˈɔfər ʌp / (AmE), / ˈɒfə ʌp / (BrE)
to present or give something, especially prayers or sacrifices, as an act of worship or devotion
present as an act of worship

Examples

Every morning, she offers up a quiet prayer before starting her day.
The community gathered to offer up thanks during the special ceremony.
He offered up flowers at the temple as a sign of respect.

Synset Examples

offer prayers to the gods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: present - to formally give something
