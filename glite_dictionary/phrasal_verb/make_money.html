to make money

1. VERB

/ meɪk ˈmʌni / (AmE), / meɪk ˈmʌni / (BrE)
to earn income or profit, especially for oneself
earn income or profit

Examples

She started her own business to make money instead of working for someone else.
Many people try to make money by investing in the stock market.
Freelancers often find creative ways to make money online.

Synset Examples

She found ways to make money while traveling.
Investing wisely can help you make money quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to earn - to receive money as payment for work that you do
