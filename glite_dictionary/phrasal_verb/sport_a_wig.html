to sport a wig

1. VERB

/ spɔrt ə wɪɡ / (AmE), / spɔːt ə wɪɡ / (BrE)
to wear or show off a wig, especially in a noticeable or stylish way
to wear or display a wig

Examples

The actress sporting a wig looked completely different from her usual self.
At the party, he decided to sport a wig to match his flamboyant costume.
It’s not unusual for celebrities to sport a wig on the red carpet.

Synset Examples

The actress sported a glamorous wig for her role.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wear a wig - to have a wig on one's head

Etymology

From the verb 'sport' (to display or wear ostentatiously) + 'wig'.
