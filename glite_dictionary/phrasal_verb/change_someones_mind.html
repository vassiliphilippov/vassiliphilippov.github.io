to change someone's mind

1. VERB

/ tʃeɪndʒ ˈsʌmwʌnz maɪnd / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ ˈsʌmwʌnz maɪnd / (BrE)
to persuade someone to alter their opinion or decision
persuade someone to change their opinion

Examples

No matter what I said, I couldn't change her mind about moving to another city.
He brought new evidence to try to change the jury's mind.
Sometimes it takes a personal story to change someone's mind on an important issue.

Synset Examples

He tried to change her mind about moving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to convince - to persuade someone to do or believe something
  • Synonym: to persuade - to cause someone to do or believe something by reasoning or argument
