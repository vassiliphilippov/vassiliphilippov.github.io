to take the day off

1. VERB

/ teɪk ðə deɪ ɔf / (AmE), / teɪk ðə deɪ ɒf / (BrE)
to be absent from work or duties for a day, typically to rest or for personal reasons
be absent from work for a day

Examples

I'm going to take the day off tomorrow to spend time with my family.
She decided to take the day off after finishing the big project.
He wasn’t feeling well, so he took the day off to rest.

Synset Examples

He took the day off to relax.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to take time off - to be absent from work or duties for a period of time
