to rise early

1. VERB

/ raɪz ˈɜrli / (AmE), / raɪz ˈɜːli / (BrE)
to get out of bed or wake up early in the morning
wake up early in the morning

Examples

I try to rise early to make the most of my day.
People who rise early often feel more productive.
During the trip, she rose early to see the sunrise over the mountains.

Synset Examples

She plans to rise early for her morning run.
