to catch someone's attention

1. VERB

/ kætʃ sʌmˌwʌnz əˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / katʃ sʌmˌwʌnz əˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
to attract someone's notice or interest; to make someone pay attention
attract someone's notice or interest

Examples

The colorful display in the store window really caught my attention as I walked by.
She raised her hand to catch the teacher's attention during the lecture.
Bold headlines are often used to catch readers' attention in newspapers.

Synset Examples

His standom at the event really caught everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attract attention - to cause people to notice
  • Synonym: to draw attention - to direct notice or focus towards something
  • Antonym: escape notice - to avoid being noticed
