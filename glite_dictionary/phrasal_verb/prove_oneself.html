to prove oneself

1. VERB

/ pruv wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / pruːv wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to show that one is capable, skilled, or courageous, especially by successfully meeting a challenge or fulfilling expectations
show ability or courage

Examples

After a difficult year in the new job, she finally managed to prove herself to her colleagues.
He joined the army to prove himself and earn his family's respect.
The young athlete was eager to prove himself in the national competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to demonstrate - to show clearly by giving proof or evidence
  • Antonym: to disprove - to show that something is not true or valid
