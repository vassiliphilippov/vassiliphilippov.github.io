to chase skirt

1. VERB

/ tʃeɪs skɝt / (AmE), / tʃeɪs skɜːt / (BrE)
(informal, sometimes considered dated or derogatory) To pursue women for romantic or sexual purposes, often with the implication of doing so habitually or superficially.
pursue women for sex or romance
informal slang

Examples

He was known to chase skirt during his younger years, rarely settling down with just one woman.
It's obvious that Tom goes to nightclubs mainly to chase skirt, not for the music.
Some people criticize men who chase skirt instead of building meaningful relationships.

Synset Examples

During college, he would often chase skirt at parties.
He's always trying to chase skirt at the bar.
It’s not cool to chase skirt when she’s not interested.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: womanizer - a man who frequently seeks romantic or sexual relationships with women
  • Synonym: skirt-chaser - a man who habitually seeks romantic or sexual encounters with women

Etymology

From "chase" (pursue) and "skirt" (slang for woman, from the garment).
