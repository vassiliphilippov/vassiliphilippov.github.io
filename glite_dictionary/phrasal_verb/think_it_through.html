to think it through

1. VERB

/ θɪŋk ɪt θru / (AmE), / θɪŋk ɪt θruː / (BrE)
to consider all the details and possible effects of something before making a decision or taking action
consider something carefully

Examples

Before making your final decision, you should think it through to avoid mistakes.
She almost accepted the job offer immediately, but then decided to think it through first.
It's important to think it through before agreeing to something that could affect your future.

Synset Examples

Don't rush in without thinking it through.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to consider - think carefully about something
  • Antonym: rush into - do something too quickly without thinking
