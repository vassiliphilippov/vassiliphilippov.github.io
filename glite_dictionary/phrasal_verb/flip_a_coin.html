to flip a coin

1. VERB

/ flɪp ə kɔɪn / (AmE), / flɪp ə kɔɪn / (BrE)
to toss a coin into the air and let it fall, used as a way of making a decision by chance
toss a coin to decide by chance

Examples

To decide who would go first, they agreed to flip a coin.
If you can't pick a movie, let's just flip a coin.
We had to flip a coin to settle the argument.

Synset Examples

Let's flip a coin—heads or tails to decide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toss a coin - to throw a coin in the air to make a decision by chance
