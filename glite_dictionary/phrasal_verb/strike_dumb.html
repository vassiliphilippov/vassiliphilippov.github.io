to strike dumb

1. VERB

/ straɪk dʌm / (AmE), / straɪk dʌm / (BrE)
to make someone unable to speak, usually because of surprise, shock, or fear
render someone speechless with surprise or shock

Examples

The audience was struck dumb by the breathtaking performance.
He was struck dumb with disbelief when he heard the news.
Her unexpected question struck dumb everyone in the room.

Synset Examples

we were struck dumb by the candidate's announcement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stun - to shock or surprise someone so much that they are temporarily unable to react
  • Synonym: to astound - to surprise or shock someone very much
  • Antonym: to find one's voice - to begin to speak or respond again after being silent, especially after shock
