to connect with people

1. VERB

/ kəˈnɛkt wɪð ˈpipəl / (AmE), / kəˈnɛkt wɪð ˈpiːpəl / (BrE)
to establish a relationship, understanding, or rapport with other people
form relationships or bonds with others

Examples

It's important to connect with people when starting a new job.
She quickly learned how to connect with people from different backgrounds.
Many leaders strive to connect with people on a personal level.

Synset Examples

As a toucher, he connects with people easily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bond with someone - form a close relationship with another person
