to fall upon

1. VERB

/ fɔl əˈpɑn / (AmE), / fɔːl əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to find or discover something unexpectedly, often by chance
find or discover by chance

Examples

During their excavation, the workers fell upon a hidden chamber beneath the foundation.
She fell upon an old letter while cleaning out the attic last weekend.
We fell upon a cheerful little café tucked away in a narrow alley.

Synset Examples

the archeologists chanced upon an old tomb
she struck a goldmine
The hikers finally struck the main path to the lake

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to come across - to find by chance
  • Synonym: stumble upon - to accidentally find or encounter
