to hoof it

1. VERB

/ hʊf ɪt / (AmE), / hʊf ɪt / (BrE)
(informal) to walk, especially to travel by foot rather than by vehicle.
walk (informal)
informal

Examples

After missing the bus, we had to hoof it all the way home.
If you don't have a car, you'll just have to hoof it to work in the morning.
They decided to hoof it through the park instead of taking a taxi.

Synset Examples

let's hoof it to the disco

Related Terms

  • Synonym: walk - to move at a regular pace by lifting and setting down each foot in turn
