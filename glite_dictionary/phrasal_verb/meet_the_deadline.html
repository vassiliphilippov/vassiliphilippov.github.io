to meet the deadline

1. VERB

/ mit ðə ˈdɛdlaɪn / (AmE), / miːt ðə ˈdɛdlaɪn / (BrE)
to finish a task or project by the time it is due
finish on time; by a set date

Examples

The team worked overtime to meet the deadline for the new software release.
It is important for students to meet the deadline when submitting their assignments.
Despite the setbacks, we managed to meet the deadline for the project report.

Synset Examples

She sped through her tasks to meet the deadline.
She excels at work, always meeting her deadlines.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: miss the deadline - fail to finish something by the required time
