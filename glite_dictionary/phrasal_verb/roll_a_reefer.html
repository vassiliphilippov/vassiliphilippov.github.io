to roll a reefer

1. VERB

/ roʊl ə ˈriːfər / (AmE), / rəʊl ə ˈriːfə / (BrE)
to make a marijuana cigarette by wrapping cannabis in a rolling paper
prepare a marijuana cigarette
slang

Examples

They decided to roll a reefer before the movie started.
He showed his friends how to roll a reefer with just one hand.
It's illegal to roll a reefer in many states.

Synset Examples

They rolled a reefer to enjoy during the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roll a joint - to make a marijuana cigarette by rolling cannabis in a paper

Etymology

From 'reefer' (slang for marijuana cigarette) and 'roll' (to wrap into a cylindrical shape).
