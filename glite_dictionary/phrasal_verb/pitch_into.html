to pitch into

1. VERB

/ pɪtʃ ˈɪntu / (AmE), / pɪtʃ ˈɪntu / (BrE)
to attack someone or something violently, physically or verbally
attack violently (physically or verbally)

Examples

The protesters pitched into the police with sticks and stones.
She pitched into him for his careless behavior during the meeting.
As soon as the fight broke out, several players pitched into each other.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attack - to take aggressive or violent action against
  • Antonym: to defend - to protect from attack
