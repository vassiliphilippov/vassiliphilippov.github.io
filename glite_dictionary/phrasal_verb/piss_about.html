to piss about

1. VERB

/ pɪs əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / pɪs əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to waste time by behaving in a silly or unproductive way; to avoid serious work or effort
waste time or act foolishly
informal vulgar british

Examples

Stop pissing about and finish your homework before dinner.
The team pissed about all morning and accomplished nothing.
He tends to piss about when he's supposed to be helping.
