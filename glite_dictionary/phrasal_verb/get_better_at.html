to get better at

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈbɛtər æt / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈbɛtə æt / (BrE)
to improve one's skill or ability in a particular activity or subject
to improve in a skill or ability

Examples

She hopes to get better at public speaking before her next presentation.
Over time, you will get better at cooking if you practice often.
After a few lessons, he started to get better at driving.

Synset Examples

I hope to get better at playing the guitar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to improve - to become better or make something better
