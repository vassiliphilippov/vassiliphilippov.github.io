to unhook the latch

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈhʊk ðə lætʃ / (AmE), / ʌnˈhʊk ðə lætʃ / (BrE)
to release or disengage a latch by removing its hook or fastening
release a latch

Examples

Before you open the gate, make sure to unhook the latch first.
She carefully unhooked the latch on the window to let in some fresh air.
The toolbox was locked until he unhooked the latch with a quick twist.

Synset Examples

You must unhook the latch before opening the door.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: latch - a fastening for a door or gate
