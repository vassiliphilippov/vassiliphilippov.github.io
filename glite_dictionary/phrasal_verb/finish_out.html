to finish out

1. VERB

/ ˈfɪnɪʃ aʊt / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃ aʊt / (BrE)
to complete or bring to an end, especially by adding the final touches or details
complete or finalize something

Examples

The contractor will finish out the project by installing the light fixtures next week.
We need to finish out the year strong to meet our sales goals.
They added a few final touches to finish out the design of the garden.

Synset Examples

These studies round out the results of many years of research

Related Terms

  • Synonym: complete - to make whole or finish making something
  • Synonym: to finalize - to make final; complete all details
