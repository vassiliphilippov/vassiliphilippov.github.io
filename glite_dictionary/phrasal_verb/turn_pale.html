to turn pale

1. VERB

/ tɝn peɪl / (AmE), / tɜːn peɪl / (BrE)
to become very pale, especially as a result of shock, fear, or illness
become very pale

Examples

She began to turn pale as the bad news sank in.
If you turn pale suddenly, you should probably sit down and rest.
He couldn't help but turn pale at the sight of the accident.

Synset Examples

He turned pale when he heard the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to blanch - to suddenly become pale, especially from shock or fear
