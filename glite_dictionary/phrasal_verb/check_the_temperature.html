to check the temperature

1. VERB

/ tʃɛk ðə tɛm.pəˌreɪ.tʃɚ / (AmE), / tʃɛk ðə tɛm.pəˌreɪ.tʃə / (BrE)
to measure or determine the degree of heat or cold in something, especially the air, a room, or a substance
measure heat or cold

Examples

Don't forget to check the temperature before taking your medicine.
She always checks the temperature outside before getting dressed in the morning.
The chef checked the temperature of the oven to make sure it was hot enough.

Synset Examples

Check the temperature before you go outside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: measure the temperature - to determine the heat or cold level using an instrument
