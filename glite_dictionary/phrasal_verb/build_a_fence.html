to build a fence

1. VERB

/ bɪld ə fɛns / (AmE), / bɪld ə fɛns / (BrE)
to construct a barrier, typically made of wood, metal, or wire, usually around a property or area for protection or privacy
construct a barrier around an area

Examples

We decided to build a fence to keep the deer out of our garden.
The neighbors are planning to build a fence between the two properties this spring.
She spent the weekend building a fence around her new backyard.

Synset Examples

They built a fence around the yard for privacy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: erect a fence - to put up or set up a fence
