to monkey around

1. VERB

/ ˈmʌŋki əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to behave in a silly or aimless way, or to spend time doing unplanned activities with little purpose
act aimlessly or idly
informal

Examples

The kids were monkeying around in the backyard instead of doing their homework.
Stop monkeying around and get to work on your project!
They spent the afternoon monkeying around with the new video game console.

Synset Examples

The old lady is usually mucking about in her little house

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to mess about - to spend time doing things that are not important or serious
  • Synonym: to fool around - to behave in a playful or silly way, often instead of doing something serious
