to fork up

1. VERB

/ fɔrk ʌp / (AmE), / fɔːk ʌp / (BrE)
to give or hand over something, especially money, usually reluctantly
hand over (often reluctantly), esp. money
informal

Examples

He finally forked up the cash after hours of negotiation.
You’ll have to fork up the fine before they let you go.
If you don't fork up what you owe, they'll take you to court.
