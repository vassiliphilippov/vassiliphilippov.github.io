to deal with haters

1. VERB

/ ˈdiːl wɪð ˈheɪ.tɚz / (AmE), / ˈdiːl wɪð ˈheɪ.təz / (BrE)
to manage, respond to, or handle criticism or negativity from people who are hostile or critical
manage criticism or negativity from hostile people
informal

Examples

Famous athletes often have to deal with haters in the media and on social platforms.
She learned how to deal with haters without letting their words affect her confidence.
Social media influencers frequently deal with haters who post negative comments online.
