to lay the foundation

1. VERB

/ leɪ ðə faʊnˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / leɪ ðə faʊnˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
to establish the basic conditions or groundwork for something to develop or succeed in the future
establish groundwork for future development

Examples

Her early research laid the foundation for major scientific advancements in her field.
Good communication between team members will lay the foundation for a successful project.
Teachers lay the foundation for lifelong learning in their students.

Synset Examples

She laid the foundation for her career in college.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pave the way - to make progress easier or possible for something that follows
