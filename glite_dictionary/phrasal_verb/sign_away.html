to sign away

1. VERB

/ saɪn əˈweɪ / (AmE), / saɪn əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to formally give up ownership or rights to something, usually by signing a legal document
formally give up ownership or rights

Examples

After careful consideration, she signed away her shares in the company.
He regretted having signed away his inheritance so hastily.
You should always read the contract before you sign away your rights.

Synset Examples

She signed away her rights

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to relinquish - to voluntarily give up claim or possession
  • Synonym: surrender - to give up possession or control, often formally
