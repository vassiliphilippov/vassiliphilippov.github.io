to dudded up

1. VERB

/ ˈdʌdəd ʌp / (AmE), / ˈdʌdɪd ʌp / (BrE)
dressed up elegantly or adorned, especially for a special occasion
dressed up elegantly
informal

Examples

He always gets dudded up for weddings and special dinners.
They dudded up in vintage outfits for the themed party.
She loves dudding up when there's an important event to attend.

Synset Examples

He was dudded up for the gala event last night.
Everyone dudded up to celebrate the anniversary.
She dudded up in her best dress for the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressed up - wearing formal or fancy clothes
  • Antonym: dressed down - wearing casual or less formal clothes

Etymology

From 'duds' meaning clothes, plus the phrasal form 'dud up.'
