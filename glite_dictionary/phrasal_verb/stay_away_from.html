to stay away from

1. VERB

/ steɪ əˈweɪ frəm / (AmE), / steɪ əˈweɪ frəm / (BrE)
to avoid going near or getting involved with a person, place, or thing, often for safety or prudence
avoid someone or something

Examples

Please stay away from the construction site for your own safety.
He tries to stay away from sweets to maintain a healthy diet.
They advised their children to stay away from strangers.

Synset Examples

She advised him to stay away from negative influences.
You should stay away from that neighborhood at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to avoid - to keep away from or refrain from doing something
  • Antonym: approach - to come near or nearer to someone or something
