to turn into

1. VERB

/ tɝn ˈɪntu / (AmE), / tɜːn ˈɪntuː / (BrE)
to become something different, or to cause something or someone to become different; to transform or change into.
become or transform into something else

Examples

After years of hard work, her small shop finally turned into a thriving business.
If you leave milk out in the sun, it will turn into cheese.
The disagreement quickly turned into a heated argument.

Synset Examples

The caterpillar will turn into a butterfly soon.
He managed to turn two base hits into runs.
He managed to turn into a successful entrepreneur.
The storm may turn into a hurricane tonight.
He turned his lamage into a funny story.
The old story featured a crapaud that turned into a prince.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to transform - to change completely the appearance or character of something or someone, especially so that that thing or person is improved
  • Synonym: change - to make or become different

2. VERB

/ tɝn ˈɪntu / (AmE), / tɜːn ˈɪntu / (BrE)
To become something different; to transform or change into a particular thing or state.
become or transform into

Examples

The caterpillar will turn into a butterfly after a few weeks.
Over the years, the small town has turned into a busy city.
What started as a hobby is now turning into a successful business.

Synset Examples

This project is turning into a cuntinent disaster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to become - to start to be; to begin to be
