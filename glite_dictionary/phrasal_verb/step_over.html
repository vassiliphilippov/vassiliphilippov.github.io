to step over

1. VERB

/ stɛp ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / stɛp ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to move by lifting one's foot or leg over something, especially to avoid touching or stepping on it
move by lifting leg over something

Examples

Be careful to step over the cracks in the sidewalk.
She stepped over the sleeping dog without waking it.
The hiker paused to step over a stream of water on the trail.

Synset Examples

Please step over the puddle to avoid wet shoes.
He had to step over the fallen branch on the path.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: step around - to move by going around something rather than over it
