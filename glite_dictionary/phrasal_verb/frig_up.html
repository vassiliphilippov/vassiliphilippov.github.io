to frig up

1. VERB

/ frɪɡ ʌp / (AmE), / frɪɡ ʌp / (BrE)
to do something badly or ruin it; to make a mistake that causes failure or problems
to mess up or ruin something
informal slang

Examples

If you frig up the timing, the whole plan will fail.
She accidentally frigged up the computer by pressing the wrong key.
Try not to frig up your chances by arriving late.

Synset Examples

I can't believe you frigged up the presentation!
Don't frig up this time; we can't afford mistakes.
He frigged up the recipe by adding too much salt.
I really frigged up my chance at that job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to mess up - to handle or do something badly
  • Synonym: to fuck up - to ruin, damage, or make a serious mistake (vulgar slang)

Etymology

Euphemistic variant of 'fuck up', with 'frig' used as a milder substitute; originally British slang.
