to stick to one's diet

1. VERB

/ stɪk tə wʌnz ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / stɪk tʊ wʌnz ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
to continue following a diet plan without deviating from it
adhere to a diet plan

Examples

It's difficult to stick to one's diet when there are so many tempting desserts around.
She managed to stick to her diet throughout her vacation.
Many people make resolutions to stick to their diet after the New Year.

Synset Examples

He couldn't stick to his diet during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stick to - to continue doing or using something and not change it
