to declare oneself

1. VERB

/ dɪˈklɛr wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / dɪˈklɛə wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to formally announce one's intention to marry someone, especially by asking for their hand in marriage
ask someone to marry; propose marriage

Examples

After years of friendship, he finally declared himself to her during a quiet evening at home.
In many old novels, a gentleman would declare himself to the lady he loved.
She waited anxiously, wondering if he would ever declare himself and ask for her hand in marriage.

Synset Examples

he popped the question on Sunday night
she proposed marriage to the man she had known for only two months
The old bachelor finally declared himself to the young woman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to propose - to ask someone to marry you
