to unwind after work

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈwaɪnd ˈæftɚ wɝk / (AmE), / ʌnˈwaɪnd ˈɑːftə wɜːk / (BrE)
to relax and reduce stress following work or a day's activities
relax following work

Examples

Many people watch television to unwind after work.
I like to take a walk in the park to unwind after work.
A hot cup of tea helps me unwind after work every evening.

Synset Examples
