to stay humble

1. VERB

/ steɪ ˈhʌmbəl / (AmE), / steɪ ˈhʌmbəl / (BrE)
to continue to be modest and not become arrogant or self-important, especially after achieving success
remain modest and unpretentious

Examples

Despite her achievements, she always tries to stay humble.
His coach reminded him to stay humble after winning the tournament.
It's important to stay humble even when you reach your goals.

Synset Examples

Don't be cocky; remember to stay humble.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be humble - to be modest and not arrogant
  • Antonym: to show off - to behave in a way that is intended to attract attention or admiration, often regarded as excessive
