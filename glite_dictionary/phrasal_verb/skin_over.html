to skin over

1. VERB

/ skɪn ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / skɪn ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
(of a wound or injury) to become covered with a new layer of skin as part of the healing process.
(of a wound) form skin as it heals

Examples

The cut on his arm finally began to skin over after a week.
If you don’t touch the wound, it should skin over soon.
As the burn healed, it slowly skinned over, leaving a faint scar.
