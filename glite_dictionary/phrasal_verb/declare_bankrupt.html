to declare bankrupt

1. VERB

/ dɪˈklɛr ˈbæŋkrʌpt / (AmE), / dɪˈkleə ˈbaŋkrʌpt / (BrE)
to be officially recognized by a court as unable to pay one's debts
be officially recognized as unable to pay debts
legal

Examples

After his company failed, he was declared bankrupt by the court.
Many small business owners fear being declared bankrupt during an economic crisis.
She tried to negotiate with creditors before being officially declared bankrupt.

Synset Examples

He was declared bankrupt after years of poor investments.
