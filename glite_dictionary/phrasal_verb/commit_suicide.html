to commit suicide

1. VERB

/ kəˈmɪt ˈsuː.ɪ.saɪd / (AmE), / kəˈmɪt ˈsuː.ɪ.saɪd / (BrE)
to intentionally kill oneself
to kill oneself deliberately

Examples

After years of struggling with depression, he decided to commit suicide.
It is important to reach out to those who may be at risk of committing suicide.
The novel explores the reasons why its main character commits suicide.

Synset Examples

the terminally ill patient committed suicide

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to kill oneself - to take one's own life
  • Synonym: to take one's own life - to intentionally end one's own life
