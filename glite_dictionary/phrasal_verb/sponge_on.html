to sponge on

1. VERB

/ spʌndʒ ɑn / (AmE), / spʌndʒ ɒn / (BrE)
to apply (a liquid, paint, etc.) to a surface using a sponge
apply with a sponge

Examples

The artist decided to sponge on a layer of blue paint for a textured effect.
Make sure to sponge on the cleaner gently so as not to damage the delicate fabric.
She sponged on water to remove the last traces of dust from the wall.

Synset Examples

The painter sponged on his washes
