to make it happen

1. VERB

/ meɪk ɪt ˈhæpən / (AmE), / meɪk ɪt ˈhapən / (BrE)
to cause something to occur or to bring about a desired outcome through action or effort.
cause something to occur

Examples

With enough determination, you can make it happen despite all obstacles.
They needed funding, so she worked hard to make it happen.
The team set a goal and collaborated to make it happen.

Synset Examples

We can make it happen if we work together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to bring about - to cause to happen; to result in
  • Antonym: to prevent - to stop something from happening
