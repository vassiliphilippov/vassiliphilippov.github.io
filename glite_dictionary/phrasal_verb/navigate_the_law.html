to navigate the law

1. VERB

/ ˈnævɪˌɡeɪt ðə lɔ / (AmE), / ˈnævɪˌɡeɪt ðə lɔː / (BrE)
to understand and apply legal rules effectively, especially when dealing with complex legal systems or situations
understand and apply legal rules

Examples

As a first-time business owner, she struggled to navigate the law and comply with regulations.
Paralegals are trained to help clients navigate the law in complicated cases.
Immigrants often face challenges when trying to navigate the law in a new country.

Synset Examples

He became an attorney to help others navigate the law.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interpret the law - to explain the meaning of laws or legal texts
  • Antonym: break the law - to fail to obey legal rules
