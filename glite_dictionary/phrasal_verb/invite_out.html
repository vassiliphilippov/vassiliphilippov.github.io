to invite out

1. VERB

/ ɪnˈvaɪt aʊt / (AmE), / ɪnˈvaɪt aʊt / (BrE)
to ask someone to join you for a social activity, often as a date
ask someone on a date

Examples

He finally gathered the courage to invite out his coworker for dinner.
She was surprised when Tom decided to invite out her for lunch.
Do you think it's too soon to invite out someone you just met?

Synset Examples

has he asked you out yet?
he asked me to a dance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ask out - to invite someone on a date
