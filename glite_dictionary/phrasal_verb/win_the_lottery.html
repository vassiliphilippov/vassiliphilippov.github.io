to win the lottery

1. VERB

/ wɪn ðə ˈlɑːt̬əri / (AmE), / wɪn ðə ˈlɒtəri / (BrE)
to achieve a large sum of money by winning a lottery, typically through luck or chance
achieve a big win in a lottery

Examples

If I ever win the lottery, I'll travel around the world.
She dreams that one day she will win the lottery and buy a big house.
We joked that our only hope of paying off debt was to win the lottery.

Synset Examples

She was ecstatic when she finally won the lottery.
I can't believe you won the lottery; don't tell me!
They celebrated after they won the lottery last night.
He won the lottery of all things!
Holy fuck, I just won the lottery!
In the game, my Sim just won the lottery!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to hit the jackpot - to win a large prize, often by chance
