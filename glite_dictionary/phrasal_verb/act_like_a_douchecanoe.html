to act like a douchecanoe

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈduːʃ kəˌnu / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈduːʃ kəˌnjuː / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish, obnoxious, or extremely irritating manner
behave in a foolish or irritating way (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar

Examples

Stop acting like a douchecanoe and try listening to what people are saying.
He apologized eventually, but only after acting like a douchecanoe all night.
If you keep acting like a douchecanoe, your friends won't want to hang out with you.

Synset Examples

Stop acting like a douchecanoe and just apologize.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to act like a jerk - to behave in an unpleasant or obnoxious way

Etymology

Formed from 'douche', a vulgar insult, and 'canoe' as a humorous compound, used in internet slang.
