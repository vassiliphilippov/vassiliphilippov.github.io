to shake out the rug

1. VERB

/ ʃeɪk aʊt ðə rʌɡ / (AmE), / ʃeɪk aʊt ðə rʌɡ / (BrE)
to clean a rug by lifting and shaking it to remove dust and debris
to clean a rug by shaking it

Examples

Before guests arrived, she decided to shake out the rug on the porch.
Could you please shake out the rug before putting it back in the living room?
They shook out the rug outside to get rid of all the dust and crumbs.

Synset Examples

Let's shake out the rug to remove the dirt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beat the rug - to clean a rug by hitting it to remove dust
