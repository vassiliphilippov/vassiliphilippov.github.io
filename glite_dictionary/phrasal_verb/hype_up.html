to hype up

1. VERB

/ haɪp ʌp / (AmE), / haɪp ʌp / (BrE)
to make someone feel very excited or enthusiastic, especially in anticipation of something
make excited or enthusiastic
informal

Examples

The coach tried to hype up the team before the big game.
Too much coffee can really hype up some people.
The advertisements were designed to hype up the new product.

Synset Examples

The children were all psyched up after the movie

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psyche up - prepare oneself mentally; become excited or motivated
  • Antonym: to calm down - make or become less excited or agitated
