to repel pest

1. VERB

/ rɪˈpɛl pɛst / (AmE), / rɪˈpɛl pɛst / (BrE)
to drive away insects or animals considered harmful, especially from crops, homes, or living spaces.
drive away harmful insects or animals

Examples

Certain herbs are known to repel pests in the vegetable garden.
The spray is formulated to repel pests without harming pets or children.
Using essential oils can help repel pests from your home naturally.

Synset Examples

Plants secrete substances to repel pests naturally.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to repel - to drive or force back or away
