to keep in mind

1. VERB

/ kip ɪn maɪnd / (AmE), / kiːp ɪn maɪnd / (BrE)
to remember or consider something when making decisions or thinking about something else
remember; consider

Examples

Keep in mind that the museum closes at 5 p.m. sharp.
Please keep in mind the safety instructions while operating the machine.
He always keeps in mind his mother's advice when making important decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to remember - to keep something in one's memory
  • Antonym: to forget - to fail to remember
