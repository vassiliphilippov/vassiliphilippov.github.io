to sling the ball

1. VERB

/ slɪŋ ðə bɔl / (AmE), / slɪŋ ðə bɔːl / (BrE)
to throw the ball quickly or casually
throw the ball quickly or casually

Examples

The pitcher turned and sling the ball to first base in one quick motion.
He likes to sling the ball across the yard when playing fetch with his dog.
Without looking, she managed to sling the ball over her shoulder to a teammate.

Synset Examples

Please sling the ball to me when you're ready.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: throw the ball - to send the ball through the air with the arm

Etymology

From the verb 'sling', meaning to throw or cast, likely from Old Norse 'slyngva'.
