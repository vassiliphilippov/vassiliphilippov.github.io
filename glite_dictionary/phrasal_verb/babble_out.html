to babble out

1. VERB

/ ˈbæbəl aʊt / (AmE), / ˈbab(ə)l aʊt / (BrE)
to reveal confidential information or secrets in a careless or uncontrolled manner
reveal secrets carelessly

Examples

She accidentally babbled out the surprise party plans before the big day.
Don’t babble out our secrets just because you’re nervous!
He babble out confidential details under pressure from the reporter.

Synset Examples

Be careful — his secretary talks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to blurt out - to say something suddenly and without thinking, especially a secret
  • Antonym: to keep quiet - to refrain from revealing information or secrets
