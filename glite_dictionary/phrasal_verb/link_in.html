to link in

1. VERB

/ lɪŋk ɪn / (AmE), / lɪŋk ɪn / (BrE)
to include or connect (something), especially by adding a hyperlink or integrating into a document or system
to include or connect, often digitally

Examples

Could you link in the updated charts before you send the email?
When writing your thesis, remember to link in the relevant references.
The website allows users to link in their social media accounts.

Synset Examples

Please link in the document for further reading.
Can you link in your sources for the report?
Make sure to link in the images for the presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to attach - to join or add something to something else
  • Synonym: insert - to put something into something else
