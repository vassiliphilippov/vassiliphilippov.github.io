to freshen the air

1. VERB

/ ˈfrɛʃən ði ɛr / (AmE), / ˈfrɛʃ(ə)n ðiː eə / (BrE)
to make the air in a space smell cleaner or more pleasant
make air smell cleaner or better

Examples

I opened all the windows to freshen the air before our guests arrived.
She lit a scented candle to freshen the air in the living room.
Houseplants can help freshen the air naturally by absorbing odors.

Synset Examples

He used a spray to freshen the air in the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air freshener - a device or product that freshens the air
