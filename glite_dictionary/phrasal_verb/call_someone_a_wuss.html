to call someone a wuss

1. VERB

/ kɔl ˈsʌmwʌn ə wʊs / (AmE), / kɔːl ˈsʌmwʌn ə wʊs / (BrE)
to insult someone by saying they are cowardly or weak
insult as cowardly or weak
informal

Examples

He called someone a wuss just because they wouldn't ride the roller coaster.
It's not nice to call someone a wuss if they're afraid of the dark.
She regrets having called someone a wuss during the heated argument.

Synset Examples

He called me a wuss for not joining the hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: call someone a coward - to insult someone as lacking courage
  • Antonym: praise someone - to express admiration rather than insult
