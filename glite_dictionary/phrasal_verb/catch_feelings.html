to catch feelings

1. VERB

/ kætʃ ˈfiːlɪŋz / (AmE), / katʃ ˈfiːlɪŋz / (BrE)
(informal) To begin to develop romantic or emotional attachment to someone.
develop romantic feelings for someone
informal

Examples

Be careful not to catch feelings too early in a new relationship.
He tried to keep things casual but ended up catching feelings anyway.
Sometimes you don't realize you've caught feelings until it's too late.

Synset Examples

It’s easy to catch feelings when you’re close.
I didn’t mean to catch feelings for him.
She worried she might catch feelings too quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fall for - to begin to feel strong romantic feelings for someone
