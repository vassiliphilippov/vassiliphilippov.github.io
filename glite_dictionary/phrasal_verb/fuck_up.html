to fuck up

1. VERB

/ fʌk ʌp / (AmE), / fʌk ʌp / (BrE)
to make a serious mistake or ruin something; to spoil or damage badly, often through carelessness or incompetence (vulgar)
to mess up or ruin (vulgar)
vulgar informal

Examples

If you fuck up this report again, the boss will be furious.
He totally fucked up the interview by forgetting the company's name.
Try not to fuck up this time; it's your last chance.

Synset Examples

I botched the dinner and we had to eat out
the pianist screwed up the difficult passage in the second movement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to screw up - to do something badly or make a mess of something (informal)
  • Synonym: botch - to carry out a task badly or carelessly
  • Synonym: to mess up - to make a mistake or spoil something
