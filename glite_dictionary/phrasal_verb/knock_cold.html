to knock cold

1. VERB

/ nɑk koʊld / (AmE), / nɒk kəʊld / (BrE)
to knock (someone) unconscious or senseless, especially with a blow
knock someone unconscious

Examples

The boxer managed to knock cold his opponent with a single punch.
If you’re not careful, that heavy door could knock cold anyone who stands behind it.
Stunned by the blow, the thief was knocked cold before he could escape.

Synset Examples

the boxing champion knocked out his opponent in a few seconds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to knock out - to make someone unconscious, especially by hitting them
