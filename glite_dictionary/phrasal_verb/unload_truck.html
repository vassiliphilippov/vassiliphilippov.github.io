to unload truck

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈloʊd trʌk / (AmE), / ʌnˈləʊd trʌk / (BrE)
to remove cargo or goods from a truck
remove goods from a truck

Examples

We had to unload truck after a long day of deliveries.
The team will unload truck and sort the packages at the warehouse.
He injured his back while helping to unload truck yesterday.

Synset Examples

Let's unload the truck before it gets dark.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: load truck - to put cargo or goods into a truck
