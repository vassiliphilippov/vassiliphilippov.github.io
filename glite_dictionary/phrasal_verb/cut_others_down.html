to cut others down

1. VERB

/ kʌt ˈʌðɚz daʊn / (AmE), / kʌt ˈʌðəz daʊn / (BrE)
to criticize or belittle other people, often in a hurtful or dismissive way
belittle or criticize others

Examples

She always tries to cut others down to make herself feel better.
As a manager, he prefers to support his team rather than cut others down.
Instead of cutting others down, you should try to encourage them.

Synset Examples

He plays the role of a cockmuncher, always cutting others down.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to belittle - make someone or something seem unimportant
  • Antonym: praise - express warm approval or admiration of someone or something
