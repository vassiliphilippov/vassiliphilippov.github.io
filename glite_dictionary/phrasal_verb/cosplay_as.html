to cosplay as

1. VERB

/ ˈkɑsˌpleɪ æz / (AmE), / ˈkɒsˌpleɪ æz / (BrE)
to dress up in costume as a specific fictional character, especially from a film, book, video game, or comic, often as part of fan culture or at conventions
dress up as a fictional character

Examples

Every Halloween, she loves to cosplay as her favorite movie heroes.
Hundreds of participants cosplayed as characters from the popular anime series at the expo.
They decided to cosplay as classic video game icons for the themed party.

Synset Examples

Many fans cosplayed as Toriel at the convention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dress up as - to wear clothing to look like another person or character

Etymology

From Japanese cosplay, blend of 'costume' and 'play', plus 'as' indicating the character role.
