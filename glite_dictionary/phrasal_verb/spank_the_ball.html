to spank the ball

1. VERB

/ spæŋk ðə bɔl / (AmE), / spæŋk ðə bɔːl / (BrE)
to hit a ball forcefully, especially in sports such as tennis, baseball, or volleyball
hit a ball forcefully (sports)

Examples

With a powerful swing, she managed to spank the ball across the court for a winner.
The batter timed the pitch perfectly and spanked the ball into left field.
Whenever he feels confident, he doesn't hesitate to spank the ball as hard as he can.

Synset Examples

She decided to spank the ball over the net.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smash - to hit something with great force, especially in sports
  • Antonym: tap the ball - to hit the ball lightly
