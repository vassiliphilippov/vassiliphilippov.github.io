to scratch off

1. VERB

/ skrætʃ ɔf / (AmE), / skrætʃ ɒf / (BrE)
to write something quickly or hurriedly, often without much care
write quickly or hurriedly

Examples

I quickly scratched off a letter before leaving for the airport.
She scratched off a note to remind herself about the appointment.
He scratched off a thank-you card after the party.

Synset Examples

She dashed off a note to her husband saying she would not be home for supper
He scratched off a thank-you note to the hostess

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to dash off - to write something in haste
  • Synonym: scrawl - to write in a hurried, careless way
