to wash up

1. VERB

/ wɑʃ ʌp / (AmE), / wɒʃ ʌp / (BrE)
(of an object or body) to be carried by water and deposited on land, especially a shore.
be carried by water onto land

Examples

Hundreds of starfish washed up after the storm.
Some debris washes up on the shore every morning.
A message in a bottle washed up on the beach years later.
