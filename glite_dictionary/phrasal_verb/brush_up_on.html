to brush up on

1. VERB

/ brʌʃ ʌp ɑn / (AmE), / brʌʃ ʌp ɒn / (BrE)
to review or improve one's knowledge of something, especially after not having used it for some time
review or refresh knowledge

Examples

I need to brush up on my French before our trip to Paris.
Teachers often brush up on classroom technology over the summer break.
She decided to brush up on her piano skills after many years away from music.

Synset Examples

She decided to brush up her math skills for the exam.
I need to brush up on my Spanish before the trip.
I need to brush up on my geekspeak for the meeting.
He took a class to brush up on his writing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revise - to review or go over material again
  • Synonym: to refresh - renew or update knowledge
