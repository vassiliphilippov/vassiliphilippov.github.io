to keep square

1. VERB

/ kip skwɛr / (AmE), / kiːp skweə / (BrE)
to maintain financial stability or ensure that accounts are balanced, with neither a surplus nor a deficit
remain financially balanced or stable

Examples

It's essential for small business owners to keep square with their finances.
After a rough year, she worked hard to keep square and avoid any debt.
The company relies on a good accountant to keep square every month.

Synset Examples

It's important to keep your finances square.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to balance the books - ensure accounts are accurate and balanced
