to put ice in

1. VERB

/ pʊt aɪs ɪn / (AmE), / pʊt aɪs ɪn / (BrE)
to add ice cubes to a drink in order to make it cold
add ice cubes to a drink

Examples

Could you put ice in my glass, please?
She always puts ice in her lemonade during summer.
Before serving, they put ice in the pitchers of tea.

Synset Examples

They put ice in the drinks to keep them cold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: add ice - to put ice cubes into something, especially a drink
