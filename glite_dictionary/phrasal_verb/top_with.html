to top with

1. VERB

/ tɑp wɪð / (AmE), / tɒp wɪð / (BrE)
to add or put something on top of something else, especially as a final layer or decoration
add as a top layer

Examples

He likes to top with fresh strawberries before serving the cake.
The chef topped with grated cheese to finish the dish.
You can top with whipped cream for extra sweetness.

Synset Examples

She topped her latte with a beautiful foam design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cover - to put something over something else so as to protect or conceal it
