to turn red

1. VERB

/ tɝn rɛd / (AmE), / tɜːn rɛd / (BrE)
to become visibly red in the face, especially from embarrassment, shame, or anger
become red-faced from emotion

Examples

When she realized her mistake in front of the crowd, she immediately turned red.
He turns red every time someone compliments him on his singing.
The little boy was turning red from embarrassment after forgetting his lines.

Synset Examples

His face turned red after the embarrassing comment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blush - to become red in the face, typically from embarrassment or shame
  • Antonym: pale - to become lighter in color, often due to shock or fear
