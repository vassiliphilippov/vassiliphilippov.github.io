to mock up

1. VERB

/ mɑk ʌp / (AmE), / mɒk ʌp / (BrE)
to construct a model or replica, often as a preliminary version for study, testing, or display
construct a model or replica

Examples

The engineers decided to mock up a full-size car interior to test the new dashboard design.
They mocked up the exhibition booth using cardboard before building the final version.
Before production, we always mock up the product to check for any design flaws.

Synset Examples

model an airplane

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prototype - an original model used as a pattern for future versions
  • Synonym: replica - an exact copy or model of something
