to extend oneself

1. VERB

/ ɪkˈstɛnd wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnd wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to make a significant effort beyond one's usual limits; to exert oneself to the utmost
make a great effort; exert oneself fully

Examples

She is always willing to extend herself to help others in need.
It's important not to extend yourself too much or you might get exhausted.
He really extended himself to make the event a success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exert oneself - to make a strenuous physical or mental effort
