to separate off

1. VERB

/ ˈsɛpəˌreɪt ɔf / (AmE), / ˈsɛpəreɪt ɒf / (BrE)
to divide or partition (something), especially by means of a physical barrier such as a wall, screen, or curtain
divide or partition by a barrier

Examples

They used a curtain to separate off the kitchen from the living area.
A temporary wall can separate off the workspace from the rest of the office.
The nursery was separated off from the main room using folding screens.

Synset Examples

screen off this part of the room

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partition - to divide into parts, especially with a barrier
  • Synonym: divide - to separate into parts or groups
