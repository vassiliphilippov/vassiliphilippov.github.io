to loll around

1. VERB

/ lɑl əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / lɒl əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to spend time in a lazy or relaxed way, doing very little
act lazily or idly

Examples

On weekends, I love to just loll around with a good book and some coffee.
The cat spent the entire afternoon lolling around in a sunny spot by the window.
Instead of getting any work done, he lolled around watching television all day.

Synset Examples

Her son is just bumming around all day

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laze around - to relax and do nothing, to be lazy
  • Antonym: work - to be engaged in physical or mental activity; the opposite of being idle
