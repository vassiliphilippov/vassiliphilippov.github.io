to agree to the terms

1. VERB

/ əˈɡriː tə ðə tɜrmz / (AmE), / əˈɡriː tə ðə tɜːmz / (BrE)
to consent to the specified conditions, especially those of a contract or agreement
consent to specified conditions

Examples

Before signing the contract, she needed time to agree to the terms.
The app will not function until users agree to the terms of service.
They finally agreed to the terms after several days of negotiation.

Synset Examples

With a nod, he agreed to the terms of the deal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accept the terms - to receive or take the conditions as offered
  • Antonym: reject the terms - to refuse to consent to the conditions
