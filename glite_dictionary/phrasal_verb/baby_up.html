to baby up

1. VERB

/ ˈbeɪ.bi ʌp / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi ʌp / (BrE)
to pamper or spoil someone by giving them excessive care and attention
pamper or spoil with too much care
informal

Examples

She loves to baby up her younger siblings, always doing everything for them.
If you baby up the puppy too much, he'll never learn to behave on his own.
They try not to baby up their son, even though he's the youngest in the family.

Synset Examples

They decided to baby up their first child with all the latest gadgets.
She tends to baby up her pets, treating them like children.
It's easy to baby up a toddler with so many toys around.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pamper - to give someone too much care or special attention
  • Synonym: spoil - to harm someone's character by giving them everything they want
