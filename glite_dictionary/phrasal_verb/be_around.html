to be around

1. VERB

/ bi əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / bi əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to be present in a place or available to help; to exist or remain in a place for a period of time
to be present or available

Examples

I'm glad you could be around this weekend to celebrate with us.
She will be around if you need any assistance during your stay.
Technology has been around for centuries, constantly evolving.

Synset Examples

I’m glad you could be around to help me.
He has been around long enough to know better.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be present - to exist or be in a particular place
