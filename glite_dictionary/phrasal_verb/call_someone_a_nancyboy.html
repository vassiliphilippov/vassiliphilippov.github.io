to call someone a nancyboy

1. VERB

/ kɔl ˈsʌmwʌn ə ˈnænsiˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / kɔːl ˈsʌmwʌn ə ˈnænsiˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
to insult or mock someone by calling them effeminate, generally in a derogatory or offensive way
to insult as effeminate (offensive)
offensive slang british

Examples

He was hurt when classmates called him a nancyboy because of his interests.
Some people still use the phrase to call someone a nancyboy, not realizing how offensive it is.
Despite being called a nancyboy by bullies, he remained proud of who he was.

Synset Examples

He embraced his style, not caring if people called him a nancyboy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insult - to speak to or treat with disrespect or scornful abuse
  • Synonym: call someone a sissy - to call someone weak or effeminate (offensive)

Etymology

'Nancy boy' is a British English slang term from early 20th century, where 'Nancy' was a feminine name used as a derogatory label for effeminate men.
