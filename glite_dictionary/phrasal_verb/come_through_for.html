to come through for

1. VERB

/ kʌm θru fɔr / (AmE), / kʌm θruː fə / (BrE)
to provide help, support, or assistance to someone when they need it most
provide needed help or support

Examples

Whenever I need advice, my older sister always comes through for me.
I'm grateful that my team came through for me during the busy season.
You can count on him to come through for you when it really matters.

Synset Examples

I knew she would come through for us in the end.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to help out - to assist someone, especially in a difficult situation
