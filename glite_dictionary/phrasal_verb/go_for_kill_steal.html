to go for kill steal

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ fəɹ kɪl stil / (AmE), / ɡəʊ fə kɪl stiːl / (BrE)
(in gaming) to attempt to secure credit for a kill by delivering the final blow, often when another player has done most of the work.
attempt to steal the final kill (gaming)
informal slang

Examples

In online matches, some players only go for kill steal rather than helping the team genuinely.
He was criticized for always going for kill steal at the last moment.
She decided not to go for kill steal and instead supported her teammate.

Synset Examples

She got a reputation for always going for kill steals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kill steal - to take the final blow and thus credit for a kill that another deserves (gaming slang)
