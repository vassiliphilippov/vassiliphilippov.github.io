to hash out a deal

1. VERB

/ hæʃ aʊt ə diːl / (AmE), / hæʃ aʊt ə diːl / (BrE)
to discuss the details of an agreement thoroughly in order to reach a final decision
negotiate an agreement through discussion

Examples

The two companies were finally able to hash out a deal after several rounds of negotiation.
It took the diplomats all night to hash out a deal that satisfied both parties.
They are currently hashing out a deal to merge their businesses.

Synset Examples

They managed to hash out a deal after hours of discussion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to negotiate - discuss to reach agreement
  • Synonym: hammer out a deal - reach agreement after hard discussion

Etymology

From the verb 'hash out' (to discuss thoroughly), likely from 'hash' meaning to chop or cut into pieces, figuratively referring to parsing details in discussion.
