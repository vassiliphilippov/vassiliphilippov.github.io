to lash together

1. VERB

/ læʃ təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / læʃ təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to fasten two or more things securely with rope or cord
fasten things with rope or cord

Examples

The sailors lashed together several logs to make a raft.
Can you lash together these poles to build a shelter?
During the storm, we quickly lashed together the barrels to keep them from drifting apart.

Synset Examples

Lash together these barrels!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tie together - to fasten or bind multiple things with string or rope
  • Synonym: bind - to fasten or secure with a cord, rope, or other means
