to island hop

1. VERB

/ ˈaɪ.lənd hɑp / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.lənd hɒp / (BrE)
to travel from one island to another, especially as part of a journey or holiday
travel from one island to another

Examples

They decided to island hop through Greece during their summer vacation.
Many tourists island hop in the Caribbean to experience different cultures and beaches.
Last year, we island hopped from Oahu to Maui and Kauai.

Synset Examples

on the cruise, we did some island-hopping
