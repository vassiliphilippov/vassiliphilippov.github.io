to chance upon

1. VERB

/ tʃæns əˈpɒn / (AmE), / tʃɑːns əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to find or discover something unexpectedly, usually by accident
find or discover by accident

Examples

While walking in the woods, they chanced upon a beautiful waterfall hidden from view.
You might chance upon a rare painting at the antique market if you're lucky.
The journalist chanced upon new evidence while researching another story.

Synset Examples

the archeologists chanced upon an old tomb
she struck a goldmine
The hikers finally struck the main path to the lake

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to come across - find or meet by chance
  • Synonym: stumble upon - find or encounter unexpectedly
