to bust a cap

1. VERB

/ bʌst ə kæp / (AmE), / bʌst ə kap / (BrE)
(slang) to shoot someone with a gun, often in a violent or threatening situation.
shoot someone (slang)
slang american

Examples

If you keep threatening him, he might just bust a cap in you.
The gangster warned he would bust a cap if anyone crossed him.
During the robbery, one of the thieves tried to bust a cap to scare the crowd.

Synset Examples

In the movie, the hero had to bust a cap to save his friend.
