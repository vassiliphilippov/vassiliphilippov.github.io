to mesh with

1. VERB

/ mɛʃ wɪð / (AmE), / mɛʃ wɪð / (BrE)
to harmonize or fit together well; to be compatible or work well in combination
to harmonize or fit together

Examples

Their work styles mesh with each other perfectly, creating a productive team environment.
Her views did not mesh with the company's vision for the future.
The new software needs to mesh with our existing systems to avoid problems.

Synset Examples

Her thoughts began to mesh with his during the discussion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fit in - to be compatible or in harmony with a group or system
  • Antonym: clash with - to conflict, not fit well together
