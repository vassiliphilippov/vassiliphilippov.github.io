to toss out

1. VERB

/ tɑs aʊt / (AmE), / tɒs aʊt / (BrE)
to throw something away; to discard or get rid of something no longer wanted
throw away; discard

Examples

She decided to toss out all the old magazines cluttering the living room.
You should toss out any expired foods from the fridge.
The committee voted to toss out the proposal after much discussion.

Synset Examples

Put away your worries

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to throw away - to get rid of something by discarding it
  • Antonym: keep - to retain in one's possession
