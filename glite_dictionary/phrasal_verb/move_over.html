to move over

1. VERB

/ muːv ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / muːv ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to change your position so that there is space for someone or something else
make room by shifting position

Examples

Could you move over a little so I can sit down?
He politely asked his coworker to move over and share the bench.
As more people entered the elevator, everyone had to move over to make space.

Synset Examples

The park gave way to a supermarket
‘Move over,’ he told the crowd

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make room - to create space for someone or something
