to cope with

1. VERB

/ koʊp wɪθ / (AmE), / kəʊp wɪð / (BrE)
to deal effectively with something difficult or challenging
deal effectively with difficulties

Examples

After her injury, she found new ways to cope with daily challenges.
It's important to cope with stress in a healthy manner.
Many parents struggle to cope with the demands of work and family life.

Synset Examples

She learned to cope with schizophrenia through therapy.
They develop strategies to cope with challenging situations.
He tried to cope with the stress of his job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deal with - to take action to solve a problem or manage a situation
