to look forward to

1. VERB

/ lʊk ˈfɔrwɚd tu / (AmE), / lʊk ˈfɔːwəd tuː / (BrE)
to anticipate something with pleasure; to be excited or eager about a future event
anticipate with pleasure

Examples

I always look forward to spending the holidays with my family.
The students look forward to the summer break every year.
She is looking forward to starting her new job next month.
