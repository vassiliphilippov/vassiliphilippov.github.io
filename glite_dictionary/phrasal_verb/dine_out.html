to dine out

1. VERB

/ daɪn aʊt / (AmE), / daɪn aʊt / (BrE)
to eat a meal at a restaurant or at someone else's home instead of at your own home
eat away from home

Examples

We prefer to dine out on special occasions rather than cook at home.
Many people dine out on weekends to enjoy different cuisines.
She loves dining out with friends at new restaurants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to eat out - to eat at a restaurant
