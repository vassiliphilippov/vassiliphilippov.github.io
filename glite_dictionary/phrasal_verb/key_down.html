to key down

1. VERB

/ ki daʊn / (AmE), / kiː daʊn / (BrE)
to write or type notes or information, especially using a keyboard or computer
write or type notes or information

Examples

The manager asked me to key down the client's feedback during the call.
She quickly keyed down the phone number as it was dictated to her.
Please key down all the important points from today's seminar.

Synset Examples

Please key down your notes during the lecture.
I need you to key down the meeting minutes.
Can you key down that information for me?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: type - to write by pressing keys on a keyboard
  • Synonym: to write down - to record information by writing it
