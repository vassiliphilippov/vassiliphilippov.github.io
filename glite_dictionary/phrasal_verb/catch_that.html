to catch that

1. VERB

/ kætʃ ðæt / (AmE), / katʃ ðat / (BrE)
to hear and understand something that was said, especially when it may not have been clear
to hear and understand (something said)

Examples

Sorry, I didn't catch that—could you repeat the last part?
Did you catch that joke he made during the meeting?
I can't believe I caught that over all the background noise.

Synset Examples

I didn't catch that, could you say again?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get that - to understand or hear what was said
  • Antonym: miss that - to fail to hear or understand something that was said
