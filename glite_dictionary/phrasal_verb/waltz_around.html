to waltz around

1. VERB

/ wɔlts əˈɹaʊnd / (AmE), / wɔːlts əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to dance a waltz around a room or area, moving gracefully in the manner of the waltz.
to dance a waltz in a space

Examples

During the ball, couples would waltz around the grand ballroom all evening.
She loves to waltz around the living room when her favorite song comes on.
The dancers waltzed around the stage with effortless grace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waltz - to dance in triple time as in a waltz
