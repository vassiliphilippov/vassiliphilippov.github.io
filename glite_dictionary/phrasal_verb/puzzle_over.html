to puzzle over

1. VERB

/ ˈpʌzəl ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈpʌzəl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to think carefully about something because it is difficult to understand or solve
think hard to solve a problem

Examples

She puzzled over the strange message for hours, trying to make sense of it.
Scientists have puzzled over this phenomenon for decades with no clear answer.
As I was puzzling over the crossword, my coffee went cold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ponder - to think about something carefully, especially before making a decision
  • Synonym: to figure out - to succeed in understanding or solving something
