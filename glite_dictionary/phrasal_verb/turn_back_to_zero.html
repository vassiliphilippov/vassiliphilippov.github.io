to turn back to zero

1. VERB

/ tɝn bæk tə ˈzɪəɹoʊ / (AmE), / tɜːn bæk tə ˈzɪərəʊ / (BrE)
to reset an instrument, gauge, or counter to its starting position or zero reading
reset an instrument to zero

Examples

Before weighing the next item, remember to turn back to zero the kitchen scale.
The technician carefully turned back to zero the counter after each measurement cycle.
You should always turn back to zero the gauge before starting a new experiment.

Synset Examples

I need to turn the scale back to zero before weighing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reset - to set again or differently, especially to a starting position
