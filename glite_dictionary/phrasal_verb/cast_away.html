to cast away

1. VERB

/ kæst əˈweɪ / (AmE), / kɑːst əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to throw something away or get rid of it, especially something unwanted or unnecessary
throw or get rid of something

Examples

She decided to cast away all the old letters she had kept for years.
It's time to cast away your doubts and start the new project.
They cast away any items that were broken during the move.

Synset Examples

Put away your worries

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discard - to throw something away; to get rid of something as unwanted
  • Antonym: keep - to retain or continue to have something; not throw away
