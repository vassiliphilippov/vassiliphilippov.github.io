to chisel away

1. VERB

/ ˈtʃɪzəl əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪzəl əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to gradually remove or reduce something, usually by persistent effort
gradually remove or reduce

Examples

Over the years, the river chiseled away the rock to form a deep canyon.
She worked every day to chisel away her debt until it was gone.
With patience, the sculptor chisels away at the marble to reveal the statue inside.

Synset Examples

With a steady hand, he began to chisel away the excess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to chip away - to gradually remove small pieces from something
