to totes forget

1. VERB

/ ˈtoʊts fərˈɡɛt / (AmE), / ˈtəʊts fəˈɡɛt / (BrE)
(informal) To completely forget something; to fail to remember at all.
informal: completely forget
informal slang

Examples

I totes forgot to bring my wallet to the store.
She totes forgot about the meeting until it was too late.
We totes forgot to invite Sarah to the party, and now I feel terrible.

Synset Examples

I totes forgot about our lunch date today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: totally forget - to forget something completely

Etymology

Blend of 'totes' (slang shortening of 'totally') and 'forget'.
