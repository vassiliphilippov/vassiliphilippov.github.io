to make someone smile

1. VERB

/ meɪk ˈsʌmwʌn smaɪl / (AmE), / meɪk ˈsʌmwən smaɪl / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel happy or amused, often resulting in them smiling
cause someone to feel happy or amused

Examples

Your thoughtful note really made me smile after a difficult day.
He always knows how to make someone smile with his silly stories.
The puppy's playful antics made her smile instantly.

Synset Examples

'Arry can always make you smile with his jokes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cheer up - to make someone feel happier or less sad
  • Antonym: make someone cry - to cause someone to feel sad or emotional to the point of tears
