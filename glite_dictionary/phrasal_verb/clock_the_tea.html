to clock the tea

1. VERB

/ klɑk ðə ti / (AmE), / klɒk ðə tiː / (BrE)
to monitor or keep track of the time while tea is brewing to ensure proper steeping
time the brewing of tea

Examples

It's important to clock the tea to avoid over-steeping it.
She forgot to clock the tea and it turned out too strong.
He uses his phone to clock the tea every morning.

Synset Examples

Remember to clock the tea; it needs five minutes.
Make sure to clock the tea before it overbrews.
He always forgets to clock the tea, and it gets bitter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: time - to measure the duration of something; to keep track of how long something takes
