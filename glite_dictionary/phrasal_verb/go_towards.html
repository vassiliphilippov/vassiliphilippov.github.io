to go towards

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ təˈwɔrdz / (AmE), / ɡəʊ təˈwɔːdz / (BrE)
to be used or contributed for a particular purpose, especially money or resources.
be contributed or used for a purpose

Examples

All ticket sales will go towards rebuilding the community center.
A portion of her salary goes towards paying for her daughter's education.
The money raised at the event went towards medical supplies for the hospital.

Synset Examples

They plan to go towards funding the new project.
All donations will go towards the animal shelter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contribute to - to give in order to help achieve or provide something
