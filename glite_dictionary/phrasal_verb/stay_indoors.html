to stay indoors

1. VERB

/ steɪ ɪnˈdɔrz / (AmE), / steɪ ɪnˈdɔːz / (BrE)
to remain inside a building, especially to avoid bad weather or danger
remain inside a building

Examples

During the severe storm, we were advised to stay indoors for our safety.
If it gets too hot outside, I prefer to stay indoors and read.
Doctors recommended that people with allergies should stay indoors when the pollen count is high.

Synset Examples

We decided to stay indoors because it was going to rain.
What with the rain, we decided to stay indoors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stay inside - to remain within a building or enclosed space
