to close the window

1. VERB

/ kloʊz ðə ˈwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / kləʊz ðə ˈwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
to shut a window so that it is not open
shut a window

Examples

Could you please close the window? It's getting chilly in here.
She remembered to close the window before leaving the house.
After hearing the noise outside, he quickly got up to close the window.

Synset Examples

We need to close the window before it rains.
The garbage stank so badly that we had to close the windows.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: open the window - to make a window open
  • Synonym: shut the window - to make a window not open
