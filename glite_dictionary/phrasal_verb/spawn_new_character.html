to spawn new character

1. VERB

/ spɔn nu ˈkærəktɚ / (AmE), / spɔːn njuː ˈkærəktə / (BrE)
(in video games) to create a new character that appears in the game, often as a player or non-player entity.
create a new character in a video game

Examples

Some games let you spawn new characters whenever your avatar dies.
Players can spawn new characters to explore different roles and abilities.
After the update, it's easier to spawn new characters with unique skills.

Synset Examples

The game allows players to spawn new characters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to create - to bring (something) into existence
  • Antonym: to delete - to remove or erase (especially a character or entity) in a computer context

Etymology

From 'spawn', originally meaning to produce offspring, used figuratively in computing and gaming.
