to kill it

1. VERB

/ ˈkɪl ɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɪl ɪt / (BrE)
to perform exceptionally well; to succeed with great skill or impressively in a task or activity
perform exceptionally well
informal

Examples

She practiced for weeks and really killed it in the final performance.
If you keep working this hard, you'll kill it at your next interview.
Despite his nerves, he absolutely killed it during the presentation.

Synset Examples

They always kill it when performing live.
He hopes to kill it in his new job.
She really killed it at the talent show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nail it - to do something perfectly or successfully
  • Synonym: ace - to perform very well or succeed easily at something
