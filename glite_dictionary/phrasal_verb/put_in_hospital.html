to put in hospital

1. VERB

/ pʊt ɪn ˈhɑːspɪtl̩ / (AmE), / pʊt ɪn ˈhɒspɪtl̩ / (BrE)
to admit or cause someone to be admitted to a hospital for medical treatment, usually as a result of illness or injury
admit to a hospital for treatment

Examples

The car accident put him in hospital for nearly a month.
Her severe infection put her in hospital unexpectedly.
After the fall, doctors had no choice but to put him in hospital for observation.

Synset Examples

His actions put her in hospital after the confrontation.
The accident put him in hospital for several weeks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to hospitalize - to admit someone to hospital; to place in a hospital as a patient
