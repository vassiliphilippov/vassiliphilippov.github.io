to ship out

1. VERB

/ ʃɪp aʊt / (AmE), / ʃɪp aʊt / (BrE)
to send goods or items to customers or other recipients, usually by mail or freight
send goods by mail or freight

Examples

The company will ship out your order within two business days.
Please ship out these parts to our overseas branch as soon as possible.
We need to ship out the holiday packages before the deadline.

Synset Examples

We need to ship out the orders by Friday.
Make sure to ship out the gifts before the holidays.
They will ship out the new products next month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dispatch - to send goods or packages to a recipient
  • Antonym: to receive - to get or be given something that was sent
