to stitch up

1. VERB

/ stɪtʃ ʌp / (AmE), / stɪtʃ ʌp / (BrE)
to mend, repair, or finish a hem or seam by sewing it together
sew a hem or seam closed

Examples

She had to stitch up the tear in her skirt before leaving for work.
Can you stitch up this sleeve for me while I get the thread?
He quickly stitched up the hem that had come loose.

Synset Examples

I need to stitch up this hem before the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sew up - to close or repair by sewing
