to have a blast

1. VERB

/ hæv ə blæst / (AmE), / hæv ə blɑːst / (BrE)
to have a very enjoyable and fun time; to enjoy oneself greatly
enjoy oneself greatly; have lots of fun
informal

Examples

The kids were having a blast at the amusement park all day long.
We had a blast celebrating Jamie's birthday last night.
Even though it rained, everyone was having a blast at the festival.

Synset Examples

This party is the shizzle; everyone's having a blast.
The party was chavtastic, with everyone having a blast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: have fun - to enjoy oneself
  • Antonym: be bored - to feel uninterested or unamused
