to confer with

1. VERB

/ kənˈfɜr wɪð / (AmE), / kənˈfɜː wɪð / (BrE)
to discuss something with someone in order to exchange ideas or reach a decision
discuss to make a decision

Examples

The management team will confer with legal experts before making a final decision.
She often confers with her colleagues to ensure everyone agrees on the project plan.
After the presentation, we conferred with the board members to review the results.

Synset Examples

Consult your local broker
They had to consult before arriving at a decision

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to consult - to seek advice or information from someone
