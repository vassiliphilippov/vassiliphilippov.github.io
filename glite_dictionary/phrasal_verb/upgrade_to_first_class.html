to upgrade to first class

1. VERB

/ ˈʌpˌɡreɪd tu fɝst klæs / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌɡreɪd tuː fɜːst klɑːs / (BrE)
to be moved or to move into a higher and more expensive class of service, especially on an aircraft or train, typically from economy or business class to first class.
move to a higher travel class

Examples

After arriving at the gate, I was upgraded to first class for free.
He hopes to upgrade to first class using his frequent flyer points.
Many travelers dream of upgrading to first class on long-haul flights.

Synset Examples

He got upgraded to first class for being a frequent flyer.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: downgrade - to move to a lower or less expensive class or status
