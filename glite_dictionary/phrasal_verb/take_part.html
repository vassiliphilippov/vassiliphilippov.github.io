to take part

1. VERB

/ teɪk pɑrt / (AmE), / teɪk pɑːt / (BrE)
to participate or share in an activity or event with others
participate in an activity

Examples

Many students chose to take part in the science competition this year.
She was eager to take part in the community clean-up event.
All employees are encouraged to take part in the discussion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to participate - to take part in something
  • Antonym: to sit out - to not participate or take part in something
