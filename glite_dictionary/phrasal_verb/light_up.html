to light up

1. VERB

/ laɪt ʌp / (AmE), / laɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to begin to burn with a bright flame; to become ignited and luminous
begin to burn brightly

Examples

The logs in the fireplace lit up as soon as I struck the match.
The streetlights light up the entire avenue at dusk.
He stood back as the campfire lit up the surrounding darkness.

Synset Examples

The coal in the BBQ grill finally lit up

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ignite - to catch fire or cause to catch fire
