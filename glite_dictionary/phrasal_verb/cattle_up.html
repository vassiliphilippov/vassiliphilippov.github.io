to cattle up

1. VERB

/ ˈkæt̬l ʌp / (AmE), / ˈkætl ʌp / (BrE)
to gather or round up cattle or other livestock, typically for management or transport
gather or round up livestock

Examples

The ranchers worked together to cattle up before the storm arrived.
We always cattle up at sunset to keep the herd safe from predators.
Someone needs to cattle up the strays that wandered near the creek.
