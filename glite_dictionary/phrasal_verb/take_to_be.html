to take to be

1. VERB

/ teɪk tə bi / (AmE), / teɪk tə bi / (BrE)
to regard or consider someone or something as being a particular thing; to assume or suppose to be.
consider or regard as

Examples

Many people take to be harmless what is actually quite dangerous.
She took him to be a doctor because of his white coat.
I took her to be your sister when I first met her.

Synset Examples

she looked on this affair as a joke
He thinks of himself as a brilliant musician
He is reputed to be intelligent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to consider - to think of or regard in a particular way
  • Synonym: to regard as - to think of as; to consider someone or something to be
