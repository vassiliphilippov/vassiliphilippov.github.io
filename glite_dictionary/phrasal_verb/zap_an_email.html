to zap (an email)

1. VERB

/ zæp ən ˈiːˌmɛɪl / (AmE), / zæp ən ˈiːˌmeɪl / (BrE)
to send an email rapidly and with little delay, often in an informal or urgent context
send an email quickly
informal

Examples

Could you zap that email over before the end of the meeting?
She always zaps that email to me the moment she gets important news.
I’ll zap that email to the team so everyone stays updated.

Synset Examples

Please zap that email to me as soon as possible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: send (an email) - to cause an email to go from one electronic address to another

Etymology

Originally 'zap' meant to destroy or strike suddenly (from comic book sound effects), later adapted to mean acting or transmitting quickly.
