to introduce oneself

1. VERB

/ ˌɪn.trəˈdus wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trəˈdjuːs wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to present yourself to others by stating your name or identity
announce one's own name or identity

Examples

It can feel awkward to introduce oneself at large gatherings, but it's a good way to meet new people.
Before the meeting began, each participant stood up to introduce oneself to the group.
She always takes a moment to introduce herself whenever she joins a new team.

Synset Examples

She introduced herself, saying, 'I am mademoiselle.'
She introduced herself as Meagain at the reunion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to introduce - to make someone known by name to another
