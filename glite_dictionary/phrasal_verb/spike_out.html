to spike out

1. VERB

/ spaɪk aʊt / (AmE), / spaɪk aʊt / (BrE)
(of a plant) to produce a spike or elongated flowering stem.
of a plant, to produce a spike

Examples

The orchids in the greenhouse are starting to spike out this spring.
Many bulbs will spike out before producing any visible blooms.
As the lilies spike out, gardeners anticipate a burst of color in the beds.

Synset Examples

my hyacinths and orchids are spiking now

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flower - to produce flowers or bloom (of a plant)
