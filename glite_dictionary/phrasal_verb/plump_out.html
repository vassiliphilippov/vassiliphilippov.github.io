to plump out

1. VERB

/ plʌmp aʊt / (AmE), / plʌmp aʊt / (BrE)
to make (someone or something) fatter or rounder; to fill out in size or shape
make fatter or fuller

Examples

A generous meal will surely plump out the kittens.
His cheeks have begun to plump out since he started eating better.
The cook tried to plump out the stew with extra vegetables.

Synset Examples

We will plump out that poor starving child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fatten - to make fatter or increase weight
  • Antonym: to slim down - to make or become thinner or slimmer
