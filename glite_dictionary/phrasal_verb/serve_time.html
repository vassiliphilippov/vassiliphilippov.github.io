to serve time

1. VERB

/ sɝv taɪm / (AmE), / sɜːv taɪm / (BrE)
to spend a period in prison as punishment for a crime
spend a period in prison

Examples

He hoped to turn his life around after he served time for robbery.
Many inmates serve time away from their families, often missing important events.
She is currently serving time in a state penitentiary for fraud.

Synset Examples

He had to serve time for his mistakes.
He learned a lot while serving time in prison.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: do time - spend time in prison
