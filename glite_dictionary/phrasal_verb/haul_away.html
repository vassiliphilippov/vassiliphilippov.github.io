to haul away

1. VERB

/ hɔl əˈweɪ / (AmE), / hɔːl əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to take away or remove something, especially large or heavy items, using a vehicle
remove by vehicle

Examples

The company will haul away our old refrigerator tomorrow morning.
After the concert, workers hauled away the stage equipment by truck.
They need to haul away all the debris left after the storm.

Synset Examples

They carted off the old furniture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cart off - to take something away, especially with effort or by vehicle
  • Antonym: to bring - to take something or someone to a place (opposite direction)
