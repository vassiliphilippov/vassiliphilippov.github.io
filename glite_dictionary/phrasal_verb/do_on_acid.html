to do on acid

1. VERB

/ du ɑn ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / duː ɒn ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
to create or perform something while under the influence of LSD (acid), or to make something appear psychedelic or surreal as if influenced by LSD.
create or perform under the influence of LSD
informal slang

Examples

The mural looked like it was done on acid with all those swirling patterns and neon colors.
He wrote that poem as if he had done it on acid.
Some of their music videos seem to be done on acid, full of bizarre and trippy imagery.

Synset Examples

The colors in the painting looked vibrant, almost like it was done on acid.

Etymology

Refers to LSD, commonly called 'acid,' a hallucinogenic drug; 'do' meaning 'make' or 'perform'.
