to fall under

1. VERB

/ fɔl ˈʌndɚ / (AmE), / fɔːl ˈʌndə / (BrE)
to be included in or classified as part of a particular category or group
be classified in a category

Examples

These regulations fall under the new workplace safety policy.
Academic misconduct falls under the category of serious offenses.
Does this kind of transaction fall under taxable income?

Synset Examples

This falls under the rubric ‘various’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be included in - to be part of a whole or group
  • Synonym: be classified as - to be assigned to a category
