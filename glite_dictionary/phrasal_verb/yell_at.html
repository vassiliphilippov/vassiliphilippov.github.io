to yell at

1. VERB

/ jɛl æt / (AmE), / jɛl æt / (BrE)
to shout angrily at someone, often as an expression of anger or frustration
shout angrily at someone

Examples

She promised not to yell at the children when they made mistakes.
I get nervous when my boss starts to yell at people during meetings.
If you yell at your dog, it may become scared or confused.

Synset Examples

Please don't yell at me; I was just asking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shout at - to speak very loudly to someone, often angrily or to get attention
  • Antonym: speak calmly to - to address someone in a calm and moderate tone
