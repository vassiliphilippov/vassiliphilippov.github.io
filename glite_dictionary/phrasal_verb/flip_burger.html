to flip burger

1. VERB

/ flɪp ˈbɝːɡɚ / (AmE), / flɪp ˈbɜːɡə / (BrE)
to cook hamburgers by turning them over on a grill, especially as part of a job in a casual or fast food setting
cook hamburgers by turning them over

Examples

She got her first job at a fast-food restaurant, where she had to flip burgers during busy lunch hours.
He spent all weekend flipping burgers at the family barbecue in the backyard.
Many people have flipped burgers at some point in their lives while working summer jobs.

Synset Examples

She can flip burgers like a pro at the diner.
He learned to flip burgers at his first job.
During summer, I often flip burgers at the barbecue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grill - to cook food over direct heat
