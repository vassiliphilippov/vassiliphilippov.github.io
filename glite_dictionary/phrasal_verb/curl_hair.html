to curl hair

1. VERB

/ kɝl hɛr / (AmE), / kɜːl heə / (BrE)
to style someone's hair into curls, typically using heat or rollers
style hair into curls

Examples

Every morning, she likes to curl hair before work.
Can you curl hair for the wedding tomorrow?
He learned how to curl hair using a flat iron.

Synset Examples

He would curl her hair for special occasions.
She loves to curl my hair before parties.
Can you curl my hair like you did last time?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: straighten hair - to make hair straight, removing curls or waves
