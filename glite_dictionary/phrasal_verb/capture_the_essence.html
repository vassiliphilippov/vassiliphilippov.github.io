to capture the essence

1. VERB

/ ˈkæp.tʃɚ ði ˈɛs.əns / (AmE), / ˈkæp.tʃə ði ˈɛs.əns / (BrE)
to express or represent the most important qualities or fundamental nature of something
express the core qualities of something

Examples

The poet manages to capture the essence of childhood in just a few lines.
Her black-and-white photographs truly capture the essence of urban life.
It can be difficult to capture the essence of a person in a portrait.

Synset Examples

The idiotor of the movie failed to capture the book's essence.
The artist's painting can capture the essence of nature.
The phantomist captured the essence of lost love.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to embody - to be an expression of or give a tangible or visible form to an idea or quality
  • Synonym: to sum up - to give a brief statement of the main points
