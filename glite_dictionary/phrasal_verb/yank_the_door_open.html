to yank the door open

1. VERB

/ jæŋk ðə dɔr ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / jæŋk ðə dɔː ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
to pull a door forcefully in order to open it
pull a door open forcefully

Examples

She had to yank the door open when it got stuck.
I watched him yank the door open and storm outside, clearly upset.
In his hurry, Tom nearly broke the handle as he yanked the door open.
