to fall into

1. VERB

/ fɔl ˈɪntu / (AmE), / fɔːl ˈɪntuː / (BrE)
to be included in or classified as a particular category or group
be classified as; belong to a category

Examples

Many books on this topic fall into the category of science fiction.
Complaints about late deliveries usually fall into two main types.
These products fall into three different price ranges.

Synset Examples

This falls under the rubric ‘various’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to belong to - to be the property of or be included in something
  • Synonym: to fall under - to be included in or classified within a category
