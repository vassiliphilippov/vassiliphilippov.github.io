to poop out

1. VERB

/ pup aʊt / (AmE), / puːp aʊt / (BrE)
(informal) To become exhausted or to stop functioning due to a lack of energy.
become exhausted; stop functioning
informal

Examples

After running for an hour, I started to poop out and had to slow down.
The old lawn mower finally pooped out in the middle of the yard.
They were full of energy at the start, but soon pooped out from the heat.

Synset Examples

At the end of the march, I pooped out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to tire out - to cause someone to feel very tired
  • Synonym: to run out of steam - (idiom) to lose energy, enthusiasm, or strength
