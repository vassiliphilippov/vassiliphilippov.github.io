to think twice

1. VERB

/ θɪŋk twaɪs / (AmE), / θɪŋk twaɪs / (BrE)
to consider carefully before taking action, especially to avoid making a mistake
consider carefully before acting

Examples

You should always think twice before making a major purchase.
She didn't think twice about helping a stranger in need.
We need to think twice before accepting the offer.

Synset Examples

Think twice before you have a child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to reconsider - to consider again, especially with the possibility of change
  • Antonym: act impulsively - to act without careful consideration
