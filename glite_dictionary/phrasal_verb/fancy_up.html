to fancy up

1. VERB

/ ˈfænsi ʌp / (AmE), / ˈfænsi ʌp / (BrE)
to dress or decorate oneself or something in a way that is more stylish, elaborate, or attractive than usual
make look more stylish or attractive
informal

Examples

He decided to fancy up his apartment with new curtains and colorful pillows.
They always fancy up the front yard with lights for the holidays.
She likes to fancy up before going out to special events.

Synset Examples

She never dresses up, even when she goes to the opera
The young girls were all fancied up for the party

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to dress up - to put on formal or special clothes
