to run late

1. VERB

/ rʌn leɪt / (AmE), / rʌn leɪt / (BrE)
to be delayed or arrive later than expected or planned
to be delayed; not on time

Examples

I'm going to run late for the meeting if this traffic doesn't clear up soon.
She apologized for running late and promised to be there as soon as possible.
He often runs late because he underestimates how long things will take.

Synset Examples

She tends to run late when getting ready.
I might run late due to traffic.
Oh my heck, we are running late again!
He always runs late for his appointments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be late - to not arrive at the expected or agreed time
