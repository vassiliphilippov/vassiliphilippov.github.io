to lay on

1. VERB

/ leɪ ɑn / (AmE), / leɪ ɒn / (BrE)
to add or provide something, especially for a particular purpose
add or provide (something, esp. for a purpose)

Examples

They decided to lay on extra buses to handle the concert crowd.
Can you lay on some refreshments for the meeting?
The hotel will lay on entertainment for its guests every evening.

Synset Examples

I’ll lay on the extra blankets for warmth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to provide - to make available or supply
