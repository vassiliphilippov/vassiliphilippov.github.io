to adhere to

1. VERB

/ ədˈhɪr tu / (AmE), / ədˈhɪə tuː / (BrE)
to act in accordance with a rule, law, agreement, or set of instructions; to follow closely or obey
follow or obey rules or guidelines

Examples

All participants must adhere to the safety guidelines during the experiment.
It is important to adhere to the policies set by the organization.
If you adhere to these instructions, you will avoid common mistakes.

Synset Examples

Make sure to adhere to the guidelines for safety.
You must adhere to the rules during the competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comply with - to act in accordance with rules or orders
  • Antonym: to violate - to break or disregard a rule or law

Etymology

From Latin 'adhaerēre', meaning 'to stick to'.
