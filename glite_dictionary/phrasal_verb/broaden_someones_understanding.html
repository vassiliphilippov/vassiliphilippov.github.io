to broaden someone's understanding

1. VERB

/ ˈbrɔːdn ˈsʌmwʌnz ˌʌndərˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdn ˈsʌmwʌnz ˌʌndəˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
to make someone know or understand more about a particular subject or the world in general
expand someone's knowledge or awareness

Examples

Traveling abroad can broaden someone's understanding of different cultures and global issues.
The workshop aims to broaden your understanding of renewable energy technologies.
Reading widely in various fields has really broadened my understanding of science and philosophy.

Synset Examples

Learning about Qaren customs has broadened my understanding of the community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expand someone's knowledge - increase what someone knows
  • Synonym: widen someone's perspective - make someone consider more viewpoints
