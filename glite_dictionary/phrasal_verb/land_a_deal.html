to land a deal

1. VERB

/ lænd ə diːl / (AmE), / lænd ə diːl / (BrE)
to successfully secure or obtain a business agreement or contract
successfully secure a business agreement

Examples

After months of negotiations, the company finally landed a deal with a major retailer.
It's not easy to land a deal in such a competitive market.
She hopes to land a deal that will launch her business to the next level.

Synset Examples

They hope to land a deal by the end of the month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: close a deal - to bring a business agreement to a successful conclusion
  • Antonym: lose a deal - to fail to secure a business agreement
