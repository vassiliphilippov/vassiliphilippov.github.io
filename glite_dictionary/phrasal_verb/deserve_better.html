to deserve better

1. VERB

/ dɪˈzɝv ˈbɛtər / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːv ˈbɛtə / (BrE)
to be worthy of something more favorable, superior, or appropriate than what one currently has or receives
be worthy of something superior

Examples

After years of hard work, she truly deserves better than a thankless job.
Everyone agreed that the city deserves better public transportation than it currently has.
You shouldn't settle for less; you deserve better in life.

Synset Examples

She thinks he deserves better than that.
