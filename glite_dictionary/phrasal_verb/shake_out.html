to shake out

1. VERB

/ ʃeɪk aʊt / (AmE), / ʃeɪk aʊt / (BrE)
to remove dust or debris from something by shaking it vigorously
remove dust by shaking

Examples

She took the rug outside to shake out all the dust.
Before packing away the tablecloth, make sure to shake out the crumbs.
He shook out his jacket to remove the dirt after hiking.

Synset Examples

She will shake out the blanket before using it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to dust off - to remove dust from something, typically using a cloth or by brushing
