to free up space

1. VERB

/ fri ʌp speɪs / (AmE), / friː ʌp speɪs / (BrE)
to make more room or capacity available by removing or reorganizing things
make room or capacity available

Examples

Deleting old messages can free up space on your phone.
We need to free up space in the garage for the new car.
He regularly organizes his computer files to free up space on his hard drive.

Synset Examples

They decided to jettison old files to free up space.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clear space - remove items to make space available
