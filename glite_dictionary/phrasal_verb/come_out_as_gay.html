to come out as gay

1. VERB

/ kʌm aʊt æz ɡeɪ / (AmE), / kʌm aʊt əz ɡeɪ / (BrE)
to publicly reveal that one is homosexual, often for the first time
reveal one's homosexuality

Examples

After years of secrecy, he finally came out as gay to his family.
She decided to come out as gay at the school assembly, inspiring others to share their stories.
Many people find it difficult to come out as gay in conservative communities.

Synset Examples

He proudly came out as gay to his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to come out of the closet - to reveal previously concealed sexual orientation or identity

Etymology

From the slang phrase 'come out of the closet', meaning to make a previously concealed identity known publicly.
