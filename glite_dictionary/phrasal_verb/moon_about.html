to moon about

1. VERB

/ mun əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / muːn əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to behave in a dreamy, listless, or despondent manner, often as a result of longing or sadness
wander or act in a dreamy, listless way

Examples

He spent the afternoon mooning about the house after his friend moved away.
Stop mooning about and do something productive!
She was mooning about the garden, lost in her own thoughts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to mope - to be sunk in dejection or listless apathy
  • Synonym: to moon around - to act in a dreamy or listless manner
