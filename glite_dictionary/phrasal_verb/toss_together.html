to toss together

1. VERB

/ tɑs təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / tɒs təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to mix ingredients quickly and casually, often without much preparation or precision
mix ingredients casually

Examples

She likes to toss together a pasta dish when she doesn't have much time to cook.
You can toss together a salad with just a few fresh ingredients from the fridge.
He quickly tossed together the marinade before grilling the chicken.

Synset Examples

He tossed together a quick salad before heading out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mix - to combine two or more substances so that they become a single substance
