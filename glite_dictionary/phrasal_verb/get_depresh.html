to get depresh

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt dɪˈprɛʃ / (AmE), / ɡɛt dɪˈprɛʃ / (BrE)
To become depressed or feel down, often used informally or humorously.
become depressed (slang, informal)
informal slang

Examples

After the holidays, I always get depresh when everyone leaves.
He starts to get depresh if it rains for too many days in a row.
Whenever she fails a test, she tends to get depresh for a while.

Synset Examples

He tends to get depresh when he doesn't get enough sleep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get depressed - become depressed or feel down
  • Antonym: to cheer up - become happier, recover from sadness

Etymology

Blend of 'get' and clipped form 'depresh' from 'depressed.'
