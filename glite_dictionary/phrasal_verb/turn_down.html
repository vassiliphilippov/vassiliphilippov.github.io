to turn down

1. VERB

/ tɝn daʊn / (AmE), / tɜːn daʊn / (BrE)
to reduce the level, volume, or intensity of something, especially sound or heat
reduce volume, intensity, etc.

Examples

Could you turn down the music a bit? It's too loud.
He turned down the heat because the room was getting too warm.
Please turn down the television so we can talk.
