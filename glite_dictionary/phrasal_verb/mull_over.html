to mull over

1. VERB

/ mʌl ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / mʌl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to think carefully and deeply about something before making a decision or reaching a conclusion
think about something carefully

Examples

She needed more time to mull over the proposal before giving her answer.
I often mull over past conversations, wondering if I said the right thing.
He sat by the window, quietly mulling over his recent career choices.

Synset Examples

I mulled over the events of the afternoon
philosophers have speculated on the question of God for thousands of years
The scientist must stop to observe and start to excogitate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ponder - to think about something carefully
  • Synonym: to consider - to think about carefully, especially in decision making
  • Antonym: to dismiss - to decide not to think about something

Etymology

Likely from 'mull' (to ponder), origin uncertain, possibly from Middle English 'mullen' (to grind), metaphorically extended to 'turn over in the mind'.
