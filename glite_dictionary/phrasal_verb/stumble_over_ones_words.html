to stumble over one's words

1. VERB

/ ˈstʌmbəl ˈoʊvər wʌnz wɜrdz / (AmE), / ˈstʌmbəl ˈəʊvə wʌnz wɜːdz / (BrE)
to make mistakes or hesitate while speaking, often because of nervousness or uncertainty
make mistakes in speaking

Examples

When giving his speech, he began to stumble over his words and lost his place.
If you stumble over your words during the interview, just pause and start again.
She often stumbles over her words when asked a difficult question.

Synset Examples

I tend to stumble over my words when nervous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to falter - to speak hesitantly or unsteadily
  • Antonym: speak fluently - to speak smoothly and without mistakes
