to swing by

1. VERB

/ swɪŋ baɪ / (AmE), / swɪŋ baɪ / (BrE)
to visit a place briefly, especially on the way to somewhere else
make a brief visit
informal

Examples

Let's swing by the supermarket on our way home and pick up some bread.
If you have a minute, could you swing by my apartment this afternoon?
I'll swing by your desk after the meeting to drop off the report.

Synset Examples

Let’s swing by the café for coffee.
Can you swing by my office later?
I’ll swing by the store after work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to drop by - to visit someone or somewhere informally and briefly
  • Synonym: to call in - to pay a short, informal visit
