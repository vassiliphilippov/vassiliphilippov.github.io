to bless someone with

1. VERB

/ blɛs ˈsʌmwʌn wɪð / (AmE), / blɛs ˈsʌmwən wɪð / (BrE)
to give someone a desirable quality, gift, or advantage
give someone a gift or advantage

Examples

Her parents blessed her with unwavering support throughout her career.
We felt truly fortunate when life blessed us with such loyal friends.
The artist was blessed with remarkable creativity from a young age.

Synset Examples

She blessed him with a beautiful necklace for his birthday.
They blessed us with their presence at the event.
The teacher blessed her students with wisdom and encouragement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endow someone with - to provide or equip someone with a quality or ability
