to seek adventure

1. VERB

/ siːk ədˈvɛn.tʃɚ / (AmE), / siːk ədˈvɛn.tʃə / (BrE)
to look for new and exciting experiences, often involving some risk or novelty
look for exciting experiences

Examples

Many young people travel the world to seek adventure and broaden their horizons.
He decided to leave his regular job and seek adventure in the mountains.
She joined the scientific expedition, eager to seek adventure beyond her quiet hometown.

Synset Examples

Becoming a mercenary was his way of seeking adventure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pursue excitement - actively look for thrilling experiences
