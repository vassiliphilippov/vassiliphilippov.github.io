to use the washroom

1. VERB

/ juːs ðə ˈwɑʃˌruːm / (AmE), / juːz ðiː ˈwɒʃruːm / (BrE)
to go to the toilet; to visit a restroom, often expressed as a polite or indirect way of stating the need to urinate or defecate
go to the toilet (polite phrase)

Examples

Could I use the washroom before we leave?
He excused himself to use the washroom during the dinner.
If you need to use the washroom, it's down the hall to the right.

Synset Examples

I’ll be back; I need to use the washroom.
She asked if she could use the washroom during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to go to the bathroom - to go to the toilet; alternative polite euphemism
  • Synonym: to relieve oneself - to urinate or defecate (formal/euphemistic)
