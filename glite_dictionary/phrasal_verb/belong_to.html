to belong to

1. VERB

/ bɪˈlɔŋ tu / (AmE), / bɪˈlɒŋ tuː / (BrE)
to be part of or connected with a particular group, place, or collection
be part of or connected with something

Examples

Those books belong to the library, so you must return them.
Does this jacket belong to you or someone else?
Some of the fossils belong to a previously unknown species.

Synset Examples

the uniform looks like it belonged to a museum collection
These pages don't belong

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be part of - to be included in or form a part of something
