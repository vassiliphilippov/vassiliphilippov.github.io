to lift weights

1. VERB

/ lɪft weɪts / (AmE), / lɪft weɪts / (BrE)
to exercise by raising and lowering heavy objects for the purpose of building muscle strength or fitness
exercise by lifting heavy objects

Examples

She goes to the gym every morning to lift weights before work.
He started to lift weights to improve his physical strength and endurance.
Many athletes lift weights as part of their training program.

Synset Examples

Everydude at the gym seems to be lifting heavier weights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weightlifting - the sport or activity of lifting heavy weights
