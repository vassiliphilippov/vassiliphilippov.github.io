to work in

1. VERB

/ wɝːk ɪn / (AmE), / wɜːk ɪn / (BrE)
to add or incorporate something into a mixture or situation by mixing, blending, or introducing it carefully
add or incorporate by mixing or inserting

Examples

You should work in the eggs gradually to prevent the mixture from curdling.
She managed to work in a reference to her book during the interview.
The chef worked in some fresh herbs at the last minute to enhance the flavor.

Synset Examples

work in the butter and the dough will get the right consistency
In his speech, the presidential candidate worked in a lot of learned words

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incorporate - to include something as part of a whole
