to set off

1. VERB

/ sɛt ɔf / (AmE), / sɛt ɒf / (BrE)
to make something noticeable or attractive by providing a contrast or background that highlights it
highlight by contrast

Examples

The cream curtains set off the dark wood floors beautifully.
She wore a red scarf to set off her green eyes.
Quotation marks set off direct speech in the text.

Synset Examples

This dress accentuates your nice figure!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to accentuate - to make something more noticeable
  • Synonym: highlight - to make something stand out or more prominent
