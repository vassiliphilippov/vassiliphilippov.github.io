to tap the keg

1. VERB

/ tæp ðə kɛɡ / (AmE), / tæp ðə kɛɡ / (BrE)
to begin serving beer by opening a keg, usually by fitting it with a tap
begin serving beer from a keg

Examples

Who wants to tap the keg for the first round?
They tapped the keg as soon as the guests arrived.
It's a tradition for the host to tap the keg at midnight.

Synset Examples

He will tap the keg for the party tonight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pour a draft - to serve beer from a keg or cask
