to slam the door

1. VERB

/ slæm ðə dɔr / (AmE), / slæm ðə dɔː / (BrE)
to close a door loudly and forcefully, often because of anger or impatience
close a door forcefully

Examples

Please don't slam the door; it wakes the baby.
She stormed out of the room and slammed the door so hard the pictures shook.
Every time he's frustrated, he slams the door instead of talking about it.

Synset Examples

He got angry and slammed the door behind him.
She always slams the door when she's upset.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: shut the door - to close a door (not necessarily forcefully)
