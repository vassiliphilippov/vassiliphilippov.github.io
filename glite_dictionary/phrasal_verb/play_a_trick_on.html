to play a trick on

1. VERB

/ pleɪ ə trɪk ɑn / (AmE), / pleɪ ə trɪk ɒn / (BrE)
to deceive or mislead someone, often as a joke or prank
deceive as a prank

Examples

The children decided to play a trick on their babysitter by pretending to be asleep.
Someone played a trick on me by switching the sugar with salt in my coffee.
She laughed when she realized her friends were playing a trick on her for her birthday.

Synset Examples

We tricked the teacher into thinking that class would be cancelled next week

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trick - an action intended to deceive or outwit someone
