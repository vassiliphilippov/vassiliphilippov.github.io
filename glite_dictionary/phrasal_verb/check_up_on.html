to check up on

1. VERB

/ tʃɛk ʌp ɑn / (AmE), / tʃɛk ʌp ɒn / (BrE)
to examine or investigate something in order to determine its accuracy, quality, or condition
examine or investigate the condition of something

Examples

The manager will check up on the progress of the renovation every week.
I need to check up on the report before it goes to the client.
She always checks up on her children's homework to make sure it's done properly.

Synset Examples

check the brakes
Check out the engine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to inspect - to look at something closely to assess its condition or quality
  • Antonym: overlook - to fail to notice or check something
