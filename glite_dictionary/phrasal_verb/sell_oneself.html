to sell oneself

1. VERB

/ sɛl wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / sɛl wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to promote or present oneself in a way that makes a favorable impression, especially to attract opportunities or approval
promote oneself effectively

Examples

If you want the job, you need to sell yourself during the interview.
Successful freelancers know how to sell themselves to potential clients.
It's important to sell yourself without exaggerating your abilities.

Synset Examples

She knows how to sell herself in meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-promote - to promote oneself or one's own interests
