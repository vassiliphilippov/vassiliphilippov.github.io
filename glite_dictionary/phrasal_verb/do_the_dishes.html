to do the dishes

1. VERB

/ du ðə ˈdɪʃɪz / (AmE), / duː ðə ˈdɪʃɪz / (BrE)
to wash the plates, cups, and other items used for eating and cooking after a meal
wash plates and utensils after a meal

Examples

After dinner, I always do the dishes while my partner dries them.
She promised to do the dishes if he cooked the meal.
We took turns to do the dishes during the family holiday.

Synset Examples

I cook and my husband washes up after dinner

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to wash up - to wash the plates, cups, knives, forks, etc. after a meal (especially in British English)
