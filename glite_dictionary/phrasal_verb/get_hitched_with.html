to get hitched with

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈhɪtʃt wɪð / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈhɪtʃt wɪð / (BrE)
(informal) To get married to someone.
become married (informal)
informal

Examples

After dating for five years, they decided to get hitched with each other in a small ceremony.
Many couples choose to get hitched with their best friend and soulmate.
She never thought she'd get hitched with someone she met online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to marry - to become husband and wife

Etymology

From informal use of 'hitched' meaning 'married,' originally American slang from the idea of horses being hitched together.
