to play roulette

1. VERB

/ pleɪ ruːˈlɛt / (AmE), / pleɪ ruːˈlɛt / (BrE)
to gamble money by participating in a game of roulette
to gamble on a roulette game

Many people dream of striking it rich when they play roulette.
He lost a lot of money last night when he decided to play roulette.
