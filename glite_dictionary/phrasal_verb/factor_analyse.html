to factor analyse

1. VERB

/ ˈfæk.tɚ əˌnæl.aɪz / (AmE), / ˈfæk.tə əˌnæl.aɪz / (BrE)
to perform a statistical factor analysis on correlational data, in order to identify underlying variables or patterns
perform factor analysis (statistics)

Examples

The psychologist decided to factor analyse the survey responses to uncover the main personality traits.
Researchers chose to factor analyse the data to identify underlying factors impacting job satisfaction.
After collecting the results, the team will factor analyse the correlations to draw meaningful conclusions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: factor analysis - a statistical method for identifying underlying relationships between variables

Etymology

From 'factor' + 'analyse'; 'factor analysis' coined in early 20th-century psychology and statistics.
