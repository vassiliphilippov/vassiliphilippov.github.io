to pay up

1. VERB

/ peɪ ʌp / (AmE), / peɪ ʌp / (BrE)
to give someone the money that you owe them, especially after being asked or told to.
settle a debt or pay what is owed

Examples

You have to pay up before they will release your package.
He refused to pay up until he saw the final bill.
The landlord gave the tenants an ultimatum to pay up or move out.

Synset Examples

pay up, please!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: settle up - to pay the money owed, especially when splitting bills
