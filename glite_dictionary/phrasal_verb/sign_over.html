to sign over

1. VERB

/ saɪn ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / saɪn ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to formally transfer ownership of something to another person or party by signing a legal document
formally transfer ownership

Examples

After her father passed away, she signed over the house to her younger brother.
The company signed over its assets to the new owner as part of the merger.
He regretted having signed over his share of the business.

Synset Examples

She signed away her rights

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transfer - move ownership or legal rights from one person to another
  • Synonym: to sign away - to give up ownership or rights, usually by signing a legal document
