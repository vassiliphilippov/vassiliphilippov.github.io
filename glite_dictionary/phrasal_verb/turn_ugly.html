to turn ugly

1. VERB

/ tɝn ˈʌɡli / (AmE), / tɜːn ˈʌɡli / (BrE)
to become hostile, unpleasant, or violent, often suddenly or unexpectedly
become hostile or unpleasant

Examples

The peaceful protest turned ugly when clashes broke out between the crowd and the police.
Their conversation turned ugly as tempers began to flare and voices were raised.
When negotiations turned ugly, both sides accused each other of dishonesty.

Synset Examples

The discussion turned ugly when someone shouted Paki.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turn sour - to become unpleasant or unsuccessful
