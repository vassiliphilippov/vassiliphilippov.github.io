to apply oneself to

1. VERB

/ əˈplaɪ wʌnˈsɛlf tu / (AmE), / əˈplaɪ wʌnˈsɛlf tuː / (BrE)
to concentrate effort and attention on a task or activity
devote effort and attention to a task

Examples

If you apply yourself to your studies, you'll see great improvement.
She decided to apply herself to learning a new language this year.
Although he struggled at first, he really applied himself to the project and succeeded.

Synset Examples

Please apply yourself to your homework

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devote oneself to - to give most or all of one's time or resources to something
  • Antonym: neglect - to fail to give proper attention to something
