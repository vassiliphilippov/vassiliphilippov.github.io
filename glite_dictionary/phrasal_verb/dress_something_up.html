to dress something up

1. VERB

/ drɛs ˈsʌmθɪŋ ʌp / (AmE), / drɛs ˈsʌmθɪŋ ʌp / (BrE)
to make something more attractive or decorative, often for a special occasion
make something more attractive or decorative

Examples

We decided to dress the living room up with new curtains for the party.
She loves to dress up her emails with emojis and gifs.
They dressed the storefront up for the holiday season with lights and ribbons.

Synset Examples
