to touch down

1. VERB

/ tʌtʃ ˈdaʊn / (AmE), / tʌtʃ ˈdaʊn / (BrE)
(of an aircraft or spacecraft) to land or make contact with the ground after flight.
land (of an aircraft or spacecraft)

Examples

The plane will touch down at 3 PM after a long flight.
We watched as the helicopter touched down on the hospital roof.
Heavy rain made it difficult for the jet to touch down safely.

Synset Examples

the plane touched down at noon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: land - (of an aircraft) to come to the ground after flight
  • Antonym: to take off - (of an aircraft) to become airborne
