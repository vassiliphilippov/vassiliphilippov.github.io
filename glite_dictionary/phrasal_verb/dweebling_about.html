to dweebling about

1. VERB

/ ˈdwiːblɪŋ əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ˈdwiːblɪŋ əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish, silly, or aimless manner; to waste time idly or absent-mindedly
behaving foolishly or aimlessly
informal slang

Examples

Stop dweebling about and help me with this project.
He spent the afternoon dweebling about instead of doing his homework.
Whenever I visit them, they're always dweebling about and laughing at silly things.

Synset Examples

He was just dweebling about, not taking things seriously.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: messing about - behaving in a silly or aimless way
  • Synonym: fooling around - wasting time or behaving in a light-hearted, aimless manner

Etymology

From the noun "dweeb" (slang for a socially inept person), with the informal verb form and about as adverbial particle.
