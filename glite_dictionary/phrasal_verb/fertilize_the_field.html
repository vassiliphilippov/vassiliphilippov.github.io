to fertilize the field

1. VERB

/ ˈfɝːt̬əˌlaɪz ðə fiːld / (AmE), / ˈfɜːtəlaɪz ðə fiːld / (BrE)
to add nutrients or fertilizer to soil to improve its fertility and support crop growth
add nutrients to soil for crops

Examples

Farmers usually fertilize the field in spring to prepare for planting season.
After a soil test, they decided to fertilize the field with organic compost.
Modern machines allow us to efficiently fertilize the field on large farms.

Synset Examples

They plan to fertilize the fields before planting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manure - animal dung used for fertilizing land
  • Synonym: fertilizer - a chemical or natural substance added to soil to increase its fertility
