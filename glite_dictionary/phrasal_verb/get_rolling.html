to get rolling

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈroʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈrəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
to begin to be active; to start something or get something underway
begin to be active
informal

Examples

Let’s get rolling before it gets too late in the day.
After the introductions, the team quickly got rolling on the project.
It took us a while to pack, but now we’re finally getting rolling.

Synset Examples

Get cracking, please!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to get started - to begin doing something
  • Synonym: start - to begin an activity or process
  • Antonym: to wrap up - to finish or bring something to a conclusion
