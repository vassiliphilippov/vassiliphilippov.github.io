to pack light

1. VERB

/ pæk laɪt / (AmE), / pæk laɪt / (BrE)
to bring only the minimum amount of luggage or belongings when traveling
bring minimal luggage when traveling

Examples

On my last business trip, I decided to pack light and only brought a carry-on bag.
When you pack light, you can move through airports much more easily.
She always packs light for weekend getaways to avoid waiting at baggage claim.

Synset Examples

For a short haul flight, we packed light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: travel light - to travel with very little luggage or few possessions
