to send on

1. VERB

/ sɛnd ɑn / (AmE), / sɛnd ɒn / (BrE)
to send or forward something from an intermediate point to its intended destination
forward something in transit

Examples

Could you send on my documents to my new address?
Please send on any important mail that arrives after I move.
The company will send on the package from New York to London.

Synset Examples

forward my mail

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forward - send (mail or information) onward to another address or destination
