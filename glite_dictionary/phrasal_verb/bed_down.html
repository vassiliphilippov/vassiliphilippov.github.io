to bed down

1. VERB

/ bɛd daʊn / (AmE), / bɛd daʊn / (BrE)
to go to bed or settle down to sleep, especially in a temporary or improvised place
go to bed; settle to sleep

Examples

We found a quiet place to bed down for the night.
The hikers bedded down under the stars after a long day of walking.
During the snowstorm, the animals bed down in the barn to stay warm.

Synset Examples

We bedded down at midnight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to turn in - to go to bed
  • Antonym: to wake up - to stop sleeping and become awake
