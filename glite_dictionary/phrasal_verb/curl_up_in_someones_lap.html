to curl up in someone's lap

1. VERB

/ kɜrl ʌp ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz læp / (AmE), / kɜːl ʌp ɪn ˈsʌmwənz læp / (BrE)
to sit or lie closely with one's body curved comfortably while resting on another person's lap, often for warmth or affection
to snuggle closely while sitting on someone's lap

Examples

The puppy loves to curl up in my lap every evening when I watch TV.
After a long day, she likes to curl up in her mother's lap and listen to stories.
When it started to thunder, the child quickly curled up in his father's lap for comfort.

Synset Examples

My cat loves to curl up in my lap for a nap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snuggle - to settle or move into a warm, comfortable position closely against someone or something
  • Synonym: to nestle - to lie or be located in a sheltered or comfortable position
