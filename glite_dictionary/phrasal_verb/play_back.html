to play back

1. VERB

/ pleɪ ˈbæk / (AmE), / pleɪ ˈbak / (BrE)
to reproduce a recording by playing it on a device such as a tape recorder or player
reproduce a recording on a device

Examples

After finishing the interview, she played back the recording to check for errors.
The security guard played back the video footage from last night.
Can you play back that message so I can hear it again?

Synset Examples

The lawyers played back the conversation to show that their client was innocent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: replay - to play something again, especially a recording or a piece of music
