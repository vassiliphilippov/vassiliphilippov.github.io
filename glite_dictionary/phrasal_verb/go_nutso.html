to go nutso

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ ˈnʌtsoʊ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈnʌtsəʊ / (BrE)
to become extremely excited, crazy, or wildly enthusiastic, often suddenly or in reaction to something
become very excited or crazy
informal slang

Examples

As soon as the band took the stage, the crowd went nutso.
He went nutso when he found out he’d won the contest.
The kids went nutso at the sight of the birthday cake.

Synset Examples

He went nutso over the new video game release.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go crazy - become mentally unstable or extremely excited

Etymology

Informal blend of 'go nuts' (go crazy) and the playful extension 'nutso' from 'nuts.'
