to serve as a conduit

1. VERB

/ sɜrv æz ə ˈkɑn.du.ɪt / (AmE), / sɜːv æz ə ˈkɒn.djuː.ɪt / (BrE)
to act as an intermediary, especially as a means of passing information or communication between parties
act as a channel for communication

Examples

He was asked to serve as a conduit for information between the teams.
In her role as coordinator, she often serves as a conduit for feedback from staff to management.
The organization aims to serve as a conduit for dialogue between local communities and policymakers.

Synset Examples

He served as a conduit between the two departments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mediate - to act as a go-between in resolving differences or facilitating communication
  • Synonym: intermediary - a person who acts as a link between people to try to bring about an agreement
