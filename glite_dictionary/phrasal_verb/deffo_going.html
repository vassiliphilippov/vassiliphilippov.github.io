to deffo going

1. VERB

/ ˈdɛfoʊ ˈɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɛfəʊ ˈɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
(informal) Certainly intending to attend or participate in something; definitely going.
certainly attending; definitely going
informal slang british

Examples

I'm deffo going to the concert next week, no matter what happens.
If you're deffo going to the party, let me know so I can book tickets.
Sophie said she's deffo going to the football match, even if it rains.

Synset Examples

I'm deffo going to the concert next week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: definitely going - certainly attending or participating

Etymology

'Deffo' is a clipped slang form of 'definitely' that originated in British informal speech.
