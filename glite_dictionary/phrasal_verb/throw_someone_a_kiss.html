to throw someone a kiss

1. VERB

/ θroʊ ˈsʌmwʌn ə kɪs / (AmE), / θrəʊ ˈsʌmwən ə kɪs / (BrE)
to make a kissing gesture towards someone by kissing one's hand and blowing or gesturing it toward them, as a sign of affection.
make a gesture of sending a kiss

Examples

She smiled brightly and threw him a kiss from across the room.
As the bus pulled away, the little girl threw her mother a kiss.
Before stepping on stage, he threw the audience a kiss to show his appreciation.

Synset Examples

He waved goodbye and threw her a kiss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blow someone a kiss - to send a kiss toward someone by blowing a kiss
