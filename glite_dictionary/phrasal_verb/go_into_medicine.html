to go into medicine

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ ˈɪntu ˈmɛdəsən / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈɪntu ˈmɛdəsɪn / (BrE)
to begin a career in medicine, especially to train as a doctor or work in healthcare
pursue a career in medicine

Examples

After much consideration, she decided to go into medicine instead of engineering.
Many students are inspired to go into medicine after volunteering at hospitals.
He always knew he wanted to go into medicine and help people.

Synset Examples

I want to go into medicine after graduation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go into law - pursue a career in law
