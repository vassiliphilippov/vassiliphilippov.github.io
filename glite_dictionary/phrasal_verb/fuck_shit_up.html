to fuck shit up

1. VERB

/ fʌk ʃɪt ʌp / (AmE), / fʌk ʃɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to violently cause chaos, disorder, or destruction; to ruin or damage something completely
to violently create disorder or destroy
informal vulgar

Examples

The vandals managed to fuck shit up before the police arrived.
If you keep acting like this, you're just going to fuck shit up for everyone.
They came in yelling and started fucking shit up all over the place.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to mess up - to make a mistake or cause disorder; to spoil
  • Synonym: to destroy - to completely ruin or demolish; to put an end to
  • Antonym: repair - to restore something that is damaged or broken
