to turn heated

1. VERB

/ tɝn ˈhiːtɪd / (AmE), / tɜːn ˈhiːtɪd / (BrE)
to become intense or passionate, especially in the context of a discussion or argument
become intense or passionate

Examples

As the meeting dragged on, the discussion turned heated and people began raising their voices.
The friendly match turned heated after the referee made a controversial call.
Their conversation turned heated when they started discussing politics.

Synset Examples

The debate turned heated, with everyone calling each other politards.
The debate turned heated with Santorum's comments.
The discussion turned heated over the phrase 'magic nigger.'
The debate turned heated when he labeled her a commiecrat.
The discussion turned heated when 'Nobama' was mentioned.
The debate turned heated when the Whiggamores were mentioned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: become heated - become intense or emotionally charged
  • Antonym: to cool down - become less intense or less passionate
