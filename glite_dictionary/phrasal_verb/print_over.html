to print over

1. VERB

/ prɪnt ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / prɪnt ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to print additional text or colors onto an already printed or imprinted surface
add print over an existing print

Examples

The designer decided to print over the original invitation with the correct date.
Sometimes printers print over existing labels to update information without replacing the whole material.
The error was fixed by printing over the old price with the new one.
