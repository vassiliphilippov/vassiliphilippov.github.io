to add character

1. VERB

/ æd ˈkɛrɪktɚ / (AmE), / æd ˈkarɪktə / (BrE)
to make something more interesting or distinctive in appearance, style, or feeling
make more interesting or distinctive

Examples

The exposed brick walls add character to the apartment.
A few handmade decorations can really add character to a modern living room.
Restoring the original wood floors will add character to the old house.

Synset Examples

The antique vase had a beautiful patina that added character.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to enhance - to improve the quality or value of something
  • Antonym: detract from - to reduce the value or quality of something
