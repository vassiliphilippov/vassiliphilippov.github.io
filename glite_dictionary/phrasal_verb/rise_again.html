to rise again

1. VERB

/ raɪz əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / raɪz əˈɡɛn / (BrE)
to return to life after being dead; to come back to existence or activity after a period of absence or defeat
to come back to life or activity

Examples

According to legend, the hero will rise again when the kingdom is in danger.
Every spring, the ancient myth said the god would rise again from the underworld.
The defeated team vowed to rise again stronger next season.

Synset Examples

They joked about the movie featuring Zombie Jesus rising again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to resurrect - to bring back to life
  • Synonym: to revive - to restore to life or consciousness
