to just come out

1. VERB

/ dʒʌst kʌm aʊt / (AmE), / dʒʌst kʌm aʊt / (BrE)
to be released, published, or made available very recently
be released or published recently

Examples

The novel just came out last week and is already a bestseller.
A new smartphone just came out, and everyone is talking about it.
Have you heard the album that just came out this month?

Synset Examples

She recommended a movie that just came out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: release - to make something available to the public; to issue
