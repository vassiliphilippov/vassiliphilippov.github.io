to come across as

1. VERB

/ kʌm əˈkrɑs æz / (AmE), / kʌm əˈkrɒs æz / (BrE)
to appear or be perceived in a particular way by others
appear or seem to others

Examples

He tried to come across as friendly, but people found him arrogant.
During the meeting, she came across as very knowledgeable and prepared.
Sometimes your jokes come across as insensitive, even if you don't mean them that way.

Synset Examples

He came across as very confident during the interview.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to seem - to give the impression of being a certain way
  • Synonym: strike (someone) as - to give a particular impression to someone
