to take a bath

1. VERB

/ teɪk ə bæθ / (AmE), / teɪk ə bɑːθ / (BrE)
to immerse oneself in water in order to wash or relax
bathe for cleanliness or relaxation

Examples

After working outside all day, I couldn't wait to take a bath.
She likes to take a bath every night before going to sleep.
If you feel stressed, you should take a bath to unwind.

Synset Examples
