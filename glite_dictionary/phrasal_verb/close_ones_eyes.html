to close one's eyes

1. VERB

/ kloʊz wʌnz aɪz / (AmE), / kləʊz wʌnz aɪz / (BrE)
to shut one's eyes, typically for relaxation, rest, or to avoid seeing something
shut one's eyes, usually to relax or rest

Examples

He likes to close his eyes and listen to the rain falling outside.
If you close your eyes tightly, maybe the headache will pass.
She told him to close his eyes and make a wish before blowing out the candles.

Synset Examples

She closed her eyes to enjoy the music.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: open one's eyes - to open one's eyelids; become alert or aware
