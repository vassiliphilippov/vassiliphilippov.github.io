to feed the meter

1. VERB

/ fid ðə ˈmitɚ / (AmE), / fiːd ðə ˈmiːtə / (BrE)
to put money or a payment into a parking meter to pay for parking time
put money in a parking meter

Examples

You should feed the meter before leaving your car on the street.
She quickly ran outside to feed the meter when she saw the parking officer nearby.
Don't forget to feed the meter if you plan on staying longer than an hour.

Synset Examples

Make sure to feed the meter or we’ll be towed.
I had to feed the meter to avoid a parking ticket.
Don't forget to feed the meter before the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to feed the meter - insert coins into a payphone or similar device
