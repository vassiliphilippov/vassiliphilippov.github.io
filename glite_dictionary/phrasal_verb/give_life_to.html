to give life to

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv laɪf tu / (AmE), / ɡɪv laɪf tʊ / (BrE)
to make something more lively, interesting, or energetic; to cause something to appear vibrant or full of life
make lively or vibrant

Examples

The new manager's enthusiasm helped give life to the struggling company.
Fresh paint and lighting can give life to an otherwise dreary room.
Her humor and energy really give life to our meetings.

Synset Examples

They aimed to give life to the old building with renovations.
The artist wanted to give life to her paintings.
His words can give life to the dullest stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to revitalize - to give new energy or life to something
  • Synonym: to invigorate - to make someone or something feel more lively or energetic
  • Antonym: drain - to remove energy or vitality from
