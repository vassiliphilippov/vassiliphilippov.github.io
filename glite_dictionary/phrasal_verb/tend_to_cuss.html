to tend to cuss

1. VERB

/ tɛnd tə kʌs / (AmE), / tɛnd tə kʌs / (BrE)
to frequently use profanity or swear words, especially as a habit
to habitually use swear words

Examples

My uncle tends to cuss when he watches sports on TV.
She tends to cuss under her breath when things go wrong at work.
People who are stressed often tend to cuss more than usual.

Synset Examples

He tends to cuss when he's frustrated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to swear - to use rude or offensive language
  • Synonym: curse - to utter offensive words; to use profanity
