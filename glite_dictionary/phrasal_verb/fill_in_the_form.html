to fill in the form

1. VERB

/ fɪl ɪn ðə fɔrm / (AmE), / fɪl ɪn ðə fɔːm / (BrE)
to complete a form by entering the necessary information in the appropriate spaces
complete a form with information

Examples

You must fill in the form with your personal details before registration.
He quickly filled in the form and handed it back to the receptionist.
If you have any trouble filling in the form, please ask for assistance.

Synset Examples

Please fill in the form before submitting your application.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fill out the form - to complete a form with required information (alternate expression, especially in American English)
