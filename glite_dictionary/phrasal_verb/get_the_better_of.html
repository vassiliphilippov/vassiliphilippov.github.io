to get the better of

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ðə ˈbɛtər əv / (AmE), / ɡɛt ðə ˈbɛtə rɒv / (BrE)
to defeat or prevail over someone or something; to gain an advantage or control over
defeat; prevail over

Examples

Her curiosity got the better of her, so she opened the mysterious box.
Despite his efforts to stay calm, anger got the better of him during the meeting.
The team's nerves got the better of them in the final minutes of the match.

Synset Examples

You must overcome all difficulties
defeat your enemies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to overcome - to succeed in dealing with or controlling a problem or feeling
  • Synonym: defeat - to win a victory over someone in a contest or battle
