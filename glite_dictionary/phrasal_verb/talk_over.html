to talk over

1. VERB

/ tɑk ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / tɔːk ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to discuss something carefully and thoroughly with someone before making a decision
discuss in detail with others

Examples

Let's talk over the new project before we make a decision.
They talked over their vacation plans at dinner last night.
You should talk over your options with a financial advisor.

Synset Examples

We discussed our household budget

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to discuss - to talk about something with others, especially in detail
