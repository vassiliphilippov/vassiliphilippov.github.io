to miss a deadline

1. VERB

/ mɪs ə ˈdɛdˌlaɪn / (AmE), / mɪs ə ˈdɛdˌlaɪn / (BrE)
to fail to submit or complete something by the agreed or required time
fail to meet a due date

Examples

If you miss a deadline, the application may be automatically rejected.
She panicked when she realized she was about to miss a deadline for her report.
Our team cannot afford to miss a deadline during this critical project phase.

Synset Examples

With this technique, I never miss a deadline.
He’s such a dorkwad for missing the deadline again.
She realized her stupidy after missing the deadline.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: meet a deadline - to finish or submit something by the required time
