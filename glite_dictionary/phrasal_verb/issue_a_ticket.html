to issue a ticket

1. VERB

/ ˈɪʃu ə ˈtɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪʃuː ə ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
to give an official notice, especially a penalty or fine, typically for breaking a law or regulation
give a penalty or fine notice

Examples

The police officer decided to issue a ticket to the speeding driver.
Parking enforcement officers regularly issue tickets to cars parked in no-parking zones.
If you don’t pay the meter, the city may issue a ticket for your vehicle.

Synset Examples

The officer issued a ticket for speeding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fine - a sum of money exacted as a penalty for an offense
