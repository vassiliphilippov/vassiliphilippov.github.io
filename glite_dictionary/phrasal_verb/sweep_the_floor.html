to sweep the floor

1. VERB

/ swip ðə flɔr / (AmE), / swiːp ðə flɔː / (BrE)
to clean the floor by moving dirt or dust away with a broom
to clean a floor with a broom

Examples

Please sweep the floor after you finish cooking to keep the kitchen tidy.
He swept the floor quickly before the visitors arrived.
She was sweeping the floor when the phone rang.
