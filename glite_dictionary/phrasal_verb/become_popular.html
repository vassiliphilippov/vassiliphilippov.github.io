to become popular

1. VERB

/ bɪˈkʌm ˈpɑːpjələr / (AmE), / bɪˈkʌm ˈpɒpjʊlə / (BrE)
to gain widespread acceptance or admiration; to attract the interest of many people
gain widespread acceptance

Examples

The singer's new single became popular almost overnight, topping charts across the country.
When social media first became popular, few realized how much it would change communication.
Board games are becoming popular again among people of all ages.

Synset Examples

The QT app quickly became popular among gamers.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fall out of favor - to lose popularity or approval
