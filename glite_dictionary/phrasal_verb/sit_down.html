to sit down

1. VERB

/ sɪt daʊn / (AmE), / sɪt daʊn / (BrE)
to move from a standing position to a seated position
move from standing to sitting

Examples

Please sit down and make yourself comfortable while you wait.
She sat down on the grass to enjoy the sunshine.
They were sitting down at the table when dinner was served.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to stand up - to move from a sitting or lying position to standing
