to manage stress

1. VERB

/ ˈmænɪdʒ strɛs / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒ stres / (BrE)
to deal with stress in a way that reduces its negative effects or helps maintain well-being
deal with stress effectively

Examples

Learning how to manage stress can improve both your mental and physical health.
She uses meditation and exercise to manage stress during exam periods.
Companies often offer workshops to help employees manage stress at work.

Synset Examples

Managing stress is crucial for preventing stomach ulcers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cope with stress - to handle or endure stress
  • Synonym: reduce stress - to lessen stress or its effects
