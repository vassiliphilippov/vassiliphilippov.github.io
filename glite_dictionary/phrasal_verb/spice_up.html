to spice up

1. VERB

/ spaɪs ʌp / (AmE), / spaɪs ʌp / (BrE)
to add herbs or spices to food in order to enhance its flavor
add herbs or spices to food

Examples

She decided to spice up the soup with some fresh basil and oregano.
Try adding chili flakes to spice up a bland pasta dish.
He always likes to spice up his meals with extra garlic and pepper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: season - to add salt, herbs, or spices to food to enhance its flavor
