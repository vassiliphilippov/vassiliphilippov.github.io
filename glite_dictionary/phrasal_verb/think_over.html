to think over

1. VERB

/ ˈθɪŋk ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈθɪŋk ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to consider something carefully and thoroughly, often before making a decision
consider carefully; reflect on

Examples

I need some time to think over your proposal before I decide.
She thought over her friend's advice very carefully.
The night before the interview, he kept thinking over what he was going to say.

Synset Examples

I mulled over the events of the afternoon
philosophers have speculated on the question of God for thousands of years
The scientist must stop to observe and start to excogitate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ponder - to think about something carefully, especially before making a decision
  • Synonym: to consider - to think about something carefully, especially in order to make a decision
  • Antonym: to ignore - to pay no attention to something
