to act superior

1. VERB

/ ækt səˈpɪriɚ / (AmE), / ækt səˈpɪəriə / (BrE)
to behave as if one is better than or has authority over others; to act in a condescending or domineering manner
to behave in a condescending or domineering way

Examples

He tends to act superior when discussing his job with his friends.
Nobody likes it when you act superior to everyone else in the group.
She tried not to act superior despite her impressive achievements.

Synset Examples

He is lording it over the students

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to lord it over - to behave in a domineering or superior manner toward others
