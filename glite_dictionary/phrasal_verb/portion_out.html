to portion out

1. VERB

/ ˈpɔrʃən aʊt / (AmE), / ˈpɔːʃən aʊt / (BrE)
to divide something into parts and distribute to different people
divide and distribute in shares

Examples

She carefully portioned out the cake so everyone would get an equal slice.
The teacher portioned out the supplies among the students at the start of class.
Volunteers were portioning out food to families in need.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to distribute - to give out shares or items to a number of people
  • Synonym: to allocate - to assign portions or shares for a specific purpose
