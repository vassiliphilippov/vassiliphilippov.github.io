to hop up on

1. VERB

/ hɑp ʌp ɑn / (AmE), / hɒp ʌp ɒn / (BrE)
to jump onto a surface, such as a table, counter, or bed, in a quick movement
to jump onto a surface quickly

Examples

The child managed to hop up on the kitchen stool without any help.
Can you hop up on the exam table for me, please?
She watched the cat hop up on the bookshelf to reach her favorite spot.

Synset Examples

He asked her to hop up on the counter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to jump on - to leap onto a surface
