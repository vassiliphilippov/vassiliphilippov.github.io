to brighten up the room

1. VERB

/ ˈbraɪtən ʌp ðə ruːm / (AmE), / ˈbraɪtən ʌp ðə ruːm / (BrE)
to make a place feel more lively, cheerful, or pleasant
make a place more cheerful

Examples

Her laughter always seems to brighten up the room whenever she visits.
The new curtains really brighten up the room and make it feel more welcoming.
He tried to brighten up the room with fresh flowers after the gloomy weather.

Synset Examples

Her daughter always brightens up the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cheer up - to make or become happier or more cheerful
