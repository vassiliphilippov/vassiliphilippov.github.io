to look upon

1. VERB

/ lʊk əˈpʌn / (AmE), / lʊk əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to consider or regard someone or something in a particular way
consider or regard someone/something

Examples

Many people look upon honesty as the most important virtue.
She tends to look upon her colleagues as friends rather than just coworkers.
Historians often look upon these events as pivotal moments in history.

Synset Examples
