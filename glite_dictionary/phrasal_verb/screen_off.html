to screen off

1. VERB

/ skrin ɔf / (AmE), / skriːn ɒf / (BrE)
to separate or partition part of a space using a screen or divider
separate an area with a screen or divider

Examples

We decided to screen off a corner of the studio to create some privacy.
A curtain was used to screen off the storage area from the rest of the hall.
They screened off the construction zone to keep dust from spreading.

Synset Examples

screen off this part of the room

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partition - to divide into parts or sections, often with a physical barrier
