to hit the gas

1. VERB

/ hɪt ðə ɡæs / (AmE), / hɪt ðə ɡas / (BrE)
to accelerate or increase speed, especially by pressing a vehicle’s accelerator pedal; to move or act more quickly
to accelerate or speed up
informal

Examples

She hit the gas as soon as the light turned green.
If you want to finish before noon, you’ll have to hit the gas.
The car ahead suddenly hit the gas and sped away.

Synset Examples

As soon as I saw the deadline, I hit the gas.
He hit the gas to make it on time.
She decided to hit the gas and finish early.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to speed up - to increase speed or accelerate
  • Antonym: to slow down - to reduce speed or move more slowly
