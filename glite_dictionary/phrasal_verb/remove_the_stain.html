to remove the stain

1. VERB

/ rɪˈmuːv ðə steɪn / (AmE), / rɪˈmuːv ðə steɪn / (BrE)
to get rid of a mark or blemish from a surface, material, or reputation
eliminate a mark or blemish

Examples

She used a special detergent to remove the stain from her shirt.
It's important to remove the stain quickly before it sets in.
Professional cleaners can remove the stain without damaging the fabric.

Synset Examples

Can you help me remove this stain?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clean - to make something free of dirt, marks, or stains
