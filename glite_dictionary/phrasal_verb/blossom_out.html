to blossom out

1. VERB

/ ˈblɑsəm aʊt / (AmE), / ˈblɒsəm aʊt / (BrE)
to develop or progress towards maturity, success, or a more promising state
develop or reach a promising stage

Examples

After taking extra classes, she began to blossom out into a confident public speaker.
His artistic talent blossomed out during his final year at university.
The small business blossomed out into a thriving enterprise within just a few years.

Synset Examples

Youth blossomed into maturity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mature - to become fully grown or developed
  • Antonym: to wither - to fade or decline, especially in vitality or vigor
