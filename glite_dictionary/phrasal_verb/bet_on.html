to bet on

1. VERB

/ bɛt ɑn / (AmE), / bɛt ɒn / (BrE)
to risk money or something valuable predicting the outcome of an event or the success of someone or something
place a wager predicting an outcome

Examples

Are you going to bet on the outcome of the game tonight?
She likes to bet on long shots at the racetrack.
Many investors bet on technology stocks during the boom.

Synset Examples

Which horse are you backing?
I'm betting on the new horse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wager on - to risk money by predicting an outcome
  • Synonym: back - to support or bet on (a person or thing) to win or succeed
