to be a tithead

1. VERB

/ bi ə ˈtɪthɛd / (AmE), / biː ə ˈtɪthɛd / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or stupid manner; to act like an idiot
act foolishly or stupidly
slang vulgar

Examples

Stop being a tithead and listen to what I'm saying.
He was a tithead for jumping into the pool with his phone in his pocket.
If you keep being a tithead, you'll get yourself into trouble.

Synset Examples

Don't be a tithead; think before you act.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a person of low intelligence or who behaves foolishly
  • Synonym: fool - someone who acts unwisely or imprudently
