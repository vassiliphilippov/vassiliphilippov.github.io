to matter to

1. VERB

/ ˈmætər tu / (AmE), / ˈmætə tuː / (BrE)
to be important or have significance for (someone or something)
to be important to someone

Examples

It doesn't matter to me where we go for dinner.
Her opinion matters to him more than anyone else's.
What really mattered to the team was working together toward a common goal.

Synset Examples

This matters to me!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be important to - to have significance or value for someone or something
