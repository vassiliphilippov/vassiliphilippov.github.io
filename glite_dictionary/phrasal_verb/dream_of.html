to dream of

1. VERB

/ dɹim əv / (AmE), / dɹiːm ɒv / (BrE)
to wish for or imagine something that you would like to happen or achieve, especially something unlikely or ideal
to wish or imagine something you want

Examples

Ever since she was a child, she would dream of singing on stage in front of thousands.
Many people dream of traveling the world, but few actually do it.
He still dreams of owning a home by the ocean someday.
