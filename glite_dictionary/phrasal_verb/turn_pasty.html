to turn pasty

1. VERB

/ tɝn ˈpeɪsti / (AmE), / tɜːn ˈpeɪsti / (BrE)
(of a person's face or skin) to become pale and unhealthy-looking, often because of shock, illness, or fear.
become pale and unhealthy-looking

Examples

She turned pasty when she saw the blood on her hand.
After hours without food, he began to turn pasty and weak.
The little boy turned pasty with fear as the dog barked loudly.

Synset Examples

His face turned pasty after the shocking news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to turn pale - to become very pale, especially suddenly
