to blaze up

1. VERB

/ bleɪz ʌp / (AmE), / bleɪz ʌp / (BrE)
to begin burning brightly and intensely
begin to burn brightly

Examples

The campfire suddenly blazed up as we added more wood.
Dry grass can blaze up quickly in the summer heat.
When the match was struck, the pile of papers blazed up instantly.

Synset Examples

Every star seemed to flare with new intensity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to flare up - to suddenly burn brightly or intensely
