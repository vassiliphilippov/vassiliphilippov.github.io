to call for help

1. VERB

/ kɑl fər hɛlp / (AmE), / kɔːl fə hɛlp / (BrE)
to ask loudly or urgently for assistance in a difficult or dangerous situation
request urgent assistance

Examples

The hikers had to call for help when they got lost in the mountains.
She reached for her phone to call for help after the accident.
If there is a fire, remember to call for help immediately.

Synset Examples

We got stuck in a bog and had to call for help.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cry for help - to shout or call loudly for assistance
  • Synonym: to ask for help - to request assistance from someone
