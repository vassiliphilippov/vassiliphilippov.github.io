to clean one's system

1. VERB

/ klin wʌnz ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / kliːn wʌnz ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
to remove unwanted, harmful, or toxic substances from one's body or from another system
remove toxins or unwanted elements

Examples

After drinking lots of water and eating healthy, she decided to clean her system over the weekend.
Some people believe herbal teas can help clean your system of toxins.
The IT technician advised me to clean my system to remove all the unwanted software.

Synset Examples

She had to clean her system of scumware yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to detoxify - to remove toxic substances, especially from the body
  • Synonym: purge - to clear of impurities or unwanted elements
