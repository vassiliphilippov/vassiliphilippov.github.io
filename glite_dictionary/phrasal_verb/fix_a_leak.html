to fix a leak

1. VERB

/ fɪks ə lik / (AmE), / fɪks ə liːk / (BrE)
to repair a crack or hole that allows liquid or gas to escape, especially from a pipe or container
repair a leak (in a pipe, etc.)

Examples

The plumber arrived quickly to fix a leak in the kitchen ceiling.
She had to fix a leak in the garden hose before watering the plants.
They hired a professional to fix a leak that was flooding the basement.

Synset Examples

He fixed the leak in the pipe under the sink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repair - to restore something that is broken to good condition
  • Synonym: seal - to close an opening so that liquid or gas cannot escape
