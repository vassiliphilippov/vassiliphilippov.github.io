to lick out the bowl

1. VERB

/ lɪk aʊt ðə boʊl / (AmE), / lɪk aʊt ðə bəʊl / (BrE)
to use one's tongue to remove and eat the remaining contents from a bowl, often after food preparation
use tongue to clean out a bowl

Examples

As soon as I finished baking, the children rushed to lick out the bowl.
She loves to lick out the bowl after making chocolate cake batter.
He waited until everyone had finished before he licked out the bowl of icing.

Synset Examples

The kids will lick out the bowl after baking.
