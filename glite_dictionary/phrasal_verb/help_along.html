to help along

1. VERB

/ hɛlp əˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / hɛlp əˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
to assist or facilitate the progress of someone or something
assist progress of someone or something

Examples

Her encouragement really helped along my recovery after surgery.
The manager's advice helped along the negotiations between the two companies.
Volunteers helped along the event by setting up chairs and decorations.

Synset Examples

His explanation helped along my understanding.
Can you help along the process with your feedback?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assist - to give support or aid to
  • Antonym: to impede - to slow or prevent the progress of something
