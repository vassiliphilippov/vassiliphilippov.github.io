to recognize for

1. VERB

/ ˈrɛkəɡˌnaɪz fər / (AmE), / ˈrɛkəɡˌnaɪz fə / (BrE)
to acknowledge or reward someone for a particular quality, action, or achievement
acknowledge or award for achievement

Examples

She was recognized for her outstanding service to the community.
The company was recognized for its efforts in promoting sustainability.
He hopes to be recognized for his contributions to the project.

Synset Examples

They were recognized for their teamwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honor - to show respect or recognition for someone or something
  • Antonym: overlook - to fail to notice or acknowledge someone or something
