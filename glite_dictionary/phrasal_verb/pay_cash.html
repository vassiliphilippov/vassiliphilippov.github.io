to pay cash

1. VERB

/ peɪ kæʃ / (AmE), / peɪ kæʃ / (BrE)
to pay for something using physical money, rather than by check or credit card
pay using physical money

Examples

We decided to pay cash for the new sofa to avoid interest charges.
If you pay cash, you might get a small discount at this store.
She always prefers to pay cash rather than use her card.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pay by credit card - to pay for something with a credit card
  • Antonym: pay by check - to pay for something using a check
