to pay the rent

1. VERB

/ peɪ ðə rɛnt / (AmE), / peɪ ðə rɛnt / (BrE)
to make a regular payment to a landlord for the use of property, usually a home or apartment
make regular rental payment

Examples

She always makes sure to pay the rent on the first of every month.
After losing his job, he struggled to pay the rent on time.
They worked extra hours just to pay the rent and keep their apartment.

Synset Examples

It's challenging to pay the rent on a single income.
They worked hard to pay the rent each month.
He was relieved to finally pay the rent ahead of time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pay - to give money for goods or services
