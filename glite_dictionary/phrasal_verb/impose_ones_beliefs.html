to impose one's beliefs

1. VERB

/ ɪmˈpoʊz wʌnz bɪˈlifz / (AmE), / ɪmˈpəʊz wʌnz bɪˈliːfs / (BrE)
to force one's own opinions or convictions on other people, especially in a way that is unwelcome or overbearing
force own opinions on others

Examples

She tries not to impose her beliefs on her children, allowing them to make their own choices.
It is unfair to impose your beliefs in a workplace discussion where others may not agree.
They felt frustrated when community leaders began to impose their beliefs through strict new regulations.

Synset Examples

Don't impose your beliefs on others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impose one's views - to force one's views or opinions on others
