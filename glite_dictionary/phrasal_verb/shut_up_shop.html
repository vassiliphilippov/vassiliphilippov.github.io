to shut up shop

1. VERB

/ ʃʌt ʌp ʃɑp / (AmE), / ʃʌt ʌp ʃɒp / (BrE)
to close a business for the day or stop trading, temporarily or permanently
close a business for the day

Examples

The café always shuts up shop at six o'clock on weekdays.
Because of the national holiday, they'll shut up shop earlier than usual today.
After working late, she finally shut up shop and went home.

Synset Examples

After the last customer, they shut up shop for the day.
They decided to shut up shop early due to the rain.
It's time to shut up shop and head home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to close up shop - to cease business activities, usually at the end of the day
