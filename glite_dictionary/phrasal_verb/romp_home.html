to romp home

1. VERB

/ rɑmp hoʊm / (AmE), / rɒmp həʊm / (BrE)
to return home in a playful or energetic way, often with lively movement or enthusiasm
return home playfully or energetically

Examples

After the rain stopped, the children romped home through the puddles, laughing and shouting.
The puppy loves to romp home after a walk in the park.
She romped home from school, eager to tell her family about her day.

Synset Examples
