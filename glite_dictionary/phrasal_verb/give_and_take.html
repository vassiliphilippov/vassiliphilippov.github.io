to give and take

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ənd teɪk / (AmE), / ɡɪv ənd teɪk / (BrE)
to make mutual concessions; to compromise by accepting some demands of others while giving up some of one's own
to make mutual concessions; to compromise

Examples

Successful negotiations always require both sides to give and take.
A healthy relationship relies on the ability to give and take.
In our team, everyone must be willing to give and take to achieve our goals.

Synset Examples

In life you have to give and take

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compromise - to settle differences by making concessions
