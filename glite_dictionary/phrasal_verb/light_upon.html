to light upon

1. VERB

/ laɪt əˈpɑn / (AmE), / laɪt əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to discover or find something by chance; to come across unexpectedly.
discover something by chance

Examples

While cleaning the attic, she lighted upon a box of old family letters.
The scientists lighted upon a rare species during their expedition.
He lit upon the solution quite by accident while re-reading the notes.

Synset Examples

the archeologists chanced upon an old tomb
she struck a goldmine
The hikers finally struck the main path to the lake

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to come across - to find something or meet someone by chance
  • Synonym: stumble upon - to discover something unexpectedly
