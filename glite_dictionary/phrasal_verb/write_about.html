to write about

1. VERB

/ ˈraɪt əˌbaʊt / (AmE), / ˈraɪt əˌbaʊt / (BrE)
to compose text or provide information concerning a particular topic or subject
compose text on a topic

Examples

She plans to write about her travels in a new book.
Many journalists write about politics and current events.
He has written about the challenges facing modern education.

Synset Examples

Snow wrote about China

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to describe - to give an account of something in words
