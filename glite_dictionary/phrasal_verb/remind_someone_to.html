to remind someone to

1. VERB

/ rɪˈmaɪnd ˈsʌmwʌn tu / (AmE), / rɪˈmaɪnd ˈsʌmwən tuː / (BrE)
to tell someone so that they do not forget to do something
tell someone not to forget an action

Examples

Could you remind someone to lock the door before leaving?
She always reminds someone to take their medicine on time.
Please remind someone to bring their homework tomorrow.

Synset Examples

Please remind me to call her tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to remind - to cause someone to remember something
