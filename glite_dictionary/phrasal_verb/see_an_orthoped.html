to see an orthoped

1. VERB

/ siː ən ˈɔrθəˌpɛd / (AmE), / siː ən ˈɔːθəpɛd / (BrE)
to consult or have an appointment with a specialist in bone and joint disorders
consult a bone and joint doctor

Examples

You should see an orthoped if your shoulder pain doesn’t improve.
After breaking his ankle, Toby was advised to see an orthoped for further evaluation.
My doctor recommended that I see an orthoped about my recurring back problems.

Synset Examples

After the accident, I had to see an orthoped for my knee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to see a doctor - to consult a medical doctor for treatment or advice
  • Synonym: see an orthopedist - to consult a specialist in orthopedics (bones and joints)
