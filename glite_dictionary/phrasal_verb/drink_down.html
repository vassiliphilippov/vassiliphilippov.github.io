to drink down

1. VERB

/ drɪŋk daʊn / (AmE), / drɪŋk daʊn / (BrE)
to drink something completely, especially quickly or in one go
drink something completely

Examples

She managed to drink down the bitter medicine in one gulp.
The guests were encouraged to drink down their champagne before the toast.
He tried to drink down his embarrassment by finishing his beer quickly.

Synset Examples

He downed three martinis before dinner
She killed a bottle of brandy that night
They popped a few beers after work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: down - to swallow something quickly or in one motion
