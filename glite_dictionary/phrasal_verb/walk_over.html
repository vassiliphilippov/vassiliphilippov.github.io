to walk over

1. VERB

/ wɑk ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / wɔːk ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to defeat someone or something very easily, often with little effort
defeat easily

Examples

The champion walked over his opponent in straight sets.
They expected a tough game, but ended up walking over the other team.
She walked over her rivals in the spelling competition.

Synset Examples

The local team walked over their old rivals for the championship

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to trounce - to defeat thoroughly
  • Synonym: thrash - to defeat heavily in a contest
  • Antonym: to lose - to be defeated
