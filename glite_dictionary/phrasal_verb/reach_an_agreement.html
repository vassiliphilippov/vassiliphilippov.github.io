to reach an agreement

1. VERB

/ ɹitʃ ən əˈɡɹimənt / (AmE), / ɹiːtʃ ən əˈɡriːm(ə)nt / (BrE)
to come to a mutual decision or settle on the terms of something after discussion or negotiation
come to a mutual decision

Examples

After several rounds of negotiation, both sides were able to reach an agreement.
It took weeks of discussion before the two companies could reach an agreement on the merger.
The committee is hopeful that they can reach an agreement before the deadline.

Synset Examples

After a long hour, they finally reached an agreement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to agree - to have the same opinion or come to a settlement
  • Antonym: to disagree - to have a different opinion or fail to come to a settlement
