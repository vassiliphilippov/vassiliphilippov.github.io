to snag a deal

1. VERB

/ snæɡ ə diːl / (AmE), / snæɡ ə diːl / (BrE)
to successfully obtain or secure something advantageous, especially a bargain or discount
obtain something advantageous
informal

Examples

She always knows how to snag a deal when she goes shopping online.
If you check the website early, you might snag a deal on flights.
They snagged a deal on their new car during the holiday sales.

Synset Examples

I managed to snag a great deal on shoes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get a deal - to obtain a bargain or advantageous offer
  • Antonym: miss a deal - to fail to obtain an advantageous offer
