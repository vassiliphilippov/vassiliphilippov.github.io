to head out

1. VERB

/ hɛd aʊt / (AmE), / hɛd aʊt / (BrE)
to leave a place, especially to begin a journey or go somewhere else
to leave or depart

Examples

We should head out soon if we want to catch the last train.
She grabbed her keys and headed out the door.
After saying goodbye, he began heading out towards his car.

Synset Examples

It's almost seven o'clock; we should head out.
I'm heading out now, so I'll be seeing ya later!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leave - to go away from a place or person
  • Synonym: to depart - to leave, especially to start a journey
