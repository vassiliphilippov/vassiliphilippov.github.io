to whomp up

1. VERB

/ wɑmp ʌp / (AmE), / wɒmp ʌp / (BrE)
To prepare or cook (food) quickly or hastily, often in an improvised way.
prepare or cook hastily
informal

Examples

She managed to whomp up dinner for six using only leftovers in the fridge.
The chef can whomp up something delicious in just a few minutes.
With little time before the guests arrived, he hurried to whomp up some snacks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to rustle up - prepare or provide quickly, especially food
