to twist around

1. VERB

/ twɪst əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / twɪst əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to change or distort the meaning of something, especially deliberately in order to mislead or deceive
distort or misrepresent meaning

Examples

He tried to twist around my statement to make me look guilty.
Politicians sometimes twist around the facts to suit their arguments.
Please don't twist around what I said; that's not what I meant.

Synset Examples

Don't twist my words

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to distort - to change the meaning of something so that it is no longer true or accurate
  • Synonym: to misrepresent - to give a false or misleading account of
  • Antonym: to clarify - to make something clear or easier to understand
