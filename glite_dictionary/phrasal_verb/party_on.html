to party on

1. VERB

/ ˈpɑrti ɑn / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti ɒn / (BrE)
to continue celebrating or having a good time, especially despite difficulties or setbacks
continue having fun despite difficulties
informal

Examples

Even though the lights went out, they decided to party on and enjoy the evening.
No matter what happens, she always finds a way to party on.
Despite the bad weather, we chose to party on under the tent.

Synset Examples

Let's just party on despite the challenges ahead.
He smiled and said, 'Let's party on!' after the news.
Even after the rain, they decided to party on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to celebrate - to observe or honor a special day or event with festivities
