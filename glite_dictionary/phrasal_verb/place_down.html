to place down

1. VERB

/ pleɪs daʊn / (AmE), / pleɪs daʊn / (BrE)
to put something or someone into a settled or resting position
put into a settled position

Examples

Please place down your coat on the chair when you arrive.
He carefully placed down the delicate vase on the table.
The child placed down her toys before leaving the room.

Synset Examples

set down your bags here

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to set down - to place something in a particular position
