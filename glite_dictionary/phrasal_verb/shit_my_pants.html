to shit my pants

1. VERB

/ ʃɪt maɪ pænts / (AmE), / ʃɪt maɪ pænts / (BrE)
(vulgar, informal) To be extremely shocked, surprised, or frightened; to react very strongly to something startling.
be extremely surprised or frightened (vulgar, informal)
vulgar informal

Examples

When the fireworks exploded unexpectedly, I nearly shit my pants.
He told such a scary story that I almost shit my pants.
If I saw a snake in my bed, I would definitely shit my pants.

Synset Examples

I almost shit my pants when I saw the price.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freak out - to suddenly become very anxious, upset, or scared
  • Synonym: be terrified - to be extremely afraid or scared
