to trumpet one's achievements

1. VERB

/ ˈtrʌmpɪt wʌnz əˈtʃivmənts / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪt wʌnz əˈtʃiːvmənts / (BrE)
to boast publicly about one's successes or accomplishments
boast about one's successes

Examples

At the awards dinner, he could not resist trumpeting his achievements to everyone present.
Some people prefer to stay humble instead of trumpeting their achievements in public.
She was accused of trumpeting her achievements at every opportunity she got.

Synset Examples

He decided to trumpet his achievements at the meeting.
She loves to trumpet her children's successes to everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boast - to talk with excessive pride about one's achievements
  • Synonym: brag - to talk boastfully about achievements or possessions
  • Antonym: to downplay - to make something seem less important

Etymology

From the verb 'trumpet', meaning to proclaim loudly, derived from the brass instrument 'trumpet'.
