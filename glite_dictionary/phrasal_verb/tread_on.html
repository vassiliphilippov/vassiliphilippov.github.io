to tread on

1. VERB

/ trɛd ɑn / (AmE), / trɛd ɒn / (BrE)
to place or press the foot on something, especially by accident
set or press the foot on

Examples

Be careful not to tread on the flowers in the garden.
He trod on my foot during the crowded train ride.
Watch your step so you don't tread on any sharp objects.

Synset Examples

He stepped on the hem of her long gown

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to step on - to set one's foot on something
