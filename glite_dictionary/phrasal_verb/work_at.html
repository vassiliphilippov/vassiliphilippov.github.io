to work at

1. VERB

/ wɝːk ət / (AmE), / wɜːk ət / (BrE)
to put effort into achieving or completing something, especially over a period of time
make effort toward achieving something

Examples

She continued to work at her painting until it was perfect.
If you work at your math skills every day, you'll improve quickly.
He is working at learning French before his trip to Paris.

Synset Examples

the child worked at the multiplication table until she had it down cold

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to strive - to make great effort to achieve or obtain something
