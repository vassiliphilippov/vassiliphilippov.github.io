to let off fireworks

1. VERB

/ lɛt ɔf ˈfaɪrˌwɜrks / (AmE), / lɛt ɒf ˈfaɪəwɜːks / (BrE)
to set fire to fireworks so that they explode or display in celebration or entertainment
ignite and display fireworks

Examples

Every year, the town lets off fireworks for Independence Day.
The children watched excitedly as their parents let off fireworks in the garden.
It's illegal to let off fireworks in this park without a permit.

Synset Examples

They let off fireworks to celebrate the New Year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: set off fireworks - to cause fireworks to explode or display
