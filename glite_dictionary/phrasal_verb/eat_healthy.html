to eat healthy

1. VERB

/ it ˈhɛlθi / (AmE), / iːt ˈhɛlθi / (BrE)
to consume food that is good for one's health; to maintain a diet rich in nutrients and low in unhealthy additives.
consume nutritious food

Examples

Many people try to eat healthy by choosing fruits and vegetables over processed foods.
She realized she needed to eat healthy to have more energy during the day.
After his health scare, Mark decided to eat healthy and exercise regularly.

Synset Examples

She tried to ungage from the buffet and eat healthy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eat well - to eat in a healthy or nutritionally balanced way
  • Antonym: eat junk food - to eat food that is high in calories and low in nutritional value
