to get cracking

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈkrækɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈkrækɪŋ / (BrE)
to begin doing something quickly and energetically; to start taking action promptly
begin actively; start quickly
informal

Examples

If we want to finish before lunch, we'd better get cracking right now.
The deadline's approaching, so let's get cracking on the report.
She told the team to get cracking if they wanted to win the competition.

Synset Examples

Get cracking, please!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to get going - to begin moving or doing something; to start taking action
