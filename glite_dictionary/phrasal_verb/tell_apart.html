to tell apart

1. VERB

/ tɛl əˈpɑrt / (AmE), / tɛl əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
to recognize or show the difference between two or more people or things; to distinguish one from another
distinguish between; differentiate

Examples

It's sometimes hard to tell apart identical twins.
Can you tell apart these two kinds of mushrooms?
Their voices are so similar that I can't tell them apart on the phone.

Synset Examples

We distinguish several kinds of maple

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to distinguish - to recognize or treat something as different
  • Synonym: to differentiate - to recognize or show that things are different
  • Antonym: to confuse - to mistake one thing for another
