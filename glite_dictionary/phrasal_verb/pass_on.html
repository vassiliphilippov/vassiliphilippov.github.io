to pass on

1. VERB

/ pæs ɑn / (AmE), / pɑːs ɒn / (BrE)
to distribute or transmit something to others, especially by handing it or telling it to someone else
distribute or transmit to others

Examples

Could you pass on this message to your colleagues?
The manager will pass on the memo to all team members.
All outdated documents should be shredded rather than passed on to others.

Synset Examples

This letter is being circulated among the faculty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to circulate - to pass from person to person or place to place

Synset POS: v

/ pæs ɑn / (AmE), / pɑːs ɒn / (BrE)
to give information, news, or a message to someone else; to communicate something to others
communicate or transmit information

Examples
