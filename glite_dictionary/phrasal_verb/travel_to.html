to travel to

1. VERB

/ ˈtrævəl tu / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl tʊ / (BrE)
to go from one place to another, especially for pleasure, exploration, or work
go to a place, especially to visit

Examples

I hope to travel to Japan next year for vacation.
She often travels to different cities for work conferences.
We have traveled to many countries together over the years.

Synset Examples

Did you ever visit Paris?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visit - to go to see and spend time in a place
