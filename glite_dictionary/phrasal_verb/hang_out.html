to hang out

1. VERB

/ hæŋ aʊt / (AmE), / hæŋ aʊt / (BrE)
to spend time relaxing or socializing in a particular place or with particular people
spend time relaxing with others
informal

Examples

We usually hang out at Jake's house after school.
She loves to hang out with her friends on weekends.
They were hanging out at the park until it got dark.

Synset Examples

She hangs out at the corner cafe

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chill - relax or take it easy, often informally
