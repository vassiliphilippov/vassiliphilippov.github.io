to jolly up

1. VERB

/ ˈdʒɑli ʌp / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒli ʌp / (BrE)
to try to make someone feel happier or more cheerful
make someone feel happier

Examples

We brought balloons and cake to jolly up the party after the bad news.
She tried to jolly up her friend with some silly jokes and stories.
The manager decided to jolly up the team after their disappointing performance.

Synset Examples

She tried to cheer up the disappointed child when he failed to win the spelling bee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cheer up - to make someone feel happier or more cheerful
