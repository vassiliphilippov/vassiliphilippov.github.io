to get started

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈstɑːɹtɪd / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈstɑːtɪd / (BrE)
to begin to do something; to start being active or involved in an activity
to begin an activity or task

Examples

We should get started on our homework before it gets too late.
Once everyone arrives, we can get started with the meeting agenda.
He needed some coffee to get started this morning.

Synset Examples

Get cracking, please!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to begin - to start doing something; to commence
  • Antonym: finish - to bring an activity to an end
