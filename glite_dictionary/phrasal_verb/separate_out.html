to separate out

1. VERB

/ ˈsɛpəˌreɪt aʊt / (AmE), / ˈsɛpəreɪt aʊt / (BrE)
to remove something from a mixture, especially by using a filter or other method
remove from a mixture

Examples

After the mixture settled, the technician separated out the solid particles from the liquid.
The lab uses a centrifuge to separate out the different components of blood.
You can separate out impurities from water by passing it through a filter.

Synset Examples

filter out the impurities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to filter out - to remove unwanted material by means of a filter
