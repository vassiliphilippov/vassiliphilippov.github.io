to rooted in

1. VERB

/ ˈruːtɪd ɪn / (AmE), / ˈruːtɪd ɪn / (BrE)
firmly established or originating from a particular source or basis
based on or established in something

Examples

Many modern traditions are rooted in ancient customs and beliefs.
Her passion for music is rooted in her childhood experiences.
These social problems are deeply rooted in economic inequality.

Synset Examples

That concept is rooted in derogatory stereotypes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: based on - founded or built upon something
