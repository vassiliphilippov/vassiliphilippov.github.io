to act reflexively

1. VERB

/ ækt rɪˈflɛksɪvli / (AmE), / ækt rɪˈflɛksɪvli / (BrE)
to act in response to something without conscious thought or control; to respond automatically
respond automatically, without conscious thought

Examples

Whenever startled, she tends to act reflexively rather than consider her response.
If you act reflexively in stressful situations, you might regret your decisions later.
Many animals act reflexively when they sense danger, moving instantly to protect themselves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: act automatically - to perform actions without conscious thought
