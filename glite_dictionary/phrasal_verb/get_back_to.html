to get back to

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈbæk tu / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈbak tə / (BrE)
to reply to someone or provide them with information at a later time
reply or respond later

Examples

I'll get back to you once I have more information.
She promised to get back to him after checking her schedule.
Sorry for the delay; I meant to get back to your email yesterday.

Synset Examples

Please get back to me later today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to respond - to say something in return; reply
  • Antonym: to ignore - to deliberately pay no attention to someone or something
