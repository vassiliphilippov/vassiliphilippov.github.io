to treat oneself

1. VERB

/ trit wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / triːt wʌnˈself / (BrE)
to do something special for oneself as a form of indulgence or reward
indulge oneself, reward oneself

Examples

After a long week at work, she decided to treat herself to a nice dinner.
You should treat yourself once in a while—you deserve it!
He planned to treat himself with a day off at the spa.

Synset Examples

Feeling like a po, she decided to treat herself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pamper oneself - to indulge or spoil oneself with comfort or luxury
