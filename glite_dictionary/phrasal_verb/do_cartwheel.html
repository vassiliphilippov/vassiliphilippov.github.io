to do cartwheel

1. VERB

/ du ˈkɑːrtˌwiːl / (AmE), / duː ˈkɑːtwiːl / (BrE)
to perform a gymnastic movement in which the body turns sideways in a complete circle, with hands and feet touching the ground alternately; to express extreme joy through this movement
perform a sideways gymnastic flip

Examples

After the announcement, she could barely contain her joy and did a cartwheel in the hallway.
During recess, the children love to do cartwheels on the grass.
He was so excited about his new job that he did cartwheel right outside the office.

Synset Examples

After winning the game, he did cartwheels with joy.
She can do cartwheels across the entire playground.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cartwheel - sideways rotary movement performed by gymnasts
