to get it right

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ɪt raɪt / (AmE), / ɡɛt ɪt raɪt / (BrE)
to do something correctly; to achieve the correct or desired result
to achieve the correct result

Examples

She practiced every day until she finally got it right.
If you don't get it right the first time, keep trying.
It's important to get it right when assembling the furniture.

Synset Examples

She kept practicing til she got it right.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: get it wrong - to fail to do something correctly
