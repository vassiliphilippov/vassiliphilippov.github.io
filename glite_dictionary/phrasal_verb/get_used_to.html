to get used to

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈjust tu / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈjuːst tuː / (BrE)
to become familiar with something or someone through repeated experience or exposure so that it no longer feels strange or difficult
become accustomed to something or someone

Examples

After several weeks, I finally got used to waking up early for work.
It can take time for children to get used to a new school.
You'll soon get used to driving on the left side of the road in the UK.

Synset Examples

I finally got used to my husband's odd habits

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to adapt - to adjust to new conditions or a new environment
  • Synonym: to accustom - to make someone or yourself familiar with something so that it seems normal or usual

2. VERB

to become familiar with something so that it no longer seems strange or new
become accustomed or familiar

Examples

You will get used to the city noise after a few weeks.

Synset Examples
