to file a patent

1. VERB

/ faɪl ə ˈpætənt / (AmE), / faɪl ə ˈpatənt / (BrE)
to formally submit an application for legal protection of an invention to the appropriate authority
submit an application for a patent

Examples

The inventor decided to file a patent before presenting his idea to investors.
It's crucial to file a patent as soon as possible to protect your intellectual property.
After years of research, she finally filed a patent for her revolutionary medical device.

Synset Examples

He filed a patent for his innovative new gadget.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apply for a patent - to request the grant of a patent on an invention
