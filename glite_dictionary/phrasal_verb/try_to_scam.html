to try to scam

1. VERB

/ traɪ tə skæm / (AmE), / traɪ tə skam / (BrE)
to attempt to deceive someone, especially for financial gain
attempt to deceive for money

Examples

She realized someone was trying to scam her with a fake lottery email.
If you try to scam people online, you could face criminal charges.
He tried to scam the bank by using forged documents.

Synset Examples

I can't believe that fucknigga tried to scam me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scam - to cheat or trick someone, especially to get money illegally
