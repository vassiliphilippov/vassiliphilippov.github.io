to put in

1. VERB

/ pʊt ɪn / (AmE), / pʊt ɪn / (BrE)
to place, fit, or thrust something into another thing
place or insert into something

Examples

Please put in your card to start the machine.
She carefully put in the key and turned the lock.
Before baking, put in the vegetables with the meat.

Synset Examples

Insert your ticket here

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insert - put or place something into another thing
  • Antonym: remove - take something out from another thing
