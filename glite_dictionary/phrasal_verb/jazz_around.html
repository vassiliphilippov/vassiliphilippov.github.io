to jazz around

1. VERB

/ dʒæz əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / dʒæz əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to wander aimlessly, typically in search of pleasure or entertainment
wander aimlessly for pleasure
informal

Examples

We spent the afternoon jazzing around the city just for fun.
Instead of going home, he decided to jazz around with some friends downtown.
She likes to jazz around on weekends, exploring new cafés and shops.
