to put under

1. VERB

/ pʊt ˈʌndər / (AmE), / pʊt ˈʌndə / (BrE)
to administer an anesthetic drug to someone so they become unconscious, especially before surgery or a medical procedure
administer anesthesia; make unconscious

Examples

The doctors will put under the patient before starting the procedure.
I was very nervous before being put under for my wisdom tooth extraction.
He had never been put under with general anesthesia before his knee operation.

Synset Examples

The patient must be anesthetized before the operation
anesthetize the gum before extracting the teeth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to anesthetize - to administer an anesthetic to make someone unconscious
