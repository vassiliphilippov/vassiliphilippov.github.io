to chase after people

1. VERB

/ tʃeɪs ˈæftɚ ˈpipəl / (AmE), / tʃeɪs ˈɑːftə ˈpiːpəl / (BrE)
to actively pursue the attention, affection, or approval of other people, often persistently or eagerly
pursue others' attention or affection

Examples

He would often chase after people who were not interested in him.
You shouldn't chase after people just to feel accepted.
After her move to the city, she stopped chasing after people and focused on herself.

Synset Examples

Don’t chase after people who don’t appreciate you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pursue - to follow or chase in order to catch or engage with
