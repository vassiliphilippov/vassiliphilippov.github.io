to chew over

1. VERB

/ tʃuː ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / tʃuː ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to think about or discuss something carefully and for a long time
think about carefully

Examples

I need some time to chew over your proposal before making a decision.
They chewed over the problem for hours but couldn't find a solution.
She was chewing over the idea of changing careers.

Synset Examples

I mulled over the events of the afternoon
philosophers have speculated on the question of God for thousands of years
The scientist must stop to observe and start to excogitate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to mull over - to think about deeply or consider carefully
  • Synonym: to ponder - to think about something carefully
