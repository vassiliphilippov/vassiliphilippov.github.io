to boost immunity

1. VERB

/ buːst ɪˈmjunəti / (AmE), / buːst ɪˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
to enhance the body's ability to resist diseases or infections
increase the body's disease resistance

Examples

Eating a balanced diet can help boost immunity and keep you healthy during flu season.
Doctors recommend regular exercise to boost immunity in both children and adults.
Certain vitamins are believed to boost immunity against common colds.

Synset Examples

The herbal tincture was supposed to boost immunity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strengthen the immune system - make the immune system more effective
