to piss away

1. VERB

/ pɪs əˈweɪ / (AmE), / pɪs əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to waste time, money, or resources carelessly or foolishly
waste or squander something carelessly
informal vulgar

Examples

He managed to piss away his entire paycheck in just two days.
If you piss away your time on distractions, you'll regret it later.
She regretted pissing away the opportunity to study abroad.

Synset Examples

He can't afford to piss away my time like this.
Don't piss away your chances at success.
He tends to piss away his money on unnecessary things.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to squander - to waste something in a reckless manner
  • Antonym: conserve - to prevent waste or loss; to save
