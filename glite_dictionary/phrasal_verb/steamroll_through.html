to steamroll through

1. VERB

/ ˈstiːmˌroʊl θruː / (AmE), / ˈstiːmˌrəʊl θruː / (BrE)
to dominate, overcome, or defeat someone or something with ease, often by using great force or determination
dominate or overwhelm easily

Examples

The top-seeded team managed to steamroll through the entire tournament without losing a game.
He hopes to steamroll through his exams after months of preparation.
Despite the challenges, the new policy seemed to steamroll through the committee with barely any opposition.

Synset Examples

She plans to steamroll through the competition this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crush - to defeat or overpower completely
  • Synonym: to overwhelm - to defeat or overpower greatly
  • Antonym: struggle through - to progress with difficulty, opposite of dominate easily

Etymology

From 'steamroller' (a heavy road roller powered by steam), used metaphorically for crushing opposition.
