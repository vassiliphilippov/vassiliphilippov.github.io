to block out distractions

1. VERB

/ blɑk aʊt dɪˈstrækʃənz / (AmE), / blɒk aʊt dɪˈstrækʃənz / (BrE)
to deliberately ignore or eliminate things that interrupt concentration or focus
ignore or eliminate interruptions

Examples

She puts on headphones to block out distractions at work.
Many students find it difficult to block out distractions in a noisy environment.
Meditation can help you block out distractions and improve your concentration.

Synset Examples

I need to block out distractions while studying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ignore - to pay no attention to something
