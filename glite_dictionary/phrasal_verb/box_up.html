to box up

1. VERB

/ bɑks ʌp / (AmE), / bɒks ʌp / (BrE)
to put something into a box or to enclose or confine as if in a box
enclose or pack into a box

Examples

We need to box up these dishes before we move.
She boxed up all her winter clothes for storage during summer.
The old toys were carefully boxed up and given to charity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pack - to put things into a container for storage or transport
