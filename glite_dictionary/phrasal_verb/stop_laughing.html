to stop laughing

1. VERB

/ stɑp ˈlæfɪŋ / (AmE), / stɒp ˈlɑːfɪŋ / (BrE)
to cease to laugh or to no longer find something amusing
cease to laugh

Examples

She was so amused that she couldn't stop laughing during the movie.
Once the joke was over, he managed to stop laughing with some effort.
Please stop laughing and pay attention to the lesson.

Synset Examples

I couldn't stop laughing at the monkey show.
I couldn't stop laughing at the absurdity of that schlockumentary.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: start laughing - to begin to laugh
