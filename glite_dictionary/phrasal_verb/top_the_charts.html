to top the charts

1. VERB

/ tɑp ðə tʃɑrts / (AmE), / tɒp ðə tʃɑːts / (BrE)
to reach the highest position in a music chart, especially in terms of sales or popularity
reach highest position in a music chart

Examples

Her debut single is topping the charts all over Europe this summer.
The band finally topped the charts after years of hard work and touring.
If the song keeps gaining popularity, it could top the charts by next week.

Synset Examples

Khia's new album is topping the charts this week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chart-topper - a song or album that is at the top of the music charts
