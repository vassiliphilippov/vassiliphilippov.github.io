to look good on

1. VERB

/ lʊk ɡʊd ɑn / (AmE), / lʊk ɡʊd ɒn / (BrE)
to appear attractive when worn by someone
to be attractive when worn

Examples

That dress really looks good on you.
I don't think this color looks good on him at all.
Bright jewelry often looks good on darker skin tones.

Synset Examples

He wore a greenish sweater that looked good on him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suit - to be right or appropriate for someone, especially in appearance
