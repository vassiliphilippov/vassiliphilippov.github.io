to connect up

1. VERB

/ kəˈnɛkt ʌp / (AmE), / kəˈnɛkt ʌp / (BrE)
to establish a link or connection between devices, systems, or components, typically using cables or other means
establish a connection between devices or systems

Examples

Before you can use the speakers, you need to connect up the wires.
The technician will connect up your internet tomorrow afternoon.
Did you remember to connect up the computer to the power supply?

Synset Examples

We need to connect up the new printer to the network.
It's important to connect up all the devices properly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to connect - to join together; to establish a relationship or link
