to bowl up

1. VERB

/ boʊl ʌp / (AmE), / bəʊl ʌp / (BrE)
to prepare or serve food in a bowl, especially as a convenient way to present it for eating
to put food in a bowl for serving

Examples

Can you bowl up some soup for everyone while I set the table?
After a long day, she likes to bowl up a quick stir-fry for dinner.
He always bowls up fruit and yogurt for breakfast before work.

Synset Examples

She always bowls up a big salad for gatherings.
Let's bowl up some pasta for dinner tonight.
They decided to bowl up some snacks for the party.
