to square with

1. VERB

/ skwɛr wɪð / (AmE), / skweə wɪð / (BrE)
to pay someone and settle a debt; to make payment in full to someone
pay and settle a debt

Examples

She promised to square with him by the end of the week.
After the job was finished, he squared with the contractor promptly.
I'll square with you for the tickets tomorrow.

Synset Examples

I squared with him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: settle up - to pay what is owed; to settle a debt
