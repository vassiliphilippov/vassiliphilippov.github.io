to sit right

1. VERB

/ sɪt raɪt / (AmE), / sɪt raɪt / (BrE)
to feel comfortable, appropriate, or suitable in a particular situation
feel comfortable or appropriate
informal

Examples

Something about his explanation just doesn't sit right with me.
That decision didn't sit right with the rest of the team.
If the arrangement doesn't sit right, let us know and we'll make adjustments.

Synset Examples

He doesn't sit right in that uncomfortable chair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to feel right - to seem comfortable or appropriate
  • Synonym: sit well - to be acceptable or suitable
