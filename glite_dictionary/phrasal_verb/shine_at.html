to shine at

1. VERB

/ ʃaɪn æt / (AmE), / ʃaɪn at / (BrE)
to be very good at something or perform particularly well in a specific area or activity
be very good at something

Examples

She really shines at playing the piano in front of an audience.
Our team shone at the regional science competition last year.
He shines at finding creative solutions to tough problems.

Synset Examples

She shines at math

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to excel at - to do extremely well at something
  • Antonym: struggle with - to have difficulty with something
