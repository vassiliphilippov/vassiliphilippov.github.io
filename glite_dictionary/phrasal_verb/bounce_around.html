to bounce around

1. VERB

/ baʊns əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / baʊns əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to discuss or exchange ideas or thoughts informally, often in a group setting
discuss ideas informally
informal

Examples

Let's bounce around some ideas before we make a final decision.
She loves to bounce around concepts with her colleagues at lunch.
New proposals were bounced around until everyone agreed on a plan.

Synset Examples

Ideas started to bounce around during the brainstorming session.
They let their thoughts bounce around until a solution emerged.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to brainstorm - to hold a group discussion to produce ideas or solve problems
