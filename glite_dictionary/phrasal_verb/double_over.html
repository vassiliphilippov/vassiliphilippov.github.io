to double over

1. VERB

/ ˈdʌbəl ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to bend forward or curl up suddenly, typically because of pain or laughter
bend forward suddenly (with pain, laughter)

Examples

She doubled over in pain after eating something bad.
He doubled over with laughter at the comedian's joke.
The boy doubled over and clutched his stomach during the soccer match.

Synset Examples

He doubled and vomited violently

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to straighten up - to return to an upright position after being bent or curled
