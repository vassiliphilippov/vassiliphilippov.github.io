to knock it off

1. VERB

/ nɑk ɪt ɔf / (AmE), / nɒk ɪt ɒf / (BrE)
(informal) used to tell someone to stop doing something that is annoying or disruptive.
stop doing something annoying
informal

Examples

If you don't knock it off, you'll have to leave the room.
The teacher told the students to knock it off when they started shouting.
Hey, knock it off! You're making too much noise.

Synset Examples

Please knock it off; I'm trying to focus.
If you don't knock it off, you'll get in trouble.
He told the kids to knock it off and settle down.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cut it out - stop doing something annoying (informal)
  • Synonym: to give it a rest - stop talking about or doing something annoying (informal)
