to get through

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈθruː / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈθruː / (BrE)
to succeed in establishing contact or communication with someone, especially by phone or other means
establish communication with

Examples

I tried calling her all morning, but I couldn't get through.
It took several attempts before he managed to get through to the customer service representative.
She finally got through to her parents after the storm knocked out the phone lines.

Synset Examples

Our advertisements reach millions
He never contacted his children after he emigrated to Australia

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reach - to succeed in communicating with someone
  • Synonym: contact - to get in touch with someone

/ ɡɛt ˈθru / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈθruː / (BrE)
(of an idea, message, or feeling) to become understood, accepted, or felt by someone; to succeed in communicating or making an impression.
reach or affect emotionally or mentally

Examples
