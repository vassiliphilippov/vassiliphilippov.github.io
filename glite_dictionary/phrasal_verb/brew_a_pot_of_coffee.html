to brew a pot of coffee

1. VERB

/ bru ə pɑt əv ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / bruː ə pɒt əv ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
to prepare a quantity of coffee by letting hot water infuse ground coffee beans, typically using a coffee pot or machine
make coffee by infusion

Examples

Every morning, she likes to brew a pot of coffee before starting work.
Could you brew a pot of coffee for our guests?
After dinner, he brewed a pot of coffee for everyone to enjoy.

Synset Examples

He brewed a pot of coffee for the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make coffee - to prepare coffee for drinking
