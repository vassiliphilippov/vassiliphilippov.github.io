to look like

1. VERB

/ lʊk laɪk / (AmE), / lʊk laɪk / (BrE)
to resemble someone or something in appearance
resemble in appearance

Examples

Many people say I look like my grandfather when he was young.
The clouds look like giant animals floating across the sky.
She looked like she had just seen a ghost.

Synset Examples

She looks like her mother

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to resemble - to be similar in appearance or character to someone or something
