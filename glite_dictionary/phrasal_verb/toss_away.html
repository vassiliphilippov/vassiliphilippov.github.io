to toss away

1. VERB

/ tɑs əˈweɪ / (AmE), / tɒs əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to throw or discard something so that it is no longer wanted or used
to throw or discard something

Examples

She decided to toss away her old clothes during the spring cleaning.
Don't toss away your chance to study abroad.
He absentmindedly tossed away the receipt after leaving the store.

Synset Examples

Put away your worries

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to throw away - to get rid of something by discarding it
  • Antonym: keep - to retain possession of something instead of discarding
