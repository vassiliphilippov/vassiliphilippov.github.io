to call someone a moke

1. VERB

/ kɔl ˈsʌmwʌn ə moʊk / (AmE), / kɔːl ˈsʌmwʌn ə məʊk / (BrE)
to refer to someone as a fool or idiot, often as an insult
describe someone as a fool or idiot
informal

Examples

I can't believe you would call someone a moke just for making a mistake.
It's not nice to call someone a moke during an argument.
He regretted calling someone a moke after he calmed down.

Synset Examples

She called him a moke for not standing up for himself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insult - to speak to or treat with disrespect or scornful abuse
