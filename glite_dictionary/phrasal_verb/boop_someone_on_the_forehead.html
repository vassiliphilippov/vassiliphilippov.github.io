to boop someone on the forehead

1. VERB

/ buːp ˈsʌmwʌn ɑn ðə ˈfɔːrhɛd / (AmE), / buːp ˈsʌmwən ɒn ðə ˈfɔːhɛd / (BrE)
to tap someone lightly and playfully on the forehead, often to be affectionate or silly
tap someone lightly on the forehead
informal

Examples

Whenever he was being silly, he would boop someone on the forehead to make them laugh.
She gently booped someone on the forehead and called it her signature move.
During the meeting, Tom resisted the urge to boop someone on the forehead for making a funny comment.

Synset Examples

He playfully booped her on the forehead during the movie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boop - to tap someone or something gently, usually on the nose

Etymology

Imitative of a light, playful tap, popularized in internet and pop culture contexts.
