to strike hard

1. VERB

/ straɪk hɑrd / (AmE), / straɪk hɑːd / (BrE)
to deliver a sharp or forceful blow or push to something or someone
deliver a sharp or forceful blow

Examples

The boxer struck hard with his right fist, knocking his opponent down.
She struck hard at the nail, driving it deep into the wood.
If you strike hard enough, the piñata will finally break open.

Synset Examples

He knocked the glass clear across the room

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hit - to strike someone or something with force
  • Degree Lower: tap - to strike lightly or gently
