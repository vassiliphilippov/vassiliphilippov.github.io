to command attention

1. VERB

/ kəˈmænd əˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / kəˈmɑːnd əˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
to attract people's notice or focus; to cause people to pay attention
attract notice or focus

Examples

The bold colors of the painting command attention as soon as you enter the room.
Her powerful voice commands attention during every meeting she leads.
He commanded attention with his confident manner and sharp suit.

Synset Examples

With a long figure, he effortlessly commanded attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attract attention - to draw people's focus or notice
  • Synonym: to draw attention - to make people notice something or someone
