to ascend the mountain

1. VERB

/ əˈsɛnd ðə ˈmaʊntən / (AmE), / əˈsɛnd ðə ˈmaʊntɪn / (BrE)
to climb or travel upwards on a mountain
climb up a mountain

Examples

Early in the morning, the hikers began to ascend the mountain before sunrise.
She trained for months to ascend the mountain without assistance.
As clouds gathered, they hesitated to ascend the mountain in challenging weather.

Synset Examples

We will ascend the mountain at dawn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: climb a mountain - to go up the side of a mountain using one's feet or hands
