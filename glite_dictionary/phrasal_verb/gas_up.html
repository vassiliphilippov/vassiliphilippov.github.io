to gas up

1. VERB

/ ɡæs ʌp / (AmE), / ɡæs ʌp / (BrE)
to fill a vehicle's fuel tank with gasoline
fill with gasoline
informal american

Examples

I need to gas up before we head out on the highway.
We stopped at the service station to gas up the truck.
Make sure you gas up the rental car before returning it.

Synset Examples

Gas up the car

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to refuel - to fill a vehicle or machine with more fuel
  • Synonym: to fill up - to make full, often used for putting fuel in a vehicle
