to check over

1. VERB

/ tʃɛk ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / tʃɛk ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to examine something carefully in order to determine its accuracy, quality, or condition
examine for accuracy or condition

Examples

Before we sign the contract, let's check over all the terms and conditions carefully.
She always checks over her essays before submitting them to catch any mistakes.
The mechanic checked over the car to make sure it was safe to drive.

Synset Examples

check the brakes
Check out the engine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to inspect - to look at something carefully in order to learn more about it or find problems
  • Antonym: overlook - to fail to notice or do something, the opposite of carefully examining
