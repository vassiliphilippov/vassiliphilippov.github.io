to bring to

1. VERB

/ brɪŋ tu / (AmE), / brɪŋ tuː / (BrE)
to cause a person to remember or think of something; to recall to mind
cause to recall or remember

Examples

This painting brings to mind memories of my childhood.
The old song brought to him feelings he thought he had forgotten.
Seeing her after many years brought to light many happy memories.

Synset Examples

These pictures bring back sad memories

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recall - to remember something; to bring back to mind
  • Synonym: to evoke - to bring a feeling, memory, or image into the mind
  • Antonym: to forget - to fail to remember
