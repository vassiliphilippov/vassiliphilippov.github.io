to serve alcohol

1. VERB

/ sɝv ˈælkəˌhɑl / (AmE), / sɜːv ˈælkəhɒl / (BrE)
to provide or sell alcoholic drinks to customers or guests, especially in a bar, restaurant, or event setting
provide alcoholic drinks to customers or guests

Examples

It is illegal for restaurants to serve alcohol to minors under the age of 21.
Many venues require a special license to serve alcohol at private parties.
After 10 p.m., the club stops serving alcohol to customers.

Synset Examples

They served alcohol at the wedding reception.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: serve - to provide food or drinks to someone
