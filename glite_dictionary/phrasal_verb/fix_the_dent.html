to fix the dent

1. VERB

/ fɪks ðə dɛnt / (AmE), / fɪks ðə dɛnt / (BrE)
to repair a small area of damage where the surface is bent inward, especially on a vehicle or metal object
repair a small inward bend or damage

Examples

The mechanic managed to fix the dent in my car door within an hour.
She tried to fix the dent herself, but it still showed under the paint.
After the accident, they had to call a specialist to fix the dent in the fender.

Synset Examples

He tried to fix the dent, but it was too deep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repair - to restore something to a good condition after damage
