to act erratically

1. VERB

/ ækt ɪˈræ.tɪ.kli / (AmE), / ækt ɪˈræ.tɪ.kli / (BrE)
to behave in a way that is unpredictable or inconsistent, often suddenly changing actions or moods without clear reason
behave in an unpredictable way

Examples

He started to act erratically after receiving the unexpected news.
Whenever she's under stress, she can act erratically at work.
The dog began to act erratically when it heard loud noises outside.

Synset Examples

She tends to act erratically when she's off her meds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: act unpredictably - to behave in an unexpected or unplanned way
