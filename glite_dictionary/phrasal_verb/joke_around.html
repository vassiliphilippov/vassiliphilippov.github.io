to joke around

1. VERB

/ dʒoʊk əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / dʒəʊk əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to engage in playful or lighthearted conversation, often without taking things seriously
engage in playful conversation

Examples

The kids joke around together after school, laughing and telling silly stories.
At work, we sometimes joke around to relieve stress during busy days.
She told them to stop joking around and focus on the task at hand.

Synset Examples

They joke around like they’re asshole buddies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fool around - to behave in a silly or playful way
  • Antonym: be serious - to act or speak without joking or playfulness
