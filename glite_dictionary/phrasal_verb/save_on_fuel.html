to save on fuel

1. VERB

/ seɪv ɑn fjuəl / (AmE), / seɪv ɒn fjuːəl / (BrE)
to use less fuel in order to reduce costs or conserve energy
use less fuel to save money or conserve energy

Examples

Many people try to save on fuel by carpooling to work.
Driving at a steady speed can help you save on fuel.
He switched to a more efficient car to save on fuel costs.

Synset Examples

She drove in fourth gear to save on fuel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conserve fuel - to use fuel carefully and avoid waste
