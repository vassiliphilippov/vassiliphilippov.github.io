to prim up

1. VERB

/ prɪm ʌp / (AmE), / prɪm ʌp / (BrE)
to dress or arrange oneself in a neat, proper, or formal way
dress or arrange oneself neatly and formally

Examples

She decided to prim up for the company dinner, wearing her best dress and pearls.
Before the interview, he took a moment to prim up in front of the mirror.
Whenever there's a family photo, my aunt insists that everyone prim up first.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to dress up - to put on formal or special clothes
