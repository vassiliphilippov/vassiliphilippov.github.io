to get in touch

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ɪn tʌtʃ / (AmE), / ɡɛt ɪn tʌtʃ / (BrE)
to establish communication or contact with someone, especially after a period without communication
make contact or communicate with someone

Examples

If you have any questions, please get in touch with our support team.
After moving abroad, it took months for her to get in touch with old friends.
He promised to get in touch as soon as he arrived in Paris.

Synset Examples

did you finally connect with your long-lost cousin?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contact - communicate with someone
  • Antonym: lose touch - to stop communicating with someone
