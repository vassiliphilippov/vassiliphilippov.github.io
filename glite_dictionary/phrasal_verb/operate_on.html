to operate on

1. VERB

/ ˈɑpəˌreɪt ɑn / (AmE), / ˈɒpəreɪt ɒn / (BrE)
to perform a surgical procedure on (a person or animal)
perform surgery on

Examples

The surgeon will operate on your knee tomorrow morning.
She has successfully operated on hundreds of patients during her career.
After reviewing his condition, the doctors decided to operate on him immediately.

Synset Examples

The doctors operated on the patient but failed to save his life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perform surgery - to carry out a surgical operation
