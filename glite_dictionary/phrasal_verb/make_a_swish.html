to make a swish

1. VERB

/ meɪk ə swɪʃ / (AmE), / meɪk ə swɪʃ / (BrE)
to produce a soft, swooshing sound, or in basketball, to score by making the ball go through the hoop without touching the rim
produce a swishing sound; score in basketball cleanly

Examples

Her silk skirt made a swish every time she turned around.
The crowd cheered when he made a swish from the three-point line.
As the broom passed over the floor, it made a swish that echoed through the quiet hallway.

Synset Examples

Her dress made a delightful swish as she walked.
He made a swish with his basketball shot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swish - a soft, swooshing sound
