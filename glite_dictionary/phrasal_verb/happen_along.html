to happen along

1. VERB

/ ˈhæp.ən əˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈhæp.ən əˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
to come across or arrive at a place by chance
arrive or find by chance

Examples

If you happen along this street after dark, you'll see the city lights sparkling.
We happened along a hidden bookstore while wandering the old part of town.
She often meets interesting people when she happens along new places during her travels.

Synset Examples

I was lucky to happen along that quaint café.
If you happen along my house, stop by!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to come across - to find or meet by chance
  • Synonym: stumble upon - to discover by accident
