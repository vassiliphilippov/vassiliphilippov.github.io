to ask for a discount

1. VERB

/ æsk fər ə dɪˈskaʊnt / (AmE), / ɑːsk fə ə dɪsˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
to request that the price of something be reduced
request a lower price

Examples

It's always smart to ask for a discount before finalizing a big purchase.
If you don't ask for a discount, you might be missing out on savings.
She politely asked for a discount, and the shopkeeper agreed to lower the price.

Synset Examples

Always ask for a discount when shopping.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bargain - to negotiate for a better price
