to band together

1. VERB

/ bænd təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / bænd təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
If people band together, they form a group or unite in order to achieve something or support each other.
to join together as a group

Examples

Local businesses decided to band together to support the community during tough times.
The villagers banded together to protest against the factory's pollution.
When facing a common enemy, people often band together despite their differences.

Synset Examples

The groups banded together

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to unite - join together for a common purpose
  • Antonym: split up - to break apart or disperse a group
