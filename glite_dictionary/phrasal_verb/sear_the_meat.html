to sear the meat

1. VERB

/ sɪr ðə mit / (AmE), / sɪə ðə miːt / (BrE)
to quickly cook the surface of meat at a high temperature to form a browned crust
brown the surface of meat quickly

Examples

Professional chefs always sear the meat before roasting it in the oven.
If you sear the meat first, it locks in the juices and flavor.
He forgot to sear the meat, so the stew lacked a rich, caramelized taste.

Synset Examples

Be sure to sear the meat for flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brown the meat - to cook the surface of meat until it turns brown
