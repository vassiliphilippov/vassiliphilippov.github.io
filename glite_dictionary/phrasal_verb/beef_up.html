to beef up

1. VERB

/ bif ʌp / (AmE), / biːf ʌp / (BrE)
to make something stronger, more effective, or more substantial
make stronger or more effective

Examples

The company decided to beef up its security after the incident.
We need to beef up our marketing efforts to attract more customers.
After getting feedback, the author beefed up the final chapter of his book.

Synset Examples

This exercise will strengthen your upper body
strengthen the relations between the two countries

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to strengthen - make stronger
  • Synonym: to fortify - make stronger or more secure

Etymology

From 'beef' (slang for muscular strength or substance), plus 'up'.
