to keep up with

1. VERB

/ kip ʌp wɪð / (AmE), / kiːp ʌp wɪð / (BrE)
to stay informed about the latest news, developments, or trends in a particular field or area
stay informed about developments

Examples

It's important to keep up with the latest research in your field.
She tries to keep up with her friends by reading their social media updates.
I find it challenging to keep up with all the new TV shows these days.

Synset Examples

He loves keeping up with the latest technology trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to follow - to keep oneself informed about something or someone
  • Antonym: to fall behind - to fail to maintain the same level or progress as others
