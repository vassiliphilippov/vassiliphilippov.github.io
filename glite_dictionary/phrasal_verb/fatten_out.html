to fatten out

1. VERB

/ ˈfætn̩ aʊt / (AmE), / ˈfatən aʊt / (BrE)
to make (someone or something) fatter or plumper; to cause to become rounder or thicker by increasing fat or bulk
make fatter or plumper

Examples

The farmer tried to fatten out his pigs before the market season.
They hope the new diet will help fatten out the underweight dog.
Over the summer, she fattened out the chickens with extra feed.

Synset Examples

We will plump out that poor starving child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to plump up - to make (something) fuller or rounder, often by increasing bulk or filling
