to want to puke

1. VERB

/ wɑnt tə pjuk / (AmE), / wɒnt tə pjuːk / (BrE)
to feel extremely nauseated or disgusted, often as a reaction to something unpleasant
feel nauseated or disgusted
informal

Examples

The sight of the spoiled food made me want to puke.
He was so nervous before his speech that he felt like he might want to puke.
Her rude comments were so awful they made me want to puke.

Synset Examples

He thought the movie was so bad it made him want to barf.
The smell made me want to puke right there.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: want to vomit - to feel the urge to throw up
  • Synonym: want to throw up - to feel like vomiting, especially due to disgust or nausea
