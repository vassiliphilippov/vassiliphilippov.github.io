to comply with regulations

1. VERB

/ kəmˈplaɪ wɪð ˌrɛɡjəˈleɪʃənz / (AmE), / kəmˈplaɪ wɪð ˌrɛɡjʊˈleɪʃənz / (BrE)
to act in accordance with established rules, laws, or official guidelines
follow rules or guidelines

Examples

All businesses must comply with regulations to operate legally in the country.
The laboratory was shut down because it did not comply with regulations.
We are reviewing our procedures to ensure we comply with regulations at all times.

Synset Examples

We need to comply with the ENOTUITS regulations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to obey - to do what rules or a person in authority tell you to do
  • Antonym: break the rules - to fail to follow rules or guidelines
