to call for

1. VERB

/ kɔl fər / (AmE), / kɔːl fə / (BrE)
to publicly request or demand the participation, presence, or submission of something or someone
request or demand participation/presence

Examples

The committee will call for volunteers to help with the event.
The government has decided to call for public input on the new policy.
Every year, the foundation calls for proposals from researchers around the world.

Synset Examples

The organizers invite submissions of papers for the conference

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invite - to request formally or politely for someone to attend or take part
