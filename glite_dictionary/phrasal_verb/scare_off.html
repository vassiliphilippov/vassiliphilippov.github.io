to scare off

1. VERB

/ skɛr ɔf / (AmE), / skɛə ɒf / (BrE)
to make someone or something go away or stop doing something by frightening them
frighten away; cause to leave out of fear

Examples

The loud noise scared off the birds in the garden.
Rising prices may scare off potential buyers.
Many investors were scared off by the company's poor financial report.

Synset Examples

dashed by the refusal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to frighten away - make someone or something go away by making them afraid
