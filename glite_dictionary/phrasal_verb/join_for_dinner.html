to join for dinner

1. VERB

/ dʒɔɪn fər ˈdɪnər / (AmE), / dʒɔɪn fə ˈdɪnə / (BrE)
to participate in a meal with others, especially as an invitation to share dinner together
to share a dinner with others

Examples

We invited Sarah to join for dinner after the conference ended.
Would you like to join for dinner at our place tomorrow night?
He couldn't join for dinner because he had another engagement.

Synset Examples

I would love to join you for dinner.
Thomas will join us for dinner tonight.
Join us for dinner; it's going to be fun!
You're joining us for dinner, am I right?
We invited Darren to join us for dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: have dinner with - to eat dinner together with someone
