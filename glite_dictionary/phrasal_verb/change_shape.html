to change shape

1. VERB

/ tʃeɪndʒ ʃeɪp / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ ʃeɪp / (BrE)
to assume a different shape or form; to alter one's outward appearance or physical structure
assume a different shape or form

Examples

When heated, plastic can change shape and become flexible.
The clouds seem to change shape constantly as they drift across the sky.
Over time, your shoes may change shape to fit your feet better.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to transform - to change in form, appearance, or structure
  • Synonym: to morph - to change smoothly from one image to another by small gradual steps
