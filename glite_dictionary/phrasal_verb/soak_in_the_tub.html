to soak in the tub

1. VERB

/ soʊk ɪn ðə tʌb / (AmE), / səʊk ɪn ðə tʌb / (BrE)
to relax by immersing oneself in a bathtub filled with water for an extended period
bathe and relax in a bathtub

Examples

After a stressful meeting, she loves to soak in the tub for an hour.
He often listens to music while soaking in the tub at night.
Many people find it relaxing to soak in the tub before bed.

Synset Examples

I like to soak in the tub after a long day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to take a bath - to wash oneself in a bathtub
