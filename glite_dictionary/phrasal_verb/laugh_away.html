to laugh away

1. VERB

/ læf əˈweɪ / (AmE), / lɑːf əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to treat a problem or difficulty as unimportant by joking or laughing about it
dismiss a problem by laughing

Examples

He tried to laugh away his embarrassment during the meeting.
She managed to laugh away their criticism and stay positive.
It's not always possible to laugh away serious concerns.

Synset Examples

She laughs away all these problems

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to shrug off - to dismiss or ignore something as unimportant
  • Synonym: to brush off - to reject or dismiss something casually
