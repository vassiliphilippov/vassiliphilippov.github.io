to part ways

1. VERB

/ pɑrt weɪz / (AmE), / pɑːt weɪz / (BrE)
to leave each other and go in different directions; to end a relationship or association
separate or go different ways

Examples

After graduation, the two friends parted ways but stayed in touch through email.
The company parted ways with its longtime CEO last year.
We decided it was best to part ways and pursue our own interests.

Synset Examples

They parted ways with a cheerful hasta!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: separate - to stop being together or connected
