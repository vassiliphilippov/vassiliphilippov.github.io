to shine through

1. VERB

/ ʃaɪn θru / (AmE), / ʃaɪn θruː / (BrE)
(of a quality or characteristic) to be clearly visible or evident, especially despite difficulties or obscuring factors.
be clearly visible or evident

Examples

Despite his nervousness, his passion for teaching really shone through during the class.
Her natural kindness always manages to shine through in her actions.
No matter the challenge, his sense of humor shines through every time.

Synset Examples

Her talent will surely shine through in this competition.
Even in tough times, his optimism shines through.
Her Perthonality shone through during the presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stand out - to be very noticeable or prominent
  • Antonym: to fade away - to become less visible or evident
