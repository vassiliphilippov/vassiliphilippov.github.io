to rear-end

1. VERB

/ ˈrɪr ˌɛnd / (AmE), / ˈrɪə ˌɛnd / (BrE)
to crash into the back of another vehicle, especially in a traffic accident
collide with the back of a vehicle

Examples

Be careful not to rear-end the car in front of you when traffic stops suddenly.
He was late because another driver rear-ended his vehicle on the highway.
The insurance company asked if she had ever rear-ended someone at a stoplight.

Synset Examples

The car rear-ended me

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crash into - to collide with something or someone
