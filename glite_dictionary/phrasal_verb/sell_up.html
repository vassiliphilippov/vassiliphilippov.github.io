to sell up

1. VERB

/ sɛl ʌp / (AmE), / sɛl ʌp / (BrE)
to sell all of one's possessions or stock, often in order to leave or close a business
sell all possessions or stock

Examples

After her husband died, she decided to sell up and move to France.
The company is planning to sell up its assets and cease operations this year.
Many farmers were forced to sell up due to the economic downturn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to liquidate - sell assets for cash, especially to settle debts
