to fight monsters

1. VERB

/ faɪt ˈmɑnstɚz / (AmE), / faɪt ˈmɒnstəz / (BrE)
to battle or engage in combat with fictional or mythical creatures, especially in video games or stories
to battle fictional creatures, especially in games

Examples

In many role-playing games, players fight monsters to gain experience and treasures.
She spent the afternoon fighting monsters with her friends in their favorite online game.
Children love to imagine they are heroes who fight monsters and save the world.

Synset Examples

You can explore caves and fight monsters in Minecraft.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: battle - to fight against someone or something
  • Synonym: face off against - to confront in a direct challenge
