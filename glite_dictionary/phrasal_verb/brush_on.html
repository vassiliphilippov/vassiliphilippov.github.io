to brush on

1. VERB

/ brʌʃ ɑn / (AmE), / brʌʃ ɒn / (BrE)
to apply (a substance) to a surface using a brush
apply using a brush

Examples

To give the chicken a crisp finish, brush on a little olive oil before baking.
She carefully brushed on the first layer of paint to the canvas.
Before serving, brush on some melted butter for added flavor.

Synset Examples

Brush butter on the roast

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spread - to apply (a substance) over a surface without a brush
