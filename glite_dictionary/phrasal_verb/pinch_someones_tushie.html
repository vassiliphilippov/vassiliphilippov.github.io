to pinch someone's tushie

1. VERB

/ pɪntʃ ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈtʊʃi / (AmE), / pɪntʃ ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈtʊʃi / (BrE)
to playfully squeeze someone’s buttocks, often as a gesture of affection or flirtation
playfully squeeze someone’s buttocks
informal american

Examples

She couldn't resist and pinched his tushie as he walked by.
It surprised him when his partner pinched his tushie in the kitchen.
They both laughed after Sarah pinched Mike's tushie at the party.

Synset Examples

He laughed when she pinched his tushie playfully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pinch - to squeeze tightly between the finger and thumb

Etymology

Tushie is a North American informal term, diminutive of 'tush', likely from Yiddish 'tokhes'.
