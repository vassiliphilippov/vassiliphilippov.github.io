to expose oneself to the sun

1. VERB

/ ɪkˈspoʊz wʌnˌsɛlf tu ðə sʌn / (AmE), / ɪkˈspəʊz wʌnˌsɛlf tuː ðə sʌn / (BrE)
to deliberately be in direct sunlight for a period of time, especially for tanning or warmth
to spend time in direct sunlight

Examples

Doctors advise not to expose oneself to the sun during the hottest hours of the day.
She loves to expose herself to the sun when she goes to the beach on vacation.
If you expose yourself to the sun too much, be sure to wear sunscreen to protect your skin.

Synset Examples

You shouldn't expose yourself to the sun for too long.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sunbathe - to sit or lie in the sun to get a suntan
