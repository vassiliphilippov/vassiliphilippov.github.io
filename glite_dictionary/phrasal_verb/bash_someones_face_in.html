to bash someone's face in

1. VERB

/ bæʃ ˈsʌmwʌnz feɪs ɪn / (AmE), / bæʃ ˈsʌmwənz feɪs ɪn / (BrE)
to strike someone violently in the face, often with the intention of causing serious injury
attack someone violently in the face
informal

Examples

If you threaten him again, he'll bash your face in without hesitation.
The thug tried to bash her face in, but she managed to escape.
He told the bully not to bash his face in over a trivial argument.

Synset Examples

You can't just bash someone's face in over a disagreement.
Don't bash someone's head in a fit of rage.
The movie had a scene where he bashed someone's face in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to beat up - to attack someone physically and injure them
  • Synonym: to knock out - to hit someone so hard that they become unconscious
