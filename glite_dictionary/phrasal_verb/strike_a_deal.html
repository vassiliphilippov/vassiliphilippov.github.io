to strike a deal

1. VERB

/ straɪk ə dil / (AmE), / straɪk ə diːl / (BrE)
to reach an agreement or compromise, especially after negotiation.
reach an agreement after negotiation

Examples

After hours of negotiation, the two companies finally struck a deal on the merger.
It took several meetings before the government and the union could strike a deal.
Both parties are eager to strike a deal that benefits everyone involved.

Synset Examples

They will strike a deal by the end of the week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make a deal - to come to an agreement, especially in business
  • Synonym: to reach an agreement - to achieve a mutual arrangement or understanding
