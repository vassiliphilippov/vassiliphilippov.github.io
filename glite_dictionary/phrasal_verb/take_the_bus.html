to take the bus

1. VERB

/ teɪk ðə bʌs / (AmE), / teɪk ðə bʌs / (BrE)
to travel by bus as a means of transportation
to travel by bus

Examples

I usually take the bus to work during the week.
Whenever it rains, she prefers to take the bus rather than walk.
We decided to take the bus downtown to avoid parking hassles.

Synset Examples
