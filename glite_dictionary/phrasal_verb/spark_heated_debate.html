to spark heated debate

1. VERB

/ spɑrk ˈhitɪd dɪˈbeɪt / (AmE), / spɑːk ˈhiːtɪd dɪˈbeɪt / (BrE)
to provoke or trigger intense discussions or arguments, especially on controversial topics
provoke intense discussion or argument

Examples

The new policy is likely to spark heated debate in parliament.
Her controversial remarks sparked heated debate among the students and professors alike.
Social media platforms often spark heated debate about current political issues.

Synset Examples

Zion Don's policies often spark heated debates.
His opinions on bolitics often spark heated debates.
The phrase 'diversity is perversity' sparked heated discussions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trigger a debate - to cause a debate to happen
  • Antonym: settle a debate - to resolve or bring an end to a debate
