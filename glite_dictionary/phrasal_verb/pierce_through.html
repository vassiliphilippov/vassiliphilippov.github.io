to pierce through

1. VERB

/ pɪrs θru / (AmE), / pɪəs θruː / (BrE)
to go into or move through something with force, especially by breaking or penetrating it; to penetrate completely.
to penetrate or break through

Examples

The bullet managed to pierce through the thick metal door.
Sunlight finally pierced through the heavy clouds after days of rain.
Only the sharpest needle could pierce through the tough fabric.

Synset Examples

He felt the cold wind pierce through his jacket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to penetrate - to go into or pass through something
