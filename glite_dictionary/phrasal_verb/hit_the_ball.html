to hit the ball

1. VERB

/ hɪt ðə bɔl / (AmE), / hɪt ðə bɔːl / (BrE)
to strike a ball, typically with a bat, club, or racket, in a game or sport.
strike a ball in a game or sport

Examples

She managed to hit the ball over the fence for a home run.
Try to hit the ball squarely with the center of your racket.
He missed twice before finally hitting the ball cleanly.

Synset Examples

She hit the ball with a big bat, sending it flying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strike the ball - to hit a ball forcefully in a game
