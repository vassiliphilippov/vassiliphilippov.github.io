to aim high

1. VERB

/ eɪm haɪ / (AmE), / eɪm haɪ / (BrE)
to set ambitious goals or aspirations; to strive for significant achievement
set ambitious goals

Examples

If you want to make a difference, you should always aim high in your endeavors.
Many successful people attribute their achievements to the decision to aim high and never settle.
She told her son to aim high and never give up on his dreams.

Synset Examples

To succeed, you need to aim high and work hard.
She always encourages her students to aim high.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: set one's sights high - to have high or ambitious goals
