to happen upon

1. VERB

/ ˈhæpən əˈpɑn / (AmE), / ˈhæpən əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to come across or discover by chance; to encounter unexpectedly
discover or encounter by chance

Examples

While hiking in the woods, we happened upon an abandoned cabin.
She happened upon an old friend at the market yesterday.
They happened upon a solution to the problem quite by accident.

Synset Examples

We find this idea in Plato
I happened upon the most wonderful bakery not very far from here
She chanced upon an interesting book in the bookstore the other day

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to chance upon - to find or meet something or someone by chance
  • Synonym: stumble across - to find something by accident

2. VERB

/ ˈhæp.ən əˈpɑn / (AmE), / ˈhæp.ən əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to find or discover something by chance, unexpectedly
find or discover by chance

Examples

While cleaning the attic, I happened upon a box of my grandmother's letters.
They happened upon a charming little café while exploring the old town.
If you happen upon any old coins during your walk, let me know.

Synset Examples

the archeologists chanced upon an old tomb
she struck a goldmine
The hikers finally struck the main path to the lake

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to come across - find or encounter something by chance
  • Synonym: stumble upon - discover by accident
  • Antonym: to look for - try to find something, usually intentionally
