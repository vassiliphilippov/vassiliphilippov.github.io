to gather in

1. VERB

/ ˈɡæðɚ ɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡæðə ɪn / (BrE)
to fold up or bring together, especially fabric or material, so that it occupies less space
fold or draw something together

Examples

Please gather in the sails before the storm hits.
She gathered in the fabric at the waist to make the dress fit better.
He quickly gathered in the picnic blanket as the rain started to fall.

Synset Examples

take in the sails

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fold up - to bend or bring together parts so as to make compact; to fold
