to hole out

1. VERB

/ hoʊl aʊt / (AmE), / həʊl aʊt / (BrE)
(in golf) to hit the ball into the hole, usually to complete a hole.
hit a golf ball into the hole

Examples

She managed to hole out with just two putts.
After several tries in the bunker, he finally holed out.
It took him three strokes to hole out on the last green.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sink - (in golf) to put the ball into the hole with a shot
