to not feel it

1. VERB

/ nɑt fil ɪt / (AmE), / nɒt fiːl ɪt / (BrE)
to not be interested in or enthusiastic about something; to not have a positive feeling or motivation toward an activity or idea
lack enthusiasm or interest
informal

Examples

Sorry, but I'm not feeling it tonight and would rather stay home.
I tried yoga, but I was not feeling it after a few weeks.
The audience was not feeling it, so the comedian cut his set short.

Synset Examples

He admitted he's not feeling it with this project.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: be into - to be enthusiastic about or interested in something
  • Synonym: not care for - to not like or be interested in something
