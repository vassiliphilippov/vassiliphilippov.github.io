to hinge upon

1. VERB

/ hɪndʒ əˈpɑn / (AmE), / hɪndʒ əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to depend entirely on or be determined by something
depend on; be contingent on

Examples

The entire decision will hinge upon the outcome of the negotiations.
Her promotion may hinge upon the successful completion of this project.
Whether we go on vacation this year will hinge upon our financial situation.

Synset Examples

The outcomes rides on the results of the election
Your grade will depend on your homework

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to depend on - to be determined by or rely on something
  • Synonym: to rest on - to depend on or be based on something

Etymology

From 'hinge' (the support on which a door swings), figuratively meaning a critical point of dependence.
