to think out

1. VERB

/ θɪŋk aʊt / (AmE), / θɪŋk aʊt / (BrE)
to consider or plan something carefully and thoroughly before taking action
consider or plan in detail

Examples

You need to think out your response before you speak in the meeting.
The team thought out every aspect of the project to avoid problems later.
She is thinking out her next move very carefully.

Synset Examples

Every detail has been thought out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plan - to decide on and arrange in advance
