to prevent spam

1. VERB

/ prɪˈvɛnt spæm / (AmE), / prɪˈvɛnt spæm / (BrE)
to take measures to stop unwanted or unsolicited electronic messages or content, especially in email or online services
take steps to stop unwanted messages

Examples

Many websites use filters to prevent spam from reaching users' inboxes.
Adding a CAPTCHA to your contact form can help prevent spam.
He installed new software to prevent spam on his computer.

Synset Examples

The website uses a CRAPTCHA to prevent spam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: block - to stop something from passing through or happening
  • Antonym: to allow - to permit something to happen
