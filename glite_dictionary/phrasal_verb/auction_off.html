to auction off

1. VERB

/ ˈɔkʃən ɔf / (AmE), / ˈɔːkʃən ɒf / (BrE)
to sell something to the highest bidder at an auction, especially when disposing of goods or assets
sell by public auction

Examples

The company decided to auction off its old equipment to raise funds.
She plans to auction off her vintage car next month.
Hundreds of rare books were auctioned off at the charity event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sell - to give or hand over something in exchange for money
  • Antonym: buy - to acquire something by paying for it
