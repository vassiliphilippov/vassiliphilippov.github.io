to navigate office politics

1. VERB

/ ˈnævɪˌɡeɪt ˈɑːfɪs pəˈlɪtɪks / (AmE), / ˈnævɪɡeɪt ˈɒfɪs pəˈlɪtɪks / (BrE)
to manage and deal with the complex interpersonal relationships, power dynamics, and informal networks within a workplace
manage workplace relationships and power dynamics

Examples

Learning how to navigate office politics is crucial for anyone hoping to advance their career.
She found it challenging to navigate office politics in such a competitive environment.
New hires often struggle to navigate office politics during their first few months.

Synset Examples

Navigating office politics is like a minefield.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: office politics - the informal, often manipulative relationships and power structures in a workplace
