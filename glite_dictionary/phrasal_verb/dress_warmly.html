to dress warmly

1. VERB

/ drɛs ˈwɔrmli / (AmE), / drɛs ˈwɔːmli / (BrE)
to put on enough warm clothes to protect yourself from cold weather
put on warm clothing for cold weather

Examples

Remember to dress warmly before you go out into the snow.
She always tells her children to dress warmly in winter.
If you're planning to hike today, make sure you dress warmly for the cold mountain air.

Synset Examples

It's kind of chilly outside today, so dress warmly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to wrap up - to dress in warm clothes
