to train together

1. VERB

/ treɪn təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / treɪn təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to practise or exercise as a group to improve skills or performance
practise as a group

Examples

The team decided to train together before the tournament to build better teamwork.
Many couples choose to train together at the gym for motivation and support.
Because they train together every morning, their performance is steadily improving.

Synset Examples

They decided to train together for better results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: train - to practise to improve skills or fitness
