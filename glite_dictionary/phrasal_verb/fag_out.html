to fag out

1. VERB

/ fæɡ aʊt / (AmE), / fæɡ aʊt / (BrE)
to become extremely tired or exhausted, especially from physical effort or stress
become exhausted through exertion

Examples

After running the marathon, he really fagged out by the finish line.
Don't fag out before the end of the exam; pace yourself.
She fagged out after studying all night for her finals.

Synset Examples

We wore ourselves out on this hike

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to wear out - to make someone very tired or exhausted
  • Synonym: exhaust - to make very tired or fatigued

Etymology

From British slang "fag" meaning to tire or exhaust, origin unknown.
