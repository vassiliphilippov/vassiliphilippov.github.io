to play as

1. VERB

/ pleɪ æz / (AmE), / pleɪ az / (BrE)
to take on the role of a specific character or entity in a video game or similar activity
take role of a character in a game

Examples

In the latest adventure, you can play as a dragon and explore the magical world.
She chose to play as the main villain for an extra challenge.
Players often debate which character is best to play as in competitive matches.

Synset Examples

The kids loved playing as Wario in the new game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: play - to engage in a game or activity for enjoyment
