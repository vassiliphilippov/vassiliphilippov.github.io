to liven up

1. VERB

/ ˈlaɪvən ʌp / (AmE), / ˈlaɪvən ʌp / (BrE)
to make something or someone more lively, interesting, or energetic; to become more lively or energetic
make or become more lively

Examples

We added some colorful pillows to liven up the living room.
A good joke can really liven up a dull meeting.
The party didn't start to liven up until more people arrived.

Synset Examples

let's liven up this room a bit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to brighten up - to make or become brighter and more cheerful

Etymology

From "liven" (to make alive) + "up", first attested in the 19th century.
