to check for leaks

1. VERB

/ tʃɛk fər liks / (AmE), / tʃɛk fə liːks / (BrE)
to examine something to see if there is any leakage of liquid or gas
inspect to find leaks

Examples

After installing the new pipe, the plumber will check for leaks before finishing the job.
You should regularly check for leaks under your kitchen sink to avoid water damage.
The technician arrived promptly and began to check for leaks in the gas lines.

Synset Examples

She joked that it was time to check her eyelids for leaks.
The technician checked the boiler for any leaks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to inspect - to look at something carefully to find problems or errors
  • Synonym: test for leaks - to conduct a test to determine if something is leaking
