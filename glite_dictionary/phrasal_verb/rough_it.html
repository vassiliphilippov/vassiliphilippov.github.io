to rough it

1. VERB

/ ˈrʌf ɪt / (AmE), / ˈrʌf ɪt / (BrE)
to live without the usual comforts or conveniences, typically for a short period
live without comforts or luxuries

Examples

We decided to rough it on our trip by staying in a tent instead of a hotel.
If you plan to rough it in the mountains, bring only the essentials.
He wasn't sure he could rough it for a whole week without running water.

Synset Examples

He learned to rough it while backpacking across Europe.
They chose to rough it in the wilderness for a week.
During our camping trip, we decided to rough it without any luxuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camp - to sleep outdoors or in simple shelters, often for recreation
