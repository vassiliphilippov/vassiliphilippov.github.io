to help with

1. VERB

/ hɛlp wɪθ / (AmE), / hɛlp wɪð / (BrE)
to assist someone in performing or completing a particular task or project
assist with a task or project

Examples

She offered to help with organizing the school fundraiser.
Can you help with the dishes after dinner tonight?
The volunteer team came to help with disaster relief efforts.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
