peed off

1. ADJ

/ pid ɑf / (AmE), / piːd ɒf / (BrE)
(informal) Annoyed or angry; irritated.
annoyed; irritated
informal

Examples

He was peed off when his computer crashed in the middle of his work.
She gets peed off if people are late for meetings.
I'm really peed off about the way they treated us.

Synset Examples

They were all peed off about the unexpected meeting.
He was so peed off after losing the game.
She got peed off when her plans were canceled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angry - feeling or showing strong annoyance or displeasure
  • Antonym: calm - not showing or feeling any anger or agitation
