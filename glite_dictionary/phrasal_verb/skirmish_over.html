to skirmish over

1. VERB

/ ˈskɝː.mɪʃ ˈoʊ.vɚ / (AmE), / ˈskɜː.mɪʃ ˈəʊ.və / (BrE)
to have a minor conflict or disagreement about something
have a minor conflict about something

Examples

The two friends skirmished over where to eat dinner that night.
Members of the committee skirmished over the wording of the proposal.
Siblings often skirmish over trivial matters like who gets the remote.

Synset Examples

They had a skirmish over the last piece of cake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to argue over - to argue about something
  • Synonym: quarrel over - to have a heated dispute about something
