to have on

1. VERB

/ hæv ɑn / (AmE), / hæv ɒn / (BrE)
to be wearing (a garment or item of clothing)
be wearing (clothes)

Examples

She always has on her favorite sweater when it's cold.
What did you have on at the party last night?
He had on a new suit for the interview.

Synset Examples

She was wearing yellow that day

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wear - to have clothing, jewelry, etc. on your body
