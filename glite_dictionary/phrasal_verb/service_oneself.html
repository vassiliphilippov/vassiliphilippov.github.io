to service oneself

1. VERB

/ ˈsɜrvɪs wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs wʌnˈself / (BrE)
to take care of one's own needs or perform tasks independently without relying on others
take care of one's own needs independently

Examples

She taught her children how to service oneself when getting ready for school each morning.
Travelers in a hostel often service oneself by making their own meals in the shared kitchen.
It's important for elderly people to remain able to service oneself for as long as possible.

Synset Examples

Learning to service oneself is empowering.
He prefers to service oneself rather than rely on others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to help oneself - to take something for oneself without asking; to serve oneself
