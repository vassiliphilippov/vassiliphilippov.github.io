to catch someone at

1. VERB

/ kætʃ ˈsʌmwʌn æt / (AmE), / kæʧ ˈsʌmwən ət / (BrE)
to meet or arrange to meet someone at a particular place
meet someone at a location

Examples

I'll catch you at the park after work if you're free.
Let's catch them at the train station before they leave town.
She asked if she could catch me at the office later this afternoon.

Synset Examples

I’ll catch you at the café; see you later!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meet - to come together with someone by arrangement
