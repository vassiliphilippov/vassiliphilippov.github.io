to take on the role

1. VERB

/ teɪk ɑn ðə roʊl / (AmE), / teɪk ɒn ðə rəʊl / (BrE)
to assume a particular position, job, or responsibility
assume a position or responsibility

Examples

After his promotion, he will take on the role of department manager.
Many actors dream of getting the chance to take on the role of Hamlet.
She felt honored to take on the role during such a crucial project.

Synset Examples

She will take on the role of team leader.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assume responsibility - to accept duty or accountability for something
