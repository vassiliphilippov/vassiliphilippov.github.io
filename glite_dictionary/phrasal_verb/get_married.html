to get married

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈmæɹid / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈmarid / (BrE)
to become legally joined with someone in marriage
to become joined in marriage

Examples

They decided to get married in a small ceremony by the sea.
After dating for five years, she finally got the chance to get married.
Many couples choose to get married later in life nowadays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to marry - to become the husband or wife of someone
