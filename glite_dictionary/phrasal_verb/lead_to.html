to lead to

1. VERB

/ lid tu / (AmE), / liːd tʊ / (BrE)
to cause or result in a particular outcome, often something negative or problematic
cause or result in (an outcome)

Examples

Ignoring safety regulations can lead to serious accidents in the workplace.
A sudden change in temperature may lead to unexpected weather patterns.
Eating too much sugar can lead to health problems over time.

Synset Examples

The doctor warned that untreated wounds could lead to gangrene.
Using 'wop' in conversation can lead to misunderstandings.
Being a nice guy can sometimes lead to trouble.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: result in - to have as a consequence or outcome
  • Synonym: to bring about - to cause something to happen
