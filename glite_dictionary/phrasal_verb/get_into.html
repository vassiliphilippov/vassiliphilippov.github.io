to get into

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈɪntu / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈɪntu / (BrE)
to put on or dress oneself in a particular piece of clothing
put clothing on oneself

Examples

She struggled to get into her tight new dress.
He quickly got into his uniform before the parade started.
It took me a while to get into all the layers for winter.
