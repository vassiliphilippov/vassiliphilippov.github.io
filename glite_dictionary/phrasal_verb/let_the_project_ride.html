to let the project ride

1. VERB

/ lɛt ðə ˈprɑdʒɛkt raɪd / (AmE), / lɛt ðə ˈprɒdʒɛkt raɪd / (BrE)
to allow a project to continue or progress without intervening or making changes
allow a project to proceed without interference
informal

Examples

The manager decided to let the project ride instead of stepping in with more suggestions.
Sometimes you need to trust your team and just let the project ride for a while.
We agreed to let the project ride until we saw clear results from the new strategy.

Synset Examples

Sometimes it’s best to let the project ride until the deadline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to let it ride - to allow a situation or decision to proceed without change or interference

Etymology

Based on the idiom 'let it ride,' originally from gambling slang meaning to leave a bet as is.
