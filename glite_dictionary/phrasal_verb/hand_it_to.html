to hand it to

1. VERB

/ ˈhænd ɪt tu / (AmE), / ˈhænd ɪt tə / (BrE)
to give credit or praise to someone for their achievements or actions
give credit or praise to someone

I’ll hand it to the team; their presentation was outstanding.
