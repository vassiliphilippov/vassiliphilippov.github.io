to crush out

1. VERB

/ krʌʃ aʊt / (AmE), / krʌʃ aʊt / (BrE)
to extinguish something completely by crushing it, especially a cigarette or similar burning object
extinguish by crushing

Examples

She crushed out her cigarette before entering the building.
The firefighter quickly crushed out the small flames to prevent them from spreading.
He crushed out the ember with his boot to make sure the campfire was really out.

Synset Examples

stub out your cigarette now
stub out your cigar

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stub out - to extinguish a cigarette by pressing its end against a surface
