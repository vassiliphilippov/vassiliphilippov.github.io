to spout bullpoo

1. VERB

/ spaʊt ˈbʊlˌpu / (AmE), / spaʊt ˈbʊlˌpuː / (BrE)
to talk nonsense or say things that are not true, especially at length or in an exaggerated way
talk nonsense or untruths
informal slang

Examples

I wish you wouldn't spout bullpoo every time we have a serious discussion.
The politician continued to spout bullpoo despite the lack of evidence.
If you spout bullpoo during the interview, you won't get the job.

Synset Examples

He always spouts bullpoo during meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spout nonsense - to say things that do not make sense or are not true
  • Synonym: spout bullshit - to speak nonsense or lies, with a vulgar tone

Etymology

Bullpoo is a euphemistic variation of 'bullshit', a slang term for nonsense or lies.
