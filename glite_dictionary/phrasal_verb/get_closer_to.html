to get closer to

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈkloʊsər tu / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈkləʊsə tuː / (BrE)
to approach a goal, completion, or a desired result; to make progress toward something.
approach a goal or completion

Examples

After months of hard work, we are finally getting closer to launching the new website.
With every practice session, she felt she was getting closer to mastering the piano piece.
Are you getting closer to your fundraising goal this year?

Synset Examples

Are you getting any closer to finishing that project?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: approach - to come nearer in distance or time
  • Degree Higher: reach - to arrive at a particular point or goal
