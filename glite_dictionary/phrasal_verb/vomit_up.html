to vomit up

1. VERB

/ ˈvɑːmɪt ʌp / (AmE), / ˈvɒmɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to force the contents of the stomach out through the mouth; to throw up.
eject stomach contents through the mouth

Examples

He felt so ill that he had to vomit up his lunch.
The cat vomited up a hairball on the carpet.
After eating spoiled food, she began vomiting up everything in her stomach.

Synset Examples

After drinking too much, the students vomited
He purged continuously
The patient regurgitated the food we gave him last night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to throw up - to vomit; to eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth
  • Synonym: to regurgitate - to bring swallowed food back up into the mouth
  • Antonym: to retain - to keep something in the stomach; not to vomit
