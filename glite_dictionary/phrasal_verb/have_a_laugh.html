to have a laugh

1. VERB

/ hæv ə læf / (AmE), / hæv ə lɑːf / (BrE)
to enjoy an amusing or entertaining moment, often by laughing together with others
enjoy an amusing moment

Examples

We usually have a laugh over dinner on Fridays.
Whenever things get stressful, it's good to have a laugh with friends.
They decided to watch stand-up comedy just to have a laugh.

Synset Examples

We always have a laugh when we reminisce about college.
It's important to have a laugh every now and then.
Let's have a laugh and watch a comedy tonight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laugh - to make the spontaneous sounds and movements of the face and body that are the instinctive expressions of lively amusement
