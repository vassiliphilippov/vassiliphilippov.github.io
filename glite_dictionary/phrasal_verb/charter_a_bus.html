to charter a bus

1. VERB

/ ˈtʃɑɚtəɹ ə bʌs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːtə ə bʌs / (BrE)
to hire a bus for exclusive use, typically for a group or special event
hire a bus for a group or event

Examples

The school had to charter a bus for the trip.
We decided to charter a bus to take everyone to the wedding venue.
The company chartered a bus for the staff outing last weekend.

Synset Examples

The school had to charter a bus for the trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rent a bus - to pay to use a bus temporarily
  • Synonym: charter a plane - to hire a plane for private use

Etymology

From 'charter' meaning to hire or lease a vehicle, from Old French 'chartre' (a charter, contract).
