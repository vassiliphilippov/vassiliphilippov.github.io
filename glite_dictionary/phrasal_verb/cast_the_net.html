to cast the net

1. VERB

/ kæst ðə nɛt / (AmE), / kɑːst ðə nɛt / (BrE)
to throw a fishing net into the water to try to catch fish
throw a net to catch fish

Examples

The fishermen cast the net at dawn, hoping for a good catch.
When you cast the net near the rocks, you might get more fish.
They skillfully cast the net into the choppy water.

Synset Examples
