to sympathize with

1. VERB

/ ˈsɪmpəˌθaɪz wɪθ / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpəθaɪz wɪð / (BrE)
to share or understand the feelings, especially suffering or sorrow, of another person
share or understand someone's suffering or feelings

Examples

I sympathize with your situation and hope things improve soon.
Many people sympathize with the victims of the natural disaster.
She truly sympathized with him after he lost his job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: empathize with - to understand and share the feelings of another
  • Synonym: pity - feel sorrow or compassion for someone
  • Antonym: indifferent - having no particular interest or sympathy
