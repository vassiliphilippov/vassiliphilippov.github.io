to score off

1. VERB

/ skɔr ɔf / (AmE), / skɔːr ɒf / (BrE)
to gain an advantage over someone, especially by outdoing or defeating them in competition or argument
gain advantage over someone

The debate became heated as each candidate attempted to score off the other.
She enjoys scoring off her opponents with witty replies.
