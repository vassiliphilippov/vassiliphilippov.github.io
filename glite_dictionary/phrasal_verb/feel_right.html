to feel right

1. VERB

/ fil raɪt / (AmE), / fiːl raɪt / (BrE)
to seem appropriate, correct, or suitable in a particular situation
seem appropriate or correct

Examples

If the job doesn't feel right, it's okay to turn down the offer.
The decision didn't feel right at the time, but it worked out for the best.
Trust your instincts and do what feels right for you.

Synset Examples

Just do what feels right to you.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: feel wrong - to seem inappropriate or incorrect
