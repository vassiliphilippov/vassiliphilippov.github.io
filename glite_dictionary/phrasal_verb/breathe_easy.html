to breathe easy

1. VERB

/ briːð ˈiːzi / (AmE), / briːð ˈiːzi / (BrE)
to feel relaxed or relieved after a period of stress, worry, or uncertainty
feel relieved after stress or worry

Examples

You can breathe easy now that your test results are back and everything is fine.
Once the negotiations ended successfully, the team could finally breathe easy.
She didn't breathe easy until her kids arrived home safe.

Synset Examples

You can breathe easy; the project is approved.
Once the deadline passed, I could finally breathe easy.
After hearing the good news, he could finally breathe easy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to relax - to become less tense or anxious
  • Antonym: worry - to feel anxious or troubled
  • Antonym: to tense up - to become nervous or anxious
