to bowl out

1. VERB

/ boʊl aʊt / (AmE), / bəʊl aʊt / (BrE)
(in cricket) to dismiss all the batsmen of the opposing team by getting them out.
cricket: dismiss all batsmen

Examples

The team managed to bowl out their opponents for just 150 runs.
If we want to win this match, we have to bowl out the opposition quickly.
They struggled to bowl out the last batsman before lunchtime.
