to go dead

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ dɛd / (AmE), / ɡəʊ dɛd / (BrE)
to stop functioning, become silent, or cease activity suddenly
to become silent or stop working

Examples

The phone suddenly went dead in the middle of our conversation.
The room went dead after she made that awkward comment.
My flashlight went dead during the camping trip, leaving us in the dark.

Synset Examples

The discussion went dead when no one had anything to add.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fall silent - to become silent suddenly
  • Synonym: stop working - to cease to function
