to prioritize quality over quantity

1. VERB

/ praɪˈɔr.əˌtaɪz ˈkwɑ.lə.ti ˈoʊ.vɚ ˈkwɑn.tə.ti / (AmE), / praɪˈɒr.ɪ.tʌɪz ˈkwɒl.ɪ.ti ˈəʊ.və ˈkwɒn.tɪ.ti / (BrE)
to give greater importance to quality rather than the number or amount of something
emphasize quality over amount

If you prioritize quality over quantity, your work will stand out from the rest.
