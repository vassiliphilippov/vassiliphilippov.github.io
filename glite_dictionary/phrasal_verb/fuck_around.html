to fuck around

1. VERB

/ fʌk əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / fʌk əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to waste time or behave in a careless or irresponsible way
waste time or act irresponsibly (vulgar)
vulgar informal

Examples

Stop fucking around and get started on your homework right now.
We spent the afternoon fucking around at the park with nothing to do.
If you keep fucking around, you'll never get this project finished.

Synset Examples

She was furious when he decided to fuck around instead of helping.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to mess around - to behave in a way that is not serious or productive
  • Synonym: to fool around - to spend time doing things that are not useful or serious
