to lux up

1. VERB

/ lʌks ʌp / (AmE), / lʌks ʌp / (BrE)
to improve or embellish something by adding luxurious features or upgrades
make more luxurious
informal

Examples

Many hotels try to lux up their rooms with high-end amenities and designer decor.
After winning the lottery, he decided to lux up his wardrobe with some expensive suits.
They wanted to lux up the restaurant's atmosphere to attract a more upscale clientele.

Synset Examples

She luxed up her car with new leather seats.
