to talk about

1. VERB

/ tɑk əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / tɔːk əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to discuss or mention something in speech or conversation
discuss or mention

Examples

We need to talk about our travel plans before we book anything.
She never likes to talk about her past with anyone.
At dinner, they talked about the upcoming project and shared their ideas.

Synset Examples

they talked of many things

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to discuss - to talk about something with someone, especially formally and in detail
  • Synonym: mention - to refer to something briefly without going into detail
