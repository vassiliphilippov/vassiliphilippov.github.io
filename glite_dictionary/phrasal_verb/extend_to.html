to extend to

1. VERB

/ ɪkˈstɛnd tu / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnd tʊ / (BrE)
to reach out as far as a particular point or place; to continue up to a particular limit or boundary
to reach as far as a limit or boundary

Examples

The forest extends to the edge of the river.
His property extends to almost five acres.
The celebrations extended to every corner of the city.

Synset Examples

The sunlight reached the wall
Can he reach?
The chair must not touch the wall

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reach - to extend as far as; come to or arrive at a place or point
