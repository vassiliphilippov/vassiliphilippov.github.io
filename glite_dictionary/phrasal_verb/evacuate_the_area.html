to evacuate the area

1. VERB

/ ɪˈvæk.ju.eɪt ði ˈɛɹ.i.ə / (AmE), / ɪˈvæk.ju.eɪt ði ˈeə.ri.ə / (BrE)
to leave a particular place, especially because it is dangerous or on official orders, for safety reasons
leave a place for safety

Examples

Authorities instructed residents to evacuate the area due to the approaching wildfire.
The police used loudspeakers to urge everyone to evacuate the area after the bomb threat was reported.
All personnel were ordered to evacuate the area as a precaution during the chemical spill.

Synset Examples

Officials ordered everyone to evacuate the area immediately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to evacuate - leave a place, especially for safety
