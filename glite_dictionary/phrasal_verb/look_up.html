to look up

1. VERB

/ lʊk ʌp / (AmE), / lʊk ʌp / (BrE)
to try to find information about something, especially in a book, list, or online resource.
search for information

Examples

Whenever I encounter a new word, I look up its meaning in the dictionary.
He forgot the address, so he had to look up the directions online.
Students were instructed to look up important terms before the exam.

Synset Examples

You should consult the dictionary
refer to your notes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to consult - to seek advice or information from a source
  • Synonym: refer to - to turn to a source for information
