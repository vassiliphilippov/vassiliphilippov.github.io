to join the discussion

1. VERB

/ dʒɔɪn ðə dɪˈskʌʃən / (AmE), / dʒɔɪn ðə dɪˈskʌʃən / (BrE)
to take part in a conversation or exchange of ideas, especially one already in progress
participate in a conversation

Examples

She finally decided to join the discussion after listening quietly for several minutes.
Feel free to join the discussion by adding your comments below the article.
New members are encouraged to join the discussion at any time during the meeting.

Synset Examples

Don’t be such a chud; join the discussion!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: join the conversation - participate in a discussion or conversation
  • Synonym: enter the debate - begin to participate in a debate
