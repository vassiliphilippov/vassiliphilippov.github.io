to make sure

1. VERB

/ meɪk ʃʊr / (AmE), / meɪk ʃɔː / (BrE)
to take action in order to be certain that something happens or is true
act to ensure something

Examples

Please make sure you lock the door before you leave.
I always make sure to double-check my work for errors.
He called ahead to make sure the restaurant was open.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ensure - make certain that something will happen
  • Synonym: to double-check - to check something again for certainty

2. VERB

/ meɪk ʃʊr / (AmE), / meɪk ʃɔː / (BrE)
to ensure that something happens or is the case; to check or confirm something
ensure or confirm something

Examples

Please make sure all the lights are turned off before you leave.
She called ahead to make sure the restaurant was open.
He checked twice to make sure he had locked the door.
