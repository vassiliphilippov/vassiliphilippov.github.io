to play the accordion

1. VERB

/ pleɪ ði əˈkɔrdiən / (AmE), / pleɪ ði əˈkɔːdiən / (BrE)
to perform music by using an accordion as the instrument
perform music on an accordion

Examples

She can play the accordion beautifully at family gatherings.
After years of practice, he is now able to play the accordion with great skill.
Many street musicians play the accordion to entertain tourists in European cities.

Synset Examples

She learned to play the accordion during summer camp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: play the piano - perform music using a piano
  • Synonym: play the guitar - perform music using a guitar
