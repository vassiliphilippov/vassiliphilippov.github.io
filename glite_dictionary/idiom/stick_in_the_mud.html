stick in the mud

1. NOUN

/ stɪk ɪn ðə mʌd / (AmE), / stɪk ɪn ðə mʌd / (BrE)
a person who is dull, unadventurous, or resistant to change or new ideas
a person who resists change or excitement

Examples

Don't be such a stick in the mud; come and dance with us.
He's always been a stick in the mud when it comes to trying new foods.
My friends called me a stick in the mud because I didn't want to go skydiving.

Synset Examples

She called him a stick in the mud for not trying anything new.
He was a stick in the mud and refused to dance.
Don't be such a stick in the mud; join the fun!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuddy-duddy - a person who is old-fashioned and fussy
  • Antonym: party animal - a person who enjoys lively social activities
