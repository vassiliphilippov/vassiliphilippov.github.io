to scunge on the bill

1. VERB

/ skʌndʒ ɑn ðə bɪl / (AmE), / skʌndʒ ɒn ðə bɪl / (BrE)
to avoid paying one's share of a bill, especially in a selfish or sly manner
avoid paying one's share of a bill
informal

Examples

He always tries to scunge on the bill when we go out to eat together.
Don't even think about scunging on the bill this time—everyone needs to pay their share.
She accused him of scunging on the bill after he disappeared just before the check arrived.

Synset Examples

I can't believe you're scungeing on the bill again!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dine and dash - to leave a restaurant without paying
  • Antonym: to pay up - to pay what one owes
