to take pains

1. VERB

/ teɪk peɪnz / (AmE), / teɪk peɪnz / (BrE)
to make a great effort or take care in doing something
make a great effort

Examples

She always takes pains to make her guests feel comfortable.
The artist took pains to ensure every detail was perfect.
He takes pains with his work, never settling for less than his best.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to take care - to be cautious or make an effort to do something carefully
