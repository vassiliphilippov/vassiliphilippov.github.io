head and shoulders above

1. ADV

/ hɛd ənd ˈʃoʊldɚz əˈbʌv / (AmE), / hɛd ənd ˈʃəʊldəz əˈbʌv / (BrE)
significantly better than someone or something else; outstandingly superior
outstandingly superior to

Examples

Her artistic skills are head and shoulders above those of her peers.
This new model is head and shoulders above the competition in terms of performance.
The winner was head and shoulders above everyone else in the tournament.

Synset Examples

in intelligence he was head and shoulders above the others in his class

Related Terms

  • Synonym: superior - better than someone or something else
  • Antonym: inferior - lower in quality or status than
