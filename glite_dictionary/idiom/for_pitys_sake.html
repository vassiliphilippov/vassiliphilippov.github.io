for pity's sake

1. X

/ fər ˈpɪtiz seɪk / (AmE), / fə ˈpɪtiz seɪk / (BrE)
used to express exasperation, annoyance, or urgency.
expression of exasperation or urgency

Examples

For pity's sake, could you close the window? It's freezing in here.
Stop arguing, for pity's sake, and help me clean up this mess.
For pity's sake, listen to what I'm trying to tell you!

Synset Examples

Don't cry, for pity's sake, it will be fine.
Put that down, for pity's sake, it's dangerous!
Try to be more careful, for pity's sake!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for heaven's sake - expression of exasperation or urgency
  • Synonym: for goodness' sake - expression used to show frustration or urgency
