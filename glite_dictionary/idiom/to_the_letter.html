to the letter

1. ADV

/ tu ðə ˈlɛt̬ɚ / (AmE), / tə ðə ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
exactly as instructed or intended; in every detail, without deviation
in every detail, exactly

Examples

She followed the recipe to the letter and the cake turned out perfectly.
The inspector expects the safety rules to be observed to the letter.
He completed his boss's instructions to the letter, leaving nothing out.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to a T - exactly; precisely matching or suiting

2. ADV

/ tə ðə ˈlɛtɚ / (AmE), / tə ðə ˈletə / (BrE)
exactly as specified or instructed, without any deviation
exactly as instructed

Examples

You must follow the instructions to the letter for the best results.
The lawyer advised them to comply to the letter with the court's order.
She carried out her duties to the letter, leaving no room for error.

Synset Examples
