to make hair stand up

1. VERB

/ meɪk hɛr stænd ʌp / (AmE), / meɪk heə stænd ʌp / (BrE)
to cause a strong feeling of fear or surprise, often resulting in a tingling sensation as if one's hair is rising
provoke fear or surprise (idiom)

Examples

The horror movie was so scary it made my hair stand up.
Hearing a wolf howl in the dark forest made my hair stand up.
The loud crash behind me suddenly made my hair stand up.

Synset Examples

The static electricity made my hair stand up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: give someone goosebumps - to cause a sensation of fear or excitement that makes the skin form small bumps
  • Synonym: to chill to the bone - to cause someone to feel intense fear or cold
