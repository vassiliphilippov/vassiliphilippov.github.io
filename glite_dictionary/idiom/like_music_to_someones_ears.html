like music to someone's ears

1. X

/ laɪk ˈmjuːzɪk tə ˈsʌmwʌnz ɪrz / (AmE), / laɪk ˈmjuːzɪk tə ˈsʌmwənz ɪəz / (BrE)
used to describe something that is very pleasant or gratifying to hear, especially when it brings comfort or pleasure
very pleasant or gratifying to hear

Examples

Her promotion was like music to her ears after years of hard work.
The news that the project was finished on time was like music to their ears.
Hearing the baby's laughter every morning is like music to his ears.

Synset Examples

His praise was like music to her ears after the long project.
The sound of rain was music to my ears as I fell asleep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: music to one's ears - something very pleasing to hear
