level best

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛvəl bɛst / (AmE), / ˈlɛvəl best / (BrE)
the greatest effort one is capable of making; one's utmost effort
utmost effort; very best one can do

Examples

Despite being tired, she gave her level best to finish the marathon.
He always does his level best to help his friends succeed.
They tried their level best, but unfortunately, the project was cancelled.

Synset Examples

She gave her level best in the competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utmost - the greatest or highest degree possible
