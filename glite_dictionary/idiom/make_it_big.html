to make it big

1. VERB

/ ˈmeɪk ɪt bɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈmeɪk ɪt bɪɡ / (BrE)
to achieve great success, especially in a particular activity or profession
achieve great success

Examples

Many young actors move to Hollywood hoping to make it big in the film industry.
After years of hard work, the small bakery finally made it big and became a nationwide brand.
She dreamed of becoming a singer and making it big one day.

Synset Examples

That band really made it big after they played in Peoria.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to succeed - to achieve what you aimed for; to be successful
  • Antonym: to fail - to not achieve success in what you try to do
