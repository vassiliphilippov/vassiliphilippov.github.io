built like a brick shithouse

1. ADJ

/ bɪlt laɪk ə brɪk ˈʃɪthaʊs / (AmE), / bɪlt laɪk ə brɪk ˈʃɪthaʊs / (BrE)
(of a person, usually a man) having a very strong, muscular, and solid physique.
(idiom) very strong or muscular
vulgar

Examples

After years of weightlifting, he's built like a brick shithouse and impresses everyone at the gym.
That rugby player is built like a brick shithouse—no one wants to try tackling him.
You have to be built like a brick shithouse to work on a construction crew like that.

Synset Examples
