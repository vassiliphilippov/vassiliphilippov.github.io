nuts to butts

1. X

/ nʌts tə bʌts / (AmE), / nʌts tə bʌts / (BrE)
(informal) Packed very closely together, typically used to describe people or vehicles in close quarters.
packed closely together (informal)
informal vulgar

Examples

During the concert, we were standing nuts to butts near the stage.
The subway was so crowded it was nuts to butts all the way to my stop.
They packed the boxes in the truck nuts to butts to fit everything in.

Synset Examples

We drove nuts to butts on the crowded highway.
The bus was filled nuts to butts during rush hour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crammed - filled or packed tightly together
  • Synonym: jam-packed - extremely full or crowded
