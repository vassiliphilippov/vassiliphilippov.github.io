enough to go around

1. X

/ ɪˈnʌf tə ɡoʊ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ɪˈnʌf tə ɡəʊ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
sufficient for everyone in a group; adequate for all to have a share
sufficient for everyone

Examples

There is enough to go around, so everyone can have some.
The teacher made sure there were supplies enough to go around for the whole class.
We bought extra food to make sure there would be enough to go around at the party.

Synset Examples

There's plenty of cake enough to go around.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plenty to go around - a lot available for everyone; more than enough for all
