better luck next time

1. X

/ ˈbɛt̬ɚ lʌk nɛkst taɪm / (AmE), / ˈbɛtə lʌk nɛkst taɪm / (BrE)
used to express encouragement after someone fails, hoping they will have more success on the next attempt
expression of encouragement after failure

Examples

I'm sorry you didn't win the competition, but better luck next time.
She missed the last shot, but her coach smiled and said, 'better luck next time!'
It's tough to lose the game, but just remember—better luck next time.

Synset Examples

Great effort, but better luck next time!
You played well; better luck next time!
Sorry you lost; better luck next time!
