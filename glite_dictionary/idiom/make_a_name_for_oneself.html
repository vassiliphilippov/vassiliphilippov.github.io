to make a name for oneself

1. VERB

/ meɪk ə neɪm fər wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / meɪk ə neɪm fə wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to become well-known or achieve recognition, especially through one's own efforts
become well-known or recognized

Examples

Young chefs often try to make a name for themselves by creating unique dishes and bold flavors.
The company managed to make a name for itself by delivering high-quality products.
