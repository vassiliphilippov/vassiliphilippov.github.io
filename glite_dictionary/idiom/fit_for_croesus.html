fit for Croesus

1. ADJ

/ fɪt fɔr ˈkriːsəs / (AmE), / fɪt fə ˈkriːsəs / (BrE)
Extremely luxurious or expensive; suitable for someone extremely wealthy.
extremely luxurious or expensive

Examples

The dining room was decorated in a style fit for Croesus, with gold accents and expensive furniture.
She wore a necklace that looked fit for Croesus, dazzling everybody at the gala.
Their wedding was an affair fit for Croesus, with every luxury money could buy.

Synset Examples

He splurged on a car fit for Croesus himself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fit for a king - extremely luxurious or suitable for royalty

Etymology

References Croesus, a king of Lydia famed for his great wealth in ancient times.
