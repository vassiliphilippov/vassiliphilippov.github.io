to take one's breath away

1. VERB

/ teɪk wʌnz brɛθ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / teɪk wʌnz brɛθ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to astonish or impress someone so much that they are left speechless or in awe
astonish or impress greatly

Examples

The beauty of the ancient cathedral really took my breath away.
Her stunning performance takes your breath away every time you watch it.
The news of their unexpected victory took our breath away.

Synset Examples

The view from the mountain top took our breath away.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to astound - to surprise or shock someone very much
  • Synonym: to amaze - to fill someone with great surprise or wonder
