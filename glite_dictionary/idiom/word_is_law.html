word is law

1. X

/ wɝd ɪz lɑ / (AmE), / wɜːd ɪz lɔː / (BrE)
if someone's word is law, whatever they say is followed or obeyed without question or argument
(idiom) command that is always obeyed

Examples

In this household, Dad's word is law when it comes to bedtime.
As the head of the department, her word is law on all important decisions.
When the coach speaks, his word is law for the rest of the team.

Synset Examples

Among friends, her word is law for planning outings.
