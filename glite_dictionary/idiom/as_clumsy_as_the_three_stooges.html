as clumsy as the Three Stooges

1. ADJ

/ æz ˈklʌmzi æz ðə θri ˈstuːdʒɪz / (AmE), / æz ˈklʌmzi æz ðə θri ˈstuːdʒɪz / (BrE)
Extremely clumsy or awkward, especially in a way that is exaggerated or comic.
extremely clumsy or awkward (simile)
informal american

Examples

He dropped every dish—he was as clumsy as the Three Stooges at the party.
Whenever I try to dance, I feel as clumsy as the Three Stooges.
After tripping over the dog and knocking over a chair, she laughed and admitted she was as clumsy as the Three Stooges.

Synset Examples

He was as clumsy as the Three Stooges yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or handling things

Etymology

From the American comedy team 'The Three Stooges', known for their slapstick physical humor and clumsiness.
