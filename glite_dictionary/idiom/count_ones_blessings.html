to count one's blessings

1. VERB

/ kaʊnt wʌnz ˈblɛsɪŋz / (AmE), / kaʊnt wʌnz ˈblɛsɪŋz / (BrE)
to be grateful for the good things in one's life, especially when facing difficulties
be grateful for what one has

Examples

Whenever I feel discouraged, I try to count my blessings instead of focusing on problems.
Even though their house was small, they would count their blessings every day.
She realized she needed to count her blessings after hearing about her friend's difficulties.

Synset Examples

After losing my job, I learned to count my blessings.
In tough times, I try to count my blessings.
Every day, she reminds herself to count her blessings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to appreciate - to recognize the value of something or someone
  • Antonym: to take for granted - to fail to appreciate the value of something
