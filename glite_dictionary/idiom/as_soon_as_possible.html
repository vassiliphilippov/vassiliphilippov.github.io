as soon as possible

1. ADV

/ əz sun əz ˈpɑsəbl̩ / (AmE), / əz suːn əz ˈpɒsəbl̩ / (BrE)
at the earliest opportunity; without any unnecessary delay
at the earliest opportunity

Examples

Please submit your application as soon as possible to avoid any delays.
The doctor said I should come in as soon as possible for further tests.
Let me know as soon as possible if you are able to attend the meeting.

Synset Examples

Please send the report as soon as possible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immediately - without delay; at once
  • Antonym: eventually - in the end; after a period of time
