to do right by

1. VERB

/ du ˈraɪt baɪ / (AmE), / duː ˈraɪt baɪ / (BrE)
to treat someone in a fair, honest, or supportive way
treat fairly or support

Examples

Her main concern was to do right by her employees during the company restructuring.
He wanted to do right by his parents and pay back everything they had done for him.
I trust that you will do right by our agreement.

Synset Examples

You should do right by your friends and support them.
He always tries to do right by his family.
It's important to do right by your commitments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treat fairly - to act honestly or justly toward someone
  • Synonym: to do justice to - to treat or represent someone or something as deserved
