the cat's meow

1. NOUN

/ ðə kæts miˈaʊ / (AmE), / ðə kæts miˈaʊ / (BrE)
something regarded as outstandingly good, impressive, or admirable
something regarded as excellent or admirable

Examples

Her homemade chocolate cake was the cat's meow at the bake sale.
Ever since he got that job, he thinks he's the cat's meow.
This smartphone is the cat's meow of modern technology.

Synset Examples

That new cafe is the cat's meow in town.
He thinks his band is the cat's meow of the music scene.
Her new dress was the cat's meow at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the bee's knees - an excellent or outstanding person or thing

Etymology

American English, originating in the 1920s, possibly coined by cartoonist Thomas A. Dorgan.
