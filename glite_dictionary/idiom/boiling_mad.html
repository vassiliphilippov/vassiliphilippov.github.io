boiling mad

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɔɪlɪŋ ˈmæd / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪlɪŋ ˈmad / (BrE)
Extremely angry; furious.
extremely angry

Examples

She was boiling mad when she found her phone had been stolen.
After hearing the unfair decision, he became boiling mad and stormed out of the room.
The teacher was boiling mad when no one handed in their assignments on time.

Synset Examples

He got boiling mad after being cut off in traffic.
The manager was boiling mad at the missed deadline.
She was boiling mad when she found out the truth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: furious - very angry; enraged
  • Synonym: livid - furiously angry
  • Antonym: calm - not showing or feeling anger
