to rip your face off

1. VERB

/ rɪp jər feɪs ɔf / (AmE), / rɪp jɔː feɪs ɒf / (BrE)
(informal) Used as a threat of severe harm or retribution, typically expressing extreme anger or warning someone against a certain action.
threat of severe harm or retribution (idiomatic)
informal

Examples

If you touch my phone again, I'll rip your face off.
He was so angry, I thought he was going to rip your face off.
She threatened to rip his face off if he didn't apologize.

Synset Examples

I’ll rip your face off if you touch my stuff.
