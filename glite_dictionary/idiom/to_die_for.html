to die for

1. ADJ

/ tə ˈdaɪ fɔr / (AmE), / tə ˈdaɪ fɔː / (BrE)
Extremely desirable or appealing; so good that one would go to great lengths to have it.
extremely desirable or appealing
informal

Examples

The pasta at that new Italian restaurant is to die for—I've never tasted anything better.
She wore a dress that was absolutely to die for at the party last night.
Those vintage shoes are to die for, but they're way out of my price range.

Synset Examples

Her chocolate cake is absolutely to die for.
The view from the mountain is to die for.
His new cologne is really to die for.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irresistible - impossible to resist; extremely attractive or appealing
  • Antonym: unappealing - not attractive or desirable
