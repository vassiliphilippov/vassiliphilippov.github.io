in an eyeblink

1. ADV

/ ɪn ən ˈaɪˌblɪŋk / (AmE), / ɪn ən ˈaɪˌblɪŋk / (BrE)
so quickly or instantly that it happens in the brief moment it takes to blink one's eye
very quickly; instantly

Examples

The city skyline changed in an eyeblink after the new skyscraper was built.
The opportunity was gone in an eyeblink before he could react.
The solution appeared in an eyeblink, surprising everyone in the room.

Synset Examples

The change happened in an eyeblink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in a flash - very quickly; instantly
  • Synonym: in the blink of an eye - extremely quickly; in an instant
