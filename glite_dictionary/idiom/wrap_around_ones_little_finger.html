to wrap around one's little finger

1. VERB

/ ræp əˈraʊnd wʌnz ˈlɪt̬l̩ ˈfɪŋɡɚ / (AmE), / ræp əˈraʊnd wʌnz ˈlɪtl̩ ˈfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
to have complete control over someone and be able to make them do whatever one wants, often by persuasion or charm
easily control or manipulate someone

Examples

She knows how to wrap around her boss's little finger to get what she wants.
It was obvious he could wrap her around his little finger with just a compliment.
Over time, the charming politician managed to wrap the committee around his little finger.

Synset Examples

He managed to wrap the client around his little finger.
He can wrap her around his little finger with just a smile.
She knows how to wrap her boss around her little finger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: have someone eating out of one's hand - to have someone completely under one's influence or control
