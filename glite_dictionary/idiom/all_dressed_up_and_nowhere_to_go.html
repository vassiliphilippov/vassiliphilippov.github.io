all dressed up and nowhere to go

1. X

/ ɔl drɛst ʌp ənd ˈnoʊwɛr tu ɡoʊ / (AmE), / ɔːl drɛst ʌp ənd ˈnəʊweə tuː ɡəʊ / (BrE)
Wearing smart or formal clothes with no event or occasion to attend.
dressed up with no event to attend

Examples

After getting ready for the gala, he was all dressed up and nowhere to go when it was unexpectedly canceled.
She spent hours preparing, only to find herself all dressed up and nowhere to go after her date called off.
When the meeting was postponed, the team was all dressed up and nowhere to go.

Synset Examples

She felt all dressed up and nowhere to go at the party.
With the event canceled, she was all dressed up and nowhere to go.
He was all dressed up and nowhere to go after the cancellation.
