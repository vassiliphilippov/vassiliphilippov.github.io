monkey suit

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌŋki suːt / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki suːt / (BrE)
a formal suit or costume, often referred to in a humorous or mocking way
humorous term for a formal suit or costume

Examples

He complained about having to wear a monkey suit to the wedding.
For the performance, all the actors dressed up in monkey suits and funny hats.
She laughed when he showed up at her door in a monkey suit and oversized shoes.

Synset Examples

The actor donned a monkey suit for the role.
He wore a monkey suit for the costume party.
She joked about needing a monkey suit for the gala.
