to live a good life

1. VERB

/ lɪv ə ɡʊd laɪf / (AmE), / lɪv ə ɡʊd laɪf / (BrE)
to have a fulfilling, happy, and morally positive existence
have a fulfilling and happy existence

Examples

She hopes to live a good life and make a difference in her community.
Many people believe that to live a good life, you must stay true to your values.
After retiring, he focused on how to live a good life instead of just working hard.

Synset Examples

She always talks about living a good life in the countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: live well - to have a comfortable and enjoyable life
