to laugh my head off

1. VERB

/ læf maɪ hɛd ɔf / (AmE), / lɑːf maɪ hɛd ɒf / (BrE)
to laugh uncontrollably or very loudly and for a long time
laugh uncontrollably

Examples

I laughed my head off when I heard the joke.
She always laughs her head off at silly comedies.
We were laughing our heads off throughout the entire movie.

Synset Examples

I laughed my head off when I saw that meme.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to split one's sides - to laugh very hard
  • Synonym: to burst out laughing - to suddenly start laughing loudly
  • Antonym: cry - to shed tears, usually from sadness, the opposite of laughing
