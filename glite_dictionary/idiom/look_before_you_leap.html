to look before you leap

1. VERB

/ lʊk bɪˈfɔr ju lip / (AmE), / lʊk bɪˈfɔː ju liːp / (BrE)
think carefully about possible risks or consequences before taking action
consider before acting; exercise caution

Examples

You should look before you leap when starting a new business venture.
It's always best to look before you leap in important decisions.
Many regret their choices because they didn't look before they leap.

Synset Examples

Always look before you leap into new opportunities.
Remember to look before you leap when making decisions.
It's wise to look before you leap into investments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to think twice - consider carefully before acting
  • Antonym: to rush in - to act hastily without thinking
