price on someone's head

1. NOUN

/ praɪs ɑn sʌmwʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / praɪs ɒn sʌmwʌnz hɛd / (BrE)
a reward offered for capturing or killing a particular person, usually because they are wanted by authorities
reward for capturing or killing someone

Examples

The outlaw knew there was a price on his head and kept moving from town to town.
After stealing the treasure, she had a price on her head throughout the kingdom.
Rumors spread that the government had put a price on his head for his crimes.

Synset Examples

There’s a price on his head for betraying the gang.
The bounty hunter was looking for the price on his head.
