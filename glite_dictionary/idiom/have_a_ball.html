to have a ball

1. VERB

/ hæv ə bɔl / (AmE), / hæv ə bɔːl / (BrE)
to enjoy oneself very much; to have a lot of fun
enjoy oneself greatly
informal

Examples

We had a ball at the wedding last night and danced until midnight.
The kids are having a ball playing in the backyard.
You’ll have a ball on your trip to New York if you visit all the museums.

Synset Examples

We had a ball at the party and didn't come home until 2 AM

Related Terms

  • Synonym: have fun - to enjoy oneself or have a good time
  • Synonym: to have a blast - to have a very enjoyable or exciting time

2. VERB

/ hæv ə bɔl / (AmE), / hæv ə bɔːl / (BrE)

informal
