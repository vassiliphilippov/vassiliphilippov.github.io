on the point of

1. ADP

/ ɑn ðə pɔɪnt əv / (AmE), / ɒn ðə pɔɪnt əv / (BrE)
very close to doing something; about to do something soon
about to do something

Examples

She was on the point of tears when her friend comforted her.
Just as I was on the point of leaving, the phone rang.
The company was on the point of announcing the merger when the deal fell through.

Synset Examples

They were on the point of giving up when they succeeded.
She was on the point of crying after the news.
I was on the point of leaving when he arrived.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: about to - very close to doing something; almost ready to do it
  • Synonym: on the verge of - very close to experiencing or doing something
