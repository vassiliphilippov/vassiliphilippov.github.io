ad infinitum

1. ADV

/ æd ɪnˌfɪˈnaɪtəm / (AmE), / ad ɪnˌfɪˈniːtəm / (BrE)
(of something) endlessly; without limit or end.
endlessly; without limit

Examples

The debate continued ad infinitum without any resolution.
If we keep adding conditions, the process will go on ad infinitum.
She can repeat that story ad infinitum and never get bored.

Synset Examples

talked on and on ad infinitum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endlessly - in a way that has or seems to have no end
  • Synonym: forever - for all time; continually
  • Antonym: finite - having limits or bounds

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'to infinity'.
