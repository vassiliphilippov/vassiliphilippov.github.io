all double Dutch

1. NOUN

/ ɔl ˌdʌbəl ˈdʌtʃ / (AmE), / ɔːl ˌdʌbəl ˈdʌtʃ / (BrE)
language or speech that is completely incomprehensible or impossible to understand.
completely incomprehensible language

Examples

I tried to read the instructions, but it was all double Dutch to me.
Whenever my accountant talks about taxes, it sounds like all double Dutch.
The foreign movie had no subtitles, so the dialogue was all double Dutch for most viewers.

Synset Examples

When she spoke, it was all double Dutch to me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gibberish - language that is meaningless or unintelligible
  • Synonym: nonsense - spoken or written words that have no meaning or make no sense
  • Antonym: clear - easy to understand, not difficult to comprehend

Etymology

From the derogatory use of 'Dutch' in English slang, originally referring to the Dutch language as confusing or unintelligible to English speakers.
