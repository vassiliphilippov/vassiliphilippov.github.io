sharp as a tack

1. ADJ

/ ʃɑrp æz ə tæk / (AmE), / ʃɑːp æz ə tæk / (BrE)
very intelligent or mentally quick; extremely mentally acute or perceptive
very intelligent or quick-witted

Examples

Even in her nineties, Grandma is still sharp as a tack and remembers every birthday.
The new intern is sharp as a tack and picks up new skills very quickly.
Despite the complex puzzle, she remained sharp as a tack and solved it in minutes.

Synset Examples

His memory is sharp as a tack despite his age.
He’s not just smart; he’s sharp as a tack.
She's sharp as a tack when it comes to math.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quick-witted - able to think and respond quickly; intelligent
  • Antonym: slow-witted - slow to understand or perceive; not intelligent
