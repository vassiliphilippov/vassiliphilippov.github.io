reputation is everything

1. X

/ ˌrɛpjəˈteɪʃən ɪz ˈɛvriˌθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌrɛpjʊˈteɪʃən ɪz ˈɛvriːˌθɪŋ / (BrE)
used to emphasize that reputation is the most important factor in a particular context or generally in life
reputation is most important

Examples

In the hospitality industry, reputation is everything because customers rely on reviews and word of mouth.
For many politicians, reputation is everything, so they go to great lengths to protect it.
When it comes to luxury brands, reputation is everything and can make or break a business.

Synset Examples

In this bidness, reputation is everything.
