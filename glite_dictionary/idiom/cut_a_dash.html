to cut a dash

1. VERB

/ kʌt ə dæʃ / (AmE), / kʌt ə dæʃ / (BrE)
to make a striking impression, especially by a smart or stylish appearance
make a striking stylish impression

Examples

At the awards ceremony, she cut a dash with her sparkling gown and confident smile.
They certainly cut a dash when they entered the restaurant in designer clothes.
Even in casual settings, he manages to cut a dash with his sense of style.

Synset Examples

They cut a dash as they arrived in fancy cars.
She cut a dash at the gala with her elegant dress.
He always cuts a dash in his tailored suits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stand out - to be easily noticed or attract attention
  • Antonym: to blend in - to be similar to other people or things and not be easily noticeable
