fall seven times, stand up eight

1. X

/ fɔl ˈsɛvən taɪmz stænd ʌp eɪt / (AmE), / fɔːl ˈsɛvən taɪmz stænd ʌp eɪt / (BrE)
Used to express the idea that true perseverance means continuing to try and recover, no matter how many times you fail.
proverb about perseverance despite failure

Examples

Whenever life got tough, she reminded herself to fall seven times, stand up eight.
His journey to success was a classic case of fall seven times, stand up eight.
The coach told the team to remember to fall seven times, stand up eight after their recent losses.

Synset Examples

She embodies the saying, fall seven times, stand up eight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perseverance - continued effort to do or achieve something despite difficulties, failure, or opposition

Etymology

Calque of a Japanese proverb ‘Nanakorobi yaoki’ (七転び八起き), literally ‘fall seven times, get up eight’.
