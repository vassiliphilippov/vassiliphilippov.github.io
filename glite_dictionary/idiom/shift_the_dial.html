to shift the dial

1. VERB

/ ʃɪft ðə ˈdaɪəl / (AmE), / ʃɪft ðə ˈdaɪəl / (BrE)
to cause a significant change in a situation, process, or outcome, especially by making a noticeable impact
make a significant change

Examples

The company hopes its new app will shift the dial in the crowded marketplace.
Environmentalists are urging lawmakers to shift the dial on climate change policy.
It will take more than minor improvements to shift the dial in our favor.

Synset Examples

Innovative marketing strategies can shift the dial significantly.
We need to shift the dial towards sustainability.
They worked hard to shift the dial on public opinion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to move the needle - to have a noticeable effect or bring about measurable progress
