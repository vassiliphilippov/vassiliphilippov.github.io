to stop a clock

1. VERB

/ stɑp ə klɑk / (AmE), / stɒp ə klɒk / (BrE)
(of a person) to be so attractive or striking in appearance as to draw immediate attention.
be extremely attractive or impressive

Examples

She walked into the room and her beauty could stop a clock.
His stunning performance would stop a clock with its brilliance.
People say her smile could stop a clock and light up any occasion.

Synset Examples

He had a face that would stop a clock, truly striking.
With his charm and a face that would stop a clock, he drew attention.
