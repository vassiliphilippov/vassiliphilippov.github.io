like shit on toast

1. X

/ laɪk ʃɪt ɑn toʊst / (AmE), / laɪk ʃɪt ɒn təʊst / (BrE)
used to describe something that is extremely disappointing, unpleasant, or of very poor quality
extremely disappointing or bad (vulgar)
informal vulgar

Examples

The movie was like shit on toast—I couldn't believe how terrible it was.
His presentation came off like shit on toast compared to the others.
Honestly, the food tasted like shit on toast and I couldn't finish it.

Synset Examples

That performance was like shit on toast; totally disappointing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abysmal - extremely bad; appalling
  • Synonym: terrible - of very poor quality; extremely bad
