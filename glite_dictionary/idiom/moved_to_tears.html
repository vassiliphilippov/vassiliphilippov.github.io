moved to tears

1. ADJ

/ muːvd tə tɪrz / (AmE), / muːvd tə tɪəz / (BrE)
overcome with emotion and caused to cry
overcome by emotion, made to cry

Examples

She was moved to tears by the kindness of strangers.
The speech was so powerful it left us all moved to tears.
He often finds himself moved to tears by beautiful music.

Synset Examples

The heartfelt letter moved him to tears.
The passage in the book moved me to tears.
The movie moved her to tears at the end.
His farewell speech moved everyone to tears.
The oration moved many to tears during the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: touched - emotionally affected
