you smelt it, you dealt it

1. X

/ ju smɛlt ɪt ju dɛlt ɪt / (AmE), / ju smɛlt ɪt ju dɛlt ɪt / (BrE)
used humorously to say that the person who notices or complains about an unpleasant smell is probably the one responsible for causing it, especially with regard to flatulence
joke blaming accuser for a bad smell
informal

Examples

When the unpleasant odor wafted through the room, someone teased, 'you smelt it, you dealt it.'
He laughed and said, 'you smelt it, you dealt it,' turning the blame back on his friend.
In school, whenever someone complained about a bad smell, we'd all yell, 'you smelt it, you dealt it!'

Synset Examples

In this game, you smelt it, you dealt it.

If you smelt it, you dealt it; let's be honest.
