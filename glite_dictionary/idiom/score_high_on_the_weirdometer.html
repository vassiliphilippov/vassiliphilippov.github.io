to score high on the weirdometer

1. VERB

/ skɔr haɪ ɑn ðə ˈwɪɹdoʊˌmiːtɚ / (AmE), / skɔː haɪ ɒn ðə ˈwɪədəʊˌmiːtə / (BrE)
to be considered very strange or highly unusual, especially in a noticeable or remarkable way
be extremely strange or unusual
informal

Examples

That art installation really scored high on the weirdometer with its mix of neon lights and taxidermy.
Her behavior during the meeting scored high on the weirdometer and left everyone baffled.
The new performance piece scores high on the weirdometer, but it's also strangely captivating.

Synset Examples

This film definitely scores high on the weirdometer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bizarre - very strange or unusual
  • Antonym: normal - not strange or unusual
