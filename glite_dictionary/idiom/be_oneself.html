to be oneself

1. VERB

/ bi wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / bi wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to act in a natural and genuine way, without pretending or trying to fit someone else's expectations
act naturally, true to oneself

Examples

It's important to always be oneself, even when meeting new people.
She felt free to be oneself around her closest friends.
In stressful situations, remembering to be oneself can help reduce anxiety.

Synset Examples

In interviews, always try to be oneself.
It’s important to be oneself in any situation.
To truly succeed, you must learn to be oneself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be authentic - to be true to one's real character or feelings
  • Antonym: put on an act - to behave in an insincere or artificial way
