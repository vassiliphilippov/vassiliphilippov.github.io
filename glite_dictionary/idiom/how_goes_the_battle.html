how goes the battle

1. X

/ haʊ ɡoʊz ðə ˈbæt̬l̩ / (AmE), / haʊ ɡəʊz ðə ˈbætl / (BrE)
used to ask someone how their work, task, or situation is progressing, often in a friendly or informal way
inquiring about progress or situation
informal

Examples

Every morning, he greets his colleagues by saying, 'How goes the battle?'
Whenever I see my friend struggling with homework, I ask, 'How goes the battle?'
Sending a quick text, she wrote, 'How goes the battle with your moving plans?'

Synset Examples

How goes the battle with your current project?
I asked him how goes the battle at work.
She smiled and said, 'How goes the battle for you?'
