mad as a cut snake

1. ADJ

/ mæd æz ə kʌt sneɪk / (AmE), / mad az ə kʌt sneɪk / (BrE)
Extremely angry or upset; behaving in a wild or irrational manner due to anger.
extremely angry or upset (idiom)
informal british

Examples

She was mad as a cut snake when her car broke down on the highway.
After the argument with his neighbor, he stormed inside, mad as a cut snake.
When the referee made a bad call, the coach became mad as a cut snake.

Synset Examples

She was mad as a cut snake when her car broke down.
After losing his wallet, he was mad as a cut snake.
He became mad as a cut snake over the unfair treatment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: furious - extremely angry
  • Antonym: calm - not feeling or showing anger

Etymology

Australian English idiom, from the image of a snake becoming wildly agitated when injured.
