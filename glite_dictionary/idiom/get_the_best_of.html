to get the best of

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ðə bɛst ʌv / (AmE), / ɡɛt ðə bɛst ɒv / (BrE)
to defeat or overcome someone or something, often in a way that is not due to their fault or weakness
overcome or defeat someone or something

Examples

Despite his efforts, fatigue began to get the best of him during the race.
Sometimes, unexpected problems can get the best of even the most prepared teams.
She refuses to let her emotions get the best of her during negotiations.

Synset Examples

Heart disease can get the best of us

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defeat - to win a victory over; to beat in a contest
  • Synonym: to succumb - to fail to resist; to give way to a force
