to go to the polls

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ tə ðə poʊlz / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə ðə pəʊlz / (BrE)
to participate in an election by casting a vote
participate in an election

Examples

Millions of citizens will go to the polls next week to choose their new president.
Voters are encouraged to go to the polls despite the rainy weather.
She proudly went to the polls for the first time after becoming a citizen.

Synset Examples

It's time to go to the polls and make our voices heard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vote - to express one's choice or opinion in an election
