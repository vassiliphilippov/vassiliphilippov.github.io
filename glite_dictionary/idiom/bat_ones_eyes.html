to bat one's eyes

1. VERB

/ bæt wʌnz aɪz / (AmE), / bæt wʌnz aɪz / (BrE)
to flutter one's eyelashes rapidly, often in a playful or flirtatious manner
to flutter eyelashes flirtatiously

Examples

She batted her eyes and asked for a little more time to finish the project.
He batted his eyes innocently, pretending he didn't know what had happened.
Whenever she wanted something, she would bat her eyes at her parents.

Synset Examples

She batted her eyes and smiled sweetly.
She batted her eyes to get his attention.
He batted his eyes, trying to charm her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flutter one's eyelashes - to move eyelashes quickly up and down, often to attract attention
