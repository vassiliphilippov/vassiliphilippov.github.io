easy does it

1. X

/ ˈiːzi ˈdʌz ɪt / (AmE), / ˈiːzi ˈdʌz ɪt / (BrE)
used to advise someone to proceed carefully, gently, or slowly, especially to avoid mistakes or accidents
proceed carefully or slowly

Examples

Easy does it; there’s no need to hurry with those boxes.
When lowering the fragile vase, remember, easy does it.
Just a bit at a time—easy does it or you’ll spill the paint.

Synset Examples

Easy does it; we don't want to rush this project.
