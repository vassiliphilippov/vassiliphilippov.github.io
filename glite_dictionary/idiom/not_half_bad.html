not half bad

1. ADJ

/ nɑt hæf bæd / (AmE), / nɒt hɑːf bæd / (BrE)
(informal) Surprisingly good; better than one expected.
quite good; better than expected
informal

Examples

The new restaurant was not half bad, so I would definitely go again.
His singing voice is not half bad for someone with no training.
The weather turned out to be not half bad despite the gloomy forecast.

Synset Examples

His performance was not half bad for a beginner.
That dish you made was not half bad at all.
The movie was not half bad, surprisingly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pretty good - fairly good; better than average
  • Antonym: not great - not very good; unsatisfactory
