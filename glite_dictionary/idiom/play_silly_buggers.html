to play silly buggers

1. VERB

/ pleɪ ˈsɪli ˈbʌɡərz / (AmE), / pleɪ ˈsɪli ˈbʌɡəz / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish, annoying, or distracting way instead of taking things seriously
act in a foolish or distracting way
informal british

Examples

If you keep playing silly buggers, we'll never get the job done.
The teacher warned the class not to play silly buggers during the exam.
Instead of playing silly buggers, try to help your teammates.

Synset Examples

Stop playing silly buggers and focus on the task.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to mess about - behave in a silly or aimless way
  • Antonym: act seriously - to behave in a serious or responsible manner
