down the road

1. ADV

/ daʊn ðə ɹoʊd / (AmE), / daʊn ðə rəʊd / (BrE)
At a time in the future; eventually.
at a future point

Examples

We may have to update our software down the road as new requirements arise.
It's hard to predict what will happen five years down the road.
They plan to expand the business down the road if all goes well.

Synset Examples

There are challenges we will face down the road.
Let's go down the road and explore the new park.
We might consider moving down the road.
She suggested we look for jobs down the road, not across the street.
We’ll go down the road and find a good restaurant.
Let's revisit this issue down the road.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the future - At a later time than now
  • Synonym: down the line - At some point in the future
  • Degree Lower: soon - In a short time from now
  • Degree Higher: eventually - At some time later, after delays or a long wait
