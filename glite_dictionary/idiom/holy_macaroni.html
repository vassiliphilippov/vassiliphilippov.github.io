holy macaroni

1. INTJ

/ ˈhoʊli ˌmækəˈroʊni / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli ˌmækəˈrəʊni / (BrE)
used to express surprise, disbelief, or astonishment
expression of surprise or astonishment
informal

Examples

Holy macaroni! That was the best concert I've ever been to.
Holy macaroni, I never expected him to win the competition!
