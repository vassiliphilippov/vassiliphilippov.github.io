king shit of turd island

1. NOUN

/ kɪŋ ʃɪt ʌv tɝd ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / kɪŋ ʃɪt ɒv tɜːd ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
A derisive term for someone who regards themselves as very important or superior despite actually being insignificant or in a context that is unimpressive.
someone who thinks they're important but isn't
informal vulgar

He struts around the office like the king shit of turd island, but no one really cares what he thinks.
She called him the king shit of turd island after he bragged about his minor achievement.
Despite managing only a small team, he acts like the king shit of turd island.
