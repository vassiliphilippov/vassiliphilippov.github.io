to become one with

1. VERB

/ bɪˈkʌm wʌn wɪð / (AmE), / bɪˈkʌm wʌn wɪð / (BrE)
to achieve complete harmony or unity with something or someone, often in a spiritual or emotional sense
achieve harmony or unity with

Examples

Through meditation, he hoped to become one with nature and find inner peace.
She felt she could become one with the music while performing on stage.
After years of practice, the artist seemed to become one with his canvas.

Synset Examples

Yoga allows her to become one with her inner self.
