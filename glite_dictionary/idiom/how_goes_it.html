how goes it

1. X

/ haʊ ɡoʊz ɪt / (AmE), / haʊ ɡəʊz ɪt / (BrE)
(informal) used as a greeting to ask someone how they are or how things are going.
informal greeting or inquiry
informal

Examples

"How goes it?" he asked as he walked into the room.
"How goes it with your studies this semester?"
Whenever they meet, he greets her with a cheerful "how goes it?"

Synset Examples

It's been a while! How goes it?
Hey, how goes it with your new job?
I haven't seen you in ages; how goes it?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: how are you - common greeting asking about someone's wellbeing
