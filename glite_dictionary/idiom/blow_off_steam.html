to blow off steam

1. VERB

/ bloʊ ɑf stim / (AmE), / bləʊ ɒf stiːm / (BrE)
to do something that helps relieve stress or strong emotions
relieve stress or strong emotions

Examples

After the stressful meeting, she went for a long run to blow off steam.
Some people play video games to blow off steam after a tough day at work.
He joined his friends at the bar to blow off steam on Friday night.

Synset Examples
