dead on target

1. ADJ

/ dɛd ɑn ˈtɑrɡɪt / (AmE), / dɛd ɒn ˈtɑːɡɪt / (BrE)
placed, thrown, or directed with complete accuracy; exactly on the intended target
completely accurate; exactly on target

Examples

The goalkeeper's dive was perfect, but the shot was dead on target and scored a goal.
Her predictions about the market were dead on target, impressing her colleagues.
That throw was dead on target and knocked over every bottle on the shelf.

Synset Examples

his aim was true
he was dead on target

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dead on - exactly correct or accurate
