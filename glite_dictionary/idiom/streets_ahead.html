streets ahead

1. ADJ

/ stɹits əˈhɛd / (AmE), / stɹiːts əˈhɛd / (BrE)
considerably more advanced, superior, or better than someone or something else
far superior; much more advanced
british

Examples

When it comes to customer service, this company is streets ahead of its rivals.
Her scientific research is streets ahead of anything seen before in the field.
In smartphone technology, their latest release is streets ahead of previous models.

Synset Examples

Technologically, this phone model is streets ahead of others.
In terms of innovation, they are streets ahead of everyone else.
Her design ideas are streets ahead of the competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ahead of the pack - much better or more advanced than competitors
  • Antonym: lagging behind - falling far behind others in progress or achievement
