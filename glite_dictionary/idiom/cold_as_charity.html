cold as charity

1. ADJ

/ koʊld əz ˈtʃær.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / kəʊld əz ˈtʃær.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
emotionally distant, lacking in warmth or compassion
emotionally distant; unfeeling idiom

Examples

Her voice was cold as charity when she gave him the bad news.
He greeted his guests with a cold as charity smile that made everyone uncomfortable.
Although she helped, her manner was cold as charity, making it hard to feel grateful.

Synset Examples

His response was as cold as charity, lacking any warmth.
The food they offered was cold as charity and unappetizing.
The reception was cold as charity, making her feel unwelcome.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cold - lacking warmth or emotion
  • Antonym: warm - emotionally friendly or affectionate

Etymology

Refers to the idea that acts of charity may be given without warmth or personal connection. The phrase dates to the 18th–19th centuries.
