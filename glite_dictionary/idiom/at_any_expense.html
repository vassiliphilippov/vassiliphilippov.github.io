at any expense

1. ADV

/ ət ˈeni ɪkˈspɛns / (AmE), / ət ˈeni ɪkˈspɛns / (BrE)
no matter what the cost or sacrifice is; regardless of the price or effort required
regardless of the cost or sacrifice

Examples

She was determined to protect her children at any expense.
The company aimed to succeed in the market at any expense.
He insisted on defending his reputation at any expense.

Synset Examples

he wanted to save her life at all cost

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at all costs - no matter what cost; whatever must be done
