from a mile away

1. ADV

/ frəm ə maɪl əˈweɪ / (AmE), / frəm ə maɪl əˈweɪ / (BrE)
used to describe something that is easily seen, recognized, or detected, often from a great distance or with little effort
very easily detected or recognized

Examples

He could tell she was upset from a mile away.
You can spot a scam from a mile away if you know what to look for.
Her excitement about the trip was obvious from a mile away.

Synset Examples

He knew she was lying from a mile away.
Real fans can spot a fakefan from a mile away.
She could recognize her friend from a mile away.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: a mile off - very easy to notice or recognize
