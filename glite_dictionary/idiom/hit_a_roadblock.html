to hit a roadblock

1. VERB

/ hɪt ə ˈroʊdˌblɑk / (AmE), / hɪt ə ˈrəʊdblɒk / (BrE)
to experience an unexpected obstacle or problem that impedes progress
encounter an obstacle or problem

Examples

We hit a roadblock during negotiations and had to pause the discussion.
Every time she tries to move forward, she seems to hit a roadblock.
The development team hit a roadblock that delayed the release of the app.

Synset Examples

We hit a roadblock with the project's funding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: encounter an obstacle - to face something that blocks one's way or progress
  • Antonym: to overcome - to successfully deal with or gain control of an obstacle or problem
