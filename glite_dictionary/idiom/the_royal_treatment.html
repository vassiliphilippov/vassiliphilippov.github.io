the royal treatment

1. NOUN

/ ðə ˈrɔɪəl ˈtritmənt / (AmE), / ðə ˈrɔɪəl ˈtriːtmənt / (BrE)
special or lavish care and attention; exceptionally attentive or luxurious service
exceptional care or service

Examples

Whenever celebrities visit, the staff give them the royal treatment from arrival to departure.
After winning the award, she enjoyed the royal treatment at the exclusive spa.
His friends gave him the royal treatment on his birthday, complete with a limo and dinner at a fancy restaurant.

Synset Examples

They always give their VIP guests the royal treatment.
At the hotel, we were given the royal treatment.
She planned to give her mother the royal treatment for her birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pamper - to treat with excessive care or indulgence
  • Antonym: neglect - fail to care for properly
