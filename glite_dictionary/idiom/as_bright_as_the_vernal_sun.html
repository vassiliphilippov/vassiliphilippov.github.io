as bright as the vernal sun

1. ADJ

/ æz braɪt æz ðə ˈvɝnəl sʌn / (AmE), / az braɪt az ðə ˈvɜːnəl sʌn / (BrE)
extremely cheerful or happy; radiantly joyful, like the bright sun of spring
extremely cheerful or happy

Examples

She walked into the room looking as bright as the vernal sun, spreading happiness to everyone.
After hearing the good news, he felt as bright as the vernal sun all day long.
Her laughter was as bright as the vernal sun, filling the room with warmth and joy.

Synset Examples

Her mood was as bright as the vernal sun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as happy as a lark - very happy; extremely cheerful
