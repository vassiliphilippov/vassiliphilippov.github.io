pip pip

1. INTJ

/ pɪp pɪp / (AmE), / pɪp pɪp / (BrE)
an old-fashioned or informal expression used to say goodbye, especially in British English
informal old-fashioned farewell
informal archaic british

Examples

As the train pulled away, he waved and called out pip pip to his friends on the platform.
She smiled, tipped her hat, and with a jaunty pip pip disappeared into the crowd.
At the end of the meeting, the quirky professor ended with his usual pip pip before leaving.

Synset Examples

He waved goodbye with a cheerful pip pip.
She said pip pip before heading off on her trip.
They left the party with a friendly pip pip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - a standard expression used to say farewell
