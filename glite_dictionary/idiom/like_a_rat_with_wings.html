like a rat with wings

1. X

/ laɪk ə ræt wɪð wɪŋz / (AmE), / laɪk ə ræt wɪð wɪŋz / (BrE)
used humorously to describe someone or something that is very quick and agile, often in a slightly mischievous or surprising way.
humorous simile for being quick and agile

Examples

The cat darted through the garden like a rat with wings, chasing the leaves blown by the wind.
He zipped across the soccer field like a rat with wings, weaving around the defenders.
She snatched her bag and ran for the door like a rat with wings as soon as the bell rang.

Synset Examples

She moves swiftly, almost like a rat with wings.
