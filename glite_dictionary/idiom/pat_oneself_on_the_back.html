to pat oneself on the back

1. VERB

/ pæt wʌnˈsɛlf ɑn ðə bæk / (AmE), / pæt wʌnˈsɛlf ɒn ðə bæk / (BrE)
to praise or congratulate oneself, often excessively or with self-importance
praise or congratulate oneself

Examples

Instead of patting himself on the back, he thanked the team for their hard work.
You shouldn't spend too much time patting yourself on the back after a small success.
She was busy patting herself on the back for the outcome of the meeting.

Synset Examples

He really broke his arm patting himself on the back after that win.
She’s breaking her arm patting herself on the back for the project.
Stop breaking your arm patting yourself on the back, it was a team effort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: congratulate oneself - to express congratulation or pride about one's own achievement
  • Degree Higher: boast - to talk with excessive pride about one's achievements or possessions
