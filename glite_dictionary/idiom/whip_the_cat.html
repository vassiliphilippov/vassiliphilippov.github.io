to whip the cat

1. VERB

/ wɪp ðə kæt / (AmE), / wɪp ðə kæt / (BrE)
to engage in playful behavior or distract oneself through lighthearted indulgence
engage in playful or distracting activity

Examples

When she's feeling restless, she likes to whip the cat by baking cookies or trying a new hobby.
He tends to whip the cat when he wants to forget about work for a while.
Rather than dwelling on his problems, he decided to whip the cat with some playful jokes.
