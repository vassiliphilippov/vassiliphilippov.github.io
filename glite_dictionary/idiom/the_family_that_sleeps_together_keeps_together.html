the family that sleeps together keeps together

1. X

/ ðə ˈfæməli ðæt sliːps təˈɡɛðər kiːps təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / ðə ˈfæmɪli ðət sliːps təˈɡɛðə kiːps təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
A proverb meaning that families who spend quality time together, especially through shared routines or experiences such as sleeping or resting together, develop stronger bonds.
proverb about family unity through togetherness

Examples

During their annual camping trip, Sarah always remembered the saying: the family that sleeps together keeps together.
He repeated, 'the family that sleeps together keeps together,' as he tucked his children in for the night.
Some parents believe the family that sleeps together keeps together, choosing to co-sleep with their young children.

Synset Examples

Their close bond proves that the family that sleeps together keeps together.

They always cuddle during movies; the family that sleeps together keeps together.
