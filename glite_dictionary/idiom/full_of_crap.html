full of crap

1. ADJ

/ fʊl əv kræp / (AmE), / fʊl əv kræp / (BrE)
saying things that are nonsense or untrue; not telling the truth
saying nonsense or untruths
informal vulgar

Examples

He is always full of crap when he talks about how much money he makes.
The politician's promises sounded full of crap to the skeptical audience.
Don't believe her story—it's entirely full of crap.

Synset Examples

That movie was just full of crap and didn't make sense.
Her excuses were completely full of crap and unconvincing.
Don't listen to him; he's full of crap about his achievements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: full of it - used to describe someone who is not telling the truth or is exaggerating
  • Synonym: lie - a false statement made with the intention to deceive
  • Antonym: truthful - telling the truth; not lying or deceiving
