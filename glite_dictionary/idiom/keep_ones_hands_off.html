to keep one's hands off

1. VERB

/ kip wʌnz hændz ɔf / (AmE), / kiːp wʌnz hændz ɒf / (BrE)
to not touch or interfere with someone or something; to stay away from or avoid involvement
not touch or interfere with

Examples

Please keep your hands off my laptop while I'm gone.
The boss warned everyone to keep their hands off confidential files.
She told her brother to keep his hands off her birthday cake.

Synset Examples

Keep your hands off my wife!
Keep your distance from this man — he is dangerous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keep away from - to stay at a distance from; to avoid coming near
  • Synonym: stay clear of - to avoid or not become involved with
