to feel like a total porker

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə ˈtoʊt̬l ˈpɔrkər / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈtəʊtəl ˈpɔːkə / (BrE)
to feel as though one has overindulged in eating, or to feel overweight, often with a sense of self-criticism or humor
feel overly full or overweight, humorously
informal

Examples

After eating two slices of cake, I feel like a total porker.
Whenever I skip the gym for a week, I start to feel like a total porker.
She laughed and said she felt like a total porker after the holiday feast.

Synset Examples

During the holidays, I feel like a total porker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pig out - to eat large quantities of food, especially in a greedy or excessive way
