to go shit oneself

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ ʃɪt wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ʃɪt wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to be extremely shocked or startled, often to the point of losing control; used for strong, sudden surprise or fear
be extremely shocked or startled (vulgar)
informal vulgar

Examples

When the fireworks exploded right behind him, he practically went shit himself.
She would go shit herself if she saw her childhood idol walking into the room.
I nearly went shit myself when I got that unexpected phone call.

Synset Examples

When he heard the news, he almost went shit himself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go crap oneself - to be extremely shocked or startled (vulgar idiom)
  • Synonym: be flabbergasted - to be extremely surprised or astounded
