to play the angle

1. VERB

/ pleɪ ði ˈæŋɡəl / (AmE), / pleɪ ði ˈæŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
to cleverly manipulate a situation to one's own advantage
manipulate a situation for personal gain

Examples

She knows how to play the angle when negotiating business deals.
Some politicians will play the angle to gain more support from voters.
If you play the angle right, you could get a better price on the car.

Synset Examples

He always knows how to play the angles to get ahead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exploit - to use (a situation) for one's own advantage
  • Synonym: maneuver - to manipulate a situation to achieve a goal
