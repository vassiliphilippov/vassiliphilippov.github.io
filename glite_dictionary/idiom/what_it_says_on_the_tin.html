what it says on the tin

1. X

/ wʌt ɪt sɛz ɑn ðə tɪn / (AmE), / wɒt ɪt sɛz ɒn ðə tɪn / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is exactly as described or does exactly what was promised or advertised
exactly as described or promised
british

Examples

You don't have to worry about this cleaner; it does what it says on the tin.
The app is easy to use and really does what it says on the tin.
His explanation was simple—it was what it says on the tin, no surprises.

Synset Examples

This product will do exactly what it says on the tin.
The instructions do exactly what it says on the tin.
Trust me, it will do exactly what it says on the tin.

Etymology

Originated from a UK advertising slogan for Ronseal wood stain products in the 1990s: "Does exactly what it says on the tin."
