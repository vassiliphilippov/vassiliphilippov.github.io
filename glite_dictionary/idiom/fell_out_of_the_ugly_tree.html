fell out of the ugly tree

1. X

/ fɛl aʊt əv ði ˈʌɡli tri / (AmE), / fɛl aʊt əv ði ˈʌɡli triː / (BrE)
(informal) Used humorously to say that someone is very unattractive.
humorous idiom for being unattractive
informal

Examples

Many students joked that the new math teacher fell out of the ugly tree and hit every branch on the way down.
Some people are just plain, but he's so unfortunate, it's like he fell out of the ugly tree.
She laughed, saying her old school photo made her look like she'd fell out of the ugly tree.

Synset Examples

He looked like he fell out of the ugly tree!
