wouldn't you say

1. X

/ ˈwʊdənt ju ˈseɪ / (AmE), / ˈwʊdnt ju ˈseɪ / (BrE)
Used to ask for agreement or confirmation about a statement just made.
used to seek agreement

Examples

The movie was quite entertaining, wouldn't you say?
It's time we left, wouldn't you say?
He handled the situation well, wouldn't you say?

Synset Examples

It's a beautiful day, wouldn't you say?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: don't you think - used to ask for agreement or confirmation
