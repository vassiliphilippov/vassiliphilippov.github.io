to pave one's way

1. VERB

/ peɪv wʌn(z) weɪ / (AmE), / peɪv wʌn(z) weɪ / (BrE)
to create opportunities for oneself to achieve progress or success, especially in a career or journey
create opportunities for oneself to succeed

Examples

She worked hard to pave her way to a successful career in medicine.
Many young artists struggle to pave their way in the competitive world of music.
He took extra courses to pave his way for a promotion at work.

Synset Examples

She wanted to pave her way in the industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pave the way - to make progress or success possible for others
