on the brink of tears

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə brɪŋk əv tɪrz / (AmE), / ɒn ðə brɪŋk əv tɪəz / (BrE)
about to start crying; very close to tears.
almost crying

Examples

The boy looked on the brink of tears after his toy broke.
Hearing the sad news, she was on the brink of tears during the meeting.
He spoke so softly, as if he was on the brink of tears at any moment.

Synset Examples

She was on the brink of tears after the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: close to tears - about to cry; nearly crying
