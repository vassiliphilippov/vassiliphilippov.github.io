left holding the baby

1. X

/ lɛft ˈhoʊldɪŋ ðə ˈbeɪbi / (AmE), / lɛft ˈhəʊldɪŋ ðə ˈbeɪbi / (BrE)
having to deal with a problem or responsibility alone after others have left or avoided it
left with responsibility after others leave

Examples

When the project collapsed, she was left holding the baby while everyone else disappeared.
After the partners quit the venture, John was left holding the baby and had to sort out the mess.
Whenever there's extra work, it seems I'm always left holding the baby at the end.

Synset Examples

She felt abandoned, left holding the baby after the breakup.
