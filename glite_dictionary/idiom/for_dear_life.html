for dear life

1. ADV

/ fər dɪr laɪf / (AmE), / fə dɪə laɪf / (BrE)
with great urgency, desperation, or determination, as if one's life depended on it
with extreme urgency or desperation

Examples

She clung to the rail for dear life as the boat rocked in the storm.
He was running for dear life when he saw the angry dog.
I grabbed my phone for dear life as it nearly slipped from my hand.
