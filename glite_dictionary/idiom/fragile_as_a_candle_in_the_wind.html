fragile as a candle in the wind

1. ADJ

/ ˈfrædʒəl æz ə ˈkændl̩ ɪn ðə wɪnd / (AmE), / ˈfrædʒaɪl æz ə ˈkændl ɪn ðə wɪnd / (BrE)
extremely delicate or easily harmed; vulnerable to being broken, lost, or destroyed.
extremely delicate or vulnerable

Examples

After hearing the criticism, his self-esteem was fragile as a candle in the wind.
Their newfound peace was fragile as a candle in the wind, easily threatened by old arguments.
She felt fragile as a candle in the wind after the stressful week.

Synset Examples

Her confidence was as fragile as a candle in the wind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragile - easily broken or damaged
