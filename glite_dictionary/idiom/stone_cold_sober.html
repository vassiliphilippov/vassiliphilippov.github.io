stone cold sober

1. ADJ

/ stoʊn koʊld ˈsoʊbɚ / (AmE), / stəʊn kəʊld ˈsəʊbə / (BrE)
completely sober; not affected by alcohol or drugs at all
completely sober; not under influence

Examples

Even though everyone else was drinking, she remained stone cold sober throughout the night.
He was stone cold sober when he gave his statement to the police.
Despite the wild atmosphere at the bar, I was stone cold sober the entire time.

Synset Examples

She was stone cold sober at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; not drunk
  • Antonym: drunk - affected by alcohol
