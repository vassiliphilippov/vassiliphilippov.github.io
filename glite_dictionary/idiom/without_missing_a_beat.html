without missing a beat

1. ADV

/ wɪˈðaʊt ˈmɪsɪŋ ə bit / (AmE), / wɪˈðaʊt ˈmɪsɪŋ ə biːt / (BrE)
immediately and confidently, without any pause or hesitation
without pause or hesitation

Examples

When asked a difficult question, she answered without missing a beat.
He continued walking without missing a beat after stumbling on the curb.
The comedian handled the interruption without missing a beat, turning it into part of the joke.
