to talk out of one's ass

1. VERB

/ tɑk ˈaʊt əv wʌnz ˈæs / (AmE), / tɔːk ˈaʊt əv wʌnz ˈæs / (BrE)
to speak nonsense, especially about something one does not understand
speak nonsense without knowledge
informal vulgar

Examples

He always talks out of his ass when it comes to politics.
Don't talk out of your ass if you haven't read the report.
She realized halfway through her presentation that she was starting to talk out of her ass.

Synset Examples

He tends to talk out of his ass during debates.
Stop talking out of your ass and give us facts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talk rubbish - to speak nonsense
  • Antonym: talk sense - to speak logically or knowledgeably

2. VERB

/ tɑk aʊt ʌv wʌnz æs / (AmE), / tɔːk aʊt ɒv wʌnz ɑːs / (BrE)
to speak nonsense, or to speak as if knowledgeable about something when actually ignorant; to talk without knowing what one is talking about
to speak nonsense or without knowledge
informal vulgar

Examples

Stop talking out of your ass and admit you don't know the answer.
He tends to talk out of his ass when discussing things he hasn't researched.
During the meeting, she realized she was talking out of her ass and quickly changed the subject.

Synset Examples

She always talks out of her ass when she’s nervous.
She often talks through her hat when discussing politics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to talk through one's hat - to speak nonsense or without understanding
