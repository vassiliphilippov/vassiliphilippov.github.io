suck a big one

1. X

/ sʌk ə bɪɡ wʌn / (AmE), / sʌk ə bɪɡ wʌn / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) Used as an insulting or dismissive expression toward someone, often to show disdain or tell someone off.
insulting or dismissive retort (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar

Examples

After hearing the criticism, he just told them to suck a big one and walked out.
Some people online were rude, telling her to suck a big one when she disagreed.
If you don't like my music, you can suck a big one!

Synset Examples

After the loss, he felt like saying, 'suck a big one!'.
When he complained, I just said, 'suck a big one!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fuck off - an expression telling someone to go away angrily
  • Synonym: get lost - go away; leave me alone (informal)
