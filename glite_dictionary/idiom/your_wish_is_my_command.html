your wish is my command

1. X

/ jʊr wɪʃ ɪz maɪ kəˈmænd / (AmE), / jɔː wɪʃ ɪz maɪ kəˈmɑːnd / (BrE)
used to say that you are very willing to do what someone has asked you to do
expressing readiness to fulfill a request

Examples

Whenever you need help, just remember: your wish is my command.
He smiled and replied, "Your wish is my command, madam."
If you want me to bring you some tea, your wish is my command.

Synset Examples

If you want to go out, your wish is my command.

I'll help with the project; your wish is my command.
