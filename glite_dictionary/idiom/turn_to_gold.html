to turn to gold

1. VERB

/ tɝn tə ɡoʊld / (AmE), / tɜːn tə ɡəʊld / (BrE)
to become very successful or profitable, often with little effort; to lead to exceptional success or wealth
to become very successful or profitable

Examples

She has such business acumen that every venture she launches turns to gold.
It seems that whatever project he works on turns to gold in the end.
With her talent, every idea she touches turns to gold.

Synset Examples

In his career, it feels like everything one touches turns to gold.
Since he started investing, everything he touches turns to gold.
Her new business is thriving; everything she touches turns to gold.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to turn to dust - to fail completely or be destroyed
