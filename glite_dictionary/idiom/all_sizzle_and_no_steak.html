all sizzle and no steak

1. X

/ ɔl ˈsɪzəl ənd noʊ steɪk / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈsɪzəl ənd nəʊ steɪk / (BrE)
attractive or impressive in appearance but lacking real substance, value, or quality
appearance without real substance or value

Examples

The new smartphone ad campaign was all sizzle and no steak, promising features it couldn't deliver.
Many trendy diets are all sizzle and no steak, offering hype but no real health benefits.
Her speech looked impressive, but in reality, it was all sizzle and no steak.

Synset Examples

The movie was all sizzle and no steak.
His presentation was all sizzle and no steak.
Don't get fooled; it's all sizzle and no steak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: superficial - not thorough, deep, or complete; lacking depth
  • Synonym: all talk and no action - talking about doing something without ever actually doing it

Etymology

Originally American; derives from the idea that the sound of sizzling steak is appealing, but if there is no 'steak,' there's no substance.
