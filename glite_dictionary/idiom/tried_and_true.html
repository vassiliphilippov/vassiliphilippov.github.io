tried and true

1. ADJ

/ traɪd ənd tru / (AmE), / traɪd ənd truː / (BrE)
reliably proven by testing or experience; dependable because it has been shown to work well
proven reliable by use or testing

Examples

We used a tried and true recipe that has been passed down for generations.
The company relies on tried and true methods to ensure customer satisfaction.
She always goes back to her tried and true workout routine when training for a race.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reliable - consistently good in quality or performance; able to be trusted
  • Synonym: trustworthy - able to be relied on as honest or truthful
