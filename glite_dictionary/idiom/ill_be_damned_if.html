I'll be damned if

1. X

/ aɪl bi ˈdæmd ɪf / (AmE), / aɪl bi ˈdæmd ɪf / (BrE)
used to express strong refusal, defiance, or disbelief about something suggested or expected
expression of strong refusal or disbelief
informal

Examples

I'll be damned if I'm going to let them take credit for my work.
I'll be damned if that's the truth—there's no way he finished that quickly.
I'll be damned if I ever trust him again after what happened.

Synset Examples

I'll be damned if you think I'll give up.
