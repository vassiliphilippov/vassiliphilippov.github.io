motion of the ocean

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊ.ʃən əv ði ˈoʊ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.ʃən əv ði ˈəʊ.ʃən / (BrE)
used humorously to emphasize that skill, quality, or effectiveness is more important than size or appearance, particularly in intimate or competitive contexts
skill or quality matters more than size
informal
