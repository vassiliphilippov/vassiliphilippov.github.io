on track

1. ADJ

/ ɑn tɹæk / (AmE), / ɒn tɹæk / (BrE)
making progress as planned or expected; proceeding in the right direction to achieve a goal.
progressing as planned or expected

Examples

After revising the schedule, the team is on track to finish ahead of deadline.
If she keeps practicing every day, she'll stay on track for the championship.
Despite the challenges, our business plan remains on track.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: off track - not progressing as planned; deviating from the expected path
