worth every step

1. ADJ

/ wɝːθ ˈɛv.ri stɛp / (AmE), / wəːθ ˈɛv.ri stɛp / (BrE)
so valuable or rewarding that all the effort or difficulty is justified
fully justifies all effort taken

Examples

The view from the top was worth every step of the challenging climb.
She said the years of training were worth every step when she finally won the award.
Completing the marathon was tough, but it felt worth every step in the end.

Synset Examples

The hike to langit point was worth every step.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worth it - having value that justifies the effort or cost
