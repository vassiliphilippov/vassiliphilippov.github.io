too hot to hold

1. ADJ

/ tu hɑt tu hoʊld / (AmE), / tuː hɒt tuː həʊld / (BrE)
so popular or in such high demand that it is difficult to keep or manage
extremely popular; in high demand

Examples

The band's latest album is too hot to hold, selling out within hours of release.
Those limited edition sneakers were too hot to hold and disappeared from stores almost instantly.
Her new book became too hot to hold, topping bestseller lists around the world.

Synset Examples

The new smartphone is so popular, it's too hot to hold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in demand - sought after; wanted by many people
