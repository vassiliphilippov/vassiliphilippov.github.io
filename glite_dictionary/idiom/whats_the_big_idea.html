what's the big idea

1. X

/ wʌts ðə bɪɡ aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / wɒts ðə bɪɡ aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
used to ask why someone is doing something, especially when you are annoyed or surprised by it
used to question someone's motives, often with annoyance

Examples

What's the big idea waking me up so early on a Sunday?
He stormed into the room shouting, "What's the big idea?" when he saw the mess.
If you take her phone without asking, don't be surprised when she demands, "What's the big idea?"

Synset Examples

She raised her eyebrows and asked, what's the big idea?
