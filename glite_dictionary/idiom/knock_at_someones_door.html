to knock at someone's door

1. VERB

/ nɑk æt ˈsʌmwʌnz dɔr / (AmE), / nɒk ət ˈsʌmwənz dɔː / (BrE)
(of an opportunity or possibility) to become available or imminent; to present itself for consideration or action.
opportunity becoming available

Examples

A great business deal is knocking at your door, so don't miss it.
When opportunity knocks at her door, she always makes the most of it.
New chances may soon be knocking at your door—be ready to seize them.

Synset Examples

Success is knocking at your door; don’t ignore it.
