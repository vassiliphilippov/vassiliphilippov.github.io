to send shivers down someone's spine

1. VERB

/ sɛnd ˈʃɪvɚz daʊn ˈsʌmwʌnz spaɪn / (AmE), / sɛnd ˈʃɪvəz daʊn ˈsʌmwənz spaɪn / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel a sudden and intense sensation of fear, excitement, or thrill
cause a strong sensation of fear or excitement

Examples

The eerie music sent shivers down my spine as I walked through the empty house.
His powerful performance sent shivers down everyone’s spine during the final scene.
The announcement of the surprise twist sent shivers down her spine.

Synset Examples

Her ghost story sent shivers down everyone's spine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chill - a sudden feeling of coldness or fear
