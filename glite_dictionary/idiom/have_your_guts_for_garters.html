have your guts for garters

1. X

/ hæv jər ɡʌts fər ˈɡɑrtərz / (AmE), / hæv jɔː ˈɡʌts fə ˈɡɑːtəz / (BrE)
(informal, British) to threaten to punish someone very severely, often used humorously or as a warning.
threaten severe punishment (British idiom)
informal british

Examples

If you break his favorite mug, he'll have your guts for garters when he gets home.
She warned her little brother that Mum would have his guts for garters if he touched the cake again.
If the boss finds out about this mistake, he'll have our guts for garters.

Synset Examples

If he finds out, he'll have your guts for garters!
