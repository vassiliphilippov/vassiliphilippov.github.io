man among men

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæn əˌmʌŋ ˈmɛn / (AmE), / ˈmæn əˌmʌŋ ˈmɛn / (BrE)
a man who is greatly respected and admired by other men for his qualities, achievements, or character
exceptional, respected man

Examples

Through his courage and generosity, he became a man among men in the village.
Many consider Nelson Mandela to have been a man among men for his moral leadership.
He earned the title of man among men by always standing up for what is right.

Synset Examples

In his leadership, he proved to be a man among men.
Everyone respects him as a man among men in our community.
He truly is a man among men, always helping others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: role model - a person looked to by others as an example to be imitated
  • Synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group, organization, or country
