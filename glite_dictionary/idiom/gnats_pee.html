gnat's pee

1. NOUN

/ næts piː / (AmE), / næts piː / (BrE)
an issue, amount, or matter so trivial or insignificant as to be barely worth considering
something trivial or insignificant

Examples

After all that worry, the mistake turned out to be a mere gnat's pee.
In the grand scheme of things, your complaint is just a gnat's pee.
They argued for hours over a gnat's pee in the report.

Synset Examples

He realized he was fretting over a gnat's pee of an issue.
Don't worry about the gnat's pee; it's not worth the stress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trifle - something of little value or importance
  • Synonym: bagatelle - a thing of little importance or value
