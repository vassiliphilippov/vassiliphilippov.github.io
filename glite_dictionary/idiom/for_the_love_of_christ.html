for the love of Christ

1. X

/ fəɹ ðə lʌv əv kraɪst / (AmE), / fə ðə lʌv əv kraɪst / (BrE)
used to express strong feelings such as exasperation, disbelief, or to make an emphatic plea
expression of exasperation or strong plea

Examples

For the love of Christ, could you please turn down that music?
He begged them, for the love of Christ, to stop arguing and listen to him.
For the love of Christ, finish your homework before watching TV.

Synset Examples

She exclaimed, for the love of Christ, how could you do that?
I can't believe, for the love of Christ, you forgot my birthday!
For the love of Christ, please be quiet during the movie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for the love of God - used to express strong feelings or make an emphatic plea

Etymology

Refers to Christ in Christian tradition, inserting religious emphasis into the plea.
