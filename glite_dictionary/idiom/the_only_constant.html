the only constant

1. NOUN

/ ði oʊnli ˈkɑnstənt / (AmE), / ði ˈəʊnli ˈkɒnstənt / (BrE)
the one factor that does not change, especially when everything else does
the one unchanging factor

Examples

For many people, change is the only constant throughout their lives.
In the world of technology, innovation seems to be the only constant.
As she reflected on her career, she realized that adaptation had been the only constant.

Synset Examples

'Change is the only constant in life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: constant - something that remains unchanged
  • Antonym: variable - something that changes or can be changed
