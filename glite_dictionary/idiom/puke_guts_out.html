to puke guts out

1. VERB

/ pjuːk ɡʌts aʊt / (AmE), / pjuːk ɡʌts aʊt / (BrE)
to vomit with extreme force or in great quantity; to be violently sick
to vomit violently
informal

Examples

After eating the spoiled seafood, he thought he was going to puke guts out all night.
She felt so nauseous during the boat ride that she almost puked guts out over the side.
He was so hungover that he spent the morning puking guts out in the bathroom.

Synset Examples

Running a marathon made her feel like she would puke her guts out.
He was so sick he thought he would puke his guts out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vomit - to eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth
