that's the bunny

1. X

/ ðæts ðə ˈbʌni / (AmE), / ðæts ðə ˈbʌni / (BrE)
used to refer to the key point or main issue in a discussion or situation
the main issue; the key point

Examples

If you want to solve the problem, look for the root cause—that's the bunny.
After hours of discussion, she finally realized that's the bunny everyone was avoiding.
Listen to his last statement; that's the bunny in this whole debate.

Synset Examples

When it comes to this issue, that's the bunny.
If you want to win, just focus; that's the bunny.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: that's the point - the key issue; what matters most
  • Synonym: that's the crux - the critical point of an issue
