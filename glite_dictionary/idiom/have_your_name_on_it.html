to have your name on it

1. VERB

/ hæv jʊr neɪm ɑn ɪt / (AmE), / hæv jɔː neɪm ɒn ɪt / (BrE)
(of something) to be intended for, suited to, or destined for a particular person.
be intended or suited for someone

Examples

That promotion really has your name on it—you should apply for it.
This last piece of cake has your name on it, so go ahead and take it.
The award for best performance has your name on it this year.

Synset Examples

I know this opportunity has your name on it.
That trophy has your name on it; you earned it!
If the job is meant for you, it will have your name on it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be meant for - to be intended for someone
