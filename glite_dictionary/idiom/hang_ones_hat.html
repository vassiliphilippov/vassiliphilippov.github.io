to hang one's hat

1. VERB

/ hæŋ wʌnz hæt / (AmE), / hæŋ wʌnz hæt / (BrE)
to settle, establish oneself, or feel at home in a particular place
settle or feel at home someplace

Examples

After moving to the countryside, he finally found a place to hang his hat.
This town is as good as any to hang your hat and start fresh.
Many artists hang their hats in Paris, drawn by its vibrant culture.

Synset Examples

They finally found a spot to hang their hats.
This café is where I hang my hat.
He found a place to hang his hat in the city.
He decided to hang up his hat in a small town.
