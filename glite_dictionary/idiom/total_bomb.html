total bomb

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬əl bɑm / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl bɒm / (BrE)
A complete failure, especially in terms of financial results or public reception.
a complete failure (esp. financial/public)

Examples

The new software release was a total bomb with users and had to be quickly withdrawn.
Critics expected the play to succeed, but it turned out to be a total bomb.
Despite the star-studded cast, the film became a total bomb at the box office.

Synset Examples

The movie was a total bomb at the box office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flop - an utter failure, especially of a performance or product
  • Antonym: success - the achievement of an aim or purpose; the opposite of failure
