at pains

1. ADJ

/ ət ˈpeɪnz / (AmE), / ət ˈpeɪnz / (BrE)
making a great effort to do something, especially in order to avoid misunderstandings or mistakes
making a great effort

Examples

The manager was at pains to explain the new policy to the staff.
She was at pains to avoid offending her hosts at dinner.
He is always at pains to ensure that his work is accurate.

Synset Examples

He was at pains to ensure everyone understood.
They were at pains to be polite during the meeting.
She was at pains to clarify her statement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make an effort - to try hard to do something
