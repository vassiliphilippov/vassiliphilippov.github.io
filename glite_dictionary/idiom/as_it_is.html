as it is

1. ADV

/ æz ɪt ɪz / (AmE), / æz ɪt ɪz / (BrE)
used to refer to the actual situation, especially when it differs from what might have happened or been expected.
in the actual situation; contrary to expectation

Examples

We were hoping to finish the project early but, as it is, we'll just meet the deadline.
I would love to help you, but as it is, I have too much work to do already.
The weather could have been much worse; as it is, we only got a little rain.

Synset Examples

he might have been killed; as it is, he was severely injured
