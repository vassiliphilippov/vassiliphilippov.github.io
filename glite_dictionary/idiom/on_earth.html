on earth

1. ADV

/ ɑn ɜrθ / (AmE), / ɒn ɜːθ / (BrE)
used with question words to emphasize surprise, shock, or disbelief
used to express surprise or disbelief

Examples

Where on earth did you put my keys?
How on earth did he finish the project so quickly?
