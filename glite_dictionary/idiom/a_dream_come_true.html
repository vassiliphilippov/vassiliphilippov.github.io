a dream come true

1. NOUN

/ ə driːm kʌm truː / (AmE), / ə driːm kʌm truː / (BrE)
An event or achievement that fulfills one's deepest wishes or aspirations.
something that fulfills one's wishes

Examples

Winning the national championship was a dream come true for the team.
Her acceptance into the university felt like a dream come true.
For many people, owning a home is a dream come true.
