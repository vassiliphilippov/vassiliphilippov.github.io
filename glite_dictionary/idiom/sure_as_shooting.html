sure as shooting

1. ADJ

/ ʃʊr əz ˈʃuːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ʃɔː əz ˈʃuːtɪŋ / (BrE)
Absolutely certain; without any doubt.
absolutely certain

Examples

If you leave food uncovered outside, ants will find it sure as shooting.
He’ll forget his keys again, sure as shooting, just like he always does.
Sure as shooting, the sun was shining when we woke up.

Synset Examples

it is sure as shooting that they will come

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sure - confident, certain, without doubt

without any doubt; certainly
certainly; without any doubt (idiom)
informal

Examples

If you leave the window open, sure as shooting a bird will fly in.
He'll forget his keys again, sure as shooting.

Etymology
