sharp as a bowling ball

1. ADJ

/ ʃɑrp əz ə ˈboʊlɪŋ bɑl / (AmE), / ʃɑːp əz ə ˈbəʊlɪŋ bɔːl / (BrE)
(humorous, sarcastic) Very unintelligent or lacking in perceptiveness.
very unintelligent (humorous, sarcastic idiom)
informal

Examples

Everyone else quickly understood the instructions, but Tom was sharp as a bowling ball.
You don't have to be sharp as a bowling ball to see why that plan failed.
Linda joked that her brother is sharp as a bowling ball when it comes to remembering birthdays.

Synset Examples

When it comes to math, he's sharp as a bowling ball.
His jokes are sharp as a bowling ball, lacking any cleverness.
She was sharp as a bowling ball during the debate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: not the sharpest tool in the shed - not very intelligent; another humorous idiom
  • Antonym: sharp as a tack - very intelligent or perceptive; opposite meaning
