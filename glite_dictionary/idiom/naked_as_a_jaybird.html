naked as a jaybird

1. ADJ

/ ˈneɪkɪd əz ə ˈdʒeɪˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪd əz ə ˈdʒeɪˌbɜːd / (BrE)
(informal) Completely unclothed; having no clothes on at all.
completely unclothed; with no clothes on
informal

Examples

He ran out of the bathroom naked as a jaybird when he heard the fire alarm.
The toddler was naked as a jaybird after her bath, giggling all the while.
When we went skinny-dipping in the lake, we were naked as a jaybird under the moonlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naked - having no clothes on; unclothed

Etymology

American English, 20th century; origins uncertain but likely related to the fanciful idea that jaybirds have no feathers when hatched.
