to feel empty inside

1. VERB

/ fil ˈɛmpti ɪnˈsaɪd / (AmE), / fiːl ˈɛmpti ɪnˈsaɪd / (BrE)
to experience a deep sense of emotional emptiness, lack of fulfillment, or unhappiness
to feel emotionally empty or unfulfilled

Examples

After the breakup, she couldn't help but feel empty inside for months.
Winning the award didn't stop him from feeling empty inside every night.
Even with a crowd around him, he sometimes feels empty inside.

Synset Examples

Living a lie left him feeling empty inside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feel hollow - to feel emotionally hollow or lacking inside
  • Antonym: feel fulfilled - to feel content and satisfied with life
