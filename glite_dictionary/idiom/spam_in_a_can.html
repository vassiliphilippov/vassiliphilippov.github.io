spam in a can

1. NOUN

/ spæm ɪn ə kæn / (AmE), / spæm ɪn ə kan / (BrE)
something regarded as being of low quality, little substance, or poor value, especially in a dismissive or humorous way
something of low quality; not valuable
informal

Examples

Many critics dismissed the sequel as just spam in a can with no original ideas.
After the repairs, my phone felt like spam in a can compared to before.
She described the tourist souvenir as spam in a can—cheap and uninspired.

Synset Examples

The movie was just another spam in a can.
The project felt like spam in a can, lacking substance.
He joked that his old car was like spam in a can.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: junk - something of little value or poor quality
  • Synonym: trash - something regarded as worthless or of inferior quality

Etymology

Modelled on the name of the canned meat product 'Spam', implying something cheap and packaged.
