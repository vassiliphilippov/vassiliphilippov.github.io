moment for the ages

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊmənt fər ði ˈeɪdʒɪz / (AmE), / ˈməʊmənt fə ði ˈeɪdʒɪz / (BrE)
an occasion or event that is remarkable and likely to be remembered for a long time
memorable event or occasion

Examples

The team's victory in the final was truly a moment for the ages.
Her speech at the ceremony became a moment for the ages in the company's history.
When the underdog won the championship, it was hailed as a moment for the ages by fans and commentators.

Synset Examples

That performance was truly a moment for the ages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: historic moment - an event regarded as highly significant in history
  • Antonym: forgettable moment - an event of little importance or lasting impact
