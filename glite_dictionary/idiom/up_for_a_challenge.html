up for a challenge

1. ADJ

/ ʌp fər ə ˈtʃælɪndʒ / (AmE), / ʌp fə ə ˈtʃælɪndʒ / (BrE)
willing and ready to undertake a difficult or demanding task
willing to take on something difficult

Examples

She was up for a challenge when offered the chance to lead the project.
If you're up for a challenge, try hiking this mountain trail in one day.
They asked if I was up for a challenge, and I immediately agreed.

Synset Examples

He's always up for a challenge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: willing - ready or prepared to do something
  • Antonym: reluctant - unwilling or hesitant to do something
