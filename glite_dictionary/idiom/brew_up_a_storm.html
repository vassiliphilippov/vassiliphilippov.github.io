to brew up a storm

1. VERB

/ bruː ʌp ə stɔrm / (AmE), / bruː ʌp ə stɔːm / (BrE)
to create a great deal of excitement, activity, or commotion
cause excitement or commotion

Examples

The band's performance really brewed up a storm at the festival last night.
Their announcement on social media brewed up a storm of interest and comments.
The protests outside the building brewed up a storm that lasted for hours.

Synset Examples

They plan to brew up a storm for the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raise a ruckus - create a noisy disturbance or commotion
  • Synonym: to kick up a fuss - make a disturbance or show strong emotion about something
