top notch

1. ADJ

/ tɑp nɑtʃ / (AmE), / tɒp nɒtʃ / (BrE)
of the highest quality or standard; excellent
of the highest quality; excellent

Examples

The chef's creations are always top notch and attract customers from all over the city.
Their customer service is truly top notch, going above and beyond to help clients.
The conference featured several top notch speakers from around the world.

Synset Examples

Her performance in the play was top notch.
The restaurant received top notch reviews this year.
We only hire top notch professionals for our team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first-rate - of the best quality
  • Synonym: superior - better than average or than others
  • Antonym: mediocre - of only average quality; not very good
