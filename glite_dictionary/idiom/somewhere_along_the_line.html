somewhere along the line

1. ADV

/ ˈsʌmˌwɛr əˈlɔŋ ðə laɪn / (AmE), / ˈsʌmwɛːr əˈlɒŋ ðə laɪn / (BrE)
at some unspecified point during a process or series of events
at an unspecified stage in a process

Examples

Somewhere along the line, we must have taken a wrong turn because we ended up lost.
She decided to change careers somewhere along the line after having children.
Somewhere along the line, he developed a passion for photography.

Synset Examples

He realized somewhere along the line that he was wrong.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at some point - at an unspecified time or stage
