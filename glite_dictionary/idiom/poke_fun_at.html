to poke fun at

1. VERB

/ poʊk fʌn æt / (AmE), / pəʊk fʌn at / (BrE)
to make jokes about someone, often in a playful or mocking way
tease or mock someone humorously

Examples

The students would often poke fun at their teacher's unusual accent.
It's not kind to poke fun at someone's mistakes.
During the show, the host poked fun at several celebrities.

Synset Examples

He likes to poke fun at his friends during gatherings.
The media often pokes fun at Obiden's gaffes.
The comedian's jokes often poked fun at Strine culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to make fun of - to mock or tease someone
  • Synonym: ridicule - to mock or make fun of someone in a cruel way
