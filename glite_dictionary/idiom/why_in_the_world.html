why in the world

1. X

/ waɪ ɪn ðə wɝld / (AmE), / waɪ ɪn ðə wɜːld / (BrE)
used to express surprise, confusion, or disbelief about something
expression of disbelief or confusion

Examples

Why in the world did you bring an umbrella on such a sunny day?
Why in the world would they cancel the show without any explanation?
She asked, "Why in the world are you awake at this hour?"

Synset Examples

Why in the world would you say that?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: why on earth - expression of disbelief or confusion
