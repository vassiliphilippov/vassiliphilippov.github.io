made of money

1. ADJ

/ meɪd əv ˈmʌni / (AmE), / meɪd əv ˈmʌni / (BrE)
having a lot of money; very wealthy, often used to deny excessive requests for money
very wealthy; having a lot of money

Examples

My parents always say they're not made of money when I ask for expensive gifts.
Just because she buys designer clothes doesn't mean she's made of money.
Do you think I'm made of money? I can't pay for everyone!

Synset Examples

They think I’m made of money, always asking for loans.
He acts like he's made of money, spending freely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rich - having a lot of money
  • Antonym: poor - having little or no money
