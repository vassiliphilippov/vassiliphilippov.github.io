pink elephants

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋk ˈɛlɪfənts / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈɛlɪfənts / (BrE)
imaginary things, especially hallucinations, supposedly seen by someone who is extremely drunk
hallucinations seen under heavy drinking

Examples

After a weekend of nonstop partying, he started seeing pink elephants in his hotel room.
The old cartoon joked that drinking too much would make anyone see pink elephants.
She claims to have seen pink elephants, but everyone knew she had been hitting the bottle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hallucination - an experience involving the perception of something not present

Etymology

Alludes to the popular idea of seeing bizarre animals when intoxicated; recorded in American English from the early 20th century.
