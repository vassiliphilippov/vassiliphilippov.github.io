to know one's shit

1. VERB

/ noʊ wʌnz ʃɪt / (AmE), / nəʊ wʌnz ʃɪt / (BrE)
to be very knowledgeable or skilled in a particular subject or activity
be highly knowledgeable or skilled
informal vulgar

Examples

People listen to Lisa because she really knows her shit about computers.
If you want to impress the boss, make sure you know your shit.
During the interview, it was clear that he knew his shit.

Synset Examples
