to go commando

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ kəˈmændoʊ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ kəˈmændəʊ / (BrE)
to not wear any underwear under one's outer clothing
not wear underwear
informal slang

Examples

On a dare, he decided to go commando at the formal dinner.
Some people find it more comfortable to go commando in hot weather.
She realized she had to go commando after forgetting to pack enough underwear for the trip.

Synset Examples

On hot days, I often choose to go commando.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go without underwear - to not wear underwear

Etymology

Slang phrase, possibly originating from the notion that commandos do not wear underwear for practical reasons.
