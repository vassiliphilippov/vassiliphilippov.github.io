life of the celebration

1. NOUN

/ laɪf əv ðə ˌsɛl.əˈbreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / laɪf əv ðə ˌsɛl.ɪˈbreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the most lively and entertaining person at an event or gathering, who makes it especially enjoyable for others
most lively person at an event

Examples

She was truly the life of the celebration, keeping everyone laughing and dancing all night.
Whenever he's around, he's the life of the celebration and lifts everyone's spirits.
At the reunion, Aunt Clara was the life of the celebration with her stories and jokes.

Synset Examples

At the party, Gino was the life of the celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life of the party - the most lively and entertaining person at a party
