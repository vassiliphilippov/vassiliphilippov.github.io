in full gear

1. ADV

/ ɪn fʊl ɡɪr / (AmE), / ɪn fʊl ɡɪə / (BrE)
fully prepared and functioning; ready to operate at full capacity.
fully prepared; fully active

Examples

By noon, the festival was in full gear, with crowds filling the streets and music playing.
The emergency room was in full gear as patients arrived after the accident.
When the new CEO arrived, the office was in full gear preparing for his inspection.

Synset Examples

She was in full gear, preparing for her presentation.
They were in full gear for the upcoming competition.
The construction team was in full gear by sunrise.
