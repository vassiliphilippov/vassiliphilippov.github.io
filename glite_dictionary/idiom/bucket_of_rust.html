bucket of rust

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌkət əv rʌst / (AmE), / ˈbʌkɪt əv rʌst / (BrE)
an object, especially a vehicle or machine, that is very old, rusted, and in poor condition
very old, rusty object

Examples

My first car was a real bucket of rust, but I loved it anyway.
That old truck sitting in the field is nothing more than a bucket of rust.
After decades of neglect, the once-proud locomotive became just a bucket of rust.

Synset Examples

The shed is just a bucket of rust now.
That old bicycle is more like a bucket of rust.
After years outside, it turned into a bucket of rust.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wreck - an old, badly damaged vehicle or machine
  • Synonym: jalopy - an old, dilapidated automobile
