to go halfsies

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ ˈhæfsiːz / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈhɑːfsiːz / (BrE)
to share the cost of something equally between two people
share the cost equally (informal)
informal

Examples

If you want to save money, we can go halfsies on the subscription.
They always go halfsies when ordering food together.
Let’s go halfsies on a taxi to the airport.

Synset Examples

Let's go halfsies on that pizza.

If we go halves, it won't be so expensive.
Let's go halves on the dinner bill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go halves - to share the cost of something equally
