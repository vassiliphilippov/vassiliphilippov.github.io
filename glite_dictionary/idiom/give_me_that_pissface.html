give me that pissface

1. X

/ ɡɪv mi ðæt ˈpɪsˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ɡɪv miː ðæt ˈpɪsˌfeɪs / (BrE)
(informal) Used to express annoyance at someone's sulking or resentful facial expression, usually when the speaker feels the reaction is unreasonable or childish.
expressing annoyance at a sulky expression
informal vulgar

Examples

She rolled her eyes, and he snapped, 'Don't give me that pissface.'
Whenever he loses, he can't help but give me that pissface.
Teachers get tired when students give me that pissface over extra homework.

Synset Examples

Don't give me that pissface; just admit you were wrong.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sulk - to show displeasure by being silent or withdrawing
  • Synonym: scowl - to frown with displeasure or anger
