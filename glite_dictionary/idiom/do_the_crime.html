to do the crime

1. VERB

/ du ðə kraɪm / (AmE), / duː ðə kraɪm / (BrE)
to commit a crime or offense
commit a crime

Examples

If you do the crime, you must be prepared to face the consequences.
Many believe that those who do the crime should always serve time in prison.
Before you do the crime, think about how it might impact your future.

Synset Examples

The police agreed: he who said the rhyme did the crime.
When the evidence surfaced, it was clear: he who said the rhyme did the crime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commit a crime - to perform an act that is against the law
