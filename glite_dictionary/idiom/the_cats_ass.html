the cat's ass

1. NOUN

/ ðə kæts æs / (AmE), / ðə kæts ɑːs / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) A person who believes they are exceptionally important or impressive; someone with an inflated sense of self-importance.
person who thinks highly of themselves
slang vulgar

Examples

Ever since the promotion, Mark walks around like he's the cat's ass at work.
She really believes she's the cat's ass just because she got a fancy car.
Don't act like you're the cat's ass—everyone knows you're not that special.

Synset Examples

She strutted around like she was the cat's ass at the event.
He thinks he's the cat's ass because of his new car.
His arrogance makes him believe he's the cat's ass.

Related Terms
