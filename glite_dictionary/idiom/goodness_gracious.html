goodness gracious

1. INTJ

/ ˈɡʊdnɪs ˈɡreɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˈɡʊdnɪs ˈɡreɪʃəs / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express surprise, shock, or amazement
expression of surprise or astonishment

Examples

Goodness gracious, how did you manage to finish so quickly?
Goodness gracious, I didn't expect to see you here!
Goodness gracious, look at the size of that cake!

Synset Examples

When he won the lottery, he exclaimed, 'Goodness gracious!'
Goodness gracious, it's freezing outside!
Goodness gracious, I forgot my wallet at home!
