eternal triangle

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈtɜrnəl ˈtraɪæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ɪˈtɜːnəl ˈtrʌɪæŋɡl / (BrE)
a romantic relationship involving three people, typically marked by love and emotional complications among them
love affair involving three people

Examples

The classic novel tells the story of an eternal triangle filled with jealousy and betrayal.
Their complicated friendship eventually turned into an eternal triangle neither could escape.
Many soap operas center around at least one dramatic eternal triangle.

Synset Examples

He wrote a novel about an eternal triangle among friends.
