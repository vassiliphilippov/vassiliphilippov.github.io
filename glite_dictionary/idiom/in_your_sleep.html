in your sleep

1. ADV

/ ɪn jər slip / (AmE), / ɪn jɔː sliːp / (BrE)
used to say that someone can do something very easily, almost without effort or conscious thought
with great ease; effortlessly

Examples

She’s so skilled at playing the piano that she could perform this piece in your sleep.
After years of experience, he can do his job in your sleep.
This math problem is so simple—you could solve it in your sleep.

Synset Examples

This task is so easy, you could do it in your sleep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: with ease - without difficulty or effort
  • Synonym: without effort - requiring no effort; easily
