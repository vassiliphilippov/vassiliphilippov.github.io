to act like a putz

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə pʌts / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə pʌts / (BrE)
To behave in a foolish or inept manner.
behave foolishly or ineptly
informal slang

Examples

Please stop acting like a putz and start listening to everyone’s suggestions.
Every time he gets nervous, he starts acting like a putz in front of the boss.
You’ll never finish on time if you keep acting like a putz instead of focusing.

Synset Examples

Stop acting like a putz and take it seriously.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: act like a fool - behave in a foolish or silly way
  • Antonym: be sensible - act in a wise or reasonable way

Etymology

From 'putz', a slang term of Yiddish origin meaning a fool or an inept person.
