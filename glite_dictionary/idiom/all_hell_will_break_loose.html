all hell will break loose

1. X

/ ɔl hɛl wɪl breɪk lus / (AmE), / ɔːl hɛl wɪl breɪk luːs / (BrE)
used to say that there will be a sudden eruption of chaos, anger, or trouble; an uncontrollable and chaotic situation will occur
an outbreak of chaos or disorder

Examples

If they find out about the mistake, all hell will break loose in the office.
The moment the referee blew the whistle, all hell broke loose on the field.
Whenever someone mentions layoffs, all hell breaks loose at our company.

Synset Examples

If he doesn’t arrive soon, all hell will break loose at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to break loose - to become free or escape from restraint
  • Synonym: chaos - a state of complete disorder and confusion
