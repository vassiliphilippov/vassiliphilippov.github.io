trip to Xanadu

1. NOUN

/ trɪp tu ˈzænədu / (AmE), / trɪp tə ˈzænəduː / (BrE)
a journey or vacation to an imaginary place of great luxury and beauty; an idealized or fantastical getaway
idealized or luxurious vacation

Examples

After months of hard work, their cruise felt like a trip to Xanadu.
Winning the lottery let him take a trip to Xanadu he had always dreamed of.
She described her week at the resort as a trip to Xanadu.

Synset Examples

He described his vacation as a trip to Xanadu.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paradise - an ideal or perfect place
  • Synonym: utopia - an imagined place of perfection
  • Antonym: hell - a place of extreme suffering; opposite of paradise

Etymology

Xanadu refers to the summer capital of Kublai Khan’s empire, popularized in English by Samuel Taylor Coleridge’s poem 'Kubla Khan' (1816) as a symbol of opulence and splendor.
