to sleep on it

1. VERB

/ sliːp ɑn ɪt / (AmE), / sliːp ɒn ɪt / (BrE)
to delay making a decision until the next day so that you have time to think about it carefully
delay a decision until after thinking carefully

Examples

Let me sleep on it and I'll give you an answer tomorrow.
He decided to sleep on it before accepting the job offer.
Whenever I have a tough choice to make, I like to sleep on it.

Synset Examples

I’ll sleep on it before making my final decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to think over - to consider something carefully
  • Antonym: to make up one's mind - to decide after thinking about options
