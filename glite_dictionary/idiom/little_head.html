little head

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬.əl hɛd / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl hɛd / (BrE)
(informal) Used to refer to a man's sexual impulses or emotions guiding his decisions rather than reason or intellect.
decisions guided by sexual urges
informal

Examples

He's made too many mistakes by letting his little head do the thinking.
You need to stop listening to your little head and start using your brain.
When it comes to relationships, he follows his little head more than his heart.

Synset Examples

Stop thinking with your little head and consider the consequences.
He often thinks with his little head instead of the big one.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: big head - someone thinking rationally or intellectually (as opposed to their sexual impulses)
