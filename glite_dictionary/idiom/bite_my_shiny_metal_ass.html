bite my shiny metal ass

1. X

/ baɪt maɪ ˈʃaɪni ˈmɛtl ˈæs / (AmE), / baɪt maɪ ˈʃaɪni ˈmɛtl ˈɑːs / (BrE)
(humorous, defiant) Used to dismiss or ridicule someone, often in response to criticism or defeat; typically expresses irreverence or mockery.
humorous, defiant dismissal or retort
informal slang

Examples

When the boss criticized his work, he muttered, "bite my shiny metal ass," under his breath.
After losing the argument, she walked away and yelled, "bite my shiny metal ass!" at her opponent.
Fans of Futurama often quote Bender's catchphrase, "bite my shiny metal ass," to joke around.

Synset Examples

After losing the game, he just said, "Bite my shiny metal ass!"

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kiss my ass - an insolent or defiant retort

Etymology

Popularized by the animated TV series Futurama (1999), spoken by the character Bender.
