in heart of hearts

1. ADV

/ ɪn hɑrt əv hɑrts / (AmE), / ɪn hɑːt əv hɑːts / (BrE)
used to emphasize that one truly or sincerely believes or feels something, even if it is not openly admitted
truly or sincerely, deep down

Examples

In heart of hearts, she knew she would always return to her hometown.
He pretended to be confident, but in heart of hearts, he was still unsure.
In heart of hearts, we all want to be understood and accepted.

Synset Examples

She said, in her heart of hearts, she wanted to travel.
He believed, in his heart of hearts, that love conquers all.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deep down - used to mean at a deep, sincere, or underlying level
