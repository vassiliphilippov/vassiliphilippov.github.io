fresh out of options

1. ADJ

/ frɛʃ aʊt əv ˈɑpʃənz / (AmE), / frɛʃ aʊt əv ˈɒpʃənz / (BrE)
having no alternatives or choices left; with no other possible solutions available
with no alternatives left

Examples

After reviewing all their plans, they realized they were fresh out of options.
He felt fresh out of options and didn't know what else to try.
When her car broke down in the middle of nowhere, she was fresh out of options.

Synset Examples

They felt fresh out of options during the negotiation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of options - having no alternatives left
