until hell freezes over

1. SCONJ

/ ʌnˈtɪl hɛl ˈfriːzɪz ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɪl hɛl ˈfriːzɪz ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
for an extremely long time; used to say that something will never happen or will not happen for a very long time.
never; for a very long time

Examples

I will keep fighting for my rights until hell freezes over.
She said she wouldn't talk to him until hell freezes over.
You can wait for an apology until hell freezes over—it will never come.

Synset Examples

She said she’d never forgive him until hell freezes over.
I'll wait for him until hell freezes over.
He’ll keep trying to win her back until hell freezes over.
She’ll admit she was wrong when Hell freezes over.
He’ll apologize when Hell freezes over.
I’ll go skydiving when Hell freezes over.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: when hell freezes over - never; only if something impossible happens
