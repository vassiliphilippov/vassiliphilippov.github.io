lovely weather for ducks

1. X

/ ˈlʌvli ˈwɛðər fər dʌks / (AmE), / ˈlʌvli ˈwɛðə fə dʌks / (BrE)
used humorously to refer to wet, rainy weather that only ducks would enjoy
humorous way to describe rainy weather

Examples

After hours of rain, my dad declared it was lovely weather for ducks as we looked out the window.
Whenever it drizzles all day, people jokingly say it's lovely weather for ducks.
She grabbed her umbrella and laughed, 'It's lovely weather for ducks out there!'

Synset Examples

The children splashed in puddles, declaring it lovely weather for ducks.
