one of a kind

1. ADJ

/ ˌwʌn əv ə ˈkaɪnd / (AmE), / ˌwʌn əv ə ˈkaɪnd / (BrE)
unique; the only one of its kind; unlike anything else.
unique; unlike anything else

Examples

The artist created a one of a kind sculpture for the gallery.
Her sense of humor is truly one of a kind and makes everyone smile.
This dress is one of a kind, so nobody else will have it at the party.
