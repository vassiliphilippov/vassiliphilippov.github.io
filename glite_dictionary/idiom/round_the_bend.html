round the bend

1. ADJ

/ raʊnd ðə bɛnd / (AmE), / raʊnd ðə bɛnd / (BrE)
(informal) Crazy or mentally unstable; not thinking or behaving normally.
crazy; mentally unstable (informal idiom)
informal

Examples

Everyone thought he was round the bend after he quit his job to live in the woods.
That loud noise nearly drove me round the bend last night.
If you keep doing that, you’ll send me round the bend for sure.

Synset Examples

it used to drive my husband balmy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged or behaving in an unusual way
  • Antonym: sane - mentally sound; not crazy
