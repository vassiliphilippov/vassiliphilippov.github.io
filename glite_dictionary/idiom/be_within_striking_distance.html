to be within striking distance

1. VERB

/ bi wɪˈðɪn ˈstraɪkɪŋ ˈdɪstəns / (AmE), / bi wɪˈðɪn ˈstraɪkɪŋ ˈdɪstəns / (BrE)
to be close enough to achieve or obtain something soon or easily
close enough to achieve or reach

Examples

After months of hard work, the team is within striking distance of the title.
With only two exams left, she feels within striking distance of graduation.
Sales figures show that the company is within striking distance of beating last year's record.

Synset Examples

She felt the championship was within striking distance this year.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: be out of reach - impossible to achieve or obtain
