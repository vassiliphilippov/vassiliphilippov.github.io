to the backbone

1. ADV

/ tə ðə ˈbækˌboʊn / (AmE), / tə ðə ˈbækbəʊn / (BrE)
with complete firmness or deep commitment; thoroughly and unwaveringly.
thoroughly; with firm commitment

Examples

She was loyal to the backbone and never wavered in her support.
He opposes injustice to the backbone, no matter what it costs him.
Their patriotism was to the backbone, even during difficult times.

Synset Examples

He believed in the mission to the backbone.
Her commitment to the project was to the backbone.
The team supported each other to the backbone during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: through and through - completely; in every respect
