Godzilla-like fury

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑdˈzɪl.ə laɪk ˈfjʊr.i / (AmE), / ɡɒdˈzɪl.ə laɪk ˈfjʊə.ri / (BrE)
A rage that is extremely intense, violent, and destructive, likened to the havoc caused by the fictional monster Godzilla.
extremely intense, destructive rage

Examples

When criticized unfairly, his Godzilla-like fury shocked everyone in the meeting.
Residents feared that the mayor's Godzilla-like fury would lead to drastic changes in the city.
After months of frustration, she finally unleashed her Godzilla-like fury at the press conference.

Synset Examples

Her temper can unleash a Godzilla-like fury.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rage - violent and uncontrolled anger
  • Synonym: wrath - strong vengeful anger or indignation
  • Antonym: calm - absence of anger or agitation

Etymology

Godzilla is a famous Japanese monster film character known for catastrophic destruction; 'fury' refers to violent anger.
