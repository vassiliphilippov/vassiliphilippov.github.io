chances are

1. X

/ ˈtʃænsɪz ɑr / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːnsɪz ɑː / (BrE)
used to say that something is likely or probable
it is likely; probably

Examples

Chances are she'll call you again tomorrow.
If you leave now, chances are you'll get there on time.
Chances are the movie will be sold out by the afternoon.

Synset Examples

If you study hard, chances are you'll pass.
Chances are they’ll arrive late again.
Chances are it'll rain tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: probably - used to mean that something is likely to happen or be true
  • Antonym: unlikely - not probable; not likely to happen
