to lose one's cookies

1. VERB

/ luːz wʌnz ˈkʊkiz / (AmE), / luːz wʌnz ˈkʊkiz / (BrE)
to become very upset or frightened, often in response to a stressful or shocking situation
become very upset or frightened
informal

Examples

I thought I would lose my cookies during the scary movie.
He lost his cookies when he found out he was fired.
When the deadline was moved up, she almost lost her cookies.
