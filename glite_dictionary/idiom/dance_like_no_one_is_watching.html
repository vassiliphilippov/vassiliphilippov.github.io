to dance like no one is watching

1. VERB

/ dæns laɪk noʊ wʌn ɪz ˈwɑtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / dɑːns laɪk nəʊ wʌn ɪz ˈwɒtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
to dance freely and confidently without worrying about being observed or judged by others
dance freely without self-consciousness

Examples

Whenever she hears her favorite song, she just dance like no one is watching and lets herself go.
He encouraged the crowd to dance like no one is watching at the festival.
Sometimes you need to forget your worries and simply dance like no one is watching.

Synset Examples

He can dance like no one is watching.
