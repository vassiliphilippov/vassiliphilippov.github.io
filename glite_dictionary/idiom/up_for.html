up for

1. ADJ

/ ʌp fər / (AmE), / ʌp fə / (BrE)
willing to take part in something or being considered as a candidate for a position or opportunity
willing or considered for something

Examples

Are you up for a movie night later this week?
Three candidates are currently up for the position of manager.
He wasn’t feeling up for a long hike after a busy day at work.

Synset Examples

I'm up for dinner anytime this week.
She's up for a promotion this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the running - being considered as a candidate for something
  • Antonym: not interested - not willing to participate in something
