played out

1. ADJ

/ pleɪd aʊt / (AmE), / pleɪd aʊt / (BrE)
No longer effective, fresh, or usable because of having been used too much; worn out.
worn out from overuse

Examples

The team's strategy felt played out after several seasons without change.
That joke is played out and doesn't make anyone laugh anymore.
The old furniture was completely played out and needed replacing.

Synset Examples

a played out deck of cards

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worn out - damaged or no longer effective from use
