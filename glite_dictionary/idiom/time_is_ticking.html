time is ticking

1. X

/ taɪm ɪz ˈtɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / taɪm ɪz ˈtɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
used to express that time is passing, especially with a sense of urgency or a deadline approaching
time is passing, deadline approaching (idiom)

Examples

We need to finish this assignment quickly because time is ticking.
With the deadline approaching, everyone felt that time is ticking.
Remember, time is ticking, so make your decision soon.

Synset Examples

We should hurry; time is ticking on our deadline.
As the exam approaches, time is ticking faster.
He realized that time is ticking for his big presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to run out of time - to have no time left to complete something
