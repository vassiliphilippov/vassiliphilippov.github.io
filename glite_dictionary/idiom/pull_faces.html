to pull faces

1. VERB

/ pʊl ˈfeɪsɪz / (AmE), / pʊl ˈfeɪsɪz / (BrE)
to make funny or exaggerated expressions with one's face, often to amuse or annoy others
make funny facial expressions

Examples

During the school play, several children began to pull faces at the audience.
She tried not to pull faces while getting her picture taken, but couldn't help herself.
Whenever he's bored, he tends to pull faces to entertain his friends.

Synset Examples

The kids love to pull faces during family photos.
They often pull faces at each other when bored.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make faces - to make silly or exaggerated facial expressions
