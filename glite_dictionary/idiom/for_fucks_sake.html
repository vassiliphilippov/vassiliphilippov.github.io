for fuck's sake

1. INTJ

/ fər fʌks seɪk / (AmE), / fə fʌks seɪk / (BrE)
used to express strong frustration, annoyance, or impatience; considered very vulgar.
vulgar exclamation of frustration or annoyance
vulgar

Examples

For fuck's sake, just listen to what I'm saying for once.
She was late again, and I thought, 'for fuck's sake, not again!'
Can someone for fuck's sake fix this computer before I lose my mind?

Synset Examples

For fuck's sake, can you stop making that noise?

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: for heaven's sake - used to express annoyance or emphasis (milder form)
  • Degree Lower: for Christ's sake - used to express annoyance, frustration, or emphasis (less vulgar)
