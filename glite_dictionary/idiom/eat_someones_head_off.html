to eat someone's head off

1. VERB

/ it ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd ɑf / (AmE), / iːt ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd ɒf / (BrE)
to react with extreme anger toward someone, often by shouting or criticizing harshly
scold or criticize angrily

Examples

If you come home late again, Mom will eat your head off.
He tends to eat people's heads off when he's stressed at work.
She ate my head off just because I forgot to call her back.

Synset Examples

He was ready to eat my head off after a long day.
She’ll eat your head off if you interrupt her.
Don’t ask her for help now; she’ll eat your head off.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bite someone's head off - to speak to someone angrily for no good reason
