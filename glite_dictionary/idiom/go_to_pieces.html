to go to pieces

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ tə ˈpiːsɪz / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə ˈpiːsɪz / (BrE)
to lose emotional or mental control, often as a result of shock or distress.
lose emotional or mental control

Examples

After hearing the bad news, she went to pieces in front of everyone.
He goes to pieces whenever he has to speak in public.
It was clear he was going to pieces after the failed exam results.

Synset Examples

She fell apart when her only child died

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fall apart - to lose emotional control or become unable to function due to distress
