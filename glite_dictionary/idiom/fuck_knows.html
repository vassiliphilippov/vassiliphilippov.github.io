fuck knows

1. X

/ fʌk noʊz / (AmE), / fʌk nəʊz / (BrE)
(vulgar, informal) Used to emphasize that you do not know something and have no idea about it.
used for strong emphasis of not knowing
informal vulgar

Examples

Fuck knows where my keys are; I've searched everywhere.
"Is she coming to the party tonight?" "Fuck knows, she hasn't replied to my texts."
Nobody seemed to understand the instructions—fuck knows what we're supposed to do.

Synset Examples

Where he went? Fuck knows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: who knows - used to express uncertainty or an unknown answer
