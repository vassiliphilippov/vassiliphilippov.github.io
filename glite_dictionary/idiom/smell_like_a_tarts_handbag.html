to smell like a tart's handbag

1. VERB

/ smɛl laɪk ə tɑrts ˈhændˌbæɡ / (AmE), / smɛl laɪk ə tɑːts ˈhændˌbæɡ / (BrE)
(of a place or object) to have a strong, unpleasant, and often artificial odor, often reminiscent of heavy perfume mixed with mustiness.
have an overpowering, unpleasant smell
informal british

Examples

That old bottle of perfume smells like a tart's handbag and gives me a headache.
After weeks in the damp closet, the coat smelled like a tart's handbag.
If your living room smells like a tart's handbag, you might want to air it out.

Synset Examples

The old perfume reminded her of a smell like a tart's handbag.
