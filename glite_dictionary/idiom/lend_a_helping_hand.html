to lend a helping hand

1. VERB

/ lɛnd ə ˈhɛlpɪŋ hænd / (AmE), / lɛnd ə ˈhɛlpɪŋ hænd / (BrE)
to offer assistance or support to someone
offer assistance or support

Examples

Whenever her neighbors are in trouble, she is quick to lend a helping hand.
Volunteers came together to lend a helping hand after the flood devastated the town.
He offered to lend a helping hand with the preparations for the party.

Synset Examples
