in the pay of

1. ADP

/ ɪn ðə peɪ ʌv / (AmE), / ɪn ðə peɪ ɒv / (BrE)
receiving regular payment or financial support from someone, especially secretly or in a way that implies questionable loyalty or influence
secretly receiving payment from someone

Examples

The senator was rumored to be in the pay of powerful business interests.
Several officials were found to be in the pay of the criminal syndicate.
He denied being in the pay of any foreign government.

Synset Examples

The journalist was accused of being in the pay of lobbyists.
They claim he's in the pay of foreign interests.
He's suspected of being in the pay of the company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on the payroll - employed by and receiving payment from an organization
