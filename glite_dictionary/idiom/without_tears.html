without tears

1. ADV

/ wɪˈðaʊt tɪrz / (AmE), / wɪˈðaʊt tɪəz / (BrE)
in a way that is free from difficulty, distress, or hardship; easily or smoothly
without difficulty or distress

Examples

The recipe was so simple that even a beginner could complete it without tears.
She managed to move to her new apartment without tears, thanks to her friends' help.
After hours of practice, he delivered the speech without tears.

Synset Examples

He passed the exam without tears this time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: effortless - achieved with little or no difficulty
  • Synonym: with ease - in an easy manner, without effort or difficulty
  • Antonym: with difficulty - in a manner that is not easy or straightforward
