a little bit of bread and no cheese

1. X

/ ə ˈlɪt̬l̩ bɪt əv ˈbrɛd ənd noʊ tʃiz / (AmE), / ə ˈlɪtl bɪt əv ˈbrɛd ənd nəʊ tʃiːz / (BrE)
used to describe something that is lacking, insufficient, or unsatisfactory; something that does not meet expectations
insufficient or unsatisfactory; lacking

Examples

The team's effort in the second half was a little bit of bread and no cheese, leaving fans disappointed.
Her promise to help was just a little bit of bread and no cheese when I needed real support.
All the praise but no real recognition felt like a little bit of bread and no cheese to him.

Synset Examples

Her apology felt like a little bit of bread and no cheese.
That explanation was a little bit of bread and no cheese.
His excuses were just a little bit of bread and no cheese.
