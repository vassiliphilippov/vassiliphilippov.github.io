on the block

1. X

/ ɑn ðə blɑk / (AmE), / ɒn ðə blɒk / (BrE)
offered for sale or auction, especially at a public auction
available for sale or auction

Examples

After the company went bankrupt, its assets were put on the block to pay creditors.
Many art lovers attended the event to see masterpieces on the block this weekend.
He couldn't believe his childhood home was finally on the block after so many years.

Synset Examples

She’s putting her rare collectibles on the block.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for sale - available to be bought
  • Antonym: off the market - no longer available for purchase
