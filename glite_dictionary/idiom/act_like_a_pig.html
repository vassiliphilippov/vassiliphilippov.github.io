to act like a pig

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə pɪɡ / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə pɪɡ / (BrE)
to behave in a greedy, messy, or inconsiderate manner, especially when eating or dealing with others
behave in a greedy or messy way

Examples

Whenever there’s pizza, Tim acts like a pig and grabs half the slices before anyone else.
She acted like a pig at the dinner table, leaving a huge mess behind her plate.
At the party, some guests were acting like pigs, piling their plates with food and dropping crumbs everywhere.

Synset Examples

He acted like a pig at the buffet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pig out - to eat greedily or excessively
