as sweet as a songbird

1. ADJ

/ əz swit əz ə ˈsɔŋbɝd / (AmE), / əz swiːt əz ə ˈsɒŋbɜːd / (BrE)
extremely pleasant or melodious, like the singing of a songbird
very pleasant or melodious

Examples

Her laughter was as sweet as a songbird, lighting up the whole room.
He played the violin as sweet as a songbird, making everyone stop to listen.
The child's singing voice was as sweet as a songbird and charmed the audience.

Synset Examples

Her voice was as sweet as a songbird’s melody.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as sweet as honey - extremely sweet or pleasant (similar simile)
  • Synonym: melodious - having a pleasant musical sound
