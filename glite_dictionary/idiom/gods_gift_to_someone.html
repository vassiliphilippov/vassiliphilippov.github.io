God's gift to someone

1. X

/ ɡɑdz ɡɪft tu ˈsʌmwʌn / (AmE), / ɡɒdz ɡɪft tu ˈsʌmwʌn / (BrE)
used to describe someone who is extremely attractive or talented, often implying they have a high opinion of themselves; commonly used with a gender specified, as in 'God's gift to women.'
exceptionally attractive or talented person (idiom)

Examples

He walked into the room acting like God's gift to someone, expecting everyone to admire him.
She laughed at his arrogance, thinking he believed he was God's gift to someone.
Despite his talent, no one on the team considers him God's gift to someone.

Synset Examples

With her intelligence and beauty, she’s truly God's gift to men.
His ability to listen makes him God's gift to women.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: God's gift to women - an especially attractive man, often with a sense of self-importance
  • Synonym: God's gift to men - an especially attractive woman, often with a sense of self-importance
