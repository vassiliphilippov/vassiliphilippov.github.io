bump in the road

1. NOUN

/ bʌmp ɪn ðə roʊd / (AmE), / bʌmp ɪn ðə rəʊd / (BrE)
a minor difficulty or setback in progress
minor setback or obstacle

Examples

Everyone faces a bump in the road at some point in their career.
The sudden budget cut was a bump in the road, but the team recovered quickly.
Losing their star player was just another bump in the road for the determined club.

Synset Examples

She encountered a bump in the road during her project.
A slight bump in the road won't stop us.
The flat tire was just a bump in the road.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: setback - a reversal or check in progress
  • Synonym: obstacle - something that prevents progress
