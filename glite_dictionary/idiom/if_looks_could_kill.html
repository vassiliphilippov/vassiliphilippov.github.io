if looks could kill

1. X

/ ɪf lʊks kʊd kɪl / (AmE), / ɪf lʊks kʊd kɪl / (BrE)
used to say that someone looks at another person in a very angry or hostile way, as though their gaze alone could harm or kill them
used when someone gives a very angry glare

Examples

She glared at him from across the room as if looks could kill.
When he walked in late, his boss gave him a stare that said if looks could kill.
During the meeting, their argument grew tense, and for a moment, if looks could kill, he wouldn’t have survived.

Synset Examples

He gave her a death stare; if looks could kill, watch out.
She shot him a glare; if looks could kill, he'd be dead.
His expression was fierce; if looks could kill, she’d be terrified.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: death stare - an intensely hostile or angry look
  • Synonym: glare - an angry or fierce look
