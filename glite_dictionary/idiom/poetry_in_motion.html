poetry in motion

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊətri ɪn ˈmoʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈpəʊətri ɪn ˈməʊʃən / (BrE)
movement that is so graceful and beautiful that it is likened to poetry; especially used to describe artistic or athletic performances
graceful, beautiful movement

Examples

The young figure skater was poetry in motion on the ice.
As the horse galloped across the field, it was pure poetry in motion.
The way she paints is truly poetry in motion, captivating everyone who watches.

Synset Examples

The dancer's performance was true poetry in motion.
Her artwork felt like poetry in motion.
Watching the athletes was like poetry in motion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graceful - elegant in movement or manner
  • Antonym: awkward - lacking grace or smoothness in movement
