by any means necessary

1. X

/ baɪ ˈɛni minz ˈnɛsəsɛri / (AmE), / baɪ ˈɛni miːnz ˈnɛsəsəri / (BrE)
using whatever methods are needed to achieve a goal, often regardless of the difficulty or consequences
using whatever methods are needed

Examples

The activists vowed to achieve their goals by any means necessary.
She was determined to win the competition by any means necessary.
The company aimed to expand its market share by any means necessary.

Synset Examples

You can succeed by any means necessary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at all costs - regardless of the expense or effort required
