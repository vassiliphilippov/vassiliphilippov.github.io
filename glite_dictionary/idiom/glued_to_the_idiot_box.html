glued to the idiot box

1. ADJ

/ ɡluːd tuː ði ˈɪdiət bɑks / (AmE), / ɡluːd tuː ði ˈɪdiət bɒks / (BrE)
spending a long period continuously watching television, often with great attention
watching TV continuously
informal slang

Examples

Whenever her favorite series airs, she is glued to the idiot box for hours.
The children were glued to the idiot box all Saturday morning, barely moving from the sofa.
Instead of playing outside, he's often glued to the idiot box after school.

Synset Examples

The kids were glued to the idiot box all day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: couch potato - a person who spends a lot of time watching television

Etymology

‘Idiot box’ is an informal slang for television, popularized in the mid-20th century; ‘glued to’ is a common idiom for being unable to stop watching.
