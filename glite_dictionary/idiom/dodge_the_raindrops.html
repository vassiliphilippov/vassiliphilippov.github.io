to dodge the raindrops

1. VERB

/ dɑdʒ ðə ˈreɪnˌdrɑps / (AmE), / dɒdʒ ðə ˈreɪndrɒps / (BrE)
to try to avoid getting wet when it is raining, especially by moving quickly between sheltered areas
avoid getting wet in rain

Examples

He hurried down the street, trying to dodge the raindrops before the storm worsened.
Tourists in the plaza were forced to dodge the raindrops as sudden showers swept through.
You can't always dodge the raindrops—sometimes you just get soaked.

Synset Examples

He had to dodge the raindrops on his way home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avoid the rain - take action to not get wet during rain
