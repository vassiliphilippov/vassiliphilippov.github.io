to saw wood

1. VERB

/ sɔ wʊd / (AmE), / sɔː wʊd / (BrE)
(informal) To snore loudly while sleeping.
snore loudly while asleep (informal)
informal

Examples

My grandfather used to saw wood so loudly that we could hear him from the next room.
If you hear someone sawing wood at the campsite tonight, it's probably just Jim snoring again.
He was so tired after the hike that he immediately fell asleep and started to saw wood.

Synset Examples

she complained that her husband snores

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snore - to breathe noisily while sleeping

Etymology

Alludes to the sound of sawing resembling loud snoring.
