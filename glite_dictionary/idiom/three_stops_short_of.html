three stops short of

1. X

/ θɹi stɑps ʃɔrt ʌv / (AmE), / θɹi stɒps ʃɔːt ɒv / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly British) Not completely sane or rational; somewhat crazy or eccentric.
not quite sane; eccentric (British idiom)
informal british

Examples

If you believe that rumor, you're three stops short of making sense.
She acts like she's three stops short of the end of the line sometimes.
He must be three stops short of common sense to try that.

Synset Examples

He’s three stops short of Dagenham if he thinks that will work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: not all there - not completely sane; slightly crazy
  • Synonym: off one's rocker - crazy or eccentric
