to lump it

1. VERB

/ lʌmp ɪt / (AmE), / lʌmp ɪt / (BrE)
To accept a situation or decision, however unpleasant, without complaining or protest.
accept without complaining

Examples

If you don't agree with the rules, you'll just have to lump it.
Sometimes, when things don't go your way, you have to lump it.
She had to lump it when her vacation was canceled at the last minute.

Synset Examples

If you don't like the job, you'll just have to lump it.
He didn't get the promotion, so he'll just have to lump it.
Sometimes, life isn't fair, and you just have to lump it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to put up with - to tolerate or endure something without protest
  • Synonym: to grin and bear it - accept a difficult situation without complaining
