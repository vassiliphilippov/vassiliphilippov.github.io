world over

1. ADV

/ wɝːld ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / wɜːld ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
in all parts of the world; everywhere across the globe.
everywhere in the world

Examples

People from the world over visited the city to see the famous exhibition.
Musicians world over have been influenced by his innovative style.
Fashion trends spread rapidly world over thanks to social media.

Synset Examples

People celebrate New Year's Eve the world over.
The charity event raised funds the world over.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worldwide - extending or reaching throughout the world
  • Synonym: globally - in a way that involves the whole world
