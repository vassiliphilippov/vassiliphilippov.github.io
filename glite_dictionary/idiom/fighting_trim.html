fighting trim

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪtɪŋ trɪm / (AmE), / ˈfaɪtɪŋ trɪm / (BrE)
in excellent or optimal physical condition or readiness
in top physical or operational condition

Examples

After months of training, the boxer was finally in fighting trim before the big match.
She kept her bicycle in fighting trim so it was always ready for long rides.
The company stayed in fighting trim by constantly improving their products and services.

Synset Examples

He keeps his car in fighting trim for road trips.
She exercises daily to stay in fighting trim.
The athlete is always in fighting trim for competitions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fit - in good physical shape

Etymology

From the use of 'trim' (nautical: ready for action) combined with 'fighting', indicating readiness for contest or challenge.
