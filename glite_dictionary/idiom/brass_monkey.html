brass monkey

1. NOUN

/ bræs ˈmʌŋki / (AmE), / brɑːs ˈmʌŋki / (BrE)
used informally to describe extremely cold weather or conditions
extremely cold weather (informal idiom)
informal slang british

Examples

It was a brass monkey morning, and the car wouldn’t start.

The temperature outside is brass monkey cold tonight.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

Etymology

Apparently from the fuller phrase 'cold enough to freeze the balls off a brass monkey', origin uncertain, possibly British Navy slang.
