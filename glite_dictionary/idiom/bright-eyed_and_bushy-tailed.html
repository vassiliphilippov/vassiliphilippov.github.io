bright-eyed and bushy-tailed

1. ADJ

/ ˌbraɪt ˌaɪd ənd ˈbʊʃi teɪld / (AmE), / ˌbraɪt ˌaɪd ənd ˈbʊʃi teɪld / (BrE)
Full of energy and enthusiasm, especially in the morning or when starting something new.
energetic and enthusiastic (idiom)

Examples

She arrived bright-eyed and bushy-tailed for her first day at work.
After a restful night, the children were bright-eyed and bushy-tailed at breakfast.
Despite the early hour, he looked bright-eyed and bushy-tailed before the meeting.

Synset Examples

She arrived bright-eyed and bushy-tailed for her first day.
The kids were bright-eyed and bushy-tailed after a good night's sleep.
He woke up bright-eyed and bushy-tailed, ready for adventure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: energetic - having or showing a lot of energy
  • Antonym: lethargic - feeling sluggish or lacking energy

Etymology

Alludes to the alertness and lively appearance of animals, such as squirrels or foxes, with bright eyes and bushy tails.
