just as the next guy

1. X

/ dʒʌst æz ðə nɛkst ɡaɪ / (AmE), / dʒʌst æz ðə nɛkst ɡaɪ / (BrE)
used to indicate that someone is similar to others in a particular respect, usually in terms of a common interest or feeling
like anyone else

Examples

I get nervous about public speaking just as the next guy does.
She loves a good cup of coffee just as the next guy.
I'm concerned about the environment just as the next guy.

Synset Examples

I like sports, just as the next guy does.
He loves pizza, just as the next guy does.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: like everyone else - being similar to other people in some respect
  • Synonym: just like the next person - another version of the phrase; being similar to other people
