you're killing me

1. X

/ jʊr ˈkɪlɪŋ mi / (AmE), / jɔː ˈkɪlɪŋ mi / (BrE)
used to express exaggerated frustration, disbelief, or exasperation toward someone's actions or words
expression of strong frustration or disbelief
informal

Examples

You're killing me with all these impossible questions!
If you show up late again, you're killing me.
Every time you tell that joke, you're killing me—it never gets old.

Synset Examples

He suggested we run another mile and I replied, 'You're killing me, Smalls!'
When he brought up the embarrassing story, I exclaimed, 'You're killing me, Smalls!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: give me a break - expression requesting relief from exasperation or disbelief
