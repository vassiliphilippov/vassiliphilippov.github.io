to bring to its knees

1. VERB

/ brɪŋ tu ɪts niz / (AmE), / brɪŋ tu ɪts niːz / (BrE)
to cause someone or something to be defeated, overwhelmed, or rendered unable to function
cause to be defeated or powerless

Examples

The economic crisis brought the company to its knees within a matter of months.
A series of cyberattacks have brought the network to its knees, crippling communication systems.
Her inspiring speech helped the team recover after being brought to its knees by their defeat.

Synset Examples

The unexpected storm brought the city to its knees.
Her relentless determination brought the competition to their knees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defeat - to win a victory over; to overcome
  • Synonym: to overcome - to succeed in dealing with or controlling a problem
  • Synonym: to prevail - to be victorious; to triumph over
