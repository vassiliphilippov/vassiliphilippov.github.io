of a sudden

1. ADV

/ əv ə ˈsʌdən / (AmE), / əv ə ˈsʌdən / (BrE)
all at once; suddenly and without warning
suddenly and without warning

Examples

Of a sudden, the lights went out and the room was plunged into darkness.
Of a sudden, he realized he had left his bag on the train.
The weather had been calm, but of a sudden, a storm swept in from the sea.

Synset Examples

he stopped suddenly
suddenly she felt a sharp pain in her side
he decided to go to Chicago on the spur of the moment
he made up his mind suddenly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suddenly - quickly and unexpectedly
  • Synonym: all of a sudden - very quickly and unexpectedly
