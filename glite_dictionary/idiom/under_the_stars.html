under the stars

1. ADV

/ ˈʌndɚ ðə stɑɹz / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ðə stɑːz / (BrE)
Outdoors at night in the open air, especially in a setting where the stars are visible, often evoking romance, wonder, or natural beauty.
outdoors at night, in sight of stars

Examples

They spent the night under the stars, listening to the sounds of the forest.
We had a quiet picnic under the stars to celebrate our anniversary.
The movie was shown outdoors under the stars on a warm summer evening.
