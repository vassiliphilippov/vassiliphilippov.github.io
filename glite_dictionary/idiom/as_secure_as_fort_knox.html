as secure as Fort Knox

1. ADJ

/ əz səˈkjʊr əz fɔrt nɑks / (AmE), / əz sɪˈkjʊə əz fɔːt nɒks / (BrE)
Extremely secure or safe; protected against any possible harm or theft.
extremely secure or safe

Examples

The bank’s vault is as secure as Fort Knox, so clients trust it with their valuables.
With three locks and a security system, the apartment is as secure as Fort Knox.
He keeps his passwords as secure as Fort Knox, never sharing them with anyone.

Synset Examples

Her jewelry collection is as secure as Fort Knox.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impregnable - unable to be broken into or captured
  • Antonym: vulnerable - able to be easily attacked or harmed

Etymology

From Fort Knox, a well-protected United States Bullion Depository, in Kentucky.
