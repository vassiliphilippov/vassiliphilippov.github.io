pure quill

1. NOUN

/ pjʊr kwɪl / (AmE), / pjʊə kwɪl / (BrE)
used to describe something or someone that is genuine and completely authentic
completely genuine or authentic

Examples

The critic described the latest novel as pure quill, praising its heartfelt honesty.
You can be sure that every detail in his memoir is pure quill.
Her straightforward response was pure quill, without a trace of pretense.

Synset Examples
