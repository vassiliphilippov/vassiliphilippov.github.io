to be a wally

1. VERB

/ bi ə ˈwɑli / (AmE), / biː ə ˈwɒli / (BrE)
to behave in a silly or foolish way; to act ineptly or embarrassingly
act foolishly; behave like a fool
informal british

Examples

Please don't be a wally and forget your keys again.
If you be a wally in front of the boss, he'll never take you seriously.
She was being a wally by arguing over something so trivial.

Synset Examples

Don't be a wally; just ask for help!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be a fool - to act in a silly or foolish way
  • Antonym: be sensible - to act wisely or reasonably
