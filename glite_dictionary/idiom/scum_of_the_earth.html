scum of the earth

1. NOUN

/ skʌm əv ði ɝθ / (AmE), / skʌm əv ði ˈɜːθ / (BrE)
used to describe people regarded as the most despicable, worthless, or contemptible.
most despicable or contemptible people

Examples

After betraying his closest friends, many considered him the scum of the earth.
She said the con artists who prey on the elderly are scum of the earth.
The newspaper labeled the corrupt officials as scum of the earth.

Synset Examples

He referred to them as the scum of the earth.
They treat those people like the scum of the earth.
I can't believe he hangs out with the scum of the earth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: low-life - a person regarded as morally unacceptable
  • Synonym: vermin - people considered as being disgusting or despicable
