to feel like a love machine

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə lʌv məˈʃin / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə lʌv məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
to feel highly attractive, confident, and ready for romance or affection
to feel romantically attractive or confident
informal

Examples

Whenever I'm dressed up for a night out, I feel like a love machine.
That compliment made me feel like a love machine all day long.
After his new hairstyle, he couldn't help but feel like a love machine.

Synset Examples

That song always makes me feel like a love machine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be on fire - to feel exceptionally energetic or confident
