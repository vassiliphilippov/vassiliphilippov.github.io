piece of the puzzle

1. NOUN

/ pis əv ðə ˈpʌzəl / (AmE), / piːs ɒv ðə ˈpʌzl̩ / (BrE)
a necessary or important element that contributes to understanding a larger situation or solving a problem
essential part in understanding or solving something

Examples

Every witness statement was another piece of the puzzle for the investigators.
Getting her signature was the final piece of the puzzle in securing the contract.
Each financial document provided a piece of the puzzle in unraveling the company's history.

Synset Examples

This clue is a crucial piece of the puzzle.
She finally found her piece of the puzzle.
Finding that document was a piece of the puzzle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: missing piece - a part needed to complete something, often used similarly in puzzles and figuratively
