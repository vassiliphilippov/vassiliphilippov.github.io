hello yourself

1. INTJ

/ həˈloʊ jərˈsɛlf / (AmE), / həˈləʊ jəˈsɛlf / (BrE)
used as a sarcastic or pointed response when someone ignores or rudely responds to a greeting
sarcastic retort when ignored

Examples

When I waved and he walked past without a word, I muttered, 'hello yourself.'
She barely nodded at me, so I shot back, 'hello yourself.'
After being ignored all morning, Tom finally said 'hello yourself' as she walked by.

Synset Examples

When he ignored my greeting, I said, 'Hello yourself!'
