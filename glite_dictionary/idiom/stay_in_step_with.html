to stay in step with

1. VERB

/ steɪ ɪn stɛp wɪð / (AmE), / steɪ ɪn stɛp wɪð / (BrE)
to keep pace with or adapt to changes or developments; to not fall behind
keep pace with or adapt to

Examples

To succeed in technology, companies must stay in step with rapid innovation.
She tries to stay in step with the latest fashion trends each season.
If we don't stay in step with these regulations, we risk penalties.

Synset Examples

It's important to stay in step with the latest trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to keep up with - to move or progress at the same rate as someone or something
  • Antonym: to fall behind - to fail to keep pace with others
