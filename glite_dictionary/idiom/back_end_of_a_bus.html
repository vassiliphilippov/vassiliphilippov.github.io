back end of a bus

1. NOUN

/ bæk ɛnd əv ə bʌs / (AmE), / bak ɛnd əv ə bʌs / (BrE)
(informal) Used in similes to describe someone or something as unattractive or unpleasant in appearance.
very unattractive in appearance (informal simile)
informal

Examples

He wore a shirt so loud it made him look like the back end of a bus.
After the long flight, I felt like the back end of a bus.
She joked that her new shoes made her feet look like the back end of a bus.

Synset Examples

Her dress was so mismatched, it looked like the back end of a bus.
His new haircut looks like the back end of a bus.
She laughed, saying her morning face was like the back end of a bus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eyesore - something very ugly or unpleasant to look at
