dirty dog

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɝːti dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈdɜːti dɒɡ / (BrE)
an informal and often playful term for a person regarded as despicable, contemptible, or mischievous
a despicable or contemptible person (informal)
informal

Examples

He turned out to be a dirty dog who lied to everyone around him.
You dirty dog! How could you sneak out without telling anyone?
Some called him a dirty dog for betraying his closest friends.
