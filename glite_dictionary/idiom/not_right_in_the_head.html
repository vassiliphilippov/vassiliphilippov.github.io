not right in the head

1. ADJ

/ nɑt raɪt ɪn ðə hɛd / (AmE), / nɒt raɪt ɪn ðə hɛd / (BrE)
(informal) Having impaired judgment or sanity; mentally unstable or irrational.
mentally unstable or irrational (idiom)
informal

Examples

People whispered that he was not right in the head after the bizarre incident.
If you believe that conspiracy theory, you're not right in the head.
She did something so reckless last night that everyone said she was not right in the head.

Synset Examples

He's acting like he's not right in the head lately.
She must be not right in the head to try that.
If you think that plan will work, you're not right in the head.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged, insane
  • Antonym: sane - mentally healthy and rational
