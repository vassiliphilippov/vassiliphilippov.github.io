to grow a pair

1. VERB

/ ɡroʊ ə pɛr / (AmE), / ɡrəʊ ə pɛə / (BrE)
to become braver or more assertive, especially in situations that require courage or confidence
become braver or more assertive
informal slang vulgar

Examples

If you want to succeed, you need to grow a pair.
It's time to grow a pair and stand up for yourself.
He finally grew a pair and told his boss what he really thought.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to man up - (informal) to be brave or assertive
  • Degree Higher: courageous - having or showing bravery

Etymology

Refers metaphorically to growing testicles, associated with bravery in slang usage.
