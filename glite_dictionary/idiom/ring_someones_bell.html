to ring someone's bell

1. VERB

/ rɪŋ sʌmwʌnz bɛl / (AmE), / rɪŋ sʌmwənz bɛl / (BrE)
to strongly attract, excite, or appeal to someone; to catch someone's attention or interest in a striking way
excite or strongly appeal to someone

Examples

That new movie really rang my bell—I can't stop thinking about it.
Does classical music ring your bell or do you prefer something more modern?
His witty sense of humor definitely rings her bell every time they talk.

Synset Examples

This song will surely ring her bell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appeal to - to be attractive or interesting to someone
  • Synonym: turn someone on - to excite or arouse someone, often sexually or emotionally
