for love or money

1. ADV

/ fɚ ˈlʌv ər ˈmʌni / (AmE), / fə ˈlʌv ɔː ˈmʌni / (BrE)
under any circumstances; no matter what inducement or incentive is offered
in no circumstances; not at any price

Examples

I wouldn't sell my grandmother's ring for love or money.
You won't find that book anywhere now, for love or money.
She won't move to the city for love or money.

Synset Examples

she wouldn't give up her pets for love or money
