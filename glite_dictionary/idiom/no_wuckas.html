no wuckas

1. X

/ noʊ ˈwʌkəz / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈwʌkəz / (BrE)
(Australian slang) said to indicate that there is no problem or cause for concern; no worries.
Australian slang for 'no worries' or 'no problem'
slang

Examples

Thanks for your help today, no wuckas!
If you can't make it on Friday, no wuckas, we can reschedule.
He offered to pick me up, and I just said, 'no wuckas.'

Synset Examples

Thanks for helping me out, mate, no wuckas.
I can lend you my book, no wuckas at all.
If you need anything, just ask, no wuckas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no worries - expression indicating there is no problem or cause for concern

Etymology

Australian alteration of 'no worries', with 'wuckas' being a playful spoonerism of 'worries'.
