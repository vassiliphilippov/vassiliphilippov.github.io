to be a dolt

1. VERB

/ bi ə doʊlt / (AmE), / bi ə dəʊlt / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or stupid manner
act in a foolish manner

Examples

Don't be a dolt; check your work before submitting.
He tends to be a dolt when he ignores good advice.
If you be a dolt in the meeting, people may not take you seriously.

Synset Examples

Don't be a dolt; check your work before submitting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dolt - a stupid person
  • Synonym: be a fool - to act in a foolish way
  • Antonym: be wise - to act in a sensible or prudent way

Etymology

Dolt: of uncertain origin, possibly from Middle English 'dolt' (fool, stupid person).
