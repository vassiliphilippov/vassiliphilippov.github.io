no way, Jose

1. INTJ

/ noʊ weɪ hoʊˈzeɪ / (AmE), / nəʊ weɪ həʊˈzeɪ / (BrE)
used to emphasize a strong refusal or rejection; definitely not
emphatic refusal or rejection
informal

Examples

"Can I borrow your laptop for a week?" "No way, Jose, I need it for work."
When asked if he'd ever try skydiving, he replied, "No way, Jose."
No way, Jose—I am not eating that spicy food again!

Synset Examples

Can you finish this project by tomorrow? No way, Jose.
Will you lend me your car? No way, Jose.
I’m not going to that party. No way, Jose!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no way - used for strong refusal
