to be a dink

1. VERB

/ bi ə dɪŋk / (AmE), / bi ə dɪŋk / (BrE)
to act in a foolish or irritating manner
act foolishly or annoyingly
informal

Examples

Please don’t be a dink; cooperate with the rest of the team.
She told him not to be a dink about losing the game.
If you be a dink at the party, people won’t want to invite you again.

Synset Examples

Don't be a dink, share the snacks!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be a jerk - to behave in an unpleasant or obnoxious way
