to die on one's arse

1. VERB

/ daɪ ɑn wʌnz ɑrs / (AmE), / daɪ ɒn wʌnz ɑːs / (BrE)
To feel extremely bored or uninterested, usually due to an unengaging situation or event.
be extremely bored
informal british

I was so uninterested in the lecture that I thought I might die on my arse.
If the movie doesn't get better soon, I'm going to die on my arse.
He looked so bored during the meeting, it seemed he would die on his arse.
