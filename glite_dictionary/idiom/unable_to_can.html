unable to can

1. ADJ

/ əˈneɪbəl tə kæn / (AmE), / əˈneɪbl tə kæn / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) Unable to manage, cope, or deal with something; overwhelmed or exhausted to the point of incapacity.
unable to cope; overwhelmed (slang)
informal slang

After all the bad news today, I am unable to can right now.
She was so tired after finals week that she was just unable to can.
Sometimes life gets overwhelming and you feel unable to can anything.
