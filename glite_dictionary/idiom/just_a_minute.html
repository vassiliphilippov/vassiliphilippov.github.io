just a minute

1. X

/ dʒʌst ə ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / dʒʌst ə ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
used to ask someone to wait for a very short time; a brief moment.
wait a brief moment

Examples

Just a minute, I'll be right with you.
Can you hold on just a minute?
She asked me to wait just a minute while she finished her call.

Synset Examples

Wait, just a minute, I need to grab my bag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: just a second - used to ask someone to wait for a very short time
  • Synonym: in a moment - very soon; after a short time
