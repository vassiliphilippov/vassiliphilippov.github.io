to get amongst it

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt əˈmʌŋst ɪt / (AmE), / ɡɛt əˈmʌŋst ɪt / (BrE)
to actively participate in a group, activity, or situation, especially with enthusiasm
to actively join in or participate
informal british
