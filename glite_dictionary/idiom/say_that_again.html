to say that again

1. VERB

/ seɪ ðæt əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / seɪ ðæt əˈɡen / (BrE)
used to ask someone to repeat what they have said; also used informally to strongly agree with or emphasize a remark just made.
request repetition or express strong agreement

Examples

Could you say that again so I can write it down?
"That meal was fantastic!" "You can say that again!"
I'm sorry, I didn't catch that—can you say that again, please?

Synset Examples

That movie was amazing; you can say that again!
Can you say that again for me?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absolutely - used to agree or emphasize a statement
  • Synonym: repeat - to say or do something again
