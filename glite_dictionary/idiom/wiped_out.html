wiped out

1. ADJ

/ waɪpt aʊt / (AmE), / waɪpt aʊt / (BrE)
Having lost all one's money or assets; ruined financially.
financially ruined or bankrupt

Examples

After the stock market crash, thousands of investors were wiped out and left penniless.
The company was wiped out by a series of poor management decisions and heavy debts.
She worked hard for years, but a single bad investment left her completely wiped out.

Synset Examples

the broken fortunes of the family

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bankrupt - legally declared unable to pay debts
  • Synonym: insolvent - unable to pay debts owed
  • Antonym: prosperous - successful, wealthy
