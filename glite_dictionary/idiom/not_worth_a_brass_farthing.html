not worth a brass farthing

1. ADJ

/ nɑt wɝθ ə bræs ˈfɑrðɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒt wɜːθ ə brɑːs ˈfɑːðɪŋ / (BrE)
of no value whatsoever; completely worthless
completely worthless
british

Examples

He realized the guarantee was not worth a brass farthing when the product broke.
Your promises are not worth a brass farthing after so many disappointments.
The jewels turned out to be fake and not worth a brass farthing.

Synset Examples

That old car is not worth a brass farthing.
His excuses are not worth a brass farthing.
This item is not worth a brass farthing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worthless - having no value or use
  • Antonym: valuable - having great value or worth

Etymology

'Farthing' was a former British coin of very low value, and 'brass' refers to a coin made of a base metal instead of silver or gold.
