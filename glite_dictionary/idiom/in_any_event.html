in any event

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈɛni ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / ɪn ˈɛni ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
used to say that something will remain true or will happen whatever the circumstances; regardless of what happens.
whatever the circumstances; regardless what happens

Examples

We hoped for good weather, but in any event, the festival will proceed as planned.
You might be late, but in any event, call me when you arrive.
The project has challenges; in any event, our team is prepared.

Synset Examples

I’ll see you at the party; in any event, it will be fun.
The meeting will happen; in any event, don’t forget to prepare.
We should stay optimistic; in any event, we did our best.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anyway - in any case; regardless; one way or another
  • Synonym: regardless - without paying attention to the current situation or conditions
