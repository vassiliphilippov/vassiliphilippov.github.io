mad as hops

1. ADJ

/ mæd əz hɑps / (AmE), / mad əz hɒps / (BrE)
Extremely angry or furious.
extremely angry; furious (idiom)
informal archaic

Examples

She was mad as hops when she heard about the mistake.
If you break his prized vase, he'll be mad as hops.
They were mad as hops after learning about the unfair decision.

Synset Examples

He was mad as hops when he lost his keys.
After the argument, she was mad as hops.
He gets mad as hops over little things.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: furious - extremely angry
  • Antonym: calm - not showing anger or agitation
