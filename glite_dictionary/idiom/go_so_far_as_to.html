to go so far as to

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ soʊ fɑr æz tu / (AmE), / ɡəʊ səʊ fɑːr æz tuː / (BrE)
to do something considered extreme or beyond what is usual or expected
take an extreme action; go beyond the norm

Examples

She didn't go so far as to say he was lying, but she clearly didn't trust him.
I wouldn't go so far as to call it a masterpiece, but the film was impressive.
They went so far as to boycott the event in protest.

Synset Examples

He would even go so far as to lie for her.
She will go so far as to cancel her plans.
They would go so far as to break the rules.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go to extremes - to do things to a very great or excessive degree
