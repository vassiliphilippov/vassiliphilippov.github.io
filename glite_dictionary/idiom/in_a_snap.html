in a snap

1. ADV

/ ɪn ə snæp / (AmE), / ɪn ə snap / (BrE)
very quickly or with little effort; immediately.
very quickly or easily
informal

Examples

She finished her homework in a snap because it was so easy.
If you follow these steps, you'll assemble the furniture in a snap.
He learned to play the new song in a snap.

Synset Examples

He fixed the issue in a snap.
I can finish that report in a snap.
She solved the puzzle in a snap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in no time - very quickly; without delay
  • Synonym: in a flash - in an instant; very quickly
