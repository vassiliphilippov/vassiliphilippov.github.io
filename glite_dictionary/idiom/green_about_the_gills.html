green about the gills

1. ADJ

/ ɡrin əˈbaʊt ðə ɡɪlz / (AmE), / ɡriːn əˈbaʊt ðə ɡɪlz / (BrE)
looking pale and sick, especially from nausea
looking pale and sick

Examples

You don't look well—you're green about the gills; maybe you should go home.
