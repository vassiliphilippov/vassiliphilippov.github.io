to wear the trousers

1. VERB

/ wɛr ðə ˈtraʊzɚz / (AmE), / wɛə ðə ˈtraʊzəz / (BrE)
(of one person in a couple or family) to be the person who has the main authority or control.
be the dominant or controlling partner

Examples

Everyone knows that Maria wears the trousers in their relationship.
It's obvious who wears the trousers in this house—it's certainly not Tom.
Jenny may be quiet, but she definitely wears the trousers when it comes to major decisions.

Synset Examples

Who is calling the shots in this house?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be in charge - to have responsibility or control over people or an activity
  • Synonym: to call the shots - to be the person who makes the important decisions

Etymology

Refers to the historical association of trousers with male authority in the household.
