to knock out of the box

1. VERB

/ nɑk aʊt əv ðə bɑks / (AmE), / nɒk aʊt əv ðə bɒks / (BrE)
to achieve something with outstanding success or to impress greatly, often surpassing expectations
to surpass expectations with great success
informal

Examples

The new smartphone will knock out of the box any sales records we've seen before.
Her solo performance really knocked out of the box the entire audience at the concert.
If you want to win, you’ll have to knock out of the box with your presentation.

Synset Examples

The new product will knock out of the box sales expectations.
His performance was so good, it knocked everyone out of the box.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blow away - to impress so much as to overwhelm
  • Antonym: to fall flat - to fail to impress or succeed
