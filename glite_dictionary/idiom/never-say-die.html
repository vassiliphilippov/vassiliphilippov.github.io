never-say-die

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɛvər seɪ ˈdaɪ / (AmE), / ˌnɛvə seɪ ˈdaɪ / (BrE)
showing persistent optimism and determination; refusing to give up
persistently optimistic; refusing to give up

Examples

She faced every challenge with a never-say-die attitude.
The team's never-say-die spirit inspired fans across the country.
Even after repeated setbacks, his never-say-die optimism never wavered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determined - having a firm decision not to give up
  • Synonym: resilient - able to withstand or recover quickly from difficulties
  • Antonym: defeatist - expecting or accepting failure too easily
