on someone's deathbed

1. ADP

/ ɑn ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈdɛθbɛd / (AmE), / ɒn ˈsʌmwənz ˈdɛθbɛd / (BrE)
during the final moments of someone's life, especially when they are expected to die soon
close to the time of death

Examples

She forgave her brother on her deathbed, ending years of bitterness.
Many important confessions are made on someone's deathbed.
He asked to see his family one last time on his deathbed.

Synset Examples

The old man told stories from his youth on his deathbed.
