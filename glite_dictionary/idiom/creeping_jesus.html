Creeping Jesus

1. INTJ

/ ˈkripɪŋ ˈdʒiːzəs / (AmE), / ˈkriːpɪŋ ˈdʒiːzəs / (BrE)
An exclamation expressing surprise or shock.
exclamation of surprise or shock
informal archaic

Examples

Creeping Jesus, what was that noise in the attic?
Creeping Jesus, you almost made me spill my coffee!
Creeping Jesus, I can’t believe how much the price has gone up!

Synset Examples

Creeping Jesus, I didn’t expect that surprise party at all!
Creeping Jesus, you scared me when you jumped out!

Etymology

Originally an epithet for someone displaying ostentatious piety; later used as an exclamation (early 20th century).
