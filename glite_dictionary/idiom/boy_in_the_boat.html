boy in the boat

1. NOUN

/ bɔɪ ɪn ðə boʊt / (AmE), / bɔɪ ɪn ðə bəʊt / (BrE)
a person who feels isolated and confused, or someone singled out to play a particular role, especially when feeling alone or out of place
person who feels isolated or singled out

Examples

After moving to a new school, she often felt like the boy in the boat during lunch.
With everyone looking to him for leadership, he became the boy in the boat by default.
Despite being invited to the event, he remained the boy in the boat, unsure where he truly belonged.

Synset Examples

Everyone expected him to be the boy in the boat.
He felt like the boy in the boat, lost and alone.
