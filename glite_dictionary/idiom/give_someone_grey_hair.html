to give someone grey hair

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ˈsʌmwʌn ɡreɪ hɛr / (AmE), / ɡɪv ˈsʌmwən ɡreɪ heə / (BrE)
to cause someone stress, worry, or anxiety, often to the point of premature aging
cause someone stress or worry

Examples

Her teenage son's antics are enough to give her grey hair.
Managing this project might give you grey hair if you're not careful.
Don't let work give you grey hair; try to relax more often.

Synset Examples

Her reckless driving habits are sure to give her parents grey hair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stress out - to cause someone to feel very stressed or anxious
