to spread like stink on shit

1. VERB

/ sprɛd laɪk stɪŋk ɒn ʃɪt / (AmE), / sprɛd laɪk stɪŋk ɒn ʃɪt / (BrE)
(of information, feelings, etc.) to spread very quickly and in a way that is hard to contain, usually in an unpleasant or unwelcome manner.
spread rapidly and unpleasantly
informal vulgar

Examples

The gossip about the manager's mistake spread like stink on shit around the warehouse.
Once the news broke, panic spread like stink on shit throughout the community.
Online rumors can spread like stink on shit if people aren't careful.

Synset Examples

The rumors spread like stink on shit in the office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to spread like wildfire - spread very quickly and uncontrollably
