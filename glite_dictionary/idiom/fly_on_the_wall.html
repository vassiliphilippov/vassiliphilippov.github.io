fly on the wall

1. NOUN

/ flaɪ ɑn ðə wɔl / (AmE), / flaɪ ɒn ðə wɔːl / (BrE)
someone who is able to observe a situation without being noticed or taking part in it
unnoticed observer or listener

Examples

I wish I could be a fly on the wall during the negotiations to hear what is really said.
She managed to act as a fly on the wall throughout the entire meeting, gathering valuable information.
His quiet nature allowed him to be a fly on the wall in social situations.

Synset Examples

I wish I could be a fly on the wall during that meeting.
As a fly on the wall, I witnessed their secret discussion.
He felt like a fly on the wall at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: observer - person who watches something
  • Antonym: participant - person who takes part in something
