we have a winner

1. X

/ wi ˈhæv ə ˈwɪnɚ / (AmE), / wi ˈhav ə ˈwɪnə / (BrE)
used to announce that someone has succeeded or something has worked, especially after a process of selection or competition
announcing a successful outcome or winner

Examples

After several failed attempts, she finally guessed the password and exclaimed, we have a winner!
When the right answer appeared on the screen, the teacher said, 'we have a winner!'
The chef tried different recipes, but when everyone loved the new dish, she announced, 'we have a winner.'

Synset Examples

The host exclaimed, ding, ding, ding, we have a winner!
Ding, ding, ding, we have a winner of the contest!
When the buzzer sounded, I yelled, ding, ding, ding, we have a winner!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: winner - a person or thing that wins
