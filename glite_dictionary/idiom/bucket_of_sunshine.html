bucket of sunshine

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌkət əv ˈsʌnʃaɪn / (AmE), / ˈbʌkɪt əv ˈsʌnʃʌɪn / (BrE)
someone or something that brings great joy or happiness; a source of delight
source of joy or happiness

Examples

Her positive attitude is a real bucket of sunshine in our office.
After days of rain, the children’s laughter was like a bucket of sunshine.
He walked in with a smile and brought a bucket of sunshine to everyone’s mood.

Synset Examples

Her smile is like a bucket of sunshine.
The children's laughter felt like a bucket of sunshine.
He brought a bucket of sunshine to the gloomy day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ray of sunshine - a person or thing that brings happiness
