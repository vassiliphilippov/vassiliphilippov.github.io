anyone's guess

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛniˌwʌnz ˌɡɛs / (AmE), / ˈeniwʌnz ˌɡɛs / (BrE)
used to say that something is very uncertain or impossible to predict.
something very uncertain or unpredictable

Examples

With so many delays, when the road will reopen is anyone's guess.
How the election will turn out is anyone's guess at this stage.
The reason for his sudden resignation remains anyone's guess.

Synset Examples

What she'll choose is anyone's guess.
When the package will arrive is anyone's guess.
Where he went is anyone's guess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uncertain - not able to be predicted or known
  • Antonym: predictable - able to be predicted or foreseen
