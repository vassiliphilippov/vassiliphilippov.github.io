university of life

1. NOUN

/ juːˈnɪvɚsəti əv laɪf / (AmE), / juːˈnɪvəsəti əv laɪf / (BrE)
learning gained through real-life experiences, rather than formal education
learning from real-life experience

Examples

He credits the university of life for teaching him how to succeed in business.
After years of traveling, I feel like I've graduated from the university of life.
Her wisdom doesn't come from books, but from the university of life.

Synset Examples

He learned invaluable lessons at the university of life.
Traveling is like attending the university of life.
The hardships taught her the true university of life.
