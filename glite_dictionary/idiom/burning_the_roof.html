burning the roof

1. X

/ ˈbɝːnɪŋ ðə ɹuːf / (AmE), / ˈbɜːnɪŋ ðə ɹuːf / (BrE)
Having a great time or holding a wild, exuberant, or excessively loud party.
having a wild or lively party
informal

Examples

The club was absolutely burning the roof last night during the headline DJ's set.
Whenever they celebrate, they end up burning the roof well into the early hours.
Guests were dancing, laughing, and burning the roof at the New Year's Eve bash.

Synset Examples

The concert was so good it felt like we were burning the roof.
