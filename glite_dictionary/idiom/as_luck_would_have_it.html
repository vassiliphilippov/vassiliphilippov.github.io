as luck would have it

1. ADV

/ æz lʌk wʊd hæv ɪt / (AmE), / æz lʌk wəd hæv ɪt / (BrE)
used to indicate that something happened by chance, especially something fortunate or convenient
by chance, often luckily

Examples

As luck would have it, she arrived just as the meeting was starting.
As luck would have it, there was an empty seat on the crowded train.
As luck would have it, we found our lost dog within a few hours.

Synset Examples

fortunately the weather was good

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fortunately - by good fortune; luckily
  • Antonym: unluckily - by bad fortune; unfortunately
