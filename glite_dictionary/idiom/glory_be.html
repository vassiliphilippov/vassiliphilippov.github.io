glory be

1. INTJ

/ ˈɡlɔri ˈbi / (AmE), / ˈɡlɔːri ˈbiː / (BrE)
used as an exclamation to express relief, joy, or surprise
expression of relief or joy
informal

Examples

Glory be, the sun is finally shining after all that rain!
Glory be! I thought I'd lost my wallet, but here it is.
She exclaimed, "Glory be, what a wonderful surprise!"

Synset Examples

Glory be, we finally finished the project!
Glory be, I can't believe it's already Friday!

Etymology

From the opening words of the Christian doxology 'Glory be to the Father.'
