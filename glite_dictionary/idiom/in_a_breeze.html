in a breeze

1. ADV

/ ɪn ə briːz / (AmE), / ɪn ə briːz / (BrE)
very easily or quickly; with little effort or difficulty
very easily or quickly

Examples

She finished the project in a breeze and had time to spare.
With his experience, he solved the problem in a breeze.
The team won the match in a breeze, barely breaking a sweat.

Synset Examples

She finished the exam in a breeze.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: with ease - without difficulty; easily
  • Synonym: effortlessly - in a manner requiring no effort
