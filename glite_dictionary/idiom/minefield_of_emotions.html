minefield of emotions

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪnˌfiːld əv ɪˈmoʊʃənz / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnˌfiːld əv ɪˈməʊʃənz / (BrE)
a situation that is complicated and full of emotional challenges or potential for emotional distress
situation with emotional difficulties

Examples

After her breakup, each conversation felt like a minefield of emotions she carefully navigated.
Dealing with family disputes can quickly turn into a minefield of emotions.
The counselor helped them work through the minefield of emotions surrounding the loss.

Synset Examples

She had to tiptoe through a minefield of emotions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emotional minefield - situation full of emotional dangers or difficulties
