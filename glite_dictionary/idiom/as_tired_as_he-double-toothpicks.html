as tired as HE-double-toothpicks

1. ADJ

/ æz ˈtaɪɚd æz ˌeɪtʃiː ˌdʌbəl ˈtuːθ pɪks / (AmE), / az ˈtaɪəd az ˌeɪtʃiː ˌdʌbəl ˈtuːθ pɪks / (BrE)
Extremely tired; completely exhausted (humorous euphemism).
extremely tired (humorous idiom)
informal

Examples

After finishing the marathon, I felt as tired as HE-double-toothpicks and could barely move.
She worked a double shift and came home as tired as HE-double-toothpicks.
By the end of exam week, all the students looked as tired as HE-double-toothpicks.

Synset Examples

She laughed and said, 'I'm as tired as HE-double-toothpicks right now!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog-tired - extremely tired
  • Synonym: exhausted - completely very tired

Etymology

Euphemistic play on 'hell' (HE-double-toothpicks) to avoid swearing.
