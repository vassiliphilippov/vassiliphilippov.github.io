one brick short of a full load

1. ADJ

/ wʌn brɪk ʃɔrt əv ə fʊl loʊd / (AmE), / wʌn brɪk ʃɔːt əv ə fʊl ləʊd / (BrE)
slightly stupid or not very intelligent; somewhat foolish
slightly stupid; not very intelligent
informal

Examples

Anyone who tries to fix electronics with water is clearly one brick short of a full load.
My neighbor is nice, but she's a bit one brick short of a full load sometimes.
