from womb to tomb

1. ADV

/ frəm wum tə tum / (AmE), / frəm wuːm tə tuːm / (BrE)
throughout the whole of a person's life, from birth to death
throughout one's entire life

Examples

The company provides insurance from womb to tomb, ensuring lifelong coverage.
She dedicated herself to the cause from womb to tomb.
Public services are promised from womb to tomb in many welfare states.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cradle to grave - from birth to death; the whole of a person's life
