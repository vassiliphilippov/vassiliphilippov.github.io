to sleep around

1. VERB

/ sliːp əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / sliːp əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
(of a person) to have sexual relations with multiple partners, often without commitment.
have sexual relations with multiple partners
informal

Examples

Some people judge those who sleep around, but others think it’s nobody else’s business.
He was hurt when he discovered his partner had been sleeping around during their relationship.
