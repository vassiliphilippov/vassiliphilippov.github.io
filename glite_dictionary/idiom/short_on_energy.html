short on energy

1. ADJ

/ ʃɔrt ɑn ˈɛnɚdʒi / (AmE), / ʃɔːt ɒn ˈɛnədʒi / (BrE)
lacking energy or vitality; feeling tired or depleted
lacking energy

Examples

I always feel short on energy after a busy week at work.
Many people are short on energy when they don't get enough sleep.
He was short on energy during the final miles of the marathon.

Synset Examples

I’m feeling short on energy today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tired - feeling in need of rest or sleep
  • Antonym: energetic - having or showing energy
