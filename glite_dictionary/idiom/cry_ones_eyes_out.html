to cry one's eyes out

1. VERB

/ kraɪ wʌnz aɪz aʊt / (AmE), / kraɪ wʌnz aɪz aʊt / (BrE)
to cry very intensely or for a long time, usually because of great sadness
cry intensely or heavily (idiom)

Examples

She cried her eyes out when she heard the tragic news.
After losing the championship, the whole team cried their eyes out in the locker room.
I cried my eyes out watching that heartbreaking film last night.

Synset Examples

The child bawled her eyes out over the lost toy.
She cried her eyes out during the sad movie.
He cried his eyes out when his dog passed away.
I cried my eyes out at the wedding speeches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to bawl one's eyes out - to cry very loudly or intensely, usually from distress
