gone fishing

1. ADJ

/ ɡɑn ˈfɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡɒn ˈfɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
inattentive or absent-minded; mentally disengaged or not concentrating on the present situation
inattentive or absent-minded
informal

Examples

Sorry, could you repeat the question? I must have gone fishing for a moment.
During the long meeting, half the team looked like they had gone fishing.
Whenever he's tired, it's as if his mind has completely gone fishing.

Synset Examples

I think my mind has gone fishing today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daydreaming - lost in thought and not paying attention to surroundings
  • Synonym: absent-minded - forgetful or inattentive
  • Antonym: alert - paying close attention; fully awake
