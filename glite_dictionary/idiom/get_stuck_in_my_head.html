to get stuck in my head

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt stʌk ɪn maɪ hɛd / (AmE), / ɡɛt stʌk ɪn maɪ hɛd / (BrE)
(of a song, tune, or phrase) to be repeatedly and involuntarily thought of, especially when difficult to ignore or forget.
be unable to stop thinking about a song or tune

Examples

That new commercial jingle always gets stuck in my head after I hear it.
I can't concentrate in class because the song we heard this morning got stuck in my head.
No matter what I do, that melody seems to get stuck in my head for hours.

Synset Examples

The eppy theme song always gets stuck in my head.
That catchy song got stuck in my head; I'm brain-wormed.
That catchy jingle is stuck in my head!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: earworm - a catchy song or tune that repeats in one's mind
