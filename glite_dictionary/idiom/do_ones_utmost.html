to do one's utmost

1. VERB

/ du wʌnz ˈʌtmoʊst / (AmE), / duː wʌnz ˈʌtməʊst / (BrE)
to make the greatest possible effort; to try as hard as one can
make the greatest effort

Examples

She promised to do her utmost to support the team during the project.
We should do our utmost to protect the environment for future generations.
He will do his utmost to ensure the event is a success.

Synset Examples

She will do her utmost to achieve her goals.
He promised to do his utmost in the upcoming competition.
We need to do our utmost to help the community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to do one's best - try as hard as possible
