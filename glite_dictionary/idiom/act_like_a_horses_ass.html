to act like a horse's ass

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə hɔrsɪz æs / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə hɔːsɪz ɑːs / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or rude manner; to act in a way that annoys or embarrasses others
behave foolishly or rudely
informal

Examples

He couldn't stop acting like a horse's ass at the party, embarrassing everyone.
If you act like a horse's ass during the interview, you probably won’t get the job.
She told him not to act like a horse's ass in front of her parents.

Synset Examples

He acted like a horse's ass during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: act like a fool - to behave in a foolish or silly way
  • Synonym: to act like a jerk - to behave in an unpleasant or inconsiderate manner
