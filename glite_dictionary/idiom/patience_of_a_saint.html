patience of a saint

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪʃəns əv ə seɪnt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪʃəns əv ə seɪnt / (BrE)
an exceptional or extreme level of patience and tolerance, especially in challenging or trying situations
exceptional patience or tolerance

Examples

She cared for her ailing mother with the patience of a saint.
Teachers who work in challenging schools often need the patience of a saint.
He dealt with the endless paperwork with the patience of a saint.

Synset Examples

  • Synonym: saintly patience - patience similar to that of a saint
