not much of anything

1. X

/ nɑt mʌtʃ əv ˈɛn.iˌθɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒt mʌtʃ əv ˈen.iˌθɪŋ / (BrE)
almost nothing; very little or of insignificant amount or value.
almost nothing; very little

Examples

After all the cleaning, there was not much of anything left in the cupboards.
We searched his pockets but found not much of anything valuable.
By the time I got to the sale, there was not much of anything worth buying.
