low-hanging fruit

1. NOUN

/ ˌloʊ ˌhæŋɪŋ ˈfrut / (AmE), / ˌləʊ ˌhæŋɪŋ ˈfruːt / (BrE)
tasks, problems, or goals that are easy to achieve or solve and can be addressed with little effort or risk
easily achievable tasks or goals

Examples

Rather than overcomplicating the project, let's address the low-hanging fruit first.
The manager advised the team to target the low-hanging fruit to demonstrate quick progress.
In the first phase, we focused on the low-hanging fruit to maximize early results.

Synset Examples

They spent time on high-hanging fruit to boost sales.
Identifying low-hanging fruit will help us save time.
Let's focus on the high-hanging fruit first.
Let's tackle the low-hanging fruit first.
Targeting high-hanging fruit can lead to quick wins.
We should focus on the low-hanging fruit to boost sales.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: easy pickings - things that are easy to obtain or achieve
