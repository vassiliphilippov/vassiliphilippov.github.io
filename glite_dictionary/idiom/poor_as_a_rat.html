poor as a rat

1. ADJ

/ pʊr əz ə ræt / (AmE), / pɔː əz ə ræt / (BrE)
extremely poor or destitute
extremely poor

Examples

After the company went bankrupt, he was poor as a rat for months.
She described her childhood as being poor as a rat, with barely enough to eat.
When the rent went up, they suddenly found themselves poor as a rat.

Synset Examples

After the bills, my wallet is as poor as a rat.
That old car makes him feel poor as a rat.
Since losing his job, he's been poor as a rat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poor as a church mouse - extremely poor or impoverished
  • Antonym: well-off - having plenty of money or resources
