in the pink of health

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ðə pɪŋk əv hɛlθ / (AmE), / ɪn ðə pɪŋk əv hɛlθ / (BrE)
in very good health; in a state of robust physical well-being
in very good health

Examples

After weeks of rest and good nutrition, she was in the pink of health again.
The doctor said my grandfather is in the pink of health for his age.
Despite his busy schedule, Mark manages to stay in the pink of health.

Synset Examples

He's always been in the pink of health.
She is in the pink of health after the vacation.
After her recovery, she feels in the pink of health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fit as a fiddle - in very good health; physically fit

Etymology

From the use of 'pink' meaning the highest or peak condition, combined with 'health.'
