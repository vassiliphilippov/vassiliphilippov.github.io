to feel like a total bumhole

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə ˈtoʊtəl ˈbʌmˌhoʊl / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈtəʊtəl ˈbʌmhəʊl / (BrE)
to feel extremely worthless or deeply embarrassed, especially after doing something foolish or shameful
feel very worthless or embarrassed
informal slang

Examples

After forgetting his girlfriend's birthday, he felt like a total bumhole for days.
The failed presentation made her feel like a total bumhole in front of her team.
He tripped over nothing at the meeting and instantly felt like a total bumhole.

Synset Examples

The prank made him feel like a total bumhole.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feel like an idiot - to feel foolish or stupid
  • Antonym: feel proud - to feel a strong sense of pride or self-worth
