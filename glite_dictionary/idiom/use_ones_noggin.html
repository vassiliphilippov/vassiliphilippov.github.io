to use one's noggin

1. VERB

/ juːz wʌnz ˈnɑɡɪn / (AmE), / juːz wʌnz ˈnɒɡɪn / (BrE)
to think carefully or wisely; to use one's intelligence or common sense
think carefully or use common sense (informal)
informal

Examples

Before answering such a tricky question, you should use your noggin and think it through.
My dad always tells me to use my noggin whenever I face a tough decision.
She forgot to use her noggin and ended up making a silly mistake.

Synset Examples

He reminded her to use her noggin and not rush.
You really need to use your noggin during the exam.
Make sure to use your noggin when solving this puzzle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: think - to use one's mind actively to form ideas or solve problems
  • Synonym: to use one's head - to think carefully or sensibly; to use one's intelligence

Etymology

"Noggin" as slang for head, of uncertain origin, first attested in the 18th century.
