none of your business

1. X

/ nʌn əv jʊr ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / nʌn əv jɔː ˈbɪznəs / (BrE)
used to tell someone that something does not concern them or is private
used to tell someone something is private

Examples

What I do after work is none of your business.
She made it clear that her plans were none of your business.
If I want to leave early, it's none of your business.

Synset Examples

What I do on weekends is none of your business.
His personal life is none of your business, respect that.
Her finances are none of your business, so stop asking.
I prefer to keep my finances private; it's none of your business.
His personal life is off-limits; it's none of your business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to mind your own business - used to tell someone not to interfere or ask about private matters
