the rest is history

1. X

/ ðə ɹɛst ɪz ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / ðə ɹɛst ɪz ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
used to indicate that the events following what has just been described are well-known, notable, or unsurprising
what happened next is well-known

Examples

She landed her first role in a major film, and the rest is history.
They met in college, fell in love, and the rest is history.
After founding his company, the rest is history.

Synset Examples

They started with a small idea, and the rest is history.
She wrote a bestseller, and the rest is history.
He won the lottery, and the rest is history.
