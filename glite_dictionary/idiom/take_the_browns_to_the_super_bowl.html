to take the Browns to the Super Bowl

1. VERB

/ teɪk ðə braʊnz tu ðə ˈsuːpɚ boʊl / (AmE), / teɪk ðə braʊnz tuː ðə ˈsuːpə bəʊl / (BrE)
(slang) To defecate; to have a bowel movement (used humorously as a euphemism).
slang euphemism for defecating
slang informal american

Examples

He left the room suddenly, saying he needed to take the Browns to the Super Bowl.
She laughed and confessed she had to take the Browns to the Super Bowl before the movie started.
It became a running joke that every meeting he would disappear to take the Browns to the Super Bowl.

Synset Examples

You can always count on him to take the Browns to the Super Bowl.
Every Sunday, he claims to take the Browns to the Super Bowl.
He joked about taking the Browns to the Super Bowl for fun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to defecate - to discharge feces from the body

Etymology

Humorous reference to the Cleveland Browns (an American football team) and the color brown as a euphemism for feces.
