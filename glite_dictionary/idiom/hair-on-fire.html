hair-on-fire

1. ADJ

/ ˌhɛr ɑn ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˌheə ɒn ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
used to describe a situation or person characterized by extreme urgency, energy, or frantic activity, especially under pressure
extremely urgent or frantic
informal

Examples

Our team is adopting a hair-on-fire approach to finish the project on time.
She rushed down the hall in a hair-on-fire panic, trying to meet the deadline.
During the crisis, management maintained a hair-on-fire pace to address every issue immediately.

Synset Examples

The project manager was hair-on-fire about meeting the deadline.
His hair-on-fire attitude kept the team motivated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frantic - acting with hurried, uncontrolled energy due to worry or fear
