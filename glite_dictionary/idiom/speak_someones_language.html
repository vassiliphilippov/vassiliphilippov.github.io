to speak someone's language

1. VERB

/ spiːk ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / spiːk ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
to communicate in a way that is easily understood or appreciated by someone; to relate to someone effectively
to communicate in a way someone understands

Examples

The new manager really speaks my language when it comes to innovation and teamwork.
She felt comfortable talking to the counselor, who spoke her language about anxiety and stress.
As a teacher, it's important to speak your students' language to help them learn effectively.

Synset Examples

As a marketer, I really speak her language.
He can relate to kids; he really speaks their language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relate to - to understand and empathize with someone
