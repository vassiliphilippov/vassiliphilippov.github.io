to give a rat's arse

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ə ræts ɑrs / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə ræts ɑːs / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly British) To care at all; to be concerned in the slightest.
not care at all (British slang)
informal slang british

Examples

He really doesn’t give a rat's arse about what people think of him.
Most fans don’t give a rat's arse about management changes at the club.
She acts as if she doesn’t give a rat's arse about the criticism.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
