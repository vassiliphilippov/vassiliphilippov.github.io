on the fingers of one hand

1. X

/ ɑn ðə ˈfɪŋɡɚz əv wʌn hænd / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ˈfɪŋɡəz ɒv wʌn hænd / (BrE)
used to indicate that the number of people or things referred to is very small; easily counted.
very few in number

Examples

I can count my closest friends on the fingers of one hand.
The times I've been late to work could be counted on the fingers of one hand.
She has made mistakes so rarely that you could list them on the fingers of one hand.

Synset Examples

I can count on the fingers of one hand my true friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: few - a small number of something
  • Synonym: scarce - not enough or very small in number or amount
