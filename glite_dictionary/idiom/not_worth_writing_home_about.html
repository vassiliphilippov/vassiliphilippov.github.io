not worth writing home about

1. ADJ

/ nɑt wɜrθ ˈraɪtɪŋ hoʊm əˌbaʊt / (AmE), / nɒt wɜːθ ˈraɪtɪŋ həʊm əˌbaʊt / (BrE)
not particularly impressive, remarkable, or noteworthy; mediocre or ordinary
not impressive or noteworthy

Examples

The concert was not worth writing home about; the band seemed tired and the songs dragged on.
His performance at the meeting was not worth writing home about, but at least he showed up.
The hotel had clean rooms, but the service was not worth writing home about.

Synset Examples

The food was not worth writing home about.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mediocre - of only average quality; not very good
  • Synonym: unremarkable - not especially interesting or surprising
  • Antonym: impressive - evoking admiration through size, quality, or skill

Etymology

From the idea that only especially newsworthy events would be mentioned in a letter home.
