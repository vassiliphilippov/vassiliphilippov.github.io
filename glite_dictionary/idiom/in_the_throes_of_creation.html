in the throes of creation

1. X

/ ɪn ðə θroʊz əv kriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈθrəʊz əv kriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
deeply engaged or absorbed in a process of creating something, often with intensity or struggle
intensely engaged in creating

Examples

She was in the throes of creation and barely noticed the phone ringing.
The artist locked himself away, in the throes of creation for days on end.
While in the throes of creation, he found inspiration in the smallest details.

Synset Examples

In the throes of creation, he forgot the time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the throes of - experiencing or undergoing something very intense or difficult

Etymology

From 'throes' meaning intense struggle or pain, metaphorically applied to creative effort.
