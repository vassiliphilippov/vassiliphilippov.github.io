equal to

1. ADJ

/ ˈiːkwəl tu / (AmE), / ˈiːkwəl tʊ / (BrE)
having the necessary ability, strength, or qualities required for something
having the necessary qualities for

Examples

She was not equal to the challenge of leading the school project.
Do you think he's equal to such a demanding job?
I'm afraid I'm not equal to running a marathon this year.

Synset Examples

equal to the task
the work isn't up to the standard I require

Related Terms

  • Synonym: up to - having the required qualities or standards
