bag of tricks

1. NOUN

/ bæɡ əv trɪks / (AmE), / bæɡ əv trɪks / (BrE)
a collection of methods, resources, or techniques used to solve problems or achieve something, especially when varied or ingenious
set of methods or resources for solving problems

Examples

Every experienced teacher has a bag of tricks to keep students engaged.
The magician amazed the audience with another illusion from his bag of tricks.
Whenever a problem arises, the engineer searches her bag of tricks for a solution.

Synset Examples

every good mechanic has a large bag of tricks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toolkit - set of tools or resources for a purpose

2. NOUN

/ bæɡ əv trɪks / (AmE), / bæɡ əv trɪks / (BrE)
a set of clever methods, techniques, or resources used to solve problems or entertain
set of clever methods or resources

Examples

The manager dipped into his bag of tricks to find a new marketing idea.
Whenever she is losing an argument, she uses her bag of tricks to change the subject.
A seasoned chef has a bag of tricks for creating delicious dishes with simple ingredients.

Synset Examples

He reached into his bag of tricks for a quick solution.
The magician pulled a rabbit from his bag of tricks.
She always had a bag of tricks to entertain the kids.
