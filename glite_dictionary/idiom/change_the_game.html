to change the game

1. VERB

/ tʃeɪndʒ ðə ɡeɪm / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ ðə ɡeɪm / (BrE)
to significantly alter the situation or the established rules, often resulting in a major impact
significantly alter a situation or rules

Examples

The new medical breakthrough could change the game for patients with chronic illnesses.
Her creative marketing strategy completely changed the game for the company.
The introduction of wireless internet really changed the game in communication technology.

Synset Examples

Her innovative ideas really change the game in marketing.
This new technology will change the game for everyone.
The discovery could change the game for renewable energy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: game changer - a person or thing that dramatically changes the outcome of something
