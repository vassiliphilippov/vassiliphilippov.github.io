around the bend

1. ADJ

/ əˈraʊnd ðə bɛnd / (AmE), / əˈraʊnd ðə bend / (BrE)
(informal) Mentally unbalanced or crazy.
informal: insane, crazy
informal

Examples

Ever since the accident, some people say he's around the bend.
The long hours at the office were enough to drive anyone around the bend.
She thought her neighbor had gone around the bend when he started talking to plants.
