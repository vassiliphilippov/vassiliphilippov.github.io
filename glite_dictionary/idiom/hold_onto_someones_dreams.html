to hold onto someone's dreams

1. VERB

/ hoʊld ˈɑn tu ˈsʌmwʌnz driːmz / (AmE), / həʊld ˈɒn tuː ˈsʌmwʌnz driːmz / (BrE)
to continue to cherish or pursue one's aspirations despite difficulties or setbacks
cherish or persist in aspirations

Examples

No matter how hard things get, always hold onto your dreams.
She encouraged her son to hold onto his dreams and never lose hope.
It's important to hold onto your dreams even when others doubt you.

Synset Examples

Hold onto your dreams and never let go.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pursue one's dreams - actively try to achieve one's aspirations or goals
  • Antonym: give up on one's dreams - stop trying to achieve one's aspirations
