to stay hungry for growth

1. VERB

/ steɪ ˈhʌŋɡri fər ɡroʊθ / (AmE), / steɪ ˈhʌŋɡri fə ɡrəʊθ / (BrE)
to maintain a strong desire and determination to improve and advance
maintain strong motivation to achieve

Examples

Successful entrepreneurs often stay hungry for growth, always seeking new opportunities to expand their businesses.
It's important to stay hungry for growth if you want to reach your full potential in your career.
The team's ability to stay hungry for growth helped them achieve record-breaking results this year.

Synset Examples

In business, it's important to stay hungry for growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambitious - having a strong desire for success or achievement
  • Antonym: complacent - satisfied with the current situation and not wanting to change or improve it
