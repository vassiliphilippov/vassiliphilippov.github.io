to brighten one's day

1. VERB

/ ˈbraɪtn̩ wʌnz deɪ / (AmE), / ˈbraɪtn wʌnz deɪ / (BrE)
to make someone feel happier or more cheerful
make someone feel happier

Examples

Her kind words always brighten my day when I'm feeling down.
A simple smile from a stranger can brighten your day unexpectedly.
That surprise gift really brightened his day after a tough week.

Synset Examples

The fanny merchant brightened our day with her jokes.
Stacy always knows how to brighten my day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cheer up - to make or become happier
  • Antonym: to depress - to make someone feel sad or discouraged
