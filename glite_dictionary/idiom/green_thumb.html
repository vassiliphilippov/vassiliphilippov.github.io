green thumb

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡrin ˈθʌm / (AmE), / ˌɡriːn ˈθʌm / (BrE)
an exceptional ability to make plants grow well, especially easily or with apparent natural skill
talent for making plants grow

Examples

My grandmother has a real green thumb; her garden is always bursting with flowers.
Even with a green thumb, some plants can be tricky to keep alive indoors.
He’s known for his green thumb and gives gardening tips to all his neighbors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gardener - a person who tends and cultivates a garden as a pastime or for a living
