all bark and no bite

1. X

/ ɔl bɑrk ənd noʊ baɪt / (AmE), / ɔːl bɑːk ənd nəʊ baɪt / (BrE)
used to describe someone who talks in a threatening way but does not take action or cause harm
talks tough but does not act

Examples

The manager is all bark and no bite, so the staff aren't afraid of him.
My big brother sounds intimidating, but he’s really all bark and no bite.
Many politicians are all bark and no bite when it comes to real reform.

Synset Examples

Don't worry about him; he's just all bark and no bite.
He talks tough, but he's all bark and no bite.
Her threats are all bark and no bite, trust me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bluster - loud, aggressive, or indignant talk with little effect
