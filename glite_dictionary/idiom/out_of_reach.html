out of reach

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv ritʃ / (AmE), / aʊt əv riːtʃ / (BrE)
not able to be accessed, obtained, or achieved; unattainable or inaccessible at present
not accessible or attainable

Examples

For many young people, home ownership feels out of reach these days.
She placed the documents just out of reach so her toddler couldn't grab them.
With the deadline extended, success was no longer out of reach for the team.

Synset Examples

The top shelf is always out of reach for me.
That luxury car is just out of reach for my budget.
His dreams seem out of reach right now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unattainable - not able to be achieved or reached
  • Antonym: within reach - able to be reached or attained
