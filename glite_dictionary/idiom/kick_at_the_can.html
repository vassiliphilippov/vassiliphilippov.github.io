to kick at the can

1. VERB

/ kɪk æt ðə kæn / (AmE), / kɪk ət ðə kan / (BrE)
to make another attempt at something, especially after a previous failure or to try again
to try something again

Examples

After failing to get the job, he decided to kick at the can and apply once more.
Despite the setback, the team is ready to kick at the can next season.
She wasn't discouraged by rejection and wanted to kick at the can again.

Synset Examples

She decided to kick at the can one more time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to try again - to make another attempt at something
