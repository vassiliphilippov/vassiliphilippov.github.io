after the dust settles

1. SCONJ

/ ˈæf.tɚ ðə dʌst ˈsɛt.əlz / (AmE), / ˈɑːf.tə ðə dʌst ˈsɛt.əlz / (BrE)
when a situation becomes clear or calm after a period of confusion or activity
when things become clear or calm

Examples

Let's discuss our next steps after the dust settles from the recent changes.
Many businesses wait after the dust settles before making investments during uncertain times.
After the dust settles, we'll have a better idea of the company's future direction.

Synset Examples

The project will be reviewed after the dust settles.
Once the dust settles, we can evaluate our options.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: when the dust settles - when things have calmed down and the situation is clearer
