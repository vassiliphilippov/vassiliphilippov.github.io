broken heart

1. NOUN

/ ˈbroʊ.kən hɑrt / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊ.kən hɑːt / (BrE)
A state of deep emotional pain or grief, especially caused by the loss of a loved one or great disappointment.
emotional pain from loss or disappointment

Examples

After the end of her long relationship, she was left with a broken heart.
Songs about a broken heart often resonate with anyone who's experienced loss.
It took him years to recover from the broken heart he suffered after his father's death.

Synset Examples

He returned home to a broken heart and shattered dreams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartbreak - intense sorrow or grief, especially from romantic loss
