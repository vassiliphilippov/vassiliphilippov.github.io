blood on someone's head

1. NOUN

/ blʌd ɑn ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / blʌd ɒn ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (BrE)
responsibility for someone else's misfortune or death
bearing responsibility for harm or loss

Examples

If you don't warn them, any injury will be blood on your head.
She feared she'd have blood on her head if the risky operation failed.
The judge said the defendant had blood on his head for the tragic accident.

Synset Examples

If this plan fails, you'll have his blood on your head.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blood on one's hands - responsibility for causing someone's death or harm
