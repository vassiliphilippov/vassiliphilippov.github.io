king of the hill

1. NOUN

/ kɪŋ əv ðə hɪl / (AmE), / kɪŋ əv ðə hɪl / (BrE)
a person, group, or organization that is in a dominant, leading, or superior position compared to others, especially after competition
person in a dominant or top position

Examples

After the company merger, she became king of the hill in her department.
He trained hard to become king of the hill in the boxing world.
They all competed for the promotion, but only one could be king of the hill.

Synset Examples

He climbed to the top, feeling like the king of the hill.
After winning the game, he was king of the hill.
In the office, she always aims to be king of the hill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top dog - a person who is dominant or victorious
  • Antonym: underdog - a competitor thought to have little chance of winning
