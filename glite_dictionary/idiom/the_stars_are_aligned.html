the stars are aligned

1. X

/ ðə stɑrz ɑr əˈlaɪnd / (AmE), / ðə stɑːz ə ˈlaɪnd / (BrE)
circumstances are especially favorable for a particular event or outcome
circumstances are favorable

Examples

With the market booming and investors lining up, the stars are aligned for our company's expansion.
It felt like the stars are aligned when she found her dream job just after graduating.
Whenever the stars are aligned, everything seems to go smoothly and effortlessly.

Synset Examples

When the stars are aligned, opportunities arise effortlessly.
I believe the stars are aligned for our success.
It seems the stars are aligned for my promotion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in your favor - circumstances supporting your success
  • Antonym: the odds are stacked against you - circumstances are unfavorable

Etymology

Alludes to the ancient belief that the positions of stars influence events on Earth.
