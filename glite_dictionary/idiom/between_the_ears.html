between the ears

1. X

/ bɪˈtwin ðə ɪrz / (AmE), / bɪˈtwiːn ði ˈɪəz / (BrE)
referring to a person's mind or mental capabilities, as opposed to external factors
relating to mental capacity

Examples

Her confidence comes from what happens between the ears, not just her physical skills.
Sometimes the biggest challenges in sports are between the ears.
He realized the real barrier to learning was between the ears, not the curriculum.

Synset Examples

He realized the issue was between the ears, not the equipment.
Success is often just a matter of what's between the ears.
The problem is often between the ears, not the tools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in one's head - existing or happening in a person's mind
