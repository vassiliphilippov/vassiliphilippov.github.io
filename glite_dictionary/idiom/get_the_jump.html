to get the jump

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ðə dʒʌmp / (AmE), / ɡɛt ðə dʒʌmp / (BrE)
to be first to act or gain an advantage over others
to act first; gain an advantage

Examples

We need to get the jump on the market before our competitors catch up.
If you wake up early, you can get the jump on your studies for the day.
They managed to get the jump on everyone by releasing their product a week early.

Synset Examples

They had gotten the jump on their competitors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get a head start - to start before others
  • Synonym: to beat to the punch - to act before someone else
