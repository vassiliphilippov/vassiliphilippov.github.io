mad about

1. ADJ

/ mæd əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / mad əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
extremely enthusiastic or passionate about someone or something.
very enthusiastic or passionate
informal british

Examples

She's absolutely mad about jazz and listens to it day and night.
Ever since they met, he's been mad about her.
People in the office are mad about the new coffee machine.

Synset Examples

He was mad about the new video game release.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy about - extremely enthusiastic or infatuated with someone or something
  • Synonym: keen on - enthusiastic about or interested in something
