if you know, you know

1. X

/ ɪf ju noʊ ju noʊ / (AmE), / ɪf juː nəʊ juː nəʊ / (BrE)
used to indicate that only people with specific knowledge or experience will understand what is meant
expression for shared or insider knowledge
informal

Examples

Everyone at the concert cheered during that song—if you know, you know.
There's a little bakery downtown that makes the best croissants—if you know, you know.
Some moments in life are hard to explain, but if you know, you know.

Synset Examples

The joke is inside; if you know, you know.

It's a secret only a few understand; if you know, you know.
