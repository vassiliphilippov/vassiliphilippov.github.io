not going to lie

1. X

/ nɑt ˈɡoʊɪŋ tə laɪ / (AmE), / nɒt ˈɡəʊɪŋ tə laɪ / (BrE)
used to indicate that the following statement is sincere, honest, or blunt, even if it may be unexpected or unpopular
introducing a sincere or honest opinion
informal

Examples

Not going to lie, I was really nervous before my interview.
Not going to lie, this is the best cake I've ever tasted.
Not going to lie, I didn't expect you to finish the project so quickly.

Synset Examples

I'm not going to lie; that movie was terrible.
