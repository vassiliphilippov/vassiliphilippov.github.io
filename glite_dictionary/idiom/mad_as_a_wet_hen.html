mad as a wet hen

1. ADJ

/ mæd æz ə wɛt hɛn / (AmE), / mad az ə wet hɛn / (BrE)
extremely angry or upset
very angry or upset (idiom)

Examples

After hearing the unfair decision, he was mad as a wet hen and stormed out of the room.
She gets mad as a wet hen if someone touches her things without asking.
The neighbors were mad as a wet hen about the noise from the party last night.

Synset Examples

He felt mad as a wet hen after losing the game.
She was mad as a wet hen when dinner was late.
They were mad as a wet hen about the surprise change.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: furious - extremely angry
  • Degree Lower: angry - having strong feelings of displeasure
