cold as a witch's tit

1. ADJ

/ koʊld æz ə wɪtʃɪz tɪt / (AmE), / kəʊld az ə wɪtʃɪz tɪt / (BrE)
(informal) Extremely cold; unpleasantly or intensely cold.
extremely cold (idiomatic, informal)
informal vulgar

Examples

The basement was cold as a witch's tit all winter long.
When we stepped outside, it felt cold as a witch's tit in the wind.
The old cabin gets cold as a witch's tit once the sun goes down.

Synset Examples
