to eat one's gun

1. VERB

/ it wʌnz ɡʌn / (AmE), / iːt wʌnz ɡʌn / (BrE)
(informal) to contemplate or express suicidal thoughts out of extreme frustration or hopelessness, often used in a darkly humorous or exaggerated way.
express suicidal frustration or hopelessness (idiom)
informal

Examples

After another failed job interview, he joked that he might eat his gun.
When life gets overwhelming, Sarah sometimes threatens to eat her gun, though everyone knows she's exaggerating.
The comedian said he would eat his gun if he had to sit through another boring meeting.

Synset Examples

During tough times, he often says he'd eat his gun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: end it all - to commit suicide, especially by one's own hand
