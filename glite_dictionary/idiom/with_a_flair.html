with a flair

1. ADV

/ wɪð ə flɛr / (AmE), / wɪð ə fleə / (BrE)
In a stylish, creative, or distinctive manner; showing skill or originality.
in a stylish or creative manner

Examples

She decorated the room with a flair that impressed all her friends.
The chef prepares every dish with a flair that makes dining a memorable experience.
He gave his presentation with a flair that captivated the entire audience.

Synset Examples

He scored that goal with a flair from the East German judge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: with style - in a stylish or impressive manner
  • Synonym: with panache - in a confident and flamboyant way
