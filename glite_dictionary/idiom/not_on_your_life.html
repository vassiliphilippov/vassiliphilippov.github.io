not on your life

1. X

/ nɑt ɑn jʊr laɪf / (AmE), / nɒt ɒn jɔː laɪf / (BrE)
used to emphasize that you would never do something or that something will definitely not happen, under any circumstances
emphatic refusal or denial

Examples

Would I try skydiving? Not on your life!
You want to borrow my car? Not on your life.
He asked if I’d help him cheat—not on your life.

Synset Examples

He won't change his mind, not on your life.
I’m not doing that, not on your life!
I would not go back there, not on your life!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: not a chance - absolutely no possibility; refusal
  • Synonym: over my dead body - used to assert that something will not happen
