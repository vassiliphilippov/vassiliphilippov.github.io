like Bambi on ice

1. X

/ laɪk ˈbæmbi ɑn aɪs / (AmE), / laɪk ˈbambi ɒn aɪs / (BrE)
moving in an awkward or uncoordinated way, especially as someone inexperienced or unsure.
moving awkwardly or clumsily

Examples

After twisting his ankle, he walked like Bambi on ice across the room.
On her first day skiing, she looked like Bambi on ice going down the beginners' slope.
The puppy chased the ball across the tiled floor, slipping like Bambi on ice.

Synset Examples

He moved like Bambi on ice during his first skate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or action; lacking coordination

Etymology

From the animated film 'Bambi' (1942) by Walt Disney, referencing a scene where the young deer Bambi slips on ice.
