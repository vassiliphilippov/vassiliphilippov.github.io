to run off with someone's heart

1. VERB

/ rʌn ɔf wɪð ˈsʌmwʌnz hɑrt / (AmE), / rʌn ɒf wɪð ˈsʌmwʌnz hɑːt / (BrE)
to win someone's affection or love, often suddenly or completely
to win someone's love

Examples

It's a classic story—someone arrives in town and runs off with everyone's heart with their kindness.
