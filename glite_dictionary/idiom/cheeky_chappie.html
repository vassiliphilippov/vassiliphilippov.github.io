cheeky chappie

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃiki ˈtʃæpi / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːki ˈtʃæpi / (BrE)
a lively, playful, and mischievous young man who is charming despite his cheekiness, especially in British English
playful and mischievous young man
british informal

Examples

He plays the role of the cheeky chappie in most of his films, always making people laugh.
Everyone knows Tom as the cheeky chappie who lightens up any gathering.
The cheeky chappie winked at the audience before delivering his next joke.

Synset Examples

Everyone loves his cheeky chappie attitude at work.
He's known as the cheeky chappie of the group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rascal - a mischievous or cheeky person, especially a child or man
  • Synonym: scamp - a person, especially a child, who is mischievous in a likable or amusing way
