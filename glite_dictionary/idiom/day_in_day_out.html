day in, day out

1. ADV

/ ˌdeɪ ɪn deɪ ˈaʊt / (AmE), / ˌdeɪ ɪn deɪ ˈaʊt / (BrE)
(informal) Continuously or repeatedly over a long period of time; every day for a long time, often with a sense of monotony.
continuously over a long time
informal

Examples

He goes to the same office day in, day out, never taking a break.
I hear the same complaints day in, day out, and it gets exhausting.
They work hard day in, day out to support their family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: night and day - continuously, all the time without stopping
