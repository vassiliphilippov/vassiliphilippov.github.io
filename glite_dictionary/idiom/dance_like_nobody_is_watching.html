to dance like nobody is watching

1. VERB

/ dæns laɪk ˈnoʊˌbɑːdi ɪz ˈwɑːtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / dɑːns laɪk ˈnəʊˌbədi ɪz ˈwɒtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
to dance freely and joyfully without worrying about being observed or judged by others
dance freely without self-consciousness

Examples

She decided to dance like nobody is watching and soon the whole room joined in her energy.
Whenever he feels stressed, Tom puts on music and dances like nobody is watching.
If you want to truly enjoy life, sometimes you just need to dance like nobody is watching.

Synset Examples

At the party, Billy Wigglestick danced like nobody was watching.
