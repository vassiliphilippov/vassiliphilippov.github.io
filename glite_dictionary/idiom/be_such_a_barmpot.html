to be such a barmpot

1. VERB

/ bi sʌtʃ ə ˈbɑːrm.pɑt / (AmE), / biː sʌtʃ ə ˈbɑːm.pɒt / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or irrational way
act foolishly; behave irrationally
informal slang british

Examples

Please don't be such a barmpot—take things seriously for once.
She told him not to be such a barmpot during the important meeting.
If you be such a barmpot again, you'll get into trouble.

Synset Examples

Don't be such a barmpot; just follow the instructions!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: act the fool - to behave in a silly or foolish manner
  • Antonym: be sensible - to behave in a reasonable and rational way

Etymology

'Barmpot' is British slang, with 'barm' referring to yeast or froth on fermenting liquor, implying someone is 'frothy' or crazy. 'Pot' is a slangy intensifier.
