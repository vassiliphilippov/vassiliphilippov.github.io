castle in Spain

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæsəl ɪn speɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɑːsəl ɪn speɪn / (BrE)
An idle or fanciful dream; an impractical or unattainable hope or plan.
unrealistic or fanciful dream

Examples

He spends most of his time building castles in Spain instead of taking action.
Her plans for instant fame were just castles in Spain.
It's fine to dream, but don't let castles in Spain distract you from real opportunities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pipe dream - an unrealistic or unattainable hope or plan
  • Synonym: daydream - pleasant thoughts not based in reality, often while awake
  • Antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist

Etymology

From the old French expression 'châteaux en Espagne', referring to fanciful, unattainable projects.
