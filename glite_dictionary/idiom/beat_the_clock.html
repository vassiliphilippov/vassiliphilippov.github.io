to beat the clock

1. VERB

/ bit ðə klɑk / (AmE), / biːt ðə klɒk / (BrE)
to succeed in finishing something before a deadline or before time runs out
finish before a deadline

Examples

We had to beat the clock to get to the airport before check-in closed.
If you want the bonus, you must beat the clock and submit your work now.
The chef struggled to beat the clock, preparing all the dishes before the guests arrived.

Synset Examples

He raced to beat the clock before the deadline.
We need to beat the clock to make the delivery on time.
She managed to beat the clock and finish her project early.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make it in time - to succeed in doing something before the appointed time
  • Antonym: miss the deadline - to fail to finish something before the deadline
