right up someone's street

1. X

/ raɪt ʌp ˈsʌmwʌnz strit / (AmE), / raɪt ʌp ˈsʌmwʌnz striːt / (BrE)
exactly suited to someone's interests, tastes, or abilities
well-suited to someone's interests or abilities
informal british

Examples

If you love art and history, this museum will be right up your street.
That new science fiction series sounds right up his street.
A job in environmental conservation would be right up her street.

Synset Examples

This kind of music is right up my street.
Cooking Italian food is right up her street.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: right up someone's alley - something that suits one's tastes or interests (chiefly American)
