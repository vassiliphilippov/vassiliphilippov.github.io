of a certain age

1. ADJ

/ əv ə ˈsɝːtn̩ eɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɒv ə ˈsɜːtn eɪdʒ / (BrE)
used to describe someone who is no longer young, especially someone who is middle-aged or older, often in a tactful or euphemistic way.
not young; middle-aged or older (tactfully expressed)

Examples

Many of the club members are gentlemen of a certain age who have known each other for years.
She is an actress of a certain age, still active and admired by many fans.
Finding stylish clothing can be difficult for women of a certain age.

Synset Examples

He prefers classic movies, being of a certain age.
