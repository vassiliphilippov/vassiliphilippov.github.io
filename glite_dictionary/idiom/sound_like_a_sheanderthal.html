to sound like a sheanderthal

1. VERB

/ saʊnd laɪk ə ʃiːˈæn.dərˌθæl / (AmE), / saʊnd laɪk ə ʃiːˈæn.dəˌθal / (BrE)
to appear or be regarded as having outdated, primitive, or backward views, especially regarding gender equality.
seem to have primitive views, esp. on gender
informal slang

Examples

He really sounds like a sheanderthal when he talks about women's roles at work.
Don't sound like a sheanderthal—it's the 21st century now!
Whenever he complains about modern relationships, he sounds like a sheanderthal.

Synset Examples

His outdated views make him sound like a sheanderthal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Neanderthal - a person with very old-fashioned attitudes, especially one with primitive views

Etymology

Blend of 'she' and 'Neanderthal'; 'Neanderthal' refers to an extinct species of human, often used metaphorically for someone with primitive views.
