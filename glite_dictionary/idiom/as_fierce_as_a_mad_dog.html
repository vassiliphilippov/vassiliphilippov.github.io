as fierce as a mad dog

1. X

/ əz fɪrs əz ə mæd dɔɡ / (AmE), / əz fɪəs əz ə mæd dɒɡ / (BrE)
extremely aggressive or threatening in manner or behavior
extremely aggressive; very threatening

Examples

When he yelled at the referee, he looked as fierce as a mad dog.
The manager stormed into the room as fierce as a mad dog, demanding explanations.
During the argument, she spoke as fierce as a mad dog, not holding back her anger.

Synset Examples

The stray dog looked as fierce as a mad dog.
