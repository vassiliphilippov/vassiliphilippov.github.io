a hundred and ten percent

1. NOUN

/ ə ˈhʌndrəd ənd tɛn pərˈsɛnt / (AmE), / ə ˈhʌndrəd ənd tɛn pəˈsɛnt / (BrE)
used to indicate giving more than the maximum possible effort or commitment; to do something with absolute dedication
maximum or excessive effort; complete commitment

Examples

If you want to succeed on this team, you need to give a hundred and ten percent every day.
Her dedication means she's always putting in a hundred and ten percent, no matter the challenge.
The coach encourages the players to try a hundred and ten percent during every practice.

Synset Examples

I need you to try a hundred and ten percent this time.
She gives a hundred and ten percent in every task.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: give your all - to put forth all one's effort; to try one's best
  • Synonym: go the extra mile - to do more than what is expected or required
