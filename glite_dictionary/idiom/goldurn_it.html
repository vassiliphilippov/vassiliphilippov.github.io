goldurn it

1. INTJ

/ ɡoʊlˈdɜrn ɪt / (AmE), / ɡəʊlˈdɜːn ɪt / (BrE)
An exclamation used to express annoyance or mild frustration.
expression of annoyance or frustration
archaic

Examples

I missed the bus again, goldurn it!
Goldurn it, that's the third time I've dropped my phone today.
Goldurn it, I can't remember where I put my glasses.

Synset Examples

I lost my keys again, goldurn it!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doggone it - exclamation of annoyance or mild anger
  • Synonym: darn it - mild exclamation of annoyance

Etymology

Alteration of 'God darn it' as a euphemism to avoid profanity.
