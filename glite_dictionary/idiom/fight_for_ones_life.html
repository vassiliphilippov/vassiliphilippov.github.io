to fight for one's life

1. VERB

/ faɪt fər wʌnz laɪf / (AmE), / faɪt fə wʌnz laɪf / (BrE)
to make a great effort to survive a dangerous or life-threatening situation
struggle to survive; make effort to avoid death

Examples

After the crash, he had to fight for his life in the hospital.
Firefighters were fighting for their lives as the flames surrounded them.
Despite the odds, she continued to fight for her life during the illness.

Synset Examples

He had to fight for his life after the accident.
She was determined to fight for her life against cancer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: struggle for survival - make great efforts to stay alive
