under the covers

1. ADV

/ ˈʌndɚ ðə ˈkʌvɚz / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ðə ˈkʌvəz / (BrE)
in bed and covered by blankets, usually for warmth or comfort
in bed, covered by blankets

Examples

On cold winter nights, I love staying under the covers and reading a good book.
The children hid under the covers when they heard the thunder.
Sometimes it's tempting to remain under the covers on a rainy morning.

Synset Examples

They cuddled under the covers during the storm.
She read her book under the covers.
He fell asleep under the covers, dreaming peacefully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in bed - lying or resting in bed
