built like a brick outhouse

1. ADJ

/ bɪlt laɪk ə brɪk ˈaʊthaʊs / (AmE), / bɪlt laɪk ə brɪk ˈaʊthaʊs / (BrE)
(of a person) very strong and muscular, with a solid and powerful build.
very strong or muscular (person)
informal

Examples

Everyone said the new security guard was built like a brick outhouse.
