full as a goog

1. ADJ

/ fʊl æz ə ɡuɡ / (AmE), / fʊl æz ə ɡuːɡ / (BrE)
extremely full or satisfied after eating, especially to the point of discomfort
extremely full after eating
informal

Examples

After eating the huge Thanksgiving dinner, I was full as a goog and could barely move.
He declared himself full as a goog after the three-course meal.
I'm full as a goog, so dessert will have to wait until later.

Synset Examples

He was full as a goog after the buffet.
After the feast, I felt full as a goog.
I can't eat another bite; I'm full as a goog.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stuffed - very full after eating
  • Synonym: satiated - completely satisfied, especially with food

Etymology

Australian informal origin; 'goog' is an old slang term for 'egg', meaning 'full as an egg'.
