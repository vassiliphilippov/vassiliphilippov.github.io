queer bird

1. NOUN

/ kwɪr bɝːd / (AmE), / kwɪə bɜːd / (BrE)
a person who is considered eccentric, odd, or unusual in behavior or character
eccentric or odd person
informal

Examples

Everyone in the office thinks old Mr. Jenkins is a real queer bird.
She’s a bit of a queer bird, but that’s what makes her interesting.
You meet all sorts of queer birds at these art gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - a person with unconventional or odd habits
  • Synonym: odd duck - an unusual or eccentric person

Etymology

From 'queer' (strange, odd) + 'bird' (person, often used in informal expressions).
