hard on the eyes

1. ADJ

/ hɑrd ɑn ði aɪz / (AmE), / hɑːd ɒn ðiː aɪz / (BrE)
unpleasant or straining to look at; not visually appealing or comfortable.
unpleasant or straining to look at

Examples

The new neon sign is hard on the eyes when you pass by at night.
Some websites use color combinations that are hard on the eyes and difficult to read.
Even though the wallpaper was expensive, its bold pattern was hard on the eyes.

Synset Examples

The bright lights are hard on the eyes in here.
That color scheme is really hard on the eyes.
Her dress was beautiful but quite hard on the eyes.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: easy on the eyes - pleasant or attractive to look at
