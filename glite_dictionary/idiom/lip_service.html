lip service

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪp ˌsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / ˈlɪp ˌsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
An insincere expression of approval or support, not backed by genuine feelings or actions.
insincere support or agreement

Examples

The company's commitment to diversity turned out to be just lip service.
He paid lip service to environmental issues but never supported real change.
Politicians sometimes give lip service to reform without taking meaningful action.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: empty words - insincere or meaningless talk
