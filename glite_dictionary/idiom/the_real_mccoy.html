the real McCoy

1. NOUN

/ ðə ɹiəl məˈkɔɪ / (AmE), / ðə ɹiːəl məˈkɔɪ / (BrE)
the genuine article; something that is authentic or original and not a copy or imitation
the genuine article; authentic thing

Examples

Experts confirmed that the signed baseball was the real McCoy.
Accept no substitutes—this is the real McCoy when it comes to luxury watches.
Is that antique clock the real McCoy, or just a clever reproduction?

Synset Examples

This vintage car is the real McCoy.
Her handmade jewelry is the real McCoy.
I want the real McCoy, not a cheap imitation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the real thing - the genuine or authentic article, not a copy
  • Synonym: genuine - truly what something is said to be; authentic
  • Antonym: imitation - a copy intended to resemble something authentic

Etymology

Probably from Scottish English, possibly named after the prizefighter Kid McCoy or the Scottish engineer Elijah McCoy, whose inventions were highly valued.
