in high spirits

1. ADJ

/ ɪn haɪ ˈspɪrɪts / (AmE), / ɪn haɪ ˈspɪərɪts / (BrE)
feeling cheerful, happy, and energetic; in a good mood
cheerful and energetic mood

Examples

She arrived at the party in high spirits after hearing the good news.
The team was in high spirits following their big win.
By the end of the celebrations, everyone was clearly in high spirits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheerful - happy and positive in attitude
  • Synonym: elated - very happy and excited
  • Antonym: downcast - sad or depressed
