to throw an eye

1. VERB

/ θroʊ ən aɪ / (AmE), / θrəʊ ən aɪ / (BrE)
to look quickly or briefly at something; to glance
look quickly or briefly

Examples

Could you throw an eye on these documents before the meeting?
She asked me to throw an eye over her report for mistakes.
I'll throw an eye at the schedule and let you know if there are any conflicts.

Synset Examples

Let's throw an eye on the new proposals.
Can you throw an eye on my presentation?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to glance at - to take a brief or hurried look
  • Degree Lower: to look at - to direct one’s gaze toward something
