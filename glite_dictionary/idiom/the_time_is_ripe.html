the time is ripe

1. X

/ ðə taɪm ɪz raɪp / (AmE), / ðə taɪm ɪz raɪp / (BrE)
the moment is ideal or appropriate for taking action or making a change
ideal moment for action or change

Examples

After years of planning, the time is ripe to launch our new business.
Many investors believe the time is ripe to enter the market now.
When change is necessary, the time is ripe for bold decisions.

Synset Examples

Now that she’s graduated, the time is ripe for travel.
The time is ripe for new opportunities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the moment is right - the present time is suitable for something

Etymology

From the metaphor of fruit being ripe when it is ready for picking.
