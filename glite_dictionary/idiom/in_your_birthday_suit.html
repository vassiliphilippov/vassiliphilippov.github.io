in your birthday suit

1. ADJ

/ ɪn jər ˈbɝːθ.deɪ suːt / (AmE), / ɪn jɔː ˈbɜːθ.deɪ suːt / (BrE)
completely naked; wearing no clothes at all
completely naked

Examples

He answered the door in his birthday suit, shocking the delivery driver.
After the swim, the children ran around the locker room in their birthday suits.
Caught in the hallway in her birthday suit, she quickly grabbed a towel to cover up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naked - without clothes; not covered
  • Antonym: clothed - wearing clothes

Etymology

Alludes humorously to being as unclothed as on the day of birth.
