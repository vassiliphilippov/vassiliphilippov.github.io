dressed to kill

1. ADJ

/ drɛst tə kɪl / (AmE), / drɛst tə kɪl / (BrE)
wearing fancy or strikingly fashionable clothing, intended to attract notice or admiration
wearing very stylish or attention-grabbing clothes

Examples

She arrived at the party dressed to kill in a shimmering red gown.
Whenever he has an important meeting, he makes sure he's dressed to kill.
They were both dressed to kill for the awards ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressed to the nines - dressed very elegantly or smartly
  • Antonym: casually dressed - wearing informal or everyday clothes
