to knock for a loop

1. VERB

/ nɑk fər ə lup / (AmE), / nɒk fə ə luːp / (BrE)
to surprise or shock someone greatly
to shock or surprise someone

Examples

The announcement of the merger knocked me for a loop and left me speechless.
It really knocked him for a loop when he learned he had won the lottery.
The sudden resignation of the CEO knocked investors for a loop and shook the company.

Synset Examples

Losing the match knocked him for a loop.

Related Terms
