at the disposal of

1. ADP

/ æt ðə dɪˈspoʊzəl əv / (AmE), / ət ðə dɪˈspəʊzəl ɒv / (BrE)
available for someone to use or control as they wish
available for someone's use or control

Examples

The company placed a car at the disposal of the visiting executive.
All the research data is at the disposal of our team whenever we need it.
During emergencies, the entire staff is at the disposal of the crisis management unit.

Synset Examples

You have my expertise at the disposal of your needs.
The resources are at the disposal of the project team.
Funds are at the disposal of the charity for aid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: available - able to be used or obtained
