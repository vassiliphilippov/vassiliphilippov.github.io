daily grind

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪli ˈɡraɪnd / (AmE), / ˈdeɪli ˈɡraɪnd / (BrE)
the monotonous and tiring routine of daily work or chores
the routine of daily work

Examples

After coming home from the daily grind, he liked to relax with a good book.

Many people dream of escaping the daily grind for a more adventurous life.
