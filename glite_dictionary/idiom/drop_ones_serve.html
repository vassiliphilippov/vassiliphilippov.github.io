to drop one's serve

1. VERB

/ drɑp wʌnz sɝv / (AmE), / drɒp wʌnz sɜːv / (BrE)
(in tennis) to lose a game in which one is serving.
lose a tennis game while serving

Examples

Despite playing well, she dropped her serve twice in the final set.
If you drop your serve early, it can be hard to recover in a close match.
He hasn't dropped his serve the entire tournament.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hold serve - win a game in which one is serving (in tennis)
