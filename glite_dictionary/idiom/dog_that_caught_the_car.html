dog that caught the car

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ ðət kɑt ðə kɑr / (AmE), / dɒɡ ðət kɒt ðə kɑː / (BrE)
A person who achieves a long-sought goal but is unprepared for the consequences or unsure how to proceed afterward.
succeed but unprepared for outcome

Examples

After years of pursuing the promotion, he finally got it and felt like a dog that caught the car.
She campaigned hard for the role but was a dog that caught the car when faced with its challenges.
Winning the lottery left him feeling like a dog that caught the car, uncertain about his next steps.

Synset Examples

She was the dog that caught the car, unsure what to do next.
Winning feels great, but I'm like a dog that caught the car.
