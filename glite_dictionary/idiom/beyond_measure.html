beyond measure

1. ADV

/ biˈjɑnd ˈmɛʒər / (AmE), / bɪˈjɒnd ˈmɛʒə / (BrE)
to a very great extent; without limit or in excess
to a great or unlimited extent

Examples

She was delighted beyond measure when she saw her friends waiting for her.
His generosity was beyond measure, helping anyone in need.
The team's victory pleased their supporters beyond measure.

Synset Examples

amazed beyond measure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immeasurably - to a degree that is too great to be measured
