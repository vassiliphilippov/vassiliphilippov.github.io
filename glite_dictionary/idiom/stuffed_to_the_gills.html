stuffed to the gills

1. ADJ

/ stʌft tə ðə ɡɪlz / (AmE), / stʌft tə ðə ɡɪlz / (BrE)
Extremely full or overfilled; packed to capacity.
very full or overfilled
informal

Examples

After the Thanksgiving meal, I was stuffed to the gills and couldn't eat another bite.
The train was stuffed to the gills with commuters during rush hour.
Her backpack was stuffed to the gills with textbooks and notes.

Synset Examples

After the buffet, I was stuffed to the gills.
The suitcase was stuffed to the gills with clothes.
He was stuffed to the gills after dinner.
The classroom was stuffed to the gills with students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: packed - filled tightly with things or people
  • Synonym: crammed - filled completely so that there is no more room
  • Antonym: empty - having nothing inside; not filled
