to act like a rockstar

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈrɑkˌstɑr / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈrɒkˌstɑː / (BrE)
to behave in a flamboyant, extravagant, or attention-seeking manner, like a famous rock musician
behave flamboyantly or extravagantly

Examples

When she won the award, she started to act like a rockstar and demanded special treatment.
He tends to act like a rockstar whenever he walks into a room, drawing everyone's attention.
Instead of staying humble after the promotion, Jim began to act like a rockstar at work.

Synset Examples

Every band member wanted to act like a rockstar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to show off - to behave in a way intended to attract attention or admiration
  • Antonym: to play it cool - to behave in a calm or restrained manner
