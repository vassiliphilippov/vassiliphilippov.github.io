cold as balls

1. ADJ

/ koʊld æz bɔlz / (AmE), / kəʊld æz bɔːlz / (BrE)
(slang) Extremely cold; used to emphasize how very cold something is, especially in a humorous or exaggerated way.
extremely cold (slang, humorous)
slang informal american

Examples

It was cold as balls in that movie theater, so I had to wear my jacket the whole time.
After walking home in the snow, my feet felt cold as balls and numb.
Camping in the mountains during winter is cold as balls—make sure to bring extra blankets!

Synset Examples

His hands felt cold as balls after being outside.
The water in the lake was cold as balls today.
The wind was cold as balls during our hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freezing - extremely cold
  • Antonym: boiling - extremely hot (slang)
