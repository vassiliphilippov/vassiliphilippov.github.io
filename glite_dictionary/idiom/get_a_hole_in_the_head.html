to get a hole in the head

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ə hoʊl ɪn ðə hɛd / (AmE), / ɡɛt ə həʊl ɪn ðə hɛd / (BrE)
to do something that is extremely foolish or unnecessary
do something extremely foolish

Examples

Volunteering for extra work now would be like getting a hole in the head.
She said buying that old car would be getting a hole in the head.
You'd have to get a hole in the head to trust him with your money!

Synset Examples

Going back now would be like getting a hole in the head.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment
  • Antonym: wise - showing good judgment
