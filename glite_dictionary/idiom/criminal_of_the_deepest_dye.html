criminal of the deepest dye

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪmɪnəl əv ðə ˈdiːpɪst daɪ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmɪnəl əv ðə ˈdiːpɪst daɪ / (BrE)
A person who is extremely immoral or wicked in their criminal actions.
extremely wicked or immoral criminal

Examples

The judge described him as a criminal of the deepest dye who showed no remorse.
She warned that the suspect was a criminal of the deepest dye and a danger to society.
History remembers him as a criminal of the deepest dye, responsible for countless atrocities.

Synset Examples

He was a criminal of the deepest dye, unrepentant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archcriminal - a major or chief criminal
  • Antonym: saint - a person of exceptional holiness or moral virtue

Etymology

From the use of 'dye' to refer metaphorically to the degree of moral staining or guilt.
