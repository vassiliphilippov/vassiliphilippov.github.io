wild as a wet hen

1. ADJ

/ waɪld əz ə wɛt hɛn / (AmE), / waɪld əz ə wɛt hɛn / (BrE)
extremely angry or agitated
extremely angry or agitated (idiom)

Examples

When he realized he had missed the train, he was wild as a wet hen.
The customer became wild as a wet hen after discovering the mistake on her bill.
She was wild as a wet hen when she found out someone had taken her parking spot.

Synset Examples

He got wild as a wet hen when the game ended.

Related Terms
