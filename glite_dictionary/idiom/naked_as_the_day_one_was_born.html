naked as the day one was born

1. ADJ

/ ˈneɪkɪd æz ðə deɪ wʌn wəz bɔrn / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪd əz ðə deɪ wɒn wəz bɔːn / (BrE)
completely naked; having no clothes on, as at the moment of birth
completely naked

Examples

He stepped out of the shower naked as the day one was born and reached for a towel.
The statue depicts a figure naked as the day one was born, standing proudly in the sun.
After losing the bet, he had to run across the lawn naked as the day one was born.
