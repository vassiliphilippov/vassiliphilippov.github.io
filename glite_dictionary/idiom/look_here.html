look here

1. INTJ

/ lʊk hɪr / (AmE), / lʊk hɪə / (BrE)
used to attract someone's attention or to emphasize what one is about to say, especially when expressing criticism or urgency
used to attract attention before saying something

Examples

Look here, we need to talk about your performance.
If you don't believe me, look here: these are the facts.
Look here, I won't tolerate any more disrespect.

Synset Examples

Look here, we can't ignore this issue any longer.
