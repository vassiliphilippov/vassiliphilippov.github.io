for mercy's sake

1. INTJ

/ fɔr ˈmɜrsiːz seɪk / (AmE), / fə ˈmɜːsiːz seɪk / (BrE)
used to express exasperation, impatience, or an urgent plea
expression of exasperation or plea

Examples

For mercy's sake, could you please keep the noise down while I'm working?
Just tell me the truth, for mercy's sake, so we can move on.
For mercy's sake, don't forget to lock the door when you leave.

Synset Examples

For mercy sake's alive, stop making that noise!
Take a break, for mercy's sake!
Don't make me repeat myself, for mercy's sake!
Help me with this, for mercy's sake!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for goodness' sake - expression of exasperation or emphasis
