not worth a plug nickel

1. ADJ

/ nɑt wɜrθ ə plʌɡ ˈnɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒt wɜːθ ə plʌɡ ˈnɪkəl / (BrE)
of no value at all; completely worthless
completely worthless
informal american

Examples

His old car is not worth a plug nickel anymore.
She realized the antique vase was not worth a plug nickel after it cracked.
In that condition, the jacket you found is not worth a plug nickel.

Synset Examples

The deal he offered was not worth a plug nickel.
His opinion on the matter is not worth a plug nickel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worthless - having no value or use
  • Antonym: of value - having worth or usefulness

Etymology

Originates from US English, where a 'plug nickel' was a debased or counterfeit coin of little or no value.
