to fry someone's bacon

1. VERB

/ fraɪ ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈbeɪkən / (AmE), / fraɪ ˈsʌmwənz ˈbeɪkən / (BrE)
to help someone out of a difficult situation or do them a favor
help someone out of trouble

Examples

Thanks for staying late to fry my bacon when the deadline was so tight.
She always knows how to fry someone's bacon when a crisis arises at work.
Could you fry my bacon by covering my shift tomorrow?

Synset Examples

If you help me, I can fry someone's bacon for the project.
He always finds a way to fry someone's bacon when things go wrong.
I need to work hard to fry someone's bacon at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: save someone's bacon - rescue someone from trouble or danger
