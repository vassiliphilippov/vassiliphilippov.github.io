without a scratch

1. ADV

/ wɪˈðaʊt ə skrætʃ / (AmE), / wɪˈðaʊt ə skrætʃ / (BrE)
Without suffering any injury or damage.
without injury or damage

Examples

Despite the accident, all the passengers walked away without a scratch.
The ancient statue was shipped overseas and arrived without a scratch.
He managed to get through the tough negotiations without a scratch.

Synset Examples

She fell off the swing but got up without a scratch.
