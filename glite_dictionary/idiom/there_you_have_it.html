there you have it

1. INTJ

/ ðɛr ju ˈhæv ɪt / (AmE), / ðeə ju ˈhæv ɪt / (BrE)
used to indicate that something has been completed or explained, or to emphasize a result or conclusion
expression to emphasize a result or completion

Examples

She followed the steps carefully, and there you have it: a perfectly baked cake.
After months of research and hard work, there you have it, the finished product is ready.
Just add all the ingredients together, stir, and there you have it.

Synset Examples

He explained the procedure, and there you have it.
She won the lottery; there you have it!
