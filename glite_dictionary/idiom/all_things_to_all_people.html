all things to all people

1. X

/ ɔl θɪŋz tu ɔl ˈpipəl / (AmE), / ɔːl θɪŋz tʊ ɔːl ˈpiːpəl / (BrE)
trying to please or satisfy everyone, often in an unrealistic or impractical way.
to try to please everyone

Examples

As a manager, you can't be all things to all people if you want to make tough decisions.
She learned the hard way that trying to be all things to all people leads to burnout.
Politicians often struggle because they attempt to be all things to all people during campaigns.

Synset Examples

It's impossible to be all things to all people in politics.
You can't be all things to all people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: people pleaser - a person who tries hard to make others happy or to please everyone
