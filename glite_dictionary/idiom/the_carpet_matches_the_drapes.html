the carpet matches the drapes

1. X

/ ðə ˈkɑːɹpɪt ˈmætʃɪz ðə dreɪps / (AmE), / ðə ˈkɑːpɪt ˈmatʃɪz ðə dreɪps / (BrE)
used to describe a situation where the carpet and drapes in a room are similar or complementary in color or design, indicating harmony in interior decor.
decor items are coordinated in appearance

Examples

The designer ensured that the carpet matches the drapes in the new hotel suite.
She takes pride in her decor; the carpet matches the drapes perfectly.
Guests immediately noticed that the carpet matches the drapes, creating a unified look.

Synset Examples

She was proud that the carpet matches the drapes in her living room.
His new office decor is stunning; the carpet matches the drapes.
Everyone noticed that the carpet matches the drapes perfectly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coordinate - to match or fit together, especially in color or design
  • Antonym: clash - to be in conflict, especially in color or style
