the pits

1. NOUN

/ ðə pɪts / (AmE), / ðə pɪts / (BrE)
a situation or place that is extremely unpleasant or miserable
a very unpleasant situation or place
informal

Examples

After failing my exam and missing the bus, my day was really the pits.
Working in that windowless office all summer was the pits.
Being stuck in traffic for hours is just the pits.

Synset Examples

the hell of battle
the inferno of the engine room
when you're alone Christmas is the pits

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hell - a place or situation of extreme suffering
  • Antonym: nirvana - a place or state of perfect happiness (opposite)
