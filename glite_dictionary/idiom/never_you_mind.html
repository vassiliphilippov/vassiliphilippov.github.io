never you mind

1. X

/ ˈnɛvɚ ju ˈmaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈnɛvə juː ˈmaɪnd / (BrE)
used to tell someone not to worry about or concern themselves with something
don't concern yourself; never mind

Examples

Never you mind the cost; it's already been taken care of.
If anyone asks where I went, just say never you mind.
He tried to explain, but I told him, 'never you mind about that.'

Synset Examples

Never you mind the noise, just keep working.
Never you mind what others say, follow your heart.
Never you mind about my past, focus on the present.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: never mind - used to tell someone not to worry or not to ask about something
