for the drawer

1. ADV

/ fər ðə ˈdrɔɚ / (AmE), / fə ðə ˈdrɔːə / (BrE)
(of writing) created without intention of publication, kept private or for personal satisfaction only.
not intended for publication

Examples

She wrote most of her poetry for the drawer, never showing it to anyone.
His memoir remained for the drawer until it was discovered after his death.
Many authors begin by writing for the drawer before sharing their work with the world.

Synset Examples

Writing for the drawer helps clarify my emotions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: private - kept for or known to oneself; not for public knowledge
