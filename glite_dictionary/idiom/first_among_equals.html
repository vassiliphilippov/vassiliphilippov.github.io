first among equals

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɝst əˌmʌŋ ˈikwəlz / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst əˌmʌŋ ˈiːkwəlz / (BrE)
the most prominent or important member of a group who is otherwise considered on the same level as the other members
most prominent among equals in a group

Examples

Despite being president, she acts as first among equals with her colleagues.
The chief justice is considered first among equals on the Supreme Court.
He was respected as first among equals by his fellow scientists.

Synset Examples

In our group, she is definitely first among equals.
As CEO, he sees himself as first among equals.
He was considered first among equals in the committee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leader - a person who leads a group or organization
