as the wind blows

1. SCONJ

/ əz ðə wɪnd bloʊz / (AmE), / əz ðə wɪnd bləʊz / (BrE)
used to indicate unpredictability or rapid changes, often referring to something subject to chance or shifting circumstances
indicates unpredictability or change

Examples

Her mood shifts as the wind blows, making her difficult to predict.
Political alliances form and dissolve as the wind blows in this volatile government.
We made plans, but we might change them as the wind blows.

Synset Examples

Life is unpredictable, as the wind blows.
Plans can change, as the wind blows.
Things can shift quickly, as the wind blows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: changeable - likely to change frequently or suddenly
  • Synonym: unpredictable - not able to be predicted; changeable
  • Antonym: constant - not changing; staying the same
