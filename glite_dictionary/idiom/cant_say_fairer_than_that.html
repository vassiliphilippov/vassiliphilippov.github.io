can't say fairer than that

1. X

/ kænt seɪ ˈfɛrɚ ðæn ðæt / (AmE), / kɑːnt seɪ ˈfeərə ðən ðæt / (BrE)
used to indicate that an offer or arrangement is as fair as possible and cannot be improved upon.
offer as fair as possible
british

Examples

I've lowered the price as much as I can; can't say fairer than that.
If you finish the task today, you'll get a bonus—can't say fairer than that.
They offered to fix the car and give a discount; you can't say fairer than that.

Synset Examples

The offer is generous; you can't say fairer than that.
