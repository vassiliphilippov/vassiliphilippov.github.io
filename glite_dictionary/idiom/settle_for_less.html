to settle for less

1. VERB

/ ˈsɛtl fər lɛs / (AmE), / ˈsɛtl fə lɛs / (BrE)
to accept something that is below the standard or quality you want or deserve
accept less than desired

Examples

You should never settle for less when it comes to your dreams.
She refused to settle for less and kept searching for the perfect job.
Many people settle for less out of fear of change.

Synset Examples

When it comes to relationships, I don't settle for less.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: settle - to accept or agree to something, especially less than what was wanted
