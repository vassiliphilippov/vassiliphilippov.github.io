to watch this space

1. VERB

/ wɑtʃ ðɪs speɪs / (AmE), / wɒtʃ ðɪs speɪs / (BrE)
used to tell people to pay attention for further information or developments on a subject
await updates or future developments

Examples

We don't have all the details yet, but watch this space for the official announcement.
Our team has big changes planned—watch this space in the coming weeks.
There may be some exciting new features soon, so watch this space for more information.

Synset Examples

The artist hinted at a big reveal, urging fans to watch this space.
We have exciting news coming soon, so watch this space!
The company is launching a new product, so watch this space for updates.
