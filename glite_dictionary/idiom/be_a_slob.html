to be a slob

1. VERB

/ bi ə slɑb / (AmE), / biː ə slɒb / (BrE)
to behave in a messy, careless, or lazy way
act messily or lazily
informal

Examples

If you be a slob at work, your boss will probably notice.
She didn't want to be a slob when her friends came over.
Try not to be a slob—keep your things organized.

Synset Examples

Don't be a slob; clean up your room.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: be neat - to behave in a tidy or orderly way
