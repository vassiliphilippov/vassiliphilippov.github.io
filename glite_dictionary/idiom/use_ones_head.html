to use one's head

1. VERB

/ juz wʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / juːz wʌnz hɛd / (BrE)
to think carefully and sensibly; to use good judgment or intelligence
think carefully; use good judgment

Examples

If you use your head, you'll find a solution to this problem.
It's important to use your head rather than acting impulsively.
She always tells her children to use their heads before making big decisions.

Synset Examples

She advised him to use his head in tricky situations.
You need to use your head before making that decision.
It's time to use your head and think logically.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: think - to have or form ideas; to use the mind
  • Synonym: use one's brain - to think carefully or sensibly
