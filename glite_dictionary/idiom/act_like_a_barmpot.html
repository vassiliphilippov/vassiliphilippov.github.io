to act like a barmpot

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈbɑːrmˌpɑt / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈbɑːmpɒt / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish, eccentric, or crazy manner
behave foolishly or crazily
informal british

Examples

Whenever the team wins, he acts like a barmpot, dancing around and shouting.
Don't act like a barmpot just because you're excited about the promotion.
She acted like a barmpot at the party, making everyone laugh at her antics.

Synset Examples

He acted like a barmpot during the meeting today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: act the fool - behave in a silly or foolish way

Etymology

'Barmpot' is British slang for someone considered crazy, originally from 'barm', meaning froth or foam (suggesting emptiness or light-headedness).
