in the can

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ðə kæn / (AmE), / ɪn ðə kan / (BrE)
(of a film, recording, or project) finished and ready for release or public use.
completed and ready for release

Examples

After months of editing, the director announced that the movie was finally in the can.
The research report is in the can and will be published next week.
Once the commercial was in the can, the team celebrated their hard work.

Synset Examples

The film is finally in the can after months of shooting.
The documentary is in the can and awaiting release.
We have the project in the can and ready to launch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: completed - fully done or finished

Etymology

From early film industry jargon, where 'the can' referred to the metal container used to store completed film reels.
