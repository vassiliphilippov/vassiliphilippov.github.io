out for revenge

1. ADJ

/ aʊt fər rɪˈvɛndʒ / (AmE), / aʊt fə rɪˈvɛndʒ / (BrE)
determined to retaliate or get vengeance for a perceived wrong or injury
seeking vengeance or retaliation

Examples

After the harsh criticism, she was clearly out for revenge against her detractors.
He played the game as if he were out for revenge after last season's loss.
The villain is out for revenge following his defeat in the previous film.

Synset Examples

They seem to be out for revenge after the loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vengeful - seeking to harm someone in return for a perceived injury
  • Antonym: forgiving - willing to forgive and not seek revenge
