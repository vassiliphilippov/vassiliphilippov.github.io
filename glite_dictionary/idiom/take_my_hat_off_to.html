to take my hat off to

1. VERB

/ teɪk maɪ hæt ɑf tu / (AmE), / teɪk maɪ hæt ɒf tʊ / (BrE)
to show respect or admiration for someone and their achievements
express admiration or respect

Examples

I really take my hat off to the team for organizing such a fantastic event.
We should all take our hat off to the nurses for their dedication during the crisis.
You have to take your hat off to anyone who can run a business successfully.

Synset Examples

I take my hat off to the volunteers for their hard work.
I take my hat off to her for finishing the marathon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to admire - to regard someone or something with respect or warm approval
