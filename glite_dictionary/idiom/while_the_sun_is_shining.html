while the sun is shining

1. X

/ waɪl ðə sʌn ɪz ˈʃaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / waɪl ðə sʌn ɪz ˈʃaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
take advantage of good conditions or opportunities while they last
take advantage of good conditions

Examples

You should save money while the sun is shining, because tough times may come later.
Let's finish the outdoor work while the sun is shining, before the rain starts.
It's smart to take advantage of a quiet office while the sun is shining and get ahead on your tasks.

Synset Examples

It's wise to fix the roof while the sun is shining.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to make hay while the sun shines - act while conditions are favorable
