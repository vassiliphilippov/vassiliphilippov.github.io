tinderbox waiting to ignite

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪndɚˌbɑks ˈweɪtɪŋ tu ɪɡˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɪndəbɒks ˈweɪtɪŋ tʊ ɪɡˈnaɪt / (BrE)
a situation that is highly tense or unstable and could easily erupt into conflict or chaos
volatile situation likely to explode

Examples

The region has become a tinderbox waiting to ignite, with tensions running high on all sides.
With so much resentment in the workplace, it was a tinderbox waiting to ignite.
The disagreement over the policy created a tinderbox waiting to ignite within the community.

Synset Examples

The political climate felt like a tinderbox waiting to ignite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powder keg - a situation that could easily erupt in conflict or violence

Etymology

From 'tinderbox', an early fire-lighting container, metaphorically extended to tense situations.
