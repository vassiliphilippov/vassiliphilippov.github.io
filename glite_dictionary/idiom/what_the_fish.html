what the fish

1. INTJ

/ wət ðə fɪʃ / (AmE), / wɒt ðə fɪʃ / (BrE)
used to express surprise, disbelief, or confusion, often as a mild or humorous substitute for a stronger exclamation
expression of surprise or disbelief
informal

Examples

When I saw the final bill, I couldn't help but exclaim, what the fish!
He suddenly started dancing in the middle of the street, and I thought, what the fish?
After reading the strange message, she messaged back, 'what the fish is going on here?'

Synset Examples

He said he lost his keys again, and I thought, what the fish?
She showed up late to the meeting, and I was like, what the fish?
After that performance, all I could say was, what the fish?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: what the heck - mild exclamation of surprise or disbelief
  • Synonym: what the fuck - vulgar exclamation of surprise or disbelief

Etymology

A playful euphemism modeled after 'what the f***', replacing the expletive with 'fish' to soften the expression.
