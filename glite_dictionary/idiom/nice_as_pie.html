nice as pie

1. ADJ

/ naɪs æz paɪ / (AmE), / naɪs əz paɪ / (BrE)
(informal) Very pleasant, friendly, or agreeable in manner.
very pleasant or agreeable
informal

Examples

After the disagreement, she was nice as pie to everyone involved.
He may seem strict at work, but outside the office he's nice as pie.
Once she realized her mistake, his attitude turned nice as pie.

Synset Examples

Her apology was nice as pie after the argument.
Despite the tough competition, he was nice as pie.
He welcomed us in, nice as pie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as sweet as pie - equally pleasant or agreeable; alternative simile
  • Degree Lower: nice - pleasant; agreeable
