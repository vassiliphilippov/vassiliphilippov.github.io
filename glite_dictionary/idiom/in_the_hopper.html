in the hopper

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ðə ˈhɑpər / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈhɒpə / (BrE)
awaiting action, decision, or development; in the process of being prepared or considered
pending or in preparation

Examples

We have several new proposals in the hopper awaiting review.
There are a few exciting projects in the hopper for next year.
The committee kept the suggestion in the hopper until more information was available.

Synset Examples

We have a few proposals in the hopper waiting for approval.
Several new projects are in the hopper for next year.
There's a great idea in the hopper that could work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pending - awaiting decision or settlement
  • Synonym: in progress - currently being developed or carried out

Etymology

Originally from the hopper of a mill or industrial process, where items are stored before processing.
