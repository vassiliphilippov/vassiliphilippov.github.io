power of the purse

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊɚ əv ðə pɝs / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə əv ðə pɜːs / (BrE)
the ability to influence or control events and decisions through financial means, especially by managing or allocating funds
control or influence through financial resources

Examples

Throughout history, Congress has exercised the power of the purse to influence government policy.
She gained the power of the purse after inheriting her family's wealth.
Marketers know that mothers often hold the power of the purse in households.

Synset Examples

The campaign aimed to leverage the power of the purse.
Women hold the power of the purse in this market.
She realized the power of the purse in influencing decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: financial leverage - the use of financial resources to gain influence or control
  • Antonym: power of the sword - the ability to enforce decisions by military or forceful means; a contrasting type of power
