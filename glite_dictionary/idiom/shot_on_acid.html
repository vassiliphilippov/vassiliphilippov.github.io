shot on acid

1. ADJ

/ ʃɑt ɑn ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / ʃɒt ɒn ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
filmed or visualized using a surreal or psychedelic style, as if influenced by hallucinogenic drugs
filmed in a surreal or psychedelic style
informal

Examples

The music video looked as if it had been shot on acid, filled with swirling colors and distorted shapes.
Critics described the fantasy scenes as being shot on acid due to their bizarre and hallucinatory visuals.
Some viewers loved how the footage was shot on acid, while others found it overwhelming.

Synset Examples

The movie's visuals were surreal, as if shot on acid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychedelic - relating to or denoting drugs (especially LSD) that produce hallucinations and apparent expansion of consciousness, or relating to any style characterized by surreal distortion
  • Antonym: realistic - representing familiar things in a way that is accurate or true to life

Etymology

Metaphorical use referencing the hallucinogenic effects of LSD ('acid') to describe surreal visual styles.
