to hold on a second

1. VERB

/ hoʊld ɑn ə ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / həʊld ɒn ə ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
used to ask someone to wait for a short moment
ask to wait briefly
informal

Examples

Hold on a second, I’ll be right with you.
Could you hold on a second while I check my schedule?
If you hold on a second, I can find that information for you.

Synset Examples

Hold on a second, I need to grab my keys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wait a moment - used to ask someone to wait for a short time
