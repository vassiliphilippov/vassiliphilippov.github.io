at a loss for ideas

1. ADJ

/ æt ə lɔs fər aɪˈdiəz / (AmE), / ət ə lɒs fə aɪˈdɪəz / (BrE)
uncertain or confused about what to think or say; unable to generate ideas
uncertain or unable to think of ideas

Examples

After hours of brainstorming, the team was at a loss for ideas on how to proceed.
He felt at a loss for ideas during the challenging interview.
Whenever I have writer's block, I'm completely at a loss for ideas.

Synset Examples

She stood there at a loss for ideas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at a loss - uncertain or unable to know what to do or say
