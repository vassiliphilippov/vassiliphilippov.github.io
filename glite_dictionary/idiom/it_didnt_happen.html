it didn't happen

1. X

/ ɪt ˈdɪdnt ˈhæpən / (AmE), / ɪt ˈdɪdnt ˈhæpən / (BrE)
used to express disbelief in a claim or to suggest that something claimed has not actually occurred, often demanding proof
expression of disbelief or skepticism
informal

Examples

If you don't have any evidence, then it didn't happen.
People online often reply with it didn't happen when they doubt your story.
Without witnesses, everyone assumed it didn't happen.

Synset Examples

She showed no evidence of the trip, so it didn't happen.
He claims he ran a marathon, but it didn't happen.
If you claim you met a celebrity, show me pics or it didn't happen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pics or it didn't happen - informal demand for photographic proof to believe a claim, often online
