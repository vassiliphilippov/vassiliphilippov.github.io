to the limit

1. ADV

/ tə ðə ˈlɪmɪt / (AmE), / tə ðə ˈlɪmɪt / (BrE)
as much as possible; as fully or intensely as something can be done, experienced, or used
as fully or intensely as possible

Examples

She pushed herself to the limit in order to finish the marathon.
The engine was tested to the limit during the race.
He always works to the limit, never settling for anything less than his best.

Synset Examples

you are in this to the hilt

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to the hilt - as much as possible; to the utmost degree
  • Synonym: to the max - to the maximum; as much as possible
  • Antonym: to some extent - partly; not completely
