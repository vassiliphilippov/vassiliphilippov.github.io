off the scale

1. ADJ

/ ɑf ðə skeɪl / (AmE), / ɒf ðə skeɪl / (BrE)
at an extremely high or intense level; beyond the normal range or maximum standard
extremely high or intense level

Examples

Sales numbers last month were off the scale compared to our projections.
