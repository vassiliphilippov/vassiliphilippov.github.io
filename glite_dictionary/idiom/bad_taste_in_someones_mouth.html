bad taste in someone's mouth

1. NOUN

/ bæd teɪst ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz maʊθ / (AmE), / bæd teɪst ɪn ˈsʌmwənz maʊθ / (BrE)
an unpleasant feeling or sense of disapproval after an experience, event, or interaction
unpleasant feeling after an experience

Examples

The way he ended the conversation left a bad taste in my mouth.
Her rude comments gave everyone a bad taste in their mouth.
Even months after the meeting, I still had a bad taste in my mouth about the decision.

Synset Examples

The argument left a bad taste in my mouth.
The incident gave me a bad taste in my mouth about the company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unpleasant aftertaste - a lingering bad feeling or sensation after something occurs
