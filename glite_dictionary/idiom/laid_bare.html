to laid bare

1. VERB

/ leɪd bɛr / (AmE), / leɪd beə / (BrE)
exposed or revealed completely; made clear and open to view
exposed or revealed completely

Examples

The report laid bare the company's financial problems.
Her honest diary entries laid bare her deepest fears.
The investigation laid bare the extent of the corruption.

Synset Examples

His emotions were laid bare, completely in the raw.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exposed - made visible or open to view; not hidden
  • Antonym: concealed - kept hidden or secret; not revealed
