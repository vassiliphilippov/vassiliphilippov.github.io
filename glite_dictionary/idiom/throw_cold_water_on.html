to throw cold water on

1. VERB

/ θroʊ koʊld ˈwɔtɚ ɑn / (AmE), / θrəʊ kəʊld ˈwɔːtə ɒn / (BrE)
to discourage enthusiasm for something or to express disapproval, making others less excited or optimistic
discourage; dampen enthusiasm

Examples

The manager didn’t mean to throw cold water on your idea, but she had some concerns.
Just when we were excited about the plan, he threw cold water on it with all his criticisms.
She’s always throwing cold water on our dreams instead of supporting us.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to discourage - to make someone less confident or enthusiastic about something

to criticize or discourage something, reducing enthusiasm or excitement about it
discourage or dampen enthusiasm

Examples
