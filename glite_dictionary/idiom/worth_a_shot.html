worth a shot

1. X

/ wɝːθ ə ʃɑt / (AmE), / wɜːθ ə ʃɒt / (BrE)
used to say that something is worth trying, even if the outcome is uncertain
worth trying; worth an attempt
informal

Examples

It might not work, but it's worth a shot.
I know the odds are low, but applying for the job is worth a shot.
The recipe looks tricky, but I think it's worth a shot for dinner tonight.

Synset Examples

Oh well, it was worth a shot!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to give it a try - attempt something to see if it works
  • Synonym: worth a try - worth making an attempt
  • Antonym: not worth the effort - not deserving of being tried or attempted
