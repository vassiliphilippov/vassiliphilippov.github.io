to keep it real

1. VERB

/ kip ɪt ɹiəl / (AmE), / kiːp ɪt ɹɪəl / (BrE)
to remain genuine, honest, and true to oneself; to avoid pretension or pretense
be genuine and honest
informal

Examples

Even when he's on stage, he always keeps it real with his fans.
I respect people who keep it real rather than pretending to be something they're not.
You don't have to impress anyone—just keep it real.

Synset Examples

It's important to keep it real in our conversations.
She told him to keep it real and not sugarcoat anything.
I appreciate you for always keeping it real with me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be yourself - act in a natural and authentic way
