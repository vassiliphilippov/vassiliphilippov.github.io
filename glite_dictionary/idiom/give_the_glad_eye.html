to give the glad eye

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ðə ɡlæd aɪ / (AmE), / ɡɪv ðə ɡlæd aɪ / (BrE)
to look at someone in a way that shows sexual attraction or flirtation
look at someone flirtatiously
british

Examples

She noticed him give the glad eye to every woman who walked into the bar.
He was clearly giving the glad eye to his coworker at the party.
If you give the glad eye to strangers, don’t be surprised if someone approaches you.
