to go fuck oneself

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ fʌk ˈwʌnˌsɛlf / (AmE), / ɡəʊ fʌk ˈwʌnˌsɛlf / (BrE)
(vulgar, offensive) Used to rudely and angrily tell someone to leave or express intense dismissal or contempt.
express strong anger or dismissal (vulgar)
vulgar offensive

Examples

After their argument, he told her to go fuck herself and slammed the door.
If someone is that rude to you, just tell them to go fuck themselves.
She was so angry at the criticism that she shouted, "Go fuck yourself!"

Synset Examples

I was so angry that I wanted to tell him to go fuck himself.
When she complained, he told her to go fuck herself.
After that rude comment, I just told him to go fuck himself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to go to hell - used to angrily tell someone to leave or go away
