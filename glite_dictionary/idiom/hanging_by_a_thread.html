hanging by a thread

1. X

/ ˈhæŋɪŋ baɪ ə θrɛd / (AmE), / ˈhæŋɪŋ baɪ ə θrɛd / (BrE)
in a very precarious or risky situation where failure or disaster could happen at any moment
in a very precarious situation

Examples

The company is hanging by a thread after months of poor sales.
Her hopes for recovery were hanging by a thread as the illness worsened.
The peace talks are hanging by a thread due to rising tensions.

Synset Examples

His job security is hanging by a thread.
Their relationship is hanging by a thread.
The project is hanging by a thread due to budget cuts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on the brink - close to a particular (often bad) outcome
  • Antonym: safe - not at risk or in danger
