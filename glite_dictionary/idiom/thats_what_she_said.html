that's what she said

1. X

/ ðæts wət ʃi sɛd / (AmE), / ðæts wət ʃi sed / (BrE)
a humorous phrase used to highlight a possible sexual innuendo in what someone else just said
humorous phrase to indicate sexual innuendo
informal slang

Examples

Whenever someone makes an accidental double meaning, someone is sure to say that's what she said.
He laughed after I said 'it's so hard to handle' and immediately added that's what she said.
People often use that's what she said to turn normal statements into jokes.

Synset Examples

I just think he’s too confident—that's what she said.
He claimed he could run a mile in five minutes—oh, that's what she said!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: innuendo - an indirect or subtle reference, often suggestive or disparaging
