dash it

1. INTJ

/ dæʃ ɪt / (AmE), / dæʃ ɪt / (BrE)
used to express mild annoyance or frustration.
expression of mild annoyance or frustration
british

Examples

Dash it, I left my umbrella on the train again!
Dash it, why didn't I remember to call her back?

Synset Examples

Etymology

Euphemistic substitution for 'damn it', likely from 18th-century British English.
