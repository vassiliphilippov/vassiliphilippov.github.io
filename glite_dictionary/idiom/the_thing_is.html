the thing is

1. X

/ ðə ˈθɪŋ ɪz / (AmE), / ðə ˈθɪŋ ɪz / (BrE)
used to introduce or emphasize an important or clarifying point, especially when explaining a situation or giving an opinion
introduces an important point or explanation
informal

Examples

The thing is, we can't just ignore the problem any longer.
I'd love to join you, but the thing is, I already made other plans.
The thing is, not everyone agrees with your proposal.

Synset Examples

The thing is, we need more time to decide.
The thing is, I don't really enjoy that movie.
The thing of it is, everyone has their own struggles.
The thing of it is, we can't change the past.
