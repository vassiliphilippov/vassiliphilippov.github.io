the time of one's life

1. NOUN

/ ðə taɪm ʌv wʌnz laɪf / (AmE), / ðə taɪm ɒv wʌnz laɪf / (BrE)
an extremely enjoyable, exciting, or memorable experience
a very exciting or memorable experience

Examples

She had the time of her life during her semester abroad in Italy.
The children were having the time of their lives at the amusement park.
He said that winning the championship was the time of his life.

Synset Examples

She felt she was having the time of her life at the concert.
He said his trip to Europe was the time of his life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the best time ever - the best or most enjoyable time in one's life
