hand to God

1. X

/ hænd tə ɡɑd / (AmE), / hænd tə ɡɒd / (BrE)
used to emphasize that what one is saying is true or sincere; honestly, I swear
used to assert sincerity or truthfulness
informal

Examples

Hand to God, I’ve never seen that man before in my life.
She said, hand to God, she would never do such a thing again.
Hand to God, that’s the truth as I know it.

Synset Examples

He promised, hand to God, that he would help.
She said, hand to God, she would never lie.
I swear, hand to God, I didn't take it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to swear - to state something is true, often invoking something sacred
  • Synonym: honestly - truly; used to emphasize sincerity
