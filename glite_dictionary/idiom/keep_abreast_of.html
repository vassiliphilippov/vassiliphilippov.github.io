to keep abreast of

1. VERB

/ kip əˈbrɛst ʌv / (AmE), / kiːp əˈbrɛst ɒv / (BrE)
to remain informed about recent developments or news in a particular subject or area
stay informed about developments

Examples

She always tries to keep abreast of the latest research in her field.
It's important to keep abreast of changes in technology for your job.
We subscribe to several journals to keep abreast of global economic trends.

Synset Examples

She keeps abreast of industry trends through webinars.
I read the news daily to stay abreast of current events.
He always stays abreast of the latest technology developments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stay up to date - remain informed about the latest developments
