crest of the wave

1. NOUN

/ krɛst əv ðə weɪv / (AmE), / krɛst əv ðə weɪv / (BrE)
at the highest point of success, popularity, or achievement
at peak success or popularity

Examples

After years of hard work, the company is finally at the crest of the wave in its industry.
Winning the award put her on the crest of the wave in her career.
The team was riding the crest of the wave after their championship victory.

Synset Examples

The artist is riding the crest of the wave with her latest album.
He felt like he was riding the crest of the wave after the big win.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at one's peak - at the point of greatest achievement
  • Synonym: on top of the world - feeling extremely successful or happy
