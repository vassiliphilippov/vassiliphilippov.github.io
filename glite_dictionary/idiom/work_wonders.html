to work wonders

1. VERB

/ wɝk ˈwʌndɚz / (AmE), / wɜːk ˈwʌndəz / (BrE)
to have a very beneficial or remarkable effect; to produce excellent or surprising results
have a remarkable positive effect

Examples

A cup of hot tea can work wonders when you're feeling tired.
Their new marketing strategy has worked wonders for the company's sales figures.
Regular practice really works wonders for improving your language skills.

Synset Examples

This remedy is known to work wonders for headaches.
She believes that positivity can work wonders in life.
A good night's sleep can work wonders for your health.
He bought a new peeler that works wonders on apples.
WD-40 works wonders on rusted bolts.
The soothing balm worked wonders on my sunburn.
The breast mint worked wonders for her confidence.
The corn-cracker worked wonders at the summer barbecue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: work miracles - produce equally remarkable or beneficial results
