on the back of

1. ADP

/ ɑn ðə bæk əv / (AmE), / ɒn ðə bæk əv / (BrE)
following as a result of something; as a consequence of.
as a result of; following

Examples

The company's profits soared on the back of strong holiday sales.
She received several job offers on the back of her award-winning research.
Their reputation grew on the back of their exceptional customer service.

Synset Examples

On the back of their victory, morale is high.
He received praise on the back of his recent achievements.
On the back of her success, she launched a new product.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: following - coming after or as a consequence of something
  • Synonym: because of - for the reason of; due to
