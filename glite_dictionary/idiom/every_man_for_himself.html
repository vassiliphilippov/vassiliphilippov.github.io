every man for himself

1. X

/ ˈɛvri mæn fər hɪmˈsɛlf / (AmE), / ˈɛvri mæn fə hɪmˈsɛlf / (BrE)
used to describe a situation in which each person must act or survive independently, without regard for others
each person must act independently

Examples

When the alarm went off and the doors opened, it quickly became every man for himself.
In the abandoned city, it was every man for himself as people searched for supplies.
As soon as the boat started sinking, panic set in and it turned into every man for himself.

Synset Examples

In that chaotic moment, it was every man for himself.
In the competition, it was truly every man for himself.
During the storm, it felt like every man for himself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog eat dog - a situation where people compete ruthlessly for their own advantage
  • Antonym: all for one and one for all - a situation where people act together in support of each other
