oh my gosh

1. INTJ

/ oʊ maɪ ɡɑʃ / (AmE), / əʊ maɪ ɡɒʃ / (BrE)
used to express surprise, shock, or disbelief, often as a milder alternative to 'oh my God'
exclamation of surprise or disbelief
informal

Examples

Oh my gosh, you scared me when you walked in so quietly!
Oh my gosh, that dress looks amazing on you.
I can't believe this is happening—oh my gosh!

Synset Examples

Oh my gosh, I can’t believe you did that!
Oh my gosh, is that really your new car?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oh my God - an exclamation of surprise, shock, or disbelief; sometimes considered blasphemous or offensive
