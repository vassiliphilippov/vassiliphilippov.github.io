high on life

1. ADJ

/ haɪ ɑn laɪf / (AmE), / haɪ ɒn laɪf / (BrE)
feeling extremely happy, enthusiastic, or euphoric, often without any external cause
feeling very happy or euphoric
informal

Examples

After running the marathon, she was high on life for days.
He’s always high on life, inspiring everyone with his energy and optimism.
Traveling to new places makes me feel high on life.

Synset Examples
