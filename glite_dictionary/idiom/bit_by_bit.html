bit by bit

1. ADV

/ bɪt baɪ bɪt / (AmE), / bɪt baɪ bɪt / (BrE)
gradually; in small steps or stages rather than all at once
gradually; in small steps

Examples

He learned French bit by bit, practicing a little every day.
The old paint peeled away bit by bit as we scraped the wall.
Bit by bit, they rebuilt their lives after the storm.

Synset Examples

the snake moved gradually toward its victim

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gradually - slowly and steadily over time
  • Antonym: all at once - all in a single moment or simultaneously
