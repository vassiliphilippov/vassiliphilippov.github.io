to throw things at the wall and see what sticks

1. VERB

/ θroʊ θɪŋz æt ðə wɑl ənd si wət stɪks / (AmE), / θrəʊ θɪŋz æt ðə wɔːl ənd siː wɒt stɪks / (BrE)
to try out a number of different ideas or methods in order to find one that is successful or effective
try various ideas to find one that works

Examples

The team decided to throw things at the wall and see what sticks to solve the persistent problem.
Marketing can sometimes mean you just throw things at the wall and see what sticks.
When starting a novel, some authors throw things at the wall and see what sticks before settling on a plot.

Synset Examples

In brainstorming, we often just throw things at the wall and see what sticks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: experiment - try out new methods or ideas to see what works
