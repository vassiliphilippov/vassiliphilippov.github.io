bright as a new penny

1. ADJ

/ braɪt əz ə nuː ˈpɛni / (AmE), / braɪt əz ə njuː ˈpɛni / (BrE)
extremely bright or shiny, especially as if new or freshly polished
very bright or shiny

Examples

Her freshly cleaned kitchen floor looked bright as a new penny in the morning sun.
The little girl's eyes were bright as a new penny when she received her gift.
After a fresh coat of paint, the old shop looked bright as a new penny.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shiny - reflecting light, bright in appearance
