house of ill repute

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊs əv ɪl rɪˈpjut / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs əv ɪl rɪˈpjuːt / (BrE)
a place, especially a brothel, regarded as being associated with immoral or disreputable activities
brothel; disreputable place

Examples

Rumors spread quickly when a house of ill repute opened on the edge of town.
The mayor promised to shut down every house of ill repute in the district.
In the 19th century, many cities tried to regulate the activities inside a house of ill repute.

Synset Examples

The neighborhood had cleaned up the house of ill repute.
The old building was known as a house of ill repute.
He was warned to stay away from that house of ill repute.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brothel - a house where people pay to have sex with prostitutes
