cheap at half the price

1. X

/ tʃip æt hæf ðə praɪs / (AmE), / tʃiːp ət hɑːf ðə praɪs / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is a real bargain, even more so than its nominal price suggests
used to stress something is a bargain

Examples

The concert tickets were cheap at half the price, considering the quality of the performance.
For the amount of features included, this software is cheap at half the price.
Their lunch special is cheap at half the price, making it popular among students.

Synset Examples

I found a restaurant that serves meals that are cheap at half the price.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bargain - something purchased for less than the usual price
