few and far between

1. ADJ

/ ˌfjuː ən ˌfɑːr bəˈtwiːn / (AmE), / ˌfjuː ən ˌfɑː bəˈtwiːn / (BrE)
very rare or not happening often
very rare; not frequent

Examples

Large gatherings in this rural town are few and far between.
Job opportunities for recent graduates were few and far between last year.
Clear, sunny days are few and far between during the rainy season.

Synset Examples

Friends who keep in touch are few and far between.
Good sales in this area are few and far between.
Opportunities like this are few and far between.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rare - not occurring often
  • Antonym: common - occurring frequently or regularly
