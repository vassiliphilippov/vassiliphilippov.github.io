home from home

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm frəm hoʊm / (AmE), / həʊm frəm həʊm / (BrE)
a place where one feels as comfortable and at ease as in their own home
place as comfortable as home

Examples

Their beach cottage quickly became a home from home for the whole family.
The hotel aims to provide guests with a true home from home experience.
Staying at my grandparents' house is always a home from home for me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: home away from home - a place where one feels as comfortable as in their own home
