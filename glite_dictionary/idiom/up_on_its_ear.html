up on its ear

1. X

/ ʌp ɑn ɪts ɪr / (AmE), / ʌp ɒn ɪts ɪə / (BrE)
in a state of confusion, disorder, or upheaval; greatly upset or disrupted
in confusion or upheaval

Examples

The new law proposal set the entire city up on its ear for weeks.
His unexpected resignation threw the department up on its ear.
News of the merger turned the company up on its ear.

Synset Examples

The scandal turned the company up on its ear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chaotic - in a completely confused or disordered state
  • Antonym: calm - in a state of tranquility or absence of disturbance
