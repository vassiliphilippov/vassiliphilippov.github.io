to give 110%

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv wʌn hʌn.drəd ænd ˈtɛn pər ˈsɛnt / (AmE), / ɡɪv wʌn ˈhʌn.drɪd ænd ˈtɛn pə ˈsɛnt / (BrE)
to make an exceptionally great effort; to try as hard as possible beyond what is expected or required
make an exceptional effort
informal

Examples

Whenever there is an important deadline, she always gives 110% to get the job done.
The coach told the players they would have to give 110% if they wanted to win the match.
He believes in giving 110% on every assignment, no matter how small.

Synset Examples

She always gives 110% in everything she does.
In this project, we need to give 110% to succeed.
To win the championship, the team must give 110%.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: try one's best - to make as much effort as possible
  • Synonym: go the extra mile - to make more effort than is expected
