to take a silly pill

1. VERB

/ teɪk ə ˈsɪli pɪl / (AmE), / teɪk ə ˈsɪli pɪl / (BrE)
to suddenly behave in a foolish, nonsensical, or uncharacteristically silly way
behave foolishly or nonsensically
informal

Examples

Did you take a silly pill this morning, or are you always this goofy?
She must have taken a silly pill before the meeting; she couldn't stop making jokes.
Whenever he acts that way, I wonder if he took a silly pill for breakfast.

Synset Examples

After that comment, he definitely seemed like he took a silly pill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: act foolish - to behave in a silly or irrational manner
