in one's back pocket

1. X

/ ɪn wʌnz bæk ˈpɑːkɪt / (AmE), / ɪn wʌnz bæk ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
kept secretly or reserved for possible future use or advantage
kept secretly for future use

Examples

She always has a backup plan in her back pocket in case things go wrong.
He kept the job offer in his back pocket while considering other opportunities.
The negotiator had several concessions in his back pocket to use during the talks.

Synset Examples

She had a secret recipe in her back pocket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: up one's sleeve - kept secretly in reserve, ready to use if needed
