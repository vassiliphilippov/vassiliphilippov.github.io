to travel the world

1. VERB

/ ˈtrævəl ðə wɜɹld / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl ðə wɜːld / (BrE)
to journey to many countries or explore different places around the globe
journey to many places worldwide

Examples

After graduating, she decided to travel the world before starting her career.
They hope to travel the world together and experience new cultures.
He spent several years traveling the world as a freelance photographer.

Synset Examples

They traveled the world, saying life is too short to wait.
Aunt Thomasina loves to travel the world.
Let's travel the world while we're young.
They believed they could travel the world in their dreams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: see the world - to visit many places worldwide
