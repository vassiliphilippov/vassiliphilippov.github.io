to see what sticks

1. VERB

/ si wʌt stɪks / (AmE), / si wɒt stɪks / (BrE)
to try different ideas or methods to find out which ones are successful or effective
try different things to see what works

Examples

We're going to launch several marketing campaigns and see what sticks.
She pitches lots of ideas in meetings just to see what sticks.
When cooking for kids, I often try new recipes to see what sticks.

Synset Examples

I’ll experiment with my writing style and see what sticks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to try out - to test something to see if it works
  • Synonym: experiment - to test different options to see which works
