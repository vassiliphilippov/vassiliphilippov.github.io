to cast in someone's teeth

1. VERB

/ kæst ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz tiθ / (AmE), / kɑːst ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz tiːθ / (BrE)
to reproach someone by reminding them of something embarrassing or negative, often in a pointed or hurtful way
remind reproachfully of something negative

Examples

Though years had passed, he still cast in her teeth the mistakes she made.
She hated when her brother would cast in her teeth every failure from their childhood.
After losing the debate, his opponent cast in his teeth the errors from his previous arguments.

Synset Examples

She felt insulted when he cast it in her teeth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to remind - to cause someone to remember something
  • Synonym: reproach - to express disapproval or disappointment
