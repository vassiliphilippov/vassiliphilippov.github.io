happy as a lark

1. ADJ

/ ˈhæpi əz ə lɑrk / (AmE), / ˈhæpi əz ə lɑːk / (BrE)
Extremely happy or joyful; in very high spirits.
extremely happy or joyful

Examples

After hearing the good news, she was happy as a lark all day.
On the first day of vacation, the children woke up happy as a lark.
He left the interview happy as a lark, feeling confident about his chances.

Synset Examples

The kids were happy as a lark at the park.
He felt happy as a lark on his birthday.
After winning the game, she was happy as a lark.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overjoyed - extremely happy
  • Synonym: ecstatic - feeling or expressing overwhelming happiness
  • Antonym: miserable - very unhappy or uncomfortable

Etymology

Refers to the lark, a songbird known for its cheerful singing, symbolizing happiness.
