lap of the gods

1. NOUN

/ læp əv ðə ɡɑdz / (AmE), / læp əv ðə ɡɒdz / (BrE)
used to say that something is beyond human control or depends on fate or chance.
outside human control; up to fate

Examples

The outcome of the match was left in the lap of the gods as neither team could score.
After all his efforts, the decision was in the lap of the gods.
Whether their investment pays off is now in the lap of the gods.

Synset Examples

there is nothing more I can do: it's in the lap of the gods now

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of one's hands - not within one's power to influence or control
  • Synonym: in God's hands - under the control of a higher power or fate
