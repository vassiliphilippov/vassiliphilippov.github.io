fuhgeddaboud him

1. X

/ fəˈgɛdəˌbaʊt ɪm / (AmE), / fəˈgɛdəˌbaʊt ɪm / (BrE)
(informal, slang) Used to dismiss or suggest disregarding a particular man or issue, often with emphasis, in the style of a New York accent; means 'forget about him.'
slang for 'forget about him'; ignore him
informal slang american

Examples

He broke your heart? Fuhgeddaboud him and focus on yourself.
Whenever trouble comes his way, he just says, 'fuhgeddaboud him.'
If you think he’ll apologize, fuhgeddaboud him—he never admits he's wrong.

Synset Examples

If he hurt you, fuhgeddaboud him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forget about him - To disregard or not think about a particular man; the standard phrase

Etymology

An intentionally exaggerated spelling of 'forget about him', imitating the accented pronunciation associated with New York City or New Jersey speech.
