hell or high water

1. X

/ hɛl ɚ haɪ ˈwɑtɚ / (AmE), / hɛl ɔː haɪ ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something will be done no matter what difficulties or obstacles arise
no matter what difficulties

Examples

She was determined to finish the marathon, hell or high water.
We will open the store on time, hell or high water.
He promised to protect his family, hell or high water.

Synset Examples

I will finish this project, hell or high water.
