spiffed up

1. ADJ

/ spɪft ʌp / (AmE), / spɪft ʌp / (BrE)
Dressed in stylish, fancy, or formal clothing; made to look attractive or neat.
dressed in fancy or formal clothes
informal

Examples

He really spiffed up for the awards ceremony last night.
The office was spiffed up with new furniture before the big client meeting.
She always likes to get spiffed up before going out for dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressed up - wearing formal or fancy clothes
  • Antonym: scruffy - wearing untidy or old clothes
