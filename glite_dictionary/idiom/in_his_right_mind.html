in his right mind

1. ADJ

/ ɪn hɪz raɪt maɪnd / (AmE), / ɪn hɪz raɪt maɪnd / (BrE)
thinking or behaving in a sensible and rational way; not crazy or irresponsible.
thinking sensibly; rational

Examples

No one in his right mind would make such a risky investment.
She wouldn't do that in her right mind; something must be wrong.
You have to be in your right mind to run a marathon in this heat.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: out of his mind - acting irrationally or appearing insane
