to worship the porcelain god

1. VERB

/ ˈwɜːrʃɪp ðə pɔːrˈsəlɪn ɡɑd / (AmE), / ˈwɜːʃɪp ðə pɔːˈsəʊlɪn ɡɒd / (BrE)
to vomit, especially as a result of heavy drinking
vomit from heavy drinking (idiom)
informal slang

Examples

He drank a little too much last night and ended up worshipping the porcelain god until dawn.
If you mix beer and wine, you might soon find yourself worshipping the porcelain god.
She excused herself from the table, feeling that she was about to worship the porcelain god.

Synset Examples

She spent the evening worshiping the porcelain god after drinking too much.
After the wild party, she was worshiping the porcelain goddess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vomit - to bring up the contents of the stomach through the mouth
  • Synonym: to throw up - to vomit; to be sick
