heavy as a dead donkey

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛvi æz ə dɛd ˈdɑŋki / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi əz ə dɛd ˈdɒŋki / (BrE)
extremely heavy or difficult to lift; much heavier than expected
extremely heavy; hard to lift

Examples

After filling it with books, my bag was heavy as a dead donkey all afternoon.
The old safe in the basement is heavy as a dead donkey to move by yourself.
That box looks small, but it's heavy as a dead donkey when you try to pick it up.

Synset Examples

Her backpack felt heavy as a dead donkey after hiking.

Related Terms
