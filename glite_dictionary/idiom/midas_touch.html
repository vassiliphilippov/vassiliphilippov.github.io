Midas touch

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪdəs tʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪdəs tʌtʃ / (BrE)
an ability to make money or be successful in everything one undertakes
ability to make money easily

Examples

Ever since she started her business, it seems like she has the Midas touch.
He has the Midas touch when it comes to investments and always makes a profit.
With his Midas touch, every project turns into a huge success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golden touch - the ability to earn money or be successful at everything

Etymology

Refers to King Midas in Greek mythology, who was said to turn everything he touched into gold.

2. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪdəs tʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪdəs tʌtʃ / (BrE)
the ability to turn any undertaking into success; an exceptional talent for making money or succeeding in any activity
exceptional ability to achieve success

Examples

After three profitable startups, investors now believe she has the Midas touch.
His Midas touch transformed a struggling company into a major industry player within two years.
People often seek his advice, hoping some of his Midas touch will rub off on them.

Synset Examples

He seems to have a Midas touch when it comes to business.
Everything she invests in turns to gold; she has the Midas touch.
With her Midas touch, every project becomes a success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golden touch - the ability to make any venture successful; synonym for Midas touch

Etymology

From King Midas, a figure in Greek mythology who was granted the ability to turn everything he touched into gold.
