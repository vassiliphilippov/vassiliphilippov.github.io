to drop a chalupa

1. VERB

/ drɑp ə tʃəˈlupə / (AmE), / drɒp ə tʃəˈluːpə / (BrE)
(informal) To drop food accidentally, or to make a significant mistake or blunder.
to make a big mistake; drop food
informal slang american
