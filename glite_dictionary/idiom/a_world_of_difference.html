a world of difference

1. NOUN

/ ə wɜrld əv ˈdɪfərəns / (AmE), / ə wɜːld əv ˈdɪfərəns / (BrE)
a very great or important difference; a significant effect or improvement
a significant difference or effect

Examples

Having a mentor can make a world of difference to your career.
A few kind words made a world of difference to her mood.
The improved technology created a world of difference in production efficiency.

Synset Examples

Small changes can create a world of difference over time.
Her support made a world of difference to my confidence.
A good education makes a world of difference in life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: a big difference - a notable distinction or change
