behind closed doors

1. ADV

/ bɪˈhaɪnd kloʊzd dɔɹz / (AmE), / bɪˈhaɪnd kləʊzd dɔːz / (BrE)
in a way that is private or not open to the public; in secret.
in secret; in private

Examples

The committee met behind closed doors to discuss the sensitive issues.
Many important decisions are made behind closed doors away from public scrutiny.
They prefer to negotiate behind closed doors rather than in the media.

Synset Examples

Decisions were made behind closed doors without public input.
They resolved their issues behind closed doors.
Everyone knew she was a cuntbitch behind closed doors.
The negotiations happened behind closed doors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in private - in a way not seen by the public
  • Antonym: openly - in a way that is not secret or hidden; in public
