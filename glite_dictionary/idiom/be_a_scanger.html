to be a scanger

1. VERB

/ bi ə ˈskæŋər / (AmE), / biː ə ˈskæŋə / (BrE)
to behave as a mooch, taking things from others without giving back or to take advantage of people's generosity
to act as a mooch or take advantage
slang informal

Examples

He always tries to be a scanger at parties, never bringing anything to share.
Don't be a scanger—pay your own way for once.
Some people be a scanger whenever they get the chance.

Synset Examples

Don't be a scanger; just ask for help.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mooch - a person who tries to get something free by asking or taking from others
  • Synonym: freeloader - someone who takes advantage of others' generosity without giving anything in return

Etymology

From Irish slang 'scanger', of uncertain origin, referring to someone who habitually begs or takes advantage.
