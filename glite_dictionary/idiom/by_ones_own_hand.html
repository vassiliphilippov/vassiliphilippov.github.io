by one's own hand

1. ADV

/ baɪ wʌnz oʊn hænd / (AmE), / baɪ wʌnz əʊn hand / (BrE)
personally; done by the person themselves rather than by someone else.
personally; by oneself

Examples

The artist painted this portrait by his own hand, displaying his unique style.
She wanted everyone to know that the poem was written by her own hand.
He apologized in a letter by his own hand, rather than using email.

Synset Examples

He signed the document by his own hand.
The letter was written by his own hand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personally - done by oneself
  • Antonym: by proxy - done by someone else on another's behalf
