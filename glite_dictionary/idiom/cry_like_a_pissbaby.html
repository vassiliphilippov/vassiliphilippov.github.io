to cry like a pissbaby

1. VERB

/ kraɪ laɪk ə ˈpɪsˌbeɪbi / (AmE), / kraɪ laɪk ə ˈpɪsˌbeɪbi / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) To cry in an excessive or immature way, often implying a lack of resilience or self-control.
cry excessively or childishly (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar

Examples

Don't cry like a pissbaby just because you didn't get your way.
Whenever things get tough, he tends to cry like a pissbaby instead of facing the problem.
She told me not to cry like a pissbaby after I lost the bet.

Synset Examples

He cried like a pissbaby when he lost the game.
