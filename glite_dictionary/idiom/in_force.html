in force

1. ADJ

/ ɪn fɔrs / (AmE), / ɪn fɔːs / (BrE)
(of a law, rule, or agreement) currently in effect; operative or valid at the present time.
currently valid; operative (especially of laws or rules)

Examples

The new tax regulations are now in force throughout the country.
Our contract remains in force until the end of the year.
Several old laws are still technically in force, even if rarely enforced.

Synset Examples

the law is effective immediately
a warranty good for two years
the law is already in effect (or in force)

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in effect - currently valid or operative (especially of rules or laws)
