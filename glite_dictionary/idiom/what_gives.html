what gives

1. X

/ wʌt ɡɪvz / (AmE), / wɒt ɡɪvz / (BrE)
used to ask what is happening, especially when something is wrong or unexpected; an informal inquiry into the cause of a situation or behavior
informal inquiry about what is happening
informal

Examples

I noticed you're not eating your lunch—what gives?
The meeting was supposed to start an hour ago; what gives?
You were so excited yesterday, but today you seem down. What gives?

Synset Examples

This project is behind schedule, what gives?
You’re unusually quiet today, what gives?
You seem upset, what gives?
