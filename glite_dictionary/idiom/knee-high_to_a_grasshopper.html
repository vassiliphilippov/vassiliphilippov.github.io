knee-high to a grasshopper

1. ADJ

/ ˌniː ˌhaɪ tə ə ˈɡræsˌhɑpɚ / (AmE), / ˌniː ˌhaɪ tə ə ˈɡrɑːsˌhɒpə / (BrE)
(informal) Very small, young, or short in stature, especially referring to a child or young animal.
very small or young (idiom)
informal

Examples

As a child, I was knee-high to a grasshopper when we first visited the zoo.
He has lived in this town since he was knee-high to a grasshopper.
My grandmother always says she remembers me being knee-high to a grasshopper.
