by inches

1. ADV

/ baɪ ˈɪntʃɪz / (AmE), / baɪ ˈɪntʃɪz / (BrE)
In small amounts or by slight degrees; little by little.
slowly or by small amounts

Examples

The glacier is receding by inches each year due to climate change.
He made progress on the puzzle by inches, working late into the night.
The boat inched forward by inches as the captain carefully maneuvered it through the narrow canal.

Synset Examples

they moved it by inches
