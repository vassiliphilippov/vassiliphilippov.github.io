fair shake

1. NOUN

/ fɛr ʃeɪk / (AmE), / feə ʃeɪk / (BrE)
an equitable or impartial chance to succeed or be treated fairly
equitable opportunity; fair chance

Examples

Every candidate deserves a fair shake during the interview process.
She felt she was not given a fair shake after the mistake at work.
It's important to give everyone a fair shake before making a final decision.
