come what may

1. X

/ kʌm wʌt meɪ / (AmE), / kʌm wɒt meɪ / (BrE)
No matter what happens; regardless of the circumstances.
regardless of what happens

Examples

She vowed to complete the race, come what may.
I'll be there to help you, come what may.
They planned to stick together, come what may.

Synset Examples

She promised to stand by him, come what may.
I will support you, come what may.
We will finish this project, come what may.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no matter what - in any circumstances; whatever happens
  • Synonym: regardless - without paying attention to the current situation or circumstances
