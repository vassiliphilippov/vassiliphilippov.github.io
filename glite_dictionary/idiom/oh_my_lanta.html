oh my lanta

1. INTJ

/ oʊ maɪ ˈlæntə / (AmE), / əʊ maɪ ˈlæntə / (BrE)
used to express surprise, shock, or disbelief
expression of surprise or disbelief
informal american

Examples

Oh my lanta, I can't believe how much you've grown!
When she saw the final score, she exclaimed, "Oh my lanta, we actually won!"
Oh my lanta, that cake looks delicious!

Synset Examples

Oh my lanta, this place is packed!
Oh my lanta, I can't believe we won!
Oh my lanta, that was an amazing performance!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oh my gosh - expression of surprise or disbelief
  • Synonym: oh my lord - expression of surprise or disbelief
  • Synonym: oh my word - expression of surprise or shock

Etymology

Popularized by American television sitcoms, notably 'Full House'; a euphemistic variation of ‘oh my lord’ and ‘oh my Atlanta’.
