knocking on heaven's door

1. X

/ ˈnɑkɪŋ ɑn ˈhɛvənz dɔr / (AmE), / ˈnɒkɪŋ ɒn ˈhɛvənz dɔː / (BrE)
very close to death or in immediate danger of dying
very near death

Examples

After the severe heart attack, he was knocking on heaven's door for days.
The doctors fought hard when she was knocking on heaven's door in the ICU.
During the expedition, lack of food left many explorers knocking on heaven's door.

Synset Examples

The doctor said he was knocking on heaven's door.
After that last fight, he was knocking on heaven's door.
He felt like he was knocking on heaven's door after his accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at death's door - very close to death

Etymology

Popularized by the 1973 song 'Knockin' on Heaven's Door' by Bob Dylan.
