now and again

1. ADV

/ naʊ ənd əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / naʊ ənd əˈɡɛn / (BrE)
from time to time; occasionally; not very often.
occasionally; from time to time

Examples

Now and again, I treat myself to a slice of cake after dinner.
He checks his old email account now and again, just in case he’s missed something important.
Now and again, the town holds a festival that brings everyone together.

Synset Examples

he was arrogant and occasionally callous
open areas are only occasionally interrupted by clumps of trees
they visit New York on occasion
now and again she would take her favorite book from the shelf and read to us
as we drove along, the beautiful scenery now and then attracted his attention

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occasionally - at some times; not regularly
  • Antonym: frequently - happening very often; regularly
