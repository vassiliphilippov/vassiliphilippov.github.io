to act like an old fart

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ən oʊld fɑrt / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ən əʊld fɑːt / (BrE)
to behave in an overly conservative, boring, or outdated manner, especially in social situations
behave in a boring or outdated way
informal

Examples

Stop acting like an old fart and come dance with us!
He apologized for acting like an old fart about the new music choices.
She teased her dad for acting like an old fart when he complained about technology.

Synset Examples

She teased him for acting like an old fart at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuddy-duddy - a person who is old-fashioned and fussy
  • Synonym: old fogey - someone who has old-fashioned ideas and is resistant to change

Etymology

Derived from the slang term 'old fart' (a dull or old-fashioned person), with 'act like' indicating behavior.
