at once

1. ADV

/ æt wʌns / (AmE), / ət wʌns / (BrE)
at the same time; simultaneously.
simultaneously; at the same time

Examples

All the lights in the house went out at once during the storm.
She tried to answer several questions at once, which made her confused.
The children began to talk at once, making it hard to understand anyone.

Synset Examples

he took three cookies at a time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simultaneously - at the same time

2. ADV

/ ət ˈwʌns / (AmE), / ət ˈwʌns / (BrE)
immediately; without any delay or hesitation.
immediately, without delay

Examples

Please come here at once—it's urgent.
She recognized him at once when he walked into the room.
If any problems occur, let me know at once.

Synset Examples

he answered immediately
found an answer straightaway
an official accused of dishonesty should be suspended forthwith
Come here now!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immediately - without any delay; at once
  • Synonym: straightaway - immediately; without delay
  • Antonym: later - at a time in the future; not immediately
