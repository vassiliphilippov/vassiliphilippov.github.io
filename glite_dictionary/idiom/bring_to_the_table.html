to bring to the table

1. VERB

/ brɪŋ tə ðə ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / brɪŋ tə ðə ˈteɪbl̩ / (BrE)
to contribute something useful or offer something of value to a group or discussion
contribute something valuable

Examples

Each candidate needs to show what they can bring to the table during the interview process.
Her technical skills really bring to the table new possibilities for the team.
Everyone appreciates the creative ideas you bring to the table at our meetings.

Synset Examples

Let's see what each member can bring to the table.
He always brings valuable ideas to the table.
Her experience in marketing will bring a lot to the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to contribute - to give or add something in order to help achieve or provide something
