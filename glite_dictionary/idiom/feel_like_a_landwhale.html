to feel like a landwhale

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə ˈlændˌweɪl / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈlændˌweɪl / (BrE)
to feel extremely full or bloated, typically after eating too much
feel extremely full or bloated
informal

Examples

After three helpings of dinner, I felt like a landwhale and couldn't move from the couch.
Whenever he eats too much, he says he feels like a landwhale for hours afterwards.
She regretted the extra slice of cake, admitting she was feeling like a landwhale all afternoon.

Synset Examples

He felt like a landwhale after the buffet.
