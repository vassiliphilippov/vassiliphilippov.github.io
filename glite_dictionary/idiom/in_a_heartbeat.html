in a heartbeat

1. ADV

/ ɪn ə ˈhɑrtˌbit / (AmE), / ɪn ə ˈhɑːtˌbiːt / (BrE)
immediately; without any hesitation or delay.
instantly; without hesitation

Examples

She would accept the job in a heartbeat if it were offered.
I'd do it again in a heartbeat—no questions asked.
He said he'd help me in a heartbeat whenever I needed.

Synset Examples

He agreed to help her in a heartbeat.
I would travel the world in a heartbeat.
She would change jobs in a heartbeat if offered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at once - immediately, without delay
  • Synonym: without delay - immediately, straight away
  • Antonym: after a while - after some time has passed
