on the wing

1. ADV

/ ɑn ðə wɪŋ / (AmE), / ɒn ðə wɪŋ / (BrE)
in flight; while flying through the air, especially of birds or insects
in flight; flying through the air

Examples

We watched a flock of geese on the wing as they migrated south for the winter.
The photographer captured a butterfly on the wing, its wings blurred by motion.
Swallows are agile hunters, catching insects on the wing at remarkable speed.

Synset Examples

we saw the ducks in flight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in flight - while flying, especially of birds; airborne
