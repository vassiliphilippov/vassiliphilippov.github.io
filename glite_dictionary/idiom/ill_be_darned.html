I'll be darned

1. INTJ

/ aɪl bi ˈdɑrn(d) / (AmE), / aɪl bi ˈdɑːnd / (BrE)
Used to express surprise or disbelief.
expression of surprise or disbelief
informal

Examples

I'll be darned, I never thought you'd make it on time!
When I saw the final score, all I could say was 'I'll be darned.'
I'll be darned if that isn't the strangest thing I've ever seen.

Synset Examples

I'll be darned, she won the competition!
I'll be darned if he actually showed up.
I'll be darned, that was a great performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: I'll be damned - expression of surprise or disbelief (stronger/less polite version)
