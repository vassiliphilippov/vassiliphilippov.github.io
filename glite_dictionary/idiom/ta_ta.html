ta ta

1. INTJ

/ tɑ ˈtɑ / (AmE), / tɑ ˈtɑ / (BrE)
an informal way of saying goodbye
informal farewell
informal british

Examples

She smiled and said ta ta as she left the party.
"Ta ta, see you next time!" he called from the doorway.
After our coffee, we hugged and said ta ta before parting ways.

Synset Examples

She waved goodbye, saying ta ta to everyone.
I have to go now, ta ta!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - used to express good wishes when parting
  • Synonym: farewell - an expression used to say goodbye

Etymology

Originated in early 20th-century British English, imitating a child's word for goodbye.
