to have one's wig snatched

1. VERB

/ hæv wʌnz wɪɡ snætʃt / (AmE), / hav wʌnz wɪɡ snætʃt / (BrE)
To experience a sudden and shocking surprise or revelation.
to be suddenly and shockingly surprised
slang

Examples

When I saw the plot twist at the end of the movie, I felt like I had my wig snatched.
His performance was so incredible that everyone in the audience basically had their wig snatched.
After hearing the shocking announcement, the team was left having their wig snatched.

Synset Examples

The unexpected news felt like having her wig snatched.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blown away - to be amazed or astonished

Etymology

From the slang image of a wig being abruptly removed, popularized in drag and internet culture to indicate being stunned or overwhelmed.
