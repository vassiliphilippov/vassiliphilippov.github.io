to can't get enough of

1. VERB

/ kænt ɡɛt ɪˈnʌf ʌv / (AmE), / kɑːnt ɡɛt ɪˈnʌf ɒv / (BrE)
to like or enjoy something so much that you always want more of it
enjoy something very much; always want more
informal

Examples

I can't get enough of this new TV series; it's so addictive!
She can't get enough of chocolate ice cream, eating it almost every day.
My friends can't get enough of that band's music since their last concert.

Synset Examples

He can't get enough of that delicious pizza.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: love - to have a great affection for something or someone
  • Antonym: hate - to dislike intensely or passionately
