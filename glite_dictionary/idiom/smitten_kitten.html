smitten kitten

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmɪtn ˈkɪtn / (AmE), / ˈsmɪtn ˈkɪtn / (BrE)
a person who is deeply infatuated or lovestruck, especially in a noticeably obvious or endearing way
person who is infatuated or lovestruck
informal

Examples

Ever since he met her, he's been a total smitten kitten, grinning at his phone all day.
She turned into a smitten kitten after their romantic dinner by the river.
Everyone could tell he was a smitten kitten because he couldn't stop talking about his new crush.

Synset Examples

That smitten kitten can't stop talking about him.
He acts like a smitten kitten around her.
She's a smitten kitten ever since their first date.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infatuated - experiencing an intense but short-lived passion or admiration for someone
  • Synonym: lovestruck - affected powerfully and suddenly by love for someone
