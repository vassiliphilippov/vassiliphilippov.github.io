and then some

1. ADV

/ ənd ðɛn sʌm / (AmE), / ənd ðɛn sʌm / (BrE)
used to indicate that there is more than what has already been mentioned; and considerably more in addition.
and much more in addition

Examples

The project took all weekend and then some to complete.
Her generosity extends to her neighbors and then some.
We reached our fundraising goal and then some thanks to everyone’s help.

Synset Examples

it cost me a week's salary and then some
