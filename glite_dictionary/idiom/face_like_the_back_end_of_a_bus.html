face like the back end of a bus

1. NOUN

/ feɪs laɪk ði bæk ɛnd əv ə bʌs / (AmE), / feɪs laɪk ðə bæk end əv ə bʌs / (BrE)
(informal) Used to describe someone with a very unattractive or unpleasant face.
an extremely unattractive face (informal)
informal british

Examples

He was so self-deprecating, often saying he had a face like the back end of a bus.
When she woke up with no makeup, she joked she looked like she had a face like the back end of a bus.
It’s rude to say someone has a face like the back end of a bus, so please be kind!

Synset Examples

After the night out, he had a face like the back end of a bus.
