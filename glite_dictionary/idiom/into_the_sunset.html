into the sunset

1. ADV

/ ˈɪn.tu ðə ˈsʌn.sɛt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tu ðə ˈsʌn.sɛt / (BrE)
toward a happy, romantic, or ideal future, especially at the end of a story or period in one's life
toward a happy, ideal future

Examples

After retiring, they decided to travel the world and ride into the sunset together.
The movie ends with the hero walking into the sunset, symbolizing a new beginning.
They sold their business and moved into the sunset phase of their lives.
