out of my league

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv maɪ liːɡ / (AmE), / aʊt əv maɪ liːɡ / (BrE)
at a much higher level than one can match or is qualified for, especially in social or romantic contexts
beyond one's level or ability

Examples

Ever since he met her, he felt completely out of my league.
That advanced math competition is out of my league, so I didn't enter.
I tried to join the elite chess club but quickly realized it was out of my league.

Synset Examples

Dating her feels like I'm out of my league.
That job is definitely out of my league.
He realized she was out of his league after the first date.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of your league - beyond your own ability or social status
