for kicks

1. ADV

/ fər kɪks / (AmE), / fə kɪks / (BrE)
for fun or enjoyment; just for pleasure rather than for any serious purpose
for fun or enjoyment
informal

Examples

We went skydiving just for kicks, not because we wanted to break any records.
She signed up for the dance class for kicks and ended up loving it.
They tried the spicy dish at the new restaurant for kicks.

Synset Examples

They organized a talent show for kicks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for fun - for enjoyment rather than for a serious reason
