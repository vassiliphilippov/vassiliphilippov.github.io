to carry the mail

1. VERB

/ ˈkɛri ðə meɪl / (AmE), / ˈkæri ðə meɪl / (BrE)
to reliably fulfill one's responsibilities or duties, especially in a competent or effective manner
fulfill responsibilities or duties

You can always count on him to carry the mail when a big project comes his way.
If we want this event to succeed, we need someone who can really carry the mail.
