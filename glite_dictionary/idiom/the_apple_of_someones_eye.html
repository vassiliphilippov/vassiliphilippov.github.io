the apple of someone's eye

1. NOUN

/ ði ˈæpəl əv ˈsʌmwʌnz aɪ / (AmE), / ði ˈæpəl əv ˈsʌmwənz aɪ / (BrE)
a person who is cherished, loved, or highly valued above all others by someone
someone cherished or beloved

Examples

Ever since his daughter was born, she has been the apple of his eye.
As a teacher, her star pupil quickly became the apple of her eye.
The little boy is clearly the apple of his grandmother's eye.

Synset Examples

Her son is the apple of her eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: darling - a person dearly loved; a favorite
  • Synonym: favorite - a person who is preferred over others

Etymology

Originally from Old English and biblical texts, referring metaphorically to the pupil of the eye, seen as precious and protected.
