offer he couldn't refuse

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑː.fɚ hi ˈkʊdnt rɪˈfjuz / (AmE), / ˈɒ.fə hi ˈkʊdnt rɪˈfjuːz / (BrE)
an extremely attractive or compelling proposal that cannot be rejected, often because of its advantages or the consequences of refusal
irresistible proposal or deal

Examples

When the company doubled his salary, it was an offer he couldn't refuse.
She made me an offer he couldn't refuse on that vintage car.
For the right price, every collectible becomes an offer he couldn't refuse.

Synset Examples

She presented an opportunity that was an offer he couldn't refuse.
The promotion was an offer he couldn't refuse.
He made her an offer she couldn't refuse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irresistible offer - an offer that cannot be resisted or rejected

Etymology

Popularized by the 1972 film The Godfather, derived from the phrase 'make someone an offer they can't refuse.'
