to work hard and play hard

1. VERB

/ wɜrk hɑrd ənd pleɪ hɑrd / (AmE), / wɜːk hɑːd ənd pleɪ hɑːd / (BrE)
to put a lot of effort into one's work and also actively enjoy one's leisure time
put effort into both work and leisure

Examples

At our company, we believe in the motto: work hard and play hard.
She managed to work hard and play hard throughout her time at university.
Many successful people claim that to have a balanced life, you must work hard and play hard.

Synset Examples

She wants to work hard and play hard, but can't have it both ways.
