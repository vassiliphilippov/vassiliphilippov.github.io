fair and square

1. ADV

/ ˌfɛr ənd ˈskwɛr / (AmE), / ˌfeə ənd ˈskweə / (BrE)
in a just and honest way; according to the rules or with complete fairness
in a just and honest way

Examples

She won the competition fair and square by following all the rules.
He told me fair and square that he wasn't interested in the project.
We settled our disagreement fair and square, with no hard feelings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honest - free of deceit; truthful and sincere
  • Antonym: deceitful - dishonest or misleading
