spring in someone's step

1. NOUN

/ sprɪŋ ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz stɛp / (AmE), / sprɪŋ ɪn ˈsʌmwənz stɛp / (BrE)
an energetic and lively way of walking, often showing happiness or confidence
energetic, lively manner of walking

Examples

After the good news, there was a noticeable spring in his step as he walked to work.
She greeted everyone with a smile and a spring in her step on Monday morning.
Winning the award put a real spring in his step for the rest of the week.

Synset Examples

He walked down the street with a spring in his step.
After her promotion, she had a noticeable spring in her step.
The children danced with a spring in their step at the fair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bounce in one's step - energetic or lively way of walking
