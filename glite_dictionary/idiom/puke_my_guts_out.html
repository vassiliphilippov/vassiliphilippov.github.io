to puke my guts out

1. VERB

/ pjuːk maɪ ɡʌts aʊt / (AmE), / pjuːk maɪ ɡʌts aʊt / (BrE)
to vomit violently and repeatedly, especially as a result of illness or excessive drinking
vomit violently and repeatedly
informal slang

Examples

I was so sick with the flu that I thought I would puke my guts out all night.
After eating the spoiled food, she had to puke my guts out in the bathroom.
He drank too much at the club and ended up puking my guts out outside.

Synset Examples

After the party, I felt like I would puke my guts out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to throw up - to vomit (more neutral or standard term)
  • Degree Lower: retch - make the sound and movement of vomiting without bringing anything up
