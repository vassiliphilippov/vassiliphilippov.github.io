quiet as a mouse

1. ADJ

/ ˈkwaɪət əz ə maʊs / (AmE), / ˈkwaɪət əz ə maʊs / (BrE)
extremely quiet or silent, especially so as not to be noticed
extremely quiet or silent

Examples

During the test, the students sat quiet as a mouse so they wouldn't disturb each other.
She entered the dark room quiet as a mouse to avoid waking her family.
He remained quiet as a mouse when the teacher called on someone to answer the question.

Synset Examples

The children were quiet as a mouse during the movie.
He crept in, quiet as a mouse, hoping not to wake anyone.
She sat there, quiet as a mouse, not wanting to disturb anyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silent as the grave - completely silent
  • Antonym: noisy - making a lot of noise
