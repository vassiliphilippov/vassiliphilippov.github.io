to grab a bite

1. VERB

/ ɡræb ə baɪt / (AmE), / ɡræb ə baɪt / (BrE)
to have a quick meal, especially informally or in a hurry
have a quick meal
informal

Examples

We have twenty minutes before the meeting, so let's grab a bite nearby.
Before his next class, Mark decided to grab a bite at the café.
Do you want to grab a bite after work?

Synset Examples

It's sandwich time; let's grab a bite!
Let's grab a bite to eat before the movie.
I'm Hank Marvin; let's grab some food!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to have a bite - to eat a small amount of food, especially quickly
