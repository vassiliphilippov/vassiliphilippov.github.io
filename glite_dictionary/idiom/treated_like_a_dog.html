treated like a dog

1. X

/ ˈtritɪd laɪk ə dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈtriːtɪd laɪk ə dɒɡ / (BrE)
Treated poorly or with a lack of respect.
treated poorly; disrespected

Examples

After years at that company, he felt he was treated like a dog and finally quit.
She was tired of being treated like a dog by the other members of the team.
Nobody wants to be treated like a dog just because they made a mistake.

Synset Examples

He always gets treated like a dog in meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treated like dirt - treated as unimportant or with contempt
  • Antonym: treated with respect - treated in an honorable or considerate way
