arrow in someone's quiver

1. NOUN

/ ˈæroʊ ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈkwɪvər / (AmE), / ˈærəʊ ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈkwɪvə / (BrE)
an additional skill, resource, or advantage that someone can make use of when needed
an added skill or resource

Examples

Learning a new programming language gave her another arrow in her quiver at work.
Having strong communication skills is an important arrow in your quiver when managing a team.
The extra training provided him with an additional arrow in his quiver for handling complex situations.

Synset Examples

She added language skills as an arrow in her quiver.
Having a mentor is an important arrow in my quiver.
This new strategy is another arrow in our quiver.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tool in someone's toolbox - a useful resource or skill available to someone

Etymology

From the imagery of archery, where a quiver holds multiple arrows for different uses.
