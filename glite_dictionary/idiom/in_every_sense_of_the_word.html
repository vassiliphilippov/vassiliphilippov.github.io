in every sense of the word

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈɛvri sɛns əv ðə wɜrd / (AmE), / ɪn ˈɛvri sɛns əv ðə wɜːd / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is true in every possible way or interpretation of the word mentioned
true in all respects or interpretations

Examples

He was a gentleman in every sense of the word—kind, honest, and generous.
Their trip to Antarctica was an adventure in every sense of the word.
She is a professional in every sense of the word, always punctual and prepared.

Synset Examples

He is a leader in every sense of the word.
She is a hero in every sense of the word.
This is a challenge in every sense of the word.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: literally - truly and in a factual sense
