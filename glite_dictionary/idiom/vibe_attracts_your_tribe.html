vibe attracts your tribe

1. X

/ vaɪb əˈtrækt͡s jɚ traɪb / (AmE), / vaɪb əˈtrækts jə traɪb / (BrE)
your attitude or energy influences the kind of people you attract into your life
your energy attracts like-minded people
informal

Examples

She always says 'vibe attracts your tribe' when explaining why she surrounds herself with positive people.
Remember that vibe attracts your tribe, so try to keep a friendly attitude at work.
If you want reliable friends, focus on being trustworthy yourself because vibe attracts your tribe.

Synset Examples
