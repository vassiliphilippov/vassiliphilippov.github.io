blue moon

1. NOUN

/ bluː ˈmuːn / (AmE), / bluː ˈmuːn / (BrE)
A very rare occurrence; something that happens only at long intervals.
something very rare; rare occurrence

Examples

Events like this only happen once in a blue moon.
Seeing a comet with the naked eye is a blue moon experience.
He visits his hometown once in a blue moon.

Synset Examples

something that happens once in blue moon almost never happens

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rare event - an event that does not happen often
  • Antonym: common occurrence - something that happens frequently

Etymology

From the rare astronomical phenomenon where a second full moon appears in a calendar month, called a 'blue moon'.
