to keep it up

1. VERB

/ kip ɪt ʌp / (AmE), / kiːp ɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to continue doing something well or at the same level; to maintain one's effort or performance
continue good work or effort

Examples

Your project is impressive, so keep it up and you'll impress your boss.
You've trained very hard—keep it up for the final week before the race.
If you keep it up, you'll master the skill in no time.

Synset Examples

Keep it up, and you'll reach your goals soon.
If you want to succeed, just keep it up.
You're doing great, just keep it up!
If you keep that up, I kill you later.
If you keep this up, you'll be late for your own funeral.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to keep going - to continue without stopping
  • Antonym: to give up - to stop trying or abandon an effort
