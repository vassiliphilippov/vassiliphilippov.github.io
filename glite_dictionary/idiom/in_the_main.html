in the main

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðə meɪn / (AmE), / ɪn ðə meɪn / (BrE)
for the most part; generally; usually, without making distinctions between particular cases
generally; for the most part

Examples

In the main, the students performed well on the exam, though there were a few exceptions.
The changes have, in the main, improved the company's performance.
Their views are, in the main, supportive of the new proposal.

Synset Examples

he is interested in snakes in general

Related Terms

  • Synonym: generally - usually; in most cases
  • Synonym: on the whole - overall; taking everything into account
  • Antonym: specifically - clearly defined or identified, as opposed to generally
