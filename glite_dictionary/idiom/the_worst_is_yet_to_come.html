the worst is yet to come

1. X

/ ðə wɝst ɪz jɛt tu kʌm / (AmE), / ðə wɜːst ɪz jɛt tuː kʌm / (BrE)
used to say that although bad things have happened, even more serious or difficult problems may happen in the future
even worse events may still happen

Examples

After seeing the first quarter's losses, she warned that the worst is yet to come.
Despite the recent difficulties, many believe the worst is yet to come for the company.
The politician ended his speech by saying that the worst is yet to come unless action is taken.

Synset Examples

I fear the worst is yet to come.
