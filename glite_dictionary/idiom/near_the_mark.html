near the mark

1. ADJ

/ nɪr ðə mɑrk / (AmE), / nɪə ðə mɑːk / (BrE)
close to being correct or accurate; nearly right.
almost correct or accurate

Examples

Her guess was near the mark, just a bit off.
His prediction was surprisingly near the mark this time.
Your answer is near the mark, but you need a little more detail.
