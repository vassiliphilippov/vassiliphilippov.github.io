to make an ass of oneself

1. VERB

/ meɪk ən æs əv wʌnˌsɛlf / (AmE), / meɪk ən ɑːs əv wʌnˌsɛlf / (BrE)
to behave in a way that makes oneself look foolish or ridiculous in front of others
embarrass oneself; look foolish
informal vulgar

Examples

He didn't want to make an ass of himself during the interview, so he prepared thoroughly.
If you forget your lines on stage, you'll only make an ass of yourself in front of the audience.
She realized too late that her joke might make an ass of herself at the meeting.

Synset Examples

Don’t make an ass of your team during the presentation.
She felt she’d make an ass of herself if she spoke up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to make a fool of oneself - to behave stupidly or embarrass oneself
  • Antonym: to save face - to avoid embarrassment or preserve dignity

Etymology

From the use of 'ass' meaning 'fool' or 'idiot', originating from the animal, associated in English with stupidity.
