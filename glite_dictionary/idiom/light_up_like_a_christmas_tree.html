to light up like a Christmas tree

1. VERB

/ laɪt ʌp laɪk ə ˈkrɪs.məs ˌtri / (AmE), / laɪt ʌp laɪk ə ˈkrɪs.məs ˌtriː / (BrE)
(of a person or their expression) to appear suddenly very excited, happy, or animated, often with a visible change in facial expression or demeanor.
to become visibly excited or happy

Examples

Her son’s face lit up like a Christmas tree when he spotted the puppy in the window.
Whenever he talks about his favorite hobby, his whole demeanor lights up like a Christmas tree.
The team lit up like a Christmas tree after hearing they'd won the championship.

Synset Examples

His eyes light up like a Christmas tree whenever he talks about his new project.
The kids light up like a Christmas tree when they open gifts.
Her face lit up like a Christmas tree when she saw the surprise party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to light up - to become brighter or look very happy
