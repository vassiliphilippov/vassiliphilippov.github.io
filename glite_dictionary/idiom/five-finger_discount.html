five-finger discount

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪv ˌfɪŋɡɚ dɪsˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪv ˌfɪŋɡə dɪsˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
the act of stealing, especially shoplifting, often used humorously or as a euphemism
euphemism for stealing/shoplifting
informal slang

Examples

He was caught trying to get a five-finger discount at the electronics store.
Some teenagers think a five-finger discount is a harmless prank, but it's still a crime.
She joked about using a five-finger discount when she couldn't afford the shoes.

Synset Examples

He got caught trying to use a five-finger discount.
She joked about getting a five-finger discount on her lunch.
He got that jacket through a five-finger discount.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoplifting - the act of stealing goods from a shop
  • Synonym: steal - to take without permission or legal right

Etymology

Humorous formation referring to stealing with one's hand (five fingers).
