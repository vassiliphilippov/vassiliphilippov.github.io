until the cows come home

1. ADV

/ ənˈtɪl ðə kaʊz kʌm hoʊm / (AmE), / ənˈtɪl ðə kaʊz kʌm həʊm / (BrE)
for a very long time; forever or seemingly without end
for a very long time

Examples

She could read that book until the cows come home and still find something new.
