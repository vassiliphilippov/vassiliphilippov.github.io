from soup to nuts

1. ADV

/ frəm suːp tə nʌts / (AmE), / frəm suːp tə nʌts / (BrE)
Including everything; from beginning to end or covering all aspects of something.
including everything, from start to finish

Examples

The company will manage your project from soup to nuts, so you don't have to worry about a thing.
Her trip was planned from soup to nuts, including flights, hotels, and tours.
The report covers every detail from soup to nuts to ensure nothing is missed.

Synset Examples

Their services cover the project from soup to nuts.
The wedding planner handled everything from soup to nuts.
The meal included everything from soup to nuts.
Her presentation covered everything soup-to-nuts.
The event was planned soup-to-nuts by the committee.
They provide soup-to-nuts services for every occasion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: from start to finish - from beginning to end
  • Synonym: soup-to-nuts - adjective or adverb meaning 'comprehensive' or 'complete'

Etymology

Refers to the traditional structure of a large multi-course meal, which began with soup and ended with nuts.
