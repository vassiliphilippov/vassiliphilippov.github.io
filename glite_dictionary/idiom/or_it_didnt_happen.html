or it didn't happen

1. X

/ ɔr ɪt ˈdɪdənt ˈhæpən / (AmE), / ɔːr ɪt ˈdɪdnt ˈhæpən / (BrE)
used to express doubt about the truth of a statement, demanding evidence or proof
expression of skepticism; demand for proof
informal

Examples

You say you climbed the tallest mountain? Prove it, or it didn't happen.
He claims he scored the winning goal, but or it didn't happen.
She often tells wild stories, so I always reply, 'or it didn't happen.'

Synset Examples

He said he met a celebrity, but I say or it didn't happen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pics or it didn't happen - internet phrase demanding photographic proof of a claim
