to employ a steam engine to crack a nut

1. VERB

/ ɪmˈplɔɪ ə stiːm ˈɛn.dʒɪn tə kræk ə nʌt / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔɪ ə stiːm ˈɛn.dʒɪn tə kræk ə nʌt / (BrE)
to use much more effort, power, or resources than necessary to solve a simple problem
use excessive means for a simple task

Examples

Building a custom website for a simple contact form seems like employing a steam engine to crack a nut.
She brought a whole legal team for a minor disagreement, truly employing a steam engine to crack a nut.
Writing a fifty-page report to explain one small error feels like employing a steam engine to crack a nut.

Synset Examples

That's like employing a steam engine to crack a nut!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: use a sledgehammer to crack a nut - to use disproportionate force or resources for a simple task
