to sit on one's ass

1. VERB

/ sɪt ɑn wʌnz æs / (AmE), / sɪt ɒn wʌnz as / (BrE)
to be inactive or lazy, especially instead of working or making an effort
be inactive or lazy (idiom)
informal vulgar

Examples

Instead of studying for his exams, he just sat on his ass watching television all weekend.
She tends to sit on her ass while everyone else does the hard work.
Don't sit on your ass; help me clean up the house.

Synset Examples

He tends to sit on his ass instead of helping out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laze around - to spend time being idle or avoiding work
  • Antonym: get off one's ass - begin to take action; stop being lazy
