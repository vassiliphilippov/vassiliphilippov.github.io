his word is law

1. X

/ hɪz wɝːd ɪz lɑ / (AmE), / hɪz wɜːd ɪz lɔː / (BrE)
used to say that someone's decisions or commands must be obeyed without question
one's authority is absolute

Examples

In his household, his word is law and everyone follows his rules.
When it comes to software design, his word is law on the team.
The manager made it clear: his word is law during the busy season.

Synset Examples

At work, his word is law when it comes to project decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to call the shots - to be in control and make the decisions
  • Synonym: to rule the roost - to be the person in charge; to be dominant
