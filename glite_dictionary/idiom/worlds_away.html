worlds away

1. ADV

/ wɜrldz əˈweɪ / (AmE), / wɜːldz əˈweɪ / (BrE)
extremely distant or very different in character, feeling, or situation.
very distant or very different

Examples

The bustling city now felt worlds away from the quiet countryside.
Even though we live in the same town, our lives are worlds away from each other.
Her carefree college days seemed worlds away as she managed her hectic job.

Synset Examples

His dreams seemed worlds away from reality.
In that moment, he was worlds away from his troubles.
She felt worlds away from her childhood home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: far apart - at a great distance from each other
  • Antonym: close - near in space or relationship
