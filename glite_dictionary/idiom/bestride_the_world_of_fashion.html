to bestride the world of fashion

1. VERB

/ bɪˈstraɪd ðə wɝːld əv ˈfæʃən / (AmE), / bɪˈstraɪd ðə ˈwɜːld əv ˈfæʃən / (BrE)
To dominate or excel in the fashion industry.
dominate the fashion industry

Examples

With her innovative designs, she managed to bestride the world of fashion within just a few years.
Many aspire to bestride the world of fashion, but only a few truly leave a mark.
He hopes one day his label will bestride the world of fashion as Chanel once did.

Synset Examples

She will bestride the world of fashion soon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to rule the roost - to be in control or dominate a group or activity
  • Antonym: to fall behind - to fail to keep up with others in progress
