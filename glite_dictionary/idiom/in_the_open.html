in the open

1. ADV

/ ɪn ði ˈoʊ.pən / (AmE), / ɪn ði ˈəʊ.pən / (BrE)
In a way that is public and not hidden; without concealment.
publicly; without concealment

Examples

The two leaders discussed the controversial issue in the open to promote transparency.
It is healthier to talk about your feelings in the open rather than bottling them up.
They finally admitted their mistake in the open, much to everyone's surprise.

Synset Examples

When you're ready, let’s have it out in the open.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: openly - in a manner that is not concealed or secret; frankly
  • Antonym: in private - not in public; secretly
