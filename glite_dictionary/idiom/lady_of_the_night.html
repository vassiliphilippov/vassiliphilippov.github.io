lady of the night

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪdi əv ðə naɪt / (AmE), / ˈleɪdi əv ðə naɪt / (BrE)
A polite or euphemistic term for a prostitute.
euphemism for prostitute

Examples

The detective interviewed a lady of the night who might have witnessed the crime.
He wrote a novel about the struggles faced by a lady of the night in Victorian London.
In some cities, the term lady of the night is used to describe women engaged in prostitution.
