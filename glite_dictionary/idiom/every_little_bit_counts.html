every little bit counts

1. X

/ ˈɛv.ri ˈlɪt̬.əl bɪt kaʊnts / (AmE), / ˈɛv.ri ˈlɪt.əl bɪt kaʊnts / (BrE)
Even small efforts or contributions are important and make a difference when added together.
small contributions are valuable

Examples

Every little bit counts when you're saving for a big trip, so put aside whatever you can.
Even though my donation was small, I knew that every little bit counts for the cause.
When cleaning up the park, every little bit counts toward making a difference in our community.

Synset Examples

Every little bit counts; don't forget to get penny for charity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: every little helps - every small contribution is useful
