to know your beans

1. VERB

/ noʊ jɔr binz / (AmE), / nəʊ jɔː bɪːnz / (BrE)
to be knowledgeable or well-informed about a particular subject
be knowledgeable about something

Examples

If you want to work here, you really have to know your beans about computers.
She certainly knows her beans when it comes to Italian cuisine.
The new manager didn't know his beans about the industry and struggled at first.

Synset Examples

Being a Bernista means knowing your beans well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: know your stuff - to be very knowledgeable or skilled in a particular area
  • Synonym: be an expert - to have a lot of knowledge or skill in a field
