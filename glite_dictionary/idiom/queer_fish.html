queer fish

1. NOUN

/ kwɪr fɪʃ / (AmE), / kwɪə fɪʃ / (BrE)
a person who is considered strange, unconventional, or eccentric
strange or eccentric person

Examples

The new teacher was something of a queer fish, always wearing mismatched socks and quoting obscure poets.
She admitted to being a bit of a queer fish in her conservative hometown.
At family gatherings, Uncle Albert stood out as a queer fish with his peculiar hobbies and stories.

Synset Examples

He's always felt like a queer fish among his peers.
Being a queer fish has its challenges, but it's also liberating.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: odd duck - an unusual or peculiar person
  • Synonym: eccentric - a person who behaves in a noticeably unconventional way
  • Antonym: conformist - a person who conforms to accepted behavior or established practices
