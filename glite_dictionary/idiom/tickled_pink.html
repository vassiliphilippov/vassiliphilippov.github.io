tickled pink

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɪkəld pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈtɪkəld pɪŋk / (BrE)
Very pleased or delighted; extremely happy or amused.
very pleased or delighted

Examples

I was tickled pink when I heard that I'd won the contest.
She was tickled pink by the clever birthday card her son made.
They were tickled pink to see their old friends after many years.

Synset Examples

He was tickled pink to receive the surprise gift.
Receiving her promotion made her tickled pink.
The compliments from friends left him tickled pink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delighted - feeling or showing great pleasure
  • Synonym: overjoyed - extremely happy
  • Antonym: displeased - not pleased or satisfied
