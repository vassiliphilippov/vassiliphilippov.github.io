cloud-cuckoo-land

1. NOUN

/ ˌklaʊd ˈkuːkuː ˌlænd / (AmE), / ˌklaʊd ˈkuːkuː ˌlænd / (BrE)
an imaginary state or place where people are out of touch with reality, especially due to extreme or unrealistic optimism
state of unrealistic optimism

Examples

If you think the project will finish on time, you must be living in cloud-cuckoo-land.
Her plans for world peace were dismissed as being from cloud-cuckoo-land.
He accused the government of policies dreamed up in cloud-cuckoo-land.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fantasy world - an imagined or unreal place or state
  • Antonym: reality - the world or the state of things as they actually exist

Etymology

Originally from German 'Wolkenkuckucksheim', first used in Aristophanes' comedy 'The Birds'.
