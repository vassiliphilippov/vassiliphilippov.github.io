a world of possibilities

1. NOUN

/ ə wɜrld əv pəˈsɪbələtiz / (AmE), / ə wɜːld əv pəˈsɪbɪlətiz / (BrE)
a situation in which there are many different options, opportunities, or outcomes available
many different options or opportunities

Examples

Winning the scholarship opened a world of possibilities for her future career.
The internet creates a world of possibilities for learning new skills.
With renewable energy, we have a world of possibilities for improving our environment.

Synset Examples

He opened a world of possibilities with his invention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endless possibilities - an unlimited number of options
  • Antonym: no choice - a lack of options or alternatives
