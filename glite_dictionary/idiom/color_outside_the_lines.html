to color outside the lines

1. VERB

/ ˈkʌlər aʊtˈsaɪd ðə laɪnz / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə aʊtˈsaɪd ðə laɪnz / (BrE)
to act or think in a creative or unconventional way, not restricted by traditional rules or expectations
act or think unconventionally

Examples

Great inventors are willing to color outside the lines to find unique solutions.
Teachers encourage students to color outside the lines and express their individuality.
Sometimes it's good to color outside the lines and break from tradition.

Synset Examples

He loves to color outside the lines when he draws.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: think outside the box - to think creatively, unrestrained by conventional ideas
