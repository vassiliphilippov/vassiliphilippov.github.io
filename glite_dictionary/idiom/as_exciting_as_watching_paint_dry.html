as exciting as watching paint dry

1. X

/ əz ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋ əz ˈwɑtʃɪŋ peɪnt draɪ / (AmE), / əz ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋ əz ˈwɒtʃɪŋ peɪnt draɪ / (BrE)
used to describe something that is extremely boring or uninteresting
extremely boring; uninteresting
informal

Examples

The documentary was as exciting as watching paint dry, so I nearly fell asleep halfway through.
I've never seen a game of chess move so slowly; it was as exciting as watching paint dry.
His speech was as exciting as watching paint dry and didn't hold the audience's attention at all.

Synset Examples

He said the presentation was as exciting as watching paint dry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boring - not interesting; dull
  • Antonym: thrilling - exciting; causing strong excitement
