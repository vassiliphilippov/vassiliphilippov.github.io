down with the kids

1. ADJ

/ daʊn wɪð ðə kɪdz / (AmE), / daʊn wɪð ðə kɪdz / (BrE)
aware of or familiar with trends popular among younger generations; in touch with youth culture or modern ideas
familiar with youth culture or trends
informal

Examples

He tries to stay down with the kids by using the latest slang at work.
The new teacher is really down with the kids and knows all the popular bands.
Some brands update their image to appear more down with the kids in advertising.

Synset Examples

Her style keeps her down with the kids.
Being down with the kids helps her relate better.
