down and out

1. ADJ

/ daʊn ənd aʊt / (AmE), / daʊn ən aʊt / (BrE)
In a state of poverty, destitution, or extreme difficulty, often lacking the basic necessities of life.
destitute or extremely poor

Examples

After losing his business, he ended up down and out on the streets.
The movie follows a down and out boxer struggling to make a comeback.
Despite being down and out, she never gave up hope for a better future.

Synset Examples

She was down and out but found a new opportunity.
After losing his job, he felt down and out.
The charity helps those who are down and out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: destitute - lacking the basic necessities of life
  • Antonym: prosperous - wealthy and successful
