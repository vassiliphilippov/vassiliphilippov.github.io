to draw a bead on

1. VERB

/ drɔ ə bid ɑn / (AmE), / drɔː ə biːd ɒn / (BrE)
to aim carefully at someone or something, especially with a gun
to aim at, especially with a gun

Examples

The sniper drew a bead on his target and waited for the perfect moment to fire.
She quietly drew a bead on the deer, careful not to make a sound.
Just as the robber tried to escape, the officer drew a bead on him and ordered him to stop.

Synset Examples

The hunter drew a bead on the rabbit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to aim at - to point or direct a weapon or object toward a target

Etymology

From the use of the 'bead' (small metal sight) on a gun barrel to assist with aiming.
