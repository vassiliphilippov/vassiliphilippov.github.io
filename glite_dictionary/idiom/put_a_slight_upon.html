to put a slight upon

1. VERB

/ pʊt ə slaɪt əˈpɑn / (AmE), / pʊt ə slaɪt əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to show disrespect or insult to someone; to treat with disdain or disregard
show disrespect or insult

Examples

He was careful not to put a slight upon his colleagues during the meeting.
She was deeply hurt when they put a slight upon her efforts in public.
To put a slight upon old traditions is considered disrespectful in some cultures.

Synset Examples

She felt he put a slight upon her hard work.
Don’t put a slight upon your friends with those comments.
His remarks put a slight upon her achievements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insult - to speak or treat with disrespect or scorn
  • Antonym: honor - to show respect towards someone
