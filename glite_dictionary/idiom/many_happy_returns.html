many happy returns

1. X

/ ˌmɛni ˌhæpi rɪˈtɝnz / (AmE), / ˌmɛni ˌhæpi rɪˈtɜːnz / (BrE)
used to express good wishes to someone on their birthday or a special occasion, hoping they experience happiness again and again
expression of good wishes for happiness, esp. on birthdays

Examples

On her birthday, he smiled and said, "Many happy returns of the day!"
Cards wishing 'many happy returns' lined the mantelpiece after his party.
It is traditional to say 'many happy returns' when someone celebrates a significant milestone.

Synset Examples

Her friends wished her many happy returns each year.
He greeted her with many happy returns on her anniversary.
