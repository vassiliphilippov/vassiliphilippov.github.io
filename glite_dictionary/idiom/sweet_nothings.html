sweet nothings

1. NOUN

/ swit ˈnʌθɪŋz / (AmE), / swiːt ˈnʌθɪŋz / (BrE)
Words of affection or endearment spoken to someone, often considered romantic but lacking substantive meaning.
affectionate but insubstantial words

Examples

He leaned over to whisper sweet nothings in her ear during the concert.
Couples often exchange sweet nothings on romantic walks.
She smiled as he murmured sweet nothings to make her blush.

Synset Examples

he whispered sweet nothings into her ear

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endearment - an expression of affection
