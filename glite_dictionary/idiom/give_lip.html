to give lip

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv lɪp / (AmE), / ɡɪv lɪp / (BrE)
to speak to someone in a rude or disrespectful manner; to talk back
speak disrespectfully or talk back
informal

Examples

If you give lip to the coach again, you'll be benched for the season.
Teenagers often give lip when asked to do chores around the house.
The officer warned him not to give lip during the interview.

Synset Examples

She always tries to give lip during meetings.
Don't give lip to your parents; it's disrespectful.
He got grounded for giving lip to the teacher.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to talk back - to reply defiantly or rudely to someone in authority
