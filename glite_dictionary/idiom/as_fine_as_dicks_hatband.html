as fine as Dick's hatband

1. ADJ

/ əz faɪn əz dɪks ˈhætˌbænd / (AmE), / əz faɪn əz dɪks ˈhætˌbænd / (BrE)
Extremely fine or elegant; exceptionally smart or showy in appearance or quality.
very fine or elegant (idiom)

Examples

She arrived at the ball looking as fine as Dick's hatband, turning everyone's heads.
The new curtains are as fine as Dick's hatband and brighten up the entire room.
After his makeover, he felt as fine as Dick's hatband and walked with confidence.

Synset Examples

Her dress is as fine as Dick's hatband.
The meal was as fine as Dick's hatband, truly exquisite.
He looked as fine as Dick's hatband at the wedding.

Etymology

Origin uncertain; references a fanciful or proverbial 'Dick' and his hatband, which became a British and American idiom in the 18th–19th century.
