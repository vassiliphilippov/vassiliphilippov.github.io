to give a good account of oneself

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ə ɡʊd əˈkaʊnt əv ˈwʌnˌsɛlf / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə ɡʊd əˈkaʊnt ɒv ˈwʌnˌsɛlf / (BrE)
to perform well or present oneself in a positive manner, especially in a challenging situation
perform or present oneself well

Examples

She worked hard to give a good account of herself during the interview process.
The young athlete managed to give a good account of himself despite being injured.
If you want to succeed, it's important to give a good account of yourself in every opportunity.

Synset Examples

She always aims to give a good account of herself in meetings.
The team hopes to give a good account of themselves in the finals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make a good impression - to create a favorable initial effect on others
