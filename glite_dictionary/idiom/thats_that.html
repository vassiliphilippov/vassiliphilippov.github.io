that's that

1. X

/ ðæts ðæt / (AmE), / ðats ðat / (BrE)
used to indicate that a decision or situation is final and there will be no further discussion or change
expression of finality or conclusion

Examples

We can't afford a vacation this year, so that's that.
I'm not going to argue anymore—that's that.
The manager made her choice, and that's that.

Synset Examples
