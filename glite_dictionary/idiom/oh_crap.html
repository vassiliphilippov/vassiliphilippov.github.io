oh crap

1. INTJ

/ oʊ kræp / (AmE), / əʊ kræp / (BrE)
used to express frustration, dismay, or annoyance, often upon realizing something unpleasant or making a mistake
expression of frustration or dismay
informal

Examples

Oh crap, I left my keys inside the car again.
Oh crap, the deadline is tomorrow and I haven't started my assignment.
He looked at the broken vase and muttered, "oh crap."

Synset Examples

I spilled coffee on my shirt, and crap, it was my favorite one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oh no - expression of surprise or distress
  • Synonym: damn - mild expletive for annoyance or frustration
