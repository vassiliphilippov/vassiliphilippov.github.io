for the moment

1. ADV

/ fɔr ðə ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / fɔː ðə ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
for a short period of time; temporarily
temporarily; for now

Examples

Let's leave the problem aside for the moment and discuss it later.
For the moment, the shop is closed due to renovations.
We're postponing any decisions for the moment until we have more information.

Synset Examples
