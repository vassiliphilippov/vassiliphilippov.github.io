in the dead of winter

1. ADP

/ ɪn ðə dɛd əv ˈwɪntɚ / (AmE), / ɪn ðə dɛd əv ˈwɪntə / (BrE)
at the coldest and darkest period of winter; in the middle of winter, typically when conditions are harshest
the middle of winter

Examples

The lake froze completely in the dead of winter, surprising everyone in the village.
Traveling by car in the dead of winter can be quite dangerous due to icy roads.
Birds are rarely seen here in the dead of winter, when temperatures drop below freezing.

Synset Examples

We hiked through the forest in the dead of winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: midwinter - the middle of the winter season
