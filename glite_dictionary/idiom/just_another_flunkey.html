just another flunkey

1. NOUN

/ dʒʌst əˈnʌðɚ ˈflʌŋki / (AmE), / dʒʌst əˈnʌðə ˈflʌŋki / (BrE)
used to describe someone regarded as an unimportant or subservient assistant
an unimportant assistant

Examples

After the reorganization, he realized he was just another flunkey with no real influence.
Don't let them treat you like just another flunkey—stand up for yourself.
Despite his title, many saw him as just another flunkey doing tedious work.

Synset Examples

She dismissed him as just another flunkey in the office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flunkey - a person who does minor jobs for someone important
