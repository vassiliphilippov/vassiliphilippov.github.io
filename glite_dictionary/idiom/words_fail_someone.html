words fail (someone)

1. X

/ wɝdz feɪl ˈsʌmwʌn / (AmE), / wɜːdz feɪl ˈsʌmwʌn / (BrE)
used to say that someone is so shocked, surprised, or overwhelmed that they cannot find anything to say
unable to speak due to emotion or surprise

Examples

When she saw the devastation left by the storm, words failed her.
At the sight of the surprise party, words failed him and he stood in stunned silence.
Sometimes words fail me when I try to express how much I appreciate your help.

Synset Examples

At that moment, words failed her completely.
When he saw the surprise, words failed him.
