home away from home

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm əˈweɪ frəm hoʊm / (AmE), / həʊm əˈweɪ frəm həʊm / (BrE)
a place where you feel as comfortable and relaxed as in your own home
place where you feel at home

Examples

The cozy cabin quickly became a home away from home during our summer vacation.
For many students, the campus library is a home away from home during exams.
The hotel prides itself on providing a home away from home for weary travelers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: second home - a place where one spends a lot of time, like a vacation house

2. NOUN

/ hoʊm əˈweɪ frəm hoʊm / (AmE), / həʊm əˈweɪ frəm həʊm / (BrE)
a place where you feel as comfortable as you do in your own home, though it is not your actual home
place as comfortable as your home

Examples

For many international students, the university library becomes a home away from home during exam season.
The friendly atmosphere at the guesthouse made it a real home away from home for weary travelers.
He considers his grandmother's cottage his home away from home when he visits the countryside.
