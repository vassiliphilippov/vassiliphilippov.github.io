out of this world

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv ðɪs wɝːld / (AmE), / aʊt əv ðɪs wɜːld / (BrE)
extremely good or impressive; exceptional.
exceptionally good; impressive

Examples

The performance last night was out of this world and received a standing ovation.
Her chocolate cake is absolutely out of this world—the best I've ever had.
The view from the top of the mountain was out of this world and took my breath away.

Synset Examples
