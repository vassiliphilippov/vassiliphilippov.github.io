to keep the jokes flowing

1. VERB

/ kip ðə dʒoʊks ˈfloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / kiːp ðə dʒəʊks ˈfləʊɪŋ / (BrE)
to maintain a steady and continuous sequence of jokes or humorous remarks
maintain steady humor

Examples

She knew how to keep the jokes flowing during even the dullest meetings.
At the party, his quick wit helped him keep the jokes flowing all night long.
The comedian managed to keep the jokes flowing despite a few interruptions from the audience.

Synset Examples

The straight man kept the jokes flowing smoothly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to keep the conversation going - to maintain a continuous exchange of talk
