dawn to dusk

1. ADV

/ dɔn tə dʌsk / (AmE), / dɔːn tə dʌsk / (BrE)
From the beginning of the day until the evening; throughout the entire day.
all day, from morning to evening

Examples

The guards kept watch dawn to dusk without a break.
She was out in the fields dawn to dusk during the harvest season.
The shop is open dawn to dusk every day in summer.

Synset Examples

They worked from dawn to dusk to finish the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all day - for the entire day
