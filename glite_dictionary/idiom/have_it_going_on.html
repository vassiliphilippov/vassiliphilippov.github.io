to have it going on

1. VERB

/ hæv ɪt ˈɡoʊɪŋ ɑn / (AmE), / hæv ɪt ˈɡəʊɪŋ ɒn / (BrE)
To be impressive, successful, attractive, or lively; to excel in a way that is noticed by others.
be impressive, successful, or lively
informal
