to put the fear of God into

1. VERB

/ pʊt ðə fɪr əv ɡɑd ˈɪntu / (AmE), / pʊt ðə fɪə əv ɡɒd ˈɪntu / (BrE)
to make someone feel very frightened or respect someone or something greatly, often as a result of a warning or display of authority
instill great fear or respect

Examples

The new manager's strict approach put the fear of God into the lazy staff.
That terrifying documentary really put the fear of God into me about climate change.
His angry reaction put the fear of God into everyone in the room.

Synset Examples

That movie put the fear of God into me!
His stern warning put the fear of God into the children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to frighten - to make someone afraid or scared
  • Degree Higher: to terrify - to cause extreme fear
