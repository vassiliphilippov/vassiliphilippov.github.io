as useful as a screen door on a submarine

1. ADJ

/ əz ˈjusfəl æz ə skrin dɔr ɑn ə ˌsʌbˈməriːn / (AmE), / əz ˈjuːsfʊl əz ə skriːn dɔːr ɒn ə ˌsʌbməˈriːn / (BrE)
completely useless; of no practical value or function.
utterly useless idiom

Examples

Trying to fix that broken vase with tape was as useful as a screen door on a submarine.
Most of the advice he gave me turned out to be as useful as a screen door on a submarine.
Bringing a flashlight to the picnic at noon was as useful as a screen door on a submarine.

Synset Examples

His idea was as useful as a screen door on a submarine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: useless - having no use, function, or effective result
  • Synonym: as useful as a chocolate teapot - equally useless idiom for something that is of no practical value
