like a shot

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə ʃɑt / (AmE), / laɪk ə ʃɒt / (BrE)
Immediately and without hesitation; very quickly.
immediately; very quickly

Examples

When I called her name, she turned around like a shot.
If you offered him the job, he'd accept like a shot.
The dog bolted out the door like a shot when it saw the cat.
