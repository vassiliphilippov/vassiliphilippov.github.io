to let it alone

1. VERB

/ lɛt ɪt əˈloʊn / (AmE), / lɛt ɪt əˈləʊn / (BrE)
to leave something undisturbed or unchanged; to choose not to interfere with a situation or object
not interfere; leave as is

Examples

When the cat is sleeping, it's best to let it alone so you don't wake it up.
If the paint still looks fine, I'd just let it alone for now rather than repainting.
Whenever an argument starts to escalate, sometimes the smartest thing is to let it alone.

Synset Examples

Sometimes it's best to let it alone and not interfere.
He figured it was better to let it alone than to argue.
She chose to let it alone instead of stressing about it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leave it alone - to refrain from interfering with something
