to called on the mat

1. VERB

/ kɔld ɑn ðə mæt / (AmE), / kɔːld ɒn ðə mæt / (BrE)
to be reprimanded or held accountable for one's actions, especially by a superior or authority figure.
to be reprimanded by an authority

Examples

After missing several deadlines, Jessica was called on the mat by her manager.
The officer was called on the mat for not following protocols during the operation.
When the accounts didn't add up, the CFO was promptly called on the mat by the board.

Synset Examples

He was called on the mat for his poor performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reprimand - to rebuke someone, especially officially
  • Antonym: to let off the hook - to not hold someone accountable

Etymology

Originally from wrestling, where a participant is called to the mat for a bout; adopted figuratively for being summoned to answer for one's actions.
