good gracious

1. INTJ

/ ɡʊd ˈɡreɪʃəs / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈɡreɪʃəs / (BrE)
used to express surprise, shock, or amazement
expression of surprise or amazement

Examples

Good gracious, is that really you after all these years?
Good gracious, I didn't expect to see the house in such a mess!
When the thunder shook the windows, she exclaimed, "Good gracious!"

Synset Examples

Good gracious, that dog is huge!
When she won the award, I said, good gracious!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodness gracious - expression of surprise or shock
  • Synonym: good heavens - expression of astonishment or surprise
