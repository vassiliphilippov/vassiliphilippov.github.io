to rub one's hands together

1. VERB

/ rʌb wʌnz hændz təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / rʌb wʌnz hændz təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to move one's hands rapidly across each other, typically as a sign of expectation, excitement, or satisfaction
show excitement or anticipation by rubbing hands

Examples

He rubbed his hands together eagerly as the meal was brought out.
The investors rubbed their hands together at the prospect of high profits.
She rubbed her hands together with excitement before announcing the winner.

Synset Examples

He rubbed his hands together in anticipation of the surprise.
She rubbed her hands together, excited for the upcoming event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to anticipate - to look forward to something with excitement
