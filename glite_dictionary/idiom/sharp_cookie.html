sharp cookie

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑrp ˈkʊki / (AmE), / ʃɑːp ˈkʊki / (BrE)
A clever or intelligent person, especially someone who is quick to understand or solve problems.
clever or intelligent person
informal american

Examples

You have to be a sharp cookie to solve these kinds of puzzles so quickly.
It didn't take a sharp cookie like her long to notice the error in the report.
Even as a child, he was recognized as a sharp cookie with a knack for business.

Synset Examples

That kid is a sharp cookie with a bright future.
She's a sharp cookie who always finds solutions.
You need to be a sharp cookie to ace this exam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smart cookie - an intelligent or clever person

Etymology

Modeled after the idiom "smart cookie"; "cookie" as person dates from early 20th-century American English.
