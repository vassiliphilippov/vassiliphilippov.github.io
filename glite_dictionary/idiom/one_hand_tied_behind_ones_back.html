one hand tied behind one's back

1. X

/ wʌn hænd taɪd bɪˈhaɪnd wʌnz bæk / (AmE), / wʌn hænd taɪd bɪˈhaɪnd wʌnz bæk / (BrE)
used to say that someone is able to do something easily, even with significant disadvantages or limitations
with significant disadvantages; easily despite limitations

Examples

She finished the exam in record time, as if she had one hand tied behind her back.
Even with one hand tied behind his back, he was able to solve the complex puzzle before anyone else.
The champion can win matches with one hand tied behind her back.

Synset Examples

She completed the task with one hand tied behind her back.
He could beat anyone with one hand tied behind his back.
He plays the piano beautifully, even with one hand tied behind his back.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: with ease - easy; without difficulty
  • Antonym: at a disadvantage - in an unfavourable position or with limitations
