as varied as

1. X

/ æz ˈvɛriːd æz / (AmE), / az ˈvɛːrɪd az / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something has a great deal of diversity or difference, often by comparison to another diverse entity
emphasizing great diversity in comparison

Examples

The city’s neighborhoods are as varied as its cultural festivals.
Her interests are as varied as painting, hiking, and quantum physics.
Opinions on the subject were as varied as the people discussing it.

Synset Examples

Hinduland's cuisine is as varied as its people.
