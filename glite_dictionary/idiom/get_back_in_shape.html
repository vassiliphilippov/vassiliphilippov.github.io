to get back in shape

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˌbæk ɪn ʃeɪp / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˌbæk ɪn ʃeɪp / (BrE)
to return to a good level of physical fitness, typically after a period of inactivity or unhealthiness
return to good physical condition

Examples

After months of not exercising, I decided it was time to get back in shape.
She joined a gym so she could get back in shape following her surgery.
Many people resolve to get back in shape at the start of the new year.

Synset Examples

I need to get back in shape for the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get in shape - to become physically fit
