shove it up your ass

1. X

/ ˈʃʌv ɪt ʌp jʊr æs / (AmE), / ˈʃʌv ɪt ʌp jɔːr ɑːs / (BrE)
(vulgar) Used to rudely reject a suggestion, demand, or person, indicating strong dismissal or refusal.
vulgar expression of rejection or dismissal
vulgar

If you don't like my decision, you can shove it up your ass.

She replied, 'You can shove it up your ass,' and walked out of the room.
