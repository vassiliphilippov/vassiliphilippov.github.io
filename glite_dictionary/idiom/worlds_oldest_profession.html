world's oldest profession

1. NOUN

/ wɝːldz ˈoʊldɪst prəˈfɛʃən / (AmE), / wɜːldz ˈəʊldɪst prəˈfɛʃən / (BrE)
a humorous or euphemistic term for prostitution
euphemism for prostitution

Examples

Some people refer to prostitution as the world's oldest profession.
He joked that politicians have much in common with those in the world's oldest profession.
The phrase world's oldest profession is commonly used to avoid directly mentioning prostitution.

Synset Examples

He claimed that journalism is the world's oldest profession.
Many people refer to teaching as the world's oldest profession.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prostitution - the practice of engaging in sexual activity for payment

Etymology

Refers humorously to the supposed antiquity of prostitution, first popularized in the late 19th century.
