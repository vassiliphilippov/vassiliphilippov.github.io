believe it or not

1. X

/ bəˈliv ɪt ər nɑt / (AmE), / bɪˈliːv ɪt ɔː nɒt / (BrE)
used to introduce something that is surprising or hard to believe
used to introduce something surprising

Examples

Believe it or not, I managed to fix the computer myself.
She ran a marathon last weekend, believe it or not.
Believe it or not, he has never traveled abroad.
