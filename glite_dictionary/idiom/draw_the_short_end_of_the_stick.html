to draw the short end of the stick

1. VERB

/ drɔ ðə ʃɔrt ɛnd əv ðə stɪk / (AmE), / drɔː ðə ʃɔːt end əv ðə stɪk / (BrE)
to be selected to do something unpleasant or to be at a disadvantage, especially by chance
be chosen for an undesirable outcome

Examples

She always seems to draw the short end of the stick when chores are assigned.
Unfortunately, our team drew the short end of the stick and had to work the holiday shift.
If you don't want to draw the short end of the stick, you'd better volunteer early.

Synset Examples

In this project, we drew the short end of the stick.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to draw the short straw - to be chosen to do something unpleasant by chance

Etymology

Refers to an old practice where sticks of unequal length are drawn to determine who gets an undesirable task.
