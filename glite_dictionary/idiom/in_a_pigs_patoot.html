in a pig's patoot

1. X

/ ɪn ə pɪɡz pəˈtuːt / (AmE), / ɪn ə pɪɡz pəˈtuːt / (BrE)
(informal) Used to refer to something that is nonsense, untrue, or trivial.
nonsense or trivial matter (idiom)
informal

Examples

His explanation turned out to be just in a pig's patoot, and nobody believed a word.
She dismissed the rumor as in a pig's patoot during the meeting.
All of his supposed evidence was clearly in a pig's patoot.

Synset Examples

Everything he says is just in a pig's patoot.
His excuses are as valid as in a pig's patoot.
Her claims are nonsense, just in a pig's patoot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - ideas, statements, or beliefs that are absurd or meaningless
