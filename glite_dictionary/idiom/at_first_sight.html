at first sight

1. ADV

/ æt ˈfɝst saɪt / (AmE), / ət ˈfɜːst saɪt / (BrE)
Immediately upon seeing someone or something for the first time, without deeper knowledge or consideration.
immediately upon first seeing

Examples

For Mia, it was friendship at first sight when she met her new neighbor.
The painting captivated him at first sight, and he knew he had to buy it.
Problems with the car weren’t apparent at first sight, but a closer inspection revealed several issues.

Synset Examples

it was love at first sight

2. ADV

/ æt fɝst saɪt / (AmE), / at fɜːst saɪt / (BrE)
when something appears to be a certain way upon first seeing or considering it, often before further knowledge or experience changes that impression
upon initial impression, before knowing more

Examples

At first sight, the painting seemed abstract, but closer inspection revealed a hidden landscape.
At first sight, the problem appeared simple, yet it turned out to be very complex.
At first sight, she struck me as unfriendly, but she was actually quite warm once we spoke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at first glance - on initial consideration; upon first impression
