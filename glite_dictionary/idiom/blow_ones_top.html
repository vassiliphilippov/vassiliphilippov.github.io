to blow one's top

1. VERB

/ bloʊ wʌnz tɑp / (AmE), / bləʊ wʌnz tɒp / (BrE)
to suddenly become very angry or lose one's temper
to become very angry suddenly

Examples

When Jack saw someone scratch his car, he blew his top and yelled at everyone nearby.
She blew her top after hearing that her project was canceled without warning.
If you don't stop teasing him, he's going to blow his top sooner or later.

Synset Examples

She blew her top over the team's poor performance.
He blew his top when he saw the mess.
After waiting for hours, I finally blew my top.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to lose one's temper - to become suddenly and violently angry
  • Antonym: to keep one's cool - to remain calm
