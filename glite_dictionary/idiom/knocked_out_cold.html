knocked out cold

1. ADJ

/ nɑkt aʊt koʊld / (AmE), / nɒkt aʊt kəʊld / (BrE)
completely unconscious, especially as a result of a blow or sudden impact
rendered completely unconscious
informal

Examples

He was knocked out cold after taking a punch to the jaw.
The falling branch hit her on the head and she was knocked out cold instantly.
The boxer was knocked out cold in the second round.

Synset Examples

He was knocked out cold, lights out for good.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unconscious - not awake and aware, especially due to injury or trauma
  • Antonym: to come to - to regain consciousness
