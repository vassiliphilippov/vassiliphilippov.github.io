full tilt boogie

1. ADV

/ fʊl tɪlt ˈbuːɡi / (AmE), / fʊl tɪlt ˈbuːɡi / (BrE)
at the highest level of energy, speed, or intensity; with full enthusiasm or vigor
at maximum energy or intensity
informal slang

Examples

The party was full tilt boogie from the moment we arrived until sunrise.
Once the deadline was announced, the team worked full tilt boogie to finish the project on time.
When the music started, the crowd went full tilt boogie on the dance floor.

Synset Examples

They danced full tilt boogie until sunrise.
The band played a full tilt boogie that got everyone dancing.
When the festival started, it was full tilt boogie all night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: full tilt - at full speed or with maximum effort
  • Synonym: flat out - as fast or as hard as possible
  • Synonym: all out - using all one's energy or resources

Etymology

Blend of 'full tilt' (at top speed, from jousting) and 'boogie' (slang for energetic dancing).
