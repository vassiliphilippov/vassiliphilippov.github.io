close at hand

1. ADJ

/ kloʊs æt hænd / (AmE), / kləʊs æt hand / (BrE)
near in space; easily accessible or within reach.
nearby; within reach

Examples

The museum is conveniently close at hand if you want to visit after lunch.
I always keep a notebook close at hand to jot down my ideas.
Help was close at hand when the hikers got lost.

Synset Examples

the town is close at hand

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nearby - close in distance; not far away
  • Antonym: distant - far away in space or time

2. ADJ

/ kloʊs ət hænd / (AmE), / kləʊs ət hand / (BrE)
(of an event or time) likely to happen very soon; imminent or approaching.
imminent or about to happen

Examples

The deadline for the project is close at hand, so we all need to work quickly.
She sensed that victory was close at hand after months of preparation.
With winter close at hand, many animals begin to gather food and seek shelter.

Synset Examples

retribution is at hand
some people believe the day of judgment is close at hand
in imminent danger
his impending retirement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imminent - about to happen very soon
  • Synonym: impending - likely to occur soon; forthcoming
  • Antonym: distant - far away in time; not soon to happen
