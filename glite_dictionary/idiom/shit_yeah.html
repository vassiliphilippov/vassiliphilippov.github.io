shit yeah

1. INTJ

/ ʃɪt jæ / (AmE), / ʃɪt jæ / (BrE)
an expression used to show strong excitement, approval, or enthusiasm
expression of strong excitement or approval
informal slang vulgar

Examples

When he told me we were getting free tickets, I yelled, 'shit yeah!'
She aced her finals and texted me, 'shit yeah! I did it!'
If they invite us to the concert, my answer is definitely 'shit yeah.'

Synset Examples

When they won the game, everyone shouted, 'Shit yeah!'
I got the promotion—shit yeah!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hell yeah - expression of strong agreement or approval

2. INTJ

/ ʃɪt jɛə / (AmE), / ʃɪt jɛə / (BrE)
used to express enthusiastic agreement or approval, often with a sense of excitement or exhilaration
enthusiastic expression of approval
informal vulgar

Examples

When he got the promotion, he shouted, 'Shit yeah!' to his coworkers.
A friend asked if she wanted to go to the concert, and she replied, 'Shit yeah!'
The team scored the winning goal and the fans screamed, 'Shit yeah!' in celebration.

Synset Examples

When the band started playing, the crowd cheered, 'Shit yeah!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hell yeah - enthusiastic agreement or approval, less vulgar
