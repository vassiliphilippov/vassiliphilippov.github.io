off (one's) guard

1. ADJ

/ ɑf wʌnz ɡɑrd / (AmE), / ɒf wʌnz ɡɑːd / (BrE)
not prepared for something; not paying enough attention to possible danger or problems
not prepared or vigilant

Examples

The sudden question caught her off her guard during the meeting.
She was completely off her guard when the camera flash went off unexpectedly.
If you let yourself become off her guard, you might miss important details.

Synset Examples

the blow caught him napping
caught in an off-guard moment
found him off his guard

Related Terms

  • Synonym: off guard - not prepared or vigilant
  • Antonym: on (one's) guard - prepared or vigilant
