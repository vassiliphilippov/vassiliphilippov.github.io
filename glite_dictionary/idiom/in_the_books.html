in the books

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ðə bʊks / (AmE), / ɪn ðə bʊks / (BrE)
officially recorded, completed, or finalized; having been formally entered into records or concluded
formally recorded or completed

Examples

After months of preparation, the merger is finally in the books.
The judge announced that the verdict was in the books by noon.
With the budget approved, another successful year is in the books.

Synset Examples

With the meeting concluded, all decisions are in the books.
Once the contract is signed, it's officially in the books.
The final grades are now in the books.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on the record - officially documented or recorded
