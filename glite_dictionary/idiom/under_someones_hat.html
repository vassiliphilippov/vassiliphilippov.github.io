under someone's hat

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌndɚ ˈsʌmwʌnz hæt / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ˈsʌmwənz hæt / (BrE)
secret or confidential; kept undisclosed by someone
kept secret; confidential

Examples

She asked me to keep the news under my hat until she was ready to tell everyone.
You should keep these figures under your hat until we finalize the deal.
He has a new job offer but is keeping it under his hat for now.

Synset Examples

She decided to keep her promotion under her hat until the announcement.
He has a few plans under his hat for the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keep something under wraps - to keep something secret or hidden
  • Antonym: to spill the beans - to reveal secret information
