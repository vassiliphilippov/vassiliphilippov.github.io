hurrah's nest

1. NOUN

/ həˈrɑz nɛst / (AmE), / hʊˈrɑːz nɛst / (BrE)
a lively and chaotic celebration, often involving excitement and disorder
lively, chaotic celebration

Examples

After the championship game, the locker room became a hurrah's nest of singing and laughter.
As midnight struck, the living room turned into a hurrah's nest with kids running everywhere.
When the announcement was made, the office turned into a hurrah's nest of cheering and excitement.

Synset Examples

When the team won, it became a hurrah's nest of joy.
Her surprise birthday party was a real hurrah's nest.
The party turned into a hurrah's nest with everyone celebrating.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uproar - a state of noisy confusion or excitement
  • Synonym: pandemonium - wild and noisy disorder or confusion
