to talk that shit

1. VERB

/ tɑk ðæt ʃɪt / (AmE), / tɔːk ðæt ʃɪt / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) To speak nonsense, empty boasting, or trivial matters, often in a provocative or showy way.
speak nonsense or boast provocatively (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar

Examples

He loves to talk that shit when his friends are around, but he's quiet when challenged.
They were talking that shit all night, but nobody took them seriously.
If you talk that shit here, be ready to back it up.

Synset Examples

Stop talking that shit and get to work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talk nonsense - to say things that are not true or make no sense
  • Synonym: boast - to talk with excessive pride
