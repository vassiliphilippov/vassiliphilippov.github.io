in her right mind

1. ADJ

/ ɪn hɚ raɪt maɪnd / (AmE), / ɪn hə raɪt maɪnd / (BrE)
thinking and behaving in a sensible and rational way; not crazy or irresponsible.
sane or rational state of mind

Examples

No one in her right mind would agree to such a dangerous plan.
She wouldn't make that decision in her right mind.
If Lisa were in her right mind, she would never quit her job without another offer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in one's right mind - thinking and behaving rationally; not insane
  • Antonym: out of one's mind - not sane; crazy or irrational
