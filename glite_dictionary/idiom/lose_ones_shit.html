to lose one's shit

1. VERB

/ luːz wʌnz ʃɪt / (AmE), / luːz wʌnz ʃɪt / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) To suddenly become very angry or extremely upset, especially in a way that is hard to control.
become very angry or upset (informal, vulgar)
informal vulgar

Examples

When the team lost the game, the coach completely lost his shit in the locker room.
She tried to keep calm, but eventually lost her shit over the constant interruptions.
If you keep pushing him, he's going to lose his shit sooner or later.

Synset Examples

She lost her shit when she saw the mess in the kitchen.
He tends to lose his shit when things don't go his way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to lose one's temper - to become suddenly angry
  • Antonym: to keep one's cool - to remain calm and not become angry

2. VERB

/ luːz wʌnz ʃɪt / (AmE), / luːz wʌnz ʃɪt / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar, slang) To become extremely angry, upset, or emotionally overwhelmed, often in a sudden and dramatic way.
become extremely angry or upset (vulgar slang)
informal slang vulgar

Examples

He lost his shit when he found out someone had scratched his new car.
She was so frustrated by the constant interruptions that she nearly lost her shit during the meeting.
Don't lose your shit over a minor mistake—it's not the end of the world.

Synset Examples

When the meeting was canceled, he almost lost his shit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to lose one's temper - to become angry or irritable suddenly
  • Antonym: to keep one's cool - to remain calm in a difficult situation
