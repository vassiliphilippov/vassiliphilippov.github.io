everything will be alright

1. X

/ ˈɛvriˌθɪŋ wɪl bi ɔlˈraɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛvrɪθɪŋ wɪl biː ɔːlˈraɪt / (BrE)
used to reassure someone that a situation will improve or end well
reassurance things will turn out fine

Examples

No matter how tough things are now, everything will be alright in the end.
She hugged her son and whispered, 'everything will be alright, I promise.'
Sometimes you just need to hear that everything will be alright to feel a bit better.

Synset Examples

Don't worry, dearie, everything will be alright.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: everything will be OK - used to tell someone not to worry; things will be fine
  • Synonym: don't worry - used to comfort someone and suggest there's no need to be anxious
