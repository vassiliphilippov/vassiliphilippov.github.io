behind the scenes

1. ADV

/ bɪˈhaɪnd ðə siːnz / (AmE), / bɪˈhaɪnd ðə siːnz / (BrE)
in a secret or private way, especially in connection with work or activities not meant to be seen by the public
privately; not visible to the public

Examples

Many important negotiations happen behind the scenes, away from the cameras.
The celebrity's calm image hides the chaos going on behind the scenes.
She prefers to work behind the scenes rather than take public credit.

Synset Examples

She’s the unsung hero of the charity, always working behind the scenes.
They worked behind the scenes to make the event successful.
The film's magic happens behind the scenes.
Much of the work is done behind the scenes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in secret - in a way not known or seen by others
