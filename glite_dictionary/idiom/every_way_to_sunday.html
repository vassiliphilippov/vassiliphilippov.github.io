every way to Sunday

1. X

/ ˈɛv.ɹi weɪ tə ˈsʌn.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛv.ɹi weɪ tə ˈsʌn.deɪ / (BrE)
Using every possible method or in every possible way.
in every possible way

Examples

We've checked this contract every way to Sunday and still can't find any loopholes.
She sorted those files every way to Sunday to make sure nothing was missing.
The detective investigated the crime every way to Sunday, but couldn't find any solid evidence.

Synset Examples

They approached the challenge every way to Sunday.
She tried every way to Sunday to fix the issue.
He examined the problem every way to Sunday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: every which way - in all possible directions or manners
