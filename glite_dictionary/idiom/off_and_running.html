off and running

1. ADJ

/ ɔf ənd ˈrʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ɒf ənd ˈrʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
having started successfully and progressing well
started successfully and progressing

Examples

The new marketing campaign is off and running, showing positive results in its first week.
Now that the children are back at school, the semester is off and running.
As soon as the bell sounded, the competitors were off and running on the track.

Synset Examples

Once we secured funding, we were off and running.
The project is off and running after the kickoff.
With the new software, the team is off and running.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underway - having started and in progress
  • Antonym: stalled - stopped or unable to proceed

Etymology

From horse racing, referencing the start of a race.
