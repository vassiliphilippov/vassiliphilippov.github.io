way with words

1. NOUN

/ weɪ wɪð wɜrdz / (AmE), / weɪ wɪð wɜːdz / (BrE)
the ability to express oneself in a very effective, pleasant, or persuasive way
skill in expressing oneself eloquently

Examples

She certainly has a way with words and always finds the perfect thing to say in any situation.
His way with words helped him become a successful speechwriter.
People admire her way with words during important presentations.

Synset Examples

He has a way with words that captivates everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eloquence - fluent, persuasive speaking or writing
  • Synonym: gift of the gab - the ability to speak with eloquence and fluency
