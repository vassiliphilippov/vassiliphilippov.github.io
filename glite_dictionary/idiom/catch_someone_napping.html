to catch (someone) napping

1. VERB

/ kætʃ mi ˈnæpɪŋ / (AmE), / kætʃ mi ˈnæpɪŋ / (BrE)
to find someone off guard, unprepared, or inattentive
find someone off guard or unprepared

Examples

The thieves managed to catch the security guard napping and slipped into the building unnoticed.
I hadn't studied the new material, so the test really caught me napping.
If you ever catch me napping at work, please give me a nudge!

Synset Examples

If you catch me napping, be sure to wake me up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catch (someone) off guard - to surprise someone when they are not prepared
  • Synonym: surprise - to encounter someone when not expected or prepared
