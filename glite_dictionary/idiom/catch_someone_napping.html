to catch someone napping

1. VERB

/ kætʃ ˈsʌmwʌn ˈnæpɪŋ / (AmE), / katʃ ˈsʌmwʌn ˈnapɪŋ / (BrE)
to discover someone being careless or not paying attention, especially when they are expected to be alert
discover someone being inattentive

Examples

The security guard was caught napping during his night shift when the alarm went off.
Our competitors managed to catch us napping and launched their product first.
Be prepared for questions so the panel doesn’t catch you napping during the interview.

Synset Examples

Don't let the boss catch you napping on the job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catch someone off guard - to surprise someone who is unprepared
