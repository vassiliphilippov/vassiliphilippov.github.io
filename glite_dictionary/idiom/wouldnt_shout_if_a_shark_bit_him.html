wouldn't shout if a shark bit him

1. X

/ ˈwʊdənt ʃaʊt ɪf ə ʃɑrk bɪt hɪm / (AmE), / ˈwʊd(ə)nt ʃaʊt ɪf ə ʃɑːk bɪt hɪm / (BrE)
(Of a person) extremely calm or unexcitable, even in the face of great provocation or crisis.
extremely calm, unexcitable person
british

Examples

No matter what happens at work, he wouldn't shout if a shark bit him.
Even during the emergency, she wouldn't shout if a shark bit him, staying perfectly composed.
People say our manager wouldn't shout if a shark bit him, he's so calm under pressure.

Synset Examples

In a crisis, he wouldn't shout if a shark bit him.

Related Terms
