on the verge of

1. ADP

/ ɑn ðə vɜrdʒ əv / (AmE), / ɒn ðə vɜːdʒ əv / (BrE)
very close to experiencing something or to doing something; almost at the point of a particular event or feeling
almost about to experience or do something

Examples

After months of hard work, the team was on the verge of discovering a cure.
She looked as if she was on the verge of fainting from excitement.
The city is on the verge of major reforms that could change everything.

Synset Examples

The toddler is a wobbler, always on the verge of falling.
He stood on the verge of making a big decision.
She was on the verge of tears after the news.
The company is on the verge of a breakthrough.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: about to - very close to doing something; on the point of
  • Synonym: on the brink of - very close to a transition or significant change
