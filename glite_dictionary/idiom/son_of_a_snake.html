son of a snake

1. NOUN

/ sʌn əv ə sneɪk / (AmE), / sʌn əv ə sneɪk / (BrE)
an insult for a deceitful or untrustworthy person
insult for a deceitful person
informal

Examples

I can't believe that son of a snake tried to sabotage our project.
She warned me that he was a son of a snake and not to trust him.
After stealing the money, everyone called him a son of a snake behind his back.

Synset Examples

He's a real son of a snake for betraying us.
That son of a snake lied right to my face.
I can't believe he turned out to be a son of a snake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snake - a treacherous or deceitful person (figurative use)
  • Synonym: son of a gun - an exclamation or euphemistic insult
