up to one's ears

1. ADJ

/ ʌp tə wʌnz ɪrz / (AmE), / ʌp tə wʌnz ɪəz / (BrE)
extremely busy or deeply involved, especially to the point of being overwhelmed
extremely busy or deeply involved

Examples

I'm up to my ears in paperwork this week.
She's up to her ears with holiday preparations and barely has time to rest.
We're up to our ears in debt after the house renovation.

Synset Examples

I'm up to my ears in work right now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swamped - overwhelmed with work or duties
  • Synonym: snowed under - overloaded with work or responsibilities
  • Antonym: free - not busy or occupied
