throwing an ant into a sty

1. X

/ ˈθroʊɪŋ ən ænt ˈɪntu ə staɪ / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊɪŋ ən ænt ˈɪntuː ə stʌɪ / (BrE)
an action that has little or no effect on a larger situation; a futile or pointless gesture
futile or pointless action

Examples

Trying to change the company's culture alone felt like throwing an ant into a sty.
Donating one dollar to such a massive campaign is just throwing an ant into a sty.
Fixing that one error in the broken codebase was like throwing an ant into a sty.

Synset Examples

His complaints about the project felt like throwing an ant into a sty.
Investing in that company is like throwing an ant into a sty.
Trying to fix that system is like throwing an ant into a sty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drop in the ocean - a very small amount compared to what is needed or expected
  • Synonym: futile - incapable of producing any useful result; pointless
