in the snap of someone's fingers

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðə snæp əv ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈfɪŋɡɚz / (AmE), / ɪn ðə snæp əv ˈsʌmwənz ˈfɪŋɡəz / (BrE)
very quickly or with little effort; in an instant
very quickly or easily

Examples

The answer came to her in the snap of her fingers.
He fixed the computer in the snap of his fingers, amazing everyone in the office.
You can call a cab in the snap of your fingers using this new app.

Synset Examples
