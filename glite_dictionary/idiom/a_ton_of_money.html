a ton of money

1. NOUN

/ ə tʌn əv ˈmʌni / (AmE), / ə tʌn əv ˈmʌni / (BrE)
a very large amount of money
a large sum of money

Examples

He must have spent a ton of money renovating his house.
Launching a new business usually requires a ton of money.
She inherited a ton of money from her grandparents.

Synset Examples

She spent an asston of money on that new car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: a fortune - a very large amount of money
