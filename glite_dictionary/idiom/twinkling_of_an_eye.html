twinkling of an eye

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɪŋklɪŋ əv ən aɪ / (AmE), / ˈtwɪŋklɪŋ əv ən aɪ / (BrE)
a very brief moment; something happening extremely quickly or suddenly
a very short moment; instantly

Examples

The news spread throughout the town in the twinkling of an eye.
Everything changed in the twinkling of an eye when the alarm sounded.
She solved the puzzle in the twinkling of an eye, amazing everyone around her.

Synset Examples

They disappeared in the twinkling of an eye.
The changes happened in the twinkling of an eye.
The kids grew up in the twinkling of an eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in a flash - very quickly; instantly
  • Synonym: in no time - almost immediately; very quickly

Etymology

Biblical origin, found in 1 Corinthians 15:52: "in a moment, in the twinkling of an eye".
