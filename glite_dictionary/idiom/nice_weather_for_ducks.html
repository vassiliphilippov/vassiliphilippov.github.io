nice weather for ducks

1. X

/ naɪs ˈwɛðɚ fər dʌks / (AmE), / naɪs ˈwɛðə fə dʌks / (BrE)
used humorously to refer to very wet and rainy weather, suggesting that only ducks would enjoy such conditions
very rainy or wet weather (humorous)

Examples

It's nice weather for ducks today, so remember to take your raincoat.
We looked outside at the pouring rain and joked, "Well, it's nice weather for ducks."
After days of sunshine, yesterday was suddenly nice weather for ducks again.

Synset Examples

Don’t forget your umbrella; it’s nice weather for ducks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pouring - raining very heavily
  • Antonym: dry weather - weather without rain or precipitation
