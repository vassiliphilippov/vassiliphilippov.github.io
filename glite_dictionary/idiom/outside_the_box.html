outside the box

1. ADV

/ ˌaʊtˌsaɪd ðə bɑks / (AmE), / ˌaʊtsaɪd ðə bɒks / (BrE)
in a way that is creative and not limited by conventional ideas or rules
in a creative or unconventional way

Examples

The marketing team approached the campaign outside the box, resulting in unexpected success.
Great inventions often come from people who think outside the box.
We're looking for candidates who can solve problems outside the box.
