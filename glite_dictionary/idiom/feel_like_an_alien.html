to feel like an alien

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ən ˈeɪliən / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ən ˈeɪliən / (BrE)
to feel as though you do not belong or fit in with those around you; to feel out of place
feel out of place or not belonging

Examples

After moving to a new country, she often felt like an alien among her classmates.
Wearing a suit to the casual event made him feel like an alien.
Sometimes I feel like an alien at family gatherings because our interests are so different.

Synset Examples

She felt like an alien in a room full of strangers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feel out of place - to feel uncomfortable or as though you do not belong
