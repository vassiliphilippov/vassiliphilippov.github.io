on a dime

1. ADV

/ ɑn ə daɪm / (AmE), / ɒn ə daɪm / (BrE)
Very quickly or with great precision, especially in changing direction, position, or plans.
with great speed or precision

Examples

The new drone can turn on a dime to avoid obstacles.
Our manager expects us to adapt on a dime when clients change their minds.
Thanks to the advanced brakes, the bike can stop on a dime in any weather.

Synset Examples

She pivoted her career on a dime.
He can change his plans on a dime.
The car stopped on a dime when the light turned red.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in a flash - very quickly, instantly
  • Synonym: on the spot - immediately, without delay

Etymology

Refers to the small size of a dime (a ten-cent coin), highlighting agility or precision.
