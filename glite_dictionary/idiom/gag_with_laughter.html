to gag with laughter

1. VERB

/ ɡæɡ wɪð ˈlæftɚ / (AmE), / ɡæɡ wɪð ˈlɑːftə / (BrE)
to laugh so uncontrollably that one feels physically overwhelmed or choked
laugh uncontrollably

Examples

The children gagged with laughter during the clown's silly performance.
I nearly gagged with laughter at his outrageous story.
She was gagging with laughter all through the comedy show.

Synset Examples

The comedian's joke made the audience gag with laughter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laugh uncontrollably - to laugh without control or restraint
