heavy lift

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛvi lɪft / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi lɪft / (BrE)
a task or responsibility that is particularly challenging, demanding, or difficult to accomplish
challenging or demanding task

Examples

Taking over as CEO of the struggling company was a real heavy lift.
Organizing the conference will be a heavy lift for such a small team.
Negotiating that peace agreement proved to be a heavy lift for all involved.

Synset Examples

That project will be a heavy lift for the team.
Moving the fridge was a heavy lift for everyone.
The charity event was a heavy lift but worth it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tough nut to crack - a problem or situation that is difficult to solve or handle
