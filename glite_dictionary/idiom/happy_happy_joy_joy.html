happy, happy, joy, joy

1. X

/ ˈhæpi ˈhæpi ˈdʒɔɪ ˈdʒɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈhæpi ˈhæpi ˈdʒɔɪ ˈdʒɔɪ / (BrE)
used to express exuberant happiness or excitement, often in a playful or ironic way
expression of exuberant happiness
informal

She danced around the room, shouting happy, happy, joy, joy after hearing she got the part.
