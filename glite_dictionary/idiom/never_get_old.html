to never get old

1. VERB

/ ˈnɛvɚ ɡɛt oʊld / (AmE), / ˈnɛvə ɡɛt əʊld / (BrE)
(of something) to remain enjoyable, interesting, or amusing no matter how often it happens or is experienced.
remain enjoyable despite repetition

Examples

That joke never gets old—it makes me laugh every time.
Watching the sunset over the mountains never gets old for me.
The taste of fresh strawberries never gets old in the summer.

Synset Examples

That story never gets old, no matter how many times I hear it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: never lose its charm - to stay appealing or delightful over time
