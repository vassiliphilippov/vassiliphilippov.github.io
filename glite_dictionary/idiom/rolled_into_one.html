rolled into one

1. ADJ

/ ˈroʊld ɪnˌtu ˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˈrəʊld ɪnˌtjuː ˈwʌn / (BrE)
combined or consisting of several different things or qualities in one person or thing.
combined in a single entity

Examples

She is a teacher, counselor, and friend rolled into one.
The device is a camera, phone, and computer rolled into one.
He managed to be charming, witty, and intelligent all rolled into one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: combined - made by joining or mixing two or more things together
  • Antonym: separate - forming or viewed as a unit apart; not joined or combined
