broth of a man

1. NOUN

/ brɑθ əv ə mæn / (AmE), / brɒθ əv ə mæn / (BrE)
An outstanding or admirable man, often used with affectionate emphasis.
an outstanding or admirable man
archaic

Examples

Everyone agreed that Seamus was a real broth of a man, always ready to lend a hand.
She admired her father—a true broth of a man, with a heart of gold.
He's the very broth of a man, strong, kind, and full of spirit.

Etymology

Alludes to the idea of a man as the 'essence' or 'best part', like a savory broth made by boiling down ingredients.
