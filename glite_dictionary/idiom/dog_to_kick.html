dog to kick

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ tə kɪk / (AmE), / dɒɡ tə kɪk / (BrE)
a person or thing that is unfairly singled out for blame or to vent anger upon; a scapegoat
person or thing blamed or vented upon

Examples

Whenever something goes wrong, he becomes the dog to kick in the office.
The new manager refused to serve as the team's dog to kick.
She warned against using interns as a convenient dog to kick when problems arise.

Synset Examples

Stop looking for a dog to kick; it's not their fault.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scapegoat - a person who is blamed for the wrongdoings of others
