half a mo

1. NOUN

/ ˌhæf ə ˈmoʊ / (AmE), / ˌhɑːf ə ˈməʊ / (BrE)
a very short amount of time; a brief moment
a very brief moment
informal british

Examples

I'll be ready in half a mo if you wait by the door.
Could you hold on for half a mo while I grab my keys?
Just give me half a mo to finish this email.
