to turn to shit

1. VERB

/ tɝːn tə ʃɪt / (AmE), / tɜːn tə ʃɪt / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) To deteriorate badly or fail completely; to become ruined or useless.
to deteriorate badly or fail completely
informal vulgar

Examples

After the new manager arrived, the project quickly turned to shit.
Whenever he tries to organize something, it just turns to shit.
The vacation turned to shit when our luggage was lost and the hotel was overbooked.

Synset Examples

When she took over, everything one touches turned to shit.
Lately, it seems everything he touches turns to shit.
After his mistakes, everything one touches turns to shit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fall apart - to break down or collapse
  • Synonym: to go wrong - to develop problems or fail
  • Antonym: to improve - to become better
