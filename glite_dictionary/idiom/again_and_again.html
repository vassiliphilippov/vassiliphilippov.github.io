again and again

1. ADV

/ əˈɡɛn ənd əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / əˈɡɛn ənd əˈɡɛn / (BrE)
many times; on repeated occasions; repeatedly.
repeatedly, many times

Examples

He asked the same question again and again until he got an answer.
Again and again, she checked her phone for new messages.
The coach emphasized the importance of practice again and again during the training session.

Synset Examples

the unknown word turned up over and over again in the text

Related Terms

  • Synonym: over and over - repeatedly, time after time
  • Degree Lower: once - one single time
