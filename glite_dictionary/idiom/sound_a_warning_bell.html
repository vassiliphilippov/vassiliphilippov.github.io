to sound a warning bell

1. VERB

/ saʊnd ə ˈwɔrnɪŋ bɛl / (AmE), / saʊnd ə ˈwɔːnɪŋ bɛl / (BrE)
to alert people to a potential danger or serious problem
alert to a potential danger

Examples

The increase in cyber attacks should sound a warning bell for all Internet users.
Experts hope the data will sound a warning bell about climate change.
The sudden drop in sales figures sounded a warning bell for the company.

Synset Examples

The environmental changes should sound a warning bell for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raise the alarm - warn people of danger or trouble
  • Synonym: sound the alarm - alert or warn of danger, especially suddenly
