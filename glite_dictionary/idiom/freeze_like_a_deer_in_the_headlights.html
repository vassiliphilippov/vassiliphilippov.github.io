to freeze like a deer in the headlights

1. VERB

/ friz laɪk ə dɪr ɪn ðə ˈhɛdlaɪts / (AmE), / friːz laɪk ə dɪə ɪn ðə ˈhɛdlaɪts / (BrE)
to suddenly become unable to move or act because of fear or surprise
become paralyzed by fear or shock

Examples

When the interviewer asked an unexpected question, she froze like a deer in the headlights.
He tends to freeze like a deer in the headlights whenever he has to speak in public.
Caught off guard by the sudden loud noise, I froze like a deer in the headlights for a moment.

Synset Examples

She froze like a deer in the headlights during the surprise quiz.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freeze up - to become unable to act or speak due to fear or embarrassment
  • Synonym: panic - to suddenly feel uncontrollable fear or anxiety

Etymology

From the behavior of deer that freeze motionless when caught in car headlights, common in North America.
