until the last dog is hung

1. X

/ ənˈtɪl ðə læst dɔɡ ɪz hʌŋ / (AmE), / ənˈtɪl ðə lɑːst dɒɡ ɪz hʌŋ / (BrE)
(informal, idiomatic) To continue supporting or persisting with something until the very end, no matter how long it takes.
persist or support until the very end
informal

Examples

The fans promised to support the club until the last dog is hung, no matter how many games they lost.
If you believe in the cause, stick with it until the last dog is hung.
She fought for her client's innocence until the last dog is hung.

Synset Examples

We will support our team until the last dog is hung.
