out of place

1. ADV

/ aʊt əv pleɪs / (AmE), / aʊt əv pleɪs / (BrE)
in a setting where one is or feels inappropriate, incongruous, or uncomfortable
feeling inappropriate or uncomfortable in a situation

Examples

He always felt out of place at fancy parties with unfamiliar people.
Her old furniture looked out of place in the modern apartment.
Wearing jeans to the formal dinner, he was clearly out of place.

Synset Examples

he felt out of place in the lingerie shop

Related Terms

  • Antonym: at home - feeling comfortable or appropriate in a situation
  • Synonym: incongruous - not in harmony or keeping with the surroundings
