through the wazoo

1. ADV

/ θru ðə wɑːˈzu / (AmE), / θruː ðə wæˈzuː / (BrE)
in extremely large quantities; to an excessive or overwhelming extent
in excessive or overwhelming amounts
informal

Examples

They've got complaints through the wazoo after that botched event.
Our team has resources through the wazoo this quarter, so there's no excuse for delays.
She received gifts through the wazoo on her birthday.

Synset Examples

He had ideas through the wazoo for the new project.
The party decorations were through the wazoo with colors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out the wazoo - to an excessive or extreme extent
