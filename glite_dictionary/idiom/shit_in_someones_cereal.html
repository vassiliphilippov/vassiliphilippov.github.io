to shit in someone's cereal

1. VERB

/ ʃɪt ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈsɪəriəl / (AmE), / ʃɪt ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈsɪəriəl / (BrE)
(vulgar, informal) To spoil someone's good mood or ruin their enjoyment of a situation.
spoil someone's mood or enjoyment (vulgar, informal)
informal vulgar

Examples

I didn't mean to shit in your cereal, but I had to tell you about the layoffs.
Nothing like a surprise bill to shit in someone's cereal on a Friday afternoon.
He really shitted in her cereal by criticizing her project at the meeting.
