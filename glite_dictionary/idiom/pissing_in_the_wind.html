pissing in the wind

1. X

/ ˈpɪsɪŋ ɪn ðə wɪnd / (AmE), / ˈpɪsɪŋ ɪn ðə wɪnd / (BrE)
wasting time or effort on a task that is pointless or doomed to fail
futile or pointless action
informal vulgar

Examples

He realized he was just pissing in the wind trying to change the company's policy.
If you ask him to apologize, you're pissing in the wind.
They kept protesting, but it was like pissing in the wind against the new law.

Synset Examples

Arguing with her is just pissing in the wind.
I feel like I'm pissing in the wind with this project.
Trying to convince him is like pissing in the wind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beating a dead horse - to waste effort on something that has no chance of succeeding
  • Synonym: fighting a losing battle - to be engaged in a struggle with little hope of success
