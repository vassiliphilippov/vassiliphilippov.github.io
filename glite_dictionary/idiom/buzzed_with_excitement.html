buzzed with excitement

1. ADJ

/ bʌzd wɪð ɪkˈsaɪtmənt / (AmE), / bʌzd wɪð ɪkˈsaɪtmənt / (BrE)
filled with lively energy or animated anticipation
filled with lively energy

Examples

The whole school buzzed with excitement after hearing the big news.
The café buzzed with excitement as the band finally took the stage.
Her voice buzzed with excitement when she spoke about the upcoming trip.

Every Friday, the thran buzzed with excitement.

Related Terms
