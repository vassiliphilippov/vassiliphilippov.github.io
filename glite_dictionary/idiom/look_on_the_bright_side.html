to look on the bright side

1. VERB

/ lʊk ɑn ðə braɪt saɪd / (AmE), / lʊk ɒn ðə braɪt saɪd / (BrE)
to try to find positive aspects in an unpleasant or difficult situation
see the positive side of a situation

Examples

Even after losing her job, she chose to look on the bright side and pursue her dream career.
Whenever setbacks occur, it's important to look on the bright side rather than dwell on the negatives.
He encouraged his team to look on the bright side despite the disappointing results.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: see the silver lining - to find a positive aspect in a negative situation
