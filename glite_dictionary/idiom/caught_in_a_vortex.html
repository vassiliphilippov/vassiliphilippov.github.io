caught in a vortex

1. ADJ

/ kɔt ɪn ə ˈvɔrtɛks / (AmE), / kɔːt ɪn ə ˈvɔːtɛks / (BrE)
In a situation where one is overwhelmed or swept up by chaos, confusion, or difficult events beyond one's control.
overwhelmed by chaotic circumstances

Examples

After the scandal broke, the politician was caught in a vortex of media attention and speculation.
Maria felt caught in a vortex of responsibilities at work and home, unable to find respite.
As the project spiraled out of control, he realized he was caught in a vortex of unexpected problems.

Synset Examples

She felt like she was caught in a vortex of deadlines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overwhelmed - feeling buried or drowned by circumstances
  • Synonym: in over one's head - involved in something too difficult to handle
