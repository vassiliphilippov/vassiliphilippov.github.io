to vanish off the face of the earth

1. VERB

/ ˈvæ.nɪʃ ɑf ðə feɪs əv ði ɝθ / (AmE), / ˈvæ.nɪʃ ɒf ðə feɪs əv ðiː ɜːθ / (BrE)
to disappear completely so that no trace remains and no one knows what happened
disappear completely without trace

Examples

After losing the championship, the whole team seemed to vanish off the face of the earth for months.
My keys have vanished off the face of the earth—I can't find them anywhere.
She moved to another country and vanished off the face of the earth as far as her old friends were concerned.

Synset Examples

Some old friends seem to vanish off the face of the earth.
After the argument, he seemed to vanish to the four winds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disappear without a trace - to disappear so completely that no sign remains
