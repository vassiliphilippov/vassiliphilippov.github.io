no way to treat a lady

1. X

/ noʊ weɪ tə trit ə ˈleɪdi / (AmE), / nəʊ weɪ tə triːt ə ˈleɪdi / (BrE)
used to say that someone's behavior toward a woman is inappropriate or disrespectful
inappropriate way to treat a woman

Examples

He realized that ignoring her at the party was no way to treat a lady.
Yelling at someone in public is no way to treat a lady.
She told him that forgetting her birthday was no way to treat a lady.

Synset Examples

He apologized, realizing that’s no way to treat a lady.
