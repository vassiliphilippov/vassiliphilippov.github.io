in good taste

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ɡʊd teɪst / (AmE), / ɪn ɡʊd teɪst / (BrE)
conforming to generally accepted social or aesthetic standards; showing propriety or refinement.
conforming to social or aesthetic standards

Examples

Her wedding decorations were chosen carefully and were definitely in good taste.
The comedian's jokes were funny and in good taste, making everyone comfortable.
It is important that the artwork displayed in the lobby is in good taste.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in bad taste - not conforming to accepted social or aesthetic standards; offensive or inappropriate
  • Synonym: tasteful - showing good aesthetic judgment or appropriateness
