as trivial as a gnat's pee

1. ADJ

/ əz ˈtrɪviəl əz ə næts pi / (AmE), / əz ˈtrɪviəl əz ə næts piː / (BrE)
Used to emphasize that something is extremely insignificant or of no importance at all.
extremely insignificant; unimportant
informal

Examples

The difference between the two proposals is as trivial as a gnat's pee and hardly worth debating.
He became upset over an issue that was as trivial as a gnat's pee.
Her worries seemed as trivial as a gnat's pee compared to the real problems they faced.

Synset Examples

Their argument was over something as trivial as a gnat's pee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trivial - of little value or importance
