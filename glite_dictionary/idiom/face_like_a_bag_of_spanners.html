face like a bag of spanners

1. NOUN

/ feɪs laɪk ə bæg əv ˈspænɚz / (AmE), / feɪs laɪk ə bæg əv ˈspænəz / (BrE)
(British, informal) a face that is very unattractive or unpleasant to look at.
very unattractive face (British idiom)
informal british

Examples

After football practice, Tom had a face like a bag of spanners from all the knocks he took.
Everyone said the mugshot made him look like he had a face like a bag of spanners.
She laughs about having a face like a bag of spanners in her childhood photos.

Synset Examples

After the accident, he had a face like a bag of spanners.
