push comes to shove

1. X

/ pʊʃ kʌmz tə ʃʌv / (AmE), / pʊʃ kʌmz tə ʃʌv / (BrE)
used to describe a situation when things become critical or when decisive action is needed
when decisive action is needed

Examples

I’m not usually outspoken, but when push comes to shove, I will defend my beliefs.
We may disagree now, but if push comes to shove, we’ll work together to solve the problem.
If push comes to shove, we can always call for extra help.

Synset Examples

I'll help you out when push comes to shove.
We’ll figure it out when push comes to shove.
She’ll stand up for herself when push comes to shove.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in a pinch - when in a critical or emergency situation
