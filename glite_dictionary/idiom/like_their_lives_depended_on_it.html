like their lives depended on it

1. ADV

/ laɪk ðɛr laɪvz dɪˈpɛndɪd ɑn ɪt / (AmE), / laɪk ðɛə laɪvz dɪˈpɛndɪd ɒn ɪt / (BrE)
with intense urgency, seriousness, or desperation, as if much depended on the outcome
with intense urgency or desperation

Examples

She cleaned the house like their lives depended on it, not stopping for a moment.
The team played defense like their lives depended on it in the final minutes of the game.
They worked through the night like their lives depended on it to meet the deadline.

Synset Examples

They argued like their lives depended on it during the debate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as if their life depended on it - acting with the same desperation or seriousness
