not your costume

1. X

/ nɑt jʊr ˈkɑstuːm / (AmE), / nɒt jɔː ˈkɒstjuːm / (BrE)
used to assert that cultural symbols or dress should not be appropriated or imitated by those outside the culture, as it is disrespectful or offensive
assertion against cultural appropriation

Examples

Choosing to wear traditional Native regalia as a fashion statement ignores that it is not your costume.
At the party, she reminded him that a kimono is not your costume but a part of someone's cultural heritage.
Many activists have stressed to festival-goers that indigenous attire is not your costume.

Synset Examples

Wearing that outfit is offensive; my culture is not your costume.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cultural appropriation - the adoption or use of elements of one culture by members of another culture
