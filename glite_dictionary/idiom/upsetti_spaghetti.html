upsetti spaghetti

1. NOUN

/ ʌpˈsɛti spəˈɡɛti / (AmE), / ʌpˈsɛti spəˈɡɛti / (BrE)
A humorous or playful way to refer to someone who is upset or unhappy.
playful phrase for being upset
informal slang

Examples

I was a total upsetti spaghetti after missing the concert.
No need to be an upsetti spaghetti—tomorrow will be a better day.
Whenever things don't go his way, he turns into an upsetti spaghetti.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

Etymology

Formed by rhyming 'upset' with 'spaghetti,' imitating a childlike or playful expression.
