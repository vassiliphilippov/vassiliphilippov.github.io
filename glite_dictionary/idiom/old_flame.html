old flame

1. NOUN

/ oʊld fleɪm / (AmE), / əʊld fleɪm / (BrE)
a person with whom one had a romantic relationship in the past
former romantic partner

Examples

She bumped into her old flame while shopping downtown.
Sometimes people feel nostalgic when they remember an old flame.
His old flame sent him a message out of the blue.

Synset Examples

He still thinks about his old flame from college.
She ran into an old flame at the reunion.
Meeting her old flame brought back many memories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ex - a former romantic partner
