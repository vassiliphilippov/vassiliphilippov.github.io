to work one's fingers to the bone

1. VERB

/ wɝk wʌnz ˈfɪŋɡɚz tə ðə boʊn / (AmE), / wɜːk wʌnz ˈfɪŋɡəz tə ðə bəʊn / (BrE)
to work extremely hard, especially in physical or demanding tasks, often to the point of exhaustion
work extremely hard

Examples

After buying the farm, they worked their fingers to the bone just to make ends meet.
You don't have to work your fingers to the bone to impress your boss.
She worked her fingers to the bone preparing for the opening of her new business.

Synset Examples

He worked his fingers to the bone for his family.
She worked her fingers to the bone to finish the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toil - to work very hard
