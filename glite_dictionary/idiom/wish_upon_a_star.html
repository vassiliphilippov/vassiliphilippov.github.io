to wish upon a star

1. VERB

/ wɪʃ əˈpɑn ə stɑr / (AmE), / wɪʃ əˈpɒn ə stɑː / (BrE)
to make a wish in the hope that it will come true while looking at a star, often associated with children's traditions or fanciful hopes
make a wish while looking at a star

Examples

As a child, she would always wish upon a star before going to bed.
He closed his eyes and wished upon a star, hoping his dreams would come true.
Many fairy tales begin with a character who dares to wish upon a star.

Synset Examples

It's fun to wish upon a star when you're a kid.
She made a wish upon a star every night.
I still believe you can wish upon a star.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make a wish - express a strong hope or desire for something
