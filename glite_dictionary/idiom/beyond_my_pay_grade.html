beyond my pay grade

1. X

/ biˈjɑnd maɪ peɪ ɡreɪd / (AmE), / bɪˈjɒnd maɪ peɪ ɡreɪd / (BrE)
outside one's level of responsibility, authority, or expertise, especially in a professional context
outside one's responsibility or authority

Examples

Deciding the company's strategy is beyond my pay grade, so you'll need to ask the director.
The technical details are beyond my pay grade, but I can refer you to an expert.
It's beyond my pay grade to approve budgets—please consult the finance department.

Synset Examples

The decision is beyond my pay grade, so I’ll defer.
Handling that issue is beyond my pay grade.
That task is beyond my pay grade, ask the manager.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: above my pay grade - with more responsibility or authority than one's current position

Etymology

From the military and civil service ranking system where pay rates correspond to responsibility and authority.
