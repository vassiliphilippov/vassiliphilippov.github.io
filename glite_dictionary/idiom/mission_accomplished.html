mission accomplished

1. X

/ ˈmɪʃən əˈkɑmplɪʃt / (AmE), / ˈmɪʃ.ən əˈkʌm.plɪʃt / (BrE)
used to indicate that a particular goal or task has been successfully completed
goal or task successfully completed

Examples

After a long day of climbing, Sarah finally reached the summit and exclaimed, mission accomplished.
When the team fixed the software bug, their manager said, 'mission accomplished, everyone!', and thanked them for their hard work.
They looked around at the cleaned-up park and agreed it was truly a case of mission accomplished.

Synset Examples

After weeks of planning, we finally declared mission accomplished.
He smiled and said, 'Mission accomplished!' after the project.
With the event a success, it felt like mission accomplished.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: job done - task completed successfully
  • Synonym: task completed - a job or activity finished with success
