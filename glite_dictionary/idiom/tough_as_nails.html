tough as nails

1. ADJ

/ tʌf əz neɪlz / (AmE), / tʌf əz neɪlz / (BrE)
(of a person) very strong, resilient, or able to endure difficult conditions without being easily hurt or upset.
very tough or resilient (idiom)

Examples

Even as a child, her grandmother was tough as nails and never showed any fear.
You'll have to be tough as nails to succeed in such a competitive industry.
Despite the harsh criticism, he remained tough as nails and kept working towards his goal.

Synset Examples

That boxer is tough as nails and never backs down.
The job requires someone who's tough as nails.
Despite her small size, she's tough as nails.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hard as nails - equally tough or unyielding (idiom)
