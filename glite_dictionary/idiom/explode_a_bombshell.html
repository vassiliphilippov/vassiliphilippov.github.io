to explode a bombshell

1. VERB

/ ɪkˈsploʊd ə ˈbɑmˌʃɛl / (AmE), / ɪkˈspləʊd ə ˈbɒmʃɛl / (BrE)
to make a shocking or unexpected announcement or reveal surprising news
make a shocking announcement

Examples

During the meeting, the CEO exploded a bombshell by announcing the company's merger with a rival firm.
She exploded a bombshell when she revealed her plans to move abroad next year.
After months of speculation, the politician finally exploded a bombshell about his resignation.

Synset Examples

Father exploded a bombshell when he forbade us to go to the prom

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to drop a bombshell - to make an unexpected or shocking announcement
