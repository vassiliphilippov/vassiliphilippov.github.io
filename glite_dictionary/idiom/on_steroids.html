on steroids

1. ADV

/ ɑn ˈstɛɹˌɔɪdz / (AmE), / ɒn ˈstɛɪˌrɔɪdz / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is much more powerful, impressive, or extreme than usual
much more powerful or extreme than usual
informal

Examples

The graphics in the new video game look like realism on steroids.
Her efficient team is like customer service on steroids.
Imagine your favorite dessert, but on steroids—even richer and more decadent than before.

Synset Examples

The new phone features a camera on steroids.

Etymology

From the performance-enhancing effects of anabolic steroids in sports, figuratively extended to other contexts.

2. ADV

/ ɑn ˈstɛrɔɪdz / (AmE), / ɒn ˈstɛrɔɪdz / (BrE)
used to describe something that is much more intense, powerful, or extreme than usual.
much more intense or extreme than usual
informal

Examples

This new computer is technology on steroids.
Her training program is like fitness on steroids, pushing every limit.
The action scenes in the movie are violence on steroids.

Synset Examples

His workout routine is like training on steroids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supercharged - very powerful or intensified
  • Degree Higher: extreme - at the farthest or highest degree of something

Etymology

From the literal use of 'steroids' (performance-enhancing drugs), used metaphorically since the late 20th century.
