in my mind's eye

1. X

/ ɪn maɪ maɪndz aɪ / (AmE), / ɪn maɪ maɪndz aɪ / (BrE)
in one's imagination or as pictured in one's mind; as seen or visualized mentally
in one's imagination; as visualized mentally

Examples

Whenever I think of my childhood home, I see it clearly in my mind's eye.
Even though he's gone, I can still picture his smile in my mind's eye.
Writers often create elaborate worlds in their mind's eye before putting words on paper.

Synset Examples

I can see the scene in my mind's eye.
In my mind's eye, I picture the future.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to imagine - to form a mental image or concept
  • Synonym: picture - to visualize or form a mental image of something

Etymology

Refers to the figurative use of 'eye' to mean the faculty of mental vision, with origins dating back to Shakespeare.
