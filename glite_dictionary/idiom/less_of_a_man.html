less of a man

1. X

/ lɛs əv ə mæn / (AmE), / lɛs əv ə mæn / (BrE)
used to describe feeling inferior or not fully masculine, typically due to a perceived weakness or shortcoming
feeling inferior as a man

Examples

He worried that losing his job would make him feel less of a man in front of his family.
Some people believe that crying makes you less of a man, but that's just a stereotype.
The injury left him feeling less of a man, though his friends reassured him otherwise.

Synset Examples

Being a moffie shouldn’t make you feel less of a man.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unmanly - not having qualities traditionally associated with men
  • Synonym: emasculated - deprived of strength or masculinity
  • Antonym: more of a man - having greater perceived masculinity or strength
