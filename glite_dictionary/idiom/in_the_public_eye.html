in the public eye

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ðə ˈpʌblɪk aɪ / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈpʌblɪk aɪ / (BrE)
well known to the public and often discussed or watched; under public attention or scrutiny
well known; under public attention

Examples

After winning the award, she found herself in the public eye more than ever.
Celebrities often struggle with the pressure of being in the public eye all the time.
The scandal brought the company in the public eye for several months.

Synset Examples

a person in the public eye

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the limelight - receiving a great deal of public attention
