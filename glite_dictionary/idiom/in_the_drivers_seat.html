in the driver's seat

1. ADP

/ ɪn ðə ˈdraɪvɚz siːt / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈdraɪvəz siːt / (BrE)
in control of a situation or in a position of power and responsibility
in control; in a position of power

Examples

After the merger, the new CEO was clearly in the driver's seat and made all the major decisions.
With so much experience, she found herself in the driver's seat during key negotiations.
The unexpected election results put the minority party in the driver's seat for the first time in years.

Synset Examples

Now that she has the promotion, she’s in the driver’s seat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in charge - having control or responsibility for something
  • Synonym: at the helm - in control or in charge of an organization
