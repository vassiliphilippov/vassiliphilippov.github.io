scared spitless

1. ADJ

/ skɛrd ˈspɪtləs / (AmE), / skɛəd ˈspɪtləs / (BrE)
extremely frightened or terrified; so scared as to be unable to react.
extremely frightened
informal

Examples

The children were scared spitless by the haunted house attraction.
I was scared spitless when I heard footsteps outside my window late at night.
After hearing the loud crash, he stood there scared spitless and unable to move.

Synset Examples

She was scared spitless when the dog barked unexpectedly.
The thunderstorm left me scared spitless at night.
Watching that movie left him scared spitless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrified - extremely frightened
  • Antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness, anger, or other strong emotions
