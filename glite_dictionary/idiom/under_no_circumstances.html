under no circumstances

1. ADV

/ ˌʌndɚ ˈnoʊ ˈsɝːkəmˌstænsɪz / (AmE), / ˌʌndə ˈnəʊ ˈsɜːkəmˌstænsɪz / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something must never happen or be allowed, whatever the situation may be
never allowed in any situation

Examples

Under no circumstances should you attempt to fix the machine yourself.
The manager explained that under no circumstances were employees to disclose client information.
Under no circumstances is smoking permitted in the operating room.

Synset Examples

Under no circumstances can you enter this area.
Under no circumstances should you share your password.
Under no circumstances will I tolerate that behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on no account - not for any reason or in any situation
