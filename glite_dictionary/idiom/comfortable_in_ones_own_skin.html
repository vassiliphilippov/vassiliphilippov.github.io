comfortable in one's own skin

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌmfərtəbəl ɪn wʌnz oʊn skɪn / (AmE), / ˈkʌmfətəbəl ɪn wʌnz əʊn skɪn / (BrE)
feeling self-accepting and confident; being at ease with oneself and one's identity
self-accepting and confident

Examples

After years of struggling with anxiety, he finally felt comfortable in his own skin.
It's inspiring to see someone so comfortable in her own skin despite societal pressures.
Many people find it difficult to be comfortable in their own skin during adolescence.

Synset Examples

Being comfortable in her own skin boosted her confidence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-confident - having confidence in oneself and one's abilities
  • Antonym: insecure - not confident or assured; uncertain and anxious about oneself
