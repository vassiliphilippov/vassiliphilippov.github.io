steel trap

1. NOUN

/ stil træp / (AmE), / stiːl træp / (BrE)
A mind or intelligence that is extremely quick to notice or understand things, especially one that is highly alert and incisive.
extremely sharp or quick mind

Examples

She has a mind like a steel trap and never forgets a detail.
His steel trap intelligence impressed everyone in the meeting.
People say she's as sharp as a steel trap when it comes to solving puzzles.

Synset Examples

he's as sharp as a steel trap
a mind like a steel trap

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sharp - quick to notice and understand things; intelligent

Etymology

Refers to the literal steel trap, known for its quick and decisive snapping action, used metaphorically for mental sharpness.
