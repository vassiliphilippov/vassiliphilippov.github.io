then and there

1. ADV

/ ðɛn ənd ðɛr / (AmE), / ðen ənd ðeə / (BrE)
immediately or at that exact moment; without delay
immediately; at that exact moment

Examples

He decided then and there to quit his job and pursue his dream.
She made her decision then and there, without consulting anyone else.
I apologized then and there for my mistake, not wanting to wait another minute.

Synset Examples

He made a decision then and there to change careers.
She accepted the job offer then and there.
I apologized to her then and there for my mistake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on the spot - immediately, without delay
