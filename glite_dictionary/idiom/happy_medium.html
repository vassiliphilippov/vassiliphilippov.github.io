happy medium

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæpi ˈmiːdiəm / (AmE), / ˈhæpi ˈmiːdiəm / (BrE)
a satisfactory compromise between two extremes
a compromise between extremes

Examples

We tried to find a happy medium between saving money and enjoying our vacation.
His parenting style strikes a happy medium between strictness and leniency.
For dinner, we chose a happy medium that suited both vegetarians and meat-eaters.

Synset Examples

Finding a happy medium is key to resolving conflicts.
The couple sought a happy medium in their differing opinions.
She found a happy medium between work and play.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compromise - an agreement reached by mutual concessions
  • Synonym: middle ground - an intermediate position or compromise between two extremes
  • Antonym: extreme - either of the two ends of a range; the furthest points
