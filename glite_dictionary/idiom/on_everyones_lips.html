on everyone's lips

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ˈɛvriˌwʌnz lɪps / (AmE), / ɒn ˈɛvriˌwʌnz lɪps / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is being widely discussed or is very popular at a particular time
widely discussed; popular topic

Examples

The politician's latest remark quickly became on everyone's lips throughout the city.
This catchy song is on everyone's lips this summer.
Rumors about the merger are on everyone's lips at the office.

Synset Examples

That movie has been on everyone's lips since its release.
The new restaurant is on everyone's lips this week.
Her scandalous affair is on everyone's lips at the office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talk of the town - person or thing currently the subject of most interest or gossip
  • Synonym: in the spotlight - receiving a lot of public attention; widely noticed
