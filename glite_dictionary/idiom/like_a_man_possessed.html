like a man possessed

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə mæn pəˈzɛst / (AmE), / laɪk ə mæn pəˈzɛst / (BrE)
with intense energy, urgency, or focus, as if driven by something uncontrollable
with intense energy or urgency

Examples

He practiced the piano like a man possessed, spending hours perfecting each piece.
She organized the event like a man possessed, missing meals and sleep to ensure everything was perfect.
The artist painted like a man possessed, creating several masterpieces in a single week.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
