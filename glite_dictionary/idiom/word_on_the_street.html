word on the street

1. NOUN

/ wɝd ɑn ðə strit / (AmE), / wɜːd ɒn ðə striːt / (BrE)
informal information or rumors circulating among the public; what people are saying unofficially
circulating rumor or informal information
informal

Examples

The word on the street is that the company will launch a new product next month.
If you believe the word on the street, the mayor might resign soon.
She always knows the latest word on the street before anyone else.

Synset Examples

I heard from the word on the street that a big event is coming.
The word on the street is that they're getting a new manager.
According to the word on the street, the café is closing soon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rumor - unverified information spread among people
