more chins than Chinatown

1. X

/ mɔr tʃɪnz ðən ˈtʃaɪnaˌtaʊn / (AmE), / mɔː tʃɪnz ðən ˈtʃaɪnətaʊn / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) Used to describe someone who has a lot of excess weight in the face, especially having multiple chins.
very overweight, especially in the face (humorous idiom)
informal slang

Examples

After the holidays, he joked that he had more chins than Chinatown.
She laughed and said she had more chins than Chinatown after finishing the giant meal.
When he saw himself in the mirror, he thought he had more chins than Chinatown.

Synset Examples

He realized he had more chins than Chinatown after the buffet.

Related Terms
