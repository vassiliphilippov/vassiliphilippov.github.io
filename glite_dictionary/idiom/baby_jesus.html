baby Jesus

1. X

/ ˈbeɪ.bi ˈdʒiː.zəs / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi ˈdʒiː.zəs / (BrE)
A humorous or ironic expression used to indicate sadness or disapproval, often with exaggerated or playful tone.
humorous expression of disapproval
informal

Examples

If you keep leaving your socks on the floor, baby Jesus will cry.
Wasting food always makes baby Jesus sad, or so my grandmother says.
Drinking before noon would make baby Jesus very disappointed in you.

Synset Examples

Eating that much junk food would make baby Jesus cry.
