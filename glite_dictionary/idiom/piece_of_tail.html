piece of tail

1. NOUN

/ pis əv teɪl / (AmE), / piːs əv teɪl / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar, offensive) A person regarded solely as an object for sexual activity, often in a casual or disrespectful way.
sexual partner (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar offensive
