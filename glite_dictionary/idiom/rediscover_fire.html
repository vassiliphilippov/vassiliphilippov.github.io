to rediscover fire

1. VERB

/ ˌriːdɪˈskʌvər ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˌriːdɪˈskʌvə ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
to find new inspiration, energy, or to create something significant as if for the first time
find new inspiration or innovation

Examples

The new manager helped the team rediscover fire and approach challenges with renewed passion.
After years of routine, she traveled abroad to rediscover fire in her photography.
Their music allowed listeners to rediscover fire in the genre, bringing freshness to familiar sounds.

Synset Examples

They hope to rediscover fire in their innovative project.
The artist aimed to rediscover fire in her latest work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to reinvent the wheel - to waste time creating something that already exists
