to call your number

1. VERB

/ kɔl jɔr ˈnʌmbɚ / (AmE), / kɔːl jɔː ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
(of fate, life, or an authority) to single you out for consequences, judgment, or unavoidable fate.
to face consequences or unavoidable fate
informal

Examples

If you keep breaking the rules, eventually fate will call your number.
The time may come when justice will finally call your number.
She lived recklessly, always worried that life would one day call her number.

Synset Examples

If you don’t change your attitude, life will call your number.
You keep ignoring the signs; fate will call your number soon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meet one's fate - to experience one's destined or inevitable outcome
  • Synonym: pay the price - to experience negative consequences for one's actions
