to give a whap

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ə wæp / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə wæp / (BrE)
to hit or strike something forcefully, often making a sharp sound
to hit or strike forcefully

Examples

He decided to give a whap to the old television set when it stopped working.
Whenever the vending machine jams, I give a whap to the side and it usually starts again.
The child gave a whap to the piñata with her stick.

Synset Examples

He gave the ball a whap and sent it flying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to whap - to strike with a sharp sound
  • Synonym: hit - to strike forcefully
