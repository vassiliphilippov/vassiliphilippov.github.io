ears pinned back

1. X

/ ɪrz pɪnd bæk / (AmE), / ɪəz pɪnd bæk / (BrE)
to be severely reprimanded or scolded; sometimes, to be extremely attentive or alert
to be severely reprimanded; sometimes, extremely alert

Examples

After the mistake, the coach had my ears pinned back in front of the whole team.
The manager's harsh words left him with his ears pinned back.
Whenever she listens to a lecture, she sits there with her ears pinned back, not missing a word.

Synset Examples

When the manager spoke, I felt my ears pinned back.
He didn't like to have his ears pinned back during the argument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rebuke - a sharp criticism or reprimand
  • Synonym: scold - to criticize angrily
