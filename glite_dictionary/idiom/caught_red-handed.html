caught red-handed

1. ADJ

/ kɔt ˌrɛd ˈhændɪd / (AmE), / kɔːt ˌrɛd ˈhændɪd / (BrE)
discovered in the act of doing something wrong or illegal
discovered while committing a wrongdoing

Examples

The burglar was caught red-handed as he tried to climb out of the window.
She was caught red-handed copying answers during the exam.
Several employees were caught red-handed taking money from the cash register.

Synset Examples

He was caught red-handed stealing cookies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caught in the act - discovered while committing an offense

Etymology

From the literal expression of having blood on one's hands after committing a violent act, especially found in Scottish usage from the 15th century.
