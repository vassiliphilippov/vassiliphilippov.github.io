sacré bleu

1. INTJ

/ sæˌkreɪ ˈblu / (AmE), / saˌkreɪ ˈblɜː / (BrE)
an exclamation expressing surprise, shock, or disbelief, often used humorously to imitate French usage
expression of surprise or shock (imitating French)

Examples

He opened the letter and cried, sacré bleu! What a fortune!
Sacré bleu, how did you manage to solve the puzzle so quickly?
Every time something unexpected happened, he would exclaim sacré bleu in a thick French accent.

Synset Examples

Sacré bleu! I can't believe you did that!
She exclaimed, 'Sacré bleu! What a surprise!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: my goodness - expression of surprise or astonishment
  • Synonym: oh my god - exclamation of shock or surprise

Etymology

French, from 'sacré' (holy) and 'bleu' (blue), historically a euphemism for 'sacré Dieu' (holy God).
