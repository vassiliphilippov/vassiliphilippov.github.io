to look like a dog's breakfast

1. VERB

/ lʊk laɪk ə dɔɡz ˈbrɛkfəst / (AmE), / lʊk laɪk ə dɒɡz ˈbrɛkfəst / (BrE)
to appear very messy, disorganized, or untidy
appear messy or disorganized
british informal

Examples

After the storm, the garden looked like a dog's breakfast with branches and leaves everywhere.
His attempt at making a cake looked like a dog's breakfast when he pulled it out of the oven.
She wore so many clashing colors that her outfit looked like a dog's breakfast.

Synset Examples

After the party, the living room looked like a dog's breakfast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: look a mess - to appear untidy or disordered
