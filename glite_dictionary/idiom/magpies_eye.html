magpie's eye

1. NOUN

/ ˈmægˌpaɪz aɪ / (AmE), / ˈmæɡ.paɪz aɪ / (BrE)
an instinct or strong attraction for noticing and collecting shiny or attractive things
instinct for shiny or attractive things

Examples

She has a magpie's eye for beautiful jewelry.
Anyone with a magpie's eye will love browsing the antique market.
His magpie's eye quickly spotted the rare coin among the old trinkets.

Synset Examples

She has a magpie's eye for beautiful jewelry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magpie - a long-tailed bird known for collecting shiny objects

Etymology

From the magpie bird's reputation for collecting shiny objects.
