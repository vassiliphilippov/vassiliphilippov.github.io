pain in the backside

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪn ɪn ðə ˈbækˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈpeɪn ɪn ðə ˈbæk.saɪd / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes annoyance or trouble; a nuisance
an annoying or troublesome person or thing
informal

Examples

Filling out all these forms is a real pain in the backside.
She's lovely, but sometimes her perfectionism is a pain in the backside.
The constant delays have become quite a pain in the backside for everyone involved.

Synset Examples

That project has been a real pain in the backside.
Her constant interruptions were a pain in the backside.
Fixing the car turned out to be a pain in the backside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nuisance - a person or thing causing inconvenience or annoyance
  • Synonym: pain in the neck - someone or something that is very annoying
  • Antonym: pleasure - a feeling of happy satisfaction and enjoyment
