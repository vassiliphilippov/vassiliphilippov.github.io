at the drop of a hat

1. ADV

/ ət ðə drɑp əv ə hæt / (AmE), / ət ðə drɒp əv ə hæt / (BrE)
immediately and without any hesitation
immediately, without hesitation

Examples

He agreed to join the project at the drop of a hat, showing his enthusiasm.
She is ready to pack her bags and travel at the drop of a hat.
If you ever need help, just call; I'll be there at the drop of a hat.

Synset Examples

She helps her friends at the drop of a hat.
They would fight for justice at the drop of a hat.
He would travel anywhere at the drop of a hat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: instantly - immediately, without delay
  • Antonym: hesitantly - with hesitation or delay

Etymology

Possibly from the practice of dropping a hat as a signal for starting an event or contest in the 19th century.
