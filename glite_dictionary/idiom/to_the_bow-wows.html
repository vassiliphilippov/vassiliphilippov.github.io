to the bow-wows

1. X

/ tə ðə ˈbaʊˌwaʊz / (AmE), / tə ðə ˈbaʊˌwaʊz / (BrE)
(informal, dated) Into a state of ruin, disaster, or total failure.
into disaster or ruin
informal

Examples

After the scandal, the politician's reputation went to the bow-wows and never recovered.
If we don't act now, this project might go to the bow-wows within months.
Their investment strategy took a wrong turn and the portfolio went to the bow-wows.

Synset Examples

The company's finances went to the bow-wows after mismanagement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: down the drain - lost, wasted, or ruined
  • Synonym: to the dogs - falling into ruin or disrepair
