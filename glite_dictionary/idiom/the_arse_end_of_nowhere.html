the arse end of nowhere

1. NOUN

/ ði ɑrs ɛnd əv ˈnoʊwɛr / (AmE), / ði ɑːs end əv ˈnəʊweə / (BrE)
an extremely remote or isolated place, far from cities or civilization, often considered undesirable or inconvenient to reach
an extremely remote or isolated place
informal british

Examples

She moved to the arse end of nowhere to escape city life.
It takes forever to get supplies out here in the arse end of nowhere.
We got lost and ended up in the arse end of nowhere last night.

Synset Examples

This place feels like the arse end of nowhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the middle of nowhere - a place that is very remote or isolated

Etymology

Derived from British English slang 'arse' (buttocks) combined with 'nowhere' to emphasize extreme remoteness.
