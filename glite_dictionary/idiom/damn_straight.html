damn straight

1. ADV

/ dæm streɪt / (AmE), / dæm streɪt / (BrE)
used to strongly agree with or confirm something; certainly; absolutely yes.
used to strongly confirm or agree
informal slang

Examples

"Are you coming to the game?" "Damn straight, I wouldn't miss it for the world!"
If you think I’m proud of you, you’re damn straight.
"You really finished that in one day?" "Damn straight, I did."

Synset Examples

You’re damn straight I’ll support you no matter what.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absolutely - used to emphasize agreement or confirmation
  • Synonym: definitely - without doubt; certainly
  • Antonym: no way - absolutely not; refusal or strong disagreement
