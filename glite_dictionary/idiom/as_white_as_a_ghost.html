as white as a ghost

1. ADJ

/ əz waɪt əz ə ɡoʊst / (AmE), / əz waɪt əz ə ɡəʊst / (BrE)
extremely pale, often because of fear, shock, or illness
extremely pale (idiom)

Examples

When she heard the bad news, she looked as white as a ghost.
After stepping off the rollercoaster, he was as white as a ghost.
The children became as white as a ghost when they saw the shadow in the hallway.

Synset Examples

He turned white as a ghost after the shock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as pale as a sheet - extremely pale, especially from shock or fear
