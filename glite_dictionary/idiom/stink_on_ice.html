stink on ice

1. NOUN

/ stɪŋk ɑn aɪs / (AmE), / stɪŋk ɒn aɪs / (BrE)
An informal phrase meaning a complete failure or something exceptionally bad.
utter failure; exceptionally bad situation
informal

Examples

Their new advertising campaign was a complete stink on ice and failed to attract any customers.
The movie turned out to be a stink on ice, with critics universally panning it.
His attempt at baking a cake was a stink on ice—it was burnt and inedible.

Synset Examples

That performance was a total stink on ice.
Her attitude stinks on ice today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disaster - a sudden event, such as an accident or a natural catastrophe, that causes great damage or loss
  • Synonym: catastrophe - an event causing great and often sudden damage or suffering; a disaster
  • Antonym: success - the accomplishment of an aim or purpose
