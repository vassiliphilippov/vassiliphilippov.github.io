to hit my funny bone

1. VERB

/ hɪt maɪ ˈfʌni boʊn / (AmE), / hɪt maɪ ˈfʌni bəʊn / (BrE)
to make someone laugh or amuse someone greatly
to make someone laugh

Examples

That comedian's joke hit my funny bone and I couldn't stop laughing.
Her clever puns always hit my funny bone during meetings.
The movie hit my funny bone with its witty dialogue and quirky characters.

Synset Examples

Her stories really hit my funny bone.
He has a knack for hitting my funny bone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to crack up - to suddenly laugh a lot
  • Synonym: to tickle someone's funny bone - to amuse someone; to make someone laugh
