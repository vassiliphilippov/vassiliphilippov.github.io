to lift one's spirits

1. VERB

/ lɪft wʌnz ˈspɪrɪts / (AmE), / lɪft wʌnz ˈspɪrɪts / (BrE)
to make someone feel happier or more cheerful
make someone feel happier

Examples

The surprise birthday party really lifted my spirits after a tough week.
Listening to cheerful music can often lift your spirits on a gloomy day.
Her kind words went a long way to lift his spirits during his illness.

Synset Examples

We enjoyed the pagalee during the festival, lifting our spirits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cheer up - to become or make someone happier
  • Antonym: to bring down - to make someone feel less happy
