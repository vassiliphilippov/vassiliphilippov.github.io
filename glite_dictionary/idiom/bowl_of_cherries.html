bowl of cherries

1. NOUN

/ boʊl əv ˈtʃɛriz / (AmE), / bəʊl əv ˈtʃɛəriz / (BrE)
a situation that is very pleasant or enjoyable
very pleasant or enjoyable situation

Examples

She thought moving to the city would be a bowl of cherries, but it came with its own set of challenges.
Winning the lottery turned their lives into a real bowl of cherries.
He warned me that not every relationship is a bowl of cherries.

Synset Examples

He described his new job as a bowl of cherries.
She found herself in a bowl of cherries after the promotion.
Life isn't always a bowl of cherries, but I try to stay positive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bed of roses - an easy, comfortable situation
  • Synonym: piece of cake - something very easy or enjoyable
  • Antonym: not a bed of roses - a situation that is not pleasant or easy
