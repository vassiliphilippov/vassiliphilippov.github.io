to turn pain into lemonade

1. VERB

/ tɝn peɪn ˈɪntu ˌlɛməˌneɪd / (AmE), / tɜːn peɪn ˈɪntuː ˌlɛmənˈeɪd / (BrE)
to create something positive or beneficial out of a painful or negative situation
make something good from pain or adversity

Examples

After losing her job, she tried to turn pain into lemonade by starting her own business.
He used his difficult childhood as inspiration and managed to turn pain into lemonade.
The artist's music is proof that you can turn pain into lemonade with creativity and courage.

Synset Examples

After the breakup, she decided to turn her pain into lemonade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to make lemonade out of lemons - respond to adversity by making the best of it

Etymology

Modelled on the idiom 'when life gives you lemons, make lemonade,' associating 'lemons' (sourness) with adversity.
