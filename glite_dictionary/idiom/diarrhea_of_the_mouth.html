diarrhea of the mouth

1. NOUN

/ daɪˈəriə əv ðə maʊθ / (AmE), / dɪəˈriːə əv ðə maʊθ / (BrE)
informal excessive or uncontrollable talking, especially when it is annoying or unwelcome
excessive or uncontrollable talking
informal

Examples

Sometimes my little brother suffers from diarrhea of the mouth when he's excited.
The teacher had to interrupt him because his diarrhea of the mouth was distracting the whole class.
After three cups of coffee, he was unstoppable—his diarrhea of the mouth became legendary at work.

Synset Examples

She couldn't stop talking, it was pure diarrhea of the mouth.
During the meeting, she had diarrhea of the mouth.
His constant chatter was just diarrhea of the mouth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chatterbox - a person who talks incessantly
  • Synonym: motor mouth - a person who talks very rapidly or excessively
