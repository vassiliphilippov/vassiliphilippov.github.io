to pull a muscle

1. VERB

/ pʊl ə ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / pʊl ə ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
to injure a muscle by overstretching it or straining it too much
injure a muscle by overstretching

Examples

She pulled a muscle in her back while lifting heavy boxes.
After running too fast, he realized he had pulled a muscle in his calf.
If you don't warm up before exercise, you might pull a muscle.

Synset Examples

I pulled a muscle in my thigh while jogging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strain - to injure or overstretch a muscle or tendon
