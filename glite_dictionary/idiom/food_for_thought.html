food for thought

1. NOUN

/ fuːd fər ˈθɔt / (AmE), / fuːd fə ˈθɔːt / (BrE)
something that provides mental stimulation or encourages careful consideration
something that stimulates thinking

Examples

Her comments gave me food for thought about how we can improve the project.
The article provided a lot of food for thought on the subject of climate change.
His presentation was full of food for thought and sparked a lively discussion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brainchild - an idea or invention considered as the result of creative thinking

2. NOUN

/ fuːd fər ˈθɔt / (AmE), / fuːd fə ˈθɔːt / (BrE)
an idea or piece of information that is worth serious consideration or reflection
something to think about seriously

Examples

Her latest novel offers plenty of food for thought about society and technology.
The professor's comments gave the students much food for thought after the lecture.
Listening to the debate provided me with some real food for thought.

Synset Examples
