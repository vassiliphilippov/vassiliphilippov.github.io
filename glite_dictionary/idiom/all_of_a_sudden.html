all of a sudden

1. ADV

/ ɔl əv ə ˈsʌdən / (AmE), / ɔːl əv ə ˈsʌdn̩ / (BrE)
very quickly and without any warning; unexpectedly
quickly and unexpectedly

Examples

All of a sudden, the lights went out and the room was plunged into darkness.
He was walking down the street when, all of a sudden, it started to rain heavily.
The dog seemed calm, and then all of a sudden, it started barking loudly.

Synset Examples

he stopped suddenly
suddenly she felt a sharp pain in her side
he decided to go to Chicago on the spur of the moment
he made up his mind suddenly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suddenly - quickly and without warning
  • Synonym: unexpectedly - in an unanticipated way
