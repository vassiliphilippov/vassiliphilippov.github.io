guess what

1. INTJ

/ ɡɛs wʌt / (AmE), / ɡɛs wɒt / (BrE)
used to introduce surprising, interesting, or important news, often to prompt curiosity in the listener
used to introduce surprising news

Examples

Guess what? We're going on vacation next week!
Guess what? Emily just got engaged!
Guess what? The test has been postponed until Friday.

Synset Examples

Guess what? You'll never guess where I found my lost ring!
Guess what? I finally finished my book.
Guess what? I got the job!
