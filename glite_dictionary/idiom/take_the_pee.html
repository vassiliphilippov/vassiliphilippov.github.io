to take the pee

1. VERB

/ teɪk ðə piː / (AmE), / teɪk ðə piː / (BrE)
to mock or tease someone, often in a light-hearted or joking way
mock or tease someone (British informal)
informal british

Examples

He always takes the pee whenever I talk about my hobbies.
Are you taking the pee? I worked hard on that presentation!
They were just taking the pee, so try not to take it personally.

Synset Examples

He always takes the pee when I try to be serious.
Stop taking the pee, this is a serious discussion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: take the piss - to mock or tease someone, often in a light-hearted way

Etymology

A euphemistic variant of the British slang expression "take the piss," with "pee" substituting for "piss".
