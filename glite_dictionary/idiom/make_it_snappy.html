to make it snappy

1. VERB

/ ˈmeɪk ɪt ˈsnæpi / (AmE), / ˈmeɪk ɪt ˈsnæpi / (BrE)
used to tell someone to do something quickly, without delay
do something quickly
informal

Examples

If you want to grab lunch, make it snappy because the meeting starts soon.
The coach shouted, 'Make it snappy!' as the players got ready to leave.
When ordering at the counter, she told the server to make it snappy as she was in a hurry.

Synset Examples

The boss asked us to make it snappy for the report.
Please make it snappy; we’re running out of time.
He always says to make it snappy when we're late.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to hurry up - to move or act more quickly
  • Antonym: take your time - do something without rushing
