toodle pip

1. INTJ

/ ˈtuːdəl pɪp / (AmE), / ˈtuːdəl pɪp / (BrE)
An informal, cheerful way to say goodbye.
informal way to say goodbye
informal british

Examples

As she left the party, she smiled and said, 'toodle pip!'
He called out 'toodle pip' as he hurried out the door to catch his train.
It's been lovely chatting—toodle pip until next time!

Synset Examples

She waved goodbye, saying toodle pip cheerfully.
I have to run now, so toodle pip!
I’ll see you later, toodle pip for now!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - An expression of farewell
  • Synonym: ta-ta - Another informal way to say goodbye
  • Antonym: hello - A greeting or salutation

Etymology

Possibly derived from British slang 'toodle-oo' (itself from French 'à tout à l'heure'), combined with 'pip', a British interjection for good luck or goodbye.
