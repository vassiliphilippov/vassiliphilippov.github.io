one man's fish is another man's poisson

1. X

/ wʌn mænz fɪʃ ɪz əˈnʌðɚ mænz ˈpwæsɑ̃ / (AmE), / wʌn mænz fɪʃ ɪz əˈnʌðə mænz ˈpwasɒ̃ / (BrE)
used to express that tastes and preferences vary from person to person; what one person likes or values, another may dislike or find worthless.
people have different tastes or values

Examples

You may find modern art boring, but one man's fish is another man's poisson.
He disliked the trendy restaurant, but I loved it—one man's fish is another man's poisson.
In music, one man's fish is another man's poisson; what's popular for some may annoy others.

Synset Examples

What I love, you may hate; one man's fish is another man's poisson.
Remember, one man's fish is another man's poisson in business.
In art, one man's fish is another man's poisson.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one man's meat is another man's poison - different things appeal to different people

Etymology

Plays on the proverb 'one man's meat is another man's poison', with 'poisson' (French for 'fish') as a pun.
