to rend the fabric of reality

1. VERB

/ rɛnd ðə ˈfæbrɪk əv riˈæləti / (AmE), / rɛnd ðə ˈfæbrɪk ɒv rɪˈalɪti / (BrE)
to violently disrupt or challenge accepted perceptions of reality, often in a profound or unsettling way
disrupt perceptions of reality

Examples

The artist's latest film seems to rend the fabric of reality, leaving viewers questioning what is possible.
Some science fiction novels rend the fabric of reality with bold theories and twisted timelines.
Witnessing the bizarre event in person felt like rending the fabric of reality.

Synset Examples

The artist's painting seemed to rend the fabric of reality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shatter illusions - to destroy false beliefs or perceptions
  • Synonym: warp reality - to twist or distort what is real
