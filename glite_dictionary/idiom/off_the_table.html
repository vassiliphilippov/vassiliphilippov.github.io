off the table

1. ADJ

/ ɑf ðə ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ɒf ðə ˈteɪbəl / (BrE)
(of an offer, proposal, or option) no longer available or being considered.
no longer available or considered

Examples

After weeks of negotiation, the deal is now off the table.
Due to budget cuts, hiring new staff is off the table this year.
His compromise was rejected and quickly taken off the table.

Synset Examples

The offer is off the table now.
After the latest news, the proposal is off the table.
His suggestion was quickly taken off the table.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: on the table - available or being considered
