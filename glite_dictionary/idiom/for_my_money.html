for my money

1. ADV

/ fɔr maɪ ˈmʌni / (AmE), / fə maɪ ˈmʌni / (BrE)
used to express one's personal opinion or belief about something
in my opinion

Examples

For my money, this is the most interesting part of the exhibition.
For my money, no one does jazz better than Miles Davis.
The repair work is, for my money, not worth the price.

Synset Examples

This book is, for my money, a must-read.
For my money, this is the best restaurant.
That movie is, for my money, a classic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in my opinion - used to state one's personal view
