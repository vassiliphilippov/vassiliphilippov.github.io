to don't believe a word of

1. VERB

/ doʊnt bəˈliv ə wɝd ʌv / (AmE), / dəʊnt bɪˈliːv ə wɜːd ɒv / (BrE)
to not believe anything that someone says; to have no trust in any part of their statement
not believe anything said

Examples

She lied so often that I don't believe a word of what she tells me anymore.
Whenever he makes grand promises, I don't believe a word of it until I see results.
I heard the rumors, but I don't believe a word of them.

Synset Examples

Pish, I don't believe a word of what he said.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: not believe a word - not trust any part of a statement
