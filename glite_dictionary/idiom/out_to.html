out to

1. ADJ

/ aʊt tu / (AmE), / aʊt tuː / (BrE)
having a strong intention or determination to achieve or do something
determined to achieve something

Examples

The team is out to prove everyone wrong this season.
She was clearly out to make a good impression at the interview.
He’s out to break the marathon record this year.

Synset Examples

bent on going to the theater
dead set against intervening
out to win every event

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bent on - determined to do something
  • Synonym: dead set on - firmly determined to do something
