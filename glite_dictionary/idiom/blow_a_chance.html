to blow a chance

1. VERB

/ bloʊ ə tʃæns / (AmE), / bləʊ ə tʃɑːns / (BrE)
to waste an opportunity; to fail to take advantage of a favorable situation
waste an opportunity

Examples

If you arrive late to the interview, you might blow a chance at the job.
He realized he'd blown his chance when he missed the winning shot.
Don’t blow a chance to impress the manager by being unprepared.

Synset Examples

She didn't want to blow her chance to study abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to miss an opportunity - to fail to use a chance to do something
  • Antonym: seize an opportunity - to take advantage of a chance
