village bicycle

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪlɪdʒ baɪˈsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈvɪlɪdʒ ˈbaɪsɪkl̩ / (BrE)
a person, especially a woman, who is considered promiscuous or is regarded as being sexually available to many people within a community
promiscuous person (slang, often derogatory)
slang offensive

Examples

She hated being called the village bicycle after rumors spread about her dating life.
The movie was criticized for portraying the main female character as the village bicycle.
People often unfairly label women as the village bicycle for behavior that goes unnoticed in men.

Synset Examples

Everyone in town knows she's the village bicycle.
Don't be the village bicycle; keep some mystery.
He joked that she was the village bicycle, always dating.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slut - a woman who has many casual sexual partners (offensive slang)
  • Synonym: promiscuous - having or involving many sexual partners
