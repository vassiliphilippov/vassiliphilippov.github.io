forever and a day

1. ADV

/ fəˈrɛvɚ ənd ə deɪ / (AmE), / fəˈrɛvə ənd ə deɪ / (BrE)
For a very long time; seemingly without end.
for an extremely long time

Examples

We waited forever and a day for the train to finally arrive.
She promised to love him forever and a day.
The negotiations dragged on forever and a day before they reached an agreement.

Synset Examples

she took forever to write the paper
we had to wait forever and a day

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forever - for all time; always; endlessly
  • Synonym: eternally - for all time; permanently
