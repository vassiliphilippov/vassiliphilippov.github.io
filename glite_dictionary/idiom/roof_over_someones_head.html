roof over someone's head

1. NOUN

/ ɹuːf ˈoʊvɚ sʌmˌwʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / ɹuːf ˈəʊvə sʌmwʌnz hɛd / (BrE)
a place to live or shelter; somewhere to stay or reside
shelter or place to live

Examples

After losing his job, he worried about keeping a roof over his head.
Many people are grateful just to have a roof over their heads each night.
Charities work to ensure that everyone has a roof over their head.

Synset Examples

He found a modest apartment to provide a roof over his head.
She worked hard to ensure her family had a roof over their heads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shelter - a place giving temporary protection from bad weather or danger
  • Synonym: home - the place where one lives permanently, especially as a member of a family or household
