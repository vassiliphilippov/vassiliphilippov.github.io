as black as one is painted

1. ADJ

/ əz blæk æz wʌn ɪz ˈpeɪntɪd / (AmE), / əz blæk æz wʌn ɪz ˈpeɪntɪd / (BrE)
not as bad or wicked as people say or believe
not as bad as perceived

Examples

Despite the rumors, he is not as black as one is painted and has helped many people.
Everyone assumes the villain is evil, but she isn’t as black as one is painted.
They realized the company’s new manager wasn’t as black as one is painted after meeting her in person.

Synset Examples

Her reputation is not as black as one is painted.
He's not as black as one is painted; he's quite kind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: misunderstood - not understood or judged correctly

Etymology

From the metaphor of painting someone as evil or bad in stories and descriptions.
