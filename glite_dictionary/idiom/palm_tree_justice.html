palm tree justice

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑm ˌtri ˈdʒʌstɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɑːm ˌtriː ˈdʒʌstɪs / (BrE)
an informal or community-based method of resolving disputes, often based on mutual understanding rather than formal law
informal, community-based dispute resolution

Examples

After the players argued on the field, the coach suggested they rely on palm tree justice to resolve the matter.
In villages without formal courts, palm tree justice is often seen as fair and binding.
They laughed about how every neighborhood dispute was settled with a bit of palm tree justice.

Synset Examples

She said they needed palm tree justice to settle their argument.
They joked that all disagreements ended in palm tree justice.
The locals believe in palm tree justice for petty disputes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rough justice - informal or summary justice, often without strict adherence to law
  • Synonym: vigilante justice - taking the law into one's own hands to deliver justice
