most importantly

1. ADV

/ moʊst ɪmˈpɔrtəntli / (AmE), / məʊst ɪmˈpɔːtəntli / (BrE)
used to emphasize that one thing is more significant than anything else mentioned
above all; chiefly

Examples

Most importantly, we must ensure everyone's safety during the event.
She finished her project on time and, most importantly, met all the requirements.
The house is beautiful and, most importantly, within our budget.

Synset Examples

above all, you must be independent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: above all - more than anything else
