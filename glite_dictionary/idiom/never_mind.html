never mind

1. INTJ

/ ˌnɛvɚ ˈmaɪnd / (AmE), / ˌnɛvə ˈmaɪnd / (BrE)
used to tell someone not to worry about something or to disregard what has just been said.
don't worry; disregard

Examples

Never mind the delay; we can start the meeting now.
If you can't find the answer, never mind, we'll try something else.
She apologized and I told her to never mind, it was no trouble at all.

Synset Examples

Never mind the details, let’s focus on the results.
Never mind what they think, just be yourself.
I forgot my keys, but never mind, I’ll manage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forget it - used to tell someone not to concern themselves further
  • Synonym: don't worry - expression to reassure someone that there is no problem
