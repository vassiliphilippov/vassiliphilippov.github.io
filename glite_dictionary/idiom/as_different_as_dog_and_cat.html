as different as dog and cat

1. ADJ

/ əz ˈdɪfɚənt əz dɔɡ ənd kæt / (AmE), / əz ˈdɪfərənt əz dɒɡ ənd kæt / (BrE)
used to describe two people or things that are extremely different from each other in important ways
extremely different from each other

Examples

Although they're twins, their personalities are as different as dog and cat.
Our tastes in music are as different as dog and cat.
Eva and Max are as different as dog and cat when it comes to their approach to problem-solving.

Synset Examples

The siblings are as different as dog and cat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chalk and cheese - used for two things that are completely different
  • Antonym: as alike as two peas in a pod - used for two things that are extremely similar
