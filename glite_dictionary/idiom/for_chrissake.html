for chrissake

1. INTJ

/ fər krɪˈseɪk / (AmE), / fə krɪˈseɪk / (BrE)
used to express irritation, frustration, or urgency, often for emphasis
expression of frustration or urgency
informal

Examples

For chrissake, just answer the question already!
Could you close the window, for chrissake, it's freezing in here.
I'm late, for chrissake, so please hurry up.

Synset Examples

Don't forget your keys for chrissake, it's important!
Stop complaining for chrissake and just get it done!
Can you be quiet for chrissake while I work?
Keep it down, for chrissakes, it's late!
Try to be polite, for chrissakes, it’s a meeting.
I can't believe you did that, for chrissakes!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for heaven's sake - used to express annoyance, surprise, or urgency
  • Synonym: for Pete's sake - used to express frustration or emphasis, less direct than referencing Christ

Etymology

Alteration of 'for Christ's sake,' with euphemistic spelling to avoid blasphemy.
