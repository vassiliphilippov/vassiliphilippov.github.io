as the day is long

1. ADV

/ æz ðə deɪ ɪz lɔŋ / (AmE), / æz ðə deɪ ɪz lɒŋ / (BrE)
(informal) For a very long time; to a great extent or degree, often used to emphasize continuity, intensity, or busyness.
for a very long time; extremely busy or intense
informal

Examples

He can keep talking as the day is long if you let him.
Sarah's kindness is genuine as the day is long.
I've been working as the day is long to finish this project.

Synset Examples

He’s been busy, as the day is long.
She’s been working hard, as the day is long.
They will argue, as the day is long.
