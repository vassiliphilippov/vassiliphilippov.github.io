no bones about it

1. X

/ noʊ boʊnz əˈbaʊt ɪt / (AmE), / nəʊ bəʊnz əˈbaʊt ɪt / (BrE)
used to emphasize that there is no doubt or uncertainty about something; definitely or certainly
without doubt; definitely

Examples

There are no bones about it; this is the best restaurant in town.
She is, no bones about it, the most talented player on the team.
He made his feelings clear, no bones about it.

Synset Examples

There are no bones about it; I love this job.
She was, no bones about it, the best in class.
He made it clear, no bones about it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: without question - without any doubt; certainly
  • Synonym: without a doubt - used to stress that something is true or certain
  • Antonym: with reservations - with doubts or hesitations

Etymology

Probably evolved in the 19th century from an earlier phrase 'to make no bones,' meaning to have no objection.
