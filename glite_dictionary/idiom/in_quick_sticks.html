in quick sticks

1. ADV

/ ɪn kwɪk stɪks / (AmE), / ɪn kwɪk stɪks / (BrE)
very quickly or hurriedly; at great speed.
very quickly; hurriedly

Examples

When she heard the news, she left the office in quick sticks to catch the last train.
We had to pack our bags in quick sticks before the taxi arrived.
The team finished the task in quick sticks to meet the deadline.

Synset Examples
