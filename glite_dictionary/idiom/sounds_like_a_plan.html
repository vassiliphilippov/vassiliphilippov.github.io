sounds like a plan

1. X

/ saʊndz laɪk ə plæn / (AmE), / saʊndz laɪk ə plæn / (BrE)
used to express agreement or approval of a suggested idea or proposal
expression of agreement or approval
informal

Examples

"Let's order pizza and watch a movie." "Sounds like a plan!"
If you want to start early tomorrow, sounds like a plan.
"We'll split the work evenly." "Sounds like a plan to me."

Synset Examples

Let’s meet at noon; sounds like a plan!
If you handle the setup, I’ll bring the food; sounds like a plan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: works for me - expression indicating acceptance or agreement
  • Synonym: fine by me - shows acceptance of a suggestion
  • Antonym: not a chance - expression of strong refusal or disagreement
