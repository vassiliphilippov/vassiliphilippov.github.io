green-eyed monster

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡriːn aɪd ˈmɑn.stɚ / (AmE), / ˌɡriːn aɪd ˈmɒn.stə / (BrE)
jealousy, especially in the context of envy toward a rival
jealousy (idiom)

Examples

Her green-eyed monster appeared whenever she saw her colleague praised in front of the team.
Don't let the green-eyed monster ruin your friendship over something so trivial.
He tried to hide the green-eyed monster, but his envy was obvious to everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jealousy - a feeling of envy toward someone for their success or advantages
  • Synonym: envy - a feeling of wishing you had what someone else has

Etymology

First used by William Shakespeare in Othello to personify jealousy as a 'green-eyed monster'.
