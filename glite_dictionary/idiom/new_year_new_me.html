new year, new me

1. X

/ nuː jɪr nuː miː / (AmE), / njuː jɪə njuː miː / (BrE)
a phrase expressing the intention to make a fresh start or improve oneself at the beginning of the new year
making a fresh start in the new year

Examples

Every January, social media is full of people proclaiming new year, new me with their resolutions.
He laughed and said, 'new year, new me,' as he joined the gym for the first time.
It's that time of the year again—new year, new me—but will the motivation last past February?

Synset Examples

After the holidays, he joked about his new year, new me transformation.
She posted her resolutions online, claiming it's a new year, new me.
This year, I’m determined to embrace a new year, new me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fresh start - a new beginning; starting over
