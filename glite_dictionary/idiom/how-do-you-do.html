how-do-you-do

1. NOUN

/ ˌhaʊ də ju ˈdu / (AmE), / ˌhaʊ də juː ˈduː / (BrE)
an awkward, difficult, or problematic situation; a commotion or fuss
awkward or problematic situation

Examples

After she spilled the drinks at the meeting, it was quite a how-do-you-do.
There was a real how-do-you-do when the wrong speech was played at the ceremony.
If there's another how-do-you-do at the office party this year, I'll be surprised.

Synset Examples

that's a fine how-d'ye-do

Related Terms

  • Synonym: predicament - A difficult, unpleasant, or embarrassing situation
  • Synonym: commotion - A state of confused and noisy disturbance

Etymology

From the phrase 'how do you do?' used facetiously to mean a disturbance or awkward predicament.
