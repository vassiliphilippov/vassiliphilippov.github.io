to smoke your own dope

1. VERB

/ smoʊk jʊr oʊn doʊp / (AmE), / sməʊk jɔːr əʊn dəʊp / (BrE)
to act in a way that is self-destructive or that undermines one's own efforts, especially by believing in one's own exaggerated ideas or hype
act in a self-destructive or self-deluding way
informal slang

Examples

If you start to smoke your own dope, you may lose sight of reality and make poor decisions.
In the tech industry, leaders sometimes smoke their own dope and ignore critical feedback.
She warned him not to smoke his own dope if he wanted the project to succeed.

Synset Examples

It's risky to smoke your own dope in business.
Don't smoke your own dope if you want to succeed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to shoot yourself in the foot - to do something that damages your own interests
  • Synonym: self-sabotage - deliberately or unconsciously hindering one's own success
  • Synonym: to undermine - to make something, especially someone's confidence or authority, gradually weaker

Etymology

From the literal meaning of 'smoke' (inhale from a cigarette/pipe) and 'dope' (slang for drugs), later used figuratively to mean believing your own hype.
