as luck may have it

1. X

/ æz lʌk meɪ hæv ɪt / (AmE), / əz lʌk meɪ həv ɪt / (BrE)
used to say that something happened by chance or due to luck, especially when introducing a fortunate or unfortunate event
by chance or fortune

Examples

As luck may have it, my friend happened to be driving by just when I needed a ride.
As luck may have it, the store was still open when we arrived late at night.
We missed our train, but as luck may have it, the next one came just five minutes later.

Synset Examples

As luck may have it, she won the lottery.
As luck may have it, I found my lost keys.
As luck may have it, the rain stopped just in time.
As luck would have it, I got the last ticket.
As luck would have it, we met at the café.
As luck would have it, the job was still available.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: by chance - without planning; accidentally
  • Synonym: as fate would have it - used to introduce an event caused by fate or chance
