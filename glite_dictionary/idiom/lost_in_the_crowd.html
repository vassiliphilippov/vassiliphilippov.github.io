lost in the crowd

1. ADJ

/ lɔst ɪn ðə kraʊd / (AmE), / lɒst ɪn ðə kraʊd / (BrE)
not able to be noticed or found because there are many people or things around
unable to be noticed among many people

Examples

She felt lost in the crowd at her new school, surrounded by strangers.
His talent was lost in the crowd of artists at the exhibition.
The message got lost in the crowd of emails I receive every day.

Synset Examples

The little man was lost in the crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lost - unable to be found or lacking direction
