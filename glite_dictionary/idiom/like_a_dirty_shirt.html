like a dirty shirt

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə ˈdɝti ʃɝt / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈdɜːti ʃɜːt / (BrE)
(informal) Very abruptly or without hesitation, especially when leaving quickly or getting involved eagerly.
without hesitation; abruptly
informal

Examples

After the final whistle, the fans filed out like a dirty shirt.
He jumped into the conversation like a dirty shirt, not waiting his turn.
The cat bolted out the door like a dirty shirt when it saw the open window.

Synset Examples

He left the party off like a dirty shirt after the argument.
