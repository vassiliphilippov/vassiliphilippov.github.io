to leave it be

1. VERB

/ ˈliːv ɪt biː / (AmE), / ˈliːv ɪt biː / (BrE)
to refrain from interfering with or changing something; to let something remain as it is
to not interfere or change something

Examples

If the paint is still wet, it's best to leave it be until it dries.
She wanted to tidy his desk, but he asked her to leave it be.
Sometimes the best solution is to leave it be and move on.

Synset Examples

I’ll just leave it be and see what happens.
Let’s leave it be and not interfere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to let it be - to not interfere or take further action; to leave things as they are
