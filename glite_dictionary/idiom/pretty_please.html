pretty please

1. X

/ ˈprɪti pliːz / (AmE), / ˈprɪti pliːz / (BrE)
used to make a request more polite or insistent, often in a pleading or childlike manner
emphatic or polite request phrase
informal

Examples

Can I borrow your car, pretty please?
Would you mind reviewing my paper, pretty please?
Let’s go to the park, pretty please!

Synset Examples

She asked him to help her, pretty please.
Could you pass the salt, pretty please?
Can I have a cookie, pretty please?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: please - used to politely ask for something
