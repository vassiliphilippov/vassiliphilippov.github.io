to catch the sun

1. VERB

/ kætʃ ðə sʌn / (AmE), / katʃ ðə sʌn / (BrE)
to sunbathe or spend time in sunlight to enjoy its warmth
to sunbathe or enjoy sunlight

Examples

Let's go outside and catch the sun before it gets cloudy.
After a long winter, everyone was eager to catch the sun in the park.
She loves to catch the sun by the pool during summer holidays.

Synset Examples

We set up our towels to catch the sun on the beach.
He painted the garden to catch the sun's rays beautifully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sunbathe - to sit or lie in the sun, especially to tan the skin
