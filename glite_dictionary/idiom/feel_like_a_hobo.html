to feel like a hobo

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə ˈhoʊboʊ / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈhəʊbəʊ / (BrE)
to feel as if one is homeless or extremely poor
feel as if homeless or impoverished
informal

Examples

After living out of her suitcase for weeks, she felt like a hobo.
Wearing old clothes and eating soup from a can, he felt like a hobo.
Sometimes, when money is tight, I feel like a hobo.

Synset Examples

She felt like a hobo after losing her job.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to feel like a million bucks - to feel very good or wealthy
