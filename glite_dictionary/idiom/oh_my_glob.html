oh my glob

1. INTJ

/ oʊ maɪ ɡlɑb / (AmE), / əʊ maɪ ɡlɒb / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express surprise, excitement, or astonishment, often as a playful or euphemistic alternative to 'oh my God'
exclamation of surprise or excitement
informal slang

Examples

Oh my glob, I can't believe how much snow fell overnight!
Oh my glob, this is the best cake I've ever tasted!
Oh my glob, did you see what just happened on the field?

Synset Examples

Oh my glob, look at that cute puppy!
Oh my glob, that movie was so funny!
Oh my glob, you won the lottery!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oh my God - exclamation of surprise or amazement

Etymology

Popularized by the animated television series 'Adventure Time' as a playful variant of 'oh my God'.
