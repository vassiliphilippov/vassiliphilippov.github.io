to bitch and moan

1. VERB

/ bɪtʃ ənd moʊn / (AmE), / bɪtʃ ənd məʊn / (BrE)
to complain persistently and in a way that is perceived as annoying or tiresome.
to complain persistently
informal

Examples

Every time something goes wrong, he starts to bitch and moan instead of fixing it.
Stop bitching and moaning about your workload and try to be productive.
She tends to bitch and moan when things don't go her way.

Synset Examples

Stop bitching and moaning; just solve the problem.
She always bitches and moans during meetings.
He loves to bitch and moan about the weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to complain - to express dissatisfaction or annoyance about something
