to cross one's path

1. VERB

/ krɔs wʌnz pæθ / (AmE), / krɒs wʌnz pɑːθ / (BrE)
to meet or encounter someone unexpectedly, often by chance
to meet someone unexpectedly

Examples

I never thought I would cross her path again after graduation.
You never know who might cross your path when you travel to new places.
She crossed his path at the conference and they reconnected after many years.

Synset Examples

I never expected to cross her path in this city.
He was surprised to cross her path at the mall.
Sometimes, you cross paths with interesting people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to run into - to meet someone unexpectedly, especially by chance
