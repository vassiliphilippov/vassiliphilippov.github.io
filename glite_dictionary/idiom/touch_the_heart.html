to touch the heart

1. VERB

/ tʌtʃ ðə hɑrt / (AmE), / tʌtʃ ðə hɑːt / (BrE)
to cause strong feelings of emotion or sympathy
evoke strong emotion or sympathy

Examples

Her generosity and kindness truly touched the heart of everyone in the room.
The film's ending touched the heart and left many viewers in tears.
His story about overcoming adversity touched the heart of the audience.

Synset Examples

The sonneteer wrote verses that touched the heart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: move - to cause deep emotion
  • Synonym: affect - to influence or touch emotionally
