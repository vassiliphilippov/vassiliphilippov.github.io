the world revolves around someone

1. X

/ ðə wɝːld ɹɪˈvɑːlvz əˈɹaʊnd ˈsʌmwʌn / (AmE), / ðə wɜːld rɪˈvɒlvz əˈraʊnd ˈsʌmwʌn / (BrE)
used to describe someone who acts as if they are the most important person and everything should center on them
someone who thinks they are the most important

Examples

She always talks as if the world revolves around her and no one else matters.
Some celebrities behave like the world revolves around them, ignoring everyone's needs but their own.
You need to learn that the world revolves around no one, not even you.

Synset Examples

She behaves as if she thinks the world revolves around her.
He acts like he thinks the world revolves around him.
Stop acting like you think the world revolves around you.
