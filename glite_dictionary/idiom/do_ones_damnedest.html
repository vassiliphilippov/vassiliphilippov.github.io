to do one's damnedest

1. VERB

/ du wʌnz ˈdæmdɪst / (AmE), / duː wʌnz ˈdæmdɪst / (BrE)
to try as hard as possible; to make an all-out effort
try as hard as possible

Examples

No matter how much I do my damnedest to help, it never seems to be enough.
They did their damnedest to win the game despite the odds.
She always does her damnedest to support her friends.

Synset Examples

She did her damnedest to finish the project on time.
He did his damnedest to impress the judges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to do one's best - to make the greatest possible effort; synonymic phrase

Etymology

From 'damned' (emphatic intensifier) + '-est' (superlative suffix).
