shoulder to cry on

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊldər tə kraɪ ɑn / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə tə kraɪ ɒn / (BrE)
a person who provides comfort or sympathy to someone in distress
a person who offers emotional support

Examples

After her loss, she needed a shoulder to cry on and turned to her best friend.
He always tries to be a shoulder to cry on for his colleagues when they're having a bad day.
Sometimes we all need a shoulder to cry on when things get overwhelming.

Synset Examples

She offered her shoulder to cry on during tough times.
After the breakup, I needed a shoulder to cry on.
Friends are always there to be a shoulder to cry on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comforter - someone who consoles others
  • Synonym: support system - a network of people who provide emotional help
