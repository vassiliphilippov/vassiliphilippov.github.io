to keep an eye out

1. VERB

/ kip ən aɪ aʊt / (AmE), / kiːp ən aɪ aʊt / (BrE)
to remain alert or watchful for something or someone
be alert or watchful

Examples

Please keep an eye out for any suspicious activity near the office.
She promised to keep an eye out for the missing cat while walking home.
While driving, always keep an eye out for pedestrians crossing the street.

Synset Examples

Make sure to keep an eye out for scams.
Keep an eye out for the delivery truck.
I’ll keep an eye out for your email.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to watch out - be careful or on alert for danger or trouble
