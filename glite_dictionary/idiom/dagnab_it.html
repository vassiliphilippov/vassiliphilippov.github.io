dagnab it

1. INTJ

/ ˈdæɡ.næb ɪt / (AmE), / ˈdæɡ.næb ɪt / (BrE)
An exclamation used to express frustration, annoyance, or irritation.
expression of frustration or annoyance
informal

Examples

Dagnab it, I dropped my phone in the parking lot!
Dagnab it, why does this always happen to me?
He muttered 'dagnab it' when the car refused to start.

Synset Examples

Dagnab it, I lost my keys again!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: damn it - exclamation of annoyance or anger

Etymology

Alteration of "damn it" to avoid swearing, possibly influenced by similar minced oaths.
