like hot cakes

1. ADV

/ laɪk hɑt keɪks / (AmE), / laɪk hɒt keɪks / (BrE)
very quickly or in large quantities, especially when sold or distributed
very quickly or in large quantities

Examples

The new smartphones are selling like hot cakes this holiday season.
After the advertisement aired, her homemade cookies started moving like hot cakes.
Tickets to the concert went like hot cakes within hours of going on sale.
