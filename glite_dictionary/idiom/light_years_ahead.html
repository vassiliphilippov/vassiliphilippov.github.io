light years ahead

1. ADJ

/ laɪt jɪɹz əˈhɛd / (AmE), / laɪt jɪəz əˈhɛd / (BrE)
Much more advanced than others; significantly superior in development, progress, or ability.
far more advanced than others

Examples

This software is light years ahead of the previous version and offers many new features.
The medical research team is light years ahead in their field, making groundbreaking discoveries.
Our company's sustainability efforts are light years ahead of industry standards.

Synset Examples

The new technology is light years ahead of its competitors.
Her understanding of the subject is light years ahead of her classmates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ahead of the curve - performing better or being more advanced than others
  • Antonym: behind the times - not keeping up with current developments; old-fashioned

Etymology

From the astronomical distance unit 'light year', used metaphorically to mean a vast distance or difference.
