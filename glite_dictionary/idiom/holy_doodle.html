holy doodle

1. INTJ

/ ˈhoʊli ˈduːdəl / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli ˈduːdəl / (BrE)
Used as an exclamation to express surprise or amazement.
expression of surprise or amazement
informal

Examples

Holy doodle, that was a close call on the highway!
When she saw the price of the dress, she exclaimed, "Holy doodle!"
Holy doodle, I can’t believe we actually won the competition!

Synset Examples

Holy doodle, that cake looks delicious!
