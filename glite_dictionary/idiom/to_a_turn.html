to a turn

1. ADV

/ tə ə tɝn / (AmE), / tə ə tɜːn / (BrE)
exactly or perfectly; done to the precise or ideal degree needed
to perfection; exactly right

Examples

She baked the cake to a turn, and it came out moist and light.
His performance matched the director’s expectations to a turn.
The steak was cooked to a turn, juicy and full of flavor.

Synset Examples

The recipe was followed to a turn, resulting in a delicious dish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfectly - in a perfect or flawless manner
  • Synonym: to a tee - exactly right or accurate
