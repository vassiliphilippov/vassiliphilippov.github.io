to set the tone

1. VERB

/ sɛt ðə toʊn / (AmE), / sɛt ðə təʊn / (BrE)
to establish a particular mood or atmosphere, especially at the beginning of an event, activity, or process
establish a mood or atmosphere

Examples

The manager's positive speech at the start of the meeting set the tone for the entire team.
The first scene of the film sets the tone for its dark and mysterious story.
By arriving early and being well prepared, she set the tone for the rest of the group.

Synset Examples

The opening batsman set the tone with a quick fifty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to set the mood - to create a particular atmosphere or feeling
