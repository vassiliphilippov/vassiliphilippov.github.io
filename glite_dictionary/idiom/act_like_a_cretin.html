to act like a cretin

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈkret.ən / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈkriː.tɪn / (BrE)
to behave in a very foolish or stupid way
behave in a foolish or stupid way
offensive

Examples

If you act like a cretin in public, people won't take you seriously.
She realized she had acted like a cretin and quickly apologized for her behavior.
Stop acting like a cretin—listen to what I'm saying for once.

Synset Examples

He acted like a cretin during the meeting, embarrassing everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: act like an idiot - to behave very foolishly
  • Antonym: act sensibly - to behave in a wise or reasonable manner
