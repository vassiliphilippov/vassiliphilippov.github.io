ecky thump

1. INTJ

/ ˈɛki θʌmp / (AmE), / ˈɛki θʌmp / (BrE)
(Northern English, informal) An exclamation expressing surprise or disbelief.
exclamation of surprise or disbelief (Northern English)
informal british

Examples

Ecky thump! That news was the last thing I expected.
When he saw the bill, he cried out 'ecky thump!' in shock.
Ecky thump, it’s freezing out here today!

Synset Examples

Ecky thump! That was a close call!
When she won the lottery, she shouted 'ecky thump!' in disbelief.
He exclaimed 'ecky thump!' when he saw the surprise.

Etymology

Northern English dialect; popularized in British comedy, possibly a playful variation of "heck, thump!"
