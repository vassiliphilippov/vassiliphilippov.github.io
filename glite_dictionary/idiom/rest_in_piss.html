rest in piss

1. X

/ ˈrɛst ɪn pɪs / (AmE), / ˈrɛst ɪn pɪs / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) An intentionally disrespectful and humorous variant of 'rest in peace', used to express contempt or ridicule for someone or something no longer present or functioning.
disrespectful parody of 'rest in peace'
slang vulgar

Examples

After the company went bankrupt, many former employees said it could rest in piss.
His critics online posted memes telling his failed project to rest in piss.
When her old laptop finally stopped working, she joked it could rest in piss.

Synset Examples

After that awful performance, the band could only hope to rest in piss.
He joked that his old car should rest in piss after its last breakdown.
After the defeat, the team's reputation seemed to rest in piss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rest in peace - expression wishing peaceful rest to the deceased

Etymology

Modelled on 'rest in peace', replacing 'peace' with 'piss' as a crude pun.
