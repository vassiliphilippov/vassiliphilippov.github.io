to come out of someone's ass

1. VERB

/ kʌm aʊt əv ˈsʌmwʌnz æs / (AmE), / kʌm aʊt əv ˈsʌmwʌnz ɑːs / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) To appear, emerge, or be produced without thought, preparation, or credibility.
appear without thought or preparation (vulgar idiom)
informal vulgar

Examples

That excuse just seemed to come out of his ass with no real explanation.
She makes up facts that appear to come out of her ass during meetings.
It annoys me when suggestions come out of someone's ass instead of being carefully considered.
