to tell the truth

1. X

/ tə tɛl ðə truθ / (AmE), / tə tɛl ðə truːθ / (BrE)
used to admit the truth about something, often when it may be surprising or embarrassing
to admit the true situation

Examples

To tell the truth, I completely forgot about the appointment.
I didn't like the movie much, to tell the truth.
To tell the truth, I'm not really hungry right now.

Synset Examples

To tell the truth, I forgot about our meeting.
To tell the truth, I wasn't impressed by the movie.
To tell the truth, I prefer tea over coffee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to be honest - used to introduce a frank statement
