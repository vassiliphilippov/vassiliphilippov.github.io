through with

1. ADJ

/ θɹu wɪθ / (AmE), / θɹuː wɪð / (BrE)
having finished or ended involvement with something or someone; no longer concerned with or responsible for it
finished with; no longer involved

Examples

I'm through with this job and ready for a new challenge.
After years of struggling, she's finally through with her bad habits.
He told his friends he was through with the project and wouldn't work on it anymore.

Synset Examples

he was through with school and he was through with family
done with gambling
done with drinking
