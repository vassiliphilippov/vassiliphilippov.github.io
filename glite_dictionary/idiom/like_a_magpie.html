like a magpie

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə ˈmægˌpaɪ / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈmæɡˌpaɪ / (BrE)
attracted to shiny or glittering objects, much like a magpie is believed to collect such items
attracted to shiny objects

Examples

She collects jewelry and trinkets like a magpie when she visits markets.
Ever since he was a child, he has been like a magpie—unable to resist anything that sparkles.
His desk was covered in gadgets and shiny pens; truly, he is like a magpie.

Synset Examples

She collected shiny trinkets like a magpie.

Etymology

Based on the reputation of the magpie bird for collecting shiny things.
