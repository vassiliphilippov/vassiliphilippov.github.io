to harm a hair on someone's head

1. VERB

/ hɑrm ə hɛr ɑn ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / hɑːm ə heə ɒn ˈsʌmwənz hɛd / (BrE)
to hurt or injure someone in any way, even to the slightest extent
to cause any harm to someone

Examples

Despite their heated argument, she swore she would never harm a hair on his head.
He loved his family so much that he would not let anyone harm a hair on their head.
The police assured the witnesses that no one would harm a hair on their head while under protection.

Synset Examples

No one would dare to harm a hair on her head in this neighborhood.
She promised not to harm a hair on anyone's head during the protest.
I would never harm a hair on his head; he's my best friend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hurt a hair on someone's head - to cause the slightest harm to someone
