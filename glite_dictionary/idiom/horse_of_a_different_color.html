horse of a different color

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs əv ə ˈdɪfɚənt ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / hɔːs ɒv ə ˈdɪfərənt ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
a situation or issue that is very different from what has been discussed or expected
an entirely different issue or matter

Examples

We were talking about budget cuts, but hiring new staff is a horse of a different color.
If you need help moving on Saturday, that's a horse of a different color.
His apology is a horse of a different color after all he's done.

Synset Examples

His opinion on the matter is a horse of a different color.
Her talent for singing is a horse of a different color.
That's a horse of a different color; I didn't expect that.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: another matter - something different or unrelated to the current topic
