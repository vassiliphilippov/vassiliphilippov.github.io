sure enough

1. ADV

/ ʃʊr ɪˈnʌf / (AmE), / ʃɔː ɪˈnʌf / (BrE)
used to say that something happens or is true exactly as expected or predicted; indeed, as anticipated.
as expected; indeed

Examples

I looked out the window, and sure enough, it was starting to snow just as the forecast said.
We thought the restaurant might be closed on Sundays, and sure enough, it was.
If you leave food outside, sure enough, the animals will find it.

Synset Examples

the results are surely encouraging
she certainly is a hard worker
it's going to be a good day for sure
they are coming, for certain
they thought he had been killed sure enough
he'll win sure as shooting
they sure smell good
sure he'll come

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certainly - used to emphasize that something is definitely true or will happen
  • Synonym: indeed - used to emphasize a statement or response confirming something already suggested
