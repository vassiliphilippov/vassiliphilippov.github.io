written all over someone's face

1. X

/ ˈrɪtn ɔl ˈoʊvɚ ˈsʌmwʌnz feɪs / (AmE), / ˈrɪtn ɔːl ˈəʊvə ˈsʌmwənz feɪs / (BrE)
(of a feeling or emotion) very obvious and easily seen by looking at a person's face.
emotion easily seen on the face

Examples

Her excitement was written all over her face when she opened the gift.
Guilt was written all over his face as he tried to explain what happened.
The shock was written all over her face when she heard the news.

Synset Examples

The fear was written all over her face.
The truth was written all over her face.
Her disappointment was written all over her face.
