no longer with us

1. X

/ noʊ ˈlɔŋɡər wɪð ʌs / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈlɒŋɡə wɪð ʌs / (BrE)
used as a polite or indirect way to say that someone has died or that something no longer exists
euphemism for deceased or non-existent

Examples

After a long illness, her grandfather is no longer with us.
The historic theater is no longer with us after being demolished.
He spoke fondly of colleagues who are no longer with us.

Synset Examples

His grandmother is no longer with us, sadly.
The old cafe is no longer with us; it’s closed.
He mentioned that his childhood friend is no longer with us.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to passed away - died (euphemism)
  • Synonym: deceased - no longer living; dead
