tower of strength

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaʊɚ əv ˈstrɛŋθ / (AmE), / ˈtaʊə əv ˈstrɛŋθ / (BrE)
a person who gives strong support or comfort in difficult times; someone you can rely on for help and reassurance
reliable source of strength/support

Examples

During her illness, her husband was a real tower of strength to her.
We all turned to Michael in times of trouble because he was our tower of strength.
You've been my tower of strength throughout this challenging year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rock - a person who provides steady support

Etymology

Figurative use of 'tower' as a symbol of protection or support, dating to at least the 16th century.
