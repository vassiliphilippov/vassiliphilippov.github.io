in the toilet

1. ADP

/ ɪn ðə ˈtɔɪlət / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈtɔɪlɪt / (BrE)
in a very bad or unsuccessful state; ruined or failing
in a very bad state
informal

Examples

After the scandal, his career was in the toilet and he couldn't find work.
Profits are in the toilet this quarter due to unexpected expenses.
The team's chance of winning the championship is in the toilet after their recent losses.

Synset Examples

His reputation is in the toilet after that scandal.
The budget is in the toilet after unexpected expenses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: down the drain - wasted or lost; having failed or been ruined
  • Synonym: in a mess - in a confused, untidy, or problematic state
  • Antonym: in good shape - in a good or healthy condition
