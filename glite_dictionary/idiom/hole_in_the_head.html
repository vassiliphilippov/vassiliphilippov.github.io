hole in the head

1. NOUN

/ hoʊl ɪn ðə hɛd / (AmE), / həʊl ɪn ðə hɛd / (BrE)
a foolish, senseless, or obviously false excuse or reason
foolish or nonsensical excuse

Examples

His story about missing the meeting was just a hole in the head and nobody believed him.
She told me another hole in the head to explain why she hadn't finished her work.
You must think I was born yesterday if you expect me to accept that hole in the head as a reason.

Synset Examples

His excuse for being late was a hole in the head.
