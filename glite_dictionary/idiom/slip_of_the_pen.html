slip of the pen

1. NOUN

/ slɪp əv ðə pɛn / (AmE), / slɪp əv ðə pɛn / (BrE)
a minor mistake made in writing, often accidentally
minor writing error

Examples

It was just a slip of the pen in the report.
She corrected the slip of the pen before submitting the essay.
A simple slip of the pen led to a misunderstanding in the contract.

Synset Examples

It was just a slip of the pen in the report.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slip of the tongue - an error made in speaking
