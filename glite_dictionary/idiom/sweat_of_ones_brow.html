sweat of one's brow

1. NOUN

/ swɛt əv wʌnz braʊ / (AmE), / swɛt əv wʌnz braʊ / (BrE)
hard work or effort, especially as a means of earning a living or accomplishing something
hard work or effort to achieve something

Examples

She managed to open her own restaurant by the sweat of her brow.
The house was paid for through the sweat of his brow and years of saving.
They built their small business from scratch by the sweat of their brow.

Synset Examples

She believes in living off the sweat of her brow.
He earned every penny through the sweat of his brow.
The farm was built through the sweat of their brows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hard work - great effort or exertion to achieve something
  • Synonym: toil - long, hard, and continuous work

Etymology

Alludes to Biblical references to earning a living by hard labor, notably in Genesis ('By the sweat of your brow you shall eat bread').
