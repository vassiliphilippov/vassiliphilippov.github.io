bite my ass

1. X

/ baɪt maɪ æs / (AmE), / baɪt maɪ as / (BrE)
(vulgar, slang) Used as a dismissive or defiant retort to show contempt, disbelief, or disregard for someone's words or actions.
defiant vulgar dismissive retort
vulgar slang

Examples

If you don't like my decision, you can bite my ass.
He told the critic to bite my ass after hearing the negative review.
When they doubted her story, she snapped, 'Bite my ass!'

Synset Examples

After that comment, she said, 'Bite my ass!'
If you think that, you can bite my ass!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kiss my ass - a similarly vulgar expression of defiance or dismissal
  • Synonym: screw you - an informal, rude way to express anger or rejection
