to poop oneself

1. VERB

/ pup wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / puːp wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to involuntarily defecate in one's clothing, especially as a result of extreme fear, shock, or excitement
lose bowel control from fear or excitement
informal

She thought she might poop oneself during the terrifying haunted house tour.
If I ever see a bear up close, I might just poop oneself.
