what the Devil

1. INTJ

/ wʌt ðə ˈdɛvəl / (AmE), / wɒt ðə ˈdɛvəl / (BrE)
An exclamation used to express surprise, confusion, or annoyance.
expression of surprise or confusion

Examples

What the Devil are you doing in my kitchen at this hour?
She stared at the letter, wondering what the Devil it could mean.
What the Devil happened to all the coffee mugs in the office?

Synset Examples

What the Devil are you doing in my office?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: what the hell - an exclamation for surprise or irritation
  • Synonym: what on earth - an expression of surprise or bewilderment

Etymology

Uses 'Devil' as a mild oath; phrase dates to at least the 17th century.
