to have a good time

1. VERB

/ hæv ə ɡʊd taɪm / (AmE), / hæv ə ɡʊd taɪm / (BrE)
to enjoy oneself greatly; to experience pleasure or amusement during an activity or event
enjoy oneself; experience pleasure

Examples

I hope you have a good time at your birthday party tonight!
We always have a good time when we visit Grandma’s house.
She was clearly having a good time at the concert, dancing and singing along.

Synset Examples

We had a ball at the party and didn't come home until 2 AM

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enjoy oneself - to take delight or pleasure in an activity or situation
