to come in handy

1. VERB

/ kʌm ɪn ˈhændi / (AmE), / kʌm ɪn ˈhandi / (BrE)
to be useful or convenient in a particular situation
be useful or convenient

Examples

A pocketknife can come in handy when you are hiking in the woods.
Her years of language study came in handy during her trip to Spain.
You never know when a spare battery might come in handy.

Synset Examples

Your advice really came in handy yesterday.
This tool will come in handy during repairs.
A flashlight always comes in handy for camping.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be useful - to have utility; to be of use
  • Antonym: be useless - to have no utility or purpose; to be of no use
