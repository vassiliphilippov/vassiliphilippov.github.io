smart money

1. NOUN

/ smɑrt ˈmʌni / (AmE), / smɑːt ˈmʌni / (BrE)
people considered to have expert knowledge or inside information, especially in financial or betting contexts
people with expert or insider knowledge

Examples

According to the smart money, the team's new coach will turn things around quickly.
The smart money is betting on a rise in technology stocks this quarter.
When the smart money started to leave the market, others soon followed.

Synset Examples

the smart money said Truman would lose the election

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insider - person with special access to information
