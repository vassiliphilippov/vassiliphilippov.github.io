to capture the moment

1. VERB

/ ˈkæp.tʃɚ ðə ˈmoʊ.mənt / (AmE), / ˈkæp.tʃə ðə ˈməʊ.mənt / (BrE)
to take a photograph or make a record that perfectly preserves a particular time, feeling, or event
take a photo that preserves a feeling or time

Examples

She always brings her camera so she can capture the moment during family gatherings.
The photographer hoped to capture the moment when the bride saw the groom for the first time.
With a quick snap, he managed to capture the moment as the sun set behind the mountains.

Synset Examples

They managed to capture the moment perfectly in the photograph.
She captured the moment with her photographic skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to immortalize - to make something last forever, especially in memory or records
