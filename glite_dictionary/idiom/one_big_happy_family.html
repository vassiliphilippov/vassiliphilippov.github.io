one big happy family

1. NOUN

/ wʌn bɪɡ ˈhæpi ˈfæməli / (AmE), / wʌn bɪɡ ˈhæpi ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a group of people who have close, friendly relationships with each other, similar to those in a loving family
close, harmonious group

Examples

After only a few weeks, the members of the club became one big happy family.
Despite their differences, the roommates managed to live together like one big happy family.
The company's annual picnic made everyone feel part of one big happy family.

Synset Examples
