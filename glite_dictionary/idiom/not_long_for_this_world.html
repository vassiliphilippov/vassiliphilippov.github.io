not long for this world

1. ADJ

/ nɑt lɔŋ fər ðɪs wɝld / (AmE), / nɒt lɒŋ fə ðɪs wɜːld / (BrE)
unlikely to live or exist for much longer; near the end of life or existence.
unlikely to last much longer

Examples

After the latest breakdown, it was clear his old bicycle was not long for this world.
The patient’s worsening condition indicated that she was not long for this world.
That abandoned amusement park is not long for this world and will probably be demolished soon.

Synset Examples

That building is beautiful but not long for this world.
He realized his car was not long for this world.
The old tree is not long for this world, unfortunately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doomed - certain to fail or be destroyed
  • Antonym: immortal - living or lasting forever; not subject to death or destruction
