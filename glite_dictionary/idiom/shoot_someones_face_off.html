to shoot someone's face off

1. VERB

/ ʃut ˈsʌmwʌnz feɪs ɔf / (AmE), / ʃuːt ˈsʌmwʌnz feɪs ɒf / (BrE)
to have a powerful or startling impact on someone, often in a humorous or exaggerated way
make a strong or startling impression
informal

Examples

The band's new single will shoot someone's face off with its sheer energy.
That spicy curry will shoot your face off if you're not careful!
The opening scene was so loud, it nearly shot my face off in the theater.
