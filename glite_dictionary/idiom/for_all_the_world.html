for all the world

1. ADV

/ fər ɔl ðə wɝld / (AmE), / fə ɔːl ðə wɜːld / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something would not be done under any circumstances, no matter what is offered or happens.
under no circumstances; not for anything

Examples

I wouldn't trade my childhood memories for all the world.
He wouldn't reveal her secret for all the world.
She says she would not change her decision for all the world.

Synset Examples

she wouldn't give up her pets for love or money

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for love or money - in any circumstances; at any price (emphatic negative idiom)
