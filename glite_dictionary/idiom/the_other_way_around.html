the other way around

1. ADV

/ ði ˈʌðɚ weɪ əˈɹaʊnd / (AmE), / ði ˈʌðə weɪ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
with the order, direction, or arrangement reversed; the opposite way
with the order reversed

Examples
