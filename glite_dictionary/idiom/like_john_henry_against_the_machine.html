like John Henry against the machine

1. ADV

/ laɪk dʒɑn ˈhɛnɹi əˈɡɛnst ðə məˈʃin / (AmE), / laɪk dʒɒn ˈhɛnri əˈɡɛnst ðə məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
With great determination and effort, especially when facing overwhelming odds or competing against something much stronger or more powerful.
with great determination and effort

Examples

She fought through her illness like John Henry against the machine, refusing to give up.
The small team competed like John Henry against the machine in the national finals.
He finished the marathon like John Henry against the machine, pushing himself past every limit.

Synset Examples

He tackled the task like John Henry against the machine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: work like a Trojan - work very hard with great determination
  • Synonym: give it your all - use all your determination and strength to do something

Etymology

Alludes to John Henry, a legendary African-American folk hero said to have outperformed a steam-powered drill with his own strength.
