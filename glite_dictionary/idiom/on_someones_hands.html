on someone's hands

1. ADP

/ ɑn ˈsʌmwʌnz hændz / (AmE), / ɒn ˈsʌmwʌnz hændz / (BrE)
if something is on someone's hands, they are responsible or obliged to deal with it
in someone's responsibility or care

Examples

With three sick children on her hands, she barely had time to rest.
The company’s future is now on his hands after the sudden resignation of the CEO.
There’s a lot of work on your hands before the event tomorrow.

Synset Examples

The project is now on my hands to complete.
The decision is on your hands now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in charge of - having responsibility for something or someone
  • Antonym: off one's hands - no longer under one's responsibility or care
