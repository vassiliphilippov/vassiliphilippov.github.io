to put in a hundred and ten percent

1. VERB

/ pʊt ɪn ə ˈhʌndrəd ənd tɛn pərˈsɛnt / (AmE), / pʊt ɪn ə ˈhʌndrəd ənd tɛn pəˈsɛnt / (BrE)
to give one's maximum possible effort; to do much more than what is expected or required
give the highest possible effort

Examples

If we want to win this championship, we'll all need to put in a hundred and ten percent every day.
She always puts in a hundred and ten percent on every project, no matter the challenges.
During the busy season, the whole team put in a hundred and ten percent to meet the deadline.

Synset Examples

He always puts in a hundred and ten percent at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go the extra mile - to make a special effort to achieve something
  • Synonym: to do one's best - to try as hard as one can
  • Antonym: to slack off - to reduce the amount of work one does
