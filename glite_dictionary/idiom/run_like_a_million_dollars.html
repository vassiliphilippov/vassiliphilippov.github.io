to run like a million dollars

1. VERB

/ rʌn laɪk ə mɪljən dɑlɚz / (AmE), / rʌn laɪk ə mɪljən dɒləz / (BrE)
to function extremely well or smoothly, especially after repair or adjustment
function extremely well or smoothly

Examples

After a full service, my old motorcycle now runs like a million dollars.
Since the update, the computer runs like a million dollars again.
Once the mechanic fixed the engine, our boat ran like a million dollars.

Synset Examples

The car runs like a million dollars after the tune-up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: run smoothly - to operate in a smooth and effective way
