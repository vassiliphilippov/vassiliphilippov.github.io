out of the picture

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv ðə ˈpɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / aʊt əv ðə ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
no longer involved in or relevant to a situation; not informed or participating
no longer involved; irrelevant

Examples

Ever since the promotion, Mark has been out of the picture when it comes to team decisions.
After her move to another city, she became out of the picture socially.
I'm not surprised I wasn't invited—they clearly want me out of the picture.

Synset Examples

Since the incident, he's been out of the picture.
After the merger, he was out of the picture entirely.
She felt out of the picture during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in the picture - involved, aware, or informed about a situation
