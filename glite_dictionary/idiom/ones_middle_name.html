one's middle name

1. NOUN

/ wʌnz ˈmɪd.əl neɪm / (AmE), / wʌnz ˈmɪd.əl neɪm / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is a prominent or defining characteristic of a person
indicates a defining characteristic

Examples

For Jessica, efficiency is her middle name, and she always finishes work quickly.
Adventure must be his middle name because he's always traveling to new places.
When it comes to honesty, you could say it's my middle name.

Synset Examples

For him, danger is one's middle name, always seeking thrills.
