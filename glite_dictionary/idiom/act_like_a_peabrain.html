to act like a peabrain

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈpiːˌbreɪn / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈpiːˌbreɪn / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or senseless manner
behave foolishly or senselessly
informal slang

Examples

If you keep acting like a peabrain, you'll never finish your work on time.
She realized she was acting like a peabrain when she forgot her keys for the third time.
Please stop acting like a peabrain and help us solve the problem.

Synset Examples

Stop acting like a peabrain and focus on the task.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: act foolishly - to behave in a silly or thoughtless way
  • Antonym: act sensibly - to behave in a rational or thoughtful way

Etymology

Formed from 'act like' and the slang insult 'peabrain', referring to someone with a small brain (pea-sized), hence foolish.
