to go to great lengths

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ tə ɡreɪt leŋkθs / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə ɡreɪt leŋkθs / (BrE)
to make a great or extreme effort to achieve something
make a great effort

Examples

She was willing to go to great lengths to make her project succeed.
They went to great lengths to keep the meeting secret from the public.
He always goes to great lengths to make his guests feel comfortable.

Synset Examples

They will go to great lengths to protect their brand.
She goes to great lengths to support her family.
He goes to great lengths to ensure quality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make an effort - to try hard to do something
  • Synonym: to go out of one's way - to take special trouble to do something
