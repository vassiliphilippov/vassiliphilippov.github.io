whale of an adventure

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪl əv ən ədˈvɛntʃər / (AmE), / ˈweɪl əv ən ədˈvɛntʃə / (BrE)
a very exciting or significant adventure; an especially notable or memorable experience
very exciting or significant adventure
informal

Examples

Last summer, we went hiking in the Andes and had a whale of an adventure.
The children thought their journey through the old castle was a whale of an adventure.
With unexpected twists and breathtaking views, their road trip turned into a whale of an adventure.

Synset Examples

That fishing trip was a whale of an adventure!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whopper - something unusually large or significant
  • Degree Lower: adventure - an unusual, exciting, and possibly dangerous experience

Etymology

Uses whale, meaning 'something exceptionally large', in a figurative sense to emphasize greatness.
