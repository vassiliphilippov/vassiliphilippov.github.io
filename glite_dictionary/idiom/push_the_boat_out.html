to push the boat out

1. VERB

/ pʊʃ ðə boʊt aʊt / (AmE), / pʊʃ ðə bəʊt aʊt / (BrE)
to spend a lot of money, especially for a special celebration or occasion
spend lavishly for a special occasion
british
