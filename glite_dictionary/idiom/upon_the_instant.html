upon the instant

1. ADV

/ əˈpɑn ði ˈɪnstənt / (AmE), / əˈpɒn ði ˈɪnstənt / (BrE)
immediately after something happens; without any delay
immediately; without delay

Examples

The manager offered her the job upon the instant she finished the interview.
He apologized upon the instant he realized his mistake.
The troops retreated upon the instant the order was given.

Synset Examples

The proposal was accepted upon the instant it was presented.
He responded to the question upon the instant with confidence.
She made her decision upon the instant without hesitation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immediately - without delay; at once
  • Synonym: instantly - at once; immediately
