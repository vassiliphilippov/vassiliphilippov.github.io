in kind

1. ADV

/ ɪn kaɪnd / (AmE), / ɪn kaɪnd / (BrE)
in the same manner or with something of the same type, especially as repayment or response
in the same way or form

Examples

When they helped us move, we returned the favor in kind.
Criticism was met in kind, leading to a heated argument.
The company promised to support the community in kind with goods instead of money.

Synset Examples

she pays him back in kind
