dog's breakfast

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡz ˈbrɛkfəst / (AmE), / dɒɡz ˈbrɛkfəst / (BrE)
a complete mess or something done very badly; a confused or poorly executed situation or job
a complete mess; a botched job
british informal

Examples

The new manager turned the simple project into a dog's breakfast within days.
She tried to fix the leaking tap but made a complete dog's breakfast of it.
That report was a dog's breakfast—nothing was clear or organized.

Synset Examples

they made a real dog's breakfast of that job

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shambles - a state of total disorder
  • Synonym: dog's dinner - something messy or badly executed (variant idiom)
  • Antonym: success - the opposite of a botched or messy job

2. NOUN

/ dɔɡz ˈbrɛkfɪst / (AmE), / dɒɡz ˈbrɛkfəst / (BrE)
a situation that is very messy, confused, or badly organized
messy or confused situation
british informal

Examples

The new software rollout turned into a dog's breakfast due to lack of preparation.
After the meeting, the agenda was still a complete dog's breakfast.
His attempt to fix the engine made things even more of a dog's breakfast.

Synset Examples

The project turned into a dog's breakfast after the last-minute changes.
Her explanation was such a dog's breakfast that no one understood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog's dinner - a situation that is messy or disorderly

Etymology

British idiom; from the idea of a dog's meal being a messy mixture.
