pearl of wisdom

1. NOUN

/ pɝl əv ˈwɪzdəm / (AmE), / pɜːl əv ˈwɪzdəm / (BrE)
a wise or valuable remark, insight, or piece of advice
wise or valuable advice

Examples

Every time my mentor speaks, she offers another pearl of wisdom that inspires me.
He gave me a pearl of wisdom about always double-checking my work.
The book is full of pearls of wisdom for overcoming life's challenges.

Synset Examples

Her advice was a pearl of wisdom amidst chaos.
He shared a pearl of wisdom about saving money.
Her grandmother's advice was a pearl of wisdom.
This book is filled with pearls of wisdom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gem of wisdom - an especially valuable or insightful piece of advice

Etymology

Refers metaphorically to a pearl as something precious and rare, likening valuable advice to a prized pearl.
