back of beyond

1. NOUN

/ ˌbæk əv biˈɑnd / (AmE), / ˌbæk əv bɪˈɒnd / (BrE)
an extremely remote or inaccessible place; a location far from civilization or regular contact
remote or inaccessible place

Examples

After moving to the back of beyond, he rarely saw any visitors.
Their new cottage is located in the back of beyond, miles from the nearest town.
We got lost in what felt like the back of beyond during our road trip.

Synset Examples

you'd have to go to the back of beyond to find one of those

Related Terms

  • Synonym: middle of nowhere - a place that is remote and isolated
