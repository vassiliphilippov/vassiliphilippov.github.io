woman of ill repute

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊmən əv ɪl rɪˈpjuːt / (AmE), / ˈwʊmən əv ɪl rɪˈpjuːt / (BrE)
a woman who is considered to have a morally improper or scandalous reputation, especially in relation to sexual behavior
woman considered morally improper

Examples

She was unfairly labeled a woman of ill repute because of rumors spread around the village.
The novel's main character is a woman of ill repute who struggles against social prejudice.
Although known as a woman of ill repute, she was deeply respected by those who knew her true nature.

Synset Examples

The story painted her as a woman of ill repute in the town.
Despite being a woman of ill repute, she had many admirers.
She was labeled a woman of ill repute for her scandalous behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: woman of the night - a euphemism for a prostitute; a woman engaged in sex work
  • Synonym: lady of easy virtue - an old-fashioned term for a promiscuous woman or prostitute
