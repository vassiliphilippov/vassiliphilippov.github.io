to look like a shitpot

1. VERB

/ lʊk laɪk ə ˈʃɪtpɑt / (AmE), / lʊk laɪk ə ˈʃɪtpɒt / (BrE)
to appear extremely messy or dirty; to be in a filthy or untidy state
appear very messy or dirty
informal vulgar

Examples

After the party, the living room looked like a shitpot with empty bottles everywhere.
Whenever the kids finish playing, their bedroom always looks like a shitpot.
If you don't clean up soon, your office will look like a shitpot.

Synset Examples

The kitchen looked like a shitpot after dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: look like a pigsty - to appear extremely untidy or dirty, like a pigsty
  • Synonym: messy - untidy or dirty in appearance
