to feel like an old man

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ən oʊld mæn / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ən əʊld mæn / (BrE)
to feel exhausted, weary, or physically worn out, like someone much older
to feel exhausted or worn out

Examples

After running the marathon, I felt like an old man the next morning.
Whenever he stays up too late, he complains that he feels like an old man the next day.
Carrying those heavy boxes all afternoon made her feel like an old man.

Synset Examples

He felt like an old man after a long day of work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feel tired - to be weary or in need of rest
  • Antonym: feel rejuvenated - to feel restored or youthful again
