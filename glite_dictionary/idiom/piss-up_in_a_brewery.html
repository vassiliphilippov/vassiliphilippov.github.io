piss-up in a brewery

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪs ʌp ɪn ə ˈbruːəri / (AmE), / ˈpɪs ʌp ɪn ə ˈbrʊəri / (BrE)
(chiefly British, informal) Used to emphasize that someone is very poor at organizing things, especially in the form 'couldn't organize a piss-up in a brewery', meaning someone is incompetent or disorganized.
an example of incompetence or poor organization
informal british vulgar

Examples

Even if they had all the resources, they couldn't organize a piss-up in a brewery.
It's a wonder anything goes right here; you lot couldn't run a piss-up in a brewery.
He joked that their attempt at the event was as messy as a piss-up in a brewery.

Synset Examples

With her planning skills, she couldn't organise a piss-up in a brewery.
They couldn't organise a piss-up in a brewery last week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mess - a situation that is confused or full of problems
  • Synonym: shambles - a state of total disorder
