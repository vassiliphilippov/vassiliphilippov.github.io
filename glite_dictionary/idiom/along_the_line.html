along the line

1. ADV

/ əˈlɔŋ ðə laɪn / (AmE), / əˈlɒŋ ðə laɪn / (BrE)
at some unspecified point during a process or period of time, especially as events unfold or progress.
at some point during a process or period

Examples

Along the line, we realized our goals had shifted.
Somewhere along the line, I lost interest in the project.
You'll probably need to revise your plans along the line.

Synset Examples

Somewhere along the line, things began to change.
Somewhere along the line, we lost track of time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at some point - at an unspecified moment in time
