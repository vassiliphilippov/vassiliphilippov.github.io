to give it a try

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ɪt ə traɪ / (AmE), / ɡɪv ɪt ə traɪ / (BrE)
to attempt to do something, especially for the first time, to see if you like it or can do it
to attempt something, usually once

Examples

You should give it a try before deciding it's not for you.
Even though she was nervous about public speaking, she decided to give it a try.
I've never eaten sushi before, but I'm willing to give it a try.

Synset Examples

I never sat on a horse before but I'll give it a go

Related Terms

  • Synonym: give it a go - to try something; make an attempt
  • Synonym: to have a go - to attempt something, often for the first time
  • Synonym: try - to make an attempt to do something
