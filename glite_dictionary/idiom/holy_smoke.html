holy smoke

1. INTJ

/ ˌhoʊli ˈsmoʊk / (AmE), / ˌhəʊli ˈsməʊk / (BrE)
used to express surprise, astonishment, or admiration, often in response to something unexpected or impressive
exclamation of surprise or astonishment
informal

Examples

Holy smoke, that startup just raised a million dollars in its first week!
I opened the door and, holy smoke, the room was filled with balloons!
Holy smoke, we're almost out of gas already!

Synset Examples

Holy smoke, that was a close call!
When he finished the project early, I thought, holy smoke!
Holy smoke, what a beautiful sunset!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good grief - exclamation expressing surprise, shock, or exasperation
  • Synonym: to wow - exclamation used to express astonishment or admiration
  • Synonym: what the heck - informal exclamation of surprise or puzzlement
