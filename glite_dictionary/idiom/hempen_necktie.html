hempen necktie

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛmpən ˈnɛktaɪ / (AmE), / ˈhɛmpən ˈnɛktaɪ / (BrE)
a rope, especially used in execution by hanging; a hangman's noose
hangman's noose (execution rope)

Examples

The outlaw faced the hempen necktie after his conviction for murder.
Many feared crossing the sheriff, knowing a hempen necktie awaited those who broke the law.
Stories of the Old West often mention men meeting their end by the hempen necktie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noose - a loop with a running knot, used for hanging or securing
  • Synonym: gallows - structure used for execution by hanging

Etymology

From 'hemp' (the material from which rope is traditionally made) + 'necktie' (worn around the neck), used euphemistically for a noose.
