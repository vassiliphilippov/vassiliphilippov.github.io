to sing like a jaybird

1. VERB

/ sɪŋ laɪk ə ˈdʒeɪˌbɝd / (AmE), / sɪŋ laɪk ə ˈdʒeɪˌbɜːd / (BrE)
to sing very loudly, joyfully, or uninhibitedly
sing loudly or joyfully

Examples

After receiving the good news, she sang like a jaybird all evening.
The children sang like a jaybird during the school play, filling the auditorium with music.
He always sings like a jaybird when his favorite song comes on the radio.

Synset Examples

She sang like a jaybird, filling the room with joy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sing one's heart out - to sing with great passion or enthusiasm
