like hell

1. ADV

/ laɪk hɛl / (AmE), / laɪk hɛl / (BrE)
(informal) With great intensity, speed, effort, or emphasis.
with great intensity or effort (informal)
informal

Examples

He ran like hell to catch the last train.
This cut on my hand hurts like hell.
We worked like hell to finish the project on time.
