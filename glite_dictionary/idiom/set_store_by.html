to set store by

1. VERB

/ sɛt stɔr baɪ / (AmE), / sɛt stɔː baɪ / (BrE)
to value or regard something as important or significant
consider important; value highly

Examples

He doesn't set store by expensive clothing and prefers comfort over luxury.
She has always set store by honesty in her relationships.
Many companies set store by employee feedback during decision-making.

Synset Examples

He doesn’t set store by material possessions.
Many people set store by their traditions and values.
She sets store by her close friendships above all else.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: value - to consider something to be important or beneficial
