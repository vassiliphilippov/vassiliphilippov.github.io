to hit with the clue stick

1. VERB

/ hɪt wɪð ðə klu stɪk / (AmE), / hɪt wɪð ðə kluː stɪk / (BrE)
to provide someone with obvious or necessary information, often after they have failed to understand something
provide obvious information to someone
informal slang

Examples

After several failed attempts, I finally had to hit him with the clue stick and explain the solution.
Sometimes you just have to hit your coworker with the clue stick to get the point across.
She didn't understand the instructions until someone hit her with the clue stick.

Synset Examples

He finally hit her with the clue stick after her mistakes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to spell out - explain something very clearly
  • Synonym: to clue in - to inform someone about something they didn't know
