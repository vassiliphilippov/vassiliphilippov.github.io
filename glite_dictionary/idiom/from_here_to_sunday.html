from here to Sunday

1. ADV

/ frəm hɪr tu ˈsʌn.deɪ / (AmE), / frəm hɪə tu ˈsʌn.deɪ / (BrE)
for a long time or to a great extent; thoroughly or excessively
for a long time; thoroughly

Examples

You can argue from here to Sunday, but it won't change my mind.
The shop was packed from here to Sunday during the holiday sale.
She cleaned the house from here to Sunday before the guests arrived.

Synset Examples

The instructions are clear from here to Sunday.
