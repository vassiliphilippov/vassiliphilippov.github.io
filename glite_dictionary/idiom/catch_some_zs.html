to catch some Z's

1. VERB

/ kætʃ səm ziːz / (AmE), / kætʃ səm ziːz / (BrE)
to sleep; to get some rest by sleeping
to go to sleep
informal

Examples

I have an exam tomorrow, so I need to catch some Z's tonight.
After the long flight, he just wanted to catch some Z's at the hotel.
She likes to catch some Z's on the sofa after a busy day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleep - to be in the state of rest with eyes closed and consciousness suspended

Etymology

From the cartoon convention of representing sleep with the letter 'Z', referring to the snoring sound.
