to beat the shit out of

1. VERB

/ bit ðə ʃɪt aʊt ʌv / (AmE), / biːt ðə ʃɪt aʊt ɒv / (BrE)
to attack someone physically with extreme violence; to defeat someone completely in a fight or competition (very informal, vulgar)
attack or defeat someone violently (vulgar)
informal vulgar

Examples

If you mess with him again, he'll beat the shit out of you.
After losing his temper, he tried to beat the shit out of his rival.
They threatened to beat the shit out of anyone who talked to the police.

Synset Examples

He was furious and wanted to beat the shit out of the guy.
During the fight, he really beat the shit out of his opponent.
She warned him not to beat the shit out of her little brother.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to beat up - to attack someone and injure them
