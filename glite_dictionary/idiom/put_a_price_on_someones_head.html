to put a price on someone's head

1. VERB

/ pʊt ə praɪs ɑn ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / pʊt ə praɪs ɒn ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (BrE)
to offer a reward for the capture or killing of a particular person
offer reward for capture or death

Examples

After the notorious robbery, the authorities put a price on his head to ensure his capture.
The gang leader put a price on his head, making him a target for every bounty hunter in the city.
She knew the king had put a price on her head and fled the country to stay alive.

Synset Examples

He put a price on his enemy's head.
