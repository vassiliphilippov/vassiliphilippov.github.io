second childhood

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd ˈtʃaɪldhʊd / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ˈtʃaɪldhʊd / (BrE)
a state of mental decline or childishness occurring in old age, marked by a return to childlike behavior or thought
state of childishness in old age

Examples

After his wife passed away, he seemed to enter a second childhood, often acting forgetful and playful like a child.
Some people mistakenly interpret signs of dementia as simply a second childhood.
His family noticed that his second childhood brought both challenges and moments of unexpected joy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: senility - mental and physical weakness associated with old age
