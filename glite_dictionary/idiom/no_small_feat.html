no small feat

1. NOUN

/ noʊ smɔl fit / (AmE), / nəʊ smɔːl fiːt / (BrE)
a significant or impressive achievement, especially one that is difficult to accomplish
significant or impressive achievement

Examples

Climbing Mount Everest is no small feat, requiring months of training and preparation.
Launching a successful startup in this market is no small feat.
Completing the marathon in under three hours was no small feat for her.

Synset Examples

Winning the lottery is no small feat; that ain't hay.
Their achievements were due in no small part to innovation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accomplishment - something successfully achieved or completed
  • Antonym: trivial - of little value or importance; opposite of significant
