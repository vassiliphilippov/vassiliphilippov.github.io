day in and day out

1. ADV

/ ˌdeɪ ɪn ənd ˌdeɪ aʊt / (AmE), / ˌdeɪ ɪn ənd ˌdeɪ aʊt / (BrE)
continuously or repeatedly over a long period, especially in a monotonous or routine manner
continuously; all the time

Examples

He went to the same job day in and day out for twenty years without complaint.
She practices the piano day in and day out to prepare for her concert.
It can be exhausting to do the same chores day in and day out without any change.

Synset Examples

he plays chess day in and day out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: constantly - continuously over a period of time
  • Synonym: continuously - without interruption
