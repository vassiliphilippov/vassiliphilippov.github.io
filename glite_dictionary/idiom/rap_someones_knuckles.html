to rap someone’s knuckles

1. VERB

/ ræp ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈnʌkəlz / (AmE), / ræp ˈsʌmwənz ˈnʌkəlz / (BrE)
to criticize or reprimand someone for a mistake or wrongdoing, often mildly or formally
mildly scold or reprimand

Examples

The teacher rapped her knuckles for forgetting to complete the assignment.
After the error was discovered, the boss rapped his knuckles during the staff meeting.
The government was rapped on the knuckles by the watchdog group for failing to act sooner.

Synset Examples

The manager rapped her knuckles for missing the deadline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reprimand - to express sharp disapproval or criticism of someone
  • Synonym: scold - to angrily criticize someone for a mistake or bad behavior

Etymology

From the literal act of tapping a person's knuckles as a mild punishment, used figuratively since the 19th century.
