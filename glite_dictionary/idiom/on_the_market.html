on the market

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə ˈmɑrkɪt / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
available to be bought or sold; offered for sale to the public
available for sale

Examples

The house has been on the market for over six months without any offers.
Once the new phone is on the market, it is expected to sell quickly.
There are dozens of companies on the market competing for a larger share of customers.

Synset Examples

The latest drug on the market has shown impressive results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for sale - available for purchase
