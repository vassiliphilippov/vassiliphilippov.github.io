in fun

1. ADV

/ ɪn fʌn / (AmE), / ɪn fʌn / (BrE)
intended as a joke or without serious intent; not meant to be taken seriously.
as a joke; not seriously

Examples

I only made that comment in fun, so please don’t be offended.
The brothers often tease each other in fun and never mean any harm.
Sometimes jokes told in fun can be misunderstood by others.

Synset Examples

some of the girls did go to extremes, but it was all in fun
let's pretend we don't like Mary, just for fun

Related Terms

  • Antonym: seriously - with earnest intent or seriousness
