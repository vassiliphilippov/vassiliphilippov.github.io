honest-to-god

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑː.nɪst.təˈɡɑːd / (AmE), / ˌɒ.nɪst.təˈɡɒd / (BrE)
(informal) Used to emphasize that something is real, genuine, or true; not fake or pretended.
emphatically real or genuine
informal

Examples

The restaurant serves an honest-to-god Italian pizza just like you’d find in Rome.
I couldn't believe it when I saw an honest-to-god celebrity at the grocery store.
This is an honest-to-god miracle, not just a lucky coincidence.

Synset Examples

a real honest-to-god live cowboy
went upriver to look at a sure-enough fish wheel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genuine - truly what something is said to be; authentic
  • Synonym: authentic - of undisputed origin; genuine
  • Antonym: fake - not genuine; counterfeit
