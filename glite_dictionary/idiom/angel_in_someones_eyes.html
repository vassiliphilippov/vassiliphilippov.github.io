angel in someone's eyes

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪn.dʒəl ɪn ˈsʌm.wʌnz aɪz / (AmE), / ˈeɪn.dʒəl ɪn ˈsʌm.wənz aɪz / (BrE)
a person regarded as exceptionally good, kind, or admirable by someone
person seen as exceptionally good

Examples

Despite her mistakes, she remained an angel in his eyes, always forgiving and loving.
The teacher was an angel in my eyes for helping me through a tough year.
No matter what others said, he was an angel in her eyes because of his honesty.

Synset Examples

Her kindness makes her an angel in my eyes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angel - a person of exemplary conduct or virtue
