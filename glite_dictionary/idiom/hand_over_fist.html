hand over fist

1. ADV

/ hænd oʊvɚ fɪst / (AmE), / hænd əʊvə fɪst / (BrE)
very quickly or at a rapid pace, especially in acquiring money or success
very quickly; at a rapid pace

Examples

With the stock market booming, investors were earning money hand over fist.
