off the boil

1. ADJ

/ ɔf ðə bɔɪl / (AmE), / ɒf ðə bɔɪl / (BrE)
No longer boiling; having cooled below boiling point and thus suitable for further preparation or use.
no longer boiling; cooled below boiling point

Examples

Let the milk come off the boil before you add the chocolate powder.
The tea kettle is off the boil, so it's the perfect time to pour.
Always wait until the sauce is off the boil before stirring in the eggs.

Synset Examples

Make sure the water is off the boil before adding pasta.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: boiling - reaching the temperature at which a liquid boils
