to give the gate

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ðə ɡeɪt / (AmE), / ɡɪv ðə ɡeɪt / (BrE)
to dismiss someone or terminate a relationship abruptly, especially in a sudden or harsh manner
dismiss or break up with someone abruptly
informal

Examples

After years of disagreements, she finally gave the gate to her business partner.
The coach gave the gate to several players after the disappointing season.
When he found out about the betrayal, he didn't hesitate to give the gate.

Synset Examples

Mary gave John the axe after she saw him with another woman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: give the boot - to dismiss or fire someone
  • Synonym: to give the axe - to dismiss someone from a job or relationship suddenly
