dumb as a bag of hammers

1. ADJ

/ dʌm əz ə bæɡ əv ˈhæmɚz / (AmE), / dʌm əz ə bæɡ əv ˈhæməz / (BrE)
extremely foolish or unintelligent
very unintelligent or foolish (idiom)
informal

Examples

When it comes to math, he can be dumb as a bag of hammers sometimes.
She proved she was dumb as a bag of hammers by believing every word of that scam.
Don't act dumb as a bag of hammers; ask questions if you're unsure.

Synset Examples

He’s as dumb as a bag of hammers sometimes.

Related Terms
