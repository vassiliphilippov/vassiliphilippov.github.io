to get one's hands on

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt wʌnz hændz ɑn / (AmE), / ɡɛt wʌnz hændz ɒn / (BrE)
to obtain or acquire something, especially something that is difficult to find or obtain
acquire or obtain (something)

Examples

Collectors are eager to get their hands on the new limited edition sneakers.
She finally managed to get her hands on the book after months of searching.
If I could get my hands on those concert tickets, I'd be thrilled.

Synset Examples

Everyone wants to get their hands on Foxxo's latest collection.
Everyone wants to get their hands on Chipzilla this holiday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to acquire - to get or gain something
  • Synonym: to obtain - to come into possession of something
