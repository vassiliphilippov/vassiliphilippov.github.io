in the tank

1. ADP

/ ɪn ðə tæŋk / (AmE), / ɪn ðə taŋk / (BrE)
(informal) In a very poor, weak, or low state, especially financially or in terms of confidence, performance, or prospects.
in a very poor or weak state
informal

Examples

After several failed products, the company's reputation is in the tank.
Mary's motivation has been in the tank since she lost her job.
The economy was in the tank during the financial crisis.

Synset Examples

His confidence is in the tank after that failure.
The company’s profits are in the tank this quarter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: down and out - in a very poor or unsuccessful state
  • Antonym: flourishing - thriving or prospering
