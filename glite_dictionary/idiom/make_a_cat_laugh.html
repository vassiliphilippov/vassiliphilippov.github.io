to make a cat laugh

1. VERB

/ meɪk ə kæt læf / (AmE), / meɪk ə kæt lɑːf / (BrE)
to be extremely funny or cause great amusement, often suggesting that something is so absurdly funny that even a cat would laugh
be extremely funny; cause great amusement

Examples

That joke was so ridiculous it could make a cat laugh.
Her silly dance moves would make a cat laugh.
Some of these comedy films are enough to make a cat laugh.

Synset Examples

His jokes were enough to make a cat laugh.
Her antics were enough to make a cat laugh.
That story is enough to make a cat laugh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to make someone laugh - cause someone to laugh
  • Synonym: to crack up - to cause someone to laugh a lot
