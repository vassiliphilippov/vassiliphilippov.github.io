all the gear and no idea

1. X

/ ɔl ðə ɡɪr ənd noʊ aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / ɔːl ðə ɡɪə ənd nəʊ aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
used to describe a person who has the necessary equipment or possessions for a particular activity but lacks the knowledge, skill, or plan to use them effectively
having equipment but lacking knowledge or skills
informal

Examples

He turned up at the tennis court with all the gear and no idea, barely knowing how to play.
She spent a fortune on camping equipment but had all the gear and no idea when it came to pitching the tent.
At the gym, many people have all the gear and no idea about proper exercise techniques.

Synset Examples

They showed up with all the gear and no idea for the hike.
She has all the gear and no idea how to use it.
He bought a new camera but has all the gear and no idea.
