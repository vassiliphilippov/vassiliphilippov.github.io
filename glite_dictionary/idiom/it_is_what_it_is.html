it is what it is

1. X

/ ɪt ɪz wʌt ɪt ɪz / (AmE), / ɪt ɪz wɒt ɪt ɪz / (BrE)
used to express acceptance of a situation that cannot be changed or is unavoidable
expression of acceptance of an unchangeable situation

Examples

I didn't get the job, but it is what it is, so I'll move on.
The weather ruined our plans; it is what it is.
We did our best, but it is what it is.

Synset Examples

My schedule is packed; it is what it is.
We lost the game, and frankly, it is what it is.
The project failed, but it is what it is.
