to act like a dingus

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈdɪŋɡəs / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈdɪŋɡəs / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or clumsy manner
behave foolishly or clumsily
informal slang

Examples

If you keep acting like a dingus, people might not take you seriously.
She realized she was acting like a dingus when she forgot her own phone number.
During the meeting, Tom couldn't stop acting like a dingus and making everyone laugh.

Synset Examples

Stop acting like a dingus and focus on the task.
Don't be a dingus; just ask for help.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: act foolishly - to behave in a silly or unwise manner
  • Antonym: act sensibly - to behave in a wise or prudent manner
