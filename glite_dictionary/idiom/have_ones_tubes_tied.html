to have one's tubes tied

1. VERB

/ hæv wʌnz tuːbz taɪd / (AmE), / hæv wʌnz tjuːbz taɪd / (BrE)
(of a woman) to undergo a sterilization procedure in which the fallopian tubes are closed to prevent pregnancy.
undergo female sterilization
informal medical

Examples

After her third child, she considered having her tubes tied for peace of mind.
He's joked about having his tubes tied, though technically only women have the procedure.
Many women choose having their tubes tied as a permanent birth control option.

Synset Examples

He joked about having his tubes tied after the third child.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sterilization - the process of making someone unable to have children, typically by a medical procedure
