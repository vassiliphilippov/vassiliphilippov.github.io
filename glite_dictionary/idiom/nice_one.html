nice one

1. INTJ

/ ˈnaɪs wʌn / (AmE), / ˈnaɪs wʌn / (BrE)
used to express approval, admiration, or congratulations for something well done or achieved
expression of approval or congratulations
informal

Examples

You finished the report before the deadline? Nice one!
When he scored the winning goal, his teammates shouted, "Nice one!"
That's a really creative solution to the problem—nice one.

Synset Examples

You solved that puzzle? Nice one!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: well done - an expression used to congratulate someone for a good job
  • Synonym: good job - used to praise someone for doing something well
