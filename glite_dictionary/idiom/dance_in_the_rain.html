to dance in the rain

1. VERB

/ dæns ɪn ðə reɪn / (AmE), / dɑːns ɪn ðə reɪn / (BrE)
to enjoy life freely and without worrying about problems or hardships
enjoy life without worries

Examples

After losing his job, Mark decided to dance in the rain and travel the world with no regrets.
Sometimes you just have to dance in the rain and forget about your troubles for a while.
Her positive attitude inspired others to dance in the rain even during difficult times.

Synset Examples

The batty man danced in the rain without a care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: live life to the fullest - to experience life as much as possible without hesitation or regret
  • Antonym: worry - to feel uneasy or anxious about something
