to flip the bitch switch

1. VERB

/ flɪp ði bɪtʃ swɪtʃ / (AmE), / flɪp ði bɪtʃ swɪtʃ / (BrE)
to suddenly become very angry or aggressive, often in a noticeable or abrupt way
suddenly become angry or aggressive
slang vulgar

Examples

If you keep pushing her, she might flip the bitch switch at any moment.
He tends to flip the bitch switch when things don't go his way.
Everyone noticed when Lauren suddenly flipped the bitch switch during the meeting.

Synset Examples

She flipped the bitch switch when he was late.

Related Terms
