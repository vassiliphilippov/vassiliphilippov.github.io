bundle of nerves

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌn.dəl əv nɝːvz / (AmE), / ˈbʌn.dəl əv nɜːvz / (BrE)
a person who is extremely anxious or tense; a state of extreme nervousness.
very anxious or tense person

Examples

Before her interview, she was a bundle of nerves and could hardly sit still.
I'm always a bundle of nerves before flying, no matter how many times I've traveled.
He turned into a bundle of nerves when he realized he had to give a speech.

Synset Examples

The athlete was a bundle of nerves before the championship match.
She was a bundle of nerves before her big presentation.
He felt like a bundle of nerves waiting for the test results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nervous wreck - person in a state of extreme nervousness or emotional distress
