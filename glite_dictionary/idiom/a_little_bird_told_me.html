a little bird told me

1. X

/ ə ˈlɪtl̩ bɝd toʊld mi / (AmE), / ə ˈlɪtl bɜːd təʊld miː / (BrE)
used to say that you have heard a piece of information from someone, especially when you want to keep their identity secret
said when revealing a secret source
informal

Examples

A little bird told me you got a promotion—congratulations!
A little bird told me that you're moving to New York next month.
A little bird told me you might be interested in joining our team.

Synset Examples

A little bird told me you're planning a surprise party.
A little bird told me she has a secret.
