like Christ on a stick

1. X

/ laɪk kraɪst ɑn ə stɪk / (AmE), / laɪk kraɪst ɒn ə stɪk / (BrE)
used to describe something or someone as very weak, powerless, or ineffective
very weak or powerless (idiom)

Examples

His excuses were like Christ on a stick—no one believed they would work.
The new manager's leadership was like Christ on a stick during the crisis.
Every time he tries to fix the computer, it's like Christ on a stick—completely useless.

Synset Examples

His argument was weak, like Christ on a stick.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ineffective - not producing the intended result; powerless or weak
  • Synonym: powerless - lacking the ability to act or influence
