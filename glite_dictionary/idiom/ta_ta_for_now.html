ta ta for now

1. X

/ tɑː ˈtɑː fər ˈnaʊ / (AmE), / tɑː ˈtɑː fə ˈnaʊ / (BrE)
an informal way of saying goodbye, often indicating that you will see or speak to the person again soon
informal goodbye expression
informal

Examples

I have to get back to work, so ta ta for now!
It's been great chatting with you—ta ta for now.
She waved and called out, 'ta ta for now,' as she left the room.

Synset Examples

It was nice to see you, ta ta for now!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - an expression used to bid farewell
  • Synonym: see you later - an informal expression meaning goodbye for now

Etymology

Popularized as an extension of 'ta ta' (informal farewell), with 'for now' added for temporary parting.
