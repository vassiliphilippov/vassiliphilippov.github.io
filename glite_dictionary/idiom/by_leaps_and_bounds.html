by leaps and bounds

1. ADV

/ baɪ lips ənd baʊndz / (AmE), / baɪ liːps ənd baʊndz / (BrE)
extremely quickly and by large amounts; with rapid and significant progress
very quickly; rapidly and greatly

Examples

Her confidence has grown by leaps and bounds since she started her new job.
The company's technology is advancing by leaps and bounds every year.
His language skills improved by leaps and bounds after moving abroad.

Synset Examples

We made progress by leaps and bounds last quarter.
The project advanced in leaps and bounds.
Her skills improved by leaps and bounds this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rapidly - at a great speed; quickly
  • Antonym: slowly - at a slow speed; not quickly
