to waste one's time

1. VERB

/ weɪst wʌnz taɪm / (AmE), / weɪst wʌnz taɪm / (BrE)
to spend time doing things that are not useful or productive; to be idle or unoccupied with meaningful activity
spend time doing unproductive things

Examples

If you keep playing video games all day, you're just wasting your time.
She realized she had wasted her time waiting for him to call.
Stop wasting your time worrying about things you can't change.

Synset Examples

Her son is just bumming around all day

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idle away - spend time doing nothing in particular
  • Antonym: make good use of one's time - spend time usefully and productively
