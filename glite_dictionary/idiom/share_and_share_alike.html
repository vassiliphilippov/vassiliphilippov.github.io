share and share alike

1. X

/ ʃɛr ənd ʃɛr əˈlaɪk / (AmE), / ʃeə ənd ʃeə əˈlaɪk / (BrE)
used to say that everyone should receive an equal portion or benefit, especially when dividing something among people
everyone having an equal share

Examples

Our parents taught us to share and share alike with our siblings.
The potluck dinner encouraged everyone to share and share alike, ensuring no one was left out.
In challenging times, it's important to remember to share and share alike.

Synset Examples

She always said, share and share alike, when we pooled resources.
In our club, we believe in share and share alike.
The siblings grew up with the motto, share and share alike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fairness - impartial and just treatment or behavior without favoritism or discrimination
