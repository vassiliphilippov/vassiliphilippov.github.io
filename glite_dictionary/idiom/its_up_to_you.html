it's up to you

1. X

/ ɪts ʌp tə ju / (AmE), / ɪts ʌp tə juː / (BrE)
used to say that the decision or choice belongs to the person being spoken to
the choice or decision is yours

Examples

If you want to go to the party or stay home, it's up to you.
We can leave now or wait a bit longer—it's up to you.
Whether we watch a movie or play a game tonight, it's up to you.

Synset Examples

You can join us for dinner, or not, it's up to you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: your call - the choice or decision is yours
  • Synonym: the ball is in your court - it is now your responsibility to make a decision
