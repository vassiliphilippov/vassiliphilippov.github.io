to strike a blow for justice

1. VERB

/ straɪk ə bloʊ fər ˈdʒʌstɪs / (AmE), / straɪk ə bləʊ fə ˈdʒʌstɪs / (BrE)
to take action that supports or advances the cause of justice
take action for justice

Examples

The activists hoped to strike a blow for justice with their peaceful demonstration.
She felt her testimony in court would strike a blow for justice and help others.
By passing the new law, the government struck a blow for justice.

Synset Examples

The activists aimed to strike a blow for justice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stand up for - to support or defend something or someone
