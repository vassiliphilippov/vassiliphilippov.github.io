to can't help but groan

1. VERB

/ kænt hɛlp bət ɡroʊn / (AmE), / kɑːnt hɛlp bət ɡrəʊn / (BrE)
to feel so frustrated or annoyed that one cannot avoid groaning; to be compelled to react with a groan
be unable to avoid groaning from frustration

Examples

Every time the deadline gets extended, I can't help but groan at the extra work.
She can't help but groan when she hears the alarm so early in the morning.
When I saw the long queue at the bank, I can't help but groan.

Synset Examples

I can't help but groan when I see that traffic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: can't help but... - to be unable to avoid doing something
