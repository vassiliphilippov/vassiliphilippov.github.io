back into the swing of things

1. X

/ bæk ˈɪntu ðə swɪŋ əv ˈθɪŋz / (AmE), / bæk ˈɪntuː ðə swɪŋ əv ˈθɪŋz / (BrE)
returning to a normal routine, especially after a period of disruption or inactivity
returning to one's usual routine

Examples

It can be difficult to get back into the swing of things after a long illness.
After the holidays, teachers help students get back into the swing of things at school.
Now that he's moved to a new city, it's taken some time to get back into the swing of things.

Synset Examples

Getting back into the swing of things after vacation is always tough.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: back in the saddle - returning to an activity or routine after a break
