stone deaf

1. ADJ

/ stoʊn dɛf / (AmE), / stəʊn dɛf / (BrE)
completely unwilling to listen to advice or unable to hear at all
completely unwilling to listen or unable to hear

Examples

Despite repeated advice from her friends, she remained stone deaf to their concerns.
The company was stone deaf to criticism about its new policy.
He is stone deaf when it comes to constructive feedback.

Synset Examples

Despite her warnings, he remained stone deaf to advice.
She seemed stone deaf to my concerns about the plan.
He was stone deaf to their pleas for help.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deaf - unable to hear or unwilling to listen
  • Degree Lower: hard of hearing - having partial hearing loss
