New York minute

1. NOUN

/ nuː ˈjɔrk ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / njuː ˈjɔːk ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
An extremely short amount of time; an instant.
a very short time; an instant
informal

Examples

If you hesitate, the opportunity could be gone in a New York minute.
Traffic can change in a New York minute during rush hour.
He finished the assignment in a New York minute, impressing his supervisor.

Synset Examples

if I had the chance, I'd do it in a flash

Related Terms

  • Synonym: instant - an extremely short moment
  • Synonym: heartbeat - a brief moment, as quick as a heartbeat

Etymology

Refers humorously to the fast pace of life associated with New York City.
