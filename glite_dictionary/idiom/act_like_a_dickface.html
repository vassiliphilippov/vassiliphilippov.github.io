to act like a dickface

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈdɪkfeɪs / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈdɪkfeɪs / (BrE)
to behave in an extremely rude, obnoxious, or offensive manner
behave rudely or obnoxiously
offensive vulgar informal

Examples

Please don't act like a dickface when we're out in public.
He tends to act like a dickface when things don't go his way.
You shouldn't act like a dickface just because you're upset.

Synset Examples

Why act like a dickface when you can be nice?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to act like a jerk - behave in a rude or unpleasant way
