Jewish penicillin

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːɪʃ ˌpɛnɪˈsɪlɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːɪʃ ˌpɛnɪˈsɪlɪn / (BrE)
Chicken soup, especially as regarded for its supposed healing and comforting properties, particularly in treating colds or minor illnesses.
chicken soup considered healing

Examples

My grandmother always made Jewish penicillin whenever anyone in the family was feeling under the weather.
Many people swear by Jewish penicillin as the best remedy for a common cold.
The restaurant’s menu boasts homemade Jewish penicillin just like mom used to make.

Synset Examples

Chicken soup is often referred to as Jewish penicillin for its healing properties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken soup - a soup made from chicken, often considered comforting

Etymology

Refers humorously to the reputation of chicken soup in Jewish culture as a remedy for illness, likening it to the antibiotic penicillin.
