an eye for detail

1. NOUN

/ ən aɪ fər dɪˈteɪl / (AmE), / ən aɪ fə dɪˈteɪl / (BrE)
the ability to notice and appreciate small details; careful attention to every part of something
ability to notice small details

Examples

She always demonstrates an eye for detail when reviewing financial reports.
The architect’s an eye for detail made the building’s design truly impressive.
You need an eye for detail to excel in quality control.

Synset Examples

She really has an eye for detail in her paintings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attention to detail - thoroughness in noticing small elements
  • Synonym: meticulous - showing great attention to detail
  • Antonym: careless - not giving enough attention to detail
