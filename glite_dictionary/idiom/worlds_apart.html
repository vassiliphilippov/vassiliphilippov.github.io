worlds apart

1. ADJ

/ wɝldz əˈpɑrt / (AmE), / wɜːldz əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
Extremely different or dissimilar; having no similarities or common ground.
extremely different; very dissimilar

Examples

Though they come from the same town, their personalities are worlds apart.
The two proposals were worlds apart in terms of cost and feasibility.
Even after years of friendship, their dreams were still worlds apart.

Synset Examples

Though they are siblings, their lifestyles are worlds apart.
The two cultures are truly worlds apart in their beliefs.
Their opinions on the matter are worlds apart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chalk and cheese - things that are fundamentally different
  • Antonym: similar - having a resemblance or likeness
