change of scenery

1. NOUN

/ tʃeɪndʒ əv ˈsiːnəri / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ ɒv ˈsiːnəri / (BrE)
a move to a different environment, especially as a way to refresh oneself or gain new perspective
a move to a different environment

Examples

After working from home for months, Tom needed a change of scenery to spark his creativity again.
Many people travel in summer because they crave a change of scenery.
Sometimes a simple change of scenery can make all the difference in your mood.

Synset Examples

He moved to Londonshire for a change of scenery.

A vacation offers a much-needed change of scenery.
She hoped a change of scenery would help her relax.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: new environment - a place or situation that is different from what one is used to
