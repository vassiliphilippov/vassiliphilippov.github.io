doghouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔɡˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡˌhaʊs / (BrE)
in disfavor; out of favor, especially with someone in authority or a partner.
in disfavor; out of favor

Examples

He messed up the project at work and has been in the doghouse with his boss ever since.
If you come home late again, you'll find yourself in the doghouse.
