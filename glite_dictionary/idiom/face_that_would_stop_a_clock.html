face that would stop a clock

1. X

/ feɪs ðət wʊd stɑp ə klɑk / (AmE), / feɪs ðət wʊd stɒp ə klɒk / (BrE)
a face that is extremely unattractive or displeasing to look at
very unattractive face (idiom)
informal

Examples

His mother said he had a face that would stop a clock, but she loved him anyway.
The villain in the story is described as having a face that would stop a clock.
Some comedians make fun of themselves by joking they have a face that would stop a clock.

Synset Examples

She entered the room with a face that would stop a clock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: face like the back of a bus - an extremely unattractive face (idiom)
