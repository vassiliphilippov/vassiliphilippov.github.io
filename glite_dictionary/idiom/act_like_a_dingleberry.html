to act like a dingleberry

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈdɪŋəlˌbɛri / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈdɪŋ(ɡ)əlˌbɛri / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or annoying way
behave foolishly or annoyingly
informal

Examples

If you act like a dingleberry at work, people might not take you seriously.
Please don't act like a dingleberry when we're meeting my parents tonight.
He tends to act like a dingleberry whenever he's nervous.

Synset Examples

Stop acting like a dingleberry and focus!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: act like a fool - behave in a silly or stupid way
