to feel like a lardbucket

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə ˈlɑrdˌbʌkɪt / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈlɑːdˌbʌkɪt / (BrE)
to feel overweight, heavy, or sluggish, especially after overeating or being inactive
to feel overweight or sluggish
informal

Examples

After the third slice of cake, I felt like a lardbucket and just wanted to nap.
She always feels like a lardbucket after skipping her morning run.
If you eat too much pizza, you'll probably feel like a lardbucket all evening.

Synset Examples

After eating all that junk food, he felt like a lardbucket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sluggish - moving or feeling slow and lacking energy
