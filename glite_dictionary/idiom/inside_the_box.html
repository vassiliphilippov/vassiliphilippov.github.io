inside the box

1. ADV

/ ɪnˌsaɪd ðə bɑks / (AmE), / ɪnˌsaɪd ðə bɒks / (BrE)
thinking in a conventional or uncreative way; not considering new or innovative ideas
conventional or uncreative thinking

Examples

Her solutions tend to be inside the box, following established procedures rather than trying something new.
The committee was criticized for thinking inside the box and missing innovative opportunities.
If we always stay inside the box, we risk falling behind our competitors.

Synset Examples

His ideas are often too inside the box for innovation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: outside the box - thinking in an original or creative way; not limited by conventional ideas
