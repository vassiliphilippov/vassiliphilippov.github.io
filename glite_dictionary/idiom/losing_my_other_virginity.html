losing my other virginity

1. X

/ ˈluːzɪŋ maɪ ˈʌðɚ vərˈdʒɪnəti / (AmE), / ˈluːzɪŋ maɪ ˈʌðə vɜːˈdʒɪnəti / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) Experiencing something new or significant for the second time, especially after a long interval or in a new context.
experiencing something new for the second time
informal

After years away from dating, going on a first date again felt like losing my other virginity.
Moving to a new country at thirty was like losing my other virginity all over again.
Trying sushi for the first time in years, I joked about losing my other virginity.
