to tickle someone's pickle

1. VERB

/ ˈtɪkəl sʌmˌwʌnz ˈpɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈtɪkəl sʌmˌwʌnz ˈpɪkəl / (BrE)
(informal) To make someone very happy, amused, or delighted.
make someone very happy or amused (informal)
informal

Examples

That joke really tickled my pickle and made my whole day brighter.
Nothing tickles his pickle more than a surprise visit from friends.
Winning the contest absolutely tickled her pickle after such a tough year.

Synset Examples

Finding that rare book really tickled her pickle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delight - to give great pleasure or enjoyment
  • Synonym: to amuse - to make someone laugh or smile
  • Antonym: to disappoint - to fail to satisfy or make someone unhappy
