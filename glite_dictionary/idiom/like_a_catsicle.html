like a catsicle

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə ˈkæt.sɪk.əl / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈkæt.sɪk.əl / (BrE)
extremely calm, cool, and relaxed, often in a way that suggests ease or contentment, like a cat resting on something cool.
extremely relaxed and cool, like a cat
informal

Examples

After a long meeting, she stretched out on the sofa like a catsicle and refused to move.
He lay like a catsicle under the fan, enjoying the afternoon breeze.
The kitten napped in the sunroom like a catsicle, completely at ease.

Synset Examples

On hot days, my cat enjoys lying on the cool tiles like a catsicle.
