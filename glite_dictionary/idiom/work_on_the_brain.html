work on the brain

1. X

/ wɝk ɑn ðə breɪn / (AmE), / wɜːk ɒn ðə breɪn / (BrE)
always thinking about work, even when not at work; preoccupied with thoughts of work.
preoccupied with work-related thoughts

Examples

Even at family dinners, she seems to have work on the brain.
If you have work on the brain, it's hard to relax on the weekend.
He confessed that he constantly has work on the brain, even when he's supposed to be unwinding.

Synset Examples

He’s got work on the brain, even during his vacation.
