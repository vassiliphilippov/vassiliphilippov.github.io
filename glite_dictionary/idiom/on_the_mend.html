on the mend

1. ADV

/ ɑn ðə mɛnd / (AmE), / ɒn ðə mend / (BrE)
recovering or improving, especially after illness, injury, or difficulty
recovering or improving

Examples

After a week of rest, her condition was finally on the mend.
The business suffered losses last year but is now on the mend.
Doctors say that the patient is on the mend and should be home soon.

Synset Examples

After surgery, she is finally on the mend.
His broken leg is on the mend, thankfully.
The economy is showing signs of being on the mend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recovering - becoming healthy after illness or injury
  • Synonym: improving - becoming better or making progress
  • Antonym: relapsing - becoming ill or deteriorating again after improvement
