to drop the mic

1. VERB

/ drɑp ðə maɪk / (AmE), / drɒp ðə maɪk / (BrE)
to finish speaking, performing, or making a point in a confident and impressive manner, often with a sense that nothing more needs to be said or done
end an act or speech impressively
informal

Examples

After winning the debate, she finished her argument and drop the mic in style.
His clever response silenced everyone; it was truly a drop the mic moment.
He delivered the punchline, paused for effect, and then drop the mic before leaving the stage.

Synset Examples

After his speech, he decided to drop the mic dramatically.
He finished his presentation and dropped the mic.
She nailed her performance and dropped the mic afterward.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to make a statement - to say something significant or impressive
  • Synonym: to walk off - to leave after making a significant impact

Etymology

Popularized from hip-hop culture, where performers would sometimes physically drop the microphone after a powerful performance.
