fuck you

1. INTJ

/ fʌk ju / (AmE), / fʌk juː / (BrE)
A very vulgar expression of anger, contempt, or insult directed at someone.
very vulgar insult of anger or contempt
vulgar

Examples

He shouted fuck you at the referee after being given a red card.
She stormed out of the room yelling fuck you at her boss.
If you keep making fun of me, I'll just say fuck you and walk away.

Synset Examples

She couldn't believe he said, 'Fuck you!' in public.
When he insulted her, she replied, 'Fuck you!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screw you - an informal, less vulgar way to express annoyance or anger (insult)
  • Antonym: thank you - polite phrase expressing gratitude
