up to eleven

1. ADV

/ ʌp tə ɪˈlɛv.ən / (AmE), / ʌp tʊ ɪˈlɛv.ən / (BrE)
to an extreme or maximum level; pushed beyond normal limits
to an extreme or maximum level

Examples

Whenever she decorates her house for holidays, she takes it up to eleven.
The director pushed the action scenes up to eleven for maximum excitement.
They turned the celebration up to eleven with fireworks and a live band.

Synset Examples

He always takes his workouts up to eleven.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to the limit - as much or as far as possible
  • Synonym: over the top - excessively extreme or exaggerated

Etymology

Popularized by the 1984 mockumentary 'This Is Spinal Tap,' where an amplifier's volume knob exceeds the usual maximum of ten.
