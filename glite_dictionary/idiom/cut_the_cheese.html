to cut the cheese

1. VERB

/ kʌt ðə tʃiz / (AmE), / kʌt ðə tʃiːz / (BrE)
(informal) to pass gas; to expel intestinal gas from the anus, usually used humorously or euphemistically.
pass gas (flatulence), informal idiom
informal

Examples

Someone in the elevator must have cut the cheese, because it smells terrible.
Try not to cut the cheese during the meeting, please.
Whenever he laughs too hard, he often ends up cutting the cheese.

Synset Examples

They decided to cut the cheese in front of everyone.
It's time to cut the cheese before serving.
He was nervous and accidentally cut the cheese too early.
