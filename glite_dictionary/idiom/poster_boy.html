poster boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstər bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstə bɔɪ / (BrE)
A man or boy who is regarded as a prominent or ideal example or symbol of a particular quality, cause, or movement.
male symbol or leading example of something

Examples

After his successful recovery, he became the poster boy for the hospital's new treatment program.
The young entrepreneur has turned into the poster boy for innovation in the tech industry.
He never wanted to be the poster boy for environmental activism, but his work made him one.

Synset Examples

He's the poster boy for our charity campaign.
She is the poster boy for perseverance and hard work.
The athlete became the poster boy for healthy living.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poster child - person seen as a symbol of a cause or quality, regardless of gender

Etymology

Originally referring to boys or men featured on promotional posters, now used figuratively.
