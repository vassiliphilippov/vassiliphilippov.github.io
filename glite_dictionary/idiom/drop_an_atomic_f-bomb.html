to drop an atomic F-bomb

1. VERB

/ drɑp ən əˈtɑmɪk ɛf bɑm / (AmE), / drɒp ən əˈtɒmɪk ɛf bɒm / (BrE)
to use very strong or shocking profanity, especially the word 'fuck,' often for dramatic effect
use very strong profanity
informal slang

Examples

The comedian didn't hesitate to drop an atomic F-bomb during the live show, shocking the audience.
Tensions were high in the meeting until Jake finally dropped an atomic F-bomb and everyone went silent.
She accidentally dropped an atomic F-bomb in front of her boss and immediately regretted it.

Synset Examples

He dropped an atomic F-bomb during the heated debate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drop the F-bomb - use the word 'fuck' in speech or writing

Etymology

From slang term 'F-bomb' for the expletive 'fuck,' with 'atomic' intensifying its impact, likening it to an atomic bomb.
