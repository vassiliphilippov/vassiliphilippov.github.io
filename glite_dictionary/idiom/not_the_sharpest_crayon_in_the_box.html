not the sharpest crayon in the box

1. ADJ

/ nɑt ðə ˈʃɑrpəst ˈkreɪˌɑn ɪn ðə bɑks / (AmE), / nɒt ðə ˈʃɑːpɪst ˈkreɪən ɪn ðə bɒks / (BrE)
(informal) Used to describe someone who is not very intelligent or quick-witted.
not very intelligent (idiom)
informal

Examples

She’s kind, but she’s not the sharpest crayon in the box when it comes to math problems.
Even though he’s not the sharpest crayon in the box, he means well and works hard.
You shouldn’t make fun of her for being not the sharpest crayon in the box.

Synset Examples

He’s not the sharpest crayon in the box, but he tries.
I love him, but he's not the sharpest crayon in the box.
Don't expect him to understand; he's not the sharpest crayon in the box.

Related Terms
