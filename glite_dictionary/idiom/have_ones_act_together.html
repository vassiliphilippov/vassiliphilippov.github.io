to have one's act together

1. VERB

/ hæv wʌnz ækt təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / hæv wʌnz ækt təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to be well-organized and efficient, dealing effectively with one's responsibilities
be organized and effective
informal

Examples

After years of struggling, she finally has her act together and is succeeding at work.
The team really needs to have its act together before the big presentation tomorrow.
If you want to impress the boss, make sure you have your act together before the meeting.

Synset Examples

She seems to have her act together after all the chaos.
He finally has his act together and is managing his time well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to get one's act together - to become organized and effective
