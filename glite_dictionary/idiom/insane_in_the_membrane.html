insane in the membrane

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈseɪn ɪn ðə məˈmbreɪn / (AmE), / ɪnˈseɪn ɪn ðə ˈmɛm.breɪn / (BrE)
feeling mentally unstable, wild, or irrational, often in a humorous or exaggerated way
feeling wild or irrational
informal slang

Examples

After studying for twelve hours straight, I felt absolutely insane in the membrane.
He started acting insane in the membrane after drinking too much energy drink.
She laughed and said she was feeling insane in the membrane at the party.

Synset Examples

His ideas were so wild, it was just insane in the membrane.
After all that coffee, I'm feeling insane in the membrane!
She laughs and says she’s feeling insane in the membrane today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged, extremely wild or irrational

Etymology

Popularized by the 1993 song 'Insane in the Brain' by Cypress Hill; based on 'insane' and the colloquial use of 'membrane' for brain.
