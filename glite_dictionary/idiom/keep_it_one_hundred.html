to keep it one hundred

1. VERB

/ kip ɪt wʌn hʌndrəd / (AmE), / kiːp ɪt wʌn hʌndrəd / (BrE)
to be completely honest, genuine, or authentic; to act with total sincerity
be completely honest or authentic
informal american

Examples

In our group, we always keep it one hundred and tell each other the truth.
No matter what, she prefers to keep it one hundred instead of pretending.
When giving feedback, it's important to keep it one hundred so progress can be made.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to keep it real - to be genuine and truthful

Etymology

Originated in African American Vernacular English; 'one hundred' symbolizes completeness and authenticity (100%).
