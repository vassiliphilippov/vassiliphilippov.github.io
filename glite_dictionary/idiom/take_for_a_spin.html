to take for a spin

1. VERB

/ teɪk fər ə spɪn / (AmE), / teɪk fə ə spɪn / (BrE)
to try out or test drive a vehicle or device for a short period to see how it performs
test drive or try something briefly

Examples

I'm going to take for a spin the new electric scooter to see how it handles.
They let us take for a spin the latest model before making a decision.
Whenever he gets a new gadget, he loves to take it for a spin right away.

Synset Examples

She offered to take her friend's boat for a spin.
Let's take the new car for a spin this weekend.
I can't wait to take my bike for a spin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: test drive - to drive a vehicle to assess its performance
