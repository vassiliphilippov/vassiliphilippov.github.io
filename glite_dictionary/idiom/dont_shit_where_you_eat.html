don't shit where you eat

1. X

/ doʊnt ʃɪt wɛr ju it / (AmE), / dəʊnt ʃɪt weə juː iːt / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) Do not mix your personal life with your professional life, as it can lead to trouble or complications.
avoid mixing personal and professional matters
informal vulgar

Examples

He warned me, don't shit where you eat, after I started dating someone from the office.
She learned the hard way why it's best to don't shit where you eat.
Office romances rarely end well; it's usually smart to don't shit where you eat.

Synset Examples

It's risky to date a coworker; don't shit where you eat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: don't mix business with pleasure - do not combine work and personal affairs
