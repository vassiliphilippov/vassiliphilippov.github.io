none of your beeswax

1. X

/ nʌn əv jʊr ˈbizˌwæks / (AmE), / nʌn əv jɔː ˈbiːzwæks / (BrE)
used to say that something does not concern the other person; none of your business
not your concern; none of your business
informal american

Examples

What time I go to bed is none of your beeswax.
He snapped back, saying it was none of your beeswax and to leave him alone.
If you must know, it's none of your beeswax where I'm going tonight.

Synset Examples

It's none of your beeswax what I do.
She said it’s not your beeswax, so drop it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: none of your business - used to say something does not concern the other person

Etymology

Playful alteration of 'none of your business,' popularized in early 20th-century American English.
