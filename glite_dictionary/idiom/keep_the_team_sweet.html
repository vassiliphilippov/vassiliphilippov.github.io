to keep the team sweet

1. VERB

/ kip ðə tim swit / (AmE), / kiːp ðə tiːm swiːt / (BrE)
to maintain a positive and harmonious atmosphere within a team, often through small considerate actions or gestures
maintain team harmony
informal

Examples

Regular feedback sessions help keep the team sweet and motivated.
He bought everyone coffee to keep the team sweet ahead of the big presentation.
Managers should make time to keep the team sweet with small acts of appreciation.

Synset Examples

Little gestures really help keep the team sweet.
