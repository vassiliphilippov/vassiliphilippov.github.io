scratch a liberal and you'll find a fascist

1. X

/ skrætʃ ə ˈlɪbərəl ənd juːl faɪnd ə ˈfæʃɪst / (AmE), / skrætʃ ə ˈlɪbərəl ənd juːl faɪnd ə ˈfæʃɪst / (BrE)
A provocative statement suggesting that people who claim to be liberal may reveal authoritarian tendencies when challenged.
accusation of hidden authoritarianism in liberals

Examples

Whenever censorship debates arise, he says, "scratch a liberal and you'll find a fascist."
Some commentators argue that, in times of crisis, scratch a liberal and you'll find a fascist.
After the heated argument, she remarked, "scratch a liberal and you'll find a fascist, as the saying goes."

Synset Examples

In heated debates, I often think scratch a liberal and you'll find a fascist.
That phrase, scratch a liberal and you'll find a fascist, resonates in today's politics.
