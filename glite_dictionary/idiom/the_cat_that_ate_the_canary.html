the cat that ate the canary

1. NOUN

/ ðə kæt ðət eɪt ðə kəˈnɛri / (AmE), / ðə kæt ðət eɪt ðə kəˈneəri / (BrE)
a person who looks very pleased or self-satisfied, often because of a secret success or advantage
self-satisfied-looking person

Examples

After closing the deal, he looked like the cat that ate the canary.
She strutted into the room with a grin, proud as the cat that ate the canary.
You have a smile like the cat that ate the canary—what have you done?

Synset Examples

She walked in with a smile, looking like the cat that ate the canary.
He was acting like the cat that ate the canary after his promotion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smug - having or showing excessive pride in oneself or achievements
  • Antonym: modest - not drawing attention to oneself or one's achievements
