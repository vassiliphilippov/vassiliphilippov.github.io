to call it stumps

1. VERB

/ kɔl ɪt stʌmps / (AmE), / kɔːl ɪt stʌmps / (BrE)
to declare that an activity has ended, especially after a period of work or play
declare an activity ended

Examples

After working late into the night, the team decided to call it stumps and continue in the morning.
Heavy rain forced the match officials to call it stumps earlier than planned.
Let's call it stumps for now and pick this up next week.

Synset Examples

The team agreed to call it stumps and head home.
After the last over, they called it stumps for the day.
The players decided to call it stumps due to rain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to call it a day - to decide to stop working for the day

Etymology

From the game of cricket, where 'stumps' are the three upright posts at each end of the pitch, and 'calling stumps' signals the end of play for the day.
