on fire

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ˈfaɪr / (AmE), / ɒn ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
performing with exceptional skill, enthusiasm, or success; doing extremely well
performing extremely well
informal

Examples

After missing his first few shots, the basketball player was suddenly on fire and scored fifteen points in a row.
Her sales numbers were on fire last quarter, beating every target by a wide margin.
During the debate, his arguments were sharp and confident; he was truly on fire that evening.

Synset Examples

My new project is on fire; everyone loves it!
She was literally on fire during her performance.
He was on fire during the meeting, impressing everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot - performing extremely well or successfully
