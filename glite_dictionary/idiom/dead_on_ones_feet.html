dead on one's feet

1. ADJ

/ dɛd ɑn wʌnz fit / (AmE), / dɛd ɒn wʌnz fiːt / (BrE)
extremely tired or exhausted, usually as a result of physical or mental effort
extremely tired or exhausted

Examples

After staying up all night working on her project, she felt dead on her feet at the office.
By the end of the shift, the nurses looked absolutely dead on their feet.
He ran the entire race but was dead on his feet when he finally reached the finish line.

Synset Examples

After the long meeting, I was dead on my feet.
She was dead on her feet after working a double shift.
He felt dead on his feet after the marathon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausted - extremely tired
  • Antonym: refreshed - energetic, full of energy after rest
