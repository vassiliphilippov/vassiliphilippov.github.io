to give the once over

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ðə wʌns oʊvər / (AmE), / ɡɪv ðə wʌns əʊvə / (BrE)
to look at someone or something quickly but critically, usually to judge or inspect them
inspect or judge with a quick look

Examples

Before buying the used car, the mechanic gave the once over to the engine and brakes.
She gave the once over to her reflection before stepping onto the stage.
The security guard gave the once over to each visitor entering the building.

Synset Examples

When the movie star entered, all the women gave him the once over

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to inspect - to look at something carefully to check its condition
  • Degree Higher: to scrutinize - to examine closely and thoroughly
