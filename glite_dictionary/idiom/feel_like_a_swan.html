to feel like a swan

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə swɑn / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə swɒn / (BrE)
to feel graceful and beautiful, often as a result of one's appearance or poise.
to feel graceful and beautiful

Examples

When she performed the ballet, she said she felt like a swan gliding across the stage.
In her wedding gown, Maya felt like a swan and walked with confidence.
After her makeover, Anna felt like a swan for the first time in her life.

Synset Examples

She felt like a swan in her elegant new dress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graceful - having elegance or beauty of movement
  • Antonym: awkward - lacking grace or smoothness
