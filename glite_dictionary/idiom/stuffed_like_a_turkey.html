stuffed like a turkey

1. ADJ

/ stʌft laɪk ə ˈtɝki / (AmE), / stʌft laɪk ə ˈtɜːki / (BrE)
feeling extremely full after eating, especially to the point of discomfort
extremely full after eating

Examples

After eating three helpings of dinner, I was stuffed like a turkey.
She always feels stuffed like a turkey after family gatherings.
He returned from the all-you-can-eat restaurant stuffed like a turkey and ready for a nap.

Synset Examples

She left the buffet feeling stuffed like a turkey.
After the feast, I felt stuffed like a turkey.
By the end of Thanksgiving, we were all stuffed like turkeys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stuffed - very full, especially from eating
