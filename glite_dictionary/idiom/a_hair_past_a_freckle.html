a hair past a freckle

1. NOUN

/ ə hɛr pæst ə ˈfrɛkəl / (AmE), / ə heə pɑːst ə ˈfrɛkl̩ / (BrE)
(informal) Used humorously to mean a very short time after a particular point on the clock or timeline.
a very short time after a point (humorous)
informal

Examples

We got there a hair past a freckle and the show had already started.
She said to meet her at a hair past a freckle, so I arrived just after noon.
He joked that his appointment was set for a hair past a freckle, meaning it was not exact.

Synset Examples
