like finding a hen's hiney

1. X

/ laɪk ˈfaɪndɪŋ ə hɛnz ˈhaɪni / (AmE), / laɪk ˈfaɪndɪŋ ə hɛnz ˈhaɪni / (BrE)
used to describe something that is very difficult or rare to find
extremely difficult or rare to find

Examples

Winning the lottery is like finding a hen's hiney these days.
Getting everyone to agree on the proposal was like finding a hen's hiney.
A parking spot downtown on a Friday night is like finding a hen's hiney.

Synset Examples

Finding a four-leaf clover is like finding a hen's hiney.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: like finding a needle in a haystack - something that is very difficult to locate
