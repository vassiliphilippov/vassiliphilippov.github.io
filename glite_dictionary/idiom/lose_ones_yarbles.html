to lose one's yarbles

1. VERB

/ luːz wʌnz ˈjɑrbəlz / (AmE), / luːz wʌnz ˈjɑːbəlz / (BrE)
(slang) to become mentally unstable or act irrationally.
(slang) become mentally unstable or irrational
slang informal

Examples

After the breakup, he lost his yarbles and started acting completely out of character.
My boss almost lost her yarbles when the project was delayed again.
Sometimes, it feels like I’m losing my yarbles with all this stress.

Synset Examples

He lost his yarbles after that stressful meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to lose one's mind - to become insane or act irrationally

Etymology

Slang adaptation; 'yarbles' as a euphemism for 'testicles' popularized by Anthony Burgess's novel "A Clockwork Orange".
