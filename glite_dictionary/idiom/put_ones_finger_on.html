to put one's finger on

1. VERB

/ pʊt wʌnz ˈfɪŋɡɚ ɑn / (AmE), / pʊt wʌnz ˈfɪŋɡə ɒn / (BrE)
to identify or understand something precisely, often something elusive or hard to define
identify or pinpoint exactly

Examples

Although I sensed something was wrong, I couldn't put my finger on it.
It's hard to put your finger on what makes this movie so captivating.
He tried to put his finger on the reason for their sudden success.

Synset Examples

She couldn't put her finger on why he was upset.
I can't quite put my finger on the problem.
Can you put your finger on the exact moment it happened?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pinpoint - to find or identify with precision
  • Synonym: to identify - to recognize or clearly determine something
