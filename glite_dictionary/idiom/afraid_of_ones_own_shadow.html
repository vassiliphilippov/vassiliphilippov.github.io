afraid of one's own shadow

1. ADJ

/ əˈfreɪd əv wʌnz oʊn ˈʃædoʊ / (AmE), / əˈfreɪd əv wʌnz əʊn ˈʃædəʊ / (BrE)
extremely timid, nervous, or easily frightened; excessively fearful or cautious, often with little cause
extremely timid or easily frightened

Examples

Ever since the burglary, she has been afraid of her own shadow and jumps at every little noise.
He is so shy that he seems almost afraid of his own shadow at school.
Don't let yourself become afraid of your own shadow; try to face your fears.

Synset Examples

She won't go out alone; she's afraid of her own shadow.
He's so timid, he's afraid of his own shadow.
Don't be afraid of your own shadow; take the leap!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: timid - lacking in courage or confidence; easily frightened
  • Antonym: fearless - without fear; very brave
