damned by association

1. X

/ ˈdæmd baɪ əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈdamd baɪ əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
Regarded or treated harshly because of one's connection or association with people or groups viewed negatively.
judged harshly due to one's associations

Examples

After the controversy, several employees felt damned by association despite having no direct involvement.
Politicians often risk being damned by association when their allies are implicated in scandals.
She resigned, fearing she would be damned by association with the disgraced company.

Synset Examples

She felt damned by association once her friends got in trouble.
He worried he’d be damned by association after the scandal.
