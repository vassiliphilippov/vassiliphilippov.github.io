stuck in the muck

1. ADJ

/ stʌk ɪn ðə mʌk / (AmE), / stʌk ɪn ðə mʌk / (BrE)
Unable to make progress due to being trapped in a messy, difficult, or unproductive situation.
trapped in a difficult situation

Examples

After hours of paperwork, I felt stuck in the muck and completely drained.
Whenever our project faces new obstacles, the team gets stuck in the muck and can't move forward.
She realized she was stuck in the muck of regret and needed a fresh start.

Synset Examples

Don't get stuck in the muck of procrastination.
