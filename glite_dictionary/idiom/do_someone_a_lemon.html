to do someone a lemon

1. VERB

/ du ˈsʌmwʌn ə ˈlɛmən / (AmE), / duː ˈsʌmwən ə ˈlɛmən / (BrE)
to trick or deceive someone, especially in a way that leaves them at a disadvantage
trick or deceive someone
informal british
