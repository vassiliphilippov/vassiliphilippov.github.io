to trust the process

1. VERB

/ trʌst ðə ˈprɑː.sɛs / (AmE), / trʌst ðə ˈprəʊ.sɛs / (BrE)
to have confidence in the course of actions or steps being taken, especially when the outcome is uncertain, believing that they will lead to eventual success
have faith in ongoing actions or steps

Examples

You may not see results right away, but it's important to trust the process.
The coach reminded her team to trust the process after a tough loss.
During difficult times, I remind myself to trust the process and stay patient.

Synset Examples

Sometimes you have to leave it be and trust the process.
Just trust the process; it will all come right.
Sometimes you just have to take the ride and trust the process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: have faith - to maintain belief or confidence, especially without proof
  • Antonym: doubt - to feel uncertain or lack belief
