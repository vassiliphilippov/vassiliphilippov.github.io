life in the fast lane

1. NOUN

/ laɪf ɪn ðə fæst leɪn / (AmE), / laɪf ɪn ðə fɑːst leɪn / (BrE)
a lifestyle that is exciting and full of activity, often involving risk or pleasure-seeking
exciting, fast-paced lifestyle

Examples

After getting his promotion, he started living life in the fast lane and rarely stayed home.
Many celebrities are drawn to life in the fast lane, but it can be exhausting.
She decided to leave life in the fast lane behind and move to the countryside.

Synset Examples

They enjoy life in the fast lane, traveling and partying.
