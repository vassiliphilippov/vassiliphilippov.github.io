something fierce

1. ADV

/ ˈsʌmθɪŋ ˈfɪrs / (AmE), / ˈsʌmθɪŋ ˈfɪəs / (BrE)
to a very great degree; intensely or extremely
very intensely or extremely
informal

Examples

The wind was blowing something fierce last night.
After the announcement, the excitement in the office was something fierce.
She wanted ice cream something fierce after her run.

Synset Examples
