bundle of laughs

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌndəl əv læfs / (AmE), / ˈbʌndəl əv lɑːfs / (BrE)
A situation, event, or person that is very amusing or enjoyable.
something or someone very amusing

Examples

The whole weekend was a bundle of laughs from start to finish.
Whenever she's around, the office turns into a bundle of laughs.
That comedy show last night was really a bundle of laughs.

Synset Examples

The comedians provided a bundle of laughs during the show.
Our road trip turned into a bundle of laughs and memories.
That movie is a bundle of laughs; I couldn't stop giggling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barrel of laughs - something very funny or entertaining
