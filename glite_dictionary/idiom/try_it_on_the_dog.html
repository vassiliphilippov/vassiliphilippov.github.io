to try it on the dog

1. VERB

/ ˈtraɪ ɪt ɑn ðə dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ ɪt ɒn ðə dɒɡ / (BrE)
To test something in practice or on a less important subject before using it more widely or with higher stakes.
test on a less important subject

Examples

Before launching the product, the team decided to try it on the dog with a small group of users.
If you're unsure about the new recipe, why not try it on the dog before the party?
They always try it on the dog before making a big investment in a new idea.

Synset Examples

Before buying, let's try it on the dog.
She suggested we try it on the dog first.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: test the waters - to try something tentatively to see if it will succeed
