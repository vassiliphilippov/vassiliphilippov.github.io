a nod's as good as a wink

1. X

/ ə nɑdz əz ɡʊd əz ə wɪŋk / (AmE), / ə nɒdz əz ɡʊd əz ə wɪŋk / (BrE)
Used to say that a hint or suggestion is enough to convey a message or agreement without the need for explicit words.
a hint or gesture is enough

Examples

He didn’t say anything, but you know, a nod's as good as a wink.
With just a smile, she let him know she understood: a nod's as good as a wink.
He glanced at his friend, and a nod's as good as a wink told him to act.

Synset Examples

He didn’t understand, but a nod's as good as a wink to a blind bat.
When she agreed silently, it was a nod's as good as a wink to a blind bat.
I told him my plan; a nod's as good as a wink to a blind bat.
