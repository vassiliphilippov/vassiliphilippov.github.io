an ounce of regret

1. NOUN

/ æn aʊns əv rɪˈɡrɛt / (AmE), / æn aʊns əv rɪˈɡrɛt / (BrE)
not even a small amount of remorse or sorrow for something done or left undone.
not even a small amount of remorse

Examples

He didn't have an ounce of regret after making the tough decision.
Despite the chaos she caused, she showed not an ounce of regret.
If you truly care, you'll feel at least an ounce of regret for your actions.

Synset Examples

He didn't have an ounce of regret after the decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regret - a feeling of sadness or disappointment over something that has happened
