ayy lmao

1. INTJ

/ eɪ ləˈmoʊ / (AmE), / eɪ ləˈməʊ / (BrE)
An exclamation used online to express amusement, laughter, or disbelief, often in reaction to humorous or absurd situations.
internet exclamation of amusement or disbelief
informal slang

Examples

When I saw the video of the cat dancing, all I could say was ayy lmao.
Some memes are so absurd, my only response is ayy lmao.
He texted me, 'I just failed the test,' so I replied, 'ayy lmao.'

Synset Examples

When he tripped, all I could say was ayy lmao.

Related Terms
