on someone's watch

1. ADP

/ ɑn ˈsʌmwʌnz wɑtʃ / (AmE), / ɒn ˈsʌmwʌnz wɒtʃ / (BrE)
while someone is responsible for something; during the time when someone is in charge or supervising
during one's responsibility or supervision

Examples

I can assure you that nothing like this will happen on my watch.
The documents went missing on his watch, so he felt responsible.
No accidents occurred on her watch thanks to her careful supervision.

Synset Examples

Things went missing on her watch last week.
He promised to keep everything safe on his watch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: under someone's watch - during someone's supervision or guard
  • Synonym: in charge - having responsibility or control over something
  • Antonym: off duty - not responsible or not on duty at a particular time
