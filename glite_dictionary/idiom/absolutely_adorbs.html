absolutely adorbs

1. ADJ

/ ˌæb.səˈluːt.li əˈdɔrbz / (AmE), / ˌæb.səˈluːt.li əˈdɔːbz / (BrE)
Extremely cute or adorable, often used informally to express strong affection or admiration for something or someone.
extremely cute or adorable
informal slang

Examples

That puppy in the pink sweater is absolutely adorbs!
Your photos from the birthday party were absolutely adorbs.
Those mini cupcakes are absolutely adorbs; I almost don't want to eat them!

Synset Examples

The kittens playing together are absolutely adorbs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adorbs - informal/slang for 'adorable', meaning cute or charming
  • Synonym: adorable - inspiring great affection; cute or charming
