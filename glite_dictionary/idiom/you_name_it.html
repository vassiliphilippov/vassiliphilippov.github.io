you name it

1. X

/ juː ˈneɪm ɪt / (AmE), / juː ˈneɪm ɪt / (BrE)
used to indicate that anything you can think of is included or available.
anything you can think of

Examples

We have a huge selection of fruits—apples, oranges, bananas, you name it.
Her expertise covers painting, sculpting, pottery—you name it.
The store sells books, toys, clothes, electronics—you name it.

Synset Examples

We have everything for the party: drinks, snacks, you name it.
They offer all sorts of services: cleaning, gardening, you name it.
She can cook Italian, Mexican, you name it!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: and so on - used to indicate a continuation of a list
  • Synonym: and the like - and similar things
