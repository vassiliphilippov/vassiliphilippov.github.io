no poop on the stoop

1. X

/ noʊ puːp ɑn ðə stuːp / (AmE), / nəʊ puːp ɒn ðə stuːp / (BrE)
used humorously to prohibit pets, especially dogs, from defecating on a porch or doorsteps
humorous sign banning pet fouling on steps
informal

Examples

The sign outside the apartment read, 'no poop on the stoop,' making passersby smile.
After her neighbor complained, she put up a 'no poop on the stoop' notice for dog owners.
We joked about making a 'no poop on the stoop' rule for our front steps.

Synset Examples

The kids laughed at the sign saying 'No poop on the stoop.'
