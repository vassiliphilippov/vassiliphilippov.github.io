to walk on air

1. VERB

/ wɑk ɑn ɛr / (AmE), / wɔːk ɒn ɛə / (BrE)
to feel extremely happy or elated
feel extreme happiness

Examples

After hearing the good news, she felt like she could walk on air all day.
Winning the championship made the whole team walk on air for weeks.
He walked on air when he got accepted into his dream university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: over the moon - extremely happy or delighted
  • Synonym: on cloud nine - in a state of perfect happiness
  • Antonym: down in the dumps - feeling sad or depressed
