inside and out

1. ADV

/ ˌɪn.saɪd ənd aʊt / (AmE), / ˌɪn.saɪd ənd aʊt / (BrE)
in every detail; thoroughly or completely
thoroughly; in every detail

Examples

After years of working at the museum, she knows its collection inside and out.
The mechanic checked the car inside and out to make sure it was safe.
He studied the contract inside and out before signing it.

Synset Examples

She knows the software inside and out.
He knows the city inside and out after living here for years.
They know the rules of the game inside and out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoroughly - completely, in a thorough manner
  • Antonym: superficially - on the surface only; not thoroughly
