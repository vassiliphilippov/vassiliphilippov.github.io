nothing but nonsense

1. X

/ ˈnʌθɪŋ bət ˈnɑnsɛns / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ bə ˈnɒnsəns / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is completely untrue, worthless, or insincere; merely rubbish or pointless talk or claims.
utter rubbish or insincerity

Examples

The rumors about the company's bankruptcy were nothing but nonsense.
His excuses sounded like nothing but nonsense to me.
The advertisement promised quick results, but it turned out to be nothing but nonsense.

Synset Examples

The politician’s promises were nothing but bullplop.
All this talk is nothing but horse manure.
This product is nothing but McShit, don't buy it.
The critic described the book as nothing but pap.
The critics accused the film of being nothing but popery.
That store sells nothing but schlock at inflated prices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - words or ideas that have no meaning or make no sense
  • Synonym: rubbish - something considered worthless or nonsensical
  • Antonym: sense - something logical or reasonable
