year in, year out

1. ADV

/ jɪr ɪn jɪr aʊt / (AmE), / jɪə ɪn jɪə aʊt / (BrE)
every year for many years, without interruption or change
every year without change

Examples

She goes hiking in the mountains year in, year out without missing a single trip.
The family gathers here for dinner year in, year out, no matter what happens.
He has been playing for the same football club year in, year out since his twenties.

Synset Examples

She volunteers year in, year out at the shelter.
He trains hard year in, year out for the competition.
They celebrate this festival year in, year out.
