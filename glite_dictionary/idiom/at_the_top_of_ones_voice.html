at the top of one's voice

1. X

/ ət ðə tɑp əv wʌnz vɔɪs / (AmE), / ət ðə tɒp əv wʌnz vɔɪs / (BrE)
as loudly as possible; shouting or speaking at the maximum volume one can produce
as loudly as possible

Examples

She screamed at the top of her voice when she saw the spider.
During the protest, people chanted at the top of their voices in the streets.
He shouted instructions at the top of his voice so everyone could hear him.

Synset Examples

He called out at the top of his voice for help.
They laughed at the top of their voice during the show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shout - to speak or call out very loudly
  • Antonym: whisper - to speak very quietly
