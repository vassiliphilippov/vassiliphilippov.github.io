in thunderation

1. INTJ

/ ɪn ˌθʌndəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪn ˌθʌndəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
used to express surprise or disbelief
expression of surprise or disbelief
archaic

Examples

In thunderation, where did you find that old book?
In thunderation, I never thought this day would come!
In thunderation, who left the door wide open?

Synset Examples

In thunderation, what a surprise to see you here!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good gracious - used to express surprise
  • Synonym: heavens above - used to express astonishment

Etymology

Formed from 'thunder' as a euphemistic substitute for stronger oaths; 'thunderation' modeled after similar exclamations like 'thunderation!' in 19th-century American English.
