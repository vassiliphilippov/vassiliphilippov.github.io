man on the street

1. NOUN

/ mæn ɑn ðə strit / (AmE), / mæn ɒn ðə striːt / (BrE)
an ordinary member of the general public; a typical or average person
typical member of the general public

Examples

The news program interviewed the man on the street to get public reactions to the election.
As a man on the street, he rarely paid attention to political debates.
Pollsters often seek the opinions of the man on the street to gauge public sentiment.

Synset Examples

The video featured a man on the street sharing thoughts.
Asking a man on the street revealed surprising insights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: everyman - an ordinary or typical person
  • Synonym: average Joe - an average or typical man
  • Synonym: public opinion - the views commonly held by the general public
