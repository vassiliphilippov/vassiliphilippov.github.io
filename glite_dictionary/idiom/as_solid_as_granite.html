as solid as granite

1. ADJ

/ æz ˈsɑlɪd æz ˈɡrænɪt / (AmE), / æz ˈsɒlɪd æz ˈɡrænɪt / (BrE)
Extremely strong, firm, or unshakeable; not likely to change or break under pressure.
extremely strong or unshakeable

Examples

After years of challenges, their partnership remained as solid as granite.
The old fortress walls are as solid as granite even after centuries.
His belief in justice was as solid as granite, never wavering in the face of doubt.

Synset Examples

Her determination was as solid as granite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rock-solid - completely firm or dependable
  • Antonym: fragile - easily broken or damaged
