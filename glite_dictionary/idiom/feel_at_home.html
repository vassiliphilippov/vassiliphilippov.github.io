to feel at home

1. VERB

/ fil ət hoʊm / (AmE), / fiːl ət həʊm / (BrE)
to feel comfortable and relaxed, as if in one's own home; to feel welcome and at ease in a place or situation
to feel comfortable and welcome

Examples

The staff did everything they could to make the guests feel at home.
After a few weeks, I finally began to feel at home in my new job.
Whenever I'm with my best friends, I always feel at home.

Synset Examples

She felt at home in the new city almost instantly.
The decor was very housey, making everyone feel at home.
She felt at home in the realm of fantasy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be at home - to feel comfortable or confident in a situation
  • Antonym: to feel uncomfortable - to feel uneasy or not at ease
