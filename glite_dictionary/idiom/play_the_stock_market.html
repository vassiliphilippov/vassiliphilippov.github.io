to play the stock market

1. VERB

/ pleɪ ði stɑk ˈmɑrkət / (AmE), / pleɪ ðə stɒk ˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
to actively engage in buying and selling stocks, often with the aim of making a profit
to buy and sell stocks for profit

Examples

Many people dream of getting rich by playing the stock market, but it carries significant risk.
After losing money twice, he decided not to play the stock market anymore.
Some see playing the stock market as gambling rather than investing.

Synset Examples

She plays the stock market like a shark in water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invest in the stock market - to put money into stocks for long-term growth or income
