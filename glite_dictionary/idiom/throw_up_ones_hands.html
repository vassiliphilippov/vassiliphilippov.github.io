to throw up one's hands

1. VERB

/ θroʊ ʌp wʌnz hændz / (AmE), / θrəʊ ʌp wʌnz hændz / (BrE)
to raise one's hands as a gesture of frustration, surrender, or helplessness
show frustration or helplessness

Examples

After looking at the confusing instructions, he threw up his hands in exasperation.
She threw up her hands when the project ran into yet another delay.
Sometimes you just want to throw up your hands and walk away from it all.

Synset Examples

She simply threw up her hands and walked away.
He threw up his hands when asked about the missing files.
After hours of searching, I just threw up my hands in frustration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to give up - to stop trying; to surrender or quit
