until the wheels fall off

1. X

/ ənˈtɪl ðə wilz fɔl ɔf / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɪl ðə wiːlz fɔːl ɒf / (BrE)
to carry on with an activity, behavior, or commitment with persistence or enthusiasm until it is no longer possible or until exhaustion or failure occurs
persist or continue until something fails or can't go on
informal

Examples

They promised to support each other until the wheels fall off.
She plans to keep running her business until the wheels fall off.
We'll keep playing music until the wheels fall off.

Synset Examples

They’ll party until the wheels fall off!
We'll keep this tradition alive until the wheels fall off.
I’ll drive this car until the wheels fall off.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: until hell freezes over - keep doing something as long as possible, with no end in sight
