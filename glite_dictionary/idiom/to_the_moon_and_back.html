to the moon and back

1. X

/ tə ðə mun ənd bæk / (AmE), / tə ðə muːn ənd bæk / (BrE)
used to emphasize the great extent of one's love or affection for someone or something
immense love or affection

Examples

I love you to the moon and back, and nothing can ever change that.
She promised her child that she would do anything for him, to the moon and back.
He supports his friends to the moon and back, never hesitating to help when needed.

Synset Examples
