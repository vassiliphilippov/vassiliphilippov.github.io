to hang an arse

1. VERB

/ hæŋ ən ɑrs / (AmE), / hæŋ ən ɑːs / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly British) To behave in a lazy or inactive manner; to dawdle or delay doing something.
be lazy or inactive (informal, chiefly British)
informal british
