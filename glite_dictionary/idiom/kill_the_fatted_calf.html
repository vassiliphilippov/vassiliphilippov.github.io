to kill the fatted calf

1. VERB

/ kɪl ðə ˈfæt̬ɪd kæf / (AmE), / kɪl ðə ˈfætɪd kɑːf / (BrE)
to celebrate a special occasion or welcome someone with lavish hospitality
celebrate or welcome lavishly

Examples

When Sam returned from his travels, his parents decided to kill the fatted calf and throw him a big party.
The company killed the fatted calf to honor its retiring founder.
It was their wedding anniversary, so they killed the fatted calf for a grand celebration.

Synset Examples

It's time to kill the fatted calf and celebrate success.
They decided to kill the fatted calf for the celebration.
After his promotion, they killed the fatted calf for a feast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to celebrate - to observe an occasion with festivities
  • Synonym: welcome with open arms - to receive someone very warmly

Etymology

Alludes to the biblical parable of the prodigal son, in which a fattened calf is killed for a celebratory feast.
