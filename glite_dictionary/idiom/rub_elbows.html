to rub elbows

1. VERB

/ rʌb ˈɛlboʊz / (AmE), / rʌb ˈɛlbəʊz / (BrE)
to associate or mingle closely with other people, especially in social or professional settings
to socialize closely with others

Examples

She loves to rub elbows with artists and musicians at gallery openings.
Young entrepreneurs come here to rub elbows with industry veterans and share ideas.
During the gala, politicians and philanthropists rubbed elbows in an atmosphere of excitement.

Synset Examples

He often rubs elbows with influential people in politics.
At the conference, I got to rub elbows with industry leaders.
She loves to rub elbows with celebrities at charity events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to mingle - to mix or socialize with others at an event
  • Synonym: associate - to spend time with someone or be connected socially
