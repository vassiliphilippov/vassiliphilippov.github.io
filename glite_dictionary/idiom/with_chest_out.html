with chest out

1. ADV

/ wɪθ tʃɛst aʊt / (AmE), / wɪð tʃɛst aʊt / (BrE)
in a confident or bold manner, often displaying physical or emotional assurance
confidently or boldly

Examples

She entered the meeting with chest out, ready to present her ideas.
He always walks with chest out after a big victory.
Despite the tough questions, the politician responded with chest out and unwavering composure.

Synset Examples

He faced the criticism with his chest out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confidently - in a way that shows confidence
  • Antonym: timidly - in a way that shows shyness or lack of confidence
