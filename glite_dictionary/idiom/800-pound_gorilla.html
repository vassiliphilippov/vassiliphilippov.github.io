800-pound gorilla

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪt ˌhʌn.drəd paʊnd ɡəˈrɪl.ə / (AmE), / ˈeɪt ˌhʌn.drəd paʊnd ɡəˈrɪl.ə / (BrE)
an entity that is so powerful or dominant that it operates with little regard for opposition or constraints, especially in a market or industry
a dominant or powerful entity

Examples

In the streaming industry, Netflix remains the 800-pound gorilla that new competitors must challenge.
Any negotiation with the 800-pound gorilla requires a different strategy than with smaller firms.
The presence of an 800-pound gorilla can intimidate even established companies in the sector.

Synset Examples

That company is the 800-pound gorilla in tech innovation.
When it comes to market share, they're the 800-pound gorilla.
You can't ignore the 800-pound gorilla in the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: big fish - an important or influential person or entity in a group or organization
  • Antonym: underdog - a competitor thought to have little chance of winning

Etymology

From a riddle: 'Where does an 800-pound gorilla sit? Anywhere it wants.' Used since the late 20th century in American business jargon.
