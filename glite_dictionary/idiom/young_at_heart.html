young at heart

1. ADJ

/ jʌŋ ət hɑrt / (AmE), / jʌŋ ət hɑːt / (BrE)
having a youthful spirit or outlook, regardless of one's age
having a youthful spirit

Examples

My grandmother may be eighty, but she's still young at heart and loves to dance.
They are young at heart, always eager to try new things and adventures.
Despite his age, he approaches every day with a young at heart attitude.

Synset Examples

Even at seventy, he feels young at heart.
She may be old, but she's still young at heart.
Despite the wrinkles, you're young at heart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: youthful - having qualities typical of youth
