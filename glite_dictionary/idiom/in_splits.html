in splits

1. ADJ

/ ɪn splɪts / (AmE), / ɪn splɪts / (BrE)
laughing uncontrollably or in a state of great amusement, often used in Indian English.
laughing uncontrollably
informal

Examples

His hilarious story had the whole audience in splits for minutes.
Whenever she tries to dance, it leaves everyone in splits.
The show was so funny that we were in splits throughout.

Synset Examples

Her antics left us all in splits.
The movie was so funny, I was in splits.
The comedian had everyone in splits with his jokes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to crack up - to begin laughing suddenly and uncontrollably
  • Synonym: in stitches - laughing very hard; unable to stop laughing
