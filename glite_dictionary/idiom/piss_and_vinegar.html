piss and vinegar

1. NOUN

/ pɪs ənd ˈvɪnɪɡər / (AmE), / pɪs ənd ˈvɪnɪɡə / (BrE)
a great deal of energy, enthusiasm, or irritability, often in a feisty or spirited way
energy or spirited enthusiasm (idiom)
informal vulgar

Examples

The new manager came in full of piss and vinegar, eager to make bold changes.
Even at eighty, she still has plenty of piss and vinegar and rarely sits still.
After the victory, the whole team was bubbling with piss and vinegar.
