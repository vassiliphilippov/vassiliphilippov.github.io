to sleep with the fishes

1. VERB

/ sliːp wɪð ðə ˈfɪʃɪz / (AmE), / sliːp wɪð ðə ˈfɪʃɪz / (BrE)
to be killed and disposed of in water, often used humorously to suggest someone's death at the hands of criminals
to be killed and disposed of in water

Examples

If you cross the wrong people, you might sleep with the fishes.
Rumors spread that the missing informant now sleeps with the fishes.
He jokingly threatened that anyone who cheated him would sleep with the fishes.

Synset Examples

After the betrayal, he joked he might as well sleep with the fishes.
The mob boss warned him he'd end up sleeping with the fishes.
If he doesn't pay up, he'll be sleeping with the fishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: die - to stop living
  • Synonym: murder - the unlawful killing of another person

Etymology

Popularized by the phrase in the film The Godfather (1972), referencing Mafia euphemism.
