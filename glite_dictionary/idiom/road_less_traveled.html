road less traveled

1. NOUN

/ roʊd lɛs ˈtrævəld / (AmE), / rəʊd lɛs ˈtravəld / (BrE)
an unconventional or unpopular choice; a course of action not commonly pursued by others
an unconventional or unpopular choice

Examples

By starting her own eco-friendly business, she took the road less traveled and inspired many others.
He often encourages his students to follow the road less traveled in their careers.
Pursuing art instead of law was definitely the road less traveled for him.

Synset Examples

She chose the road less traveled for her hiking adventure.
He believes the road less traveled is where true freedom lies.
Taking the road less traveled often leads to unique experiences.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: path of least resistance - the easiest or most conventional option

Etymology

Popularized by Robert Frost's poem 'The Road Not Taken' (1916).
