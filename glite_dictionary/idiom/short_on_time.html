short on time

1. ADJ

/ ʃɔrt ɑn taɪm / (AmE), / ʃɔːt ɒn taɪm / (BrE)
having little or not enough time available
not having enough time

Examples

I can't stay for coffee because I'm short on time this morning.
When you're short on time, try preparing quick meals instead of complicated recipes.
She apologized for leaving early, explaining that she was short on time today.

Synset Examples

He is always short on time before meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pressed for time - not having enough time to do something
  • Synonym: short of time - having little time available
