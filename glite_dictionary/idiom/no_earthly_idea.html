no earthly idea

1. NOUN

/ noʊ ˈɜrθli aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈɜːθli aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
no knowledge or understanding at all about something
no knowledge or understanding

Examples

I have no earthly idea how this machine works.
She looked at the document and realized she had no earthly idea what it meant.
We had no earthly idea that the event had been cancelled.

Synset Examples

He had no earthly idea what was going on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no idea - no knowledge or understanding
