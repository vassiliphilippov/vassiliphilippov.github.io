to ride luck

1. VERB

/ raɪd lʌk / (AmE), / raɪd lʌk / (BrE)
to take risks or rely on good fortune to achieve success
take risks or depend on luck

Examples

He tends to ride luck when making business investments, hoping things turn out well.
Sometimes you just have to ride luck and see where it takes you.
She managed to win the competition by riding luck and trusting her instincts.

Synset Examples

She often rides her luck with last-minute decisions.
