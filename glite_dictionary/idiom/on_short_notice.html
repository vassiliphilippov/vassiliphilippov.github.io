on short notice

1. ADV

/ ɔn ʃɔrt ˈnoʊɾɪs / (AmE), / ɒn ʃɔːt ˈnəʊtɪs / (BrE)
with very little time to prepare or arrange something
with little time to prepare

Examples

They asked me to fill in for the speaker on short notice, so I had little time to prepare.
We had to cancel the trip on short notice because of the storm.
She organized the entire meeting on short notice and still did a great job.

Synset Examples

I can’t believe they plan to gow on such short notice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at short notice - with very little time to prepare (variant form)
