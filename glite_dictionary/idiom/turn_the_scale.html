to turn the scale

1. VERB

/ tɝn ðə skeɪl / (AmE), / tɜːn ðə skeɪl / (BrE)
to gain an advantage or to cause a situation to favor one side over another
gain an advantage; tip the balance

Examples

A late goal could turn the scale in their favor and secure victory.
The new evidence might finally turn the scale for the prosecution.
Her calm explanation helped turn the scale during the difficult negotiations.

Synset Examples

They hope to turn the scale on their side soon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tip the scales - to influence the outcome in favor of one side
  • Synonym: tip the balance - to change a situation so that one side is favored
