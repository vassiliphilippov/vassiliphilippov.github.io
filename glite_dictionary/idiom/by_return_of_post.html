by return of post

1. ADP

/ baɪ rɪˈtɝn əv poʊst / (AmE), / baɪ rɪˈtɜːn əv pəʊst / (BrE)
by the next mail delivery; as soon as possible via the next available mail.
by the next mail delivery
british

Examples

The invoice should be paid by return of post to avoid any late charges.
She requested feedback by return of post, stressing the urgency of the matter.
