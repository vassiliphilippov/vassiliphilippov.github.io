in ages

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈeɪdʒɪz / (AmE), / ɪn ˈeɪdʒɪz / (BrE)
for a very long time; after a long period has passed
after a very long time

Examples

I haven't been to the cinema in ages, so I really want to go tonight.
She hadn't laughed that hard in ages.
They hadn't heard from him in ages before he suddenly called.

Synset Examples

I haven't seen you in ages; what's new?
He couldn't believe he hadn't seen her in ages.
I haven't seen you in ages, how are you diddling?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for ages - for a very long time
