all my born days

1. NOUN

/ ɔl maɪ bɔrn deɪz / (AmE), / ɔːl maɪ bɔːn deɪz / (BrE)
all of one's life; during the whole time one has been alive
throughout one's entire life

Examples

In all my born days, I've never met anyone as kind as Mrs. Jenkins.
I have never tasted anything so delicious in all my born days.
He said he had never heard such nonsense in all his born days.

Synset Examples

This is the worst weather I've experienced in all my born days.
I've never seen anything like this in all my born days.
