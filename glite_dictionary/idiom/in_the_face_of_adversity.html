in the face of adversity

1. X

/ ɪn ðə feɪs əv ədˈvɜrsəti / (AmE), / ɪn ðə feɪs əv ədˈvɜːsəti / (BrE)
When confronted with or experiencing difficult or challenging conditions.
when facing difficulties

Examples

She remained optimistic in the face of adversity and never gave up on her dreams.
The community pulled together in the face of adversity after the devastating storm.
Many great leaders have shown strength in the face of adversity throughout history.

Synset Examples

The Human spirit is resilient in the face of adversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: against all odds - despite extremely low chances of success or survival
  • Synonym: in difficult circumstances - while conditions are hard or unfavorable
