to give a rat

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ə ræt / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə ræt / (BrE)
(informal) To care at all about something; to have any concern or interest.
care at all (informal idiom)
informal

Examples

I don't give a rat what people say about my clothes.
Do you really give a rat about their opinion?
She never gives a rat about deadlines, which annoys her coworkers.

Synset Examples

He doesn't give a rats what others think of him.
I don’t give a rats about the latest gossip.
She really does give a rats about her community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to give a damn - to care at all; to have any concern
  • Synonym: care - feel concern or interest; attach importance to something
