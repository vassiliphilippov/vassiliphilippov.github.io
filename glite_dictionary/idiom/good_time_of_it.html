good time of it

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd taɪm əv ɪt / (AmE), / ɡʊd taɪm əv ɪt / (BrE)
to have an enjoyable or pleasant experience in a particular situation or period
to have an enjoyable experience

Examples

He really had a good time of it during his trip to Europe.
If you plan ahead, you'll have a good time of it at the party.
Despite the rain, the children managed to have a good time of it at the park.
