to call it a crock

1. VERB

/ kɔl ɪt ə krɑk / (AmE), / kɔːl ɪt ə krɒk / (BrE)
to dismiss something as nonsense or untrue
dismiss as nonsense
informal

Examples

Many critics call it a crock, arguing that there is no scientific proof for the theory.
When she heard his excuse, she immediately called it a crock and refused to listen further.
Some people still call it a crock whenever new technology is introduced.

Synset Examples

He called it a crock when she suggested he cook.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: call it nonsense - to describe something as nonsense
