to put one's mind to it

1. VERB

/ pʊt wʌnz maɪnd tu ɪt / (AmE), / pʊt wʌnz maɪnd tʊ ɪt / (BrE)
to focus all one's attention and effort on achieving or accomplishing something
focus effort to achieve something

Examples

If you really put your mind to it, you can finish the project early.
He achieved his goal because he put his mind to it and worked hard every day.
Anyone can learn a new language if they put their mind to it.

Synset Examples

She can do anything if she puts her mind to it.
If you put your mind to it, you can succeed.
You should put your mind to it and finish the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concentrate - to focus one's mental effort or attention
