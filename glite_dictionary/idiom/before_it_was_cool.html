before it was cool

1. ADV

/ bɪˈfɔr ɪt wəz kuːl / (AmE), / bɪˈfɔːr ɪt wəz kuːl / (BrE)
used to indicate that someone did or liked something before it became fashionable or popular
before something became fashionable or trendy
informal

Examples

He was wearing vintage sneakers before it was cool.
She started using reusable bags before it was cool.
They supported the indie band before it was cool.

Synset Examples

He started hiking before it was cool to do so.
I was listening to that band before it was cool.
