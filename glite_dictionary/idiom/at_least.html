at least

1. ADV

/ ət ˈlist / (AmE), / ət ˈliːst / (BrE)
used to indicate the smallest or minimal amount or to introduce a positive aspect when the overall situation is less than ideal
to indicate a minimal acceptable outcome

Examples

The weather was terrible, but at least we arrived safely.
He forgot my birthday, but at least he called to apologize later.
It may not be much, but at least it’s a start.

Synset Examples

Oh well, at least we learned something.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: at most - indicates the largest or maximal amount
