to hide the sausage

1. VERB

/ haɪd ðə ˈsɔsɪdʒ / (AmE), / haɪd ðə ˈsɒsɪdʒ / (BrE)
(humorous, sometimes euphemism, slang) To conceal something; often used as a euphemism for sexual intercourse or innuendo.
conceal something; sexual euphemism
informal slang
