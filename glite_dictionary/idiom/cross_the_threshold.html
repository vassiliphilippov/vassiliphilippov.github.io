to cross the threshold

1. VERB

/ krɑs ðə ˈθrɛʃhoʊld / (AmE), / krɒs ðə ˈθrɛʃhəʊld / (BrE)
to enter a new stage or phase of life, development, or experience; to begin something new
enter a new phase or stage

Examples

After years of hard work, she finally crossed the threshold into a successful career.
When their child left for college, they felt they had crossed the threshold into a new chapter of life.
He crossed the threshold from student to professional when he received his first job offer.

Synset Examples

He crossed the threshold into adulthood last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enter a new stage - to move into a different phase or period
