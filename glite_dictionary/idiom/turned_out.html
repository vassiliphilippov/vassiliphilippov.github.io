turned out

1. ADJ

/ tɝnd aʊt / (AmE), / tɜːnd aʊt / (BrE)
dressed in a neat, stylish, or impressive way
dressed smartly or stylishly

Examples

The students were turned out in their best uniforms for the ceremony.
He was always turned out impeccably, even on casual Fridays.
Everyone at the wedding was beautifully turned out for the occasion.

Synset Examples

the girls were well turned out and smart

Related Terms

  • Synonym: well-dressed - wearing smart or attractive clothes
