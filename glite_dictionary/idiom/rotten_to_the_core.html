rotten to the core

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɑːtən tə ðə kɔr / (AmE), / ˈrɒt.ən tə ðə kɔː / (BrE)
completely corrupt, dishonest, or morally bad to the very center or essence.
utterly corrupt or morally bad

Examples

Many believed the company was rotten to the core after years of scandals and cover-ups.
The investigation revealed that the political system was rotten to the core.
She realized too late that the charity was rotten to the core and a front for fraud.

Synset Examples

That deal was rotten to the core from the beginning.
His lies proved he was rotten to the core.
The organization was rotten to the core.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corrupt - willing to act dishonestly for personal gain
  • Antonym: upright - honest and morally good
