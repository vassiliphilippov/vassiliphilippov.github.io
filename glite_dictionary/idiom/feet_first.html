feet first

1. ADV

/ fit ˈfɝst / (AmE), / fiːt ˈfɜːst / (BrE)
With eagerness and boldness; entering a situation enthusiastically and without hesitation.
with boldness or eagerness

Examples

James dived feet first into his new career, ready to face any challenge.
She often takes on responsibility feet first, not waiting for someone else to lead.
The young entrepreneurs went feet first into launching their own business.

Synset Examples

He approached the challenge feet first, without hesitation.
She tackled the project feet first, eager to start.
He jumped feet first into the swimming pool.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headfirst - with the head leading; boldly or rashly
