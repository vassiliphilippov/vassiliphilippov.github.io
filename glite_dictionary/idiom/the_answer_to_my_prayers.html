the answer to my prayers

1. NOUN

/ ði ˈænsɚ tuː maɪ prɛrz / (AmE), / ði ˈɑːnsə tuː maɪ preəz / (BrE)
a thing or person that provides exactly what is needed or desired, especially as a solution to a major problem or difficulty
exactly what is needed or wished for

Examples

Winning the scholarship was the answer to my prayers after months of financial worry.
The new medication turned out to be the answer to my prayers for pain relief.
When the mechanic fixed the car so quickly, it was the answer to my prayers before my big meeting.

Synset Examples

His timely advice was the answer to my prayers.
The new job was the answer to my prayers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: godsend - a valuable or welcome person or thing, especially when unexpected
  • Synonym: miracle - an extraordinary or unexpected event, often welcomed as a solution
