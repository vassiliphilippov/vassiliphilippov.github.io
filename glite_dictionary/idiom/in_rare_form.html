in rare form

1. ADJ

/ ɪn rɛr fɔrm / (AmE), / ɪn rɛə fɔːm / (BrE)
behaving or performing in an exceptionally energetic, impressive, or unusual way
performing exceptionally well or unusually

Examples

The tennis player was in rare form during the final match, winning every set with ease.
Whenever she’s in rare form, her stories have everyone captivated.
Our chef is in rare form tonight—the meal was absolutely outstanding.

Synset Examples

He’s in rare form today, full of energy and jokes.
The comedian was in rare form, making everyone laugh.
She was in rare form at the party last night.
