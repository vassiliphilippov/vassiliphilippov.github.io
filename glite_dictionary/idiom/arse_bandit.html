arse bandit

1. NOUN

/ ɑːrs ˈbændɪt / (AmE), / ɑːs ˈbændɪt / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) An insulting term for an annoying person, thief, or (offensively) a homosexual man, often used humorously or abusively.
insulting term for an annoying or disliked person
slang vulgar offensive british

Examples

Someone called me an arse bandit after I won the game for the third time in a row.
He can be a real arse bandit when he's trying to get his way.
Don't let that arse bandit take credit for your work!

Synset Examples

That arse bandit stole my parking spot!
I can't believe that arse bandit cut in line.
He's just an arse bandit pretending to be nice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ass bandit - offensive term used similarly in American English

Etymology

From British English 'arse' (buttocks) + 'bandit' (thief or outlaw), originally used as a derogatory term for homosexual men.
