to eat like a pig

1. VERB

/ it laɪk ə pɪɡ / (AmE), / iːt laɪk ə pɪɡ / (BrE)
to eat in a very messy or greedy way, often consuming a lot of food
eat messily or greedily

Examples

He tends to eat like a pig when there's pizza involved.
If you eat like a pig at the party, people will notice your lack of manners.
She was embarrassed to realize she had eaten like a pig in front of her date.

Synset Examples

I can't believe you ate like a pig at the buffet.
She didn't hold back and ate like a pig at dinner.
He tends to eat like a pig during holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to wolf down - to eat something very quickly and greedily
  • Antonym: eat daintily - to eat in a delicate and refined way
