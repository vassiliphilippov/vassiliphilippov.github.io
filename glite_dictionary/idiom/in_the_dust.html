in the dust

1. X

/ ɪn ðə dʌst / (AmE), / ɪn ðə dʌst / (BrE)
far ahead of others or outpacing them so much that they are left behind
far ahead of others

Examples

After months of hard work, the team's innovation left its competitors in the dust.
Her new book soared up the bestseller list, leaving the previous bestsellers in the dust.
With his quick wit and sharp answers, he left the rest of the contestants in the dust during the debate.

Synset Examples

The car was a beast on the racetrack, leaving competitors in the dust.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ahead - in front or further forward in progress
  • Antonym: behind - at the back or not as far ahead
