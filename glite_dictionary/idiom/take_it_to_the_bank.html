to take it to the bank

1. VERB

/ teɪk ɪt tə ðə bæŋk / (AmE), / teɪk ɪt tə ðə bæŋk / (BrE)
to be certain that something is true or will happen; to trust something completely
be confident that something is true or certain

Examples

You can take it to the bank that this plan will succeed if everyone follows the instructions.
If he says he'll be on time, you can take it to the bank.
Whenever she gives you financial advice, you can take it to the bank.

Synset Examples

If he says he'll help, you can take it to the bank.
Her advice is solid; you can take it to the bank.
You can take that promise to the bank.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bet your life - be absolutely sure about something
