1. X

/ dʌz ə bɛr kræp ɪn ðə wʊdz / (AmE), / dʌz ə beə kræp ɪn ðə wʊdz / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) Used to indicate that the answer to a question is obviously or emphatically yes.

informal vulgar
