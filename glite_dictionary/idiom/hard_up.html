hard up

1. ADJ

/ hɑrd ʌp / (AmE), / hɑːd ʌp / (BrE)
lacking enough money to pay for basic needs; having very little money.
having little or no money

Examples

After losing his job, Tom was hard up and struggled to pay his bills.
Many students are hard up at the end of the semester.
When you're hard up, even a small expense can seem overwhelming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broke - having no money
  • Degree Higher: destitute - lacking the basic necessities of life
