look ahere

1. X

/ lʊk əˈhɪr / (AmE), / lʊk əˈhɪə / (BrE)
An informal way of telling someone to pay attention or focus on what is being pointed out or said.
informal expression to direct attention
informal

Examples

Look ahere, you need to listen to what I'm saying.
If you're lost, just look ahere for the instructions on the wall.
Look ahere, this isn't the way we do things around here.

Synset Examples

Look ahere for the answers to your questions.
Look ahere, this is the best part of the tour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: look here - used to direct someone's attention or express emphasis
