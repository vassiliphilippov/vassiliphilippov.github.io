like Speedy Gonzales

1. ADV

/ laɪk ˌspiːdi ɡɒnˈzæləs / (AmE), / laɪk ˌspiːdi ɡɒnˈzɑːliːz / (BrE)
very quickly; in a rapid or speedy manner (in reference to the fast cartoon character Speedy Gonzales)
very quickly; rapidly (allusion to a cartoon character)
informal

Examples

He darted out of the room like Speedy Gonzales when he heard the fire alarm.
The team finished the project like Speedy Gonzales, surprising their manager with their speed.
Whenever she hears the ice cream truck, she runs outside like Speedy Gonzales.

Synset Examples

She finished her homework like Speedy Gonzales.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: like lightning - very quickly; at great speed
  • Synonym: like a flash - in an instant; extremely quickly
  • Antonym: slowly - at a low speed; not quickly

Etymology

Refers to Speedy Gonzales, a Mexican cartoon mouse known for his speed, from Warner Bros. cartoons.
