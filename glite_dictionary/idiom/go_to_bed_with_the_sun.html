to go to bed with the sun

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ tə bɛd wɪð ðə sʌn / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə bɛd wɪð ðə sʌn / (BrE)
to go to sleep early in the evening, as the sun sets
go to sleep at sunset

Examples

Many rural families still go to bed with the sun during the harvest season.
After switching off all electronics, she decided to go to bed with the sun for a week.
If you go to bed with the sun, you often wake up feeling refreshed in the morning.

Synset Examples

The farmer goes to bed with the sun to rise early.
In summer, we often go to bed with the sun.
After a long hike, I prefer to go to bed with the sun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turn in early - go to sleep earlier than usual
  • Antonym: stay up late - go to bed much later than usual
