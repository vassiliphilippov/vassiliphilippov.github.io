to give someone the eye

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ˈsʌmwʌn ði aɪ / (AmE), / ɡɪv ˈsʌmwən ðiː aɪ / (BrE)
to look at someone in a way that shows romantic or sexual interest
look at with romantic or sexual interest

Examples

Every time he walked into the café, someone would give him the eye.
She noticed Mark giving her the eye from across the room.
If you give someone the eye, they might come over and talk to you.

Synset Examples

She gave him the eye, indicating he should come over.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make eyes at - to look at someone with sexual or romantic interest
