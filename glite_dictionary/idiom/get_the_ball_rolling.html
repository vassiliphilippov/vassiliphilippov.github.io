to get the ball rolling

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ðə bɔl ˈroʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡɛt ðə bɔːl ˈrəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
to start an activity, process, or event by taking the first action
to start an activity or process

Examples

Once she got the ball rolling, the team quickly joined in and contributed their ideas.
Can someone get the ball rolling by sharing their thoughts first?
