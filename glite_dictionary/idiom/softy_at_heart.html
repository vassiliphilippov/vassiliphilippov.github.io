softy at heart

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔfti æt hɑrt / (AmE), / ˈsɒfti æt hɑːt / (BrE)
A person who is genuinely kind and gentle, even if they appear tough or unemotional on the outside.
someone kind and gentle underneath

Examples

Although he acts stern at work, he's really a softy at heart.
Everyone thought Maria was strict, but she proved to be a softy at heart when she adopted the stray cat.
Despite his gruff exterior, Jack is a softy at heart who donates to charity every month.

Synset Examples

He’s a softy at heart, always helping others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: softy - someone who is easily moved emotionally; a sentimental person
  • Antonym: tough at heart - someone who is genuinely strong or resilient in their nature
