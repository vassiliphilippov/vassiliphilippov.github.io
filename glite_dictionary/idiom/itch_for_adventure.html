to itch for adventure

1. VERB

/ ɪtʃ fər ədˈvɛntʃər / (AmE), / ɪtʃ fə rəˈvɛntʃə / (BrE)
to have a strong and restless desire for excitement or new experiences
strong desire for excitement or novelty

Examples

After months of routine at work, she began to itch for adventure and started planning a trip abroad.
Many young people itch for adventure when they finish school and want to see the world.
Though he enjoyed his stable life, there was always an itch for adventure he couldn't quite satisfy.

Synset Examples

He had an itch for adventure that couldn't be ignored.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: itch - a restless desire (for something)
