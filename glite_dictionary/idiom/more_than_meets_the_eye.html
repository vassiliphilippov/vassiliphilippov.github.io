more than meets the eye

1. X

/ mɔr ðən mits ði aɪ / (AmE), / mɔː ðən miːts ðiː aɪ / (BrE)
used to say that something is more complex or interesting than it appears at first glance
more complex than it appears

Examples

There is more than meets the eye to that old building; it has a fascinating history.
At first, the puzzle seems easy, but there's more than meets the eye.
Her explanation sounded sincere, but I felt there was more than meets the eye.

Synset Examples

This painting has more than meets the eye.
The job offers more than meets the eye.
Her story had more than meets the eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deceptive - giving an appearance or impression different from the true one
  • Antonym: obvious - easily perceived or understood; clear, self-evident, or apparent
