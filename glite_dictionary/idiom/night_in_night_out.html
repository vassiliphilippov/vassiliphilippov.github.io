night in, night out

1. ADV

/ naɪt ɪn naɪt aʊt / (AmE), / naɪt ɪn naɪt aʊt / (BrE)
happening every night over a period of time; done consistently every night
every single night; consistently

Examples

He works late at the office night in, night out to meet deadlines.
They practiced dancing night in, night out for the upcoming performance.
She reads to the children night in, night out as part of their bedtime routine.

Synset Examples

He studies night in, night out to pass his exams.
They watch movies night in, night out during winter.
She jogs night in, night out to stay fit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: day in, day out - happening every day over a period of time
