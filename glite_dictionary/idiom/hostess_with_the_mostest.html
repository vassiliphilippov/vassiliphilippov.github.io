hostess with the mostest

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊstɪs wɪð ðə ˈmoʊstɪst / (AmE), / ˈhəʊstɪs wɪð ðə ˈməʊstɪst / (BrE)
a woman who is exceptionally good at entertaining guests and making them feel welcome
exceptional female host
informal american

Examples

Everyone loves coming to her parties because she is the hostess with the mostest.
With her attention to detail and warm personality, she truly earned the title of hostess with the mostest.
At every event, she proves herself to be the hostess with the mostest, making sure everyone feels at home.

Synset Examples

Everyone loves her; she's the hostess with the mostest.
She’s the hostess with the mostest, always charming her guests.
As the hostess with the mostest, she knows how to entertain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life of the party - person who is lively and entertaining at social events
  • Antonym: wallflower - person who is shy or unpopular at parties; not sociable

Etymology

Popularized in the 1950s, originating as a rhyming catchphrase in American English.
