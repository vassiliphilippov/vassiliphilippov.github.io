real McCoy

1. NOUN

/ ˌriːəl məˈkɔɪ / (AmE), / ˌrɪəl məˈkɔɪ / (BrE)
the genuine article; something or someone that is authentic and not a copy or imitation
the genuine or authentic thing or person
informal

Examples

Collectors always want the real McCoy and not a cheap replica.
When she showed her credentials, we knew she was the real McCoy.
That antique vase is the real McCoy—it dates back to the 18th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genuine article - something that is real and authentic, not an imitation
  • Antonym: imitation - a copy or something made to look like something else, not original

Etymology

Possibly from the Scottish phrase 'the real MacKay', later popularized in America as 'the real McCoy'.
