to be a hardo

1. VERB

/ bi ə ˈhɑrˌdoʊ / (AmE), / bi ə ˈhɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
to behave in a way that is overly tough, serious, or earnest, often to the point of being off-putting
act overly tough or serious
informal slang

Examples

Stop trying to be a hardo—just relax and have fun with us.
There's no need to be a hardo about this assignment; everyone makes mistakes.
He tends to be a hardo at the gym, always lifting more than necessary.

Synset Examples

Don’t be a hardo; just enjoy the party!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to show off - to behave in a way that is intended to attract attention or admiration, often by exaggerating abilities
