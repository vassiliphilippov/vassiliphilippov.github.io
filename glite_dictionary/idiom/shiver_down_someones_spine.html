shiver down someone's spine

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪvɚ daʊn ˈsʌmwʌnz spaɪn / (AmE), / ˈʃɪvə daʊn ˈsʌmwənz spaɪn / (BrE)
a sudden feeling of fear, excitement, or unease, often experienced as a physical sensation along the back
sudden feeling of fear or unease

Examples

The eerie silence sent a shiver down my spine as I walked through the old house.
Just thinking about the accident gives me a shiver down my spine.
The ghost story was so terrifying it sent a shiver down everyone's spine.

Synset Examples

The chilling music sent shivers down my spine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: send a chill down someone's spine - to cause someone to feel sudden fear or excitement
