a hatful of surprises

1. NOUN

/ ə ˈhæt.fəl əv səˈpraɪ.zɪz / (AmE), / ə ˈhæt.fʊl əv səˈpraɪ.zɪz / (BrE)
an unexpectedly large number of surprises or unexpected occurrences
a large number of surprises

Examples

The new game is a hatful of surprises from start to finish.
Every report from the expedition brought a hatful of surprises to the audience.
Her birthday party turned into a hatful of surprises that no one expected.

Synset Examples

The event was full of a hatful of surprises.
