clever clogs

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɛvər klɑɡz / (AmE), / ˈklɛvə klɒɡz / (BrE)
a person who is irritatingly or ostentatiously knowledgeable or intelligent, especially in a way that shows off to others
an irritatingly smart or know-it-all person
british informal

Examples

Don’t be such a clever clogs — nobody likes a know-it-all in the group.
Whenever we have a quiz, Sarah turns into a total clever clogs.
He always acts like a clever clogs in meetings, correcting everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: know-it-all - a person who acts as if they know everything
  • Synonym: smart aleck - someone who is irritatingly clever or smart-mouthed
