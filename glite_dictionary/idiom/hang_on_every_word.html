to hang on every word

1. VERB

/ hæŋ ɑn ˈɛvri wɝd / (AmE), / hæŋ ɒn ˈɛvri wɜːd / (BrE)
to listen very attentively to what someone is saying, paying close attention to each word
listen with intense attention

Examples

The children hung on every word during the exciting story.
She hangs on every word when her favorite author speaks.
They were hanging on every word as the coach explained the final strategy.

Synset Examples

  • Antonym: to tune out - to stop listening or paying attention
