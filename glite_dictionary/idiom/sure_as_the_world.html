sure as the world

1. X

/ ʃʊr æz ðə wɝld / (AmE), / ʃɔːr əz ðə wɜːld / (BrE)
Absolutely certain; guaranteed without doubt.
absolutely certain; without doubt

Examples

If you forget your umbrella, it’ll rain, sure as the world.
He’ll be late again, sure as the world.
You’ll regret it, sure as the world, if you don’t apologize now.
