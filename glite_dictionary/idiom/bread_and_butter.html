bread and butter

1. NOUN

/ brɛd ənd ˈbʌtər / (AmE), / brɛd ənd ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
a person's main source of income or means of earning a living; livelihood
main source of income

Examples

For many artists, teaching is their bread and butter while they pursue their creative projects.
Freelance work became his bread and butter after he lost his office job.
Her bread and butter comes from writing articles for several online magazines.

Synset Examples

each child was expected to pay for their keep
he applied to the state for support
he could no longer earn his own livelihood

Related Terms

  • Synonym: livelihood - means of securing the necessities of life
  • Synonym: income - money received, especially on a regular basis, for work or through investments
  • Antonym: windfall - a sum of money received unexpectedly

Etymology

Originally referred to the basics of a meal, later coming to mean the essentials of one's livelihood.
