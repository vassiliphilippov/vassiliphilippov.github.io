gas on a stick

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ɑn ə stɪk / (AmE), / ɡas ɒn ə stɪk / (BrE)
an expression describing something extremely unpleasant or difficult to deal with, especially in terms of taste or odor
extremely unpleasant thing (taste, odor)
informal

Examples

The blue cheese tasted like gas on a stick, and I had to spit it out.
When the garbage truck passed, the air turned into gas on a stick.
Trying that new energy drink was like swallowing gas on a stick.

Synset Examples

I couldn't finish my meal; it was like gas on a stick.
That new dessert tasted like gas on a stick.
The smell was so strong, it felt like gas on a stick.
