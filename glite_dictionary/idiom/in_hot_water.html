in hot water

1. ADV

/ ɪn hɑt ˈwɔtɚ / (AmE), / ɪn hɒt ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
in trouble or experiencing difficulties, especially as a result of one's actions
in trouble; facing difficulties

Examples

If you keep lying to your boss, you'll end up in hot water.
The company got in hot water with regulators over safety violations.
She knows she'll be in hot water if her parents find out.

Synset Examples

She was in hot water for breaking the rules.
He found himself in hot water after missing the deadline.
They landed in hot water for their reckless behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the doghouse - in trouble or out of favor
