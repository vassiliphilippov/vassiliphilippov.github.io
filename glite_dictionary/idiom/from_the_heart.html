from the heart

1. ADV

/ frəm ðə hɑrt / (AmE), / frəm ðə hɑːt / (BrE)
in a very sincere and genuine way, without pretense or affectation
sincerely; genuinely

Examples

She thanked everyone from the heart for their support during a difficult time.
His apology came from the heart and was accepted by all.
When she spoke about her family, it was clear the words were from the heart.

Synset Examples

he spoke from the heart

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartfelt - expressed with deep sincerity
