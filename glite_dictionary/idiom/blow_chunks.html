to blow chunks

1. VERB

/ bloʊ tʃʌŋks / (AmE), / bləʊ tʃʌŋks / (BrE)
(informal, slang) To vomit with force, often as a result of nausea or illness.
to vomit forcefully (slang)
informal slang

Examples

After seeing the gruesome movie scene, he had to blow chunks in the bathroom.
She felt so seasick on the boat that she nearly blew chunks.
If you eat twelve hot dogs in five minutes, you might end up blowing chunks.

Synset Examples

After the rollercoaster, he felt sick and blew chunks.
The dog ate something bad and blew chunks all over the floor.
She ate too much candy and ended up blowing chunks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vomit - to eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth
  • Synonym: to throw up - (informal) to vomit
