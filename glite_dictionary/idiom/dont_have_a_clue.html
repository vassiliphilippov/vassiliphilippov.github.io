to don't have a clue

1. VERB

/ doʊnt hæv ə kluː / (AmE), / dəʊnt hav ə kluː / (BrE)
to have no knowledge or understanding about something; to have no idea at all.
to have no idea or knowledge
informal

Examples

I don't have a clue how to fix this computer problem.
She doesn't have a clue what they're talking about.
They asked me for directions, but I didn't have a clue.

Synset Examples

I don't have a clue where she went.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: have no idea - to not know or understand something at all
