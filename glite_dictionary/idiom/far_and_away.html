far and away

1. ADV

/ fɑr ənd əˈweɪ / (AmE), / fɑː ənd əˈweɪ / (BrE)
by a very large amount; by a considerable margin, used to emphasize a superlative degree.
by a considerable margin

Examples

That was far and away the best performance of the evening.
She is far and away the most qualified candidate for the position.
This is far and away the most comfortable chair I've ever owned.

Synset Examples

she was by far the smartest student
it was far and away the best meal he had ever eaten

Related Terms

  • Synonym: by far - by a considerable amount

2. ADV

/ fɑr ənd əˈweɪ / (AmE), / fɑː ənd əˈweɪ / (BrE)
by a very large amount; by a wide margin; definitely
by a wide margin; definitely

Examples

This is far and away the best pizza I've ever tasted.
She was far and away the most talented artist at the exhibition.
His latest film is far and away more successful than his previous works.

Synset Examples

This is far and away my favorite book of all time.
She is far and away the best candidate for the job.
He is far and away the fastest runner in the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: by far - by a great amount; much
