wet firecracker

1. NOUN

/ wɛt ˈfaɪɚˌkrækɚ / (AmE), / wɛt ˈfaɪəˌkrækə / (BrE)
a person or thing that fails to excite or impress; someone or something lacking energy or enthusiasm
person or thing lacking excitement or energy

Examples

Unfortunately, the grand opening turned out to be a wet firecracker with barely any guests showing up.
The much-anticipated sequel was a wet firecracker and didn't live up to the hype.
He tried to energize the audience, but his jokes landed like a wet firecracker.

Synset Examples

After that speech, the crowd felt like a wet firecracker.
The party was a wet firecracker, lacking excitement.
Her enthusiasm faded, making the event feel like a wet firecracker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: damp squib - an event or thing that is disappointing or not as exciting as expected
  • Synonym: letdown - something that is disappointing
  • Synonym: failure - lack of success
