to grin like the cat that ate the canary

1. VERB

/ ɡɹɪn laɪk ðə kæt ðət eɪt ðə kəˈnɛɹi / (AmE), / ɡɹɪn laɪk ðə kæt ðət eɪt ðə kəˈneəri / (BrE)
to smile with a self-satisfied or smug expression, as if pleased with one's own cleverness or secret success
smile smugly or self-satisfiedly

Examples

He walked into the meeting with a grin like the cat that ate the canary, clearly pleased with himself.
After hearing the news, she couldn't help but wear a grin like the cat that ate the canary.
Whenever he manages to close a big deal, he gets that unmistakable grin like the cat that ate the canary.

Synset Examples

After winning the award, he wore a grin like the cat that ate the canary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smirk - to smile in an irritatingly smug or conceited way

Etymology

Alludes to the image of a cat appearing smug after catching and eating a canary, first recorded in early 20th-century American English.
