sledgehammer to crack a nut

1. X

/ ˈslɛdʒˌhæmər tə krǽk ə nʌt / (AmE), / ˈslɛdʒˌhamə tə ˈkræk ə nʌt / (BrE)
to use far more force or resources than necessary to accomplish a simple or minor task
use excessive force for a trivial problem

Examples

Introducing a complicated new system was a sledgehammer to crack a nut for such a minor issue.
Rewriting all the software seemed like a sledgehammer to crack a nut for fixing a small bug.
Hiring a lawyer over a simple misunderstanding is using a sledgehammer to crack a nut.

Synset Examples

Don't use a sledgehammer to crack a nut on this small task.
His approach seemed like using a sledgehammer to crack a nut.
She realized she was using a sledgehammer to crack a nut with that solution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overkill - use of excessive resources beyond what is needed

Etymology

From the literal image of using a heavy sledgehammer to open a small nut, emphasizing overkill.
