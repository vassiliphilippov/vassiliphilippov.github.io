a closed mouth gathers no feet

1. X

/ ə kloʊzd maʊθ ˈɡæðərz noʊ fiːt / (AmE), / ə kləʊzd maʊθ ˈɡæðəz nəʊ fiːt / (BrE)
remaining silent avoids making mistakes or getting into trouble; one cannot say something foolish if one does not speak
it is safer to remain silent than to risk saying something foolish

Examples

After embarrassing himself in front of his boss, Tom realized that a closed mouth gathers no feet.
Whenever arguments start at family dinners, my grandmother always says, 'A closed mouth gathers no feet.'
If you are unsure what to say, remember that a closed mouth gathers no feet.

Synset Examples

He learned that a closed mouth gathers no feet after his blunder.
She stayed silent during the meeting; a closed mouth gathers no feet.
Remember, a closed mouth gathers no feet when gossip starts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keep your mouth shut - stay silent; do not speak
  • Antonym: put your foot in your mouth - say something embarrassing or inappropriate
