where the bodies are buried

1. X

/ wɛɹ ðə ˈbɑdiz ɑɹ ˈbɛɹid / (AmE), / wɛə ðə ˈbɒdiz ɑː ˈbɛrid / (BrE)
to know the secret, often unpleasant, details of a situation, organization, or past events, especially those that are hidden from outsiders
knowledge of hidden secrets or insider information

Examples

As the former manager, he knows where the bodies are buried in this company.
Only a few trusted staff really know where the bodies are buried.
If you want to fix the system, you must find out where the bodies are buried.
