what the heck

1. INTJ

/ wʌt ðə hɛk / (AmE), / wɒt ðə hɛk / (BrE)
used to express surprise, disbelief, or confusion.
expression of surprise or disbelief
informal

Examples

What the heck is going on in here?
He forgot my birthday again, and I thought, 'what the heck?'
If you want to try something new, just say 'what the heck' and go for it.

Synset Examples

What the heck are you doing here?
You want to quit now? What the heck!
You ate the last cookie? What the heck!
I can't believe what the heck just happened!
She couldn’t believe it, saying, 'What the heck?'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: what the hell - expression of surprise or disbelief (stronger variation)
