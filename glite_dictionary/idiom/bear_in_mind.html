to bear in mind

1. VERB

/ bɛr ɪn maɪnd / (AmE), / beə ɪn maɪnd / (BrE)
to remember or consider something when making a decision or judgment
remember or consider something

Examples

Please bear in mind that the deadline is tomorrow.
When making your decision, bear in mind how much it will cost.
She always bears in mind the needs of her team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to keep in mind - to remember or consider something when making decisions
  • Antonym: to forget - to fail to remember

2. VERB

/ bɛr ɪn maɪnd / (AmE), / beə ɪn maɪnd / (BrE)
to remember or consider something when making a decision or thinking about something
to remember or consider

Examples

Bear in mind that the office will be closed on public holidays.
It's important to bear in mind the risks before investing your money.
We should bear in mind his advice during the meeting.

Synset Examples

I always bear in mind what you said.

You should bear in mind your budget when shopping.
