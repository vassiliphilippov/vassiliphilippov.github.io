to get one's panties in a pretzel

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt wʌnz ˈpæntiz ɪn ə ˈprɛtsəl / (AmE), / ɡɛt wʌnz ˈpæntiːz ɪn ə ˈprɛtsl̩ / (BrE)
to become overly upset or anxious, especially about something minor or trivial
become overly upset or anxious
informal

Examples

Every time there's a small delay, he gets his panties in a pretzel and starts complaining.
Don't get your panties in a pretzel over a little mistake; it's not the end of the world.
She tends to get her panties in a pretzel when things don't go exactly as planned.

Synset Examples

He got his panties in a pretzel when things didn’t go as planned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get one's knickers in a twist - become unduly upset or agitated (British idiom)
