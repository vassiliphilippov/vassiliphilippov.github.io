to put on a game face

1. VERB

/ pʊt ɑn ə ɡeɪm feɪs / (AmE), / pʊt ɒn ə ɡeɪm feɪs / (BrE)
to adopt a confident or determined attitude, especially in preparation for a challenge or competition
adopt a determined or confident attitude

Examples

Before walking into the negotiating room, she put on a game face to hide her nerves.
Athletes often put on a game face moments before a big match.
It's important to put on a game face when you're facing tough competition.

Synset Examples

He put on his game face before the big presentation.
You need to wear your game face to compete.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: game face - the expression showing determination or seriousness, especially before a challenge
