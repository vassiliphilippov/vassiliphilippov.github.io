in the offing

1. ADV

/ ɪn ði ˈɔf.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪn ði ˈɒf.ɪŋ / (BrE)
likely to happen or appear very soon; imminent or about to occur.
imminent; likely to happen soon

Examples

With the rumor of layoffs circulating, everyone sensed that big changes were in the offing.
A breakthrough in the negotiations could be in the offing by next week.
There's a new restaurant in the offing just around the corner from my apartment.

Synset Examples

I sense a big change is in the offing.
A new project is in the offing for next year.
A promotion is in the offing for her hard work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imminent - about to happen very soon
  • Synonym: impending - about to take place; looming

Etymology

Originally a nautical term; 'offing' meant the part of the sea visible from the shore, where ships would first appear when approaching land.
