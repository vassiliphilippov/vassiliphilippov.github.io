hell on earth

1. NOUN

/ hɛl ɑn ɝθ / (AmE), / hɛl ɒn ɜːθ / (BrE)
a situation or place that causes intense suffering, misery, or chaos
place or situation of great misery

Examples

The city after the bombing was truly hell on earth for its surviving residents.
For years, the prison was described as hell on earth by former inmates.
Working in that toxic environment felt like living in hell on earth every day.

Synset Examples

the hell of battle
the inferno of the engine room
when you're alone Christmas is the pits

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inferno - a place or situation resembling hell, especially in intensity of fire or suffering
  • Antonym: heaven on earth - a situation or place of great happiness and peace
