duck soup

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʌk ˈsuːp / (AmE), / ˌdʌk ˈsuːp / (BrE)
something that is very easy to do or accomplish; a task requiring little effort.
an easy task
informal american

Examples

Convincing them to agree was duck soup compared to what I expected.
For her, solving complex math problems is just duck soup.
Cooking dinner for two people is duck soup when you usually cook for a family.

Synset Examples

marketing this product will be no picnic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piece of cake - an easy task or job
  • Synonym: no-brainer - something that requires little thought or effort
  • Antonym: challenge - a difficult task or problem

Etymology

Possibly from early 20th-century American English slang, suggesting something as easy to eat as duck soup.
