for dummies

1. X

/ fər ˈdʌmiz / (AmE), / fə ˈdʌmiz / (BrE)
used to describe instructional materials or explanations intended for beginners or people with little experience in a particular subject
for beginners; instructional for novices

Examples

This manual is written for dummies who have never used a computer before.
The new app includes a guide for dummies to explain basic functions.
He bought a guitar book for dummies to help him get started.

Synset Examples

This book is perfect for dummies like me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beginner - a person who is just starting to learn something
  • Synonym: novice - a person new to or inexperienced in a field or situation
