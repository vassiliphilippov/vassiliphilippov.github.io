big girl's blouse

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ɡɜrlz blaʊz / (AmE), / bɪɡ ɡɜːlz blaʊz / (BrE)
an informal term for someone regarded as weak, cowardly, or overly sensitive, especially a man
term for someone seen as weak or cowardly
informal british

Examples

Don’t be such a big girl's blouse—it’s only a little spider!
He was always called a big girl's blouse for refusing to play rough sports.
When she saw the exam paper, she turned into a big girl's blouse and wanted to leave.

Synset Examples
