loose as a goose

1. ADJ

/ lus əz ə ɡus / (AmE), / luːs əz ə ɡuːs / (BrE)
very relaxed and carefree; behaving in a free-spirited, easygoing manner
very relaxed and carefree (idiom)
informal

Examples

After the massage, he felt loose as a goose and ready to face the day.
She was loose as a goose during the vacation, not worrying about work at all.
Whenever he's with old friends, Mark becomes loose as a goose and cracks jokes nonstop.

Synset Examples

He was loose as a goose at the party, laughing with everyone.
The kids were running around, loose as a goose in the park.
After a few drinks, she felt loose as a goose and started dancing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laid-back - relaxed and easygoing
  • Antonym: uptight - tense, nervous, or anxious
