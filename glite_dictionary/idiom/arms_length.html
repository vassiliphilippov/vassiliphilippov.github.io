arm's length

1. NOUN

/ ɑrmz lɛŋkθ / (AmE), / ɑːmz leŋθ / (BrE)
a safe or deliberate distance from a person or situation, especially to avoid involvement or influence
a safe or deliberate distance

Examples

She kept the reporters at arm's length to avoid media attention.
They prefer to remain at arm's length from office politics.
It is best to deal with him at arm's length to avoid any trouble.

Synset Examples

They maintained an arm's length from the conflict.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distance - the amount of space between two points
  • Antonym: closeness - the state of being near or involved
