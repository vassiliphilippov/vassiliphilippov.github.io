to not look a day over

1. VERB

/ nɑt lʊk ə deɪ oʊvɚ / (AmE), / nɒt lʊk ə deɪ əʊvə / (BrE)
(of a person) to appear younger than a specified age, especially remarkably so.
appear younger than a given age

Examples

My grandfather does not look a day over sixty, even though he's in his seventies.
She doesn't look a day over thirty, thanks to her healthy lifestyle.
People are always surprised that he does not look a day over fifty.

Synset Examples

With her youthful energy, she does not look a day over forty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: look young for one's age - to appear to be younger than one's actual age
