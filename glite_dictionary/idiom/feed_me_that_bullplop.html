feed me that bullplop

1. X

/ fid mi ðæt ˈbʊlˌplɑp / (AmE), / fiːd miː ðæt ˈbʊlˌplɒp / (BrE)
(informal) to tell someone nonsense or lies; to try to deceive or mislead someone
tell someone nonsense or lies (informal)
informal slang

Examples

Don't feed me that bullplop; I know when someone is lying to me.
If he tries to feed me that bullplop again, I'm walking away from the deal.
She could tell he was feeding her that bullplop and didn't believe a word of it.

Synset Examples

Don't feed me that bullplop; I see right through it.
Stop feeding me that bullpoop; I know better.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feed me that bull - tell me lies; informal phrase for lying or deceiving
  • Synonym: feed me that nonsense - to tell someone something untrue or nonsensical

Etymology

Blend of the phrase 'feed me' (i.e., supply someone with information) and 'bullplop' (euphemism for 'bullshit', meaning nonsense).
