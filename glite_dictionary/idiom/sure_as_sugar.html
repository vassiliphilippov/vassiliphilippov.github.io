sure as sugar

1. ADV

/ ʃʊr əz ˈʃʊɡɚ / (AmE), / ʃɔː əz ˈʃʊɡə / (BrE)
Used to emphasize certainty; without any doubt.
emphatic idiom meaning 'certainly' or 'definitely'
informal

Examples

If you forget your umbrella, it'll rain, sure as sugar.
She'll call back, sure as sugar.
You can bet he'll show up late, sure as sugar.

Synset Examples

They'll win the game, sure as sugar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sure - certain; having no doubt about something
  • Synonym: certainly - without any doubt; for sure
