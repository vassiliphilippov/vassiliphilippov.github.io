of all places

1. X

/ əv ɔl ˈpleɪsɪz / (AmE), / əv ɔːl ˈpleɪsɪz / (BrE)
used to emphasize that a particular place is surprising or unexpected in a given context
used to stress an unexpectedly surprising place

Examples

I ran into my old teacher at a sushi bar, of all places.
She found her missing keys behind the refrigerator, of all places.
The concert was held in a laundromat, of all places.

Synset Examples

I bumped into her in Paris, of all places.
He proposed in Venice, of all places!
They met at the beach, of all places.
