yeoman's service

1. NOUN

/ ˈjoʊmənz ˈsɜːrvɪs / (AmE), / ˈjəʊmənz ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
faithful and diligent service, especially work that is useful and reliable
faithful and diligent service

Examples

The volunteers did yeoman's service to keep the festival running smoothly.
She performed yeoman's service in cataloguing the entire archive.
His tireless efforts were truly yeoman's service to the committee.

Synset Examples

They offered yeoman's service by volunteering all weekend.
She did a yeoman's service in organizing the fundraiser.
His yeoman's service helped the team achieve its goals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: valuable service - service that is useful and important

Etymology

From 'yeoman', originally meaning a servant or farmer of reputable standing in medieval England.
