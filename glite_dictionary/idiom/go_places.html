to go places

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ ˈpleɪsɪz / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈpleɪsɪz / (BrE)
to achieve success or become popular or important in life, work, or a particular field
achieve success or advancement

Examples

If you keep working hard, you'll go places in your career.
She's determined and talented, so she's really going places.
Many believe that with his skills, he will go places in the business world.

Synset Examples

If you keep working hard, you'll go places.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to succeed - to achieve the desired aim or result
  • Synonym: advance - to move forward or make progress in your career or life
