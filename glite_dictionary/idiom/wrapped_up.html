wrapped up

1. ADJ

/ ræpt ʌp / (AmE), / ræpt ʌp / (BrE)
deeply involved or absorbed in something, often with strong interest or devotion
deeply absorbed in something

Examples

He's so wrapped up in his work that he barely notices the time passing.
She became wrapped up in her studies and stopped going out with friends.
It's easy to get wrapped up in daily routines and forget to relax.

Synset Examples

bound up in her teaching
is wrapped up in his family

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absorbed in - deeply interested or involved in something
  • Synonym: engrossed in - completely focused on or preoccupied with something
  • Antonym: distracted - unable to concentrate due to preoccupation
