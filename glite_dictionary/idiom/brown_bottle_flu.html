brown bottle flu

1. NOUN

/ ˈbraʊn ˈbɑtəl flu / (AmE), / ˈbraʊn ˈbɒtəl fluː / (BrE)
A humorous term for a hangover caused by excessive beer drinking.
humorous term for a hangover
informal

Examples

She called in sick to work, blaming the brown bottle flu after last night's party.
Many college students have experienced the brown bottle flu on a Sunday morning.
He claimed the brown bottle flu was the reason for missing his early meeting.
