around the corner

1. ADV

/ əˈraʊnd ðə ˈkɔrnər / (AmE), / əˈraʊnd ðə ˈkɔːnə / (BrE)
very near in space or time; close by or about to happen
very near or imminent

Examples

The grocery store is just around the corner if you need anything.
With the deadline around the corner, everyone worked extra hours to finish the project.
Spring is around the corner and flowers are beginning to bloom.

Synset Examples

He lives just around the corner from my house.
There's a great surprise waiting just around the corner.
There was a beautiful park just around the corner.
The new cafe is just around the corner from my house.
