to turn the air blue

1. VERB

/ tɝn ði ɛr blu / (AmE), / tɜːn ðiː eə bluː / (BrE)
to use language that is very vulgar or full of swearing
use vulgar or offensive language
informal vulgar

He turned the air blue in the locker room after hearing the bad news.
