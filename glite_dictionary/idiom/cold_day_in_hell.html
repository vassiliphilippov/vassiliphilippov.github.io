cold day in Hell

1. NOUN

/ koʊld deɪ ɪn hɛl / (AmE), / kəʊld deɪ ɪn hɛl / (BrE)
A time or event that is extremely unlikely to happen or will never happen.
something very unlikely to happen

Examples

It'll be a cold day in Hell before I lend him my car again.
You can expect a cold day in Hell before she admits she was wrong.
The company giving away free products? That would be a cold day in Hell.

Synset Examples

It'll be a cold day in Hell before she agrees to that.
Expecting him to apologize is a cold day in Hell scenario.
I'll believe he can change on a cold day in Hell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: when pigs fly - another idiom meaning something impossible or very unlikely
  • Synonym: never - at no time; not ever
