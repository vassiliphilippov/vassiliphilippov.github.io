all over the map

1. ADJ

/ ɔl ˈoʊvər ðə mæp / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈəʊvə ðə mæp / (BrE)
disorganized, inconsistent, or covering a wide and unfocused range of topics or elements.
disorganized or highly varied

Examples

During the team meeting, the discussion was all over the map with no clear direction.
Her emotions are all over the map since the announcement.
Critics say the director's latest film is all over the map, lacking a central theme.

Synset Examples

The service was all over the map last night.
His thoughts were all over the map during the presentation.
The restaurant's menu is all over the map with cuisines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scattered - lacking organization or concentrated focus
  • Antonym: focused - concentrated and organized
