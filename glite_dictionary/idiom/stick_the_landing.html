to stick the landing

1. VERB

/ stɪk ðə ˈlændɪŋ / (AmE), / stɪk ðə ˈlændɪŋ / (BrE)
to land a jump or complete a maneuver smoothly and without error, especially in sports such as gymnastics or diving
land a jump successfully

Examples

The gymnast managed to stick the landing perfectly in her final routine.
Despite the difficult routine, he was determined to stick the landing in front of the judges.
After so much practice, she finally learned how to stick the landing every time.

Synset Examples

The gymnast managed to stick the landing perfectly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nail - to perform a task perfectly or complete it with great success
