birthday suit

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːθ.deɪ suːt / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθ.deɪ sjuːt / (BrE)
humorous term for being naked, as one was at birth
humorous way to say naked
informal

Examples

When the child wandered into the room in his birthday suit, everyone laughed.
She forgot her towel and had to dash to the bathroom in her birthday suit.
After swimming in the lake, he sunbathed in his birthday suit on the deserted shore.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the altogether - informal idiom meaning naked
  • Synonym: in the raw - informal idiom meaning naked

Etymology

Refers humorously to being unclothed as on the day of one's birth.
