to stunk on ice

1. VERB

/ stʌŋk ɑn aɪs / (AmE), / stʌŋk ɒn aɪs / (BrE)
(informal, idiomatic) Was extremely poor, disappointing, or of very low quality.
be extremely poor or disappointing
informal american

Examples

The service at that restaurant stunk on ice and I will never go back.
His performance in the play stunk on ice, disappointing everyone.
The new software update stunk on ice and caused more problems than it solved.

Synset Examples

The movie was so bad, it stunk on ice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stink - to be of very poor quality or to smell very bad
  • Synonym: bombed - failed badly or was extremely unsuccessful
  • Antonym: shone - was very good or impressive
