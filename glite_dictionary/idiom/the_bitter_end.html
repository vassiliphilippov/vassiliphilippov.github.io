the bitter end

1. NOUN

/ ðə ˈbɪt̬ɚ end / (AmE), / ðə ˈbɪtə end / (BrE)
the very end of a difficult or unpleasant situation, especially one that ends badly; the final, often unpleasant, outcome of something when all options have been exhausted
the unpleasant final outcome

Examples

He remained loyal to the company until the bitter end.
Despite all the setbacks, she stayed in the competition to the bitter end.
They negotiated unsuccessfully right up to the bitter end.

Synset Examples

We fought until the bitter end of the match.
Their friendship lasted until the bitter end.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the end - the final point or conclusion of something
