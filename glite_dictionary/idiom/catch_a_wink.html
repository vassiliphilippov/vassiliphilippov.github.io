to catch a wink

1. VERB

/ kætʃ ə wɪŋk / (AmE), / kætʃ ə wɪŋk / (BrE)
to have a brief sleep or nap, especially for a short period of time
have a brief nap

Examples

I couldn't catch a wink on the noisy train ride home.
After working all night, she tried to catch a wink before her morning meeting.
He usually catches a wink during his lunch break to recharge.

Synset Examples

She naps everyday after lunch for an hour

Related Terms

  • Synonym: take a nap - to sleep for a short period during the day
  • Synonym: doze - to sleep lightly or nap
