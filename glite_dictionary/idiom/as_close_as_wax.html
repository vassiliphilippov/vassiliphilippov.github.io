as close as wax

1. ADJ

/ æz kloʊs æz wæks / (AmE), / az kləʊs az waks / (BrE)
(of two or more people) very close or intimate in relationship.
very close or intimate (of relationship)

Examples

The two friends have been as close as wax since childhood, rarely seen apart.
The sisters are as close as wax, always sharing their secrets with each other.
Although they come from different backgrounds, they quickly became as close as wax.

Synset Examples

They were as close as wax during their childhood.
He and his best friend are as close as wax.
The sisters are close as wax, sharing everything.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as thick as thieves - very close or friendly (of people)
