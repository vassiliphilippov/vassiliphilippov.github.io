child's play

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪldz pleɪ / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪldz pleɪ / (BrE)
an activity or task that is very easy to do
something easy to do

Examples

Compared to fixing the car, installing the new light fixture was child's play.
For her, solving these math problems was child's play.
After years of experience, public speaking became child's play for him.

Synset Examples

marketing this product will be no picnic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piece of cake - something that is very easy to do
  • Antonym: no picnic - something that is not easy or pleasant
