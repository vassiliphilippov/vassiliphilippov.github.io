to break the deadlock

1. VERB

/ breɪk ðə ˈdɛdˌlɑk / (AmE), / breɪk ðə ˈdedlɒk / (BrE)
to resolve a situation in which no progress can be made due to disagreement or opposition
resolve a stalemate or impasse

Examples

The committee hopes to break the deadlock by proposing a new solution.
After several hours of negotiation, the two leaders managed to break the deadlock.
Introducing a neutral mediator helped break the deadlock between the two sides.

Synset Examples

It's essential to break the deadlock for progress.
Negotiators finally managed to break the deadlock in talks.
A compromise helped break the deadlock between parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resolve the impasse - find a solution to a deadlock or standstill
