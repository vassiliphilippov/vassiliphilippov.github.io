business as usual

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs əz ˈjuʒuəl / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs əz ˈjuːʒuəl / (BrE)
the continuation of normal operations or activities despite problems or unusual circumstances
normal operations despite difficulties

Examples

In the aftermath of the storm, the company tried to maintain business as usual.
Despite budget cuts, most departments are operating as business as usual.
After the CEO resigned, it was business as usual at the office.

Synset Examples

Despite the challenges, it's business as usual for the team.
Even with the new regulations, it's business as usual.
After the merger, it was business as usual for everyone involved.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: status quo - the existing state of affairs
