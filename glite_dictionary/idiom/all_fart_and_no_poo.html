all fart and no poo

1. X

/ ɔl fɑrt ənd noʊ pu / (AmE), / ɔːl fɑːt ənd nəʊ puː / (BrE)
(informal) Used to describe someone or something that talks a lot or makes big claims but fails to produce real results or action.
saying lots but doing nothing
informal

Examples

Despite their loud advertising campaign, the company turned out to be all fart and no poo.
I was worried the new policy would be all fart and no poo, but it actually brought change.
Whenever Kevin promises to clean the garage, he's all fart and no poo.

Synset Examples

The project was all fart and no poo last year.
His promises are just all fart and no poo.
They talk a lot, but it’s all fart and no poo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all talk and no action - makes promises but never acts; just talk, no results
