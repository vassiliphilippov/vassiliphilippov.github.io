apple-pie order

1. NOUN

/ ˌæp.əlˌpaɪ ˈɔɹ.dɚ / (AmE), / ˌap.əlˌpaɪ ˈɔː.də / (BrE)
a state of perfect order and neatness; complete organization and tidiness
perfect order; neatness

Examples

After spring cleaning, every room in the house was in apple-pie order.
She prided herself on keeping her kitchen in apple-pie order.
The office manager insists that all paperwork be kept in apple-pie order.

Synset Examples

The files were in apple-pie order on her desk.
Her closet was always in apple-pie order.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tidy - neat and arranged in order
  • Antonym: disarray - a state of disorder or untidiness

Etymology

Possibly from the French 'nappe pliée' (folded napkin), though the origin is uncertain.
