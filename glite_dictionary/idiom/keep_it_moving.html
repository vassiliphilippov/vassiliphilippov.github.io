to keep it moving

1. VERB

/ kip ɪt ˈmuːvɪŋ / (AmE), / kiːp ɪt ˈmuːvɪŋ / (BrE)
to continue progressing without stopping or delaying; to make steady forward progress
to continue without delay or stoppage
informal

Examples

If we want to finish before sunset, we need to keep it moving.
The manager told the team to keep it moving and not get distracted by small setbacks.
During the clean-up, volunteers were reminded to keep it moving so everything would be done on schedule.

Synset Examples

He encouraged everyone to keep it moving during the race.
Let’s keep it moving to finish on time.
We need to keep it moving to avoid delays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to keep going - to continue proceeding without stopping
  • Antonym: to hold up - to delay or stop progress
