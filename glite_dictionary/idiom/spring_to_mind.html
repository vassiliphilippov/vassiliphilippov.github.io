to spring to mind

1. VERB

/ sprɪŋ tə maɪnd / (AmE), / sprɪŋ tə maɪnd / (BrE)
(of an idea, memory, or thought) to be immediately remembered or brought to one's attention.
to be immediately remembered

Examples

Whenever I hear that song, memories of my childhood spring to mind.
No solutions immediately spring to mind, but let's keep thinking.
Her kindness always springs to mind when I think about good friends.

Synset Examples

His name comes to mind when you mention the strike

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to come to mind - to be remembered or thought of; to occur to someone

2. VERB

/ sprɪŋ tə maɪnd / (AmE), / sprɪŋ tə maɪnd / (BrE)
(of a thought or memory) to come into your mind suddenly and without effort.
occur to someone suddenly

Examples

Whenever someone mentions Paris, the Eiffel Tower immediately springs to mind.
No other candidates spring to mind for the position at this moment.
If you need ideas for dinner, pasta often springs to mind.

Synset Examples

The best vacation spots spring to mind when planning a trip.
Her name always springs to mind during our discussions.
When I think of summer, ice cream springs to mind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occur to - to come into someone's mind
