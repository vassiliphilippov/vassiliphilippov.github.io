to win hearts

1. VERB

/ wɪn hɑrts / (AmE), / wɪn hɑːts / (BrE)
to gain the affection or admiration of people
gain affection or admiration

Examples

The young politician quickly won hearts with her sincerity and passion.
Her beautiful singing won hearts throughout the entire city.
The team's fair play and sportsmanship won hearts even when they lost the match.

Synset Examples

The kitten's cuteness won everyone’s hearts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to captivate - to attract and hold the interest and attention of
  • Antonym: lose hearts - to lose the affection or admiration of people
