to take you to brown town

1. VERB

/ ˈteɪk ju tə ˈbraʊn taʊn / (AmE), / ˈteɪk juː tə ˈbraʊn taʊn / (BrE)
(slang) To cause someone to experience digestive upset, especially resulting in a sudden need to use the toilet.
cause digestive upset (slang)
slang informal

Examples

That new curry is so spicy it might take you to brown town if you're not careful.
Be careful with those street tacos—they could take you to brown town before you get home.
His chili was delicious, but it absolutely took me to brown town last night.
