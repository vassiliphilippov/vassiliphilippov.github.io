to light up the room

1. VERB

/ laɪt ʌp ðə ruːm / (AmE), / laɪt ʌp ðə ruːm / (BrE)
to make a place feel joyful, lively, or welcoming through one's presence, enthusiasm, or personality
make a place joyful or lively with one's presence

Examples

Her infectious laughter can light up the room even on the gloomiest day.
He always manages to light up the room with his positive attitude.
When she walked in, her smile seemed to light up the room instantly.
