to count noses

1. VERB

/ kaʊnt ˈnoʊzɪz / (AmE), / kaʊnt ˈnəʊzɪz / (BrE)
to check the number of people present, often to ensure everyone is accounted for or ready
check number of people present

Examples

Before the bus leaves, let's count noses to make sure we're not missing anyone.
Every morning, the teacher would count noses to see that all students were present.
It’s important to count noses before leaving the venue after the event.

Synset Examples

Before departing, let's count noses to be sure.
We need to count noses to ensure everyone is here.
It's time to count noses before the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head count - the act of counting people to see how many are present
