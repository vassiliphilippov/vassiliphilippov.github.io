smell you later

1. X

/ smɛl ju ˈleɪtɚ / (AmE), / smɛl ju ˈleɪtə / (BrE)
(informal) Used as a humorous or casual way to say goodbye.
informal way to say goodbye
informal slang

Examples

All right, I'll catch you tomorrow. Smell you later!
As they left the party, Tom grinned and said, 'smell you later!'
He tossed his backpack over his shoulder and called out, 'smell you later,' before leaving.

Synset Examples

After the meeting, he said, 'Smell you later!'
As they parted ways, he joked, 'Smell you later!'
She waved goodbye and shouted, 'Smell you later!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: see you later - an informal way to say goodbye
  • Synonym: goodbye - expression used when parting
