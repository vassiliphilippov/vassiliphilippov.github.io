to herd cats

1. VERB

/ hɝd kæts / (AmE), / hɜːd kæts / (BrE)
to try to manage or organize people or situations that are very difficult to control
try to manage the unmanageable

Examples

Trying to organize teenagers for a group photo is like herding cats.
Managing creative teams can often feel like you're herding cats.
Whenever he tries to coordinate everyone's schedules, he says it's like herding cats.

Synset Examples

It's hard to herd cats in a chaotic meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herd - to gather or manage animals as a group
