to need a breather

1. VERB

/ nid ə ˈbriðɚ / (AmE), / niːd ə ˈbriːðə / (BrE)
to require a short break or rest, especially after exertion or activity
require a short rest or break

Examples

After jogging for twenty minutes, I need a breather before continuing the run.
She told her team that they need a breather after all the intense work.
Sometimes you just need a breather to clear your head during a stressful day.

Synset Examples

I need a breather after that long meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to take a breather - to have a short rest or pause
  • Synonym: rest - period of relaxation or pause from activity
