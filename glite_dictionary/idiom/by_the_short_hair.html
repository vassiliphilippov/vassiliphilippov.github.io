by the short hair

1. X

/ baɪ ðə ʃɔrt hɛr / (AmE), / baɪ ðə ʃɔːt heə / (BrE)
to have someone firmly under control or at a disadvantage, leaving them little or no choice
to have someone firmly under control
informal

Examples

She really has her boss by the short hair after discovering that mistake.
The evidence held the suspect by the short hair, making a confession unavoidable.
With the final piece of information, they finally had the rival company by the short hair.

Synset Examples

She has him by the short and curlies with that ultimatum.
He knows he has me by the short hairs in this negotiation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: have someone over a barrel - to have someone in a helpless or powerless situation
  • Synonym: in the driver's seat - in control of a situation
