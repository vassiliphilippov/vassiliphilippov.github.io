my gosh

1. INTJ

/ maɪ ɡɑʃ / (AmE), / maɪ ɡɒʃ / (BrE)
used to express surprise, admiration, or mild shock
expression of surprise or admiration

Examples

My gosh, I can’t believe how quickly the time has gone!
My gosh, that was an incredible performance by the orchestra.
She opened the box and exclaimed, "my gosh, it’s beautiful!"

Synset Examples

My gosh, she looks stunning in that dress!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: my goodness - expression of surprise or admiration
  • Synonym: oh my - expression of surprise or awe

Etymology

Euphemistic alteration of 'my God' to avoid blasphemy.
