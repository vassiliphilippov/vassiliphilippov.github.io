simplicity itself

1. NOUN

/ sɪmˈplɪsəti ɪtˈsɛlf / (AmE), / sɪmˈplɪsəti ɪtˈsɛlf / (BrE)
extremely simple or easy; the very essence of simplicity.
extremely simple or easy
