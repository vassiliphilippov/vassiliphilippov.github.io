stay hungry and never settle

1. X

/ steɪ ˈhʌŋɡri ənd ˈnɛvər ˈsɛtəl / (AmE), / steɪ ˈhʌŋɡri ənd ˈnɛvə ˈsɛtəl / (BrE)
used to encourage someone to remain ambitious and not be content with mediocrity or complacency
remain ambitious and never be complacent

Examples

She reminded her team to stay hungry and never settle if they wanted to lead their industry.
His guiding principle was to stay hungry and never settle for mediocrity in his career.
The coach's motto was to stay hungry and never settle, pushing the athletes to their limits.

Synset Examples

He told them to stay hungry and never settle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stay hungry - remain ambitious and eager for success
