in the buff

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ðə bʌf / (AmE), / ɪn ðə bʌf / (BrE)
wearing no clothes; completely naked (informal)
completely naked (informal)
informal

Examples

The children were caught running in the buff through the sprinklers.
He accidentally walked into the hallway in the buff after his shower.
They posed in the buff for the artist's figure drawing class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naked - without any clothes
  • Antonym: clothed - wearing clothes

Etymology

From 'buff', meaning a light yellowish-brown color, referring to the color of undyed leather and, by extension, bare skin.
