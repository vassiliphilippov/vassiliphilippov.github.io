every man Jack

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛvri mæn ˈdʒæk / (AmE), / ˌɛvri mæn ˈdʒæk / (BrE)
every single person, without exception
everyone; every single person

Examples

Every man Jack of the crew was soaked after the storm.
Every man Jack in the audience applauded the performance.
When the boss called for help, every man Jack in the department volunteered.

Synset Examples

Every man Jack of them showed up on time.
Every man Jack in the office agreed to the plan.
Every man Jack of the team has a role.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: everyone - all people; every person
  • Antonym: nobody - no person; not anyone

Etymology

From the generic use of 'Jack' as a term for an ordinary man; used in English since at least the 18th century.
