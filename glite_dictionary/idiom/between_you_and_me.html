between you and me

1. X

/ bɪˈtwin ju ənd mi / (AmE), / bɪˈtwiːn juː ənd miː / (BrE)
used to indicate that what is being said is meant to be kept secret or confidential between the speaker and the listener
in confidence; confidentially

Examples

Between you and me, I don't think this project will succeed.
She whispered, "between you and me, I really dislike my boss."
Between you and me, that's the best restaurant in town.

Synset Examples

Between you, me, and the bedpost, I think he's lying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in confidence - used to denote private or confidential information
