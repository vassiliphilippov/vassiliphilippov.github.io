beyond a doubt

1. ADV

/ biˈjɑnd ə daʊt / (AmE), / bɪˈjɒnd ə daʊt / (BrE)
without any possibility of being doubted; unquestionably certain
with absolute certainty

Examples

She is beyond a doubt the best candidate for the job.
The evidence proved beyond a doubt that he was innocent.
His honesty is beyond a doubt, and everyone trusts him.

Synset Examples

it was immediately and indubitably apparent that I had interrupted a scene of lovers
his guilt was established beyond a shadow of a doubt

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undoubtedly - without doubt; certainly
  • Synonym: without a doubt - certainly; with no doubt
  • Antonym: possibly - maybe; used to show uncertainty
