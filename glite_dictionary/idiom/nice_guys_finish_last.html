nice guys finish last

1. X

/ naɪs ɡaɪz fɪnɪʃ læst / (AmE), / naɪs ɡaɪz fɪnɪʃ lɑːst / (BrE)
used to suggest that people who behave kindly or fairly are less likely to succeed than those who are selfish or ruthless
kind people are less successful

Examples

After losing out on another promotion, he complained that nice guys finish last.
Many believe that nice guys finish last in the corporate world, but kindness can be a strength.
She told her friend not to change, even if it sometimes seems like nice guys finish last.

Synset Examples

She left him, proving nice guys finish last.
He was kind-hearted but learned that nice guys finish last.

Etymology

Popularized by American baseball manager Leo Durocher in the 1940s.
