ye gods

1. INTJ

/ ji ɡɑdz / (AmE), / ji ɡɒdz / (BrE)
used to express surprise, disbelief, or amazement
expression of surprise or disbelief
archaic

Examples

Ye gods, what happened to your car?
Ye gods, I never thought I’d see the day!
Ye gods, that storm came out of nowhere!

Synset Examples

Ye gods, how did we end up in this mess?
Ye gods, that was the funniest thing I've ever seen!
Ye gods, I can't believe you did that!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good heavens - expression of surprise or shock
  • Synonym: my goodness - expression of mild surprise
  • Synonym: good grief - expression of exasperation or shock

Etymology

From archaic 'ye' (you, or the), here invoking multiple gods in exclamation.
