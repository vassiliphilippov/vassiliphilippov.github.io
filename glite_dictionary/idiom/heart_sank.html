to heart sank

1. VERB

/ hɑrt sæŋk / (AmE), / hɑːt sæŋk / (BrE)
used to say that someone suddenly felt disappointed or sad
to feel sudden disappointment or sadness

Examples

When she saw the empty house, her heart sank at once.
My heart sank when I realized I had missed the last train.
His heart sank as he listened to the verdict.

Synset Examples

His heart sank when he received the bad news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lose heart - to become discouraged
