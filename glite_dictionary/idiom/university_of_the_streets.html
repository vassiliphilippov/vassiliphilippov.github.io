university of the streets

1. NOUN

/ juːˌnɪvɚsəti əv ðə strits / (AmE), / juːˌnɪvəsəti ɒv ðə striːts / (BrE)
learning acquired through practical experience and real-life challenges, rather than through formal education
learning from life experience

Examples

He gained valuable wisdom at the university of the streets after leaving school early.
Many successful entrepreneurs are graduates of the university of the streets, not traditional universities.
She credits the university of the streets for teaching her skills no classroom could impart.

Synset Examples

He gained his knowledge from the university of the streets.
She learned life lessons from the university of the streets.
Surviving hardships is like attending the university of the streets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: school of hard knocks - learning through difficult real-world experiences
