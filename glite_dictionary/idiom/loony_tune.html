loony tune

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːni tuːn / (AmE), / ˈluːni tjuːn / (BrE)
an idea or plan that is considered very crazy or nonsensical
crazy or absurd idea
informal

Examples

Everyone thought her plan to build a flying bicycle was a real loony tune.
The committee dismissed his proposal as just another loony tune.
Sometimes it takes a loony tune to spark real innovation.

Synset Examples

His latest project was a real loony tune of an idea.
That scheme sounds like a loony tune to me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy idea - an idea that is wild or senseless
  • Antonym: sensible idea - an idea that is reasonable and logical

Etymology

From 'loony' (crazy) and 'tune', likely influenced by the animated series title 'Looney Tunes'.
