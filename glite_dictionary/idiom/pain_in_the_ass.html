pain in the ass

1. NOUN

/ peɪn ɪn ði æs / (AmE), / peɪn ɪn ði ɑːs / (BrE)
a person or thing that is very annoying or troublesome
an irritating or troublesome person or thing
informal slang

Examples

Dealing with the paperwork was a real pain in the ass.
My little brother can be such a pain in the ass when he's bored.
The constant traffic noise is a pain in the ass for everyone living on this street.
