to do someone a frighten

1. VERB

/ du ˈsʌmwʌn ə ˈfraɪtn̩ / (AmE), / dʊ ˈsʌmwʌn ə ˈfraɪtn̩ / (BrE)
to scare or startle someone, typically used humorously or informally
humorous way to say 'scare someone'
informal

Examples

The movie suddenly did me a frighten with that jump scare.
Please don't sneak up on me like that—you'll do me a frighten!
He did his cat a frighten by dropping the vacuum cleaner.

Synset Examples

The loud thunder did her a frighten last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to frighten - make someone afraid or startled
  • Synonym: scare - cause fear; startle
