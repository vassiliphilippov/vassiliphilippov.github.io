in the shitter

1. X

/ ɪn ðə ˈʃɪtər / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈʃɪtə / (BrE)
in a very difficult, unpleasant, or disastrous situation; in serious trouble
in a very bad or difficult situation
vulgar

Examples

After a series of bad investments, the company was in the shitter financially.
If we miss this deadline, we'll all be in the shitter with the boss.
He realized he was in the shitter when his car broke down in the middle of nowhere.

Synset Examples

He was in the shitter after losing his job.
She realized she was in the shitter for not studying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in hot water - in trouble or difficulty
  • Synonym: in dire straits - in a very difficult or dangerous situation
