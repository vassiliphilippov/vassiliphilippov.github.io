the greatest thing since sliced bread

1. NOUN

/ ðə ˈɡreɪtəst ˈθɪŋ sɪns slaɪst brɛd / (AmE), / ði ˈɡreɪtɪst ˈθɪŋ sɪns slaɪst brɛd / (BrE)
an extremely useful, innovative, or impressive new invention, idea, or development, often used humorously or enthusiastically
remarkably innovative or useful thing

Examples

Many people think that smartphones are the greatest thing since sliced bread, given how much they changed modern life.
When the new coffee machine arrived at the office, my colleagues said it was the greatest thing since sliced bread.
He told everyone that his homemade pizza recipe was the greatest thing since sliced bread.

Synset Examples

That new app is the greatest thing since sliced bread!
She believes online shopping is the greatest thing since sliced bread.
He joked that his invention is the greatest thing since sliced bread.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: game changer - something that significantly alters a situation or activity
  • Synonym: revolutionary - something causing or relating to a dramatic change
  • Antonym: old hat - something old-fashioned or unoriginal, the opposite of innovative

Etymology

From the phrase 'the best thing since sliced bread,' referring to the introduction of pre-sliced bread as a major innovation in the 1920s.
