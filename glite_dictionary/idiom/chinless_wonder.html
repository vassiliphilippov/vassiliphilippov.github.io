chinless wonder

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪnləs ˈwʌndɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪnləs ˈwʌndə / (BrE)
an ineffectual or unimpressive person, typically a man with a weak chin or lacking strong qualities, often used humorously or disparagingly
an unimpressive or weak person (humorous, often British)
informal british

Examples

He was dismissed as a mere chinless wonder with no chance of leadership.
The tabloids loved mocking the royal family as a group of chinless wonders.
She called her ex-boyfriend a chinless wonder after their breakup.

Synset Examples

Everyone laughed when they called him a chinless wonder.
