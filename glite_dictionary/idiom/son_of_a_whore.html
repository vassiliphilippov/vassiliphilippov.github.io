son of a whore

1. NOUN

/ sʌn əv ə hɔɹ / (AmE), / sʌn əv ə hɔː / (BrE)
An extremely offensive and vulgar insult directed at a person regarded as dishonorable, irritating, or contemptible.
highly offensive insult; contemptible person
offensive vulgar

Examples

He called me a son of a whore during the argument, which shocked everyone in the room.
Nobody wants to work with that son of a whore after what he did to the team.
If you cross him, you'll hear every insult in the book, including son of a whore.

Synset Examples

Only a son of a whore would treat his friends like that.
I can’t stand that son of a whore; he’s always causing trouble.
You think he’s trustworthy? He’s just a son of a whore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: son of a bitch - offensive insult for an unpleasant or contemptible person
  • Synonym: bastard - offensive term for a contemptible person
