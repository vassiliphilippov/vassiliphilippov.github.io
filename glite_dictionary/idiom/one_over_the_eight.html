one over the eight

1. ADJ

/ wʌn ˈoʊvər ði eɪt / (AmE), / wʌn ˈəʊvə ði eɪt / (BrE)
slightly drunk or tipsy; just over the usual or expected limit of alcoholic drinks
slightly drunk (British idiom)
informal british

Examples

After the second glass of wine, she was already one over the eight.
He admitted feeling one over the eight after celebrating his promotion.
You must have been one over the eight to agree to such a wild plan.

Synset Examples

She was one over the eight after the party last night.
After a long week, I was feeling one over the eight.
He stumbled home, clearly one over the eight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tipsy - slightly drunk
  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol

Etymology

British slang; refers to having one drink more than eight, considered the customary limit before intoxication.
