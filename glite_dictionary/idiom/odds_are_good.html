odds are good

1. X

/ ɑdz ɑr ɡʊd / (AmE), / ɒdz ɑː ɡʊd / (BrE)
it is likely that a particular event or outcome will happen; the chances are favorable
chances are favorable

Examples

With her qualifications, the odds are good she'll land the job.
The doctor said the odds are good that the treatment will be successful.
If we leave early, the odds are good we'll avoid the traffic.

Synset Examples

In this job pool, the odds are good, but the goods are odd.
Dating in this city feels like the odds are good, but the goods are odd.
In this market, the odds are good, but the goods are odd.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: odds are against - the chances are unfavorable; something is unlikely to happen
