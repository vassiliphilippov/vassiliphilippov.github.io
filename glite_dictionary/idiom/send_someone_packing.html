to send someone packing

1. VERB

/ sɛnd ˈsʌmwʌn ˈpækɪŋ / (AmE), / sɛnd ˈsʌmwən ˈpakɪŋ / (BrE)
to dismiss someone or make someone leave, especially abruptly or forcefully
dismiss someone abruptly

Examples

After the argument, the coach decided to send him packing from the team immediately.
When she caught him lying again, she finally sent him packing for good.
Their proposal was so weak that the investors sent them packing within minutes.

Synset Examples

The manager had to send him packing after the incident.
Her poor performance sent him packing last week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fire - to dismiss someone from a job
  • Synonym: to oust - to force someone out of a position or place
