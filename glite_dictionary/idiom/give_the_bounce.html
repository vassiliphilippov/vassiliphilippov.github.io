to give the bounce

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ðə baʊns / (AmE), / ɡɪv ðə baʊns / (BrE)
to abruptly terminate a relationship, especially by dismissing someone or ending an association suddenly.
abruptly end a relationship
informal

Examples

After only one week on the job, the manager gave the bounce to the new assistant.
When she found out he was lying, she gave the bounce without a second thought.
Tired of constant arguments, Tom finally gave the bounce to his roommate.

Synset Examples

Mary gave John the axe after she saw him with another woman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to give the axe - to dismiss someone from their job or end a relationship suddenly
