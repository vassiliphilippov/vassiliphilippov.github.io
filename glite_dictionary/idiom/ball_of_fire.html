ball of fire

1. NOUN

/ bɔl əv ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / bɔːl əv ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
a person who is very energetic, enthusiastic, or dynamic
a very energetic person

Examples

Ever since she joined the team, she's been a real ball of fire, motivating everyone around her.
Despite his age, my grandfather is still a ball of fire when it comes to gardening.
The new manager is a ball of fire, always coming up with fresh ideas and enthusiasm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: live wire - a very lively or energetic person
  • Synonym: dynamo - a person with great energy or enthusiasm

2. NOUN

/ bɔl əv ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / bɔːl əv ˈfaɪə / (BrE)

an energetic or rapidly successful person

Examples

She was a real ball of fire during her first year at the company and quickly earned a promotion.
Everyone admires Thomas because he’s such a ball of fire and always finishes his projects early.
The new recruit turned out to be a ball of fire, impressing everyone with her drive and ideas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dynamic - characterized by energy or effective action
