to act like a cuntrag

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈkʌntræɡ / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈkʌntræɡ / (BrE)
to behave in an extremely vulgar or offensive manner, especially in a way that is deliberately rude or insulting
behave in a disgusting, offensive way
offensive vulgar

Examples

Please don't act like a cuntrag during important meetings.
He started to act like a cuntrag after losing the argument.
There's never a good reason to act like a cuntrag to your friends.

Synset Examples

No reason to act like a cuntrag in public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: behave offensively - to act in a rude or disrespectful manner
