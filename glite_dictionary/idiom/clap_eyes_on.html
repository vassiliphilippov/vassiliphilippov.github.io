to clap eyes on

1. VERB

/ klæp aɪz ɑn / (AmE), / klæp aɪz ɒn / (BrE)
to see or notice someone or something, often for the first time
see or notice (idiom)

Examples

She couldn't believe she finally got to clap eyes on the Grand Canyon.
I've never clapped eyes on such a beautiful piece of jewelry before.
When I first clapped eyes on him, I knew he looked familiar.

Synset Examples

She was excited to clap eyes on her favorite actor.
He couldn't wait to clap eyes on the new exhibit.
I finally clapped eyes on the famous artist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: set eyes on - to see or look at someone or something, often for the first time
