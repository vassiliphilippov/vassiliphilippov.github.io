with child

1. ADJ

/ wɪθ tʃaɪld / (AmE), / wɪð tʃaɪld / (BrE)
pregnant, especially in an advanced stage; expecting a baby.
pregnant (archaic idiom)
archaic

Examples

In the old portrait, the woman is depicted with child.
She was said to be with child when the couple moved to the city.
The midwife cared for several women with child in the small village.

Synset Examples

was big with child
