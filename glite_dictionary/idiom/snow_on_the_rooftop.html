snow on the rooftop

1. NOUN

/ snoʊ ɑn ðə ˈroʊfˌtɑp / (AmE), / snəʊ ɒn ðə ˈruːftɒp / (BrE)
(idiomatic) Gray or white hair that appears with age; old age, or, less commonly, literal snow lying on the roof of a building.
gray or white hair due to aging; also, roof with snow

Examples

Though there's snow on the rooftop, his eyes still flash with the energy of youth.
She laughed and said, "Just because there's snow on the rooftop doesn't mean I'm slowing down!"
After the storm, real snow on the rooftop sparkled in the morning sunlight.

Synset Examples

She dances like a pro; there may be snow on the rooftop but there is fire in the furnace.
At 70, he still runs marathons; there may be snow on the rooftop but there is fire in the furnace.
He still plays soccer every weekend; there may be snow on the rooftop but there is fire in the furnace.
You could see snow on the rooftop after the blizzard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gray hair - hair that has turned gray with age
  • Synonym: old age - the later part of life; advanced years
