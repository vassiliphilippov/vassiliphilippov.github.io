itchy trigger finger

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪtʃi ˈtrɪɡər ˈfɪŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈɪtʃi ˈtrɪɡə ˈfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
a tendency to act impulsively or without sufficient thought, especially to take action too quickly
tendency to act impulsively

Examples

After winning the lottery, his itchy trigger finger led him to buy a new car the very next day.
She regretted her itchy trigger finger after sending the angry email without thinking twice.
With an itchy trigger finger, he accepted the job offer before discussing it with his family.

Synset Examples

With an itchy trigger finger, she quickly bought the shoes online.
He had an itchy trigger finger during the video game.
His itchy trigger finger led to several impulsive purchases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impulsive - acting without thought or planning
  • Antonym: cautious - acting with care and thought; avoiding impulsiveness

Etymology

Alludes to the literal urge to fire a gun quickly, transferred figuratively to general impulsiveness.
