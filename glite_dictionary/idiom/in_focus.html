in focus

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ˈfoʊ.kəs / (AmE), / ɪn ˈfəʊ.kəs / (BrE)
clearly defined, sharp, or concentrated, especially in visual representation or mental attention
clearly defined or sharply concentrated

Examples

After adjusting his camera, the photographer got the mountains in focus.
It's important to keep your priorities in focus during stressful times.
After a few minutes of explanation, the problem became much clearer and finally came in focus.

Synset Examples

The camera lens adjusted to keep the subject in focus.
Keep your goals in focus to achieve success.
Her mind was finally in focus after meditation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: out of focus - unclear or blurred in visual representation or attention
