to be a choad

1. VERB

/ bi ə tʃoʊd / (AmE), / biː ə tʃəʊd / (BrE)
To act in a foolish, irritating, or obnoxious way.
act foolishly or obnoxiously (slang)
informal slang

Examples

Please don't be a choad about this; just help us out.
If you be a choad at the meeting, people won't take you seriously.
She told her brother not to be a choad when guests were over.

Synset Examples

Don't be a choad; just admit you were wrong.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be a jerk - to behave in an unpleasant or irritating way
  • Synonym: be a nuisance - to behave in a way that annoys others
