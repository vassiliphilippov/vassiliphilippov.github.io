you said it

1. ADV

/ ju sɛd ɪt / (AmE), / juː sɛd ɪt / (BrE)
used to express strong agreement with what someone has said.
expression of strong agreement

Examples

“This summer has been unbearably hot.” “You said it—I can't wait for cooler weather.”
“That was the best film of the year.” “You said it!”
“Work has been exhausting lately.” “You said it, I could really use a break.”

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absolutely - used to indicate complete agreement
  • Synonym: exactly - used to show strong agreement with a statement
