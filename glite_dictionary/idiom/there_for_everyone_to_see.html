there for everyone to see

1. X

/ ðɛr fɔr ˈɛvrɪˌwʌn tu si / (AmE), / ðeə fə ˈevrɪwʌn tuː siː / (BrE)
openly visible or accessible to all; available for public viewing or scrutiny
openly visible; publicly accessible

Examples

The mistake was there for everyone to see, yet nobody said a word about it.
Her generosity was there for everyone to see during the charity event.
He left the evidence there for everyone to see on the kitchen table.

Synset Examples
