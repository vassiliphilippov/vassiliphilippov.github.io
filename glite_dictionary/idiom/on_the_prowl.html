on the prowl

1. ADV

/ ɑn ðə praʊl / (AmE), / ɒn ðə praʊl / (BrE)
actively searching or looking for something, especially with keen attention or for prey or opportunities
actively searching or looking for something

Examples

He has been on the prowl for interesting antiques at yard sales all summer.
Late at night, the fox was on the prowl near the chicken coop.
After her breakup, she joked that she was on the prowl for a new relationship.

Synset Examples

He’s been on the prowl for a new job lately.
She noticed the raccoon was on the prowl for food.
The cat was on the prowl for mice at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on the hunt - actively searching, especially for something specific
