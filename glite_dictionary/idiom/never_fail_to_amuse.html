to never fail to amuse

1. VERB

/ ˈnɛvɚ feɪl tə əˈmjuz / (AmE), / ˈnɛvə feɪl tə əˈmjuːz / (BrE)
to always provide entertainment or make people laugh, no matter the circumstances
always entertains or makes people laugh

Examples

Your stories never fail to amuse me, no matter how often I hear them.
That comedian never fails to amuse with his quirky jokes and impressions.
Their antics at family gatherings never fail to amuse everyone present.

Synset Examples

She has one joke that never fails to amuse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to entertain - to provide amusement or enjoyment
