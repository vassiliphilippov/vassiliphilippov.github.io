in all likelihood

1. ADV

/ ɪn ɔl ˈlaɪklihʊd / (AmE), / ɪn ɔːl ˈlaɪklɪhʊd / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is very likely to happen or be true; almost certainly.
almost certainly; very likely

Examples

In all likelihood, the meeting will run late tonight.
In all likelihood, she has already finished her assignment by now.
The flight has been delayed, so in all likelihood, we won't arrive before midnight.

Synset Examples

He is probably out of the country
in all likelihood we are headed for war

Related Terms

  • Synonym: probably - very likely; almost certainly
  • Degree Lower: possibly - might happen or be true, but less certain
