every now and then

1. ADV

/ ˈɛvɚi naʊ ənd ðɛn / (AmE), / ˈɛvri naʊ ənd ðɛn / (BrE)
from time to time; occasionally; at irregular intervals
occasionally; from time to time

Examples

Every now and then, I treat myself to a fancy cup of coffee from the café.
He likes to go hiking every now and then when the weather is nice.
You should back up your computer files every now and then to prevent data loss.

Synset Examples

every so often she visits her father

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occasionally - from time to time; not regularly
  • Degree Higher: frequently - happening often or regularly
  • Degree Lower: rarely - not often; seldom
