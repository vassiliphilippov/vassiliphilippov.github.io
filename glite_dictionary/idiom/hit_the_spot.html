to hit the spot

1. VERB

/ hɪt ðə spɑt / (AmE), / hɪt ðə spɒt / (BrE)
to satisfy a need or desire exactly, especially for food or drink
to satisfy a need or craving precisely

Examples

After a long run, a glass of lemonade really hit the spot.
When you're cold, a bowl of hot soup just hits the spot.
That spicy curry hit the spot after a day in the mountains.

Synset Examples

That chocolate cake really hit the spot.
After hiking, a hearty meal hit the spot.
A cold drink on a hot day hits the spot.
On hot days, a special lassi hits the spot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to satisfy - to meet the needs or desires of someone; to fulfill
  • Antonym: to miss the mark - to fail to reach the desired result or effect
