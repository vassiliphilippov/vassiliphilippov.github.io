out of kilter

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv ˈkɪltər / (AmE), / aʊt əv ˈkɪltə / (BrE)
not working correctly; out of order or not in harmony
not functioning properly; not in harmony

Examples

Ever since the computers crashed, the entire system feels out of kilter.
His sleep schedule is completely out of kilter after working night shifts.
The team's performance has been out of kilter since the coach left.

Synset Examples

Her emotions were out of kilter after the breakup.
The schedule has been out of kilter all week.
Things feel out of kilter since the merger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of order - not working properly; malfunctioning
  • Antonym: in sync - working in harmony; properly aligned

Etymology

Origin of "kilter" is uncertain, possibly from dialectal English.
