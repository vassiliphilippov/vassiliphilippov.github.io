to grease the mayor's palm

1. VERB

/ ɡriːs ðə meɪ.ɚz pɑːm / (AmE), / ɡriːs ðə meɪ.əz pɑːm / (BrE)
to bribe an official, especially a mayor, in order to obtain a favor or special treatment
bribe an official for a favor
informal

Examples

Rumors spread that he tried to grease the mayor's palm in hopes of securing the contract.
Investigations revealed several businessmen had attempted to grease the mayor's palm over the years.
She refused to grease the mayor's palm, believing in fair and transparent processes.

Synset Examples

He tried to grease the mayor's palm for a favor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bribe - to give someone money or a gift to persuade them to do something, especially something dishonest
  • Synonym: to grease someone's palm - to bribe someone

Etymology

From the metaphor of putting grease on someone's hand (palm) to make it slip money in as a bribe.
