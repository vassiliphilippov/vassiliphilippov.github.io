poor white trash

1. NOUN

/ pɔr waɪt træʃ / (AmE), / pɔː waɪt træʃ / (BrE)
an extremely offensive and derogatory term used to refer to impoverished or lower-class white people, especially in the southern United States
offensive term for poor white people
offensive vulgar american

Examples

He was deeply hurt when his classmates called him poor white trash.
Films often perpetuate harmful stereotypes like the one implied by poor white trash.
Using the phrase poor white trash is considered extremely offensive and should be avoided.

Synset Examples

He felt ashamed to be labeled as poor white trash.
The term 'poor white trash' is often used derogatorily.
