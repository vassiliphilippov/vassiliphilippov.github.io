all in all

1. ADV

/ ˌɔl ɪn ˈɔl / (AmE), / ˌɔːl ɪn ˈɔːl / (BrE)
taking everything into account; considering all aspects or factors
considering all factors

Examples

All in all, it was a very successful trip despite a few minor setbacks.
All in all, I think we made the right decision to move.
There were some problems with the project, but all in all it went smoothly.

Synset Examples

altogether, I'm sorry it happened
all in all, it's not so bad

Related Terms

  • Synonym: altogether - completely; considering everything
