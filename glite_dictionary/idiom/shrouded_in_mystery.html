shrouded in mystery

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃraʊdɪd ɪn ˈmɪstəri / (AmE), / ˈʃraʊdɪd ɪn ˈmɪstəri / (BrE)
used to describe something that is hidden or difficult to understand; surrounded by secrecy or uncertainty
hidden or difficult to understand

Examples

The ancient legend remains shrouded in mystery, fascinating historians for generations.
Their sudden disappearance was shrouded in mystery and sparked numerous theories.
Details of the agreement are shrouded in mystery, with no one willing to discuss them publicly.

Synset Examples

It was a misty night, shrouded in mystery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enigmatic - difficult to interpret or understand; mysterious
  • Antonym: transparent - easy to perceive or detect; clear and obvious
