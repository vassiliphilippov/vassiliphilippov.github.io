short drop and a sudden stop

1. NOUN

/ ʃɔrt drɑp ənd ə ˈsʌdn stɑp / (AmE), / ʃɔːt drɒp ənd ə ˈsʌdn stɒp / (BrE)
a sudden and disastrous or fatal ending, often used metaphorically for outcomes that are abrupt and harsh
sudden harsh or fatal ending

Examples

Many old pirate tales warned criminals of a short drop and a sudden stop if they were caught.
After the company’s financial scandal, its success ended with a short drop and a sudden stop.
He always drove carefully, knowing that one mistake might mean a short drop and a sudden stop.

Synset Examples

Driving recklessly can lead to a short drop and a sudden stop.
Mistakes can sometimes lead to a short drop and a sudden stop.
He learned that life can be a short drop and a sudden stop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meet one's end - to die or come to a fatal conclusion
  • Synonym: abrupt halt - a sudden stop or ending

Etymology

Originally refers to execution by hanging with a short drop, causing a sudden stop (death).
