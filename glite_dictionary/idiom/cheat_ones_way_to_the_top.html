to cheat one's way to the top

1. VERB

/ tʃit wʌnz weɪ tə ðə tɑp / (AmE), / tʃiːt wʌnz weɪ tə ðə tɒp / (BrE)
to achieve success or a position of power by using dishonest or unfair methods
gain success dishonestly

Examples

He managed to cheat his way to the top without anyone noticing his tactics.
People who cheat their way to the top often lack real respect from their peers.
She felt frustrated seeing others cheat their way to the top while she worked honestly.

Synset Examples

That bastard cheated his way to the top.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheat - to act dishonestly or unfairly in order to gain an advantage
  • Degree Lower: rise to the top - to achieve a high rank or position, often implying merit
