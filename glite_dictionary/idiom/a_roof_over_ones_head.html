a roof over one's head

1. NOUN

/ ə ruːf ˈoʊvɚ wʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / ə ruːf ˈəʊvə wʌnz hɛd / (BrE)
a place to live; shelter or accommodation
having a place to live

Examples

Many families struggle to keep a roof over their head during difficult times.
She was grateful to finally have a roof over her head after many months of homelessness.
Having a roof over one's head is something many people take for granted.

Synset Examples

Having a roof over one's head is essential.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shelter - a place giving protection from bad weather or danger
