to stop and smell the roses

1. VERB

/ stɑp ənd smɛl ðə ˈroʊzɪz / (AmE), / stɒp ənd smɛl ðə ˈrəʊzɪz / (BrE)
to take time to relax and appreciate the simple pleasures of life, especially when busy
take time to appreciate life

Examples

It's important to stop and smell the roses instead of rushing through every day.
She realized she hadn't truly stopped and smelled the roses in years.
After a stressful week, he decided to stop and smell the roses on his walk home.

Synset Examples

Sometimes, you need to stop and smell the roses.
After the long hike, they decided to stop and smell the roses.
She reminded him to stop and smell the roses during busy days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appreciate the moment - to value and enjoy the present time
