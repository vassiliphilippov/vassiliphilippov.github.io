black and blue

1. ADJ

/ blæk ənd bluː / (AmE), / blæk ənd bluː / (BrE)
Having bruises or discolored marks on the skin, typically as a result of being hit or injured.
with bruises or bruised marks

Examples

He came home black and blue after the football match.
After slipping on the stairs, her legs were black and blue for a week.
The wrestler was black and blue following the brutal competition.

Synset Examples

He came home black and blue after the football match.
The child fell and got black and blue all over.
I woke up black and blue from last night's fight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bruised - having dark marks on the skin after injury
