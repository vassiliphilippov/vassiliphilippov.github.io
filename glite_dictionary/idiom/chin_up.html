chin up

1. X

/ tʃɪn ʌp / (AmE), / tʃɪn ʌp / (BrE)
used to tell someone to remain positive and optimistic in a difficult situation.
remain positive; keep optimistic

Examples

Even after losing the match, his coach told him to chin up and try again.
When you feel discouraged, just remember to chin up and keep going.
She managed to chin up throughout the challenging times at work.

Synset Examples

With a chin up attitude, she faced the challenges ahead.
Chin up; things will get better soon!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keep your head up - remain confident and positive
