slap and tickle

1. NOUN

/ slæp ənd ˈtɪkəl / (AmE), / slap ənd ˈtɪkəl / (BrE)
playful or flirtatious activity, especially involving physical affection or sexual innuendo, typically in a lighthearted manner
playful or flirtatious behavior
informal british

Examples

Their night out ended with a bit of slap and tickle behind the pub.
The film's humor relies on the usual mix of slap and tickle and witty dialogue.
They insisted it was nothing serious—just harmless slap and tickle.

Synset Examples
