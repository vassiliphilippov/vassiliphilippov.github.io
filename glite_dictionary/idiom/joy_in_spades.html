joy in spades

1. NOUN

/ dʒɔɪ ɪn speɪdz / (AmE), / dʒɔɪ ɪn speɪdz / (BrE)
an abundance of joy; a very large amount of happiness or delight
a great deal of joy

Examples

She found joy in spades when her grandchildren came to visit.
After winning the award, the whole team was filled with joy in spades.
The festival brought joy in spades to the small town every summer.

Synset Examples

They celebrated their victory with joy in spades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: happiness - the state of being happy
  • Synonym: delight - great pleasure or satisfaction
  • Antonym: misery - a state of great unhappiness or discomfort

Etymology

From the expression 'in spades', meaning 'in large amounts', originally from the game of cards where spades are the highest suit.
