in the soup

1. X

/ ɪn ðə suːp / (AmE), / ɪn ðə suːp / (BrE)
in a difficult or troublesome situation; in trouble
in trouble or difficulty

Examples

After the audit, the company found itself in the soup financially.
If you forget your tickets, you'll be in the soup at the gate.
She realized she was in the soup when her alibi fell apart.

Synset Examples
