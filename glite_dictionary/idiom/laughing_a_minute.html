laughing a minute

1. ADJ

/ ˈlæfɪŋ ə ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɑːfɪŋ ə ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
constantly laughing or in a state of amusement
constantly amused; laughing often

Examples

She was laughing a minute during the entire show; her laughter never stopped.
Whenever we're together, we're laughing a minute—there's never a dull moment.
The party was so much fun that I was laughing a minute from start to finish.

Synset Examples

Her jokes had everyone in the meeting laughing a minute.
