to get to third base

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt tə θɝd beɪs / (AmE), / ɡɛt tə θɜːd beɪs / (BrE)
to reach a certain level of physical intimacy, especially kissing and touching below the waist, but not intercourse
reach a level of physical intimacy
informal american

Examples

Rumor has it that Alex managed to get to third base on their first date.
They were surprised when the movie star admitted she wouldn't get to third base with someone she just met.
He kept bragging about how he hoped to get to third base at the party last night.

Synset Examples

She joked that he always tries to get to third base too quickly.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: to go all the way - to have sexual intercourse
  • Degree Lower: to make out - to engage in kissing and hugging

Etymology

From American baseball slang, where 'third base' is metaphorically used to represent a level of sexual intimacy.
