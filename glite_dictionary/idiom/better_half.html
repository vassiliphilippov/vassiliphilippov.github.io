better half

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛtər hæf / (AmE), / ˈbɛtə hɑːf / (BrE)
a person's spouse or life partner, especially used humorously or affectionately
spouse or life partner

Examples

He always introduces his wife as his better half at parties.
When I travel for work, I miss my better half terribly.
She joked that her better half keeps her organized and on time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spouse - a husband or wife
  • Synonym: partner - a person's significant other or companion

Etymology

From the notion that one's spouse is the 'better' or more significant part of oneself, recorded since the 16th century.
