days on end

1. ADV

/ deɪz ɑn ɛnd / (AmE), / deɪz ɒn end / (BrE)
for many consecutive days without interruption.
many consecutive days

Examples

He stayed in bed for days on end recovering from the flu.
The rain continued days on end, flooding the streets.
They argued days on end without reaching a solution.

Synset Examples

I've been working on this task for days on end.
She practiced the piano for days on end.
