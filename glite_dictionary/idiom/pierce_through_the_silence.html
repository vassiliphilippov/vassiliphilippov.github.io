to pierce through the silence

1. VERB

/ pɪrs θru ðə ˈsaɪləns / (AmE), / pɪəs θruː ðə ˈsaɪləns / (BrE)
to suddenly break or interrupt a period of silence or quietness
break a quiet atmosphere suddenly

Examples

A scream pierced through the silence of the night, startling everyone nearby.
Suddenly, the loud ringing of the phone pierced through the silence in the office.
The sound of the train's whistle pierced through the silence of the sleeping village.

Synset Examples

The news will pierce through the silence.
