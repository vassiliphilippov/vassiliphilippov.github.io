opinions are like assholes

1. X

/ əˈpɪnjənz ɑr laɪk ˈæsˌhoʊlz / (AmE), / əˈpɪnjənz ɑː laɪk ˈæsˌhəʊlz / (BrE)
used to emphasize that everyone has their own opinions, often implying that these opinions are common, unsolicited, or of little value
everyone has their own opinion (idiom)
informal vulgar

Examples

Whenever people start arguing about movies, someone reminds them that opinions are like assholes.
He shrugged and said, 'Well, you know, opinions are like assholes.'
Some say opinions are like assholes: everyone has one, but that doesn't mean you want to hear about it.

Synset Examples

He reminded me that opinions are like assholes; ignore the noise.
She laughed, saying opinions are like assholes, and everyone has one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: everyone's a critic - idiom meaning everyone has opinions, often negative or unsolicited
