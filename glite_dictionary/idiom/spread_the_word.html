to spread the word

1. VERB

/ sprɛd ðə wɝd / (AmE), / sprɛd ðə wɜːd / (BrE)
to tell others about something so that it becomes widely known
inform others; make something known

Examples

We need to spread the word about the changes to the schedule as soon as possible.
Sarah posted flyers around town to spread the word about the charity concert.
Can you spread the word at your office about our new product launch?

Synset Examples

Please spread the word about the upcoming sale.
He asked his friends to spread the word about his new book.
Let's spread the word to get more participants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to publicize - to make something widely known
  • Antonym: to keep quiet - to not tell others; to keep information private
