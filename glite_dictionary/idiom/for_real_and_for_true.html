for real and for true

1. ADV

/ fər ˈriːəl ənd fər ˈtruː / (AmE), / fə ˈrɪəl ənd fə ˈtruː / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is completely genuine or sincere
genuinely; sincerely (emphatic)
informal

Examples

Are you coming to the party, for real and for true, or are you joking?
He promised, for real and for true, he would never lie to her again.
This time she's moving out, for real and for true.

Synset Examples

I promise, for real and for true, I’ll be there.
She loves him, for real and for true.
He’s actually moving away, for real and for true.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for real - used to indicate sincerity or genuineness
  • Synonym: honestly - used to stress that one is telling the truth
