on second thought

1. ADV

/ ɑn ˈsɛkənd θɔt / (AmE), / ɒn ˈsɛkənd θɔːt / (BrE)
used to indicate that one has reconsidered a decision or opinion after further reflection
after reconsidering a decision

Examples

I was going to order dessert, but on second thought, I decided to skip it.
On second thought, maybe we should wait until tomorrow to make a decision.
He initially agreed to help, but on second thought he changed his mind.

Synset Examples

He took the job offer, but on second thought, he regretted it.
On second thought, I think I’ll stay home today.
She decided to cancel the trip on second thought.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on reflection - after thinking again about a decision or opinion
  • Synonym: after consideration - after taking more time to think about something
