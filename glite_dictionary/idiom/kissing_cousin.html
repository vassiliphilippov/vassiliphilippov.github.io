kissing cousin

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪsɪŋ ˈkʌzən / (AmE), / ˈkɪsɪŋ ˈkʌzən / (BrE)
a distant relative or a close friend; sometimes, a cousin with whom one is affectionate but not romantically involved
distant relative or close friend

Examples

Everyone in that small town seemed like a kissing cousin to each other.
She introduced me as her kissing cousin at the family picnic.
At the reunion, I met several people who turned out to be my kissing cousins.

Synset Examples

In a small town, everyone feels like a kissing cousin.
