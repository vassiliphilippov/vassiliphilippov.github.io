in token of

1. ADP

/ ɪn ˈtoʊkən ʌv / (AmE), / ɪn ˈtəʊkən ɒv / (BrE)
as a symbol or sign of; as an expression intended to represent or acknowledge something
as a symbol or sign of something

Examples

She sent him a letter in token of her gratitude.
They raised a glass in token of their friendship.
He wore the badge in token of his service.

Synset Examples

The award was presented in token of her hard work.
They gave her a gift in token of their appreciation.
In token of friendship, he brought her flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as a mark of - used to indicate that something is done as a sign of something

Etymology

From Middle English 'token', meaning a sign or symbol, from Old English 'tācen' (sign).
