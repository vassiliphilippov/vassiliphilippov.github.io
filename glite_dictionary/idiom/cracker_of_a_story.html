cracker of a story

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrækər əv ə ˈstɔri / (AmE), / ˈkrækə əv ə ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
an excellent or very entertaining story
an excellent, entertaining story
informal british

Examples

That was a cracker of a story, I couldn't stop laughing.
Everyone at the party agreed it was a cracker of a story.
He told us a cracker of a story about his travels in India.

Synset Examples

She always brings a cracker of a story to our gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cracker - something or someone excellent or impressive

Etymology

From 'cracker' (British/Australian slang for something outstanding or impressive) + 'of a story'.
