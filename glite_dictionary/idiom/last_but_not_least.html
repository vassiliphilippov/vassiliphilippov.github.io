last but not least

1. ADV

/ læst bət nɑt list / (AmE), / lɑːst bət nɒt liːst / (BrE)
used to emphasize that the final person or thing mentioned is equally important as those already named
introducing the final important item

Examples

Last but not least, I would like to thank my parents for their support.
The cake was delicious, and last but not least, it was homemade.
Last but not least, we must acknowledge the efforts of our volunteers.

Synset Examples

2. ADV

/ læst bət nɑt list / (AmE), / lɑːst bət nɒt liːst / (BrE)
used to introduce the final person or thing in a list, emphasizing that it is just as important as the others
introducing an important final item

Examples

Last but not least, I want to thank my family for their support.
She introduced all her colleagues and, last but not least, her mentor.
Last but not least, remember to turn off the lights before you leave.
