come on vacation, leave on probation

1. X

/ kʌm ɑn veɪˈkeɪʃən, liv ɑn proʊˈbeɪʃən / (AmE), / kʌm ɒn vəˈkeɪʃən, liːv ɒn prəʊˈbeɪʃən / (BrE)
(saying) Used to warn that inappropriate or risky behavior during a work vacation could result in disciplinary action upon return.
warning about risky behavior on work leave

Examples

At the orientation, HR reminded new hires, "Remember: come on vacation, leave on probation if you party too hard and break the rules."
When photos of the trip surfaced online, he joked, "Looks like it's come on vacation, leave on probation for me!"
The company has strict policies because they've dealt with come on vacation, leave on probation situations in the past.

Synset Examples

He learned that 'come on vacation, leave on probation' can cost you your job.
