to could eat a horse

1. VERB

/ kʊd it ə hɔɹs / (AmE), / kʊd iːt ə hɔːs / (BrE)
used to say that you are extremely hungry
to be extremely hungry (idiom)

Examples

After skipping breakfast, I was so hungry I could eat a horse.
By the time dinner was ready, she felt like she could eat a horse.
"I'm absolutely famished; I could eat a horse right now!" he joked.

Synset Examples

I'm so hungry, I could eat a horse!
After that workout, I could eat a horse.
After skipping lunch, I could eat a horse now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: starving - extremely hungry
