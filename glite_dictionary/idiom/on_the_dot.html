on the dot

1. ADV

/ ɑn ðə dɑt / (AmE), / ɒn ðə dɒt / (BrE)
exactly at the stated time; precisely on time.
exactly at the scheduled time

Examples

The meeting starts at 10 a.m. on the dot, so please don't be late.
She arrived on the dot, just as she promised.
The train departed on the dot, right on schedule.

Synset Examples

‘Precisely, my lord,’ he said

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punctually - at the expected or planned time
  • Antonym: late - after the arranged or expected time

2. ADV

/ ɑn ðə dɑt / (AmE), / ɒn ðə dɒt / (BrE)
at exactly the specified or scheduled time; precisely punctual
exactly at the specified time

Examples

The meeting is scheduled to start at 3 o'clock on the dot, so don't be late.
She likes to arrive home from work on the dot every evening.
The class begins at nine on the dot every day.

Synset Examples

The train left at 6 PM on the dot.
He always calls me back at 7 PM on the dot.
She arrived at twelve on the dot.
She arrived at the meeting on the dot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punctually - at the arranged or correct time
  • Antonym: late - not at the expected or scheduled time
