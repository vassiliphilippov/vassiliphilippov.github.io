face like a fiddle

1. NOUN

/ feɪs laɪk ə ˈfɪdəl / (AmE), / feɪs laɪk ə ˈfɪdəl / (BrE)
a face that appears healthy, youthful, well-preserved, or smooth
having a healthy and youthful face

Examples

After returning from her spa retreat, she had a face like a fiddle.
Even at eighty years old, his friends said he still had a face like a fiddle.
Despite her busy lifestyle, she always manages to keep a face like a fiddle.

Synset Examples

After the vacation, she returned with a face like a fiddle.
