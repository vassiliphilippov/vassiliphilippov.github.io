made in the shade

1. ADJ

/ meɪd ɪn ðə ʃeɪd / (AmE), / meɪd ɪn ðə ʃeɪd / (BrE)
in a comfortable, secure, or favourable position, especially one that requires little effort or worry
in a secure or easy position
informal

Examples

Ever since she landed the new job with flexible hours, she's made in the shade.
If we win this contract, we'll be made in the shade for years to come.
After finishing their work early, the team felt they were made in the shade for the afternoon.
