head-in-the-clouds

1. ADJ

/ ˌhɛd ɪn ðə ˈklaʊdz / (AmE), / ˌhɛd ɪn ðə ˈklaʊdz / (BrE)
having ideas, dreams, or thoughts that are unrealistic or not grounded in practical reality; often dreamy or absent-minded.
absent-minded or dreamy

Examples

She always has her head-in-the-clouds and forgets about her appointments.
His head-in-the-clouds attitude makes it hard for him to focus on daily tasks.
Teachers worry he is too head-in-the-clouds to pay attention in class.

Synset Examples

flighty young girls

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dreamy - lost in thought, having a dream-like quality
  • Synonym: absent-minded - forgetful or inattentive to present surroundings
  • Antonym: down-to-earth - practical and realistic
