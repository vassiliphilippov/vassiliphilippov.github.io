cold sober

1. ADJ

/ koʊld ˈsoʊbər / (AmE), / kəʊld ˈsəʊbə / (BrE)
completely sober; not at all affected by alcohol or drugs.
completely sober

Examples

Despite the late hour, he was still cold sober when he left the party.
She made an important decision while cold sober, not under any influence.
It's easier to judge a situation when you're cold sober.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stone-cold sober - completely sober; entirely free from alcohol's effects
