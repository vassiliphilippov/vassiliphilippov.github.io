to grab someone's attention

1. VERB

/ ɡræb ˈsʌmwʌnz əˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / ɡræb ˈsʌmwʌnz əˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
to attract or catch someone's notice or interest suddenly and strongly
to attract someone's notice suddenly

Examples

The bold headline immediately grabbed my attention when I entered the room.
Her colorful presentation really grabbed my attention and made the topic interesting.
The unusual noise grabbed my attention as I was walking past the building.

Synset Examples

The blinkie on the website grabbed my attention instantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attract attention - to cause people to notice
  • Antonym: lose someone's attention - to cease to have someone's interest or notice
