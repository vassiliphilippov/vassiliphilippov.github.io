down to a fine art

1. X

/ daʊn tə ə faɪn ɑrt / (AmE), / daʊn tʊ ə faɪn ɑːt / (BrE)
To master a skill or process to a very high level of skill or precision, often through experience and practice.
with great skill or precision

Examples

She has budgeting down to a fine art and never overspends.
After years of practice, he got parallel parking down to a fine art.
They have meal preparation down to a fine art in that kitchen.

Synset Examples

He has negotiation skills down to a fine art.
They’ve got teamwork down to a fine art.
She has cooking down to a fine art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mastery - complete control or superior skill
  • Synonym: expert - a person with a high degree of skill or knowledge
  • Antonym: clumsy - lacking skill or precision
