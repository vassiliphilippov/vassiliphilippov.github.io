in a big way

1. ADV

/ ɪn ə bɪɡ weɪ / (AmE), / ɪn ə bɪɡ weɪ / (BrE)
to a significant degree or on a large and impressive scale
to a great extent or impressively

Examples

After years of saving, they finally traveled to Europe in a big way.
The company entered the market in a big way, launching several new products at once.
She decided to support the charity in a big way by making a large donation.

Synset Examples

They announced the project in a big way to gain attention.
He celebrated his promotion in a big way.
She wants to help the community in a big way.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in a small way - to a limited or modest extent
