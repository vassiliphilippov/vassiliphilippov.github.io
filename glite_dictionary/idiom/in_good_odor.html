in good odor

1. X

/ ɪn ɡʊd ˈoʊdɚ / (AmE), / ɪn ɡʊd ˈəʊdə / (BrE)
Regarded with respect or approval; having a positive reputation.
well regarded; respected

Examples

After the investigation, the manager was once again in good odor with the board.
The company stayed in good odor by consistently engaging in fair business practices.
Wanting to remain in good odor, he volunteered for extra work.

Synset Examples

The brand remains in good odor despite recent controversies.
The restaurant is renowned for being in good odor with locals.
His reputation is in good odor after the charity work.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in bad odor - regarded with disapproval; having a bad reputation

Etymology

From the Middle English use of 'odor' in the sense of reputation, with 'good' indicating favorable standing.
