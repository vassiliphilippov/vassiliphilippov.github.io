to pay a visit

1. VERB

/ peɪ ə ˈvɪzɪt / (AmE), / peɪ ə ˈvɪzɪt / (BrE)
to visit a person or place, usually briefly or for a short period
to visit someone or someplace

Examples

We should pay a visit to your parents before leaving town.
Whenever he's in the area, he likes to pay a visit to his old teachers.
Tourists often pay a visit to the famous cathedral in the city.

Synset Examples

I plan to pay a visit to my grandma this weekend.
She decided to pay a visit to the new coffee shop.
It's been ages since I paid a visit to that museum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visit - to go to see and spend time with (someone); an act of visiting
