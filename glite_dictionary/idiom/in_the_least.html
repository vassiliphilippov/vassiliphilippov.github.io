in the least

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðə list / (AmE), / ɪn ðə liːst / (BrE)
to any extent at all; even slightly (used especially in negative contexts)
to any extent; even slightly

Examples

If you are in the least concerned, please let us know immediately.
She wasn't in the least surprised by the unexpected news.
I'm not in the least interested in watching that movie.

Synset Examples

are you in the least interested?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at all - to any extent or degree (used in negatives or questions)
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00151616-r
Synset POS: r

2. ADV

/ ɪn ðə list / (AmE), / ɪn ðə liːst / (BrE)
not at all; in the slightest degree or in any respect.
not at all; in the slightest degree

Examples

She wasn't in the least worried about the outcome of the test.
If you are in the least interested, let me know as soon as possible.
He didn't seem in the least upset by the news.

Synset Examples

Are you at all interested? No, not at all
was not in the least unfriendly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at all - to any extent; in any way
