to give it one's all

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ɪt wʌnz ɔl / (AmE), / ɡɪv ɪt wʌnz ɔːl / (BrE)
to make the greatest possible effort; to do something with maximum energy and commitment
put forth maximum effort

Examples

Despite her exhaustion, she promised to give it her all in the final race.
The team knew they had to give it their all to win the championship.
If you want to succeed in this business, you must give it your all every single day.

Synset Examples

She gave it her all out during the performance.
Don’t bring it weak to the competition; give it your all!
She gives her all in every performance.
She played the match for England, giving it her all.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: try one's hardest - make as much effort as possible
  • Antonym: to hold back - to not give one's maximum effort
