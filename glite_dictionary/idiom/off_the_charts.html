off the charts

1. ADV

/ ɑf ðə tʃɑrts / (AmE), / ɒf ðə tʃɑːts / (BrE)
extremely high in level, amount, or degree; far beyond the usual limits
extremely high or extreme

Examples

The energy in the stadium was off the charts during the final match.
Her enthusiasm for the project is off the charts.
Sales numbers this quarter are off the charts compared to last year.

Synset Examples

His jokes were off the charts on the cringeometer.
