in your world

1. ADV

/ ɪn jʊr wɝːld / (AmE), / ɪn jɔː wɜːld / (BrE)
used to refer to someone's personal point of view, experience, or imagination, often suggesting it differs from reality or from others' perspectives.
from your personal perspective or imagination

Examples

In your world, maybe money grows on trees, but in reality, things are different.
She always seems lost in her world, dreaming of adventures I'll never understand.
What would happen in your world if everyone could fly?

Synset Examples

When dreaming, what color is the sun in your world?
