made of stone

1. ADJ

/ meɪd əv stoʊn / (AmE), / meɪd əv stəʊn / (BrE)
showing no emotion or sympathy; emotionally unresponsive or tough.
emotionally unfeeling or tough

Examples

Even when she received bad news, she remained made of stone and showed no emotion.
His boss seemed made of stone, never praising anyone or showing kindness.
Some people think you need to be made of stone to survive in that business.

Synset Examples

He approached challenges like he was made of stone.
After that loss, he felt made of stone inside.
Her heart seemed made of stone, unfeeling and cold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heart of stone - an idiom meaning a cruel or unfeeling nature
