to put the bad mouth on

1. VERB

/ pʊt ðə bæd maʊθ ɑn / (AmE), / pʊt ðə bæd maʊθ ɒn / (BrE)
to criticize or speak unfavorably about someone or something
criticize or speak badly of
informal

Examples

Why do you always put the bad mouth on your coworkers when they're not around?
She got upset when she heard people put the bad mouth on her new business.
Please don't put the bad mouth on the book before you've actually read it.

Synset Examples

I heard he put the bad mouth on the new project.
She tends to put the bad mouth on those she dislikes.
Don't put the bad mouth on my friend; he means well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to badmouth - to speak critically or disparagingly about someone or something
