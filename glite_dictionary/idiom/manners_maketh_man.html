manners maketh man

1. X

/ ˈmænərz ˈmeɪkəθ mæn / (AmE), / ˈmænəz ˈmeɪkɪθ man / (BrE)
good manners are essential to one's character and show a person's true nature
good manners define one's character (idiom)

Examples

In school assemblies, the principal would remind us that manners maketh man.
He always holds the door open for others, truly believing that manners maketh man.
Her grandmother often quoted, 'manners maketh man,' to encourage polite behavior.

Synset Examples

In our family, we believe that manners maketh man.
His mother always said, 'manners maketh man'.
She taught her children that manners maketh man.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good manners - polite or respectful behavior

Etymology

A proverbial expression dating to at least the 15th century; 'maketh' is archaic English for 'makes'.
