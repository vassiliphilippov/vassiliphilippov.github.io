to cry over spilt milk

1. VERB

/ kraɪ ˈoʊvɚ spɪlt mɪlk / (AmE), / kraɪ ˈəʊvə spɪlt mɪlk / (BrE)
to be upset or waste time worrying about something that has already happened and cannot be changed
worry about the past; regret something unchangeable

Examples

There's no point in crying over spilt milk; let's focus on solving the problem.
Even though the project failed, she refused to cry over spilt milk.
They told him not to cry over spilt milk and just move on with his life.
