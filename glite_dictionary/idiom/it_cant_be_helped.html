it can't be helped

1. X

/ ɪt kænt bi hɛlpt / (AmE), / ɪt kɑːnt bi hɛlpt / (BrE)
used to express acceptance of a situation that cannot be changed or avoided, often with a sense of resignation
expression of resigned acceptance

Examples

The flight was canceled due to snow, and it can't be helped.
We missed the deadline, but it can't be helped now.
Sometimes things just go wrong; it can't be helped.

Synset Examples

If the weather ruins our plans, it can't be helped.
When delays occur, it can't be helped; we must wait.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: that's life - expression used to accept disappointing or unlucky events
  • Synonym: such is life - used to express acceptance when bad things happen
