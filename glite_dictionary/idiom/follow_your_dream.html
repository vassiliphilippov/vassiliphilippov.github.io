to follow your dream

1. VERB

/ ˈfɑloʊ jʊr driːm / (AmE), / ˈfɒləʊ jɔː driːm / (BrE)
to pursue your personal ambitions or aspirations, often despite difficulties or obstacles
pursue your aspirations

Examples

She decided to follow her dream and open a bakery in her hometown.
Many people move to big cities to follow their dream of becoming an actor.
Despite the obstacles, he continued to follow his dream of becoming a doctor.

Synset Examples

Her success will spur others to follow their dreams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pursue your dream - try to achieve your ambition
