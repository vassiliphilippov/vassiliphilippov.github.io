worth it

1. ADJ

/ wɜrθ ɪt / (AmE), / wɜːθ ɪt / (BrE)
deserving the time, effort, or money spent; justified by the outcome or benefits
deserving the effort or cost

Examples

Climbing to the top was exhausting, but the breathtaking view made it worth it.
Do you think renovating the old house is worth it?
She spent hours studying, and when she passed the exam, she knew it was worth it.

Synset Examples

The hike was rocky, but the view was worth it.
Trust me, I tell you it’s worth it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worthwhile - useful, important, or deserving of time and effort
