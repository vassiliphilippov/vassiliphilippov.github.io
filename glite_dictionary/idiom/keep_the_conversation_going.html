to keep the conversation going

1. VERB

/ kip ðə ˌkɑnvərˈseɪʃən ˈɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / kiːp ðə ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃən ˈɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
to maintain or continue a dialogue or discussion without allowing it to lapse
maintain ongoing dialogue

Examples

She asked several questions to keep the conversation going during the interview.
He told a funny story just to keep the conversation going at dinner.
Sometimes you need to share a bit about yourself to keep the conversation going.

Synset Examples

He simply replied, 'Yes. And?' to keep the conversation going.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: break the ice - to initiate conversation in a social situation
