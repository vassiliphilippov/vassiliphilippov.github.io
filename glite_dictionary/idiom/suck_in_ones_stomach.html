to suck in one's stomach

1. VERB

/ sʌk ɪn wʌnz stʌmək / (AmE), / sʌk ɪn wʌnz stʌmək / (BrE)
to contract the abdominal muscles in order to make the stomach appear slimmer
contract abdomen to appear slimmer

Examples

He tried to suck in his stomach as he walked past the mirror.
Whenever the photographer said smile, she would quickly suck in her stomach.
If you suck in your stomach all day, it can become uncomfortable.

Synset Examples

He tried to suck in his stomach for the photo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hold in one's stomach - to keep one's abdominal muscles tense to look slimmer
