on a high note

1. ADV

/ ɑn ə haɪ noʊt / (AmE), / ɒn ə haɪ nəʊt / (BrE)
in a way that is successful or positive, especially at the end of something
ending positively or successfully

Examples

The team finished the season on a high note by winning their last game.
Let's try to end the meeting on a high note with some good news.
After a difficult year, she wanted to start the new one on a high note.

Synset Examples

The meeting concluded on a high note with great ideas shared.
He always tries to start the day on a high note.
She ended her performance on a high note, leaving the audience cheering.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: on a low note - ending in a disappointing or negative way
