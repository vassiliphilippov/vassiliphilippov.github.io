pardonnez mon français

1. X

/ paʁ.dɔ.ne mɔ̃ fʁɑ̃.sɛ / (AmE), / paʁ.dɔ.ne mɔ̃ fʁɑ̃.sɛ / (BrE)
A humorous or playful expression, borrowed from French, meaning 'forgive my language,' typically used when someone is about to use or has used offensive or strong language.
humorous phrase excusing language

Examples

She smiled and said, 'pardonnez mon français,' before uttering a mild swear word.
He blushed and muttered, 'pardonnez mon français,' after making a crude joke.
Before voicing her frustration, she added jokingly, 'pardonnez mon français.'

Synset Examples

She said, 'pardonnez mon français,' before making a mistake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pardon my French - idiomatic English phrase used before or after swearing or using improper language

Etymology

Borrowed from French, literally meaning 'forgive my French.'
