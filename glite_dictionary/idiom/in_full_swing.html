in full swing

1. ADV

/ ɪn fʊl swɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪn fʊl swɪŋ / (BrE)
at the peak of activity, fully active or operating with maximum energy
at the peak of activity

Examples

By midnight, the celebration was in full swing and everyone was dancing.
Construction on the new bridge is in full swing despite the rainy weather.
The conference was in full swing when I arrived late in the morning.
