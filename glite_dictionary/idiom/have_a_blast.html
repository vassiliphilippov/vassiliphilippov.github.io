to have a blast

1. VERB

/ hæv ə blæst / (AmE), / hæv ə blɑːst / (BrE)
to have a very enjoyable or exciting time; to have a lot of fun
enjoy oneself greatly; have a lot of fun
informal

Examples

We had a blast celebrating my birthday at the beach last weekend.
The kids had a blast playing games at the party.
Everyone had a blast at the concert despite the rain.
