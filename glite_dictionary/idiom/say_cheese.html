to say cheese

1. VERB

/ seɪ tʃiːz / (AmE), / seɪ tʃiːz / (BrE)
used to prompt people to smile when taking a photograph
prompt to smile for a photo

Examples

The photographer asked everyone to say cheese before snapping the picture.
Whenever we take family photos, my mom always says, "Say cheese!" to make us smile.
You can hear the children shout "say cheese!" as they pose for the group photo.

Synset Examples

Everyone laughed when the kids forgot to say cheese.
She reminded him to say cheese before the flash.
The photographer told us to say cheese for the picture.
