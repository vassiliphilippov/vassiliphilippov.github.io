the living end

1. NOUN

/ ðə ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌɛnd / (AmE), / ðə ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌɛnd / (BrE)
something or someone considered the best, most impressive, or most exciting of its kind; the ultimate.
the ultimate or best

Examples

Everyone agreed that the concert was the living end and talked about it for weeks.
That new restaurant really is the living end; the food and atmosphere are unbeatable.
When he bought his dream car, he felt like he owned the living end of automobiles.

Synset Examples

That party was the living end of fun!
The vacation was the living end of relaxation.
Her performance was the living end of entertainment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the last word - the ultimate example; something considered definitive or the best
  • Synonym: the be-all and end-all - the most important or ultimate factor
