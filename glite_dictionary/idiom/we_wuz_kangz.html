we wuz kangz

1. X

/ wi wʌz kæŋz / (AmE), / wi wʌz kæŋz / (BrE)
A derogatory internet meme phrase mocking perceived revisionist claims about African civilizations, often using exaggerated African American Vernacular English.
derogatory meme mocking claims about African history
offensive slang

Examples

The phrase we wuz kangz is often used online to ridicule exaggerated historical claims.
He got banned from the forum for repeatedly posting we wuz kangz memes.
Many people find the use of we wuz kangz to be offensive and insulting.

Synset Examples

Some people still claim we wuz kangz.
He joked about how 'we wuz kangz' in history class.
The phrase 'we wuz kangz' is often misused.

Etymology

Deliberate misspelling and mock use of African American Vernacular English ('we were kings'); became popularized as an internet meme in the 2010s.
