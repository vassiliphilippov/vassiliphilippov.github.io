to give the lie to

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ðə laɪ tu / (AmE), / ɡɪv ðə laɪ tuː / (BrE)
to show or prove that something is false or untrue
reveal as false or untrue

Examples

Her calm manner gave the lie to any suggestion that she was nervous.
The report gives the lie to earlier statements made by the company.
Recent discoveries give the lie to the theory that the site was uninhabited.

Synset Examples

Witnesses can give the lie to his story.
His actions give the lie to his words.
The facts give the lie to the rumors.
The evidence gives the lie to his alibi.
Her smile gives the lie to her sadness.
Her behavior gives the lie to her claims of innocence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to disprove - to show that something is not true or correct
