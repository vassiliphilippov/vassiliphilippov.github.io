to shot through like a Bondi tram

1. VERB

/ ʃɑt θru laɪk ə ˈbɒn.di træm / (AmE), / ʃɒt θruː laɪk ə ˈbɒn.di træm / (BrE)
(Australian, informal) to leave or depart very quickly or hurriedly.
depart quickly (Australian idiom)
informal british

Examples

As soon as the meeting ended, she shot through like a Bondi tram.
He shot through like a Bondi tram before anyone could ask him questions.
When trouble started, the boys shot through like a Bondi tram.

Synset Examples

She finished her work and shot through like a Bondi tram.
When the deadline approached, he shot through like a Bondi tram.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoot through - to leave or depart quickly (Australian slang)

Etymology

Refers to trams serving Bondi in Sydney, Australia, known for their speed; Australian slang.
