up for grabs

1. ADJ

/ ʌp fər ˈɡræbz / (AmE), / ʌp fə ˈɡræbz / (BrE)
available to be taken, used, or won by anyone; not yet claimed or decided upon
available for anyone to take or claim

Examples

The lead in the championship is still up for grabs with one race remaining.
With so many prizes up for grabs, everyone has a chance to win something.
After the merger, several jobs will be up for grabs in the new company structure.

Synset Examples

Tickets to the concert are up for grabs tomorrow.
The job position is still up for grabs.
The last piece of cake is up for grabs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: available - able to be used or obtained
  • Antonym: unavailable - not able to be used or obtained
