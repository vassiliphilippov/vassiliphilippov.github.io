to beat as one

1. VERB

/ biːt æz wʌn / (AmE), / biːt æz wʌn / (BrE)
to act or function in perfect harmony or unison, especially as a coordinated group
act in complete unison

Examples

After weeks of practice, the dancers learned to beat as one during the performance.
In times of crisis, the citizens must beat as one to overcome challenges.
The choir was able to beat as one, creating a powerful sense of unity.
