in the pipeline

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ðə ˈpaɪplaɪn / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈpaɪplaɪn / (BrE)
being planned, prepared, or developed and likely to happen or appear soon
in progress or development

Examples

The marketing team has several campaigns in the pipeline for next quarter.
A major update to the software is currently in the pipeline and will launch soon.
Several job openings are in the pipeline, so we're not hiring just yet.

Synset Examples

We have several candidates in the pipeline for the role.
The new app features are still in the pipeline.
There are changes coming soon; they’re in the pipeline.
There’s a new project in the pipeline for next year.
We have several new projects in the pipeline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underway - in progress; being done or carried out
  • Antonym: on hold - delayed or temporarily stopped

Etymology

From the imagery of material moving through a pipe, used figuratively since the mid-20th century.
