to refresh my mind

1. VERB

/ rɪˈfrɛʃ maɪ maɪnd / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɛʃ maɪ maɪnd / (BrE)
to clear one's thoughts or regain mental clarity, especially after fatigue or distraction
regain mental clarity or focus

Examples

A quick nap always helps to refresh my mind when I'm feeling tired at work.
She went outside to refresh her mind after a long meeting.
Reading fiction in the evening helps refresh my mind and reduce stress.

Synset Examples

I need to refresh my mind with a short walk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clear my head - to remove mental fatigue or confusion
  • Synonym: collect my thoughts - to compose oneself and think clearly again
