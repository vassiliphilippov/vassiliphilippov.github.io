in no time

1. ADV

/ ɪn noʊ taɪm / (AmE), / ɪn nəʊ taɪm / (BrE)
very quickly; in a very short amount of time
very quickly; very soon

Examples

She finished packing in no time and was ready to leave early.
The technician fixed my computer in no time at all.
Don’t worry, you’ll understand the rules in no time.

Synset Examples

she finished the assignment in no time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quickly - at a fast speed; rapidly
  • Synonym: in a flash - very quickly; immediately
  • Antonym: slowly - at a slow speed
