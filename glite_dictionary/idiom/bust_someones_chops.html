to bust someone's chops

1. VERB

/ bʌst sʌmˌwʌnz tʃɑps / (AmE), / bʌst sʌmˌwʌnz tʃɒps / (BrE)
to nag, annoy, or tease someone, often in a persistent or playful manner
tease or annoy someone
informal american

Examples

He's always busting my chops about how slowly I work.
If you keep busting her chops, she's going to get really annoyed.
I wish you would stop busting my chops and give me a break.

Synset Examples

Stop busting my chops and let me focus!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tease - to make fun of or try to provoke in a playful way
  • Synonym: to pester - to annoy someone persistently
  • Antonym: praise - to express admiration or approval

Etymology

American slang; 'chops' here refers to the jaws or mouth, as in talking.
