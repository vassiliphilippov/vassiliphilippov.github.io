pobody's nerfect

1. X

/ ˈpoʊˌbɑdiz ˈnɜrˌfɪkt / (AmE), / ˈpəʊˌbɒdiz ˈnɜːˌfɪkt / (BrE)
A humorous way of saying 'nobody's perfect', usually to acknowledge human imperfection after a mistake.
humorous way to admit imperfection
informal

Examples

Whenever someone admits a mistake, I like to remind them that pobody's nerfect.
He shrugged off the criticism with a grin, saying, 'Pobody's nerfect.'
After misspelling a word in her speech, she joked, 'Pobody's nerfect!'.

Synset Examples

Remember, pobody's nerfect; we all make mistakes.
She laughed, saying pobody's nerfect after her slip-up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nobody's perfect - no one is without faults or mistakes

Etymology

Humorous alteration of 'nobody's perfect' by transposing the initial consonant sounds.
