to feel like a kidsicle

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə ˈkɪdˌsɪkəl / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈkɪdˌsɪkl̩ / (BrE)
to feel excessively hot and uncomfortable, as if melting in the heat
feel extremely hot and uncomfortable
informal

Examples

After mowing the lawn in July, I felt like a kidsicle dripping in the sun.
Sitting in the crowded subway, she began to feel like a kidsicle as the temperature rose.
By the time the air conditioning broke down, we all felt like kidsicles in the house.

Synset Examples

The summer heat made her feel like a kidsicle.

Etymology

Playful blend of 'kid' and 'popsicle', likening oneself to a melting popsicle.
