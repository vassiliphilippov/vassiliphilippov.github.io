by the same token

1. ADV

/ baɪ ðə seɪm ˈtoʊkən / (AmE), / baɪ ðə seɪm ˈtəʊkən / (BrE)
used to introduce a statement that is similar to or follows logically from a previous one, meaning 'for the same reason' or 'similarly'
for the same reason; similarly

Examples

She prefers tea over coffee; by the same token, she rarely drinks soda.
Tom is very detail-oriented; by the same token, his reports are always thorough.
She finds loud noises distracting; by the same token, she avoids crowded places.

Synset Examples

She loves hiking; by the same token, she enjoys nature walks.
He’s a great cook; by the same token, he bakes wonderfully.
She studies hard; by the same token, she plays hard.
