to suit my book

1. VERB

/ suːt maɪ bʊk / (AmE), / suːt maɪ bʊk / (BrE)
to be convenient for or in accordance with one's own wishes, preferences, or plans
to fit one's preferences or plans

Examples

The early morning meeting doesn't suit my book as I'm not an early riser.
If it suits your book, we could reschedule the discussion for next week.
Her proposed solution perfectly suits my book and priorities.

Synset Examples

That job offer really suits my book and career goals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suit me - to be acceptable or convenient for me
