to put butter on someone's bread

1. VERB

/ pʊt ˈbʌtər ɑn ˈsʌmwʌnz brɛd / (AmE), / pʊt ˈbʌtə ɒn ˈsʌmwʌnz brɛd / (BrE)
to provide someone with financial support or the means to earn a living
provide financial support or livelihood

Examples

This new contract finally puts butter on my bread after years of struggling.
She works two jobs to put butter on her family's bread.
His carpentry skills put butter on his bread throughout hard times.

Synset Examples

This job really puts butter on my bread.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to put food on the table - to provide enough income to feed oneself or one's family
