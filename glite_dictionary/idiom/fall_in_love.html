to fall in love

1. VERB

/ fɔl ɪn lʌv / (AmE), / fɔːl ɪn lʌv / (BrE)
to develop strong romantic feelings for someone or something
develop romantic feelings

Examples

It's easy to fall in love when you're traveling to new and beautiful places.
Many people fell in love with the book's main character after the first chapter.
She realized she was falling in love with him after their third date.

Synset Examples

She fell in love with the charm of Bari's old town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fall for - to become attracted to someone emotionally or romantically
