now now

1. INTJ

/ naʊ ˈnaʊ / (AmE), / naʊ ˈnaʊ / (BrE)
Used as a gentle way of expressing disapproval or urging someone to calm down or behave.
gentle mild rebuke

Examples

Now now, there's no need to raise your voice.
Now now, don't be so quick to judge.
“Now now, everything will be alright,” she said soothingly.
