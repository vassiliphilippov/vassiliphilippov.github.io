to ring a bell

1. VERB

/ rɪŋ ə bɛl / (AmE), / rɪŋ ə bɛl / (BrE)
to sound familiar to someone or cause someone to remember something; to evoke a memory
sound familiar or recall a memory

Examples

His name doesn't ring a bell, but I might recognize his face.
Does this photo ring a bell for you?
That strange word rings a bell, but I can't remember where I heard it.

Synset Examples

Does that story ring a bell with anyone?
The name doesn't ring a bell for me.
That song really rings a bell from my childhood.
That movie definitely rings her bell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jog someone's memory - to help someone remember something they had forgotten
  • Synonym: sound familiar - to seem familiar or recognizable
