to feel like a colossus

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə kəˈlɑsəs / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə kəˈlɒsəs / (BrE)
to feel extremely powerful, important, or strong
feel extremely powerful or strong

Examples

After winning the debate, she felt like a colossus in front of her classmates.
Completing the project ahead of schedule made him feel like a colossus at work.
Standing on top of the mountain, I felt like a colossus, overlooking the world.

Synset Examples

He felt like a colossus after finishing the marathon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feel invincible - to feel impossible to defeat or overcome
  • Antonym: feel insignificant - to feel unimportant or lacking power

Etymology

From 'colossus', meaning a statue of gigantic size; figuratively, a person or thing of enormous size, importance, or ability.
