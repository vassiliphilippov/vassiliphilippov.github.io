to cross paths

1. VERB

/ krɔs pæθs / (AmE), / krɒs pɑːθs / (BrE)
to meet or encounter someone, often unexpectedly
meet or encounter by chance

Examples

We finally crossed paths again after many years apart.
If you ever cross paths with her, be sure to say hello.
Their careers crossed paths during a project in London.

Synset Examples

I hope we cross paths again someday.
We might cross paths at the reunion next month.
Their lives unexpectedly crossed paths at the conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to run into - to meet someone by chance
  • Synonym: encounter - to meet or come across someone unexpectedly
