to act like a mirror

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈmɪrɚ / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈmɪrə / (BrE)
to reflect an image or light perfectly, as a mirror does
to reflect perfectly, like a mirror

Examples

The calm sea acted like a mirror, reflecting the evening sky in stunning detail.
After the rain stopped, the wet pavement acted like a mirror and showed our reflections clearly.
The new skyscraper's glass walls act like a mirror, bouncing sunlight across the city.

Synset Examples

The lake acted like a mirror, reflecting the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to reflect - to throw back light, heat, or an image from a surface
