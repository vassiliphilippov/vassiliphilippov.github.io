up to (one's) neck

1. ADJ

/ ʌp tə wʌnz nɛk / (AmE), / ʌp tə wʌnz nɛk / (BrE)
deeply involved in or burdened by something, especially a difficult or overwhelming situation
deeply involved or burdened

Examples

She's up to her neck in bills after buying that new car.
We're up to our necks in work this week, so I can't take any more projects.
After agreeing to organize the event, he found himself up to his neck in responsibilities.

Synset Examples

neck-deep in work
up to their necks in debt

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neck-deep - deeply involved in something
