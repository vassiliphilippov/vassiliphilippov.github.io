to draw the curtain

1. VERB

/ drɔ ðə kɝːtn̩ / (AmE), / drɔː ðə kɜːtn / (BrE)
to close a curtain by pulling it across a window or opening
to close the curtain

Examples

Could you draw the curtain so we can watch the movie in the dark?
Every evening, she draws the curtain before turning on the lights.
He quietly drew the curtain to keep the room warm.

Synset Examples

Can you draw the curtains for some privacy?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: open the curtain - to pull the curtain aside so it is not covering the window
