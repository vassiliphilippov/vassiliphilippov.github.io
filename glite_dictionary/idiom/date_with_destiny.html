date with destiny

1. NOUN

/ deɪt wɪð ˈdɛstɪni / (AmE), / deɪt wɪð ˈdɛstɪni / (BrE)
an event or occasion regarded as having great importance or as determining one's future
fateful or pivotal event

Examples

The election results marked the country's date with destiny as a new era began.
Standing at the altar, he knew it was his date with destiny.
For the astronauts, the launch day was a true date with destiny.

Synset Examples

She believed her audition was a date with destiny.
The championship game was their date with destiny.
Graduation day felt like a date with destiny.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turning point - a time at which a decisive change in a situation occurs
  • Synonym: watershed - an event marking a unique or important historical change of course
