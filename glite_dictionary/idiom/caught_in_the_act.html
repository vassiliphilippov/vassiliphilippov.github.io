caught in the act

1. X

/ kɔt ɪn ði ækt / (AmE), / kɔːt ɪn ði ækt / (BrE)
discovered while doing something, especially something wrong or forbidden
discovered doing something wrong

Examples

The thief was caught in the act by security cameras last night.
She was caught in the act of cheating during the exam.
He denied everything until he was caught in the act by his parents.

Synset Examples

He got caught in the act of a nose-pick.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red-handed - discovered in the process of committing a crime or fault
