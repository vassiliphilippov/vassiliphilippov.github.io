reduced to ashes

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈdust tu ˈæʃɪz / (AmE), / rɪˈdjuːst tuː ˈaʃɪz / (BrE)
completely destroyed by burning so that only ashes remain
destroyed by burning into ashes

Examples

The old mansion was reduced to ashes after the fierce wildfire swept through the valley.
His dreams were reduced to ashes by the devastating news.
The documents were accidentally reduced to ashes in the office fire.

Synset Examples

He watched as the paper burned, reduced to ashes in flames.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burn to the ground - completely destroyed by fire
  • Synonym: to incinerate - to burn something completely so that only ashes remain
