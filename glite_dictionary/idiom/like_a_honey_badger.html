like a honey badger

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə ˈhʌni ˈbædʒər / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈhʌni ˈbadʒə / (BrE)
bravely or fiercely, without fear or hesitation, often in the face of challenges or danger
fearless; bravely or fiercely
informal

Examples

Despite criticism, she defended her decision like a honey badger.
He tackled each obstacle at work like a honey badger, never backing down.
Whenever he feels threatened, he reacts like a honey badger and stands his ground.

Synset Examples

He faced challenges like a honey badger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fearless - not afraid; brave
  • Antonym: timid - lacking courage or confidence; easily frightened

Etymology

Refers to the honey badger (Mellivora capensis), known for its fearless behavior.
