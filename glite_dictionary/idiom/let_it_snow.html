let it snow

1. X

/ ˌlɛt ɪt ˈsnoʊ / (AmE), / ˌlɛt ɪt ˈsnəʊ / (BrE)
used to express acceptance or welcome of snowfall, especially during the winter or holiday season.
expression welcoming snowfall

Examples

The forecast predicts more flurries tonight, so I say let it snow!
Everyone was excited for a white Christmas and happily sang 'Let it snow, let it snow!'
With warm cocoa in hand, she looked out the window and whispered, 'let it snow.'

Synset Examples

The holidays are here, so let it snow!
