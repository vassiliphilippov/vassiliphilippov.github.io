to get religion about

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ɹɪˈlɪdʒən əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ɡɛt ɹɪˈlɪdʒ.ən əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
(informal) To become serious or committed about a particular issue or course of action.
become serious or committed
informal

Examples

It's time to get religion about your spending habits if you want to save more.
Many companies are starting to get religion about cybersecurity after recent breaches.
She finally got religion about her health and started exercising regularly.

Synset Examples

It’s time to get religion about your spending habits.

Etymology

Based on the literal phrase 'get religion', originally meaning to adopt religious faith; extended figuratively to commitment in general.
