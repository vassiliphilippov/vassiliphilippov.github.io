to end one's life

1. VERB

/ ɛnd wʌnz laɪf / (AmE), / ɛnd wʌnz laɪf / (BrE)
to kill oneself; to commit suicide
commit suicide

Examples

In times of despair, some people may contemplate ways to end their life.
