tough break

1. NOUN

/ tʌf breɪk / (AmE), / tʌf breɪk / (BrE)
an unlucky or unfortunate event; a setback
an unlucky setback or misfortune
informal

Examples

It was a tough break when Sarah lost her job just before the holidays.
Missing the train by seconds was a tough break for him this morning.
They suffered another tough break when their star player was injured.

Synset Examples

Losing the game was a tough break for the team.
Getting injured just before the finals was a tough break.
It's a tough break, but we need to keep moving forward.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad luck - unfortunate chance; misfortune
  • Synonym: setback - a reversal or check in progress
