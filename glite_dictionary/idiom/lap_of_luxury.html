lap of luxury

1. NOUN

/ læp əv ˈlʌkʃəri / (AmE), / læp əv ˈlʌkʃəri / (BrE)
a state of great comfort and wealth; living in very luxurious conditions
in great comfort and wealth

Examples

After winning the lottery, she found herself living in the lap of luxury in a penthouse apartment.
He never knew hardship, having grown up in the lap of luxury since childhood.
The spa promises to let guests relax in the lap of luxury for a weekend getaway.

Synset Examples

he was raised in the lap of luxury

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luxury - great comfort, wealth, and expensive living
