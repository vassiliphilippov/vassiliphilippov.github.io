with kid gloves

1. ADV

/ wɪð kɪd ɡlʌvz / (AmE), / wɪð kɪd ɡlɒvz / (BrE)
in a very careful, tactful, or gentle way to avoid upsetting someone or making a situation worse
very cautiously or delicately

Examples

The boss handled the employee's complaints with kid gloves to avoid any potential conflict.
You need to approach this sensitive matter with kid gloves if you want a positive outcome.
After the incident, everyone treated her with kid gloves for several months.

Synset Examples

she ventured cautiously downstairs
they handled the incident with kid gloves

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gently - in a way that is soft or careful
  • Antonym: harshly - in a rough or severe way
  • Synonym: cautiously - in a careful way to avoid danger or mistakes

Etymology

Refers to gloves made from kid (young goat) leather, which were soft and delicate; the phrase has been used since the 19th century.
