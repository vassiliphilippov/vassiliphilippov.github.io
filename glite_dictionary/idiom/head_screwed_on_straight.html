head screwed on straight

1. ADJ

/ hɛd skruːd ɑn streɪt / (AmE), / hɛd skruːd ɒn streɪt / (BrE)
having good judgment or practical sense; sensible.
sensible; having good judgment

Examples

Everyone trusts Jill because she really has her head screwed on straight.
After talking to him, I could tell he had his head screwed on straight when it came to business.
If you want sensible advice, ask Maya—she always has her head screwed on straight.

Synset Examples

She has her head screwed on straight, making wise decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensible - showing good sense or judgment
  • Synonym: practical - concerned with actual use; realistic and pragmatic
  • Antonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment
