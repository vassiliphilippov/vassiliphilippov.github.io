holy moley

1. INTJ

/ ˌhoʊli ˈmoʊli / (AmE), / ˌhəʊli ˈməʊli / (BrE)
An exclamation used to express surprise, amazement, or disbelief.
expression of surprise or amazement
informal

Examples

Holy moley, I can't believe we actually won the contest!
Holy moley, that's the biggest pizza I've ever seen!
When she saw the price tag, she whispered, 'holy moley.'

Synset Examples

I just found twenty bucks—holy moley!
Holy moley, look at that massive wave!
Holy moley, this cake is delicious!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holy cow - an expression of surprise or amazement
  • Synonym: holy mackerel - an expression of surprise or amazement
  • Synonym: to wow - an exclamation of surprise or admiration

Etymology

Alteration of 'holy Moses', a mild euphemism that avoids taking a religious figure's name in vain.
