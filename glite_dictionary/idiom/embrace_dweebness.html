to embrace dweebness

1. VERB

/ ɪmˈbreɪs ˈdwiːbnəs / (AmE), / ɪmˈbreɪs ˈdwiːbnəs / (BrE)
to accept and take pride in one's nerdy or socially awkward qualities rather than hiding them
accept one's nerdy or awkward traits
informal

Examples

After years of pretending to be someone else, he finally decided to embrace dweebness and be himself.
Many students find it empowering to embrace dweebness instead of feeling ashamed of their interests.
She encourages her followers to embrace dweebness and celebrate what makes them unique.

Synset Examples

He embraced his dweebness rather than trying to fit in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embrace - to accept something willingly or enthusiastically
  • Synonym: accept oneself - to recognize and be comfortable with one's own characteristics
  • Synonym: dweeb - a person regarded as socially awkward or nerdy
