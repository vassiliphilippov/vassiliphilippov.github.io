nine-day wonder

1. NOUN

/ ˌnaɪn deɪ ˈwʌndər / (AmE), / ˌnaɪn deɪ ˈwʌndə / (BrE)
Something that is briefly popular or attracts great attention but is soon forgotten.
something briefly popular, soon forgotten

Examples

The invention was a nine-day wonder and was soon forgotten by the public.
Her viral video became a nine-day wonder, but the fame quickly faded.
Many internet memes are just a nine-day wonder in today's fast-paced world.

Synset Examples

Their project was a nine-day wonder, quickly forgotten.
The nine-day wonder faded as quickly as it started.
The new trend turned out to be a nine-day wonder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flash in the pan - something that suddenly attracts attention or success but lasts only briefly
  • Synonym: fad - an intense and widely shared enthusiasm for something, especially one that is short-lived

Etymology

Refers to an old English proverb about events that arouse public interest for only nine days.
