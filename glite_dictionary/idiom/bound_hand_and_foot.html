bound hand and foot

1. ADJ

/ baʊnd hænd ənd fʊt / (AmE), / baʊnd hænd ənd fʊt / (BrE)
completely restricted or constrained; prevented from acting freely or independently
completely constrained or restricted

Examples

She felt bound hand and foot by her responsibilities at work.
The organization was bound hand and foot by government regulations.
Despite their efforts, they remained bound hand and foot and unable to take decisive action.

Synset Examples

They felt bound hand and foot by the strict rules.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tied up - prevented from acting freely, restricted
  • Antonym: free - not restricted or constrained
