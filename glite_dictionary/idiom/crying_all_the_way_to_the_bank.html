crying all the way to the bank

1. X

/ ˈkraɪɪŋ ˌɔl ðə weɪ tə ðə bæŋk / (AmE), / ˈkraɪɪŋ ˌɔːl ðə weɪ tə ðə bæŋk / (BrE)
to make money or profit easily, especially while pretending to be unhappy or despite criticism or adversity
profit despite adversity or criticism

Examples

After his critics laughed at his investment, he was crying all the way to the bank when it paid off.
Though the film was panned by reviewers, the director ended up crying all the way to the bank.
She may act upset about the merger, but she's really crying all the way to the bank.

Synset Examples

He'll be crying all the way to the bank after this deal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: making a killing - earning a large amount of money quickly or easily
  • Antonym: losing out - not benefiting or gaining, especially financially
