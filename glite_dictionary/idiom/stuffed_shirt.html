stuffed shirt

1. NOUN

/ stʌft ʃɝːt / (AmE), / stʌft ʃɜːt / (BrE)
a person who is overly formal, pompous, and old-fashioned, often considered dull or tedious
a pompous, overly formal person

Examples

The new manager turned out to be a real stuffed shirt, insisting on strict rules for everything.
Everyone at the party steered clear of Tom because he was such a stuffed shirt.
Despite his qualifications, his stuffed shirt attitude made him unpopular with colleagues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snob - a person who believes they are superior to others
  • Synonym: bore - a person who is dull or uninteresting

2. NOUN

/ stʌft ʃɝt / (AmE), / stʌft ʃɜːt / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, who behaves in a very formal, self-important, or pompous way
pompous or overly formal person

Examples

The new CEO turned out to be just another stuffed shirt with no original ideas.
Nobody wanted to sit next to the stuffed shirt at dinner because he never joked or relaxed.
Critics described the politician as a typical stuffed shirt who never really connected with the public.

Synset Examples

He acts like such a stuffed shirt at parties.
Nobody likes to work with that stuffed shirt manager.
The meeting was led by a stuffed shirt who ignored everyone's ideas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snob - a person who looks down on others; considers themselves superior
  • Synonym: egotist - a person who is excessively conceited or self-absorbed
  • Antonym: modest person - someone who is humble and not self-important
