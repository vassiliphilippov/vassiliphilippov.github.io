see his last gum tree

1. X

/ si hɪz læst ɡʌm tri / (AmE), / siː hɪz lɑːst ɡʌm triː / (BrE)
(of a person) to be at the end of their career, prospects, or existence; to be finished or done for.
be finished or done for (idiomatic)

Examples

After the accident, it seemed that Tom had seen his last gum tree and would never race again.
The coach knew he had seen his last gum tree when the team lost every game that season.
If the company doesn't improve its finances soon, it will have seen its last gum tree.

Synset Examples

After that performance, he has seen his last gum tree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: done for - to be finished or ruined
