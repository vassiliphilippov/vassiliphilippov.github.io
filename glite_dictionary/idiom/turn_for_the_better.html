turn for the better

1. NOUN

/ tɝn fər ðə ˈbɛtər / (AmE), / tɜːn fə ðə ˈbɛtə / (BrE)
a change that leads to an improvement in a situation or circumstances
improvement in a situation

Examples

After several setbacks, the company's fortunes finally took a turn for the better.
The weather took a turn for the better just in time for our vacation.
His mood showed a noticeable turn for the better after he heard the good news.

Synset Examples

The project took a turn for the better with new funding.
Her health took a turn for the better after treatment.
Their relationship took a turn for the better after counseling.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: turn for the worse - a change that makes a situation worse
