late for one's own funeral

1. X

/ leɪt fər wʌnz oʊn ˈfjunərəl / (AmE), / leɪt fə wʌnz əʊn ˈfjuːnərəl / (BrE)
(humorous) Extremely likely to be late; said of someone who is habitually or notoriously tardy.
extremely habitually late (humorous idiom)

Examples

If Tom doesn't leave soon, he'll be late for his own funeral!
She's so slow getting ready she'll probably be late for her own funeral.
My boss is perpetually tardy—they say he'll be late for his own funeral.

Synset Examples

He’s always late; he'll be late for his own funeral.
If you don’t hurry, you'll be late for your own funeral!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: always late - habitually late; not arriving on time
