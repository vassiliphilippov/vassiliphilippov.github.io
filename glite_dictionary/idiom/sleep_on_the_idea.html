to sleep on the idea

1. VERB

/ sliːp ɑn ði aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / sliːp ɒn ði aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
to postpone making a decision about something until after considering it overnight
consider something overnight before deciding

Examples

I need some time to sleep on the idea before giving you an answer.
Before making such a big change, she wanted to sleep on the idea.
He advised his team to sleep on the idea and discuss it again in the morning.

Synset Examples

She asked him to sleep on the idea before responding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sleep on it - to delay making a decision until the next day
