to stay hungry for knowledge

1. VERB

/ steɪ ˈhʌŋɡri fɚ ˈnɑlɪdʒ / (AmE), / steɪ ˈhʌŋɡri fə ˈnɒlɪdʒ / (BrE)
to continue to have a strong and eager desire to learn new things
maintain a strong desire to learn

Examples

As a teacher, I always encourage my students to stay hungry for knowledge throughout their lives.
She believes that to succeed in any field, you must stay hungry for knowledge.
Even after years in his profession, he continues to stay hungry for knowledge and improve himself.

Synset Examples

To succeed, you must stay hungry for knowledge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curious - eager to know or learn something
  • Antonym: complacent - satisfied with how things are, lacking desire to improve or learn
