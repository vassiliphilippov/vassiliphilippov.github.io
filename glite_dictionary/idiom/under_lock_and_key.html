under lock and key

1. ADV

/ ˌʌndɚ ˌlɑk ənd ˈki / (AmE), / ˌʌndə ˌlɒk ənd ˈki / (BrE)
kept in a place that is securely locked; safely stored or protected from access or theft
securely locked away

Examples

The secret formula is kept under lock and key at the headquarters.
She stores her important jewelry under lock and key for safety.
All confidential files remain under lock and key when not in use.

Synset Examples

The valuable documents are kept under lock and key.
Her diary was under lock and key in her room.
He secured the treasure chest under lock and key.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secure - safe from danger or loss; free from threat
  • Antonym: exposed - not protected or covered; vulnerable to access or danger
