no fun at parties

1. ADJ

/ noʊ fʌn ət ˈpɑrtiz / (AmE), / nəʊ fʌn ət ˈpɑːtiz / (BrE)
(of a person) not enjoyable to be around or lacking liveliness in social gatherings.
not lively or enjoyable at social events

Examples

Everyone says Mark is no fun at parties because he never jokes or joins in the games.
I thought she'd be outgoing, but she turned out to be no fun at parties.
If you're always quiet and reserved, people might think you're no fun at parties.

Synset Examples

She doesn’t dance; she’s no fun at parties.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: life of the party - a person who is lively and enjoyable at social gatherings
