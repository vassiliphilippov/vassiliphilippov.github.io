install a screen door on a submarine

1. X

/ ɪnˈstɔl ə skrin dɔr ɑn ə ˌsʌbməˈrin / (AmE), / ɪnˈstɔːl ə skriːn dɔːr ɒn ə ˌsʌbməˈriːn / (BrE)
to attempt something that is obviously pointless or impossible to achieve
futile, pointless task

Examples

He knew arguing with his boss about the new policy would be like installing a screen door on a submarine.
Any effort to repair the ancient plumbing felt like installing a screen door on a submarine.
Trying to teach my stubborn dog tricks is just installing a screen door on a submarine.

Synset Examples

Trying to fix that old car is like installing a screen door on a submarine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herding cats - trying to control or organize people or things that are inherently uncontrollable; a futile task
