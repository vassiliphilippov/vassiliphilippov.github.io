to listen to reason

1. VERB

/ ˈlɪsən tə ˈɹiːzən / (AmE), / ˈlɪsən tə ˈɹiːzən / (BrE)
to be willing to consider sensible advice or arguments; to act rationally instead of stubbornly
be willing to act sensibly

Examples

I'm trying to get him to listen to reason, but he won't change his mind.
She finally agreed to listen to reason and postponed the risky trip.
If you'll just listen to reason, you'll see why this plan won't work.

Synset Examples

Don't be such a fuckhead; just listen to reason.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: see sense - become willing to accept what is sensible or reasonable
  • Antonym: be pigheaded - refuse to listen to reasonable advice; be stubborn
