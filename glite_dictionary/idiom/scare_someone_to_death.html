to scare someone to death

1. VERB

/ skɛr ˈsʌmwʌn tə dɛθ / (AmE), / skɛə ˈsʌmwən tə dɛθ / (BrE)
to frighten someone very severely or extremely
frighten someone extremely

Examples

The sound of footsteps behind her in the empty street scared her to death.
That rollercoaster ride scared me to death!
Don't sneak up on me like that—you nearly scared me to death!

Synset Examples

The horror movie was so intense it nearly scared me to death.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frighten someone to death - cause someone to feel extreme fear
  • Synonym: to terrify - cause someone to feel great fear
