to mark my words

1. VERB

/ mɑrk maɪ wɝdz / (AmE), / mɑːk maɪ wɜːdz / (BrE)
used to tell someone to pay attention because what you are saying is important and will happen or be proven true
pay close attention to what I say

Examples

Mark my words, you'll regret this decision someday.
He said, "mark my words, she will be famous one day."
If you don't study harder, mark my words, you'll fail the test.

Synset Examples

Mark my words, this plan will succeed.
