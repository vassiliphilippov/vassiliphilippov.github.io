of the first water

1. ADJ

/ əv ðə ˈfɝst ˈwɑtɚ / (AmE), / əv ðə ˈfɜːst ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
Of the highest quality or excellence.
of the highest quality

Examples

Her talent for painting is of the first water and has been recognized internationally.
The violinist's performance last night was of the first water and left the audience in awe.
He is a leader of the first water, inspiring everyone around him to do their best.

Synset Examples

Her talent for painting is of the first water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first-rate - of the best quality or class
  • Antonym: second-rate - of poor to mediocre quality

Etymology

Originally referring to the highest grade of diamonds, which were judged by the clarity of water.
