a long way

1. X

/ ə lɔŋ weɪ / (AmE), / ə lɒŋ weɪ / (BrE)
used to indicate that something has a significant impact or effect, often more than expected.
have a significant impact or effect

Examples

Even a small gesture of kindness can a long way toward improving someone's day.
Good communication skills go a long way in building strong relationships.
Saving a little money each month can a long way over time.

Synset Examples

A little of something goes a long way in making people happy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to go a long way - (idiom) to be very effective or helpful; to have a big effect
