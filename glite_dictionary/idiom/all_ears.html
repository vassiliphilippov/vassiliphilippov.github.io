all ears

1. ADJ

/ ɔl ˈɪrz / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈɪəz / (BrE)
fully attentive and ready to listen with great interest
very attentive, eager to listen

Examples

Whenever my supervisor gives feedback, I am all ears so I don't miss anything important.
Tell me what happened—I'm all ears for your story!
The children were all ears as the teacher read the fairy tale aloud.

Synset Examples

When she speaks, I'm always all ears.
I'm all ears for your big news!
I’m all ears, so let’s have it!
He promised to listen; he's all ears now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attentive - paying close attention
  • Antonym: inattentive - not paying attention
