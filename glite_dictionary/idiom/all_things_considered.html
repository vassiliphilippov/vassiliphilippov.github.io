all things considered

1. ADV

/ ɔl θɪŋz kənˈsɪdərd / (AmE), / ɔːl θɪŋz kənˈsɪdəd / (BrE)
taking everything into account; considering all aspects of a situation.
taking everything into account

Examples

All things considered, the conference was a great success for our team.
She did quite well, all things considered.
All things considered, it's probably best that we waited before making a decision.

Synset Examples

All things considered, I think we did well.
All things considered, we made the right choice.
All things considered, it was a successful event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on balance - after considering all the relevant facts
  • Synonym: in the final analysis - after considering everything
