to shat one's pants

1. VERB

/ ʃæt wʌnz pænts / (AmE), / ʃæt wʌnz pænts / (BrE)
to defecate involuntarily in one’s clothing, often due to fear, surprise, or extreme anxiety
involuntarily defecate in clothing
vulgar informal

Examples

He was so scared on the roller coaster that he almost shat his pants.
I laughed so hard at the joke I thought I might shat my pants.
The horror movie was so terrifying that some viewers claimed they shat their pants.

Synset Examples
