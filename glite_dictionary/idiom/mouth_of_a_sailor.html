mouth of a sailor

1. NOUN

/ maʊθ əv ə ˈseɪlər / (AmE), / maʊθ əv ə ˈseɪlə / (BrE)
used to describe someone who frequently uses coarse or vulgar language
uses a lot of coarse or vulgar language

Examples

My grandmother sometimes has a mouth of a sailor when she gets frustrated with technology.
Despite his professional appearance, he has the mouth of a sailor and curses constantly.
Her friends joke that she’s got the mouth of a sailor whenever she watches sports.

Synset Examples

Her father had the mouth of a sailor when upset.
He was known for his mouth of a sailor in the office.
He spoke with the mouth of a sailor at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: potty mouth - a person who uses a lot of vulgar language
