heart in the right place

1. X

/ hɑrt ɪn ðə raɪt pleɪs / (AmE), / hɑːt ɪn ðə raɪt pleɪs / (BrE)
having good or kind intentions, even if not always successful or skillful
having good intentions

Examples

Although she makes mistakes, her heart is in the right place.
The volunteers may lack experience, but their hearts are in the right place.
He sometimes speaks bluntly, but you can tell his heart is in the right place.

Synset Examples

You can trust him; he has his heart in the right place.
She may not be perfect, but her heart is in the right place.
Even if he messes up, his heart is in the right place.
They have their hearts in the right place for charity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good-hearted - kind and generous in nature
  • Synonym: well-intentioned - having good intentions, even if not always resulting in success
