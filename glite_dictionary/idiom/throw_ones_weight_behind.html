to throw one's weight behind

1. VERB

/ θɹoʊ wʌnz weɪt bɪˈhaɪnd / (AmE), / θɹəʊ wʌnz weɪt bɪˈhaɪnd / (BrE)
to use your influence or authority to support someone or something
to support with influence or authority

Examples

The senator decided to throw his weight behind the new healthcare bill.
Many celebrities have thrown their weight behind environmental causes in recent years.
She is planning to throw her weight behind the project to ensure its success.

Synset Examples

He threw his weight behind the initiative to improve local schools.
We've thrown our weight behind the campaign for cleaner energy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: support - to give assistance or approval to something or someone
