to stuff the ballot box

1. VERB

/ stʌf ðə ˈbælət bɑks / (AmE), / stʌf ðə ˈbælət bɒks / (BrE)
to illegally influence the outcome of an election by adding fraudulent votes
fraudulently add votes in an election

Examples

The corrupt officials tried to stuff the ballot box to remain in power.
Authorities implemented strict measures to prevent anyone from stuffing the ballot box.
He was arrested after being caught stuffing the ballot box during the city council election.

Synset Examples

Activists warned against attempts to stuff the ballot box.
It's illegal to stuff the ballot box with fake votes.
They were caught trying to stuff the ballot box during the election.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vote rigging - the act of dishonestly organizing an election to achieve a desired result
  • Synonym: electoral fraud - illegal interference with the election process
