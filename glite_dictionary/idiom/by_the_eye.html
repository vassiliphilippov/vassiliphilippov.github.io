by the eye

1. ADV

/ baɪ ði aɪ / (AmE), / baɪ ði aɪ / (BrE)
by making a visual estimate rather than measuring with instruments or tools.
by visual estimate, without instruments

Examples

The mechanic adjusted the part by the eye and it fit perfectly.
She calculated the diameter of the cake by the eye before slicing it.
They painted the lines on the field by the eye, not with any measurements.

Synset Examples

They cut the wood by the eye without any tools.
She judged the fabric length by the eye.
He measured the distance by the eye, not a ruler.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: by eye - by using visual estimation, without exact measurement
