joined at the hip

1. ADJ

/ dʒɔɪnd ət ðə hɪp / (AmE), / dʒɔɪnd ət ðə hɪp / (BrE)
(of two or more people) extremely close and inseparable, spending a great deal of time together.
inseparable; extremely close

Examples

Ever since they met in college, they've been joined at the hip.
Fans often refer to the famous duo as being joined at the hip due to their close friendship.
The two companies are now so closely aligned, they're almost joined at the hip.

Synset Examples

Those two are joined at the hip since childhood.
They work together so closely, they're practically joined at the hip.
In class, she and her best friend are always joined at the hip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inseparable - impossible to separate; always together
