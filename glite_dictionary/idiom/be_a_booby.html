to be a booby

1. VERB

/ bi ə ˈbuːbi / (AmE), / biː ə ˈbuːbi / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or silly way; to act like a fool
act foolishly

Examples

If you be a booby in front of the class, you might embarrass yourself.
Don't be a booby—listen to the instructions before starting the test.
She realized she had been a booby to trust the scam email.

Synset Examples

Don't be a booby; just ask for help!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be a fool - to act in a stupid or silly way
  • Antonym: be sensible - to act with good judgment
