to turn a number of shades of red

1. VERB

/ tɝn ə ˈnʌmbɚ əv ʃeɪdz əv rɛd / (AmE), / tɜːn ə ˈnʌmbə əv ʃeɪdz əv rɛd / (BrE)
to blush or show signs of embarrassment, often to varying degrees
blush in embarrassment

Examples

Whenever she speaks in public, she tends to turn a number of shades of red.
He turned a number of shades of red when everyone sang 'Happy Birthday' to him at the restaurant.
During the awkward interview question, she couldn't help but turn a number of shades of red.

Synset Examples

Seeing her crush made her turn a number of shades of red.
He turned a number of shades of red after the embarrassing mistake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blush - to become red in the face from embarrassment or shame
