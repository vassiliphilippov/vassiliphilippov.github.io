beyond wildest dreams

1. X

/ biˈɑnd ˈwaɪldɪst driːmz / (AmE), / bɪˈjɒnd ˈwaɪldɪst driːmz / (BrE)
so remarkable or impressive that it exceeds anything one could have imagined or hoped for
more than one could imagine

Examples

When she saw the final product, it was beyond wildest dreams—far better than she had expected.
The view from the mountain summit was beyond wildest dreams, stunning in every direction.
His recovery after the accident was beyond wildest dreams for his doctors and family.

Synset Examples

Winning the award was beyond her wildest dreams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unimaginable - impossible to imagine; inconceivable
  • Antonym: ordinary - not unusual or special; normal
