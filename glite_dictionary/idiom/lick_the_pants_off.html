to lick the pants off

1. VERB

/ lɪk ðə pænts ɔf / (AmE), / lɪk ðə pɑːnts ɒf / (BrE)
to defeat someone thoroughly or to flatter someone excessively
defeat completely or flatter excessively (idiom)
informal

Examples

She could lick the pants off any of her rivals in a debate.
He tried to lick the pants off his boss with nonstop compliments.
Our team managed to lick the pants off the defending champions.

Synset Examples

He licked the pants off his date with compliments.
She can lick the pants off her competition easily.
He could lick the pants off anyone with charm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lick - to beat or surpass someone, especially in a competition
  • Synonym: to flatter - to praise someone excessively
  • Synonym: to trounce - to defeat decisively
