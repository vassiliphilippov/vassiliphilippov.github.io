in heaven's name

1. X

/ ɪn ˈhɛvənz neɪm / (AmE), / ɪn ˈhɛvənz neɪm / (BrE)
used to emphasize a question or command, especially to express surprise, disbelief, frustration, or urgency
used to add emphasis, showing surprise or urgency

Examples

In heaven's name, can you please explain what happened in the meeting?
In heaven's name, why would anyone believe such a story?
Tell me, in heaven's name, where did you put the keys this time?

Synset Examples

In heaven's name, stop making excuses and take action!
In heaven's name, what could have happened here?
Why, in heaven's name, did you choose that option?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on earth - used to emphasize a question, especially to express surprise or confusion (e.g., 'What on earth are you doing?')
  • Synonym: in God's name - used to emphasize a question or command, similar in meaning and usage
