all your base are belong to us

1. X

/ ɔl jʊr beɪs ɑr bɪˈlʌŋ tu ʌs / (AmE), / ɔːl jɔː beɪs ɑː bɪˈlɒŋ tʊ ʌs / (BrE)
A humorous catchphrase popularized as an internet meme to indicate victory or superiority, originally from a poorly translated line in a video game.
internet meme indicating humorous victory
informal slang

Examples

After their team won unexpectedly, they jokingly posted 'all your base are belong to us' on social media.
Whenever someone dominates an online game, someone inevitably comments 'all your base are belong to us'.
The meme 'all your base are belong to us' still circles around internet forums years after its origin.

Synset Examples

The phrase 'all your base are belong to us' is a classic in internet culture.
He jokingly said, 'all your base are belong to us' after winning the match.
In the video game, the phrase 'all your base are belong to us' became a meme.

Etymology

From a mistranslation in the 1992 English release of the Japanese video game Zero Wing.
