down but not out

1. ADJ

/ daʊn bət nɑt aʊt / (AmE), / daʊn bət nɒt aʊt / (BrE)
still having the possibility to recover or succeed despite experiencing difficulties or setbacks; not defeated
not defeated despite setbacks

Examples

Even though the company is struggling financially, it's down but not out and plans to reorganize.
After losing the first two games, the team remained down but not out.
She felt down but not out after being rejected for the promotion.

Synset Examples

She lost the match, but she’s down but not out.
After the setback, he felt down but not out.
The team is down but not out; they can still win.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resilient - able to recover quickly from difficulties
  • Antonym: defeated - having been beaten or overcome in a contest or conflict
