to reach for the stars

1. VERB

/ ritʃ fər ðə stɑrz / (AmE), / riːtʃ fə ðə stɑːz / (BrE)
to aim for very high or ambitious goals
aspire to achieve great goals

Examples

From a young age, her parents taught her to reach for the stars in everything she did.
Despite many setbacks, he continued to reach for the stars and eventually achieved his dreams.
Graduates are often told to reach for the stars as they begin their careers.

Synset Examples

Don't be afraid to reach for the stars in life.
He encourages students to reach for the stars.
If you want to succeed, you must reach for the stars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to aim high - to set ambitious goals for oneself
