mile a minute

1. ADV

/ ˌmaɪl ə ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / ˌmaɪl ə ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
at a very fast rate; extremely quickly, usually used to describe speed of speech or action
very fast; extremely quickly (idiom)

Examples

When he gets excited, he talks mile a minute and it's hard to follow.
The movie's action scenes moved mile a minute, keeping everyone on the edge of their seats.
Sarah's ideas were coming mile a minute during the brainstorming session.

Synset Examples

She always talks a mile a minute during meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breakneck - dangerously or extremely fast (adjective)
  • Antonym: slowly - at a slow speed; not quickly (adverb)
