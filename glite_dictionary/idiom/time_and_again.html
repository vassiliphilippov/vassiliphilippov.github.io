time and again

1. ADV

/ taɪm ənd əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / taɪm ənd əˈɡɛn / (BrE)
very often; repeatedly
repeatedly; very often

Examples

Time and again, she warned him about the risks, but he wouldn't listen.
The team has proven time and again that they can handle pressure in critical matches.
Problems like these arise time and again in large organizations.

Synset Examples

the unknown word turned up over and over again in the text

Related Terms

  • Synonym: over and over - repeatedly, again and again
  • Synonym: repeatedly - happening or done many times
  • Antonym: once - on one occasion only
