all thumbs

1. ADJ

/ ɔl θʌmz / (AmE), / ɔːl θʌmz / (BrE)
very awkward or clumsy, especially in handling things with one's hands
clumsy or awkward with one's hands

Examples

Whenever I try to wrap gifts, I'm all thumbs and make a mess of the tape.
He admitted he was all thumbs when it came to using power tools.
Despite being all thumbs in the kitchen, she volunteered to help bake cookies.

Synset Examples

He felt all thumbs while assembling the furniture.
She’s all thumbs in the kitchen, but tries anyway.
I'm all thumbs when it comes to fixing things.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or handling things
