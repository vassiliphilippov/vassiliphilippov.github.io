land of fruits and nuts

1. NOUN

/ lænd əv fruts ənd nʌts / (AmE), / lænd əv fruːts ənd nʌts / (BrE)
A humorous or informal nickname for California, referring to its agricultural richness and often its reputation for having quirky inhabitants.
nickname for California
informal american

Examples

Many people move to the land of fruits and nuts in search of a new lifestyle.
The writer described California as the land of fruits and nuts in his memoir.
Tourists are always amazed by the variety of produce in the land of fruits and nuts.

Synset Examples

California is often called the land of fruits and nuts.
