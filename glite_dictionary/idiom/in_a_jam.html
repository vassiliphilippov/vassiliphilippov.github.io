in a jam

1. X

/ ɪn ə dʒæm / (AmE), / ɪn ə dʒam / (BrE)
in a difficult or challenging situation; experiencing trouble or a problem that is hard to resolve
in a difficult situation
informal

Examples

I forgot my keys and now I'm in a jam outside my apartment.
If you ever find yourself in a jam, don't hesitate to ask for help.
She was in a jam when her car broke down on the way to work.

Synset Examples

She found herself in a jam during the meeting.
When I lose my wallet, I’m in a jam.
I'm really in a jam with my car broken down.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in trouble - experiencing difficulties or problems
  • Synonym: in a pickle - in a difficult or awkward situation
  • Antonym: in the clear - free from trouble or suspicion
