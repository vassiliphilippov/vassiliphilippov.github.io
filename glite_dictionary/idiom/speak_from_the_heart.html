to speak from the heart

1. VERB

/ spiːk frəm ðə hɑrt / (AmE), / spiːk frəm ðə hɑːt / (BrE)
to express oneself in a sincere, honest, or deeply emotional way
speak sincerely or emotionally

Examples

During the memorial service, the mayor spoke from the heart about the community's resilience.
You could tell she was speaking from the heart during the interview.
He always tries to speak from the heart, no matter how difficult the topic is.

Synset Examples

She spoke from the heart during her speech.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speak frankly - to speak openly and honestly
