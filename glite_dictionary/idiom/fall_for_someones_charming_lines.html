to fall for someone's charming lines

1. VERB

/ fɔl fɔr ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈtʃɑrmɪŋ laɪnz / (AmE), / fɔːl fə ˈsʌmwənz ˈtʃɑːmɪŋ laɪnz / (BrE)
to be tricked or deceived by someone's flattering or persuasive words
be deceived by flattery

Examples

Despite her experience, she almost fell for his charming lines at the party.
It surprised everyone when the usually cautious manager fell for his charming lines.
Don't fall for his charming lines; he's used the same trick before.

Synset Examples

She didn't fall for his charming lines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fall for - to be deceived by or to be attracted to someone or something
