against time

1. ADV

/ əˈɡɛnst taɪm / (AmE), / əˈɡɛnst tʌɪm / (BrE)
in a race to finish something as quickly as possible before a deadline or time limit
in a race to meet a deadline

Examples

The engineers worked against time to restore power before the storm hit.
She finished the project against time, submitting it just before the deadline.
During the surgery, the doctors raced against time to save the patient's life.

Synset Examples

it was a race against the clock

Related Terms

  • Synonym: against the clock - in a race to finish before time runs out
