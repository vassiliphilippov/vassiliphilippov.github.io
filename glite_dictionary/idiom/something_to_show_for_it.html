something to show for (it)

1. X

/ ˈsʌmθɪŋ tə ˈʃoʊ fər ɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌmθɪŋ tə ˈʃəʊ fə ɪt / (BrE)
a visible or tangible result or outcome achieved after effort or work
a result achieved after effort

Examples

Years of hard work paid off, and now she finally has something to show for it.
Without proper planning, you might end up with nothing to show for it.
He put a lot of time into the project, but at least he has something to show for it now.

Synset Examples

After all that effort, I finally have something to show for it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: result - an outcome produced by something
