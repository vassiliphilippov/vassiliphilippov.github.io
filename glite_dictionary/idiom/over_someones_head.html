over someone's head

1. ADV

/ ˈoʊvɚ ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / ˈəʊvə ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (BrE)
(of a subject or idea) too difficult for someone to understand or grasp.
too difficult to understand

Examples

The professor's explanation went over my head, and I had to ask for clarification.
Most of the jokes in the film were over the children's heads.
Quantum mechanics can easily go over anyone's head without a strong background in physics.

Synset Examples

The proposal went over her head; she needed more explanation.
The math problems were over her head in class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beyond one's grasp - impossible for someone to understand
