sham Abraham

1. NOUN

/ ʃæm ˈeɪbrəˌhæm / (AmE), / ʃam ˈeɪbrəham / (BrE)
A deceptive or insincere tactic, especially one where someone pretends to be honest while being deceitful.
insincere or deceptive tactic
archaic
