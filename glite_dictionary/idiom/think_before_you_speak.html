to think before you speak

1. VERB

/ θɪŋk bɪˈfɔr ju spiːk / (AmE), / θɪŋk bɪˈfɔː juː spiːk / (BrE)
to consider the possible effects of your words before saying something
consider consequences before speaking

Examples

You should always think before you speak to avoid offending others.
She reminded her son to think before you speak in difficult situations.
It's wise to think before you speak, especially at work meetings.

Synset Examples

Don't react Andy; think before you speak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hold your tongue - to remain silent instead of speaking
  • Antonym: to blurt out - to say something suddenly and without thinking
