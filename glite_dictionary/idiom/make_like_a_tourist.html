to make like a tourist

1. VERB

/ meɪk laɪk ə ˈtʊrɪst / (AmE), / meɪk laɪk ə ˈtʊərɪst / (BrE)
to behave as a tourist; to explore a place in a casual or curious manner, as if visiting for leisure
behave as a tourist, explore casually

Examples

On his day off, he decided to make like a tourist and visit all the local museums.
Whenever she moves to a new city, she likes to make like a tourist for the first week.
We rented bikes and spent the afternoon making like tourists through the historic district.

Synset Examples

He decided to make like a tourist and explore the city.
