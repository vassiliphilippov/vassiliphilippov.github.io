good enough to eat

1. ADJ

/ ɡʊd ɪˈnʌf tu it / (AmE), / ɡʊd ɪˈnʌf tʊ iːt / (BrE)
so attractive or appealing in appearance that it seems as if it could be eaten
very attractive or appealing in appearance

Examples

The dress was so beautiful it looked good enough to eat.
Those pastries are so well decorated, they're good enough to eat.
His artwork was so vibrant and colorful that it appeared good enough to eat.

Synset Examples

The cake looks good enough to eat!
Her decorations were beautiful, just good enough to eat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mouthwatering - extremely appetizing or appealing, especially in appearance or smell
  • Antonym: unappetizing - not appealing or inviting to eat or look at
