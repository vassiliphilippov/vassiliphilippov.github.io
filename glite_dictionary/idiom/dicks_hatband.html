Dick's hatband

1. NOUN

/ dɪks ˈhætˌbænd / (AmE), / dɪks ˈhætˌbænd / (BrE)
used to describe someone or something that is unusually showy, flashy, or eccentric in style or appearance
ostentatiously flashy or eccentric style
informal

Examples

He wore a neon suit that looked like Dick's hatband at the wedding.
The new sports car, with its gold rims, is as flashy as Dick's hatband.
She decorated the room with so many colors, it was starting to resemble Dick's hatband.

Synset Examples

He wore a vibrant tie, looking like Dick's hatband at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flashy - having a bright or showy appearance
  • Antonym: understated - not showy or ostentatious in style

Etymology

Uncertain origin; possibly British or American folk idiom, first recorded in 19th-century English.
