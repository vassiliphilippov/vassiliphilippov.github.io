in record time

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈrɛkərd taɪm / (AmE), / ɪn ˈrɛkɔːd taɪm / (BrE)
faster than has ever been done before; extremely quickly or more quickly than usual
faster than ever before; extremely quickly

Examples

The chef prepared dinner in record time for his hungry guests.
Our team completed the project in record time and impressed the client.
She solved the puzzle in record time, surprising everyone in the room.

Synset Examples

  • Antonym: slowly - in a slow manner; not quickly
