to put in mind of

1. VERB

/ pʊt ɪn maɪnd ʌv / (AmE), / pʊt ɪn maɪnd ɒv / (BrE)
to cause someone to remember or think of someone or something; to remind someone of something
remind someone of something

Examples

That old photograph always puts in mind of my childhood holidays by the sea.
This melody puts in mind of my grandmother's house every time I hear it.
The way she spoke put in mind of my favorite teacher from school.

Synset Examples

That song puts me in mind of our road trip.
That song puts me in mind of my childhood.
Her smile puts me in mind of summer days.
This painting puts me in mind of my childhood.
Her painting puts me in mind of Van Gogh.
The smell of cookies puts me in mind of home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to remind - to cause someone to remember someone or something
