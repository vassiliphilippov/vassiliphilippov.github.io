to treat like royalty

1. VERB

/ trit laɪk ˈrɔɪəlti / (AmE), / triːt laɪk ˈrɔɪəlti / (BrE)
to treat someone with exceptional care, respect, and attention, as if they were a king or queen.
treat with exceptional care and respect

Examples

Her parents always treat her like royalty on her birthday, showering her with gifts and special meals.
The hotel staff treated us like royalty during our stay, anticipating every need we had.
Whenever his grandchildren visit, he treats them like royalty and makes their favorite desserts.

Synset Examples

We treat our grandcat like royalty during visits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pamper - to give someone a lot of attention and care; to spoil
  • Antonym: neglect - to fail to care for someone properly
