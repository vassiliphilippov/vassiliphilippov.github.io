gift that keeps on giving

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪft ðæt kips ɑn ˈɡɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡɪft ðæt kiːps ɒn ˈɡɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
something that continues to provide benefits or enjoyment over a long period of time.
something with lasting benefits

Examples

Donating to clean water projects is truly a gift that keeps on giving.
Her wisdom is a gift that keeps on giving in my daily life.
Good health is a gift that keeps on giving for years to come.

Synset Examples

This charity is a gift that keeps on giving.
Her support was a gift that keeps on giving.
Investing in education is a gift that keeps on giving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: windfall - an unexpected gain or piece of good fortune
