to fall from the ugly stick

1. VERB

/ fɔl frəm ði ˈʌɡli stɪk / (AmE), / fɔːl frəm ði ˈʌɡli stɪk / (BrE)
(slang) to become or appear suddenly unattractive; to look notably less attractive than before.
suddenly look unattractive (slang)
slang

Examples

After his disastrous haircut, everyone agreed he must have fallen from the ugly stick.
She used to be quite fashionable, but lately it seems like she fell from the ugly stick.
The internet trolls told him he had fallen from the ugly stick, which was just mean.

Synset Examples

I think she fell from the ugly stick yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be hit with the ugly stick - (slang) to be or look unattractive

Etymology

From humorous slang, referencing the idea of someone being hit by an 'ugly stick' as an exaggerated cause of ugliness.
