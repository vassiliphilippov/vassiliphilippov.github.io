to piss in someone's cornflakes

1. VERB

/ pɪs ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈkɔrnˌfleɪks / (AmE), / pɪs ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈkɔːnˌfleɪks / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) To spoil someone's enjoyment, enthusiasm, or mood; to ruin something for someone.
spoil someone's enjoyment or mood (idiom, vulgar)
informal vulgar

Examples

Nobody likes it when you piss in their cornflakes by criticizing every idea.
He apologized for pissing in her cornflakes after realizing how negative he had been.
Just because you're having a bad day doesn't mean you should piss in my cornflakes.

Synset Examples
