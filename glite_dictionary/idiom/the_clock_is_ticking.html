the clock is ticking

1. X

/ ðə klɑk ɪz ˈtɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ðə klɒk ɪz ˈtɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
used to indicate that time is running out and there is a limited period to complete something
time is running out

Examples

We have only a few hours left until the deadline—the clock is ticking.
Remember, the clock is ticking on your chance to submit your application.
He realized the clock is ticking as he hurried to complete the assignment.

Synset Examples

With the deadline approaching, the clock is ticking for our team.
Don't forget, the clock is ticking for the competition!
The clock is ticking; we need to finish this project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: time is running out - there is not much time left to do something
