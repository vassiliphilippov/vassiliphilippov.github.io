to put our pants on one leg at a time

1. VERB

/ pʊt aʊɚ ˈpænts ɑn wʌn lɛɡ æt ə taɪm / (AmE), / pʊt ˈaʊə ˈpɑːnts ɒn wʌn lɛɡ æt ə taɪm / (BrE)
(informal) To perform actions in the same ordinary, straightforward way as everyone else; to emphasize that nobody is extraordinary or above others.
do things in a normal, ordinary way
informal

Examples

Despite his success, he reminds everyone that he puts his pants on one leg at a time like the rest of us.
She told her fans that even celebrities put their pants on one leg at a time.
Let's not be intimidated; they put their pants on one leg at a time just like we do.

Synset Examples

Everyone struggles; we all put our pants on one leg at a time.
Remember, we all put our pants on one leg at a time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: down-to-earth - practical and unpretentious
  • Synonym: humble - having or showing a modest view of one's importance
