my ears are burning

1. X

/ maɪ ɪrz ɑr ˈbɜrnɪŋ / (AmE), / maɪ ɪəz ə ˈbɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
used to say that you have a feeling that people are talking about you, especially in your absence
feeling that someone is talking about you

Examples

Whenever people whisper and laugh, I can't help but think my ears are burning.
You must have been discussing me at lunch; my ears are burning!
She walked into the room and said, 'My ears are burning; were you talking about me?'

Synset Examples

I must be popular; my ears are burning!
