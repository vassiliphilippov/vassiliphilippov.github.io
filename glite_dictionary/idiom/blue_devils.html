blue devils

1. NOUN

/ bluː ˈdɛvəlz / (AmE), / bluː ˈdɛvəlz / (BrE)
a feeling of sadness or depression; low spirits
a state of sadness or depression

Examples

After losing his job, Tom was plagued by the blue devils for weeks.
Listening to old songs sometimes brings on the blue devils during rainy days.
Whenever she feels the blue devils coming on, she tries to go for a long walk.

Synset Examples

he had a bad case of the blues

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blues - a feeling of sadness or melancholy
  • Synonym: depression - feeling of severe despondency and dejection

Etymology

19th-century American English, likely from the imagined vision of blue-colored devils as a metaphor for depressive feelings.
