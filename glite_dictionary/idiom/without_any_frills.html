without any frills

1. ADJ

/ wɪˈðaʊt ˈɛni frɪlz / (AmE), / wɪˈðaʊt ˈɛni frɪlz / (BrE)
Simple and straightforward; having no unnecessary extras or decorations.
lacking unnecessary extras; simple

Examples

Their new office is without any frills, but it’s efficient and comfortable.
The menu is without any frills, offering just a few basic dishes done well.
She prefers a lifestyle that is without any frills or unnecessary complexity.

Synset Examples

He likes to keep his presentations without any frills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plain - simple or unadorned
  • Antonym: elaborate - detailed or decorated, opposite of simple
