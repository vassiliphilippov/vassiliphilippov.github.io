holy shamoley

1. INTJ

/ ˈhoʊli ʃəˈmoʊli / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli ʃəˈməʊli / (BrE)
An exclamation used to express surprise, amazement, or disbelief.
expression of surprise or amazement
informal

Examples

Holy shamoley, that was the best concert I've ever been to!
When I saw the price of the house, all I could say was holy shamoley.
Holy shamoley, it's snowing in June!
