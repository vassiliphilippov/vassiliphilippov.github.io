inch by inch

1. ADV

/ ɪntʃ baɪ ɪntʃ / (AmE), / ɪntʃ baɪ ɪntʃ / (BrE)
slowly and gradually; by small degrees or steps at a time
gradually, little by little

Examples

The team advanced inch by inch despite the thick fog.
She improved her skills inch by inch with daily practice and patience.
The old ship creaked forward inch by inch through the icy waters.

Synset Examples

The workers cleared the debris inch by inch.
He studied for his exams inch by inch, not rushing.
They moved inch by inch through the dense forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: little by little - gradually, bit by bit
  • Synonym: bit by bit - gradually, a small amount at a time
