sound asleep

1. ADJ

/ saʊnd əˈslip / (AmE), / saʊnd əˈsliːp / (BrE)
sleeping very deeply and peacefully; not easily awakened
sleeping deeply and peacefully

Examples

After a long day at work, he was sound asleep within minutes.
The children were sound asleep by the time their parents got home.
She didn't hear the thunderstorm because she was sound asleep all night.

Synset Examples

She was sound asleep when the phone rang.
The baby was sound asleep in her crib.
