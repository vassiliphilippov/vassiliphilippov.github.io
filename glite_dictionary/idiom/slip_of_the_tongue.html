slip of the tongue

1. NOUN

/ slɪp əv ðə tʌŋ / (AmE), / slɪp əv ðə tʌŋ / (BrE)
an accidental and minor mistake made while speaking
minor accidental speaking mistake

Examples

It was just a slip of the tongue when he called her by the wrong name.
During the speech, she made a slip of the tongue that made everyone laugh.
He apologized for the slip of the tongue, saying he didn't mean to offend anyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lapsus linguae - Latin term for a slip of the tongue
  • Degree Higher: blunder - a careless mistake
  • Degree Higher: error - a mistake or inaccuracy

2. NOUN

/ slɪp əv ðə tʌŋ / (AmE), / slɪp əv ðə tʌŋ / (BrE)
an unintentional mistake made while speaking, such as saying the wrong word or phrase
unintentional error in speech

Examples

He apologized for his slip of the tongue during the meeting.
Sometimes a slip of the tongue can lead to embarrassing moments.
Her slip of the tongue made everyone in the room laugh.

Synset Examples

Her slip of the tongue revealed her true feelings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lapsus linguae - a formal term for a slip of the tongue
