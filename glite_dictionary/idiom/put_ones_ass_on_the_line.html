to put one's ass on the line

1. VERB

/ pʊt wʌnz æs ɑn ðə laɪn / (AmE), / pʊt wʌnz æs ɒn ðə laɪn / (BrE)
to take a significant risk or expose oneself to danger or potential loss, often for a cause, project, or another person
take a serious personal risk
informal vulgar

Examples

When the company was in trouble, she was ready to put her ass on the line to help save it.
If you want real change, sometimes you have to put your ass on the line and speak up.
He put his ass on the line by standing up to the boss for the team.

Synset Examples

She’s always willing to put her ass on the line.
He put his ass on the line for the project.
You need to put your ass on the line to succeed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: risk one's neck - to put oneself in danger or take a big risk
  • Synonym: to stick one's neck out - to take a personal risk by supporting someone or something
