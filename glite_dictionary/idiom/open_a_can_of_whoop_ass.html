to open a can of whoop ass

1. VERB

/ ˈoʊpən ə kæn əv ˈwʊp ˌæs / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən ə kæn ɒv ˈwʊp ˌæs / (BrE)
to respond to someone or something with sudden, vigorous, and often aggressive punishment or criticism
respond aggressively or with force
informal american

Examples

If you keep teasing him, he's going to open a can of whoop ass on you.
The principal threatened to open a can of whoop ass if anyone cheated again.
She really opened a can of whoop ass during the debate and silenced her critics.

Synset Examples

If you keep bothering me, I'll open a can of whoop ass!
He warned that the coach would open a can of whoop ass after the loss.
Don't mess with her; she knows how to open a can of whoop ass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to retaliate - to make an attack in return for a similar attack
  • Synonym: to punish - to inflict a penalty or sanction on someone
  • Antonym: let someone off easy - to treat someone leniently or without strong punishment

Etymology

Popularized in American slang in the late 20th century; 'whoop ass' plays on the idea of unleashing physical punishment.
