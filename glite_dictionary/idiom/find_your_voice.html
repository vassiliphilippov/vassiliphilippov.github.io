to find your voice

1. VERB

/ faɪnd jʊr vɔɪs / (AmE), / faɪnd jɔː vɔɪs / (BrE)
to discover and express your unique style, perspective, or identity, especially in creative or communicative activities
discover your unique style or perspective

Examples

Many artists struggle for years before they finally find their voice and feel confident in their work.
After months of hesitation, she began to find her voice and speak up in meetings.
Creative writing classes helped him find his voice as an author.

Synset Examples

It's essential to find your voice in creative writing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: develop your voice - to cultivate or refine your distinctive style or way of expressing yourself
