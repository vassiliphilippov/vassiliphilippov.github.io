to strain every nerve

1. VERB

/ streɪn ˈɛvri nɝv / (AmE), / streɪn ˈɛvri nɜːv / (BrE)
to make the greatest possible effort to achieve something
make the greatest effort

Examples

The whole team had to strain every nerve to win the final match.
She strained every nerve to make sure the event was a success.
We must strain every nerve to meet the company's ambitious targets this year.

Synset Examples

She had to strain every nerve to finish the marathon.
They strained every nerve to meet the project deadline.
He strained every nerve to keep his composure during the interview.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exert oneself - to make a strenuous physical or mental effort
  • Synonym: to go all out - to make one's utmost effort
  • Antonym: to relax - to make less effort or exertion
