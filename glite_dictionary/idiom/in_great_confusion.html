in great confusion

1. ADV

/ ɪn ɡreɪt kənˈfjuʒən / (AmE), / ɪn ɡreɪt kənˈfjuːʒən / (BrE)
in a state of frantic or disorderly haste; with much confusion or disarray
in disorderly haste; frantically

Examples

When the fire alarm went off, people rushed out in great confusion.
The students left the classroom in great confusion after the unexpected announcement.
Papers and books were scattered everywhere in great confusion following the earthquake.

Synset Examples

we ran head over heels toward the shelter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in confusion - in a confused state; not clearly or orderly
  • Synonym: in great haste - very quickly and often carelessly
