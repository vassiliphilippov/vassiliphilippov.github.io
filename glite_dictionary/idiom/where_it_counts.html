where it counts

1. ADV

/ wɛr ɪt kaʊnts / (AmE), / weə ɪt kaʊnts / (BrE)
in the most important or significant areas; in aspects that truly matter
in crucial or significant areas

Examples

She may seem quiet, but she has confidence where it counts.
Focus your energy where it counts for the best results.
The team is strong where it counts, especially in defense.

Synset Examples

Invest your time in projects that matter where it counts.
He knows how to save money where it counts.
