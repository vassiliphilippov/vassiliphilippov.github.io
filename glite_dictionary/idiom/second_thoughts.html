second thoughts

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd ˌθɔts / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ˌθɔːts / (BrE)
doubts or a change of mind about a decision that has already been made.
doubts or reconsideration about a decision

Examples

After reading the contract, he began to have second thoughts about signing it.
She almost accepted the job offer but suddenly got second thoughts.
If you are having second thoughts, it's not too late to change your mind.

Synset Examples

After seeing the price, I had second thoughts.
She started to have second thoughts before the big move.
He had second thoughts about quitting his job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reservation - doubt or hesitation about something planned
  • Antonym: certainty - the state of being completely confident or having no doubt
