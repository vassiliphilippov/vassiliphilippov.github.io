roaring for a priest

1. X

/ ˈrɔrɪŋ fər ə prist / (AmE), / ˈrɔːrɪŋ fə ə priːst / (BrE)
desperately needing or calling for spiritual assistance, especially in a crisis or at the point of death
desperately needing spiritual help
archaic

Examples

As the ship took on water, many passengers were roaring for a priest to give them comfort.
The wounded soldier lay on the battlefield, roaring for a priest before he lost consciousness.
She was so distraught after the accident that she was nearly roaring for a priest.

Synset Examples

She felt like she might die roaring for a priest during the crisis.
