as far as I'm concerned

1. X

/ əz fɑr æz aɪm kənˈsɜrnd / (AmE), / əz fɑːr æz aɪm kənˈsɜːnd / (BrE)
used to indicate that the statement reflects one's own opinion or viewpoint
in my opinion; as I see it

Examples

As far as I'm concerned, we can leave whenever you're ready.
As far as I'm concerned, that movie is the best one released this year.
You can choose whichever option you like; as far as I'm concerned, they're all good.

Synset Examples

As far as I'm concerned, if you recommend it, you're the doctor.
As far as I’m concerned, it’s all good.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in my opinion - expressing a personal view
  • Synonym: as far as I know - according to what I know or am aware of
