to open doors

1. VERB

/ ˈoʊpən dɔrz / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən dɔːz / (BrE)
to provide or create new opportunities, especially to enable progress or success
create opportunities for progress or success

Examples

Her volunteer work helped open doors in the field of international relations.
Learning to code can open doors to many exciting careers.
Moving to a new city opened doors for him both personally and professionally.

Synset Examples

Being a polyglot has opened many doors for him.
His A-level results opened doors to top universities.
Being a Cantab opened many doors in academia.
Networking at the sercon opened many doors for her career.
Learning a new language opens many doors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: create opportunities - to make opportunities available
  • Antonym: close doors - to prevent opportunities or make progress difficult
