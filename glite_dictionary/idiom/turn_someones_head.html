to turn someone's head

1. VERB

/ tɝn ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / tɜːn ˈsʌmwənz hɛd / (BrE)
to attract attention or admiration, often in a way that makes people look at you
attract attention or admiration

Examples

The new sports car really turned heads as it drove down the street.
Her stylish dress turned everyone's head at the party last night.
You know you're succeeding when your work turns heads in the industry.

Synset Examples

He wore a bright red jacket that turned everyone's head.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attract attention - to cause people to notice something or someone
  • Antonym: go unnoticed - not attract any attention at all
