horizontal dancing

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɔːrɪˈzɑːntl ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌhɒrɪˈzɒntl ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
A humorous or euphemistic expression for sexual intercourse, sometimes used to refer to dances performed close to the ground.
humorous euphemism for sex; ground-level dancing
informal

Examples

Some comedians use horizontal dancing as a lighthearted euphemism for intimate relationships.
After a few drinks, the conversation turned to jokes about horizontal dancing.
The film contains several scenes alluding to horizontal dancing without being explicit.
