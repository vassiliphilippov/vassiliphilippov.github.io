to make things tick

1. VERB

/ meɪk θɪŋz tɪk / (AmE), / meɪk θɪŋz tɪk / (BrE)
to ensure that a process, organization, or system operates efficiently and effectively
ensure smooth functioning

Examples

Her strong leadership helps make things tick in the office.
It's the dedicated staff who make things tick behind the scenes.
Good communication is what really makes things tick in any successful team.

Synset Examples

The project manager can make things tick efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: run smoothly - function without problems or interruptions
  • Antonym: to break down - cease to function or operate properly
