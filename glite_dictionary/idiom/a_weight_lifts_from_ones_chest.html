a weight lifts from one's chest

1. X

/ ə ˈweɪt lɪfts frəm wʌnz tʃɛst / (AmE), / ə ˈweɪt lɪfts frəm wʌnz tʃɛst / (BrE)
used to describe the feeling of relief after sharing a burden or confessing something troubling
feeling relief after sharing a burden

Examples

After telling her friend the truth, she felt a weight lift from her chest.
When the doctor said everything was fine, a weight lifted from his chest.
Writing the letter made a weight lift from my chest and I finally relaxed.

Synset Examples

She felt a weight lift from her chest after confessing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: a load off one's mind - feeling relief after worrying less
