to talking trash

1. VERB

/ ˈtɔkɪŋ træʃ / (AmE), / ˈtɔːkɪŋ træʃ / (BrE)
making boastful, insulting, or disparaging remarks, often to provoke or intimidate someone, especially in sports or competition
boasting or insulting remarks (esp. in sports)
informal

Examples

The basketball players kept talking trash to each other during the heated game.
She enjoys talking trash before a big match to shake her opponent’s confidence.
He got in trouble for talking trash about his coworkers online.

Synset Examples

That guy is such a pissbreath for talking trash all the time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trash talk - to insult or boast in a competitive context
