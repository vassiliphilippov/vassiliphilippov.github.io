to powder one's nose

1. VERB

/ ˈpaʊdɚ wʌnz ˈnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə wʌnz ˈnəʊz / (BrE)
(of a woman) to go to the restroom, especially to attend to one's makeup or personal grooming, often used as a discreet or euphemistic expression.
to discreetly visit the restroom (often for grooming)

Examples

She excused herself to powder her nose before the meal arrived.
At the party, she slipped away quietly to powder her nose.
Before the interview, he joked that he needed to powder his nose.

Synset Examples

She excused herself to powder her nose.
She took a moment to powder her nose at the event.
He went to powder his nose before the meeting.

Etymology

Refers to the custom of using face powder as part of a woman's makeup routine.
