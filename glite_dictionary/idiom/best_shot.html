best shot

1. NOUN

/ bɛst ʃɑt / (AmE), / bɛst ʃɒt / (BrE)
an attempt made with the greatest effort possible; the best effort you can give
attempt made with your greatest effort

Examples

She knew it was a tough challenge, but she decided to give it her best shot.
If you want to make the team, you have to give every try your best shot.
Even though he doubted he'd win, he still gave the competition his best shot.

Synset Examples

Just give it your best shot and see what happens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to give it a try - to attempt to do something
