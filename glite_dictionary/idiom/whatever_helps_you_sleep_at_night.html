whatever helps you sleep at night

1. X

/ wəˈtɛvɚ hɛlps ju slip æt naɪt / (AmE), / wəˈtɛvə hɛlps ju sliːp ət naɪt / (BrE)
used to indicate that someone can justify their actions however they want, even if others do not agree or believe them, often in a dismissive or skeptical way
dismissive phrase about self-justification
informal

Examples

You can believe your excuses, but whatever helps you sleep at night.
If lying makes you feel better, then whatever helps you sleep at night.
He claims it was for the best, but whatever helps you sleep at night.

Synset Examples

He justified his actions as whatever helps you sleep at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whatever floats your boat - used to say that someone can do what they like, even if you do not agree
  • Synonym: suit yourself - used to tell someone they can do what they want, even if you do not approve
