man of few words

1. NOUN

/ mæn əv fju wɝdz / (AmE), / mæn əv fjuː wɜːdz / (BrE)
a man who speaks very little, often considered thoughtful or wise
man who says little

Examples

Although Mr. Lee was a man of few words, everyone respected his opinions.
She valued her friend's honesty, even though he was a man of few words.
As a man of few words, Tom preferred to let his actions speak for him.

Synset Examples

As a man of few words, he prefers listening.
Being a man of few words, he lets actions speak.
He's a man of few words but very wise.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: man of many words - a man who talks a lot
