to squash the beef

1. VERB

/ skwɑʃ ðə bif / (AmE), / skwɒʃ ðə biːf / (BrE)
to resolve a disagreement or conflict; to put an end to a quarrel
resolve a disagreement
informal

Examples

It's time to squash the beef and start working together again.
After years of silence, the two musicians met to squash the beef.
She called her sister to finally squash the beef and rebuild their relationship.

Synset Examples

Let's squash the beef and move on from this argument.
They finally decided to squash the beef over lunch.
He reached out to squash the beef with his old friend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to make peace - to resume friendly relations after a conflict
  • Antonym: to hold a grudge - to maintain resentment or anger against someone
