dressed to the nines

1. ADJ

/ drɛst tə ðə naɪnz / (AmE), / drɛst tə ðə naɪnz / (BrE)
Wearing very elegant, fashionable, or formal clothing.
wearing very elegant or formal clothes

Examples

Everyone at the gala was dressed to the nines, making it a glamorous affair.
He arrived at the award ceremony dressed to the nines in a sharp tuxedo.
Whenever she goes out for a special occasion, she's always dressed to the nines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressed up - wearing formal or special clothes

Etymology

Possibly from the phrase 'to the eyes' or 'to the nines,' meaning to perfection; exact origin uncertain.
