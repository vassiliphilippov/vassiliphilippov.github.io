for shits and giggles

1. ADV

/ fər ʃɪts ənd ˈɡɪɡəlz / (AmE), / fə ʃɪts ənd ˈɡɪɡlz / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is done purely for fun or amusement, without any serious purpose
just for fun; for amusement
slang vulgar informal

Examples

He bought a brightly colored suit for shits and giggles, just to make his friends laugh.
Sometimes, she likes to try out new recipes for shits and giggles.
We went to the amusement park for shits and giggles on a rainy afternoon.

Synset Examples

I joined the contest for shits and giggles.
They played the game for shits and giggles.
We decided to prank him for shits and giggles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for fun - for enjoyment or amusement
  • Synonym: for the hell of it - for no particular reason; just for fun
