and so on

1. X

/ ənd soʊ ɑn / (AmE), / ənd səʊ ɒn / (BrE)
used to refer to other things of the same kind that have been mentioned or are understood without being named.
and other similar things

Examples

She listed all her favorite sports: tennis, swimming, and so on.
When packing for the trip, remember to bring clothes, toiletries, and so on.
The class covers biology, chemistry, physics, and so on.

Synset Examples

She plays piano, guitar, and so on.
The project includes research, development, and so on.
We need to buy groceries, toiletries, and so on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: et cetera - and other similar things; often abbreviated as etc.
  • Synonym: and the like - and other similar examples or kinds of things
