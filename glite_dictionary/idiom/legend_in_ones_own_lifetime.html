legend in one's own lifetime

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛdʒənd ɪn wʌnz oʊn ˈlaɪftaɪm / (AmE), / ˈlɛdʒənd ɪn wʌnz əʊn ˈlaɪftaɪm / (BrE)
a person who is greatly admired and famous while still alive; someone whose achievements are widely celebrated during their lifetime
someone widely celebrated while still alive

Examples

Nelson Mandela became a legend in his own lifetime for his fight against apartheid.
The musician was regarded as a legend in his own lifetime after decades of pioneering work.
Thanks to her groundbreaking research, she was considered a legend in her own lifetime.

Synset Examples

Her contributions to science made her a legend in her own lifetime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: living legend - a person who is famous and admired while still alive
