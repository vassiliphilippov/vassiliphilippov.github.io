easy time of it

1. NOUN

/ ˈizi taɪm əv ɪt / (AmE), / ˈiːzi taɪm əv ɪt / (BrE)
a situation or experience that is not difficult and involves little trouble or effort
situation without difficulty; little trouble

Examples

If you prepare ahead, you'll have an easy time of it during the interview.
Despite the challenging task, they ended up having an easy time of it.
He thought the hike would be tough, but in fact he had an easy time of it.

Synset Examples

If you study, you will have an easy time of it on the exam.
She always seems to have an easy time of it at work.
With the new software, we’ll have an easy time of it.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hard time - a situation that is difficult or troublesome
