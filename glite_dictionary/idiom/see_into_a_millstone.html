to see into a millstone

1. VERB

/ si ɪn.tu ə ˈmɪl.stoʊn / (AmE), / siː ˈɪn.tuː ə ˈmɪl.stəʊn / (BrE)
to attempt to understand or solve something that is extremely confusing or impossible to figure out
tackle the impossible or confusing

Examples

For most outsiders, company politics are like trying to see into a millstone.
Understanding the new tax code is as difficult as trying to see into a millstone.
Even experts admitted it was impossible to see into a millstone when predicting the market.

Synset Examples

Her explanations were so convoluted, it felt like trying to see into a millstone.
Deciphering his motives was akin to seeing into a millstone.
Trying to understand his reasoning was like seeing into a millstone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puzzle - something difficult to understand or solve
  • Synonym: enigma - a person or thing that is mysterious or difficult to understand
