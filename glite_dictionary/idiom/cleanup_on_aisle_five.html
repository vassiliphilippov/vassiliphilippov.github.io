cleanup on aisle five

1. X

/ ˈklinˌʌp ɑn ˈaɪl faɪv / (AmE), / ˈkliːnˌʌp ɒn ˈaɪl faɪv / (BrE)
an announcement, typically in a store, indicating that a mess in a particular location needs immediate attention or cleaning
public announcement about a mess needing cleanup

Examples

After the glass jar broke, the loudspeaker called for a cleanup on aisle five.
Whenever something spilled in the supermarket, you'd hear someone request a cleanup on aisle five.
He joked about his messy desk, saying it looked like he needed a cleanup on aisle five.

Synset Examples

They announced a cleanup on aisle five after a spill.
There's a cleanup on aisle three; be careful.
The store manager called for a cleanup on aisle two.
