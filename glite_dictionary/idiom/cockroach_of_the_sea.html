cockroach of the sea

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑkˌroʊtʃ əv ði ˈsi / (AmE), / ˈkɒkˌrəʊtʃ əv ðə ˈsiː / (BrE)
A derogatory term for shrimp, lobster, or crabs, likening them to cockroaches because of their scavenger habits and appearance.
derogatory term for shrimp, lobster, or crabs
offensive

Examples

Many people refuse to eat shrimp, calling them the cockroach of the sea.
He jokingly referred to lobster as the cockroach of the sea at the seafood restaurant.
Some seafood lovers dislike crabs being labeled the cockroach of the sea.

Synset Examples
