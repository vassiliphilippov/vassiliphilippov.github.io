how's tricks

1. X

/ haʊz trɪks / (AmE), / haʊz trɪks / (BrE)
(informal) Used as a friendly and casual way of asking someone how they are or how things are going in their life.
casual greeting; how are things?
informal

Examples

When I bumped into my old friend, I greeted him with, how's tricks?
She called me last night just to say how's tricks and catch up on life.
It's good to see you—how's tricks these days?

Synset Examples

At the reunion, I couldn't help but ask, how's tricks with you?
He smiled and replied, how's tricks in your life?
It's been a while; I asked him, how's tricks?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: how's it going - informal greeting asking how someone is
  • Synonym: what's up - informal greeting asking about current situation or well-being
