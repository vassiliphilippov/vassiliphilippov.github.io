to be a numskull

1. VERB

/ bi ə ˈnʌmˌskʌl / (AmE), / biː ə ˈnʌmskʌl / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or stupid way; to act without thinking
act foolishly or stupidly

Examples

Stop being a numskull and check your work before submitting it.
You were a numskull to forget your keys again.
Don't be a numskull; read the instructions carefully next time.

Synset Examples

Don't be a numskull; think before you act.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently
  • Antonym: act sensibly - to behave in a wise or prudent manner

Etymology

From 'numskull,' a 16th-century term for a foolish person, literally meaning 'numb skull' (dull-witted).
