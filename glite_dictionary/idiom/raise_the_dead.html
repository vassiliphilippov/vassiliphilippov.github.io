to raise the dead

1. VERB

/ reɪz ðə dɛd / (AmE), / reɪz ðə dɛd / (BrE)
to bring someone or something back to life after death, often with supernatural means
bring back to life

Examples

The legend says a powerful sorcerer could raise the dead with a secret spell.
No medication exists that can raise the dead, despite what early alchemists believed.
His singing is so loud, it could raise the dead!

Synset Examples

In the movie, they used ancient magic to raise the dead.
The magician claimed he could raise the dead with his tricks.
The folklore says he can raise the dead with a chant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to resurrect - to bring someone back to life
  • Synonym: to revive - restore to life or consciousness
