so be it

1. X

/ soʊ ˈbi ɪt / (AmE), / səʊ ˈbiː ɪt / (BrE)
used to indicate acceptance of a situation or a decision, even when it is disappointing or contrary to one's wishes.
expression of acceptance of a situation

Examples

If the weather is bad and we have to cancel the picnic, so be it.
They said I couldn't join the team, and I thought, so be it.
If management won't listen to our concerns, then so be it; we'll move on.

Synset Examples

If the plan fails, then so be it.
If they disagree, so be it; I won't change my mind.
If he wants to leave, then so be it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amen - expression of agreement or acceptance
  • Synonym: to let it be - expression of acceptance or resignation
