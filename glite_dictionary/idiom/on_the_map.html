on the map

1. ADV

/ ɑn ðə mæp / (AmE), / ɒn ðə mæp / (BrE)
known or recognized as important or significant; famous or prominent
become well-known or significant

Examples

The new art museum really put our small town on the map.
Her breakthrough novel placed her on the map as a major writer.
The technology boom brought the region on the map for international investors.

Synset Examples

His invention put our company on the map.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to put on the map - make famous or widely known
