to get creative juices flowing

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt kriˈeɪtɪv ˈʤusɪz ˈfloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡɛt kriˈeɪtɪv ˈdʒuːsɪz ˈfləʊɪŋ / (BrE)
to stimulate creativity or inspire new ideas; to activate one's creative thinking
to stimulate creativity or inspiration

Examples

Some people find that walking outdoors helps get creative juices flowing before starting a project.
A brainstorming session is designed to get creative juices flowing among team members.
She uses drawing exercises to get her creative juices flowing when facing writer's block.
