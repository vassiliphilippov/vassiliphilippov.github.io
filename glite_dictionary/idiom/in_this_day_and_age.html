in this day and age

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðɪs deɪ ənd eɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɪn ðɪs deɪ ənd eɪdʒ / (BrE)
at the present time; in the current era or period, especially in contrast with the past
at the present time; currently

Examples

In this day and age, most people rely heavily on their smartphones.
It's surprising to see such beliefs still exist in this day and age.
Many businesses have gone digital in this day and age.

Synset Examples

Finding a job can be challenging in this day and age.

In this day and age, communication is instant.
