to think twice

1. VERB

/ θɪŋk twaɪs / (AmE), / θɪŋk twaɪs / (BrE)
to consider something carefully or to reconsider before acting, especially to avoid mistakes or bad consequences
consider or reconsider carefully before acting

Examples

You should think twice before sharing personal information online.
Sarah always thinks twice before making big purchases.
He didn't think twice about quitting his job, but later he regretted it.

Synset Examples

I always think twice about spending money on luxury items.
You should think twice before making that decision.
He regretted not thinking twice about his words.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to reconsider - to think again about a decision or opinion
  • Antonym: rush into - to do something too quickly without proper consideration
