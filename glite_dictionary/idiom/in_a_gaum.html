in a gaum

1. ADV

/ ɪn ə ɡɔm / (AmE), / ɪn ə ɡɔːm / (BrE)
in a confused or disoriented state
in a state of confusion

Examples

After the accident, she wandered around in a gaum, unsure of what to do next.
The team looked in a gaum after the unexpected announcement.
Early mornings always leave me in a gaum until I've had my coffee.

Synset Examples

He was in a gaum after staying up all night.
