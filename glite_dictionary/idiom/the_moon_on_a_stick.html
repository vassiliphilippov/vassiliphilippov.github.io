the moon on a stick

1. NOUN

/ ðə mun ɑn ə stɪk / (AmE), / ðə muːn ɒn ə stɪk / (BrE)
something that is impossible to obtain or an unrealistically excessive demand.
something unattainable or excessively desired
british

Examples

No matter what she gets, she always asks for the moon on a stick.
He acted as if he deserved the moon on a stick for completing such a simple task.
Negotiating with him is difficult because he expects the moon on a stick.

Synset Examples

He wanted the moon on a stick, expecting perfection in every detail.
Her demands were unreasonable; she wanted the moon on a stick.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: the earth - everything possible or imaginable; a great amount
