to rechew old ideas

1. VERB

/ riˈtʃu oʊld aɪˈdiəz / (AmE), / riˈtʃuː əʊld aɪˈdɪəz / (BrE)
to reconsider or revisit concepts that have already been discussed or examined, often in search of new insights
reconsider previously discussed concepts

Examples

The team decided to rechew old ideas before launching a new campaign.
It's sometimes productive to rechew old ideas when brainstorming solutions to complex problems.
She encouraged her students to rechew old ideas to gain fresh perspectives.

Synset Examples

Sometimes you need to rechew old ideas to find new solutions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to reconsider - to think about again, especially to consider changing a decision
  • Synonym: rethink - to think about something again, especially with the possibility of changing your mind
