two a penny

1. ADJ

/ tu ə ˈpɛni / (AmE), / tuː ə ˈpɛni / (BrE)
very common or of little value; easy to find and not rare or special
very common; inexpensive

Examples

In this city, coffee shops are two a penny and easy to stumble upon.
Talented writers seem two a penny these days, but true originality is rare.
Promises from politicians are two a penny during election season.

Synset Examples

Good ideas are two a penny; execution matters more.
In this town, good restaurants are two a penny.
Those cheap souvenirs are two a penny at the market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dime a dozen - very common and of little value
