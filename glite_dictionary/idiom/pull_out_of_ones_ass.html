to pull out of one's ass

1. VERB

/ pʊl aʊt əv wʌnz æs / (AmE), / pʊl aʊt əv wʌnz ɑːs / (BrE)
to produce or invent something without any basis or justification, often implausibly or deceptively
make up something implausibly
slang vulgar informal

Examples

He seemed to pull those figures out of his ass during the presentation with no real data to back them up.
Don't just pull a solution out of your ass; we need proper analysis first.
She always pulls excuses out of her ass when she's late to work.
