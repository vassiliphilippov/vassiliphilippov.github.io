come to think of it

1. X

/ kʌm tə ˈθɪŋk əv ɪt / (AmE), / kʌm tə ˈθɪŋk əv ɪt / (BrE)
used to introduce a sudden realization, recollection, or new idea that has just occurred to the speaker
used to introduce a sudden realization

Examples

Come to think of it, I haven't seen her all week.
Come to think of it, we never received that package.
Come to think of it, the meeting was scheduled for next Monday, not this one.

Synset Examples

Come to think of it, I do need a new phone.
Come to think of it, that was a great idea!
