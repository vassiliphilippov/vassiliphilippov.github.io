from head to toe

1. ADV

/ frəm hɛd tu toʊ / (AmE), / frəm hɛd tə təʊ / (BrE)
covering or affecting the entire body, from the top of the head to the soles of the feet.
over the entire body

Examples

The doctor examined him from head to toe before making a diagnosis.
They dressed the bride from head to toe in traditional attire.
Mud splattered her from head to toe after she slipped in the rain.

Synset Examples

he was armed cap-a-pie

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cap-a-pie - completely covered, from head to foot

2. ADV

/ frəm hɛd tə toʊ / (AmE), / frəm hɛd tə təʊ / (BrE)
completely; covering or affecting the whole of someone or something, from top to bottom
completely; entirely (top to bottom)

Examples

The doctor examined her from head to toe to look for injuries.
After falling in the puddle, he was soaked from head to toe.
They searched the building from head to toe for any sign of evidence.

Synset Examples

  • Synonym: from top to bottom - completely, over the entire extent
