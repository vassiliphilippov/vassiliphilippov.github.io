to get one's lumps

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt wʌnz lʌmps / (AmE), / ɡɛt wʌnz lʌmps / (BrE)
to receive punishment or suffer the consequences for one’s actions or mistakes
suffer consequences for actions

Examples

After cheating on the exam and getting caught, he had to get his lumps.
Sometimes you just have to get your lumps and move on.
The team got their lumps this season but came back stronger next year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to take one's medicine - accept unpleasant consequences or punishment
  • Antonym: to get away with - escape punishment or consequences
