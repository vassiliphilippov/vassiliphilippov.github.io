up to no good

1. ADJ

/ ʌp tə noʊ ɡʊd / (AmE), / ʌp tə nəʊ ɡʊd / (BrE)
behaving in a way that is likely to be dishonest, mischievous, or suspicious
engaged in suspicious or mischievous activity

Examples

I think those boys hanging around the corner are up to no good.
The manager became suspicious when he realized the employees were up to no good after hours.
Whenever the dog is quiet for too long, I suspect he's up to no good.

Synset Examples

Those kids are always up to no good after school.
He tends to skulk around when he's up to no good.
I saw them sneaking around; they were up to no good.
He looks like he's up to no good in that alley.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suspicious - causing a feeling that something illegal or wrong is happening
  • Antonym: innocent - not intending to cause harm or do wrong
