as the next person can

1. X

/ æz ðə nɛkst ˈpɝːsən kæn / (AmE), / æz ðə nɛkst ˈpɜːsən kæn / (BrE)
to the same degree as anyone else; like any average person.
like anyone else

Examples

I enjoy a good movie as the next person can, but I don't watch TV every day.
She gets nervous when public speaking as the next person can.
He likes a challenge as the next person can, but sometimes he prefers to play it safe.

Synset Examples

I appreciate art, as the next girl might.
He can play guitar, as the next guy can.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: like anyone else - in the same way as any normal person
