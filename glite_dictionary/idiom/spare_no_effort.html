to spare no effort

1. VERB

/ spɛr noʊ ˈɛfɚt / (AmE), / speə nəʊ ˈefət / (BrE)
to make the greatest possible effort to achieve something; to try as hard as one possibly can
make every possible effort

Examples

Despite the difficulties, the team spared no effort to complete the project on time.
Our school principal spares no effort to provide students with the best opportunities.
They are sparing no effort to make the annual festival a memorable event.

Synset Examples

We will spare no effort to ensure your satisfaction.
They spared no effort in organizing the charity event.
He spared no effort to win the championship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to do one's utmost - make the greatest possible effort
  • Synonym: give it your all - put in all possible effort
