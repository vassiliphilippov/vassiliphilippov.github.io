that is to say

1. ADV

/ ðæt ɪz tə seɪ / (AmE), / ðæt ɪz tə seɪ / (BrE)
used to introduce a clearer or more precise explanation, restatement, or example of what has just been mentioned
used to clarify or restate

Examples

Several witnesses saw the man leave the building, that is to say, he was observed by many people.
There are three options available, that is to say, you can choose A, B, or C.
The house is undergoing renovations, that is to say, no one can live there right now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: namely - used to introduce specified details or examples
  • Synonym: in other words - used to express something in a different way
