in the same shoes

1. X

/ ɪn ðə seɪm ʃuz / (AmE), / ɪn ðə seɪm ʃuːz / (BrE)
in the same situation or circumstances as someone else; experiencing the same thing as another person
in the same situation as someone else

Examples

Having lost her job, she supported her friend, saying she was in the same shoes.
It's easier to understand someone's feelings when you are in the same shoes.
If I were in the same shoes, I'd probably make the same choice.

Synset Examples

Seeing her friend upset, she realized she was in the same shoes.
He empathized, knowing he had been in the same shoes before.
If I were in the same shoes, I’d feel nervous too.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the same boat - sharing the same difficult situation as someone else
  • Synonym: in someone else's shoes - in the same situation or circumstances as another person
