easy on the eyes

1. ADJ

/ ˈiːzi ɑn ðə aɪz / (AmE), / ˈiːzi ɒn ðiː aɪz / (BrE)
pleasant or attractive to look at; visually appealing.
visually attractive; pleasant to look at
informal

Examples

The new website layout is easy on the eyes and simple to use.
She has a style that's both unique and easy on the eyes.
The film's cinematography is particularly easy on the eyes, with beautiful scenery throughout.
