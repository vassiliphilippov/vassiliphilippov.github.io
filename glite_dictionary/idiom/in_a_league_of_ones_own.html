in a league of one's own

1. X

/ ɪn ə liɡ əv wʌnz oʊn / (AmE), / ɪn ə liːɡ əv wʌnz əʊn / (BrE)
so superior to others in ability, quality, or achievement that they are unmatched or incomparable
superior and unmatched in ability

Examples

When it comes to mathematics, she is truly in a league of her own.
That singer’s voice puts him in a league of his own among modern performers.
Their innovation has placed the company in a league of its own within the tech industry.

Synset Examples

His skills make him in a league of his own.
This chef is in a league of his own.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peerless - having no equal; unmatched
  • Synonym: unparalleled - having no parallel or equal
  • Antonym: ordinary - with no special or distinctive features; normal
  • Antonym: average - typical; not outstanding in any way
