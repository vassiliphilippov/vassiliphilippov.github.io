as the next person

1. X

/ æz ðə nɛkst ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / æz ðə nɛkst ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
(used in comparisons) to the degree that most people do; like any other person.
to the same degree as anyone else

Examples

I enjoy a good movie as the next person, but I don't go to the cinema often.
He's concerned about the environment as the next person is.
She likes to relax after work as the next person does.

Synset Examples

I'm interested in travel, as the next person is.
She enjoys reading, just as the next person does.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: like everyone else - in the same way as most people
