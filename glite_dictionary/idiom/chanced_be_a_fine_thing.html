chance'd be a fine thing

1. X

/ ˈtʃænsd ˌbi ə ˌfaɪn ˈθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːnst ˌbiː ə ˌfaɪn ˈθɪŋ / (BrE)
Used to express the belief that something is very unlikely to happen, or that it would be wonderful if it did.
expression of unlikely or desired outcome
british

Examples

He said he might pay for dinner—chance'd be a fine thing!
If my boss ever gave me a week off,chance'd be a fine thing.
You think the train will be on time? Chance'd be a fine thing.

Synset Examples

She wants a vacation, but chance'd be a fine thing.
If he thinks she'll agree, chance'd be a fine thing.
