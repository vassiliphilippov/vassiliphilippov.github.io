ahead of the pack

1. ADV

/ əˈhɛd əv ðə pæk / (AmE), / əˈhɛd əv ðə pæk / (BrE)
in a position of being more advanced, successful, or innovative than others in a group or field
more advanced or successful than others

Examples

She worked hard to stay ahead of the pack in her industry.
Technological innovation keeps the company ahead of the pack each year.
His creative thinking puts him ahead of the pack among his peers.

Synset Examples

She consistently stays ahead of the pack in her field.
He is always ahead of the pack in innovation.
Our company remains ahead of the pack in sales.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lead the field - to be the most successful or advanced in a particular area
  • Antonym: trail behind - to be less advanced or less successful than others
