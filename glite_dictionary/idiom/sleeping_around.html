sleeping around

1. NOUN

/ ˈsliːpɪŋ əˈɹaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈsliːpɪŋ əˈɹaʊnd / (BrE)
to have sexual relations with multiple partners without commitment or exclusivity.
having casual sexual relationships
informal

Examples

Rumors spread quickly that she was sleeping around after breaking up with her boyfriend.
He admitted to sleeping around during his college years, but said he’s more settled now.
Some people judge others for sleeping around, but attitudes about casual sex have changed over time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promiscuous - having many casual sexual partners
