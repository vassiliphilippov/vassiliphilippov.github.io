HE-double-L yeah

1. INTJ

/ ˌeɪtʃ.iː ˌdʌb.əl ˈɛl ˈjæ / (AmE), / ˌeɪtʃ.iː ˌdʌb.əl ˈɛl ˈjæ / (BrE)
An enthusiastic expression of strong agreement or approval, used as a euphemism for 'hell yeah'.
enthusiastic euphemistic agreement
informal

Examples

Are you coming to the concert tonight? HE-double-L yeah!
When they asked if he wanted free pizza, he replied, 'HE-double-L yeah!'
Did she accept the job offer? She said, 'HE-double-L yeah!'

Synset Examples

She was like, 'HE-double-L yeah, I'm in!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hell yeah - An enthusiastic expression of strong agreement or approval
  • Antonym: no way - Strong refusal or disagreement

Etymology

Formed as a euphemistic spelling of 'hell yeah', using the letters 'H-E-double-L'.
