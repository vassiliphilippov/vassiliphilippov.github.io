to put one's darnedest

1. VERB

/ pʊt wʌnz ˈdɑrn.dəst / (AmE), / pʊt wʌnz ˈdɑːn.dɪst / (BrE)
to make one's greatest effort; to try as hard as possible.
do one's very best
informal

Examples

Despite her fatigue, she put her darnedest into finishing the project on time.
The team put their darnedest into winning the championship, but fell just short.
You have to put your darnedest into studying if you want to pass the exam.

Synset Examples

They put their darnedest into making the event a success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to do one's best - to make the greatest possible effort
  • Synonym: try one's hardest - to put forth the greatest possible effort

Etymology

Formed from the superlative 'darnedest,' a euphemistic variant of 'damnedest'; 'put one's...' is a common idiomatic construction for making an effort.
