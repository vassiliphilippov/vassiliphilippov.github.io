as if there were no tomorrow

1. X

/ æz ɪf ðɛr wɝr noʊ təˈmɑːroʊ / (AmE), / æz ɪf ðeə wə nəʊ təˈmɒrəʊ / (BrE)
In a way that shows great enthusiasm, energy, or carelessness, without concern for future consequences.
with reckless enthusiasm or disregard for consequences

Examples

She danced as if there were no tomorrow, laughing with joy every step of the way.
He spent money as if there were no tomorrow during his vacation overseas.
They partied as if there were no tomorrow, refusing to think about the consequences.

Synset Examples
