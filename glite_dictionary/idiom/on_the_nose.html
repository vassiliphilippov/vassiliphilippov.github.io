on the nose

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə noʊz / (AmE), / ɒn ðə nəʊz / (BrE)
Exactly correct or precise, without any deviation from a specified standard or value.
exactly correct; precise

Examples

Her estimate of the final cost was on the nose.
The scientist's prediction was on the nose, matching the results exactly.
Your answer was on the nose—that's exactly what I was looking for.

Synset Examples

the prediction for snow was right on the button

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spot on - exactly correct or accurate
  • Antonym: off the mark - not correct or accurate
