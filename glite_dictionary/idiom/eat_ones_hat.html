to eat one's hat

1. VERB

/ it wʌnz hæt / (AmE), / iːt wʌnz hæt / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is so unlikely that, if it were to happen, the speaker would do something absurd
asserting disbelief or strong doubt

Examples

If that team actually wins, I’ll eat my hat!
She said she’d eat her hat if he managed to finish the marathon.
I’d eat my hat if they ever invited us to their wedding.

Synset Examples

If she finishes that project on time, I’ll eat my hat.
I’d eat my hat if they win this game.
He promised to eat his hat if it rains tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bet - to risk something, usually for a wager
