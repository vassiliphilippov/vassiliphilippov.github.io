by an eyelash

1. ADV

/ baɪ ən ˈaɪˌlæʃ / (AmE), / baɪ ən ˈaɪˌlæʃ / (BrE)
by a very small margin; very narrowly or closely
by a very narrow margin

Examples

The two runners finished the race so closely that the winner was decided by an eyelash.
He got into the college by an eyelash, scoring just above the cutoff.
Their team escaped elimination by an eyelash after winning the last game.

Synset Examples

He passed the test by an eyelash.
The car missed the barrier by an eyelash.
She won the competition by an eyelash.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: by a hair - by a very small amount; narrowly
  • Synonym: by a whisker - by a very small margin; only just
