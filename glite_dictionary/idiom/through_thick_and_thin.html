through thick and thin

1. X

/ θru ˈθɪk ənd ˈθɪn / (AmE), / θruː ˈθɪk ən ˈθɪn / (BrE)
in all situations, whether good or bad; showing unwavering support or loyalty regardless of difficulties
in all situations, good or bad

Examples

Her best friend supported her through thick and thin during challenging times.
They remained loyal through thick and thin, never abandoning each other in hard situations.
A true partner stays with you through thick and thin, no matter the circumstances.

Etymology

Attested since the late Middle Ages, referring metaphorically to moving through both thick (difficult) and thin (easy) conditions.
