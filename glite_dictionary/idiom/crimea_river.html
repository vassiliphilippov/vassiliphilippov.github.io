Crimea river

1. X

/ kraɪˈmiːə ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / kraɪˈmiːə ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) Used to mockingly dismiss someone else's complaints, as a pun on 'cry me a river.'
mocking dismissal of complaints (pun)
informal slang

Examples

When she started complaining about her workload again, he just rolled his eyes and said, 'Crimea river.'
If you're looking for sympathy, you won't get it here—Crimea river!
Every time he grumbles about his misfortunes, someone inevitably replies with 'Crimea river.'

Synset Examples

Whenever he complains, I just say, 'Crimea river!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cry me a river - an expression used to dismiss someone's complaints or sadness

Etymology

Pun on 'cry me a river', with 'Crimea' substituting as a homophone of 'cry me a'. Refers to the Crimea region for comic effect.
