to wrestle with a pig

1. VERB

/ ˈrɛsəl wɪð ə pɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈrɛsəl wɪð ə pɪɡ / (BrE)
to engage in a pointless or futile argument, often with someone who is unreasonable or uncooperative
engage in a futile argument

Examples

Whenever I try to complain to customer service, it feels like wrestling with a pig.
He warned her that arguing with him would just be wrestling with a pig.
Debating internet trolls usually ends up being like wrestling with a pig.

Synset Examples

It's pointless to wrestle with a pig over this issue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waste one's breath - make a futile attempt to persuade or argue
