Christ on a stick

1. X

/ kraɪst ɑn ə stɪk / (AmE), / kraɪst ɒn ə stɪk / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express disbelief, surprise, or ridicule toward something absurd or nonsensical
expression of disbelief or ridicule
informal

Examples

Christ on a stick, how did this project get approved?
When he saw the mess, he muttered, 'Christ on a stick!'
You spent how much on shoes? Christ on a stick, that's ridiculous!

Synset Examples

He realized his excuse sounded like Christ on a stick.
She dismissed his claims as Christ on a stick.
