to be a dickwad

1. VERB

/ bi ə ˈdɪkwɑd / (AmE), / biː ə ˈdɪkwɒd / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish, obnoxious, or irritating manner, typically in a way that angers or annoys others
act in an irritating or obnoxious way
informal vulgar

Examples

If you keep being a dickwad to your coworkers, no one will want to help you.
She told him not to be a dickwad about the situation and just accept the decision.
There’s no need to be a dickwad—just listen to what they have to say.

Synset Examples

Don’t be a dickwad; just apologize already.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be a jerk - to behave in a rude or unpleasant way
  • Synonym: be an asshole - to act in a mean or spiteful way
