guns blazing

1. ADV

/ ɡʌnz ˈbleɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡʌnz ˈbleɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
in a very aggressive or enthusiastic manner, showing determination or energy
with energy and aggression

Examples

The team entered the final round with guns blazing, determined to claim the trophy.
He came into the meeting guns blazing, ready to challenge everyone’s ideas.
Whenever she starts a project, she goes in guns blazing and never holds back.

Synset Examples

She approached the competition with guns blazing, determined to win.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all guns blazing - with maximum force or effort, a variant idiom
