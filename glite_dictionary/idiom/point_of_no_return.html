point of no return

1. NOUN

/ pɔɪnt əv noʊ rɪˈtɜrn / (AmE), / pɔɪnt əv nəʊ rɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
the stage in a process when it is no longer possible to change or stop what has been started
irreversible stage in a process

Examples

After crossing the bridge, the army had reached the point of no return.
Once the surgery began, the doctors recognized they had passed the point of no return.
Accepting the final offer was the business's point of no return.

Synset Examples

After signing the contract, we reached the point of no return.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irreversible - impossible to reverse back to a previous state
  • Degree Lower: turning point - a critical moment when a significant change occurs
