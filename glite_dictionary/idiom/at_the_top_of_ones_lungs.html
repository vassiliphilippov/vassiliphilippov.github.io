at the top of one's lungs

1. ADV

/ ət ðə tɑp əv wʌnz lʌŋz / (AmE), / ət ðə tɒp əv wʌnz lʌŋz / (BrE)
very loudly; as loudly as possible, usually referring to shouting, singing, or speaking
as loudly as possible

Examples

The children were singing at the top of their lungs in the back seat.
He called for help at the top of his lungs after getting lost in the woods.
During the protest, people were chanting at the top of their lungs for change.

Synset Examples

She sang at the top of her lungs during the concert.
He shouted at the top of his lungs in excitement.
They cheered at the top of their lungs for the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loudly - in a loud manner
  • Antonym: softly - in a quiet or gentle manner
