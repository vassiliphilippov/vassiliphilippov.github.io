to gather one's thoughts

1. VERB

/ ˈɡæðɚ wʌnz θɑts / (AmE), / ˈɡæðə wʌnz θɒts / (BrE)
to take a moment to collect and organize your ideas or feelings before speaking or taking action
collect and organize your ideas or feelings

Examples

Before answering the tough question, she paused to gather her thoughts.
He needed a quiet place to gather his thoughts after the heated argument.
Let me gather my thoughts before I continue with my presentation.

Synset Examples

Just give me one moment to gather my thoughts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collect oneself - to regain composure or self-control
