on someone's bullshit

1. ADJ

/ ɑn sʌmˌwʌnz ˈbʊlˌʃɪt / (AmE), / ɒn sʌmˌwʌnz ˈbʊlˌʃɪt / (BrE)
behaving in a deceitful, insincere, or evasive manner, often to avoid responsibility or hide the truth
acting deceitfully or insincerely
informal vulgar

Examples

She was clearly on her bullshit during the interview, making up answers as she went along.
Stop on your bullshit and tell me what really happened last night.
Whenever he's late, he's always on his bullshit with some unbelievable excuse.

Synset Examples

She always seems to be on her bullshit during meetings.
Don't be on your bullshit when it’s time to deliver.
He was clearly on his bullshit with that excuse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lie - to say or do something false with intent to deceive
  • Antonym: honest - free of deceit; truthful and sincere
