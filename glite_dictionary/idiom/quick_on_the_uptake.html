quick on the uptake

1. ADJ

/ kwɪk ɑn ði ˈʌpˌteɪk / (AmE), / kwɪk ɒn ði ˈʌpteɪk / (BrE)
able to understand or grasp ideas quickly; intelligent or perceptive
able to understand quickly

Examples

Her new assistant is quick on the uptake and needs very little supervision.
If you're quick on the uptake, this complex process will make sense in no time.
The teacher praised Tom for being quick on the uptake during today's lesson.

Synset Examples

He's quick on the uptake and understands concepts fast.
She’s quick on the uptake, grasping new ideas immediately.
The new employee is surprisingly quick on the uptake.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: slow on the uptake - slow to understand or learn
