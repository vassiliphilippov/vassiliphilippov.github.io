milk in the coconut

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlk ɪn ðə ˈkoʊkəˌnʌt / (AmE), / ˈmɪlk ɪn ðə ˈkəʊkənʌt / (BrE)
an expression used to indicate that something is amiss or unusual in a situation
expression meaning something is not right

Examples

There's definitely some milk in the coconut if the project keeps getting delayed.
When the accounts don't add up, you know there's milk in the coconut.
Everyone sensed milk in the coconut when she started acting so strangely.

Synset Examples

His tardiness this week has been unusual; that accounts for the milk in the coconut.
She didn't finish her work again, and that accounts for the milk in the coconut.
