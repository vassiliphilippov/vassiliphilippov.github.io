on the QT

1. ADV

/ ɑn ði ˌkjuːˈtiː / (AmE), / ɒn ði ˌkjuːˈtiː / (BrE)
in a secret or confidential manner; not openly or publicly
secretly; confidentially
informal

Examples

They made the agreement on the QT to avoid unwanted attention.
She told me on the QT that she was looking for a new job.
The company hired extra staff on the QT before the official announcement.

Synset Examples

met secretly to discuss the invasion plans
the children secretly went to the movies when they were supposed to be at the library
they arranged to meet in secret

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in secret - privately or not openly
  • Antonym: openly - in a manner that is not secret; publicly

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'on the quiet,' with 'QT' representing the letters Q and T for 'quiet.'
