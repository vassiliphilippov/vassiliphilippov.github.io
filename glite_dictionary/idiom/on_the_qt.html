on the Q.T.

1. ADV

/ ɑn ðə ˌkjuː ˈtiː / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ˌkjuː ˈtiː / (BrE)
In secret; not openly or publicly.
secretly; not openly
informal

Examples

We are handling this project on the Q.T. until the official announcement.
He told me about the new policy on the Q.T. so that no one else would know.
They met on the Q.T. to discuss the deal without anyone noticing.

Synset Examples

met secretly to discuss the invasion plans
the children secretly went to the movies when they were supposed to be at the library
they arranged to meet in secret

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secretly - in a secret or covert manner
  • Antonym: openly - in a public or overt manner

Etymology

From the abbreviation Q.T., meaning 'quiet', likely from 'quiet tip' or 'quiet time' in late 19th-century American slang.
