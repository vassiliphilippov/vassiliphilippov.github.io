that guy

1. NOUN

/ ðæt ɡaɪ / (AmE), / ðæt ɡaɪ / (BrE)
Used to refer to a person, usually male, whose behavior is considered annoying, inappropriate, or undesirable in a particular situation.
person behaving in an undesirable way
informal

Examples

Try not to be that guy who takes the last slice of pizza without asking.
Everyone remembers that guy who kept talking during the movie.
No one wants to be that guy who ignores basic etiquette at the gym.

Synset Examples

At the party, don't be that guy who leaves early.
In meetings, don't be that guy who interrupts everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person
  • Synonym: that girl - female counterpart of 'that guy'
