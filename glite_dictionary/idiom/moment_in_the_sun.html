moment in the sun

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊmənt ɪn ðə sʌn / (AmE), / ˈməʊmənt ɪn ðə sʌn / (BrE)
a brief period during which someone enjoys success, attention, or recognition
brief period of success or recognition

Examples

After years of hard work, she finally had her moment in the sun when her novel became a bestseller.
Every athlete hopes for a moment in the sun during their career.
The local band enjoyed a brief moment in the sun after their song went viral online.

Synset Examples

She finally had her moment in the sun at the award ceremony.
Everyone deserves a moment in the sun once in a while.
He was waiting for his moment in the sun to shine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: time in the spotlight - period when someone receives a lot of attention
