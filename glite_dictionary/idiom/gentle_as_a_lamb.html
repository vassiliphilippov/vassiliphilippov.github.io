gentle as a lamb

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɛn.tl̩ əz ə læm / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛn.tl̩ əz ə læm / (BrE)
extremely gentle, kind, or mild in manner or temperament; as gentle as a lamb.
extremely gentle and kind

Examples

Despite his gruff appearance, he was gentle as a lamb around his grandchildren.
Our new puppy is gentle as a lamb and never bites anyone.
She always acts gentle as a lamb when caring for the elderly.

Synset Examples
