for all the world to see

1. X

/ fər ɔl ðə wɜrld tə si / (AmE), / fə ɔːl ðə wɜːld tə siː / (BrE)
openly visible or obvious to everyone; in a way that is public and clear to all people
openly visible to everyone

Examples

The mural stands on the main street for all the world to see.
She displayed her gold medal for all the world to see at the ceremony.
