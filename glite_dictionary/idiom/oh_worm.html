oh worm

1. INTJ

/ oʊ wɜrm / (AmE), / əʊ wɜːm / (BrE)
used as an exclamation to express mild dismay, disappointment, or regret, especially in online contexts
expression of mild dismay or regret
informal slang

Examples

Oh worm, I left my keys at home again.
Oh worm, looks like it’s going to rain right before our picnic.
She saw the disappointing exam result and just muttered, 'oh worm.'

Synset Examples

Oh worm, I forgot to bring my lunch!
Oh worm, that’s not what I wanted to hear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oh no - expression of dismay or concern
  • Synonym: oh dear - expression of mild surprise or regret
