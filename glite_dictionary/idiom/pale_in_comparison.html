to pale in comparison

1. VERB

/ peɪl ɪn kəmˈpɛr.sən / (AmE), / peɪl ɪn kəmˈpæɹ.sən / (BrE)
to seem insignificant or less impressive when compared to something else
seem less impressive by comparison

Examples

The beauty of the mountains pales in comparison to the vastness of the ocean.
All other candidates pale in comparison to her qualifications.
The performance last night truly paled in comparison to their previous shows.

Synset Examples

His problems pale in comparison to what others face.
Her achievements pale in comparison to his success.
The new project pales in comparison to our last one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dwarf - to make something seem small or unimportant by comparison
