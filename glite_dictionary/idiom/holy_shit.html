holy shit

1. INTJ

/ ˈhoʊli ʃɪt / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli ʃɪt / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express surprise, shock, or excitement
expression of surprise or shock
informal vulgar

Examples

Holy shit, did you see the size of that wave?
When he opened the gift, he shouted, 'holy shit!'
Holy shit, I wasn't expecting you to arrive so early!

Synset Examples

Holy shit, did you see that fight?
I just won the lottery—holy shit!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holy cow - an exclamation expressing surprise or astonishment (milder and less vulgar)
  • Synonym: holy moly - an exclamation used to express surprise or astonishment (mild, non-vulgar)
