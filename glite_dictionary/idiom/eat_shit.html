to eat shit

1. VERB

/ it ʃɪt / (AmE), / iːt ʃɪt / (BrE)
(very vulgar) Used to express contempt, dismissal, or to indicate someone will suffer severe consequences or embarrassment.
express contempt or foresee someone suffering consequences (vulgar)
offensive vulgar

Examples

He stormed out of the meeting and told them all to eat shit.
If you cheat in this game, you're going to eat shit sooner or later.
After making such a public blunder, she really felt like she'd eaten shit.

Synset Examples

He told his boss to eat shit after being unfairly treated.
Sometimes you just have to tell people to eat shit.
She felt like she was going to eat shit after that mistake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to go to hell - used to angrily dismiss or insult someone
  • Synonym: to suffer - to experience pain, humiliation, or hardship
