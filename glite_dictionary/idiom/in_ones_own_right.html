in one's own right

1. ADV

/ ɪn wʌnz oʊn raɪt / (AmE), / ɪn wʌnz əʊn raɪt / (BrE)
having a position, status, or quality because of one's own achievements or qualities, rather than because of a connection with someone else.
by virtue of one's own achievements

Examples

She is an accomplished artist in her own right, not just the wife of a famous painter.
He became a respected scientist in his own right after years of dedicated research.
The book, while part of a series, stands out in its own right as a remarkable work.
