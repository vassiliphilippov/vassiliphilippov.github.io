in concert with

1. ADP

/ ɪn ˈkɑnsɚt wɪθ / (AmE), / ɪn ˈkɒnsət wɪð / (BrE)
acting together with someone or something; in harmony or agreement with.
in agreement or harmony with

Examples

The new law was drafted in concert with environmental groups.
The design team worked in concert with the engineers to create the product.
Foreign aid was given in concert with local charities to ensure its effectiveness.

Synset Examples

The dancers moved in concert with the music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in harmony with - in agreement or accord with
