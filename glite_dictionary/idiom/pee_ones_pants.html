to pee one's pants

1. VERB

/ piː wʌnz pænts / (AmE), / piː wʌnz pænts / (BrE)
to lose control of one's bladder, typically because of extreme excitement, fear, or laughter
lose bladder control from excitement or fear
informal

Examples

She laughed so hard at the joke that she almost peed her pants.
I was so scared during the thunderstorm I thought I might pee my pants.
If I see my favorite celebrity in person, I might just pee my pants from excitement.

Synset Examples

He was so excited, he felt like he might pee in his pants.
Seeing the surprise party made her nearly pee her pants with joy.
The scary movie made me feel like I might pee my pants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to wet one's pants - to involuntarily urinate in one's trousers, especially from fear or laughter
