at someone's feet

1. ADV

/ æt ˈsʌmwʌnz fit / (AmE), / at ˈsʌmwɒnz fiːt / (BrE)
in a position of loyalty, admiration, or submission to someone
in submission or loyalty to someone

Examples

Ever since he rescued her, she has been at his feet, grateful for everything.
The young musician was at the maestro's feet, eager to learn from the best.
He seemed content to be at her feet, admiring her intelligence and charm.

Synset Examples

She found a loyal friend at her feet.
The puppy lay happily at my feet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: under someone's thumb - under someone's control or influence
  • Antonym: in charge - having control or authority
