to work to the bone

1. VERB

/ wɝːk tə ðə boʊn / (AmE), / wɜːk tə ðə bəʊn / (BrE)
to work extremely hard, often to the point of exhaustion
work extremely hard

Examples

She worked to the bone trying to finish the project before the deadline.
Farmers often work to the bone during the busy harvest season.
He worked to the bone to provide for his family.

Synset Examples

They worked their fingers to the bone to save money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to work one's fingers to the bone - to work extremely hard
