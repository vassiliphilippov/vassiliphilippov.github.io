on the improve

1. X

/ ɑn ði ɪmˈpruv / (AmE), / ɒn ði ɪmˈpruːv / (BrE)
showing signs of progress or getting better after a previous lower state; improving.
improving; getting better

Examples

After a difficult season, the team's morale is finally on the improve.
Thanks to new medication, her symptoms are definitely on the improve.
The company's financial situation is on the improve since the recent restructuring.

Synset Examples

His performance at work is definitely on the improve.
Her health is on the improve after the treatment.
The economy is finally on the improve after years of recession.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to improve - to become better or make something better
