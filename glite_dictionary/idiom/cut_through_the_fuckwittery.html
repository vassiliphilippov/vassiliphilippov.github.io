to cut through the fuckwittery

1. VERB

/ kʌt θru ðə fʌkˈwɪtəri / (AmE), / kʌt θruː ðə fʌkˈwɪtəri / (BrE)
(vulgar, informal) To disregard pointless nonsense or stupidity and focus on the main issue.
ignore nonsense and focus on what matters
informal vulgar

Examples

Can we just cut through the fuckwittery and talk about the real problem?
The manager insisted they cut through the fuckwittery to reach a decision quickly.
Whenever meetings get bogged down, Julia likes to cut through the fuckwittery and restore focus.

Synset Examples

Let's cut through the fuckwittery and get to the point.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cut to the chase - get to the main point quickly
