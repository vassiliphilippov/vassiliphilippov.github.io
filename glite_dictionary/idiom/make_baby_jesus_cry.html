to make baby Jesus cry

1. VERB

/ meɪk ˌbeɪbi ˈdʒiːzəs kraɪ / (AmE), / meɪk ˌbeɪbi ˈdʒiːzəs kraɪ / (BrE)
to cause great disappointment or sadness, often in a humorous or exaggerated way
humorously describe causing deep disappointment or sadness

Examples

If you waste good chocolate, you'll make baby Jesus cry.
Leaving all the lights on is enough to make baby Jesus cry with frustration.
Her terrible singing would probably make baby Jesus cry.

Synset Examples

Her rude comments could make baby Jesus cry.
That loud argument really made baby Jesus cry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make someone cry - to cause someone to cry
