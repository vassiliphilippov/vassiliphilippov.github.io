to sing the praises of

1. VERB

/ sɪŋ ðə ˈpreɪzɪz əv / (AmE), / sɪŋ ðə ˈpreɪzɪz ɒv / (BrE)
to praise or commend someone or something enthusiastically
praise enthusiastically

Examples

The mayor did not hesitate to sing the praises of the new community center at the ribbon-cutting ceremony.
Colleagues often sing the praises of her professionalism and dedication at work.
After the successful concert, critics everywhere began singing the praises of the young musician.

Synset Examples

The teacher always sings the praises of her students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: praise - to express approval or admiration for someone or something
