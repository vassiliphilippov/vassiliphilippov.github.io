to scream bloody murder

1. VERB

/ skrim ˈblʌdi ˈmɝdɚ / (AmE), / skriːm ˈblʌdi ˈmɜːdə / (BrE)
to shout or protest very loudly, especially in pain, anger, or distress
shout/cry loudly in distress or anger

Examples

When she saw the spider on her pillow, she screamed bloody murder.
Parents would scream bloody murder if the school canceled recess.
He screamed bloody murder after accidentally dropping the heavy box on his foot.

Synset Examples

He screamed bloody murder when he stubbed his toe.
The child screamed bloody murder when he lost his toy.
She'll scream bloody murder if you touch her phone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yell - to shout loudly, often in anger or excitement
  • Synonym: shriek - to make a loud, high-pitched cry
