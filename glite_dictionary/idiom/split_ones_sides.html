to split one's sides

1. VERB

/ splɪt wʌnz saɪdz / (AmE), / splɪt wʌnz saɪdz / (BrE)
to laugh so much that it feels as if your sides will burst; to laugh very hard
laugh very hard

Examples

The joke was so funny that I was splitting my sides with laughter.
During the comedy show, the audience was splitting their sides at every punchline.
He was splitting his sides just remembering that story from last night.

Synset Examples

We were splitting our sides over the hilarious movie.
The comedian had the audience splitting their sides.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: die laughing - to laugh uncontrollably or extremely
  • Antonym: keep a straight face - to avoid laughing even though something is funny
