God forbid

1. INTJ

/ ɡɑd fərˈbɪd / (AmE), / ɡɒd fəˈbɪd / (BrE)
used to express the hope that something bad will not happen
expression of hoping to avoid misfortune

Examples

God forbid anything happens to the children while we're away.
God forbid that we ever have to experience another war like that.
If, God forbid, the company goes bankrupt, many jobs will be lost.

Synset Examples

He said, 'God forbid we lose the match again'.
God forbid something happens to her on the trip.
God forbid we run out of supplies during the hike.
