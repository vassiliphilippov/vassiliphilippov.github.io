trick in one's hip pocket

1. NOUN

/ trɪk ɪn wʌnz hɪp ˈpɑkɪt / (AmE), / trɪk ɪn wʌnz hɪp ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
a secret strategy, technique, or advantage kept in reserve for future use
secret or reserve strategy

Examples

Whenever he felt stuck, he always seemed to have a trick in his hip pocket to get ahead.
She impressed the interviewers with a trick in her hip pocket that no one expected.
It's wise to keep a trick in your hip pocket for difficult negotiations.

Synset Examples

He had a few tricks in his hip pocket for the presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ace up one's sleeve - a secret advantage or resource kept in reserve
