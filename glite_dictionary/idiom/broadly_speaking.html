broadly speaking

1. ADV

/ ˈbrɔdli ˈspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdli ˈspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
used to indicate that a statement is true in general, without regard to specific details or exceptions
in general terms; generally speaking

Examples

Broadly speaking, the new policy will benefit most employees.
We can, broadly speaking, divide the population into three main groups.
Broadly speaking, these problems arise from a lack of communication.

Synset Examples

he interprets the law broadly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: generally speaking - in most cases; without going into detail
