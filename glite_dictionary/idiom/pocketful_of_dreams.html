pocketful of dreams

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkɪtˌfʊl əv ˈdriːmz / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪtˌfʊl əv ˈdriːmz / (BrE)
A large number of aspirations or hopes, often held by someone looking forward to the future optimistically.
many hopes or aspirations

Examples

With nothing but a pocketful of dreams, she set out to travel the world.
Though he had little money, he always carried a pocketful of dreams.
A pocketful of dreams was all he needed to stay motivated through hard times.

Synset Examples

He had a pocketful of dreams he wanted to pursue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dream - a cherished aspiration, ambition, or ideal
