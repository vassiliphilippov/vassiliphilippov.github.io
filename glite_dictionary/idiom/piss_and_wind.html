piss and wind

1. NOUN

/ pɪs ənd wɪnd / (AmE), / pɪs ənd wɪnd / (BrE)
empty talk or nonsense; speech without substance or real value
empty or insubstantial talk
informal vulgar

Examples

The politician’s speech was just piss and wind, offering no real solutions.
When challenged on details, his arguments turned out to be nothing but piss and wind.
She grew tired of all the piss and wind at the meeting and asked for practical suggestions.

Synset Examples

I can't stand his piss and wind attitude anymore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot air - empty or exaggerated talk intended to impress
  • Synonym: nonsense - language or ideas that are absurd or meaningless
