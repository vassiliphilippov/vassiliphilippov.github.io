right up someone's strasse

1. X

/ raɪt ʌp sʌmwʌnz strɑs / (AmE), / raɪt ʌp sʌmwʌnz ˈstrɑːs / (BrE)
perfectly suited to someone's tastes, interests, or abilities
exactly suited to someone
british informal

Examples

That new science fiction series is right up my strasse.
A relaxing holiday by the sea sounds right up her strasse.
If you like spicy food, this restaurant will be right up your strasse.

Synset Examples

The art exhibit is right up her strasse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: right up someone's street - perfectly suited to someone; the more common variant

Etymology

From British slang, modelled on 'right up one's street', with 'strasse' being the German word for 'street'.
