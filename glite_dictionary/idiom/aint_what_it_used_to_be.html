ain't what it used to be

1. X

/ eɪnt wʌt ɪt ˈjuːst tuː bi / (AmE), / eɪnt wɒt ɪt ˈjuːst tuː biː / (BrE)
no longer as good, effective, or impressive as it once was
not as good as before
informal

Examples

Many fans say the team ain't what it used to be after several top players left.
Some believe that customer service ain't what it used to be in today's fast-paced world.
The old cinema ain't what it used to be, but it's still open for business.

Synset Examples

Her singing ain't what it used to was, but she still loves it.
