going to be sick

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡoʊɪŋ tə bi sɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊɪŋ tə bi sɪk / (BrE)
to feel as if one is about to vomit; to feel extremely nauseous or distressed.
about to vomit; very nauseous
informal

Examples

I think I'm going to be sick after that roller coaster ride.
She ran to the bathroom, saying she was going to be sick.
The smell was so awful that he felt he was going to be sick.

Synset Examples

I'm going to be sick if I hear that again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vomit - to eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth
  • Synonym: nauseous - feeling like you are about to vomit
