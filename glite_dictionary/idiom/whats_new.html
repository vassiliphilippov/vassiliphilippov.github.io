what's new

1. X

/ wʌts nu / (AmE), / wɒts njuː / (BrE)
used to ask about recent developments, events, or changes in someone's life or a particular situation
phrase asking about recent developments

Examples

Whenever I call my brother, he always greets me with 'what's new?'.
When I met her at the café, she smiled and said, 'what's new?'.
After returning from vacation, everyone at the office wanted to know what's new in the project.

Synset Examples

Hey, what's new with you lately?
She asked me what's new at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: how's it going - idiom asking about someone's well-being or current state
  • Synonym: what's up - idiom used to ask about what is happening or new
