like a fart in a windstorm

1. X

/ laɪk ə fɑrt ɪn ə ˈwɪnd.stɔrm / (AmE), / laɪk ə fɑːt ɪn ə ˈwɪnd.stɔːm / (BrE)
used to describe something that goes completely unnoticed or is ignored by everyone
unnoticed or ignored; not attracting attention
informal

Examples

Her suggestions were like a fart in a windstorm, hardly anyone noticed them during the meeting.
The new campaign was like a fart in a windstorm and failed to make any impact on the voters.
His apology was like a fart in a windstorm—quickly forgotten and ignored by everyone.

Synset Examples

She joked that it was like a fart in a windstorm, unheard.
