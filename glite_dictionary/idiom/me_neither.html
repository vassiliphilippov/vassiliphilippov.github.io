me neither

1. INTJ

/ mi ˈniðər / (AmE), / mi ˈniːðə / (BrE)
used to say that you also do not want or do not do something, agreeing with a negative statement someone else made
agreement with a negative statement
informal

Examples

I don't like spinach.Me neither, so I always avoid it at dinner.
She isn't coming to the meeting tonight. Me neither, I have other plans.
'You don't trust him?' 'Me neither, he's been acting strangely lately.'

Synset Examples

You don't want dessert? Me neither.
I won't be attending the party, me neither.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neither do I - also expressing agreement with a negative statement
