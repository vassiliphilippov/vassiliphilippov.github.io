year dot

1. NOUN

/ jɪr dɑt / (AmE), / jɪə dɒt / (BrE)
(British, informal) A time in the distant past; as long ago as anyone can remember.
very long time ago
informal british

Examples

He has lived in this village since the year dot.
These rules have been in place since the year dot and have rarely been changed.
My grandmother has told that story since the year dot.

Synset Examples

he has been a conductor since the year dot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: time immemorial - A time that goes so far back it cannot be remembered
