time of day

1. NOUN

/ taɪm əv deɪ / (AmE), / taɪm əv deɪ / (BrE)
any attention or acknowledgment; usually with a negative to emphasize being ignored.
attention or acknowledgment (usually negative idiom)

Examples

He walked by without giving me the time of day.
She felt hurt that her colleagues didn't even offer her the time of day.
After their argument, he refused to give her the time of day.

Synset Examples

He ignored her and didn't give her the time of day.
