to be the ticket

1. VERB

/ bi ðə ˈtɪkɪt / (AmE), / biː ðə ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
to be the thing that is essential or especially effective for achieving a desired result
to be essential or the key to success

Examples

For anyone aiming to get into graduate school, high test scores are the ticket.
Early reservations are the ticket if you want the best seats at the concert.
If you want to relax on vacation, an all-inclusive package is the ticket.

Synset Examples

To gain experience, internships are the ticket.
If you want success, hard work is the ticket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be the key - to be essential for something
  • Synonym: be the answer - to be the ideal or effective solution
