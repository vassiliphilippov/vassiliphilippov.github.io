mum's the word

1. X

/ mʌmz ðə wɝd / (AmE), / mʌmz ðə wɜːd / (BrE)
used to say that someone should not reveal something secret or confidential; keep quiet about something
keep something secret; say nothing

Examples

If you hear about the surprise party, remember that mum's the word.
We all agreed: mum's the word until the official announcement.
When asked about the new project, she just smiled and said, 'mum's the word.'

Synset Examples

When it comes to surprises, mum's the word.
She promised to keep it a secret; mum's the word.
For now, let's just say mum's the word on this.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to keep quiet - to not speak or reveal information
  • Antonym: to spill the beans - to reveal secret information

Etymology

From 'mum', meaning silence, from Middle English 'mum', imitative of the sound made with closed lips.
