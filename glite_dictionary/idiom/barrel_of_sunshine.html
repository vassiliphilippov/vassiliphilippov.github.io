barrel of sunshine

1. NOUN

/ ˈbærəl əv ˈsʌnˌʃaɪn / (AmE), / ˈbærəl əv ˈsʌnʃaɪn / (BrE)
A person or thing that brings joy, happiness, or warmth to others.
source of joy or cheerfulness (idiom)

Examples

Even on tough days, her presence is a real barrel of sunshine for everyone at the office.
The children's laughter was a barrel of sunshine that filled the whole house with happiness.
After a gloomy week, his cheerful email was a much-needed barrel of sunshine.

Synset Examples

Her smile is like a barrel of sunshine on a cloudy day.
The puppy was a barrel of sunshine, brightening our day.
He brings a barrel of sunshine wherever he goes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ray of sunshine - a person who brings happiness to others
