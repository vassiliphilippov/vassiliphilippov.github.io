too hot to handle

1. ADJ

/ tu hɑt tə ˈhændl̩ / (AmE), / tuː hɒt tə ˈhændl / (BrE)
(of a subject or issue) too controversial, dangerous, or difficult to deal with.
very controversial or difficult to deal with

Examples

The scandal was too hot to handle for the press at that time.
Some political topics are simply too hot to handle in a public debate.
The new report proved too hot to handle and was quickly withdrawn.

Synset Examples

That topic is too hot to handle in this meeting.
