at your fingertips

1. ADV

/ ət jʊr ˈfɪŋɡərˌtɪps / (AmE), / ət jɔː ˈfɪŋɡətɪps / (BrE)
readily available or easily accessible for use or reference
readily available; easy to access

Examples

I always keep important phone numbers at my fingertips in case of emergency.
With the new software, you have a wealth of information at your fingertips.
Doctors now have patients' medical records at their fingertips thanks to digital systems.

Synset Examples

He had the entire world at his fingertips.
The app puts recipes at your fingertips.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handy - conveniently available for use
