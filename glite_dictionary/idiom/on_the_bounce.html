on the bounce

1. ADV

/ ɑn ðə baʊns / (AmE), / ɒn ðə baʊns / (BrE)
immediately after something has bounced or without any delay or preparation
immediately after a bounce; without delay

Examples

She responded on the bounce, impressing everyone with her quick thinking.
The goalkeeper caught the ball on the bounce and quickly passed it down the field.
He took the offer on the bounce, without stopping to think it through.

Synset Examples

He caught the ball on the bounce during the game.
She answered the questions on the bounce with confidence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: off the cuff - without preparation or planning
