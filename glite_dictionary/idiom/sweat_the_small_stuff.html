to sweat the small stuff

1. VERB

/ swɛt ðə smɔl stʌf / (AmE), / swɛt ðə smɔːl stʌf / (BrE)
to worry needlessly about unimportant or minor issues
worry about trivial matters

Examples

You shouldn't sweat the small stuff when preparing for your trip; just enjoy yourself.
My therapist advised me not to sweat the small stuff and to focus on the bigger picture.
It's easy to sweat the small stuff at work, but it's more productive to prioritize major tasks.

Synset Examples

It's pointless to sweat the small stuff in life.
Don't sweat the small stuff; focus on the big picture.
He learned not to sweat the small stuff at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: don't sweat the small stuff - phrase meaning 'do not worry about unimportant things'
