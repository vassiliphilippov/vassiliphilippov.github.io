to look like a million dollars

1. VERB

/ lʊk laɪk ə mɪljən dɑlɚz / (AmE), / lʊk laɪk ə mɪljən dɒləz / (BrE)
to appear extremely attractive, stylish, or impressive
appear very attractive or impressive

Examples

She looked like a million dollars in that elegant black dress at the party.
Even after a long flight, he managed to look like a million dollars.
With her hair and makeup done professionally, she really looked like a million dollars.

Synset Examples

After her makeover, she looked like a million dollars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: look like a million bucks - to appear extremely attractive or impressive (American usage)
