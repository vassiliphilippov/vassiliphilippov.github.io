over the moon

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvər ðə ˈmun / (AmE), / ˌəʊvə ðə ˈmuːn / (BrE)
extremely happy or delighted; elated.
extremely happy; elated

Examples

When my team won the final, I was over the moon and celebrated all night.
She was over the moon when she got the acceptance letter from her dream university.
They were over the moon about the birth of their first child.

Synset Examples

They were over the moon about their engagement.
She was over the moon when she got the job.
He felt over the moon after winning the lottery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecstatic - feeling or expressing overwhelming happiness or joyful excitement
  • Antonym: down in the dumps - feeling sad or depressed
