to each other's face

1. ADV

/ tə iːtʃ ˈʌðərz feɪs / (AmE), / tə iːtʃ ˈʌðəz feɪs / (BrE)
directly and openly in the presence of one another, without avoiding confrontation or speaking behind someone's back
directly and openly to one another

Examples

It's better to say what you think to each other's face than to gossip.
The two colleagues discussed their disagreements to each other's face during the meeting.
I wish people would resolve issues to each other's face instead of online.

Synset Examples

They argued openly to each other's face.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to someone's face - directly in someone's presence; openly
  • Antonym: behind someone's back - without someone's knowledge; secretly or deceptively
