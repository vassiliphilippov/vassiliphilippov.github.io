bored shitless

1. ADJ

/ bɔrd ˈʃɪtləs / (AmE), / bɔːd ˈʃɪtləs / (BrE)
Extremely bored; experiencing a very high degree of boredom.
extremely bored (vulgar, idiomatic)
informal vulgar

Examples

I was bored shitless during the endless meeting.
She gets bored shitless whenever her brother talks about cars.
They were bored shitless waiting for the train to arrive.

Synset Examples

I was bored shitless at the family reunion.
They were bored shitless waiting for the movie.
She complained of being bored shitless during the flight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bored stiff - extremely bored (informal idiom)
