at heart

1. ADV

/ æt hɑrt / (AmE), / æt hɑːt / (BrE)
used to say what someone is really like despite how they may appear or behave
in reality; in one's true nature

Examples

She may seem strict, but she is very kind at heart.
Although he acts tough, he's a gentle person at heart.
The company is conservative at heart, despite its modern image.

Synset Examples

she is very kind at heart

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deep down - used to talk about someone's real feelings or nature, beneath the surface
