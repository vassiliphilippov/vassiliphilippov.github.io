in an uproar

1. ADV

/ ɪn ən ˈʌpˌrɔr / (AmE), / ɪn ən ˈʌp.rɔː / (BrE)
in a state of turmoil, confusion, or disturbance; highly agitated or excited
in a state of turmoil or excitement

Examples

The entire office was in an uproar after the surprise announcement.
Fans were in an uproar when the concert was unexpectedly canceled.
The city was in an uproar following the controversial court decision.
