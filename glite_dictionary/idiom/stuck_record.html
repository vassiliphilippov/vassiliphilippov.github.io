stuck record

1. NOUN

/ stʌk ˈrɛkərd / (AmE), / stʌk ˈrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
a person who keeps saying the same thing again and again, especially in a way that becomes tiresome or annoying
person who keeps repeating themselves

Examples

I don’t want to sound like a stuck record, but we really do need to save more money.
He complained about the same issue all week, like a stuck record.
Whenever I ask her about her job, she goes on like a stuck record about her boss.

Synset Examples

She keeps playing that stuck record about her ex.
He sounds like a stuck record repeating the same complaint.
Whenever they argue, it’s like a stuck record.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broken record - someone who keeps saying the same thing

Etymology

Refers to a vinyl record that skips and repeats a section of music due to a physical fault.
