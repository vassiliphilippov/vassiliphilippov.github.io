like jagweed

1. ADV

/ laɪk ˈdʒæɡˌwid / (AmE), / laɪk ˈdʒæɡˌwiːd / (BrE)
In a rude or dismissive manner; to treat someone with disdain or disrespect.
treat in a rude, dismissive way
slang

Examples

She glared at him and spoke to him like jagweed all night.
If you treat your teammates like jagweed, don't expect them to support you.
They dismissed my suggestions like jagweed, making me feel unwelcome.

Synset Examples

He treated me like jagweed during the whole meeting.
