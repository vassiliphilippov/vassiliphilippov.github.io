at the feet of the guru

1. X

/ ət ðə fit əv ðə ɡuˈru / (AmE), / ət ðə fiːt əv ðə ˈɡʊəruː / (BrE)
in a position of learning from or being taught by a wise or spiritual teacher
learning from a wise teacher

Examples

The young monk spent years at the feet of the guru, absorbing his wisdom.
Many writers have learned their craft at the feet of the guru before developing their own style.
She considered herself blessed to study at the feet of the guru during her travels in India.

Synset Examples

The students gathered at the feet of the guru.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disciple - a follower or student of a teacher, especially a religious one
  • Synonym: guru - a spiritual teacher, especially in Indian religions

Etymology

From Indian traditions where pupils receive teachings while seated at the feet of their guru.
