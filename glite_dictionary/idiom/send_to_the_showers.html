to send to the showers

1. VERB

/ sɛnd tə ðə ˈʃaʊɚz / (AmE), / sɛnd tə ðə ˈʃaʊəz / (BrE)
(of a player) to dismiss someone from a game, usually because of a violation or mistake, so they must leave the field or court and cannot continue playing.
be dismissed from a game

Examples

The coach decided to send to the showers the player who broke the rules.
After a heated argument with the referee, he was sent to the showers.
One more foul and you’ll be sent to the showers like the others.

Synset Examples

The coach sent him to the showers after the foul.
After that mistake, the referee sent him to the showers.
He was sent to the showers for unsportsmanlike conduct.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to eject - to force a player to leave a game, usually for breaking rules
  • Synonym: to disqualify - to officially prevent someone from continuing in a competition or game
