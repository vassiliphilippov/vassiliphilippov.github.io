with a vengeance

1. ADV

/ wɪð ə vənˈdʒɛns / (AmE), / wɪð ə vənˈdʒəns / (BrE)
Used to indicate that something is done with great intensity, force, or vigor.
with great intensity or force

Examples

After her recovery, she began practicing with a vengeance to make up for lost time.
The weeds grew with a vengeance after the heavy rains.
He cleaned the garage with a vengeance, determined to organize everything.

Synset Examples

She returned to training with a vengeance after her injury.
The storm hit the coast with a vengeance last night.
After the breakup, he worked out with a vengeance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intensely - with great force or strength
