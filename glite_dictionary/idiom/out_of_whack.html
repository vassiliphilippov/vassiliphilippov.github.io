out of whack

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv wæk / (AmE), / aʊt əv wæk / (BrE)
not working properly; not in correct adjustment, order, or condition
not working properly; out of adjustment
informal

Examples

The heating system is out of whack again, so the house is freezing.
After the software update, the settings seemed out of whack.
His sleep schedule has been out of whack since he started the night shift.

Synset Examples

the front wheel of my bicycle is out of whack

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of order - not functioning correctly
  • Synonym: out of kilter - not in proper condition or alignment
  • Synonym: out of tune - not correctly adjusted; not harmonious
