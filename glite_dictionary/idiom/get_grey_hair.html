to get grey hair

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ɡreɪ hɛr / (AmE), / ɡɛt ɡreɪ heə / (BrE)
to develop gray hair, often as a result of aging or stress
develop gray hair due to age or stress

Examples

If you keep worrying about your job, you’ll get grey hair before you know it.
Many parents say their teenagers make them get grey hair early.
She started to get grey hair after the stressful year she had.

Synset Examples

You can get grey hair from too much caffeine.
