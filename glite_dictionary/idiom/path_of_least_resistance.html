path of least resistance

1. NOUN

/ pæθ əv list rəˈzɪstəns / (AmE), / pɑːθ əv liːst rɪˈzɪstəns / (BrE)
the easiest or simplest course of action, especially one that avoids difficulty or confrontation
the easiest course of action

Examples

When faced with a challenge, he often chooses the path of least resistance rather than confronting his problems.
Many people follow the path of least resistance at work to avoid conflict with colleagues.
She realized that the path of least resistance would not help her achieve her long-term goals.

Synset Examples

In marrying him she simply took the path of least resistance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: easy way out - an action or solution that is easiest but avoids effort
  • Antonym: hard way - a more difficult but sometimes more rewarding course of action
