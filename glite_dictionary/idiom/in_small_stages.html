in small stages

1. ADV

/ ɪn smɔl ˈsteɪdʒɪz / (AmE), / ɪn smɔːl ˈsteɪdʒɪz / (BrE)
by proceeding gradually, taking one step at a time rather than attempting everything at once
doing something step by step

Examples

She learned the language in small stages, practicing a little each day.
The project progressed in small stages rather than with major leaps.
If we tackle this task in small stages, it won't feel so overwhelming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gradually - in a slow and steady manner over time
  • Antonym: all at once - happening completely at one time, not gradually
