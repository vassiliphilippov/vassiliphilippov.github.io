to rearrange someone's face

1. VERB

/ ˌriːəˈreɪndʒ ˈsʌmwʌnz feɪs / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈreɪndʒ ˈsʌmwənz feɪs / (BrE)
to physically harm someone by punching or striking them in the face, so as to alter their appearance
to punch and injure someone's face
informal

Examples

If you insult him again, he might just rearrange your face.
She threatened to rearrange his face if he didn't leave her alone.
After the brutal match, it looked like someone had tried to rearrange his face.

Synset Examples

After the bar fight, he looked like someone had tried to rearrange his face.
