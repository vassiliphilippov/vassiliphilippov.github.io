when they go low, we go high

1. X

/ wɛn ðeɪ ɡoʊ loʊ wi ɡoʊ haɪ / (AmE), / wɛn ðeɪ ɡəʊ ləʊ wiː ɡəʊ haɪ / (BrE)
used to advise responding to unethical or negative behavior with dignity and moral integrity rather than retaliation
responding to negativity with integrity

Examples

Whenever she faced personal attacks during the campaign, she would say, "When they go low, we go high."
It isn't always easy to remember when they go low, we go high in heated debates.
At work, his motto was: when they go low, we go high, no matter the provocation.

Synset Examples

In politics, she believes that when they go low, we go high.
In arguments, remember: when they go low, we go high.

Etymology

Popularized by Michelle Obama during the 2016 US presidential campaign.
