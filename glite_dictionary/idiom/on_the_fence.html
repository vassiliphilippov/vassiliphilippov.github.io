on the fence

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə fɛns / (AmE), / ɒn ðə fɛns / (BrE)
undecided or not committed to a particular course of action or side in a dispute
undecided; not taking sides

Examples

After hours of discussion, she was still on the fence about whether to accept the job offer.
Many voters remain on the fence, waiting for more information before making a decision.
I was on the fence until I saw the final presentation.

Synset Examples

some who had been on the fence came out in favor of the plan
too many voters still declare they are undecided

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undecided - not having made a decision yet
  • Antonym: firm - resolute or determined in decision or action

Etymology

From the image of sitting literally on a fence, hesitating which way to go.
