where we came in

1. X

/ wɛr wi keɪm ɪn / (AmE), / wɛə wi keɪm ɪn / (BrE)
the point at which a situation returns to a previous or earlier state, especially after a cycle or repetition
return to an earlier point in a situation

Examples

After hours of discussion, we ended up where we came in, with no decision made.
It seems we've gone in circles and are back to where we came in.
By the end of the movie, the story brought us right back to where we came in.

Synset Examples

After all that, this is where we came in.
