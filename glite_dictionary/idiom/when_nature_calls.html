when nature calls

1. X

/ wɛn ˈneɪtʃɚ kɔlz / (AmE), / wɛn ˈneɪtʃə kɔːlz / (BrE)
used as a polite or humorous way to say that someone needs to use the bathroom
euphemism for needing the bathroom

Examples

Excuse me for a moment, when nature calls, you have to listen.
Even during a long meeting, she had to slip out when nature calls.
Traveling with kids can be tricky because you never know when nature calls.

Synset Examples

When nature calls, there's no ignoring it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: answer the call of nature - use the bathroom; respond to the urge to urinate or defecate
