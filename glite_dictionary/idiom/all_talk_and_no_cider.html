all talk and no cider

1. X

/ ɔl tɑk ənd noʊ ˈsaɪdər / (AmE), / ɔːl tɔːk ənd nəʊ ˈsaɪdə / (BrE)
said of someone who talks about doing things or makes promises, but never follows through with action
makes promises but doesn't act

Examples

People say the mayor will fix things, but he's all talk and no cider.
After months of promising change, the manager proved to be all talk and no cider.
She warned me that her ex-boyfriend was all talk and no cider about making plans.

Synset Examples

She realized he was all talk and no cider.
He's all talk and no cider when it comes to helping.
Their promises are all talk and no cider.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all talk and no action - talks a lot but never does what they say
