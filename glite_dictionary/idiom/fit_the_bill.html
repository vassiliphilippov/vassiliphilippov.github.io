to fit the bill

1. VERB

/ fɪt ðə bɪl / (AmE), / fɪt ðə bɪl / (BrE)
to be suitable or satisfactory for a particular purpose or requirement
to be suitable or satisfactory

Examples

This quiet seaside town really fits the bill if you're looking for a peaceful holiday.
We searched for an affordable venue, and this community center fitted the bill perfectly.
If you want a lightweight laptop for travel, this model fits the bill nicely.

Synset Examples

Does this restaurant fit the bill for the celebration?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suit - to be appropriate or satisfactory for
  • Synonym: meet the requirements - to satisfy the conditions or criteria needed
