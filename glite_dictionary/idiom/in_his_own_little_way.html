in his own little way

1. X

/ ɪn hɪz oʊn ˈlɪt̬l̩ weɪ / (AmE), / ɪn hɪz əʊn ˈlɪtl weɪ / (BrE)
in a manner that is unique or distinctive to oneself, especially when referring to a modest or understated contribution or action
in a personal, modest manner

Examples

She tries to make a difference in her own little way, volunteering at the library once a week.
He brings joy to the office in his own little way, always sharing a cheerful greeting.
Each child contributed in their own little way to the community project.

Synset Examples

He helps others in his own little way.
He expresses creativity in his own little way.
