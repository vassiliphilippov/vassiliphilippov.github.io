rose-colored glasses

1. NOUN

/ ˌroʊz ˈkʌlɚd ˈɡlæsɪz / (AmE), / ˌrəʊz ˈkʌləd ˈɡlɑːsɪz / (BrE)
an attitude or way of seeing things that is unrealistically positive or optimistic, often ignoring negatives or problems
unrealistically optimistic outlook

Examples

She tends to see her childhood through rose-colored glasses, forgetting the difficult times.
It's unwise to approach every situation with rose-colored glasses because problems can be overlooked.
After the promotion, he started viewing the company with rose-colored glasses.

Synset Examples

Despite the challenges, he wears rose-colored glasses every day.
She tends to wear rose-colored glasses about her relationships.
It's easy to wear rose-colored glasses when reminiscing about the past.
They looked at the future with rose-coloured glasses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimism - hopefulness and confidence about the future or the success of something
  • Antonym: pessimism - a tendency to see the worst aspect of things or believe that the worst will happen
