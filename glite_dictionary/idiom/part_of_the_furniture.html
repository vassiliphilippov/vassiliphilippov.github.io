part of the furniture

1. NOUN

/ pɑrt əv ðə ˈfɜrnɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / pɑːt əv ðə ˈfɜːnɪtʃə / (BrE)
A person or thing that has been in a place or organization for so long as to become a familiar and unremarkable feature.
long-standing, familiar member of a place or group

Examples

After so many years at the company, he feels like part of the furniture.
When I returned after my leave, everyone treated me as if I were just part of the furniture.
Sometimes, long-time waiters in that café are considered part of the furniture by regular customers.

Synset Examples

At this point, he’s just part of the furniture in the office.
After years of working here, I'm part of the furniture.
She felt like part of the furniture at the event.
