at first glance

1. ADV

/ ət ˈfɝst ɡlæns / (AmE), / ət ˈfɜːst ɡlɑːns / (BrE)
when first looking at something, before a more thorough examination; apparently at first
on initial observation

Examples

At first glance, the document appeared genuine, but further analysis revealed several inconsistencies.
She seemed confident at first glance, but was actually quite nervous.
At first glance, his artwork looks abstract, yet up close, detailed figures emerge.

Synset Examples

it was love at first sight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at first sight - on initial view or impression

2. ADV

/ æt ˈfɝst ɡlæns / (AmE), / æt ˈfɜːst ɡlɑːns / (BrE)
when something is considered based only on an initial, superficial impression, before a deeper examination reveals otherwise
based on initial impression

Examples

At first glance, the painting appears simple, but it is actually full of hidden details.
At first glance, the problem seemed easy to solve, yet it turned out to be quite complex.
The proposal looked perfect at first glance, but several issues emerged upon closer inspection.
