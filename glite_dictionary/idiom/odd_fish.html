odd fish

1. NOUN

/ ɑd fɪʃ / (AmE), / ɒd fɪʃ / (BrE)
a person who is considered eccentric, unusual, or strange
eccentric or unusual person

Examples

He was always considered an odd fish by his colleagues, never quite fitting in with the rest.
She's a bit of an odd fish—collecting spoons from every country she visits.
At school, being an odd fish made it hard for him to make friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - a person with unconventional or odd behaviour
  • Synonym: weirdo - a strange or eccentric person
