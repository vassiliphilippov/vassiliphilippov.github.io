of the deepest dye

1. ADJ

/ əv ðə ˈdiːpəst daɪ / (AmE), / ɒv ðə ˈdiːpɪst daɪ / (BrE)
of the uttermost severity, intensity, or extremeness in character or quality
of the highest intensity or extremity

Examples

Many saw the dictator as a villain of the deepest dye, capable of any crime.
It was a scheme of the deepest dye, shocking everyone who heard about it.
She was a romantic of the deepest dye, never giving up hope of true love.

Synset Examples

Her betrayal proved she was of the deepest dye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: of the worst kind - of the lowest or most negative type
  • Synonym: of the highest degree - of the utmost extent or intensity

Etymology

From the metaphor of dyeing, where the deepest dye produces the most intense color, hence the greatest degree of a quality.
