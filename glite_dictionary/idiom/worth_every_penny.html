worth every penny

1. ADJ

/ wɝθ ˈɛvri ˈpɛni / (AmE), / wɜːθ ˈɛvri ˈpɛni / (BrE)
completely worth the amount of money paid; fully deserving its cost
completely worth the price

Examples

Her new laptop was worth every penny because it made her work so much easier.
The vacation was expensive, but it was worth every penny for the experience.
He said the museum membership was worth every penny considering how often he visits.

Synset Examples

The meal was delicious and worth every penny.
That concert was worth every penny I spent.
Her advice was worth every penny I paid for it.
Hiring a dustperson was worth every penny.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worth it - deserving the value or effort required
