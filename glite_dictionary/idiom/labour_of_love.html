labour of love

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪ.bɚ əv ˈlʌv / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bə əv ˈlʌv / (BrE)
work that is done for pleasure or out of passion, not for payment or material reward
work done for pleasure, not pay

Examples

Renovating the old house was a true labour of love for Sarah and her family.
He writes his blog as a labour of love, never expecting any money from it.
Creating the community garden became a labour of love for the local residents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volunteer work - work done freely and without pay
