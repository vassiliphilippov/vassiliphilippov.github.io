on the coattails of

1. ADP

/ ɑn ðə ˈkoʊtˌteɪlz ʌv / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ˈkəʊtteɪlz ɒv / (BrE)
Benefiting from the success or influence of another person, often without making much effort oneself.
benefiting from another's success

Examples

Many young politicians have found success on the coattails of experienced leaders.
She was promoted on the coattails of her manager’s achievements rather than her own merit.
Several startups rose to prominence on the coattails of the tech boom.

Synset Examples

He's always been on the coattail of his famous brother.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ride someone's coattails - benefit from another's success
