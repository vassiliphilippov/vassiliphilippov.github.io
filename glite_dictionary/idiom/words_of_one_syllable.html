words of one syllable

1. NOUN

/ wɝdz əv wʌn ˈsɪləbl̩ / (AmE), / wɜːdz əv wʌn ˈsɪləbəl / (BrE)
(of language or an explanation) expressed in very simple, easy-to-understand terms.
very simple and clear language

Examples

The instructions were written in words of one syllable to ensure everyone could understand them.
He asked her to explain the contract in words of one syllable.
Whenever I am confused, I appreciate it when someone uses words of one syllable to help me.

Synset Examples

He spoke in words of one syllable for clarity.

The teacher explained it in words of one syllable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plain English - language that is clear and easy to understand
