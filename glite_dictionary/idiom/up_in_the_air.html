up in the air

1. ADJ

/ ʌp ɪn ði ɛr / (AmE), / ʌp ɪn ðiː eə / (BrE)
not yet decided or settled; very uncertain
not yet decided; uncertain

Examples

The date of the meeting is still up in the air due to scheduling conflicts.
Everything was up in the air until we received confirmation from our partners.
Plans for the new park remain up in the air as the funding has not been approved.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undecided - not having made a decision
  • Antonym: settled - resolved or decided
