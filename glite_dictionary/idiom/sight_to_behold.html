sight to behold

1. NOUN

/ saɪt tə bɪˈhoʊld / (AmE), / saɪt tə bɪˈhəʊld / (BrE)
a person, place, or thing that is especially impressive, remarkable, or worth seeing, usually because of its appearance
something visually impressive or remarkable

Examples

The mountain view at sunrise was a sight to behold.
Her wedding dress was truly a sight to behold as she walked down the aisle.
After the storm, the rainbow over the city was a sight to behold.

Synset Examples

The parade for Merdeka was a sight to behold.
The lush garden was a sight to behold.
The northern lights are a sight to behold.
He was a sight to behold in his new suit.
Her artwork is truly a sight to behold.
The parade was a colorful sight to behold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spectacle - an impressive or eye-catching display
