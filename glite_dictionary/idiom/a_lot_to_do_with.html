a lot to do with

1. X

/ ə ˈlɑt tə du wɪð / (AmE), / ə ˈlɒt tə duː wɪð / (BrE)
having a significant connection or influence; closely related to or caused by something
significant connection or influence

Examples

His success has a lot to do with his determination and persistence.
The team's victory had a lot to do with their excellent coach.
Your health has a lot to do with your daily habits and diet.

Synset Examples

Her success has a lot to do with hard work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: related to - connected or linked with something
