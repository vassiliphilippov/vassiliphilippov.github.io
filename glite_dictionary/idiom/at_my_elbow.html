at my elbow

1. ADV

/ ət maɪ ˈɛlboʊ / (AmE), / ət maɪ ˈɛlbəʊ / (BrE)
very close by; within easy reach or immediate assistance, either physically or figuratively.
very close by; nearby (person)

Examples

Anna was always at my elbow to offer advice whenever I needed it.
During the meeting, his assistant remained at my elbow, ready to help.
Whenever I felt lost, someone was inevitably at my elbow guiding me forward.

Synset Examples

He leaned in close, speaking softly at my elbow.
He was always there at my elbow during tough times.
She stood at my elbow, providing quiet support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: by my side - close by; next to me
  • Synonym: at hand - nearby; easily accessible
