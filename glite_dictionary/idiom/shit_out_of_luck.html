shit out of luck

1. ADJ

/ ʃɪt aʊt əv lʌk / (AmE), / ʃɪt aʊt əv lʌk / (BrE)
having no options left; experiencing bad luck or being in an unfortunate situation
having no options; very unlucky
informal vulgar

Examples

If you don't have a ticket, you're shit out of luck and can't get in.
She realized she was shit out of luck when her phone died miles from help.
After the shop closed early, he was shit out of luck for getting groceries.

Synset Examples

If you miss the deadline, you're shit out of luck.
When the bus broke down, I was shit out of luck.
He forgot his wallet and was shit out of luck at the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of luck - having no luck; unsuccessful or without options
