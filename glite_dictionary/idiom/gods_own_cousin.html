God's own cousin

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑdz oʊn ˈkʌzən / (AmE), / ɡɒdz əʊn ˈkʌzən / (BrE)
A person who acts with excessive self-importance or behaves as if they are superior to everyone else.
someone behaving as if greatly superior
informal

Examples

After his promotion, he strutted around the office as if he were God's own cousin.
You don't have to act like God's own cousin just because you passed the exam.
She rolled her eyes at his God's own cousin attitude and walked away.

Synset Examples

He acts like he thinks he's God's own cousin.
She walks around as if she thinks she's God's own cousin.
I can't stand him; he truly thinks he's God's own cousin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holier-than-thou - self-righteously superior or morally smug
  • Synonym: arrogant - having or revealing an exaggerated sense of one's own importance
