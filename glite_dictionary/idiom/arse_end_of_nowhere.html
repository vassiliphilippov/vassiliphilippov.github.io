arse end of nowhere

1. NOUN

/ ɑːrs end əv ˈnoʊwɛr / (AmE), / ɑːs end əv ˈnəʊweə / (BrE)
a very remote or isolated place; an extremely out-of-the-way location
very remote place (British informal idiom)
informal british

Examples

He grew up in the arse end of nowhere and rarely visited a city.
Getting an internet connection at the arse end of nowhere can be a real challenge.
They thought the cabin was at the arse end of nowhere, but it turned out to be perfect for a quiet weekend.

Synset Examples

They live in the arse end of nowhere.
We drove to the arse end of nowhere for a vacation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: middle of nowhere - an extremely remote or isolated place (idiom)
