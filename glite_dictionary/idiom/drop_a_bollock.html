to drop a bollock

1. VERB

/ drɑp ə ˈbɑlək / (AmE), / drɒp ə ˈbɒlək / (BrE)
to make a serious or embarrassing mistake, often due to carelessness
make a serious mistake (Brit. informal)
informal slang british vulgar

Examples

I really dropped a bollock on that presentation and forgot my notes.
She realized too late that she'd dropped a bollock by missing the deadline.
He can't believe he dropped a bollock in such an important meeting.

Synset Examples

I really dropped a bollock on that presentation.
He couldn't believe he dropped a bollock in front of the boss.
She dropped a bollock by forgetting the deadline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to make a blunder - to make a careless or stupid mistake
  • Synonym: to mess up - to perform poorly or make a mistake

Etymology

British slang; 'bollock' is a vulgar term for testicle in British English, often used for emphasis in idioms.
