in a jolly mood

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ə ˈdʒɑli mud / (AmE), / ɪn ə ˈdʒɒli muːd / (BrE)
feeling cheerful and happy; in good spirits
cheerful or happy mood

Examples

After the party, everyone was in a jolly mood and laughed all the way home.
He is always in a jolly mood during the holidays, spreading joy to those around him.
The sunny weather put her in a jolly mood for the entire afternoon.

Synset Examples

She was in a jolly mood after receiving good news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheerful - feeling or showing happiness and optimism
  • Antonym: downcast - feeling sad or dispirited
