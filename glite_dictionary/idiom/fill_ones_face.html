to fill one's face

1. VERB

/ fɪl wʌnz feɪs / (AmE), / fɪl wʌnz feɪs / (BrE)
to eat greedily or excessively, especially in a way that suggests a lack of restraint
eat greedily or excessively
informal

Examples

After the marathon, she was starving and couldn't wait to fill her face at the nearest diner.
Everyone at the party filled their faces with pizza and cake.
Don't fill your face before dinner or you'll spoil your appetite.

Synset Examples

At the buffet, he couldn't help but fill his face with all the desserts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pig out - eat a lot or eat greedily
  • Synonym: binge - eat or drink excessively in a short period
