to stink to high heaven

1. VERB

/ stɪŋk tə haɪ ˈhɛv.ən / (AmE), / stɪŋk tə haɪ ˈhɛv.ən / (BrE)
to have an extremely strong and unpleasant smell; to be extremely objectionable or offensive
smell extremely bad or be highly objectionable

Examples

His shoes stink to high heaven after a long day of hiking.
The garbage in the alley stinks to high heaven during the summer.
This room stinks to high heaven; we need to open the windows immediately.

Synset Examples

The old socks stink to high heaven.
Her cooking stinks to high heaven.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reek - to smell very strongly and unpleasantly
