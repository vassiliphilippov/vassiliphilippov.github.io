silly me

1. INTJ

/ ˈsɪli mi / (AmE), / ˈsɪli miː / (BrE)
used to express mild embarrassment or self-deprecation after making a mistake or oversight
expression of mild self-deprecation or embarrassment

Examples

Silly me, I completely forgot to send the email before leaving work.
I brought an umbrella on a sunny day—silly me.
Silly me, I locked my keys in the car again.
