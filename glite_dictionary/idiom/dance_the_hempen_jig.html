to dance the hempen jig

1. VERB

/ dæns ðə ˈhɛmpən dʒɪɡ / (AmE), / dɑːns ðə ˈhɛmpən dʒɪɡ / (BrE)
(slang, archaic) To be hanged by the neck until dead; to undergo execution by hanging.
to be hanged (archaic slang idiom)
slang archaic

Examples

The condemned man bravely declared he was ready to dance the hempen jig at dawn.
Many a pirate feared the day he might dance the hempen jig before a jeering crowd.
He joked darkly about dancing the hempen jig if he ever got caught.

Synset Examples

The old pirate claimed he would dance the hempen jig soon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hang - to kill someone by tying a rope around their neck and removing support from beneath them

Etymology

From 'hempen' (made of hemp, the material used for rope), and 'jig' (a lively dance), used figuratively since the 18th century for execution by hanging.
