filled with crap

1. ADJ

/ fɪld wɪθ kræp / (AmE), / fɪld wɪð kræp / (BrE)
containing a large amount of nonsense or worthless material; not credible or of poor quality
full of nonsense or worthless material
informal vulgar

Examples

The article was filled with crap and offered no useful information.
Don't waste your time on that movie; it's just filled with crap.
Her explanation was so filled with crap that nobody believed her.

Synset Examples

This report is filled with crap and needs revision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: full of nonsense - containing nothing of value or sense
  • Synonym: filled with rubbish - containing a lot of junk or nonsense
