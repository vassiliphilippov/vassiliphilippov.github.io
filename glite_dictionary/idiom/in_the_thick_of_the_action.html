in the thick of the action

1. ADP

/ ɪn ðə ˈθɪk əv ði ˈækʃən / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈθɪk əv ði ˈækʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
actively involved at the most intense or central part of an activity or event
actively involved at the center of activity

Examples

He always manages to be in the thick of the action during important meetings.
The reporters rushed to get in the thick of the action at the protest.
As a firefighter, she’s often in the thick of the action when emergencies happen.

Synset Examples

She was in the thick of the action during the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the thick of things - actively involved in the most important or busy part of something
