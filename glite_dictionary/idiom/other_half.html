other half

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌðɚ hæf / (AmE), / ˈʌðə hɑːf / (BrE)
a person's spouse, romantic partner, or significant other, often implying a close and complementary relationship.
a person's romantic partner or spouse
informal

Examples

He always saves a seat for his other half at family gatherings.
Julia and her other half are planning a vacation together.
Whenever my other half is away, I miss her terribly.

Synset Examples

She’s my other half and we complete each other.
My other half loves to cook on weekends.
He bought a gift for his other half on their anniversary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: better half - a more affectionate way to refer to one's spouse or partner

Etymology

Refers to the idea of two people making a whole, as in 'my better half.'
