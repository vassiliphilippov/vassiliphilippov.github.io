to go to Nopeville

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ tuː ˈnoʊpvɪl / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tuː ˈnəʊpvɪl / (BrE)
to head towards failure or a situation with no positive outcome
move toward failure or a dead end
informal slang

If we ignore these warning signs, the whole plan will go to Nopeville in no time.

Without more support, this startup could go to Nopeville by the end of the year.
