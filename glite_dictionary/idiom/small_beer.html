small beer

1. NOUN

/ smɔl bɪr / (AmE), / smɔːl bɪə / (BrE)
Something that is considered unimportant or trivial.
something unimportant or trivial

Examples

The money we spent on advertising was small beer compared to our overall profits.
Many see these technical issues as small beer in light of the project's success.
To her, winning the local competition was small beer after her achievements on the national stage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triviality - something of little importance
  • Antonym: big deal - something of great importance

Etymology

Originally referred to a weak beer of low alcohol content, considered inferior; later used figuratively for anything insignificant.
