to blow the pants off

1. VERB

/ bloʊ ðə pænts ɔf / (AmE), / bləʊ ðə pænts ɒf / (BrE)
to impress someone greatly or to surpass someone or something by a wide margin
impress or surpass greatly
informal

Examples

Her performance blew the pants off the judges and won her first prize.
The new phone’s camera blew the pants off anything else on the market.
His presentation blew the pants off all our expectations.

Synset Examples

She danced so well, it blew the pants off everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blow away - to impress someone greatly
  • Synonym: knock the socks off - to greatly impress someone
