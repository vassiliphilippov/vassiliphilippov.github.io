the ways of the world

1. NOUN

/ ðə weɪz əv ðə wɜrld / (AmE), / ðə weɪz əv ðə wɜːld / (BrE)
the typical behaviors, customs, and realities of society or human nature, often implying practical worldly wisdom
typical customs and realities of society

Examples

After traveling for years, she became familiar with the ways of the world.
Young people often learn the ways of the world through experience.
He was naïve, unacquainted with the ways of the world.

Synset Examples
