well and truly

1. ADV

/ wɛl ənd ˈtruːli / (AmE), / wɛl ænd ˈtruːli / (BrE)
completely and definitely; thoroughly or fully
completely; thoroughly

Examples

The old bridge was well and truly destroyed by the storm.
By the time we arrived, the meeting had well and truly started.
She was well and truly convinced by his argument.

Synset Examples

He was well and truly ready for the challenge ahead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoroughly - in a very detailed and complete way
  • Antonym: partially - not completely; only to some extent
