for luck

1. ADV

/ fɔr lʌk / (AmE), / fə lʌk / (BrE)
used to describe doing something in the hope that it will bring good fortune or success
in the hope of attracting good fortune

Examples

He carried a rabbit's foot for luck during the exam.
She wore her grandmother's necklace for luck on her wedding day.
They touched the statue for luck before entering the competition.

Synset Examples

She tossed a coin into the fountain for luck.
