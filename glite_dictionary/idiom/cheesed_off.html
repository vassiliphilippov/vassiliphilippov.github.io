cheesed off

1. ADJ

/ tʃizd ɑf / (AmE), / tʃiːzd ɒf / (BrE)
Annoyed or irritated; out of patience.
annoyed or irritated
informal british

Examples

He seemed really cheesed off after hearing the bad news.
I'm getting cheesed off with all these delays at the station.
She was cheesed off by the manager's constant criticism.

Synset Examples

had an exasperated look on his face
felt exasperated beyond endurance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annoyed - slightly angry; irritated
  • Synonym: irritated - feeling slight anger or impatience
  • Antonym: content - in a state of peaceful happiness

Etymology

British slang, probably from earlier army slang; precise origin unknown.
