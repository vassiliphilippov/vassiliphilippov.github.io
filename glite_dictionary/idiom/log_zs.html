1. VERB

/ lɔg ziːz / (AmE), / lɒg ziːz / (BrE)
to be asleep; to sleep
be asleep (idiom, informal)
informal

Examples

He went upstairs early to log Z's before his big meeting.
After a long flight, I just wanted to log Z's for a few hours.
She was so tired last night that she started logging Z's on the couch.

Related Terms

Etymology

From the letter 'Z' representing the sound of snoring in English comics and cartoons.
