meant to be

1. ADJ

/ mɛnt tə bi / (AmE), / mɛnt tə bi / (BrE)
intended or expected to occur or exist, or seen as destined to happen.
intended or destined to happen

Examples

Sometimes, unexpected events are just meant to be.
The project was meant to be completed by Friday, but there were delays.
They believe they were meant to be together forever.

Synset Examples

She was meant to be here by now.
He said it was meant to be a joke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supposed to - expected to happen or required, similar sense of intention or obligation
