all it's cracked up to be

1. X

/ ɔl ɪts krækt ʌp tə bi / (AmE), / ɔːl ɪts krækt ʌp tə bi / (BrE)
as good, impressive, or valuable as people say or expect; living up to its reputation (typically used in negative constructions)
living up to expectations (usually negative)

Examples

The new smartphone wasn't all it's cracked up to be, so I returned it after a week.
That famous vacation spot turned out to be not all it's cracked up to be.
Some critics say the bestselling novel really is all it's cracked up to be.

Synset Examples

The movie wasn't all it's cracked up to be, unfortunately.
The restaurant was not all it's cracked up to be.
I found out the hype was all it's cracked up to be.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disappointing - not as good as expected; causing disappointment
  • Synonym: to live up to - to fulfill expectations or reputation
