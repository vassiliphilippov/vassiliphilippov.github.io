safe and sound

1. ADJ

/ seɪf ənd saʊnd / (AmE), / seɪf ənd saʊnd / (BrE)
Not harmed or injured; free from danger or injury.
free from harm or danger

Examples

We arrived home safe and sound after the long journey through the storm.
After the earthquake, everyone was relieved to find their family safe and sound.
Thankfully, the missing hikers returned safe and sound after a night in the wilderness.

Synset Examples

the children were found safe and sound

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unharmed - not hurt or injured
  • Antonym: endangered - at risk of harm or danger
