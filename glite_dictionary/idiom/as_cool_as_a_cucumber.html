as cool as a cucumber

1. ADJ

/ əz kul əz ə ˈkjuːkəmˌbɚ / (AmE), / əz kuːl əz ə ˈkjuːkəmbə / (BrE)
very calm and showing no signs of nervousness, even in a difficult or stressful situation.
very calm and composed

Examples

Despite the chaos in the office, Marcus stayed as cool as a cucumber and solved the problem swiftly.
She walked up to the stage as cool as a cucumber, showing no trace of nerves.
Even when faced with tough questions, the candidate remained as cool as a cucumber.

Synset Examples

He remained as cool as a cucumber during the interview.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unflappable - not easily upset or perturbed; calm under pressure
  • Antonym: nervous - easily agitated or alarmed; not calm or composed
