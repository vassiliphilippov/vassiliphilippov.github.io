in a league of her own

1. X

/ ɪn ə liɡ əv hɚ oʊn / (AmE), / ɪn ə liːɡ əv həː əʊn / (BrE)
exceptionally good or unique; having no equal in ability, achievement, or quality
exceptionally unique or unmatched

Examples

When it comes to problem solving, she is truly in a league of her own.
The chef's creativity puts him in a league of her own among his peers.
Their designs are in a league of her own, unmatched by any competitor.

Synset Examples

Her talent puts her in a league of her own.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in a class of one's own - having no equal; unique in quality or ability
