in someone's wildest dreams

1. X

/ ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈwaɪldəst driːmz / (AmE), / ɪn ˈsʌmwənz ˈwaɪldɪst driːmz / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is far beyond what one could ever imagine or expect, even in the most optimistic or fanciful thoughts.
beyond what could be imagined

Examples

Not in his wildest dreams did he think he would become a famous actor.
She never believed she would travel the world, not in her wildest dreams.
Winning the lottery was something he dared not hope for in his wildest dreams.

Synset Examples

He couldn't have predicted this success in his wildest dreams.
I never imagined this, not in my wildest dreams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to imagine - form a mental image or concept of something
