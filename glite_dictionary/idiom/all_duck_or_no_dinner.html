all duck or no dinner

1. X

/ ɔl dʌk ɚ noʊ ˈdɪnɚ / (AmE), / ɔːl dʌk ɔː nəʊ ˈdɪnə / (BrE)
(informal) Used to describe a high-stakes situation in which full commitment is required for any chance of success; it's all or nothing.
high-stakes, all-or-nothing situation
informal

Examples

In this competition, it's all duck or no dinner; there's no reward for second place.
He approached the final negotiations with an all duck or no dinner mentality.
When the clock was running out, the coach shouted, "All duck or no dinner! Take the risk!"

Synset Examples

If you want to win, it's all duck or no dinner.
In this game, it's all duck or no dinner!
You have to commit; it's all duck or no dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all or nothing - succeed completely or not at all
