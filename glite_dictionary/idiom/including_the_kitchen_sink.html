including the kitchen sink

1. X

/ ɪnˈkluːdɪŋ ðə ˈkɪtʃən sɪŋk / (AmE), / ɪnˈkluːdɪŋ ðə ˈkɪtʃɪn sɪŋk / (BrE)
used to indicate that virtually everything possible has been included, often unnecessarily or to excess
including everything possible

Examples

The new law includes regulations on nearly everything including the kitchen sink.
She packed for her trip as if she were bringing including the kitchen sink.
Our report covers every possible angle, including the kitchen sink.

Synset Examples

They threw everything including the kitchen sink into the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: everything but the kitchen sink - an idiom meaning almost everything possible
