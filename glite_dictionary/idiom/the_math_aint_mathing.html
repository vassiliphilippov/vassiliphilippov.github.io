the math ain't mathing

1. X

/ ðə mæθ eɪnt ˈmæθɪŋ / (AmE), / ðə mɑːθ eɪnt ˈmæθɪŋ / (BrE)
(informal) Used to say that calculations, reasoning, or a situation do not make sense or add up logically.
something does not add up or make sense
informal slang

Examples

Despite his explanation, the math ain't mathing when it comes to those expenses.
Looking at their timeline, the math ain't mathing—something doesn't fit.
I tried following her reasoning, but honestly, the math ain't mathing at all.

Synset Examples

After reviewing the numbers, I said, 'The math ain't mathing here.'
When I added it up, I realized the math ain't mathing.
Her explanation was confusing; clearly, the math ain't mathing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: not add up - to not make sense logically or mathematically
