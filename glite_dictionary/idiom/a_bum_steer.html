a bum steer

1. NOUN

/ ə bʌm stɪr / (AmE), / ə bʌm stɪə / (BrE)
misleading or incorrect advice or guidance, especially one that causes confusion or leads someone in the wrong direction
misleading advice or guidance
informal

Examples

He got a bum steer from his coworker and ended up at the wrong office.
Sorry if I gave you a bum steer about the schedule earlier.
Her directions were a bum steer, and we missed the turn completely.

Synset Examples

That advice was a bum steer; it led me nowhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad advice - advice that is incorrect or unhelpful
