jackass of all trades

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒækˌæs əv ɔl treɪdz / (AmE), / ˈdʒækˌas əv ɔːl treɪdz / (BrE)
A person who has some skill in many areas but is not an expert in any, often suggesting a lack of real proficiency.
person with superficial skills in many areas
informal

Examples

Ever since college, Mark has been called a jackass of all trades by his friends.
She admits she's a jackass of all trades, dabbling in everything from carpentry to coding but mastering none.
In the workplace, being a jackass of all trades can sometimes be more a curse than a blessing.

Synset Examples

She joked about being a jackass of all trades in her hobbies.
Being a jackass of all trades means never mastering anything.
He calls himself a jackass of all trades, but lacks skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jack of all trades - a person who has a range of skills but is not an expert at any

Etymology

Variant of 'jack of all trades,' with 'jackass' used to imply foolishness or incompetence.
