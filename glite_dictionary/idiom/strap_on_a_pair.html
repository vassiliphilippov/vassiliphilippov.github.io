to strap on a pair

1. VERB

/ stræp ɑn ə pɛr / (AmE), / stræp ɒn ə peə / (BrE)
(informal, slang) To gather courage or become brave, especially when facing a difficult or intimidating situation.
gather courage; be brave (idiom)
informal slang
