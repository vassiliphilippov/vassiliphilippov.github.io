to take the market by storm

1. VERB

/ teɪk ðə ˈmɑːrkɪt baɪ stɔːrm / (AmE), / teɪk ðə ˈmɑːkɪt baɪ stɔːm / (BrE)
to become very successful or popular in a market in a very short time
quickly achieve great market popularity

Examples

The new smartphone took the market by storm, selling out within days of its release.
Her innovative fashion designs took the market by storm and appeared in all the top magazines.
Streaming services have taken the market by storm over the past decade.

Synset Examples

The new game took the market by storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to take by storm - to achieve sudden success or popularity somewhere
