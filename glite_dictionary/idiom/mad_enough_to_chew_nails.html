mad enough to chew nails

1. ADJ

/ mæd ɪˈnʌf tə tʃuː neɪlz / (AmE), / mæd ɪˈnʌf tə tʃuː neɪlz / (BrE)
extremely angry or upset; so angry that one appears capable of something extreme
extremely angry or upset

Examples

When I saw the mess they left, I was mad enough to chew nails.
He was mad enough to chew nails after losing the game in the final seconds.
After the unfair accusation, she looked mad enough to chew nails.

Synset Examples

She looked mad enough to chew nails when she heard the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: furious - very angry
  • Synonym: livid - furiously angry
