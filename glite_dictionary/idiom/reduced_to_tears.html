reduced to tears

1. X

/ rɪˈdjuːst tə tɪrz / (AmE), / rɪˈdjuːst tə tɪəz / (BrE)
caused to cry due to a strong emotional reaction, such as sadness, joy, or frustration
moved to cry by emotion

Examples

During the performance, the actor's powerful monologue reduced to tears many members of the audience.
She was reduced to tears when she heard the devastating news about her old friend.
The teacher's kindness reduced to tears a student who was struggling.

Synset Examples

The movie's ending was so sad it reduced me to tears.
The heartbreaking news reduced him to tears.
Her heartfelt speech at the ceremony reduced everyone to tears.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brought to tears - caused to start crying
