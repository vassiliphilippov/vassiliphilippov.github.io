darn tootin'

1. ADV

/ ˌdɑrn ˈtuːtən / (AmE), / ˌdɑːn ˈtuːtɪn / (BrE)
Used to express strong agreement, emphasis, or certainty; absolutely; definitely.
emphatic expression meaning 'absolutely' or 'definitely'
informal american

Examples

You’re darn tootin' I’m coming to the party tonight!
It’s darn tootin' cold out here in January.
“Are you finished?” “Darn tootin'!”

Synset Examples

He was darn tootin' sure about his decision.
It's a darn tootin' great day for a picnic!
She said it was darn tootin' fun at the fair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absolutely - emphatic expression of agreement or certainty

Etymology

Alteration of 'damn' and 'tooting' (as in tooting one's own horn), American colloquialism.
