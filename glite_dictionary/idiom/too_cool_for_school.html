too cool for school

1. ADJ

/ tuː kuːl fər skuːl / (AmE), / tuː kuːl fə skuːl / (BrE)
extremely confident, stylish, or fashionable, often to the point of appearing aloof or affected
very confident or stylish (idiom)
informal

Examples

He acted too cool for school and barely acknowledged anyone at the party.
Everyone thought she was too cool for school in her designer jacket and sunglasses.
His too cool for school attitude made him seem unapproachable to new classmates.

Synset Examples

She walked in like she was too cool for school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stylish - having a fashionable or elegant appearance
  • Synonym: aloof - not friendly or forthcoming; cool and distant
  • Antonym: modest - not displaying confidence or arrogance
