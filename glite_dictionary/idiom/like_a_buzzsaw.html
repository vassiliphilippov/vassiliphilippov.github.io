like a buzzsaw

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə ˈbʌzˌsɔ / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈbʌzˌsɔː / (BrE)
(of a person or process) with relentless and efficient energy, moving through tasks or obstacles forcefully and without stopping.
relentlessly and efficiently, like a buzzsaw

Examples

She worked like a buzzsaw, finishing each project faster than anyone else.
The new manager cut through bureaucracy like a buzzsaw, streamlining all the operations.
After the break, the team came out like a buzzsaw and quickly took the lead.

Synset Examples

He tackled tasks like a buzzsaw, relentless and efficient.
