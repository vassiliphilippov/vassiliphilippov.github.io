what it takes

1. X

/ wʌt ɪt teɪks / (AmE), / wɒt ɪt teɪks / (BrE)
the necessary qualities, skills, or attributes needed to achieve something or succeed at a task
the needed skills or qualities for success

Examples

She worked hard to prove she had what it takes to become a doctor.
Not everyone has what it takes to run a marathon, both physically and mentally.
Do you believe you have what it takes to lead this team to success?

Synset Examples

Do you think she has what it takes to win?
He has what it takes to be a great leader.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: have what it takes - to possess the necessary qualities, skills, or attributes
