dog's dinner

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡz ˈdɪnɚ / (AmE), / dɒɡz ˈdɪnə / (BrE)
a situation, job, or piece of work that is messy, badly organized, or of poor quality
a mess; a badly done job

Examples

He made a complete dog's dinner of the presentation and forgot his main points.
The redecorated kitchen looks a bit of a dog's dinner after all that painting.
If you try to rush, you'll just turn it into a dog's dinner.

Synset Examples

they made a real dog's breakfast of that job

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog's breakfast - a mess or something done badly
