fucking hell

1. INTJ

/ ˈfʌkɪŋ hɛl / (AmE), / ˈfʌkɪŋ hɛl / (BrE)
Used to express strong feelings of anger, annoyance, shock, or disbelief.
exclamation of annoyance or shock
vulgar

Examples

Fucking hell, why didn't anyone tell me the meeting was today?
Fucking hell! That car nearly hit me.
When he saw the mess in his room, he just shouted, "fucking hell!"

Synset Examples

I forgot my keys again; fucking hell!
Fucking hell, I can't believe we missed the train!

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: bloody hell - exclamation of annoyance or surprise (less vulgar)
