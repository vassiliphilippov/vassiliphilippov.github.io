thorn in someone's side

1. NOUN

/ θɔrn ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz saɪd / (AmE), / θɔːn ɪn ˈsʌmwənz saɪd / (BrE)
a person or thing that repeatedly causes trouble or annoyance for someone
persistent source of annoyance

Examples

The new regulations have become a thorn in the company's side since their introduction.
Her persistent questioning was a thorn in his side throughout the entire meeting.
Repeated technical failures proved to be a thorn in our side during the project.

Synset Examples

The delays became a thorn in the manager's side.
The noisy neighbors became a thorn in their side.
His constant complaints were a thorn in her side.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nuisance - someone or something that causes inconvenience or annoyance
  • Synonym: irritation - something that annoys or bothers you regularly
