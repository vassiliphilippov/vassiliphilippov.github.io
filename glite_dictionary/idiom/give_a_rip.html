to give a rip

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ə rɪp / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə rɪp / (BrE)
(informal) To care at all; to be concerned in the slightest degree (usually used in negative statements).
care at all (usually in the negative)
informal

Examples

I don’t give a rip what other people think of my music.
She never gives a rip about office gossip and stays focused on her work.
Why should we give a rip if they’re late again?

Synset Examples

He didn’t give a rip about the deadline.
I don't give a rip about the results.
She gives a rip only when it affects her directly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: care - to feel concern or interest
  • Synonym: to give a damn - to care at all (informal, often negative)
  • Synonym: to give a hoot - to care at all (informal, often negative)
