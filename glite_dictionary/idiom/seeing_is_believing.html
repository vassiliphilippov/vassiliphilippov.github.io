seeing is believing

1. X

/ ˈsiːɪŋ ɪz bəˈlivɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːɪŋ ɪz bɪˈliːvɪŋ / (BrE)
used to express that only physical or direct evidence is convincing
only direct evidence convinces

Examples

I've heard rumors about the exhibit, but seeing is believing.
She doubted the magician's skills until she saw the trick herself—seeing is believing.
If you don't think the cake is huge, wait until you see it—seeing is believing.

Synset Examples

They insisted the miracle happened; seeing is believing.
I thought the story was fake, but seeing is believing.
He showed me the results; seeing is believing.
