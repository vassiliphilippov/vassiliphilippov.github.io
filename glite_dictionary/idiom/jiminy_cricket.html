Jiminy Cricket

1. INTJ

/ ˈdʒɪmɪni ˈkrɪkət / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪmɪni ˈkrɪkɪt / (BrE)
used as a mild exclamation of surprise or annoyance.
exclamation of surprise or annoyance
informal archaic

Examples

Jiminy Cricket, it’s cold outside tonight!
Jiminy Cricket, I can’t believe how fast that was!
Jiminy Cricket, don’t sneak up on me like that!

Synset Examples

Jiminy Cricket, she really surprised us with that news!
Jiminy Cricket, I forgot my keys again!

Etymology

Alteration of 'Jesus Christ', used as a euphemism.
