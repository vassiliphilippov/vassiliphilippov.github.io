to live rent-free in someone's head

1. VERB

/ lɪv rɛnt ˈfri ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / lɪv rɛnt ˈfriː ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (BrE)
to dominate someone's thoughts or attention persistently, often in an unwanted or involuntary way
dominate someone's thoughts persistently
informal

Examples

That embarrassing moment seemed to live rent-free in his head for years after it happened.
The losing team's victory celebration lives rent-free in their heads to this day.
She was annoyed that her ex-boyfriend was living rent-free in her head, even months after the breakup.

Synset Examples

He realized those past mistakes no longer live rent-free in his head.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to preoccupy - to dominate the mind or thoughts of someone
  • Synonym: to obsess - to fill the mind of someone continually
