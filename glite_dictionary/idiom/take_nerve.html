to take nerve

1. VERB

/ teɪk nɜrv / (AmE), / teɪk nɜːv / (BrE)
to require courage or bravery; to demand boldness or audacity.
require courage or bravery

Examples

It really takes nerve to quit a secure job and start your own business.
It takes nerve to stand up to your boss when you think they're wrong.
Going skydiving for the first time definitely takes nerve.

Synset Examples

It takes nerve to speak in front of a crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: take guts - require courage or bravery
  • Synonym: take courage - require courage
  • Antonym: timid - lacking courage or bravery
