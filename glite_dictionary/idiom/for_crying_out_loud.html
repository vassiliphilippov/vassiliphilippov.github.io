for crying out loud

1. INTJ

/ fər ˈkraɪɪŋ aʊt laʊd / (AmE), / fə ˈkraɪɪŋ aʊt laʊd / (BrE)
used to express strong annoyance, frustration, or impatience
expression of annoyance or frustration
informal

Examples

For crying out loud, could you please turn down that music?
I can't find my keys, for crying out loud!
For crying out loud, stop arguing and help me clean up.

Synset Examples

Stop yelling for crying out loud, it's annoying.
