motion of my ocean

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊʃən əv maɪ ˈoʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈməʊʃən ɒv maɪ ˈəʊʃən / (BrE)
A poetic metaphor for an emotional response or the stirring of deep feelings within a person.
poetic metaphor for deep feelings

Examples

His poetry awakened the motion of my ocean and left me speechless.
Whenever she speaks, it's like she stirs the motion of my ocean.
The beauty of the sunrise set in motion the motion of my ocean within.
