the plural of anecdote is not data

1. X

/ ðə ˈplʊrəl əv ˈænɪkˌdoʊt ɪz nɑt ˈdeɪtə / (AmE), / ðə ˈplʊərəl əv ˈænɪkdəʊt ɪz nɒt ˈdeɪtə / (BrE)
an expression used to emphasize that multiple personal stories or experiences do not constitute reliable scientific evidence
expression urging evidence over personal stories

Examples

Many people believe home remedies work, but the plural of anecdote is not data.
In medical research, experts remind us that the plural of anecdote is not data.
When arguing their case, she cautioned her team that the plural of anecdote is not data.

Synset Examples

Just because it happened to you, the plural of anecdote is not data.
In science, the plural of anecdote is not data.
Remember, the plural of anecdote is not data in research.
