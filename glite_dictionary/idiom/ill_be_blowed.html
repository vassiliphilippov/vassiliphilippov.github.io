I'll be blowed

1. INTJ

/ aɪl bi bloʊd / (AmE), / aɪl bi bləʊd / (BrE)
an informal expression used to indicate surprise or disbelief
expression of surprise or disbelief
informal british

I'll be blowed, you've finished the work already!
