to operate on Tongan time

1. VERB

/ ˈɑːpəˌreɪt ɑn ˈtɔŋən taɪm / (AmE), / ˈɒpəˌreɪt ɒn ˈtɒŋən taɪm / (BrE)
to follow a relaxed or unhurried schedule, often disregarding strict punctuality
follow a relaxed schedule
informal

Examples

At the beach party, everyone seemed to operate on Tongan time and arrived whenever they liked.
If you want to operate on Tongan time, don't stress about being a few minutes late.
Our hosts love to operate on Tongan time, so dinner didn't start until an hour after we'd arrived.

Synset Examples

They always operate on Tongan time at family gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: run on island time - to operate according to a relaxed or flexible schedule

Etymology

Refers to the perceived relaxed attitude towards timekeeping in Tongan culture.
