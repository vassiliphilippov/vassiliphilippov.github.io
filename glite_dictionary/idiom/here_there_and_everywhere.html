here, there and everywhere

1. ADV

/ hɪr ðɛr ənd ˈɛvriˌwɛr / (AmE), / hɪə ðeə ənd ˈɛvrɪwɛə / (BrE)
in many different places or situations; everywhere
in many places or everywhere

Examples

The children were here, there and everywhere, making it hard to keep track of them.
He searched here, there and everywhere for his missing wallet but couldn't find it.
During the festival, volunteers were here, there and everywhere helping out with various tasks.
