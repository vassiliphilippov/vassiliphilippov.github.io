and how

1. ADV

/ ænd haʊ / (AmE), / ænd haʊ / (BrE)
used to express strong or emphatic agreement with what has just been said.
expression of strong agreement
informal

Examples

“That was an amazing concert!” “And how!”
If you say running a marathon is tiring, and how!
“Was the movie funny?” “And how—I laughed the whole time!”

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absolutely - used to show strong agreement
  • Synonym: you bet - informal phrase expressing agreement or affirmation
