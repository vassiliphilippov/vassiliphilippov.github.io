to keep a tab open

1. VERB

/ kip ə tæb ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / kiːp ə tæb ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
to maintain continuous access to a web page or to remain persistently aware of something, as by keeping information readily available
maintain access to a webpage or information

Examples

I always keep a tab open for important news updates throughout the day.
She likes to keep a tab open with her calendar so she doesn't miss any appointments.
Many people keep a tab open for their email to respond quickly to messages.

Synset Examples

I always keep a tab open for my favorite sites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to keep an eye on - to watch or monitor closely
