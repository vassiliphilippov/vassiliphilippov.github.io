blue funk

1. NOUN

/ bluː fʌŋk / (AmE), / bluː fʌŋk / (BrE)
A state of depression or deep sadness.
state of deep sadness or depression

Examples

After hearing the bad news, he sank into a blue funk for days.
A blue funk sometimes hits her when the weather turns cold and gloomy.
Don't let a blue funk keep you from enjoying your life.
