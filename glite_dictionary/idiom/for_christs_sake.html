for Christ's sake

1. X

/ fər kraɪsts seɪk / (AmE), / fə kraɪsts seɪk / (BrE)
used to express strong frustration, annoyance, surprise, or urgency
used to express frustration or urgency

Examples

Just listen to me, for Christ's sake, and stop arguing.
For Christ's sake, can someone answer the phone?
She forgot her keys again, for Christ's sake!

Synset Examples

Just listen to me for Christ's sake!
Don't be late again, for Christ's sake.
Why are you acting like this, for Christ's sake?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for heaven's sake - used to express frustration or urgency without religious reference
  • Synonym: for God's sake - used to express frustration or urgency, often with stronger religious reference

Etymology

Refers to Christ (Jesus) as an exclamation; common in English since the 19th century. Considered blasphemous by some.
