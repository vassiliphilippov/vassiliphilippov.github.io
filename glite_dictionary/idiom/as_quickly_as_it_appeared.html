as quickly as it appeared

1. ADV

/ əz ˈkwɪkli æz ɪt əˈpɪrd / (AmE), / əz ˈkwɪkli æz ɪt əˈpɪəd / (BrE)
used to describe someone or something that disappears or ceases suddenly after arriving or occurring unexpectedly
disappears suddenly after appearing

Examples

The thief vanished as quickly as it appeared, leaving no trace behind.
The excitement faded as quickly as it appeared, replaced by silence.
The rainbow seemed to melt away as quickly as it appeared.

Synset Examples

The darkling vanished as quickly as it appeared.
