say what

1. INTJ

/ seɪ wʌt / (AmE), / seɪ wɒt / (BrE)
used to express surprise or disbelief at something just heard or said
expression of surprise or disbelief
informal

Examples

You spent that much on shoes? Say what!
They canceled the flight again, say what?
He wants to move to Antarctica, say what?

Synset Examples

You did what? Say what!
She just quit her job, say what?
He just won a million dollars, say what?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to come again - used to ask someone to repeat something, often with surprise or disbelief
