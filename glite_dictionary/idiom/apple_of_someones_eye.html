apple of someone's eye

1. NOUN

/ ˈæp.əl əv ˈsʌmˌwʌnz aɪ / (AmE), / ˈæp.əl ɒv ˈsʌmwʌnz aɪ / (BrE)
a person or thing that is cherished or adored above all others
person/thing greatly cherished

Examples

Her granddaughter is truly the apple of her eye and always brings her joy.
The teacher's pet is undoubtedly the apple of his eye in the classroom.
Ever since they adopted Max, the dog has become the apple of their eye.

Synset Examples

The puppy quickly became the apple of his eye.
That vintage car is the apple of his eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: darling - a person who is very dear to someone
  • Synonym: favorite - a person or thing regarded with special favor or preference

Etymology

From Old English and biblical usage, where the 'apple' referred to the pupil of the eye, symbolizing something precious.
