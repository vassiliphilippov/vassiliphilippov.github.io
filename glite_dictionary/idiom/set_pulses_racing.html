to set pulses racing

1. VERB

/ sɛt ˈpʌlsɪz ˈreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / sɛt ˈpʌlsɪz ˈreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
to make someone feel very excited or thrilled, often in a sudden or intense way
cause sudden excitement or thrill

Examples

The team's dramatic last-minute goal set pulses racing throughout the stadium.
That new sports car is guaranteed to set pulses racing among enthusiasts.
The trailer for the new action film set pulses racing ahead of its release.
