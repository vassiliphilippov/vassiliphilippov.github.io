time and time again

1. ADV

/ taɪm ənd taɪm əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / taɪm ənd taɪm əˈɡen / (BrE)
very often; on many occasions; repeatedly
repeatedly; very often

Examples

I have told you time and time again not to leave your shoes in the hallway.
That mistake happens time and time again when people rush their work.
Scientists have observed this phenomenon time and time again in their experiments.
