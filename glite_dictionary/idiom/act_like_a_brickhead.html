to act like a brickhead

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈbrɪkˌhɛd / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈbrɪkˌhɛd / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or stubborn manner
behave foolishly or stubbornly
informal

Examples

If you continue to act like a brickhead, you'll never solve the problem.
She told him to stop acting like a brickhead and just apologize.
Don't act like a brickhead when someone offers you good advice.

Synset Examples

Stop acting like a brickhead and listen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: act like a fool - to behave foolishly
  • Synonym: stubborn - unwilling to change or listen
