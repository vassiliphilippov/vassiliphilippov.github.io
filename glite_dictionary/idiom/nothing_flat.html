nothing flat

1. ADV

/ ˌnʌθɪŋ ˈflæt / (AmE), / ˌnʌθɪŋ ˈflæt / (BrE)
in a very short amount of time; very quickly or without delay.
very quickly; without delay
informal

Examples

The fire spread through the building in nothing flat.
Give me five minutes and I’ll have this done in nothing flat.
He solved the puzzle in nothing flat, amazing everyone in the room.

Synset Examples

The car can go from zero to sixty in nothing flat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in no time - very quickly; without delay
