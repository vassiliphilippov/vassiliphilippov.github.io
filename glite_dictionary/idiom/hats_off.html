hats off

1. X

/ ˈhæts ˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈhæts ˌɒf / (BrE)
used to express respect, admiration, or congratulations for someone’s achievement or effort
expression of respect or admiration

Examples

Hats off to the chef for preparing such a wonderful meal.
Hats off to everyone who volunteered their time for the community project.
He won the championship on his first try—hats off to him!

Synset Examples

Hats off, he really nailed that presentation.
Hats off to the team for their hard work!
Hats off to her for finishing the marathon!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kudos - praise and honor for an achievement
  • Synonym: congratulations - words expressing praise for an achievement

Etymology

Originally referring to the custom of removing one's hat as a gesture of respect.
