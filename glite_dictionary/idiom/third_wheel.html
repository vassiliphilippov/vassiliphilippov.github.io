third wheel

1. NOUN

/ θɜrd wil / (AmE), / θɜːd wiːl / (BrE)
a person who feels out of place or unnecessary in a group, especially when accompanying a couple
unnecessary person in a group, esp. with a couple

Examples

Whenever I hang out with them, I feel like a third wheel.
She didn't want to come to the movie and be a third wheel.
It can be awkward acting as the third wheel when your friends are dating.

Synset Examples

He always feels like the third wheel with his couple friends.
Don't invite me if I'm just going to be a third wheel.
I felt like a third wheel on their date.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fifth wheel - an extra person or thing that is unnecessary or superfluous
