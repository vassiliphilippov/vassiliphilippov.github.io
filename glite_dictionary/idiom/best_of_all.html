best of all

1. ADV

/ bɛst əv ɔl / (AmE), / bɛst əv ɔːl / (BrE)
used to indicate the most desirable or fortunate aspect among several; most importantly or most appealingly
most fortunate or desirable aspect

Examples

Best of all, the weather stayed sunny throughout our entire trip.
She enjoyed the concert, but best of all, she got to meet the band afterwards.
He tried several recipes, and best of all, they were all delicious.

Synset Examples

best of all, we don't have any homework!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: most importantly - introducing the most important point
  • Antonym: worst of all - indicating the least desirable aspect
