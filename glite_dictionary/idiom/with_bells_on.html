with bells on

1. ADV

/ wɪð bɛlz ɑn / (AmE), / wɪð bɛlz ɒn / (BrE)
Full of enthusiasm or excitement; eager and ready to participate.
enthusiastically; with excitement

Examples

I'll be there with bells on to help you celebrate your promotion.
She promised to join the team with bells on for the big match tomorrow.
Whenever there's a festival, he shows up with bells on and a big smile.

Synset Examples

He accepted the invitation with bells on.
They arrived at the event with bells on.
She'll come to the party with bells on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enthusiastically - in a way that shows intense excitement or eagerness

Etymology

Possibly from the custom of decorating horses or costumes with bells for festivities, indicating a readiness to celebrate; exact origin uncertain.
