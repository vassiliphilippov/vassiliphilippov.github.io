sisters before misters

1. X

/ ˈsɪstɚz bɪˈfɔr ˈmɪstɚz / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəz bɪˈfɔː ˈmɪstəz / (BrE)
used to express the idea that friendships between women should be prioritized over romantic relationships with men
prioritizing female friendships over romantic interests
informal

Examples

We promised to always remember sisters before misters when faced with tough decisions about dating.
Whenever a new boyfriend came along, they reminded themselves of sisters before misters.
She chose a night out with her friends over a date, sticking to sisters before misters.
