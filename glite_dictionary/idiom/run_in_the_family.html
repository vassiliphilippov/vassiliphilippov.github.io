to run in the family

1. VERB

/ rʌn ɪn ðə ˈfæməli / (AmE), / rʌn ɪn ðə ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
(of a characteristic or trait) to be hereditary or commonly found among members of the same family.
be hereditary; shared by family members

Examples

Asthma seems to run in the family on my mother's side.
A knack for music runs in the family as all the siblings are talented musicians.
His blue eyes run in the family, just like his father's and grandmother's.

Synset Examples

Health issues like diabetes run in the family.
Artistic talent seems to run in the family.
Intelligence definitely runs in the family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hereditary - (of a characteristic) passed from parents to offspring genetically
  • Synonym: genetic - relating to genes or heredity
