all over oneself

1. ADV

/ ɔl ˈoʊvɚ wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈəʊvə wʌnˈself / (BrE)
behaving in an excessively self-conscious, awkward, or eager manner, often drawing excessive attention to oneself
excessively self-conscious or awkward
informal

Examples

He was all over himself trying to impress the new manager, barely able to sit still.
She gets all over herself with excitement whenever she talks about her favorite band.
After receiving the compliment, he became all over himself and started blushing awkwardly.

Synset Examples

When he won the award, he was all over himself with pride.
He was all over himself trying to impress her.
She was all over herself with excitement after the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-conscious - nervous or uncomfortable because you are worried about what people think of you
  • Synonym: awkward - feeling embarrassment or inconvenience; lacking ease
