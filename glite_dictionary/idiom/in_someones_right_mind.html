in someone's right mind

1. X

/ ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz raɪt maɪnd / (AmE), / ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz raɪt maɪnd / (BrE)
thinking and behaving in a rational or sensible way; sane.
sane; thinking rationally

Examples

No one in their right mind would choose to walk home in this weather.
She must not be in her right mind to invest all her money in that scheme.
Would you honestly consider that idea if you were in your right mind?

Synset Examples

He wouldn't sell his car in his right mind.
No one in their right mind would do that.
Only someone in their right mind would take that job.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: out of one's mind - insane; unable to think rationally
