with age comes

1. X

/ wɪð eɪdʒ kʌmz / (AmE), / wɪð eɪdʒ kʌmz / (BrE)
used to express the idea that as people grow older, they gain more knowledge, wisdom, or experience
gaining wisdom or experience as one grows older

Examples

With age comes a better understanding of how to handle difficult situations.
People often say that with age comes patience and perspective.
She truly believes that with age comes the ability to make wiser decisions.

Synset Examples

With age comes wisdom and experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wisdom comes with age - another phrase meaning people become wiser as they get older
