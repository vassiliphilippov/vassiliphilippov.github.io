under the carpet

1. X

/ ˈʌndɚ ðə ˈkɑːɹpɪt / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ðə ˈkɑːpɪt / (BrE)
If something is swept or pushed under the carpet, it is kept secret or ignored instead of being dealt with.
kept secret or ignored

Examples

Many companies choose to keep financial troubles under the carpet rather than address them openly.
He always tried to brush his mistakes under the carpet instead of apologizing.
The issue was quietly swept under the carpet and never discussed again.

Synset Examples

The scandal was hidden under the carpet for years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sweep under the rug - to conceal or ignore problems
  • Antonym: to bring to light - to reveal or make known
