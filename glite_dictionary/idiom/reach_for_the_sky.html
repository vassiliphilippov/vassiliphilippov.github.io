to reach for the sky

1. VERB

/ ritʃ fər ðə skaɪ / (AmE), / riːtʃ fə ðə skaɪ / (BrE)
to try to achieve very ambitious goals or dreams
aspire to achieve great things

Examples

No matter what others say, always reach for the sky and believe in yourself.
She encouraged her students to reach for the sky and never settle for less.
If you reach for the sky, you might just achieve more than you imagined.

Synset Examples

With hard work, you can reach for the sky.
She always tells her kids to reach for the sky.
His ambition is to reach for the sky in his career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to aim high - to set one's goals or ambitions very high
  • Synonym: to dream big - to have big ambitions or goals
