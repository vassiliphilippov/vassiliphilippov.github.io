all over but the shouting

1. X

/ ɔl ˈoʊvɚ bət ðə ˈʃaʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈəʊvə bə ðə ˈʃaʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
used to say that the result of something is already decided, and only minor details or formalities remain to be completed
result already decided; details remain

Examples

With their team leading by four goals, the game was all over but the shouting.
The jury returned their verdict, so it's all over but the shouting.
Once she accepted the offer, the deal was all over but the shouting.

Synset Examples

Now that the project is approved, it's all over but the shouting.
The contract is signed; it's all over but the shouting.
With the final whistle blown, it's all over but the shouting.
