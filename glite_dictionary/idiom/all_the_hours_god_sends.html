all the hours God sends

1. X

/ ɔl ðə aʊɚz ɡɑd sɛndz / (AmE), / ɔːl ðiː aʊəz ɡɒd sends / (BrE)
used to emphasize that someone is doing something, especially working, during every available moment without rest or pause.
working or doing something as long as possible
british

Examples

She worked all the hours God sends to finish her project by the deadline.
He studies all the hours God sends during exam season, hardly taking a break.
The nurses were on duty all the hours God sends during the emergency.

Synset Examples

He’s been studying all the hours God sends lately.
She works all the hours God sends at her job.
During peak season, we work all the hours God sends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: around the clock - continuously, all day and night
  • Synonym: day and night - continuously, all the time without stopping
