on the money

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə ˈmʌni / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ˈmʌni / (BrE)
Exactly right or accurate; precisely correct.
exactly right; accurate
informal

Examples

Her explanation of the situation was on the money and helped everyone understand.
Your estimate for the repair costs was on the money this time.
His answer in the final round was on the money, winning the team the game.

Synset Examples

Her advice was on the money for our marketing strategy.
The prediction was on the money as usual.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spot on - exactly correct; precisely right
