pics or it didn't happen

1. X

/ pɪks ɔɹ ɪt dɪdənt ˈhæpən / (AmE), / pɪks ɔː ɪt dɪdnt ˈhæpən / (BrE)
(informal) used to demand photographic proof as evidence that a claim is true, especially in digital or online conversations.
demand for photographic proof
informal slang

Examples

You say you met a celebrity? Pics or it didn't happen!
Whenever someone boasts online, people respond with pics or it didn't happen.
She didn't believe my story and messaged, 'Pics or it didn't happen.'

Synset Examples
