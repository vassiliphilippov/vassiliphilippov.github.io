love at first sight

1. NOUN

/ lʌv ət ˈfɜrst saɪt / (AmE), / lʌv ət ˈfɜːst saɪt / (BrE)
the experience of falling in love with someone immediately upon first seeing or meeting them
falling in love instantly

Examples

When they met at the party, it was love at first sight for both of them.
Many people are skeptical about love at first sight, but some swear it happens.
Their parents met through friends and experienced love at first sight.

Synset Examples

It was truly love at first sight when they met.
They experienced love at first sight during the concert.
She believed in love at first sight after their first date.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infatuation - an intense but short-lived passion or admiration for someone
  • Antonym: slow burn - a romantic relationship that develops gradually over time
