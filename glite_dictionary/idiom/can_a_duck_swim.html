can a duck swim

1. X

/ kæn ə dʌk swɪm / (AmE), / kan ə dʌk swɪm / (BrE)
used as a rhetorical question to indicate that the answer to a previous question is obviously 'yes'
rhetorical question meaning 'obviously yes'

Examples

"Will you be at the party tonight?" "Can a duck swim? Of course I will!"
When asked if she likes ice cream, she replied, "Can a duck swim?"
You might as well ask, "Can a duck swim?"—it's that obvious.

Synset Examples

Everyone knows can a duck swim in any pond.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: does a bear shit in the woods - another rhetorical question meaning 'obviously yes'
  • Synonym: is the Pope Catholic - another rhetorical question meaning 'obviously yes'
