not an ounce of

1. X

/ nɑt ən aʊns əv / (AmE), / nɒt ən aʊns əv / (BrE)
not even a very small amount of something; absolutely none
absolutely no amount of something

Examples

There was not an ounce of truth in his statement.
She showed not an ounce of hesitation before jumping into the water.
He performed with not an ounce of fear in front of the crowd.

Synset Examples

Not an ounce of fear crossed her mind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: not a shred of - not even the smallest amount of
  • Synonym: not a bit of - absolutely none; not even a little
  • Antonym: full of - containing or having a lot of something (opposite meaning)
