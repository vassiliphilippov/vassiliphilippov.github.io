to undress with one's eyes

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈdrɛs wɪð wʌnz aɪz / (AmE), / ʌnˈdrɛs wɪð wʌnz aɪz / (BrE)
to look at someone in a way that suggests sexual interest, as if imagining them without their clothes
look at someone with sexual interest

Examples

He made her uncomfortable by undressing her with his eyes during the meeting.
She could tell that he was undressing her with his eyes from across the room.
It's inappropriate to undress someone with your eyes, especially in a professional environment.

Synset Examples
