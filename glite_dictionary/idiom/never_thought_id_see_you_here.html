never thought I'd see you here

1. X

/ ˈnɛvɚ θɔt aɪd ˈsi ju hɪr / (AmE), / ˈnɛvə θɔːt aɪd ˈsiː juː hɪə / (BrE)
used to express surprise at encountering someone in an unexpected place or situation
expression of surprise at seeing someone unexpectedly

Examples

Never thought I'd see you here—I had no idea you even liked this restaurant!
She walked into the meeting and said, "Never thought I'd see you here, Mark."
After so many years apart, I never thought I'd see you here at this reunion.

Synset Examples

Well, I never thought I'd see you here!
