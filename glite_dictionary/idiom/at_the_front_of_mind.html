at the front of mind

1. X

/ ət ðə frʌnt əv maɪnd / (AmE), / ət ðə frʌnt əv maɪnd / (BrE)
if something is at the front of your mind, it is your main concern or focus at a particular time.
main concern; primary focus

Examples

The question of sustainability was always at the front of mind during the project's planning stage.
Customer satisfaction is at the front of mind for our entire team.
She wanted to make sure the children's safety remained at the front of mind throughout the event.

Synset Examples

Budget constraints are at the front of mind this quarter.
Safety is always at the front of mind for us.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top of mind - main concern; foremost in thoughts
  • Antonym: back of one's mind - something not currently focused on; lingering concern
