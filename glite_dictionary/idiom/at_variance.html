at variance

1. ADJ

/ ət ˈvɛriəns / (AmE), / ət ˈvɑːrɪəns / (BrE)
not in agreement or harmony; in conflict or disagreement
not in agreement; in conflict

Examples

Her account of the events was at variance with what the others said.
The scientist's findings are at variance with current theories.
His actions were clearly at variance with the organization's policy.

Synset Examples

desires at variance with his duty
widely discrepant statements

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in accord - in agreement or harmony
