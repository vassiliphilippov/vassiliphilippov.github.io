lightning in a bottle

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪtnɪŋ ɪn ə ˈbɑːɾl̩ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtnɪŋ ɪn ə ˈbɒtl̩ / (BrE)
something that is very difficult or unlikely to achieve, especially a rare and exceptional success or event
rare and exceptional occurrence

Examples

Scoring the winning goal in the last second was like capturing lightning in a bottle.
Her debut novel was lightning in a bottle, receiving praise from critics everywhere.
Finding such a talented team was truly lightning in a bottle for the company.

Synset Examples

That song was lightning in a bottle; it became a hit overnight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: once in a blue moon - very rarely; hardly ever happens
  • Synonym: fluke - a stroke of luck, especially something unlikely to happen again
