moderation is key

1. X

/ ˌmɑːdəˈreɪʃən ɪz kiː / (AmE), / ˌmɒdəˈreɪʃən ɪz kiː / (BrE)
It is important to avoid extremes and pursue a balanced approach in order to achieve the best results.
balance is essential for success

Examples

When it comes to eating sweets, moderation is key if you want to stay healthy.
Some people believe you should work as much as possible, but I think moderation is key.
Whether exercising or relaxing, moderation is key to maintaining a balanced lifestyle.

Synset Examples

He said, don't diet, try it; moderation is key.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: everything in moderation - the idea that nothing should be done to excess
