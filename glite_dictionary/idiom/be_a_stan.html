to be a stan

1. VERB

/ bi ə stæn / (AmE), / biː ə stan / (BrE)
to act as an obsessive or extremely devoted fan, especially of a celebrity or artist
to act as an obsessive fan
slang

Examples

She admits to being a stan for that band since she was a teenager.
On social media, many users are proud of being a stan for their favorite actor.
People sometimes tease him for being a stan of superhero comics.

Synset Examples

Being a stan means you support your favorite artist unconditionally.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stan - an obsessive fan of a celebrity or artist
  • Degree Lower: supporter - a person who supports a particular person, group, or cause

Etymology

From 'stan', popularized by Eminem's 2000 song of the same name, meaning an obsessive fan.
