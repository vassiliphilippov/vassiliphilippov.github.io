better for it

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛtər fər ɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɛtə fə ɪt / (BrE)
having improved or benefited as a result of an experience, especially a difficult one
improved as a result of experience

Examples

She faced many challenges, but she is better for it today.
The team learned from their mistakes and became better for it in the next season.
After a difficult year, he feels he is better for it.

Synset Examples

He lost the game but is better for it.
They struggled but are better for it now.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: worse for wear - in a worse condition after something
