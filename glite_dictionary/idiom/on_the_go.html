on the go

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə ɡoʊ / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ɡəʊ / (BrE)
(of a person) very busy and active, often moving from one task or place to another without pause.
very busy and active

Examples

With three young children, she's constantly on the go from morning till night.
He prefers vacations where he's always on the go instead of just relaxing.
Even on weekends, Mark manages to stay on the go with work and hobbies.

Synset Examples
