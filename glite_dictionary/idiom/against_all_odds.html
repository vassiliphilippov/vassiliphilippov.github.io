against all odds

1. ADV

/ əˈɡɛnst ɔl ɑdz / (AmE), / əˈɡɛnst ɔːl ɒdz / (BrE)
despite extreme difficulties or unlikely circumstances, succeeding when success seemed impossible
despite extreme difficulties or obstacles

Examples

She started her own company against all odds and made it a success.
The team managed to finish the project on time against all odds.
He survived the harsh winter in the mountains against all odds.
