your shit doesn't stink

1. X

/ jɔɹ ʃɪt ˈdʌzənt stɪŋk / (AmE), / jɔː ʃɪt ˈdʌzənt stɪŋk / (BrE)
used to describe someone who thinks they are perfect and cannot do anything wrong; displaying arrogance or self-importance
idiom for self-importance or arrogance
informal vulgar

Examples

She walks around acting like your shit doesn't stink, but everyone knows that's not true.
Don't let him make you feel inferior; he just thinks your shit doesn't stink.
People won't like you if you behave as if your shit doesn't stink.

Synset Examples

Stop acting like you think your shit doesn't stink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arrogant - having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities
  • Antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's own importance
