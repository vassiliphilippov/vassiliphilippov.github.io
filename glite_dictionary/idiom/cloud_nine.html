cloud nine

1. NOUN

/ klaʊd naɪn / (AmE), / klaʊd naɪn / (BrE)
a state of extreme happiness or bliss; great euphoria
a state of great happiness

Examples

After hearing the news of her promotion, she was on cloud nine all day.
Winning the championship put the entire team on cloud nine.
He felt like he was on cloud nine after their first date.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecstasy - an overwhelming feeling of great happiness or joyful excitement
  • Antonym: misery - a state of great unhappiness or emotional distress

Etymology

First recorded in the 1950s, likely from U.S. English weather classification, with 'cloud nine' considered the highest or most cumulonimbus.
