to be a nobber

1. VERB

/ bi ə ˈnɑbɚ / (AmE), / biː ə ˈnɒbə / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or annoying way
act foolishly or annoyingly
slang british

Examples

Stop being a nobber and help us out.
If you keep being a nobber, nobody will want to work with you.
She told him to quit being a nobber during the meeting.

Synset Examples

Stop being a nobber and help us out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be a prat - to act in a silly or irritating way (British slang)
  • Synonym: be a jerk - to behave in an obnoxious or irritating manner (informal)

Etymology

From British slang 'nobber', used as an insult meaning fool or annoying person.
