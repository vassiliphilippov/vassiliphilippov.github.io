the cat got your tongue

1. X

/ ðə kæt ɡɑt jɔr tʌŋ / (AmE), / ðə kæt ɡɒt jɔː tʌŋ / (BrE)
used to say that someone is unusually quiet and does not say anything when expected to speak
someone is unexpectedly silent

Examples

Lucy sat quietly through the whole meeting, as if the cat got her tongue.
Everyone expected him to argue back, but the cat got his tongue.
When the teacher called on me unexpectedly, it felt like the cat got my tongue.

Synset Examples

She was unusually quiet; it was like the cat got her tongue.
When asked about his plans, the cat got his tongue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silent - not making any sound; not speaking
  • Synonym: speechless - unable to speak, often from surprise or shock
