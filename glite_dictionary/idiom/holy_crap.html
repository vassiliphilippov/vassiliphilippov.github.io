holy crap

1. INTJ

/ ˈhoʊli kræp / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli kræp / (BrE)
used to express surprise, shock, or astonishment
expression of surprise or shock
informal

Examples

Holy crap, that's the biggest fish I've ever seen!
When I looked at my bill, I said, 'holy crap, that's expensive.'
Holy crap! I can't believe we won the game.

Synset Examples

Holy crap, did you see that goal?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holy cow - used to express surprise or astonishment
  • Synonym: holy mackerel - used as an exclamation of surprise
