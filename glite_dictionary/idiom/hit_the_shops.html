to hit the shops

1. VERB

/ hɪt ðə ʃɑps / (AmE), / hɪt ðə ʃɒps / (BrE)
to go shopping, especially with the intention of visiting several stores
go shopping
informal

Examples

We're planning to hit the shops early to avoid the crowds.
She loves to hit the shops whenever there's a big sale.
After breakfast, we decided to hit the shops and see the new arrivals.

Synset Examples

They decided to hit the shops for some last-minute gifts.
I plan to hit the shops this weekend for new clothes.
Let's hit the shops and see what's on sale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go shopping - to visit shops to buy things
