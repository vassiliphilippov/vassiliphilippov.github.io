seventh heaven

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛvənθ ˈhɛvən / (AmE), / ˈsɛvənθ ˈhɛvən / (BrE)
a state of intense happiness or bliss
state of extreme happiness

Examples

When Helen heard the good news, she was in seventh heaven all day.
Winning the championship put the whole team in seventh heaven.
After receiving the promotion, he felt like he was in seventh heaven.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecstasy - an overwhelming feeling of great happiness
  • Synonym: bliss - perfect happiness; great joy
  • Antonym: misery - a state of great distress or discomfort

Etymology

From ancient Jewish and Islamic cosmology, in which the seventh heaven is the highest and most exalted level of heaven.
