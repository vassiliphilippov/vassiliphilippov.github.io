hot to trot

1. ADJ

/ hɑt tə trɑt / (AmE), / hɒt tə trɒt / (BrE)
very eager or enthusiastic to begin something or take part in an activity
very eager; enthusiastic to start
informal

Examples

After the interview, she was hot to trot for her new job.
The kids are hot to trot for the amusement park trip.
Once the gates opened, fans were hot to trot to see their favorite band.

Synset Examples

She's hot to trot for a new project at work.
He's hot to trot for the big game this weekend.
After the workout, I'm feeling hot to trot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keen - very eager or enthusiastic
  • Antonym: reluctant - unwilling or hesitant to do something
