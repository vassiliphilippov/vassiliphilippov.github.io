like a whale

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə weɪl / (AmE), / laɪk ə weɪl / (BrE)
in a clumsy, awkward, or unskilled manner, especially in movement or singing
in a clumsy or awkward manner

Examples

She danced like a whale at the school talent show, but she had fun anyway.
He sang like a whale during the audition, missing almost every note.
During the gymnastics class, Tom moved like a whale and made everyone laugh.

Synset Examples

His performance was very like a whale, amusing yet awkward.
He sang very like a whale at the karaoke night.
She danced very like a whale at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awkwardly - in a clumsy or ungainly way
  • Antonym: gracefully - in an elegant and smooth manner
