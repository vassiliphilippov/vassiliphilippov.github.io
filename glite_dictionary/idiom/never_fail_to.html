to never fail to

1. VERB

/ ˈnɛvɚ feɪl tu / (AmE), / ˈnɛvə feɪl tʊ / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something always happens in the stated way or always succeeds in achieving the intended result
always does what is expected/mentioned

Examples

My father's stories never fail to make everyone in the room laugh.
Chocolate never fails to lift my spirits after a long day.
The sunrise over the mountains never fails to amaze me.

Synset Examples

Shrek's adventures never fail to make me laugh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invariably - always, on every occasion
