wheel within a wheel

1. NOUN

/ wil wɪˈðɪn ə wil / (AmE), / wiːl wɪˈðɪn ə wiːl / (BrE)
an intricate or interdependent system; a complicated situation with many interconnected parts or factors
complex, interdependent system

Examples

The political alliance turned out to be a wheel within a wheel with hidden motives behind every action.
Running a modern hospital is a wheel within a wheel of regulations, procedures, and coordination.
He was fascinated by the wheel within a wheel nature of international trade.

Synset Examples

The company's operations are a wheel within a wheel of complex systems.
Her life felt like a wheel within a wheel, constantly moving.

Etymology

Refers to imagery in the Book of Ezekiel (Old Testament), used metaphorically for complex systems.
