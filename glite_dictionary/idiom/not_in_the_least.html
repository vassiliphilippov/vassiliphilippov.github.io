not in the least

1. ADV

/ nɑt ɪn ðə list / (AmE), / nɒt ɪn ðə liːst / (BrE)
not at all; not even slightly
not at all; not even slightly

Examples

Despite the criticism, she was not in the least discouraged.
He was not in the least interested in their proposal.
Are you worried about the test? Not in the least.

Synset Examples

I do not feel guilty, not in the least.

He was not in the slightest interested in her opinion.
I don't care about the rumors, not in the slightest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: not in the slightest - not at all; not even a little bit
  • Synonym: at all - to any extent or degree
