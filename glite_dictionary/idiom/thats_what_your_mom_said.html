that's what your mom said

1. X

/ ðæts wət jʊr mɑm sɛd / (AmE), / ðæts wɒt jɔː mɒm sɛd / (BrE)
A humorous or teasing retort used to jokingly sexualize or undermine a preceding statement by referencing someone's mother.
joking retort referencing someone's mother
informal slang

Examples

Whenever I complain about being tired, he always replies, 'that's what your mom said.'
After I mentioned how hard it was, she laughed and quipped, 'that's what your mom said.'
In our group chat, someone inevitably types 'that's what your mom said' whenever a double entendre pops up.

Synset Examples

That's what your mom said last weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: your mom - an insult or joke involving someone's mother
  • Synonym: that's what she said - a humorous retort transforming an innocuous phrase into innuendo
