out of Absurdistan

1. X

/ aʊt ʌv əbˈsɝdɪˌstæn / (AmE), / aʊt ɒv əbˈsɜːdɪˌstæn / (BrE)
suggesting an extreme degree of absurdity or irrationality, as if originating from a place characterized by absurdity
suggesting extreme absurdity

Examples

The new university regulations seem like something out of Absurdistan.
Her explanation for missing the meeting was straight out of Absurdistan.
Many citizens compared the chaotic election to something out of Absurdistan.

Synset Examples

This policy feels like something out of Absurdistan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absurd - utterly unreasonable or illogical

Etymology

Modelled after place names like Afghanistan; "Absurdistan" was coined in German in the 1970s to satirically denote a place governed by absurdity.
