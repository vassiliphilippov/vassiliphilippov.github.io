the sky's the limit

1. X

/ ðə skaɪz ðə ˈlɪmɪt / (AmE), / ðə skaɪz ðə ˈlɪmɪt / (BrE)
there is no limit to what can be achieved or how far someone can go
no limits to achievement

Examples

With your determination, the sky's the limit for what you can accomplish.
Once I set my mind to something, the sky's the limit.
For our company's growth this year, the sky's the limit.
