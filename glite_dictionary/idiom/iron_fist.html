iron fist

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪərn fɪst / (AmE), / ˈaɪən fɪst / (BrE)
rigorous or ruthless control or authority, often exercised in a harsh or authoritarian manner
strict, ruthless control

Examples

Despite his popularity, the leader maintained power with an iron fist.
She managed the company with an iron fist, tolerating no mistakes.
The regime's iron fist crushed all political opposition.

Synset Examples

she rules the office with an iron fist
it takes an iron fist to contain the dissenting factions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firm hand - strict or strong control
  • Antonym: velvet glove - gentle or diplomatic approach (often contrasted with 'iron fist')
