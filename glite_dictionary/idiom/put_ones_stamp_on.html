to put one's stamp on

1. VERB

/ pʊt wʌnz stæmp ɑn / (AmE), / pʊt wʌnz stæmp ɒn / (BrE)
to make a personal contribution or addition that reflects one's individual style or influence
make a personal mark or influence

Examples

The new CEO quickly put his stamp on the company by introducing bold changes.
As a director, she always puts her stamp on every film she makes.
You can put your stamp on the presentation by adding your unique perspective.

Synset Examples

The artist put her stamp on the mural with vibrant colors.
He put his stamp on the design, making it uniquely his.
She always puts her stamp on every project she works on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to personalize - to make something suitable for a particular person, often by adding a name or characteristic
  • Synonym: to individualize - to make something individual or distinct
