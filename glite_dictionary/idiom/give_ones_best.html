to give one's best

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv wʌnz bɛst / (AmE), / ɡɪv wʌnz bɛst / (BrE)
to perform a task or action as well as one possibly can; to put forth maximum effort
do as well as possible

Examples

No matter what happens, just give your best and be proud of your effort.
The athlete promised to give her best in the upcoming final match.
He always gives his best at work, no matter how challenging the task.

Synset Examples

The cast gives full measure every night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to do one's best - to make the greatest effort possible
