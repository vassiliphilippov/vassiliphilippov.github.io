at the worst

1. ADV

/ ət ðə wɜrst / (AmE), / ət ðə wɜːst / (BrE)
Under the least favorable or most adverse circumstances; as the worst possible outcome.
in the worst case scenario

Examples

At the worst, the meeting will only be delayed until next week.
You'll be fined, or at the worst, lose your license for a month.
At the worst, their plan will cost us some time but nothing more.

Synset Examples

at worst we'll go to jail

Related Terms

  • Antonym: at best - in the most favorable case; as the best possible outcome
