like the back of one's hand

1. X

/ laɪk ðə bæk əv wʌnz hænd / (AmE), / laɪk ðə bæk əv wʌnz hand / (BrE)
very well; extremely familiar, as with something or somewhere from frequent experience
extremely familiar with something

Examples

After living here for twenty years, I know this neighborhood like the back of my hand.
The guide led us through the forest, which he knew like the back of his hand.
She can navigate the library like the back of her hand after so many years of working there.

Synset Examples

He knows this city like the back of his hand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: by heart - know something exactly and can recite or do from memory
