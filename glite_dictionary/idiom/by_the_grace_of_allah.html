by the Grace of Allah

1. X

/ baɪ ðə ɡreɪs əv əˈlɑː / (AmE), / baɪ ðə ɡreɪs əv əˈlɑː / (BrE)
used to express gratitude or acknowledgment that something was achieved or made possible through the mercy or favor of Allah.
by Allah's mercy or favor

Examples

We managed to complete the project, by the Grace of Allah.
The community survived the drought by the Grace of Allah.
She recovered from her illness by the Grace of Allah.

Synset Examples

We are here today, by the Grace of Allah.
They found shelter, by the Grace of Allah, during the storm.
He succeeded, by the Grace of Allah, against all odds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: by the grace of God - used to attribute success or safety to God's mercy
