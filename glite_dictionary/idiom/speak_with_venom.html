to speak with venom

1. VERB

/ spiːk wɪð ˈvɛnəm / (AmE), / spiːk wɪð ˈvɛnəm / (BrE)
to speak in a harsh, bitter, or spiteful manner
to speak harshly or bitterly

Examples

She spoke with venom when she confronted her former friend.
During the debate, the politician spoke with venom about his opponent's policies.
Whenever his name was mentioned, she couldn't help but speak with venom.

Synset Examples

She spoke with venom, expressing her true feelings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speak bitterly - to speak in a sharp or resentful way
  • Antonym: speak gently - to speak in a kind or soft manner
