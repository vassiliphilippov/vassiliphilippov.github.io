to make yourself comfortable

1. VERB

/ meɪk jərˈsɛlf ˈkʌmfərtəbəl / (AmE), / meɪk jɔːˈsɛlf ˈkʌmfətəbəl / (BrE)
to settle in and relax; to act as though at home in order to feel at ease
relax and feel at ease

Examples

Please come in and make yourself comfortable while you wait for the doctor.
If you're staying for a while, just make yourself comfortable on the sofa.
She told her guests to make themselves comfortable before she brought out the snacks.

Synset Examples

Please sit down and make yourself comfortable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to relax - to rest and become less anxious or tense
