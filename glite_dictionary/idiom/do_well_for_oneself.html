to do well for oneself

1. VERB

/ duː wɛl fər wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / duː wɛl fə wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to become successful, prosper, or improve one's own circumstances, especially in a material or financial sense
prosper or achieve personal success

Examples

After moving to the city, he really began to do well for himself in business.
Many aspire to do well for themselves but also to help their community.
Despite having little support, she managed to do well for herself.

Synset Examples

It's important to do well for oneself and others.
He managed to do well for himself despite challenges.
She worked hard to do well for herself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to prosper - to succeed, especially financially; to thrive
  • Synonym: to thrive - to grow, develop, or be successful
