to remain to be seen

1. VERB

/ rɪˈmeɪn tə bi ˈsiːn / (AmE), / rɪˈmeɪn tə bi ˈsiːn / (BrE)
If something remains to be seen, its outcome or result is still unknown or uncertain.
outcome is still unknown or uncertain

Examples

It remains to be seen whether the new government policies will be successful.
How the situation will develop remains to be seen over the next few months.
The long-term impact of the decision still remains to be seen.

Synset Examples

Whether he will succeed remains to be seen.
