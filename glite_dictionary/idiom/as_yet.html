as yet

1. ADV

/ æz ˈjɛt / (AmE), / æz ˈjet / (BrE)
up to the present time; so far; until now
up to the present time

Examples

There have been no new announcements, as yet, regarding the project.
Many questions remain unanswered as yet in the investigation.
The issue has not been resolved as yet, but they are working on it.

Synset Examples

No one has called, as yet.
The results are not in, as yet.
I haven't decided, as yet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: so far - up to this point in time; until now
  • Synonym: yet - up to now; until the present
