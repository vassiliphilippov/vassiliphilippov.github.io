off one's tree

1. ADJ

/ ɔf wʌnz tri / (AmE), / ɒf wʌnz tri / (BrE)
(informal) Crazy or irrational; behaving in an unreasonable or eccentric way.
crazy or irrational (idiom)
informal

Examples

You must be off your tree if you think that plan will succeed.
After staying up all night, he felt completely off his tree.
My friends thought I was off my tree when I quit my job to travel the world.

Synset Examples

He must be off his tree to think that would work.
I felt off my tree after that news.
She was acting like she was off her tree during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: off one's rocker - crazy or eccentric; not thinking clearly (idiom)
  • Synonym: nuts - (informal) crazy or mentally unstable
  • Antonym: sane - having a healthy mind; not crazy
