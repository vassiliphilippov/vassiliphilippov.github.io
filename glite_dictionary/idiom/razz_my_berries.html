to razz my berries

1. VERB

/ ræz maɪ ˈbɛriz / (AmE), / ræz maɪ ˈbɛriz / (BrE)
to tease or annoy someone in a lighthearted or playful way
tease or annoy playfully
informal

Examples

Don't razz my berries about my new haircut, okay?
He loves to razz my berries whenever I make a silly mistake.
Whenever we're together, she can't help but razz my berries for fun.

Synset Examples

Don't razz my berries; I worked hard on this!
She always knows how to razz my berries playfully.
He loves to razz my berries about my cooking skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tease - make fun of or attempt to provoke in a playful way
  • Synonym: to annoy - make someone slightly angry; irritate
