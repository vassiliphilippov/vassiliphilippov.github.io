shitton of

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪt tʌn əv / (AmE), / ˈʃɪt tʌn ɒv / (BrE)
a very large amount of something; an extremely large quantity
an extremely large amount
informal slang vulgar

Examples

There was a shitton of food left after the party.
She brought a shitton of clothes on her trip.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
