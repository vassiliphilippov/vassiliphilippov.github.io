to have her tubes tied

1. VERB

/ hæv hɚ tuːbz taɪd / (AmE), / hæv hə tjuːbz taɪd / (BrE)
(of a woman) to undergo a surgical procedure to become permanently sterile by having the fallopian tubes closed or blocked.
undergo surgery for female sterilization

Examples

After her third child, she decided to have her tubes tied for permanent birth control.
Many women choose to have her tubes tied when their families are complete.
She discussed the decision to have her tubes tied with her doctor at the clinic.

Synset Examples

They agreed it was time to have her tubes tied.
After two kids, she decided to have her tubes tied.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tubal ligation - surgical procedure for female sterilization
  • Antonym: have a vasectomy - undergo a surgical procedure for male sterilization
