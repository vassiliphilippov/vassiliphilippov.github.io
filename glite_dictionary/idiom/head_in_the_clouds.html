head in the clouds

1. X

/ hɛd ɪn ðə klaʊdz / (AmE), / hɛd ɪn ðə klaʊdz / (BrE)
to be daydreaming, unrealistic, or not paying attention to what is going on around one
daydreaming or unrealistic

Examples

She always has her head in the clouds and forgets about important assignments.
If you keep your head in the clouds, you'll miss the opportunities right in front of you.
Despite his talent, his head in the clouds attitude makes him unreliable at work.

Synset Examples

He often has his head in the clouds during meetings.
With her head in the clouds, she missed the deadline.
Don’t get your head in the clouds; focus on your work!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daydream - to be lost in one's thoughts and unaware of reality
  • Antonym: down to earth - practical and realistic
