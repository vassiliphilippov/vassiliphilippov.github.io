out of the box

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv ðə bɑks / (AmE), / aʊt əv ðə bɒks / (BrE)
used to describe ideas, solutions, or thinking that are innovative or unconventional; not restricted by traditional ways of doing things.
innovative or unconventional thinking

Examples

The company's out of the box solutions helped it stand out from its competitors.
She encouraged her team to think out of the box when tackling the complex problem.
His out of the box perspective brought fresh ideas to the discussion.

Synset Examples

His ideas are always out of the box and creative.
The designer's out of the box ideas impressed everyone.
Her out of the box approach made the project a success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: original - not derived from something else; new and unique
  • Antonym: conventional - following traditional forms or conventions
