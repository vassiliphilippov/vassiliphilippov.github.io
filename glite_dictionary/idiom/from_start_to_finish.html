from start to finish

1. ADV

/ frəm ˈstɑrt tə ˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / frəm ˈstɑːt tə ˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
throughout the entire process, duration, or experience; completely from the beginning to the end
entirely, from beginning to end

Examples

She planned the entire event from start to finish without any outside help.
The procedure was explained to us from start to finish so we knew exactly what to expect.
He watched the marathon from start to finish and cheered for every runner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: from beginning to end - from the first moment to the last; completely
