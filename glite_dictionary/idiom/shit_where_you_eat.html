to shit where you eat

1. VERB

/ ʃɪt wɛɹ ju it / (AmE), / ʃɪt weə juː iːt / (BrE)
to act in a way that creates problems or acts unethically in your own environment, especially in the workplace or a community
cause trouble in your own environment
informal vulgar

Examples

You shouldn't shit where you eat if you want to keep your job and your reputation.
They warned him that shitting where you eat never ends well for anyone involved.
Gossiping about your boss is a classic example of shitting where you eat.

Synset Examples

He learned the hard way that you shouldn't shit where you eat.
In business, remember not to shit where you eat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bite the hand that feeds you - to act against those who support you
