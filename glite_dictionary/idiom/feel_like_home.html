to feel like home

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk hoʊm / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk həʊm / (BrE)
(of a place or situation) to give a sense of comfort, belonging, and welcome, as if it were one's own home.
to feel comfortable and welcomed

Examples

After a few weeks, the new apartment finally felt like home.
The warm welcome from the locals made the town feel like home to us.
With her family and favorite books around, anywhere can feel like home to Jane.

Synset Examples

The vibrant community in Hongcouver feels like home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to feel at home - to feel comfortable and relaxed in a place
