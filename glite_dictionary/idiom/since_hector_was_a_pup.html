since Hector was a pup

1. X

/ sɪns ˈhɛktɚ wəz ə pʌp / (AmE), / sɪns ˈhɛktə wəz ə pʌp / (BrE)
used humorously to mean for a very long time; since far back in the past
for a very long time (humorous idiom)
informal
