as useful as a chocolate teapot

1. ADJ

/ æz ˈjusfəl æz ə ˈʧɔklət ˈtiˌpɑt / (AmE), / əz ˈjuːsfʊl əz ə ˈtʃɒklət ˈtiːpɒt / (BrE)
completely useless; having no practical value or effectiveness
completely useless (British idiom)
british

Examples

When the power went out, my electronic devices were as useful as a chocolate teapot.
His advice on fixing the car was as useful as a chocolate teapot.
Without the proper tools, the instruction manual was as useful as a chocolate teapot.

Synset Examples

Her plan was as useful as a chocolate teapot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: useless - not fulfilling any useful purpose
  • Synonym: worthless - having no real value or use

Etymology

British English idiom—humorous reference to the absurdity of making a teapot from chocolate, which would melt and be useless.
