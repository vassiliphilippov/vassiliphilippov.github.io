in good time

1. ADV

/ ɪn ɡʊd taɪm / (AmE), / ɪn ɡʊd taɪm / (BrE)
at the appropriate or proper time; not too late or too early
at the appropriate time

Examples

Please make sure you arrive in good time for the meeting.
If you start preparing now, you'll be ready in good time.
The flowers bloomed in good time for the festival.

Synset Examples

we'll get to this question in due course

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in due course - at the appropriate or expected time
