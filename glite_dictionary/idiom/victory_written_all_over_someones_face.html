victory written all over someone's face

1. X

/ ˈvɪk.tɚ.i ˈrɪt.n̩ ɔl ˈoʊ.vɚ sʌmˌwʌnz feɪs / (AmE), / ˈvɪk.tər.i ˈrɪt.n ɔːl ˈəʊ.və sʌmˌwʌnz feɪs / (BrE)
(of a person's facial expression) clearly showing signs of success, satisfaction, or happiness.
showing clear signs of success or happiness

Examples

When she got the promotion, she had victory written all over her face.
After winning the match, there was victory written all over his face.
You can tell from his expression—he's got victory written all over his face.

Synset Examples

He had victory written all over his face.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: written all over one's face - to be clearly apparent from someone's facial expression
