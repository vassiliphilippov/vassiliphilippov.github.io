up the creek

1. ADV

/ ʌp ðə krik / (AmE), / ʌp ðə kriːk / (BrE)
in a difficult or troublesome situation
in trouble; in a difficult situation

Examples

If we don't find our tickets soon, we'll be up the creek.
After her car broke down in the middle of nowhere, she knew she was up the creek.
Without proper documentation, the team found themselves up the creek during the audit.

Synset Examples

She realized she was up the creek with no solution.
If we miss the deadline, we'll be up the creek.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in trouble - experiencing difficulty or problems
