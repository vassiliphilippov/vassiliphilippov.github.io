in concert

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈkɑnsɚt / (AmE), / ɪn ˈkɒnsət / (BrE)
acting together or in agreement towards a common goal or plan; in cooperation.
together in agreement or cooperation

Examples

The two companies worked in concert to develop the new product.
Several nations acted in concert to address the global crisis.
The team members responded in concert to ensure the project’s success.

Synset Examples

act in concert

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cooperate - to act jointly toward the same end
  • Antonym: independently - not in conjunction or cooperation

Etymology

From the noun 'concert', which comes from Italian 'concerto' (agreement, harmony), from Latin 'concertare' (to bring into agreement).
