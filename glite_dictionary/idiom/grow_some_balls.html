to grow some balls

1. VERB

/ ɡroʊ səm bɔlz / (AmE), / ɡrəʊ səm bɔːlz / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) To develop courage or become braver, especially in situations that require taking risks or facing challenges.
become braver or more courageous
informal vulgar

Examples

If you want to get that promotion, you need to grow some balls and ask for more responsibility.
She told him to grow some balls and finally tell his boss the truth.
It's time to grow some balls and stop letting others push you around.

Synset Examples

If you want to succeed, you must grow some balls.
It's time to grow some balls and take risks.
You need to grow some balls and speak up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to man up - become more courageous; act bravely
  • Antonym: coward - someone who lacks courage
