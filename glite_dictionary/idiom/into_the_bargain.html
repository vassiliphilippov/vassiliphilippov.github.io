into the bargain

1. ADV

/ ˈɪn.tu ðə ˈbɑr.ɡən / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tə ðə ˈbɑː.ɡɪn / (BrE)
in addition to what has already been mentioned; as well; over and above what is expected
in addition; as well

Examples

He gave us all a ride and picked up the groceries into the bargain.
She's an excellent cook and, into the bargain, speaks three languages fluently.
The house was spacious and, into the bargain, very affordable.

Synset Examples

He lost his wife in the bargain

Related Terms
