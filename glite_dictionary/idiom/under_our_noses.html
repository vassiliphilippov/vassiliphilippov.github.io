under our noses

1. ADV

/ ˈʌndɚ aʊɚ ˈnoʊzɪz / (AmE), / ˈʌndə aʊə ˈnəʊzɪz / (BrE)
so close or obvious that it should have been noticed, but was overlooked
in close proximity but overlooked

Examples

The keys were under our noses the whole time, but none of us noticed them.
The answer was hidden under our noses in the text, but it took hours to find it.
The thief managed to steal the painting under our noses during the busy exhibition.

Synset Examples

The treasure was hidden in plain sight, right under our noses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in plain sight - clearly visible or observable
