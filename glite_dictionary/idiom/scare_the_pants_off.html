to scare the pants off

1. VERB

/ skɛr ðə pænts ɔf / (AmE), / skeə ðə pænts ɒf / (BrE)
to frighten someone extremely; to terrify someone
frighten someone very much
informal

Examples

The haunted house last year scared the pants off me.
That roller coaster ride scares the pants off most people who try it.
The thunderstorm scared the pants off our dog last night.

Synset Examples

The movie trailer is sure to scare the pants off audiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to frighten - to make someone afraid or scared
  • Synonym: scare stiff - to make someone extremely frightened
