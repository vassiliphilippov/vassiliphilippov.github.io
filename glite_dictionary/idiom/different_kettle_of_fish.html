different kettle of fish

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪfɚənt ˈkɛtəl əv fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdɪf(ə)r(ə)nt ˈkɛtəl əv fɪʃ / (BrE)
a situation or subject that is completely different from what has just been mentioned
completely different situation or issue

Examples

Managing a small team is one thing, but running an entire company is a different kettle of fish.
Remodeling the kitchen will be a different kettle of fish compared to painting the walls.
I can handle questions about finance, but legal issues are a different kettle of fish entirely.

Synset Examples

Once we start, it’ll be a different kettle of fish.
This project is a whole different kettle of fish.
That issue is a completely different kettle of fish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: another matter - a different situation or issue
  • Synonym: entirely different - completely unlike or unrelated
