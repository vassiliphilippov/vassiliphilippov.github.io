to get the finger

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ðə ˈfɪŋɡɚ / (AmE), / ɡɛt ðə ˈfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
to receive a rude or dismissive gesture, especially someone raising their middle finger, as a form of rejection or insult
receive a rude or dismissive gesture
informal

Examples

He was so rude to the staff that he got the finger as he left the restaurant.
After arguing with the driver, she got the finger instead of an apology.
When his proposal was rejected, he joked that he had really got the finger from the board.

Synset Examples

He got the finger for being late again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to flip the bird - to make a rude gesture by raising the middle finger
  • Synonym: give the finger - to make an obscene gesture by raising the middle finger

Etymology

From the gesture of raising the middle finger, a rude signal in Western cultures.
