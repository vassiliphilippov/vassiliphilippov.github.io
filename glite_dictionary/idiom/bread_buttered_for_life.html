bread buttered for life

1. X

/ brɛd ˈbʌtɚd fɚ laɪf / (AmE), / brɛd ˈbʌtəd fə laɪf / (BrE)
to be financially secure or well-provided for throughout one's life
to be financially secure for life

Examples

Thanks to his inheritance, he has his bread buttered for life.
Winning the lottery meant she now had her bread buttered for life.
After selling his company, Mark made sure his bread buttered for life.

Synset Examples

He feels secure because he has his bread buttered for life.
With that trust fund, he has his bread buttered for life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: set for life - to be in a position of lasting financial security
  • Synonym: financially secure - having sufficient money to live without worry
