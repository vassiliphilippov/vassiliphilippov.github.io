famous for being famous

1. ADJ

/ ˈfeɪməs fər ˈbiːɪŋ ˈfeɪməs / (AmE), / ˈfeɪməs fə ˈbiːɪŋ ˈfeɪməs / (BrE)
used to describe a person who is widely known primarily because of their celebrity status, rather than for any notable achievements or talents
well-known only for celebrity status

Examples

Some reality TV stars are famous for being famous rather than for any particular skill.
The tabloids love to feature people who are famous for being famous.
He built his career by being famous for being famous, not through talent or hard work.

Synset Examples
