average bear

1. NOUN

/ ˈæv.ɚ.ɪdʒ bɛr / (AmE), / ˈæv.ər.ɪdʒ beə / (BrE)
an ordinary or typical person; a person with average abilities or qualities
an ordinary or typical person
informal

Examples

Though he works hard, he's just an average bear trying to make ends meet.
I never claimed to be smarter than the average bear.
Like the average bear, I enjoy spending weekends at home with my family.

Synset Examples

I'm not any smarter than the average bear.
Just like the average bear, I enjoy simple pleasures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ordinary person - a person who is not exceptional or remarkable; common individual
  • Antonym: exceptional - outstanding or much better than average

Etymology

Popularized by the cartoon character Yogi Bear, who often said, "I'm smarter than the average bear."
