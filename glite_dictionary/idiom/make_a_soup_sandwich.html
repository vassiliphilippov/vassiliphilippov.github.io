to make a soup sandwich

1. VERB

/ meɪk ə suːp ˈsændwɪtʃ / (AmE), / meɪk ə suːp ˈsændwɪtʃ / (BrE)
to attempt something that is extremely difficult or inevitably turns out messy and disorganized
attempt the impossible or messy
informal

Examples

Trying to manage that team was like making a soup sandwich.
His attempt to fix the old computer ended up making a soup sandwich.
They compared planning the festival to making a soup sandwich because of all the unexpected problems.

Synset Examples

Trying to organize that event was like making a soup sandwich.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herding cats - trying to control or organize the uncontrollable
  • Synonym: impossible - not able to be done
  • Antonym: easy as pie - very easy to do
