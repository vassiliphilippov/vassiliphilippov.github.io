cream of the crop

1. NOUN

/ kriːm əv ðə krɑp / (AmE), / kriːm əv ðə krɒp / (BrE)
the very best people or things in a particular group
the very best in a group

Examples

Only the cream of the crop were chosen for the prestigious scholarship program.
The company is known for hiring the cream of the crop from top universities.
Winning this award proves she is truly the cream of the crop in her field.

Synset Examples

It's time to cream the crop in this talent search.
She always hires the cream of the crop for her team.
His performance in the competition proved he was the cream of the crop.
This university attracts the cream of the crop from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: best - of the highest quality or excellence
  • Synonym: elite - a select group that is superior in terms of ability or qualities

Etymology

From the traditional notion that the 'cream' rising to the top of milk is the best part.
