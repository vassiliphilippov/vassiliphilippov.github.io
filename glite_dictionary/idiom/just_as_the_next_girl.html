just as the next girl

1. X

/ dʒʌst æz ðə nɛkst ɡɜrl / (AmE), / dʒʌst æz ðə nɛkst ɡɜːl / (BrE)
used to indicate that one's qualities, abilities, or desires are equal to those of an average person, especially a woman
no different from anyone else (woman)

Examples

I love chocolate just as the next girl, but I try to eat it in moderation.
She enjoys traveling just as the next girl does, whenever she gets the chance.
I'm nervous about public speaking just as the next girl, but I know it's important.

Synset Examples

I can cook, just as the next girl.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: just as the next man - indicates equality or similarity to others (man)
  • Synonym: just as the next guy - indicates equality or similarity to others (guy)
