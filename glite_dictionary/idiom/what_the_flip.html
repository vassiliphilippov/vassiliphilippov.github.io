what the flip

1. INTJ

/ wʌt ðə flɪp / (AmE), / wɒt ðə flɪp / (BrE)
used to express surprise or disbelief, often as a milder substitute for a stronger expletive
expression of surprise or disbelief
informal

Examples

What the flip is going on here?
You spent all that money on shoes? What the flip!
He just walked out in the middle of the meeting—what the flip?

Synset Examples

He quit his job without notice; what the flip?
You forgot to bring the tickets? What the flip!
She dyed her hair purple? What the flip!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: what the heck - used to express mild surprise or disbelief
  • Degree Higher: what the hell - expression of surprise or disbelief, slightly stronger

Etymology

Formed as a euphemistic variant of "what the hell" or "what the fuck."
