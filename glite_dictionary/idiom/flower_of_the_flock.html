flower of the flock

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚ əv ðə flɑk / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə əv ðə flɒk / (BrE)
the best or most admirable person in a particular group
the best person in a group

Examples

Of all the students in her class, Jessica was considered the flower of the flock.
Many believe their captain is truly the flower of the flock on the team.
His generosity and leadership made him the flower of the flock in his community.

Synset Examples

In our team, she's the flower of the flock.
Despite challenges, he remained the flower of the flock.
Her talent makes her the flower of the flock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cream of the crop - the best of a group, people or things considered to be the very best
