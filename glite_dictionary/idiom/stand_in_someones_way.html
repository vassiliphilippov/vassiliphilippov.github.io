to stand in someone's way

1. VERB

/ stænd ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz weɪ / (AmE), / stænd ɪn ˈsʌmwənz weɪ / (BrE)
to prevent or hinder someone from achieving their aims or progressing
obstruct or hinder someone's progress

Examples

No one will stand in his way now that he has made up his mind.
You shouldn't let fear stand in your way when pursuing your dreams.
Her parents never stood in her way when she decided to travel abroad.

Synset Examples

Don't let anyone stand in your way of success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to hold back - to prevent progress or development
  • Synonym: block - to physically or figuratively prevent movement or progress
  • Antonym: help - to make it easier or possible for someone to do something
