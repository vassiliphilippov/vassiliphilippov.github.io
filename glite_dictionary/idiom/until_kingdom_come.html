until kingdom come

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈtɪl ˈkɪŋdəm kʌm / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɪl ˈkɪŋdəm kʌm / (BrE)
for a very long time; forever or indefinitely
forever; indefinitely

Examples

We could argue until kingdom come, but it won't change anything.
They promised to keep the secret until kingdom come.
He said he would love her until kingdom come.

Synset Examples

They'll be arguing about this until kingdom come.
I’ll support you until kingdom come.
You can argue until kingdom come, but I won't change my mind.
We'll wait until kingdom come if necessary.
He promised to keep the secret until kingdom come.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forever - for all time; eternally
  • Synonym: always - at all times; on all occasions

Etymology

Refers to the biblical phrase 'kingdom come', meaning the time of the final judgment or the end of the world.
