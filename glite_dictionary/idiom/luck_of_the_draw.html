luck of the draw

1. NOUN

/ lʌk əv ðə drɔ / (AmE), / lʌk əv ðə drɔː / (BrE)
used to say that the outcome or result depends entirely on chance or random selection
outcome determined by chance

Examples

It was just the luck of the draw that our team got to play first.
Choosing seats at the concert is the luck of the draw—you get what’s available.
She became the company’s thousandth customer by the luck of the draw.

Synset Examples

We ended up in that hotel by the luck of the draw.
In poker, sometimes it's all about the luck of the draw.
Winning that prize was just the luck of the draw.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chance - the occurrence or outcome of events without apparent cause
  • Synonym: random - made, done, or happening without method or conscious decision
