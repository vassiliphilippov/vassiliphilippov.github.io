fit to wake the dead

1. ADJ

/ fɪt tə weɪk ðə dɛd / (AmE), / fɪt tə weɪk ðə dɛd / (BrE)
extremely loud or disruptive; so loud that it could awaken someone from the dead
extremely loud or disruptive

Examples

The thunder was fit to wake the dead, shaking the whole neighborhood.
When she shouted across the room, her voice was fit to wake the dead.
The alarm clock rang fit to wake the dead every morning at six.

Synset Examples

His snoring was fit to wake the dead all night.
The music was so loud, it was fit to wake the dead.
The party was so lively, it was fit to wake the dead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deafening - so loud as to make hearing impossible
  • Synonym: ear-splitting - extremely or painfully loud
  • Antonym: quiet - making little or no noise
