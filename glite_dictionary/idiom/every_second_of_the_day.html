every second of the day

1. ADV

/ ˈɛvri ˈsɛkənd əv ðə deɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛvri ˈsɛkənd ɒv ðə deɪ / (BrE)
all the time; constantly or without interruption
constantly; all the time

Examples

She thinks about her childhood every second of the day.
The phone kept ringing every second of the day while I was trying to relax.
He seemed to be working every second of the day to finish the project.

Synset Examples

He worries about his grades every second of the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: constantly - happening all the time or very often
  • Antonym: occasionally - happening from time to time, not often
