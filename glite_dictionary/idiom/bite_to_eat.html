bite to eat

1. NOUN

/ baɪt tə it / (AmE), / baɪt tʊ iːt / (BrE)
a small meal or light snack, especially when taken quickly or informally
a small meal or snack

Examples

Let's stop at a café and get a bite to eat before the show.
After the hike, everyone was hungry for a bite to eat.
She always grabs a quick bite to eat between meetings.

Synset Examples
