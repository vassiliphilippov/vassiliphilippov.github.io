for sure

1. ADV

/ fər ʃʊr / (AmE), / fə ʃɔː / (BrE)
Without any doubt; certainly.
without doubt; certainly

Examples

I'll be at the meeting tomorrow for sure, so save me a seat.
No one knows for sure how the accident happened.
She is coming to the party for sure, and she's bringing a friend.
