jaw in lap

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑː ɪn læp / (AmE), / dʒɔː ɪn læp / (BrE)
in a state of great shock or astonishment, often with an open mouth due to surprise
amazed or in shock

Examples

When she saw the final bill, her jaw in lap expression said it all.
The magician's last trick left every child's jaw in lap.
After hearing the unexpected announcement, I sat there with my jaw in lap.

Synset Examples

She heard the news and sat with her jaw in her lap.
The surprise party left him with his jaw in his lap.
He watched the magic show with his jaw in his lap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jaw drop - to be extremely surprised or shocked
