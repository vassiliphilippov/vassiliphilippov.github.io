to thank one's lucky stars

1. VERB

/ θæŋk wʌnz ˈlʌki stɑrz / (AmE), / θæŋk wʌnz ˈlʌki stɑːz / (BrE)
to be very grateful for good luck or a fortunate event
be very grateful for good luck

Examples

I thank my lucky stars every day that I have such a supportive family.
You should thank your lucky stars the train was running late, or you would have missed it.
She thanked her lucky stars when the interview went better than expected.

Synset Examples

I thank my lucky stars for finding my wallet.
She thanks her lucky stars for her supportive friends.
After the accident, I thanked my lucky stars I was unharmed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be grateful - to feel or show thanks for something good
  • Antonym: to take for granted - to fail to appreciate something or someone

Etymology

Refers to the ancient belief that a person's fate is influenced by the stars; 'lucky stars' means favorable circumstances.
