within living memory

1. ADV

/ wɪˈðɪn ˈlɪvɪŋ ˈmɛməri / (AmE), / wɪˈðɪn ˈlɪvɪŋ ˈmɛməri / (BrE)
at a time during which some or all people currently alive can remember; in recent or contemporary history
in the time people can still remember

Examples

This has been the coldest winter within living memory.
No athlete has broken the record within living memory.
Such a political shift has not happened within living memory.

Synset Examples

This event is the worst disaster within living memory.
He is the oldest person in town within living memory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in recent memory - recently enough that people still remember it
