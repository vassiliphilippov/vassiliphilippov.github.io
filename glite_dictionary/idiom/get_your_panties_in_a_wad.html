to get your panties in a wad

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt jɔɹ ˈpæn.tiz ɪn ə wɑd / (AmE), / ɡɛt jɔː ˈpæn.tiz ɪn ə wɒd / (BrE)
to become overly upset or agitated, especially about something minor or trivial
become unnecessarily upset
informal american

Examples

There's no need to get your panties in a wad about a little mistake.
She tends to get her panties in a wad over the smallest issues at work.
He told me not to get my panties in a wad just because they were late.

Synset Examples

No need to get your panties in a wad about the delay.
Try not to get your panties in a wad over the minor setback.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to make a mountain out of a molehill - to exaggerate a minor problem
  • Synonym: lose your cool - to lose one's composure; become angry or agitated
