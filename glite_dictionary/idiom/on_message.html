on message

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ˈmɛsɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɒn ˈmɛsɪdʒ / (BrE)
speaking or acting in a way that is consistent with the official or intended message, especially in public communication or politics
consistent with an intended message

Examples

The spokesperson remained on message throughout the entire press conference.
It's crucial for every team member to stay on message during the campaign.
She trained the staff to be on message in all customer interactions.

Synset Examples

He delivered the speech, ensuring he was always on message.
All our communications must be on message to avoid confusion.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: off message - not consistent with the intended or official message
