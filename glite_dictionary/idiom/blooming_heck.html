blooming heck

1. INTJ

/ ˈbluːmɪŋ hɛk / (AmE), / ˈbluːmɪŋ hɛk / (BrE)
An exclamation used to express surprise or annoyance, considered a milder alternative to stronger phrases.
mild exclamation of surprise or annoyance
informal british

Examples

Blooming heck, that car almost hit the curb!
He shouted 'blooming heck!' when the results were announced.
Blooming heck, I can't believe how much homework we have.

Synset Examples

He dropped his phone and yelled, "Blooming heck!"
When she saw the mess, she exclaimed, "Blooming heck!"
Blooming heck, that was a close call!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blinking heck - another mild exclamation of surprise or annoyance
  • Degree Higher: bloody hell - a stronger British exclamation expressing surprise or annoyance

Etymology

'Blooming' is a euphemistic intensifier from British English, used in place of stronger words.
