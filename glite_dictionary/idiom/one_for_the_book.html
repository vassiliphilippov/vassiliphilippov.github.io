one for the book

1. NOUN

/ wʌn fər ðə bʊk / (AmE), / wʌn fə ðə bʊk / (BrE)
an event or occurrence that is especially memorable or remarkable; worth remembering
memorable or noteworthy event

Examples

Our victory last night was truly one for the book.
When the power went out during the wedding, it became one for the book.
That surprise party was definitely one for the book.

Synset Examples

This event is one for the book, truly memorable.
