stuck in the weeds

1. ADJ

/ stʌk ɪn ðə wiːdz / (AmE), / stʌk ɪn ðə wiːdz / (BrE)
Focusing too much on small details, especially so that you lose sight of the main issue or objective.
overly focused on minor details

Examples

We spent most of the afternoon stuck in the weeds and made little progress on the big decisions.
During the project planning, the team got stuck in the weeds and never addressed the overall strategy.
Try not to get stuck in the weeds—let's focus on the main objectives first.

Synset Examples

We got stuck in the weeds during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to bogged down - to become encumbered with details or difficulties
