honest to a fault

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑnəst tu ə ˈfɔlt / (AmE), / ˈɒnɪst tuː ə ˈfɔːlt / (BrE)
excessively honest, even to the point where it may cause problems or difficulties
excessively honest, to a problematic degree

Examples

She is honest to a fault, never hesitating to tell people the complete truth.
His honest to a fault nature sometimes offends others who are used to more tact.
Being honest to a fault can be admirable, but it may also create awkward situations.

Synset Examples

He is honest to a fault, sometimes brutally so.
