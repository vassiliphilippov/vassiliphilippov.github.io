word for word

1. ADV

/ wɝd fər wɝd / (AmE), / wɜːd fə wɜːd / (BrE)
using exactly the same words; exactly as originally stated or written
in exactly the same words

Examples

She copied his statement word for word in her notes.
The article quoted the interviewee word for word, without any changes.
He memorized the poem word for word for the recital.

Synset Examples

he repeated her remarks verbatim

Related Terms

  • Synonym: verbatim - word for word; in exactly the same words
