dead asleep

1. ADJ

/ dɛd əˈslip / (AmE), / dɛd əˈsliːp / (BrE)
in a very deep or sound sleep; completely asleep
in a very deep sleep

Examples

I tried to wake her, but she was dead asleep and didn't stir at all.
He was dead asleep when the fire alarm went off in the building.
After a long day of hiking, the kids were dead asleep by eight o'clock.

Synset Examples

She was dead asleep when the phone rang.
He fell dead asleep on the couch after dinner.
The baby was dead asleep despite the noise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fast asleep - sleeping soundly and deeply
