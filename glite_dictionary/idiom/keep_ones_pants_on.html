to keep one's pants on

1. VERB

/ kip wʌnz pænts ɑn / (AmE), / kiːp wʌnz pænts ɒn / (BrE)
(informal) To remain calm and patient; to avoid getting overly excited, impatient, or agitated.
remain calm and patient (idiom)
informal

If you just keep your pants on, we'll have dinner ready in five minutes.
He was eager to start, but I told him to keep his pants on and wait for everyone else.
I wish she'd keep her pants on instead of getting so worked up over small things.
