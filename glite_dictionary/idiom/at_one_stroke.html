at one stroke

1. ADV

/ ət wʌn stroʊk / (AmE), / ət wʌn strəʊk / (BrE)
all at once, with a single action or effort
all at once; with one action

Examples

With a clever idea, she solved the complicated puzzle at one stroke.
The government reduced bureaucracy at one stroke with the new policy.
He erased months of hard work at one stroke by deleting the file.

Synset Examples

He made a fortune at one stroke with his investment.
At one stroke, he changed the company's future.
She solved the problem at one stroke, impressing everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in one fell swoop - with a single decisive action
