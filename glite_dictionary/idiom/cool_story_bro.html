cool story bro

1. X

/ kul ˈstɔɹi broʊ / (AmE), / kuːl ˈstɔːri brəʊ / (BrE)
Used sarcastically to dismiss a story or comment considered boring, irrelevant, or unimpressive.
sarcastic dismissive remark
informal slang

Examples

She kept talking about her high school achievements, and I just said, 'cool story bro.'
After his long explanation, Mark replied with a sarcastic 'cool story bro.'
If someone shares a pointless detail, you might respond with 'cool story bro.'

Synset Examples

He shared his weekend plans, and I just replied, 'Cool story bro.'
When he bragged about his car, I thought, 'Cool story bro.'
She told a boring tale, and I couldn't help but say, 'Cool story bro.'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whatever - indifferent or dismissive response
  • Synonym: who cares - expression of indifference or lack of interest
  • Antonym: fascinating - genuine expression of strong interest; opposite in tone
