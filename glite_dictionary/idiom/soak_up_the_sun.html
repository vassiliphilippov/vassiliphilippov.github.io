to soak up the sun

1. VERB

/ soʊk ʌp ðə sʌn / (AmE), / səʊk ʌp ðə sʌn / (BrE)
to spend time enjoying the sunlight, especially by being outside in warm, sunny weather
enjoy the sunlight outdoors

Examples

After a long winter, everyone was eager to soak up the sun in the park.
She lay by the pool to soak up the sun with a good book.
We spent the afternoon soaking up the sun on the rooftop terrace.

Synset Examples

I love to soak up the sun at the beach.
We spent our vacation soaking up the sun in Mexicoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sunbathe - to sit or lie in the sun to tan one's skin
