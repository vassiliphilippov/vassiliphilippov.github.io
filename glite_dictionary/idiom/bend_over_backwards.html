to bend over backwards

1. VERB

/ bɛnd oʊvər ˈbækwɚdz / (AmE), / bɛnd əʊvə ˈbækwədz / (BrE)
to make a great effort to help or please someone, often more than is expected or necessary
make a great effort to help or please

Examples

The staff always bend over backwards to make guests feel at home.
Even though it wasn't her responsibility, she bent over backwards to help me finish the project.
He is bending over backwards to impress his new boss.

Synset Examples

She falls over backwards when she sees her mother-in-law

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to go out of one's way - to make a special effort to do something
