up and away

1. X

/ ʌp ənd əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ʌp ənd əˈweɪ / (BrE)
(of a project, journey, etc.) Successfully launched and progressing, or having departed or begun energetically.
successfully started or launched; departed

Examples

The rocket was up and away within seconds of the countdown ending.
With our plans finalized, we're finally up and away on this new venture.
As soon as the team received approval, the project was up and away.

Synset Examples

With the new launch, the project is up and away.
Once the plane took off, it was up and away.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to take off - to begin flight or start successfully
  • Synonym: to set out - to begin a journey or undertaking
