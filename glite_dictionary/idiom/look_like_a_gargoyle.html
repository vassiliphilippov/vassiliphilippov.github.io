to look like a gargoyle

1. VERB

/ lʊk laɪk ə ˈɡɑrˌɡɔɪl / (AmE), / lʊk laɪk ə ˈɡɑːɡɔɪl / (BrE)
to have an appearance that is particularly ugly or grotesque, often in a striking or dramatic way
appear especially ugly or grotesque

Examples

After pulling an all-nighter, he looked like a gargoyle in the meeting.
The old man hunched over on the bench, looking like a gargoyle watching over the park.
With her wild hair and scowl, she looked like a gargoyle guarding the entrance.

Synset Examples

He looked like a gargoyle perched on the roof.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grotesque - comically or repulsively ugly or distorted
