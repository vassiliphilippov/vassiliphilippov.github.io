the cavalry showed up

1. X

/ ðə ˈkælvəri ʃoʊd ʌp / (AmE), / ðə ˈkælvəri ʃəʊd ʌp / (BrE)
Used to say that help or rescue has arrived, often unexpectedly or at a critical moment.
help arrived unexpectedly

Examples

Just when we thought we'd lost, the cavalry showed up and turned things around.
I was struggling with the project, but the cavalry showed up and helped me finish on time.
Don't worry, if it gets too tough, the cavalry will show up sooner or later.

Synset Examples

The team rallied when the cavalry showed up to help.

Etymology

Alludes to cavalry coming to the rescue in battles or Western films.
