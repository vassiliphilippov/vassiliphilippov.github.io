he who smelt it dealt it

1. X

/ hi hu smɛlt ɪt dɛlt ɪt / (AmE), / hiː huː smɛlt ɪt dɛlt ɪt / (BrE)
used humorously to suggest that the person who first draws attention to an embarrassing situation, such as a bad smell, is likely to have caused it
humorous phrase blaming the first to notice for causing something
informal

Examples

When the unpleasant odor filled the room, Sam quickly said, 'he who smelt it dealt it,' making everyone laugh.
As soon as someone noticed the mess, Lisa blurted out 'he who smelt it dealt it.'
In school, whenever someone accused another of passing gas, the response was always 'he who smelt it dealt it.'

Synset Examples

In the group, everyone laughed, saying, 'He who smelt it dealt it!'
During the car ride, they all pointed fingers, claiming, 'He who smelt it dealt it.'
