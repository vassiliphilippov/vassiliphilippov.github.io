something is wrong

1. X

/ ˈsʌmθɪŋ ɪz rɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌmθɪŋ ɪz rɒŋ / (BrE)
used to say that a situation or thing is not correct, or that there is a problem or fault
there is a problem or fault

Examples

I can tell something is wrong by the way you're acting today.
Whenever the computer makes that noise, you know something is wrong.
If you feel something is wrong with your car, you should get it checked out.

Synset Examples

If you think that's right, then something's alwrong.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: something is amiss - used to say something is not quite right
  • Synonym: something is off - used to say there is something not as expected or correct
