for the love of me

1. X

/ fər ðə ləv əv mi / (AmE), / fə ðə lʌv əv miː / (BrE)
(used with a negative) despite great effort or desire; no matter how hard one tries or wants to.
despite one's best efforts

Examples

I can't, for the love of me, remember where I put my keys.
She couldn't, for the love of me, understand what the professor was explaining.
Why he quit his job, I just can't, for the love of me, figure out.

Synset Examples

He won’t help, for the love of me, even if I ask nicely.
I can't, for the love of me, figure out this puzzle.
For the love of me, I just can't remember his name.
