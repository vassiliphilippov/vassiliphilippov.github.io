to trans the gay away

1. VERB

/ trænz ðə ɡeɪ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / tranz ðə ɡeɪ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to attempt to change someone's sexual orientation or dismiss LGBTQ+ identities, often by encouraging or enforcing gender transition as a supposed solution to being gay or queer
attempt to 'fix' queerness by enforcing gender transition
informal slang
