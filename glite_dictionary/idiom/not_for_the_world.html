not for the world

1. ADV

/ nɑt fər ðə wɝld / (AmE), / nɒt fə ðə wɜːld / (BrE)
used to emphasize that someone would never do something, whatever might happen; absolutely not under any circumstances
absolutely not; under no circumstances

Examples

I wouldn't sell this painting not for the world.
Not for the world would I lie to you about something so important.
She said she wouldn't miss her daughter's recital not for the world.

Synset Examples

I wouldn't miss that concert, not for the world.
He wouldn't trade his job, not for the world.
She wouldn't betray her friend, not for the world.
