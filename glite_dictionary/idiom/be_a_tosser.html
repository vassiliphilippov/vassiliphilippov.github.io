to be a tosser

1. VERB

/ bi ə ˈtɑsər / (AmE), / biː ə ˈtɒsə / (BrE)
(informal, British, slang) To act in a foolish or stupid manner; to behave like an idiot.
act foolishly or stupidly (Brit. slang)
informal slang british

Examples

Stop trying to show off and don't be a tosser in front of your friends.
You don't have to be a tosser just because you lost the game.
Jane told Sam not to be a tosser when he started making rude jokes.

Synset Examples

Don't be a tosser; just admit your mistake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - A person who acts unwisely or imprudently
  • Synonym: idiot - A stupid person; someone acting without sense

Etymology

From British slang 'tosser' (foolish or contemptible person), related to the vulgar verb 'to toss'.
