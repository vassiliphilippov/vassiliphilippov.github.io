to convulse with laughter

1. VERB

/ kənˈvʌls wɪð ˈlæftɚ / (AmE), / kənˈvʌls wɪð ˈlɑːftə / (BrE)
to laugh so hard that one's body shakes uncontrollably
laugh uncontrollably

Examples

Her story was so funny that it made us all convulse with laughter during dinner.
The children convulsed with laughter as the clown performed his tricks.
He often makes his friends convulse with laughter with his witty remarks.

Synset Examples

The audience began to convulse with laughter at the comedian's jokes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to crack up - to suddenly start laughing a lot
  • Synonym: to burst out laughing - to begin to laugh loudly and suddenly
