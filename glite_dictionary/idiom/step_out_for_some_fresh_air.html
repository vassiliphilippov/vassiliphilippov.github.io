to step out for some fresh air

1. VERB

/ stɛp aʊt fər səm frɛʃ ɛr / (AmE), / stɛp aʊt fə səm frɛʃ eə / (BrE)
to briefly go outside, usually to take a break and get fresh air
go outside briefly for a break

Examples

I need to step out for some fresh air after sitting at my desk all morning.
She told her colleagues she would step out for some fresh air during the meeting break.
Whenever things get overwhelming, he likes to step out for some fresh air to clear his mind.

Synset Examples

She decided to step out for some fresh air.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to step out - to leave a place briefly or for a short time
