round the clock

1. ADV

/ raʊnd ðə klɑk / (AmE), / raʊnd ðə klɒk / (BrE)
continuously, throughout the entire day and night, without stopping
continuously, day and night

Examples

The hospital provides care round the clock for its patients.
Security guards monitor the building round the clock to ensure safety.
The team worked round the clock to finish the project on time.
