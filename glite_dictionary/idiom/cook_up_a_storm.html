to cook up a storm

1. VERB

/ kʊk ʌp ə stɔɹm / (AmE), / kʊk ʌp ə stɔːm / (BrE)
to prepare a large amount of food, especially with enthusiasm or energy
prepare a lot of food energetically

Examples

Whenever his friends visit, he loves to cook up a storm in the kitchen.
After winning the competition, she decided to cook up a storm for her family.
The chef promised to cook up a storm at the charity event.

Synset Examples

He loves to cook up a storm for holiday gatherings.
She can cook up a storm when guests arrive.
They cooked up a storm, filling the house with delicious aromas.
