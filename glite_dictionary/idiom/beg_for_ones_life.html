to beg for one's life

1. VERB

/ bɛɡ fər wʌnz laɪf / (AmE), / bɛɡ fə wʌnz laɪf / (BrE)
to plead desperately with someone not to kill or seriously harm you
plead to avoid being killed

Examples

When the soldiers entered the village, some residents tried to beg for their lives to avoid execution.
Caught by the kidnapper, he fell to his knees and started to beg for his life.
She was so terrified during the home invasion that she could only beg for her life.

Synset Examples

In the movie, she had to beg for her life.
He had to beg for his life after the robbery.
The villain began to beg for his life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to plead - to make an emotional or earnest appeal
  • Synonym: to implore - to beg earnestly or desperately
