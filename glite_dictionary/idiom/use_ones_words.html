to use one's words

1. VERB

/ juz wʌnz wɝdz / (AmE), / juːz wʌnz wɜːdz / (BrE)
to express one's feelings, needs, or thoughts verbally instead of through actions, gestures, or behavior
express feelings or needs verbally

Examples

Whenever he feels upset, his mother encourages him to use his words instead of crying.
Teachers often ask students to use their words to solve conflicts in the classroom.
It's important for young children to use their words when they need help or feel frustrated.

Synset Examples

The teacher reminded the children to use their words.
