to laugh one's head off

1. VERB

/ læf wʌnz hɛd ɔf / (AmE), / lɑːf wʌnz hɛd ɒf / (BrE)
to laugh very loudly or uncontrollably; to find something extremely funny
laugh very loudly or uncontrollably

Examples

We laughed our heads off at the comedian's performance last night.
The kids laughed their heads off at the silly movie scene.
She always manages to make me laugh my head off with her stories.

Synset Examples

We laughed our heads off at the comedian's routine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to crack up - to suddenly start laughing a lot
  • Synonym: to split one's sides - to laugh extremely hard
