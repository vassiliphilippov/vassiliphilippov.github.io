weather for ducks

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛðɚ fər dʌks / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə fə dʌks / (BrE)
used humorously to describe very rainy weather, implying that only ducks would enjoy it
very rainy weather (idiom)

Examples

It's been raining all day—definitely weather for ducks out there.
Looking out the window, she sighed at the weather for ducks before her morning commute.
Bring an umbrella—it's weather for ducks this afternoon!

Synset Examples

It’s pouring outside; nice weather for ducks!
With all this rain, it’s nice weather for ducks.
