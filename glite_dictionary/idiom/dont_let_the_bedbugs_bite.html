don't let the bedbugs bite

1. X

/ doʊnt lɛt ðə ˈbɛdˌbʌɡz baɪt / (AmE), / dəʊnt lɛt ðə ˈbɛdˌbʌɡz baɪt / (BrE)
A playful phrase said to someone before they go to sleep, wishing them good dreams and a restful night.
playful bedtime wish for good sleep

Examples

She kissed her daughter goodnight and whispered, "Don't let the bedbugs bite."
As he left the room, he called out, "Don't let the bedbugs bite!" with a smile.
Every night, Grandma would say, "Sleep tight, don't let the bedbugs bite."

Synset Examples

As she tucked him in, she whispered, 'Don't let the bedbugs bite.'
Sleep tight, and don't let the bedbugs bite!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleep tight - wish for a good night's sleep
