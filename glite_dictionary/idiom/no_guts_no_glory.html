no guts, no glory

1. X

/ noʊ ɡʌts noʊ ˈɡlɔri / (AmE), / nəʊ ɡʌts nəʊ ˈɡlɔːri / (BrE)
Success or rewards cannot be achieved without taking risks or showing courage.
bravery is needed for success

Examples

He accepted the challenge, reminding himself no guts, no glory.
The coach told the team, 'No guts, no glory!' before the big game.
Starting your own business can be scary, but no guts, no glory.

Synset Examples

She took the risk because no guts, no glory.
If you want to succeed, remember: no guts, no glory.
In business, it's often no guts, no glory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fortune favors the bold - Fortune rewards those who take risks
