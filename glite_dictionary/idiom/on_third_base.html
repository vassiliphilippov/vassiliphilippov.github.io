on third base

1. X

/ ɑn ˈθɜrd beɪs / (AmE), / ɒn ˈθɜːd beɪs / (BrE)
close to achieving a particular goal or stage, especially sexual intimacy, in a romantic relationship
close to romantic or sexual intimacy
informal

Examples

By the end of the movie, they were clearly on third base.
She laughed when her friend joked that he was already on third base after one date.
Some people think it's old-fashioned to talk about relationships in terms of being on third base.

Synset Examples

They were definitely on third base by the end of the night.
He felt they were already on third base after their first date.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: on first base - at the initial stage of romantic or sexual intimacy
  • Degree Lower: on second base - having reached a further stage of intimacy, beyond first base
  • Degree Higher: home run - having completely achieved sexual intercourse or success in romance

Etymology

From baseball, where reaching third base is a step away from scoring a run; figuratively used for progress in courtship or intimacy.
