shit rolls downhill

1. X

/ ʃɪt roʊlz ˈdaʊnhɪl / (AmE), / ʃɪt rəʊlz ˈdaʊnhɪl / (BrE)
Problems, blame, or responsibilities are often passed from those in authority down to subordinates within an organization.
problems or blame are passed down in hierarchy
informal vulgar

Examples

In large corporations, shit rolls downhill, so employees often suffer the consequences of management's mistakes.
Whenever there’s a big screw-up, shit rolls downhill and the lowest-ranking staff take the blame.
It’s a classic case of shit rolls downhill in government offices, with each department blaming the one below.

Synset Examples

If there's blame to assign, shit rolls downhill in this company.
In our office, shit rolls downhill from management to us.
