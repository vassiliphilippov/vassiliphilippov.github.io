in plain sight

1. ADV

/ ɪn pleɪn saɪt / (AmE), / ɪn pleɪn saɪt / (BrE)
in a place where something can be easily seen; not hidden or concealed
easily visible; not hidden

Examples

He left the spare key in plain sight on the windowsill.
The answer was in plain sight the whole time, but nobody noticed it.
She keeps her jewelry in plain sight on her dresser.

Synset Examples

He hid the gift in plain sight to surprise her.
Leaving valuables in plain sight makes them theftable.
The best place to hide in plain sight is among a crowd.
The treasure was hidden in plain sight, right under our noses.
The treasure was hidden, but it was in plain sight.
The keys were left in plain sight on the table.
The documents were left in plain view on the desk.
The car was parked in plain view of the entrance.
The artwork was displayed in plain view for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in plain view - in a position where something is easily seen; openly visible
