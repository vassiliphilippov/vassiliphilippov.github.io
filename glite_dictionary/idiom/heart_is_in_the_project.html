heart is in the project

1. X

/ hɑɹt ɪz ɪn ðə ˈprɑdʒɛkt / (AmE), / hɑːt ɪz ɪn ðə ˈprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
used to say that someone is emotionally committed or truly cares about what they are doing
emotionally committed to something

Examples

You can tell her heart is in the project by how much effort she puts in.
It's obvious that his heart is in the project from the passion he brings every day.
If your heart is in the project, you'll go the extra mile to make it succeed.

Synset Examples

You can tell someone's heart is in the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heart is in it - emotionally committed to something
  • Antonym: heart is not in it - not emotionally committed
