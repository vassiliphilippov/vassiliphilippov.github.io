all heart

1. ADJ

/ ɔl hɑːrt / (AmE), / ɔːl hɑːt / (BrE)
(of a person) full of kindness, compassion, and dedication to others.
full of compassion and dedication

Examples

Everyone in the community agrees that Maria is all heart when helping those in need.
Despite the criticism, his all heart attitude inspired the whole team to keep going.
You can always count on Julie; she’s all heart and always ready to help.

Synset Examples

The charity event was organized by an all heart team.
She is all heart when it comes to helping others.
His all heart approach won him many friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kind-hearted - having a kind and sympathetic nature
  • Antonym: cold-hearted - lacking compassion or kindness
