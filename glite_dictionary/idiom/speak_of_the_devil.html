to speak of the devil

1. VERB

/ spiːk əv ðə ˈdɛvəl / (AmE), / spiːk əv ðə ˈdɛvəl / (BrE)
used when a person being talked about appears unexpectedly at that moment
when someone appears just as they're mentioned

Examples

We were just talking about Peter when he walked in—speak of the devil!
Speak of the devil, here comes Sarah right now.
We were discussing our new boss, and speak of the devil, she called me.
