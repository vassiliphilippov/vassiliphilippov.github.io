to make an ass out of you and me

1. VERB

/ meɪk ən æs aʊt ʌv ju ənd mi / (AmE), / meɪk ən æs aʊt ɒv juː ənd miː / (BrE)
to make a foolish mistake or error in judgment by making assumptions, typically resulting in embarrassment or misunderstanding
make a foolish error by assuming

Examples

Remember, if you don't know all the facts, you might make an ass out of you and me.
He warned us not to make an ass out of you and me by assuming the meeting was cancelled.
When we didn't check the details, we really did make an ass out of you and me.

Synset Examples

Remember, when you assume, you make an ass out of you and me.
She learned that when you assume, you make an ass out of you and me.
Don't forget, when you assume, you make an ass out of you and me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to jump to conclusions - to make a decision or judgment too quickly
