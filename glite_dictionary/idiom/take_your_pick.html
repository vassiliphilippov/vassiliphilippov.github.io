to take your pick

1. VERB

/ teɪk jɔr pɪk / (AmE), / teɪk jɔː pɪk / (BrE)
choose freely from a range of available options
choose freely from available options

Examples

There are plenty of sandwiches to eat, so just take your pick.
You can take your pick of any book on the shelf.
The festival offers activities for everyone—simply take your pick.
