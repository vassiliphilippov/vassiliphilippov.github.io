hey presto

1. INTJ

/ heɪ ˈprestoʊ / (AmE), / heɪ ˈprestəʊ / (BrE)
Used to indicate that something has happened suddenly or as if by magic.
expression for a sudden, magical result

Examples

She waved her hand over the box and, hey presto, a pigeon flew out.
Just add hot water and, hey presto, your meal is ready!
He pulled the cloth and, hey presto, the table was perfectly set.

Synset Examples

She pressed a button and, hey presto, the lights dimmed.
With a flick of the wand, hey presto, the rabbit appeared!
He mixed the ingredients and, hey presto, a cake appeared.

Etymology

From stage magic; 'presto' comes from Italian meaning 'quickly', used by magicians when performing tricks.
