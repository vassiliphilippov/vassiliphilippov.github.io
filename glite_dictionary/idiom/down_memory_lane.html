down memory lane

1. X

/ daʊn ˈmɛməri leɪn / (AmE), / daʊn ˈmɛməri leɪn / (BrE)
reminiscing or reflecting nostalgically on past experiences or memories
recalling the past nostalgically

Examples

Every year at the reunion, we take a trip down memory lane and share our favorite stories.
Sorting through his childhood toys sent him down memory lane.
Listening to that old song instantly took her down memory lane.

Synset Examples

His stories always lead us down memory lane.
Looking at old photos takes me down memory lane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to reminisce - To talk or think about past experiences, often with pleasure
  • Synonym: nostalgia - A sentimental longing for the past
