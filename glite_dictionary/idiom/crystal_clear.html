crystal clear

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɪstl klɪr / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstl klɪə / (BrE)
extremely easy to see, hear, or understand; obvious or evident.
very clear or obvious

Examples

She made her expectations crystal clear before the meeting began.
His motives were crystal clear to everyone present in the room.
It needs to be crystal clear how the new policy will affect us.

Synset Examples

It was crystal clear that he was unhappy.
The instructions were crystal clear, leaving no room for confusion.
Her explanation was crystal clear and easy to follow.
She admired the crystal clear water of the lake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obvious - easily perceived or understood; clear, self-evident
  • Antonym: ambiguous - having more than one possible meaning; not clear
  • Antonym: unclear - not easy to see, hear, or understand
