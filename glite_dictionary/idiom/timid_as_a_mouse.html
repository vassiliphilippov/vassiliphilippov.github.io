timid as a mouse

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɪmɪd əz ə maʊs / (AmE), / ˈtɪmɪd əz ə maʊs / (BrE)
extremely shy, quiet, or fearful, like a mouse
extremely shy or fearful

Examples

Whenever she speaks to strangers, she becomes timid as a mouse.
At job interviews, he feels timid as a mouse and struggles to answer questions confidently.
The puppy was timid as a mouse when it entered its new home.

Synset Examples

She was as timid as a mouse during the presentation.
The new student was timid as a mouse in class.
He approached the stage, timid as a mouse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shy - nervous and uncomfortable with other people
  • Synonym: timid - lacking in courage or self-confidence, easily frightened
  • Antonym: confident - sure of oneself; having no uncertainty about one's own abilities
