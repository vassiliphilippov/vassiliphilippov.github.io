to fall off a truck

1. VERB

/ fɔl ɑf ə trʌk / (AmE), / fɔːl ɒf ə trʌk / (BrE)
(informal) to be stolen or acquired illegally, especially said humorously or euphemistically about goods.
be stolen or obtained illegally (idiom)
informal

Examples

He bought a diamond watch that supposedly fell off a truck.
Those designer handbags look like they may have fallen off a truck.
I don't ask too many questions when electronics fall off a truck.

Synset Examples

He joked that the new TV must have fallen off a truck.
He sold me a watch that must have fallen off the back of a truck.
They said the furniture they bought fell off a truck.
They offered electronics that clearly fell off the back of a truck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot - stolen goods (slang)
