rock bottom

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈbɑt̬.əm / (AmE), / rɒk ˈbɒt.əm / (BrE)
the lowest or worst possible level in a situation, especially regarding finances, feelings, or circumstances
the lowest possible point

Examples

After losing his job and his home, Tom felt he had hit rock bottom.
The company reached rock bottom before starting to recover financially.
She realized she was at rock bottom when she couldn't afford groceries.

Synset Examples

Her finances reached rock bottom this month.
They found themselves at rock bottom after the divorce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nadir - the lowest point in a person's fortunes or situation
  • Antonym: peak - the highest point or level of something
