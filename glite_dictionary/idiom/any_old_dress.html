any old dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛni oʊld drɛs / (AmE), / ˈeni əʊld drɛs / (BrE)
an ordinary or unremarkable dress, with no special qualities
an ordinary or unremarkable dress

Examples

She didn’t care what she wore; she just threw on any old dress.
Don’t bother dressing up—any old dress will do for this party.
You can’t wear any old dress to a formal event; you need something special.

Synset Examples

She would wear any old dress for the occasion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ordinary dress - a dress with nothing special or distinctive
  • Synonym: any old thing - any ordinary or unremarkable thing
