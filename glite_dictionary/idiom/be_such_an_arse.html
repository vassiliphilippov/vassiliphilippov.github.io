to be such an arse

1. VERB

/ bi sʌtʃ ən ɑrs / (AmE), / biː sʌtʃ ən ɑːs / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish, annoying, or objectionable way
behave foolishly or annoyingly
informal british

Examples

Don't be such an arse about the situation.
Stop being such an arse and help us carry these boxes.
He can't help being such an arse when he's in a bad mood.

Synset Examples
