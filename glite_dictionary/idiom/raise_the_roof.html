to raise the roof

1. VERB

/ ˌreɪz ðə ˈruːf / (AmE), / ˌreɪz ðə ˈruːf / (BrE)
to become very angry and express strong emotions loudly or forcefully
become very angry and loud

Examples

If my dad finds out I broke his watch, he’ll raise the roof.
She raised the roof when she saw the mess in the kitchen.
The manager is sure to raise the roof if the project isn’t finished on time.

Synset Examples

He will raise the roof when he hears this

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to hit the roof - to suddenly become very angry
