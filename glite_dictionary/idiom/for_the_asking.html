for the asking

1. ADV

/ fər ðə ˈæskɪŋ / (AmE), / fə ði ˈɑːskɪŋ / (BrE)
available to anyone who requests it; obtainable simply by asking
easily obtainable by asking

Examples

Information about the program is available for the asking.
You can get a free sample for the asking at the front desk.
Help was there for the asking, but he never reached out.

Synset Examples

advice was free for the asking
