arms of Morpheus

1. NOUN

/ ɑrmz əv ˈmɔrfiəs / (AmE), / ɑːmz əv ˈmɔːfiəs / (BrE)
a literary expression meaning the state of being asleep
state of sleep (literary idiom)

Examples

After reading for a while, he drifted into the arms of Morpheus.
The gentle rain lulled her into the arms of Morpheus.
Long hours of exhaustion finally brought him into the arms of Morpheus.

Synset Examples

She found peace in the arms of Morpheus after the stressful week.

Etymology

From Morpheus, the Greek god of dreams, symbolizing sleep.
