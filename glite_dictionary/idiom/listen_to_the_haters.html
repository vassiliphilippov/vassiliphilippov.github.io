to listen to the haters

1. VERB

/ ˈlɪs.ən tə ðə ˈheɪ.tərz / (AmE), / ˈlɪs.ən tə ðə ˈheɪ.təz / (BrE)
pay attention to critics, especially those expressing negativity or hostility
pay attention to critical or negative people
informal

Examples

She refuses to listen to the haters and follows her own path.
Don't listen to the haters; focus on what makes you happy.
Even when I succeed, some people try to bring me down, but I don't listen to the haters.

Synset Examples

Don't listen to the haters; just stay focused.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ignore the haters - to disregard or not pay attention to critics or negativity
