not worth a Continental

1. ADJ

/ nɑt wɜrθ ə kənˈtɪnəntəl / (AmE), / nɒt wɜːθ ə kənˈtɪnəntəl / (BrE)
Of no value; completely worthless.
completely worthless

Examples

After seeing the results, she said the idea was not worth a Continental.
His excuses were not worth a Continental to the manager.
They realized the contract was not worth a Continental after reading the fine print.

Synset Examples

He realized the project was not worth a Continental.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worthless - having no value or use
  • Antonym: valuable - having great value; worth a lot

Etymology

Refers to Continental currency issued during the American Revolution, which quickly became nearly worthless.
