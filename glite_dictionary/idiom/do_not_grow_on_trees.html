do not grow on trees

1. X

/ duː nɑt ɡroʊ ɑn triːz / (AmE), / duː nɒt ɡrəʊ ɒn triːz / (BrE)
said to emphasize that something is scarce, valuable, or not easily obtained
not easily obtained; scarce

Examples

You should be careful with your money; it doesn't grow on trees.
Remember, good jobs do not grow on trees these days.
She reminded her children that patience doesn't grow on trees.

Synset Examples

Remind me that resources do not grow on trees.
Good opportunities don't grow on trees.
