top of the line

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑp əv ðə laɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɒp əv ðə laɪn / (BrE)
of the highest quality or most expensive in a particular range of products or merchandise.
highest quality or most expensive item

Examples

This laptop is top of the line, so it comes with all the latest features.
They only use top of the line ingredients in their restaurant.
He bought a top of the line smartphone for his work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top-notch - of the highest quality
  • Antonym: bottom of the barrel - the lowest quality in a range
