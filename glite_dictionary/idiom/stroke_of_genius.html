stroke of genius

1. NOUN

/ ˌstroʊk əv ˈdʒiːniəs / (AmE), / ˌstrəʊk əv ˈdʒiːniəs / (BrE)
a sudden brilliant or inspired idea or action
sudden brilliant idea

Examples

Her decision to invest in the new technology was a real stroke of genius.
Hiring a celebrity spokesperson proved to be a stroke of genius for the advertising team.
The chef's unique ingredient choice was a stroke of genius that made the dish unforgettable.

Synset Examples

She decided to strike a stroke of genius in her design.
That new marketing strategy was a real stroke of business genius.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brainwave - a sudden clever idea or inspiration
  • Synonym: flash of inspiration - a sudden creative or inspired idea
