stinker of an idea

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪŋkər əv ən aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkə əv ən aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
an idea that is very bad or poorly conceived
a very bad idea
informal

Examples

Everyone agreed that building a highway through the park was a stinker of an idea.
He admitted later that his suggestion was truly a stinker of an idea.
That proposal turned out to be a stinker of an idea from the start.

Synset Examples

She called the new policy a stinker of an idea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrible idea - an idea that is very bad
  • Antonym: brilliant idea - an idea of exceptional quality
