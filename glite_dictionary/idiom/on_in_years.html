on in years

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ɪn jɪrz / (AmE), / ɒn ɪn jɪəz / (BrE)
used to describe someone who is advanced in age or getting older
advanced in age; elderly

Examples

Although my neighbors are on in years, they still love to travel the world.
Her father is getting on in years, so he appreciates visits from his grandchildren more than ever.
Many people who are on in years enjoy reminiscing about the past.

Synset Examples

My grandfather is on in years but still very active.
He's really changed; he's on in years now.
As he's on in years, he prefers quiet evenings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elderly - old; advanced in age
  • Synonym: aged - having lived for many years; old
  • Antonym: youthful - having young qualities or characteristics
