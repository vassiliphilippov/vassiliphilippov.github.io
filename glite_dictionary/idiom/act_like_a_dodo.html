to act like a dodo

1. VERB

/ ˈækt laɪk ə ˈdoʊdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈækt laɪk ə ˈdəʊdəʊ / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or clueless manner; to act as if lacking common sense
behave foolishly or cluelessly

Examples

If you keep acting like a dodo, you'll never finish the project on time.
She realized she was acting like a dodo when she forgot her own phone number.
Don't act like a dodo—check your work before handing it in.

Synset Examples

He's acting like a dodo by missing the obvious clues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: act like a fool - to behave foolishly or without good sense
  • Antonym: act sensibly - to behave in a sensible or prudent manner

Etymology

From 'dodo,' a now-extinct flightless bird, figuratively meaning a fool or simpleton.
