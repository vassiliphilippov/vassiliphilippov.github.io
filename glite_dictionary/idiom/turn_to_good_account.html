to turn to good account

1. VERB

/ tɝn tu ɡʊd əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / tɜːn tʊ ɡʊd əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
to use something in a way that is productive, beneficial, or advantageous
use something effectively or advantageously

Examples

She managed to turn her skills to good account in her new job.
The chef tried to turn leftover ingredients to good account by creating a new dish.
We should turn our free time to good account and learn something new.

Synset Examples

He aims to turn his failure to good account.
She hopes to turn her skills to good account.
We can turn this experience to good account later.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make good use of - to use something beneficially or effectively
