box of tricks

1. NOUN

/ bɑks əv trɪks / (AmE), / bɒks əv trɪks / (BrE)
a set of skills, methods, or techniques that a person can use to solve problems or perform tasks, especially in a clever or resourceful way
set of skills or methods for solving problems

Examples

Every good teacher has a box of tricks to keep students engaged.
Her box of tricks made the presentation both informative and entertaining.
When the plan failed, he reached into his box of tricks for an alternative solution.

Synset Examples

He has a box of tricks for every occasion.

Every chef needs a box of tricks to impress guests.
She has a whole box of tricks to solve problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arsenal - collection of resources or skills available for use
  • Synonym: toolkit - set of tools or skills for a particular purpose
