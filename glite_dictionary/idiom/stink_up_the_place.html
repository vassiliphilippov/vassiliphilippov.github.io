to stink up the place

1. VERB

/ stɪŋk ʌp ðə pleɪs / (AmE), / stɪŋk ʌp ðə pleɪs / (BrE)
to cause a location or area to smell very unpleasant
cause a place to smell bad
informal

Examples

The garbage started to stink up the place after a few hot days.
If you burn popcorn, it will stink up the place for hours.
Please take your shoes outside—they're really stinking up the place.

Synset Examples

You really stink up the place with that perfume.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stink - have a strong unpleasant smell
