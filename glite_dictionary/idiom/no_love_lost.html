no love lost

1. X

/ noʊ lʌv lɔst / (AmE), / nəʊ lʌv lɒst / (BrE)
Used to indicate mutual dislike or lack of affection between people.
mutual dislike; lack of affection

Examples

After the argument, there was no love lost between the two coworkers.
Even after the deal was finished, there was clearly no love lost between the companies.
It was obvious to everyone that there was no love lost between the rival teams.

Synset Examples

After the argument, there was no love lost between the two.
They ended their partnership with no love lost.
They had a falling out, and there’s no love lost between them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enmity - the state or feeling of being actively opposed or hostile to someone
  • Synonym: hostility - hostile behavior; unfriendliness or opposition
