to knock the stuffing out of

1. VERB

/ nɑk ðə ˈstʌfɪŋ aʊt ʌv / (AmE), / nɒk ðə ˈstʌfɪŋ aʊt ɒv / (BrE)
to make someone feel extremely tired, weak, or disheartened; to overwhelm or exhaust someone
overwhelm or exhaust someone

Examples

That hike really knocked the stuffing out of me, and I needed a long nap afterward.
Her criticism knocked the stuffing out of his confidence for weeks.
The hot weather knocks the stuffing out of anyone trying to work outside all day.

Synset Examples

The storm really knocked the stuffing out of us.
His speech knocked the stuffing out of the audience.
That workout knocked the stuffing out of me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to wear out - to make very tired or exhausted
  • Antonym: to invigorate - to give someone more energy or strength
