to turn one's life around

1. VERB

/ tɝːn wʌnz laɪf əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / tɜːn wʌnz laɪf əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to make significant positive changes in the course of one's life, especially after difficulties or failures
make significant positive life changes

Examples

After losing his job, he decided to turn his life around and went back to school.
She credits her mentor for helping her turn her life around after years of hardship.
Many people find that a single decision can turn their life around completely.
