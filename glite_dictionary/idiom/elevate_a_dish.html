to elevate a dish

1. VERB

/ ˈɛləˌveɪt ə dɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪveɪt ə dɪʃ / (BrE)
to improve the flavor, quality, or appeal of food, often by adding a special ingredient or technique
improve the quality of food

Examples

A squeeze of lemon can instantly elevate a dish and bring the flavors to life.
Fresh herbs are often used by chefs to elevate a dish from ordinary to exquisite.
Adding a touch of truffle oil can elevate a dish to restaurant quality at home.

Synset Examples

A little salt can really elevate a dish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to enhance - to improve the quality or value of something
