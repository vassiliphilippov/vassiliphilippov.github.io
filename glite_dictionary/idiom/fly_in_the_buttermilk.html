fly in the buttermilk

1. NOUN

/ flaɪ ɪn ðə ˈbʌt̬ɚˌmɪlk / (AmE), / flaɪ ɪn ðə ˈbʌtəˌmɪlk / (BrE)
a small flaw or problem that spoils an otherwise pleasant or perfect situation
minor flaw that spoils a good situation

Examples

Despite winning the award, her cold was a fly in the buttermilk during the celebration.
There always seems to be a fly in the buttermilk when we plan our vacations.
His late arrival was the only fly in the buttermilk on an otherwise smooth day.

Synset Examples

There’s always a fly in the buttermilk at family gatherings.
His criticism was the fly in the buttermilk of an otherwise flawless event.
Everything was perfect until that fly in the buttermilk appeared.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fly in the ointment - a minor drawback or difficulty that spoils an otherwise satisfactory situation
