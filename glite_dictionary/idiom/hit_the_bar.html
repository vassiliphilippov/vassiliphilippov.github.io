to hit the bar

1. VERB

/ hɪt ðə bɑr / (AmE), / hɪt ðə bɑː / (BrE)
(informal) To go to a bar, typically for a drink or socializing.
go to a bar for drinks or socializing
informal

Examples

After work, do you want to hit the bar with us?
The team celebrated their victory by deciding to hit the bar downtown.
Whenever they're stressed, they love to hit the bar and unwind together.
