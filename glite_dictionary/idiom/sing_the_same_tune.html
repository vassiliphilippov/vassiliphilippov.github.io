to sing the same tune

1. VERB

/ sɪŋ ðə seɪm tun / (AmE), / sɪŋ ðə seɪm tjuːn / (BrE)
to express the same opinion or agree with others, especially publicly
to express agreement or shared opinion

Examples

All members of the committee sing the same tune when it comes to supporting the project.
It's obvious that both companies are singing the same tune about the benefits of the merger.
He used to oppose the new policy, but now he sings the same tune as the rest of the team.

Synset Examples

At work, we need to sing the same tune.
They might disagree, but they sing the same tune.
Despite disagreements, they always sing the same tune.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be on the same page - to have the same understanding or agreement
  • Antonym: sing a different tune - to express a different or opposite opinion
