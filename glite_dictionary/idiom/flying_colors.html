flying colors

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈkʌlərz / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈkʌləz / (BrE)
with complete success; having achieved something very successfully or triumphantly.
complete success; triumphant achievement

Examples

She passed the final exam with flying colors after months of hard work.
The team completed the project ahead of schedule and with flying colors.
He came through the interview with flying colors, impressing everyone on the panel.

Synset Examples

they passed inspection with flying colors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triumph - a great victory or achievement
  • Antonym: failure - lack of success

Etymology

From the naval custom of returning to port with flags (colors) flying to signify victory.
