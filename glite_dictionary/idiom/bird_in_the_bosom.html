bird in the bosom

1. NOUN

/ bɝd ɪn ðə ˈboʊsəm / (AmE), / bɜːd ɪn ðə ˈbəʊsəm / (BrE)
Something that is kept safe, cherished, or held in close affection.
something cherished or kept safe

Examples

She kept the memory of her grandmother as a bird in the bosom throughout her life.
The letter was his little bird in the bosom, bringing him comfort on difficult days.
For many, hope remains a bird in the bosom even in hard times.

Synset Examples

The phrase 'a bird in the bosom' is quite charming.
