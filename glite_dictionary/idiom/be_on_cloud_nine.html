to be on cloud nine

1. VERB

/ bi ɑn klaʊd naɪn / (AmE), / bi ɒn klaʊd naɪn / (BrE)
to feel extremely happy or elated; to be in a state of great joy
be extremely happy

Examples

She was on cloud nine after hearing about her promotion.
He felt on cloud nine when he met his favorite author in person.
They were on cloud nine all weekend after winning the championship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be over the moon - to be extremely pleased or happy
  • Antonym: be in the dumps - to be sad or depressed
