there you go

1. INTJ

/ ðɛr ju ˈɡoʊ / (AmE), / ðeə ju ˈɡəʊ / (BrE)
Used to acknowledge that something has been done, given, or achieved, or to indicate satisfaction with the result.
expression acknowledging something done or given
informal

Examples

Here’s your change—there you go.
You fixed the problem yourself; there you go.
She finally passed the exam—there you go!

Synset Examples
