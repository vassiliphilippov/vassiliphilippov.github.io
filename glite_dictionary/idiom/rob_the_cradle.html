to rob the cradle

1. VERB

/ rɑb ðə ˈkreɪ.dəl / (AmE), / rɒb ðə ˈkreɪ.dl̩ / (BrE)
to date or marry someone who is much younger, often implying the age difference is socially notable or inappropriate
date or marry someone much younger

Examples

Some people think it's scandalous when a celebrity robs the cradle by dating a much younger partner.
He was teased at work for robbing the cradle after marrying someone half his age.
The tabloids accused the actress of robbing the cradle with her 22-year-old boyfriend.

Synset Examples

It's not uncommon for older men to rob the cradle.
She joked that he was robbing the cradle again.
He was criticized for robbing the cradle with his young girlfriend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cradle-snatch - to be romantically involved with a much younger person (British equivalent)
