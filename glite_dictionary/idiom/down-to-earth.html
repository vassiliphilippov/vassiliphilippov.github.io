down-to-earth

1. ADJ

/ ˌdaʊn tə ˈɜrθ / (AmE), / ˌdaʊn tʊ ˈɜːθ / (BrE)
practical, realistic, and unpretentious in manner or attitude
practical and unpretentious

Examples

Despite her fame, she remains down-to-earth and approachable.
His down-to-earth advice helped me solve my problem quickly.
I admire their down-to-earth approach to life, focusing on what truly matters.

Synset Examples

Everyone loves the First Laddie for his down-to-earth nature.
Her down-to-earth personality makes her easy to talk to.
She gave a down-to-earth speech that inspired everyone.
We appreciate his down-to-earth approach to problem-solving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: practical - concerned with actual use or practice
  • Synonym: realistic - having or showing a sensible and practical idea of what can be achieved or expected
  • Antonym: pretentious - attempting to impress by affecting greater importance or merit than actually possessed
