by the bye

1. ADV

/ baɪ ðə baɪ / (AmE), / baɪ ðə baɪ / (BrE)
used to introduce a new or incidental topic; incidentally.
used to introduce a side remark
archaic

Examples

By the bye, have you seen my keys anywhere?
By the bye, the meeting has been moved to Thursday.
She mentioned, by the bye, that she might be leaving early today.

Synset Examples

incidentally, I won't go to the party

Related Terms

  • Synonym: by the way - used to introduce a new or incidental topic
  • Synonym: incidentally - used to add a comment not directly related to the main subject

Etymology

From archaic 'bye', meaning 'side issue' or 'secondary matter'.
