to stuff one's face

1. VERB

/ stʌf wʌnz feɪs / (AmE), / stʌf wʌnz feɪs / (BrE)
to eat a large amount of food quickly or greedily
eat greedily or quickly
informal

Examples

Whenever there is free pizza, he can't help but stuff his face.
She stuffed her face with cookies before dinner, ruining her appetite.
At the party, everyone was stuffing their face with snacks.

Synset Examples

He always stuffs his face at family dinners.
She can't help but stuff her face with popcorn at movies.
Don't just stuff your face; enjoy the flavors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gorge - to eat a large amount greedily; fill oneself with food
  • Synonym: to pig out - to eat a lot, especially in a greedy or messy way
  • Antonym: nibble - to eat something by taking small bites
