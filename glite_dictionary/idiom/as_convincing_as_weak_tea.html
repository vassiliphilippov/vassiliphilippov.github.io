as convincing as weak tea

1. ADJ

/ əz kənˈvɪnsɪŋ əz wik ti / (AmE), / əz kənˈvɪnsɪŋ əz wiːk tiː / (BrE)
(of an argument, excuse, etc.) not convincing at all; very weak or unpersuasive.
completely unconvincing (idiom)

Examples

His promises were as convincing as weak tea and nobody believed him.
The lawyer's defense was as convincing as weak tea, easily dismissed by the jury.
Her excuse for being late was as convincing as weak tea and met with raised eyebrows.

Synset Examples

His argument was as convincing as weak tea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unconvincing - not capable of persuading; lacking credibility
