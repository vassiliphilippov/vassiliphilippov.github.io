dirty old man

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɝːɾi oʊld mæn / (AmE), / ˈdɜːti əʊld mæn / (BrE)
an older man who behaves in a lecherous or sexually inappropriate manner, especially towards younger people
lecherous older man

Examples

Her mother warned her to stay away from the dirty old man who lived down the street.
The film features a comedic portrayal of a dirty old man who constantly flirts with the younger women.
He was tired of being labeled a dirty old man just for making a playful comment.

Related Terms
