as you know

1. SCONJ

/ æz ju noʊ / (AmE), / æz juː nəʊ / (BrE)
used to indicate that the listener is already aware of the information being mentioned
used to indicate shared knowledge

Examples

As you know, the project deadline has been moved up to Friday.
As you know, our company has been expanding rapidly over the past year.
There's no need to explain it further, as you know the situation already.

Synset Examples

As you know, the meeting starts at nine.
