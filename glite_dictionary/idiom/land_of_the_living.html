land of the living

1. NOUN

/ ˈlænd əv ðə ˈlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlænd əv ðə ˈlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the state or condition of being alive; the world inhabited by the living, as opposed to the dead.
state of being alive

Examples

After his illness, he was grateful to be back in the land of the living.
Coffee helps me join the land of the living every morning.
She hasn't responded yet, but I hope she's still in the land of the living.
