the fear of God

1. NOUN

/ ðə fɪr əv ɡɑd / (AmE), / ðə fɪə əv ɡɒd / (BrE)
a strong feeling of fear or awe, often inspired by authority or the threat of punishment
intense fear or awe, especially of authority

Examples

The new manager quickly put the fear of God into the entire staff with her strict rules.
When he saw the headmaster coming, the fear of God was written all over his face.
A thunderstorm like that can instill the fear of God in anyone, especially children.

Synset Examples

The coach really put the fear of God into the players.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awe - a feeling of reverential respect mixed with fear or wonder
  • Degree Higher: terror - a feeling of extreme fear
