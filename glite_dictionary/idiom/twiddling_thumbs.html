to twiddling thumbs

1. VERB

/ ˈtwɪd.lɪŋ ˈθʌmz / (AmE), / ˈtwɪd.lɪŋ ˈθʌmz / (BrE)
to do nothing in particular; to be idle, especially while waiting for something to happen
to be idle while waiting

Examples

We were left twiddling thumbs in the waiting room for nearly an hour.
Instead of twiddling thumbs, why not help with the dishes?
During power outages, many people end up twiddling thumbs until the electricity returns.

Synset Examples

He spent the afternoon twiddling his thumbs at home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idle - not active or working; doing nothing
