cup that cheers

1. NOUN

/ kʌp ðət tʃɪrz / (AmE), / kʌp ðət tʃɪəz / (BrE)
a comforting or enjoyable beverage, especially tea, that lifts the spirits or provides pleasure
comforting beverage (often tea)
british

Examples

After a stressful day, she finds solace in a cup that cheers every evening.
The host offered his guests a cup that cheers to make them feel at home.
Nothing revives the spirits on a rainy morning like a steaming cup that cheers.

Synset Examples

He believes a cup that cheers can brighten any mood.
For her, tea is the cup that cheers her spirit.
A warm cup that cheers is perfect on cold days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cup of tea - a cup of tea; something pleasant or familiar

Etymology

British origin; derived from the traditional association of tea (or sometimes coffee) with comfort and cheerfulness.
