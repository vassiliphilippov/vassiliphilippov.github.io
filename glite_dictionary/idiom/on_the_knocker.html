on the knocker

1. ADV

/ ɑn ðə ˈnɑkɚ / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ˈnɒkə / (BrE)
persistent or insistent in asking for something, or actively seeking or searching for it
persistent in asking or searching
british informal

Examples

Every year, charities are on the knocker for donations during the holidays.
Reporters were on the knocker for a statement after the scandal broke.
Children went on the knocker around the neighborhood asking for treats.

Synset Examples

The kids are always on the knocker for candy at Halloween.
I found a flyer on the knocker this morning.
He was on the knocker, looking for a lost cat.
