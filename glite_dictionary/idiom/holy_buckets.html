holy buckets

1. INTJ

/ ˈhoʊli ˈbʌkɪts / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli ˈbʌkɪts / (BrE)
used as an exclamation to express surprise, shock, or astonishment
exclamation of surprise or shock
informal
