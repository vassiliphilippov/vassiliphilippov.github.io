too rich for my blood

1. X

/ tuː rɪtʃ fər maɪ blʌd / (AmE), / tuː rɪtʃ fə maɪ blʌd / (BrE)
too expensive for one's financial means or preference
too expensive for someone

Examples

A private jet is too rich for my blood, so I settled for first class.
That designer watch is definitely too rich for my blood at that price.
The bidding quickly got too rich for my blood, so I dropped out.

Synset Examples

That luxury car is too rich for my blood.
Buying a yacht feels too rich for my blood.
The restaurant's prices are too rich for my blood.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: affordable - inexpensive enough for someone to buy
  • Synonym: out of (one's) price range - too expensive for someone to consider purchasing
