heart in one's work

1. NOUN

/ hɑrt ɪn wʌnz wɝk / (AmE), / hɑːt ɪn wʌnz wɜːk / (BrE)
to show great dedication and passion for what one is doing, especially in one's job
dedication and passion for one's job

Examples

You can tell she really has her heart in her work by the care she takes with every detail.
The best employees are those who put their heart in their work and strive to improve.
Although the task was difficult, he kept his heart in his work until it was finished.

Synset Examples

He always has his heart in his work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: put one's heart into - to give all of one's effort and passion to something
