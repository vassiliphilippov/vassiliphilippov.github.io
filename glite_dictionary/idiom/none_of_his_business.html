none of his business

1. X

/ nʌn əv hɪz ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / nʌn əv hɪz ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
If something is none of his business, it does not concern him and he should not be involved or inquire about it.
not his concern; does not involve him

Examples

She told him bluntly that it was none of his business and to stop asking questions.
What I do in my free time is none of his business.
He always wants to know everything, even when it's none of his business.

Synset Examples

She told him directly that it's none of his business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: none of your business - not your concern; does not involve you
  • Synonym: to mind your own business - do not interfere in matters that do not concern you
