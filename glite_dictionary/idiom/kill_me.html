kill me

1. X

/ kɪl mi / (AmE), / kɪl mi / (BrE)
used to express strong frustration, exasperation, or disbelief, often in response to something annoying or overwhelming
expression of strong frustration or exasperation
informal

Examples

If my boss gives me one more project, kill me now.
When I saw the exam questions, I just thought, 'kill me.'
He forgot our anniversary again—just kill me!
