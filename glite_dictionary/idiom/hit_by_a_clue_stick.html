to hit by a clue stick

1. VERB

/ hɪt baɪ ə klu stɪk / (AmE), / hɪt baɪ ə kluː stɪk / (BrE)
Suddenly realize or understand something that should have been obvious.
suddenly understand something obvious
informal

Examples

It took me a while, but I finally got hit by a clue stick and saw my mistake.
He didn't understand the joke until he was hit by a clue stick during lunch.
Sometimes I need to be hit by a clue stick before I realize the obvious.

Synset Examples

She felt like she got hit by a clue stick when the answer was revealed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to see the light - suddenly understand or realize something
  • Synonym: have an epiphany - have a sudden realization
