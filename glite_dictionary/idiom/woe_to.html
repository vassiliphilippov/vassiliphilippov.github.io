woe to

1. X

/ woʊ tu / (AmE), / wəʊ tuː / (BrE)
used to warn that someone will experience trouble or misfortune if they do a particular thing
warning of coming misfortune

Examples

Woe to anyone who ignores the safety instructions during the experiment.
Woe to those who disregard the rules of the competition.
Woe to the person who betrays his friends.

Synset Examples

Woe to the man who underestimates her talent.
Woe to the villain who crosses her path.
Woe to anyone who tries to cheat this game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to beware - be cautious of; warning to avoid danger or harm
