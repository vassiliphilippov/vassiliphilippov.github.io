to wouldn't be seen dead

1. VERB

/ ˈwʊdənt bi ˈsiːn dɛd / (AmE), / ˈwʊdnt bi ˈsiːn dɛd / (BrE)
used to emphasize that someone is completely unwilling to do something or be associated with something, usually because they consider it embarrassing or beneath them
refuse to do or wear something; find it unacceptable

Examples

She wouldn't be seen dead shopping at that discount store.
He wouldn't be seen dead wearing bright pink trousers.
My friends wouldn't be seen dead listening to classical music.

Synset Examples

He wouldn't be seen dead in that outfit.
She wouldn't be seen dead in a cheap car.
