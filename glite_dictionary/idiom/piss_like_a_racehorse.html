to piss like a racehorse

1. VERB

/ pɪs laɪk ə ˈreɪsˌhɔrs / (AmE), / pɪs laɪk ə ˈreɪshɔːs / (BrE)
to have a very strong urge to urinate or to urinate with great force, especially after drinking a lot of liquid
have a strong urge to urinate (idiom)
informal slang vulgar

Examples

After holding it in during the movie, I had to piss like a racehorse as soon as it ended.
He joked that the coffee made him piss like a racehorse all morning.
She ran to the restroom, saying she needed to piss like a racehorse.

Synset Examples
