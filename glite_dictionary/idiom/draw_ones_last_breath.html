to draw one's last breath

1. VERB

/ drɔ ʌnz læst brɛθ / (AmE), / drɔː wʌnz lɑːst brɛθ / (BrE)
to die; to breathe for the last time before death
die; breathe for the last time

Examples

As the sun set, the hero drew his last breath on the battlefield.
The patient drew his last breath surrounded by his loving family.
Everyone in the room fell silent as the old man drew his last breath.

Synset Examples

The old man drew his last breath peacefully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: die - to stop living
  • Synonym: to pass away - to die (euphemistic)
