au courant

1. ADJ

/ oʊ kuˈrɑnt / (AmE), / əʊ kʊˈrɒ̃ / (BrE)
aware of or familiar with current developments, especially well informed or up to date in knowledge about something
well informed; up to date

Examples

She always stays au courant with changes in fashion trends.
The reporter is au courant on political issues and recent developments.
Reading industry journals helps you remain au courant in your field.

Synset Examples

kept abreast of the latest developments
constant revision keeps the book au courant
always au fait on the latest events
up on the news

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abreast - up to date with the latest information
  • Synonym: informed - having or showing knowledge about something

Etymology

From French 'au courant' (in the current, up to date).
