eat an elephant one bite at a time

1. X

/ it ən ˈɛləˌfənt wən baɪt ət ə ˈtaɪm / (AmE), / iːt ən ˈelɪfənt wʌn baɪt ət ə ˈtaɪm / (BrE)
to approach a very large or difficult task by dividing it into smaller, manageable steps
tackle a big task in small steps

Examples

Whenever faced with a huge project, remember to eat an elephant one bite at a time.
She completed her thesis by eating an elephant one bite at a time.
Learning a new language is easier if you eat an elephant one bite at a time.

Synset Examples

When overwhelmed, remember to eat an elephant one bite at a time.
To finish this project, just eat the elephant one bite at a time.
She tackles her goals by eating an elephant one bite at a time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: step by step - gradually, in stages
  • Synonym: in stages - doing something part by part
  • Antonym: all at once - doing everything together, not gradually
