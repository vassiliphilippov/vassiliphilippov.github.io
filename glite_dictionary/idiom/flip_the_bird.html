to flip the bird

1. VERB

/ flɪp ðə bɝd / (AmE), / flɪp ðə bɜːd / (BrE)
to make an obscene gesture by raising the middle finger, typically as an expression of contempt or anger
make an obscene gesture (middle finger)
informal vulgar

Examples

After being cut off in traffic, Jake couldn't resist and flipped the bird at the other driver.
She was so frustrated with her boss that she nearly flipped the bird during the meeting.
The player got a penalty for flipping the bird at the referee after a bad call.

Synset Examples

She couldn't help but flip the bird after the insult.
He was so angry he just flipped the bird.
He flipped the bird at the rude driver.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: give the finger - to make an obscene gesture with the middle finger
