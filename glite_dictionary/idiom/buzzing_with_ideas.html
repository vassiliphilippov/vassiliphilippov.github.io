buzzing with ideas

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʌzɪŋ wɪð aɪˈdiəz / (AmE), / ˈbʌzɪŋ wɪð aɪˈdɪəz / (BrE)
full of lively thoughts, creativity, or excitement about new ideas
full of creative energy or ideas

Examples

The team was buzzing with ideas after the inspirational talk.
Her mind was buzzing with ideas as she prepared for the meeting.
The studio became buzzing with ideas every time a new project began.

Synset Examples

The hackery was buzzing with ideas for the new app.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brimming with ideas - full to the top with ideas or creativity
