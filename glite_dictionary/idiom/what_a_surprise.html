what a surprise

1. INTJ

/ wʌt ə səˈpraɪz / (AmE), / wɒt ə səˈprʌɪz / (BrE)
used to express astonishment or unexpectedness at something
expression of astonishment

Examples

What a surprise to see you here after all these years!
When I walked into the room, what a surprise it was to find the lights already on.
She opened the gift and exclaimed, 'What a surprise! I wasn’t expecting this at all.'

Synset Examples

Ho, what a surprise to find you at this event!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: how surprising - expression used to denote surprise
