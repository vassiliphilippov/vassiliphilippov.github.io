to be a chutiya

1. VERB

/ bi ə tʃuːˈtiːjə / (AmE), / biː ə tʃʊˈtiːjə / (BrE)
(slang) To act foolishly or carelessly; to behave in a stupid or naive way.
act foolishly; behave like an idiot (slang)
slang offensive

Examples

If you don't double-check your answers, you'll be a chutiya and mess up the test.
Don't be a chutiya—lock the door when you leave the house!
She told him not to be a chutiya during the important meeting.

Synset Examples

Don't be a chutiya and forget your keys again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be an idiot - to act in a stupid way

Etymology

Adopted from Hindi slang 'chutiya', meaning a fool or idiot.
