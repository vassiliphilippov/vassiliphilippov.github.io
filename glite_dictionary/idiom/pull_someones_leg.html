to pull someone's leg

1. VERB

/ pʊl ˈsʌmwʌnz lɛɡ / (AmE), / pʊl ˈsʌmwənz lɛɡ / (BrE)
to tease or trick someone in a playful way; to joke with someone by telling them something untrue as a joke
tease or joke with playfully

Examples

Are you pulling my leg or did you really win the lottery?
He loves to pull his friends' legs by telling ridiculous stories.
When she told me she had met the president, I thought she was just pulling my leg.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tease - to make fun of or attempt to provoke in a playful way
  • Synonym: joke - to say something intended to make people laugh, especially by teasing
