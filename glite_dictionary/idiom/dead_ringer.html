dead ringer

1. NOUN

/ dɛd ˈrɪŋər / (AmE), / dɛd ˈrɪŋə / (BrE)
a person who looks almost exactly like another person; an exact double or lookalike.
exact double; lookalike

Examples

Everyone said the new student was a dead ringer for the principal’s son.
That actor is a dead ringer for my uncle—it's uncanny!
She’s a dead ringer for that famous singer from the 1980s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doppelgänger - someone who looks exactly like another person
  • Synonym: lookalike - a person who closely resembles another

Etymology

Probably from 19th-century horse-racing slang, where 'ringer' meant a substituted horse; 'dead' intensifies the exactness.
