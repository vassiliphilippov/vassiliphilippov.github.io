her shit doesn't stink

1. X

/ hɚ ʃɪt ˈdʌzənt stɪŋk / (AmE), / hə ʃɪt ˈdʌzənt stɪŋk / (BrE)
used to say that someone acts as though they are flawless or superior to others, often with arrogance or self-importance
acts as if perfect or above reproach
slang vulgar

Examples

She walks around the office like her shit doesn't stink, expecting everyone to admire her.
He truly believes his shit doesn't stink and never admits to making mistakes.
Whenever confronted with criticism, she acts as if her shit doesn't stink and ignores everyone else's opinions.

Synset Examples

She always brags; it's like she thinks her shit doesn't stink.
He never apologizes; he really thinks his shit doesn't stink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: full of oneself - excessively self-important or conceited
  • Antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's importance
