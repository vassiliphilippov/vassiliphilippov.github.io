watch your ass

1. X

/ wɑtʃ jɚ æs / (AmE), / wɒtʃ jɔːr ɑːs / (BrE)
Used to warn someone to be very careful or vigilant, especially when facing possible danger or trouble.
be careful; stay vigilant
informal vulgar

Examples

If you plan to cross him, you'd better watch your ass.
She told her friend to watch your ass when driving through that part of town at night.
You really need to watch your ass with those two around.

Synset Examples

You better watch your ass or you'll get fired.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watch your back - be careful or vigilant; idiomatic warning of danger
