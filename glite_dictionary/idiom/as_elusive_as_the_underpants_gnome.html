as elusive as the underpants gnome

1. ADJ

/ æz ɪˈluːsɪv əz ði ˈʌndərˌpænts noʊm / (AmE), / az ɪˈluːsɪv əz ði ˈʌndəˌpænts nəʊm / (BrE)
Used to describe something that is very difficult to understand, explain, or grasp, often with humorous or ironic intent.
difficult to grasp or understand (humorous)
informal american

Examples

The solution to the puzzle remained as elusive as the underpants gnome throughout the competition.
Her reasoning was as elusive as the underpants gnome, leaving everyone at the meeting confused.
Trying to follow his instructions was as elusive as the underpants gnome—none of us understood what to do.

Synset Examples

Their strategy felt as elusive as the underpants gnome's.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elusive - difficult to find, catch, or achieve

Etymology

From the 'Underpants Gnomes,' characters in the animated TV show South Park (US, 1998), known for a nonsensical business plan.
