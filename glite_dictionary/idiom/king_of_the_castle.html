king of the castle

1. NOUN

/ kɪŋ əv ðə ˈkæsəl / (AmE), / kɪŋ əv ðə ˈkɑːsəl / (BrE)
a person who is in a position of power, control, or security, especially in their own environment
person feeling in power or secure

Examples

Ever since she got the corner office, she feels like the king of the castle at work.
After winning the championship, he strutted around school like the king of the castle.
At home, Dad is definitely the king of the castle.

Synset Examples

At the party, he acted like the king of the castle.
After the promotion, he felt like the king of the castle.
In his new apartment, he was the king of the castle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top dog - a person who is dominant or in a superior position
