to make my day

1. VERB

/ meɪk maɪ deɪ / (AmE), / meɪk maɪ deɪ / (BrE)
to do something that brings great happiness or pleasure to someone
to bring someone great happiness

Examples

A simple phone call from you would make my day.
Her kind words really made my day after a tough morning.
Finding my lost keys just before leaving for work made my day.

Synset Examples

A compliment from him would definitely make my day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cheer up - cause someone to become happier
