scared shitless

1. ADJ

/ skɛrd ˈʃɪtləs / (AmE), / skɛəd ˈʃɪtləs / (BrE)
Extremely frightened; terrified.
extremely frightened (vulgar, informal)
informal vulgar

Examples

I was scared shitless when I saw a snake in my kitchen.
They were scared shitless during the thunderstorm last night.
She admitted that she was scared shitless before the big exam.

Synset Examples

He was scared shitless after reading the ghost stories.
I was scared shitless during the horror movie.
She was scared shitless when she heard the loud bang.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petrified - so frightened that one is unable to move; terrified
  • Synonym: terrified - extremely frightened or scared
  • Antonym: unafraid - not frightened or scared
