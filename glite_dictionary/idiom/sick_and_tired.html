sick and tired

1. ADJ

/ sɪk ənd ˈtaɪɚd / (AmE), / sɪk ənd ˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
Fed up or thoroughly annoyed with something to the point of exhaustion or frustration.
fed up; frustrated and exhausted

Examples

I'm sick and tired of cleaning up after everyone in this house.
She grew sick and tired of hearing the same complaints every day.
He was sick and tired of dealing with delays at the airport.

Synset Examples

He felt sick and tired of the constant drama.
I'm sick and tired of the same old excuses.
She was sick and tired of waiting for him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fed up - annoyed or bored, especially with something that has continued for too long
