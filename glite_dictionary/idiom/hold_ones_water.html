to hold one's water

1. VERB

/ hoʊld wʌnz ˈwɔt̬ɚ / (AmE), / həʊld wʌnz ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
to refrain from urinating; to deliberately avoid urinating for a period of time
refrain from urinating

Examples

After drinking two bottles of water, she struggled to hold her water during the long meeting.
Young children are sometimes praised when they can hold their water overnight.
If you can't hold your water for another hour, let me know and we'll find a restroom.

Synset Examples

I can't hold my water much longer on this road trip.
Make sure you can hold your water before the hike.
The toddler finally learned to hold his water during the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hold it - to delay urinating, usually as an imperative or exclamation
