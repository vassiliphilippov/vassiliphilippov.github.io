left in stitches

1. ADJ

/ lɛft ɪn ˈstɪtʃɪz / (AmE), / lɛft ɪn ˈstɪtʃɪz / (BrE)
Laughing very hard; greatly amused, especially as the result of something very funny.
laughing very hard; greatly amused

Examples

The comedian's performance had the whole audience left in stitches for over an hour.
His witty remarks always leave his friends left in stitches at parties.
After hearing the hilarious story, I was left in stitches and couldn't stop laughing.

Synset Examples

The poor zappee was left in stitches after the joke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in stitches - laughing uncontrollably or very hard
