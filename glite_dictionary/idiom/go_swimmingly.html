to go swimmingly

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ ˈswɪmɪŋli / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈswɪmɪŋli / (BrE)
to progress very well or smoothly, without problems
proceed smoothly; without issues

Examples

Despite initial concerns, the entire event went swimmingly from start to finish.
If all meetings go swimmingly, we should launch the product ahead of time.
She hoped her plans would go swimmingly, just as they had in the trial run.

Synset Examples

Everything is going swimmingly with the new team.
Her presentation went swimmingly; she nailed it.
The project went swimmingly, ahead of schedule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go well - to proceed successfully or as planned
  • Antonym: go badly - to proceed with problems or unsuccessfully
