for short

1. ADV

/ fər ʃɔrt / (AmE), / fə ʃɔːt / (BrE)
used to indicate a name, term, or phrase that is abbreviated or shortened in reference
as an abbreviation or shortened form

Examples

My full name is Robert, but you can call me for short Bob.
The Federal Bureau of Investigation, or FBI for short, investigates federal crimes.
Her nickname is Liz, for short, instead of Elizabeth.
