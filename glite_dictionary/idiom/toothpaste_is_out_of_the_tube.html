toothpaste is out of the tube

1. X

/ ˈtuːθˌpeɪst ɪz aʊt əv ðə tuːb / (AmE), / ˈtuːθˌpeɪst ɪz aʊt əv ðə tjuːb / (BrE)
used to say that something irreversible has happened and cannot be undone or taken back
situation that cannot be reversed

Examples

After the leak, toothpaste is out of the tube—there’s no way to keep the information secret now.
Once her comments went viral, the toothpaste is out of the tube and the situation can't be fixed.
Now that the plan has been announced, toothpaste is out of the tube for the company.

Synset Examples

Now that it's public, the toothpaste is out of the tube.
Once the deal was announced, toothpaste is out of the tube.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no use crying over spilt milk - there is no point in regretting something that cannot be changed
  • Synonym: the genie is out of the bottle - something has happened that cannot be controlled or reversed
