busy bee

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪzi bi / (AmE), / ˈbɪzi biː / (BrE)
A person who is always busy or active and works hard.
a very active, industrious person

Examples

Lisa is a busy bee, always moving from one task to another.
As a busy bee, he rarely takes breaks during the day.
My grandmother has been a busy bee since she retired, joining several clubs.

Synset Examples

She's a busy bee, constantly buzzing around the office.
She was a bizzie bee, always working on new projects.
He's always been a busy bee, organizing events and volunteering.
My sister is a busy bee, juggling work and family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workaholic - a person who compulsively works hard and long hours
  • Antonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
