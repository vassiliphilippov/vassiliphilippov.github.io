to make a boob

1. VERB

/ meɪk ə buːb / (AmE), / meɪk ə buːb / (BrE)
to make a foolish mistake or blunder
make a foolish mistake (idiom)
informal british

Examples

He admitted to making a boob when he read the wrong figures aloud.
You'll be fine as long as you avoid making a boob in front of the boss.
I was mortified after making a boob in the meeting yesterday.

Synset Examples

He was embarrassed after making a boob during the presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blunder - a stupid or careless mistake
  • Synonym: to slip up - to make a careless mistake
