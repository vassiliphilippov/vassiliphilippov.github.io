without a hitch

1. ADV

/ wɪˈðaʊt ə hɪtʃ / (AmE), / wɪˈðaʊt ə hɪtʃ / (BrE)
with no problems or difficulties; smoothly and successfully
without problems; smoothly

Examples

The conference was organized without a hitch and everyone was impressed.
The new system was installed without a hitch, which surprised the whole team.
Their travel plans went without a hitch despite the unpredictable weather.

Synset Examples

All transactions were processed without a hitch.
The wedding went off without a hitch.
The event was organized smoothly and without a hitch.
