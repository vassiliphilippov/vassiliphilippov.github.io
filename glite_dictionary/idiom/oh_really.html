oh really

1. INTJ

/ oʊ ˈrɪli / (AmE), / əʊ ˈrɪəli / (BrE)
Used to express surprise, interest, or mild disbelief in response to what someone has just said.
expression of surprise or interest
informal

Examples

Oh really? I didn't know you spoke three languages!
"He quit his job yesterday." "Oh really? I had no idea."
She raised her eyebrows and said, "Oh really? Tell me more."

Synset Examples

Oh really, that sounds interesting!
