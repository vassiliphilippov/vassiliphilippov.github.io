fall guy

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔl ɡaɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːl ɡaɪ / (BrE)
a man who is easily tricked and made to take the blame for others' mistakes or crimes; a scapegoat or dupe.
person easily blamed or tricked

Examples

They needed a fall guy to take the blame for the missing funds.
He was the fall guy in the whole operation, even though he was innocent.
The company used him as a fall guy to distract from their own mistakes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scapegoat - a person who is blamed for the wrongdoings of others
  • Synonym: dupe - a person who is easily deceived or tricked

Etymology

Originated in early 20th-century American slang, possibly from the sense of 'fall' as to be arrested.
