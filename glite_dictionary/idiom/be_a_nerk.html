to be a nerk

1. VERB

/ bi ə nɜrk / (AmE), / biː ə nɜːk / (BrE)
to act in a foolish or ineffective way; to behave stupidly
act foolishly or ineffectively
informal

Examples

If you be a nerk in front of your boss, you might miss out on a promotion.
Stop being a nerk and listen to what I'm telling you.
You don't have to be a nerk just because things aren't going your way.

Synset Examples

Don't be a nerk and skip the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be a fool - act in a silly or foolish way
  • Antonym: be sensible - act in a prudent or reasonable way
