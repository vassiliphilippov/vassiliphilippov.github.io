as happy as a jaybird

1. ADJ

/ æz ˈhæpi æz ə ˈdʒeɪbɝd / (AmE), / æz ˈhæpi æz ə ˈdʒeɪbɜːd / (BrE)
extremely happy; filled with great joy
extremely happy

Examples

After hearing the good news, she was as happy as a jaybird all afternoon.
He walked out of the interview as happy as a jaybird, confident he had gotten the job.
The kids were as happy as a jaybird when they saw their birthday presents.

Synset Examples

He was as happy as a jaybird after winning the lottery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overjoyed - very happy; filled with joy

Etymology

Likely derived from the lively and seemingly cheerful behavior of jaybirds; modeled after similar animal similes in English.
