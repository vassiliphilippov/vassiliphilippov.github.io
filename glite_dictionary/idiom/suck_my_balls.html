suck my balls

1. X

/ sʌk maɪ bɔlz / (AmE), / sʌk maɪ bɔːlz / (BrE)
(vulgar, offensive) Used as an insult or expression of contempt, telling someone to perform a demeaning sexual act.
vulgar insult; expression of contempt
vulgar offensive

He stormed out of the office, shouting, "suck my balls!" at his coworker.
She rolled her eyes and muttered, "suck my balls," under her breath after the argument.
During the heated debate, one of the players yelled, "suck my balls!" across the field.
