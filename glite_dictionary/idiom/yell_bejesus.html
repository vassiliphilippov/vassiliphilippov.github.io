to yell bejesus

1. VERB

/ jɛl bəˈdʒiːzəs / (AmE), / jɛl bɪˈdʒiːzəs / (BrE)
(informal) To exclaim loudly in surprise or shock.
exclaim in surprise or shock
informal slang

Examples

I nearly yelled bejesus when the cat jumped on my lap.
She yelled bejesus the moment the lights went out unexpectedly.
We all yelled bejesus when the fire alarm blared in the middle of the night.

Synset Examples

The sudden noise made me jump and yell bejesus!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to exclaim - to speak or shout suddenly with strong emotion

Etymology

From 'bejesus', an exclamation derived as a euphemism for 'by Jesus' in Irish English.
