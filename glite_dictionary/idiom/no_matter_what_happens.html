no matter what happens

1. ADV

/ noʊ ˈmætər wʌt ˈhæpənz / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈmætə wɒt ˈhæp(ə)nz / (BrE)
regardless of what may occur; in spite of all possible difficulties or obstacles
regardless of circumstances; in any case

Examples

I promise to support you no matter what happens.
She said she would finish the project no matter what happens.
We are determined to succeed no matter what happens.

Synset Examples

we'll go to Tibet come hell or high water

Related Terms

  • Synonym: come hell or high water - in spite of everything; whatever may happen
  • Synonym: at all costs - regardless of the difficulty or expense
