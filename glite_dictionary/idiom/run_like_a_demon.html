to run like a demon

1. VERB

/ rʌn laɪk ə ˈdiːmən / (AmE), / rʌn laɪk ə ˈdiːmən / (BrE)
to run extremely quickly or with great speed and energy
run extremely quickly

Examples

As soon as the dogs started chasing him, he ran like a demon across the field.
She ran like a demon to make it to her meeting on time.
During the final lap, he ran like a demon and overtook all his competitors.

Synset Examples

He ran like a demon to catch the bus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: run like the wind - to run very fast
