to open someone's eyes

1. VERB

/ ˈoʊpən sʌmˈwʌnz aɪz / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən sʌmˈwʌnz aɪz / (BrE)
to make someone realize or become aware of something, especially something previously unknown or misunderstood
make someone aware or realize something

Examples

Living in another country really opened his eyes to new perspectives.
The documentary opened my eyes to the effects of climate change.
Her volunteer work opened her eyes to the struggles faced by the homeless.

Synset Examples

Traveling abroad opened her eyes to global issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to enlighten - to give someone greater knowledge or understanding about a subject
