in all my born days

1. X

/ ɪn ɔl maɪ bɔrn deɪz / (AmE), / ɪn ɔːl maɪ bɔːn deɪz / (BrE)
at any time in one's life; ever (used to emphasize surprise or disbelief)
at any time in one's life

Examples

In all my born days, I have never seen such a strange animal.
Honestly, in all my born days, I never expected to find myself in this situation.
In all my born days as a teacher, I've rarely had a class so enthusiastic.

Synset Examples

In all my born days, I've never tasted such delicious food.

  • Synonym: ever - at any time; at all times
