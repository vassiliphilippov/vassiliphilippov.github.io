sleep tight

1. X

/ slip taɪt / (AmE), / sliːp taɪt / (BrE)
used to wish someone a good night’s sleep; often said before bedtime.
wish for a restful night

Examples

Goodnight, I hope you sleep tight and wake up refreshed.
She hugged her daughter and said, "Sleep tight, I'll see you in the morning."
After a long day, he wished his friend to sleep tight before leaving.

Synset Examples

Goodnight, sweet pea; sleep tight and dream big!
Sleep tight, and don't let the bedbugs bite!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good night - a phrase used to wish someone a pleasant or restful night
