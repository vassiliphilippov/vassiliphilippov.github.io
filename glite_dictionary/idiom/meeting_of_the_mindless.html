meeting of the mindless

1. NOUN

/ ˈmitɪŋ əv ðə ˈmaɪndləs / (AmE), / ˈmiːtɪŋ əv ðə ˈmaɪndləs / (BrE)
an unproductive meeting or gathering characterized by a lack of intelligence, creativity, or meaningful ideas
unproductive or unintelligent meeting
informal

Examples

The weekly staff update quickly devolved into a meeting of the mindless, with no productive results.
Whenever they gather without an agenda, it becomes a meeting of the mindless.
We joked that yesterday's conference call was just another meeting of the mindless.

Synset Examples

It felt like a meeting of the mindless with no new ideas.
The discussion turned into a meeting of the mindless.
We left the meeting of the mindless feeling frustrated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to talk shop - to discuss work-related topics, often tediously, outside of work
