on one's mind

1. ADP

/ ɑn wʌnz maɪnd / (AmE), / ɒn wʌnz maɪnd / (BrE)
continuously thinking about something; preoccupied or concerned with something
preoccupied or concerned with something

Examples

Lately, the upcoming job interview has been on my mind constantly.
If something is on your mind, it's a good idea to talk to someone about it.
The safety of her children was always on her mind during the trip.

Synset Examples

She had the meeting on her mind all week.
He had the upcoming exam on his mind all week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to preoccupy - to dominate or engross the mind of someone to the exclusion of other thoughts
  • Synonym: to worry about - to feel anxious or concerned about something
  • Antonym: off one's mind - no longer worrying or thinking about something
