to blow one's load

1. VERB

/ bloʊ wʌnz loʊd / (AmE), / bləʊ wʌnz ləʊd / (BrE)
(informal, sometimes vulgar) To use up all one's effort, resources, or energy at once, often prematurely; to reveal all one has too soon. Also used as a vulgar slang term meaning to ejaculate.
expend all effort or resources at once; prematurely reveal everything
informal vulgar

Examples

He blew his load at the beginning of the debate and had nothing left to say.
The team blew their load in the first half and couldn't keep up later.
She tends to blow her load all at once when she's excited about a new project.

Synset Examples

After working on it for weeks, he finally blew his load at the presentation.
He tends to blow his load too quickly in negotiations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to expend - to use up energy or resources
  • Antonym: to hold back - to reserve effort or avoid revealing everything
