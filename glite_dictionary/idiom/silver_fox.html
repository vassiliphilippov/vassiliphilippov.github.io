silver fox

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ fɑks / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə fɒks / (BrE)
an attractive, stylish older man, typically with grey or silver hair.
an attractive older man with grey hair
informal

Examples

Ever since he let his hair go grey, he has become quite the silver fox.
The new CEO is a silver fox with a magnetic presence.
Many people at the event were drawn to the distinguished silver fox by the bar.

Synset Examples

The silver fox in the corner was the center of attention.
