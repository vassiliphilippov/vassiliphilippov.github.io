the cure is worse than the disease

1. X

/ ðə ˈkjʊɹ ɪz wɝs ðæn ðə dɪˈziz / (AmE), / ðə ˈkjʊə ɪz wɜːs ðən ðə dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
used to say that the attempt to solve a problem has made the situation worse than the original problem itself
when a solution makes things worse

Examples

Many believe that introducing such strict controls will prove that the cure is worse than the disease.
After trying to fix the car myself, I realized the cure is worse than the disease when it ended up costing more.
Sometimes, imposing new regulations just shows the cure is worse than the disease.

Synset Examples

In this case, the cure is worse than the disease, complicating everything.
His drastic diet change proved that the cure is worse than the disease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of the frying pan into the fire - to go from a bad situation to a worse one
  • Synonym: to make matters worse - to make a situation more difficult or unpleasant
