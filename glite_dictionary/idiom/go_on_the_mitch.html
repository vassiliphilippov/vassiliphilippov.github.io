to go on the mitch

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ ɑn ðə mɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ɒn ðə mɪtʃ / (BrE)
to sneak away or leave suddenly, often to have secret fun or go on a spree, sometimes to avoid responsibilities
sneak away secretly or go on a spree
informal
