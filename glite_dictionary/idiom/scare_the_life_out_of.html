to scare the life out of

1. VERB

/ skɛr ðə laɪf aʊt əv / (AmE), / skeə ðə laɪf aʊt əv / (BrE)
to frighten someone very much; to shock or startle someone severely
frighten someone greatly

Examples

The thunderstorm scared the life out of me last night.
When the cat jumped onto the table, it scared the life out of everyone in the room.
That horror movie scared the life out of my little brother.

Synset Examples

That jump scare scared the life out of everyone.
The sudden noise scared the life out of me.
The prank was so intense it scared the life out of her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to terrify - to frighten someone greatly
  • Synonym: to startle - to surprise or shock someone suddenly
