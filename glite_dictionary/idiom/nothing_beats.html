to nothing beats

1. VERB

/ ˈnʌθɪŋ bits / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ biːts / (BrE)
used to say that there is nothing better than a particular thing or activity
used to mean nothing is better than

Examples

Nothing beats a walk in the fresh air after a long day at work.
For breakfast, nothing beats homemade pancakes with maple syrup.
Nothing beats watching the sunset over the ocean with friends.

Synset Examples

On cold nights, nothing beats a hot bowl of ramen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nothing compares to - nothing is as good as
