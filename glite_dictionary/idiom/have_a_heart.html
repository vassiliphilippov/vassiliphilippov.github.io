to have a heart

1. VERB

/ hæv ə hɑrt / (AmE), / hæv ə hɑːt / (BrE)
to show compassion or kindness, especially when someone is in distress
show compassion or kindness

Examples

Please have a heart and listen to her side of the story.
After seeing the stray dog, she decided to have a heart and bring it home.
You need to have a heart sometimes, even if the rules say otherwise.

Synset Examples

It's important to have a heart in your profession.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be kind - to act with kindness
  • Antonym: be harsh - to act without compassion or kindness
