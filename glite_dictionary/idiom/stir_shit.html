to stir shit

1. VERB

/ stɝ ʃɪt / (AmE), / stɜː ʃɪt / (BrE)
to deliberately cause trouble or provoke conflict, especially by making inflammatory remarks or actions
deliberately cause trouble or conflict
informal vulgar

Examples

He always tries to stir shit when things seem calm at work.
Stop stirring shit just to get a reaction from people.
She enjoys stirring shit online by posting controversial opinions.

Synset Examples
