from the get-go

1. ADV

/ frəm ðə ˈɡɛt ˌɡoʊ / (AmE), / frəm ðə ˈɡɛt ˌɡəʊ / (BrE)
from the very beginning; right from the start of an activity, process, or situation.
from the very beginning
informal american

Examples

It was clear from the get-go that the two teams were evenly matched.
From the get-go, she impressed everyone with her dedication and creative ideas.
You should have been honest from the get-go instead of hiding the truth.
