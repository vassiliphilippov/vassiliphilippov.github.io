worth the watch

1. ADJ

/ wɝθ ðə wɑtʃ / (AmE), / wɜːθ ðə wɒtʃ / (BrE)
good enough to justify the time spent watching it; deserving of being watched
deserving to be watched

Examples

The documentary was challenging but definitely worth the watch.
Is that new series really worth the watch, or should I skip it?
Despite the slow start, the film turned out to be worth the watch.

Synset Examples

That movie was totally worth the watch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worth seeing - deserving to be seen; worth going to see
