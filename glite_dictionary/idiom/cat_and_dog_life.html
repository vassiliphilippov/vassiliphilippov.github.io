cat and dog life

1. NOUN

/ ˌkæt ənd ˌdɔɡ ˈlaɪf / (AmE), / ˌkæt ənd ˌdɒɡ ˈlaɪf / (BrE)
a life marked by continual arguing or conflict, especially between people who live together
life filled with constant conflict or quarrelling

Examples

After years of living a cat and dog life, the couple finally decided to separate.
The movie depicts the cat and dog life of two business partners who rarely agree on anything.
Growing up in a cat and dog life household taught him to value peace and compromise.

Synset Examples

You could tell they had a cat and dog life by their constant arguing.
Their cat and dog life made for some funny moments.
The siblings lived a cat and dog life, always bickering.

Etymology

From the common phrase "fight like cats and dogs", likening constant quarreling to the stereotyped hostility between cats and dogs.
