on the right track

1. X

/ ɑn ðə raɪt trǽk / (AmE), / ɒn ðə raɪt træk / (BrE)
doing or thinking the correct thing to achieve a desired result; making good progress toward a correct outcome
making progress toward a correct outcome

Examples

Your solution isn’t perfect yet, but you are definitely on the right track.
Keep working hard; you’re on the right track to success.
