don't go there

1. X

/ doʊnt goʊ ðɛr / (AmE), / dəʊnt gəʊ ðeə / (BrE)
used to warn someone not to mention or discuss a particular subject because it is sensitive or likely to cause upset or trouble
warn against discussing a sensitive topic
informal

Examples

If you want to avoid an argument, I'd suggest you don't go there with her.
Whenever someone brings up his ex-girlfriend, he immediately says, "Don't go there."
Trust me, you don't go there if you value your friendship.

Synset Examples

Trust me, it's best if you just don't go there.
When discussing politics, just remember—don't go there.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: touchy subject - a subject likely to cause discomfort or offense
