rags to riches

1. NOUN

/ ræɡz tə ˈrɪtʃɪz / (AmE), / ræɡz tə ˈrɪtʃɪz / (BrE)
used to describe a situation in which someone rises from poverty to great wealth or success
transition from poverty to wealth

Examples

The company's rags to riches history inspires many young entrepreneurs.
He often tells his rags to riches story to motivate students.
Many classic novels are based on the rags to riches theme.

Synset Examples

His journey from rags to riches is a success story.
Her journey from rags to riches is incredible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cinderella story - a story or situation where someone rises from obscurity to greatness or wealth
