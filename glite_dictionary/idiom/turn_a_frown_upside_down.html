to turn a frown upside down

1. VERB

/ tɝn ə fraʊn ˌʌp.saɪd ˈdaʊn / (AmE), / tɜːn ə fraʊn ˌʌp.saɪd ˈdaʊn / (BrE)
to make someone feel happier, especially when they are sad or upset
cheer someone up

Examples

Whenever I'm sad, my sister tries to turn my frown upside down with her jokes.
The teacher told a funny story to turn the students' frowns upside down after the tough exam.
A surprise party might just turn your frown upside down tonight.

Synset Examples

He always knows how to turn my frown upside down.
Let’s watch a comedy to turn that frown upside down.
A good joke can turn your frown upside down.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cheer up - to become or make someone happier
