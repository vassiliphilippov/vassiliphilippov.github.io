to keep one's eyes off

1. VERB

/ kip wʌnz aɪz ɔf / (AmE), / kiːp wʌnz aɪz ɒf / (BrE)
to deliberately avoid looking at someone or something
deliberately avoid looking

Examples

I couldn't keep my eyes off the new painting in the gallery.
Please keep your eyes off my work until I'm finished.
She tried to keep her eyes off the tempting desserts on the table.

Synset Examples

Keep your hands off my wife!
Keep your distance from this man — he is dangerous

Related Terms

  • Antonym: keep one's eyes on - to watch or observe closely
  • Synonym: avert one's eyes - to turn one's eyes away from something intentionally
