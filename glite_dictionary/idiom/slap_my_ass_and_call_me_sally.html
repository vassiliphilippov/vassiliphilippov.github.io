slap my ass and call me Sally

1. X

/ slæp maɪ æs ənd kɔl mi ˈsæli / (AmE), / slæp maɪ ɑːs ənd kɔːl mi ˈsæli / (BrE)
(informal) Used to express surprise, disbelief, or astonishment at something unexpected.
expression of great surprise or disbelief
informal vulgar

Slap my ass and call me Sally—the results were better than anyone imagined.
