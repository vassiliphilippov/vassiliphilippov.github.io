every hour God sends

1. ADV

/ ˌɛvri ˈaʊɚ ɡɑd sɛndz / (AmE), / ˌɛvri ˈaʊə ɡɒd sɛndz / (BrE)
as often as possible; on every possible occasion; frequently
very frequently; whenever possible

Examples

She calls her mother every hour God sends to check if she's all right.
The cat comes into the kitchen every hour God sends looking for food.
He worked in the garden every hour God sends during the spring.

Synset Examples
