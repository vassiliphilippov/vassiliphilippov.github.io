icing on the cake

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪsɪŋ ɑn ðə keɪk / (AmE), / ˈaɪsɪŋ ɒn ðə keɪk / (BrE)
an additional advantage or benefit that makes a good situation even better
additional benefit to something good

Examples

Getting accepted to her first-choice university was wonderful, but the scholarship was the icing on the cake.
The beautiful weather on our wedding day was truly the icing on the cake.
Completing the project ahead of schedule was great; the praise from the client was the icing on the cake.

Synset Examples

Her promotion was just the icing on the cake.
The bonus was the icing on the cake after a successful year.
Winning the award was the icing on the cake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cherry on top - an extra benefit making something better
