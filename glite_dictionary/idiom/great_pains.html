great pains

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪt peɪnz / (AmE), / ɡreɪt peɪnz / (BrE)
A lot of effort or care used to achieve or ensure something.
significant effort or care

Examples

She took great pains to write a detailed and accurate report.
The chef goes to great pains to source only the freshest ingredients.
He always takes great pains with his appearance before important meetings.

Synset Examples

They took great pains to prepare for the big event.
She takes great pains to ensure her presentations are perfect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: care - serious attention or consideration applied to doing something correctly
  • Synonym: effort - exertion of physical or mental energy
