to feel like a dinosaur

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə ˈdaɪnəˌsɔr / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈdaɪnəsɔː / (BrE)
to feel old-fashioned or out of date, especially in comparison to newer trends or technologies
to feel old-fashioned or outdated
informal

Examples

Whenever new technology comes out, I sometimes feel like a dinosaur.
After hearing my students talk about apps I’ve never used, I really felt like a dinosaur.
He laughed and admitted he was feeling like a dinosaur at the modern art exhibit.

Synset Examples

Her outdated software feels like a dinosaur.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obsolete - no longer in use or no longer useful; out of date
