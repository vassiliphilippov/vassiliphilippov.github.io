to hit it big

1. VERB

/ hɪt ɪt bɪɡ / (AmE), / hɪt ɪt bɪɡ / (BrE)
to achieve great success, especially in a career or endeavor
achieve great success
informal

Examples

After years of playing in small clubs, the band finally hit it big with their latest album.
Many actors move to Hollywood, hoping they'll hit it big someday.
She never thought her little bakery would hit it big and become a franchise.

Synset Examples

She finally hit it big after years of hard work.
He hopes to hit it big with his new startup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to make it big - to become very successful or famous
