in a puff of smoke

1. X

/ ɪn ə pʌf əv smoʊk / (AmE), / ɪn ə pʌf əv sməʊk / (BrE)
used to describe someone or something that suddenly and mysteriously disappears or ceases to exist
suddenly and mysteriously disappearing

Examples

The magician made the rabbit vanish in a puff of smoke, astonishing the children.
All their hopes disappeared in a puff of smoke after the announcement.
The file was there a minute ago, and now it's gone in a puff of smoke.

Synset Examples

He disappeared in a puff of smoke, leaving everyone amazed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vanish into thin air - disappear completely and mysteriously
