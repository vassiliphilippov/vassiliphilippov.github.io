to show (someone) a good time

1. VERB

/ ʃoʊ ə ɡʊd taɪm / (AmE), / ʃəʊ ə ɡʊd taɪm / (BrE)
to entertain someone and ensure they have an enjoyable experience
entertain someone; ensure they have fun

Examples

We really wanted to show her a good time while she was visiting.
They promised to show us a good time during our stay in New York.
He always tries to show his friends a good time when they come over.

Synset Examples

I promised to show him a good time in the city.
