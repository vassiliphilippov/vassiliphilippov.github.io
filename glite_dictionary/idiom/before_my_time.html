before my time

1. X

/ bɪˈfɔr maɪ taɪm / (AmE), / bɪˈfɔː maɪ taɪm / (BrE)
used to refer to a period or event that took place before one was born or was aware; before one's personal experience
before one was born or aware

Examples

Many of the city's oldest buildings were constructed before my time.
The scandal happened before my time, so I only know about it from history books.
That actor was famous well before my time, but my parents remember him.

Synset Examples

That song was popular well before my time.
He often shares stories from before my time.
