to awake to the reality

1. VERB

/ əˈweɪk tu ðə riˈæləti / (AmE), / əˈweɪk tʊ ðə rɪˈæləti / (BrE)
to become aware of or recognize the truth or actual situation, often after ignoring or misunderstanding it
become aware of the truth or facts

Examples

After the accident, she was awake to the reality of how dangerous driving can be.
Many people only become awake to the reality of climate change when it affects them personally.
It was only after he lost his job that he was awake to the reality of his financial situation.

Synset Examples

He was finally awake to the realities of life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wake up to - become aware of or start to understand something
  • Synonym: face the facts - accept the reality of a situation
