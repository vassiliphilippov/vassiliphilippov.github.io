to give a go

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ə ɡoʊ / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə ɡəʊ / (BrE)
to attempt or try something, especially something new
try or attempt something

Examples

I decided to give a go at learning Italian during the summer.
Why don't you give a go at fixing the car yourself?
He was nervous about skiing but wanted to give a go anyway.

Synset Examples

She encouraged me to give painting a go.
Let's give this new restaurant a go tonight.
I decided to give baking a go this weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: try - to attempt to do or accomplish something
  • Synonym: attempt - to make an effort to achieve or complete something
