number one with a bullet

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌm.bɚ wʌn wɪð ə ˈbʊl.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈnʌm.bə wʌn wɪð ə ˈbʊl.ɪt / (BrE)
an undisputed leader or a decisive favorite, especially in rankings or popularity lists
a clear, decisive favorite
informal american

Examples

The band's latest single is number one with a bullet on the charts this week.
Her innovative product launch made her number one with a bullet in the tech industry.
After the movie premiered, it became number one with a bullet at the box office.

Synset Examples

Her new book is number one with a bullet this week.

Etymology

Originally from U.S. music charts, where a 'bullet' symbol indicated a song rising quickly to number one.
