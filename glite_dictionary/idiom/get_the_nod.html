to get the nod

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ðə nɑd / (AmE), / ɡɛt ðə nɒd / (BrE)
to receive approval or permission to proceed with something
receive approval or permission

Examples

After weeks of waiting, the team finally got the nod to start the project.
She was excited to get the nod for the promotion after her interview.
The movie only went into production once the director got the nod from the studio.

Synset Examples

He was thrilled to get the nod from his boss.
Once the proposal was approved, we knew we would get the nod.
After the meeting, she finally got the nod for the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get the green light - to receive permission to proceed
