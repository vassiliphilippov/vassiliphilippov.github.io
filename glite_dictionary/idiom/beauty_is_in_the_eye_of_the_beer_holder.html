beauty is in the eye of the beer holder

1. X

/ ˈbjuːti ɪz ɪn ði aɪ əv ðə ˈbɪr ˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈbjuːti ɪz ɪn ði aɪ əv ðə ˈbɪə ˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
Used humorously to suggest that alcohol can make people appear more attractive, emphasizing that perceptions of beauty are subjective and can be influenced by drinking.
humorous idiom about attraction and alcohol
informal

Examples

After three pints, he insisted that beauty is in the eye of the beer holder.
The sign over the bar jokes that beauty is in the eye of the beer holder.
She laughed and said, 'Well, beauty is in the eye of the beer holder tonight!'

Synset Examples

In this bar, beauty is in the eye of the beer holder.
After a few drinks, beauty is in the eye of the beer holder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty is in the eye of the beholder - proverb meaning perception of beauty is subjective

Etymology

A humorous play on the proverb 'beauty is in the eye of the beholder,' substituting 'beer holder' for comic effect.
