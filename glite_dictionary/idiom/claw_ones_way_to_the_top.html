to claw one's way to the top

1. VERB

/ klɔ wʌnz weɪ tə ði tɑp / (AmE), / klɔː wʌnz weɪ tə ðə tɒp / (BrE)
to achieve success by fighting and struggling against difficulties or opposition, often in a ruthless or determined manner
achieve success through hard struggle

Examples

After years of hard work and sacrifice, she finally clawed her way to the top of the company.
Many believe that you must claw your way to the top in a competitive industry.
He was willing to claw his way to the top, no matter what it cost him.

Synset Examples

She felt like she had to claw her way to the top.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fight one's way up - to succeed by persistent effort and struggle
