handbags at dawn

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændˌbæɡz ət dɔn / (AmE), / ˈhan(d)bæɡz ət dɔːn / (BrE)
a humorous expression for a minor or trivial quarrel, especially one characterized by exaggerated or melodramatic confrontation.
