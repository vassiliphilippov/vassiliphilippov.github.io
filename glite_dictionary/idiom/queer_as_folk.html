queer as folk

1. X

/ kwɪr æz foʊk / (AmE), / kwɪə əz fəʊk / (BrE)
used to express the idea that people are strange or unpredictable
people are strange or unpredictable (idiom)
british

Examples

There's nowt so queer as folk, as my grandmother used to say.
You think you've seen it all, but honestly, queer as folk never ceases to amaze me.
When it comes to human behavior, it's true that there's nothing queer as folk.
