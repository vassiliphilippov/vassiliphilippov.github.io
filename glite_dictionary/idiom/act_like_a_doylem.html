to act like a doylem

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈdɔɪləm / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈdɔɪləm / (BrE)
to behave in a foolish or clumsy manner; to act like an idiot or a fool
behave foolishly or clumsily
informal

Examples

Stop acting like a doylem and help me with this task.
Whenever he drinks too much, he tends to act like a doylem.
Don’t act like a doylem in front of your boss!

Synset Examples

He acted like a doylem at the party last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fool around - to behave in a silly or playful way
  • Antonym: behave sensibly - to act in a rational and prudent manner
