song and dance

1. NOUN

/ sɔŋ ənd dæns / (AmE), / sɒŋ ənd dɑːns / (BrE)
an elaborate or implausible story, often used as an excuse or pretext
implausible story or excuse

Examples

He gave me some song and dance about missing the train to explain why he was late.
I'm tired of your song and dance every time something goes wrong.
They came up with a complicated song and dance to avoid taking responsibility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tall tale - an exaggerated or unlikely story
  • Synonym: excuse - a reason or explanation given to justify a fault or offense
