goodness me

1. INTJ

/ ˈɡʊdnəs miː / (AmE), / ˈɡʊdnɪs miː / (BrE)
used to express surprise, shock, or admiration.
expression of surprise or admiration

Examples

Goodness me, I had no idea the meeting was today!
Goodness me, what a wonderful performance that was!
Goodness me, I nearly spilled my coffee.

Synset Examples

Goodness me, I didn't expect to see you here!
She said, 'Goodness me, what a beautiful sunset!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: my goodness - expression of surprise or admiration
  • Synonym: good gracious - expression of surprise or astonishment
