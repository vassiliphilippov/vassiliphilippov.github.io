after a fashion

1. ADV

/ ˌæftɚ ə ˈfæʃən / (AmE), / ˌɑːftə ə ˈfæʃən / (BrE)
to some extent; not very well
to some extent; imperfectly

Examples

He can cook after a fashion, but few would call it gourmet cuisine.
She managed to understand the lecture after a fashion, though much of it was beyond her.
The repairs worked after a fashion, but we still need a real solution.

Synset Examples

he speaks French after a fashion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: more or less - approximately; to a certain degree but not exactly
  • Synonym: somewhat - to a limited extent or degree
