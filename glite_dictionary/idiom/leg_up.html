leg up

1. NOUN

/ lɛg ʌp / (AmE), / lɛg ʌp / (BrE)
an advantage or assistance that makes it easier to achieve something
advantage or support

Examples

That internship will give you a leg up in the competitive job market.
Her older brother's advice gave her a real leg up in organizing the event.
Small business grants can provide a valuable leg up to new entrepreneurs.
