to beat all expectations

1. VERB

/ bit ɔl ˌɛkspɛkˈteɪʃənz / (AmE), / biːt ɔːl ˌɛkspɛkˈteɪʃənz / (BrE)
to do much better than was expected or predicted
do much better than expected

Examples

The team’s performance beat all expectations and earned them the top prize.
Sales figures for the new product beat all expectations in the first quarter.
Her recovery after surgery beat all expectations and amazed the medical staff.

She managed to beat all expectations with her stunning presentation.

Related Terms
