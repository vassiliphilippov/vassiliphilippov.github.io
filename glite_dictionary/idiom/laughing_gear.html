laughing gear

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæfɪŋ ɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈlɑːfɪŋ ɡɪə / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) a person's mouth or, by extension, the ability to speak or laugh.
(informal) mouth; ability to laugh or talk
informal british

Examples

He broke out his laughing gear when he heard the comedian's joke.
Bring your laughing gear if you want to enjoy the night!
She wiped the crumbs off her laughing gear after eating the cake.

Synset Examples

Her laughing gear was on full display at the party.
Don’t forget to bring your laughing gear tonight!
He unleashed his laughing gear at the hilarious movie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mouth - the opening in the face for eating, speaking, or making sounds

Etymology

Colloquial phrase; 'laughing' refers to laughter, 'gear' used humorously for equipment, as in Australian and British slang.
