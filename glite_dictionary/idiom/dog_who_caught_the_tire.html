dog who caught the tire

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ hu kɑt ðə ˈtaɪər / (AmE), / dɒɡ huː kɒt ðə ˈtaɪə / (BrE)
someone who unexpectedly achieves success or a desired outcome but then feels unsure what to do next
person surprised by their own success

Examples

After landing the lead role in the play, she felt like the dog who caught the tire.
Winning the major contract left him feeling like the dog who caught the tire.
When her business idea succeeded, she realized she was the dog who caught the tire and didn’t know what to do next.

Synset Examples

He felt like the dog who caught the tire after his promotion.
Winning feels like being the dog who caught the tire.
