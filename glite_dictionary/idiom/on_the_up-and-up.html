on the up-and-up

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ði ˈʌp ənd ˈʌp / (AmE), / ɒn ði ˈʌp ənd ˈʌp / (BrE)
Honest and legitimate; conducted in a truthful or lawful way.
honest and legitimate
informal

Examples

He assured us that the business deal was on the up-and-up and there was nothing to worry about.
Are you sure this contract is on the up-and-up? It seems too good to be true.
The charity aims to keep all its operations on the up-and-up.

Synset Examples

Their dealings are completely on the up-and-up.
I trust him; he's always been on the up-and-up.
We need to ensure everything's on the up-and-up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: above board - honest and open; not secretive or deceptive
