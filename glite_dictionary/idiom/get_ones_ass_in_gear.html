to get one's ass in gear

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt wʌnz æs ɪn ɡɪr / (AmE), / ɡɛt wʌnz ɑːs ɪn ɡɪə / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) To hurry up or start working more efficiently; to begin acting with greater energy or urgency.
act faster or more efficiently (vulgar, informal)
informal vulgar

Examples

If we don't get our ass in gear, we'll miss the start of the meeting.
She finally decided to get her ass in gear and finish the assignment.
You need to get your ass in gear if you want to meet the deadline.

Synset Examples

You better get your ass in gear if you want to catch the train.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to get moving - to start moving or act more quickly
  • Synonym: to get going - to begin moving or making progress
  • Antonym: to dawdle - to move or act slowly and waste time
