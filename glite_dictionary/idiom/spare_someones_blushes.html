to spare someone's blushes

1. VERB

/ spɛr sʌmwʌnz ˈblʌʃɪz / (AmE), / spɛə ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈblʌʃɪz / (BrE)
to avoid causing someone embarrassment or awkwardness
avoid embarrassing someone

Examples

He tried to spare her blushes by answering the question himself.
She quickly changed the topic to spare his blushes in front of the group.
To spare their blushes, we edited out the awkward moment from the video.

Synset Examples
