to jump salty

1. VERB

/ dʒʌmp ˈsɔlti / (AmE), / dʒʌmp ˈsɒlti / (BrE)
To become angry or irritable suddenly; to react with annoyance or upset.
become angry or irritable quickly
informal american

Examples

If you keep teasing him, he might jump salty and start yelling.
She tends to jump salty whenever she's criticized at work.
Don't jump salty just because plans changed at the last minute.
