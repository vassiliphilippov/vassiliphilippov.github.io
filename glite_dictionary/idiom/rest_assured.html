to rest assured

1. VERB

/ rɛst əˈʃʊrd / (AmE), / rɛst əˈʃɔːd / (BrE)
used to emphasize that you should not worry because something is certain or guaranteed
be confident that something is certain

Examples

Rest assured, our team will resolve your issue quickly.
You can rest assured that all necessary precautions have been taken.
Rest assured, your feedback is important to us and will be acted upon.

Synset Examples

Rest assured, we will handle everything for the event.
Rest assured, your secret is safe with me.
You can rest assured that your package will arrive on time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be certain - to feel sure about something
  • Antonym: doubt - to feel uncertain or unsure
