confound it

1. INTJ

/ kənˈfaʊnd ɪt / (AmE), / kənˈfaʊnd ɪt / (BrE)
used to express annoyance or frustration, similar to 'damn it' or 'blast it'
expression of annoyance or frustration
archaic

Examples

Confound it, where did I leave my wallet this time?
Confound it, the printer has jammed again just before my deadline.
Confound it, why won’t this door open?

Synset Examples

Confound it, why does this always happen to me?
Confound it, I can't believe I missed the meeting!
Confound it, I forgot my keys again!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: damn it - an exclamation of annoyance or frustration
  • Synonym: blast it - used to express annoyance or anger

Etymology

Derived from the verb 'confound', used as a milder substitute for stronger exclamations.
