butter on someone's bread

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ɚ ɑn ˈsʌmwʌnz brɛd / (AmE), / ˈbʌtə ɒn ˈsʌmwənz brɛd / (BrE)
a source of income or support; the means by which a person earns a living or sustenance
means of financial support

Examples

Having a steady job puts butter on his bread and pays the bills.
Writing articles every week is what puts butter on my bread.
She relies on her art sales for butter on her bread.

Synset Examples

His investments put butter on his bread and keep him comfortable.
Freelancing puts butter on my bread each month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: livelihood - means of securing the necessities of life
  • Synonym: breadwinner - the person who earns money to support a family
