the place to be

1. NOUN

/ ðə pleɪs tə bi / (AmE), / ðə pleɪs tə biː / (BrE)
The most desirable or popular location to be at a particular time.
most popular location

Examples

On New Year's Eve, Times Square is the place to be for celebrations.
According to the locals, that café is the place to be on Saturday mornings.
During the summer, the rooftop bar quickly becomes the place to be after work.

Synset Examples

The beach is the place to be on a sunny day.
The festival is the place to be for fun.
This concert is the place to be tonight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hotspot - a popular or trendy place
