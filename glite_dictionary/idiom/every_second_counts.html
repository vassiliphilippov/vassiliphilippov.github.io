every second counts

1. X

/ ˈɛvɹi ˈsɛkənd kaʊnts / (AmE), / ˈɛvɹi ˈsɛkənd kaʊnts / (BrE)
used to emphasize that time is extremely valuable and urgent action is needed
time is crucial; urgency

Examples

In emergency situations, every second counts, so paramedics must respond quickly.
When the fire alarm rings, every second counts for a safe evacuation.
During a heart attack, every second counts when it comes to getting medical help.

Synset Examples

They need to act quickly, as every second counts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the nick of time - at the last possible moment; just in time
  • Synonym: time is of the essence - time is a crucial factor
  • Antonym: to waste time - to use time inefficiently or carelessly
