full of beans

1. ADJ

/ fʊl əv binz / (AmE), / fʊl əv biːnz / (BrE)
Very energetic, lively, and enthusiastic.
energetic and lively
informal

Examples

After a good night's sleep, she woke up full of beans and ready to start her day.
The children were full of beans after eating birthday cake at the party.
His new puppy is always full of beans, running all over the house.

Synset Examples

She arrived at work full of beans and ready to tackle anything.
The kids were full of beans after the party.
His puppy is always full of beans, bouncing around the house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lively - full of life and energy
  • Synonym: energetic - having or showing a lot of energy
  • Antonym: tired - feeling a need to rest; lacking energy
