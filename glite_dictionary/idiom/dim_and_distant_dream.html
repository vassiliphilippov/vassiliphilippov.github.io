dim and distant dream

1. NOUN

/ dɪm ənd ˈdɪstənt driːm / (AmE), / dɪm ənd ˈdɪstənt driːm / (BrE)
an aspiration or hope that is vague, faint, or unlikely to be realized
faint or unlikely aspiration

Examples

For many people, owning a house in the city is a dim and distant dream.
His plans of becoming a famous actor remained a dim and distant dream throughout his life.
Access to clean water is still a dim and distant dream in some remote communities.

Synset Examples

He spoke of his dim and distant dreams of fame.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pipe dream - an unrealistic or fanciful hope
