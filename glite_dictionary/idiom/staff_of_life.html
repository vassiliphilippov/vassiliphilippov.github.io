staff of life

1. NOUN

/ stæf əv laɪf / (AmE), / stɑːf əv laɪf / (BrE)
bread, regarded as the basic sustenance of life or a staple food, especially in the form made from flour and baked
bread as a staple food

Examples

For many cultures, bread is truly the staff of life, sustaining generations through hardship.
During the war, people struggled to find even the basic staff of life on some days.
Whole grain varieties of the staff of life are valued for their nutritional benefits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bread - food made from baked dough of flour or meal

Etymology

From 'staff' meaning support and 'life', referring to bread as the essential support of life.
