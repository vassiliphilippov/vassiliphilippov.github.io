at your door

1. ADP

/ ət jɔr dɔr / (AmE), / ət jɔː dɔː / (BrE)
(of an opportunity or event) immediately available to you or imminent.
immediately available or impending

Examples

After years of hard work, success is finally at your door.
With new technology at your door, it's easier than ever to start a business.
Change may be at your door before you even realize it.

Synset Examples

The opportunity to travel is at your door.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at hand - close in time or space; readily available
  • Synonym: on the horizon - likely to happen soon; approaching
