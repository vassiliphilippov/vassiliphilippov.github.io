to write the book on

1. VERB

/ raɪt ðə bʊk ɑn / (AmE), / raɪt ðə bʊk ɒn / (BrE)
to be a leading authority or expert on a particular subject, often through extensive knowledge or experience
be a leading authority on a subject

Examples

She really wrote the book on interior design, setting trends that others still follow today.
If anyone can write the book on network security, it's our lead engineer.
When it comes to Italian cuisine, he practically wrote the book on it.

Synset Examples

If anyone can write the book on leadership, it's her.
They claim to write the book on customer service excellence.
He decided to write the book on marketing strategies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expert - a person with a high level of knowledge or skill in a particular subject
  • Synonym: authority - a person who is an accepted source of knowledge on a subject
