as a wet hen

1. ADV

/ æz ə wɛt hɛn / (AmE), / az ə wɛt hɛn / (BrE)
extremely unhappy, annoyed, or dejected
extremely unhappy or annoyed

Examples

After hearing the bad news, he looked as a wet hen all evening.
She was as a wet hen when her team lost the match.
He stormed out of the meeting, as a wet hen after being criticized.

Synset Examples

She felt miserable as a wet hen after losing her job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miserable - feeling very unhappy or uncomfortable
  • Antonym: content - feeling satisfied and at ease
