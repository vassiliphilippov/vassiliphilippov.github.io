dead and buried

1. ADJ

/ dɛd ənd ˈbɛrid / (AmE), / dɛd ənd ˈbɛrid / (BrE)
completely finished, resolved, or no longer relevant; something that is over and will not return or be discussed again
completely finished or resolved

Examples

After the merger, the old company policies were dead and buried.
She insists that the controversy is now dead and buried.
With the new agreement in place, their disagreements are finally dead and buried.

Synset Examples

The project is dead and buried after the funding cut.
Their feud is dead and buried after the apology.
All rumors about her resignation are dead and buried.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: over - finished; at an end
  • Synonym: settled - resolved; decided and unlikely to change
