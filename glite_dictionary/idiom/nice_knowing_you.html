nice knowing you

1. X

/ naɪs ˈnoʊɪŋ ju / (AmE), / naɪs ˈnəʊɪŋ juː / (BrE)
used to express polite farewell, especially when expecting not to see someone again.
farewell expression; polite goodbye

Examples

It was nice knowing you; I wish you all the best in your new job.
She smiled and said, "Nice knowing you," before walking out of the office for the last time.
After graduation, he hugged his friends and whispered, "Nice knowing you."

Synset Examples

After the meeting, I said nice knowing you to the team.
As he left, she waved, saying nice knowing you.
I guess it's time to say nice knowing you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - a farewell used to express parting
  • Synonym: farewell - expression of good wishes on parting
