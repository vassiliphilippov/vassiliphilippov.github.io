to close one's eyes for the last time

1. VERB

/ kloʊz wʌnz aɪz fɔr ðə læst taɪm / (AmE), / kləʊz wʌnz aɪz fə ðə lɑːst taɪm / (BrE)
to die; used as a gentle or euphemistic way of referring to death
to die (euphemism)

Examples

After a long illness, she finally closed her eyes for the last time in peace.
Many believe he closed his eyes for the last time surrounded by those he loved.
As the storm raged outside, the old man closed his eyes for the last time with a smile.

Synset Examples

As the sun set, he felt ready to close his eyes for the last time.
He closed his eyes for the last time, surrounded by family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pass away - to die, used as a polite or gentle expression
  • Synonym: die - to stop living
