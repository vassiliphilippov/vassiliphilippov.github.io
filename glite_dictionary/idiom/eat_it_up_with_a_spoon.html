to eat it up with a spoon

1. VERB

/ it ʌp wɪð ə spun / (AmE), / iːt ɪt ʌp wɪð ə spuːn / (BrE)
To enjoy something extremely; to take great delight or pleasure in something, often used in reference to delicious food or highly enjoyable experiences.
enjoy something very much

Examples

The children ate it up with a spoon when the magician performed his final trick.
Everyone ate it up with a spoon when the band returned for an encore.
The guests ate it up with a spoon as the chef described the dessert menu.

Synset Examples

The ice cream was so delicious, I wanted to eat it up with a spoon.
That cake is so good, I could eat it up with a spoon!
His grandma’s soup is so comforting, you could eat it up with a spoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relish - to greatly enjoy or take pleasure in something
  • Synonym: savor - to enjoy or appreciate something fully, especially food or an experience
