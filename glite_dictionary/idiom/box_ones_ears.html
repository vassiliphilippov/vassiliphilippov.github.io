to box one's ears

1. VERB

/ bɑks wʌnz ɪrz / (AmE), / bɒks wʌnz ɪəz / (BrE)
to hit someone on the side of the head or ears, usually as a punishment or reprimand
to hit someone on the ears
archaic

Examples

If you don't behave, I'll box your ears like your grandmother used to.
The teacher threatened to box his ears for talking back in class.
She jokingly warned she would box his ears if he forgot again.

Synset Examples

He threatened to box my ears for being late.
If I don't finish my homework, my dad will box my ears.
She jokingly said she'd box his ears for his mistake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strike - to hit someone or something with force
  • Synonym: slap - to hit someone with the flat part of the hand
