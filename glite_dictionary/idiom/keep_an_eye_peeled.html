to keep an eye peeled

1. VERB

/ kip ən aɪ pild / (AmE), / kiːp ən aɪ piːld / (BrE)
to be alert and watchful, especially for something specific
be alert; watch carefully

Examples

When you're walking alone at night, make sure to keep an eye peeled for any unusual activity.
Tourists are advised to keep an eye peeled for pickpockets in crowded areas.
I'll keep an eye peeled for your friend at the entrance.

Synset Examples

Keep an eye peeled for the new restaurant opening.
Keep an eye peeled during the event for surprises.
You should keep an eye peeled for any discounts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to keep an eye out - to watch carefully for something
  • Synonym: keep a lookout - to watch carefully for something to happen
