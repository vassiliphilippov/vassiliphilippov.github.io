off the chain

1. ADJ

/ ɒf ðə tʃeɪn / (AmE), / ɒf ðə tʃeɪn / (BrE)
(informal) Extremely good or impressive; also, literally, released or free to move around.
Extremely impressive; released/free
informal

Examples

Her performance was off the chain and the crowd went wild.
That new video game is off the chain—you have to try it!
As soon as we let the dog off the chain, he sprinted around the yard.

Synset Examples

Her performance at the concert was off the chain.
The party was off the chain with music and dancing all night.
They decided to let fluffy off the chain for some exercise.
When I let fluffy off the chain, he ran straight to the park.
That new restaurant is off the chain with its unique dishes.

Related Terms
