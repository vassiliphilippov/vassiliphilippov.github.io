pain in the neck

1. NOUN

/ peɪn ɪn ðə nɛk / (AmE), / peɪn ɪn ðə nɛk / (BrE)
a person or thing that is very annoying or troublesome
annoying or troublesome person or thing

Examples

My little brother can be such a pain in the neck when he follows me everywhere.
All the paperwork is a real pain in the neck for small business owners.
Trying to find parking downtown during rush hour is a pain in the neck.
