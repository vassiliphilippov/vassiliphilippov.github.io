fire in someone's heart

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚ ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz hɑrt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz hɑːt / (BrE)
a strong passion, enthusiasm, or inner drive for something
strong passion or enthusiasm

Examples

Despite many failures, she never lost the fire in her heart for discovery.
The coach's fire in his heart inspired the whole team to play better.
Even at eighty, he had a fire in his heart for learning new things.

Synset Examples

He has a fire in his heart for helping others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passion - strong and barely controllable emotion
  • Synonym: enthusiasm - intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval
