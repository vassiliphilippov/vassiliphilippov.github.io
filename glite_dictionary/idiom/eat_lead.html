to eat lead

1. VERB

/ it lɛd / (AmE), / iːt lɛd / (BrE)
(informal) to be shot at with bullets, especially in games, movies, or slang expressions.
to be shot at with bullets (idiom)
informal slang

Examples

The gangster shouted, 'Time to eat lead!', as he aimed his gun at the heroes.
If you don't duck in this game, you'll eat lead pretty quickly.
In the old western movies, the sheriff made the outlaws eat lead.

Synset Examples

The villain threatened to make him eat lead.
In the video game, you eat lead with every shot.
He joked that he could eat lead if necessary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get shot - to be hit by a bullet

Etymology

From slang use of 'lead' to refer to bullets due to their material; 'eat' used figuratively for receiving something unwelcome.
