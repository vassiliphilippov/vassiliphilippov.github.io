floating in ether

1. X

/ ˈfloʊtɪŋ ɪn ˈiːθər / (AmE), / ˈfləʊtɪŋ ɪn ˈiːθə / (BrE)
feeling light, detached, or disconnected from reality, as if in a dreamlike or euphoric state
feeling detached from reality

Examples

After the news, he walked home floating in ether, oblivious to the world around him.
She described her experience as floating in ether after recovering from the anesthesia.
For hours after the concert, I felt like I was floating in ether.

Synset Examples

She felt as if she was floating in ether.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of touch - unaware of reality or current events
  • Antonym: grounded - emotionally or mentally stable and connected to reality
