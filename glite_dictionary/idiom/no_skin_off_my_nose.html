no skin off my nose

1. X

/ noʊ skɪn ɔf maɪ noʊz / (AmE), / nəʊ skɪn ɒf maɪ nəʊz / (BrE)
used to say that one does not care about or is not affected by what happens.
not affected or concerned

Examples

If you want to stay out late, it’s no skin off my nose.
It’s no skin off my nose if they don’t invite me to the meeting.
He can disagree all he wants—it's no skin off my nose either way.

Synset Examples

Her decision doesn’t bother me; it’s no skin off my nose.
You can dress however you like; it’s no skin off my nose.
If he wants to quit, it’s no skin off my nose.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no skin off my back - used to say that something does not affect or concern someone
