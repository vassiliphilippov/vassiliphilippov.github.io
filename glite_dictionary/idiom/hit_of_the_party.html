hit of the party

1. NOUN

/ hɪt əv ðə ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / hɪt əv ðə ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
the most popular or successful person, thing, or element at a party or social event
most popular element at an event

Examples

Her homemade cookies were the hit of the party and disappeared within minutes.
The magician's tricks turned out to be the hit of the party for both kids and adults.
Everyone agreed that the giant piñata was the hit of the party this year.

Synset Examples

His froggo costume was the hit of the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life of the party - the person who is most lively and entertaining at a party
