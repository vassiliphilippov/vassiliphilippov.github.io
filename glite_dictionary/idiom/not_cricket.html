not cricket

1. X

/ ˌnɑt ˈkrɪkət / (AmE), / ˌnɒt ˈkrɪkɪt / (BrE)
used to describe behavior that is unfair, improper, or not in accordance with accepted rules or standards, especially in a sporting or social context
unfair or improper conduct
british

Examples

It's simply not cricket to insult someone behind their back.
Many considered his business tactics not cricket and refused to work with him.
She apologized, realizing her outburst at the meeting was not cricket.

Synset Examples

It's not cricket to lie about your score.
Cheating in the game is just not cricket.
His behavior at the match was not cricket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unfair - not just or equitable
  • Synonym: dishonest - behaving in a way that is not truthful or fair
  • Antonym: fair - in accordance with the rules or standards; legitimate

Etymology

From the British sport of cricket, where fair play is emphasized
