to lift a finger

1. VERB

/ lɪft ə ˈfɪŋɡɚ / (AmE), / lɪft ə ˈfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
to make even a small effort to help; to do anything to assist someone or something, usually used in negative constructions.
to make any effort to help

Examples

Even though I was sick, he didn't lift a finger to help me.
She won't lift a finger around the house unless you ask her repeatedly.
They expect me to do all the work while they never lift a finger.

Synset Examples

He refuses to lift a finger for charity.
He wouldn’t lift a finger to help.
She didn't lift a finger during the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: help - to give assistance or support
