at the wheel

1. ADV

/ æt ðə wiːl / (AmE), / at ðə wiːl / (BrE)
in control or in charge of a situation, especially guiding or leading a group or effort
in control or in charge; leading

Examples

With a new CEO at the wheel, the company began to thrive once again.
I feel confident knowing you’re at the wheel during this critical project phase.
It was clear someone needed to be at the wheel to guide the discussion productively.

Synset Examples

She has been at the wheel throughout this challenging phase.
He’s been at the wheel of the project since day one.
I trust her to be at the wheel during the presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in charge - having control over or responsibility for something
  • Synonym: at the helm - in charge or in control of an organization or situation
