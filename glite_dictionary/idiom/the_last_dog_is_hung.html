the last dog is hung

1. X

/ ðə læst dɔɡ ɪz hʌŋ / (AmE), / ðə lɑːst dɒɡ ɪz hʌŋ / (BrE)
used to emphasize persistence or continued effort until everything is finished or resolved, no matter how long it takes
persist until everything is finished

Examples

She vowed to pursue her dreams until the last dog is hung, no matter the challenges.
Negotiations dragged on until the last dog is hung, with no side willing to give up.
The team kept trying until the last dog is hung, refusing to concede defeat.

Synset Examples

I will fight for justice until the last dog is hung.
He promised to work hard until the last dog is hung.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: until the bitter end - persisting until the absolute conclusion
  • Synonym: see it through - to persist until completion
