like a porcelain doll

1. ADJ

/ laɪk ə ˈpɔrs(ə)lɪn dɑl / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈpɔːs(ə)lɪn dɒl / (BrE)
(of a person) having an appearance that is delicate, fragile, and beautiful, like that of a finely crafted porcelain doll.
delicate and beautiful in appearance

Examples

Her skin was so smooth and pale, she looked like a porcelain doll in the soft light.
The little girl, dressed in lace and ribbons, appeared like a porcelain doll at the party.
He described her features as delicate and perfect, almost like a porcelain doll.

Synset Examples

She looked delicate, like a porcelain doll in that dress.
