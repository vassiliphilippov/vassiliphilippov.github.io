all over bar the shouting

1. X

/ ɔl ˈoʊvər bɑr ðə ˈʃaʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈəʊvə bɑː ðə ˈʃaʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
used to indicate that the result or outcome of a situation is already decided, with only minor formalities remaining
outcome is already decided; only formalities left

Examples

After the committee's vote, it was all over bar the shouting for the proposal.
With such a big lead, the match was all over bar the shouting by halftime.
The merger is all over bar the shouting, just waiting for signatures.

Synset Examples

With that last-minute goal, it's all over bar the shouting.
The negotiations are done; it's all over bar the shouting.
The game was all over bar the shouting after the last goal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: a foregone conclusion - an outcome that is certain or inevitable
