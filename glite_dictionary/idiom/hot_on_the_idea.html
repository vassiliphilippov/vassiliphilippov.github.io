hot on the idea

1. ADJ

/ hɑt ɑn ði aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / hɒt ɒn ði aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
very enthusiastic or keen about a particular idea or proposal
very enthusiastic about an idea
informal

Examples

The investors were hot on the idea of expanding into new markets.
I'm not hot on the idea of working overtime every weekend.
City officials are suddenly hot on the idea of adding more bike lanes to the streets.

Synset Examples

She’s hot on the idea of starting a new project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keen on - very interested in or enthusiastic about something
  • Antonym: lukewarm about - showing little enthusiasm for something
