sacr bleu

1. INTJ

/ sak ˈbluː / (AmE), / sak ˈbluː / (BrE)
an exclamation expressing surprise or shock, used to imitate French speakers or evoke a French context
expression of surprise or shock

Examples

Sacr bleu! I forgot my passport at home!
The chef exclaimed 'sacr bleu!' as the sauce boiled over the pan.
When he saw the score, he could only mutter, 'sacr bleu!'

Synset Examples

He shouted, 'Sacr bleu! That’s an outrageous claim!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodness gracious - used to express surprise or shock
  • Synonym: oh my god - used to express surprise or astonishment

Etymology

From French 'sacré bleu', a mild oath meaning 'holy blue', historically invoking the Virgin Mary.
