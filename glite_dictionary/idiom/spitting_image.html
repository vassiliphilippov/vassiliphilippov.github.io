spitting image

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪtɪŋ ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈspɪtɪŋ ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
a person or thing that closely resembles another, especially in appearance; an exact likeness
exact likeness or close resemblance

Examples

Ever since she was born, people have said she’s the spitting image of her grandmother.
Standing next to his father, he is truly the spitting image of him.
All her classmates agree that she’s the spitting image of her older sister.

Synset Examples

She’s the spitting image of her mother.
That kid is the spitting image of his father.
You are the spitting image of your sister!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double - a person who looks exactly like another
  • Synonym: lookalike - a person who closely resembles another
  • Antonym: opposite - completely different in appearance

Etymology

Possibly an alteration of 'spit and image' or 'spit image', where 'spit' means exact likeness, dating to the 19th century.
