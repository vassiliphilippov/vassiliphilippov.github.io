whatever it takes

1. X

/ wəˈtɛvər ɪt teɪks / (AmE), / wəˈtɛvə ɪt teɪks / (BrE)
used to express a willingness to do anything necessary to achieve a goal, regardless of difficulty or obstacles.
prepared to do anything necessary

Examples

He promised to protect his family, whatever it takes.
They are determined to get the job done, whatever it takes.
She said she'd make the team, whatever it takes.

Synset Examples

I'll win this race, whatever it takes.
We will succeed, whatever it takes.
She will finish the project, whatever it takes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: by any means - using any methods or resources necessary
  • Synonym: at all costs - no matter what difficulties or expense arise
