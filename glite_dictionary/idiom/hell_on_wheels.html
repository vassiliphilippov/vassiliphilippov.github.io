hell on wheels

1. NOUN

/ hɛl ɑn wilz / (AmE), / hɛl ɒn wiːlz / (BrE)
(informal) A person or thing that is exceptionally energetic, aggressive, reckless, or exciting, often in a way that is difficult to control.
very energetic or aggressive person or thing
informal

Examples

When he plays hockey, he's hell on wheels, charging at every opponent.
That old sports car may look tame, but on the track it's hell on wheels.
Don't mess with her when she's angry—she's hell on wheels in an argument.

Synset Examples

That new motorcycle is a hell on wheels.
He drives like a hell on wheels, always speeding.
She’s a hell on wheels when she gets angry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wild - unrestrained or out of control
  • Synonym: reckless - without regard for danger
  • Antonym: calm - not excited or aggressive; peaceful
