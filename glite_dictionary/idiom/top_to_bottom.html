top to bottom

1. ADV

/ tɑp tə ˈbɑtəm / (AmE), / tɒp tə ˈbɒtəm / (BrE)
in every part; completely or thoroughly
completely; in every part

Examples

The committee reviewed the proposal top to bottom before making a decision.
She cleaned the kitchen top to bottom in preparation for the party.
After the merger, the company was reorganized top to bottom.
