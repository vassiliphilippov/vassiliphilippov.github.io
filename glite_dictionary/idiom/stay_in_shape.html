to stay in shape

1. VERB

/ steɪ ɪn ʃeɪp / (AmE), / steɪ ɪn ʃeɪp / (BrE)
to maintain physical fitness and keep the body healthy and fit
maintain physical fitness

Examples

It's important to stay in shape if you want to have enough energy throughout the day.
She joins a fitness class every week to stay in shape.
Many people stay in shape by cycling or walking to work.

Synset Examples

He stays in shape by jogging every morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keep fit - remain physically healthy and in good condition
