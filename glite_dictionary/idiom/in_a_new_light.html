in a new light

1. ADV

/ ɪn ə nuː laɪt / (AmE), / ɪn ə njuː laɪt / (BrE)
considered or shown from a different or changed perspective, often revealing new qualities or aspects.
seen from a different perspective

Examples

After reading the biography, I began to see the famous author in a new light.
The new evidence cast the accused man's actions in a new light.
Her explanation helped us view the situation in a new light.

Synset Examples

The artist will portray the city in a new light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: from another angle - considering something from a different perspective
  • Synonym: in a different light - viewed or interpreted differently
