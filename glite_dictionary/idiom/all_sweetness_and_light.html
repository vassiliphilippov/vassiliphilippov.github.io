all sweetness and light

1. NOUN

/ ɔl ˈswiːtnəs ænd laɪt / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈswiːtnəs ənd laɪt / (BrE)
a state or atmosphere marked by harmony, pleasantness, and absence of conflict
state of harmony and positivity

Examples

The meeting was all sweetness and light until disagreements arose.
Despite their differences, their public appearance was all sweetness and light.
Don't be fooled—the friendly tone was all sweetness and light just for the cameras.

Synset Examples

The meeting was all sweetness and light until disagreements arose.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harmony - a state of agreement or peaceful coexistence
  • Antonym: discord - a lack of agreement or harmony

Etymology

Popularized in English by Matthew Arnold in the 19th century, originally drawn from Jonathan Swift's works.
