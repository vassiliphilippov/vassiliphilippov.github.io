by heart

1. ADV

/ baɪ hɑrt / (AmE), / baɪ hɑːt / (BrE)
from memory; by memorizing completely without needing to refer to written material.
from memory; memorized

Examples

She recited the entire speech by heart in front of the audience.
He can play dozens of songs by heart without looking at the sheet music.
Students were asked to learn the periodic table by heart for the exam.

Synset Examples

she knew the poem by heart

Related Terms

  • Synonym: by rote - by mechanical or habitual repetition without understanding
