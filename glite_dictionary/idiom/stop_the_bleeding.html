to stop the bleeding

1. VERB

/ stɑp ðə ˈbliːdɪŋ / (AmE), / stɒp ðə ˈbliːdɪŋ / (BrE)
to halt the loss of blood or to prevent further financial loss or damage in a difficult situation
halt loss of blood or worsening losses

Examples

The quick-thinking nurse was able to stop the bleeding before the doctor arrived.
Management implemented new policies to stop the bleeding after heavy financial losses.
We must stop the bleeding in our team morale before it affects productivity further.
