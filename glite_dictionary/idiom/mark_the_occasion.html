to mark the occasion

1. VERB

/ mɑrk ði əˈkeɪʒən / (AmE), / mɑːk ði əˈkeɪʒən / (BrE)
to celebrate or commemorate a significant event in a special way
celebrate or commemorate an event

Examples

We opened a bottle of champagne to mark the occasion.
They planted a tree in the garden to mark the occasion of their anniversary.
The company held a ceremony to mark the occasion of its 50th anniversary.

Synset Examples
