base over apex

1. ADV

/ beɪs oʊvər ˈeɪpɛks / (AmE), / beɪs əʊvə ˈeɪpɛks / (BrE)
In an upside-down or tumbling manner, often used to describe someone or something that overturns completely in a fall or movement.
headlong tumble; overturned

Examples

The cyclist went base over apex after hitting a hidden bump in the road.
When the chair tipped over, she tumbled base over apex onto the floor.
The dog rolled base over apex chasing after the ball.

Synset Examples

She landed base over apex after misjudging the jump.
He fell base over apex during the skateboarding trick.
The gymnast went base over apex on the uneven bars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head over heels - In a tumbling or completely inverted position; also, very much in love

Etymology

Modelled on the older idiom 'head over heels', replacing anatomical terms with the geometric 'base' and 'apex'.
