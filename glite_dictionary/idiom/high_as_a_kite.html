high as a kite

1. ADJ

/ haɪ əz ə kaɪt / (AmE), / haɪ əz ə kaɪt / (BrE)
extremely happy, euphoric, or in a state of great excitement; sometimes refers to being intoxicated, especially by drugs or alcohol.
extremely happy or euphoric
informal

Examples

After getting the good news about her promotion, she felt high as a kite all day.
The children were high as a kite after eating so much birthday cake and candy.
He walked into the room looking high as a kite, grinning from ear to ear.
