written all over it

1. X

/ ˈrɪt.ən ɔl ˈoʊ.vɚ ɪt / (AmE), / ˈrɪt.ən ɔːl ˈəʊ.vər ɪt / (BrE)
used to say that something is very obvious or clearly evident, often about a particular quality or emotion.
very obvious; clearly evident

Examples

Her excitement was written all over it when she opened the acceptance letter.
The guilt was written all over it, even though he tried to deny everything.
This plan has success written all over it.

Synset Examples

That idea has potential written all over it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plain as day - completely obvious or clear
  • Antonym: hidden - not obvious or easily seen
