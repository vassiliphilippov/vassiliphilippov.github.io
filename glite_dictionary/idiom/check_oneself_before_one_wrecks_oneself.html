to check oneself before one wrecks oneself

1. VERB

/ tʃɛk wʌnˈsɛlf bɪˈfɔɹ wʌn rɛks wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / tʃɛk wʌnˈsɛlf bɪˈfɔː wʌn rɛks wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to reconsider or carefully evaluate one's actions, attitudes, or words to avoid causing harm or getting into trouble
pause to avoid harming oneself
informal

Examples

Before responding in anger, it's wise to check oneself before one wrecks oneself.
He always tells his team to check oneself before one wrecks oneself when making big decisions.
If you want to avoid drama, just remember to check oneself before one wrecks oneself.

Synset Examples

He learned to check himself before he wrecked himself in arguments.
She often says, 'Check yourself before you wreck yourself' to her friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to think twice - to reconsider or reflect before acting

Etymology

Popularized in American hip-hop culture in the early 1990s, notably by rapper Ice Cube.
