clear and to the point

1. ADJ

/ klɪr ənd tu ðə pɔɪnt / (AmE), / klɪə ənd tə ðə pɔɪnt / (BrE)
expressed in a straightforward and concise manner, without unnecessary details
straightforward and concise; direct

Examples

The manager's instructions were clear and to the point, making the task easy to understand.
Please keep your presentations clear and to the point to respect everyone's time.
Her feedback was always clear and to the point, so I knew exactly what to change.

Synset Examples

Her advice was clear and to the point.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: succinct - expressed clearly and in a few words
  • Synonym: direct - straightforward in manner or speech
  • Antonym: long-winded - using too many words or overly detailed
