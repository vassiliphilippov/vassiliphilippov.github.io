jaw dropped

1. X

/ dʒɑ ˈdrɑpt / (AmE), / dʒɔː ˈdrɒpt / (BrE)
used to express that someone is very surprised or shocked, shown by opening their mouth wide
expression of shock or surprise

Examples

Everyone's jaw dropped when the magician pulled a rabbit out of the hat.
My jaw dropped as I looked at the price tag on the designer dress.
Their jaw dropped when they heard the unexpected news.

Synset Examples

When she won the award, his jaw dropped.
Seeing the surprise party, his jaw dropped.
Her jaw dropped at the stunning view.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jaw-dropping - extremely surprising or impressive
  • Synonym: stunned - too surprised or shocked to speak
