to steal someone's heart

1. VERB

/ stil sʌmˈwʌnz hɑrt / (AmE), / stiːl sʌmˈwʌnz hɑːt / (BrE)
to win or gain someone's affection or love, often unexpectedly or charmingly
win someone's affection or love

Examples

The charming actor managed to steal everyone's heart with his performance.
It took only one smile for her to steal his heart.
The orphaned kitten easily stole the hearts of the whole family.

Synset Examples

He knew he could steal her heart with kindness.
She always knew how to steal someone's heart effortlessly.
The puppy's antics quickly stole everyone's heart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: win someone's heart - to gain the love or affection of someone
