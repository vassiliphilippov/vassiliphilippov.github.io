to piss oneself

1. VERB

/ pɪs wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / pɪs wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) To laugh so hard or become so frightened that one loses control of one's bladder; often used hyperbolically to mean laugh uncontrollably.
lose bladder control from laughter or fear (vulgar, informal)
informal vulgar

Examples

When he told that joke, I nearly pissed myself laughing.
The haunted house was so scary that Tom thought he might piss himself.
She was laughing so hard she actually pissed herself.

Synset Examples

Watching that movie made me piss myself from laughing.
He laughed so hard he nearly pissed himself.
I nearly pissed myself when that prank happened.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wet oneself - to urinate involuntarily, usually from fear or laughter
