the joke is on someone

1. X

/ ðə dʒoʊk ɪz ɑn ˈsʌmwʌn / (AmE), / ðə dʒəʊk ɪz ɒn ˈsʌmwʌn / (BrE)
used to say that someone who tried to cause trouble or make others look foolish has ended up looking foolish themselves
to end up looking foolish

Examples

He tried to embarrass me during the meeting, but in the end, the joke was on him.
They planned an elaborate prank, but the joke was on them when their trick failed.
If you think you can fool her that easily, the joke is on you.

Synset Examples

He thought he was clever, but the joke is on him.
They tried to prank me, but the joke is on them.
If you believe that, the joke is on you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get a taste of one's own medicine - someone experiences the same unpleasantness they have caused others
