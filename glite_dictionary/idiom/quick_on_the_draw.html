quick on the draw

1. ADJ

/ kwɪk ɑn ðə drɔ / (AmE), / kwɪk ɒn ðə drɔː / (BrE)
able to react or respond quickly, especially in a challenging or tense situation
reacting or responding quickly

Examples

When the alarm sounded, the security team was quick on the draw and responded within seconds.
You have to be quick on the draw if you want to succeed in this fast-paced industry.
Her wit makes her quick on the draw during heated discussions.

Synset Examples

In a debate, she’s quick on the draw with facts.
He’s quick on the draw when it comes to making decisions.
The sheriff was known to be quick on the draw.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: slow on the uptake - not understanding or reacting quickly

Etymology

Originally from the language of the American Old West, referring to drawing a gun quickly.
