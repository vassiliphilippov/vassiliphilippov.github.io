to scream one's head off

1. VERB

/ skrim wʌnz hɛd ɔf / (AmE), / skriːm wʌnz hɛd ɒf / (BrE)
to scream extremely loudly and for a prolonged period, often due to fear, excitement, or distress
shout or scream very loudly

Examples

The kids screamed their heads off during the scary movie.
She screamed her head off when she got the news.
Fans screamed their heads off as the band walked on stage.

Synset Examples

We screamed our heads off on the rollercoaster.
She screamed her head off when she saw the surprise party.
They screamed their heads off at the rock concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yell - to shout loudly
  • Synonym: shriek - to make a high-pitched piercing cry
  • Antonym: whisper - to speak very quietly
