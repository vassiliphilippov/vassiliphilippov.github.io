praise to the skies

1. X

/ ˈpreɪz tə ðə skaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpreɪz tə ðə skaɪz / (BrE)
to praise someone or something extremely highly or enthusiastically.
extreme or enthusiastic praise

Examples

The critics gave her performance praise to the skies after the premiere.
His volunteers were given praise to the skies for their dedication and hard work.
The new park design has drawn praise to the skies from the local community.

Synset Examples
