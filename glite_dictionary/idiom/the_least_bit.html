the least bit

1. ADV

/ ðə list bɪt / (AmE), / ðə liːst bɪt / (BrE)
in the smallest possible degree; at all.
in the slightest degree; at all

Examples

She didn't look the least bit worried about the upcoming exam.
If you're the least bit tired, you should take a rest.
He wasn't the least bit interested in what I had to say.

Synset Examples

Are you at all interested? No, not at all
was not in the least unfriendly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at all - in any way or to any extent
