see ya

1. INTJ

/ si ˈjə / (AmE), / si ˈjə / (BrE)
(informal) Used to say goodbye to someone; see you later.
informal farewell expression
informal

Examples

"See ya!" she called out as she left the office.
I’ll see ya at the party tonight.
He waved and said, "See ya tomorrow morning!"

Synset Examples

After the meeting, I'll be seeing ya at lunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - an expression used to indicate parting
  • Synonym: see you - a casual way to say goodbye
