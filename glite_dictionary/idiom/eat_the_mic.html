to eat the mic

1. VERB

/ it ðə maɪk / (AmE), / iːt ðə maɪk / (BrE)
to perform or speak into a microphone with great confidence and energy, often engaging the audience
speak or perform energetically into a microphone
informal

Examples

The band's lead singer really knows how to eat the mic during live shows.
When she started to eat the mic, the audience immediately paid attention.
Radio hosts often need to eat the mic to create an energetic atmosphere.
