in the stratosphere

1. ADP

/ ɪn ðə ˈstrætəsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈstratəˌsfɪə / (BrE)
(of prices, ambitions, ideas, etc.) extremely high, lofty, or out of the ordinary range.
extremely high or lofty (idiom)

Examples

Since the announcement, house prices have gone in the stratosphere in this area.
Her expectations for the project were in the stratosphere, making it difficult to meet them.
Interest rates were in the stratosphere during the economic crisis.

Synset Examples

The prices for those products are in the stratosphere.
Her ambitions are in the stratosphere, aiming for the top.
His ideas are always in the stratosphere, beyond ordinary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sky-high - extremely high (especially of prices or numbers)
  • Synonym: astronomical - extremely large or high
