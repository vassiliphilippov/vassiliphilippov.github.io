to go climb a tree

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ klaɪm ə triː / (AmE), / ɡəʊ klaɪm ə triː / (BrE)
(informal) used to tell someone to go away or stop annoying you.
tell someone to go away (informal idiom)
informal

Examples

If you're going to keep complaining, go climb a tree and leave me alone.
He shrugged off the rude comment and simply said, 'Go climb a tree.'
When Tim started whining again, his sister told him to go climb a tree.

Synset Examples

When he complained again, she told him to go climb a tree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go fly a kite - used to tell someone to go away or stop bothering you
  • Synonym: get lost - go away; stop bothering someone
