in the running

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ðə ˈrʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈrʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
having a chance of being selected or winning a competition, award, or position; still being considered as a candidate.
having a chance of being selected or winning

Examples

Despite his late start, he is still in the running for top honors.
Only three teams are now in the running for the championship title.
Her impressive performance has put her in the running for a promotion.

Synset Examples

Several candidates are still in the running for the position.
He remains in the running for the athlete of the year award.
She is in the running for the scholarship this year.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: out of the running - no longer considered as a candidate or able to win
