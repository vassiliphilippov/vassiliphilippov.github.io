cock and bull story

1. NOUN

/ kɑk ənd bʊl ˈstɔri / (AmE), / kɒk ənd bʊl ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
an absurd or implausible story invented to deceive or explain something away
an unlikely, invented story

Examples

I don't believe that cock and bull story about why you were late.
The politician gave a cock and bull story to avoid answering the real question.
Everyone could see through his cock and bull story about the missing documents.

Synset Examples

His excuse was just a Banbury story of a cock and a bull.
That was a typical Banbury story of a cock and a bull!
She didn't believe a word of his Banbury story of a cock and a bull.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tall story - an exaggerated or unbelievable story
  • Antonym: truth - something that is true or factual

Etymology

Possibly from fanciful tales involving a cock and a bull, popularized in 17th-century England; origin uncertain.
