like all possessed

1. ADV

/ laɪk ɔl pəˈzɛst / (AmE), / laɪk ɔːl pəˈzɛst / (BrE)
with intense energy, effort, or frenzy; as if driven by a powerful force
with intense energy or frenzy

Examples

The children ran around the yard like all possessed until they were exhausted.
She cleaned the house like all possessed before her guests arrived.
During the final minutes, the team fought like all possessed to win the game.

Synset Examples

He played the game like all possessed, ignoring the score.
She worked on that project like all possessed, fueled by coffee.
They were dancing like all possessed at the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: like mad - with great energy or intensity
  • Synonym: like crazy - with wild, uncontrolled energy or enthusiasm

Etymology

From the phrase 'possessed,' meaning controlled by a spirit or strong emotion; alludes to acting as if under supernatural influence.
