of import

1. ADJ

/ ʌv ɪmˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ɒv ɪmˈpɔːt / (BrE)
of great significance or value; important
significant; important

Examples

The decision made by the committee is of import to the entire community.
She only discusses matters of import in these meetings.
Historical events of import often shape a nation's identity for generations.

Synset Examples

important people
the important questions of the day

Related Terms

  • Synonym: important - having great significance or value

Etymology

From Latin 'importare' (to bring in), root of 'import' meaning significance in this sense.
