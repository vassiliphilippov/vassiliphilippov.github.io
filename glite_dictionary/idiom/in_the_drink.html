in the drink

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ðə drɪŋk / (AmE), / ɪn ðə drɪŋk / (BrE)
In a state of drunkenness or intoxication.
drunk; intoxicated
informal

Examples

After getting bad news, he spent the night in the drink with old friends.
She was so in the drink that she couldn't find her way home.
He promised not to fall in the drink again at office parties.

Synset Examples

He ended up in the drink at the party last night.
She found herself in the drink after the breakup.
After a tough week, he spent the evening in the drink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inebriated - being drunk or intoxicated
  • Antonym: sober - not under the influence of alcohol
