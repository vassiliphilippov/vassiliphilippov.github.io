to bring the party

1. VERB

/ brɪŋ ðə ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / brɪŋ ðə ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
to create a lively and enjoyable atmosphere, especially in a social setting
make a place lively or fun
informal

Examples

Whenever Chloe arrives, she truly brings the party to life.
If you want to bring the party, make sure to bring your best playlist and enthusiasm.
Tyler knows how to bring the party, turning even a dull gathering into something memorable.

Synset Examples

Big Mike always brings the party wherever he goes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to liven up - to make something more lively or exciting
  • Antonym: dampen the mood - to reduce excitement or enjoyment in a situation
