to come to mind

1. VERB

/ kʌm tə maɪnd / (AmE), / kʌm tə maɪnd / (BrE)
(of an idea, memory, or thought) to be remembered or thought of suddenly or naturally.
occur or be recalled to one's mind

Examples

Whenever I think of summer, long days at the beach come to mind.
Her kindness is the first thing that comes to mind when I hear her name.
No good reason came to mind when he was asked to explain his absence.

Synset Examples

The best memories come to mind when I see that photo.
His name didn't come to mind during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occur to - to come into someone's mind as a thought
  • Antonym: to forget - to fail to remember
