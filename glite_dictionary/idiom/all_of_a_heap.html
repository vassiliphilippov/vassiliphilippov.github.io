all of a heap

1. ADV

/ ˈɔl əv ə ˈhip / (AmE), / ˈɔːl əv ə ˈhiːp / (BrE)
in a sudden, disorganized, or uncontrolled manner; into a state of confusion or disorder
suddenly and disorganized; in confusion

Examples

After sprinting to the finish line, he collapsed all of a heap on the grass.
The papers fell all of a heap when I knocked over the stack.
She dropped all of a heap onto the sofa, exhausted from the day's work.

Synset Examples

The books were scattered all of a heap on the floor.
