heart is in it

1. X

/ ˈhɑrt ɪz ɪn ɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɑːt ɪz ɪn ɪt / (BrE)
Fully committed or passionate about an activity or cause.
being fully committed or passionate

Examples

She works incredibly hard because her heart is in it.
If your heart is in it, you'll find the motivation to keep going even when it's tough.
Their heart is in it when volunteering for the animal shelter.

Synset Examples

When someone's heart is in it, success follows.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heart isn't in it - not fully committed or lacking passion
