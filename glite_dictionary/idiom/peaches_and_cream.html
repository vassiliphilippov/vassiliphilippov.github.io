peaches and cream

1. NOUN

/ ˈpitʃɪz ənd ˈkrim / (AmE), / ˈpiːtʃɪz ənd ˈkriːm / (BrE)
A situation or condition that is very pleasant, harmonious, or enjoyable.
very pleasant or harmonious situation

Examples

Winning the lottery made everything peaches and cream for him for a while.
Their marriage hasn't always been peaches and cream, but they work through difficulties together.
Some people think life as a celebrity is all peaches and cream, but it has its challenges.

Synset Examples

Their friendship was as perfect as peaches and cream.
Life in the countryside felt like peaches and cream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bed of roses - an easy or pleasant situation
