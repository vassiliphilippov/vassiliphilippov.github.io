on purpose

1. ADV

/ ɑn ˈpɝpəs / (AmE), / ɒn ˈpɜːpəs / (BrE)
Intentionally; with the aim of achieving a particular result; not by accident.
intentionally; not by accident

Examples

Did you leave the door open on purpose, or was it a mistake?
She broke the vase on purpose to get attention.
I think he ignored my message on purpose because he's upset.

Synset Examples

he used that word intentionally
I did this by choice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intentionally - deliberately; with intention
  • Antonym: accidentally - by accident; not on purpose

2. ADV

/ ɑn ˈpɝːpəs / (AmE), / ɒn ˈpɜːpəs / (BrE)
intentionally; with deliberate intention, not by accident
intentionally; not by accident

Examples

I bumped into you on purpose because I needed your attention.
She left the door unlocked on purpose to see if anyone would notice.
Did he really break the vase on purpose, or was it an accident?

Synset Examples

He forgot the meeting on purpose to avoid confrontation.
I did that on purpose to test your reaction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deliberately - done consciously and intentionally
  • Antonym: accidentally - happening by chance or without intention
