to work on all cylinders

1. VERB

/ wɜrk ɑn ɔl ˈsɪlɪndɚz / (AmE), / wɜːk ɒn ɔːl ˈsɪlɪndəz / (BrE)
to operate with maximum energy, efficiency, or effectiveness
operate at maximum efficiency

Examples

Whenever the team faces a big project, they work on all cylinders to get it done on time.
During the busy season, she's working on all cylinders to manage all her responsibilities.
The company needs everyone to work on all cylinders if it wants to meet its targets.

Synset Examples

She was working fire on all cylinders to meet the deadline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fire on all cylinders - to operate at peak efficiency

Etymology

From the imagery of multi-cylinder engines performing at full power.
