good God

1. INTJ

/ ɡʊd ɡɑd / (AmE), / ɡʊd ɡɒd / (BrE)
used as an exclamation to express surprise, dismay, or disbelief
expression of surprise or dismay

Examples

Good God, look at the size of that cake!
When he heard the news, he exclaimed, "good God!"
Good God, what happened to your car?

Synset Examples

Good God, it's freezing outside!
When I saw the bill, I thought, good God!
Good God, I can't believe you did that!
