poor devil

1. NOUN

/ pɔr ˈdɛvəl / (AmE), / pɔː ˈdɛvəl / (BrE)
a person deserving of pity or sympathy; someone whom you feel sorry for
someone deserving of pity

Examples

He was just a poor devil who got caught up in the wrong place at the wrong time.
You can't help but feel sorry for that poor devil after everything he's been through.
The poor devil had lost his job and his home within the same week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wretch - an unfortunate or pitiable person
  • Antonym: fortunate - someone having good luck or favorable circumstances
