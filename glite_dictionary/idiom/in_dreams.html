in dreams

1. ADV

/ ɪn driːmz / (AmE), / ɪn driːmz / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is very unlikely or impossible to happen
highly unlikely or impossible

Examples

Winning the championship this year is possible only in dreams.
He imagined owning a castle, but that would happen only in dreams.
That movie star dating a regular fan? Maybe in dreams!

Synset Examples

She thinks he’ll ask her out, but that's just in her dreams.
He'll win the lottery only in his dreams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: not in a million years - used to emphasize something will never happen
  • Synonym: when pigs fly - used to indicate something impossible or extremely unlikely
