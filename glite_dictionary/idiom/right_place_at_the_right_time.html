right place at the right time

1. NOUN

/ raɪt pleɪs æt ðə raɪt taɪm / (AmE), / raɪt pleɪs æt ðə raɪt taɪm / (BrE)
in a situation or position where circumstances are most favorable for success, often by chance
in a favorable or fortunate situation due to timing

Examples

She got the job because she was in the right place at the right time.
Sometimes success comes down to being in the right place at the right time.
He met his future business partner simply by being in the right place at the right time.

Synset Examples

He was in the right place at the right time for the promotion.
You need luck to be in the right place at the right time.
Being in the right place at the right time helped her career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good luck - chance happening leading to fortune or success
