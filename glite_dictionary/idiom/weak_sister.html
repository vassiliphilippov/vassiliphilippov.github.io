weak sister

1. NOUN

/ wik ˈsɪstər / (AmE), / wiːk ˈsɪstə / (BrE)
a member of a group who is considered cowardly or the least capable compared to others
least capable or cowardly member of a group

Examples

Whenever a challenge arose, he was labeled the weak sister of the team.
Despite her achievements, some still called her the weak sister in the family business.
The company's downfall was blamed on a weak sister in upper management.

Synset Examples

They call her the weak sister for always backing down.
In our group, she's the weak sister who needs help.
The project failed because he was the weak sister.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weakling - a person lacking in strength or character
  • Synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
  • Antonym: strongman - a person who is strong or the strongest in a group
