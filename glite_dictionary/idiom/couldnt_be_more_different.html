couldn't be more different

1. X

/ ˈkʊdənt bi mɔr ˈdɪfrənt / (AmE), / ˈkʊdnt bi ˈmɔː ˈdɪfrənt / (BrE)
used to emphasize that two or more people or things are extremely different from each other
emphasizes extreme difference

Examples

Although they are twins, their personalities couldn't be more different.
The two political parties couldn't be more different in their approaches to the economy.
Our opinions on the film couldn't be more different; I loved it, but she hated it.

Synset Examples

Their tastes in music couldn't be more different.
Their personalities couldn't be more different.
The two movies couldn't be more different in style.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: couldn't be more alike - used to emphasize that two or more things are extremely similar
