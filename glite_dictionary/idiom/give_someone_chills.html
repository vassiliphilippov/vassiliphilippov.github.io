to give someone chills

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ˈsʌmwʌn tʃɪlz / (AmE), / ɡɪv ˈsʌmwən tʃɪlz / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel a sudden sensation of fear, excitement, or awe, often accompanied by a physical reaction such as shivering
cause a feeling of fear or excitement

Examples

The haunting melody of the song gave me chills every time I listened to it.
Watching the suspenseful scene in the movie gave her chills.
The chilling news report gave everyone chills in the newsroom.

Synset Examples

The dank atmosphere of the cave gave me chills.
The legend of Spiders Georg still gives me chills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to send shivers down someone's spine - to cause someone to feel fear or excitement, often with a shivering sensation
  • Degree Higher: to terrify - to fill with intense fear
