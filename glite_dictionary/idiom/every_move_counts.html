every move counts

1. X

/ ˈɛv.ri muv kaʊnts / (AmE), / ˈɛv.ri muːv kaʊnts / (BrE)
Every action or decision is significant and can affect the final outcome.
each action is important

Examples

When you're trying to beat a world record, every move counts.
During the negotiation, she knew that every move counts.
In a chess tournament, experienced players understand that every move counts.

Synset Examples
