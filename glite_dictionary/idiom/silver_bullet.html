silver bullet

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ ˌbʊlət / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˌbʊlɪt / (BrE)
A simple and seemingly magical solution to a complicated or difficult problem, especially one that is unlikely to exist.
simple solution to a complex problem

Examples

There is no silver bullet for fixing the education system overnight.
Many hoped that the new software would be a silver bullet, but it only solved a few issues.
The CEO warned the team not to expect a silver bullet to resolve all their challenges.

Synset Examples

no silver bullet can make the world safe from terrorism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panacea - a solution or remedy for all difficulties or diseases

Etymology

From the folklore belief that supernatural creatures could only be killed by bullets made of silver.
