to grab life by the throat

1. VERB

/ ɡræb laɪf baɪ ðə θroʊt / (AmE), / ɡræb laɪf baɪ ðə θrəʊt / (BrE)
to act decisively and energetically to take full advantage of opportunities in life
seize opportunities in life boldly

Examples

After years of hesitation, she decided to grab life by the throat and start her own business.
He encouraged his son to grab life by the throat rather than let fear hold him back.
If you want to achieve your dreams, you need to grab life by the throat and take bold steps.

Synset Examples
