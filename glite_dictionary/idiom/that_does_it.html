that does it

1. X

/ ðæt ˈdʌz ɪt / (AmE), / ðæt ˈdʌz ɪt / (BrE)
used to say that a final limit has been reached and no more will be tolerated; enough is enough
final limit has been reached

Examples

That does it, I'm not going to tolerate this behavior anymore.
He forgot my birthday again—that does it!
You've lied to me one too many times; that does it.

Synset Examples

I've had enough; that does it!
If you break another thing, that does it!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enough is enough - used to say that you will not accept a situation any longer
