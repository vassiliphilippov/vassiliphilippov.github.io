to start a new life

1. VERB

/ stɑɹt ə nu laɪf / (AmE), / stɑːt ə njuː laɪf / (BrE)
to begin living in a completely different way, often after a major change or event
begin living differently after change

Examples

After the divorce, he decided to start a new life in another country.
Many people move to big cities to start a new life and pursue their dreams.
She wanted to leave her past behind and start a new life with her children.

Synset Examples

After serving time, she started a new life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make a fresh start - to begin again with a new approach or attitude
