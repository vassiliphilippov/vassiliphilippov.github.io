to bend one's elbow

1. VERB

/ bɛnd wʌnz ˈɛlboʊ / (AmE), / bɛnd wʌnz ˈɛlbəʊ / (BrE)
(informal) To drink alcoholic beverages, especially habitually or in a social setting.
(idiom) drink alcohol
informal

Examples

After a long week's work, he loves to bend his elbow at the local tavern.
Many old friends gathered to bend their elbows and share stories at the reunion.
She prefers tea and rarely bends her elbow even on special occasions.

Synset Examples

She likes to bend her elbow with friends at the bar.
He doesn’t often bend his elbow at social events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drink - to consume liquids, especially alcohol
