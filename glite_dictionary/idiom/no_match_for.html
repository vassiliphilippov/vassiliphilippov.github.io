no match for

1. ADJ

/ noʊ mætʃ fɔr / (AmE), / nəʊ mætʃ fɔː / (BrE)
not equal to or able to compete with someone or something; inferior in strength, ability, or quality
not equal to; unable to compete with

Examples

Despite his best efforts, Tom was no match for the professional chess player.
The tiny boat was no match for the powerful storm on the lake.
Her arguments were no match for his years of experience in the field.

Synset Examples

The challengers will be no match for the champion.
His skills will be no match for her experience.
Her enthusiasm will be no match for his determination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inferior to - of lower rank, status, or quality than
  • Antonym: superior to - of higher rank, status, or quality than
