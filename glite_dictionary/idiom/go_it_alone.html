to go it alone

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ ɪt əˈloʊn / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ɪt əˈləʊn / (BrE)
to do something without the help or support of others
do something without help or support

Examples

After much consideration, she decided to go it alone and open her own restaurant.
Some entrepreneurs prefer to go it alone rather than seek business partners.
You don't have to go it alone—we can help you with your project.

Synset Examples

He decided to go it alone and start his own business.
I always thought I'd go it alone if needed.
She decided to go-it-alone for the project.
He prefers to go-it-alone rather than team up.
Going it alone can be rewarding but tough.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go solo - to do something independently, without others
  • Antonym: to team up - to join or work with others for a shared goal
