in the heat of

1. ADP

/ ɪn ðə hit ʌv / (AmE), / ɪn ðə hiːt ɒv / (BrE)
during a very intense or emotional moment or situation
during a very intense moment

Examples

He admitted that, in the heat of the debate, he said things he did not mean.
The error occurred in the heat of battle, when everyone was under pressure.
She apologized for her words spoken in the heat of the moment.
