without cease

1. ADV

/ wɪˈðaʊt sis / (AmE), / wɪˈðaʊt siːs / (BrE)
continuously; without stopping
continuously, without stopping

Examples

The rain fell without cease throughout the night.
He worked without cease to finish the project on time.
The protests continued without cease for several weeks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ceaseless - never ending or stopping
  • Synonym: unceasingly - in a manner that does not stop
