proud as a peacock

1. ADJ

/ praʊd əz ə ˈpiː.kɑk / (AmE), / praʊd əz ə ˈpiː.kɒk / (BrE)
extremely proud or self-satisfied, especially in a noticeable or showy way
extremely self-satisfied or showy

Examples

Ever since his promotion, he's been proud as a peacock around the office.
The team stood proud as a peacock after their big win in the championship game.
When she aced her final exams, her father was proud as a peacock.

Synset Examples

The new parents were proud as a peacock at the baby shower.
She walked into the room, proud as a peacock.
He was proud as a peacock after winning the award.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proud - feeling deep pleasure or satisfaction as a result of achievements
  • Antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's importance
