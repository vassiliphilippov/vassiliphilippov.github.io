to bend my brain

1. VERB

/ bɛnd maɪ breɪn / (AmE), / bɛnd maɪ breɪn / (BrE)
to think intensely or creatively, often causing mental strain or confusion
think hard or with difficulty
informal

Examples

This complex math problem is really bending my brain.
She had to bend her brain to come up with an original idea for the project.
Don't bend your brain over the details—we'll sort them out together.

Synset Examples

I need to bend my brain to solve this riddle.
Don't bend your brain too much over this problem.
This puzzle is really bending my brain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rack my brain - to try very hard to remember or think of something
