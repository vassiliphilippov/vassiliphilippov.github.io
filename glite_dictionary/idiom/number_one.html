number one

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌmbɚ ˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbə ˈwʌn / (BrE)
(informal) Yourself; used especially to refer to putting your own interests or needs above those of others.
oneself; own interests
informal

Examples

He always puts number one first, even if it means hurting others.
When making tough decisions, she tends to look out for number one.
It's important not to neglect others while you're focusing on number one.
