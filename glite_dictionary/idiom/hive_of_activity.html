hive of activity

1. NOUN

/ haɪv əv ækˈtɪv.ə.ti / (AmE), / haɪv əv ækˈtɪv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
A place that is very busy, with many people moving around and doing things energetically.
a very busy and active place

Examples

When the concert ended, the lobby became a hive of activity as fans headed for the exits.
During the holiday season, the kitchen is always a hive of activity.
As soon as the store opened, it turned into a hive of activity with shoppers everywhere.

Synset Examples

The market was a hive of activity on Saturday mornings.
The construction site was a hive of activity all day long.
The office became a hive of activity before the deadline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beehive - a structure where bees live and work, often used metaphorically for busy activity

Etymology

Metaphor from a beehive, which is always busy with bees working.
