caught up in office politics

1. X

/ kɔt ʌp ɪn ˈɔfɪs pəˈlɪtɪks / (AmE), / kɔːt ʌp ɪn ˈɒfɪs pəˈlɪtɪks / (BrE)
become involved in conflicts, rivalries, or maneuverings among colleagues in the workplace, often to one's disadvantage
involved in workplace conflicts

Examples

After just a few weeks, she was caught up in office politics and felt overwhelmed by colleagues' alliances.
Many people leave jobs because they get caught up in office politics instead of focusing on their work.
Try not to become caught up in office politics, as it can drain your motivation and energy.

Synset Examples

He got caught up in office politics and lost trust.
