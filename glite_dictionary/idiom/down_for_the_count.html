down for the count

1. ADJ

/ daʊn fər ðə kaʊnt / (AmE), / daʊn fə ðə kaʊnt / (BrE)
completely exhausted or incapacitated; unable to continue
exhausted; unable to continue

Examples

By midnight, everyone at the party was down for the count after hours of dancing.
The flu had him down for the count for over a week.
After running the marathon, she was so tired she felt down for the count all day.

Synset Examples

After the exhausting race, he was down for the count.
She was feeling tired and down for the count yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausted - extremely tired
  • Synonym: incapacitated - unable to function or continue

Etymology

From boxing, where a competitor knocked down is counted out if unable to rise within a set count.
