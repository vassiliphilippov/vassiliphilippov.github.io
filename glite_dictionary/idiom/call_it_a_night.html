to call it a night

1. VERB

/ kɔl ɪt ə naɪt / (AmE), / kɔːl ɪt ə naɪt / (BrE)
to stop what you are doing for the evening, especially in order to go to bed or leave for home
stop activities for the evening

Examples

It's late, so let's call it a night and finish up tomorrow.
After four hours of studying, they agreed to call it a night and get some rest.
The group decided to call it a night after the last song played.

Synset Examples

I think we should call it a night and relax.
It's getting late; let's call it a night.
She decided to call it a night after the concert.
Let's call it a night and head home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to call it a day - to stop what you are doing, especially work, for the rest of the day
