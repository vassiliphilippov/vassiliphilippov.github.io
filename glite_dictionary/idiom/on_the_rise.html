on the rise

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə raɪz / (AmE), / ɒn ðə raɪz / (BrE)
increasing in amount, frequency, or popularity
increasing; becoming more common or popular

Examples

Homeownership is on the rise as mortgage rates decrease.
Reports of tick-borne illnesses are on the rise in rural communities.
Interest in plant-based diets is on the rise among young adults.

Synset Examples

Interest in renewable energy is on the rise globally.
The company's profits are on the rise this quarter.
Crime rates in the area are unfortunately on the rise.
Hate speech from heteronazi factions is on the rise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: increasing - becoming greater or larger in amount or degree
  • Antonym: on the decline - decreasing; becoming less common or popular
