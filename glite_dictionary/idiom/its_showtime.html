it's showtime

1. INTJ

/ ɪts ˈʃoʊ.taɪm / (AmE), / ɪts ˈʃəʊ.taɪm / (BrE)
Used to signal the beginning of an important event or performance, often with excitement or anticipation.
start of an important event or performance

Examples

As the lights dimmed, the director whispered, "It's showtime, everyone!"
Before the presentation began, Mark smiled at his team and said, "It's showtime."
Standing behind the curtain, she took a deep breath and reminded herself, "It's showtime now."

Synset Examples

He declared, 'It's showtime!' before stepping onto the stage.
