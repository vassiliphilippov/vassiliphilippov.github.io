ready to roll

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛdi tə roʊl / (AmE), / ˈrɛdi tə rəʊl / (BrE)
fully prepared to begin or start an activity or event
prepared to begin
informal

Examples

As soon as everyone is here, we’ll be ready to roll.
The team is ready to roll for the big game tonight.
Once all the equipment was checked, she declared they were ready to roll.

Synset Examples

Once the lights dimmed, we were ready to roll.
The project is approved and we’re ready to roll!
The band was finally ready to roll after soundcheck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: set - prepared and ready
  • Synonym: good to go - fully prepared to start
