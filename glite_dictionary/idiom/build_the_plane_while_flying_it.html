build the plane while flying it

1. X

/ bɪld ðə pleɪn waɪl flaɪɪŋ ɪt / (AmE), / bɪld ðə pleɪn waɪl flaɪɪŋ ɪt / (BrE)
to work on creating or improving something while it is already in use or operation, often under pressure or difficult circumstances
improve or create while already operating
