to sleep with one eye open

1. VERB

/ sliːp wɪð wʌn aɪ ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / sliːp wɪð wʌn aɪ ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
to remain watchful or alert even while resting or sleeping, usually because of fear or suspicion
be alert or cautious while resting

Examples

After hearing strange noises outside, he decided to sleep with one eye open that night.
When traveling alone, she always feels she has to sleep with one eye open.
The security guard claimed he could sleep with one eye open, never missing a thing.

Synset Examples

In a new neighborhood, I had to sleep with one eye open.
You should sleep with one eye open if you're camping in the wild.
After the break-in, she learned to sleep with one eye open.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be on guard - to be watchful in order to avoid danger or trouble
  • Antonym: let one's guard down - to stop being alert or cautious
