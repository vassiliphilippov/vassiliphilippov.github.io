moonbat theory

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːnbæt ˈθɪəri / (AmE), / ˈmuːnbæt ˈθɪəri / (BrE)
an informal and often derogatory term for an eccentric or bizarre theory, typically implausible or lacking evidence
eccentric or bizarre theory (informal, derogatory)
informal slang

Examples

Many dismissed his moonbat theory about the government controlling the weather.
Social media can spread a moonbat theory far beyond its original audience.
Her uncle delights in telling yet another moonbat theory every holiday dinner.

Synset Examples

His moonbat theories about aliens were hard to take seriously.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conspiracy theory - an explanation for an event or situation that invokes a conspiracy

Etymology

From 'moonbat', US political slang for an eccentric or delusional person, combining 'moon' and 'bat' (suggesting lunacy).
