to soar above the rim

1. VERB

/ sɔr əˈbʌv ðə rɪm / (AmE), / sɔː əˈbʌv ðə rɪm / (BrE)
to jump very high during basketball play, especially when attempting to score by dunking the ball
jumping high to score in basketball

Examples

The crowd cheered as he soared above the rim for a thunderous dunk.
In the final seconds, she soared above the rim to secure the game-winning basket.
Young players dream of one day soaring above the rim on a professional court.

Synset Examples

His dunk was spectacular, soaring above the rim effortlessly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dunk - to score by thrusting the ball through the basket from above in basketball
  • Degree Lower: to lay up - a close-range shot in basketball, typically off the backboard
