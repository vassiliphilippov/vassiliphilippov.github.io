at the ready

1. ADJ

/ æt ðə ˈrɛdi / (AmE), / at ðə ˈrɛdi / (BrE)
available and prepared for immediate use or action.
prepared for immediate use

Examples

The fire extinguisher was kept at the ready in case of emergencies.
The guards stood at the ready as the convoy approached the gate.
She always has snacks at the ready for unexpected visitors.

Synset Examples

soldiers with guns at the ready
students with pens and notebooks at the ready

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ready - prepared for immediate action or use

2. ADV

/ ət ðə ˈrɛdi / (AmE), / ət ðə ˈrɛdi / (BrE)
prepared for immediate use or action; ready to be used or acted upon instantly
ready for immediate use

Examples

The paramedics had their equipment at the ready in case of an emergency.
She always keeps several pens at the ready for note-taking during meetings.
Soldiers waited at the ready, anticipating the command to move forward.

Synset Examples
