measure twice, cut once

1. X

/ ˈmɛʒɚ twaɪs kʌt wʌns / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒə twaɪs kʌt wʌns / (BrE)
used to advise that one should check something carefully before taking action, to avoid making mistakes
double-check before acting

Examples

You should always measure twice, cut once when doing any home improvement project.
Her father taught her to measure twice, cut once whenever making important decisions.
The slogan measure twice, cut once is displayed in the workshop as a reminder to work carefully.

Synset Examples

In carpentry, it's crucial to measure twice and cut once.
Remember to measure twice and cut once for accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to look before you leap - think carefully before acting
