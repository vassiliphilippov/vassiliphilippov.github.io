man of the cloth

1. NOUN

/ mæn əv ðə klɔθ / (AmE), / mæn əv ðə klɒθ / (BrE)
a male member of the clergy, especially in the Christian Church
male member of the clergy

Examples

After years as a teacher, he became a man of the cloth and dedicated his life to helping others.
The man of the cloth addressed the congregation with compassion and wisdom.
Despite being a man of the cloth, he enjoyed discussing philosophy and science with his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clergyman - a male priest, minister, or religious leader
  • Antonym: woman of the cloth - a female member of the clergy
