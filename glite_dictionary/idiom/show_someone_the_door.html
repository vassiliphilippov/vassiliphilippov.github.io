to show someone the door

1. VERB

/ ʃoʊ ˈsʌmwʌn ðə dɔɹ / (AmE), / ʃəʊ ˈsʌmwʌn ðə dɔː / (BrE)
to ask someone to leave a place, especially in a forceful or unfriendly way; to dismiss or eject someone
forcefully dismiss someone

Examples

After his repeated complaints, the manager decided to show him the door.
If you insult the staff again, we will have to show you the door.
She politely but firmly showed him the door after the meeting.

Synset Examples

When he got rude, I had to show him the door.
If he can't behave, I'll show him the door.
She decided to show him the door after the argument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to dismiss - to send someone away; to remove from a place or position
  • Synonym: to expel - to force someone to leave a place or organization
