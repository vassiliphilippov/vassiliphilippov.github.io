to stand up for justice

1. VERB

/ stænd ʌp fər ˈdʒʌstɪs / (AmE), / stænd ʌp fə ˈdʒʌstɪs / (BrE)
to speak or act in support of fairness or moral rightness, especially when it is challenged or threatened
defend the cause of fairness

Examples

It takes courage to stand up for justice when others remain silent.
She has always inspired her students to stand up for justice wherever they see wrongdoing.
Many leaders throughout history risked their lives to stand up for justice.

Synset Examples

They made a stunning and brave choice to stand up for justice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to defend - to protect or support someone or something from attack or criticism
  • Antonym: to oppose - to act against something, often perceived as unfair or wrong
