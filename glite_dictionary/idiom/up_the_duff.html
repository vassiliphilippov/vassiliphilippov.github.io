up the duff

1. ADJ

/ ʌp ðə dʌf / (AmE), / ʌp ðə dʌf / (BrE)
(informal, British) Pregnant.
pregnant (informal, British)
informal british

They joked that Sally must be up the duff because she's been feeling unwell.
