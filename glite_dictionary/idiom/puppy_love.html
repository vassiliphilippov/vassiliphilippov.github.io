puppy love

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌpi lʌv / (AmE), / ˈpʌpi lʌv / (BrE)
a strong but shallow romantic attraction, especially common among young people or adolescents
adolescent or superficial romantic feelings

Examples

Many people remember their first puppy love from middle school.
What seemed like puppy love turned into a lasting friendship after the summer.
Parents often dismiss puppy love as unimportant, but it feels very real to teenagers.

Synset Examples

Their puppy love faded after the summer ended.
They giggled about their puppy love during lunch.
Teenage puppy love often feels like the real thing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infatuation - an intense but short-lived passion or admiration for someone
