no siree Bob

1. INTJ

/ ˌnoʊ səˈriː ˈbɑb / (AmE), / ˌnəʊ səˈriː ˈbɒb / (BrE)
used to express strong or emphatic refusal; an emphatic way of saying 'no'.
emphatic expression of refusal
informal

Examples

No siree Bob, I wouldn't dream of trusting him with my car.
When asked if I wanted to skydive, I replied, 'no siree Bob!'
No siree Bob, there's no way I'm eating that much spicy food again.

Synset Examples

I’m not going back to that restaurant, no siree Bob.
When asked if I’d jump off that cliff, I said no siree Bob!
You'll never catch me doing that again, no siree Bob.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no way - used to show refusal or that something is impossible
  • Synonym: not on your life - emphatic refusal or negative response

Etymology

Alteration of 'no sir', with added emphasis by 'Bob' for rhythm and force; 'Bob' is arbitrary, not referring to a specific person.
