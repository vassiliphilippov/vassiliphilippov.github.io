at its peak

1. ADV

/ æt ɪts piːk / (AmE), / æt ɪts piːk / (BrE)
at the highest point or level of strength, success, or intensity
at the highest point or level

Examples

The athlete was at its peak during the championship, breaking multiple records.
The company's profits were at its peak last year before declining.
Wildflowers are at its peak in early summer, creating a colorful landscape.

Synset Examples

Noontime is when the sun is at its peak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at its height - at the most intense or successful stage
