to perfection

1. ADV

/ tə pərˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / tə pəˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
in a way that is perfect or shows the highest possible standard; in every detail
perfectly; in every detail

Examples

She baked the cake to perfection, with a golden crust and moist center.
He tailored the suit to perfection, so it fit her flawlessly.
The chef prepared the steak to perfection, satisfying even the most demanding customers.

Synset Examples

the new house suited them to a T

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfectly - in a perfect or flawless way
