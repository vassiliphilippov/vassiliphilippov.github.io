to get weaving

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈwiːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈwiːvɪŋ / (BrE)
(informal) to begin doing something quickly; to start to be active or to hurry up.
start to act quickly; hurry up
informal
