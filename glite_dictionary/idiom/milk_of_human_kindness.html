milk of human kindness

1. NOUN

/ mɪlk əv ˈhjuːmən ˈkaɪnd.nəs / (AmE), / mɪlk əv ˈhjuːmən ˈkaɪnd.nəs / (BrE)
compassion and innate human generosity; a natural tendency to be kind and understanding towards others
compassionate and generous nature

Examples

Showing the milk of human kindness, the neighbors brought food to the family after their loss.
During difficult times, the milk of human kindness can bring hope and comfort to many people.
Her decision to forgive showed she was full of the milk of human kindness.

Synset Examples

The community's support was the milk of human kindness in action.
During tough times, he showed the milk of human kindness to everyone.
Her gentle words were truly the milk of human kindness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compassion - sympathetic pity and concern for others
  • Synonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate

Etymology

Popularized by Shakespeare in Macbeth (c. 1606): 'It is too full o' the milk of human kindness.'
