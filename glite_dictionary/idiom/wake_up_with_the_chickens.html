to wake up with the chickens

1. VERB

/ weɪk ʌp wɪð ðə ˈtʃɪk.ənz / (AmE), / weɪk ʌp wɪð ðə ˈtʃɪk.ɪnz / (BrE)
to wake up very early in the morning, typically at sunrise
wake up very early in the morning

Examples

My grandmother always wakes up with the chickens to tend her garden before breakfast.
If you want to catch the sunrise, you'll need to wake up with the chickens tomorrow.
He hates having to wake up with the chickens for work, but he enjoys the quiet.

Synset Examples

She wakes up with the chickens to start her day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rise with the sun - to get up at sunrise or very early
