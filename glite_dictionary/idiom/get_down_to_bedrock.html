to get down to bedrock

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt daʊn tə ˈbɛdˌrɑk / (AmE), / ɡɛt daʊn tə ˈbɛd.rɒk / (BrE)
to address or examine the most basic or fundamental issues of a situation
address fundamental issues

Examples

Before the company can recover, we have to get down to bedrock and address the underlying problems.
This report helps us get down to bedrock in our analysis of the financial crisis.
It's time to put aside minor concerns and get down to bedrock on what really matters.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to get to the bottom of - find the real cause or basic truth
