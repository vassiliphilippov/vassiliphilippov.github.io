to give the mitten

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ðə ˈmɪtn̩ / (AmE), / ɡɪv ðə ˈmɪtn̩ / (BrE)
to reject or dismiss someone, especially by ending a romantic relationship
to reject or end a romantic relationship

Examples

After several months of dating, she decided to give the mitten to her boyfriend.
He was devastated when his girlfriend gave the mitten after their argument.
Sometimes it's kinder to give the mitten than remain in an unhappy relationship.

Synset Examples

He was unhappy and decided to give her the mitten.
After their argument, he gave her the mitten.
She had to give the mitten to her unreliable partner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: give the boot - to dismiss or fire someone
  • Synonym: dump - to abruptly end a romantic relationship with someone

Etymology

From 19th-century American slang, possibly relating to the practice of giving a mitten to a rejected suitor in European customs.
