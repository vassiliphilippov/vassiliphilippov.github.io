light on one's feet

1. ADJ

/ laɪt ɑn wʌnz fit / (AmE), / laɪt ɒn wʌnz fiːt / (BrE)
able to move quickly and easily, especially with grace or agility
nimble and agile in movement

Examples

The ballet dancer is so light on her feet that she seems to float across the stage.
Even in his sixties, he remains remarkably light on his feet while playing tennis.
Our new puppy is incredibly light on his feet, darting around the house with ease.

Synset Examples

She is surprisingly light on her feet for a dancer.
He moved light on his feet during the performance.
The boxer was light on his feet, dodging punches effortlessly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nimble - quick and light in movement or action
  • Antonym: clumsy - moving awkwardly or without grace
