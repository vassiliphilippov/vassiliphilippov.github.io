straight shooter

1. NOUN

/ streɪt ˈʃuːtər / (AmE), / streɪt ˈʃuːtə / (BrE)
A person who is honest and direct in their speech or actions, without evasion or deceit.
person who is honest and direct

Examples

Everyone on the team respects her because she's a straight shooter who never hides the facts.
If you want honest feedback, ask Tom—he's a total straight shooter.
The company needs a straight shooter to lead them through these challenges.

Synset Examples

She appreciated his straight shooter approach to feedback.
Being a straight shooter helps build strong relationships.
You can trust him; he's a straight shooter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: truth-teller - someone who tells the truth
  • Antonym: liar - a person who tells lies
