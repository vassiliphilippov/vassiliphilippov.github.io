built like a hulk

1. ADJ

/ bɪlt laɪk ə hʌlk / (AmE), / bɪlt laɪk ə hʌlk / (BrE)
(of a person) having an extremely muscular and powerful physique.
very muscular and strong (of physique)
informal

Examples

Everyone at the gym noticed he was built like a hulk after months of training.
People say my uncle is built like a hulk because of his years as a wrestler.
Even in a suit, you can tell she's built like a hulk.

Synset Examples

He’s built like a hulk and can lift heavy weights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muscular - having well-developed muscles
  • Antonym: skinny - very thin, lacking muscle mass
