as timeless as Adam and Eve

1. ADJ

/ əz ˈtaɪmləs æz ˌædəm ənd ˈiv / (AmE), / əz ˈtaɪmləs əz ˌædəm ən ˈiːv / (BrE)
used to describe something as enduring, classic, or eternal, often referencing the biblical couple's association with the beginning of human stories
eternal; enduring like Adam and Eve

Examples

Their love for each other was as timeless as Adam and Eve, unbroken through decades.
Some fashions remain as timeless as Adam and Eve, never going out of style.
The tale is as timeless as Adam and Eve, resonating with people of every generation.

Synset Examples

Their love story was as timeless as Adam and Eve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: timeless - without beginning or end; eternal
