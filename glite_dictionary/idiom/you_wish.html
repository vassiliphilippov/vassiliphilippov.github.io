you wish

1. INTJ

/ ju wɪʃ / (AmE), / juː wɪʃ / (BrE)
used to express disbelief, sarcasm, or to tell someone that what they want is unlikely to happen
expression of disbelief or sarcasm
informal

Examples

"I can borrow your car for the weekend?" "You wish!"
When he said he'd finish all his work before noon, I just said, "You wish."
She thinks the teacher will let her skip the exam? You wish!

Synset Examples

I can have that car? You wish!
She thinks she can win the lottery? You wish!
