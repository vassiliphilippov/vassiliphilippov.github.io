with a view to

1. SCONJ

/ wɪð ə vjuː tu / (AmE), / wɪð ə vjuː tuː / (BrE)
with the intention of doing something; for the purpose of achieving something
with the intention of; for the purpose of

Examples

She saved money with a view to buying a house.
The company invested in research with a view to improving product quality.
He attended the meeting with a view to forming new business partnerships.

Synset Examples

he took the computer with a view to pawning it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in order to - with the aim of doing something
