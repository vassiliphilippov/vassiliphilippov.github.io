to know beans about

1. VERB

/ noʊ binz əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / nəʊ biːnz əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to have very little or no knowledge about something
have little knowledge about
informal

Examples

I don't know beans about computer programming, so I hired an expert.
She doesn't know beans about wine, but she likes to pretend she does.
Even after months of training, he still doesn't know beans about the new software.

Synset Examples

I don't know beans about this subject.
She claims to know beans about investing.
He doesn't know beans about cooking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: know nothing about - to not have any knowledge about something
  • Synonym: be clueless about - to have no idea or understanding about something
