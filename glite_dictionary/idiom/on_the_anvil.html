on the anvil

1. ADV

/ ɑn ði ˈænvəl / (AmE), / ɒn ði ˈænvɪl / (BrE)
in the process of being developed, considered, or planned, but not yet completed or finalized
in development or planning stage

Examples

The new policy is still on the anvil and will be announced next month.
Several technological innovations are on the anvil at this research institute.
Her latest book is on the anvil, awaiting the final round of editing.

Synset Examples

Her promotion is on the anvil, pending approval.
Several new projects are currently on the anvil.
The reforms are still on the anvil, not finalized yet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the pipeline - in the process of being developed or completed

Etymology

From the literal image of an anvil as a place where metal is shaped or worked, figuratively referring to something being 'worked on.'
