what a hassle

1. INTJ

/ wʌt ə ˈhæsəl / (AmE), / wɒt ə ˈhæsəl / (BrE)
used to express annoyance or frustration about something that is difficult or inconvenient
expression of annoyance or inconvenience

Examples

I had to redo all the paperwork after a computer error—what a hassle!
She missed her train and lost her ticket, what a hassle that was.
The website kept crashing during checkout, and I thought, what a hassle.

Synset Examples

He forgot the password again-what a hassle!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hassle - difficulty or inconvenience; something that causes annoyance
