outta here

1. X

/ ˈaʊɾə hɪr / (AmE), / ˈaʊtə hɪə / (BrE)
used to say that someone is leaving a place, typically suddenly or quickly
leaving quickly; departing
informal

Examples

It's late, so I'm outta here for the night.
After finishing his work, Jake said he was outta here.
Once she heard the bad news, she was outta here in a flash.

Synset Examples

I’m outta here before the snowstorm hits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leave - to go away from a place
  • Antonym: to arrive - to reach a place, opposite of leave
