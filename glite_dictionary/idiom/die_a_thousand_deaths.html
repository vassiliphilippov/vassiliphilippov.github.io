to die a thousand deaths

1. VERB

/ daɪ ə ˈθaʊzənd dɛθs / (AmE), / daɪ ə ˈθaʊzənd dɛθs / (BrE)
to experience extreme anxiety, fear, or distress, often repeatedly or intensely
experience intense anxiety or dread

Examples

I thought I would die a thousand deaths while waiting for my turn to speak.
He died a thousand deaths when he realized he had lost his passport.
She was dying a thousand deaths as the suspense grew before the winner was announced.

Synset Examples

Waiting for the results made me feel like I'd die a thousand deaths.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to agonize - to suffer extreme physical or mental pain or anguish
  • Synonym: worry - to feel anxious or concerned about something
