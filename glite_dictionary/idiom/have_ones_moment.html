to have one's moment

1. VERB

/ hæv wʌnz ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / hæv wʌnz ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
to experience occasions when one displays notable qualities or behavior, especially when these are not typical.
experience notable episodes or behavior

Examples

She may seem quiet, but she certainly has her moments of brilliance at work.
Even the strictest teachers have their moments of kindness and humor.
The old actor had his moment in the spotlight during the awards ceremony.

Synset Examples

He certainly has his moments of humor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shine - to perform extremely well or be outstanding
