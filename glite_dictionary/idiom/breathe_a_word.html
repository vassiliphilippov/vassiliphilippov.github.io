to breathe a word

1. VERB

/ briːð ə wɜrd / (AmE), / briːð ə wɜːd / (BrE)
to reveal or disclose a secret or information; to say anything about something that should be kept confidential
reveal or share a secret

Examples

If you breathe a word of this plan, the whole surprise will be ruined.
She was so trustworthy that she didn't breathe a word to anyone, not even her family.
He begged his friend not to breathe a word about the mistake to the boss.

Synset Examples

Please don’t breathe a word about this to anyone.
He promised not to breathe a word of our secret.
I won’t breathe a word if you don’t want me to.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to spill the beans - to reveal a secret unintentionally or prematurely
  • Antonym: to keep quiet - to not say anything about something, especially a secret
