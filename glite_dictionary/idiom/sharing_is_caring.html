sharing is caring

1. X

/ ˈʃɛrɪŋ ɪz ˈkɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃeə.rɪŋ ɪz ˈkeə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
used to express the idea that it is good and kind to share things or help others, and that generosity is a virtue
it is good and kind to share

Examples

My mother always reminded us that sharing is caring when we argued over toys.
The company encourages teamwork with the motto, 'sharing is caring.'
At the party, the host said, 'sharing is caring,' as she offered everyone dessert.

Synset Examples

He always says, sharing is caring, so he lent me his book.
The teacher reminded us that sharing is caring during class.
In our family, we live by the motto: sharing is caring.
