dumb as a doornail

1. ADJ

/ dʌm əz ə ˈdɔrˌneɪl / (AmE), / dʌm əz ə ˈdɔːneɪl / (BrE)
Extremely foolish, senseless, or unresponsive.
very foolish or unresponsive
informal

Examples

Some people thought the plan was dumb as a doornail from the very beginning.
During the interview, he sat there dumb as a doornail and said nothing.
If you keep ignoring the instructions, you'll seem dumb as a doornail.

Synset Examples

That idea was dumb as a doornail from the start.
He was dumb as a doornail during the meeting.
Don’t be dumb as a doornail; pay attention!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dumb - lacking intelligence or good sense
  • Synonym: dead as a doornail - completely dead or devoid of life or activity (idiom)
  • Antonym: sharp as a tack - very intelligent or mentally quick (idiom)

Etymology

Patterned after the older idiom 'dead as a doornail,' where 'doornail' refers to the large nail on an old door, used in English since at least the 14th century.
