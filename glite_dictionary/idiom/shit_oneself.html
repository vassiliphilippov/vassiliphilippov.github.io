to shit oneself

1. VERB

/ ʃɪt wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / ʃɪt wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
(vulgar, informal) To lose control of one's bowels through extreme fear or shock, or to be extremely frightened, surprised, or excited.
lose control of bowels from fear; be extremely frightened
informal vulgar

Examples

When the fireworks exploded unexpectedly, I almost shit myself from the shock.
He told me the haunted house was so scary that he nearly shit himself.
She was so nervous before the big speech that she thought she might shit herself.
