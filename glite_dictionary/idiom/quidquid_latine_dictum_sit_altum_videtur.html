quidquid Latine dictum sit altum videtur

1. X

/ ˈkwɪd.kwɪd læˈtiː.ni ˈdɪk.tʊm sɪt ˈæl.tʊm vɪˈdeɪ.tur / (AmE), / ˈkwɪd.kwɪd læˈtiː.ni ˈdɪk.tʊm sɪt ˈæl.tʊm vɪˈdiː.tə / (BrE)
A humorous Latin expression meaning 'whatever is said in Latin sounds profound,' often used to poke fun at the use of Latin to give statements an air of importance.
Latin phrase meaning 'anything said in Latin seems profound'
