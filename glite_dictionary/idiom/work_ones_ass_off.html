to work one's ass off

1. VERB

/ wɜrk wʌnz æs ɔf / (AmE), / wɜːk wʌnz ɑːs ɒf / (BrE)
to work extremely hard; to put in a great deal of effort or exertion
work very hard (informal, vulgar)
informal vulgar

Examples

She worked her ass off to graduate at the top of her class.
I've been working my ass off to finish this assignment before the deadline.
They work their asses off every day just to keep the business running.
