don't sweat it

1. X

/ doʊnt swɛt ɪt / (AmE), / dəʊnt swet ɪt / (BrE)
used to tell someone not to worry or be anxious about something; it's not a problem
used to reassure someone not to worry
informal

Examples

You forgot your umbrella? Don't sweat it, I have a spare one you can use.
If you can't solve the problem now, don't sweat it; we can revisit it later.
I know you're running late, but don't sweat it, the movie hasn't started yet.

Synset Examples

You can always try again; don't sweat it.
If you make a mistake, don't sweat it; we all do!
If you miss the meeting, don't sweat it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: don't worry - used to reassure someone that a situation is not a problem
