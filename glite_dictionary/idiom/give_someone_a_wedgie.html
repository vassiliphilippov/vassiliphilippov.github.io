to give someone a wedgie

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ˈsʌmwʌn ə ˈwɛdʒi / (AmE), / ɡɪv ˈsʌmwən ə ˈwɛdʒi / (BrE)
to pull someone's underwear sharply upward as a prank or joke, causing discomfort and embarrassment
prank: yank someone's underwear up
informal

Examples

The older kids threatened to give him a wedgie if he didn’t hand over his lunch money.
During recess, someone tried to give Zack a wedgie, but he dodged at the last second.
She laughed so hard when her brother gave her a wedgie on her birthday.

Synset Examples

That prank gave him a wedgie right in front of everyone.
