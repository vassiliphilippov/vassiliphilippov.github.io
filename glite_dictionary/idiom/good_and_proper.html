good and proper

1. ADV

/ ɡʊd ənd ˈprɑpɚ / (AmE), / ɡʊd ənd ˈprɒpə / (BrE)
(informal) Thoroughly; in a complete and satisfactory way.
thoroughly; completely
informal

Examples

Let's clean the kitchen good and proper before our guests arrive.
They were caught good and proper by the surprise storm.
You need to apologize good and proper if you want her to forgive you.

Synset Examples

He gave her a good and proper explanation.
The event was planned good and proper to ensure success.
They celebrated their victory good and proper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoroughly - completely, in a thorough manner
  • Antonym: halfway - partially, not completely
