to dance like a demon

1. VERB

/ dæns laɪk ə ˈdimən / (AmE), / dɑːns laɪk ə ˈdiːmən / (BrE)
to dance with great energy and passion, often in a wild or uninhibited manner
dance energetically and passionately

Examples

When the music started, she began to dance like a demon, much to everyone's amazement.
He would dance like a demon every time his favorite song came on.
They encouraged the children to let loose and dance like a demon at the festival.

Synset Examples

She danced like a demon at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dance wildly - to dance in a wild or unrestrained way
  • Antonym: dance sedately - to dance in a calm, restrained manner
