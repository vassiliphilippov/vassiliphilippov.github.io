about time

1. X

/ əˈbaʊt taɪm / (AmE), / əˈbaʊt tʌɪm / (BrE)
used to express that something has happened later than expected and should have happened sooner
something happened later than expected

Examples

You got the promotion? Well, it's about time!
They finally fixed the heating—it's about time we weren't freezing in the office.
She apologized for her mistake, and I thought it was about time too.

Synset Examples

You started exercising regularly? It's about time.
They decided it was about time to renovate the house.
He finally apologized; it was about time.
It’s about time we addressed this issue.
We got our raise after months; it's about time.
You finally arrived! It's about time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at last - finally, after a long wait or delay
