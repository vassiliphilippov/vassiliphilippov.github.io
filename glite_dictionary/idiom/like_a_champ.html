like a champ

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə tʃæmp / (AmE), / laɪk ə tʃamp / (BrE)
in a way that shows great skill, confidence, or resilience in facing tasks or challenges
with great skill or confidence
informal

Examples

She answered all the tough questions like a champ during the interview.
Even after falling, he got back on his bike like a champ and finished the race.
Despite feeling nervous, she delivered her speech like a champ.

Synset Examples

He finished the marathon like a champ.
She handled the pressure of the presentation like a champ.
He tackled that difficult puzzle like a champ.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: like a pro - in a manner showing professional-level skill or expertise
  • Synonym: like an expert - in a way demonstrating expert knowledge or proficiency
