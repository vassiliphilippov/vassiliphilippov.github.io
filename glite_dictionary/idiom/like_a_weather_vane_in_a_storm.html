like a weather vane in a storm

1. X

/ laɪk ə ˈwɛðər veɪn ɪn ə stɔrm / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈwɛðə veɪn ɪn ə stɔːm / (BrE)
changing unpredictably or frequently, as a weather vane does in strong wind
changing unpredictably or often

Examples

When it comes to making decisions, he is like a weather vane in a storm—never sticking to one choice for long.
Her loyalty to different trends is like a weather vane in a storm, shifting every week.
Public opinion these days seems like a weather vane in a storm, swaying wildly with each new headline.

Synset Examples

His mood changed like a weather vane in a storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fickle - changing frequently, especially as regards one's loyalties or affections
  • Antonym: steadfast - firm and unwavering
