skunk at a garden party

1. NOUN

/ skʌŋk ət ə ˈɡɑrdn ˌpɑrti / (AmE), / skʌŋk ət ə ˈɡɑːdn ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
a person whose presence or behavior is unwelcome or disrupts the mood of a gathering
unwelcome or disruptive person

Examples

I felt like the skunk at a garden party when I disagreed with everyone during the celebration.
His constant criticism made him the skunk at a garden party at family gatherings.
No one wants to be the skunk at a garden party, but sometimes it's necessary to speak up.
