for all it was worth

1. ADV

/ fɚ ɔl ɪt wəz wɝθ / (AmE), / fə ɔːl ɪt wəz wɜːθ / (BrE)
to the greatest possible extent; as much as possible
to the fullest extent possible

Examples

She negotiated for all it was worth to get the best possible deal.
He ran the old car for all it was worth before finally trading it in.
During the sale, shoppers grabbed bargains for all it was worth.

Synset Examples

He invested in the project for all it was worth.
If you get the chance, play it for all it's worth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for all it's worth - to the greatest possible extent; as much as possible (variant with contraction and present tense)
