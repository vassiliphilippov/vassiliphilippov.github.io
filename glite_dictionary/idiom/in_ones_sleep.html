in one's sleep

1. ADV

/ ɪn wʌnz sliːp / (AmE), / ɪn wʌnz sliːp / (BrE)
(informal) Used to say that someone can do something very easily or effortlessly, as if without thinking.
very easily; effortlessly
informal

Examples

After years of practice, he could solve those equations in his sleep.
She was so familiar with the routine that she performed it in her sleep.
I have done this job so many times, I could do it in my sleep.

Synset Examples

She practices daily; she could do it in her sleep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: with ease - without difficulty or effort
  • Synonym: in no time - very quickly or easily
