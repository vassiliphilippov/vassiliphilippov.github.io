in other words

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈʌðɚ wɝdz / (AmE), / ɪn ˈʌðə wɜːdz / (BrE)
used to introduce a different way of expressing something, often to clarify or restate the previous statement
to express something differently or more clearly

Examples

The project is over budget; in other words, we spent more than we planned.
He was let go from his job—in other words, he was fired.
She found the task challenging; in other words, it was very difficult for her.

Synset Examples

in other words, we are broke

Related Terms

  • Synonym: that is to say - used to explain or clarify something just mentioned
  • Synonym: namely - used to introduce more exact information
