to that effect

1. ADV

/ tə ðæt ɪˈfɛkt / (AmE), / tə ðæt ɪˈfɛkt / (BrE)
having the general meaning or implication just mentioned; to mean something similar to what was stated
with that (similar) meaning or implication

Examples

She mentioned being late, or said something to that effect.
The manager nodded, and made a comment to that effect during the staff meeting.
He sent an email to that effect after their conversation.
