eat my shorts

1. X

/ it maɪ ʃɔrts / (AmE), / iːt maɪ ʃɔːts / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) Used to express defiance, strong refusal, or disbelief, often as a joking retort.
humorous retort expressing defiance or refusal
informal

Examples

When his brother told him to stop playing video games, he replied, 'eat my shorts!'
If you think I'm waking up before dawn on a Sunday, you can eat my shorts.
He knew the challenge was impossible, so he laughed and said, 'If you can do it, I'll eat my shorts.'

Synset Examples

He jokingly told his friend to eat my shorts if he disagreed.
I’d rather eat my shorts than watch that movie.
If I lose this bet, I’ll eat my shorts!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bite me - a similarly defiant or dismissive retort

Etymology

Popularized in the 1980s by the animated TV character Bart Simpson in The Simpsons.
