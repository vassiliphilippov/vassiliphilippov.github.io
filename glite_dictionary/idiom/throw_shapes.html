to throw shapes

1. VERB

/ θroʊ ʃeɪps / (AmE), / θrəʊ ʃeɪps / (BrE)
to dance energetically or in an expressive way, especially in a noticeable or flamboyant manner
dance energetically or expressively
informal british

Examples

As soon as the music started, Mark ran to the dance floor to throw shapes.
Everyone loves to throw shapes at the club on Saturday nights.
The enthusiastic teens threw shapes until the party ended.

Synset Examples
