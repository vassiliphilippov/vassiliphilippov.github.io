danger is one's middle name

1. X

/ ˈdeɪndʒɚ ɪz wʌnz ˈmɪdəl neɪm / (AmE), / ˈdeɪndʒə ɪz wʌnz ˈmɪdəl neɪm / (BrE)
used humorously to describe someone who habitually engages in risky or dangerous activities
takes risks; very daring (humorous)

Examples

For Alice, danger is one's middle name; she goes mountain climbing every weekend.
When it comes to investing in volatile markets, some say danger is one's middle name.
He leaped off the cliff without hesitation—clearly, danger is one's middle name.

Synset Examples

She joked that danger is one's middle name when skydiving.
With reckless stunts, it's clear danger is one's middle name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daredevil - a person who enjoys taking dangerous risks
  • Antonym: cautious - someone who avoids risks
