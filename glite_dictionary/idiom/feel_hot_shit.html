to feel hot shit

1. VERB

/ fil hɑt ʃɪt / (AmE), / fiːl hɒt ʃɪt / (BrE)
to feel extremely confident, important, or successful, often to the point of arrogance
to feel very confident or successful
informal vulgar

Examples

After his big promotion at work, he started to feel hot shit and bragged to everyone.
You may feel hot shit now, but remember to stay humble.
She was feeling hot shit after her painting sold for a record price.

Synset Examples

After winning the championship, he's feeling hot shit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be full of oneself - to be excessively self-confident or arrogant
