to get this circus on the road

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ðɪs ˈsɝːkəs ɑn ðə roʊd / (AmE), / ɡɛt ðɪs ˈsɜːkəs ɒn ðə rəʊd / (BrE)
to start or begin an activity or event, especially when it has taken effort or preparation to arrange
start an activity or event
informal

The equipment's set up, so it's time to get this circus on the road.
After weeks of planning, they could finally get this circus on the road.
