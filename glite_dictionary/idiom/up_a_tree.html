up a tree

1. ADV

/ ʌp ə tri / (AmE), / ʌp ə triː / (BrE)
in a difficult, helpless, or perplexed situation
in a difficult or helpless situation

Examples

When the final exam was rescheduled to tomorrow, she found herself up a tree.
He's really up a tree now that his car broke down and he has no way to get to work.
Without my phone or my wallet, I was completely up a tree.

Synset Examples

He was up a tree without a solution to his problem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in a bind - in a difficult situation
  • Synonym: in a tight spot - in a difficult or challenging situation
  • Synonym: at a loss - unable to know what to do, perplexed
