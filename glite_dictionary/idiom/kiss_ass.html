to kiss ass

1. VERB

/ kɪs æs / (AmE), / kɪs ɑːs / (BrE)
to flatter or ingratiate oneself with someone in power in order to gain an advantage
flatter for personal gain
informal slang

Examples

He's always trying to kiss ass when the manager is around.
People who kiss ass rarely earn genuine respect from their coworkers.
Instead of working hard, she preferred to kiss ass to get ahead.

Synset Examples

Don't kiss ass; just do your job well.
He always tries to kiss ass to get promotions.
She told her critics to kiss her ass.
She refuses to kiss anyone's ass for a promotion.
She’s always kissing ass to the boss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to brown-nose - to act overly eager to please, especially to someone in authority
  • Synonym: to suck up - to ingratiate oneself with someone to gain favor
  • Antonym: stand up to - to refuse to submit to or flatter someone in authority
