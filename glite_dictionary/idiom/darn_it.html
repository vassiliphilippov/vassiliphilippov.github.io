darn it

1. INTJ

/ ˈdɑrn ɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɑːn ɪt / (BrE)
used to express frustration, annoyance, or disappointment
expression of frustration or annoyance
informal

Examples

Darn it, I left my keys in the car again!
Darn it, this printer just won't work anymore.
Just when I thought I was done, I found another mistake—darn it!

Synset Examples

She exclaimed, 'Darn it!' after losing the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: damn it - expression of frustration or annoyance

Etymology

Euphemistic alteration of 'damn it', first recorded in 18th-century English.
