in a spot of bother

1. X

/ ɪn ə spɑt əv ˈbɑðər / (AmE), / ɪn ə spɒt əv ˈbɒðə / (BrE)
in a difficult or troublesome situation; experiencing some problems or difficulties
in a difficult situation

Examples

After the car broke down in the middle of nowhere, they found themselves in a spot of bother.
If you lose your passport while traveling, you could be in a spot of bother.
The company was in a spot of bother when their website crashed during a big online sale.

Synset Examples

She was in a spot of bother after missing the deadline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in trouble - experiencing problems or difficulties
  • Antonym: out of trouble - free from problems or difficulties
