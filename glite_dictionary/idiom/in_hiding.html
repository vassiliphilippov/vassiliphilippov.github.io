in hiding

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈhaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪn ˈhʌɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
in a concealed or secret place to avoid being found, especially by authorities or enemies
concealed to avoid detection

Examples

After the robbery, the suspect remained in hiding for several months.
The witness went in hiding under police protection.
Many artists lived in hiding during times of political unrest.

Synset Examples

he lay doggo

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on the run - avoiding capture by remaining undetected
  • Antonym: in the open - not concealed or hidden; visible
