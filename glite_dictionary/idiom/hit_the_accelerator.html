to hit the accelerator

1. VERB

/ hɪt ði ˌæk.səˈleɪ.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / hɪt ði ˌæk.səˈleɪ.tər / (BrE)
to increase speed or effort, especially in order to achieve a goal more quickly
increase speed or effort

Examples

When sales slowed down, the company decided to hit the accelerator with an aggressive marketing campaign.
