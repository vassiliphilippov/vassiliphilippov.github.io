to talk someone's leg off

1. VERB

/ tɔk ˈsʌmwʌnz lɛg ɔf / (AmE), / tɔːk ˈsʌmwʌnz lɛg ɒf / (BrE)
to talk to someone for a very long time, often excessively or to the point of tiring them
talk to someone at great length

Examples

If you get him started on sports, he'll talk your leg off for hours.
She managed to talk my leg off during the entire train ride.
Be careful mentioning movies around Tim—he'll talk your leg off about his favorites.

Synset Examples

I barely managed to leave; he was talking my leg off.
He will talk your leg off if you mention fishing.
If you ask him about his hobbies, he’ll talk your leg off.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talk the hind leg off a donkey - to talk endlessly or excessively
