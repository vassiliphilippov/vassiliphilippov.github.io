piss on my leg and tell me it's raining

1. X

/ pɪs ɑn maɪ lɛɡ ənd tɛl mi ɪts ˈreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / pɪs ɒn maɪ lɛɡ ənd tɛl mi ɪts ˈreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) To blatantly lie to someone or deceive them, especially while expecting them to believe the lie.
to blatantly lie or deceive
informal vulgar

Examples

Don't piss on my leg and tell me it's raining; I know exactly what's going on.
He tried to piss on my leg and tell me it's raining, but I wasn't fooled for a second.
Whenever politicians make false promises, it feels like they piss on my leg and tell me it's raining.

Synset Examples

If you're lying, don't piss on my leg and tell me it's raining.
I won't believe your excuses; don't piss on my leg and tell me it's raining.
When he tried to cover up the mistake, I told him, 'Don't piss on my leg and tell me it's raining.'

Related Terms
