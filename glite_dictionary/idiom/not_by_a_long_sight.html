not by a long sight

1. ADV

/ nɑt baɪ ə lɔŋ saɪt / (AmE), / nɒt baɪ ə lɒŋ saɪt / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is definitely not the case or far from being true
definitely not; far from the case

Examples

This isn't finished not by a long sight, so we still have a lot of work to do.
He thinks he's the best, but he's not by a long sight the smartest in the class.
The problem isn't solved not by a long sight; there's still much to be done.

Synset Examples

the prize is by no means certain
and that isn't all, not by a long sight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: by no means - not at all; in no way
  • Synonym: not at all - in no way; not in the slightest
