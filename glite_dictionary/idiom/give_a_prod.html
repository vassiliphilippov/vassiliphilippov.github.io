to give a prod

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ə prɑd / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə prɒd / (BrE)
to offer a gentle push or encouragement to prompt someone to take action
gently encourage someone to act

Examples

She decided to give a prod to her colleague to finish the report.
Sometimes you just need to give a prod to get things moving.
He appreciated it when his friend would give a prod before an important deadline.

Synset Examples

She gave him a prod to motivate him to start.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to encourage - give support, confidence, or hope to (someone)
  • Synonym: nudge - a gentle push or prompt
