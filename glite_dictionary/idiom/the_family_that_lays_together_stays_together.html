the family that lays together stays together

1. X

/ ðə ˈfæmli ðət leɪz təˈgɛðər steɪz təˈgɛðər / (AmE), / ðə ˈfæmli ðət leɪz təˈɡeðə steɪz təˈɡeðə / (BrE)
An expression meaning families are strengthened and remain united by spending time and sharing experiences together.
families stay united through togetherness
informal

Examples

Every Friday night, they have a movie marathon, believing the family that lays together stays together.
Sunday mornings in bed prove to them that the family that lays together stays together.
Photos of vacations fill their walls, reminding them that the family that lays together stays together.

Synset Examples

They believe the family that lays together stays together during tough times.
Every Sunday, they have brunch, proving the family that lays together stays together.
Game nights show that the family that lays together stays together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the family that prays together stays together - original idiom about family unity through shared religious practice

Etymology

A play on the earlier phrase 'the family that prays together stays together' (20th c.), substituting 'lays' for 'prays.'
