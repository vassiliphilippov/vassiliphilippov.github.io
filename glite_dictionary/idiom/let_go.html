to let go

1. VERB

/ lɛt ɡoʊ / (AmE), / lɛt ɡəʊ / (BrE)
to dismiss someone from a job or position; to fire someone
dismiss someone from a job

Examples

After months of budget cuts, the company had to let go several employees.
Unfortunately, they decided to let him go due to downsizing.
She was devastated when her manager chose to let her go last week.

Synset Examples

They decided to let him go after the merger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fire - to dismiss someone from a job
  • Synonym: to dismiss - to remove someone from their job
