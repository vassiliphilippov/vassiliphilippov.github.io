to cover one's ass

1. VERB

/ ˈkʌvɚ wʌnz æs / (AmE), / ˈkʌvə wʌnz ɑːs / (BrE)
to take action to protect oneself or someone else from blame, criticism, or negative consequences, often by creating evidence of compliance or shifting responsibility
protect oneself from blame or criticism
informal vulgar

Examples

He kept copies of all his emails to cover his ass in case of a dispute.
The staff were told to document every step to cover their ass if problems arose.
She added extra details in the report to cover her ass after the mistake was discovered.
