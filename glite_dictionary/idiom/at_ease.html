at ease

1. ADJ

/ æt iːz / (AmE), / æt iːz / (BrE)
feeling comfortable and relaxed, not nervous or worried
comfortable and relaxed

Examples

She always feels at ease when she's among close friends.
Public speaking made him nervous at first, but now he's completely at ease on stage.
The warm atmosphere of the cafe put everyone at ease.

Synset Examples

His gentle sex attitude made her feel at ease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comfortable - feeling physically relaxed or free from pain; at ease
  • Synonym: relaxed - free from tension and anxiety; at ease
  • Antonym: uneasy - feeling anxious or uncomfortable; not at ease
