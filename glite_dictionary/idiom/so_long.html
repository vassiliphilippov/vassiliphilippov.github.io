so long

1. INTJ

/ soʊ ˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / səʊ ˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
used as an informal way to say goodbye when parting from someone
informal farewell expression
informal

Examples

She smiled and said, 'so long,' before leaving the café.
Well, it's been fun—so long until we meet again!
He waved at the crowd and shouted, 'so long!' as he boarded the train.

Synset Examples

He waved goodbye, saying, 'So long, and thanks for all the fish!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - used to express good wishes when parting
  • Synonym: farewell - an act of saying goodbye
