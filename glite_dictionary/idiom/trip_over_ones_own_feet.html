to trip over one's own feet

1. VERB

/ trɪp ˈoʊvɚ wʌnz oʊn fit / (AmE), / trɪp ˈəʊvə wʌnz əʊn fiːt / (BrE)
to stumble or fall awkwardly due to one's own clumsiness
to stumble clumsily

Examples

I was so nervous during my speech that I nearly tripped over my own feet walking to the stage.
She always seems to trip over her own feet when there's something important happening.
He tripped over his own feet and spilled coffee everywhere in front of his colleagues.

Synset Examples

She tripped over her own feet, goldurn!
That dorkazoid just tripped over his own feet.
That galoot tripped over his own feet at the party.
He tripped over his own feet like a witless wonder.
When he tripped over his own feet, it is to laugh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stumble - to trip or momentarily lose balance while walking
