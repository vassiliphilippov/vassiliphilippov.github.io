small voice

1. NOUN

/ smɔl vɔɪs / (AmE), / smɔːl vɔɪs / (BrE)
an inner feeling or sense that quietly guides or judges your actions, often in regard to conscience or morality
quiet inner sense of conscience

Examples

She tried to ignore the small voice telling her to do the right thing.
Whenever he considered cheating, a small voice inside warned him against it.
The small voice of conscience can be easy to dismiss, but it's always there.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conscience - internal moral sense of right and wrong
