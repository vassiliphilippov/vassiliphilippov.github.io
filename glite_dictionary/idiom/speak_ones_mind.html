to speak one's mind

1. VERB

/ spiːk wʌnz maɪnd / (AmE), / spiːk wʌnz maɪnd / (BrE)
to express one's opinions or thoughts frankly and openly, without hesitation or concealment
express one's honest opinion openly

Examples

Even if her opinion is unpopular, she always speak's her mind in meetings.
He decided to speak his mind about the new policy.
You should feel free to speak your mind during the discussion.
