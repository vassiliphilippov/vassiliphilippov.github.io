to catch the match

1. VERB

/ kætʃ ðə mætʃ / (AmE), / kætʃ ðə mætʃ / (BrE)
to see or watch a sports event, especially one involving a particular match
watch a sports event
informal

Examples

Did you catch the match last night on TV?
I'm hoping to catch the match this weekend with some friends.
If we leave now, we can still catch the match before it ends.

Synset Examples

Wazzer, did you catch the match last night?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watch the match - to observe a sports event
