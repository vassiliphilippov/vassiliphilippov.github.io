son of a motherless goat

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌn əv ə ˈmʌðərləs ɡoʊt / (AmE), / ˈsʌn əv ə ˈmʌðələs ɡəʊt / (BrE)
A humorous or mocking insult for an unpleasant, contemptible, or foolish person.
insult for a contemptible or foolish person
informal

Examples

He called his rival a son of a motherless goat after the heated debate.
When the deal fell through, she muttered, "That son of a motherless goat tricked me!"
During the game, one fan shouted, "You son of a motherless goat!" at the referee.

Synset Examples

Don’t be a son of a motherless goat in this meeting.
He’s a real son of a motherless goat when angry.
What a son of a motherless goat he turned out to be!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bastard - an unpleasant or despicable person (insult)
  • Synonym: jerk - informal term for an annoying or contemptible person
