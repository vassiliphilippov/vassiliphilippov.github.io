to feel like a million dollars

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə ˈmɪljən ˈdɑlərz / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈmɪljən ˈdɒləz / (BrE)
to feel extremely healthy, happy, or good about oneself
feel exceptionally good or healthy

Examples

After a week of rest and relaxation, I feel like a million dollars.
She put on her new dress and felt like a million dollars at the party.
Since recovering from his illness, he says he feels like a million dollars every morning.

Synset Examples

he feels like a million after he got the promotion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feel on top of the world - to feel very happy or elated
  • Antonym: feel under the weather - to feel unwell or sick
