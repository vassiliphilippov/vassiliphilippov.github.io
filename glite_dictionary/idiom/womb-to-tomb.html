womb-to-tomb

1. ADJ

/ wum tə tum / (AmE), / wuːm tə tuːm / (BrE)
lasting for the whole of a person's life, from birth to death
lasting throughout one's entire life

Examples

He remained a womb-to-tomb friend, always by my side through every stage of life.
Their commitment to education is a womb-to-tomb philosophy, encouraging learning at every age.
The documentary charts the womb-to-tomb journey of a famous artist.
