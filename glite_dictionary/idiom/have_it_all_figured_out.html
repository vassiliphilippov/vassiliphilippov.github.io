to have it all figured out

1. VERB

/ hæv ɪt ɔl ˈfɪɡjɚd aʊt / (AmE), / hæv ɪt ɔːl ˈfɪɡəd aʊt / (BrE)
to understand or solve something completely, often implying confidence in one's own understanding or plans
understand or solve everything completely

Examples

She acts like she has it all figured out, but she's actually quite uncertain.
Just when I thought I had it all figured out, something unexpected happened.
He believes he'll have it all figured out by the end of the week.

Synset Examples

They seem to have it all figured out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to figure out - to understand or solve something
