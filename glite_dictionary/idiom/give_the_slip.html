to give the slip

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ðə slɪp / (AmE), / ɡɪv ðə slɪp / (BrE)
to escape or evade someone who is pursuing or watching you
escape or evade someone

Examples

She tried to follow him through the crowd, but he gave the slip and disappeared.
The suspect gave the slip to the police by ducking into an alley.
After a long chase, the thief finally gave the slip to his pursuers.

Synset Examples

He managed to give the security guard the slip.
They almost caught him, but he gave them the slip.
She tried to catch him but he gave her the slip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to evade - to escape from or avoid someone or something
  • Synonym: escape - to get away from a place or person
