under one's own steam

1. ADV

/ ˌʌndɚ wʌnz oʊn stiːm / (AmE), / ˌʌndə wʌnz əʊn stiːm / (BrE)
without help from others; using one's own effort or resources
independently; without assistance

Examples

After losing his job, he started his own company under his own steam.
The team finished the project under their own steam despite limited resources.
She traveled across the country under her own steam without any financial help.

Synset Examples

He moved out and established his own life under his own steam.
After years of training, she finally completed the marathon under her own steam.
They traveled to Europe under their own steam, without any help.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: independently - without assistance or outside control

Etymology

Originally from the literal use of steam-powered engines operating independently.
