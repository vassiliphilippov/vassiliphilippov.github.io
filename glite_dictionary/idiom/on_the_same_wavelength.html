on the same wavelength

1. X

/ ɑn ðə seɪm ˈweɪvlɛŋθ / (AmE), / ɒn ðə seɪm ˈweɪvlɛŋθ / (BrE)
having a similar way of thinking or understanding as someone else; in agreement with another person, especially in terms of ideas or communication.
sharing similar thoughts or understanding

Examples

After just a few minutes of conversation, I realized that Sarah and I were on the same wavelength about the project's goals.
It's much easier to collaborate when team members are on the same wavelength.
Despite their different upbringings, they are clearly on the same wavelength when it comes to values.

Synset Examples

We always seem to be on the same wavelength during brainstorming sessions.
It's great to work with someone who's on the same wavelength.
They were not on the same wavelength about the project.
It's great when you and your partner are on the same wavelength.
Despite our different backgrounds, we are on the same wavelength.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to see eye to eye - to agree or think in the same way
  • Antonym: be at odds - to disagree or be in conflict

Etymology

Based on the metaphor of radio waves, where being 'on the same wavelength' allows clear communication.
