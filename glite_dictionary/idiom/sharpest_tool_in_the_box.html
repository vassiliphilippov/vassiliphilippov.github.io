sharpest tool in the box

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑrpɪst tuːl ɪn ðə bɑks / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːpɪst tuːl ɪn ðə bɒks / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) Used to describe someone who is not very intelligent or quick-witted.
not very intelligent (humorous)
informal

Examples

He tries hard at work, but he's not exactly the sharpest tool in the box.
She forgot her own birthday, proving she's not the sharpest tool in the box.
No one said you had to be the sharpest tool in the box to join the quiz team.

Synset Examples

She’s sweet, just not the sharpest tool in the box.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bright spark - an intelligent or clever person (often used sarcastically)
  • Synonym: sharpest tool in the shed - idiom meaning very intelligent or quick-witted
