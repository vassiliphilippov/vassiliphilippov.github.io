in everyone's thoughts

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈɛvrɪwʌnz θɔts / (AmE), / ɪn ˈɛvrɪwʌnz θɔːts / (BrE)
in the minds or awareness of all people at a particular time; commonly talked or worried about by many
widely on people's minds

Examples

The missing hiker was in everyone's thoughts throughout the long night.
During the crisis, the safety of the community was in everyone's thoughts.
With the big exam approaching, passing the test was in everyone's thoughts.

Synset Examples

Christmas was in the air

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on everyone's mind - being thought about by everyone at once
  • Antonym: forgotten - not remembered or considered by people anymore
