in bloom

1. ADJ

/ ɪn bluːm / (AmE), / ɪn bluːm / (BrE)
(of a plant or flower) in the state of flowering or blossoming.
flowering; blossoming

Examples

The cherry trees are in bloom every spring, attracting many tourists to the park.
The countryside is especially beautiful when the wildflowers are in bloom.
She loves to paint gardens while the roses are in bloom.

Synset Examples

The garden was filled with lovely English roses in bloom.
The garden was filled with violet flowers in bloom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in flower - in the state of flowering
