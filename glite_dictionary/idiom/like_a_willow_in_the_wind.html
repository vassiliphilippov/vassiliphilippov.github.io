like a willow in the wind

1. X

/ laɪk ə ˈwɪloʊ ɪn ðə wɪnd / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈwɪləʊ ɪn ðə wɪnd / (BrE)
adaptable and resilient; able to bend or adjust to changing circumstances while maintaining inner strength.
adaptable and resilient (simile)

Examples

Throughout her career, she remained like a willow in the wind, adapting to every challenge that came her way.
Leaders must be like a willow in the wind to thrive in a rapidly changing world.
He faced criticism and adversity like a willow in the wind, never breaking under pressure.

Synset Examples

His resolve was like a willow in the wind, flexible yet strong.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resilient - able to quickly recover from difficulties; flexible
