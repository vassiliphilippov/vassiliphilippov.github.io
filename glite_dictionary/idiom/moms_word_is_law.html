mom's word is law

1. X

/ mɑmz wɝd ɪz lɑ / (AmE), / mɒmz wɜːd ɪz lɔː / (BrE)
used to say that whatever the mother decides or commands must be obeyed without question
mother's authority is absolute

Examples

In our house, mom's word is law, so we always do our chores on time.
Whenever there’s a disagreement, remember that mom's word is law.
It's clear to everyone here that mom's word is law when it comes to family rules.

Synset Examples

In this family, mom's word is law.
