to beat my brain

1. VERB

/ biːt maɪ breɪn / (AmE), / biːt maɪ breɪn / (BrE)
to think intensely or struggle to remember something; make a great mental effort
think hard or struggle to remember

Examples

She beat my brain trying to solve the difficult puzzle.
Sometimes I have to beat my brain to recall old memories.
He beat my brain for hours but still couldn't remember the code.

Synset Examples

I had to beat my brain for the right answer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rack my brain - to think very hard or try hard to remember something
