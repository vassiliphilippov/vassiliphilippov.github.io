days are numbered

1. X

/ ˈdeɪz ɑr ˈnʌmbɚd / (AmE), / ˈdeɪz ɑː ˈnʌmbəd / (BrE)
said to mean that someone or something does not have much time left before an end is expected, usually due to imminent danger or failure
not much time left; near the end

Examples

With the company's financial troubles, it's clear that its days are numbered.
After the accident, he realized his reckless behavior meant his days are numbered.
Many believe that print newspapers' days are numbered in the digital age.

Synset Examples

With the company struggling, her days are numbered.
The doctor warned him that his days are numbered if he doesn't change.
If he keeps driving like that, his days are numbered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on borrowed time - living or existing beyond the time normally expected to end
