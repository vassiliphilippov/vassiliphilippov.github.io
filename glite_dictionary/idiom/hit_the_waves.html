to hit the waves

1. VERB

/ hɪt ðə weɪvz / (AmE), / hɪt ðə weɪvz / (BrE)
to go surfing, especially as an enjoyable or energetic activity
go surfing
informal

Examples

Every summer morning, dozens of locals wake up early to hit the waves before work.
She can't wait to hit the waves on her new surfboard this weekend.
After breakfast, we gathered our gear and headed to the beach to hit the waves.

Synset Examples

Don't forget to wax the surfboard before hitting the waves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surf - to ride on the crest of a wave, typically with a surfboard
