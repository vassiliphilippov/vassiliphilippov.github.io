in the heat of the moment

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðə hit əv ðə ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ɪn ðə hiːt əv ðə ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
while experiencing strong emotions or excitement, often causing one to act impulsively without thinking
while experiencing strong emotions, impulsively

Examples

He apologized later, saying he said it in the heat of the moment.
She quit her job in the heat of the moment, only to regret it later.
Regrettably, he made that promise in the heat of the moment and couldn't keep it.

Synset Examples

He shouted 'Avada Kedavra' in the heat of the moment.
