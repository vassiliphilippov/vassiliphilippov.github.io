chocolate teapot

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑːklət ˈtiːpɑːt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət ˈtiːpɒt / (BrE)
Something utterly useless or impractical, especially in the context for which it is intended.
something completely useless or impractical
british

Examples

Many people saw the policy as a chocolate teapot—utterly useless in solving the issue.
His instructions were about as much help as a chocolate teapot during the crisis.
We joked that the new software was a chocolate teapot for our department's problems.

Synset Examples

He realized his idea was a chocolate teapot from the start.
Trying to explain that was like using a chocolate teapot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white elephant - something that is more troublesome than it is worth
  • Antonym: useful - able to be used for a practical purpose or benefit
