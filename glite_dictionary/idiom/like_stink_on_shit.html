like stink on shit

1. X

/ laɪk stɪŋk ɑn ʃɪt / (AmE), / laɪk stɪŋk ɒn ʃɪt / (BrE)
(Very informal and vulgar) Used to describe something that clings or adheres extremely closely or persistently.
clinging or adhering very closely (vulgar, idiom)
informal vulgar

Examples

He followed her like stink on shit, never letting her out of his sight.
The rumor stuck to the politician like stink on shit, no matter what he did.
Those stickers adhere to the wall like stink on shit—they're nearly impossible to remove.

Synset Examples

The smell clung to him like stink on shit.
The paint fumes lingered like stink on shit.
