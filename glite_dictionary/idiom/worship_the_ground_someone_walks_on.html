to worship the ground someone walks on

1. VERB

/ ˈwɝːʃɪp ðə ɡraʊnd ˈsʌmwʌn wɔks ɑn / (AmE), / ˈwɜːʃɪp ðə ɡraʊnd ˈsʌmwɒn wɔːks ɒn / (BrE)
to admire or adore someone to an excessive or uncritical degree
admire or adore someone excessively

Examples

Ever since they started dating, she clearly worships the ground he walks on.
People say he worships the ground she walks on, but she hardly notices his devotion.
It's not healthy to worship the ground someone walks on and ignore their faults.

Synset Examples

He acts like he worships the ground she walks on.
They were so in love, they worshiped the ground each other walked on.
Despite his flaws, she worshipped the ground he walked on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to idolize - to admire or revere someone greatly
  • Synonym: to adore - to love and respect someone deeply
  • Antonym: to despise - to feel contempt or intense dislike for someone
