oh brother

1. INTJ

/ oʊ ˈbrʌðər / (AmE), / əʊ ˈbrʌðə / (BrE)
used to express frustration, annoyance, or exasperation.
expression of frustration or exasperation
informal

Examples

Oh brother, here we go again with the same old argument.
When the computer crashed, he just sighed and said, "oh brother."
Oh brother, can't you be serious for one minute?

Synset Examples

Oh, brother, not this again!
Oh, brother, I thought we were past that.
Oh, brother, I can’t believe they did that.
