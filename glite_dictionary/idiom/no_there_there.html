no there there

1. X

/ noʊ ðɛr ðɛr / (AmE), / nəʊ ðeə ðeə / (BrE)
used to indicate that something lacks substance, significance, or validity
lacking substance or significance

Examples

Many critics argued that the movie was overrated because there was no there there.
After reading the lengthy report, she concluded there was no there there behind the statistics.
People were excited about the startup, but once investigated, it turned out there was no there there.

Synset Examples

The project was all talk; there is no there there.
His claims were empty; clearly, there is no there there.
After visiting, I realized there is no there there.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: empty - lacking meaning or substance
  • Synonym: insubstantial - not solid or real; lacking substance

Etymology

Coined by Gertrude Stein in reference to her childhood home in Oakland, California, meaning 'nothing substantial here.'
