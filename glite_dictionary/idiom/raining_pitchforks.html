to raining pitchforks

1. VERB

/ ˈreɪnɪŋ ˈpɪtʃˌfɔrks / (AmE), / ˈreɪnɪŋ ˈpɪtʃˌfɔːks / (BrE)
Used to describe rain falling very heavily or violently; pouring with rain.
raining very heavily

Examples

We had to cancel the match because it was raining pitchforks all afternoon.
She dashed to her car as it started raining pitchforks.
It's best not to travel when it's raining pitchforks like this.

Synset Examples

The storm was so fierce, it was raining pitchforks outside.
I stayed indoors because it was raining pitchforks.
It was raining pitchforks during our picnic yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raining cats and dogs - used to say it is raining very heavily
