busy little beaver

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪzi ˈlɪtəl ˈbiːvər / (AmE), / ˈbɪzi ˈlɪtl ˈbiːvə / (BrE)
a person who is very active, hardworking, and industrious
very hardworking or industrious person (idiom)

Examples

Ever since he started his new job, Mark has been a busy little beaver around the office.
She’s a busy little beaver when it comes to organizing events for the community.
Whenever there's a group project, you can count on Jake to be the busy little beaver who gets things done.

Synset Examples

He’s a busy little beaver, always working on new projects.
Look at her, that busy little beaver, cleaning the entire house.
That busy little beaver finished the report ahead of schedule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workaholic - a person who compulsively works hard and long hours
  • Antonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort

Etymology

Refers to the diligent behavior of beavers building dams; originated in early 20th-century North American English.
