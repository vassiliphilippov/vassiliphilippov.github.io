as stubborn as an anvil

1. ADJ

/ əz ˈstʌbɚn əz ən ˈæn.vəl / (AmE), / əz ˈstʌb.ən əz ən ˈæn.vɪl / (BrE)
extremely resistant to change or persuasion; completely unyielding in attitude or opinion
extremely unyielding or resistant

Examples

Despite everyone's advice, he remained as stubborn as an anvil throughout the negotiations.
No amount of reasoning could change her mind; she was as stubborn as an anvil.
My brother is as stubborn as an anvil when it comes to admitting he's wrong.

Synset Examples

She was as stubborn as an anvil when it came to her opinions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as stubborn as a mule - very stubborn and unwilling to change one's mind
