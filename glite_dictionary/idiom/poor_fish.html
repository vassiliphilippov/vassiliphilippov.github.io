poor fish

1. NOUN

/ pʊr fɪʃ / (AmE), / pɔː fɪʃ / (BrE)
a person who lacks intelligence or is easily duped; a simple or naïve person
naïve or unintelligent person

Examples

He always tries to help, but sometimes he acts like a poor fish.
Don't be such a poor fish; think before you speak!
The con artist looked for a poor fish to trick in the crowd.

Synset Examples

The economy, stupid!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simpleton - a foolish or gullible person
  • Synonym: dupe - a person who is easily deceived or tricked
