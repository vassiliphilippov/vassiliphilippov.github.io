as pellucid as crystal

1. ADJ

/ əz pəˈluːsɪd əz ˈkrɪstl̩ / (AmE), / əz pɛˈluːsɪd əz ˈkrɪstl / (BrE)
extremely clear, transparent, or easy to understand; perfectly lucid.
extremely clear or easy to understand

Examples

Her explanation was as pellucid as crystal, leaving no room for confusion.
The report is written in language as pellucid as crystal—even a layperson can grasp it easily.
He praised the novelist's style for being as pellucid as crystal.

Synset Examples

Her arguments were as pellucid as crystal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crystal clear - perfectly clear or easy to understand

Etymology

pellucid from Latin pellucidus (transparent). Crystal is used figuratively for clarity.
