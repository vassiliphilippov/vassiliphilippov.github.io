to jump for joy

1. VERB

/ dʒʌmp fər dʒɔɪ / (AmE), / dʒʌmp fə dʒɔɪ / (BrE)
to feel or express extreme happiness or elation, often by leaping into the air
express great happiness

Examples

She couldn't help but jump for joy when she heard she got the job.
The children jumped for joy when school was cancelled because of snow.
After winning the match, he was jumping for joy with his teammates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to rejoice - feel or show great joy or delight

2. VERB

/ dʒʌmp fər dʒɔɪ / (AmE), / dʒʌmp fə dʒɔɪ / (BrE)
to show great happiness or excitement, often by jumping or moving energetically
to express great happiness or excitement

Examples

She couldn't help but jump for joy when she got her dream job.
The children jumped for joy when the bell rang for summer vacation.
He was so thrilled at the news that he started jumping for joy in the hallway.
