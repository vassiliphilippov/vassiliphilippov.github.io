your father's vehicle

1. X

/ jʊr ˈfɑðɚz ˈviɪkəl / (AmE), / jɔː ˈfɑːðəz ˈviːɪkl / (BrE)
Used to describe something as modern or different from older, traditional versions, especially in advertising to emphasize innovation.
modern, not traditional; updated version

Examples

The new electric model is your father's vehicle in name only; it's packed with advanced features.
This isn't your father's vehicle—it's sleek, efficient, and eco-friendly.
Forget what you know: today's SUV is not your father's vehicle.

Synset Examples

The new car design is definitely not your father's vehicle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: not your father's - phrase used to indicate something is not traditional or is newer
