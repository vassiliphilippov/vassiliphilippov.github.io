to bite one's ass

1. VERB

/ baɪt wʌnz æs / (AmE), / baɪt wʌnz ɑːs / (BrE)
(vulgar, informal) To respond to someone with defiance, irritation, or rudeness, often as a retort to criticism or authority.
respond rudely or defiantly (vulgar)
vulgar informal

Examples

After the constant nitpicking from management, Sam finally told his supervisor to bite his ass.
She looked her accuser in the eye and said, 'You can bite my ass if you don't like it.'
When the company ignored his ideas, he quit and told them all to bite his ass.

Synset Examples

He told his boss to bite his ass after the unfair critique.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: kiss one's ass - to flatter excessively or behave obsequiously toward someone
