life is too short for unhappiness

1. X

/ laɪf ɪz tu ʃɔrt fər ʌnˈhæpinəs / (AmE), / laɪf ɪz tuː ʃɔːt fə ʌnˈhapinəs / (BrE)
used to express the belief that one should not waste time being unhappy and should instead seek enjoyment and fulfillment in life
enjoy life; don't dwell on unhappiness

Examples

After the breakup, she reminded herself that life is too short for unhappiness and took a trip to Europe.
He realized life is too short for unhappiness, so he chose to follow his passion for painting.
Whenever work feels overwhelming, just remember that life is too short for unhappiness.

Synset Examples

He quit his job because life is too short for unhappiness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: you only live once - expression urging people to make the most of the present
