to sing the praises

1. VERB

/ sɪŋ ðə ˈpreɪzɪz / (AmE), / sɪŋ ðə ˈpreɪzɪz / (BrE)
to praise someone or something enthusiastically and publicly
praise enthusiastically

Examples

The coach continued to sing the praises of his team's dedication and skill.
Many fans have sung the praises of the new smartphone's impressive features.
She never misses a chance to sing the praises of her favorite author.

Synset Examples

He loves to sing the praises of his hometown.
She often sings the praises of organic food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: praise - to express approval or admiration
  • Antonym: to criticize - to express disapproval or find fault
