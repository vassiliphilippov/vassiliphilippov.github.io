for the hell of it

1. ADV

/ fɔɹ ðə hɛl əv ɪt / (AmE), / fə ðə hɛl əv ɪt / (BrE)
for no particular reason or simply for fun; without a serious purpose
for no particular reason; just for fun
informal

Examples

Sometimes I take a different route home for the hell of it, just to see something new.
He bought a bright yellow suit for the hell of it, even though he'll probably never wear it.
They decided to bake an enormous cake for the hell of it on a quiet Sunday afternoon.

Synset Examples

He ran the marathon for the hell of it.
We decided to go skydiving for the hell of it.
She painted her room bright pink for the hell of it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for fun - for amusement or enjoyment, not for a specific purpose
  • Synonym: for the sake of it - without a real reason; just because
