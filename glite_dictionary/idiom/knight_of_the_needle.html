knight of the needle

1. NOUN

/ naɪt əv ðə ˈniːdəl / (AmE), / naɪt əv ðə ˈniːdl̩ / (BrE)
a highly skilled tailor or seamstress, especially one proficient in creating garments or quilts
expert tailor or seamstress

Examples

Regarded as the knight of the needle, she created bespoke gowns for wealthy clients.
His reputation as a knight of the needle was known throughout the city.
The community honored their local knight of the needle at the festival for his beautiful quilting work.

Synset Examples

As the knight of the needle, he made stunning quilts.
She’s a knight of the needle, crafting beautiful garments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tailor - a person who makes, repairs, or alters clothing professionally

Etymology

Alludes to the honor of medieval knights, metaphorically applied to mastery in sewing.
