on someone's ass

1. ADP

/ ɑn ˈsʌmwʌnz æs / (AmE), / ɒn ˈsʌmwʌnz æs / (BrE)
to put pressure on someone or constantly demand attention or action from them, often in a persistent or critical manner
apply pressure or demand attention (informal, vulgar)
informal vulgar

Examples

My boss has been on my ass about meeting the sales targets this month.
If you don't study, your parents will be on your ass all semester long.
The editor was on the reporter's ass to finish the article before the deadline.

Synset Examples

The coach was on the players' asses to practice harder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on someone's back - to constantly nag or pressure someone
