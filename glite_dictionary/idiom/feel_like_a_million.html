to feel like a million

1. VERB

/ fiːl laɪk ə ˈmɪljən / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈmɪljən / (BrE)
To feel extremely well or be in very high spirits.
feel extremely well

Examples

After her vacation, she felt like a million and ready to tackle new challenges.
With a good night's sleep, I feel like a million this morning.
He started feeling like a million once his flu finally went away.

Synset Examples

he feels like a million after he got the promotion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feel great - to feel very good or healthy
  • Antonym: feel terrible - to feel very unwell or in low spirits

Etymology

Refers to the idea of having a million dollars, symbolizing great fortune or well-being.
