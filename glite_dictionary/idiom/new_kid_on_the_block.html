new kid on the block

1. NOUN

/ nuː kɪd ɑn ðə blɑk / (AmE), / njuː kɪd ɒn ðə blɒk / (BrE)
a person or thing that has recently arrived in a particular place, group, or field and is unfamiliar to the others there
recent newcomer to a group or area

Examples

As the new kid on the block, she was eager to make friends at her new job.
That software is the new kid on the block in the market, offering unique features.
Being the new kid on the block can be challenging, but it's also an opportunity to learn.

Synset Examples

The gadget is the new kid on the block for tech enthusiasts.
She’s the new kid on the block, shaking things up.
As the new kid on the block, he had to prove himself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newcomer - a person who has recently arrived
