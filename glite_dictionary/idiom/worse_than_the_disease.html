worse than the disease

1. X

/ wɝs ðæn ðə dɪˈziz / (AmE), / wɜːs ðən ðə dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
(of a solution or remedy) causing more harm than the problem it is meant to solve.
solution that causes more harm than the problem

Examples

Some argue that censorship is worse than the disease when it limits free speech.
The side effects of the treatment made it worse than the disease itself.
He believes that rushing into drastic reforms could be worse than the disease.

Synset Examples

Sometimes, the cure is worse than the disease when side effects kick in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cure worse than the disease - an equivalent idiom expressing the same idea
