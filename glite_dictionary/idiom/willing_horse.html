willing horse

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪlɪŋ hɔrs / (AmE), / ˈwɪlɪŋ hɔːs / (BrE)
a person who is always eager to help or volunteer, often taking on extra work
person always eager to help

Examples

Jessica is always a willing horse when extra help is needed around the office.
He became known as the willing horse of the team, never refusing a new task.
Don't overload the willing horse, or you risk burning them out.

Synset Examples

She’s a willing horse when it comes to volunteering.
Being a willing horse made him popular among peers.
He was a willing horse, ready to help with anything.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eager beaver - someone who is very enthusiastic and eager to work
