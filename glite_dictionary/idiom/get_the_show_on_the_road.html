to get the show on the road

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ðə ʃoʊ ɑn ðə roʊd / (AmE), / ɡɛt ðə ʃəʊ ɒn ðə rəʊd / (BrE)
to begin or proceed with an activity, event, or project; to start something after preparations are complete
begin or proceed with an activity

Examples

After hours of waiting, it's time to get the show on the road and start the presentation.
The team needs to get the show on the road so they finish before the deadline.
Let's get the show on the road—everyone is ready and waiting for us to begin.
