to put on the map

1. VERB

/ pʊt ɑn ðə mæp / (AmE), / pʊt ɒn ðə mæp / (BrE)
to make a place, product, or person well-known or prominent, especially for the first time
make something well-known or notable

Examples

The new art gallery has truly put on the map this quiet neighborhood.
Winning the award helped put on the map their innovative technology.
The chef's inventive cuisine has put on the map that once-overlooked restaurant.

Synset Examples

The festival really put our small town on the map.
They’re trying to put this restaurant on the map.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to popularize - to make something popular or widely known
  • Antonym: obscure - not widely known or prominent
