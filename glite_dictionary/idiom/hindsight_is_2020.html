hindsight is 20/20

1. X

/ ˈhaɪnd.saɪt ɪz ˌtwɛnti ˌtwɛnti / (AmE), / ˈhaɪnd.sʌɪt ɪz ˌtwɛn.ti ˌtwɛn.ti / (BrE)
it is easy to understand what you should have done in a situation after it has already happened
easy understanding after the fact

Examples

Looking back, I realize I should have invested earlier, but hindsight is 20/20.
We all make mistakes, and it's easy to judge decisions after the fact because hindsight is 20/20.
She regretted not preparing more for the exam, knowing that hindsight is 20/20.

Synset Examples

They laughed, saying hindsight is 20/20 after the mistake.

Etymology

Uses "20/20" as a reference to perfect visual acuity, highlighting clear understanding in retrospect.
