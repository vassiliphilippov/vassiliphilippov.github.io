to the gods smile on someone

1. VERB

/ ðə ɡɑdz smaɪl ɑn ˈsʌmwʌn / (AmE), / ðə ɡɒdz smaɪl ɒn ˈsʌmwʌn / (BrE)
used to say that someone is experiencing good luck or favorable circumstances, as if blessed by higher powers
be especially lucky or fortunate

Examples

After receiving two promotions in a year, it seemed the gods smile on someone like her.
He aced every exam without studying, as if the gods smile on someone such as him.
Whenever she enters a raffle, the gods smile on someone and she usually walks away with a prize.

Synset Examples

He won the lottery; the gods smile on someone like him.
When luck strikes, you know the gods smile on someone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luck - chance or fortune, especially as bringing good or bad outcomes
  • Synonym: fortune smiles on someone - said when someone experiences a run of good luck
