to drop the soap

1. VERB

/ drɑp ðə soʊp / (AmE), / drɒp ðə səʊp / (BrE)
(slang, euphemistic) To become vulnerable to sexual assault, especially in prison, often used as a warning or joke about the dangers of incarceration.
become vulnerable in prison (slang euphemism)
slang vulgar

Examples

In prison, they always warn newcomers not to drop the soap in the shower.
After watching a prison drama, he made a dark joke about never wanting to drop the soap.
The phrase drop the soap has become a grim warning about vulnerability among inmates.

Synset Examples

In prison, they always warn you, don't drop the soap.
He joked about being careful not to drop the soap.
In prison, dropping the soap can be quite dangerous.
