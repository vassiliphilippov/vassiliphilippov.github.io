at leisure

1. ADV

/ æt ˈliʒər / (AmE), / ət ˈlɛʒə / (BrE)
without hurry; when it is convenient for oneself.
without hurry or at one's convenience

Examples

You may complete the form at leisure, as there is no deadline.
Please review the documents at leisure and let me know if you have questions.
She enjoyed wandering through the museum at leisure, taking time to admire each work of art.

Synset Examples

read the manual at your leisure
he traveled leisurely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leisurely - acting or done without haste
