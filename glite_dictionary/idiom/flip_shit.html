to flip shit

1. VERB

/ flɪp ʃɪt / (AmE), / flɪp ʃɪt / (BrE)
To suddenly become extremely angry or upset; to lose one's temper very abruptly.
become extremely angry or upset
informal slang vulgar

Examples

He completely flipped shit when he found out about the mistake.
If my parents see my report card, they're going to flip shit.
Try not to flip shit over something so minor.

Synset Examples

Don't flip shit; it wasn't your fault.
She'll flip shit if you touch her things.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to lose it - to lose control of one's emotions, especially becoming angry
  • Synonym: to fly off the handle - to suddenly become very angry
