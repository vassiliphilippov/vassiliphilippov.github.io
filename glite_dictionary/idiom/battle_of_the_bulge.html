battle of the bulge

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæt̬.əl əv ðə bʌldʒ / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl əv ðə bʌldʒ / (BrE)
an informal struggle to lose weight or prevent weight gain, often humorously referencing dieting or personal fitness efforts
struggle to lose or control weight
informal

Examples

After years of trying different diets, Jane finally won her battle of the bulge.
Every January, gym memberships surge as people renew their battle of the bulge.
He often jokes about fighting the battle of the bulge whenever dessert is served.

Synset Examples

Her efforts in the battle of the bulge paid off after months.
The battle of the bulge is tough for many people.
He struggled with the battle of the bulge during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dieting - controlling food intake to lose weight
  • Antonym: weight gain - increase in body weight, often due to fat accumulation

Etymology

Humorous allusion to the Battle of the Bulge (World War II campaign, 1944–45) referring to a personal 'battle' against body fat.
