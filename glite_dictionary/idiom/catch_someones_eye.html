to catch someone's eye

1. VERB

/ kætʃ sʌmwʌnz aɪ / (AmE), / katʃ sʌmwʌnz aɪ / (BrE)
to attract someone's attention, especially by being interesting, noticeable, or unusual
attract someone's attention

Examples

The bold artwork in the gallery immediately caught my eye as I walked in.
A bright flash from the street outside caught my eye during the meeting.
She wore a red scarf that caught my eye among the crowd.

Synset Examples

That dress really caught my eye in the store.
The fluoro colors on the poster caught my eye.
The magazine had a beautiful gloss that caught my eye.
She caught my eye with her radiant smile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attract attention - to make people notice something or someone
