the life of the party

1. NOUN

/ ðə laɪf əv ðə ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / ðə laɪf əv ðə ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
a person who is lively, entertaining, and makes social gatherings more enjoyable for others
person who livens up a party

Examples

Even when she's tired, Susan is always the life of the party and keeps everyone laughing.
James's jokes and stories made him the life of the party at every reunion.
Without Mark, the gathering just didn't have the life of the party feel it usually does.
