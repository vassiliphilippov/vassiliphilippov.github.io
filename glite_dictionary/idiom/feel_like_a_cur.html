to feel like a cur

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə kɜr / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə kɜː / (BrE)
to feel cowardly or lacking in honor
to feel cowardly or dishonorable

Examples

After lying to her friend, she couldn't help but feel like a cur.
He began to feel like a cur for abandoning his teammates during the crisis.
Apologizing sincerely made him stop feeling like a cur about his earlier behavior.

Synset Examples

She felt like a cur for not standing up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feel ashamed - to experience feelings of shame or guilt
  • Antonym: feel proud - to experience feelings of self-respect or honor
