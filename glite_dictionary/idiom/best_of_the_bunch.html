best of the bunch

1. NOUN

/ bɛst əv ðə bʌntʃ / (AmE), / bɛst əv ðə bʌntʃ / (BrE)
the best or most outstanding person or thing in a group
the most outstanding in a group

Examples

Of all the wines we tasted, this vintage was the best of the bunch.
Maria's essay was easily the best of the bunch in the class competition.
The new smartphone is the best of the bunch this year according to reviewers.

Synset Examples

That apple pie was the best of the bunch.
This book is the best of the bunch I've read.
Her performance was easily the best of the bunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cream of the crop - the best of a group; the finest available
