to ride bitch

1. VERB

/ raɪd bɪtʃ / (AmE), / raɪd bɪtʃ / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) To sit as a passenger on a motorcycle, typically behind the driver.
sit as a passenger on a motorcycle
slang vulgar

Examples

He asked her to ride bitch so they could head out on his new Harley.
Whenever I ride bitch, I hold on extra tight during sharp turns.
Some people prefer to drive, but I don’t mind riding bitch once in a while.

Synset Examples

He told her to ride bitch while they drove.
It's fun to ride bitch when the wind is in your hair.
She laughed, taking her place to ride bitch on the motorcycle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ride pillion - sit as a passenger behind the driver on a motorcycle
