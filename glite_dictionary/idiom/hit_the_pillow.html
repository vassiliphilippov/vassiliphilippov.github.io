to hit the pillow

1. VERB

/ hɪt ðə ˈpɪloʊ / (AmE), / hɪt ðə ˈpɪləʊ / (BrE)
to go to bed or fall asleep very quickly, often due to tiredness
go to bed or fall asleep quickly

Examples

After a long day at work, I was exhausted and ready to hit the pillow.
As soon as she got home, she just wanted to hit the pillow and forget everything.
Most nights, I hit the pillow and fall asleep within minutes.

Synset Examples

He fell sound asleep as soon as he hit the pillow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to hit the sack - go to bed, fall asleep (idiom)
