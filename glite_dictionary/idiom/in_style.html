in style

1. ADJ

/ ɪn staɪl / (AmE), / ɪn staɪl / (BrE)
fashionable or following the current trend or fashion.
fashionable; according to current trends

Examples

Those shoes are definitely in style this season.
She likes to dress in style, always keeping up with trends.
Bright colors are back in style this year.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: out of style - not fashionable or no longer popular
  • Synonym: trendy - very fashionable or up-to-date
