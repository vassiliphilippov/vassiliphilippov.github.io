soup sandwich

1. NOUN

/ suːp ˈsæn.dʒɪtʃ / (AmE), / suːp ˈsæn.dɪtʃ / (BrE)
a situation or thing that is extremely messy, disorganized, or a complete disaster
very messy or disastrous situation
informal

Examples

After the new manager started, the whole department quickly became a soup sandwich.
Trying to relocate the office during the busiest season turned into a real soup sandwich.
Without proper instructions, their group project was a total soup sandwich from day one.

Synset Examples

His plans fell apart, leaving him with a soup sandwich.
With no strategy, the project ended up a soup sandwich.
