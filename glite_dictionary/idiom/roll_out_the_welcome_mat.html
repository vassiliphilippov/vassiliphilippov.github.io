to roll out the welcome mat

1. VERB

/ roʊl aʊt ðə ˈwɛlkəm mæt / (AmE), / rəʊl aʊt ðə ˈwɛlkəm mæt / (BrE)
to make someone feel very welcome, typically by giving special attention or hospitality
make someone feel very welcome

Examples

Whenever a new employee joins, we roll out the welcome mat to make them feel at home.
The hotel staff rolled out the welcome mat for the VIP guest, offering complimentary services.
At Thanksgiving, my grandmother loves to roll out the welcome mat for the entire family.

Synset Examples

They rolled out the welcome mat for their guests.
