to make love

1. VERB

/ meɪk lʌv / (AmE), / meɪk lʌv / (BrE)
to engage in sexual activity, especially as an expression of love or affection
have sexual intercourse

Examples

They decided to make love for the first time on their anniversary.
Some couples believe it's important to make love regularly to maintain intimacy.
He was nervous before trying to make love with his new partner.

Synset Examples
