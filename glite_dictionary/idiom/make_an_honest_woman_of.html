to make an honest woman of

1. VERB

/ meɪk ən ˈɑnɪst ˈwʊmən ʌv / (AmE), / meɪk ən ˈɒnɪst ˈwʊmən ɒv / (BrE)
(of a man) to marry a woman, especially after a romantic or sexual relationship, so as to legitimize the relationship or ensure her respectability according to traditional standards.
marry a woman to legitimize a relationship

Examples

After years of dating, he asked her to marry him and finally make an honest woman of her.
Some people still think a man should make an honest woman of his partner if they've been living together.
He joked that he would make an honest woman of her by proposing marriage.

Synset Examples

He proposed to make an honest woman of his longtime girlfriend.
They got married to make an honest woman of her.
He finally decided to make an honest woman out of her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to tie the knot - to get married

Etymology

Refers to the dated social expectation that a woman's virtue is restored or confirmed through marriage, especially if a relationship was previously seen as improper.
