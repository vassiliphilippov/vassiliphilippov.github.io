to spit nails

1. VERB

/ spɪt neɪlz / (AmE), / spɪt neɪlz / (BrE)
To be extremely angry; to show intense anger or rage.
to be extremely angry

Examples

After the argument with his boss, he was spitting nails for the rest of the day.
She looked like she could spit nails when she heard the news.
When the team lost the championship, fans began spitting nails at the referees.

Synset Examples

He was so angry he looked like he could spit nails.
After that meeting, she was spitting nails all week.
When he lost the game, he was spitting nails at everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to seethe - to be filled with intense but unexpressed anger
  • Synonym: fume - to show or feel anger, especially in a visible way
