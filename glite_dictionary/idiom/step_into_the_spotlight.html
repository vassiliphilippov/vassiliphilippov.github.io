to step into the spotlight

1. VERB

/ stɛp ˈɪntu ðə ˈspaɪtˌlɑɪt / (AmE), / stɛp ˈɪntuː ðə ˈspaɪtˌlaɪt / (BrE)
to become the center of public attention or gain fame
become the center of attention

Examples

After years of hard work, she finally stepped into the spotlight with her debut novel.
The young athlete stepped into the spotlight after winning the championship.
Many actors dream of one day stepping into the spotlight on Broadway.

Synset Examples

He stepped into the spotlight for his big moment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: take center stage - to become the most important or prominent person or thing
  • Antonym: fade into the background - to become less noticeable or prominent
