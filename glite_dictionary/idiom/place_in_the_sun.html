place in the sun

1. NOUN

/ pleɪs ɪn ðə sʌn / (AmE), / pleɪs ɪn ðə sʌn / (BrE)
a position of success, recognition, or happiness, especially one that has been longed for or deserved
position of success or happiness

Examples

After years of hard work, she finally achieved her place in the sun at the company.
Many immigrants came hoping to find their place in the sun and build a better future.
He spent his entire career striving for a place in the sun among the literary greats.

Synset Examples

She dreamed of finding her place in the sun.
He finally found his place in the sun after years.
Everyone deserves a place in the sun to thrive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prominence - the state of being important or famous
  • Antonym: obscurity - the state of being unknown or inconspicuous
