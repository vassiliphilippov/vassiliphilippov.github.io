to put the cat among the pigeons

1. VERB

/ pʊt ðə kæt əˌmʌŋ ðə ˈpɪdʒənz / (AmE), / pʊt ðə kæt əˌmʌŋ ðə ˈpɪdʒɪənz / (BrE)
to do or say something that causes trouble, confusion, or strong reactions among people
cause trouble or confusion

Examples

His announcement about the merger really put the cat among the pigeons at the company.
Her question at the meeting put the cat among the pigeons and sparked a heated debate.
If you reveal that secret now, it will surely put the cat among the pigeons.

Synset Examples
