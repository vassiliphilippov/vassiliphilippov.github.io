catch me if you can

1. X

/ kætʃ mi ɪf ju kæn / (AmE), / katʃ mi ɪf ju kan / (BrE)
Used to challenge someone to try to catch or overtake you, often playfully or defiantly.
challenge to catch or follow

Examples

As the children played in the yard, one shouted, 'Catch me if you can!' before sprinting away.
He sent a cryptic message to the police, taunting them with 'catch me if you can'.
During the chase, she yelled back at her friend, 'catch me if you can!'

Synset Examples

Catch me if you can, I’m going for a run!
