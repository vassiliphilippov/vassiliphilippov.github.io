world is one's lobster

1. X

/ wɝːld ɪz wʌnz ˈlɑbstɚ / (AmE), / wɜːld ɪz wʌnz ˈlɒbstə / (BrE)
used humorously to mean that many opportunities are available to someone
opportunity is widely available (humorous)
informal

Examples

Now that he's graduated, the world is his lobster and anything is possible.
She finished her last exam and declared, "The world is my lobster!"
With their business off the ground, the world is their lobster from here on out.

Synset Examples

After graduating, she felt the world is her lobster.
Now that he has the skills, the world is his lobster.
With that promotion, the world is his lobster now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the world is your oyster - many opportunities are open to you

Etymology

Humorous mishearing or reinterpretation of the idiom 'the world is your oyster'.
