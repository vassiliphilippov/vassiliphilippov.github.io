little room for doubt

1. X

/ ˈlɪt̬l ruːm fər daʊt / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ruːm fə daʊt / (BrE)
Almost no possibility that something is uncertain or ambiguous; completely certain.
virtually no possibility of uncertainty

Examples

Her statement left little room for doubt about her intentions.
The final results gave little room for doubt that he was the winner.
The witness's detailed description left little room for doubt in the jury's mind.

Synset Examples

Her explanation left little room for doubt.
The evidence was clear, with no room for doubt.
His explanation left no room for doubt; it was clear cut.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no doubt - used to say that something is very likely or certain
  • Antonym: uncertain - not able to be relied on; not known or definite
