top of the tree

1. NOUN

/ tɑp əv ðə tri / (AmE), / tɒp əv ðə tri / (BrE)
at the highest level of success, influence, or achievement within a particular field or profession
the highest position in a field or profession

Examples

After years of hard work, she finally reached the top of the tree in her profession.
Very few athletes make it to the top of the tree in international sports.
He was determined to climb to the top of the tree no matter what obstacles he faced.

Synset Examples

She feels secure now that she’s at the top of the tree.
He’s currently at the top of the tree in his career.
Only a few reach the top of the tree in this industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at the top - at the highest position or level
  • Antonym: bottom of the ladder - the lowest position or rank in a hierarchy
