to touch a chord

1. VERB

/ tʌtʃ ə kɔrd / (AmE), / tʌtʃ ə kɔːd / (BrE)
to evoke a strong emotional reaction or response in someone
evoke an emotional reaction

Examples

Her moving speech touched a chord with everyone at the ceremony.
The film touches a chord in audiences by dealing with themes of loss and hope.
When he described his childhood struggles, it touched a chord in many listeners.

Synset Examples

this writer strikes a chord with young women
The storyteller touched a chord

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to strike a chord - to cause someone to remember something or feel sympathy; to evoke an emotional response
