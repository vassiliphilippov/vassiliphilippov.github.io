bum rap

1. NOUN

/ bʌm ræp / (AmE), / bʌm ræp / (BrE)
an unfair or undeserved criticism, blame, or punishment
unfair criticism or punishment
informal american

Examples

Many people felt the mayor got a bum rap for the budget crisis.
After reviewing the facts, the judge agreed it was a bum rap and overturned the verdict.
Sometimes students get a bum rap for issues they didn't cause.

Synset Examples

He didn’t deserve that bum rap from his peers.
She always gets a bum rap for being late.
He felt he got a bum rap for that mistake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad rap - unfair criticism or reputation

Etymology

American slang, from "bum" (bad, worthless) and "rap" (reproach, blame).
