oh my heck

1. INTJ

/ oʊ maɪ hɛk / (AmE), / əʊ maɪ hɛk / (BrE)
used to express surprise or disbelief, often as a mild or euphemistic exclamation
expression of surprise or disbelief
informal american

Examples

Oh my heck, I can't believe we actually won the game!
She opened the letter and exclaimed, 'oh my heck, it's from my favorite author!'
Oh my heck, this cake is even better than last year's!

Synset Examples

Oh my heck, this weather is unbelievable!
Oh my heck, did she really say that?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oh my gosh - an exclamation expressing surprise or disbelief
  • Synonym: oh my God - an exclamation expressing strong emotion, surprise, or disbelief

Etymology

Euphemistic alteration of 'oh my heck' for 'oh my God' or 'oh my heck', chiefly in US English, especially Utah.
