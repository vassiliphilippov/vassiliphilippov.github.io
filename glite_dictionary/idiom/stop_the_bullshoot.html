to stop the bullshoot

1. VERB

/ stɑp ðə ˈbʊlˌʃuːt / (AmE), / stɒp ðə ˈbʊlˌʃuːt / (BrE)
used to tell someone to stop saying things that are not true, important, or helpful and to speak or act seriously
stop speaking nonsense; get serious
informal

Examples

Can you stop the bullshoot and just answer my question?
It's time to stop the bullshoot and focus on what really matters.
He told his team to stop the bullshoot before starting the important discussion.

Synset Examples

Stop the bullshoot and get to the point.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stop the bullshit - stop saying nonsense, be serious

Etymology

Euphemistic alteration of 'bullshit', replacing 'shit' with 'shoot'.
