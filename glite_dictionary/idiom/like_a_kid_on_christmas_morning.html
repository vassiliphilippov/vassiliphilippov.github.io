like a kid on Christmas morning

1. X

/ laɪk ə kɪd ɑn ˈkrɪsməs ˈmɔrnɪŋ / (AmE), / laɪk ə kɪd ɒn ˈkrɪsməs ˈmɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
extremely excited or joyful, like a child receiving presents on Christmas morning
very excited or joyful

Examples

He looked like a kid on Christmas morning when he got the news about his promotion.
When she entered the candy store, she was like a kid on Christmas morning.
After scoring the winning goal, he felt like a kid on Christmas morning.

Synset Examples

She acted like a kid on Christmas morning when she saw the gift.
