under the circumstances

1. ADV

/ ˈʌndɚ ðə ˈsɝː.kəmˌstænsɪz / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ðə ˈsɜː.kəmˌstɑːnsɪz / (BrE)
because of, or considering, the present conditions or situation
in view of the current situation

Examples

Under the circumstances, postponing the meeting was the best decision we could make.
I think we did well under the circumstances and should be proud of our effort.
Under the circumstances, it's understandable that she was upset.

Synset Examples

under the circumstances I cannot buy the house

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the circumstances - in view of the situation; synonym of 'under the circumstances'
