for beans

1. ADV

/ fər binz / (AmE), / fə ˈbiːnz / (BrE)
Not at all; having no value or importance.
of no value; not at all
informal

Examples

She doesn't care for beans about what people think of her.
I can't play the piano for beans, but I still enjoy trying.
That old car isn't worth for beans after all these years.

Synset Examples

I wouldn't do that for beans, it's not worth it.
