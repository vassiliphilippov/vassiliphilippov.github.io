like a kid in a candy store

1. X

/ laɪk ə kɪd ɪn ə ˈkændi stɔr / (AmE), / laɪk ə kɪd ɪn ə ˈkændi stɔː / (BrE)
Extremely excited and happy, often because there are many appealing choices available.
very excited and happy

Examples

When she entered the bookstore, she was like a kid in a candy store.
He felt like a kid in a candy store when he saw all the new gadgets at the expo.
Surrounded by puppies, she was like a kid in a candy store.

Synset Examples

He'll be like a kid in a candy store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: like a child in a sweetshop - very excited and happy (British equivalent idiom)
  • Synonym: over the moon - extremely happy or pleased
