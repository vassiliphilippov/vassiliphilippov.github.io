near as damn it

1. ADV

/ nɪr əz ˈdæm ɪt / (AmE), / nɪə əz ˈdæm ɪt / (BrE)
Almost exactly; as close as possible without being exact.
almost exactly; very nearly
informal

Examples

The repairs cost, near as damn it, a thousand dollars.
It's near as damn it finished, just a few more details to sort out.
The result was, near as damn it, what we predicted.

Synset Examples

That was, near as damn it, the best performance ever.
The project is near as damn it to completion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: almost - very nearly but not quite
  • Antonym: exactly - in a precise or accurate way
