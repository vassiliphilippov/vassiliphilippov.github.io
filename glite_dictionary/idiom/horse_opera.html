horse opera

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs ˈɑpərə / (AmE), / hɔːs ˈɒpərə / (BrE)
A film or television show about life in the American West, typically featuring cowboys, horses, and frontier adventures.
a Western film or TV show
informal

Examples

He grew up watching every horse opera that played at the local theater.
These days, it's rare to see a good old-fashioned horse opera on television.
The director's latest film is a modern take on the classic horse opera genre.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Western - A film genre about the American West

Etymology

Modelled after 'soap opera,' with 'horse' referring to the Western genre's association with horses.

melodramatic or exaggerated situation
informal american
