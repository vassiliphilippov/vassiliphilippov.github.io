fit for a king

1. ADJ

/ fɪt fər ə kɪŋ / (AmE), / fɪt fə ə kɪŋ / (BrE)
of a standard or quality that is exceptionally high, suitable for royalty; luxurious or splendid.
of exceptional quality; luxurious

Examples

The hotel suite was fit for a king, with marble floors and gold-plated fixtures.
She prepared a breakfast fit for a king, complete with fresh pastries and fruit.
The anniversary dinner was so lavish it truly was fit for a king.

Synset Examples

Her luxurious bed and breakfast felt fit for a king.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luxurious - very comfortable and expensive; rich in quality or comfort
  • Synonym: opulent - rich and luxurious or lavish
