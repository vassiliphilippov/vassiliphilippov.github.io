safe pair of hands

1. NOUN

/ seɪf pɛr əv hændz / (AmE), / seɪf peə əv handz / (BrE)
a person who can be trusted to do something carefully and well, especially in important situations
reliable, trustworthy person for handling tasks

Examples

With years of experience, she is considered a safe pair of hands for managing complex projects.
Whenever the company faces a challenge, they turn to him as a safe pair of hands.
The team needed a safe pair of hands to lead them through the transition.

Synset Examples

We trust her; she's a safe pair of hands.
In emergencies, he has always been a safe pair of hands.
He's a safe pair of hands for this project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reliable - consistently good in quality or performance; able to be trusted
  • Synonym: trustworthy - able to be relied on as honest or truthful
