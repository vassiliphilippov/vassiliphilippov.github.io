to turn up on its ear

1. VERB

/ tɝn ʌp ɑn ɪts ɪr / (AmE), / tɜːn ʌp ɒn ɪts ɪə / (BrE)
to disrupt or completely change a situation, especially in a surprising or dramatic way
to completely disrupt or change

Examples

The new evidence turned up on its ear everything we thought we knew about the case.
Her sudden resignation turned up on its ear the company’s plans for the next year.
The invention of smartphones turned up on its ear the entire communication industry.

Synset Examples

The unexpected event turned her world up on its ear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turn upside down - to cause something to be in a state of confusion or disorder
  • Synonym: turn on its head - to reverse or thoroughly change
