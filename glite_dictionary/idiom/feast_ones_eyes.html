to feast one's eyes

1. VERB

/ fist wʌnz aɪz / (AmE), / fiːst wʌnz aɪz / (BrE)
to look at something with great pleasure or admiration
look with great admiration or pleasure

Examples

We stood on the balcony and feasted our eyes on the sunset over the ocean.
She feasted her eyes on the exquisite collection of paintings in the gallery.
Tourists feast their eyes on the colorful markets every weekend.

Synset Examples

She feasted her eyes on the Tuscan landscape

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gaze - look steadily and intently
  • Synonym: to ogle - look at with obvious pleasure or desire
