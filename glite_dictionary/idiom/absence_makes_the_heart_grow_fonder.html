absence makes the heart grow fonder

1. X

/ ˈæb.səns meɪks ðə hɑrt ɡroʊ ˈfɑn.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈæb.səns meɪks ðə hɑːt ɡrəʊ ˈfɒn.də / (BrE)
used to say that being separated from someone makes you care for them even more
missing someone increases affection

Examples

When her partner traveled for work, she truly understood that absence makes the heart grow fonder.
They reunited after a year apart and agreed that absence makes the heart grow fonder.
As the saying goes, absence makes the heart grow fonder, and their long-distance relationship proved it.

Synset Examples
