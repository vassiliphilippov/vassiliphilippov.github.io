to give the shits

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ðə ʃɪts / (AmE), / ɡɪv ðə ʃɪts / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) To cause someone anxiety, distress, or significant irritation.
cause anxiety or distress (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar

Dealing with bureaucracy can truly give you the shits after a while.
