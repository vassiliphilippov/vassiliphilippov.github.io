a whale of a time

1. NOUN

/ ə weɪl əv ə taɪm / (AmE), / ə weɪl əv ə taɪm / (BrE)
An extremely enjoyable or fun time.
an extremely enjoyable time

Examples

We had a whale of a time at the beach last weekend.
The children had a whale of a time at the birthday party.
You'll have a whale of a time if you join us on our trip.

Synset Examples

We had a whale of a time at the concert.
The kids really had a whale of a time at the amusement park.
She is sure to have a whale of a time on her vacation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: great time - a very enjoyable time
