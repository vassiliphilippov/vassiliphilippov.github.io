plug nickel

1. NOUN

/ plʌɡ ˈnɪkəl / (AmE), / plʌɡ ˈnɪkəl / (BrE)
something considered to have no value or worth at all
something worthless; of no value

Examples

He said he wouldn't pay a plug nickel for that painting.
Their promises aren't worth a plug nickel.
That collection of broken toys isn't worth a plug nickel.

Synset Examples

That old car is not worth a plug nickel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worthless - having no value or usefulness
  • Antonym: valuable - having great value or worth

Etymology

Originally referred to an altered nickel coin with the center removed (the 'plug'), making it worthless.
