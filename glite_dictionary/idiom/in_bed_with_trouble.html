in bed with trouble

1. X

/ ɪn bɛd wɪð ˈtrʌbəl / (AmE), / ɪn bɛd wɪð ˈtrʌbəl / (BrE)
involved in a problematic or difficult situation
involved in trouble

Examples

After lying to her boss, she found herself in bed with trouble.
Jason realized too late that he was in bed with trouble because of his risky investments.
If you don't fix this mistake soon, you'll be in bed with trouble with the company.

Synset Examples

She realized she was in bed with trouble after the deal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in hot water - in trouble or difficulty, especially with authority
  • Antonym: out of the woods - no longer in danger or difficulty
