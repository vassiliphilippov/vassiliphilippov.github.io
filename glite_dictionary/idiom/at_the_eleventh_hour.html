at the eleventh hour

1. ADV

/ ət ði ɪˈlɛvənθ ˈaʊɚ / (AmE), / ət ði ɪˈlɛvənθ ˈaʊə / (BrE)
at the last possible moment before something must be done or before a deadline
at the last possible moment

Examples

They managed to finish the report at the eleventh hour before the deadline.
The peace agreement was signed at the eleventh hour, averting a crisis.
She always seems to start her assignments at the eleventh hour.

Synset Examples

They submitted the project at the eleventh hour.
The team pulled together at the eleventh hour.
His eleventh-hour intervention changed everything.
She made a decision in the eleventh hour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: last minute - the latest possible time before an event or deadline

Etymology

From the biblical story in Matthew 20:1–16, where laborers are hired at the 'eleventh hour'.
