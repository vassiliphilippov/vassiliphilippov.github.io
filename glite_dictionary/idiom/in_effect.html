in effect

1. ADV

/ ɪn ɪˈfɛkt / (AmE), / ɪn ɪˈfɛkt / (BrE)
(of a law, rule, or policy) currently operative or being enforced; in force.
currently operative or enforced

Examples

The new regulations are now in effect across the country.
The ban will remain in effect until further notice.
His license was not in effect at the time of the accident.

Synset Examples

the law is effective immediately
a warranty good for two years
the law is already in effect (or in force)

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in force - currently valid or operative (law, rule, etc.)
