that time of the month

1. NOUN

/ ðæt taɪm əv ðə mʌnθ / (AmE), / ðat taɪm əv ðə mʌnθ / (BrE)
used to refer to the time when a woman has her menstrual period
menstrual period (idiomatic)

Examples

She tries to rest more when it's that time of the month.
Many women feel emotional changes during that time of the month.
He bought her favorite snacks because he knew it was that time of the month.

Synset Examples

She always feels tired during that time of the month.
It's that time of the month when she craves chocolate.
He knows to be extra kind during her time of the month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: period - menstrual flow in women
  • Synonym: menstruation - discharge of blood and tissue from the uterus at regular intervals
