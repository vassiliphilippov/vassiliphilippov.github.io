to jog one's memory

1. VERB

/ dʒɑɡ wʌnz ˈmɛməri / (AmE), / dʒɒɡ wʌnz ˈmɛməri / (BrE)
to cause someone to remember something that they may have forgotten
remind someone of something forgotten

Examples

Can you show me something that might jog my memory about that night?
Seeing her childhood home really jogged her memory about old family holidays.
The detective tried to jog his memory by describing the scene in detail.

Synset Examples

That song always jogs my memory of summer vacations.
A photo can jog someone's memory of the event.
Did that movie jog your memory about our trip?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to remind - to cause someone to remember something
