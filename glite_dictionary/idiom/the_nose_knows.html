the nose knows

1. X

/ ðə noʊz noʊz / (AmE), / ðə nəʊz nəʊz / (BrE)
used to suggest that intuition or instinct is reliable, especially when sensing something suspicious or unusual
intuition or instinct is reliable

Examples

Sometimes, you just have to trust that the nose knows when faced with a difficult decision.
Whenever he senses trouble, he always says, 'the nose knows!'
She ignored everyone's advice and trusted that the nose knows about the new project.

Synset Examples

Trust your instincts; the nose knows when something is off.
She always says, 'The nose knows,' when we smell trouble.
He followed his gut feeling; after all, the nose knows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gut feeling - a strong intuitive feeling about something
  • Synonym: intuition - the ability to understand something instinctively
  • Antonym: ignore your instincts - to disregard intuition or instinct
