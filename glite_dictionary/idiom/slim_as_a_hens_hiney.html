slim as a hen's hiney

1. ADJ

/ slɪm əz ə hɛnz ˈhaɪni / (AmE), / slɪm əz ə henz ˈhaɪni / (BrE)
Extremely slim or unlikely to happen.
very unlikely to happen
informal

Examples

With four minutes left and ten points behind, their chances were slim as a hen's hiney.
Getting tickets at this late hour is slim as a hen's hiney.
The odds of rain in the desert this month are slim as a hen's hiney.

Synset Examples

His chances of winning are slim, like a hen's hiney.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slim chance - a very small possibility of something happening
  • Synonym: fat chance - almost no possibility of something happening
