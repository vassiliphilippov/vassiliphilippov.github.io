By Toutatis

1. INTJ

/ baɪ tuːˈteɪtɪs / (AmE), / baɪ taʊˈtætɪs / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express surprise or emphasis, originally referencing a Celtic deity, often used humorously or in imitation of ancient Gauls
exclamation expressing surprise or emphasis

Examples

By Toutatis, I can’t believe how quickly this year has gone by!
The chef shouted, 'By Toutatis, that’s the best soufflé I’ve ever made!'
By Toutatis, did you see who just walked into the room?

Synset Examples

By Toutatis, that was an unexpected twist!
He exclaimed, 'By Toutatis, what a fierce storm!'
She joked, 'By Toutatis, where did all this come from?'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: By Jove - an exclamation of surprise or emphasis referencing the Roman god Jupiter

Etymology

From the name of the Celtic god Toutatis, popularized in English by the Asterix comic series.
