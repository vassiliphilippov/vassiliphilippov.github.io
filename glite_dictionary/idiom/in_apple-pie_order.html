in apple-pie order

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ˈæpəlˌpaɪ ˈɔrdər / (AmE), / ɪn ˌæp.əlˈpaɪ ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
in perfectly neat or well-organized condition
in perfect order or arrangement

Examples

Her desk is always in apple-pie order, with every pen and paper neatly arranged.
The event planner made sure that everything was in apple-pie order before the guests arrived.
After hours of cleaning, the kitchen was finally in apple-pie order.

Synset Examples

He likes things to be in apple-pie order before a meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orderly - arranged in a neat, tidy way
  • Antonym: chaotic - in a state of complete disorder and confusion

Etymology

Possibly from the French 'nappe pliée' (folded napkin), though origin is uncertain.
