nutty as a fruitcake

1. ADJ

/ ˈnʌti æz ə ˈfruːtˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈnʌti əz ə ˈfruːtkeɪk / (BrE)
(informal) Extremely eccentric or crazy.
very eccentric or crazy (idiom)
informal

Examples

Everyone thought he was nutty as a fruitcake for building a boat in his backyard.
The twins are nutty as a fruitcake, but they're a lot of fun to be around.
Some of her theories sound nutty as a fruitcake, but she believes them wholeheartedly.

Synset Examples

He's as nutty as a fruitcake when it comes to conspiracy theories.
That plan sounds nutty as a fruitcake!
Her ideas are often nutty as a fruitcake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged or wildly eccentric
  • Antonym: sane - having a healthy mind; not crazy
