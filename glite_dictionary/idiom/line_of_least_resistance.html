line of least resistance

1. NOUN

/ laɪn əv ˈlist rɪˈzɪstəns / (AmE), / laɪn əv ˈliːst rɪˈzɪstəns / (BrE)
the easiest or most convenient course of action, especially one that avoids difficulty or confrontation
the easiest or least troublesome way

Examples

In difficult situations, he always seems to choose the line of least resistance.
Rather than address the problem, she took the line of least resistance and stayed silent.
The company followed the line of least resistance by approving the simplest solution.

Synset Examples

In marrying him she simply took the path of least resistance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: path of least resistance - the easiest or most convenient route or method, especially to avoid difficulty
