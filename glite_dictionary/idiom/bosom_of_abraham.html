bosom of Abraham

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊzəm əv ˈeɪbrəhæm / (AmE), / ˈbʊzəm əv ˈeɪbrəham / (BrE)
a place of comfort and peace in heaven for the righteous after death, according to biblical tradition
resting place of the righteous in heaven

Examples

After a life of kindness and generosity, many believed she would rest in the bosom of Abraham.
The preacher spoke of the bosom of Abraham as a place of eternal peace for the faithful.
Classical paintings often depict the bosom of Abraham as a comforting embrace for the souls of the just.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paradise - a place or state of perfect happiness; heaven

Etymology

From biblical imagery in the Gospel of Luke, referring to Abraham as the patriarchal figure embracing the righteous after death.
