by guess or by gosh

1. ADV

/ baɪ ɡɛs ər baɪ ɡɑʃ / (AmE), / baɪ ɡɛs ɔː baɪ ɡɒʃ / (BrE)
in a way that involves guessing or acting without a definite plan or sufficient knowledge
without certainty or planning

Examples

He tried to repair the car by guess or by gosh and hoped for the best.
The expedition was planned by guess or by gosh, with little preparation.
Sometimes you have to make decisions by guess or by gosh when there's no clear answer.

Synset Examples

He solved the puzzle by guess or by gosh.
I'm not sure how to fix this, so it's by guess or by gosh.
We’ll have to proceed by guess or by gosh with this project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: by guess and by golly - to act by guesswork or without planning
