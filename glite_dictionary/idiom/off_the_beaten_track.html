off the beaten track

1. ADJ

/ ɑf ðə ˈbitən træ̩k / (AmE), / ɒf ðə ˈbiːtən træk / (BrE)
in a place that is far from where most people go, or not often visited or known about
in a remote or little-known place

Examples

We stayed in a small village that was truly off the beaten track.
She prefers to travel off the beaten track to discover hidden gems.
The restaurant is a little off the beaten track, but the food is worth the journey.

Synset Examples

they found a quiet out-of-the-way resort

Related Terms

  • Synonym: off the beaten path - in a place not commonly visited (synonym)
  • Synonym: remote - being far from the main centers of population (synonym)
  • Antonym: popular - often visited or favored (antonym in this context)

2. ADV

/ ɑf ðə ˈbitn̩ ˈtræk / (AmE), / ɒf ðə ˈbiːtn ˈtræk / (BrE)
in or to a place that is far from where people usually go; away from the usual routes or popular locations
away from usual routes or popular places

Examples

We found a charming little inn off the beaten track, far from the usual attractions.
If you want real adventure, try hiking off the beaten track instead of following tour groups.
Many great restaurants can be discovered off the beaten track in the old part of the city.

Synset Examples

in order to experience something different, you need to go off the beaten path

Related Terms

  • Synonym: off the beaten path - away from common or popular routes (alternative idiom)
