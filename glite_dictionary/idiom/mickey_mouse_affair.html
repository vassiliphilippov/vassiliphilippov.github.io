Mickey Mouse affair

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪki ˌmaʊs əˈfɛr / (AmE), / ˈmɪki ˌmaʊs əˈfeə / (BrE)
an event or situation that is trivial, unimportant, or not to be taken seriously
trivial or unimportant event/situation
informal

Examples

He dismissed the scandal as just a Mickey Mouse affair that would soon be forgotten.
Many employees were frustrated at having to attend such a Mickey Mouse affair during work hours.
The committee's decisions were viewed as a Mickey Mouse affair by the public.

Synset Examples

He called the meeting a Mickey Mouse affair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triviality - the quality of being unimportant or insignificant
  • Antonym: serious matter - an important or significant situation

Etymology

From Mickey Mouse, the cartoon character, whose name became associated with things considered childish or insignificant.
