neither use nor ornament

1. X

/ ˌniːðər ˈjus nɔr ˈɔrnəmənt / (AmE), / ˌnaɪðə ˈjuːs nɔː ˈɔːnəmənt / (BrE)
not useful in any practical way nor attractive or decorative
neither useful nor decorative

Examples

After years in the attic, the vase was neither use nor ornament to anyone.
That broken chair is neither use nor ornament and should be thrown out.
The souvenir was neither use nor ornament, just taking up space on the shelf.

Synset Examples

The old equipment was neither use nor ornament anymore.
The report was neither use nor ornament, lacking substance.
