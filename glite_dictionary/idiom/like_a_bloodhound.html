like a bloodhound

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə ˈblʌdˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈblʌdˌhaʊnd / (BrE)
with a keen ability to track or pursue, especially in a thorough and persistent way
having keen or persistent tracking ability

Examples

She followed the financial trail like a bloodhound until she found the missing funds.
Reporters pursued the story like a bloodhound, never giving up despite setbacks.
He investigates tax fraud like a bloodhound, always uncovering even the smallest details.

Synset Examples

He tracked the scent like a bloodhound chasing its prey.
The detective was like a bloodhound on the case.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: bloodhound - a breed of dog known for its powerful sense of smell, used for tracking
