Sweet Jesus

1. INTJ

/ swit ˈd͡ʒiːzəs / (AmE), / swiːt ˈdʒiːzəs / (BrE)
An exclamation used to express surprise, shock, or strong emotion.
expression of surprise or shock
informal

Examples

Sweet Jesus, what happened to your car?
He muttered Sweet Jesus when he saw the final bill.
Sweet Jesus, I can't believe you finished the marathon!

Synset Examples

Sweet Jesus, that was an unexpected turn of events!
She gasped, 'Sweet Jesus, did you really do that?'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Good Lord - expression of surprise or shock
  • Synonym: Jesus Christ - used as an exclamation of surprise

Etymology

Refers to Jesus Christ in a vocative sense; 'sweet' as an intensifier.
