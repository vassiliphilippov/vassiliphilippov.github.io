thick and thin

1. X

/ θɪk ənd θɪn / (AmE), / θɪk ənd θɪn / (BrE)
through all difficulties and situations, both good and bad, especially by remaining loyal to someone or something.
in good and bad times; through all situations

Examples

She stood by her best friend through thick and thin when times got tough.
Their loyalty to each other has survived thick and thin over the years.
He promised to support her in thick and thin, no matter what happened.

Synset Examples

Our relationship has lasted thick and thin over the years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ups and downs - a mixture of good and bad things that happen in life or in a relationship
