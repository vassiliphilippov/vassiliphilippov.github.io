to have a bun in the oven

1. VERB

/ hæv ə bʌn ɪn ði ˈoʊvən / (AmE), / hæv ə bʌn ɪn ði ˈəʊvən / (BrE)
(informal) To be pregnant.
be pregnant (idiom, informal)
informal

Examples

Rumors spread quickly that Laura might have a bun in the oven after she refused a glass of wine.
When I told my mom I had a bun in the oven, she burst into happy tears.
