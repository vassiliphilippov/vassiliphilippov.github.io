beyond a shadow of a doubt

1. ADV

/ biˈjɑnd ə ˈʃædoʊ əv ə ˈdaʊt / (AmE), / bɪˈjɒnd ə ˈʃædəʊ əv ə ˈdaʊt / (BrE)
used to say that something is completely certain and cannot be doubted
with absolute certainty; without any doubt

Examples

The evidence proved his innocence beyond a shadow of a doubt.
She is beyond a shadow of a doubt the best candidate for the job.
It was clear beyond a shadow of a doubt that the painting was a forgery.

Synset Examples

it was immediately and indubitably apparent that I had interrupted a scene of lovers
his guilt was established beyond a shadow of a doubt

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certainly - without any doubt; definitely
  • Synonym: undoubtedly - without doubt; certainly
  • Antonym: possibly - not certain; may or may not be true

2. ADV

/ biˈjɑnd ə ˈʃædoʊ əv ə ˈdaʊt / (AmE), / biˈɒnd ə ˈʃadəʊ əv ə ˈdaʊt / (BrE)
with absolute certainty; without any doubt at all
with absolute certainty

Examples

She knew beyond a shadow of a doubt that she had made the right decision.
This experiment proves beyond a shadow of a doubt that the theory is correct.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beyond doubt - certain; clear and not open to question
  • Antonym: questionable - open to doubt or uncertainty; not certain
