top of the corporate ladder

1. NOUN

/ tɑp əv ðə ˈkɔrpərət ˈlædər / (AmE), / tɒp ɒv ðə ˈkɔːpərət ˈlædə / (BrE)
the highest position or status in a company's hierarchy or organizational structure
highest position in a company

Examples

After years of hard work, she finally reached the top of the corporate ladder.
Many executives at the top of the corporate ladder enjoy high salaries and power.
Not everyone is interested in climbing to the top of the corporate ladder.

Synset Examples

Many employees aspire to reach the top of the corporate ladder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corporate ladder - the hierarchical structure of positions within a company
