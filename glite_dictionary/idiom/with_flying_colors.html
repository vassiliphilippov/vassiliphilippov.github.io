with flying colors

1. ADV

/ wɪð ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈkʌlɚz / (AmE), / wɪð ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈkʌləz / (BrE)
if you do something with flying colors, you do it very successfully or with distinction
very successfully; with distinction

Examples

She passed her driving test with flying colors on her very first attempt.
After months of preparation, the team completed the project with flying colors.
The candidate impressed the interviewers and got the job with flying colors.

Synset Examples

A vast majority of students passed the exam with flying colors.

I hope to pass this exam with flying colors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: successfully - in a way that achieves a desired outcome

Etymology

From naval tradition, where victorious ships would return to port with their flags (colors) flying.
