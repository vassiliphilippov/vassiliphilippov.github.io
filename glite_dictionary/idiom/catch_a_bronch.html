to catch a bronch

1. VERB

/ kætʃ ə brɑŋk / (AmE), / kætʃ ə brɒŋk / (BrE)
(informal) To develop bronchitis.
develop bronchitis (informal)
informal

Examples

She missed a week of school because she caught a bronch during the winter.
If you don't wear a scarf, you might catch a bronch in this cold weather.
After that rainy soccer game, he caught a bronch and had to see a doctor.

Synset Examples

He caught a bronch and couldn't stop coughing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bronchitis - inflammation of the bronchial tubes
