it figures

1. X

/ ɪt ˈfɪɡjɚz / (AmE), / ɪt ˈfɪɡəz / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is expected or unsurprising; not surprising given the circumstances.
used to express something is predictable

Examples

She forgot her keys again; it figures given how forgetful she is.
He didn't study and failed the test—it figures.
The train was late—it figures during rush hour.

Synset Examples

She was late again; it figures, considering her track record.
He lost the game; it figures, he didn't practice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as expected - used to indicate something is not surprising
  • Antonym: surprisingly - in a way that is unexpected or not predictable
