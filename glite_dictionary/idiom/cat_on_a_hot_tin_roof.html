cat on a hot tin roof

1. NOUN

/ kæt ɑn ə hɑt tɪn ruːf / (AmE), / kæt ɒn ə hɒt tɪn ruːf / (BrE)
extremely restless or anxious; unable to remain still due to nervousness or excitement
extremely restless or anxious

Examples

She paced the waiting room like a cat on a hot tin roof before her job interview.
Ever since the storm began, he's been a cat on a hot tin roof, jumping at every sound.
The students sat like cats on a hot tin roof as they waited for their exam results.

Synset Examples

He fidgeted in his seat like a cat on a hot tin roof.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on edge - nervous and unable to relax
  • Antonym: calm - not anxious or restless; peaceful

Etymology

From the literal image of a cat jumping and moving frantically to avoid the hot surface of a tin roof; popularized by the title of Tennessee Williams' 1955 play.
