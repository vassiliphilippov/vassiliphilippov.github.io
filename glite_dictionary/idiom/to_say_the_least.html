to say the least

1. ADV

/ tə seɪ ðə list / (AmE), / tə seɪ ðə liːst / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is described in the mildest or most understated way possible
used to understate a situation

Examples

The room was crowded, to say the least, with barely any space to move.
Her reaction was surprising, to say the least, given the circumstances.
The project encountered some difficulties, to say the least.

Synset Examples

That was a stupidish idea, to say the least.
His performance was disappointing, to say the least.
The trip was exhausting, to say the least.
The weather was unpleasant, to say the least.
His performance was suckful, to say the least.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at the very least - to describe the minimum possibility
