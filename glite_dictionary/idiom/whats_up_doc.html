what's up Doc

1. X

/ wʌts ʌp dɑk / (AmE), / wɒts ʌp dɒk / (BrE)
A casual and humorous greeting, made famous by cartoons, used to ask someone how they are or what's happening.
humorous casual greeting popularized by cartoons
informal

Examples

He leaned against the wall and said, what's up Doc?, imitating Bugs Bunny.
When he answered the phone, his friend greeted him with a cheerful what's up Doc.
At the costume party, everyone laughed when she walked in and said what's up Doc.

Synset Examples

As he walked in, he jokingly said, what's up Doc?
In the cartoon, Bugs Bunny always asks, what's up Doc?
She greeted him with a smile, saying, what's up Doc?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: what's up - informal greeting meaning 'what is happening' or 'how are you?'

Etymology

Popularized by the Bugs Bunny cartoons beginning in the 1940s.
