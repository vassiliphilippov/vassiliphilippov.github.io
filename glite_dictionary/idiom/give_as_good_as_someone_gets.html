to give as good as someone gets

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv æz ɡʊd æz ˈsʌmwʌn ɡɛts / (AmE), / ɡɪv æz ɡʊd æz ˈsʌmwən ɡɛts / (BrE)
to respond to an action or conflict by matching the intensity or force received, especially in arguments or competition
respond equally in conflict or treatment

Examples

Even when criticized, she always gives as good as she gets and defends her opinions firmly.
Don't underestimate him; he can give as good as he gets in a debate.
In negotiations, the team gave as good as they got, refusing to back down.

Synset Examples

She can give as good as she gets during arguments.
In this family, we all give as good as we get.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fight back - actively defend oneself or retaliate
  • Antonym: to turn the other cheek - not retaliate when attacked or wronged
