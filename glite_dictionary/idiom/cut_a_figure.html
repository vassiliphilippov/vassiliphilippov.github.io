to cut a figure

1. VERB

/ kʌt ə ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / kʌt ə ˈfɪɡə / (BrE)
to create a striking or memorable impression by one's appearance or behavior
make a memorable impression

Examples

She cut a figure in her elegant red gown at the gala.
He always cuts a figure during meetings with his sharp suits and confident manner.
Together, they cut a figure as the most fashionable couple in the room.

Synset Examples

They cut a figure together as a power couple.
She cut a figure in her new dress at the party.
He always cuts a figure when he walks into a room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stand out - to be very noticeable or distinct
  • Antonym: to blend in - to not attract attention, to be inconspicuous
