to wake up with the blues

1. VERB

/ weɪk ʌp wɪð ðə bluːz / (AmE), / weɪk ʌp wɪð ðə bluːz / (BrE)
to feel sad, down, or depressed when you wake up in the morning, often for no clear reason
feel sad upon waking

Examples

Some days I just wake up with the blues and can't seem to shake the feeling.
He tends to wake up with the blues during the winter months.
When you wake up with the blues, it's good to take a walk outside to lift your mood.

Synset Examples

Sometimes I just wake up with the blues for no reason.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wake up on the wrong side of the bed - to feel grumpy or irritable when you wake up
