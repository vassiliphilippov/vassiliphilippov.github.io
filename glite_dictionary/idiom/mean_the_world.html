to mean the world

1. VERB

/ min ðə wɝːld / (AmE), / miːn ðə wɜːld / (BrE)
to be extremely important or valued by someone
be very important or valued

Examples

You mean the world to me, so please take care of yourself.
Her encouragement means the world to us when we're feeling down.
After losing his job, finding a friend who listened meant the world to him.

Synset Examples

You mean the world to me, don’t forget that.
Her support means the world to him during tough times.
Family means the world to her, especially now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: matter - to be important or significant
