dire straits

1. NOUN

/ ˌdaɪɚ ˈstreɪts / (AmE), / ˌdaɪə ˈstreɪts / (BrE)
a very difficult or serious situation, especially one involving financial hardship or extreme distress
a very difficult or serious situation

Examples

After losing her job and apartment, she found herself in dire straits.
The company is in dire straits and may have to close soon.
Many families ended up in dire straits after the economic downturn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hardship - severe suffering or deprivation
  • Synonym: predicament - a difficult, unpleasant, or embarrassing situation
