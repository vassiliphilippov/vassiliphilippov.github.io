on the sick

1. ADV

/ ɑn ðə sɪk / (AmE), / ɒn ðə sɪk / (BrE)
absent from work because of illness or on sick leave
absent from work due to illness
british

Examples

After his surgery, Mark was on the sick for almost three months.
If you are on the sick, the company requires a doctor's note.
While on the sick, she caught up on some reading.

Synset Examples

He was on the sick for a week due to the flu.
Being on the sick means I need to rest and recover.
I can't come to work today; I'm on the sick.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on sick leave - officially absent from work due to illness
