like crazy

1. ADV

/ laɪk ˈkreɪzi / (AmE), / laɪk ˈkreɪzi / (BrE)
very intensely or to an extreme degree; excessively
very intensely; excessively
informal

Examples

The children were running like crazy in the playground after lunch.
I've been studying like crazy for my final exams this week.
The phone has been ringing like crazy all morning.

Synset Examples
