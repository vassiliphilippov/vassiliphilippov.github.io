to be a gamble

1. VERB

/ bi ə ˈɡæmbəl / (AmE), / biː ə ˈɡæmbəl / (BrE)
to involve a risk or uncertainty, where the outcome cannot be guaranteed
be risky or uncertain

Examples

Buying electronic gadgets from unverified sellers can be a gamble.
Eating street food in a new city might be a gamble for sensitive stomachs.
Investing in unknown startups can often be a gamble.

Synset Examples

Buying from Wish.com can be a gamble.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: risky - involving a risk or danger
  • Antonym: safe - not involving risk; secure
