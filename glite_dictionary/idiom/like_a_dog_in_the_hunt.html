like a dog in the hunt

1. X

/ laɪk ə dɔɡ ɪn ðə hʌnt / (AmE), / laɪk ə dɒɡ ɪn ðə hʌnt / (BrE)
Extremely eager, persistent, or determined in pursuit of something.
intensely eager or determined

Examples

Whenever she sets her mind on a project, she's like a dog in the hunt and won't give up.
He pursued the truth like a dog in the hunt, ignoring all distractions.
Reporters, like a dog in the hunt, followed every lead to uncover the story.

Synset Examples

He's like a dog in the hunt for his lost keys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dogged - showing tenacity and grim persistence
