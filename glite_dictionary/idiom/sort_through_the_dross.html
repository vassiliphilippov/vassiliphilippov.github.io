to sort through the dross

1. VERB

/ sɔrt θru ðə drɑs / (AmE), / sɔːt θruː ðə drɒs / (BrE)
to examine a collection of things and separate what is valuable or useful from what is worthless or unimportant
separate valuable from worthless items

Examples

Each week, he must sort through the dross to find rare collectibles.
It's important to sort through the dross when reviewing large amounts of information online.
Before publishing, the editor had to sort through the dross to find well-written articles.

Synset Examples

He quickly sorted through the dross to find valuable items.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sift through - to examine something thoroughly, especially to separate what is important or useful
  • Synonym: to filter out - to remove unwanted elements from a group or mixture
