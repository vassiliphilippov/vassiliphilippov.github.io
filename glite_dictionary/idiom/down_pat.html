down pat

1. ADJ

/ daʊn pæt / (AmE), / daʊn pæt / (BrE)
(of something learned or memorized) fully mastered or understood perfectly.
perfectly memorized or mastered

Examples

She has her presentation down pat and is ready for tomorrow's meeting.
After weeks of practice, he finally got his dance routine down pat.
Make sure you know the procedure down pat before taking the test.

Synset Examples

had his algebra problems down

Related Terms

  • Synonym: memorized - learned or committed to memory
  • Synonym: by heart - memorized exactly and able to be recalled
