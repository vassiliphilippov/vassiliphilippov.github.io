for old times' sake

1. ADV

/ fɚ oʊld taɪmz seɪk / (AmE), / fə əʊld taɪmz seɪk / (BrE)
used to do something as a way of remembering or honoring past experiences or relationships
in memory of past experiences

Examples

They organized a reunion for old times' sake, bringing back many happy memories.
We decided to watch our favorite movie for old times' sake.
She called her college friend for old times' sake and they talked for hours.

Synset Examples

We visited our childhood home for old times' sake.
Let's meet for coffee for old times' sake.
He played our favorite song for old times' sake.
