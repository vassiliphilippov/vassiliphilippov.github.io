after my own heart

1. ADJ

/ ˈæf.tɚ maɪ oʊn hɑɹt / (AmE), / ˈɑːf.tə maɪ əʊn hɑːt / (BrE)
deeply appealing or suited to one’s tastes, values, or passions; very much to one’s liking
very much to one's liking

Examples

Her dedication to animal welfare is truly after my own heart.
The new library’s quiet reading rooms are after my own heart.
A good mystery novel is always after my own heart.

Synset Examples

This project is exactly after my own heart.
That charity work is truly after my own heart.
His commitment to helping others is after my own heart.

Etymology

From Biblical allusions (e.g., 'a man after my own heart'), meaning someone or something in harmony with one’s innermost wishes.
