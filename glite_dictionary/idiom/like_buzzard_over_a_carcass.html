like buzzard over a carcass

1. ADV

/ laɪk ˈbʌzərd ˈoʊvər ə ˈkɑːrkəs / (AmE), / laɪk ˈbʌzəd ˈəʊvə ə ˈkɑːkəs / (BrE)
In a predatory or opportunistic manner, especially when taking advantage of another's misfortune.
in a predatory or opportunistic way

Examples

When news of the bankruptcy broke, reporters swarmed the building like buzzard over a carcass.
Competitors waited like buzzard over a carcass for the company to fail.
She hated how they hovered around her like buzzard over a carcass after her mistake.

Synset Examples

The deal fell apart, leaving them like buzzards over a carcass.
