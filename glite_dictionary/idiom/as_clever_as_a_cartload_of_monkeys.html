as clever as a cartload of monkeys

1. ADJ

/ æz ˈklɛvɚ æz ə ˈkɑrtˌloʊd əv ˈmʌŋkiz / (AmE), / az ˈklɛv.ə az ə ˈkɑːt.ləʊd əv ˈmʌŋ.kiz / (BrE)
Extremely clever or cunning, often in a mischievous way.
extremely clever or cunning (idiom)

Examples

She left the negotiations looking as clever as a cartload of monkeys after securing that deal.
He thinks he’s as clever as a cartload of monkeys, but sometimes he goes too far.
Their prank was as clever as a cartload of monkeys—no one saw it coming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as sly as a fox - extremely cunning or crafty
  • Antonym: as thick as two short planks - very unintelligent (opposite meaning)
