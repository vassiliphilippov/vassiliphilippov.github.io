to stick to your ribs

1. VERB

/ stɪk tu jʊr rɪbz / (AmE), / stɪk tʊ jɔː rɪbz / (BrE)
(of food) to be filling and satisfying, providing long-lasting nourishment or energy.
be very filling or sustaining (of food)

Examples

This homemade lasagna will really stick to your ribs on a cold winter night.
A bowl of oatmeal is the kind of breakfast that can stick to your ribs.
She prefers hearty meals that truly stick to your ribs when she's working long hours.

Synset Examples

This hearty stew will really stick to your ribs!
A good breakfast will stick to your ribs all morning.
He believes in meals that stick to your ribs for energy.
