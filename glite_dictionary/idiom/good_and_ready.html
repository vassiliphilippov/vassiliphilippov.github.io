good and ready

1. ADJ

/ ɡʊd ənd ˈrɛdi / (AmE), / ɡʊd ən ˈrɛdi / (BrE)
completely prepared and willing to do something
fully prepared and willing

Examples

Let me know when you are good and ready to begin.
She was good and ready to face whatever challenges lay ahead.
He waited until he felt good and ready before making his decision.

Synset Examples

He is good and ready for the challenge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ready - prepared for what is about to happen or for doing something
