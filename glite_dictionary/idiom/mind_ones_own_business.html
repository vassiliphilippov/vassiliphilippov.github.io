to mind one's own business

1. VERB

/ maɪnd wʌnz oʊn ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / maɪnd wʌnz əʊn ˈbɪznəs / (BrE)
to refrain from interfering or getting involved in other people's affairs; to focus on one's own matters.
refrain from interfering in others' matters

Examples

You should mind your own business instead of gossiping about others.
He quietly minded his own business at the party and avoided unnecessary drama.
She told Tom to mind his own business and stop asking about her personal life.

Synset Examples

He prefers to mind his own business and stay out of drama.
She told him to mind his own business about her plans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to butt out - to stop interfering in someone else's business
