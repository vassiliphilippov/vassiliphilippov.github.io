dressed like a sophisto

1. ADJ

/ drɛst laɪk ə səˈfɪstoʊ / (AmE), / drɛst laɪk ə səˈfɪstəʊ / (BrE)
wearing clothes that are fashionable and sophisticated, in the manner of someone who is stylish and cosmopolitan
dressed in a stylish, sophisticated way
informal

Examples

He always arrived at work dressed like a sophisto, making quite an impression at meetings.
At the party, she was dressed like a sophisto and stood out in the crowd.
People admired him for being consistently dressed like a sophisto, no matter the occasion.

Synset Examples

He dressed like a sophisto, always in the latest trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stylish - fashionably elegant and sophisticated
  • Synonym: fashionable - following the latest trends in fashion

Etymology

From 'sophisto', an informal shortening of 'sophisticate'.
