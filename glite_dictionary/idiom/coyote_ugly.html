coyote ugly

1. ADJ

/ kaɪˌoʊti ˈʌɡli / (AmE), / kaɪˌəʊti ˈʌɡli / (BrE)
Feeling very unattractive or experiencing regret, especially after drinking, partying, or waking up after a night out.
feeling very unattractive or regretful after partying
informal slang american

Examples

After seeing herself in the mirror the next morning, she felt coyote ugly and just wanted to go home.
He laughed and said he looked coyote ugly after staying out all night at the club.
Waking up with a headache and smeared makeup, she texted her friend about being coyote ugly.

Synset Examples

He joked about feeling coyote ugly this morning.
I looked coyote ugly after that long night out.
She felt coyote ugly after waking up without makeup.

Etymology

Originates from North American slang, likening a person’s regret after a night out to a coyote chewing off its own leg to escape a trap.
