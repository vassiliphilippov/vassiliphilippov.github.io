to hang the moon and stars

1. VERB

/ hæŋ ðə mun ənd stɑɹz / (AmE), / hæŋ ðə muːn ənd stɑːz / (BrE)
to regard someone with great admiration or affection, often idealizing them as perfect or extraordinary
regard with great admiration

Examples

Ever since they started dating, he acts like she hung the moon and stars.
Her children believe their mother hangs the moon and stars for them.
He always talked about his coach as if she hung the moon and stars.

Synset Examples

She really thinks he hung the moon and stars.
He treats her like she hung the moon and stars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to idolize - to admire or revere someone excessively
  • Synonym: worship - to show great reverence or adoration for someone
