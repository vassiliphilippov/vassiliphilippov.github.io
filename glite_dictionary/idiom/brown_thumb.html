brown thumb

1. NOUN

/ ˈbraʊn ˌθʌm / (AmE), / ˈbraʊn ˌθʌm / (BrE)
A lack of skill in gardening; the inability to grow plants successfully.
inability to grow plants well

Examples

Despite her enthusiasm, her brown thumb meant that every houseplant wilted.
If you have a brown thumb, you might want to stick to plastic flowers.
He blamed his brown thumb for the empty garden beds.

Synset Examples

I have a brown thumb; all my plants keep dying.
Her brown thumb is why she avoids gardening altogether.
He joked about having a brown thumb after killing the cactus.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: green thumb - An aptitude or talent for growing plants successfully
