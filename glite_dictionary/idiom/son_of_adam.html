son of Adam

1. NOUN

/ sʌn əv ˈædəm / (AmE), / sʌn əv ˈadəm / (BrE)
a human being; a person, often used to emphasize the shared nature, flaws, or responsibilities of humanity
a human being (literary)
archaic

Examples

As a son of Adam, he often struggled with temptation and weakness.
No son of Adam can claim to be perfect.
Each son of Adam carries the burden of choice and consequence.

Synset Examples

As a son of Adam, he feels the weight of humanity's flaws.
Every son of Adam must face the consequences of his actions.
He proudly identifies as a son of Adam in our community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: human being - a person; a member of the species Homo sapiens

Etymology

From the Biblical figure Adam, regarded as the first human in Judeo-Christian tradition.
