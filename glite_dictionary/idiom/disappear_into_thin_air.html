to disappear into thin air

1. VERB

/ dɪsəˈpɪr ˌɪntu θɪn ɛr / (AmE), / dɪsəˈpɪə ˌɪntu ˌθɪn ˈeə / (BrE)
to vanish completely and mysteriously, often without explanation
vanish completely and mysteriously

Examples

After the accident, the suspect seemed to disappear into thin air, leaving no trace behind.
The data was lost and appeared to disappear into thin air overnight.
Her childhood friend seemed to disappear into thin air after moving to another city.

Synset Examples

The magician made the rabbit disappear into thin air.

The funds seemed to vanish into thin air overnight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vanish into thin air - to disappear suddenly and completely
