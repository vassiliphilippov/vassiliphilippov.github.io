on the table

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ˈteɪbl̩ / (BrE)
available to be discussed, considered, or negotiated; not yet decided or finalized.
available for discussion or negotiation

Examples

The offer is still on the table if you'd like to reconsider.
Any compromise is on the table during these negotiations.
Several proposals remain on the table as the meeting continues.

Synset Examples

negotiable demands
the proposal is still on the table

Related Terms

  • Antonym: off the table - not available for consideration or discussion

WordNet ID: oewn-01028015-s
Synset POS: s

2. ADV

/ ɑn ðə ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ˈteɪbəl / (BrE)
(of an issue, proposal, etc.) available for discussion, consideration, or negotiation.
available for discussion or consideration

Examples

The budget proposal is still on the table for discussion by the board.
Until both sides agree, all options remain on the table.
His resignation was put on the table during yesterday's meeting.

Synset Examples

Just put it there, on the table, please.
The documents are on the table for everyone to review.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: off the table - withdrawn from discussion or not available for consideration
