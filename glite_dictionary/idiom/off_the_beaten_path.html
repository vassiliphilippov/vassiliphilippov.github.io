off the beaten path

1. ADV

/ ɑf ðə ˈbitn̩ pæθ / (AmE), / ɒf ðə ˈbiːtn pɑːθ / (BrE)
in or to a place not commonly visited; away from frequently traveled routes or typical locations
not commonly visited place or route

Examples

They found a charming little café off the beaten path during their trip to Paris.
If you want to avoid crowds, try hiking off the beaten path in the mountains.
Her stories always take readers off the beaten path and into unfamiliar worlds.

Synset Examples

in order to experience something different, you need to go off the beaten path

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remote - far from populated areas; distant
  • Synonym: secluded - quiet, private, and not near people, roads, or buildings
