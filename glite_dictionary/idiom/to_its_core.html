to its core

1. ADV

/ tu ɪts kɔr / (AmE), / tə ɪts kɔː / (BrE)
to a very profound or fundamental extent; deeply or fundamentally affected or changed
deeply or fundamentally affected

Examples

The earthquake devastated the region to its core, leaving a lasting impact on the community.
The betrayal hurt her to its core, changing her outlook forever.
Scandal rocked the political party to its core, forcing major reforms.

Synset Examples

This scandal has shaken the community to its core.
The virulent criticism shook the organization to its core.
