to turn on one's head

1. VERB

/ tɝn ɑn wʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / tɜːn ɒn wʌnz hɛd / (BrE)
to completely change or reverse something; to cause a radical transformation or rethinking of an idea, situation, or plan
completely reverse or radically change

Examples

The new evidence has turned the case on its head, forcing the police to reconsider everything.
Her book turns traditional theories on their head, offering a completely new perspective.
Plans for the merger were turned on their head after the sudden resignation of the CEO.

Synset Examples

That news turned our plans on their head.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to upend - to turn upside down or completely change
  • Synonym: overturn - to reverse the previous state or decision
