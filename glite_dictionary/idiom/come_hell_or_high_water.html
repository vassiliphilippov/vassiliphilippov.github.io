come hell or high water

1. ADV

/ kʌm hɛl ɔr haɪ ˈwɑt̬ɚ / (AmE), / kʌm hɛl ɔː haɪ ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
no matter what difficulties or obstacles may arise; despite all problems or challenges
despite all obstacles

Examples

She promised to support him come hell or high water.
We're finishing this project come hell or high water, even if it takes all night.
He said he'd be at the wedding come hell or high water.
