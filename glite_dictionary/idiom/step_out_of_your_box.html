to step out of your box

1. VERB

/ stɛp aʊt əv jɔɹ bɑks / (AmE), / stɛp aʊt əv jɔː bɒks / (BrE)
to think in an unconventional or creative way; to approach problems from a new perspective
think creatively or unconventionally

Examples

If we want to solve this issue, we should step out of your box and consider new strategies.
The company encouraged employees to step out of your box during brainstorming sessions.
Sometimes, it takes courage to step out of your box and suggest something unusual.

Synset Examples

You need to step out of your box to innovate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: think outside the box - approach problems in an innovative or creative way
