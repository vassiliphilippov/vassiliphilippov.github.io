for the time being

1. ADV

/ fər ðə taɪm ˈbiɪŋ / (AmE), / fə ðə taɪm ˈbiːɪŋ / (BrE)
for now; temporarily; until a later time or decision is made
temporarily; for now

Examples

Let’s leave these boxes here for the time being until we decide where to put them.
The shop will remain closed for the time being due to renovations.
I’m living with my parents for the time being while I look for a new apartment.

Synset Examples

we'll stop for the time being

Related Terms

  • Synonym: temporarily - for a limited time; not permanently
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00054865-r
Synset POS: r

2. ADV

/ fɔɹ ðə taɪm ˈbiɪŋ / (AmE), / fə ðə taɪm ˈbiːɪŋ / (BrE)
used to indicate that something will continue for now, but not necessarily permanently; temporarily.
temporarily, for now

Examples

You can stay in my apartment for the time being until you find a place of your own.
They are using a backup generator for the time being while the main power is out.
Let's keep this arrangement for the time being and reassess next month.

Synset Examples

We can use this room for the time being.
Let’s keep things as they are for the time being.
I’ll stay here for the time being.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: temporarily - for a limited period; not permanently
  • Antonym: permanently - in a way that lasts forever; not temporary
