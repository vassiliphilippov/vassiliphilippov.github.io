in love

1. ADJ

/ ɪn lʌv / (AmE), / ɪn lʌv / (BrE)
experiencing strong romantic or passionate feelings for someone; infatuated or enamored
experiencing romantic affection

Examples

He realized that he was in love with his best friend.
They met at college and quickly fell in love with each other.
She walked around all day smiling as if she were completely in love.

Synset Examples

he was infatuated with her
Narcissus was a beautiful Greek youth who became enamored of his own reflection

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infatuated - possessed by foolish or unreasoning love or attraction
  • Synonym: enamored - filled with a feeling of love for someone or something
