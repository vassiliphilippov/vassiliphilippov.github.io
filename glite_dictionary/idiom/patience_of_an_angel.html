patience of an angel

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪ.ʃəns əv ən ˈeɪn.dʒəl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ.ʃəns əv ən ˈeɪn.dʒəl / (BrE)
exceptional patience or tolerance, especially under trying circumstances
extraordinary level of patience

Examples

A nurse must have the patience of an angel to care for patients day after day.
Despite the chaos, he handled the situation with the patience of an angel.
Dealing with toddlers requires the patience of an angel and a good sense of humor.

Synset Examples

Her patience of an angel made her an excellent teacher.
He waited with the patience of an angel for her to arrive.
Parenting requires the patience of an angel at times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infinite patience - unlimited ability to wait or endure
  • Antonym: impatience - lack of patience or tolerance
