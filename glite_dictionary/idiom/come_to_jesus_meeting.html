come to Jesus meeting

1. NOUN

/ kʌm tu ˈdʒiːzəs ˈmitɪŋ / (AmE), / kʌm tʊ ˈdʒiːzəs ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
an honest, often stern discussion intended to address and resolve important issues or problems
serious discussion to resolve problems
american informal

Examples

After several missed deadlines, the manager called a come to Jesus meeting with the team.
We had a come to Jesus meeting to clear the air and set new expectations.
Sometimes a come to Jesus meeting is needed to get everyone back on track.

Synset Examples

The team needs a come to Jesus meeting about deadlines.
After the confrontation, he had a serious come to Jesus talk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heart-to-heart - an honest and sincere conversation
  • Synonym: showdown - a decisive confrontation or meeting

Etymology

Reference to evangelical Christian meetings intending moral awakening; figuratively used for moments of reckoning.
