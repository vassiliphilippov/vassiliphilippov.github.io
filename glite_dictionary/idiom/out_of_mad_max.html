out of Mad Max

1. X

/ aʊt əv mæd mækˈs / (AmE), / aʊt əv mæd mæks / (BrE)
used to describe something, especially driving or situations, that is extremely chaotic, wild, or reminiscent of the dystopian world of the Mad Max films
resembling the chaos of Mad Max films

Examples

The city streets during rush hour look like something out of Mad Max.
His garage is filled with custom vehicles that seem straight out of Mad Max.
After the storm, the devastated landscape appeared out of Mad Max.

Synset Examples

His driving style is like something out of Mad Max.

Etymology

Reference to the Mad Max film series (from 1979), known for its post-apocalyptic, chaotic scenarios.
