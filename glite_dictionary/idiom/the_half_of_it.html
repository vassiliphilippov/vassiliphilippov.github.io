the half of it

1. NOUN

/ ði hæf əv ɪt / (AmE), / ði hɑːf əv ɪt / (BrE)
Only a part of the situation or story, especially when much more remains to be revealed or explained.
only part of the situation or story

Examples

She thinks missing the meeting was bad, but that's just the half of it.
You think this project is tough? You don't know the half of it.
He explained what happened, but I suspect we still don't know the half of it.

Synset Examples

That was just the half of it; there’s more to come.

I only saw the half of it during the presentation.
