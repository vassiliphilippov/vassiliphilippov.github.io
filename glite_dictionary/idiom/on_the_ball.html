on the ball

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə bɔl / (AmE), / ɒn ðə bɔːl / (BrE)
alert, knowledgeable, and quick to understand or react to things
alert and competent

Examples

Her assistant is always on the ball and never misses a detail.
You have to be on the ball in this fast-paced industry.
Stay on the ball if you want to succeed in your new job.

Synset Examples

She’s really on the ball with her project deadlines.
The new manager is sharp and definitely on the ball.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sharp - quick to notice or understand things
  • Synonym: alert - quick to notice and respond
  • Antonym: asleep at the wheel - not paying attention or failing to react when needed
