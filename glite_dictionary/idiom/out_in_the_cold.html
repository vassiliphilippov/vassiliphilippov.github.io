out in the cold

1. ADJ

/ aʊt ɪn ðə koʊld / (AmE), / aʊt ɪn ðə kəʊld / (BrE)
excluded, ignored, or neglected, especially by a group or organization
excluded or neglected

Examples

After the management reshuffle, many employees felt out in the cold and unsure of their future.
When the new project started, she was left out in the cold despite her experience.
If you don't respond to the invitation soon, you might find yourself out in the cold.

Synset Examples

He didn't invite her, leaving her out in the cold.
The team decision left him out in the cold again.
They planned the event without me, leaving me out in the cold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: left out - not included or forgotten
  • Antonym: included - made part of a group or activity
