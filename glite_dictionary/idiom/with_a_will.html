with a will

1. ADV

/ wɪθ ə wɪl / (AmE), / wɪð ə wɪl / (BrE)
With determination, energy, and effort.
with determination and energy

Examples

She approached the new assignment with a will and impressed her supervisor.
The volunteers cleaned the park with a will, finishing the job in record time.
Whenever he sets a goal, he pursues it with a will until he succeeds.

Synset Examples

He worked on his fitness goals with a will.
She tackled the project with a will, finishing ahead of schedule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determinedly - in a way that shows determination
  • Antonym: half-heartedly - without much effort or enthusiasm
