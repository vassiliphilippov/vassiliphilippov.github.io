dead from the neck up

1. ADJ

/ dɛd frəm ðə nɛk ʌp / (AmE), / dɛd frəm ðə nɛk ʌp / (BrE)
mentally exhausted, dull, or unable to think clearly; temporarily lacking mental alertness or ability
mentally dull or exhausted

Examples

After a long day at work, I feel dead from the neck up and just want to relax.
She was so tired during the exam that she was practically dead from the neck up.
Without my morning coffee, I'm completely dead from the neck up until noon.

Synset Examples

On Mondays, I often feel dead from the neck up.
He joked that he felt dead from the neck up during the meeting.
After staying up late, I was dead from the neck up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brain-dead - temporarily unable to think clearly or react mentally
  • Antonym: alert - able to think clearly and react quickly
