blood in someone's eye

1. NOUN

/ blʌd ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz aɪ / (AmE), / blʌd ɪn ˈsʌmwənz aɪ / (BrE)
a state of anger, desire for revenge, or intense determination
anger, vengefulness, or fierce resolve

Examples

After the unfair decision, the coach had blood in his eye and demanded a rematch.
You could tell she had blood in her eye as she entered the courtroom.
With blood in his eye, he sprinted past the defenders and scored the final goal.

Synset Examples

He had blood in his eye after losing the match.
After that argument, there was blood in her eye for days.
His rivals could see the blood in his eye during the competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revenge - the act of retaliating for a wrong or injury
  • Synonym: determination - firmness of purpose or resolve
  • Antonym: forgiveness - letting go of anger or revenge
