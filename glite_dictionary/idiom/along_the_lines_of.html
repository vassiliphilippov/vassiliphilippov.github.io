along the lines of

1. ADP

/ əˈlɔŋ ðə laɪnz ʌv / (AmE), / əˈlɒŋ ðə laɪnz ɒv / (BrE)
similar to or resembling something; in accordance with a particular type, style, or example
similar to; resembling

Examples

The new policy will be along the lines of previous efforts at reform.
He wrote a novel along the lines of classic detective stories.
Let's come up with something along the lines of last year's event.

Synset Examples

The movie was along the lines of a classic thriller.
He suggested a solution along the lines of what we discussed yesterday.
We need a project along the lines of our last successful campaign.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: like - having the same or similar qualities
  • Synonym: in keeping with - in accordance with or similar to something
