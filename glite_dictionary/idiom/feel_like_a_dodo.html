to feel like a dodo

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə ˈdoʊdoʊ / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈdəʊdəʊ / (BrE)
to feel foolish or silly, especially due to one's own actions or mistakes
to feel foolish or silly

Examples

I felt like a dodo when I realized I had left my keys in the car.
Whenever I forget someone's name, I just feel like a dodo.
He feels like a dodo for not double-checking his calculations before submitting the report.

Synset Examples

She feels like a dodo for not studying for the exam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feel foolish - to feel stupid or lacking in good sense
  • Antonym: feel smart - to feel clever or intelligent

Etymology

From the dodo, an extinct flightless bird, used as a symbol of foolishness or naivety.
