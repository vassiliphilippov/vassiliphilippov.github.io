better than sex

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛtər ðæn sɛks / (AmE), / ˈbɛtə ðən seks / (BrE)
used to describe something that is extremely pleasurable or satisfying, often more so than one would expect from sexual activity
extremely pleasurable or satisfying
informal

Examples

The chef’s new dessert is so good, some say it’s better than sex.
For many fans, seeing their team win the championship was better than sex.
The massage was so relaxing, it honestly felt better than sex.

Synset Examples

Finding that rare book was better than sex.
