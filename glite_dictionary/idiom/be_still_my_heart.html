be still my heart

1. X

/ bi stɪl maɪ hɑrt / (AmE), / bi stɪl maɪ hɑːt / (BrE)
used to express astonishment, admiration, or being overwhelmed by a strong emotion, especially affection or excitement
expression of astonishment or strong emotion

Examples

When I received the unexpected invitation, I thought, be still my heart!
He smiled at me from across the room—be still my heart.
As the orchestra began to play my favorite song, I whispered, be still my heart.

Synset Examples

When she walked in wearing that dress, I said, be still my heart!
