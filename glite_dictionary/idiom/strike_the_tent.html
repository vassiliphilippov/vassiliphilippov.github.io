to strike the tent

1. VERB

/ straɪk ðə tɛnt / (AmE), / straɪk ðə tɛnt / (BrE)
to take down a tent; often used figuratively to mean leaving a place or bringing an event to an end
to take down a tent or depart

Examples

As the sun set, the group decided it was time to strike the tent and head back home.
After the festival ended, volunteers worked together to strike the tent quickly.
Sensing that the meeting was over, the chairman told everyone it was time to strike the tent.

Synset Examples

We should strike the tent before it starts raining.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pack up - to collect your things and prepare to leave
