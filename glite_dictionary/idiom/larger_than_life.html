larger than life

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɑrdʒɚ ðən laɪf / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdʒə ðən laɪf / (BrE)
(of a person) having a very impressive, charismatic, or exaggerated personality that attracts attention.
very impressive or exaggerated personality

Examples

The famous actor was known for his larger than life presence both on and off the screen.
Her larger than life energy made every team meeting unforgettable.
Many politicians try to present themselves as larger than life to inspire their followers.

Synset Examples

His personality is larger than life at every party.
His nickname, Muskolini, reflects his larger-than-life personality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charismatic - having a compelling charm or appeal
