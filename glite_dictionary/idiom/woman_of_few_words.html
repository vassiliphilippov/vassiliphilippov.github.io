woman of few words

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊmən əv fju ˈwɝdz / (AmE), / ˈwʊmən əv fju ˈwɜːdz / (BrE)
a woman who speaks very little, often implying thoughtfulness or reserve
a reserved woman; says little

Examples

Though respected by her peers, Maria was known as a woman of few words at company meetings.
The mayor, a woman of few words, delivered her victory speech in just two minutes.
Everyone listened carefully when the woman of few words finally decided to speak.

Synset Examples

Being a woman of few words, she listens more than she speaks.
She's a woman of few words but deep thoughts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: man of few words - a man who speaks very little
