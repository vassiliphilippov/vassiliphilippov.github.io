to feel like a total boob

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə ˈtoʊtəl buːb / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈtəʊtəl buːb / (BrE)
to feel foolish, clumsy, or embarrassed due to one's actions or mistakes
feel foolish or embarrassed
informal

Examples

After tripping over the microphone cord, she couldn't help but feel like a total boob during her speech.
He always feels like a total boob when he forgets his lines on stage.
Sending that email to the wrong person made me feel like a total boob at work.

Synset Examples

Making that mistake made him feel like a total boob.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to feel like a fool - to feel stupid or embarrassed
  • Antonym: feel proud - to feel pleased or satisfied with yourself
