to make a night of it

1. VERB

/ meɪk ə naɪt əv ɪt / (AmE), / meɪk ə naɪt əv ɪt / (BrE)
to spend the entire evening enjoying oneself or engaging in enjoyable activities, often extending a planned event or outing
spend an enjoyable evening out
informal

Examples

We decided to make a night of it by going to a late dinner and a concert.
Why don't we make a night of it and check out the new comedy club after the game?
After winning the competition, the team chose to make a night of it and celebrate together.

Synset Examples

Let's make a night of it and celebrate our anniversary.
We can make a night of it by watching movies and ordering pizza.
They decided to make a night of it with dinner and dancing.
