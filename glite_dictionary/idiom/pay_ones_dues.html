to pay one's dues

1. VERB

/ peɪ wʌns duz / (AmE), / peɪ wʌnz djuːz / (BrE)
to earn respect, a position, or success through long-term effort, hard work, or difficult experiences
earn respect through hard work

Examples

After years of working late nights and weekends, she finally paid her dues and got promoted.
Many young musicians must pay their dues before gaining recognition in the industry.
He felt he had really paid his dues after a decade in tough entry-level jobs.

Synset Examples

He finally feels he has paid his dues in the industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: earn one's stripes - to achieve respect or status through effort or experience
