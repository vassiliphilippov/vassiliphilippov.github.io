light of someone's eye

1. NOUN

/ laɪt əv ˈsʌmwʌnz aɪ / (AmE), / laɪt ɒv ˈsʌmwʌnz aɪ / (BrE)
A person who is cherished and adored above all others; someone's most beloved person.
a cherished or adored person

Examples

Her grandson is truly the light of her eye and always brings her joy.
Since they adopted their dog, he's become the light of their eye.
Julia is the light of her parents' eye, loved and treasured by them both.

Synset Examples

Her daughter is the light of her eye.
He treats his new puppy as the light of his eye.
His granddaughter is the light of his eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apple of someone's eye - someone who is cherished above all others

Etymology

From the literal sense of the 'light' being essential for vision, and figuratively for someone being extremely precious.
