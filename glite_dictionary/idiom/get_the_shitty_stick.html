to get the shitty stick

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ðə ˈʃɪti stɪk / (AmE), / ɡɛt ðə ˈʃɪti stɪk / (BrE)
to receive unfair or unpleasant treatment; to be the one who suffers from an unfavorable situation or decision
receive unfair or unpleasant treatment
informal vulgar

Examples

Every time promotions come around, I feel like I get the shitty stick.
He got the shitty stick when they assigned him all the unpleasant tasks again.
It's unfair that she always gets the shitty stick while others do nothing.

Synset Examples

After that meeting, I felt like I got the shitty stick.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get the short end of the stick - to receive the worst, least desirable, or unfair outcome

Etymology

From the vulgar extension of the British idiom 'the shitty end of the stick', referring to the worse part of a situation.
