like a demon

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə ˈdiːmən / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈdiːmən / (BrE)
with great energy, enthusiasm, or intensity; in a fiercely determined way
with great energy or intensity

Examples

She practiced the piano like a demon to prepare for her big recital.
During the sale, shoppers rushed through the store like a demon unleashed.
He cleaned the garage like a demon and finished in half the usual time.

Synset Examples

He worked on that project like a demon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: like mad - with great intensity or enthusiasm
  • Synonym: like the devil - with fierce speed or energy
