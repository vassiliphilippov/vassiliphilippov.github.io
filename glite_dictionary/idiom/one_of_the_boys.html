one of the boys

1. NOUN

/ ˌwʌn əv ðə ˈbɔɪz / (AmE), / ˌwʌn əv ðə ˈbɔɪz / (BrE)
a man who is accepted as part of a group of men and treated as their peer or equal, especially in a social context
man accepted as part of a male group

Examples

He worked hard to become one of the boys at his new job.
Despite being the manager, he never stopped being one of the boys after hours.
It took some time for him to feel like one of the boys in the football team.

Synset Examples

he quickly became one of the boys

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insider - a person who is accepted as part of a particular group
