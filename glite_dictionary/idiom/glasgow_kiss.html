Glasgow kiss

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlæsɡoʊ kɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːzɡəʊ kɪs / (BrE)
a headbutt delivered as an act of aggression, especially in a fight
an aggressive headbutt
informal british

Examples

During the heated argument, he threatened to give him a Glasgow kiss.
The bouncer broke up the fight just as someone tried to land a Glasgow kiss.
You should watch out—he’s known for ending disputes with a quick Glasgow kiss.

Synset Examples

He gave his opponent a Glasgow kiss during the brawl.
The fight escalated quickly, ending with a Glasgow kiss.
After the argument, a Glasgow kiss was exchanged.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to headbutt - a blow delivered with the head

Etymology

Originally British slang; derives from Glasgow’s reputation for street violence.
