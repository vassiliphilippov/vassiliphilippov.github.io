hit with the ugly stick

1. X

/ hɪt wɪð ði ˈʌɡli stɪk / (AmE), / hɪt wɪð ði ˈʌɡli stɪk / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) Used to describe someone as very unattractive in appearance, typically as a joke.
humorous idiom for very unattractive
informal

Examples

His friends always tease him, saying he was hit with the ugly stick as a child.
People laughed when the comedian joked that he’d been hit with the ugly stick.
She may have been hit with the ugly stick, but she's incredibly kind and talented.

Synset Examples

He joked that his brother must have been hit with the ugly stick.
He was hit with the ugly stick, but his personality shines.
