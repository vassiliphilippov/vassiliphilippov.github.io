to get down to the nitty-gritty

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt daʊn tu ðə ˌnɪtiˈɡrɪti / (AmE), / ɡɛt daʊn tuː ðə ˌnɪtiˈɡrɪti / (BrE)
to begin to consider or deal with the most essential and basic facts of a situation or problem
address the most essential aspects
informal

Examples

It's time to get down to the nitty-gritty and discuss the project's budget.
Once they got down to the nitty-gritty, the negotiations moved quickly.
We need to stop with the formalities and get down to the nitty-gritty of this issue.

Synset Examples

Let's get down to the nitty details of the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to get to the point - to address the essential matter directly
