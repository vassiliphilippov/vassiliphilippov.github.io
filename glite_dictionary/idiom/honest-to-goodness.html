honest-to-goodness

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑːnəst tə ˈɡʊdnəs / (AmE), / ˌɒnɪst tə ˈɡʊdnəs / (BrE)
genuine; real and not fake or pretended (used informally, especially for emphasis)
genuine; real and authentic
informal

Examples

She served us an honest-to-goodness home-cooked meal.
It was my first honest-to-goodness job after college, and I was nervous.
They finally bought an honest-to-goodness car instead of relying on public transport.

Synset Examples

a real honest-to-god live cowboy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genuine - truly what something is said to be; authentic
  • Antonym: phony - not genuine; fraudulent
