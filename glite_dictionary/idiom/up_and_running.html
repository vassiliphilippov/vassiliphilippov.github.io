up and running

1. ADJ

/ ʌp ənd ˈrʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌp ənd ˈrʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a system, service, or process) operating and functioning properly, especially after being started or repaired.
operational and functioning properly

Examples

The IT department worked through the night to get the servers up and running again.
The new branch will be up and running by the end of the month.
Once the generator was up and running, the lights came back on.

Synset Examples

The new software is finally up and running.
After the maintenance, the system is up and running smoothly.
We need to get the project up and running by next week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: operational - in working order; functioning as intended
