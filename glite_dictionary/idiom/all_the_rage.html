all the rage

1. X

/ ɔl ðə reɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɔːl ðə reɪdʒ / (BrE)
Extremely popular or fashionable at a particular time.
very popular or trendy

Examples

Those vintage dresses are all the rage this summer.
Minimalist decor is all the rage among young professionals right now.
Eco-friendly products have become all the rage in recent years.

Synset Examples

His new app is all the rage in tech circles.
These shoes are all the rage this season.
Crabcore was all the rage at the underground show.
That hairstyle is all the rage among teens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in vogue - in fashion; currently popular
  • Antonym: out of fashion - not fashionable or popular any more
