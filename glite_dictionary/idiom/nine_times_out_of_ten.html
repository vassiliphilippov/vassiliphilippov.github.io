nine times out of ten

1. ADV

/ naɪn taɪmz aʊt əv tɛn / (AmE), / naɪn taɪmz aʊt ɒv tɛn / (BrE)
very frequently; in almost every case
almost always; very often

Examples

Nine times out of ten, if you call ahead, the restaurant can accommodate you.
Nine times out of ten, children will prefer playing outside to sitting indoors.
If you follow the instructions, nine times out of ten the recipe turns out perfectly.

Synset Examples

He wins nine times out of ten at poker.
Nine times out of ten, she arrives late to parties.
Nine times out of ten, the weather is perfect here.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: almost always - on nearly every occasion; very frequently
  • Antonym: rarely - not often; seldom
