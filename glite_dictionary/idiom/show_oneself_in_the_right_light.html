to show oneself in the right light

1. VERB

/ ʃoʊ wʌnˈsɛlf ɪn ðə raɪt laɪt / (AmE), / ʃəʊ wʌnˈsɛlf ɪn ðə raɪt laɪt / (BrE)
to present oneself in a way that makes a good impression or highlights one's positive qualities
present oneself positively

Examples

In an interview, it's important to show oneself in the right light to stand out among other candidates.
He made sure to show himself in the right light when meeting his girlfriend's parents for the first time.
Athletes often try to show themselves in the right light to sponsors and media.

Synset Examples

To succeed, you must show yourself in the right light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: put your best foot forward - make the best possible impression
