like a ninja

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə ˈnɪndʒə / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈnɪndʒə / (BrE)
With great stealth, agility, or skill, especially in a way that goes unnoticed or avoids detection.
with stealth and skill

Examples

She slipped out of the room like a ninja so no one noticed.
He cleaned up the mess like a ninja before his parents came home.
The cat jumped onto the table like a ninja, silent and quick.

Synset Examples

She moves through the crowd like a ninja.
He finished the project like a ninja in the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stealthily - in a secretive or sneaky way
  • Antonym: clumsily - in a way that lacks skill or stealth

Etymology

From the Japanese word 'ninja', meaning a covert agent or mercenary skilled in stealth, espionage, and combat.
