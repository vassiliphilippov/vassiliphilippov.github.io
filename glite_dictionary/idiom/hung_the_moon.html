to hung the moon

1. VERB

/ hʌŋ ðə mun / (AmE), / hʌŋ ðə muːn / (BrE)
to regard someone as exceptionally admirable or perfect; to think very highly of someone
to think someone is perfect or exceptional

Examples

Ever since their son was born, Sarah acts like he hung the moon in her eyes.
She truly believes her mentor hung the moon and can do no wrong.
His fans think he hung the moon after seeing his latest performance.

Synset Examples

To her, he could do no wrong; he hung the moon.
He acts like he thinks he hung the moon.
Her parents think she hung the moon with her talents.
Her parents believe she hung the moon in their eyes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to think the world of - to regard someone with great affection or admiration
  • Synonym: to worship the ground someone walks on - to adore or admire someone greatly
