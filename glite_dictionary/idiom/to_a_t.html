to a T

1. ADV

/ tə ə ti / (AmE), / tə ə ti / (BrE)
exactly; in every detail; perfectly fitting or matching the requirements
exactly; in every detail

Examples

The custom-made suit fits him to a T.
Her description of the suspect was accurate to a T.
This recipe turned out to a T thanks to your instructions.

Synset Examples

the new house suited them to a T

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exactly - completely correct or precise
  • Synonym: perfectly - in a perfect manner; without fault

Etymology

Origin uncertain, but possibly from the phrase 'to a tittle,' in which 'tittle' means a tiny part or detail.
