talk of the town

1. NOUN

/ tɑk əv ðə taʊn / (AmE), / tɔːk əv ðə taʊn / (BrE)
a person, event, or subject that is widely discussed, especially a source of gossip or rumor in a community.
something widely talked about; subject of gossip

Examples

After her stunning performance, she became the talk of the town.
The new cafe's unique decor has made it the talk of the town.
Rumors about the mayor's resignation were the talk of the town for weeks.

Synset Examples

there has been talk about you lately

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gossip - casual or unconstrained conversation about other people, typically involving details that are not confirmed as being true
