a bit dotty

1. ADJ

/ ə bɪt ˈdɑti / (AmE), / ə bɪt ˈdɒti / (BrE)
Slightly eccentric or a little crazy, often in an endearing or harmless way.
slightly eccentric or crazy
informal british

Examples

My aunt is a bit dotty, but everyone in the family loves her.
His a bit dotty ideas always make our meetings more interesting.
Some people thought the inventor was a bit dotty for his unusual experiments.

Synset Examples

The old man was a bit dotty, but very kind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - unconventional and slightly strange
  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged or extremely foolish
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind; not mad or mentally ill
