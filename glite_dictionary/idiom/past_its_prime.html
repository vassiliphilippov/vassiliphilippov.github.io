past its prime

1. ADJ

/ pæst ɪts praɪm / (AmE), / pɑːst ɪts praɪm / (BrE)
no longer at its best or most effective; no longer in peak condition or quality
no longer at its best

Examples

This painting, though once celebrated, is now past its prime and shows signs of fading.
The fruit looked fresh yesterday, but today it's definitely past its prime.
That old theater has charm, but it's clearly past its prime and needs renovation.

Synset Examples

That beer tastes skunky; it's definitely past its prime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: over the hill - no longer young or at one's best
  • Antonym: in one's prime - at the peak of one's abilities or condition
