one and the same

1. ADJ

/ wʌn ənd ðə seɪm / (AmE), / wʌn ənd ðə seɪm / (BrE)
used to emphasize that two people or things are exactly the same or identical.
identical or exactly the same

Examples

She realized that the author and the speaker were one and the same person.
The company and its parent organization are legally one and the same.
To the children, Halloween and their birthday seemed one and the same celebration.

Synset Examples

The twins are one and the same in appearance.
Her two personas are one and the same.
The theories presented were one and the same.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: identical - exactly alike; being the very same thing
  • Antonym: different - not the same; unlike in nature or quality
