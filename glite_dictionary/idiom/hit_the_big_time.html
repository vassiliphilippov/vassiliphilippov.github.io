to hit the big time

1. VERB

/ hɪt ðə bɪɡ taɪm / (AmE), / hɪt ðə bɪɡ taɪm / (BrE)
to achieve great success or recognition, especially in a particular career or activity
achieve major success or recognition

Examples

After winning the award, the young actor finally hit the big time.
Many start-ups dream of hitting the big time in Silicon Valley.
The novelist hit the big time when her book became a bestseller.

Synset Examples

The band hit the big time with their new album.
He hopes to hit the big time with his next movie.
After years of hard work, she finally hit the big time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to make it - to succeed or achieve one's goals
  • Synonym: to make a name for oneself - to become well known and respected
