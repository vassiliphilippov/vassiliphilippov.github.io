to make the team tick

1. VERB

/ meɪk ðə tim tɪk / (AmE), / meɪk ðə tiːm tɪk / (BrE)
to be the essential factor that enables a team to function effectively or successfully
be the key to the team's success

Examples

Strong leadership can make the team tick when challenges arise.
Trust between members is what really makes the team tick.
Communication and shared goals are essential for making the team tick.

Synset Examples

Good communication is what makes the team tick.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make something tick - to be the crucial factor that makes something function well
