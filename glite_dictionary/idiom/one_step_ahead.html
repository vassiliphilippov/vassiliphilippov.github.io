one step ahead

1. ADJ

/ wʌn stɛp əˈhɛd / (AmE), / wʌn stɛp əˈhɛd / (BrE)
in a position of advantage by being better prepared or more advanced than others
in a more advanced or prepared position

Examples

She always tries to stay one step ahead of her competitors in the market.
By planning carefully, he is usually one step ahead of any problems that might arise.
Good detectives must be one step ahead of the criminals they pursue.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
