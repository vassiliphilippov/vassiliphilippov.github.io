as soft as velvet

1. ADJ

/ əz sɔft əz ˈvɛl.vɪt / (AmE), / əz sɒft əz ˈvɛl.vɪt / (BrE)
extremely soft to the touch, like velvet fabric
extremely soft

Examples

Her baby’s skin was as soft as velvet against her cheek.
The new blanket feels as soft as velvet and keeps me warm all night.
He stroked the bunny’s fur, marveling that it was as soft as velvet.
