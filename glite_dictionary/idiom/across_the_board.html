across the board

1. ADV

/ əˈkrɔs ðə bɔrd / (AmE), / əˈkrɒs ðə bɔːd / (BrE)
affecting or applying to all people, groups, or things without exception
applying to all; without exception

Examples

The company announced an across the board salary increase for all employees.
Budget cuts will be implemented across the board to reduce costs.
There was disappointment when the new rule was applied across the board, with no exceptions.

Synset Examples

we got a pay raise across the board

Related Terms

  • Synonym: universal - relating to or done by all people or things in the world
