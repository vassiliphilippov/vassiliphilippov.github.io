to turn one's stomach

1. VERB

/ tɝn wʌnz ˈstʌmək / (AmE), / tɜːn wʌnz ˈstʌmək / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel nauseous or disgusted
cause nausea or disgust

Examples

The smell of rotten eggs can really turn one's stomach.
Watching the graphic movie scene turned my stomach.
The sight of blood always turns her stomach.

Synset Examples
