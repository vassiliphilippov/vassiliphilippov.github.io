to climb the walls

1. VERB

/ klaɪm ðə wɔlz / (AmE), / klaɪm ðə wɔːlz / (BrE)
to feel extremely restless, anxious, or agitated, especially due to boredom or frustration
feel extremely restless or anxious

Examples

I was so bored during the lockdown that I thought I would climb the walls.
After waiting for hours with no updates, he began to climb the walls from anxiety.
With nothing to occupy her time, she was practically ready to climb the walls.

Synset Examples

I was so bored, I felt like I would climb the walls.
With nothing to do, she was ready to climb the walls.
He was so anxious, he could have climbed the walls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bounce off the walls - to be extremely energetic or restless
