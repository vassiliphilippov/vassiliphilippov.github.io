ten a penny

1. ADJ

/ tɛn ə ˈpɛni / (AmE), / tɛn ə ˈpɛni / (BrE)
very common or easily available; not rare or valuable
extremely common; easily found
british

Examples

In large cities, coffee shops are ten a penny on every street corner.
These types of cheap souvenirs are ten a penny at tourist attractions.
Talented musicians may seem ten a penny, but true stars are rare.
