butterflies in one's stomach

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ɚˌflaɪz ɪn wʌnz ˈstʌmək / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəflaɪz ɪn wʌnz ˈstʌmək / (BrE)
a feeling of nervousness or excitement in the stomach, especially before an important event or performance
feeling of nervousness or excitement

Examples

Just before stepping onto the stage, she felt butterflies in her stomach.
He always gets butterflies in his stomach before a big exam.
I had butterflies in my stomach as I waited for the results.

Synset Examples

The butterflies in her stomach were a-flutter before the big speech.
I felt butterflies in my stomach before the interview.
She had butterflies in the pit of her stomach before the date.
