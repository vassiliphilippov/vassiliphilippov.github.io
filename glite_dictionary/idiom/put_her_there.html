put her there

1. X

/ pʊt hɚ ðɛr / (AmE), / pʊt hə ðeə / (BrE)
used to invite someone to shake hands in a friendly greeting or show of agreement
invitation to shake hands (idiom)
informal

Examples

He grinned and said, "Put her there!" as he extended his hand for a handshake.
After the deal was made, they smiled and one said, "Put her there!"
She approached with a big smile and offered, "Put her there, partner!"

Synset Examples

He smiled and said, 'Put her there, friend!'
They greeted each other with a handshake and a 'put her there'.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to shake hands - to clasp hands as a greeting or agreement
