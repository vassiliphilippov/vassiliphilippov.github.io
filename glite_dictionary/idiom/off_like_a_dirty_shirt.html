off like a dirty shirt

1. X

/ ɑf laɪk ə ˈdɝti ʃɝt / (AmE), / ɒf laɪk ə ˈdɜːti ʃɜːt / (BrE)
very quickly or abruptly leaving a place or situation; departing suddenly and without delay.
departing suddenly or quickly
informal

Examples

As soon as the meeting ended, he was off like a dirty shirt.
The moment the final bell rang, the children were off like a dirty shirt.
She heard the sirens and was off like a dirty shirt before anyone noticed.

Synset Examples
