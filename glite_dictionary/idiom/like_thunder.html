like thunder

1. ADV

/ laɪk ˈθʌndɚ / (AmE), / laɪk ˈθʌndə / (BrE)
Very quickly, forcefully, or intensely; used to emphasize speed, strength, or intensity.
with great speed or intensity
informal

Examples

She finished the test like thunder, surprising her classmates with her speed.
The football team came out of the locker room like thunder after halftime.
When the alarm sounded, people ran from the building like thunder.
