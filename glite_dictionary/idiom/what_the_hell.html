what the hell

1. INTJ

/ wʌt ðə hɛl / (AmE), / wɒt ðə hɛl / (BrE)
used to express disbelief, surprise, annoyance, or confusion
expression of disbelief or confusion
informal

Examples

What the hell are you doing in my room at this hour?
What the hell just happened out there?
I can't believe it—you paid that much? What the hell!

Synset Examples

What the hell you say? That can't be true!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: what on earth - expression of surprise or confusion
  • Synonym: what the heck - milder variant expressing disbelief or surprise
