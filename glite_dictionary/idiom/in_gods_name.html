in God's name

1. X

/ ɪn ɡɑdz neɪm / (AmE), / ɪn ɡɒdz neɪm / (BrE)
used to add emphasis in questions expressing disbelief, frustration, urgency, or strong emotion
used for emphasis in questions or requests

Examples

In God's name, why are you shouting at me like that?
Why in God's name would anyone want to live in such a remote place?
In God's name, stop arguing and listen to each other.

Synset Examples

In God's name, why would you do such a thing?
Why in God's name would you go there alone?
In God's name, please help us find a solution.
Why in God's name did you do that?
Why in God's name is this happening to me?
What, in God's name, are you thinking?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on earth - used for emphasis in questions (e.g. Why on earth...)
  • Synonym: at all - used to add emphasis in negative sentences or questions
