wisdom beyond one's years

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪzdəm bɪˈjɑnd wʌnz jɪrz / (AmE), / ˈwɪzdəm bɪˈjɒnd wʌnz jɪəz / (BrE)
greater understanding, judgment, or maturity than is typical for someone of a particular age
maturity or judgment unusual for age

Examples

Everyone remarked on the wisdom beyond her years shown in her recommendations.
Despite being only sixteen, he often gives advice with wisdom beyond his years.
Her wisdom beyond her years impressed both teachers and classmates alike.

Synset Examples

She shows wisdom beyond her years in her advice.
His insights prove he’s wise beyond his years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wise beyond one's years - displaying wisdom or insight greater than expected for one's age
