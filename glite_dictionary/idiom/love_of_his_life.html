love of his life

1. NOUN

/ lʌv əv hɪz laɪf / (AmE), / lʌv əv hɪz laɪf / (BrE)
the person who is the most significant or cherished romantic partner in someone's life
most significant romantic partner

Examples

She always believed she would meet the love of his life in college.
When John moved to Paris, he unexpectedly found the love of his life.
The novel tells the story of a man searching for the love of his life.

Synset Examples

Finding the love of his life changed everything.
He finally met the love of his life last summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soulmate - a person ideally suited to another as a close friend or romantic partner
