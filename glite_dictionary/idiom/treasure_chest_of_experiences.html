treasure chest of experiences

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɛʒɚ tʃɛst əv ɪkˈspɪriənsɪz / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒə tʃɛst əv ɪkˈspɪərɪənsɪz / (BrE)
a figurative phrase referring to a large and valuable collection of personal memories or life events
a collection of valuable memories

Examples

Her journal became a treasure chest of experiences she often revisited to relive her adventures.
Over the years, he built up a treasure chest of experiences from traveling around the world.
Grandparents often have a treasure chest of experiences to share with younger generations.

Synset Examples

Her memories are a treasure chest of experiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storehouse of memories - a place or collection where memories are kept
  • Synonym: treasure trove - a valuable collection found or possessed
