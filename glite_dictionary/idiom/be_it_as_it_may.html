be it as it may

1. X

/ bi ɪt æz ɪt meɪ / (AmE), / biː ɪt æz ɪt meɪ / (BrE)
Used to indicate that what has just been said may be true, but it does not affect what follows; regardless of the circumstances.
regardless of the circumstances
archaic

Examples

Be it as it may, we have to make a decision by tomorrow.
He disagrees with the proposal, but be it as it may, the company will proceed.
Be it as it may, the outcome is beyond our control.

Synset Examples

Be it as it may, we must move forward.
The project is delayed, but be it as it may.
Be it as it may, I still believe in you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be that as it may - used to mean nevertheless or in spite of that
