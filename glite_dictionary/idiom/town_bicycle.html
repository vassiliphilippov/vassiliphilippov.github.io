town bicycle

1. NOUN

/ taʊn ˈbaɪsɪkəl / (AmE), / taʊn ˈbaɪsɪkl / (BrE)
a person regarded as promiscuous or as having had romantic or sexual relationships with many people, typically in a community.
person considered promiscuous
informal slang

Examples

Rumors spread quickly, and soon everyone was calling her the town bicycle.
He disliked being labeled the town bicycle after his breakups.
Though people whispered about him being the town bicycle, he didn't care about their opinions.

Synset Examples

She joked that she's the town bicycle, everyone has had a ride.
He became known as the town bicycle after his multiple relationships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: town bike - person considered promiscuous
