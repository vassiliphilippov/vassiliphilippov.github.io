to think before you act

1. VERB

/ θɪŋk bɪˈfɔr ju ˈækt / (AmE), / θɪŋk bɪˈfɔː juː ˈækt / (BrE)
to consider the possible consequences before taking any action
consider consequences before acting

Examples

It's important to think before you act when making big decisions.
If you always think before you act, you'll avoid many problems.
Teachers often remind students to think before you act to prevent accidents.

Synset Examples

Don't be an imbecile; think before you act.
Don't be an idiot; think before you act.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to look before you leap - to think carefully before taking big or risky actions
