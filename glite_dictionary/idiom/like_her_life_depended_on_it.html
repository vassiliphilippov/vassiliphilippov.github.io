like her life depended on it

1. ADV

/ laɪk hɚ laɪf dɪˈpɛndɪd ɑn ɪt / (AmE), / laɪk hə laɪf dɪˈpɛndɪd ɒn ɪt / (BrE)
in a way that shows extreme urgency or seriousness, as if someone's life were at stake
with extreme urgency or seriousness

Examples

She practiced the piano like her life depended on it before the big concert.
He started running to catch the bus like his life depended on it.
They cleaned the house like their life depended on it before their guests arrived.

Synset Examples

She studied like her life depended on it before the finals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as if her life depended on it - with intense urgency; synonym for 'like her life depended on it'
