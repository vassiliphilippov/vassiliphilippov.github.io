guilty as sin

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɪlti əz sɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡɪlti əz sɪn / (BrE)
feeling or appearing extremely guilty or ashamed
extremely guilty or ashamed (idiom)

Examples

When I asked who broke the vase, Peter looked guilty as sin and wouldn't meet my eyes.
She felt guilty as sin after reading her friend's private messages.
The thief was caught on camera and looked guilty as sin standing in court.

Synset Examples

He was guilty as sin for eating her dessert.
He looked guilty as sin when confronted.
She admitted she felt guilty as sin after lying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guilty - feeling responsible for a wrongdoing or offense
