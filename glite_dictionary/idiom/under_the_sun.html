under the sun

1. ADV

/ ˈʌndɚ ðə sʌn / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ðə sʌn / (BrE)
used to refer to everything that exists or happens in the world; sometimes to being outdoors or in the open air
everything that exists; outdoors

Examples

There is every kind of information under the sun on the internet.
They discussed every topic under the sun during their long conversation.
We spent the entire day hiking under the sun in the mountains.

Synset Examples

You can find answers to everything under the sun online.
The beach is crowded with people under the sun.
We played soccer under the sun all afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in existence - existing; present
  • Synonym: outdoors - in the open air; outside
