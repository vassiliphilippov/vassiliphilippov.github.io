never knows when to quit

1. X

/ ˈnɛvɚ noʊz wɛn tu kwɪt / (AmE), / ˈnɛvə nəʊz wɛn tuː kwɪt / (BrE)
(Of a person) continues pursuing something stubbornly and does not stop or give up, even when it would be wise to do so.
stubbornly refuses to stop or give up

Examples

My brother never knows when to quit, even if everyone else has given up.
She never knows when to quit, which is admirable but sometimes exhausting for those around her.
It's good to be determined, but sometimes he never knows when to quit and pushes things too far.

Synset Examples

That prickface never knows when to quit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: persistent - continuing firmly despite difficulty
  • Synonym: stubborn - unwilling to change or quit despite reason
  • Antonym: gives up easily - stops or quits quickly and without resistance
