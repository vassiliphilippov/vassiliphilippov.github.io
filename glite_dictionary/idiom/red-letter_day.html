red-letter day

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛd ˌlɛtɚ deɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɛd ˌlɛtə deɪ / (BrE)
a day that is memorable for being especially happy or noteworthy
memorable or special day

Examples

Our wedding was truly a red-letter day that we'll never forget.
Winning the award made yesterday a red-letter day for everyone in the company.
The birth of their first child became a red-letter day in the family's history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milestone - an important event or stage in life or progress

Etymology

From the practice of marking feast days and special occasions in red ink on church calendars.
