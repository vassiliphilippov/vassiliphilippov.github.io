to make matters worse

1. VERB

/ meɪk ˈmætɚz wɝs / (AmE), / meɪk ˈmætəz wɜːs / (BrE)
to cause a situation to become more difficult or unpleasant than it already is
exacerbate a difficult situation

Examples

She apologized, but her explanation only made matters worse.
Don't try to fix it if you think you'll just make matters worse.
The late delivery made matters worse for the frustrated customers.

Synset Examples

His rude comments only made matters worse.
She didn't mean to make matters worse with her remarks.
They realized their argument would just make matters worse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to exacerbate - to make a problem or situation worse
