holy cow

1. INTJ

/ ˌhoʊli ˈkaʊ / (AmE), / ˌhəʊli ˈkaʊ / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express surprise, amazement, or disbelief
expression of surprise or amazement
informal

Examples

Holy cow, I can't believe you ran the marathon so quickly!
When I saw the final bill, I whispered, "holy cow, that's expensive!"
Holy cow, did you see that shooting star just now?

Synset Examples

Holy cow, that was an amazing performance!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holy mackerel - informal exclamation of surprise or astonishment
  • Synonym: good grief - informal exclamation expressing surprise, annoyance, or distress

Etymology

Euphemistic exclamation in use since the early 20th century, likely avoiding direct blasphemy by referencing sacred cows in Hinduism.
