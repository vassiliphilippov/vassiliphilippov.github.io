screen door on a submarine

1. NOUN

/ skrin dɔr ɑn ə səˈmɛrin / (AmE), / skriːn dɔːr ɒn ə səˈmɑːriːn / (BrE)
an argument, action, or thing that is useless or pointless; something that serves no purpose because it is fundamentally unsuited to its context
something pointless or futile

Examples

Trying to fix that problem with a quick patch is like putting a screen door on a submarine.
Debating with him about the rules was a screen door on a submarine.
Her efforts to keep secrets in that family were as useful as a screen door on a submarine.

Synset Examples

She realized that arguing was a screen door on a submarine in their relationship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: futile - incapable of producing any useful result; pointless
  • Synonym: pointless - having no meaning or purpose; ineffective
