love it or lump it

1. X

/ ˈlʌv ɪt ɔɹ ˈlʌmp ɪt / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ɪt ɔː ˈlʌmp ɪt / (BrE)
used to say that someone must accept a situation as it is, whether they like it or not
accept a situation as it is or not at all

Examples

You can love it or lump it, but the decision has been made.
The rules are strict—love it or lump it.
She told them it was her way, love it or lump it.

Synset Examples

You can love it or lump it; the party is happening.
He told her to love it or lump it regarding his decision.
She said, love it or lump it, this is my choice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: take it or leave it - an offer that must be accepted or rejected as it stands

Etymology

Possibly from British slang; 'lump' meaning 'to put up with'.
