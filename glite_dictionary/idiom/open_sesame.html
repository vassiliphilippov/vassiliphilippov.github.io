open sesame

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊpən ˈsɛsəmi / (AmE), / ˌəʊpən ˈsɛsəmi / (BrE)
a magic formula or special thing that provides instant access, success, or the means to achieve something easily
key to instant access or success

Examples

For many, a college degree is seen as the open sesame to a promising career.
Her charm was an open sesame that gained her entry into exclusive social circles.
Technology often acts as an open sesame for business expansion in modern markets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magic bullet - a quick and simple solution to a difficult problem
  • Synonym: panacea - a solution or remedy for all problems or difficulties

Etymology

From the magic phrase 'Open sesame' used in the story 'Ali Baba and the Forty Thieves' in The Arabian Nights.
