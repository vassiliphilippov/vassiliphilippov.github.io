still green

1. ADJ

/ stɪl ɡrin / (AmE), / stɪl ɡriːn / (BrE)
inexperienced or lacking knowledge; not yet fully trained or skilled
inexperienced; lacking knowledge

Examples

She was still green in the industry and relied on her mentor for advice.
Though he's talented, he's still green and learning the ropes.
The rookie police officer was still green and made a few mistakes during his first month.

Synset Examples

The new employee was still green and needed guidance.

Related Terms
