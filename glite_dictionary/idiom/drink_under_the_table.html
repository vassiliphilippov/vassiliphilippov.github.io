to drink under the table

1. VERB

/ drɪŋk ˈʌndɚ ðə ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / drɪŋk ˈʌndə ðə ˈteɪbl / (BrE)
to drink more alcohol than (another person), often until the other is drunk or incapacitated
outdrink someone with alcohol

Examples

He claimed he could drink under the table anyone at the party.
She tried to drink under the table her friends, but had to give up halfway through the night.
After decades as a bartender, he could easily drink under the table even the regulars.

Synset Examples

That boozer can drink anyone under the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outdrink - to drink more than someone else, especially alcohol
