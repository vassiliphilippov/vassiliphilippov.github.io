knight of the pen

1. NOUN

/ naɪt əv ðə pɛn / (AmE), / naɪt əv ðə pɛn / (BrE)
A distinguished writer or author, regarded with honor for their literary talent and achievements.
distinguished or honored writer

Examples

The young novelist quickly became known as a knight of the pen for his moving stories.
Literary critics referred to her as a knight of the pen because of her influential essays.
Earning the reputation of a knight of the pen is a high honor in the world of literature.

Synset Examples

The knight of the pen received an award for his poetry.
She wrote passionately, earning her title as the knight of the pen.
He is a knight of the pen, writing novels that inspire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: author - a person who writes books or other works
  • Synonym: writer - someone who writes, especially as a profession
