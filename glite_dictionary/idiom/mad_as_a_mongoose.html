mad as a mongoose

1. ADJ

/ mæd əz ə ˈmuŋˌɡus / (AmE), / mad əz ə ˈmʊŋˌɡuːs / (BrE)
Extremely angry; in a very agitated or furious state.
extremely angry (idiom)

Examples

After hearing the news, he became mad as a mongoose and slammed the door shut.
She was mad as a mongoose when she realized her phone was stolen.
The coach was mad as a mongoose after the team lost the important match.

Synset Examples

He stormed out, mad as a mongoose over the incident.
After the argument, she was mad as a mongoose.
He was mad as a mongoose when his favorite show got canceled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: furious - extremely angry
  • Antonym: calm - not angry, peaceful
