what do you think, eh?

1. X

/ wʌt duː juː ˈθɪŋk eɪ / (AmE), / wɒt dʊ juː ˈθɪŋk eɪ / (BrE)
used for asking someone for their opinion or agreement, often in casual conversation
asking for opinion or agreement
informal

Examples

That movie was pretty exciting, what do you think, eh?
We're making good progress, what do you think, eh?
This is the best pizza I've ever had, what do you think, eh?

Synset Examples

They all laughed, 'What do you think, eh?'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: what do you think? - used for asking someone for their opinion
