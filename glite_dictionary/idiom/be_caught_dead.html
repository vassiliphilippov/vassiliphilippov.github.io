to be caught dead

1. VERB

/ bi kɔt dɛd / (AmE), / bi kɔːt dɛd / (BrE)
to never do something under any circumstances, often used to emphasize strong dislike or refusal
refuse to do, even if forced

Examples

I wouldn't be caught dead eating at that restaurant—it's filthy.
He said he'd never be caught dead driving a minivan.
She wouldn't be caught dead wearing such loud colors.

Synset Examples

She would not be caught dead wearing that outfit.
