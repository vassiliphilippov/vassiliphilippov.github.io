one hell of a mess

1. NOUN

/ wʌn hɛl əv ə mɛs / (AmE), / wʌn hɛl əv ə mɛs / (BrE)
an extremely large, chaotic, or difficult mess or situation
a very large chaotic mess
informal

Examples

After the party, the living room was one hell of a mess and took hours to clean.
The new manager came in and found one hell of a mess left by the previous staff.
His experiment caused one hell of a mess in the laboratory.

Synset Examples

He made one hell of a mess in the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mess - a state of disorder or untidiness
