to be a good girl

1. VERB

/ bi ə ɡʊd ɡɝːl / (AmE), / bi ə ɡʊd ɡɜːl / (BrE)
to behave well or follow instructions, especially as directed to a girl by an authority figure
behave well; follow instructions (to a girl)

Examples

Her teacher asked her to be a good girl and listen to instructions carefully.
If you be a good girl, you can have dessert after dinner.
Please be a good girl while I'm at the meeting and don't make any trouble.

Synset Examples

Be a good girl and finish your homework.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be a good boy - behave well; follow instructions (to a boy)
  • Antonym: be naughty - not behave well; disobey instructions
