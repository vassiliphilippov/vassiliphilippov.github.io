when in doubt

1. X

/ wɛn ɪn daʊt / (AmE), / wɛn ɪn daʊt / (BrE)
used to suggest that if you are uncertain about what to do, you should follow a particular advice or take a safe action
if unsure, follow this advice

Examples

When in doubt, check with your supervisor before making a decision.
I always remember the motto: when in doubt, ask questions instead of guessing.
When in doubt, it's best to be honest about what you don't know.

Synset Examples

When in doubt, head to the whelk stall for a delicious treat.
When in doubt, use Foo for testing purposes.
When in doubt, I always summon auntie for advice.
When in doubt, just do your best.
