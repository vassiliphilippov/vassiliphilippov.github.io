to have a gas

1. VERB

/ hæv ə ɡæs / (AmE), / hæv ə ɡæs / (BrE)
To have a very enjoyable and fun time.
have lots of fun; enjoy oneself
informal american

Examples

We had a gas on our trip to New York last summer.
Everyone had a gas at Mike's birthday barbecue.
I hope you have a gas at the festival this weekend!

Synset Examples

We had a gas at the party last night!
The concert was a blast; we really had a gas!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to have a blast - to have a great time; enjoy oneself greatly
  • Synonym: have fun - to enjoy oneself; experience pleasure or amusement
