to suit the action to the word

1. VERB

/ sut ði ˈækʃən tʊ ðə wɝd / (AmE), / sjuːt ði ˈækʃən tə ðə wɜːd / (BrE)
to act in accordance with one's promises or stated intentions; to ensure one's actions match one's words
to act as one says or promises
