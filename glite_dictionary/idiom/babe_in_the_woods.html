babe in the woods

1. NOUN

/ beɪb ɪn ðə wʊdz / (AmE), / beɪb ɪn ðə wʊdz / (BrE)
a person who is inexperienced or naive and therefore vulnerable
inexperienced or naive person

Examples

He was a babe in the woods when he started his first business and learned quickly from his mistakes.
Despite her intelligence, Emily was a babe in the woods in the world of politics.
After transferring to a new school mid-year, he felt like a babe in the woods among his classmates.

Synset Examples

As a newcomer, he was a babe in the woods.
He was a babe in the woods at his first job.
After moving to the city, she felt like a babe in the woods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: innocent - a person lacking experience or sophistication
  • Synonym: greenhorn - a person who is new to or inexperienced in a particular activity
  • Antonym: sophisticate - a person with much worldly experience and knowledge

Etymology

From the image of a young, defenseless child lost in the woods; first recorded in American English in the early 19th century.
