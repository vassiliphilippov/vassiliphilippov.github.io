good old days

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd oʊld deɪz / (AmE), / ɡʊd əʊld deɪz / (BrE)
a time in the past that is remembered with affection or nostalgia, often considered better than the present
times in the past remembered fondly

Examples

My grandparents often talk about the good old days when life was simpler.
Sometimes I miss the good old days of my childhood summers.
He always reminisces about the good old days when he first started working here.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nostalgia - a sentimental longing for the past
