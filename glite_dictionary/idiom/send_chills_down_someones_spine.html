to send chills down someone's spine

1. VERB

/ sɛnd tʃɪlz daʊn ˈsʌmwʌnz spaɪn / (AmE), / sɛnd tʃɪlz daʊn ˈsʌmwənz spaɪn / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel a sudden, intense sensation of fear or excitement
cause sudden fear or excitement

Examples

The ghost story was so terrifying it sent chills down my spine.
His voice was so haunting it sent chills down her spine.
The news of the accident sent chills down our spines as we listened.

Synset Examples

The banshee's scream sent chills down our spines.
The horror movie really sent shivers down my spine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to send shivers down someone's spine - to cause a strong feeling of fear or excitement
