on the lookout for

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə ˈlʊk.aʊt fər / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ˈlʊk.aʊt fə / (BrE)
actively watching or searching for someone or something; being alert and attentive in order to notice or find.
being alert to notice or find something

Examples

The police were on the lookout for the stolen car throughout the night.
Be on the lookout for any suspicious emails that ask for your personal information.
She is always on the lookout for new opportunities to advance her career.

Synset Examples

He’s always on the lookout for the next germ outbreak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watchful - being alert and observant
  • Antonym: unaware - not knowing about or noticing something
