just the ticket

1. NOUN

/ dʒʌst ðə ˈtɪkɪt / (AmE), / dʒʌst ðə ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
exactly what is needed or desired; the perfect thing for a particular purpose
exactly what is needed; the perfect thing

Examples

A hot cup of tea is just the ticket after a long day outside in the cold.
If you want to relax, this quiet little café is just the ticket.
A new laptop was just the ticket for improving my productivity at work.

Synset Examples

this car could be just the ticket for a small family

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfect - being without fault or defect; completely right
