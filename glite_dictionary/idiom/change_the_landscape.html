to change the landscape

1. VERB

/ tʃeɪndʒ ðə ˈlændskeɪp / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ ðə ˈlændskeɪp / (BrE)
to significantly alter the appearance, character, or situation of something
transform something significantly

Examples

The introduction of affordable smartphones has changed the landscape of communication around the world.
Her innovative research changed the landscape of environmental science forever.
Streaming services are changing the landscape of the music industry.

Synset Examples

Erosion can dramatically change the landscape over time.
Jane Roe's case changed the landscape of abortion rights.
Sigmund's theories changed the landscape of psychology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to transform - make a thorough or dramatic change in the form or appearance of something
  • Synonym: to alter - change or cause to change in character
