life's a bitch

1. X

/ laɪfs ə bɪtʃ / (AmE), / laɪfs ə bɪtʃ / (BrE)
used to express the idea that life is often difficult, unfair, or harsh
life is difficult or unfair (idiom)
informal vulgar

Examples

After failing the exam, he shrugged and said, life's a bitch.
Sometimes life's a bitch, but you have to keep moving forward.
When she lost her job, her friend comforted her by saying, "life's a bitch, but things will get better."

Synset Examples

After losing the game, she muttered life's a bitch.
He sighed, saying life's a bitch but we must keep going.
He laughed and said, life's a bitch, deal with it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life is hard - life can be difficult or challenging
