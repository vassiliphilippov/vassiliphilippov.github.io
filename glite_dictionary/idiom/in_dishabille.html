in dishabille

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ˌdɪsəˈbiːl / (AmE), / ɪn ˌdɪsəˈbiːl / (BrE)
partly dressed, or dressed in a loose or careless manner
partly or carelessly dressed
archaic

Examples

She answered the door in dishabille, her hair uncombed and wearing only a thin robe.
Caught in dishabille, he hurried to straighten his shirt before greeting the guests.
The artist painted his subject in dishabille, highlighting both vulnerability and casual elegance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undressed - not wearing clothes or only partially dressed
  • Antonym: fully dressed - wearing proper or complete clothing

Etymology

From French 'déshabillé' (undressed, negligee).
