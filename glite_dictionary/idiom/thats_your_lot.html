that's your lot

1. X

/ ðæts jɔr lɑt / (AmE), / ðats jɔː lɒt / (BrE)
used to say that nothing more is available or possible; that is all you are going to get or have
that's all there is; nothing more to come
informal

Examples

We've finished the cake, so that's your lot for dessert tonight.
Sorry, no more refills—that's your lot.
After the last episode airs, that's your lot for the season.

Synset Examples

We've run out of cookies; that's your lot for today.
The concert tickets sold out quickly; that's your lot.
I can't help you anymore; that's your lot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: that's all - no more remains or is available
  • Synonym: that's it - used to indicate a limit or something is finished
