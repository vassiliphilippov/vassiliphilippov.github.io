in the dead of night

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðə dɛd əv naɪt / (AmE), / ɪn ðə dɛd əv naɪt / (BrE)
at a very late hour of the night, when it is very quiet and dark
late at night, when it is very quiet

Examples

They quietly slipped out of the house in the dead of night to avoid being seen.
A mysterious knock on the door woke me in the dead of night.
Power was restored only in the dead of night after the storm had passed.

Synset Examples

The storm hit us in the dead of night without warning.
They left the party in the dead of night to avoid traffic.
She heard a strange noise in the dead of night.
