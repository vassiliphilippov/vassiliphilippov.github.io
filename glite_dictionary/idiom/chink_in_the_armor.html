chink in the armor

1. NOUN

/ tʃɪŋk ɪn ði ˈɑrmɚ / (AmE), / tʃɪŋk ɪn ði ˈɑːmə / (BrE)
a weakness or vulnerable point in an otherwise strong person, organization, or system
a vulnerability in someone or something strong

Examples

The new tax laws revealed a chink in the armor of the financial system.
Her lack of confidence was a chink in the armor that competitors quickly exploited.
After years of success, the scandal exposed a chink in the armor of the company.

Synset Examples

Every team has a chink in the armor at some point.
His mistake was a chink in the armor of his reputation.
The late delivery was a chink in the armor of their success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weakness - a weak point or flaw
  • Antonym: strength - a strong point or advantage

Etymology

From the literal meaning of 'chink' (a small opening or crack) and 'armor', referring to a gap in protective covering, later used figuratively.
