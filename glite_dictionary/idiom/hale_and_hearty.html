hale and hearty

1. ADJ

/ heɪl ənd ˈhɑrti / (AmE), / heɪl ənd ˈhɑːti / (BrE)
(of a person) healthy and strong, especially for their age.
healthy and strong (especially elderly)

Examples

After his surgery, he was back to being hale and hearty within weeks.
My grandmother is 90 but still hale and hearty, tending her garden every day.
The reunion proved that all our classmates were as hale and hearty as ever.

Synset Examples

After the treatment, she was hale and hearty again.
The old man was still hale and hearty, running every morning.
Despite his age, he remains hale and hearty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: healthy - in good physical or mental condition
  • Synonym: fit - in good health, physically strong and active

Etymology

From 'hale' (meaning healthy, from Old English 'hal') and 'hearty' (vigorous, from 'heart').
