cock in the henhouse

1. NOUN

/ kɑk ɪn ðə ˈhɛnˌhaʊs / (AmE), / kɒk ɪn ðə ˈhɛnhaʊs / (BrE)
A person who causes disruption or is out of place in a group, often by their presence or actions.
person causing disruption or out of place
