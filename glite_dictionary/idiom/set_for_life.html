set for life

1. ADJ

/ sɛt fɔr laɪf / (AmE), / sɛt fə laɪf / (BrE)
having enough money or resources to be financially secure for the rest of one's life
financially secure for life

Examples

After selling his company, he knew he was set for life and could retire early.
Winning that inheritance means her family will be set for life.
He dreams of inventing something that would make him set for life.

Synset Examples

Investing wisely can help you be set for life.
With her savings, she feels set for life.
Winning the lottery means he's set for life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: financially secure - having sufficient money to live comfortably and without worry
