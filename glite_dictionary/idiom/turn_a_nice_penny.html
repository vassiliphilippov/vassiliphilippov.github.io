to turn a nice penny

1. VERB

/ tɝn ə naɪs ˈpɛni / (AmE), / tɜːn ə naɪs ˈpɛni / (BrE)
to make a satisfactory or good profit, especially in business or from some activity
make a good profit

Examples

The local bakery managed to turn a nice penny by catering to special events.
She hoped her latest investment would turn a nice penny over the holiday season.
They always find a way to turn a nice penny, even in tough economic times.

Synset Examples

The company turned a nice dime after a short time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to turn a profit - to gain profit from a business or activity
