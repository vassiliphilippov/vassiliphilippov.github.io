a damn sight better

1. ADJ

/ ə dæm saɪt ˈbɛtər / (AmE), / ə dæm saɪt ˈbɛtə / (BrE)
much better than something or someone else; by a considerable amount better
much better than something else
informal

Examples

This new phone is a damn sight better than my old one.
She's a damn sight better at chess than I expected.
Your results are a damn sight better than last year's.

Synset Examples

This burger is a damn sight better than the last one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: a lot better - much better
  • Antonym: a lot worse - much worse
