love knows no boundaries

1. X

/ lʌv noʊz noʊ ˈbaʊn.dəriz / (AmE), / lʌv nəʊz nəʊ ˈbaʊn.dəriz / (BrE)
There are no limits to whom or how one can love; love is not restricted by social, cultural, or other conventional barriers.
love is unlimited or unrestricted

Examples

She believes that love knows no boundaries, and people should follow their hearts regardless of background.
Stories from around the world remind us that love knows no boundaries.
Their marriage is proof that love knows no boundaries and triumphs over prejudice.

Synset Examples

As a pansexualist, she believes love knows no boundaries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: love knows no bounds - love is limitless or unrestricted
