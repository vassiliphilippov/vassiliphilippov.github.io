behind the eight-ball

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈhaɪnd ði ˈeɪt bɔl / (AmE), / bɪˈhaɪnd ði ˈeɪt bɔːl / (BrE)
in a difficult or disadvantageous situation, especially one that is hard to escape or improve
in a difficult or disadvantageous situation

Examples

After forgetting the deadline, Maria was behind the eight-ball at work for the next week.
With so much debt, the company is really behind the eight-ball financially.
When his computer crashed before the exam, he knew he was behind the eight-ball.

Synset Examples

The project was delayed, leaving us behind the eight-ball.
She was behind the eight-ball due to unforeseen circumstances.
He found himself behind the eight-ball after missing deadlines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in a bind - in a difficult situation
  • Synonym: in a jam - in trouble or a tough situation

Etymology

From American pool (billiards), where being behind the eight-ball puts a player in a hopeless position.
