to give a hand

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ə hænd / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə hænd / (BrE)
to help someone with something, or to applaud or show appreciation for someone
to help or to applaud

Examples

If you need help carrying those boxes, I can give a hand.
Let’s all give a hand to the performers for a wonderful show!
She was happy to give a hand with the preparations for the party.

Synset Examples

Let’s give them a hand for their hard work.
I can give you a hand with your homework.
He offered to give her a hand moving the furniture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to lend a hand - to help someone do something
