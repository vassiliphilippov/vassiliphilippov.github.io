notch on someone's bedpost

1. NOUN

/ nɑtʃ ɑn ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈbɛdˌpoʊst / (AmE), / nɒtʃ ɒn ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈbɛdˌpəʊst / (BrE)
an achievement or accomplishment added to someone's record, often used informally to show progress or success in a series of pursuits
an achievement; mark of accomplishment
informal

Examples

Getting published in a major journal was a significant notch on her bedpost.
For him, every successful negotiation was just another notch on his bedpost.
Landing the new contract became the next notch on their bedpost at the firm.

Synset Examples

She considered the promotion a notch on her bedpost.
Winning this award is a real notch on my bedpost.
Each new client is just another notch on my bedpost.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feather in one's cap - an honor or achievement to be proud of

Etymology

Refers to the historical practice of marking victories or accomplishments by making notches on objects such as bedposts.
