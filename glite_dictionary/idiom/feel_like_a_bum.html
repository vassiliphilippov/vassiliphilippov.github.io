to feel like a bum

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə bʌm / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə bʌm / (BrE)
to feel worthless, lazy, or unproductive, often due to failure or inactivity
feel worthless or unproductive
informal

Examples

After failing the exam, he couldn't help but feel like a bum for days.
Whenever I spend a whole day watching TV, I feel like a bum.
She felt like a bum when all her friends got promotions and she didn't.

Synset Examples

He felt like a bum after losing his job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to feel down - to feel sad or depressed
  • Antonym: feel successful - to feel accomplished or effective
