on the pull

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə pʊl / (AmE), / ɒn ðə pʊl / (BrE)
actively seeking to attract a romantic or sexual partner, especially in social settings
seeking romantic or sexual partner
informal british

You can tell she's on the pull by how she's chatting to everyone at the bar.
Whenever they're on the pull, they dress to impress and hit the clubs.
