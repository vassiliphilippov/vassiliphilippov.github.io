according to Hoyle

1. ADV

/ əˈkɔrdɪŋ tə ˈhɔɪl / (AmE), / əˈkɔːdɪŋ tə ˈhɔɪl / (BrE)
in accordance with established rules or conventions; following standard or correct procedure
according to established rules or standards

Examples

The committee insisted everything be done according to Hoyle to maintain transparency.
Sarah always runs her meetings according to Hoyle to ensure fairness.
The team followed the procedure according to Hoyle to avoid any mistakes.

Synset Examples

The contract was written according to Hoyle's standards.
Everything must be done according to Hoyle to avoid disputes.
They played the game according to Hoyle's rules.

Etymology

From Edmond Hoyle (1672–1769), English writer on the rules of card games, whose name became synonymous with authoritative rules.
