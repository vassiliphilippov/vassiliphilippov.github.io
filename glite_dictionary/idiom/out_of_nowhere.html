out of nowhere

1. ADV

/ aʊt əv ˈnoʊ.wɛr / (AmE), / aʊt əv ˈnəʊ.weə / (BrE)
used to describe something happening unexpectedly or without warning
unexpectedly; without warning

Examples

The car appeared out of nowhere and startled the cyclist.
She received a job offer out of nowhere after months of searching.
A solution to the problem came to him out of nowhere one evening.

Synset Examples

He won the competition out of nowhere.
In thunderation, that storm came out of nowhere!
She showed up at the party out of nowhere.
The storm hit us out of nowhere last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unexpectedly - in a way that was not foreseen
