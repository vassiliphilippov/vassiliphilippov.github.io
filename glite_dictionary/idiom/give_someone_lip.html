to give (someone) lip

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ˈsʌmwʌn lɪp / (AmE), / ɡɪv ˈsʌmwən lɪp / (BrE)
to speak to someone in a disrespectful or cheeky manner; to talk back
talk back or act disrespectfully
informal

Examples

If you give me lip again, you'll be grounded for a week.
Teenagers often give their parents lip when they don't want to follow the rules.
The coach warned the players not to give him lip during practice.

Synset Examples

Don't give me any lip; I expect respect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to talk back - to reply rudely or defiantly
