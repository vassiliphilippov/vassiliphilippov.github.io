to give it a whirl

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ɪt ə wɜrl / (AmE), / ɡɪv ɪt ə wɜːl / (BrE)
to attempt to do something, especially for the first time or to see if it works
try something out

Examples

I've never tried sushi before, but I think I'll give it a whirl.
If you're not sure about yoga, just give it a whirl and see how you like it.
They decided to give it a whirl despite having doubts about the new software.

Synset Examples

let's give it a whirl!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: try - make an attempt or effort to do something
  • Synonym: give it a go - to try something
  • Antonym: give it a miss - decide not to try or take part in something
