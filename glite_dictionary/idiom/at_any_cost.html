at any cost

1. ADV

/ ət ˈɛni kɑst / (AmE), / ət ˈɛni kɒst / (BrE)
regardless of the difficulty, risk, or expense; no matter what must be done or sacrificed
regardless of any expense or sacrifice

Examples

She was determined to win the competition at any cost.
They promised to protect their children at any cost.
The company aimed to succeed at any cost, even if it meant taking great risks.

Synset Examples

he wanted to save her life at all cost

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at all costs - regardless of what must be given up or done
