to be dynamite

1. VERB

/ bi ˈdaɪnəˌmaɪt / (AmE), / bi ˈdaɪnəmaɪt / (BrE)
to be excellent, outstanding, or very impressive
to be excellent or impressive
informal

Examples

Her performance in the play was dynamite and received a standing ovation.
This new phone is dynamite—I've never used anything so fast!
The coach said our teamwork was dynamite in the second half of the game.

Synset Examples

The new recipe is dynamite, everyone loved it!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be amazing - to be very impressive or surprising
  • Antonym: be awful - to be very bad or unpleasant
