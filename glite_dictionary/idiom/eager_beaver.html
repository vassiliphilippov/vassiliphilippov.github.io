eager beaver

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːɡɚ ˈbiːvɚ / (AmE), / ˈiːɡə ˈbiːvə / (BrE)
a person who is extremely enthusiastic and hardworking, often more so than others
enthusiastic, hardworking person
informal

Examples

The new intern is an eager beaver and volunteers for every project.
My manager appreciates eager beavers who take initiative and work hard.
Don't be such an eager beaver; it's okay to relax sometimes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go-getter - an energetic and ambitious person who is determined to succeed
  • Antonym: slacker - a person who is lazy or avoids work and responsibility
