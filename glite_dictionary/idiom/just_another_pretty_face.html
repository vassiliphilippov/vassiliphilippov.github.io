just another pretty face

1. NOUN

/ dʒʌst əˈnʌðɚ ˈprɪti feɪs / (AmE), / dʒʌst əˈnʌðə ˈprɪti feɪs / (BrE)
A person who is attractive but considered to have little substance, intelligence, or talent beyond physical appearance.
attractive but lacking substance

Examples

She refuses to be seen as just another pretty face at her new job.
Although he started as just another pretty face in Hollywood, he soon proved his acting talent.
Journalists often ask if she's just another pretty face, but her business achievements speak for themselves.

Synset Examples

He’s not just another pretty face; he has talent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: superficial - concerned only with appearance or outward qualities
