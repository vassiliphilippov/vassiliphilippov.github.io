big enchilada

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ɛn.tʃɪˈlɑː.də / (AmE), / bɪɡ ɛn.tʃɪˈlɑː.də / (BrE)
an important and influential person, especially one with power in a particular field or organization
important or influential person
informal american

Examples

After the promotion, everyone started treating him like the big enchilada in the office.
Don't mess with her—she's the big enchilada when it comes to funding decisions.
At the gala, all the big enchiladas from the industry showed up.

Synset Examples

he thinks he's a big shot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: big shot - an important or influential person
  • Synonym: big cheese - a person of importance or power
  • Synonym: big fish - an important or influential person

Etymology

From American English slang; 'enchilada' borrowed from Spanish, used humorously to denote something significant or noteworthy.
