that's saying something

1. X

/ ðæts ˈseɪɪŋ ˈsʌmθɪŋ / (AmE), / ðæts ˈseɪɪŋ ˈsʌmθɪŋ / (BrE)
used to emphasize that what has just been mentioned is especially significant or impressive
expression of significance or impressiveness

Examples

Finishing the marathon in under three hours at her age—that's saying something.
If he thought the movie was boring, that's saying something, because he usually enjoys everything.
For him to apologize, that's saying something, since he rarely admits he's wrong.

Synset Examples

Winning the award is impressive; that's saying something.
