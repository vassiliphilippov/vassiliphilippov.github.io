dumb as a post

1. ADJ

/ dʌm æz ə poʊst / (AmE), / dʌm æz ə pəʊst / (BrE)
Extremely stupid or unresponsive; completely lacking in intelligence or reaction.
very stupid or unresponsive (idiom)
informal

Examples

He sat there looking dumb as a post, not understanding a word we said.
After forgetting her keys for the third time this week, she felt dumb as a post.
No matter how many times I explain it, he just stays dumb as a post.

Synset Examples

She just stood there, dumb as a post.
His answers were as dumb as a post.
He feels dumb as a post after that comment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dumb as a rock - extremely stupid (idiom)
  • Degree Lower: dumb - lacking intelligence; stupid
