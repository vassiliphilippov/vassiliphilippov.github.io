to give the game away

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ðə ɡeɪm əˌweɪ / (AmE), / ɡɪv ðə ɡeɪm əˌweɪ / (BrE)
to reveal a secret or disclose a plan, often unintentionally
reveal a secret or plan

Examples

Try not to give the game away before the big reveal at the party.
She almost gave the game away when she started talking about the surprise.
If you keep dropping hints, you'll give the game away and ruin the plan.

Synset Examples

He accidentally gave the game away with his smile.
Don't tell her the surprise; you'll give the game away.
The hint he dropped might have given the game away.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to let the cat out of the bag - to reveal a secret unintentionally
