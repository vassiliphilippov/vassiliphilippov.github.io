to lighten one's purse

1. VERB

/ ˈlaɪtn̩ wʌnz pɜrs / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtn wʌnz pɜːs / (BrE)
to reduce one's funds by spending money, especially noticeably or heavily
spend money; reduce funds

Examples

That five-star hotel stay really lightened my purse.
The holiday shopping spree quickly lightened her purse.
A series of unexpected repairs can lighten one's purse quite a bit.

Synset Examples

The expensive dinner really lightened my purse.
Buying that car will definitely lighten someone's purse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spend money - to use money to buy things or pay for services
