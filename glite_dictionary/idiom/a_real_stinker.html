a real stinker

1. NOUN

/ ə ˈɹiːəl ˈstɪŋkər / (AmE), / ə ˈɹiːəl ˈstɪŋkə / (BrE)
something that is very bad or unpleasant
something very bad or unpleasant
informal

Examples

Last night's play was a real stinker and the audience left early.
The new video game turned out to be a real stinker, disappointing all the fans.
He tried his best, but that assignment was a real stinker to finish.

Synset Examples

That movie was a real stinker; I regret watching it.

Related Terms
