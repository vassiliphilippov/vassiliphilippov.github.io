stark naked

1. ADJ

/ stɑrk ˈneɪkɪd / (AmE), / stɑːk ˈneɪkɪd / (BrE)
completely unclothed; having no clothing on at all
completely unclothed
informal

Examples

The toddler ran out of the bathroom stark naked, laughing as he escaped his mother.
She was caught stark naked when the fire alarm suddenly went off.
After jumping into the lake stark naked, they quickly realized how cold the water was.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naked - having no clothes on
  • Synonym: bare - not clothed or covered

Etymology

From 'stark' meaning 'utter' or 'complete' (from Old English 'stearc') joined with 'naked'.
