to get bent

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt bɛnt / (AmE), / ɡɛt bɛnt / (BrE)
(informal, rude) Used to tell someone to go away or to express strong dismissal or insult towards them.
rude way to tell someone to go away
informal

Examples

She was so angry she shouted, "Get bent!" before storming out of the room.
If you're going to be that rude, you can just get bent.
After I criticized his idea, he told me to get bent.

Synset Examples

She was annoyed and told him to get bent.
He told me to get bent after my suggestion.
I couldn't believe he told me to get bent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get lost - used to rudely tell someone to go away
  • Synonym: to go away - to leave or depart
