gone off the face of the earth

1. X

/ ɡɔn ˌɔf ðə ˈfeɪs əv ði ˈɜrθ / (AmE), / ɡɒn ˌɒf ðə ˈfeɪs əv ði ˈɜːθ / (BrE)
to disappear completely or vanish entirely, with no trace or information left
to disappear completely
