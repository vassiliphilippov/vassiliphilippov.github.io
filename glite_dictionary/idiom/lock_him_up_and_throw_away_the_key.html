to lock him up and throw away the key

1. VERB

/ lɑk ɪm ʌp ənd θroʊ əˈweɪ ðə ki / (AmE), / lɒk ɪm ʌp ənd θrəʊ əˈweɪ ðə kiː / (BrE)
to imprison someone and ensure they will never be released; to keep someone in prison indefinitely as punishment
imprison someone permanently

Examples

After the scandal, many people wanted to lock him up and throw away the key.
They said the crime was so serious that the judge should lock him up and throw away the key.
Some argue that for repeat offenders, society should just lock him up and throw away the key.

Synset Examples

After his crime spree, they wanted to lock him up and throw away the key.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to imprison - to put someone in prison
