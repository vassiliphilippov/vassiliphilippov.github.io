to itch to travel

1. VERB

/ ɪtʃ tə ˈtrævəl / (AmE), / ɪtʃ tə ˈtrævəl / (BrE)
to have a strong and restless desire to travel or go to new places.
strong desire to travel

Examples

After months at home, I really itch to travel and see new places.
Every spring, she itches to travel somewhere she's never been before.
He said he was itching to travel after finishing his university exams.

Synset Examples

I have an itch to travel somewhere new.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wanderlust - a strong desire to travel and explore the world

Etymology

From the noun 'itch' (an urge or strong desire) and the infinitive 'to travel.'
