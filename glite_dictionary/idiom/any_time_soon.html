any time soon

1. ADV

/ ˈɛni taɪm sun / (AmE), / ˈɛni taɪm suːn / (BrE)
at any time in the near future; soon (usually used in negative statements)
in the near future (usually negative)

Examples

I don't think the repairs will be finished any time soon.
He's not planning to move out any time soon.
If you're expecting an answer any time soon, you'll be disappointed.

Synset Examples

We won't see them any time soon.
He won't be back any time soon.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: soon - in or after a short time
  • Degree Higher: imminently - happening very soon; at any moment
