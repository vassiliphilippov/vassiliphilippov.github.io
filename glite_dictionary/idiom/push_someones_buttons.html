to push someone's buttons

1. VERB

/ pʊʃ ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈbʌtənz / (AmE), / pʊʃ ˈsʌmwənz ˈbʌtənz / (BrE)
to deliberately provoke a strong emotional reaction, especially irritation or anger, in someone
provoke a strong emotional reaction

Examples

She's very good at pushing his buttons when she wants to get a reaction.
Don’t let them push your buttons during the meeting, just stay calm.
He knew exactly how to push my buttons and make me angry.

Synset Examples

That shitbird really knows how to push buttons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to provoke - to cause a reaction, often anger or annoyance
