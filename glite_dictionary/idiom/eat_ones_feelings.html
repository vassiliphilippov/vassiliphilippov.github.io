to eat one's feelings

1. VERB

/ it wʌnz ˈfiːlɪŋz / (AmE), / iːt wʌnz ˈfiːlɪŋz / (BrE)
(informal) To consume food as a way of coping with negative emotions such as sadness, stress, or frustration.
consume food to cope with emotions
informal

Examples

Whenever she was stressed, she would eat her feelings with a pint of ice cream.
After the breakup, he found himself eating his feelings late at night.
Some people eat their feelings instead of talking about what's bothering them.

Synset Examples

Eating her feelings led to gaining extra weight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comfort eating - the act of eating to make yourself feel better emotionally
