lost and at sea

1. ADJ

/ lɔst ənd æt si / (AmE), / lɒst ənd ət siː / (BrE)
Feeling confused and unsure what to do or where to go.
confused and uncertain

Examples

She was lost and at sea during her first week at university.
After reading the instructions, he still felt lost and at sea.
Whenever I try to follow that conversation, I'm completely lost and at sea.

Synset Examples

He felt lost and at sea in the new project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uncertain - not sure or confident about something
  • Antonym: confident - certain about one's abilities or course
