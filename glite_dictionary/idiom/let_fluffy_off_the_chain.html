to let fluffy off the chain

1. VERB

/ lɛt ˈflʌfi ɑf ðə tʃeɪn / (AmE), / lɛt ˈflʌfi ɒf ðə tʃeɪn / (BrE)
To allow someone to be free or to act without restrictions, often after a period of restraint or limitation.
allow someone to act freely
informal

Examples

Whenever the project is done, the team leader likes to let fluffy off the chain and celebrate.
After a stressful week, it's good to let fluffy off the chain and relax with friends.
At the party, she finally let fluffy off the chain and danced all night.

Synset Examples

It's time to let fluffy off the chain and enjoy the sunshine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: let off the leash - to allow someone or something to act freely
