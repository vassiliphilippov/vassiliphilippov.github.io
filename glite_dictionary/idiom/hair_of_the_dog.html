hair of the dog

1. NOUN

/ hɛr əv ðə dɔg / (AmE), / heə əv ðə dɒg / (BrE)
An alcoholic drink taken to relieve the effects of a hangover.
alcoholic drink for hangover relief

Examples

Some people believe that a hair of the dog is the best way to recover after a heavy night of drinking.
He poured himself a Bloody Mary as his hair of the dog this morning.
Despite the popular advice, I find that a hair of the dog never really helps my hangover.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hangover cure - a remedy for a hangover

Etymology

Shortened from 'the hair of the dog that bit you,' referring to an old folk remedy.

2. NOUN

/ hɛɚ əv ðə dɔg / (AmE), / heə əv ðə dɒg / (BrE)
an alcoholic drink taken to relieve a hangover, typically one consumed in the morning after heavy drinking
alcoholic drink to relieve hangover

Examples

After a night of partying, he claimed a hair of the dog was the only cure for his headache.
Some believe that drinking a hair of the dog delays the effects of a hangover.
She poured herself a Bloody Mary as a hair of the dog before breakfast.

Synset Examples

He ordered a beer as the hair of the dog.
A shot of whiskey is my hair of the dog remedy.
They say the hair of the dog helps with hangovers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hangover cure - a remedy for the after-effects of drinking alcohol

Etymology

From the longer phrase 'hair of the dog that bit you', referring to the folk remedy of treating a rabid dog bite with hair from the dog itself.
