for grins

1. ADV

/ fər ɡrɪnz / (AmE), / fə ɡrɪnz / (BrE)
Used to indicate that something is done for fun or enjoyment, not for any serious reason.
for fun or enjoyment
informal

Examples

They went to the amusement park just for grins.
Sometimes I wear mismatched socks for grins.
She joined the dance class for grins, not to compete.

Synset Examples

He tried skydiving for grins last summer.
We took silly photos just for grins.
They played board games for grins on Friday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for kicks - for fun or excitement
  • Synonym: for laughs - for amusement or entertainment
