ray of sunshine

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪ əv ˈsʌnʃaɪn / (AmE), / ˈreɪ əv ˈsʌnʃaɪn / (BrE)
a person or thing that brings happiness or cheerfulness to others
person/thing that brings happiness

Examples

Her cheerful attitude is a real ray of sunshine during difficult days.
The teacher described him as a ray of sunshine in the classroom.
After his operation, his granddaughter became the ray of sunshine in his life.

Synset Examples

He always brings a ray of sunshine to meetings.
Her laughter is like a ray of sunshine.
The puppy is a ray of sunshine in my life.
