sound as a bell

1. ADJ

/ saʊnd əz ə bɛl / (AmE), / saʊnd əz ə bɛl / (BrE)
in perfect condition; reliable or functioning well
in perfect condition or reliable

Examples

After the mechanic's inspection, the engine was sound as a bell and ready for the road.
You can trust this secondhand laptop; it's still sound as a bell.
Even after years of use, the bridge remains sound as a bell.

Synset Examples

The old clock runs sound as a bell after repairs.
He assured me his car was sound as a bell.
Her explanation was sound as a bell and clear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in good shape - in a healthy or satisfactory condition
  • Antonym: to run down - in poor or deteriorated condition

Etymology

Refers to the clear, reliable tone of a well-made bell.
