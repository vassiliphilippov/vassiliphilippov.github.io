like a kid on a rollercoaster

1. X

/ laɪk ə kɪd ɑn ə ˈroʊlərˌkoʊstər / (AmE), / laɪk ə kɪd ɒn ə ˈrəʊləˌkəʊstə / (BrE)
extremely excited or joyful, especially in a way that is obvious and enthusiastic
extremely excited or joyful

Examples

When the band finally took the stage, she was like a kid on a rollercoaster, cheering and jumping with excitement.
He opened the present and reacted like a kid on a rollercoaster, shouting with pure joy.
After getting her dream job, Mia felt like a kid on a rollercoaster for days.

Synset Examples
