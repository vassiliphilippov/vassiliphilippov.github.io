to choose violence over silence

1. VERB

/ tʃuz ˈvaɪələns oʊvɚ ˈsaɪləns / (AmE), / tʃuːz ˈvaɪələns ˈəʊvə ˈsaɪləns / (BrE)
to opt for an aggressive or confrontational response instead of remaining passive or silent, often used figuratively to describe assertive action or outspoken behavior
to favor confrontation over passivity

Examples

In heated discussions, she often chooses violence over silence to make her point clearly.
He decided to choose violence over silence when addressing the unfair policy at work.
Sometimes it's necessary to choose violence over silence and confront the issues head-on.

Synset Examples

In this debate, I choose violence over silence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to take action - to do something rather than remaining inactive
  • Antonym: hold one's peace - to remain silent or passive
