as far as possible

1. ADV

/ əz fɑr əz ˈpɑsəbəl / (AmE), / əz fɑː əz ˈpɒsəbl / (BrE)
to the extent that is feasible or achievable; as much as can be done.
to the feasible or possible extent

Examples

We tried to maintain normal routines as far as possible despite the changes.
Please avoid making noise as far as possible during the ceremony.
The company aims to reduce waste as far as possible for environmental reasons.

Synset Examples

she helped him as much as possible

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as much as possible - to the greatest feasible degree or amount
  • Synonym: as far as practicable - to the extent that can be done in practice
