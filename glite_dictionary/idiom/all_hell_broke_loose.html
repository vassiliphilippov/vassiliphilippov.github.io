all hell broke loose

1. X

/ ɔl hɛl broʊk lus / (AmE), / ɔːl hɛl brəʊk luːs / (BrE)
used to describe a situation in which sudden chaos, confusion, or disorder erupts
sudden chaos or disorder begins

Examples

As soon as the announcement was made, all hell broke loose in the meeting room.
When the fire alarm rang, all hell broke loose and people ran in every direction.
The referee made a controversial call and suddenly all hell broke loose on the field.

Synset Examples

When the power went out, all hell broke loose.
The moment she realized she forgot the tickets, all hell broke loose.
