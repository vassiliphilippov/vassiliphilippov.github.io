the other day

1. ADV

/ ði ˈʌðɚ deɪ / (AmE), / ði ˈʌðə deɪ / (BrE)
a few days ago; at some recent time in the past
a few days ago

Examples

I ran into my old teacher the other day at the grocery store.
Didn’t you say you lost your keys the other day?
She called me the other day to catch up.

Synset Examples

She mentioned a great restaurant the other day.
