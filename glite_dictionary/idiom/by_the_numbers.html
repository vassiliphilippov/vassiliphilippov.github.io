by the numbers

1. ADV

/ baɪ ðə ˈnʌmbɚz / (AmE), / baɪ ðə ˈnʌmbəz / (BrE)
according to a prescribed set of rules or steps; in a precise, methodical way
according to prescribed procedure

Examples

The auditor reviewed the finances by the numbers to ensure compliance with regulations.
She ran the laboratory test by the numbers, not improvising at any step.
All new employees are trained to handle emergencies by the numbers before they can take charge.

Synset Examples

They planned the event by the numbers for maximum efficiency.
He followed the recipe by the numbers to avoid mistakes.
The project was completed by the numbers, ensuring accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: by the book - strictly according to the rules or official procedures
