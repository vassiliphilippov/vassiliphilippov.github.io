hidden in plain sight

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɪdən ɪn pleɪn saɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɪdən ɪn pleɪn saɪt / (BrE)
easily seen or available but not recognized or noticed, often because it is so obvious.
obvious but not noticed

Examples

The answer was hidden in plain sight throughout the investigation, but no one realized it.
The thief stood hidden in plain sight among the crowd, unnoticed by the police.
Her true feelings were hidden in plain sight, visible to anyone who paid close attention.

Synset Examples

Some argue that crypto-fascism is hidden in plain sight.
Her talent was hidden in plain sight until she performed at the event.
Sometimes, the best solutions are hidden in plain sight.
