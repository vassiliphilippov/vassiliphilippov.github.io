this is why we can't have nice things

1. X

/ ðɪs ɪz waɪ wi kænt hæv naɪs θɪŋz / (AmE), / ðɪs ɪz waɪ wi kɑːnt hæv naɪs θɪŋz / (BrE)
used to express frustration that careless, destructive, or thoughtless behavior has spoiled something or prevented improvement
expression of frustration at ruined opportunities

Examples

After the kids spilled juice on the new sofa, Mom shook her head and said, this is why we can't have nice things.
Every time someone breaks the office coffee maker, someone inevitably quips, this is why we can't have nice things.
He scratched the car again, and his sister groaned, 'This is why we can't have nice things.'

Synset Examples

She laughed and said, 'This is why we can't have nice things!'
