to have Jesus in one's heart

1. VERB

/ hæv ˈdʒiːzəs ɪn wʌnz hɑrt / (AmE), / hæv ˈdʒiːzəs ɪn wʌnz hɑːt / (BrE)
to embrace Christian faith or belief, particularly as a source of inner peace and moral guidance
embrace Christian faith for inner peace

Examples

She believes that to truly have Jesus in your heart is the path to lasting happiness.
Many parents hope their children will have Jesus in their hearts as they grow up.
His words inspired others to seek to have Jesus in their hearts during times of trouble.

Synset Examples

She always says to have Jesus in your heart for peace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: find salvation - be saved or find deliverance in a religious sense
