in the spotlight

1. ADP

/ ɪn ðə ˈspɑːtˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈspɒtˌlaɪt / (BrE)
Receiving public attention or being the focus of interest.
receiving public attention

Examples

After the scandal, the politician was suddenly in the spotlight again.
The small business found itself in the spotlight after winning a national award.
The breakthrough performance put the young artist in the spotlight.

Synset Examples

The new exhibit is in the spotlight this week.
The charity event put the cause in the spotlight.
She loves being in the spotlight during performances.
The actor was finally in the spotlight at the awards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the limelight - at the center of public attention
  • Antonym: out of the spotlight - not receiving public attention
