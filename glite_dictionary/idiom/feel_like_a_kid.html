to feel like a kid

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə kɪd / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə kɪd / (BrE)
to experience a sense of carefree joy and innocence, as one does in childhood
feel carefree and joyful, like a child
informal

Examples

Every time I go to the amusement park, I feel like a kid again.
Baking cookies with my grandmother makes me feel like a kid.
Winning the game last night made the entire team feel like a kid.

Synset Examples

Seeing the puppy made him feel like a kid on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feel young again - to feel as if you are young once more
