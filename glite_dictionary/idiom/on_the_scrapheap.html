on the scrapheap

1. X

/ ɑn ðə ˈskræpˌhip / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ˈskrapˌhiːp / (BrE)
no longer wanted, useful, or considered valuable; discarded or rejected as obsolete or unimportant
considered discarded or obsolete

Examples

After the restructuring, many senior employees feared they would end up on the scrapheap.
The filmmaker brought back an old genre that many thought was on the scrapheap.
His innovative ideas kept him relevant in a field where so many land on the scrapheap.

Synset Examples

He felt like his dreams were on the scrapheap after the failure.
The company's outdated technology was thrown on the scrapheap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obsolete - no longer produced or used; out of date
  • Synonym: redundant - no longer needed or useful; superfluous
  • Antonym: in demand - wanted or sought after; popular or necessary
