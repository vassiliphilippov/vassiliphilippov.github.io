to feel like a million bucks

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə ˈmɪljən bʌks / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈmɪljən bʌks / (BrE)
to feel extremely good, healthy, or happy
to feel extremely good or well
informal american

Examples

After her morning workout, she felt like a million bucks and was ready to start the day.
Getting a full night's sleep made me feel like a million bucks the next morning.
He always feels like a million bucks after a relaxing vacation.

Synset Examples
