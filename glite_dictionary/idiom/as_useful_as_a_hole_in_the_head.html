as useful as a hole in the head

1. X

/ æz ˈjusfəl æz ə hoʊl ɪn ðə hɛd / (AmE), / æz ˈjuːsfʊl æz ə həʊl ɪn ðə hɛd / (BrE)
utterly useless; not helpful or wanted in any way
completely useless or unwanted

Examples

Another meeting about the same topic would be as useful as a hole in the head.
Buying a snow shovel in the desert is as useful as a hole in the head.
He said a new law like that would be as useful as a hole in the head.

Synset Examples

That idea is about as useful as a hole in the head.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: useless - not useful; having no benefit or value
