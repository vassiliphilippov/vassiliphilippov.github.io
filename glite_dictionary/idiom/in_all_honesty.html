in all honesty

1. ADV

/ ɪn ɔl ˈɑnəsti / (AmE), / ɪn ɔːl ˈɒnɪsti / (BrE)
used to emphasize that one is speaking truthfully and sincerely
emphasizing truthfulness or sincerity

Examples

In all honesty, I think your plan could use some improvement.
In all honesty, she wasn't prepared for the exam at all.
It's not my favorite restaurant, in all honesty, but the dessert is delicious.

Synset Examples

In all honesty, I didn't enjoy the movie.
He said, in all honesty, he preferred tea over coffee.
In all honesty, I think we need a new approach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honestly - in a truthful or sincere manner
