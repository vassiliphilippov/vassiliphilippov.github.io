hot and heavy

1. ADJ

/ hɑt ənd ˈhɛvi / (AmE), / hɒt ənd ˈhɛvi / (BrE)
(of a situation or relationship) very intense, serious, or passionate, especially in a romantic or sexual sense.
intense or passionate, esp. romantically

Examples

The discussion about politics quickly turned hot and heavy as everyone voiced their opinions.
Their romance got hot and heavy within just a few weeks of meeting.
Rumors spread that things became hot and heavy at the after-party last night.

Synset Examples

The debate became hot and heavy as tensions rose.
Things got hot and heavy between them quickly.
They were getting hot and heavy at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intense - showing strong feeling or extreme degree
