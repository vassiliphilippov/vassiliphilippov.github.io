tight as Dick's hatband

1. ADJ

/ taɪt əz dɪks ˈhætˌbænd / (AmE), / taɪt əz dɪks ˈhætˌbænd / (BrE)
(informal) Extremely tight or restricted, especially referring to situations involving nervousness or financial constraints.
extremely tight or restricted (idiom)
informal

Examples

After losing his job, money was tight as Dick's hatband for several months.
She laughed nervously, saying she was tight as Dick's hatband waiting for her test results.
With so many unexpected expenses, their budget became tight as Dick's hatband this year.

Synset Examples

She felt tight as Dick's hatband before the exam.

The budget is tight as Dick's hatband this quarter.

Etymology

Possible 19th-century American or British origin; reference to an impossibly tight or uncomfortable hatband. Exact origin unclear.
