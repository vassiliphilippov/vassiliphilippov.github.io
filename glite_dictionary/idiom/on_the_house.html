on the house

1. ADV

/ ɑn ðə haʊs / (AmE), / ɒn ðə haʊs / (BrE)
provided free of charge by the owner or establishment, especially in restaurants or bars
provided free of charge

Examples

The manager brought us a bottle of wine on the house to celebrate our anniversary.
After waiting so long for our meals, the desserts were on the house.
Our coffees were on the house because it was our first visit.

Synset Examples

Your first drink is on the house tonight!
The dessert was on the house as a thank you.
They offered us appetizers on the house with our meal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: free - given without payment
