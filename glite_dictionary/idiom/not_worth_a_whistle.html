not worth a whistle

1. ADJ

/ nɑt wɜrθ ə ˈwɪsəl / (AmE), / nɒt wɜːθ ə ˈwɪsl / (BrE)
Of no value or importance; not worth considering or obtaining.
of no value or importance

Examples

He realized his old laptop was not worth a whistle compared to modern models.
The antique vase looked nice, but an expert said it was not worth a whistle.
After reading the negative reviews, she decided the book was not worth a whistle.

Synset Examples

This project is not worth a whistle anymore.
That restaurant is not worth a whistle, trust me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worthless - having no value; useless
  • Antonym: valuable - having great value or importance
