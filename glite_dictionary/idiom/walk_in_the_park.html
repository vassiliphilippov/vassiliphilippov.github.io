walk in the park

1. NOUN

/ wɑk ɪn ðə pɑrk / (AmE), / wɔːk ɪn ðə pɑːk / (BrE)
something that is very easy to do; a task or activity that requires little effort
an easy or simple task

Examples

Finishing the exam was a walk in the park for her, since she had studied thoroughly.
Compared to last year's marathon, this 5K run is a walk in the park.
Don't worry about the interview—public speaking is a walk in the park for you.

Synset Examples

For him, coding is a walk in the park.
Completing the assignment was a walk in the park.
The project was a walk in the park for her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piece of cake - an easy task or activity
  • Synonym: breeze - something easy to do
  • Synonym: cakewalk - something very easy to achieve
  • Antonym: challenge - something difficult to do or requiring effort
