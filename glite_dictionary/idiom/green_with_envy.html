green with envy

1. ADJ

/ ɡriːn wɪð ˈɛnvi / (AmE), / ɡriːn wɪð ˈɛnvi / (BrE)
extremely envious or jealous of someone else's success, possessions, or qualities
extremely envious or jealous

Examples

She was green with envy when she saw her friend's new sports car.
He tried to hide it, but he was green with envy over his colleague's award.
Whenever her sister travels abroad, she feels green with envy.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

Etymology

Refers to the color green's long association with jealousy and envy, dating back to Shakespeare.
