call of nature

1. NOUN

/ kɔl əv ˈneɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / kɔːl əv ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
a polite or humorous way of referring to the need to urinate or defecate
need to urinate or defecate (polite)
informal

Examples

He left the room discreetly to answer the call of nature.
During the long bus ride, several passengers had to stop for the call of nature.
She excused herself from the table, apologizing for the sudden call of nature.

Synset Examples
