oh my days

1. INTJ

/ oʊ maɪ deɪz / (AmE), / əʊ maɪ deɪz / (BrE)
used to express surprise, shock, or excitement
expression of surprise or excitement
informal british

Examples

Oh my days, I can't believe you actually did it!
When she walked in wearing that dress, I just thought, oh my days.
Oh my days, this is the best birthday surprise ever!

Synset Examples
