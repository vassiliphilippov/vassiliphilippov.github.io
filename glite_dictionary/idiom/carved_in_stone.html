carved in stone

1. ADJ

/ kɑrvd ɪn stoʊn / (AmE), / kɑːvd ɪn stəʊn / (BrE)
(of a decision, rule, or arrangement) fixed and unchangeable; not able to be altered.
permanently fixed; unchangeable

Examples

The terms of the contract are not carved in stone and may be negotiated.
Remember, our weekend plans aren't carved in stone if something comes up.
His opinion is strong, but it's not carved in stone and he might reconsider.

Synset Examples

the agreement is not yet set in stone

Related Terms

  • Synonym: set in stone - (of a plan or decision) fixed and unchangeable
  • Antonym: subject to change - liable to be altered or modified
