off one's nut

1. ADJ

/ ɑf wʌnz nʌt / (AmE), / ɒf wʌnz nʌt / (BrE)
behaving in a crazy or irrational way; mentally unstable
crazy or irrational (idiom)
informal

Examples

I thought he was off his nut for jumping into the freezing lake.
She went completely off her nut when she heard the news.
After staying up for three days straight, he was acting off his nut.

Synset Examples

She's been acting off her nut since the breakup.
He was off his nut after staying up all night.
I thought he was off his nut for trying that.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: off one's rocker - behaving in a crazy or insane way (idiom)
  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged, insane, or wildly foolish
  • Antonym: sane - having a healthy mind, not crazy or irrational
