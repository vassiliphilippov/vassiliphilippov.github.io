once in a while

1. ADV

/ wʌns ɪn ə waɪl / (AmE), / wʌns ɪn ə waɪl / (BrE)
from time to time; occasionally but not often.
occasionally; not often

Examples

Once in a while, I treat myself to a fancy dinner at a nice restaurant.
We only see our cousins once in a while since they live abroad.
It's important to take a break once in a while to recharge your energy.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occasionally - at infrequent or irregular intervals; now and then
