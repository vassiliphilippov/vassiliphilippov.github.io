to enjoy the journey

1. VERB

/ ɪnˈdʒɔɪ ðə ˈdʒɜrn.i / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɔɪ ðə ˈdʒɜː.ni / (BrE)
to take pleasure in and appreciate the process or experience of life or a particular task, rather than focusing only on the end result
appreciate the process of life or work

Examples

She reminded her team to enjoy the journey during the challenging project.
It's important to enjoy the journey instead of stressing about reaching the final goal.
Many people forget to enjoy the journey while pursuing their dreams.

Synset Examples

Let's enjoy the journey and be ourselves.
