pure as the driven snow

1. ADJ

/ pjʊr æz ðə ˈdrɪvən snoʊ / (AmE), / pjʊə æz ðə ˈdrɪvən snəʊ / (BrE)
Completely innocent, untainted, and morally upright.
completely innocent or morally pure

Examples

Even after years in politics, her reputation remained pure as the driven snow.
He claimed to be pure as the driven snow, but the evidence suggested otherwise.
People trusted her advice, believing she was pure as the driven snow in her motives.

Synset Examples

Despite the chaos around him, he remained pure as the driven snow.
Her intentions were pure as the driven snow, always helping others.
He was pure as the driven snow, never telling a lie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotless - entirely clean or morally pure
  • Synonym: innocent - free from moral wrong, pure

Etymology

Alludes to the image of freshly fallen, undisturbed snow, associated with purity and cleanliness.
