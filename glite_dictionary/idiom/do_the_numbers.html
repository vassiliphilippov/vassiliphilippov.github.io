to do the numbers

1. VERB

/ du ðə ˈnʌmbɚz / (AmE), / duː ðə ˈnʌmbəz / (BrE)
to calculate or analyze financial details, especially to determine the feasibility or profitability of something
calculate or analyze financial details

Examples

Before we make any big purchases, let's do the numbers to be sure we can afford it.
The finance team will do the numbers and report back next week.
She always insists on do the numbers herself before agreeing to invest.

Synset Examples

Let's do the numbers to see if we can afford it.
I need to do the numbers before the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crunch the numbers - perform complicated or detailed calculations, especially financial ones
  • Synonym: to do the math - calculate or figure something out, often with numbers
