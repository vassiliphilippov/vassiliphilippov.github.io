behind the wheel

1. ADV

/ bɪˈhaɪnd ðə wil / (AmE), / bɪˈhaɪnd ðə wiːl / (BrE)
in control of or driving a motor vehicle.
driving a vehicle

Examples

After his accident, he was hesitant to get behind the wheel again.
Many teenagers look forward to being behind the wheel for the first time.
You should never use your phone while behind the wheel.

Synset Examples

After months of practice, he is confident behind the wheel.
She enjoys being behind the wheel during road trips.
He felt nervous behind the wheel for the first time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at the wheel - in control of the steering of a vehicle; driving
