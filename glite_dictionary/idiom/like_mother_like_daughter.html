like mother, like daughter

1. X

/ laɪk ˈmʌðɚ, laɪk ˈdɔtɚ / (AmE), / laɪk ˈmʌðə, laɪk ˈdɔːtə / (BrE)
used to say that a daughter has qualities or behaviors similar to her mother
daughter has qualities of her mother

Examples

She became a doctor just like her mom—like mother, like daughter.
They both have a passion for gardening; like mother, like daughter.
Whenever I see them laugh together, I think, 'like mother, like daughter.'

Synset Examples

Her daughter is a great cook; like mother, like daughter.
She loves painting, just like mother, like daughter.
They both have the same laugh; it's like mother, like daughter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: like father, like son - used to say a son is similar to his father
