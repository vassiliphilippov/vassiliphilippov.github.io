to push it to the limit

1. VERB

/ pʊʃ ɪt tə ðə ˈlɪmɪt / (AmE), / pʊʃ ɪt tə ðə ˈlɪmɪt / (BrE)
to use all your energy or resources, or to take risks as far as possible, often to test your abilities or endurance
go as far as possible; maximize effort or risk

Examples

She decided to push it to the limit during her final marathon run.
Sometimes entrepreneurs have to push it to the limit to achieve their dreams.
I wouldn't push it to the limit if I were you; it's a risky move.

Synset Examples

Let’s push it to the limit and see what happens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to go all out - make as much effort as possible
  • Antonym: to hold back - to not use full effort or potential
