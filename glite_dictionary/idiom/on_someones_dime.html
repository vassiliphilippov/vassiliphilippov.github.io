on someone's dime

1. ADP

/ ɑn ˈsʌmwʌnz daɪm / (AmE), / ɒn ˈsʌmwʌnz daɪm / (BrE)
at someone else's expense; paid for by someone else
at someone else's expense

Examples

He always insists on eating out on someone’s dime whenever he travels for work.
The company retreat was all expenses paid, so everyone enjoyed the weekend on the boss's dime.
They completed the entire renovation on their parents' dime.

Synset Examples

I wouldn’t go to the concert on someone’s dime.
She stayed at the hotel on her parents' dime.
They traveled to Paris on someone’s dime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on someone else's nickel - at someone else's expense (American informal synonym)
