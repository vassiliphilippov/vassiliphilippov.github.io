to fix the roof while the sun is shining

1. VERB

/ fɪks ðə ruːf waɪl ðə sʌn ɪz ˈʃaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / fɪks ðə ruːf waɪl ðə sʌn ɪz ˈʃaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
to take action or make necessary preparations while conditions are favorable
act while conditions are favorable

Examples

You should fix the roof while the sun is shining instead of waiting for a crisis to act.
Businesses must fix the roof while the sun is shining by investing during prosperous times.
It’s wise to fix the roof while the sun is shining rather than waiting for problems to appear.

Synset Examples

Let's fix the roof while the sun is shining today.
He believes in fixing the roof while the sun is shining.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to make hay while the sun shines - to take advantage of favorable conditions; to act while an opportunity exists
