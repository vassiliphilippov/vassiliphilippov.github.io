to beat the stuffing out of

1. VERB

/ bit ðə ˈstʌfɪŋ aʊt ʌv / (AmE), / biːt ðə ˈstʌfɪŋ aʊt ɒv / (BrE)
to physically overpower or defeat someone with great force, often in a fight
severely defeat or overpower
informal

Examples

The bully threatened to beat the stuffing out of anyone who crossed him.
If you cheat, my brother will beat the stuffing out of you.
The underdog team managed to beat the stuffing out of their much-favored rivals.

Synset Examples

The boxer was ready to beat the stuffing out of his rival.
He felt he could beat the stuffing out of anyone who challenged him.
She was determined to beat the stuffing out of her fears.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to beat up - to attack and injure someone by hitting them repeatedly
  • Synonym: thrash - to defeat someone thoroughly, especially in a fight or competition
