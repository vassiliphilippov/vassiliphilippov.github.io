to sit one's ass down

1. VERB

/ sɪt wʌnz æs daʊn / (AmE), / sɪt wʌnz ɑːs daʊn / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) To take a seat, usually used emphatically to tell someone to sit down or calm down.
sit down or calm down (vulgar, emphatic)
informal vulgar american
