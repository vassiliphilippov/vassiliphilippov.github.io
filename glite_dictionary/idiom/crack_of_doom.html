crack of doom

1. NOUN

/ kræk əv duːm / (AmE), / kræk əv duːm / (BrE)
the last Judgment; the day at the end of the world when all people are judged by God
Judgment Day; end of the world

Examples

According to legend, the trumpets will sound at the crack of doom.
The prophecy claims that the world will be remade after the crack of doom.
Some poets have described eternal love lasting until the crack of doom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: judgment day - the day of God's final judgment of humanity
  • Synonym: doomsday - the last day of the world's existence; Judgment Day

Etymology

Popularized by Shakespeare in Macbeth; 'crack' refers to a loud sound, such as a trumpet signaling the end of time.
