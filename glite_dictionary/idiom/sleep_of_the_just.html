sleep of the just

1. NOUN

/ sliːp əv ðə dʒʌst / (AmE), / sliːp əv ðə dʒʌst / (BrE)
a peaceful and undisturbed sleep, especially that which is thought to be deserved by a person who has lived a good life
peaceful, undisturbed sleep (deserved by the just)

Examples

After volunteering all afternoon, she enjoyed the sleep of the just that night.
His conscience was clear, so he drifted into the sleep of the just.
Finally at peace, the old man settled into the sleep of the just.

Synset Examples

He cherished the rare moments of the sleep of the just.

The kids were finally settled and in the sleep of the just.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sound sleep - deep undisturbed sleep
