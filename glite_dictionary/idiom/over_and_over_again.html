over and over again

1. ADV

/ ˈoʊvər ənd ˈoʊvər əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / ˈəʊvə ənd ˈəʊvə əˈɡɛn / (BrE)
repeatedly; many times, especially in the same way
repeatedly; many times

Examples

She listened to the same song over and over again until she memorized the lyrics.
The teacher explained the solution over and over again to the confused students.
I have read that book over and over again, and it never gets old.

Synset Examples

They told him the answer over and over again.
She practiced the song over and over again until perfect.
He watched that movie over and over again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repeatedly - again and again; many times
  • Antonym: once - one single time; not repeated
