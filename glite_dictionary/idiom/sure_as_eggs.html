sure as eggs

1. ADV

/ ʃʊr əz ɛɡz / (AmE), / ʃɔːr əz ɛɡz / (BrE)
Certainly; without a doubt; used to emphasize that something will definitely happen or is unequivocally true.
without a doubt; certainly

Examples

He'll be there first thing in the morning, sure as eggs.
You can count on her support, sure as eggs.
It's going to rain later, sure as eggs.

Synset Examples

He will be late, sure as eggs.
She'll forget the meeting, sure as eggs is eggs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certainly - without any doubt; surely
  • Synonym: undoubtedly - without doubt; surely
  • Antonym: possibly - perhaps; with a chance of happening

Etymology

Likely a shortening of the idiom 'sure as eggs is eggs,' a humorous alteration of 'as sure as X equals X.'
