wink of an eye

1. NOUN

/ wɪŋk əv ən aɪ / (AmE), / wɪŋk əv ən aɪ / (BrE)
in an extremely short time; very quickly
very quickly; in no time

Examples

The news spread across town in the wink of an eye.
She packed her bags in the wink of an eye and rushed to the airport.
Trouble can start in the wink of an eye if you're not careful.

Synset Examples

He finished the task in the wink of an eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in a flash - very quickly; immediately
  • Synonym: in no time - very quickly; almost immediately
  • Synonym: in a heartbeat - very quickly; without hesitation
