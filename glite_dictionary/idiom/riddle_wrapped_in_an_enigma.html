riddle wrapped in an enigma

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪdəl ræpt ɪn ən ɪˈnɪɡmə / (AmE), / ˈrɪdəl ræpt ɪn ən ɪˈnɪɡmə / (BrE)
A person or thing that is very mysterious or difficult to understand.
something very mysterious or puzzling

Examples

The disappearance of the explorer remains a riddle wrapped in an enigma even after decades of research.
Her true intentions were a riddle wrapped in an enigma to everyone around her.
For historians, the ancient manuscript is a riddle wrapped in an enigma waiting to be solved.

Synset Examples

Understanding her feelings is a riddle wrapped in an enigma.
His motives are a riddle wrapped in an enigma.
That situation is a riddle wrapped in an enigma.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mystery - something difficult or impossible to understand or explain
  • Synonym: puzzle - a thing that is difficult to understand or explain

Etymology

Popularized by British Prime Minister Winston Churchill in a 1939 speech.
