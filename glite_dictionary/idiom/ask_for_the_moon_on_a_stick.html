to ask for the moon on a stick

1. VERB

/ æsk fər ðə mun ɑn ə stɪk / (AmE), / ɑːsk fə ðə muːn ɒn ə stɪk / (BrE)
to request something that is impossible or extremely unrealistic
ask for something impossible

Examples

When you expect everything to be perfect, you're basically asking for the moon on a stick.
She felt as if she were asking for the moon on a stick by wanting both a high salary and flexible hours.
It's not like I'm asking for the moon on a stick; I just want a decent cup of coffee.

Synset Examples

It's like asking for the moon on a stick to expect such results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ask for the moon - to make a request for something unattainable
