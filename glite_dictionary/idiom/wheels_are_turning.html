wheels are turning

1. X

/ wilz ɑr ˈtɝnɪŋ / (AmE), / wiːlz ɑː ˈtɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
used to indicate that someone is thinking deeply or planning something, often not immediately apparent to others
someone is thinking or planning

Examples

I could tell that wheels are turning in her mind as she listened to my proposal.
Whenever he gets quiet like this, you know his wheels are turning.
It was obvious from her expression that the wheels are turning as she worked through the problem.

Synset Examples

Her wheels are turning as she considers all the options.
You can tell his wheels are turning; he’s got a new idea.
I can see your wheels are turning; what are you thinking?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gears are turning - used to describe someone thinking or processing information
