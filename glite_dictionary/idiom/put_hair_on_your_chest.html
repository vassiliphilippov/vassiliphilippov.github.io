to put hair on your chest

1. VERB

/ pʊt hɛr ɑn jɔr ʧɛst / (AmE), / pʊt heə ɒn jɔː ʧɛst / (BrE)
(informal) to make someone stronger, tougher, or more manly, often used humorously or exaggeratedly.
make someone tougher or more manly (idiom)
informal

Examples

Some people claim that drinking black coffee will put hair on your chest.
A coach might say that doing early morning runs will put hair on your chest.
That cold plunge in the lake will really put hair on your chest.

Synset Examples

That spicy chili will really put hair on your chest!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to toughen up - to make someone tougher or more resilient
