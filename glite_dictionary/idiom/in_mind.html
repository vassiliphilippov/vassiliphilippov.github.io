in mind

1. ADP

/ ɪn maɪnd / (AmE), / ɪn maɪnd / (BrE)
considered or being thought about; in one's thoughts or plans
being considered or thought about

Examples

She always has your best interests in mind when making decisions.
Do you have anyone in mind for the job opening?
I went to the store with a specific item in mind to buy.

Synset Examples

She has in mind a new project for next year.
