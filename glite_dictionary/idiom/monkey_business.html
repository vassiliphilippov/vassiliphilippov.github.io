monkey business

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌŋki ˌbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki ˌbɪznɪs / (BrE)
mischievous or deceptive behavior, usually considered playful or slightly dishonest
mischievous or deceitful behavior

Examples

The kids were up to some monkey business when the teacher left the room.
The accountant was fired for suspected monkey business with the company accounts.
Stop your monkey business and get back to work!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mischief - playful misbehavior or troublemaking
  • Antonym: honesty - the quality of being truthful; opposite of deceit
