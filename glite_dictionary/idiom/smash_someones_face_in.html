to smash someone's face in

1. VERB

/ smæʃ sʌmˌwʌnz feɪs ɪn / (AmE), / smæʃ sʌmˌwʌnz feɪs ɪn / (BrE)
to violently attack someone, especially by hitting them in the face very hard, often causing serious harm
to violently hit someone in the face
informal vulgar

Examples

If you insult him again, he's going to smash your face in.
The thug threatened to smash someone's face in if they didn't hand over their wallet.
He got so angry in the argument that he almost tried to smash Tom's face in.
