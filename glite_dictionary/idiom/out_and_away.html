out and away

1. ADV

/ aʊt ənd əˈweɪ / (AmE), / aʊt ənd əˈweɪ / (BrE)
by a considerable margin; to a great degree compared to others
by a considerable margin

Examples

The new design is out and away better than anything we've seen before.
Her performance was out and away the highlight of the evening.
That solution is out and away the most effective among all the options.

Synset Examples

she was by far the smartest student
it was far and away the best meal he had ever eaten

Related Terms

  • Synonym: far and away - by a great amount; by far
