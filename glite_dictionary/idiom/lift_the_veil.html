to lift the veil

1. VERB

/ lɪft ðə veɪl / (AmE), / lɪft ðə veɪl / (BrE)
to reveal or make known something that was previously hidden or secret
reveal something hidden or secret

Examples

The journalist's report helped to lift the veil on government corruption.
The scientist's discovery lifted the veil on the origins of the mysterious illness.
New evidence may finally lift the veil from the decades-old mystery.

Synset Examples

He tried to lift the veil on her secret.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to uncover - to make something known that was hidden
  • Synonym: to reveal - to make something visible or known
  • Antonym: to conceal - to keep something hidden or secret
