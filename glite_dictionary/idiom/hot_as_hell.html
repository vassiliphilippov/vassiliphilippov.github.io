hot as hell

1. ADJ

/ hɑt əz hɛl / (AmE), / hɒt əz hɛl / (BrE)
extremely hot; used to describe weather or conditions that are uncomfortably or excessively hot.
extremely hot (idiom)
informal vulgar

Examples

It was hot as hell in the small apartment with no air conditioning.
After running for an hour, I felt hot as hell and needed a cold drink.
The desert was hot as hell even in the early morning.

Synset Examples

The car's interior was hot as hell after parking.
The summer heat feels hot as hell this year.
It was hot as hell during our hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boiling - extremely hot (weather or temperature)
  • Synonym: scorching - very hot; burning
  • Antonym: freezing - extremely cold
