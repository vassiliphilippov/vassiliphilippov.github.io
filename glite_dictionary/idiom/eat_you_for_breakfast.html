to eat you for breakfast

1. VERB

/ it ju fɚ ˈbrɛkfəst / (AmE), / iːt juː fə ˈbrɛkfəst / (BrE)
to defeat or overwhelm someone easily, often showing superior skill or power
defeat someone easily (idiom)

Examples

If you challenge Carla to chess, she'll eat you for breakfast.
Newcomers to the sales team are often eaten for breakfast by more experienced colleagues.
That debate team was so strong, they could eat their competition for breakfast.

Synset Examples

In this industry, they'll eat you for breakfast if you're not prepared.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wipe the floor with someone - to defeat someone completely
