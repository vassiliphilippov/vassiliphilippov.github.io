by all accounts

1. ADV

/ baɪ ɔl əˈkaʊnts / (AmE), / baɪ ɔːl əˈkaʊnts / (BrE)
according to what everyone says or all the reports available
according to all reports or opinions

Examples

By all accounts, the new manager has improved team morale.
The concert was, by all accounts, a resounding success.
By all accounts, he handled the situation with great professionalism.

Synset Examples

By all accounts, she is a dedicated worker.
The restaurant is, by all accounts, fantastic.
By all accounts, the event was a huge success.
