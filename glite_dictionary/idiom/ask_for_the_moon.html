to ask for the moon

1. VERB

/ æsk fəɹ ðə mun / (AmE), / ɑːsk fə ðə muːn / (BrE)
to request something that is impossible or unreasonable to provide
make an impossible or unreasonable request

Examples

Don't ask for the moon; we only have a limited budget for this project.
He felt embarrassed when he realized he had asked for the moon in the negotiations.
It's perfectly reasonable to make requests, but sometimes she asks for the moon and gets disappointed.

Synset Examples

You can't expect to ask for the moon and get it.
She realized he was asking for the moon with his demands.
He always asks for the moon but never delivers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ask too much - to make an excessive or unreasonable request
  • Antonym: within reason - within a reasonable limit, not excessive
