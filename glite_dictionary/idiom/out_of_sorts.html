out of sorts

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv sɔrts / (AmE), / aʊt əv sɔːts / (BrE)
feeling slightly unwell, upset, or irritable
feeling slightly unwell or irritable

Examples

I'm sorry for being grumpy; I'm just out of sorts today.
She seemed out of sorts after staying up too late last night.
Whenever the seasons change, he often feels out of sorts for a few days.

Synset Examples

I'm feeling a bit out of sorts today.
She was out of sorts after the bad news.
He's been out of sorts since his vacation ended.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: under the weather - feeling slightly ill or unwell
