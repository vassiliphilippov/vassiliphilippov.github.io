time on one's hands

1. NOUN

/ taɪm ɑn wʌnz hændz / (AmE), / taɪm ɒn wʌnz hændz / (BrE)
a period when someone has nothing to do and is free to spend their time as they wish
free time; spare time

Examples

He suddenly found himself with a lot of time on his hands after his children left home.
With time on her hands, she decided to take a painting class.
If you have time on your hands, why not pick up a new hobby?

Synset Examples

She often volunteers because she has time on her hands.
Since retiring, I have time on my hands.
Do you have time on your hands to help me?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: free time - time when one is not occupied; leisure time
  • Synonym: spare time - time not taken up by usual duties; free time
