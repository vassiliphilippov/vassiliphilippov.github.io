the hell you say

1. INTJ

/ ðə hɛl ju seɪ / (AmE), / ðə hɛl juː seɪ / (BrE)
used to express disbelief or astonishment at something just said
expression of disbelief or surprise
informal

Examples

The hell you say! I can't believe she actually quit her job.
"They finished the marathon in under three hours." – The hell you say!
When he admitted he had never seen snow before, I replied, "The hell you say!"

Synset Examples

The hell you say? I thought he was innocent!
The hell you say! I never expected that to happen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no way - used to express disbelief or surprise
  • Synonym: you're kidding - expression to indicate disbelief or surprise
