pile of horse manure

1. NOUN

/ paɪl əv hɔrs məˈnur / (AmE), / paɪl əv hɔːs məˈnjʊə / (BrE)
used to describe something that is nonsense or blatantly untrue
nonsense; something untrue
informal

Examples

Don't believe him; his story is a pile of horse manure.
She dismissed the rumor as just a pile of horse manure.
That excuse he gave you is a total pile of horse manure.

Synset Examples

That excuse is just a pile of horse manure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - spoken or written words with no meaning or that are untrue
