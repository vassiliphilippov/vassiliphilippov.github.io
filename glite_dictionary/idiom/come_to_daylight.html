to come to daylight

1. VERB

/ kʌm tə ˈdeɪˌlaɪt / (AmE), / kʌm tʊ ˈdeɪ.laɪt / (BrE)
(of information, truth, etc.) to become known or be revealed after being hidden or kept secret.
become known or revealed

Examples

After years of speculation, the truth finally came to daylight during the investigation.
Many hidden facts about the project are starting to come to daylight now.
Her secret eventually came to daylight, surprising everyone in the family.

Synset Examples

The truth finally came to daylight after years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to come to light - to become known or be revealed
