scholar of the first water

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑːlɚ əv ðə ˈfɜrst ˈwɔːtɚ / (AmE), / ˈskɒlə əv ðə ˈfɜːst ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
A scholar of the highest distinction or excellence; a highly respected expert in their field.
scholar of the highest distinction

Examples

Her reputation as a scholar of the first water was recognized across the academic world.
The committee sought advice from a scholar of the first water on ancient manuscripts.
Despite his youth, many already consider him a scholar of the first water.

Synset Examples

He is a scholar of the first water in his field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expert - a person with a high level of knowledge or skill in a particular area

Etymology

From the phrase 'of the first water,' originally used to describe diamonds of the highest quality.
