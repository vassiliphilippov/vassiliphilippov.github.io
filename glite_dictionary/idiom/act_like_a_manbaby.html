to act like a manbaby

1. VERB

/ ækt laɪk ə ˈmænˌbeɪbi / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈmænˌbeɪbi / (BrE)
to behave in an extremely immature or childish way, especially as an adult
behave in a childishly immature way
informal

Examples

Whenever he doesn't get his way, he tends to act like a manbaby and sulk.
Stop acting like a manbaby and take responsibility for your mistake.
She was embarrassed when her brother acted like a manbaby in front of their friends.

Synset Examples

He acted like a manbaby when he didn't get his way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to throw a tantrum - behave very angrily and irrationally, like a child
  • Antonym: act mature - behave in a responsible and adult-like manner
