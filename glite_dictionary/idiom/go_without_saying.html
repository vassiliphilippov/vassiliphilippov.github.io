to go without saying

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ wɪðaʊt ˈseɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ wɪðaʊt ˈseɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
to be so obvious or self-evident that it does not need to be stated
be obvious, not need stating

Examples

It goes without saying that trust is important in any relationship.
Your hard work goes without saying, and it's greatly appreciated by everyone.
It goes without saying that one should always double-check their work before submitting it.

Synset Examples

It should go without saying that we need to prepare.
It goes without saying that she won the award.
It goes without saying that honesty is crucial.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-evident - clearly true without needing proof or explanation
  • Synonym: obvious - easily perceived or understood; clear, self-evident
