to get (one's) juices flowing

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈdʒuːsɪz ˈfloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈdʒuːsɪz ˈfləʊɪŋ / (BrE)
to stimulate creativity, energy, or enthusiasm; to become excited or inspired to act or think
become inspired or energized
informal

Examples

The brainstorming session really got our juices flowing and resulted in some innovative solutions.
Listening to energetic music always gets my juices flowing before I start working on a project.
A cup of strong coffee in the morning helps get my juices flowing for the day ahead.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to inspire - to fill someone with the urge to do or feel something creative
  • Synonym: to motivate - to provide someone with a reason to take action
