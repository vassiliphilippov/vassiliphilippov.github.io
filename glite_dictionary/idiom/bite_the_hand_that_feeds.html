to bite the hand that feeds

1. VERB

/ baɪt ðə hænd ðæt fidz / (AmE), / baɪt ðə hænd ðət fiːdz / (BrE)
to harm or act against someone who is helping or supporting you
harm someone who helps you

Examples

You shouldn't bite the hand that feeds if you want to stay on good terms with your employer.
Realizing she was about to bite the hand that feeds, she apologized for her criticism.
Despite his frustration, he decided not to bite the hand that feeds and accepted their support.

Synset Examples

It's foolish to bite the hand that feeds one when times are tough.
She realized she was biting the hand that feeds her.
Don't bite the hand that feeds you, or you'll regret it.
Don't bite the hand that feeds you!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to turn against - to stop supporting or become hostile to someone
