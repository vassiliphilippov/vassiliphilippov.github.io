toe to toe

1. ADV

/ ˌtoʊ tə ˈtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌtəʊ tə ˈtəʊ / (BrE)
in direct confrontation or close competition, especially where opponents are face to face and ready to engage in conflict or rivalry
in direct confrontation or competition

Examples

The candidates went toe to toe during the heated debate on live television.
She wasn't afraid to go toe to toe with her supervisor over the issue.
The two companies have been battling toe to toe for dominance in the market.

Synset Examples

They argued toe to toe until one finally backed down.
The boxers stood toe to toe, ready to fight.
The two rivals went toe to toe in the final match.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head-to-head - in direct competition or confrontation
  • Synonym: to face off - engage in a direct confrontation, often before a contest or dispute
