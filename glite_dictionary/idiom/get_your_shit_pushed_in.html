to get your shit pushed in

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt jɔr ʃɪt pʊʃt ɪn / (AmE), / ɡɛt jɔː ʃɪt pʊʃt ɪn / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) To suffer a severe defeat or experience serious consequences, especially as a result of confrontation or conflict.
suffer a severe defeat (vulgar slang)
slang vulgar

Examples

He talked tough at the start, but ended up getting his shit pushed in by the other team.
You'd better prepare properly, or you'll get your shit pushed in during the competition.
The new recruit quickly realized he could get his shit pushed in if he wasn't careful.

Synset Examples

If you keep bothering him, you're going to get your shit pushed in.
Don't mess with them, or you'll get your shit pushed in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get beaten up - to be physically assaulted or severely defeated
  • Antonym: win - to be victorious or successful in a contest or conflict
