barrel of monkeys

1. NOUN

/ ˈbærəl əv ˈmʌŋkiz / (AmE), / ˈbærəl əv ˈmʌŋkiz / (BrE)
a situation or activity that is very amusing or enjoyable
very amusing or fun situation

Examples

The children thought the carnival was a barrel of monkeys and didn't want to leave.
Organizing this event has been a barrel of monkeys so far!
He expected the job to be serious, but it turned out to be a barrel of monkeys.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fun - enjoyment, amusement, or lighthearted pleasure
