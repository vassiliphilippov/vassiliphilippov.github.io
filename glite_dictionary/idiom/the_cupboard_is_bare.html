the cupboard is bare

1. X

/ ðə ˈkʌbərd ɪz bɛr / (AmE), / ðə ˈkʌbəd ɪz beə / (BrE)
there are no supplies, especially of food, left; nothing is available
nothing left; out of supplies

Examples

With the rent due and the cupboard is bare, he worried about next week.
After the party, the cupboard is bare and they need to go shopping again.
If the cupboard is bare after dinner, we'll have to order takeout.

Synset Examples

With no groceries left, I realized the cupboard is bare.
She sighed, knowing the cupboard is bare before the big dinner.
After a long week, it was clear the cupboard is bare.
