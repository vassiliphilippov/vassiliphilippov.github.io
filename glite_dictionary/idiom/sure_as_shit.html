sure as shit

1. X

/ ʃʊr əz ʃɪt / (AmE), / ʃɔːr əz ʃɪt / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is definitely true or certain
certainly; without doubt (vulgar emphasis)
informal vulgar

Examples

You can sure as shit count on him to be late again.
It's going to rain tomorrow, sure as shit.
If she says she'll do it, then she will, sure as shit.

Synset Examples

He'll take the job, sure as shit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sure as hell - used for strong assurance, less vulgar variant
