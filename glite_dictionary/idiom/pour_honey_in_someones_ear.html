to pour honey in someone's ear

1. VERB

/ pɔr ˈhʌni ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz ɪr / (AmE), / pɔː ˈhʌni ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz ɪə / (BrE)
To flatter or deceive someone with insincere praise or sweet words.
flatter or deceive with sweet words

Examples

He was trying to pour honey in her ear with compliments, but she saw through his act.
Whenever they need a favor, they suddenly start pouring honey in my ear.
Stop pouring honey in my ear and just tell me the truth.

Synset Examples

He was trying to pour honey in her ear with compliments.
She always knows how to pour honey in his ear.
Stop pouring honey in my ear and be honest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butter someone up - to flatter someone, often insincerely
  • Synonym: to sweet-talk - to talk to someone persuasively and insincerely
