that's all she wrote

1. X

/ ðæts ɔl ʃi roʊt / (AmE), / ðats ɔːl ʃiː rəʊt / (BrE)
used to indicate that there is nothing more to be said or done; the end of something
phrase indicating finality or conclusion

Examples

After the final whistle blew, the team knew that's all she wrote for their season.
When the company announced bankruptcy, that's all she wrote for hundreds of employees.
Once the cake was gone, the kids shrugged and said, 'that's all she wrote.'

Synset Examples

He finally quit his job; that's all she wrote.
