with every breath

1. ADV

/ wɪð ˈɛvri brɛθ / (AmE), / wɪð ˈɛvri brɛθ / (BrE)
With complete effort and determination; persistently and wholeheartedly in every moment.
with complete effort and determination

Examples

She pursued her goals with every breath, never losing focus on what mattered most.
He defended his family's honor with every breath, refusing to give up.
The activist promised to fight injustice with every breath until real change was achieved.

Synset Examples

She fought for her dreams with every breath.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wholeheartedly - with complete sincerity and commitment
  • Antonym: halfheartedly - without enthusiasm or real effort, lacking commitment
