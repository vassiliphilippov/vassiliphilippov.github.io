to go to the ends of the earth

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ tə ði ɛndz əv ði ɝθ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə ði endz əv ði ɜːθ / (BrE)
to make every possible effort to achieve something, no matter how difficult or far-fetched
make every possible effort

Examples

She said she would go to the ends of the earth to find her missing dog.
He is willing to go to the ends of the earth for his family.
They promised to go to the ends of the earth to uncover the truth.

Synset Examples

They promised to go to the ends of the earth for justice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to move heaven and earth - make every possible effort
  • Synonym: to leave no stone unturned - search thoroughly or make every effort
