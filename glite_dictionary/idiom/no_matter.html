no matter

1. ADV

/ noʊ ˈmætər / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈmætə / (BrE)
used to say that the result or action will be the same whatever the circumstances; regardless of difficulties or other factors.
whatever the circumstances; regardless

Examples

No matter what the weather is like, the race will go on as scheduled.
She promised to support him no matter what happened.
You should always do your best, no matter how hard things get.

Synset Examples

he carried on regardless of the difficulties

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regardless - without paying attention to the present situation
  • Synonym: despite - without being affected by; in spite of
  • Synonym: in spite of - despite; regardless of
