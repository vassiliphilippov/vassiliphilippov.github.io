once or twice

1. ADV

/ wʌns ər twaɪs / (AmE), / wʌns ɔː twaɪs / (BrE)
on one or two occasions; a few times but not very often
occasionally; a few times

Examples

I have only visited Paris once or twice, but I loved it each time.
You might get lucky once or twice, but you can't rely on it.
She has mentioned your name once or twice in conversation.

Synset Examples

I've seen that movie once or twice.
He called me once or twice last week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occasionally - sometimes; not very often
  • Degree Lower: rarely - not often; seldom
  • Degree Higher: frequently - often; many times
