taken up

1. ADJ

/ ˈteɪkən ʌp / (AmE), / ˈteɪkən ʌp / (BrE)
Having or showing excessive or compulsive concern or absorption with something.
absorbed or preoccupied with something

Examples

She was completely taken up with planning the upcoming event and hardly noticed the time passing.
He became so taken up with his new hobby that he forgot to return his friend's call.
The teacher was taken up with grading papers and did not hear the knock at the door.

Synset Examples

became more and more haunted by the stupid riddle
was absolutely obsessed with the girl
got no help from his wife who was preoccupied with the children
he was taken up in worry for the old woman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preoccupied - absorbed in thought or concern about something
  • Synonym: obsessed - having an excessive or compulsive fixation on something
