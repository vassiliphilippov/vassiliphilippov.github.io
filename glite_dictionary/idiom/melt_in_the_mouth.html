melt in the mouth

1. ADJ

/ mɛlt ɪn ðə maʊθ / (AmE), / mɛlt ɪn ðə maʊθ / (BrE)
(of food) to be extremely tender, soft, or delicious, dissolving or becoming very easy to eat when put in the mouth.
very tender, delicious food idiom

Examples

The steak was so tender it seemed to melt in the mouth.
Her pastries always melt in the mouth, leaving everyone wanting more.
These chocolates simply melt in the mouth with every bite.

Synset Examples

The chocolate truffles truly melt in the mouth.
Her homemade cookies melt in the mouth with each bite.
The dessert was heavenly, melting in my mouth.
This steak is so tender, it melts in the mouth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: succulent - (of food) tender, juicy, and tasty
