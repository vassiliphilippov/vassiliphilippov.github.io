upon the tapis

1. X

/ əˈpɑn ðə ˈtæpɪs / (AmE), / əˈpɒn ðə ˈtæpɪs / (BrE)
under consideration or being discussed, especially in formal or literary contexts
under discussion; being considered
archaic

Examples

The matter of the budget was upon the tapis at yesterday's council meeting.
A proposal to renovate the library is once again upon the tapis.
Many pressing issues were upon the tapis during the recent session.

Synset Examples

The new policy was placed upon the tapis for discussion.
Her promotion was upon the tapis during the board meeting.
Various issues were raised upon the tapis at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on the table - under review or being discussed
  • Synonym: under consideration - being thought about or discussed

Etymology

From French 'tapis' meaning 'carpet'—the idea of something 'on the table' for discussion.
