for the most part

1. ADV

/ fɔr ðə moʊst pɑrt / (AmE), / fɔː ðə məʊst pɑːt / (BrE)
mainly; mostly; in most respects, but not entirely
mainly; for the most part

Examples

For the most part, the weather stayed sunny during our vacation.
Teenagers, for the most part, use social media daily.
The committee was, for the most part, satisfied with the proposal.

Synset Examples

These accounts are largely inactive

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mostly - mainly; for the greatest part
  • Antonym: partly - to some extent; not completely
