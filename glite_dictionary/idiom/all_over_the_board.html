all over the board

1. ADV

/ ɔl ˈoʊvɚ ðə bɔrd / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈəʊvə ðə bɔːd / (BrE)
varying widely in range or inconsistency; affecting all aspects or elements
widely variable or affecting everything
american

Examples

The company's policies are all over the board and hard to understand.
Test scores this year were all over the board, ranging from very low to exceptionally high.
Feedback from customers was all over the board, making it difficult to identify trends.

Synset Examples

The grades were all over the board this semester.
Her opinions were all over the board during the meeting.
The budget cuts affected programs all over the board.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all over the place - varying greatly; lacking consistency or focus
  • Antonym: consistent - always behaving or happening in the same way
