by rights

1. ADV

/ baɪ raɪts / (AmE), / baɪ raɪts / (BrE)
according to what is fair, just, or correct; properly or justifiably
according to what is fair or just

Examples

By rights, this land should belong to the local community.
She knew that, by rights, she ought to apologize first.
By rights, he should have received an award for his efforts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rightfully - in a way that is morally or legally correct
  • Antonym: wrongfully - in a way that is morally or legally wrong
