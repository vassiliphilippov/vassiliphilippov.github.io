in disguise

1. ADV

/ ɪn dɪsˈɡaɪz / (AmE), / ɪn dɪsˈɡaɪz / (BrE)
wearing clothes or using a costume in order to hide one's true identity
concealing one's identity with costume

Examples

The detective entered the club in disguise to observe the suspects unnoticed.
Many celebrities travel in disguise to avoid attracting public attention.
The thief managed to escape in disguise during the chaotic scene.

Synset Examples

He attended the party in disguise as a superhero.
The spy went in disguise to gather information.
She went to the event in disguise to avoid fans.
