to be a pig

1. VERB

/ bi ə pɪɡ / (AmE), / biː ə pɪɡ / (BrE)
to behave in a greedy or selfish manner, especially by taking more than one's fair share
act greedily or selfishly
informal

Examples

Stop being a pig and leave some cake for the rest of us.
You don't have to be a pig every time we go out to eat.
She told her brother not to be a pig with the candy.

Synset Examples

Don't be such a pig when sharing snacks!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greedy - having an excessive desire for more, especially food or wealth
  • Synonym: selfish - concerned excessively or exclusively with oneself
