rare bird

1. NOUN

/ rɛr bɝd / (AmE), / reə bɜːd / (BrE)
a person who is unusual, unique, or uncommon in some way
an uncommon or unique person

Examples

She's a rare bird in the world of classical music, combining technical mastery with true originality.
Finding someone with such integrity in politics is a rare bird indeed.
He was always considered a rare bird because of his innovative ideas and unconventional approach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one of a kind - a unique or singular person or thing
  • Synonym: eccentric - a person who is unconventional or unusual
