wrong side of the bed

1. NOUN

/ ɹɔŋ saɪd əv ðə bɛd / (AmE), / ɹɒŋ saɪd əv ðə bɛd / (BrE)
used in the phrase 'get up on the wrong side of the bed' to mean to be in a bad mood for no apparent reason
being in a bad mood (idiom)

Examples

She must have gotten up on the wrong side of the bed because she's grumpy today.
Whenever he wakes up on the wrong side of the bed, the whole office can feel it.
If I seem irritable, it's just because I started my day on the wrong side of the bed.

Synset Examples

I think I got up on the wrong side of the bed this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad mood - a state of being irritable or unhappy
  • Antonym: good mood - a state of being happy or cheerful
