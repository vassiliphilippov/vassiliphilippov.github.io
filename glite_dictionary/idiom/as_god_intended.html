as God intended

1. ADV

/ æz ɡɑd ɪnˈtɛndəd / (AmE), / æz ɡɒd ɪnˈtɛndɪd / (BrE)
used to say that something is done in the way it was meant to be by divine will or natural order
as was meant by divine will

Examples

They preferred to eat seasonal fruits as God intended, without any added sugar.
He chose to live simply, as God intended, appreciating the basics of life.
She let her garden grow wild and free, just as God intended.

Synset Examples

They raised their children as God intended.
He built the house as God intended, with love.
She baked the bread as God intended, with simple ingredients.
