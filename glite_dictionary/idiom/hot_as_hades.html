hot as Hades

1. ADJ

/ hɑt əz ˈheɪdiːz / (AmE), / hɒt əz ˈheɪdiːz / (BrE)
extremely hot; used to describe very high temperatures or intense spiciness
extremely hot (temperature/spiciness)
informal

Examples

The kitchen was hot as Hades with all the ovens running.
Her chili is hot as Hades, so be careful!
It gets hot as Hades in August, especially in this part of the country.

Synset Examples

It's hot as Hades in the desert right now.
The sauna was hot as Hades this morning.
They said the curry was hot as Hades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot as hell - extremely hot; similar idiom

Etymology

From Hades, the Greek god of the underworld, traditionally depicted as a fiery, hot place.
