to give someone pause

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ˈsʌmwʌn pɔz / (AmE), / ɡɪv ˈsʌmwən pɔːz / (BrE)
to make someone stop and think carefully about something before acting
cause someone to reconsider or hesitate

Examples

The unexpected question gave him pause before answering.
Her strange behavior gave me pause during the meeting.
The risks involved should give anyone pause who is considering the plan.

Synset Examples

His comment gave me pause to reconsider my decision.
The news report gave pause to many viewers.
