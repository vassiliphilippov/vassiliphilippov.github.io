up the wazoo

1. ADV

/ ʌp ðə wɑˈzu / (AmE), / ʌp ðə wɒˈzuː / (BrE)
(informal) In very large quantities; to an excessive degree.
in excessive amounts; very much
informal

Examples

The company has regulations up the wazoo that everyone must follow.
We've been getting orders up the wazoo since the product launch.
He found technical glitches up the wazoo during the system test.

Synset Examples

There were snacks up the wazoo at the party.
She has paperwork up the wazoo to finish this week.
He's got excuses up the wazoo for being late.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in abundance - in large quantities; plentifully
  • Antonym: scarce - in short supply or lacking
