on the same page

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə seɪm peɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɒn ðə seɪm peɪdʒ / (BrE)
in agreement or having a shared understanding with others, especially about a plan or goal
having shared understanding or agreement

Examples

Let's make sure we're all on the same page before moving forward with the plan.
It's important for the whole team to be on the same page during this transition.
Are you on the same page with the changes to the project outline?

Synset Examples

Before starting, we need to ensure everyone is on the same page.
It's crucial that the team is on the same page for this project.
Let’s have a quick meeting to get us all on the same page.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in agreement - sharing the same opinion or understanding
  • Antonym: at odds - in conflict or disagreement
