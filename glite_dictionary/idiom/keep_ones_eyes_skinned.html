to keep one's eyes skinned

1. VERB

/ kip wʌnz aɪz skɪnd / (AmE), / kiːp wʌnz aɪz skɪnd / (BrE)
to be especially watchful or alert; to pay close attention to possible danger or opportunity
be especially watchful or alert
informal

Examples

When hiking in the forest, it's wise to keep your eyes skinned for any wildlife on the trail.
The detective told his team to keep their eyes skinned for suspicious activity near the warehouse.
If you want to find a bargain at the market, keep your eyes skinned for the best deals.

Synset Examples

Keep your eyes peeled for any policemen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to keep one's eyes peeled - to be alert and watchful
