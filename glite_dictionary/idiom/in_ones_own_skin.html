in one's own skin

1. X

/ ɪn wʌnz oʊn skɪn / (AmE), / ɪn wʌnz əʊn skɪn / (BrE)
feeling comfortable or confident with oneself; being at ease with who one is
feeling at ease with oneself

Examples

After taking up yoga, she became much more in her own skin.
He didn't feel truly in his own skin until he accepted his background.
It takes courage to feel completely in your own skin in a new environment.

Synset Examples

He finally feels comfortable in his own skin after years of struggle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comfortable in one's own skin - being relaxed and accepting of oneself
  • Antonym: self-conscious - nervous or uncomfortable about oneself
