daddy's girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈdædiz ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈdædiz ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl or woman who is especially close to or fond of her father
girl/woman attached to her father

Examples

Even as an adult, she remains a daddy's girl who seeks her father's advice.
Growing up, people always called her a daddy's girl because they were so inseparable.
Her friends tease her about being a daddy's girl, but she doesn't mind.

Synset Examples

She’s always been a daddy's girl at heart.
Every weekend, the daddy's girl goes out with her dad.
As a daddy's girl, she loves spending time with him.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mama's boy - a boy or man who is overly attached to his mother
