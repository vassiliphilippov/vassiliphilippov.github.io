for the life of one

1. X

/ fər ðə laɪf əv wʌn / (AmE), / fə ðə laɪf əv wʌn / (BrE)
used to emphasize that one is unable to do something, even after making a great effort
unable to do something despite effort

Examples

I can't remember his birthday for the life of me, no matter how hard I try.
She couldn't solve the puzzle for the life of her, even after hours of thinking.
They couldn't find the missing document for the life of them before the meeting.

Synset Examples

He couldn't remember her name for the life of one.
She couldn't understand the math problem for the life of one.
I can’t find my keys for the life of one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: try as one might - no matter how hard one tries
