painful on the eyes

1. ADJ

/ ˈpeɪnfəl ɑn ði aɪz / (AmE), / ˈpeɪnfəl ɒn ði aɪz / (BrE)
visually unpleasant or harsh to look at; causing discomfort to one's sight
visually unpleasant or harsh

Examples

The new wallpaper is so bright and busy that it's actually painful on the eyes.
The way he mixes colors in his paintings can be downright painful on the eyes.
Her fluorescent outfit was bold but completely painful on the eyes.

Synset Examples

The clutter in the room was really painful on the eyes.
That bright neon sign is painful on the eyes.
The color scheme of the website is quite painful on the eyes.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: eye-catching - attracting attention by being visually striking
  • Antonym: easy on the eyes - pleasant to look at; attractive or visually enjoyable
