in the bargain

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðə ˈbɑːrɡən / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈbɑːɡɪn / (BrE)
as an extra or unexpected addition to what has already been mentioned or received
in addition; as an extra advantage or disadvantage

Examples

He inherited his uncle’s house and got a car in the bargain.
She finished the project ahead of schedule and gained her boss’s trust in the bargain.
They purchased the sofa at a discount and received a free rug in the bargain.

Synset Examples

He lost his wife in the bargain

Related Terms

  • Synonym: into the bargain - as an extra addition; besides
  • Synonym: besides - in addition to; apart from
