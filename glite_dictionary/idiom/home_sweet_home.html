home sweet home

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm swit hoʊm / (AmE), / həʊm swiːt həʊm / (BrE)
A phrase expressing an affectionate or sentimental feeling about being at one's own home.
sentimental phrase about one's home

Examples

After a long business trip, nothing feels better than returning to home sweet home.
She posted a photo of her cozy living room with the caption 'home sweet home.','As soon as he walked through his front door, he smiled and whispered, 'home sweet home.'

Synset Examples
