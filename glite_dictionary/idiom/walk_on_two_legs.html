to walk on two legs

1. VERB

/ wɔkt ɑn tu lɛgz / (AmE), / wɔːkt ɒn tuː lɛɡz / (BrE)
(of a person) used to emphasize a particular quality, often highlighting someone as the most remarkable human in a certain respect.
emphasizing a human's exceptional quality

Examples

She is the kindest person that ever walked on two legs.
He claims to be the fastest athlete that ever walked on two legs.
You are the most determined writer that ever walked on two legs.

Synset Examples
