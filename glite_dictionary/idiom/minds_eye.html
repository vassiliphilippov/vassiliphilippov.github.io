mind's eye

1. NOUN

/ maɪndz aɪ / (AmE), / maɪndz aɪ / (BrE)
the mental ability to visualize images or scenes in one's imagination or memory
visualization in imagination or memory

Examples

In my mind's eye, I could still see the house where I grew up.
Even after all these years, she appears vividly in his mind's eye.
Writers often use their mind's eye to create imaginary worlds.

Synset Examples

I could see her clearly in my mind's eye

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imagination - the faculty of forming new ideas, images, or concepts
  • Synonym: memory - the faculty by which the mind stores and remembers information
