far and near

1. ADV

/ fɑr ənd nɪr / (AmE), / fɑː ənd nɪə / (BrE)
in or to all places; everywhere, across great areas or distances
everywhere; across great distances

Examples

The search party looked far and near for the missing dog, but found no trace.
She is known far and near for her generosity and kindness.
Rumors about the event spread far and near within hours.

Synset Examples

he traveled far and wide
the news spread far and wide
people came from far and near
searched for the child far and near

Related Terms

  • Synonym: far and wide - in or to all places; everywhere
