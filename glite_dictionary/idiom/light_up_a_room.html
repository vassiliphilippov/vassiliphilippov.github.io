to light up a room

1. VERB

/ laɪt ʌp ə ruːm / (AmE), / laɪt ʌp ə ruːm / (BrE)
to bring happiness, energy, or positivity to others, making the atmosphere or mood noticeably brighter
make others feel happier or more positive

Examples

Her laughter could always light up a room and put everyone at ease.
He managed to light up a room simply by entering and cracking a witty joke.
Even on a gloomy day, Sarah's positive attitude would light up a room.
