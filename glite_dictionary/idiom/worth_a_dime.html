worth a dime

1. ADJ

/ wɝːθ ə daɪm / (AmE), / wɜːθ ə daɪm / (BrE)
of little or no value; not valuable or useful
of little or no value

Examples

Most of the old equipment isn't worth a dime these days.
His apology wasn't worth a dime after what he did.
If the software isn't worth a dime, we shouldn't buy it.

Synset Examples

In my opinion, it’s not worth a dime anymore.
His advice wasn't worth a dime's worth in my book.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worthless - having no value or use
  • Antonym: valuable - worth a lot of money or useful
