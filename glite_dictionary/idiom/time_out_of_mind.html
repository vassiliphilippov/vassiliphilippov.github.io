time out of mind

1. NOUN

/ taɪm aʊt əv maɪnd / (AmE), / taɪm aʊt əv maɪnd / (BrE)
a period so long ago that it is beyond memory or record; the distant past.
the distant past beyond memory

Examples

Stories of the ancient kingdom have existed since time out of mind.
The forest has stood here from time out of mind, unchanged by the world around it.
Traditions rooted in time out of mind are still celebrated in this village.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: time immemorial - a time long past beyond human memory or legal record
