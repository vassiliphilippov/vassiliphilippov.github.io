that's the ticket

1. INTJ

/ ðæts ðə ˈtɪkət / (AmE), / ðats ðə ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
used to say that something is the right solution, answer, or approach to a situation
used to indicate the right solution or answer
informal

Examples

"I think a hot cup of tea will help you feel better." "That's the ticket!"
If you want to improve your pronunciation, regular practice is that's the ticket.
A little bit of spice in the soup—that's the ticket!

Synset Examples

Networking effectively? That's the ticket!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: that's right - used to confirm correctness or agreement
  • Synonym: that's it - used to indicate something is correct or finished
