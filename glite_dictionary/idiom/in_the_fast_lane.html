in the fast lane

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðə fæst leɪn / (AmE), / ɪn ðə fɑːst leɪn / (BrE)
living a life that is full of excitement, danger, or fast-paced activity
leading an exciting, fast-paced lifestyle

Examples

After her promotion, she found herself in the fast lane of corporate life.
Many celebrities choose to live in the fast lane, surrounded by parties and adventure.
He enjoyed moving in the fast lane, but it eventually wore him out.

Synset Examples

He decided to live life in the fast lane.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in the slow lane - living a more relaxed, less exciting life
