to stand by one's man

1. VERB

/ stænd baɪ wʌnz mæn / (AmE), / stænd baɪ wʌnz mæn / (BrE)
to support or remain loyal to one's husband or partner, especially in difficult situations
remain loyal to one's partner

Examples

Throughout the trial, she stood by her man even when his reputation suffered.
Many admired how Gloria stood by her man during years of hardship.
She has always stood by her man, no matter how tough things got.

Synset Examples

The moll always stood by her man, no matter what.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stand by - to support or remain loyal to someone
