out of character

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv ˈkærəktɚ / (AmE), / aʊt əv ˈkærəktə / (BrE)
not typical of a person's usual behavior or personality
not typical of usual behavior

Examples

It was very out of character for Jane to be late for work.
Sudden laughter seemed out of character for such a reserved man.
His harsh response was completely out of character given his usual patience.

Synset Examples

That sarcastic remark was really out of character for him.
It’s out of character for her to skip the meeting.
His sudden anger was completely out of character.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in character - typical of someone's usual behavior
