made for each other

1. ADJ

/ meɪd fɔr iːtʃ ˈʌðər / (AmE), / meɪd fə iːtʃ ˈʌðə / (BrE)
(of two people) perfectly suited to one another, especially as romantic partners.
perfect romantic partners or match

Examples

Everyone agreed that Sarah and Tom were made for each other.
Their shared interests show they are truly made for each other.
After years together, it was obvious they were made for each other.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
