a monkey's uncle

1. X

/ ə ˈmʌŋkiz ˈʌŋkəl / (AmE), / ə ˈmʌŋkɪz ˈʌŋkl / (BrE)
an expression used to indicate great surprise or disbelief
expression of disbelief or surprise

Examples

A monkey's uncle! I never thought he would actually show up to the party.
If she finishes that puzzle in five minutes, I'll be a monkey's uncle.
A monkey's uncle is what you'll be if you can convince him to go skydiving.

Synset Examples

I'll be a monkey's uncle if they win again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: I'll be damned - expression of surprise or disbelief
  • Synonym: no way - used to show disbelief or surprise
