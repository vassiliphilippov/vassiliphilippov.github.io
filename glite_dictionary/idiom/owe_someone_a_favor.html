to owe someone a favor

1. VERB

/ oʊ ˈsʌmwʌn ə ˈfeɪvɚ / (AmE), / əʊ ˈsʌmwʌn ə ˈfeɪvə / (BrE)
to be under an obligation to help someone in return for a help they have given you
be obliged to help in return

Examples

After she helped me move last year, I definitely owe her a favor.
You know you owe me a favor, so don't forget to help with the project.
He couldn't refuse because he owed them a favor from before.

Synset Examples

You owe me a favor, and I claim my five pounds now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: return a favor - to do something helpful in response to someone helping you
