coin of the realm

1. NOUN

/ kɔɪn əv ðə ɹɛlm / (AmE), / kɔɪn əv ðə rɛlm / (BrE)
something that is highly valued, accepted, or recognized in a particular context or society
something highly valued or accepted

Examples

In academia, published research is the coin of the realm for career advancement.
Creativity has become the coin of the realm in today's fast-changing business environment.
For some companies, customer loyalty is the true coin of the realm.

Synset Examples

Hard work is the coin of the realm in this business.
Respect is the coin of the realm in our community.
In this town, kindness is the true coin of the realm.

Etymology

Originally referring to the official currency issued by a sovereign state.
