in the swim

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ðə swɪm / (AmE), / ɪn ðə swɪm / (BrE)
actively involved with or informed about current events or activities in a particular group or environment
actively involved or informed about events

Examples

Since joining the committee, she feels in the swim of community events.
He likes to stay in the swim when it comes to technology trends.
After months away, it took her a while to get in the swim of things again.

Synset Examples

She’s always in the swim of local gossip.
I finally feel in the swim with my new job.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: out of the swim - not actively involved or informed about current events
