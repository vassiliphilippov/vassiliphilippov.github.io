trainwreck in slow motion

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪnˌrɛk ɪn sloʊ ˈmoʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnˌrek ɪn sləʊ ˈməʊʃən / (BrE)
A disastrous situation, event, or process that gradually falls apart in a way that people can see but are powerless to stop; used metaphorically.
disaster unfolding gradually and visibly

Examples

For weeks, the merger played out as a trainwreck in slow motion, with every meeting worsening the crisis.
Her career decline in the tabloids looked like a trainwreck in slow motion to her fans.
The response to the crisis was such a trainwreck in slow motion that everyone felt helpless to intervene.

Synset Examples

Watching the project fail was like a trainwreck in slow motion.
Their breakup felt like a trainwreck in slow motion.
His public speech was a trainwreck in slow motion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trainwreck - a completely disastrous situation or event
