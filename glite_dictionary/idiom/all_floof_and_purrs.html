all floof and purrs

1. X

/ ɔl fluːf ənd pɜrz / (AmE), / ɔːl fluːf ənd pɜːz / (BrE)
used to describe a cat that is extremely fluffy and affectionate
very fluffy and affectionate cat
informal

Examples

Her new kitten was all floof and purrs, curling up beside her every night.
Despite his size, the big tomcat was all floof and purrs when he wanted attention.
Every time the vet visits, Luna becomes all floof and purrs to win them over.

Synset Examples

She loved to hug her cat, all floof and purrs.
