seasoned to the tonsils

1. ADJ

/ ˈsizənd tə ðə ˈtɑn.səlz / (AmE), / ˈsiː.zənd tə ðə ˈtɒn.səlz / (BrE)
(of food) extremely well-seasoned or strongly flavored, often to the point of excess.
very well-seasoned or flavored

Examples

The curry was seasoned to the tonsils and left our mouths tingling.
I like my food flavorful, but this stew is seasoned to the tonsils!
Her chili is always seasoned to the tonsils, with bold spices in every bite.

Synset Examples

The soup was seasoned to the tonsils with spices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: well-seasoned - flavored with a lot of seasoning
  • Antonym: bland - lacking strong flavor
