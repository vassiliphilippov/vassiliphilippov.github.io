to make head spin

1. VERB

/ meɪk hɛd spɪn / (AmE), / meɪk hɛd spɪn / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel confused, dizzy, or overwhelmed by information or events
cause confusion or dizziness

Examples

The professor's complicated theories made my head spin during the lecture.
All the conflicting advice from friends will make your head spin.
The fast-paced action in the movie almost made my head spin.

Synset Examples

His rapid-fire speech made my head spin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to confuse - to make someone unable to think clearly
  • Synonym: to overwhelm - to affect someone so strongly they cannot think clearly
