two steps ahead

1. ADV

/ tu stɛps əˈhɛd / (AmE), / tuː stɛps əˈhɛd / (BrE)
significantly more advanced in preparation, understanding, or progress than others
well prepared or advanced compared to others

Examples

In every meeting, he tries to be two steps ahead of his competitors.
Successful investors stay two steps ahead by anticipating market trends.
Her creativity keeps our team two steps ahead of the rest.

Synset Examples

In negotiations, he’s always two steps ahead.
To succeed, you need to stay two steps ahead.
She always seems to be two steps ahead of everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ahead of the game - to be more prepared or successful than others
