everything in the garden is rosy

1. X

/ ˈɛvriˌθɪŋ ɪn ðə ˈɡɑrdn ɪz ˈroʊzi / (AmE), / ˈɛvriˌθɪŋ ɪn ðə ˈɡɑːdn ɪz ˈrəʊzi / (BrE)
used to say that the situation is very good and that all is well
all is well; everything is going well

Examples

Ever since the merger, everything in the garden is rosy at the company.
She makes it seem like everything in the garden is rosy, but there are still problems to solve.
Just because sales are up doesn't mean everything in the garden is rosy.

Synset Examples

Despite the challenges, everything in the garden is rosy now.
After the promotion, everything in the garden is rosy for her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rosy - showing or promising good fortune or happiness
