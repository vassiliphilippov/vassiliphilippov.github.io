penny for your thoughts

1. X

/ ˈpɛni fər jʊr θɑts / (AmE), / ˈpɛni fə jɔː θɒts / (BrE)
used to ask someone what they are thinking, usually when they have been silent for a while
request to know what someone is thinking

Examples

You seem lost in thought—penny for your thoughts?
After a long pause, John said, 'penny for your thoughts?','She gazed out the window silently, so I asked, 'penny for your thoughts?'

Synset Examples

After a long silence, I asked, 'A penny for your thoughts?'
You seem deep in thought; a penny for your thoughts?
She looked troubled, so I said, 'A penny for your thoughts?'
