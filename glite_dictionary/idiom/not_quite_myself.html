not quite myself

1. ADJ

/ nɑt kwaɪt maɪˈsɛlf / (AmE), / nɒt kwaɪt maɪˈsɛlf / (BrE)
feeling unwell, out of sorts, or not in one's usual state of health or mind.
feeling unusually unwell or different

Examples

I haven't been sleeping well lately, so I'm not quite myself today.
She apologized for her mood, saying she was not quite herself after the bad news.
After recovering from the flu, he still felt not quite himself for several days.

Synset Examples

I'm feeling a bit peakish today, not quite myself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of sorts - feeling slightly unwell or irritable
