to drop the McNuke

1. VERB

/ drɑp ðə məkˈnuːk / (AmE), / drɒp ðə məkˈnjuːk / (BrE)
to suddenly introduce a highly controversial or provocative subject into a discussion, often changing its tone or direction drastically
introduce a controversial subject
informal

Examples

He knew the meeting was too quiet, so he decided to drop the McNuke about the budget cuts.
Whenever politics comes up at dinner, someone inevitably drops the McNuke and things get heated.
She warned me not to drop the McNuke during the job interview if I wanted to be hired.

Synset Examples

The debate turned ugly when he dropped the McNuke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to drop a bombshell - to announce unexpected and often shocking news
