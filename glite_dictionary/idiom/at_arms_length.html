at arm's length

1. ADV

/ ət ɑrmz lɛŋkθ / (AmE), / ət ɑːmz lɛŋkθ / (BrE)
at a distance from someone or something, especially so as to avoid becoming too involved or familiar
at a distance; emotionally or socially distant

Examples

He always keeps his colleagues at arm's length and rarely shares personal details.
The negotiations were friendly, but both parties remained at arm's length.
She prefers to stay at arm's length from office gossip.

Synset Examples

keep someone at arm's length

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distant - not close in relationship or physical proximity
  • Antonym: close - near in relationship or distance

2. ADV

/ ət ɑrmz lɛŋkθ / (AmE), / ət ɑːmz lɛŋθ / (BrE)
at a distance that avoids intimacy or involvement; deliberately keeping someone or something away to avoid closeness or entanglement
deliberately avoiding closeness

Examples

During the business meeting, she kept her personal feelings at arm's length to stay professional.
He tries to keep new acquaintances at arm's length until he gets to know them better.
For legal reasons, the company maintains its auditors at arm's length.
