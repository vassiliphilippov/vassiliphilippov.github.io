to recharge one's batteries

1. VERB

/ riˈtʃɑrdʒ wʌnz ˈbæt̬.ɚ.iz / (AmE), / riːˈtʃɑːdʒ wʌnz ˈbæt.ər.iz / (BrE)
to rest or take a break in order to restore one's energy and feel refreshed
to rest and regain energy

Examples

After finishing the big project, she took a holiday to recharge her batteries.
A quiet weekend at home is sometimes all you need to recharge your batteries.
Doctors recommend getting enough sleep at night to recharge your batteries.

Synset Examples

After a long week, she took a day off to recharge her batteries.
He spent the weekend camping to recharge his batteries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rest - to cease work or movement to relax or recover strength
  • Synonym: to take a break - to pause an activity for relaxation or recuperation
