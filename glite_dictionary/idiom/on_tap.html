on tap

1. ADJ

/ ɑn tæp / (AmE), / ɒn tæp / (BrE)
ready and available for immediate use or access.
readily available

Examples

We always have fresh coffee on tap for our guests.
If you need technical support, there's a team on tap to help you.
With plenty of new ideas on tap, the company is ready to innovate.

Synset Examples

extra personnel on tap
other sports and entertainment facilities are on tap
there are numerous projects on tap

Related Terms

  • Synonym: available - able to be used or obtained; at someone's disposal
  • Synonym: in stock - readily available in supply

Etymology

Originally referring to drinks available from a tap in bars, extended to mean readily available in general.
