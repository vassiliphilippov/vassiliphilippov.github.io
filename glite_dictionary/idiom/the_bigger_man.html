the bigger man

1. NOUN

/ ðə ˈbɪɡɚ mæn / (AmE), / ðə ˈbɪɡə mæn / (BrE)
a person who behaves maturely and takes responsibility in a conflict, especially by being forgiving or acting with integrity
one who acts maturely in conflict

Examples

Even though he was angry, he decided to be the bigger man and apologize first.
In family arguments, being the bigger man can help heal old wounds.
She encouraged him to be the bigger man and forgive his colleague.

Synset Examples

In disagreements, it's best to be the bigger man.
Sometimes you have to be the bigger man and apologize.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the better person - someone who acts more morally or maturely in a situation
