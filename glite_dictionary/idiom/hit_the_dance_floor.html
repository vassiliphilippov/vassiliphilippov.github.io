to hit the dance floor

1. VERB

/ hɪt ðə dæns flɔr / (AmE), / hɪt ðə dɑːns flɔː / (BrE)
to begin dancing or to go out and start dancing, especially at a party or club
to start dancing

Examples

As soon as the DJ started playing, everyone rushed to hit the dance floor.
She loves to hit the dance floor when her favorite song comes on.
We decided to hit the dance floor after dinner and danced all night.

Synset Examples

It's time to bust a move and hit the dance floor!
Let’s hit the dance floor and get our groove on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dance - to move rhythmically to music
