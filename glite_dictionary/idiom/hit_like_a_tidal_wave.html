to hit like a tidal wave

1. VERB

/ hɪt laɪk ə ˈtaɪdəl weɪv / (AmE), / hɪt laɪk ə ˈtaɪdəl weɪv / (BrE)
to have a sudden and overwhelming impact, especially in an emotional or metaphorical sense
have a sudden, overwhelming impact

Examples

The news hit like a tidal wave and left everyone in shock.
When the reality of her loss finally hit like a tidal wave, she broke down in tears.
The company merger hit like a tidal wave and changed everything overnight.

Synset Examples

The news hit us like a tidal wave.

Related Terms
