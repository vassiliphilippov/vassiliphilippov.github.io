under a lucky star

1. X

/ ˈʌndɚ ə ˈlʌki stɑɹ / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ə ˈlʌki stɑː / (BrE)
experiencing exceptionally good fortune or luck; favored by fate
experiencing good fortune

Examples

Ever since he won the lottery, he seems to be under a lucky star.
They felt under a lucky star after escaping the accident unharmed.
Isabella believes she was born under a lucky star because things always go her way.

Synset Examples

With her new promotion, she’s definitely under a lucky star.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fortunate - receiving good fortune; lucky
  • Antonym: unlucky - experiencing bad luck or misfortune

Etymology

Alludes to the ancient belief that a person’s fate was influenced by the position of the stars at their birth.
