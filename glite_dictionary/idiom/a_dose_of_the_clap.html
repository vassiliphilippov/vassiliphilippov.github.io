a dose of the clap

1. NOUN

/ ə doʊs əv ðə klæp / (AmE), / ə dəʊs əv ðə klæp / (BrE)
used to describe something extremely unpleasant or undesirable, especially when referring to humor that is not funny at all
something extremely unpleasant (esp. unfunny joke)
informal vulgar

Examples

His latest stand-up routine was about as funny as a dose of the clap.
She rolled her eyes and said his jokes always landed like a dose of the clap.
Hearing that tired old pun was like getting a dose of the clap—painful and best avoided.

Synset Examples

She said his puns were funny as a dose of the clap.
That joke was about as funny as a dose of the clap.
His attempt at humor was funny as a dose of the clap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the clap - slang term for the sexually transmitted infection gonorrhea

Etymology

Refers to 'the clap', slang for gonorrhea, used figuratively for unpleasantness.
