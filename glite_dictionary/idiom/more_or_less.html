more or less

1. ADV

/ mɔːr ər lɛs / (AmE), / mɔːr ɔː lɛs / (BrE)
used to indicate that a statement or figure is not exact but reasonably close; approximately.
approximately; not exactly

Examples

There were twenty people at the meeting, more or less.
The repairs will cost $600, more or less, depending on the parts needed.
The hike took us two hours, more or less, to complete.
