to give as good as one gets

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv æz ɡʊd æz wʌn ɡɛts / (AmE), / ɡɪv az ɡʊd az wʌn ɡɛts / (BrE)
to respond with equal force, skill, or effectiveness, especially in an argument or confrontation
respond equally in confrontation

Examples

Even when faced with fierce criticism, she always gives as good as she gets.
In office politics, Mark gives as good as he gets and never lets anyone push him around.
During the heated debate, both candidates gave as good as they got.

Synset Examples

He always gives as good as he gets in debates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stand one's ground - refuse to back down in an argument or confrontation
