to lay on the sauce

1. VERB

/ leɪ ɑn ðə sɔs / (AmE), / leɪ ɒn ðə sɔːs / (BrE)
to add an excessive amount of sauce or flavoring to food; by extension, to use or apply something in an excessive or exaggerated way
add excessive sauce or do something excessively
informal

Examples

Whenever he cooks pasta, he likes to lay on the sauce for extra flavor.
She told the chef to lay on the sauce because she loves her food spicy.
The critic complained that the restaurant tends to lay on the sauce a bit too heavily.

Synset Examples

Please lay on the sauce to enhance the flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pour it on thick - to behave in an exaggerated or insincere way, or to use excess of something
