rogue of the deepest dye

1. NOUN

/ roʊɡ əv ðə ˈdipəst daɪ / (AmE), / rəʊɡ əv ðə ˈdiːpɪst daɪ / (BrE)
a person who is thoroughly untrustworthy or dishonest; an incorrigible rogue
completely untrustworthy person

Examples

Everyone in the village knew he was a rogue of the deepest dye who could not be trusted.
She realized too late that her business partner was a rogue of the deepest dye.
Though he appeared charming, his actions revealed him as a rogue of the deepest dye.

Synset Examples

The villain was described as a rogue of the deepest dye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scoundrel - a wicked or dishonest person
  • Synonym: villain - a person who is evil or cruel

Etymology

The phrase 'of the deepest dye' uses 'dye' metaphorically to mean a quality that is full or intense, dating to the 17th century.
