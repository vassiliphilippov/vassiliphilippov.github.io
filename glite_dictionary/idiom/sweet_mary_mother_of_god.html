Sweet Mary mother of God

1. INTJ

/ swit ˈmɛri ˈmʌðɚ əv ɡɑd / (AmE), / swiːt ˈmɛːri ˈmʌðə əv ɡɒd / (BrE)
An exclamation expressing surprise, shock, or astonishment.
expression of surprise or shock

Examples

Sweet Mary mother of God, I can't believe how high the bill is this month!
Sweet Mary mother of God, that was the scariest movie I've ever seen.
When he heard the news, he exclaimed, "Sweet Mary mother of God, that's unbelievable!"

Synset Examples

Sweet Mary mother of God, look at that sunset!
He cried, 'Sweet Mary mother of God, that was close!'
Sweet Mary mother of God, I never expected this!

Etymology

Invokes the name of the Virgin Mary, a common tradition in exclamatory language.
