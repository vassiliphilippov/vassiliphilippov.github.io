fashionably late

1. ADJ

/ ˈfæʃənəbli leɪt / (AmE), / ˈfæʃnəbli leɪt / (BrE)
arriving intentionally later than the scheduled time, often to appear stylish or to conform to social conventions
arriving stylishly late

Examples

He likes to arrive fashionably late to every party, making a dramatic entrance each time.
The guests were told to come at eight, but most showed up fashionably late.
She made sure to be fashionably late so all eyes would be on her when she entered.

Synset Examples

Her Maj arrived fashionably late to the event.
He always shows up fashionably late to events.
Being fashionably late is part of her charm.
She arrived fashionably late to the dinner party.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: late - arriving or happening after the expected or usual time
  • Synonym: tardy - delayed beyond the proper or expected time; late
