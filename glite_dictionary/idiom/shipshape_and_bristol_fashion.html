shipshape and Bristol fashion

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɪpˌʃeɪp ənd ˈbrɪstl ˈfæʃən / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpʃeɪp ənd ˈbrɪstl ˈfæʃən / (BrE)
(of a place or things) in excellent order or neat and tidy condition.
neat and in good order

Examples

Before the inspection, the entire facility was made shipshape and Bristol fashion.
Her desk is always shipshape and Bristol fashion, with every item perfectly arranged.
The maintenance team ensured the hotel rooms were shipshape and Bristol fashion for the guests' arrival.

Synset Examples

He keeps his tools shipshape and Bristol fashion.
The garden was kept shipshape and Bristol fashion for the event.
The crew ensured everything was shipshape and Bristol fashion before departure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shipshape - neat and tidy; in good order

Etymology

Originates from naval slang, where 'Bristol' referred to the English port known for high standards of ship maintenance and tidiness.
