bent out of shape

1. ADJ

/ bɛnt aʊt əv ʃeɪp / (AmE), / bɛnt aʊt ɒv ʃeɪp / (BrE)
very upset or angry, especially about something minor or unimportant
very upset or angry (idiom)

Examples

He got bent out of shape when his friend forgot his birthday.
Try not to get bent out of shape over something so trivial.
She was really bent out of shape after hearing the unfair criticism.

Synset Examples

Don't get bent out of shape over small issues.
She tends to get bent out of shape easily.
I won't get bent out of shape about criticism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angry - feeling or showing strong annoyance or displeasure
  • Antonym: calm - not showing or feeling anger or strong emotion
