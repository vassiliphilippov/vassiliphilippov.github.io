no use

1. NOUN

/ noʊ juːs / (AmE), / nəʊ juːs / (BrE)
used to say that something will not have any effect or bring any benefit; pointless or futile
pointless; futile; ineffective

Examples

There's no use complaining about the weather—we can't change it.
It's no use trying to fix the old car; it's beyond repair.
He realized it was no use arguing, so he stayed quiet.

Synset Examples

There's no use arguing if you won't listen.
There's no use waiting any longer; let's go.
I tried to call but it’s no use.
It’s no use arguing with him; he won’t change.
I told her to stop; it's no use arguing now.
There’s no use in worrying about things we can't control.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pointless - having no purpose or effect
  • Synonym: useless - having no practical use or benefit
  • Antonym: worthwhile - useful, important, or beneficial
