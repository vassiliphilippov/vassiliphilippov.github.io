Bumfuck, Egypt

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌmfʌk ˈiːdʒɪpt / (AmE), / ˈbʌmfʌk ˈiːdʒɪpt / (BrE)
(vulgar slang) A remote, insignificant, or isolated place, typically used for humorous or emphatic effect.
remote or insignificant place (vulgar slang)
vulgar slang american

Examples

We ended up in Bumfuck, Egypt after taking the wrong highway exit.
He complained his new job was out in Bumfuck, Egypt, miles from civilization.
Every time we visit her parents, it feels like we're driving to Bumfuck, Egypt.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: middle of nowhere - a very remote or isolated place
  • Synonym: boondocks - rural, remote, or isolated area
  • Synonym: backwater - a place or situation in which little progress is occurring

Etymology

From U.S. English vulgar slang 'bumfuck' (an isolated place) and 'Egypt' (used in American English for remote rural regions).
