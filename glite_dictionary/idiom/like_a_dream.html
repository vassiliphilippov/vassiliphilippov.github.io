like a dream

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə driːm / (AmE), / laɪk ə driːm / (BrE)
extremely well or effectively; as if perfect or ideal, often referring to smooth, beautiful, or flawless performance or appearance.
extremely well or ideally; flawlessly

Examples

The new computer runs like a dream, even with multiple programs open.
After the repairs, the machine worked like a dream again.
On her wedding day, everything went like a dream.

Synset Examples

In the distance, the mirage shimmered like a dream.
This new car works like a dream on the highway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfectly - in a perfect or flawless way
  • Synonym: smoothly - without difficulty or problems
  • Antonym: like a nightmare - in a very distressing or unpleasant manner (opposite)
