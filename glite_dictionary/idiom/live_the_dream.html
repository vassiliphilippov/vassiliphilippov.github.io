to live the dream

1. VERB

/ lɪv ðə driːm / (AmE), / lɪv ðə driːm / (BrE)
to experience the life you have always wanted, especially after effort or as an ideal
to achieve an ideal or desired life

Examples

After years of saving and planning, he finally gets to live the dream by moving to Paris.
Many people work hard so that one day they can live the dream.
She is living the dream now that she travels the world for a living.

Synset Examples

He quit his job to travel and live the dream.
After years of hard work, she finally gets to live the dream.
They started a business together to live the dream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fulfill one's dreams - to achieve everything one has hoped or longed for
