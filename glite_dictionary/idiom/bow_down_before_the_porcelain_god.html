to bow down before the porcelain god

1. VERB

/ baʊ daʊn bɪˈfɔɹ ðə pɔr.sə.lɪn ɡɑd / (AmE), / baʊ daʊn bɪˈfɔː ðə ˈpɔː.səl.ɪn ɡɒd / (BrE)
(informal) To vomit into a toilet, especially after excessive drinking.
vomit into a toilet (after drinking)
informal
