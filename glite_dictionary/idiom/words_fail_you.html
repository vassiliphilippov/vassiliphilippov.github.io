to words fail you

1. VERB

/ wɝdz feɪl ju / (AmE), / wɜːdz feɪl juː / (BrE)
used to say that you cannot find anything to say, usually because you are extremely surprised, moved, or unable to express your feelings adequately
unable to express oneself in words

Examples

When she saw the devastation left by the hurricane, words fail you.
Sometimes, words fail you when you're overwhelmed by gratitude.
In moments of true wonder, words fail you and silence says everything.

Synset Examples

When faced with beauty, sometimes words fail you.
