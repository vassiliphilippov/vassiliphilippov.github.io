bon voyage

1. NOUN

/ bɑn vwɑˈjɑʒ / (AmE), / bɒn vwɑːˈjɑːʒ / (BrE)
used to wish someone a good and safe journey
good wishes for a journey

Examples

Before Emma left for Paris, her friends gathered to say bon voyage.
He waved from the deck, as his family called out bon voyage from the pier.
A big banner reading 'bon voyage' hung above the entrance to the departure lounge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farewell - expression of good wishes on parting

Etymology

From French 'bon voyage' meaning 'good journey'.
