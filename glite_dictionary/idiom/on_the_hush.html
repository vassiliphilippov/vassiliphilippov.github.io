on the hush

1. ADV

/ ɑn ðə hʌʃ / (AmE), / ɒn ðə hʌʃ / (BrE)
in secret; quietly and without attracting attention
secretly; without being noticed
informal

Examples

The negotiations were carried out on the hush to avoid media attention.
They managed to handle the situation on the hush, so nobody found out.
We decided to keep our plans on the hush until everything was finalized.

Synset Examples

He told me the news, but only on the hush.
We planned the surprise party on the hush.
Let's keep this meeting on the hush for now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in secret - so as not to be seen or known by others
  • Antonym: publicly - in a way that is open or known to people in general
