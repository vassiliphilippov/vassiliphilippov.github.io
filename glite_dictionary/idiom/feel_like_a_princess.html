to feel like a princess

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə ˈprɪnsəs / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈprɪnsɪs / (BrE)
to feel very special, pampered, or treated with exceptional care or luxury
feel very special or pampered

Examples

After the makeover, she truly felt like a princess and couldn't stop smiling.
The luxurious hotel made every guest feel like a princess.
On her birthday, Kate felt like a princess thanks to all the special attention.

Synset Examples

She felt like a princess on her wedding day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feel like a king - to feel very special or pampered
  • Antonym: feel neglected - to feel ignored or uncared for
