good news

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd nuːz / (AmE), / ɡʊd njuːz / (BrE)
information or news that is positive, pleasant, or favorable
positive or favorable information

Examples

She was excited to share the good news with her family.
We all need a little good news every now and then.
After weeks of waiting, they finally received some good news.

Synset Examples

I called Gina to share the good news.
She called her friend to share the good news.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bad news - information or news that is negative or unfavorable
