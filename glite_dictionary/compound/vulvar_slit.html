vulvar slit

1. NOUN

/ ˈvʌl.vɚ slɪt / (AmE), / ˈvʌl.və slɪt / (BrE)
the opening or fissure between the labia majora of the vulva
the fissure between the labia majora
medical

Examples

The doctor carefully examined the vulvar slit for signs of infection.
Some conditions can cause irritation around the vulvar slit.
Proper hygiene of the vulvar slit is important for preventing certain health issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vulva - the external female genital organs

Etymology

From Latin 'vulva' (womb, covering) + English 'slit'.
