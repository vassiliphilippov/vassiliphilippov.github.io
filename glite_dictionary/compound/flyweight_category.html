flyweight category

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaɪˌweɪt ˈkætəˌɡɔri / (AmE), / ˈflaɪˌweɪt ˈkatəɡəri / (BrE)
a weight division in sports such as boxing or wrestling for competitors who are lighter than bantamweight but heavier than light flyweight
a light weight class in boxing or wrestling

Examples

Many aspiring boxers aim to compete in the flyweight category due to its competitive nature.
The reigning champion defended his title in the flyweight category last night.
Rules for the flyweight category often differ slightly between boxing organizations.

Synset Examples

She trains daily to compete in the flyweight category.
He won the championship in the flyweight division.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flyweight - a boxer or competitor in the flyweight division
  • Degree Higher: bantamweight category - the weight class directly above flyweight in boxing and wrestling
  • Degree Lower: light flyweight category - the weight class directly below flyweight in boxing and wrestling

Etymology

From 'flyweight' (a very light weight) and 'category' meaning class or division.
