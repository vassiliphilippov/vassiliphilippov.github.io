bathing cap

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪðɪŋ kæp / (AmE), / ˈbeɪðɪŋ kap / (BrE)
A close-fitting cap worn to keep the hair dry while swimming.
tight cap worn while swimming

Examples

She pulled her bathing cap over her hair before diving into the pool.
Most public pools require swimmers to wear a bathing cap for hygiene reasons.
His bright blue bathing cap helped his friends spot him easily in the water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimming cap - a cap worn while swimming to keep hair dry
