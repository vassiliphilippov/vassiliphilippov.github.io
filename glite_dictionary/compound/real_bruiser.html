real bruiser

1. NOUN

/ ˈɹiːəl ˈbruːzɚ / (AmE), / ˈɹɪəl ˈbruːzə / (BrE)
an especially tough or aggressive person, particularly in sport or physical confrontation
very tough or aggressive person
informal

Examples

He’s a real bruiser in the boxing ring, never backing down from a fight.
Our new defender is a real bruiser, making it difficult for opponents to score.
Even off the pitch, you can tell he’s a real bruiser by the way he carries himself.

Synset Examples

He's a real bruiser on the rugby field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bruiser - a tough or aggressive person, especially a man
