cup morel

1. NOUN

/ kʌp mɔːˈrɛl / (AmE), / kʌp ˈmɒr.ɛl / (BrE)
an edible morel mushroom with a cup-shaped or saucer-shaped fruiting body, distinguished by wrinkles radiating from the center inside the cup
an edible cup-shaped morel mushroom

Examples

The forest floor was dotted with cup morels, their saucer-shaped bodies catching morning light.
Because cup morel can be confused with toxic species, proper identification is crucial.
After the rain, she found a large cup morel near the old oak tree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: morel - an edible mushroom with a honeycomb appearance
