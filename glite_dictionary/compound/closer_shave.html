closer shave

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊsər ʃeɪv / (AmE), / ˈkləʊsə ʃeɪv / (BrE)
a shave that removes hair very thoroughly and leaves the skin especially smooth
removal of hair very smoothly when shaving

Examples

This razor promises a closer shave than the previous model.
Many men prefer a closer shave for a cleaner look at work.
She switched to a different brand to achieve a closer shave on her legs.

Synset Examples

He bought a new shaver for a closer shave.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: close shave - a shave in which hair is cut very close to the skin
