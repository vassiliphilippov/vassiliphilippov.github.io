to cook evenly

1. VERB

/ kʊk ˈivənli / (AmE), / kʊk ˈiːvənli / (BrE)
to cook so that all parts are heated and finished at the same rate, without undercooked or overcooked areas
to cook uniformly throughout

Examples

To ensure the roast will cook evenly, let it come to room temperature before placing it in the oven.
Always spread the vegetables in a single layer so they cook evenly on the baking sheet.
He flipped the pancakes frequently to help them cook evenly on both sides.

Synset Examples

He stirred the stew, making sure everything cooked evenly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cook thoroughly - to cook food completely through to the center
