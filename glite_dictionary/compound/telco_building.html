telco building

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlkoʊ ˈbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɛlkəʊ ˈbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
A building that houses telecommunications equipment for a telephone company or telecom operator.
building housing telecom equipment

Examples

The main server rooms are located in the telco building downtown.
Security at the telco building was increased after the attempted break-in.
Several telecom providers share the same telco building to reduce infrastructure costs.

Synset Examples

the telecom hotels are prime targets for bombing

Etymology

Shortened from 'telecommunications company', combined with 'building'.
