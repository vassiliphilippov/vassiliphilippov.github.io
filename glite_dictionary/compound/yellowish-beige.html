yellowish-beige

1. ADJ

/ ˈjɛloʊɪʃˌbeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈjɛlɪʊɪʃˌbiːʒ / (BrE)
a shade of beige with a slight yellow tint
beige color tinged with yellow

Examples

She painted the kitchen walls a yellowish-beige to brighten the space.
The carpet came in a yellowish-beige that matched the curtains perfectly.
His coat was a yellowish-beige color that looked almost golden in the sunlight.
