visual signal

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪʒuəl ˈsɪɡnəl / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒuəl ˈsɪɡnəl / (BrE)
a signal transmitted or conveyed through visual means, such as lights, signs, or gestures
signal conveyed by sight

Examples

The lifeguard used a visual signal to call swimmers out of the water.
Train drivers must watch for the visual signals along the tracks to know when to stop or go.
The device emits a visual signal whenever it needs maintenance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: signal - a gesture or sign that conveys information or instructions
