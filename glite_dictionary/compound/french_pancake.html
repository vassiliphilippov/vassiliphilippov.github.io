French pancake

1. NOUN

/ frɛntʃ ˈpænkeɪk / (AmE), / frɛntʃ ˈpankeɪk / (BrE)
a very thin, small pancake, typically served rolled or folded with sweet or savory filling; also called a crêpe
a thin, small pancake; a crêpe

Examples

The restaurant offers delicious French pancakes with chocolate and strawberries.
She learned to make French pancakes during her trip to Paris.
Their brunch menu features both traditional pancakes and French pancakes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crêpe - a thin, light pancake, usually of French origin
