New England boiled dinner

1. NOUN

/ nuː ˈɪŋɡlənd bɔɪld ˈdɪnər / (AmE), / njuː ˈɪŋɡlənd bɔɪld ˈdɪnə / (BrE)
A traditional dish of corned beef simmered with onions, cabbage, and typically other vegetables, especially potatoes and carrots.
corned beef simmered with vegetables

Examples

My grandmother makes a delicious New England boiled dinner every St. Patrick’s Day.
The restaurant’s special was a hearty New England boiled dinner with plenty of potatoes and carrots.
He grew up eating New England boiled dinner on cold winter nights in Vermont.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corned beef and cabbage - dish made of corned beef and cabbage, sometimes with other vegetables
