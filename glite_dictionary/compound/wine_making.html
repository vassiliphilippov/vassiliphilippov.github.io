wine making

1. NOUN

/ waɪn ˈmeɪ.kɪŋ / (AmE), / waɪn ˈmeɪ.kɪŋ / (BrE)
the process and science of cultivating grapes and producing wine
the production of wine from grapes

Examples

He has dedicated his life to the art of wine making.
Advances in technology have revolutionized modern wine making.
French wine making traditions are respected around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: viniculture - the cultivation of grapevines for winemaking
  • Antonym: brewing - the process of making beer or similar beverages
