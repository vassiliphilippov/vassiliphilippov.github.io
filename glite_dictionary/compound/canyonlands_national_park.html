Canyonlands National Park

1. PROPN

/ ˈkænjənˌlændz ˌnæʃəˈnæl ˈpɑrk / (AmE), / ˈkænjənˌlændz ˌnæʃəˈnæl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in southeastern Utah, USA, known for its dramatic canyons, rock formations, and ancient cliff dwellings, formed by the Colorado and Green Rivers
national park in Utah with canyons and rock formations

Examples

We spent a week hiking in Canyonlands National Park and were amazed by its breathtaking scenery.
Canyonlands National Park is famous for its deep canyons carved by the Colorado and Green Rivers.
The ancient cliff dwellings in Canyonlands National Park offer a glimpse into early Native American history.
