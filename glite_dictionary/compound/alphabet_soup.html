alphabet soup

1. NOUN

/ ˈæl.fə.bɛt suːp / (AmE), / ˈæl.fə.bɛt suːp / (BrE)
A soup, typically for children, containing small noodle pieces shaped like letters of the alphabet.
soup with letter-shaped noodles

Examples

When I was a child, I loved eating alphabet soup and searching for my initials.
They served alphabet soup at the kids' birthday party along with sandwiches.
The little boy spent lunch time spelling words with the letters from his alphabet soup.
