movie theater

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvi ˌθiətər / (AmE), / ˈmuːvi ˌθɪətə / (BrE)
a building where films are shown to the public
cinema; place where films are shown
american

Examples

We went to the movie theater to see the latest action film.
The new movie theater in town features reclining seats and 3D screens.
She loves the smell of popcorn at the movie theater.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: movie house - another term for movie theater
