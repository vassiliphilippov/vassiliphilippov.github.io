moderate breeze

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑdɚət briːz / (AmE), / ˈmɒdərət briːz / (BrE)
A wind with a speed of 13–18 knots (about 24–34 km/h); corresponds to force 4 on the Beaufort scale.
13–18 knot wind; Beaufort force 4

Examples

Sailing was perfect today, thanks to a moderate breeze coming from the west.
The picnic was pleasant, with a moderate breeze keeping the air cool and comfortable.
According to the forecast, a moderate breeze is expected to last through the afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gentle breeze - A wind of 8–12 knots; Beaufort force 3
  • Degree Higher: fresh breeze - A wind of 19–24 knots; Beaufort force 5
