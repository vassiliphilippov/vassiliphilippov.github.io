zoological science

1. NOUN

/ zuː.əˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / zuː.əˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kəl ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
the scientific study of animals and animal life, especially as a branch of biology
science of animal biology

Examples

She decided to specialize in zoological science during her university studies.
Breakthroughs in zoological science have deepened our understanding of animal behavior and physiology.
The museum's exhibits are based on research from the field of zoological science.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zoology - the scientific study of animals

Etymology

From zoo- (relating to animals) and science; ultimately from Greek zōion (animal) and Latin scientia (knowledge).
