bladdernut family

1. NOUN

/ ˈblædərˌnʌt ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈblædənʌt ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of dicotyledonous plants (Staphyleaceae) of the order Sapindales, found mostly in the north temperate zone
family of temperate plants (Staphyleaceae)

Examples

The bladdernut family includes several species of shrubs native to temperate regions.
Botanists have studied the unique characteristics of the bladdernut family.
One member of the bladdernut family is known for its distinctive inflated seed pods.
