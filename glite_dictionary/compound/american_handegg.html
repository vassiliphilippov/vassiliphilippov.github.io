American handegg

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛɹɪkən ˈhændˌɛɡ / (AmE), / əˈmɛɹɪkən ˈhændˌɛɡ / (BrE)
A humorous or colloquial term for American football, often used to highlight that the sport primarily involves handling an oval-shaped ball with hands rather than kicking it.
