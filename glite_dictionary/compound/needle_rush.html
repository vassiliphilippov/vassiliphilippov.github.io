needle rush

1. NOUN

/ ˈniː.dl̩ ˌrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈniː.dl̩ ˌrʌʃ / (BrE)
A fine-leaved aquatic plant of the spike rush group, often used as an aerator in aquariums.
fine-leaved aquatic spike rush

Examples

We added a needle rush to our aquarium to help oxygenate the water.
The delicate appearance of needle rush makes it a favorite among aquascaping enthusiasts.
In its natural habitat, needle rush grows along the edges of slow-moving freshwater streams.
