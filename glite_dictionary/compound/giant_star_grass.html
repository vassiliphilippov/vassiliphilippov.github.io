giant star grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt stɑr ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt stɑː ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A perennial grass with stems 3 to 4 feet high, grown mainly in Africa and India for pasture and hay.
perennial grass used for pasture and hay

Examples

The farmer planted giant star grass to provide nutritious feed for his cattle.
In some African regions, giant star grass is valued highly for its drought resistance.
They harvested giant star grass to use as hay during the dry season.
