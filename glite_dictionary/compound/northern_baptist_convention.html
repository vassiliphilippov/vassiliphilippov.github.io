Northern Baptist Convention

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɔrðərn ˈbæptɪst kənˈvɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈbæptɪst kənˈvɛnʃən / (BrE)
a former association of Baptist churches in the northern United States, established in 1907 and later renamed American Baptist Churches USA
former U.S. Baptist church association

Examples

The Northern Baptist Convention played a key role in the development of Baptist churches in the United States.
In 1950, the Northern Baptist Convention changed its name to American Baptist Churches USA.
Many historical records from the early 20th century mention the Northern Baptist Convention and its activities.
