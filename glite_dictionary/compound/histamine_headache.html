histamine headache

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪstəˌmiːn ˈhɛdˌeɪk / (AmE), / ˈhɪstəmiːn ˈhɛdeɪk / (BrE)
A recurring headache triggered by the release of histamine from body cells, often causing intense pain.
headache caused by histamine release
medical

Examples

He suffered from a severe histamine headache after being exposed to certain allergens.
Some patients experience histamine headaches in cycles that can last for weeks.
Doctors may prescribe medications to help manage the pain of a histamine headache.
