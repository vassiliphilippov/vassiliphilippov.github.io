TV station

1. NOUN

/ ˌtiːˈviː ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtiːˈviː ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A company or facility that produces and transmits television programs for public viewing.
place producing and transmitting television programs

Examples

The local TV station aired a special report on community events last night.
She got a job as a producer at a national TV station.
Many TV stations switched to digital broadcasting in the last decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: television station - station that produces and transmits television broadcasts
  • Degree Lower: radio station - station for the production and transmission of radio broadcasts

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'television station.'
