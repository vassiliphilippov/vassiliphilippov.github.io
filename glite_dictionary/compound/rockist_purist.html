rockist purist

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑːkɪst ˈpjʊrɪst / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪst ˈpjʊərɪst / (BrE)
a person who strictly adheres to traditional standards of rock music, often dismissing other genres as inferior
someone rigidly devoted to rock music standards

Examples

As a rockist purist, he refused to listen to any hip-hop or electronic music.
The debate between rockist purists and pop enthusiasts is common in music forums.
Many critics argue that being a rockist purist limits one’s appreciation for modern music innovation.

Synset Examples

She dismissed their opinions, calling them rockist purists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rockist - a person who considers traditional rock music superior to other genres
  • Synonym: purist - a person who insists on strict adherence to traditional rules or structures

Etymology

Formed from 'rockist' (derived from 'rock' music, with the -ist suffix) and 'purist' (from French puriste, ultimately from Latin purus meaning 'pure').
