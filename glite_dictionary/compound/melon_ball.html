melon ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛlən bɔl / (AmE), / ˈmɛlən bɔːl / (BrE)
a small, round piece of melon cut into a sphere, typically using a special scoop
ball-shaped piece of melon

Examples

She decorated the fruit salad with colorful melon balls.
Using a melon baller, he scooped perfect melon balls for the dessert platter.
Each skewer held a melon ball alongside a cube of cheese.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ball - a spherical object or shape
