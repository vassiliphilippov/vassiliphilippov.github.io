family history of cancer

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˈhɪstəri əv ˈkænsɚ / (AmE), / ˈfæməli ˈhɪstəri əv ˈkænsə / (BrE)
a record or account of cancer diagnoses among one's biological relatives, often used to assess genetic risk
record of cancer in family members
medical

Examples

Because of her family history of cancer, her doctor recommended genetic counseling.
Insurance companies often ask about your family history of cancer on application forms.
He is proactive about regular screenings due to his family history of cancer.

Synset Examples

His family history of cancer made him vigilant about screenings.
