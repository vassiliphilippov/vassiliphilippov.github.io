testicular artery

1. NOUN

/ tɛˈstɪkjələr ˈɑːr.t̬ə.ri / (AmE), / tɛˈstɪkjʊlə ˈɑː.tər.i / (BrE)
an artery branching from the abdominal aorta that supplies blood to the testicles
artery supplying the testicles
medical

Examples

The surgeon carefully avoided damaging the testicular artery during the procedure.
Blockage of the testicular artery can lead to impaired blood flow to the testicle.
The testicular artery originates from the abdominal aorta and runs alongside the spermatic cord.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ovarian artery - artery in females supplying the ovaries

Etymology

From 'testicular', relating to the testicle, and 'artery', from Latin 'arteria'.
