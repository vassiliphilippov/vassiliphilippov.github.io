in floods

1. ADJ

/ ɪn flʌdz / (AmE), / ɪn flʌdz / (BrE)
in a state of being covered or affected by excessive water or flooding
experiencing flooding or inundation

Examples

After days of nonstop rain, the city was in floods and many roads were impassable.
The basement was in floods after the storm last night.
Reports showed entire fields in floods, damaging this season's crops.

Synset Examples

The town was in floods after the heavy rain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flooded - covered or submerged with water
  • Antonym: dry - free from water or moisture
