large person

1. NOUN

/ lɑrdʒ ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / lɑːdʒ ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is larger than average in size or build
person bigger than average size

Examples

As a large person, he often finds it hard to find comfortable airplane seats.
The club welcomes everyone, whether you are a large person or not.
Doctors often give special advice to a large person regarding appropriate exercise.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: small person - a person who is smaller than average in size
  • Synonym: large - greater than average size or amount
