pop bottle

1. NOUN

/ pɑp ˈbɑt̬əl / (AmE), / pɒp ˈbɒtl̩ / (BrE)
a bottle designed for holding soft drinks or carbonated beverages
bottle for soft drinks

Examples

He recycled the empty pop bottle after finishing his drink.
The store offers a refund for returning each pop bottle.
She filled a pop bottle with homemade lemonade for the picnic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soda bottle - bottle for holding soda or soft drinks
  • Synonym: soft drink - a nonalcoholic, carbonated beverage
