Lake Victoria

1. PROPN

/ leɪk vɪkˈtɔriə / (AmE), / leɪk vɪkˈtɔːrɪə / (BrE)
the largest lake in Africa and the second largest freshwater lake in the world, located between Uganda, Kenya, and Tanzania; a primary source of the Nile River
the largest lake in Africa

Examples

The fishermen depend on Lake Victoria for their livelihood and daily catch.
Scientists study the biodiversity found only in Lake Victoria.
She sailed across Lake Victoria during her trip to East Africa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lake Tanganyika - the second deepest lake in the world, located in East Africa
  • Synonym: Lake Superior - the largest freshwater lake in North America

Etymology

Named after Queen Victoria of the United Kingdom.
