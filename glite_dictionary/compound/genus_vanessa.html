genus Vanessa

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs vəˈnɛs.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs vəˈnɛs.ə / (BrE)
A genus of butterflies that includes species such as the painted lady and the red admiral.
genus of butterflies including the painted lady

Examples

The genus Vanessa includes several well-known butterflies found across the world.
Many gardeners hope to attract butterflies of the genus Vanessa to their gardens with flowering plants.
Research on the genus Vanessa has helped scientists understand butterfly migration patterns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Vanessa cardui - the painted lady butterfly, a species in genus Vanessa
  • Synonym: red admiral - a butterfly species (Vanessa atalanta) in genus Vanessa

Etymology

From New Latin Vanessa, the name given by entomologist Johan Christian Fabricius, possibly after the literary pseudonym 'Vanessa'.
