hatha yoga

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːtə ˈjoʊɡə / (AmE), / ˈhɑːθə ˈjəʊɡə / (BrE)
A system of yoga that focuses on physical postures and controlled breathing to enhance physical and mental well-being.
yoga system of physical postures and breathing

Examples

She attends hatha yoga classes every week to improve her flexibility.
Many people find hatha yoga helpful for reducing stress and increasing concentration.
The studio offers beginner-level hatha yoga sessions for those new to this practice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yoga - a Hindu spiritual and ascetic discipline; includes breath control, meditation, and postures

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'haṭha' (forceful) and 'yoga' (union).
