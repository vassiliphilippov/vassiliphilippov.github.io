shower room

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃaʊər ruːm / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊə ruːm / (BrE)
a room equipped with several showers, typically for communal or public use
room with several showers

Examples

After gym class, all the students used the shower room to rinse off.
The shower room at the pool was recently renovated with new fixtures.
Staff are required to clean the shower room every evening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: changing room - a room for changing clothes, often in sports facilities
