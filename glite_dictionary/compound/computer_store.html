computer store

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtɚ stɔːr / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə stɔː / (BrE)
A shop or retail outlet that sells computers and related products to individuals and small businesses.
shop selling computers and related products

Examples

I went to the computer store to buy a new laptop for college.
Many computer stores offer free technical support for their customers.
The local computer store had a wide selection of accessories and software.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electronics store - a shop selling a variety of electronic goods
