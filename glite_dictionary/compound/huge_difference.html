huge difference

1. NOUN

/ hjudʒ ˈdɪfɚɛns / (AmE), / hjuːdʒ ˈdɪfərəns / (BrE)
a very significant effect, change, or distinction that impacts a situation or outcome
significant impact or change

Examples

The new treatment made a huge difference in her recovery.
A little encouragement from teachers can make a huge difference to students' confidence.
Switching to renewable energy could have a huge difference on climate change.

Synset Examples

A fellowess can make a huge difference in teamwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: big difference - a large or important distinction or change
  • Antonym: small difference - an unimportant or minor distinction or change
