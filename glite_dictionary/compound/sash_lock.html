sash lock

1. NOUN

/ sæʃ lɑk / (AmE), / sæʃ lɒk / (BrE)
a lock attached to the sashes of a double-hung window to secure them in a closed position
lock for double-hung window sashes

Examples

The old wooden window wouldn't stay shut until I replaced the sash lock.
For extra security, she installed a new sash lock on each window in the living room.
Check that the sash lock is engaged before leaving the house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: window lock - a general lock used to secure a window
