Battle of Minden

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt.əl əv ˈmɪn.dən / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl əv ˈmɪn.dən / (BrE)
a battle during the Seven Years' War in 1759 in which English and allied forces defeated the French
1759 battle in the Seven Years' War

Examples

The anniversary of the Battle of Minden is still commemorated by certain British regiments.
Many historians view the Battle of Minden as a turning point in the Seven Years' War.
At the Battle of Minden, Anglo-German troops inflicted a decisive defeat on the French.
