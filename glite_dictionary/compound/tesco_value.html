Tesco Value

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛsˌkoʊ ˈvæljuː / (AmE), / ˈtɛskəʊ ˈvaljuː / (BrE)
a former brand of low-cost products sold by the British supermarket chain Tesco, known for their basic quality and packaging
budget product brand from Tesco
british

Examples

Many students buy Tesco Value products to keep their grocery bills low.
The packaging on Tesco Value items is usually plain and simple.
He stocked his pantry with Tesco Value canned goods before the long winter.

Synset Examples

The Tesco Value brand offers quality at a low price.
I always choose Tesco Value products to save money.

Etymology

Tesco is a British supermarket chain; 'Value' denotes low-cost, basic products.
