chapalang mix

1. NOUN

/ tʃæpəˈlɑŋ mɪks / (AmE), / tʃəpəˈlæŋ mɪks / (BrE)
an eclectic or diverse collection of different kinds or types, especially in an informal or haphazard way
diverse or eclectic mix
informal

Examples

The party had a chapalang mix of decorations from different cultures.
She wore a chapalang mix of clothing styles that somehow worked together.
My playlist is a chapalang mix of pop, jazz, and classical music.

Synset Examples

We found a chapalang mix of flavors in that dish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hodgepodge - a confused mixture
  • Synonym: medley - a varied mixture of people or things
  • Synonym: blend - a mixture of different substances or other things

Etymology

From colloquial Malaysian/Singaporean English 'chapalang', of uncertain origin, probably from Cantonese 雜冷 (zaap6 laang5, 'mixed') or Hokkien 雜冷 (cha̍p-lâng, 'random, mixed').
