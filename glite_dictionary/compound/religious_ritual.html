religious ritual

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈrɪtʃuəl / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈrɪtjʊəl / (BrE)
a ceremony or set of actions performed according to religious tradition or custom
ceremony with religious significance

Examples

The religious ritual included prayers, singing, and the lighting of candles.
During the festival, villagers participate in an ancient religious ritual to honor their ancestors.
He found comfort in the familiar patterns of his weekly religious ritual.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ceremony - a formal event performed on a special occasion
  • Synonym: rite - a religious or other ceremonial practice or act
