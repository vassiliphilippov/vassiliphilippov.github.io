young man

1. NOUN

/ jʌŋ mæn / (AmE), / jʌŋ mæn / (BrE)
a male who is a teenager or a young adult
teenage or young adult male

Examples

The young man waited anxiously for his job interview to begin.
A group of young men gathered at the park for a game of football.
As a young man, he dreamed of traveling the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boy - a male child or young male person
  • Antonym: young woman - a teenage or young adult female
