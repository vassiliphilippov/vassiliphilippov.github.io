roasted turkey

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊstɪd ˈtɝːki / (AmE), / ˈrəʊstɪd ˈtɜːki / (BrE)
Turkey meat that has been cooked by roasting, typically served as a main dish.
turkey cooked by roasting

Examples

We enjoyed roasted turkey with stuffing and cranberry sauce on Thanksgiving.
The chef prepared a delicious roasted turkey for the restaurant's special dinner.
Leftover roasted turkey can be used in sandwiches or salads the next day.

Synset Examples

He roasted a turkey that fed the entire family.

Related Terms
