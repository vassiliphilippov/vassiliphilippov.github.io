mental representation

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛntəl ˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɛntl ˌrɛprɪzɛnˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
an idea, image, or concept held in the mind to represent something perceived, remembered, or imagined
an idea or image in the mind

Examples

Our thoughts about the world are shaped by our mental representations of it.
A child's mental representation of numbers evolves as they learn arithmetic.
Dreams are sometimes seen as vivid mental representations created during sleep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idea - a thought or suggestion about a possible course of action
  • Synonym: concept - an abstract idea; a general notion
