squit of laughter

1. NOUN

/ skwɪt əv ˈlæftɚ / (AmE), / skwɪt əv ˈlɑːftə / (BrE)
a short, sudden burst of laughter
sudden short burst of laughter

Examples

She let out a squit of laughter when she saw the surprise.
The comedian's joke drew a squit of laughter from the audience.
He suppressed a squit of laughter during the serious meeting.

Synset Examples

She let out a squit of laughter at the joke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snort of laughter - a sudden, explosive sound made while laughing
