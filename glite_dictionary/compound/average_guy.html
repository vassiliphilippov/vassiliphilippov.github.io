average guy

1. NOUN

/ ˈævərɪdʒ ɡaɪ / (AmE), / ˈævərɪdʒ ɡaɪ / (BrE)
an ordinary or typical man; a man who is not outstanding in any particular way
ordinary, typical man

Examples

Despite his fame, Tom still thinks of himself as just an average guy.
The movie tells the story of an average guy who becomes a hero.
Most voters consider themselves to be the average guy, not too rich or too poor.

Synset Examples

Everyone knows Joe Random is just an average guy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: everyman - an ordinary or typical person, especially a man
  • Synonym: regular guy - an ordinary or typical man
