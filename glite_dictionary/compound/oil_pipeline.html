oil pipeline

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl ˈpaɪp.laɪn / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈpaɪp.laɪn / (BrE)
a large pipe or system of pipes used to transport oil over long distances
pipe system for transporting oil

Examples

The new oil pipeline will help transport crude from the fields to the refinery.
Several countries depend on the transnational oil pipeline for energy imports.
A major spill occurred when the oil pipeline ruptured near the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pipeline - a long pipe for conveying oil, gas, etc. over long distances
