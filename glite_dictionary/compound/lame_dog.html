lame dog

1. NOUN

/ leɪm dɔg / (AmE), / leɪm dɒg / (BrE)
a person who is weak or disadvantaged, often one in need of help or support
a weak or disadvantaged person

Examples

The charity's mission is to offer hope to every lame dog in need of a second chance.
He was always the first to help a lame dog struggling in the company.
Society should not turn its back on a lame dog just because they are vulnerable.

Synset Examples

It's important to help a lame dog over a stile when needed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underdog - a person or group expected to lose a contest or struggle
