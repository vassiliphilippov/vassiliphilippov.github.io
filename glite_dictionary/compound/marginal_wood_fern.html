marginal wood fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːrdʒɪnəl wʊd fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈmɑːdʒɪnəl wʊd fɜːn / (BrE)
a North American fern with evergreen fronds, commonly found in forests and shady areas
a North American evergreen fern

Examples

The marginal wood fern is a common sight in shaded, moist North American forests.
Gardeners value the marginal wood fern for its resilient, evergreen fronds.
We identified several marginal wood ferns growing alongside the hiking trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fern - non-flowering plant with feathery leaves
