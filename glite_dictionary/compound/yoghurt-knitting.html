yoghurt-knitting

1. NOUN

/ ˈjoʊgərt ˌnɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈjɒɡət ˌnɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the (humorous) activity of knitting with yoghurt, used as a whimsical or satirical reference to eccentric hobbies
satirical or whimsical craft; knitting with yoghurt

Examples

At the quirky arts festival, she organized a yoghurt-knitting workshop that drew curious onlookers.
His friends joked about joining the yoghurt-knitting club to try something truly unique.
Some satirical articles mention yoghurt-knitting as the latest trend in eccentric crafts.

Synset Examples

She spent the weekend yoghurt-knitting at the community center.

Yoghurt-knitting has become a popular hobby among retirees.

Etymology

Formed from 'yoghurt' and 'knitting', as a tongue-in-cheek compound to suggest a highly impractical hobby.
