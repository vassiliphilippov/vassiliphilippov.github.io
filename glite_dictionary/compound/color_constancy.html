color constancy

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlər ˈkɑnstənsi / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ˈkɒnstənsi / (BrE)
the phenomenon by which a color appears unchanged to the human eye under different lighting conditions
color perceived as constant under varying light

Examples

Thanks to color constancy, the color of the apple looked the same in sunlight and in the shade.
Artists often rely on their understanding of color constancy when choosing colors for paintings.
The phenomenon of color constancy helps us recognize objects despite changes in lighting.
