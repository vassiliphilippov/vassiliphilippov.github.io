Lolo language

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊloʊ ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈləʊləʊ ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Loloish language spoken by the Yi (Lolo) people in China, Vietnam, and Thailand
a language of the Yi/Lolo people in Asia

Examples

The Lolo language has several distinct dialects spoken across different provinces.
Researchers are working to document the grammar and vocabulary of the Lolo language.
Her grandmother taught her traditional songs in the Lolo language.
