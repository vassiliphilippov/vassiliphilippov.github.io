fauxgressive attitude

1. NOUN

/ foʊˈɡrɛsɪv ˈætɪˌtjud / (AmE), / fəʊˈɡrɛsɪv ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
An insincere or pretended progressive stance, especially to appear socially conscious without genuine commitment to progressive values.
insincere progressive stance
informal

Examples

Many criticized the politician for his fauxgressive attitude during the campaign.
Her fauxgressive attitude was obvious when she spoke about equality but never acted on it.
Online debates often call out influencers with a fauxgressive attitude who only support causes when it's trending.

Synset Examples

His fauxgressive attitude didn't impress the real activists.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: progressive - favoring or implementing social reform or new ideas

Etymology

Blend of 'faux' (French for 'false') and 'progressive', plus 'attitude'.
