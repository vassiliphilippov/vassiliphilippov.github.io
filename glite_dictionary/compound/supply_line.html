supply line

1. NOUN

/ səˈplaɪ laɪn / (AmE), / səˈplaɪ laɪn / (BrE)
a route along which supplies are transported to a particular destination, especially for military or logistical purposes
route for delivering supplies

Examples

The army's supply line was cut off, leaving the soldiers without food or ammunition.
After the hurricane, maintaining an open supply line was crucial for getting aid to affected areas.
Any disruption to the supply line can cause significant delays in production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supply route - a route used for transporting supplies
