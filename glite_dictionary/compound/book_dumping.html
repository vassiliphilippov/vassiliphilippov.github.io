book dumping

1. NOUN

/ bʊk ˈdʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / bʊk ˈdʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of discarding books irresponsibly, often in public or unofficial places
irresponsible discarding of books

Examples

The city council has introduced penalties to discourage book dumping in parks and alleyways.
Recent book dumping incidents have left piles of unsold textbooks outside the school gate.
Volunteers organized a cleanup after complaints about book dumping behind the community center.

Synset Examples

The library faced issues with book dumping in the alley.
