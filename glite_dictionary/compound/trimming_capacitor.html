trimming capacitor

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɪmɪŋ kəˈpæsɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈtrɪmɪŋ kəˈpæsɪtə / (BrE)
a small variable capacitor used to make fine adjustments in an electronic circuit
variable capacitor for fine adjustments

Examples

The technician adjusted the trimming capacitor to fine-tune the receiver's frequency response.
A faulty trimming capacitor can cause instability in sensitive electronic circuits.
To achieve the correct oscillation, engineers often rely on a trimming capacitor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: variable capacitor - a capacitor whose capacitance can be adjusted
