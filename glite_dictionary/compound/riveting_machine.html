riveting machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪvətɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈrɪvɪtɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine designed to drive or install rivets into materials, typically used in construction or manufacturing
machine for installing rivets

Examples

The factory upgraded its equipment to include a state-of-the-art riveting machine for assembling large metal panels.
A skilled operator can use the riveting machine to join thousands of parts each day.
The maintenance team performed a routine inspection on the riveting machine to ensure its safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rivet gun - a hand-held tool for driving rivets
