Winnie the Pooh

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪni ðə pu / (AmE), / ˈwɪni ði ˈpuː / (BrE)
A fictional teddy bear and the main character in A.A. Milne's children's stories, known for his gentle and friendly nature.
fictional bear from A.A. Milne's stories

Examples

My sister has a collection of Winnie the Pooh books from her childhood.
The new Winnie the Pooh movie was a hit with children and adults alike.
He decorated his baby's room with a Winnie the Pooh theme.

Synset Examples

He dressed as Winnie the Pooh for the costume party.
Winnie the Pooh always reminds me of childhood innocence.

Etymology

Named by author A.A. Milne after his son's teddy bear, 'Winnie', and a swan called 'Pooh'.
