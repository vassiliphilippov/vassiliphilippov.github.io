baby-walker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪbi ˌwɔkər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbi ˌwɔːkə / (BrE)
a frame on wheels or casters that supports a baby learning to walk
device for helping babies learn to walk

Examples

We bought a baby-walker to help our daughter move around more easily.
The pediatrician advised against using a baby-walker due to possible safety concerns.
She pushed the baby-walker across the living room, smiling at her new independence.
