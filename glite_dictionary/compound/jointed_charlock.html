jointed charlock

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɔɪntɪd ˈtʃɑrˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪntɪd ˈtʃɑːlɒk / (BrE)
A Eurasian weed with yellow, mauve, or white flowers and podlike fruits.
Eurasian weed with podlike fruits

Examples

The field was overrun with jointed charlock, crowding out the young crops.
Gardeners often struggle to control the spread of jointed charlock in fertile soil.
I recognized the yellow blooms of jointed charlock growing by the riverbank.
