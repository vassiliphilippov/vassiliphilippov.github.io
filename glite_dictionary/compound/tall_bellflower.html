tall bellflower

1. NOUN

/ tɔl ˈbɛlˌflaʊər / (AmE), / tɔːl ˈbɛlˌflaʊə / (BrE)
a North American plant with tall spikes of blue or white bell-shaped flowers, found as both annual and perennial varieties
a North American plant with tall blue or white flowers

Examples

We found several tall bellflower plants blooming along the forest path.
The garden was brightened by clusters of tall bellflowers swaying in the wind.
She identified the wildflower as a tall bellflower because of its distinctive blue blossoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bellflower - any plant of the genus Campanula with bell-shaped flowers
