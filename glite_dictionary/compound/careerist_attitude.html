careerist attitude

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɪrɪst æˈtɪtjud / (AmE), / kəˈrɪərɪst ˈætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
an approach to one's job marked by an excessive focus on personal advancement, often at the expense of others or wider goals
self-serving approach to one's job

Examples

Many people found his careerist attitude off-putting in a team-focused environment.
She was criticized for displaying a careerist attitude rather than genuine commitment to the project's goals.
A careerist attitude can sometimes lead to short-term gains but damage long-term relationships.

Synset Examples

Her careerist attitude alienated her coworkers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambition - a strong desire to succeed or achieve something
  • Antonym: altruistic attitude - an approach marked by selfless concern for others
