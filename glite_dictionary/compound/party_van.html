party van

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑɹ.ti væn / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.ti væn / (BrE)
a vehicle equipped and decorated for group celebrations and social gatherings, often rented for events or outings
vehicle for group celebrations

Examples

We decorated the party van with balloons and streamers for the bachelorette party.
After the concert, everyone piled into the party van to keep the celebration going.
They hired a party van to take the group around the city for the birthday tour.

Synset Examples

They rented a party van for their weekend getaway.
We loaded the party van with drinks and snacks.
The kids arrived in a colorful party van for the celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party bus - a bus modified for group celebrations
