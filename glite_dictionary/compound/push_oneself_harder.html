to push oneself harder

1. VERB

/ pʊʃ wʌnˈsɛlf ˈhɑrdɚ / (AmE), / pʊʃ wʌnˈsɛlf ˈhɑːdə / (BrE)
to make a greater effort or force oneself to work harder
exert more effort; work harder

Examples

If I want to improve my running time, I have to push myself harder during training.
She realized she hadn’t achieved her goals because she didn’t push herself harder in school.
John promised to push himself harder this year to earn a promotion.

Synset Examples

I need to push myself harder to finish this project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exert oneself - to make a physical or mental effort
