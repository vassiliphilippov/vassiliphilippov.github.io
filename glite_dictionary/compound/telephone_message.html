telephone message

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlɪˌfoʊn ˈmɛsɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn ˈmɛsɪdʒ / (BrE)
a message conveyed or communicated by telephone, especially one left for someone who is not available to answer the call
message communicated by telephone

Examples

Please leave a telephone message if I do not answer the call.
She received an important telephone message from her doctor this morning.
After several missed calls, there was finally a telephone message waiting for him at the front desk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: voicemail - a recorded message left by a caller when the recipient is unavailable
