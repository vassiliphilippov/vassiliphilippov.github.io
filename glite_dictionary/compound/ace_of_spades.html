ace of spades

1. NOUN

/ eɪs əv speɪdz / (AmE), / eɪs əv speɪdz / (BrE)
the playing card with a single spade symbol and an 'A', sometimes associated with bad luck or death
the ace card in the spade suit

Examples

He drew the ace of spades and everyone at the table gasped.
The ace of spades is often seen as an unlucky card in some cultures.
She kept the ace of spades tucked inside her book as a good luck charm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ace of hearts - the ace in the heart suit
  • Synonym: spade - one of the four suits in a deck of playing cards, symbolized by a black leaf-like shape
