Indian file

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən faɪl / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən faɪl / (BrE)
a line of people or things arranged one behind another
line in single file, one behind another

Examples

The children walked in Indian file through the narrow forest path.
We proceeded down the hallway in Indian file to avoid blocking the way.
Bicyclists rode in Indian file to stay safe on the busy road.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: single file - a line of people or things arranged one behind another

Etymology

From the observation of Native Americans moving in single file, as described by early European settlers.
