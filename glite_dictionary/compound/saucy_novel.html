saucy novel

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔsi ˈnɑvəl / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsi ˈnɒvəl / (BrE)
a book that is lively, sexually suggestive, or mildly indecent in content
lively or risque book

Examples

The new author became famous for her saucy novel about the adventures of a rebellious heroine.
Critics described the book as a saucy novel that pushed the boundaries of its time.
She blushed when she discovered that her grandmother owned a saucy novel from the 1920s.

Synset Examples

The saucy novel kept me up all night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: risqué novel - a novel considered slightly indecent or suggestive
