leatherleaf wood fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛðərˌlif wʊd fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈlɛðəˌliːf wʊd fɜːn / (BrE)
A North American fern with evergreen fronds, often found in woodlands.
North American evergreen fern

Examples

The leatherleaf wood fern thrives in shady woodland environments throughout North America.
Gardeners value the leatherleaf wood fern for its attractive, evergreen fronds.
Hikers easily recognize the leatherleaf wood fern by the leathery texture of its leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fern - a nonflowering vascular plant with feathery fronds
