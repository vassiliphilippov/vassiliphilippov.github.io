tinned goods

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪnd ɡʊdz / (AmE), / ˈtɪnd ɡʊdz / (BrE)
food items that have been preserved and sealed in cans for storage and later consumption.
food preserved in cans

Examples

The food bank is asking for donations of tinned goods to help families in need.
She stocked her pantry with tinned goods to prepare for winter.
Many campers rely on tinned goods because they are easy to transport and have a long shelf life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: canned goods - food preserved by canning, especially in the US
