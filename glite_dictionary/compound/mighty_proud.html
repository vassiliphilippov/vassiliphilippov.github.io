mighty proud

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaɪdi praʊd / (AmE), / ˈmaɪti praʊd / (BrE)
Feeling extremely proud; very proud or pleased with oneself or one's achievements.
very proud; extremely pleased
informal

Examples

She was mighty proud of her son for graduating with honors.
After winning the contest, he walked around mighty proud of himself.
The team felt mighty proud after their hard-fought victory.

Synset Examples

He felt mighty proud of his accomplishment.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: proud - feeling deep pleasure or satisfaction as a result of achievements
