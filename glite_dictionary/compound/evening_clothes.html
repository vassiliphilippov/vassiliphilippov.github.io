evening clothes

1. NOUN

/ ˈivnɪŋ kloʊðz / (AmE), / ˈiːvnɪŋ kləʊðz / (BrE)
formal attire worn on special occasions in the evening, such as tuxedos or evening gowns
formal attire for evening events

Examples

He wore his evening clothes to the charity gala.
Please remember to bring your evening clothes for the banquet tonight.
Her evening clothes included a long black dress and diamond earrings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: formal wear - clothing appropriate for formal events
  • Antonym: casual clothes - clothes suitable for informal occasions
