Sherwood Forest

1. PROPN

/ ˈʃɝː.wʊd ˈfɔːr.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈʃɜː.wʊd ˈfɒr.ɪst / (BrE)
an ancient forest in central England, formerly a royal hunting ground and famously associated with the legend of Robin Hood
ancient forest in central England, home of Robin Hood legend

Examples

Many tourists visit Sherwood Forest each year to explore its legendary connection to Robin Hood.
The ancient oaks of Sherwood Forest are protected for future generations.
Local folklore is alive and well in the villages surrounding Sherwood Forest.
