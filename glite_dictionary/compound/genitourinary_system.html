genitourinary system

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒɛnɪtoʊˈjʊrəˌnɛri ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛnɪtəʊˈjʊərəˌnɛri ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
the system of organs involved in reproduction and the formation and excretion of urine
reproductive and urinary organ system
medical

Examples

Diseases affecting the genitourinary system can impact both urinary and reproductive health.
The doctor specializes in conditions of the genitourinary system.
Proper functioning of the genitourinary system is essential for waste removal and reproduction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urinary system - system of organs for producing and excreting urine
  • Synonym: reproductive system - system of organs involved in reproduction

Etymology

From Latin 'genitus' (born) and 'urinarius' (pertaining to urine).
