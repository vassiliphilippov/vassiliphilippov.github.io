line triple

1. NOUN

/ laɪn ˈtrɪpəl / (AmE), / laɪn ˈtrɪpəl / (BrE)
(baseball) A triple hit achieved by striking a line drive.
baseball triple via line drive

Examples

The crowd cheered when he hit a line triple to center field.
Her line triple in the seventh inning sparked the team's comeback.
After his impressive line triple, two runners scored easily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triple - a hit in baseball that allows the batter to reach third base
  • Synonym: line drive - a ball hit hard and straight in baseball
