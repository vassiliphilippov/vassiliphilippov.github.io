doorlock

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔɹˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈdɔːˌlɒk / (BrE)
A lock fitted to a door, especially one designed for exterior doors to provide security.
lock fitted to a door

Examples

Make sure you replace the doorlock if it shows any sign of wear.
He forgot to turn the key in the doorlock before leaving.
A sturdy doorlock is essential for home security.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lock - a mechanical or electronic fastening device used to secure doors, containers, or other enclosures
