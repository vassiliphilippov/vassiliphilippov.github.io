flat bench

1. NOUN

/ flæt bɛntʃ / (AmE), / flæt bɛntʃ / (BrE)
a sturdy, horizontal bench on which a person lies while performing weightlifting or exercise routines, especially bench presses
exercise bench for weightlifting

Examples

He performed three sets of bench presses on the flat bench at the gym.
Please wipe down the flat bench after your workout to keep it clean for others.
The new gym has a flat bench and several adjustable benches for different exercises.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bench press - an exercise performed lying on a bench, pushing a weight upward from chest level
