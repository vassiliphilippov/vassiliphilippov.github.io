railroad woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪlˌrɑd ˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈreɪlˌrɔːd ˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who is employed to work for a railroad company.
female railroad employee

Examples

The first railroad woman in her town inspired many young girls to pursue the same career.
As a railroad woman, she worked long hours ensuring the trains ran on time.
The documentary featured stories from a railroad woman who managed freight schedules during World War II.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: railroad man - A man employed by a railroad company
