management consulting

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænɪdʒmənt kənˈsʌltɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒmənt kənˈsʌltɪŋ / (BrE)
the professional practice of providing expert advice to organizations aimed at improving their management and business performance
professional business advice service

Examples

After several years in finance, she decided to switch to management consulting for greater career flexibility.
Many companies turn to management consulting firms during periods of major organizational change.
He built a reputation for excellence in management consulting by helping clients increase efficiency and growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consulting - the practice of giving expert advice in a particular field
