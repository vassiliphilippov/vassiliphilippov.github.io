horny jail

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrni dʒeɪl / (AmE), / ˈhɔːni dʒeɪl / (BrE)
a humorous term for imagined punishment or exile imposed on someone for making sexual remarks or behaving flirtatiously, especially online
humorous term for punishment for sexual remarks/flirting
informal slang

Examples

After making a series of questionable jokes, his friends sent him straight to horny jail.
Posting those comments is a one-way ticket to horny jail these days.
She playfully threatened to lock him in horny jail for his flirty messages.
