peroxide blond

1. NOUN

/ pəˈrɑːksaɪd blɑːnd / (AmE), / pəˈrɒksaɪd blɒnd / (BrE)
a person whose blond hair is achieved by bleaching with hydrogen peroxide
person with bleached blond hair

Examples

She became a peroxide blond after changing her hair color for the movie role.
Rumor had it that the new model was actually a natural brunette, not a peroxide blond.
The fashion magazine featured a striking peroxide blond on its cover this month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blond - person with pale yellow hair
