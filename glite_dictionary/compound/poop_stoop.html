poop stoop

1. NOUN

/ pup stuːp / (AmE), / puːp stuːp / (BrE)
an area, often in a yard or public space, designated for the collection or cleaning of dog waste
area for dog waste collection

Examples

Every week, she had to clean the poop stoop behind the apartment building.
Signs directed dog owners to use the poop stoop at the park's entrance.
The community installed a new poop stoop to keep the sidewalks clean.

Synset Examples

Cleaning the poop stoop was the worst part of dog ownership.
