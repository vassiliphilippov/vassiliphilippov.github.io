practice of medicine

1. NOUN

/ ˈpræk.tɪs əv ˈmɛd.ə.sɪn / (AmE), / ˈpræk.tɪs əv ˈmed.ɪ.sɪn / (BrE)
the professional pursuit and application of medical knowledge, especially by physicians, to prevent, diagnose, treat, or cure diseases and injuries
the work of a medical doctor or physician

Examples

He moved to a rural area to establish his own practice of medicine.
The practice of medicine requires not only scientific knowledge but also compassion.
Ethical dilemmas often arise in the practice of medicine.

Synset Examples

he studied medicine at Harvard

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medicine - the science or practice of diagnosing, treating, and preventing diseases
  • Synonym: medical profession - the body of persons engaged in the practice of medicine
