detective story

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈtɛktɪv ˌstɔri / (AmE), / dɪˈtɛktɪv ˌstɔːri / (BrE)
a story or novel in which a detective investigates and solves a crime
crime-solving narrative or novel

Examples

She curled up with a classic detective story on a rainy afternoon.
The detective story kept me guessing until the very last page.
Agatha Christie is known for her brilliant detective stories featuring Hercule Poirot and Miss Marple.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mystery - a novel or story focusing on solving a mysterious crime or event
  • Degree Higher: thriller - a novel or story with exciting, suspenseful events
