blue-winged teal

1. NOUN

/ bluː wɪŋd tiːl / (AmE), / bluː wɪŋd tiːl / (BrE)
a small duck (Anas discors) found in North America, recognized by the blue patch on its wings
a North American species of duck

Examples

We spotted a flock of blue-winged teal resting by the marsh at sunrise.
The blue-winged teal is known for its striking blue wing patches and quick flight.
During migration, blue-winged teal are among the first ducks to head south.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teal - a small freshwater duck
