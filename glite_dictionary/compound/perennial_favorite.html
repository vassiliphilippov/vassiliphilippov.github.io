perennial favorite

1. NOUN

/ pəˈrɛniəl ˈfeɪvərɪt / (AmE), / pəˈrɛniəl ˈfeɪvərɪt / (BrE)
an item, person, or choice that is consistently liked or preferred over a long period
something consistently liked or preferred

Examples

Pumpkin pie is a perennial favorite during the holiday season.
The local band's music is a perennial favorite at community events.
Chocolate remains a perennial favorite among children and adults alike.

Synset Examples

Roses are her perennial favorite for any occasion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all-time favorite - something regarded as the best or most loved at any time

Etymology

From Latin "perennis" (lasting throughout the year) and "favorite," meaning one preferred above others.
