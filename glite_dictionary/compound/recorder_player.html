recorder player

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈkɔrdər ˈpleɪər / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːdə ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays the recorder musical instrument, especially as a performer or enthusiast
person who plays the recorder

Examples

The school hired a talented recorder player to teach the children about Baroque music.
As a recorder player, she often performs at local music festivals.
He became a skilled recorder player after years of practicing daily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recorder - a woodwind musical instrument with a whistle mouthpiece
