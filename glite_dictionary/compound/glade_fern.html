glade fern

1. NOUN

/ ɡleɪd fɝn / (AmE), / ɡleɪd fɜːn / (BrE)
A North American fern with narrow fronds and yellowish leafstalks, commonly found in moist, wooded areas.
a North American fern species

Examples

The glade fern thrives in shady woodland environments throughout eastern North America.
I spotted several glade ferns near the creek during our hike.
Gardeners often choose the glade fern for its delicate, elegant appearance in shaded garden beds.
