plate compactor

1. NOUN

/ pleɪt kəmˈpæktər / (AmE), / pleɪt kəmˈpæktə / (BrE)
an engine-powered machine with a flat plate used to compact soil, gravel, or sand in construction work
engine-powered machine for compacting soil and materials

Examples

The workers used a plate compactor to pack down the gravel before laying the asphalt.
Renting a plate compactor is essential for any patio or driveway installation project.
After pouring the sand, we ran a plate compactor across the surface to create a solid base.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compactor - a machine or device for compressing material
