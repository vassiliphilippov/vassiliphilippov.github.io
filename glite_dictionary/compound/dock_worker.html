dock worker

1. NOUN

/ dɑk ˈwɝkɚ / (AmE), / dɒk ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person employed to load and unload ships at a dock or port
laborer who loads/unloads ships

Examples

The dock worker began unloading crates as soon as the ship arrived.
After years as a dock worker, he became skilled at operating heavy machinery.
Every morning, hundreds of dock workers gather at the port to start their shift.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stevedore - person employed at a dock to load and unload ships
  • Synonym: longshoreman - a laborer who works on the docks loading and unloading ships
