carob bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛrəb bɑr / (AmE), / ˈkærəb bɑː / (BrE)
A bar-shaped confection made with carob powder, often used as a chocolate substitute.
chocolate-like candy bar made with carob

Examples

She packed a carob bar in her child's lunch as a healthy alternative to chocolate.
Many people with chocolate allergies enjoy snacking on a carob bar.
The carob bar tasted sweet and slightly earthy compared to regular chocolate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chocolate bar - a bar of candy made with chocolate

Etymology

From 'carob', ultimately from Arabic kharrūb, and 'bar' as in bar-shaped confection.
