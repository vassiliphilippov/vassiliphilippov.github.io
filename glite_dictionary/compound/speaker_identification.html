speaker identification

1. NOUN

/ ˈspiːkər ˌaɪˌdɛntəfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈspiːkə ˌaɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of identifying a person by analyzing the sound and characteristics of their voice
identifying a person by their voice

Examples

To enhance security, the company implemented speaker identification to grant access to sensitive areas.
Modern banking apps sometimes use speaker identification to authenticate users over the phone.
Researchers are improving speaker identification systems to distinguish between similar-sounding voices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: voice recognition - automatic identification or verification of a person using their voice
