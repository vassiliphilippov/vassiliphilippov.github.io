Rock Cornish

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈkɔrnɪʃ / (AmE), / rɒk ˈkɔːnɪʃ / (BrE)
a small, plump chicken breed developed by crossbreeding Plymouth Rock and Cornish fowl, often raised for meat
a crossbred chicken, valued for meat

Examples

The restaurant served roasted Rock Cornish with seasonal vegetables.
Many gourmet recipes recommend using Rock Cornish hens for their tender meat.
We bought a pair of Rock Cornish hens for the holiday meal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cornish hen - a small breed of chicken, often used interchangeably with Rock Cornish

Etymology

Named from its parent breeds: 'Plymouth Rock' and 'Cornish'.
