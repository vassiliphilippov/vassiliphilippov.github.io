toxic behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑksɪk bəˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɒksɪk bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
harmful or poisonous actions or attitudes towards others, often causing emotional or psychological damage
harmful or poisonous conduct

Examples

Her toxic behavior made the work environment unbearable for everyone.
Repeated toxic behavior can destroy friendships and trust.
He recognized his own toxic behavior and decided to make positive changes.

Synset Examples

Her toxic behavior drove everyone away from her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abusive behavior - actions characterized by abuse, often emotional or physical
  • Antonym: supportive behavior - actions that provide help or encouragement to others
