Battle of Guadalcanal

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬l əv ˌɡwɑd.əl.kəˈnæl / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl əv ˌɡwæd.əl.kəˈnal / (BrE)
a major battle in World War II (1942–1943) fought in the Pacific, where American forces recaptured Guadalcanal Island from Japanese occupation.
World War II battle in the Pacific (1942–1943)

Examples

The Battle of Guadalcanal marked a turning point in the Pacific Theater of World War II.
Many soldiers lost their lives during the fierce fighting at the Battle of Guadalcanal.
Historians continue to study the strategies used in the Battle of Guadalcanal.
