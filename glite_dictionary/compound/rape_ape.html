rape ape

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪp eɪp / (AmE), / ˈreɪp eɪp / (BrE)
A highly offensive and derogatory term referring to a person who commits sexual violence, or as a slur invoking harmful stereotypes.
derogatory slur; sexual violence perpetrator
