business calling card

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs ˈkɔlɪŋ kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbɪznəs ˈkɔːlɪŋ kɑːd / (BrE)
a small card bearing a person's professional contact information, typically exchanged in a business context
professional contact information card

Examples

She handed her business calling card to the client after their meeting.
You should always bring a business calling card to networking events.
His business calling card features his name, job title, and company logo.

Synset Examples

She always carries her business calling card for networking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: business card - a small card with a person's professional details for business purposes
