biological warfare defence

1. NOUN

/ baɪəˈlɑdʒɪkəl ˈwɔrfɛr dɪˈfɛns / (AmE), / baɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˈwɔːféə dɪˈfɛns / (BrE)
Measures and strategies designed to protect people and resources from attacks involving biological agents used as weapons.
defense against biological weapons

Examples

The military invested heavily in biological warfare defence systems during the conflict.
Recent advances in biological warfare defence have improved the detection of harmful pathogens.
Many countries collaborate on biological warfare defence to prevent large-scale outbreaks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biological warfare - use of biological toxins or pathogens as weapons
  • Synonym: chemical warfare defence - defense against chemical warfare
