research project

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsɝːtʃ ˈprɑːdʒɛkt / (AmE), / rɪˈsɜːtʃ ˈprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
a planned and organized investigation undertaken to answer specific scientific or academic questions
planned scientific investigation

Examples

She is leading a research project on renewable energy sources at the university.
Funding for his research project was provided by the National Science Foundation.
Their research project aims to improve early diagnosis of the disease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: experiment - a scientific procedure to test a hypothesis
