coral drops

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrəl drɑps / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəl drɒps / (BrE)
a half-hardy Mexican plant, cultivated for its drooping clusters of showy red-and-white flowers
ornamental Mexican plant with red-and-white flowers

Examples

The vibrant coral drops in the garden attracted many hummingbirds with their bright flowers.
Gardeners recommend planting coral drops for a splash of color in late summer borders.
She admired the delicate clusters of coral drops swaying gently in the afternoon breeze.
