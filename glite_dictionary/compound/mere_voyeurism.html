mere voyeurism

1. NOUN

/ mɪr ˈvɔɪjərˌɪzəm / (AmE), / mɪə ˈvɔɪjərɪzəm / (BrE)
a superficial or unengaged interest in observing others, especially lacking depth or genuine involvement
superficial interest in others' experiences

Examples

The documentary was criticized for being mere voyeurism rather than meaningful journalism.
Some reality shows are often accused of encouraging mere voyeurism among viewers.
She worried that reading the diary would be mere voyeurism instead of a tribute to the author.

Synset Examples

He dismissed the show as mere voyeurism without depth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: voyeurism - the practice of gaining pleasure from watching others, especially in private situations
  • Degree Lower: curiosity - a strong desire to know or learn something
