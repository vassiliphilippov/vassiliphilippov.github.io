dead ahead

1. ADV

/ dɛd əˈhɛd / (AmE), / dɛd əˈhɛd / (BrE)
directly in front; exactly ahead.
directly ahead of you

Examples

The mountain peak was dead ahead as they hiked through the valley.
If you keep driving straight, the school will be dead ahead.
She pointed to a ship dead ahead on the horizon.

Synset Examples

the laboratory is dead ahead

Related Terms

  • Synonym: straight ahead - directly in front
  • Antonym: to the side - not directly ahead; to the left or right

Etymology

From 'dead' meaning 'exactly' + 'ahead'.
