blue cardinal flower

1. NOUN

/ blu ˈkɑrdɪnəl ˈflaʊɚ / (AmE), / bluː ˈkɑːdɪnl ˈflaʊə / (BrE)
a tall perennial herb of eastern North America with dense spikes of blue flowers
tall North American herb with blue flowers

Examples

The blue cardinal flower adds a vivid splash of color to shaded gardens in late summer.
Hikers along the river were delighted to spot several blooming blue cardinal flowers near the water's edge.
Botanists studied the habitat of the blue cardinal flower to monitor its population in the wild.
