Malto language

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæltoʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmæltəʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Dravidian language spoken by the Malto people in eastern India
language of the Malto people

Examples

The Malto language is still spoken by thousands in the hills of Jharkhand.
Researchers have compiled a dictionary of the Malto language.
Children in the village learn Hindi as well as their native Malto language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dravidian language - a language from the Dravidian family
