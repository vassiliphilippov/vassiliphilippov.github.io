common cockscomb

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈkɑksˌkoʊm / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈkɒksˌkəʊm / (BrE)
A garden annual plant (Celosia argentea var. cristata) with bright red or yellow featherlike flower spikes, widely grown for its ornamental value.
garden plant with bright, featherlike flowers

Examples

The vibrant red blooms of the common cockscomb add color to many summer gardens.
She planted common cockscomb along the walkway for their striking appearance.
Children love the unusual shapes of the common cockscomb flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cockscomb - plant with a crested, comb-like flower head (Celosia)
