part name

1. NOUN

/ pɑrt neɪm / (AmE), / pɑːt neɪm / (BrE)
a word that denotes a part or component of a larger whole
word for a part of a whole

Examples

In anatomy, 'femur' is a part name for the thigh bone.
Knowing the part name of a machine helps in identifying its components for repair.
Terms like 'wing', 'tail', and 'beak' are part names for birds.

Synset Examples

‘brim’ and ‘crown’ are meronyms of ‘hat’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meronym - a word denoting a part of something
