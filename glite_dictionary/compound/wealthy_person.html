wealthy person

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛlθi ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈwɛlθi ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who has a great deal of money, property, or other assets; a rich person
person who has a lot of money or assets

Examples

The charity relies on donations from at least one wealthy person each year.
She grew up as a wealthy person in a privileged neighborhood.
Many believe that being a wealthy person brings happiness, but that's not always the case.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rich person - a person who is rich; has a lot of money or assets
  • Antonym: pauper - a very poor person
