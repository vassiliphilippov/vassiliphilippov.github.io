social networking service

1. NOUN

/ ˌsoʊʃl ˈnɛtwɜrkɪŋ ˈsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / ˌsəʊʃl ˈnɛtwɜːkɪŋ ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a website or online platform that enables people to connect, communicate, and share content with others, typically based on shared interests or relationships
online platform for social connections

Examples

Many people use a social networking service to keep in touch with friends and family.
The company launched a new social networking service aimed at professionals.
Concerns about privacy often arise when using any social networking service.

Synset Examples

Social networking services are increasingly being used in legal and criminal investigations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: social network - a network of social interactions and personal relationships
