Gheg dialect

1. NOUN

/ ɡɛɡ ˈdaɪ.əˌlɛkt / (AmE), / ɡɛɡ ˈdaɪ.ə.lɛkt / (BrE)
the northern dialect of the Albanian language, spoken primarily in northern Albania and parts of the Balkans, including areas of the former Yugoslavia
northern dialect of Albanian

Examples

The Gheg dialect is used predominantly in northern Albania and Kosovo.
Linguists have studied the Gheg dialect to understand its historical development.
Unlike the southern Tosk variety, the Gheg dialect retains certain archaic features.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Tosk dialect - southern dialect of the Albanian language
