cheap drunk

1. NOUN

/ tʃip drʌŋk / (AmE), / tʃiːp drʌŋk / (BrE)
a person who becomes intoxicated after consuming only a small amount of alcohol
person easily made drunk
informal

Examples

Sarah is a cheap drunk and feels tipsy after just one glass of wine.
He joked that being a cheap drunk saves him money at parties.
If you're a cheap drunk, you probably shouldn't compete in drinking games.

Synset Examples

She always becomes a cheap drunk after two cocktails.
I realized I was a cheap drunk when I felt tipsy so fast.
After just one drink, he was a cheap drunk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lightweight - a person who cannot tolerate much alcohol before becoming drunk
