breathing place

1. NOUN

/ ˈbriðɪŋ pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈbriːðɪŋ pleɪs / (BrE)
A short period of rest or relief from something difficult or stressful.
short period of rest

Examples

After hours of tense negotiation, the teams finally had a breathing place to collect their thoughts.
She welcomed the breathing place offered between meetings during the busy conference.
The unexpected delay provided a much-needed breathing place in their hectic schedule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respite - a short period of rest or relief from something difficult
