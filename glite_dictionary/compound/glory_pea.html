glory pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlɔːri piː / (AmE), / ˈɡlɔːri piː / (BrE)
any of several shrubs or vines of the genus Clianthus, noted for their compound leaves and drooping racemes of bright red, pea-like flowers
a shrub or vine with red, pea-like flowers (Clianthus genus)

Examples

The botanical garden cultivated a rare glory pea in its Australian plant collection.
Brilliant red blossoms made the glory pea a favorite among local gardeners.
We learned that the glory pea is native to parts of Australia.
