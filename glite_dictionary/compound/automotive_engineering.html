automotive engineering

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔtəˌmoʊtɪv ˌɛnʤəˈnɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəməʊtɪv ˌɛnʤɪˈnɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
The branch of engineering concerned with the design, development, and manufacturing of motor vehicles and their components.
engineering field for designing automobiles

Examples

Many breakthroughs in automotive engineering have made modern cars safer and more fuel-efficient.
He studied automotive engineering at university before joining a major car manufacturer.
Advancements in electric vehicles are rapidly changing the field of automotive engineering.
