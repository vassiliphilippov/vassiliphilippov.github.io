leaf cast

1. NOUN

/ liːf kæst / (AmE), / liːf kɑːst / (BrE)
a disease of conifers in which the needles fall prematurely
disease causing needle loss in conifers

Examples

Foresters noted an increase in leaf cast affecting pine plantations this spring.
The presence of leaf cast was confirmed by examining the fallen needles under a microscope.
If left untreated, leaf cast can seriously reduce the vitality of conifer trees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: needle cast - a similar disease causing conifer needles to fall
