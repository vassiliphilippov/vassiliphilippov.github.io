camphor oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæm.fɚ ˌɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈkæm.fə ˌɔɪl / (BrE)
an essential oil obtained by distilling camphor resin, used in medicine, aromatherapy, and as a flavoring or fragrance
essential oil from camphor resin

Examples

The pharmacist recommended using camphor oil to help relieve the chest congestion.
Some traditional remedies involve massaging sore muscles with camphor oil for relief.
When diffused, camphor oil creates a refreshing aroma that can clear the air.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camphor - a white, volatile substance with a strong smell, obtained from camphor trees
