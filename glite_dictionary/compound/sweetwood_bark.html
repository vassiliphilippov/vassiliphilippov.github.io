sweetwood bark

1. NOUN

/ ˈswiːt.wʊd bɑrk / (AmE), / ˈswiːt.wʊd bɑːk / (BrE)
The aromatic bark of the cascarilla tree, used as a tonic and in making incense.
aromatic bark of the cascarilla tree

Examples

The villagers collect sweetwood bark to use in traditional remedies.
Sweetwood bark is valued for its pleasant aroma and medicinal properties.
Incense made from sweetwood bark is popular in some Caribbean cultures.
