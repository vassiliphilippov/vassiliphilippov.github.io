huge success

1. NOUN

/ hjudʒ səkˈsɛs / (AmE), / hjuːdʒ səkˈsɛs / (BrE)
a very large or significant achievement or positive outcome
very large achievement; major success

Examples

The new software launch was a huge success and exceeded everyone's expectations.
Her first novel became a huge success both critically and commercially.
After years of hard work, the festival was finally a huge success in its second year.

Synset Examples

Her presentation on climate change was a huge success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: success - the achievement of an aim or purpose
  • Antonym: failure - lack of success in achieving something
