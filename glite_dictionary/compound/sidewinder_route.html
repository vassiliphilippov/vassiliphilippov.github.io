sidewinder route

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪdˌwaɪndər ruːt / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌwaɪndə ruːt / (BrE)
an indirect or circuitous path or method, often taken to avoid obstacles or detection
an indirect path or method

Examples

He took the sidewinder route to avoid traffic on the main road.
Sometimes a sidewinder route is the best way around difficult problems.
They suspected he was using a sidewinder route instead of dealing with the issue directly.

Synset Examples

He took the sidewinder route to avoid traffic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detour - a longer or less direct route taken to avoid something or reach a destination
  • Antonym: shortcut - a quicker, more direct route

Etymology

From 'sidewinder', a type of rattlesnake known for its lateral, winding movement.
