Apostolic Palace

1. NOUN

/ əˌpɑː.stəˈlɪk ˈpæl.ɪs / (AmE), / əˌpɒs.təˈlɪk ˈpæl.ɪs / (BrE)
the official residence of the Pope in Vatican City, serving as the papal apartments and administrative offices
Pope's official residence at Vatican City

Examples

The Pope greeted visitors from the balcony of the Apostolic Palace.
Hundreds gathered in St. Peter's Square to see the lights of the Apostolic Palace at night.
Restoration work was recently completed on several rooms in the Apostolic Palace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Vatican Palace - another name for the Apostolic Palace, Pope's residence in Vatican City

Etymology

From Latin apostolicus (apostolic, relating to the apostles) and Old French palais (palace).
