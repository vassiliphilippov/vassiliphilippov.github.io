social media

1. NOUN

/ ˌsoʊʃəl ˈmiːdiə / (AmE), / ˌsəʊʃl ˈmiːdiə / (BrE)
Websites and applications that enable users to create and share content or to participate in social networking.
online platforms for sharing and interaction

Examples

She spends several hours a day on social media connecting with friends and following news updates.
The company uses social media to promote its products and engage with customers.
Many people get their information from social media rather than traditional news sources.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: social network - an online platform for building social relations
  • Degree Lower: mass media - means of communication that reach large audiences

Etymology

Formed from 'social' and 'media'; 'social' from Latin 'socialis', and 'media' from Latin 'medium'.
