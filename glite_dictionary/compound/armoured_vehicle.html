armoured vehicle

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmərd ˈviːɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈɑːməd ˈviːɪkəl / (BrE)
A vehicle fitted with armor plating for protection, especially used in military or security operations.
vehicle protected by armor plate

Examples

The convoy included several armoured vehicles to protect against attacks.
An armoured vehicle rolled down the street during the unrest.
They transported the valuables in a secure armoured vehicle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tank - a heavy armored fighting vehicle with tracks
