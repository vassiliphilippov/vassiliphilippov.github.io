mixing bowl

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪksɪŋ ˌboʊl / (AmE), / ˈmɪksɪŋ bəʊl / (BrE)
a large bowl in which ingredients are mixed, especially in cooking or baking
large bowl for mixing ingredients

Examples

She poured the flour into the mixing bowl before adding the eggs.
The recipe says to beat the butter and sugar together in a large mixing bowl.
After washing the mixing bowl, he prepared it for the next batch of dough.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowl - a round, deep dish or basin used for food or liquid
