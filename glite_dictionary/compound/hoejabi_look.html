hoejabi look

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈdʒɑːbi lʊk / (AmE), / həʊˈdʒɑːbi lʊk / (BrE)
A style or appearance that combines modest Islamic dress such as a hijab with revealing or provocative fashion elements.
modest dress mixed with provocative fashion
informal slang

Examples

Many influencers on social media have popularized the hoejabi look in recent years.
Her signature hoejabi look features bold makeup and a stylish headscarf.
The hoejabi look often sparks debate within the Muslim community about fashion and modesty.

Synset Examples

Her hoejabi look turned heads at the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hijabi - a woman who wears a hijab

Etymology

Blend of "hoe" (slang for a promiscuous woman) and "hijabi" (woman who wears the hijab).
