rabbit hutch

1. NOUN

/ ˈræbɪt ˌhʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˈræbɪt ˌhʌtʃ / (BrE)
a small structure or cage, often with a wire mesh front, designed to house rabbits
cage or shelter for rabbits

Examples

He built a spacious rabbit hutch in the backyard for his children's pets.
The old rabbit hutch needed repair before it could be used again.
She cleaned out the rabbit hutch and filled it with fresh hay.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hutch - a box or cage for keeping small animals
