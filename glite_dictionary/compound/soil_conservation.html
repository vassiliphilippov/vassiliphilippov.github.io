soil conservation

1. NOUN

/ sɔɪl ˌkɑnsərˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / sɔɪl ˌkɒnsəˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the prevention of soil erosion or deterioration through various management practices
protecting soil from erosion or degradation

Examples

Farmers are adopting soil conservation techniques to protect their land for future generations.
The government launched a soil conservation campaign to combat desertification.
Proper soil conservation helps maintain fertile farmland and prevents environmental damage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: erosion control - measures to prevent soil erosion
