totes excited

1. ADJ

/ toʊts ɪkˈsaɪtɪd / (AmE), / təʊts ɪkˈsaɪtɪd / (BrE)
(informal, slang) Very excited; extremely enthusiastic or eager about something.
very excited (informal)
informal slang

Examples

She was totes excited for the concert this weekend.
I'm totes excited to start my new job on Monday!
The kids were totes excited when they saw the puppy under the tree.

Synset Examples

She was totes excited for the concert this weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excited - very enthusiastic or eager
  • Antonym: calm - not showing excitement; relaxed

Etymology

Formed from 'totes', a slang shortening of 'totally', plus 'excited'.
