pea weevil

1. NOUN

/ pi ˈwiːvəl / (AmE), / piː ˈwiːvəl / (BrE)
A small beetle whose larvae live in and feed on the seeds of the pea plant, often causing damage to crops.
beetle larva that feeds on pea seeds

Examples

The farmer discovered that his crop was infested with pea weevils.
Several pea weevil larvae were found inside the damaged seeds.
Controlling the pea weevil is important to prevent losses in pea production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weevil - a small beetle with a long snout, many of which are pests
