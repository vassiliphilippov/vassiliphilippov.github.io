dishwashing detergent

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪʃˌwɑʃɪŋ dɪˈtɚdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈdɪʃˌwɒʃɪŋ dɪˈtɜːdʒənt / (BrE)
a cleaning agent with low suds, formulated for cleaning dishes in dishwashers
cleaning agent for dishwashers

Examples

I need to buy more dishwashing detergent before we run out.
Some dishwashing detergents are specially formulated to remove tough grease and stains.
Always check that your dishwashing detergent is suitable for automatic dishwashers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dish soap - liquid soap for washing dishes by hand
