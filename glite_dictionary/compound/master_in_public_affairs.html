Master in Public Affairs

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæstɚ ɪn ˌpʌblɪk əˈfɛrz / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstə ɪn ˌpʌblɪk əˈfeəz / (BrE)
A master's degree program focused on policy, administration, and issues of public concern.
master's degree in public policy and administration

Examples

She earned her Master in Public Affairs to pursue a career in government.
Many universities now offer a Master in Public Affairs with various specializations.
After completing his Master in Public Affairs, he started working for an international organization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Master of Public Administration - A related master's degree focused on public sector management and policy
  • Synonym: Master of Public Policy - A related master's degree focusing on the analysis and development of public policy
