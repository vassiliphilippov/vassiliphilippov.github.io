sawdust saloon

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔː.dʌst səˈluːn / (AmE), / ˈsɔː.dʌst səˈluːn / (BrE)
a saloon, especially of the past, where the floor is covered with sawdust, typically to absorb spills or keep dirt down
saloon with a sawdust-covered floor

Examples

The old Western movie featured a sawdust saloon where cowboys gathered after a long day.
Patrons loved the informal feel of the sawdust saloon with its crunchy floors and lively music.
Stories about the gold rush often mention the rough charm of a sawdust saloon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saloon - a bar or public room for drinking and socializing
