rocket cress

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑkɪt ˌkrɛs / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪt ˌkrɛs / (BrE)
a noxious plant with yellow flowers, sometimes placed in the genus Sisymbrium; also known as wild rocket or hedge mustard
a plant with yellow flowers, sometimes called wild rocket

Examples

Some gardeners struggle to control rocket cress because it spreads quickly in lawns.
Wild salads often include the leaves of rocket cress for their peppery flavor.
The yellow flowers of rocket cress brighten up roadsides and open fields in spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hedge mustard - another common name for rocket cress
  • Synonym: Sisymbrium - the genus to which rocket cress is sometimes assigned
