to lighten tensions

1. VERB

/ ˈlaɪtən tɛnʃənz / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtən tɛnʃənz / (BrE)
to make a tense or stressful situation more relaxed or calm
make a situation less tense

Examples

Her jokes helped to lighten tensions during the stressful meeting.
The mediator was brought in to lighten tensions between the two negotiating parties.
He tried to lighten tensions by sharing a funny story.

Synset Examples

The winker of the group always knows how to lighten tensions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ease tensions - make tensions less severe
  • Antonym: escalate tensions - make tensions worse
