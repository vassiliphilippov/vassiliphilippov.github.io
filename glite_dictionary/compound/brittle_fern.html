brittle fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪtəl fɝn / (AmE), / ˈbrɪtəl fɜːn / (BrE)
a delicate fern with thin, finely divided fronds and brittle stems, found widely in North America and Europe
delicate fern with brittle stems

Examples

The shaded hillsides were covered with brittle fern thriving in the cool, damp soil.
I picked a frond of brittle fern, but the stem snapped easily in my hand.
Botanists identified the plant as a brittle fern due to its distinctive thin leaves and fragile stalks.
