refrigerant dryer

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈfrɪdʒɚənt ˈdraɪɚ / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɪdʒər(ə)nt ˈdraɪə / (BrE)
a machine that removes moisture from compressed air by means of refrigeration
device removing moisture using refrigeration

Examples

A refrigerant dryer is commonly used in industrial compressed air systems to prevent moisture damage.
The technician recommended installing a refrigerant dryer to improve air quality in the workshop.
Regular maintenance of your refrigerant dryer ensures the efficiency and longevity of your equipment.

Synset Examples

Refrigeration dryers employ two heat exchangers, one for air-to-air and one for air-to-refrigeration.
