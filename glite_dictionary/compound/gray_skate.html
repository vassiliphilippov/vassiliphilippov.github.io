gray skate

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪ skeɪt / (AmE), / ɡreɪ skeɪt / (BrE)
a large European skate (fish), typically with a grayish color, commonly used as food.
European edible skate (fish)

Examples

Fresh gray skate is often sold in markets along the Atlantic coast.
The chef prepared a delicious dish using grilled gray skate and lemon butter.
Fishmongers recommend buying gray skate when it's in season for the best flavor.
