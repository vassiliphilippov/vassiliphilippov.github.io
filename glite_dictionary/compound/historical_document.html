historical document

1. NOUN

/ hɪˈstɔrɪkəl ˈdɑkjumənt / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪkəl ˈdɒkjumənt / (BrE)
a piece of writing or record that has historical significance or value
writing with historical significance

Examples

The museum recently acquired a historical document from the 18th century.
Researchers spent years deciphering the ancient historical document found in the ruins.
A copy of the Magna Carta is considered a significant historical document.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archive - a collection of historical documents or records
