5-hitter

1. NOUN

/ faɪvˈhɪtɚ / (AmE), / faɪvˈhɪtə / (BrE)
(in baseball) a game in which the pitcher allows the opposing team exactly five hits.
baseball game allowing five hits

Examples

The pitcher celebrated his first career 5-hitter against the top-ranked team.
Despite allowing a 5-hitter, she still managed to secure the win for her team.
He was proud of pitching a 5-hitter in the championship game.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: no-hitter - a baseball game in which a pitcher allows no hits
  • Degree Higher: one-hitter - a baseball game in which a pitcher allows one hit
  • Degree Higher: four-hitter - a baseball game in which a pitcher allows four hits
  • Degree Lower: six-hitter - a baseball game in which a pitcher allows six hits
