department of history

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑːrt.mənt əv ˈhɪs.tə.ri / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːt.mənt əv ˈhɪs.tər.i / (BrE)
an academic division within a college or university responsible for teaching and researching history
university division teaching history

Examples

She decided to major in history and joined the department of history at her university.
The department of history offers a wide range of courses on ancient and modern events.
Several professors in the department of history have published books on medieval Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: history department - alternative phrasing for department of history
