Entsi language

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛntsi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɛntsi ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Uralic language spoken by the Entsi people of Siberia
Uralic language of the Entsi people

Examples

The Entsi language is considered critically endangered, with only a few dozen native speakers remaining.
Linguists have documented the grammar and vocabulary of the Entsi language to help preserve it.
Efforts are underway to teach the Entsi language to younger generations in the community.
