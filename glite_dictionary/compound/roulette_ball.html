roulette ball

1. NOUN

/ ruˈlɛt bɔl / (AmE), / ruːˈlɛt bɔːl / (BrE)
A small, hard ball used in the game of roulette, spun around the edge of the roulette wheel to determine the winning number or color.
ball used in roulette game

Examples

The roulette ball landed on number seventeen, making her an instant winner.
A croupier polishes the roulette ball before every game for a fair spin.
As the roulette ball slowed down, everyone at the table held their breath.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roulette wheel - the rotating wheel used in the game of roulette
