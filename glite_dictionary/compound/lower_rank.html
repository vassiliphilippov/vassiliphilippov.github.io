lower rank

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊər ræŋk / (AmE), / ˈləʊə ræŋk / (BrE)
a position or state of being inferior or subordinate in hierarchy or importance
inferior status or position

Examples

Despite his lower rank, his expertise was essential to the team’s success.
Employees of lower rank sometimes feel overlooked by management.
She worked her way up from a lower rank to a leadership position.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inferiority - the condition of being lower in status or quality
  • Antonym: higher rank - the state of being superior in hierarchy or importance
