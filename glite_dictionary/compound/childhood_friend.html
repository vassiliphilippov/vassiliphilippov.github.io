childhood friend

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪldˌhʊd frɛnd / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪldhʊd frɛnd / (BrE)
a person whom one has known and been friends with since early childhood
friend from early childhood

Examples

After moving away, I lost contact with my childhood friend but recently reconnected online.
She and her childhood friend have stayed close despite living in different countries.
At the reunion, he was delighted to see his childhood friend after so many years.

Synset Examples

That bint over there is my childhood friend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: old friend - a friend one has known for a long time
