state of bonkersdom

1. NOUN

/ steɪt əv ˈbɑŋkərzdəm / (AmE), / steɪt əv ˈbɒŋkəzˌdəm / (BrE)
a humorous or exaggerated condition of craziness or confusion
extreme craziness or confusion (humorous)
informal slang

Examples

After two sleepless nights, she found herself in a state of bonkersdom at the meeting.
The shocking announcement pushed the whole office into a state of bonkersdom.
Sometimes, dealing with tax forms sends me into a full state of bonkersdom.

Synset Examples

He entered a state of bonkersdom after the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insanity - the state of being seriously mentally ill; madness
  • Synonym: confusion - the state of being bewildered or unclear in one's mind

Etymology

From 'bonkers' (slang for crazy) + '-dom' (domain or state).
