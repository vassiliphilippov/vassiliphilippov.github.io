man date

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæn ˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ˈmæn ˌdeɪt / (BrE)
an informal, planned social outing or meeting between male friends, often for leisure or entertainment
planned outing between male friends
informal slang

Examples

He always enjoys going on a man date with his best friend to watch sports.
They planned a man date to try the new barbecue restaurant downtown.
After a busy week, a man date at the golf course was just what they needed.

Synset Examples

He looks forward to his monthly man date with friends.
Their man date involved bowling and beer.
They scheduled a man date to catch the game together.

Etymology

Blend of 'man' and 'date', humorously formed by analogy with 'date' (romantic meeting).
