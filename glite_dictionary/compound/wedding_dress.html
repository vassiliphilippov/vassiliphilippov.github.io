wedding dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ drɛs / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ drɛs / (BrE)
a long formal gown worn by a bride during her wedding ceremony
gown worn by a bride at a wedding

Examples

She looked beautiful in her white wedding dress as she walked down the aisle.
The designer created a custom wedding dress adorned with delicate lace and pearls.
After the ceremony, she carefully packed away her wedding dress as a keepsake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bridal gown - an alternative term for a wedding dress
