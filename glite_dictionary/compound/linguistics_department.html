linguistics department

1. NOUN

/ lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks dɪˈpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
an academic department at a college or university that specializes in teaching and research in linguistics
university division for linguistics

Examples

The university's linguistics department offers both undergraduate and postgraduate degree programs.
She joined the linguistics department as a lecturer after completing her doctorate.
The linguistics department regularly hosts seminars on language acquisition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: department - a division of an organization with a specific function
