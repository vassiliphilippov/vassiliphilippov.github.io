eastern cricket frog

1. NOUN

/ ˈistərn ˈkrɪkɪt frɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈiːstən ˈkrɪkɪt frɒɡ / (BrE)
a small frog (Acris crepitans) found in wetland areas in the eastern United States, known for its distinctive clicking call
small frog native to eastern United States

Examples

The eastern cricket frog is commonly heard near ponds and streams during the summer.
Her research focused on the habitat preferences of the eastern cricket frog.
We spotted several eastern cricket frogs calling after the rainstorm.
