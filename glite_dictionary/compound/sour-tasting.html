sour-tasting

1. ADJ

/ ˈsaʊɚˌteɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaʊəˌteɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
having a sharp, acidic, or tart flavor characteristic of something sour
having a sour or acidic taste

Examples

The lemonade was so sour-tasting that I couldn't finish my glass.
She bit into the sour-tasting candy, wincing at its sharpness.
Some sour-tasting fruits, like grapefruits, are an acquired taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sour - having an acidic taste like lemon or vinegar
  • Antonym: sweet-tasting - having a sugary or sweet flavor
