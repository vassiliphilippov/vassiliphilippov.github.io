unauthorized absence

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌnɔːˈθɔrɪzd ˈæbsəns / (AmE), / ˌʌnɔːˈθɒrɪzd ˈæbsəns / (BrE)
an absence from duty or place of assignment without official permission, especially in a military context
absence without official permission (military)
legal

Examples

The soldier was reprimanded for his unauthorized absence during field exercises.
Repeated instances of unauthorized absence can lead to serious disciplinary action in the military.
Her unauthorized absence from the base was reported to her commanding officer.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: desertion - the act of abandoning duty or post without permission, especially in the armed forces
