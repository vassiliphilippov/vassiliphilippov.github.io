Destroy Dick December

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈstrɔɪ dɪk dɪˈsɛmbɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɔɪ dɪk dɪˈsɛmbə / (BrE)
a humorous internet challenge in which participants are encouraged to abstain from sexual activity or engage in specific sexual acts daily throughout the month of December, typically as a follow-up to No Nut November
humorous online abstinence or sexual challenge in December
slang informal

He and his friends joked about attempting Destroy Dick December after completing No Nut November.
Some online forums debated the supposed rules of Destroy Dick December.
Articles have discussed whether Destroy Dick December is meant to be taken seriously or as a parody.
