good health

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd hɛlθ / (AmE), / ɡʊd hɛlθ / (BrE)
the state of being physically and mentally vigorous and free from disease
state of being healthy

Examples

After her surgery, she worked hard to regain her good health.
Wishing you good health and happiness in the coming year.
Regular exercise and a balanced diet are key to maintaining good health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: health - state of physical or mental well-being
  • Antonym: ill health - state of being unhealthy or unwell
