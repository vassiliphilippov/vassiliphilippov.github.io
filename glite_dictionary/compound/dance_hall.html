dance hall

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæns hɔl / (AmE), / ˈdɑːns hɔːl / (BrE)
A large room or building intended mainly for dancing, especially to live or recorded music.
large room or building for dancing

Examples

The old dance hall was renovated into a vibrant nightclub.
Couples gathered in the dance hall to enjoy the big band music.
The city used to have a famous dance hall near the waterfront.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ballroom - a large room for formal dancing
