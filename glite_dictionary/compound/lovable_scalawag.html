lovable scalawag

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌvəbəl ˈskæləˌwæɡ / (AmE), / ˈlʌvəb(ə)l ˈskæləˌwæɡ / (BrE)
A person who is mischievous or roguish in a way that is endearing or charming rather than offensive.
charming, mischievous person

Examples

Everyone agrees that Jake is a lovable scalawag despite his endless practical jokes.
In the movie, the main character plays a lovable scalawag who outsmarts the villains with charm.
Her grandfather had been a lovable scalawag in his youth, always getting into harmless mischief.

Synset Examples

He's a lovable scalawag who always pulls pranks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rascal - a mischievous or cheeky person, typically a child
  • Synonym: rogue - a dishonest or unprincipled but likable person
  • Synonym: scoundrel - a person who behaves badly or dishonestly
