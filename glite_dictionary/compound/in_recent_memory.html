in recent memory

1. NOUN

/ ɪn ˈriːsənt ˈmɛməri / (AmE), / ɪn ˈriːsənt ˈmɛməri / (BrE)
within the period of time that people can remember from the recent past
during the recent past

Examples

This is the worst storm in recent memory for the region.
The country has experienced rapid economic growth in recent memory.
No other artist has achieved such popularity in recent memory.

Synset Examples

He’s the best player in recent memory for our team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recently - not long ago; in the near past
