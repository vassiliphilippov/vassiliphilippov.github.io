thorn apple

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɔrn ˌæpəl / (AmE), / ˈθɔːn ˌæpəl / (BrE)
any of several plants of the genus Datura, typically with spiny fruits and trumpet-shaped flowers; some species are toxic or hallucinogenic
plant of the Datura genus; often poisonous

Examples

The seeds and leaves of the thorn apple are highly poisonous if ingested.
A thorn apple plant can be recognized by its large, spiny fruit and white trumpet-shaped flowers.
Farmers often remove thorn apple plants from their fields to protect livestock from poisoning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: datura - genus of poisonous plants with trumpet-shaped flowers
