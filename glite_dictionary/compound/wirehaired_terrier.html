wirehaired terrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪərˌhɛrd təˈrɪr / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəhɛəd ˈtɛrɪə / (BrE)
a breed of terrier with a rough, wiry coat of hair
a terrier breed with wiry hair

Examples

The wirehaired terrier won first place at the dog show for its distinctive coat.
My neighbors just adopted a playful wirehaired terrier from the local shelter.
Many people appreciate the spirited personality of the wirehaired terrier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrier - a breed of dog originally bred for hunting vermin
