personal relation

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːsənl rɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl rɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a relationship between two or more individuals, often characterized by emotional or social connections
relationship between people

Examples

The company's success depends on strong personal relations between employees and managers.
She valued her personal relations more than her professional achievements.
Building good personal relations with clients is essential in this business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relationship - the way in which two or more people or things are connected
