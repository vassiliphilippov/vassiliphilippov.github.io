strawlike hair

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɑːˌlaɪk hɛr / (AmE), / ˈstrɔːˌlaɪk heə / (BrE)
hair that is dry, coarse, and stiff, resembling the texture of straw
hair resembling straw in texture

Examples

She ran her fingers through his strawlike hair, surprised by its roughness.
Years of neglect had turned her once-shiny locks into strawlike hair.
The sun and sea had left his strawlike hair brittle and colorless.

Synset Examples

His strawlike hair caught the sunlight beautifully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coarse hair - hair that is thick or rough in texture
