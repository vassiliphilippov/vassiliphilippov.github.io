tea cozy

1. NOUN

/ ti ˈkoʊzi / (AmE), / tiː ˈkəʊzi / (BrE)
A padded or knitted cover placed over a teapot to keep the tea warm.
padded cover for a teapot

Examples

She placed a tea cozy over the pot to keep the tea hot during the afternoon.
My grandmother crocheted a lovely tea cozy as a gift.
The colorful tea cozy added charm to their kitchen table.

Etymology

From 'tea' and 'cozy', meaning warm or snug.
