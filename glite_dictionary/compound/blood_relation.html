blood relation

1. NOUN

/ blʌd rɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / blʌd rɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who is related to another by birth rather than by marriage; a biological relative
biological family member

Examples

Although we share no blood relation, she feels like a sister to me.
He discovered that his new coworker was actually a distant blood relation.
Inheritance laws often favor a direct blood relation over other claimants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relative - a person connected by blood or marriage
  • Antonym: in-law - a person related by marriage
