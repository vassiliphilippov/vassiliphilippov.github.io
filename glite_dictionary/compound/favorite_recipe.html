favorite recipe

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪvərɪt ˈrɛsəpi / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərɪt ˈrɛsɪpi / (BrE)
a person's most preferred set of instructions for preparing a particular dish
a most preferred cooking method

Examples

My grandmother's favorite recipe is her legendary apple pie, which she bakes for every holiday.
Whenever friends visit, Jane always makes her favorite recipe for homemade lasagna.
He wrote down his favorite recipe for chocolate chip cookies to share with the class.

Synset Examples

The Dutchwoman shared her favorite recipes with us.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recipe - a set of instructions for preparing a dish
