tree surgery

1. NOUN

/ tri ˈsɝdʒəri / (AmE), / tri ˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
the practice of treating and repairing damaged or decaying trees to promote their health and longevity
treatment of damaged or decaying trees

Examples

After the storm, tree surgery was required to save several old oaks in the park.
Many companies now offer tree surgery as part of their landscaping services.
Experts in tree surgery can help restore trees damaged by disease or harsh weather conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arboriculture - the cultivation and management of trees and shrubs
