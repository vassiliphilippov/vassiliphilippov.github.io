Dangla language

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæŋɡlə læŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdaŋɡlə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in Chad, characterized by seven vowels and distinctions in vowel length
a language of Chad (Chadic family)

Examples

The Dangla language is primarily spoken in the southern region of Chad.
Linguists have documented the seven-vowel system of the Dangla language.
Her research focuses on documenting endangered features of the Dangla language.
