traditional topper

1. NOUN

/ trəˈdɪʃənl ˈtɑpər / (AmE), / trəˈdɪʃənəl ˈtɒpə / (BrE)
a classic formal hat, especially a top hat, typically worn at special occasions such as weddings or formal ceremonies
formal hat, usually a top hat

Examples

At the royal event, the gentlemen all donned a traditional topper as part of their attire.
His grandfather's traditional topper has been passed down for three generations.
She admired how the groom looked in his morning suit and traditional topper.

Synset Examples

He wore a traditional topper at the wedding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top hat - a tall, flat-crowned, broad-brimmed formal hat
