tennis racket

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnɪs ˌrækɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs ˌrakɪt / (BrE)
A light, oval-headed racket used for hitting the ball in the game of tennis.
racket used for playing tennis

Examples

She bought a new tennis racket before the tournament.
His tennis racket snapped during the intense match.
You need a good tennis racket to improve your game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racket - an implement with a handle and an oval frame strung with tight cords, used to hit a ball or shuttlecock
