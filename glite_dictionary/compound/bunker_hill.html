Bunker Hill

1. PROPN

/ ˌbʌŋkər ˈhɪl / (AmE), / ˌbʌŋkə ˈhɪl / (BrE)
the site of the first major battle of the American War of Independence in 1775, where British forces defeated colonial troops
site of a key Revolutionary War battle in 1775

Examples

The Battle of Bunker Hill demonstrated the determination of the colonial forces early in the Revolution.
Many tourists visit the Bunker Hill Monument when exploring Boston's historic sites.
She wrote a research paper on the significance of Bunker Hill in American history.
