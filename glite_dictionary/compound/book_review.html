book review

1. NOUN

/ bʊk rɪˈvju / (AmE), / bʊk rɪˈvjuː / (BrE)
a critical evaluation or discussion of a book, usually recently published, typically published in newspapers, magazines, or online platforms
critical evaluation or discussion of a book

Examples

She wrote a detailed book review for the local newspaper.
The school's website featured a weekly book review column for students.
His honest book review helped many readers decide whether to buy the novel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: review - a critical assessment of something
