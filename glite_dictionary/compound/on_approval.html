on approval

1. ADV

/ ɑn əˈpruːvəl / (AmE), / ɒn əˈpruːvəl / (BrE)
(of goods) supplied for examination with the option to buy or return them if not satisfactory.
supplied for examination with option to buy

Examples

The boutique allows customers to take jewelry on approval before making a final decision.
She ordered the paintings on approval and returned the ones she didn't like.
You can try out the sofa at home on approval and return it if it doesn't suit you.

Synset Examples

they took the VCR on approval

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on consignment - goods left in the care of another to be sold
