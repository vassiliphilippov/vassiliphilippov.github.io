the Big One

1. NOUN

/ ðə bɪɡ wʌn / (AmE), / ðə bɪɡ wʌn / (BrE)
a major catastrophic event, especially a devastating earthquake (commonly expected to occur in California)
a major catastrophic event, especially an earthquake

Examples

Scientists warn that the Big One could strike the San Andreas Fault at any time.
Emergency officials held a drill to prepare for the Big One.
Many residents have earthquake kits ready in anticipation of the Big One.
