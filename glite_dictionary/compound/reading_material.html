reading material

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːdɪŋ məˌtɪriəl / (AmE), / ˈriːdɪŋ məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
written content such as books, magazines, or articles, intended to be read for information or enjoyment
content intended to be read

Examples

She packed a few magazines as reading material for the flight.
The library offers a wide variety of reading material for all ages.
While waiting for the doctor, he browsed through some reading material on the table.

Synset Examples

the teacher assigned new readings
he bought some reading material at the airport

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reading matter - written material intended to be read
