telecom hotel

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlɪˌkɑm hoʊˈtɛl / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌkɒm həʊˈtɛl / (BrE)
A building used to house telecommunications equipment, often serving as a central location for service providers to interconnect their networks.
building for telecommunications equipment

Examples

After the company upgraded the telecom hotel, network reliability improved dramatically.
Many Internet service providers lease rack space in a telecom hotel downtown.
Security was tightened around the city's largest telecom hotel to prevent unauthorized access.

Synset Examples

the telecom hotels are prime targets for bombing

Etymology

Formed from 'telecom' (short for telecommunications) and 'hotel', suggesting a shared facility for equipment.
