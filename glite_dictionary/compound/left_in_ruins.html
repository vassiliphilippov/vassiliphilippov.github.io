left in ruins

1. ADJ

/ lɛft ɪn ˈruːɪnz / (AmE), / lɛft ɪn ˈruːɪnz / (BrE)
destroyed or severely damaged; in a state of devastation
destroyed; in a devastated state

Examples

The ancient castle was left in ruins after years of neglect and conflict.
His hopes for the future were left in ruins by the sudden loss of his job.
Much of the city was left in ruins following the devastating wildfire.

Synset Examples

After the earthquake, many homes were left in ruins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devastated - completely destroyed or very badly damaged
  • Antonym: intact - not damaged or impaired in any way; complete
