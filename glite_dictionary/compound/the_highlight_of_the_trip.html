the highlight of the trip

1. NOUN

/ ðə ˈhaɪˌlaɪt əv ðə trɪp / (AmE), / ðə ˈhaɪ.laɪt əv ðə trɪp / (BrE)
the most memorable or enjoyable part of a trip or journey
the best part of a trip

Examples

Climbing the mountain was easily the highlight of the trip for everyone.
Dinner at the little seaside café became the highlight of the trip for our group.
For many visitors, seeing the northern lights is the highlight of the trip.

Synset Examples

Visiting Kangla was the highlight of our trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: highlight - the best or most interesting part of something
