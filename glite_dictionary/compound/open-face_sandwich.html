open-face sandwich

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊ.pən feɪs ˈsænd.wɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.pən feɪs ˈsæn.wɪtʃ / (BrE)
A sandwich consisting of one slice of bread with toppings and no upper piece of bread.
sandwich with one slice of bread

Examples

The restaurant serves a delicious open-face sandwich with smoked salmon and dill.
She prefers an open-face sandwich for lunch because it's lighter than a regular sandwich.
We made an open-face sandwich with grilled vegetables and melted cheese for dinner.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: sandwich - two slices of bread with filling between them
