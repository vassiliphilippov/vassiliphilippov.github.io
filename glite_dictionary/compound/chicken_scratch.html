chicken scratch

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkən ˌskrætʃ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn ˌskrætʃ / (BrE)
cramped or messy handwriting that is difficult to read
illegible or messy handwriting

Examples

His notes were all written in chicken scratch, making them almost impossible to decipher.
The teacher couldn't read the chicken scratch on James's homework.
Please try to write neatly this time—last week's essay was just chicken scratch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scrawl - carelessly written or scribbled handwriting
  • Synonym: scribble - written hastily or sloppily, often hard to read
  • Antonym: calligraphy - decorative handwriting or handwritten lettering

2. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪk.ɪn skrætʃ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.ɪn skrætʃ / (BrE)
handwriting that is messy and difficult to read
illegible handwriting
informal

Examples

I couldn't make sense of his chicken scratch on the form.
The teacher handed back my essay covered in comments written in chicken scratch.
Whenever he writes fast, his notes turn into complete chicken scratch.

Synset Examples

She complained that his notes were just chicken scratch.
His handwriting looked like chicken scratch on the page.
The contract was filled with chicken scratch; I couldn't read it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scrawl - irregular or careless handwriting
  • Synonym: scribble - written carelessly or hurriedly, making it hard to read
  • Antonym: calligraphy - decorative and elegant handwriting

Etymology

From the resemblance of messy handwriting to the random marks made by chickens when scratching the ground.
