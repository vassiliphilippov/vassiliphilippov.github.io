actor's agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈæktərz ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈæktəz ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to represent actors and find work opportunities for them, negotiating contracts and managing their careers
representative who finds work for actors

Examples

After years of struggling, he finally found an actor's agent who believed in his talent.
Her actor's agent secured her an audition for a major film role.
Most successful performers rely on a trusted actor's agent to handle negotiations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talent agent - an agent who finds jobs for people in the entertainment industry
