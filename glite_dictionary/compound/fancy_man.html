fancy man

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæn.si mæn / (AmE), / ˈfæn.si mæn / (BrE)
a man who lives off the earnings of a prostitute or procures customers for prostitutes; a pimp
man living off a prostitute's earnings
british offensive

Examples

The police arrested the fancy man suspected of running the brothel.
She was seen with her fancy man outside the club last night.
In old London, a fancy man would often linger near taverns seeking new clients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pimp - a man who controls prostitutes and arranges clients for them

2. NOUN

/ ˈfænsi mæn / (AmE), / ˈfænsi mæn / (BrE)
a man who is a woman's lover, especially one who is not her husband
a woman's lover (not her husband)
informal

Examples

Everyone in the village suspected she had a fancy man on the side.
He was known as her fancy man long before the rumors became public.
She left her husband for her fancy man from the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lover - a person having a romantic or sexual relationship with someone
  • Antonym: mistress - a woman who is a man's lover, especially one who is not his wife
