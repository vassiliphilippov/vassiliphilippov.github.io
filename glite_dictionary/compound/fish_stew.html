fish stew

1. NOUN

/ fɪʃ stu / (AmE), / fɪʃ stjuː / (BrE)
A dish consisting of fish cooked slowly in liquid, often with vegetables and seasonings.
stew made with fish

Examples

She ordered the fish stew because it was the restaurant's specialty.
My grandmother has a unique recipe for fish stew passed down through generations.
Nothing warms you up on a cold night like a hearty fish stew.
