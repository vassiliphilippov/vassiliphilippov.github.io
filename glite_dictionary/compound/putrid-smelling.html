putrid-smelling

1. ADJ

/ ˈpjuː.trɪd ˌsmɛl.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpjuː.trɪd ˌsmɛl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
having the foul, decaying odor typical of rotting organic matter
having a foul, rotting odor

Examples

The putrid-smelling garbage had been festering in the alley for days.
Emergency crews avoided the putrid-smelling sludge as they began the cleanup.
When he opened the refrigerator, a putrid-smelling stench hit him instantly.
