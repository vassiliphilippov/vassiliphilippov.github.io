potato wart

1. NOUN

/ pəˈteɪtoʊ wɔrt / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ wɔːt / (BrE)
a fungal disease of potatoes characterized by dark, warty, spongy growths on the tubers
fungal potato disease with warty growths

Examples

Farmers are concerned about potato wart spreading to this region and damaging their crops.
The inspector found signs of potato wart on several harvested tubers.
Because potato wart is highly contagious, strict quarantine measures were imposed.
