gasoline mileage

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæsəliːn ˈmaɪlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɡæsəliːn ˈmaɪlɪdʒ / (BrE)
the number of miles a vehicle travels per gallon of gasoline used; a measure of fuel efficiency
miles traveled per gallon of gasoline

Examples

Hybrid cars often have better gasoline mileage than traditional vehicles.
The van's gasoline mileage dropped noticeably when we drove through the mountains.
I'm looking for a car with high gasoline mileage to save money on fuel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuel economy - the efficiency with which a vehicle converts fuel into distance traveled
