academic relation

1. NOUN

/ əˈkædəmɪk rɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / əˈkadəmɪk rɪˈleɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a professional relationship between instructors and their students within an educational context
professional relationship in education

Examples

Building a strong academic relation with your professor can enhance your learning experience.
The university encourages healthy academic relations between faculty and students.
Misconduct in an academic relation can lead to disciplinary action.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teacher-student relationship - the relationship between a teacher and a student

Etymology

From 'academic' (relating to education or scholarship) and 'relation' (connection or association).
