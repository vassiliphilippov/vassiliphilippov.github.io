Silver State

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪlvər steɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə steɪt / (BrE)
an informal name for the state of Nevada, relating to its history of silver mining
nickname for Nevada, US state
informal american

Examples

Many tourists visit the Silver State to explore its casinos and natural scenery.
Nevada is commonly known as the Silver State because of its rich history in silver mining.
The license plates in the Silver State often display images related to its mining heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nevada - a state in the western United States; commonly called the Silver State

Etymology

Refers to Nevada's history of silver mining and the importance of silver to the state's economy.
