X-ray machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛks reɪ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈɛks reɪ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a device that produces X rays, especially for medical imaging or analysis
device for producing X rays

Examples

The doctor used an X-ray machine to examine the patient’s broken arm.
Technicians routinely maintain the X-ray machine to ensure accurate imaging results.
Airport security requires all luggage to pass through an X-ray machine before boarding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: X-ray - a type of electromagnetic radiation used for imaging or analysis
