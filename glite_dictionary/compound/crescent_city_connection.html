Crescent City Connection

1. PROPN

/ ˈkrɛsənt ˈsɪti kəˈnɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈkrɛsənt ˈsɪti kəˈnɛkʃən / (BrE)
the pair of cantilever bridges spanning the Mississippi River at New Orleans, Louisiana
bridges in New Orleans over Mississippi River

Examples

The Crescent City Connection is a vital route for commuters traveling between the east and west banks of New Orleans.
