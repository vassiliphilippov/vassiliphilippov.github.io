school bus

1. NOUN

/ skuːl bʌs / (AmE), / skuːl bʌs / (BrE)
a large vehicle used for transporting children to and from school
bus for transporting children to school

Examples

The children eagerly climbed onto the school bus every morning.
A yellow school bus stopped outside the building to pick up students.
Parents waited at the curb for the school bus to arrive after classes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bus - a large road vehicle for carrying many passengers
