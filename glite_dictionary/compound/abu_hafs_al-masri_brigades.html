Abu Hafs al-Masri Brigades

1. PROPN

/ ˌɑːbuː ˈhæfs ælˈmæsri brɪˈɡeɪdz / (AmE), / ˌɑːbuː ˈhæfs ælˈmæsri brɪˈɡeɪdz / (BrE)
a terrorist group that has been associated with Al Qaeda and has claimed responsibility for several attacks in Iraq and Turkey
Al Qaeda-affiliated terrorist group

Examples

The Abu Hafs al-Masri Brigades claimed responsibility for the bombing in Istanbul.
Security agencies have linked the Abu Hafs al-Masri Brigades to several attacks in the Middle East.
Reports suggest the Abu Hafs al-Masri Brigades have ties with other extremist organizations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Al Qaeda - an international Islamist extremist terrorist organization

Etymology

Named after Abu Hafs al-Masri, an Egyptian member of Al Qaeda; 'Brigades' indicates an organized group.
