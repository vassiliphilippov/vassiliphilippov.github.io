frat rat

1. NOUN

/ fræt ræt / (AmE), / fræt ræt / (BrE)
A student who spends an excessive amount of time involved in the social activities of a college fraternity, often to the exclusion of other interests.
student overly involved in fraternity life
slang american

Examples

After joining the fraternity, he quickly became known as a frat rat among his classmates.
Some parents worry that their children will turn into a frat rat and neglect their studies.
She avoided the house where frat rats partied every weekend.

Synset Examples

The frat rat stayed up all night studying with friends.

He became a frat rat after joining the brotherhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fraternity brother - a male member of a college fraternity
