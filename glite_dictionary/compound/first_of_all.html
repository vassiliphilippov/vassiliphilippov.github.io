first of all

1. ADV

/ fɝst əv ɔl / (AmE), / fɜːst əv ɔːl / (BrE)
used to introduce the most important or the first point in a series of statements or questions
introducing the main or initial point

Examples

First of all, I want to thank everyone for coming to the meeting today.
You need to wash your hands first of all before preparing any food.
First of all, check that you have all the required documents with you.

Synset Examples

first we must consider the garter snake

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firstly - used to introduce the first point or reason
  • Antonym: last of all - used to introduce the final point in a list

2. ADV

/ ˈfɝst əv ɔl / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst əv ɔːl / (BrE)
used to introduce the main or most important point at the beginning of a discussion or list
used to introduce the first point

Examples

First of all, let's review the agenda for today's meeting.
We need to, first of all, ensure everyone's safety before proceeding.
First of all, I'd like to clarify a few misunderstandings.

Synset Examples

First of all, let me thank everyone for coming.
First of all, we must address the main issue.
First of all, we need to set a budget.
