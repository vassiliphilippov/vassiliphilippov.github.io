Mobuto Lake

1. PROPN

/ moʊˈbuːtoʊ leɪk / (AmE), / məʊˈbuːtəʊ leɪk / (BrE)
a shallow lake located on the border between Uganda and the Democratic Republic of the Congo, in the Great Rift Valley
lake on the Uganda–Congo border in the Great Rift Valley

Examples

The fishing communities near Mobuto Lake rely on its waters for their livelihood.
