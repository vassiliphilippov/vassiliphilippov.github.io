Christmas stocking

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪs·məs ˌstɑː.kɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.məs ˌstɒk.ɪŋ / (BrE)
A large sock or stocking hung up by children at Christmas for Santa Claus to fill with small gifts.
stocking for Christmas gifts

Examples

On Christmas Eve, the children hung their Christmas stockings by the fireplace.
Each year, my grandmother knits a new Christmas stocking for every grandchild.
Small toys and candy are common treats found in a Christmas stocking.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: stocking - a close-fitting covering for the foot and leg
