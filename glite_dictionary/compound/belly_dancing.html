belly dancing

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛli ˌdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɛli ˌdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
A Middle Eastern dance in which the dancer makes sensuous movements of the hips and abdomen.
Middle Eastern dance with hip movements

Examples

The art of belly dancing requires precise control of the abdominal muscles.
She has been studying belly dancing for five years and now performs at local cultural festivals.
Belly dancing costumes often feature sparkling beads and flowing fabrics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oriental dance - another term for belly dancing, emphasizing regional origin
