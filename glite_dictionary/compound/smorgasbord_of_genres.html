smorgasbord of genres

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmɔrɡəzˌbɔrd əv ˈʒɑnrəz / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːɡəzˌbɔːd əv ˈʒɒnrəz / (BrE)
a wide and varied selection of musical or artistic types; an assortment of different genres
wide variety of genres

Examples

The music festival featured a smorgasbord of genres, attracting fans from all walks of life.
Her new album is praised for its smorgasbord of genres, blending jazz, pop, and rock seamlessly.
The film's soundtrack offers a smorgasbord of genres, keeping viewers engaged throughout the story.

Synset Examples

Her playlist was a smorgasbord of genres and artists.
There was a smorgasbord of options at the food festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: variety - a number of different types of the same general thing
  • Synonym: selection - a range of different things from which a choice can be made

Etymology

From Swedish 'smörgåsbord' (sandwich table, buffet); 'genre' from French 'genre' (kind, sort).
