bog rose

1. NOUN

/ bɑɡ ɹoʊz / (AmE), / bɒɡ ɹəʊz / (BrE)
A type of bog orchid, typically found in Canada, with a single fragrant magenta-pink flower and a wide open corolla.
a Canadian bog orchid with pink flower

Examples

The bog rose is known for its vivid magenta-pink blossom and pleasant fragrance.
Botanists discovered several bog roses thriving in the marshy areas of northern Canada.
A single bog rose can add a splash of color to an otherwise plain wetland.
