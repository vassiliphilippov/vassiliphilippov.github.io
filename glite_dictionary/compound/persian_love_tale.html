Persian love tale

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrʒən lʌv teɪl / (AmE), / ˈpɜːʃən lʌv teɪl / (BrE)
a traditional romantic narrative originating from Persian literature, often involving legendary lovers and poetic themes
romantic story from Persian literature

Examples

Her novel draws inspiration from classic Persian love tales like Layla and Majnun.
Many Persian love tales explore the themes of longing and unfulfilled desire.
The exhibition showcased manuscripts of famous Persian love tales from the 12th century.

Synset Examples

She wrote a poem inspired by Persian love tales.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: romance - a love story, especially in literature
  • Synonym: tragic love story - a love story that ends in tragedy
