harpoon gun

1. NOUN

/ hɑrˈpun ˌɡʌn / (AmE), / hɑːˈpuːn ˌɡʌn / (BrE)
A firearm or mechanical device designed to launch harpoons, typically used in fishing, whaling, or underwater activities.
device for firing harpoons

Examples

The whaling crew loaded the harpoon gun as they spotted a whale in the distance.
Divers used a harpoon gun to capture large fish during their expedition.
The museum displayed an old harpoon gun used in 19th-century whaling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harpoon - a long spear-like instrument for catching fish or whales
  • Synonym: spear gun - a gun designed to fire a spear underwater, primarily used in fishing
