Hot Springs National Park

1. PROPN

/ hɑt sprɪŋz nӕʃənəl pɑrk / (AmE), / hɒt sprɪŋz ˈnæʃənəl pɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in Arkansas, United States, famous for its natural hot springs, which have been used for bathing and therapy.
national park in Arkansas famed for hot springs

Examples

Thousands of visitors travel to Hot Springs National Park every year to experience its renowned thermal baths.
The history of Hot Springs National Park dates back to the early 19th century.
Scientists study the mineral content of the water found in Hot Springs National Park.
