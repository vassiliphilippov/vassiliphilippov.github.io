upper cannon

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌpɚ ˈkænən / (AmE), / ˈʌpə ˈkanən / (BrE)
A piece of plate armor that covers the upper part of the arm.
armor protecting the upper arm

Examples

The knight was missing the upper cannon on his left arm after the last battle.
Restorers carefully polished the upper cannon before displaying the armor in the museum.
The blacksmith customized the upper cannon to fit the soldier's measurements exactly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vambrace - arm guard covering the forearm in plate armor
