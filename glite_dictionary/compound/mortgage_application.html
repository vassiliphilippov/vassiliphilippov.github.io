mortgage application

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrɡɪdʒ ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɔːɡɪdʒ ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a formal request or document submitted to a lender to obtain a mortgage loan
request for a mortgage loan

Examples

She filled out a mortgage application with her local bank last week.
The bank rejected his mortgage application due to insufficient credit history.
Submitting a mortgage application is the first step to buying a new home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loan application - application to obtain any type of loan
  • Degree Higher: mortgage approval - official agreement by a lender to grant a mortgage
