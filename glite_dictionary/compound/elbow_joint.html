elbow joint

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛlboʊ dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈɛlbəʊ dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
the hinge joint between the upper arm and the forearm in humans and corresponding joint in the forelimb of quadrupeds
hinge joint of arm or forelimb

Examples

He injured his elbow joint while playing tennis last weekend.
Arthritis can sometimes affect the elbow joint, causing pain and stiffness.
The veterinarian examined the dog’s elbow joint for signs of inflammation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elbow - the joint between the upper and lower parts of the human arm
