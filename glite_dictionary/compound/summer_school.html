summer school

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmɚ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə skuːl / (BrE)
a course or set of classes held during the summer, often for students needing additional instruction or credits
academic classes held in summer

Examples

After failing math, she had to attend summer school to catch up before the new year.
Many students take summer school classes to get ahead or explore new subjects.
The university offers intensive summer school programs for international students.
