chili dog

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪli dɔg / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪli dɒg / (BrE)
A hot dog topped with chili con carne, typically served in a bun.
hot dog with chili con carne
american

Examples

I ordered a chili dog with extra onions at the baseball game.
He makes the best chili dog in town, always topped with sharp cheddar cheese.
Would you like a chili dog or just a plain hot dog for lunch?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot dog - a cooked sausage served in a sliced bun

Etymology

From 'chili' (short for chili con carne) and 'dog' (short for hot dog), both American English.
