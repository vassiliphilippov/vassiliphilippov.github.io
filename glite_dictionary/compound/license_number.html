license number

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪsəns ˌnʌmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪsəns ˌnʌmbə / (BrE)
the series of numbers or letters displayed on a vehicle's license plate to identify it uniquely
number on a vehicle's license plate

Examples

The police officer wrote down the license number of the speeding car.
You must provide your vehicle's license number when registering for a parking permit.
She memorized the license number in case she needed to report the incident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: license plate - a plate attached to a vehicle showing its registration number
