by press release

1. ADP

/ baɪ prɛs rɪˈliːs / (AmE), / baɪ prɛs rɪˈliːs / (BrE)
by means of an official announcement issued to the media for public dissemination
via official media announcement

Examples

The company announced its merger by press release yesterday afternoon.
Many scientific discoveries are first made public by press release, rather than in academic papers.
He received news of the policy change by press release before any official email was sent.

Synset Examples

She learned about the findings through science by press release.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: press release - an official statement issued to the media
