explosive trace detection

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈsploʊsɪv treɪs dɪˈtɛkʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˈspləʊsɪv treɪs dɪˈtɛkʃən / (BrE)
a method or system for detecting minute traces of explosive substances, especially by screening luggage using swabs and sensitive machines
method for detecting traces of explosives

Examples

Many airports rely on explosive trace detection to enhance security at checkpoints.
The new security policy mandates explosive trace detection for all checked baggage.
Technicians calibrated the explosive trace detection equipment before the morning rush.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: explosives detection - the process of detecting explosive materials
