House of Representatives

1. PROPN

/ ˈhaʊs əv ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪvz / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs əv ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪvz / (BrE)
the lower legislative chamber of the United States Congress, consisting of elected representatives from each state, whose number is based on population.
lower chamber of U.S. Congress
american

Examples

The House of Representatives voted to pass the new economic stimulus bill yesterday.
Each state has a different number of seats in the House of Representatives based on its population.
Debates in the House of Representatives are often broadcast on national television.
