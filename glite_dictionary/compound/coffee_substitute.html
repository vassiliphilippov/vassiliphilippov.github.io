coffee substitute

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔfi səbsˌtɪtut / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi səbˌstɪtjuːt / (BrE)
a drink that resembles coffee and is used as an alternative to it
drink used instead of coffee

Examples

Some people prefer a coffee substitute made from roasted barley or chicory.
During the war, soldiers often drank a coffee substitute when real coffee was unavailable.
She offered her guests a coffee substitute for those who didn't want caffeine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffee - a popular hot drink made from roasted coffee beans
