hair follicle

1. NOUN

/ hɛr ˈfɑlɪkəl / (AmE), / heə ˈfɒlɪkl̩ / (BrE)
A small tubular cavity in the skin that contains the root of a hair and is associated with small muscles and sebaceous glands.
tubular skin cavity containing hair root
medical

Examples

The dermatologist explained that each hair follicle can produce a new strand of hair after one falls out.
Damage to a hair follicle can sometimes result in bald patches on the scalp.
Sebum is secreted by glands attached to the hair follicle to help moisturize the hair and skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: follicle - small cavity or sac in the body
