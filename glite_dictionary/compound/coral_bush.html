coral bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔr.əl bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɒr.əl bʊʃ / (BrE)
an Australian shrub with simple obovate leaves and brilliant scarlet flowers, often cultivated as an ornamental plant
Australian shrub with scarlet flowers

Examples

The coral bush in the botanical garden was covered in vivid scarlet flowers.
Gardeners often choose the coral bush for its brilliant blooms and easy maintenance.
Native to Australia, the coral bush thrives in sunny, well-drained soils.
