Mustagh Range

1. PROPN

/ ˈmʌstɑːɡ reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈmʌstɑːɡ reɪndʒ / (BrE)
a mountain range in northern Kashmir, forming an extension of the Hindu Kush and containing the world's second highest peak, K2.
mountain range in northern Kashmir

Examples

The Mustagh Range is famous for its dramatic peaks and glacial landscapes.
Climbers from around the world aspire to conquer K2 in the Mustagh Range.
Weather conditions in the Mustagh Range can change rapidly, making expeditions challenging.
