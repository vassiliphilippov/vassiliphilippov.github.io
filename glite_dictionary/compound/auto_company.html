auto company

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːtoʊ ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊ ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
A company that manufactures and sells automobiles.
company manufacturing and selling cars

Examples

Tesla is a leading auto company known for its electric vehicles.
The auto company announced plans to open a new factory in Texas.
Many auto companies are investing in autonomous driving technology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automaker - a company that manufactures automobiles
