apple aphid

1. NOUN

/ ˈæpəl ˌeɪfɪd / (AmE), / ˈap(ə)l ˌeɪfɪd / (BrE)
a small, bright green aphid that feeds on apple trees and causes the leaves to curl
a green aphid pest of apple trees

Examples

The apple aphid can quickly infest an orchard and damage the fruit trees.
Gardeners often spray their apple trees to prevent apple aphid infestations.
If you notice curled leaves on your apple tree, it may be the work of the apple aphid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aphid - small sap-sucking insect
