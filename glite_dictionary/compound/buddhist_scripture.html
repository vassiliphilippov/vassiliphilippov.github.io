Buddhist scripture

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊdɪst ˈskrɪptʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʊdɪst ˈskrɪptʃə / (BrE)
a sacred text of Buddhism, forming part of its religious teachings and doctrine
sacred text in Buddhism

Examples

Monks study the Buddhist scriptures as part of their daily practice.
The Buddhist scripture was translated into English for a wider audience.
Scholars debate the origins of various Buddhist scriptures found across Asia.
