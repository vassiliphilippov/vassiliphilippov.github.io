roof garden

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːf ˌɡɑːrdən / (AmE), / ˈruːf ˌɡɑːdən / (BrE)
a garden situated on the flat roof of a building, often used for recreation or to improve the environment
a garden on a building's roof

Examples

The hotel features a beautiful roof garden with panoramic views of the city.
She tends to her vegetables and flowers in the roof garden every weekend.
Many cities encourage the creation of roof gardens to combat urban heat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rooftop garden - an alternative term for a garden on a building's roof
  • Degree Lower: garden - a piece of ground where flowers, shrubs, vegetables, or fruit are grown
