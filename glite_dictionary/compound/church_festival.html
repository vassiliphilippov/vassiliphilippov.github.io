church festival

1. NOUN

/ tʃɝtʃ ˈfɛstəvəl / (AmE), / tʃɜːtʃ ˈfɛstɪvəl / (BrE)
A festival held by or at a church for religious purposes or to commemorate a religious event.
religious festival held by a church

Examples

The town organizes a church festival every summer to celebrate its patron saint.
Families gathered for food and music at the annual church festival.
The church festival featured a special service, games, and traditional dances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: religious festival - a festival with religious significance
