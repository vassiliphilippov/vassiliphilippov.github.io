rivet clincher

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪvɪt ˈklɪntʃər / (AmE), / ˈrɪvɪt ˈklɪntʃə / (BrE)
a hand tool used to bend and secure the ends of tubular rivets after insertion
tool for securing tubular rivets

Examples

The mechanic used a rivet clincher to fasten the metal brackets securely.
With a rivet clincher, assembling the leather straps became much easier and cleaner.
After positioning the rivet, he grabbed the rivet clincher to complete the repair.
