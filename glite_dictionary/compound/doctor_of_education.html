Doctor of Education

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɑːktər əv ˌɛdʒəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɒktə əv ˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
A doctoral degree awarded for advanced study and research in the field of education.
doctoral degree in education

Examples

After five years of research, she was finally awarded her Doctor of Education degree.
Many university presidents hold a Doctor of Education as their highest qualification.
He earned his Doctor of Education focusing on curriculum development and assessment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: EdD - abbreviation for Doctor of Education
  • Degree Lower: Doctor of Philosophy - doctoral degree in various disciplines; commonly abbreviated as PhD
