navy base

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪvi beɪs / (AmE), / ˈneɪvi beɪs / (BrE)
A military installation where naval vessels are housed, maintained, and supplied, often serving as a command center for naval operations.
military facility for naval forces

Examples

The fleet returned to the navy base after completing its training exercises.
Tight security is always maintained at the navy base to protect sensitive operations.
He was stationed at a remote navy base for two years during his military service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naval base - a military base for a fleet of naval ships
