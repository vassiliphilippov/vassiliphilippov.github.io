rice cooker

1. NOUN

/ raɪs ˈkʊkər / (AmE), / raɪs ˈkʊkə / (BrE)
An electric kitchen appliance designed to automatically cook or steam rice.
electric appliance for cooking rice

Examples

She bought a new rice cooker to make meal preparation easier.
The rice cooker keeps the rice warm after it is done cooking.
Many households in Asia use a rice cooker every day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steamer - appliance for cooking food with steam
  • Synonym: slow cooker - appliance for slow cooking foods
