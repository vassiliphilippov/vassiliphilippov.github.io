medical man

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl mæn / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl man / (BrE)
a male doctor or physician; a man who practices medicine, especially as referred to in historical or literary contexts
male doctor or physician (historical)

Examples

The village medical man visited the patient every day during his illness.
Her father, a respected medical man, worked at the city hospital for decades.
In Victorian times, a medical man was often consulted for both physical and mental ailments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doctor - a person qualified to practice medicine
  • Synonym: physician - a medical practitioner
