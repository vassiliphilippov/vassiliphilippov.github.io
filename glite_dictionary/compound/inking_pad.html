inking pad

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋkɪŋ pæd / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkɪŋ pad / (BrE)
a small block of absorbent material soaked with ink, used to apply ink evenly to a rubber stamp
pad soaked with ink for stamping

Examples

She pressed the stamp onto the inking pad before marking the postcard.
The inking pad needs to be replaced when it dries out.
Teachers often use a inking pad for grading papers with rubber stamps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stamp pad - a pad soaked with ink for inking stamps
