yuppity attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈjʌpɪti ˈætɪtjud / (AmE), / ˈjʌpɪti ˈætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
An attitude or mindset characterized by materialism, superficiality, and preoccupation with status, often associated with young urban professionals.
materialistic, status-driven attitude
informal

Examples

Her yuppity attitude was obvious when she talked only about designer brands and exclusive parties.
The new manager's yuppity attitude alienated many of the longtime workers in the company.
Despite his talents, his yuppity attitude left a negative impression during the interview.

Synset Examples

His yuppity attitude made it hard to connect with others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yuppie - a young, well-paid professional who is concerned with status and material possessions

Etymology

Formed from "yuppity" (a playful extension of "yuppie", itself from "young urban professional") and "attitude".
