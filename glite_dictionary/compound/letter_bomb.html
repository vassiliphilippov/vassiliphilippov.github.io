letter bomb

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛtər bɑm / (AmE), / ˈlɛtə bɒm / (BrE)
An explosive device concealed in an envelope or package, designed to detonate when opened.
explosive device sent in mail

Examples

The authorities intercepted a letter bomb before it reached its intended recipient.
Police are investigating after a letter bomb exploded at a government office yesterday.
Security staff are trained to recognize suspicious packages that may contain a letter bomb.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parcel bomb - an explosive concealed in a package or parcel
