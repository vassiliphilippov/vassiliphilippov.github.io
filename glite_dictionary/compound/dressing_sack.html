dressing sack

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɛsɪŋ sæk / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsɪŋ sak / (BrE)
A woman's loose, informal jacket, typically worn over other clothing while dressing or at home.
loose women's jacket worn while dressing

Examples

She wore a dressing sack while getting ready for the evening party.
In Victorian times, a dressing sack was a common garment among fashionable women.
Her grandmother's dressing sack was made of delicate silk and lace.
