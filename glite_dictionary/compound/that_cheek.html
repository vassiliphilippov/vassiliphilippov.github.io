that cheek

1. NOUN

/ ðæt tʃik / (AmE), / ðət tʃiːk / (BrE)
used to express surprise or annoyance at someone's audacity or impudence
expression of audacity or impudence

Examples

I can't believe he spoke to the teacher like that—that cheek!
You'd think he'd apologize, but no; that cheek just left the room smiling.
She tried to return the gift she broke—that cheek never fails to amaze me.

Synset Examples

With that cheek, I can't believe he still has friends.
She had the cheek to wear white to the wedding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impudence - rude or disrespectful behavior; audacity
  • Synonym: cheek - audacity or disrespect; impudence (informal sense)
