bluish black

1. ADJ

/ ˈbluːɪʃ ˈblæk / (AmE), / ˈbluːɪʃ ˈblak / (BrE)
Of a black color with a slight tinge or hint of blue.
black with a hint of blue

Examples

The bird's feathers shimmered with a bluish black hue in the morning light.
She wore a dress of bluish black silk that seemed almost iridescent.
The artist mixed paints to achieve the perfect bluish black for the night sky.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: black - the darkest color, the color of coal or soot
  • Synonym: blue-black - a deep shade between blue and black
