walking horse

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑkɪŋ hɔrs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkɪŋ hɔːs / (BrE)
a breed of horse known for its stamina and trained to perform a fast, smooth running walk
breed of horse trained for a running walk

Examples

The walking horse impressed the judges with its smooth, gliding gait.
Many riders choose a walking horse for trail riding because of its comfort and endurance.
This farm specializes in breeding walking horses for competitions and shows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tennessee Walking Horse - a breed of walking horse especially known for its distinctive running walk
