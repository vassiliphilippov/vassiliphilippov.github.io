northern oak fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðərn oʊk fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən əʊk fɜːn / (BrE)
a yellow-green fern found in rocky areas of the Northern Hemisphere, known for its delicate, spreading fronds.
yellow-green fern of the Northern Hemisphere

Examples

We spotted a cluster of northern oak ferns growing along the shaded hillside trail.
The northern oak fern thrives best in cool, damp, and rocky environments.
Botanists often study the unique leaf pattern of the northern oak fern in boreal forests.
