time and motion study

1. NOUN

/ taɪm ənd ˈmoʊʃən ˈstʌdi / (AmE), / taɪm ən ˈməʊʃən ˈstʌdi / (BrE)
an analysis of a specific task or job to determine the most efficient method by measuring the time and effort required for each part of the process
analysis for job efficiency improvement

Examples

The company ordered a time and motion study to streamline its manufacturing process.
After conducting a time and motion study, the team identified several areas for improvement.
A thorough time and motion study can significantly increase workplace productivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: efficiency - the ability to accomplish a job with minimum expenditure of time and effort
