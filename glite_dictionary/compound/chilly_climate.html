chilly climate

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪli ˈklaɪmət / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪli ˈklaɪmət / (BrE)
a climate characterized by cold or moderately cold weather conditions
cold or cool weather conditions

Examples

The chilly climate in the mountains makes warm clothing essential most of the year.
Many plants struggle to grow in a chilly climate due to the low temperatures.
She moved south to escape the chilly climate of her hometown.

Synset Examples

I prefer living in a chilly climate over a hot one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cold climate - a climate with low temperatures
