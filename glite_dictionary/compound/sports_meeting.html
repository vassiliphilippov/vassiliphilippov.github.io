sports meeting

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrts ˌmitɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts ˌmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
an event at which various athletic contests are held, often between different teams or athletes
athletic competition event

Examples

The annual sports meeting will be held at the city stadium this weekend.
Many students are excited to participate in the school's sports meeting next month.
At the national sports meeting, he won two gold medals for sprinting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athletics meeting - event for athletic competitions
