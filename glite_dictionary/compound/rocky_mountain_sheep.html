Rocky Mountain sheep

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɑki ˈmaʊntən ʃiːp / (AmE), / ˌrɒki ˈmaʊntɪn ʃiːp / (BrE)
a wild sheep native to the mountainous regions of western North America, noted for its large, curved horns
a wild sheep of western North America

Examples

We spotted a herd of Rocky Mountain sheep climbing the steep slopes at dawn.
The Rocky Mountain sheep is renowned for its impressive curled horns.
Photographers travel for miles to capture images of Rocky Mountain sheep in their natural habitat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bighorn sheep - wild sheep species native to North America, known for large curved horns
