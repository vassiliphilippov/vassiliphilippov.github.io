rosy-purple

1. ADJ

/ ˈroʊzi ˈpɝːpəl / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzi ˈpɜːpl / (BrE)
Having the color of purple with a rose or pinkish tinge.
purple with a pinkish tinge

Examples

The sunset painted the clouds a beautiful rosy-purple hue.
She chose a rosy-purple dress for the evening party.
The artist mixed red and blue to achieve a perfect rosy-purple shade.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: purple - a color intermediate between red and blue
  • Degree Lower: rose - a shade of pink or red named after the rose flower
