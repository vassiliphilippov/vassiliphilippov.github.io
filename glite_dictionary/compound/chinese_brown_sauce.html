Chinese brown sauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪniz braʊn sɔs / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪniːz braʊn sɔːs / (BrE)
a dark, savory sauce used in Chinese cuisine, typically made with soy sauce, broth, and other seasonings
savory sauce in Chinese cuisine

Examples

The beef with Chinese brown sauce is one of the most popular dishes at the restaurant.
He asked for extra Chinese brown sauce to pour over his vegetables.
Homemade Chinese brown sauce can be prepared using soy sauce, garlic, and ginger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soy sauce - a dark, salty sauce made from fermented soybeans
