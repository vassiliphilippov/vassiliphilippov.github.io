sleepy dick

1. NOUN

/ ˈsliːpi dɪk / (AmE), / ˈsliːpi dɪk / (BrE)
A common Old World herb (Ornithogalum umbellatum) with grasslike leaves and clusters of star-shaped white flowers with green stripes, naturalized in the eastern United States.
