to share a laugh

1. VERB

/ ʃɛr ə læf / (AmE), / ʃeə ə lɑːf / (BrE)
to enjoy a humorous moment together with someone
enjoy a humorous moment together

Examples

We shared a laugh after recalling a funny story from our childhood.
The team shared a laugh before returning to work, lightening the mood in the office.
They often share a laugh over silly movies on Friday nights.

Synset Examples

They shared a laugh over coffee, just like amigos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laugh together - to laugh at the same time with another person
