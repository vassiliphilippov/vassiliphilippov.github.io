hand blower

1. NOUN

/ hænd ˈbloʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / hænd ˈbləʊ.ə / (BrE)
A hand-held electric device that blows warm air for drying and styling hair.
electric device for drying hair

Examples

After her shower, she used the hand blower to dry her hair quickly before work.
The hotel room was equipped with a small hand blower attached to the bathroom wall.
He replaced his old hand blower with a newer, quieter model.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair dryer - an electric device for drying hair with a stream of warm air
