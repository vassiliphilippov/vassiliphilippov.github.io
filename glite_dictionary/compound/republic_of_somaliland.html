Republic of Somaliland

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌsoʊməˈlænd / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌsɒməˈlænd / (BrE)
a self-declared, de facto independent state in the Horn of Africa, generally recognized internationally as an autonomous region of Somalia
self-declared state in the Horn of Africa

Examples

The Republic of Somaliland declared its independence from Somalia in 1991.
Many countries do not officially recognize the Republic of Somaliland as a sovereign nation.
Despite lack of recognition, the Republic of Somaliland maintains its own government and currency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Somalia - country in East Africa, internationally recognized as including Somaliland
