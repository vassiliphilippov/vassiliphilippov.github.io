honey cake

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌni keɪk / (AmE), / ˈhʌni keɪk / (BrE)
a sweet, spicy cake made with honey as a principal sweetener, often associated with traditional or festive recipes
sweet, spicy cake made with honey

Examples

For Rosh Hashanah, my grandmother always bakes a delicious honey cake.
The aroma of honey cake filled the kitchen during the winter holidays.
She brought a homemade honey cake to the office party, and it was gone in minutes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gingerbread - a sweet, spiced cake or biscuit typically flavored with ginger and molasses or honey
