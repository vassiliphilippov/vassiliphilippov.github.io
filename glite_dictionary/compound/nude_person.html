nude person

1. NOUN

/ nuːd ˈpɜrsn̩ / (AmE), / njuːd ˈpɜːs(ə)n / (BrE)
a person who is not wearing any clothes; a naked person
person not wearing clothes

Examples

The artist hired a nude person to pose for her latest painting.
Hotels sometimes offer special pools or saunas for nude persons.
In some cultures, seeing a nude person in public is considered shocking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nude - a person not wearing clothes (as a noun)
  • Antonym: clothed person - a person who is wearing clothes
