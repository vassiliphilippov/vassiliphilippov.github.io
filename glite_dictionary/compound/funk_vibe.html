funk vibe

1. NOUN

/ fʌŋk vaɪb / (AmE), / fʌŋk vaɪb / (BrE)
A lively and rhythmic atmosphere, typically associated with funk music or culture.
lively, rhythmic atmosphere (music, culture)

Examples

The new club downtown has a real funk vibe that attracts music lovers each weekend.
Her playlist brings a funk vibe to every party she hosts.
The artist’s use of bright colors and bold patterns gives the room a modern funk vibe.

Synset Examples

The party had a great funk vibe going on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groove - a rhythmic quality or feel, especially in music
