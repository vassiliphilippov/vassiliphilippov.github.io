honing machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊ.nɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈhəʊ.nɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine tool used for the abrasive machining process of honing, typically to improve the finish or geometry of a surface
machine for abrasive honing process

Examples

The new honing machine in the workshop significantly improved the quality of the engine cylinders.
A skilled operator is required to set up a honing machine for precision work.
Before final assembly, each gear passes through the honing machine to achieve a flawless finish.

Synset Examples

A honing machine, ironically, is relatively inaccurate and compliant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hone - to sharpen or finish by rubbing with an abrasive

Etymology

From 'hone' (to sharpen) and 'machine' (mechanical device).
