Cypriot pound

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪpriət paʊnd / (AmE), / ˈsɪprɪət paʊnd / (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in Cyprus, equal to 100 cents, replaced by the euro in 2008.
former currency of Cyprus

Examples

Before adopting the euro, Cyprus used the Cypriot pound as its currency.
Tourists exchanged their euros for Cypriot pounds when visiting Cyprus before 2008.
Old banknotes of the Cypriot pound are now collectors' items.
