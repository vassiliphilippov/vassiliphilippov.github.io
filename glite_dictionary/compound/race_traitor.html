race traitor

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪs ˌtreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈreɪs ˌtreɪtə / (BrE)
A person who is regarded as betraying or being disloyal to their own race or racial group, often used as a derogatory accusation.
person accused of betraying their race
offensive

Examples

She was called a race traitor after publicly supporting the opposing side.
The internet forum erupted when one user accused another of being a race traitor.
He rejected the label of race traitor, insisting that his views were based on universal values.

Synset Examples

Some accused her of being a race traitor for supporting diversity.
He was labeled a race traitor for his outspoken views.
The term 'race traitor' is often used in heated debates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traitor - a person who betrays someone or something
