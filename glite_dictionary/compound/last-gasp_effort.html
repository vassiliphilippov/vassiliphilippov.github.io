last-gasp effort

1. NOUN

/ læst ɡæsp ˈɛfɚt / (AmE), / lɑːst ɡɑːsp ˈɛfət / (BrE)
a final, desperate attempt to achieve something when failure seems imminent
final desperate attempt

Examples

The team made a last-gasp effort to score before the final whistle blew.
Her last-gasp effort to finish the project paid off in the end.
In a last-gasp effort, he submitted his application just minutes before the deadline.

Synset Examples

The last-gasp effort surprised everyone in the audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: last-ditch effort - final attempt before giving up
