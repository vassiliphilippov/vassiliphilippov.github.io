explication de texte

1. NOUN

/ ɛkˌsplɪkeɪʃən də ˈtɛkst / (AmE), / ɛkˌsplɪkeɪʃən də ˈtɛkst / (BrE)
A method of literary criticism that involves close, detailed analysis of a text to uncover its structure and meaning.
method of close literary analysis

Examples

The professor asked us to perform an explication de texte on the opening paragraph of the novel.
His essay demonstrated a thorough explication de texte that revealed the subtle symbolism in the poem.
In French literary studies, explication de texte is considered an essential analytical skill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: close reading - careful, detailed interpretation of a literary text

Etymology

From French 'explication de texte' (explanation of text).
