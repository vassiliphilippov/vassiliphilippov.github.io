hot coffee

1. NOUN

/ hɑt ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / hɒt ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
Coffee served at a high temperature, typically fresh and steaming.
coffee served hot

Examples

He burned his tongue on a cup of hot coffee this morning.
After the long walk in the snow, she enjoyed some hot coffee by the fire.
The café serves hot coffee in ceramic mugs to keep it warm.

Synset Examples

It was a chilly morning, perfect for hot coffee.
She prefers her coffee hot, not lukewarm.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: iced coffee - coffee served cold with ice
