squash racquet

1. NOUN

/ skwɑʃ ˈrækət / (AmE), / skwɒʃ ˈrækɪt / (BrE)
a small, lightweight racket with a long handle, used for playing squash
racket used for playing squash

Examples

She gripped her squash racquet tightly before stepping onto the court.
A new squash racquet can significantly improve your performance in the game.
You must never hit the walls with your squash racquet, as it might get damaged.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tennis racket - racket used for playing tennis
  • Synonym: badminton racket - racket used for playing badminton
