ghetto blaster

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɛtoʊ ˈblæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡɛtəʊ ˈblɑːstə / (BrE)
a large, portable stereo, especially one used to play loud music in public places
large portable stereo, often played loudly
slang

Examples

He carried his ghetto blaster everywhere, playing hip-hop on the street corner.
In the 1980s, a ghetto blaster was an essential item for music lovers.
They turned up the ghetto blaster and started an impromptu dance party in the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boombox - a large portable radio and cassette player with powerful speakers

Etymology

Slang term from 1980s America, referencing urban neighborhoods (‘ghetto’) and the device's loud music (‘blaster’).
