ethnic music

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛθ.nɪk ˈmju.zɪk / (AmE), / ˈɛθ.nɪk ˈmjuː.zɪk / (BrE)
Traditional music characteristic of a particular cultural group, often passed down orally and reflecting the customs and life of a community.
traditional music of a cultural group

Examples

Many festivals feature performances of ethnic music from around the world.
She studies ethnic music to better understand the cultures of different communities.
His collection includes rare recordings of ethnic music from Eastern Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: folk music - traditional music originating among the common people of a country or region
