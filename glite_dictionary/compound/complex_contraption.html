complex contraption

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈplɛks kənˈtræp.ʃən / (AmE), / kəmˈplɛks kənˈtræp.ʃən / (BrE)
a complicated device or piece of equipment, often with many parts and an unclear purpose
complicated device or machine

Examples

The inventor presented a complex contraption that could perform multiple tasks at once.
We spent hours trying to assemble the complex contraption according to the manual.
No one understood how the complex contraption in the garage was supposed to work.

Synset Examples

She marveled at the complex contraption he designed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contraption - an unusual or strange device
  • Synonym: device - a thing made for a particular purpose
  • Synonym: machine - a piece of equipment with moving parts
