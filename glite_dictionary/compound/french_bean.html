French bean

1. NOUN

/ frɛntʃ bin / (AmE), / frɛntʃ biːn / (BrE)
A variety of common bean with long, slender green pods, often eaten as a vegetable.
a type of slender green bean

Examples

She sautéed the French beans with garlic for a healthy side dish.
A handful of fresh French beans adds color and crunch to any salad.
The market had a large basket of crisp French beans for sale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: green bean - a common bean with tender green pods eaten as a vegetable
