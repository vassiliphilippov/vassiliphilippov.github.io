bed-wettingly funny

1. ADJ

/ bɛdˌwɛtɪŋli ˈfʌni / (AmE), / bɛdˌwɛtɪŋli ˈfʌni / (BrE)
Extremely funny; so amusing as to make someone lose self-control.
extremely funny; uproariously amusing
informal

Examples

His stand-up routine was bed-wettingly funny and kept the audience in stitches all night.
The comedian's new special is bed-wettingly funny—I laughed so hard I had to pause it.
We found the sketch bed-wettingly funny and couldn't stop talking about our favorite jokes afterward.

The movie was bed-wettingly funny from start to finish.
