beet blight

1. NOUN

/ bit blaɪt / (AmE), / biːt blaɪt / (BrE)
a disease affecting beet plants, typically caused by fungi or bacteria, resulting in damage or reduced growth
disease affecting beet plants

Examples

Farmers lost much of their crop to beet blight last season.
Researchers are developing resistant varieties to combat beet blight.
Symptoms of beet blight include yellowing leaves and wilting plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blight - a plant disease marked by wilting, lesions, or decay
