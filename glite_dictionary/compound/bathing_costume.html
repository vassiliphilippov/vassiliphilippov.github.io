bathing costume

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪðɪŋ ˌkɑstjum / (AmE), / ˈbeɪðɪŋ ˌkɒstjuːm / (BrE)
a tight-fitting piece of clothing worn for swimming
swimming garment

Examples

She slipped into her bathing costume before heading down to the pool.
In the 1920s, women's bathing costumes were much more modest than today.
Don't forget to pack your bathing costume for the beach holiday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimsuit - garment worn for swimming
  • Degree Lower: bikini - two-piece swimsuit for women
  • Degree Lower: swimming trunks - shorts worn for swimming, usually by men
