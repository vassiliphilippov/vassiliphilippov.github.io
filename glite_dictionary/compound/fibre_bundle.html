fibre bundle

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪbər ˈbʌndəl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪbə ˈbʌndəl / (BrE)
a collection of fibers grouped together, especially referring to a bundle of nerve fibers in biology or anatomy
a group of fibers, especially nerve fibers

Examples

The optic nerve is composed of a fibre bundle that transmits visual information to the brain.
The surgeon carefully isolated a fibre bundle during the procedure to avoid damaging the nerve.
Advanced imaging techniques can reveal each fibre bundle within the spinal cord.
