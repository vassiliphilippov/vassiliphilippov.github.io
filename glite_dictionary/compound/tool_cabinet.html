tool cabinet

1. NOUN

/ tuːl ˈkæbɪnət / (AmE), / tuːl ˈkæbɪnɪt / (BrE)
a storage cabinet, box, or chest designed for holding hand tools
cabinet or box for storing tools

Examples

He keeps all his screwdrivers neatly arranged in the tool cabinet in the garage.
The mechanic unlocked the tool cabinet to access his specialized wrenches.
A well-organized tool cabinet can save you time during repair projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toolbox - a box for holding tools
