superordinate word

1. NOUN

/ suːˈpɚˌɔːrdn̩ət wɝːd / (AmE), / suːˈpɔːr.dɪ.nət wɜːd / (BrE)
A word with a broader, more general meaning than another word; a term whose referent includes that of a more specific word.
word with a more general meaning

Examples

In the animal kingdom, the superordinate word for 'sparrow' is 'bird'.
'Furniture' is a superordinate word that includes chairs, tables, and sofas.
The teacher asked her students to identify a superordinate word for 'van' and 'car'.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hyponym - a word of more specific meaning than a general term
  • Synonym: hypernym - a word with a broad meaning constituting a category into which words with more specific meanings fall

Etymology

From Latin superordinare (to place above) + English word.
