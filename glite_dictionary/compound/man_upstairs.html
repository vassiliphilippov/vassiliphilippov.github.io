Man Upstairs

1. NOUN

/ mæn ʌpˈstɛɹz / (AmE), / mæn ʌpˈstɛəz / (BrE)
(informal) A colloquial term for God, used in informal speech to refer to a divine being watching over people.
colloquial term for God
informal

Examples

Many people turn to the Man Upstairs when they are in trouble or need guidance.
He joked that the Man Upstairs must really have a sense of humor.
Whenever something goes wrong, she blames or praises the Man Upstairs.

Synset Examples

I believe the Man Upstairs is watching over us.
She prayed to the Man Upstairs for guidance.
The Man Upstairs has a plan for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: God - the supreme being or creator in monotheistic religions
