knob celery

1. NOUN

/ nɑb ˈsɛl.ə.ri / (AmE), / nɒb ˈsɛl.əri / (BrE)
A type of celery cultivated for its thick, edible, aromatic root, commonly used in cooking; also known as celeriac.
celery grown for its edible root

Examples

She added diced knob celery to the stew for extra flavor.
The farmer grows both leafy celery and knob celery in his fields.
Many recipes for winter soups call for knob celery as a main ingredient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celeriac - another term for knob celery
