middle-aged man

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪd.əl ˈeɪdʒd mæn / (AmE), / ˌmɪd.əl ˈeɪdʒd mæn / (BrE)
A man who is roughly between the ages of 45 and 65.
man aged 45–65

Examples

His father is a middle-aged man who enjoys playing tennis on weekends.
The company was founded by a middle-aged man with years of business experience.
At the meeting, a middle-aged man stood up to ask a question about the new policy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: middle-aged woman - a woman who is roughly between 45 and 65 years old
