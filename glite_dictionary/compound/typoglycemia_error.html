typoglycemia error

1. NOUN

/ taɪpoʊɡlaɪˈsiːmiə ˈɛrər / (AmE), / tʌɪpəʊɡlʌɪˈsiːmɪə ˈɛrər / (BrE)
an error in text where the letters within words are jumbled, but the text remains readable to most readers
text error with jumbled yet readable words
informal
