birdseed grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝd.siːd ˌɡɹæs / (AmE), / ˈbɜːd.siːd ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a type of grass, especially canary grass, whose seeds are commonly used as food for caged birds
grass grown for birdseed

Examples

We planted birdseed grass in the garden to feed the finches.
The pet store stocks several varieties of birdseed grass for exotic birds.
After harvesting, the farmer sold bags of birdseed grass seeds to local bird owners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: canary grass - a grass cultivated for its seeds, used as bird food
