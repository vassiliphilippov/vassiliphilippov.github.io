petrol tank

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛtroʊl tæŋk / (AmE), / ˈpɛtrəl taŋk / (BrE)
a container in a vehicle for storing petrol (gasoline) to supply the engine
container in a vehicle for petrol
british

Examples

After filling the petrol tank, she checked the oil and tire pressure.
The car's petrol tank can hold up to fifty liters.
He accidentally put diesel fuel into the petrol tank, which caused engine problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gas tank - container in a vehicle for gasoline (American term)
