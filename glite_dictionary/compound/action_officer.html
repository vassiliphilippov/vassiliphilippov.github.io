action officer

1. NOUN

/ ˈækʃən ˌɑfəsər / (AmE), / ˈækʃən ˌɒfɪsə / (BrE)
an officer, especially in intelligence or the military, responsible for carrying out a specific task or mission, often in covert or sensitive operations
officer assigned to carry out a specific mission

Examples

The action officer was briefed on his assignment early that morning.
During the covert mission, the action officer played a crucial role in its success.
Her experience as an action officer made her an asset to the intelligence team.
