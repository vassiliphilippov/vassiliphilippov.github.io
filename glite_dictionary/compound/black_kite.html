black kite

1. NOUN

/ blæk kaɪt / (AmE), / blak kaɪt / (BrE)
a medium-sized bird of prey with dark plumage, found in the Old World and feeding chiefly on carrion
a dark Old World bird of prey

Examples

The black kite soared above the river, searching for scraps of food.
During our safari, we spotted a black kite feeding on carrion near the edge of the forest.
Black kites are often seen circling over cities, especially where garbage is abundant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kite - a medium-sized bird of prey in the family Accipitridae
