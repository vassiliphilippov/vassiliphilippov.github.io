inner monster

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnɚ ˈmɑːnstɚ / (AmE), / ˈɪnə ˈmɒnstə / (BrE)
a metaphorical term for a person's inner fears, struggles, or darker instincts that they must confront and overcome
a person's internal fears or struggles

Examples

It's important to acknowledge your inner monster before it controls your actions.
The novel explores how each character confronts their own inner monster.
Meditation helped him tame the inner monster that once caused him anxiety.

Synset Examples

She faced her inner monster and overcame her fears.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inner demon - a person's internal struggles or negative emotions
