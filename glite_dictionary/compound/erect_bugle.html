erect bugle

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈrɛkt ˈbjuːɡl̩ / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛkt ˈbjuːɡl̩ / (BrE)
an upright perennial plant with bright blue flowers, native to southern Europe, and having rhizomatous roots
upright European plant with blue flowers

Examples

The erect bugle blooms in early spring, adding a splash of color to the garden.
Botanists observed the distinct blue petals of the erect bugle along the forest edge.
Gardeners appreciate the hardy nature of the erect bugle in temperate climates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bugle - a plant of the genus Ajuga, especially with blue flowers
