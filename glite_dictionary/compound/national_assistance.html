national assistance

1. NOUN

/ ˌnæʃənl əˈsɪstəns / (AmE), / ˌnæʃənəl əˈsɪstəns / (BrE)
benefits provided by the government to ensure individuals' incomes reach a minimum level established by law
government benefits to meet minimum income

Examples

After losing his job, he applied for national assistance to support his family.
The introduction of national assistance helped reduce poverty across the country.
She relied on national assistance when her pension was not enough to cover her expenses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welfare - government support for the well-being of citizens, especially financial aid
  • Synonym: public assistance - government-provided support for people in need
