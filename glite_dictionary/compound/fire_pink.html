fire pink

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪər pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə pɪŋk / (BrE)
A perennial herb native to eastern North America, known for its bright red flowers with narrow, notched petals.
North American plant with red, notched flowers

Examples

The fire pink stands out in the woodland with its brilliant red flowers.
Gardeners value fire pink for its ability to attract hummingbirds.
We spotted several fire pinks blooming along the nature trail in early summer.
