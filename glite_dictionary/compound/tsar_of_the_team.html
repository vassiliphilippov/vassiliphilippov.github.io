tsar of the team

1. NOUN

/ zɑr əv ðə tim / (AmE), / zɑːr əv ðə tiːm / (BrE)
a person who is the dominant or authoritative leader within a team or group
dominant leader of a team

Examples

He quickly became the tsar of the team, making all the important decisions.
As tsar of the team, she set the tone for every meeting and discussion.
Many felt intimidated by the new manager, who acted like the tsar of the team.

Synset Examples

The project manager was a real tsar of the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group, organization, or country
  • Degree Higher: dictator - a ruler with total power over a country, typically one who has obtained control by force
  • Degree Lower: member - an individual belonging to a group, but not holding a leadership role

Etymology

From 'tsar', a term for a Russian emperor, used in English to denote a powerful leader; extended figuratively to teams and organizations.
