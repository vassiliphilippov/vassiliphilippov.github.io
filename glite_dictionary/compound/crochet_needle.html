crochet needle

1. NOUN

/ ˈkroʊʃeɪ ˌniːdəl / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊʃeɪ ˌniːdl / (BrE)
A slender tool with a hook at one end, used for drawing thread or yarn through loops to make crochet fabric.
hooked needle for crocheting

Examples

She picked up her crochet needle and began working on a new scarf.
A crochet needle with a comfortable handle can make crocheting easier.
You need yarn and a crochet needle to start your first project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crochet hook - a tool with a hook for crocheting; alternative term for crochet needle
