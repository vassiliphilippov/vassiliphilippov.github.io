fund-raising effort

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌndˌɹeɪzɪŋ ˈɛfɚt / (AmE), / ˈfʌndˌɹeɪzɪŋ ˈefət / (BrE)
an organized activity or campaign undertaken to collect money for a particular purpose, especially for a charity or cause
organized campaign to raise money

Examples

The school's annual fund-raising effort helped them build a new playground.
Their fund-raising effort attracted support from hundreds of donors in the community.
After the hurricane, a massive fund-raising effort was launched to aid the victims.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fund-raiser - a person or event that raises money for a cause
