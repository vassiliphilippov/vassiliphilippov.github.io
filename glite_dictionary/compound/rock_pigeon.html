rock pigeon

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈpɪdʒən / (AmE), / rɒk ˈpɪdʒɪn / (BrE)
a Eurasian pigeon (Columba livia) with pale grey plumage and black-striped wings, from which most domestic pigeons have descended
a species of pale grey Eurasian pigeon

Examples

The rock pigeon can be found in cities and rural areas around the world.
Most domestic pigeons are descended from the wild rock pigeon.
The rock pigeon has distinctive black stripes on its wings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pigeon - any of various birds of the family Columbidae
