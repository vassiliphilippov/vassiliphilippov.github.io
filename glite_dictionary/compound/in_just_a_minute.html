in just a minute

1. ADV

/ ɪn dʒəst ə ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / ɪn dʒəst ə ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
very soon; after a very short time
very soon; shortly

Examples

I'll call you back in just a minute when I finish this email.
The waiter said our food would arrive in just a minute.
If you have a question, I can help you in just a minute.

Synset Examples

I’ll be with you in just a minute.

Related Terms
