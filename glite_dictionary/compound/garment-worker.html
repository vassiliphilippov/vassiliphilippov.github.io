garment-worker

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrmənt ˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːmənt ˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person employed to make or sew garments, especially in factories or workshops.
person who makes garments professionally

Examples

The garment-worker spent long hours sewing shirts in the factory.
Many garment-workers organized for better pay and safer working conditions.
The story highlights the daily struggles faced by a young garment-worker in Bangladesh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seamstress - a woman whose occupation is sewing
  • Synonym: tailor - a person whose occupation is making fitted clothes
  • Synonym: dressmaker - a person who makes women's dresses
