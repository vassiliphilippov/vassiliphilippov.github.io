English teacher

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈtitʃər / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈtiːtʃə / (BrE)
a person who teaches the English language as a subject, either as a first or additional language
a person who teaches English

Examples

My sister is an English teacher at the local high school.
He became an English teacher after studying literature at university.
The English teacher assigned us a new book to read over the holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school
