NYSE MKT

1. PROPN

/ ˌɛn ˌwaɪ ˌɛs ˈiː ˈɛm ˈkeɪ ˈmɑrkət / (AmE), / ˌɛn ˌwaɪ ˌiːs ˈɛm ˈkeɪ ˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
a stock exchange in New York City, formerly known as the New York Curb Exchange
New York stock exchange, former Curb Exchange

Examples

Shares of XYZ Corp began trading on the NYSE MKT this morning.
The NYSE MKT caters to small and mid-sized companies seeking public investment.
Many tech startups have chosen the NYSE MKT as their listing venue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New York Stock Exchange - the largest stock exchange in the world

Etymology

NYSE is an acronym for New York Stock Exchange; MKT from 'market'.
