seed cake

1. NOUN

/ sid keɪk / (AmE), / siːd keɪk / (BrE)
a sweet cake flavored with seeds such as caraway or sesame, often with added lemon
sweet cake flavored with seeds and lemon

Examples

She served homemade seed cake with tea in the afternoon.
The recipe for traditional seed cake calls for caraway seeds and lemon zest.
He bought a seed cake from the bakery for the family picnic.
