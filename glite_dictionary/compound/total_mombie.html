total mombie

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬əl ˈmɒm.bi / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl ˈmɒm.bi / (BrE)
An extremely tired and unresponsive person, often due to lack of sleep, especially in the context of parenting.
person severely tired and unresponsive
informal

Examples

After caring for her newborn all week, she turned into a total mombie at work.
I'm such a total mombie today after staying up with the baby all night.
He joked that his wife was a total mombie until she had her morning coffee.

Synset Examples

After a sleepless night, she felt like a total mombie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zombie - a person in a state of stupor or energylessness

Etymology

Blend of 'mom' and 'zombie', with 'total' intensifying the effect.
