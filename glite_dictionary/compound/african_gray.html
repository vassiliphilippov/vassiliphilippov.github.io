African gray

1. NOUN

/ ˌæfrɪkən ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˌaf.rɪ.kən ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
a species of medium-sized, grey-colored parrot native to equatorial Africa, known for its intelligence and ability to mimic human speech
species of intelligent, grey African parrot

Examples

The African gray is prized for its remarkable ability to mimic human speech.
Many bird enthusiasts keep an African gray as a companion animal because of its intelligence.
Veterinarians recommend a balanced diet for an African gray to ensure its long life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parrot - a bird, often colorful, known for mimicking sounds
