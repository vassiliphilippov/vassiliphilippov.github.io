heteronazi rhetoric

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɛt̬.əɹ.oʊˈnɑː.zi ˈɹɛt.ɚ.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɛt.ə.ˈnɑː.zi ˈrɛt.ər.ɪk / (BrE)
extremely hostile or harmful speech directed against LGBTQ+ people, typically likened to fascist or Nazi attitudes
hostile speech likened to fascist anti-LGBTQ+ sentiment
offensive

Examples

The activist spoke out against the heteronazi rhetoric spreading on social media.
Many people were shocked by the blatant heteronazi rhetoric in the politician's speech.
Teachers work to create a safe environment free from heteronazi rhetoric and discrimination.

Synset Examples

The online group was condemned for its heteronazi rhetoric.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hate speech - speech that attacks or demeans a group based on characteristics such as race, religion, or sexual orientation
  • Synonym: homophobic rhetoric - speech expressing hostility toward LGBTQ+ people

Etymology

Blend of "hetero" (heterosexual) and "Nazi" (from the National Socialist German Workers' Party), combined with "rhetoric".
