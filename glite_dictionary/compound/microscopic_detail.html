microscopic detail

1. NOUN

/ ˌmaɪkrəˈskɑpɪk dɪˈteɪl / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəˈskɒpɪk dɪˈteɪl / (BrE)
an extremely small or intricate feature that can only be seen clearly under magnification or through careful examination
very small, intricate feature

Examples

The scientist examined the microscopic detail of the tissue sample under the microscope.
She spent hours perfecting every microscopic detail of her miniature sculpture.
The forensic team analyzed the microscopic detail left on the evidence.

Synset Examples

The microscopic details in the painting were stunning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minute detail - tiny or very small detail
