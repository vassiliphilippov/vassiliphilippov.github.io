brass ring

1. NOUN

/ bræs rɪŋ / (AmE), / brɑːs rɪŋ / (BrE)
an opportunity for success, wealth, or achievement; a sought-after prize or reward
an opportunity for great success or reward

Examples

After years of hard work, she finally grabbed the brass ring in her career.
Many young entrepreneurs move to Silicon Valley hoping to catch the brass ring.
He reached for the brass ring and landed the promotion he always wanted.

Synset Examples

he missed the brass ring at the Miami convention

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prize - a reward for victory or achievement
  • Synonym: opportunity - a chance to do something or achieve success

Etymology

From the practice at American carousels where riders could grab a brass ring to win a prize.
