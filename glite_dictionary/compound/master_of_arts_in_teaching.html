Master of Arts in Teaching

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæs.tɚ əv ɑrts ɪn ˈti.tʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.stər əv ɑːts ɪn ˈtiː.tʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a postgraduate degree awarded for advanced study and practice in teaching methods and education theory
postgraduate degree in teaching

Examples

She earned a Master of Arts in Teaching before starting her career as an educator.
Many universities now offer an online Master of Arts in Teaching program for working professionals.
Holding a Master of Arts in Teaching can improve job prospects for aspiring teachers.
