transmission mechanism

1. NOUN

/ trænzˈmɪʃ.ən ˈmɛk.əˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / trænzˈmɪʃ.ən ˈmɛk.ə.nɪ.zəm / (BrE)
a process or method by which an infectious agent is spread from a source to a human being
method by which infection is spread
medical

Examples

Droplet spread is a common transmission mechanism for many respiratory viruses.
Understanding the transmission mechanism of the disease helped doctors control the outbreak.
Insects can serve as a transmission mechanism for a variety of infectious agents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vector - an organism that transmits an infectious pathogen
  • Synonym: route of transmission - the path by which a disease is spread
