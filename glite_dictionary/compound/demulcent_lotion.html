demulcent lotion

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈmʌlsənt ˈloʊʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈmʌlsənt ˈləʊʃən / (BrE)
a lotion containing a soothing substance that relieves irritation of the skin or mucous membranes
soothing lotion for irritation
medical

Examples

After getting sunburned, she applied a demulcent lotion to reduce the redness and discomfort.
Dermatologists often recommend a demulcent lotion for patients with eczema or sensitive skin.
The pharmacist suggested using a demulcent lotion to help soothe the boy’s irritated hands.

Synset Examples

He used a demulcent lotion to soothe his dry skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soothing lotion - lotion designed to soothe irritated skin

Etymology

From Latin 'demulcere' (to caress, soothe) + 'lotion' (from Latin 'lotio', washing).
