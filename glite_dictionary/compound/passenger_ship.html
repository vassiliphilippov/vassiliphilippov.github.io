passenger ship

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæsəndʒɚ ˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈpæsɪndʒə ˌʃɪp / (BrE)
a large ship designed to carry people rather than cargo
a ship for carrying people

Examples

The passenger ship set sail for its maiden voyage across the Atlantic Ocean.
Hundreds of tourists boarded the passenger ship for a two-week cruise.
After years of service, the old passenger ship was retired and turned into a museum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cruise ship - a passenger ship used for pleasure voyages
  • Antonym: cargo ship - a ship designed to carry goods rather than people
