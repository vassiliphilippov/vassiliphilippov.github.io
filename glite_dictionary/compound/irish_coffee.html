Irish coffee

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪrɪʃ ˈkɔfi / (AmE), / ˈaɪrɪʃ ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
A drink made with hot coffee, Irish whiskey, sugar, and a topping of whipped cream.
coffee-based drink with whiskey and cream

Examples

After dinner, she ordered an Irish coffee to warm up.
The bartender carefully floated cream atop the Irish coffee.
I've never tried making Irish coffee at home before.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffee - a hot drink made from roasted and ground coffee beans
