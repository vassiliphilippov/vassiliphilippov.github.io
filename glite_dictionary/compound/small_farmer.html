small farmer

1. NOUN

/ smɔl ˈfɑrmər / (AmE), / smɔːl ˈfɑːmə / (BrE)
a person who owns or manages a small farm, typically cultivating the land with limited resources or acreage
person managing a small farm

Examples

The small farmer struggled to compete with large agricultural businesses in the region.
Government subsidies helped the small farmer invest in better equipment and seeds.
Many small farmers sell their produce directly at local markets.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: large farmer - a person owning or managing a large farm
