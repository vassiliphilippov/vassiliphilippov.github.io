last minute

1. NOUN

/ læst ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / lɑːst ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
the latest possible time before an action must be taken or a decision made
the latest possible moment

Examples

He handed in his application at the last minute and almost missed the deadline.
They cancelled the event at the last minute due to bad weather.
She always leaves her packing until the last minute.

Synset Examples

money became available at the eleventh hour
at the last minute the government changed the rules

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eleventh hour - the latest possible moment

2. NOUN

/ læst ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / lɑːst ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)

the latest possible time before an event

Examples
