to donate a kidney

1. VERB

/ doʊˈneɪt ə ˈkɪdni / (AmE), / dəʊˈneɪt ə ˈkɪdni / (BrE)
to give one of your kidneys to another person in need of a transplant, usually through a medical procedure
give a kidney for transplant

Examples

After learning about her sister's illness, she decided to donate a kidney to help.
Many people choose to donate a kidney to a stranger through anonymous donation programs.
He recovered quickly after choosing to donate a kidney to his father.

Synset Examples

She donated a kidney to save her friend's life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kidney donation - the act of giving a kidney for transplantation
