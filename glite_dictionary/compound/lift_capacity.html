lift capacity

1. NOUN

/ lɪft kəˈpæsəti / (AmE), / lɪft kəˈpæsəti / (BrE)
the maximum load that a crane or similar lifting equipment is designed to safely raise
maximum load a crane can lift

Examples

Always check the lift capacity of the equipment before beginning work on a construction site.
The new crane significantly increases our lift capacity, allowing us to handle heavier components.
Operators must never exceed the stated lift capacity to ensure safe operation.

Synset Examples

This rough terrain crane has a lift capacity of 60 tons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: load capacity - maximum load that something can safely support or carry
