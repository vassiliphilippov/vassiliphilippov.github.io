good guy

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ɡaɪ / (AmE), / ɡʊd ɡaɪ / (BrE)
a person who is considered to be on your side or acting in a morally right way, often contrasted with a villain or opponent
person on your side or acting morally
informal

Examples

Everyone knew Tom was the good guy in the story, always helping others and standing up for what is right.
In this competition, you have to decide who the good guy really is.
She liked to believe that she was the good guy in every disagreement.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: villain - an evil or antagonistic character or person
  • Synonym: hero - a person admired for courageous or noble qualities
