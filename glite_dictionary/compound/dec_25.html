Dec 25

1. NOUN

/ dɛk twɛnti faɪv / (AmE), / dɛk twɛnti faɪv / (BrE)
December 25; Christmas Day, celebrated as a Christian holiday commemorating the birth of Jesus Christ.
Christmas Day; Dec 25th

Examples

Many families gather to celebrate Dec 25 with gifts and festive meals.
Most offices are closed on Dec 25 for the holiday break.
Children often wake up early on Dec 25 to open presents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Christmas - Christian festival celebrating the birth of Jesus Christ, observed on December 25
  • Synonym: December 25 - The twenty-fifth day of December; date of Christmas in the Gregorian calendar
