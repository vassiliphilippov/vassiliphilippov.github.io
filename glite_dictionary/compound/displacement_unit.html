displacement unit

1. NOUN

/ dɪsˈpleɪsmənt ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / dɪsˈpleɪsmənt ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a unit of measurement used to express the volume or capacity of an object or substance.
unit of measurement for volume or capacity

Examples

The volume of the engine was measured in displacement units.
Engineers often convert gallons to cubic meters, which are standard displacement units.
Accurate calculation of displacement units is crucial for shipbuilders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cubic meter - SI unit of volume
  • Synonym: liter - metric unit of capacity, equal to 1,000 cubic centimeters
