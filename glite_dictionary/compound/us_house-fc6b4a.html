US House

1. NOUN

/ ˌjuː ɛs ˈhaʊs / (AmE), / ˌjuː ɛs ˈhaʊs / (BrE)
the lower legislative chamber of the United States Congress, formally known as the House of Representatives.
lower chamber of US Congress
american

Examples

The new bill was approved by the US House before moving to the Senate.
Several members of the US House held a press conference today.
She closely follows debates in the US House on national issues.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: US Senate - the upper legislative chamber of the United States Congress
  • Synonym: House of Representatives - the full formal name of the US House
