early purple orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈɜrli ˈpɝpəl ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈɜːli ˈpɜːpl ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A Eurasian orchid (Orchis mascula) with showy pink or purple flowers arranged in a loose spike.
a Eurasian orchid with purple flowers

Examples

We spotted an early purple orchid growing at the edge of the meadow.
The early purple orchid blooms in spring before many other wildflowers appear.
A cluster of early purple orchids added vibrant color to the woodland path.
