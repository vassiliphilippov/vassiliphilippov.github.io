potato beetle

1. NOUN

/ pəˈteɪtoʊ ˌbiːtəl / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ ˌbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a black-and-yellow beetle that feeds on potato plants, especially in both its adult and larval stages
beetle that feeds on potato plants

Examples

Farmers worry that potato beetle infestations will destroy their crops.
The bright yellow stripes help the potato beetle stand out among the leaves.
Gardeners must check their plants for signs of potato beetle larvae during the summer.
