fashion consultant

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃən kənˈsʌltənt / (AmE), / ˈfæʃən kənˈsʌltənt / (BrE)
a person who offers advice on fashionable clothing and personal style
expert on fashion and style

Examples

She hired a fashion consultant to help revamp her wardrobe for the new season.
The celebrity's fashion consultant selected all the outfits for the photo shoot.
As a fashion consultant, Mark keeps up with the latest trends to advise his clients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashion adviser - a person who advises others about clothing trends and style
