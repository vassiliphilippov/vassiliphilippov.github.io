Battle Born State

1. PROPN

/ ˈbætl bɔrn steɪt / (AmE), / ˈbætl bɔːn steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the U.S. state of Nevada, referring to its admission to the Union during the American Civil War
nickname for Nevada (U.S. state)

Examples

Nevada is famously known as the Battle Born State due to its Civil War heritage.
The Battle Born State attracts many visitors with its casinos and natural beauty.
The phrase 'Welcome to the Battle Born State' can be seen on Nevada's license plates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nevada - a state in the western United States

Etymology

Refers to Nevada's admission to the Union during the Civil War ('born in battle').
