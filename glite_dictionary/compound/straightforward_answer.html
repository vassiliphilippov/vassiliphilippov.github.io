straightforward answer

1. NOUN

/ ˌstreɪtˈfɔrwərd ˈæn.sɚ / (AmE), / ˌstreɪtˈfɔːwəd ˈɑːn.sə / (BrE)
a response that is simple, direct, and easy to understand, without unnecessary complexity or ambiguity
simple and direct response

Examples

She appreciated his straightforward answer to her difficult question.
It's best to give a straightforward answer when someone needs urgent information.
I admire people who offer a straightforward answer rather than avoiding the issue.

Synset Examples

He preferred straightforward answers rather than complicated responses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: direct answer - an answer given without hesitation or evasion
  • Antonym: evasive answer - an answer that avoids giving a clear response
