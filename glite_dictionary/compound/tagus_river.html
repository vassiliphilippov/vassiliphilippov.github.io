Tagus River

1. PROPN

/ ˈteɪɡəs ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈteɪɡəs ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
the longest river on the Iberian Peninsula, flowing through Spain and Portugal into the Atlantic Ocean.
the longest river in the Iberian Peninsula

Examples

The Tagus River forms part of the border between Spain and Portugal.
Lisbon is built on the estuary of the Tagus River.
Many historic towns can be found along the Tagus River.

Etymology

From Latin 'Tagus', the name of the river in ancient sources.
