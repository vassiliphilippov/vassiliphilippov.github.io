loony bin

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːni bɪn / (AmE), / ˈluːni bɪn / (BrE)
An informal and often offensive term for a psychiatric hospital or mental institution.
offensive term for mental hospital
informal slang offensive

Examples

After his breakdown, he spent several weeks in the loony bin recovering.
Some comedians make jokes about the loony bin, but mental health is a serious matter.
She threatened to send him to the loony bin if he didn't calm down.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asylum - a hospital for people with mental illnesses
  • Synonym: psychiatric hospital - a hospital specializing in the treatment of mental illness

Etymology

From "loony" (slang for insane, from lunatic) + "bin" (container; here, metaphor for a place to put people).

2. NOUN

/ ˈluːni ˌbɪn / (AmE), / ˈluːni ˌbɪn / (BrE)
A slang term for a mental hospital, or by extension, a place that seems wild or chaotic.
slang for mental hospital or chaotic place
slang offensive

Examples

After three days with screaming kids, my house felt like a loony bin.
He threatened to send me to a loony bin if I kept talking nonsense.
The office turns into a loony bin every time there's a deadline.

Synset Examples

After that wild party, I felt like I needed a loony bin.
She felt like she was in a loony bin during the meeting.
He joked that his messy room looked like a loony bin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental hospital - a hospital for the care of people with mental illness
  • Synonym: asylum - historical term for a hospital for the mentally ill

Etymology

From 'loony' (slang for 'lunatic'), from obsolete term for a mentally ill person, plus 'bin' as slang for a container or place.
