birthing coach

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɜrθɪŋ ˌkoʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθɪŋ ˌkəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a person, often a partner or trained assistant, who provides emotional and practical support to a woman during childbirth, helping her use techniques learned in childbirth-preparation classes
support person during childbirth

Examples

The birthing coach stayed by her side, offering words of encouragement throughout her labor.
Many expectant mothers choose a birthing coach to help them remain calm and focused during delivery.
He took classes to become a birthing coach before the birth of their first child.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doula - a trained woman who supports another woman during labor and childbirth
