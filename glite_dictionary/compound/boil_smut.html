boil smut

1. NOUN

/ bɔɪl smʌt / (AmE), / bɔɪl smʌt / (BrE)
a common plant disease of Indian corn that produces greyish white swellings which rupture to reveal a black spore mass
plant disease affecting corn

Examples

The farmer noticed boil smut on several ears of corn in his field.
Infected corn plants with boil smut often have swollen tissue that eventually bursts.
Controlling boil smut requires crop rotation and using disease-resistant varieties.
