African tea

1. NOUN

/ ˈæf.rɪ.kən ti / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən tiː / (BrE)
the leaves of the Catha edulis shrub, which are chewed or brewed as tea for their euphoric stimulant effect
leaves of Catha edulis used as a stimulant

Examples

Traditional ceremonies sometimes include the sharing of African tea among elders.
Some people drink African tea to experience heightened alertness and mild euphoria.
The sale and use of African tea are regulated in several countries.
