creme de menthe

1. NOUN

/ ˌkrɛm də ˈmɛnt / (AmE), / ˌkrɛm də ˈmɒ̃t / (BrE)
a sweet, mint-flavoured liqueur that is usually green or white in colour, used in cocktails and desserts
sweet mint-flavoured liqueur

Examples

Many desserts are flavored with creme de menthe for a refreshing mint taste.
He ordered a grasshopper cocktail with creme de menthe as its main ingredient.
The recipe called for a splash of creme de menthe to add color and flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peppermint schnapps - a clear, strong, peppermint-flavoured liqueur

Etymology

From French, meaning 'cream of mint'.
