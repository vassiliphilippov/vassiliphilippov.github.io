keen-eyed

1. ADJ

/ kin aɪd / (AmE), / kiːn aɪd / (BrE)
Having very sharp or acute eyesight; able to notice small details.
having very sharp eyesight

Examples

The keen-eyed detective quickly noticed the footprint by the window.
Only a keen-eyed observer would spot the rare bird in the dense foliage.
Her keen-eyed assistant caught the error before the report was printed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sharp-eyed - having keen or sharp eyesight
  • Synonym: eagle-eyed - having very keen vision
