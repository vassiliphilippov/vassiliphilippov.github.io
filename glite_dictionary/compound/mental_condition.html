mental condition

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.tl̩ kənˈdɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈmen.tl kənˈdɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a state of a person's mental or emotional health, especially as characterized by relatively stable psychological qualities
state of mental or emotional health

Examples

After his accident, doctors monitored his mental condition closely to ensure he was recovering well.
Many factors, including genetics and environment, can influence a person's mental condition.
Anxiety is a common mental condition that can affect people of all ages.

Synset Examples

Stress isn't the only emotional or mental state that can benefit from regular meditation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental state - the condition of a person's thoughts and feelings at a particular time
  • Degree Higher: psychological disorder - a disorder affecting mental health or behavior
