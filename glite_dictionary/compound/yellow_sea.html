Yellow Sea

1. PROPN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈsi / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈsiː / (BrE)
an arm of the Pacific Ocean lying between China and the Korean Peninsula
sea between China and Korea

Examples

Many cargo ships travel through the Yellow Sea on their way to major Asian ports.
The Yellow Sea experiences large tides and rich fishing grounds.
Environmental issues have affected the marine life in the Yellow Sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: East China Sea - a marginal sea to the south of the Yellow Sea
