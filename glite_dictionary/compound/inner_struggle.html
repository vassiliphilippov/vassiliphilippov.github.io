inner struggle

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnɚ ˈstrʌɡəl / (AmE), / ˈɪnə ˈstrʌɡl / (BrE)
a conflict or difficulty within a person's mind, often involving emotions, desires, or beliefs
internal emotional conflict

Examples

The protagonist's inner struggle was central to the novel's plot.
Meditation helped her cope with her inner struggle over past mistakes.
He spoke openly about his inner struggle with self-doubt.

Synset Examples

Some consider demonism a metaphor for inner struggles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: internal conflict - a psychological struggle within the mind of a character or person
  • Antonym: peace of mind - a mental state of calmness and absence of inner conflict
