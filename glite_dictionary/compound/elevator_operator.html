elevator operator

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛləˌveɪt̬ɚ ˈɑpəˌreɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪˌveɪtə ˈɒpəˌreɪtə / (BrE)
a person employed to operate a passenger elevator, especially in a large building
person who operates an elevator

Examples

The elevator operator greeted everyone with a cheerful smile as the doors closed.
For many years, the elevator operator was a familiar figure in luxury hotels.
She worked as an elevator operator in the city before moving to another job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lift operator - person who operates a lift (British English for elevator)
