giant hyssop

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt ˈhɪsəp / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈhɪsəp / (BrE)
any of several aromatic plants of the genus Agastache, often cultivated for their fragrant leaves and colorful flowers
aromatic plant of the genus Agastache

Examples

Many gardeners grow giant hyssop to attract bees and butterflies to their yards.
The leaves of giant hyssop are often used to make herbal teas.
Giant hyssop thrives in sunny, well-drained soil and blooms throughout the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hyssop - an aromatic herb of the mint family (Hyssopus officinalis)
