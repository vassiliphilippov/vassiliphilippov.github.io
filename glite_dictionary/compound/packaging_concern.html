packaging concern

1. NOUN

/ ˈpækɪdʒɪŋ kənˈsɝn / (AmE), / ˈpækɪdʒɪŋ kənˈsɜːn / (BrE)
a company that packages goods for sale, shipment, or storage
company that packages goods

Examples

The contract was awarded to a local packaging concern specializing in food products.
Several packaging concerns are investing in eco-friendly materials.
The multinational corporation owns a large packaging concern in the industrial zone.
