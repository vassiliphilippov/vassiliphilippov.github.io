western poppy

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛstərn ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
A showy annual plant native to California, characterized by red flowers.
California annual with red flowers

Examples

The western poppy blooms spectacularly each spring in California's desert regions.
Gardeners admire the vibrant color of the western poppy in wildflower displays.
During our hike, we spotted several western poppies along the trail.
