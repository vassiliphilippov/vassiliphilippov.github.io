immense talent

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmɛns ˈtæl.ənt / (AmE), / ɪˈmɛns ˈtæl.ənt / (BrE)
exceptional or extremely great skill or ability in a particular field or activity
exceptional skill or ability

Examples

The director recognized her immense talent and cast her in the lead role.
Despite his immense talent, he remains remarkably humble.
The art exhibition showcased works that only someone with immense talent could create.

Synset Examples

His immense talent is evident in every performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: great ability - very high level of skill or proficiency
  • Antonym: mediocrity - the quality or state of being average or unremarkable in talent or ability
