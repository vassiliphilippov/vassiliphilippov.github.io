Battle of Chancellorsville

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæɾl əv ˌtʃænslərzˈvɪl / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˌtʃænsləzˈvɪl / (BrE)
A major battle during the American Civil War (1863), in which Confederate forces under Robert E. Lee defeated Union forces led by Joseph Hooker.
major Civil War battle (1863) in Virginia

Examples

The Battle of Chancellorsville is considered one of Robert E. Lee's greatest military achievements.
Thousands of soldiers were wounded or killed during the Battle of Chancellorsville.
Many historians study the strategies employed at the Battle of Chancellorsville in Civil War history.
