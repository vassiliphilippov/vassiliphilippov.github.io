total scrote

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊtəl skroʊt / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl skrəʊt / (BrE)
(slang, British) An unpleasant or despicable person, often used as an insult.
unpleasant or despicable person (slang, British)
slang vulgar british

Examples

Nobody wanted to work with him because he was a total scrote at the office.
After stealing the bike, everyone called the thief a total scrote.
Don't be such a total scrote; help us clean up after the party.

Synset Examples

He referred to the annoying guy as a total scrote.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scrote - slang for a despicable or contemptible person

Etymology

From British slang 'scrote', a shortened form of 'scrotum', used pejoratively.
