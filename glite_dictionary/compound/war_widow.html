war widow

1. NOUN

/ wɔr ˈwɪdoʊ / (AmE), / wɔː ˈwɪdəʊ / (BrE)
A woman whose husband has died as a result of serving in war.
woman whose husband died in war

Examples

Many war widows received support from government programs after the conflict.
The anniversary ceremony honored the war widows of fallen soldiers.
She became a war widow at a very young age during the Second World War.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: widow - a woman whose spouse has died
