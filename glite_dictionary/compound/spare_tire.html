spare tire

1. NOUN

/ spɛr ˈtaɪr / (AmE), / spɛə ˈtaɪə / (BrE)
an informal term for excess fat around a person's waistline, resembling the shape of a tire
excess fat around waist
informal

Examples

After the holidays, I noticed I had developed a bit of a spare tire around my waist.
He decided to join the gym to get rid of his spare tire.
Wearing tight clothes made her more aware of her spare tire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: love handles - fat deposits on the sides of the waist
