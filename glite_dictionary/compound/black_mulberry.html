black mulberry

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈmʌlˌbɛri / (AmE), / blæk ˈmʌlbəri / (BrE)
a mulberry tree native to southwestern Asia, known for its dark purple edible fruit and heart-shaped leaves
species of mulberry tree with dark edible fruit

Examples

The black mulberry produces sweet, juicy berries popular in desserts and jams.
Farmers often cultivate the black mulberry for its delicious fruit and attractive foliage.
A mature black mulberry tree can provide generous shade in the garden.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: white mulberry - species of mulberry tree with white or pinkish fruit
