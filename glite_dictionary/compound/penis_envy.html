penis envy

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːnɪs ˈɛnvi / (AmE), / ˈpiːnɪs ˈɛnvi / (BrE)
(In psychoanalysis) a female's supposed envy of the male's penis, regarded as influencing female psychosexual development.
(psychoanalysis) supposed female envy of the male's penis
medical

Examples

Freud's theory of penis envy remains controversial among modern psychologists.
Some feminists have criticized the concept of penis envy as outdated and sexist.
The therapist discussed penis envy in relation to early childhood development.
