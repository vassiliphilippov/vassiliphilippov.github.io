soy boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔɪ ˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪ ˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
A derogatory term for a man who is perceived as weak, effeminate, or lacking traditional masculine qualities, often associated with progressive or vegan lifestyles.
insult for a weak or effeminate man
slang offensive

Examples

Some people use soy boy to mock men they see as lacking traditional masculinity.
He was called a soy boy because he preferred a vegan burger over steak.
Online trolls often use the insult soy boy to belittle gentle or sensitive men.

Synset Examples

The term soy boy is often used in a derogatory manner.
Some consider him a soy boy for his gentle demeanor.
He was teased for being a soy boy, always opting for vegan options.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wimp - a weak or ineffectual person
  • Antonym: manly - having qualities traditionally associated with men, such as strength or courage

Etymology

From 'soy', referencing soy-based diets stereotypically associated with progressives, combined with 'boy'.
