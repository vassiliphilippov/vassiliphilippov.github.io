tearing down

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛrɪŋ daʊn / (AmE), / ˈtɪərɪŋ daʊn / (BrE)
the act of completely demolishing or destroying a building or structure
demolition of a building

Examples

The sudden tearing down of the old theater surprised local residents.
City officials approved the tearing down of several historic buildings to make space for new developments.
Many protested the tearing down of the school, calling for its preservation instead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demolition - the act of deliberately destroying a building
