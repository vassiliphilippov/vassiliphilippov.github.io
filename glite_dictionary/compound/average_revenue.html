average revenue

1. NOUN

/ ˈæv.ɚ.ɪdʒ ˈrɛv.əˌnu / (AmE), / ˈæv.ər.ɪdʒ ˈrɛv.ə.njuː / (BrE)
The total revenue earned from sales divided by the number of units sold, typically over a specific period.
revenue per unit sold

Examples

The company's average revenue per unit increased as they focused on higher-value products.
If the average revenue drops below production cost, the business may face losses.
It is important to compare average revenue across different quarters to track sales performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: average cost - total cost divided by number of units produced
