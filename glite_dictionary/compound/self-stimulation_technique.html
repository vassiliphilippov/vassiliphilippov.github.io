self-stimulation technique

1. NOUN

/ sɛlf ˌstɪmjəˈleɪʃən tɛkˈniːk / (AmE), / sɛlf ˌstɪmjʊˈleɪʃən tɛkˈniːk / (BrE)
a method involving intentional self-induced actions or movements to relieve stress or regulate emotions
method to relieve stress through self-induced actions

Examples

He practices a self-stimulation technique like squeezing a stress ball when feeling overwhelmed.
Therapists sometimes teach self-stimulation techniques to help clients calm themselves.
Using a self-stimulation technique can be as simple as rhythmic tapping or deep breathing.

Synset Examples

Many people use self-stimulation techniques to relieve stress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coping mechanism - a strategy or behavior used to manage stress or emotions
