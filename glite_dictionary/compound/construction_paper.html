construction paper

1. NOUN

/ kənˈstrʌkʃən ˈpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / kənˈstrʌkʃən ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
a thick, colored paper used for art and crafts, especially by children
colored paper for art and crafts

Examples

The teacher handed out construction paper and scissors for the class art project.
She used red construction paper to make a Valentine’s Day card for her friend.
Colorful shapes cut from construction paper decorated the walls of the classroom.
