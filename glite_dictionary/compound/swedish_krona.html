Swedish krona

1. NOUN

/ ˈswidɪʃ ˈkroʊnə / (AmE), / ˈswiːdɪʃ ˈkrəʊnə / (BrE)
the official currency and basic unit of money in Sweden
currency of Sweden

Examples

I exchanged my dollars for Swedish krona before traveling to Stockholm.
The price was listed in both euros and Swedish krona at the airport.
She saved a few Swedish krona as a souvenir from her trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: krona - the basic monetary unit of Sweden and Iceland
  • Synonym: Swedish crown - another term for Swedish krona

Etymology

Swedish, from 'krona' meaning 'crown', referring to the monetary unit introduced in 1873.
