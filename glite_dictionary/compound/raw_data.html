raw data

1. NOUN

/ rɔː ˈdeɪtə, ˈdætə / (AmE), / rɔː ˈdeɪtə, ˈdɑːtə / (BrE)
data that has not yet been processed, analyzed, or interpreted
unprocessed or unanalyzed data

Examples

Scientists collect raw data during fieldwork before conducting any analysis.
Please upload the raw data so the research team can begin their review.
The raw data contains errors that must be corrected before it can be used.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: processed data - data that has been organized or analyzed
