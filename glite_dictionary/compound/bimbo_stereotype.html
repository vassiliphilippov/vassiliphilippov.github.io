bimbo stereotype

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪmboʊ ˈstɛriəˌtaɪp / (AmE), / ˈbɪmbəʊ ˈstɪəriətaɪp / (BrE)
A stereotype of an attractive but unintelligent woman, often portrayed as shallow and lacking depth.
stereotype of an unintelligent attractive woman

Examples

She was frustrated by being cast in the bimbo stereotype in every romantic comedy.
The bimbo stereotype perpetuates negative views of women in media and popular culture.
Critics argue that the bimbo stereotype is outdated and harmful.

Synset Examples

That movie character was a typical bimbo stereotype.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bimbo - an attractive but unintelligent young woman
