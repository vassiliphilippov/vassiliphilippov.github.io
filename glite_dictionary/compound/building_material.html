building material

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪldɪŋ məˌtɪriəl / (AmE), / ˈbɪldɪŋ məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
a substance or product used in the construction of buildings, such as wood, brick, concrete, or steel
material used in building construction

Examples

The contractor chose recycled building material to minimize environmental impact.
Stone has been a common building material throughout history.
Modern architecture often incorporates glass as a key building material.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: construction material - material used for construction purposes
