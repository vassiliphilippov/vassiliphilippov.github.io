false rue

1. NOUN

/ fɔls ru / (AmE), / fɔːls ruː / (BrE)
A slender, upright perennial plant native to eastern North America, with tuberous roots and pink-tinged white flowers, resembling meadow rue.
North American perennial flowering plant

Examples

The shady garden bed was filled with false rue, its delicate flowers swaying in the breeze.
False rue is often mistaken for meadow rue due to their similar appearance.
Gardeners appreciate false rue for its hardiness and attractive early summer blooms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meadow rue - perennial herbaceous plant similar in appearance to false rue
