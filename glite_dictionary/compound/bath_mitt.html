bath mitt

1. NOUN

/ bæθ mɪt / (AmE), / bɑːθ mɪt / (BrE)
A glove-shaped pad made of coarse fiber, used for washing and scrubbing the body.
glove-shaped body scrubbing pad

Examples

She uses a bath mitt to exfoliate her skin in the shower.
After lathering soap, he scrubbed his arms and legs with a bath mitt.
The hotel provided each guest with a fresh towel and a soft bath mitt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loofah - a coarse, fibrous pad used for washing and exfoliating the skin
