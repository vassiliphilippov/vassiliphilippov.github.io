angle of inclination

1. NOUN

/ ˈæŋɡəl əv ˌɪnklɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡl əv ˌɪnklɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
(geometry) The angle formed between a given line and the positive x-axis, measured counterclockwise from the x-axis.
the angle between a line and the x-axis in geometry

Examples

The angle of inclination of the line was measured using a protractor.
To find the angle of inclination, you need to know the slope of the line.
When the angle of inclination is 45 degrees, the line rises at the same rate it runs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inclination - the angle at which something is inclined; slope
