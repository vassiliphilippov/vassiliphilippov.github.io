smooth softshell

1. NOUN

/ smuð ˈsɔftˌʃɛl / (AmE), / smuːð ˈsɒftˌʃɛl / (BrE)
a river turtle found in the Mississippi basin with a smooth, leathery shell, often inhabiting running water
river turtle with smooth soft shell

Examples

The smooth softshell can be found basking on riverbanks during warm afternoons.
Unlike other turtles, the smooth softshell has a flattened, flexible shell.
Scientists are studying the habitat preferences of the smooth softshell in the Mississippi basin.
