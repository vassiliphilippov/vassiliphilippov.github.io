green broom

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin bruːm / (AmE), / ɡriːn bruːm / (BrE)
a deciduous, erect, and spreading shrub native to western Europe, widely grown for its bright yellow flowers
a shrub with yellow flowers, native to western Europe

Examples

The green broom in our garden is covered with vibrant yellow blossoms each spring.
Gardeners often choose green broom for its hardiness and striking appearance.
After pruning the green broom, we noticed even more flowers blooming the next year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broom - a shrub of the genus Cytisus or Genista
