good laugh

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd læf / (AmE), / ɡʊd lɑːf / (BrE)
an enjoyable or humorous experience that makes people laugh together
enjoyable or humorous moment

Examples

We all had a good laugh at the comedian's new routine.
Dinner with friends always guarantees a good laugh.
Looking back, even his mistake was worth a good laugh.

Synset Examples

We had a good laugh at Teck's Ass during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joke - something said or done to provoke laughter
  • Synonym: laugh - the act of laughing
