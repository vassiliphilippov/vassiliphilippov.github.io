high jump

1. NOUN

/ haɪ dʒʌmp / (AmE), / haɪ dʒʌmp / (BrE)
an athletic event in which competitors jump over a horizontal bar at various heights without dislodging it
athletic event of jumping over a bar

Examples

She broke the school record in the high jump at the track meet.
The high jump requires a combination of speed, strength, and technique.
During the Olympics, he competed in both the high jump and the long jump.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pole vault - an athletic event where competitors jump over a high bar using a pole

2. NOUN

/ haɪ dʒʌmp / (AmE), / haɪ dʒʌmp / (BrE)
an athletic event in which competitors try to jump over a horizontal bar placed at measured heights without knocking it down
athletic event of jumping over a bar

Examples

She won the gold medal in the high jump at the Olympics.
The school track team held tryouts for the high jump event.
During the high jump, each athlete gets three attempts at clearing the bar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pole vault - athletic event involving jumping over a high bar using a pole
