fall dandelion

1. NOUN

/ fɔl ˈdændəˌlaɪən / (AmE), / fɔːl ˈdændɪˌlaɪ.ən / (BrE)
a European herb with yellow flowers that blooms in the fall, now naturalized in North America
a yellow-flowered fall-blooming herb

Examples

The field was covered in blooming fall dandelion late into October.
Botanists have noted the spread of fall dandelion throughout the northeastern United States.
Unlike common dandelion, fall dandelion produces flowers during the autumn months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dandelion - a common wild plant with bright yellow flowers and seed heads with tufts of hairs
