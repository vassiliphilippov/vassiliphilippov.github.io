so lame

1. ADJ

/ soʊ leɪm / (AmE), / səʊ leɪm / (BrE)
extremely unfunny, unimpressive, or disappointing; considered dull or uncool.
extremely disappointing or uncool
informal

Examples

Everyone thought his presentation was so lame compared to last year’s winner.
I can't believe you made us watch that movie; it was so lame!
Her excuse for being late was so lame that nobody believed her.

Synset Examples

That joke was so lame, I couldn't help but cringe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lame - unimpressive, dull, or uncool (informal sense)
