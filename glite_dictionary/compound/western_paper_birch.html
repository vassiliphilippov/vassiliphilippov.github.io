Western paper birch

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛstərn ˈpeɪpər bɜrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən ˈpeɪpə bɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a birch tree of the western United States, similar to the paper birch but with brownish bark
a birch tree with brownish bark, western US

Examples

The Western paper birch is easily identified by its brownish bark and elegant branches.
In the mountainous regions, Western paper birch stands often mix with other native trees.
Conservationists are studying the Western paper birch to understand its role in the forest ecosystem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paper birch - a North American birch tree with white peeling bark
