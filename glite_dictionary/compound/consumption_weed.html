consumption weed

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsʌmpʃən wid / (AmE), / kənˈsʌmpʃən wiːd / (BrE)
a shrub found in salt marshes of eastern and south central North America and the West Indies, with fruit surrounded by white plumelike hairy tufts
salt marsh shrub of North America and West Indies

Examples

The consumption weed thrives in the salty marshes along the Atlantic Coast.
Botanists have studied the unique hairy fruit of the consumption weed.
Migrating birds play a role in spreading the seeds of consumption weed.
