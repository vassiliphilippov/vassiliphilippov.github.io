army engineer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmi ɛnˌdʒɪnɪr / (AmE), / ˈɑːmi ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə / (BrE)
a member of the military who is trained in engineering and construction duties, such as building bridges, roads, and fortifications
military engineering specialist

Examples

The army engineer was responsible for building temporary bridges during the operation.
As an army engineer, she learned to construct fortifications in challenging locations.
Several army engineers worked together to clear the debris from the landing zone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: combat engineer - a military engineer who specializes in battlefield engineering
