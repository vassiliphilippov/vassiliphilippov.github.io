meatball surgery

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːtˌbɔl ˈsɝdʒəri / (AmE), / ˈmiːtbɔːl ˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
informal or humorous term for a rough, basic, or hastily performed surgical operation, often implying minimal concern for finesse or aesthetics
basic or hasty surgical operation (slang)
informal slang american
