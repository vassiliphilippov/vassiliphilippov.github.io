hard times

1. NOUN

/ hɑrd taɪmz / (AmE), / hɑːd taɪmz / (BrE)
a period of financial, emotional, or general difficulty or hardship
a period of difficulty or hardship

Examples

Many families went through hard times during the economic recession.
She always remained optimistic, even in hard times.
Businesses often struggle to survive in hard times.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: prosperity - a period of success or wealth
  • Synonym: hardship - severe suffering or difficulty
