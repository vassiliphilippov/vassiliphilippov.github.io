ingrown hair

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnˌɡroʊn hɛr / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌɡrəʊn heə / (BrE)
A hair that curls back or grows sideways into the skin instead of rising up from it, often causing irritation or inflammation.
hair growing back into the skin
medical

Examples

I had to go to the dermatologist because an ingrown hair became infected.
Shaving too closely can often cause an ingrown hair to develop on your chin or legs.
She used a warm compress to soothe the area around the ingrown hair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: folliculitis - inflammation of one or more hair follicles
  • Synonym: pimple - small inflamed swelling of the skin
