thrown and twisted

1. ADJ

/ θroʊn ənd ˈtwɪstɪd / (AmE), / θrəʊn ənd ˈtwɪstɪd / (BrE)
(of filaments or threads) spun together and twisted into a single, stronger thread or yarn.
filaments twisted into thread or yarn

Examples

The fabric was made from thrown and twisted silk for added strength.
Only thrown and twisted threads were used in this high-quality tapestry.
She inspected the thrown and twisted yarn before starting to weave.

Synset Examples

thrown silk is raw silk that has been twisted and doubled into yarn
