bad boy image

1. NOUN

/ bæd bɔɪ ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / bæd bɔɪ ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
an attractive or appealing persona characterized by rebelliousness and disregard for conventional rules, often associated with masculinity
persona of a rebellious, appealing man

Examples

Many celebrities cultivate a bad boy image to attract attention from the media.
Despite his bad boy image, he is actually quite kind and generous.
The movie star's bad boy image made him popular with fans.

Synset Examples

The bad boy image can be quite charming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rebel - a person who resists authority or control
  • Synonym: bad boy - a man known for rebellious or unconventional behavior
  • Antonym: nice guy - a man with a pleasant or gentle character
