snow-clad

1. ADJ

/ ˈsnoʊ klæd / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊ klæd / (BrE)
(of a surface or area) covered with snow.
covered with snow

Examples

We admired the snow-clad peaks under the clear blue sky.
The village looked magical, its roofs snow-clad after the storm.
During winter, the snow-clad roads made travel difficult.

Synset Examples

snow-clad hills
snow-covered roads
a long snowy winter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snow-covered - covered by snow
  • Antonym: bare - not covered with snow or any other substance

Etymology

From 'snow' + 'clad' (meaning 'covered').
