liquor licence

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪkər ˈlaɪsəns / (AmE), / ˈlɪkə ˈlaɪsəns / (BrE)
an official permit allowing the holder to sell alcoholic beverages legally
permit to sell alcoholic drinks
legal

Examples

The restaurant lost its liquor licence after repeated violations of the law.
You need a liquor licence to serve alcohol at your event.
Obtaining a liquor licence can be a long and expensive process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alcohol licence - permit to sell alcohol; alternative term
  • Degree Lower: licence - permit issued by an authority
