medical intern

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈɪnˌtɝn / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈɪntɜːn / (BrE)
An advanced student or recent graduate in medicine who is gaining supervised practical experience in a hospital or clinic.
student doctor gaining practical experience

Examples

During her year as a medical intern, she rotated through several hospital departments.
The medical intern assisted the surgeon during the operation.
Supervision from experienced doctors is essential for every medical intern.
