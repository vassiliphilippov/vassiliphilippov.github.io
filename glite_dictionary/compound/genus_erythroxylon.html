genus Erythroxylon

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ɪˌrɪθ.rɒkˈsaɪ.lɒn / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ɪˌrɪθ.rɒkˈsaɪ.lɒn / (BrE)
a large genus of South American shrubs and small trees in the family Erythroxylaceae, including species such as the coca plant
genus of South American shrubs/small trees

Examples

The genus Erythroxylon includes the coca plant, which is valued for its alkaloids.
Scientists studied the medicinal properties of several species within the genus Erythroxylon.
Many species of the genus Erythroxylon are native to tropical regions of South America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Erythroxylum coca - a species in the genus Erythroxylon, source of coca leaves

Etymology

From Greek 'erythros' (red) + 'xylon' (wood), referencing red wood of some species.
