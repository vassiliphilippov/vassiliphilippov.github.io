tuberous begonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːbərəs bɪˈɡoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈtjuːbərəs bɪˈɡəʊniə / (BrE)
a type of begonia with large, fleshy, tuberous roots, cultivated for its showy flowers
begonia with tuberous roots

Examples

The tuberous begonia is prized for its bright, vibrant blossoms throughout the summer.
She planted several tuberous begonias along the garden walkway for a striking display.
Unlike other varieties, the tuberous begonia requires careful protection from frost.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: begonia - a plant of the Begoniaceae family with showy flowers and ornamental foliage
