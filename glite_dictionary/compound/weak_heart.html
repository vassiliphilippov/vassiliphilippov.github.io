weak heart

1. NOUN

/ wiːk hɑɹt / (AmE), / wiːk hɑːt / (BrE)
a person who is easily frightened or sensitive to emotional shocks
easily frightened person

Examples

If you have a weak heart, you might want to skip the haunted house.
The roller coaster ride is not for those with a weak heart.
She admitted to having a weak heart when it comes to scary stories.

Synset Examples

The horror movie will petrify anyone with a weak heart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
  • Antonym: brave - showing courage, not easily frightened
