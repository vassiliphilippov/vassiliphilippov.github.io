self-identified incel

1. NOUN

/ sɛlf aɪˈdɛntɪˌfaɪd ˈɪnsɛl / (AmE), / sɛlf aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪd ˈɪnsɛl / (BrE)
a person who describes themselves as involuntarily celibate, typically as part of an online community
person who calls themselves involuntarily celibate

Examples

The documentary explored the experiences of several self-identified incels within online communities.
A self-identified incel explained his views on dating and society during the interview.
Many self-identified incels share their personal stories in virtual support groups.

Synset Examples

The forum was filled with discussions among self-identified incels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incel - person who identifies as involuntarily celibate

Etymology

From 'self-' (by oneself) + 'identified' (recognized as) + 'incel', itself from 'involuntary celibate'.
