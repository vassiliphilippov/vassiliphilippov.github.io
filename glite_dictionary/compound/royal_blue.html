royal blue

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɔɪəl bluː / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪəl bluː / (BrE)
a deep, vivid shade of blue tinged with purple
a deep, purplish blue color

Examples

The team wore royal blue uniforms for the championship game.
She painted her bedroom walls a stunning shade of royal blue.
His tie was a perfect royal blue, adding a pop of color to his suit.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: navy blue - a very dark shade of blue
  • Degree Lower: azure - a bright blue color like that of a clear sky

Etymology

Named for its association with the robes of royalty in the 18th century.
