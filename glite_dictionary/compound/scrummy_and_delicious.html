scrummy and delicious

1. ADJ

/ ˈskrʌmi ənd dɪˈlɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˈskrʌmi ənd dɪˈlɪʃəs / (BrE)
Extremely tasty or appetizing; very delicious.
extremely tasty or appetizing
informal british

Examples

The chocolate cake was scrummy and delicious, and everyone asked for seconds.
Her homemade scones turned out scrummy and delicious with strawberry jam and cream.
Everything at that bakery is scrummy and delicious, from the pastries to the sandwiches.

Synset Examples

The cupcakes look scrummy and delicious.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delicious - tasting very good; highly appetizing

Etymology

'Scrummy' is a British informal word from 'scrumptious' (delicious, splendid).
