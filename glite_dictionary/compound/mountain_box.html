mountain box

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊn.tən ˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪn ˌbɒks / (BrE)
an evergreen, mat-forming shrub native to North America and northern Eurasia, with small white flowers, red berries, and leaves that turn red in autumn
evergreen shrub with red berries

Examples

The mountain box is prized for its vibrant red berries and autumn foliage.
Hikers spotted a patch of mountain box growing along the trail in Alaska.
Gardeners often plant mountain box as ground cover in shady areas.
