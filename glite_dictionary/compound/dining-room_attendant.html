dining-room attendant

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪnɪŋ ˌruːm əˈtɛn.dənt / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnɪŋ ˌruːm əˈtɛn.dənt / (BrE)
a person employed to provide service, such as serving food or assisting guests, in a dining room
a person serving in a dining room

Examples

The dining-room attendant greeted us with a warm smile as we entered the restaurant.
After the guests left, the dining-room attendant quickly cleared the tables and reset them for the next service.
As a new dining-room attendant, he learned how to serve dishes with professionalism and courtesy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waiter - person who serves food at a table, especially in a restaurant
  • Synonym: waitress - female person who serves food at a table, especially in a restaurant
