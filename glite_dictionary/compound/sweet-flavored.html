sweet-flavored

1. ADJ

/ ˈswit ˌfleɪvɚd / (AmE), / ˈswiːt ˌfleɪvəd / (BrE)
Having a taste characteristic of sweetness, often due to the addition of sugar or other sweeteners.
having a sweet taste

Examples

The bakery offers a delightful selection of sweet-flavored pastries every morning.
He prefers sweet-flavored drinks over traditional black coffee.
This sweet-flavored sauce pairs well with grilled chicken.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bitter - having a sharp, pungent, or disagreeable taste
  • Antonym: sour - having an acid taste like lemon or vinegar
  • Antonym: savory - having a salty or spicy, rather than sweet, flavor
