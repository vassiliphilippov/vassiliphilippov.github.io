detonating fuse

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛt̬əˌneɪtɪŋ fjuz / (AmE), / ˈdɛt.əˌneɪ.tɪŋ fjuːz / (BrE)
A fuse containing an explosive charge that is used to initiate detonation in an explosive device or charge.
explosive fuse for initiating detonation

Examples

The demolition team carefully inserted the detonating fuse into the dynamite charge.
If the detonating fuse fails, the entire operation could be compromised.
Technicians are trained to handle and install a detonating fuse safely.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: safety fuse - a fuse that burns to ignite explosives but is not itself detonating
  • Synonym: detonator - a device used to set off explosives

Etymology

From 'detonate' (from Latin detonare 'to thunder down') + 'fuse'.
