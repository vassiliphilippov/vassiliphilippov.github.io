something like

1. X

/ ˈsʌmθɪŋ laɪk / (AmE), / ˈsʌmθɪŋ laɪk / (BrE)
used to indicate similarity to, or approximation of, a person, thing, or amount.
used for similarity or approximation

Examples

She earns something like sixty thousand dollars a year.
It looks something like the house we used to live in.
He said he wanted something like the job he had before.

Synset Examples

I could use something like a vacation now.
He wants something like a new car for his birthday.
