refrigeration system

1. NOUN

/ rɪˌfrɪdʒəˈreɪʃən ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / rɪˌfrɪdʒəˈreɪʃən ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a system designed to cool or freeze objects or spaces, typically to preserve food or other perishable items
system for chilling or freezing, often for preservation

Examples

The supermarket installed a new refrigeration system to improve food safety and energy efficiency.
Most restaurants rely on a well-maintained refrigeration system to keep ingredients fresh.
Technicians inspected the refrigeration system to ensure it maintained the correct temperature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cooling system - a system or device for removing heat from an area or substance
  • Antonym: heating system - a system for providing heat, typically for buildings
