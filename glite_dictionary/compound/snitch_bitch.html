snitch bitch

1. NOUN

/ snɪtʃ bɪtʃ / (AmE), / snɪtʃ bɪtʃ / (BrE)
A highly offensive and vulgar slang term for someone who informs on others; an informer, especially used to insult or demean.
offensive slang for an informer
slang offensive vulgar

Examples

He got ostracized because his friends thought he was a snitch bitch.
Nobody wants to hang out with a snitch bitch around here.
After reporting the theft, she unfairly got called a snitch bitch by her classmates.

Synset Examples

He was labeled a snitch bitch for reporting them.
Don't be a snitch bitch and tell the teacher.
Calling her a snitch bitch didn't solve anything.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snitch - A person who informs on someone else
  • Synonym: informer - A person who informs against others, often to the authorities
