defensive measure

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛnsɪv ˈmɛʒər / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛnsɪv ˈmɛʒə / (BrE)
an action or resource designed to protect a country, organization, or group from potential threats or attacks, especially in military contexts
military action or resource for defense

Examples

The government invested heavily in new defensive measures to safeguard its borders.
The installation of anti-missile systems is considered an important defensive measure during times of conflict.
Every nation must evaluate its defensive measures against emerging security threats.

Synset Examples

they died in the defense of Stalingrad
they were developed for the defense program

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defense - the act of protecting against attack
  • Antonym: offensive measure - an action designed to attack or gain advantage over an opponent
