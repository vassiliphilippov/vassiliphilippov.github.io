Tera language

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛrə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtɛərə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in northeastern Nigeria, characterized by three tones
a Chadic language of northeastern Nigeria

Examples

The Tera language is known for its complex tone system.
Researchers are documenting the grammar and vocabulary of the Tera language.
There are efforts to preserve the Tera language among younger generations in Nigeria.
