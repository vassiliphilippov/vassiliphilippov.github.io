for the long run

1. ADP

/ fər ðə lɔŋ rʌn / (AmE), / fə ðə lɒŋ rʌn / (BrE)
over an extended or indefinite period of time; with consideration for what will happen far into the future
over an extended period; in the distant future

Examples

We need to invest in education for the long run if we want sustainable growth.
You might lose money initially, but it's a sound decision for the long run.
Healthy eating habits benefit you for the long run.

Synset Examples

We should consider sustainability for the long run.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the long term - over a long period of time
  • Antonym: short term - a brief or limited period in the future
