mythic tale

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪθɪk teɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɪθɪk teɪl / (BrE)
A story involving legendary, supernatural, or fantastical elements, often passed down through generations.
legendary or fantastical story

Examples

The villagers shared a mythic tale about a dragon that once guarded their valley.
Her novel weaves a mythic tale into the experiences of modern life.
The movie draws inspiration from a mythic tale of tragic love and heroism.

Synset Examples

They told a mythic tale of ancient warriors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: myth - A traditional story explaining a belief or natural phenomenon
  • Synonym: legend - A traditional story sometimes popularly regarded as historical but not authenticated
