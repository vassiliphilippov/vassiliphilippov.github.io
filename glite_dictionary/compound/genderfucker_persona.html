genderfucker persona

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛndərˌfʌkər pərˈsoʊnə / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛndəˌfʌkə pəˈsəʊnə / (BrE)
an identity or public image that deliberately challenges or subverts traditional gender norms and expectations
identity subverting gender norms
vulgar

Examples

The band's lead singer adopted a bold genderfucker persona that blurred lines between masculinity and femininity.
Her genderfucker persona is a statement against restrictive societal definitions of gender.
Media coverage often highlighted their genderfucker persona as part of their public image.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gender nonconformity - behavior or identity not conforming to traditional gender norms
  • Synonym: androgyny - the quality of having characteristics of both genders
  • Synonym: genderfluid - a gender identity that is flexible rather than fixed

Etymology

From 'gender' + vulgar slang 'fucker' (one who disrupts or violates) + 'persona' (Latin for mask or character).
