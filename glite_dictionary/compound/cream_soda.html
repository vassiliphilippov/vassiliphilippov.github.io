cream soda

1. NOUN

/ kriːm ˈsoʊdə / (AmE), / kriːm ˈsəʊdə / (BrE)
a sweet, carbonated soft drink flavored with vanilla, usually clear or light in color
sweet vanilla-flavored soft drink

Examples

She ordered a cream soda with her burger at the diner.
On hot days, I love to drink ice-cold cream soda in the backyard.
The store was sold out of cream soda, so he chose root beer instead.
