kitty litter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪti ˌlɪtər / (AmE), / ˈkɪti ˌlɪtə / (BrE)
granulated material, typically clay, placed in a container to absorb the waste products of a cat or other pet
granular absorbent for pet waste

Examples

The cat prefers to use kitty litter that is unscented and clumps easily.
Please remember to change the kitty litter every few days for hygiene.
Spilled kitty litter can help absorb oil stains in the garage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: litter - absorbent material for animal waste
