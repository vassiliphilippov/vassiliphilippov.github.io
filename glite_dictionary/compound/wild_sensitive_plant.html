wild sensitive plant

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ˈsɛnsətɪv plænt / (AmE), / waɪld ˈsɛnsətɪv plɑːnt / (BrE)
a tropical American plant whose leaflets react to touch; sometimes classified in the genus Cassia
tropical plant with touch-sensitive leaflets

Examples

The wild sensitive plant closes its leaflets quickly when disturbed by touch.
Botanists studied the wild sensitive plant found along the riverbanks in South America.
Some gardeners grow the wild sensitive plant for its unusual, touch-responsive foliage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensitive plant - plant whose leaflets fold when touched; Mimosa pudica
