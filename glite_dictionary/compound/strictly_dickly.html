strictly dickly

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrɪktli ˈdɪkli / (AmE), / ˈstrɪktli ˈdɪkli / (BrE)
(slang) Exclusively or uncompromisingly sexually attracted to men; interested only in men (usually used humorously by gay men about themselves).
gay slang for exclusive male attraction
slang

He told his friends he's strictly dickly and not interested in women.
At the club, Mark joked about being strictly dickly for life.
The comedian described himself as strictly dickly during the interview.

Synset Examples

Etymology

From 'strictly' (entirely, only) + 'dickly' (slang, from 'dick', meaning penis, used here to refer to men).
