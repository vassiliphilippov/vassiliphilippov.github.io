the sperminator

1. NOUN

/ ðə ˈspɝːmɪˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ðə ˈspɜːmɪˌneɪtə / (BrE)
a man, often referenced humorously, who has fathered a large number of children or is notable for his role in reproduction
humorous term for a prolific father
informal

Examples

The local newspaper dubbed him the sperminator after he fathered over twenty children.
She laughed when she heard her friend was dating the sperminator from that viral news story.
In the documentary, the sperminator talks about donating sperm to hundreds of families.

Synset Examples

The movie featured a character known as the sperminator.

Etymology

Blend of 'sperm' and 'Terminator' (from the 1984 film title), used humorously.
