lobster tail

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑbstɚ teɪl / (AmE), / ˈlɒbstə teɪl / (BrE)
The tail section of a lobster, especially the edible meat from this part, often from a spiny rock lobster.
edible tail section of a lobster

Examples

The chef served grilled lobster tail with lemon butter sauce for the main course.
She ordered a lobster tail at the seafood restaurant for her birthday dinner.
Frozen lobster tails can be found in many supermarkets and are easy to prepare at home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lobster - a large marine crustacean with a muscular tail and pincers
