to detonate controversy

1. VERB

/ ˈdɛt̬.əˌneɪt kənˈtrɝː.vɚ.si / (AmE), / ˈdɛt.ə.neɪt kənˈtrɒ.və.si / (BrE)
to cause a situation in which there is significant public disagreement or conflict.
cause major public disagreement

Examples

His statement is expected to detonate controversy within parliament.
The new law has detonated controversy across the country.
Publishing the report will likely detonate controversy among shareholders.

Synset Examples

His comments are likely to detonate controversy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spark controversy - to initiate a situation of significant disagreement or conflict
  • Synonym: provoke controversy - to cause anger, argument, or disagreement in public
