Republic of Korea

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv kəˈriə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv kəˈriə / (BrE)
the official name for the country occupying the southern part of the Korean Peninsula; commonly known as South Korea
official name of South Korea

Examples

The Republic of Korea became a member of the United Nations in 1991.
Many students from the Republic of Korea choose to study abroad.
The Republic of Korea is known for its rapid economic development and advanced technology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: South Korea - country in East Asia, officially the Republic of Korea
  • Antonym: North Korea - country occupying the northern part of the Korean Peninsula
