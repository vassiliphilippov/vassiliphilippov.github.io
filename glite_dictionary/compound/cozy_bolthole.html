cozy bolthole

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊzi ˈboʊltˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈkəʊzi ˈbəʊltˌhəʊl / (BrE)
a comfortable and secure place where one can retreat and relax, often hidden or away from usual surroundings
comfortable retreat or hideaway

Examples

After a long week at work, she escapes to her cozy bolthole by the lake.
The author wrote his entire novel in a cozy bolthole nestled in the forest.
They decorated their cozy bolthole with warm lights and soft blankets for winter.

Synset Examples

He has a cozy bolthole in the countryside for weekends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retreat - a quiet or secluded place where one can rest and relax
  • Synonym: hideaway - a place used for hiding or as a retreat

Etymology

Cozy from English 'cosy' (comfort), bolthole referring to a hole for escape or hiding.
