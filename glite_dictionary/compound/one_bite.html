one bite

1. NOUN

/ wʌn baɪt / (AmE), / wʌn baɪt / (BrE)
A single mouthful or piece taken at once when eating.
a single mouthful

Examples

He finished the chocolate bar in one bite.
Don't try to eat the cookie in one bite, or you'll choke.
The sandwich was so small that it disappeared with one bite.

Synset Examples

The apple was juicy enough to eat in one bite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mouthful - the amount of food or drink that fills your mouth
