Kingdom of Nepal

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm əv nɛˈpɑl / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm əv nɪˈpɑːl / (BrE)
the official name of Nepal as a monarchy, existing from 1768 to 2008, a landlocked country in the Himalayas between India and China
former official name of Nepal (1768–2008)

Examples

The Kingdom of Nepal became a constitutional monarchy after years of political unrest.
Many travelers visited the Kingdom of Nepal to see Mount Everest and other Himalayan peaks.
In 2008, the Kingdom of Nepal was officially abolished, marking the start of the Federal Democratic Republic of Nepal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nepal - a landlocked country in South Asia, north of India
