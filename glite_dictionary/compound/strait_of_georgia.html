Strait of Georgia

1. PROPN

/ streɪt əv ˈdʒɔrdʒə / (AmE), / streɪt əv ˈdʒɔːdʒə / (BrE)
a strait of the Pacific Ocean separating Vancouver Island from the Canadian mainland in British Columbia, Canada
strait between Vancouver Island and Canadian mainland

Examples

The Strait of Georgia is well known for its rich marine life and scenic beauty.
Many ferries cross the Strait of Georgia each day, connecting Vancouver Island and the mainland.
Scientists are studying water quality in the Strait of Georgia to monitor environmental changes.
