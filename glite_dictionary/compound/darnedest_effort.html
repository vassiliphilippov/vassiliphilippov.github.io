darnedest effort

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑrnɪdst ˈɛfərt / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnɪdst ˈɛfət / (BrE)
the greatest effort or determination that someone can give
maximum effort; utmost determination

Examples

She gave her darnedest effort during the competition.
Despite the difficulties, he put forth his darnedest effort to finish the project on time.
Even when tired, the team always gives their darnedest effort in every match.

Synset Examples

She gave her darnedest effort during the competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: best effort - maximum possible effort
  • Antonym: minimal effort - the least amount of effort possible
