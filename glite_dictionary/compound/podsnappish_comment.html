Podsnappish comment

1. NOUN

/ pɒdˈsnæpɪʃ ˈkɑːmɛnt / (AmE), / pɒdˈsnæpɪʃ ˈkɒmɛnt / (BrE)
a remark that is brisk, dismissive, or self-satisfied in a way that shows disregard for others' opinions
brisk or dismissive remark

Examples

The manager was known for making Podsnappish comments during meetings, often shutting down new ideas.
His Podsnappish comment left everyone feeling belittled and unwelcome.
She regretted her Podsnappish comment as soon as she saw the hurt it caused.

Synset Examples

Her Podsnappish comments shocked the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snide remark - a derogatory or mocking comment
  • Antonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration

Etymology

From 'Podsnap', a character in Dickens' 'Our Mutual Friend', noted for his complacent and dismissive attitude, combined with 'comment'.
