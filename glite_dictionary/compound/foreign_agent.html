foreign agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrən ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈfɒrən ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person who secretly collects and transmits information on behalf of a foreign government; a spy working for another country.
spy working for another country

Examples

The government arrested a foreign agent who was passing classified documents to another country.
During the Cold War, many foreign agents operated under false identities.
She was suspected of being a foreign agent after her secret meetings with diplomats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spy - a person who secretly collects and reports information about another country or organization
