anal retentive

1. ADJ

/ ˌeɪ.nəl rɪˈtɛn.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.nəl rɪˈtɛn.tɪv / (BrE)
(Psychology) Relating to a personality type characterized by excessive orderliness, perfectionism, and control, thought to result from fixation during the anal stage of psychosexual development.
excessively orderly or perfectionistic (psychological term)
informal medical

Examples

My boss is so anal retentive that every paperclip in the office must be perfectly aligned.
He’s a bit anal retentive about cleaning his apartment; everything has its exact place.
Although her anal retentive nature keeps the house spotless, it can be exhausting for everyone else.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfectionist - someone who refuses to accept any standard short of perfection
  • Antonym: disorganized - lacking order or method

Etymology

From psychoanalytic theory: 'anal' (relating to the anus) + 'retentive' (tending to withhold); coined in early 20th century psychology.
