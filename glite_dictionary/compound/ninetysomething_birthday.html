ninetysomething birthday

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪntiˌsʌmθɪŋ ˈbɝθ.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪntiˌsʌmθɪŋ ˈbɜːθ.deɪ / (BrE)
a birthday marking any age from ninety to ninety-nine years old
birthday at any age in the nineties

Examples

My grandmother enjoyed a big party for her ninetysomething birthday last spring.
It’s rare to meet someone energetic during their ninetysomething birthday celebrations.
We sent a card to our neighbor for his ninetysomething birthday this year.
