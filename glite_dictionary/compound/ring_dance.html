ring dance

1. NOUN

/ rɪŋ dæns / (AmE), / rɪŋ dɑːns / (BrE)
a folk dance in which participants form a circle and move in coordinated steps, often holding hands
a folk dance performed in a circle

Examples

The children performed a traditional ring dance during the village festival.
In many cultures, a ring dance is used to celebrate the arrival of spring.
The group learned the steps to a Scottish ring dance at the workshop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circle dance - a dance where participants form a circle; similar meaning
