fireman's carry

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪərmənz ˈkæri / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəmənz ˈkæri / (BrE)
a method of carrying a person draped over one's shoulders, typically used in rescue situations
way of carrying a person over the shoulders

Examples

The firefighter used a fireman's carry to rescue the unconscious man from the burning building.
She learned how to perform a fireman's carry during her first aid training course.
He lifted his injured friend in a fireman's carry and hurried to get help.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: piggyback - riding on someone's back and shoulders

Etymology

From 'fireman' (a firefighter) + 'carry', referencing the method traditionally used by firefighters.
