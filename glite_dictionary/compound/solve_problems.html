to solve problems

1. VERB

/ sɑlv ˈprɑbləmz / (AmE), / sɒlv ˈprɒbləmz / (BrE)
to find solutions or answers to difficulties or challenging situations
find solutions to difficulties

Examples

Engineers are trained to solve problems using critical thinking and technical knowledge.
She enjoys solving problems that require creative solutions.
In business, the ability to solve problems efficiently can set you apart from others.

Synset Examples

My pa always knows how to solve problems.
