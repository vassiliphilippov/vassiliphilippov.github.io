evergreen bittersweet

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛv.ɚˌɡriːn ˈbɪt.ərˌswiːt / (AmE), / ˈɛvəɡriːn ˈbɪtəˌswiːt / (BrE)
A broad, bushy climbing shrub native to Asia, with pinkish fruit and many subspecies or varieties.
Asiatic shrub with pinkish fruit

Examples

The garden was adorned with blooming evergreen bittersweet that climbed along the fence.
Botanists have identified several varieties of evergreen bittersweet in East Asian forests.
The pinkish fruit of evergreen bittersweet adds a splash of color in autumn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bittersweet - a genus of woody vines and shrubs producing orange or pinkish fruit
