grape jelly

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪp ˈdʒɛli / (AmE), / ɡreɪp ˈdʒɛli / (BrE)
A sweet, clear, semi-solid spread made from grape juice and sugar, commonly used as a condiment.
sweet spread made from grape juice

Examples

She spread grape jelly on her toast for breakfast.
The classic peanut butter and grape jelly sandwich is a favorite lunch for many children.
We bought a jar of homemade grape jelly at the farmer's market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jelly - a sweet, clear, semi-solid food made from fruit juice and sugar
  • Synonym: jam - a sweet spread made from fruit and sugar, but with fruit pieces
