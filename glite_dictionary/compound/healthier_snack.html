healthier snack

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθiɚ snæk / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθiə snæk / (BrE)
a snack that is considered better for your health than typical alternatives, often because it contains less fat, sugar, or artificial ingredients
a more healthful snack option

Examples

Instead of chips, she reached for a healthier snack like carrot sticks.
Many people are choosing healthier snacks to support their fitness goals.
The vending machine now offers healthier snack options such as granola bars and dried fruit.

Synset Examples

The nutrider helped us choose healthier snacks.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: healthy snack - a snack that is good for health
