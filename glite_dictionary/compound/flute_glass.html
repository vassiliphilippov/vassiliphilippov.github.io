flute glass

1. NOUN

/ fluːt ɡlæs / (AmE), / fluːt ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
A tall, narrow wineglass, typically used for serving champagne or sparkling wines.
tall narrow wineglass

Examples

The waiter poured the sparkling wine into a flute glass for each guest.
She raised her flute glass in a toast during the celebration.
Carefully washing each flute glass, he set them aside to dry after the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: champagne flute - a tall, narrow glass for champagne
