surgical procedure

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːdʒɪkəl prəˈsiːdʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːdʒɪkəl prəˈsiːdʒə / (BrE)
a medical operation that involves making an incision with instruments to treat a disease or injury in a living body
medical operation involving incisions
medical

Examples

The patient underwent a complex surgical procedure to remove the tumor.
Some surgical procedures require a lengthy recovery period.
Advance preparation is essential before any surgical procedure is performed.
