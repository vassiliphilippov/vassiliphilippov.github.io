weather forecast

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛðɚ ˌfɔːrkæst / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə ˌfɔːkɑːst / (BrE)
a prediction or report about the future condition of the weather for a particular area and time period
prediction of future weather conditions

Examples

According to the weather forecast, it will rain tomorrow afternoon.
I always check the weather forecast before planning a picnic.
The weather forecast predicted unusually high temperatures for this weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weather report - an account or summary of the current and predicted weather conditions
