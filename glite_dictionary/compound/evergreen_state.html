Evergreen State

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛv.ɚˌɡriːn steɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛvəˌɡriːn steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the state of Washington in the northwestern United States, known for its abundant evergreen forests; capital: Olympia.
nickname for Washington State (U.S.)
american

Examples

Visitors to the Evergreen State are often struck by the lush green scenery and dramatic coastline.
The nickname Evergreen State reflects the dense forests that cover much of Washington.
After moving to the Evergreen State, she discovered a love for hiking in its many national parks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Washington - a state in northwestern United States with capital Olympia

Etymology

From Washington's reputation for evergreen forests.
