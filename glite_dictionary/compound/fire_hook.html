fire hook

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚ hʊk / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə hʊk / (BrE)
a metal rod with a handle, used for stirring a fire or manipulating burning materials
rod used for stirring a fire

Examples

The old cabin still had a fire hook hanging above the hearth for stoking the flames.
She used a fire hook to push the burning logs into place.
After the fire died down, he reached for the fire hook to stir the embers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poker - a metal rod for stirring or rearranging a fire
