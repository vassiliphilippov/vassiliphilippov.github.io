water-washed

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɔtər ˌwɑʃt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌwɒʃt / (BrE)
(of a surface or object) cleaned by having been washed in water.
cleaned by being washed in water

Examples

The freshly water-washed vegetables gleamed in the morning sunlight.
All drinking glasses must be water-washed before use in the laboratory.
After the rain, the stones looked as if they had been water-washed and polished.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: washed - cleaned by being washed, typically with water
