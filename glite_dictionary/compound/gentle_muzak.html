gentle Muzak

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛntəl ˈmjuːˌzæk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntəl ˈmjuːzæk / (BrE)
soft, unobtrusive background music, typically played in public places to create a relaxed atmosphere
soft, unobtrusive background music

Examples

Soft lighting and gentle Muzak created a pleasant environment in the café.
I could hear gentle Muzak playing as I waited in the elevator.
The hotel lobby featured gentle Muzak that helped guests feel at ease.

Synset Examples

They enjoyed lunch with gentle Muzak in the background.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: easy listening - a genre of soft, mellow music for relaxation
  • Synonym: background music - music played subtly to provide atmosphere

Etymology

From 'gentle' + 'Muzak', the brand name of a company supplying background music, which became a generic term.
