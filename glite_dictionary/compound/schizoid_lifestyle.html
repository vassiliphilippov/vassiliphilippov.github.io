schizoid lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪt.sɔɪd ˈlaɪf.staɪl / (AmE), / ˈskɪt.sɔɪd ˈlaɪf.staɪl / (BrE)
A lifestyle marked by avoidance of social interaction and emotional detachment.
lifestyle avoiding social interaction
medical

Examples

He embraced a schizoid lifestyle, choosing solitude over socializing with friends.
Her schizoid lifestyle meant she avoided most family gatherings and preferred to spend time alone.
Living a schizoid lifestyle can make it difficult to form close relationships.

Synset Examples

He preferred a schizoid lifestyle, avoiding large gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: introverted - tending to be shy or reserved, enjoying solitude
  • Antonym: sociable - enjoying or seeking the company of others

Etymology

From 'schizoid', from Greek 'skhizein' (to split), used in psychiatry to describe avoidance of social relationships.
