trumpet weed

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmpɪt wiːd / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪt wiːd / (BrE)
A North American herb with whorled leaves and terminal clusters of flowers spotted with purple.
North American herb with purple-spotted flowers

Examples

The field was dotted with patches of trumpet weed swaying in the summer breeze.
Gardeners often remove trumpet weed to prevent it from overtaking other plants.
Clusters of purple-spotted flowers make trumpet weed easy to identify in the wild.
