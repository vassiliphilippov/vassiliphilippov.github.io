cycle rickshaw

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪkəl ˈrɪkˌʃɔ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪkəl ˈrɪkʃɔː / (BrE)
a small, three-wheeled vehicle, usually pedalled by a driver, used to carry passengers for hire
pedal-powered passenger tricycle for hire

Examples

Tourists preferred to explore the old city by cycle rickshaw rather than by taxi.
A cycle rickshaw waited outside the hotel to offer rides to the market.
Many drivers rely on their cycle rickshaw for their daily income.

Synset Examples

boys who once pulled rickshaws now pedal pedicabs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pedicab - a cycle rickshaw; a small passenger vehicle powered by a bicyclist
  • Degree Lower: rickshaw - a light two-wheeled passenger vehicle pulled by one person
