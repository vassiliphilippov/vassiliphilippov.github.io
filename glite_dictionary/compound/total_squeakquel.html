total squeakquel

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊtəl ˈskwik.kwəl / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl ˈskwiːk.kwəl / (BrE)
a sequel, especially to a film, that is unoriginal or poorly made
a poor, unoriginal sequel
informal slang

Examples

The new action movie is just a total squeakquel of the original, with nothing fresh to offer.
Fans were disappointed when the highly anticipated sequel turned out to be a total squeakquel.
Critics agreed that the film was a total squeakquel, lacking innovation or excitement.

Synset Examples

The sequel to that movie is a total squeakquel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sequel - a published, broadcast, or recorded work that continues the story

Etymology

Formed from 'squeak' and 'sequel', popularized by the film title 'Alvin and the Chipmunks: The Squeakquel' (2009).
