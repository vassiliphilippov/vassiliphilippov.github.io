armored sea robin

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmərd si ˈrɑbɪn / (AmE), / ˈɑːməd siː ˈrɒbɪn / (BrE)
a type of sea robin fish with bony plates (scutes) on its body and barbels on its chin, typically found along the continental slope
fish with bony scutes and chin barbels

Examples

The armored sea robin is often found in waters near the continental slope.
Unlike other fish in its habitat, the armored sea robin has distinctive bony scutes.
Marine biologists recently studied the diet of the armored sea robin in the Gulf of Mexico.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea robin - a type of fish known for winglike pectoral fins
