haricots verts

1. NOUN

/ ˌærɪˌkoʊ ˈvɛr / (AmE), / ˌarɪˌkəʊ ˈvɛə / (BrE)
A type of very slender, small green bean, often used in French cuisine and served as a vegetable.
slender green bean used in cooking

Examples

She prepared a side of haricots verts sautéed with garlic and butter.
The restaurant serves haricots verts instead of regular green beans for a more delicate taste.
At the market, I found fresh haricots verts perfect for tonight's dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: green bean - an edible green pod of the common bean plant

Etymology

From French 'haricots verts' meaning 'green beans'.
