Cadillac lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ ˈkædɪˌlæk ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈkadɪlak ˈlʌɪfstʌɪl / (BrE)
A luxurious or extravagant way of living, often implying conspicuous consumption or display of wealth.
luxurious way of living

Examples

He always dreamed of escaping poverty and living a Cadillac lifestyle.
Critics argue that the celebrity chef leads a Cadillac lifestyle while preaching modesty.
Despite his modest job, he managed to maintain a Cadillac lifestyle through careful savings.

Synset Examples

His neighbors accused him of living in a welfare Cadillac lifestyle.

Etymology

Refers to Cadillac, a brand of luxury automobiles, used as a symbol of wealth and high status.
