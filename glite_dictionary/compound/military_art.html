military art

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪləˌtɛri ɑrt / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ɑːt / (BrE)
a genre of visual art that depicts military subjects, such as battles, soldiers, or military life
art depicting military themes

Examples

He has a keen interest in military art and collects prints of historic battles.
The museum's military art gallery features paintings from various wars.
Courses in military art often explore how artists represented soldiers and campaigns.

Synset Examples

The military art of Robert Chapman represents the fighting man from the late 17th Century to the present day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: battle painting - paintings depicting battles or military engagements
  • Synonym: war art - artworks focused on the subject of war
