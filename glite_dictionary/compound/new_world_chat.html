New World chat

1. NOUN

/ nuː wɝːld tʃæt / (AmE), / njuː wɜːld tʃæt / (BrE)
a bird of the Americas, typically with a chattering call, belonging to various genera in the family Icteriidae or related families.
American bird with a chattering call

Examples

The New World chat is famous for its constant chattering in the forest understory.
Birdwatchers are delighted to spot a New World chat during their trips to Central America.
Some New World chats display bright plumage in contrast to their noisy behavior.
