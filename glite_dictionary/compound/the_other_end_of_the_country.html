the other end of the country

1. NOUN

/ ði ˈʌðɚ ˈɛnd əv ðə ˈkʌntri / (AmE), / ði ˈʌðə ˈɛnd əv ðə ˈkʌntri / (BrE)
a location that is as far away as possible within the same country; the most distant part of the country relative to a reference point.
the farthest place within a country

Examples

After graduation, she took a job at the other end of the country.
Visiting my cousin means driving to the other end of the country.
He felt homesick after moving to the other end of the country.

Synset Examples

They’ve moved to the other end of the country.
