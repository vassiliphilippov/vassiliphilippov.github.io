Strayan culture

1. NOUN

/ ˈstreɪ.ən ˈkʌl.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈstreɪ.ən ˈkʌl.tʃə / (BrE)
Australian culture; the traditions, values, and customs typical of Australia, especially referred to informally or humorously.

Related Terms

Etymology

Formed from 'Strayan,' an informal or humorous pronunciation of 'Australian,' and 'culture.'
