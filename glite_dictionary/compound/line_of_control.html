Line of Control

1. PROPN

/ laɪn əv kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / laɪn əv kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
the military control line that separates the Indian- and Pakistani-controlled parts of the former princely state of Jammu and Kashmir.
boundary between Indian- and Pakistani-controlled Kashmir

Examples

The ceasefire agreement along the Line of Control brought a period of relative peace to the region.
Military tensions frequently escalate near the Line of Control in Kashmir.
Journalists are often prohibited from crossing the Line of Control without proper authorization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: border - a line separating two political or geographical areas
  • Synonym: demarcation line - a boundary marking something off from something else
