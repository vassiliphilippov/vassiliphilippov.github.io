premature ejaculation

1. NOUN

/ ˌpriːməˈtʃʊr iˌdʒækjuˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːməˈtʃʊə iˌdʒækjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a condition in which a man ejaculates sooner during sexual activity than he or his partner would like, often occurring shortly after penetration
early ejaculation during sexual activity
medical

Examples

Some men experience premature ejaculation due to psychological factors such as anxiety or stress.
There are various treatments available to help those suffering from premature ejaculation.
Open communication with a partner can help address issues related to premature ejaculation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: delayed ejaculation - difficulty or inability to ejaculate during sexual activity

Etymology

From Latin prae- (before) + maturus (ripe, mature) and Latin ejaculatio (a throwing out).
