pigeon breast

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪdʒən brɛst / (AmE), / ˈpɪdʒɪn brɛst / (BrE)
A deformity in which the breastbone protrudes outward abnormally, often as a result of rickets or other chest disorders.
breastbone protrusion deformity
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed the child with pigeon breast after observing his chest protrusion.
In some cases, pigeon breast can be corrected with braces or surgery.
Children with rickets are at higher risk of developing pigeon breast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pectus carinatum - medical term for outward protrusion of the breastbone
