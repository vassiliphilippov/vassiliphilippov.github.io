Madagascar cat

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæd.əˈɡæs.kər kæt / (AmE), / ˌmæd.əˈɡæs.kə kæt / (BrE)
a small lemur native to Madagascar, noted for its long tail with black and white rings
lemur with a ringed tail from Madagascar

Examples

The Madagascar cat is famous for its strikingly banded tail and expressive face.
In the wild, a Madagascar cat spends much of its time sunbathing and grooming.
Several Madagascar cats were observed leaping between the forest trees at sunrise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ring-tailed lemur - lemur with a distinctive black and white ringed tail

Etymology

Refers to its origin in Madagascar and its cat-like appearance; commonly known as the ring-tailed lemur.
