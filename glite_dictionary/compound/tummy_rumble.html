tummy rumble

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌmi ˌrʌmbəl / (AmE), / ˈtʌmi ˌrʌmbəl / (BrE)
a low, growling noise made by the stomach, often caused by hunger
sound from the stomach due to hunger
informal

Examples

Her tummy rumble was so loud that everyone in the classroom could hear it.
If you skip breakfast, you're likely to experience a tummy rumble before lunch.
The little girl's tummy rumble made her parents realize it was time to eat.

Synset Examples

My tummy rumbled during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stomach rumble - the sound made by the stomach due to hunger or digestion
