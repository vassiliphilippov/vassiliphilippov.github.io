onion butter

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌn.jən ˈbʌt.ər / (AmE), / ˈʌn.jən ˈbʌt.ə / (BrE)
A mixture of butter blended with minced onion, used as a spread or flavoring in cooking.
butter mixed with minced onion

Examples

She spread onion butter on the freshly baked bread for extra flavor.
The steak was topped with a dollop of onion butter before serving.
For the sauce, melt some onion butter over low heat and stir in herbs.
