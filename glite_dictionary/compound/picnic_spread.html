picnic spread

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪknɪk sprɛd / (AmE), / ˈpɪknɪk sprɛd / (BrE)
an assortment of food arranged and laid out for a picnic, typically outdoors
food arranged for a picnic

Examples

We set out our picnic spread under the shade of a large oak tree.
She prepared a delicious picnic spread with sandwiches, fruit, and lemonade.
Ants quickly discovered the picnic spread, marching across the blanket in search of crumbs.

Synset Examples

The bluebottle landed near the picnic spread.
