Strayan accent

1. NOUN

/ ˈstreɪ.ən ˈæk.sɛnt / (AmE), / ˈstreɪ.ən ˈæk.sənt / (BrE)
An informal term for the distinctive accent of people from Australia.
Australian accent (informal term)
informal

Examples

You can always tell he's from Sydney by his Strayan accent.
Her Strayan accent became much stronger after her visit home.
The movie features characters who all speak with a genuine Strayan accent.

Synset Examples

His Strayan accent made him stand out in the crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Australian accent - the accent spoken in Australia

Etymology

From a colloquial shortening of 'Australian.'
