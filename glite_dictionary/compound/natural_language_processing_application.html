natural language processing application

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃɚl ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ ˌprɑːsɛsɪŋ ˌæpləˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈnætʃrəl ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ ˌprɒsɛsɪŋ ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a software program designed to process, analyze, or generate text in natural human language
program for processing human language text

Examples

The company developed a natural language processing application to automatically summarize news articles.
A good natural language processing application can understand the intent behind your queries.
Many chatbots use a natural language processing application to interpret customer requests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: NLP - abbreviation for natural language processing
  • Synonym: speech recognition - the ability of a machine to identify words and phrases in spoken language
