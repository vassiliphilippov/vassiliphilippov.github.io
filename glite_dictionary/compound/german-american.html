German-American

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒɜrmən əˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / ˌdʒɜːmən əˈmɛrɪkən / (BrE)
of, relating to, or characteristic of German Americans or their culture
relating to German Americans or their culture

Examples

The city hosts an annual German-American festival celebrating the heritage of its immigrant community.
She grew up in a German-American household, learning both languages from a young age.
Many German-American traditions have influenced the culture of the Midwest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: German - of or relating to Germany or its people
  • Synonym: American - of or relating to the United States or its people
