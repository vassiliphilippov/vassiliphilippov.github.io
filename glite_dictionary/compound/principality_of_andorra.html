Principality of Andorra

1. PROPN

/ prɪnˌsɪp.əl.ə.ti əv ænˈdɔːr.ə / (AmE), / prɪnˌsɪp.əl.ə.ti əv ænˈdɒr.ə / (BrE)
a small, independent country situated in the eastern Pyrenees mountains between Spain and France
European country in the Pyrenees

Examples

The Principality of Andorra attracts tourists with its skiing resorts and tax-free shopping.
Despite its size, the Principality of Andorra maintains its own parliament and laws.
Nestled between France and Spain, the Principality of Andorra has a rich cultural heritage.

Etymology

From Latin 'principatus' (principality) and 'Andorra,' of uncertain origin; traditionally believed to derive from a pre-Roman local name.
