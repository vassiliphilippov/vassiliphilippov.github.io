surface gauge

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːfɪs ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfɪs ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument consisting of a scriber mounted on an adjustable stand, used for marking and testing the accuracy of flat surfaces.
gauge for marking or testing flat surfaces

Examples

The machinist used a surface gauge to mark precise lines on the metal plate.
A surface gauge can check whether a surface is truly flat.
Proper calibration of the surface gauge ensured accurate measurements during the inspection.
