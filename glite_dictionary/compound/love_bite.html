love bite

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌv ˌbaɪt / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ˌbaɪt / (BrE)
A temporary red or purple mark on the skin caused by kissing or sucking, especially by a lover.
mark on skin caused by kissing or sucking

Examples

She wore a scarf to hide the love bite on her neck.
He was embarrassed when his friend noticed the love bite after their date.
Some people use makeup to cover up a visible love bite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hickey - A mark on the skin caused by sucking or biting, especially during kissing
