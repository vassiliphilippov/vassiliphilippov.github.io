snorkel diving

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnɔr.kəl ˈdaɪ.vɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsnɔː.kəl ˈdaɪ.vɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of swimming underwater while using a snorkel to breathe at the surface
swimming using a snorkel

Examples

The clear waters of the bay are perfect for snorkel diving during the summer months.
She bought a new mask and fins for her next snorkel diving adventure.
Many marine reserves offer guided snorkel diving tours to observe colorful reef fish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scuba diving - swimming underwater using a self-contained breathing apparatus
