mustard sauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌstɚd sɔs / (AmE), / ˈmʌstəd sɔːs / (BrE)
A sauce made from prepared mustard, typically thinned with vinegar and oil and flavored with sugar and seasonings.
sauce made from prepared mustard

Examples

He served the grilled salmon with a side of mustard sauce for extra flavor.
The recipe calls for a creamy mustard sauce to be drizzled over the chicken.
You can easily make your own mustard sauce with basic pantry ingredients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mayonnaise - A thick, creamy dressing made from oil, egg yolk, and vinegar or lemon juice
