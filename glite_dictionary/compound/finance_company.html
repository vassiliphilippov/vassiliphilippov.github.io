finance company

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪnæns ˌkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / ˈfaɪ.næns ˌkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)
a company that provides loans to individuals or businesses, usually without accepting deposits, often as part of a larger group or manufacturer
company that makes loans

Examples

After being refused by the bank, she went to a finance company for a car loan.
Many small businesses rely on finance companies to obtain working capital.
The finance company approved his application within a day.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bank - financial institution accepting deposits and making loans
