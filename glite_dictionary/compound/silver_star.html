Silver Star

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ stɑr / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə stɑː / (BrE)
a United States military decoration awarded for gallantry in action
US military decoration for gallantry

Examples

He was awarded the Silver Star for heroic actions during the battle.
Receiving the Silver Star is one of the highest honors in the U.S. Army.
The general commended those who had earned the Silver Star for their bravery.
