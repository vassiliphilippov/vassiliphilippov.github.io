riot control

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪət kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / ˈraɪət kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
the methods and measures used by police or authorities to manage and suppress riots or large public disturbances
measures to control riots

Examples

The police deployed special riot control equipment to disperse the crowd.
Effective riot control is essential during large protests or demonstrations.
Training in riot control is mandatory for all members of the tactical unit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crowd control - measures to manage or direct crowds
