fence lizard

1. NOUN

/ fɛns ˈlɪzərd / (AmE), / fɛns ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
a spiny lizard commonly found basking on fences or rocks in the United States and northern Mexico
a North American spiny lizard

Examples

We spotted a fence lizard sunning itself on the backyard wall.
The fence lizard quickly darted away as we approached.
In some regions, the fence lizard is a common sight on old wooden fences.
