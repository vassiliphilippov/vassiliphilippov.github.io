yucky smell

1. NOUN

/ ˈjʌki smɛl / (AmE), / ˈjʌki smɛl / (BrE)
an unpleasant or disgusting odor; a smell that is offensive to the senses
an unpleasant odor
informal

Examples

There was a yucky smell coming from the kitchen sink this morning.
My little brother complained about the yucky smell in his shoes after soccer practice.
The yucky smell of rotten eggs quickly filled the air when the container broke.

Synset Examples

I think that yucky smell is coming from the trash.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad smell - an unpleasant odor
  • Antonym: pleasant smell - a smell that is agreeable or pleasing

Etymology

From 'yucky', an informal term meaning disgusting or unpleasant, probably imitative in origin, combined with 'smell'.
