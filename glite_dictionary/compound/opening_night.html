opening night

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊpənɪŋ naɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊpənɪŋ naɪt / (BrE)
the first public performance of a theatrical production, film, or similar event
first public performance

Examples

The cast was nervous before their opening night performance at the theater.
Tickets for the opening night sold out weeks in advance.
She received flowers on opening night after her starring role in the play.

Synset Examples

the opening received good critical reviews

Related Terms

  • Synonym: premiere - the first public showing of a play, film, etc.
