kludgy solution

1. NOUN

/ ˈkluːdʒi səˈluːʃən / (AmE), / ˈkluːdʒi səˈluːʃən / (BrE)
an awkward, makeshift, or inelegant fix to a problem, typically one that is temporary or unreliable
awkward or makeshift fix

Examples

Because there was no time to rewrite the code, he came up with a kludgy solution that barely worked.
The temporary fix turned out to be a kludgy solution that caused more issues down the line.
In software engineering, a kludgy solution is often more trouble than it's worth.

Synset Examples

His kludgy solution failed during the presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workaround - a method for overcoming a problem or limitation in a system or program
  • Synonym: hack - a quick solution that is inelegant or temporary, often in software or engineering
  • Antonym: elegant solution - a well-designed, efficient, and pleasing fix to a problem

Etymology

From 'kludge', possibly of 1960s US computing origin, meaning an inelegant workaround.
