early coral root

1. NOUN

/ ˈɜrli ˈkɔɹəl ˌɹuːt / (AmE), / ˈɜːli ˈkɒɹəl ˌruːt / (BrE)
a North American orchid with pale yellow or greenish stems and flowers, almost leafless, found from northern New Mexico to Alaska.
a North American orchid species

Examples

We spotted an early coral root blooming in the shadowy forest near the river.
Because early coral root lacks true leaves, it often goes unnoticed among the undergrowth.
The botanist collected samples of early coral root for her research on native orchids.
