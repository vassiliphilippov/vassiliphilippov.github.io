mathematical symbol

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæθəˈmætɪkəl ˈsɪmbəl / (AmE), / ˌmæθɪˈmatɪkəl ˈsɪmbəl / (BrE)
a character or sign used to represent a mathematical relation, operation, quantity, or value
sign indicating a mathematical relation or operation

Examples

The plus sign is a common mathematical symbol taught in early arithmetic classes.
He wasn't sure what the strange mathematical symbol meant in the equation.
Each mathematical symbol has a precise meaning in this formula.
