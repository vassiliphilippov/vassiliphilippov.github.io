sorta kinda

1. ADV

/ ˈsɔɹtə ˈkaɪnə / (AmE), / ˈsɔːtə ˈkaɪnə / (BrE)
(informal) Used to indicate an uncertain or approximate degree; somewhat or rather.
informal: somewhat; rather; hedged approximation
informal

Examples

I’m sorta kinda excited about the new project, but I’m also a little nervous.
She was sorta kinda annoyed by his comments, though she tried not to show it.
The movie was sorta kinda funny, but not as good as I expected.

Synset Examples

I’m sorta kinda tired after that workout.
He’s sorta kinda interested in joining the club.
It’s sorta kinda like a vacation, but I still work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: somewhat - to a moderate extent or degree
