steamship company

1. NOUN

/ ˈstiːmˌʃɪp ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈstiːmˌʃɪp ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a company engaged in operating steamships, typically providing passenger or cargo transport services by water
company operating steamships

Examples

The steamship company expanded its routes to include travel between Europe and the Americas.
In the late 19th century, a steamship company played a vital role in global trade.
He inherited shares in a family-owned steamship company after his grandfather passed away.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shipping line - company that operates ships that transport cargo or passengers
