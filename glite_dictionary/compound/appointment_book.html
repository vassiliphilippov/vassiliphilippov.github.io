appointment book

1. NOUN

/ əˈpɔɪntmənt bʊk / (AmE), / əˈpɔɪntmənt bʊk / (BrE)
A book with a calendar and spaces used to record and organize appointments or scheduled events.
book for recording appointments

Examples

She glanced at her appointment book to confirm her schedule for the week.
He forgot to write the meeting in his appointment book and missed it entirely.
My appointment book is completely full next month with clients and deadlines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diary - a book in which to write daily records and plans
  • Synonym: planner - a book or pad for organizing and scheduling appointments and tasks
