eye operation

1. NOUN

/ aɪ ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / aɪ ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a surgical procedure performed on the eye to correct vision or treat eye conditions
surgical procedure on the eye

Examples

The doctor recommended an eye operation to correct her cataracts.
After his eye operation, Jim was able to see clearly again.
She was nervous before undergoing the eye operation last month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eye surgery - surgery performed on the eye
