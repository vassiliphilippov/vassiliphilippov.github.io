canna lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈkænə ˌlɪli / (AmE), / ˈkanə ˌlɪli / (BrE)
a tropical plant with large, brightly colored yellow to red flowers and broad leaves, commonly grown as an ornamental
ornamental plant with bright flowers

Examples

The vibrant canna lily brightened up the entire garden.
She planted a row of canna lilies along the fence for a splash of color.
The large leaves of the canna lily provided a striking contrast to the smaller plants.

Etymology

From Latin 'Canna' (reed), referencing the plant's reed-like stems, and 'lily', for its flower resemblance.
