protective cover

1. NOUN

/ prəˈtɛktɪv ˈkʌvɚ / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktɪv ˈkʌvə / (BrE)
A covering that is designed to shield something from damage or injury.
covering for protection

Examples

The car was kept in good condition thanks to a protective cover during the winter months.
She bought a protective cover for her phone to prevent it from getting scratched.
Please make sure the barbecue has its protective cover on when it rains.

Synset Examples

they had no protection from the fallout
wax provided protection for the floors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cover - something that covers or protects
  • Synonym: protective covering - a covering designed to protect something
