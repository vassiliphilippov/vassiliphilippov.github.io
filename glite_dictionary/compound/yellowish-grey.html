yellowish-grey

1. ADJ

/ ˈjɛloʊɪʃ ˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊ.ɪʃ ˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a grey color tinged with yellow; having a light, slightly yellow tone added to grey.
grey color with a yellow tint

Examples

The kitten had yellowish-grey fur that made her look almost like a cloud in sunset light.
He chose a yellowish-grey paint for the living room walls to create a warm but neutral atmosphere.
The fog appeared yellowish-grey near the street lamps, coloring everything in a strange glow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grey - color between black and white; having a neutral tone
