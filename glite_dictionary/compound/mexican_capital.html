Mexican capital

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛksɪkən ˈkæpɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛksɪkən ˈkapɪtəl / (BrE)
the capital city of Mexico, officially called Mexico City, which serves as the country's political, cultural, and economic center
capital city of Mexico

Examples

The Mexican capital is known for its vibrant food scene and historic landmarks.
After moving to the Mexican capital, she found a job in international business.
Air pollution is a significant concern in the Mexican capital, especially during the dry season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mexico City - official name of the Mexican capital city
