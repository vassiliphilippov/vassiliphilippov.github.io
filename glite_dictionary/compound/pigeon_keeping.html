pigeon keeping

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪdʒən ˌkiːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪdʒɪn ˌkiːpɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of breeding and caring for domestic pigeons, often as a hobby or for sport
breeding and caring for domestic pigeons

Examples

He became interested in pigeon keeping as a boy after receiving his first pair of birds.
Advancements in pigeon keeping have led to healthier and more diverse pigeon breeds.
There are clubs and competitions dedicated to the art of pigeon keeping worldwide.

Synset Examples

For a healthy, immune-competent person, pigeon keeping in accord with current avicultural standards is a very safe activity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aviculture - the breeding and care of birds as a hobby
