national holiday

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæʃnəl ˈhɑlɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈnæʃnəl ˈhɒlɪdeɪ / (BrE)
a public holiday recognized and authorized by a nation's government, during which work and official business are generally suspended
public holiday officially observed nationwide

Examples

The entire country celebrates national holiday with parades and fireworks every year.
Banks and government offices are closed on a national holiday.
Independence Day is considered a national holiday in many countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public holiday - a day when most people do not have to work by law
