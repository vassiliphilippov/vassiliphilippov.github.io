sexual desire

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛk.ʃu.əl dɪˈzaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛk.ʃu.əl dɪˈzaɪə / (BrE)
A strong feeling of wanting to experience sexual intimacy or sexual activity with another person.
longing for sexual intimacy

Examples

Many people experience sexual desire as a normal part of healthy relationships.
Certain medications can reduce sexual desire as a side effect.
His sexual desire diminished after a long period of stress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: libido - sexual drive or desire
  • Synonym: lust - strong sexual desire
