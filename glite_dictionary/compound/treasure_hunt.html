treasure hunt

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɛʒɚ hʌnt / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒə hʌnt / (BrE)
a game in which players search for hidden objects or prizes by following a series of clues
game searching for hidden objects using clues

Examples

The children spent the afternoon on a treasure hunt around the park.
We organized a treasure hunt for my sister's birthday party.
Clues for the treasure hunt were hidden in every room of the house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scavenger hunt - a game in which participants collect a list of items
