skating rink

1. NOUN

/ ˈskeɪtɪŋ rɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈskeɪtɪŋ rɪŋk / (BrE)
a building or area with a specially prepared surface for ice skating or roller skating
place for ice or roller skating

Examples

Every winter, the city opens an outdoor skating rink in the park.
She celebrated her birthday at the local skating rink with friends.
The skating rink offers lessons for both beginners and advanced skaters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice rink - place with an ice surface for skating
