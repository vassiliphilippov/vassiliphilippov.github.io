insecticidal soap

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˌsɛktɪˈsaɪdəl soʊp / (AmE), / ɪnˌsɛktɪˈsaɪdəl səʊp / (BrE)
a soap-based solution used to control or kill insects, especially pests on plants
soap solution for killing insects

Examples

Many gardeners prefer to use insecticidal soap as a safer alternative to chemical pesticides.
Spraying insecticidal soap on aphid-infested plants can help control their population.
It is important to follow the instructions on the insecticidal soap label to avoid damaging your plants.

Synset Examples

Using insecticidal soap helped eliminate the pea louse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pesticide - a substance used to kill pests
  • Synonym: insecticide - a substance specifically formulated to kill insects
