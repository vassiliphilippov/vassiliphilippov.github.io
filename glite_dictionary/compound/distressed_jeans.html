distressed jeans

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈstrɛst dʒinz / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɛst dʒiːnz / (BrE)
Jean trousers that have been deliberately ripped, faded, or worn to give a rugged, aged appearance.
jeans purposely torn or faded for style

Examples

She paired her distressed jeans with a simple white T-shirt and sneakers.
Many fashion brands now sell distressed jeans that look worn and torn.
His favorite outfit includes a leather jacket and distressed jeans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ripped jeans - jeans with intentional tears or holes
