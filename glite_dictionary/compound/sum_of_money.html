sum of money

1. NOUN

/ sʌm əv ˈmʌni / (AmE), / sʌm ɒv ˈmʌni / (BrE)
an amount of money; a quantity of money considered as a single total
amount of money

Examples

She donated a considerable sum of money to the local hospital.
He inherited a small sum of money from his uncle.
The company invested a large sum of money into research and development.
