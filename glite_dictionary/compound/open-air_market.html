open-air market

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊpən ˈɛr ˈmɑrkɪt / (AmE), / ˌəʊpən ˈeə ˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
a market held outdoors, where vendors sell food and other goods to the public
outdoor public market for goods and food

Examples

Every Saturday, the town hosts an open-air market full of fresh produce and handmade crafts.
Tourists enjoyed browsing antiques at the famous open-air market near the river.
Vendors set up their stalls early in the morning for the bustling open-air market in the city square.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: market - a regular gathering to buy and sell provisions, livestock, etc.
