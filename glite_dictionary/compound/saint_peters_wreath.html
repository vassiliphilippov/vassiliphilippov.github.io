Saint Peter's wreath

1. NOUN

/ seɪnt ˈpiːtɚz riːθ / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈpiːtəz riːθ / (BrE)
a shrub with abundant small white flowers that bloom in spring, often grown as an ornamental plant
shrub with white spring flowers

Examples

In the garden, the Saint Peter's wreath was covered with tiny white blossoms.
Many landscapers recommend planting Saint Peter's wreath for its beautiful spring display.
She trimmed the Saint Peter's wreath after it had finished flowering in late spring.
