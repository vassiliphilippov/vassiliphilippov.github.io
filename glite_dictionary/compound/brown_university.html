Brown University

1. PROPN

/ braʊn juːnɪˈvɜrsəti / (AmE), / braʊn juːnɪˈvɜːsəti / (BrE)
A private Ivy League university located in Providence, Rhode Island, United States.
Ivy League university in Rhode Island

Examples

She received her degree from Brown University in 2010.
Many students aspire to attend prestigious institutions like Brown University.
The campus of Brown University is known for its historic buildings and vibrant community.

Etymology

Named after Nicholas Brown, Jr., a philanthropist and early benefactor of the university.
