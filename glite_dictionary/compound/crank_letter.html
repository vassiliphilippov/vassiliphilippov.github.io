crank letter

1. NOUN

/ kræŋk ˈlɛtər / (AmE), / kræŋk ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
a hostile or threatening letter, typically sent anonymously
hostile anonymous letter

Examples

The politician received a crank letter that threatened his safety.
After publishing her article, she was dismayed to find a crank letter in her mailbox.
The police were able to trace several crank letters to the same disgruntled former employee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poison pen letter - an anonymous letter containing abusive or malicious statements
