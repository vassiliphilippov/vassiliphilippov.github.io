great care

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪt kɛr / (AmE), / ɡreɪt keə / (BrE)
attentive and thorough caution or attention, exceeding the usual level of prudence
exceptional caution or attention

Examples

The antique vase was wrapped and shipped with great care to avoid any damage.
Please handle these confidential documents with great care when moving them to the archive.
She approached the wild animal with great care, careful not to startle it.

Synset Examples

the pilot exercised great care in landing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attention - focused mental effort or concentration
  • Antonym: negligence - failure to take proper care
