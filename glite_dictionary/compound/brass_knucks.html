brass knucks

1. NOUN

/ bræs nʌks / (AmE), / brɑːs nʌks / (BrE)
A small metal weapon worn over the knuckles to increase the force of a blow.
metal weapon for knuckles
slang

Examples

The police confiscated a pair of brass knucks during the search.
He carried brass knucks in his jacket pocket for self-defense.
Possession of brass knucks is illegal in many states due to their dangerous nature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brass knuckles - metal weapon worn over the knuckles
