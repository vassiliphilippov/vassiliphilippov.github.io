superkid attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuː.pɚ.kɪd ˈæt̬.ɪ.tuːd / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pə.kɪd ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
a highly positive, confident, or exceptional mindset, especially one resembling the resilience or optimism attributed to a 'superkid'
exceptional positive mindset
informal

Examples

Her superkid attitude inspires everyone around her.
Facing adversity, he kept his superkid attitude and never gave up hope.
Teachers admire students who bring a superkid attitude to every challenge in school.
