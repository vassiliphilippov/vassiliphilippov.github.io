china closet

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪ.nə ˌklɑ.sət / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪ.nə ˌklɒz.ɪt / (BrE)
A cabinet, usually with glass doors, used for storing and displaying fine china and dishes.
cabinet for displaying china dishes

Examples

Her grandmother kept her prized tea set in the china closet by the dining room window.
We admired the delicate plates on display in the old oak china closet.
He carefully arranged the porcelain figurines inside the glass shelves of the china closet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: china cabinet - cabinet with glass doors for displaying china

Etymology

From 'china' (porcelain dishes) and 'closet' (a small room or cabinet).
