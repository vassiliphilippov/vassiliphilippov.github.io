stone cress

1. NOUN

/ stoʊn krɛs / (AmE), / stəʊn krɛs / (BrE)
Any of several small Old World herbs of the genus Aethionema, typically found in sunny, limestone-rich habitats.
Old World herb of the genus Aethionema

Examples

The stone cress thrives on rocky, sunlit slopes across southern Europe.
Gardeners admire stone cress for its delicate pink flowers and hardiness.
Some species of stone cress are grown as ornamental ground cover in limestone gardens.
