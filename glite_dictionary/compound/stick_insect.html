stick insect

1. NOUN

/ stɪk ˈɪn.sɛkt / (AmE), / stɪk ˈɪn.sɛkt / (BrE)
a slender, often elongated insect resembling a twig or stick, mainly found in tropical regions
insect resembling a twig

Examples

The stick insect was so well camouflaged that it was almost invisible on the branch.
My cousin collects different species of stick insects from around the world.
We watched a stick insect slowly move along the fence, blending in perfectly with the twigs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phasmid - another term for stick insect or member of the same family
