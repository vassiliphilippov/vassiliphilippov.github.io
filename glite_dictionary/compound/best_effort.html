best effort

1. NOUN

/ bɛst ˈɛfɚt / (AmE), / bɛst ˈɛfət / (BrE)
the greatest amount of effort that someone can give to achieve something
maximum possible effort

Examples

She gave her best effort during the job interview, hoping to impress the employer.
Despite his best effort, he was unable to solve the puzzle in time.
The team put in their best effort, but still fell short of a victory.

Synset Examples

He gave his best effort in the competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maximum effort - the highest possible effort expended on something
  • Antonym: minimal effort - the least possible effort required to do something
