correspondence course

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɑːspəndəns kɔrs / (AmE), / kəˈrɒspəndəns kɔːs / (BrE)
a course of study in which lessons and assignments are sent and returned by mail or online, allowing students to learn remotely
course studied remotely via mail or online

Examples

He completed a correspondence course in accounting while working full-time.
Many universities now offer correspondence courses that can be taken entirely online.
She received her diploma after finishing a correspondence course in graphic design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distance learning - instruction and learning delivered remotely, often electronically
