smooth crabgrass

1. NOUN

/ smuð ˈkræbˌɡræs / (AmE), / smuːð ˈkrabˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a low-growing annual weed (Digitaria ischaemum) with smooth stems and leaves that spreads by seeds and by rooting at the lower joints
a low-growing annual weed (Digitaria ischaemum)

Examples

The lawn was overrun with smooth crabgrass by midsummer.
Gardeners often struggle to remove smooth crabgrass from their flowerbeds.
Early detection and removal can help prevent smooth crabgrass from spreading.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crabgrass - a common name for various weedy grasses of the genus Digitaria
