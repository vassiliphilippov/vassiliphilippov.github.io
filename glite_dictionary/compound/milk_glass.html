milk glass

1. NOUN

/ mɪlk ɡlæs / (AmE), / mɪlk ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a type of glass that is milky white and either translucent or opaque, often used for decorative objects and tableware
milky white translucent or opaque glass

Examples

The antique vase was made of beautiful milk glass, giving it a soft glow in the sunlight.
Collectors often seek out milk glass dishes from the early twentieth century.
She decorated her kitchen shelves with several pieces of vintage milk glass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opal glass - glass that is opalescent and white, used for similar purposes
