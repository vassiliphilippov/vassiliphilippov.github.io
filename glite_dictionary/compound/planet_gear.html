planet gear

1. NOUN

/ ˈplænət ɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈplænɪt ɡɪə / (BrE)
A gear in an epicyclic gear train that revolves around a central sun gear and meshes with both the sun and the outer ring gear.
gear revolving around a sun gear

Examples

The planet gear rotates around the sun gear within the gearbox assembly.
Regular maintenance is required to ensure the planet gears operate smoothly in heavy machinery.
A malfunctioning planet gear can cause the entire transmission to fail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sun gear - central gear in an epicyclic gear train
  • Synonym: ring gear - outer gear in an epicyclic gear train

Etymology

Derived from the analogy to planets orbiting around the sun, referring to the gear's movement pattern.
