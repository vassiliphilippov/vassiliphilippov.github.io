yellow foxtail

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈfɑksˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈfɒksˌteɪl / (BrE)
a common weedy grass with bristly seed heads, found in nearly all temperate regions
common weedy bristly grass

Examples

The field was overrun with yellow foxtail, making it difficult for crops to grow.
Many gardeners consider yellow foxtail a persistent and troublesome weed.
You can identify yellow foxtail by its bristly, yellow-tipped seed heads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foxtail - a type of grass with a bushy seed head resembling a fox's tail
