pine tree

1. NOUN

/ paɪn ˌtri / (AmE), / paɪn ˌtriː / (BrE)
An evergreen coniferous tree with needle-shaped leaves and cones, belonging to the genus Pinus.
an evergreen coniferous tree (genus Pinus)

Examples

We planted a pine tree in our backyard last spring.
The forest was dense with tall pine trees and the air smelled fresh.
Many birds make their nests in the branches of the pine tree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pine - an evergreen coniferous tree of the genus Pinus
