Hawaii Island

1. PROPN

/ həˈwaɪ.i ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / həˈwaɪ.i ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
the largest and southernmost island of Hawaii, known for its volcanic peaks and often called the 'Big Island'
the largest island in Hawaii

Examples

Many tourists visit the Hawaii Island to see its active volcanoes and beautiful black sand beaches.
The climate on Hawaii Island varies greatly from one part of the island to another.
Scientists frequently study volcanic activity on Hawaii Island due to its unique geology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Big Island - another name for Hawaii Island
  • Degree Lower: Maui - the second largest Hawaiian island

Etymology

From Hawaiian 'Hawai‘i'.
