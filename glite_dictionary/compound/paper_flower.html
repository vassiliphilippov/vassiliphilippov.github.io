paper flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpɚ ˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A tropical South American woody vine (Bougainvillea) with showy, colorful bracts that resemble paper, often grown as an ornamental plant.
showy-flowered tropical vine (Bougainvillea)

Examples

The garden was brightened by vibrant paper flowers climbing along the fence.
She planted a paper flower in a large pot near the patio for extra color.
Bougainvillea, commonly called the paper flower, thrives in warm, sunny climates.
