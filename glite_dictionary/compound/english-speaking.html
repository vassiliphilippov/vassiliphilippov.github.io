English-speaking

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˌspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˌspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
able to speak or communicate in English; used to describe persons, countries, or communities where English is spoken.
able to speak English

Examples

Canada is an English-speaking country, but it also has a large French-speaking population.
The company is looking for an English-speaking assistant to help with international clients.
Her new neighbors are English-speaking, making it easier for her to communicate.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: non-English-speaking - not able to speak or not using English
