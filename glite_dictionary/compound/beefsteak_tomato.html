beefsteak tomato

1. NOUN

/ ˈbifˌsteɪk təˈmeɪtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbiːfsteɪk təˈmɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
a very large variety of tomato with thick, juicy flesh, often eaten sliced in sandwiches or salads
large, thick-fleshed tomato variety

Examples

He sliced a ripe beefsteak tomato for the perfect summer sandwich.
The salad was topped with thick wedges of beefsteak tomato and fresh mozzarella.
We grew several beefsteak tomato plants in our backyard garden this year.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: tomato - a glossy red, or occasionally yellow, pulpy edible fruit eaten as a vegetable
