angular furniture

1. NOUN

/ ˈæŋɡjələr ˈfɝːnɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡjʊlə ˈfɜːnɪtʃə / (BrE)
Furniture characterized by sharp, geometric, and angular shapes, often associated with modern or contemporary styles.
furniture with sharp, geometric shapes

Examples

The living room was decorated with angular furniture to give it a sleek, modern appearance.
Designers often use angular furniture to create a sense of sophistication in contemporary spaces.
She replaced her old, rounded chairs with angular furniture to match the new minimalist décor.

Synset Examples

He preferred angular furniture for its modern look.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: curved furniture - furniture with rounded or flowing shapes
