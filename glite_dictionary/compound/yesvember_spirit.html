Yesvember spirit

1. NOUN

/ ˌjɛsˈvɛmbər ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˌjɛsˈvɛmbə ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
An attitude of optimism and openness to new opportunities during the month of November, often encouraged as part of motivational or positive-thinking campaigns.
optimistic attitude in November
informal

Examples

The Yesvember spirit helped our team tackle every challenge with enthusiasm.
Embracing the Yesvember spirit, she agreed to try new experiences all month.
Let's spread the Yesvember spirit and encourage everyone to stay positive in November.

Synset Examples

We can achieve anything in this Yesvember spirit!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimism - The tendency to be hopeful and confident about the future
  • Antonym: No-vember - A play on 'November' focusing on saying 'no', often contrasted with 'Yesvember'

Etymology

Blend of 'yes' and 'November', patterned after 'Movember' and similar wordplays.
