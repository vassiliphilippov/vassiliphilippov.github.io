flamboyant tree

1. NOUN

/ flæmˈbɔɪənt tri / (AmE), / flamˈbɔɪənt triː / (BrE)
a tropical tree or shrub with showy yellow to orange-red flowers, native to Madagascar and often grown as an ornamental
tropical tree with showy, bright flowers

Examples

The flamboyant tree bloomed early this year, brightening the street with its fiery red petals.
In many Caribbean towns, the flamboyant tree is planted as a symbol of summer.
Tourists often take photos of the flamboyant tree because of its dramatically colored flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: royal poinciana - another name for the flamboyant tree
