floorboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɔrˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈflɔːˌbɔːd / (BrE)
A long, flat piece of wood that forms part of a floor in a building.
board forming part of a floor

Examples

Be careful not to trip over the loose floorboard near the kitchen door.
They pulled up the floorboards to check for any hidden wiring.
Creaking floorboards echoed through the old house at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flooring - material used for covering a floor
