International Association of Lions Clubs

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪntɚˈnæʃənəl əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən əv ˈlaɪənz klʌbz / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃnəl əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃn əv ˈlaɪənz klʌbz / (BrE)
a worldwide service organization dedicated to promoting responsible citizenship, good government, and community welfare through volunteer work.
global civic service organization

Examples

The International Association of Lions Clubs organizes vision screenings and community projects worldwide.
He became a member of the International Association of Lions Clubs to volunteer in his community.
The International Association of Lions Clubs celebrated its centennial with service events across many countries.

Etymology

Founded in 1917 in the United States; 'Lions' chosen to symbolize courage and strength.
