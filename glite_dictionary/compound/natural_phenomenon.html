natural phenomenon

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃrəl fəˈnɑːməˌnɑːn / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl fɪˈnɒmɪnən / (BrE)
an event or process in nature not caused by human activity
event in nature, not artificial

Examples

A rainbow is a famous natural phenomenon that occurs after rain when sunlight is refracted by water droplets.
Earthquakes are powerful natural phenomena that shape the planet's surface.
The northern lights are a beautiful natural phenomenon visible in polar regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phenomenon - an observable occurrence or event
  • Antonym: artificial phenomenon - event resulting from human intervention

2. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃrəl fəˈnɑːməˌnɑːn / (AmE), / ˈnatʃrəl fɪˈnɒmɪnən / (BrE)
an event or process that occurs in nature and can be observed, without human intervention
event in nature

Examples

A rainbow is a natural phenomenon that occurs when sunlight passes through rain droplets.
Volcano eruptions are awe-inspiring examples of a natural phenomenon.
The scientists studied the migration of birds as a natural phenomenon.

Synset Examples

They explained that swamp gas is a natural phenomenon.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: miracle - an extraordinary event attributed to a supernatural cause
