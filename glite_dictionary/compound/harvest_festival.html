harvest festival

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrvɪst ˈfɛstəvəl / (AmE), / ˈhɑːvɪst ˈfɛstɪvəl / (BrE)
A festival held to celebrate the completion of the annual harvest, often marked by feasting, rituals, and community gatherings.
celebration marking the end of the harvest

Examples

The entire community came together to enjoy the harvest festival after weeks of hard work in the fields.
Many cultures have unique traditions for their annual harvest festival, including dances, music, and special foods.
Children made decorations out of corn husks for the local harvest festival in October.

Synset Examples

They celebrated the equinox with a harvest festival.
Every ploughperson in the village gathered for the harvest festival.
