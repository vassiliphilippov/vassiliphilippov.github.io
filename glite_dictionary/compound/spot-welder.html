spot-welder

1. NOUN

/ spɑt ˈwɛldər / (AmE), / spɒt ˈwɛldə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to join pieces of metal together by spot welding
person who performs spot welding

Examples

The spot-welder carefully joined the car panels together on the assembly line.
After years as a spot-welder, she became a supervisor in the factory.
They hired an experienced spot-welder to ensure the metal joints were strong.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welder - a person whose job is to weld metals together
