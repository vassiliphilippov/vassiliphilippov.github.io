chiliast movement

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪliæst ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / ˈkɪlɪast ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
a religious movement that believes in the imminent coming of a thousand-year period of peace and righteousness, often based on millenarian prophecy
religious movement expecting a coming millennium

Examples

The chiliast movement drew crowds who awaited the prophesied golden age.
Many religious groups in history have emerged as part of a chiliast movement.
Debates about the authenticity of the chiliast movement filled the community's gatherings.

Synset Examples

The chiliast movement gained followers during the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millenarianism - belief in a coming major transformation, especially a thousand-year reign
  • Synonym: apocalypticism - belief in the end of the world or a great transformation

Etymology

From Greek 'chilioi' (thousand) and 'movement'. Related to 'chiliast', a believer in the millennium.
