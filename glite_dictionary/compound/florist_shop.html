florist shop

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɔːrɪst ʃɑːp / (AmE), / ˈflɒrɪst ʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop where flowers and ornamental plants are sold.
shop selling flowers and plants

Examples

I stopped by the florist shop to pick up a bouquet for my friend.
The florist shop downtown has beautiful arrangements for every occasion.
She decided to open her own florist shop after years of working with flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flower shop - a shop where flowers are sold
