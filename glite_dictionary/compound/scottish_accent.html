Scottish accent

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑːtɪʃ ˈæksənt / (AmE), / ˈskɒtɪʃ ˈæksənt / (BrE)
the distinctive way of pronouncing English spoken by people from Scotland
pronunciation of English from Scotland

Examples

Her Scottish accent made her instantly recognizable in the London office.
He lost most of his Scottish accent after living abroad for many years.
The actor adopted a convincing Scottish accent for his role in the film.

Synset Examples

He spoke with a strong Scottisher accent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Scottish English - the variety of English spoken in Scotland
  • Degree Lower: English accent - an accent from England
