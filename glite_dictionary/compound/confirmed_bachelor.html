confirmed bachelor

1. NOUN

/ kənˈfɝːmd ˈbætʃələr / (AmE), / kənˈfɜːmd ˈbætʃələ / (BrE)
a man who has chosen to remain unmarried, often for a long period or permanently
man who chooses never to marry

Examples

His friends joked about him being a confirmed bachelor forever.
After years of living alone, he embraced the title of confirmed bachelor.
The group consisted mostly of confirmed bachelors who enjoyed their independent lifestyles.
