corn lily

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn ˈlɪli / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈlɪli / (BrE)
Any of several South African plants of the genus Ixia with grasslike leaves and clusters of showy, variously colored, lily-like flowers, widely cultivated.
South African plant with showy, lily-like flowers

Examples

The garden was vibrant with blooming corn lilies of many different colors.
She planted a row of corn lilies along the path for their striking appearance.
Many gardeners value the corn lily for its hardy nature and beautiful flowers.
