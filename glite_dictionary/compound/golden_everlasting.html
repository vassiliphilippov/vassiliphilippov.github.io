golden everlasting

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˌɛvərˈlæstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˌɛvəˈlɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
an Australian plant with bright yellow to deep gold flowers, often used as a dried flower
Australian plant with golden flowers

Examples

She decorated the table with a bouquet of golden everlasting for a touch of color.
Many gardeners choose golden everlasting because its flowers retain their vibrancy when dried.
The golden everlasting thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and attracts butterflies.
