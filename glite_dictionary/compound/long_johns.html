long johns

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋ ˈdʒɑnz / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈdʒɒnz / (BrE)
Warm underwear with long legs, typically worn as a base layer in cold weather.
thermal underwear with long legs

Examples

In the harsh winter, I always wear long johns under my trousers to stay warm.
My grandmother bought me a new pair of long johns for our trip to the mountains.
When he forgot his long johns, the cold weather made hiking unbearable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermals - underwear designed to retain body heat
