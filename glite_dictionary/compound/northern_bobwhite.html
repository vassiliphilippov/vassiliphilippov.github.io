northern bobwhite

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðərn bɑbˌwaɪt / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈbɒbwaɪt / (BrE)
a North American quail, Colinus virginianus, widely found in the eastern and central United States and known as a popular game bird
a North American game bird (quail)

Examples

The hunter spotted a northern bobwhite hidden among the tall grasses.
Conservation efforts have helped increase the population of northern bobwhites in recent years.
We heard the distinctive call of a northern bobwhite during our morning hike in Tennessee.

Etymology

From 'bobwhite,' imitating the bird’s call, and 'northern' to distinguish its range.
