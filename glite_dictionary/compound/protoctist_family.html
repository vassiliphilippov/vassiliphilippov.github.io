protoctist family

1. NOUN

/ proʊˈtɒktɪst ˈfæməli / (AmE), / prəʊˈtɒktɪst ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
A taxonomic family within the kingdom Protoctista, comprising related groups of single-celled or simple multicellular organisms distinct from plants, animals, and fungi.
a family in the Protoctista kingdom

Examples

Scientists discovered a new protoctist family in the depths of the ocean.
This textbook provides detailed information about each protoctist family and their characteristics.
Classification within a protoctist family can be challenging due to diverse morphological features.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protoctist - an organism belonging to the kingdom Protoctista

Etymology

From 'protoctist', based on Greek 'prōtos' (first) and 'ktistos' (established); 'family' is from Latin 'familia'.
