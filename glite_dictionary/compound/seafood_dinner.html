seafood dinner

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːˌfʊd ˈdɪnər / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌfuːd ˈdɪnə / (BrE)
a meal consisting mainly of fish and shellfish, often served as a main course
meal with fish and shellfish

Examples

We celebrated our anniversary with a lavish seafood dinner at the new restaurant.
The hotel offers a special seafood dinner buffet every Friday night.
She cooked a delicious seafood dinner featuring lobster and shrimp for her guests.

Synset Examples

We had a fantastic seafood dinner in San Francisco.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seafood - edible fish and shellfish from the sea
  • Synonym: fish dinner - a dinner in which fish is the main course
