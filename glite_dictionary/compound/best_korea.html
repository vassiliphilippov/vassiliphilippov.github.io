Best Korea

1. PROPN

/ bɛst kəˈriːə / (AmE), / bɛst kəˈriːə / (BrE)
A colloquial or sarcastic reference to North Korea, often used humorously or ironically, especially in online contexts.
sarcastic name for North Korea
informal slang

Examples

Some online forums refer to North Korea as Best Korea with a touch of irony.
He said he would move to Best Korea if his team lost the championship.
Memes about Best Korea often mock the country's official propaganda.

Synset Examples

Best Korea has a unique culture and history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: North Korea - country in East Asia, officially the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
  • Antonym: South Korea - country in East Asia, officially the Republic of Korea; commonly contrasted with North Korea

Etymology

Originated as an internet meme, contrasting North Korea as the 'best' Korea to mock official propaganda and juxtapose it with South Korea.
