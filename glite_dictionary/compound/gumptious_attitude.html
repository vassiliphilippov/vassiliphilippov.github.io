gumptious attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌmpʃəs ætɪˌtjud / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmpʃəs ˈætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
a bold, energetic, and enterprising manner or outlook, especially in approaching challenges or opportunities
bold and enterprising approach

Examples

Her gumptious attitude helped her land the job despite fierce competition.
The team's gumptious attitude was evident as they tackled each obstacle with enthusiasm.
A gumptious attitude is essential for entrepreneurs facing unpredictable challenges.

Synset Examples

Her gumptious attitude helped her land the job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gumption - resourcefulness and initiative

Etymology

Formed from 'gumptious' (energetic, enterprising) + 'attitude'. 'Gumptious' is a derivative of 'gumption'.
