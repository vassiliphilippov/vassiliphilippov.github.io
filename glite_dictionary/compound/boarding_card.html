boarding card

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔrdɪŋ kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdɪŋ kɑːd / (BrE)
an official document or card that passengers must show before being allowed to board a plane or ship
official card for boarding a plane or ship

Examples

Please present your boarding card at the gate before entering the plane.
She realized she had left her boarding card at the security checkpoint.
You cannot proceed to the aircraft without a valid boarding card.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boarding pass - an official document or card for boarding a plane or ship
