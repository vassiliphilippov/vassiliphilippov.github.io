Tamil Nadu

1. PROPN

/ ˌtɑːmɪl ˈnɑdu / (AmE), / ˌtæmɪl ˈnɑːduː / (BrE)
a state in southeastern India, located on the Bay of Bengal, south of Andhra Pradesh; formerly known as Madras.
a state in southeastern India

Examples

We traveled across Tamil Nadu to experience its rich cultural heritage and historic temples.
The cuisine of Tamil Nadu is famous for its distinctive use of spices and rice dishes.
Chennai is the capital city of Tamil Nadu and an important economic center in India.
