worst patient

1. NOUN

/ wɝst ˈpeɪʃənt / (AmE), / wɜːst ˈpeɪʃənt / (BrE)
A patient who is especially difficult, noncompliant, or neglectful of their own health, often used humorously or critically to describe doctors who do not follow medical advice themselves.
difficult or noncompliant patient, esp. a doctor who neglects their own health

Examples

It's a common joke that doctors make the worst patient.
Even as a medical professional, she admitted to being the worst patient when it came to following advice.
You'd think he'd take care of himself, but as a doctor, he's sometimes the worst patient.

Synset Examples

It's a common joke that doctors make the worst patients.
He kept ignoring his symptoms because doctors make the worst patients.
I should know; doctors make the worst patients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noncompliant patient - a patient who does not follow medical advice
