pickle barrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪkəl ˌbærəl / (AmE), / ˈpɪkəl ˌbarəl / (BrE)
a large wooden barrel used to hold vinegar and cucumbers or other vegetables for pickling
barrel used for pickling vegetables

Examples

The store kept a large pickle barrel near the counter for customers who wanted a snack.
In the old days, vegetables were often preserved in a pickle barrel in the cellar.
She reached into the pickle barrel and pulled out a crisp, sour cucumber.
