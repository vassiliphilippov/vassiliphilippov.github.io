Turkoman language

1. NOUN

/ tɜːrˈkoʊmən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / tɜːˈkəʊmən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken by the Turkmen people, primarily in Turkmenistan and nearby regions
a Turkic language of Turkmenistan

Examples

She is fluent in both Russian and Turkoman language.
The Turkoman language shares features with other Turkic languages.
Many poems were originally written in the Turkoman language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Turkmen language - the official language of Turkmenistan, also known as Turkoman

Etymology

From Turkoman (variant of Turkmen) + language; Turkoman from Persian 'Turkmān'.
