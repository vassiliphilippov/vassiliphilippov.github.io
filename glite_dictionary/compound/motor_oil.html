motor oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊtər ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə ɔɪl / (BrE)
oil used to lubricate the moving parts of an internal combustion engine, especially in vehicles.
oil for engine lubrication

Examples

You should check the motor oil level every month to keep your car running smoothly.
The mechanic recommended using synthetic motor oil for better engine performance.
She spilled some motor oil on the driveway while changing it herself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: engine oil - oil used to lubricate the parts of an engine
