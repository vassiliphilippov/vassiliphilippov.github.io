discussion section

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈskʌʃən ˈsɛkʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈskʌʃən ˈsɛkʃən / (BrE)
a small group of students within a larger course who meet separately for discussion or instruction, usually led by a teaching assistant or graduate student
small class within a larger course for extra discussion

Examples

The discussion section meets twice a week to review the material covered in the main lecture.
Attending a discussion section helped her better understand difficult concepts in the course.
Students are required to choose a discussion section that fits their schedule.

Synset Examples

a graduate student taught sections for the professor's lecture course
