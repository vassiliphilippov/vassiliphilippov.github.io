home movie

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm ˈmuːvi / (AmE), / həʊm ˈmuːvi / (BrE)
a film made by an amateur, typically at home or during personal events, for private use rather than commercial release
amateur film made at home

Examples

My grandfather showed us an old home movie from his childhood days.
They enjoy making a home movie of every family holiday.
Watching the home movie brought back wonderful memories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: home video - video recording made by an amateur, usually at home or of family events
