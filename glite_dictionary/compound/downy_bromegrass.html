downy bromegrass

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaʊni ˈbroʊmˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈdaʊni ˈbrəʊmˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
an annual or winter annual grass of Mediterranean origin, with softly hairy leaves, widely considered a weed in many regions
a Mediterranean annual grass with soft, hairy leaves

Examples

Downy bromegrass spreads rapidly in overgrazed pastures and can outcompete native plants.
The softly hairy leaves are a distinctive feature of downy bromegrass.
Controlling downy bromegrass is important for maintaining healthy rangelands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheatgrass - a common name for downy bromegrass
