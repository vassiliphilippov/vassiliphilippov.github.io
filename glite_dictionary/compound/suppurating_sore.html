suppurating sore

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌpjəˌreɪtɪŋ sɔr / (AmE), / ˈsʌpjʊˌreɪtɪŋ sɔː / (BrE)
A sore that is inflamed and produces pus.
sore with pus formation
medical

Examples

The doctor examined the suppurating sore on his leg and prescribed antibiotics.
If left untreated, a minor cut can become a suppurating sore.
She was hospitalized due to a large suppurating sore that wouldn't heal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abscess - a swollen area of body tissue containing pus
  • Synonym: ulcer - an open sore on an external or internal surface of the body

Etymology

From Latin suppurare (to form or discharge pus), from sub- (under) + pus, puris (pus).
