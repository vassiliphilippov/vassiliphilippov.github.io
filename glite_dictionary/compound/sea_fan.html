sea fan

1. NOUN

/ si ˈfæn / (AmE), / siː ˈfan / (BrE)
A type of coral with a treelike or fan-shaped skeleton, commonly found in shallow tropical seas.
fan-shaped marine coral

Examples

Divers marveled at the beauty of the sea fan swaying gently with the current.
The scientist collected a sample of sea fan to study its structure.
Colorful fish weave through the branches of the sea fan on the reef.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coral - marine invertebrate with a hard skeleton
