corn-fed

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɔrnˌfɛd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌfɛd / (BrE)
(of livestock) raised on a diet consisting largely or entirely of corn, often resulting in a particular flavor or tenderness of meat.
livestock raised on a corn-based diet

Examples

The ranch produces only corn-fed beef for its customers.
He prefers the taste of corn-fed chicken over free-range varieties.
The advertisement boasted of their tender, juicy corn-fed pork chops.

Synset Examples

corn-fed livestock

Related Terms

  • Antonym: grass-fed - (of livestock) raised primarily on grass rather than grain
