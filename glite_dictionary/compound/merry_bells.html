merry bells

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛri bɛlz / (AmE), / ˈmɛri bɛlz / (BrE)
Any of several North American plants of the genus Uvularia, characterized by yellow, drooping, bell-shaped flowers.
North American plant with yellow bell-shaped flowers

Examples

The merry bells in the woodland bloomed brightly each spring.
She planted merry bells along the shady side of her garden for their delicate flowers.
Hikers admired the clusters of merry bells nestled among the ferns.
