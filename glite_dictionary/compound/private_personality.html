private personality

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪvət pɚˌsænəˈlɪti / (AmE), / ˈpraɪvət pɜːsəˈnælɪti / (BrE)
a tendency to keep personal thoughts, feelings, or matters to oneself and avoid sharing them with others
tendency to keep personal matters to oneself

Examples

She has a private personality and prefers to keep her thoughts to herself.
His private personality makes it hard for him to open up to new people.
As a result of her private personality, she rarely shares details about her family life.

Synset Examples

He has a very private personality and doesn't share much.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reserved - slow to reveal emotions or opinions
  • Antonym: outgoing personality - tending to be sociable and willing to share thoughts and feelings
