poke check

1. NOUN

/ poʊk tʃɛk / (AmE), / pəʊk tʃɛk / (BrE)
an act of knocking the puck away from an opponent by jabbing at it with the hockey stick, especially in ice hockey
hockey move: jabbing puck away

Examples

The defenseman used a quick poke check to steal the puck from the attacker.
She perfected her poke check to become an effective defender on the team.
A well-timed poke check can stop a breakaway in ice hockey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stick check - using the stick to impede an opponent's play in hockey
