mid-thirties

1. NOUN

/ mɪd ˈθɜr.tiz / (AmE), / mɪd ˈθɜː.tiz / (BrE)
the period of a person's life when they are aged between about 34 and 36
person's age between 34 and 36

Examples

She started her own business in her mid-thirties after working in finance for a decade.
By his mid-thirties, he had already traveled to over twenty countries.
Many people experience significant career changes in their mid-thirties.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: thirties - the decade of life from age 30 to 39
  • Degree Lower: early thirties - the first few years of one's thirties, typically 30-33
  • Degree Higher: late thirties - the latter years of one's thirties, typically 37-39
