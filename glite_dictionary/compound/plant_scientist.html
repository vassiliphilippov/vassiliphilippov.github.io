plant scientist

1. NOUN

/ plænt ˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / plɑːnt ˈsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
a biologist who specializes in the study of plants
specialist in plant biology

Examples

The plant scientist presented her latest research on drought-resistant crops at the conference.
As a plant scientist, he often works in greenhouses and laboratories to study plant growth.
Many plant scientists are involved in developing sustainable agricultural practices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: botanist - a scientist who studies plants
