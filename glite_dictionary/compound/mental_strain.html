mental strain

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.t̬əl streɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.təl streɪn / (BrE)
A state of nervousness or anxiety caused by prolonged mental effort or stress.
nervous tension from mental stress

Examples

After months of caring for his sick mother, he felt an overwhelming mental strain.
The mental strain of preparing for the final exam took a toll on her health.
He struggled to hide the mental strain caused by constant workplace pressure.

Synset Examples

his responsibilities were a constant strain
the mental strain of staying alert hour after hour was too much for him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental stress - psychological pressure caused by demanding circumstances
  • Degree Lower: mental fatigue - state of tiredness caused by prolonged mental activity
  • Degree Higher: mental breakdown - a state of severe mental distress or collapse
