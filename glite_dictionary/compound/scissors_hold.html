scissors hold

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪzərz hoʊld / (AmE), / ˈsɪzəz həʊld / (BrE)
A wrestling hold in which a wrestler wraps their legs around an opponent's body or head and squeezes by pressing the feet together.
wrestling hold using wrapped legs

Examples

She managed to break free from his scissors hold just in time.
The wrestler applied a tight scissors hold around his opponent's waist.
He won the match by trapping his rival in a scissors hold around the neck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: body scissors - a wrestling hold where the legs are wrapped around the opponent's body
