high-finished material

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈfɪnɪʃt məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / haɪ ˈfɪnɪʃt məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
a material that has been given a polished or highly refined surface finish
material with a polished or refined surface

Examples

The designer chose high-finished materials to create a sleek and modern look in the lobby.
Using high-finished material on the countertops makes the kitchen easy to clean and elegant.
Furniture made from high-finished materials can drastically improve the appearance of any space.

Synset Examples

The room was decorated with high-finished materials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polished material - material with a smooth, shiny surface
  • Antonym: unfinished material - material lacking a final surface finish
