middle age

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪdəl eɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.əl eɪdʒ / (BrE)
the period of life between young adulthood and old age, typically between about 40 and 60 years old
life stage between youth and old age

Examples

Many people begin to reflect on their achievements as they enter middle age.
She started a new career in middle age after raising her children.
Concerns about health often increase during middle age.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: old age - the period later in life following middle age
  • Degree Lower: youth - the period of life before middle age
