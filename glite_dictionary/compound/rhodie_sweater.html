Rhodie sweater

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊdi ˈswɛtɚ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdi ˈswɛtə / (BrE)
A sweater featuring symbols, logos, or other representations of Rhode Island, especially worn to show affiliation or support.
sweater representing Rhode Island

Examples

She proudly wore her Rhodie sweater to the game.
Every fall, new students purchase a Rhodie sweater from the campus store.
His Rhodie sweater had the university crest displayed on the front.
