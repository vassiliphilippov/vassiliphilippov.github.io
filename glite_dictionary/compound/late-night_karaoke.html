late-night karaoke

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪt ˌnaɪt kɛriˈoʊki / (AmE), / ˈleɪt ˌnaɪt kɑːrɪˈəʊki / (BrE)
karaoke sessions that take place late in the evening, typically after regular hours
karaoke held late in the evening

Examples

After midnight, the bar turns into a hotspot for late-night karaoke.
They stayed out for hours enjoying late-night karaoke with friends.
Nothing compares to the fun of late-night karaoke at your favorite club.

Synset Examples

Being a saloonatic, she loves late-night karaoke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: karaoke - a form of entertainment in which people sing along to recorded music using a microphone
