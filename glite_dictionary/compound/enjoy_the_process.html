to enjoy the process

1. VERB

/ ɪnˈdʒɔɪ ðə ˈproʊ.sɛs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɔɪ ðə ˈprəʊ.ses / (BrE)
to take pleasure in the experience or journey of doing something, rather than just focusing on the end result
take pleasure in the journey or experience

Examples

It's important to enjoy the process rather than stress about the final result.
She learned to enjoy the process of baking, not just the finished cake.
Athletes who enjoy the process of training often achieve better results in the long run.

Synset Examples

You need to enjoy the process; that's the thing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to enjoy - to take delight or pleasure in something
