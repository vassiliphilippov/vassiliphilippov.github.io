orchard apple tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrtʃərd ˈæpəl tri / (AmE), / ˈɔːtʃəd ˈæpl triː / (BrE)
A Eurasian tree (Malus domestica) widely cultivated for its firm, rounded, edible fruits, commonly known as apples.
tree cultivated for edible apples

Examples

The orchard apple tree is a staple in many European and American farms.
Each orchard apple tree in the garden blooms with white and pink flowers in spring.
Proper pruning helps the orchard apple tree produce more fruit each year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apple tree - tree producing apples
  • Degree Lower: crab apple tree - wild apple tree with small, tart fruit
