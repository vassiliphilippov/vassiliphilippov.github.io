stall bar

1. NOUN

/ stɔl bɑr / (AmE), / stɔːl bɑː / (BrE)
a gymnastic apparatus consisting of upright posts attached to a wall and connected by horizontal rungs, used for strengthening exercises
gym apparatus with horizontal rungs

Examples

The physiotherapist recommended exercises on the stall bar to improve flexibility.
Gymnastics classes often include stretching routines using the stall bar.
He gripped the top rung of the stall bar to start his shoulder exercises.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wall bars - another term for stall bar, especially in British English
