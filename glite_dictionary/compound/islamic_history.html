Islamic history

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈslɑːmɪk ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / ɪˈslæmɪk ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
the study of the historical development, events, and culture of Islamic civilization from its origins to the present day
study of the history of Islamic civilization

Examples

She specialized in Islamic history during her postgraduate studies.
The teacher introduced the class to important figures in Islamic history.
Books on Islamic history can provide insight into the development of science and art in the Muslim world.

Synset Examples

He studies Islamic history as part of his degree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Islamic civilization - the societies and cultures historically shaped by Islam
