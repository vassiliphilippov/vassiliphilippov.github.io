one side

1. NOUN

/ wʌn saɪd / (AmE), / wʌn saɪd / (BrE)
a particular viewpoint or position taken in a discussion, debate, or argument
a viewpoint in a discussion

Examples

He defended one side of the controversial issue with well-reasoned arguments.
It's important to listen to one side before judging the whole situation.
In every dispute, you should consider more than just one side of the story.

Synset Examples

He argued one side of the debate passionately.
You can't see one side without understanding the other.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: side - a position or viewpoint in a conflict or discussion
