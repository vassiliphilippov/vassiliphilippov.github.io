nip of winter

1. NOUN

/ nɪp əv ˈwɪntɚ / (AmE), / nɪp əv ˈwɪntə / (BrE)
A sharp, cold sensation characteristic of winter air.
sharp, cold feeling of winter

Examples

As he stepped outside, the nip of winter made him shiver.
You can already feel the nip of winter in the early morning air.
The nip of winter signaled that the holidays were not far off.

Synset Examples

He felt the chill in the nip of winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chill - a feeling of coldness in the air or atmosphere
