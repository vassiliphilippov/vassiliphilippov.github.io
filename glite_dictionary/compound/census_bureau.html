Census Bureau

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɛnsəs ˌbjuːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsəs ˌbjʊərəʊ / (BrE)
the government agency in the United States responsible for collecting and analyzing census data and demographic statistics
U.S. agency for census and demographics
american

Examples

The Census Bureau releases new population estimates every year.
Data from the Census Bureau is used to redraw congressional districts.
Many researchers rely on Census Bureau statistics for their studies.
