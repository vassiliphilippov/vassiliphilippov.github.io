ticket agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪkɪt ˌeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt ˌeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to sell tickets for events or travel, such as for theaters, concerts, trains, or airlines
person who sells tickets for events or travel

Examples

The ticket agent helped us find seats for the afternoon play.
She asked the ticket agent about available flights to Chicago.
A ticket agent was stationed at the entrance to sell tickets for the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sales clerk - person who sells goods or services in a retail environment
