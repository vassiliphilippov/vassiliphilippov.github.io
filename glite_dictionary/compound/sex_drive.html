sex drive

1. NOUN

/ sɛks draɪv / (AmE), / sɛks draɪv / (BrE)
a person's natural desire or urge for sexual activity.
desire for sexual activity

Examples

Testosterone plays a significant role in regulating the sex drive in men.
Some medications can cause a decrease in a person's sex drive.
After the treatment, she noticed her sex drive had returned to normal.

Synset Examples

testosterone is responsible for the male sex drive

Related Terms

  • Synonym: libido - sexual desire or drive
  • Synonym: sexual desire - a strong feeling of wanting sexual activity
  • Antonym: asexuality - lack of sexual attraction to others or low interest in sexual activity
