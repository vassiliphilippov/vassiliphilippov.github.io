funny phiz

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌni fɪz / (AmE), / ˈfʌni fɪz / (BrE)
a humorous or amusing facial expression, often made to provoke laughter
humorous facial expression
informal

Examples

He made a funny phiz at the children, who burst out laughing.
She wore a funny phiz during the meeting to lighten the mood.
His funny phiz was enough to make even the strictest teacher smile.

Synset Examples

He made a funny phiz, causing everyone to laugh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: face - the front part of a person's head

Etymology

'Phiz' is an informal shortening of 'physiognomy', originating in the 19th century.
