violation of privacy right

1. NOUN

/ vaɪˈleɪʃən əv ˈpraɪvəsi raɪt / (AmE), / vaɪəˈleɪʃən əv ˈprɪvəsi raɪt / (BrE)
an act of breaching an individual's legal right to privacy
breach of privacy rights
legal
