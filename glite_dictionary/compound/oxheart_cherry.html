oxheart cherry

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑksˌhɑrt ˈʧɛri / (AmE), / ˈɒkshɑːt ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
a type of sweet cherry with large, juicy, heart-shaped fruit, often cultivated for eating fresh
variety of sweet, heart-shaped cherry

Examples

The oxheart cherry is prized for its juicy, heart-shaped fruit in summer desserts.
She bought a basket of oxheart cherries at the farmers' market for making jam.
Gardeners often recommend the oxheart cherry for its sweet taste and attractive appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweet cherry - a species of cherry tree producing edible sweet fruit

Etymology

Named for its heart-like shape, resembling an ox's heart.
