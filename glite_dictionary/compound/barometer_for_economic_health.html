barometer for economic health

1. NOUN

/ bəˈrɑːmɪtər fər iːkəˈnɑːmɪk hɛlθ / (AmE), / bəˈrɒmɪtə fə ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪk hɛlθ / (BrE)
An indicator or measure of a country's economic conditions or financial wellbeing.
indicator of economic conditions

Examples

Analysts view consumer spending as a key barometer for economic health in the country.
Unemployment rates are often used as a barometer for economic health.
Housing starts can act as a barometer for economic health in the construction industry.

Synset Examples

The stock market often serves as a barometer for economic health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indicator - something that shows the state or level of something

Etymology

Barometer: from Greek 'baros' (weight) + '-meter' (measurer); metaphorical use in economics dates from the 19th century.
