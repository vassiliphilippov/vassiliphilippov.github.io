confirmed attendance

1. NOUN

/ kənˈfɝːmd ətˈɛndəns / (AmE), / kənˈfɜːmd əˈtɛndəns / (BrE)
an official acknowledgement that someone will be present at an event
acknowledged presence at an event

Examples

She received an email asking for confirmed attendance by Friday.
We require confirmed attendance to finalize the seating arrangements.
His confirmed attendance at the conference was appreciated by the organizers.

Synset Examples

The team confirmed attendance for the meeting. Copy that.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: RSVP - a request for a response to an invitation
