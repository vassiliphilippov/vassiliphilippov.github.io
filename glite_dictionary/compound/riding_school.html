riding school

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪdɪŋ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈraɪdɪŋ skuːl / (BrE)
A school where people are taught how to ride horses and improve their horsemanship skills.
school for teaching horseback riding

Examples

Sophie attends a riding school every weekend to improve her equestrian skills.
The city recently opened a new riding school for both children and adults.
Lessons at the riding school include grooming, saddling, and riding techniques.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: riding academy - a school where riding and horsemanship are taught
