aluminum foil

1. NOUN

/ əˈluː.mə.nəm ˌfɔɪl / (AmE), / ˌal.jʊˈmɪn.i.əm ˌfɔɪl / (BrE)
A thin sheet of aluminum metal used chiefly for wrapping food, cooking, and insulation.
thin sheet of aluminum for wrapping or cooking

Examples

Please cover the dish with aluminum foil before placing it in the oven.
She wrapped the leftover cake in aluminum foil to keep it fresh.
You can use aluminum foil to line the baking tray for easy cleanup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tin foil - thin foil originally made from tin, now often aluminum
