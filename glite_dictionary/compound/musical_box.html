musical box

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪkəl bɑks / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl bɒks / (BrE)
A mechanical device that plays music by means of pins on a revolving cylinder striking tuned metal teeth.
mechanical device that plays music

Examples

The old musical box played a familiar tune when she opened it.
He collects antique musical boxes from different centuries.
The melody from the musical box filled the room with nostalgia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: music box - American term for musical box, a mechanical device that plays music
