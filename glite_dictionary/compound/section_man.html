section man

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃən mæn / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃən man / (BrE)
a man who teaches a section of a large college course, typically leading smaller discussion or lab groups under a main instructor
male teacher of a college course section

Examples

The section man was responsible for leading weekly discussions for the biology course.
Each section man held office hours to help students with difficult concepts.
The students found their section man to be approachable and knowledgeable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teaching assistant - a person who assists a professor in teaching college classes, often leading sections
