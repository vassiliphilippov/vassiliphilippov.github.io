to rekindle friendship

1. VERB

/ riˈkɪndəl ˈfrɛndʃɪp / (AmE), / riˈkɪnd(ə)l ˈfrɛn(d)ʃɪp / (BrE)
to restore or renew a friendship after a period of distance or estrangement
restore a friendship

Examples

After years of not speaking, they decided to rekindle friendship at their high school reunion.
Social media made it easier for old classmates to rekindle friendship after losing touch.
Travelling together again helped them rekindle friendship that had faded over time.

Synset Examples

He tried to rekindle the romance with a surprise dinner.
They hope to rekindle their friendship after years apart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to reconcile - to restore friendly relations after a conflict
