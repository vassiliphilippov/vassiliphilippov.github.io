Book of Malachi

1. PROPN

/ ˈbʊk əv ˈmæləˌkaɪ / (AmE), / ˈbʊk əv ˈmæləkaɪ / (BrE)
the last book of the Old Testament, containing the prophecies attributed to the prophet Malachi
last book of the Old Testament

Examples

Many scholars have studied the Book of Malachi for its prophetic significance.
The Book of Malachi is often quoted in discussions of biblical prophecy.
She referenced the Book of Malachi during her lecture on the Old Testament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Old Testament - the first part of the Christian Bible, based on the Hebrew Scriptures
  • Synonym: Book of Isaiah - an Old Testament book containing the prophecies of Isaiah
  • Synonym: Book of Zechariah - an Old Testament book containing the prophecies of Zechariah

Etymology

Malachi from Hebrew Mal'ākhî meaning 'my messenger'.
