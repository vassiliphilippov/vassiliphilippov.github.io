Gulf of Finland

1. PROPN

/ ɡʌlf əv ˈfɪnlənd / (AmE), / ɡʌlf əv ˈfɪnlənd / (BrE)
the eastern part of the Baltic Sea, situated between southern Finland and northern Estonia.
eastern arm of the Baltic Sea between Finland and Estonia

Examples

Large cargo ships frequently pass through the Gulf of Finland on their way to St. Petersburg.
The coasts along the Gulf of Finland are popular destinations for tourists during the summer.
Environmental issues in the Gulf of Finland have been the subject of international cooperation.
