face for television

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪs fɔɚ ˌtɛləˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈfeɪs fə ˌtɛlɪˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
a face that is considered attractive or appealing enough to appear on television
attractive appearance suitable for TV

Examples

Despite his young age, he already has a face for television and a charming personality.
The casting director said she was searching for someone with a face for television.
People often comment that he has a face for television thanks to his dazzling smile.

Synset Examples

With her charm, she definitely has a face for television.
Her confidence shines; she really has a face for television.
He's got a face for television and a voice for radio.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good-looking - physically attractive
  • Antonym: face for radio - an unattractive face, often said humorously
