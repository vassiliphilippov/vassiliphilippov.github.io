caper sauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪpər sɔs / (AmE), / ˈkeɪpə sɔːs / (BrE)
A rich white sauce made with stock and thickened with egg yolks or cream, flavored with capers.
white sauce with capers

Examples

He served the steamed vegetables with a generous portion of caper sauce on the side.
The fish was perfectly complemented by a tangy caper sauce.
She learned to prepare caper sauce during her culinary training in France.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: allemande sauce - a rich white sauce thickened with egg yolks or cream
