island dispenser

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.lənd dɪˈspɛns.ər / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.lənd dɪˈspens.ə / (BrE)
a fuel pump at a service station that delivers gasoline from underground storage tanks
fuel pump at a gas station

Examples

The mechanic checked the island dispenser to ensure it was pumping gasoline efficiently.
Each island dispenser at the station is regularly inspected for safety compliance.
A new island dispenser was installed to accommodate more customers during peak hours.

Related Terms
