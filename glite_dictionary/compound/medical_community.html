medical community

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl kəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
the group of people who are qualified and work as doctors, nurses, and other healthcare professionals
all qualified medical professionals as a group

Examples

The medical community is working hard to develop new treatments for rare diseases.
Members of the medical community voiced their concerns about public health funding cuts.
Advances in technology are rapidly changing the way the medical community provides care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: healthcare professionals - persons who work in the various fields of healthcare
