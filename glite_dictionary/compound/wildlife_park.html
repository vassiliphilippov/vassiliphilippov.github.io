wildlife park

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪldlaɪf pɑrk / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldlaɪf pɑːk / (BrE)
a park where wild animals are kept in naturalistic conditions for people to observe them
park for observing wild animals

Examples

We visited a wildlife park and saw lions roaming in large enclosures.
Many endangered species are protected in the new wildlife park near the city.
The children enjoyed a guided tour of the wildlife park during their school trip.

Synset Examples

The wildlife park is home to many exotic animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zoo - an establishment where wild animals are kept for exhibition
