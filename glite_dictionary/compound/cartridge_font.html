cartridge font

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːrtrɪdʒ fɑnt / (AmE), / ˈkɑːtrɪdʒ fɒnt / (BrE)
a font stored in a cartridge that can be inserted into a computer printer to provide additional typefaces
font stored in a printer cartridge

Examples

The old printer only supports a few cartridge fonts for special documents.
Upgrading your cartridge font lets you print in new styles without changing the software.
She ordered a new cartridge font to expand her design options.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: font - a set of type of one particular face and size
