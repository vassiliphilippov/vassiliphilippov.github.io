military unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a group that forms part of a country's armed forces, organized for a particular purpose or operation
group within the armed forces

Examples

The military unit was deployed overseas for a peacekeeping mission.
Each military unit has its own command structure and operational duties.
The general inspected the military unit before the start of the exercise.

Synset Examples
