field sparrow

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːld ˌspæroʊ / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌspærəʊ / (BrE)
a small North American finch (Spizella pusilla) found in brushy pastures and open fields
North American finch species

Examples

A field sparrow landed quietly on the fence post, surveying the overgrown pasture.
During spring, the sweet song of the field sparrow can be heard in rural meadows across the region.
The distinctive pink bill of the field sparrow helps birdwatchers distinguish it from other similar finches.
