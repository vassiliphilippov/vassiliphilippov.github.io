ball boy

1. NOUN

/ bɔl bɔɪ / (AmE), / bɔːl bɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy whose job is to retrieve balls for players during a tennis match or similar sporting event
boy who retrieves balls in sports matches

Examples

During the tournament, the ball boy quickly retrieved stray balls from the court.
The ball boy handed a new ball to the player before she served.
Becoming a ball boy at Wimbledon is a dream for many young tennis fans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ball girl - a girl who retrieves balls for tennis players
