in particular

1. ADV

/ ɪn pərˈtɪkjələr / (AmE), / ɪn pəˈtɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
especially or specifically, as distinct from others in the group mentioned.
especially, specifically distinguished

Examples

She enjoys classical music, and in particular loves the works of Mozart.
I found the whole exhibition interesting, but one painting in particular caught my eye.
The teacher praised the students, highlighting Sarah in particular for her improvement.

Synset Examples

loves Bach, particularly his partitas
recommended one book in particular
trace major population movements for the Pueblo groups in particular

Related Terms

  • Synonym: especially - particularly; to a distinctly greater extent
  • Synonym: specifically - in a detailed and exact way; particularly
