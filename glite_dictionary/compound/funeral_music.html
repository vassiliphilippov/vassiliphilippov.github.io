funeral music

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuː.nɚ.əl ˈmjuː.zɪk / (AmE), / ˈfjuː.nər.əl ˈmjuː.zɪk / (BrE)
music played during a funeral ceremony, typically solemn in nature
music played at a funeral

Examples

The church organist played solemn funeral music as mourners entered the chapel.
They selected traditional funeral music to honor their grandmother's memory.
Soft funeral music filled the room, adding to the somber atmosphere of the service.

Synset Examples

They chose funerial music for the sad event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: requiem - a Mass or musical composition for the dead
  • Synonym: dirge - a mournful song or piece of music for funerals
