to utter nonsense

1. VERB

/ ˈʌtər ˈnɑnsəns / (AmE), / ˈʌtə ˈnɒnsəns / (BrE)
to say something completely false or senseless
say something completely senseless

Examples

He tends to utter nonsense when he is nervous.
Please don't utter nonsense during such a serious discussion.
She realized she had uttered nonsense after seeing everyone's reaction.

Synset Examples

You can't utter such nonsense in this meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talk rubbish - to speak nonsense; say things that are not true or sensible
  • Antonym: to make sense - to say or do something reasonable or logical
