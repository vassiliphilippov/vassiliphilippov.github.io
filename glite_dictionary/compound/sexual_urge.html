sexual urge

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl ɝrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl ɜːdʒ / (BrE)
a strong desire or impulse to engage in sexual activity
desire to engage in sexual activity

Examples

Adolescents often experience a strong sexual urge as they go through puberty.
He struggled to control his sexual urge in certain situations.
Some medications can reduce a person's sexual urge as a side effect.

Synset Examples

he wanted a better sex life
the film contained no sex or violence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: libido - sexual desire or drive
  • Synonym: sexual desire - desire to have sexual relations
