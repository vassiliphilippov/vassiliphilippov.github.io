putting green

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌtɪŋ ɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈpʌtɪŋ ɡriːn / (BrE)
an area of finely cut grass on a golf course, surrounding a hole, where players make their final strokes to complete a hole
golf course area around a hole

Examples

After hours of practice, she could read every break on the putting green.
The tournament was delayed because rain flooded the putting green.
Golfers gathered around the putting green to watch the playoff putts.

Synset Examples

the ball rolled across the green and into the bunker

Related Terms

  • Synonym: green - an area of short grass on a golf course, especially around the hole
