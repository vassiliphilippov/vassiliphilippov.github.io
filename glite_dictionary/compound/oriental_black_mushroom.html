Oriental black mushroom

1. NOUN

/ ɔːˈriːəntəl blæk ˈmʌʃruːm / (AmE), / ˌɒr.iˈen.təl blæk ˈmʌʃ.rʊm / (BrE)
An edible East Asian mushroom with a dark brown or blackish cap and an inedible stem, used in cooking.
edible East Asian mushroom with a dark cap

Examples

The recipe calls for dried Oriental black mushroom to add depth of flavor.
Fresh Oriental black mushroom can be found at many Asian markets.
She sautéed sliced Oriental black mushroom with garlic and soy sauce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shiitake - East Asian mushroom, often used in cooking
