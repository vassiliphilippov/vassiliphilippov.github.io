peculiar institution

1. NOUN

/ pɪˈkjuljɚ ˌɪnstəˈtuʃən / (AmE), / pɪˈkjuːliə ˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃən / (BrE)
a historical euphemism for slavery, especially as it existed in the southern United States before the Civil War
euphemism for slavery in the U.S.
