order Uredinales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər jʊˌrɛdəˈneɪliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə jʊəˌrɛdɪˈneɪliːz / (BrE)
a taxonomic group of parasitic fungi known as rust fungi, which cause rust diseases in plants
taxonomic order of rust fungi
medical

Examples

Many crops can be threatened by order Uredinales infections, leading to significant agricultural losses.
Scientists study members of the order Uredinales to develop better methods of disease control.
The distinctive orange spores are characteristic of fungi classified within order Uredinales.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rust fungus - a fungus that causes rust disease in plants
  • Synonym: order Pucciniales - updated name for the same taxonomic group of rust fungi

Etymology

From New Latin Uredinales, based on Latin 'uredo' (a kind of plant disease) plus the taxonomic suffix '-ales.'
