religious holiday

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈhɑːlɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈhɒlɪdeɪ / (BrE)
a day set aside for religious observance and celebration
day for religious observance

Examples

Christmas is a religious holiday celebrated by Christians around the world.
Ramadan is an important religious holiday in the Muslim calendar.
Many businesses close for major religious holidays to allow observance by their employees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holiday - a day of festivity or recreation when no work is done
