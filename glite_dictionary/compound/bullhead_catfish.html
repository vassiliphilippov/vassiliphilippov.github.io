bullhead catfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlˌhɛd ˈkætˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌhɛd ˈkatˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a common name for several species of small, stout North American freshwater catfish of the genus Ameiurus, characterized by a broad, flat head and barbels around the mouth
a type of North American freshwater catfish

Examples

The angler proudly displayed the bullhead catfish he caught in the local river.
Many lakes in the Midwest are home to abundant populations of bullhead catfish.
She learned to distinguish a bullhead catfish from other species by its rounded, broad head.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catfish - a type of freshwater fish with whisker-like barbels around the mouth
