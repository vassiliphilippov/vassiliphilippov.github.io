thirteenth grade

1. NOUN

/ θɚˈtinθ ɡreɪd / (AmE), / θɜːˈtiːnθ ɡreɪd / (BrE)
an informal term for an additional year of high school or the experience of remaining at a high school level, either literally or metaphorically
informal extra year of high school
informal american

Examples

After failing several classes, he ended up spending a thirteenth grade at his high school.
The community college was jokingly referred to as 'thirteenth grade' by recent graduates.
Some students feel like they're in thirteenth grade because they're not ready to move on.

Synset Examples

Some say high school feels like a thirteenth grade.
He joked about being in thirteenth grade for too long.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twelfth grade - final year of high school in the U.S.
