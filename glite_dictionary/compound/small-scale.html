small-scale

1. ADJ

/ smɔl skeɪl / (AmE), / smɔːl skeɪl / (BrE)
Created, made, or existing on a small scale; showing or representing something at a reduced size or extent.
made or shown at a small scale

Examples

The architect presented a small-scale model of the proposed building.
Our company started as a small-scale operation before expanding nationwide.
She used a small-scale map to plan her hiking route.

Synset Examples

small-scale maps
a small-scale model

Related Terms

  • Antonym: large-scale - made or shown at a large scale
