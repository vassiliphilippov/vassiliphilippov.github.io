legal beagle

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːɡəl ˈbiːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl ˈbiːɡəl / (BrE)
an informal term for a skilled lawyer or legal expert
skilled lawyer; legal expert (informal)
informal

Examples

After years of experience in court, she became known as the office legal beagle.
As a legal beagle, he quickly spotted flaws in the argument.
The startup consulted a legal beagle before finalizing the contract.

Synset Examples

She’s a real legal beagle when it comes to contracts.
The firm hired a legal beagle to handle the case.
He's known as a legal beagle in our circles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lawyer - a person who practices or studies law; an attorney

Etymology

Wordplay based on the word 'legal' and the dog breed 'beagle', suggesting alertness and skill in legal matters.
