rich history

1. NOUN

/ rɪtʃ ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / rɪtʃ ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
a history characterized by many notable events, depth, and significance, often reflecting cultural or historical wealth
notable and significant historical background

She spent years researching her country's rich history for her new book.
