tacnuke strategy

1. NOUN

/ tæk.nuːk ˈstræt.ə.dʒi / (AmE), / tæk.njuːk ˈstræt.ɪ.dʒi / (BrE)
a gaming strategy that relies on the use of tactical nuclear weapons or their in-game equivalents to achieve victory
gaming tactic involving tactical nukes

Examples

Her tacnuke strategy caught her opponents off guard in the final round.
Mastering the tacnuke strategy can turn the tide of a difficult game.
Some players consider the tacnuke strategy risky but highly rewarding.

Synset Examples

She executed a perfect tacnuke strategy to win.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rush strategy - an aggressive gaming tactic aiming for early victory

Etymology

Blend of 'tacnuke' (short for 'tactical nuclear weapon') and 'strategy'; 'tacnuke' from 'tactical' and 'nuke'.
