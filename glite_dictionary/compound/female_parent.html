female parent

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːmeɪl ˈpɛrənt / (AmE), / ˈfiːmeɪl ˈpeərənt / (BrE)
a woman who has given birth to or brings up a child; a mother
a woman who is a parent; a mother

Examples

My female parent works at the local hospital as a nurse.
He introduced me to his female parent at the party last night.
Every female parent receives a special gift on Mother's Day.

Synset Examples

the mother of three children

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mother - a woman in relation to her child or children
  • Antonym: male parent - a man who has fathered a child; a father
