ticket office

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪkɪt ˌɑːfɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt ˌɒfɪs / (BrE)
a place or office where tickets for events, travel, or admission are sold to the public
place where tickets are sold

Examples

We stood in line at the ticket office for over half an hour before getting our tickets.
The ticket office closes promptly at 6 p.m., so make sure to arrive early.
You can pick up your reserved seats at the ticket office near the entrance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: box office - office where tickets to performances are sold
