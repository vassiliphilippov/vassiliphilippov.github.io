ultranerdy T-shirt

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌltrəˈnɜːrdi ˈti ˌʃɜrt / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈnɜːdi ˈtiː ˌʃɜːt / (BrE)
A T-shirt that shows extreme enthusiasm for or identification with nerd culture, often featuring references to video games, science fiction, or technology.
T-shirt with extreme nerd-culture themes
informal

Examples

She wore an ultranerdy T-shirt covered in references to classic sci-fi movies.
His closet is filled with ultranerdy T-shirts displaying mathematical jokes and programming puns.
That ultranerdy T-shirt with a pixelated robot made everyone at the comic con smile.

Synset Examples

She wore an ultranerdy T-shirt featuring her favorite video game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geeky T-shirt - A T-shirt associated with geek or nerd culture
