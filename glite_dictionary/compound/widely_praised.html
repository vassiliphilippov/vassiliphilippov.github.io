widely praised

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪdli preɪzd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪdli preɪzd / (BrE)
Having received much positive recognition or approval from many people.
receiving much public praise

Examples

The novel became widely praised after winning the national literary award.
Her performance in the play was widely praised by both audiences and critics.
The new policy has been widely praised for its positive impact on the community.

Synset Examples

The movie was widely praised by critics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acclaimed - publicly praised or celebrated
  • Antonym: criticized - expressing disapproval or finding fault with
