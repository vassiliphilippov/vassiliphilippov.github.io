tape recording

1. NOUN

/ teɪp rɪˈkɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / teɪp rɪˈkɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
A recording of sound or images made on magnetic tape.
recording made on magnetic tape

Examples

She listened to the old tape recording of her grandfather's voice.
The interview was saved as a tape recording for future reference.
Many music studios used to produce tape recordings before the digital era.

Synset Examples

the several recordings were combined on a master tape

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recording - a sound or visual reproduction stored for later playback
