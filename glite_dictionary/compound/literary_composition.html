literary composition

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬əˌrɛri ˌkɑmpəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈlɪtərəri ˌkɒmpəˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
a piece of writing that is imaginative or creative in nature, such as a novel, poem, or essay
imaginative or creative writing piece

Examples

Her most famous literary composition is a novel about life during the war.
The student submitted a short literary composition for her English class.
He admired the beauty and depth of her literary composition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: work of literature - a written work considered to have artistic or intellectual value
