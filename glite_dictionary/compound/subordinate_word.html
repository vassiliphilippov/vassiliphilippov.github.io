subordinate word

1. NOUN

/ səˈbɔrdɪnət wɝd / (AmE), / səˈbɔːdɪnət wɜːd / (BrE)
A word that denotes a more specific concept than another, broader word (a superordinate) within a hierarchy.
word more specific than another; linguistic term

Examples

"Poodle" is a subordinate word of "dog" because it is more specific.
In the hierarchy of terms, "rose" is a subordinate word under "flower".
Linguists often discuss how subordinate words relate to more general categories.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: superordinate word - a word that is more general than another; the broader term in a hierarchy
  • Synonym: hyponym - a word whose meaning is included within that of another word
