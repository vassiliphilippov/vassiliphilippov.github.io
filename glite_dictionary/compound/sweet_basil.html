sweet basil

1. NOUN

/ swit ˈbeɪzəl / (AmE), / swiːt ˈbæzəl / (BrE)
A fragrant herb with small white flowers, commonly used in cooking for its aromatic leaves.
aromatic culinary herb

Examples

Fresh sweet basil adds a vibrant flavor to tomato sauces and salads.
She grows sweet basil in her herb garden for cooking.
The recipe calls for a handful of chopped sweet basil leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basil - a culinary herb; the general term for the genus Ocimum, especially Ocimum basilicum

2. NOUN

/ swit ˈbeɪzəl / (AmE), / swiːt ˈbæzɪl / (BrE)
an aromatic herb with broad green leaves, used fresh or dried to flavor food, especially in Mediterranean and Asian cuisines
aromatic herb used in cooking

Examples

She sprinkled chopped sweet basil over the tomato salad before serving.
Fresh sweet basil gives pesto its distinctive flavor and aroma.
The recipe calls for a handful of dried sweet basil leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basil - an aromatic herb used for seasoning
