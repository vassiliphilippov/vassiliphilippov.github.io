special duty allowance

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛʃəl ˈduːti əˈlaʊəns / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəl ˈdjuːti əˈlaʊəns / (BrE)
an extra payment made to employees assigned special responsibilities or additional tasks beyond their normal duties
extra payment for special work duties

Examples

The company introduced a special duty allowance for employees working on high-priority projects.
Staff members who take on extra administrative roles are eligible for a special duty allowance.
To compensate for additional responsibilities, a special duty allowance was included in his monthly salary.
