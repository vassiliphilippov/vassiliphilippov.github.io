cat distribution system

1. NOUN

/ kæt dɪˌstrɪbjuˈʃən ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / kæt dɪsˌtrɪbjuːˈʃən ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
an organized method or system for managing stray cats and arranging their adoption into homes
system for managing and adopting stray cats

Examples

The new cat distribution system in our neighborhood has helped dozens of stray cats find homes.
Volunteers manage the cat distribution system to ensure every rescued cat is properly cared for and adopted.
After implementing a cat distribution system, the city saw a significant decrease in stray cat populations.

Synset Examples

The city’s cat distribution system ensures all strays are cared for.
They implemented a cat distribution system to handle adoptions.
