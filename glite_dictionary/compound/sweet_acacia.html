sweet acacia

1. NOUN

/ swit əˈkeɪʃə / (AmE), / swiːt əˈkeɪsiə / (BrE)
A tropical American shrub or small tree with thorny branches and fragrant yellow flowers, used in perfumery.
tropical shrub with fragrant yellow flowers

Examples

The sweet acacia bloomed early this year, filling the garden with its delicate scent.
Perfume makers value the fragrance of sweet acacia flowers for its rich, sweet aroma.
A row of sweet acacia trees lines the driveway, providing shade and bursts of yellow color.
