boat deck

1. NOUN

/ boʊt dɛk / (AmE), / bəʊt dɛk / (BrE)
the upper deck of a ship where lifeboats are stored and from which they may be launched
upper ship deck for lifeboats

Examples

Passengers assembled on the boat deck when the emergency drill alarm sounded.
The boat deck offered a clear view of the surrounding sea and sky.
Crews inspected the lifeboats regularly on the boat deck to ensure they were ready for emergencies.
