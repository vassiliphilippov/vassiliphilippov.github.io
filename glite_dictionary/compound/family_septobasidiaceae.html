family Septobasidiaceae

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛptoʊˌbæsɪdiəˈsiːiː / (AmE), / ˌsɛptə(ʊ)ˌbæsɪdɪəˈsiːiː / (BrE)
A family of fungi in the subdivision Basidiomycota, including species that often form associations with scale insects.
a family of Basidiomycota fungi
medical

Examples

The family Septobasidiaceae includes fungi that commonly form symbiotic relationships with scale insects.
Researchers discovered a new species within the family Septobasidiaceae on the leaves of tropical plants.
Fungi in the family Septobasidiaceae can have significant ecological effects on their plant hosts.

Etymology

From the genus name Septobasidium, type genus of the family, and Latin 'aceae' denoting a family.
