United States Supreme Court

1. PROPN

/ ˌjuːnəˌteɪtɪd ˌsteɪts səˈpriːm kɔrt / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪtɪd ˌsteɪts səˈpriːm kɔːt / (BrE)
the highest federal court in the United States, with ultimate appellate authority and jurisdiction over all other courts in the country
the highest federal court in the US

Examples

The United States Supreme Court handed down a landmark decision on civil rights yesterday.
Several controversial cases are expected to reach the United States Supreme Court this year.
The first woman appointed to the United States Supreme Court was Sandra Day O'Connor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Supreme Court - the highest court in a country or state
