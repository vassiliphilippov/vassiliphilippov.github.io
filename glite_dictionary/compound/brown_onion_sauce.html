brown onion sauce

1. NOUN

/ braʊn ˈʌnjən sɔs / (AmE), / braʊn ˈʌnjən sɔːs / (BrE)
A brown sauce made with sautéed chopped onions, parsley, and dry white wine or vinegar, commonly served with meats or vegetables.
brown sauce with onions and wine or vinegar

Examples

She served the roast beef with a rich brown onion sauce on the side.
A classic brown onion sauce adds depth to stews and grilled meats.
The chef recommended pairing lamb chops with brown onion sauce and fresh herbs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: onion sauce - sauce made primarily with onions
