spotted ray

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑt̬ɪd ˌreɪ / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd ˌreɪ / (BrE)
A species of ray with a back covered in white or yellow spots, found widely in warm seas.
a ray with spotted back

Examples

Divers often spot a spotted ray gliding gracefully over sandy sea floors in tropical regions.
The aquarium's new exhibit features a spotted ray with striking yellow patterns on its back.
A spotted ray can be identified by the distinctive white spots scattered across its dark skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ray - a type of cartilaginous fish with a flattened body and long tail
