cultivated crab apple

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌl.təˌveɪ.tɪd kræb ˈæp.əl / (AmE), / ˈkʌl.tɪ.veɪ.tɪd kræb ˈæp.əl / (BrE)
A variety of crab apple tree grown for its small, tart fruit, often used in preserves, or for its attractive blossoms as an ornamental plant.
variety of crab apple grown for fruit or blossoms

Examples

The cultivated crab apple is prized both for its vibrant spring blossoms and for its fruit, which is perfect for making jelly.
Many gardeners plant cultivated crab apples to add color and interest to their landscapes.
She harvested a basket of cultivated crab apples to make a batch of homemade preserves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab apple - wild or cultivated small, tart apple tree or its fruit
