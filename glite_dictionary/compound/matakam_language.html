Matakam language

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɑːtəˈkɑːm ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌmatəˈkam ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in the Far North Region of Cameroon, mainly south of Lake Chad
a Chadic language of Cameroon

Examples

The Matakam language is primarily spoken in the mountainous regions south of Lake Chad.
Researchers have documented several dialects within the Matakam language.
He is fluent in both French and the Matakam language.
