spicy veggie momo

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪsi ˈvɛdʒi ˈmoʊmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪsi ˈvɛdʒi ˈməʊməʊ / (BrE)
A type of dumpling filled with spiced vegetables, often steamed or fried and commonly eaten in South Asian cuisine.
spiced vegetable dumpling

Examples

She ordered a plate of spicy veggie momo at the new Tibetan restaurant downtown.
The spicy veggie momo was served hot with a side of tangy dipping sauce.
My favorite street food snack is definitely spicy veggie momo stuffed with cabbage and carrots.

Synset Examples

At the party, we all enjoyed spicy veggie momo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dumpling - small mass of dough with filling, cooked by steaming or frying

Etymology

Momo is from Tibetan and Nepalese languages, referring to a dumpling; 'veggie' an informal shortening of 'vegetable,' 'spicy' denotes seasoning.
