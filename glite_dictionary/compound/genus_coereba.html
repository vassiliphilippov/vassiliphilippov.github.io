genus Coereba

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs koʊˈɛrəbə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs kəʊˈɛrɪbə / (BrE)
the type genus of the bird family Coerebidae, comprising species such as the Bananaquit.
type genus of Coerebidae (birds)

Examples

The genus Coereba is best known for the Bananaquit, a small, active songbird.
Ornithologists have debated the classification of the genus Coereba within the tanager family.
Species within the genus Coereba are found primarily in the tropical Americas.

Etymology

From Latin 'genus' (kind, type) and modern Latin 'Coereba', the genus name.
