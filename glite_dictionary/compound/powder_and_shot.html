powder and shot

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊdɚ ənd ʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə ənd ʃɒt / (BrE)
ammunition consisting of gunpowder and bullets, especially as used for muskets in historical contexts
historical ammunition for muskets

Examples

The soldiers ran low on powder and shot before the battle ended.
He kept a supply of powder and shot in a leather pouch by his side.
Without enough powder and shot, the militia was forced to retreat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ammunition - supply of bullets and shells used in guns
