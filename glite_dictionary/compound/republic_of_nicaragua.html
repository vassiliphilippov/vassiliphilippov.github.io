Republic of Nicaragua

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌnɪkəˈrɑːɡwə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌnɪkəˈræɡwə / (BrE)
a country in Central America, bordered by Honduras and Costa Rica, that gained independence from Spain in 1821
country in Central America

Examples

The Republic of Nicaragua is known for its beautiful lakes and volcanoes.
Trade agreements between the United States and the Republic of Nicaragua have increased in recent years.
After traveling through Central America, she finally arrived in the Republic of Nicaragua.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Nicaragua', the name of an indigenous people and their territory.
