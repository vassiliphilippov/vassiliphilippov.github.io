ab initio

1. ADV

/ æb ɪˈnɪʃioʊ / (AmE), / æb ɪˈnɪʃɪəʊ / (BrE)
from the beginning; starting from the origin or outset.
from the beginning

Examples

The contract was declared void ab initio by the court.
All calculations in the study were performed ab initio.
The error was present ab initio, affecting the entire experiment.

Synset Examples

at first he didn't notice anything strange

Etymology

From Latin 'ab initio' meaning 'from the beginning'.
