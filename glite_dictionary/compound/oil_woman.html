oil woman

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who owns or operates oil wells, especially as a business
female oil well owner or operator

Examples

The new CEO of the energy company is a prominent oil woman with decades of experience.
She became an oil woman after inheriting several wells from her family.
As an oil woman, she advocates for more sustainable drilling practices in the industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oilman - a man who owns or operates oil wells
