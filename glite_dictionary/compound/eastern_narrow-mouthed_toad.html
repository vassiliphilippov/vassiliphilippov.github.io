eastern narrow-mouthed toad

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːstərn ˈnæroʊ maʊθt toʊd / (AmE), / ˈiːstən ˈnærəʊ maʊθt təʊd / (BrE)
A small species of toad native to the southeastern United States, known for its narrow mouth.
small toad of the southeastern U.S.

Examples

We spotted an eastern narrow-mouthed toad in the leaf litter near the creek.
The eastern narrow-mouthed toad is known for its distinctive call during mating season.
Herpetologists are studying the habitat preferences of the eastern narrow-mouthed toad.
