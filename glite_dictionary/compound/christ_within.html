Christ Within

1. NOUN

/ kraɪst wɪˈðɪn / (AmE), / kraɪst wɪˈðɪn / (BrE)
the divine presence believed by Quakers and some Christians to dwell within each person, providing spiritual guidance and enlightenment
the indwelling divine presence in Quaker belief

Examples

Quaker teachings emphasize the importance of listening to the Christ Within for personal spiritual guidance.
She found strength in her belief in the Christ Within during times of hardship.
Many people interpret the Christ Within as a call to seek truth through inner reflection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Inner Light - the spiritual illumination believed by Quakers to exist in every person
