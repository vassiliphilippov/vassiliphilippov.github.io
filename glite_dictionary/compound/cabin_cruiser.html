cabin cruiser

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæbɪn ˌkruːzɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæbɪn ˌkruːzə / (BrE)
a large motorboat with a cabin, plumbing, and other facilities suitable for living on board
large motorboat with living facilities

Examples

They spent the summer traveling up the coast in their cabin cruiser.
The new cabin cruiser has enough space for six people to sleep comfortably.
He dreamed of retiring and living aboard a cabin cruiser in the Caribbean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yacht - a large, often luxurious boat for recreation or racing
