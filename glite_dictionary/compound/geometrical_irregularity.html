geometrical irregularity

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪkəl ɪˌrɛɡjəˈlærɪti / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪkəl ɪˌrɛɡjʊˈlarɪti / (BrE)
an uneven or unevenly distributed shape or spatial pattern; a lack of symmetry in geometry
uneven or asymmetrical shape or pattern

Examples

The ancient vase was notable for the geometrical irregularity of its markings and shape.
In architecture, geometrical irregularity can add a unique visual interest to a building.
A diamond's value may decrease if it has any significant geometrical irregularity in its cut.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: symmetry - balanced and regular arrangement of parts
