fart-breath

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑrtˌbrɛθ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːtˌbrɛθ / (BrE)
Breath that smells extremely unpleasant, often likened humorously or insultingly to the odor of flatulence.
very unpleasant-smelling breath (slang, insult)
slang vulgar

Examples

His fart-breath was so overpowering that it made everyone open a window.
She covered her mouth, worried her fart-breath would offend her date.
Kids teased him on the playground by calling him fart-breath after lunch.

Synset Examples

Her fart-breath made everyone at the table giggle.
After lunch, he had that unmistakable fart-breath.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: halitosis - chronic bad breath (medical)
  • Synonym: bad breath - unpleasant-smelling breath
