top drawer

1. NOUN

/ tɑp ˈdrɔr / (AmE), / tɒp ˈdrɔː / (BrE)
Of the highest quality or status; also, the uppermost drawer in a piece of furniture.
highest quality; uppermost drawer

Examples

Only top drawer wines are served at this exclusive restaurant.
His performance at the audition was truly top drawer.
She found the missing passport in the top drawer of her dresser.

Synset Examples

She always keeps her best clothes in the top drawer.
Only top drawer items are displayed in the store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first-class - of the best quality or highest standard
  • Antonym: second-rate - of inferior or lesser quality
