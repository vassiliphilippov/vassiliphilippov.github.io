24/7

1. NOUN

/ twɛnti fɔr ˈsɛvən / (AmE), / twɛnti fɔː ˈsɛvən / (BrE)
Available or operating twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week; continuously without interruption.
available all the time, nonstop

Examples

The customer support line is open 24/7 for your convenience.
Many hospitals operate 24/7 to provide emergency care at any time.
She works at a 24/7 gym that never closes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: round-the-clock - available or happening all day and night without stopping
  • Synonym: nonstop - without interruption or pause
