contour map

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑnˌtʊr mæp / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tʊə ˌmæp / (BrE)
a map that shows the elevation and shape of the land using contour lines connecting points of equal height above sea level
map showing land elevation with contour lines

Examples

The hiker used a contour map to navigate the mountainous terrain.
Students learned how to read a contour map in their geography class.
The contour map clearly indicated the steepness of the slopes in the valley.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: topographic map - a map showing natural and man-made features of an area, including elevation
