Ordnance Survey

1. PROPN

/ ˈɔrdnəns ˈsɝːveɪ / (AmE), / ˈɔːdnəns ˈsɜːveɪ / (BrE)
the official government agency responsible for mapping and cartography in Great Britain
British government mapping agency
british

Examples

The Ordnance Survey produces detailed maps used by hikers across the United Kingdom.
Many emergency services rely on Ordnance Survey data for accurate location information.
She collected several vintage Ordnance Survey maps to study historical changes in the countryside.

Etymology

From 'ordnance' meaning military supplies, reflecting its historical role in military survey, and 'survey' meaning measurement of land.
