red porgy

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈpɔrɡi / (AmE), / rɛd ˈpɔːɡi / (BrE)
a species of food fish (Pagrus pagrus) found in the Mediterranean and along the Atlantic coasts of Europe and America
