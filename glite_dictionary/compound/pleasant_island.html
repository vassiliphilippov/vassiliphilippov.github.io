Pleasant Island

1. PROPN

/ ˈplɛz.ənt ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / ˈplɛz.ənt ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
an earlier name for Nauru, a small island country in the central Pacific Ocean, southwest of Hawaii and west of the Gilbert Islands
former name for Nauru, Pacific island

Examples

In the nineteenth century, Pleasant Island was the name commonly used for what we now call Nauru.
Explorers regarded Pleasant Island as a strategic stopover in the Pacific region.
The phosphate-rich soil of Pleasant Island attracted mining companies in the early twentieth century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nauru - small island country in the Pacific, formerly known as Pleasant Island
