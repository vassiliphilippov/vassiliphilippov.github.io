microeconomic expert

1. NOUN

/ ˌmaɪkroʊˌɛkəˈnɑmɪk ˈɛkspɜrt / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˌiːkəˈnɒmɪk ˈɛkspɜːt / (BrE)
an economist who specializes in microeconomics, the study of individual markets and economic agents
economist specializing in microeconomics

Examples

She is a leading microeconomic expert who frequently advises government agencies.
The conference invited a microeconomic expert to discuss the effects of pricing strategies on competition.
As a microeconomic expert, he focuses primarily on market behaviors and consumer choice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microeconomist - an expert in microeconomics
