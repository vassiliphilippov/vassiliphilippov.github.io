oil rig

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔɪl rɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈɔɪl rɪɡ / (BrE)
a large structure with equipment for drilling for oil or gas, either on land or at sea
structure for drilling oil or gas

Examples

The workers spent months living on the oil rig in the North Sea.
A fire broke out on the offshore oil rig during the night.
With new technology, the oil rig can now drill deeper than ever before.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drilling platform - structure for supporting equipment used to extract oil or gas from beneath the earth
