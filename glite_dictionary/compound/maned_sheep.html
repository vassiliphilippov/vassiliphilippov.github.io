maned sheep

1. NOUN

/ meɪnd ʃiːp / (AmE), / meɪnd ʃiːp / (BrE)
a wild sheep native to northern Africa, characterized by a mane of long hair on its neck and chest
wild sheep of northern Africa

Examples

The maned sheep is well adapted to the rocky mountains of the Sahara.
At the zoo, we saw a maned sheep grazing near the cliffs.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the maned sheep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aoudad - wild sheep of northern Africa, also known as Barbary sheep
