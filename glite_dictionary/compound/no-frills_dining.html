no-frills dining

1. NOUN

/ ˌnoʊ ˈfrɪlz ˈdaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnəʊ ˈfrɪlz ˈdaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
A style of eating or restaurant service that is deliberately simple, offering only basic food and minimal amenities, without unnecessary extras or luxury.
simple, basic style of eating out

Examples

If you're looking for value, this place offers no-frills dining with generous portions.
The casual café specializes in no-frills dining that's both quick and affordable.
Many students prefer no-frills dining options when they're on a tight budget.

Synset Examples

The restaurant offers no-frills dining experiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no-frills - lacking unnecessary extras or features

Etymology

Formed from 'no frills', meaning without unnecessary extras, and 'dining'.
