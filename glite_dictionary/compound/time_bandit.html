time bandit

1. NOUN

/ taɪm ˈbændɪt / (AmE), / taɪm ˈbændɪt / (BrE)
a person or thing that habitually wastes one's time or interrupts one's work
person or thing that wastes time
informal

Examples

The constant notifications from my phone became a huge time bandit during my workday.
She realized that chatting with coworkers was her main time bandit in the office.
Social media often acts as a time bandit when I'm trying to focus on studying.

Synset Examples

She called her procrastination a time bandit.
He avoided distractions to beat the time bandit.
Social media is a real time bandit for me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: time-waster - a person or thing that wastes time
