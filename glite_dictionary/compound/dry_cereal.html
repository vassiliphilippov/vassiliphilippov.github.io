dry cereal

1. NOUN

/ ˌdraɪ ˈsɪriəl / (AmE), / ˌdraɪ ˈsɪəriəl / (BrE)
A breakfast cereal that is ready to eat without heating or cooking.
ready-to-eat breakfast cereal

Examples

She poured milk over her dry cereal before eating breakfast.
Many children prefer to eat dry cereal straight from the box as a snack.
The grocery store stocks a wide variety of dry cereal brands on its shelves.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hot cereal - a cereal cooked or heated before serving
