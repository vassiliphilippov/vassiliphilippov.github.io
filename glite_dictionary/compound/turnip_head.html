turnip head

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝː.nɪp hɛd / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.nɪp hɛd / (BrE)
A foolish or silly person, especially one who acts in a senseless way.
foolish or silly person
informal

Examples

I can't believe you forgot your keys again, you turnip head!
Mark was such a turnip head for leaving his presentation at home.
Stop being a turnip head and pay attention to what's happening.

Synset Examples

He's acting like a turnip head today.
Don't be a turnip head; just listen to reason.
She called him a turnip head for forgetting her birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely; a simpleton
  • Synonym: idiot - a stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - an extremely intelligent person
