road hog

1. NOUN

/ roʊd hɑɡ / (AmE), / rəʊd hɒɡ / (BrE)
a driver who selfishly obstructs other vehicles on the road, often by occupying more than one lane or refusing to yield
driver who obstructs others

Examples

That road hog nearly ran me off the highway this morning.
Please don’t be a road hog; let other cars merge ahead of you.
The police pulled over a road hog who was blocking both lanes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reckless driver - a person who drives dangerously without regard for traffic laws or safety

2. NOUN

/ ˈroʊd hɑg / (AmE), / ˈrəʊd hɒg / (BrE)
a driver who selfishly takes up more than their fair share of the road, often disregarding other motorists
selfish driver who hogs the road

Examples

Don’t be a road hog; let others merge!
The highway was jammed because of a road hog blocking both lanes.
My dad called the guy who cut him off a road hog during our road trip.

Synset Examples
