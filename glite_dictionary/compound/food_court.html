food court

1. NOUN

/ fuːd kɔrt / (AmE), / fuːd kɔːt / (BrE)
an area, typically in a shopping mall or similar venue, with multiple stalls or restaurants selling different types of food around a shared seating area
area with multiple eateries and shared seating

Examples

We decided to have lunch at the food court in the new shopping center.
The food court offers a variety of cuisines, from Italian to Asian.
After shopping for hours, they sat down to eat together in the food court.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cafeteria - self-service restaurant or dining area
