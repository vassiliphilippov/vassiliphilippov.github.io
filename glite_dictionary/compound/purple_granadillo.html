purple granadillo

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl ɡrəˌnædɪˈjoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpəl ɡrəˌnadɪˈjəʊ / (BrE)
A Brazilian passionflower cultivated for its deep purple fruit, often eaten fresh or used in beverages and desserts.
Brazilian passionflower with deep purple fruit

Examples

The juice of the purple granadillo is especially popular in Brazilian cuisine.
She bought a purple granadillo at the market to serve with dessert.
Farmers cultivate the purple granadillo for its vibrant, flavorful fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passion fruit - the fruit of several species of passionflower, especially Passiflora edulis
