lunch meeting

1. NOUN

/ lʌntʃ ˈmitɪŋ / (AmE), / lʌntʃ ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a meeting held over lunch, typically to discuss business matters
meeting during lunchtime for business

Examples

She scheduled a lunch meeting with her supervisor to discuss the new project.
Many deals are finalized during a lunch meeting at a restaurant downtown.
Let's arrange a lunch meeting next week to go over the proposal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: business lunch - a lunch at which business is discussed
