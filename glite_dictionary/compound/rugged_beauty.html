rugged beauty

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌɡɪd ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / ˈrʌɡɪd ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
A quality of wild, natural attractiveness, typically referring to landscapes that are striking and untamed, or to people with strong, distinctive features.
wild, natural attractiveness

Examples

The national park is famous for its rugged beauty and dramatic cliffs.
She admired the rugged beauty of the mountain landscape on her hike.
Many visitors are drawn to the island's rugged beauty and remote charm.

Synset Examples

The Shitland Islands are known for their rugged beauty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: natural beauty - attractiveness deriving from nature; unspoiled charm
