deadly venom

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛdli ˈvɛnəm / (AmE), / ˈdɛdli ˈvɛnəm / (BrE)
Venom that is highly toxic and can cause death.
poisonous substance that can kill

Examples

The snake's deadly venom can kill an adult human within hours if untreated.
Some frogs produce deadly venom as a defense mechanism against predators.
Researchers are studying deadly venom to develop new medicines and antivenoms.

Synset Examples

The asp is known for its deadly venom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poison - A substance causing harm or death when absorbed or ingested
  • Synonym: toxin - A poisonous substance produced by living organisms
  • Antonym: antidote - A substance that counteracts a poison
