assembly plant

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɛmbli plænt / (AmE), / əˈsɛmbli plɑːnt / (BrE)
A factory where manufactured parts are put together to make a finished product.
factory for assembling products

Examples

The new car assembly plant opened last year, creating hundreds of jobs for the community.
Many electronic devices are put together in an assembly plant before being shipped to stores.
Visiting the assembly plant gave students insight into the process of modern manufacturing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: factory - a building or group of buildings where goods are manufactured
