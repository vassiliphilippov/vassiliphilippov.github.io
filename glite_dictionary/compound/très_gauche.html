très gauche

1. ADJ

/ tʁɛ ɡoʊʃ / (AmE), / tʁɛ ɡəʊʃ / (BrE)
very awkward or lacking social grace; clumsily or ineptly behaved, especially in social situations.
very socially awkward

Examples

His attempts at conversation were très gauche and made everyone uncomfortable.
She smiled politely, ignoring his très gauche remark.
The candidate's très gauche mannerisms cost him the support of the audience.

Synset Examples

His comments seemed très gauche and inappropriate.
His behavior at the party was très gauche.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awkward - lacking grace or skill; socially uncomfortable

Etymology

From French 'très' (very) and 'gauche' (awkward, left).
