research center

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsɜrtʃ ˈsɛntər / (AmE), / rɪˈsəːtʃ ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
an institution or facility dedicated to conducting research in a specific field or range of fields
institution for conducting research

Examples

She works at a prestigious research center focused on renewable energy.
Several breakthroughs at the research center have led to new medical treatments.
The university opened a new research center to study climate change.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laboratory - a room or building equipped for scientific experiments, research, or teaching
