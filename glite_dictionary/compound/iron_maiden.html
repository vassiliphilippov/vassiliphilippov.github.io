iron maiden

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪərn ˈmeɪdən / (AmE), / ˈaɪən ˈmeɪdn̩ / (BrE)
a medieval torture device consisting of a human-shaped iron cabinet with spikes inside, used to impale a person enclosed within
medieval torture device with interior spikes

Examples

The museum's most gruesome exhibit was a preserved iron maiden from the Middle Ages.
Historians debate whether the iron maiden was actually used or if it was a later invention.
Legends about the iron maiden contribute to its fearsome reputation in popular culture.
