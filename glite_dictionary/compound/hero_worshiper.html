hero worshiper

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪroʊ ˌwɜrʃɪpər / (AmE), / ˈhɪərəʊ ˌwɜːʃɪpə / (BrE)
a person who admires or idolizes someone, especially to an excessive or uncritical degree
one who excessively admires heroes

Examples

He became a hero worshiper after reading countless biographies of famous leaders.
The teenager's hero worshiper phase led her to fill her room with posters of pop stars.
As a historian, she tried not to be a hero worshiper and maintained a balanced perspective.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admirer - someone who admires another person
  • Antonym: critic - someone who expresses disapproval of someone or something
