beef tenderloin

1. NOUN

/ bif ˈtɛndərˌlɔɪn / (AmE), / biːf ˈtɛndəˌlɔɪn / (BrE)
a cut of beef taken from the loin muscle, known for being tender and used in dishes such as filet mignon
tender beef loin cut

Examples

The chef prepared a perfectly cooked beef tenderloin for the special dinner menu.
I bought a beef tenderloin to roast for the holiday meal.
Slicing the beef tenderloin thinly makes it tender and juicy in sandwiches.
