waste of money

1. NOUN

/ weɪst əv ˈmʌni / (AmE), / weɪst əv ˈmʌni / (BrE)
an amount of money that is spent without achieving a worthwhile result or benefit
money spent with little or no benefit

Examples

Many people consider buying an expensive gym membership a waste of money if they rarely use it.
The manager canceled all the unnecessary subscriptions, calling them a waste of money.
Some think luxury brands are a waste of money, while others see them as a worthwhile investment.

Synset Examples

the senator said that the project was a waste of money

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waste of time - time spent with little or no benefit
