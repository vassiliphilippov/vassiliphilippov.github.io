ear lobe

1. NOUN

/ ɪr loʊb / (AmE), / ɪə ləʊb / (BrE)
the soft, fleshy lower part of the external human ear that hangs down from the ear
the soft lower part of the ear

Examples

She touched her ear lobe absentmindedly as she listened to the song.
The piercing in his ear lobe took only a few seconds to heal.
Some people are born with attached ear lobes, while others have free ones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lobe - a rounded fleshy part, especially of an organ or plant
