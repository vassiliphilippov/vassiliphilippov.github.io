great yellow gentian

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪt ˈjɛloʊ ˈdʒɛnʃən / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈjɛləʊ ˈdʒɛnʃən / (BrE)
a robust perennial plant native to Europe, with clusters of yellow flowers, known scientifically as Gentiana lutea
European perennial plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The great yellow gentian is cultivated for its medicinal properties and distinctive appearance.
In the mountains, we spotted a cluster of great yellow gentian among the wildflowers.
Herbalists often use the roots of the great yellow gentian to prepare digestive tonics.
