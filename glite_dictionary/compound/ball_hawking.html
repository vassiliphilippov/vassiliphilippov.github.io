ball hawking

1. NOUN

/ bɔl ˈhɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / bɔːl ˈhɔːkɪŋ / (BrE)
The skillful act of catching balls or stealing the ball from the opposing team, especially in sports like football or basketball.
skill of intercepting or stealing the ball (sports)

Examples

His ball hawking skills made him a valuable asset to the defense.
The coach praised her for her aggressive ball hawking throughout the basketball game.
Effective ball hawking often turns defense into offense in football.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interception - the act of catching a ball thrown by the opponent (sports)
  • Synonym: steal - an act of taking the ball away from the opposing team (sports)
