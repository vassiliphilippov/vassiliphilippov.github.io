political program

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪdɪkəl ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
a written statement outlining the aims, principles, and policies of a political party or candidate
statement of party aims and policies

Examples

The party released its political program ahead of the upcoming elections.
Voters often choose candidates based on the promises outlined in their political program.
Debate erupted over whether the political program was realistic or too ambitious.

Synset Examples

their candidate simply ignored the party platform
they won the election even though they offered no positive program

Related Terms

  • Synonym: platform - the declared policy of a political party or group
  • Synonym: manifesto - a public declaration of policy and aims
