passenger van

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæsəndʒɚ væn / (AmE), / ˈpæsɪndʒə væn / (BrE)
a van designed or modified for carrying passengers rather than cargo
a van for carrying passengers

Examples

The school purchased a new passenger van to transport the students to their field trip.
Our team rented a passenger van for the weekend tournament.
The passenger van comfortably seats up to fifteen people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minivan - a small van, typically designed for transporting passengers
  • Antonym: cargo van - a van designed primarily for carrying goods rather than people
