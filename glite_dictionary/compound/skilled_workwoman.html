skilled workwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪld ˈwɜrkˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈskɪld ˈwɜːkˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who has acquired special training or abilities to perform her work with expertise.
a woman with specialized work skills

Examples

The company hired a skilled workwoman to manage the assembly line.
As a skilled workwoman, she was paid more than her untrained counterparts.
The union recognized her as a respected skilled workwoman in her field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skilled worker - a person who has acquired special skills for work
  • Antonym: unskilled workwoman - a female worker without special skills or training
