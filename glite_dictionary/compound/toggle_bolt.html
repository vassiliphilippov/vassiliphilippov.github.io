toggle bolt

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑɡəl boʊlt / (AmE), / ˈtɒɡəl bəʊlt / (BrE)
a fastener consisting of a threaded bolt and a hinged, spring-loaded part that opens inside a hollow wall to secure objects in place
type of wall fastener with a spring toggle

Examples

I used a toggle bolt to hang the heavy mirror on the drywall.
The shelf is anchored with toggle bolts to provide extra support for the weight.
She recommended using a toggle bolt for mounting items on hollow plaster walls.

Etymology

From 'toggle' (a pin or bar that pivots) and 'bolt' (a metal fastener).
