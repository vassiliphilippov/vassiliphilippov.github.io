defense policy

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛns ˌpɑləsi / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns ˌpɒləsi / (BrE)
A government's plan or strategy for protecting a country against military or external threats.
government military strategy

Examples

The administration announced changes to its defense policy in response to new global threats.
A strong defense policy is essential for maintaining national security.
Many citizens debate the amount of money allocated to defense policy each year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foreign policy - a government's strategy for dealing with other nations
  • Synonym: military strategy - a plan for achieving military objectives
