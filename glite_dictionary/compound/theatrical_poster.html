theatrical poster

1. NOUN

/ θiˈætrɪkəl ˈpoʊstər / (AmE), / θɪˈætrɪkəl ˈpəʊstə / (BrE)
a large printed notice or picture used to advertise a theatrical show or play
poster advertising a theatre show or play

Examples

The entrance was decorated with a vintage theatrical poster from the original production.
She designed a striking theatrical poster to promote the upcoming play at the community theatre.
Collectors often search for rare theatrical posters from famous Broadway shows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: playbill - a printed program or advertisement for a play
  • Degree Lower: movie poster - a poster advertising a film or movie
