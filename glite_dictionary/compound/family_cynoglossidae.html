family Cynoglossidae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˌsɪnoʊɡlɒˈsɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæməli ˌsɪnəʊˌɡlɒsɪˈdiː / (BrE)
A family of flatfishes, commonly known as tonguefishes, found in marine and brackish waters.
family of flatfishes (tonguefishes)

Examples

Scientists have discovered a new species in the family Cynoglossidae living in the river delta.
The family Cynoglossidae comprises many species adapted to living on sandy seabeds.
Researchers are studying the distribution patterns of the family Cynoglossidae in different marine regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tonguefish - a flatfish of the family Cynoglossidae

Etymology

From New Latin Cynoglossidae; based on Greek 'kynoglōssos' (dog-tongued), referring to the tongue-like shape.
