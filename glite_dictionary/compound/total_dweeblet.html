total dweeblet

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊt̬l ˈdwiːb.lət / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl ˈdwiːb.lət / (BrE)
A very socially awkward or uncool person, especially in a playful or diminutive sense.
very socially awkward person (slang)
informal slang

Examples

Whenever he spoke in class, he felt like a total dweeblet in front of everyone.
Stop worrying about looking like a total dweeblet; just be yourself!
They called him a total dweeblet for bringing comic books to the dance.

Synset Examples

He acts like a total dweeblet around his crush.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dweeb - an awkward, socially inept person

Etymology

Formed from 'total' (emphasizing extent) and 'dweeblet', a playful diminutive of 'dweeb' (slang for an awkward person).
