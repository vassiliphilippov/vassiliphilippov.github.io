chickweed phlox

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkˌwid floʊks / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkwiːd flɒks / (BrE)
A low, mat-forming herb of rocky areas in the United States, known for its small, star-shaped flowers.
North American mat-forming herb

Examples

The chickweed phlox thrived in the rocky soil of the garden despite the harsh weather.
She spotted delicate clusters of chickweed phlox blooming between the stones on her hike.
Gardeners often choose chickweed phlox as a ground cover for sunny, rugged landscapes.
