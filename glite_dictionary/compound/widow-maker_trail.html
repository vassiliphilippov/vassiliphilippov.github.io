widow-maker trail

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪdoʊ ˌmeɪkər treɪl / (AmE), / ˈwɪdəʊ ˌmeɪkə treɪl / (BrE)
A hiking trail considered particularly dangerous and potentially fatal for those who attempt it.
dangerous hiking trail

Examples

The ranger warned us that the widow-maker trail should only be attempted by experienced hikers.
Many accidents have occurred on the notorious widow-maker trail in recent years.
Despite the risks, thrill-seekers are often drawn to the challenging widow-maker trail.

Synset Examples

Hiking there can be dangerous; it's a widow-maker trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dangerous trail - a hiking path known to be hazardous
  • Antonym: easy trail - a hiking path considered safe and simple

Etymology

From 'widow-maker' (something likely to cause death) + 'trail' (path for walking or hiking).
