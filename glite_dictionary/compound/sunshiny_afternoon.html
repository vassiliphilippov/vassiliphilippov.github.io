sunshiny afternoon

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌʃaɪni ˌæftɚˈnun / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌʃaɪni ˌɑːftəˈnuːn / (BrE)
an afternoon characterized by bright sunshine and pleasant weather
afternoon with bright sunshine

Examples

We enjoyed a picnic on the sunshiny afternoon by the lake.
Children played outside during the sunshiny afternoon, their laughter filling the air.
A sunshiny afternoon always lifts my spirits after a long week.

Synset Examples

It was a sunshiny afternoon for a stroll.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunny - bright with sunlight
