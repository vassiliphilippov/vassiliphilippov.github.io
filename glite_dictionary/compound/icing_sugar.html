icing sugar

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪsɪŋ ˌsʌɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈaɪsɪŋ ˌsʌɡə / (BrE)
a very finely powdered sugar used especially for making icings and decorations in baking
finely powdered sugar for icing
british

Examples

She sifted icing sugar over the top of the cake for a snowy finish.
To make the frosting, mix butter with icing sugar and a little milk.
The biscuits were decorated with icing sugar and colorful sprinkles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confectioners' sugar - finely powdered sugar, chiefly used in American English
