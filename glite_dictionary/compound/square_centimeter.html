square centimeter

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr ˈsɛntɪˌmiːtər / (AmE), / skwɛː ˈsɛntɪmiːtə / (BrE)
a unit of area equal to a square that measures one centimeter on each side, used in the metric system
unit of area in the metric system

Examples

The label on the package indicated an area of 20 square centimeters.
Her drawing covered only about four square centimeters of the notebook page.
Each tile is precisely one square centimeter in size, making calculation easy.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: square millimeter - a unit of area equal to a square that measures one millimeter on each side
  • Degree Higher: square meter - a unit of area equal to a square measuring one meter on each side

Etymology

From Latin "centum" (hundred) and "metrum" (measure), via French "centimètre".
