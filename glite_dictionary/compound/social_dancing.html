social dancing

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊ.ʃəl ˈdæn.sɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃəl ˈdæn.sɪŋ / (BrE)
Dancing performed at social gatherings or events, not intended for public performance or competition.
dancing at social events

Examples

At the wedding reception, everyone enjoyed the social dancing that followed dinner.
He took lessons in social dancing to feel more comfortable at parties.
Many community centers offer social dancing events for people of all ages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ballroom dancing - formal social dancing in a ballroom setting
