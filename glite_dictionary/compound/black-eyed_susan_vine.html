black-eyed Susan vine

1. NOUN

/ ˌblæk aɪd ˈsuːzən vaɪn / (AmE), / ˌblæk aɪd ˈsjuːzən vaɪn / (BrE)
a tropical African climbing plant with yellow flowers featuring a dark purple center, commonly grown as an ornamental
tropical climbing plant with yellow, dark-centered flowers

Examples

The black-eyed Susan vine quickly covered the trellis with its vibrant yellow blooms.
Gardeners love planting the black-eyed Susan vine for its fast-growing nature and striking flowers.
She admired the black-eyed Susan vine winding along the fence in her backyard.
