food color

1. NOUN

/ fuːd ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / fuːd ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
a substance added to food to give it a particular color, usually safe for consumption
substance for coloring food

Examples

Many bakers use food color to create vibrant cakes and pastries.
Adding a drop of food color can turn plain icing into a rainbow of shades.
The chef avoided artificial food color and opted for natural dyes instead.

Synset Examples

food color made from vegetable dyes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: food colouring - substance used to add color to food, especially in British English
