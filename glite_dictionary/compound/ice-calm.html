ice-calm

1. ADJ

/ aɪs kɑlm / (AmE), / aɪs kɑːm / (BrE)
extremely calm and composed, especially in stressful or tense situations
extremely calm and unshaken

Examples

She remained ice-calm even as tempers flared around her.
His ice-calm demeanor impressed the interviewers despite the tough questions.
During the emergency landing, the pilot's ice-calm voice reassured all the passengers.

Synset Examples

His ice-calm attitude helped everyone relax.
She handled the crisis with ice-calm composure.
He was ice-calm during the stormy situation.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness, anger, or other strong emotions
  • Synonym: cool-headed - not easily excited or upset; able to think clearly in stressful situations
