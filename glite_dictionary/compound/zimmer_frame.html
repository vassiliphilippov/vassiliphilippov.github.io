Zimmer frame

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɪmɚ freɪm / (AmE), / ˈzɪmə freɪm / (BrE)
a metal frame with handles, often on wheels or rubber stoppers, used to help people who have difficulty walking keep their balance
walking aid with handles for the elderly or disabled
british

Examples

After her surgery, she used a Zimmer frame to move around the house safely.
The hospital provided a Zimmer frame for the elderly patient during his rehabilitation.
He relied on his Zimmer frame to maintain independence after his fall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: walker - a frame used to aid walking, especially in North America

Etymology

Named after the Zimmer company in the United States, which first manufactured these walking aids.
