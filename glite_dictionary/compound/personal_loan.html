personal loan

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːsənəl loʊn / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəl ləʊn / (BrE)
A loan made to an individual for personal, rather than business, use, typically unsecured and based on the borrower's creditworthiness.
a loan for personal use, usually unsecured

Examples

She applied for a personal loan to cover the cost of renovating her kitchen.
Many banks offer a personal loan option for consolidating credit card debt.
A personal loan can help you pay for unexpected expenses without needing collateral.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loan - a sum of money that is borrowed and expected to be paid back with interest
