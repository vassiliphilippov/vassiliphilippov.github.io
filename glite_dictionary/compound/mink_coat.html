mink coat

1. NOUN

/ mɪŋk koʊt / (AmE), / mɪŋk kəʊt / (BrE)
A fur coat made from the soft, glossy fur of minks.
coat made of mink fur

Examples

She wore her luxurious mink coat to the gala event last Saturday night.
After inheriting a vintage mink coat, Julia had it professionally cleaned before wearing it.
Protesters demonstrated outside the store that sold expensive mink coats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fur coat - a coat made from the fur of animals
