bread and salt

1. NOUN

/ brɛd ənd sɑlt / (AmE), / brɛd ənd sɒlt / (BrE)
A traditional symbol or gesture of hospitality or friendship, often expressed by offering guests bread and salt.
gesture of hospitality or friendship

Examples

At the ceremony, the hosts welcomed the visitors with bread and salt as a mark of respect.
Sharing bread and salt is an important tradition in many Eastern European countries.
They honored the guests by offering bread and salt when they arrived.

Synset Examples

They treated me well, as if I had taken bread and salt with them.
When they invited me in, they offered to take bread and salt.

Etymology

Refers to ancient customs in various cultures where offering bread and salt to guests symbolizes welcome and goodwill.
