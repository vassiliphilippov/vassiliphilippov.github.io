camphor ice

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæm.fər aɪs / (AmE), / ˈkæm.fə aɪs / (BrE)
A topical medicinal preparation made from camphor, wax, spermaceti, and castor oil, used for soothing the skin.
medicated camphor-based skin balm
medical

Examples

He applied camphor ice to the sore on his lip to ease the pain.
Old-fashioned remedies like camphor ice were common in my grandmother's medicine cabinet.
Camphor ice is sometimes used to soothe chapped lips in cold weather.
