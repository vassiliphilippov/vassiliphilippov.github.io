yellow trumpet

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈtrʌmpɪt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈtrʌmpɪt / (BrE)
a pitcher plant native to the southeastern United States, with erect yellow trumpet-shaped pitchers and wide mouths
a southeastern U.S. pitcher plant

Examples

The yellow trumpet thrives in boggy areas across the southeastern United States.
Gardeners are drawn to the unusual shape of the yellow trumpet pitcher plant.
Conservationists monitor yellow trumpet populations due to their habitat's vulnerability.
