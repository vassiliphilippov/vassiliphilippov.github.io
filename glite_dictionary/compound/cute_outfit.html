cute outfit

1. NOUN

/ kjuːt ˈaʊtˌfɪt / (AmE), / kjuːt ˈaʊt.fɪt / (BrE)
an attractive or appealing set of clothes, especially in terms of style or appearance
attractive set of clothes

Examples

She received many compliments on her cute outfit at the party.
That is such a cute outfit for a summer picnic!
He wore a cute outfit to impress his friends.

Synset Examples

She dressed her baby boy in a cute outfit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stylish outfit - a set of clothes considered fashionable or stylish
  • Antonym: ugly outfit - a set of clothes considered unattractive
