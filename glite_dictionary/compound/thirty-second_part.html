thirty-second part

1. NOUN

/ ˌθɜrtiˈsɛkənd pɑrt / (AmE), / ˌθɜːtiˈsɛkənd pɑːt / (BrE)
One of thirty-two equal portions into which a whole can be divided; 1/32.
one of thirty-two equal parts; 1/32
