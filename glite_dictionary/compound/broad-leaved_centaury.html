broad-leaved centaury

1. NOUN

/ brɔd livd sɛnˈtɔri / (AmE), / brɔːd liːvd sɛnˈtɔːri / (BrE)
A variety of centaury plant distinguished by its broad leaves.
centaury plant with broad leaves

Examples

The broad-leaved centaury can often be found in meadows and grasslands across Europe.
Thanks to its distinctive foliage, the broad-leaved centaury is easy to spot among other wildflowers.
Botanists conducted a survey to record the distribution of broad-leaved centaury in the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: centaury - a small plant of the gentian family, often with pink flowers
