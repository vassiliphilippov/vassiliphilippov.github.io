ring girl

1. NOUN

/ rɪŋ ɡɜrl / (AmE), / rɪŋ ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a young woman who enters the boxing ring between rounds to display a sign showing the upcoming round number
woman displaying round cards in boxing

Examples

The ring girl walked around the ring, holding a sign for round three.
Some sports organizations have discussed replacing the traditional ring girls with digital displays.
During the break, the crowd cheered as the ring girl entered to announce the next round.
