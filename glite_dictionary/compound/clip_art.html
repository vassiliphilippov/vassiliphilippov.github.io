clip art

1. NOUN

/ klɪp ɑrt / (AmE), / klɪp ɑːt / (BrE)
ready-made digital images or graphics that can be inserted into documents for decoration or illustration
pre-made digital images for documents

Examples

He added some clip art to his presentation slides to make them more engaging.
The software comes with a large library of clip art for users to choose from.
Teachers often use clip art to illustrate worksheets for students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graphic - a picture or design created on a computer

Etymology

From 'clip' in the sense of a cutting, plus 'art'. Originally referring to images clipped from magazines before the digital age.
