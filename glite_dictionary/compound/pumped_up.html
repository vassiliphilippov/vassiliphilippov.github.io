pumped up

1. ADJ

/ pʌmpt ʌp / (AmE), / pʌmpt ʌp / (BrE)
full of excitement and enthusiasm, often as a result of anticipation or adrenaline
filled with excitement or enthusiasm
informal

Examples

Everyone was pumped up before the big game and ready to win.
The band was pumped up after hearing the crowd cheer so loudly.
She drank a coffee and felt pumped up for her final exam.

Synset Examples

we were really pumped up for the race
he was so pumped he couldn't sleep

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excited - feeling or showing excitement
  • Synonym: energized - filled with energy and enthusiasm
  • Antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness or excitement
