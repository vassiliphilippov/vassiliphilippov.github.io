tennis lesson

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnɪs ˌlɛsən / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs ˌlɛsən / (BrE)
a period of instruction or training in how to play tennis, usually given by a coach or instructor
instruction in playing tennis

Examples

She signed up for a tennis lesson to improve her backhand.
After his first tennis lesson, he felt much more confident on the court.
The club offers group tennis lessons every Saturday morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lesson - a period of instruction or teaching
