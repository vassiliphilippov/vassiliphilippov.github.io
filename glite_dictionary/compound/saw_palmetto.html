saw palmetto

1. NOUN

/ sɔ pælˈmɛtoʊ / (AmE), / sɔː pælˈmɛtəʊ / (BrE)
a small, hardy, clump-forming palm with spiny leaves, native to the southeastern United States
small spiny palm of the southeastern US

Examples

The berries of the saw palmetto are often used in herbal remedies.
A dense thicket of saw palmetto covered much of the forest floor.
We saw a saw palmetto growing near the edge of the swamp.
