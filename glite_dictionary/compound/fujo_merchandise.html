fujo merchandise

1. NOUN

/ ˈfuː.dʒoʊ ˈmɜr.tʃən.daɪs / (AmE), / ˈfuː.dʒəʊ ˈmɜː.tʃən.daɪs / (BrE)
products or goods related to Fujoshi culture, typically catering to female fans of manga and anime who enjoy male-male romantic relationships.
goods linked to Fujoshi anime/manga fandom
informal

Examples

Akira spent a fortune on limited edition fujo merchandise at the convention.
Many online shops now specialize in selling fujo merchandise to fans around the world.
Her room is decorated with posters and figurines, all part of her fujo merchandise collection.

Synset Examples

His collection of fujo merchandise is impressive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Fujoshi - a female fan of manga or anime featuring romantic relationships between men

Etymology

From Japanese 'fujoshi' (female fans of manga featuring male-male romance) + English 'merchandise'.
