metal mouth

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛɾl ˌmaʊθ / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəl ˌmaʊθ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who wears braces or dental appliances, usually referring to the visible metal parts
person with braces (slang)
informal

Examples

She was nervous about her new braces and worried that everyone would call her a metal mouth.
During middle school, he was nicknamed metal mouth by his classmates.
Even after her braces came off, the memory of being called metal mouth stuck with her.

Synset Examples

She joked about being a metal mouth during her orthodontic treatment.
