Lent lily

1. NOUN

/ lɛnt ˈlɪli / (AmE), / lent ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A species of lily native to the eastern Mediterranean and the Balkans, having broad, funnel-shaped white flowers.
type of Mediterranean lily with white flowers

Examples

The Lent lily blooms in early spring, brightening the garden with its white flowers.
She planted a Lent lily near the gate for a touch of Mediterranean beauty.
Botanists studied the Lent lily to understand its adaptation to dry climates.
