slender-waisted

1. ADJ

/ ˈslɛndər ˌweɪstɪd / (AmE), / ˈslɛndə ˌweɪstɪd / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having a slim or narrow waist.
having a slim waist

Examples

The model was praised for her slender-waisted silhouette and elegant posture.
Some species of wasps are known for their distinctly slender-waisted bodies.
He described the dancer as slender-waisted and graceful on stage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slim-waisted - having a slim waist
