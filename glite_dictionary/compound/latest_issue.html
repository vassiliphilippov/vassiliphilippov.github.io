latest issue

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪtəst ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈleɪtɪst ˈɪʃuː / (BrE)
the most recent edition or release of a magazine, newspaper, or other periodical publication
most recent edition of a publication

Examples

I picked up the latest issue of the magazine at the bookstore yesterday.
The latest issue features an exclusive interview with the author.
Have you read the latest issue of the journal yet?

Synset Examples

He collected the latest issue of Screws of the World magazine.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: back issue - an earlier edition of a publication, not the most recent
