medical expense

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl ɪkˈspɛns / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ɪkˈspɛns / (BrE)
an amount of money spent for the diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of medical problems
cost for medical care

Examples

The company reimbursed her for every medical expense she incurred during her illness.
Unexpected medical expenses can place a heavy burden on families without insurance.
He kept all his receipts to claim his medical expenses on his tax return.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: healthcare cost - cost associated with providing medical services
