computer keyboard

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtɚ ˈkiːbɔɹd / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈkiːbɔːd / (BrE)
A panel of keys used as a data input device for computers, typically arranged like a typewriter keyboard.
keyboard device for computers

Examples

She spilled coffee on her computer keyboard and had to buy a replacement.
Many gamers prefer a mechanical computer keyboard for faster response times.
You can connect a wireless computer keyboard to your laptop for increased comfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keyboard - A panel of keys for typing or controlling a machine
