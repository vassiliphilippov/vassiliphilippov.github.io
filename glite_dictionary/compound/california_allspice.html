California allspice

1. NOUN

/ kælɪˈfɔrniə ˈɔlˌspaɪs / (AmE), / kælɪˈfɔːniə ˈɔːlˌspaɪs / (BrE)
an aromatic shrub of the southwestern United States with fragrant brown flowers, known for its scent
aromatic shrub of the southwestern US

Examples

The garden features a California allspice that fills the air with a sweet fragrance every spring.
Many native plant enthusiasts choose California allspice for its aromatic flowers and attractive foliage.
A mature California allspice can add both beauty and scent to any southwestern garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spicebush - aromatic shrub with spicy-scented leaves and bark
