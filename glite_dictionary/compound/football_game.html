football game

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌbɔl ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈfʊt.bɔːl ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A match in which two teams compete to score points by moving a football into the opposing team's goal, according to the specific rules of football played (such as soccer or American football).
match where teams play football

Examples

The championship football game attracted thousands of fans to the stadium.
He broke his leg during a football game last season.
Several friends gathered to watch the football game on television.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soccer match - a game of association football (soccer) between two teams
  • Synonym: American football game - a game of American football between two teams
