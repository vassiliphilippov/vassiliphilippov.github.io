hair spray

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛr spreɪ / (AmE), / ˈheə spreɪ / (BrE)
A commercial product in the form of a fine mist, sprayed onto the hair to hold it in place.
product sprayed on hair to hold style

Examples

She sprayed a generous amount of hair spray to keep her curls in place all night.
The strong wind was no match for the hold provided by his favorite hair spray.
He accidentally grabbed the bottle of hair spray instead of deodorant this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mousse - a light, foamy hair styling product
