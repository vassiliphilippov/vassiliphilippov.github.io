vascular spider

1. NOUN

/ ˈvæskjələr ˈspaɪdər / (AmE), / ˈvæskjʊlə ˈspaɪdə / (BrE)
a small, red mark on the skin consisting of a central red spot with tiny blood vessels radiating from it, caused by dilated superficial capillaries
red skin mark with radiating blood vessels
medical

Examples

The doctor identified the red mark on her cheek as a vascular spider.
Chronic liver disease can lead to the appearance of vascular spiders on the skin.
He was concerned about several vascular spiders that appeared on his forearms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spider angioma - another term for vascular spider; red skin mark with radiating blood vessels
