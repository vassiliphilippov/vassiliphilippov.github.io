tropical rain forest

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɑːpɪkəl ˈreɪn ˌfɔrɪst / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪkəl ˈreɪn ˌfɒrɪst / (BrE)
A dense, evergreen forest found in tropical regions, characterized by high rainfall and rich biodiversity.
dense forest in the tropics with high rainfall

Examples

The Amazon is the largest tropical rain forest in the world, home to countless species.
Many medicines originate from plants found in the tropical rain forest ecosystem.
Deforestation is threatening the biodiversity of the tropical rain forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rain forest - a forest with high annual rainfall
