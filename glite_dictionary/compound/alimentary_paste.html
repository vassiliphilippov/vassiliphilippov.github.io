alimentary paste

1. NOUN

/ ˌælɪˈmɛn.tɛri pæst / (AmE), / ˌalɪˈmɛn.təri pɑːst / (BrE)
pasta or similar food made from shaped and dried dough, typically of flour and water and sometimes egg.
pasta-like shaped dried dough food

Examples

Spaghetti and macaroni are types of alimentary paste commonly found in supermarkets.
The chef used fresh alimentary paste for the lasagna recipe.
Manufacturers often enrich alimentary paste with vitamins and minerals for added nutrition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pasta - Italian food made from dough, shaped and cooked

Etymology

From Latin 'alimentarius' (pertaining to food) and Italian 'pasta'.
