Warlpiri language

1. NOUN

/ wɑːrˈliːpi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / wɑːlˈpɪəri ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an Australian Aboriginal language spoken primarily in the Northern Territory of Australia
Australian Aboriginal language

Examples

Many linguists study the Warlpiri language to understand unique features of Australian Aboriginal languages.
She is fluent in both English and the Warlpiri language.
