rock hound

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑk ˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈrɒk ˌhaʊnd / (BrE)
A person who collects rocks and minerals as a hobby.
person who collects rocks/minerals

Examples

Ever since childhood, he has been a passionate rock hound fascinated by geology.
The local club for rock hounds organizes field trips to mineral-rich sites.
As a devoted rock hound, she knows where to find the rarest gemstones in the area.

Synset Examples

As a rock hound, she spends weekends exploring quarries.
He's a rock hound who collects minerals everywhere he travels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rock collector - a person who collects rocks
