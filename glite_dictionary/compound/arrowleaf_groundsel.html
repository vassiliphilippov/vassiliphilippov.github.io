arrowleaf groundsel

1. NOUN

/ ˈæroʊˌlif ˈɡraʊndsəl / (AmE), / ˈærəʊˌliːf ˈɡraʊn(d)səl / (BrE)
a perennial plant of western North American mountains, with sharply toothed, triangular leaves and clusters of yellow flowers, typically found in moist places
mountain plant with triangular leaves and yellow flowers

Examples

We spotted an arrowleaf groundsel blooming near the mountain creek during our hike.
The arrowleaf groundsel is easily recognized by its bright yellow flower clusters and arrow-shaped leaves.
Botanists study arrowleaf groundsel to understand how mountain plants adapt to moist environments.
