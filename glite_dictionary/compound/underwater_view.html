underwater view

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌndɚˈwɔtɚ vju / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈwɔːtə vjuː / (BrE)
a view seen from below the surface of the water or looking into the water
view below or into water

Examples

The underwater view through the glass-bottomed boat revealed a world full of colorful corals.
Divers often photograph the underwater view to capture the stunning marine life.
He was amazed by the underwater view from his hotel room’s submerged window.

Synset Examples

The underwater view from the boat was breathtaking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scenic view - a pleasing or picturesque view
