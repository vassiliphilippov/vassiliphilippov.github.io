rye vodka

1. NOUN

/ raɪ ˈvɑːdkə / (AmE), / raɪ ˈvɒdkə / (BrE)
Vodka that is distilled mainly from rye grains, giving it a characteristic flavor profile.
vodka distilled from rye

Examples

Many craft distilleries in Poland produce high-quality rye vodka using traditional methods.
The bartender recommended a cocktail made with rye vodka for a sharper, spicier taste.
Among Eastern European spirits, rye vodka is prized for its distinct flavor and smoothness.

Synset Examples

Among grain vodkas, rye and wheat vodkas are generally considered superior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vodka - a clear distilled alcoholic beverage
