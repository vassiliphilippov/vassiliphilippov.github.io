corn gluten

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn ˈɡluːtn̩ / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈɡluːtn̩ / (BrE)
a protein substance obtained from corn, used especially in food processing and as animal feed
protein from corn, used in food and feed

Examples

Many animal feeds are supplemented with corn gluten to increase protein content.
Some organic gardeners use corn gluten as a natural pre-emergent herbicide.
The food industry utilizes corn gluten in products requiring a plant-based protein source.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gluten - an elastic protein substance present in wheat and some other grains
