biological science

1. NOUN

/ ˌbaɪəˈlɑdʒɪkəl ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˈsʌɪəns / (BrE)
the branch of science concerned with the study of living organisms and life processes
science studying living organisms

Examples

She decided to major in biological science because of her fascination with living organisms.
Advances in biological science have led to important medical breakthroughs.
The university offers several courses in biological science for undergraduate students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biology - the scientific study of living organisms
