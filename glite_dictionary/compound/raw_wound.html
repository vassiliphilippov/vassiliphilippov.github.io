raw wound

1. NOUN

/ rɔː ˈwuːnd / (AmE), / rɔː ˈwuːnd / (BrE)
a wound in which the skin is broken and the underlying tissue is exposed
wound with exposed tissue

Examples

The hiker tended to his raw wound carefully to avoid infection.
Her leg had a raw wound where she had scraped it against the rocks.
It took weeks for the raw wound to start healing properly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abrasion - a wound caused by scraping the skin
  • Synonym: laceration - a deep cut or tear in skin or flesh
