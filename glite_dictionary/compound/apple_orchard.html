apple orchard

1. NOUN

/ ˈæp.əl ˈɔr.tʃɚd / (AmE), / ˈæp.əl ˈɔː.tʃəd / (BrE)
an area of land where apple trees are grown for their fruit
place where apple trees are cultivated

Examples

The family owns a large apple orchard in the countryside.
We visited an apple orchard last autumn to pick fresh fruit.
Most of the region's apple orchard was damaged by the storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchard - an area of land planted with fruit trees
