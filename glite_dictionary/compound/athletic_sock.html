athletic sock

1. NOUN

/ əθˈlɛtɪk sɑk / (AmE), / æθˈlɛtɪk sɒk / (BrE)
A sock designed to be worn during athletic activities, providing comfort, support, and moisture control.
sock for athletic activities

Examples

I always wear athletic socks when I go jogging to prevent blisters.
He packed several pairs of athletic socks for the basketball tournament.
These athletic socks are made from breathable fabric to keep your feet dry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sports sock - a sock worn for sports
