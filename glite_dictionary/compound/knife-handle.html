knife-handle

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪf ˌhændl̩ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪf ˌhændl / (BrE)
A marine clam with a long, narrow, curved, and thin shell, resembling the handle of a knife.
marine clam with a narrow curved shell

Examples

The shell collector was thrilled to find a rare knife-handle among the tidal debris.
A knife-handle clam burrowed deeply into the wet sand at low tide.
Biologists documented the habitat range of the knife-handle along the Atlantic coast.
