Bantersaurus Rex

1. NOUN

/ ˌbæn.tɚˌsɔːr.əs ˈrɛks / (AmE), / ˌbæn.təˌsɔː.rəs ˈreks / (BrE)
a humorous nickname for a very witty or entertaining person, especially someone known for lively banter
very witty or entertaining person (humorous)
informal

Examples

Whenever someone needs cheering up, they call on the Bantersaurus Rex of the office.
He's proud to be called the Bantersaurus Rex by his friends.
As the Bantersaurus Rex of the party, she always keeps the conversation lively.

Synset Examples

He’s the Bantersaurus Rex of the group, always cracking jokes.
At parties, he transforms into the Bantersaurus Rex, entertaining everyone.
Her reputation as the Bantersaurus Rex precedes her wherever she goes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joker - a person who makes jokes or is playful
  • Synonym: wit - a person with a clever and amusing way of expressing themselves

Etymology

Humorous blend of 'banter' and 'Tyrannosaurus rex', with 'saurus' referencing dinosaurs.
