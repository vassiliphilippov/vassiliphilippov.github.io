smooth green snake

1. NOUN

/ smuð ɡrin sneɪk / (AmE), / smuːð ɡriːn sneɪk / (BrE)
a slender, nonvenomous snake of western and central United States with smooth green scales, known scientifically as Opheodrys vernalis
a slender green nonvenomous American snake

Examples

We saw a smooth green snake slithering quietly through the grass.
The smooth green snake is often found in meadows and open woods.
Children are often fascinated by the bright color of the smooth green snake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Opheodrys vernalis - scientific name for the smooth green snake
