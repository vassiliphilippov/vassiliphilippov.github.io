bundle of joy

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌndəl əv ˈdʒɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈbʌndəl əv ˈdʒɔɪ / (BrE)
A newborn baby, or something or someone that brings great happiness or delight.
a newborn baby or source of happiness

Examples

Their baby arrived last night, and everyone is eager to meet the new bundle of joy.
When I adopted my puppy, she quickly became a real bundle of joy in my life.
Every time I visit, my niece is a little bundle of joy, always smiling and laughing.

Synset Examples

The baby was a crotchful bundle of joy.
Receiving their letter was a bundle of joy for me.
The baby was a bundle of joy for the family.
Her dog is a bundle of joy, always wagging its tail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blessing - something that brings happiness or is lucky
  • Antonym: burden - something that causes worry or difficulty
