stock breeder

1. NOUN

/ stɑk ˈbriːdər / (AmE), / stɒk ˈbriːdə / (BrE)
a person who breeds domestic animals, especially livestock, for commercial or agricultural purposes
person who breeds livestock

Examples

The stock breeder carefully selected animals with the best traits for the next generation.
Many stock breeders attend agricultural fairs to showcase their livestock.
As a successful stock breeder, she kept detailed records of each animal's lineage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breeder - a person who breeds plants or animals
  • Synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm

Etymology

From 'stock' (livestock) + 'breeder'.
