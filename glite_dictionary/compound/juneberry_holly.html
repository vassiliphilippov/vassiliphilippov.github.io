juneberry holly

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːnˌbɛri ˈhɑːli / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːnbəri ˈhɒli / (BrE)
A species of holly shrub (Ilex amelanchier) known for its small berry-like fruit.
a species of holly shrub

Examples

The juneberry holly blooms in early spring, producing small white flowers.
Gardeners often select juneberry holly for hedges because of its dense foliage.
Birds are attracted to the bright berries of the juneberry holly in late summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holly - evergreen shrub or tree with glossy leaves and red berries
