painted Jezebel

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪntɪd ˈdʒɛzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪd ˈdʒɛzəbəl / (BrE)
an immoral or seductive woman, especially one who dresses provocatively or flamboyantly
immoral or seductive woman

Examples

She was branded a painted Jezebel by the local gossip column.
In the play, the villain refers to his adversary as a painted Jezebel.
Tabloid headlines often sensationalized her image as a painted Jezebel.

Synset Examples

The character in the movie was portrayed as a painted Jezebel.
She dressed like a painted Jezebel for the costume party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jezebel - an impudent, shameless, or morally unrestrained woman

Etymology

Painted refers to heavy makeup; Jezebel refers to the biblical queen infamous for immorality.
