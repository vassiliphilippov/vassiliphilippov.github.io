total depressoid

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬əl dɪˈprɛsˌɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl dɪˈprɛsˌɔɪd / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is extremely depressed or persistently gloomy
person who is extremely gloomy or depressed
informal slang

Examples

After the breakup, Tom was a total depressoid and barely left his room for days.
Don't be such a total depressoid—things will get better soon.
Her posts on social media make her sound like a total depressoid sometimes.

Synset Examples

Her negative outlook made her seem like a total depressoid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gloomy - having a dark or depressed mood
  • Antonym: optimist - a person who tends to be hopeful and confident about the future

Etymology

Blend of 'depressed' and the informal suffix '-oid' (resembling), with 'total' for emphasis.
