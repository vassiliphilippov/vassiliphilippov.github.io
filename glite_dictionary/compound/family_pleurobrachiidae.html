family Pleurobrachiidae

1. NOUN

/ ˌplʊroʊˌbrækiˈɪdiː / (AmE), / ˌplʊərəˌbrækɪˈɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of marine invertebrates known as sea gooseberries, belonging to the phylum Ctenophora.
family of comb jellies (sea gooseberries)

Examples

The family Pleurobrachiidae includes several species of sea gooseberries found in marine environments worldwide.
Scientists are studying the unique locomotion of the family Pleurobrachiidae to better understand ctenophore movement.
Members of the family Pleurobrachiidae are notable for their transparent, globular bodies and ciliary rows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pleurobrachia - A genus of sea gooseberries in the family Pleurobrachiidae

Etymology

From Greek 'pleura' (side) + 'brachion' (arm), referring to features of the type genus Pleurobrachia.
