grapefruit juice

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪpˌfruːt ˌdʒus / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪpˌfruːt ˌdʒuːs / (BrE)
the juice extracted from grapefruits, often consumed as a beverage or used as an ingredient in recipes
juice of grapefruits

Examples

She ordered a glass of grapefruit juice with her breakfast.
Some people like to mix grapefruit juice with sparkling water for a refreshing drink.
It is important to avoid grapefruit juice when taking certain medications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orange juice - juice from oranges
  • Degree Lower: grapefruit - large citrus fruit with a slightly bitter taste
