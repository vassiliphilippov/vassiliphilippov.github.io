Vepsian language

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛpsiən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈvɛpsiən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Finnic language spoken by the Veps people, mainly in northwestern Russia
Finnic language of the Veps people

Examples

Linguists have documented ancient songs and stories written in Vepsian language.
She studied the Vepsian language to understand the oral traditions of the Veps people.
The Vepsian language is considered endangered, with only a few thousand fluent speakers remaining.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Finnish language - the official language of Finland, closely related Finnic language

Etymology

From Veps (the self-designated name of the ethnic group) + English language.
