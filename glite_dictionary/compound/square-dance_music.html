square-dance music

1. NOUN

/ ˈskwɛər dæns ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / ˈskweə dɑːns ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
music composed or performed to accompany square dancing, typically with a rhythm suitable for coordinated group movements
music for square dancing

Examples

The festival featured lively square-dance music that had everyone clapping along.
Traditional square-dance music often includes fiddles, banjos, and energetic rhythms.
He collected rare recordings of vintage square-dance music from the 1940s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: square dancing - a type of folk dance in which four couples form a square and perform set movements
