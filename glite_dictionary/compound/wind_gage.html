wind gage

1. NOUN

/ wɪnd ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / wɪnd ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument for measuring and recording the speed and direction of the wind
instrument measuring wind speed and direction

Examples

The weather station technician calibrated the wind gage before the storm arrived.
By checking the wind gage, sailors could adjust their sails for optimal speed.
The new wind gage on the roof provides real-time data to the meteorology team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anemometer - an instrument for measuring the speed of the wind
