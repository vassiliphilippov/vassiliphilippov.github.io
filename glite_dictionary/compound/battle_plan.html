battle plan

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæt̬.əl ˌplæn / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl ˌplæn / (BrE)
a detailed strategy or plan for conducting a battle or military engagement
military strategy plan

Examples

The general spent the entire night revising the battle plan for the coming offensive.
Each division received its own battle plan before the soldiers moved out at dawn.
The success of the campaign depended on how well the battle plan was executed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strategy - a plan of action designed to achieve a long-term or overall aim
  • Synonym: tactics - specific actions or methods used to achieve a goal
