Victory over Japan Day

1. PROPN

/ ˈvɪktəri oʊvər dʒəˈpæn deɪ / (AmE), / ˈvɪktəri əʊvə dʒəˈpæn deɪ / (BrE)
the day marking the Allied victory over Japan in World War II, observed on 15 August in the UK and 14 August or 2 September in the US
day commemorating Japan's surrender in WWII

Examples

Parades and celebrations were held across the country on Victory over Japan Day in 1945.
The announcement of Victory over Japan Day marked the official end of World War II.
Many veterans remember the emotional scenes that took place on Victory over Japan Day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: V-E Day - the day marking victory in Europe in World War II
  • Synonym: Armistice Day - the day marking the end of World War I
