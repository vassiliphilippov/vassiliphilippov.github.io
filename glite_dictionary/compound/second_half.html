second half

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛk.ənd hæf / (AmE), / ˈsek.ənd hɑːf / (BrE)
The period of play following halftime in a sporting contest, being the latter of two equal parts into which the game is divided.
latter period of play in a game

Examples

The team made a remarkable comeback during the second half of the match.
Both coaches gave motivational speeches at halftime before the second half commenced.
He scored twice in the second half, securing the victory for his team.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: first half - the first of two halves of play
