bank withdrawal

1. NOUN

/ bæŋk wɪðˈdrɔːəl / (AmE), / bæŋk wɪðˈdrɔːəl / (BrE)
an act of taking money out of a bank account
removal of money from a bank account

Examples

She made a bank withdrawal to pay for her new furniture.
The limit for a single bank withdrawal at the ATM is $500.
Frequent bank withdrawals can result in additional fees.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: deposit - an act of putting money into a bank account
