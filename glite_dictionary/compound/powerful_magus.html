powerful magus

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊ.ɚ.fəl ˈmeɪ.ɡəs / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə.fəl ˈmæɡ.əs / (BrE)
a sorcerer with great magical power, typically in fantasy stories or legends
a sorcerer with great magical power

Examples

In the ancient tale, a powerful magus guarded the hidden treasure with his spells.
The villagers sought the help of a powerful magus to end the drought.
Legends speak of a powerful magus who could bend the wind and rain to his will.

Synset Examples

The story featured a powerful magus who could control the elements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sorcerer - a person who claims or is believed to have magical powers
  • Synonym: wizard - a man who has magical powers, especially in legends and stories

Etymology

From Latin 'magus' (magician, wise man), with 'powerful' (having great power) from Old French and Latin origins.
