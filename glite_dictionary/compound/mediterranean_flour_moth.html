Mediterranean flour moth

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən flɔr mɑθ / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən flɔː mɒθ / (BrE)
a small species of moth whose larvae damage stored grain and flour products
a moth pest of stored grain and flour

Examples

The Mediterranean flour moth is a common pest in bakeries and food storage facilities.
Infestations of Mediterranean flour moth larvae can ruin large quantities of stored grain products.
Proper sanitation helps prevent the spread of the Mediterranean flour moth in warehouses.
