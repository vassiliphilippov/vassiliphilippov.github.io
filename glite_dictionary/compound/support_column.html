support column

1. NOUN

/ səˈpɔrt ˈkɑləm / (AmE), / səˈpɔːt ˈkɒləm / (BrE)
A vertical structure designed to bear and transfer weight from above to a foundation below, supporting heavy loads in buildings and other constructions.
vertical structural supporting pillar

Examples

The engineer recommended adding a support column to reinforce the sagging floor.
Several support columns were installed to hold up the new bridge deck.
During the renovation, one support column was found to be severely corroded and needed replacement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pillar - a tall, vertical structure used as a support or decoration
  • Synonym: column - an upright pillar, especially one that supports a structure
