health risk

1. NOUN

/ hɛlθ rɪsk / (AmE), / hɛlθ rɪsk / (BrE)
a factor or situation that can potentially cause harm to a person's health
factor or situation posing danger to health

Examples

Smoking is considered a significant health risk by medical professionals worldwide.
The report highlights the health risks associated with processed foods.
Before approving the new drug, regulators analyzed its potential health risks.

Synset Examples

Asbestos exposure can lead to serious health risks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hazard - something that can cause harm or danger
  • Antonym: health benefit - a factor or situation that promotes good health
