wax plant

1. NOUN

/ wæks plænt / (AmE), / waks plɑːnt / (BrE)
a succulent climbing plant native to southern Asia, with thick, waxy leaves and clusters of pink and white star-shaped flowers, often cultivated as a houseplant
succulent climbing plant with waxy leaves and star-shaped flowers

Examples

The wax plant produces clusters of beautiful, fragrant flowers in the summer.
She keeps a large wax plant in her living room because it thrives indoors.
Gardeners appreciate the wax plant for its easy care and attractive foliage.
