toxic dumpsite

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑksɪk ˈdʌmpˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɒksɪk ˈdʌmpˌsaɪt / (BrE)
a place where toxic waste has been or is disposed of, often illegally or unsafely
site for toxic waste disposal

Examples

Government officials are working to clean up the toxic dumpsite found near the residential area.
The illegal operation created a toxic dumpsite that continues to pollute the river.
Residents protested against the construction of a new toxic dumpsite in their community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: landfill - a site for the disposal of waste materials by burial
  • Synonym: toxic waste - harmful waste material that poses a threat to health or environment
