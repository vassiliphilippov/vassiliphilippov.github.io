king's spear

1. NOUN

/ kɪŋz spɪr / (AmE), / kɪŋz spɪə / (BrE)
a perennial plant, Asphodeline lutea, with a leafy stem and spikes of fragrant yellow flowers, native to southern Europe
a plant with yellow flowers (Asphodeline lutea)

Examples

The tall spikes of king's spear brighten Mediterranean gardens in late spring.
Gardeners prize the king's spear for its fragrant yellow flowers and sturdy growth.
She planted several king's spear plants along the garden path for a splash of color.
