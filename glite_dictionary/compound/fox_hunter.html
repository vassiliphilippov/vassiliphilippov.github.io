fox hunter

1. NOUN

/ fɑks ˈhʌntər / (AmE), / fɒks ˈhʌntə / (BrE)
a person, typically mounted on horseback, who takes part in the sport of hunting foxes with hounds
person who hunts foxes with hounds

Examples

As a dedicated fox hunter, he spent most weekends in the countryside following the hounds.
The tradition of the fox hunter in England dates back several centuries.
She joined the club after moving to the rural village and quickly became an experienced fox hunter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fox hunting - the sport of hunting foxes with hounds, usually on horseback
