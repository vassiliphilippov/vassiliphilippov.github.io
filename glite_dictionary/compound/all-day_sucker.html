all-day sucker

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔlˈdeɪ ˈsʌkər / (AmE), / ˌɔːlˈdeɪ ˈsʌkə / (BrE)
A large, hard candy on a stick that is designed to be consumed slowly.
large hard lollipop-type candy

Examples

As a child, I loved getting an all-day sucker at the fair because it lasted for hours.
The colorful all-day sucker was almost as big as her face.
He handed his daughter an all-day sucker to keep her occupied during the car ride.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lollipop - a sweet, hard candy on a stick
