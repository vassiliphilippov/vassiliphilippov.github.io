Second Advent

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd ædˈvɛnt / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd adˈvɛnt / (BrE)
(in Christian theology) the return of Jesus Christ to earth, when he will judge humanity at the Last Judgment.
the return of Christ for the Last Judgment

Examples

Many Christians believe in the Second Advent as a core tenet of their faith.
The idea of the Second Advent has inspired countless works of art and literature.
Preachers often reference the Second Advent when discussing hopes for the future.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Second Coming - the anticipated return of Christ to judge the world
  • Synonym: Parousia - the theological term for the second coming of Christ

Etymology

From Latin 'adventus' meaning 'arrival' or 'coming', used in Christian tradition.
