hit and giggle

1. NOUN

/ hɪt ənd ˈɡɪɡəl / (AmE), / hɪt ænd ˈɡɪɡəl / (BrE)
an event or activity that is approached in a lighthearted and playful, rather than serious, manner; a fun and relaxed time
lighthearted, playful event or activity
informal

Examples

We decided to make the tennis match just a hit and giggle instead of taking it seriously.
Friday’s meeting turned into more of a hit and giggle than a formal discussion.
Everyone was in a great mood at the hit and giggle after work.
