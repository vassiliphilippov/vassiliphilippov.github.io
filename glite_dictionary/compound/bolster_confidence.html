to bolster confidence

1. VERB

/ ˈboʊlstər kənˈfɪdəns / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlstə kənˈfɪdəns / (BrE)
to make someone's confidence stronger or increase their self-assurance
boost or strengthen confidence

Examples

Taking a few practice tests can bolster confidence before the real exam.
Her encouraging words helped bolster confidence in the team after their loss.
A mentor can bolster confidence by offering constructive feedback and support.

Synset Examples

To bolster his confidence, he practiced his speech several times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: build confidence - to gradually increase self-assurance
  • Antonym: to undermine confidence - to weaken self-assurance or confidence
