showy daisy

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊ.i ˈdeɪ.zi / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.i ˈdeɪ.zi / (BrE)
A plant native to the northwestern United States, having branching leafy stems and solitary, especially showy flower heads with many narrow pink, lavender, or white rays.
plant with showy solitary flower head, NW US

Examples

We spotted a beautiful showy daisy along the trail in the Cascade Mountains.
The garden's border was filled with clusters of showy daisies in full bloom.
Botanists studied the habitat preferences of the showy daisy in the region.
