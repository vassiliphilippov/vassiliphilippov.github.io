evening trumpet flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈivnɪŋ ˈtrʌmpɪt ˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈiːvnɪŋ ˈtrʌmpɪt ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
a poisonous woody evergreen vine of the southeastern United States, noted for its fragrant yellow, funnel-shaped flowers
poisonous vine with yellow funnel-shaped flowers

Examples

The evening trumpet flower thrives in warm, humid climates across the southeastern United States.
Gardeners are drawn to the evening trumpet flower because of its large, fragrant blooms.
Despite its beauty, the evening trumpet flower is poisonous and should be handled with care.
