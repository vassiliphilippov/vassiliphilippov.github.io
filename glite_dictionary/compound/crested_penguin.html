crested penguin

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛstɪd ˈpɛŋɡwɪn / (AmE), / ˈkrɛstɪd ˈpɛŋɡwɪn / (BrE)
a small penguin characterized by yellow crests on the head, native to the Falkland Islands, New Zealand, and surrounding subantarctic islands
small penguin with head crests, native to subantarctic islands

Examples

The crested penguin is easily recognized by the bright yellow feathers above its eyes.
Several species of crested penguin are found on subantarctic islands in the Southern Hemisphere.
A group of crested penguins was spotted gathering on the rocky shores of New Zealand.
