body-build

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːdi bɪld / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi bɪld / (BrE)
the physical constitution or structure of a human body, especially as it relates to shape, size, and strength
physical structure of the body

Examples

Athletes often have a different body-build compared to non-athletes due to intense training.
Her slender body-build makes her well-suited for long-distance running.
The doctor noted that his strong body-build was a positive factor in his recovery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physique - the form, size, and development of a person's body
  • Synonym: build - the shape and size of a person's body
