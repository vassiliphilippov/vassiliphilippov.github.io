Chelsea tractor

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛl.si ˈtræk.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛl.si ˈtræk.tə / (BrE)
A large luxury sport utility vehicle (SUV), especially one used in city or suburban settings rather than for off-road driving.
large luxury SUV, esp. in cities
informal british

Examples

Her new Chelsea tractor barely fits in the narrow city streets.
There's been a surge in the number of Chelsea tractors parked outside cafes.
He traded his old hatchback for a flashy Chelsea tractor.

Synset Examples

His Chelsea tractor is perfect for off-road adventures.
The Chelsea tractor stood out among the compact cars.
They drove their Chelsea tractor to the countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: SUV - sport utility vehicle; a large vehicle often used for both city and off-road driving

Etymology

British slang; refers to the affluent London district of Chelsea, alluding to city dwellers using rural-style vehicles.
