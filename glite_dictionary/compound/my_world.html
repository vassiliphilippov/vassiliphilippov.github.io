my world

1. NOUN

/ maɪ wɜrld / (AmE), / maɪ wɜːld / (BrE)
a person’s own experience, perspective, or set of circumstances, often emphasizing how these differ from others’ or are unique
a person's personal perspective or circumstances

Examples

Since losing my job, my world has turned upside down.
When you become a parent, my world suddenly includes someone else’s needs.
He invited her into my world of books and quiet evenings.

Synset Examples

He sighed, 'Welcome to my world of chaos and fun.'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: point of view - a particular attitude or perspective toward something
  • Synonym: my life - all that happens to someone personally
