sexual conquest

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl kɑːnˌkwɛst / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl ˈkɒŋkwɛst / (BrE)
the act of successfully seducing someone and engaging in sexual intercourse with them, often regarded as an achievement
successful seduction leading to sex

Examples

He bragged to his friends about his latest sexual conquest at the party.
The novel explores the emptiness behind his endless pursuit of sexual conquests.
Some people wrongly equate self-worth with the number of their sexual conquests.

Synset Examples

calling his seduction of the girl a ‘score’ was a typical example of male slang
