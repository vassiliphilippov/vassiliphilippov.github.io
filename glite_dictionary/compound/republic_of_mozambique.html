Republic of Mozambique

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌmoʊzəmˈbiːk / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌməʊzæmˈbiːk / (BrE)
A country on the southeastern coast of Africa, bordering the Mozambique Channel, which became independent from Portugal in 1975.
country in southeastern Africa

Examples

The Republic of Mozambique gained its independence from Portugal in 1975.
Many tourists visit the coastline of the Republic of Mozambique for its natural beauty and wildlife.
Aid organizations are working to improve healthcare infrastructure in the Republic of Mozambique.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mozambique - country in southeastern Africa, also known as the Republic of Mozambique
