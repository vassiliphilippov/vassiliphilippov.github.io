pleasure boat

1. NOUN

/ ˈplɛʒɚ ˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈplɛʒə ˌbəʊt / (BrE)
a large motorboat equipped with a cabin and amenities for living on board, used chiefly for recreation
recreational boat with living amenities

Examples

They spent the summer traveling the coast on their pleasure boat.
The marina was crowded with pleasure boats of all sizes and styles.
After retiring, he bought a pleasure boat to explore the lakes with his family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yacht - a medium-sized sailing or motor-driven vessel used for pleasure trips or racing
  • Synonym: houseboat - a boat that is or can be used as a house
