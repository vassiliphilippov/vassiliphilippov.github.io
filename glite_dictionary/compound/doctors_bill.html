doctor's bill

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑktərz bɪl / (AmE), / ˈdɒktəz bɪl / (BrE)
a statement showing the charges for medical services provided by a doctor
statement of charges for medical services

Examples

I was shocked when I saw how high the doctor's bill was after my surgery.
She paid the doctor's bill with her credit card before leaving the clinic.
Many people worry about how to afford their doctor's bills without insurance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medical bill - statement of charges for medical treatment from any healthcare provider
