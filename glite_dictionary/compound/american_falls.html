American Falls

1. PROPN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən fɔlz / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən fɔːlz / (BrE)
the section of Niagara Falls located in western New York, north of Buffalo, forming part of the US-Canada border.
part of Niagara Falls in New York

Examples

Millions of tourists visit American Falls each year to experience the breathtaking view.
From the observation deck, you can see both the American Falls and the Horseshoe Falls.
The mist rising from American Falls was visible from miles away on the American side.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Horseshoe Falls - the largest section of Niagara Falls, mostly on the Canadian side
  • Synonym: Bridal Veil Falls - the smallest of the three waterfalls that make up Niagara Falls
