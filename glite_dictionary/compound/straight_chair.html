straight chair

1. NOUN

/ streɪt tʃɛr / (AmE), / streɪt tʃɛː / (BrE)
A chair with a straight back and no arms.
straight-backed, armless chair

Examples

He placed a straight chair by the window to sit and read his newspaper.
The antique shop displayed a beautiful oak straight chair from the nineteenth century.
Many dining rooms feature a set of matching straight chairs around the table.
