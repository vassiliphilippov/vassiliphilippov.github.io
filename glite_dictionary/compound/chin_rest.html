chin rest

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪn ˌrɛst / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪn ˌrɛst / (BrE)
A support attached to a violin or viola, on which the player places their chin for comfort and stability while playing.
support for a violinist's chin

Examples

The chin rest on her violin was made of polished ebony.
He had to adjust the chin rest for a better fit before playing.
Some musicians prefer a custom chin rest for added comfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoulder rest - support placed under a violin for shoulder comfort
