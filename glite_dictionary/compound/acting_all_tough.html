to acting all tough

1. VERB

/ ˈæktɪŋ ɔl tʌf / (AmE), / ˈæktɪŋ ɔːl tʌf / (BrE)
pretending to be strong, brave, or intimidating, especially to impress others or hide one's true feelings
pretending to be strong or brave
informal

Examples

Stop acting all tough; we know you're scared, too.
He spends the whole day acting all tough, but he's really kind-hearted.
She was acting all tough in front of her friends after the argument.

Synset Examples

He was acting all tough, but he's really bitch-made.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: put on a brave face - to pretend to be brave or not affected by something
