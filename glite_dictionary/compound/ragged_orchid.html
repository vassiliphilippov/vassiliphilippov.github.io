ragged orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈræɡɪd ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈræɡɪd ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A fringed orchid of the eastern United States, known for its greenish flower with a deeply lacerated lip.
a fringed orchid with lacerated flower lip

Examples

The ragged orchid is most commonly found in moist meadows and woodlands in the eastern United States.
Botanists admire the distinctive fringed appearance of the ragged orchid's greenish flowers.
During spring hikes, I always look for the delicate blooms of the ragged orchid among the wildflowers.
