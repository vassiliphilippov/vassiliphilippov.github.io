the new normal

1. NOUN

/ ðə nu ˈnɔrml̩ / (AmE), / ðə njuː ˈnɔːml / (BrE)
a situation, condition, or behavior that has become typical or expected after a significant change from previous norms
a new standard situation after change

Examples

Remote work is part of the new normal in many industries since the pandemic.
For students, online learning is rapidly becoming the new normal.
Wearing face masks in crowded places is considered the new normal in some countries.

Synset Examples

Working from home has become the new normal for many.
Adapting to the new normal took some time for everyone.
Masks in public places are now the new normal.
