to raise a middle finger

1. VERB

/ reɪz ə ˈmɪdəl ˈfɪŋɡər / (AmE), / reɪz ə ˈmɪdəl ˈfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
to make an obscene gesture by extending the middle finger upright, usually as an insult or expression of contempt
make an obscene hand gesture
vulgar

Examples

When the driver cut him off, he raised a middle finger out the window.
She couldn't help but raise a middle finger at the rude comment.
Teenagers sometimes raise a middle finger as a rebellious gesture.

Synset Examples

She raised her middle finger in frustration at the traffic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to flip the bird - make the same obscene gesture by extending the middle finger
  • Synonym: give the finger - make the same rude gesture with the middle finger
