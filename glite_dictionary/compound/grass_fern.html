grass fern

1. NOUN

/ ɡræs fɜrn / (AmE), / ɡrɑːs fɜːn / (BrE)
An epiphytic fern found in lowland forests of tropical America, known for its grassy appearance.
epiphytic fern of tropical America

Examples

The grass fern thrives in the humid canopies of South American rainforests.
Botanists discovered a rare variety of grass fern during their expedition.
Many epiphytes, such as the grass fern, rely on trees for structural support.
