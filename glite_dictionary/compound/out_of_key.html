out of key

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv ki / (AmE), / aʊt əv kiː / (BrE)
not in the correct musical pitch; out of tune.
not in correct musical pitch

Examples

The soloist was clearly out of key during the difficult section of the song.
If the piano is out of key, the whole band sounds off.
The audience noticed that the opening chords were badly out of key.

Synset Examples

The choir sounded off because they were out of key.
His guitar was slightly out of key for the song.
She was singing out of key during the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of tune - not in musical harmony or correct pitch
  • Antonym: in tune - in correct musical pitch or harmony
