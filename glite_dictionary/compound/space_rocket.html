space rocket

1. NOUN

/ speɪs ˈrɑːkɪt / (AmE), / speɪs ˈrɒkɪt / (BrE)
a rocket that is powerful enough to travel beyond Earth's atmosphere into outer space
rocket designed for travel into space

Examples

The engineers designed a new space rocket to transport astronauts to Mars.
A space rocket must withstand intense heat during launch and re-entry.
The launch of the space rocket was broadcast live around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rocket - a missile or vehicle propelled by rocket engines
