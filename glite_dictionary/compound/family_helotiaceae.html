family Helotiaceae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli hɛˌloʊtiˈeɪsiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli hɛˌləʊtɪˈeɪsiː / (BrE)
A family of fungi in the order Helotiales, typically found in tropical areas, comprising 117 genera and 826 species.
a family of tropical fungi

Examples

Researchers identified several species within the family Helotiaceae in the rainforest soil samples.
Many plant diseases are caused by members of the family Helotiaceae.
The family Helotiaceae contains a large diversity of fungi primarily in tropical regions.

Etymology

From New Latin Helotiaceae, based on the type genus Helotium.
