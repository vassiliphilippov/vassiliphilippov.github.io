electronic dictionary

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk dɪkˈʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk dɪkˈʃənəri / (BrE)
A dictionary available in digital form, usually searchable and accessible via computers or electronic devices, and typically organized alphabetically.
digital version of a dictionary

Examples

Many students find that an electronic dictionary is faster to use than a traditional paper version.
He downloaded an electronic dictionary app to help him learn English vocabulary.
Most modern e-readers include a built-in electronic dictionary for quick reference while reading.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dictionary - a book or digital resource listing words and their meanings
