music department

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪk dɪˌpɑɹtmənt / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪk dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the division of a school, college, or university responsible for teaching music and music appreciation
academic division for music studies

Examples

She applied to the university's music department to pursue a degree in composition.
The music department organized a concert featuring student musicians last night.
Funding cuts have affected many programs, but the music department remains strong thanks to community support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: department - an administrative unit within an institution
