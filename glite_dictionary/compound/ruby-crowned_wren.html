ruby-crowned wren

1. NOUN

/ ˌruːbiːˈkraʊnd ˈrɛn / (AmE), / ˌruːbiːˈkraʊnd ˈrɛn / (BrE)
a small North American songbird, especially the male, which has a distinctive red patch on its crown
small North American songbird with red crown patch

Examples

We spotted a ruby-crowned wren singing cheerfully in the pine grove.
The male ruby-crowned wren displays a brilliant red patch when excited.
Birdwatchers are eager to catch a glimpse of the elusive ruby-crowned wren.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kinglet - small songbird with a brightly coloured crown
