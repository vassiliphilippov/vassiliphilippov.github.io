baked potato

1. NOUN

/ beɪkt pəˈteɪɾoʊ / (AmE), / beɪkt pəˈteɪtəʊ / (BrE)
A potato cooked by baking it in its skin, typically in an oven, until soft inside.
potato cooked by baking in its skin

Examples

She ordered a baked potato with sour cream for lunch.
A baked potato is easy to prepare and makes a hearty side dish.
For dinner, he topped his baked potato with cheese and chives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jacket potato - (British) a potato baked in its skin
