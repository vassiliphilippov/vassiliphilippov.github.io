Serbo-Communist regime

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɜrboʊ kəˈmjunɪst rɪˈʒim / (AmE), / ˌsɜːbəʊ kəˈmjuːnɪst rɪˈʒiːm / (BrE)
A political regime characterized by or associated with Serbo-Communism, typically referring to governments in former Yugoslavia influenced by both Serbian and Communist ideologies.
political regime linked to Serbo-Communism

Examples

Many historians study the political tactics of the Serbo-Communist regime in the 20th century.
Opposition groups criticized the harsh policies implemented by the Serbo-Communist regime.
The fall of the Serbo-Communist regime led to significant political change in the region.

Synset Examples

The Serbo-Communist regime faced widespread opposition.
