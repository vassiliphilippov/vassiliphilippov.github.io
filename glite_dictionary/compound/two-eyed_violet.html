two-eyed violet

1. NOUN

/ ˌtuː.aɪd ˈvaɪ.əl.ət / (AmE), / ˌtjuː.aɪd ˈvaɪ.əl.ət / (BrE)
a violet native to the Pacific coast of North America, having white petals tinged with yellow and deep violet
Pacific North American violet with distinctive petals

Examples

We found a beautiful two-eyed violet growing near the forest trail.
The two-eyed violet is easily recognized by its unique petal coloration.
Gardeners appreciate the two-eyed violet for its vibrant spring flowers.
