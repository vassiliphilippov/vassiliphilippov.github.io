eye candy

1. NOUN

/ aɪ ˈkændi / (AmE), / aɪ ˈkændi / (BrE)
visual content or objects that are attractive or pleasing to look at but are not intellectually stimulating
attractive but superficial visual content
informal

Examples

The presentation was full of eye candy but lacked real information.
This blockbuster movie offers plenty of eye candy for visual effects fans.
While the website is eye candy, it isn't very user-friendly.

Synset Examples

he wanted to put some eye candy on their web site

Related Terms

  • Synonym: window dressing - something added to make things appear attractive without adding value
  • Antonym: substance - the quality of having meaningful content
