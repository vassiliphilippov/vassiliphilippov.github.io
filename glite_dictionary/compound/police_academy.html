police academy

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis əˈkædəmi / (AmE), / pəˈliːs əˈkædəmi / (BrE)
a school or institution where people are trained to become police officers
school for police officer training

Examples

After graduating from the police academy, she was assigned to her first precinct.
The police academy offers rigorous physical and academic training for recruits.
He met several of his lifelong friends while attending the police academy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police college - a college providing education and training for police officers
