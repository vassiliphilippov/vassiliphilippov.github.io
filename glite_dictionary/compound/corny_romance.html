corny romance

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔːrni roʊˈmæns / (AmE), / ˈkɔːni rəʊˈmæns / (BrE)
A romance story, film, or novel that is excessively sentimental or overly emotional, often perceived as lacking originality or subtlety.
overly sentimental romance story or film
informal

Examples

She rolled her eyes at the idea of watching another corny romance on Valentine's Day.
Some people enjoy a corny romance now and then, especially when they're in the mood for something light.
That movie was such a corny romance, but I have to admit I cried at the ending.

Synset Examples

I can't believe you watched that corny romance movie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sentimental - having or showing excessive emotion or tenderness
  • Degree Lower: romantic comedy - a comedy film or story centered on romantic relationships
  • Degree Higher: tearjerker - a story or film designed to evoke sadness or sentimentality

Etymology

From 'corny' (trite, overly sentimental), originally American slang, and 'romance' (a love story).
