brownish-speckled

1. ADJ

/ ˈbraʊnɪʃ ˈspɛkəld / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnɪʃ ˈspɛkəld / (BrE)
Marked with small spots or speckles of a brownish color.
marked with brownish spots

Examples

The brownish-speckled eggs were well camouflaged among the leaves.
She admired the brownish-speckled feathers on the bird's back.
We found a brownish-speckled mushroom growing at the base of the tree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotted - having small spots or marks of a different color
  • Synonym: speckled - covered or marked with many small spots
