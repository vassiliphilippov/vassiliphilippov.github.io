winter squash plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪn.tɚ skwɑʃ plænt / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə skwɒʃ plɑːnt / (BrE)
any of various plants of the species Cucurbita maxima and Cucurbita moschata that produce squashes with hard rinds, harvested in autumn
plant producing hard-rinded winter squashes

Examples

We planted several winter squash plants in the garden to enjoy hearty soups during autumn.
The winter squash plant requires a long growing season to produce its thick-skinned fruits.
She showed me how to distinguish a winter squash plant from its summer squash relatives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squash plant - any plant of the genus Cucurbita producing squashes or gourds
  • Antonym: summer squash plant - plant producing squashes with softer rinds; harvested during summer
