filet de boeuf en croute

1. NOUN

/ fiˌleɪ də ˌbəʊf ɑ̃ ˈkruːt / (AmE), / fiˌleɪ də ˌbəʊf ɑ̃ ˈkruːt / (BrE)
A dish consisting of rare-roasted beef tenderloin coated with mushroom paste and wrapped in puff pastry before baking.
beef tenderloin in pastry

Examples

He ordered the filet de boeuf en croute for his birthday dinner at the upscale restaurant.
The recipe for filet de boeuf en croute requires careful preparation of the pastry and mushroom paste.
At the cooking class, we learned how to prepare a classic filet de boeuf en croute from scratch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beef Wellington - Beef tenderloin with mushroom duxelles and pâté in pastry

Etymology

French, literally 'fillet of beef in crust'.
