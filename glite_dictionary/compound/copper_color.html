copper color

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑpər ˌkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈkɒpə ˌkʌlə / (BrE)
a reddish-brown color resembling that of polished copper metal
a reddish-brown color (like polished copper)

Examples

Her hair gleamed with a striking copper color in the sunlight.
The antique vase was finished in a beautiful copper color that caught everyone's attention.
He chose a car with a unique copper color that stood out from the rest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: russet - a reddish-brown color
  • Synonym: bronze - a brownish metal color similar to copper
