sand dab

1. NOUN

/ ˈsænd dæb / (AmE), / ˈsænd dab / (BrE)
the lean flesh of a small flounder, especially from the Pacific coast of North America, used as food
lean flesh of a small Pacific flounder, as food

Examples

We ordered grilled sand dab at the seafood restaurant by the bay.
The chef recommended the fresh sand dab for its delicate flavor and texture.
Fishermen in California often catch sand dabs during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flounder - a flatfish of several species or the flesh used as food

2. NOUN

/ sænd dæb / (AmE), / sænd dæb / (BrE)
a small, flat fish found along the Pacific coast of North America, valued as a food fish
a small flat fish of the Pacific coast

Examples

Fresh sand dab is a delicacy served in many California seafood restaurants.
Fishermen along the coast catch sand dabs for both sport and food.
We grilled the sand dabs with lemon and herbs for a simple dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dab - a small flatfish of coastal waters
