new adventure

1. NOUN

/ nu ædˈvɛntʃɚ / (AmE), / njuː ədˈvɛntʃə / (BrE)
an exciting or unfamiliar experience that offers novelty or challenges
fresh or exciting experience

Examples

For us, moving to a different city was a new adventure full of uncertainty and excitement.
She always approaches each project as a new adventure to learn and grow.
Traveling with friends makes every trip feel like a new adventure.

Synset Examples

Every single time we meet, it’s a new adventure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adventure - an unusual, exciting, and sometimes dangerous experience
