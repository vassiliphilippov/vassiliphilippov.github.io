green salad

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin ˈsæləd / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈsæləd / (BrE)
A cold dish consisting mainly of raw leafy vegetables such as lettuce, spinach, or arugula, usually served with a dressing.
salad made of leafy greens

Examples

She ordered a green salad as a side for her lunch.
A simple green salad pairs well with grilled chicken.
He tossed a fresh green salad with olive oil and vinegar.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: salad - a cold dish of mixed vegetables, sometimes with added meat, fish, or other ingredients
