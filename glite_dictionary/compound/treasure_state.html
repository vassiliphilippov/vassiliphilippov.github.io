Treasure State

1. PROPN

/ ˈtrɛʒər steɪt / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒə steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for Montana, a state in the northwestern United States bordering Canada
nickname for the state of Montana

Examples

Many tourists are drawn to the Treasure State for its breathtaking national parks and natural beauty.
Montana is often called the Treasure State because of its rich mineral deposits.
She grew up in the Treasure State and takes great pride in her home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Montana - a state in the northwestern US, on the Canadian border

Etymology

Refers to Montana's rich mineral resources discovered in the 19th century.
