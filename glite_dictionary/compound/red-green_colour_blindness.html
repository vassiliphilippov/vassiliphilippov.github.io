red-green colour blindness

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛd ˈɡrin ˈkʌlər ˌblaɪnd.nəs / (AmE), / ˌrɛd ˈɡriːn ˈkʌlə ˌblaɪnd.nəs / (BrE)
a type of color vision deficiency characterized by difficulty distinguishing between red and green hues
inability to distinguish red and green
medical british

Examples

Many people with red-green colour blindness have trouble telling traffic lights apart.
He was diagnosed with red-green colour blindness after failing a vision test at school.
Designers should consider those with red-green colour blindness when choosing color schemes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: color blindness - inability to distinguish some or all colors
