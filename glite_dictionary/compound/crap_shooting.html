crap shooting

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræp ˌʃuːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkræp ˌʃuːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of playing craps, a gambling game involving dice
playing the dice game craps

Examples

He spent the entire night crap shooting in the casino with his friends.
There are strict rules against crap shooting in some establishments.
Many people believe crap shooting is all about luck rather than skill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craps - a gambling game played with two dice

Etymology

From 'crap(s)' (the dice game) + 'shooting' (the act of throwing dice).
