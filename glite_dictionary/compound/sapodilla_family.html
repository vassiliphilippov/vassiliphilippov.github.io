sapodilla family

1. NOUN

/ ˌsæpəˈdɪlə ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˌsæpəˈdɪlə ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
A family of tropical trees or shrubs that produce milky juice and often have edible, fleshy fruit, including the sapodilla.
family of tropical trees or shrubs

Examples

The sapodilla family includes many tropical fruit trees valued for their edible fruit.
Members of the sapodilla family typically have white, milky sap in their stems and leaves.
The chiku fruit belongs to the sapodilla family and is popular in Southeast Asia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sapotaceae - the botanical family name for the sapodilla family
