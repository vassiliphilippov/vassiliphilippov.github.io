size queen

1. NOUN

/ saɪz kwiːn / (AmE), / saɪz kwiːn / (BrE)
a person who has a strong preference for larger sizes, especially in fashion or other contexts
person with strong preference for large sizes
informal slang

Examples

As a size queen, she always chooses the biggest handbag in the store.
He calls himself a size queen when shopping for TVs and monitors.
Everyone knows Mark is a size queen when it comes to choosing cars.
