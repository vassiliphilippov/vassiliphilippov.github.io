reading program

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːdɪŋ ˌproʊɡræm / (AmE), / ˈriːdɪŋ ˌprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
An organized course or set of activities designed to develop or improve literacy skills in learners.
course to improve literacy skills

Examples

The school implemented a new reading program to help struggling students.
Parents are encouraged to enroll their children in the reading program over the summer break.
The reading program focuses on phonics and comprehension skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: literacy program - an educational program aimed at teaching reading and writing
