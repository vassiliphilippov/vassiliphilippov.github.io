webbing clothes moth

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛbɪŋ kloʊðz mɑθ / (AmE), / ˈwɛbɪŋ kləʊðz mɒθ / (BrE)
a small pale moth (Tineola bisselliella) whose larvae spin webs and feed on fabrics, often causing damage to clothes and textiles
moth that damages clothes by web-spinning

Examples

The webbing clothes moth can cause serious damage to wool sweaters stored in closets.
Homeowners often discover holes in their garments after a webbing clothes moth infestation.
Regular cleaning and vacuuming help deter the webbing clothes moth from settling in your home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clothes moth - a moth whose larvae feed on fabrics
