Battle of Marengo

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬.əl əv məˈrɛŋ.goʊ / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl əv məˈrɛŋ.gəʊ / (BrE)
a decisive battle fought in 1800 near Alessandria, Italy, in which Napoleon Bonaparte's French army defeated the Austrians
1800 battle: Napoleon's victory over Austria

Examples

The Battle of Marengo marked a turning point in Napoleon's Italian campaign.
Many historians regard the Battle of Marengo as a testament to Napoleon's military genius.
After the Battle of Marengo, French control over northern Italy was firmly established.
