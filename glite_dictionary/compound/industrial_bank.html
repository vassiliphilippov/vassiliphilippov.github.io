industrial bank

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdʌstriəl bæŋk / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəl baŋk / (BrE)
a financial institution that provides small loans, especially to industrial workers or businesses
financial institution offering small loans

Examples

The workers applied for a loan at the industrial bank to buy new equipment.
An industrial bank often serves those who cannot access traditional commercial banks.
Several local businesses rely on the industrial bank for short-term financing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finance company - business that makes loans to individuals and businesses
