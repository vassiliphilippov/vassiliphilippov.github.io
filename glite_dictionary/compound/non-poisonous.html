non-poisonous

1. ADJ

/ nɑn ˈpɔɪzənəs / (AmE), / nɒn ˈpɔɪzənəs / (BrE)
Not producing, containing, or involving poison; not capable of causing poisoning.
not poisonous; not producing poison

Examples

The berries are non-poisonous and safe to eat.
She made sure the paint was non-poisonous before using it in the nursery.
Many snakes in this region are non-poisonous and not dangerous to humans.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: poisonous - producing or capable of causing poisoning
  • Antonym: toxic - containing or being poisonous material

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'non-' (not) + 'poisonous'.
