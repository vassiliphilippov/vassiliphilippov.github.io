scientific research

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪk rɪˈsɝtʃ / (AmE), / ˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪk rɪˈsɜːtʃ / (BrE)
The systematic investigation and study of materials and sources to establish facts and reach new conclusions in science.
systematic study in science

Examples

She is conducting scientific research on renewable energy sources.
Funding for scientific research is crucial to medical advancement.
The results of the scientific research were published in a leading journal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: research - systematic investigation to discover facts or principles
