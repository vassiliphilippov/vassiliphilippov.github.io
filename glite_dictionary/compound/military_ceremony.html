military ceremony

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəɹi ˈsɛrəˌməʊni / (BrE)
a formal custom or ritual performed by military personnel to mark an event or anniversary
formal ritual in the military

Examples

The parade was a traditional military ceremony honoring the fallen soldiers.
During the military ceremony, medals were presented to distinguished officers.
A trumpet sounded at the end of the military ceremony commemorating the anniversary of the battle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ceremony - a formal event held on a special occasion

Examples

A military ceremony was held at the base to commemorate the unit's anniversary.
