bladder issue

1. NOUN

/ ˈblædər ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈblædə ˈɪʃu / (BrE)
a problem or disorder affecting the urinary bladder, such as infection, irritation, or incontinence
problem affecting the urinary bladder

Examples

After age 50, many men experience bladder issues that affect their quality of life.
The doctor asked if she had any bladder issues such as pain or frequent urination.
Certain medications can help manage bladder issues in older adults.

Synset Examples

She suffered from bladder issues after her pregnancy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urinary tract infection - infection in any part of the urinary system, including the bladder
  • Synonym: incontinence - lack of voluntary control over urination
