movie projector

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvi prəˈdʒɛktɚ / (AmE), / ˈmuːvi prəˈdʒɛktə / (BrE)
a device that projects a series of images from a reel of film onto a screen to create the appearance of moving pictures
device for projecting films

Examples

The old theater kept its original movie projector in perfect working order for classic film nights.
She repaired the broken movie projector so the audience could continue watching the film.
Digital technology has largely replaced the traditional movie projector in modern cinemas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: film projector - device projecting images from a reel of film
