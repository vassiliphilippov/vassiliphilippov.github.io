Mogul empire

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊɡəl ˈɛmˌpaɪr / (AmE), / ˈməʊɡəl ˈɛmˌpaɪə / (BrE)
the empire established by the Mogul (Mughal) conquerors of India, ruling from 1526 to 1857
historic empire in India (1526–1857)

Examples

The Mogul empire was renowned for its rich culture and impressive architectural achievements.
During the height of the Mogul empire, trade flourished across the Indian subcontinent.
The decline of the Mogul empire paved the way for British colonial rule in India.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mughal Empire - historic empire in India ruled by Mughal dynasty

Etymology

From Persian *Mughal*, meaning 'Mongol', referencing the Mongol origin of the dynasty.
