tripe and onions

1. NOUN

/ traɪp ənd ˈʌnjənz / (AmE), / traɪp ən ˈʌnjənz / (BrE)
a traditional dish made from stewed tripe and onions, often regarded as old-fashioned or unappetizing
dish of stewed tripe and onions
british
