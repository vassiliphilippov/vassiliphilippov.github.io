press run

1. NOUN

/ prɛs rʌn / (AmE), / prɛs rʌn / (BrE)
the period or session during which a printing press operates to produce one issue of a newspaper or a batch of printed materials.
printing session for a publication

Examples

The newspaper had to extend its press run due to high demand for the special edition.
Mistakes discovered during the press run led to a costly recall of the printed copies.
They finished the press run early this morning to meet the delivery deadline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: printing - the process of producing text and images, especially with a printing press
  • Synonym: edition - a particular version or printing of a publication
