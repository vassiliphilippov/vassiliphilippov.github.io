elevator boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛl.əˌveɪ.tɚ bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛl.ɪˌveɪ.tə bɔɪ / (BrE)
a man, typically a young man, employed to operate an elevator, especially in a hotel or office building
person who operates an elevator

Examples

The hotel elevator boy greeted every guest with a polite smile.
As an elevator boy in the city, he became familiar with all the building's tenants.
In the early twentieth century, being an elevator boy was a common job for young men.

Synset Examples

in England they call an elevator man a ‘liftman’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liftman - a man employed to operate a lift (British English)
