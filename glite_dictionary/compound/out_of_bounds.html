out of bounds

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv baʊndz / (AmE), / aʊt əv baʊndz / (BrE)
not within the permitted limits or area; not allowed or inappropriate
not within permitted limits; not allowed

Examples

The soccer ball was declared out of bounds and play stopped immediately.
Certain topics are out of bounds during the interview process.
This staircase is out of bounds for students without staff supervision.

Synset Examples

His behavior was out of bounds at the meeting.
The kids were playing out of bounds.
That area is out of bounds for visitors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forbidden - not allowed; prohibited
  • Antonym: permissible - allowed by rules or law; permitted
