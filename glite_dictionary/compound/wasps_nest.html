wasps' nest

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑːsps ˌnɛst / (AmE), / ˈwɒsps ˌnɛst / (BrE)
a structure built by wasps or hornets as a place to live and raise their young
a habitation built by wasps or hornets

Examples

We found a large wasps' nest under the eaves of the roof.
A wasps' nest can be dangerous if disturbed, so it's best to call a professional.
Last summer, there was a wasps' nest in the garden shed that we had to remove.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beehive - a hive built by bees as their habitation
