toilet bowl

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔɪlət ˌboʊl / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlɪt ˌbəʊl / (BrE)
the large, basin-like part of a toilet that receives waste and can be flushed with water
basin part of a toilet

Examples

He dropped his keys into the toilet bowl by accident.
It's important to clean the toilet bowl regularly for hygiene reasons.
The plumber replaced the cracked toilet bowl with a new one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toilet - a fixture used for defecation and urination
