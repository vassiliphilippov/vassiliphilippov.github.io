son of a bitch

1. NOUN

/ ˌsʌn əv ə ˈbɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˌsʌn əv ə ˈbɪtʃ / (BrE)
an offensive and vulgar insult used to refer to someone regarded as stupid, irritating, or contemptible
offensive insult for an irritating or contemptible person
offensive vulgar

Examples

I can’t believe that son of a bitch lied to me again.
She called him a son of a bitch after the argument.
He muttered ‘son of a bitch’ under his breath when the car wouldn’t start.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bastard - offensive term for a contemptible person
