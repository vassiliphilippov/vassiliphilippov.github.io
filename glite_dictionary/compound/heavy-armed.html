heavy-armed

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛvi ˈɑrmd / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi ˈɑːmd / (BrE)
(of a person) having large, muscular, or massive arms.
having large or muscular arms

Examples

The boxer was a heavy-armed opponent, intimidating everyone with his sheer strength.
Her heavy-armed build made her an exceptional swimmer and athlete.
He stood at the door, heavy-armed and broad-shouldered, filling the frame.

Synset Examples

he was big-chested, big-shouldered and heavy-armed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muscular - having well-developed muscles
  • Antonym: slender-armed - having slender or thin arms
