mountain chain

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊn.tən tʃeɪn / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪn tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a series of mountains or hills connected by high ground, often forming a continuous elevated line
connected series of mountains or hills

Examples

The Andes are the longest mountain chain in the world.
A great mountain chain separates the two countries.
The birds migrated across the vast mountain chain each spring.

Synset Examples

the valley was between two ranges of hills
the plains lay just beyond the mountain range

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mountain range - a line or group of mountains connected by high ground
