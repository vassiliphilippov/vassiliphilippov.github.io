white-pink

1. ADJ

/ waɪt pɪŋk / (AmE), / waɪt pɪŋk / (BrE)
(of a color) pink tinged with white; a very light shade of pink.
very light shade of pink

Examples

She chose a white-pink dress for the summer wedding.
The delicate petals were a beautiful shade of white-pink.
The artist mixed paints to achieve the perfect white-pink hue for her portrait.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: pink - a pale red color
  • Degree Lower: white - the lightest color, like fresh snow
