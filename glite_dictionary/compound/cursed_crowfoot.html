cursed crowfoot

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɜrsɪd ˈkroʊˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈkɜːsɪd ˈkrəʊˌfʊt / (BrE)
An annual herb that grows in marshy places, commonly known for its distinctive leaves and small flowers.
an annual herb of marshy places

Examples

The wetland was filled with cursed crowfoot blooming along the water’s edge.
Botanists studied the spread of cursed crowfoot in the marshland ecosystem.
Farmers sometimes regard cursed crowfoot as a troublesome weed in their fields.
