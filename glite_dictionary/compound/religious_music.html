religious music

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)

music for religious ceremonies

Examples

The choir performed beautiful religious music during the Sunday service.
Many composers have written religious music as expressions of their faith.
He specializes in the study of medieval religious music and its influence on modern hymns.
