mountain hemlock

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntən ˈhɛmlək / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˈhɛmlək / (BrE)
a large evergreen tree (Tsuga mertensiana) native to the western United States and Canada, with wood much harder than that of the Canadian hemlock
a North American evergreen tree species

Examples

The mountain hemlock grows best at higher elevations in the Pacific Northwest.
Several hikers admired the tall mountain hemlock standing by the trail.
Lumber from the mountain hemlock is valued for its hardness and durability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hemlock - a type of evergreen coniferous tree or a poisonous herb
