print seller

1. NOUN

/ prɪnt ˈsɛlɚ / (AmE), / prɪnt ˈsɛlə / (BrE)
a person or business that sells prints, such as etchings, engravings, or other artworks.
person or shop selling art prints

Examples

The print seller displayed rare engravings from the 18th century in his gallery.
She became a well-known print seller in the art district.
Many print sellers attend art fairs to connect with collectors and artists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: art dealer - person who buys and sells works of art
