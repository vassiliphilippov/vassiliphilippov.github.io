racy thriller

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪsi ˈθrɪlər / (AmE), / ˈreɪsi ˈθrɪlə / (BrE)
A suspenseful story or film that is both exciting and slightly provocative, often featuring daring or risqué elements.
provocative and exciting suspense story

Examples

The bestselling author is known for her racy thriller novels that keep readers on the edge of their seats.
He picked up a racy thriller from the library for his holiday reading.
Movie lovers flocked to see the director's latest racy thriller, praising its fast pace and daring plot.

Synset Examples

She loves racy thrillers full of suspense.

Related Terms
