experimental procedure

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˌspɛrəmɛnˈtæl prəˈsiːdʒər / (AmE), / ɪkˌspɛrɪmɛnˈtæl prəˈsiːdʒə / (BrE)
the specific methods or techniques employed in carrying out a scientific experiment
methods used in a scientific experiment

Examples

The experimental procedure must be described in detail to allow other scientists to replicate the study.
Before beginning the research, she reviewed the entire experimental procedure for accuracy and safety.
Different results may occur if the experimental procedure is not followed precisely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protocol - a detailed plan of a scientific experiment or medical treatment
