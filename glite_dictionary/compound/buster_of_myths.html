buster of myths

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌstər əv mɪθs / (AmE), / ˈbʌstə əv mɪθs / (BrE)
a person who challenges and disproves widely held but false beliefs or ideas
someone who debunks false beliefs

Examples

As a buster of myths, she never hesitates to correct popular misconceptions.
The documentary features a renowned buster of myths who exposes health fads.
He became known as the office buster of myths for always fact-checking rumors.

Synset Examples

She's the buster of myths in our friend group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: debunker - a person who exposes the falseness of an idea or belief
  • Degree Lower: myth - a widely held but false belief or idea
