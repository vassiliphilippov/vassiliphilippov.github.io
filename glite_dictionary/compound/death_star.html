death star

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛθ stɑr / (AmE), / ˈdɛθ stɑː / (BrE)
A powerful or dominant organization, project, or entity, often regarded negatively or as a threat by others.
dominant or threatening entity

Examples

The tech giant's new app is seen as a death star destined to crush its rivals.
Many employees feared that the merger would create a corporate death star in the industry.
Startups struggle to survive when a major player launches its own death star solution.

Synset Examples

They referred to the new project as the team's death star.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: juggernaut - a huge, powerful, and overwhelming force or institution
  • Antonym: underdog - a competitor with little chance to win against a dominant entity

Etymology

From the fictional space station 'Death Star' in the Star Wars film series, symbolizing overwhelming power.
