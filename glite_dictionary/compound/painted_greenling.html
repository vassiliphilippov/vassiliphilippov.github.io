painted greenling

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪntɪd ˈɡriːnlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪd ˈɡriːnlɪŋ / (BrE)
a small fish with a whitish body marked with black bands, found in coastal waters of the northeastern Pacific Ocean
a Pacific fish with black bands

Examples

We spotted a painted greenling hiding among the rocks during our snorkeling trip.
The aquarium featured a display with several painted greenlings swimming in a large tank.
Marine biologists often study the coloration patterns of the painted greenling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greenling - a fish belonging to the family Hexagrammidae
