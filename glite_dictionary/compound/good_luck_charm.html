good luck charm

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd lʌk tʃɑrm / (AmE), / ɡʊd lʌk tʃɑːm / (BrE)
an object that is believed to bring good luck to its owner
object believed to bring good luck

Examples

She carries a good luck charm in her pocket wherever she goes.
Many athletes have a good luck charm they believe will help them win.
He gave me a rabbit's foot as a good luck charm before my exam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talisman - an object thought to have magical powers and bring good fortune
  • Synonym: amulet - an ornament or small object worn as a charm against evil or injury
