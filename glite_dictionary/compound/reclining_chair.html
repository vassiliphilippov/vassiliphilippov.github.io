reclining chair

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈklaɪnɪŋ tʃɛr / (AmE), / rɪˈklaɪnɪŋ tʃɛə / (BrE)
an armchair with a back that can be tilted backward and a footrest that can be raised, allowing the user to recline
armchair with adjustable back and footrest

Examples

He spent the afternoon reading in his favorite reclining chair by the window.
We bought a new reclining chair for the living room to add comfort and style.
The reclining chair adjusts easily for maximum relaxation after a long day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recliner - an armchair that reclines when the sitter lowers the back and raises the footrest
