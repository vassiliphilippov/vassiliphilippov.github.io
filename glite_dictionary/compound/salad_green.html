salad green

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæləd ɡrin / (AmE), / ˈsæləd ɡriːn / (BrE)
a leafy vegetable suitable for eating raw in salads
leafy vegetable for salads

Examples

She bought fresh salad greens from the farmers' market for tonight's dinner.
A mix of salad greens adds color and flavor to your dish.
Wash your salad greens thoroughly before eating them raw.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lettuce - a common type of salad green
  • Synonym: spinach - another leafy vegetable often used as a salad green
