flood lamp

1. NOUN

/ flʌd læmp / (AmE), / flʌd lamp / (BrE)
A powerful lamp that produces a broad beam of light, used for illuminating large areas or in photography.
lamp with a broad, powerful beam

Examples

The photographer adjusted the flood lamp to eliminate shadows in the portrait.
They installed a new flood lamp above the garage to light up the driveway at night.
A single flood lamp illuminated the outdoor stage during the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: floodlight - a broad-beamed, high-intensity artificial light
  • Antonym: spotlight - a lamp projecting a narrow, intense beam of light
