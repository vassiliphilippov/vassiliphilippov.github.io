torrent of rain

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔːrənt əv reɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɒrənt əv reɪn / (BrE)
a sudden and very heavy downpour of rain
very heavy rainfall

Examples

A torrent of rain battered the windows, making it nearly impossible to see outside.
We were caught in a torrent of rain on our way home from school.
The festival was cancelled after a torrent of rain flooded the entire park.

Synset Examples

A torrent of rain flooded the streets last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: downpour - a heavy, continuous fall of rain
