religious rite

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlɪdʒəs raɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs raɪt / (BrE)
a formal ceremony or ritual prescribed or practiced as part of a religion
formal religious ceremony

Examples

They attended the religious rite that marked the beginning of the festival.
A baptism is a religious rite observed in many Christian denominations.
The priest explained the meaning behind each step of the religious rite.

Synset Examples

the rite of baptism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ritual - a religious or solemn ceremony consisting of a series of actions performed according to a prescribed order
