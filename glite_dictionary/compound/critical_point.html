critical point

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪd.ɪ.kəl pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈkrɪt.ɪ.kəl pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a moment of crisis or a situation when an important decision must be made
moment of crisis or decision

Examples

The negotiations reached a critical point when neither side was willing to compromise.
After months of uncertainty, the company hit a critical point in its restructuring plan.
We are approaching a critical point in the talks where a decision must be made.

Synset Examples

at that juncture he had no idea what to do
he must be made to realize that the company stands at a critical point

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crisis - a time of intense difficulty or danger
  • Synonym: turning point - a moment when a significant change occurs
