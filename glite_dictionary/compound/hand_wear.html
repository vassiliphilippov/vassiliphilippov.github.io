hand wear

1. NOUN

/ hænd wɛr / (AmE), / hænd weə / (BrE)
clothing items worn on the hands for protection, warmth, or fashion, such as gloves or mittens
clothing for the hands, like gloves

Examples

Winter hand wear is essential for keeping your fingers warm in the snow.
Many workers are required to put on protective hand wear before entering the laboratory.
She chose elegant lace hand wear to complement her wedding dress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glove - a close-fitting covering for the hand
  • Synonym: mitten - a type of hand covering with a separate section for the thumb
