prettification process

1. NOUN

/ ˌprɛt̬ɪfɪˈkeɪʃən ˈprɑːsɛs / (AmE), / ˌprɛtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən ˈprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
the process of making something more attractive or pleasing in appearance
process of making something more attractive

Examples

The city's prettification process transformed a drab square into a lively public space.
They initiated a prettification process to improve the office's aesthetic appeal before the company rebranding.
The prettification process involved new landscaping, fresh paint, and decorative lighting throughout the neighborhood.

Synset Examples

Prettification of the presentation made it more appealing.
The city's prettification process transformed the old park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beautification - the process of making something beautiful

Etymology

From 'prettification' (from 'prettify') and 'process'. 'Prettify' comes from 'pretty' + '-fy' (to make).
