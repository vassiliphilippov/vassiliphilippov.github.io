sense of fullness

1. NOUN

/ sɛns əv ˈfʊlnəs / (AmE), / sɛns əv ˈfʊlnəs / (BrE)
the feeling of being full or satisfied after eating, often resulting in a lack of desire to eat more
feeling of being full after eating

Examples

Many people experience a sense of fullness after eating high-fiber foods.
He ate quickly and did not notice his sense of fullness until later.
The medication can cause a prolonged sense of fullness during meals.

Synset Examples

She felt a sense of fullness after the big meal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: satiety - the feeling or state of being sated or satisfied (especially with food)
