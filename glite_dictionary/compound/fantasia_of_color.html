fantasia of color

1. NOUN

/ fænˈteɪʒiə əv ˈkʌlɚ / (AmE), / fænˈteɪzɪə əv ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
a striking and imaginative display of vibrant colors
vivid display of colors

Examples

The artist's new painting is a true fantasia of color, drawing visitors from across the city.
Spring in the valley revealed a fantasia of color across the hillsides.
The intricate stained-glass windows create a fantasia of color when sunlight pours through them.

Synset Examples

The art exhibit felt like a fantasia of color and creativity.

Etymology

From Italian 'fantasia' (imagination, fantasy) + English 'of color'.
