supraorbital ridge

1. NOUN

/ ˌsuːprəˈɔːrbɪtl rɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˈɔːbɪtəl rɪdʒ / (BrE)
a prominent ridge of bone on the frontal bone above each eye socket
ridge of bone above the eye socket
medical

Examples

The ancient skull displayed a pronounced supraorbital ridge above each eye socket.
Scientists often examine the supraorbital ridge to study human evolution and ancestry.
He suffered a minor fracture near his supraorbital ridge after the accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brow ridge - ridge of bone above the eyes; another term for supraorbital ridge
  • Degree Lower: frontal bone - bone that forms the forehead and upper part of the eye sockets

Etymology

From Latin supra- (above) + orbita (orbit, eye socket).
