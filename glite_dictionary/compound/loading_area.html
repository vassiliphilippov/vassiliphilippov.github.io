loading area

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊdɪŋ ˌɛəriə / (AmE), / ˈləʊdɪŋ ˌeəriə / (BrE)
a designated place where vehicles are loaded or unloaded with goods or passengers
place for loading or unloading vehicles

Examples

The truck driver parked in the loading area to deliver the shipment.
Only authorized vehicles are allowed in the loading area behind the store.
A security guard monitors the loading area to prevent theft during deliveries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loading bay - an area in a building where goods vehicles are loaded or unloaded
