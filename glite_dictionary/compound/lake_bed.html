lake bed

1. NOUN

/ leɪk bɛd / (AmE), / leɪk bɛd / (BrE)
the ground or surface at the bottom of a lake
the bottom surface of a lake

Examples

Scientists discovered ancient fossils preserved in the lake bed.
The drought caused the water level to drop, exposing the cracked lake bed.
Sediment slowly settles on the lake bed over many years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: riverbed - the bottom of a river
  • Synonym: seabed - the bottom of the sea or ocean
