wage hike

1. NOUN

/ weɪdʒ haɪk / (AmE), / weɪdʒ haɪk / (BrE)
an increase in the amount of salary or wages paid to employees
an increase in wages or salary

Examples

After months of negotiations, the workers agreed to a 5% wage hike.
Many teachers are demanding a wage hike to keep up with the rising cost of living.
The company announced a wage hike for all its hourly employees starting next month.

Synset Examples

he got a 3% raise
he got a wage hike

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raise - an increase in one's salary
  • Antonym: pay cut - a reduction in wages or salary
