notion of success

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊʃən əv səkˈsɛs / (AmE), / ˈnəʊʃən əv səkˈsɛs / (BrE)
the particular idea or belief that someone has about what it means to be successful
belief or idea about success

Examples

Her notion of success involves balancing a fulfilling career with a happy family life.
Each generation seems to have its own notion of success, shaped by social values and economic conditions.
The company's notion of success prioritizes innovation and customer satisfaction over profit alone.

Synset Examples

His notion of success differs from mine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concept of success - the general idea or principle of what success means
