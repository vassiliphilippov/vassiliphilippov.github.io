firefighting technique

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪərˌfaɪtɪŋ tɛkˈniːk / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌfaɪtɪŋ tɛkˈniːk / (BrE)
a method or procedure used to extinguish or control fires
method to extinguish fires

Examples

Improved firefighting technique allowed the crew to suppress the blaze more effectively.
Every fire department trains its staff in at least one specialized firefighting technique.
The seminar focused on the latest firefighting techniques for tackling wildfires.

Synset Examples

Firefighting techniques have improved significantly over the years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firefighting - the act of extinguishing fires
  • Synonym: method - a way of doing something
