Republic of Indonesia

1. PROPN

/ rəˈpʌblɪk əv ˌɪndəˈniʒə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌɪndəˈniːziə / (BrE)
the official name of Indonesia, a country in Southeast Asia consisting of thousands of islands, with Jakarta as its capital
official name of Indonesia; Southeast Asian country

Examples

The Republic of Indonesia declared its independence from the Netherlands in 1945.
Jakarta is the capital city of the Republic of Indonesia.
The Republic of Indonesia is the world's largest archipelagic state.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Indonesia - country in Southeast Asia and Oceania, known as the Republic of Indonesia
