wee small voice

1. NOUN

/ wi smɔl vɔɪs / (AmE), / wiː smɔːl vɔɪs / (BrE)
a quiet sense of conscience or inner moral guidance that judges one's actions or decisions
inner voice of conscience

Examples

Despite the pressure to cheat, his wee small voice held him back.
Whenever I tell a lie, my wee small voice makes me feel guilty.
She tried to ignore the wee small voice that told her it was wrong.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conscience - a person's moral sense of right and wrong, viewed as acting as a guide
