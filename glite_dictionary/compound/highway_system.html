highway system

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪˌweɪ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌweɪ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A network of major roads designed for the movement of motor vehicles, typically connecting cities and regions.
network of major motor roads

Examples

The new highway system reduced travel times between cities across the country.
A well-maintained highway system is crucial for economic growth and public safety.
After the flood, parts of the highway system were closed for repairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: road network - a connected system of roads
  • Antonym: railway system - a transportation system consisting of railroads for trains
