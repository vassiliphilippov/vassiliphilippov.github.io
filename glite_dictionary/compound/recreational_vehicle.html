recreational vehicle

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃənl ˈviːɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃənəl ˈviːɪkl̩ / (BrE)
A large motor vehicle equipped with living space and amenities for recreational travel and camping.
motor vehicle for camping/travel

Examples

They spent the summer traveling the country in their recreational vehicle.
A recreational vehicle is perfect for family road trips and exploring new places.
The campsite offered special hookups for guests arriving in a recreational vehicle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: RV - abbreviation of 'recreational vehicle'
  • Degree Lower: camper van - a smaller vehicle designed for camping, often without full amenities
  • Synonym: motorhome - a large, fully equipped recreational vehicle for living and traveling
