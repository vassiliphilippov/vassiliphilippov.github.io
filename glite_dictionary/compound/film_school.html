film school

1. NOUN

/ fɪlm skuːl / (AmE), / fɪlm skuːl / (BrE)
an educational institution where students learn about filmmaking, including production, theory, digital media, and screenwriting.
institution for filmmaking studies

Examples

After graduating from film school, she directed her first short movie.
Many aspiring directors attend film school to learn both theory and practical skills.
He teaches screenwriting at a prestigious film school in Los Angeles.

Synset Examples

I cannot help wondering why people choose to go to film school in the face of abundant evidence that the most talented and successful filmmakers in history made their own destiny by eschewing film school and building a reel instead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: film academy - an institution for advanced study in film and media arts
  • Synonym: art school - an educational institution specializing in the visual arts
