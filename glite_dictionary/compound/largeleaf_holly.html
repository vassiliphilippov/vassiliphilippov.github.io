largeleaf holly

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑrdʒˌlif ˈhɑli / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdʒˌliːf ˈhɒli / (BrE)
a species of holly tree with notably large leaves, often cultivated as an ornamental plant
holly tree with large leaves

Examples

The largeleaf holly is prized for its deep green foliage and bright red berries.
Gardeners often choose largeleaf holly to create attractive hedges and borders.
Birds are attracted to the largeleaf holly for its abundant berries in winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holly - a tree or shrub with prickly leaves and red berries
