trash heap

1. NOUN

/ træʃ hiːp / (AmE), / træʃ hiːp / (BrE)
a pile or accumulation of refuse, garbage, or discarded material
pile of rubbish or discarded material

Examples

Flies buzzed around the overflowing trash heap behind the building.
After cleaning the garage, he threw all the broken tools onto the trash heap.
The city council ordered the old trash heap to be cleared for safety reasons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rubbish heap - a pile of unwanted material or refuse
  • Synonym: garbage dump - a place where refuse is dumped
