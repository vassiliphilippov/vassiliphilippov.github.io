global initiative

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡloʊ.bəl ɪˈnɪʃ.ə.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊ.bəl ɪˈnɪʃ.ə.tɪv / (BrE)
a coordinated project or action designed to address problems or challenges affecting the whole world
project to address worldwide issues

Examples

The United Nations launched a global initiative to combat climate change.
Many countries are participating in the global initiative to eradicate polio worldwide.
A new global initiative was unveiled to improve access to clean water in developing nations.

Synset Examples

As a globularist, he supports global initiatives.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: international initiative - project or effort involving multiple countries but not necessarily global
