cylindrical-stemmed

1. ADJ

/ sɪˈlɪndrɪkəl stɛmd / (AmE), / sɪˈlɪndrɪkəl stɛmd / (BrE)
having a stem that is shaped like a cylinder; possessing a round, tube-like stem
having a cylindrical stem

Examples

Researchers observed several cylindrical-stemmed species in the rainforest ecosystem.
A cylindrical-stemmed structure is typical of many grasses and bamboo.
