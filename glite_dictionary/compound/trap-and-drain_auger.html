trap-and-drain auger

1. NOUN

/ træp ənd dreɪn ˈɔgər / (AmE), / træp ənd dreɪn ˈɔːɡə / (BrE)
a plumbing tool consisting of a flexible, coiled wire used to clear blockages from traps and drains
plumbing tool for clearing drains

Examples

The plumber used a trap-and-drain auger to clear the stubborn clog in the kitchen sink.
Before calling for help, she tried a trap-and-drain auger to fix the slow bathroom drain.
A trap-and-drain auger is essential in any homeowner's toolkit for dealing with minor pipe blockages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plumber's snake - a flexible wire used to clear drain blockages
