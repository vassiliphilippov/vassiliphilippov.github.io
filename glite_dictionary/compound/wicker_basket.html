wicker basket

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪkər ˈbæskət / (AmE), / ˈwɪkə ˈbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
a basket woven from pliable twigs, reeds, or similar materials
basket made from woven wicker

Examples

She filled the wicker basket with fresh apples from the orchard.
A wicker basket sat in the corner, overflowing with laundry to be folded.
They brought a wicker basket to the picnic, packed with sandwiches and fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basket - a container used to hold or carry things, typically with handles and made of rigid material
