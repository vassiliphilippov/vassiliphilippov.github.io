Rob Roy

1. NOUN

/ rɑb ˈrɔɪ / (AmE), / rɒb ˈrɔɪ / (BrE)
a cocktail made with Scotch whisky, sweet vermouth, and bitters, similar to a Manhattan but using Scotch instead of rye or bourbon
cocktail with Scotch, vermouth, and bitters

Examples

She ordered a Rob Roy at the bar because she preferred Scotch over bourbon.
The classic Rob Roy is garnished with a cherry or a twist of lemon peel.
He learned to make a Rob Roy during his bartending course.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Manhattan - cocktail made with whiskey, sweet vermouth, and bitters
  • Degree Lower: Old Fashioned - whiskey cocktail with bitters and sugar

Etymology

Named after the Scottish folk hero Rob Roy MacGregor.
