jewelry store

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuəlri stɔr / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːəlri stɔː / (BrE)
A shop or business that sells and often buys jewelry such as rings, necklaces, and watches.
shop selling and buying jewelry

Examples

She bought her engagement ring from the local jewelry store.
The jewelry store reopened after renovations with an impressive new collection.
A thief broke into the jewelry store during the night but was quickly caught by the police.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jeweler - a person or business that makes, sells, or repairs jewelry
