place of worship

1. NOUN

/ pleɪs əv ˈwɜrʃɪp / (AmE), / pleɪs əv ˈwɜːʃɪp / (BrE)
a building or location where people gather to perform religious ceremonies or pray
building for religious gatherings

Examples

The town has several places of worship for different religious communities.
Visitors are welcome to the place of worship during service hours.
People gathered at the local place of worship to celebrate the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: church - Christian building for public worship
  • Synonym: mosque - Muslim place of worship
  • Synonym: temple - Building for religious worship in some faiths
