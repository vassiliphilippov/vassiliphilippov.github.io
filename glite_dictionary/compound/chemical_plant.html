chemical plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛmɪkəl plænt / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl plɑːnt / (BrE)
an industrial facility where chemicals are manufactured or processed on a large scale
industrial facility for manufacturing chemicals

Examples

The new chemical plant was built near the river to facilitate easy transportation of raw materials.
Stringent safety measures are essential in any chemical plant to prevent accidents and exposure to hazardous substances.
Hundreds of jobs were created when the chemical plant expanded its production lines last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: factory - a building or group of buildings where goods are manufactured
  • Degree Lower: pharmaceutical plant - an industrial facility that produces pharmaceutical products
