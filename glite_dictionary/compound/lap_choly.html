lap choly

1. NOUN

/ læp ˈkoʊli / (AmE), / læp ˈkəʊli / (BrE)
An informal abbreviation for a laparoscopic cholecystectomy, the surgical removal of the gall bladder using small incisions and a laparoscope.
surgical removal of gall bladder via laparoscopy
informal medical

Examples

The surgeon scheduled a lap choly for the patient with gallstones.
After my lap choly, I was able to go home the same day.
Nurses explained what to expect before and after a lap choly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laparoscopic cholecystectomy - surgical removal of the gall bladder using a laparoscope

Etymology

Shortened from 'laparoscopic cholecystectomy.'
