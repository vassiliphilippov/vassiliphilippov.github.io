lower jawbone

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.ər ˈdʒɔːˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.ə ˈdʒɔːbəʊn / (BrE)
the bone forming the lower part of the jaw in vertebrates, hinged to open and close the mouth; the mandible
lower part of the jaw; the mandible

Examples

The dentist examined her lower jawbone for signs of infection.
Fossil records reveal that the shape of the lower jawbone varies among species.
He fractured his lower jawbone in the accident and needed surgery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mandible - the lower jawbone, especially in vertebrates
