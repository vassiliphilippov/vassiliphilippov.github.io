Yi language

1. NOUN

/ ji ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ji ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Loloish language spoken by the Yi people in China, Vietnam, and Thailand.
language of the Yi people, China/Southeast Asia

Examples

The Yi language has its own unique syllabic script that is still used today.
Many researchers study the Yi language to better understand the region's cultural history.
Efforts are being made to preserve the Yi language as younger generations shift toward Mandarin.
