baseball coach

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl koʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl kəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a person who trains and directs a baseball team or its players
person who coaches a baseball team

Examples

After retiring as a player, he became a successful baseball coach at his alma mater.
The baseball coach called for a team meeting before practice.
Our school's baseball coach emphasizes teamwork and discipline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coach - person who trains sports players or a team
