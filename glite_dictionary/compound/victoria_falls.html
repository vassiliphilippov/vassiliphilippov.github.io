Victoria Falls

1. PROPN

/ vɪkˈtɔriə fɔlz / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːriə fɔːlz / (BrE)
A large waterfall on the Zambezi River located at the border between Zimbabwe and Zambia, known for its impressive width and seasonal changes in water flow.
waterfall on the Zambezi River, Africa

Examples

Tourists from around the world travel to see the breathtaking Victoria Falls every year.
The spray from Victoria Falls can be seen from miles away during the rainy season.
Local communities near Victoria Falls depend on tourism for their livelihoods.

Etymology

Named after Queen Victoria of England by explorer David Livingstone in 1855.
