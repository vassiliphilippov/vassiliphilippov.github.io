space heater

1. NOUN

/ ˈspeɪs ˌhiːtər / (AmE), / ˈspeɪs ˌhiːtə / (BrE)
A self-contained, usually portable, device used to heat a single room or a small area.
portable device for heating a room

Examples

We brought in a space heater to keep the living room warm during the cold snap.
The old building has poor insulation, so each office has its own space heater.
Be sure to turn off the space heater before leaving the house for safety reasons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heater - a device that generates heat
