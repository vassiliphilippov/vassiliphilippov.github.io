Selkup language

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛl.kʊp ˌlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.kʊp ˌlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Uralic language spoken by the Selkup people of northern Siberia
Uralic language of the Selkup people

Examples

Linguists are working to preserve the endangered Selkup language.
She wrote her thesis on the grammar of the Selkup language.
Children in some Siberian villages are learning the Selkup language at school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Uralic languages - a family of languages spoken across northern Eurasia

Etymology

From the name Selkup, which refers to the indigenous people of northern Siberia who speak the language.
