Seyhan River

1. PROPN

/ ˈseɪ.hɑn ˈrɪv.ər / (AmE), / ˈseɪ.hæn ˈrɪv.ə / (BrE)
A river in southern Turkey that flows southwest into the Mediterranean Sea.
river in southern Turkey

Examples

The Seyhan River is an important source of water for the city of Adana.
After heavy rains, the Seyhan River sometimes overflows its banks.
Many migratory birds can be spotted along the Seyhan River during spring.
