dark comedy

1. NOUN

/ dɑrk ˈkɑmədi / (AmE), / dɑːk ˈkɒmədi / (BrE)
A type of comedy that uses grim or satirical humor and incorporates themes or elements that are disturbing, bleak, or taboo.
comedy with grim or satirical humor

Examples

The film is a classic example of dark comedy, blending murder with witty dialogue.
Many viewers appreciate the way dark comedy tackles taboo subjects with humor and sensitivity.
His stand-up routine often crosses into dark comedy, addressing depression and mortality with sharp wit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black comedy - comedy that makes light of serious or taboo subjects
