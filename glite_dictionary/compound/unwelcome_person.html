unwelcome person

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈwɛlkəm ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛlkəm ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is not wanted or not accepted in a particular place or group
someone not wanted or accepted

Examples

After the incident, he became an unwelcome person at the club.
Making rude remarks will make you an unwelcome person at most gatherings.
She felt like an unwelcome person in her own home after the argument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: persona non grata - a person who is not acceptable or welcome
