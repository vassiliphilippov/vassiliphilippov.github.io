electrical work

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl wɝk / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl wɜːk / (BrE)
the installation, maintenance, or repair of electrical systems and equipment, typically performed by an electrician
work involving electrical systems

Examples

The company specializes in electrical work for residential and commercial buildings.
He hired a professional to ensure all the electrical work met safety standards.
After the renovation, some electrical work still needed to be completed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electrician - a person who installs and maintains electrical equipment
