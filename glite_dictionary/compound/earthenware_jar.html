earthenware jar

1. NOUN

/ ˈɝːθənˌwɛr dʒɑr / (AmE), / ˈɜːθənˌweə dʒɑː / (BrE)
A jar made of earthenware (baked clay), typically used for storing food or liquids.
jar made of baked clay

Examples

She found an earthenware jar filled with olives in the pantry.
The archaeologists carefully lifted the ancient earthenware jar from the excavation site.
He stored the homemade pickles in a large earthenware jar to keep them fresh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pottery - objects made of fired clay
  • Synonym: ceramic jar - jar made from any ceramic material

Etymology

From 'earthenware' (baked clay pottery) + 'jar', likely from Portuguese or Spanish 'jarra'.
