green tea

1. NOUN

/ ɡriːn tiː / (AmE), / ɡriːn tiː / (BrE)
Tea made from leaves that have been steamed and dried without fermentation, resulting in a light color and delicate flavor.
type of unfermented tea

Examples

Many people prefer to drink green tea for its refreshing taste and health benefits.
She bought a box of jasmine green tea from the specialty store.
Scientists have studied the antioxidants found in green tea extensively.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: black tea - tea made from fully fermented tea leaves
