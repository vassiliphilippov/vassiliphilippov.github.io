family Peronosporaceae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli pəˌroʊnoʊspɔːˈreɪsiˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli pəˌrəʊnɒspɒˈriːʃiiː / (BrE)
A family of parasitic fungi commonly known as downy mildews, which infect a variety of plants.
family of downy mildew fungi

Examples

The grape crop was severely affected by family Peronosporaceae, leading to a major loss.
Many important agricultural pathogens belong to the family Peronosporaceae.
Researchers are studying the life cycle of fungi in the family Peronosporaceae to develop better plant treatments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: downy mildew - a fungal-like disease affecting plants
  • Synonym: Peronospora - a genus of fungi in the family Peronosporaceae

Etymology

From Neo-Latin 'Peronosporaceae', based on the type genus 'Peronospora'.
