yours truly

1. X

/ jʊrz ˈtruːli / (AmE), / jɔːz ˈtruːli / (BrE)
used as a polite formula at the end of a letter or email before the signature, often in formal or semi-formal correspondence.
conventional letter closing

Examples

Thank you for your time and consideration, yours truly.
If you have any further questions, please contact me. Yours truly, Emily.
It was wonderful working with you this year. Yours truly, John Smith.

Synset Examples

I always enjoy our chats, yours trulies.
Best wishes for your future, yours truly.
It was a pleasure meeting you, yours truly.
I hope to hear from you soon, yours truly.
