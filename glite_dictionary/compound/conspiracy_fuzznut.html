conspiracy fuzznut

1. NOUN

/ kənˈspɪrəsi ˈfʌzˌnʌt / (AmE), / kənˈspɪrəsi ˈfʌzˌnʌt / (BrE)
An informal, often dismissive or mocking term for a person who believes in conspiracy theories.
informal term for a conspiracy theorist
informal slang

Examples

He dismissed her as just another conspiracy fuzznut ranting about secret organizations.
The internet has given conspiracy fuzznuts a place to share their wild ideas.
At the party, he got stuck talking to a conspiracy fuzznut who believed the moon landing was faked.
