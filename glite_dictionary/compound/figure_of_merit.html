figure of merit

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪɡjɚ əv ˈmɛrɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡə əv ˈmɛrɪt / (BrE)
a numerical value that expresses the efficiency or effectiveness of a system, material, or process, often used for comparison purposes
measure of efficiency or effectiveness

Examples

The scientist calculated the figure of merit to compare the performance of different materials.
A high figure of merit indicates that the new battery is more efficient than existing models.
This device has a better figure of merit than its competitors in terms of energy consumption.
