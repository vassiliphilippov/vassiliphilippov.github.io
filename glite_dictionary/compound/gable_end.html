gable end

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪbəl ɛnd / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪbəl end / (BrE)
The vertical triangular wall at the end of a building, between the sloping sides of a gable roof.
triangular wall at the end of a gabled roof

Examples

The old cottage has a beautiful stone gable end facing the lane.
They decided to install a large window in the gable end for more light.
The mural was painted directly onto the gable end of the building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gable - the part of a wall that encloses the end of a pitched roof
