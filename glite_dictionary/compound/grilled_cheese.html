grilled cheese

1. NOUN

/ ɡrɪld tʃiz / (AmE), / ɡrɪld tʃiːz / (BrE)
A hot sandwich made by grilling bread with cheese as the main filling, typically until the bread is toasted and the cheese is melted.
hot sandwich with melted cheese filling

Examples

For lunch, I made a classic grilled cheese with tomato soup.
She ordered a grilled cheese at the café because it's her favorite comfort food.
The smell of grilled cheese always reminds me of my childhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheese toastie - British term for a similar hot sandwich with melted cheese

2. NOUN

/ ɡrɪld tʃiz / (AmE), / ɡrɪld tʃiːz / (BrE)
a hot sandwich consisting of melted cheese between slices of bread, grilled or fried until golden brown
hot sandwich with melted cheese

Examples

I made a grilled cheese for lunch with a side of pickles.
At the café, their grilled cheese comes with a cup of homemade soup.
Nothing beats a classic grilled cheese on a cold, rainy day.
