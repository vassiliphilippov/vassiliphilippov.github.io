raisin bread

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪzən brɛd / (AmE), / ˈreɪzɪn brɛd / (BrE)
bread made with raisins mixed into the dough, often eaten sliced and toasted
bread made with raisins

Examples

I like to eat raisin bread with butter for breakfast.
Freshly baked raisin bread filled the kitchen with a sweet aroma.
She made French toast using thick slices of raisin bread.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fruit bread - bread containing dried or candied fruit
