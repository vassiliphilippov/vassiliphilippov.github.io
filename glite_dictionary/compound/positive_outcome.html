positive outcome

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑzətɪv ˈaʊtkʌm / (AmE), / ˈpɒzətɪv ˈaʊtkʌm / (BrE)
a result or consequence that is favorable or desirable
favorable result or consequence

Examples

The company is hoping for a positive outcome from the negotiations.
After months of hard work, they finally achieved a positive outcome in the project.
Counseling can often lead to a positive outcome for individuals struggling with stress.

Synset Examples

The meeting ended trimmingly with positive outcomes.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: negative outcome - an unfavorable or undesirable result
