governor's race

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌvərnərz ˌreɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvənəz ˌreɪs / (BrE)
an election campaign in which candidates compete to become the governor of a state or territory
election for governor

Examples

The governor's race attracted national attention due to its close polling numbers.
Several candidates announced their intention to run in the upcoming governor's race.
Turnout was especially high in the state during last year's governor's race.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gubernatorial race - an election for a governor; alternative term
