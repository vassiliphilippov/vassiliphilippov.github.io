hiring process

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪɹɪŋ ˌprɑsɛs / (AmE), / ˈhaɪərɪŋ ˌprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
the series of steps an organization follows to recruit and select candidates for employment
steps for recruiting and selecting employees

Examples

The hiring process at this company includes several rounds of interviews and background checks.
He was frustrated by how long the hiring process took after submitting his application.
Improving the hiring process can help organizations attract better candidates.

Synset Examples

The hiring process can be lengthy and complicated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recruitment - the act of finding new people to join an organization or support a cause
  • Antonym: layoff - the act of ending an employee's job, often for economic reasons
