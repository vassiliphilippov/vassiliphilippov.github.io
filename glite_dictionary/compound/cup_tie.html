cup tie

1. NOUN

/ kʌp taɪ / (AmE), / kʌp taɪ / (BrE)
a match played between teams as part of an elimination round in a cup competition, especially in football (soccer)
elimination match in a cup competition
british

Examples

Manchester United will play Chelsea in the cup tie next Saturday.
Thousands of fans attended the cup tie to support their local team.
The unexpected defeat in the cup tie ended their hopes of winning the tournament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knockout match - an elimination match where the loser is removed from the competition
