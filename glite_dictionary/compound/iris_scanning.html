iris scanning

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪrɪs ˌskænɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaɪərɪs ˌskænɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of identifying a person by scanning the unique patterns of the iris in the eye
biometric identification by scanning the iris

Examples

The airport implemented iris scanning for passenger identification and security.
Many smartphones now offer iris scanning as a secure way to unlock the device.
Concerns about privacy have been raised regarding the use of iris scanning in public spaces.

Synset Examples

biometric technologies such as iris scanning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retina scanning - biometric identification by scanning the retina
