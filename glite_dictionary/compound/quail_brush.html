quail brush

1. NOUN

/ ˈkweɪl ˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈkweɪl ˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
A spiny shrub with silvery, scurfy foliage, found on alkaline plains in the southwestern United States and Mexico.
spiny shrub of southwestern US and Mexico

Examples

The quail brush thrives in arid, alkaline soils where few other plants can survive.
After hiking for miles, we finally spotted a covey of quail hiding beneath a quail brush.
Botanists are studying the adaptation mechanisms of quail brush to harsh desert climates.
