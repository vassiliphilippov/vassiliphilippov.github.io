clock golf

1. NOUN

/ klɑk ɡɑlf / (AmE), / klɒk ɡɒlf / (BrE)
A game in which players putt golf balls from points arranged like the numbers on a clock face around a hole.
recreational golf putting game

Examples

They set up a clock golf course on the lawn for the afternoon party.
Children enjoy playing clock golf because it is easy to learn and fun for groups.
The hotel advertised amenities like croquet and clock golf for its summer guests.
