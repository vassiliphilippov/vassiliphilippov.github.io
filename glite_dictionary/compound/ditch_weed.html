ditch weed

1. NOUN

/ dɪtʃ wiːd / (AmE), / dɪtʃ wiːd / (BrE)
Wild marijuana or cannabis plants that grow naturally and are usually considered of low quality or undesirable.
wild, low-quality marijuana plant

Examples

He was surprised to spot ditch weed along the edge of the cornfield.
Authorities clarified that ditch weed contains little to no psychoactive substance.
Some farmers find ditch weed spreading quickly despite their best efforts to control it.

Synset Examples

He found some ditch weed growing in his backyard.
Ditch weed is often seen as a nuisance by gardeners.

Related Terms
