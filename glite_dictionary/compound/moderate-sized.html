moderate-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɑdərət saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈmɒdərət saɪzd / (BrE)
Of intermediate or average size; neither large nor small.
of intermediate size

Examples

We chose a moderate-sized car for our family vacation.
The office has a moderate-sized conference room that seats about 12 people.
She bought a moderate-sized house in the suburbs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medium-sized - of average or moderate size
  • Degree Higher: large - of great size
  • Degree Lower: small - of a little size
