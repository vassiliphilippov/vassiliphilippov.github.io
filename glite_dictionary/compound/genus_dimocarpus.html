genus Dimocarpus

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs dɪˈmɑː.kɑːrpəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs dɪˈmɒ.kɑːpəs / (BrE)
A genus of tropical trees in the family Sapindaceae, which includes the longan fruit.
genus of tropical trees (longan family)

Examples

The genus Dimocarpus includes trees that bear the popular longan fruit.
Botanists recently discovered a new species in the genus Dimocarpus in Southeast Asia.
Research into the genus Dimocarpus has improved our understanding of tropical fruit trees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: longan - An edible fruit produced by Dimocarpus species

Etymology

From New Latin 'Dimocarpus', describing the joined (dimo-) and fruit (-carpus) features.
