borky design

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔːrki dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɔːki dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
A design that is confusing, poorly functioning, or unreliable, typically in software or digital products.
confusing or poorly functioning design
informal slang

Examples

The borky design of the app made it hard to navigate.
Users complained that the website’s borky design caused frequent errors and frustration.
We need to avoid a borky design if we want customers to stay on our platform.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buggy - containing many errors or glitches
  • Antonym: reliable design - design that functions well and as intended

Etymology

From 'bork,' a slang term meaning to break or malfunction, possibly imitating the sound of a malfunction or influenced by the word 'broke.'
