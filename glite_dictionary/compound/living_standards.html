living standards

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌstændɚdz / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌstændədz / (BrE)
The level of material comfort and wealth available to a person, group, or society, measured by the quantity and quality of goods and services they can access.
level of material comfort and wealth

Examples

Rising living standards have increased life expectancy across the region.
Many people moved to the city in search of better living standards.
The government's new policies aim to improve living standards for low-income families.

Synset Examples

they enjoyed the highest standard of living in the country
the lower the standard of living, the easier it is to introduce an autocratic production system

Related Terms

  • Synonym: standard of living - the level of wealth and material comfort in a community
