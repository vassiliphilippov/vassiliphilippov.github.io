guest night

1. NOUN

/ ɡɛst naɪt / (AmE), / ɡɛst naɪt / (BrE)
An evening event at a club or college when members are allowed to bring friends as guests.
evening event for bringing guests

Examples

The annual guest night at the club was always well attended by members and their friends.
She invited her roommate to the college's guest night to experience the formal dinner.
On guest night, alumni are encouraged to bring colleagues who may be interested in joining.
