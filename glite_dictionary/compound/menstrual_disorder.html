menstrual disorder

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛnstɹuəl dɪsˈɔɹdɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɛnstruːəl dɪsˈɔːdə / (BrE)
any abnormal condition affecting the menstrual cycle, such as irregular, painful, or missed periods
abnormal condition affecting menstruation
medical

Examples

Many women seek medical advice when they experience menstrual disorders such as heavy bleeding or missed periods.
Stress and lifestyle changes can contribute to the development of menstrual disorders.
Treatment for menstrual disorders often depends on the underlying cause and individual health needs.

Synset Examples

Menstrual disorders can affect women of all ages.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: amenorrhea - absence of menstruation
  • Synonym: dysmenorrhea - painful menstruation
  • Degree Higher: menorrhagia - abnormally heavy menstrual bleeding
