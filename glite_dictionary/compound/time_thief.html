time thief

1. NOUN

/ taɪm θiːf / (AmE), / taɪm θiːf / (BrE)
something or someone that consumes your time without resulting in productivity; a distraction or source of wasted time.
something that wastes your time

Examples

Checking your email every five minutes is a major time thief during the workday.
Television can be a time thief if you're not careful with your schedule.
Meetings without a clear agenda often become a time thief for the whole team.

Synset Examples

Social media can be a real time thief if you let it.
He realized that distractions were the biggest time thief in his life.
Procrastination is the ultimate time thief for students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distraction - something that takes your attention away
  • Synonym: time-waster - something that wastes time
