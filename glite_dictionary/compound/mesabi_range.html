Mesabi Range

1. PROPN

/ məˈsɑbi reɪndʒ / (AmE), / məˈsɑːbi reɪndʒ / (BrE)
a range of hills in northeastern Minnesota, USA, known for rich iron ore deposits discovered in 1887
iron ore range in Minnesota

Examples

The Mesabi Range played a crucial role in the development of the American steel industry.
She visited the Mesabi Range to learn about its historical iron ore mining operations.
Vast reserves of high-grade iron ore were once mined from the Mesabi Range.

Etymology

From the Ojibwe word *Misaabe-wajiw* ('Giant's Mountain'), reflecting the indigenous origin of the name.
