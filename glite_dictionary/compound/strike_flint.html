to strike flint

1. VERB

/ straɪk flɪnt / (AmE), / straɪk flɪnt / (BrE)
to produce a spark by hitting a piece of flint, especially in order to start a fire
to create a spark by hitting flint

Examples

The campers had to strike flint several times before a spark finally caught.
In the old days, people would strike flint to light their lanterns.
She learned how to strike flint during the outdoor survival course.

Synset Examples

He struck flint to start the fire.
