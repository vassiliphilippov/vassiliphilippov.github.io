waterskin

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌskɪn / (BrE)
a bag or container made from animal skin, used for holding and carrying water
container made from skin for water

Examples

The traveler drank from his waterskin before continuing his journey across the desert.
She filled the waterskin at the stream, preparing for the long hike ahead.
Ancient warriors often carried a small waterskin tied to their belts.
