liqueur glass

1. NOUN

/ lɪˈkɜr ɡlæs / (AmE), / lɪˈkjʊə ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a small glass used to serve a small amount of liqueur, typically after dinner
small glass for serving liqueur

Examples

She poured a golden liqueur into a delicate liqueur glass after dinner.
Each guest received a beautifully cut liqueur glass filled with amaretto.
The antique set included four liqueur glasses with intricate designs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shot glass - a small glass for serving strong alcoholic drinks in shots
