hurt feelings

1. NOUN

/ hɝt ˈfiːlɪŋz / (AmE), / hɜːt ˈfiːlɪŋz / (BrE)
emotional pain or distress caused by someone's words or actions
emotional pain or distress

Examples

I'm sorry if my comments hurt feelings at the meeting yesterday.
The criticism from his boss really hurt feelings and made him doubt his abilities.
She tried to avoid saying anything that might hurt feelings during the discussion.

Synset Examples

His scathing review hurt the artist's feelings deeply.

Related Terms
