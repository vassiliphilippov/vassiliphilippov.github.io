no-go area

1. NOUN

/ ˌnoʊ ˈɡoʊ ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˌnəʊ ˈɡəʊ ˌeəriə / (BrE)
an area that is considered too dangerous or is legally forbidden for people to enter
dangerous or forbidden area

Examples

The police warned reporters not to enter the no-go area because of ongoing violence.
Parts of the city have become a no-go area for emergency services after dark.
Without proper security, the old warehouse district has turned into a no-go area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restricted area - an area where entry is limited by law or regulation
  • Antonym: safe haven - a place where people are protected from danger
