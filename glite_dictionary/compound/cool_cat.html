cool cat

1. NOUN

/ kuːl kæt / (AmE), / kuːl kat / (BrE)
A person who is stylish, confident, or relaxed, often admired for their composure or sense of style.
stylish or relaxed person
informal

Examples

After his flawless performance, everyone agreed he was a real cool cat on stage.
She's the kind of cool cat who never seems to get flustered under pressure.
Back in the day, jazz musicians were known as cool cats for their smooth style and attitude.

Synset Examples

She walked in like a cool cat, turning heads.
He's such a cool cat, always calm and collected.
Everyone wants to be friends with that cool cat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hipster - a person who follows the latest trends and fashions, especially in a non-mainstream way
  • Antonym: nerd - a person seen as boring or unfashionable, opposite of 'cool cat'

Etymology

Popularized in 1940s-50s American jazz circles, using 'cool' (stylish, composed) plus 'cat' (a person).
