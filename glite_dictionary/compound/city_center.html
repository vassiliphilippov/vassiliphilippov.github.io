city center

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪti ˈsɛntər / (AmE), / ˈsɪti ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
the central and most important part of a city, often containing the main shops, offices, and public buildings
the central area of a city

Examples

The city center is always bustling with people during the weekend.
We booked a hotel in the city center so we could walk to all the main attractions.
Shops and restaurants in the city center usually stay open late.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: downtown - the main business district of a city, especially in American English
  • Antonym: suburb - an outlying residential district of a city
