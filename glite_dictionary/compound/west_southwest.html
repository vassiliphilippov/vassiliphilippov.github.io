west southwest

1. NOUN

/ wɛst saʊθˈwɛst / (AmE), / wɛst saʊθˈwɛst / (BrE)
The compass point midway between west and southwest, corresponding to a direction of 247.5° on a compass.
compass point between west and southwest

Examples

The wind today is coming from the west southwest, bringing cooler air.
They set their course towards the west southwest to reach the island by sunset.
On the map, the village lies directly west southwest of the capital.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: southwest - the compass point between south and west
  • Degree Lower: west - the compass point opposite east
