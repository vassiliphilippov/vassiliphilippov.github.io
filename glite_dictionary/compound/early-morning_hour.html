early-morning hour

1. NOUN

/ ˈɜrli ˈmɔrnɪŋ aʊr / (AmE), / ˈɜːli ˈmɔːnɪŋ aʊə / (BrE)
an hour occurring during the early part of the morning, typically around dawn or shortly after
an hour early in the morning

Examples

She likes to take long walks during the early-morning hour before the city wakes up.
He caught the train in the early-morning hour to avoid traffic.
The bakery opens in the early-morning hour to prepare fresh bread for the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dawn - the first appearance of light in the sky before sunrise
