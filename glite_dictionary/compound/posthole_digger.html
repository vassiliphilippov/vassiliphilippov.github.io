posthole digger

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstˌhoʊl ˈdɪɡər / (AmE), / ˈpəʊsthəʊl ˈdɪɡə / (BrE)
a tool with two blades or scoops used for digging narrow, deep holes for fence posts or similar structures
tool for digging postholes

Examples

He used a posthole digger to make holes for the new fence posts.
Our gardening shed has a posthole digger hanging on the wall.
Before setting up the sign, they borrowed a posthole digger from the neighbor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shovel - tool for digging or lifting material
