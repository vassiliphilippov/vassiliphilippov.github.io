wash leather

1. NOUN

/ wɑʃ ˈlɛðɚ / (AmE), / wɒʃ ˈlɛðə / (BrE)
a soft, absorbent piece of chamois leather used for cleaning and polishing surfaces
chamois leather for cleaning

Examples

He used a wash leather to clean the windows until they shone.
Always dry your car with a wash leather for a streak-free finish.
The wash leather is soft and gentle, making it perfect for polishing delicate surfaces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chamois leather - soft, pliable leather made from the skin of the chamois or sheep, especially used for cleaning and polishing
