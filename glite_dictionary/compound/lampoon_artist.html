lampoon artist

1. NOUN

/ læmˈpun ˈɑrtɪst / (AmE), / læmˈpuːn ˈɑːtɪst / (BrE)
a cartoonist who creates parodies or satirical illustrations of cultural, social, or political situations
satirical or parody cartoonist

Examples

The lampoon artist used clever drawings to criticize government policies in the satirical magazine.
During the exhibition, a famous lampoon artist presented biting parodies of celebrity culture.
As a lampoon artist, she often pushed the boundaries of political correctness with her cartoons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: satirist - a person who uses satire in writing or art
  • Synonym: cartoonist - an artist who draws cartoons, especially those that are humorous or satirical
  • Synonym: caricaturist - an artist who specializes in exaggerated portraits, often for satire or humor

Etymology

Lampoon from French 'lampoon', meaning a satirical poem, and artist from Old French 'artiste'.
