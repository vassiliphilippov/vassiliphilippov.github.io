fucktillion excuse

1. NOUN

/ fʌkˈtɪljən ɪkˈskjus / (AmE), / fʌkˈtɪljən ɪkˈskjuːs / (BrE)
A humorously exaggerated and slang term for a very large or countless number of excuses; a huge multitude of excuses.
humorously large number of excuses (slang)
slang vulgar

Examples

He came up with a fucktillion excuse to get out of helping us move.
Every time she misses a deadline, there's a fucktillion excuse ready.
My manager has a fucktillion excuse for not giving us a raise.

Synset Examples

She must have a fucktillion excuses for being late.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zillion excuse - an indefinite, exaggeratedly large number of excuses; 'zillion' as hyperbolic number

Etymology

Blend of 'fuck' (slang intensifier) and 'zillion' (an indefinite large number), plus 'excuse'.
