method of choice

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛθəd əv tʃɔɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɛθəd əv tʃɔɪs / (BrE)
the preferred or most effective method for achieving a desired result, especially among available alternatives
the preferred or most effective method

Examples

For chronic pain management, acupuncture is sometimes considered the method of choice.
PCR has become the method of choice for detecting viral infections in laboratories.
The committee debated which method of choice would yield the most reliable results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preferred method - a method regarded as better than others
  • Degree Lower: method - a particular procedure for accomplishing or approaching something
