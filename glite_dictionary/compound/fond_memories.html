fond memories

1. NOUN

/ fɑnd ˈmɛməriz / (AmE), / fɒnd ˈmɛməriz / (BrE)
pleasant and affectionate recollections of the past, often accompanied by a sense of nostalgia
pleasant nostalgic recollections

Examples

Seeing old photographs always brings back fond memories of my childhood.
They shared fond memories of their university days at the reunion.
When I visit my hometown, I am filled with fond memories of family gatherings.

Synset Examples

The holiday jingle brought back fond memories.
Grandma's kugel always brings back fond memories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: happy memories - recollections of joyful past experiences
  • Antonym: painful memories - recollections of distressing or hurtful past experiences
