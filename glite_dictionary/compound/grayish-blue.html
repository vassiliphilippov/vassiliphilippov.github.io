grayish-blue

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪɪʃ bluː / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪɪʃ bluː / (BrE)
Of a blue color tinged with grey; between blue and grey in appearance.
bluish color tinged with grey

Examples

The sky had a grayish-blue hue just before sunrise.
She painted her bedroom walls a soothing grayish-blue color.
He wore a grayish-blue sweater that matched his eyes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slate-blue - a greyish-blue color, resembling the color of slate
  • Synonym: bluish-grey - grey tinged with blue; similar shade on the spectrum
