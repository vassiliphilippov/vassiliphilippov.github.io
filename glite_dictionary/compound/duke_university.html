Duke University

1. PROPN

/ duːk juːnəˈvɝːsəti / (AmE), / djuːk juːnɪˈvɜːsəti / (BrE)
a private university located in Durham, North Carolina, known for its academic excellence and research programs
private university in Durham, North Carolina

Examples

My sister will be attending Duke University this fall to study biomedical engineering.
Duke University is renowned for its strong basketball program and competitive academic environment.
Several groundbreaking medical studies have been conducted at Duke University in recent years.

Etymology

Named after James Buchanan Duke, a benefactor whose endowment supported the university's expansion in the early 20th century.
