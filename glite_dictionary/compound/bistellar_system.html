bistellar system

1. NOUN

/ baɪˈstɛlɚ ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / baɪˈstelə ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A star system composed of exactly two stars orbiting each other.
star system with two stars

Examples

The astronomers observed a bistellar system through the new telescope.
A newly found bistellar system challenges existing theories about star formation.
Light from the bistellar system flickered as the two stars eclipsed each other.

Synset Examples

Astronomers discovered a bistellar system with two stars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: binary star - two stars orbiting a common center of mass

Etymology

From Latin 'bi-' (two) + 'stellar' (of stars), meaning a system with two stars.
