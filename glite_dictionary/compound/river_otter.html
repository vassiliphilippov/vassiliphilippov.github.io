river otter

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪvɚ ˌɑtɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɪvə ˌɒtə / (BrE)
a semi-aquatic mammal of North America, known for its playful behavior and found along rivers and lakeshores
North American aquatic mammal

Examples

A river otter slipped into the water, leaving only ripples behind.
Children laughed as they watched a river otter play with a stone near the shore.
You might spot a river otter if you visit the lakes at dawn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: otter - a carnivorous mammal that lives in water, related to weasels
