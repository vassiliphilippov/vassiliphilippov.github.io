science laboratory

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪəns ˌlæbɚˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈsaɪəns ˌlæbərətəri / (BrE)
A room or building equipped for scientific experiments, research, or teaching.
room for scientific experiments or research

Examples

The students conducted chemistry experiments in the science laboratory.
A new science laboratory was built for advanced physics research.
Proper safety procedures are essential when working in a science laboratory.
