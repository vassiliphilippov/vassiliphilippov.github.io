West Point

1. PROPN

/ wɛst pɔɪnt / (AmE), / wɛst pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the site of the United States Military Academy, a United States Army installation on the west bank of the Hudson River, north of New York City
site of the US Military Academy

Examples

After graduating from West Point, she began her career as an officer in the U.S. Army.
Many famous generals, including Dwight D. Eisenhower, studied at West Point.
Admission to West Point is highly competitive and requires a congressional nomination.

Related Terms
