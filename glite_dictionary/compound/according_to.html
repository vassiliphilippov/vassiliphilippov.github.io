according to

1. ADP

/ əˈkɔrdɪŋ tu / (AmE), / əˈkɔːdɪŋ tʊ / (BrE)
as stated, reported, or indicated by someone or something; as per someone's opinion or information
as stated by someone/something

Examples

According to the weather forecast, it will rain tomorrow.
According to local tradition, this church is over 500 years old.
According to her doctor, the treatment was a complete success.
