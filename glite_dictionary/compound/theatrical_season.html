theatrical season

1. NOUN

/ θiˈætrɪkəl ˈsiːzən / (AmE), / θɪˈætrɪkəl ˈsiːzn̩ / (BrE)
the period of the year when theatres present new plays and performances
period when new plays are staged

Examples

Every autumn, the city launches its theatrical season with a major festival of new plays.
The director announced the lineup for the upcoming theatrical season at the local playhouse.
Many actors look forward to the theatrical season as a chance to work on fresh productions.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: season - a period during which a particular activity takes place
