hidden treasure

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪdən ˈtrɛʒər / (AmE), / ˈhɪdən ˈtrɛʒə / (BrE)
valuable objects that have been concealed and are not easily found
valuables that are concealed or hard to find

Examples

For years, historians searched the old castle for any sign of hidden treasure.
The attic was full of dusty boxes and even a hidden treasure or two.
Children love books about pirates in search of hidden treasure.
