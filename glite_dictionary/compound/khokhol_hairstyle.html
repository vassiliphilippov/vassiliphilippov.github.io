khokhol hairstyle

1. NOUN

/ xoʊˈxoʊl ˈhaɪrstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈxɒxɒl ˈheəstaɪl / (BrE)
a traditional Ukrainian hairstyle featuring a lock of hair left on an otherwise shaved head
traditional Ukrainian hairstyle

Examples

The khokhol hairstyle was commonly worn by Ukrainian Cossacks in the 17th century.
He chose to recreate the khokhol hairstyle for a folk festival.
Books on Ukrainian history often depict the famous khokhol hairstyle in illustrations.

Synset Examples

The khokhol hairstyle is quite traditional.

Etymology

From Ukrainian 'khokhol', a term for a traditional lock of hair worn by Cossacks.
