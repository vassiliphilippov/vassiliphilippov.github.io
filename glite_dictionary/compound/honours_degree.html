honours degree

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːnərz dɪˈɡriː / (AmE), / ˈɒnəz dɪˈɡriː / (BrE)
A university degree awarded with honors, typically indicating a higher standard of achievement than a standard degree.
university degree with honors

Examples

He graduated with an honours degree in biology from Oxford University.
Obtaining an honours degree usually requires higher grades and a research project.
She proudly received her honours degree during the commencement ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ordinary degree - a university degree without honors
