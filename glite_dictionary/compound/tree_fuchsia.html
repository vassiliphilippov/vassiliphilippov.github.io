tree fuchsia

1. NOUN

/ tri ˈfjuːʃə / (AmE), / triː ˈfjʊʃə / (BrE)
An erect deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand, noted for its maroon flowers.
New Zealand shrub or tree with maroon flowers

Examples

The tree fuchsia adds vibrant color to many New Zealand gardens with its maroon blossoms.
Gardeners are increasingly interested in cultivating the tree fuchsia for its decorative appeal.
We admired a tall tree fuchsia growing beside the forest trail during our hike.
