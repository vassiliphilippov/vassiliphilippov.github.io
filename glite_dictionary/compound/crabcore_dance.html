crabcore dance

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræbˌkɔr dæns / (AmE), / ˈkrabˌkɔː dɑːns / (BrE)
A style of dance characterized by energetic, crouched movements, typically associated with the crabcore subgenre of metalcore music.
energetic dance linked to crabcore music

Examples

The band encouraged the crowd to join in the crabcore dance during the chorus.
His energetic crabcore dance impressed everyone at the show.
Videos of people doing the crabcore dance have gone viral online.

Synset Examples

They laughed at the crabcore dance at the concert.

Etymology

From 'crabcore' (a subgenre of metalcore taking its name from the crab-like stance of performers) + 'dance'.
