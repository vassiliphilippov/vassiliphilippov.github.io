street corner

1. NOUN

/ stɹit ˈkɔɹnɚ / (AmE), / stɹiːt ˈkɔːnə / (BrE)
the place where two streets meet or intersect, often forming a corner
intersection of two streets

Examples

A group of teenagers gathered on the street corner near the café.
She waited for the bus at the street corner every morning.
Two artists were sketching city life from opposite sides of the street corner.

Synset Examples

standing on the corner watching all the girls go by

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intersection - a point where two or more roads cross or meet

2. NOUN

/ strit ˈkɔrnər / (AmE), / striːt ˈkɔːnə / (BrE)
the place where two streets meet or intersect, typically forming a corner
corner where two streets meet

Examples

The bakery is right on the street corner, making it easy to find.
A musician was playing guitar at the street corner last night.
Children often gather near the street corner to wait for the school bus.

Synset Examples

She saw her friend waiting on the street corner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intersection - the point where two or more things intersect, especially roads
