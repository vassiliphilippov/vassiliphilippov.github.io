Republic of Chile

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈtʃɪli / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈtʃɪli / (BrE)
The official name for Chile, a country in southern South America located on the western slopes of the Andes along the Pacific coast.
official name of the country of Chile

Examples

The UN welcomed the Republic of Chile as a full member in 1945.
Exports contribute significantly to the economy of the Republic of Chile.
The Constitution of the Republic of Chile establishes it as a democratic nation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chile - country in South America, commonly used form

Etymology

From Spanish 'República de Chile'. 'Chile' possibly from the indigenous Mapuche word 'chili', meaning 'where the land ends'.
