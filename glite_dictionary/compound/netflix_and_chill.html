to Netflix and chill

1. VERB

/ ˈnɛtflɪks ənd tʃɪl / (AmE), / ˈnɛtflɪks ənd tʃɪl / (BrE)
to spend time relaxing and watching Netflix, often used as a euphemism for engaging in casual sexual activity or intimacy
informal euphemism for relaxing, often with sexual overtones
informal slang

Examples

After a stressful week, they planned to Netflix and chill at her apartment.
He asked if she wanted to Netflix and chill, but she wondered if he had other intentions.
Some people use the phrase Netflix and chill as a subtle invitation for a romantic evening.

Synset Examples

Our plan for the evening was just Netflix and chill.
They decided to Netflix and chill after a long week.
He invited her over for a Netflix and chill session.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to hook up - to have a casual sexual encounter
  • Degree Lower: to hang out - to spend time relaxing or socializing

Etymology

Formed from the brand name Netflix (a streaming service) and 'chill' meaning relax; phrase evolved online around 2014 as a euphemism for casual sex.
