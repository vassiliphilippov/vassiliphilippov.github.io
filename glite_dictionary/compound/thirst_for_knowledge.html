thirst for knowledge

1. NOUN

/ θɜrst fər ˈnɑlɪdʒ / (AmE), / θɜːst fə ˈnɒlɪdʒ / (BrE)
a strong desire to learn and understand new things; intense curiosity that drives investigation and study
strong desire to learn

Examples

Her thirst for knowledge led her to read hundreds of books on different subjects.
The scientist's thirst for knowledge inspired his lifelong dedication to research.
Children often have a natural thirst for knowledge about the world around them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curiosity - a strong desire to know or learn something
  • Synonym: inquisitiveness - eagerness to know and learn things
