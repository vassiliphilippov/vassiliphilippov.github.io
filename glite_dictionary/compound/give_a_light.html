to give a light

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ə laɪt / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə laɪt / (BrE)
to provide a flame or ignition, especially for lighting a cigarette, candle, or similar item
to provide a flame for lighting

Examples

Would you mind if I give a light to your cigarette?
He asked the stranger to give a light for his cigar in the park.
She always carries matches in case someone needs her to give a light.

Synset Examples

She was kind enough to give a light for my candle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: light - to ignite or set fire to; give light to
