cleaning device

1. NOUN

/ ˈkliːnɪŋ dɪˌvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈkliːnɪŋ dɪˌvʌɪs / (BrE)
an implement or tool designed or used for cleaning surfaces, objects, or spaces
tool or apparatus for cleaning

Examples

The company manufactures a new cleaning device that removes pet hair from carpets.
Every hospital room is equipped with at least one cleaning device for sanitation.
She demonstrated how to use the cleaning device to disinfect kitchen counters.

Related Terms
