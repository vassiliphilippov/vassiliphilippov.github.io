ski pants

1. NOUN

/ ski ˌpænts / (AmE), / ski ˌpænts / (BrE)
Trousers, typically made from stretch material and held taut by a strap under the foot, worn for skiing or as a fashion garment.
trousers for skiing or fashion

Examples

She wore her new ski pants on the slopes during their winter holiday.
Retro-style ski pants have become popular as streetwear in recent years.
The store has a wide selection of ski pants for both men and women.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ski trousers - trousers designed for skiing
