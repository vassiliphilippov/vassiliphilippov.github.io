sea green

1. NOUN

/ si ˈɡrin / (AmE), / siː ˈɡriːn / (BrE)
a moderate green color that resembles the color of the sea
moderate green resembling sea water

Examples

The living room walls were painted a lovely shade of sea green.
She picked a dress in sea green because it reminded her of the ocean.
The artist mixed blue and yellow to achieve the perfect sea green for her seascape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aqua - a greenish-blue color, similar to the color of water
  • Degree Higher: turquoise - a greenish-blue color
  • Degree Lower: mint green - a pale green color similar to that of mint leaves
