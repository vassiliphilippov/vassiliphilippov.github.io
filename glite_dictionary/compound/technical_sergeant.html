technical sergeant

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛknɪkəl ˈsɑrdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈtɛknɪkəl ˈsɑːdʒənt / (BrE)
a noncommissioned officer ranking below a master sergeant in the air force or marines
military rank below master sergeant

Examples

He was promoted to technical sergeant after five years of dedicated service.
The responsibilities of a technical sergeant include supervising junior enlisted personnel.
Her grandfather served as a technical sergeant in the US Air Force during World War II.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: master sergeant - noncommissioned officer rank above technical sergeant
  • Degree Lower: staff sergeant - noncommissioned officer rank below technical sergeant
