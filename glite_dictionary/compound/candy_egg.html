candy egg

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæn.di ɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈkæn.di eɡ / (BrE)
A sweet or confection shaped like an egg, often given as a treat, especially during Easter.
egg-shaped sweet or confection

Examples

Every year, children search for candy eggs hidden in the garden on Easter morning.
The bakery sells homemade candy eggs in a variety of colors and flavors.
I bought a box of chocolate candy eggs to share with my coworkers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Easter egg - an egg-shaped chocolate or candy given at Easter
