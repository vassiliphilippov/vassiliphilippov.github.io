dome-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈdoʊm ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈdəʊm ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape or form of a dome; rounded and convex like the roof of a dome.
having the form of a dome

Examples

The observatory has a dome-shaped roof that opens at night for stargazing.
The fungus forms a dome-shaped cap that stands out in the forest.
They built a dome-shaped tent designed to withstand strong winds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: domed - having a rounded or hemispherical shape
