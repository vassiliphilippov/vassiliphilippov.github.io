St Joseph

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈdʒoʊzəf / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈdʒəʊzɪf / (BrE)
A Christian holy day commemorating St. Joseph, the husband of the Virgin Mary and foster father of Jesus, observed in various Christian traditions.
Christian feast day for St. Joseph

Examples

The church held a special mass on St Joseph to honor the patron saint.
Many communities celebrate St Joseph with parades and traditional foods.
On St Joseph, people reflect on the life and virtues of the saint.

Etymology

From Latin Sanctus Joseph, after Joseph, husband of Mary in the New Testament.
