unsinkable confidence

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈsɪŋkəbəl kənˈfɪdəns / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪŋkəbl̩ kənˈfɪd(ə)ns / (BrE)
confidence that cannot be shaken or diminished, no matter the circumstances
unshakeable self-assurance

Examples

She approached the negotiations with unsinkable confidence, refusing to be intimidated by her rivals.
His unsinkable confidence inspired the rest of the team through difficult times.
Even after repeated setbacks, her unsinkable confidence never seemed to waver.

Synset Examples

His confidence felt unsinkable despite the challenges ahead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unshakeable confidence - confidence that is not easily shaken or weakened
