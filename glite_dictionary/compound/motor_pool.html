motor pool

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊtər puːl / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə puːl / (BrE)
a group of vehicles, typically military, managed by a single organization and made available for use as needed
fleet of vehicles managed by one agency

Examples

The army relies on its motor pool to provide transportation for personnel during operations.
Whenever we needed a vehicle, we had to request one from the motor pool.
Maintenance of the motor pool vehicles is conducted every month to ensure reliability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fleet - a group of vehicles or ships under one control
