royal velvet plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɔɪəl ˈvɛlvət ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪəl ˈvɛlvɪt ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
A tropical plant native to Java, grown for its attractive, velvety leaves covered with violet-purple hairs.
tropical plant with velvety violet leaves

Examples

The royal velvet plant is prized for its striking purple leaves in many gardens.
She bought a royal velvet plant to add a vibrant touch to her indoor collection.
Because of its velvety texture, the royal velvet plant stands out among other houseplants.
