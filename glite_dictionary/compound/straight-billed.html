straight-billed

1. ADJ

/ ˈstreɪt bɪld / (AmE), / ˈstreɪt bɪld / (BrE)
(of a bird) having a beak that is straight rather than curved or hooked.
having a straight beak (of birds)

Examples

The straight-billed woodcreeper can be found in South American forests.
Many ornithologists study the feeding habits of straight-billed birds.
A straight-billed kingfisher uses its beak to hunt for fish in shallow waters.
