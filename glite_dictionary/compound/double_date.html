double date

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌbəl deɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʌbl deɪt / (BrE)
an occasion on which two couples go out together socially
social outing for two couples

Examples

Tom and his friend went on a double date to the movies last weekend.
It was their first time going on a double date, and they had a great time.
Some people prefer a double date because it feels less awkward than going out as a pair.
