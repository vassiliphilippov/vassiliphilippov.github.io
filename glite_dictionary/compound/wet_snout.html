wet snout

1. NOUN

/ wɛt snaʊt / (AmE), / wɛt snaʊt / (BrE)
the moist nose of an animal, especially a dog, known for its cool, damp surface
the moist nose of a dog or animal

Examples

The puppy pressed its wet snout against the window, leaving a little smudge.
She could feel the dog's wet snout nuzzling her hand for attention.
After playing in the yard, Max returned with grass stuck to his wet snout.

Synset Examples

The dog sniffed around with its wet snout.
