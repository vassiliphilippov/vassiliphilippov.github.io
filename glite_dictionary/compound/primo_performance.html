primo performance

1. NOUN

/ ˈpriːmoʊ pərˈfɔːrməns / (AmE), / ˈpriːməʊ pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
an excellent or outstanding performance; a show or act of the highest quality
top-quality or outstanding performance
informal

Examples

The actor gave a primo performance that left the audience in awe.
Critics praised the pianist's primo performance at the sold-out concert.
We all celebrated after our team delivered a primo performance in the championship match.

Synset Examples

That was a primo performance by the orchestra.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stellar performance - an exceptional or outstanding performance
  • Antonym: mediocre performance - an average or unremarkable performance

Etymology

"Primo" from Italian "primo" (first), used in English slang for something first-rate or of top quality; "performance" from Old French via Latin.
