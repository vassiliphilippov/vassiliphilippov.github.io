frozen pudding

1. NOUN

/ ˈfroʊ.zən ˈpʊd.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfrəʊ.zən ˈpʊd.ɪŋ / (BrE)
a chilled dessert made from a mixture of custard, nuts, and sometimes liquor, typically served frozen
chilled dessert of custard and nuts

Examples

For dessert, we had homemade frozen pudding topped with fresh berries.
He reminisced about eating frozen pudding every summer at his grandmother's house.
The recipe for classic frozen pudding often includes candied fruit and a splash of rum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice cream - sweet frozen dessert made from milk or cream
