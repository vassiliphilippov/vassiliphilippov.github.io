point of intersection

1. NOUN

/ pɔɪnt əv ˌɪn.tɚˈsɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / pɔɪnt əv ˌɪn.təˈsɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
the exact point at which two or more lines cross each other
point where lines cross

Examples

The two roads meet at a point of intersection near the old church.
On the graph, the point of intersection represents the solution to both equations.
The surveyor marked each point of intersection between the property lines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intersection - the place where two or more lines or roads cross
