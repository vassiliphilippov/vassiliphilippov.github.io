religious offering

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈɔfərɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈɒfərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of presenting bread and wine during the Eucharist in Christian worship
presentation of bread and wine in Christian service

Examples

During Mass, the priest prepared the religious offering of bread and wine for the congregation.
The religious offering is a central part of the Eucharist in many Christian churches.
Choir members sang hymns while the religious offering was brought to the altar.
