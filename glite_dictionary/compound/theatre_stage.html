theatre stage

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiət̮ər steɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈθɪətə steɪdʒ / (BrE)
the area in a theatre where actors perform in front of an audience
area in a theatre for performances

Examples

The actors took their places on the theatre stage as the lights dimmed.
She dreamed of performing on a famous theatre stage one day.
The crew quickly prepared the theatre stage between scenes during intermission.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stage - raised area for performances or presentations
