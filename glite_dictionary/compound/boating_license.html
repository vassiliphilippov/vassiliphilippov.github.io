boating license

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊtɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (AmE), / ˈbəʊtɪŋ ˌlaɪsns / (BrE)
an official document certifying that a person has completed approved boater education and is authorized to operate certain types of boats
permit to operate a boat

Examples

You must show your boating license if you want to rent a motorboat at the marina.
After passing the safety course, she received her boating license in the mail.
Many states require a boating license for anyone operating a personal watercraft.

Synset Examples

A boating license or boater education card is required in most states in order to operate a motorized vessel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: driving license - official permit for operating motor vehicles
