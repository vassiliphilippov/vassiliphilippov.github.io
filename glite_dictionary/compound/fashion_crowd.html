fashion crowd

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃən kraʊd / (AmE), / ˈfæʃən kraʊd / (BrE)
a group of people who are especially interested in fashion trends and styles
people interested in fashion trends

Examples

The designer's bold new collection caught the attention of the fashion crowd in Paris.
At the opening, the fashion crowd filled the gallery with vibrant style and energy.
Magazines always feature photos of the fashion crowd attending runway shows.

Synset Examples

The pink puffer was a hit among the fashion crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashionista - a person who is very interested in fashion
  • Synonym: trendsetter - a person who leads the way in fashion or ideas
