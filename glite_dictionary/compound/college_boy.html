college boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑlɪdʒ bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈkɒlɪdʒ bɔɪ / (BrE)
a male student or former student at a college or university
male student at a college or university

Examples

The new college boy struggled to adjust to the busy campus life.
Many parents are proud when their son becomes a college boy after high school.
The alumni association welcomed every college boy who graduated in the last ten years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: college student - a student enrolled in a college or university
  • Antonym: schoolboy - a boy attending school, especially a primary or secondary school
