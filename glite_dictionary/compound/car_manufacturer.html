car manufacturer

1. NOUN

/ kɑɹ ˌmæn.jəˈfæk.tʃɚ / (AmE), / kɑː ˌman.jʊˈfæk.tʃə / (BrE)
a company or business that makes and produces cars
company that makes cars

Examples

Toyota is a leading car manufacturer with factories around the world.
Many people dream of working for a German car manufacturer such as BMW or Mercedes-Benz.
The electric vehicle market is attracting every major car manufacturer to develop new models.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automaker - a company that manufactures automobiles
  • Antonym: car dealer - a business that sells cars
