word play

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːd ˌpleɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːd ˌpleɪ / (BrE)
the clever or witty use of words and language, especially involving puns or other forms of linguistic humor
clever use of words; puns or language humor

Examples

The novel is full of brilliant word play that delights language lovers.
Shakespeare’s use of word play adds humor and depth to his plays.
Crossword puzzles often rely on clever word play to challenge solvers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pun - a joke exploiting the different possible meanings of a word or words that sound alike
