antelope chipmunk

1. NOUN

/ ˈæntəˌloʊp ˈtʃɪpmʌŋk / (AmE), / ˈantɪləʊp ˈtʃɪpmʌŋk / (BrE)
a small ground squirrel native to the western United States, known for its striped back and agile movements
small ground squirrel of western US

Examples

We caught a glimpse of an antelope chipmunk darting between the rocks.
The antelope chipmunk is well adapted to dry, rocky habitats in the western US.
Children were fascinated by the quick movements of the antelope chipmunk during the field trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chipmunk - small striped North American rodent
