much less

1. ADV

/ mʌt͡ʃ lɛs / (AmE), / mʌtʃ lɛs / (BrE)
used to introduce a statement that is even less likely or less possible than the one just mentioned
used to introduce something less likely

Examples

I can't afford a car, much less a new one.
He didn't finish his homework, much less his science project.
She can't speak Spanish, much less Japanese.

Synset Examples

I can't afford a car, much less a new one.
She can't swim, much less dive.
He struggles to cook, much less bake.
