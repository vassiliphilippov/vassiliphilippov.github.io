flow meter

1. NOUN

/ ˈfloʊ ˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈfləʊ ˌmiːtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure the rate at which a liquid or gas flows through a system
device for measuring fluid flow rate

Examples

The technician installed a new flow meter to monitor the water entering the building.
A flow meter can help engineers ensure the proper functioning of a chemical plant.
We calibrated the flow meter before starting the experiment to obtain accurate measurements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flowmeter - alternative spelling of flow meter
