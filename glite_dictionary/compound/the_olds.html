the olds

1. NOUN

/ ði oʊldz / (AmE), / ði əʊldz / (BrE)
(informal) Older people collectively; elders.
older people; elders (informal)
informal

Examples

Every Sunday afternoon, the kids go next door to visit the olds and listen to their stories.
At family gatherings, the olds often share wisdom and memories from their youth.
It can be challenging for the olds to keep up with the latest technology trends.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: the young - young people collectively
