traffic delay

1. NOUN

/ ˈtræfɪk dɪˈleɪ / (AmE), / ˈtræfɪk dɪˈleɪ / (BrE)
a period during which the movement of vehicles is slowed or halted due to congestion, accidents, or other obstructions on the road
slowed or halted movement of vehicles

Examples

There was a traffic delay on the highway due to road construction.
Morning commuters experienced a major traffic delay after an accident blocked two lanes.
Unexpected traffic delays forced us to reschedule the meeting.

Synset Examples

The accident caused significant traffic delays this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: congestion - the state of being overcrowded, especially with traffic
  • Synonym: traffic jam - a long line of vehicles unable to move or moving very slowly
