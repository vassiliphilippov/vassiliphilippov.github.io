printing machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪntɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈprɪntɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used for printing text or images onto paper or other materials
machine for printing text or images

Examples

The old newspaper office still uses a printing machine from the 1960s.
They bought a new printing machine to speed up their production process.
A malfunctioning printing machine can halt the entire publishing schedule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: printer - a person or machine that prints
