lightning speed

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪtnɪŋ spiːd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtnɪŋ spiːd / (BrE)
a very high rate of speed; extreme swiftness
extremely fast speed

Examples

The runner dashed around the track at lightning speed, leaving his competitors far behind.
Thanks to technological advances, information now travels across the world at lightning speed.
She typed her report at lightning speed and finished hours before the deadline.

Synset Examples

The cabbie drove us through the city at lightning speed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breakneck speed - an extremely fast and dangerous rate of speed
  • Antonym: snail's pace - an extremely slow speed
  • Degree Lower: rapid - moving or happening very quickly
