social season

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ˈsiːzən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˈsiːzn̩ / (BrE)
the time of year when major social events and gatherings are traditionally held, especially in fashionable or upper-class society
period for major social events

Examples

Many aristocrats travel to London for the social season each spring.
During the social season, invitations to galas and balls are highly sought after.
The charity auction became the highlight of the social season this year.
