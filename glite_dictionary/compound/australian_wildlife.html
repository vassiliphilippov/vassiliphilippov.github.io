Australian wildlife

1. NOUN

/ əˈstreɪljən ˈwaɪl(d)ˌlaɪf / (AmE), / ɒˈstreɪlɪən ˈwaɪl(d)laɪf / (BrE)
animals native to Australia, including its unique species of mammals, birds, reptiles, and other fauna
animals native to Australia

Examples
