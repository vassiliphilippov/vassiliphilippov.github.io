bath dodger

1. NOUN

/ bæθ ˈdɑdʒɚ / (AmE), / bɑːθ ˈdɒdʒə / (BrE)
a person who habitually avoids bathing or washing themselves
person who avoids bathing
informal british

Examples

If you don't shower after football, you'll start being known as a bath dodger.
His flatmates teased him relentlessly for being a bath dodger.
No one wants to sit next to a bath dodger on the bus.

Synset Examples

He was called a bath dodger by his friends.
My son is a real bath dodger; he avoids it at all costs.
Being a bath dodger, she often ends up smelling bad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slob - a lazy or messy person
