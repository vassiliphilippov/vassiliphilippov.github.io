Clinch River

1. PROPN

/ klɪntʃ ˈrɪvɚ / (AmE), / klɪntʃ ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river that rises in southwestern Virginia and flows southwest across eastern Tennessee to join the Tennessee River
river in Virginia and Tennessee, USA

Examples

The Clinch River is known for its rich biodiversity and scenic beauty.
During the summer, many families go fishing along the Clinch River.
A bridge was built over the Clinch River to improve access to the state park.
