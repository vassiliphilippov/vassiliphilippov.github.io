sunny-side up

1. ADJ

/ ˌsʌni saɪd ˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌsʌni saɪd ˈʌp / (BrE)
(Of an egg) fried on only one side, with the yolk left runny and visible on top.
egg fried on one side only

Examples

I prefer my eggs sunny-side up with a sprinkle of pepper.
She ordered two sunny-side up eggs for breakfast.
The chef cooked perfect sunny-side up eggs for the customers.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: over easy - egg fried on both sides, yolk still runny
