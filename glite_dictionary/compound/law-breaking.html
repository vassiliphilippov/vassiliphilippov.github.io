law-breaking

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔˌbreɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɔːˌbreɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of violating or disobeying laws; criminal activity.
the act of violating laws

Examples

The police increased patrols in areas with a high rate of law-breaking.
He was arrested for his involvement in several acts of law-breaking.
Strict new measures aim to reduce law-breaking in the city.

Synset Examples

a long record of crimes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crime - an action or omission that constitutes an offense and is punishable by law
  • Antonym: law-abiding - obedient to laws and rules
