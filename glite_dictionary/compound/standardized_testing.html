standardized testing

1. NOUN

/ ˈstændərˌdaɪzd ˈtɛstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstændədaɪzd ˈtɛstɪŋ / (BrE)
a method of evaluation in which tests are administered and scored in a consistent, uniform manner to assess the performance of students or groups
uniform method of student assessment

Examples

Most schools rely on standardized testing to compare student performance across the country.
The debate over the fairness of standardized testing continues among educators.
Many students feel anxious when preparing for standardized testing each year.

Synset Examples

The educrat emphasized the importance of standardized testing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assessment - the act of evaluating or appraising a person's ability or knowledge
  • Synonym: examination - a formal test of a person's knowledge or proficiency in a subject or skill
