hyena dog

1. NOUN

/ haɪˈinə dɒg / (AmE), / haɪˈiːnə dɒg / (BrE)
a large, powerful wild canine of southern and eastern Africa, living and hunting in packs; also called African wild dog or painted wolf
African wild dog; pack-hunting African canine

Examples

The hyena dog is known for its highly coordinated hunting techniques.
Once common, the hyena dog has become rare due to habitat loss.
Wildlife photographers spent weeks tracking a pack of hyena dogs in Botswana.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: African wild dog - another name for hyena dog, a wild pack-hunting African canine
  • Synonym: painted wolf - another common name for the hyena dog; noted for its mottled coat
  • Antonym: hyena - a carnivorous mammal of the family Hyaenidae, often mistaken for the hyena dog
