cubeb vine

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjuː.bɛb vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bɛb vaɪn / (BrE)
A tropical Southeast Asian shrubby vine that produces peppery, berrylike fruits used as a spice or medicinally.
spicy-fruited Southeast Asian vine

Examples

The cubeb vine thrives in the humid climates of Southeast Asia.
Spices derived from the cubeb vine were highly prized in medieval Europe.
The berries of the cubeb vine are sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cubeb - the spicy, pepper-like fruit of the cubeb vine

Etymology

From Arabic *kabāba*, via Medieval Latin *cubeba*, referring to the plant and its fruit.
