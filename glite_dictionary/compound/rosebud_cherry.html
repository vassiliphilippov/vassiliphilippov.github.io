rosebud cherry

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊzbʌd ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzbʌd ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
a shrub or small tree native to Japan, cultivated for its ornamental rose-pink flowers
ornamental Japanese shrub/tree with pink flowers

Examples

The rosebud cherry blossoms early each spring, filling the garden with vivid pink hues.
We planted a rosebud cherry to enhance the beauty of our backyard.
Many parks feature rows of rosebud cherry trees for their spectacular springtime display.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cherry blossom - flower of any tree of genus Prunus, especially ornamental Japanese species
