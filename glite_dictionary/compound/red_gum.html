red gum

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛd ˌɡʌm / (AmE), / ˈrɛd ˌɡʌm / (BrE)

Examples

The distinctive leaves of the red gum turn vibrant shades in autumn.

He collected fragrant sap from the red gum for use in homemade incense.
