spark arrester

1. NOUN

/ spɑrk əˈrɛstɚ / (AmE), / spɑːk əˈrɛstə / (BrE)
A device, typically a wire mesh, fitted to an open fireplace or smokestack to prevent the escape of sparks and reduce the risk of fire.
device to prevent escape of sparks

Examples

The old train was fitted with a spark arrester to prevent accidental fires along the tracks.
You should make sure the spark arrester on your chimney is cleaned regularly for safety.
A proper spark arrester can significantly reduce the risk of wildfires caused by machinery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fireguard - screen placed in front of a fireplace to prevent sparks from escaping

2. NOUN

/ spɑrk əˈrɛstər / (AmE), / spɑːk əˈrɛstə / (BrE)
a device used to reduce or prevent sparking when electrical contacts are opened or closed
device reducing electrical sparking

Examples

The spark arrester is crucial for preventing dangerous electrical fires in industrial equipment.
After installing the spark arrester, the technician noticed a significant reduction in visible sparks.
Many electrical panels are equipped with a spark arrester to enhance operational safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arc suppressor - device to suppress electrical arcing
