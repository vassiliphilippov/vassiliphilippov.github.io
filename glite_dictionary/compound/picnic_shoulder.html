picnic shoulder

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪk.nɪk ˈʃoʊl.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɪk.nɪk ˈʃəʊl.də / (BrE)
the lower part of a pig's shoulder, often smoked or cured, used as a cut of pork
cut of pork from a pig's shoulder

Examples

The recipe calls for a picnic shoulder to be slow-roasted for several hours.
We found a smoked picnic shoulder at the butcher for our barbecue.
Pulled pork made from picnic shoulder is especially flavorful and tender.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pork shoulder - upper part of a pig's shoulder, another pork cut
  • Degree Higher: ham - meat from a pig's upper hind leg, cured or smoked
