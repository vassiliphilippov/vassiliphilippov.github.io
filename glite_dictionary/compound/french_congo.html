French Congo

1. PROPN

/ frɛntʃ ˈkɑŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / frɛntʃ ˈkɒŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a former French colony in west-central Africa, now known as the Republic of the Congo, which gained independence in 1960.
former name of the Republic of the Congo

Examples

The territory known as French Congo became the Republic of the Congo after independence in 1960.
Colonial administrators oversaw resource extraction in the French Congo during the late 19th century.
Many historical documents refer to present-day Congo as the French Congo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Republic of the Congo - current nation, formerly known as French Congo
  • Degree Higher: French Equatorial Africa - federation including French Congo in colonial period
