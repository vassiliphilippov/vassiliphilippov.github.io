essential ingredient

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈsɛnʃəl ɪnˈɡriːdiənt / (AmE), / ɪˈsɛnʃl ɪnˈɡriːdiənt / (BrE)
a component that is absolutely necessary for a recipe, process, or result to succeed or function properly
necessary component or part

Examples

Flour is an essential ingredient in most bread recipes.
Trust is an essential ingredient in any successful relationship.
Tomatoes are the essential ingredient that make this sauce unique.

Synset Examples

The containant held all the essential ingredients securely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: key ingredient - an important or decisive component
  • Synonym: main ingredient - the most important or largest component in a mixture or recipe
  • Antonym: optional ingredient - a component that can be included but is not necessary
