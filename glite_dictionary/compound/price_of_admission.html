price of admission

1. NOUN

/ praɪs əv ædˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / praɪs əv ədˈmɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the amount of money required to enter a place, event, or activity
fee to enter a place or event

Examples

The price of admission to the concert includes a free drink.
Many museums offer discounts on the price of admission for students.
People were surprised by how high the price of admission had become over the years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admission fee - money paid for entry to an event or place
