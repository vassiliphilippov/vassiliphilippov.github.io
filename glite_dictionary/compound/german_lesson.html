German lesson

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɝːmən ˈlɛsən / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmən ˈlɛsən / (BrE)
a period of instruction in the German language
language class in German

Examples

She attends a German lesson every Monday evening at the community center.
My favorite German lesson focused on ordering food in restaurants.
The teacher made the German lesson both fun and informative for all students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: French lesson - instruction in the French language
  • Synonym: language lesson - instruction session in a language
