spending cut

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛndɪŋ kʌt / (AmE), / ˈspɛndɪŋ kʌt / (BrE)
a reduction in the amount of money spent, especially by a government or organization
a reduction in expenditure

Examples

The government announced a major spending cut to reduce the deficit.
Many public services were affected by the spending cut last year.
After the recession, the council imposed a spending cut across all departments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: budget cut - a reduction made to a budget
