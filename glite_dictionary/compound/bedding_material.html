bedding material

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdɪŋ məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / ˈbɛdɪŋ məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
material, such as straw, shavings, or other substances, used to provide a bed or sleeping area for animals
material used as a bed for animals

Examples

The farmer replaced the old bedding material in the chicken coop with fresh straw.
Good bedding material helps keep livestock warm and comfortable during winter.
Some pet owners prefer shredded newspaper as bedding material for small animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedding - material for animals or people to lie on
