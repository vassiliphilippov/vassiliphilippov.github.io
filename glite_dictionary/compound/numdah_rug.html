numdah rug

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌm.də rʌg / (AmE), / ˈnʌm.də rʌg / (BrE)
an embroidered rug made from coarse Indian felt, typically featuring bright colors and distinctive designs
embroidered Indian felt rug

Examples

The living room was decorated with a colorful numdah rug from Kashmir.
She bought a numdah rug featuring intricate embroidery during her trip to India.
A numdah rug adds texture and warmth to any floor.

Etymology

From Hindi 'namdā' meaning 'felt rug'.
