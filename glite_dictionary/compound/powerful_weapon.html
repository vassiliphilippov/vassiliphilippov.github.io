powerful weapon

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊɚfəl ˈwɛpən / (AmE), / ˈpaʊəfəl ˈwɛpən / (BrE)
something that gives a strong advantage or effectiveness, often used metaphorically to refer to a tool, skill, or quality that can achieve significant results.
strong advantage or effective tool (often metaphorical)

Examples

Education can be a powerful weapon against poverty.
The team's creativity proved to be their most powerful weapon in the competition.
Her voice became a powerful weapon for justice and equality.

Synset Examples

Knowledge is a powerful weapon in negotiations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tool - means or device for achieving something
  • Synonym: weapon - an object used to attack or defend
