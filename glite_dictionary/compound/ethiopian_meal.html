Ethiopian meal

1. NOUN

/ ˌiːθiˈoʊpiən miːl / (AmE), / ˌiːθiˈəʊpiən miːl / (BrE)
A meal consisting of dishes that are characteristic of Ethiopian cuisine, often including injera bread and spiced stews.
meal typical of Ethiopian cuisine

Examples

We ordered an Ethiopian meal with doro wat and injera for dinner.
She prepared an elaborate Ethiopian meal for her friends, complete with traditional coffee ceremony.
Their first experience of an Ethiopian meal introduced them to unique flavors and communal dining.

Synset Examples

We enjoyed a delicious Ethiopian meal at the new restaurant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ethiopian food - dishes or food from Ethiopian cuisine
