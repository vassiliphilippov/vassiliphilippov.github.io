Washington's Birthday

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑːʃɪŋtənz ˈbɜːrθ.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃɪŋtnz ˈbɜːθ.deɪ / (BrE)
A federal holiday in the United States honoring the birth of George Washington, the country's first president.
U.S. federal holiday celebrating George Washington
american

Examples

Many schools and government offices close for Washington's Birthday each year.
My family has a tradition of visiting historic sites on Washington's Birthday.
Some people use Washington's Birthday as a time to reflect on American history.
