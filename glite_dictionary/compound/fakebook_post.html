Fakebook post

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪk.bʊk poʊst / (AmE), / ˈfeɪk.bʊk pəʊst / (BrE)
a social media post that mocks, parodies, or exaggerates standard content usually seen on Facebook
mock or parody social media post
informal slang

Examples

The group shared a hilarious Fakebook post making fun of classic vacation photos.
Her latest Fakebook post perfectly imitates over-the-top engagement announcements.
After the event, he created a Fakebook post poking fun at everyone's checked-in statuses.

Synset Examples

They laughed about the absurdity of Fakebook posts.

Etymology

Formed by blending 'fake' and 'Facebook' to parody Facebook posts.
