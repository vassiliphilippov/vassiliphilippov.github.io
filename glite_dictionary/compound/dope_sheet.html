dope sheet

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊp ʃiːt / (AmE), / ˈdəʊp ʃiːt / (BrE)
A publication containing information on horses and results of horse races, used for betting and analysis.
racing publication about horses and results

Examples

He consulted the dope sheet before placing his bets at the track.
Seasoned gamblers rely on the dope sheet to make informed decisions.
The dope sheet lists each horse's performance statistics and odds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racing form - a publication giving past performances of horses in races
