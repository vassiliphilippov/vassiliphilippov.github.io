spinal fluid

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪnəl ˈfluɪd / (AmE), / ˈspaɪnəl ˈfluːɪd / (BrE)
the clear, colorless liquid that surrounds and protects the brain and spinal cord
clear fluid around brain and spinal cord
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that a sample of spinal fluid would help diagnose the infection.
A leak of spinal fluid can cause severe headaches and other complications.
Researchers are studying spinal fluid to find markers for neurological diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cerebrospinal fluid - the technical term for the clear fluid around the brain and spine
