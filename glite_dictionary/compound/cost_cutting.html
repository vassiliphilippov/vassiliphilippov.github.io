cost cutting

1. NOUN

/ kɑst ˈkʌtɪŋ / (AmE), / kɒst ˈkʌtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of reducing expenses, especially in business or organizational contexts
reducing expenses

Examples

The company announced a new cost cutting initiative to improve profitability.
Due to declining sales, cost cutting has become a top priority for management.
Aggressive cost cutting led to the closure of several branches across the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: budgeting - the process of planning how to spend money
