human behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmən bɪˈheɪvjər / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
the range of actions and mannerisms exhibited by humans, including their interactions with others and response to their environment
actions and interactions of humans

Examples

Psychologists study human behavior to better understand why people make certain choices.
The documentary examined the impact of culture on human behavior around the world.
Technology has dramatically changed human behavior in recent decades.

Synset Examples

Psychology is an interesting ology that explores human behavior.
This book explores the interesting condition of human behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: behavior - the way a person behaves or acts
