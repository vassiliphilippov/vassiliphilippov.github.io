steam room

1. NOUN

/ stiːm ruːm / (AmE), / stiːm ruːm / (BrE)
a room filled with steam in which people bathe for relaxation, health, or cleansing purposes
room for steam bathing

Examples

After his workout, Jake relaxed in the steam room to soothe his muscles.
The hotel spa offers both a sauna and a steam room for guests.
Many gyms now include a steam room as part of their wellness facilities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sauna - a small room used as a hot-air or steam bath for cleaning and refreshing the body
  • Synonym: vapour bath - a bath or room where steam is used for therapeutic or cleansing purposes (British term)
