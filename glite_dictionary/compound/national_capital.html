national capital

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæʃənl ˈkæpɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈnæʃənəl ˈkapɪtəl / (BrE)
the capital city of a nation, serving as the center of government and administration
the capital city of a nation

Examples

Paris is the national capital of France and a major center for culture and politics.
Many tourists visit the national capital to see historic government buildings and museums.
The decision to move the national capital was met with both support and opposition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: capital - the city where a government is based
