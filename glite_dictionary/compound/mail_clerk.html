mail clerk

1. NOUN

/ meɪl klɜrk / (AmE), / meɪl klɑːk / (BrE)
a person whose job is to sort, process, and distribute mail in a post office or other mailroom setting
person who sorts and handles mail

Examples

The mail clerk sorted hundreds of envelopes each morning at the office.
If you have a question about a package, ask the mail clerk at the counter.
After college, she found a temporary job as a mail clerk in a large company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: postal clerk - a person who works in a post office handling mail and customer service
