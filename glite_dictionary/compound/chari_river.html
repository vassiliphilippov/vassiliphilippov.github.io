Chari River

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃɑri ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːri ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in central Africa that flows northwest into Lake Chad, serving as a major water source for the region
African river flowing into Lake Chad

Examples

The Chari River is the main source of water for Lake Chad.
Fishermen rely on the Chari River for their livelihoods and transportation.
During the rainy season, the Chari River swells and often floods nearby villages.
