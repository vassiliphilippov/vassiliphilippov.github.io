thank you

1. NOUN

/ ˈθæŋk ju / (AmE), / ˈθæŋk juː / (BrE)
A polite expression used to show gratitude to someone.
expression of gratitude

Examples

Thank you for your help with the project yesterday.
He gave me a big smile and a heartfelt thank you.
Remember to say thank you when someone does something nice for you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thanks - informal expression of gratitude
  • Synonym: cheers - British informal word for thanks
