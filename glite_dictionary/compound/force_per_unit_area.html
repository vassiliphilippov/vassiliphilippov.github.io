force per unit area

1. NOUN

/ fɔrs pɚ ˈjunət ˈɛriə / (AmE), / fɔːs pə ˈjuːnɪt ˈeəriə / (BrE)
the amount of force applied to a surface divided by the area over which it is applied, typically measured in pascals or dynes
force applied per area; scientific term for pressure

Examples

Atmospheric force per unit area decreases with increasing altitude.
The engineer calculated the force per unit area on the dam wall to ensure its stability.
Tires are designed to distribute the vehicle's weight and reduce the force per unit area on the road surface.

Synset Examples

the compressed gas exerts an increased pressure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pressure - continuous physical force exerted on or against an object by something in contact with it
