ski parka

1. NOUN

/ ski ˈpɑrkə / (AmE), / ski ˈpɑːkə / (BrE)
a warm, often waterproof jacket designed to be worn while skiing, typically featuring a hood and insulation
jacket for skiing, often insulated

Examples

He zipped up his ski parka before heading out onto the slopes.
Make sure your ski parka is both warm and water-resistant for mountain weather.
She bought a new ski parka in bright red for her winter holiday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parka - A hooded jacket for cold weather
