bee sting

1. NOUN

/ ˈbi ˌstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbiː ˌstɪŋ / (BrE)
a painful or swelling wound caused by the sting of a bee
sting or wound caused by a bee

Examples

He had an allergic reaction to a bee sting and had to go to the hospital.
After stepping on a flower barefoot, she discovered a painful bee sting on her toe.
Applying ice can help reduce the swelling from a bee sting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wasp sting - a sting inflicted by a wasp
