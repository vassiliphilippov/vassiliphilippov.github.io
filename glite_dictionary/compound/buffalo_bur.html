buffalo bur

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌfəloʊ bɜr / (AmE), / ˈbʌfələʊ bɜː / (BrE)
A North American nightshade plant (Solanum rostratum) with prickly leaves and stems and yellow, racemose flowers.
prickly North American nightshade plant

Examples

The fields were overrun with buffalo bur, making it difficult for cattle to graze.
She wore gloves while removing the buffalo bur from her garden to avoid the prickly stems.
The bright yellow flowers of the buffalo bur caught his attention during the hike.

Etymology

Named for its prickly 'bur' and association with buffalo habitats in North America.
