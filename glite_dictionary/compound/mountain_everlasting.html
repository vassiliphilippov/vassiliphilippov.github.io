mountain everlasting

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊn.tən ˌɛv.ɚˈlæst.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪn ˈɛvəˌlɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
A perennial plant of the daisy family with white, woolly flower heads, found chiefly in mountainous regions of Europe and North America (Antennaria dioica).
a woolly perennial plant of mountain regions

Examples

The slopes were covered with clusters of mountain everlasting in full bloom.
Gardeners often plant mountain everlasting for its attractive silvery foliage.
Hikers were delighted to spot mountain everlasting growing along the alpine trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cat's foot - a plant of the genus Antennaria, with woolly leaves and tiny flowers
