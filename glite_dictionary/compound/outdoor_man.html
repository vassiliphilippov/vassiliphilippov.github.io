outdoor man

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtˌdɔr mæn / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌdɔː mæn / (BrE)
a man who enjoys or prefers outdoor activities such as hiking, fishing, or camping
man who enjoys outdoor activities

Examples

As an outdoor man, he spends most weekends hiking in the mountains.
You can always spot an outdoor man by his rugged boots and practical clothing.
He considered himself an outdoor man who would rather be fishing than sitting in an office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outdoorsman - a person who spends a lot of time outdoors or enjoys outdoor activities
