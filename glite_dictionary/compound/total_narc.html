total narc

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊt̬l nɑrk / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl nɑːk / (BrE)
(informal, slang) A person who informs or betrays others, especially to the police or authorities, often in a criminal context.
a person who informs on others (slang)
informal slang american

Examples

Nobody trusted him after he proved to be a total narc during the investigation.
Calling someone a total narc at school could get you into trouble with your friends.
She acted like a total narc when she told the teacher about the prank.

Synset Examples

Everyone avoids him because he's a total narc.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: narc - an informer, especially to the police
  • Synonym: snitch - a person who tells on others; an informer
  • Synonym: grass - a person who informs on others to authorities (British slang)

Etymology

From 'narc', short for 'narcotics agent' (US slang), and 'total' as an intensifier.
