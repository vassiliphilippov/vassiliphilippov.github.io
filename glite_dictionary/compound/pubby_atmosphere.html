pubby atmosphere

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌbi ˈætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ˈpʌbi ˈætməsfɪə / (BrE)
a casual and friendly environment that resembles the atmosphere of a pub
casual, pub-like environment

Examples

The restaurant creates a pubby atmosphere with wooden tables and dim lighting.
They wanted to maintain a pubby atmosphere, so they added dart boards and live music.
The new bar's pubby atmosphere makes everyone feel welcome and relaxed.

Synset Examples

The new café has a pubby atmosphere that I love.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pub-like atmosphere - an atmosphere similar to that of a pub
