Master in Business

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæs.tɚ ɪn ˈbɪz.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.stər ɪn ˈbɪz.nəs / (BrE)
A postgraduate academic degree focusing on business studies, such as management, finance, or marketing.
postgraduate business degree

Examples

After earning her Master in Business, she quickly advanced to a managerial position.
Many companies prefer candidates with a Master in Business for leadership roles.
He moved abroad to pursue his Master in Business at a top European university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: MBA - Master of Business Administration, a common business master's degree
