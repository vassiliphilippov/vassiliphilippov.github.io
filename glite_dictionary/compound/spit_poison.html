to spit poison

1. VERB

/ spɪt ˈpɔɪzən / (AmE), / spɪt ˈpɔɪz(ə)n / (BrE)
to speak with intense anger or malice, expressing bitterness or hostility through one's words
speak with intense anger or bitterness

Examples

Whenever he feels betrayed, he will spit poison at anyone around him.
She was so upset that she couldn't help but spit poison in her reply.
During the heated debate, he started spitting poison at his opponent.

Synset Examples

His words were like spit poison during the argument.
She tends to spit poison whenever she's provoked.
She could spit poison when she’s angry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speak venom - to speak with malice or spite
