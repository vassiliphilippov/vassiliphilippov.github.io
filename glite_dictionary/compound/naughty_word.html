naughty word

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔti wɝd / (AmE), / ˈnɔːti wɜːd / (BrE)
a word considered inappropriate, offensive, or improper, especially for children
inappropriate or offensive word

Examples

Timmy was told not to say that naughty word at the dinner table.
The teacher warned the class that using a naughty word would result in detention.
Parents sometimes spell out a naughty word so their children won't repeat it.

Synset Examples

He was scolded for using a naughty word in class.
That naughty word got him into trouble at school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swear word - word considered offensive or vulgar
  • Synonym: expletive - offensive or obscene word; swear word
  • Antonym: polite word - word considered acceptable and non-offensive
