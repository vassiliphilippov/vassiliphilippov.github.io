safety feature

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪfti ˌfiːtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti ˌfiːtʃə / (BrE)
A design element or component of a product or system added to help prevent harm or injury to users.
feature for user safety

Examples

Airbags are a standard safety feature in most modern cars.
The new playground includes several safety features, such as soft ground surfaces and secure gates.
Adding a fire alarm is an important safety feature for every home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: precaution - an action taken in advance to prevent harm
  • Antonym: hazard - a danger or risk
