Leaning Tower

1. PROPN

/ ˈliːnɪŋ ˈtaʊər / (AmE), / ˈliːnɪŋ ˈtaʊə / (BrE)
a famous freestanding, round marble bell tower in Pisa, Italy, renowned for its unintended tilt due to unstable foundation soil
tilted bell tower in Pisa, Italy

Examples

The Leaning Tower attracts millions of tourists to the city of Pisa every year.
Photographs of the Leaning Tower often show people pretending to hold it up with their hands.
After years of restoration, the Leaning Tower is now considered stable and safe for visitors.
