tie-on

1. ADJ

/ ˈtaɪ ɑn / (AmE), / ˈtaɪ ɒn / (BrE)
designed to be attached by tying a string, cord, or similar fastening
meant to be fastened by tying

Examples

The tie-on apron is easy to wear and adjust.
Please attach these tie-on tags to the luggage.
He bought a set of tie-on seat cushions for the chairs.
