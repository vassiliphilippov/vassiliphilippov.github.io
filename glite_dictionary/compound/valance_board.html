valance board

1. NOUN

/ ˈvæl.əns bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈvæl.əns bɔːd / (BrE)
a decorative board fixed at the top of a window to hide curtain fittings or hardware
decorative board hiding curtain fixtures

Examples

The living room's elegant valance board added a touch of refinement above the new curtains.
She painted the valance board to match the color of the walls for a seamless look.
Their custom valance board concealed the unsightly curtain rods beautifully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: valance - a short piece of decorative drapery across the top of a window
