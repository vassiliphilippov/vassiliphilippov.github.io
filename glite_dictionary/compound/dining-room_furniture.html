dining-room furniture

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪnɪŋ ruːm ˈfɜrnɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnɪŋ ruːm ˈfɜːnɪtʃə / (BrE)
furniture designed or intended for use in a dining room, such as tables, chairs, and sideboards.
furniture for a dining room

Examples

We bought a new set of dining-room furniture for the housewarming party.
Their antique dining-room furniture gives the space a classic and elegant feel.
The store has a wide variety of dining-room furniture to suit every style and budget.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: furniture - large movable equipment for a room, such as tables and chairs
