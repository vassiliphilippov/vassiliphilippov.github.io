Black American

1. NOUN

/ blæk əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən / (AmE), / blæk əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən / (BrE)
An American citizen whose ancestors are from Africa, especially from Sub-Saharan Africa; commonly refers to people of African descent living in the United States.
American of African descent

Examples

The documentary explored the history of Black American culture in the twentieth century.
She is proud to be a Black American and celebrates her heritage every year.
Black American leaders have made significant contributions to the arts, sciences, and politics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: African American - An American of African, especially Black African, descent
