general manager

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒɛnərəl ˈmænədʒər / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛnərəl ˈmænɪdʒə / (BrE)
the person responsible for the overall management and operation of an organization, department, or business
top manager in an organization

Examples

The general manager announced new policies at the team meeting.
After years as assistant, she was promoted to general manager of the hotel.
As general manager, he oversees all departments to ensure efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: manager - a person responsible for controlling or administering an organization or group of staff
  • Degree Higher: chief executive officer - the highest-ranking executive in a company or organization
