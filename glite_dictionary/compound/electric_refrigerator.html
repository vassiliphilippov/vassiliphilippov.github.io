electric refrigerator

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk rɪˌfrɪdʒəˈreɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk rɪˌfrɪdʒəˈreɪtə / (BrE)
a refrigerator that uses an electric motor to circulate coolant for cooling and preserving food and beverages
refrigerator powered by electricity

Examples

After buying a new electric refrigerator, the family's food stayed fresh much longer.
The apartment comes equipped with an electric refrigerator and a modern stove.
In the early twentieth century, the electric refrigerator replaced iceboxes in many homes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refrigerator - an appliance used for keeping food and drinks cold
