vulgar term

1. NOUN

/ ˈvʌlɡɚ tɝm / (AmE), / ˈvʌlɡə tɜːm / (BrE)
an expression that is crude or offensive, especially relating to sex or bodily functions
crude or offensive expression

Examples

The teacher reminded the class not to use any vulgar term during the discussion.
He was reprimanded for shouting a vulgar term at the referee.
Many people consider that word to be a vulgar term and avoid saying it in public.

Synset Examples

She found the movie's humor quite vulgar and offensive.

Related Terms
