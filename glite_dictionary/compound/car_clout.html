car clout

1. NOUN

/ kɑr klaʊt / (AmE), / kɑː klaʊt / (BrE)
Influence or social prestige derived from the type of car someone owns.
influence based on car ownership
informal

Driving a brand-new sports car gave him instant car clout in his neighborhood.
She bought a luxury SUV to boost her car clout among her coworkers.
In some circles, the kind of vehicle you own determines your level of car clout.
