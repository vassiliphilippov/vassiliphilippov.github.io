silver oak

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ oʊk / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə əʊk / (BrE)
a small slender tree native to northern Australia, with narrow grey-green hanging leaves and white or cream-colored flowers
Australian tree with grey-green leaves

Examples

The silver oak is often planted as an ornamental tree in Australian gardens.
A row of silver oaks provided welcome shade along the rural roadside.
Many birds nest in the graceful branches of the silver oak.
