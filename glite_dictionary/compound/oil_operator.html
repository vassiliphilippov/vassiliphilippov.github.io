oil operator

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl ˈɑːpəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈɒpəˌreɪtə / (BrE)
a person or company that owns or manages oil wells for the purpose of extracting petroleum
person or company running oil wells

Examples

The experienced oil operator managed several wells across Texas.
Becoming an oil operator requires significant investment and technical knowledge.
The company partnered with a local oil operator to boost their exploration efforts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oilman - a person involved in the production or sale of oil
  • Synonym: driller - a person who operates drilling equipment
