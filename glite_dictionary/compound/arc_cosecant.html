arc cosecant

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrk koʊˈsiːkənt / (AmE), / ˈɑːk kəʊˈsiːkənt / (BrE)
the inverse trigonometric function that gives the angle whose cosecant is a given number
inverse trigonometric function of cosecant

Examples

The calculator does not have a dedicated button for the arc cosecant function.
Finding the arc cosecant of a value helps determine the original angle in trigonometric problems.
In advanced mathematics, the arc cosecant is often written as arccsc(x).

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arccsc - common abbreviation for arc cosecant
  • Degree Lower: cosecant - the reciprocal of sine in trigonometry
  • Synonym: arc sine - inverse function of sine

Etymology

From Latin 'arcus' (bow, arc) and 'cosecant', itself from 'co-' (complementary) and 'secant'.
