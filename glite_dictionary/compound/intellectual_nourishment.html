intellectual nourishment

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.t̬əˈlɛk.tʃu.əl ˈnɝː.ɪʃ.mənt / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈlɛk.tʃu.əl ˈnʌr.ɪʃ.mənt / (BrE)
anything that provides mental stimulation or encourages active thought and learning.
mental stimulus or encouragement for thought

Examples

Books have always been a source of intellectual nourishment for curious minds.
She sought intellectual nourishment in lively debates and challenging discussions.
The museum visit provided the intellectual nourishment he craved outside the classroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspiration - a sudden brilliant or timely idea
  • Synonym: stimulation - the act of encouraging something to happen or develop
  • Degree Lower: entertainment - activities that interest or amuse people
