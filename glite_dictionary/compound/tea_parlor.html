tea parlor

1. NOUN

/ ti ˈpɑrlɚ / (AmE), / ti ˈpɑːlə / (BrE)
A restaurant or café where tea and light meals are served, often in a relaxed or elegant setting.
restaurant serving tea and light meals

Examples

We met at her favorite tea parlor for sandwiches and scones one afternoon.
The tea parlor was decorated with vintage china and floral wallpaper.
Many Victorian-era tea parlors were known for their refined atmosphere and delicate pastries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tea room - a room or establishment serving tea and light refreshments
