black-coated

1. ADJ

/ ˈblæk ˌkoʊt̬ɪd / (AmE), / ˈblæk ˌkəʊtɪd / (BrE)
Having a coat, covering, or surface that is black in color, especially referring to animals with black fur.
covered with a black coat or surface

Examples

The black-coated dog stood out against the white snow.
Many black-coated horses are prized for their elegant appearance.
A group of black-coated scientists gathered in the lab for experiments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black - having the color black
  • Antonym: white-coated - having a coat or covering that is white
