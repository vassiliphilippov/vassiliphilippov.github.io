Kriss Kringle

1. NOUN

/ krɪs ˈkrɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / krɪs ˈkrɪŋɡəl / (BrE)
A name for Santa Claus, the legendary figure who is said to bring presents to children at Christmas.
another name for Santa Claus

Examples

Every Christmas Eve, the children hang stockings in hopes that Kriss Kringle will fill them with gifts.
The story explained that Kriss Kringle travels the world delivering presents to good children.
In some regions, people still use the name Kriss Kringle instead of Santa Claus for the gift-giver.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Santa Claus - the legendary figure who brings gifts to children at Christmas

Etymology

From German 'Christkindl', meaning 'Christ child', altered in American English.
