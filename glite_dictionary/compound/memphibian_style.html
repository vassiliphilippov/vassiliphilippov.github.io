Memphibian style

1. NOUN

/ mɛmˈfɪbiən staɪl / (AmE), / mɛmˈfɪbiən staɪl / (BrE)
a cultural style characterized by influences from Memphis, especially in music, art, or fashion
cultural style linked to Memphis

Examples

The band adopted a Memphibian style for their latest album, reflecting the city's storied musical traditions.
Her decor showcased a Memphibian style, blending blues-inspired motifs with urban Memphis flair.
Young designers are incorporating Memphibian style elements into street fashion this season.

Synset Examples

In Memphibian style, they embraced the city's music scene.

Etymology

Blend of 'Memphis' and 'amphibian,' probably as a playful term for something or someone from Memphis.
