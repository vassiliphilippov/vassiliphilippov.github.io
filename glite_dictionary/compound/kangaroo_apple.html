kangaroo apple

1. NOUN

/ ˌkæŋɡəˌru ˈæpəl / (AmE), / ˌkæŋɡəˌruː ˈæpəl / (BrE)
an Australian plant of the nightshade family, with racemes of purple flowers and edible yellow, egg-shaped fruit
Australian plant with purple flowers and yellow fruit

Examples

The kangaroo apple thrives in the warm climate of southeastern Australia.
Locals sometimes eat the fruit of the kangaroo apple once it is ripe and yellow.
Gardeners often grow kangaroo apple for its striking purple flowers and ornamental value.
