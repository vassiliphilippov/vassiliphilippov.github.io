memory bank

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛməri bæŋk / (AmE), / ˈmɛməri bæŋk / (BrE)
a store or collection of memories or experiences in one's mind, or a place where data is stored electronically
collection of memories or stored data

Examples

Looking back, my memory bank is filled with moments of joy and laughter.
She often adds new stories to her ever-growing memory bank.
He drew inspiration from his memory bank when writing his novel.

Synset Examples

Her childhood stories are stored in my memory bank.
He has a rich memory bank filled with adventures.
I keep a memory bank of our best moments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recollection - the action or faculty of remembering something
  • Antonym: forgetfulness - the inability to remember
