roaming freely

1. ADV

/ ˈroʊmɪŋ ˈfriːli / (AmE), / ˈrəʊmɪŋ ˈfriːli / (BrE)
moving about without restrictions or confinement
moving about without restrictions

Examples

Wild horses were seen roaming freely across the plains at dawn.
Children enjoyed roaming freely in the park all afternoon.
With the gate left open, the chickens were roaming freely around the yard.

Synset Examples

We saw donkeys roaming freely in the countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wander freely - to move about or travel without restrictions
  • Antonym: restricted - kept within limits or confinement
