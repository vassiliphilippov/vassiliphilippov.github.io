real shark

1. NOUN

/ ˈɹiːəl ʃɑɹk / (AmE), / ˈɹɪəl ʃɑːk / (BrE)
A highly skilled and aggressive person, especially in negotiation or business.
skilled, aggressive negotiator or businessperson

Examples

You have to be a real shark to succeed in the world of corporate law.
She's a real shark in negotiations, always getting the best deals for her clients.
Everyone in the office knows Jake is a real shark when it comes to closing sales.

Synset Examples

He's a real shark when it comes to negotiation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shark - a person who is ruthless or dishonest, especially in business

Etymology

From the use of 'shark' as slang for a ruthless or cunning person.
