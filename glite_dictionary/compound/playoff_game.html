playoff game

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪˌɔf ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈpleɪɒf ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a single game that is part of a series of games played to determine a champion in sports or competitions
one game in a playoff series

Examples

The team advanced to the finals after winning a tough playoff game last night.
Tickets for the upcoming playoff game sold out within minutes.
Emotions ran high during the decisive playoff game as both teams fought for victory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: playoff - a series of contests to determine a champion
