shrimp cocktail

1. NOUN

/ ʃrɪmp ˈkɑk.teɪl / (AmE), / ʃrɪmp ˈkɒk.teɪl / (BrE)
A cold appetizer consisting of cooked shrimp served with a spiced sauce, typically cocktail sauce.
cold appetizer with shrimp and sauce

Examples

She ordered a shrimp cocktail as an appetizer before the main course arrived.
The restaurant is famous for its spicy shrimp cocktail served with homemade sauce.
At the party, trays of shrimp cocktail disappeared almost instantly.
