docking facility

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑkɪŋ fəˌsɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˈdɒkɪŋ fəˌsɪlɪti / (BrE)
a place in a harbor where ships are loaded, unloaded, or repaired, often equipped with gates to control water levels
harbor area for loading or repairing ships

Examples

The port recently built a new docking facility to accommodate larger cargo ships.
Routine maintenance was performed at the docking facility after a long voyage.
The storm damaged the old docking facility, making it unsafe for vessels to moor.

Synset Examples

the ship arrived at the dock more than a day late

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dock - an area of water in a port for loading, unloading, or repairing ships
  • Synonym: dry dock - a dock from which water can be drained, used for repairing ships
