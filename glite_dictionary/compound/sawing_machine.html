sawing machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔɪɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈsɔːɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
A power-driven machine used for cutting wood, metal, or other materials with a saw blade.
power tool for cutting materials

Examples

The carpenter used a sawing machine to cut the wooden boards to the correct size.
Modern furniture factories often rely on automated sawing machines for mass production.
Always wear safety goggles when operating a sawing machine in the workshop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saw - a hand tool or power tool for cutting material, typically wood
