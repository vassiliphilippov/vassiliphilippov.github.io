billiard table

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪljɚd ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈbɪliəd ˌteɪbəl / (BrE)
a large, heavy table with a smooth, cloth-covered surface, used for playing billiards, pool, or snooker
table for playing billiards or pool

Examples

The old pub featured a beautifully restored billiard table in its main room.
They carefully brushed the felt surface of the billiard table before every game.
Installing a billiard table made their basement the perfect spot for weekend gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pool table - table on which the game of pool is played
