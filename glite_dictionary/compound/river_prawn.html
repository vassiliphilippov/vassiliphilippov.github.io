river prawn

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪvɚ prɔn / (AmE), / ˈrɪvə prɑːn / (BrE)
a large freshwater prawn, especially found in Australian rivers, valued as food
large freshwater prawn, esp. in Australia

Examples

The fisherman caught a massive river prawn in the Murray River yesterday.
Grilled river prawns are a specialty in many Australian restaurants.
She prepared a delicious curry using fresh river prawns from the market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prawn - a small edible sea or freshwater crustacean

2. NOUN

/ ˈrɪvər prɑːn / (AmE), / ˈrɪvə prɔːn / (BrE)
a large edible freshwater prawn, often exceeding a foot in length, commonly found in Australian rivers
large edible freshwater prawn of Australia

Examples

The chef specialized in dishes that feature the tender meat of river prawns.
During our trip to northern Australia, we caught a massive river prawn in the creek.
Freshly grilled river prawns are a delicacy in many riverside restaurants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prawn - a small edible shellfish with a long body and legs, similar to a shrimp
  • Synonym: shrimp - a small edible marine crustacean similar to a prawn
