front of mind

1. NOUN

/ frʌnt əv maɪnd / (AmE), / frʌnt əv maɪnd / (BrE)
(of an idea, issue, or consideration) being foremost in one's thoughts; a primary focus or immediate concern.
foremost consideration or concern

Examples

Keeping safety at the front of mind is crucial in construction work.
Sustainability remains front of mind for many businesses today.
As you prepare your speech, try to keep the audience's interests at the front of mind.

Synset Examples

Customer feedback should be at the front of mind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top of mind - being foremost in one's thoughts or awareness
