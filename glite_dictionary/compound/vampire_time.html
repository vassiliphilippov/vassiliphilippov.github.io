vampire time

1. NOUN

/ ˈvæmˌpaɪər taɪm / (AmE), / ˈvæmpaɪə taɪm / (BrE)
the late-night hours when nightlife is active, typically marked by a lively or energetic atmosphere
late-night hours with active nightlife
informal

Examples

During vampire time, the city's bars and clubs are packed with people enjoying themselves.
If you're out and about during vampire time, you'll see a different side of the city.
She prefers working late shifts because she's naturally energetic at vampire time.

Synset Examples

At vampire time, the city transforms into a nightlife paradise.
She always feels more alive during vampire time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nightlife - social activities and entertainment available at night

Etymology

Alludes to vampires, mythical creatures traditionally believed to be active at night.
