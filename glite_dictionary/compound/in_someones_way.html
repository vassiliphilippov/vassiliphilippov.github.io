in someone's way

1. ADV

/ ɪn sʌmˌwʌnz weɪ / (AmE), / ɪn sʌmˌwʌnz weɪ / (BrE)
preventing someone from doing something by being an obstacle or hindrance.
acting as an obstacle; hindering someone

Examples

She couldn't finish her project because unexpected problems were in her way.
He moved the boxes that were in my way so I could walk through the hallway.
If you want to succeed, don't let anyone stand in your way.

Synset Examples

she might have succeeded in her ambition, had not circumstances been in her way

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stand in someone's way - to prevent or obstruct someone from doing something
  • Synonym: in the way - providing an obstruction or hindrance
