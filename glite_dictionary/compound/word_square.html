word square

1. NOUN

/ wɝd skwɛr / (AmE), / wɜːd skweə / (BrE)
a puzzle consisting of a square grid in which words run the same horizontally and vertically, so that each row and column contains the same set of words
a puzzle with words reading the same across and down

Examples

He completed the word square in under five minutes.
Each row in a word square must spell a valid word.
The puzzle magazine featured a challenging word square on its back cover.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crossword - a puzzle in which words are filled into a grid from clues, crossing each other
