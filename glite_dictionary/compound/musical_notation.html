musical notation

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪkəl noʊˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl nəʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a system of symbols used to represent music in written form so that it can be performed by musicians
system for writing music

Examples

Learning to read musical notation is essential for most classical musicians.
The composer wrote the symphony using traditional musical notation.
Many people find musical notation challenging at first, but it becomes easier with practice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: notation - a set of symbols used to represent something, typically in writing or mathematics
