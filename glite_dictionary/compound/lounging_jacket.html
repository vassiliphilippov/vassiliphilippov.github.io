lounging jacket

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaʊndʒɪŋ ˌdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaʊndʒɪŋ ˌdʒækɪt / (BrE)
a man's soft jacket, often with a tie belt, designed for wearing at home
soft men's jacket for home wear

Examples

He changed into his lounging jacket as soon as he got home from work.
The lounging jacket was made from luxurious velvet and had a comfortable tie belt.
In the evenings, he preferred wearing a lounging jacket while reading by the fire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoking jacket - a soft jacket worn by men at home, often while smoking
