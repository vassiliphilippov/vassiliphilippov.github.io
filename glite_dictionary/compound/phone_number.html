phone number

1. NOUN

/ foʊn ˈnʌmbɚ / (AmE), / fəʊn ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
a sequence of numbers assigned to a telephone line, used to make or receive calls from that line
number used to call a telephone

Examples

Could you give me your phone number so I can contact you tomorrow?
She changed her phone number after moving to a new city.
Please write your phone number at the top of the form.

Synset Examples

he has an unlisted number

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telephone number - number used to call a particular telephone
