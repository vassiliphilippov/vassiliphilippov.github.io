party animal

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːrti ˌænɪməl / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti ˌænɪməl / (BrE)
an enthusiastic person who frequently attends parties and enjoys socializing
person who loves going to parties
informal

Examples

Ever since college, he's been known as the group's party animal.
Despite her long work hours, Mia is still a party animal on weekends.
If you're looking for a party animal, Jake is the life of every event.

Synset Examples

My friend is such a party animal, always organizing events.
He’s a real party animal, always the last to leave.
As a party animal, she never misses a good time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: socialite - a person who is well known in fashionable society and enjoys social activities
