high point

1. NOUN

/ haɪ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / haɪ pɔɪnt / (BrE)
The most enjoyable, exciting, or important part of an event or experience.
most enjoyable or important part

Examples

Winning the championship was the high point of her career.
The high point of the trip was watching the sunrise over the mountains.
For many fans, the encore performance was the high point of the concert.

Synset Examples

the trumpet solo was the high point of the concert

Related Terms

  • Synonym: highlight - the best or most interesting part of something
  • Antonym: low point - the least enjoyable or worst part of something
