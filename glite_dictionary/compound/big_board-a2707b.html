big board

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ bɔrd / (AmE), / bɪɡ bɔːd / (BrE)
the large electronic display at the New York Stock Exchange that shows the prices and activity of stocks traded on the exchange
large display at NYSE showing stock prices
american

Examples

The big board lit up as trading began on Monday morning.
On Wall Street, all eyes are often on the big board during times of high volatility.
Tourists visiting the New York Stock Exchange are often impressed by the size of the big board.
