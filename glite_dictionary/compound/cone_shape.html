cone shape

1. NOUN

/ koʊn ʃeɪp / (AmE), / kəʊn ʃeɪp / (BrE)
a solid or surface with a circular base that tapers smoothly to a single point at the top
geometric shape with a circular base and pointed top

Examples

The ice cream was served in a cone shape that made it easy to hold.
Architects designed the roof with a cone shape to allow for better water drainage.
Pine trees often have a natural cone shape that helps shed heavy snowfall.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cylinder - a solid with straight parallel sides and a circular or oval cross-section, not tapering to a point
  • Synonym: pyramid - a solid with a polygonal base and triangular faces meeting at a point
