flood of nostalgia

1. NOUN

/ flʌd əv nəˈstældʒə / (AmE), / flʌd əv nɒsˈtaldʒiə / (BrE)
a sudden and overwhelming feeling of nostalgia
overwhelming feeling of nostalgia

Examples

Hearing that old song brought a flood of nostalgia over him.
As she looked through the photo album, a flood of nostalgia washed over her.
Returning to his hometown always triggers a flood of nostalgia for him.

Synset Examples

He spoke about his childhood in floods of nostalgia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wave of nostalgia - a strong feeling of nostalgia that rises quickly
