enterprise zone

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛn.tɚˌpraɪz zoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɛn.tə.praɪz zəʊn / (BrE)
an area in a city where businesses receive special tax concessions and other benefits to encourage development and investment.
city area with business tax benefits

Examples

The government hopes the creation of an enterprise zone will attract new companies to the area.
Businesses in the enterprise zone benefit from generous tax breaks and easier planning regulations.
Several jobs were created after the city established an enterprise zone downtown.
