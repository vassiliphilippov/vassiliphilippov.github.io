River Adige

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑː.dɪdʒeɪ / (AmE), / ˈædɪdʒeɪ / (BrE)
a river in northern Italy that flows southeast into the Adriatic Sea, passing through cities such as Verona
river in northern Italy

Examples

The city of Verona is situated on the banks of the River Adige.
Flooding of the River Adige has historically posed challenges to local communities.
The River Adige is famous for its scenic beauty and vineyards along its course.

Synset Examples

Verona is on the Adige

Etymology

From Latin Athesis, Italian Adige.
