natural language processing

1. NOUN

/ ˌnætʃɚl ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ ˌproʊˈsɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnætʃrəl ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ ˌprəʊˈsɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
the area of computer science and information science that focuses on enabling computers to understand and process human language
field of computer science for language understanding

Examples

Many modern applications like chatbots and voice assistants rely on natural language processing to understand user commands.
Advancements in natural language processing have made it possible for computers to translate text between languages automatically.
The course covers the fundamental algorithms used in natural language processing and their real-world applications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: computational linguistics - the scientific study of language from a computational perspective
  • Degree Lower: machine learning - field of study that gives computers the ability to learn without being explicitly programmed
