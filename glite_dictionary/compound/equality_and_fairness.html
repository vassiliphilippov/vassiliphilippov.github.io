equality and fairness

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈkwɑləti ənd ˈfɛrnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɒləti ənd ˈfeənɪs / (BrE)
the state of being just, impartial, and treating all people equally
justice and impartial treatment

Examples

The new policy was designed to ensure equality and fairness for all employees.
She fought tirelessly for equality and fairness in education.
Social reformers emphasize the importance of equality and fairness in a healthy democracy.

Synset Examples

Many believe socialism promotes equality and fairness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: justice - the quality of being fair and reasonable
  • Synonym: impartiality - treating all parties equally without bias
