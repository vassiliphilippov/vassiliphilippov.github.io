captain's cloak

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæp.tɪnz kloʊk / (AmE), / ˈkæp.tɪnz kləʊk / (BrE)
A symbolic garment or mark representing the role or qualities of a leader.
symbolic garment of leadership

Examples

After being promoted, she accepted the captain's cloak and led her team with confidence.
He struggled at first with the weight of the captain's cloak, but eventually grew into his leadership role.
Passing on the captain's cloak to her successor, the outgoing chair thanked everyone for their support.

Synset Examples

She wore her captain's cloak with pride, leading the team to victory.
He donned the captain's cloak, ready to face the challenges ahead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mantle - a garment symbolizing authority or responsibility
