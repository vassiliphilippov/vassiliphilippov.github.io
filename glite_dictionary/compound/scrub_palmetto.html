scrub palmetto

1. NOUN

/ skrʌb pælˈmɛtoʊ / (AmE), / skrʌb pælˈmɛtəʊ / (BrE)
a small, hardy, clump-forming palm with spiny leaves found in the southern United States
small spiny palm of the southern US

Examples

Wildfires can help the scrub palmetto thrive in its native habitat.
The scrub palmetto is a key feature of Florida's pine flatwoods ecosystem.
Many animals seek shelter among the dense clumps of scrub palmetto.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saw palmetto - a related palm commonly found in the southeastern US
