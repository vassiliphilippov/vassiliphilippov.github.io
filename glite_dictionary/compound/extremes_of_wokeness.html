extremes of wokeness

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈstrimz əv ˈwoʊk.nəs / (AmE), / ɪkˈstriːmz əv ˈwəʊk.nəs / (BrE)
the most radical or intense forms of social awareness or activism, often regarded as excessive or dogmatic
radical forms of social activism or awareness
informal

Examples

Some commentators argue that the extremes of wokeness can undermine open discussion.
The debate focused on how extremes of wokeness affect university policies.
She believes the extremes of wokeness distract from practical solutions to social issues.

Synset Examples

Critics often mock the extremes of wokeness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excessive political correctness - excessive adherence to socially progressive attitudes, often seen as overzealous
  • Degree Lower: wokeness - the state of being aware of social and political issues, especially injustice

Etymology

From 'extreme' (from Latin extremus, outermost) and 'wokeness' (from 'woke', African American Vernacular English for 'awake to social injustice').
