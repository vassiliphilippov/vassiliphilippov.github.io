wire gage

1. NOUN

/ waɪɚ ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / waɪə ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument or tool used to measure the diameter or thickness of wire
tool for measuring wire thickness

Examples

The technician used a wire gage to check the thickness of the copper wire.
Always verify the appropriate size with a wire gage before installation.
This wire gage is designed for measuring both metric and American wire sizes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gauge - an instrument or device for measuring the magnitude, amount, or contents of something
