fast one

1. NOUN

/ fæst wʌn / (AmE), / fɑːst wʌn / (BrE)
a clever or deceitful trick or action, especially one meant to deceive someone
deceitful trick or ruse
informal

Examples

Be careful—he might try to pull a fast one if you're not paying attention.
They caught her trying to play a fast one during the game.
You really thought you could get away with a fast one like that?

Synset Examples

he played a trick on me
he pulled a fast one and got away with it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trick - an action intended to deceive someone
  • Synonym: ruse - a cunning plan or action used to deceive or trick someone
