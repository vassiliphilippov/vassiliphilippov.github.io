target practice

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑrɡət ˌpræktɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɑːɡɪt ˌpræktɪs / (BrE)
the activity of improving one's shooting skills by repeatedly firing at set targets.
shooting at targets for practice

Examples

The soldiers spent the morning doing target practice on the firing range.
He goes to the shooting range every weekend for target practice.
Archers use hay bales in their backyard for target practice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marksmanship - skill in shooting; proficiency with firearms or archery

2. NOUN

/ ˈtɑrɡɪt ˌpræktɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɑːɡɪt ˌpræktɪs / (BrE)
the activity of shooting at targets to improve one's skill with firearms or other projectile weapons
shooting at targets for skill improvement

Examples

The soldiers spent the afternoon engaged in target practice at the firing range.
She signed up for target practice to improve her aim before the championship.
Many hunters do target practice regularly to maintain accuracy with their rifles.
