treadmill test

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɛdˌmɪl tɛst / (AmE), / ˈtrɛd.mɪl tɛst / (BrE)
a medical test in which a person walks on a moving treadmill while their heart and breathing rates are monitored, typically to evaluate cardiovascular health
test where you walk on a treadmill for heart monitoring
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended a treadmill test to assess his risk of heart disease.
During the treadmill test, she felt only mild shortness of breath.
A treadmill test can help identify problems with blood flow to the heart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stress test - a test measuring the heart's ability to respond to external stress
