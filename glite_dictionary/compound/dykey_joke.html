dykey joke

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪki ˌdʒoʊk / (AmE), / ˈdaɪki ˌdʒəʊk / (BrE)
A joke that involves or refers to stereotypes about lesbian culture, often in a manner that may be considered offensive or insensitive.
joke referencing lesbian stereotypes
slang offensive

Examples

He made a dykey joke that lightened the mood, though some found it inappropriate.
The comedian was criticized for including a dykey joke in her routine.
She warned her friend that a dykey joke might offend some people in the audience.

Synset Examples

He made a dykey joke that lightened the mood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lesbian joke - a joke relating to lesbian people or culture
  • Degree Higher: homophobic joke - a joke that expresses or promotes prejudice against LGBTQ people

Etymology

From 'dykey', derived from 'dyke' (slang for a lesbian), plus 'joke'.
