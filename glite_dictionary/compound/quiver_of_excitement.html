quiver of excitement

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɪvɚ əv ɪkˈsaɪtmənt / (AmE), / ˈkwɪvə əv ɪkˈsaɪtmənt / (BrE)
A slight or brief feeling or sensation of excitement.
slight feeling of excitement

Examples

As she read the letter, a quiver of excitement ran through her.
He felt a quiver of excitement when his name was announced as the winner.
A quiver of excitement passed through the crowd as the band took the stage.

Synset Examples

She felt a quiver of excitement before the performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrill - a sudden strong feeling of excitement or pleasure
