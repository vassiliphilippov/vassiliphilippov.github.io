carpet tack

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːr.pɪt tæk / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pɪt tæk / (BrE)
a small, sharp tack used for securing carpets to the floor
tack used to fasten carpets

Examples

The installer used a carpet tack to fasten the edge of the rug.
I found an old carpet tack under the floorboards while renovating.
Be careful not to step on a stray carpet tack when moving furniture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tack - a small nail with a sharp point and a flat head
