apple pie

1. NOUN

/ ˈæpəl paɪ / (AmE), / ˈæpəl paɪ / (BrE)
A sweet baked dish consisting of a pastry crust filled with sliced apples and sugar, often with a top crust.
a pie filled with apples and sugar

Examples

My grandmother bakes an amazing apple pie every Thanksgiving.
We served hot apple pie with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
The aroma of apple pie filled the entire kitchen.
