Republic of Kosovo

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv koʊˈsoʊvoʊ / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈkɒsəvəʊ / (BrE)
a partially recognized country in southeastern Europe, which declared independence from Serbia in 2008; capital: Pristina
country in southeastern Europe

Examples

The Republic of Kosovo declared its independence from Serbia in 2008.
Many countries have recognized the Republic of Kosovo as a sovereign state.
The capital city of the Republic of Kosovo is Pristina.
