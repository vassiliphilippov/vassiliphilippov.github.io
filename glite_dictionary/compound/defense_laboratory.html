defense laboratory

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛns ˌlæbrəˌtɔri / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns ˌlæbrəˈtɒri / (BrE)
a laboratory dedicated to research and development in support of national defense and military purposes
research lab for national defense

Examples

The university partnered with a defense laboratory to develop new surveillance technology.
Many breakthroughs in radar systems originated in a defense laboratory during the Cold War.
Security protocols are strict at every defense laboratory involved in weapons research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: research laboratory - a laboratory for scientific research
