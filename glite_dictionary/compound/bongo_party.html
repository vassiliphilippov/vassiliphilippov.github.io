bongo party

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑŋɡoʊ ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / ˈbɒŋɡəʊ ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
A party or celebration in which bongo drums or bongo music play a central role.
party with bongo music

Examples

We organized a bongo party for Sarah's birthday, complete with live drumming.
The atmosphere at the bongo party was vibrant and full of energy.
After a long week, everyone looked forward to the upcoming bongo party at the community center.

Synset Examples

The kids loved the bongo party with music and games.
