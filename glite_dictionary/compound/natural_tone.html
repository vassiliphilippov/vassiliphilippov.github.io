natural tone

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃɚəl toʊn / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl təʊn / (BrE)
a normal and unforced manner of speaking, without exaggeration or affectation
normal, unforced speaking style

Examples

The coach encouraged her to use a natural tone when giving instructions.
During interviews, speaking in a natural tone helps build trust with others.
He delivered his speech in such a natural tone that the audience felt at ease.

Synset Examples

He spoke in a natural tone during the presentation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: monotone - a continuous unchanging tone, especially of voice
