five hundred

1. NUM

/ faɪv ˈhʌndrəd / (AmE), / faɪv ˈhʌndrəd / (BrE)
the cardinal number equal to five times one hundred; 500.
the number 500

Examples

There are five hundred students enrolled at the college this year.
The stadium can hold up to five hundred people for special events.
She counted exactly five hundred dollars in cash for the deposit.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: five - the cardinal number 5
  • Degree Higher: five thousand - the cardinal number 5000
