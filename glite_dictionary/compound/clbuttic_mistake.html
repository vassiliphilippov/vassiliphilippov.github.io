clbuttic mistake

1. NOUN

/ klˈbʌtɪk mɪˈsteɪk / (AmE), / klˈbʌtɪk mɪˈsteɪk / (BrE)
An embarrassing or significant error, especially one caused by automated text filters that produce unintended and often humorous results.
embarrassing error, esp. from censorship filters
informal slang

Examples

The company's website contained a clbuttic mistake where the word 'classic' was changed to something nonsensical.
His embarrassing typo became a clbuttic mistake that everyone in the forum laughed about.
If you're not careful with automated filters, you might end up making a clbuttic mistake in your text.

Synset Examples

He made a clbuttic mistake during the presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classic mistake - a typical or commonly made error

Etymology

A blend of 'classic mistake' with 'clbutt', from software that automatically replaced 'ass' with 'butt', producing strings like 'clbuttic'.
