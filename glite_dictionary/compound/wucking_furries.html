wucking furries

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌkɪŋ ˈfɜriz / (AmE), / ˈwʌkɪŋ ˈfʌriz / (BrE)
(Slang) A humorous euphemism for 'fucking worries', used to show a lack of concern or indifference, especially in Australian English.
euphemism for 'no worries'; indifference
slang informal

Examples

Don't panic about the deadline—it's wucking furries as far as I'm concerned.
He shrugged and said, 'Mate, wucking furries!' when I apologized for being late.
When plans changed, she replied with a smile, 'Oh, wucking furries.'

Synset Examples

Stop the drama, it’s no wucking furries to me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no worries - phrase meaning 'there is no problem' or 'don't worry about it'
  • Synonym: no problem - phrase meaning 'it's not an issue'

Etymology

Playful alteration of 'fucking worries', using spoonerism and misspelling, popularized in Australian English.
