passbook savings account

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæsbʊk ˈseɪvɪŋz əˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsbʊk ˈseɪvɪŋz əˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
a type of savings account where all transactions, including deposits and withdrawals, are recorded in a passbook provided to the account holder
savings account with transactions recorded in a passbook

Examples

She opened a passbook savings account to keep track of her deposits more easily.
The bank issued a new passbook when he lost his passbook savings account.
Many older customers still prefer a passbook savings account over digital banking options.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: savings account - a bank account that earns interest, used for saving money
