miles per hour

1. NOUN

/ maɪlz pɚ ˈaʊɚ / (AmE), / maɪlz pə ˈaʊə / (BrE)
A unit of speed equal to the number of miles traveled in one hour.
unit of speed; miles traveled in an hour

Examples

The speed limit on this highway is 65 miles per hour.
The train was traveling at more than 100 miles per hour.
He can run up to 20 miles per hour during a race.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kilometers per hour - unit of speed; kilometers traveled in an hour
