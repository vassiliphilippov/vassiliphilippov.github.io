coffee royal

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔfi rɔɪˈæl / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi rɔɪˈal / (BrE)
A beverage made from black coffee, Cognac, lemon peel, and sugar.
black coffee with Cognac and lemon peel

Examples

After dinner, he ordered a coffee royal to wind down the evening.
The menu included a coffee royal, which paired rich coffee with a splash of Cognac.
She enjoyed the aroma of the lemon peel in her coffee royal.

Etymology

From French 'café royal' (royal coffee), referring to the addition of Cognac.
