entertainment deduction

1. NOUN

/ ɛn.tɚˈteɪn.mənt dɪˈdʌk.ʃən / (AmE), / ɛn.təˈteɪn.mənt dɪˈdʌk.ʃən / (BrE)
a deduction permitted for certain entertainment expenses incurred for business purposes, usually subject to specific limitations
tax deduction for business entertainment expenses
legal

Examples

The company claimed an entertainment deduction for taking clients to a sporting event.
Certain meals with business associates may qualify for an entertainment deduction under IRS rules.
He was audited after listing a large entertainment deduction on his tax return.
