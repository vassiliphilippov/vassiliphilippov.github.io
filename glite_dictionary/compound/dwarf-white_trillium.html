dwarf-white trillium

1. NOUN

/ dwɔrf waɪt trɪliəm / (AmE), / dwɔːf waɪt trɪliəm / (BrE)
A low-growing perennial plant with white flowers, found in the southeastern United States.
low white-flowered plant of southeastern US

Examples

We spotted a cluster of dwarf-white trillium growing near the riverbank during our hike.
The delicate petals of the dwarf-white trillium made it a favorite among local botanists.
Protecting the habitat of the dwarf-white trillium is important for maintaining regional biodiversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trillium - a genus of plants with three-petaled flowers
