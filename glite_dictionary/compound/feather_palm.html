feather palm

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛðər pɑm / (AmE), / ˈfɛðə pɑːm / (BrE)
A type of palm tree with long, divided, featherlike (pinnate) leaves.
palm with featherlike leaves

Examples

The majestic feather palm lined the avenue, adding an exotic touch to the landscape.
Gardeners prefer the feather palm for its elegant, arching fronds.
Several species of feather palm thrive in tropical and subtropical climates around the world.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fan palm - palm tree with fan-shaped leaves, contrasting leaf type
