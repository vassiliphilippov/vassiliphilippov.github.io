visual display unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪʒuəl dɪˈspleɪ ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒjʊəl dɪˈspleɪ ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
A device with a screen that displays output from a computer; a monitor, especially in British English.
computer monitor or screen (British)
british

Examples

The office was equipped with new visual display units to improve productivity.
Employees are advised to take breaks when working at a visual display unit for long periods.
He adjusted the angle of his visual display unit to reduce glare.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monitor - a screen that displays information from a computer
