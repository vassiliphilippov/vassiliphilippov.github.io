map-reader

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæpˌriːdər / (AmE), / ˈmæpˌriːdə / (BrE)
a person who is skilled at interpreting or using maps
person skilled at reading maps

Examples

She proved to be an excellent map-reader during the hike.
A good map-reader never gets lost, no matter how confusing the terrain is.
As the designated map-reader, he guided the group through the city.

Synset Examples

he is a good map-reader

Related Terms

  • Synonym: navigator - a person who navigates, especially using maps or charts
