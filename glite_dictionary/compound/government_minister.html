government minister

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌvərnmənt ˈmɪnəstɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvənmənt ˈmɪnɪstə / (BrE)
a member of the government who is in charge of a particular department or has a specific role, usually appointed to a high office
high-ranking official in government

Examples

The government minister announced a new policy on education reform today.
Protesters gathered outside the meeting, demanding the resignation of the government minister involved in the scandal.
As a government minister, she was responsible for overseeing the country's healthcare system.

Synset Examples

Minister of Finance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minister - a high-ranking government official in charge of a department
