car mechanic

1. NOUN

/ kɑːr məˈkænɪk / (AmE), / kɑː məˈkænɪk / (BrE)
a person whose job is to repair and maintain automobiles
person who repairs cars

Examples

The car mechanic diagnosed the engine problem within minutes.
She took her vehicle to a trusted car mechanic for regular servicing.
A skilled car mechanic can extend the life of your automobile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mechanic - person skilled in operating, repairing machinery
  • Synonym: auto mechanic - mechanic who repairs and maintains cars
