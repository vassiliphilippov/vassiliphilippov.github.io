Ossete language

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːsɛt ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɒsɛt ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A northeastern Iranian language spoken primarily in the Russian region of North Ossetia.
Iranian language of North Ossetia, Russia

Examples

The Ossete language is taught in some schools in North Ossetia.
Many older residents still prefer to speak the Ossete language at home.
She wrote her thesis on the grammar of the Ossete language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ossetian - Relating to the people or language of Ossetia

Etymology

From Ossetian *ирон æвзаг* (iron ævzag, 'Ossetian language'); 'Ossete' from Russian *осетин* (osetin).
