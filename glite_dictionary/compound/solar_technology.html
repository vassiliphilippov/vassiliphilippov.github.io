solar technology

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊlər tɛkˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə tɛkˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the practical application of scientific knowledge and equipment for harnessing energy from the sun
technology for using solar energy

Examples

Advances in solar technology have made solar panels much more efficient and affordable.
Many homes now rely on solar technology for their electricity and heating needs.
The workshop will cover the latest trends in solar technology and installation methods.

Synset Examples

She identifies as a solarist, focusing on solar technologies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solar power - energy generated from the sun's rays
  • Degree Higher: renewable energy - energy from sources that are naturally replenished
