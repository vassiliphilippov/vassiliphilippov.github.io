Lord's Day

1. NOUN

/ lɔrdz deɪ / (AmE), / lɔːdz deɪ / (BrE)
the first day of the week, Sunday, observed by most Christians as a day of rest and worship
Sunday as a Christian day of rest

Examples

Many families attend church together on the Lord's Day.
She prefers to keep the Lord's Day free from work and business.
The tradition of resting on the Lord's Day dates back centuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sunday - the first day of the week, commonly observed as a day of rest and worship by Christians

Etymology

From Christian tradition, referring to Sunday as the day commemorating the resurrection of Jesus Christ.
