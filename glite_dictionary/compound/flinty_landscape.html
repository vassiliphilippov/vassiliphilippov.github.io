flinty landscape

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɪnti ˈlændˌskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈflɪnti ˈlændskeɪp / (BrE)
an area of land that is rugged, harsh, and full of stones, especially flint
rugged, stony terrain

Examples

The flinty landscape stretched for miles, with barely a tree in sight.
She hiked across the flinty landscape, marveling at its forbidding beauty.
Travelers often found the flinty landscape difficult to cross due to the sharp stones and uneven ground.

Synset Examples

The flinty landscape was harsh yet beautiful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rocky terrain - an area of land covered with rocks
  • Antonym: fertile plain - an area of flat, fertile land
