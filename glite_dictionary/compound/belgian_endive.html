Belgian endive

1. NOUN

/ bɛl.dʒən ˈɛn.daɪv / (AmE), / ˈbɛl.dʒən ˈɛn.dɪv / (BrE)
a young endive plant grown in darkness to produce a narrow, pale, tender head, often used in salads and cooking
a pale-leaved vegetable; type of endive

Examples

She prepared a salad using fresh Belgian endive and walnuts.
The pale leaves of Belgian endive add a crisp texture to any dish.
Roasted Belgian endive pairs well with citrus and herbs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endive - a leafy vegetable of the daisy family, often used in salads
