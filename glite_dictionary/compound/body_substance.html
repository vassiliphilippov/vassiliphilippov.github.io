body substance

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdi ˈsʌbstəns / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˈsʌbstəns / (BrE)
any of the various materials, fluids, or tissues that make up or are produced by the body
material or fluid of the body

Examples

Blood is a body substance that plays a crucial role in transporting oxygen and nutrients.
Medical tests often examine samples of body substances such as urine or saliva.
Each body substance can provide important clues about a person's health.
