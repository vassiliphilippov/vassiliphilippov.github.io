throwing board

1. NOUN

/ ˈθroʊɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
a device, similar to a sling, used in various traditional societies to propel a dart or spear with greater force and distance
device for launching darts or spears

Examples

The ancient hunters used a throwing board to hurl their spears further and more accurately.
A well-crafted throwing board allowed warriors to increase the speed of their projectiles.
Archaeologists discovered a clay throwing board at the excavation site, revealing its historical significance.
