banded rattlesnake

1. NOUN

/ ˈbændɪd ˈrætlˌsneɪk / (AmE), / ˈbændɪd ˈrætlˌsneɪk / (BrE)
A venomous rattlesnake species with distinct dark bands, found in rugged regions of the eastern United States; also known as the timber rattlesnake.
a venomous rattlesnake of eastern US

Examples

Hikers in the Appalachians should be alert for the banded rattlesnake in rocky areas.
The banded rattlesnake is known for the bold black bands running across its body.
Encountering a banded rattlesnake in the wild can be a startling experience for campers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: timber rattlesnake - another name for the banded rattlesnake, Crotalus horridus
