true to

1. ADJ

/ truː tu / (AmE), / truː tə / (BrE)
faithful and loyal, especially by not having a sexual relationship with anyone else
sexually faithful; loyal in relationships

Examples

After many years of marriage, she remained true to her husband.
He promised to stay true to his partner, no matter what happened.
Despite being apart for months, they were both true to each other.

Synset Examples

she was true to her significant other

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faithful - remaining loyal and steadfast, especially in relationships
  • Antonym: unfaithful - not loyal; having a sexual relationship outside one's primary relationship
