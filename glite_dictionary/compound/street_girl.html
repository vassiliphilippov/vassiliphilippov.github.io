street girl

1. NOUN

/ striːt ɡɝːl / (AmE), / striːt ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a prostitute who solicits customers by walking the streets
prostitute soliciting on the street

Examples

He mistook her for a street girl because of where she was standing.
The novel depicts the harsh realities faced by many street girls living in poverty.
