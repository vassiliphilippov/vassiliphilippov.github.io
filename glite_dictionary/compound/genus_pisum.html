genus Pisum

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˈpaɪsəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈpaɪzəm / (BrE)
A small genus of annual Eurasian climbing plants in the pea family, including the common pea.
genus of Eurasian climbing plants including peas

Examples

The genus Pisum includes the well-known garden pea.
Botanists study the genus Pisum to understand the evolution of legumes.
Several varieties within the genus Pisum are cultivated for food worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pea - the edible seed or plant in the genus Pisum
  • Degree Higher: legume - a plant in the pea family (Fabaceae) that bears pods containing seeds

Etymology

From Latin 'Pisum' meaning 'pea', used in scientific classification.
