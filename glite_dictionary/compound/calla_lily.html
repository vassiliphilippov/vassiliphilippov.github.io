calla lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈkælə ˌlɪli / (AmE), / ˈkælə ˌlɪli / (BrE)
A South African plant with a large, showy white spathe and yellow spadix, widely grown as an ornamental.
ornamental plant with white spathe and yellow spadix

Examples

The bride carried a bouquet of calla lilies on her wedding day.
In her garden, she grows beautiful calla lilies every spring.
A single calla lily in a vase can brighten any room.

Etymology

From modern Latin genus name Calla and English lily.
