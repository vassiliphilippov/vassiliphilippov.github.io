games-mistress

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪmz ˌmɪstrəs / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmz ˌmɪstrɪs / (BrE)
a female teacher responsible for sports and physical education at a school, especially in British schools
female sports teacher at a school
british

Examples

The games-mistress organized the school's annual sports day with great enthusiasm.
As a former games-mistress, she promoted fair play and teamwork among her students.
In her first year as games-mistress, she introduced new activities to the physical education program.
