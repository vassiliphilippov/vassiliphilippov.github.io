Silver Star Medal

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ stɑr ˈmɛdəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə stɑː ˈmɛdl̩ / (BrE)
A United States military decoration awarded for gallantry in action.
US military decoration for gallantry

Examples

He was awarded the Silver Star Medal for his bravery during the battle.
Her grandfather received the Silver Star Medal after serving in World War II.
The general pinned the Silver Star Medal on the soldier's uniform during the ceremony.
