losing a pet

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːzɪŋ ə pɛt / (AmE), / ˈluːzɪŋ ə pɛt / (BrE)
the experience of grief and sorrow following the death of a pet
grieving after a pet's death

Examples

Many people underestimate how painful losing a pet can be.
After losing a pet, it's important to allow yourself time to grieve.
Children often need extra comfort when losing a pet for the first time.

Synset Examples

The horror of losing a pet is unimaginable.
