all over the country

1. ADV

/ ɔl ˈoʊvɚ ðə ˈkʌntri / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈəʊvə ðə ˈkʌntri / (BrE)
in or from many different places throughout the nation; nationwide
nationwide; across the entire country

Examples

The competition drew participants from all over the country who wanted to showcase their skills.
News of the discovery quickly spread all over the country, exciting people far and wide.
People traveled all over the country to attend the once-in-a-lifetime concert.

Synset Examples

The festival in Ashvegas attracted visitors from all over the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nationwide - existing or happening in all parts of a nation
