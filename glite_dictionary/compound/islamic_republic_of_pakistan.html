Islamic Republic of Pakistan

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈzlæmɪk rɪˈpʌblɪk əv pæˈkɪstæn / (AmE), / ɪˈzlæmɪk rɪˈpʌblɪk əv pækɪˈstɑːn / (BrE)
The official name for Pakistan, a country in South Asia located primarily in the Indus River valley, established as an independent Muslim republic in 1947.
official name of Pakistan (country)

Examples

The official name of the country is the Islamic Republic of Pakistan.
She visited the Islamic Republic of Pakistan to study its rich culture and history.
Diplomatic relations were established between the UK and the Islamic Republic of Pakistan in 1947.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pakistan - South Asian country, formerly part of India
