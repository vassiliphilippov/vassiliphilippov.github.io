creative solution

1. NOUN

/ kriˈeɪtɪv səˈluːʃən / (AmE), / kriˈeɪ.tɪv səˈluː.ʃən / (BrE)
an original or innovative answer to a problem, especially one that departs from conventional ideas
an original answer to a problem

Examples

The team came up with a creative solution to reduce waste in the company.
Her creative solution impressed the judges during the innovation competition.
We need a creative solution to address budget constraints without sacrificing quality.

Synset Examples

He's known for his creative solutions in challenging situations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: innovative solution - a solution that introduces new ideas or methods
