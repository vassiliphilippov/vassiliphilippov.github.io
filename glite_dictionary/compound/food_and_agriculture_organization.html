Food and Agriculture Organization

1. PROPN

/ fuːd ənd ˈæɡrɪˌkʌltʃər ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / fuːd ənd ˈæɡrɪˌkʌltʃə ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an agency of the United Nations that coordinates international efforts to improve nutrition and food security, and to advance agriculture worldwide
UN agency for food and agriculture

Examples

The Food and Agriculture Organization works globally to eliminate hunger and promote sustainable farming practices.
Italy is home to the headquarters of the Food and Agriculture Organization.
The Food and Agriculture Organization publishes annual reports on world food security.
