Mount Asama

1. PROPN

/ maʊnt əˈsɑːmə / (AmE), / maʊnt əˈsɑːmə / (BrE)
a volcano in central Honshu, Japan, near Nagano, and one of the largest in the country, rising to 8,340 feet (2,548 meters)
volcano in central Honshu, Japan

Examples

The eruption of Mount Asama in 1783 was one of the most devastating volcanic events in Japanese history.
From the hiking trail, visitors enjoy panoramic views of Mount Asama and the surrounding countryside.
Scientists monitor Mount Asama closely due to its frequent volcanic activity.

Etymology

From Japanese 浅間山 (Asama-yama), meaning 'Mount Asama'.
