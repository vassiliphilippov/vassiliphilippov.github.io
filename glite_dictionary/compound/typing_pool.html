typing pool

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪpɪŋ puːl / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpɪŋ puːl / (BrE)
a group of typists, typically in an office, assigned to do typing work for various people or departments
group of office typists

Examples

The company once had a large typing pool to handle all correspondence.
She worked her way up from the typing pool to a senior management position.
With the advent of personal computers, the typing pool became obsolete in many offices.
