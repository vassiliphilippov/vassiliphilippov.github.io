mountain parsley fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntn ˈpɑrsli fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˈpɑːsli fɜːn / (BrE)
a small fern of Europe and Asia Minor with a short, slender rhizome and densely tufted bright green fronds resembling parsley
a small fern resembling parsley

Examples

The mountain parsley fern thrives in rocky areas of Europe and Asia Minor.
Gardeners admire the mountain parsley fern for its bright green, parsley-like fronds.
We found several mountain parsley ferns growing beside the mountain trail.
