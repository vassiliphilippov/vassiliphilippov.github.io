family Lobeliaceae

1. NOUN

/ fəˈmɪli ˌloʊˌbɛliˈeɪsiˌaɪ / (AmE), / fəˈmɪli ˌləʊˌbiːlɪˈeɪsiːaɪ / (BrE)
A family of flowering plants, sometimes recognized separately but often included within the Campanulaceae in some botanical classifications.
plant family, sometimes part of Campanulaceae

Examples

There are several genera within the family Lobeliaceae that are of horticultural interest.
Some taxonomists choose to merge the family Lobeliaceae into the Campanulaceae.
The family Lobeliaceae comprises mostly herbaceous plants found in tropical and temperate regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Campanulaceae - A family of flowering plants, known as the bellflower family

Etymology

From genus Lobelia (named after botanist Matthias de l'Obel) + Latin botanical family suffix -aceae.
