town drunkard

1. NOUN

/ taʊn ˈdrʌŋkərd / (AmE), / taʊn ˈdrʌŋkəd / (BrE)
a person in a community who is known for habitual drunkenness
habitual drunkard in a community

Examples

Everyone in the village recognized old Henry as the town drunkard who spent his evenings at the bar.
The children were told to stay away from the town drunkard when he wandered through the square.
In stories set in small towns, there is often a town drunkard who knows all the local gossip.

Synset Examples

He was known as the town drunkard for years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drunkard - a person who is habitually drunk
