mental dexterity

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.tl dɛkˈstɛr.ɪ.ti / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.təl dɛkˈstɛr.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the ability to think quickly and intelligently; quickness and alertness of mind
quickness and intelligence of mind

Examples

Her mental dexterity allowed her to solve complex problems in no time.
The game challenges your mental dexterity as much as your physical skills.
His mental dexterity impressed everyone during the debate.
