wind tee

1. NOUN

/ wɪnd tiː / (AmE), / wɪnd tiː / (BrE)
a T-shaped weather vane placed at an airfield to indicate wind direction to pilots
T-shaped device showing wind direction at airfields

Examples

The pilot checked the wind tee before making his approach to the runway.
A brightly painted wind tee stands at the center of the airfield.
Maintenance crews adjusted the wind tee after noticing it was not rotating freely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: windsock - a conical textile tube that shows the direction and strength of the wind, used at airfields
