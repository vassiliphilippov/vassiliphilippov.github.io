man versus time

1. NOUN

/ mæn ˈvɝːsəs taɪm / (AmE), / mæn ˈvɜːsəs taɪm / (BrE)
a situation involving a struggle to complete something before a deadline or within limited time
struggle against time constraints

Examples

Completing the assignment before midnight was a real man versus time situation.
During the rescue mission, it quickly became a classic man versus time struggle.
Every exam feels like a man versus time challenge to some students.

Synset Examples

The project became a true man versus time challenge.

In this race, it’s a man versus time scenario.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: race against time - an urgent effort to finish before a deadline
