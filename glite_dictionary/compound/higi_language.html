Higi language

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiːɡi læŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhiːɡi ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken south of Lake Chad, particularly in Adamawa State, Nigeria, and the Mandara mountains region
Chadic language of Nigeria and Mandara mountains

Examples

Linguists have documented the grammar of the Higi language in several academic publications.
Many people in the Mandara mountains speak the Higi language as their mother tongue.
Researchers recorded oral histories in the Higi language to help preserve local traditions.
