trash dump

1. NOUN

/ træʃ dʌmp / (AmE), / træʃ dʌmp / (BrE)
a place where waste materials are deposited and collected, usually for disposal
place for dumping waste materials

Examples

The city decided to close the old trash dump due to environmental concerns.
Wind scattered plastic bags from the trash dump across the nearby fields.
They found the abandoned sofa at the edge of the trash dump.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: landfill - a site for the disposal of waste materials by burial
  • Synonym: garbage dump - a place where garbage is dumped
  • Antonym: recycling center - a place where recyclable materials are collected and processed
