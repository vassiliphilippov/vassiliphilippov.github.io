plant-based diet

1. NOUN

/ ˈplænt beɪst ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / ˈplɑːnt beɪst ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet consisting mainly or entirely of foods derived from plants, such as vegetables, grains, nuts, seeds, legumes, and fruits, with few or no animal products
diet focused on plant foods

Examples

She switched to a plant-based diet to improve her health and reduce her environmental impact.
Many athletes attribute their increased stamina to a plant-based diet.
Following a plant-based diet can help lower cholesterol and the risk of certain diseases.

Synset Examples

He espouses the benefits of a plant-based diet.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: vegan diet - a diet excluding all animal products
  • Degree Lower: vegetarian diet - a diet excluding meat and fish but may include dairy or eggs
