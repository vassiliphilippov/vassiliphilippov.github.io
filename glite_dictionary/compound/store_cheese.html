store cheese

1. NOUN

/ stɔr tʃiːz / (AmE), / stɔː tʃiːz / (BrE)
An informal name for American cheddar cheese, typically the type sold pre-packaged in stores.
informal name for American cheddar cheese
informal american

Examples

My grandmother always buys store cheese for sandwiches because it melts well.
The recipe calls for sharp cheddar, not store cheese.
He prefers artisan cheese over the processed taste of store cheese.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheddar - A type of firm, smooth cheese originally from England
