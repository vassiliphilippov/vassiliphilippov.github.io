vapor bath

1. NOUN

/ ˈveɪpɚ bæθ / (AmE), / ˈveɪpə bɑːθ / (BrE)
a bath in which a person is exposed to steam or vapor, typically to induce sweating for therapeutic or cleansing purposes
bath with exposure to steam

Examples

After a long hike, he relaxed in a vapor bath to soothe his sore muscles.
Some spas offer a traditional vapor bath followed by a cold shower.
Doctors once prescribed a vapor bath as a remedy for certain ailments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steam bath - a bath in which a person sits in steam for cleaning or health

2. NOUN

/ ˈveɪ.pər bæθ / (AmE), / ˈveɪ.pə bɑːθ / (BrE)
A room or enclosure filled with steam where people bathe to cleanse and refresh the body.
room filled with steam for bathing

Examples

After his workout, he relaxed in the vapor bath for twenty minutes.
Many luxury spas offer a vapor bath as part of their wellness routines.
The hotel’s vapor bath is popular with guests looking to unwind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steam bath - a bath in which the body is exposed to steam
  • Synonym: sauna - a small room used as a hot-air or steam bath for cleaning and refreshing the body
