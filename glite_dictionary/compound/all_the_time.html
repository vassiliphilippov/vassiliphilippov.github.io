all the time

1. ADV

/ ɔl ðə taɪm / (AmE), / ɔːl ðə taɪm / (BrE)
continuously; without interruption or respite; always
continuously; always

Examples

She complains all the time about her job.
It's unhealthy to worry all the time about things you can't control.
He leaves his TV on all the time, even when he's not home.

Synset Examples

he plays chess day in and day out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: constantly - happening all the time; continuously
  • Antonym: intermittently - not happening all the time; occurring at intervals
