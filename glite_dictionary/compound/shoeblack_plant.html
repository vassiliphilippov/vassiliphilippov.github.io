shoeblack plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃuˌblæk ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈʃuːˌblæk ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
a large, showy shrub or small tree native to Asia, cultivated for its single or double deep red flowers
large Asiatic shrub with red flowers

Examples

The garden's centerpiece is a shoeblack plant covered in vivid red blossoms.
Grandma brewed tea using dried petals from the shoeblack plant.
In many tropical countries, the shoeblack plant is commonly grown as an ornamental shrub.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hibiscus - genus of flowering plants including the shoeblack plant
  • Synonym: rose mallow - another common name for various Hibiscus species
