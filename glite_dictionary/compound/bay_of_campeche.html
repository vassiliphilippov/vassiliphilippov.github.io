Bay of Campeche

1. PROPN

/ beɪ əv kəmˈpeɪtʃi / (AmE), / beɪ əv kəmˈpeɪtʃi / (BrE)
a large bay in the southern Gulf of Mexico, bordered by the Yucatán Peninsula to the east
bay in the southern Gulf of Mexico

Examples

Many oil platforms operate in the Bay of Campeche due to its rich petroleum reserves.
The hurricane shifted direction as it crossed the Bay of Campeche toward the Gulf Coast.
Fishermen in the Bay of Campeche rely on its abundant marine life for their livelihoods.

Etymology

Named after the Mexican state of Campeche along its coast.
