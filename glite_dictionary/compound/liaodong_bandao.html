Liaodong Bandao

1. PROPN

/ li.aʊˈdʊŋ bɑnˈdaʊ / (AmE), / li.aʊˈdɒŋ bænˈdaʊ / (BrE)
a peninsula in northeastern China, extending into the Yellow Sea between the Bohai Sea and Korea Bay
peninsula in northeastern China

Examples

Many ports on the Liaodong Bandao serve as key gateways for trade in the region.
During the Russo-Japanese War, the Liaodong Bandao was a major site of conflict.
Tourists visit the Liaodong Bandao for its scenic coastal landscapes and historical sites.

Etymology

From Chinese 辽东半岛 (Liáodōng Bàndǎo), meaning 'Liaodong Peninsula'.
