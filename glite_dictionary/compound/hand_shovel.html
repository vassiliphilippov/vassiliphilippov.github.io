hand shovel

1. NOUN

/ hænd ˈʃʌvəl / (AmE), / hænd ˈʃʌvəl / (BrE)
A small shovel designed to be operated with one hand, used for digging or moving small quantities of material.
small shovel for use by hand

Examples

She used a hand shovel to plant the flower bulbs in the garden.
The gardener misplaced his hand shovel after finishing the weeding.
A hand shovel is essential for small digging tasks in tight spaces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spade - a tool for digging, similar to a shovel but with a flatter blade
