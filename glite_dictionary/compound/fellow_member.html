fellow member

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛloʊ ˈmɛmbər / (AmE), / ˈfɛləʊ ˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
a person who belongs to the same group, organization, or society as another
person in the same group or organization

Examples

She was congratulated by every fellow member of the committee.
As a fellow member of the gym, he often helped newcomers learn the equipment.
Each fellow member contributed ideas during the team meeting.
