pointing trowel

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔɪntɪŋ ˌtraʊəl / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪntɪŋ ˌtraʊəl / (BrE)
a small trowel with a pointed blade, used to fill and finish masonry joints with mortar or cement
small tool for finishing masonry joints

Examples

The mason used a pointing trowel to carefully fill the gaps between the bricks.
A pointing trowel is essential for detailed work when repairing old mortar joints.
She skillfully applied the cement with her pointing trowel to achieve a smooth finish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trowel - a small hand tool with a flat blade used for spreading or smoothing plaster or mortar
