security blanket

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈkjʊrəti ˌblæŋkət / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərəti ˌblaŋkɪt / (BrE)
A blanket or soft object that a child carries for comfort and to reduce anxiety.
comfort blanket or toy for children

Examples

Even as a toddler, Lily refused to go anywhere without her security blanket.
He clutched his security blanket tightly on the first day of preschool.
Some children become attached to a particular toy or security blanket for years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comfort object - an object, such as a toy or blanket, used for reassurance and comfort

2. NOUN

/ sɪˈkjʊrəti ˌblæŋkət / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərɪti ˌblaŋkɪt / (BrE)
Something that provides psychological comfort or reassurance, especially when used habitually to ease anxiety.
object or habit giving emotional comfort

Examples

For many adults, their morning coffee acts as a security blanket before facing the day.
She always keeps her planner with her as a security blanket in stressful situations.
His trusty old jacket serves as a kind of security blanket whenever he travels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comfort object - an object that provides psychological comfort

Etymology

From the literal meaning referring to a blanket providing comfort to a child.
