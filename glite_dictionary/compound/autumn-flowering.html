autumn-flowering

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔtəm ˈflaʊərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəm ˈflaʊərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant) blooming or producing flowers in the autumn season.
blooming in autumn

Examples

The autumn-flowering crocuses bring color to the garden after summer's end.
Many autumn-flowering plants are valued for their ability to brighten late-season borders.
We added several autumn-flowering species to our collection to extend the blooming period.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: spring-flowering - blooming in the spring
  • Antonym: summer-flowering - blooming in the summer
