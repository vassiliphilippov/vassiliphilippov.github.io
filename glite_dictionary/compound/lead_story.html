lead story

1. NOUN

/ lid ˈstɔri / (AmE), / liːd ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
the most important news story featured in a newspaper, broadcast, or website
the main news story

Examples

The resignation of the prime minister was the lead story on every major news channel.
Tonight's lead story focuses on the economic recovery following the pandemic.
She was thrilled when her reporting became the lead story in the morning edition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headline - the heading or title of a newspaper article
