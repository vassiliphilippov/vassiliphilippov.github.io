flying squirrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaɪɪŋ ˌskwɝrəl / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ ˌskwɪr.əl / (BrE)
a small, nocturnal rodent that can glide between trees using membrane-like folds of skin extending from its forelimbs to hindlimbs
gliding nocturnal rodent

Examples

The flying squirrel glided effortlessly from one tree branch to another.
At night, you can sometimes spot a flying squirrel searching for food.
Unlike other rodents, the flying squirrel uses flaps of skin to glide through the forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squirrel - a small rodent with a bushy tail
