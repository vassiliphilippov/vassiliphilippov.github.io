marijuana cigarette

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛrɪˈwɑnə ˈsɪɡəˌrɛt / (AmE), / ˌmærɪˈwɑːnə ˈsɪɡərɛt / (BrE)
A cigarette made by rolling marijuana leaves for smoking.
a cigarette containing marijuana

Examples

She rolled a marijuana cigarette before the party started.
Possession of a marijuana cigarette can result in legal consequences in some countries.
The distinct smell of a marijuana cigarette drifted through the window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joint - a marijuana cigarette (informal/slang synonym)
