losing a loved one

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːzɪŋ ə ˈlʌvd wʌn / (AmE), / ˈluːzɪŋ ə ˈlʌvd wʌn / (BrE)
the experience of having someone close to you die, especially a family member or close friend
the experience of bereavement

Examples

Losing a loved one can be an overwhelming and life-changing event for many people.
He struggled for months after losing a loved one, finding comfort only in the support of friends.
Many people experience anxiety and sadness when losing a loved one unexpectedly.

Synset Examples

Losing a loved one is never easy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bereavement - the state of having lost a loved one through death
  • Synonym: grief - intense sorrow caused by loss, especially death
