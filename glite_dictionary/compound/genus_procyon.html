genus Procyon

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs proʊˈsaɪ.ɒn / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs prəˈsaɪ.ɒn / (BrE)
A genus of mammals in the family Procyonidae, comprising the raccoons.
genus of mammals including raccoons

Examples

The genus Procyon includes the common raccoon found across North America.
Researchers discovered a new species within the genus Procyon in Central America.
In zoology class, we studied the characteristics that define the genus Procyon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raccoon - medium-sized mammal native to North America

Etymology

From Latin 'procyon', adopted in scientific taxonomy.
