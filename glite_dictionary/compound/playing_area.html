playing area

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪɪŋ ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈpleɪɪŋ ˌeəriə / (BrE)
a piece of land or space specially prepared and marked for playing a sport or game
space set aside for playing sports or games

Examples

The coach inspected the playing area before the match began.
Children ran across the playing area during recess, laughing and shouting.
Strict rules prohibit food on the playing area to keep it clean for athletes.

Synset Examples

the home crowd cheered when Princeton took the field

Related Terms

  • Synonym: field - an area of land used for sports or games
  • Synonym: court - an area marked out for certain sports, such as tennis or basketball
