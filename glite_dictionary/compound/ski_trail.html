ski trail

1. NOUN

/ ski treɪl / (AmE), / ski treɪl / (BrE)
a trail or slope that has been specially prepared for skiing
specially prepared trail for skiing

Examples

The resort opened a new ski trail for intermediate skiers this winter.
After last night's snowfall, the ski trail was freshly groomed and ready for use.
She prefers the advanced ski trails that wind through the forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ski run - a prepared track or course for skiing
