flick knife

1. NOUN

/ flɪk naɪf / (AmE), / flɪk naɪf / (BrE)
A folding pocketknife with a blade that springs open automatically when a button is pressed.
spring-opening pocketknife
british

Examples

He always carried a flick knife for protection, although it was illegal.
The police found a flick knife hidden in his jacket pocket.
A flick knife can be dangerous if not handled carefully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: switchblade - a pocketknife with a blade that springs open at the press of a button (mainly American English)
