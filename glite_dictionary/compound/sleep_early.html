to sleep early

1. VERB

/ sliːp ˈɜːrli / (AmE), / sliːp ˈɜːli / (BrE)
to go to bed at an early hour; to fall asleep earlier than usual
go to bed at an early hour

Examples

I have to sleep early tonight so I can catch my morning flight.
Doctors recommend that children sleep early for healthy growth.
She tries to sleep early during the workweek to feel more rested.

Synset Examples

I need to sleep early tonight for work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go to bed early - to retire for sleep earlier than usual
  • Antonym: stay up late - to remain awake until a late hour
