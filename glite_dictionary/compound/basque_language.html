Basque language

1. NOUN

/ bæsk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / bask ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language spoken by the Basque people, unrelated to any other known language family
language of the Basque people

Examples

She learned to speak Basque language during her studies in northern Spain.
The Basque language has fascinated linguists because of its mysterious origins.
Children in parts of France and Spain are educated in the Basque language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Euskara - the native Basque name for the Basque language

Etymology

From French 'basque', ultimately from Latin 'Vascones', the name for the Basque people.
