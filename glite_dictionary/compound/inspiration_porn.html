inspiration porn

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.spəˈreɪ.ʃən pɔrn / (AmE), / ˌɪn.spɪˈreɪ.ʃən pɔːn / (BrE)
content, stories, or images that depict disabled people in a way that objectifies them for the benefit of inspiring non-disabled audiences, often ignoring their actual experiences and agency.
content objectifying disabled people as inspiration
informal

Examples

The documentary was criticized for turning her struggles into inspiration porn rather than telling her real story.
Activists spoke out against inspiration porn in the media, calling for more authentic representation.
She disliked being featured as part of inspiration porn that ignored her true feelings and needs.

Synset Examples

The article was criticized for being mere inspiration porn.
She rejected the notion of being used for inspiration porn.

Etymology

Coined in the 2010s, combining 'inspiration' and 'porn' (in the sense of material intended solely to provoke a reaction).
