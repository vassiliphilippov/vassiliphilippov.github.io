Navajo language

1. NOUN

/ ˈnævəˌhoʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈnavəhəʊ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Athabaskan language spoken by the Navajo people of the southwestern United States
language of the Navajo people

Examples

Many elders still speak Navajo language as their first language at home.
The school offers classes in both English and Navajo language to preserve the culture.
She is fluent in Navajo language and often translates for her community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Navajo - a member of the Native American people of the southwestern US

Etymology

From Navajo (Diné Bizaad), the name of the people and their language.
