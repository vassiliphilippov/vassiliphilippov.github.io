blue skullcap

1. NOUN

/ blu ˈskʌlˌkæp / (AmE), / blu ˈskʌlˌkap / (BrE)
A North American mint (Scutellaria lateriflora) that produces a resinous substance, formerly used as an antispasmodic.
North American mint, used medicinally

Examples

Some herbalists recommend blue skullcap for its calming effects on the nervous system.
The meadow was dotted with clusters of blue skullcap among the tall grass.
Early settlers used blue skullcap as an herbal remedy for muscle spasms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skullcap - any of various plants of the genus Scutellaria
