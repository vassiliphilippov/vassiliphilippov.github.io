Tulu language

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːluː ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtuːluː ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken mainly in southwestern India, particularly in the coastal regions of Karnataka and Kerala
Dravidian language of southwestern India

Examples

The Tulu language is primarily spoken in the region of coastal Karnataka.
Many folk songs and rituals are preserved through the rich tradition of Tulu language literature.
He is fluent in both Kannada and Tulu language.

Etymology

From the name of the Tulu people; Tulu is an indigenous name for the language and ethnic group.
