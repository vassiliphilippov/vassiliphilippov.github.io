king crab

1. NOUN

/ kɪŋ kræb / (AmE), / kɪŋ kræb / (BrE)
a large marine arthropod found along the Atlantic coast of North America, with a domed, horseshoe-shaped carapace and a stiff pointed tail; considered a living fossil and related to the wood louse
large Atlantic marine arthropod, domed carapace

Examples

We saw a king crab crawling along the shallow ocean floor during our beach walk.
The king crab is notable for its ancient lineage and distinctive horseshoe-shaped shell.
Scientists study the king crab to learn more about the evolution of marine arthropods.

2. NOUN

/ kɪŋ kræb / (AmE), / kɪŋ kræb / (BrE)
A large edible crab found in the northern Pacific, especially off the coasts of Alaska and Japan, valued for its meat.
large edible crab of the northern Pacific

Examples

The chef prepared the freshly caught king crab for the dinner special.
Many fishermen in Alaska rely on the annual king crab season for their livelihood.
We ordered steamed king crab legs at the seafood restaurant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red king crab - species of king crab, commonly caught for food
  • Synonym: blue king crab - species of king crab, less common than red king crab

3. NOUN

/ kɪŋ kræb / (AmE), / kɪŋ kræb / (BrE)
The meat of a large cold-water crab, especially from the legs, considered a delicacy in many cuisines.
meat of a large cold-water crab

Examples

Fresh king crab is often served with melted butter as a delicacy.
The restaurant specializes in dishes featuring Alaskan king crab.
She prepared a salad with chunks of king crab and avocado.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crabmeat - the edible flesh of a crab

4. NOUN

/ kɪŋ kræb / (AmE), / kɪŋ kræb / (BrE)
a large edible crab found in cold waters, especially of northern Europe and Asia, with long legs and a spiny shell.
a large edible spider crab

Examples

The massive king crab was the highlight of our seafood feast.
In northern Europe, fishermen often catch king crab in deep, cold waters.
A display at the aquarium features a live king crab clambering over rocks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spider crab - any crab of the family Majidae, with long legs and a rounded body
