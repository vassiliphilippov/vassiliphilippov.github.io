electric lamp

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk læmp / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk læmp / (BrE)
A device that produces light when powered by electricity, typically used for illumination.
lamp powered by electricity

Examples

She replaced the old bulb in the electric lamp on her desk.
The room was dim until he switched on the electric lamp by the sofa.
The museum displays an early model of an electric lamp from the nineteenth century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lamp - a device for giving light, especially one that can be carried or moved
