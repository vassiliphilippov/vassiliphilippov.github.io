punch pliers

1. NOUN

/ pʌntʃ ˈplaɪərz / (AmE), / pʌntʃ ˈplaɪəz / (BrE)
a tool resembling pliers, equipped with a punching mechanism, used for making holes in paper, leather, or similar materials
tool for punching holes in materials

Examples

She used the punch pliers to make holes in the leather belt.
The craftsman kept his punch pliers handy for quick repairs.
A set of punch pliers is essential in any leatherworking toolkit.
