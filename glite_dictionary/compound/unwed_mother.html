unwed mother

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˌwɛd ˈmʌðɚ / (AmE), / ʌnˌwɛd ˈmʌðə / (BrE)
a woman who has a child and is not married
woman with a child who is unmarried

Examples

The organization offers support to unwed mothers seeking housing and education.
As an unwed mother, she faced many challenges in her community.
The documentary focused on the experiences of several unwed mothers in the 1960s.

Synset Examples

She decided to raise her child as an unwed mother.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: single mother - a woman raising a child without a partner
