wasp waist

1. NOUN

/ wɑsp weɪst / (AmE), / wɒsp weɪst / (BrE)
An extremely slender or narrow waist, especially considered attractive or fashionable in certain historical periods.
very slender or narrow waist

Examples

The model's wasp waist was accentuated by the tight corset she wore.
Victorian fashion idealized the wasp waist, often achieved through restrictive clothing.
She envied the actress's wasp waist and tried to recreate the look with a wide belt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hourglass figure - a body shape with a narrow waist and wide hips and bust

Etymology

Named after the waist of a wasp, which is notably thin relative to its body size.
