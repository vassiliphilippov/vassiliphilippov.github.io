side view

1. NOUN

/ saɪd vjuː / (AmE), / saɪd vjuː / (BrE)
a view or image of an object, scene, or person as seen from the side rather than from the front or back
view from the side of something

Examples

The architect showed us a side view of the building to explain the design.
From the side view, you can see how far the wheels stick out from the car.
The artist sketched a side view of the horse to capture its shape.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: front view - view of something from the front
  • Antonym: rear view - view of something from the back
