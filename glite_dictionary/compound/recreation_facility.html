recreation facility

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃən fəˈsɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃən fəˈsɪlɪti / (BrE)
a public place or building equipped and managed for leisure activities and recreation
public place for leisure activities

Examples

The new recreation facility offers tennis courts, swimming pools, and a gymnasium for community members.
Many families visit the local recreation facility on weekends to enjoy sports and social activities.
The city is planning to build another recreation facility to accommodate the growing population.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leisure centre - public building with sports and recreation facilities
  • Synonym: sports facility - place equipped for sports and exercise
