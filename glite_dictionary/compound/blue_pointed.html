blue pointed

1. NOUN

/ bluː ˈpɔɪntɪd / (AmE), / bluː ˈpɔɪntɪd / (BrE)
a common blue-grey shark of the southwest Pacific, valued as a sport and food fish
Pacific blue-grey shark species

Examples

Many fishermen travel to New Zealand hoping to catch a blue pointed during the season.
The blue pointed is known for its streamlined body and speed in coastal waters.
As a sport fish, the blue pointed attracts anglers from around the region.
