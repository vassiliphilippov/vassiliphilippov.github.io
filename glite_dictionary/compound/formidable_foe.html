formidable foe

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrmɪdəbəl foʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmɪdəbl fəʊ / (BrE)
an opponent who is strong, difficult to defeat, or inspiring fear or respect
a strong and challenging opponent

Examples

The team faced a formidable foe in the championship match but managed to secure a win.
Throughout history, natural disasters have been humanity’s most formidable foe.
With her sharp intellect and determination, she became a formidable foe in every debate.

Synset Examples

In the movie, the green goblin was a formidable foe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adversary - someone you are competing or fighting against
  • Synonym: enemy - a person who is actively opposed to someone
  • Antonym: ally - a friend or supporter
