one of the best

1. X

/ wʌn əv ðə bɛst / (AmE), / wʌn əv ðə best / (BrE)
among the people or things with the highest quality or skill in a group
among those with the highest quality or skill

Examples

She is one of the best players on the team.
That restaurant is one of the best in the city for Italian food.
His book was considered one of the best of the decade by critics.

Synset Examples

She regarded him as one of the best authors.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: the best - the person or thing of the highest quality or skill
  • Antonym: one of the worst - among the people or things with the lowest quality or skill
