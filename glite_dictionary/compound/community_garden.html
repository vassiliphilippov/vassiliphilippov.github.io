community garden

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmjunɪti ˈɡɑrdən / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪti ˈɡɑːdn̩ / (BrE)
a piece of land gardened by a group of people, often from a local community, for growing plants.
shared garden for community use

Examples

The community garden brought neighbors together.
Many residents volunteer at the community garden during the summer months.
Fresh vegetables from the community garden are shared among local families.
