state treasurer

1. NOUN

/ steɪt ˈtrɛʒərər / (AmE), / steɪt ˈtrɛʒ(ə)rə / (BrE)
an official responsible for managing the finances and funds of a state government
official in charge of state finances

Examples

The state treasurer presented the annual budget to the legislature today.
After serving two terms as state treasurer, she decided to run for governor.
Every year, the state treasurer issues a report on government spending and revenue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treasurer - person in charge of funds of an organization or government
