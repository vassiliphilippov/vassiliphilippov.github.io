pure Bayhem

1. NOUN

/ pjʊr ˈbeɪ.hɛm / (AmE), / pjʊə ˈbeɪ.hɛm / (BrE)
extreme excitement or chaotic action, especially resembling the over-the-top, explosive style of films by director Michael Bay.
extreme excitement; chaotic action
slang

Examples

The movie was two hours of pure Bayhem and nonstop thrills.
After the team scored, the stadium erupted into pure Bayhem.
Her birthday party turned into pure Bayhem once the confetti cannons went off.

Synset Examples

During the festival, there was pure Bayhem everywhere you looked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mayhem - violent or extreme chaos or disorder

Etymology

Blend of 'pure' and 'Bayhem'—the latter a portmanteau of 'Bay' (referring to filmmaker Michael Bay) and 'mayhem'.
