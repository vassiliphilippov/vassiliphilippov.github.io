moving-picture show

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvɪŋ ˈpɪktʃɚ ʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmuːvɪŋ ˈpɪktʃə ʃəʊ / (BrE)
A form of entertainment in which a story is presented by a sequence of images and sound, creating the illusion of continuous movement.
entertainment using sequential moving images

Examples

They watched a classic moving-picture show at the old theater downtown.
The moving-picture show industry has changed dramatically with advances in digital technology.
During the 1920s, a moving-picture show was a popular Saturday night outing.

Synset Examples

they went to a movie every Saturday night
the film was shot on location

Related Terms

  • Synonym: movie - a film; cinema production as entertainment
  • Synonym: film - a story or event recorded by a camera as moving images
