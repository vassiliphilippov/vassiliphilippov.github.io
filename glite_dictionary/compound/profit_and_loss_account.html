profit and loss account

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑfɪt ənd lɔs əˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈprɒfɪt ənd lɒs əˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
an account prepared at the end of a financial period showing a business’s gross and net profit or loss
financial statement showing profit or loss

Examples

The company’s profit and loss account showed growth in net income this year.
Every business must prepare a profit and loss account at the end of its financial year.
Auditors reviewed the profit and loss account for any discrepancies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: income statement - financial statement showing a company's revenues and expenses over a period, synonym for profit and loss account
