wet dream

1. NOUN

/ wɛt driːm / (AmE), / wɛt driːm / (BrE)
an erotic dream that results in the involuntary ejaculation of semen, usually during sleep
nocturnal emission during an erotic dream

Examples

Teenagers commonly experience wet dreams as a normal part of puberty.
He woke up confused after having his first wet dream.
A wet dream can occur without any conscious sexual arousal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nocturnal emission - involuntary ejaculation during sleep

2. NOUN

/ wɛt driːm / (AmE), / wɛt driːm / (BrE)
An unrealistic hope or fantasy that is unlikely to be fulfilled.
unrealistic hope or fantasy
informal vulgar

Examples

For many aspiring musicians, landing a major record deal is just a wet dream.
His ambition of becoming a world-famous actor seemed like a wet dream to his friends.
They dismissed the idea as a political wet dream that would never become reality.

Synset Examples

She laughed, calling it a wet dream to own a mansion.
Winning the lottery was his ultimate wet dream.
His vacation plans were just a wet dream now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pipe dream - an unattainable or fanciful hope

Etymology

Originally referring to nocturnal emission; figurative use developed in the mid-20th century.
