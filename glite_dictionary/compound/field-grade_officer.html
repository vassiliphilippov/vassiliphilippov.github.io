field-grade officer

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːld ɡreɪd ˈɑːfɪsər / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ɡreɪd ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a military officer holding the rank of major, lieutenant colonel, or colonel, ranking above company-grade officers and below general officers
military officer: major, lieutenant colonel, or colonel

Examples

After his promotion, he became a field-grade officer and was assigned greater responsibilities.
Only a field-grade officer can authorize that level of operation within the battalion.
The meeting was attended by several field-grade officers and a few generals.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: company-grade officer - an officer of lower rank than a field-grade officer (lieutenant or captain)
  • Degree Higher: general officer - officers above the rank of colonel in the military
