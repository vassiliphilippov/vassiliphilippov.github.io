printing operation

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪn.tɪŋ ˌɑː.pəˈreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈprɪn.tɪŋ ˌɒp.əˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
an action or process that manages the production or display of printed information, especially in computing or technical contexts
process managing printed or displayed information

Examples

The software failed to complete the printing operation due to a connection error.
She initiated a printing operation to produce the financial reports before the meeting.
The user must confirm the settings before the printing operation begins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: print job - a single, discrete task sent to a printer
  • Synonym: display operation - an action that manages the display of information
