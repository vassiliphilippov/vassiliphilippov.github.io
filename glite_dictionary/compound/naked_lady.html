naked lady

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪkɪd ˈleɪdi / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪd ˈleɪdi / (BrE)
A species of amaryllis native to South Africa, often cultivated for its fragrant white or rose flowers; also used as a common name for other similar flowering plants.
a type of flowering amaryllis plant

Examples

The naked lady in my garden bloomed with beautiful pink flowers this summer.
Gardeners often plant naked ladies for their vibrant and fragrant blossoms in late summer.
After the leaves died back, the naked lady sent up its tall flower stalks.

2. NOUN

/ ˈneɪkɪd ˌleɪdi / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪd ˌleɪdi / (BrE)
a bulbous autumn-flowering plant (Colchicum autumnale) with white, purple, or lavender-and-white flowers, native to western and central Europe
bulbous autumn-flowering plant

Examples

The naked lady blooms in autumn, brightening gardens with its delicate purple flowers.
Gardeners often plant naked lady bulbs to add late-season color to their borders.
Unlike many bulbs, the leaves of the naked lady appear in spring and die back before the flowers emerge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colchicum - a genus of flowering plants including the autumn crocus
