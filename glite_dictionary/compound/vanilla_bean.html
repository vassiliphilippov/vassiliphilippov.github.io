vanilla bean

1. NOUN

/ vəˈnɪlə bin / (AmE), / vəˈnɪlə biːn / (BrE)
the long, narrow fruit of the vanilla orchid, whose seeds and pod are used as a flavoring
long pod used as flavoring

Examples

The pastry chef split a vanilla bean and scraped the seeds into the custard.
Real ice cream often contains tiny black flecks from the vanilla bean.
She bought a fresh vanilla bean for the recipe instead of using extract.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vanilla - the flavoring derived from the vanilla bean
