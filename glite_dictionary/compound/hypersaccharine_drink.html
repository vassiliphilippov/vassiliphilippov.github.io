hypersaccharine drink

1. NOUN

/ ˌhaɪpərˈsækəriːn drɪŋk / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəˈsakəriːn drɪŋk / (BrE)
a drink that is excessively sweetened with sugar
excessively sweetened drink

Examples

Many children prefer hypersaccharine drinks over plain water, especially during parties.
The nutritionist advised her to cut back on hypersaccharine drinks to reduce her sugar intake.
Some energy drinks are so hypersaccharine that they taste almost artificial.

Synset Examples

She loves hypersaccharine drinks loaded with sugar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sugary drink - a drink containing a large amount of sugar
  • Antonym: bitter drink - a drink with a sharp, unsweet flavor

Etymology

From hyper- (excessive) + saccharine (relating to sugar, from Latin saccharum, sugar).
