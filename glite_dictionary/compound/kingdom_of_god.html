Kingdom of God

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm əv ɡɑd / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm əv ɡɒd / (BrE)
the spiritual realm over which God reigns as king, or the fulfillment of God’s will on earth as it is in heaven, especially in Christian belief
the spiritual reign of God in Christian belief

Examples

Many Christians believe that helping others brings them closer to the Kingdom of God.
Jesus often spoke of the Kingdom of God in his teachings and parables.
Some interpret the Kingdom of God as both a present spiritual reality and a future hope.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kingdom of Heaven - an alternative term often used particularly in the Gospel of Matthew
