chattery kid

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃætəri kɪd / (AmE), / ˈtʃætəri kɪd / (BrE)
A child who talks a lot and makes a lot of noise, often in an excitable way.
talkative or noisy child

Examples

The teacher struggled to quiet the chattery kid during the lesson.
One chattery kid dominated the conversation at the birthday party.
Every classroom seems to have at least one chattery kid who can't stop talking.

Synset Examples

The chattery kids were excited about the field trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talkative - talking a lot
  • Synonym: chatty - fond of talking; informal term for talking a lot
