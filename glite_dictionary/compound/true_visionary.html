true visionary

1. NOUN

/ truː ˌvɪʒəˈnɛri / (AmE), / truː ˌvɪʒəˈnəri / (BrE)
A person who has exceptional foresight, imagination, and original ideas about what the future could be like.
person with exceptional foresight

Examples

Her colleagues respected her as a true visionary who could anticipate changes in the industry.
Steve Jobs is often remembered as a true visionary who transformed the world of technology.
It takes a true visionary to see opportunities where others see obstacles.

Synset Examples

She’s a great leader, or should I say, a true visionary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visionary - a person with original ideas about the future
