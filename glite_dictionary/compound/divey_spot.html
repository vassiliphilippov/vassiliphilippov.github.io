divey spot

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪvi spɑt / (AmE), / ˈdaɪvi spɒt / (BrE)
an informal, often shabby establishment, typically a bar, restaurant, or similar place regarded as low-quality but possibly appealing for its atmosphere or value
shabby informal place or bar
informal slang

Examples

We ended up at a divey spot on the corner that served surprisingly good cocktails.
He prefers a divey spot for dinner over a fancy restaurant any day.
That divey spot is popular for its cheap drinks and quirky vibe.

Synset Examples

Her favorite divey spot served the best tacos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dive bar - a disreputable and often run-down bar or pub

Etymology

From 'dive' (slang for a disreputable bar or club) with the informal suffix '-y', to indicate resemblance.
