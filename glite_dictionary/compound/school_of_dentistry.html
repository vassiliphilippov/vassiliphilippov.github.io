school of dentistry

1. NOUN

/ skuːl əv ˈdɛntəstri / (AmE), / skuːl əv ˈdɛntɪstri / (BrE)
a graduate or professional school where students are trained in dentistry and can earn degrees in dental medicine or surgery
institution for dental education

Examples

She hopes to be accepted by the school of dentistry after completing her undergraduate studies.
The school of dentistry organizes regular outreach programs for community dental care.
After graduating from the school of dentistry, he opened his own dental clinic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dental school - a college or part of a university where dentistry is taught
