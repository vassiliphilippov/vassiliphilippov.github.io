genus Gloriosa

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ɡloʊˈriː.oʊ.sə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ɡləʊˈrɪə.sə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants, sometimes placed in the family Colchicaceae, containing the species known as the glory lily.
genus of flowering plants; includes glory lily

Examples

The genus Gloriosa is best known for its striking glory lily, often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
Botanists debated whether to place the genus Gloriosa in the family Colchicaceae or Liliaceae.
He specialized in the study of the genus Gloriosa and collected several rare specimens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gloriosa - a genus of flowering plants including the glory lily

Etymology

From Latin 'gloriosus' (glorious), referring to the showy appearance of its flowers.
