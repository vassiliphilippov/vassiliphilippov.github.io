tip sheet

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪp ˌʃit / (AmE), / ˈtɪp ˌʃiːt / (BrE)
a publication that provides the latest tips, predictions, or information on a particular subject, especially in areas such as business, stock market, or horse racing
publication with tips or predictions

Examples

I always read the tip sheet before making investment decisions.
The racing fan consulted a tip sheet to choose which horse to bet on.
The magazine started as a simple tip sheet for music industry insiders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newsletter - a publication containing news on a particular topic
  • Synonym: bulletin - a short official statement or broadcast summary of news
