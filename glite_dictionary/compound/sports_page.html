sports page

1. NOUN

/ spɔrts peɪdʒ / (AmE), / spɔːts peɪdʒ / (BrE)
a page in a newspaper or magazine that contains articles and information about sports events and teams
page in a newspaper with sports news

Examples

He always reads the sports page first when he gets the morning newspaper.
The results of last night's game were featured prominently on the sports page.
She cut out an article from the sports page about her favorite basketball player.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sports section - the part of a newspaper devoted to sports coverage
