United Nations Secretariat

1. NOUN

/ juːˈnaɪt̬ɪd ˈneɪʃənz ˌsɛkrəˈtɛriət / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪtɪd ˈneɪʃənz ˌsɛkrəˈteəriət / (BrE)
the administrative department of the United Nations, responsible for carrying out the day-to-day work of the organization
administrative department of the United Nations

Examples

The United Nations Secretariat is responsible for organizing international conferences and maintaining official records.
Many international civil servants are employed by the United Nations Secretariat in New York.
Decisions made by the General Assembly are implemented by the United Nations Secretariat.
