roast beef plant

1. NOUN

/ roʊst bif plænt / (AmE), / rəʊst biːf plɑːnt / (BrE)
A type of iris native to southern and western Europe and North Africa, known for its purple flowers and leaves with an unpleasant smell.
iris with foul-smelling leaves and purple flowers

Examples

The roast beef plant is admired for its striking purple flowers despite its unpleasant odor.
Gardeners in Mediterranean climates sometimes cultivate the roast beef plant for its unique appearance.
She identified the plant by the smell of its leaves and confirmed it was a roast beef plant.
