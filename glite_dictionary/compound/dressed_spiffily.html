dressed spiffily

1. X

/ drɛst ˈspɪfəli / (AmE), / drɛst ˈspɪfɪli / (BrE)
Wearing clothing that is stylish and elegant.
wearing stylish or elegant clothes

Examples

He always made sure he was dressed spiffily for important meetings.
At the wedding, every guest was dressed spiffily in their finest attire.
She felt confident when she was dressed spiffily for her job interview.

Synset Examples

He dressed spiffily for the formal event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressed smartly - dressed in an elegant or neat way
  • Antonym: dressed casually - wearing informal or everyday clothes

Etymology

From 'spiffy' (stylish, elegant), likely of British origin, combined with 'dressed'.
