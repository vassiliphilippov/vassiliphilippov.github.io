gold-bearing

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡoʊldˌbɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌbɛərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of rock, soil, etc.) containing gold; having gold within it.
containing gold

Examples

The geologists conducted tests on the gold-bearing ore to estimate its value.
Early settlers were attracted to the region by rumors of gold-bearing streams.
A new gold-bearing deposit was discovered deep in the mountains.

Synset Examples

auriferous quartz veins

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auriferous - containing gold
