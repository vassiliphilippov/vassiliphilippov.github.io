wood mint

1. NOUN

/ wʊd mɪnt / (AmE), / wʊd mɪnt / (BrE)
an American herb of the genus Blephilia, with hairy leaves and clusters of purplish or bluish flowers, found in woodlands
North American herbaceous plant (Blephilia genus)

Examples

We found clusters of wood mint blooming along the shaded forest trail.
The leaves of wood mint have a faint, pleasant aroma when crushed.
Wood mint attracts bees and butterflies with its blue-lavender flowers in early summer.
