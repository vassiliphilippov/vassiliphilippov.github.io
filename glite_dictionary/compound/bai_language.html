Bai language

1. NOUN

/ baɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / baɪ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Tibeto-Burman language spoken in the Dali region of Yunnan, China
Tibeto-Burman language of Yunnan, China

Examples

The Bai language has a rich history and is primarily spoken in the Dali region of Yunnan.
She is studying the Bai language as part of her research on minority cultures in China.
Efforts are being made to preserve the Bai language among younger generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tibeto-Burman - language family including Bai and related languages

Etymology

Bai is the name of the ethnic group in Chinese (白族 Báizú).
