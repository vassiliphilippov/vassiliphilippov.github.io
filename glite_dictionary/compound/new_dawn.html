new dawn

1. NOUN

/ nu dɔn / (AmE), / njuː dɔːn / (BrE)
a fresh start or significant new beginning, especially after a period of difficulty or stagnation
fresh start; significant new beginning

Examples

The peace agreement signaled a new dawn for the country after years of conflict.
After recovering from illness, she was ready to embrace a new dawn in her life.
The company's innovative product launch marked a new dawn for the entire industry.

Synset Examples

Today marks a new dawn for our project.
This breakthrough represents a new dawn in technology.
He feels a new dawn is coming for his career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: new beginning - a fresh start; the start of something new
