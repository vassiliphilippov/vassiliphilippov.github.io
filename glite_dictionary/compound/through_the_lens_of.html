through the lens of

1. ADP

/ θru ðə lɛnz ʌv / (AmE), / θruː ðə lɛnz ɒv / (BrE)
considering or interpreting something from a particular perspective or point of view
from a particular perspective

Examples

The film examines cultural identity through the lens of immigration and adaptation.
She approached the debate through the lens of environmental justice.
His research analyzes technology through the lens of its impact on society.

Synset Examples

They studied the problem through the lens of science.
