Laplander culture

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæpˌlændər ˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈlæpˌlændə ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
the customs, traditions, arts, and social practices of the Sámi people, an indigenous group inhabiting parts of northern Scandinavia and Russia
culture of the Sámi people

Examples

Many museums in Scandinavia display artifacts celebrating Laplander culture.
Her research focused on storytelling in Laplander culture.
Tourists can experience traditional reindeer herding as part of Laplander culture.

Synset Examples

We learned about the Laplander culture during our travels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sámi culture - the culture of the Sámi people

Etymology

Laplander, referring to the region of Lapland, combined with 'culture'. 'Laplander' historically referred to Sámi people.
