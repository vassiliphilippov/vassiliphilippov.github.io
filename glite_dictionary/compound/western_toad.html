western toad

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛstərn toʊd / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən təʊd / (BrE)
a large species of toad (Anaxyrus boreas) found in a variety of habitats from southern Alaska to Baja California, west of the Rocky Mountains
species of toad native to western North America

Examples

The western toad can be found in forests, grasslands, and even deserts across its range.
Scientists are monitoring the population of the western toad due to recent declines.
Unlike other toads, the western toad often migrates long distances to reach breeding sites.
