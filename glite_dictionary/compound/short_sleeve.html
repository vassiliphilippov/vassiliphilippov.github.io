short sleeve

1. NOUN

/ ʃɔrt sliːv / (AmE), / ʃɔːt sliːv / (BrE)
a sleeve that extends from the shoulder to just above or to the elbow.
sleeve ending above or at the elbow

Examples

During the summer, I prefer shirts with short sleeves to stay cool.
The dress comes in both short sleeve and long sleeve versions.
He rolled up his short sleeves before washing the dishes.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: long sleeve - a sleeve that extends to the wrist
