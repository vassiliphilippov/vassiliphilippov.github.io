mop water

1. NOUN

/ mɑp ˈwɔtɚ / (AmE), / mɒp ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
Water used for cleaning floors, usually after being combined with cleaning agents and used with a mop.
water used for mopping floors

Examples

After scrubbing the kitchen, the mop water was filled with grime and dirt.
She poured the dirty mop water down the drain after cleaning the hallway.
Remember to change the mop water frequently for better cleaning results.

Synset Examples

The mop water became murky after cleaning the floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dirty water - water that is unclean or contaminated
