male reproductive system

1. NOUN

/ meɪl rɪˌproʊˈdʌktɪv ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / meɪl rɪˌprəʊˈdʌktɪv ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
the organs and structures in a male that are involved in producing offspring, including the testes, penis, and related glands and ducts.
organs involved in male reproduction
medical

Examples

The male reproductive system consists of organs such as the testes, epididymis, and prostate gland.
Disorders of the male reproductive system can affect fertility and overall health.
In biology class, we studied the structure and function of the male reproductive system.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: female reproductive system - the organs and structures in a female responsible for reproduction
