in view of

1. ADP

/ ɪn ˈvju əv / (AmE), / ɪn ˈvju əv / (BrE)
considering or taking into account a particular fact or circumstance
considering; in light of

Examples

In view of new regulations, the company has updated its safety policies.
In view of his performance, he was offered the promotion.
We decided to postpone the meeting in view of the weather forecast.

Synset Examples

In view of recent events, we need a new plan.
In view of the weather, the picnic is canceled.
In view of your experience, you're the best candidate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: considering - taking into account (something)
  • Synonym: in light of - because of or considering (something)
