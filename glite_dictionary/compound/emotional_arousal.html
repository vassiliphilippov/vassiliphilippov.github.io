emotional arousal

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmoʊʃənl əˈraʊzl̩ / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənl əˈraʊzəl / (BrE)
a state of being emotionally stimulated or excited, often resulting in expressive behavior or physiological changes
state of strong emotional stimulation

Examples

The film triggered intense emotional arousal in the audience, making many people cry.
High emotional arousal can affect your ability to make rational decisions.
Music is often used to increase emotional arousal during important scenes in movies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arousal - the physiological or psychological state of being awoken or stimulated
