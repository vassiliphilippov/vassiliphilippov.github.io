TV channel

1. NOUN

/ ˌtiː ˈviː ˌtʃæn.əl / (AmE), / ˌtiː ˈviː ˌtʃæn.əl / (BrE)
a specific television station and the programs it broadcasts
television station and its programming

Examples

Many people watch the news on their favorite TV channel every evening.
The movie will be broadcast on a different TV channel than usual.
She switched the TV channel to find a sports program.

Synset Examples

a satellite TV channel
surfing through the channels
they offer more than one hundred channels

Related Terms

  • Synonym: channel - a frequency band assigned to a television broadcasting station

Etymology

Shortened from 'television channel'. 'TV' is an abbreviation of 'television'.
