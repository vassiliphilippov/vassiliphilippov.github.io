recent memory

1. NOUN

/ ˈrisənt ˈmɛməri / (AmE), / ˈriːsənt ˈmɛməri / (BrE)
the period of time in the recent past that people can remember, especially in relation to notable events
period in the recent past as recalled

Examples

In recent memory, the city has not seen such rapid growth and development.
The debate was the most heated in recent memory, with both sides refusing to back down.
No other player has achieved this much success in recent memory.

Synset Examples

This is the worst storm we’ve seen in recent memory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: living memory - period remembered by people still alive
