pine nut

1. NOUN

/ paɪn nʌt / (AmE), / paɪn nʌt / (BrE)
the edible seed of certain pine trees, especially from species found in southwestern North America, often used in cooking
edible seed of a pine tree

Examples

She sprinkled toasted pine nuts over the salad for added flavor and crunch.
Pesto sauce is traditionally made with basil, garlic, olive oil, and pine nuts.
The chef recommended using pine nuts in the dessert for a unique texture.
