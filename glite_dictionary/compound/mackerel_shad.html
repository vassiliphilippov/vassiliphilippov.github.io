mackerel shad

1. NOUN

/ ˈmækɚəl ʃæd / (AmE), / ˈmækərəl ʃad / (BrE)
A small, silvery fish found along the western Atlantic coast from Nova Scotia to Brazil.
small Atlantic silvery fish

Examples

The mackerel shad is often seen in coastal waters during the summer months.
Fishermen in the region rely on mackerel shad as a source of bait and food.
A school of mackerel shad darted through the shallow cove.
