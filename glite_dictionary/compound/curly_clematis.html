curly clematis

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɜrli kləˈmætɪs / (AmE), / ˈkɜːli klɪˈmætɪs / (BrE)
A climbing plant of the southern United States with bluish-purple flowers.
climbing plant with bluish-purple flowers

Examples

The curly clematis added vibrant color to the garden with its bluish-purple flowers.
Gardeners in the southern United States often train curly clematis on trellises for a striking display.
I admired the delicate petals of the curly clematis climbing along the fence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clematis - a genus of climbing plants with showy flowers
