pungent smell

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌn.dʒənt smɛl / (AmE), / ˈpʌn.dʒənt smɛl / (BrE)
a strong, sharp, and often unpleasant smell that is easily noticed
strong, sharp smell

Examples

The pungent smell of onions made her eyes water as she chopped them.
A pungent smell wafted through the corridor from the trash bins.
After the storm, a pungent smell of wet earth and decay hung in the air.

Synset Examples

He couldn't stand the pungent odor of the fish.
The pungent smell of garlic filled the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acrid smell - a smell that is strong and unpleasantly bitter or sharp
