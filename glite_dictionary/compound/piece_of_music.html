piece of music

1. NOUN

/ pis əv ˈmjuzɪk / (AmE), / piːs əv ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
a musical work, such as a song or instrumental composition, created by a composer or musician
musical work or composition

Examples

The orchestra rehearsed a beautiful piece of music for the concert.
She composed her first original piece of music at the age of ten.
Each piece of music in the album tells a different story.

Synset Examples

the composition is written in four movements

Related Terms

  • Synonym: composition - a musical work that has been created
  • Degree Lower: song - a short musical composition with words, sung by a voice
  • Degree Higher: symphony - an elaborate musical composition for orchestra
