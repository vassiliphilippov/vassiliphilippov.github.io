staff vine

1. NOUN

/ stæf vaɪn / (AmE), / stɑːf vaɪn / (BrE)
A twining shrub native to North America, with yellow capsules that contain scarlet seeds.
North American shrub with yellow capsules and scarlet seeds

Examples

The staff vine is often admired for its brightly colored seeds.
Gardeners value the staff vine for its ornamental yellow capsules.
This trail is known for the abundant staff vine growing along the fence.
