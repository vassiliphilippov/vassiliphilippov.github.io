total psycho

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬l ˈsaɪ.koʊ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl ˈsaɪ.kəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who behaves in a completely irrational or extremely crazy way
completely irrational or crazy person
informal

Examples

During the meeting, he lost his temper and started yelling like a total psycho.
She called her ex a total psycho after his jealous messages.
They thought I was a total psycho for climbing the fence at midnight.

Synset Examples

He was acting like a total psycho during the argument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psycho - a person who is mentally unstable or extremely unbalanced
