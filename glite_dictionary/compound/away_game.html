away game

1. NOUN

/ əˈweɪ ɡeɪm / (AmE), / əˈweɪ ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a sports match played at the opponent's venue instead of the team's own home venue
sports match played at opponents' venue

Examples

The team won their away game against their biggest rival last night.
Playing an away game can be challenging due to unfamiliar surroundings.
The coaches prepared the players for a tough away game this weekend.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: home game - a sports match played at the team's own venue
