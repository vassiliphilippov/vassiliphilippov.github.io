beef roast

1. NOUN

/ bif roʊst / (AmE), / biːf rəʊst / (BrE)
a cut of beef that is suitable for roasting, often served as a main dish
cut of beef for roasting

Examples

She placed the seasoned beef roast in the oven to cook for Sunday dinner.
The menu featured a classic beef roast served with vegetables and gravy.
He carved the beef roast carefully to serve the family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roast beef - beef that has been roasted, usually served in slices
