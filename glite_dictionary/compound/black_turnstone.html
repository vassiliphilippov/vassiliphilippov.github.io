black turnstone

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈtɝːnstoʊn / (AmE), / blæk ˈtɜːnstəʊn / (BrE)
a species of wading bird native to the Pacific coast of North America, recognized by its black and white plumage
a wading bird of the Pacific coast

Examples

We spotted a flock of black turnstones feeding on the rocky shoreline.
The black turnstone is commonly seen along the Pacific coast during migration season.
Biologists studied the nesting habits of the black turnstone in Alaska.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turnstone - a type of wading bird known for turning over stones to find food
