to wear black

1. VERB

/ wɛr blæk / (AmE), / wɛə blæk / (BrE)
to dress in black clothing, especially as a sign of mourning
dress in black, often for mourning

Examples

Many people wear black to funerals as a traditional sign of respect.
She decided to wear black for a week after her friend's passing.
In some cultures, it is customary to wear black while in mourning.

Synset Examples

The widow wore black to the memorial service.
