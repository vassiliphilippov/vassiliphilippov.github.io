Islamic Republic of Mauritania

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈslæmɪk rɪˈpʌblɪk əv mɔːˈreɪniə / (AmE), / ɪˈslæmɪk rɪˈpʌblɪk əv mɒˈreɪniə / (BrE)
the official name of Mauritania, a country in northwestern Africa, largely covered by the Sahara Desert, which declared independence from France in 1960 and is governed as an Islamic republic.
official name of the West African country Mauritania

Examples

The Islamic Republic of Mauritania is known for its vast stretches of desert landscape.
Diplomatic relations between the Islamic Republic of Mauritania and its neighbors have fluctuated in recent years.
The president of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania addressed the United Nations General Assembly last week.
