insulting remark

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈsʌltɪŋ rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ɪnˈsʌltɪŋ rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
a statement that is intended to offend or show disrespect to someone
offensive or disrespectful comment

Examples

He made an insulting remark about her appearance that upset everyone.
One insulting remark was enough to end their friendship.
The politician apologized for his insulting remark during the televised debate.

Synset Examples

She found his insultant remarks very offensive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insult - a remark or action that is rude or offensive
  • Antonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration
