Swedish rye

1. NOUN

/ ˈswidɪʃ raɪ / (AmE), / ˈswiːdɪʃ raɪ / (BrE)
a moist, aromatic bread made with rye flour, molasses, and orange rind, typically leavened with yeast
a type of rye bread with molasses and orange

Examples

The bakery on Main Street sells fresh Swedish rye every morning.
Traditionally, Swedish rye is made with orange rind for added flavor.
I baked a loaf of Swedish rye to serve with soup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rye bread - bread made principally from rye flour
