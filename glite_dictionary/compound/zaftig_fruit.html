zaftig fruit

1. NOUN

/ ˈzæf.tɪɡ frut / (AmE), / ˈzæf.tɪɡ fruːt / (BrE)
A fruit that is full, plump, and juicy.
plump, juicy fruit

Examples

The zaftig fruit was perfect for making desserts.
She picked a zaftig fruit from the basket and bit into its juicy flesh.
Markets in summer are filled with zaftig fruit ready for eating or baking.

Synset Examples

The zaftig fruit was perfect for making desserts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: juicy - containing a lot of juice; succulent
  • Synonym: plump - having a full, rounded shape; chubby

Etymology

Zaftig from Yiddish 'zaftik' (juicy, plump), from German 'saftig' (juicy).
