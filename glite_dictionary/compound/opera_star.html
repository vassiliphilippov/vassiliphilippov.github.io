opera star

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑː.pɚ stɑːr / (AmE), / ˈɒp.ər ə stɑː / (BrE)
A singer who performs leading roles in operas and is widely recognized for their talent and acclaim.
famous lead opera singer

Examples

The renowned opera star took a final bow to a standing ovation.
It is every young singer’s dream to become an opera star and perform at famous venues.
The opera star dazzled the audience with her powerful and emotive voice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diva - a distinguished female opera singer
