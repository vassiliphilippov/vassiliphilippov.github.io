ice coffee

1. NOUN

/ aɪs ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / aɪs ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
a strong, sweetened coffee served over ice, often with cream or milk
coffee beverage served over ice

Examples

I ordered an ice coffee to cool down on the hot afternoon.
Some people like their ice coffee sweet, while others prefer it unsweetened.
The café's specialty is homemade ice coffee topped with whipped cream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iced coffee - coffee served cold, typically over ice; standard variant spelling
