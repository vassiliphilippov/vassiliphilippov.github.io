kidney bean

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪdni ˌbin / (AmE), / ˈkɪdni ˌbiːn / (BrE)
a large, dark red bean, usually dried, commonly used in cooking
a large, dark red edible bean

Examples

She added kidney beans to the chili for extra protein and flavor.
The salad was garnished with sliced kidney beans and fresh herbs.
Dried kidney beans must be soaked before cooking to soften them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bean - an edible seed, typically kidney-shaped, of various plants

2. NOUN

/ ˈkɪdni biːn / (AmE), / ˈkɪdni biːn / (BrE)
a variety of the common bean plant grown for its large, red, kidney-shaped beans, widely used as food
large, red, kidney-shaped bean

Examples

She added a can of kidney beans to the chili for extra protein.
Red kidney beans are commonly used in Indian and Mexican cuisines.
After soaking the kidney beans overnight, he boiled them until they were tender.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: bean - an edible seed of various plants of the legume family

Etymology

Named for their resemblance in shape to a kidney organ.
