Swiss mountain pine

1. NOUN

/ swɪs ˈmaʊntn paɪn / (AmE), / swɪs ˈmaʊntən paɪn / (BrE)
A low, shrubby pine tree native to central Europe, having short, bright green needles in pairs of two; Pinus mugo.
shrubby pine tree of central Europe

Examples

The Swiss mountain pine is commonly found on rocky slopes in the Alps.
Gardeners appreciate the Swiss mountain pine for its hardiness and compact size.
Many wildlife species use the dense branches of the Swiss mountain pine for shelter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mountain pine - another name for Pinus mugo, a shrubby pine of Europe
