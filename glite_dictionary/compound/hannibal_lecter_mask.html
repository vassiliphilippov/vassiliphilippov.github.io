Hannibal Lecter mask

1. NOUN

/ ˌhænɪbəl ˈlɛktər mæsk / (AmE), / ˌhænɪbəl ˈlɛktə mɑːsk / (BrE)
A mask resembling or inspired by the one worn by the fictional character Hannibal Lecter, typically covering the lower face and evoking an appearance associated with horror or restraint.
mask inspired by Hannibal Lecter character

Examples

For Halloween, he decided to wear a Hannibal Lecter mask to scare his friends.
The prisoner appeared in court wearing a Hannibal Lecter mask for security reasons.
Her costume was so convincing that the Hannibal Lecter mask made everyone uneasy.

Synset Examples

He wore a Hannibal Lecter mask for a chilling effect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mask - a covering for all or part of the face

Etymology

Named after the fictional character Hannibal Lecter from Thomas Harris's novels and related films.
