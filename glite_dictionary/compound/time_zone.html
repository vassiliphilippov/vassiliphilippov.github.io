time zone

1. NOUN

/ taɪm zoʊn / (AmE), / taɪm zəʊn / (BrE)
one of the regions into which the world is divided, where the same standard time is used
region sharing standard time

Examples

The meeting was scheduled to accommodate participants from every time zone.
Because she travels often for work, she is used to adjusting to a new time zone.
New York and Toronto are in the same time zone, so their clocks match.
