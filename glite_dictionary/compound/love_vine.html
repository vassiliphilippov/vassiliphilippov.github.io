love vine

1. NOUN

/ lʌv vaɪn / (AmE), / lʌv vaɪn / (BrE)
A leafless parasitic vine with dense clusters of small white bell-shaped flowers on orange-yellow stems that twine around host plants such as clover or flax.
leafless parasitic vine with orange-yellow stems

Examples

The love vine wrapped itself tightly around the clover in the field.
Botanists studied the spread of love vine and its impact on native plants.
Clusters of white flowers dotted the orange stems of the love vine growing by the roadside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dodder - another name for the love vine or related parasitic vines
