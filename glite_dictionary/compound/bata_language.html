Bata language

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːtɑ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːtə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Chadic language spoken primarily in the Adamawa State of Nigeria, especially in Yola North, Gombi, Song, and Fufore local government areas.
a Chadic language of Nigeria

Examples

The Bata language is still used in many rural communities in the Adamawa State of Nigeria.
Researchers are working to document the grammar and vocabulary of the Bata language.
She grew up bilingual, speaking both English and Bata language at home.
