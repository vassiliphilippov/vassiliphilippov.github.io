private investigator

1. NOUN

/ ˌpraɪvət ɪnˌvɛstəˈɡeɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˌpraɪvət ɪnˌvɛstɪˈɡeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who is hired to investigate and find information, especially about crimes or private matters, but who does not work as a police officer
a person hired to investigate privately

Examples

The private investigator followed the suspect for several days before gathering enough evidence.
After receiving threatening letters, she decided to hire a private investigator.
Many novels feature a clever private investigator solving complex mysteries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detective - a person, especially a police officer, whose job is to investigate and solve crimes
