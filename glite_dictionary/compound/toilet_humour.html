toilet humour

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔɪlət ˌhjuːmɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlət ˌhjuːmə / (BrE)
humour that is based on jokes about bodily functions, regarded as crude or childish
humour involving bodily functions

Examples

The comedian's show relied heavily on toilet humour to get laughs from the audience.
Some parents disapproved of the film's use of toilet humour in its jokes.
He finds toilet humour hilarious, but others think it's just immature.

Synset Examples

His jokes were all about toilet humour, very juvenile.
The kids laughed at the toilet humour in the show.
They bonded over their shared love for toilet humour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bathroom humor - humour based on bodily functions (US term)
