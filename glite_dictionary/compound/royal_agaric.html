royal agaric

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɔɪəl əˈɡærɪk / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪəl əˈɡærɪk / (BrE)
a widely distributed edible mushroom resembling the fly agaric, often recognized for its distinctive appearance
a type of edible mushroom

Examples

The royal agaric is often sought after by mushroom foragers for its delicate flavor.
Unlike the toxic fly agaric, the royal agaric is safe to eat.
Experts recommend correctly identifying the royal agaric before consuming wild mushrooms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fly agaric - a poisonous mushroom with a red cap and white spots
