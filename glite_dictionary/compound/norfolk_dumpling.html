Norfolk dumpling

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔr.fək ˈdʌm.plɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɔː.fək ˈdʌm.plɪŋ / (BrE)
a type of soft, steamed or boiled dumpling traditionally made in Norfolk, England, often served as a side dish
traditional dumpling from Norfolk, England

Examples

At the village fair, we sampled a steaming hot Norfolk dumpling with beef stew.
My grandmother always prepared Norfolk dumplings for Sunday lunch as part of a hearty meal.
The chef explained how to make a traditional Norfolk dumpling using only flour, water, and a pinch of salt.

Synset Examples

For dinner, I made delicious Norfolk dumplings.
They enjoyed Norfolk dumplings at the local restaurant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dumpling - a small mass of dough cooked by boiling or steaming
