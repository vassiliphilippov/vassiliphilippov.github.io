Royal National Eisteddfod

1. PROPN

/ ˈroʊjəl ˌnæʃənl aɪˈstɛdfɒd / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪəl ˌnæʃənəl aɪˈstɛdfɒd / (BrE)
the leading Welsh festival featuring competitions in music, drama, poetry, and the fine arts
major Welsh festival of music and arts

Examples

She won first prize for her poem at the Royal National Eisteddfod in Cardiff last year.
Every summer, thousands attend the Royal National Eisteddfod to celebrate Welsh culture and language.
The Royal National Eisteddfod showcases the best in Welsh music, drama, and poetry.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eisteddfod - a traditional Welsh festival of literature, music, and performance

Etymology

From Welsh 'eisteddfod', meaning 'session' or 'assembly'; 'Royal National' refers to its official patronage.
