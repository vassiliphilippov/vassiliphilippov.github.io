narrow-leaved bottletree

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæroʊ liːvd ˈbɑtəl triː / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ liːvd ˈbɒtl triː / (BrE)
a large Australian tree of Queensland with cream-colored flowers blotched with red inside, sometimes classified in the genus Sterculia
Australian tree with cream and red flowers

Examples

The narrow-leaved bottletree is commonly planted as an ornamental in Queensland.
Botanists are debating whether the narrow-leaved bottletree belongs in the genus Sterculia.
The cream-colored flowers of the narrow-leaved bottletree attract many native birds during blooming season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bottletree - Australian tree with a swollen trunk for water storage
