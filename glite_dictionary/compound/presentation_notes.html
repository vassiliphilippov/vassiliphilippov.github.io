presentation notes

1. NOUN

/ ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃən ˌnoʊts / (AmE), / ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃən ˌnəʊts / (BrE)
notes that are prepared to assist or accompany a spoken or visual presentation, often used as prompts or a script during the presentation
notes for a presentation

Examples

She glanced at her presentation notes before stepping onto the stage.
I accidentally left my presentation notes in the office, so I had to improvise.
He spent hours preparing detailed presentation notes for the meeting.

Synset Examples

I need a sheet for my presentation notes.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: notes - brief written records used to assist memory or understanding
