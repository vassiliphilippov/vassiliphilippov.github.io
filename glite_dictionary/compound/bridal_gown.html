bridal gown

1. NOUN

/ ˈbraɪdəl ɡaʊn / (AmE), / ˈbraɪdəl ɡaʊn / (BrE)
a long, formal dress worn by a bride at her wedding ceremony
dress worn by a bride at a wedding

Examples

She chose a beautiful lace bridal gown for her special day.
The designer specialized in creating unique bridal gowns for weddings worldwide.
After the ceremony, her bridal gown was carefully packed away as a keepsake.
