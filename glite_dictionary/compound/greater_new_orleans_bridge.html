Greater New Orleans Bridge

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡreɪtər nu ˈɔːrliənz brɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtə njuː ˈɔːliənz brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a pair of cantilever bridges crossing the Mississippi River at New Orleans, Louisiana
cantilever bridges over the Mississippi River in New Orleans

Examples

The Greater New Orleans Bridge carries thousands of vehicles across the Mississippi River every day.
During rush hour, long lines can form on the Greater New Orleans Bridge as commuters head into the city.
The Greater New Orleans Bridge is an iconic part of the New Orleans skyline.
