yoga-babble

1. NOUN

/ ˈjoʊɡə ˌbæbəl / (AmE), / ˈjəʊɡə ˌbæbəl / (BrE)
talk or language about yoga that is excessive, filled with jargon, or difficult to understand
excessive or jargon-filled talk about yoga
informal

Examples

The instructor's yoga-babble made it hard to focus on the practice.
I'm tired of all the yoga-babble and just want a straightforward explanation.
She wrote an article poking fun at common yoga-babble in popular wellness blogs.

Synset Examples

She tends to indulge in yoga-babble during classes.
Let's skip the yoga-babble and focus on the poses.
His speech was filled with yoga-babble that confused everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: babble - confused or foolish talk; meaningless chatter

Etymology

Formed by combining 'yoga' and 'babble', modeled on similar constructs like 'techno-babble'.
