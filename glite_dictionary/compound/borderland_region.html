borderland region

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔrdɚˌlænd ˈriːdʒən / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdəlænd ˈriːdʒən / (BrE)
an area of land situated near or along the boundary between two regions or countries
area close to a regional or national border

Examples

The annual celebration draws visitors from across the borderland region.
Economic development in the borderland region often depends on cross-border cooperation.
Wildlife thrives in the borderland region due to minimal human interference.

Synset Examples

The festival takes place in the borderland region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: borderland - land near a border or boundary
