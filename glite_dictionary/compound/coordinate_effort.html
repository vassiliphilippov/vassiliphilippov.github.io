to coordinate effort

1. VERB

/ koʊˈɔːrdəˌneɪt ˈɛfərt / (AmE), / kəʊˈɔːdɪneɪt ˈefət / (BrE)
to organize and direct the actions of people so they work together effectively
organize group work effectively

Examples

The project manager must coordinate effort among team members to meet the deadline.
Several organizations will coordinate effort in response to the natural disaster.
To improve productivity, we need to coordinate effort across departments.

Synset Examples

It's crucial to coordinate efforts for success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to collaborate - to work jointly with others
  • Synonym: to cooperate - to act or work together for a common purpose
