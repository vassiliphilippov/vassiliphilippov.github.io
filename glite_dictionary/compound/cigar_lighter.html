cigar lighter

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈɡɑr ˌlaɪtər / (AmE), / sɪˈɡɑː ˌlaɪtə / (BrE)
a portable device for igniting cigars or cigarettes, typically producing a flame or spark
device for lighting cigars or cigarettes

Examples

He took out his cigar lighter and offered a light to his friend.
Many cars used to come with a built-in cigar lighter on the dashboard.
I forgot my cigar lighter at home, so I had to borrow one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lighter - a portable device used to produce a flame
