urinary apparatus

1. NOUN

/ ˈjʊrəˌnɛri əˈpærətəs / (AmE), / ˈjʊərɪnəri əˈparətəs / (BrE)
the bodily system comprising all organs involved in the production, storage, and elimination of urine, sometimes also encompassing reproductive organs
bodily system for urine formation and excretion
medical

Examples

A blockage in the urinary apparatus can lead to serious health issues if left untreated.
Doctors examined the patient's urinary apparatus to diagnose the cause of his symptoms.
Damage to any part of the urinary apparatus may affect normal urine flow and bladder function.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urinary system - organs responsible for the production and excretion of urine
