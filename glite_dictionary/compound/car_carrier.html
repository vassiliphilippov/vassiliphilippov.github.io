car carrier

1. NOUN

/ kɑr ˈkæriər / (AmE), / kɑː ˈkæriə / (BrE)
a large trailer or truck designed to transport multiple cars, especially new ones, to dealerships or other locations
a vehicle for transporting multiple cars

Examples

The dealership received a shipment of new vehicles on a car carrier early this morning.
We saw a massive car carrier driving down the highway loaded with brand-new sedans.
After the auction, the classic cars were carefully loaded onto the car carrier for transport.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car transporter - a vehicle designed to transport cars (chiefly British English)

2. NOUN

/ kɑɹ ˈkæɹiɚ / (AmE), / kɑː ˈkæriə / (BrE)
A cargo ship specially equipped to transport large numbers of automobiles.
ship for transporting cars

Examples

The car carrier arrived at the port, loaded with thousands of new vehicles from overseas.
A massive car carrier blocked the view as it docked early this morning.
Environmental regulations for car carriers are becoming more stringent each year.

Synset Examples

The car carrier Auriga Leader, built in 2008 with a capacity of 6,200 cars, is the world's first partially solar powered ship.
