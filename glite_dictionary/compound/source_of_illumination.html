source of illumination

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔrs əv ɪˌluːməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɔːs əv ɪˌluːmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a device or object that emits visible light, making things visible
device or object that emits light

Examples

A candle was the only source of illumination during the power outage.
The laboratory uses a special source of illumination to conduct optical experiments.
LEDs have become a popular source of illumination in modern lighting systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: light source - a device or object that produces light
