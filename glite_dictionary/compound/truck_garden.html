truck garden

1. NOUN

/ trʌk ˈɡɑrdən / (AmE), / trʌk ˈɡɑːdn̩ / (BrE)
a farm where vegetables or other produce are grown for sale at local markets
farm for market vegetables

Examples

The family expanded their truck garden to supply fresh vegetables to the city.
In the early 20th century, many immigrants established successful truck gardens near urban centers.
They converted part of their land into a truck garden and sold produce at the farmers' market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: market garden - a garden for growing vegetables for sale, usually on a smaller scale

Etymology

From obsolete sense of 'truck' meaning barter or exchange, referring to market trading.
