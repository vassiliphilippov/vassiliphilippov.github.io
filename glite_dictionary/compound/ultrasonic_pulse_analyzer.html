ultrasonic pulse analyzer

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌltrəˈsɑnɪk pʌls əˈnælaɪzər / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈsɒnɪk pʌls əˈnælʌɪzə / (BrE)
a device that measures the strength and quality of concrete by sending ultrasonic pulses through it and analyzing the results
device measuring concrete strength with ultrasound

Examples

Engineers used an ultrasonic pulse analyzer to assess the durability of the newly poured concrete slab.
The ultrasonic pulse analyzer quickly identified internal flaws in the concrete structure.
Before construction could proceed, the team relied on an ultrasonic pulse analyzer for accurate testing results.
