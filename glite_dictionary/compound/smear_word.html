smear word

1. NOUN

/ smɪr wɝːd / (AmE), / smɪə wɜːd / (BrE)
A word or epithet used to damage someone's reputation, often unfairly or maliciously.
word used to damage reputation

Examples

The politician accused his opponent of intentionally spreading a smear word to destroy his credibility.
Using a smear word like 'traitor' can do lasting harm to someone's public image.
She refused to respond to the smear words thrown at her during the heated debate.

Synset Examples

he used the smear word ‘communist’ for everyone who disagreed with him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slur - an insulting or damaging remark
  • Synonym: epithet - a descriptive term used to characterize someone, often offensively
