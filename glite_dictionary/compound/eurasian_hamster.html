Eurasian hamster

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈreɪʒən ˈhæmstɚ / (AmE), / jʊˈreɪʒən ˈhæmstə / (BrE)
a large species of hamster native to eastern Europe and parts of Asia, known for its brown fur and white underside
large hamster species from Europe and Asia

Examples

The Eurasian hamster is studied for its unique hibernation habits in cold climates.
Farmers often encounter the Eurasian hamster in their fields during the summer.
Unlike the golden hamster, the Eurasian hamster is much larger and more aggressive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hamster - small rodent often kept as a pet
