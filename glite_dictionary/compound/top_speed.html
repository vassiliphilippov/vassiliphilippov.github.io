top speed

1. NOUN

/ tɑp spiːd / (AmE), / tɒp spiːd / (BrE)
the highest speed that a vehicle or person is able to reach
maximum attainable speed

Examples

The sports car can reach a top speed of 200 miles per hour.
The athlete sprinted at top speed to win the final race.
At top speed, the train covers the distance in just two hours.

Synset Examples

The car sped down the highway at top speed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maximum speed - the greatest possible speed that can be reached
