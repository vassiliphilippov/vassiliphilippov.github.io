queen of beasts

1. NOUN

/ kwin əv bists / (AmE), / kwiːn əv biːsts / (BrE)
an epithet for the lion or lioness, signifying strength, majesty, and dominance
title for lion or lioness, symbolizing majesty

Examples

In ancient stories, the lion is honored as the queen of beasts for her courage.
The graceful lioness is sometimes referred to as the queen of beasts in wildlife documentaries.
Many myths describe the queen of beasts ruling the savannah with wisdom and power.

Synset Examples

Lions are often called the queen of beasts in the jungle.
The lioness, as the queen of beasts, leads her pride courageously.
In many cultures, the lion is revered as the queen of beasts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: king of beasts - a title for the male lion, denoting supreme strength and majesty
