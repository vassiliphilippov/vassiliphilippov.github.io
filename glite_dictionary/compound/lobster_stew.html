lobster stew

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑbstɚ stu / (AmE), / ˈlɒbstə stjuː / (BrE)
A soup or stew made with diced lobster meat cooked in milk or cream, often seasoned and served hot.
stew with lobster in milk or cream

Examples

The restaurant's lobster stew is famous for its rich, creamy flavor.
On chilly nights, she likes to prepare a homemade lobster stew for her family.
We enjoyed a bowl of lobster stew at a seaside café in Maine.
