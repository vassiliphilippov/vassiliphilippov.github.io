LGBTQ+ rights

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛl dʒiː biː tiː kjuː plʊs raɪts / (AmE), / ˌɛl dʒiː biː tiː kjuː plʌs raɪts / (BrE)
rights advocating equal treatment and protection for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, and other sexual and gender minorities
rights for sexual and gender minorities

Examples

Many activists are working to advance LGBTQ+ rights around the world.
The debate on LGBTQ+ rights has played a prominent role in recent elections.
Legal recognition of same-sex marriage is seen as a significant achievement in LGBTQ+ rights.

Synset Examples

He proudly identifies as a pansexualist and advocates for LGBTQ+ rights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: civil rights - rights guaranteeing freedoms and equal treatment for all citizens
  • Synonym: gay rights - rights specifically for homosexual individuals

Etymology

LGBTQ+ is an acronym: lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer/questioning, plus signifying inclusivity of other identities. 'Rights' from Old English *riht* (justice, fairness).
