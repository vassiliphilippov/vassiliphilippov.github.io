Newari language

1. NOUN

/ nuˈwɑːri ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / njuːˈwɑːri ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Sino-Tibetan language spoken mainly in the Kathmandu valley of Nepal, also known as Nepal Bhasa
language spoken in Kathmandu valley, Nepal

Examples

He studied the ancient manuscripts written in Newari language to better understand Nepalese history.
Although most young people now speak Nepali, some families still use Newari language at home.
The Newari language has a rich tradition of poetry and folk songs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nepal Bhasa - another name for the Newari language
  • Degree Higher: Nepali language - the official language of Nepal, spoken nationwide

Etymology

From 'Newar', the name of the ethnic group in Nepal, and 'language'.
