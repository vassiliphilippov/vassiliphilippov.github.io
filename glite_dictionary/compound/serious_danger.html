serious danger

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪriəs ˈdeɪndʒər / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəs ˈdeɪndʒə / (BrE)
A significant risk or threat of harm, injury, or negative consequences.
significant risk or threat

Examples

Not wearing a helmet on a construction site poses a serious danger to workers.
The leaking gas created a serious danger for everyone in the building.
Climate change represents a serious danger to coastal communities worldwide.

Synset Examples

Driving without a seatbelt is a serious danger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: danger - the possibility of harm or injury
