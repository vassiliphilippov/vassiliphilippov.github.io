safety protocol

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪfti ˈproʊtəˌkɑl / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti ˈprəʊtəkɒl / (BrE)
a set of rules or procedures designed to ensure people's safety in a particular environment or activity
rules for ensuring safety

Examples

Before entering the laboratory, all students must follow the safety protocol carefully.
Our company updates its safety protocols every year to comply with new regulations.
Ignoring the safety protocol can result in serious accidents or injuries.

Synset Examples

Every dockie knew the importance of safety protocols.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safety procedure - a method or step for ensuring safety
  • Synonym: guideline - a general rule or principle for best practice
