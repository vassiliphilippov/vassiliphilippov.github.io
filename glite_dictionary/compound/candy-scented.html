candy-scented

1. ADJ

/ ˈkændi sɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈkændi sɛntɪd / (BrE)
having a smell reminiscent of candy
smelling like candy

Examples

She bought a candy-scented candle that filled the room with a sweet aroma.
The candy-scented lotion was popular among teenagers.
After washing her hair with the new shampoo, it had a distinct candy-scented fragrance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweet-scented - having a sweet smell
