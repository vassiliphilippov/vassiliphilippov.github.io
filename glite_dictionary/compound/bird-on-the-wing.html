bird-on-the-wing

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɝd ɑn ðə ˈwɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌbɜːd ɒn ðə ˈwɪŋ / (BrE)
a trailing perennial milkwort (Polygala paucifolia) of eastern North America, with wintergreen-like leaves and usually rosy-purple flowers with winged sepals
trailing North American milkwort plant

Examples

In the shaded woods, clusters of bird-on-the-wing bloomed throughout the spring.
Hikers admired the rosy-purple flowers of the bird-on-the-wing carpeting the forest floor.
The leaves of bird-on-the-wing resemble those of wintergreen, making it easy to identify.
