orange liqueur

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ lɪˈkɝ / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
A sweet alcoholic drink flavored with oranges, often used in cocktails and desserts.
a sweet alcoholic drink flavored with orange

Examples

Cointreau is a famous brand of orange liqueur used in many classic cocktails.
The recipe calls for an ounce of orange liqueur to add sweetness and flavor.
He drizzled a little orange liqueur over the dessert before serving.
