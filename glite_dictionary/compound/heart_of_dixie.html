Heart of Dixie

1. PROPN

/ ˌhɑɹt əv ˈdɪksi / (AmE), / ˌhɑːt əv ˈdɪksi / (BrE)
A nickname for the state of Alabama, referring to its central role in the culture and history of the American South.
nickname for Alabama, U.S. state

Examples

Many people refer to Alabama as the Heart of Dixie because of its deep Southern heritage.
The license plates in the 1960s proudly bore the slogan Heart of Dixie.
Tourists visiting the Heart of Dixie often explore its rich civil rights history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Alabama - a state in the southeastern United States

Etymology

Refers to Alabama's location and role in the Deep South; 'Dixie' is a historical nickname for the Southern United States.
