departure time

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑrtʃər taɪm / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtʃə taɪm / (BrE)
the time at which a train, bus, airplane, or other public transport is scheduled to leave a place
scheduled time for leaving (transport)

Examples

Please check your ticket for the exact departure time of your train.
The departure time was delayed by thirty minutes due to heavy fog.
Passengers are advised to arrive at the station at least 15 minutes before the scheduled departure time.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: arrival time - the time when a transport is scheduled to arrive
