genus Agrostis

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒiː.nəs əˈɡrɑː.stɪs / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː.nəs əˈɡrɒs.tɪs / (BrE)
a genus of annual or perennial grasses commonly known as bent grass, found widely in the Northern Hemisphere
a genus of grasses; bent grass

Examples

The golf course was seeded with genus Agrostis to create a smooth and durable playing surface.
Species in the genus Agrostis are highly valued for their use in lawns and pastures.
Botanists studied the distribution of genus Agrostis across different climates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bent grass - common name for grasses in the genus Agrostis

Etymology

From Greek agrostis (a kind of grass), from agros (field).
