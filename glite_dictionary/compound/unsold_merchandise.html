unsold merchandise

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈsoʊld ˈmɜr.tʃən.daɪs / (AmE), / ʌnˈsəʊld ˈmɜː.tʃən.daɪs / (BrE)
goods that remain available for sale because they have not been purchased by customers
goods not yet sold

Examples

At the end of the season, the store discounted all its unsold merchandise.
They donated the unsold merchandise to charity after closing the shop.
Clearance sales are a common way to get rid of unsold merchandise.

Synset Examples

The bankrupt cart was filled with unsold merchandise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inventory - the stock of goods held by a business
  • Synonym: stock - the supply of goods for sale
