white Spanish broom

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈspænɪʃ bruːm / (AmE), / waɪt ˈspænɪʃ bruːm / (BrE)
A low-growing European shrub of the broom family with trifoliate leaves and yellowish-white flowers.
a low European shrub with yellowish-white flowers

Examples

The white Spanish broom added a touch of elegance to the Mediterranean garden with its creamy flowers.
Gardeners value white Spanish broom for its drought resistance and attractive foliage.
In the spring, the white Spanish broom is covered in delicate yellowish-white blossoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broom - a shrub of the genus Cytisus or related genera
