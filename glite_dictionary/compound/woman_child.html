woman child

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊmən ˌtʃaɪld / (AmE), / ˈwʊmən ˌtʃaɪld / (BrE)
An adult woman who displays childlike innocence, naivety, or behavior.
an adult woman with childlike qualities

Examples

The novel depicts her as a woman child who struggles to cope with adult responsibilities.
Her friends often referred to her as a woman child because of her playful imagination.
Despite her age, she retained a woman child outlook that charmed everyone around her.

Synset Examples
