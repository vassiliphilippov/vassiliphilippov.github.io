calling station

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔlɪŋ ˌsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɔːlɪŋ ˌsteɪʃən / (BrE)
a designated stop where a bus, train, or other transportation service picks up or drops off passengers
official stop for passenger pick-up or drop-off

Examples

The next calling station is just five minutes away, so please have your tickets ready.
She waited anxiously at the calling station watching for the bus to arrive.
Each calling station along the route is clearly marked for passengers.

Synset Examples

The bus driver announced the next calling station over the intercom.
The train will stop at the calling station for passenger pickups.
He waited at the calling station for his friend to arrive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: station - a place where trains or other vehicles regularly stop
