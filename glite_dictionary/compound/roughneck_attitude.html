roughneck attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌfnɛk ˈætəˌtud / (AmE), / ˈrʌfnɛk ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
A tough, fearless, and sometimes aggressive manner or demeanor, typically associated with a disregard for risks or conventions.
tough and fearless demeanor

Examples

Even in the face of danger, his roughneck attitude never wavered.
The team's roughneck attitude helped them win the championship against tougher opponents.
Some people appreciate a roughneck attitude, while others prefer a more sensitive approach.

Synset Examples

She admired his roughneck attitude towards tough situations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toughness - the quality of being strong and resilient
  • Antonym: timidity - the quality of lacking courage or confidence

Etymology

Compound of 'roughneck' (slang for a tough or aggressive person) and 'attitude.'
