pension account

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛnʃən əˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈpɛnʃən əˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
an account or plan for saving money to provide income after retirement
account for retirement savings

Examples

Most employees contribute regularly to a pension account throughout their careers.
He checked the balance of his pension account before planning his retirement.
Opening a pension account early can help ensure financial security later in life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retirement account - an account for saving money for use after retirement
