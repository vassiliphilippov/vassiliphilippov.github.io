heating plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiːtɪŋ plænt / (AmE), / ˈhiːtɪŋ plɑːnt / (BrE)
a facility or system in a building that generates and distributes heat for warming rooms or spaces
facility that provides heat to a building

Examples

The apartment building's heating plant was inspected before winter began.
A new heating plant was installed to improve energy efficiency in the school.
Routine maintenance of the heating plant is essential to ensure consistent warmth throughout the building.

Synset Examples

the heating system wasn't working
they have radiant heating

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heating system - equipment or mechanism for supplying heat to a space
