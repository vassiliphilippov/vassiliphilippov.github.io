chlorpheniramine maleate

1. NOUN

/ klɔrˌfɛnɪˌræmɪn məˈleɪət / (AmE), / klɔːˌfɛnɪˈræmɪn məˈliːeɪt / (BrE)
an antihistamine drug used to relieve symptoms of allergies and the common cold
antihistamine drug for allergies/cold
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed chlorpheniramine maleate to help control my allergy symptoms during pollen season.
You can buy chlorpheniramine maleate over the counter for relief from a runny nose and sneezing.
Be sure to read the warnings before taking chlorpheniramine maleate, especially if you plan to drive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antihistamine - a drug used to counteract the effects of histamine

Etymology

From chlorpheniramine (a compound name from chemical components) and maleate (a salt or ester of maleic acid).
