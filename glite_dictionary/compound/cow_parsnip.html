cow parsnip

1. NOUN

/ kaʊ ˈpɑrsnɪp / (AmE), / kaʊ ˈpɑːsnɪp / (BrE)
a tall, coarse plant with thick stems and clusters of white or purple flowers, belonging to the genus Heracleum
tall plant with thick stems and white or purple flowers

Examples

We spotted a patch of cow parsnip growing by the riverbank during our hike.
The large white blossoms of cow parsnip attract many types of butterflies in summer.
You should wear gloves when handling cow parsnip to avoid skin irritation.
