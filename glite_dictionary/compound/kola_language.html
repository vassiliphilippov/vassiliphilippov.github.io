Kola language

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊlə ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlə ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken south of Lake Chad, mainly in the northern regions of Cameroon
a Chadic language of Cameroon

Examples

The Kola language is spoken by several thousand people in Cameroon.
Linguists have documented unique tonal patterns in the Kola language.
An endangered status has raised concerns for the future of the Kola language.
