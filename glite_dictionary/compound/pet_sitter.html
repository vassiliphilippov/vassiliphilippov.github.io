pet sitter

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛt ˌsɪtər / (AmE), / ˈpet ˌsɪtə / (BrE)
a person who looks after pets while their owners are away from home
person caring for pets in owner's absence

Examples

We hired a pet sitter to look after our dog while we were on vacation.
As a pet sitter, she visits several homes each day to care for different animals.
Finding a trustworthy pet sitter gave us peace of mind during our trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: babysitter - someone who cares for children temporarily while parents are away
