skein of yarn

1. NOUN

/ skeɪn əv jɑrn / (AmE), / skeɪn əv jɑːn / (BrE)
a length of yarn that is loosely wound into a long coil, often for ease of use in knitting or crocheting
a coil of yarn

Examples

The cat played with a skein of yarn that had fallen to the floor.
She bought a pink skein of yarn for her next sweater project.
After untangling the skein of yarn, he began winding it into a ball.

Synset Examples

She unraveled the skein of yarn to start her project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ball of yarn - a round mass of yarn wound tightly into a ball
