sensorimotor region

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛnsəriˈmoʊtər ˈriːdʒən / (AmE), / ˌsɛnsəriˈməʊtə ˈriːdʒən / (BrE)
an area of the cerebral cortex that includes both sensory and motor regions, especially the precentral and postcentral gyri, and is involved in integrating sensory input with motor output
area of the cortex combining sensory and motor functions
medical

Examples

The sensorimotor region plays a crucial role in coordinating movement and perception.
Recent research investigates how injuries to the sensorimotor region affect motor skills and sensation.
Electrical stimulation of the sensorimotor region can help restore function in patients with paralysis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motor cortex - region of the cerebral cortex involved in planning and executing movement
  • Synonym: sensory cortex - region of the cerebral cortex responsible for processing sensory information

Etymology

From 'sensory' (relating to sensation) and 'motor' (relating to movement).
