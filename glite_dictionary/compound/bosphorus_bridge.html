Bosphorus Bridge

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɑːs.fər.əs brɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbɒs.fər.əs brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a large suspension bridge spanning the Bosporus in Istanbul, Turkey, connecting the European and Asian sides of the city
suspension bridge in Istanbul

Examples

The Bosphorus Bridge is one of Istanbul’s most famous landmarks, linking the city’s European and Asian sides.
During our trip to Turkey, we drove across the Bosphorus Bridge and admired the incredible view of the strait.
Every October, thousands of runners cross the Bosphorus Bridge as part of the annual Istanbul Marathon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suspension bridge - a bridge held up by cables attached to towers
