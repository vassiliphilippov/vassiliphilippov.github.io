kid-glove care

1. NOUN

/ kɪd ɡlʌv kɛr / (AmE), / kɪd ɡlʌv keə / (BrE)
very gentle and careful treatment, often to avoid causing harm or offense
very gentle treatment

Examples

After his surgery, the nurses gave him kid-glove care to ensure a smooth recovery.
The company handled the celebrity with kid-glove care to avoid any negative publicity.
Due to the patient's fragile condition, the staff provided kid-glove care at all times.

Synset Examples

The manager treated him with kid-glove care after his accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gentle care - care provided in a gentle or cautious way

Etymology

From 'kid glove', gloves made of soft kid leather, symbolizing delicacy.
