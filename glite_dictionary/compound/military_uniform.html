military uniform

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈjunɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˈjuːnɪfɔːm / (BrE)
the standard clothing prescribed for soldiers and other members of the armed forces, worn as identification and for official duties
standard clothing for soldiers

Examples

The new military uniform features improved camouflage for soldiers in the field.
Every member of the parade wore their military uniform with pride.
She carefully pressed her military uniform before reporting for inspection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uniform - a distinctive set of clothes worn by members of an organization
