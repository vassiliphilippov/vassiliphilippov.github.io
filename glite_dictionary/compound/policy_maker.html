policy maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːləsi ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪsi ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person responsible for setting the plans and decisions of a government, organization, or institution
person who sets plans for government or organization

Examples

The policy maker addressed the audience about new changes to education funding.
Environmental concerns are increasingly influencing how policy makers develop regulations.
As a policy maker, she strives to balance economic growth with social welfare.

Synset Examples

policy makers often make the right decision for the wrong reason

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decision maker - a person who makes important choices or judgments

Etymology

From 'policy' (plan of action) + 'maker' (one who creates or establishes).
