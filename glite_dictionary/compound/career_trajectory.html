career trajectory

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɪr trəˈdʒɛktəri / (AmE), / kəˈrɪə trəˈdʒɛktəri / (BrE)
the course or direction of a person's professional development over time
path of career development

Examples

After several years in marketing, her career trajectory shifted toward management.
A mentor can greatly influence your career trajectory by providing guidance and opportunities.
The company's rapid growth offered employees a chance to accelerate their career trajectory.

Synset Examples

His career trajectory changed after that big promotion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: career path - the direction or progression of someone's working life
