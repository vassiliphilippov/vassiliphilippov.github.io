bo jio

1. X

/ boʊ dʒiːoʊ / (AmE), / bəʊ dʒiːəʊ / (BrE)
(Singapore English, informal) Used to express that someone did not invite or inform you about an event or activity.
not having been invited or informed (Singapore English, informal)
informal

Examples

He organised a lunch outing, but bo jio again as usual.
When I saw their photos from the concert, I messaged them saying, 'bo jio!'
Next time there’s a trip to the beach, don’t bo jio, okay?

Synset Examples

He didn't invite me to the party, bo jio!
Why did you not tell me about the sale, bo jio?

Etymology

From Hokkien dialect: 'bo' (no, not) + 'jio' (invite). Entered Singaporean English via local speech.
