testing room

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛstɪŋ ruːm / (AmE), / ˈtɛstɪŋ ruːm / (BrE)
a room in which examinations, experiments, or assessments are carried out
room used for tests or assessments

Examples

The students waited anxiously in the testing room before their exams began.
All medical samples must be analyzed in a certified testing room to ensure accuracy.
Because of the noise, the testing room was moved to a quieter part of the building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laboratory - a room or building equipped for scientific experiments
