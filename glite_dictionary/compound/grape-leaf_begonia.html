grape-leaf begonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪp liːf bəˈɡoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪp liːf bɪˈɡəʊniə / (BrE)
a tuberous or semi-tuberous South African begonia with shallowly lobed ovate leaves and small white flowers
a type of South African begonia plant

Examples

The grape-leaf begonia thrives in shaded corners of the greenhouse.
Collectors prize the grape-leaf begonia for its unique leaf shape and delicate blooms.
Botanists studied the adaptations of the grape-leaf begonia to its South African habitat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: begonia - a genus of flowering plants with colorful flowers and leaves
