power station

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊər ˌsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə ˌsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A building or complex where electricity is generated for distribution to a wider area.
place where electricity is generated

Examples

The city is planning to build a new power station to meet the increasing demand for electricity.
Smoke rose from the old power station at the edge of town, hinting at its continued operation.
Environmental groups protested against the construction of a coal-fired power station near the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power plant - a place where electricity is generated; synonym for 'power station'
  • Degree Lower: substation - a site at which electricity is received from a power station and distributed
