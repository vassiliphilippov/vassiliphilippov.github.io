landing party

1. NOUN

/ ˈlændɪŋ ˌpɑrti / (AmE), / ˈlændɪŋ ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
a group of people from a ship sent ashore for a specific task, especially for exploration, military action, or rescue.
group sent ashore from a ship

Examples

The landing party disembarked at dawn to survey the coastline for signs of life.
A landing party was quickly assembled to provide medical assistance on the island.
Star Trek often depicts the dangers faced by a landing party exploring unknown worlds.
