tangle orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæŋɡəl ˌɔr.kɪd / (AmE), / ˈtæŋɡəl ˌɔː.kɪd / (BrE)
an orchid of the genus Plectorrhiza, characterized by tangled roots and long wiry stems with loose clusters of small fragrant green flowers
orchid with tangled roots of genus Plectorrhiza

Examples

The tangle orchid thrives in humid forests where its roots cling to tree branches.
A botanist discovered several tangle orchids growing wild in the mountain rainforest.
The tangle orchid is prized for its unusual roots and delicate green flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Plectorrhiza - genus of orchids to which the tangle orchid belongs
