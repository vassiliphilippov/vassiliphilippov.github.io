little blue heron

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬.əl blu ˈhɛr.ən / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl blu ˈhɛr.ən / (BrE)
a small bluish-grey heron (Egretta caerulea) native to the Americas, found in wetlands and coastal regions
a small bluish-grey heron of the Americas

Examples

The little blue heron stood motionless by the water, patiently waiting for its prey.
Birdwatchers were thrilled to spot several little blue herons in the marshlands.
Unlike other herons, the little blue heron has a distinct slate-blue plumage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heron - a long-legged freshwater and coastal bird
