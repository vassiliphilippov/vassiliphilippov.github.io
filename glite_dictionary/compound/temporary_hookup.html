temporary hookup

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛmpəˌrɛri ˈhʊkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərəri ˈhʊkʌp / (BrE)
a connection or arrangement set up for a limited period, not intended to be permanent
short-term connection or arrangement

Examples

We installed a temporary hookup to supply water to the construction site during renovations.
The festival organizers arranged for a temporary hookup to electricity for the event.
She asked the landlord for a temporary hookup while waiting for her permanent internet connection.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: permanent hookup - a connection or arrangement intended to last indefinitely
