cargo area

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrɡoʊ ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈkɑːɡəʊ ˌeəriə / (BrE)
the part of a ship, aircraft, or vehicle designated for storing cargo
section for storing cargo in a ship, plane, or vehicle

Examples

The cargo area of the plane was filled with medical supplies bound for the disaster zone.
Please ensure all luggage is properly secured in the cargo area before departure.
The ship's cargo area can accommodate thousands of containers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hold - the part of a ship for storing cargo
  • Synonym: trunk - compartment in a car for carrying luggage or cargo
