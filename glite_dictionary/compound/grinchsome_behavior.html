grinchsome behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrɪntʃsəm bəˈheɪvjər / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪntʃsəm bɪˈheɪviə / (BrE)
Unkind or mean-spirited actions, especially those that spoil the enjoyment of others, in the manner of a Grinch.
mean-spirited, spoiling behavior
informal

Examples

His grinchsome behavior ruined the holiday for everyone.
People were disappointed by her grinchsome behavior at the celebration.
Try to avoid grinchsome behavior during festive occasions so everyone can enjoy themselves.

Synset Examples

His grinchsome behavior ruined the party atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unkindness - lack of kindness or consideration
  • Antonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, or considerate

Etymology

Formed from 'Grinch' (the character from Dr. Seuss's story, symbolizing someone who spoils others' fun) + '-some' (forming adjectives); 'behavior' meaning actions.
