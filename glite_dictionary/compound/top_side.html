top side

1. NOUN

/ tɑp saɪd / (AmE), / tɒp saɪd / (BrE)
the highest or uppermost surface or face of something
uppermost surface of something

Examples

Please write your name on the top side of the paper before handing it in.
The top side of the cake was perfectly frosted, but the bottom was burnt.
The paint only covers the top side of the fence, leaving the rest exposed to the weather.

Synset Examples

put your books on top of the desk
only the top side of the box was painted

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bottom side - the lowest or underside surface of something
  • Antonym: underside - the lower surface of something
