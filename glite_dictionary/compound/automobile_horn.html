automobile horn

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔːtəmoʊˌbiːl hɔrn / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəməˌbiːl hɔːn / (BrE)
a device fitted to an automobile that sounds a warning signal when activated
car warning signal device

Examples

He pressed the automobile horn loudly to alert the cyclist.
The automobile horn was broken and needed to be repaired.
She replaced the old automobile horn with a new, louder model.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horn - a device that makes a loud warning noise, especially on vehicles
