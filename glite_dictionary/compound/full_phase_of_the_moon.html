full phase of the moon

1. NOUN

/ fʊl feɪz əv ðə mun / (AmE), / fʊl feɪz əv ðə muːn / (BrE)
the time when the Moon appears completely illuminated as seen from Earth, known as the full moon
when the Moon is fully illuminated

Examples

During the full phase of the moon, the night sky is brightly lit.
Many cultures hold festivals during the full phase of the moon.
The tides are often higher at the full phase of the moon.

Synset Examples

the moon is at the full

Related Terms

  • Synonym: full moon - the lunar phase when the Moon is fully illuminated
