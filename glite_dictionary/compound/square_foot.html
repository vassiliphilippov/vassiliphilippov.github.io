square foot

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr fʊt / (AmE), / skwɛə fʊt / (BrE)
a unit of area equal to a square with sides of one foot; commonly used to measure the size of rooms, houses, or land.
unit of area (1 ft × 1 ft)

Examples

The apartment is 900 square feet in size, making it perfect for a small family.
Please measure the living room and tell me how many square feet it is.
This office space rents for $25 per square foot each year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: square meter - unit of area equal to a square with sides of one meter
