training table

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪnɪŋ ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnɪŋ ˌteɪbəl / (BrE)
a regimen of specially planned meals provided for athletes in training, typically served together in a designated dining area
planned meals for athletes in training

Examples

The college installed a training table to help the football team maintain a healthy diet.
Athletes on the training table enjoy nutritious meals designed by sports dietitians.
Many coaches believe that a well-managed training table enhances player performance.
