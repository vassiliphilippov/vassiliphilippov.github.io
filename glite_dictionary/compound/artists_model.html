artist's model

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrtɪsts ˈmɑːdəl / (AmE), / ˈɑːtɪsts ˈmɒdəl / (BrE)
a person who poses for a painter, sculptor, or other artist to be depicted in their work
a person who poses for artists

Examples

The artist's model held her pose for over an hour without moving.
Many studios hire a regular artist's model for life drawing classes.
He found work as an artist's model in Paris before starting his own art career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: model - a person who poses for photographers or artists
