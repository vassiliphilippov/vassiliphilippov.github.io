pokerish expression

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊkərɪʃ ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈpəʊkərɪʃ ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
an expression that conceals true feelings or emotions, typically revealing little of what someone is actually thinking
expression that hides real emotions

Examples

She maintained a pokerish expression throughout the heated negotiation, giving nothing away.
His pokerish expression made it impossible for his opponents to guess his next move.
Even when she heard the surprising news, she kept a pokerish expression on her face.

Synset Examples

His pokerish demeanor made it hard to read his emotions.
His pokerish expression left everyone guessing.
She gave a pokerish smile, hiding her true feelings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poker face - an expressionless face that shows no emotion, especially in poker or other situations where emotion is concealed

Etymology

Formed from 'poker' (the card game known for its blank-faced players) and the suffix '-ish', meaning 'like,' plus 'expression.'
