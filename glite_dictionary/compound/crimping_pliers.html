crimping pliers

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪmpɪŋ ˈplaɪɚz / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmpɪŋ ˈplaɪəz / (BrE)
a hand tool used for joining two pieces of metal, typically by deforming one or both to hold the other in place
tool for joining metals by deforming

Examples

The electrician used crimping pliers to secure the connector to the end of the wire.
Always choose the right size of crimping pliers for the terminal you are installing.
My toolkit includes a pair of crimping pliers for electrical repairs.

Synset Examples

Stripped wire (often stranded) is inserted through the correctly sized opening of the connector, and a crimper is used to tightly squeeze the opening against the wire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crimper - a hand tool for crimping; can refer broadly to devices that crimp, not just pliers
