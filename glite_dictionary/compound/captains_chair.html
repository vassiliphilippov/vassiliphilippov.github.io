captain's chair

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæp.tɪnz tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈkæp.tɪnz tʃɛː / (BrE)
a wooden armchair with a saddle-shaped seat and a low back with vertical spindles, often used in offices or dining rooms
a wooden armchair with a saddle seat and spindle back

Examples

He sat comfortably in the captain's chair at the head of the table.
The office was furnished with a mahogany desk and a matching captain's chair.
She bought a vintage captain's chair for her study.
