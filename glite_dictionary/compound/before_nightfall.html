before nightfall

1. ADP

/ bɪˈfɔr ˈnaɪtfɔl / (AmE), / bɪˈfɔː ˈnaɪtfɔːl / (BrE)
prior to the time when evening turns to night; before it becomes dark
prior to sunset or darkness

Examples

The hikers decided to return to the cabin before nightfall to avoid getting lost.
Farmers often finish their work before nightfall when there is no artificial light available.
They hoped to reach the next village before nightfall, fearing wild animals after dark.

Synset Examples

We need to pass the river before nightfall.
