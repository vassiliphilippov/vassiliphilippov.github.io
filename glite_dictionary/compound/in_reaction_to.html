in reaction to

1. ADP

/ ɪn riˈækʃən tu / (AmE), / ɪn riˈækʃən tʊ / (BrE)
as a response to something; as a result of being affected by a particular event or action.
as a response to something

Examples

The stock market dropped sharply in reaction to the latest economic reports.
She apologized in reaction to the criticism she received from her colleagues.
New laws were introduced in reaction to the recent surge in crime.

Synset Examples

The community organized a protest in reaction to the new law.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in response to - as a reply or answer to something
