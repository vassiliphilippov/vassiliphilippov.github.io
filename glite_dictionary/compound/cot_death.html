cot death

1. NOUN

/ kɑt dɛθ / (AmE), / kɒt dɛθ / (BrE)
sudden and unexpected death of a seemingly healthy baby during sleep, especially in infancy; also called sudden infant death syndrome (SIDS).
sudden infant death during sleep
medical british

Examples

Many parents fear cot death, though it is quite rare.
Health campaigns have raised awareness about how to prevent cot death.
The cause of cot death is still not fully understood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sudden infant death syndrome - medical term for unexplained sudden death of an infant during sleep
