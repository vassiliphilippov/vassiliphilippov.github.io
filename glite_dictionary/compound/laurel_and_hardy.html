Laurel and Hardy

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɔrəl ənd ˈhɑrdi / (AmE), / ˈlɒrəl ənd ˈhɑːdi / (BrE)
a famous American comedy duo, consisting of Stan Laurel and Oliver Hardy, known for their slapstick films from the early to mid-20th century
iconic American slapstick comedy duo

Examples

Many classic Hollywood comedies were inspired by Laurel and Hardy routines.
My grandfather still enjoys watching old Laurel and Hardy films on Sunday afternoons.
The comedy of Laurel and Hardy remains timeless for audiences of all ages.

Etymology

From the surnames of Stan Laurel and Oliver Hardy, the duo's members.
