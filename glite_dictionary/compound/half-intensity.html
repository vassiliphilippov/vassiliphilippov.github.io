half-intensity

1. NOUN

/ hæf ɪnˈtɛnsəti / (AmE), / hɑːf ɪnˈtɛnsəti / (BrE)
an intensity value that is exactly half of the maximum possible or measured intensity, often used in technical or scientific contexts
half of the maximum intensity

Examples

The filter reduced the beam to half-intensity for the experiment.
Adjust the dial until the light reaches exactly half-intensity.
At half-intensity, the sound was much more tolerable to the audience.
