north side

1. NOUN

/ nɔrθ saɪd / (AmE), / nɔːθ saɪd / (BrE)
the side or part of something that faces or is situated toward the north
side facing or toward the north

Examples

Their house is located on the north side of the lake, which gets less sun in winter.
The plants on the north side of the building grow more slowly because they receive less sunlight.
We entered the museum through the north side entrance after parking nearby.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: south side - the side that is on the south
  • Degree Lower: east side - the side that is on the east
  • Degree Higher: west side - the side that is on the west
