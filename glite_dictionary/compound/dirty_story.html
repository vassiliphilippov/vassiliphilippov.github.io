dirty story

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɝːti ˈstɔːri / (AmE), / ˈdɜːti ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
a story or joke that contains indecent or obscene content, often relating to sex
an indecent or obscene joke

Examples

He made everyone uncomfortable by telling a dirty story at the dinner table.
She couldn't help laughing at his latest dirty story.
People often share a dirty story at bachelor parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dirty joke - an indecent or obscene joke
