horned puffin

1. NOUN

/ hɔrnd ˈpʌfɪn / (AmE), / hɔːnd ˈpʌfɪn / (BrE)
a seabird of the northern Pacific, resembling the Atlantic puffin but distinguished by a small fleshy black horn above each eye
northern Pacific seabird with a horn-like feature

Examples

The horned puffin nests on cliffs along the northern Pacific coastline.
During our Alaskan cruise, we spotted a group of horned puffins fishing near the shore.
Unlike the Atlantic puffin, the horned puffin has distinctive fleshy horns above its eyes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puffin - a type of seabird with a colorful beak
