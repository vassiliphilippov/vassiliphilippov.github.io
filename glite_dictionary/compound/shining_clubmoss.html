shining clubmoss

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃaɪnɪŋ ˈklʌbˌmɑs / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪnɪŋ ˈklʌbˌmɒs / (BrE)
a species of clubmoss (Lycopodium lucidulum) with glossy, evergreen leaves, found in moist forests of North America
a species of clubmoss plant

Examples

The forest floor was carpeted with shining clubmoss, its glossy leaves catching the morning light.
Botanists surveyed the area for shining clubmoss populations along the damp, shaded hillsides.
Gardeners admire the dense, bright green foliage of shining clubmoss in native plant collections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clubmoss - a type of spore-bearing plant related to ferns
