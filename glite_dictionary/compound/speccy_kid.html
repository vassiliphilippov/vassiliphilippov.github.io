speccy kid

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛksi kɪd / (AmE), / ˈspɛksi kɪd / (BrE)
a disparaging or mocking term for a child who wears glasses
child who wears glasses (slang, often mocking)
slang british

Examples

At school, he was bullied for being the speccy kid in the front row.
She didn't want to be known as the speccy kid, so she avoided wearing her glasses in class.
The teacher asked the speccy kid to help set up the projector.

Synset Examples

The speccy kid always aced the science tests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: four-eyes - person who wears glasses (mocking slang)

Etymology

From British slang 'speccy', derived from 'spectacles' (glasses), plus 'kid'.
