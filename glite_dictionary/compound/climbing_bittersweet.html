climbing bittersweet

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈbɪtərˌswit / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈbɪtəswiːt / (BrE)
a North American twining shrub (Celastrus scandens) with yellow capsules that enclose scarlet seeds
North American twining shrub with yellow capsules

Examples

The hedgerow was covered with climbing bittersweet displaying its bright yellow capsules.
Gardeners often plant climbing bittersweet for its decorative berries and vibrant autumn color.
Birds are attracted to the red seeds of climbing bittersweet in the fall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bittersweet - a related woody climbing plant with orange capsules and red seeds, also known as 'American bittersweet'
