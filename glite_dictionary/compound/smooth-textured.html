smooth-textured

1. ADJ

/ smuð ˈtɛkstʃɚd / (AmE), / smuːð ˈtɛkstʃəd / (BrE)
having a surface that is smooth and fine-grained to the touch or appearance
having a smooth, fine-grained surface

Examples

The chef recommended a smooth-textured chocolate mousse for dessert.
I prefer using smooth-textured fabrics for summer clothing because they feel cool on the skin.
This face cream leaves your skin feeling smooth-textured and refreshed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rough-textured - having a rough, coarse surface
