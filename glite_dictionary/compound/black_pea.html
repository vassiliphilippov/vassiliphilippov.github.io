black pea

1. NOUN

/ blæk piː / (AmE), / blak piː / (BrE)
a perennial plant native to Europe and North Africa, with foliage that turns black when dried
European/North African perennial plant, blackening foliage

Examples

The black pea is often grown for ornamental purposes in cottage gardens.
After harvesting, the leaves of the black pea turn a distinctive black shade as they dry.
Botanists collected black pea samples during their field trip across the Mediterranean region.
