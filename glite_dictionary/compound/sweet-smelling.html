sweet-smelling

1. ADJ

/ swiːtˈsmɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / swiːtˈsmɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
Having a pleasant and natural fragrance; fragrant or aromatic.
having a pleasant fragrance

Examples

The garden was full of sweet-smelling roses in full bloom.
She chose a sweet-smelling lotion made from natural ingredients.
A sweet-smelling breeze drifted in through the open window, filling the room with calm.

Synset Examples

odoriferous spices
the odorous air of the orchard
the perfumed air of June
scented flowers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragrant - having a pleasant, sweet smell
  • Synonym: aromatic - having a noticeable and pleasant smell, often spicy or fragrant
  • Antonym: odorless - having no smell
