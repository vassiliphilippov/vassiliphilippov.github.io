as soon as

1. SCONJ

/ əz sun æz / (AmE), / əz suːn æz / (BrE)
immediately after the moment that; at the very moment when
immediately after another event

Examples

As soon as you finish your homework, you can watch television.
We left the restaurant as soon as the rain started pouring down.
Please call me as soon as you get home.

Synset Examples

As soon as the sun sets, we'll head out.
I will call you as soon as I arrive.
The bird began to fly as soon as it was free.
As soon as he entered, he forgot to shut the front door.
He knows their intentions as soon as look at someone.
The kids ran to the playground as soon as they arrived.
As soon as the handbags came out, the atmosphere changed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: once - as soon as; at the moment when (used as conjunction)
