night jessamine

1. NOUN

/ naɪt ˈdʒɛsəmɪn / (AmE), / naɪt ˈdʒɛsəmɪn / (BrE)
A West Indian evergreen shrub (Cestrum nocturnum) with clusters of funnel-shaped yellow-white flowers that are fragrant at night.
fragrant tropical night-blooming shrub

Examples

The night jessamine filled the garden with its sweet scent after sunset.
Many gardeners choose night jessamine for its fragrant night-blooming flowers.
A cluster of night jessamine near the porch made evenings especially enjoyable.

Etymology

From 'night', referring to its nocturnal fragrance, and 'jessamine', an older form of 'jasmine'.
