soapberry vine

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊpˌbɛri vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsəʊpˌbɛri vaɪn / (BrE)
a tendril-climbing plant of the soapberry family, often cultivated for its attractive leaves and berries
a climbing plant from the soapberry family

Examples

The soapberry vine quickly covered the old fence, adding lush greenery to the backyard.
Gardeners appreciate the soapberry vine for its decorative berries and climbing habit.
We trained the soapberry vine to grow along the trellis to create a natural screen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vine - a plant with a long, thin stem that climbs or trails
