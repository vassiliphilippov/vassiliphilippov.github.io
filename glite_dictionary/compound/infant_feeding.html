infant feeding

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.fənt ˈfiː.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪn.fənt ˈfiː.dɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of providing nourishment to a baby, especially by breastfeeding or bottle-feeding.
feeding a baby or infant

Examples

Proper infant feeding is crucial for healthy growth and development.
The nurse explained the basics of infant feeding to the new parents.
Guidelines on infant feeding often emphasize the benefits of breastfeeding when possible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breastfeeding - feeding a baby with breast milk
  • Synonym: bottle-feeding - feeding a baby with a bottle
