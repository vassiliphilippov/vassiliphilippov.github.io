soap film

1. NOUN

/ soʊp fɪlm / (AmE), / səʊp fɪlm / (BrE)
A thin layer of soap solution that forms on a surface after washing, often appearing as a transparent or shimmering residue.
thin layer left by soap on surfaces

Examples

After washing the glass, a soap film was visible in the sunlight.
The child marveled at the colors in the soap film stretched across the loop.
Make sure to rinse thoroughly, or a soap film will be left on the dishes.
