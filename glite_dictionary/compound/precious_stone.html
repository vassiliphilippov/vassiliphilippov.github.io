precious stone

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛʃəs stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃəs stəʊn / (BrE)
A valuable gem, such as a diamond, ruby, sapphire, or emerald, used in jewelry.
valuable gemstone used in jewelry

Examples

The necklace was set with a single large precious stone that sparkled in the sunlight.
She inherited a ring adorned with an unusual precious stone from her grandmother.
The jeweler carefully examined each precious stone before placing it in the setting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gemstone - a precious or semiprecious stone, especially when cut or polished for ornament

/ ˈprɛʃəs stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃəs stəʊn / (BrE)

valuable gemstone used in jewelry
