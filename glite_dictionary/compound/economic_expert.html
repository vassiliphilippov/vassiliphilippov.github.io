economic expert

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈkɑːnəmɪk ˈɛkspɚt / (AmE), / iːˈkɒnəmɪk ˈɛkspɜːt / (BrE)
A person who has specialized knowledge or skill in the science of economics.
person skilled in economics

Examples

The government invited an economic expert to advise on the national budget reforms.
She has worked as an economic expert for several international organizations.
Media outlets often call on an economic expert to explain the impact of policy changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: economist - a person who studies or is an expert in economics
