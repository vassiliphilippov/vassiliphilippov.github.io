endless optimism

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛn(dl)əs ˌɑptɪˈmɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈɛndləs ˌɒptɪˈmɪzəm / (BrE)
a persistent and unwavering attitude of hopefulness and positivity, even in difficult situations
persistent and unwavering positivity

Examples

Despite countless setbacks, Maria's endless optimism inspired everyone around her to keep trying.
Even in the face of rejection, his endless optimism kept his spirits high.
Her endless optimism makes difficult days at work a little bit brighter for everyone.

Synset Examples

Everyone loves Percy for his endless optimism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimism - the tendency to expect the best possible outcome or dwell on the most hopeful aspects of a situation
