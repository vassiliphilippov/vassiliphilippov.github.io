pooty noise

1. NOUN

/ ˈpuːti nɔɪz / (AmE), / ˈpuːti nɔɪz / (BrE)
a silly, often humorous sound that imitates flatulence, typically made by children
humorous sound imitating flatulence
informal

Examples

The toddler burst into laughter after making a pooty noise at the dinner table.
She made a pooty noise to entertain her baby brother during playtime.
Every time someone made a pooty noise, the whole class started giggling.

Synset Examples

The toddler giggled every time he made a pooty noise.
The baby made a pooty sound and everyone laughed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raspberry - a noise made by blowing air between the lips, often to imitate flatulence
