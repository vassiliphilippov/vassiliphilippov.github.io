stone bramble

1. NOUN

/ stoʊn ˈbræmbəl / (AmE), / stəʊn ˈbræmbəl / (BrE)
A trailing European bramble (Rubus saxatilis) with red, raspberry-like edible fruits and prickly stems.
a European bramble with red berries

Examples

The stone bramble is found in woodland clearings throughout Europe.
She picked a handful of ripe stone bramble fruit along the forest path.
The prickly stems of the stone bramble make it difficult to harvest its red berries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bramble - any rough, tangled, prickly shrub, especially those of the genus Rubus
