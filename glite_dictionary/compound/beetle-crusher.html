beetle-crusher

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːtəlˌkrʌʃər / (AmE), / ˈbiːtəlˌkrʌʃə / (BrE)
a device or a person used for killing beetles or bugs, often used humorously or descriptively.
device or person that kills beetles or bugs

Examples

He bought a beetle-crusher to tackle the infestation in his basement.
After finding three bugs in the kitchen, she jokingly called herself a beetle-crusher.
The old beetle-crusher in the shed hadn't been used for years.
