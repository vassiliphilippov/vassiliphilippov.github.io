rare coin

1. NOUN

/ rɛr kɔɪn / (AmE), / reə kɔɪn / (BrE)
a coin that is uncommon and usually valuable to collectors
uncommon and collectible coin

Examples

He inherited a rare coin from his grandfather's collection.
Many museums display rare coins from different historical periods.
She spent years searching for a rare coin to complete her set.

Synset Examples

He has a small collection of rare coins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collectible - an item worth collecting because of its rarity or interest
  • Antonym: common coin - a coin that is frequently found and not especially valuable
