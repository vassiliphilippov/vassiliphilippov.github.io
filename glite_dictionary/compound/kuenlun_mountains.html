Kuenlun Mountains

1. PROPN

/ kuːˌɛnˈluːn ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / kuːˌɛnˈlʊn ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a mountain range in western China, extending eastward from the Indian border for about 1,000 miles
mountain range in western China

Examples

The Kuenlun Mountains form a natural barrier between the Tibetan Plateau and the Tarim Basin.
Explorers have long been fascinated by the remote beauty of the Kuenlun Mountains.
Many rivers in western China originate in the Kuenlun Mountains.
