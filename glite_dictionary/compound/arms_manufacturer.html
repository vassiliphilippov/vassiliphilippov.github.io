arms manufacturer

1. NOUN

/ ɑrmz ˌmænjəˈfækʧər / (AmE), / ɑːmz ˌmænjʊˈfækʧə / (BrE)
a person or company that produces weapons and ammunition for sale
maker of weapons and ammunition

Examples

The country imposed strict sanctions on the arms manufacturer for violating international regulations.
As an arms manufacturer, the company must comply with numerous export control laws.
The journalist interviewed the CEO of a leading arms manufacturer about new weapon technologies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weapons manufacturer - a company or person that manufactures weapons
  • Synonym: arms dealer - a person or business that sells weapons and military equipment
