chicken Tetrazzini

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkən tɛtrəˈzini / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn tɛtrəˈziːni / (BrE)
A dish of chicken cooked in a creamy sauce with mushrooms, served over pasta and typically topped with cheese.
chicken pasta dish in cream sauce

Examples

My mother makes a delicious chicken Tetrazzini every Thanksgiving for our family dinner.
We ordered chicken Tetrazzini at the Italian restaurant, and it was rich and comforting.
Leftover turkey can easily be substituted in this classic chicken Tetrazzini recipe.

Etymology

Named after Italian opera singer Luisa Tetrazzini, early 20th century.
