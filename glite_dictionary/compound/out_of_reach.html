out of reach

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv riːtʃ / (AmE), / aʊt əv riːtʃ / (BrE)
not able to be reached, accessed, or obtained, either physically or figuratively
not able to be reached or obtained

Examples

For many families, a home in the city center is simply out of reach.
She stretched as high as she could, but the book remained out of reach on the top shelf.
Clean water is still out of reach for thousands of people in the region.

Synset Examples

an unapproachable chalet high in the mountains
an unreachable canyon
the unreachable stars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unreachable - not able to be reached or contacted
  • Antonym: accessible - able to be reached or entered
