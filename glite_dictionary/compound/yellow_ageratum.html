yellow ageratum

1. NOUN

/ ˌjɛloʊ əˈdʒɛrətəm / (AmE), / ˌjɛləʊ əˈdʒɛrətəm / (BrE)
a shrub native to the southwestern Mediterranean region, known for its yellow, daisylike flowers
Mediterranean shrub with yellow, daisylike flowers

The yellow ageratum brightens the Mediterranean hillside with its cheerful blooms each spring.
