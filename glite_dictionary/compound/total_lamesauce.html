total lamesauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊt̬əl ˈleɪmˌsɔs / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl ˈleɪmsɔːs / (BrE)
An extremely weak or unconvincing excuse or explanation.
extremely unconvincing excuse
informal slang

Examples

His reason for being late was total lamesauce and nobody believed him.
That movie's plot twist was total lamesauce, completely ruining the ending for me.
Calling in sick for the third time this week with that story is total lamesauce.

Synset Examples

That excuse was total lamesauce; try harder!
