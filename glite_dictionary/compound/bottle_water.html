to bottle water

1. VERB

/ ˈbɑːt̬l ˈwɔt̬ər / (AmE), / ˈbɒtl ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
to put water into a bottle for storage or transport
put water into a bottle

Examples

Many people choose to bottle water at home to ensure its purity.
After boiling the water, she carefully bottled water for her camping trip.
Some companies bottle water from natural springs and sell it worldwide.

Synset Examples
