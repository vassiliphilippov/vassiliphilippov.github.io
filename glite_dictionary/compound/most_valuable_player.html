most valuable player

1. NOUN

/ moʊst ˈvæljuəbl ˈpleɪər / (AmE), / məʊst ˈvaljʊəbl ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
the player considered to have contributed the most to a team or sport during a season or event, often receiving a special award
top-performing player in a sport or event

Examples

At the end of the season, Sarah was named the most valuable player for her outstanding performance.
The most valuable player award is given to the athlete who made the biggest impact on the game.
Each year, fans eagerly await the announcement of the most valuable player in the league.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: MVP - abbreviation for 'most valuable player', especially in American sports
