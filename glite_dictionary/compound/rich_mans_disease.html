rich man's disease

1. NOUN

/ ɹɪtʃ mænz dɪˈziz / (AmE), / ɹɪtʃ mænz dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
An illness or health problem commonly associated with a lifestyle of excessive wealth or luxury, such as gout or stress.
illness linked to luxury or wealth

Examples

After years of indulgence, he was diagnosed with rich man's disease.
Doctors sometimes call gout the original rich man's disease.
They once thought diabetes was only a rich man's disease.

Synset Examples

Living lavishly can sometimes lead to rich man's disease.
They joked that his lifestyle was a rich man's disease.
Stress and anxiety can be seen as a rich man's disease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gout - A type of arthritis historically associated with wealth and rich diets
