yellow-tinged

1. ADJ

/ ˈjɛloʊ tɪndʒd / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ tɪndʒd / (BrE)
Having a slight hint or trace of yellow in the color.
having a hint of yellow

Examples

The leaves turned a yellow-tinged green as autumn approached.
Her pale, yellow-tinged dress glowed softly in the afternoon sun.
The sky was a yellow-tinged gray before the storm rolled in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellowish - slightly yellow in color
