beautiful sunset

1. NOUN

/ ˈbjuːtəfəl ˈsʌnˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈbjuːtəfəl ˈsʌnˌsɛt / (BrE)
a visually impressive or pleasing view of the sun as it goes below the horizon, especially when accompanied by striking colors in the sky
visually appealing view of the setting sun

Examples

We watched the beautiful sunset together from the hilltop, enjoying the vivid colors in the sky.
Her photograph captured the beautiful sunset over the ocean perfectly.
Travelers often stop at this viewpoint to admire the beautiful sunset each evening.

Synset Examples

Kwaj is known for its beautiful sunsets and clear waters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunset - the time in the evening when the sun disappears below the horizon
