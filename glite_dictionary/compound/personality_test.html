personality test

1. NOUN

/ pɚˌsɑːnəˈlɑːɾi tɛst / (AmE), / pɜːˌsɒnəˈlɪti tɛst / (BrE)
a test designed to measure the characteristics and traits of an individual's personality
test assessing personality traits

Examples

The company required all job applicants to take a personality test as part of the hiring process.
She was curious about her character, so she decided to complete an online personality test.
Many therapists use a personality test to gain insights into their clients' behavior and preferences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychometric test - a test measuring psychological attributes such as intelligence or aptitude
