war correspondent

1. NOUN

/ wɔr ˌkɔrəˈspɑndənt / (AmE), / wɔː ˌkɒrɪˈspɒndənt / (BrE)
A journalist who reports news and commentary directly from combat zones for publication or broadcast.
journalist reporting from war zones

Examples

The war correspondent filed daily updates from the front lines for the evening news.
As a war correspondent, she risked her life to give a firsthand account of the conflict.
Many war correspondents have witnessed historic events that shaped international relations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: correspondent - a person who reports news from a particular place
