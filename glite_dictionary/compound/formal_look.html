formal look

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrməl lʊk / (AmE), / ˈfɔːməl lʊk / (BrE)
an appearance or style of dress considered appropriate for formal occasions
appearance suitable for formal occasions

Examples

She wore a navy suit and heels to create a truly formal look for the event.
A tuxedo is usually chosen for a formal look at weddings or galas.
Accessories like ties and cufflinks can enhance a formal look significantly.

Synset Examples

He prefers black shoes for a formal look.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: casual look - an appearance suitable for informal occasions
