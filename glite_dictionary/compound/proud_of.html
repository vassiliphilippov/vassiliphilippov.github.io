proud of

1. ADJ

/ ˈpraʊd əv / (AmE), / ˈpraʊd əv / (BrE)
feeling deep pleasure or satisfaction as a result of achievements, qualities, or possessions connected with oneself or someone/something one cares about
feeling pleasure due to achievement or connection

Examples

She was proud of her accomplishments at work.
They are proud of their son's achievements in school.
He felt proud of the progress his team had made.

Synset Examples

proud of their child

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ashamed of - feeling shame as a result of or in connection with oneself or someone/something one cares about
