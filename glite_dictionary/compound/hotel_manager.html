hotel manager

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈtɛl ˈmænɪdʒər / (AmE), / həʊˈtɛl ˈmænɪdʒə / (BrE)
a person who is responsible for running and overseeing the operations of a hotel
person in charge of a hotel

Examples

The hotel manager greeted guests at the entrance with a warm smile.
She worked her way up from receptionist to hotel manager in just five years.
As a hotel manager, Tom is responsible for ensuring customer satisfaction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manager - a person in charge of an organization or group
