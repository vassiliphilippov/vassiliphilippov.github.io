climate insight

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪmət ˈɪnˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmət ˈɪnsaɪt / (BrE)
an understanding or revelation about the climate, often gained from data or evidence
understanding about the climate

Examples

Her research provided a valuable climate insight regarding temperature trends over the past century.
The new satellite offered fresh climate insights into atmospheric changes.
By studying tree rings, scientists discovered an important climate insight about historical rainfall patterns.

Synset Examples

Scientists are studying mastodon fossils for climate insights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: climate knowledge - knowledge relating to climate
  • Antonym: climate ignorance - lack of understanding about climate
