dried apricot

1. NOUN

/ draɪd ˈæprɪˌkɑt / (AmE), / draɪd ˈæprɪkɒt / (BrE)
an apricot that has been preserved by drying, often used as a sweet snack or in cooking
apricot preserved by drying

Examples

I added chopped dried apricot to the salad for a touch of sweetness.
She keeps a jar of dried apricots in her pantry as a healthy snack.
The recipe calls for soaking the dried apricot before mixing it into the cake batter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apricot - a soft, round fruit with orange-yellow flesh and a stone inside
