self-cultivation

1. NOUN

/ sɛlf ˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / sɛlf ˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of developing or educating oneself, especially through personal growth and self-improvement
developing or educating oneself

Examples

She devoted herself to self-cultivation by reading widely and reflecting on her experiences.
Many philosophies encourage self-cultivation as a lifelong pursuit.
Through self-cultivation, he became more disciplined and understanding of others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-improvement - the process of improving oneself through conscious efforts

Etymology

Formed in English from 'self-' + 'cultivation'. 'Cultivation' from Latin 'cultivare', from 'cultus' (tended, cultivated).
