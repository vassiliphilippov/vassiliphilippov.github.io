hair metal

1. NOUN

/ hɛr ˈmɛt̬l̩ / (AmE), / heə ˈmetl̩ / (BrE)
A style of rock music popular in the 1980s, characterized by flashy clothes, heavy use of guitar effects, and elaborate hairstyles.
1980s glam-oriented rock music genre

Examples

Many classic hair metal bands played sold-out arenas throughout the late 1980s.
He used to tease his hair to fit in with his favorite hair metal group.
The radio station devoted Friday nights to playing vintage hair metal anthems.

Synset Examples

He still listens to hair metal from the 80s.
She loves the flashy style of hair metal bands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glam metal - another term for hair metal, emphasizing the glamourous aspect
  • Degree Lower: heavy metal - a genre of loud, aggressive rock music

Etymology

From 'hair' (referring to the elaborate hairstyles of band members) and 'metal' (from heavy metal music).
