true manliness

1. NOUN

/ truː ˈmænlinəs / (AmE), / truː ˈmanlɪnɪs / (BrE)
The qualities, such as integrity, courage, and respect, that are considered to constitute an ideal or genuine man.
qualities of an ideal or genuine man

Examples

He believed that true manliness could be measured by a person's honesty and compassion.
The novel explores themes of honor and true manliness in the face of adversity.
They admired his true manliness and the way he treated others with dignity.

Synset Examples

True manliness involves respect and integrity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masculinity - qualities or attributes regarded as characteristic of men
  • Synonym: manhood - the state or qualities of being a man
