many-faceted career

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛni ˈfæsɪtɪd kəˈrɪr / (AmE), / ˌmeni ˈfæsɪtɪd kəˈrɪə / (BrE)
a career that involves a variety of skills, roles, or activities
career with diverse aspects or roles

Examples

She has built a many-faceted career that spans journalism, public speaking, and philanthropy.
His many-faceted career has made him well-respected in several industries.
Pursuing a many-faceted career requires adaptability and lifelong learning.

Synset Examples

His many-faceted career includes writing and acting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multi-faceted career - a career with many different aspects or roles
