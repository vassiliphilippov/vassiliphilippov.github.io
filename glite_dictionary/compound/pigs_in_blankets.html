pigs in blankets

1. NOUN

/ pɪɡz ɪn ˈblæŋkɪts / (AmE), / pɪɡz ɪn ˈblaŋkɪts / (BrE)
small sausages or frankfurters wrapped in dough or bacon and baked or cooked
sausages wrapped in dough or bacon

Examples

We served pigs in blankets as appetizers at the party.
At the holiday dinner, my grandmother always makes pigs in blankets.
The kids love eating pigs in blankets for a quick snack.
