bell ringer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛl ˌrɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈbɛl ˌrɪŋə / (BrE)
a person who plays musical handbells, often as part of a group or in a church setting
person who plays handbells

Examples

The bell ringer performed a beautiful melody during the concert.
Every Sunday, the bell ringer leads the handbell choir in the church service.
She became a skilled bell ringer after years of practice with the ensemble.

3. NOUN

/ bɛl ˈrɪŋər / (AmE), / bɛl ˈrɪŋə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to ring church bells, especially to call people to a service or event
person who rings church bells

Examples

The bell ringer climbed the tower every Sunday morning to announce the start of service.
During weddings, the bell ringer performs a special series of chimes.
Local tradition honors the town’s oldest bell ringer with a yearly celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexton - person who looks after a church, sometimes including ringing the bells
