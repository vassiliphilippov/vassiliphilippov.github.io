American pit bull terrier

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛɹ.ɪ.kən pɪt bʊl təˈɹiɚ / (AmE), / əˈmɛɹ.ɪ.kən pɪt bʊl təˈɹɪə / (BrE)
a breed of muscular dog with a short, close-lying coat, developed in the United States, known for strength, agility, and determination
breed of muscular American terrier dog

Examples

The American pit bull terrier is often praised for its intelligence and loyalty.
She adopted an American pit bull terrier from the animal shelter last year.
Proper training is essential for any American pit bull terrier to ensure they are well-behaved pets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pit bull - a type of dog breed related to the American pit bull terrier

Etymology

From 'pit bull', originally referring to dogs bred for fighting in pits, and 'terrier', a type of hunting dog; breed developed in the United States.
