water pump

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔɾɚ pʌmp / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə pʌmp / (BrE)
a device in a vehicle's cooling system that circulates water or coolant to maintain the engine at an appropriate temperature
device circulating coolant in a vehicle engine

Examples

The mechanic said the water pump needed to be replaced to prevent the engine from overheating.
When the water pump fails, the car may start to leak coolant or overheat quickly.
He bought a new water pump for his old sedan after noticing coolant loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pump - a device for moving liquids or gases
