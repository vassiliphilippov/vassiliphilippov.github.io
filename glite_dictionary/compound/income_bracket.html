income bracket

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnˌkʌm ˈbrækɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌkʌm ˈbrækɪt / (BrE)
a category of people grouped according to the range of income they earn, especially for tax purposes
category of people by income range

Examples

Many families moved into a higher income bracket after receiving promotions at work.
Students from a lower income bracket may qualify for financial aid.
Tax rates increase progressively with each income bracket in the current system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tax bracket - a specific income range taxed at a particular rate
