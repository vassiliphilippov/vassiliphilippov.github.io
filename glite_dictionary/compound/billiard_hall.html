billiard hall

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪljɚd hɔl / (AmE), / ˈbɪliəd hɔːl / (BrE)
A room or establishment where people play billiards or similar cue sports.
room or venue for playing billiards

Examples

We spent the afternoon at the billiard hall with friends.
The billiard hall downtown hosts tournaments every Friday night.
After work, he often relaxes at his favorite billiard hall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pool hall - a place where people play pool (a similar cue sport)
