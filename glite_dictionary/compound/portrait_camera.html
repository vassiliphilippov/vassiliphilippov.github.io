portrait camera

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔːrtrət ˈkæməɹə / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtrɪt ˈkæm(ə)rə / (BrE)
a camera designed or equipped specifically for taking portrait photographs, often fitted with a portrait lens
camera designed for portrait photography

Examples

The photographer used a portrait camera to capture detailed headshots of the actors.
Many studios prefer a portrait camera because of its ability to produce flattering images.
She invested in a new portrait camera for her professional photography business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: portrait lens - a lens suitable for portrait photography
