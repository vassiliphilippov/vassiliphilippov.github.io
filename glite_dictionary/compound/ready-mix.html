ready-mix

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdi ˌmɪks / (AmE), / ˈrɛdi ˌmɪks / (BrE)
A commercial mixture that contains most of the necessary ingredients for making a particular dish or product, usually requiring only the addition of one or two fresh items.
commercial mixture for making a dish

Examples

We bought a ready-mix for pancakes to save time in the morning.
The cake was made using a chocolate ready-mix and tasted delicious.
Most supermarkets stock several brands of ready-mix for bread baking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mix - a commercially prepared mixture of ingredients

Etymology

Formed from 'ready' and 'mix', indicating a mixture that is prepared in advance and ready for use.
