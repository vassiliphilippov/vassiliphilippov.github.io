audio CD

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːdioʊ siː ˈdiː / (AmE), / ˈɔːdiəʊ siː ˈdiː / (BrE)
a compact disc used to record and play back sound, such as music or spoken word
compact disc for sound playback

Examples

I found my favorite album on audio CD at the record store.
Many cars still have a slot for playing audio CDs.
She prefers listening to music on audio CD rather than streaming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compact disc - a small plastic disc on which music or information is stored
