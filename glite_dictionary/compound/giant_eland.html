giant eland

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt ˈiːˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈiːlənd / (BrE)
a large dark-striped antelope native to western and central Africa, known for its impressive size and spiral horns
large antelope of western Africa

Examples

The giant eland is the largest species of antelope in Africa.
Conservationists are working to protect the endangered giant eland from habitat loss.
A herd of giant elands grazed quietly in the open savanna.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eland - a large African antelope with spiraled horns
