moon trefoil

1. NOUN

/ mun ˈtrɛfɔɪl / (AmE), / muːn ˈtriːfɔɪl / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub of the southern European highlands with downy leaves, yellow summer flowers, and distinctive snail-shaped pods
evergreen shrub with yellow flowers and snail-shaped pods

Examples

The garden's rockery was home to a striking moon trefoil in full bloom.
Botanists admire the unique snail-shaped pods produced by the moon trefoil.
She planted a moon trefoil to add evergreen foliage and summer flowers to her yard.
