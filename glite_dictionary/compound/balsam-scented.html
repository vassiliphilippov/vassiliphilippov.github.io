balsam-scented

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɔlsəm ˌsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlsəm ˌsɛntɪd / (BrE)
having the scent or aroma of balsam resin
smelling of balsam resin

Examples

The balsam-scented breeze drifted through the pine forest, refreshing everyone on the hike.
In her garden, balsam-scented candles added a soothing fragrance to the evening air.
The balsam-scented pillow reminded him of childhood vacations in the mountains.
