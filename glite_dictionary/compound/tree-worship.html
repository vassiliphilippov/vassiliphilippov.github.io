tree-worship

1. NOUN

/ ˈtriː ˌwʌrʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈtriː ˌwɜːʃɪp / (BrE)
The practice of worshipping trees, often as part of religious or spiritual beliefs.
the worship of trees

Examples

Archaeologists have discovered evidence of tree-worship in many ancient cultures.
Tree-worship played a significant role in some early pagan religions.
In her research, she studied the rituals associated with tree-worship in Asia.
