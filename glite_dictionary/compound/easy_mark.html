easy mark

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːzi mɑːrk / (AmE), / ˈiːzi mɑːk / (BrE)
a person who is easily deceived or victimized
someone easily deceived or victimized
informal

Examples

Con artists are always on the lookout for an easy mark in busy tourist areas.
Everyone in the group saw him as an easy mark due to his trusting nature.
The scammer thought she had found an easy mark, but he quickly caught on to the scheme.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: victim - a person harmed, injured, or tricked by someone or something
