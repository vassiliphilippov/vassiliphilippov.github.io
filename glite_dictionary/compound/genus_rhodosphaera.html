genus Rhodosphaera

1. NOUN

/ ˈʤinəs roʊˌdoʊˈsfɪərə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌrəʊdəʊˈsfɪərə / (BrE)
A genus of evergreen trees in the sumac family, consisting of a single species native to Australia.
genus of Australian evergreen sumac

Examples

The genus Rhodosphaera contains only one known species of evergreen tree native to Australia.
Botanists have studied the genus Rhodosphaera for its unique floral characteristics.
The seeds of the genus Rhodosphaera are an important food source for local wildlife.

Etymology

From Greek 'rhodo-' (rose) and 'sphaera' (sphere), referring to the appearance of the fruit.
