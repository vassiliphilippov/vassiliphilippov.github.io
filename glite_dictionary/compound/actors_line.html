actor's line

1. NOUN

/ ˈæktərz laɪn / (AmE), / ˈæktəz laɪn / (BrE)
a sequence of words spoken by an actor as part of a play or performance
part of a play's dialogue for an actor

Examples

The director asked her to repeat her actor's line with more emotion.
During rehearsals, he struggled to memorize every actor's line in the script.
If you forget an actor's line on stage, you must quickly recover to keep the scene moving.

Synset Examples

the actor forgot his speech

Related Terms

  • Synonym: line - a part of spoken dialogue in a play, movie, or show
  • Synonym: speech - a formal address or a spoken part in a play
