federal department

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛdəɹəl dɪˈpɑɹtmənt / (AmE), / ˈfɛdərəl dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
A department of the federal government of the United States, responsible for specific administrative functions at the national level.
department of the U.S. federal government
american

Examples

The Department of Justice is a federal department responsible for law enforcement in the United States.
Each federal department oversees different aspects of public policy and government administration.
She applied for a job at a federal department specializing in environmental protection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: government agency - an administrative unit of government
