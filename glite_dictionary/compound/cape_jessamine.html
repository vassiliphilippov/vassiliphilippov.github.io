cape jessamine

1. NOUN

/ keɪp ˈdʒɛsəˌmin / (AmE), / keɪp ˈdʒɛsəˌmiːn / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub with large, fragrant, waxy white flowers and glossy leaves, widely cultivated as an ornamental plant
evergreen shrub with fragrant white flowers

Examples

The cape jessamine in her garden filled the air with a sweet, delicate fragrance.
He planted a new cape jessamine shrub by the front path to add some year-round greenery.
After the spring rain, the blossoms of the cape jessamine appeared even more vibrant and fresh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gardenia - evergreen shrub with fragrant white flowers; often called 'cape jessamine'

Etymology

The name refers to its origin near the Cape of Good Hope and its jasmine-like flowers.
