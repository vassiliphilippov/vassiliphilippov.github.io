electrical engineer

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛktrɪkəl ɪnˈdʒɪnɪr / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrɪkəl ɪnˈdʒɪnɪə / (BrE)
a person who designs, develops, or maintains electrical systems and equipment
person specializing in electrical systems

Examples

She works as an electrical engineer at a major technology company.
The electrical engineer solved the power outage problem in just a few hours.
After graduating, he decided to become an electrical engineer specializing in renewable energy.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: engineer - a person who designs, builds, or maintains engines, machines, or structures
  • Synonym: electrical engineering - the branch of engineering focused on electricity and its applications
