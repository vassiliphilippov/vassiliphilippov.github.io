father-in-law

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑðər ɪn lɔ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðə ɪn lɔː / (BrE)
the father of one’s husband or wife
the father of one’s spouse

Examples

After the wedding, I met my father-in-law for the first time.
My father-in-law is visiting us this weekend from out of town.
She often seeks advice from her father-in-law about family matters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mother-in-law - the mother of one’s spouse
