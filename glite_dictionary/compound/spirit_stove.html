spirit stove

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrɪt stoʊv / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪt stəʊv / (BrE)
a portable stove that burns alcohol or other volatile liquid fuel for heating or cooking
portable stove fueled by alcohol

Examples

The hikers used a spirit stove to boil water at the campsite.
Her old spirit stove was reliable even in windy weather.
A compact spirit stove is great for lightweight backpacking trips.
