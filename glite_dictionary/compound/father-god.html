Father-God

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑːðər ɡɑd / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðə ɡɒd / (BrE)
God regarded as the Father, especially as the first person of the Christian Trinity.
God as the Father in Christianity

Examples

In Christian doctrine, believers pray to the Father-God as the creator and sustainer of the universe.
The concept of Father-God highlights the paternal relationship between God and humanity.
Many hymns address the Father-God with reverence and love.

Synset Examples

hear our prayers, Heavenly Father

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Heavenly Father - God as regarded in Christian prayer and liturgy
  • Degree Higher: Son of God - Jesus Christ, the second person of the Trinity
