extension phone

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈstɛnʃən foʊn / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnʃən fəʊn / (BrE)
an additional telephone connected to the same line as the main telephone, allowing calls to be made from more than one location
additional phone on same line

Examples

She answered the call on the extension phone in the kitchen so she wouldn't miss it.
Please don't pick up the extension phone while I'm on an important call.
Every office has at least one extension phone to make communication easier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extension - a telephone connected to the same line as another
