rise in prices

1. NOUN

/ raɪz ɪn ˈpraɪsɪz / (AmE), / raɪz ɪn ˈpraɪsɪz / (BrE)
an increase in the cost of goods or services
increase in costs of goods or services

Examples

Consumers complained about the rise in prices affecting their monthly grocery bills.
A worldwide rise in prices of oil can trigger economic instability.
The government is trying to control the rise in prices through new regulations.

Synset Examples

The sudden rise in prices felt like a plague to consumers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: price increase - the act of something becoming more expensive
  • Antonym: price drop - a reduction in the cost of goods or services
