damson plum tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæm.sən ˈplʌm ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈdam.sən ˈplʌm ˌtriː / (BrE)
a type of plum tree (Prunus domestica subsp. insititia) long cultivated for its small, oval, tart edible fruit known as damsons
tree bearing tart edible damson plums

Examples

The damson plum tree in our garden produces a bountiful crop every autumn.
Orchards filled with damson plum trees were once common in the English countryside.
She made jam from the fruit harvested from her damson plum tree.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: plum tree - a tree that bears plums
  • Synonym: damson - the edible fruit of the damson plum tree

Etymology

Damson from Latin 'prunum Damascenum' meaning 'plum of Damascus'.
