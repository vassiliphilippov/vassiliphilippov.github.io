from various angles

1. ADP

/ frəm ˈvɛriəs ˈæŋɡəlz / (AmE), / frəm ˈveəriəs ˈæŋɡəlz / (BrE)
considering something from different perspectives or viewpoints; in a variety of ways or positions
considering different perspectives or viewpoints

Examples

The debate was analyzed from various angles to reveal hidden implications.
We looked at the proposal from various angles before making a decision.
This painting can be appreciated from various angles, both artistically and historically.

Synset Examples

They discussed the story from various angles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: from all sides - considering something from every possible direction or perspective
