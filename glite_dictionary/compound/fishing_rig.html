fishing rig

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃɪŋ rɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪŋ rɪɡ / (BrE)
a set of fishing equipment, typically consisting of a rod, line, hook, and related tackle, assembled for catching fish
fishing equipment setup

Examples

He carefully assembled his fishing rig before casting into the lake.
Different types of fishing rig are used depending on the target fish.
Her new fishing rig includes a lightweight rod and a strong reel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tackle - equipment used in fishing
  • Synonym: fishing rod - a long, flexible rod used to catch fish
