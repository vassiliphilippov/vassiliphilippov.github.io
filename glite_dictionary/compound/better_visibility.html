better visibility

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛtər vɪˌzɪbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˈbɛtə vɪˌzɪbɪlɪti / (BrE)
greater exposure or attention, especially to make something more noticeable or prominent
greater exposure or attention

Examples

Increasing the size of your sign can give your business better visibility in the neighborhood.
