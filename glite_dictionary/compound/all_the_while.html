all the while

1. ADV

/ ɔl ðə waɪl / (AmE), / ɔːl ðiː waɪl / (BrE)
throughout the entire period of time that something is happening; during the whole time
for the whole duration of an event

Examples

She kept a calm expression on her face all the while she was speaking.
He pretended to listen all the while planning his response.
They danced all the while forgetting about their worries.

Synset Examples

He worked hard all the while dreaming of success.
She was planning a surprise all the while he was unaware.
He smiled all the while she was talking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: throughout - for the whole period of time
  • Synonym: meanwhile - at the same time
