franquist supporter

1. NOUN

/ frænˌkwiːst səˈpɔrtər / (AmE), / frænˌkwiːst səˈpɔːtə / (BrE)
a person who supports or supported Francisco Franco's regime in Spain
supporter of Franco's regime in Spain

Examples

Many franquist supporters remained influential in Spanish politics after the dictator's death.
The artist's family had been franquist supporters during the Spanish Civil War.
Some regions of Spain still have monuments built by franquist supporters.

Synset Examples

The documentary explored the lives of franquist supporters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: franquist - a supporter or advocate of Franco's regime
  • Antonym: anti-Francoist - opposed to Franco's regime in Spain

Etymology

From Spanish Franco (Francisco Franco), with -ist (supporter of) and 'supporter'.
