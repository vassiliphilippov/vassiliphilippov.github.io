balloon flower

1. NOUN

/ bəˈlun ˌflaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / bəˈluːn ˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
a perennial plant native to southwestern North America, with fragrant puffed-up white to reddish-pink flowers in long narrow clusters on erect stems
perennial plant with puffed-up clustered flowers

Examples

The desert landscape was dotted with blooming balloon flowers in the early spring.
She planted balloon flowers along the fence for their beautiful white and pink clusters.
Hikers spotted a rare balloon flower growing near the canyon trail.
