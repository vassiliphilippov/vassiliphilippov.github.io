a pinch of

1. NOUN

/ ə pɪntʃ əv / (AmE), / ə pɪntʃ ɒv / (BrE)
a very small amount of something, especially used with salt, spices, or other seasonings
a small quantity (especially of seasoning)

Examples

Add a pinch of salt to enhance the flavor of the soup.
She sprinkled a pinch of cinnamon over her oatmeal every morning.
The recipe only calls for a pinch of black pepper, so don't overdo it.

Synset Examples

The risotto was flavored with a pinch of saffron.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: a dash of - a small amount of a liquid or ingredient, often used with drinks or cooking
