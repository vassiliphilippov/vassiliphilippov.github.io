blue marguerite

1. NOUN

/ bluː mɑrɡəˈrit / (AmE), / bluː mɑːɡəˈriːt / (BrE)
A hairy South African or Australian subshrub with blue, daisylike flowers, grown as an ornamental plant.
ornamental shrub with blue daisylike flowers

Examples

The blue marguerite brightens up many gardens with its cheerful blossoms.
We planted a blue marguerite near the patio to add a splash of color.
A row of blue marguerites lined the edge of the flower bed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Felicia amelloides - Botanical name for blue marguerite
