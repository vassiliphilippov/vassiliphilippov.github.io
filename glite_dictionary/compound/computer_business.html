computer business

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtɚ ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈbɪznəs / (BrE)
A company that manufactures and sells computers and related products.
company making and selling computers

Examples

She started her own computer business after graduating with a degree in engineering.
Many jobs have been created in the computer business over the past decade.
The computer business is highly competitive, requiring constant innovation and adaptation.
