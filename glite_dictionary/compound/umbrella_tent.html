umbrella tent

1. NOUN

/ əmˈbrɛl.ə tɛnt / (AmE), / ʌmˈbrɛl.ə tɛnt / (BrE)
a small tent supported by a central pole and radiating metal ribs, resembling an umbrella in structure
a tent with a central pole and umbrella-like ribs

Examples

We set up the umbrella tent in less than ten minutes despite the breeze.
The umbrella tent provided just enough shelter for two people.
Thanks to its lightweight design, the umbrella tent was easy to carry during our hike.
