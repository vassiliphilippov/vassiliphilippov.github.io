implements of war

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪmpləmənts əv wɔɹ / (AmE), / ˈɪmplɪmənts əv wɔː / (BrE)
objects or devices, especially weapons, used for fighting in warfare; weapons considered collectively
weapons or devices used in warfare

Examples

The museum displayed ancient implements of war from various civilizations.
Nations often invest heavily in the development of new implements of war.
Diplomats hope to reduce the world's reliance on implements of war.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weapons - instruments or devices designed for inflicting damage or harm
