political science

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
the study of government, political institutions, and political behavior.
study of government and political systems

Examples

She decided to major in political science at university to better understand government systems.
Many careers in public policy require a background in political science.
The conference featured experts in political science from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: government - the group of people with the authority to govern a country or state
  • Synonym: politics - activities associated with the governance of a country or area

Etymology

From ‘political’, relating to government, and ‘science’, meaning systematic study.
