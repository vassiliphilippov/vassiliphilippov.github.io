cat lady

1. NOUN

/ kæt ˈleɪdi / (AmE), / kæt ˈleɪdi / (BrE)
a woman who owns many cats, especially to an unusual degree, and is often regarded as eccentric or socially awkward
woman who owns many cats, often eccentric

Examples

Everyone in the apartment building calls her the cat lady because of her collection of feline companions.
After adopting her seventh stray, she began to embrace her inner cat lady.
The movie featured a quirky cat lady who talked to her pets as if they were children.

Synset Examples

She proudly identifies as a cat lady with her five rescue cats.
The neighborhood knows her as the cat lady who feeds all the strays.
At the party, she joked about being a cat lady in training.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy cat lady - an informal or exaggerated variant of 'cat lady'
