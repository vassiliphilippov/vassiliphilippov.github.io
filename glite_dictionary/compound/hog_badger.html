hog badger

1. NOUN

/ hɔg ˈbædʒər / (AmE), / hɒg ˈbadʒə / (BrE)
a Southeast Asian badger with a snout resembling that of a pig
a Southeast Asian badger with a pig-like snout

Examples

The hog badger is often spotted in the forests of Southeast Asia.
Unlike other badgers, the hog badger has a long snout similar to a pig's nose.
Researchers are studying how the hog badger uses its unique snout to forage for food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: badger - a burrowing nocturnal mammal with a distinctive white-striped head
  • Degree Lower: pig - a domesticated animal with a snout used for digging
