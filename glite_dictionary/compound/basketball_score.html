basketball score

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskətˌbɔl skɔr / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtbɔːl skɔː / (BrE)

number of points in a basketball game

Examples

The final basketball score was 89 to 78 in favor of the home team.

Last night's basketball score was much closer than anyone expected.
