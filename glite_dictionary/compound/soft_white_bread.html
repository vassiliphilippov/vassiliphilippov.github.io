soft white bread

1. NOUN

/ sɒft waɪt brɛd / (AmE), / sɒft waɪt brɛd / (BrE)
bread that is light, fluffy, and pale in color, typically made from refined wheat flour and often used for sandwiches and toast
light, fluffy, pale bread

Examples

She prefers to make sandwiches with soft white bread because of its texture.
The bakery was famous for its freshly baked soft white bread.
He bought a loaf of soft white bread to use for French toast.

Synset Examples

The kids love their toast made with soft white bread.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: whole wheat bread - bread made with whole wheat flour
  • Synonym: white bread - bread made from refined white wheat flour
