investment adviser

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈvɛstmənt ədˈvaɪzər / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɛstmənt ədˈvaɪzə / (BrE)
a person or firm that provides advice to clients about investing their money
person advising on investments

Examples

She hired an investment adviser to help manage her retirement savings.
Many people consult an investment adviser before making big decisions about their portfolios.
An experienced investment adviser can recommend a balanced approach to risk and reward.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: financial adviser - a person who gives financial advice to clients
