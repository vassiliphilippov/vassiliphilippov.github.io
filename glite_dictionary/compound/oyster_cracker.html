oyster cracker

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔɪstər ˌkrækər / (AmE), / ˈɔɪstə ˌkrækə / (BrE)
a small, dry, usually round cracker often served with soups, particularly clam chowder and other seafood dishes
small dry round cracker for soup

Examples

She added a handful of oyster crackers to her bowl of chowder.
The restaurant served oyster crackers with every cup of soup.
I love munching on oyster crackers as a light snack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cracker - a thin, crisp biscuit
