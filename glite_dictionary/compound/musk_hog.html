musk hog

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsk hɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈmʌsk hɒɡ / (BrE)
a nocturnal, pig-like wild animal native to North and South America, also known as a peccary
nocturnal pig-like animal (peccary)

Examples

Hunters in the region sometimes spot a musk hog foraging at dusk.
The distinctive odor comes from the scent glands of the musk hog.
Unlike true pigs, the musk hog has a different dental structure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peccary - another name for musk hog, a wild pig-like animal of the Americas

Etymology

Refers to the musky odor produced by these animals and their hog-like appearance.
