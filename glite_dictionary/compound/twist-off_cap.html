twist-off cap

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɪst ˌɔf kæp / (AmE), / ˈtwɪst ˌɒf kap / (BrE)
A type of lid designed to be removed from a container, such as a jar or bottle, by twisting it.
removable cap that twists off

Examples

The jar of jam had a twist-off cap for easy opening.
Most soda bottles now use a twist-off cap instead of the old pry-off lids.
He struggled to loosen the twist-off cap on the bottle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screw cap - a cap that screws onto the threads of a bottle or jar
