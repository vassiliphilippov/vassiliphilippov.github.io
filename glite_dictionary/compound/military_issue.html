military issue

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˈɪʃuː / (BrE)
Supplies such as clothing, food, or ammunition officially provided by the armed forces.
official supplies given by the military

Examples

Each soldier received a military issue jacket as part of his standard equipment.
The boots he wore were standard military issue and designed for tough conditions.
During training, we lived on military issue meals for weeks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: government issue - Official items provided by a government, especially to military personnel
