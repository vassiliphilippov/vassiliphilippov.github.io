butcher block

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊtʃɚ blɑk / (AmE), / ˈbʊtʃə blɒk / (BrE)
a thick slab of hardwood, typically formed by bonding together strips of wood, used especially for cutting and preparing food
thick hardwood slab for food preparation

Examples

The chef chopped vegetables on the sturdy butcher block in the kitchen.
Our new butcher block countertop adds a rustic touch to the room.
Always clean your butcher block after preparing raw meat to avoid contamination.
