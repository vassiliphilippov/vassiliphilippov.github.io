litter box

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬ɚ bɑks / (AmE), / ˈlɪtə bɒks / (BrE)
A container, usually filled with absorbent material, used by pets such as cats to urinate and defecate indoors.
container for pet animal waste

Examples

Every morning, she empties the litter box to keep the room smelling fresh.
The kitten learned to use the litter box very quickly.
They placed a new litter box in the laundry room for their new cat.

Synset Examples

Make sure to clean the litter box daily.
He accidentally stepped in the litter box again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cat box - container with litter for pets to defecate or urinate in indoors
