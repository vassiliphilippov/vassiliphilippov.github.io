gluten bread

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡluːtən brɛd / (AmE), / ˈɡluːtən brɛd / (BrE)
bread made primarily with gluten flour, often resulting in a chewy texture and higher protein content
bread made with gluten flour

Examples

Many bakeries now offer gluten bread for customers looking for higher protein options.
The recipe calls for gluten bread instead of regular wheat bread.
Some people prefer the texture of gluten bread in their sandwiches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bread - a staple food made of flour and water, baked
