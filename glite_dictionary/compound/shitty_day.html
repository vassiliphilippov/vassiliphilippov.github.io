shitty day

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪti deɪ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪti deɪ / (BrE)
A very bad or unpleasant day; used to describe a day filled with misfortune or dissatisfaction.
a very bad or unpleasant day
informal vulgar

Examples

I just want to go home after such a shitty day at the office.
Whenever I have a shitty day, I treat myself to some comfort food.
Everyone has a shitty day now and then, but tomorrow will be better.

Synset Examples

She had a shitty day at work yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad day - a day marked by many problems or unpleasant events
