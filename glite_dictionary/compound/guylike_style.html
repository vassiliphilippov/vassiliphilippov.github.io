guylike style

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡaɪˌlaɪk staɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪˌlaɪk staɪl / (BrE)
a style in appearance, dress, or manner that is typically associated with men or masculinity
style typically associated with men

Many celebrities adopt a guylike style to project an image of confidence and independence.
