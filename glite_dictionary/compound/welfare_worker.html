welfare worker

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛlˌfɛr ˌwɝːkər / (AmE), / ˈwɛlˌfeə ˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to provide social services, especially to disadvantaged individuals or groups
provider of social services to the disadvantaged

Examples

The welfare worker visited the family to assess their living conditions and provide assistance.
As a welfare worker, she helped connect homeless individuals with housing and medical care.
Local authorities hired more welfare workers to support vulnerable communities during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: social worker - person professionally engaged in social work
