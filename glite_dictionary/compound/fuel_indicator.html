fuel indicator

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjul ɪn.dəˌkeɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəl ɪn.dɪˌkeɪ.tə / (BrE)
a device that displays the amount of fuel remaining in a vehicle's tank
device showing remaining fuel in a vehicle

Examples

The fuel indicator showed that the tank was nearly empty.
Always check the fuel indicator before starting a long drive.
When the fuel indicator reaches the red zone, it's time to refuel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuel gauge - an instrument in a vehicle that shows the amount of fuel left
