Pill Hill

1. NOUN

/ pɪl hɪl / (AmE), / pɪl hɪl / (BrE)
an informal name for a neighborhood or area with a high concentration of hospitals, clinics, or medical facilities
neighborhood with many hospitals or clinics
informal american

Examples

He moved to Pill Hill to be closer to the hospital where he worked.
Many doctors and nurses live in Pill Hill because of its proximity to medical facilities.
Pill Hill has become a landmark area due to its cluster of health clinics.

Synset Examples

The area known as Pill Hill is famous for pharmacies.
They called it Pill Hill due to the number of clinics.

Etymology

From the words 'pill' (referring to medicine) and 'hill.'
