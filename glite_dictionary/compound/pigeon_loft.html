pigeon loft

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪdʒən lɔft / (AmE), / ˈpɪdʒən lɒft / (BrE)
a raised structure or shelter where pigeons are bred and kept
shelter for keeping pigeons

Examples

He built a large pigeon loft in his backyard for his prize racing birds.
The old barn was converted into a pigeon loft for breeding rare varieties.
Many city rooftops have a hidden pigeon loft where enthusiasts tend their flocks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dovecote - a shelter with holes for domestic pigeons or doves
