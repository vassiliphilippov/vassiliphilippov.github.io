Hellenic Republic

1. PROPN

/ hɛˈlɛnɪk rɪˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / hɛˈlɛnɪk rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
the official name for the country of Greece, located in southeastern Europe on the southern part of the Balkan Peninsula
official name for the country of Greece

Examples

The Hellenic Republic officially became a member of the European Union in 1981.
Many tourists visit the Hellenic Republic for its historical sites and beautiful islands.
The Hellenic Republic is famous for its production of olives and olive oil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Greece - a country in southeastern Europe; common name for the Hellenic Republic

Etymology

From Greek 'Hellas' (the native name for Greece) and 'Republic'.
