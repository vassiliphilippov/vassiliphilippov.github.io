bread maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛd ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbrɛd ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who bakes bread or cake as a profession or regular activity
person who bakes bread or cake

Examples

As a skilled bread maker, she can create delicious loaves from scratch every morning.
He dreamed of becoming a professional bread maker and opening his own bakery.
The village bread maker was known for his sweet pastries and hearty breads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baker - a person whose job is to bake and sell bread, cakes, etc.
