beginning and ending

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈɡɪnɪŋ ənd ˈɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈɡɪnɪŋ ənd ˈɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
the start and finish of something; the points or periods where something commences and concludes
the start and finish of something

Examples

The author described the beginning and ending of the novel in great detail.
Every story must have a clear beginning and ending to satisfy its readers.
Without a beginning and ending, a project can feel aimless and unfinished.

Synset Examples

Janus symbolizes beginnings and endings in Roman culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: start and finish - the points at which something begins and ends
