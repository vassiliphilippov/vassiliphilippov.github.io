circular file

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜːrkjələr faɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊlə faɪl / (BrE)
a wastebasket; used humorously to mean a container for discarded paper and rubbish, especially in offices
wastebasket (humorous)
informal

Examples

Whenever my boss dislikes a report, it ends up in the circular file by his desk.
She tossed the unwanted memo straight into the circular file.
Emails are more eco-friendly, but paperwork still often finds its way to the circular file.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wastebasket - a container for waste paper and other dry rubbish
  • Synonym: trash can - an American English synonym for wastebasket

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɜrkjələr faɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊlə faɪl / (BrE)
a humorous or ironic term for a wastebasket or trash can, especially when referring to the act of discarding something as unimportant or unwanted
humorous term for trash can

Examples

Your report will probably end up in the circular file if you don't fix the errors.
He joked that her ideas always went straight to the circular file.
She deposited the rejected application into the circular file without hesitation.

Synset Examples

That proposal went straight to the circular file.
She knew her complaints would end up in the circular file.
He tossed the irrelevant documents into the circular file.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wastebasket - a container for paper and other dry waste, especially in offices
  • Synonym: trash can - a container for waste, especially household or office trash

Etymology

From 'file' (a container for documents) humorously referring to a trash can's round shape.
