Defense Department

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛns dɪˌpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the federal department of the United States government responsible for safeguarding national security and overseeing the armed forces
US government department managing national defense
american

Examples

The Defense Department announced a new security initiative in response to emerging threats.
Several top generals briefed the Defense Department on the situation overseas.
After the reorganization in 1947, the Defense Department assumed responsibility for all military branches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pentagon - the headquarters of the United States Department of Defense
  • Synonym: Department of Defense - official name of the Defense Department in the US
