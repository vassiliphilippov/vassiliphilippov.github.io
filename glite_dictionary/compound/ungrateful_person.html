ungrateful person

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈɡreɪtfəl ˈpɜrsən / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡreɪtfʊl ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who does not show appreciation or gratitude for what is done for them
person showing no gratitude

Examples

After all her help, he acted like an ungrateful person and never even thanked her.
No one wants to feel like an ungrateful person when someone does them a favor.
Sometimes children can seem like an ungrateful person when they take their parents for granted.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ingrate - a person who is not grateful
  • Antonym: grateful person - a person who shows gratitude
