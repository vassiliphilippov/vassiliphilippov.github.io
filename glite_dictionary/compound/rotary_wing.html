rotary wing

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊˌtɛri wɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊtəri wɪŋ / (BrE)
the long rotating airfoil on a helicopter that provides lift and supports the aircraft in the air
rotating helicopter lift airfoil

Examples

The rotary wing of the helicopter allowed it to hover steadily above the ground.
Engineers inspected the rotary wing for any signs of metal fatigue after the flight.
Many modern helicopters use composite materials in their rotary wing construction for added strength.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rotor blade - the blade of the rotor on a helicopter
