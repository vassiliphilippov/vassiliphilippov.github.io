road map

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊd ˌmæp / (AmE), / ˈrəʊd ˌmæp / (BrE)
A map that shows roads and highways, used to help plan or navigate automobile journeys.
map showing roads for travel

Examples

Before the era of GPS devices, travelers relied on a road map to navigate unfamiliar routes.
The park ranger handed us a detailed road map of the national park.
My grandfather keeps an old road map in the glove compartment of his car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atlas - a book of maps or charts
  • Degree Lower: street map - a detailed map showing individual streets
  • Degree Higher: highway map - a map specifically showing major highways and roads
