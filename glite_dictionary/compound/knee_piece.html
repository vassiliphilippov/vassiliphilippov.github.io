knee piece

1. NOUN

/ ˈni pis / (AmE), / ˈni piːs / (BrE)
a piece of armor designed to protect the knee
armor protecting the knee

Examples

The knight's knee piece was dented during the fierce battle.
In museum displays, the knee piece often stands out due to its decorative design.
Medieval armorers crafted each knee piece to fit the wearer precisely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poleyn - armor plate covering the knee
