social station

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
a person's position or rank within a social hierarchy
position in a social hierarchy

Examples

Her upbringing in an affluent family gave her a higher social station in the community.
Regardless of his social station, he treated everyone with kindness and respect.
Many historical laws were enforced differently depending on a person's social station.

Synset Examples

the British are more aware of social status than Americans are

Related Terms

  • Synonym: social status - a person's standing or importance in relation to others within a society
  • Synonym: rank - a position in a hierarchy or scale, especially in society or the military
