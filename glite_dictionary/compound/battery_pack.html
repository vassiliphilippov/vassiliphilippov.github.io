battery pack

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæɾəri pæk / (AmE), / ˈbætəri pæk / (BrE)
a portable unit consisting of one or more batteries used to power electronic devices
portable unit of batteries for powering devices

Examples

I always carry a battery pack when traveling to keep my phone charged.
The camera's battery pack needs to be replaced before the next shoot.
Her wireless headphones only work if the battery pack is fully charged.

Synset Examples

He bought the latest smartphone with a sleek design and a powerful battery pack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power bank - a portable device used to recharge electronic devices
