deep brown

1. NOUN

/ dip braʊn / (AmE), / diːp braʊn / (BrE)
a color ranging from medium brown to dark brown
medium to dark brown color

Examples

The rich deep brown of the chocolate made it look irresistible.
She chose a deep brown paint for the living room walls to create a warm atmosphere.
His eyes were a striking deep brown that seemed almost black in certain lights.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: brown - a color produced by mixing red, yellow, and black
  • Degree Higher: dark brown - a color darker than standard brown
