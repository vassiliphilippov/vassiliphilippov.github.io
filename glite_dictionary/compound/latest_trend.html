latest trend

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪtəst trɛnd / (AmE), / ˈleɪtɪst trɛnd / (BrE)
the most recent style, fashion, or direction that is currently popular in a particular field
most recent popular style or development

Examples

Social media has made it easier to follow the latest trend in fashion and beauty.
The company always tries to adopt the latest trend in technology to stay ahead of competitors.
At the conference, experts discussed the latest trend in renewable energy solutions.

Synset Examples

D&M always has the latest trends in fashion.
The conference focused on the latest trends in ophthal research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fad - a style or activity that is very popular for a short time
  • Synonym: fashion - a popular or the latest style of clothing, hair, decoration, or behaviour
  • Antonym: tradition - a long-established custom or belief
