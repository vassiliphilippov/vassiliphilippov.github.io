bush nasturtium

1. NOUN

/ bʊʃ næˈstɜrʃəm / (AmE), / bʊʃ næˈstɜːʃ(i)əm / (BrE)
an annual plant with deep yellow flowers, smaller than the common garden nasturtium, often grown as an ornamental.
annual plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The bush nasturtium brightened the border with its deep yellow blossoms.
Unlike traditional nasturtiums, the bush nasturtium stays compact and blooms profusely all summer.
She planted seeds of bush nasturtium along the walkway for a splash of color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nasturtium - a genus of plants with brightly colored flowers, commonly grown as ornamentals
