evergreen magnolia

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛvərˌɡrin mæɡˈnoʊliə / (AmE), / ˈɛvəˌɡriːn mæɡˈnəʊliə / (BrE)
A large evergreen tree native to the southern United States, noted for its stiff, glossy leaves and large, fragrant white flowers.
southern US evergreen tree with showy flowers

Examples

The evergreen magnolia is a popular ornamental tree admired for its huge, fragrant blossoms.
In spring, the garden was dominated by the scent of evergreen magnolia flowers.
Their driveway is lined with evergreen magnolia trees, providing shade and beauty all year round.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnolia - a genus of flowering trees and shrubs with large blossoms
