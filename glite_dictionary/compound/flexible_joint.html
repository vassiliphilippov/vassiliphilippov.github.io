flexible joint

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɛksəbəl dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈflɛksɪbəl dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
a joint that connects two parts and allows one part to move or swing relative to the other
joint allowing relative movement

Examples

This door uses a flexible joint so it can swing open smoothly.
Engineers installed a flexible joint to accommodate the movement caused by temperature changes.
The robotic arm's flexible joint allows for a wide range of motion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hinge - a joint that allows movement in one direction only
