sea queen

1. NOUN

/ si kwiːn / (AmE), / siː kwiːn / (BrE)
a female pirate chief, especially among Viking raiders or in maritime legend
female Viking or pirate chief

Examples

The legendary sea queen commanded her ship during the fiercest battles.
Historians argue over whether the tale of the Norse sea queen is based on fact or fiction.
In the novel, a daring sea queen leads her crew across the stormy North Sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pirate queen - a woman who is a chief or leader among pirates
  • Synonym: pirate - a person who attacks and robs ships at sea
