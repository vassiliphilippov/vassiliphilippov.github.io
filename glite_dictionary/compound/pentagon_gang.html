Pentagon Gang

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɛn.tə.ɡɑn ɡæŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.tə.ɡan ɡæŋ / (BrE)
a Filipino terrorist group that broke away from the Moro Islamic Liberation Front in 2001 and was involved in terrorism, kidnapping, and extortion
Filipino terrorist group (2001-)

Examples

The Pentagon Gang became notorious for a series of high-profile kidnappings in the southern Philippines.
After splitting from the Moro Islamic Liberation Front, the Pentagon Gang continued violent activities in the region.
Authorities launched a crackdown to dismantle the Pentagon Gang and restore security to affected communities.
