fresh cut

1. NOUN

/ frɛʃ kʌt / (AmE), / frɛʃ kʌt / (BrE)
A piece of meat that has been recently prepared or cut, typically offered for sale while still fresh.
recently prepared piece of meat

Examples

The grocery store always has a selection of fresh cuts for their customers.
She asked the butcher if he had any fresh cuts available today.
Restaurants prefer to use fresh cuts to ensure the best taste and texture.

Synset Examples

The butcher displayed fresh cuts on the meat rack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fresh meat - meat that is not preserved by freezing or curing
