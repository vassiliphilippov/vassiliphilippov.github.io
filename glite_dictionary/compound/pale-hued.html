pale-hued

1. ADJ

/ peɪl hjud / (AmE), / peɪl hjuːd / (BrE)
having a light or faint color; not deeply colored or vibrant
having a light or faint color

Examples

The artist chose a pale-hued palette for a softer effect in her landscape.
He wore a pale-hued shirt that complemented his fair complexion.
Spring mornings often bring pale-hued sunlight filtering through the mist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pale - light in color or shade; not deep or rich in color
  • Antonym: vivid - intensely deep or bright in color
