systems analysis

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪstəmz əˌnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəmz əˌnæləsɪs / (BrE)
the detailed examination of the components and interactions of a system, especially to identify its requirements, processes, and possible improvements
detailed study of a system's parts and processes

Examples

An effective systems analysis helped the company streamline its logistics operations.
The team conducted a thorough systems analysis before implementing the new software.
Modern businesses rely on systems analysis to optimize their workflow and resources.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: system analysis - alternative form meaning detailed examination of a system
