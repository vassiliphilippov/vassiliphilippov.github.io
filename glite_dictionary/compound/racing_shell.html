racing shell

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪ.sɪŋ ʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.sɪŋ ʃɛl / (BrE)
a very light, long, and narrow boat designed for competitive rowing
narrow boat for rowing races

Examples

The team practiced daily in their racing shell to prepare for the regatta.
A racing shell requires skillful handling due to its narrow design and delicate balance.
During the race, four racing shells glided swiftly across the lake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scull - a type of rowing boat, often similar to a racing shell
  • Degree Lower: rowing boat - a boat propelled by oars, typically broader and heavier than a racing shell
