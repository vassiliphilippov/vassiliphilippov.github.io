base wallah

1. NOUN

/ beɪs ˈwɑlə / (AmE), / beɪs ˈwɒlə / (BrE)
a person responsible for logistics or administrative duties at a base, especially in a military or organizational context
person handling logistics at a base

Examples

The base wallah coordinated supply deliveries flawlessly, ensuring the team had everything they needed.
Every base wallah needs to be adaptable and resourceful to manage daily operations effectively.
As a base wallah, she oversaw transportation, accommodation, and security at the remote research facility.

Synset Examples

The base wallah coordinated supply deliveries flawlessly.
Every base wallah knows how to handle emergencies.
As a base wallah, he managed the entire logistics team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wallah - person in charge of or associated with a particular duty

Etymology

From 'base' (military or organizational site) + Hindustani 'wālā' (a person associated with or responsible for something).
