operating theatre

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑpəˌreɪtɪŋ ˈθiətər / (AmE), / ˈɒpəreɪtɪŋ ˈθɪətə / (BrE)
a room in a hospital equipped and used for performing surgical operations
room for performing surgery

Examples

The nurse prepared the operating theatre before the first surgery of the day.
Strict hygiene is maintained in every operating theatre to prevent infections.
After his injury, he was quickly taken to the operating theatre for emergency surgery.
