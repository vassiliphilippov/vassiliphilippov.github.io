music library

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuzɪk ˌlaɪbrɛri / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪk ˌlaɪbrəri / (BrE)
a collection of music recordings or musical scores, often organized for easy access or reference
collection of music recordings or scores

Examples

She donated her entire music library to the local university.
His digital music library contains thousands of songs from various genres.
The school maintains a music library for students to borrow sheet music and recordings.

Synset Examples

She spent hours trying to catalogue her vast music library.
