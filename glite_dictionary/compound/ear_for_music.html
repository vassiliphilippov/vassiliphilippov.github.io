ear for music

1. NOUN

/ ɪr fɔr ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / ɪə fə ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
the natural ability to recognize, appreciate, or understand music
musical aptitude or appreciation

Examples

She has an incredible ear for music and can pick up melodies after hearing them just once.
Even without formal training, his ear for music helped him become a talented songwriter.
Developing an ear for music is essential for anyone who wants to play an instrument well.

Synset Examples

She certainly doesn’t have Van Gogh's ear for music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musical ear - an ear for recognizing pitches and musical elements
