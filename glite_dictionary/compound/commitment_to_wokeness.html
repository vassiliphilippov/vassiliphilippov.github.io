commitment to wokeness

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɪtmənt tə ˈwoʊknəs / (AmE), / kəˈmɪtmənt tə ˈwəʊknəs / (BrE)
a strong dedication to being aware of and addressing social and political injustices, especially related to issues such as racism and inequality
dedication to social awareness and activism

Examples

Her commitment to wokeness inspired others to engage with social issues more thoughtfully.
The company's commitment to wokeness is reflected in its inclusive hiring policies and training programs.
Some critics argue that a strong commitment to wokeness can hinder open discussion and debate.

Synset Examples

Their commitment to wokeness was evident in discussions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: social justice - the principle of fair and just relations within society
  • Antonym: apathy - lack of interest or concern, especially toward important issues
