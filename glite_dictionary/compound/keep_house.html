to keep house

1. VERB

/ kip haʊs / (AmE), / kiːp haʊs / (BrE)
to manage a household and perform domestic chores
manage a household

Examples

After retiring, he chose to keep house instead of hiring help.
She has been keeping house for her family for over twenty years.
When I moved out, I had to learn how to keep house myself.

Synset Examples

He learned how to keep house after moving out.
She decided to keep house while her husband works.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to housekeep - to manage a household
  • Synonym: housework - the regular work done in a house, especially cleaning and cooking
