touch sensation

1. NOUN

/ tʌtʃ sɛnˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / tʌtʃ sɛnˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
the physical feeling experienced when something comes into contact with the skin, produced by pressure receptors.
feeling perceived by contact with skin

Examples

The soft touch sensation of velvet can be very comforting to some people.
After the injury, he lost touch sensation in his fingers for several weeks.
Some fabrics enhance the touch sensation and make clothes feel luxurious on the skin.

Synset Examples

she likes the touch of silk on her skin
the surface had a greasy feeling

Related Terms

  • Synonym: touch - the sense that allows perception of pressure on the skin
  • Synonym: feeling - an instance of physical sensation
