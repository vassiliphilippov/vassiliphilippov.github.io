armored combat vehicle

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmərd ˈkɑmbət vəˈhikəl / (AmE), / ˈɑːməd ˈkɒmbət vɪˈhiːkl / (BrE)
a military vehicle with enclosed armor and equipped with a cannon, designed for combat and moving on caterpillar treads
military vehicle with armor and cannon

Examples

The army deployed an armored combat vehicle to secure the area.
During the parade, a restored armored combat vehicle drew crowds of curious onlookers.
Advances in technology have made the modern armored combat vehicle much more effective on the battlefield.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tank - a heavily armored combat vehicle, typically with a rotating turret
