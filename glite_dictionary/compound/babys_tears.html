baby's tears

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪbiz tɪrz / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbɪz tɪəz / (BrE)
a creeping Corsican plant (Soleirolia soleirolii) with dense, moss-like mats of small round leaves, often grown as a houseplant or ground cover.
creeping plant with tiny round leaves

Examples

The baby's tears plant is perfect for covering the soil in a terrarium.
She placed a pot of baby's tears on the windowsill for a delicate touch of greenery.
After a few weeks, the baby's tears had spread to form a lush green carpet.
