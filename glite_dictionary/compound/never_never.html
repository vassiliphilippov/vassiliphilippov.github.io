Never Never

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛvɚ ˈnɛvɚ / (AmE), / ˈnɛvə ˈnɛvə / (BrE)
The remote and sparsely populated outback regions of Australia, typically considered desolate or inaccessible.
remote outback regions of Australia
british

Examples

They spent two years living in the Never Never, miles from the nearest town.
Legends and stories about the Never Never still fascinate many Australians today.
Few have ventured into the harsh environment of the Never Never without careful preparation.

Etymology

Australian English; early 19th century; likely from the notion of a place 'never never' reached or inhabited.
