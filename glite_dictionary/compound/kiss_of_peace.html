kiss of peace

1. NOUN

/ kɪs əv pis / (AmE), / kɪs əv piːs / (BrE)
In the Roman Catholic Church, a ceremonial greeting signifying Christian love and unity, exchanged during the Eucharist.
ceremonial Christian greeting in the Eucharist

Examples

During Mass, the congregation exchanged the kiss of peace as a symbol of unity.
The priest invited everyone to offer a kiss of peace to those nearby.
Historically, the kiss of peace was given as an embrace or handshake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sign of peace - gesture of greeting and peace in Christian liturgy
