toxic waste

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑksɪk weɪst / (AmE), / ˈtɒksɪk weɪst / (BrE)
waste materials that are poisonous and can cause harm to people or the environment, especially due to chemicals
poisonous waste materials

Examples

The factory was fined for illegally dumping toxic waste into the river.
Exposure to toxic waste can cause serious health problems for local residents.
Scientists are developing safer methods to dispose of toxic waste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hazardous waste - waste that poses a risk to health or the environment
  • Antonym: safe waste - waste that does not pose health or environmental risks
