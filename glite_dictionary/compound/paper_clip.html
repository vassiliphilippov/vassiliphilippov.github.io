paper clip

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpər klɪp / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə klɪp / (BrE)
a small piece of bent wire or plastic used for holding sheets of paper together
wire or plastic clip for holding papers

Examples

She attached the documents together with a paper clip.
A stray paper clip was lying on the office floor.
He straightened a paper clip to reset the printer.

Synset Examples

the paper clip was invented in 1900

Related Terms

  • Synonym: staple - a short metal wire used to fasten papers together
