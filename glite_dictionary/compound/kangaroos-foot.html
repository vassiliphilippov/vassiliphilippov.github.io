kangaroo's-foot

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæŋɡəˌruːz ˈfʊt / (AmE), / ˈkaŋɡəˌruːz ˈfʊt / (BrE)
An Australian herbaceous plant with sedgelike leaves and clustered, woolly-haired flowers, especially of the genus Anigozanthos.
Australian plant with woolly flowers

Examples

Many Australian gardens feature the striking kangaroo's-foot with its unique fuzzy flowers.
The kangaroo's-foot thrives in sandy soil and sunny locations.
She photographed a vibrant red kangaroo's-foot while hiking near Perth.

Etymology

So named because the shape of the flower clusters resembles a kangaroo's foot.
