thought-provoking puzzle

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɔt pɹoʊˌvoʊkɪŋ ˈpʌzəl / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt pɹəʊˌvəʊkɪŋ ˈpʌzəl / (BrE)
a puzzle that stimulates deep or serious thinking about a subject
puzzle that stimulates deep thinking

Examples

The magazine publishes a thought-provoking puzzle every week to challenge its readers.
She spent hours working on a particularly thought-provoking puzzle that tested her reasoning skills.
Teachers often use a thought-provoking puzzle to encourage creative problem-solving in class.

Synset Examples

As a paradoxist, he enjoyed creating thought-provoking puzzles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brain-teaser - a puzzle that tests ingenuity or knowledge
