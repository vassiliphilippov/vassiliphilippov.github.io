television set

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən sɛt / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən sɛt / (BrE)
an electronic device for receiving television signals and displaying them on a screen
electronic device displaying television broadcasts

Examples

The new television set in the living room has a huge screen and great sound quality.
He bought a used television set for his dorm room last year.
Many families gathered around the television set each evening to watch the news.

Synset Examples

the British call a tv set a ‘telly’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: television - the broadcasting of moving visual images
  • Synonym: telly - British informal term for television set
