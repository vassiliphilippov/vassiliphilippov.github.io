personal relationship

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːsənl ɹɪˈleɪʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl rɪˈleɪʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
a connection or association between two or more people, involving emotional or social interaction
emotional or social connection between people

Examples

Maintaining a healthy personal relationship requires communication and trust.
She values honesty above all in any personal relationship.
Their personal relationship blossomed after working together on several projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendship - a close and trusting relationship between friends
  • Antonym: professional relationship - a relationship based on work or career, not personal connection
