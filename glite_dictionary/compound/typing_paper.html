typing paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪpɪŋ ˌpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpɪŋ ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
thin, smooth paper designed for use with a typewriter
paper for use in a typewriter

Examples

He loaded a fresh sheet of typing paper into the old typewriter.
The secretary asked for a ream of typing paper for the office supplies.
Due to its thinness, typing paper was often used for making carbon copies.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: writing paper - paper intended for writing on
