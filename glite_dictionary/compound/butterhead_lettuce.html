butterhead lettuce

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ɚˌhɛd ˈlɛt̬ɪs / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌhɛd ˈlɛtɪs / (BrE)
A type of lettuce with soft, tender leaves that form a loose head, easily broken or bruised.
lettuce variety with soft, loose leaves

Examples

She prefers butterhead lettuce in her salads because of its tender texture.
The butterhead lettuce wilted quickly in the summer heat if not refrigerated.
We harvested several heads of butterhead lettuce from our garden this morning.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: romaine lettuce - a variety of lettuce with long, crisp leaves forming a tight head
  • Synonym: butter lettuce - another name for butterhead lettuce
