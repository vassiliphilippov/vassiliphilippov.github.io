just so

1. ADV

/ dʒʌst soʊ / (AmE), / dʒʌst səʊ / (BrE)
in a careful or precise manner; exactly as required or desired
in a careful or precise manner

Examples

Every detail on the table was arranged just so for the party.
She insists that her books be stacked just so on the shelf.
To get the best result, you need to mix the ingredients just so.

Synset Examples

you must treat this plant just so

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exactly - in a precise or correct way
  • Synonym: carefully - in a careful manner
  • Antonym: imprecisely - not in a careful or exact way
