wound healing

1. NOUN

/ wund ˈhiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / wuːnd ˈhiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the process by which the body repairs and regenerates tissue following an injury
process of tissue repair after injury
medical

Examples

A balanced diet can significantly speed up wound healing after surgery.
Researchers are developing new dressings to improve wound healing in diabetic patients.
Proper hygiene is essential for optimal wound healing.

Synset Examples

Zinc is essential for wound healing and cell growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: healing - the process of becoming healthy again; restoration after injury or illness
