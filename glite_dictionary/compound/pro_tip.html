pro tip

1. NOUN

/ proʊ tɪp / (AmE), / prəʊ tɪp / (BrE)
a helpful or useful piece of advice, especially one given by an expert or professional
expert or professional advice
informal

Examples

Let me give you a pro tip: double-check your reservation before you leave for the airport.
A quick pro tip is to create strong passwords using a mix of letters and numbers.
Here's a pro tip: if you want your steak juicy, let it rest before slicing.

Synset Examples

A pro tip for travelers is to pack light.
Here's a pro tip: always back up your files.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hint - a small piece of practical advice about how to do something better or easier
  • Synonym: pointer - a useful piece of advice or tip, especially on practical matters
