rice king

1. NOUN

/ raɪs kɪŋ / (AmE), / raɪs kɪŋ / (BrE)
a person who is extremely successful or influential in rice farming or production
a leading rice producer or farmer

Examples

His innovative techniques earned him the title of rice king in his community.
The annual banquet honored the region's rice king for record-breaking yields.
Some newspapers referred to her as the new rice king after her company's rapid growth.

Synset Examples

Everyone knew him as the rice king for his unmatched harvests.
He became the rice king of the region with his successful farm.
The festival celebrated the local rice king's achievements.

Related Terms
