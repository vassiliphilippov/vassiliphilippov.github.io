mountain nyala

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊn.tən naɪˈɑː.lə / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪn naɪˈæ.lə / (BrE)
a large, shaggy antelope native to the mountains of Ethiopia, known for its long horns and striking coat
Ethiopian mountain antelope

Examples

The mountain nyala is an endangered species native to the highlands of Ethiopia.
Researchers spent months tracking the elusive mountain nyala through dense forests.
Visitors to Bale Mountains National Park hope to catch a glimpse of the rare mountain nyala.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nyala - a spiral-horned antelope native to southern Africa

Etymology

From 'mountain' plus the local Ethiopian name 'nyala'.
