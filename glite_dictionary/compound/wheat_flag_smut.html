wheat flag smut

1. NOUN

/ wiːt flæɡ smʌt / (AmE), / wiːt flæɡ smʌt / (BrE)
a fungal disease affecting the leaves and stems of wheat, caused by the fungus Urocystis tritici, characterized by dark streaks and reduced crop yield
fungal disease of wheat

Examples

The farmer lost a significant portion of his crop to wheat flag smut last season.
Researchers are developing resistant wheat varieties to combat wheat flag smut.
Early detection of wheat flag smut can help prevent widespread damage.
