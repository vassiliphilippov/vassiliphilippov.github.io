drive-by shooting

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪv baɪ ˈʃuːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdraɪv baɪ ˈʃuːtɪŋ / (BrE)
An incident in which someone is shot from a moving vehicle, typically as it passes by the victim.
shooting from a moving vehicle

Examples

The police believe last night's drive-by shooting was gang-related.
She was an innocent bystander injured during a drive-by shooting in the city.
There has been a rise in drive-by shootings in recent months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shooting - the act of firing a gun
  • Synonym: assassination - the murder of a prominent person for political or ideological reasons
