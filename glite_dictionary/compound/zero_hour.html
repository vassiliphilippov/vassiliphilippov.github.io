zero hour

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɪər.oʊ aʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈzɪə.rəʊ aʊə / (BrE)
the exact time set for the start of a planned operation or activity
scheduled start time for an action

Examples

Everyone was tense as zero hour for the launch approached.
The team reviewed their plans carefully before zero hour struck.
At zero hour, the soldiers moved swiftly into position.

Etymology

Originated in military usage, meaning the hour set for the start of a particular operation.
