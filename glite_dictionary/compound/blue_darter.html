blue darter

1. NOUN

/ bluː ˈdɑrtər / (AmE), / bluː ˈdɑːtə / (BrE)
A bluish-grey North American hawk known for its rapid, darting flight; commonly refers to the Cooper's hawk.
species of North American hawk

Examples

The blue darter is admired by birdwatchers for its agile, swift movements through dense forests.
We spotted a blue darter perched quietly on a branch near the stream.
Farmers often see the blue darter swooping low in search of prey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cooper's hawk - A North American hawk species (Accipiter cooperii) commonly called the blue darter
