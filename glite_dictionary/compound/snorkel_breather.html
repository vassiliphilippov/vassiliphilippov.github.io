snorkel breather

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnɔrkəl ˈbriðər / (AmE), / ˈsnɔːkəl ˈbriːðə / (BrE)
a retractable device with intake and exhaust pipes that allows a submarine to remain submerged for extended periods by providing an air passage
retractable air device for submarines

Examples

The engineer inspected the snorkel breather to ensure it functioned properly before the submarine submerged.
Thanks to the snorkel breather, submarines can operate underwater for days without surfacing.
A malfunction in the snorkel breather forced the captain to bring the vessel to the surface earlier than planned.
