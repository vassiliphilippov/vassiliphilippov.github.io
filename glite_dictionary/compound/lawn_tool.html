lawn tool

1. NOUN

/ lɔn tuːl / (AmE), / lɔːn tuːl / (BrE)
a tool used for working in gardens or yards, especially for maintaining a lawn
tool for working in a yard or garden

Examples

He bought a new lawn tool to help trim the edges of his yard.
All the lawn tools are stored in the shed behind the house.
A good lawn tool makes garden work much easier and faster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garden tool - a tool for cultivating and maintaining a garden
