dancing lady orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈdænsɪŋ ˌleɪdi ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnsɪŋ ˌleɪdi ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A type of orchid of the genus Oncidium, known for its slender, branching sprays of small yellow and brown flowers that resemble dancing ladies; often grown as a houseplant.
orchid with flowers like dancing ladies

Examples

The dancing lady orchid brightened up her living room with its graceful, yellow blossoms.
Many collectors prize the dancing lady orchid for its unique, elegant flowers that look like tiny dancers in motion.
He learned to care for his dancing lady orchid by ensuring it received the right amount of light and humidity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Oncidium - genus of orchids commonly called dancing lady orchids
