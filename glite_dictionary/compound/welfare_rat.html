welfare rat

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛlfɛr ˌræt / (AmE), / ˈwɛlfeə ˌræt / (BrE)
A derogatory term for a person who takes unfair advantage of government welfare benefits, especially by deceiving the system.
person who exploits welfare benefits (derogatory)
offensive

Examples

The columnist claimed that taxes were wasted on welfare rats who manipulated the system.
Some politicians use the term welfare rat to criticize recipients accused of cheating welfare programs.
She resented being called a welfare rat because she genuinely needed assistance.

Synset Examples

Critics often label fraudsters as welfare rats in their arguments.
He referred to those who cheat the system as welfare rats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welfare queen - derogatory term for a woman who exploits welfare benefits
  • Synonym: fraudster - person who commits fraud, often on welfare systems
