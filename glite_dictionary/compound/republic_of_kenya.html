Republic of Kenya

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈkɛnjə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈkiːnjə / (BrE)
An East African country, officially called the Republic of Kenya, known for its diverse wildlife, landscapes, and as the site of important archaeological discoveries.
country in eastern Africa

Examples

The Republic of Kenya is renowned for its breathtaking national parks and diverse wildlife.
After independence, the Republic of Kenya experienced rapid urban growth, particularly in Nairobi.
Many important fossils have been unearthed in the Republic of Kenya's Great Rift Valley.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kenya - East African country, commonly known by its short name

Etymology

From the name of Mount Kenya, itself from the Kikuyu, Embu, and Kamba names for the mountain.
