carbonation system

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑr.bəˈneɪ.ʃən ˈsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˌkɑː.bəˈneɪ.ʃən ˈsɪs.təm / (BrE)
a fully automatic system designed for the high-volume carbonation of products, comprising a carbonator, CO2 infusion tube, and a holding tank with transfer pump.
automated system for product carbonation

Examples

The soft drink factory installed a new carbonation system to increase production efficiency.
Routine maintenance is essential to keep the carbonation system operating at optimal levels.
Upgrading the carbonation system helped the brewery achieve consistent bubbles in every batch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carbonator - machine that carbonates beverages
