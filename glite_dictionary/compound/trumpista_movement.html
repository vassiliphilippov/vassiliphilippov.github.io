Trumpista movement

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmpistə muˈvmənt / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpiːstə ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
A political movement of supporters and advocates of Donald Trump, typically aligned with his policies and ideology.
political movement supporting Donald Trump

Examples

The Trumpista movement has become a powerful force within the Republican Party.
Many analysts have studied how the Trumpista movement has changed American politics.
The rise of social media played a significant role in organizing the Trumpista movement.

Synset Examples

The Trumpista movement has gained significant traction among conservative voters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Trumpism - the political ideology and style associated with Donald Trump

Etymology

From 'Trump', the surname of Donald Trump, combined with the Spanish suffix '-ista' (denoting a supporter), and 'movement'.
