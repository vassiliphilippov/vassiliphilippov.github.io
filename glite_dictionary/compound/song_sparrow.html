song sparrow

1. NOUN

/ sɔŋ ˈspɛroʊ / (AmE), / sɒŋ ˈspærəʊ / (BrE)
a small North American songbird with brown streaked plumage, known for its musical song
North American songbird species

Examples

A song sparrow sang sweetly from a branch near the riverbank.
During our hike, we spotted a song sparrow gathering twigs for its nest.
The song sparrow is recognized by its distinctive, melodious call.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sparrow - a small, plump, brown and grey bird
