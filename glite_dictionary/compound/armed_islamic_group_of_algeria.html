Armed Islamic Group of Algeria

1. PROPN

/ ɑrmd ɪˈslɑmɪk ɡruːp əv ælˈdʒɪriə / (AmE), / ɑːmd ɪˈslæmɪk ɡruːp əv ælˈdʒɪəriə / (BrE)
a militant Islamist organization in Algeria, active mainly in the 1990s, aiming to overthrow the secular government and establish an Islamic state, known for violent attacks
Algerian militant Islamist group active in the 1990s

Examples

The Armed Islamic Group of Algeria was responsible for numerous attacks during the Algerian Civil War.
International organizations have listed the Armed Islamic Group of Algeria as a terrorist group.
The activities of the Armed Islamic Group of Algeria declined significantly after the late 1990s.
