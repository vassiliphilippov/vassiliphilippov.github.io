Umbundu language

1. NOUN

/ umˈbʊndu ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ʌmˈbʊndu ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken chiefly in central Angola, used by the Ovimbundu people
Bantu language of central Angola

Examples

Many people in central Angola speak Umbundu language as their first language.
The Umbundu language has a rich tradition of oral storytelling and proverbs.
Researchers are working to preserve the Umbundu language through educational programs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kimbundu language - another Bantu language spoken in Angola
  • Antonym: Angolan Portuguese - the variety of Portuguese spoken in Angola

Etymology

From the name Umbundu, the self-designation of the language and people.
