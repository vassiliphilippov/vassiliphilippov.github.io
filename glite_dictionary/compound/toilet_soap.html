toilet soap

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔɪlət soʊp / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlɪt səʊp / (BrE)
a mild soap designed for personal washing and bathing
mild soap for washing the body

Examples

She always buys a gentle toilet soap for her sensitive skin.
The hotel provided complimentary toilet soap and shampoo in each bathroom.
Dermatologists often recommend fragrance-free toilet soap to avoid skin irritation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bar soap - a solid block of soap used for cleaning
  • Antonym: liquid soap - soap in liquid form, used for washing
