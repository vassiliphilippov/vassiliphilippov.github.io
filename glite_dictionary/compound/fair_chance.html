fair chance

1. NOUN

/ fɛɚ tʃæns / (AmE), / feə tʃɑːns / (BrE)
a reasonable likelihood or probability of success or accomplishment
reasonable probability of success

Examples

Everyone who enters the competition has a fair chance of winning.
With your skills, you stand a fair chance of getting the promotion.
The applicant was pleased to know she had a fair chance at the scholarship.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: good chance - a better than average probability of success
  • Degree Lower: slim chance - a low probability of success
