boy wonder

1. NOUN

/ bɔɪ ˈwʌndɚ / (AmE), / bɔɪ ˈwʌndə / (BrE)
a young male who is extremely talented or accomplished, especially at an unusually early age
exceptionally talented young male

Examples

The young chess champion was hailed as a boy wonder after defeating the world’s best players.
Tech companies are eager to recruit the latest boy wonder from top universities.
He was considered a boy wonder in mathematics, solving complex problems by age twelve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prodigy - a young person with exceptional abilities
