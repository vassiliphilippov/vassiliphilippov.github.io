Catalina Island

1. PROPN

/ kætəˈliːnə ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / kætəˈliːnə ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
an island resort located in the Pacific Ocean off the southwestern coast of California, popular for its tourism and natural beauty
island resort off California coast

Examples

We spent our summer vacation exploring the beautiful coves of Catalina Island.
A ferry from Long Beach takes visitors to Catalina Island in just over an hour.
The clear waters around Catalina Island are perfect for snorkeling and scuba diving.

Etymology

Named after Saint Catherine (Catalina in Spanish) by Spanish explorers in the 1600s.
