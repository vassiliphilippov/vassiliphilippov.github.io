Lake Balaton

1. PROPN

/ ˈbeɪlətɒn / (AmE), / ˈbælətɒn / (BrE)
a large, shallow lake in western Hungary, known as a popular tourist destination
lake in western Hungary

Examples

The shores of Lake Balaton are lined with resorts and beaches attracting visitors each summer.
Many Hungarians spend their holidays boating and swimming in Lake Balaton.
The sunsets over Lake Balaton are renowned for their beauty.
