signal level

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪɡnəl ˌlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnəl ˌlɛvəl / (BrE)
the amplitude or strength of an electrical signal, often used in telecommunications or audio engineering
strength or amplitude of a signal

Examples

You should increase the signal level to get a clearer sound from the speakers.
Engineers monitor the signal level to ensure efficient data transmission.
If the signal level drops too low, the device might lose connection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: signal strength - the strength or power carried by an electrical signal
