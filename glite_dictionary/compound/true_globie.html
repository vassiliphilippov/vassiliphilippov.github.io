true globie

1. NOUN

/ tru ˈgloʊbi / (AmE), / tru ˈɡləʊbi / (BrE)
A person who travels extensively around the world, often visiting many countries and continents.
an avid world traveler
informal

Examples

After years of backpacking across Asia and South America, she is considered a true globie by her friends.
A true globie never lets borders or language barriers stop their adventures.
He proudly displays his passport full of stamps, clear evidence that he's a true globie.

Synset Examples

He’s a true globie, exploring every continent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: globetrotter - a person who travels widely around the world
