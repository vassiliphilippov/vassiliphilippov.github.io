theme park

1. NOUN

/ θim pɑrk / (AmE), / θiːm pɑːk / (BrE)
an amusement park where all the attractions and entertainment are based on a central theme or idea
amusement park with a central theme

Examples

The family spent their vacation at a famous theme park in Orlando.
Each theme park often features rides and shows inspired by popular movies or stories.
Many children dream of visiting a theme park full of magical lands and characters.
