agricultural agent

1. NOUN

/ ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəl ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˌaɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəl ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
an official employed by the government to advise rural residents on farming practices and home economics
government advisor on rural farming methods

Examples

The agricultural agent visited our farm to offer advice on crop rotation.
Local farmers rely on the agricultural agent for information about new pesticides and fertilizers.
As an agricultural agent, she taught families how to improve both farming efficiency and household management.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extension agent - a government advisor who provides educational outreach in agriculture and home economics
