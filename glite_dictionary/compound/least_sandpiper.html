least sandpiper

1. NOUN

/ list ˈsændˌpaɪpər / (AmE), / liːst ˈsændˌpaɪpə / (BrE)
a very small American sandpiper (Calidris minutilla), noted as the smallest shorebird in North America
smallest North American sandpiper

Examples

A flock of least sandpipers swooped over the mudflats at low tide.
The least sandpiper can be identified by its tiny size and yellow-green legs.
Many birdwatchers travel to the coast during migration season to spot the elusive least sandpiper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sandpiper - a wading bird of the family Scolopacidae, typically found on shorelines and mudflats
