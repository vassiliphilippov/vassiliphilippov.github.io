Teflon Don

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛˌflɑn dɑn / (AmE), / ˈtɛˌflɒn dɒn / (BrE)
a person, especially a leader or public figure, who consistently avoids blame or punishment despite wrongdoing or controversy
person who avoids blame or punishment
informal

Examples

After the investigation, the media started calling him the Teflon Don because no charges were brought against him.
Despite years of controversy, the mayor retained his reputation as a Teflon Don.
Her colleagues called her the Teflon Don of the office since complaints never seemed to affect her position.

Synset Examples

She described him as the Teflon Don, nothing stuck.
Despite scandals, he remained the Teflon Don of politics.
He was nicknamed the Teflon Don for avoiding all charges.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: scapegoat - person who is blamed for the wrongdoing of others
  • Synonym: untouchable - person who is immune from punishment or harm

Etymology

Derived from the nickname of American mobster John Gotti, known as 'the Teflon Don' because criminal charges against him would not stick, combined with Teflon, a brand of non-stick material.
