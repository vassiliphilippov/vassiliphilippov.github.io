total pisshead

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊt̬l ˈpɪsˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl ˈpɪsˌhɛd / (BrE)
(slang) A person who drinks excessively, especially alcohol.
a heavy drinker (slang)
slang british

Examples

Despite his age, John remains a total pisshead on the weekends.
You can't invite Sarah to the pub without her brother, who's a total pisshead.
Being a total pisshead eventually affected his work and relationships.

Synset Examples

Every party, he acts like a total pisshead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drunkard - a person who is habitually drunk
  • Antonym: teetotaller - a person who abstains completely from alcohol

Etymology

From British slang 'pisshead' (heavy drinker), where 'piss' refers to alcohol consumption; 'total' as intensifier.
