Burundi franc

1. NOUN

/ bəˈrʊndi ˌfræŋk / (AmE), / bʊˈrʊndi ˌfræŋk / (BrE)
the official currency and basic unit of money in Burundi
currency of Burundi

Examples

Goods in Bujumbura can be bought using the Burundi franc.
After exchanging her dollars, she received a bundle of Burundi francs.
The value of the Burundi franc has fluctuated significantly in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: franc - the basic monetary unit of several countries, originally France
