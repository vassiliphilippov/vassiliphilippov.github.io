oxtail soup

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːkˌsteɪl suːp / (AmE), / ˈɒksˌteɪl suːp / (BrE)
a soup made from the skinned tail of an ox or cattle, often cooked with vegetables and seasonings
soup made from ox or cattle tail

Examples

He ordered a bowl of oxtail soup as a starter at the restaurant.
Traditional oxtail soup requires several hours of slow cooking to develop its rich flavor.
On a cold day, nothing is more comforting than homemade oxtail soup.
