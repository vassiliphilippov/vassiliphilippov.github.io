shaggy cap

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃæɡi kæp / (AmE), / ˈʃaɡi kap / (BrE)
a common edible mushroom with an elongated, shaggy white cap and black spores, also known as the shaggy mane
edible mushroom with a shaggy white cap

Examples

The shaggy cap is prized by many wild food enthusiasts for its delicate flavor.
When picking mushrooms, make sure you can correctly identify the shaggy cap to avoid confusion with toxic species.
The chef sautéed fresh shaggy caps with garlic and herbs for a seasonal appetizer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shaggy mane - another name for the shaggy cap mushroom
