learned person

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɜrnɪd ˈpɜrsən / (AmE), / ˈlɜːnɪd ˈpɜːs(ə)n / (BrE)
a person who has gained scholarly knowledge or has been admitted to a field of study
a scholar or academic expert

Examples

The university honored the distinguished learned person for her groundbreaking research in physics.
It is a privilege to study under a truly learned person who is respected worldwide.
During the debate, each learned person brought a unique perspective to the discussion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scholar - a specialist in a particular branch of study, especially the humanities
  • Synonym: expert - a person who is very knowledgeable about or skillful in a particular area
