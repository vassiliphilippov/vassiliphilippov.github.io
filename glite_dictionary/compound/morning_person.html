morning person

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˌpɜrsən / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who feels most energetic and alert in the morning and prefers early hours over late nights
a person who prefers mornings

Examples

My sister is a true morning person and loves to get up before sunrise.
If you're not a morning person, waking up early can feel like a struggle.
Many morning people enjoy exercising before work.

Synset Examples

He's a morning person who wakes up early.
I'm not a morning person; I'm a night person.
I'm definitely not a morning person.
Being a morning person has its advantages.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: night owl - a person who is active and alert at night
