three-D

1. NOUN

/ ˌθriˈdi / (AmE), / ˌθriːˈdiː / (BrE)
a movie presented in a way that gives the illusion of three-dimensional depth to the images seen by the viewer
three-dimensional movie

Examples

We saw the new superhero movie in three-D and the special effects were amazing.
Many animated films are now released in both standard and three-D formats.
The kids were excited to wear their glasses for the three-D experience at the cinema.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 3D film - a film using techniques to create three-dimensional visual effects

Etymology

Short for 'three-dimensional.'
