bell foundry

1. NOUN

/ bɛl ˈfaʊndɹi / (AmE), / bɛl ˈfaʊndɹi / (BrE)
a factory or workshop where bells are manufactured by casting.
place where bells are cast

Examples

The old city is famous for its historic bell foundry, which supplied churches across Europe.
During our tour, we watched skilled craftsmen at the bell foundry pour molten metal into molds.
The cathedral’s massive bells were cast at a renowned bell foundry in the 18th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foundry - a workshop or factory for casting metal
