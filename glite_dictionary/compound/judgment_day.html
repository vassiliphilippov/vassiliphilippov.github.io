Judgment Day

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌdʒ.mənt deɪ / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌdʒ.mənt deɪ / (BrE)
In Christian belief, the day at the end of the world when God will judge the actions of all people and determine their eternal destinies.
day God judges all people (Christianity)

Examples

Many people believe that on Judgment Day, everyone will be held accountable for their actions.
The preacher warned his congregation to prepare for Judgment Day and live righteous lives.
Stories about Judgment Day appear in literature and art throughout history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doomsday - the day of the final judgment by God
  • Synonym: resurrection - the rising of the dead at the end of the world (Christian belief)
