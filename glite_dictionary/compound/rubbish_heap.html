rubbish heap

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌbɪʃ hiːp / (AmE), / ˈrʌbɪʃ hiːp / (BrE)
a pile or accumulation of waste materials and discarded items
pile of waste materials

Examples

The old car was left to rust in a rubbish heap behind the factory.
Children searched the rubbish heap for anything they could reuse or sell.
After cleaning the garage, we ended up with a huge rubbish heap in the backyard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garbage heap - a heap or pile of garbage; synonym
  • Synonym: dump - a place for disposing of refuse; broader synonym
