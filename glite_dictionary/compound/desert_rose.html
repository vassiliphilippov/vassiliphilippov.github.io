desert rose

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛzɚt roʊz / (AmE), / ˈdɛzət rəʊz / (BrE)
a South African succulent shrub (Adenium obesum) with a swollen stem and showy pink and white flowers, often grown as a tropical ornamental plant
ornamental succulent shrub with pink and white flowers

Examples

The desert rose is prized for its vibrant blossoms and unique swollen stem.
After months of dryness, the desert rose finally produced its splendid white and pink flowers.
Many gardeners cultivate the desert rose as an ornamental plant in warm climates.
