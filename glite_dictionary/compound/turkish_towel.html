Turkish towel

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝːkɪʃ ˈtaʊəl / (AmE), / ˈtɜːkɪʃ ˈtaʊəl / (BrE)
A thick, absorbent bath towel with a rough, loose pile, originally made in Turkey.
thick, absorbent bath towel with loose pile

Examples

After her shower, Maria wrapped herself in a soft Turkish towel.
The hotel bathroom was stocked with large, fluffy Turkish towels.
Some people prefer a Turkish towel for its extra absorbency and comfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bath towel - a large towel used for drying the body after bathing

Etymology

From the association with towels traditionally made in Turkey, known for their quality and distinctive weave.
