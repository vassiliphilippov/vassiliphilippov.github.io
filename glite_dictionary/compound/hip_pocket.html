hip pocket

1. NOUN

/ hɪp ˈpɑk.ɪt / (AmE), / hɪp ˈpɒk.ɪt / (BrE)
a pocket located on the back of a pair of trousers, typically over the hip area
rear pocket of trousers

Examples

He quickly slipped his wallet into his hip pocket before leaving the house.
The receipt was crumpled in the hip pocket of his jeans.
She always checks her hip pocket for her phone when she stands up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: back pocket - a pocket on the rear side of trousers or pants

2. NOUN

/ hɪp ˈpɑkɪt / (AmE), / hɪp ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
A pocket located on or near the hip; also, something kept secret or in reserve for later use.
pocket on the hip; also, something kept in reserve

Examples

She kept her credit card in her hip pocket for easy access.
The team had a new strategy in their hip pocket just in case.
He reached into his hip pocket and produced a folded piece of paper.

Synset Examples

She pulled out her phone from her hip pocket.
