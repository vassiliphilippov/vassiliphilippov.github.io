rocket range

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑkɪt reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪt reɪndʒ / (BrE)
an area or facility where rocket missiles are tested or launched under controlled conditions
site for rocket missile testing/launching

Examples

The military tested the new missile at a remote rocket range in the desert.
Strict safety protocols must be followed when working at a rocket range.
Satellite launches are often conducted from a highly secure rocket range.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firing range - an area for testing weapons by firing projectiles
