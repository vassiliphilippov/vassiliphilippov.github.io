fire bell

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚ bɛl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə bɛl / (BrE)
a bell that is rung to alert people to the presence of a fire
bell used as fire alarm

Examples

The sudden ringing of the fire bell sent everyone rushing out of the building.
When the fire bell sounded, the firefighters sprang into action immediately.
They held a drill to ensure everyone knew what to do when the fire bell rang.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alarm bell - a bell used to warn of danger or summon people to action
