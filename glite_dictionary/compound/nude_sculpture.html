nude sculpture

1. NOUN

/ nud ˈskʌlptʃɚ / (AmE), / njuːd ˈskʌlptʃə / (BrE)
A statue representing a naked human figure, especially as an artistic subject.
statue of a naked human figure

Examples

The museum features a famous nude sculpture created during the Renaissance period.
Some visitors admired the grace and realism of the nude sculpture in the park.
He studied anatomy to improve his ability to create nude sculptures for his art class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: statue - a carved or cast figure of a person or animal, especially one that is life-size or larger
