friends with benefits

1. NOUN

/ frɛndz wɪð ˈbɛnɪfɪts / (AmE), / frɛndz wɪð ˈbɛnɪfɪts / (BrE)
two friends who have a sexual relationship without being in a romantic partnership
friends having a non-romantic sexual relationship
informal

Examples

After their breakups, they decided to become friends with benefits rather than start a new relationship.
She explained to her roommate that she and Alex were just friends with benefits.
Being friends with benefits worked for them, as neither wanted anything serious.

Synset Examples

Their relationship as friends with benefits worked for both.
They agreed to be friends with benefits, keeping things casual.
She clarified they were just friends with benefits, nothing more.
