local hero

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.kəl ˈhɪəɹ.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl ˈhɪə.rəʊ / (BrE)
a person admired for their outstanding achievements or good deeds within their own community
admired community member

Examples

The firefighter became a local hero after saving several families from the burning building.
She is considered a local hero for the work she has done with underprivileged children.
After the flood, many volunteers emerged as local heroes in their town.

Synset Examples

He’s a legend, or should I say, a local hero.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hero - a person admired for courage or noble qualities
