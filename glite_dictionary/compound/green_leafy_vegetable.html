green leafy vegetable

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin ˈlifi ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈliːfi ˈvɛdʒtəbl / (BrE)
a vegetable with edible green leaves, such as spinach, kale, or lettuce, often valued for its nutritional content
vegetable with edible green leaves

Examples

It's important to include at least one green leafy vegetable in your daily meals for essential nutrients.
Spinach is a popular green leafy vegetable often used in salads and smoothies.
Doctors recommend eating a green leafy vegetable with every meal to help maintain good health.

Synset Examples

Adding more green leafy vegetables increases your vitamin G intake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spinach - an edible green leafy plant, often used in salads or cooking
  • Synonym: kale - a hardy cabbage with curled, often dark green leaves, eaten as a vegetable
