shipbuilding industry

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪpˌbɪldɪŋ ˌɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpˌbɪldɪŋ ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
the industry concerned with the construction of ships.
industry building ships

Examples

The shipbuilding industry has a long tradition in coastal cities around the world.
Government policies can greatly affect the health of the shipbuilding industry.
Declining demand for new vessels has led to layoffs in the shipbuilding industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shipbuilding - the activity or business of constructing ships
  • Degree Higher: shipping industry - the business involved with transporting goods by ship
