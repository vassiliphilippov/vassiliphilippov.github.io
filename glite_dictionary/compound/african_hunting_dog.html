African hunting dog

1. NOUN

/ ˌæfrɪkən ˈhʌntɪŋ dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˌæfrɪkən ˈhʌntɪŋ dɒɡ / (BrE)
a large, highly social wild canid native to southern and eastern Africa, known for hunting in packs
wild African dog that hunts in packs

Examples

The African hunting dog is known for its intricate social structure within the pack.
Researchers have observed African hunting dogs coordinating their hunts to catch larger prey.
Due to habitat loss, the population of the African hunting dog has declined significantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: painted wolf - alternate name for the African hunting dog
  • Synonym: wild dog - general term for undomesticated canids, sometimes used to refer to the African hunting dog
