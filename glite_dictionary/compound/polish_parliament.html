Polish parliament

1. PROPN

/ ˈpoʊ.lɪʃ ˈpɑr.lə.mənt / (AmE), / ˈpɒ.lɪʃ ˈpɑː.lɪ.m(ə)nt / (BrE)
the legislative body of Poland, consisting of the lower house (Sejm) and the upper house (Senate) responsible for making national laws
legislative body of Poland

Examples

The Polish parliament is considering new economic policies this session.
During the crisis, the Polish parliament passed emergency legislation to support citizens.
Journalists gathered outside the Polish parliament to cover the important announcement.
