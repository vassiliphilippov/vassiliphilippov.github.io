media whore

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːdiə hɔɚ / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiə hɔː / (BrE)
a person who seeks out media attention obsessively or indiscriminately, often for self-promotion
person who excessively seeks media attention
slang vulgar

Examples

Many think he's a media whore because he always shows up at events with TV cameras.
Some celebrities are unfairly called media whores just for attending public functions.
She admitted to being a bit of a media whore during her early career.

Synset Examples

His antics make him a media whore, craving the spotlight.
She’s known as a media whore, always in front of the cameras.
Critics labeled him a media whore for constantly seeking attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: publicity seeker - a person who seeks public attention
  • Antonym: wallflower - a person who avoids the limelight
