silver mine

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ ˌmaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˌmaɪn / (BrE)
a mine from which silver ore is extracted
a mine extracting silver ore

Examples

The town flourished when a rich silver mine was discovered nearby.
Investors poured money into reopening the abandoned silver mine.
Working in a silver mine was considered dangerous but lucrative work in the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gold mine - a mine where gold ore is extracted
  • Synonym: mine - an excavation from which minerals and ores are extracted
