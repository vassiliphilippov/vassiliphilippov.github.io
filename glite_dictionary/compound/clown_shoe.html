clown shoe

1. NOUN

/ klaʊn ʃu / (AmE), / klaʊn ʃuː / (BrE)
an oversized shoe, typically worn by clowns as part of a costume for comedic effect
large, comic footwear worn by clowns

Examples

The performer tripped over his enormous clown shoe and the audience burst out laughing.
She found an old clown shoe at the costume shop for her circus-themed party.
His bright red clown shoe stood out on the parade float.

Synset Examples

She wore a clown shoe costume for the party.
The oversized clown shoe made walking difficult.
