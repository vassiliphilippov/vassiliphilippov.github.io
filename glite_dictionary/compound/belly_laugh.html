belly laugh

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛli læf / (AmE), / ˈbɛli lɑːf / (BrE)
a loud, deep, and hearty burst of laughter
loud, hearty laughter

Examples

The comedian's joke triggered a belly laugh from the entire audience.
She let out a belly laugh when she saw the surprise party decorations.
His stories always end in a hearty belly laugh that fills the room.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: giggle - a light, silly laugh
  • Degree Lower: chuckle - a quiet or suppressed laugh
  • Synonym: roar - a loud burst of laughter
