happy dispatch

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæpi dɪˈspætʃ / (AmE), / ˈhæpi dɪˈspætʃ / (BrE)
a message or report conveying good news; positive communication
a message with good news

Examples

The director delivered a happy dispatch to the staff about the company's performance.
After weeks of waiting, they finally received a happy dispatch from their overseas partners.
It was a rare pleasure to hear a happy dispatch in the morning briefing.

Synset Examples

We received a happy dispatch from the client today.
The team celebrated a happy dispatch of the new products.
His report was a happy dispatch, full of good news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good news - favorable or positive information or communication
