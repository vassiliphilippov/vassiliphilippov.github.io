nanny-goat

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæni ˌɡoʊt / (AmE), / ˈnæni ˌɡəʊt / (BrE)
a female goat
female goat

Examples

The farmer kept a nanny-goat in the barn for fresh milk every morning.
Their children love feeding the nanny-goat apples and carrots at the petting zoo.
A nanny-goat can be quite protective of her kids if she senses danger nearby.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: billy-goat - male goat
  • Degree Lower: goat - domesticated animal with horns and a beard

Etymology

From 'nanny', a child's word for a female animal, combined with 'goat'.
