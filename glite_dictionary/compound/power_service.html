power service

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊ.ɚ ˌsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə ˌsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
A public utility that supplies electricity to homes and businesses.
utility providing electricity

Examples

The local power service was restored after the storm knocked out electricity for hours.
If you move to a new apartment, you need to arrange for power service before you arrive.
Due to scheduled maintenance by the power service, electricity will be temporarily unavailable tomorrow morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electric utility - a company that generates and supplies electricity
