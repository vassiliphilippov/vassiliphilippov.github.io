lip fern

1. NOUN

/ lɪp fɜrn / (AmE), / lɪp fɜːn / (BrE)
Any of various terrestrial ferns of the genus Cheilanthes, found in arid and semiarid temperate or tropical regions worldwide.
a fern of the genus Cheilanthes

Examples

Several species of lip fern thrive in the rocky outcrops of the southwestern desert.
The delicate fronds of the lip fern distinguish it from other ferns found in similar arid climates.
He added a lip fern to his growing collection of drought-resistant plants.
