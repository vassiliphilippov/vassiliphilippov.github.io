genus Diphylla

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs dɪˈfɪl.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs dɪˈfɪl.ə / (BrE)
A genus of vampire bats, which includes species known for feeding on blood.
genus of vampire bats

Examples

The genus Diphylla is native to parts of Central and South America.
Scientists study the genus Diphylla to learn more about the feeding behavior of vampire bats.
Only one living species is currently classified under the genus Diphylla.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vampire bat - a bat that feeds on the blood of animals

Etymology

From Neo-Latin 'Diphylla', the designated genus name in zoological taxonomy.
