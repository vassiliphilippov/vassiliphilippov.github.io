unhandled case

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈhændəld keɪs / (AmE), / ʌnˈhænd(ə)ld keɪs / (BrE)
a situation or problem that has not been addressed, dealt with, or resolved
situation not dealt with or resolved

Examples

The software crashed due to an unhandled case in the input data.
During the investigation, every unhandled case was reviewed by the team.
Leaving an unhandled case can lead to unexpected problems later.

Synset Examples

There were several unhandled cases in the report.
The project suffered from many unhandled issues.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: resolved case - a situation or issue that has been dealt with and concluded
