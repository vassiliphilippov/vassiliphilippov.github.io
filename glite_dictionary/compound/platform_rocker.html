platform rocker

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæt.fɔrm ˈrɑː.kɚ / (AmE), / ˈplætfɔːm ˈrɒk.ə / (BrE)
a rocking chair mounted on a stationary platform base, allowing it to rock smoothly without the entire chair moving across the floor
rocking chair on a stationary base

Examples

He inherited his grandmother's antique platform rocker and placed it by the fireplace.
The platform rocker was designed to keep the floor from being scratched.
She spent the afternoon reading in the comfortable platform rocker on the porch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rocking chair - a chair mounted on rockers, allowing it to rock back and forth
