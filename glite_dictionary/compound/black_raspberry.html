black raspberry

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈræzˌbɛri / (AmE), / blæk ˈræzˌbəri / (BrE)
A species of raspberry native to eastern North America, with black, thimble-shaped fruit.
a North American raspberry with black fruit

Examples

I picked a bowl of fresh black raspberry from the bushes this morning.
The ice cream shop offers a delicious black raspberry flavor in the summer.
Black raspberry jam is my favorite topping for toast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raspberry - a small, soft red or black fruit
  • Antonym: red raspberry - raspberry with red fruit
