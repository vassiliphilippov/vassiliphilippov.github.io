line of destiny

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪn əv ˈdɛstəni / (AmE), / ˈlaɪn əv ˈdɛstəni / (BrE)
a crease on the palm of the hand, believed by palmists to indicate how successful a person will be in life
palmistry crease indicating success

Examples

The palmist traced the line of destiny on her palm and predicted great achievements ahead.
He noticed that his line of destiny was unusually long compared to others'.
According to traditional beliefs, a faint line of destiny may suggest a lack of clear goals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fate line - a crease on the palm associated with fate or life path in palmistry
