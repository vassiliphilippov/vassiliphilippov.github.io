restraint of trade

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈstreɪnt əv treɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈstreɪnt əv treɪd / (BrE)
an act or policy that restricts or prevents free competition in business or commerce
restriction of free competition in business
legal

Examples

The new legislation aims to prevent any restraint of trade by large corporations.
Several companies were sued for engaging in a restraint of trade by fixing prices.
Courts will determine if a contract constitutes an illegal restraint of trade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monopoly - exclusive control of a commodity or service in a market
  • Antonym: competition - rivalry between businesses seeking the same customers
