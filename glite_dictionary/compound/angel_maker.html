angel maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪndʒəl ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəl ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who brings kindness, joy, or beauty to others, often by their actions or influence
person who brings joy or kindness to others

He was called an angel maker for spreading hope and happiness throughout the hospital.
