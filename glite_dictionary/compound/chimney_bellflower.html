chimney bellflower

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪmni ˈbɛlˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪmni ˈbɛlˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A bellflower native to southeastern Europe, known for its tall stems and bell-shaped flowers.
bellflower of southeastern Europe

Examples

The chimney bellflower adds vibrant color to rock gardens throughout the summer.
Gardeners often plant chimney bellflower for its striking blue or purple blossoms.
Several chimney bellflowers were growing along the old stone wall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bellflower - any plant of the genus Campanula with bell-shaped flowers
