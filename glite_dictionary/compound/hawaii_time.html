Hawaii Time

1. NOUN

/ həˈwaɪ.i taɪm / (AmE), / həˈwaɪ.i taɪm / (BrE)
the standard time at the 10th time zone west of Greenwich, used in Hawaii and the western Aleutian Islands
standard time zone for Hawaii

Examples

The meeting will start at 8:00 Hawaii Time, so please adjust your schedule accordingly.
When visiting Honolulu, remember to set your watch to Hawaii Time.
Flights from Los Angeles to Maui typically arrive on Hawaii Time, which is two hours behind Pacific Time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hawaii-Aleutian Time - standard time zone observed in Hawaii and the Aleutian Islands
