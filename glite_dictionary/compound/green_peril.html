green peril

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin ˈpɛrəl / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈpɛrəl / (BrE)
the threat to nature and biodiversity from factors such as invasive species and environmental degradation
threat to nature/biodiversity

Examples

The green peril caused by invasive plants is devastating local agriculture.
Many conservationists warn about the green peril threatening native fauna.
International cooperation is needed to tackle the green peril in sensitive habitats.

Synset Examples

The documentary highlighted the green peril affecting wildlife habitats.
The green peril of invasive species threatens local ecosystems.
They discussed the green peril facing the biodiversity of the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invasive species - non-native species that threaten ecosystems
  • Synonym: environmental threat - danger or harm to the natural environment
