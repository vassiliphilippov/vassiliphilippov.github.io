swimming meet

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪmɪŋ miːt / (AmE), / ˈswɪmɪŋ miːt / (BrE)
an organized event where swimmers compete against each other, typically between teams or clubs
swimming competition between teams

Examples

Our school will host the regional swimming meet next weekend.
She set a new record at the last swimming meet of the season.
Preparing for the swimming meet, the athletes practiced every morning before class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimming competition - an event where swimmers compete
