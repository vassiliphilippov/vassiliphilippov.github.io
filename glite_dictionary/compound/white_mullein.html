white mullein

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈmʌlɪn / (AmE), / waɪt ˈmʌlɪn / (BrE)
a Eurasian herb (Verbascum lychnitis) with dense hairs and racemose white flowers, now also found in North America
a Eurasian herb with white racemose flowers

Examples

We found a tall white mullein growing at the edge of the meadow.
The white mullein is easily recognized by its dense, hairy leaves and raceme of white flowers.
Gardeners sometimes plant white mullein for its ornamental value and to attract pollinators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mullein - a group of Eurasian herbs in the genus Verbascum
