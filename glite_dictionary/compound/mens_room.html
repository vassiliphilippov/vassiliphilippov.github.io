men's room

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛnz ruːm / (AmE), / ˈmenz ruːm / (BrE)
a public toilet for use by men only
public toilet for men

Examples

Excuse me, could you tell me where the men's room is located?
He stepped into the men's room to wash his hands before dinner.
The sign on the door clearly marked it as the men's room.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ladies' room - a public toilet for use by women only
  • Synonym: restroom - a room with a toilet, especially in a public place
  • Synonym: toilet - a room containing a toilet
