white man

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪt mæn / (AmE), / ˈwaɪt mæn / (BrE)
a man who belongs to the White (Caucasian) ethnic group, typically with light skin
a man of the White ethnic group

Examples

The white man at the counter helped me with my purchase.
Many novels from the 19th century featured a white man as the protagonist.
She said she felt ignored in a room full of white men.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: black man - a man who belongs to the Black ethnic group
  • Synonym: white person - a person (male or female) who is White
