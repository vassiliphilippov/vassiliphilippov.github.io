date palm

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪt ˌpɑm / (AmE), / ˈdeɪt ˌpɑːm / (BrE)
A tall tropical palm tree native to the Middle East and North Africa, known for producing sweet edible dates.
tall palm tree producing edible dates

Examples

The date palm thrives in hot, arid regions such as parts of the Middle East and North Africa.
Farmers planted several date palms along the oasis to provide both shade and fruit.
Harvesting the fruit of the date palm is an important agricultural activity in many desert areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: date - the sweet edible fruit produced by the date palm
