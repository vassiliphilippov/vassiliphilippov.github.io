publishing house

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ haʊs / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ haʊs / (BrE)
A company or firm that publishes books, journals, or other works for sale or distribution.
company that publishes books or similar works

Examples

She submitted her novel to a renowned publishing house but is still waiting for a response.
The publishing house announced a new series of science fiction books next spring.
Working at a publishing house gave him insight into the book industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: publisher - a person or company that prepares and issues books, journals, or other material for sale
