disposal plant

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈspoʊzəl plænt / (AmE), / dɪˈspəʊzəl plɑːnt / (BrE)
a facility where sewage or waste is treated and disposed of
facility for treating and disposing of waste

Examples

The city built a new disposal plant to handle the increasing amount of sewage.
Strict regulations ensure that every disposal plant minimizes its environmental impact.
Workers at the disposal plant monitor the processes to ensure proper waste treatment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sewage treatment plant - facility for treating sewage
