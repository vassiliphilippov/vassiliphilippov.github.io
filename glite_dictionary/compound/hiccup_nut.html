hiccup nut

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪkˌʌp nʌt / (AmE), / ˈhɪkʌp nʌt / (BrE)
an ornamental African shrub or climber with red flowers, belonging to the genus Combretum
African ornamental shrub or climber

Examples

The hiccup nut is prized for its vivid red blossoms in tropical gardens.
Many gardeners grow the hiccup nut as an ornamental plant in warm climates.
The climbing nature of the hiccup nut makes it ideal for covering fences and trellises.
