unabridged dictionary

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.əˈbrɪdʒd ˈdɪk.ʃəˌner.i / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈbrɪdʒd ˈdɪk.ʃən.ər.i / (BrE)
A dictionary that contains the full list of words and definitions, without any omissions or shortening; a comprehensive dictionary.
a complete, comprehensive dictionary

Examples

She prefers using an unabridged dictionary when looking up rare words.
The library's reference section houses a giant unabridged dictionary for scholars to consult.
Unlike pocket versions, an unabridged dictionary includes detailed definitions and archaic terms.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: abridged dictionary - a dictionary with some content omitted to make it shorter

Etymology

From 'unabridged' (not shortened) + 'dictionary.'
