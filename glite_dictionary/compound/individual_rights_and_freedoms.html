individual rights and freedoms

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪndəˈvɪdʒuəl raɪts ənd ˈfriːdəmz / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəl raɪts ənd ˈfriːdəmz / (BrE)
the basic liberties and entitlements belonging to each person, especially as protected by law or principles of justice
basic personal liberties and entitlements

Examples

Protecting individual rights and freedoms is a fundamental principle in democratic societies.
The constitution guarantees several individual rights and freedoms to all citizens.
He advocates for policies that strengthen individual rights and freedoms in the workplace.

Synset Examples

Libertopianism promotes individual rights and freedoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: civil liberties - fundamental individual rights protected by law from interference by the government
