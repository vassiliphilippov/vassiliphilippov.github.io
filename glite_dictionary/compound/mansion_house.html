mansion house

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænʃən ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈmænʃən ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A large, impressive, and often stately house, typically serving as the residence of a wealthy person or family.
large, impressive residence

Examples

The old family mansion house stood at the end of a tree-lined drive.
They renovated the mansion house to serve as a museum.
Many historical mansion houses are open to visitors during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mansion - a large, impressive house
