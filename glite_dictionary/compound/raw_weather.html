raw weather

1. NOUN

/ rɔː ˈwɛðɚ / (AmE), / rɔː ˈwɛðə / (BrE)
cold and damp weather that is unpleasant to be outside in
unpleasantly cold and damp weather

Examples

We decided to postpone the hike because of the raw weather outside.
Nothing is more discouraging than having to work in raw weather all day long.
The children shivered in the raw weather, clutching their coats tightly around them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inclement weather - bad or unpleasant weather
  • Antonym: mild weather - weather that is moderate and not extreme
