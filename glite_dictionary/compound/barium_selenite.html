barium selenite

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛəriəm səˈliːnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈbeəriəm sɪˈliːnaɪt / (BrE)
an inorganic compound that is the selenite salt of barium, with the formula BaSeO₃
a chemical compound, BaSeO₃
medical

Examples

Barium selenite is highly toxic and may be fatal if inhaled, swallowed or absorbed through skin.
Laboratory staff must handle barium selenite with extreme caution because of its toxicity.
Exposure to barium selenite should be avoided, as it can pose serious health risks.

Synset Examples

Barium selenite is highly toxic and may be fatal if inhaled, swallowed or absorbed through skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barium sulfate - a compound with the formula BaSO₄, used as a radiographic contrast agent
  • Synonym: selenite - a salt or ester of selenous acid

Etymology

From 'barium', a chemical element, and 'selenite' (salt of selenous acid), reflecting its composition.
