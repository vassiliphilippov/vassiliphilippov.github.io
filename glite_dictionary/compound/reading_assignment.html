reading assignment

1. NOUN

/ ˈɹiːdɪŋ əˌsaɪnmənt / (AmE), / ˈɹiːdɪŋ əˌsaɪnmənt / (BrE)
A task given by a teacher that requires reading a specific passage or text.
task to read assigned material

Examples

Our history teacher gave us a reading assignment over the weekend.
She finished her reading assignment before watching television.
Each student must be prepared to discuss the reading assignment in class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assignment - a task or piece of work assigned
