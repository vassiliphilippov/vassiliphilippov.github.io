night watch

1. NOUN

/ naɪt wɑtʃ / (AmE), / naɪt wɒtʃ / (BrE)
a period of guard duty or surveillance carried out during the night, especially between midnight and early morning.
period of guard duty at night

Examples

The security team's night watch begins at midnight and lasts until sunrise.
During the night watch, sailors took turns keeping an eye on the horizon for danger.
He volunteered for the night watch to let his crewmates rest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watch - a period of observation or surveillance, especially by guards or sentries
  • Antonym: day watch - a period of guard duty during the daytime
