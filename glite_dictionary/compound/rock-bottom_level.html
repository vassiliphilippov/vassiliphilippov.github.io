rock-bottom level

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɑk ˈbɑtəm ˈlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˌrɒk ˈbɒtəm ˈlɛvəl / (BrE)
the lowest possible point or degree of something
the lowest possible level

Examples

After losing his job, his confidence sank to a rock-bottom level.
Consumer trust in the company reached a rock-bottom level after the scandal.
The team’s morale hit a rock-bottom level following its seventh consecutive defeat.

Synset Examples

The stocks have reached rock-bottom levels this month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lowest point - the lowest or worst point of something
