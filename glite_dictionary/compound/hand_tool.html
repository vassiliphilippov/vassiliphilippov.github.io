hand tool

1. NOUN

/ hænd tuːl / (AmE), / hænd tuːl / (BrE)
a tool designed to be used by hand, rather than powered by electricity or other means
tool operated by hand

Examples

The carpenter carried several hand tools in his toolbox.
Proper maintenance of your hand tools will make them last much longer.
She used a variety of hand tools to complete the gardening project.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: power tool - tool powered by electricity or another power source
