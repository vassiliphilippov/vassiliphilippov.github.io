inner beast

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnɚ biːst / (AmE), / ˈɪnə biːst / (BrE)
a person's primal instincts or aggressive nature, especially when typically kept under control
primal or aggressive instincts

Examples

During the heated debate, she struggled to keep her inner beast in check.
The novel explores the protagonist's battle with his inner beast as he faces moral dilemmas.
Yoga helps many people tame their inner beast and find inner peace.

Synset Examples

He unleashed his inner beast during the championship game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: animal instincts - basic, primal drives in humans
  • Antonym: self-control - the ability to restrain one's impulses
