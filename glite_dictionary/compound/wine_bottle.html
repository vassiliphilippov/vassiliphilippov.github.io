wine bottle

1. NOUN

/ waɪn ˈbɑːt̬l / (AmE), / waɪn ˈbɒtəl / (BrE)
a glass bottle used for holding and serving wine
a bottle for holding wine

Examples

She placed the wine bottle on the table to let it breathe before dinner.
Please open the wine bottle and pour everyone a glass.
The label on this wine bottle says it was made in France.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bottle - container with a narrow neck for storing liquids
  • Degree Lower: wine glass - glass used to drink wine
