Japanese barberry

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈpæniz bɑːrˈbɛri / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæn.iːz ˈbɑː.bər.i / (BrE)
a compact deciduous shrub (Berberis thunbergii) with small leaves and persistent red berries, widely grown for hedges and landscaping
a shrub with red berries used in hedges

Examples

The Japanese barberry in our garden provides bright color in the fall.
Many landscapers recommend Japanese barberry for its hardiness and attractive shape.
Birds are often drawn to the red berries of the Japanese barberry.
