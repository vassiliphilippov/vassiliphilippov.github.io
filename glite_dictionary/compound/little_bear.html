Little Bear

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪt̬əl bɛr / (AmE), / ˈlɪtəl beə / (BrE)
a constellation in the northern sky, also known as Ursa Minor or the Little Dipper, which contains the North Star
constellation containing the North Star

Examples

Polaris, the North Star, is located in the constellation Little Bear.
Many amateur astronomers learn to find the Little Bear before searching for other stars.
Legends about the Little Bear can be found in various cultures around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ursa Minor - the constellation also known as the Little Bear or Little Dipper
  • Synonym: Little Dipper - the asterism within Ursa Minor (Little Bear)

Etymology

Refers to the Latin name 'Ursa Minor', meaning 'smaller bear'.
