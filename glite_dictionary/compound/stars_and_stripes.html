Stars and Stripes

1. NOUN

/ stɑrz ənd straɪps / (AmE), / stɑːz ənd straɪps / (BrE)
the national flag of the United States of America, characterized by its stars and horizontal stripes
the US national flag

Examples

The Stars and Stripes was raised over the White House to mark the national holiday.
She saluted the Stars and Stripes as it passed in the parade.
Veterans stood proudly beside the Stars and Stripes during the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Old Glory - an informal name for the flag of the United States

Etymology

Refers to the design of the US flag featuring stars (representing states) and stripes (representing the original colonies).
