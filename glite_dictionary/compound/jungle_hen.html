jungle hen

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌŋɡəl hɛn / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋɡl hɛn / (BrE)
The female of the jungle fowl, the wild bird species ancestral to the domestic chicken.
female jungle fowl

Examples

The jungle hen builds her nest on the forest floor among the dense undergrowth.
Researchers observed a jungle hen caring for her chicks in the wild.
Unlike the domestic chicken, a jungle hen is much more wary and elusive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jungle fowl - wild bird species ancestral to domestic chickens
