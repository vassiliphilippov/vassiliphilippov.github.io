babysitter test

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.biˌsɪt.ɚ tɛst / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.biˌsɪt.ə tɛst / (BrE)
a situation or scenario designed to assess a person's ability to care for children, especially under challenging circumstances
test of childcare skills

Examples

The interview included a babysitter test where she dealt with a crying child.
Passing the babysitter test proved he was ready for more responsibility.
Some parents create a babysitter test to see how prospective sitters handle emergencies.

Synset Examples

The babysitter test involved handling a tantrum smoothly.
Her patience was put to the babysitter test with three toddlers.
