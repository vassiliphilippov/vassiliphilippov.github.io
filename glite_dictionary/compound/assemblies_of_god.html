Assemblies of God

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɛmbəliz əv ɡɑd / (AmE), / əˈsɛmbəliz əv ɡɒd / (BrE)
a Pentecostal Christian denomination known for its charismatic worship and worldwide missionary work, originating in the United States
Pentecostal Christian denomination

Examples

The Assemblies of God has thousands of churches throughout the United States and around the world.
She grew up attending services at her local Assemblies of God church every Sunday.
The Assemblies of God places a strong emphasis on missionary outreach and spiritual gifts.
