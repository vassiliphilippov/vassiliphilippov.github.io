snot-nosed brat

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnɑt ˌnoʊzd bræt / (AmE), / ˈsnɒt ˌnəʊzd bræt / (BrE)
an annoying, impudent, or disrespectful child, often used as an insult
annoying and disrespectful child (insult)
informal

Examples

The teacher was growing tired of that snot-nosed brat who always disrupted the class.
Don't let that snot-nosed brat near the cookies; he'll eat them all.
She refused to babysit for her cousin again because their son was a real snot-nosed brat.

Synset Examples

I can't stand that snot-nosed brat; he never listens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brat - an annoying or badly behaved child
