snowdrop anemone

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊˌdrɑp əˈnɛməni / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌdrɒp əˈnɛməni / (BrE)
A Eurasian herbaceous plant with solitary, nodding, fragrant white flowers, commonly cultivated as an ornamental.
herbaceous plant with white flowers

Examples

The snowdrop anemone blooms early in the spring, adding a delicate touch to the garden.
A cluster of snowdrop anemones grew beneath the old oak tree, brightening the shady spot.
Gardeners prize the snowdrop anemone for its graceful white flowers and easy care.
