retro look

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛtroʊ lʊk / (AmE), / ˈrɛtrəʊ lʊk / (BrE)
a style of dress, decoration, or design that imitates or is inspired by fashions of the past
style imitating past fashions

Examples

The actress turned heads on the red carpet with her glamorous retro look.
I love how your living room has a retro look with all those mid-century modern pieces.
He adopted a retro look by wearing bell-bottom jeans and a psychedelic shirt.

Synset Examples

He admired her retro look, complete with a spit curl.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vintage - style or item from a previous era
  • Antonym: modern look - style reflecting current trends or fashions

Etymology

From 'retro', meaning 'in imitation of a style from the recent past', and 'look', meaning 'appearance' or 'style'.
