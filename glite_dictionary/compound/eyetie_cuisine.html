Eyetie cuisine

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪtaɪ kwɪˈzin / (AmE), / ˈaɪtaɪ kwɪˈziːn / (BrE)
(offensive, slang) Italian cuisine; food characteristic of Italy or Italian people.
offensive slang for Italian cuisine
slang offensive

Examples

The small café specializes in homemade Eyetie cuisine from traditional recipes.
Many people gathered to sample the Eyetie cuisine at the annual street festival.
She claimed her grandmother's Eyetie cuisine was the best in town, especially the pasta dishes.

Synset Examples

The restaurant was famous for its Eyetie cuisine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Italian cuisine - cuisine characteristic of Italy

Etymology

Corruption of 'Italian', with 'Eyetie' as a derogatory rendering of 'I.T.' (for 'Italian').
