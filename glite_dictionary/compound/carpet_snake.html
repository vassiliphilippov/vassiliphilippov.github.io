carpet snake

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːrpɪt ˌsneɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpɪt ˌsneɪk / (BrE)
An Australian python with a variegated pattern on its back, commonly found in forests and grasslands.
Australian python with patterned back

Examples

A large carpet snake was coiled up near the roof of the garden shed.
Farmers often welcome the presence of a carpet snake because it helps control rodent populations.
While bushwalking, we spotted two carpet snakes basking in the sun.
