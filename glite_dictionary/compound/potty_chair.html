potty chair

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑti t͡ʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈpɒti tʃɛː / (BrE)
A small portable seat with a built-in chamber, used by young children as a toilet during toilet training.
toilet seat for young children

Examples

We bought a new potty chair to help our daughter during toilet training.
The toddler proudly sat on his potty chair after lunch.
She decorated the potty chair with stickers to encourage her son to use it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: potty - a child's word for toilet, often meaning a child's chamber pot or training toilet
