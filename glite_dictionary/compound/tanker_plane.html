tanker plane

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæŋkər pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈtaŋkə pleɪn / (BrE)
An aircraft designed to carry and drop large quantities of chemicals or water to fight forest fires.
aircraft for firefighting chemical drops

Examples

The crew loaded the tanker plane with fire retardant before it took off to combat the wildfire.
A tanker plane flew low over the forest, releasing its load to help stop the flames.
During fire season, several tanker planes are kept on standby at the local airfield.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air tanker - an aircraft for carrying and dispersing liquids, especially used in firefighting
