chicken taco

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkɪn ˈtɑːkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn ˈtækəʊ / (BrE)
A taco filled with a chicken-based filling, often seasoned and accompanied by toppings such as lettuce, cheese, and salsa.
taco with chicken filling

Examples

She ordered a chicken taco with extra salsa for lunch.
The food truck serves the best chicken tacos in town.
Would you like your chicken taco with mild or spicy sauce?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taco - traditional Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various mixtures
