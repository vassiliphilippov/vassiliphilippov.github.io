charming personality

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑrmɪŋ pɚˌsɑnəˈlɪɾi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːmɪŋ pəˌsɒnəˈlɪti / (BrE)
a character or demeanor that is especially attractive and pleasant to others
appealing and pleasant character

Examples

Her charming personality made her the favorite among her colleagues.
Even in difficult situations, his charming personality puts everyone at ease.
People are naturally drawn to those with a charming personality.

Synset Examples

Everyone loves Miss Fidditch for her charming personality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charisma - a compelling charm or attractiveness that can inspire devotion in others
  • Antonym: unpleasant personality - a character or demeanor that is disagreeable or unattractive
