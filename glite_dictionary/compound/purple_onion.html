purple onion

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl ˈʌnjən / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈʌnjən / (BrE)
A type of onion with a mild flavor and purplish-red skin, commonly used as a garnish in salads and hamburgers.
mild onion with purplish-red skin

Examples

She topped her burger with fresh purple onion slices for extra crunch.
The salad was garnished with rings of purple onion and ripe tomatoes.
I love the sweet, mild taste of purple onion in my sandwiches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red onion - a mild onion with reddish-purple skin and white flesh tinged with red
