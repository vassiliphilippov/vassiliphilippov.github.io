flower petal

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚ ˈpɛt.əl / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə ˈpɛt.əl / (BrE)
a part of a flower, typically brightly colored, that forms one of the segments of the corolla
segment of a flower's corolla

Examples

The rose's flower petal felt soft and delicate between my fingers.
A single flower petal drifted to the ground as the wind blew through the garden.
She pressed a flower petal into her journal as a keepsake from the wedding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petal - an individual segment of a flower's corolla, usually brightly colored
