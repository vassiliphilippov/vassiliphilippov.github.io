reading lamp

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːdɪŋ læmp / (AmE), / ˈriːdɪŋ lamp / (BrE)
a lamp designed to provide focused light suitable for reading
lamp for reading purposes

Examples

He turned on the reading lamp to finish his novel before bed.
The reading lamp beside the armchair gives off a warm, gentle light.
She bought a new reading lamp for her study desk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desk lamp - a lamp designed for use on a desk
