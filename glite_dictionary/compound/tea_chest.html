tea chest

1. NOUN

/ ti tʃɛst / (AmE), / tiː tʃɛst / (BrE)
a strong, usually wooden box used for storing or transporting tea
box for storing or transporting tea

Examples

The antique tea chest in the corner once carried cargo from India.
She collects old tea chests for her vintage storage business.
They packed the loose leaves into a large tea chest for shipping.
