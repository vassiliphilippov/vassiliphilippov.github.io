big decision

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / bɪɡ dɪˈsɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
an important or significant choice or judgment that can greatly affect one's life or situation
an important choice or judgment

Examples

She took her time before making the big decision to change careers.
Buying a house is often considered a big decision in a person's life.
After weeks of anxiety, he finally made the big decision to move abroad.

Synset Examples

He stood on the verge of making a big decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: major decision - a very important or serious choice
  • Antonym: trivial decision - a choice of little importance
