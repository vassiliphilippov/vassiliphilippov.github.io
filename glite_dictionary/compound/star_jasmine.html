star jasmine

1. NOUN

/ stɑr ˈdʒæzmɪn / (AmE), / stɑː ˈdʒæzmɪn / (BrE)
An evergreen climbing plant native to China, with shiny dark green leaves and strongly fragrant white star-shaped flowers, often grown as an ornamental.
fragrant climbing plant with white flowers

Examples

The star jasmine filled the garden with its sweet fragrance every spring.
They planted star jasmine along the fence to create a green and flowering screen.
In warm climates, star jasmine is often used to cover trellises and pergolas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jasmine - a genus of shrubs and vines with fragrant flowers

Etymology

Named for its star-shaped, jasmine-like flowers, though not a true jasmine.
