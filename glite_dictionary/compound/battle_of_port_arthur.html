Battle of Port Arthur

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬l əv pɔːrt ˈɑːrθɚ / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv pɔːt ˈɑːθə / (BrE)
A battle in 1894 during the First Sino-Japanese War in which Japanese forces captured the port and fortifications of Port Arthur from Chinese forces.
battle (1894) in First Sino-Japanese War

Examples

The Battle of Port Arthur marked a decisive turning point in the First Sino-Japanese War.
Many historians have analyzed the strategic consequences of the Battle of Port Arthur.
The Battle of Port Arthur demonstrated the effectiveness of Japanese military planning during the conflict.
