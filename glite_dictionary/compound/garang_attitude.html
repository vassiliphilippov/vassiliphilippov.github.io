garang attitude

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑːˈrɑŋ ˈæt̬ɪˌtud / (AmE), / ɡəˈræŋ ˈætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
A bold, fierce demeanor or a lively and energetic personality.
fierce or bold demeanor

Examples

Her garang attitude set her apart from the rest of the candidates during the interview.
The coach admired his player's garang attitude on the court, especially under pressure.
Even in difficult situations, she maintains her garang attitude and faces challenges head-on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bold - showing a willingness to take risks; confident and courageous
  • Antonym: timid attitude - a shy or lacking in courage demeanor

Etymology
