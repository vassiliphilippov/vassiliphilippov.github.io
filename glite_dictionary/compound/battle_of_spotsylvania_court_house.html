Battle of Spotsylvania Court House

1. PROPN

/ ˈbætəl əv ˌspɑtsɪˈvænjə kɔrt haʊs / (AmE), / ˈbætəl əv ˌspɒtsɪˈvænjə kɔːt haʊs / (BrE)
A major battle fought between the Union and Confederate armies, led by Grant and Lee, during the American Civil War's Wilderness Campaign in May 1864.
Civil War battle in 1864 between Grant and Lee

Examples

The Battle of Spotsylvania Court House marked a turning point in the Overland Campaign of the Civil War.
Many soldiers lost their lives during the fierce fighting at the Battle of Spotsylvania Court House.
Historians still study the tactics used by Grant and Lee at the Battle of Spotsylvania Court House.
