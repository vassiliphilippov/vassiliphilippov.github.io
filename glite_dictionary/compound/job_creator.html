job creator

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑb kriˈeɪtər / (AmE), / dʒɒb kriˈeɪtə / (BrE)
a person or organization that generates new employment opportunities
person or entity creating jobs

Examples

The startup quickly became a major job creator in its region, employing hundreds of people within a year.
Many politicians highlight their record as a job creator to appeal to voters.
Tax incentives often aim to reward any job creator who helps reduce unemployment.

Synset Examples

The government aims to become a significant job creator.
The new initiative is a great job creator.
She is known as a job creator in the tech industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: employer - a person or organization that hires people
  • Antonym: employee - a person who works for another or for an organization
