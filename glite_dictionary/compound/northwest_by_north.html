northwest by north

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɔrθˌwɛst baɪ ˈnɔrθ / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˌwɛst baɪ ˈnɔːθ / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point north of northwest, or 11.25° north of northwest.
compass point north of northwest

Examples

The ship changed course to northwest by north to avoid the incoming storm.
Set your heading to northwest by north and you will reach the island by dusk.
Traditional sailors were trained to identify northwest by north on the compass during navigation.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: northwest - compass point between north and west
  • Degree Higher: north-northwest - compass point between north and northwest
