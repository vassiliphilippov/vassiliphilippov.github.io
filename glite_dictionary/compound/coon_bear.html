coon bear

1. NOUN

/ kun bɛr / (AmE), / kuːn beə / (BrE)
another name for the giant panda, a large black-and-white bear-like mammal native to bamboo forests of China and Tibet
giant panda; large bear-like mammal from China

Examples

The coon bear is considered a symbol of conservation efforts worldwide.
Visitors at the zoo were excited to see the coon bear munching on bamboo.
Unlike other bears, the coon bear has a distinctive black-and-white coat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: giant panda - large black-and-white mammal of China and Tibet
