idol worship

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪdəl ˌwɜrʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈaɪdəl ˌwɜːʃɪp / (BrE)
The worship of idols or physical objects as gods, often in a religious context.
worship of idols or images as gods

Examples

In ancient times, idol worship was practiced in many cultures around the world.
Some religions strictly forbid idol worship and consider it a grave sin.
The temple was famous for its elaborate rituals of idol worship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idolatry - the worship of idols as a religious practice
