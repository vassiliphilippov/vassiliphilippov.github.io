hatcheck girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæt.tʃɛk ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈhæt.tʃɛk ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a female attendant who checks and guards hats, coats, and other belongings for customers in a public place.
female attendant for checking hats and coats

Examples

The hatcheck girl handed me a ticket for my coat as I entered the restaurant.
She worked as a hatcheck girl in a glamorous jazz club downtown.
He gave the hatcheck girl a generous tip for her attentive service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hatcheck boy - a male attendant for checking hats and coats
  • Synonym: cloakroom attendant - person who checks and guards coats and belongings

Etymology

From 'hatcheck', referring to the practice of checking hats, and 'girl', denoting a female worker.
