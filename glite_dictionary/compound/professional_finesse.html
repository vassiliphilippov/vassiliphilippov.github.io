professional finesse

1. NOUN

/ prəˈfɛʃənl fəˈnɛs / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃənl fɪˈnɛs / (BrE)
a high degree of skill and polish demonstrated in a professional context or activity
skill and polish in a professional manner

Examples

Her speech was delivered with such professional finesse that the audience was truly impressed.
The final design shows the professional finesse of an experienced architect.
He handled the negotiations with remarkable professional finesse and diplomacy.

Synset Examples

The artwork looked amateurish and lacked professional finesse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finesse - delicate skill and subtlety in action or performance
