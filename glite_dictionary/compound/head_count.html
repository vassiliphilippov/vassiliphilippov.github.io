head count

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛd ˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈhɛd ˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
the number of people present in a particular group or at an event
number of people in a group

Examples

The manager took a quick head count before the meeting started.
We need an accurate head count for the dinner reservation.
Security did a head count after the fire drill to make sure everyone was outside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attendance - the number of people present at an event
