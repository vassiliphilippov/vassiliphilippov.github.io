genus Upupa

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs juːˈpuːpə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs juːˈpuːpə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Upupidae, comprising birds known as hoopoes.
type genus of hoopoes (Upupidae)

Examples

The genus Upupa includes only a few living and extinct species of hoopoe.
Researchers studied differences in plumage within the genus Upupa to understand evolution in the family Upupidae.
Taxonomists recently debated whether African and Eurasian hoopoes should remain in the genus Upupa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Upupa epops - the Eurasian hoopoe, the most well-known member of the genus Upupa

Etymology

From Latin 'Upupa' (hoopoe), adopted in scientific taxonomy.
