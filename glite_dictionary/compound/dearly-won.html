dearly-won

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɪrli ˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˈdɪəli ˈwʌn / (BrE)
Achieved at great cost or through significant sacrifice or loss.
achieved at great cost

Examples

The country finally tasted a dearly-won peace after years of conflict.
Their dearly-won freedom was celebrated by the entire city.
She looked back at the dearly-won victory, remembering all they had sacrificed.
