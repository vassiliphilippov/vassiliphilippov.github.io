orange sneezeweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ ˈsniːzˌwid / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ˈsniːzˌwiːd / (BrE)
a stout perennial herb of the western United States with flower heads having drooping orange-yellow rays; known to cause spewing sickness in sheep
a western US perennial herb with orange-yellow flowers

Examples

The field was dotted with blooming orange sneezeweed throughout the late summer.
Farmers cautioned that grazing sheep near orange sneezeweed could lead to illness.
She photographed the bright orange sneezeweed along the hiking trail.
