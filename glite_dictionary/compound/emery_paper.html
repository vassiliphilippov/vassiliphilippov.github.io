emery paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛm.ə.ri ˌpeɪ.pɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛm.əri ˌpeɪ.pə / (BrE)
stiff paper coated with powdered emery, used as an abrasive for smoothing or polishing surfaces
abrasive paper coated with emery

Examples

He used emery paper to smooth the rough edges of the metal.
Before painting, she carefully rubbed the surface with emery paper.
A strip of emery paper can be handy for cleaning corroded electrical contacts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sandpaper - paper coated with sand or similar abrasive, used for smoothing surfaces

Etymology

From 'emery,' a granular mineral used as an abrasive, and 'paper.'
