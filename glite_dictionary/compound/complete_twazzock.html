complete twazzock

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈplit ˈtwæzək / (AmE), / kəmˈpliːt ˈtwæzək / (BrE)
An extremely foolish or irritating person (British, informal).
very foolish or annoying person (British, informal)
informal slang british

Examples

He made such a mess of things—sometimes he's a complete twazzock.
Don’t be a complete twazzock and listen to the instructions next time.
Calling the boss a liar in front of everyone was a complete twazzock move.

Synset Examples

You're being a complete twazzock right now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Synonym: moron - a person who is very stupid or foolish
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence or skill

Etymology

‘Twazzock’ is British slang of uncertain origin, popularized in northern England in the late 20th century; 'complete' adds emphasis.
