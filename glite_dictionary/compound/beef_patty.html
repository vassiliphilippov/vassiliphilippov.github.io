beef patty

1. NOUN

/ bif ˈpæti / (AmE), / biːf ˈpæti / (BrE)
a flat, round serving of ground beef, usually cooked and used in dishes such as hamburgers
flat, round portion of ground beef

Examples

She placed a juicy beef patty on the grill for dinner.
You can substitute a beef patty with a veggie alternative if you prefer.
The restaurant is known for its homemade beef patty served on freshly baked buns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hamburger patty - a patty of ground beef used in hamburgers
