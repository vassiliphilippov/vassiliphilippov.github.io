working man

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜrkɪŋ mæn / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkɪŋ mæn / (BrE)
A man who is employed to perform manual or industrial work, especially as opposed to one in a managerial or professional position.
male manual or industrial laborer

Examples

He was proud to be known as a working man who earned an honest living.
The politician promised to fight for the rights of the working man.
Stories about the struggles of the working man are common in literature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laborer - a person doing unskilled manual work
  • Synonym: worker - a person who works, especially one who does manual labor
