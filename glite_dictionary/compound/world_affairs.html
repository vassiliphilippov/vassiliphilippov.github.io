world affairs

1. NOUN

/ wɝːld əˈfɛrz / (AmE), / wɜːld əˈfeəz / (BrE)
political, economic, or social events and relationships between nations or involving the international community
international relations and events

Examples

It is important to stay informed about world affairs in our globalized society.
She majored in political science to better understand world affairs.
The summit addressed several pressing world affairs, including climate change and global security.

Synset Examples

you can't really keep up with world affairs by watching television

Related Terms

  • Synonym: international relations - the study or practice of relationships between countries
  • Antonym: domestic affairs - matters occurring within a particular country
