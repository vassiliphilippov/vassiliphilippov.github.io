stir-fried rice

1. NOUN

/ stɝː fraɪd raɪs / (AmE), / stəː fraɪd raɪs / (BrE)
Rice that is cooked quickly over high heat in a small amount of oil, often with added vegetables, meat, or seasoning.
rice cooked by stir-frying in oil

Examples

She ordered stir-fried rice with vegetables and tofu for lunch.
Making stir-fried rice is a great way to use up leftover rice from dinner.
The chef served spicy stir-fried rice as a side dish at the party.

Synset Examples

I love adding kimchi to my stir-fried rice for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fried rice - cooked rice stir-fried with various ingredients
