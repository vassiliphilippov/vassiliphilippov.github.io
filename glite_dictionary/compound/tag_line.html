tag line

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæɡ ˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈtæɡ ˌlaɪn / (BrE)
the concluding or main point of a joke or humorous story, often delivering the humor or surprise
the punchline of a joke or humorous story

Examples

The comedian built up the story before delivering the hilarious tag line.
Without a clever tag line, the joke just fell flat.
Everyone laughed when she reached the tag line of her story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punchline - the final phrase or sentence of a joke, delivering the humor
