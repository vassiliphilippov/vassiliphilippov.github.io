boy Friday

1. NOUN

/ bɔɪ ˈfraɪ.deɪ / (AmE), / bɔɪ ˈfraɪ.deɪ / (BrE)
A male assistant who does a variety of tasks or duties for someone, often in a workplace or personal setting.
male personal assistant with various duties

Examples

We hired a new boy Friday to help out around the office.
As the manager's boy Friday, he handled everything from scheduling to making coffee.
He became the company's go-to boy Friday for any urgent task.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: girl Friday - female assistant who has a range of duties

Etymology

From the fictional character 'Friday' in Daniel Defoe's novel Robinson Crusoe, who was a loyal helper.
