network architecture

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛtˌwɝk ˌɑrkɪˈtɛktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈnɛtwɜːk ˌɑːkɪˈtɛktʃə / (BrE)
the design and structure of a computer or communications network, including its components, data formats, and operating principles
design and structure of a network

Examples

The engineer explained how the network architecture would improve data transmission efficiency across the company.
Changes to the network architecture significantly increased the security of their communications system.
Understanding the network architecture is vital before upgrading any enterprise software.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: network topology - the arrangement or layout of network devices and connections
