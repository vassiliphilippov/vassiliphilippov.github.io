hard-skinned puffball

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrd ˌskɪnd ˈpʌfˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈhɑːd ˌskɪnd ˈpʌf.bɔːl / (BrE)
a type of fungus of the genus Scleroderma with a tough, hard outer skin and subterranean fruiting bodies that resemble truffles
a tough-skinned underground puffball fungus

Examples

The hard-skinned puffball can be found in sandy soils and is known for its durable outer layer.
Scientists studied the chemical composition of a hard-skinned puffball to better understand its defenses.
Unlike other puffballs, the hard-skinned puffball grows mostly underground and is often mistaken for a truffle.
