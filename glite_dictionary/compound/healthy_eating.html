healthy eating

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθi ˈiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθi ˈiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
The practice of consuming food that is nutritious and balanced to maintain or improve health.
consuming nutritious and balanced food

Examples

Many doctors recommend healthy eating as part of a well-rounded lifestyle.
Schools are promoting healthy eating among children to combat childhood obesity.
She made a commitment to healthy eating after her last physical check-up.

Synset Examples

I just published a blogpost about healthy eating.
She avoided cancerous foods and focused on healthy eating.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: balanced diet - a diet that includes all nutrients in proper proportions
