refresher course

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈfrɛʃər kɔrs / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɛʃə kɔːs / (BrE)
a course that reviews and updates subject knowledge for people who are already familiar with the topic but need to catch up on recent developments
course to review and update knowledge

Examples

After several years away from nursing, Maria took a refresher course before returning to work.
The company offers a refresher course to help employees stay updated on the latest safety protocols.
He needed a refresher course in French before moving to Paris for his new job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revision course - a course or class intended to review material already learned
  • Degree Higher: advanced course - a course offered after basic instruction, typically more in-depth
