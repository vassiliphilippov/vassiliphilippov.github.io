goosepimply chill

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡusˌpɪmpli tʃɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡuːsˌpɪmpli tʃɪl / (BrE)
a sensation of fear or excitement that causes goosebumps
sensation causing goosebumps

Examples

The creepy music in the movie gave me a goosepimply chill.
A sudden goosepimply chill ran down her spine as she opened the old door.
The haunted house is famous for inspiring a goosepimply chill in its visitors.

Synset Examples

His ghost story gave me a goosepimply chill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goosebumps - small bumps on the skin caused by fear or excitement
  • Synonym: shiver - involuntary trembling movement, often from fear or cold

Etymology

Formed from 'goosepimply' (covered with goosebumps) and 'chill'.
