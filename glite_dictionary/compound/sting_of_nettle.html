sting of nettle

1. NOUN

/ stɪŋ əv ˈnɛt̬l̩ / (AmE), / stɪŋ ɒv ˈnɛtl̩ / (BrE)
a painful irritation or burning sensation caused by contact with the hairs of nettle plants
painful reaction from touching nettles

Examples

He felt the sting of nettle on his hands after weeding the garden.
The sting of nettle usually fades within a few hours, but can be quite uncomfortable.
Children playing in the field were warned about the painful sting of nettle.

Synset Examples

The gardener warned us about the sting of nettle plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nettle rash - skin irritation or rash caused by nettles
