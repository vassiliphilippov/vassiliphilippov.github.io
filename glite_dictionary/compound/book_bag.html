book bag

1. NOUN

/ bʊk bæɡ / (AmE), / bʊk bæɡ / (BrE)
a bag used by students to carry books and other school supplies
bag for carrying books, esp. by students

Examples

She stuffed her notebooks and lunch into her book bag before leaving for school.
The new book bag has extra pockets for organizing supplies.
After school, he slung his book bag over one shoulder and headed home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backpack - a bag carried on the back with shoulder straps
