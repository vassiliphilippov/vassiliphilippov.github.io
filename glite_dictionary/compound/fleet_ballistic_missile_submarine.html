fleet ballistic missile submarine

1. NOUN

/ fliːt bəˈlɪstɪk mɪˈsaɪl səbˈmɚin / (AmE), / fliːt bəˈlɪstɪk mɪˈsaɪl səbˈmɑːriːn / (BrE)
a type of submarine designed to carry and launch ballistic missiles, typically as part of a nation's strategic nuclear deterrent force
submarine carrying ballistic missiles

Examples

The navy commissioned a new fleet ballistic missile submarine to enhance its strategic deterrent capabilities.
A fleet ballistic missile submarine can remain submerged for months at a time while on patrol.
International treaties often place restrictions on the number of fleet ballistic missile submarines each country can operate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ballistic missile submarine - a submarine equipped to launch ballistic missiles
