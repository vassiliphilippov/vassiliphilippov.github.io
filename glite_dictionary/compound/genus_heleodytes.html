genus Heleodytes

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiːli.oʊˌdaɪtiːz / (AmE), / ˈhiːli.ə(ʊ)ˌdaɪtiːz / (BrE)
A former or alternative genus classification for certain cactus wrens, sometimes used in ornithological taxonomy.
a genus of cactus wrens (taxonomy)

Examples

The species was previously classified under genus Heleodytes before taxonomic revision.
Some ornithologists still refer to the genus Heleodytes in older literature.
Desert birds from the genus Heleodytes are now usually placed in the genus Campylorhynchus.

Etymology

From Greek hêleos ('marsh') + dytēs ('diver'), used in New Latin classification.
