orange milkweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ ˈmɪlkˌwid / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ˈmɪlkˌwiːd / (BrE)
a perennial plant native to eastern and southern United States, known for its tall growth and showy orange flowers
a plant with orange flowers, native to U.S.

Examples

Monarch butterflies are often attracted to orange milkweed in gardens.
The bright flowers of orange milkweed make it popular among gardeners seeking pollinator plants.
During summer, you can spot orange milkweed blooming along country roads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butterfly weed - a synonym for orange milkweed; another common name for the same plant
