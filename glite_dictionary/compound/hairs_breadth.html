hair's breadth

1. NOUN

/ hɛrz brɛdθ / (AmE), / heəz brɛdθ / (BrE)
An extremely small distance or margin.
very small distance or margin

Examples

The bullet missed him by a hair's breadth.
Their team won the championship by a hair's breadth.
She avoided disaster by only a hair's breadth.

Synset Examples

they escaped by a hair's breadth
they lost the election by a whisker

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whisker - a very small amount or margin by which something is missed or achieved

Etymology

Refers to the width of a human hair, which is very narrow.
