horse armor

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs ˈɑːrmər / (AmE), / hɔːs ˈɑːmə / (BrE)
protective covering made of metal or other materials designed to shield a horse, especially in battle or tournaments
protective covering for a horse

Examples

The knight donned his horse armor before the battle.
The horse armor gleamed under the sunlight.
They displayed an ancient set of horse armor at the museum.
