signature recognition

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪɡnətʃɚ ˌrɛkəɡˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnətʃə ˌrɛkəɡˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of identifying a person by automatically scanning and matching their signature against stored examples using electronic systems
biometric identification by scanned signature

Examples

Many banks use signature recognition systems to verify customer transactions.
Advancements in signature recognition have improved security in online banking.
The company implemented signature recognition to reduce fraud in legal document processing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biometric authentication - identification or verification of identity using biological characteristics
