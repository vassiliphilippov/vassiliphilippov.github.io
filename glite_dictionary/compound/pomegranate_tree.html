pomegranate tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːməˌɡrænɪt triː / (AmE), / ˈpɒməˌɡranɪt triː / (BrE)
a shrub or small tree native to southwestern Asia, bearing large red fruit with many seeds
tree or shrub bearing pomegranates

Examples

The pomegranate tree in our garden produces fruit every autumn.
Farmers in this region often cultivate the pomegranate tree for its valuable fruit.
A mature pomegranate tree can live for several decades if properly cared for.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pomegranate - the large red fruit produced by the pomegranate tree
