mental fatigue

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.t̬l fəˈtiːɡ / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.tl fəˈtiːɡ / (BrE)
a state of exhaustion caused by prolonged mental effort or stress
exhaustion from prolonged mental effort

Examples

After six hours of studying, he was experiencing intense mental fatigue.
Chronic mental fatigue can affect your concentration and memory.
Regular breaks are important to prevent mental fatigue during long workdays.

Synset Examples

She struggled with mental fatigue after the long week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental exhaustion - a state of being extremely tired mentally
  • Antonym: mental alertness - state of being fully attentive and focused
