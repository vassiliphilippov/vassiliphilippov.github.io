northern Europe

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðərn ˈjʊrəp / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈjʊərəp / (BrE)
the northernmost region of Europe, typically including countries such as Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Finland, and Iceland
the northernmost region of Europe

Examples

The winters in northern Europe are often long and harsh due to its high latitude.
She traveled extensively across northern Europe, visiting Finland, Norway, and Sweden.
Trade between northern Europe and the rest of the world increased significantly in the past decade.
