exotic cheroot

1. NOUN

/ ɪgˈzɑːtɪk tʃəˈruːt / (AmE), / ɪgˈzɒtɪk tʃəˈruːt / (BrE)
a fine cigar of unusual or foreign origin, typically with unique qualities or flavors
a fine, foreign cigar

Examples

He enjoyed an exotic cheroot after dinner, savoring its rich aroma.
Only the finest selection of exotic cheroots was offered at the exclusive club.
She gifted her host an exotic cheroot she had brought from her travels abroad.

Synset Examples

An exotic cheroot was the perfect end to the evening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheroot - a cigar with both ends cut flat
  • Degree Lower: cigar - a tightly rolled bundle of dried tobacco

Etymology

'Cheroot' from Tamil *curūṭṭu* (roll of tobacco); 'exotic' from Greek *exōtikos*, meaning 'from the outside'.
