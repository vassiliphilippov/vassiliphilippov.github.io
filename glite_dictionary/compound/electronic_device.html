electronic device

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
A device that operates using electric currents to process or transmit information, signals, or perform functions electronically.
device using electronic technology

Examples

Please turn off your electronic device before the plane takes off.
He spent hours repairing the broken electronic device in the laboratory.
Many children today own at least one electronic device for learning or entertainment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gadget - a small mechanical or electronic device with a particular purpose
