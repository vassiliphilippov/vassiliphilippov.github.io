vinegarish green

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪnəɡərɪʃ ɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈvɪnɪɡərɪʃ ɡriːn / (BrE)
a shade of green that is tinged with a yellowish or sour, acidic tone, resembling the color of vinegar
a yellowish, acidic shade of green

Examples

The living room was painted a bold vinegarish green, which made it stand out.
She wore a scarf of vinegarish green that gave her outfit a unique flair.
Artists sometimes use vinegarish green to evoke an unsettling atmosphere in their paintings.

Synset Examples

The color of the wall was a vinegarish green.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellow-green - a green color with a tint of yellow
