bond trading

1. NOUN

/ bɑnd ˈtreɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / bɒnd ˈtreɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of buying and selling bonds, typically on a financial exchange or through a broker
buying and selling of bonds

Examples

He built his financial career through bond trading at a major investment bank.
Advances in technology have transformed the way bond trading is conducted.
Companies often use bond trading to manage their portfolios and risks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stock trading - buying and selling of stocks or shares
  • Synonym: bond investing - the long-term act of purchasing bonds as an investment
