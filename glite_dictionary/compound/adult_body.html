adult body

1. NOUN

/ əˈdʌlt ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / əˈdʌlt ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
the fully developed physical body of a mature human being
fully developed human body

Examples

She was amazed at how quickly her son transformed from a child's body to an adult body.
The adult body requires different nutrients than a growing adolescent's.
Artists study the proportions of the adult body to improve their figure drawings.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: child's body - the body of a child
