wire stripper

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪɚ ˌstrɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪə ˌstrɪpə / (BrE)
a hand tool for removing the insulation from electric wires
tool for removing wire insulation

Examples

The electrician used a wire stripper to prepare the cables for installation.
Make sure you choose the correct notch on the wire stripper to avoid damaging the wire.
She bought a new wire stripper for her DIY electrical projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wire cutter - a tool for cutting wire
