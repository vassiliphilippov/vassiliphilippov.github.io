pepper mill

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛpər mɪl / (AmE), / ˈpepə mɪl / (BrE)
a device for grinding whole peppercorns into ground pepper for use in cooking or at the table
device for grinding peppercorns

Examples

She added freshly ground pepper from the pepper mill to the salad.
The elegant wooden pepper mill matched the salt shaker on the table.
He refilled the pepper mill with new peppercorns before dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pepper grinder - a device for grinding pepper; synonym for pepper mill
