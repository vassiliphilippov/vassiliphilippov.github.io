annual fair

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.ju.əl fɛr / (AmE), / ˈan.ju.əl feə / (BrE)
a fair held every year, usually featuring entertainment, food, games, and local attractions
a fair held every year

Examples

Every summer, our town hosts an annual fair that attracts visitors from nearby cities.
Children eagerly await the rides and games at the annual fair each year.
Local farmers display their produce at the annual fair, making it a community highlight.

Synset Examples

The village hall was decorated for the annual fair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fair - a gathering with entertainment, rides, and stalls
