copper mine

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑpər ˌmaɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɒpə ˌmaɪn / (BrE)
a mine from which copper is extracted from the ground
mine where copper is extracted

Examples

The abandoned copper mine was once the main source of employment for the village.
They invested millions to modernize the old copper mine in Arizona.
Environmental concerns have halted construction of the new copper mine near the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gold mine - a mine where gold is extracted from the earth
  • Synonym: coal mine - a mine from which coal is dug
  • Degree Higher: mine - an excavation from which minerals are extracted
