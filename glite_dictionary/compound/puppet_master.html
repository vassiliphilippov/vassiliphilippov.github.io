puppet master

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌpɪt ˌmæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈpʌpɪt ˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who controls or manipulates others, especially in a covert or behind-the-scenes way
one who manipulates or controls others

Examples

Many believe the real puppet master of the company remains hidden from view.
The politician was seen as a puppet master, orchestrating events from the shadows.
Despite her quiet demeanor, she was the puppet master controlling the entire operation.

Synset Examples

The director acted like a puppet master with the cast.
In this game, he played the puppet master expertly.
She was the puppet master behind the scenes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manipulator - a person who controls or influences others cleverly or unscrupulously

Etymology

From 'puppet' (a figure moved by strings or controls) + 'master' (one who controls or directs).
