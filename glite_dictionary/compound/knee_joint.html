knee joint

1. NOUN

/ ˈniː ˌdʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈniː ˌdʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
the joint in the human leg where the thigh meets the lower leg, protected at the front by the kneecap (patella)
joint connecting thigh and lower leg
medical

Examples

The surgeon explained the procedure for replacing a damaged knee joint.
After the accident, she experienced pain and swelling in her knee joint.
Physical therapy helped him regain strength and flexibility in his knee joint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knee - the joint between the thigh and lower leg
