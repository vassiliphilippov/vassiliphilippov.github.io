carbon group element

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrbən ɡruːp ˈɛləmənt / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbən ɡruːp ˈelɪmənt / (BrE)
any of the chemical elements belonging to Group 14 of the periodic table, which includes carbon, silicon, germanium, tin, lead, and flerovium.
element in Group 14 of the periodic table

Examples

Carbon group element atoms have four electrons in their outermost shell.
Silicon, a well-known carbon group element, is widely used in computer chips.
The chemical similarities among carbon group elements lead to interesting trends in their properties.

Synset Examples

Carbon, the lightest carbon group element, sublimates at 3825 °C.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Group 14 element - another term for carbon group element; elements in Group 14 of the periodic table
