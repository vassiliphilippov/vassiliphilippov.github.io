lamp oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæmp ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈlamp ɔɪl / (BrE)
a flammable oil used as fuel in lamps and heaters
oil used to fuel lamps and heaters

Examples

We filled the old lantern with lamp oil before going on our camping trip.
The scent of lamp oil lingered in the air long after the power outage.
She bought a bottle of lamp oil to use in her decorative glass lamp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kerosene - a thin oil used as fuel in heaters and lamps, often synonymous with lamp oil
