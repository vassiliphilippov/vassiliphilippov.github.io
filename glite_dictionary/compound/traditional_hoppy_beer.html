traditional hoppy beer

1. NOUN

/ trəˈdɪʃənəl ˈhɑpi ˌbɪr / (AmE), / trəˈdɪʃənəl ˈhɒpi ˌbɪə / (BrE)
a style of beer characterized by a strong hop flavor, typical of many classic beer varieties
beer with strong hop flavor

Examples

Many craft breweries focus on brewing traditional hoppy beers for enthusiasts who love bold flavors.
She sampled a flight of traditional hoppy beers at the tasting event.
Some people find traditional hoppy beer too bitter, while others enjoy the intensity.

Synset Examples

He prefers gruit over traditional hoppy beers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ale - a type of beer brewed using a warm fermentation method
