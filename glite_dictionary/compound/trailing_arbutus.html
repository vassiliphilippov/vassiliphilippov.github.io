trailing arbutus

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪlɪŋ ɑːrˈbjuːtəs / (AmE), / ˈtreɪlɪŋ ɑːˈbjuːtəs / (BrE)
a low-growing evergreen shrub of eastern North America with leathery leaves and clusters of fragrant pink or white flowers
low-growing North American evergreen shrub

Examples

Every spring, the woods are filled with the sweet scent of trailing arbutus.
We spotted patches of trailing arbutus blooming along the forest path.
The trailing arbutus is protected in some areas due to its rarity.
