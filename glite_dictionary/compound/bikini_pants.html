bikini pants

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈkini pænts / (AmE), / bɪˈkiːni pænts / (BrE)
A pair of women's underpants that are small, tight-fitting, and cut in a style similar to a bikini bottom.
small, tight-fitting women's underpants

Examples

She prefers to wear bikini pants because they are comfortable and don't show under her clothes.
Many stores carry a variety of bikini pants in different colors and fabrics.
After buying new bikini pants, she felt confident in her new summer outfits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bikini briefs - a similar style of women's underpants
  • Antonym: boyshorts - a style of women's underwear with more coverage

Etymology

From 'bikini', referring to the style of two-piece swimsuit, and 'pants'.
