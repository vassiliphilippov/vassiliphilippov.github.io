larger-than-life

1. ADJ

/ ˌlɑrdʒɚ ðən ˈlaɪf / (AmE), / ˌlɑːdʒə ðən ˈlaɪf / (BrE)
Extremely impressive or imposing; surpassing the ordinary in size, character, or scale.
exceptionally impressive or extraordinary

Examples

The movie's protagonist was a larger-than-life hero who always saved the day.
Her larger-than-life personality made her the center of attention wherever she went.
The artist's larger-than-life sculptures dominated the city square.
