lottery winner

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑtəri ˈwɪnər / (AmE), / ˈlɒtəri ˈwɪnə / (BrE)
a person who has won a lottery and received its prize
person who has won a lottery

Examples

The new lottery winner celebrated by buying a new house for her family.
As a lottery winner, he was interviewed on national television about his plans for the money.
Many lottery winners choose to remain anonymous for privacy reasons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jackpot winner - person who wins the top prize in a lottery
  • Antonym: lottery loser - person who did not win a lottery prize
