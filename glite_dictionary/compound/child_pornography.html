child pornography

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪld ˌpɔrnəˌɡræfi / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌɪld ˌpɔːnəˌɡrafi / (BrE)
Sexually explicit material depicting children, which is illegal and considered a criminal offense in most jurisdictions.
illegal sexually explicit material involving children
legal

Examples

The police raided the house and discovered evidence of child pornography on several computers.
Laws against child pornography are strictly enforced in many countries around the world.
Organizations work together internationally to combat the production and distribution of child pornography.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: child abuse - the physical, sexual, or emotional mistreatment of children
  • Degree Lower: pornography - sexually explicit material intended to cause sexual arousal
