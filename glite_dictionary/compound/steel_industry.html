steel industry

1. NOUN

/ stil ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / stiːl ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector of industry involved in the production of steel and steel products
industry producing steel and steel products

Examples

China has become the world leader in the steel industry over the past decade.
The local economy relies heavily on jobs provided by the steel industry.
Environmental regulations are having a major impact on the steel industry worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iron industry - industry producing iron and iron products
