water orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
a tropical aquatic plant with floating leaves and spikes of large blue flowers, often problematic for clogging waterways, especially in the southern United States
tropical aquatic plant with blue flowers

Examples

The spread of water orchid in the river has made boating almost impossible.
Local authorities are working to remove water orchid from nearby lakes to prevent clogs.
We spotted beautiful water orchid blossoms floating near the riverbank during our trip.
