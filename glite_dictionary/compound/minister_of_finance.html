minister of finance

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪnɪstɚ əv faɪˈnæns / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪstə əv faɪˈnɑːns / (BrE)
a government official responsible for managing a country's finances, including taxation, spending, and economic policy
government official in charge of finances

Examples

The minister of finance announced new policies to stimulate economic growth.
After serving as minister of finance, she was appointed head of the central bank.
The prime minister consulted the minister of finance before proposing the new tax reforms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finance minister - a government official responsible for a country's finance; alternative phrasing
