brick red

1. NOUN

/ brɪk rɛd / (AmE), / brɪk rɛd / (BrE)
a strong reddish-brown colour, like that of a typical red brick
a reddish-brown colour

Examples

The walls were painted a deep brick red to create a warm, inviting atmosphere.
She wore a scarf in a striking brick red that complemented her outfit.
The car's brick red finish made it stand out on the road.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: red - the colour at the end of the visible spectrum; the colour of blood
  • Degree Higher: scarlet - a brilliant red colour with a slight orange tinge
