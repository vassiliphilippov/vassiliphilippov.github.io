local treasure

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.kəl ˈtrɛʒ.ər / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl ˈtrɛʒ.ə / (BrE)
a person, place, object, or tradition highly valued or cherished within a particular community or region
something highly valued in a specific community

Examples

The historic library is considered a local treasure by many residents.
Her handmade quilts have become a local treasure over the years.
The annual festival is a local treasure that brings the whole town together.

Synset Examples

The old shandrydan was a local treasure for tourists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: landmark - a building or place recognized for its importance or significance
