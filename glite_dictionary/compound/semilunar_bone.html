semilunar bone

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛməˈluːnɚ boʊn / (AmE), / ˌsɛmɪˈluːnə bəʊn / (BrE)
a crescent-shaped bone in the wrist, also known as the lunate bone, one of the eight carpal bones
crescent-shaped wrist bone (lunate bone)
medical

Examples

The semilunar bone is located in the center of the wrist among the carpal bones.
An injury to the semilunar bone may cause pain or limited movement in the hand.
Doctors identified a fracture in the patient's semilunar bone after the fall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunate bone - the carpal bone in the wrist also called semilunar bone

Etymology

From Latin 'semilunaris' meaning 'crescent-shaped', from 'semi-' (half) and 'luna' (moon).
