Battle of Buena Vista

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæt̬l əv ˌbweɪnə ˈvɪstə / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˌbweɪnə ˈvɪstə / (BrE)
a major 1847 battle during the Mexican–American War in which U.S. forces led by Zachary Taylor defeated Mexican forces under Santa Anna in northern Mexico
1847 battle in the Mexican–American War

Examples

The Battle of Buena Vista marked a turning point in the Mexican–American War.
Historians debate the tactics used at the Battle of Buena Vista.
Many soldiers received honors for their bravery during the Battle of Buena Vista.
