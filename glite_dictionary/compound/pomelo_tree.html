pomelo tree

1. NOUN

/ pəˈmɛloʊ tri / (AmE), / pəˈmiːləʊ triː / (BrE)
A tree native to Southeast Asia that produces large citrus fruits resembling grapefruits.
Southeast Asian citrus fruit tree

Examples

The pomelo tree thrives in tropical climates with plenty of sunlight and water.
Farmers cultivate the pomelo tree for its sweet, juicy fruit.
The pomelo tree can grow quite tall, with broad, green leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pomelo - a large, yellow citrus fruit similar to grapefruit
  • Degree Lower: grapefruit tree - tree producing grapefruits

Etymology

From Malay 'pamelo' or Dutch 'pomelo', ultimately of unknown origin.
