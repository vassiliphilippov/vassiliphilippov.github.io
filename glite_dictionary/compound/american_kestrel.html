American kestrel

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən ˈkɛstrəl / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈkɛstrəl / (BrE)
a small North American falcon with a reddish-brown back and tail, blue-grey wings, and distinctive black facial markings
small North American falcon

Examples

An American kestrel hovered above the field, searching for prey.
The American kestrel is recognized by its colorful plumage and sharp eyesight.
We spotted two American kestrels perched on the fence post during our hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: falcon - bird of prey with long pointed wings
