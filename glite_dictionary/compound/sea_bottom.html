sea bottom

1. NOUN

/ si ˈbɑːtəm / (AmE), / siː ˈbɒtəm / (BrE)
the floor or base of a sea or ocean; the seabed
the floor of the sea or ocean

Examples

Divers explored the sea bottom, searching for signs of shipwrecks.
Strange creatures were discovered living on the sea bottom at great depths.
The submarine settled gently onto the sea bottom after its descent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seabed - the bed or floor of a sea or ocean
  • Synonym: ocean floor - the ground at the bottom of the ocean
