postal address

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstl əˌdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstl əˌdrɛs / (BrE)
the address to which mail can be sent to a person or business, which may differ from the physical or street address
address for receiving mail

Examples

Please write your postal address clearly on the form so we can send your documents.
Her postal address is a P.O. Box because she does not receive mail at her house.
The university requires a valid postal address for all students in case of emergency correspondence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mailing address - address to which mail is sent
