communazi attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːmjəˈnɑzi ˈætəˌtud / (AmE), / ˌkɒmjʊˈnɑːzi ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
A derogatory term referring to a perceived combination of communist and Nazi ideologies or principles.
mix of communist and Nazi ideologies (derogatory)
offensive

Examples

Her communazi attitude alienated potential supporters.
Critics accused the regime of promoting a communazi attitude in its policies.
The politician's remarks were condemned as reflecting a dangerous communazi attitude.

Synset Examples

Her communazi attitude alienated potential supporters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communist - Supporter of a system based on common ownership of property
  • Synonym: Nazi - Member of the National Socialist German Workers' Party (NSDAP) or a supporter of its ideologies

Etymology

Blend of 'communist' and 'Nazi', formed as a portmanteau to express contempt.
