Miao language

1. NOUN

/ miˈaʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / miˈaʊ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A language or group of languages spoken by the Hmong people in southern China and Southeast Asia, of uncertain linguistic affiliation.
a language of the Hmong people

Examples

The Miao language is spoken by millions of Hmong people across several Asian countries.
Linguists classify the Miao language as part of the Hmong-Mien family, though its origins are debated.
She studied the Miao language to better understand local folklore and oral traditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hmong - an ethnic group native to southern China and Southeast Asia

Etymology

From Chinese 苗 (Miáo), the name for the Hmong people.
