Captain Cook

1. PROPN

/ ˈkæptɪn kʊk / (AmE), / ˈkæptɪn kʊk / (BrE)
(1728–1779) English navigator and explorer who charted the east coast of Australia and several Pacific islands, claiming them for Britain.
English explorer and navigator (1728–1779)

Examples

Captain Cook sailed around the world on voyages of discovery in the 18th century.
Many places in Australia were first mapped by Captain Cook and his crew.
The achievements of Captain Cook had a lasting impact on world exploration.
