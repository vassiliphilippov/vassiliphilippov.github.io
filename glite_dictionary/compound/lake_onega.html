Lake Onega

1. PROPN

/ leɪk oʊˈnɛɡə / (AmE), / leɪk əʊˈnɛɡə / (BrE)
a large lake in northwestern Russia near the border with Finland, the second largest lake in Europe
large lake in northwestern Russia

Examples

Thousands of tourists visit Lake Onega every year to enjoy its natural beauty and rich history.
The islands of Lake Onega are home to some of the oldest wooden churches in Russia.
Fishermen report that Lake Onega is abundant with various species of fish throughout the year.
