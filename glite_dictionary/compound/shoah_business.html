Shoah business

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊ.ɑː ˈbɪz.nəs / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.ɑː ˈbɪz.nɪs / (BrE)
serious matters or discussions relating to the Holocaust, often referencing the cultural, academic, or commercial aspects connected with its remembrance and representation
serious matters related to the Holocaust

Examples

Many documentaries about the Holocaust are considered part of the Shoah business.
The historian criticized the commercialization found in some aspects of the Shoah business.
Debates about museums and memorials often touch on the complexities of the Shoah business.

Synset Examples

This isn't just a joke; it's Shoah business.
Discussing that topic is serious Shoah business.

Etymology

Shoah from Hebrew (meaning 'catastrophe', used for the Holocaust) combined with English 'business'.
