pumpkin seed

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌmp.kɪn siːd / (AmE), / ˈpʌmp.kɪn siːd / (BrE)
the edible seed of a pumpkin, often eaten as a snack or used as an ingredient in recipes
the edible seed of a pumpkin

Examples

Sprinkling toasted pumpkin seed on salads adds a delicious crunch.
She bought a bag of salted pumpkin seed at the market for a healthy snack.
The recipe calls for ground pumpkin seed to thicken the sauce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunflower seed - the edible seed of a sunflower plant
