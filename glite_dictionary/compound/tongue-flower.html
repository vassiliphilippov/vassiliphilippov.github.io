tongue-flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌŋˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʌŋˌflaʊə / (BrE)
An orchid with blue to purple flowers characterized by tongue-shaped or strap-shaped protuberances (calli) at the base of the lip.
type of orchid with tongue-shaped protuberances

Examples

The tongue-flower is prized by collectors for its unusual blue-violet blossoms.
Botanists were excited to discover a new species of tongue-flower in the rainforest.
Each tongue-flower has a distinctive lip with a protruding, strap-shaped callus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchid - a plant with complex, often colorful flowers; family Orchidaceae
