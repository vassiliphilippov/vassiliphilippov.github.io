working out

1. NOUN

/ ˌwɝːkɪŋ ˈaʊt / (AmE), / ˌwɜːkɪŋ ˈaʊt / (BrE)
the act or process of developing something in intricate and painstaking detail; the detailed elaboration or resolution of a problem or plan
detailed development or elaboration

Examples

The architect's working out of the building's design took several weeks of careful planning.
In mathematics, showing your working out is often required to demonstrate how you solve a problem.
Her detailed working out of the proposal impressed everyone at the meeting.
