work clothes

1. NOUN

/ wɝːk kloʊðz / (AmE), / wɜːk kləʊðz / (BrE)
clothing worn for doing manual labor or practical tasks, especially designed to be durable and suitable for work environments
clothing for manual labor

Examples

She changed into her work clothes before starting to paint the house.
The company supplies all employees with protective work clothes.
His work clothes were covered in dust after the long shift.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overalls - a one-piece protective garment worn for manual work
  • Synonym: uniform - a standard set of clothing worn by members of an organization while participating in that organization's activity
