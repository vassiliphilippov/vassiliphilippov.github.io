to be honest

1. ADV

/ tə bi ˈɑnəst / (AmE), / tə bi ˈɒnɪst / (BrE)
used to introduce a statement in which the speaker expresses sincerity, frankness, or candour
used to express frankness or sincerity
informal

Examples

To be honest, I find this topic a bit confusing.
You did a great job, but to be honest, I think you can do even better next time.
She said, 'to be honest, I was surprised by the results.'

Synset Examples

To be honest, I’m feeling a bit overwhelmed.
To be honest, I didn’t enjoy the movie.
To be honest, I expected a better outcome.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frankly - used to emphasize honest or direct speech
  • Synonym: honestly - in an honest manner; used to emphasize sincerity or truthfulness
