county council

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊnti ˈkaʊnsəl / (AmE), / ˈkaʊnti ˈkaʊnsəl / (BrE)
the elected local governing body responsible for administering a county's public services and affairs
elected body governing a county

Examples

The county council approved the new budget for local schools and roads.
She was elected to the county council after years of community service.
A representative from the county council attended the town meeting to answer questions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: city council - elected governing body of a city or town
