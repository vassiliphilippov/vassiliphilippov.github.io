operating table

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑpəˌreɪtɪŋ ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈɒpəˌreɪtɪŋ ˌteɪbəl / (BrE)
A special table on which a patient lies during a surgical operation.
table used in surgery

Examples

The surgeon instructed the staff to prepare the operating table before the patient arrived.
After the lengthy procedure, the patient was wheeled away from the operating table.
All tools must be sterile before placing them on the operating table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surgical table - table used for surgical procedures; synonym of operating table
