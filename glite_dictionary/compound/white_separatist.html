white separatist

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈsɛp.əˌreɪ.tɪst / (AmE), / waɪt ˈsɛp.ər.ə.tɪst / (BrE)
a person who advocates for white people to live separately from members of other racial groups
advocate of racial segregation for whites

Examples

The group is led by a well-known white separatist who opposes multicultural policies.
Several communities have rejected proposals from white separatists seeking to establish exclusive enclaves.
He has repeatedly denied being a white separatist, but his actions suggest otherwise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white supremacist - a person who believes whites are superior to others
  • Synonym: segregationist - a person who supports the separation of racial groups
  • Antonym: integrationist - a person who supports social integration of different racial groups
