colorific painting

1. NOUN

/ ˌkʌlərˈɪfɪk ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌkʌləˈrɪfɪk ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
a painting that is vibrant and full of vivid colors
painting with vibrant, vivid colors

Examples

The museum featured a colorific painting that attracted visitors with its intense hues.
Every colorific painting in the exhibit seemed to radiate energy and life.
She became known for her signature style, producing one colorific painting after another.

Synset Examples

Her colorific painting brightened up the dull hallway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colorful painting - a painting that is full of bright or varied colors
