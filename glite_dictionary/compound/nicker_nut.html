nicker nut

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪkər nʌt / (AmE), / ˈnɪkə nʌt / (BrE)
the hard, shiny, grey seed of a bonduc tree, often used to make jewelry or ornaments
shiny grey seed of bonduc tree

Examples

She wore a necklace made from polished nicker nuts gathered during her travels.
Children collected nicker nuts on the beach to use in games and crafts.
Artisans value the durability and lustre of nicker nuts for handmade jewelry.
