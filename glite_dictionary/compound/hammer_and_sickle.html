hammer and sickle

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæmɚ ənd ˈsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈhamə ənd ˈsɪkəl / (BrE)
a symbol consisting of a hammer crossed with a sickle, representing industrial and agricultural workers; used especially as an emblem of the Soviet Union and communism
Soviet and communist emblem: hammer crossed with sickle

Examples

The hammer and sickle was displayed prominently on the Soviet flag.
He wore a pin shaped like the hammer and sickle on his jacket.
After the fall of the USSR, the hammer and sickle was removed from government buildings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communism - a political and economic ideology advocating communal ownership
  • Synonym: red star - another communist symbol, often used with the hammer and sickle
