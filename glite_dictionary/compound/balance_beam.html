balance beam

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæləns biːm / (AmE), / ˈbæləns biːm / (BrE)
A narrow, usually wooden apparatus raised off the ground, used in gymnastics for routines involving walking, turning, and acrobatic skills, especially by women.
narrow gymnastics apparatus for routines

Examples

The gymnast performed a flawless routine on the balance beam during the competition.
Practicing on the balance beam can help develop excellent balance and coordination.
A slight wobble on the balance beam can make the difference between winning and losing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beam - a long, sturdy piece of squared timber or metal used to support weight
