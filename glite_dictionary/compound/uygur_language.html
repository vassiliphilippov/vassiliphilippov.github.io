Uygur language

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːɡər ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈwiːɡə ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
The Turkic language spoken by the Uighur people, primarily in the Xinjiang region of northwestern China.
language of the Uighur people

Examples

Many students in Xinjiang are fluent in both Chinese and Uygur language.
Linguists have studied the unique script and grammar of the Uygur language.
She is translating a folktale from the Uygur language into English.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Turkic - relating to the family of Turkic languages

Etymology

From Uygur (name of the people) plus English 'language'; Uygur from Old Turkic 'Uyghur'.
