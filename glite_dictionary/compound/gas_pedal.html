gas pedal

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈpɛɾl / (AmE), / ɡas ˈpɛdl / (BrE)
the pedal in a vehicle that regulates the engine's throttle and controls its speed
pedal controlling a vehicle's throttle
american

Examples

He pressed the gas pedal to accelerate quickly onto the highway.
She took her foot off the gas pedal as she approached the traffic light.
If you step too hard on the gas pedal, the car will lurch forward.

Synset Examples

he stepped on the gas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accelerator - the pedal in a vehicle used to increase speed
  • Antonym: brake pedal - the pedal used to slow down or stop a vehicle
