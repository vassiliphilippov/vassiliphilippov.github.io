unweaned calf

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈwiːnd kælf / (AmE), / ʌnˈwiːnd kɑːf / (BrE)
a young calf that has not yet been weaned from its mother's milk
calf not yet weaned from milk

Examples

The farmer inspected each unweaned calf to ensure they were healthy and gaining weight.
Raising an unweaned calf requires feeding it with milk replacer several times a day.
After a few months, the unweaned calf will be ready to transition to solid food.

Synset Examples

Unweaned calves need to be fed formula regularly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calf - a young bovine animal
  • Antonym: weaned calf - a calf that has been weaned from milk
