family Balaenicipitidae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli bəˌliːnɪˌsɪpɪˈtaɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæməli bəˌliːnɪˌsɪpɪˈtaɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of large, African wading birds consisting of the shoebill (Balaeniceps rex).
bird family containing the shoebill

Examples

The family Balaenicipitidae consists of just one species, the distinctive shoebill.
Ornithologists closely study the family Balaenicipitidae due to its unique evolutionary traits.
Unlike other bird families, family Balaenicipitidae has only a single living representative.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoebill - large African wading bird, the only member of family Balaenicipitidae

Etymology

From New Latin Balaenicipitidae, based on genus Balaeniceps (the shoebill) + -idae (family suffix).
