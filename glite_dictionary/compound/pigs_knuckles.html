pigs' knuckles

1. NOUN

/ pɪɡz ˈnʌkəlz / (AmE), / pɪɡz ˈnʌkəlz / (BrE)
The feet or knuckles of pigs, prepared and eaten as food, often pickled, stewed, or jellied.
feet or knuckles of pigs used as food

Examples

He ordered pigs' knuckles with sauerkraut at the German restaurant.
My grandmother used to cook pigs' knuckles in a flavorful broth every winter.
Many people enjoy pigs' knuckles as a traditional delicacy during holidays.
