Cape Cod Bay

1. PROPN

/ keɪp kɑd beɪ / (AmE), / keɪp kɒd beɪ / (BrE)
a large bay in the southern part of Massachusetts Bay, off the coast of Massachusetts, United States
bay off the coast of Massachusetts, USA

Examples

The beaches along Cape Cod Bay are popular destinations for summer tourists.
Fishing is a traditional industry in Cape Cod Bay.
Sunsets over Cape Cod Bay are often breathtaking.
