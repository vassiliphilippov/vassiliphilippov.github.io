vest pocket

1. NOUN

/ vɛst ˈpɑkɪt / (AmE), / vɛst ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
A small pocket in a man's vest, typically used to hold a watch or small items.
small pocket in a vest

Examples

He kept his grandfather's watch in his vest pocket for safekeeping.
The tailor added a decorative lining to the vest pocket of the suit.
She found an old photograph tucked inside the vest pocket.
