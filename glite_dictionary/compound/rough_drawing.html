rough drawing

1. NOUN

/ rʌf ˈdrɔɪŋ / (AmE), / rʌf ˈdrɔːɪŋ / (BrE)
a preliminary sketch made without fine details, used to outline the basic elements of a design or picture
preliminary, informal sketch

Examples

She began her project with a rough drawing before working on the final piece.
The architect showed us a rough drawing to illustrate his initial ideas.
Please submit a rough drawing of your design for approval.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sketch - a simple, quickly-made drawing or painting
