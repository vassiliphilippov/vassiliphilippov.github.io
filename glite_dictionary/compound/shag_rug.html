shag rug

1. NOUN

/ ʃæɡ rʌɡ / (AmE), / ʃaɡ rʌɡ / (BrE)
a rug with a deep, thick pile that is especially long and shaggy in appearance
rug with long, thick pile

Examples

The living room was decorated with a bright orange shag rug in the center.
After years of use, the shag rug had lost some of its original softness.
She vacuumed the thick shag rug to keep it clean and fluffy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpet - a floor covering made from thick woven fabric

Etymology

From 'shag' referring to long rough hair or nap, and 'rug'.
