golden star

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən stɑr / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən stɑː / (BrE)
A California plant (Bloomeria crocea) with grasslike leaves and showy orange-yellow flowers, often found in dry, open areas.
California plant with orange flowers

Examples

The hillsides were dotted with blooming golden star during springtime hikes.
Gardeners in California sometimes plant golden star for its ornamental value.
She identified the wildflower as golden star by its distinct orange blossoms.
