Little Missouri

1. PROPN

/ ˌlɪt̬.əl məˈzɜːr.i / (AmE), / ˌlɪt.əl məˈzʊə.ri / (BrE)
a river in the northern United States that flows from Wyoming through Montana and South Dakota to join the Missouri River in North Dakota
river in Wyoming, Montana, South Dakota, and North Dakota

Examples

The Little Missouri winds through the scenic badlands of North Dakota.
We camped along the Little Missouri during our road trip last summer.
The Little Missouri is known for its clear waters and beautiful landscapes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Missouri River - major river in the central United States
