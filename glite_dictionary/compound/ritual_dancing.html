ritual dancing

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪtʃuəl ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃuəl ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
a dance performed as part of a religious or ceremonial ritual
dance as part of a ritual or ceremony

Examples

The tribe performed ritual dancing to celebrate the harvest festival.
Many cultures have forms of ritual dancing that mark transitions such as weddings or funerals.
The film explores the significance of ritual dancing in indigenous spiritual practices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ceremonial dance - a dance performed as part of a ceremony
