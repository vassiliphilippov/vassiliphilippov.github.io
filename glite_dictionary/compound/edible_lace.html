edible lace

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛdəbəl leɪs / (AmE), / ˈɛdɪbl leɪs / (BrE)
a decorative, lace-like sheet or pattern made from edible ingredients, used to adorn cakes and desserts
decorative lace made from edible ingredients
