profit sharing

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːfɪt ˌʃɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɒfɪt ˌʃeərɪŋ / (BrE)
a system by which employees receive a share of a company's net profits, typically as part of their compensation
system of sharing company profits with employees

Examples

The company introduced profit sharing to motivate its employees and boost productivity.
With profit sharing, workers have a direct financial stake in the company's success.
Many businesses have adopted profit sharing as a way to reward long-term employee loyalty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bonus - extra payment to employees based on company performance
  • Synonym: commission - payment to employees based on sales or performance
