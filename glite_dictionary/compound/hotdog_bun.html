hotdog bun

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑtˌdɔɡ bʌn / (AmE), / ˈhɒtˌdɒɡ bʌn / (BrE)
a soft, elongated bread roll specially shaped to hold a hotdog or frankfurter
bread roll for holding a hotdog

Examples

I bought some hotdog buns for our picnic this weekend.
He carefully toasted the hotdog bun before placing the sausage inside.
A freshly baked hotdog bun makes all the difference to a good hotdog.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hotdog - a cooked sausage served in a long bun
  • Degree Higher: bread roll - a small, usually round or oblong individual loaf of bread
