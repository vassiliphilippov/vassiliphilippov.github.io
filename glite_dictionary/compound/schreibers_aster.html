Schreiber's aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃraɪbɚz ˈæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃraɪbəz ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
A species of aster (Eurybia schreberi), a perennial wildflower native to parts of North America, with clusters of small lavender or white flowers.
a species of North American aster

Examples

The meadow was dotted with the pale flowers of Schreiber's aster in late summer.
Botanists study Schreiber's aster to better understand its role in woodland ecosystems.
She carefully identified the plant as Schreiber's aster during the nature walk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a common name for flowering plants in the family Asteraceae, typically with star-shaped flower heads
