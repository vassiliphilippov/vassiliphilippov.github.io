blow drier

1. NOUN

/ ˈbloʊ ˌdraɪər / (AmE), / ˈbləʊ ˌdraɪə / (BrE)
A handheld electric device that blows warm air to dry and style hair.
handheld hair-drying device

Examples

She used a blow drier to finish styling her hair before the meeting.
After his shower, he grabbed the blow drier to quickly dry his hair.
Some hotels provide a blow drier in every bathroom for guest convenience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair dryer - a device for drying hair by blowing warm air
