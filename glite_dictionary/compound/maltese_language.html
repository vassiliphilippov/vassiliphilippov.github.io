Maltese language

1. NOUN

/ mɔlˈtiz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / mɔːlˈtiːz ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the national language of Malta, a Semitic language influenced by Italian, Spanish, and Norman-French
national language of Malta

Examples

Although English is widely spoken in Malta, the official language is Maltese language.
She is fluent in the Maltese language thanks to her family heritage.
The unique blend of Arabic roots and European vocabulary makes the Maltese language fascinating to linguists.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Arabic - a Central Semitic language closely related to Maltese
  • Degree Higher: Italian - the Romance language greatly influencing Maltese vocabulary

Etymology

Maltese from Malta + -ese; language from Latin lingua (tongue, speech).
