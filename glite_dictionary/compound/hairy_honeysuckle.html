hairy honeysuckle

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛri ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (AmE), / ˈheəri ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (BrE)
A twining deciduous shrub native to northeastern America, with hairy leaves and spikes of yellow-orange flowers.
a North American shrub with hairy leaves and yellow-orange flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with fragrant hairy honeysuckle climbing along the fence.
We identified the shrub as hairy honeysuckle by its distinctive yellow-orange blooms.
Many birds are attracted to hairy honeysuckle when it flowers in early summer.
