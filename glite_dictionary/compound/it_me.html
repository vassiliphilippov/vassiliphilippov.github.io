it me

1. X

/ ɪt mi / (AmE), / ɪt mi / (BrE)
(informal, internet) Used to express that something strongly resembles or accurately describes oneself.
internet phrase for identifying with something
informal slang

Examples

Whenever I see a cat lying around doing nothing all day, I just think, it me.
That tweet about forgetting your keys again? It me.
When someone mentions staying up too late watching TV, my only response is it me.

Synset Examples

The meme about procrastination? Yeah, it me.
When I heard the news, I thought, 'it me.'
