inchmeal information

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪntʃˌmil ˌɪnfərˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈɪntʃˌmiːl ˌɪnfəˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
Information delivered gradually or bit by bit, rather than all at once.
information given piece by piece

Examples

The manager shared the project updates as inchmeal information, leaving the team eager for more details.
She disliked receiving inchmeal information and preferred to know everything upfront.
The investigation released inchmeal information to the public to control rumors and speculation.

Synset Examples

He preferred inchmeal information rather than a big reveal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piecemeal - In separate pieces; bit by bit

Etymology

Formed from 'inchmeal' (by inches, gradually) + 'information'. 'Inchmeal' from inch + Old English mǣlum (by measure, piece by piece).
