bald-headed

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɔldˌhɛdəd / (AmE), / ˈbɔːldˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
Having little or no hair on the scalp; bald.
having little or no hair on the scalp

Examples

The bald-headed man smiled warmly at everyone in the room.
He wore a hat to protect his bald-headed scalp from the sun.
People often recognize the bald-headed professor from across campus.

Synset Examples

a bald pate
a bald-headed gentleman

Related Terms
