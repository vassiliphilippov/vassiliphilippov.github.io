high technology

1. NOUN

/ haɪ tɛkˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / haɪ tɛkˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
Technology that is highly advanced, especially involving sophisticated electronics or cutting-edge developments.
advanced or cutting-edge technology

Examples

The company invests heavily in high technology to stay ahead of competitors.
Many jobs in high technology sectors require specialized training and knowledge.
The region is known for its strong focus on high technology industries such as biotechnology and electronics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high tech - Abbreviation of 'high technology'; advanced technological development
