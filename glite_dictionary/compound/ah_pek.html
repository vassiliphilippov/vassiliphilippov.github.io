Ah Pek

1. NOUN

/ ɑ ˈpɛk / (AmE), / ɑː ˈpɛk / (BrE)
(in Singapore) an informal term for an older man, especially one seen as a typical elderly local, often used with a tone of familiarity or affection.
older man (Singapore colloquial)
informal

Examples

The Ah Pek at the coffee shop greeted everyone with a big smile.
After retiring, he started dressing just like a typical Ah Pek.
Some people say I walk like an Ah Pek when I'm tired.

Synset Examples

The Ah Pek loves to share his fishing stories.
He's the typical Ah Pek who enjoys his morning tea.
Everyone in the neighborhood knows the friendly Ah Pek.

Etymology

From Hokkien Chinese ah (a familiar prefix) + pek (uncle or old man).
