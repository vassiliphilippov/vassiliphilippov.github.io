oasis in the city

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈeɪsɪs ɪn ðə ˈsɪti / (AmE), / əʊˈeɪsɪs ɪn ðə ˈsɪti / (BrE)
a peaceful or restful place offering relief from the noise and activity of city life
peaceful spot in a busy city

Examples

The central park became an oasis in the city for people needing a break from traffic and noise.
Her rooftop garden was an oasis in the city, filled with flowers and quiet corners.
Many coffee shops try to be an oasis in the city for urban dwellers seeking relaxation.

Synset Examples

The garden felt like an oasis in the bustling city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oasis - a place in a desert with water and plants
