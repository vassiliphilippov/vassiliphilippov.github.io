common man

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən mæn / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən mæn / (BrE)
an ordinary male person without any title, rank, or special privileges
an ordinary male person

Examples

The politician claimed to represent the interests of the common man.
Literature often depicts the struggles of the common man in society.
He considered himself just a common man with modest ambitions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: everyman - an ordinary or typical person
