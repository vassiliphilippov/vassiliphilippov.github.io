search and destroy mission

1. NOUN

/ sɜrtʃ ənd dɪˈstrɔɪ ˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / sɜːtʃ ənd dɪˈstrɔɪ ˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
a military operation in which troops move through an area to locate and eliminate enemy forces, particularly associated with United States strategy during the Vietnam War
Vietnam War military operation

Examples

The soldiers were briefed on their search and destroy mission before heading into the dense jungle.
During the Vietnam War, search and destroy missions often resulted in fierce combat with hidden enemy forces.
Their unit participated in a search and destroy mission to clear Viet Cong fighters from the area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patrol - an expedition by a military unit to observe or secure an area
