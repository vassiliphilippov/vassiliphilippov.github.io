charity shot

1. NOUN

/ ˈt͡ʃɛrəti ʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈtʃærɪti ʃɒt / (BrE)
A basketball shot taken from the free-throw line, awarded due to a foul by the opposing team.
free throw in basketball

Examples

After the foul, he calmly made both charity shots to tie the game.
Missing a charity shot in the final seconds cost them the victory.
She practiced charity shots every day to improve her accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: free throw - an unhindered shot from the free-throw line in basketball, awarded after certain fouls
