armamentary stockpile

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːrməˌmɛntəri ˈstɑːkpaɪl / (AmE), / ˌɑːməˈmɛntəri ˈstɒkpaɪl / (BrE)
A collection or reserve of weapons or military supplies, often maintained by a government or organization.
reserve of weapons or military supplies

Examples

The country decided to reduce its armamentary stockpile as part of the new treaty.
Inspectors were sent to verify the existing armamentary stockpile in the region.
The government's armamentary stockpile is kept under strict security protocols.

Synset Examples

The discussions focused on reducing the armamentary stockpile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arsenal - a collection of weapons and military equipment
  • Synonym: stockpile - a large accumulated stock of goods or materials

Etymology

From 'armamentary' (relating to weapons or military equipment) and 'stockpile' (a large accumulated stock of goods or materials).
