electrical plant

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl plænt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl plɑːnt / (BrE)
a facility where electricity is generated and supplied as a utility
facility for generating electricity

Examples

The new electrical plant will provide power to the entire region.
After the storm, engineers worked to restore operations at the main electrical plant.
Many jobs were created during the construction of the electrical plant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power station - a facility where electrical power is generated
  • Synonym: power plant - industrial site for generating electricity
