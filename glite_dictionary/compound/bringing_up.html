bringing up

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪŋɪŋ ʌp / (AmE), / ˈbrɪŋɪŋ ʌp / (BrE)
the process of caring for a child and teaching them how to behave as a member of society; upbringing
the act of raising a child; upbringing

Examples

Her strict bringing up taught her the value of hard work.
He often spoke about his rural bringing up and how it shaped him.
They owe their manners to their parents' careful bringing up.

Synset Examples

they debated whether nature or nurture was more important

Related Terms

  • Synonym: upbringing - the way in which a child is cared for and taught to behave while growing up
