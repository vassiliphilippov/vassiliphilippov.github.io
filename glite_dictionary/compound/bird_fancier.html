bird fancier

1. NOUN

/ bɝd ˈfænsiɚ / (AmE), / bɜːd ˈfænsiə / (BrE)
a person who has a strong interest in and often keeps or breeds birds, especially as a hobby
person interested in keeping or breeding birds

Examples

The local club for bird fanciers meets every Saturday to share tips and advice.
As a lifelong bird fancier, she enjoyed raising parrots and canaries in her home.
Many bird fanciers travel long distances to attend exotic bird shows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ornithologist - a scientist who studies birds
