fauxnetic smile

1. NOUN

/ foʊˈnɛtɪk smaɪl / (AmE), / fəʊˈnɛtɪk smaɪl / (BrE)
A fake smile that appears sincere and genuine.
fake smile that looks real

Examples

She greeted her guests with a fauxnetic smile, masking her nervousness.
His fauxnetic smile fooled most of his colleagues during the tense meeting.
It's easy to spot a fauxnetic smile if you pay close attention to the eyes.

Synset Examples

The actor's fauxnetic smile hid his true feelings.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: genuine smile - a truly sincere smile expressing real emotion
  • Synonym: fake smile - a smile made without feeling real happiness

Etymology

Blend of 'faux' (French for 'false') and 'phonetic', modeled on 'phonetic smile'.
