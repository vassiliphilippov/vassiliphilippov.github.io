dial phone

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪəl foʊn / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəl fəʊn / (BrE)
a telephone equipped with a rotary dial for entering telephone numbers manually
telephone with a rotary dial

Examples

My grandparents still have a dial phone in their hallway that they use every day.
The movie featured a character hurriedly spinning the numbers on a dial phone.
Before touch-tone phones became common, everyone used a dial phone to make calls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rotary phone - a telephone using a rotating dial to select numbers
