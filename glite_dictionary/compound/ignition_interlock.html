ignition interlock

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˈnɪʃən ˈɪntərlɑk / (AmE), / ɪɡˈnɪʃən ˈɪntəlɒk / (BrE)
a device installed in a vehicle's ignition system that prevents the engine from starting unless certain conditions are met
device preventing a vehicle from starting

Examples

The police ordered him to install an ignition interlock after his second DUI offense.
Modern cars often come equipped with an ignition interlock to prevent theft.
If the ignition interlock detects alcohol, the vehicle won’t start.

Synset Examples

car thieves know how to bypass the ignition interlock

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immobilizer - device that prevents a vehicle from being started by unauthorized people
