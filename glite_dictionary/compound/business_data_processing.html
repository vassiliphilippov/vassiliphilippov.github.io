business data processing

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznɪs ˈdeɪtə ˌprɑsɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪznəs ˈdeɪtə ˌprɒsɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
the handling and manipulation of data related to accounting or business management by computers or automated systems
data management in business or accounting

Examples

Many companies invest heavily in business data processing systems to improve efficiency and accuracy.
The accountant oversees the business data processing required for financial reporting.
Advances in business data processing have transformed how organizations handle information.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: data processing - the automated processing of information
