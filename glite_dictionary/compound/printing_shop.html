printing shop

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪntɪŋ ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈprɪntɪŋ ʃɒp / (BrE)
a place of business where printing work is carried out, such as producing books, newspapers, or brochures
business where printing is done

Examples

She picked up the flyers she ordered from the printing shop downtown.
The printing shop offers discounts for students and non-profits.
He started working part-time at a local printing shop to earn extra money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: print shop - business where printing is done
