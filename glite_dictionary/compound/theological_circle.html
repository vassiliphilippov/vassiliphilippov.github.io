theological circle

1. NOUN

/ ˌθi.əˈlɑ.dʒɪ.kəl ˈsɝ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌθɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˈsɜː.kəl / (BrE)
a group or community of people engaged in discussion or study of theology
group focused on theology

Examples

The debate over Christian sharia continues in theological circles.
Certain doctrines are fiercely contested in theological circles across denominations.
Many new ideas about interfaith dialogue originate within theological circles.
