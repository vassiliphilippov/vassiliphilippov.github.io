time-tried advice

1. NOUN

/ taɪm-traɪd ædˈvaɪs / (AmE), / taɪm-traɪd ədˈvʌɪs / (BrE)
advice that has been proven effective or reliable over a long period of time.
advice proven effective by experience

Examples

She relied on her grandmother's time-tried advice when making difficult decisions.
Many people turn to time-tried advice during moments of uncertainty.
It's wise to remember time-tried advice handed down through the generations.

Synset Examples

The time-tried advice helped me through tough times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wisdom - knowledge and good judgment based on experience
  • Antonym: old wives' tale - a traditional belief, often incorrect, passed down through generations
