academic administrator

1. NOUN

/ əˈkædəmɪk ədˌmɪnəˈstreɪtər / (AmE), / əˈkadəmɪk ədˌmɪnɪsˈtreɪtə / (BrE)
a person responsible for managing and overseeing academic affairs and operations in a college or university
college or university manager

Examples

The academic administrator addressed the faculty regarding changes in curriculum policy.
She aspired to become an academic administrator after years of teaching experience.
Every academic administrator plays a vital role in the effective functioning of the university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: administrator - a person responsible for running a business, organization, etc.
