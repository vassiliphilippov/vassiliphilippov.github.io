asparagus pea

1. NOUN

/ əˈspær.ə.ɡəs pi / (AmE), / əˈspær.ə.ɡəs piː / (BrE)
A European annual plant (Lotus tetragonolobus) with sprawling growth and distinctive 4-winged edible pods, cultivated as a vegetable.
European plant with edible 4-winged pods

Examples

We planted asparagus pea seeds in the garden for their unusual pods.
The asparagus pea yields crisp, edible pods that are delicious when stir-fried.
Fresh asparagus pea can be found at specialty markets in early summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pea - a small round green seed, or the plant that produces it
