real estate broker

1. NOUN

/ ˌriːəl ɪˈsteɪt ˈbroʊkər / (AmE), / ˌrɪəl ɪˈsteɪt ˈbrəʊkə / (BrE)
A person licensed to act as an agent in the sale, purchase, or rental of real property.
licensed agent for property sales

Examples

After passing her exams, she became a licensed real estate broker and opened her own agency.
The real estate broker arranged several property viewings for the interested buyers.
If you want to sell your house quickly, hiring a real estate broker is often a smart decision.

Synset Examples

in England they call a real estate agent a ‘land agent’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: estate agent - British term for an agent who arranges the sale or rental of property
