anatomical reference

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.əˈtɑː.mɪ.kəl ˈrɛf.ɚ.əns / (AmE), / ˌæn.əˈtɒm.ɪ.kəl ˈrɛf.ər.əns / (BrE)
an expression or term that relates to the structure of the human or animal body
expression relating to body anatomy
medical

Examples

The doctor used the anatomical reference 'distal' to explain the location of the injury.
Medical textbooks are filled with anatomical references to different parts of the body.
Understanding each anatomical reference is crucial for students in anatomy class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anatomical term - a word or phrase used to describe a part or aspect of anatomy

Etymology

From Greek 'anatomē' (dissection) and Latin 'referre' (to bring back); related to technical medical language.
