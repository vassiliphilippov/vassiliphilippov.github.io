stone dead

1. ADJ

/ stoʊn dɛd / (AmE), / stəʊn dɛd / (BrE)
completely dead, lifeless, or non-functional; utterly inactive or without any sign of life or movement
completely dead or inactive

Examples

When paramedics arrived, they found the patient was stone dead and beyond help.
The company's old website was stone dead after the server crash.
His cellphone went stone dead in the middle of an important call.

Synset Examples

The project was stone dead due to lack of funding.
After the fall, he lay stone dead on the ground.
The engine is stone dead and won't start.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dead - no longer alive; lacking power or function
