in jest

1. ADV

/ ɪn d͡ʒɛst / (AmE), / ɪn d͡ʒɛst / (BrE)
spoken or done as a joke; not intended to be taken seriously.
as a joke; not seriously

Examples

She made the remark in jest, not expecting anyone to believe her.
His offer to trade houses was clearly in jest.
Please remember that my earlier comment was meant in jest, not to offend anyone.

Synset Examples

She always called her friend a bitch in jest.
In jest, he referred to his buddy as faggotface.
In jest, they called him Trumpolini for his bravado.
I said it in jest, but he took it seriously.
He made the comment in jest, not meaning any harm.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: seriously - in a serious manner
  • Synonym: jokingly - in a joking manner
