centigrade thermometer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛn.tɪˌɡreɪd θərˈmɑː.mə.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.tɪˌɡreɪd θəˈmɒ.mɪ.tə / (BrE)
a thermometer that is calibrated in degrees Celsius (centigrade), used to measure temperature
thermometer with Celsius scale

Examples

The scientist used a centigrade thermometer to measure the temperature of the solution.
Most laboratories now prefer a centigrade thermometer over a Fahrenheit one for accuracy and consistency.
Please check if the centigrade thermometer is properly calibrated before starting the experiment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Celsius thermometer - a thermometer calibrated in Celsius degrees
  • Antonym: Fahrenheit thermometer - a thermometer calibrated in Fahrenheit degrees

Etymology

From 'centigrade', an old name for the Celsius temperature scale, and 'thermometer', from Greek 'thermos' (hot) and 'metron' (measure).
