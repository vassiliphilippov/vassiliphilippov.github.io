young woman

1. NOUN

/ jʌŋ ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / jʌŋ ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a female person who is young, typically in her teens or twenties
a female who is young

Examples

Several young women volunteered for the environmental project.
A talented young woman was chosen to lead the research team.
