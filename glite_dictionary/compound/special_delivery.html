special delivery

1. NOUN

/ ˌspɛʃəl dɪˈlɪvəri / (AmE), / ˌspɛʃəl dɪˈlɪvəri / (BrE)
a postal service for delivering mail more quickly than usual, often for an extra fee
post delivery service for faster mail

Examples

The important documents were sent by special delivery to ensure they arrived the next day.
He paid extra for special delivery because the package was urgent.
We received the invitation via special delivery this morning.
