gay lib

1. NOUN

/ ɡeɪ lɪb / (AmE), / ɡeɪ lɪb / (BrE)
the movement advocating for the rights and liberation of homosexual people from legal, social, and economic oppression
movement for gay liberation

Examples

The gay lib movement transformed public attitudes toward homosexuality in the 1970s.
Many activists marched in support of gay lib and campaigned for equal rights.
Early gay lib meetings were often met with both enthusiasm and fierce opposition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gay liberation - the broader movement for homosexual rights, especially in the 1970s

Etymology

Shortening of 'gay liberation', first used in the late 1960s and 1970s.
