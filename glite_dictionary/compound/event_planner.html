event planner

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈvɛnt ˌplænər / (AmE), / ɪˈvɛnt ˌplænə / (BrE)
a person whose profession is organizing and coordinating social or professional events for clients
person who organizes events professionally

Examples

The company hired an event planner to organize its annual conference.
As an event planner, she needs to pay attention to every detail to ensure a smooth event.
Many couples turn to a professional event planner for help with their wedding arrangements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organizer - a person who makes arrangements and preparations for an event or activity
  • Synonym: event manager - a person responsible for planning and executing events
