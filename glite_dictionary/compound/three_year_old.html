three year old

1. NOUN

/ θri jɪr oʊld / (AmE), / θriː jə əʊld / (BrE)
(in horse racing) a horse that is three years old, especially as an age category in races.
a three-year-old racehorse

Examples

The Kentucky Derby is only open to three year old horses.
That three year old showed impressive speed during the race.
She trains several three year old thoroughbreds for the upcoming season.
