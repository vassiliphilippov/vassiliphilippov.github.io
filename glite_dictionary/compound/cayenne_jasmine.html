cayenne jasmine

1. NOUN

/ kaɪˈɛn ˈdʒæzmɪn / (AmE), / kaɪˈɛn ˈdʒæzmɪn / (BrE)
a woody plant native to the Old World and widely cultivated for its large pink or red fragrant flowers
ornamental woody plant with pink to red flowers

Examples

The cayenne jasmine is prized for its vivid pink flowers and sweet fragrance.
Gardeners often plant cayenne jasmine along fences for a splash of color in spring.
A bouquet of cayenne jasmine can fill a room with its delightful scent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jasmine - any of various shrubs and vines with fragrant, often white, flowers
