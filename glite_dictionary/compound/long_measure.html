long measure

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋ ˈmɛʒər / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈmɛʒə / (BrE)
a system of units for measuring length, especially in British use, such as inches, feet, yards, and miles, as distinct from measures of capacity or weight.
system of length measurement

Examples

A yard is a common unit in the imperial long measure system.
The surveyor used long measure to determine the total distance between the two points.
Unlike liquid measure, long measure is used for calculating length rather than volume.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: linear measure - measurement of length (synonym for 'long measure')
