breast of veal

1. NOUN

/ brɛst əv viːl / (AmE), / brɛst əv viːl / (BrE)
the cut of meat from the front chest area of a calf, usually cooked by stuffing and roasting
cut of meat from a calf's chest

Examples

The chef prepared a delicious breast of veal stuffed with herbs and breadcrumbs.
During the holidays, my grandmother always roasts a breast of veal for the family dinner.
He ordered breast of veal from the menu, served with roasted vegetables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: veal - meat from a young calf
