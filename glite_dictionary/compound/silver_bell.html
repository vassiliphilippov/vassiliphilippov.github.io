silver bell

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ bɛl / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə bɛl / (BrE)
any of various North American deciduous trees of the genus Halesia, having white, bell-shaped flowers
deciduous tree with white, bell-shaped flowers

Examples

In spring, the silver bell in our garden is covered with delicate white blossoms.
The silver bell is admired for its graceful, bell-shaped flowers and attractive foliage.
Gardeners often plant silver bell trees to add beauty and fragrance to their landscapes.
