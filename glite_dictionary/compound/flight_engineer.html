flight engineer

1. NOUN

/ flaɪt ˌɛn.dʒəˈnɪr / (AmE), / flaɪt ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə / (BrE)
an aircraft crew member who is responsible for monitoring and operating mechanical and technical systems during a flight
aircraft crew member managing technical systems

Examples

The flight engineer carefully monitored the plane’s engines throughout the journey.
During emergencies, the flight engineer assists the pilots by managing system controls.
On older aircraft, a flight engineer was always present in the cockpit to oversee technical systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: engineer - a person who designs, builds, or maintains engines or machines
