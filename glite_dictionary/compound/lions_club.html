Lions Club

1. PROPN

/ ˈlaɪ.ənz klʌb / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.ənz klʌb / (BrE)
an international service organization dedicated to community service, promoting responsible citizenship, and welfare at local, national, and international levels
international service organization

Examples

The Lions Club organizes charity events and fundraisers throughout the year.
She became an active member of her local Lions Club to help improve community services.
The Lions Club is known for its international efforts to prevent blindness.

Etymology

Founded in Chicago, 1917; named after the lion as a symbol of strength and courage.
