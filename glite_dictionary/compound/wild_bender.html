wild bender

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ˈbɛndər / (AmE), / waɪld ˈbɛndə / (BrE)
an occasion or period of excessive drinking and reckless behavior, typically lasting several hours or days
excessive drinking spree
informal slang

Examples

After finals week, the students went on a wild bender to celebrate.
He woke up on Monday with no memory of the wild bender he had over the weekend.
Stories of their last wild bender still make everyone laugh.

Synset Examples

Last weekend was a wild bender for them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bender - a period of heavy drinking
