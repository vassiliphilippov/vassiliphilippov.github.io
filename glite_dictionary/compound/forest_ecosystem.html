forest ecosystem

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrɪst ˌiːkoʊˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈfɒrɪst ˌiːkəʊˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a community of living organisms, such as plants, animals, and microorganisms, interacting with each other and their physical environment in a forest
community of organisms in a forest

Examples

The health of a forest ecosystem depends on biodiversity and proper management.
Climate change poses significant challenges to the stability of many forest ecosystems.
Protecting predators is crucial for maintaining balance within a forest ecosystem.

Synset Examples

Research on fomes helps us understand forest ecosystems better.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecosystem - a community of organisms and their environment functioning together
