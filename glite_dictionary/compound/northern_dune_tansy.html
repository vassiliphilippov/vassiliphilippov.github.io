northern dune tansy

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðərn duːn ˈtænzi / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən djuːn ˈtænzi / (BrE)
a perennial plant of the Pacific coast of North America, with aromatic, feathery leaves and open clusters of small, yellow, buttonlike flowers, typically found in sand dunes
a Pacific coast perennial plant with yellow buttonlike flowers

Examples

The northern dune tansy thrives in the sandy soils along Washington’s coastal dunes.
Botanists are working to protect remaining northern dune tansy populations due to habitat loss.
She identified the fragrant, feathery leaves as those of the northern dune tansy.
