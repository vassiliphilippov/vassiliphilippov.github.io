pillow talk

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪloʊ tɔk / (AmE), / ˈpɪləʊ tɔːk / (BrE)
intimate and private conversation between lovers, especially when in bed together
intimate conversation between lovers

Examples

After a long day, they enjoyed some pillow talk before falling asleep.
Their relationship deepened through honest pillow talk about their dreams and fears.
She shared her happiest memories with him during their nightly pillow talk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conversation - an informal exchange of ideas by spoken words
