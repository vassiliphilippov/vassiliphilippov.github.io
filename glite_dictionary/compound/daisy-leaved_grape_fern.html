daisy-leaved grape fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪzi livd ˈɡreɪp fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈdeɪzi liːvd ˈɡreɪp fɜːn / (BrE)
a grape fern (Botrychium matricariifolium) of North America and Eurasia, known for its daisy-like leaves
grape fern with daisy-like leaves

Examples

The daisy-leaved grape fern thrives in moist woodlands across North America and Eurasia.
She collected a specimen of daisy-leaved grape fern for her botany project.
Unlike some other ferns, the daisy-leaved grape fern has leaves that resemble those of a daisy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grape fern - a type of fern of the genus Botrychium
