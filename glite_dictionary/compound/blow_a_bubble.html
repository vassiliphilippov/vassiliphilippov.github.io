to blow a bubble

1. VERB

/ bloʊ ə ˈbʌbəl / (AmE), / bləʊ ə ˈbʌbəl / (BrE)
to form a bubble, especially from soap, by blowing air through a thin film
form a bubble by blowing air

Examples

She blew a bubble so large it popped on the ceiling.
Whenever he blows a bubble, the children all try to catch it.
The little girl giggled as she blew a bubble and watched it float away.

Synset Examples

He blew a bubble that floated high in the air.
