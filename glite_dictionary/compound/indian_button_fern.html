Indian button fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən ˈbʌtn fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈbʌtn fɜːn / (BrE)
A fern native to tropical Asia, characterized by small, round, buttonlike bulbils.
fern with buttonlike bulbils, native to Asia

Examples

The Indian button fern thrives in shaded, humid environments typical of Southeast Asia.
Gardeners love the unique appearance of the Indian button fern for terrariums.
The Indian button fern produces small, buttonlike bulbils along its fronds.
