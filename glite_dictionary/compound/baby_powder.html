baby powder

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.bi ˌpaʊ.dər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi ˌpaʊ.də / (BrE)
a fine, soft powder applied to a baby's skin to prevent irritation and chafing, especially from diapers
soft powder for baby's skin

Examples

She sprinkled some baby powder on her newborn after the bath.
Many parents use baby powder to keep their baby's skin dry and comfortable.
The scent of baby powder always reminds me of my childhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talcum powder - soft white powder commonly used for skin and hygiene
