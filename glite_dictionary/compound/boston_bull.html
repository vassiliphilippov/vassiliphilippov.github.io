Boston bull

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑstən bʊl / (AmE), / ˈbɒstən bʊl / (BrE)
a small breed of American terrier with a short, smooth black or brindle coat and white markings, known for its pug-like face
breed of small American terrier

Examples

Her Boston bull greeted everyone at the door with an energetic bark.
The Boston bull is known for its affectionate and lively personality.
We adopted a Boston bull puppy from the animal shelter last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Boston terrier - another name for this breed of dog

Etymology

Named after the city of Boston, Massachusetts, where the breed was developed.
