deep fat fryer

1. NOUN

/ diːp fæt ˈfraɪ.ər / (AmE), / diːp fæt ˈfraɪ.ə / (BrE)
an electrical kitchen appliance used for deep frying food by immersing it in hot oil
appliance for deep frying food

Examples

She prepared homemade French fries in the deep fat fryer for their family dinner.
Always use caution when operating a deep fat fryer to prevent oil splatters and burns.
The restaurant bought a new industrial deep fat fryer to keep up with customer demand.

Synset Examples

Deep fryers are used for cooking many fast foods, and making them crisp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deep fryer - appliance for deep frying food, often synonymous
  • Degree Lower: air fryer - appliance for frying food using hot air, healthier alternative
