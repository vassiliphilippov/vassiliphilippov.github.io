nut job

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌt dʒɑb / (AmE), / ˈnʌt dʒɒb / (BrE)
An informal term for a person who is considered crazy, eccentric, or mentally unstable.
crazy or eccentric person (informal)
informal

Examples

People thought he was a nut job when he tried to build a rocket in his backyard.
The internet is full of stories about some nut job claiming to have invented time travel.
His wild, unpredictable behavior made everyone suspect he was a bit of a nut job.

Synset Examples

He was labeled a nut job after his wild claims.
Everyone thinks she's a nut job for her beliefs.
His obsession with conspiracy theories makes him a nut job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: madman - a person who is insane or wildly foolish
  • Synonym: lunatic - a person who is mentally ill or wildly foolish
  • Antonym: sane - mentally healthy and sensible

Etymology

Informal, from 'nut' (slang for head or a crazy person) + 'job' (person).
