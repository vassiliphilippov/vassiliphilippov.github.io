sex change

1. NOUN

/ sɛks tʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / sɛks tʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
a procedure or process in which a person's physical sexual characteristics are altered to align with their gender identity, often involving surgery and hormone treatment
surgical and hormonal gender transition

Examples

After undergoing a sex change, she felt much happier in her daily life.
Medical advancements have made sex change procedures safer and more effective than ever before.
The documentary tells the story of a person who decided to have a sex change.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gender reassignment - the process of changing someone's sex, especially medically or legally
  • Synonym: transition - the process of changing gender presentation or sex characteristics
