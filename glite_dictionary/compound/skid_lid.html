skid lid

1. NOUN

/ skɪd lɪd / (AmE), / skɪd lɪd / (BrE)
An informal term for a crash helmet, especially as worn by motorcyclists.
informal term for a crash helmet
informal slang

Examples

He never rides his motorcycle without his skid lid securely strapped on.
Make sure you wear a skid lid if you're going on the track.
The law requires all motorcyclists to wear a skid lid for safety.
