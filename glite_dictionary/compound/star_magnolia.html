star magnolia

1. NOUN

/ stɑr məgˈnoʊliə / (AmE), / stɑː məgˈnəʊliə / (BrE)
A small, deciduous shrub or tree native to Japan, known for its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom before the leaves appear; widely grown as an ornamental plant.
ornamental shrub with star-shaped flowers

Examples

The star magnolia in our garden is covered in beautiful white blossoms every spring.
Gardeners often choose star magnolia for its early-blooming, showy flowers and compact size.
A mature star magnolia can be a stunning focal point in any landscaped yard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnolia - any plant of the genus Magnolia, known for large, showy flowers
