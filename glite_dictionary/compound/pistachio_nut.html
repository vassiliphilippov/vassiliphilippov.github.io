pistachio nut

1. NOUN

/ pɪˈstæʃioʊ nʌt / (AmE), / pɪˈstæʃiəʊ nʌt / (BrE)
the edible green seed of a Mediterranean tree, often eaten as a snack or used in cooking and baking
edible green seed from the pistachio tree

Examples

She sprinkled chopped pistachio nuts over her salad for extra flavor and crunch.
The bakery sells delicious cakes filled with pistachio nuts and honey.
I love snacking on roasted pistachio nuts while watching movies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pistachio - the tree producing pistachio nuts

Etymology

From Italian *pistacchio*, from Latin *pistacium*, from Greek *pistákion* (pistachio nut).
