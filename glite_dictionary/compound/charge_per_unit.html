charge per unit

1. NOUN

/ tʃɑrdʒ pɚ ˈjuː.nɪt / (AmE), / tʃɑːdʒ pə ˈjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
the cost or fee charged for each individual item, measure, or unit of use or consumption
cost for each individual unit

Examples

The electricity bill lists the charge per unit of power consumed each month.
If the charge per unit increases, customers will pay more for the same amount.
They compared the charge per unit for data among several mobile network providers.

Synset Examples
