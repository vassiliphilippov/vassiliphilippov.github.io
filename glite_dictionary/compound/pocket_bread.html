pocket bread

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkɪt ˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt ˌbrɛd / (BrE)
A small, round, hollow bread, typically pita, that opens into a pocket for holding fillings.
small round bread with a pocket for filling

Examples

He stuffed his pocket bread with falafel, salad, and tahini sauce.
The recipe calls for fresh pocket bread to make delicious homemade sandwiches.
You can find pocket bread in most grocery stores near the bakery section.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pita - flat round bread that forms a pocket when baked
