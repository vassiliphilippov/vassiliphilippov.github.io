improvised explosive device

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪm.prəˌvaɪzd ɪkˈsploʊ.sɪv dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈɪm.prəˌvaɪzd ɪkˈspləʊ.sɪv dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
A bomb constructed and deployed in ways other than conventional military action, often using readily available materials.
bomb made from non-standard materials

Examples

The convoy was halted after encountering an improvised explosive device on the main road.
Soldiers receive special training to identify and disarm improvised explosive devices.
Several injuries were reported when an improvised explosive device detonated in the marketplace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bomb - an explosive weapon that detonates under certain conditions
