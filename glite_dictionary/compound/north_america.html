North America

1. PROPN

/ nɔrθ əˈmɛrɪkə / (AmE), / nɔːθ əˈmɛrɪkə / (BrE)
the third-largest continent, located in the western hemisphere and connected to South America by the Isthmus of Panama
continent in the western hemisphere

Examples

The United States, Canada, and Mexico are part of North America.
Many species of migrating birds travel through North America every year.
The Rocky Mountains stretch across the western part of North America.

Etymology

From 'north' + 'America', named after Italian explorer Amerigo Vespucci.
