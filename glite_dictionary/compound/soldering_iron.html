soldering iron

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːdərɪŋ ˌaɪərn / (AmE), / ˈsɒldərɪŋ ˌaɪən / (BrE)
a hand tool with a heated metal tip used to melt and apply solder for joining metal parts together
tool for melting and applying solder

Examples

He used a soldering iron to repair the broken circuit board.
Always allow the soldering iron to cool down after use to avoid burns.
A high-quality soldering iron makes electronics projects much easier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solder - fusible metal alloy used to join metal surfaces
