Banderite faction

1. NOUN

/ bænˈdɛraɪt ˈfækʃən / (AmE), / banˈdɛrʌɪt ˈfakʃən / (BrE)
a political group that supports or adheres to the ideology of Stepan Bandera, a Ukrainian nationalist leader
political group supporting Stepan Bandera's ideology

Examples

The Banderite faction supported the controversial policy during the political upheaval.
Several members of parliament accused their opponents of colluding with the Banderite faction.
Historians have debated the influence of the Banderite faction in post-war Ukraine.

Synset Examples

The Banderite faction supported the controversial policy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Banderite - a supporter of Stepan Bandera
  • Synonym: faction - a small organized dissenting group within a larger one

Etymology

From Banderite (follower of Stepan Bandera) and faction (Latin 'factio', a group acting together).
