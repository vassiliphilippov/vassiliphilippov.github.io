food market

1. NOUN

/ ˈfuːd ˌmɑːrkɪt / (AmE), / ˈfuːd ˌmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
A market or store where food and groceries are sold to the public.
place where food and groceries are sold

Examples

The city hosts a popular food market every Saturday, featuring local produce and baked goods.
After moving to the neighborhood, Sarah quickly found her favorite food market nearby.
He went to the food market to buy fresh vegetables for dinner.

Synset Examples

the grocery store included a meat market

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grocery store - a shop that sells food and other household goods
