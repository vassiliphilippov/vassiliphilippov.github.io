fucking shit

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌkɪŋ ʃɪt / (AmE), / ˈfʌkɪŋ ʃɪt / (BrE)
A vulgar expression used to convey strong frustration or anger, especially about problems or difficult situations.
vulgar exclamation of frustration
vulgar

Examples

I can't stand all this fucking shit at work anymore.
Why does fucking shit always happen when I'm already late?
After hearing the news, he muttered, "What is this fucking shit?"
