public assistance

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪk əˈsɪstəns / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk əˈsɪstəns / (BrE)
financial aid provided by the government to individuals or families in need, especially in the form of welfare benefits
government financial aid for those in need

Examples

Many families rely on public assistance to cover basic living expenses.
Changes to public assistance programs can greatly affect vulnerable communities.
After losing his job, he had to apply for public assistance to make ends meet.

Synset Examples

she lives on welfare

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welfare - government assistance given as financial aid to those in need
