skilled helmsman

1. NOUN

/ skɪld ˈhɛlmzmən / (AmE), / skɪld ˈhɛlmzmən / (BrE)
a person who is highly proficient at steering a ship or boat
expert at steering ships

Examples

The skilled helmsman guided the ship safely through the narrow channel.
Only a skilled helmsman could have navigated the vessel during such a fierce storm.
She aspired to be recognized as a skilled helmsman among her peers at sea.

Synset Examples

He trained to become a skilled helmsman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helmsman - a person who steers a ship or boat
  • Synonym: captain - the person in command of a ship
