smooth plane

1. NOUN

/ smuð pleɪn / (AmE), / smuːð pleɪn / (BrE)
a small hand tool used by woodworkers for giving a smooth finish to wood surfaces
woodworking tool for smoothing surfaces

Examples

He used a smooth plane to finish the surface of the table.
After sanding, she picked up a smooth plane to create a flawless finish on the wood.
A skilled carpenter always keeps his smooth plane sharp for best results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: block plane - a small hand plane for trimming and finishing wood
