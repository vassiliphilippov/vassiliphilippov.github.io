punch bag

1. NOUN

/ pʌntʃ bæɡ / (AmE), / pʌntʃ bæɡ / (BrE)
A strong bag or ball, usually suspended, which is punched repeatedly for exercise or boxing training.
bag for boxing training

Examples

The boxer trained for hours by hitting the punch bag in the gym.
She bought a punch bag to improve her strength and relieve stress at home.
After a long day, he took out his frustration on the punch bag hanging in the garage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punching bag - bag used for boxing practice
