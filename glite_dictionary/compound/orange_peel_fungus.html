orange peel fungus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ piːl ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ piːl ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a brightly colored cup- or saucer-shaped fungus, typically orange, resembling the appearance of an orange peel
a bright orange cup-shaped fungus

Examples

We spotted an orange peel fungus growing among the moss on the forest floor.
The orange peel fungus gets its name from its vivid color and texture.
Photographers are often drawn to the striking appearance of the orange peel fungus.
