oil trash

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl træʃ / (AmE), / ɔɪl træʃ / (BrE)
waste or debris consisting of or contaminated by oil, especially resulting from oil spills or leakage
waste or debris related to oil spills

Examples

The volunteers collected several bags of oil trash after the tanker accident.
Birds often mistake oil trash for food, which can be fatal to them.
Disposing of oil trash requires special procedures to prevent further environmental harm.

Synset Examples

The team organized a cleanup to remove oil trash from the water.
They found oil trash littered along the beach.
Cleaning up oil trash is crucial for marine life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oil spill - release of liquid petroleum into the environment
