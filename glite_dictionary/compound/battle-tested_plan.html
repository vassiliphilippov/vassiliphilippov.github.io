battle-tested plan

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæt̬l ˌtɛstɪd plæn / (AmE), / ˈbætl ˌtɛstɪd plan / (BrE)
a plan that has been proven effective through practical experience, especially in challenging or demanding circumstances
plan proven effective through experience

Examples

The CEO implemented a battle-tested plan to lead the company through the crisis.
Our team relied on a battle-tested plan developed from years of experience.
After several failures, they finally adopted a battle-tested plan that delivered results.

Synset Examples

Our battle-tested plans will guide us through.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proven strategy - a method or plan shown to be successful
  • Antonym: untested plan - a plan not yet proven through experience

Etymology

Formed from 'battle', meaning conflict or struggle, and 'tested', from Old French 'tester', meaning to evaluate.
