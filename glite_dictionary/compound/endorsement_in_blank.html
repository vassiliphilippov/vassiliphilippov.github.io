endorsement in blank

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdɔrs.mənt ɪn blæŋk / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɔːsmənt ɪn blaŋk / (BrE)
an endorsement on a negotiable instrument that does not specify a payee, making it payable to the bearer
unspecified endorsement on negotiable paper
legal

Examples

The check was transferred by means of an endorsement in blank, making it payable to whoever held it.
By signing an endorsement in blank, he allowed the note to be freely negotiable.
An endorsement in blank carries a higher risk of loss, as it makes the instrument payable to bearer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blank endorsement - an endorsement on a negotiable instrument that makes it payable to bearer
