punching ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌntʃɪŋ bɔl / (AmE), / ˈpʌntʃɪŋ bɔːl / (BrE)
an inflated ball or bag suspended so it can be punched for boxing training or exercise
inflated ball for boxing training

Examples

He practiced his jabs and hooks on the punching ball for an hour each day.
The gym installed a new punching ball next to the weights area.
Children enjoyed hitting the punching ball at the local recreation center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punching bag - a larger padded bag used for boxing and martial arts training
