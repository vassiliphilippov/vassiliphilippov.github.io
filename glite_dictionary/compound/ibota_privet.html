ibota privet

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈboʊtə ˈprɪvɪt / (AmE), / aɪˈbəʊtə ˈprɪvɪt / (BrE)
A fast-growing, densely branched hybrid shrub of Ligustrum ovalifolium and Ligustrum obtusifolium, often used for hedges.
hybrid privet shrub used in hedges

Examples

Gardeners often choose ibota privet for its ability to create dense, attractive hedges.
The rapid growth of ibota privet makes it ideal for privacy screens in suburban gardens.
During spring, the ibota privet produces small white flowers amid its glossy green leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: privet - any of various shrubs of the genus Ligustrum

Etymology

From Japanese 'ibota' referring to Ligustrum species, and 'privet', a common name for shrubs in the genus Ligustrum.
