giant puffball

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt ˈpʌfˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈpʌfˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a very large edible white fungus, typically spherical and sometimes reaching up to 2 feet in diameter, known for its edible qualities when young
very large edible white fungus

Examples

We found a giant puffball in the field, and it was nearly the size of a basketball.
The chef sliced the giant puffball and fried it for a unique, mushroom-flavored dish.
During the autumn hike, she spotted a giant puffball growing under the old oak tree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puffball - a type of fungus that releases spores in a burst when mature
