flying gecko

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈɡɛkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈɡɛkəʊ / (BrE)
a gecko with skin flaps along its sides, limbs, and tail that allow it to glide short distances between trees
gliding gecko species

Examples

The flying gecko can leap from tree to tree by spreading its flaps of skin.
Researchers observed a flying gecko gliding gracefully through the rainforest.
Unlike other lizards, the flying gecko uses its unique adaptations to cover long horizontal distances.
