adobe brick

1. NOUN

/ əˈdoʊbi brɪk / (AmE), / əˈdəʊbi brɪk / (BrE)
A brick made from clay and straw, dried in the sun rather than fired, used especially in hot, dry climates.
sun-dried clay brick

Examples

Traditional houses in the region are constructed from adobe brick to keep them cool in the summer.
The builder chose adobe brick for its natural insulation properties and rustic appearance.
You can see walls made of adobe brick throughout the historic part of the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adobe - sun-dried brick material

Etymology

From Spanish 'adobe', from Arabic 'aṭ-ṭūb' (the brick).
