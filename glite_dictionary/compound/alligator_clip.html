alligator clip

1. NOUN

/ ˈælɪˌɡeɪtər klɪp / (AmE), / ˈælɪˌɡeɪtə klɪp / (BrE)
a spring-loaded metal clip with serrated jaws, used to make temporary electrical connections
spring-loaded metal electrical clip

Examples

He attached the alligator clip to the battery terminal for a quick test.
Each student received a wire with an alligator clip at both ends for the experiment.
Be careful not to let the alligator clip touch anything else while it’s connected.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crocodile clip - spring-loaded electrical clip, chiefly British term

Etymology

Named for its jaws resembling an alligator’s mouth.
