stuff shot

1. NOUN

/ stʌf ʃɑt / (AmE), / stʌf ʃɒt / (BrE)
A basketball move in which the ball is forcefully propelled downward through the basket, also known as a slam dunk.
basketball shot forced downward into basket

Examples

The crowd cheered wildly when he executed a powerful stuff shot in the final seconds.
Her ability to make a stuff shot sets her apart from other players.
He amazed everyone with a reverse stuff shot during the all-star game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slam dunk - a forceful shot where the ball is thrust downward into the basket
