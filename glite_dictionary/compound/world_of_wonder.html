world of wonder

1. NOUN

/ wɝːld əv ˈwʌndɚ / (AmE), / wɜːld əv ˈwʌndə / (BrE)
a place or situation filled with exciting or amazing things, inspiring awe or delight
place or situation full of exciting or amazing things

Examples

The theme park was truly a world of wonder for children and adults alike.
Her stories transport listeners into a world of wonder and imagination.
Stepping into the ancient forest felt like entering a world of wonder.

Synset Examples

They created a world of wonder in their backyard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wonderland - an imaginary country of marvels or surprises
