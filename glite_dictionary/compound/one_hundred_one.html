one hundred one

1. NUM

/ wʌn ˈhʌndrəd wʌn / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhʌndrəd wʌn / (BrE)
The number 101; being one more than one hundred.
the number 101

Examples

There are one hundred one students enrolled in the course this year.
The building is one hundred one meters tall.
He read one hundred one books last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hundred and one - the number 101 (British usage)
