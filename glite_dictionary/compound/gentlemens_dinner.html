gentlemen's dinner

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛntlmɪnz ˈdɪnər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntlmənz ˈdɪnə / (BrE)
a formal dinner attended by men, typically to discuss business or professional matters
formal dinner for men discussing business

Examples

He was invited to the gentlemen's dinner to meet with potential clients.
At the annual gentlemen's dinner, several important deals were discussed and agreed upon.
The gentlemen's dinner required formal attire and followed a strict agenda.

Synset Examples

He attended the gentlemen's dinner to discuss business matters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: business dinner - a formal meal for business purposes
  • Synonym: banquet - large formal meal for many people
