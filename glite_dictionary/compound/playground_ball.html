playground ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪˌɡraʊnd bɑl / (AmE), / ˈpleɪɡraʊnd bɔːl / (BrE)
a large rubber ball designed for use in playground games such as four square, kickball, and dodgeball
large rubber ball for playground games

Examples

The children took a playground ball outside for a game of four square.
She bought a new playground ball for the schoolyard activities.
He kicked the playground ball over the fence during recess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kickball - a ball game played with a rubber ball, similar to baseball
  • Synonym: four square - a playground ball game played on a square court divided into four smaller squares
