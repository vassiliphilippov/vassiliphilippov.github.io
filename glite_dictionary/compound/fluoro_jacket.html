fluoro jacket

1. NOUN

/ ˈflʊəroʊ ˈdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈflʊərəʊ ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
a jacket made from fluorescent, brightly colored material, worn to increase visibility, often for safety purposes
brightly colored high-visibility jacket

Examples

Workers on the construction site must wear a fluoro jacket at all times for safety.
She grabbed her fluoro jacket before heading out for an early morning bike ride.
The volunteers dressed in fluoro jackets were directing traffic during the event.

Synset Examples

He wore a fluoro jacket for the night run.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high-visibility jacket - a jacket made to be easily seen, often for safety

Etymology

Shortened form of 'fluorescent jacket,' from Latin 'fluor' (flow) related to the optical effect of fluorescence.
