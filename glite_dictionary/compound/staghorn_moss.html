staghorn moss

1. NOUN

/ ˈstæɡhɔrn mɑs / (AmE), / ˈstæɡhɔːn mɒs / (BrE)
A species of club moss (Lycopodium complanatum), also known as Creeping Jenny or Northern Running-pine, characterized by its branching, moss-like appearance.
a species of club moss

Examples

The forest floor was covered with patches of staghorn moss, adding a lush green to the landscape.
Researchers study staghorn moss for its unique adaptation to northern environments.
She gently brushed aside the staghorn moss to reveal the soil underneath.
