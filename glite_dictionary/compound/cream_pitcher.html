cream pitcher

1. NOUN

/ ˈkriːm ˌpɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈkriːm ˌpɪtʃə / (BrE)
a small jug or vessel used for serving cream at the table
small vessel for serving cream

Examples

She poured milk into the cream pitcher before placing it on the breakfast table.
The antique cream pitcher matched the delicate china set perfectly.
Waiters quickly refilled the cream pitcher during the afternoon tea service.

Related Terms
