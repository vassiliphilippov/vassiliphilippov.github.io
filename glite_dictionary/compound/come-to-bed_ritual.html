come-to-bed ritual

1. NOUN

/ ˌkʌm tə ˈbɛd ˌrɪtʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌkʌm tə ˈbɛd ˌrɪtʃuːəl / (BrE)
a sequence of actions or customs regularly performed before going to bed, often to help prepare for sleep
bedtime routine or practice

Examples

Every night their come-to-bed ritual includes reading a story together.
My come-to-bed ritual helps me unwind after a busy day.
They developed a simple come-to-bed ritual to help their toddler sleep better.

Synset Examples

She had a sweet come-to-bed ritual with her kids.
The puppy has a come-to-bed routine every night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedtime routine - a regular set of activities before going to bed
