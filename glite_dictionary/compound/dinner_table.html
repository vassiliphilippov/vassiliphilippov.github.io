dinner table

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnər ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə ˌteɪbl̩ / (BrE)
a table at which dinner is served and eaten
table for eating dinner

Examples

We gathered around the dinner table to enjoy a meal as a family.
The dinner table was beautifully set with candles and fresh flowers.
After clearing the dinner table, the children helped wash the dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dining table - a table on which meals are served and eaten

2. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnɚ ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə ˌteɪbəl / (BrE)
a table at which meals, especially dinner, are eaten
table used for eating meals

Examples

We gather around the dinner table every evening to share our meals and stories.
Spilled milk quickly spread across the polished dinner table.
They bought a new dinner table large enough to seat the whole family.
