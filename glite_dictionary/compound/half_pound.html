half pound

1. NOUN

/ hæf paʊnd / (AmE), / hɑːf paʊnd / (BrE)
A unit of weight equal to 8 ounces (avoirdupois), or one half of a pound.
unit of weight; 8 ounces

Examples

The recipe calls for a half pound of ground beef.
She bought a half pound of cheese at the deli.
The box weighs just under a half pound including all the contents.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: pound - unit of weight equal to 16 ounces
  • Degree Lower: ounce - unit of weight, one sixteenth of a pound
