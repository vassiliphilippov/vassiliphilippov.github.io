Economic Commission for Africa

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈkɑnəmɪk kəˈmɪʃən fər əˈfrɪkə / (AmE), / iˈkɒnəmɪk kəˈmɪʃən fə ˈæfrɪkə / (BrE)
a regional commission of the United Nations Economic and Social Council responsible for promoting economic development among African nations
UN commission for African economic development

Examples

The Economic Commission for Africa released a report on sustainable growth in the region.
Several African governments collaborate closely with the Economic Commission for Africa on policy initiatives.
The Economic Commission for Africa is headquartered in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.
