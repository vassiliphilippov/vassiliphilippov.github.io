genus Eschrichtius

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ɛsˈkrɪktiəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ɛsˈkrɪktɪəs / (BrE)
The sole genus of the whale family Eschrichtiidae, containing the gray whale (Eschrichtius robustus).
genus of gray whale

Examples

Scientists classify the gray whale within the genus Eschrichtius.
The genus Eschrichtius is unique in having only one living species.
Research on the genus Eschrichtius has provided insight into whale evolution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Eschrichtius - genus containing the gray whale
  • Degree Higher: Eschrichtiidae - the whale family containing only Eschrichtius

Etymology

Genus name from Danish zoologist Daniel Eschricht; Latinized as Eschrichtius.
