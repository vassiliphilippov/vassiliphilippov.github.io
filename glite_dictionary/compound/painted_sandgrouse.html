painted sandgrouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪntɪd ˈsændˌɡraʊs / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪd ˈsændˌɡraʊs / (BrE)
a species of sandgrouse native to India, known for its striking plumage patterns
Indian sandgrouse species

Examples

During our trip to Rajasthan, we spotted a beautiful painted sandgrouse near the waterhole.
The male painted sandgrouse displays bold patterns to attract a mate.
Unlike other species, the painted sandgrouse is well-camouflaged in scrubland habitats.
