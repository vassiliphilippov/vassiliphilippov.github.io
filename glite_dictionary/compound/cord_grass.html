cord grass

1. NOUN

/ kɔrd ɡræs / (AmE), / kɔːd ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
Any of several perennial grasses of the genus Spartina, often found in coastal areas and important for stabilizing soil.
type of perennial coastal grass (Spartina)

Examples

Cord grass plays a crucial role in protecting shorelines from erosion.
The marsh was dominated by tall cord grass swaying in the breeze.
Scientists studied the growth patterns of cord grass in rehabilitated wetlands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spartina - genus of perennial coastal grasses including cord grasses
