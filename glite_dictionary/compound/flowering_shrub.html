flowering shrub

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ ʃrʌb / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ər.ɪŋ ʃrʌb / (BrE)
a shrub that is cultivated or noted mainly for its attractive flowers
shrub grown for its flowers

Examples

The garden was full of flowering shrubs in a riot of colors.
We planted a flowering shrub near the front porch to attract bees and butterflies.
This flowering shrub needs plenty of sunlight to produce its fragrant blossoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shrub - a woody plant smaller than a tree with multiple stems
