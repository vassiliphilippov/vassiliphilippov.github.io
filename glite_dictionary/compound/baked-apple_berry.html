baked-apple berry

1. NOUN

/ ˌbeɪkt ˈæpəl ˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˌbeɪkt ˈapəl ˌbɛri / (BrE)
a creeping perennial plant of northern temperate regions, producing soft yellow or orange berries that resemble and taste like baked apples
creeping plant with yellow/orange berries

Examples

In Scandinavia, the baked-apple berry is prized for its unique flavor in desserts and jams.
After a day of hiking, we found a patch of baked-apple berries growing near the edge of the forest.
The soft, golden-orange fruit of the baked-apple berry has a sweet taste reminiscent of stewed apples.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cloudberry - a similar northern berry, also known as baked-apple berry
