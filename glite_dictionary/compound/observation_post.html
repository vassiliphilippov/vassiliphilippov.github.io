observation post

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑb.zɚˈveɪ.ʃən poʊst / (AmE), / ˌɒb.zəˈveɪ.ʃən pəʊst / (BrE)
A position, often elevated, from which a wide area can be watched or monitored, especially for military purposes.
elevated position for monitoring

Examples

From the observation post, the soldiers scanned the horizon for any sign of movement.
A wooden observation post was built at the edge of the forest to monitor wildlife.
The scouts quietly approached the enemy's observation post under cover of darkness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lookout - a place for keeping watch, especially a high one
