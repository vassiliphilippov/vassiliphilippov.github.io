consumer finance company

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsuːmɚ ˈfaɪnæns ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / kənˈsjuːmə ˈfaɪnæns ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a company that provides loans or credit to individuals, typically those who have difficulty securing loans from banks
company providing loans to individuals

Examples

After being declined by their bank, they applied for a loan at a consumer finance company.
Many people use a consumer finance company to cover emergency expenses when other credit is unavailable.
A consumer finance company often charges higher interest rates than traditional banks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finance company - a company that makes loans to individuals or businesses
