Lut Desert

1. PROPN

/ lut ˈdɛz.ɚt / (AmE), / luːt ˈdɛz.ət / (BrE)
a large desert in eastern Iran, known for its extreme heat and salt flats
large desert in eastern Iran

Examples

The Lut Desert is considered one of the hottest places on Earth, with record surface temperatures.
Tourists often visit the Lut Desert to experience its unique landscape and salt formations.
Satellite imagery has revealed shifting patterns in the sand dunes of the Lut Desert.
