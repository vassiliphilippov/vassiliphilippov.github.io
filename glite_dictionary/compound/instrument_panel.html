instrument panel

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnstrəmənt ˌpænəl / (AmE), / ˈɪnstrʊmənt ˌpænəl / (BrE)
A flat panel fitted with dials, switches, and meters for monitoring and controlling the operation of an electrical device, vehicle, or machine.
control panel with dials and switches

Examples

The pilot glanced at the instrument panel to check the fuel level before takeoff.
Warning lights suddenly flashed on the instrument panel as the car lost power.
The technician repaired several faulty gauges on the main instrument panel in the control room.

Synset Examples

he checked the instrument panel
suddenly the board lit up like a Christmas tree

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dashboard - panel under the windshield of a vehicle containing controls and instruments
  • Synonym: control panel - panel with switches and controls for managing a system
