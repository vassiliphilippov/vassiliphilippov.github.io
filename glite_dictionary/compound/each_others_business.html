each other's business

1. NOUN

/ itʃ ˈʌðərz ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / iːtʃ ˈʌðəz ˈbɪznəs / (BrE)
used to refer to the private affairs or personal matters of people in a group, especially when considered by others.
other people's private affairs

Examples

In a small village, people often seem to know each other's business.
It's better not to involve yourself in each other's business if you want to avoid gossip.
They should respect privacy and not meddle in each other's business.

Synset Examples

Everyone in town knows each other's business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: private matters - matters that are personal and not meant for public knowledge
  • Synonym: personal affairs - someone's personal business or concerns
