blue jessamine

1. NOUN

/ bluː ˈdʒɛs.ə.mɪn / (AmE), / bluː ˈdʒɛs.ə.mɪn / (BrE)
A climbing plant of the southern United States with bluish-purple flowers, cultivated as an ornamental.
climbing plant with bluish-purple flowers

Examples

The blue jessamine bloomed along the fence, filling the garden with a sweet fragrance.
He planted a blue jessamine beside the porch to add color to the yard.
In spring, the blue jessamine attracts bees and butterflies with its vivid flowers.
