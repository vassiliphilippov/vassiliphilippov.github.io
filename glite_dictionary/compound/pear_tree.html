pear tree

1. NOUN

/ pɛr tri / (AmE), / pɛə triː / (BrE)
A tree native to Europe and Asia that produces sweet, juicy, gritty-textured fruit known as pears, cultivated in many varieties.
tree bearing edible pears

Examples

The old pear tree in our garden produces fruit every autumn.
Farmers planted several varieties of pear tree to improve their harvest.
The blossoms on the pear tree attract many bees in spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pear - the edible fruit of the pear tree
