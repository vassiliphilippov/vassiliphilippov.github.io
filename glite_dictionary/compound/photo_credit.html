photo credit

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊtoʊ ˈkrɛdɪt / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊ ˈkrɛdɪt / (BrE)
A note or line in a publication acknowledging the source or creator of a photograph.
acknowledgment of a photograph's source

Examples

The magazine included a photo credit beneath each image to honor the photographer.
If you use this image in your presentation, be sure to provide a proper photo credit.
She forgot to add the photo credit, and the editor asked her to correct it.
