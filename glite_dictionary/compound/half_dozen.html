half dozen

1. NOUN

/ hæf ˈdʌzən / (AmE), / hɑːf ˈdʌzən / (BrE)
A group or quantity of six items or people.
group of six items

Examples

She bought a half dozen eggs at the market.
There were only a half dozen people at the meeting.
He ordered a half dozen roses for his anniversary.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: dozen - a group of twelve items or people
  • Synonym: six - the number 6

Etymology

From 'half' + 'dozen', literally meaning half of a dozen (twelve).
