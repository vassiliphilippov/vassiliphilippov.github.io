happy snap

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæpi snæp / (AmE), / ˈhæpi snap / (BrE)
an informal photograph taken spontaneously, usually to capture a cheerful moment
casual, cheerful photograph

Examples

We took a happy snap together at the park before heading home.
She posted a happy snap of her smiling with friends on her birthday.
Every summer holiday is filled with happy snaps that bring back great memories.

Synset Examples

They captured a happy snap at the family reunion.
We shared a happy snap on social media last night.
Her happy snap from the vacation brightened my day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snapshot - an informal photograph taken quickly
