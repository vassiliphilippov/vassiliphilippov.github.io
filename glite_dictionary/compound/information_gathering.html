information gathering

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.fɚˈmeɪ.ʃən ˈɡæð.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɪn.fəˈmeɪ.ʃən ˈɡæð.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of collecting facts, data, or knowledge, especially for a specific purpose
the act of collecting information

Examples

Journalists spent weeks on information gathering before publishing the story.
Effective information gathering is crucial for making sound business decisions.
The team divided the tasks to streamline the information gathering process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: data collection - the process of gathering and measuring information
