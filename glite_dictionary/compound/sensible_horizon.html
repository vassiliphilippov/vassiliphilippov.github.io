sensible horizon

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛnsəbəl həˈraɪzn̩ / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsɪbəl həˈrʌɪz(ə)n / (BrE)
the line at which the sky and Earth (or sea) appear to meet as observed from a particular point
apparent line where sky meets Earth

Examples

The ship disappeared below the sensible horizon as it sailed into the distance.
At sunset, the sun appears to touch the sensible horizon before it finally sets.
From the top of the hill, they could see the sensible horizon stretching out endlessly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horizon - the line where the earth or sea seems to meet the sky
