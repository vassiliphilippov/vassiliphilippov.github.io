wonder bean

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌndɚ biːn / (AmE), / ˈwʌndə biːn / (BrE)
an annual bushy plant from tropical South America, grown especially for forage, bearing long pods with white seeds
tropical South American forage plant

Examples

Farmers in Brazil often plant wonder bean as a nutritious animal fodder.
The wonder bean produces long pods filled with distinctive white seeds.
Researchers are studying the wonder bean for its potential to improve soil health in tropical regions.
