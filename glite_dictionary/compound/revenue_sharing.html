revenue sharing

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛvənju ˌʃɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛvənjuː ˌʃeərɪŋ / (BrE)
the distribution of a portion of government tax income to state or local authorities
distribution of government tax income

Examples

The new law introduced revenue sharing to boost funding for local schools.
Debates over revenue sharing shaped federal-state relations in the 1970s.
Many municipalities rely on revenue sharing programs to maintain their infrastructure.
