steering gear

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪrɪŋ ɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈstɪərɪŋ ɡɪə / (BrE)
the mechanism that connects the steering wheel to the steering linkage in a motor vehicle, enabling the driver to control the vehicle's direction
mechanism linking steering wheel to wheels

Examples

The mechanic replaced the steering gear to fix the problem with the car's alignment.
Worn steering gear can make it difficult to control the vehicle safely.
Engineers improved the design of the steering gear to enhance driving precision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steering mechanism - device for controlling the direction of a vehicle
