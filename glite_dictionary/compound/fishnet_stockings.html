fishnet stockings

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃˌnɛt ˈstɑːkɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌnɛt ˈstɒkɪŋz / (BrE)
Stockings made from an open mesh fabric resembling netting, typically worn as a fashion statement.
stockings made of mesh netting

Examples

She wore a bold outfit with fishnet stockings and a leather skirt.
Fishnet stockings became particularly popular in fashion during the 20th century.
For the costume party, Mia paired her dress with black fishnet stockings.

Synset Examples

Fishnet stockings are commonplace roller derby regalia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stockings - close-fitting coverings for the feet and legs

Etymology

From 'fishnet,' named for its resemblance to fishing nets, and 'stockings.'
