Salt Lake City

1. PROPN

/ sɑlt leɪk ˈsɪti / (AmE), / sɒlt leɪk ˈsɪti / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Utah, situated near the Great Salt Lake in north-central Utah; known as the world headquarters of the Mormon Church (the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints)
capital and largest city of Utah, USA
american

Examples

We spent our vacation exploring Salt Lake City and its beautiful surroundings.
The headquarters of the Mormon Church are located in Salt Lake City.
She moved to Salt Lake City for a new job opportunity last year.

Etymology

Named after the nearby Great Salt Lake.
