sugar bowl

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃuɡɚ boʊl / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡə bəʊl / (BrE)
a small dish or container used for serving sugar at the table
container for serving sugar

Examples

She placed the sugar bowl next to the teapot on the table.
Please pass me the sugar bowl so I can sweeten my coffee.
The antique sugar bowl was decorated with delicate blue flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creamer - a small jug or container for serving cream
