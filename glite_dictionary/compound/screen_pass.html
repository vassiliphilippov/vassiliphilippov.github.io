screen pass

1. NOUN

/ skrin pæs / (AmE), / skriːn pɑːs / (BrE)
In American football, a short forward pass in which the receiver is protected by a line of blockers.
football play with blockers on a short pass
american

Examples

The quarterback threw a screen pass to the running back, who ran for a first down.
Our team executed a perfect screen pass that caught the defense off guard.
The coach called for a screen pass to counter the heavy blitz from the opposing team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forward pass - a pass thrown toward the opponent's goal line in football
