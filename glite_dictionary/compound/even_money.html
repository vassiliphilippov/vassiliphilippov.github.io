even money

1. NOUN

/ ˈivən ˈmʌni / (AmE), / ˈiːvən ˈmʌni / (BrE)
odds or chances that are equal for either outcome, especially in betting; a fifty-fifty chance
equal odds; fifty-fifty chance

Examples

It's even money whether we'll finish the project on time.
Many bettors thought the game was even money and refused to take big risks.
She said it's even money that it will rain tomorrow.

Synset Examples

It's even money whether they win the championship.
The odds are even money for both candidates to win.
I think it's even money on which team takes the trophy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fifty-fifty - having equal chances for each possible result
  • Antonym: long shot - an outcome with low probability; much worse than even odds
