range of mountains

1. NOUN

/ reɪndʒ əv ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / reɪndʒ əv ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
A line or group of mountains that are connected or closely arranged in a continuous chain.
connected line of mountains

Examples

The Andes are the longest range of mountains in South America.
Their cabin overlooked a beautiful range of mountains stretching into the distance.
Heavy snow covered the entire range of mountains by early December.

Synset Examples

the valley was between two ranges of hills
the plains lay just beyond the mountain range

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mountain range - a connected series of mountains
