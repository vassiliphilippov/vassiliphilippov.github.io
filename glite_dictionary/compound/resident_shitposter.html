resident shitposter

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛzɪdənt ˈʃɪtˌpoʊstər / (AmE), / ˈrɛzɪdənt ˈʃɪtˌpəʊstə / (BrE)
a person in an online community who is known for habitually posting irrelevant, provocative, or offensive content
someone known for posting provocative or irrelevant content online
slang informal vulgar

Examples

Tom quickly earned the title of resident shitposter after flooding the forum with memes.
Every group chat seems to have a resident shitposter who never takes anything seriously.
Even though he's the resident shitposter, his jokes sometimes lighten the mood.

Synset Examples

He's known as the resident shitposter in the group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shitposter - a person who regularly posts off-topic or provocative content online

Etymology

Formed from 'resident' (someone regularly present in a place) and 'shitposter' (slang for a person who routinely posts off-topic or inflammatory content online).
