cozy evening

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊzi ˈivnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊzi ˈiːvnɪŋ / (BrE)
An evening spent in a warm, comfortable, and relaxed atmosphere, typically at home.
comfortable and relaxing evening at home

Examples

After a long week, we enjoyed a cozy evening by the fireplace with hot chocolate.
A cozy evening indoors is sometimes all you need to recharge.
They invited friends over for a cozy evening filled with games and laughter.

Synset Examples

We planned a cozy evening at home; let it snow outside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relaxing evening - an evening spent in a relaxed manner
