Liberty Island

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪbɚti ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / ˈlɪbəti ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
an island in New York Bay, southwest of Manhattan, famous as the site of the Statue of Liberty
island in New York Bay with the Statue of Liberty

Examples

Thousands of tourists visit Liberty Island each year to see the Statue of Liberty up close.
From the ferry, you get a fantastic view of Liberty Island against the Manhattan skyline.
After the name was changed, Liberty Island became even more closely associated with American freedom.

Synset Examples

Congress officially changed the name from Bedloe's Island to Liberty Island in 1956
