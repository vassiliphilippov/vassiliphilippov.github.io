long-ass

1. ADJ

/ lɔŋ æs / (AmE), / lɒŋ as / (BrE)
(slang) Extremely long in duration or extent (especially time); used for emphasis in informal speech or writing.
very long (slang intensifier)
slang informal

Examples

After that long-ass meeting, I needed some fresh air.
We waited in a long-ass line to get concert tickets.
This has been a long-ass week and I'm ready for the weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: long - having great duration or extent

2. ADJ

/ lɔŋ æs / (AmE), / lɒŋ æs / (BrE)
(informal, slang) Extremely long; very lengthy. Used as an emphatic intensifier before nouns.
(slang) extremely long; very lengthy
informal slang american

Examples

The movie was a long-ass three hours that felt even longer.
She wrote me a long-ass email detailing every little thing.
We drove down this long-ass road that seemed to go on forever.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: long - extending a great distance from end to end
  • Degree Higher: endless - having no end; extremely or infinitely long
