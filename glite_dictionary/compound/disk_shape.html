disk shape

1. NOUN

/ dɪsk ʃeɪp / (AmE), / dɪsk ʃeɪp / (BrE)
the quality or state of having the round, flat form of a disk
quality of being round and flat like a disk

Examples

The planet appears to have a perfect disk shape when viewed from a distance.
Engineers studied the disk shape of the coin to ensure its balance.
Some bacteria have a disk shape that helps them move through liquids more efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disk - a flat, round shape or object
