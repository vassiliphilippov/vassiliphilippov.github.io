career girl

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɪr ɡɜrl / (AmE), / kəˈrɪə ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a woman whose main interest and ambition is in pursuing a career, especially as opposed to a traditional domestic role
woman focused on her career

Examples

She was determined to succeed as a career girl in the competitive world of finance.
In the 1960s, being a career girl often meant facing unique challenges in the workplace.
As a career girl, her job was her main priority, and she delayed starting a family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: career woman - a woman whose career is of paramount importance to her; synonym for 'career girl'
