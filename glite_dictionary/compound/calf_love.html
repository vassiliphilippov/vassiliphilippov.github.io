calf love

1. NOUN

/ kæf lʌv / (AmE), / kɑːf lʌv / (BrE)
Innocent or short-lived romantic infatuation experienced during adolescence.
adolescent infatuation

Examples

Her first crush was just calf love, but it felt very real to her at the time.
The novel beautifully captures the intensity of calf love between its young protagonists.
He later laughed at his experience of calf love in middle school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puppy love - inexperienced or youthful romantic feeling

Etymology

Metaphorical use of 'calf' to mean a young or immature person, paralleling the young animal.
