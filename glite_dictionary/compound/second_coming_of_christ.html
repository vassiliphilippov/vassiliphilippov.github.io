Second Coming of Christ

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛkənd ˈkʌmɪŋ əv kraɪst / (AmE), / ˌsɛkənd ˈkʌmɪŋ əv kraɪst / (BrE)
(In Christian theology) the prophesied return of Jesus Christ to earth to judge humanity at the Last Judgment.
Christ's prophesied return for the Last Judgment

Examples

Many Christians await the Second Coming of Christ as a fulfillment of biblical prophecy.
Throughout history, artists have depicted the Second Coming of Christ in their works.
Some sermons focus on preparing believers for the Second Coming of Christ.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Last Judgment - the final judgment by God of all humankind
