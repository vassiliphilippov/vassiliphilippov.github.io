lady's tobacco

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪdiz təˈbækoʊ / (AmE), / ˈleɪdiz təˈbækəʊ / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant, propagated by runners, often found in woodland areas.
North American perennial plant

Examples

The shady woodland path was lined with lady's tobacco in full bloom.
Gardeners appreciate lady's tobacco for its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
Propagation of lady's tobacco is easy, as the plant spreads naturally via runners.
