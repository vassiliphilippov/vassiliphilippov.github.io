higher up

1. ADV

/ ˈhaɪər ˈʌp / (AmE), / ˈhaɪə ˈʌp / (BrE)
at or towards a position that is above or higher than a reference point
at or to a higher position

Examples

She climbed higher up the mountain, eager to reach the summit.
The nest was built higher up in the tree to avoid predators.
As I looked higher up the wall, I noticed another crack forming.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lower down - at or to a lower position
