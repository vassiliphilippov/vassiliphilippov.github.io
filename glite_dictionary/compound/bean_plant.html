bean plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈbin ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈbiːn ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
Any of various leguminous plants cultivated for their edible seeds or pods, such as the common bean or broad bean.
plant grown for edible beans or pods

Examples

The bean plant in the garden is thriving and producing plenty of pods.
Farmers often rotate corn with bean plants to restore nitrogen to the soil.
We studied the growth cycle of a bean plant in our biology class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bean - the edible seed of various leguminous plants
  • Synonym: legume - a plant of the pea family producing pods with seeds
