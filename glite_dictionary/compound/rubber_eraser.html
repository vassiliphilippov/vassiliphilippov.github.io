rubber eraser

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌbɚ ɪˈreɪsɚ / (AmE), / ˈrʌbə ɪˈreɪsə / (BrE)
A small block of rubber or similar synthetic material used to remove pencil marks, often attached to one end of a pencil.
eraser made of rubber or similar material

Examples

She reached for the rubber eraser at the end of her pencil to fix the mistake.
His favorite rubber eraser wore down to a tiny nub after months of sketches.
You can remove most pencil marks cleanly with a rubber eraser.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eraser - an object used to remove marks made by pencil or pen
