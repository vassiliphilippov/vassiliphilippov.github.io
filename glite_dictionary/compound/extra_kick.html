extra kick

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛkstrə kɪk / (AmE), / ˈɛkstrə kɪk / (BrE)
an additional amount of flavor, excitement, or energy added to something
added flavor or excitement

Examples

The chef added a pinch of cayenne pepper for an extra kick to the soup.
Her speech had an extra kick thanks to her unexpected sense of humor.
This lemonade has an extra kick of ginger in it, making it very refreshing.

Synset Examples

She added a chilip for an extra kick.

Related Terms
