party man

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːrti mæn / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti mæn / (BrE)
a male member of a political party who loyally follows and supports the party's policies and leadership
loyal male member of a political party

Examples

Despite personal doubts, the party man voted as instructed by the party leadership.
His career advanced quickly because he was always considered a loyal party man.
As a true party man, he never once questioned the party's decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partisan - a strong supporter of a party, cause, or person
  • Antonym: dissident - a person who opposes official policy, especially that of an authoritarian state
