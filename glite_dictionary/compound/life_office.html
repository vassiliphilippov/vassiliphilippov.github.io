life office

1. NOUN

/ laɪf ˈɑːfɪs / (AmE), / laɪf ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
A company or office that provides life assurance (life insurance).
company providing life assurance
british

Examples

After his father passed away, he contacted the life office to file a claim.
The life office introduced a new policy with improved benefits for families.
Several major life offices merged to form a larger insurance provider.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insurance company - a company that sells insurance policies
  • Synonym: life insurance company - an insurance company specializing in life insurance/assurance
