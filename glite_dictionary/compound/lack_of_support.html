lack of support

1. NOUN

/ læk əv səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / læk əv səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
an absence or insufficiency of help, assistance, or backing
absence or insufficiency of help or backing

Examples

The team's failure was partly due to a lack of support from management.
Many charities struggle because of a lack of support from the government.
Students often feel discouraged when they experience a lack of support from their teachers.

Synset Examples

They criticized the lack of support at the univershitty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: support - assistance or backing given to someone
  • Synonym: help - assistance to someone to make a task easier
  • Synonym: assistance - the act of helping or supporting
