staff office

1. NOUN

/ stæf ˈɑfɪs / (AmE), / stɑːf ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
the department within an organization responsible for hiring, training, and managing personnel, as well as setting employee policies
department handling personnel and hiring

Examples

The staff office updated the handbook to reflect the new workplace policies.
If you have any questions about benefits, contact the staff office for assistance.
She applied for a position in the staff office to pursue a career in human resources.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: human resources - the department that manages employee-related functions
  • Synonym: personnel department - the division responsible for employee administration
