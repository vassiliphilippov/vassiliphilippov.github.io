Damon and Pythias

1. PROPN

/ ˈdeɪmən ənd ˈpɪθiəs / (AmE), / ˈdeɪmən ənd ˈpɪθiəs / (BrE)
In Greek legend, two friends celebrated for their loyalty to each other, after whom the story of exemplary friendship and trust is named.
legendary friends in Greek mythology

Examples

Their friendship reminded me of Damon and Pythias, so deep was their loyalty to each other.
The teacher used the story of Damon and Pythias to illustrate the virtues of trust and sacrifice.
Many works of literature refer to Damon and Pythias as symbols of perfect friendship.
