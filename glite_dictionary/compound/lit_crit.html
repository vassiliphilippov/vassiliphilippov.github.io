lit crit

1. NOUN

/ lɪt krɪt / (AmE), / lɪt krɪt / (BrE)
informal abbreviation for literary criticism; the analysis and evaluation of literature, especially as an academic discipline
abbreviation for literary criticism
informal

Examples

She took a lit crit class in her sophomore year to deepen her understanding of poetry.
Many English majors write their first serious lit crit essays as undergraduates.
The professor encouraged students to read more lit crit journals to refine their analytical skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: literary criticism - the formal study, analysis, and evaluation of literature
