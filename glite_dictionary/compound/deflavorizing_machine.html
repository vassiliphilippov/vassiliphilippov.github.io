deflavorizing machine

1. NOUN

/ diˈfleɪvɚˌaɪzɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / diˈfleɪvəˌaɪzɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used to remove or reduce flavors from products or food, often to improve or standardize taste
machine that removes flavors from food or products

Examples

The factory installed a deflavorizing machine to remove unwanted tastes from its oils.
After processing, the deflavorizing machine left the product with a neutral aroma.
Upgrading to a modern deflavorizing machine improved the consistency of the food's flavor.

Synset Examples

The factory uses a deflavorizing machine for its products.
They installed a deflavorizing machine to enhance food quality.
The chef was skeptical about the deflavorizing machine's effectiveness.
