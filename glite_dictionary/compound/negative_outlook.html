negative outlook

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛɡətɪv ˈaʊtˌlʊk / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡətɪv ˈaʊt.lʊk / (BrE)
a general tendency to expect unfavorable or undesirable outcomes; a pessimistic perspective
pessimistic perspective

Examples

After losing her job, she developed a negative outlook that affected her entire approach to life.
His negative outlook made it difficult for him to see opportunities in challenging situations.
The team struggled to succeed because a negative outlook spread among the members.

Synset Examples

She always has a negative outlook on life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pessimism - the tendency to see the worst aspect of things or believe the worst will happen
  • Antonym: optimism - hopefulness and confidence about the future or the success of something
