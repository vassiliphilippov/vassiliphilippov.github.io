medium frequency

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːdiəm ˈfriːkwənsi / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiəm ˈfriːkwənsi / (BrE)
the range of radio frequencies from 300 to 3000 kilohertz, used especially for AM radio broadcasting and maritime communication
radio frequency range: 300–3000 kHz

Examples

The ship used medium frequency signals for its navigational beacons.
Maritime radio communication often operates within the medium frequency band.
AM radio stations typically broadcast in the medium frequency range.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: high frequency - radio frequencies from 3 to 30 megahertz
  • Degree Lower: low frequency - radio frequencies from 30 to 300 kilohertz
