Secretary of the Treasury

1. PROPN

/ sɛk.rəˌtɛɹ.i əv ðə ˈtrɛʒ.ə.ɹi / (AmE), / ˈsɛk.rə.təri əv ðə ˈtrɛʒ.əri / (BrE)
the head of the United States Department of the Treasury, responsible for overseeing government revenue and financial policy
chief financial official of the U.S. Treasury Department

Examples

Janet Yellen was appointed as the Secretary of the Treasury by the President.
The Secretary of the Treasury is responsible for implementing fiscal policy in the United States.
Many economic decisions are influenced by statements from the Secretary of the Treasury.

Synset Examples

Alexander Hamilton was the first Secretary of the Treasury

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Treasury Secretary - another term for Secretary of the Treasury in casual or journalistic use
