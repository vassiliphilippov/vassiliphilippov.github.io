African scented mahogany

1. NOUN

/ ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈsɛn.tɪd məˈhɑː.ɡəni / (AmE), / ˈaf.rɪ.kən ˈsɛn.tɪd məˈhɒɡ.əni / (BrE)
A tree native to Africa, known for its lightweight, cedar-scented wood that ranges in color from pink to reddish brown.
African tree with cedar-scented wood

Examples

Many furniture makers value African scented mahogany for its pleasant aroma and attractive grain.
The reddish brown color of African scented mahogany makes it popular in interior design.
Export restrictions have been placed on African scented mahogany to protect the species.
