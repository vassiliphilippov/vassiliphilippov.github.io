watch crystal

1. NOUN

/ wɑːtʃ ˈkrɪstl̩ / (AmE), / wɒtʃ ˈkrɪstl̩ / (BrE)
the transparent protective cover over the face of a watch
protective cover on watch face

Examples

He scratched the watch crystal when he accidentally dropped his wristwatch on the floor.
Some modern smartwatches use sapphire glass as their watch crystal for extra durability.
The jeweler replaced the cracked watch crystal with a brand-new one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crystal - transparent material used for covers, especially in watches
  • Degree Lower: watch face - the front display or dial of a watch
