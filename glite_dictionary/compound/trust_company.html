trust company

1. NOUN

/ trʌst ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / trʌst ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)
a financial institution that acts as a trustee, fiduciary, or agent for individuals or organizations in handling trusts, estates, and related matters
financial institution for trusts and estates

Examples

She appointed a trust company to manage her estate after her passing.
The trust company offers services such as acting as executor and managing investment portfolios.
Many wealthy clients rely on a trust company to handle complicated financial matters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bank - an organization that provides financial services such as holding money and lending
