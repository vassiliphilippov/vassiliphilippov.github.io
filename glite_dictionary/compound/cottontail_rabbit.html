cottontail rabbit

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːtn̩ˌteɪl ˈræbɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒtntɛɪl ˈræbɪt / (BrE)
a small North American rabbit with greyish or brownish fur and a tail that is white underneath
small North American rabbit with white underside to tail

Examples

A cottontail rabbit darted across the field, its white tail flashing in the sunlight.
We spotted a young cottontail rabbit nibbling on clover in the backyard.
The cottontail rabbit is a common sight in many suburban neighborhoods during early morning hours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rabbit - a small mammal with long ears, long hind legs, and a short fluffy tail
