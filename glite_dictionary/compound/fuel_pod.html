fuel pod

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuːəl pɑd / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəl pɒd / (BrE)
a detachable container fitted to an aircraft or spacecraft for carrying additional fuel
detachable fuel container for an aircraft

Examples

The fighter jet carried an extra fuel pod to increase its operational range.
Technicians inspected the fuel pod before the aircraft's transatlantic flight.
During combat missions, the pilot often relies on the fuel pod for extended endurance.
