parlour grand piano

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑr.lɚ ˌɡrænd piˈænoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.lə ˌɡrænd piˈæ.nəʊ / (BrE)
a type of grand piano that is smaller than a concert grand, designed for use in more intimate settings such as homes or parlours
a small grand piano for homes

Examples

The couple proudly displayed their parlour grand piano in the living room.
She performed on a beautifully restored parlour grand piano at the recital.
A parlour grand piano is an ideal choice for musicians with limited space.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: baby grand piano - an even smaller type of grand piano
  • Degree Higher: concert grand piano - a larger grand piano built for concert halls

Etymology

From 'parlour', meaning a sitting room, and 'grand piano'.
