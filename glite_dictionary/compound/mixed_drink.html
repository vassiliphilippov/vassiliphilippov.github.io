mixed drink

1. NOUN

/ mɪkst drɪŋk / (AmE), / mɪkst drɪŋk / (BrE)
An alcoholic beverage made by combining two or more ingredients, such as spirits, juices, or mixers.
alcoholic beverage with mixed ingredients

Examples

She ordered a mixed drink with rum, pineapple juice, and coconut cream at the bar.
Many people prefer a mixed drink over straight spirits because of the added flavors.
Each mixed drink on the menu is crafted with fresh ingredients and unique garnishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cocktail - an alcoholic drink consisting of a spirit or spirits mixed with other ingredients
