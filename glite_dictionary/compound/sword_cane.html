sword cane

1. NOUN

/ sɔrd keɪn / (AmE), / sɔːd keɪn / (BrE)
A walking cane that contains a concealed sword or dagger inside.
cane with hidden sword or dagger

Examples

The detective carried a sword cane for both support and protection.
During the 19th century, gentlemen sometimes used a sword cane as a concealed weapon.
He revealed the hidden blade inside his sword cane when threatened.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: cane - a stick used for support while walking
  • Degree Lower: dagger - a short, pointed knife used as a weapon
