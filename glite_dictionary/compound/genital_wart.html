genital wart

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛnɪtl wɔɹt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnɪtl wɔːt / (BrE)
a small, benign growth that develops on or around the genitals or anus, usually caused by certain strains of the human papillomavirus (HPV)
benign growth on genitals or anus
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with a genital wart after a routine examination.
Using protection during sex can lower the risk of developing a genital wart.
Treatment for a genital wart may involve topical creams or medical procedures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wart - a small, hard, benign growth on the skin caused by a virus
