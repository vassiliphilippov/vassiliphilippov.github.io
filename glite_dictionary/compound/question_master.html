question master

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɛs.tʃən ˌmæs.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkwɛs.tʃən ˌmɑː.stər / (BrE)
the person who asks the questions and controls a quiz show or panel game, especially on radio or television
host of a quiz show or panel game
british

Examples

The question master welcomed the contestants and explained the rules of the game.
He was a famous question master on several British television shows in the 1980s.
As question master, she kept the quiz moving at a lively pace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quizmaster - the person who asks questions in a quiz, especially on TV or radio
