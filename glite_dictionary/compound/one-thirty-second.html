one-thirty-second

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˌθɜrtiˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / wʌn ˌθɜːti ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
one part of thirty-two equal parts; the fraction 1/32.
one in thirty-two equal parts (1/32)

Examples
