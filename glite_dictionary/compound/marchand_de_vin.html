marchand de vin

1. NOUN

/ marˌʃɑ̃ də ˈvɛ̃ / (AmE), / mɑːˌʃɒ̃ də ˈvæ̃ / (BrE)
A brown sauce made with mushrooms and red wine or Madeira, used in French cuisine.
brown sauce with mushrooms and red wine

Examples

The steak was served with a savory marchand de vin that complemented the meat perfectly.
She learned to prepare marchand de vin during her studies in French cuisine.
The chef finished the dish with a rich marchand de vin sauce, adding depth to the flavors.

Etymology

French, literally 'wine merchant'.
