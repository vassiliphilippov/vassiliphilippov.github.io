Indian Rebellion of 1857

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.di.ən rɪˈbɛl.jən əv ˌeɪtˈtiːn ˈfɪf.ti ˈsɛv.ən / (AmE), / ˈɪn.dɪ.ən rɪˈbɛl.jən əv ˌeɪtˈiːn ˈfɪf.ti ˈsɛv.ən / (BrE)
a major uprising against British rule in India in 1857–1858, involving widespread revolts and battles, especially by Indian soldiers.
major Indian uprising against British rule (1857–58)

Examples

The Indian Rebellion of 1857 marked a significant turning point in the history of British colonial rule.
Historians still debate the underlying causes of the Indian Rebellion of 1857.
The Indian Rebellion of 1857 is sometimes referred to as the First War of Indian Independence.
