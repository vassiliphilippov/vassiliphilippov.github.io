halfro hairstyle

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæf.roʊ ˈhaɪrˌstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf.rəʊ ˈheəˌstaɪl / (BrE)
A hairstyle in which only part of the hair is styled as an afro, while the remainder is left in a different style or less voluminous.
partial afro hairstyle

Examples

The comedian joked about his awkward halfro hairstyle.
She wore a halfro hairstyle for her performance, combining natural curls with a sleek side.
His unique halfro hairstyle drew compliments from friends and strangers alike.

Synset Examples

The comedian joked about his awkward halfro hairstyle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: afro - a hairstyle in which the hair is allowed to grow naturally outward

Etymology

Blend of 'half' and 'afro'.
