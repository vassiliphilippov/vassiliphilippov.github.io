assholocene era

1. NOUN

/ ˈæshoʊloʊˌsiːn ˈɛrə / (AmE), / ˈæshələʊˌsiːn ˈɪərə / (BrE)
A slang, critical term for the contemporary period, highlighting humanity's irresponsible or destructive impact on the environment and planet.
era of human-caused environmental harm (slang)
informal slang

Examples

Many environmentalists argue that we're living in the assholocene era, given how recklessly we've treated the planet.
The documentary uses the phrase assholocene era to describe humanity's ecological devastation.
Social media posts about the climate crisis increasingly refer to our time as the assholocene era.

Synset Examples

The current climate crisis feels like the assholocene era.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Anthropocene - proposed geological epoch when humans have significant impact on Earth's geology and ecosystems

Etymology

Blend of 'asshole' (slang insult) and 'Anthropocene' (current geological age of human influence).
