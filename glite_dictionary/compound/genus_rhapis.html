genus Rhapis

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˈræpɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈræpɪs / (BrE)
A genus of small, clump-forming fan palms native to China and Japan.
genus of fan palms from China and Japan

Examples

Several species within the genus Rhapis are popular as ornamental houseplants.
The genus Rhapis includes the widely cultivated lady palm.
Botanists study the genus Rhapis for its adaptability and resilience in varying climates.

Etymology

Rhapis from Greek rhapis, meaning 'needle' or 'rod', referring to the shape of the leaf segments.
