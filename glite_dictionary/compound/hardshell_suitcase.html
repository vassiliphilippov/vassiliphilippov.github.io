hardshell suitcase

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrdˌʃɛl ˈsutˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌʃɛl ˈsuːt.keɪs / (BrE)
a suitcase with a hard, rigid outer shell, designed to protect its contents during travel
suitcase with a hard, rigid exterior

Examples

I packed all my electronics in a hardshell suitcase for extra protection.
Her hardshell suitcase easily survived the rough handling at the airport.
He prefers a hardshell suitcase because it keeps his belongings secure and safe from sudden impacts.

Synset Examples

The hardshell suitcase protected my clothes during travel.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: soft-sided suitcase - a suitcase made with flexible, soft materials
