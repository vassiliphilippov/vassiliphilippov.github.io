pair of trousers

1. NOUN

/ pɛr əv ˈtraʊzɚz / (AmE), / peə əv ˈtraʊzəz / (BrE)
An item of clothing covering the body from the waist to the ankles, with a separate part for each leg, usually referred to in the plural.
garment covering both legs separately

Examples

He bought a new pair of trousers for the interview.
She accidentally spilled coffee on her favorite pair of trousers.
A good pair of trousers should fit comfortably at the waist and legs.

Synset Examples

he had a sharp crease in his trousers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trousers - outer garment covering each leg separately
  • Synonym: pants - (US) outer garment covering each leg separately; American equivalent
