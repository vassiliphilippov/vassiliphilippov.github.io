truelove knot

1. NOUN

/ ˈtruˌlʌv nɑt / (AmE), / ˈtruːlʌv nɒt / (BrE)
a knot used to tie the ends of two lines together, traditionally symbolizing affection or unity
knot joining two lines; symbol of unity

Examples

The sailor skillfully tied a truelove knot to secure the two ropes together.
She wore a pendant shaped like a truelove knot, symbolizing everlasting love.
Learning to make a truelove knot is useful for both practical and decorative purposes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fisherman's knot - a knot used for tying two lines together
