flour mill

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊɚ mɪl / (AmE), / ˈflaʊə mɪl / (BrE)
A building or machine where grain is ground to produce flour.
place where grain is ground to flour

Examples

The old flour mill by the river has been converted into a museum.
Farmers brought their wheat to the flour mill to be processed into bread flour.
A modern flour mill can produce hundreds of tons of flour every day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mill - a building or machine for grinding grain (or other materials)
