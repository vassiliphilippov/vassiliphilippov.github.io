light-hearted banter

1. NOUN

/ laɪtˌhɑːrtɪd ˈbæntər / (AmE), / laɪtˌhɑːtɪd ˈbæntə / (BrE)
playful and cheerful conversation intended to amuse without causing offense
playful, cheerful conversation

Examples

There was light-hearted banter among the friends as they prepared dinner together.
The team's light-hearted banter made the meeting more enjoyable for everyone involved.
Their light-hearted banter helped break the ice at the start of the conference.

Synset Examples

She loved the light-hearted banter between coworkers.
