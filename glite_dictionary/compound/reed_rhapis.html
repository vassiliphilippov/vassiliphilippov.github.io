reed rhapis

1. NOUN

/ rid ˈræpɪs / (AmE), / riːd ˈræpɪs / (BrE)
A species of Chinese lady palm (Rhapis humilis) known for having more slender stems and finer sheath fibers than Rhapis excelsa.
species of slender Chinese lady palm

Examples

The reed rhapis is popular with gardeners for its elegant, slender appearance.
He placed a reed rhapis in the lobby to create a tropical atmosphere.
Compared to other palms, the reed rhapis requires less maintenance and grows well indoors.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Rhapis excelsa - a related species of lady palm with thicker stems
