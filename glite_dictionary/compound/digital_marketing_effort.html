digital marketing effort

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪdʒɪt̬əl ˈmɑrkətɪŋ ˈɛfərt / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl ˈmɑːkɪtɪŋ ˈɛfət / (BrE)
an organized activity or set of activities aimed at promoting products or services through online digital channels
activities for online promotion

Examples

The company increased its digital marketing efforts to attract younger customers.
Outsourcing their digital marketing effort helped the startup grow quickly online.
A well-planned digital marketing effort can significantly boost brand awareness.

Synset Examples

The ClickOps team streamlined our digital marketing efforts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: digital marketing - use of digital channels for marketing
  • Synonym: marketing campaign - planned series of marketing activities
