snow leopard

1. NOUN

/ snoʊ ˈlɛpərd / (AmE), / snəʊ ˈlɛpəd / (BrE)
a large wild cat of central Asian mountains, with long, thick, pale fur and a long tail
large wild cat of central Asia

Examples

The snow leopard is rarely seen in the wild due to its elusive nature.
Researchers track the movements of a snow leopard in the mountains of Mongolia.
Conservation efforts have helped increase the snow leopard population.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panther - a large wild cat, also called a leopard or cougar
  • Synonym: leopard - large spotted wild cat found in Africa and Asia
