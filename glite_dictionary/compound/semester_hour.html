semester hour

1. NOUN

/ səˈmɛstɚ aʊɚ / (AmE), / sɪˈmɛstə aʊə / (BrE)
a unit of academic credit representing one hour of class per week during a semester
unit of academic credit (one hour per week per semester)

Examples

To graduate, students must complete at least 120 semester hours of coursework.
Each semester hour typically corresponds to one hour in class per week.
The course is worth three semester hours toward your degree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: credit hour - a unit representing an hour of class or equivalent per week during a term
