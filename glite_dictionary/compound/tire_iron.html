tire iron

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪɚ ˌaɪɚn / (AmE), / ˈtaɪə ˌaɪən / (BrE)
a metal hand tool used as a lever for removing or installing a pneumatic tire on a wheel
tool for changing tires

Examples

He kept a tire iron in the trunk in case of a flat tire.
Using a tire iron, she pried the stubborn tire off the wheel.
Make sure you know how to use a tire iron before attempting to change a tire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crowbar - a straight metal bar used as a lever
  • Synonym: lug wrench - tool for loosening or tightening lug nuts on vehicle wheels
