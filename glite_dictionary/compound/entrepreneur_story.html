entrepreneur story

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːr ˈstɔːri / (AmE), / ˌɒntrəprəˈnɜː ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
a narrative recounting the experiences and journey of a business founder, often focusing on challenges and successes
narrative about a business founder

Examples

The magazine featured an entrepreneur story about a tech startup's rapid rise.
Listening to an entrepreneur story can provide valuable lessons for aspiring business owners.
Her favorite entrepreneur story describes overcoming failure to achieve success.

Synset Examples

Many entrepreneur stories inspire young innovators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: founder story - narrative about a company founder's journey
