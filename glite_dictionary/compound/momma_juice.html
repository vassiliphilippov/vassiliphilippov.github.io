momma juice

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːmə dʒus / (AmE), / ˈmɒmə dʒuːs / (BrE)
A humorous term for an alcoholic beverage or coffee, especially as a comforting drink for parents.
humorous term for alcohol or coffee (comfort drink)
informal slang

Examples

After a long day with the kids, she treated herself to some momma juice on the couch.
Sunday mornings aren't complete without my cup of momma juice.
He jokingly referred to the wine as momma juice at the party.

Synset Examples

She poured herself a glass of momma juice after the kids went to bed.
He joked that his coffee was his momma juice for the day.
