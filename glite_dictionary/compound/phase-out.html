phase-out

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪs aʊt / (AmE), / ˈfeɪz aʊt / (BrE)
the act or process of stopping something gradually in a planned way
planned gradual discontinuation

Examples

The phase-out of the old software will be completed by next year.
Many countries have agreed to a phase-out of single-use plastics to protect the environment.
Our company is planning a gradual phase-out of paper records in favor of digital storage.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to phase in - the introduction of something in stages
