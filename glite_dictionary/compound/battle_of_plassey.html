battle of Plassey

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæt̬.əl əv ˈplæsi / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl əv ˈplɑːsi / (BrE)
a decisive battle fought in 1757 in Bengal, where British forces under Clive defeated Siraj-ud-daula, establishing British supremacy in Bengal
decisive 1757 battle in Bengal establishing British rule

Examples

The battle of Plassey marked the beginning of British colonial rule in India.
Historians often debate the strategies used during the battle of Plassey.
The anniversary of the battle of Plassey is still remembered in Bengal today.

Etymology

Plassey is the anglicized version of Palashi, a village in Bengal, India where the battle took place.
