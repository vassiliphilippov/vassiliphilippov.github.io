little ebony spleenwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪtl ˈɛbəni ˈspliːnwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ˈɛbəni ˈspliːnwɜːt / (BrE)
A small fern native to tropical America, found from the southern United States to the West Indies and from Mexico to Brazil.
small tropical American fern

Examples

The little ebony spleenwort grows well in shaded, humid forests across Central and South America.
Botanists discovered a mature specimen of little ebony spleenwort near the edge of the rainforest.
Gardens specializing in exotic plants sometimes feature the delicate little ebony spleenwort.
