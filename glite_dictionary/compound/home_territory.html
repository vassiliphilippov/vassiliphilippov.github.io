home territory

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm ˈtɛr.ə.tɔːr.i / (AmE), / həʊm ˈtɛr.ɪ.tər.i / (BrE)
the area in which an animal habitually lives and moves; its usual range
an animal's habitual living area

Examples

The wolf pack fiercely defended its home territory from intruders.
A robin often sings to mark its home territory in the early morning.
Changes in the environment may force animals to leave their home territory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: territory - an area defended by an animal or group of animals
