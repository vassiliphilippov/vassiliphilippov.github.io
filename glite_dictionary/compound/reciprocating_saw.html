reciprocating saw

1. NOUN

/ rəˈsɪprəˌkeɪtɪŋ sɔ / (AmE), / rɪˌsɪprəˈkeɪtɪŋ sɔː / (BrE)
a portable power tool with a blade that moves back and forth rapidly, used for cutting various materials, typically featuring interchangeable blades and a surface plate for stability
portable power tool with a back-and-forth moving blade

Examples

The plumber used a reciprocating saw to cut through the old metal pipes quickly.
You can switch blades on a reciprocating saw to handle wood, metal, or plastic materials.
Always wear safety goggles when operating a reciprocating saw to protect your eyes from flying debris.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jigsaw - a power saw with a straight, narrow blade, used for cutting curves

Etymology

From 'reciprocating' (moving back and forth) + 'saw.'
