wrapping paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈræpɪŋ ˌpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈræpɪŋ ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
strong, decorative paper used for wrapping gifts or packages
decorative paper for wrapping gifts

Examples

She chose a bright red wrapping paper for the birthday present.
After tearing off the wrapping paper, the children squealed with excitement.
I need to buy more wrapping paper before the holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gift wrap - decorative paper for wrapping presents
