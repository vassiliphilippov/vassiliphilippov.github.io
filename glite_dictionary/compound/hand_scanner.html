hand scanner

1. NOUN

/ hænd ˈskænər / (AmE), / hænd ˈskanə / (BrE)
a portable scanner device that is moved by hand over a surface to capture an image or data
portable scanner moved by hand

Examples

The technician used a hand scanner to quickly record the barcode data from each product.
Early hand scanners were often less accurate than modern flatbed scanners but offered more portability.
She passed the hand scanner over the document to digitize it for the archive.

Synset Examples

Older hand scanners were monochrome, and produced light from an array of green LEDs to illuminate the image; later ones scan in monochrome or color, as desired.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scanner - a device that scans documents or images
