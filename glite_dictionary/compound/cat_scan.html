CAT scan

1. NOUN

/ si eɪ ti skæn / (AmE), / siː eɪ tiː skæn / (BrE)
A medical image produced using computerized axial tomography to create detailed cross-sectional views of the body or organs.
medical imaging scan (computed tomography)
medical

Examples

The doctor found a small tumor in the patient's CAT scan.
After her accident, Lisa had to undergo a CAT scan to check for internal injuries.
The results of the CAT scan confirmed the diagnosis and guided the surgeon during the operation.

Synset Examples

he analyzed the brain scan
you could see the tumor in the CAT scan

Related Terms

  • Synonym: CT scan - medical image produced by computed tomography

Etymology

Acronym for 'computerized axial tomography'.
