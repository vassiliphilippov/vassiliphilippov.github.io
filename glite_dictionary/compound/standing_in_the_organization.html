standing in the organization

1. NOUN

/ ˈstændɪŋ ɪn ði ˌɔːrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈstændɪŋ ɪn ði ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the level of respect, authority, or status a person holds within a particular group or company
status or position within a group or company

Examples

After successfully leading the project, her standing in the organization rose considerably.
Conflicts with colleagues damaged his standing in the organization.
A positive reputation can significantly improve your standing in the organization.

Synset Examples

Her standing in the organization improved after her promotion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: status - a person's social or professional position in relation to others
  • Synonym: position - a person's rank or role within an organization
