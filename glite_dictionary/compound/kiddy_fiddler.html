kiddy fiddler

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪdi ˌfɪdlɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɪdi ˌfɪdlə / (BrE)
(slang, offensive, chiefly British) A contemptuous term for a person who sexually abuses children; a paedophile.
offensive slang for child abuser
slang offensive british

Examples

The tabloid described the suspect as a notorious kiddy fiddler who had evaded arrest for years.
He was ostracised by the community after rumours spread that he was a kiddy fiddler.
Using the term kiddy fiddler in conversation can be deeply hurtful and is considered highly offensive.

Synset Examples

In the story, the kiddy fiddler entertained children with songs.
The movie featured a kiddy fiddler who loved to play pranks.
He dressed as a kiddy fiddler for the costume party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paedophile - a person sexually attracted to children
  • Synonym: child molester - a person who sexually abuses children

Etymology

British slang; 'kiddy' (child) + 'fiddler' (one who fiddles, i.e. fondles).
