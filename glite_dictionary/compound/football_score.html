football score

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌbɔl skɔr / (AmE), / ˈfʊtbɔːl skɔː / (BrE)
the number of points or goals achieved by each team in a football game
number of points/goals in a football game

Examples

The football score was tied at halftime, with both teams showing strong defense.
Everyone checked their phones for the latest football score during the meeting.
Yesterday's football score surprised fans, as the underdog team won by two goals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: score - the number of points gained in a game or test
