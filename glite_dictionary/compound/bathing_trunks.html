bathing trunks

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪðɪŋ ˌtrʌŋks / (AmE), / ˈbeɪðɪŋ ˌtrʌŋks / (BrE)
a pair of shorts made of light material, worn by men for swimming
men's swimming shorts

Examples

He changed into his bathing trunks before heading to the pool.
The store has a wide selection of colorful bathing trunks for summer.
After swimming, he hung his wet bathing trunks on the balcony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimsuit - a garment worn for swimming
  • Antonym: bikini - a two-piece swimsuit for women

Etymology

From 'bathing' (swimming) and 'trunks' (shorts), early 20th century.
