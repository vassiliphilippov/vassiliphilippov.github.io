home theater projector

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm ˈθiːətər prəˈdʒɛktər / (AmE), / həʊm ˈθɪətə prəˈdʒɛktə / (BrE)
A projector designed for displaying video or images in a home entertainment setup, typically for movies or television.
projector for home entertainment

Examples

We just bought a new home theater projector for our media room.
The home theater projector creates a cinema-like experience right in your living room.
With a good home theater projector, you can enjoy movies on a big screen without leaving home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: projector - a device that projects images onto a surface
