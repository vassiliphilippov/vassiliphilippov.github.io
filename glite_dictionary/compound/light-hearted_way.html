light-hearted way

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪt ˌhɑrtɪd weɪ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt ˌhɑːtɪd weɪ / (BrE)
in a cheerful and relaxed manner; free from seriousness or worry.
in a cheerful and carefree manner

Examples

She told the story in a light-hearted way to make everyone smile.
Even though the subject was difficult, he approached it in a light-hearted way.
Let's discuss the issue in a light-hearted way to ease the tension.

Synset Examples

She referred to her vajay in a light-hearted way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: light-hearted - cheerful and unconcerned; not serious
  • Synonym: carefree - free from worries; light-hearted
  • Antonym: serious - solemn or thoughtful rather than cheerful
