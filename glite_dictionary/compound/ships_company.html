ship's company

1. NOUN

/ ʃɪps ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ʃɪps ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
the entire crew of a ship, including both officers and enlisted personnel
all crew members of a ship

Examples

The ship's company assembled on the deck for the captain's address.
Discipline and teamwork among the ship's company are essential for a successful voyage.
After the long journey, the entire ship's company celebrated their safe arrival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crew - all the people who work on and operate a ship
