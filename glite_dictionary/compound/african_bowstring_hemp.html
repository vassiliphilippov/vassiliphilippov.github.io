African bowstring hemp

1. NOUN

/ ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈboʊ.strɪŋ hɛmp / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈbəʊ.strɪŋ hɛmp / (BrE)
a species of bowstring hemp native to South Africa, known for its fibrous leaves used for making rope and cord
a South African species of bowstring hemp

Examples

The fibers of African bowstring hemp are traditionally used to make strong rope.
Many gardeners appreciate the resilience of African bowstring hemp in dry climates.
The tall, upright leaves of African bowstring hemp add an architectural element to indoor spaces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowstring hemp - tropical plant with long fibrous leaves often used for cordage
