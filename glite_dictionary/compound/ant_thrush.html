ant thrush

1. NOUN

/ ænt θrʌʃ / (AmE), / ænt θrʌʃ / (BrE)
a bird of tropical Central and South America belonging to the genera Formicarius or Chamaeza, known for feeding on ants and other insects
a bird in Formicarius or Chamaeza, insect-eating

Examples

The ant thrush follows swarms of army ants to catch insects fleeing from the ants.
Several species of ant thrush inhabit the dense undergrowth of South American forests.
Birdwatchers spotted a rare ant thrush near the riverbank this morning.
