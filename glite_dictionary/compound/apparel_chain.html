apparel chain

1. NOUN

/ əˈpɛrəl tʃeɪn / (AmE), / əˈparəl tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a company operating a group of retail stores that sell clothing, often under the same brand or management
company owning a group of clothing stores

Examples

The company plans to expand its apparel chain into international markets next year.
She got a job as a manager at a well-known apparel chain downtown.
Many apparel chains offer online shopping options in addition to their physical stores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retail chain - a company operating a group of retail stores under the same brand
