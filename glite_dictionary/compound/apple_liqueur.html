apple liqueur

1. NOUN

/ ˈæpəl lɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / ˈapəl lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
A strong alcoholic beverage with a distinct apple flavour, usually sweet and served as a digestif or in cocktails.
alcoholic drink with apple flavour

Examples

He ordered an apple liqueur to finish his meal.
The bartender used apple liqueur to add a fruity note to the cocktail.
Some homemade desserts are enhanced by a splash of apple liqueur.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liqueur - a strong, sweet alcoholic drink flavored with fruit, herbs, spices, or flowers
