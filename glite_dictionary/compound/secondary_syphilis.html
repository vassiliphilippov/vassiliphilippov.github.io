secondary syphilis

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛk.ənˌdɛr.i ˈsɪ.fɪ.lɪs / (AmE), / ˌsɛk.ənˈdeə.ri ˈsɪ.fɪ.lɪs / (BrE)
the second stage of syphilis, characterized by eruptions on the skin and mucous membranes
second stage of syphilis infection
medical

Examples

If left untreated, secondary syphilis may cause widespread skin rashes.
During secondary syphilis, patients can develop lesions on the palms and soles.
The doctor explained that secondary syphilis is highly infectious and requires prompt treatment.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: primary syphilis - first stage of syphilis, with initial sores
  • Degree Higher: tertiary syphilis - third stage of syphilis, with severe symptoms
