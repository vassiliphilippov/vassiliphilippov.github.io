medical checkup

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈtʃɛkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈtʃɛkˌʌp / (BrE)
a thorough examination by a doctor to assess a person's general health, often including various tests depending on age, sex, and medical history
routine health examination by a doctor

Examples

She scheduled her annual medical checkup to monitor her overall health.
The company offered free medical checkups to all employees as part of its wellness program.
During the medical checkup, the doctor recommended several routine tests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physical examination - a medical examination to determine a person's overall health
