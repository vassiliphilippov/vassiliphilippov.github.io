lexical entry

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛksɪkəl ˈɛntri / (AmE), / ˈlɛksɪkəl ˈɛntri / (BrE)
an item in a dictionary or lexicon containing information about a word, such as its meaning, pronunciation, and usage
dictionary or lexicon entry for a word

Examples

The lexical entry for 'run' contains over twenty different meanings in the dictionary.
When compiling a dictionary, each lexical entry must be thoroughly researched and reviewed.
Researchers analyzed the lexical entry of each word to study semantic changes over time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dictionary entry - an entry in a dictionary giving details about a word or phrase

Etymology

From 'lexical' (relating to words), from Greek 'lexikos', and 'entry', from Old French 'entree'.
