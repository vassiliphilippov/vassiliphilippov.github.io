inquiry agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnkwaɪəri ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈkwaɪəri ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a private detective employed to investigate and gather information, especially for private individuals or organizations
private detective

Examples

The family hired an inquiry agent to look into the mysterious disappearance.
As an inquiry agent, she specialized in tracking down missing persons.
The police suggested contacting an inquiry agent for further investigation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: private detective - a person employed to investigate and gather information privately
