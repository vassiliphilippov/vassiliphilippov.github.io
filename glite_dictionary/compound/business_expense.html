business expense

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznɪs ɪkˈspɛns / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ɪkˈspɛns / (BrE)
an ordinary and necessary cost incurred in the course of running a business, especially for tax purposes
cost incurred running a business

Examples

Meals with clients may qualify as business expenses if they are directly related to your work.
Travel costs to conferences are typically considered business expenses by most companies.
Please keep receipts for all business expenses to simplify the accounting process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deductible - an expense that can be subtracted from taxable income
