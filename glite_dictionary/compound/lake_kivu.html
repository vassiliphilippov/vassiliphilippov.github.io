Lake Kivu

1. PROPN

/ leɪk ˈkiːvuː / (AmE), / leɪk ˈkiːvuː / (BrE)
a lake in central Africa, located between the Democratic Republic of the Congo and Rwanda, known for its scenic beauty and geological significance
lake in central Africa, between Congo and Rwanda

Examples

The shores of Lake Kivu are popular with tourists seeking breathtaking views and relaxation.
Scientists study Lake Kivu to understand its unique methane gas reserves.
Fishing communities depend on Lake Kivu for their livelihoods.
