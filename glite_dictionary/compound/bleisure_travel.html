bleisure travel

1. NOUN

/ ˈbliːʒər ˌtrævəl / (AmE), / ˈbliːʒə ˌtrævəl / (BrE)
Travel that combines both business and leisure activities, typically on the same trip.
business and leisure travel combined

Examples

Many companies now encourage bleisure travel to enhance work-life balance for their employees.
She extended her business trip into a bleisure travel experience by exploring the city over the weekend.
The rise of remote work has made bleisure travel more accessible than ever before.

Synset Examples

Bleisure travel is becoming increasingly popular among young professionals.

Etymology

Blend of 'business' and 'leisure' plus 'travel'.
