school dictionary

1. NOUN

/ skuːl ˈdɪkʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / skuːl ˈdɪkʃənəri / (BrE)
A dictionary specially designed for students, often those learning a foreign language, containing definitions, examples, and explanations tailored to educational needs.
dictionary for students or language learners

Examples

Our teacher recommended a school dictionary to help us expand our vocabulary.
She uses a school dictionary to look up difficult English words.
The school dictionary includes example sentences and pronunciation guides for students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: learner's dictionary - dictionary written for people learning a foreign language
  • Degree Higher: dictionary - a book or electronic resource listing words of a language with their meanings
