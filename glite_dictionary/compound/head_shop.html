head shop

1. NOUN

/ hɛd ʃɑp / (AmE), / hɛd ʃɒp / (BrE)
a shop specializing in the sale of equipment and paraphernalia related to the use of recreational drugs, such as pipes and rolling papers
shop selling drug-related paraphernalia

Examples

There's a new head shop downtown that sells incense and glass pipes.
He stopped by the head shop to buy some rolling papers.
Many head shops also stock posters and novelty items.

Synset Examples

he bought some roach clips and hashish pipes at the head shop

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoke shop - a shop selling tobacco products and related items

Etymology

Attested since the 1960s; 'head' in 1960s slang referred to a user of drugs, especially cannabis.
