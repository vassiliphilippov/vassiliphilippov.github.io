digital watch

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪdʒɪtl̩ wɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtl̩ wɒtʃ / (BrE)
a watch that shows the time with digits on an electronic display rather than with hands on a dial
watch with a digital display

Examples

He checked his digital watch to see how much time was left.
My new digital watch has an alarm and a stopwatch feature.
Unlike traditional models, a digital watch displays the time as numbers.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: analog watch - a watch that displays time using hour and minute hands
