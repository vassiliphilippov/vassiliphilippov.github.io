husband-in-law

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌzbənd ɪn lɔ / (AmE), / ˈhʌzbənd ɪn lɔː / (BrE)
the husband of one's sibling-in-law; the husband of one's sibling's spouse
husband of a sibling-in-law

Examples

Her husband-in-law is a great support to her.
I met my brother's husband-in-law at the family reunion.
My sister's husband-in-law is quite the chef.

Synset Examples

Etymology

Formed from 'husband' + 'in-law', the latter denoting family by marriage.
