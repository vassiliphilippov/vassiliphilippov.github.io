cap screw

1. NOUN

/ kæp skru / (AmE), / kap skruː / (BrE)
A screw with a finished head, designed to be screwed into a tapped hole to hold machine parts together.
screw with a finished head for machine parts

Examples

The engine block was secured with several cap screws to ensure tightness.
Make sure you select the right size cap screw for the bracket.
He replaced the damaged cap screw with a new one from the toolbox.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bolt - A similar type of fastener, usually used with a nut
