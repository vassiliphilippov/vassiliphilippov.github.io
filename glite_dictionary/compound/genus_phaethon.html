genus Phaethon

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈfeɪ.ə.θɒn / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈfiː.ə.θɒn / (BrE)
The type genus of the bird family Phaethontidae, comprising the tropicbirds.
type genus of tropicbirds

Examples

The genus Phaethon includes three extant species of tropicbirds found in tropical oceans.
Researchers studied nesting habits across the genus Phaethon to compare behaviors among species.
Tropicbirds belonging to the genus Phaethon are known for their striking tail feathers.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Phaethontidae - the bird family of tropicbirds

Etymology

From Greek 'Phaethon', the name of a mythological figure associated with the sun.
