power-hungry

1. ADJ

/ ˈpaʊərˌhʌŋɡri / (AmE), / ˈpaʊəˌhʌŋɡri / (BrE)
having a strong and excessive desire for power; driven by the wish to control or dominate others
desiring power excessively

Examples

The power-hungry CEO was willing to do anything to climb to the top of the company.
Many citizens opposed the power-hungry regime, fearing it would limit their freedom.
Her power-hungry attitude made it difficult for others to trust her intentions.

Synset Examples

His power-hungry nature led to his downfall.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: ambitious - having or showing a strong desire and determination to succeed
  • Degree Higher: tyrannical - exercising power in a cruel or arbitrary way
  • Degree Higher: megalomaniacal - having a delusional sense of power or importance
