theme of morality

1. NOUN

/ θim əv məˈrælɪti / (AmE), / θiːm əv məˈrælɪti / (BrE)
a subject or idea in literature, art, or discussion that concerns questions of right and wrong behavior
moral topic in literature or art

Examples

Many classic novels center around the theme of morality and the consequences of one's actions.
In her lecture, the professor discussed how the theme of morality is portrayed through the characters' choices.
Modern films often revisit the theme of morality in the context of contemporary issues.

Synset Examples

Victorian literature often explores themes of morality and society.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: morality - principles concerning the distinction between right and wrong
  • Synonym: theme - the subject or main idea in a piece of writing, discussion, or work of art
