fast mate

1. NOUN

/ fæst meɪt / (AmE), / fɑːst meɪt / (BrE)
a very close friend or companion
close friend; companion

Examples

They became fast mates during their university years.
Even after moving to different cities, the two remained fast mates.
Everyone knew Jim and Ravi were fast mates who did everything together.

Synset Examples

They became fast mates during their university years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: close friend - a person with whom one shares a strong bond of friendship
  • Synonym: best friend - a person's closest and dearest friend
  • Antonym: acquaintance - a person one knows slightly, but who is not a close friend

Etymology

From 'fast' in the sense of 'firm, fixed' (meaning loyal or close), and 'mate' meaning companion.
