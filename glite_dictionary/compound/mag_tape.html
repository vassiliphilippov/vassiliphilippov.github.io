mag tape

1. NOUN

/ mæɡ teɪp / (AmE), / mæɡ teɪp / (BrE)
a long, thin strip of plastic coated with iron oxide, used for recording audio, video signals, or computer data
magnetic tape for data or audio/video recording

Examples

The old computer used mag tape to store its data backups.
She found a box of vintage music recorded on mag tape in the attic.
Engineers once relied on mag tape for archiving important television broadcasts.

Synset Examples

he took along a dozen tapes to record the interview

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnetic tape - tape for recording audio, video, or data using magnetic signals

Etymology

Shortened form of 'magnetic tape'.
