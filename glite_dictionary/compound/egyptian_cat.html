Egyptian cat

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈdʒɪpʃən kæt / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒɪpʃən kat / (BrE)
a domestic cat originating in Egypt, often characterized by distinctive physical features or referenced in historical contexts
domestic cat breed from Egypt

Examples

The Egyptian cat was revered and even worshipped in ancient Egypt.
Many believe the Egyptian cat is one of the oldest domesticated breeds.
Her new pet is an elegant Egyptian cat with striking green eyes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mau - an Egyptian breed of domestic cat known for its spotted coat
