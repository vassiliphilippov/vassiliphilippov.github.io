chocolate chip cookie

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑklət tʃɪp ˈkʊki / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət tʃɪp ˈkʊki / (BrE)
A flat, sweet baked cookie that contains small pieces or chips of chocolate.
cookie made with chocolate chips

Examples

She baked a batch of chocolate chip cookies for the school fundraiser.
A glass of milk goes perfectly with a warm chocolate chip cookie.
Are these chocolate chip cookies homemade or store-bought?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cookie - a small, sweet baked treat, usually round and flat
