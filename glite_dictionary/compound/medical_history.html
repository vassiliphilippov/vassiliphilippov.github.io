medical history

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
A record of a patient's past illnesses, treatments, and relevant health information, as recalled or provided by the patient.
record of a patient's past illnesses and treatments
medical

Examples

The doctor reviewed her medical history before recommending a treatment plan.
It is important to provide accurate details about your medical history during your first appointment.
A family medical history can help identify potential genetic health risks.

Related Terms
