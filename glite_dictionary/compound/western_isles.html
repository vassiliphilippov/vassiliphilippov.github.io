Western Isles

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɛstərn aɪlz / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən aɪlz / (BrE)
a group of more than 500 islands off the western coast of Scotland, also known as the Outer Hebrides.
group of islands off western Scotland
british

Examples

Many tourists visit the Western Isles in summer to experience their unique landscapes and culture.
The ferry to the Western Isles departs from the port early each morning.
She spent her holiday exploring several of the Western Isles, including Lewis and Harris.
