kiss-up attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪs ʌp ˈæ.tɪ.tud / (AmE), / ˈkɪs ʌp ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
an excessively flattering or sycophantic manner, especially to gain favor with someone in authority
excessively flattering behavior
informal

Examples

Her kiss-up attitude annoys the rest of the team.
He succeeded at the job mainly because of his kiss-up attitude toward his supervisors.
Most people dislike a kiss-up attitude, preferring genuine honesty instead.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sycophantic - behaving in an excessively obedient or attentive way to gain advantage
  • Synonym: obsequious - showing servile complaisance or deference
  • Antonym: straightforward - honest, direct, and not excessively flattering
