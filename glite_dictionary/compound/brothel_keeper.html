brothel keeper

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɑθəl ˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈbrɒθəl ˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person, especially a woman, who manages or runs a brothel
person who manages a brothel

Examples

The police arrested the brothel keeper during a raid on the premises.
As a brothel keeper, she was well known in the local community.
The court found the brothel keeper guilty of multiple offenses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: madam - woman who manages a brothel
