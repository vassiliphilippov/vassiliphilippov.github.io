Isle Royale National Park

1. PROPN

/ aɪl rɔɪˈæl ˌnæʃənəl ˈpɑrk / (AmE), / aɪl rɔɪˈal ˌnaʃənəl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
A national park located on Isle Royale in Lake Superior, Michigan, known for its wilderness and prehistoric iron mines.
national park on Isle Royale, Michigan

Examples

We spent a week camping in Isle Royale National Park exploring its rugged trails and shoreline.
Wolves and moose are iconic species that inhabit Isle Royale National Park.
The remote beauty of Isle Royale National Park attracts hikers and kayakers from across the country.
