cryptocuck joke

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪptoʊ kʌk dʒoʊk / (AmE), / ˈkrɪptəʊ kʌk dʒəʊk / (BrE)
A joke that references infidelity or cuckoldry within the context of cryptocurrency culture, often mocking or satirizing participants in crypto communities.
joke about cuckoldry in cryptocurrency circles
slang

Examples

The forum was filled with cryptocuck jokes after the latest market crash.
He made a cryptocuck joke that poked fun at people who hold losing coins.
Social media threads often feature sarcastic cryptocuck jokes whenever a token's value plummets.

Synset Examples

She laughed at her friend's cryptocuck jokes.

Etymology

Blend of 'crypto' (relating to cryptocurrency) and 'cuck' (slang for a man whose partner is unfaithful, used as an insult in online slang).
