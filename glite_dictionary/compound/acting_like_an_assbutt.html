to acting like an assbutt

1. VERB

/ ˈæktɪŋ laɪk ən ˈæsˌbʌt / (AmE), / ˈæktɪŋ laɪk ən ˈæsˌbʌt / (BrE)
Behaving in a foolish, irritating, or obnoxious manner.
behaving foolishly or obnoxiously
informal slang

Examples

If you keep acting like an assbutt, no one will want to team up with you.
She apologized for acting like an assbutt during the meeting yesterday.
Could you please stop acting like an assbutt and help us out?

Synset Examples

Stop acting like an assbutt and listen to me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acting like a jerk - behaving in a rude or obnoxious way
  • Synonym: acting like a fool - behaving in a silly or stupid manner

Etymology

Humorous compound, popularized in internet and fandom culture; 'assbutt' blends 'ass' and 'butt', both informal terms for a person's backside.
