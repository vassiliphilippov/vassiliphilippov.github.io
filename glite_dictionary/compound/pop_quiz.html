pop quiz

1. NOUN

/ pɑp kwɪz / (AmE), / pɒp kwɪz / (BrE)
a short test given to students without warning in order to assess their understanding of recent material
an unannounced classroom quiz

Examples

The teacher surprised the class with a pop quiz at the start of the lesson.
Everyone groaned when they realized today's assignment was actually a pop quiz.
Some students study regularly just in case there is a pop quiz.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quiz - a short written or oral test
