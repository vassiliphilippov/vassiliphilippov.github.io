knotty issue

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑti ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈnɒti ˈɪsjuː / (BrE)
A complicated or difficult problem that is hard to solve or deal with.
difficult or complex problem

Examples

The committee spent hours debating the knotty issue of tax reform.
Environmental regulations often give rise to knotty issues in policy making.
Finding a fair solution to the funding question was a knotty issue for the organizers.

Synset Examples

The project has some knotty issues to address.
This is a knotty problem we need to solve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: problem - something that needs to be solved
  • Synonym: complication - something making a situation more difficult
