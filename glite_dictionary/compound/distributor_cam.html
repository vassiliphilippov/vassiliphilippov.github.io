distributor cam

1. NOUN

/ dɪsˈtrɪb.jə.tɚ kæm / (AmE), / dɪsˈtrɪb.jʊ.tə kæm / (BrE)
the cam located inside a vehicle's distributor that rotates to make contact with spark plug terminals in the correct sequence
cam inside distributor for spark timing

Examples

The mechanic replaced the worn distributor cam to improve the engine's performance.
A faulty distributor cam can lead to misfiring and irregular engine operation.
He examined the distributor cam to check for any signs of wear or damage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distributor - a device in an engine that directs high-voltage current to spark plugs
