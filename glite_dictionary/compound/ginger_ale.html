ginger ale

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪndʒər eɪl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒə eɪl / (BrE)
A carbonated soft drink flavored with ginger.
carbonated ginger-flavored soft drink

Examples

She ordered a glass of ginger ale with her lunch.
Many people use ginger ale as a remedy for an upset stomach.
Could you pick up some ginger ale from the store on your way home?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ginger beer - non-alcoholic or alcoholic drink similar to ginger ale but with a stronger flavor

Etymology

From 'ginger' (the spice) + 'ale' (from Old English 'ealu', meaning 'ale' or 'beer').
