calendar year

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæləndɚ jɪr / (AmE), / ˈkælɪndə jɪə / (BrE)
the period of twelve months from January 1 to December 31, as used in the Gregorian calendar
year from January 1 to December 31

Examples

The company's profits are reported for each calendar year.
She plans to travel extensively during the next calendar year.
Tax deadlines are based on the calendar year rather than the fiscal year.
