brightly-coloured

1. ADJ

/ ˈbraɪtli ˈkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈbraɪtli ˈkʌləd / (BrE)
Having vivid and intense colors; marked by bright, striking coloration.
having vivid or bright colors
british

Examples

The parade featured brightly-coloured costumes from around the world.
She decorated her room with brightly-coloured posters and cushions.
A flock of brightly-coloured parrots flew overhead in the tropical rainforest.

Related Terms
