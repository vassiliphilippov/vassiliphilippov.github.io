bowling equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊlɪŋ ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlɪŋ ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
The set of items required to play the game of bowling, such as pins, balls, and shoes.
items used for bowling

Examples

Before joining the league, she invested in proper bowling equipment to improve her game.
Some centers offer rental bowling equipment for beginners who do not have their own.
The store specializes in high-quality bowling equipment, including balls, shoes, and bags.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowling ball - heavy ball rolled in bowling to knock down pins
  • Synonym: bowling pin - pin set up to be knocked down in bowling
