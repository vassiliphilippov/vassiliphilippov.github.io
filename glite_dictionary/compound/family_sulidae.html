family Sulidae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli suːˈlɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli sjuːˈlɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of seabirds including the gannets and boobies.
family of seabirds: gannets and boobies

Examples

The family Sulidae is known for its excellent divers, such as gannets and boobies.
Researchers studied the feeding habits of birds in the family Sulidae along coastal regions.
Several species within the family Sulidae are found on remote islands around the world.

Etymology

From scientific Latin Sulidae, based on genus Sula (gannet, booby).
