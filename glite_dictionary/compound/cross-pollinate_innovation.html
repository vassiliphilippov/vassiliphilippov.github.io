to cross-pollinate innovation

1. VERB

/ krɑs ˈpɑləˌneɪt ˌɪnəˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / krɒs ˈpɒlɪneɪt ˌɪnəˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
to introduce and blend ideas from different sources to foster creativity and new developments
blend ideas to stimulate creativity

Examples

Leaders are encouraged to cross-pollinate innovation by forming diverse teams from different departments.
The tech summit helped companies cross-pollinate innovation in artificial intelligence and healthcare.
By collaborating globally, scientists can cross-pollinate innovation and accelerate breakthrough discoveries.

Synset Examples

The conference aims to cross-pollinate innovation across industries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to cross-fertilize - to mix different ideas or influences to create something new
  • Synonym: to innovate - to introduce new methods, ideas, or products

Etymology

Figurative use of 'cross-pollinate' (from pollination in plants), applied to ideas and creativity.
