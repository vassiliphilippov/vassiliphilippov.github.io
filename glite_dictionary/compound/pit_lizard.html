pit lizard

1. NOUN

/ pɪt ˈlɪzɚd / (AmE), / pɪt ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who frequently visits nightclubs or bars and enjoys the nightlife scene
nightlife enthusiast; club or bar regular
informal

Examples

After moving to the city, he quickly became known as a pit lizard among his friends.
She used to be a pit lizard before settling down and starting a family.
Every Friday, the pit lizards flocked to their favorite downtown bar for drinks and music.

Synset Examples

He was such a pit lizard, always out late on weekends.
They jokingly called him a pit lizard for his love of nightlife.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: night owl - a person who stays up late or is active at night
  • Antonym: homebody - a person who prefers to stay at home
