histopath's report

1. NOUN

/ hɪstoʊpæθs rɪˈpɔrt / (AmE), / hɪstəʊpæθs rɪˈpɔːt / (BrE)
a medical report on the findings from microscopic examination of tissue samples
medical tissue examination report
medical

Examples

The histopath's report confirmed that the tumor was benign.
Doctors awaited the histopath's report before starting treatment.
A detailed histopath's report can provide vital clues for complex diagnoses.

Synset Examples

The histopath's report was crucial for the diagnosis.

Etymology

Shortened from 'histopathology', from Greek 'histos' (tissue) + 'pathos' (disease). 'Report' from Latin 'reportare' (to bring back).
