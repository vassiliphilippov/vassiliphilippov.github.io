bowtie pasta

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊˌtaɪ ˈpæstə / (AmE), / ˈbəʊˌtaɪ ˈpɑːstə / (BrE)
A type of pasta with scalloped edges and a pinch in the middle, resembling a bow tie.
pasta shaped like a bow tie

Examples

She made a salad using bowtie pasta and fresh vegetables.
The chef prefers bowtie pasta because it holds the sauce well.
We bought a box of bowtie pasta for tonight's dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farfalle - Italian name for bowtie-shaped pasta
