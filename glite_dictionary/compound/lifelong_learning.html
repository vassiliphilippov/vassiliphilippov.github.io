lifelong learning

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪf.lɔŋ ˈlɝː.nɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf.lɒŋ ˈlɜː.nɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of continuously developing skills and knowledge throughout one's life, often outside formal educational settings
continuous personal or professional education

Examples

The university offers programs that encourage lifelong learning for people of all ages.
Embracing lifelong learning helps individuals adapt to a rapidly changing world.
Employers increasingly value lifelong learning as technology transforms workplaces.

Synset Examples

As a schoolperson, he believed in lifelong learning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: continuing education - education for adults after leaving formal schooling
  • Synonym: adult education - systematic learning activities undertaken by adults
