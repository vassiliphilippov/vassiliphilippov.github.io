shout-out

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃaʊt aʊt / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊt aʊt / (BrE)
a public expression of recognition, thanks, or acknowledgment to someone or something
public recognition or acknowledgment
informal

Examples

I'd like to give a shout-out to all the volunteers who made this event possible.
The host gave a shout-out to the show's sponsors at the end of the episode.
She posted a shout-out on social media to thank her team for their hard work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mention - an instance of referring to someone or something
