grippy sock jail

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrɪpi sɑk dʒeɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪpi sɒk dʒeɪl / (BrE)
a slang, humorous term for a mental health facility or psychiatric ward, referencing the socks often given to patients
humorous slang for a psychiatric facility
slang informal

Examples

After my anxiety attack, I spent a week in grippy sock jail to recover.
She joked that her vacation was basically grippy sock jail, but with better food.
Some people use humor, like calling the hospital grippy sock jail, to cope with their experiences.

Synset Examples

The retreat felt like grippy sock jail, but it was relaxing.
She laughed, calling her weekend away grippy sock jail.
After a rough night, he joked about being in grippy sock jail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychiatric ward - a hospital section for psychiatric care
  • Synonym: mental hospital - a hospital for psychiatric treatment

Etymology

From 'grippy socks', slang for hospital-issue non-slip socks given to patients, combined with 'jail' to suggest forced stay.
