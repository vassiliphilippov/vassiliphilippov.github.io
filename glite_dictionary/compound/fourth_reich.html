Fourth Reich

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrθ ˈraɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɔːθ ˈraɪx / (BrE)
a hypothetical future German empire, especially referring to the idea of a resurgence or continuation of Nazi ideology
hypothetical future German empire or Nazi resurgence

Examples

Some conspiracy theorists claim that a Fourth Reich could rise in Europe.
The documentary explored fears of a Fourth Reich among European nations.
Debates about the possibility of a Fourth Reich remain controversial among historians.

Synset Examples

He warned against the ideology of a Fourth Reich.
Some conspiracy theories discuss a supposed Fourth Reich.
The concept of a Fourth Reich is often debated in history.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Third Reich - the official Nazi designation for the regime in Germany from 1933 to 1945

Etymology

From German 'Reich' meaning 'empire'; modelled after 'Third Reich,' the official name for Nazi Germany (1933–1945).
