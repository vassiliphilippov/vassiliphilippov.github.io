summer-flowering

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌmɚ ˌflaʊərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə ˌflaʊərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of plants) blooming during the summer season.
blooming in summer

Examples

The summer-flowering shrubs brightened the garden with vibrant colors in July.
Many gardeners plant summer-flowering bulbs in the spring for maximum effect.
She prefers summer-flowering perennials because they offer months of continuous blooms.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: winter-flowering - (of plants) blooming during the winter season
