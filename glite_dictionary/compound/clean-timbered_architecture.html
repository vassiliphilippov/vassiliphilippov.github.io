clean-timbered architecture

1. NOUN

/ klin ˈtɪmbɚd ˌɑrkɪˈtɛktʃɚ / (AmE), / kliːn ˈtɪmbəd ˌɑːkɪˈtɛktʃə / (BrE)
an architectural style characterized by simple, straight lines and the prominent use of wooden elements
architecture with clean lines and wood

Examples

Many modern homes feature clean-timbered architecture for a minimalist aesthetic.
The museum's clean-timbered architecture blends harmoniously with the surrounding forest.
She studied the principles of clean-timbered architecture during her design course.

Synset Examples
