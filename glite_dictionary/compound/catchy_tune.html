catchy tune

1. NOUN

/ ˈkætʃi tuːn / (AmE), / ˈkatʃi tjuːn / (BrE)
a melody that is easy to remember and likely to be repeated or sung along to
memorable, singable melody

Examples

The radio played a catchy tune that got stuck in my head all day.
Every ad jingle needs a catchy tune to be effective.
She wrote a catchy tune that became an instant hit with teenagers.

Synset Examples

That song has such a catchy tune, I can't stop humming it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: earworm - a catchy piece of music that continually repeats through a person's mind
