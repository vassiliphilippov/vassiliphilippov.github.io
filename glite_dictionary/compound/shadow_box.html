shadow box

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃædoʊ ˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈʃadəʊ ˌbɒks / (BrE)
a shallow, rectangular container with a transparent front, used to protect and display small items such as jewelry or coins
display case with a transparent front

Examples

She arranged her grandmother's medals in a shadow box to keep them safe and visible.
A shadow box makes a thoughtful gift for someone who collects vintage postcards.
The artist created a shadow box filled with miniature sculptures and delicate fabrics.
