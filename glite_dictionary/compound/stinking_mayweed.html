stinking mayweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪŋkɪŋ ˈmeɪˌwɪd / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkɪŋ ˈmeɪˌwiːd / (BrE)
a strong-smelling weed with white, daisy-like flowers and yellow centers, commonly found in cultivated ground and waste places
rank-smelling weed with daisy-like flowers

Examples

The pungent scent of stinking mayweed filled the abandoned field in midsummer.
Farmers often try to remove stinking mayweed from their crops due to its strong odor and invasive nature.
She recognized stinking mayweed by its white petals and the unpleasant smell when the leaves were crushed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mayweed - a weed with daisy-like white-rayed flower heads and yellow centers
