birth-control campaigner

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝθ kənˌtroʊl kəmˈpeɪnər / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθ kənˌtrəʊl kəmˈpeɪnə / (BrE)
a person who actively promotes the use and acceptance of birth control and family planning measures
person advocating birth control

Examples

The birth-control campaigner spoke passionately about reproductive rights at the conference.
As a renowned birth-control campaigner, she helped to change national attitudes toward contraception.
Many birth-control campaigners have argued for better access to education and resources for women.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: family planning advocate - person who supports family planning measures
