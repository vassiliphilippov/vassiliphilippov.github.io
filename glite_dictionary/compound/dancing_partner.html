dancing partner

1. NOUN

/ ˈdænsɪŋ ˌpɑrtnər / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnsɪŋ ˌpɑːtnə / (BrE)
a person with whom one dances as a pair
person you dance with

Examples

She found a new dancing partner for the upcoming competition.
At the ball, everyone waited eagerly to be chosen as someone's dancing partner.
He introduced his dancing partner to the rest of the class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partner - a person who takes part in an activity with another
