measuring instrument

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛʒərɪŋ ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒ(ə)rɪŋ ˈɪnstrʊmənt / (BrE)
A device used to determine the extent, amount, quantity, or degree of something.
device for measuring quantities or degrees

Examples

The laboratory was equipped with every measuring instrument necessary for the experiment.
A thermometer is a common measuring instrument used to check temperature.
Before beginning the survey, make sure your measuring instrument is properly calibrated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gauge - device for measuring a physical quantity
  • Synonym: meter - device that measures and records the quantity, degree, or rate of something
  • Synonym: instrument - tool or device for precise work or measurement
