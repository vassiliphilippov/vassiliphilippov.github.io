doll's house

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑlz ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈdɒlz ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a small model house, typically made as a toy for children to furnish and play with miniature figures
miniature toy house for children

Examples

Samantha proudly decorated her new doll's house with tiny furniture and curtains.
The cottage was so small it looked like a doll's house from the road.
A beautifully crafted Victorian doll's house sat on display in the museum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dollhouse - alternate spelling used mainly in US English
