cut of pork

1. NOUN

/ kʌt əv pɔrk / (AmE), / kʌt əv pɔːk / (BrE)
A particular portion of meat taken from a hog or pig, typically prepared and sold for cooking or eating.
portion of meat from a pig

Examples

The butcher displayed various cuts of pork in the refrigerated case.
For this recipe, you can use any cut of pork you prefer, such as loin or shoulder.
Each cut of pork offers a different texture and flavor when cooked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cut of beef - portion of meat from a cow
  • Synonym: pork - meat from a pig
