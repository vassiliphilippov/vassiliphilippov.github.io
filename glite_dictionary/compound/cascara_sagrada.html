cascara sagrada

1. NOUN

/ kæsˈkɑːrə səˈɡrɑːdə / (AmE), / kæsˈkɑːrə səˈɡrɑːdə / (BrE)
the dried bark of the cascara buckthorn, used as a natural laxative
dry bark used as a laxative

Examples

The herbalist recommended cascara sagrada as a gentle treatment for occasional constipation.
Many over-the-counter supplements contain cascara sagrada for its natural laxative properties.
She checked the label to confirm that cascara sagrada was included among the active ingredients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laxative - a substance that promotes bowel movements

Etymology

From Spanish, literally ‘sacred bark’.
