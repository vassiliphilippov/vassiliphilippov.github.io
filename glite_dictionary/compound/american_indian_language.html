American Indian language

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən ˈɪndiən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈɪndiən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
any of the languages traditionally spoken by the Indigenous peoples of the Americas.
a language of Indigenous peoples of the Americas

Examples

Many university departments offer courses in American Indian languages such as Navajo and Cherokee.
The preservation of American Indian languages is crucial for maintaining cultural heritage.
She wrote her thesis on the structure of an American Indian language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Native American language - a language spoken by Native Americans; alternative term
