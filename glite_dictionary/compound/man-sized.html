man-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈmæn.saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈmæn.saɪzd / (BrE)
very large; of a size suitable for or comparable to that of an adult man.
very large; adult-man-sized

Examples

He took a man-sized bite out of the sandwich, finishing it in two bites.
The woodsman carried a man-sized axe on his shoulder as he walked through the forest.
She was surprised to find a man-sized bear paw print in her garden.

Synset Examples

a man-sized piece of cake

Related Terms

  • Synonym: king-sized - extremely large; larger than normal size
