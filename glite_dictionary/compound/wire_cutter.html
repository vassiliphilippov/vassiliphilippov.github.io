wire cutter

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪɚ ˌkʌtɚ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪə ˌkʌtə / (BrE)
a hand tool designed for cutting wire, typically with two handles and sharp blades
tool for cutting wire

Examples

He reached for the wire cutter to snip the thick copper cable.
Every electrician needs a good pair of wire cutters in their toolkit.
She used the wire cutter to trim the ends of the floral wire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pliers - hand tool with holding and bending jaws
