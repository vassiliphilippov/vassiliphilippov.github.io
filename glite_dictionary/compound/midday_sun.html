midday sun

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪdˌdeɪ sʌn / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌdeɪ sʌn / (BrE)
the sun when it is at or near its highest point in the sky, typically around noon, and often very bright or intense
the sun at or near noon

Examples

We took a break to avoid the midday sun during our hike.
Working in the midday sun can lead to heat exhaustion if you're not careful.
The crops withered in the intense midday sun.

Synset Examples

The shade provided relief from the midday sun.
The noonday sun can be quite harsh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noonday sun - the sun at its highest point at noon
