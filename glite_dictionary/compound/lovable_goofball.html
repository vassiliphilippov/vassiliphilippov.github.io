lovable goofball

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌvəbəl ˈɡuːfˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈlʌvəbəl ˈɡuːfbɔːl / (BrE)
a person who is silly or goofy in a way that is charming and endearing
endearingly silly person
informal

Examples

Everyone in the office loves Mark because he’s such a lovable goofball.
Though she acts like a lovable goofball, she always cheers us up when we’re down.
He made a mistake on stage, but his lovable goofball charm won the audience over.

Synset Examples

Don't mind him; he's just a lovable goofball.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goofball - a silly or foolish person
