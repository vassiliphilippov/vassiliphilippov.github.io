breathing device

1. NOUN

/ ˈbriðɪŋ dɪˌvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈbriːðɪŋ dɪˌvaɪs / (BrE)
a medical device designed to assist or support breathing, especially in cases of respiratory failure
apparatus aiding breathing
medical

Examples

The patient was connected to a breathing device after experiencing severe respiratory distress.
Doctors explained how the breathing device would help maintain adequate oxygen levels.
Manufacturers are developing more portable breathing devices for home use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ventilator - a machine providing mechanical ventilation by moving breathable air into and out of the lungs
