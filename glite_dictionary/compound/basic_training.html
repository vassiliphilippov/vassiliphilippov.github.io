basic training

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsɪk ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsɪk ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the initial period of training for new military personnel, involving intensive physical exercise and discipline
initial military training for new recruits

Examples

After enlisting, he went through basic training before being assigned to his unit.
The recruits found basic training both challenging and rewarding.
Completing basic training is a rite of passage for all new soldiers.
