cucumber-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈkjuːkʌmbɚ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈkjuːkʌmbə ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a shape that resembles that of a cucumber—long, narrow, and cylindrical
shaped like a cucumber

Examples

The scientist described the bacteria as cucumber-shaped under the microscope.
They discovered a strange, cucumber-shaped object washed up on the beach after the storm.
The sculpture featured a cucumber-shaped glass body that caught the sunlight beautifully.
