comfortable berth

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌmfərtəbəl bɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈkʌmfətəbl bɜːθ / (BrE)
a sleeping place that is cozy and provides physical comfort, especially on a train, ship, or similar means of transport
cozy sleeping place (especially on transport)

Examples

The first-class ticket included a comfortable berth in a private compartment.
He stretched out in his comfortable berth as the train sped through the night.
She was grateful to have a comfortable berth after a long day of travel.

Synset Examples

She found a comfortable berth on the overnight train.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: berth - a fixed bunk or bed, especially on a ship or train
  • Synonym: bunk - a narrow bed, often stacked or in shared sleeping quarters
