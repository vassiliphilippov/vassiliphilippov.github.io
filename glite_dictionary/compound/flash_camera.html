flash camera

1. NOUN

/ flæʃ ˈkæm(ə)rə / (AmE), / flæʃ ˈkam(ə)rə / (BrE)
a camera that has a built-in or attached flash for illuminating subjects in low light
camera with a built-in or attached flash

Examples

The photographer used a flash camera to capture clear images at the dimly lit event.
She prefers her flash camera for indoor portraits, as it brightens up the subjects.
Older models of flash cameras required users to attach separate flashbulbs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camera - a device for recording visual images
