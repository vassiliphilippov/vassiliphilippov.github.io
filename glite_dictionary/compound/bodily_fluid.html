bodily fluid

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdli ˈfluɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɒdɪli ˈfluːɪd / (BrE)
any of the liquids produced by the body, such as blood, urine, or saliva
liquid produced by the body

Examples

Doctors took samples of bodily fluid to test for infection.
The transmission of certain diseases can occur through contact with bodily fluid.
Gloves should be worn when cleaning up any bodily fluid spills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: body fluid - any fluid produced by the body
