power hunger

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊ.ɚ ˌhʌŋ.ɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə ˌhʌŋ.ɡə / (BrE)
an intense or excessive desire to acquire power or control
intense desire for power

Examples

The senator's power hunger drove him to manipulate his allies and rivals alike.
Corporate scandals often stem from unchecked power hunger at the top levels of management.
Her power hunger became apparent as she relentlessly pursued leadership roles in every organization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambition - a strong desire to achieve success or power
  • Synonym: greed - an intense and selfish desire for something, especially wealth or power
