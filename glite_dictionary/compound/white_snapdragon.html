white snapdragon

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈsnæpˌdræɡən / (AmE), / waɪt ˈsnæpˌdraɡən / (BrE)
A California plant with slender racemes of white flowers, belonging to the genus Antirrhinum.
California plant with white flowers

Examples

The white snapdragon is prized for its elegant, delicate blooms in garden borders.
Gardeners often plant white snapdragon to add a touch of brightness to their flowerbeds.
A single stem of white snapdragon was placed in the vase for a minimalist look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snapdragon - Plant of the genus Antirrhinum, with fragrant, showy flowers
