down with the flu

1. ADJ

/ daʊn wɪð ðə flu / (AmE), / daʊn wɪð ðə fluː / (BrE)
suffering from influenza; ill due to the flu.
suffering from influenza

Examples

He's down with the flu and has to stay home from work this week.
Several students were down with the flu during the winter term.
I'm sorry, but I'm down with the flu and won't make it to the meeting.

Synset Examples

She is down with the flu this week.
She is down with the flu and can’t come out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: down with - ill or incapacitated due to a specified condition
