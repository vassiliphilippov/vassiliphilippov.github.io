floating fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈfloʊtɪŋ fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈfləʊtɪŋ fɜːn / (BrE)
Any of several aquatic ferns that float on the surface of water, commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions.
aquatic fern that floats on water

Examples

A floating fern can quickly cover the surface of a pond, blocking sunlight from reaching submerged plants.
Gardeners often use floating ferns as natural filters in decorative water features.
The lake is home to several species, including the floating fern and water lettuce.
