gun-sight

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌn.saɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌn.saɪt / (BrE)
a device attached to a gun to help the user aim accurately at a target
device for aiming a gun

Examples

The marksman carefully adjusted the gun-sight before taking aim.
Modern rifles often come equipped with a laser gun-sight.
He replaced the damaged gun-sight to improve the weapon's accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sight - a device on a gun for aiming
