Black Tai

1. NOUN

/ blæk taɪ / (AmE), / blæk taɪ / (BrE)
a Tai language spoken in northern Vietnam, especially along the Red and Black rivers, and by related ethnic groups
Tai language spoken in northern Vietnam

Examples

The Black Tai language is an essential part of the cultural identity for many people in northern Vietnam.
Linguists have studied the grammatical structure of Black Tai to compare it with related Tai languages.
She traveled to Son La province to learn more about the traditions of the Black Tai community.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Tai languages - a family of languages spoken in Southeast Asia, including Thai and Lao
