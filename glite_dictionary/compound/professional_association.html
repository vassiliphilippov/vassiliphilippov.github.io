professional association

1. NOUN

/ prəˌfɛʃənl əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / prəˌfɛʃənəl əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
an organization of people who share the same profession, formed to promote their interests, set standards, and provide development opportunities for its members
organization for members of a profession

Examples

The American Medical Association is a well-known professional association for doctors.
Joining a professional association can help with networking and career development.
Many professional associations offer continuing education and certification programs for their members.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: trade union - an organization formed by workers to protect their rights and interests
