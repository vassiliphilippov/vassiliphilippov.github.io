hard sell

1. NOUN

/ hɑrd sɛl / (AmE), / hɑːd sɛl / (BrE)
a forceful and insistent attempt to persuade someone to buy something, often using aggressive sales tactics
forceful or aggressive sales approach

Examples

The car dealership used a hard sell approach that made some customers uncomfortable.
She prefers to avoid the hard sell and let her products speak for themselves.
After experiencing the hard sell at the gym, he decided not to sign up for a membership.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: soft sell - a gentle or subtle sales approach
