bond-trading activity

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑnd ˌtreɪdɪŋ ækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˈbɒnd ˌtreɪdɪŋ ækˈtɪvəti / (BrE)
the buying and selling of bonds, typically carried out by brokers on the floor of a financial exchange
the trading of bonds on a financial exchange

Examples

After the announcement, bond-trading activity increased sharply on Wall Street.
The central bank’s decision had a noticeable impact on bond-trading activity across multiple exchanges.
Regulators have introduced new rules to monitor bond-trading activity more closely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bond market - the marketplace for buying and selling bonds
  • Degree Lower: stock-trading activity - the buying and selling of stocks on an exchange
