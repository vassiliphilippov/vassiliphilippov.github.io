peppermint patty

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛpərˌmɪnt ˈpæti / (AmE), / ˈpɛpəmənt ˈpæti / (BrE)
A sweet confection consisting of a soft, peppermint-flavored filling coated in chocolate, typically in a round, flat shape.
chocolate-covered peppermint confection

Examples

She enjoyed a peppermint patty after dinner as a refreshing treat.
The bakery sells homemade peppermint patties during the holiday season.
He offered me a peppermint patty from the box of assorted chocolates.
