horned whiff

1. NOUN

/ hɔrnd wɪf / (AmE), / hɔːnd wɪf / (BrE)
a species of flatfish (Citharichthys cornutus) found in waters from the Bahamas and northern Gulf of Mexico to Brazil, noted for the small horn-like structures on its head.
a tropical flatfish species

Examples

While diving in the Caribbean, I spotted a horned whiff resting on the sandy bottom.
The horned whiff uses its flat body to camouflage itself against predators and prey alike.
Marine biologists studied the migration patterns of the horned whiff in the Gulf of Mexico.
