movie making

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvi ˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmuːvi ˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or activity of producing movies, including all stages from conception to final release
the production of movies

Examples

She has always dreamed of working in movie making since she was a child.
The technology behind movie making has evolved rapidly in the last few decades.
Many students enroll in courses to learn the fundamentals of movie making.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filmmaking - the process of making films or movies
