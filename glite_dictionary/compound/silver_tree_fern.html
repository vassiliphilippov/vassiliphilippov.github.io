silver tree fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ tri fɝn / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə triː fɜːn / (BrE)
a showy tree fern native to New Zealand and Australia with a crown of divided fronds and silvery-white undersides
tree fern with silvery-white fronds

Examples

The silver tree fern is a national symbol of New Zealand and appears on many emblems.
Gardeners prize the silver tree fern for its striking foliage and graceful arching fronds.
During our hike, we saw a tall silver tree fern with gleaming white undersides to its leaves.
