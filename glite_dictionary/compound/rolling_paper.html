rolling paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊlɪŋ ˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlɪŋ ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a thin, strong paper used for rolling cigarettes, designed to burn evenly and control the burning of tobacco
paper for rolling cigarettes

Examples

He bought a pack of rolling paper to make his own cigarettes.
The store carries several brands of rolling paper, each with different flavors.
She carefully rolled the tobacco with a rolling paper at the kitchen table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cigarette paper - paper used to make cigarettes
