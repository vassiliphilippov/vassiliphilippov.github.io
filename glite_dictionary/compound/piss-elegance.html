piss-elegance

1. NOUN

/ pɪsˈɛlɪɡəns / (AmE), / pɪsˈɛlɪɡəns / (BrE)
a pretentious or affected style that combines notions of elegance with obvious casualness or vulgarity
pretentious, affected elegance mixed with casualness
slang vulgar

Examples

The designer's new collection displayed a certain piss-elegance that divided the critics.
You could sense the piss-elegance of the party in the mismatched chairs and lavish centerpieces.
His music videos always have that touch of piss-elegance—expensive sets clashing with cheap props.

Synset Examples

They achieved piss-elegance in their minimalist design approach.
Her dress was the epitome of piss-elegance at the gala.
The room was decorated with a piss-elegance that impressed all the guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pretentiousness - the quality of trying to appear more important or refined than is actually the case
  • Degree Lower: elegance - graceful and stylish appearance or manner

Etymology

Formed from 'piss' (slang for something contemptible) and 'elegance', mockingly implying false refinement.
