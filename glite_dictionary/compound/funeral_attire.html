funeral attire

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjunərəl əˈtaɪr / (AmE), / ˈfjʊərəl əˈtaɪə / (BrE)
clothing considered appropriate to wear at a funeral, typically dark and formal
clothing worn at a funeral

Examples

She carefully chose her funeral attire to show respect for the deceased.
Dark suits and modest dresses are standard funeral attire in many cultures.
He forgot to pack proper funeral attire and had to borrow a tie.

Synset Examples

Her funerial attire matched the solemn occasion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mourning dress - clothing worn to signify mourning, especially after the death of someone
