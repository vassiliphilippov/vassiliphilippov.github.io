wild pink

1. NOUN

/ waɪld pɪŋk / (AmE), / waɪld pɪŋk / (BrE)
a bog orchid native to Canada, usually bearing a single fragrant magenta-pink blossom with a wide, gaping corolla
a Canadian orchid with magenta-pink blossoms

Examples

Every spring, the wild pink stands out in the bogs with its vibrant color.
Botanists traveled to Canada to document the rare wild pink orchid in bloom.
The fragrance of the solitary wild pink blossom filled the morning air.

/ waɪld pɪŋk / (AmE), / waɪld pɪŋk / (BrE)
