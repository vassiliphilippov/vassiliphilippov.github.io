twilight walk

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwaɪˌlaɪt wɔk / (AmE), / ˈtwaɪ.laɪt wɔːk / (BrE)
a walk or stroll taken outside during twilight, typically when the sun is setting and daylight is fading
a walk taken at dusk

Examples

After dinner, they took a twilight walk through the quiet park.
The twilight walk offered a peaceful end to a busy day.
She treasures their twilight walks as a time to connect in the cool, fading light.

Synset Examples

She loves twilight walks along the beach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evening stroll - a relaxed walk taken in the evening
