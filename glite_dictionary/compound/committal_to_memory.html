committal to memory

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɪt̬l̩ tə ˈmɛməri / (AmE), / kəˈmɪtl̩ tə ˈmɛməri / (BrE)
the act of learning something so as to remember it exactly; memorization
the act of memorizing

Examples

Her committal to memory of the lengthy poem impressed everyone.
The teacher emphasized the importance of committal to memory for learning multiplication tables.
Successful actors rely on swift committal to memory of their scripts.

Synset Examples

the actor's memorization of his lines

Related Terms

  • Synonym: memorization - the act of committing something to memory
