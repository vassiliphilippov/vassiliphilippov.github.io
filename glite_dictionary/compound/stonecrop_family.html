stonecrop family

1. NOUN

/ ˈstoʊnˌkrɑp ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈstəʊnˌkrɒp ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of chiefly succulent herbs and shrubs, including the stonecrops (genus Sedum) and related plants, known for their ability to thrive in dry conditions
family of succulent herbs and shrubs

Examples

Many gardeners choose plants from the stonecrop family because they require minimal watering and are easy to care for.
The stonecrop family includes a wide range of beautiful succulent plants suitable for rock gardens.
Botanists classify Sedum, a common garden plant, within the stonecrop family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Crassulaceae - the botanical family to which the stonecrop family belongs
