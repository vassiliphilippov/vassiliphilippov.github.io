cottage tent

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːt̬ɪdʒ tɛnt / (AmE), / ˈkɒtɪdʒ tɛnt / (BrE)
a large tent with walls and a roof resembling a small house, designed to accommodate a family
family-sized tent with walls and roof

Examples

Our family spent the weekend camping in a comfortable cottage tent by the lake.
The cottage tent provided enough room for four people and their gear.
Many campsites offer spacious cottage tents for rent during the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tent - a portable shelter made of cloth or similar material supported by poles and ropes
