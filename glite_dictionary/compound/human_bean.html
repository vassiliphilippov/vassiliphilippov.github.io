human bean

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmən ˈbin / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən ˈbiːn / (BrE)
a playful or humorous term for a person, especially a child, based on a pun on "human being"
humorous term for a person
informal

Examples

My grandmother always calls me her little human bean when she tells stories.
In the play, a giant describes the main character as a clever human bean.
Teachers often say that every human bean is unique in their own way.

Synset Examples

I love that little human bean; she's so full of energy.
Every human bean has their quirks and charms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: human being - a member of the species Homo sapiens; a person

Etymology

Playful alteration of "human being" by substituting "being" with the homophone "bean".
