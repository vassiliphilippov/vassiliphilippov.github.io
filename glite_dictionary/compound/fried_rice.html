fried rice

1. NOUN

/ fraɪd raɪs / (AmE), / fraɪd raɪs / (BrE)
a dish of cooked rice that is stir-fried with vegetables, eggs, and often meat or seafood
rice stir-fried with vegetables and other ingredients

Examples

I ordered fried rice with shrimp at the Chinese restaurant last night.
He learned to cook fried rice using leftover vegetables and eggs.
The buffet offered several varieties of fried rice including chicken and vegetable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stir-fry - a cooking method involving quick frying in a small amount of oil
