sneezeweed yarrow

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnizˌwid ˈjæroʊ / (AmE), / ˈsniːzˌwiːd ˈjærəʊ / (BrE)
A Eurasian herb with loose heads of button-shaped white flowers and long grey-green leaves, which can cause sneezing when powdered.
Eurasian herb with sneeze-inducing leaves

Examples

Some people experience an allergic reaction when handling sneezeweed yarrow in their gardens.
The powdered leaves of sneezeweed yarrow were traditionally used to induce sneezing.
We found patches of sneezeweed yarrow growing wild along the countryside path.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yarrow - a widely distributed herb with finely divided leaves and small white or pink flowers
