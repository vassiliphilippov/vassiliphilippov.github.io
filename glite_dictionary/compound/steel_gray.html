steel gray

1. NOUN

/ stil ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / stiːl ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
a slightly purplish or bluish dark grey color, similar to the appearance of steel
slightly bluish or purplish dark grey color

Examples

The car was painted a sleek steel gray that gleamed in the sunlight.
She chose a steel gray suit for the interview, giving her a sophisticated look.
Clouds gathered in a steel gray sky, threatening to bring rain.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gray - a color between black and white; grey
  • Degree Higher: charcoal - a dark gray color, almost black

Etymology

Named after the typical color of steel metal.
