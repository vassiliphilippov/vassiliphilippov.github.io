Dominican peso

1. NOUN

/ ˌdoʊmɪˈniːkən ˈpeɪsoʊ / (AmE), / ˌdɒmɪˈniːkən ˈpeɪsəʊ / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in the Dominican Republic, equal to 100 centavos
currency of the Dominican Republic

Examples

Tourists exchanged their dollars for Dominican pesos at the hotel lobby.
The price was listed as 500 Dominican pesos per night.
She found a few old Dominican pesos tucked inside her travel bag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peso - the basic monetary unit of several countries in the Americas and the Philippines
