short ribs

1. NOUN

/ ʃɔrt rɪbz / (AmE), / ʃɔːt rɪbz / (BrE)
a cut of beef containing sections of the rib near the breastbone, often used for braising or roasting
cut of beef from rib near sternum

Examples

The chef prepared the short ribs with a rich red wine sauce for dinner.
After slow-cooking the short ribs, the meat was falling off the bone.
She ordered braised short ribs at the restaurant, which were served with mashed potatoes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beef ribs - ribs cut of beef in general
