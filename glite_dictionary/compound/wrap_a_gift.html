to wrap a gift

1. VERB

/ ræp ə ɡɪft / (AmE), / ræp ə ɡɪft / (BrE)
to cover a present in decorative paper, often with ribbon or a bow, typically before giving it to someone
cover a present in decorative paper

Examples

She asked her brother to wrap a gift for their mother's birthday.
Some people prefer to wrap a gift with simple paper and natural twine.
I spent the evening wrapping a gift for my friend's wedding.

Synset Examples

Please wrap the gift before the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrap - to cover something completely with paper or soft material
