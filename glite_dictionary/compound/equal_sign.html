equal sign

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːkwəl saɪn / (AmE), / ˈiːkwəl saɪn / (BrE)
the symbol '=' used to show that the values on either side are the same
mathematical symbol '='

Examples

In mathematics, the equal sign is used to indicate that two expressions are the same value.
He wrote an equal sign between the numbers to show they are equivalent.
Please place an equal sign after the equation to balance both sides.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equals sign - alternative term for the symbol '=' indicating equality
