government overreach

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌvərnmənt ˌoʊvərˈritʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvənmənt ˌəʊvəˈriːtʃ / (BrE)
excessive or unwarranted intervention or involvement by a government in affairs beyond its appropriate scope
excessive government intervention

Examples

Critics argue that new regulations represent government overreach into private industry.
The bill aims to protect citizens from government overreach regarding personal data.
Some see pandemic restrictions as necessary while others call them government overreach.

Synset Examples

The libertoon comic satirizes government overreach humorously.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to overreach - to go beyond what is proper or permitted, especially in authority or power
