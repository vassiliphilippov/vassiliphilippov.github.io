Homeland Security

1. PROPN

/ ˈhoʊmˌlænd sɪˈkjʊrəti / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌlænd sɪˈkjʊərɪti / (BrE)
the United States federal department responsible for protecting the country against threats and handling matters relating to national security within its borders
U.S. government department for national security
american

Examples

The Department of Homeland Security was created to coordinate efforts against terrorism.
Many Americans rely on Homeland Security to maintain safety at airports.
Homeland Security issued a warning about potential cyber threats this week.
