free-reed instrument

1. NOUN

/ ˌfri ˈrid ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / ˌfriː ˈriːd ˈɪnstrʊmənt / (BrE)
a wind instrument that produces sound as air flows past a flexible reed that vibrates freely without touching a surface
wind instrument with a free reed mechanism

Examples

The free-reed instrument family includes the accordion and the harmonica.
She enjoys playing a free-reed instrument because of its unique sound.
Many Asian musical traditions feature some type of free-reed instrument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harmonica - a common free-reed musical instrument, also known as a mouth organ
  • Synonym: accordion - a portable, free-reed musical instrument with keys and bellows
