needle-bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈnidl̩ ˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈniːdl ˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
A shrub native to eastern Australia, having rigid, needle-shaped leaves and white flowers.
Australian shrub with needle-like leaves

Examples

The needle-bush thrives in sandy soils across eastern Australia.
During our hike, we saw several needle-bushes dotted along the path, their white flowers shining.
The gardener warned me to be careful of the sharp leaves on the needle-bush.
