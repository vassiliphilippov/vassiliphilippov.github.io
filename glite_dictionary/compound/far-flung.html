far-flung

1. ADJ

/ fɑrˈflʌŋ / (AmE), / fɑːˈflʌŋ / (BrE)
(of a place) remote or distant; located far away from a central point or main area.
remote or distant (of a place)

Examples

They traveled to the far-flung villages of the Andes on a humanitarian mission.
The company now has offices in the far-flung corners of Asia and Africa.
Letters from far-flung relatives arrived just once a year.

Synset Examples

far-flung corners of the Empire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remote - located far from the main centers of population; distant
