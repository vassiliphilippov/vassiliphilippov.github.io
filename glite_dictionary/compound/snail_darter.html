snail darter

1. NOUN

/ sneɪl ˈdɑrtər / (AmE), / sneɪl ˈdɑːtə / (BrE)
a small species of freshwater perch (Percina tanasi) native to the Tennessee River, known for eating snails
species of small freshwater fish of the Tennessee River

Examples

The snail darter became famous during debates over dam construction in Tennessee.
Scientists rediscovered the snail darter in new river habitats.
Efforts to protect the snail darter helped shape U.S. environmental policy.
