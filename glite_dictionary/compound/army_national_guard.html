Army National Guard

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑrmi ˈnæʃ(ə)nəl ɡɑrd / (AmE), / ˈɑːmi ˈnæʃənəl ɡɑːd / (BrE)
the civilian reserve force of the United States Army, made up of part-time soldiers who serve in domestic emergencies and overseas missions
US Army's civilian reserve component
american

Examples
