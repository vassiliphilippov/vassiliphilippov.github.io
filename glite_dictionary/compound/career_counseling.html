career counseling

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɪr ˈkaʊn.səl.ɪŋ / (AmE), / kəˈrɪə ˈkaʊn.səl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
guidance and advice given to individuals to help them choose, change, or develop their career paths
guidance on career choices and development

Examples

She sought career counseling to explore different job options after graduation.
Many universities offer career counseling services to help students plan for the future.
Thanks to career counseling, he discovered his passion for engineering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: career guidance - advice or information to help someone choose a career
  • Degree Lower: counseling - professional guidance in resolving personal conflicts or making decisions
