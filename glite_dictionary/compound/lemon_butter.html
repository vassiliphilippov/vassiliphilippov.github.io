lemon butter

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛmən ˌbʌtər / (AmE), / ˈlemən ˌbʌtə / (BrE)
A sauce made of clarified butter slowly browned and seasoned with lemon juice and parsley, used especially with fish or vegetables.
clarified butter sauce with lemon juice and parsley

Examples

The grilled fish was topped with a generous spoonful of lemon butter for extra flavor.
She prepared lemon butter to drizzle over the steamed asparagus.
Adding lemon butter is a classic way to enhance the taste of poached vegetables.
