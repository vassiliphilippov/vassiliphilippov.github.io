Kingdom of Tonga

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm əv ˈtɔŋə / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm əv ˈtɒŋə / (BrE)
an island nation in Polynesia in the South Pacific, which is a constitutional monarchy; gained independence from the United Kingdom in 1970
an island nation in Polynesia, South Pacific

Examples

The Kingdom of Tonga is renowned for its rich cultural heritage and royal traditions.
After gaining independence in 1970, the Kingdom of Tonga established its own constitution and government.
Several Pacific explorers visited the Kingdom of Tonga during the eighteenth century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tonga - the country, often used in short form

Etymology

Tonga: from the Tongan language, meaning 'south'.
