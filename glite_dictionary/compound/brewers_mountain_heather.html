Brewer's mountain heather

1. NOUN

/ ˈbruːərz ˈmaʊntən ˈhɛðər / (AmE), / ˈbruːəz ˈmaʊntɪn ˈhɛðə / (BrE)
A low-growing evergreen shrub native to the western United States, noted for its small leaves and heather-like appearance.
evergreen shrub native to western US

Examples

We spotted several clusters of Brewer's mountain heather during our hike along the Sierra Nevada trail.
The botanist identified the plant as Brewer's mountain heather because of its distinctive evergreen leaves.
In spring, the slopes were tinged with the purple blossoms of Brewer's mountain heather.
