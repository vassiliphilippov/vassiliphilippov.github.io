toy Manchester terrier

1. NOUN

/ tɔɪ ˈmæn.tʃɪ.stər ˈtɛr.i.ər / (AmE), / tɔɪ ˈman.tʃɪs.tə ˈtɛr.i.ə / (BrE)
a small breed of Manchester terrier, bred as a companion dog and known for its smooth black-and-tan coat and alert expression
small breed of Manchester terrier dog

Examples

The toy Manchester terrier is prized for its elegance and lively personality.
She adopted a playful toy Manchester terrier from the local animal shelter.
Unlike its larger relative, the toy Manchester terrier is better suited to apartment living.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Manchester terrier - a breed of smooth-haired terrier originating in Manchester, England

Etymology

From 'toy' (small, miniature), and 'Manchester terrier' (breed named for the city of Manchester, England).
