resisting arrest

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈzɪstɪŋ əˈrɛst / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪstɪŋ əˈrɛst / (BrE)
the act of physically opposing a lawful arrest, often considered a criminal offense
physically opposing lawful arrest
legal

Examples

He was charged with resisting arrest after struggling with the police officers.
The court found her guilty of resisting arrest during the incident outside the nightclub.
Legal advice was sought after his resisting arrest led to additional charges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assault - the crime of attacking someone physically
  • Synonym: battery - the crime of physically attacking someone
