Venus's slipper

1. NOUN

/ ˈviːnəs ɪz ˈslɪpər / (AmE), / ˈviːnəs ɪz ˈslɪpə / (BrE)
any of several orchids of the genus Paphiopedilum, noted for their waxy, pouch-shaped flowers
orchid with pouch-shaped flowers

Examples

The botanical garden featured a rare Venus's slipper in its orchid collection.
Horticulturists prize the Venus's slipper for its unusual beauty and vibrant colors.
Several species within the Venus's slipper group are endangered due to habitat loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lady's slipper - orchid with slipper-shaped flowers, especially Cypripedium genus

Etymology

Named for Venus (the Roman goddess of love) and the slipper-like shape of the flower's lip.
