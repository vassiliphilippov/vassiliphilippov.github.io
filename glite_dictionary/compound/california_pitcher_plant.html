California pitcher plant

1. NOUN

/ kælɪˈfɔrniə ˈpɪtʃɚ plænt / (AmE), / kælɪˈfɔːniə ˈpɪtʃə plɑːnt / (BrE)
a marsh or bog plant of northern California and Oregon having solitary pendulous yellow-green flowers and twisted, winged pitcher-shaped leaves
a carnivorous bog plant of California

Examples

The California pitcher plant thrives in nutrient-poor bogs by trapping insects in its unique leaves.
Botanists visited northern California to study the rare California pitcher plant in its natural habitat.
A California pitcher plant can be recognized by its twisted green pitchers and pendulous yellow flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitcher plant - a carnivorous plant with pitcher-shaped leaves that trap insects
