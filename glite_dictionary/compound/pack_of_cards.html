pack of cards

1. NOUN

/ pæk əv kɑrdz / (AmE), / pæk əv kɑːdz / (BrE)
a complete set of 52 playing cards used for games
set of 52 playing cards

Examples

He shuffled the pack of cards before dealing them out to the players.
We forgot to bring a pack of cards for the camping trip.
After dinner, they played poker with a new pack of cards.
