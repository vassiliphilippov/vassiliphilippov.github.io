peach liqueur

1. NOUN

/ pitʃ lɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / piːtʃ lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
a sweet, strong alcoholic drink flavored with peaches, typically served after meals or used in cocktails
sweet, peach-flavored alcoholic liqueur

Examples

She added a splash of peach liqueur to the cocktail for extra flavor.
The dessert featured a drizzle of homemade peach liqueur as a finishing touch.
We bought a bottle of peach liqueur to serve at the party.
