complain queen

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpleɪn kwiːn / (AmE), / kəmˈpleɪn kwiːn / (BrE)
a woman or girl who is known for frequently complaining, especially in a conspicuous or habitual way
a woman/girl who complains often
informal

Examples

After waiting five minutes, the complain queen demanded to see the manager.
He's tired of being labeled a complain queen just for speaking up.
Sarah knows she can be a complain queen about slow service.

Synset Examples

Everyone knows she's the complain queen at the cafe.
She’s the complain queen when it comes to customer service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: complainer - a person who complains
  • Synonym: whiner - a person who complains persistently and unnecessarily
