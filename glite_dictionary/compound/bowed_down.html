bowed down

1. ADJ

/ boʊd daʊn / (AmE), / bəʊd daʊn / (BrE)
Heavily burdened with work, responsibilities, or cares; weighed down by difficulties.
weighed down by burden or worry

Examples

She felt bowed down by the constant demands of her job and family.
After months of bad news, he was bowed down with worry.
Social workers are sometimes bowed down with the weight of their responsibilities.

Synset Examples

bowed down with troubles
found himself loaded down with responsibilities
overburdened social workers
weighed down with cares

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weighed down - feeling burdened or oppressed by worries or difficulties
  • Synonym: overburdened - having too many responsibilities or duties
  • Antonym: carefree - free from worries or responsibilities
