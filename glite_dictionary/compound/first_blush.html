first blush

1. NOUN

/ fɝst blʌʃ / (AmE), / fɜːst blʌʃ / (BrE)
the first view or initial impression of something, before closer examination
initial impression or appearance

Examples

At first blush, his explanation seemed convincing, but later we found several contradictions.
The proposal appears promising at first blush, though there may be hidden complications.
While the report is alarming at first blush, it needs careful scrutiny before drawing conclusions.

Synset Examples

at first blush the idea possesses considerable intuitive appeal but on closer examination it fails

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first sight - the first time something is seen or considered
  • Antonym: second thought - after further consideration
