down-to-earth

1. ADJ

/ ˌdaʊn.tuˈɝθ / (AmE), / ˌdaʊn.tuˈɜːθ / (BrE)
practical and sensible; not pretentious or unrealistic.
practical and sensible

Examples

Her down-to-earth attitude makes her easy to talk to in any situation.
Despite his success, he remains completely down-to-earth and approachable.
The book offers down-to-earth advice on how to manage personal finances.

Synset Examples

has a straightforward down-to-earth approach to a problem
her earthy common sense

Related Terms

  • Synonym: practical - concerned with actual use or practice
  • Synonym: realistic - having or showing a sensible and practical idea of what can be achieved or expected
  • Antonym: impractical - not sensible or realistic
