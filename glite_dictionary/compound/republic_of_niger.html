Republic of Niger

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv niˈʒɛr / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈniːʒə / (BrE)
A landlocked country in West Africa, largely covered by the Sahara Desert, which gained independence from France in 1960.
country in West Africa

Examples

The president of the Republic of Niger addressed the nation during the independence celebrations.
Drought is a significant concern for farmers living in the Republic of Niger.
International aid organizations have increased their efforts in the Republic of Niger to combat food shortages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Niger - country in West Africa also called Republic of Niger

Etymology

From French Niger, itself from the Niger River, likely from a local Tuareg phrase or the Latin 'niger' (black).
