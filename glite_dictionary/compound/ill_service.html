ill service

1. NOUN

/ ɪl ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / ɪl ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
an action meant to help that instead causes harm or disadvantage
harmful or unhelpful assistance

Examples

By giving her false confidence, his ill service caused her to fail the exam.
Sometimes, a friend's unsolicited advice can end up as an ill service.
The company's technical support did him an ill service by suggesting the wrong solution.

Synset Examples

he did them a disservice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disservice - an action that harms rather than helps
