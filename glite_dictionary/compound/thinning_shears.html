thinning shears

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɪnɪŋ ʃɪrz / (AmE), / ˈθɪnɪŋ ʃɪəz / (BrE)
A pair of special scissors with one serrated blade, used by hairdressers to thin out hair without changing its overall length.
scissors for thinning hair

Examples

The stylist used thinning shears to remove some bulk from my hair.
After a few snips with thinning shears, my haircut looked much lighter and more natural.
Barbers often reach for thinning shears to blend layers and soften thick hair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scissors - a hand-operated cutting instrument with two blades
