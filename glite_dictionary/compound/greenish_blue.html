greenish blue

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡriːnɪʃ bluː / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnɪʃ bluː / (BrE)
A shade of blue with a noticeable tinge of green.
blue color tinged with green

Examples

Her dress was a beautiful greenish blue that shimmered in the sunlight.
The painter mixed several colors to get the perfect greenish blue for the ocean scene.
They chose a greenish blue tile for the bathroom to create a tranquil atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turquoise - a blue-green color, similar to greenish blue
  • Synonym: aquamarine - a bluish-green color, a light tint of greenish blue
