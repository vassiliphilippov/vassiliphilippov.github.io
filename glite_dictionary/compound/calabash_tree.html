calabash tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæləˌbæʃ ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈkæləbæʃ ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tropical American evergreen tree that produces large round gourds, often used for containers or utensils.
tropical tree yielding large gourds

Examples

The calabash tree in their garden stood tall and was laden with green gourds.
Many traditional vessels in the village are made from the fruit of the calabash tree.
The calabash tree grows best in warm, tropical climates with plenty of sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calabash - the gourd or fruit produced by the calabash tree
