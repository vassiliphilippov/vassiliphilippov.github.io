fine art

1. NOUN

/ faɪn ɑrt / (AmE), / faɪn ɑːt / (BrE)
art created primarily for its aesthetic value and beauty, rather than for a practical purpose; works of art collectively.
artworks for aesthetic purpose

Examples

The museum's collection of fine art spans several centuries.
He studied fine art at a prestigious university in Paris.
Many people believe that fine art should be accessible to everyone.

Synset Examples

an art exhibition
a collection of fine art

Related Terms

  • Synonym: art - the expression or application of creative skill and imagination
