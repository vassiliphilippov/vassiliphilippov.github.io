hymenally challenged

1. ADJ

/ haɪˈmɛnəli ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (AmE), / haɪˈmɛnəli ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (BrE)
A humorous or euphemistic term used to describe someone who does not have an intact hymen, especially implying sexual experience or absence of virginity.
euphemism for lacking an intact hymen
informal

Examples

She described herself as hymenally challenged during a candid conversation with her friends.
The comedian made a joke about being hymenally challenged that sparked laughter from the audience.
In some circles, the term hymenally challenged is used to discuss sexual experience with humor.

Synset Examples

She described her hymenally challenged status during the conversation.
He joked about being hymenally challenged in a light-hearted way.
The term hymenally challenged often comes up in discussions.

Etymology

From 'hymen' (a membrane at the entrance of the vagina) + '-ally challenged' (patterned after phrases like 'physically challenged').
