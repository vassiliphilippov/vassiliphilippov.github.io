inky-black

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪŋki blæk / (AmE), / ˈɪŋki blak / (BrE)
Deep black in color, like black ink.
very dark black color

Examples

The cat's fur was inky-black and shone in the sunlight.
She stepped into the inky-black darkness of the cave, unable to see anything ahead.
The night sky was inky-black, dotted with countless stars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jet-black - completely black, like jet
  • Synonym: pitch-black - completely dark or black, like pitch
