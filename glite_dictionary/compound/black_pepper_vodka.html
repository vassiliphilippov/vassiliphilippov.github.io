black pepper vodka

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈpɛpɚ ˈvoʊdkə / (AmE), / blæk ˈpepə ˈvɒdkə / (BrE)
a strong alcoholic beverage, specifically vodka, infused with the flavor of black pepper
vodka flavored with black pepper

Examples

He ordered a shot of black pepper vodka to pair with his meal.
The spicy kick of black pepper vodka makes it popular in craft cocktails.
She infused her own black pepper vodka by adding cracked peppercorns to plain vodka.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vodka - a clear distilled alcoholic beverage from Eastern Europe
  • Synonym: pepper vodka - vodka flavored with any type of pepper
