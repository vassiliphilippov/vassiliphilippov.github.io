luvvy personality

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌvi pɚˈsɑnəˌlɪti / (AmE), / ˈlʌvi pəˌsɒnəˈlɪti / (BrE)
An affectionate and sociable demeanor, often characterized by warmth and friendliness.
affectionate and sociable demeanor
informal british

Her luvvy personality makes her a favorite among colleagues and friends.

Etymology
