tax rate

1. NOUN

/ tæks reɪt / (AmE), / tæks reɪt / (BrE)
the percentage at which an individual or corporation is taxed by the government
percentage used to calculate taxes

Examples

The government decided to lower the tax rate for small businesses this year.
Many people are confused about which tax rate applies to their income bracket.
A higher tax rate can discourage investment and economic growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tax - a compulsory contribution to government revenue
