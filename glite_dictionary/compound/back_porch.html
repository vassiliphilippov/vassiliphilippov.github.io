back porch

1. NOUN

/ bæk pɔrtʃ / (AmE), / bæk pɔːtʃ / (BrE)
A porch located at the back door of a house or building.
porch at the rear entrance

Examples

We often sit on the back porch in the evenings to watch the sunset.
She swept the leaves off the back porch before her guests arrived.
The dog was waiting for me by the back porch when I got home.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: front porch - a porch at the front entrance of a building
