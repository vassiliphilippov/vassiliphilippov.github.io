marine specimen

1. NOUN

/ məˈrin ˈspɛsəmən / (AmE), / məˈriːn ˈspesɪmən / (BrE)
an organism from the sea, especially one collected for study or display
organism from the sea

Examples

The museum acquired a rare marine specimen from a deep-sea expedition.
Scientists examined the marine specimen to learn more about ocean biodiversity.
A preserved marine specimen was displayed in the glass case for visitors to study.

Synset Examples

The exhibit showcased a dead marine specimen from the ocean depths.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: specimen - an individual example of a class or group, especially for scientific study
