suicide bombing

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːɪˌsaɪd ˈbɑːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsuːɪsʌɪd ˈbɒmɪŋ / (BrE)
A terrorist attack involving the deliberate detonation of explosives by an individual who intends to kill themselves along with others.
terrorist attack by self-sacrifice

Examples

The suicide bombing caused devastation in the crowded marketplace.
Authorities increased security to prevent another suicide bombing in the capital.
After the tragic suicide bombing, many communities called for peace and understanding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suicide attack - A broader term for attacks involving the deliberate self-sacrifice of the attacker, not limited to bombings

Etymology

From 'suicide' (Latin sui caedere, to kill oneself) and 'bombing'.
