Walker hound

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔkər haʊnd / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkə haʊnd / (BrE)
A breed of American foxhound known for its speed, stamina, and ability to track and chase foxes.
American breed of foxhound
american

Examples

The Walker hound is prized by hunters for its outstanding tracking skills.
She adopted a rescue Walker hound who quickly became part of the family.
Many Walker hounds excel in agility competitions due to their speed and energy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foxhound - a breed of dog bred for fox hunting

Etymology

Named after Thomas Walker, an 18th-century breeder who helped develop the breed.
