Oklahoma City

1. PROPN

/ ˌoʊkləˌhoʊmə ˈsɪti / (AmE), / ˌəʊkləˌhəʊmə ˈsɪti / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Oklahoma, United States, known for its oil and livestock industries
capital city of Oklahoma, US
american

Examples

She moved to Oklahoma City to pursue a new job opportunity.
The thunderstorm last night caused widespread power outages in Oklahoma City.
Oklahoma City is famous for its vibrant arts scene and cowboy culture.

Etymology

Named after the state of Oklahoma and the word 'city'. 'Oklahoma' derives from the Choctaw words 'okla' (people) and 'humma' (red).
