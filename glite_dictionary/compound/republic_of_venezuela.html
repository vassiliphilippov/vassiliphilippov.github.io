Republic of Venezuela

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌvɛnəˈzweɪlə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌvɛnɪˈzweɪlə / (BrE)
the official name for Venezuela, a country in northern South America on the Caribbean Sea, known for its oil resources and its independence from Spain in 1811.
official name for Venezuela, South American country

Examples

The oil industry is a vital part of the Republic of Venezuela's economy.
