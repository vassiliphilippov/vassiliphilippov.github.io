Northeastern United States

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɔrˈθistərn ˌjuː.naɪ.t̬ɪd ˈsteɪts / (AmE), / ˌnɔːˈθiːstən ˌjuːˈnaɪ.tɪd ˈsteɪts / (BrE)
the region comprising the northeastern part of the United States, typically including states such as New York, Massachusetts, and Pennsylvania
region in the northeast of the US

Examples

She grew up in the Northeastern United States, where winters are long and snowy.
Many prestigious universities are located in the Northeastern United States.
The Northeastern United States is known for its vibrant fall foliage.
