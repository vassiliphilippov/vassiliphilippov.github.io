soda maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊdə ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdə ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a device used for adding carbon dioxide to water or beverages to make them fizzy
device used for carbonating drinks

Examples

He uses his soda maker daily to prepare sparkling water at home.
The new soda maker comes with reusable bottles and different carbonation settings.
After buying a soda maker, they stopped purchasing bottled soft drinks.

Synset Examples

Detailed and simple plans to build small in-home carbonator for under $100 in parts are freely available on the internet and can produce a liter of carbonated water for less than 2 US cents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carbonator - a device that carbonates beverages
