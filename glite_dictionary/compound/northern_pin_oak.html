northern pin oak

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðərn pɪn oʊk / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən pɪn əʊk / (BrE)
a small to medium-sized deciduous oak tree native to east-central North America, with leaves which have sharply pointed lobes
oak tree of east-central North America

Examples

The northern pin oak is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often used in landscaping.
Autumn brings brilliant red foliage to the northern pin oak trees lining the parkway.
Many birds make their nests in the branches of the northern pin oak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oak - a tree of the genus Quercus
  • Synonym: pin oak - a type of oak tree with deeply lobed leaves
