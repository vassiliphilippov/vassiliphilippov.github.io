veal roast

1. NOUN

/ vil roʊst / (AmE), / viːl rəʊst / (BrE)
a cut of veal meat suitable for cooking by roasting
cut of veal for roasting

Examples

We served a delicious veal roast with rosemary and garlic for Sunday dinner.
The butcher recommended a veal roast for our holiday meal.
She prepared the veal roast by marinating it overnight before roasting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roast beef - a cut of beef suitable for roasting
