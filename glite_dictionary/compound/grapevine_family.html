grapevine family

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪpˌvaɪn ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪpvaɪn ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of climbing plants, mainly vines, including the grapevine and related species; Vitaceae
family of climbing plants, Vitaceae

Examples

The grapevine family includes not only grapes but also other ornamental vines.
Botanists have classified over 800 species within the grapevine family.
Many important fruit crops, such as table grapes, belong to the grapevine family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Vitaceae - the botanical name for the grapevine family
