motherfreaking amazing

1. ADJ

/ ˌmʌðərˌfriːkɪŋ əˈmeɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmʌðəˌfriːkɪŋ əˈmeɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely impressive or surprising; used for emphasis, often as an informal and euphemistic variant of 'motherfucking amazing'.
Very impressive or surprising (slang)
slang informal

Examples

That was a motherfreaking amazing concert last night!
She did a motherfreaking amazing job planning the whole event by herself.
The special effects in this movie are motherfreaking amazing!

Synset Examples

That was a motherfreaking amazing concert last night!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amazing - very impressive or surprising
  • Synonym: motherfucking amazing - extremely impressive or surprising (with vulgar intensifier)

Etymology

Euphemistic alteration of 'motherfucking', from 'mother' + 'freaking' (substitute for 'fucking') + 'amazing'.
