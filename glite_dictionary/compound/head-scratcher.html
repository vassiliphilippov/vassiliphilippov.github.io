head-scratcher

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdˌskrætʃər / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌskrætʃə / (BrE)
a puzzling or confusing problem or situation that is difficult to understand or solve
a puzzling or confusing problem
informal

Examples

The new tax laws are a real head-scratcher for many business owners.
Her disappearing keys became a constant head-scratcher in the office.
Solving this puzzle is definitely a head-scratcher, even for experts.

Synset Examples

This math problem is quite the head-scratcher.
Finding the missing piece was a true head-scratcher.
The puzzle was a real head-scratcher for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conundrum - a confusing and difficult problem or question
  • Synonym: mystery - something that is difficult or impossible to understand or explain

Etymology

Formed from 'head' + 'scratcher,' alluding to the gesture of scratching one's head when puzzled.
