glandular Labrador tea

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlæn.djələr ˌlæ.brəˌdɔr ˈti / (AmE), / ˈɡlæn.djʊ.lə ˌlæ.brəˌdɔː ˈtiː / (BrE)
a shrub of the Rocky Mountains, closely related to Ledum groenlandicum, with glandular leaves and a similar appearance
Rocky Mountain shrub related to Labrador tea

Examples

During our hike, we spotted a patch of glandular Labrador tea growing near the stream.
The leaves of glandular Labrador tea are sticky to the touch and give off a strong scent.
Botanists often compare glandular Labrador tea to Ledum groenlandicum due to their striking similarities.
