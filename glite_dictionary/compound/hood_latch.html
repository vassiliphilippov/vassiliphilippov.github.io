hood latch

1. NOUN

/ hʊd lætʃ / (AmE), / hʊd latʃ / (BrE)
a device that fastens and secures the hood of a car, keeping it shut until intentionally released
car part that keeps the hood shut

Examples

The hood latch was broken, so I couldn't open the car's engine compartment.
Always make sure the hood latch is secure before driving.
After a minor accident, the hood latch wouldn't close properly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catch - device for fastening or holding something closed
  • Synonym: lock - mechanical device used for securing things
