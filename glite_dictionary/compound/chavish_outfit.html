chavish outfit

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃævɪʃ ˈaʊtfɪt / (AmE), / ˈtʃævɪʃ ˈaʊtfɪt / (BrE)
An outfit that is characteristic of or resembles the style stereotypically associated with 'chavs' in Britain, often seen as flashy, casual, and associated with lower socio-economic status.
outfit with stereotypical 'chav' style
british informal

Examples

She wore a chavish outfit that drew unwanted attention.
His friends teased him for showing up in such a chavish outfit to the fancy restaurant.
The magazine featured an article on the emergence of the chavish outfit trend in street fashion.

Synset Examples

She wore a chavish outfit that drew unwanted attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chav - a young working-class person who behaves in a brash, loutish way

Etymology

From British slang 'chav' (a young person perceived as displaying brash and loutish behaviour), plus '-ish' and 'outfit'.
