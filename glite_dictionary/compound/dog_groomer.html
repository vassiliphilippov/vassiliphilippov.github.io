dog groomer

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ ˈɡruːmər / (AmE), / dɒɡ ˈɡruːmə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to clean, trim, and style dogs' coats
person who grooms dogs professionally

Examples

After taking her poodle to the dog groomer, it looked fluffy and neat.
The dog groomer recommended a special shampoo for sensitive skin.
He decided to become a dog groomer because he loves animals and enjoys styling their coats.

Synset Examples

He works as a dog groomer at the local pet shop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groomer - a person who cleans and styles animals, especially pets
