adult education

1. NOUN

/ əˈdʌlt ˌɛdʒuˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˈdʌlt ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
education provided for adults, especially those who have left the formal education system, often through classes or courses to develop skills or knowledge.
education for adults outside regular schooling

Examples

Local community centers often offer adult education classes in the evenings.
She enrolled in an adult education course to improve her computer skills.
The government increased funding for adult education to help workers retrain for new careers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: continuing education - education for adults continuing after leaving school
