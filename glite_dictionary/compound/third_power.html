third power

1. NOUN

/ θɝd ˈpaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / θɜːd ˈpaʊ.ə / (BrE)
A quantity obtained by multiplying a number by itself twice; the cube of a number.
the cube of a number (math)

Examples

Raising two to the third power gives you eight.
In mathematics, the third power of any number is also called its cube.
Calculating the third power of negative numbers results in negative values.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cube - the number produced when a quantity is multiplied by itself twice
