Molokai Island

1. PROPN

/ ˌmoʊ.loʊˈkaɪ ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / ˌməʊ.ləˈkaɪ ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
An island in central Hawaii, situated between Maui and Oahu.
island in central Hawaii

Examples

Many visitors are drawn to Molokai Island for its natural beauty and traditional Hawaiian lifestyle.
The Kalaupapa National Historical Park is located on the northern shore of Molokai Island.
She spent her summer vacation exploring the quiet villages of Molokai Island.

Etymology

From Hawaiian Molokaʻi, the name of the island.
