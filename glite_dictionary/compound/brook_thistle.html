brook thistle

1. NOUN

/ brʊk ˈθɪsəl / (AmE), / brʊk ˈθɪsəl / (BrE)
a thistle native to central and southwestern Europe, typically found near streams and damp places
a European thistle found near streams

Examples

The brook thistle thrives in wet meadows and along stream banks in Europe.
Botanists recorded an unusually large cluster of brook thistle in the valley this year.
Gardeners appreciate the brook thistle for its spiky leaves and purple flowers.
