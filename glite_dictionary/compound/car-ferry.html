car-ferry

1. NOUN

/ kɑr ˈfɛri / (AmE), / kɑː ˈfɛri / (BrE)
A ferry designed to transport motor vehicles across a body of water.
ferry for carrying cars

Examples

We drove onto the car-ferry and crossed the lake in less than an hour.
The new car-ferry can accommodate up to 100 vehicles.
During summer, the car-ferry operates every thirty minutes between the islands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ferry - a boat or ship for conveying passengers and goods, especially over a relatively short distance and as a regular service
