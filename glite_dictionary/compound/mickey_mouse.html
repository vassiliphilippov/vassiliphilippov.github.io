Mickey Mouse

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɪki ˌmaʊs / (AmE), / ˈmɪki ˌmaʊs / (BrE)
a famous fictional mouse character created by Walt Disney, known as the mascot of The Walt Disney Company
Walt Disney's iconic cartoon mouse character

Examples

Children around the world recognize Mickey Mouse as a symbol of fun and adventure.
The original Mickey Mouse cartoons were produced in black and white.
My little cousin wore a Mickey Mouse shirt to the party.

Etymology

Created by Walt Disney and Ub Iwerks in 1928; 'Mickey' is a diminutive of Michael, and 'Mouse' describes the animal character.
