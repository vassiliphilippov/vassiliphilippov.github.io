flamingo flower

1. NOUN

/ fləˈmɪŋɡoʊ ˈflaʊɚ / (AmE), / fləˈmɪŋɡəʊ ˈflaʊə / (BrE)
A tropical plant with a bright scarlet spathe and spadix, often grown as an ornamental; an anthurium.
ornamental plant with scarlet spathe (anthurium)

Examples

My grandmother keeps a beautiful flamingo flower by the window in her living room.
The vivid red flamingo flower brightened up the entire garden display.
We chose a flamingo flower arrangement as a gift for our friend's birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anthurium - a genus of tropical American plants with brightly colored spathes
