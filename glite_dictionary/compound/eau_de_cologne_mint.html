eau de cologne mint

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊ də kəˈloʊn mɪnt / (AmE), / ˌəʊ də kəˈləʊn mɪnt / (BrE)
a variety of mint with leaves that have a scent reminiscent of bergamot orange, used in culinary and aromatic applications
type of aromatic mint plant

Examples

She planted eau de cologne mint in her herb garden for its refreshing citrus aroma.
The scent of eau de cologne mint filled the kitchen as he chopped the leaves for tea.
Many gardeners appreciate eau de cologne mint for both its ornamental and culinary uses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bergamot mint - mint variety with a fragrance similar to bergamot orange

Etymology

From French 'eau de Cologne' (cologne water), referencing the plant's fragrance.
