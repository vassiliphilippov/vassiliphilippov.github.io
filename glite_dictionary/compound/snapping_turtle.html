snapping turtle

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnæpɪŋ ˌtɝːt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈsnæpɪŋ ˌtɜːtəl / (BrE)
A large, aggressive freshwater turtle with a powerful jaw, native to North America.
large aggressive freshwater turtle

Examples

The snapping turtle lurked motionless beneath the surface of the murky pond.
A mature snapping turtle can deliver a surprisingly strong bite if threatened.
Researchers tagged several snapping turtles to monitor their movements in the wild.
