telephone wire

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌfoʊn waɪr / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn waɪə / (BrE)
A wire that transmits telephone signals between devices or locations.
wire for transmitting telephone signals

Examples

The workers installed a new telephone wire along the street.
A fallen tree damaged the telephone wire, disrupting service for several houses.
He traced the ringing sound to a loose telephone wire behind the desk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telephone line - a wire or cable that carries telephone signals
