roast beef

1. NOUN

/ roʊst bif / (AmE), / rəʊst biːf / (BrE)
Beef that has been cooked by roasting, typically served sliced as a main dish.
beef cooked by roasting

Examples

We served roast beef with Yorkshire pudding and vegetables for Sunday dinner.
The restaurant is known for its tender roast beef.
She made sandwiches with leftover roast beef for lunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beef - meat from a cow, bull, or ox
