river boat

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪvɚ ˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈrɪvə ˌbəʊt / (BrE)
a boat designed or used for travel on rivers
boat for use on a river

Examples

We took a river boat tour to see the city from the water.
The river boat docked near the old mill, ready to take passengers upstream.
Paddlewheel river boats were once a common sight on the Mississippi River.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steamboat - a boat that is powered by a steam engine, especially one used on rivers
