to have company

1. VERB

/ hæv ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / hæv ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)
to have guests or visitors at one's home or in one's presence
to have guests or visitors

Examples

Please tidy up the living room — we have company coming over later.
Whenever they have company, they serve tea and cake to their guests.
We can't watch that movie now because we have company staying with us.

Synset Examples

Shh, we have company and need to be quiet.
Don't be too loud; we have company tonight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to entertain - to have people as guests and provide for their enjoyment
  • Synonym: host - to receive or entertain guests
