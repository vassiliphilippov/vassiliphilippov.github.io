holding device

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊldɪŋ dɪˌvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊldɪŋ dɪˌvaɪs / (BrE)
A tool or apparatus designed to hold or secure an object in position.
tool used to hold objects

Examples

The laboratory uses a specialized holding device to keep test tubes secure during experiments.
Before welding, he placed the metal sheet in a holding device to keep it steady.
Engineers often design a holding device to ensure fragile parts remain undamaged during transport.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clamp - a device used to fasten or hold objects tightly together
  • Synonym: vise - a mechanical apparatus used to secure an object while work is performed on it
