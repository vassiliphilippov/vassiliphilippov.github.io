Republican Guard

1. PROPN

/ rəˌpʌblɪkən ˈɡɑrd / (AmE), / rɪˌpʌblɪkən ˈɡɑːd / (BrE)
the elite military unit of Iraq, formerly charged with protecting the government in Baghdad and playing a prominent role under Saddam Hussein's regime
Iraq's former elite military unit

Examples

The Republican Guard was known for its loyalty to Saddam Hussein and its fierce fighting during the Gulf War.
After the fall of Baghdad, many members of the Republican Guard went into hiding or fled the country.
International observers watched closely as the Republican Guard defended key government buildings in the capital.
