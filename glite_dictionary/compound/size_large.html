size large

1. NOUN

/ saɪz lɑrdʒ / (AmE), / saɪz lɑːdʒ / (BrE)
a clothing size denoting a fit between medium and extra-large in standard measurement systems
clothing size between medium and extra-large

Examples

This jacket is available in size large only.
I usually wear a size large in T-shirts for a comfortable fit.
She accidentally ordered a size large instead of medium.

Synset Examples

The shirt comes in a size large only.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: large - greater in size than average
  • Degree Lower: size medium - clothing size between small and large
  • Degree Higher: size extra-large - clothing size larger than large
