microwave oven

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪk.roʊˌweɪv ˈʌv.ən / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ.krəʊˌweɪv ˈʌv.ən / (BrE)
an electric kitchen appliance that cooks or heats food by using microwave radiation to agitate water molecules in the food
an appliance for cooking food with microwaves

Examples

We reheated the leftovers in the microwave oven before dinner.
She bought a new microwave oven because her old one stopped working.
The microwave oven is perfect for quickly making popcorn or warming up beverages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microwave - an electromagnetic wave with a wavelength between infrared and radio waves, used for cooking and communication
  • Antonym: conventional oven - a kitchen appliance that cooks food by surrounding it with hot air

Etymology

From 'microwave' (referring to electromagnetic waves) and 'oven'; 'microwave' from micro- (small) + wave.
