yellow bachelor's button

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈbætʃələrz ˈbʌtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈbætʃələz ˈbʌtn / (BrE)
A bog plant of the pine barrens in the southeastern United States, noted for its spikes of irregular yellow-orange flowers.
bog plant with yellow-orange flowers (US southeast)

Examples

The wetlands were dotted with bright yellow bachelor's button in early spring.
Conservationists are working to protect the rare yellow bachelor's button from habitat loss.
In the marsh, the yellow bachelor's button stands out with its vibrant orange-yellow blooms.
