science experiment

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪəns ɪkˌspɛrəmənt / (AmE), / ˈsaɪəns ɪkˌspɛrɪmənt / (BrE)
an investigation or test carried out to discover or demonstrate scientific principles or facts
investigation to explore scientific principles

Examples

The students designed a science experiment to test which material keeps ice frozen the longest.
Every science experiment must include a clear hypothesis and a controlled procedure.
She explained her science experiment at the school science fair, impressing the judges with her results.

Synset Examples

We conducted a science experiment for our school project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: experiment - a scientific procedure undertaken to make a discovery or test a hypothesis
