South American sea lion

1. NOUN

/ saʊθ əˈmɛrɪkən si ˈlaɪ.ən / (AmE), / saʊθ əˈmɛrɪkən si ˈlaɪ.ən / (BrE)
a large species of sea lion native to the southern coasts of South America
sea lion native to South America's southern coasts

Examples

The South American sea lion is often seen basking on the rocky shores of Patagonia.
Researchers are studying the behavior of the South American sea lion in Chile's coastal waters.
Tourists watched a group of South American sea lions playing near the docks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea lion - large marine mammal of the family Otariidae
