hand brake

1. NOUN

/ ˈhænd ˌbreɪk / (AmE), / ˈhænd ˌbreɪk / (BrE)
A brake operated by hand, typically using a lever, commonly found in vehicles to prevent movement when parked.
brake operated by hand in vehicles

Examples

Please remember to apply the hand brake before you leave the car.
The mechanic checked the hand brake to ensure it was functioning properly.
She pulled the hand brake sharply when she stopped on the hill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parking brake - brake used to keep a parked vehicle from moving
