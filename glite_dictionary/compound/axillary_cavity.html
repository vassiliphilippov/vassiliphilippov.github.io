axillary cavity

1. NOUN

/ ˈæksɪˌlɛri ˈkævəti / (AmE), / ˈæksɪləˌri ˈkævəti / (BrE)
the hollow area under the arm where it joins the shoulder, also known as the armpit, especially in medical contexts
the hollow under the arm at the shoulder
medical

Examples

The doctor carefully examined the patient's axillary cavity for signs of swelling.
During the procedure, the surgeon made an incision near the axillary cavity.
Excessive sweating in the axillary cavity can sometimes cause skin irritation.

Synset Examples

they were up to their armpits in water

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armpit - the hollow under the arm where it joins the shoulder

Etymology

From Latin axilla (armpit) + English cavity.
