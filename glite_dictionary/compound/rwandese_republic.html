Rwandese Republic

1. PROPN

/ ruːˈɑːndiz rɪˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / ruːˈændiːz rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
the former official name of Rwanda, a landlocked country in central Africa, formerly a German colony
former name of Rwanda, African country

Examples

During the early twentieth century, the territory was known as the Rwandese Republic under colonial administration.
The Rwandese Republic experienced major changes after gaining independence in 1962.
Historical documents sometimes refer to the country as the Rwandese Republic before adopting the name Rwanda.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rwanda - current name of the country in central Africa
