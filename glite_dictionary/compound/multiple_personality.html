multiple personality

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌltəpəl pɚˌsɑnˈæləti / (AmE), / ˈmʌltɪpl pɜːsəˈnælɪti / (BrE)
a rare mental disorder in which a person's identity is split into two or more distinct and independent personalities
mental disorder with multiple identities
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with multiple personality after displaying distinct identities.
Fiction often dramatizes multiple personality with characters who live secret double lives.
Modern psychiatry refers to multiple personality as dissociative identity disorder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dissociative identity disorder - the modern term for multiple personality disorder
