sweet wattle

1. NOUN

/ swit ˈwɑt̬l̩ / (AmE), / swiːt ˈwɒt.əl / (BrE)
a tropical American thorny shrub or small tree with fragrant yellow flowers, used in perfumery
tropical shrub with fragrant yellow flowers

Examples

The garden's collection of sweet wattle filled the air with a subtle, pleasant fragrance.
Perfumers highly value sweet wattle because of its unique scent.
In South America, sweet wattle trees are sometimes planted for their ornamental flowers.
