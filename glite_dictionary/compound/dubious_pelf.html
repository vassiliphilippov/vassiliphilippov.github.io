dubious pelf

1. NOUN

/ ˈduːbiəs pɛlf / (AmE), / ˈdjuːbɪəs pɛlf / (BrE)
wealth or riches of questionable or suspicious origin, often implying dishonesty or lack of legitimacy
ill-gotten or questionable wealth

The court investigated the source of his dubious pelf before making a ruling.
Writers often criticize politicians for accumulating dubious pelf through corrupt practices.
Rumors of dubious pelf surrounded the sudden rise in his fortune.
