push-bike

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊʃ ˌbaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃ ˌbaɪk / (BrE)
a bicycle that is powered by pedaling, not by a motor
pedal-powered bicycle
british

Examples

He rode his push-bike to school every morning, rain or shine.
My old push-bike is gathering dust in the garage since I bought a scooter.
The city is encouraging people to use a push-bike instead of driving cars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bicycle - a vehicle with two wheels powered by pedals
  • Antonym: motorbike - a two-wheeled vehicle powered by a motor
