emery wheel

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛməri wiːl / (AmE), / ˈɛməri wiːl / (BrE)
A circular wheel made of emery or other abrasive materials, used for grinding, polishing, or sharpening.
abrasive wheel for grinding or polishing

Examples

The machinist used an emery wheel to sharpen the tool's edge.
Always wear safety goggles when working with an emery wheel.
The emery wheel on the bench grinder needed to be replaced after extensive use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grinding wheel - wheel used for grinding, similar to emery wheel but can be made from various abrasives

Etymology

From 'emery,' a type of granular corundum used as an abrasive, and 'wheel.'
