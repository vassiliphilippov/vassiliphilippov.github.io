Syrian hamster

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪriən ˈhæmstər / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriən ˈhæmstə / (BrE)
a small, golden or light-colored species of hamster native to Syria, often kept as a pet
species of pet hamster

Examples

We decided to adopt a Syrian hamster as our new family pet.
A Syrian hamster usually prefers to live alone and can be very territorial.
Her first experience with pet ownership was caring for a Syrian hamster in elementary school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hamster - a small rodent often kept as a pet

Etymology

Named after Syria, the country of origin.
