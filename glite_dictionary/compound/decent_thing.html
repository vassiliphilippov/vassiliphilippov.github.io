decent thing

1. NOUN

/ ˈdiːsənt ˌθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdiːsənt ˌθɪŋ / (BrE)
the morally correct or proper action in a particular situation, especially one that conforms to accepted standards of behavior
the morally right or proper action

Examples

After realizing his mistake, he finally did the decent thing and admitted the truth.
Most people would agree that helping your neighbor in need is the decent thing to do.
In that situation, refusing the reward was considered the decent thing.

Synset Examples

You should do the decent thing and apologize to her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proper thing - the correct or socially acceptable action
