speech day

1. NOUN

/ spiːtʃ deɪ / (AmE), / spiːtʃ deɪ / (BrE)
an annual event in schools when speeches are delivered and prizes are awarded to students
annual school event for speeches and prizes
british

Examples

The school held its speech day last Friday, celebrating the achievements of outstanding students.
Parents and alumni attended the annual speech day ceremony to support their children.
On speech day, the headteacher gave an inspiring talk before presenting awards.
