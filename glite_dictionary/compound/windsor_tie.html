Windsor tie

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪnzər taɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɪnzə taɪ / (BrE)
a wide necktie typically tied in a loose bow or with a full Windsor knot
a wide necktie tied in a bow or full Windsor knot

Examples

He wore a crisp white shirt and a Windsor tie to the interview.
The Windsor tie has become a popular choice for formal events and ceremonies.
She gifted him a silk Windsor tie for his birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bow tie - a necktie tied in the form of a bow
  • Synonym: Windsor knot - a method of tying a necktie knot

Etymology

Named after the Duke of Windsor, who popularized the style.
