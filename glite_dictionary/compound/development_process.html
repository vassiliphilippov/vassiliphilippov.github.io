development process

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈvɛləpmənt ˈprɑːsɛs / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləpmənt ˈprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
the series of steps or stages involved in creating a product or implementing a project
series of steps to create a product

Examples

The team outlined each step in the development process before starting the project.
Effective communication is crucial throughout the development process to avoid misunderstandings.
She specializes in improving the development process for innovative software products.

Synset Examples

Her role involved overseeing the developmentation process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: production process - the series of actions to manufacture goods
