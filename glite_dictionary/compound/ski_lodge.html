ski lodge

1. NOUN

/ ski lɑdʒ / (AmE), / ski lɒdʒ / (BrE)
a hotel or building at a ski resort offering lodging and facilities for skiers
hotel at a ski resort

Examples

The family spent the weekend at a cozy ski lodge near the mountain slopes.
We enjoyed hot chocolate by the fire in the main ski lodge after a long day of skiing.
Reservations at the most popular ski lodge fill up quickly during peak season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hotel - an establishment providing accommodation, meals, and other services
