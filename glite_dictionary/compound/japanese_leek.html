Japanese leek

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈpæniz lik / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæniːz liːk / (BrE)
An Asiatic onion with slender bulbs, commonly used as an early green onion in cooking.
Asiatic onion used as green onion

Examples

You can substitute Japanese leek for scallions in this stir-fry recipe.
The flavor of Japanese leek is milder than that of regular onions.
She added chopped Japanese leek to her noodle soup for extra freshness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: green onion - A young onion with a slender white stalk and long green leaves, used in cooking
  • Synonym: leek - A vegetable with a long white shaft and flat green leaves, related to the onion
