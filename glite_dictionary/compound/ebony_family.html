ebony family

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛbəni ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈɛbəni ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of fruit and timber trees of tropical and warm regions, including ebony and persimmon
group of trees including ebony and persimmon

Examples

The ebony family contains species known for their hard, dark wood and edible fruit.
Persimmons and ebonies both belong to the ebony family.
Botanists classify Diospyros under the ebony family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Diospyros - a genus of trees within the ebony family, includes persimmon and ebony
