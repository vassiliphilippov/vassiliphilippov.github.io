drug lord

1. NOUN

/ drʌɡ lɔrd / (AmE), / drʌɡ lɔːd / (BrE)
a person who controls and manages an organization involved in the illegal drug trade
leader of a drug trafficking organization

Examples

The capture of the notorious drug lord made headlines around the world.
Police believe that a powerful drug lord is behind the recent surge in violence.
The series follows the rise and fall of a Colombian drug lord.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kingpin - a person who is essential to the success of an organization or activity, especially one involved in crime
