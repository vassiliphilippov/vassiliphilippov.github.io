conic waxycap

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑː.nɪk ˈwæksiˌkæp / (AmE), / ˈkɒ.nɪk ˈwæksiˌkæp / (BrE)
a type of fungus with a sharply conical cap and dry stalks, belonging to the genus Hygrocybe
a fungus with a conical cap and dry stalks

Examples

We found a beautiful conic waxycap growing near the edge of the forest.
The conic waxycap is notable for its vividly colored, sharply pointed cap.
Several conic waxycaps appeared on the damp grass after the autumn rain.
