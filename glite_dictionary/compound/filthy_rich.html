filthy rich

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪlθi rɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɪlθi rɪtʃ / (BrE)
extremely wealthy; having an abundance of money or assets, often to a conspicuous or excessive degree
extremely wealthy
informal

Examples

The company founders became filthy rich after selling their startup for millions.
He drives expensive cars and lives in a mansion because he's filthy rich.
Many people dream of being filthy rich, but wealth doesn't guarantee happiness.

Synset Examples

They live in a mansion because they're filthy rich.
After the lottery win, he became filthy rich overnight.
She always flaunts her lifestyle as if being filthy rich is a virtue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wealthy - having a great deal of money, resources, or assets
  • Antonym: poor - lacking sufficient money to live at a standard considered comfortable or normal
