coming attraction

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌmɪŋ əˈtrækʃən / (AmE), / ˈkʌmɪŋ əˈtrækʃən / (BrE)
a film or movie that is advertised and will be shown soon, typically used in the context of cinema previews
film advertised to be shown soon

Examples

The theater showed a coming attraction before the main feature started.
Did you see the new coming attraction for the superhero movie?
They always play several coming attractions to get the audience excited for future releases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trailer - a short promotional film for a feature movie
