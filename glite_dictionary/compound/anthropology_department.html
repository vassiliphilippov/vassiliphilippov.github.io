anthropology department

1. NOUN

/ ˌænθrəˈpɑlədʒi dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ˌænθrəˈpɒlədʒi dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
an academic department in a university or college responsible for teaching and research in anthropology
university unit for anthropology studies

Examples

She applied to study in the anthropology department at the university.
The anthropology department is hosting a seminar on cultural practices.
Many faculty members in the anthropology department conduct fieldwork abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: department - a division of a large organization such as a university
