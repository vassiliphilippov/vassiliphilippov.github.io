tom turkey

1. NOUN

/ tɑm ˈtɝːki / (AmE), / tɒm ˈtɜːki / (BrE)
a male turkey
male turkey

Examples

The farmer raised several tom turkeys for Thanksgiving each year.
During mating season, the tom turkey fans his tail feathers to attract females.
That large, colorful bird strutting across the yard is a mature tom turkey.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hen turkey - female turkey

Etymology

From 'Tom' (a common male name) used as a generic name for the male of some animals, plus 'turkey'.
