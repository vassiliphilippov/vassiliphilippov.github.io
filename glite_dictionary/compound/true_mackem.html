true Mackem

1. NOUN

/ tru ˈmækəm / (AmE), / truː ˈmækəm / (BrE)
A person from Sunderland, UK, especially one who supports the local football team.
person from Sunderland (UK), football supporter
informal british

Examples

He considers himself a true Mackem, never missing a home match at the Stadium of Light.
Only a true Mackem would queue for hours to buy the latest Sunderland kit.
As a true Mackem, she knows all the chants by heart and travels to every away game.

Synset Examples

As a true Mackem, he proudly supports his local football team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mackem - a person from Sunderland, UK

Etymology

'Mackem' is a colloquial term for a person from Sunderland, northeast England. Its origin is uncertain, but may relate to local dialect for 'make' and 'them.' 'True Mackem' emphasizes authenticity.
