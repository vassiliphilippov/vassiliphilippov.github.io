California wine

1. NOUN

/ kælɪˈfɔrnjə waɪn / (AmE), / kælɪˈfɔːnɪə waɪn / (BrE)
any wine produced in the state of California, known for its diverse varieties and major wine regions.
wine produced in California

Examples

Many tourists visit Napa Valley to taste California wine directly at local vineyards.
Her favorite dinner pairing is a red California wine from Sonoma Country.
Exports of California wine have increased steadily over the past decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wine - an alcoholic drink made from fermented grapes or other fruits
