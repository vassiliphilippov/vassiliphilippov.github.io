student lamp

1. NOUN

/ ˈstuːdənt læmp / (AmE), / ˈstjuːdənt læmp / (BrE)
a desk lamp with an adjustable or flexible neck, designed to provide focused light for reading or studying
adjustable reading desk lamp

Examples

She bought a student lamp to help her study late into the night.
The flexible neck of the student lamp allows you to direct the light where needed.
A modern student lamp can reduce eye strain during hours of reading.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desk lamp - a lamp designed for use on a desk
