belly whopper

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛli ˌwɑpər / (AmE), / ˈbɛli ˌwɒpə / (BrE)
A dive in which a person's abdomen strikes the surface of the water first, typically causing discomfort or pain.
dive landing flat on the abdomen

Examples

He attempted a fancy dive but ended up with a painful belly whopper instead.
The kids laughed every time someone did a belly whopper off the diving board.
After the belly whopper, his stomach was red for minutes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: belly flop - a dive in which the front of the body hits the water flat, causing a slapping sound and discomfort
