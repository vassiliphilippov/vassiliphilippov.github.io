peanut brittle

1. NOUN

/ ˈpinʌt ˈbrɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈpiːnʌt ˈbrɪtəl / (BrE)
A hard, flat confection made by boiling sugar and water and incorporating peanuts, which is then broken into pieces when cooled.
hard candy with peanuts

Examples

She made a batch of peanut brittle for the holiday party.
The store sells homemade peanut brittle in decorative tins.
A chunk of peanut brittle stuck to his tooth after he took a bite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brittle - A hard, crunchy candy that easily breaks into pieces

Etymology

From 'peanut' and 'brittle', describing a type of candy that is brittle, i.e., easily broken.
