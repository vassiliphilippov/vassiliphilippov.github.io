hoodweeb culture

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʊdˌwiːb ˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈhʊdˌwiːb ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
A subculture that merges elements of urban street culture and anime fandom.
blend of urban and anime fandom culture
slang

Examples

Many young people are drawn to hoodweeb culture because it allows them to express both their love of hip-hop and anime.
The fashion choices in hoodweeb culture often include streetwear mixed with anime-inspired accessories.
Social media has played a major role in spreading hoodweeb culture around the world.

Synset Examples

The hoodweeb culture combines street style and anime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: otaku - a fan of anime and manga, especially one who is very passionate

Etymology

Blend of 'hood' (slang for urban neighborhoods) and 'weeb' (slang for anime fan).
