toll bridge

1. NOUN

/ toʊl brɪdʒ / (AmE), / təʊl brɪdʒ / (BrE)
A bridge over which a fee is charged for passage.
bridge where a toll is charged

Examples

We had to pay a few dollars to cross the toll bridge into the city.
The new toll bridge reduced travel time but increased daily expenses for commuters.
The government announced plans to replace the old toll bridge with a free one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toll road - road for which a fee is charged for use
  • Antonym: free bridge - bridge for which no toll is charged
