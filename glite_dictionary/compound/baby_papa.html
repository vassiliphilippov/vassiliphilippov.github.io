baby papa

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.bi ˈpɑ.pə / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi ˈpɑː.pə / (BrE)
the father of at least one of a woman's children, typically used when the parents are not married to each other
informal term for a child's father
informal american

Examples

My baby papa always comes to our daughter's birthday parties, even though we're not together anymore.
She argued with her baby papa about who would take their son to school this week.
Lately, I've been getting along better with my baby papa for the sake of our kids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baby daddy - the father of one or more of a woman's children, often used informally
