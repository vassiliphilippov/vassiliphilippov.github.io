estate agent

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈsteɪt ˌeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ɪˈsteɪt ˌeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to arrange the sale, renting, or management of homes, land, and buildings for clients
person who arranges property sales or rentals
british

Examples

The estate agent showed us several houses before we made our decision.
If you are looking to rent a flat, a good estate agent can help you find the right place.
She contacted an estate agent to get an estimate for selling her property.

Synset Examples

in England they call a real estate agent a ‘land agent’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: real estate agent - person who arranges the sale or rental of property (US usage)
