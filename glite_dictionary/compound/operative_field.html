operative field

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːpərətɪv fiːld / (AmE), / ˈɒpərətɪv fiːld / (BrE)
the area of the body that is exposed for a surgical procedure
area exposed during surgery
medical

Examples

The surgeon carefully disinfected the operative field before making the first incision.
Maintaining a sterile operative field is essential to prevent postoperative infections.
Drapes were placed around the operative field to isolate the area from the rest of the body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surgical field - the area exposed and prepared for surgery
