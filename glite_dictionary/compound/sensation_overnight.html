sensation overnight

1. NOUN

/ sɛnˈseɪʃən ˈoʊvərˌnaɪt / (AmE), / sɛnˈseɪʃən ˈəʊvənaɪt / (BrE)
A person or thing that achieves sudden and widespread fame or popularity within a very short time.
person or thing achieving instant fame

Examples

After her song went viral, she became a sensation overnight and appeared on all the talk shows.
The new video game was a sensation overnight, with millions of downloads in its first week.
He didn't expect his invention to become a sensation overnight, but it quickly captured the public's interest.

Synset Examples

She became a sensation overnight after the show.
The gadget became a sensation overnight, selling out everywhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overnight success - sudden and unexpected achievement or fame
