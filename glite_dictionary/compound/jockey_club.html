jockey club

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɑki klʌb / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒki klʌb / (BrE)
an organization established to promote and regulate horse racing
organization promoting horse racing

Examples

Members of the jockey club met to discuss new regulations for the upcoming racing season.
The jockey club oversees the licensing of trainers and jockeys in the country.
After years of involvement, she finally became president of the jockey club.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racecourse - a track where horse races are held
