Gopher State

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡoʊfər steɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊfə steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the U.S. state of Minnesota
nickname for Minnesota (U.S. state)

Examples

Many people consider the Gopher State to be one of the best places to live in the Midwest.
The Gopher State is known for its thousands of lakes and cold winters.
She moved from the Gopher State to pursue her studies in California.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Minnesota - a state in the northern United States; the Gopher State

Etymology

Refers to the large population of gophers native to Minnesota; used as an honorific nickname since the 19th century.
