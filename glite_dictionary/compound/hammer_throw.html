hammer throw

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæmər ˌθroʊ / (AmE), / ˈhæmə ˌθrəʊ / (BrE)
a track and field event in which a heavy metal ball attached to a wire is thrown for distance
track and field event involving throwing a ball on a wire

Examples

He set a new record in the hammer throw at the national championships.
The hammer throw requires both strength and precise technique.
She has been training for the hammer throw since she was a teenager.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discus throw - track and field event of throwing a heavy disc
  • Synonym: javelin throw - track and field event of throwing a spear-like implement
  • Synonym: shot put - track and field event of putting a heavy spherical object
