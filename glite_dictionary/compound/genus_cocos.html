genus Cocos

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˈkoʊkɒs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈkəʊkɒs / (BrE)
A genus of palm trees, chiefly comprising the coconut palm (Cocos nucifera).
a genus of coconut palms

Examples

The genus Cocos includes the well-known coconut palm, valued for its fruit.
Botanists classify the coconut tree under the genus Cocos.
Only one species, Cocos nucifera, is currently recognized in the genus Cocos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cocos nucifera - the coconut palm, principal species of the genus

Etymology

From Latin 'cocos', the genus name for coconut palms.
