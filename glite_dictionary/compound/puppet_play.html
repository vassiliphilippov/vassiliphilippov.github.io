puppet play

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌpət pleɪ / (AmE), / ˈpʌpɪt pleɪ / (BrE)
a dramatic performance in which puppets are used to portray the characters instead of live actors
performance with puppets as actors

Examples

The children watched the puppet play with wide-eyed excitement at the library.
She decided to organize a puppet play for the school's arts festival.
A traditional puppet play was performed in the village square during the fair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puppet show - a performance in which puppets are manipulated by a puppeteer
