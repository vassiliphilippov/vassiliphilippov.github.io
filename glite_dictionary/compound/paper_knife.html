paper knife

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpər ˌnaɪf / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə ˌnaɪf / (BrE)
A small, usually sharp-edged knife used for opening envelopes or slitting the uncut pages of books.
small knife for opening envelopes or books

Examples

He used his paper knife to neatly open the envelope.
The antique paper knife was displayed on his writing desk.
She bought a silver paper knife as a gift for her grandfather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: letter opener - a device used to open envelopes
