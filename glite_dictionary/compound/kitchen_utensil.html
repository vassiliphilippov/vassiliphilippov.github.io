kitchen utensil

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtʃən juːˈtɛnsl̩ / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃɪn juːˈtɛnsl̩ / (BrE)
an object or tool, such as a spoon, knife, or whisk, used in the kitchen for preparing food
tool used in food preparation

Examples

She bought several new kitchen utensils for her apartment.
The wooden spoon is a classic kitchen utensil used in many recipes.
Clean your kitchen utensils thoroughly after each use to ensure hygiene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utensil - an implement or container used in a household, especially a kitchen
