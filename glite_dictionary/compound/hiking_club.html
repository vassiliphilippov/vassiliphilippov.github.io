hiking club

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪkɪŋ klʌb / (AmE), / ˈhaɪkɪŋ klʌb / (BrE)
an organized group of people who meet regularly to go hiking together
group organized for hiking activities

Examples

Maria joined the hiking club to explore local trails and meet new friends.
The university's hiking club organizes trips every weekend during the summer.
After moving to the city, he found a hiking club that welcomed beginners.

Synset Examples

He's been a member of the hiking club for years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: walking club - a group organized for regular walking activities
