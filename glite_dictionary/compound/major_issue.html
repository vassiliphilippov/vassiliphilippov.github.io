major issue

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪdʒər ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdʒə ˈɪʃuː / (BrE)
A significant or important problem that requires attention or resolution.
an important problem

Examples

Climate change has become a major issue discussed by world leaders at international conferences.
The lack of affordable housing is a major issue facing urban areas today.
Internet security is now considered a major issue for businesses and individuals alike.

Synset Examples

Healthcare is a major issue in many developing countries.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: minor issue - a small or unimportant problem
