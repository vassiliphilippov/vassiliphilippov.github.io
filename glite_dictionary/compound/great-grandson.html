great-grandson

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪt ˌɡrænˌsɑn / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪt ˌɡræn.sʌn / (BrE)
A son of one's grandson or granddaughter.
son of a grandchild

Examples

My great-grandson is visiting us for the summer holidays.
She proudly showed off a photo of her newborn great-grandson.
He attended his great-grandson's graduation ceremony last weekend.

Related Terms
