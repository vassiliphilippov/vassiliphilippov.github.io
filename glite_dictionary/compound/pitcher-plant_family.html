pitcher-plant family

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪtʃɚ plænt ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃə plɑːnt ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of insectivorous plants characterized by modified leaves that form a deep cavity, or 'pitcher', used to trap and digest insects
family of insect-trapping plants

Examples

The pitcher-plant family includes several species with unusual insect-trapping leaves.
Biologists are fascinated by the diversity within the pitcher-plant family.
Nepenthaceae is one of the best-known groups in the pitcher-plant family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitcher plant - an insectivorous plant with a deep, pitcher-shaped leaf for trapping insects
