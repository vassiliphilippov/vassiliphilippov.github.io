Uigur language

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːɡər ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈwiːɡə ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken by the Uighur people, primarily in northwestern China
Turkic language of the Uighur people

Examples

He is fluent in both Chinese and Uigur language.
Many ancient manuscripts are written in Uigur language.
The university offers courses in the Uigur language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Turkish - Major Turkic language spoken mainly in Turkey
  • Synonym: Kazakh language - Turkic language spoken in Kazakhstan and surrounding regions

Etymology

From Uighur (ethnic name) + language; Uighur from Turkic *Uyghur* (living person, speaker).
