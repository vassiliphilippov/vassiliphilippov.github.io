Atlantean ruin

1. NOUN

/ ˌætlənˈtiːən ˈruːɪn / (AmE), / ˌatlænˈtiːən ˈruːɪn / (BrE)
the remains or ruins believed to be from the mythical lost city of Atlantis
ruins from the myth of Atlantis

Examples

Archaeologists have long debated whether any Atlantean ruin actually exists beneath the ocean.
The novel describes an ancient Atlantean ruin filled with mysterious technology.
Many explorers have searched for evidence of an Atlantean ruin throughout history.

Synset Examples

The Atlantean ruins were discovered underwater.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Atlantis - mythical lost city described by Plato

Etymology

From Atlantis, a mythical island described by Plato, combined with 'ruin' meaning remains of a destroyed place.
