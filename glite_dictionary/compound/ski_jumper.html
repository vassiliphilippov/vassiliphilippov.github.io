ski jumper

1. NOUN

/ ski ˈdʒʌmpər / (AmE), / ski ˈdʒʌmpə / (BrE)
a person who participates in ski jumping by skiing down a ramp and leaping into the air
a person who does ski jumping

Examples

The ski jumper soared gracefully over the snow-covered slope.
Norway has produced many world-class ski jumpers over the years.
As a young ski jumper, he admired champions from previous Winter Olympics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ski jumping - the sport in which competitors ski down a ramp and jump as far as possible
