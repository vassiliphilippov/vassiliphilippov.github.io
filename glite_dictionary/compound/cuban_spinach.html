Cuban spinach

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjuː.bən ˈspɪ.nɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bən ˈspɪ.nɪtʃ / (BrE)
a succulent herb sometimes grown as a salad or cooking green, found on dunes and waste ground along the Pacific coast of North America
a succulent herb used as a salad green

Examples

Local farmers sometimes grow Cuban spinach as a nutritious alternative to traditional greens.
She added chopped Cuban spinach to the soup for extra flavor and texture.
Dishes featuring Cuban spinach are popular in regions along the Pacific coast.
