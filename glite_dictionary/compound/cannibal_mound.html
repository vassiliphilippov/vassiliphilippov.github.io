cannibal mound

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæn.ɪ.bəl maʊnd / (AmE), / ˈkæn.ɪ.bəl maʊnd / (BrE)
A dish consisting of ground beef mixed with raw egg and other ingredients such as onions, capers, and anchovies, eaten raw.
raw beef dish with egg and seasonings

Examples

In some restaurants, the cannibal mound is considered a delicacy for adventurous eaters.
The chef prepared a traditional cannibal mound with fresh capers and onions for the guests.
Before ordering the cannibal mound, be aware that the dish contains raw beef and egg.
