facial hair

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪʃəl hɛr / (AmE), / ˈfeɪʃəl heə / (BrE)
hair that grows on the face, especially that of a man, including the beard, mustache, or sideburns
hair that grows on the face

Examples

He started growing facial hair in his late teens.
Some people prefer to keep their facial hair neatly trimmed.
Regular shaving helps reduce the appearance of facial hair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beard - hair growing on the lower part of the face
  • Antonym: clean-shaven - having no facial hair, especially after shaving
