panda car

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæn.də kɑr / (AmE), / ˈpæn.də kɑː / (BrE)
a small police patrol car, typically marked with distinctive black-and-white or blue-and-white colors, used by police for general duty patrols in Britain
British police patrol car
british

Examples

The old panda car cruised slowly through the neighborhood on its nightly patrol.
After hearing the alarm, a panda car arrived at the scene within minutes.
Many British TV dramas feature the distinctive blue-and-white panda car as part of their police force.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police car - a vehicle used by police for patrol duties

Etymology

From the car's distinctive black-and-white (like a panda) or blue-and-white livery, originally used by British police in the 1960s.
