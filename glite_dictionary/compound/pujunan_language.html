Pujunan language

1. NOUN

/ puːˈdʒuːnən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / pjuːˈdʒuːnən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a group of Penutian languages spoken by the Maidu people of northern California.
Penutian language of the Maidu people

Examples

The Pujunan language is an important part of the Maidu people's cultural heritage.
Few speakers of the Pujunan language remain today, making its preservation critical.
Linguists have studied the Pujunan language to understand its place among Penutian languages.
