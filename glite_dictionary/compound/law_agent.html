law agent

1. NOUN

/ lɔ ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / lɔː ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
A solicitor or legal representative, especially as used in Scotland.
Scottish term for a solicitor
legal british

Examples

He appointed a law agent to handle the conveyance process in Scotland.
As a qualified law agent, she advised clients on various legal matters.
The law agent prepared the necessary documents for the property sale.

Related Terms
