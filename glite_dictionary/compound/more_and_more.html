more and more

1. ADV

/ mɔr ənd mɔr / (AmE), / mɔːr ənd mɔː / (BrE)
increasingly; to a greater and greater extent or degree over time
increasingly; to a greater extent

Examples

People are becoming more and more concerned about climate change.
She found herself relying more and more on her friends for support.
As I learned, I started to enjoy the subject more and more.

Synset Examples

she became increasingly depressed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: increasingly - to an increasing extent; more and more
