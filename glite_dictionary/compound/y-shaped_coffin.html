Y-shaped coffin

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪ ʃeɪpt ˈkɑːfɪn / (AmE), / ˈwaɪ ʃeɪpt ˈkɒfɪn / (BrE)
a coffin that has the form of the letter Y, with three branches extending from a base
coffin shaped like the letter Y

Examples

The artist designed a unique Y-shaped coffin for the exhibition.
Rumors spread about the eccentric billionaire ordering a custom Y-shaped coffin.
Each branch of the Y-shaped coffin was decorated with elaborate carvings.
