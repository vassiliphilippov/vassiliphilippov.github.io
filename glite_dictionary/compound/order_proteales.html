order Proteales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər proʊˈtiːəˌliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə prəʊˈtiːəˌliːz / (BrE)
A taxonomic plant order that includes the family Proteaceae and related families.
plant order including Proteaceae
medical

Examples

The order Proteales is notable for its diversity in Australia and South Africa.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Proteaceae - a family of flowering plants within the order Proteales

Etymology

From Latin 'Protea', genus name, plus '-ales', a standard taxonomic suffix denoting orders.
