business organisation

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs ˌɔrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈbɪznəs ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
A company or other entity that engages in commercial, industrial, or professional activities, and the people involved in it.
commercial company or enterprise

Examples

The business organisation recently expanded its operations into Asia.
She works for a multinational business organisation with offices around the world.
Starting a successful business organisation requires careful planning and a talented team.

Synset Examples

he bought his brother's business
a small mom-and-pop business
a racially integrated business concern

Related Terms

  • Synonym: company - a commercial business
  • Synonym: business - a commercial activity or enterprise
