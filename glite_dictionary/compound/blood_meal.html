blood meal

1. NOUN

/ blʌd miːl / (AmE), / blʌd miːl / (BrE)
a powder made from dried animal blood, used especially as a fertilizer or a protein supplement in animal feed
powdered animal blood used as fertilizer or feed

Examples

Many organic gardeners use blood meal to enrich the nitrogen content of their soil.
The feed was supplemented with blood meal to improve its protein levels.
Care should be taken when applying blood meal around pets, as it can attract animals.
