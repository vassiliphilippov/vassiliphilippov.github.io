chopsocky film

1. NOUN

/ tʃɑpˈsɑki fɪlm / (AmE), / tʃɒpˈsɒki fɪlm / (BrE)
A martial arts film characterized by exaggerated fight scenes and often featuring comedic elements.
martial arts film with exaggerated action and comedy
informal

Examples

The fight choreography in the new chopsocky film was wildly entertaining.
Many fans enjoy the over-the-top action sequences typical of a chopsocky film.
He has a collection of every major chopsocky film released since the 1970s.

Synset Examples

The fight scenes in that chopsocky film were amazing.
He loves those classic chopsocky films from the 80s.
They watched a hilarious chopsocky movie last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: martial arts film - a film genre focused on martial arts and combat

Etymology

Blend of 'chop sock' (imitating fighting sounds) and '-y', coined in English in the 1970s to describe Asian martial arts movies.
