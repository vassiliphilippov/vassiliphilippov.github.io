Somrai language

1. NOUN

/ somˈraɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / sɒmˈraɪ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in the Tandjile Region of Chad
Chadic language of Chad

Examples

Linguists have recently studied the phonetic structure of the Somrai language.
The Somrai language is primarily spoken northeast of Lai in Chad.
Efforts are underway to document the vocabulary of the Somrai language for future generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chadic language - a branch of the Afro-Asiatic language family spoken in Central Africa
