kukui nut oil

1. NOUN

/ kuˈkuː.i nʌt ɔɪl / (AmE), / kuˈkuː.i nʌt ɔɪl / (BrE)
an oil extracted from the nuts of Aleurites moluccanus, used in cooking, skincare, and traditional medicine, especially in Hawaii
oil from kukui nuts, used in cooking and skincare

Examples

Traditional Hawaiian healers often use kukui nut oil to soothe sunburns and dry skin.
She massaged a few drops of kukui nut oil into her hair for extra shine and moisture.
The chef drizzled kukui nut oil over the salad for a subtle nutty flavor.

Etymology

Hawaiian kukui (candlenut) + English nut oil.
