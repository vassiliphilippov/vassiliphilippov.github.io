in general

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈdʒɛnərəl / (AmE), / ɪn ˈdʒɛnərəl / (BrE)
used to indicate that a statement applies broadly, without regard to specific cases or details.
usually; broadly speaking

Examples

In general, people find the new system easier to use than the old one.
She likes fruit in general, but she doesn't care for bananas.
In general, the weather here is mild throughout the year.

Synset Examples

he is interested in snakes in general

Related Terms

  • Synonym: generally - in most cases; usually
  • Antonym: specifically - clearly defined or identified; in a detailed way

2. ADV

/ ɪn ˈdʒɛnərəl / (AmE), / ɪn ˈdʒɛnərəl / (BrE)
usually; as a rule; when considering most instances rather than specific examples
usually; generally speaking

Examples

In general, people in this region are very friendly to visitors.
In general, it takes about two hours to drive between the cities.
Students, in general, performed well on the final exam.

Synset Examples

In general, I enjoy quiet mornings with coffee.
In general, the meeting was productive and well-received.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: generally - usually; in most cases; overall
  • Synonym: overall - with everything considered; in general
