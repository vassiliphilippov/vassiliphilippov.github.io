petty argument

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛti ˈɑrɡjəmənt / (AmE), / ˈpɛti ˈɑːɡjəmənt / (BrE)
a disagreement or dispute about something that is unimportant or trivial
unimportant or trivial dispute

Examples

Their friendship ended over a petty argument about where to eat dinner.
Let's not waste our time on another petty argument when we should be working together.
The meeting was delayed by a petty argument among the team members.

Synset Examples

He got upset over the petty arguments at work.
He has no time for petty arguments at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quarrel - an angry argument or disagreement, typically between people who are usually on good terms
  • Synonym: dispute - an argument or debate, especially one that is serious or formal
