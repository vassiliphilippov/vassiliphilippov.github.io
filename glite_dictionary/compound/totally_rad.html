totally rad

1. ADJ

/ ˈtoʊtəli ræd / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəli ræd / (BrE)
Extremely cool or impressive; excellent.
extremely cool or impressive
informal american slang

Examples

That skateboard trick was totally rad!
Everyone thought her vintage jacket looked totally rad at the party.
The concert last night was totally rad; I'm still buzzing from the energy.

Synset Examples

That movie was totally rad, bro!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awesome - extremely impressive or daunting; inspiring awe
  • Antonym: lame - uncool, unimpressive, weak or dull

Etymology

Slang phrase formed in the 1980s from "totally" (completely) and "rad" (short for "radical" meaning excellent or cool in American slang).
