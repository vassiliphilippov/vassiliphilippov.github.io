shopping cart

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑpɪŋ kɑrt / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpɪŋ kɑːt / (BrE)
a large basket on wheels, pushed by hand, used by shoppers in a store to carry groceries or other goods
wheeled basket used for shopping

Examples

She filled her shopping cart with fresh produce and bread.
A loose shopping cart rolled across the parking lot on a windy day.
Please return the shopping cart to the designated area after use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trolley - British term for shopping cart

2. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑpɪŋ kɑrt / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpɪŋ kɑːt / (BrE)
a large basket on wheels provided by supermarkets and stores for customers to use while collecting items they intend to purchase
a wheeled basket used in stores for shopping

Examples

She pushed a shopping cart down the aisle, looking for fresh fruit.
After filling his shopping cart, he headed for the checkout lane.
Please return your shopping cart to the designated area after use.

Synset Examples

She added a bumper to her shopping cart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trolley - a similar cart for shopping, especially in British English
