artistic creation

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈtɪstɪk kriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪstɪk kriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or result of creating something with artistic value or significance
creation of something artistic or significant

Examples

He takes great pride in every artistic creation he produces.
