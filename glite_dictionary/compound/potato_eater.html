potato eater

1. NOUN

/ pəˈteɪtoʊ ˈiːtər / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ ˈiːtə / (BrE)
a person who enjoys eating potatoes or simple comfort food
person who loves potatoes or comfort food

Examples

She considers herself a proud potato eater and never misses a chance to try a new potato recipe.
The festival was full of potato eaters lining up for fries and baked potatoes.
Being a potato eater sometimes means finding joy in the simplest of meals.

Synset Examples

He proudly identified as a potato eater, loving all things potato.
Being a potato eater means you enjoy comfort food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foodie - a person with a particular interest in food; a gourmet
