bowed stringed instrument

1. NOUN

/ boʊd ˈstrɪŋd ˌɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / bəʊd ˈstrɪŋd ˌɪnstrʊmənt / (BrE)
A stringed musical instrument that is played by drawing a bow across its strings.
stringed instrument played with a bow

Examples

The bowed stringed instrument section played the melody beautifully during the concert.
Learning to play a bowed stringed instrument requires practice and patience.
The violin is perhaps the most popular bowed stringed instrument in classical music.

Synset Examples

the strings played superlatively well

Related Terms

  • Synonym: violin - a high-pitched bowed string instrument
  • Synonym: cello - a large bowed string instrument with a deep sound
  • Antonym: plucked stringed instrument - string instrument played by plucking rather than with a bow
