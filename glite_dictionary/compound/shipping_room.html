shipping room

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪpɪŋ ruːm / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpɪŋ ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a business or warehouse where goods are packaged and prepared for shipment
room for packaging and shipping goods

Examples

The packages left the shipping room on a daily basis for delivery across the country.
After being checked and labeled, each item was moved to the shipping room for final packaging.
She works in the shipping room, making sure every box is correctly addressed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warehouse - a large building for storing goods
