Paris-Sorbonne University

1. PROPN

/ ˌpæɹɪ sɔɹˈbɔn ˌjunəˈvɝsəti / (AmE), / ˌpaɪərɪ sɔːˈbɒn ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsəti / (BrE)
a historic university in Paris renowned as a leading intellectual and cultural center of France
historic university in Paris

Examples

She earned her degree in history at Paris-Sorbonne University before moving abroad.
Many influential philosophers have lectured at Paris-Sorbonne University over the centuries.
International students are attracted to Paris-Sorbonne University for its rich academic tradition.

Etymology

From the name of the university's location in Paris and the Sorbonne, a historic college of the University of Paris.
