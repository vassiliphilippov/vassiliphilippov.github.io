endless paperwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛndləs ˈpeɪpərˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈɛndləs ˈpeɪpəˌwɜːk / (BrE)
an excessive or seemingly never-ending amount of tedious forms and documents, especially as part of an official or bureaucratic process
a large amount of tedious documentation

Examples

After the merger, the staff spent weeks dealing with endless paperwork to update all the records.
Teachers often complain about the endless paperwork involved in grading and administration.
Despite all her efforts, she still faced endless paperwork to finalize the permit.

Synset Examples

He hated the endless paperwork required for the application.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bureaucracy - complicated administrative procedures and official rules
  • Synonym: red tape - excessive administrative procedures and formalities
