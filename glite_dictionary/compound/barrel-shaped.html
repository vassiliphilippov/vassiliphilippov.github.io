barrel-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈbærəl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈbærəl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a rounded, cylindrical shape similar to that of a barrel
rounded and cylindrical like a barrel

Examples

The large, barrel-shaped tank dominated the corner of the warehouse.
Some fish, like pufferfish, have barrel-shaped bodies that help them deter predators.
He noticed the old building’s barrel-shaped roof gleaming in the sunlight.
