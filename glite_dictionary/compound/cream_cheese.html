cream cheese

1. NOUN

/ krim tʃiz / (AmE), / kriːm tʃiːz / (BrE)
a soft, smooth cheese made from sweet milk and cream, usually spreadable and eaten fresh
soft, spreadable cheese made from milk and cream

Examples

He spread cream cheese on his bagel for breakfast.
The recipe calls for eight ounces of cream cheese.
You can mix herbs into cream cheese for a savory dip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheese - a food made from the pressed curds of milk
