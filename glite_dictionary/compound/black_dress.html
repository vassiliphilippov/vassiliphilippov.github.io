black dress

1. NOUN

/ blæk drɛs / (AmE), / blæk drɛs / (BrE)
a dress that is black in color, often considered stylish or appropriate for formal occasions
a stylish dress that is black

Examples

She wore a stunning black dress to the party.
Every woman should have a classic black dress in her wardrobe.
He bought his sister a beautiful black dress for her graduation.
