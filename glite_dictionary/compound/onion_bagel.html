onion bagel

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌnjən ˈbeɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈʌnjən ˈbeɪɡl̩ / (BrE)
A bagel flavored with onion, either mixed into the dough or sprinkled on top.
bagel flavored with onion

Examples

I ordered an onion bagel with cream cheese for breakfast.
She prefers to toast her onion bagel to enhance the flavor.
The bakery was famous for its freshly baked onion bagels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bagel - a chewy bread roll shaped like a ring
