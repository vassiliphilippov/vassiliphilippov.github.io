truck bed

1. NOUN

/ trʌk bɛd / (AmE), / trʌk bɛd / (BrE)
the flat or open cargo-carrying part at the back of a truck, designed for transporting goods
cargo area of a truck

Examples

We loaded the bikes into the truck bed before heading to the mountains.
Rainwater had collected in the truck bed overnight, so we had to dry it out.
He installed a cover to protect the truck bed from weather and theft.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flatbed - a type of truck or trailer with a flat, open bed
  • Synonym: cargo bed - the area for carrying cargo in a vehicle
