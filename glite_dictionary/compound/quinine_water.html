quinine water

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwaɪnaɪn ˈwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈkwɪniːn ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
A carbonated soft drink flavored with quinine and typically lime or lemon.
carbonated drink with quinine and citrus flavor

Examples

He ordered a glass of quinine water with a slice of lime.
The bitter taste of quinine water makes it popular as a mixer for cocktails.
Some people drink quinine water to relieve leg cramps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tonic water - a carbonated beverage containing quinine, often used in mixed drinks
