huntsman's cups

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌntsmənz kʌps / (AmE), / ˈhʌntsmənz kʌps / (BrE)
a perennial bog plant of northeastern North America, with dark red flowers and decumbent, broadly winged pitcher leaves arranged in a rosette, naturalized in parts of Europe
perennial bog plant with pitcher leaves

Examples

The bog was filled with unusual plants, including several huntsman's cups near the water's edge.
Botanists discovered a colony of huntsman's cups thriving in the wet peatland of western Ireland.
The unique pitcher leaves of the huntsman's cups help the plant capture and digest insects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitcher plant - a plant with modified leaves forming a deep cavity that traps insects
