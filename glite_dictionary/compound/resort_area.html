resort area

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈzɔrt ˌɛriə / (AmE), / rɪˈzɔːt ˌeəriə / (BrE)
an area, often developed with hotels and attractions, where many people go for recreation or holidays
area frequented for recreation or holidays

Examples

The city is famous for its resort area along the beach, which attracts thousands of tourists each year.
We spent our vacation in a beautiful resort area surrounded by mountains and lakes.
Several new hotels opened last year to accommodate the growth in the resort area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resort - place for relaxation or recreation, often with tourist facilities
