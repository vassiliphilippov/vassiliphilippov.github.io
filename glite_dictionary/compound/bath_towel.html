bath towel

1. NOUN

/ bæθ ˈtaʊəl / (AmE), / bɑːθ ˈtaʊəl / (BrE)
a large towel used for drying the body after bathing or showering
large towel for drying after bathing

Examples

She wrapped herself in a soft bath towel after stepping out of the shower.
Please make sure every guest has a fresh bath towel in their room.
The hotel provides complimentary bath towels for all guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: towel - piece of absorbent cloth for drying
