common four-o'clock

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈfɔɚ əˌklɑk / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈfɔːr əˌklɒk / (BrE)
A garden plant (Mirabilis jalapa) of North America with fragrant flowers in various colors that open late in the afternoon.
ornamental plant with fragrant flowers opening late in the day

Examples

The common four-o'clock blooms in a variety of vibrant colors throughout the summer.
She planted several common four-o'clock seeds along the garden path.
By late afternoon, the common four-o'clock began to fill the air with its sweet scent.
