cascade everlasting

1. NOUN

/ kæsˈkeɪd ˌɛvərˈlæstɪŋ / (AmE), / kæsˈkeɪd ˌɛvəˈlɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
A shrub with white woolly branches and leaves, bearing fragrant flowers in long sprays, often used for drying; sometimes classified in the genus Helichrysum.
shrub with woolly branches and fragrant sprays of flowers

Examples

The cascade everlasting looks stunning in dried flower arrangements that last for months.
Gardeners often grow cascade everlasting for its fragrant, woolly blooms and attractive foliage.
I saw a beautiful cascade everlasting in the botanical garden's Mediterranean section.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helichrysum - genus of plants including the everlasting flowers
