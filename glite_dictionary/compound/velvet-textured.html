velvet-textured

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɛlvət ˌtɛkstʃɚd / (AmE), / ˈvɛlvɪt ˌtɛkstʃəd / (BrE)
having a smooth and soft surface or quality like that of velvet, especially in appearance, sound, touch, or taste
smooth and soft like velvet

Examples

She wore a velvet-textured dress that shimmered under the lights.
The singer’s velvet-textured voice charmed everyone in the audience.
I love the velvet-textured feel of this chocolate mousse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: velvety - having the soft, smooth quality of velvet
