Yamaltu language

1. NOUN

/ jɑːˈmæl.tu ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / jɑːˈmæl.tjuː ˈlaŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language with three tones, spoken in northeastern Nigeria
Chadic language of northeastern Nigeria

Examples

The Yamaltu language is primarily spoken in the Gombe State of Nigeria.
Recent research has documented the unique tone system used in the Yamaltu language.
Efforts are being made to preserve the Yamaltu language for future generations.
