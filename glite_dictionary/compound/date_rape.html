date rape

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪt ˌreɪp / (AmE), / ˈdeɪt ˌreɪp / (BrE)
Rape committed by someone known socially to the victim, especially during or after a date.
rape by a person known to the victim, often on a date

Examples

The university offers support services for victims of date rape and other sexual assaults.
In recent years, awareness campaigns have highlighted the seriousness of date rape.
She bravely spoke out about her experience with date rape to raise awareness among college students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acquaintance rape - rape committed by a person known to the victim
