check overdraft credit

1. NOUN

/ tʃɛk ˈoʊvərˌdræft ˈkrɛdɪt / (AmE), / tʃɛk ˈəʊvəˌdrɑːft ˈkrɛdɪt / (BrE)
credit facility provided by a bank to cover a customer's overdrafts when checks overdraw their account
bank credit for covering overdrafts by check

Examples

Many customers appreciate the security of check overdraft credit when writing large checks.
Applying for check overdraft credit can help you avoid bounced check fees.
The bank charges interest on check overdraft credit used to cover shortfalls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overdraft facility - an arrangement with a bank to borrow money when an account is overdrawn
