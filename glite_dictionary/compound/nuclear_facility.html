nuclear facility

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːkliɚ fəˈsɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˈnjuːkliə fəˈsɪlɪti / (BrE)
A site where nuclear energy is produced or handled, especially for electricity generation or research.
site for nuclear energy production or handling

Examples

The government tightened security measures at the nuclear facility after the threat was discovered.
A new nuclear facility is planned to be built in the region within the next five years.
Scientists at the nuclear facility are conducting important research on radioactive materials.

Synset Examples

They built a nuclear facility near the coast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nuclear power plant - a facility that generates electricity using nuclear energy
