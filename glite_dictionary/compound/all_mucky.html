all mucky

1. ADJ

/ ɔl ˈmʌki / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈmʌki / (BrE)
Covered with dirt or mud all over; very dirty or muddy.
completely dirty or muddy
informal

Examples

After the soccer match, his shorts were all mucky from sliding in the grass.
The dog ran into the house all mucky after playing in the puddle.
Her boots got all mucky while she was working in the garden.

Synset Examples

After the rain, the garden was all mucky.
The kids came inside all mucky from playing outside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mucky - Dirty or covered with mud
