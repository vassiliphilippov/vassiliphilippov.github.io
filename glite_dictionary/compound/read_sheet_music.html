to read sheet music

1. VERB

/ ɹid ʃit ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / ɹiːd ʃiːt ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
to interpret and play music by understanding written musical notation
interpret written musical notation

Examples

It takes years of practice to read sheet music quickly and accurately.
Some talented musicians can read sheet music while playing complex pieces.
Learning to read sheet music opens up a world of new songs to play.

Synset Examples

Every band kid knows how to read sheet music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to sight-read - to perform music from written notation without preparation
