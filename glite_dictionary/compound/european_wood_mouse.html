European wood mouse

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən wʊd maʊs / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən wʊd maʊs / (BrE)
a small, nocturnal yellowish-brown mouse found in woods, fields, and gardens across Europe
nocturnal European mouse species

Examples

The European wood mouse is often seen scurrying through leaf litter at night.
Researchers tracked the population of European wood mice in the forest preserve.
Unlike house mice, the European wood mouse prefers wooded areas and gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wood mouse - a small, nocturnal mouse found in woodland areas
