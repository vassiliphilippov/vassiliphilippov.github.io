paper toweling

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpɚ ˈtaʊəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə ˈtaʊəlɪŋ / (BrE)
Absorbent paper manufactured for use as a towel, typically in sheets or rolls for drying hands, cleaning, or wiping up spills.
absorbent paper for use as toweling

Examples

Please refill the dispenser with paper toweling before we run out.
She used a piece of paper toweling to clean up the spilled juice.
Many public restrooms provide paper toweling instead of hand dryers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paper towel - a sheet of absorbent paper for wiping and cleaning
