flanker back

1. NOUN

/ ˈflæŋkər bæk / (AmE), / ˈflaŋkə bak / (BrE)
a player in American football positioned wide of the line of scrimmage, chiefly used as a pass receiver
football player position wide of scrimmage line

Examples

The coach decided to use a flanker back to stretch the defense.
Our team's flanker back caught two touchdown passes last night.
She studied the role of the flanker back for her sports journalism class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wide receiver - a player positioned wide and used as a pass catcher in football
