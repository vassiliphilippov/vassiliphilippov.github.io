bull neck

1. NOUN

/ bʊl nɛk / (AmE), / bʊl nɛk / (BrE)
A thick, short, and powerful neck, especially one resembling that of a bull.
thick, short, powerful neck

Examples

The wrestler's bull neck was his most noticeable feature in the ring.
He developed a bull neck from years of heavy lifting at the gym.
Her father had a bull neck and square shoulders, giving him a very imposing presence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thickset - having a broad, sturdy form or build
