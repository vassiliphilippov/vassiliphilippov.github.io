clip joint

1. NOUN

/ klɪp dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / klɪp dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
A place of entertainment, such as a nightclub or bar, where customers are charged excessively for poor or substandard service or entertainment, often by dishonest means.
dishonest entertainment venue with overpriced poor service
informal american

Examples

He warned us that the bar looked like a clip joint before we went inside.
Tourists are often the main targets for clip joints in that district.
After discovering it was a clip joint, she demanded a refund for the overpriced drinks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dive - cheap, disreputable bar or nightclub
  • Degree Lower: nightclub - a club that provides music and dancing and often food and drink
  • Synonym: scam - a dishonest scheme or fraud

Etymology

From 'clip' (slang for cheat or swindle) + 'joint' (slang for establishment or place), early 20th century American English.
