to land the plane

1. VERB

/ lænd ðə pleɪn / (AmE), / lænd ðə pleɪn / (BrE)
to bring an aircraft down on the ground safely, usually at an airport
bring an aircraft to ground safely

Examples

The pilot skillfully landed the plane despite the heavy crosswinds.
Emergency crews were ready as she prepared to land the plane.
Learning to land the plane is the most challenging part of flight training.

Synset Examples

She was nervous but managed to land the plane safely.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to take off - to become airborne in an aircraft
