East Africa

1. PROPN

/ iːst ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (AmE), / iːst ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (BrE)
the eastern region of the African continent, typically including countries such as Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, Burundi, and sometimes others
eastern region of Africa

Examples

She traveled across East Africa to study wildlife in the Serengeti and beyond.
Droughts in East Africa have threatened crops and water supplies in recent years.
The cultures of East Africa are known for their rich diversity and traditional music.
