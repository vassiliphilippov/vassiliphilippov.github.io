orb web

1. NOUN

/ ɔrb wɛb / (AmE), / ɔːb wɛb / (BrE)
a circular web with radiating spokes and spiral threads, typically built by certain spiders
a circular spider web

Examples

The garden spider spun an intricate orb web between two branches.
Morning dew made the orb web in the bushes shimmer beautifully.
Many species of spiders are known for their perfectly shaped orb webs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cobweb - web spun by a spider, especially when old and covered with dust

Etymology

From Latin 'orbis' (circle, disk) and 'web'.
