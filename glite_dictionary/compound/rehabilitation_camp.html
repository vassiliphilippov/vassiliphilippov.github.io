rehabilitation camp

1. NOUN

/ ˌriː.həˌbɪl.ɪˈteɪ.ʃən kæmp / (AmE), / ˌriː.həˌbɪl.ɪˈteɪ.ʃən kamp / (BrE)
a facility where individuals are detained, often forcibly, for re-education or enforced behavioral change
facility for enforced re-education or behavioral change

Examples

Many political prisoners were sent to rehabilitation camps for years at a time.
The international community condemned the use of rehabilitation camps for detaining religious minorities.
Documentaries have exposed the harsh realities faced by inmates in rehabilitation camps.

Synset Examples

Survivors of the reeducation camp shared their harrowing stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reeducation camp - a camp where people are forcibly re-educated
