Deep North

1. NOUN

/ dip nɔrθ / (AmE), / diːp nɔːθ / (BrE)
a region, especially in the far north of a country, known for its remote natural beauty and frequently referenced in cultural contexts
remote northern region noted for its beauty

Examples

Many travelers are drawn to the Deep North for its pristine wilderness and lakes.
Her novel paints a vivid picture of life in the Deep North.
Documentaries about the Deep North often highlight its harsh winters and stunning scenery.

Synset Examples

Traveling to the Deep North offers breathtaking views.
