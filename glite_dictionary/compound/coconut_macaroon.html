coconut macaroon

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊˌkʌt ˌmækəˈrun / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌkʌt ˌmækəˈruːn / (BrE)
A sweet baked confection made with shredded coconut, egg whites, and sugar, typically forming a soft, chewy center.
a sweet cookie with coconut

Examples

She brought a box of homemade coconut macaroons to the party.
My favorite treat at the bakery is the chocolate-dipped coconut macaroon.
After dinner, we enjoyed coffee and a coconut macaroon for dessert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: macaroon - a type of small, sweet cake or cookie, often made with almond paste or coconut
