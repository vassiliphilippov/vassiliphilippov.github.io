jeep girl

1. NOUN

/ dʒip ɡɜrl / (AmE), / dʒiːp ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a woman who is passionate about outdoor adventures, especially those involving a Jeep vehicle
woman passionate about Jeep adventures
informal

As a jeep girl, she enjoys meeting others who share her passion for exploring in Jeeps.
