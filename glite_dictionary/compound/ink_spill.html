ink spill

1. NOUN

/ ɪŋk spɪl / (AmE), / ɪŋk spɪl / (BrE)
an accidental release of ink, especially from a pen or ink container
accidental release of ink

Examples

The ink spill stained her shirt and the desk beyond repair.
He rushed to clean up the ink spill before anyone noticed.
After an ink spill, the drawings on the page were ruined.

Synset Examples

She placed a cap on the pen to avoid ink spills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spill - the act of accidentally pouring a liquid out of its container
