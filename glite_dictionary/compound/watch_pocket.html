watch pocket

1. NOUN

/ wɑtʃ ˈpɑkɪt / (AmE), / wɒtʃ ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
A small pocket, especially in a vest, designed to hold a pocket watch.
small pocket for a pocket watch

Examples

He reached into his watch pocket to check the time.
The antique vest still had a perfectly stitched watch pocket on the left side.
She found an old coin tucked inside her grandfather's watch pocket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pocket - a small pouch or compartment in clothing
