heart and soul

1. NOUN

/ hɑrt ənd soʊl / (AmE), / hɑːt ənd səʊl / (BrE)
the most essential, vital, or important part of something; the central core
essential or most vital part of something

Examples

The heart and soul of her argument was the need for equal opportunity.
He is considered the heart and soul of the team, holding everyone together.
The city centre is truly the heart and soul of the community.

Synset Examples

the gist of the prosecutor's argument
the heart and soul of the Republican Party
the nub of the story

Related Terms

  • Synonym: core - the central, most important part of something
  • Synonym: essence - the intrinsic nature or indispensable quality of something
  • Antonym: periphery - the outer limits or edge of something
