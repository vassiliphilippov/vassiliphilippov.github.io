imperial beard

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈpɪriəl bɪrd / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪəriəl bɪəd / (BrE)
A small, tufted beard on the chin, similar to the style worn by Emperor Napoleon III.
a small, tufted chin beard style

Examples

His imperial beard gave him a distinguished and somewhat royal appearance.
Many 19th-century portraits depict men with the fashionable imperial beard.
After trimming his beard, he shaped it into a neat imperial beard for a historical costume party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goatee - a small pointed beard on the chin
