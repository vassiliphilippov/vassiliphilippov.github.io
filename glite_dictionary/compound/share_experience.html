to share experience

1. VERB

/ ʃɛr ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / ʃeə ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
to tell others about one's knowledge or insights, especially from personal events or activities, often to help or inform them
exchange knowledge or insights

Examples

During the conference, many participants shared experience about implementing new technologies in their companies.
Teachers often share experience to help each other improve classroom management techniques.
It's valuable to share experience with colleagues to learn best practices and avoid common mistakes.

Synset Examples

They shared their eggsperience with local cuisine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exchange ideas - to discuss thoughts or opinions with others
