art school

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt skuːl / (AmE), / ɑːt skuːl / (BrE)
a school or college where students study and train in the visual arts, such as painting, sculpture, or design
school specializing in the visual arts

Examples

After graduating from high school, she enrolled in an art school to pursue her passion for painting.
Many famous artists received their formal training at an art school.
The art school offers courses in sculpture, illustration, and digital design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: art college - a college specializing in art education
