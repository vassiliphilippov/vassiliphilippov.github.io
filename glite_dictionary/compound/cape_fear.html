Cape Fear

1. PROPN

/ keɪp ˈfɪr / (AmE), / keɪp ˈfɪə / (BrE)
A cape located in southeastern North Carolina that extends into the Atlantic Ocean.
cape in southeastern North Carolina

Examples

Many ships have encountered storms off Cape Fear.
The lighthouse near Cape Fear is a popular tourist attraction.
Hurricanes often threaten the coast around Cape Fear during the summer.
