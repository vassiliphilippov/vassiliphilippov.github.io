pastry dough

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪstri doʊ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪstri dəʊ / (BrE)
a mixture of flour, water, and fat (such as butter or shortening), used as the base for making pastries
dough base for pastries

Examples

To make the tart, first prepare the pastry dough and chill it in the refrigerator.
She rolled out the pastry dough carefully before lining the pie tin.
The pastry dough should be light and flaky for the best results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pastry - baked food made from dough of flour, water, and fat
  • Synonym: dough - a mixture of flour and liquid used to make baked goods
