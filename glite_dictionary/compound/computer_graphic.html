computer graphic

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtər ˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈɡrafɪk / (BrE)
an image or visual representation created using a computer, especially by means of specialized software
image generated by a computer

Examples

The animated computer graphic showed the flow of traffic through the intersection.
He used a computer graphic to illustrate the new building design in his presentation.
Modern video games rely heavily on advanced computer graphic techniques to create realistic environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graphic - a visual image or design

Etymology

Formed from 'computer' and 'graphic', with 'graphic' from Greek 'graphikos' (of drawing or writing).
