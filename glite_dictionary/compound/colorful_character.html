colorful character

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɚfəl ˈkɛrɪktɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌləfəl ˈkærɪktə / (BrE)
a person who is interesting, unusual, or eccentric, often in a way that is lively or notable
interesting or eccentric person

Examples

The town is full of colorful characters who each have fascinating stories to tell.
As a colorful character, she never failed to liven up any gathering.
He became known as a colorful character in the local art scene.

Synset Examples

Toontown is known for its colorful characters and fun rides.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - a person with unconventional and slightly strange views or behavior
  • Synonym: oddity - an unusual or remarkable person or thing
