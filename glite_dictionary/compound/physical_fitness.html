physical fitness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈfɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈfɪtnəs / (BrE)
A state of good health and strength resulting from regular exercise and proper nutrition.
state of good physical condition

Examples

Maintaining physical fitness requires regular exercise and a balanced diet.
The military puts a strong emphasis on physical fitness for all recruits.
Her physical fitness improved dramatically after she started cycling every day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fitness - the condition of being physically fit and healthy
  • Synonym: health - the state of being free from illness or injury
