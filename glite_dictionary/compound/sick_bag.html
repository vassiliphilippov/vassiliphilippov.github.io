sick bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪk bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈsɪk bæɡ / (BrE)
a disposable bag provided to passengers, especially on airplanes, for use if they feel nauseous and need to vomit
vomit bag for airline passengers

Examples

Feeling queasy, he quickly reached for a sick bag during the turbulent flight.
Most airplanes have a sick bag in the seat pocket just in case it's needed.
She discreetly placed the used sick bag in the trash after landing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air sickness bag - a bag for vomiting, provided on aircraft
