Multikulti approach

1. NOUN

/ ˌmʌltiˈkʊlti əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈkʊlti əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
an approach or method that emphasizes or incorporates multiple cultures, especially in social, educational, or political contexts
multicultural method or perspective

Examples

The teacher's Multikulti approach created a welcoming atmosphere for students from all backgrounds.
Several politicians criticize the Multikulti approach for failing to promote integration.
Our community center adopts a Multikulti approach to encourage participation from all cultural groups.

Synset Examples

His Multikulti approach fosters unity among different groups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multiculturalism - the presence and co-existence of diverse cultural groups

Etymology

From German 'Multikulti', short for 'multikulturell' (multicultural), combined with English 'approach'.
