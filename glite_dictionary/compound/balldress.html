balldress

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔlˌdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈbɔːl.drɛs / (BrE)
A formal dress worn by a woman at a ball or formal social event.
formal dress for a ball or formal event

Examples

She purchased a stunning balldress for the charity gala.
The museum displayed an 18th-century balldress worn by a duchess.
Choosing the perfect balldress took her weeks of searching and fittings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gown - a long elegant dress worn on formal occasions
  • Synonym: evening dress - a formal dress for evening wear
  • Antonym: tuxedo - formal suit for men, often worn at balls
