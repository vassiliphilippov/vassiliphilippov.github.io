packing nut

1. NOUN

/ ˈpækɪŋ nʌt / (AmE), / ˈpakɪŋ nʌt / (BrE)
a nut used to compress packing material in a stuffing box to prevent leakage around a shaft or rod
nut for tightening packing in a stuffing box

Examples

You may need to tighten the packing nut if the faucet begins to leak.
The engineer adjusted the packing nut on the pump to stop the drip.
Replacing the worn packing nut restored the valve's proper function.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stuffing box - a casing used to prevent leakage around a moving shaft or rod
