Bantu-speaking

1. ADJ

/ ˈbæn.tuˌspiː.kɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbæn.tuːˌspiː.kɪŋ / (BrE)
able to speak or communicate in a Bantu language.
able to speak a Bantu language

Examples

She is Bantu-speaking thanks to her upbringing in Tanzania.
Several Bantu-speaking communities live in this area of the Congo.
The Bantu-speaking children in the class help translate for their classmates.

Etymology

From 'Bantu' (a group of related languages of central and southern Africa) + 'speaking'.
