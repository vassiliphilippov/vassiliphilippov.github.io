lefteye flounder

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛftˌaɪ ˈflaʊndɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɛftaɪ ˈflaʊndə / (BrE)
A flatfish species in which both eyes are located on the left side of the head, typically of the family Bothidae.
flatfish with both eyes on left side

Examples

The lefteye flounder is well-camouflaged against the sandy seabed.
Several species of lefteye flounder are found in tropical and subtropical waters.
Fishermen often identify a lefteye flounder by the position of its eyes.
