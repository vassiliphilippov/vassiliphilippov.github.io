Republic of Tajikistan

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv tɑːˈdʒiːkɪstæn / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv taːˈdʒiːkɪstæn / (BrE)
The official name of the country of Tajikistan, a landlocked, mountainous republic in southeast Central Asia north of Afghanistan.
