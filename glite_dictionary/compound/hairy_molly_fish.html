hairy molly fish

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛri ˈmɑli fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈheəri ˈmɒli fɪʃ / (BrE)
a small freshwater fish, often kept in aquariums, known for its distinctive bristly appearance
a freshwater aquarium fish with bristly features

Examples

Many aquarium enthusiasts add a hairy molly fish to their tanks for its unique look.
The hairy molly fish thrives in well-maintained freshwater environments with plenty of plants.
She pointed out the bristly fins of the hairy molly fish at the pet store.

Synset Examples

The hairy molly fish is popular in home aquariums.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: molly - a type of small, livebearing freshwater fish popular in aquariums
