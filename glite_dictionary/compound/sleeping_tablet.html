sleeping tablet

1. NOUN

/ ˈsliːpɪŋ ˌtæblət / (AmE), / ˈsliːpɪŋ ˌtæblɪt / (BrE)
a pill or tablet containing a drug taken to help induce sleep
pill taken to help induce sleep

Examples

The doctor prescribed a sleeping tablet to help him overcome his insomnia.
After taking a sleeping tablet, she was able to get a good night's rest.
It is not advisable to drive after taking a sleeping tablet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleeping pill - a pill taken to help someone sleep
