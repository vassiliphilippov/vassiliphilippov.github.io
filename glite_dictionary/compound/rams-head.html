ram's-head

1. NOUN

/ ˈræmzˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈræmzˌhɛd / (BrE)
a rare orchid of northern North America with a brownish-green flower and a red-and-white lip resembling a ram's head
a North American orchid with ram-shaped flower lip

Examples

The ram's-head orchid is a protected species in parts of Canada.
We spotted a beautiful ram's-head while hiking in the forest.
Botanists are excited by the discovery of several blooming ram's-heads this season.
