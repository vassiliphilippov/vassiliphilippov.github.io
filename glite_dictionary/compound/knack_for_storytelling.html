knack for storytelling

1. NOUN

/ næk fər ˈstɔːriˌtɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / nak fə ˈstɔːriˌtɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
an innate skill or ability for telling stories effectively
natural skill in telling stories

Examples

She has a real knack for storytelling that brings her characters to life.
His knack for storytelling makes even ordinary events sound fascinating.
The author's knack for storytelling captivates readers from the very first page.

Synset Examples

Hughie has a knack for storytelling that captivates audiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gift for storytelling - a natural talent for telling stories
  • Synonym: talent for storytelling - an aptitude for telling engaging stories
  • Synonym: knack - a natural skill or talent for doing something
