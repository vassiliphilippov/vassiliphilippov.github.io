Angora cat

1. NOUN

/ ænˈɡɔrə kæt / (AmE), / ænˈɡɔːrə kæt / (BrE)
a long-haired breed of domestic cat with a slender body and silky fur, originally from Turkey
long-haired breed of domestic cat

Examples

The Angora cat is known for its graceful appearance and fine, silky coat.
She has two pets: a Siamese and an Angora cat, both very friendly.
The Angora cat originated in Turkey and is prized for its elegant fur.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persian cat - a long-haired breed of cat with a round face and short muzzle

Etymology

Named after Ankara (historically Angora), Turkey, where the breed originated.
