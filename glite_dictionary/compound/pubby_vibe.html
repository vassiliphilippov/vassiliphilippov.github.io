pubby vibe

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌbi vaɪb / (AmE), / ˈpʌbi vaɪb / (BrE)
a casual, relaxed, and friendly atmosphere similar to that found in a pub
informal, pub-like atmosphere

Examples

The bar's pubby vibe makes it a popular spot for after-work drinks.
They decorated the basement to create a pubby vibe for their parties.
If you want a place with a real pubby vibe, this is the best in town.

Synset Examples

She prefers a pubby vibe for her casual meetups.
