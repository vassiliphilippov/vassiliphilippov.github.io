shaving soap

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃeɪvɪŋ soʊp / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪvɪŋ səʊp / (BrE)
A type of soap formulated to produce a rich lather for softening facial hair before shaving.
soap used for shaving lather

Examples

He always preferred using a traditional shaving soap and brush for his morning routine.
The shaving soap produced a thick, creamy lather that helped the razor glide smoothly over his skin.
Many barbers recommend shaving soap for achieving the closest and most comfortable shave.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shaving cream - A cream used for the same purpose as shaving soap
