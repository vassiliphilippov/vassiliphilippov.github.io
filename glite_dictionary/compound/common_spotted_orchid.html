common spotted orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈspɑtɪd ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈspɒtɪd ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A European orchid (Dactylorhiza fuchsii) with lance-shaped leaves spotted with purple, and pink to white or mauve flowers marked with deep red or purple spots or lines.
a European wild orchid with spotted flowers

Examples

We saw a beautiful common spotted orchid growing by the woodland path.
The common spotted orchid is often found in meadows throughout Europe.
Botanists studied the unique markings of the common spotted orchid during the summer survey.
