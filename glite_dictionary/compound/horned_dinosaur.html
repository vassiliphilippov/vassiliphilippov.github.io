horned dinosaur

1. NOUN

/ hɔrnd daɪˈnɔsɔr / (AmE), / hɔːnd daɪˈnəʊsɔː / (BrE)
a type of four-legged herbivorous dinosaur with a large beaked skull and horns, especially from the late Cretaceous period
herbivorous dinosaur with beaked skull and horns

Examples

The fossilized remains of a horned dinosaur were discovered in Montana.
Triceratops is perhaps the most famous horned dinosaur from the Cretaceous period.
Recent studies have revealed how the elaborate frills benefited horned dinosaurs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ceratopsian - beaked, horned dinosaurs of the Ceratopsia group
