yellow green

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈɡrin / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈɡriːn / (BrE)
a shade of green that has a noticeable yellow tint
a green shade tinged with yellow

Examples

She chose the yellow green fabric for her new dress to match the spring theme.
The leaves turned a delicate yellow green as they began to bud in early April.
Artists often use yellow green to depict sunlight filtering through fresh foliage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chartreuse - a color halfway between yellow and green
  • Degree Lower: olive - a dark yellowish-green color
  • Degree Higher: lime - a bright yellowish-green color
