quick sesh

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk sɛʃ / (AmE), / kwɪk sɛʃ / (BrE)
An informal, brief session or meeting, often for discussion or relaxation.
short informal session
informal slang

Examples

We had a quick sesh after work to finalize the project details.
Do you have time for a quick sesh before lunch?
The team likes to start Mondays with a quick sesh to set the week's agenda.

They scheduled a quick sesh to brainstorm ideas.
