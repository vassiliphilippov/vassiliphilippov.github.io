one-man rule

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌn ˌmæn ˈruːl / (AmE), / ˈwʌn ˌmæn ˈruːl / (BrE)
a system of government in which all power is held by a single individual who acts as an absolute dictator, without checks or opposition.
system of government by a single dictator

Examples

The country suffered for decades under one-man rule and lacked basic freedoms.
History has shown that one-man rule often leads to widespread corruption and repression.
Many citizens protested against the return of one-man rule after years of democracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dictatorship - a form of government where power is concentrated in the hands of a single person or group
