field event

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːld ɪˌvɛnt / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ɪˌvɛnt / (BrE)
an athletic competition that takes place on a field rather than a running track, such as jumping or throwing events
athletics event held on a field

Examples

The javelin and discus are classic field events at the Olympic Games.
She excels in both sprinting and field events like the long jump and shot put.
All field events will take place on the main athletics ground this afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: track event - an athletic event held on a running track
