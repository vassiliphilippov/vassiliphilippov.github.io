African chameleon

1. NOUN

/ ˈæfrɪkən kəˈmiːliən / (AmE), / ˈaf.rɪ.kən kəˈmiː.li.ən / (BrE)
a species of chameleon native to Africa, known for its color-changing ability and independently moving eyes
a chameleon species native to Africa

Examples

The African chameleon can rapidly change its color to blend into its environment.
Many people keep the African chameleon as an exotic pet because of its fascinating appearance.
In the forests of Madagascar, the African chameleon hunts insects using its long, sticky tongue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chameleon - a lizard known for changing its color
