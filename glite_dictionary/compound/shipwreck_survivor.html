shipwreck survivor

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪpˌrɛk səˈvaɪvər / (AmE), / ˈʃɪp.rɛk səˈvaɪ.və / (BrE)
a person who has survived a shipwreck
person who survived a shipwreck

Examples

The shipwreck survivor recounted his harrowing experiences to the news reporters.
After days adrift at sea, the shipwreck survivor was finally rescued by a passing vessel.
That book tells the story of a shipwreck survivor stranded on a deserted island.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: survivor - a person who continues to live after an accident or disaster
