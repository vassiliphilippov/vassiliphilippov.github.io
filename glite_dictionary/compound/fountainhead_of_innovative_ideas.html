fountainhead of innovative ideas

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaʊntənˌhɛd əv ˌɪnəˈveɪtɪv aɪˈdiəz / (AmE), / ˈfaʊntənˌhɛd əv ˌɪnəˈveɪtɪv aɪˈdɪəz / (BrE)
the primary and abundant source of creative and original concepts or solutions
main source of original ideas

Examples

The research institute is a fountainhead of innovative ideas for the entire industry.
Many regarded Steve Jobs as the fountainhead of innovative ideas at Apple.
This university has long been celebrated as a fountainhead of innovative ideas in science and technology.

Synset Examples

She was the fountainhead of innovative ideas in the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: source - a place, person, or thing from which something originates
  • Synonym: fountainhead - an original source of something, especially of a stream, or metaphorically of ideas
  • Synonym: wellspring - an abundant source of something, especially inspiration

Etymology

Fountainhead: from 'fountain' (a spring or source) and 'head' (top or beginning); figuratively meaning the origin or chief source. 'Innovative ideas' refers to new, creative concepts.
