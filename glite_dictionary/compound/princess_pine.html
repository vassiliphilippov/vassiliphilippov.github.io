princess pine

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪn.sɪs paɪn / (AmE), / ˈprɪn.sɪs paɪn / (BrE)
a North American evergreen plant of the clubmoss family (Lycopodium obscurum), resembling a small pine tree
a type of evergreen clubmoss plant

Examples

Each holiday season, florists use princess pine as greenery in wreaths and table decorations.
The shady forest floor was carpeted with patches of princess pine.
Some people harvest princess pine for decorative purposes, but over-collection can harm local ecosystems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ground pine - another common name for clubmosses such as Lycopodium obscurum
  • Synonym: clubmoss - any of several evergreen plants of the family Lycopodiaceae

Etymology

Named for its resemblance to a miniature pine tree and its delicate, ornamental appearance.
