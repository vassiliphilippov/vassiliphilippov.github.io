Cebuano language

1. NOUN

/ sɛˈbwɑːnoʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / sɛˈbwɑːnəʊ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
The Austronesian language spoken by the people of Cebu and in other parts of the central Philippines.
Austronesian language of Cebu, Philippines

Examples

The Cebuano language is widely spoken throughout the Visayas region of the Philippines.
She studied the Cebuano language to better communicate with local communities on Cebu.
Many Filipino films include dialogue in the Cebuano language to reflect regional diversity.

Etymology

From Cebu, the name of the Philippine island, with Spanish suffix -ano (denoting belonging), and 'language'.
