paper white

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpər waɪt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə waɪt / (BrE)
A type of daffodil with star-shaped white flowers, often grown indoors for winter blooming.
daffodil with white, star-shaped flowers

Examples

My grandmother likes to cultivate paper white bulbs in her kitchen during the holidays.
The fragrance of paper white daffodils filled the conservatory.
You can force paper white flowers to bloom indoors by planting them in shallow dishes.
