trying on

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraɪɪŋ ɑn / (AmE), / ˈtraɪɪŋ ɒn / (BrE)
the act of putting on clothes to see if they fit or suit you
putting on clothes to test fit

Examples

She spent an hour trying on dresses before the party.
The trying on of shoes can be exhausting in a busy store.
A long line formed outside the trying on rooms during the sale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fitting - the act of putting on clothes to check their fit
