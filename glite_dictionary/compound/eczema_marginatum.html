eczema marginatum

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛk.sɪ.mə mɑr.dʒɪˈneɪ.təm / (AmE), / ˈɛks.ɪ.mə mɑː.dʒɪˈneɪ.təm / (BrE)
A fungal infection affecting the groin area, mostly seen in men.
fungal infection of the groin
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed him with eczema marginatum after examining the rash in his groin area.
Athletes are sometimes prone to developing eczema marginatum due to excessive sweating and tight clothing.
Treatment for eczema marginatum usually involves antifungal creams or powders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tinea cruris - fungal infection of the groin, also known as jock itch

Etymology

From Latin 'eczema' (to boil over) and 'marginatum' (having a margin).
