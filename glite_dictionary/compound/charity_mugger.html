charity mugger

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃær.ə.t̬i ˈmʌɡ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃær.ɪ.ti ˈmʌɡ.ə / (BrE)
a person who aggressively solicits donations for charities, often in public places such as streets or shopping areas
aggressive charity fundraiser
informal british

Examples

The charity mugger blocked my path, trying to get me to sign up for a monthly donation.
Many people find charity muggers intrusive when they are just trying to go about their day.
After several encounters with charity muggers, Jane started avoiding the city center.

Synset Examples

The charity mugger approached me in the street.
I avoided the charity mugger by crossing the street.
Beware of charity muggers in busy areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chugger - informal synonym for charity mugger

Etymology

Blend of 'charity' and 'mugger', the latter used colloquially for someone who accosts others.
