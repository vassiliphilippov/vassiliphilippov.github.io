budget cut

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌdʒɪt kʌt / (AmE), / ˈbʌdʒɪt kʌt / (BrE)
a reduction in the amount of money allocated in a budget, often resulting in decreased spending or services
reduction in allocated budget

Examples

The university announced a significant budget cut that will affect several departments.
Due to a budget cut, the library will have to reduce its opening hours.
Many public services have suffered after the recent budget cut.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cutback - a reduction in something, especially in spending
