behind on

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈhaɪnd ɑn / (AmE), / bɪˈhaɪnd ɒn / (BrE)
not having completed or kept up with tasks, obligations, or payments as expected or required
not up to date with tasks or payments

Examples

I'm behind on my emails and need to catch up this afternoon.
She realized she was behind on her rent payments and needed to talk to the landlord.
After his vacation, Tom was behind on several projects at work.

Synset Examples

He was always behind on his assignments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: late - after the expected or usual time
  • Antonym: caught up - having completed all outstanding tasks or obligations
