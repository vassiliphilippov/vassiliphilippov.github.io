Denali National Park and Preserve

1. PROPN

/ dəˈnɑːli ˌnæʃəˈnæl pɑrk ənd prəˈzɜrv / (AmE), / dəˈnɑːli ˌnæʃəˈnæl pɑːk ənd prɪˈzɜːv / (BrE)
A large national park in Alaska notable for the Alaska Range, including Mount McKinley (Denali), and the prominent Denali Fault.
national park in Alaska, USA

Examples

Last summer, I visited Denali National Park and Preserve and was amazed by the sweeping mountain views.
Wildlife such as bears and moose are often seen in Denali National Park and Preserve.
Many tourists travel to Alaska each year to experience the beauty of Denali National Park and Preserve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Denali - the highest mountain peak in North America, located in Alaska

Etymology

Named after Denali, the tallest mountain in North America; 'Denali' is from the Koyukon Athabascan word meaning 'the high one'.
