derby hat

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɝːbi hæt / (AmE), / ˈdɑːbi hæt / (BrE)
A hard felt hat with a rounded crown and a narrow brim, traditionally worn by men.
rounded hard felt hat with narrow brim

Examples

He wore a derby hat and carried a cane, looking every bit the gentleman.
In old photographs, you often see men in suits and derby hats standing on city streets.
The museum display included a classic derby hat from the early twentieth century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowler hat - a hard felt hat with a rounded crown, known as a bowler hat in British English

Etymology

Named after the Derby, a British horse race where this style of hat was popularized in the mid-19th century.
