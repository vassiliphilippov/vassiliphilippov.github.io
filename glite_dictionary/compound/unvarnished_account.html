unvarnished account

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈvɑrnɪʃt əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / ʌnˈvɑːnɪʃt əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
an honest and straightforward description that is not embellished or altered
honest, straightforward description

Examples

The witness delivered an unvarnished account of what happened that night.
Readers appreciated the reporter's unvarnished account of the disaster, free from sensationalism.
He preferred to hear an unvarnished account rather than a sugar-coated version of the truth.

Synset Examples

She gave an unvarnished account of the events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frank account - an open and honest description
  • Antonym: embellished account - a description that is exaggerated or altered
