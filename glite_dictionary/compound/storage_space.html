storage space

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔːrɪdʒ speɪs / (AmE), / ˈstɔːrɪdʒ speɪs / (BrE)
an area in a building, container, or device that is used to store things
area for storing things

Examples

We need more storage space in the kitchen for our pots and pans.
My phone is running out of storage space, so I have to delete some apps.
The attic provides plenty of storage space for old furniture and boxes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storage - the action or method of storing something
