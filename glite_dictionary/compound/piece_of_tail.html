piece of tail

1. NOUN

/ pis əv teɪl / (AmE), / piːs əv teɪl / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) A person regarded purely as an opportunity for sexual intercourse; a sexual partner.
sexual partner (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar

Examples

He bragged to his friends about getting a piece of tail last night.
She disliked being treated like just a piece of tail instead of a real person.
Movies from that era often objectified women as little more than a piece of tail.
