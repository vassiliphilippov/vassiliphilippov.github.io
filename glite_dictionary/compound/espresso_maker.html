espresso maker

1. NOUN

/ ɛˈsprɛs.oʊ ˈmeɪ.kɚ / (AmE), / ɛˈspres.əʊ ˈmeɪ.kə / (BrE)
a machine for making espresso coffee by forcing steam or hot water under pressure through finely ground coffee beans
machine for making espresso coffee

Examples

She bought a new espresso maker to enjoy fresh coffee at home every morning.
The café uses a traditional Italian espresso maker for their signature drinks.
Learning how to clean your espresso maker will keep your coffee tasting great.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffee maker - a device for brewing coffee
