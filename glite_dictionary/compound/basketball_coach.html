basketball coach

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskɪtˌbɔl koʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtˌbɔːl kəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a person who trains and directs a basketball team or its individual players
coach of a basketball team

Examples

The team's basketball coach was praised for leading them to the championships.
After retiring as a player, she became a successful basketball coach.
Every young athlete looked up to their basketball coach for guidance and encouragement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coach - a person who trains and directs athletes or teams
