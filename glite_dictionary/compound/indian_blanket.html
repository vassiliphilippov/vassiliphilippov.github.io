Indian blanket

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən ˈblæŋkət / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈblaŋkɪt / (BrE)
an annual wildflower of the central United States, with showy yellow flower heads marked with scarlet or purple in the center (Gaillardia pulchella)
a North American wildflower with yellow and red flowers

Examples

The fields were bright with blooming Indian blanket in the summer sun.
We planted Indian blanket in our garden to attract butterflies and bees.
The Indian blanket is known for its vibrant yellow and red petals.
