loss of life

1. NOUN

/ lɔs əv laɪf / (AmE), / lɒs əv laɪf / (BrE)
the death of people, especially as a result of an accident, disaster, or violence
death of people in an event

Examples

The earthquake caused a significant loss of life in the region.
Strict safety regulations are designed to minimize loss of life in the workplace.
Officials are investigating the incident to determine what led to the loss of life.

Synset Examples

The accident resulted in a tragic loss of life.
Emergency measures are critical to avoid loss of life.
We must prevent any further loss of life in this conflict.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: death - the end of life; dying
