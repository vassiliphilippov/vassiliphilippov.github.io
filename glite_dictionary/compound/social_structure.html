social structure

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ˈstrʌktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl ˈstrʌktʃə / (BrE)
the organized pattern of relationships and institutions that together compose a society
the organized pattern of relationships in society

Examples

Rapid technological changes have significantly impacted the social structure of modern societies.
Scientists analyzed the social structure of the primate community to understand its hierarchy.
Traditional values continue to shape the social structure in many rural regions.

Synset Examples

the social organizations of England and America are very different
sociologists have studied the changing structure of the family

Related Terms

  • Synonym: social organization - the arrangement of relationships and roles within a society
