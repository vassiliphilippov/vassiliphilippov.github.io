bit blurry

1. ADJ

/ bɪt ˈblɜri / (AmE), / bɪt ˈblʌri / (BrE)
Slightly unclear or vague; not fully clear or distinct in appearance, memory, or understanding.
slightly unclear or vague

Examples

The details in the old photograph are bit blurry, making faces hard to recognize.
His memory of the conversation is still bit blurry after so many years.
The image became bit blurry after I zoomed in too much.

Synset Examples

The details of the event are a bit blurry now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blurry - unclear or lacking sharpness
  • Antonym: clear - easy to perceive, understand, or see
