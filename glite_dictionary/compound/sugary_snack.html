sugary snack

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃʊɡɚi snæk / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡəri snæk / (BrE)
a sweet food item that contains a high amount of sugar, often eaten as a treat between meals
sweet food high in sugar

Examples

Many parents try to limit their children's sugary snacks to promote better health.
She reached for a sugary snack whenever she felt stressed at work.
The vending machine was filled with sugary snacks rather than healthier options.

Synset Examples

Children often prefer sugary snacks over healthy ones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confectionery - sweet foods made with sugar, like candy and chocolates
  • Degree Higher: dessert - sweet course eaten at the end of a meal
  • Antonym: healthy snack - a snack considered beneficial to health, low in sugar or fat
