sex appeal

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛks əˌpiːl / (AmE), / ˈseks əˌpiːl / (BrE)
the quality of being sexually attractive to others
sexual attractiveness

Examples

Some celebrities have a unique sex appeal that attracts millions of fans around the world.
The actor’s confidence and style only added to his sex appeal.
She dressed elegantly, knowing that subtlety often increased her sex appeal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attractiveness - the quality of being pleasing or appealing to the senses
  • Synonym: charisma - compelling attractiveness or charm

Etymology

From 'sex' (sexuality) and 'appeal' (attractiveness or charm); in use since the early 20th century.
