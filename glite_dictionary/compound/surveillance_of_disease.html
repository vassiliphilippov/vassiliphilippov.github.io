surveillance of disease

1. NOUN

/ sɚˈveɪ.ləns əv dɪˈziːz / (AmE), / sɜːˈveɪ.ləns əv dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
the continuous and systematic collection, analysis, and interpretation of health data regarding an infectious disease to enable actions for its control or prevention
systematic monitoring of infectious diseases
medical

Examples

Public health agencies prioritize surveillance of disease to identify outbreaks early and respond effectively.
Effective surveillance of disease can prevent the spread of infections in vulnerable populations.
Technological advances have greatly improved the accuracy of surveillance of disease worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: epidemiology - the branch of medicine dealing with the control of diseases
  • Synonym: disease control - intentional efforts to reduce incidences of disease
