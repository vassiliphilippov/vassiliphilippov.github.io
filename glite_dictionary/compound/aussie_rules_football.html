Aussie rules football

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔzi rulz ˈfʊtbɔl / (AmE), / ˈɒzi ruːlz ˈfʊtbɔːl / (BrE)
a fast-paced football game played mainly in Australia, with teams of eighteen players competing on an oval field, aiming to score by kicking an oval ball between goalposts
Australian football game

Examples

Many Australians grow up playing Aussie rules football in school and local clubs.
The grand final of Aussie rules football attracts huge crowds every year.
She joined a women's Aussie rules football team after moving to Melbourne.

Synset Examples

Aussie rules football is popular in Melbourne.
He enjoys Australian rules football every weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Australian rules football - the official name for the sport known as Aussie rules football

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'Australian rules football', originating in Australia in the mid 19th century.
