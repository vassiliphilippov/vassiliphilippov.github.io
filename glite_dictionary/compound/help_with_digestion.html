help with digestion

1. NOUN

/ hɛlp wɪð dɪˈdʒɛstʃən / (AmE), / hɛlp wɪð dɪˈdʒɛstʃən / (BrE)
to assist or promote the process of breaking down food in the digestive system
assist in digesting food

Examples

Drinking warm water before meals can help with digestion.
Some herbal teas are believed to help with digestion after a heavy meal.
Regular exercise is known to help with digestion and prevent discomfort.

Synset Examples

Eating more fiber helps with digestion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aid digestion - help the process of breaking down food
