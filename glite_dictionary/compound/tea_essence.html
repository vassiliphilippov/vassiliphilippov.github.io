tea essence

1. NOUN

/ ti ˈɛsəns / (AmE), / tiː ˈɛsəns / (BrE)
a concentrated liquid extract obtained from tea leaves, used to prepare tea or flavor foods and beverages
concentrated liquid extract from tea leaves

Examples

The recipe called for one teaspoon of tea essence to enhance the cake's flavor.
She keeps a bottle of tea essence in her pantry for making quick iced tea.
After boiling the leaves, they bottled the tea essence for later use.

Synset Examples

Mix the tea essence with water to make it drinkable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tea extract - concentrated solution derived from tea leaves
