propenyl alcohol

1. NOUN

/ proʊˈpɛnil ˈælkəhɒl / (AmE), / prəʊˈpɛnɪl ˈælkəhɒl / (BrE)
an unsaturated primary alcohol used in the production of resins, plasticizers, and pharmaceuticals
an unsaturated alcohol used in chemistry

Examples

Propenyl alcohol is commonly used in the synthesis of various resins and plasticizers.
Exposure to propenyl alcohol should be limited due to its potential health risks.
Researchers discovered a new pharmaceutical application for propenyl alcohol.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: allyl alcohol - an unsaturated alcohol isomeric with propenyl alcohol

Etymology

From 'propenyl' (indicating the chemical structure derived from propene) and 'alcohol'.
