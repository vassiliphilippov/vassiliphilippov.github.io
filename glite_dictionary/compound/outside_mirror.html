outside mirror

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtsaɪd ˈmɪrər / (AmE), / ˈaʊtsaɪd ˈmɪrə / (BrE)
a mirror mounted on the exterior of a vehicle to reflect the view at the side and behind the car
mirror on outside of a vehicle

Examples

She adjusted the outside mirror before driving onto the busy street.
The outside mirror was damaged in the parking lot accident.
Modern cars often have heated outside mirrors to prevent them from fogging up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: side mirror - mirror mounted on the side of a vehicle for rearward view
  • Synonym: rear-view mirror - mirror inside a vehicle showing view behind
