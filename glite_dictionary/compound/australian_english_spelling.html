Australian English spelling

1. NOUN

/ əˈstreɪliən ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈspɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ɒˈstreɪliən ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈspɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
The standard system of spelling used in Australia, typically combining conventions of British and American English, with some distinctive forms.
orthography used in Australia
british

Examples

The book uses Australian English spelling throughout, so you will notice 'colour' instead of 'color'.
Teachers in Australia generally teach Australian English spelling in their classrooms.
He submits all his essays using Australian English spelling, even when applying to universities abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: British English spelling - the spelling conventions used in England and other parts of the UK
  • Synonym: American English spelling - the spelling conventions used in the United States
