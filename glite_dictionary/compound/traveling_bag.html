traveling bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəlɪŋ bæg / (AmE), / ˈtrævəlɪŋ bæg / (BrE)
a portable rectangular bag used for carrying clothes and personal items while traveling
portable bag for clothes when traveling

Examples

He packed his clothes neatly into his traveling bag before leaving for the airport.
Her traveling bag was small enough to fit in the overhead compartment.
After the meeting, he grabbed his traveling bag and headed to the train station.

Synset Examples

he carried his small bag onto the plane with him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suitcase - a case with a handle used for carrying clothes when traveling
  • Synonym: duffel bag - a cylindrical bag for carrying personal belongings, often used for travel
