wood grain

1. NOUN

/ wʊd ɡreɪn / (AmE), / wʊd ɡreɪn / (BrE)
the natural pattern or texture on the surface of wood produced by its fibers
pattern or texture in wood fibers

Examples

The dining table's wood grain gives it a beautiful, rustic appearance.
Carpenters often highlight the wood grain with a clear finish.
Each piece of oak has a unique wood grain, making every project distinctive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grain - the texture or pattern of fibers in a material or surface, especially wood
