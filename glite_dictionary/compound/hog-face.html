hog-face

1. NOUN

/ hɑɡ feɪs / (AmE), / hɒɡ feɪs / (BrE)
an exaggerated facial expression showing disgust or disapproval
facial expression of disgust or disapproval

Examples

She made a hog-face when she tasted the sour lemon.
Whenever he hears bad news, he puts on a dramatic hog-face.
The child scrunched up his nose in a hog-face after smelling the garbage.

Synset Examples

I couldn’t help but make a hog-face at the silly movie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grimace - a twisted expression on a person's face, typically expressing disgust or pain
