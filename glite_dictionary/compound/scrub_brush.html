scrub brush

1. NOUN

/ skrʌb brʌʃ / (AmE), / skrʌb brʌʃ / (BrE)
a brush with short, stiff bristles used for heavy cleaning, especially of hard surfaces
brush for heavy cleaning

Examples

She grabbed the scrub brush and started cleaning the kitchen floor.
A stiff scrub brush works best for removing mud from boots.
Please use the scrub brush to scrub the tiles in the bathroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scrubbing brush - a brush for cleaning surfaces vigorously
