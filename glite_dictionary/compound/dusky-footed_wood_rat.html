dusky-footed wood rat

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌski ˌfʊtɪd ˈwʊd ˌræt / (AmE), / ˈdʌski ˌfʊtɪd ˈwʊd ˌræt / (BrE)
a wood rat species (Neotoma fuscipes) native to western North America, known for its dusky-colored feet and large stick nests
North American wood rat species with dusky feet

Examples

The dusky-footed wood rat builds elaborate nests from sticks and leaves in the forest understory.
Researchers have studied the diet of the dusky-footed wood rat to understand its ecological impact.
We spotted a dusky-footed wood rat scurrying through the bushes during our hike in California.
