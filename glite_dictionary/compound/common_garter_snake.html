common garter snake

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈɡɑrtər sneɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈɡɑːtə sneɪk / (BrE)
a widespread species of nonvenomous North American garter snake (Thamnophis sirtalis), known for its striped appearance
a widespread North American garter snake

Examples

We found a common garter snake basking in the sun near the pond.
The common garter snake is harmless and often found in gardens across North America.
Biologists study the common garter snake for its adaptability to various environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garter snake - any nonvenomous snake of the genus Thamnophis, found in North America
