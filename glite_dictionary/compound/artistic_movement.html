artistic movement

1. NOUN

/ ɑɹˈtɪstɪk ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪstɪk ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
A group of artists who share a common philosophy or style, often expressed through collective works and principles.
group sharing artistic style or philosophy

Examples

The Impressionist artistic movement transformed Western painting in the 19th century.
Many artists joined the Cubist artistic movement to explore new ways of representing reality.
Surrealism was an influential artistic movement in both art and literature.
