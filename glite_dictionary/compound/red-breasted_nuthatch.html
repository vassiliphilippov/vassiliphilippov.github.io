red-breasted nuthatch

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛdˈbrɛstɪd ˈnʌtˌhætʃ / (AmE), / ˌrɛdˈbrɛstɪd ˈnʌtˌhætʃ / (BrE)
a small songbird with bluish-grey upperparts and a reddish breast, found in northern coniferous forests of North America
small North American songbird with a reddish breast

Examples

We spotted a red-breasted nuthatch flitting among the pine branches.
The call of the red-breasted nuthatch is distinctive and nasal-sounding.
Several red-breasted nuthatches visited our backyard feeder this winter.
