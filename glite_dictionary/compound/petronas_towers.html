Petronas Towers

1. PROPN

/ pɛˈtroʊnɑs ˈtaʊərz / (AmE), / pɛˈtrəʊnæs ˈtaʊəz / (BrE)
a pair of adjacent skyscrapers in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, completed in 1997 and once the tallest buildings in the world
twin skyscrapers in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Examples

The Petronas Towers are a major tourist attraction in Malaysia.
From her hotel room, she could see the shimmering lights of the Petronas Towers at night.
The architectural design of the Petronas Towers incorporates elements of Islamic art.

Etymology

Named after Petroliam Nasional Berhad (PETRONAS), the Malaysian national oil company.
