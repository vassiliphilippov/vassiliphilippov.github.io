launching pad

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔn.tʃɪŋ ˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈlɔːn.tʃɪŋ ˌpæd / (BrE)
A platform or structure from which rockets or spacecraft are launched.
platform for launching rockets or spacecraft

Examples

After months of preparation, the rocket finally stood ready on the launching pad.
The engineers inspected the launching pad before the space mission began.
The old launching pad was decommissioned after the last successful launch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: launch pad - short form; platform for space launch
