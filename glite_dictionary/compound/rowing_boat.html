rowing boat

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊɪŋ ˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈrəʊɪŋ bəʊt / (BrE)
A small boat that is propelled using oars, especially for recreation or sport.
small boat propelled by oars

Examples

They hired a rowing boat on the lake for the afternoon.
The old rowing boat was tied to the dock, swaying gently with the tide.
She enjoys weekend trips in her rowing boat along the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rowboat - a small boat propelled by oars
