Second World War

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛkənd ˈwɜrld ˌwɔr / (AmE), / ˌsɛkənd ˈwɜːld ˌwɔː / (BrE)
A global war that lasted from 1939 to 1945, fought between the Allies and the Axis powers.
global war (1939–45) between Allies and Axis

Examples

Many families in Europe were separated during the Second World War.
The Second World War had a profound impact on global politics and society.
She wrote her thesis on the role of women in the Second World War.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: World War II - alternative name for Second World War
  • Degree Lower: First World War - the earlier world war, fought 1914–18
