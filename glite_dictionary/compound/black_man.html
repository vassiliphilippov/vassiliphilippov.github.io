Black man

1. NOUN

/ blæk mæn / (AmE), / blak man / (BrE)
a man who has Black African ancestry or is identified as being part of the Black racial or ethnic group
a man of Black African descent

Examples

He proudly identified himself as a Black man in his autobiography.
The film tells the story of a young Black man navigating life in the city.
She spoke about the challenges her father faced as a Black man in the 1960s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Black woman - a woman who has Black African ancestry or is identified as being part of the Black racial or ethnic group
  • Antonym: White man - a man who is identified as being White (Caucasian)
