oil of wintergreen

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl əv ˈwɪntərˌɡrin / (AmE), / ɔɪl əv ˈwɪntəˌɡriːn / (BrE)
A volatile oil obtained from the creeping wintergreen or teaberry plant, used as a flavoring and in medicine.
volatile oil from the wintergreen plant

Examples

The dentist used oil of wintergreen to flavor the mouthwash.
Some massage balms contain oil of wintergreen for its soothing properties.
As a child, she recognized the smell of oil of wintergreen in her grandmother’s remedies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: methyl salicylate - the chemical compound that constitutes oil of wintergreen
