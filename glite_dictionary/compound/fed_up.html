fed up

1. ADJ

/ fɛd ʌp / (AmE), / fɛd ʌp / (BrE)
annoyed, bored, or tired, especially after having had too much of something over a period of time
annoyed or bored after too much of something
informal

Examples

I'm completely fed up with all the noise from the construction work next door.
She was fed up with his constant complaining and decided to leave the room.
After waiting for over an hour, they were fed up and left the restaurant.

  • Synonym: tired of - bored or annoyed by something after doing it for too long
