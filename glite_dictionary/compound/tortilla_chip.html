tortilla chip

1. NOUN

/ tɔrˈtiːə tʃɪp / (AmE), / tɔːˈtiːə tʃɪp / (BrE)
A thin, crisp piece of fried or baked tortilla, usually served as a snack or with dips.
crisp snack made from tortilla

Examples

She dipped a tortilla chip into the salsa at the party.
A bowl of tortilla chips sat in the center of the table.
He prefers his tortilla chips with guacamole rather than cheese dip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nacho - a tortilla chip topped with melted cheese and other toppings

Etymology

From Spanish "tortilla", meaning 'little cake', and English "chip".
