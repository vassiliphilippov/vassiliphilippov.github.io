beer run

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪr ˌrʌn / (AmE), / ˈbɪə ˌrʌn / (BrE)
a quick trip to buy beer, especially before a social event runs out of it
a quick trip to buy beer
informal american

Examples

We're almost out of drinks, so I'll do a quick beer run before the guests arrive.
He volunteered for the beer run on the way to the camping site.
A late-night beer run became a tradition during finals week.

Synset Examples
