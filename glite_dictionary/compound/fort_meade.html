Fort Meade

1. PROPN

/ fɔrt ˈmid / (AmE), / fɔːt ˈmiːd / (BrE)
A United States Army base in Maryland, which serves as the headquarters of the National Security Agency (NSA).
U.S. Army base in Maryland, NSA headquarters
american

Examples

Thousands of military personnel work at Fort Meade in Maryland.
The National Security Agency’s main offices are located at Fort Meade.
Many cybersecurity professionals are stationed at Fort Meade.

Etymology

Named after General George G. Meade, a Union general in the American Civil War.
