edible cockle

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛdəbl ˈkɑkəl / (AmE), / ˈɛdəbl ˈkɒkəl / (BrE)
a common European species of cockle that is edible and often used as food
a common edible European cockle

Examples

Fishermen collected edible cockles from the sandy shores at low tide.
The restaurant's special included fresh edible cockles steamed with garlic.
Overharvesting has led to a decline in local edible cockle populations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cockle - an edible bivalve mollusc with a rounded shell
