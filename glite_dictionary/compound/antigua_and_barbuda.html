Antigua and Barbuda

1. PROPN

/ ænˈtiːgwə ənd bɑːrˈbjuːdə / (AmE), / ænˈtiːɡwə ənd bɑːˈbjuːdə / (BrE)
an island country located in the Caribbean, comprising the islands of Antigua, Barbuda, and several smaller islands
country in the Caribbean (Antigua & Barbuda)

Examples

Many tourists visit Antigua and Barbuda for its beautiful beaches and warm climate.
The official language of Antigua and Barbuda is English.
She studied the history of Antigua and Barbuda for her geography project.

Etymology

From the Spanish 'Antigua' (ancient) and 'Barbuda' (bearded), names given by early European explorers.
