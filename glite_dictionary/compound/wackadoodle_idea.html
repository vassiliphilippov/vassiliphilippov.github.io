wackadoodle idea

1. NOUN

/ ˈwækəˌduːdəl aɪˈdiːə / (AmE), / ˈwækədʊdəl aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
an idea that is strange, eccentric, or highly unconventional
strange or unconventional idea
informal

Examples

My boss always comes up with a wackadoodle idea before each big project.
Some dismissed her proposal as just another wackadoodle idea, but it actually worked.
He pitched a wackadoodle idea to solve the traffic problem, involving drones and roller skates.

Synset Examples

Her wackadoodle ideas often surprised everyone in meetings.
He had a wackadoodle plan to travel the world by bicycle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric idea - unusual or unconventional idea
  • Antonym: conventional idea - idea that follows traditional or common patterns

Etymology

From 'wackadoodle', a blend of 'wacky' and the playful suffix '-doodle', suggesting eccentricity or craziness.
