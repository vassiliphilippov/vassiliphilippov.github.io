western narrow-mouthed toad

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛstɚn ˈnæroʊ maʊθt toʊd / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən ˈnærəʊ maʊθt təʊd / (BrE)
a small, secretive toad with smooth, tough skin found in central and western North America.
a small toad native to central and western North America

Examples

On our hike, we spotted a western narrow-mouthed toad hiding beneath the rocks.
The western narrow-mouthed toad is known for its distinctive call during the rainy season.
Researchers studied the habitat preferences of the western narrow-mouthed toad in Texas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: narrow-mouthed toad - any toad of the Microhylidae family with a narrow mouth
