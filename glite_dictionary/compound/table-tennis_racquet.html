table-tennis racquet

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəl ˌtɛnɪs ˈrækət / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəl ˌtɛnɪs ˈrækɪt / (BrE)
A small, flat paddle used to hit the ball in table tennis.
paddle used in table tennis

Examples

She gripped the table-tennis racquet tightly before serving the ball.
His new table-tennis racquet gives him better spin on his shots.
You must use a standard table-tennis racquet in official tournaments.

Related Terms
