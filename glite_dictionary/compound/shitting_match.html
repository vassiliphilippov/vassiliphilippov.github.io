shitting match

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtɪŋ mætʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtɪŋ mætʃ / (BrE)
a heated or petty argument marked by anger, hostility, or personal attacks, often with little constructive outcome
angry or petty argument
informal vulgar

Examples

Their conversation quickly became a shitting match as tempers flared over the smallest issues.
The online discussion turned into a shitting match instead of a productive exchange of ideas.
She refused to engage in a shitting match with her coworkers, preferring to stay professional.

Synset Examples

Their argument turned into a shitting match over nothing.
The debate quickly devolved into a shitting match.
Avoid a shitting match by keeping your cool.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: argument - an exchange of diverging views, typically heated
  • Synonym: quarrel - an angry dispute or disagreement
  • Degree Lower: debate - a formal discussion on a particular topic
