to performed live

1. VERB

/ pərˈfɔrmd laɪv / (AmE), / pəˈfɔːmd laɪv / (BrE)
executed or presented in real-time in front of an audience, rather than recorded or pre-produced
presented in real-time before an audience

Examples

The band performed live on national television last night.
She has never performed live before such a large crowd until now.
The comedian prefers to perform live because of the energy from the audience.

Synset Examples

The songer performed live, leaving everyone in awe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: live performance - a performance given in real-time before an audience
