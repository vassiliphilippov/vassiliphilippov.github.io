painted tongue

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪntɪd tʌŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪd tʌŋ / (BrE)
A Chilean herb (Salpiglossis sinuata) with velvety, funnel-shaped yellowish or violet flowers, noted for long tongue-like styles at the corolla throat.
Chilean herb with velvety, colorful flowers

Examples

The painted tongue adds a bright splash of color to summer borders in many gardens.
We planted painted tongue alongside petunias for a vibrant floral display.
Because of its striking flowers, painted tongue is often used in ornamental gardens.
