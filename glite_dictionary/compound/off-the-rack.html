off-the-rack

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔf ðə ˈræk / (AmE), / ˌɒf ðə ˈræk / (BrE)
(especially of clothing) made in standard sizes and sold from stock, not custom-made.
clothing sold in standard sizes, not custom-made

Examples

She bought an off-the-rack dress for the wedding to save time and money.
Most suits in the store are off-the-rack, but they also offer tailoring services.
He prefers custom suits to off-the-rack clothing because of the better fit.

Synset Examples

ready-to-wear clothes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ready-to-wear - clothing made in standard sizes; not custom-tailored
  • Antonym: custom-made - made to fit the requirements or measurements of a particular person
