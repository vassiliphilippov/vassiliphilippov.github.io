dental technician

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛn.t̬əl tɛkˈnɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl tɛkˈnɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
A person who constructs dental appliances such as bridges, crowns, and dentures to the specifications of a dentist.
one who makes dental appliances

Examples

The dental technician crafted a set of dentures for the patient.
After taking an impression of my teeth, my dentist sent it to the dental technician.
A skilled dental technician can make natural-looking crowns and bridges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dental technologist - a person skilled in the technical work of dentistry
  • Antonym: dentist - a person qualified to treat diseases and conditions that affect the teeth and gums
