wall barley

1. NOUN

/ wɔl ˈbɑr.li / (AmE), / wɔːl ˈbɑː.li / (BrE)
A European annual grass (Hordeum murinum) often found as a weed in waste grounds, especially along roadsides and hedgerows.
a common European annual grass weed

Examples

Wall barley thrives in urban areas, frequently appearing along pavements and in abandoned lots.
The spread of wall barley can pose challenges for gardeners trying to manage weeds.
During spring, wall barley produces distinctive bristly flower spikes that are easily recognizable.
