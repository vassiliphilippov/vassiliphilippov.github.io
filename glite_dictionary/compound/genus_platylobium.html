genus Platylobium

1. NOUN

/ ˌplætɪˈloʊbiəm / (AmE), / ˌplatɪˈləʊbiəm / (BrE)
a small genus of Australian evergreen leguminous shrubs or subshrubs
Australian genus of evergreen leguminous shrubs

Examples

The genus Platylobium includes several species native to southeastern Australia.
Botanists are studying the ecological role of the genus Platylobium in forest understories.
The pea-like flowers of the genus Platylobium make it distinctive among Australian flora.

Etymology

From Greek platys (broad) + lobos (pod, capsule), referring to the broad pods produced by plants of this genus.
