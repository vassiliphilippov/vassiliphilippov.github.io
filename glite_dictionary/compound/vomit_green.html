vomit green

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɑːmɪt ɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈvɒmɪt ɡriːn / (BrE)
an informal term for a very bright, unpleasant shade of green resembling the color of vomit
an unpleasant, bright green color
informal

Examples

He painted the walls a hideous shade of vomit green that everyone disliked.
The new uniforms were such a bright vomit green that they hurt my eyes.
No one wanted the vomit green sweater left at the sale.

Synset Examples
