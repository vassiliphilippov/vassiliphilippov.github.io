temporary gentleman

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛmpəˌrɛri ˈdʒɛntəlmən / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərəri ˈdʒɛntl̩mən / (BrE)
A man who displays courteous, gentlemanly behavior for a short period, often charming others but lacking genuine seriousness or intent.
man briefly acting as a gentleman

Examples

At the charity auction, he appeared to be a temporary gentleman, impressing guests with his manners.
Once the evening ended, the temporary gentleman reverted to his usual, less polished self.
She quickly recognized him as a temporary gentleman who would not stay around for long.

Synset Examples

He played the role of a temporary gentleman for the gala.
The temporary gentleman charmed everyone at the party.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gentleman - a man of good manners, courtesy, or high social status
