Spanish-speaking

1. ADJ

/ ˈspænɪʃˌspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspænɪʃˌspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
able to communicate in Spanish; using the Spanish language in speech or writing.
able to speak Spanish

Examples

The city has a large Spanish-speaking population.
She joined a group for Spanish-speaking parents.
Several Spanish-speaking countries are represented at the conference.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: English-speaking - able to communicate in English
  • Synonym: French-speaking - able to communicate in French
