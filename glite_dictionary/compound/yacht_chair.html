yacht chair

1. NOUN

/ jɑt tʃɛr / (AmE), / jɒt tʃɛː / (BrE)
a light, folding armchair designed for outdoor use, typically on the deck of a yacht or in similar settings
light folding outdoor armchair

Examples

She unfolded the yacht chair and settled in to watch the sunset from the boat.
We stored the yacht chairs below deck to keep them dry during the storm.
A classic yacht chair adds both style and comfort to any patio.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deck chair - a folding chair used outdoors, especially on a ship's deck or at the beach
