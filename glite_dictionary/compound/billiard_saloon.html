billiard saloon

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪljɚd səˈlun / (AmE), / ˈbɪlɪəd səˈluːn / (BrE)
a room or establishment where billiards is played, typically providing tables and cues for customers
room or venue for playing billiards

Examples

We met at the billiard saloon to play a few games after work.
The old billiard saloon downtown was famous for its vintage tables and lively atmosphere.
He opened a small billiard saloon hoping to attract local enthusiasts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: billiard hall - a public room or building where people play billiards
