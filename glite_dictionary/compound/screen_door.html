screen door

1. NOUN

/ skrin dɔr / (AmE), / skriːn dɔː / (BrE)
A door made from a frame fitted with wire or plastic mesh, designed to allow air to flow through while keeping insects out.
door with mesh to keep out insects

Examples

Please make sure the screen door is closed so that bugs don't get in.
She replaced the old screen door with a new one to improve ventilation.
As I stepped onto the porch, the screen door creaked behind me.

Synset Examples

he heard the screen door slam as she left

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: storm door - an additional outer door for protection against bad weather
