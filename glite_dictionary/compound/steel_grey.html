steel grey

1. NOUN

/ stil ɡreɪ / (AmE), / stiːl ɡreɪ / (BrE)
a slightly purplish or bluish dark grey colour, resembling that of polished steel
a bluish or purplish dark grey colour

Examples

The car was painted a stunning shade of steel grey that shimmered in the sunlight.
She chose steel grey curtains to give her living room a modern look.
His eyes were an unusual steel grey, cold and piercing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gray - a color between black and white; neutral color
  • Degree Lower: charcoal - a dark grey color
  • Synonym: slate - a bluish-grey color
