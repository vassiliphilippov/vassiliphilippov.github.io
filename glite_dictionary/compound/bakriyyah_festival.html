Bakriyyah festival

1. PROPN

/ bɑːˈkriː.jə ˈfɛs.tɪ.vəl / (AmE), / bɑːˈkriː.jə ˈfɛs.tɪ.vəl / (BrE)
a local cultural celebration or festival observed in some communities, often involving traditional customs and events
local cultural festival

Examples

The city comes alive each year during the Bakriyyah festival.
We attended traditional dances at the Bakriyyah festival last summer.
Many tourists visit the region to experience the unique customs of the Bakriyyah festival.
