cedar chest

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːdɚ tʃɛst / (AmE), / ˈsiːdə tʃɛst / (BrE)
a wooden chest made of cedar, typically used for storing clothes and linens to protect them from moths
wooden storage chest made of cedar

Examples

Her grandmother kept old quilts in a cedar chest at the foot of the bed.
The aroma of the cedar chest filled the entire room.
A cedar chest can help protect delicate fabrics from moth damage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hope chest - a chest in which a young woman collected items in preparation for marriage
  • Synonym: chest - a large strong box, typically used for storage
