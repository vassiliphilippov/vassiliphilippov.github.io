idol worshiper

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪdəl ˈwɜrʃɪpər / (AmE), / ˈaɪdəl ˈwɜːʃɪpə / (BrE)
a person who practices the worship of idols as objects of reverence or devotion
person who worships idols

Examples

Some ancient societies labeled anyone outside their faith as an idol worshiper.
The preacher condemned practices he saw as the acts of an idol worshiper.
Accusations of being an idol worshiper often led to social exclusion or persecution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idolater - person who worships idols
