out-tray

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtˌtreɪ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌtreɪ / (BrE)
a tray on a desk for holding documents or papers that are to be sent out
tray for outgoing papers

Examples

Please put all completed forms in the out-tray on my desk before you leave.
Her out-tray was overflowing with letters waiting to be mailed.
He checked his out-tray every hour to make sure nothing was missed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in-tray - a tray for incoming papers

Etymology

Formed from 'out' (indicating outward movement) and 'tray'.
