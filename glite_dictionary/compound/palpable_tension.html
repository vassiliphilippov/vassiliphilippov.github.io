palpable tension

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæl.pə.bəl ˈtɛn.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈpal.pə.bəl ˈtɛn.ʃən / (BrE)
a strong feeling of tension that is so intense it seems almost tangible or easily sensed
a very strong, easily felt tension

Examples

As the verdict was announced, a palpable tension filled the entire courtroom.
There was a palpable tension between the two candidates during the debate.
Everyone in the office could sense the palpable tension after the heated meeting.

Synset Examples

His unmeltingly stare made the tension in the room palpable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: palpable - able to be touched or felt; easily perceived
  • Synonym: tension - mental or emotional strain; a strained state
