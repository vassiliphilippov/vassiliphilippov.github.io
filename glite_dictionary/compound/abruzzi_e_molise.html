Abruzzi e Molise

1. PROPN

/ ɑːˌbrut.si eɪ moʊˈliːseɪ / (AmE), / aːˌbrʊt.tsi ɛ mɒˈliːzeɪ / (BrE)
a historical mountainous region of central Italy, bordering the Adriatic Sea, now divided into the regions of Abruzzo and Molise.
historical region in central Italy

Examples

The ancient traditions of Abruzzi e Molise are still celebrated today in many villages.
She spent her summer exploring the mountainous terrain of Abruzzi e Molise.
Many culinary specialties from Abruzzi e Molise are renowned throughout Italy.

Etymology

From Italian 'Abruzzi' (plural of Abruzzo) and 'Molise', the names of the regions.
