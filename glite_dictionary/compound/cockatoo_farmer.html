cockatoo farmer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːkəˌtu ˈfɑrmər / (AmE), / ˈkɒkəˌtuː ˈfɑːmə / (BrE)
a person who raises or breeds cockatoos, especially for commercial purposes
person who raises cockatoos

Examples

The cockatoo farmer started his business with just two birds and now has hundreds.
As a dedicated cockatoo farmer, she learns a lot about avian care and breeding.
Many people are surprised at the amount of work involved in being a cockatoo farmer.

Synset Examples

The cockatoo farmer had the loudest birds in the neighborhood.
He fancied himself a cockatoo farmer with all those parrots.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breeder - a person who breeds animals as a profession or hobby
