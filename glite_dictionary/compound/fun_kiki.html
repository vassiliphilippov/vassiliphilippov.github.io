fun kiki

1. NOUN

/ fʌn ˈkiːki / (AmE), / fʌn ˈkiːki / (BrE)
A lively, informal gathering or party, especially popular in LGBTQ+ communities.
lively informal party
slang

We had a fun kiki last night with friends from work.

Everyone was invited to the fun kiki at Jay's apartment.

Etymology

'Kiki' believed to originate from American LGBTQ+ slang for a social gathering; 'fun' added for emphasis.
