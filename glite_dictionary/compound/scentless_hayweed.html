scentless hayweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛntləs ˈheɪˌwid / (AmE), / ˈsɛntləs ˈheɪˌwiːd / (BrE)
A European annual weed with finely divided leaves and white flowers, found widely in North America; sometimes classified in the genus Matricaria.
annual European weed with white flowers

Examples

The field was overrun with scentless hayweed after the spring rains.
Gardeners often remove scentless hayweed to prevent it from spreading among crops.
In eastern North America, scentless hayweed is sometimes mistakenly cultivated for its delicate appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mayweed - Annual plant with similar daisy-like flowers, also called scentless mayweed
