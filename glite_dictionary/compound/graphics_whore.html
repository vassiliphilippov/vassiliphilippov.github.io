graphics whore

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡræfɪks hɔɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡræfɪks hɔː / (BrE)
A person who is excessively obsessed with high-quality graphics, especially in video games or design, often prioritizing visual aspects over gameplay or other features.
someone obsessed with graphics quality
slang

Examples

As a graphics whore, I'm always upgrading my computer to get better visuals.
Some critics call him a graphics whore for valuing appearance over gameplay.
The new console attracted many graphics whores with its stunning visuals.

Synset Examples

As a graphics whore, she only plays the best-looking games.
Being a graphics whore means I need a powerful computer.
He’s become a graphics whore, chasing the latest tech.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graphics junkie - a person with a strong obsession for graphics quality
