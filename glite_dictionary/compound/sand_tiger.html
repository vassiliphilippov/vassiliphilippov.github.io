sand tiger

1. NOUN

/ sænd ˈtaɪɡɚ / (AmE), / sænd ˈtaɪɡə / (BrE)
a large, shallow-water shark with sharp, jagged teeth, found in the Atlantic Ocean and sometimes considered dangerous to swimmers
shallow-water shark sometimes dangerous to swimmers

Examples

The sand tiger is known for its menacing appearance but rarely attacks humans without provocation.
We spotted a sand tiger gliding gracefully through the shallow coastal waters.
Marine biologists study the habits of the sand tiger to better understand its role in the ecosystem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sand tiger shark - another name for the sand tiger shark species
  • Synonym: grey nurse shark - alternative name used especially in Australia for the same species
