grass pea

1. NOUN

/ ɡræs pi / (AmE), / ɡrɑːs piː / (BrE)
An annual European plant (Lathyrus sativus) grown for forage, with seeds used as food in India and as animal feed elsewhere.
annual plant grown for forage or food

Examples

The grass pea is an important crop in regions prone to drought.
Farmers feed their livestock with grass pea fodder during the dry season.
Some traditional Indian dishes are made using grass pea seeds.
