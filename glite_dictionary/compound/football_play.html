football play

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌbɔl pleɪ / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌbɔːl pleɪ / (BrE)
An action or planned maneuver executed by the offensive team in American football, designed to advance the ball, score points, or achieve specific objectives.
action by offensive team in American football
american

Examples

The coach called a football play that relied on quick passing to the wide receivers.
Each football play must be carefully coordinated to catch the defense off guard.
They practiced the new football play repeatedly before using it during the big game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: play - a single action or maneuver in a sport or game
  • Antonym: defensive play - a play executed by the defensive team in American football
