sucktastic attitude

1. NOUN

/ sʌkˈtæstɪk ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / sʌkˈtastɪk ˈatɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
A very poor or annoying attitude, often causing frustration or annoyance in others.
extremely poor or irritating attitude
slang informal

Examples

Nobody wanted to work with her because of her sucktastic attitude.
Your sucktastic attitude is making this group project even harder for everyone.
He shrugged off criticism about his sucktastic attitude during meetings.

Synset Examples

His sucktastic attitude drove everyone away.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad attitude - unpleasant or negative attitude
  • Antonym: positive attitude - optimistic or helpful way of thinking

Etymology

Blend of 'suck' (slang: to be bad) and 'fantastic', with 'attitude'.
