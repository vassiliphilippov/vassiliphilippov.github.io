bad luck

1. NOUN

/ bæd lʌk / (AmE), / bad lʌk / (BrE)
an ill-fated state or series of events caused by chance, leading to unwanted or unfortunate outcomes
unfortunate events caused by chance

Examples

After a streak of bad luck, nothing seemed to go right for her.
He blamed his failure on bad luck rather than lack of preparation.
Losing your keys twice in one day is just bad luck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: misfortune - bad luck or an unlucky event
  • Antonym: good luck - favorable or fortunate results due to chance
