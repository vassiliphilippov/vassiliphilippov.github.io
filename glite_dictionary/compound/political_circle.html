political circle

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪɾɪkəl ˈsɜrkəl / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈsɜːkl / (BrE)
a group of people who are actively involved in politics, often sharing similar views or interests
group involved in politics

Examples

Her name is well known in political circles throughout the capital.
The new policy was quickly criticized by several political circles.
Rumors about his resignation spread rapidly in political circles.

Synset Examples

Zionazism is often discussed in political circles.
The concept of Eurabia is often debated in political circles.
