Australian Alps

1. PROPN

/ əˌstreɪliən ˈælps / (AmE), / əˌstreɪliən ˈælps / (BrE)
a range of mountains in southeastern Australia forming the southern end of the Great Dividing Range
mountain range in southeastern Australia

Examples

The Australian Alps are famous for their winter sports and beautiful scenery.
Many tourists visit the Australian Alps during the annual wildflower season.
Snow can be found in the Australian Alps even in the warmer months.
