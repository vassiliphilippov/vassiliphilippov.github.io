heavy spar

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛvi spɑr / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi spɑː / (BrE)
A white or colorless mineral, also known as barite, consisting of barium sulfate (BaSO4); the main source of barium.
a mineral; barite (barium sulfate)

Examples

The geologist identified the white crystals as heavy spar during the field survey.
Large deposits of heavy spar are often mined for their barium content.
A sample of heavy spar was sent to the laboratory for analysis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barite - A mineral consisting of barium sulfate (BaSO4), used as the main source of barium
