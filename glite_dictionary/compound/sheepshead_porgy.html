sheepshead porgy

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃipsˌhɛd ˈpɔrɡi / (AmE), / ˈʃiːpsˌhɛd ˈpɔːɡi / (BrE)
a tropical Atlantic fish, Archosargus probatocephalus, found from Florida and the Bahamas to Brazil, known for its toothy mouth and vertical stripes
a tropical Atlantic fish with striped body

Examples

Fishermen often catch sheepshead porgy along the rocky shores of Florida.
The sheepshead porgy is easily identified by its tooth-like incisors and bold stripes.
Local restaurants served grilled sheepshead porgy as a specialty during the summer.
