microprocessor chip

1. NOUN

/ ˌmaɪ.kɹoʊ.prəˈsɛs.ɚ tʃɪp / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.krəʊ.prəˈses.ə tʃɪp / (BrE)
an integrated circuit consisting of a small silicon crystal, designed to perform multiple processing functions in electronic devices
integrated circuit for processing electronic functions

Examples

The new phone model uses a faster microprocessor chip to improve performance.
Engineers tested the microprocessor chip for reliability before the product launch.
She studied how a microprocessor chip controls all major functions inside a modern computer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microprocessor - an integrated circuit that contains the functions of a central processing unit

Etymology

From 'microprocessor' (micro- + processor) and 'chip' (from Middle English chipp, a small piece).
