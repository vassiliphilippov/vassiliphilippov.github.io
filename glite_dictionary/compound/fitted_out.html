fitted out

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪtɪd aʊt / (AmE), / ˈfɪtɪd aʊt / (BrE)
equipped with all the necessary items or equipment for a particular purpose or activity
equipped with needed items

Examples

The laboratory was fitted out with the latest scientific instruments.
Their new camper van is fitted out for long journeys across the country.
The ship was fitted out with advanced navigation and communication systems.

Synset Examples

a well outfitted expedition to the South Pole

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outfitted - equipped with necessary items
