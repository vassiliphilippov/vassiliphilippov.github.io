African nation

1. NOUN

/ ˈæfɹɪkən ˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈafɹɪkən ˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
any country located on the African continent.
country on the African continent

Examples

South Africa is an African nation known for its diversity and history.
Many African nations have rapidly growing economies in recent years.
The climate varies greatly from one African nation to another.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: African country - a country in Africa
  • Antonym: European nation - country on the European continent
