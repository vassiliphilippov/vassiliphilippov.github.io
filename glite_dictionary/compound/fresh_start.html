fresh start

1. NOUN

/ frɛʃ stɑrt / (AmE), / frɛʃ stɑːt / (BrE)
an opportunity to begin something again without the influence of past problems or mistakes
a new or renewed beginning

Examples

After the company went bankrupt, she saw it as a fresh start in her career.
Moving to a new city gave them a fresh start away from their past troubles.
He wanted a fresh start after the end of his difficult relationship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: new beginning - a situation or opportunity marking the start of something new
  • Synonym: clean slate - an opportunity to start anew, free from previous mistakes
