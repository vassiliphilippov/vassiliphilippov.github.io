milk tooth

1. NOUN

/ mɪlk tuθ / (AmE), / mɪlk tuːθ / (BrE)
a temporary tooth in young mammals, especially one of the first set that appears in children and is later replaced by a permanent tooth
a child's temporary tooth

Examples

When a child loses a milk tooth, it is often placed under the pillow for the tooth fairy.
My daughter was excited when her first milk tooth became wobbly and finally fell out.
Dentists recommend taking care of every milk tooth even though they are temporary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deciduous tooth - a temporary tooth that will be replaced by a permanent one
  • Antonym: permanent tooth - an adult tooth that replaces a milk tooth
