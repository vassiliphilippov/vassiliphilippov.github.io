little person

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬l ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
A person of unusually short stature, especially someone with dwarfism; sometimes used figuratively to refer to a marginalized or powerless individual.
a person of unusually short stature (esp. with dwarfism)

Examples

The little person performed wonderfully at the talent show.
She advocates for the rights of every little person in her community.
In the story, the hero stands up for the little person against injustice.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dwarf - a person with unusually short stature due to a genetic or medical condition
