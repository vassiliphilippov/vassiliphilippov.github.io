video recording

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪdioʊ rɪˈkɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɪdiəʊ rɪˈkɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
A recording that captures both visual images and sound, especially of a movie or television program.
recording of visual images and sound

Examples

The security cameras saved a video recording of the incident for later review.
He made a video recording of his daughter's first steps.
We watched a video recording of the concert last night.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: audio recording - recording of sound only

Etymology

Formed from 'video' (from Latin 'videre', to see) and 'recording'.
