polo ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊloʊ bɔl / (AmE), / ˈpəʊləʊ bɔːl / (BrE)
a small wooden ball used in the game of polo, struck with mallets during play
wooden ball used in polo

Examples

The player expertly aimed his mallet at the polo ball and sent it flying down the field.
During the match, a cracked polo ball had to be quickly replaced.
Spectators watched as the polo ball rolled just inches from the goal line.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polo - an outdoor team sport played on horseback with mallets and a ball
