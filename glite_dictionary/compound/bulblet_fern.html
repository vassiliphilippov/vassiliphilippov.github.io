bulblet fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌlb.lət fɝn / (AmE), / ˈbʌlb.lət fɜːn / (BrE)
A North American fern, especially Cystopteris bulbifera, often producing small bulbils on its leaflets.
a North American fern with bulbils

Examples

The bulblet fern is commonly found growing along limestone outcrops in the Midwest.
Several small bulbils developed on the fronds of the bulblet fern in our garden.
Botanists examined the unique reproductive strategy of the bulblet fern during their field study.
