lemon-wood

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛmən wʊd / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən wʊd / (BrE)
A South African evergreen tree with hard, tough wood, often used in carpentry and woodworking.
South African evergreen tree with hard wood

Examples

The dense timber of the lemon-wood is prized for making durable furniture.
Carpenters in South Africa often use lemon-wood for its strength and resilience.
The botanist identified the tree as a lemon-wood by examining its tough, evergreen leaves.
