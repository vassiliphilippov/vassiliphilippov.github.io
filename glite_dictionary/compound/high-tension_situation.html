high-tension situation

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈtɛnʃən sɪtʃuˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / haɪ ˈtɛnʃən sɪtjʊˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a situation marked by significant stress, pressure, or anxiety, often requiring careful management
stressful or pressured situation

Examples

Negotiators struggled to reach a compromise in the high-tension situation after the dispute escalated.
During a high-tension situation, it's important to stay calm and think rationally.
The film's climax unfolds in a high-tension situation that keeps viewers on the edge of their seats.

Synset Examples

The high-tension situation required careful handling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tense situation - a situation characterized by anxiety or nervousness
