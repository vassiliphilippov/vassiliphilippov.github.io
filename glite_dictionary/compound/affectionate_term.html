affectionate term

1. NOUN

/ əˈfɛk.ʃə.nət tɜrm / (AmE), / əˈfɛk.ʃ(ə)nət tɜːm / (BrE)
a word or expression used to show fondness, love, or tenderness toward someone
word showing fondness or love

Examples

He always calls his daughter by an affectionate term like 'sweetheart' or 'darling'.
'Buddy' is an affectionate term some people use for close friends.
In some cultures, using an affectionate term for elders is a sign of respect.

Synset Examples

Zio is an affectionate term for my uncle in Italian.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nickname - a familiar or humorous name given to a person instead of or as well as the real name

Etymology

From 'affectionate' (from Latin 'affectionem', influence or feeling) + 'term' (from Latin 'terminus', boundary or limit).
