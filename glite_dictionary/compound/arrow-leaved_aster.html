arrow-leaved aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈæroʊ ˌlivd ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈarəʊ ˌliːvd ˈæstə / (BrE)
a North American aster with arrow-shaped leaves, belonging to the species Symphyotrichum sagittifolium (formerly Aster sagittifolius)
North American aster with arrow-shaped leaves

Examples

The arrow-leaved aster blooms in late summer, adding a splash of purple to the meadow.
We found several arrow-leaved asters growing along the forest path.
Gardeners appreciate the hardy nature of the arrow-leaved aster in native plant landscapes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a genus of flowering plants known for their star-shaped flower heads
