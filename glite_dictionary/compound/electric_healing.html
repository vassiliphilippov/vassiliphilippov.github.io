electric healing

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈhiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈhiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the therapeutic use of electricity applied to the body, especially for treating certain medical conditions such as paralysis
therapeutic use of electricity on the body
medical

Examples

Some clinics offer electric healing as part of their rehabilitation programs for patients with nerve damage.
The doctor recommended electric healing to stimulate muscle activity in the affected area.
Research on electric healing has shown promising results in treating certain forms of paralysis.
