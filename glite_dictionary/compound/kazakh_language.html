Kazakh language

1. NOUN

/ kəˈzɑk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / kəˈzɑːk ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken by the Kazakh people, primarily in Kazakhstan and surrounding regions
Turkic language of Kazakhstan

Examples

She is fluent in both Russian and Kazakh language.
The Kazakh language has undergone significant reforms since Kazakhstan gained independence.
He studied the Kazakh language at university to better understand Central Asian culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kazakh - a person of Kazakh ethnicity or relating to Kazakhstan

Etymology

From Russian 'kazakhskiy' + English 'language'; ultimately from Turkic 'Kazakh' (name of the ethnic group).
