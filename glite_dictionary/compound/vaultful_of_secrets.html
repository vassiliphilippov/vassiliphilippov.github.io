vaultful of secrets

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɔlt.fʊl əv ˈsi.krəts / (AmE), / ˈvɔːlt.fʊl əv ˈsiː.krəts / (BrE)
A very large number of hidden or closely guarded secrets.
a large number of hidden secrets

Examples

She guarded her vaultful of secrets for years, never telling a soul about her past.
The political party seemed to have a vaultful of secrets ready to burst open after the scandal.
Each diary entry hinted at a vaultful of secrets hidden deep within his mind.

Synset Examples

He had a vaultful of secrets he kept from everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secret - something kept hidden or unknown
