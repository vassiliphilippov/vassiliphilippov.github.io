the street

1. NOUN

/ ðə strit / (AmE), / ðə striːt / (BrE)
the urban environment, especially as characterized by a particular lifestyle, culture, or community; the area of a city where ordinary people live and work
urban environment or neighborhood

Examples

He learned important lessons from his time on the street.
Her art draws inspiration from the street and urban culture.
Many musicians say their sound comes directly from the street.

Synset Examples

He grew up in the streets behind the main square.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: street life - life and activities taking place on the street, often associated with urban culture
