the Street

1. NOUN

/ ðə strit / (AmE), / ðə striːt / (BrE)
The securities industry of the United States, especially Wall Street, collectively or as a financial community.
the US securities industry (esp. Wall Street)

Examples

Rumors spread quickly across the Street, influencing trading patterns throughout the day.
Many analysts on the Street predict a strong earnings season for tech companies.
When regulators announce new policies, the Street pays very close attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Wall Street - The financial district of New York City; by extension, the US financial markets or industry as a whole

Etymology

Metonymical use of 'Wall Street', the historic location of the New York Stock Exchange and financial institutions.
