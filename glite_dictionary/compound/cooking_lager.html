cooking lager

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊkɪŋ ˌlæɡər / (AmE), / ˈkʊkɪŋ ˌlɑːɡə / (BrE)
a beer that is drunk while preparing food, typically regarded as a casual or everyday beverage rather than a premium one
beer drunk while cooking
informal british

Examples

While making dinner, he always cracked open a cooking lager to relax.
She offered her guests a cooking lager as they prepared the barbecue together.
Many chefs enjoy a cold cooking lager while trying out new recipes.

Synset Examples

He enjoyed a cooking lager while preparing dinner.
