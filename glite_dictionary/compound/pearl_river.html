Pearl River

1. PROPN

/ pɜrl ˈrɪvɚ / (AmE), / pɜːl ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in the southern United States, flowing through Mississippi and Louisiana to the Gulf of Mexico
river in Mississippi and Louisiana, USA

Examples

The Pearl River serves as a natural boundary between parts of Mississippi and Louisiana.
Many communities rely on the Pearl River for their water supply.
Tourists enjoy kayaking and fishing along the scenic Pearl River.

2. PROPN

/ pɜrl ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / pɜːl ˈrɪvə / (BrE)

river in southeast China

Examples

The Pearl River is an important waterway for trade and transportation in southern China.
Many factories are located along the banks of the Pearl River.
The city of Guangzhou lies on the Pearl River delta.
