coin slot

1. NOUN

/ kɔɪn slɑt / (AmE), / kɔɪn slɒt / (BrE)
a narrow opening in a machine, such as a vending machine or payphone, through which coins are inserted
narrow opening for inserting coins in a machine

Examples

She pushed the coin into the coin slot to start the washing machine.
The vending machine's coin slot was jammed and wouldn't accept any money.
Make sure you put the right change into the coin slot to get your ticket.
