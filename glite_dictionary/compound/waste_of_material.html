waste of material

1. NOUN

/ weɪst əv məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / weɪst əv məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
the unnecessary or inefficient use or disposal of materials, resulting in loss or lack of productivity
inefficient use or loss of materials

Examples

The factory's outdated machines led to a significant waste of material during production.
Reducing waste of material can lower costs and benefit the environment.
The architect redesigned the process to minimize waste of material on the construction site.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wastage - the amount or process of wasting, especially materials
  • Antonym: conservation - careful preservation and protection of resources
