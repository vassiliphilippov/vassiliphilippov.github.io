jazz group

1. NOUN

/ dʒæz ɡruːp / (AmE), / dʒæz ɡruːp / (BrE)
a small band of musicians who play jazz music together
small band of jazz musicians

Examples

The jazz group performed a lively set at the local club.
She joined a jazz group to improve her improvisational skills.
Many famous musicians got their start in a small jazz group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jazz band - a musical group that performs jazz, often larger than a jazz group
