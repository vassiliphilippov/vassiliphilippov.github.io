lamb curry

1. NOUN

/ læm ˈkɜːri / (AmE), / læm ˈkʌri / (BrE)
A dish consisting of lamb cooked in a spiced sauce, typically in the style of Indian or South Asian cuisine.
dish of lamb stewed in curry sauce

Examples

She ordered the lamb curry with extra naan bread at the restaurant.
The aroma of lamb curry filled the entire house as it simmered on the stove.
For tonight's dinner, I'm planning to make a spicy lamb curry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken curry - a dish of chicken cooked in curry sauce
