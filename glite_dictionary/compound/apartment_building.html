apartment building

1. NOUN

/ əˈpɑːrtmənt ˈbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈpɑːtmənt ˈbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
A large building divided into separate apartments for people to live in.
building divided into apartments

Examples

They just moved into a new apartment building in the city center.
The apartment building has a security guard at the entrance.
Several families were evacuated from the apartment building after the fire alarm went off.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apartment block - a British term for a building divided into apartments
