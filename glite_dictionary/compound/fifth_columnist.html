fifth columnist

1. NOUN

/ fɪfθ ˈkɑːləmnɪst / (AmE), / fɪfθ ˈkɒləmnɪst / (BrE)
a person who secretly supports and tries to assist an enemy, especially by working within a country under attack or threat
someone secretly aiding an enemy

Examples

The authorities arrested a suspected fifth columnist who was passing military secrets to the enemy.
During wartime, governments often fear that fifth columnists may undermine their defenses from within.
He was accused of being a fifth columnist after he was discovered sabotaging critical infrastructure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traitor - a person who betrays their country
  • Antonym: patriot - a person who supports their country and is prepared to defend it

Etymology

Coined during the Spanish Civil War, allegedly after nationalist general Emilio Mola referred to his supporters within Madrid as his 'fifth column.'
