moon ray

1. NOUN

/ ˈmun ˌreɪ / (AmE), / ˈmuːn ˌreɪ / (BrE)
a ray of light coming from the moon; a beam of moonlight
a ray of light from the moon

Examples

A single moon ray slipped through the window, casting a silver line across the floor.
She watched the moon ray dance on the surface of the lake.
A pale moon ray illuminated the quiet garden at midnight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moonbeam - a beam of light from the moon
