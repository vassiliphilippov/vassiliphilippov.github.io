famous person

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪməs ˈpɜrsən / (AmE), / ˈfeɪməs ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is widely known or recognized by the public, often due to their achievements or prominence in a particular field
person widely known by the public

Examples

She became a famous person after publishing her bestselling novel.
Meeting a famous person can be an exciting experience for many fans.
Despite being a famous person, he always stayed humble and approachable.

Synset Examples

he was a baseball celebrity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celebrity - a person who is widely recognized in society, especially in entertainment
  • Antonym: unknown - a person who is not known to the public
