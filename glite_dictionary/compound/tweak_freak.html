tweak freak

1. NOUN

/ twiːk friːk / (AmE), / twiːk friːk / (BrE)
A person who is unusually obsessed with making minor adjustments or improvements to something.
person obsessed with minor adjustments
informal

Examples

My boss is such a tweak freak that meetings often run longer than necessary.
As a tweak freak, Anna spends hours perfecting her presentations.
If you're not a tweak freak, working with one can sometimes be exhausting.

Synset Examples

As a tweak freak, he constantly adjusts his projects.
Being a tweak freak can slow down progress sometimes.
She’s a tweak freak, always refining her work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfectionist - person striving for flawlessness
