restaurant attendant

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛst(ə)ˌrɑnt əˈtɛndənt / (AmE), / ˈrɛstəˌrɒnt əˈtɛndənt / (BrE)
a person employed to serve guests and provide service in a restaurant's dining room
person who serves guests in a restaurant

Examples

The restaurant attendant greeted us with a smile and showed us to our table.
If you need anything, just ask the restaurant attendant for assistance.
During her summer job, Maria worked as a restaurant attendant at a busy seaside cafe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waiter - man who serves food in a restaurant
  • Synonym: waitress - woman who serves food in a restaurant
