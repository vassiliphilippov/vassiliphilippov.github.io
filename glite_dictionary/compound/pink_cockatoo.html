pink cockatoo

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋk kəˈkɑːtu / (AmE), / pɪŋk kəˈkætuː / (BrE)
an Australian cockatoo with predominantly white feathers and a distinctive pink or rose-colored tinge, especially on the crest and face
Australian cockatoo with pink-tinged plumage

Examples

The pink cockatoo is admired for its vibrant crest and gentle appearance.
During our trip to Australia, we saw a flock of pink cockatoos near the riverbank.
The zoo has a special enclosure for the rare pink cockatoo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Major Mitchell's cockatoo - another name for the pink cockatoo
