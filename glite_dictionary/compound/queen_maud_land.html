Queen Maud Land

1. PROPN

/ kwiːn mɔd lænd / (AmE), / kwiːn mɔːd lænd / (BrE)
a region of Antarctica, situated between Enderby Land and the Weddell Sea, claimed by Norway
region of Antarctica claimed by Norway

Examples

The Norwegian research team set up a base camp in Queen Maud Land during their expedition.
Satellite images revealed several new ice formations in Queen Maud Land this winter.
Much of Queen Maud Land is covered by thick ice sheets, making exploration challenging.

Etymology

Named after Queen Maud of Norway (1869–1938).
