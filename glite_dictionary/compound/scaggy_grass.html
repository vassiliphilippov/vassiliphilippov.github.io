scaggy grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈskæɡi ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈskæɡi ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
Grass that is untidy, unkempt, or scraggly in appearance.
untidy or unkempt grass

Examples

She glanced out at the scaggy grass invading the edges of the driveway.
After weeks of rain, the lawn was little more than scaggy grass and weeds.
It's hard to believe that such scaggy grass once made up a beautiful lawn.

Synset Examples

The scaggy grass in the yard was a sign of neglect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scraggly - thin, untidy, or shabby in appearance
  • Antonym: neat grass - grass that is well-kept and tidy
