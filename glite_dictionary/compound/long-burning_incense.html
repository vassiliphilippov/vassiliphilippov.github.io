long-burning incense

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋ ˈbɝnɪŋ ˈɪnsɛns / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈbɜːnɪŋ ˈɪnsens / (BrE)
Incense that is designed to burn for an extended period of time.
incense that burns for a long time

Examples

The long-burning incense filled the room with a calming aroma for several hours.
She prefers long-burning incense because it lasts throughout her meditation sessions.
This shop sells a wide variety of long-burning incense sticks in different scents.

Synset Examples

I bought a long-burning incense stick for relaxation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incense - a substance that is burned for its fragrant smoke
