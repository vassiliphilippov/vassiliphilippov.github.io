simple pleasure

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪmpəl ˈplɛʒər / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpəl ˈplɛʒə / (BrE)
A small, uncomplicated joy or satisfaction in life, often found in everyday activities or experiences.
basic, uncomplicated joy or satisfaction

Examples

She cherished the simple pleasures of reading a book by the fire.
A walk in the park is one of life's simple pleasures.
He always appreciated the simple pleasure of a hot cup of tea on a rainy day.

Synset Examples

The country mouse enjoyed the simple pleasures of rural life.
She found joy in the simple pleasures of life.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: delight - great pleasure or joy
