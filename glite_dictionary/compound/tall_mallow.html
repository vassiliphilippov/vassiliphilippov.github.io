tall mallow

1. NOUN

/ tɔl ˈmæloʊ / (AmE), / tɔːl ˈmæləʊ / (BrE)
a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Old World, with erect or spreading stems and clusters of rosy-purple flowers, now also found in the United States
a perennial plant with rosy-purple flowers

Examples

The tall mallow blooms each summer, brightening the garden with its rosy-purple flowers.
Many gardeners admire the resilience of the tall mallow in various soil types.
We spotted a cluster of tall mallows growing wild along the country road.
