house painter

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊs ˌpeɪntər / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs ˌpeɪntə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to paint the interior or exterior of houses and similar buildings
person who paints houses

Examples

The house painter finished the exterior in just three days.
We hired a house painter to give our living room a fresh look.
As a house painter, he takes pride in his meticulous work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: painter - a person whose job is painting buildings, walls, etc.
