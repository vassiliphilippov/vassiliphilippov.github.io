salary increase

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæləri ɪnˌkris / (AmE), / ˈsæləri ɪnˌkriːs / (BrE)
an increase in the amount of money paid to someone as a salary
an increase in salary

Examples

She was thrilled when her manager confirmed her salary increase after the annual review.
The union negotiated a substantial salary increase for all staff members.
Many employees hope for a salary increase to keep up with the rising cost of living.

Synset Examples

he got a 3% raise
he got a wage hike

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raise - an increase in an employee's pay
  • Antonym: pay cut - a reduction in salary or wages
