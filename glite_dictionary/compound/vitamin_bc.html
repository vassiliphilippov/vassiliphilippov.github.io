vitamin Bc

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪtəmɪn ˌbiː ˈsiː / (AmE), / ˈvɪtəmɪn ˌbiː ˈsiː / (BrE)
another name for folic acid, a B vitamin essential for cell growth and reproduction, especially important during pregnancy
another name for folic acid (a B vitamin)
medical

Examples

Doctors recommend vitamin Bc supplements for women planning pregnancy to help prevent birth defects.
A deficiency in vitamin Bc can lead to anemia and problems in cell division.
Leafy green vegetables are a rich natural source of vitamin Bc.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: folic acid - a form of vitamin B9 essential for cell growth and metabolism
  • Synonym: vitamin B9 - another term for folic acid, a water-soluble B vitamin
