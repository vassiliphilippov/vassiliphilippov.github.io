fashion industry

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃən ˌɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˈfæʃən ˌɪndəstri / (BrE)
the business sector involved in designing, making, marketing, and selling fashionable clothing and accessories
business of making and selling fashionable clothing

Examples

She dreams of working in the fashion industry as a designer one day.
The fashion industry is constantly evolving with new trends each season.
Sustainability has become a major focus in the modern fashion industry worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garment industry - industry related to the manufacture of clothing
