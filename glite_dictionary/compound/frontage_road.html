frontage road

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrʌntɪdʒ roʊd / (AmE), / ˈfrʌntɪdʒ rəʊd / (BrE)
A local road that runs parallel to a main road or expressway, providing access to properties and local traffic.
road running parallel to a main road

Examples

We exited the highway and took the frontage road to reach the shopping center.
Many businesses are located along the frontage road beside the expressway.
Construction on the frontage road caused delays for local drivers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: service road - A road parallel to a main road for local traffic access

Etymology

From 'frontage' (land bordering a road) + 'road'.
