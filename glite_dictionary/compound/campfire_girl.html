Campfire Girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæmpˌfaɪər ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈkæmpˌfaɪə ɡɜːl / (BrE)
A girl who is a member of the Camp Fire Girls, an American youth organization for girls aged 7 to 18.
girl in the Camp Fire Girls youth group

Examples

Her daughter became a Campfire Girl at the age of eight.
The Campfire Girls organized a community cleanup event last Saturday.
As a Campfire Girl, she learned valuable leadership and outdoor skills.

Etymology

From 'Camp Fire Girls,' an American youth organization founded in 1910.
