baby oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.bi ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi ɔɪl / (BrE)
a gentle, moisturizing oil, typically formulated for use on babies' skin
skin moisturizer for babies

Examples

She gently massaged baby oil onto her newborn's skin after bath time.
Baby oil can help keep a baby's skin soft and smooth.
The label says this baby oil is fragrance-free and hypoallergenic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ointment - a smooth substance for application to the skin for medicinal or cosmetic purposes
