staunch Republican

1. NOUN

/ stɑntʃ rɪˈpʌblɪkən / (AmE), / stɔːntʃ rɪˈpʌblɪkən / (BrE)
a strong and loyal supporter of the Republican Party
loyal supporter of the Republican Party

Examples

Despite the scandals, he remained a staunch Republican throughout his life.
Her family has been staunch Republicans for several generations.
As a staunch Republican, she supports conservative policies and candidates.

Synset Examples

He's known in the office as a staunch Republican.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: staunch Democrat - strong and loyal supporter of the Democratic Party
