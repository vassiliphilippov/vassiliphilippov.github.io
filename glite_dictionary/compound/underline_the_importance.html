to underline the importance

1. VERB

/ ˌʌndərˌlaɪn ði ɪmˈpɔrtəns / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˌlaɪn ði ɪmˈpɔːtəns / (BrE)
to emphasize how significant something is; to draw attention to its importance
emphasize the significance of something

Examples

The CEO made sure to underline the importance of clear communication in his address.
Teachers often underline the importance of regular practice to their students.
She underlined the importance of healthy eating during her presentation.

Synset Examples

He chose to underline the importance of teamwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to emphasize - give special importance or prominence to something
