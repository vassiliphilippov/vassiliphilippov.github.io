fem movement

1. NOUN

/ fɛm ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / fɛm ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
a feminist social movement advocating for women's rights and gender equality
feminist social movement
informal

Examples

The fem movement has inspired many young women to become politically active.

Related Terms
