death's head

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛθz hɛd / (AmE), / ˈdɛθz hɛd / (BrE)
a human skull or its representation, often used as a symbol of death
skull symbolizing death

Examples

The pirate flag was emblazoned with a bold death's head and crossed bones.
Medieval tombstones sometimes feature a death's head to warn of mortality.
He wore a ring bearing a silver death's head as a grim reminder of fate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skull - the bony structure of the head in vertebrates
