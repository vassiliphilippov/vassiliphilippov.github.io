grain blower

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪn ˈbloʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ɡreɪn ˈbləʊ.ə / (BrE)
a machine that uses air to transport or handle grain through pneumatic means
machine for pneumatic grain transport

Examples

The farmer purchased a new grain blower to improve the efficiency of his grain handling system.
The grain blower made it easier to transport wheat from the storage bins to the truck.
During harvest, a powerful grain blower can save hours of manual labor on the farm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grain elevator - a building for storing and handling grain
  • Synonym: blower - a device that produces a stream of air
