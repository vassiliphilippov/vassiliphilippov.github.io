wild sarsaparilla

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ˌsɑːrspəˈrɪlə / (AmE), / waɪld ˌsɑːspəˈrɪlə / (BrE)
a perennial North American herb (Aralia nudicaulis) with aromatic roots used as a substitute for sarsaparilla
North American perennial herb with aromatic roots

Examples

The forest floor was covered in wild sarsaparilla, easily identified by its umbrella-shaped leaves.
Native peoples often used the roots of wild sarsaparilla for medicinal purposes.
During the hike, she pointed out some wild sarsaparilla growing alongside the trail.
