speed skater

1. NOUN

/ spid ˈskeɪt̬ɚ / (AmE), / spiːd ˈskeɪtə / (BrE)
A person who competes in races on ice skates, typically around an oval track.
competitive ice-skate racer

Examples

The speed skater set a new world record at the Winter Olympics.
After years of training, she became a national champion speed skater.
Many speed skaters transition to coaching after they retire from competition.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: figure skater - a person who performs choreographed routines on ice skates
