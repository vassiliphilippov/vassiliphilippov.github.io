bolt of lightning

1. NOUN

/ boʊlt əv ˈlaɪtnɪŋ / (AmE), / bəʊlt əv ˈlaɪtnɪŋ / (BrE)
a sudden flash of lightning in the sky, usually followed by thunder
sudden flash of lightning

Examples

A sudden bolt of lightning struck the tree outside our house.
We watched in awe as a massive bolt of lightning illuminated the night sky.
The power went out after a bolt of lightning hit the transformer nearby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lightning - a flash of light in the sky caused by electricity during a storm
