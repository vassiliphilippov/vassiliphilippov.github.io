ventriloquist's dummy

1. NOUN

/ vɛnˈtrɪləkwɪsts ˈdʌmi / (AmE), / vɛnˈtrɪlɪkwɪsts ˈdʌmi / (BrE)
a puppet, typically made of wood, operated by a ventriloquist to create the illusion of speech
puppet used by a ventriloquist

Examples

The comedian skillfully operated his ventriloquist's dummy to entertain the audience.
Her ventriloquist's dummy had an exaggerated smile and bright blue eyes.
Children were fascinated by the ventriloquist's dummy at the magic show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puppet - a movable model of a person or animal used in entertainment
