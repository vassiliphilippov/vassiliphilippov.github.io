securities analyst

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈkjʊrətiz ˈænəˌlɪst / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərɪtiz ˈænəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies and evaluates the financial performance of companies, typically to provide investment advice
financial analyst for investment advice

Examples

The securities analyst recommended buying the stock after reviewing the company's latest earnings report.
Many investors rely on the advice of a securities analyst before making major portfolio decisions.
As a securities analyst, she monitors corporate performance trends and industry outlooks regularly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: financial analyst - a person who analyzes financial data for businesses or individuals
