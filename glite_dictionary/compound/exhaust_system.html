exhaust system

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˈzɔst ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːst ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
The system in a vehicle or engine that carries away waste gases produced during combustion.
system for discharging engine waste gases

Examples

The exhaust system helps to reduce noise and direct gases away from the engine compartment.
After years of use, the exhaust system began to rust and needed to be replaced.
A modified exhaust system can improve both the sound and performance of a car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muffler - device for reducing noise in the exhaust system
