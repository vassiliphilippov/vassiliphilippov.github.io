cai png

1. NOUN

/ tsaɪ pʌŋ / (AmE), / tsaɪ pʌŋ / (BrE)
A meal consisting of rice served with a selection of various cooked dishes, commonly sold at food stalls in Singapore and Malaysia.
rice meal with assorted dishes (Singapore, Malaysia)
informal

Examples

I grabbed a cai png for lunch because it was quick and filling.
You can choose your preferred dishes when ordering cai png at the food court.
Many locals eat cai png regularly because it's affordable and offers plenty of options.

Synset Examples

We enjoyed a quick cai png lunch at the stall.
He shared a cai png meal with his friends.

Etymology

From Hokkien and Mandarin Chinese, literally 'vegetable rice' (菜饭; càifàn in Mandarin, cai png in Hokkien).
