desperate measure

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛsprət ˈmɛʒər / (AmE), / ˈdɛspərət ˈmɛʒə / (BrE)
an action that is extreme or drastic, taken when all other options have failed or seem impossible
extreme action taken as a last resort

Examples

During the crisis, the government resorted to desperate measures to restore order.
Using all their savings to pay the ransom was a desperate measure.
The company took desperate measures to avoid bankruptcy.

Synset Examples

he had to resort to desperate measures

Related Terms

  • Synonym: last resort - the final option left when all else has failed
  • Degree Lower: calculated risk - an action taken with full awareness of potential dangers, but with possible benefits
