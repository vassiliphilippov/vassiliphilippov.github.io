red nigger

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈnɪɡər / (AmE), / rɛd ˈnɪɡə / (BrE)
An extremely offensive and derogatory racial term historically used as a slur against Indigenous peoples in North America. Use of this term is strongly condemned and considered highly inappropriate.
offensive racial slur for Indigenous people
offensive

Examples

The use of red nigger in any context is utterly unacceptable and offensive.
Historical documents sometimes cite red nigger to illustrate past racial injustices.
Writers today strongly condemn the term red nigger and its legacy of harm.

Synset Examples

He was condemned for using the term red nigger casually.
The history behind the term red nigger is deeply troubling.
