granulator machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡræn.jə.leɪ.tɚ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈɡræn.jʊ.leɪ.tə məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used to break down materials, such as plastics or organic waste, into small granules
machine that forms materials into granules

Examples

The recycling plant uses a granulator machine to process discarded plastic bottles.
After shredding, the material is fed into a granulator machine for further size reduction.
A granulator machine can help convert organic waste into compostable granules.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shredder - machine that cuts materials into small pieces

Etymology

From 'granulate' (from Latin 'granulum', small grain) + 'machine'.
