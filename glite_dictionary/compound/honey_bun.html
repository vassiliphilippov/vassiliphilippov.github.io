honey bun

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌni bʌn / (AmE), / ˈhʌni bʌn / (BrE)
A sweet pastry made from rolled dough spread with sugar and nuts, sliced and baked in muffin tins with honey or sugar and butter at the bottom.
sweet pastry with honey or sugar and nuts

Examples

She enjoyed a warm honey bun with her morning coffee.
The bakery sells honey buns fresh out of the oven every day.
He bought a honey bun as a sweet treat after lunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cinnamon roll - a sweet roll with a cinnamon-sugar filling

2. NOUN

/ ˈhʌni bʌn / (AmE), / ˈhʌni bʌn / (BrE)
A sweet, sticky pastry, typically spiral-shaped and often flavored with honey and cinnamon.
sweet pastry with honey and cinnamon

Examples

Every morning, he buys a honey bun from the corner bakery.
She warmed her honey bun in the microwave before eating it.
The smell of fresh honey buns filled the kitchen.
