balsam scent

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑlsəm sɛnt / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlsəm sɛnt / (BrE)
the distinctive fragrant smell of balsam resin, often described as fresh, sweet, and woody
smell of balsam resin

Examples

The balsam scent lingering in the cabin reminded her of childhood holidays in the mountains.
A gentle balsam scent drifted from the fir branches arranged by the window.
He bought a candle so that the balsam scent could fill his apartment with a sense of the forest.

Synset Examples

The balsam scent filled the room with freshness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: balsam - an aromatic resin from certain trees and shrubs
