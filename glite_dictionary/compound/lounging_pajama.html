lounging pajama

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaʊndʒɪŋ pəˈdʒɑːmə / (AmE), / ˈlaʊndʒɪŋ pəˈdʒɑːmə / (BrE)
A set of comfortable pajamas designed for wearing while relaxing at home rather than for sleeping.
comfortable pajamas for relaxing at home

Examples

She slipped into her lounging pajamas before settling on the couch with a book.
His favorite lounging pajamas were soft, cotton, and perfect for Sunday mornings at home.
After a long day at work, she couldn't wait to put on her lounging pajamas and relax.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pajama - Loose-fitting clothes worn for sleeping or relaxing
