word meaning

1. NOUN

/ wɝd ˈminɪŋ / (AmE), / wɜːd ˈmiːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the definition or sense that is generally accepted for a particular word
the accepted definition of a word

Examples

The word meaning can change over time as language evolves.
Before using the word, make sure you understand its word meaning.
She asked the teacher to explain the word meaning in simpler terms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: definition - a statement of the exact meaning of a word
  • Degree Lower: word - a single distinct meaningful element of speech or writing
  • Degree Higher: phrase meaning - the accepted meaning of a phrase
