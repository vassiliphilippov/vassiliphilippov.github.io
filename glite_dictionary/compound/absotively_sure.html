absotively sure

1. ADJ

/ ˌæb.səˈtɪv.li ʃʊr / (AmE), / ˌæb.səˈtɪv.li ʃɔː / (BrE)
Completely certain; absolutely sure (used informally for emphasis).
completely certain (informal)
informal

Examples

I'm absotively sure that I left my keys on the table.
The team was absotively sure they would win the match after their last practice.
Are you absotively sure this is the right address?

Synset Examples

She was absotively sure of her decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absolutely sure - completely certain
  • Antonym: uncertain - not completely certain

Etymology

Blend of 'absolutely' and 'positively'.
