to appreciate the feedback

1. VERB

/ əˈpriːʃiˌeɪt ðə ˈfiːdbæk / (AmE), / əˈpriːʃieɪt ðə ˈfiːdbak / (BrE)
to be grateful for or value the comments or input received from others
express gratitude for others' comments

Examples

I really appreciate the feedback you gave on my presentation.
We appreciate the feedback from our clients to help us improve.
She said she would appreciate the feedback after everyone has tested the product.

Synset Examples

I appreciate the feedback, especially coming from you.
We appreciate your feedback on our proposal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thank you for your feedback - expression of gratitude for feedback
