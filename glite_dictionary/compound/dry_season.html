dry season

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪ ˌsiːzən / (AmE), / ˈdraɪ ˌsiːzən / (BrE)
the period in tropical climates when there is little or no rain
rainless period in the tropics

Examples

Many animals migrate when the dry season begins and water becomes scarce.
During the dry season, the riverbeds in the region often turn to dust.
Farmers must plan their planting schedules around the arrival of the dry season.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rainy season - the period in tropical climates with heavy rainfall
