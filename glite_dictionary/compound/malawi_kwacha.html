Malawi kwacha

1. NOUN

/ məˈlɑːwi kwɑːtʃə / (AmE), / məˈlɑːwi kwætʃə / (BrE)
the official currency and basic unit of money in Malawi
currency of Malawi

Examples

The price of imported goods increased as the value of the Malawi kwacha fell against the dollar.
He exchanged his dollars for Malawi kwacha at the airport bank.
All transactions in the country must be conducted in Malawi kwacha.

Etymology

From Chichewa 'kwacha', meaning 'dawn'.
