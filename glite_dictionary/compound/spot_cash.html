spot cash

1. NOUN

/ spɑt kæʃ / (AmE), / spɒt kaʃ / (BrE)
an immediate cash payment, made on the spot without delay or credit
immediate cash payment

Examples

The company offered a discount for spot cash payments.
They insisted on spot cash for all their secondhand goods.
He managed to buy the property by paying spot cash.

Synset Examples

The seller preferred spot cash for the antiques.
She offered spot cash to close the deal quickly.
He paid for the car entirely in spot cash.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cash payment - payment made using cash rather than credit or cheque

Etymology

From 'spot' (meaning 'on the spot', i.e. immediately) and 'cash'.
