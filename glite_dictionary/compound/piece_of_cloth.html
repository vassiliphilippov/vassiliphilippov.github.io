piece of cloth

1. NOUN

/ pis əv klɔθ / (AmE), / piːs əv klɒθ / (BrE)
a separate part or section of fabric, typically a small portion cut from a larger fabric or garment
a separate section of fabric

Examples

She tore a piece of cloth from her shirt to use as a bandage.
Each piece of cloth is carefully measured before sewing.
The artist chose a blue piece of cloth as the background for his painting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cloth - fabric made from woven or knitted fibers
