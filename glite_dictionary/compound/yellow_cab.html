yellow cab

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ kæb / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ kab / (BrE)
a taxi, especially one that is typically painted yellow, as commonly seen in some cities such as New York
taxi, especially one painted yellow

Examples

After dinner, we flagged down a yellow cab to take us home.
The yellow cab waited patiently outside the hotel as tourists gathered their luggage.
He prefers to ride in a yellow cab rather than using a ride-sharing app.

Synset Examples

She jumped into a yellow cab after the concert.
I hailed a yellow cab to get to the airport.
The yellow cab sped off into the busy street.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxi - a car for hire with a driver, used by passengers for transportation
