in oestrum

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ˈiːstrəm / (AmE), / ɪn ˈiːstrəm / (BrE)
in a state of sexual receptivity, especially referring to female mammals during their fertile period
in a state of sexual receptivity (animals)
medical

Examples

The veterinarian noted that the cow was in oestrum and scheduled breeding accordingly.
During spring, many animals become in oestrum as part of their reproductive cycle.
The behavior of the wolf pack changes when a female is in oestrum.

Synset Examples

The mare was in oestrum and ready to breed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in heat - in a period of sexual receptivity (animals)

Etymology

From Latin 'oestrus', meaning frenzy, inspired by the characteristic behavior during this period.
