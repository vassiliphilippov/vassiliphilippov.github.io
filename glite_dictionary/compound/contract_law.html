contract law

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑnˌtrækt lɔ / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.trækt lɔː / (BrE)
The area of law that deals with the rights and obligations of parties who enter into contracts.
area of law dealing with contracts
legal

Examples

Contract law governs the agreements made between businesses and their clients.
If you want to draft a valid agreement, you should understand the basics of contract law.
Her studies focused on contract law and its application in international trade.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tort law - area of law dealing with civil wrongs not arising from contracts
