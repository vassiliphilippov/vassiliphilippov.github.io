water wings

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər wɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə wɪŋz / (BrE)
A pair of inflatable bags worn on the upper arms to help someone, especially a child, stay afloat while learning to swim.
inflatable swimming aids for children

Examples

My daughter is more confident in the pool when she's wearing her water wings.
Many parents use water wings to help their kids learn to swim safely.
The lifeguard reminded the children that water wings are not a substitute for supervision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armbands - inflatable swimming aids worn on the arms
