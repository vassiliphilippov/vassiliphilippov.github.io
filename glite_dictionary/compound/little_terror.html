little terror

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬l ˈtɛrɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪtəl ˈtɛrə / (BrE)
A child who behaves very badly or causes a lot of trouble.
very troublesome child

Examples

Her son is a real little terror at home but behaves perfectly at school.
The twins can be little terrors when they're together, always getting into mischief.
To the relief of her parents, their little terror finally fell asleep after hours of chaos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troublemaker - a person, especially a child, who causes trouble
