binder's board

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪndɚz ˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbaɪndəz ˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a strong cardboard material used by bookbinders to make covers for books
cardboard for making book covers

Examples

The rare book had a binder's board cover that had warped with time.
Traditional bookbinding often uses binder's board to provide sturdy covers.
After cutting the binder's board, she carefully glued the cloth onto it for the book cover.
