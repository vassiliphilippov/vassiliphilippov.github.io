reindeer moss

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪnˌdɪr mɑs / (AmE), / ˈreɪndɪə mɒs / (BrE)
a greyish, branching lichen found in Arctic and some temperate regions, serving as the chief food for reindeer and caribou
a lichen eaten by reindeer and caribou

Examples

The Arctic tundra is covered in reindeer moss, providing food for migrating herds.
Scientists studied the growth patterns of reindeer moss under extreme cold conditions.
She picked some reindeer moss to use as kindling for their campfire.
