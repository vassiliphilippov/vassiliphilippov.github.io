rope-maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊpˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊpˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose occupation is making ropes by hand or with machinery
person who makes ropes

Examples

The village's last rope-maker retired in the 1980s, ending a centuries-old tradition.
A skilled rope-maker carefully twists the fibers to produce strong, reliable ropes.
Many museums today feature demonstrations by a rope-maker using antique tools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ropemaker - person whose trade is making rope
  • Synonym: ropesmith - person who makes rope, less common synonym
