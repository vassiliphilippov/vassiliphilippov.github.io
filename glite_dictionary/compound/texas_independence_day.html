Texas Independence Day

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛksəs ˌɪndəˈpɛndəns deɪ / (AmE), / ˈtɛksəs ˌɪndɪˈpɛndəns deɪ / (BrE)
The anniversary commemorating Texas's declaration of independence from Mexico in 1836, celebrated annually in Texas on March 2.
holiday marking Texas's declaration of independence

Examples

Many schools in Texas hold special events for Texas Independence Day every year.
On Texas Independence Day, people display the Lone Star flag with pride.
She wrote an article explaining the significance of Texas Independence Day for new residents.
