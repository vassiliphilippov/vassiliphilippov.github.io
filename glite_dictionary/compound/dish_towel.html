dish towel

1. NOUN

/ dɪʃ ˈtaʊəl / (AmE), / dɪʃ ˈtaʊəl / (BrE)
a towel used for drying dishes after they have been washed
towel for drying dishes

Examples

She hung the dish towel on the oven handle to dry.
After washing the plates, he reached for a clean dish towel.
Make sure to use a different dish towel for glasses to avoid streaks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tea towel - a towel used for drying dishes, especially in British English
