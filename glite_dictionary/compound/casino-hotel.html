casino-hotel

1. NOUN

/ kəˈsiːnoʊ hoʊˈtɛl / (AmE), / kəˈsiːnəʊ həʊˈtɛl / (BrE)
a building or complex that includes both a casino and a hotel, offering accommodation and gambling facilities under one roof
hotel combined with a casino

Examples

The city is famous for its luxurious casino-hotel resorts lining the main boulevard.
After a long night of gambling, he stayed upstairs at the attached casino-hotel.
Many tourists visit Las Vegas seeking the excitement of a casino-hotel experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: casino - a building for gambling games
  • Synonym: hotel - an establishment providing lodging and possibly meals

2. NOUN

/ kəˈsiːnoʊ hoʊˈtɛl / (AmE), / kəˈsiːnəʊ həʊˈtɛl / (BrE)
A business establishment that offers both casino gambling facilities and hotel accommodations.
hotel with a casino; gambling hotel

Examples

The famous Las Vegas Strip is lined with luxurious casino-hotels attracting visitors from around the world.
We stayed at a casino-hotel that offered both gaming and world-class spa services.
Many casino-hotels provide free entertainment to encourage guests to stay and play.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: casino - a building where gambling games are played
  • Synonym: hotel - an establishment providing accommodation for travelers

Etymology

Formed from 'casino' and 'hotel,' both from Italian and French roots respectively.
