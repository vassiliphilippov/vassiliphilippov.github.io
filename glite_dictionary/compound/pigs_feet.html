pigs' feet

1. NOUN

/ pɪɡz fit / (AmE), / pɪɡz fiːt / (BrE)
the feet or lower leg joints of pigs, prepared and eaten as food, often pickled, stewed, or jellied
feet or knuckles of pigs used as food

Examples

She served pigs' feet with a side of greens and cornbread.
Some people enjoy pigs' feet pickled in vinegar and spices.
At the festival, the specialty was stewed pigs' feet in a rich, flavorful broth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trotters - the feet of animals, especially pigs, eaten as food
