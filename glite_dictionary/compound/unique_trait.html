unique trait

1. NOUN

/ juˈnik treɪt / (AmE), / juːˈniːk treɪt / (BrE)
a characteristic or feature that distinguishes someone or something from others
distinctive characteristic or feature

Examples

Her kindness is her most unique trait among her colleagues.
Every snowflake has a unique trait that sets it apart from the others.
The artist's unique trait is his ability to blend classical and modern styles.

Synset Examples

The xanthodont's unique trait fascinated the paleontologists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: characteristic - a typical or noticeable feature of someone or something
  • Antonym: common trait - a trait shared by many, not unique
