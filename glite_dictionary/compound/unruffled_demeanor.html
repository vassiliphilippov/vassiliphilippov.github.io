unruffled demeanor

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈrʌfəld dɪˈmiːnɚ / (AmE), / ʌnˈrʌfəld dɪˈmiːnə / (BrE)
a calm and composed manner, especially in challenging or stressful situations
calm and composed manner

Examples

Despite the chaos, her unruffled demeanor reassured her colleagues.
He handled the probing questions with an unruffled demeanor throughout the interview.
Even under pressure, she maintained an unruffled demeanor that inspired confidence in her team.

Synset Examples

His unruffled demeanor impressed everyone during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: composure - the state of being calm and in control of oneself
  • Antonym: agitated manner - a noticeably disturbed or nervous manner
