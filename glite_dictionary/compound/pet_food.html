pet food

1. NOUN

/ ˈpet ˌfuːd / (AmE), / ˈpet ˌfuːd / (BrE)
food that is specially prepared and formulated to be eaten by domesticated animals kept as pets, such as dogs and cats.
food for domesticated pets

Examples

Most supermarkets have a wide selection of pet food for different animals.
She switched to organic pet food after learning about better nutrition for her cat.
Some people prepare homemade pet food to ensure their pets get the best ingredients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog food - food prepared specifically for dogs
  • Synonym: cat food - food prepared specifically for cats

2. NOUN

/ ˈpɛt ˌfuːd / (AmE), / ˈpɛt ˌfuːd / (BrE)
food that is specially prepared and intended for consumption by domestic pets such as dogs and cats.
food for pets such as dogs or cats

Examples

The supermarket has a wide selection of pet food for both dogs and cats.
He bought a month's worth of pet food to feed his three puppies.
My vet recommended switching to a different pet food for my aging cat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: animal feed - food for livestock or other non-pet animals
