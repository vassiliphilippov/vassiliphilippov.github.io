Hoosier State

1. PROPN

/ ˈhuːʒər steɪt / (AmE), / ˈhuːʒɪə steɪt / (BrE)
A nickname for the U.S. state of Indiana, located in the Midwest, with its capital at Indianapolis.
nickname for the state of Indiana

Examples

After moving to the Hoosier State, she quickly fell in love with Indiana's scenic landscapes.
Basketball is a hugely popular sport in the Hoosier State.
Indianapolis, the capital of the Hoosier State, hosts the famous Indy 500 race every year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Indiana - U.S. state in the Midwest, capital Indianapolis

Etymology

From 'Hoosier', a nickname for a native of Indiana; origin of 'Hoosier' is uncertain.
