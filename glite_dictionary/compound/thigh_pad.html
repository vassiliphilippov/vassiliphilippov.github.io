thigh pad

1. NOUN

/ θaɪ pæd / (AmE), / θaɪ pad / (BrE)
a protective pad worn over the thigh to prevent injury, especially in sports such as football and cricket
protective pad for the thigh (sports)

Examples

He forgot to wear his thigh pad during the match and ended up with a bruise.
Most professional football players use a thigh pad for extra protection.
The cricketer adjusted his thigh pad before facing the next ball.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shin guard - a protective covering for the shin used in sports
  • Synonym: knee pad - a protective pad worn over the knee
