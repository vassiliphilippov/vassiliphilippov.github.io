departure gate

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑrtʃɚ ɡeɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtʃə ɡeɪt / (BrE)
a gate at an airport where passengers go to board their aircraft before departure
airport boarding gate

Examples

Our flight leaves from departure gate 22, so let's head there now.
Please have your boarding pass ready when you arrive at the departure gate.
The airline staff announced a change to the departure gate for our flight to Paris.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boarding gate - gate at an airport where passengers board their flight
