wing-nut

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪŋ ˌnʌt / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋ nʌt / (BrE)
A nut with two flat projections that allow it to be tightened and loosened by hand without tools.
nut with hand-turn wings

Examples

Make sure the wing-nut is securely fastened before using the machine.
He replaced the regular nut with a wing-nut so he could adjust it without a wrench.
The shelf came with wing-nuts for easy assembly by hand.
