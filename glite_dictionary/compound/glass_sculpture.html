glass sculpture

1. NOUN

/ ɡlæs ˈskʌlptʃər / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs ˈskʌlptʃə / (BrE)
a work of art made by shaping or molding glass into a sculptural form
sculpture made from glass

Examples

The museum featured a stunning glass sculpture by a famous contemporary artist.
Sunlight streaming through the glass sculpture filled the room with colored patterns.
She learned advanced techniques to create her own glass sculpture during an art workshop.

Synset Examples

The artist used frit to create beautiful glass sculptures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sculpture - a three-dimensional work of art created by shaping materials
