clinical neurology

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɪnɪkəl nuˈrɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˈklɪnɪkəl njuːˈrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system
medical branch treating nervous system disorders
medical

Examples

After completing her residency, she decided to specialize in clinical neurology.
Advances in clinical neurology have improved the treatment of stroke patients.
He attended an international conference on clinical neurology last year.

Related Terms
