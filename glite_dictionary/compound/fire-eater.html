fire-eater

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚˌitɚ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌiːtə / (BrE)
a performer who entertains an audience by apparently swallowing and expelling fire
a performer who pretends to swallow fire

Examples

The circus featured a talented fire-eater who wowed the crowd with dangerous stunts.
As a young man, he dreamed of becoming a fire-eater at the carnival.
The fire-eater carefully practiced his routine to avoid any accidents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fire-breather - a performer who expels fire from the mouth

2. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚ ˌitɚ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌiːtə / (BrE)
a person who performs tricks by apparently swallowing or eating fire, especially as a form of entertainment
person who performs fire-swallowing tricks

Examples

The fire-eater wowed the crowd with his daring stunts at the circus.
After years of practice, she finally joined a troupe as a fire-eater.
Children watched in amazement as the fire-eater extinguished flames in his mouth.
