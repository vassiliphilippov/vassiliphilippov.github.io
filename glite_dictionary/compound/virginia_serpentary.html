Virginia serpentary

1. NOUN

/ vərˈdʒɪniə ˈsɝːpənˌteri / (AmE), / vəˈdʒɪniə ˈsɜːpənˌtɛəri / (BrE)
A woodland plant native to the eastern United States, also known as birthwort, distinguished by its pipe-shaped flowers.
a woodland plant of eastern US

Examples

The woods were filled with Virginia serpentary and other native plants.
Botanists discovered a rare specimen of Virginia serpentary during their field study.
Many herbalists once believed Virginia serpentary had medicinal properties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birthwort - plant of the genus Aristolochia, sometimes used as a synonym
