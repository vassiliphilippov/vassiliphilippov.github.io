mleccha behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈmleɪ.tʃə bəˈheɪ.vjɚ / (AmE), / ˈmlɛtʃə bɪˈheɪ.vjə / (BrE)
Behavior regarded as uncivilized or barbaric, often with reference to standards from Indian tradition.
uncivilized or barbaric behavior (Indian context)

Examples

Her mleccha behavior shocked the traditionalists at the ceremony.
The teacher criticized the students' mleccha behavior during the formal event.
Some elders worry that modern trends encourage mleccha behavior among young people.
