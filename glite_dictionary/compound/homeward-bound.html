homeward-bound

1. ADJ

/ ˈhoʊmwɚdˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmwədˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
going or moving towards one's home or a place regarded as home.
headed or going towards home

Examples

The homeward-bound train was crowded with commuters eager to end their day.
After months at sea, the sailors grew restless and excited as their homeward-bound journey neared its end.
The last rays of sunlight illuminated the homeward-bound travelers on the country road.

Synset Examples

in a homeward direction
homeward-bound commuters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homebound - confined to one's home; also, going home
  • Antonym: outbound - going away from a place, especially from home
