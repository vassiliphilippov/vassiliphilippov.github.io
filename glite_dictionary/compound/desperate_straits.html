desperate straits

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛspərət streɪts / (AmE), / ˈdɛspərət streɪts / (BrE)
a situation of extreme difficulty or distress
extreme difficulty or distress

Examples

After losing his job and falling behind on rent, he found himself in desperate straits.
The company was in desperate straits before the bank approved a loan.
Many families faced desperate straits during the economic crisis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dire straits - a situation of serious difficulty or distress
