to that end

1. ADV

/ tə ðæt ɛnd / (AmE), / tə ðæt end / (BrE)
with that aim or purpose; in order to achieve that result.
for that purpose or aim

Examples

We want to improve our services and, to that end, have hired new staff.
He invested in modern equipment; to that end, he consulted several experts.
The team needs better communication. To that end, weekly meetings have been introduced.

Synset Examples

He trained hard; to that end, he won the championship.
She studied all night; to that end, she aced the exam.
They prepared a budget proposal; to that end, they gathered all necessary data.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for that purpose - with that aim or intention; synonym for 'to that end'
