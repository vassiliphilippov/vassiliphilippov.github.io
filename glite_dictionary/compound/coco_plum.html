coco plum

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊkoʊ ˌplʌm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkəʊ ˌplʌm / (BrE)
A small, plum-shaped fruit with whitish to almost black skin, native to tropical America and often used for preserves.
tropical American fruit used for preserves

Examples

The jam was made from fresh coco plums picked that morning.
In the local market, baskets of coco plum were displayed alongside other tropical fruits.
The coco plum tree thrives in coastal areas and produces a tasty fruit.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊkoʊ ˌplʌm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkəʊ ˌplʌm / (BrE)
A small tropical American tree (Chrysobalanus icaco) that bears edible, plumlike fruit.
tropical tree with edible fruit

Examples

The coco plum is often found along coastal areas in the Caribbean.
She made a delicious jam from fresh coco plums picked this morning.
The leaves and fruit of the coco plum are used in local herbal remedies.
