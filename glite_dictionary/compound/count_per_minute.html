count per minute

1. NOUN

/ kaʊnt pɚ ˈmɪnət / (AmE), / kaʊnt pə ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
the number of occurrences or events measured or recorded within one minute
number of occurrences in one minute

Examples

The Geiger counter displayed a count per minute of 120, indicating moderate radiation.
Pulse oximeters record the heart rate as a count per minute for patient monitoring.
We measured the machine's vibrations and found the count per minute exceeded safe levels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rate - a measure or quantity measured against another quantity per unit of time
  • Synonym: frequency - the rate at which something occurs over a particular period of time
