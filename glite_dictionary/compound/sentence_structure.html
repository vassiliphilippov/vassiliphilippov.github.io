sentence structure

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛntəns ˌstrʌktʃər / (AmE), / ˈsɛntəns ˌstrʌktʃə / (BrE)
the way in which words, phrases, and clauses are arranged to form sentences in a language
grammatical arrangement of words in sentences

Examples

Teachers often explain how sentence structure affects the clarity of your writing.
Learning about sentence structure can help students improve their grammar and composition skills.
English has a flexible sentence structure, allowing various ways to express the same idea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: syntax - the set of rules, principles, and processes that govern the structure of sentences
  • Synonym: grammar - the whole system and structure of a language, including sentence structure and word forms
