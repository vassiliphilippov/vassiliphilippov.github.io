the best policy

1. NOUN

/ ðə bɛst ˈpɑləsi / (AmE), / ðə bɛst ˈpɒləsi / (BrE)
the most sensible or effective course of action in a given situation
most effective course of action

Examples

For tough negotiations, patience is often the best policy.
When in doubt, telling the truth is usually the best policy.
She realized that staying calm was the best policy during the crisis.

Synset Examples

As the man says, honesty is the best policy.
In the final analysis, honesty is the best policy.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: the worst policy - the least effective or advisable course of action
