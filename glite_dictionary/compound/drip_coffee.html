drip coffee

1. NOUN

/ drɪp ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / drɪp ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
Coffee made by brewing hot water through a filter containing ground coffee, typically using a drip coffee maker.
filtered coffee made by dripping method

Examples

I prefer drip coffee to instant because of its richer flavor.
Every morning, she brews a fresh pot of drip coffee to start her day.
Most American diners serve drip coffee throughout the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filter coffee - coffee made by filtering hot water through ground coffee
  • Antonym: espresso - strong coffee made by forcing steam through ground coffee
