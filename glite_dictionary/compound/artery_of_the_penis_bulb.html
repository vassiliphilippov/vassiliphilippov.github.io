artery of the penis bulb

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrtəri əv ðə ˈpiːnɪs bʌlb / (AmE), / ˈɑːtəri əv ðə ˈpiːnɪs bʌlb / (BrE)
the artery, branching from the internal pudendal artery in males, that supplies blood to the bulb of the penis
artery supplying the bulb of the penis
medical

Examples

During surgery, the artery of the penis bulb must be carefully preserved to avoid compromising blood flow.
Damage to the artery of the penis bulb can lead to complications with penile function.
Anatomical diagrams often highlight the artery of the penis bulb when explaining the blood supply of the penis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: internal pudendal artery - main artery in the perineum supplying pelvic organs
