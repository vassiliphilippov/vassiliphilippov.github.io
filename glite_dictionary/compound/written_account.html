written account

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪt.ən əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈrɪt.ən əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
a document in which facts or events are recorded in writing for preservation or reference
document recording facts or events in writing

Examples

The historian relied on a written account of the battle to reconstruct the sequence of events.
She kept a detailed written account of her travels across the country.
The trial was based in part on a written account submitted by an eyewitness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: record - an official written document that preserves information
  • Antonym: oral account - an account of events or facts told by word of mouth
