crack cocaine

1. NOUN

/ kræk koʊˈkeɪn / (AmE), / kræk kəʊˈkeɪn / (BrE)
A highly addictive, purified form of cocaine intended for smoking rather than snorting.
purified, smokable cocaine drug

Examples

The police found a hidden stash of crack cocaine during the raid.
Many communities have struggled with the impact of crack cocaine addiction.
Authorities launched new initiatives to combat the spread of crack cocaine use in urban areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cocaine - a powerful stimulant drug derived from coca leaves
  • Synonym: heroin - an addictive narcotic drug derived from morphine

Etymology

From 'crack' (referring to the cracking sound made when smoked) and 'cocaine' (from Spanish coca and -ine, from Latin).
