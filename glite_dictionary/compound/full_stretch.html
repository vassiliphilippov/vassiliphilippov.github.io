full stretch

1. NOUN

/ fʊl strɛtʃ / (AmE), / fʊl strɛtʃ / (BrE)
a state of exerting maximum effort or using all one's resources, especially in physical or mental activity
maximum effort or exertion

Examples

The runners were at full stretch as they approached the finish line.
After working at full stretch all week, she finally completed the project.
The rescue team operated at full stretch during the crisis.

Synset Examples

The athlete was at full stretch during the competition.
She ran at full stretch to catch the bus.
The team worked at full stretch to meet the deadline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maximum effort - the greatest possible effort
