average frustrated chump

1. NOUN

/ ˈæv.ɚ.ɪdʒ frʌˈstreɪ.t̬ɪd tʃʌmp / (AmE), / ˈæv.ər.ɪdʒ frəˈstreɪ.tɪd tʃʌmp / (BrE)
an ordinary person who feels dissatisfied, unfulfilled, or easily discouraged, especially in the face of challenges or failures
an unfulfilled, easily discouraged person
informal

Examples

He's tired of being just an average frustrated chump who never takes action with his goals.
The book offers advice for the average frustrated chump struggling with low motivation at work.
She didn't want to end up like the average frustrated chump who settles for less in life.

Synset Examples

Every day, I strive not to be an average frustrated chump.
He felt like the average frustrated chump after the meeting.
Don't be the average frustrated chump who gives up easily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loser - a person who is habitually unsuccessful or looked down upon
  • Antonym: optimist - a person who tends to be hopeful and confident about the future
