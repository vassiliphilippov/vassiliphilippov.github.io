baseball season

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl ˈsiːzən / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsˌbɔːl ˈsiːzn̩ / (BrE)
the period of the year during which official baseball games are played, typically spanning spring to early autumn
period for official baseball games

Examples

The baseball season usually begins in early spring and ends in the fall.
He spent the entire baseball season recovering from an injury.
Many fans look forward to the start of baseball season every year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: season - a period of the year characterized by a particular activity or event
