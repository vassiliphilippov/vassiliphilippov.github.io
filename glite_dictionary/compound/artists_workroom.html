artist's workroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːr.tɪsts ˈwɜːrk.ruːm / (AmE), / ˈɑː.tɪsts ˈwɜːk.ruːm / (BrE)
a room or space where an artist works, especially used as a studio for creating artworks
a room where an artist works

Examples

She spends hours every day painting in her artist's workroom.
Natural light streamed through the windows of the artist's workroom.
The artist's workroom was filled with unfinished canvases and color palettes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: studio - a room where an artist, musician, or photographer works
