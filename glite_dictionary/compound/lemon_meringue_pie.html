lemon meringue pie

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɛmən məˈrɪŋɡ pi / (AmE), / ˌlɛmən məˈrɪŋɡ paɪ / (BrE)
A pie made with a lemon-flavored custard filling and topped with a layer of meringue.
pie with lemon custard and meringue topping

Examples

She brought a homemade lemon meringue pie to the family dinner, and it was eaten within minutes.
I've always found lemon meringue pie to be the perfect balance of tart and sweet.
For dessert, they served a classic lemon meringue pie with fluffy meringue on top.
