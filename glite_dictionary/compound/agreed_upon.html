agreed upon

1. ADJ

/ əˈɡrid əˌpɑn / (AmE), / əˈɡriːd əˌpɒn / (BrE)
established or decided by mutual agreement; stipulated or contracted as part of an agreement
established by mutual agreement

Examples

The terms of the contract were agreed upon by both parties before signing.
We will proceed according to the agreed upon schedule.
Changes to the agreed upon guidelines require approval from all members.

Synset Examples

stipulatory obligations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stipulated - specified as a condition of an agreement
