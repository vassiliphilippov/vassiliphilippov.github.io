meat-packing business

1. NOUN

/ mit ˈpækɪŋ ˈbɪznɪs / (AmE), / miːt ˈpakɪŋ ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
the industry involved in the slaughtering, processing, packaging, and distribution of meat to retailers
industry processing and distributing meat

Examples

The city grew rapidly in the nineteenth century, thanks largely to its meat-packing business.
After the new regulations, the meat-packing business had to upgrade its safety procedures.
Her grandfather started a small meat-packing business that eventually became a nationwide company.
