burn rate

1. NOUN

/ bɝn reɪt / (AmE), / bɜːn reɪt / (BrE)
The rate at which an organization, especially a startup, spends its available funds, typically measured on a monthly basis.
rate at which funds are spent by a company

Examples

The company's burn rate forced them to seek additional funding much sooner than expected.
By cutting unnecessary expenses, they managed to lower their monthly burn rate.
Investors questioned the high burn rate during the board meeting.

Synset Examples

Reducing the burn rate is crucial for our survival.
They monitored their burn rate closely to extend their runway.
The startup's burn rate is alarming for investors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spending rate - the rate at which money is spent
  • Degree Higher: runway - the amount of time before a company runs out of cash at its current burn rate
