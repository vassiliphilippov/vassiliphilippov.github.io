Greek deity

1. NOUN

/ ɡriːk ˈdiːəti / (AmE), / ɡriːk ˈdiːəti / (BrE)
A god or goddess worshipped in ancient Greek religion.
a god or goddess from ancient Greek religion

Examples

Many temples were built to honor a particular Greek deity.
Zeus is one of the most well-known Greek deities in mythology.
The rituals were intended to appease the wrath of a Greek deity.
