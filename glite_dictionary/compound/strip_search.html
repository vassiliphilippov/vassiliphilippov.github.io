strip search

1. NOUN

/ strɪp sɝtʃ / (AmE), / strɪp sɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a search of someone by requiring them to remove their clothes to check for concealed weapons, drugs, or other illegal items
search involving removal of clothing

Examples

The prison guards conducted a strip search on every new inmate who arrived at the facility.
He felt humiliated during the strip search at the airport after being selected for additional screening.
The policy requires a strip search only under circumstances where concealed contraband is strongly suspected.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: body search - a search of a person's body for hidden items
