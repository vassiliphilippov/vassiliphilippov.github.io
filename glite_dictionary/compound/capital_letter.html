capital letter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæpɪtl̩ ˈlɛtər / (AmE), / ˈkæpɪtəl ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
A large letter used at the beginning of sentences and proper names or for emphasis, as distinguished from a lowercase letter.
uppercase letter used in writing and printing

Examples

Remember to start each sentence with a capital letter in your essay.
Names of countries always begin with a capital letter.
She wrote her initials in capital letters for emphasis.

Synset Examples

printers once kept the type for capitals and for small letters in separate cases; capitals were kept in the upper half of the type case and so became known as upper-case letters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uppercase letter - a letter in the larger form used in writing and printing
  • Antonym: lowercase letter - a letter in the smaller form used in writing and printing
