popular spot

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑpjələr spɑt / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊlə spɒt / (BrE)
a place that is liked or visited by many people, often because it is attractive or enjoyable
well-liked place, often visited by many people

Examples

Since the renovation, the new café downtown has quickly become a popular spot among students.
Central Park is always a popular spot for locals and tourists alike during the summer months.
The beach is the most popular spot in town on hot weekends.

Synset Examples

Dubbo has become a popular spot for family vacations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot spot - a place of significant activity or popularity
