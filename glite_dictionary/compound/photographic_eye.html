photographic eye

1. NOUN

/ ˌfoʊ.təˈɡræf.ɪk aɪ / (AmE), / ˌfəʊ.təˈɡræf.ɪk aɪ / (BrE)
an exceptional ability to notice and recall vivid details, especially visually
keen ability to notice vivid details

Examples

Her photographic eye helps her capture stunning images that others might miss.
The designer's photographic eye ensures even the smallest elements are perfectly aligned.
People often admire his photographic eye when viewing his meticulous sketches.

Synset Examples

He has a photographic eye for detail in art.
