unique identifier

1. NOUN

/ juˈnik aɪˌdɛntəˌfaɪər / (AmE), / juːˈniːk aɪˌdɛntɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a code, number, or name that is used to identify a single item, person, or record among others in a system
distinct code for identification

Examples

Every employee is assigned a unique identifier to access the building.
Database records are organized by a unique identifier for easy retrieval.
Please enter your unique identifier to verify your account.

Synset Examples

Each passoid contains unique identifiers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ID - an abbreviation for 'identification', often used similarly
