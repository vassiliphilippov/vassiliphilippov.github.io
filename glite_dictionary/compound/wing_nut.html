wing nut

1. NOUN

/ wɪŋ nʌt / (AmE), / wɪŋ nʌt / (BrE)
a nut with two large metal projections, or 'wings,' which allow it to be tightened and loosened by hand without tools
nut with winglike projections for hand tightening

Examples

Make sure the wing nut is secure before operating the machine.
She loosened the wing nut by hand to remove the panel.
The lamp's shade is held in place by a simple wing nut.
