hairstreak butterfly

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrˌstriːk ˈbʌt̬ɚˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈheəstriːk ˈbʌtəflaɪ / (BrE)
a small butterfly of the family Lycaenidae, typically having fine, hair-like lines or stripes on the underside of the wings
small butterfly with striped underwings

Examples

The hairstreak butterfly is known for the delicate lines on the underside of its wings.
Several species of hairstreak butterflies are found in temperate forests across North America.
Naturalists are excited to observe a rare hairstreak butterfly in the local meadow this summer.

Etymology

From 'hairstreak' (referring to the fine lines on its wings) and 'butterfly'.
