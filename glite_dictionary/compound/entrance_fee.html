entrance fee

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛn.trəns fiː / (AmE), / ˈɛn.trəns fiː / (BrE)
a sum of money paid for admission to a place or event
fee for admission

Examples

The museum charges an entrance fee for adult visitors.
We were disappointed to find there was a high entrance fee to the exhibition.
Children under five do not have to pay an entrance fee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admission fee - a fee paid to enter a place
