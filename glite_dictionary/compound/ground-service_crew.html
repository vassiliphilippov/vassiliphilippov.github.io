ground-service crew

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡraʊnd ˌsɜrvɪs kru / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊnd ˌsɜːvɪs kruː / (BrE)
the team of technicians and mechanics responsible for maintaining and servicing aircraft while they are on the ground
airport team servicing aircraft on the ground

Examples

The ground-service crew quickly refueled the plane and loaded the luggage before takeoff.
After each flight, the ground-service crew performs a thorough inspection of the aircraft.
Delays can happen if the ground-service crew encounters unexpected maintenance issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ground crew - the staff responsible for aircraft on the ground
