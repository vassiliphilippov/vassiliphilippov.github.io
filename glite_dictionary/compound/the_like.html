the like

1. NOUN

/ ðə laɪk / (AmE), / ðə laɪk / (BrE)
things or people of a similar kind; similar examples or types.
a similar kind; similar examples

Examples

They discussed climate change, pollution, and the like at the conference.
People like him never show respect for rules—or for the like.
The zoo had many birds—parrots, toucans, and the like.

Synset Examples

dogs, foxes, and the like
we don't want the likes of you around here

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the likes of - people or things of the same type as (someone or something) mentioned
