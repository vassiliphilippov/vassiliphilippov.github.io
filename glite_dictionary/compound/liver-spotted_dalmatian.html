liver-spotted dalmatian

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvər ˌspɑtɪd ˌdælˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈlɪvə ˌspɒtɪd ˌdælˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
A Dalmatian dog that has brown spots instead of the usual black.
Dalmatian with brown spots

Examples

Their new puppy is a beautiful liver-spotted dalmatian with expressive brown eyes.
At the dog show, the judge admired the unique markings of the liver-spotted dalmatian.
Unlike most Dalmatians, a liver-spotted dalmatian has brown spots instead of black.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dalmatian - a breed of dog known for its spotted coat
