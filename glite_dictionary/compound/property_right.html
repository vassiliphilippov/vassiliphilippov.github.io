property right

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑː.pɚ.ti raɪt / (AmE), / ˈprɒp.ə.ti raɪt / (BrE)
the legal right to possess, use, or dispose of property
legal right of ownership
legal

Examples

The property right allows the owner to sell the land to anyone.
Disputes over property rights are common in rapidly developing cities.
Intellectual property rights protect the creations of inventors and artists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ownership - the state or fact of owning something
  • Degree Lower: property - possessions or real estate owned by someone
