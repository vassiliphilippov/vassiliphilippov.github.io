feather reed grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛðɚ riːd ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈfɛðə riːd ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a tall ornamental grass of the genus Calamagrostis, known for its feathery flower spikes and use in landscaping
an ornamental grass with feathery spikes

Examples

The feather reed grass swayed gracefully in the garden breeze.
Many landscapers choose feather reed grass for its elegant appearance and hardy nature.
We planted several feather reed grasses along the pond to create a natural look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reed grass - any tall grass growing in wet places
