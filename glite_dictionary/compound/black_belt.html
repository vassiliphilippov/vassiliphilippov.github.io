black belt

1. NOUN

/ ˈblæk ˌbɛlt / (AmE), / ˈblæk ˌbɛlt / (BrE)
A person who has achieved the rank of expert in martial arts, especially judo or karate.
martial arts expert rank or holder

Examples

She is a black belt in karate and teaches classes twice a week.
After years of rigorous training, he finally earned his black belt.
The black belt demonstrated impressive technique during the competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: master - person with very high skill in a subject or art

2. NOUN

/ blæk bɛlt / (AmE), / blak belt / (BrE)
A rank or grading in martial arts such as judo or karate, signified by a black sash, denoting a high level of expertise or proficiency.
expert martial arts ranking

Examples

After years of rigorous training, she finally earned her black belt in karate.
He proudly wore his black belt during the martial arts demonstration.
Only those who have achieved the black belt can instruct at the dojo.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: brown belt - a martial arts rank below black belt
  • Degree Lower: white belt - the lowest rank in martial arts

Achieving the black belt requires both physical skill and mental discipline.
