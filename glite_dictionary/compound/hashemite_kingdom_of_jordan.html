Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan

1. PROPN

/ hæˈʃɛmɪt ˈkɪŋdəm əv ˈdʒɔrdn̩ / (AmE), / hæˈʃɛmɪt ˈkɪŋdəm əv ˈdʒɔːdn̩ / (BrE)
the official name of Jordan, an Arab kingdom in southwestern Asia bordering the Red Sea
official name of the country Jordan

Examples

The United Nations held a summit that included representatives from the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan.
Tourists frequently visit the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan to see Petra and the Dead Sea.
Trade agreements have strengthened ties between the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan and its neighboring countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jordan - country in the Middle East, common short form

Etymology

From the Hashemite dynasty, which rules the country, and 'Jordan', from the River Jordan.
