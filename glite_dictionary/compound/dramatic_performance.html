dramatic performance

1. NOUN

/ drəˈmætɪk pərˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / drəˈmætɪk pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
an event in which a drama or play is acted out before an audience
the performance of a play or drama

Examples

The school put on a dramatic performance of Shakespeare's Hamlet last spring.
Her debut in the dramatic performance was praised by both critics and the audience.
A captivating dramatic performance can transport viewers to another world entirely.

Synset Examples

the group joined together in a dramatic production

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theatrical performance - the public presentation of a drama or play
