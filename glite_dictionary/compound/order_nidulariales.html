order Nidulariales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər nɪˌdjuːləˈraɪəliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə nɪˌdjuːləˈraɪəliːz / (BrE)
A small order of basidiomycetous fungi that includes the families Nidulariaceae and Sphaerobolaceae.
fungal order including Nidulariaceae and Sphaerobolaceae

Examples

The order Nidulariales contains fungi commonly known as bird's nest fungi.
Researchers recently discovered a new species within the order Nidulariales.
Fungi from the order Nidulariales are often found on decaying wood in forests.

Etymology

From modern Latin Nidulariaceae (the genus Nidularia) and the taxonomic suffix -ales, naming an order.
