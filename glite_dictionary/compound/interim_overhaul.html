interim overhaul

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.tə.rɪm ˈoʊ.vɚˌhɔl / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tər.ɪm ˌəʊ.vəˈhɔːl / (BrE)
a maintenance procedure carried out between scheduled overhauls to ensure continued performance or prevent failure
maintenance between regular overhauls

Examples

The aircraft required an interim overhaul after showing signs of engine wear.
During the interim overhaul, technicians replaced several key components to prevent future problems.
Scheduling an interim overhaul helped the company avoid costly breakdowns later.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overhaul - thorough examination and repair of machinery
