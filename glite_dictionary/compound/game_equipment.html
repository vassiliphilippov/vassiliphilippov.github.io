game equipment

1. NOUN

/ ɡeɪm ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ɡeɪm ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
the tools, items, or apparatus needed to play a particular game
tools or apparatus for playing a game

Examples

The school purchased new game equipment for the sports teams this year.
Each board game comes with its own set of game equipment and instructions.
Properly maintaining game equipment ensures both safety and enjoyment during play.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equipment - the necessary items for a particular purpose
