Netherlands Antilles

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɛðərləndz ænˈtɪliz / (AmE), / ˈnɛðələndz ænˈtɪliːz / (BrE)
a former group of islands in the Caribbean, just north of Venezuela, that were administered by the Netherlands until their dissolution in 2010
former Caribbean island group, Dutch-administered

Examples

The Netherlands Antilles were dissolved in 2010, resulting in new political arrangements for the islands.
Tourists often visited the Netherlands Antilles for their beautiful beaches and vibrant culture.
He studied the history of colonial rule in the Netherlands Antilles for his thesis.

Etymology

From Dutch 'Nederlandse Antillen', referring to the Netherlands and the Antilles islands.
