Voyageurs National Park

1. PROPN

/ ˌvɔɪ.əˈʒɜrz ˌnæʃ.ə.nəl ˈpɑrk / (AmE), / ˌvwɑː.jəˈʒɜːz ˌnæʃ.ə.nəl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in northern Minnesota, United States, noted for its ancient rock formations, forests, and interconnected waterways
national park in Minnesota, USA

Examples

Every summer, thousands of people visit Voyageurs National Park to experience its pristine lakes and scenic views.
The lush forests of Voyageurs National Park are home to a diverse range of wildlife.
Camping in Voyageurs National Park offers a unique chance to explore Minnesota's natural beauty by canoe.

Etymology

Named after the French-Canadian fur traders known as 'voyageurs'.
