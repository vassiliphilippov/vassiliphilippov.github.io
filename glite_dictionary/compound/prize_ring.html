prize ring

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪz ˌrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpraɪz ˌrɪŋ / (BrE)
a square enclosure where boxers compete in prizefights; a boxing ring.
boxing ring for prizefights

Examples

The legendary fighter made his debut in the prize ring at just eighteen years old.
Many careers have been made and lost within the ropes of the prize ring.
The atmosphere inside the prize ring was electric as the championship fight began.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boxing ring - the area where boxing matches take place
