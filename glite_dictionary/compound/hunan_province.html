Hunan province

1. PROPN

/ ˈhuːnɑːn ˈprɑːvɪns / (AmE), / ˈhjuːnæn ˈprɒvɪns / (BrE)
A province in southeastern central China, known for its timber and mineral resources.
province in southeastern central China

Examples

Many tourists visit Hunan province to experience its spicy cuisine and scenic mountain ranges.
The government has invested heavily in infrastructure projects across Hunan province.
Timber from Hunan province is exported to several countries around the world.

Etymology

From Mandarin Chinese Húnán (湖南), meaning 'south of the lake' (referring to its position south of Dongting Lake).
