splash-guard

1. NOUN

/ ˈsplæʃ ɡɑrd / (AmE), / ˈsplæʃ ɡɑːd / (BrE)
A curved shield positioned above the wheel of a bicycle or motorcycle to protect the rider from water or mud thrown up by the wheels.
protective shield above vehicle wheels

Examples

The bicycle came with a splash-guard to keep mud from splattering the rider's clothes.
She replaced the splash-guard on her motorcycle after it was damaged in the rain.
Without a splash-guard, riding through puddles can make a real mess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fender - a part that covers and protects the wheel area
  • Synonym: mudguard - a British term for the same protective device
