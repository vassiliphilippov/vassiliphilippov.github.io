beta orbiter

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪtə ˈɔːrbɪtər / (AmE), / ˈbiːtə ˈɔːbɪtə / (BrE)
a man who regularly supports or admires a woman from the sidelines, usually without romantic or sexual reciprocation
man who supports woman without romantic success
slang

Examples

Jake realized he was just a beta orbiter after years of unrequited attention.
Some forums discuss how to avoid becoming a beta orbiter in online dating.
He spent months as her beta orbiter, always available but never more than a friend.

Synset Examples

Being a beta orbiter won't win you her heart.
She realized he was a beta orbiter, always in the background.
He was just a beta orbiter, never asking her out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nice guy - a man who claims to be kind and supportive but is unsuccessful romantically

Etymology

From 'beta' (referring to a perceived subordinate social status) + 'orbiter' (one who orbits, i.e., remains close by in hope or admiration).
