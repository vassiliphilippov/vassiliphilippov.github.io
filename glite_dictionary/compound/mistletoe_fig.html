mistletoe fig

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪsəlˌtoʊ fɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈmɪs(ə)ltəʊ fɪɡ / (BrE)
a tropical shrub or small tree (Ficus deltoidea), often grown as a houseplant, with leathery leaves resembling those of mistletoe
a houseplant with mistletoe-like leaves

Examples

The mistletoe fig thrives in bright, indirect sunlight and makes a great addition to any living room.
She watered her mistletoe fig every week to keep its waxy leaves healthy.
I bought a mistletoe fig because I love its unique, round foliage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ficus - a genus of about 850 species of woody trees and shrubs, including figs
