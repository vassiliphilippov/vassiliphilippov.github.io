downy yellow violet

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaʊni ˈjɛloʊ ˈvaɪəlɪt / (AmE), / ˈdaʊni ˈjɛləʊ ˈvaɪəlɪt / (BrE)
a violet native to eastern North America, with softly hairy leaves and stems and clear yellow flowers marked with brown-purple veins
a North American violet with yellow, downy leaves and stems

Examples

The downy yellow violet blooms early in the spring along shady woodland trails.
Gardeners admire the downy yellow violet for its distinctive hairy leaves and delicate yellow petals.
As we hiked through the forest, we spotted a cluster of downy yellow violets near the stream.
