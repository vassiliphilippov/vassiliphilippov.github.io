sex offense

1. NOUN

/ sɛks əˈfɛns / (AmE), / sɛks əˈfɛns / (BrE)
A crime in which a person knowingly causes another to engage in an unwanted sexual act by force or threat; broadly, any criminal sexual act as defined by law.
crime involving unwanted sexual acts
legal

Examples

In many jurisdictions, a sex offense carries severe penalties and requires registration as a sex offender.
The lawyer specialized in defending clients accused of sex offenses.
Education programs aim to prevent minors from becoming victims of sex offenses.

Synset Examples

most states have replaced the common law definition of rape with statutes defining sexual assault

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexual assault - unwanted sexual act carried out by force or threat
  • Synonym: rape - forced sexual intercourse without consent
