time of life

1. NOUN

/ taɪm əv laɪf / (AmE), / taɪm əv laɪf / (BrE)
a particular period or stage in a person's life, often marked by specific characteristics or experiences
a stage or phase in a person's life

Examples

In my youth, that was the best time of life to dream big and take risks.
Retirement is a time of life when many people travel and pursue hobbies.
Adolescence can be a confusing time of life as you explore your own identity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life stage - a distinct period of development in a person's life

2. NOUN

/ taɪm əv laɪf / (AmE), / taɪm əv laɪf / (BrE)
a period or occasion marked by great enjoyment and happiness; a memorable and pleasurable experience
a very enjoyable memorable experience

Examples

She had the time of her life on her trip to Paris last summer.
The kids were having the time of their lives at the amusement park.
Winning the championship was the time of his life.

Synset Examples

Their wedding was truly the time of their lives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: highlight - the most outstanding part of an event or period
  • Antonym: low point - a period of difficulty or unhappiness
