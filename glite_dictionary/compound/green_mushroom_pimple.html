green mushroom pimple

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrin ˈmʌʃˌrum ˈpɪmpəl / (AmE), / ˈɡriːn ˈmʌʃrʊm ˈpɪmpəl / (BrE)
A fungus (Hypomyces luteovirens) that infects mushrooms, forming a greenish mold on their surface.
fungus infecting mushrooms

Examples

The green mushroom pimple can often be seen covering the caps of wild mushrooms in damp forests.
Mycology students studied how the green mushroom pimple affects different species of edible fungi.
The presence of green mushroom pimple makes certain mushrooms unfit for human consumption.
