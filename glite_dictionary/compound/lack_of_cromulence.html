lack of cromulence

1. NOUN

/ læk əv ˈkɹɑmjələns / (AmE), / læk əv ˈkɹɒmjʊləns / (BrE)
The state or quality of not being appropriate, acceptable, or correct, often used humorously or ironically.
absence of acceptability or correctness (humorous)
informal slang

Examples

The proposal was rejected due to its lack of cromulence.
Critics pointed out the lack of cromulence in the newly adopted policy.
His speech was memorable for its passion but marked by a definite lack of cromulence.

Synset Examples

Her argument was criticized for its lack of cromulence.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cromulent - acceptable or legitimate (humorous, informal)
  • Synonym: inappropriateness - the state of not being appropriate

Etymology

Playful formation from 'cromulent', a coined word popularized by the TV show The Simpsons.
