core drill

1. NOUN

/ kɔr drɪl / (AmE), / kɔː drɪl / (BrE)
a drill designed to remove a cylindrical core of material from the object being drilled
drill that extracts a cylindrical core

Examples

The construction crew used a core drill to extract concrete samples from the building foundation.
We rented a core drill to make precise holes in the granite countertop.
A diamond-tipped core drill can cut through tough rock layers with ease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drill - a tool or machine for making holes
