last name

1. NOUN

/ læst neɪm / (AmE), / lɑːst neɪm / (BrE)
the part of a person's name that is shared with other members of their family, usually following the given name
family name; surname
american

Examples

Please write your last name clearly at the top of the form.
Her last name changed after she got married.
It's common for students to be called by their last name in some schools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surname - the family name shared by members of a family
  • Antonym: first name - the personal name given to someone at birth or baptism
