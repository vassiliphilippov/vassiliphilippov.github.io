Jesus hugger

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːzəs ˌhʌɡər / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːzəs ˌhʌɡə / (BrE)
An informal, sometimes slightly humorous term for a person who is very religious and affectionate about their Christian faith, especially showing strong devotion to Jesus.
very religious or devout Christian person
informal

Examples

Her grandmother is a proud Jesus hugger who never misses church on Sundays.
He was teased at work for being a Jesus hugger, but he didn't mind.
As a lifelong Jesus hugger, she sees her faith as central to her identity.

Synset Examples

Being a Jesus hugger, she volunteers at the shelter.
He's a real Jesus hugger, always spreading love.
The Jesus hugger gave me a warm embrace at the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bible basher - a derogatory term for a very religious Christian who aggressively promotes their views
  • Synonym: devout - having or showing deep religious feeling or commitment
