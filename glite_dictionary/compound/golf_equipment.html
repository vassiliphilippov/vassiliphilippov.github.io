golf equipment

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ɡɒlf ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
the collection of items such as clubs, balls, and accessories used for playing golf
items used to play golf

Examples

Before starting lessons, he invested in new golf equipment.
All golf equipment must meet strict tournament standards.
She borrowed some golf equipment from her friend for the weekend game.
