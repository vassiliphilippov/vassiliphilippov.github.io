beefsteak begonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈbifˌsteɪk bəˈɡoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈbiːfˌsteɪk bɪˈɡəʊniə / (BrE)
a begonia plant with round, fleshy leaves that are red underneath, grown as a houseplant for its ornamental foliage
ornamental begonia with red-tinged leaves

Examples

The beefsteak begonia is popular for its lush, colorful leaves.
She placed a beefsteak begonia on her office windowsill to brighten the space.
With proper care, a beefsteak begonia can thrive indoors for many years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: begonia - a plant of the genus Begonia, known for ornamental leaves and flowers
