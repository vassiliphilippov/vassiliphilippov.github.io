water-lily family

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ ˌlɪli ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌlɪli ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of dicotyledonous aquatic plants, known for their floating leaves and fragrant, often showy flowers, including the true water lilies (family Nymphaeaceae)
family of aquatic plants; Nymphaeaceae

Examples

The water-lily family includes many popular ornamental pond plants.
Researchers study the water-lily family for insights into aquatic plant evolution.
Flowers from the water-lily family are prized for their beauty and fragrance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nymphaeaceae - the scientific name for the water-lily family of aquatic plants
  • Degree Lower: water lily - an aquatic plant with large floating leaves and showy flowers
