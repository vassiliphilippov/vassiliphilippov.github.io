alfalfa sprout

1. NOUN

/ ælˈfælfə spaʊt / (AmE), / ælˈfalfə spaʊt / (BrE)
A sprouted seed of alfalfa, commonly eaten raw in salads and sandwiches.
sprouted seed of alfalfa plant

Examples

She added alfalfa sprout to her sandwich for a fresh, crunchy texture.
Many health enthusiasts enjoy salads topped with alfalfa sprout for added nutrition.
After five days, the alfalfa sprout were ready to harvest and eat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sprout - a young shoot from a seed
