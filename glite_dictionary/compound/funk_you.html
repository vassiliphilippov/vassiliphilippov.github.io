funk you

1. X

/ fʌŋk ju / (AmE), / fʌŋk juː / (BrE)
A playful or humorous expression used to show mock frustration, irritation, or as a light-hearted insult, often as a milder or less offensive variant of 'fuck you.'
playful mock insult or frustration
informal

Examples

When he lost the last round of Mario Kart, he threw up his hands and exclaimed, 'funk you!'
She laughed and said 'funk you!' when her friend stole the last slice of pizza.
After missing the shot, he mumbled 'funk you' under his breath, trying to keep the mood light.

Synset Examples

He jokingly said, 'Funk you!' after losing the game.
When he missed the bus, he just shouted, 'Funk you!'
In response to the rude comment, she replied, 'Funk you!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuck you - an offensive insult or expression of anger or contempt

Etymology

Formed by substituting 'funk' for the vulgar 'fuck' in the phrase 'fuck you,' as a euphemistic or playful alternative.
