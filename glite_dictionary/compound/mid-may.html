mid-May

1. NOUN

/ mɪd ˈmeɪ / (AmE), / mɪd ˈmeɪ / (BrE)
the period in the middle of the month of May
the middle part of May

Examples

The roses usually start to bloom in mid-May each year.
Their wedding is planned for mid-May, when the weather is pleasant.
Students often begin studying for finals in mid-May.
