loved one

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌvd ˌwʌn / (AmE), / ˈlʌvd ˌwʌn / (BrE)
a person whom you love, especially a family member or close friend
someone you love; family or close friend

Examples

During difficult times, it's important to be surrounded by your loved ones.
She visited her loved one in the hospital every evening.
People often cherish memories of their loved ones after they are gone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relative - a person connected by blood or marriage
  • Antonym: acquaintance - a person one knows but not close to
