Government Printing Office

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡʌvərnmənt ˈprɪntɪŋ ˈɑːfɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvəmənt ˈprɪntɪŋ ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
a former agency of the U.S. legislative branch that provided printing and binding services for Congress and federal departments
U.S. agency for government printing services
american

Examples

The Government Printing Office was responsible for supplying official publications to Congress.
Many historical documents were printed at the Government Printing Office in Washington, D.C.
The Government Printing Office was renamed the Government Publishing Office in 2014.
