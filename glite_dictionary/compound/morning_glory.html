morning glory

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˌɡlɔri / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˌɡlɒri / (BrE)
A climbing plant with funnel-shaped flowers that open in the morning and close late in the day, especially of the genus Ipomoea.
twining plant with funnel-shaped flowers

Examples

The morning glory in our garden blooms brightly every day before noon.
She planted blue morning glory seeds along the fence for a vibrant summer display.
By late afternoon, the morning glory flowers have usually begun to close.
