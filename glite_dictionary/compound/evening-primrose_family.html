evening-primrose family

1. NOUN

/ ˌivnɪŋ ˈprɪmˌroʊz ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˌiːvnɪŋ ˈprɪmrəʊz ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
A large family of flowering plants (Onagraceae) in the order Myrtales, widely distributed and including many herbaceous species such as the evening primrose.
family of flowering plants (Onagraceae)

Examples

The evening-primrose family includes many species found throughout North America.
Members of the evening-primrose family are known for their distinctive four-petaled flowers.
Botanists often study the evening-primrose family to learn about plant evolution and adaptation.
