banana peel

1. NOUN

/ bəˈnænə pil / (AmE), / bəˈnɑːnə piːl / (BrE)
the skin of a banana, especially when removed and discarded
skin of a banana, often discarded

Examples

He tossed the banana peel into the trash can after finishing his snack.
Someone carelessly dropped a banana peel on the sidewalk, creating a slippery hazard.
Cartoons often show characters slipping on a banana peel for comedic effect.

Synset Examples

he slipped on a banana skin and almost fell

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banana skin - the skin of a banana, especially after removal

2. NOUN

/ bəˈnænə pil / (AmE), / bəˈnɑːnə piːl / (BrE)
the skin of a banana, often used humorously to refer to something that causes someone to slip or fall
banana skin; humorous slipping hazard

Examples

The comedian pretended to trip on a banana peel during his performance, making the crowd laugh.
Watch your step—you might slip on a banana peel someone carelessly dropped on the sidewalk.
In cartoons, the villain often falls because of a surprise banana peel placed in his path.

Synset Examples

She slipped on a banana peel and fell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banana skin - the outer covering of a banana; British equivalent
