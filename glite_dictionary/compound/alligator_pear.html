alligator pear

1. NOUN

/ ˈælɪˌɡeɪtɚ pɛr / (AmE), / ˈælɪˌɡeɪtə pɛə / (BrE)
A tropical fruit with pear shape, green or dark skin, yellowish flesh, and a large central seed; also called an avocado.
another name for avocado fruit

Examples

The farmer picked a ripe alligator pear from the tree for breakfast.
Guacamole is traditionally made using fresh alligator pear as the main ingredient.
She sliced an alligator pear to add to her salad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avocado - a pear-shaped tropical fruit with leathery skin and a large seed

Etymology

Named for its rough, alligator-like skin and pear-shaped form.
