a bit

1. ADV

/ ə bɪt / (AmE), / ə bɪt / (BrE)
to a small extent; slightly; somewhat
to a small degree or extent

Examples

I'm feeling a bit tired after that long walk.
Could you turn the music down a bit, please?
The movie was a bit disappointing, but still worth watching.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: a little - to a small degree; slightly
  • Antonym: a lot - to a large extent; very much
