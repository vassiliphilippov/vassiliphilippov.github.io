Islamic State of Afghanistan

1. PROPN

/ ɪzˈlɑmɪk steɪt əv æfˈɡænɪstæn / (AmE), / ɪzˈlɑːmɪk steɪt əv æfˈɡænɪstæn / (BrE)
The official name of Afghanistan from 1992 to 2001, a mountainous landlocked country in central Asia bordered by Iran, Russia, and Pakistan.
historical name of Afghanistan (1992–2001)

Examples

The Islamic State of Afghanistan was recognized by the United Nations during most of the 1990s.
Leaders of the Islamic State of Afghanistan struggled to establish stability after the fall of the communist government.
Many refugees fled to neighboring countries during the civil war in the Islamic State of Afghanistan.

Synset Examples

Soviet troops invaded Afghanistan in 1979

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Afghanistan - country in central Asia
