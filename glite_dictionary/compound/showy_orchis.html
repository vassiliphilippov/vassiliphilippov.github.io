showy orchis

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊ.i ˈɔr.kɪs / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.i ˈɔː.kɪs / (BrE)
a North American orchid with a spike of violet-purple and white flowers, whose sepals and petals form a hood
North American orchid with purple and white flowers

Examples

We found a cluster of showy orchis beside the forest trail in spring.
The showy orchis blooms early, adding color to the woodland floor.
Botanists admire the delicate hood formed by the showy orchis petals.
