moments of doubt

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊmənts əv ˈdaʊt / (AmE), / ˈməʊmənts əv ˈdaʊt / (BrE)
short periods when a person feels uncertain or unsure, especially about a decision or situation
times of uncertainty or hesitation

Examples

Even the most experienced professionals face moments of doubt in their careers.
She had several moments of doubt before accepting the new job offer.
During the project, they all experienced moments of doubt about reaching the deadline.

Synset Examples

Every relationship will have its moments of doubt.
Everyone has their moments of doubt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doubt - uncertainty or lack of conviction
  • Antonym: certainty - the state of being confident or sure
