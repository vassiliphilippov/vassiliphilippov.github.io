harpoon log

1. NOUN

/ hɑrˈpun lɔg / (AmE), / hɑːˈpuːn lɒg / (BrE)
a cylindrical nautical instrument fitted with a device for registering the distance travelled by a vessel through water
nautical device registering distance travelled

Examples

The captain used the harpoon log to estimate the ship's progress across the Atlantic.
Before GPS, sailors relied on instruments like the harpoon log to gauge their distance travelled.
Maintenance of the harpoon log was essential for accurate navigation during long voyages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: log - a device for measuring a ship's speed or distance travelled
