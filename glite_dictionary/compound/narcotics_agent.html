narcotics agent

1. NOUN

/ nɑrˈkɑtɪks ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / nɑːˈkɒtɪks ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a law enforcement officer who investigates and enforces laws against illegal drug activities
law officer for drug violations

Examples

The narcotics agent went undercover to infiltrate the drug cartel.
Several narcotics agents testified in court about the investigation's findings.
As a narcotics agent, she specialized in combating the distribution of illegal drugs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: narcotics officer - a law officer who deals with illegal drugs
