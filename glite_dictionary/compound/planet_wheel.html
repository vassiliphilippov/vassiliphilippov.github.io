planet wheel

1. NOUN

/ ˈplænɪt wil / (AmE), / ˈplænɪt wiːl / (BrE)
an outer gear that revolves around the central sun gear in an epicyclic (planetary) gear system
gear in a planetary gear system

Examples

The planet wheel plays a crucial role in transmitting torque in a planetary gearbox.
During maintenance, the technician inspected each planet wheel for signs of wear.
Many automatic transmissions use a planet wheel configuration to provide smooth gear shifts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sun gear - the central gear in a planetary gear system
  • Synonym: epicyclic gear - a gear system in which gears (including planet wheels) rotate around a central sun gear

Etymology

Named for its motion resembling a planet orbiting a sun, from analogy to planetary motion.
