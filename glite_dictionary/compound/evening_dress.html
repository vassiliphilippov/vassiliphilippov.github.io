evening dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈivnɪŋ drɛs / (AmE), / ˈiːvnɪŋ drɛs / (BrE)
a formal dress or gown worn by women for evening social events or formal occasions
formal women's attire for evening events

Examples

She wore a stunning evening dress to the gala last night.
The invitation specified that evening dress was required for the dinner party.
Her red evening dress made her stand out at the formal event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gown - a woman's formal dress, especially one worn for formal occasions
  • Degree Lower: cocktail dress - a woman's dress suitable for semiformal occasions
