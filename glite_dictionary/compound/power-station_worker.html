power-station worker

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊɚ ˌsteɪʃən ˈwɝːkɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə ˌsteɪʃən ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who works at a power station, especially in roles related to the operation, maintenance, or management of energy generation facilities
person working at a power station

Examples

The power-station worker inspected the machinery to ensure everything was running smoothly.
After the storm, a power-station worker helped restore electricity to thousands of homes.
Being a power-station worker requires extensive safety training and technical knowledge.
