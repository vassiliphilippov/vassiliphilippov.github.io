urogenital system

1. NOUN

/ ˌjʊroʊˈdʒɛnətl̩ ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəʊˈdʒɛnɪtəl ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
the organ system in the body comprising both the reproductive and urinary organs, responsible for reproduction and the production and elimination of urine
system of reproductive and urinary organs
medical

Examples

The urogenital system plays a critical role in both reproduction and waste elimination.
Doctors examined the effects of the medication on the patient's urogenital system.
Some congenital disorders can affect the development of the urogenital system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genitourinary system - the organ system of reproductive and urinary organs; synonym for urogenital system

Etymology

From Greek 'ouron' (urine) and Latin 'genitalis' (pertaining to reproduction).
