blue green

1. NOUN

/ blu ˈɡrin / (AmE), / bluː ˈɡriːn / (BrE)
a color that is a blend of blue and green; a pigment or material of this color
a color between blue and green

Examples

She chose a blue green for the living room walls to create a calming atmosphere.
The artist mixed paints to get the perfect shade of blue green for the seascape.
A blue green gemstone sparkled in the sunlight on her necklace.
