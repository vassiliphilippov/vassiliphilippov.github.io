Assam rubber

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɑm ˈrʌbər / (AmE), / əˈsæm ˈrʌbə / (BrE)
A large tropical Asian tree (Ficus elastica), often grown as a houseplant, valued as a source of natural rubber.
tropical Asian tree; rubber source

Examples

The Assam rubber thrives in humid, warm climates and can reach impressive heights in the wild.
Many people keep Assam rubber as an indoor plant because it is easy to care for.
Historically, the Assam rubber was cultivated for its latex, which was used to produce natural rubber.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rubber plant - common name for Ficus elastica
