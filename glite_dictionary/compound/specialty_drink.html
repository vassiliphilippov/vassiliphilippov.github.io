specialty drink

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛʃəlti drɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃ(ə)lti drɪŋk / (BrE)
a beverage that is uniquely prepared, crafted, or offered, often featuring distinctive ingredients or presentation
uniquely crafted beverage

Examples

The café features a specialty drink each month to showcase seasonal ingredients.
He ordered the bartender's specialty drink, which came with an elaborate garnish.
Her favorite specialty drink at the coffee shop is a lavender honey latte.

Synset Examples

They ordered specialty drinks for one's foams at the café.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: signature drink - a drink that is characteristic of or unique to a particular place or person
