the bright side

1. NOUN

/ ðə braɪt saɪd / (AmE), / ðə braɪt saɪd / (BrE)
the positive or hopeful aspect of a situation, especially one that is otherwise difficult or negative
positive aspect of a situation

Examples

Even though it rained on our picnic, we tried to look at the bright side and enjoyed board games indoors.
When she lost her job, her friends helped her see the bright side—more time for family and new opportunities.
There's always the bright side to every problem, even if it's not obvious at first.

Synset Examples

She found the bright side in every tough situation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silver lining - a consoling or hopeful prospect in a bad situation
  • Antonym: downside - the negative aspect of something
