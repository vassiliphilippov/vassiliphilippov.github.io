vanilla orchid

1. NOUN

/ vəˈnɪlə ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / vəˈnɪlə ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
a climbing tropical orchid that produces the podlike fruits known as vanilla beans, widely cultivated in tropical regions
tropical orchid producing vanilla beans

Examples

The vanilla orchid is native to Mexico, but is now cultivated throughout tropical regions worldwide.
Farmers hand-pollinate the vanilla orchid to ensure high-quality vanilla beans for export.
She studied the growth habits of the vanilla orchid for her botany research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vanilla - the flavoring obtained from the pods of the vanilla orchid
