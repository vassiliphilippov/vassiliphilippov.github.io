removal firm

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈmuːvəl fɝːm / (AmE), / rɪˈmuːvəl fɜːm / (BrE)
a company that specializes in moving the possessions of individuals or businesses from one location to another
company specializing in moving possessions
british

Examples

We hired a removal firm to transport our furniture to the new house.
The removal firm arrived promptly and carefully packed all our belongings.
Choosing the right removal firm made our office move much less stressful.
