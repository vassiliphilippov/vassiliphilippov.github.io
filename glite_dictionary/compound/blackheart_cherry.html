blackheart cherry

1. NOUN

/ ˈblækˌhɑrt ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / ˈblakhɑːt ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
A variety of heart cherry with dark-colored flesh and skin.
a dark-fleshed variety of heart cherry

Examples

The farmer grew several blackheart cherry trees in his orchard.
Her favorite summer dessert is a tart made from blackheart cherry preserves.
The blackheart cherry has a rich, sweet flavor and deep red skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cherry - a small, round stone fruit, typically red or black
