articulated lorry

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːrˈtɪkjəˌleɪtɪd ˈlɔːri / (AmE), / ˌɑːˈtɪkjʊleɪtɪd ˈlɒri / (BrE)
A large vehicle with a cab and a trailer connected by a joint, used especially for transporting goods over long distances.
large truck with a cab and trailer
british

Examples

The articulated lorry blocked the road for hours after the accident.
Deliveries were delayed because an articulated lorry broke down on the motorway.
She had never driven an articulated lorry before starting her new job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: semi-trailer truck - A large truck consisting of a tractor and a semi-trailer; term used in American English
