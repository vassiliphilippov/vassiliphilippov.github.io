to sing in key

1. VERB

/ sɪŋ ɪn kiː / (AmE), / sɪŋ ɪn kiː / (BrE)
to sing notes at the correct pitch relative to the intended key of the music; to sing with accurate intonation.
sing at correct pitch

Examples

If you sing in key, the melody will sound harmonious with the band.
It's hard for beginners to sing in key without musical training.
She practiced daily to ensure she could sing in key at the concert.

Synset Examples

Make sure to sing in key during the performance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sing off-key - to sing at an incorrect pitch
