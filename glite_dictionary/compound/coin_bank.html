coin bank

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔɪn ˌbæŋk / (AmE), / ˈkɔɪn ˌbaŋk / (BrE)
A small container, often with a slot at the top, used for saving coins at home.
container for saving coins

Examples

Every time I had spare change, I put it in my coin bank.
His childhood coin bank was shaped like a pirate chest.
After months of saving, she counted all the money from her coin bank.

Synset Examples

the coin bank was empty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piggy bank - a small container for saving coins, often shaped like a pig
