emotional depth

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmoʊʃənəl dɛpθ / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənəl dɛpθ / (BrE)
the quality of having intense or profound feelings, especially as expressed in art, writing, or personal character
intensity and complexity of emotions

Examples

Her poetry is admired for its emotional depth and honesty.
The actor brought a surprising emotional depth to the character.
Many people are drawn to music that shows great emotional depth.

Synset Examples

He was captivated by the emotional depth of butoh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emotion - a strong feeling such as love, anger, joy, etc.
  • Antonym: emotional shallowness - lack of depth or intensity in feelings
