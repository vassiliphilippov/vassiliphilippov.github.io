autistic behavior

1. NOUN

/ ɔːˈtɪstɪk bɪˈheɪvjər / (AmE), / ɔːˈtɪstɪk bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
patterns of action or responses that are characteristic of autism spectrum disorder
actions typical of autism spectrum disorder
medical

Examples

Therapists work with children to help them understand and manage autistic behavior.
The school's staff receive training on recognizing and supporting students who display autistic behavior.
Strategies for addressing autistic behavior in the classroom are discussed during the workshop.

Synset Examples

Autistic behaviors vary greatly from person to person.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autism - a developmental disorder characterized by difficulties with social interaction and communication
