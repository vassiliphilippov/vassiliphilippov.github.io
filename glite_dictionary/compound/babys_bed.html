baby's bed

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪbiz bɛd / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbɪz bɛd / (BrE)
a small bed with high sides designed to keep a baby safe while sleeping
small bed with sides for a baby

Examples

We placed our newborn daughter gently in her baby's bed each night.
The baby's bed was set beside the parents' bed for easier nighttime care.
She decorated the nursery and bought a sturdy baby's bed with a colorful mobile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crib - a small bed with high sides for a baby
  • Synonym: cot - British term for a small enclosed bed for a baby
