cinnamon roll

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪnəmən roʊl / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəmən rəʊl / (BrE)
A sweet baked pastry made of rolled dough, filled with cinnamon and sugar, often with raisins, sliced and baked.
sweet pastry with cinnamon filling

Examples

She baked a fresh cinnamon roll for breakfast.
At the bakery, I ordered a warm cinnamon roll topped with icing.
The sweet scent of cinnamon rolls filled the kitchen.
