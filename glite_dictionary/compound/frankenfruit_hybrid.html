frankenfruit hybrid

1. NOUN

/ ˈfræŋkənfruːt ˈhaɪbrɪd / (AmE), / ˈfraŋkənfruːt ˈhʌɪbrɪd / (BrE)
A fruit, typically created through genetic modification or unusual crossbreeding, that is perceived as unnatural or artificial.
genetically modified or unusual hybrid fruit
informal

Examples

Some critics argue that frankenfruit hybrids may pose risks to biodiversity.
The company introduced a new frankenfruit hybrid that combines the flavors of plum and apricot.
Curious shoppers lined up to taste the latest frankenfruit hybrid at the fair.

Synset Examples

The market offered various frankenfruit hybrids this season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genetically modified organism - an organism whose genetic material has been altered by genetic engineering

Etymology

Blend of 'Frankenstein' (from Mary Shelley's novel, alluding to unnatural creation) and 'fruit', plus 'hybrid'.
