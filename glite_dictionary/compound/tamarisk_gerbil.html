tamarisk gerbil

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæmərɪsk ˈdʒɜrbəl / (AmE), / ˈtæmərɪsk ˈdʒɜːbɪl / (BrE)
a species of gerbil (Meriones unguiculatus) that is popular as a pet, known for its gentle temperament and ease of care
popular pet gerbil species

Examples

My children were thrilled when we brought home a tamarisk gerbil as their first pet.
The tamarisk gerbil is known for being easy to care for and quite social.
She researched the ideal habitat for a tamarisk gerbil before setting up its cage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gerbil - a small burrowing rodent often kept as a pet

Etymology

From 'tamarisk', a type of shrub common in the gerbil's native habitat, and 'gerbil', from French 'gerbille'.
