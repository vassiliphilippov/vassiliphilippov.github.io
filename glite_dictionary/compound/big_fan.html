big fan

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ fæn / (AmE), / bɪɡ fan / (BrE)
a person who is very enthusiastic about someone or something; an ardent admirer or supporter
ardent admirer or enthusiast

Examples

I've always been a big fan of her music and have all her albums.
My brother is a big fan of science fiction movies and never misses a new release.
She became a big fan of yoga after taking her first class last year.

Synset Examples

He's a big fan of the glam rock era, especially Gary Glitter.
He is a huge fan of the local football team.
I'm a huge fan of Squeenix's RPGs.
He's a huge fan of cricket and plays every Sunday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enthusiast - a person who is highly interested in a particular activity or subject
  • Synonym: supporter - a person who supports a particular person, group, or thing
  • Antonym: critic - a person who expresses unfavorable opinions about something
