pine lily

1. NOUN

/ paɪn ˈlɪli / (AmE), / paɪn ˈlɪli / (BrE)
a lily native to the southeastern United States, with cup-shaped flowers and deep yellow to scarlet recurved petals
lily with scarlet recurved petals of the southeastern US

Examples

The pine lily blooms brightly in the open pine woods each spring.
Gardeners admire the striking scarlet petals of the pine lily.
We spotted a cluster of pine lilies growing near the marsh.
