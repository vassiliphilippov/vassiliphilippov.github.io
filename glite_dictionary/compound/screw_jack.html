screw jack

1. NOUN

/ skruː dʒæk / (AmE), / skruː dʒæk / (BrE)
A mechanical lifting device that raises or lowers heavy loads by means of a screw mechanism.
device for lifting with a screw mechanism

Examples

The mechanic used a screw jack to lift the car for an oil change.
Always ensure the screw jack is on a stable surface before operating it.
A screw jack can safely carry a substantial amount of weight when used correctly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jack - general term for a mechanical lifting device
