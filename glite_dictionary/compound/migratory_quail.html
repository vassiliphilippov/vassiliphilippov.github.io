migratory quail

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪɡrəˌtɔri kweɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɪɡrəˌtɔːri kweɪl / (BrE)
an Old World quail species known for its seasonal migrations, particularly Coturnix coturnix
a species of migrating Old World quail

Examples

Every spring, researchers track the migratory quail as it travels across Europe and Asia.
Habitat loss has significantly impacted the routes of the migratory quail.
The migratory quail is prized among birdwatchers for its elusive nature and melodious call.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quail - a small, short-tailed game bird
