fully grown

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʊli ˈɡroʊn / (AmE), / ˈfʊli ˈɡrəʊn / (BrE)
(of animals, people, or plants) having reached full size or maturity; completely developed.
completely developed; mature

Examples

The cubs will leave their mother when they are fully grown.
A fully grown tree can provide plenty of shade in the summer.
It is hard to believe that such a large dog was once smaller and not yet fully grown.

Synset Examples

an adult animal
a grown woman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mature - having reached full natural growth or development
  • Antonym: immature - not fully developed or mature
