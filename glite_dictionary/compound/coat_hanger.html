coat hanger

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊt ˌhæŋər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊt ˌhæŋə / (BrE)
a device shaped like human shoulders, used for hanging coats and other garments to keep them in shape
device for hanging clothes

Examples

She hung her jacket on a coat hanger to keep it from wrinkling.
The closet was full of empty coat hangers after the guests left.
He crafted a sculpture out of twisted coat hangers for his art class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hanger - a device used for hanging clothes
