ice skating

1. NOUN

/ aɪs ˈskeɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / aɪs ˈskeɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or sport of moving on ice using ice skates
moving on ice with skates

Examples

We went ice skating at the frozen pond last winter.
She has been ice skating since she was six years old.
The local arena offers ice skating lessons for beginners.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: roller skating - skating using special shoes with wheels, typically on a smooth surface
  • Synonym: skating - moving over a surface on skates, including both ice and non-ice surfaces
