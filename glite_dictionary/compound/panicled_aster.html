panicled aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænɪkəld ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈpænɪkəld ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
a North American perennial plant (Symphyotrichum lanceolatum) with lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small white or pale purple daisy-like flowers.
a North American aster plant with small clustered flowers

Examples

During our hike, we spotted a panicled aster growing by the riverbank.
The garden featured several panicled asters that bloomed in late summer.
Botanists often study the panicled aster for its distinctive star-shaped flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a genus of flowering plants with star-shaped blooms
