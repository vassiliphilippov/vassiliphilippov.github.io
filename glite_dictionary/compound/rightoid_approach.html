rightoid approach

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtˌɔɪd əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈraɪtˌɔɪd əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
An attitude, stance, or method characterized by conservative or right-leaning political views.
conservative or right-leaning perspective
informal slang

Examples

Many students disagreed with the rightoid approach favored by the administration.
The journalist criticized the rightoid approach to economic reform in her column.
Her research explored the impact of a rightoid approach on education policy.

Synset Examples

She criticized the rightoid approach of the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conservative - favoring traditional values and views, especially in politics
  • Antonym: leftoid approach - an attitude or method characterized by left-leaning or progressive views

Etymology

From 'rightoid', a blend of 'right' (politically conservative) and '-oid' (resembling), patterned after 'leftoid'.
