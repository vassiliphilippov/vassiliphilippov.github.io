shoe polish

1. NOUN

/ ʃu ˈpɑlɪʃ / (AmE), / ʃuː ˈpɒlɪʃ / (BrE)
a substance applied to footwear to produce a shiny, protective surface
substance for shining and protecting shoes

Examples

He applied shoe polish to his boots before the interview.
You can buy brown or black shoe polish at most stores.
A soft cloth works best for buffing the shoe polish to a shine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polish - substance for creating smooth or shiny surface
