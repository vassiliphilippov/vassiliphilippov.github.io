planning commission

1. NOUN

/ ˈplænɪŋ kəˌmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈplænɪŋ kəˌmɪʃən / (BrE)
a group of officials responsible for making proposals or decisions on future activities and developments, especially in urban or regional planning
official group for development planning

Examples

The city’s planning commission approved new guidelines for residential developments.
She attended a meeting of the planning commission to voice concerns about the new shopping center.
The planning commission plays a vital role in shaping the future of urban spaces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commission - an official group with authority to perform a particular function
