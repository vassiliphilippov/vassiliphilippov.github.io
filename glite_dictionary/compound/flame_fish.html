flame fish

1. NOUN

/ fleɪm fɪʃ / (AmE), / fleɪm fɪʃ / (BrE)
a species of cardinalfish native to tropical Atlantic coastal waters, known for its bright coloration
a brightly colored cardinalfish of the Atlantic

Examples

While snorkeling, I spotted a stunning flame fish darting among the coral reefs.
The aquarium features several vibrant flame fish in its tropical marine exhibit.
Marine biologists are studying the behavior of the flame fish in its natural habitat.
