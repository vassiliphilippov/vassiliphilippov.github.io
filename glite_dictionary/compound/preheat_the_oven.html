to preheat the oven

1. VERB

/ priːˈhiːt ði ˈʌvən / (AmE), / priːˈhiːt ði ˈʌvn̩ / (BrE)
to heat an oven to a specified temperature before using it to cook food
heat oven before cooking

Examples

It's important to preheat the oven to the correct temperature before starting the recipe.
Did you remember to preheat the oven for the lasagna?
Always preheat the oven before putting in the cake batter.

Synset Examples

Please preheat the oven before baking the cookies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to preheat - to heat something beforehand, especially an appliance
