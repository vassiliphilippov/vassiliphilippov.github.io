portrait painter

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔr.trɪt ˌpeɪn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɔː.trɪt ˌpeɪn.tə / (BrE)
An artist who specializes in painting or drawing portraits of people.
artist who paints portraits

Examples

The portrait painter captured the subtle expressions of his sitter perfectly in oil.
As a portrait painter, she received many commissions from wealthy families.
He dreamed of becoming a famous portrait painter like those of the Renaissance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: portraitist - an artist who paints or draws portraits
