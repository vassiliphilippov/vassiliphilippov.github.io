exceptional craftsmanship

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈsɛpʃənəl ˈkræftsmænˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛpʃənəl ˈkrɑːftsmənʃɪp / (BrE)
the quality of displaying highly superior skill and artistry in creating or making things
superior skill in making things

Examples

The antique cabinet is a perfect example of exceptional craftsmanship.
Collectors are always looking for artwork that displays exceptional craftsmanship.
Her quilts are prized for their exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Synset Examples

Adam Tiler was known for his exceptional craftsmanship.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: craftsmanship - the quality of design and work shown in something made by hand
