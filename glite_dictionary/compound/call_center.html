call center

1. NOUN

/ kɔl ˈsɛntɚ / (AmE), / kɔːl ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
a facility where large numbers of telephone calls are handled, especially for providing customer service or taking orders
facility for handling customer phone calls

Examples

She works in a call center answering customer questions about their orders.
Many companies use a call center to provide round-the-clock technical support.
The bank's call center received thousands of calls during the outage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contact center - a center handling customer communications by various channels, not just phone
