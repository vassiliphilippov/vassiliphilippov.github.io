chair of state

1. NOUN

/ tʃɛr əv steɪt / (AmE), / tʃɛːr əv steɪt / (BrE)
a ceremonial chair, often elaborately designed, reserved for a sovereign, dignitary, or person of high rank
ceremonial chair for a powerful person

Examples

The king entered the hall and sat upon the chair of state as the ceremony began.
The ornate chair of state was displayed in the museum alongside royal regalia.
Each new president is seated in the chair of state during the inauguration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: throne - a ceremonial chair for a monarch or ruler
