tough workout

1. NOUN

/ tʌf ˈwɜrkaʊt / (AmE), / tʌf ˈwɜːkaʊt / (BrE)
an exercise session that is physically challenging and demanding
a challenging exercise session

Examples

After a tough workout, she likes to drink a protein shake to recover.
The coach promised today would be a tough workout, and he wasn't kidding.
Stretching before and after a tough workout can help prevent injuries.

Synset Examples

Ug, that was a tough workout today!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hard workout - an exercise session that is physically difficult
  • Antonym: easy workout - an exercise session that is not physically demanding
