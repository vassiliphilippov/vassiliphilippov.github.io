baton twirler

1. NOUN

/ bəˈtɑn ˈtwɝːlər / (AmE), / bəˈtɒn ˈtwɜːlə / (BrE)
a person who performs with and twirls a baton, especially in parades or competitions
someone who twirls a baton

Examples

The baton twirler wowed the crowd with her elaborate tricks during the halftime show.
As a child, he dreamed of becoming a baton twirler in the local parade.
The marching band featured a talented baton twirler leading the procession.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: majorette - a female baton twirler, especially in a marching band
