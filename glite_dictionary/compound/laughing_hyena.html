laughing hyena

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæfɪŋ haɪˈinə / (AmE), / ˈlɑːfɪŋ haɪˈiːnə / (BrE)
A large African hyena (spotted hyena) known for its distinctive, human-like, laughing call.
spotted African hyena with laughing call

Examples

The laughing hyena is famous for the eerie, giggling sounds it makes at night.
We saw a pack of laughing hyenas while on safari in the Serengeti.
Despite its nickname, the laughing hyena can be a fierce and efficient hunter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotted hyena - a large African hyena with spotted fur and a 'laughing' call
