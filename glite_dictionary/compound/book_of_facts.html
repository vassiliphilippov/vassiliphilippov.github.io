book of facts

1. NOUN

/ bʊk əv fækts / (AmE), / bʊk əv fækts / (BrE)
a book containing a collection of authoritative information or reliable factual data for reference purposes
reference book of authoritative facts

Examples

I often consult my book of facts when settling debates with friends.
The teacher recommended a book of facts as a reliable study resource.
Every library should have at least one comprehensive book of facts on its shelves.

Synset Examples

he contributed articles to the basic reference work on that topic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reference book - a book you can look at to find information
