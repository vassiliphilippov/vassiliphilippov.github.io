sharp-eyed

1. ADJ

/ ʃɑrp aɪd / (AmE), / ʃɑːp aɪd / (BrE)
Having keen or acute eyesight; able to notice small details or objects easily.
having keen eyesight

Examples

The detective was sharp-eyed and quickly noticed the tiny clue on the windowsill.
Only the most sharp-eyed players can spot the hidden details in this game.
Despite her age, she remains remarkably sharp-eyed and often finds objects others have lost.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keen-eyed - having sharp or acute eyesight
  • Synonym: sharp-sighted - possessing keen sight or perception
  • Antonym: short-sighted - unable to see things clearly unless they are close
