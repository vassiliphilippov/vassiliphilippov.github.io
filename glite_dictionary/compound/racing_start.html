racing start

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪsɪŋ stɑrt / (AmE), / ˈreɪsɪŋ stɑːt / (BrE)
the moment at which a race officially begins, typically marked by a signal or command
the official beginning of a race

Examples

The runners crouched at the racing start, waiting for the gun to go off.
A false racing start can lead to disqualification in many competitions.
She practiced her racing start to gain an advantage at the beginning of the swim meet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: start - the point in time at which something begins
