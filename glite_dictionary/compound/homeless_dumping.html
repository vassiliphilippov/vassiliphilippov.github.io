homeless dumping

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊmləs ˈdʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmləs ˈdʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of discarding waste or garbage by homeless individuals in public spaces.
discarding waste by homeless individuals

Examples

The city council proposed new sanitation policies to address homeless dumping downtown.
Residents have complained about increased homeless dumping near the waterfront.
Efforts to reduce homeless dumping include providing more public bins and outreach programs.

Synset Examples

The city is facing issues with homeless dumping near parks.
