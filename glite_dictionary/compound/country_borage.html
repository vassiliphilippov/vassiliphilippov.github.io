country borage

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌntri ˈbɔrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri ˈbɒrɪdʒ / (BrE)
an aromatic, fleshy-leaved herb native to parts of India, Sri Lanka, and South Africa, sometimes classified under the genus Plectranthus
aromatic herb native to South Asia and Africa

Examples

Country borage is widely used in traditional Indian medicine for its therapeutic properties.
The fleshy leaves of country borage add a distinctive aroma to curries and soups.
Gardeners appreciate country borage for its resilience and ornamental foliage.
