American water spaniel

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən ˈwɔtər ˈspæniəl / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈwɔːtə ˈspæniəl / (BrE)
a breed of medium-sized spaniel, developed in the United States, with a curly chocolate or liver-colored coat, known for its hunting and retrieving abilities
American breed of spaniel dog

Examples

The American water spaniel is known for its intelligence and enthusiasm for swimming.
As a puppy, their American water spaniel quickly learned to retrieve objects from the lake.
Many hunters appreciate the versatility of the American water spaniel in the field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spaniel - a type of dog bred primarily as a gun dog and companion
