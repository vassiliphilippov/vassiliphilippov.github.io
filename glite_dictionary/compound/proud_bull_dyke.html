proud bull dyke

1. NOUN

/ praʊd bʊl daɪk / (AmE), / praʊd bʊl daɪk / (BrE)
a lesbian woman who is openly confident, assertive, and unapologetically masculine in presentation, typically reclaiming a term that is often considered offensive
confident, assertive masculine lesbian woman
offensive

Examples

She identified as a proud bull dyke and never hesitated to speak her mind.
Many young lesbians looked up to her as a proud bull dyke who paved the way for others.
Despite criticism from some, she embraced her identity as a proud bull dyke with unwavering self-assurance.

Synset Examples

They admired her confidence as a proud bull dyke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dyke - a slang term for lesbian, sometimes offensive or reclaimed
  • Synonym: bull dyke - a masculine lesbian woman, usually assertive or butch, sometimes offensive or reclaimed
  • Antonym: femme - a lesbian woman who adopts a more traditionally feminine gender expression

Etymology

From 'bull dyke,' a term of obscure origin, often reclaimed by lesbians. 'Proud' denotes confidence and self-acceptance.
