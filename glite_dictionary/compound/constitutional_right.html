constitutional right

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑn.stəˈtuː.ʃə.nəl raɪt / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.stɪˈtjuː.ʃən.əl raɪt / (BrE)
A right guaranteed and protected by a country's constitution, especially those related to individual freedoms and legal protections.
right protected by a constitution
legal

Examples

The lawyer argued that her constitutional right to free speech had been violated.
Citizens are protected by constitutional rights that cannot be taken away by ordinary legislation.
He filed a lawsuit to defend his constitutional right to due process.

Synset Examples

His constitutional rights were violated during the arrest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: civil right - a right protecting an individual's freedom from infringement by governments, social organizations, and private individuals
  • Synonym: legal right - a right recognized and enforceable by law
