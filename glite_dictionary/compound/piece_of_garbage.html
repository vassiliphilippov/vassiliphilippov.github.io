piece of garbage

1. NOUN

/ pis əv ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ / (AmE), / piːs əv ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ / (BrE)
an object regarded as worthless or useless
worthless or useless object

Examples

He threw the piece of garbage into the recycling bin without a second thought.
She called the broken radio a piece of garbage and tossed it out.
Don’t let anyone tell you your work is a piece of garbage; you worked hard on it.

Synset Examples

He referred to the old chair as a piece of garbage.
Don't throw that piece of garbage on the ground.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: junk - old or discarded items; things of little value
  • Synonym: trash - waste material; something worthless
  • Antonym: treasure - something of great value or worth
