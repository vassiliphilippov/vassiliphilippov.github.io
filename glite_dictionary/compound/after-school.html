after-school

1. ADJ

/ ˈæf.tɚ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈɑːf.tə skuːl / (BrE)
Taking place or happening after regular school hours, especially referring to activities or time spent after school has ended.
taking place after regular school hours

Examples

My son attends an after-school program while I finish work.
Many students participate in sports as an after-school activity.
We offer tutoring every weekday during after-school hours.

Synset Examples

The kids were restless after-school today.
