Battle of Iwo Jima

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæt̬l əv ˌiːwoʊ ˈdʒiːmə / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˌiːwəʊ ˈdʒiːmə / (BrE)
a major World War II battle in 1945 in which American forces captured the island of Iwo Jima from Japanese defenders after intense fighting
World War II battle on Iwo Jima island (1945)

Examples

The Battle of Iwo Jima was one of the fiercest fights in the Pacific during World War II.
Many war memorials commemorate the soldiers who fought in the Battle of Iwo Jima.
The iconic photograph of U.S. Marines raising the flag was taken during the Battle of Iwo Jima.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Battle of Okinawa - a major World War II battle on Okinawa Island
  • Synonym: Battle of Midway - a pivotal naval battle in the Pacific during World War II
  • Synonym: Pacific War - the theater of World War II fought in the Pacific and Asia
