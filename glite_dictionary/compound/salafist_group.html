Salafist Group

1. PROPN

/ səˈlɑː.fɪst gruːp / (AmE), / səˈlæ.fɪst ɡruːp / (BrE)
the former name of an Algerian Islamist militant organization, known for its terrorist activities and association with al-Qaeda, seeking to overthrow the Algerian government
Algerian Islamist militant organization

Examples

The Salafist Group was responsible for numerous attacks in northern Algeria in the early 2000s.
Several European governments have increased surveillance of organizations linked to the Salafist Group.
After rebranding, the Salafist Group became part of a broader global jihadist movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: al-Qaeda - a global militant Islamist organization
  • Synonym: Armed Islamic Group - an earlier Algerian Islamist militant organization

Etymology

From Arabic 'Salaf' (predecessors) plus English 'group'; refers to members following Salafism, a conservative Islamic movement.
