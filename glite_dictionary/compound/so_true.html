so true

1. X

/ soʊ truː / (AmE), / səʊ truː / (BrE)
used to express strong agreement with a preceding statement or sentiment
expression of strong agreement
informal

Examples

She said the weather affects your mood—so true!
'Honesty is the best policy.' 'So true,' he replied, nodding.
When my friend said laughter is the best medicine, I could only say, 'so true.'

Synset Examples

When she said friendships matter most, I replied, 'So true, bestie!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absolutely - completely agree; total agreement
  • Synonym: exactly - used to show strong agreement or confirmation
