lake herring

1. NOUN

/ leɪk ˈhɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / leɪk ˈhɛrɪŋ / (BrE)
a whitefish found in cold deep lakes of North America, valued as a food fish
a whitefish found in North American lakes

Examples

Fishermen caught several lake herring during their expedition on Lake Superior.
Many recipes from the region feature lake herring as the main ingredient.
The population of lake herring has declined in some lakes due to overfishing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whitefish - a general term for several species of freshwater fish, including the lake herring

2. NOUN

/ leɪk ˈhɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / leɪk ˈhɛrɪŋ / (BrE)
a cold-water fish (Coregonus artedi) found in Lake Superior and northern waters, valued commercially for its meat
a cold-water fish found in northern lakes

Examples

Commercial fisheries harvest large numbers of lake herring each year in Lake Superior.
The population of lake herring has declined due to environmental changes.
Fishermen in Canada often catch lake herring because of its delicate flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cisco - another name for lake herring
