tech industry

1. NOUN

/ tɛk ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / tɛk ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector of business that focuses on developing, producing, or selling technology and related services or products
sector for technology-related businesses

Examples

Many people dream of working in the tech industry because of its rapid growth and innovation.
The tech industry attracts talented engineers from all around the world.
New regulations could have a major impact on the tech industry in the coming years.

Synset Examples

His ideas as an unorthodox entrepreneur revolutionized the tech industry.
Micro$oft products dominate the tech industry.
Czechnology is revolutionizing the tech industry in Prague.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: technology sector - business sector involving technology companies and services

Etymology

Formed from 'tech', a shortening of 'technology', and 'industry'. 'Tech' originated in the early 20th century.
