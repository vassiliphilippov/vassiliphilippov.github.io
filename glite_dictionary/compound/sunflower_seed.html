sunflower seed

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌflaʊər siːd / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌflɑːwə siːd / (BrE)
the edible seed of a sunflower, eaten as a snack, used in cooking, or pressed for sunflower oil
edible seed of a sunflower

Examples

She sprinkled sunflower seeds on her salad for extra crunch and flavor.
Many birds enjoy eating sunflower seeds from garden feeders.
Sunflower seeds can be pressed to produce a light and healthy oil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pumpkin seed - edible seed of a pumpkin plant
