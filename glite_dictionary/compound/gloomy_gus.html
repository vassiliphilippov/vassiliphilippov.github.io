gloomy Gus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡluːmi ɡʌs / (AmE), / ˈɡluːmi ɡʌs / (BrE)
a person who is habitually sullen, pessimistic, or gloomy
habitually gloomy person
informal

Examples

Don't be such a gloomy Gus — things will get better soon.
Whenever it rains, he turns into a real gloomy Gus and refuses to go out.
After hearing the bad news, she became a gloomy Gus for the rest of the week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grump - a person who is easily annoyed or complains a lot
  • Antonym: optimist - a person with a positive outlook

Etymology

From 'gloomy' (sad or dark) and the given name 'Gus', popularized in early 20th-century America.
