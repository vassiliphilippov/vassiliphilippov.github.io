tax revenue

1. NOUN

/ tæks ˈrɛvənju / (AmE), / taks ˈrɛvənjuː / (BrE)
income that a government receives from taxes on individuals, businesses, goods, or services
government income from taxes

Examples

The government increased tax revenue by raising the sales tax last year.
Many public services are funded using tax revenue collected from citizens.
A decrease in employment can lead to lower tax revenue for the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tax - a compulsory contribution to state revenue
  • Synonym: revenue - income, especially of an organization or government
