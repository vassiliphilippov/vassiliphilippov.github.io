volleyball court

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɑːlˌbɔl kɔrt / (AmE), / ˈvɒlɪˌbɔːl kɔːt / (BrE)
an area marked with lines where the game of volleyball is played
area where volleyball is played

Examples

The teams met at the volleyball court for practice every Saturday morning.
She reserved the volleyball court for her friends during the summer.
A net divides the volleyball court into two equal halves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basketball court - area where basketball is played
  • Synonym: tennis court - area where tennis is played
