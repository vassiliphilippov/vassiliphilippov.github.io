ground beef

1. NOUN

/ ɡraʊnd bif / (AmE), / ɡraʊnd biːf / (BrE)
Beef that has been minced or finely chopped, typically used in cooking.
beef that has been minced or chopped

Examples

She made hamburgers using fresh ground beef from the market.
The recipe calls for one pound of ground beef and several vegetables.
Taco night always includes seasoned ground beef as a filling option.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minced beef - British term for ground beef; beef that has been minced
