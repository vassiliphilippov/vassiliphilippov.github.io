dog hook

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ hʊk / (AmE), / dɒɡ hʊk / (BrE)
a sturdy lever with a sharp spike used for handling and moving logs
tool for handling logs

Examples

The lumberjack used a dog hook to roll the heavy logs across the forest floor.
You need a sturdy dog hook when working with large timber.
He learned how to grip the dog hook properly to avoid injury while moving logs.
