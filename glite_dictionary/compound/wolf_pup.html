wolf pup

1. NOUN

/ wʊlf pʌp / (AmE), / wʊlf pʌp / (BrE)
A young wolf; a baby wolf.
young wolf

Examples

The wildlife biologist gently examined the tiny wolf pup before returning it to the den.
A wolf pup relies on its mother for food and protection during its first few months.
We spotted a playful wolf pup chasing butterflies in the forest clearing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pup - a young dog or certain other mammals
  • Synonym: cub - the young of certain animals, such as bears or big cats
  • Synonym: wolf - a wild carnivorous mammal of the dog family
