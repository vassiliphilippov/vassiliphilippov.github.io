finishing school

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ skuːl / (BrE)
a private school for girls that specializes in teaching social graces, cultural activities, and etiquette to prepare them for society
private school for girls, focusing on social and cultural training

Examples

After attending a finishing school in Switzerland, she became well known for her poise and charm.
Her mother insisted that she enroll in a finishing school to learn proper etiquette and social skills.
In the past, many aristocratic families sent their daughters to a finishing school before they entered society.
