crepe fern

1. NOUN

/ kreɪp fɜrn / (AmE), / kreɪp fɜːn / (BrE)
a fern found in the rain forests of tropical Australia, New Zealand, and South Africa, known for its delicate, crinkled fronds
tropical fern with crinkled fronds

Examples

The crepe fern thrives in the humid understory of Australian rainforests.
Many gardeners appreciate the delicate appearance of the crepe fern in shaded gardens.
Botanists documented the distribution of the crepe fern across both New Zealand and South Africa.
