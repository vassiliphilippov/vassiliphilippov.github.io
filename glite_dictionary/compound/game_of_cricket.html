game of cricket

1. NOUN

/ ɡeɪm əv ˈkrɪkɪt / (AmE), / ɡeɪm əv ˈkrɪkɪt / (BrE)
an occasion when people play the sport of cricket against each other; a match of cricket
a cricket match

Examples

After lunch, the children set up stumps for a game of cricket in the garden.
The park was busy with people watching a local game of cricket.
He invited his friends to join a game of cricket on Saturday afternoon.

Synset Examples

They organized a game of cric after work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cricket match - an organized cricket game between two teams
