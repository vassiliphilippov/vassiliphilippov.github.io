Pacific ridley

1. NOUN

/ pəˈsɪfɪk ˈrɪdli / (AmE), / pəˈsɪfɪk ˈrɪdli / (BrE)
An olive-colored species of sea turtle found in the tropical Pacific, Indian, and southern Atlantic Oceans.
species of olive-colored sea turtle

Examples

The Pacific ridley faces threats from fishing nets and habitat destruction.
Researchers released several juvenile Pacific ridleys into the ocean to increase their population.
The Pacific ridley is known for its unique nesting behavior on tropical beaches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: olive ridley - a similar olive-colored sea turtle species
