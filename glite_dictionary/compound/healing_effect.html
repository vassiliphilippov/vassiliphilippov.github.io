healing effect

1. NOUN

/ ˈhilɪŋ ɪˈfɛkt / (AmE), / ˈhiːlɪŋ ɪˈfɛkt / (BrE)
a beneficial influence or result that helps to cure or restore health
beneficial effect on health; curative result

Examples

Many people believe in the healing effect of meditation and mindfulness.
Some essential oils are said to have a healing effect on the skin.
The music had a surprisingly powerful healing effect on his mood.

Synset Examples

The herbalist recommended saxifrax for its healing effects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: therapeutic effect - an effect that contributes to healing or treatment
