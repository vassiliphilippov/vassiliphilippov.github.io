cloth cap

1. NOUN

/ klɔθ kæp / (AmE), / klɒθ kap / (BrE)
a flat cap made of wool or similar fabric, with a stiff peak
flat woolen cap with a stiff peak

Examples

He wore a cloth cap pulled low over his eyes as he walked through the market.
Many traditional British workers were known for their distinctive cloth caps.
She found her grandfather's old cloth cap in the attic and decided to keep it as a memento.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flat cap - a rounded cap with a small stiff brim in front
