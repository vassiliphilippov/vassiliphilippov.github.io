binder board

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪndər bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbaɪndə bɔːd / (BrE)
A firm cardboard material used by bookbinders to make the covers of books.
cardboard used in book covers

Examples

The old library uses high-quality binder board for their archival book repairs.
You will need a sturdy binder board to construct a durable book cover.
Conservators replaced the damaged cover with new binder board.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bookboard - a board used for the cover of a book
