aerial ladder truck

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛriəl ˈlædər trʌk / (AmE), / ˈeərɪəl ˈladə trʌk / (BrE)
a fire engine equipped with an extendable, mechanical ladder used for reaching high places during firefighting or rescue operations
fire engine with an extendable ladder

Examples

The firefighters used the aerial ladder truck to rescue people from the burning apartment building.
A new aerial ladder truck was added to the city’s firefighting fleet.
Demonstrations showed how the aerial ladder truck could reach the top floors of tall structures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fire engine - a vehicle equipped for firefighting
