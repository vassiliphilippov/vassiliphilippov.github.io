Red Notice

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈnoʊtɪs / (AmE), / rɛd ˈnəʊtɪs / (BrE)
an official request issued by Interpol for the arrest of a wanted person, with a view to extradition, distributed to all member countries
Interpol notice seeking arrest of a wanted person
legal

Examples

The fugitive was apprehended after Interpol issued a Red Notice at the request of the authorities.
A Red Notice can lead to the arrest and extradition of suspects in many countries.
After years on the run, he was finally located when his name appeared on a Red Notice.

Synset Examples

an Interpol Red Notice is the closest instrument to an international arrest warrant in use today

Related Terms

  • Synonym: international arrest warrant - an official order for the arrest of an individual across international borders
