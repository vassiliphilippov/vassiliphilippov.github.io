satyr orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪtɚ ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈseɪtə ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A type of orchid with broad, ovate leaves and long-bracted, green, very irregular flowers, commonly found in southern Africa.
a type of orchid with broad leaves and irregular flowers

Examples

The botanical garden added a rare satyr orchid to its collection this year.
Many enthusiasts travel to South Africa to see the satyr orchid blooming in the wild.
With its striking, irregular flowers, the satyr orchid easily attracts the attention of visitors.

Etymology

Named for the figure 'satyr' from Greek myth, referring to the flower's fanciful appearance.
