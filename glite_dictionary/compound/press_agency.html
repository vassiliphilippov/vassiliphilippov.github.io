press agency

1. NOUN

/ prɛs ˈeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / prɛs ˈeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
an organization that collects and distributes news reports to newspapers and other media outlets
organization that supplies news to media

Examples

Reuters is a well-known press agency providing news to organizations around the world.
The newspaper subscribes to several press agencies to access international reports.
A press agency can quickly distribute breaking news to multiple media platforms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news agency - organization supplying news reports to media
