car traffic

1. NOUN

/ kɑr ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / kɑː ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
the movement or flow of cars on roads, especially as part of regular road traffic
the flow of cars on roads

Examples

Heavy car traffic made it difficult to cross the street during rush hour.
The city implemented new measures to reduce car traffic in the downtown area.
We heard the noise of car traffic even late at night from our apartment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traffic - the movement of vehicles, ships, aircraft, or people
