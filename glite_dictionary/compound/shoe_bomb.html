shoe bomb

1. NOUN

/ ʃu bɑm / (AmE), / ʃuː bɒm / (BrE)
an explosive device hidden inside the heel or sole of a shoe, designed to be detonated covertly
explosive device hidden in a shoe

Examples

The airport installed new scanners to detect shoe bombs hidden in passengers' footwear.
Security officials prevented an attempted shoe bomb attack on an international flight.
He was arrested after trying to board a plane with a shoe bomb concealed in his sneakers.
