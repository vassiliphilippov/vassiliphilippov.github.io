turnip bed

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝː.nɪp bɛd / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.nɪp bed / (BrE)
an area of soil prepared and planted for growing turnips
garden bed for turnips

Examples

We planted seeds in the turnip bed last spring, and now the vegetables are ready to harvest.
After weeding the turnip bed, she carefully watered the young plants.
A fence was built around the turnip bed to keep rabbits away.
