professional baseball

1. NOUN

/ prəˌfɛʃənəl ˈbeɪsˌbɔl / (AmE), / prəˌfɛʃənəl ˈbeɪsbɔːl / (BrE)
Baseball played by paid athletes as their occupation, rather than for recreation or as amateurs.
Baseball played for money

Examples

Many young athletes dream of playing professional baseball after college.
He signed a contract to join a professional baseball team last year.
The popularity of professional baseball continues to grow around the world.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: amateur baseball - Baseball played without financial compensation
