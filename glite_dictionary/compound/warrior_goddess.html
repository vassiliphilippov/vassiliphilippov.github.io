warrior goddess

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔriər ˈɡɑdəs / (AmE), / ˈwɒriə ˈɡɒdɪs / (BrE)
a female deity in mythology who is associated with war and combat
female deity associated with war

Examples

In ancient mythology, Athena is revered as a warrior goddess and protector of cities.
The statue depicted a fierce warrior goddess brandishing a spear.
Across cultures, stories of a warrior goddess inspire women to embrace strength and courage.

Synset Examples

Athena is often depicted as a warrior goddess in myths.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: war goddess - a goddess associated with war
