tornado cellar

1. NOUN

/ tɔːrˈneɪdoʊ ˈsɛlɚ / (AmE), / tɔːˈneɪdəʊ ˈsɛlə / (BrE)
an underground shelter designed to provide protection during a tornado or severe storm
underground storm shelter
american

Examples

During tornado season, the family kept supplies in their tornado cellar just in case.
When the sirens blared, everyone hurried into the tornado cellar beneath the house.
Many homes in the Midwest have a tornado cellar to keep residents safe during severe storms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storm shelter - a general term for a shelter designed to protect from storms or severe weather
