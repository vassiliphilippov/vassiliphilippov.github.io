spinning top

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪnɪŋ tɑp / (AmE), / ˈspɪnɪŋ tɒp / (BrE)
a conical toy for children that spins on a pointed tip when set in motion
toy that spins on a point

Examples

As a child, she loved to watch her spinning top whirl across the table.
The brightly colored spinning top made a loud humming noise as it moved.
He brought his favorite spinning top to school for show and tell.

Synset Examples

he got a bright red top and string for his birthday

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top - a toy that spins on a point
