baking mould

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪkɪŋ moʊld / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkɪŋ məʊld / (BrE)
a container or pan of a particular shape used for shaping cakes, bread, or other items while baking them in an oven
pan or container for shaping baked goods
british

Examples

She poured the batter into a baking mould shaped like a star for her daughter's birthday cake.
Please grease the baking mould before adding the dough to prevent sticking.
Modern silicone baking moulds can withstand high oven temperatures and are easy to clean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cake tin - a metal container for baking cakes
  • Synonym: baking pan - a pan used for baking pastries and cakes

Etymology

From 'bake' (to cook by dry heat) and 'mould' (from Middle English, from Old French 'modle', from Latin 'modulus', meaning 'measure').
