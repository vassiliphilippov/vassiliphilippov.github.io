theater ticket

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiːətər ˈtɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈθɪətə ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
a ticket that allows the holder entry to a theater performance or event
ticket for theater admission

Examples

We bought a theater ticket online for tonight's performance.
She lost her theater ticket and had to buy another at the door.
The theater ticket included a complimentary drink at intermission.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ticket - a piece of paper or card that gives the holder a certain right, especially to enter a place
