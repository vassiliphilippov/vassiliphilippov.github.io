high muckamuck

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈmʌkəˌmʌk / (AmE), / haɪ ˈmʌkəmʌk / (BrE)
an important or influential person, often in a self-important or overbearing way
important or influential person
informal

Examples

The high muckamuck arrived late to the meeting, making everyone wait.
She doesn't want to deal with the high muckamucks from corporate anymore.
After his promotion, he started acting like a real high muckamuck around the office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bigwig - an important, influential person
  • Synonym: VIP - a very important person

Etymology

From Chinook Jargon 'hiyu muckamuck' meaning 'plenty food', later used in English for important people.
