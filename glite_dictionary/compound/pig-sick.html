pig-sick

1. ADJ

/ pɪɡ sɪk / (AmE), / pɪɡ sɪk / (BrE)
(informal) Extremely ill or disgusted; feeling very sick or fed up.
extremely ill or disgusted (informal)
informal

Examples

After the roller coaster ride, he felt pig-sick and needed to sit down.
She was pig-sick of hearing the same complaints every day at work.
He grew pig-sick after eating too much at the festival.

Synset Examples

He was pig-sick after the long road trip.
After working nonstop, I'm feeling pig-sick today.
She became pig-sick from the endless meetings.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sick - physically or mentally ill; nauseated
  • Synonym: disgusted - feeling strong dislike or revulsion
