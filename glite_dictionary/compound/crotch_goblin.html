crotch goblin

1. NOUN

/ krɑtʃ ˈɡɑblɪn / (AmE), / krɒtʃ ˈɡɒblɪn / (BrE)
(slang, humorous, derogatory) A mischievous or unruly child, especially a young one.
slang for mischievous child
