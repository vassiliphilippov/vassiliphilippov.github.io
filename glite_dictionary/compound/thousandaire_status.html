thousandaire status

1. NOUN

/ ˈθaʊzəndˌɛr ˈsteɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈθaʊzəndˌɛː ˈsteɪtəs / (BrE)
the condition of having a net worth of at least one thousand dollars
having a net worth of at least $1,000
informal

Examples

He joked about his thousandaire status after getting his first big paycheck.
Despite her thousandaire status, she still watched her spending carefully.
Many young professionals dream of achieving thousandaire status soon after college.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: billionaire - a person whose net worth is at least one billion units of currency

Etymology

Modelled after 'millionaire', combining 'thousand' and the French-derived suffix '-aire'.
