material possession

1. NOUN

/ məˈtɪriəl pəˈzɛʃən / (AmE), / məˈtɪəriəl pəˈzɛʃən / (BrE)
a physical object or property that belongs to someone; a tangible asset
tangible property or belongings

Examples

She donated most of her material possessions to charity before moving abroad.
He realized that material possessions did not bring lasting happiness.
After the fire, all their material possessions were gone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: property - something owned by a person or group
  • Synonym: belonging - an item that is owned by someone
