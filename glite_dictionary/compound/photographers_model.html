photographer's model

1. NOUN

/ fəˈtɑːɡrəfərz ˈmɑːdəl / (AmE), / fəˈtɒɡrəfəːz ˈmɒdəl / (BrE)
a person who poses for photographers, especially as a profession or for art, fashion, or advertising purposes.
person who poses for photographers

Examples

The photographer's model held her pose patiently while the lighting was adjusted.
He began his career as a photographer's model before moving into acting.
The agency is looking for experienced photographer's models for their autumn campaign.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: model - a person employed to display clothes or pose for artists or photographers
