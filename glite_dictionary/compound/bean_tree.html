bean tree

1. NOUN

/ bin tri / (AmE), / biːn triː / (BrE)
any of several trees that have seedpods as fruits, resembling beans, especially species in the genera Catalpa or Laburnum.
tree with bean-like seedpods

Examples

The catalpa is a well-known bean tree with showy flowers and long seedpods.
Many gardeners plant the bean tree for its ornamental appearance and shade.
The bean trees in this region attract a variety of birds with their pods.
