looseness of the bowels

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːsənɪs əv ðə ˈbaʊəlz / (AmE), / ˈluːs(ə)nɪs əv ðə ˈbaʊəlz / (BrE)
frequent and watery bowel movements, usually as a symptom of illness such as infection or food poisoning
frequent, watery bowel movements
medical

Examples

The patient complained of looseness of the bowels following her return from abroad.
Certain medications can cause looseness of the bowels as a side effect.
If you experience persistent looseness of the bowels, consult a doctor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diarrhea - condition of frequent, watery bowel movements
