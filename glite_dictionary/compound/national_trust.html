National Trust

1. PROPN

/ ˈnæʃənl trʌst / (AmE), / ˈnæʃənəl trʌst / (BrE)
A British organization dedicated to preserving historic buildings, monuments, and natural areas of beauty, founded in 1895 and supported by endowments and private subscriptions.
UK body preserving historic sites and landscapes
british

Examples

The National Trust maintains several impressive gardens throughout the UK.
We joined the National Trust to support the conservation of historic houses.
Many visitors appreciate the work of the National Trust in protecting beautiful countryside.
