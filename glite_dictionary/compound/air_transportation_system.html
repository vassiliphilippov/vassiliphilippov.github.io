air transportation system

1. NOUN

/ ɛr ˌtrænspərˈteɪʃən ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / eə ˌtrænspəˈteɪʃən ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system or network for transporting passengers or goods by aircraft.
system for transport by air

Examples

The government is investing heavily in its air transportation system to improve connectivity across the country.
Delays in the air transportation system can have wide-ranging effects on global commerce.
Safety regulations are a crucial part of any national air transportation system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aviation - the operation and production of aircraft
  • Antonym: ground transportation system - system for transport by ground vehicles
