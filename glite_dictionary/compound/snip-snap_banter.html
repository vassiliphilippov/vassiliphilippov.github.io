snip-snap banter

1. NOUN

/ snɪp snæp ˈbæntər / (AmE), / snɪp snæp ˈbæntə / (BrE)
quick and witty conversational exchange, often playful or teasing in tone
quick, witty playful conversation

Examples

The snip-snap banter between the hosts made the show especially enjoyable.
Their dinner was filled with snip-snap banter and laughter, keeping the mood lively.
She missed the days of snip-snap banter with her old friends at university.

Synset Examples

Their snip-snap banter kept everyone entertained.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banter - playful and friendly exchange of teasing remarks

Etymology

Formed from the reduplicative 'snip-snap' (suggesting rapid, back-and-forth motion or remarks) and 'banter' (playful conversation).
