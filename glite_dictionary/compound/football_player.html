football player

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌbɔl ˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈfʊtbɔːl ˌpleɪə / (BrE)
an athlete who plays American football, usually as part of a team
athlete who plays American football
american

Examples

The football player scored three touchdowns in yesterday's game.
Aspiring to become a professional football player, he trains every day with his team.
The retired football player is now a popular sports commentator.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: footballer - an athlete who plays football, especially association football (soccer)
