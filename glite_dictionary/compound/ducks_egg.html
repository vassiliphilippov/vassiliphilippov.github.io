duck's egg

1. NOUN

/ dʌks ɛɡ / (AmE), / dʌks ɛɡ / (BrE)
(cricket) a score of zero by a batsman in a match.
cricket term for zero runs scored

Examples

The batsman was disappointed to finish his innings with a duck's egg against his name.
After being bowled out for a duck's egg, she vowed to improve her batting in the next match.
It's embarrassing for any player to get a duck's egg in such an important game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: duck - (cricket) a score of zero by a batsman

Etymology

From the resemblance of the digit '0' to a duck's egg; used in cricket terminology.
