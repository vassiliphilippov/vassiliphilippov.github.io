emergency room

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmɝːdʒənsi ruːm / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒənsi ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a hospital or clinic where people with urgent medical conditions receive emergency treatment and care
hospital room for emergency treatment
american medical

Examples

She was rushed to the emergency room after the accident for immediate treatment.
Doctors in the emergency room work quickly to help patients with life-threatening injuries.
The hospital expanded its emergency room to handle more patients during peak times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accident and emergency - department in a British hospital for emergency medical treatment
  • Antonym: outpatient department - area in a hospital where patients receive care without being admitted overnight
