library science

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪ.brɛr.i ˈsaɪ.əns / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.brəri ˈsaɪ.əns / (BrE)
the study of the principles and methods of organizing, managing, and operating libraries
study of library management and practices

Examples

She decided to pursue a master's degree in library science at a prestigious university.
Many innovations in digital catalogues come from ongoing research in library science.
The conference attracted professionals in library science from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: information science - the study of how information is collected, classified, stored, retrieved, and disseminated
