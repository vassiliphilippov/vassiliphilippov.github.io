cola nut

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊlə nʌt / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlə nʌt / (BrE)
the bitter brown seed of the kola tree, containing caffeine and used as a source of cola extract for flavoring drinks
seed used in cola flavoring

Examples

The cola nut is often used in traditional African medicine for its stimulating properties.
Many soft drinks originally contained extracts from the cola nut.
They chewed a cola nut to help stave off fatigue during the journey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kola nut - alternative spelling of 'cola nut'

Etymology

From the name of the kola tree, derived from West African languages.
