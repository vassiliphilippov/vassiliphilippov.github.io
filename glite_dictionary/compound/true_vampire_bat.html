true vampire bat

1. NOUN

/ tru ˈvæmpaɪr bæt / (AmE), / truː ˈvæmpaɪə bæt / (BrE)
Any of several species of tropical American bats of the family Desmodontidae that feed on the blood of mammals and birds.
blood-feeding tropical American bat

Examples

The true vampire bat feeds mainly on the blood of livestock.
Researchers studied a colony of true vampire bats in Brazil’s rainforests.
Unlike fruit bats, the true vampire bat has evolved to drink blood as its primary food source.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vampire bat - bat that feeds on blood, especially Desmodus rotundus
