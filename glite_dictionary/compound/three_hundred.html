three hundred

1. NUM

/ ˌθri ˈhʌndrəd / (AmE), / ˌθriː ˈhʌndrəd / (BrE)
the number 300; the cardinal number that is one hundred more than two hundred
the number 300

Examples

There were three hundred people in the audience last night.
The painting is estimated to be worth three hundred dollars.
After running three hundred meters, she started to feel tired.

Related Terms
