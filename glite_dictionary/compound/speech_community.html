speech community

1. NOUN

/ spiːtʃ kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / spiːtʃ kəˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
a group of people who share a common language or dialect and the norms for its use
group sharing a language or dialect

Examples

The island's speech community developed a unique dialect over centuries.
Researchers studied how norms are maintained within a speech community.
New technology can influence the boundaries of a speech community.
