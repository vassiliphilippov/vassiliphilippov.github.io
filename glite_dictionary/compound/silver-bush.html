silver-bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə bʊʃ / (BrE)
A deciduous North American shrub distinguished by its silvery leaves and fruits.
North American shrub with silvery leaves

Examples

The silver-bush is prized in gardens for its striking foliage and resistance to drought.
We planted a silver-bush along the edge of the path to add contrast to the greenery.
Botanists studied the unique adaptation of the silver-bush to sandy soils.
