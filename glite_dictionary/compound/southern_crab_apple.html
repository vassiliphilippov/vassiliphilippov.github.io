Southern crab apple

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌðərn kræb ˈæpəl / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən kræb ˈæpəl / (BrE)
A small tree or shrub native to the southeastern United States, cultivated for its ornamental rose-colored blossoms.
small ornamental tree with rose flowers

Examples

The Southern crab apple is prized for its vibrant spring blossoms in many gardens.
Wildlife often feed on the fruit of the Southern crab apple during the autumn months.
She planted a Southern crab apple in her front yard to enjoy its colorful blooms each year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab apple - a wild apple tree or its small, sour fruit
