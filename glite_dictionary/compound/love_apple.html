love apple

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌv ˌæpəl / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ˌapəl / (BrE)
an old-fashioned or literary name for a tomato, a red or yellow edible fruit native to South America and widely cultivated in many varieties
old-fashioned name for tomato

Examples

In the past, explorers brought the love apple from South America to Europe.
She prefers adding a chopped love apple to her salads for a touch of old-world flavor.
In Victorian times, the love apple was thought to have aphrodisiac properties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tomato - an edible red or yellow fruit, commonly used as a vegetable
