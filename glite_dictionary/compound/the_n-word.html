the n-word

1. NOUN

/ ði ˈɛn wɝd / (AmE), / ði ˈɛn wɜːd / (BrE)
A euphemism used to refer to a highly offensive and derogatory word directed at Black individuals, without saying the actual slur.
euphemism for racial slur against Black people
offensive
