lover's knot

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌvərz nɑt / (AmE), / ˈlʌvəz nɒt / (BrE)
A decorative knot symbolizing love and affection, often used as an emblem or motif.
decorative knot symbolizing love

Examples

She wore a delicate necklace featuring a lover's knot as a pendant.
The lover's knot brooch was a family heirloom passed down for generations.
Artists often use the lover's knot as a symbol of eternal love in their designs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bowline knot - A knot forming a secure loop at the end of a rope; often referenced as a type of 'lover's knot'.
