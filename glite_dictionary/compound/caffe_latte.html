caffe latte

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːfeɪ ˈlɑːteɪ / (AmE), / ˌkæfeɪ ˈlæt.eɪ / (BrE)
A coffee drink made with strong espresso and topped with steamed, frothed milk.
espresso with steamed milk

Examples

Would you like a caffe latte or a cappuccino with your breakfast?
He ordered a caffe latte and settled down with his book at the café.
The barista made an impressive leaf design in the foam of her caffe latte.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: latte - coffee made with espresso and hot milk

Etymology

Italian, from 'caffè' (coffee) and 'latte' (milk).
