cost increase

1. NOUN

/ kɑst ɪnˈkriːs / (AmE), / kɒst ɪnˈkriːs / (BrE)
an upward change in the amount of money required to buy or do something
a rise in cost or price

Examples

The company faced a significant cost increase after the price of raw materials doubled.
A sudden cost increase can seriously impact small businesses.
Energy bills reflect a seasonal cost increase during the winter months.

Synset Examples

they asked for a 10% rise in rates

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cost rise - an increase in cost
  • Antonym: cost decrease - a reduction in cost
