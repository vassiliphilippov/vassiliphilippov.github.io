dickish behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪkɪʃ bəˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / ˈdɪkɪʃ bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
rude or obnoxious conduct, typically characterized by selfishness, insensitivity, or aggression
rude or obnoxious conduct
informal slang

Examples

His dickish behavior made it hard for anyone to want to work with him.
People started to avoid her because of her constant dickish behavior at meetings.
There's no need for dickish behavior just because you disagree with someone.

Synset Examples

His dickish behavior turned everyone against him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dickish - having the qualities of a jerk or unpleasant person
  • Synonym: obnoxious - extremely unpleasant, rude, or offensive
  • Antonym: polite behavior - courteous and considerate conduct

Etymology

Formed from 'dick' (slang for an unpleasant person) and '-ish' (with the quality of).
