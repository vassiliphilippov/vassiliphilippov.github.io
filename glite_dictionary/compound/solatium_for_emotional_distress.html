solatium for emotional distress

1. NOUN

/ soʊˈleɪʃiəm fər ɪˌmoʊʃənl dɪˈstrɛs / (AmE), / səˈleɪtiəm fə rɪˌməʊʃənl dɪˈstrɛs / (BrE)
compensation awarded for emotional suffering or psychological harm, especially as determined by a court
compensation for emotional suffering
legal

Examples

The court awarded her a solatium for emotional distress after finding the company liable for negligence.
Claimants may receive a solatium for emotional distress in addition to compensation for physical injuries.
His lawyer argued that a solatium for emotional distress was justified due to the severe psychological impact.

Synset Examples

The court awarded her a solatium for emotional distress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: damages - money awarded by a court as compensation for loss or injury

Etymology

From Latin solatium (comfort, compensation).
