Yellow Delicious

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ dɪˈlɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ dɪˈlɪʃəs / (BrE)
A variety of apple that is sweet and has yellow skin, commonly eaten fresh.
a sweet, yellow-skinned eating apple

Examples

She packed a Yellow Delicious in her lunchbox every day for school.
The orchard specializes in growing Yellow Delicious apples for local markets.
I prefer the crispness of Yellow Delicious apples over some red varieties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Golden Delicious - another sweet apple variety with yellow skin
