hospitalization insurance

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɑːspɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən ɪnˈʃʊrəns / (AmE), / ˌhɒspɪtələˈzeɪʃən ɪnˈʃʊərəns / (BrE)
insurance that covers all or part of the cost of a patient's hospital stay
insurance covering hospital expenses

Examples

Her hospitalization insurance paid for most of her stay after the operation.
Many people obtain hospitalization insurance through their employer's benefits package.
Without hospitalization insurance, hospital bills can quickly become overwhelming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: health insurance - insurance covering medical expenses
