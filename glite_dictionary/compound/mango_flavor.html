mango flavor

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæŋɡoʊ ˈfleɪvər / (AmE), / ˈmæŋɡəʊ ˈfleɪvə / (BrE)
the characteristic taste or essence of a mango, especially as used in foods or drinks
taste or essence of a mango

Examples

The sorbet had a rich mango flavor that reminded me of summer.
Many people enjoy yogurt with mango flavor for breakfast.
Their new energy drink features a subtle mango flavor combined with other tropical fruits.

Synset Examples

I ordered a special lassi with mango flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mango - a tropical fruit with sweet yellowish flesh
