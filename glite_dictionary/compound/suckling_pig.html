suckling pig

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌklɪŋ pɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈsʌklɪŋ pɪɡ / (BrE)
A young piglet still feeding on its mother's milk, typically prepared and cooked whole for roasting.
young piglet used for roasting

Examples

The restaurant's specialty is a roasted suckling pig served with apple sauce.
At the banquet, a whole suckling pig was presented on a platter.
They ordered a suckling pig for the family celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piglet - a young pig

Etymology

From 'suckling', meaning an animal still nursing, and 'pig'.
