cultural history

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌltʃərəl ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərəl ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
the history of a society's cultural practices, beliefs, arts, and customs
study of a society's cultural development

Examples

The city is renowned for its rich cultural history and vibrant traditions.
Studying cultural history helps us understand how art and beliefs shape societies over time.
The documentary explores the cultural history of the indigenous people in the region.

Synset Examples

The Qaren tradition is rich in cultural history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: social history - the history of society and social structures
  • Antonym: political history - the history of political events and institutions
