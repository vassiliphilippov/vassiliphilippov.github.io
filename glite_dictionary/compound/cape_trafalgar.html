Cape Trafalgar

1. PROPN

/ keɪp trəˈfælɡər / (AmE), / keɪp trəˈfælɡə / (BrE)
a small headland in southwestern Spain, known as the site of the 1805 naval Battle of Trafalgar
headland in southwestern Spain

Examples

The Battle of Trafalgar took place off the coast of Cape Trafalgar in 1805.
Many tourists visit Cape Trafalgar to walk along its scenic beaches and learn about its history.
The lighthouse on Cape Trafalgar offers panoramic views of the Atlantic Ocean.

Synset Examples

Nelson defeated the French and Spanish fleets off Cape Trafalgar in 1805
