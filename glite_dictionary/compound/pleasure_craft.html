pleasure craft

1. NOUN

/ ˈplɛʒɚ kræft / (AmE), / ˈplɛʒə krɑːft / (BrE)
a large motorboat designed for recreation, equipped with a cabin, plumbing, and conveniences for living on board
large recreational motorboat with living facilities

Examples

They spent the summer living aboard their pleasure craft along the Mediterranean coast.
The marina charges higher fees for docking pleasure craft with extensive amenities.
Many retirees choose to travel from city to city on a comfortable pleasure craft.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yacht - a medium-sized sailing vessel equipped for cruising or racing
