Great Dividing Range

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡreɪt dɪˈvaɪdɪŋ reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˌɡreɪt dɪˈvaɪdɪŋ reɪndʒ / (BrE)
a major mountain range running along the eastern coast of Australia, extending from Queensland through New South Wales to Victoria
mountain range in eastern Australia

Examples

The Great Dividing Range has a significant impact on Australia’s climate and agriculture.
Last summer, we hiked several trails in the Great Dividing Range.
Many rivers in eastern Australia have their sources in the Great Dividing Range.
