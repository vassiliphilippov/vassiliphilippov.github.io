creature of the night

1. NOUN

/ ˈkriːtʃɚ əv ðə naɪt / (AmE), / ˈkriːtʃə əv ðə naɪt / (BrE)
a person who is particularly active at night or prefers nighttime activities
person active at night

Examples

As a creature of the night, she found her energy peaked after midnight.
Most creatures of the night enjoy the quiet world while others sleep.
He proudly identified himself as a creature of the night who thrived in the darkness.

Synset Examples

He was a creature of the night, often wandering alone.
He always felt like a creature of the night, thriving after dark.
She loved being a creature of the night while exploring the city.
The festival was perfect for those who are creatures of the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: night owl - a person who prefers to be active late at night
