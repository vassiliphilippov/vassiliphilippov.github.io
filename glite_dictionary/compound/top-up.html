top-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑp ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈtɒp ˌʌp / (BrE)
an additional amount of something that is added to bring it back up to a previous or desired level
additional amount to restore a level

Examples

She made a top-up to her mobile phone account before going on vacation.
After spending some money, he needed a top-up to maintain the minimum balance.
The waiter offered a top-up of wine during dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: replenishment - the process of making something full or complete again

Etymology

From the phrasal verb 'top up', meaning to fill up or replenish, first attested in the mid-20th century.
