mulled cider

1. NOUN

/ mʌld ˈsaɪdɚ / (AmE), / mʌld ˈsaɪdə / (BrE)
a hot beverage made by heating sweet cider with spices and citrus fruit
hot spiced cider drink

Examples

On cold winter evenings, I love sipping mulled cider by the fireplace.
The recipe calls for fresh apples, spices, and orange slices to make traditional mulled cider.
Guests were welcomed with a warm mug of mulled cider at the holiday party.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: cider - an alcoholic drink made from fermented apple juice
  • Synonym: mulled wine - red wine heated with spices and fruit

Etymology

From 'mull', meaning to heat or spice wine or cider, of uncertain origin.
