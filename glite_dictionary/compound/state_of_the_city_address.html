State of the City address

1. NOUN

/ steɪt əv ðə ˈsɪti əˌdrɛs / (AmE), / steɪt əv ðə ˈsɪti əˌdrɛs / (BrE)
a formal speech by a city's mayor outlining the status of the city and plans for its future
mayor's annual speech on a city's status

Examples

The mayor delivered a State of the City address focused on economic recovery and public safety.
Hundreds of citizens attended the State of the City address to hear about new development plans.
Reporters covered the live State of the City address broadcast from City Hall.

Synset Examples

Everyone waited for hizzoner to give his State of the City address.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: State of the Union address - the President's annual speech about the country's condition
