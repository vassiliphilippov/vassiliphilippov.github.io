company name

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌmpəni neɪm / (AmE), / ˈkʌmpəni neɪm / (BrE)
the official name under which a business or corporation is registered or identified
official name of a business

Examples

Every business must register its company name with the government.
The company name is printed on all official documents and marketing materials.
After rebranding, the company name was changed to reflect their new image.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trade name - the name a business uses for marketing (sometimes different from registered legal name)
