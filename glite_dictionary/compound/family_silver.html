family silver

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˈsɪlvər / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˈsɪlvə / (BrE)
valuable possessions, especially those passed down through generations as heirlooms
valuable heirlooms or assets

Examples

The company refused to sell off its family silver despite the financial crisis.
She inherited a beautiful set of family silver from her grandmother.
Politicians warned against privatizing the nation’s family silver for short-term gain.

Synset Examples

They can’t afford to sell the family silver just to survive.
He felt guilty after deciding to sell the family silver to pay debts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heirloom - a valuable object passed down through generations
