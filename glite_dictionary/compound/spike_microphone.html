spike microphone

1. NOUN

/ spaɪk ˈmaɪkroʊˌfoʊn / (AmE), / spaɪk ˈmaɪkrəˌfəʊn / (BrE)
a contact microphone designed for listening through walls, often used in surveillance or security operations
contact microphone for listening through walls

Examples

The detective used a spike microphone to eavesdrop on the conversation from the adjacent room.
Security teams sometimes deploy a spike microphone during covert investigations.
She installed a spike microphone to monitor sounds through the solid concrete wall.
