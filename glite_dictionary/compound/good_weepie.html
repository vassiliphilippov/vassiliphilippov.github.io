good weepie

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈwiːpi / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈwiːpi / (BrE)
A film or story that is very emotional and likely to make the audience cry.
film or story that makes you cry
informal

Examples

After a stressful week, sometimes I just want to watch a good weepie and let out my emotions.
The film was advertised as a good weepie, and it definitely lived up to that reputation.
My friends and I enjoy a good weepie on rainy afternoons when we're feeling nostalgic.

Synset Examples

Everyone loves a good weepie now and then.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tearjerker - A story, film, or song that is very sad and makes people cry
