island-dweller

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.lənd ˌdwɛl.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.lənd ˌdwɛl.ə / (BrE)
a person who lives on an island
person living on an island

Examples

Many island-dwellers rely on fishing as their primary source of income.
The hurricane forced the evacuation of hundreds of island-dwellers.
As an island-dweller, she was used to the sound of waves every night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: islander - a person who lives on an island
