military police officer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪləˌtɛri pəˈlis ˈɑfəsər / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri pəˈliːs ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a member of the armed forces responsible for enforcing military laws, maintaining discipline among soldiers, and guarding prisoners
member of armed forces enforcing military law

Examples

The military police officer stopped the vehicle at the checkpoint for a routine inspection.
After the fight broke out in the barracks, a military police officer quickly restored order.
The captured soldiers were escorted to the camp by a military police officer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military police - armed forces organization enforcing military law
