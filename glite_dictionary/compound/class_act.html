class act

1. NOUN

/ klæs ækt / (AmE), / klɑːs ækt / (BrE)
a person who displays impressive skill, grace, or stylish excellence, especially in difficult situations
person with stylish excellence or grace

Examples

Everyone agreed she was a class act both on and off the stage.
That's why he's seen as a class act in the sporting world.
Despite losing, he remained a class act and congratulated his opponent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: role model - person regarded as an example for others to emulate
