stock trader

1. NOUN

/ stɑk ˈtreɪdər / (AmE), / stɒk ˈtreɪdə / (BrE)
a person who buys and sells shares in the stock market, either professionally or as an investor
person who buys and sells stock shares

Examples

The stock trader carefully analyzed the market before buying new shares.
Many people dream of becoming a successful stock trader on Wall Street.
Technology has made the life of a stock trader both more complex and more accessible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: investor - a person who puts money into financial schemes or assets expecting a profit
