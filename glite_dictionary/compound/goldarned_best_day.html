goldarned best day

1. NOUN

/ ɡoʊlˈdɑrn(d) bɛst deɪ / (AmE), / ɡɒlˈdɑːnd bɛst deɪ / (BrE)
Used to emphatically refer to the greatest day, often with a folksy or old-fashioned tone.
emphatically, the greatest day
informal

Examples

Winning the championship was the goldarned best day of his life.
She said yesterday was the goldarned best day she's had in years.
After all the waiting, their wedding turned out to be the goldarned best day ever.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

Etymology

Goldarned is a euphemistic alteration of 'goddamned'.
