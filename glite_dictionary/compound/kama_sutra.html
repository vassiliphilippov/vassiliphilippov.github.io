Kama Sutra

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːmə ˈsuːtrə / (AmE), / ˌkɑːmə ˈsuːtrə / (BrE)
an ancient Sanskrit text on sensual pleasure, love, and marriage, considered a classic guide in Hindu tradition
ancient Hindu text on love and pleasure

Examples

The Kama Sutra contains detailed discussions of love, intimacy, and relationships.
Many people mistakenly think the Kama Sutra is only about sexual positions, but it covers much more.
She referenced the Kama Sutra in her research on ancient Indian culture.

Etymology

From Sanskrit kāma (desire, pleasure) + sūtra (thread, manual, guideline).
