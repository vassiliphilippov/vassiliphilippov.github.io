egg white

1. NOUN

/ ɛɡ waɪt / (AmE), / ɛɡ waɪt / (BrE)
the clear, viscous substance surrounding the yolk of an egg, consisting mainly of albumin and water, which turns white and solid when cooked
the clear part of an egg around the yolk

Examples

To make a meringue, beat the egg white until it forms stiff peaks.
The recipe calls for two egg whites and one whole egg.
She used an egg white mask to improve her skin.

Synset Examples

she separated the whites from the yolks of several eggs

Related Terms

  • Antonym: egg yolk - the yellow part of an egg, surrounded by the egg white
