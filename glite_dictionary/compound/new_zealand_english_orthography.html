New Zealand English orthography

1. NOUN

/ nu ˈziː.lənd ˈɪŋ.ɡlɪʃ ɔrˈθɑː.ɡrə.fi / (AmE), / njuː ˈziː.lənd ˈɪŋ.ɡlɪʃ ɔːˈθɒɡ.rə.fi / (BrE)
The system and conventions for spelling, hyphenation, capitalization, word breaks, and punctuation specific to New Zealand English.
spelling conventions of New Zealand English

Examples

Some words in New Zealand English orthography are spelled with 'ou' instead of 'o', such as 'colour'.
Editors must be familiar with New Zealand English orthography when preparing documents for local publication.
Students learned the differences between American and New Zealand English orthography in class.
