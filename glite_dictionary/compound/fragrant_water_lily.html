fragrant water lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈfreɪɡrənt ˈwɔtər ˈlɪli / (AmE), / ˈfreɪɡrənt ˈwɔːtə ˈlɪli / (BrE)
a type of water lily with large floating leaves and showy, fragrant flowers, found in temperate and tropical regions
water lily with fragrant floating flowers

Examples

A fragrant water lily bloomed in the middle of the pond, filling the air with its sweet scent.
The fragrant water lily is prized by gardeners for both its beauty and aroma.
We photographed the delicate petals of a fragrant water lily during our canoe trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water lily - aquatic plant with large, floating leaves and showy flowers
