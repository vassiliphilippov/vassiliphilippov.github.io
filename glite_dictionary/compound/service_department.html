service department

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝvɪs dɪˌpɑɹtmənt / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the division of a business or organization that provides assistance or services to customers, such as repairs or customer support
business division handling customer services

Examples

If your washing machine breaks down, contact the service department for repairs.
The service department handles all customer complaints and warranty issues.
He got a job in the service department of a large electronics retailer.

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːvɪs dɪˌpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the part of a company, especially a car dealership or garage, responsible for servicing and repairing vehicles
area for vehicle servicing and repairs

Examples

The service department inspected my car and replaced the faulty brake pads.
If your vehicle needs an oil change, please contact our service department.
Customers praised the professionalism of the service department at the dealership.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garage - a building where cars are repaired and maintained
  • Synonym: repair shop - a place where machinery or equipment is repaired
