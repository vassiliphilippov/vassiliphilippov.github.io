horned poppy

1. NOUN

/ hɔrn̩d ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / hɔːnd ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
A yellow-flowered Eurasian plant with bluish-green leaves, often found along sandy shores and naturalized in eastern North America.
yellow-flowered Eurasian coastal plant

Examples

The horned poppy thrives in sandy coastal soils.
Botanists studied the spread of the horned poppy along the beach.
The bright yellow flowers of the horned poppy are easy to spot among the dunes.
