lamb succory

1. NOUN

/ læm ˈsʌkəri / (AmE), / læm ˈsʌkəri / (BrE)
A small European herb with small yellow flowers, sometimes found in dry grassy places.
small European herb with yellow flowers

Examples

The fields were dotted with lamb succory blooming in the early summer light.
Botanists noted the spread of lamb succory along the hillside.
She identified the plant as lamb succory due to its distinct yellow flowers.
