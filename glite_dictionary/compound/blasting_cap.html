blasting cap

1. NOUN

/ ˈblæstɪŋ kæp / (AmE), / ˈblɑːstɪŋ kæp / (BrE)
a small tube filled with a detonating substance, used for triggering high explosives
explosive detonator device

Examples

The demolition crew used a blasting cap to safely detonate the dynamite.
Always handle a blasting cap with care, as it is highly sensitive to shock.
He explained that a blasting cap is essential for setting off larger explosive charges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detonator - a device used to set off explosives
