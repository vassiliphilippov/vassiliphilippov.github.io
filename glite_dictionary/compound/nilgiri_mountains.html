Nilgiri mountains

1. PROPN

/ ˌnɪlˈɡɪri ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / ˌnɪlˈɡɪəri ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a range of hills located in southern India, known for their scenic beauty and tea plantations
mountain range in southern India

Examples

The Nilgiri mountains are well known for their extensive tea plantations and scenic vistas.
Several rare species of flora and fauna can be found only in the Nilgiri mountains.
Tourists flock to the Nilgiri mountains for trekking and to enjoy the cool climate.

Etymology

From Tamil 'neelam' (blue) + 'giri' (hill, mountain).
