San Juan Mountains

1. PROPN

/ sæn wɑn ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / san wæn ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a mountain range in southwestern Colorado, forming part of the Rocky Mountains
mountain range in southwestern Colorado

Examples

The San Juan Mountains are known for their dramatic peaks and beautiful hiking trails.
In the winter, skiers flock to the resorts nestled in the San Juan Mountains.
Geologists study the rich mineral deposits found throughout the San Juan Mountains.

Etymology

From Spanish 'San Juan' meaning 'Saint John'.
