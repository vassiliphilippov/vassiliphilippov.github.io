United Nations

1. PROPN

/ juːˈnaɪt̬ɪd ˈneɪʃənz / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪtɪd ˈneɪʃənz / (BrE)
an international organization formed in 1945 by independent states to promote global peace, security, and cooperation
international peacekeeping organization formed in 1945

Examples

The United Nations held an emergency meeting to address the crisis.
Many countries are members of the United Nations.
The United Nations plays a key role in humanitarian aid efforts worldwide.
