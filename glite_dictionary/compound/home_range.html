home range

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm reɪndʒ / (AmE), / həʊm reɪndʒ / (BrE)
the area normally traveled and used by an animal in its daily activities
area regularly used by an animal

Examples

The wolf’s home range can span hundreds of square kilometers in the wild.
Biologists tracked the birds to determine how large their home range was during nesting season.
Habitat loss can cause animals’ home ranges to shrink significantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: territory - an area that an animal defends against others
