violet-scented

1. ADJ

/ ˈvaɪələt ˌsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈvaɪəlɪt ˌsɛntɪd / (BrE)
Having a fragrance resembling that of violets.
smelling like violets

Examples

She wore a violet-scented perfume that lingered in the air after she left the room.
The garden was filled with violet-scented breezes every spring evening.
I placed a violet-scented candle on the table to create a soothing atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell
