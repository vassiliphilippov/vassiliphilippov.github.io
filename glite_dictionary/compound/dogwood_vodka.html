dogwood vodka

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔɡwʊd ˈvɑːdkə / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡwʊd ˈvɒdkə / (BrE)
a strong liqueur made by infusing vodka with the fruit of the Cornelian cherry (Cornus mas)
vodka infused with Cornelian cherry

Examples

We sampled the local dogwood vodka at a small family-run distillery.
Many guests found the taste of dogwood vodka to be pleasantly tart and unique.
Bottles of dogwood vodka are popular souvenirs from Eastern Europe.

Synset Examples

I tried dogwood vodka on my trip to Armenia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vodka - a clear, strong alcoholic drink originating from Russia and Poland
