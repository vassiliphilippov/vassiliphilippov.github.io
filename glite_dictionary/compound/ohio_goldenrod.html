Ohio goldenrod

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈhaɪ.oʊ ˈɡoʊl.dənˌrɑd / (AmE), / əʊˈhaɪ.əʊ ˈɡɒl.dənˌrɒd / (BrE)
A species of goldenrod (Solidago ohioensis) native to the Midwestern United States, especially found in Ohio, with yellow flowers.
species of goldenrod native to Ohio

Examples

Hikers spotted an Ohio goldenrod growing along the riverbank in northeastern Ohio.
The Ohio goldenrod blooms in late summer, adding bright yellow color to wetlands and prairies.
Botanists studied the ecology of Ohio goldenrod to understand its role in native habitats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldenrod - a plant with clusters of small yellow flowers, genus Solidago
