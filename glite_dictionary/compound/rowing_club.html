rowing club

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊɪŋ klʌb / (AmE), / ˈrəʊɪŋ klʌb / (BrE)
an organization or group where people participate in the sport of rowing together
organization for rowing enthusiasts

Examples

She joined her local rowing club to improve her technique and meet other rowers.
The university's rowing club regularly trains on the river before sunrise.
At the rowing club dinner, awards were given for outstanding performances during the season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rowing - the sport or activity of propelling a boat using oars
