entrance money

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛn.trəns ˌmʌn.i / (AmE), / ˈɛn.trəns ˌmʌn.i / (BrE)
the fee paid for admission to an event, place, or organization.
fee paid for admission

Examples

Spectators must pay entrance money at the gate before entering the stadium.
The club required a substantial entrance money in addition to the annual dues.
He forgot to bring his entrance money and was not allowed into the exhibition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admission fee - a fee paid to enter a place or event
