hedge thorn

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdʒ ˌθɔrn / (AmE), / ˈhɛdʒ ˌθɔːn / (BrE)
a South African shrub with forked spines and plumlike fruit, often used for hedging
South African shrub with spines, used for hedges

Examples

The farmer planted a row of hedge thorn along the property to keep animals out.
As a natural barrier, hedge thorn is both effective and attractive.
The hedge thorn produces plumlike fruit that attracts local birds.
