anchor point

1. NOUN

/ ˈæŋkɚ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈaŋkə pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a fixed or crucial reference point that provides stability or serves as a basis for design, construction, or planning
fixed reference point for stability

Examples

The engineers selected a steel beam as the anchor point for the suspension cables.
In graphic design software, you can move each anchor point to reshape the vector image.
Establishing an anchor point early in the process helps keep the project organized.

Synset Examples

She identified the anchor point for the project’s success.
The anchor point helps in stabilizing the design.
The architect used the north wall as an anchor point.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reference point - a point used to compare or determine position or stability
