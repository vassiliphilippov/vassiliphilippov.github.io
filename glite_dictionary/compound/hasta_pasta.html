hasta pasta

1. X

/ ˈhɑstə ˈpɑstə / (AmE), / ˈhɑːstə ˈpɑːstə / (BrE)
A playful and informal way to say goodbye, often used humorously.
humorous farewell expression
informal

Examples

He waved and called out, hasta pasta as he left the party.
She said hasta pasta to her classmates after the final exam.
"Hasta pasta!" he joked as he walked out of the office for vacation.

Synset Examples

He joked, 'Hasta pasta!' as he left the dinner table.
She laughed and said, 'Hasta pasta!' while leaving.
With a wink, he exclaimed, 'Hasta pasta!' to his friends.

Etymology

Play on the Spanish farewell 'hasta' (as in 'hasta la vista') combined with 'pasta' for rhyme and humor.
