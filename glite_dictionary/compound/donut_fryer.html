donut fryer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊ.nət ˌfraɪ.ər / (AmE), / ˈdəʊ.nət ˌfraɪ.ə / (BrE)
a machine used for frying large quantities of doughnuts at the same time, commonly seen in commercial bakeries and donut shops.
machine for frying doughnuts in bulk

Examples

The bakery's new donut fryer can produce hundreds of fresh doughnuts every hour.
After cleaning the donut fryer, the staff prepared it for the next morning's batch.
Investing in a commercial donut fryer increased production and efficiency in their shop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deep fryer - a kitchen appliance used for deep frying food
