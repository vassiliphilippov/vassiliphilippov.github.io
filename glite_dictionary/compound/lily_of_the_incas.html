lily of the Incas

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪli əv ði ˈɪŋkəz / (AmE), / ˈlɪli əv ði ˈɪŋkəz / (BrE)
A South American plant of the genus Alstroemeria, with showy, lily-like pinkish or purple flowers, native to the Andes.
South American flowering plant (Alstroemeria)

Examples

She planted lily of the Incas in her garden to add a splash of color every spring.
At the florist's, I admired a bouquet featuring bright pink lily of the Incas.
The lily of the Incas is native to the Andes and thrives in well-drained soil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alstroemeria - a South American plant with showy, lily-like flowers, also known as lily of the Incas
