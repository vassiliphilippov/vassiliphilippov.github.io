orange red

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ rɛd / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ rɛd / (BrE)
a vivid shade of red with a noticeable orange tinge
a bright red-orange color

Examples

The artist chose orange red for the sunset to create a dramatic effect on the canvas.
She wore a dress in orange red that made her stand out at the party.
The flowers bloomed in brilliant shades of orange red throughout the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vermilion - a brilliant red or scarlet color
  • Degree Lower: red - the color at the end of the visible spectrum next to orange
  • Degree Lower: orange - a color between red and yellow in the spectrum
