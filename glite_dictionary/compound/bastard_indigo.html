bastard indigo

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæstərd ˈɪndoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːstəd ˈɪndɪɡəʊ / (BrE)

East Indian shrub resembling true indigo

Examples

The leaves of the bastard indigo are sometimes used locally for dyeing fabrics.
Gardeners appreciate the resilience of bastard indigo in dry and sunny climates.
Researchers studied the medicinal properties of extracts from the bastard indigo.
