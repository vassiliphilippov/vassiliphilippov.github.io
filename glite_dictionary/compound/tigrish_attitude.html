tigrish attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪɡrɪʃ ˈætɪtuːd / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡrɪʃ ˈætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
A manner or demeanor that is fierce, aggressive, or reminiscent of a tiger.
fierce or aggressive demeanor
