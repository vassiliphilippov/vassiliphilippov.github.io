Saint Francis River

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈfrænsɪs ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈfrænsɪs ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in the central United States, rising in Missouri and flowing southeast through Arkansas as a tributary of the Mississippi River
tributary of the Mississippi River in Missouri and Arkansas

Examples

The Saint Francis River is known for its scenic beauty and excellent fishing spots.
Flooding along the Saint Francis River caused several towns to be evacuated last year.
Wildlife thrives along the banks of the Saint Francis River in Arkansas.
