much to someone's delight

1. X

/ mʌtʃ tuː ˈsʌmwʌnz dɪˈlaɪt / (AmE), / mʌtʃ tʊ ˈsʌmwənz dɪˈlaɪt / (BrE)
used to indicate that something caused someone a great deal of pleasure or happiness
to someone's great pleasure or joy

Examples

Much to her delight, the gift she had longed for was waiting under the tree.
Much to their delight, their favorite band announced an extra concert date.
Much to his delight, the lost puppy returned home unharmed.

Synset Examples

He finished first in the race, much to his delight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to someone's delight - to someone's pleasure or happiness
