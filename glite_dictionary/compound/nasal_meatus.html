nasal meatus

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪzl miˈeɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈneɪzl miːˈɑːtəs / (BrE)
any of the passages in the nasal cavity formed by the projections of the nasal conchae
passage in the nasal cavity
medical

Examples

The superior nasal meatus is located between the superior and middle nasal conchae.
Inflammation of a nasal meatus can lead to breathing difficulties.
Doctors examined the nasal meatus for signs of infection during the sinus examination.

Etymology

From Latin 'nasalis' (of the nose) and 'meatus' (a passage).
