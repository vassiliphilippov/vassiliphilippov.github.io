party crasher

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːr.ti ˌkræʃ.ər / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.ti ˌkræʃ.ə / (BrE)
A person who attends a party without being invited, often causing disruption or trouble.
uninvited guest at a party

Examples

The security guard quickly escorted the party crasher out of the building.
Nobody knew the party crasher who was loudly interrupting conversations at midnight.
After a party crasher arrived, the host was visibly annoyed.

Synset Examples

Everyone noticed the party crasher as he walked in.
A party crasher showed up uninvited and stirred chaos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gatecrasher - a person who attends an event without being invited
