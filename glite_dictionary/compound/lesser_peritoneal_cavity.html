lesser peritoneal cavity

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛsər ˌpɛrətnˈiəl ˈkævəti / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˌpɛrɪtəˈniːəl ˈkævɪti / (BrE)
an isolated compartment of the peritoneal cavity located behind the stomach
anatomical compartment behind the stomach
medical

Examples

The surgeon carefully examined the lesser peritoneal cavity for signs of infection.
A cyst was found in the lesser peritoneal cavity during the abdominal scan.
Inflammation of the lesser peritoneal cavity can lead to serious complications if untreated.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: greater peritoneal cavity - the larger compartment of the peritoneal cavity

Etymology

From 'peritoneal' relating to the peritoneum, and 'cavity' meaning a hollow space; 'lesser' indicates its relative size and position.
