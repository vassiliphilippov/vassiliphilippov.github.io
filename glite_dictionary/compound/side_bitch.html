side bitch

1. NOUN

/ saɪd bɪtʃ / (AmE), / saɪd bɪtʃ / (BrE)
a slang and vulgar term for a person (usually a woman) who is someone’s secondary or unofficial romantic or sexual partner
slang term for a secondary romantic partner
slang vulgar
