New York Bay

1. PROPN

/ nuː ˈjɔrk beɪ / (AmE), / njuː ˈjɔːk beɪ / (BrE)
a bay in the North Atlantic Ocean at the mouth of the Hudson River, adjacent to New York City.
bay at Hudson River mouth, New York

Examples

Several ferries cross New York Bay each day, connecting Manhattan with Staten Island and New Jersey.
The skyline of Lower Manhattan can be seen from the waters of New York Bay.
After heavy rains, the water quality in New York Bay is closely monitored by environmental agencies.
