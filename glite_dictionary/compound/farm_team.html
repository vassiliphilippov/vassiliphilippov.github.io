farm team

1. NOUN

/ fɑrm tim / (AmE), / fɑːm tiːm / (BrE)
a minor-league sports team that is owned by or affiliated with a major-league team, used primarily to develop younger or less experienced players
minor-league team affiliated with a major-league team

Examples

The young pitcher was sent to the farm team to gain more experience before joining the main roster.
Several players were called up from the farm team after the starting catcher was injured.
He managed the farm team for three years before being promoted to a major-league coaching position.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minor league - a league of professional sports teams at a lower level than the major leagues
