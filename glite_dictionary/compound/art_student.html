art student

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt ˈstu.dənt / (AmE), / ɑːt ˈstjuː.dənt / (BrE)
a person who studies art at a school, college, or university, especially to become an artist
person studying to be an artist

Examples

Many art students spend hours in the studio working on their projects.
After graduating, the art student held her first gallery exhibition.
Paris has long been a popular city for aspiring art students from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: artist - a person who creates art, especially as a profession
