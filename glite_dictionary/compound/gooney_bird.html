gooney bird

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːni bɜrd / (AmE), / ˈɡuːni bɜːd / (BrE)
A large albatross, especially the black-footed or Laysan albatross, known for its clumsy movements on land.
a type of large albatross

Examples

A gooney bird circled above the ship before landing awkwardly on the deck.
The black-footed gooney bird is often seen nesting on Midway Atoll.
Tourists watched as a gooney bird performed its comical courtship dance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: albatross - a large seabird with long wings, typically found in the southern oceans
