Battle of the Caudine Forks

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬l əv ðə kɔːˈdaɪn ˈfɔrks / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ðə kɔːˈdaɪn ˈfɔːks / (BrE)
a battle fought in the Apennines in 321 BC, in which the Samnite forces defeated the Roman army
ancient battle where Samnites defeated Romans

Examples

The Battle of the Caudine Forks is remembered as a major humiliation for the Roman Republic.
Many historians discuss the tactics used by the Samnites during the Battle of the Caudine Forks.
Roman defeat at the Battle of the Caudine Forks led to significant military reforms.
