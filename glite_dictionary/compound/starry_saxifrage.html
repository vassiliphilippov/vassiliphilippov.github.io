starry saxifrage

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑri ˈsæksɪfrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈstɑːri ˈsæksɪfrɪdʒ / (BrE)
a small alpine plant with mat-forming growth and starlike white flowers, found in Europe
small alpine plant with starlike white flowers

Examples

The starry saxifrage blooms beautifully between rocky crevices in the mountains every spring.
She photographed the delicate white flowers of the starry saxifrage during her hike in the Alps.
Botanists noted that the starry saxifrage prefers damp, shaded habitats in alpine regions.

Etymology

From Latin 'saxifraga', meaning 'stonebreaker', combined with 'starry' describing the flower shape.
