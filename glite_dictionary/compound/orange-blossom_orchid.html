orange-blossom orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ ˌblɑsəm ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ˌblɒsəm ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A diminutive Australian orchid with loose clusters of fragrant white flowers marked with purple and orange on the lip.
Australian orchid with fragrant white flowers

Examples

The orange-blossom orchid is prized among native Australian wildflowers for its delicate fragrance and beauty.
Botanists discovered a rare orange-blossom orchid blooming in a remote region of Queensland.
Gardeners often cultivate the orange-blossom orchid for its striking purple and orange markings.
