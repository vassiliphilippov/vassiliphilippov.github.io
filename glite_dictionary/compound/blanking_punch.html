blanking punch

1. NOUN

/ ˈblæŋ.kɪŋ pʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈblæŋ.kɪŋ pʌntʃ / (BrE)
A punch used together with a blanking die to cut out shapes or blanks from sheet material.
tool for cutting shapes with a blanking die

Examples

The technician replaced the worn blanking punch to ensure clean cuts during the manufacturing process.
A high-quality blanking punch can increase the accuracy and lifespan of stamping tools.
Proper alignment between the blanking punch and die is critical for precise part production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blanking die - a die used for cutting blanks from material sheets
