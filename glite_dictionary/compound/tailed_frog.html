tailed frog

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪld ˌfrɑːɡ / (AmE), / ˈteɪld ˌfrɒɡ / (BrE)
a small frog native to western North America, distinguished by a tail-like copulatory organ in males
western North American frog with a tail-like organ

Examples

The biologist spotted a tailed frog in the fast-moving stream.
Male tailed frogs use their unique organ to mate in turbulent waters.
Some hikers were excited to see a rare tailed frog during their trip.
