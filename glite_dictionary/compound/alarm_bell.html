alarm bell

1. NOUN

/ əˈlɑrm ˌbɛl / (AmE), / əˈlɑːm ˌbɛl / (BrE)
a bell that rings to warn people of danger or signal an emergency.
bell warning of danger or emergency

Examples

The alarm bell rang loudly throughout the building, signaling everyone to evacuate.
As soon as the alarm bell sounded, firefighters rushed to respond.
Every time the alarm bell goes off, students must practice the emergency drill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alarm - a warning of danger, usually a sound or signal
