strawberry saxifrage

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔːˌbɛri ˈsæksɪˌfrædʒ / (AmE), / ˈstrɒbəri ˈsæksɪfrɪdʒ / (BrE)
a plant of the saxifrage family native to eastern Asia, having racemes of small red-and-white flowers and spreading by creeping stolons
an eastern Asian flowering plant

Examples

The strawberry saxifrage adds color to the shady parts of the garden with its delicate blossoms.
Gardeners appreciate the strawberry saxifrage for its ability to spread and cover ground quickly.
After planting several strawberry saxifrage plants, she enjoyed their bright flowers in the spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saxifrage - a plant of the genus Saxifraga, typically found in cool or mountainous regions
