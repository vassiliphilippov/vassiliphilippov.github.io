travelling bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəlɪŋ bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈtrævəlɪŋ bæɡ / (BrE)
a portable, usually rectangular bag for carrying clothes and personal items when travelling
portable bag for clothes and travel items

Examples

She packed her clothes into a travelling bag before leaving for the airport.
He forgot his toothbrush in his travelling bag after returning from the trip.
A sturdy travelling bag is essential for frequent flyers.

Synset Examples

he carried his small bag onto the plane with him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suitcase - a case with a handle used for carrying clothes when travelling
  • Synonym: duffel bag - a large cylindrical bag for carrying personal belongings, often used in travel
