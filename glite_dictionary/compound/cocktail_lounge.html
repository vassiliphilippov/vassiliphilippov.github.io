cocktail lounge

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑkˌteɪl laʊndʒ / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.teɪl laʊndʒ / (BrE)
a bar in a hotel or restaurant where cocktails and other drinks are served, often in a relaxed or stylish setting
bar serving cocktails in a hotel or restaurant

Examples

We enjoyed jazz music and martinis in the cocktail lounge before dinner.
The hotel’s cocktail lounge is known for its creative drinks and cozy atmosphere.
She waited for her friends in the cocktail lounge, sipping a classic Manhattan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bar - a place where alcoholic drinks are served
  • Synonym: pub - a tavern or bar, especially in Britain
