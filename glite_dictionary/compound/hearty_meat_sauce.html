hearty meat sauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːrti mit sɔs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːti miːt sɔːs / (BrE)
a rich and filling pasta sauce made with a substantial amount of meat, often simmered with tomatoes, herbs, and spices
rich, filling pasta sauce with meat

Examples

She made a hearty meat sauce to serve over the spaghetti for dinner.
This recipe calls for a hearty meat sauce with plenty of ground beef and tomatoes.
Nothing beats a plate of pasta topped with a homemade hearty meat sauce.

Synset Examples

He prefers his pasta with a hearty meat sauce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bolognese - an Italian meat-based pasta sauce, also known as ragù alla bolognese
