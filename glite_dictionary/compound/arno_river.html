Arno River

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑrnoʊ ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈɑːnəʊ ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A river in central Italy that flows from the Apennines through Florence and Pisa to the Ligurian Sea.
river in central Italy

Examples

The famous Ponte Vecchio bridge spans the Arno River in Florence.
After heavy rainfall, the Arno River sometimes floods parts of Pisa.
Many artists have painted beautiful views of the Arno River at sunset.
