meat packer

1. NOUN

/ mit ˈpækɚ / (AmE), / miːt ˈpækə / (BrE)
a person or company engaged in the processing, packaging, and wholesale distribution of meat products
person or company in meat processing industry

Examples

The meat packer supplied fresh beef to several grocery chains.
Working as a meat packer requires attention to hygiene and safety regulations.
The small town relied heavily on its local meat packer for employment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butcher - person who prepares and sells meat
