motor scooter

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊtər ˌskuːtər / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə ˌskuːtə / (BrE)
a small motorcycle with a platform for the operator's feet, small wheels, and a low-powered engine
small motorcycle with low-powered engine

Examples

She commutes to work every day on her motor scooter.
A motor scooter is an efficient way to get around the city.
He bought a new motor scooter for quick trips to the grocery store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scooter - a small vehicle with two or three wheels
  • Synonym: moped - a low-powered motorized bicycle or scooter
