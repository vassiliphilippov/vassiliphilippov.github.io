dog bent

1. NOUN

/ dɔg bɛnt / (AmE), / dɒg bɛnt / (BrE)
A species of common grass with slender stems and narrow leaves, often found in meadows and pastures.
a type of common grass

Examples

The field was overgrown with dog bent and wildflowers.
Farmers often remove dog bent to improve pasture quality for livestock.
The narrow leaves of dog bent make it easy to distinguish from other grasses.
