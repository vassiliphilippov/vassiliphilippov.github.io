heal all

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiːl ɔl / (AmE), / ˈhiːl ɔːl / (BrE)
A common woodland herb of temperate North America with yellow nodding flowers and small round blue fruits.
woodland herb with yellow flowers and blue fruits

Examples

The heal all thrives in shaded woodland areas throughout the northeastern United States.
Gardeners often plant heal all for its attractive yellow flowers and medicinal uses.
Birds are known to eat the blue fruits of the heal all in late summer.
