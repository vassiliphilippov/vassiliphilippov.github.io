bum head

1. NOUN

/ bʌm hɛd / (AmE), / bʌm hɛd / (BrE)
an informal or playful term for a foolish or careless person
foolish or careless person (informal)
informal

Examples

I can't believe I was such a bum head and missed the meeting.
Don't be a bum head, just follow the instructions.
She laughed and called her brother a bum head for spilling the juice.

Synset Examples

He called himself a bum head for forgetting her birthday.
Stop being a bum head and help me out!
