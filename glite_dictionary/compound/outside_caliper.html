outside caliper

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊt.saɪd ˈkæl.ə.pɚ / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.saɪd ˈkæl.ɪ.pə / (BrE)
a caliper designed to measure the external dimensions of an object, with legs that curve inward to fit around the object's outside
tool for measuring external dimensions

Examples

To check the diameter of the pipe, he used an outside caliper.
The machinist carefully adjusted the outside caliper before taking the measurement.
Outside calipers are essential tools in metalworking for ensuring precise external sizes.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inside caliper - a caliper for measuring internal dimensions
