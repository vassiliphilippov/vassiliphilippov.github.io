false rue anemone

1. NOUN

/ fɔls ruː əˈnɛməni / (AmE), / fɔːls ruː əˈnɛməni / (BrE)
a slender perennial plant of eastern North America, with tuberous roots and pink-tinged white flowers, resembling meadow rue
North American perennial plant; resembles meadow rue

Examples

The woods were dotted with blooming false rue anemone in early spring.
She confused the false rue anemone with its close relative, the meadow rue.
Hikers marveled at the delicate flowers of the false rue anemone along the forest trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meadow rue - a plant genus (Thalictrum) with feathery flowers, often confused with false rue anemone
  • Degree Higher: anemone - a genus of flowering plants commonly known as windflowers
