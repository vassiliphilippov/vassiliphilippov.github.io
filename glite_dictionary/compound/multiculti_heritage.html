multiculti heritage

1. NOUN

/ ˌmʌltiˈkʌlti ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈkʌlti ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (BrE)
Cultural identity marked by diversity and the blending of people from various ethnic or cultural backgrounds.
diverse, mixed cultural heritage

Examples

The festival celebrated our multiculti heritage through music, dance, and food from around the world.
She takes pride in her multiculti heritage and the traditions passed down from both her parents.
Schools often highlight students' multiculti heritage during international week to promote understanding and respect.

Synset Examples

The festival celebrated our multiculti heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multicultural - relating to or containing several cultural or ethnic groups within a society

Etymology

Formed from 'multiculti' (shortening of 'multicultural') and 'heritage'.
