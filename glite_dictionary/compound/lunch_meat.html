lunch meat

1. NOUN

/ lʌntʃ miːt / (AmE), / lʌntʃ miːt / (BrE)
A type of sausage or processed meat, often prepared in a loaf, that is sliced and served cold, typically in sandwiches or as a snack.
cold sliced processed meat

Examples

She made a sandwich with lunch meat and cheese for her lunch.
At the party, they served a platter of assorted lunch meats with crackers.
After grocery shopping, he put the lunch meat in the fridge to keep it fresh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cold cut - a slice of cold cooked or processed meat
  • Synonym: deli meat - cold cooked or cured meats served sliced, often at a delicatessen
