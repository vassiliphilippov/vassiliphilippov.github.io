snow bunny

1. NOUN

/ snoʊ ˈbʌni / (AmE), / snəʊ ˈbʌni / (BrE)
an informal term for a person, especially a woman, who enjoys skiing or other winter sports, often dressing fashionably for the slopes
person fond of skiing or winter sports
informal

Examples

Every winter, the resort is filled with snow bunnies hitting the slopes in colorful gear.
As a self-proclaimed snow bunny, she spends all her vacations at mountain ski lodges.
He joked that he went to the Alps to become a better skier, but really he just wanted to meet snow bunnies.

Synset Examples

She dressed her daughter like a little snow bunny.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skier - a person who skis
