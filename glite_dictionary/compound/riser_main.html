riser main

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪzɚ meɪn / (AmE), / ˈraɪzə meɪn / (BrE)
A vertical pipe in a building, typically used to distribute water, gas, or other services to different floors.
vertical service pipe in a building

Examples

The plumber installed a new riser main to carry water to the upper floors.
Building codes require the riser main to be made of fire-resistant materials.
A leak in the riser main caused water damage in several apartments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: riser - a vertical pipe or duct in a building
