man jack

1. NOUN

/ mæn ˈdʒæk / (AmE), / mæn ˈdʒæk / (BrE)
used to refer to every single person in a group, emphasizing no one is excluded
every single person (emphatic)

Examples

The manager expected every man jack of us to show up early for the meeting.
They searched for survivors, determined not to leave a single man jack behind.
Each man jack volunteered to help rebuild the village after the storm.
