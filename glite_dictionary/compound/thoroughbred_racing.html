thoroughbred racing

1. NOUN

/ ˌθɜːroʊˌbrɛd ˈreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌθʌrəbred ˈreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport of racing horses that are thoroughbreds, typically on a track.
the sport of racing thoroughbred horses

Examples

The city is famous for its annual thoroughbred racing event, which attracts enthusiasts from across the country.
He has been involved in thoroughbred racing since he was a child, following his family's tradition.
Major thoroughbred racing tracks often offer significant prize money and international prestige.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horse racing - the sport in which horses compete to be the fastest over a set course
