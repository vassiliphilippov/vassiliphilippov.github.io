expansion bit

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈspænʃən bɪt / (AmE), / ɪkˈspænʃən bɪt / (BrE)
A drill bit with an adjustable cutting blade for making holes of various diameters.
adjustable drill bit

Examples

The carpenter used an expansion bit to drill larger holes in the wooden beam.
An expansion bit allows you to adjust the cutting diameter quickly and precisely.
Before starting the project, she made sure her expansion bit was properly tightened.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auger bit - a drill bit used for boring holes in wood
