ajebutter lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːdʒeɪˈbʌtər ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (AmE), / ˌɑːdʒeɪˈbʌtə ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
A privileged way of living often associated with wealth and lack of hardship, especially among young people of affluent backgrounds in Nigeria.
privileged, wealthy lifestyle (Nigerian English)
informal

Examples

She grew up enjoying an ajebutter lifestyle, never having to worry about money.
Many people envy his ajebutter lifestyle and all the privileges it brings.
Despite her ajebutter lifestyle, she remains humble and approachable.

Synset Examples

Her ajebutter lifestyle means she can't relate to our struggles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ajebutter - a pampered or privileged child, esp. from a wealthy background (Nigerian English)
  • Synonym: aje - a privileged or wealthy person (Nigerian English, short form)
  • Antonym: streetwise - familiar with life on the streets; opposite in lifestyle or attitude

Etymology

From Nigerian Pidgin English 'ajebutter' (child from a wealthy family or who is pampered), literally 'ate butter'.
