rush rose

1. NOUN

/ rʌʃ roʊz / (AmE), / rʌʃ rəʊz / (BrE)
A woody perennial plant of the southeastern United States, bearing yellow flowers.
yellow-flowered woody plant of southeastern US

Examples

The rush rose blooms each spring with bright yellow flowers.
Botanists are studying the habitat needs of the rush rose in southeastern woodlands.
She photographed a rare rush rose growing along the trail.
