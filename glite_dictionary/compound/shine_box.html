shine box

1. NOUN

/ ʃaɪn bɑks / (AmE), / ʃaɪn bɒks / (BrE)
a box containing tools and materials used for polishing shoes
box for shoe polishing tools

Examples

He reached for the shine box when his loafers started to look dull.
The old shine box in the hallway belonged to my grandfather.
Every Sunday, Tom would set up his shine box outside the barbershop.

Synset Examples

Don't forget to get your shine box before the game.

Related Terms
