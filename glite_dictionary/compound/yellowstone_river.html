Yellowstone River

1. PROPN

/ ˈjɛloʊˌstoʊn ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈjeləʊˌstəʊn ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a major river in the western United States, flowing through Yellowstone National Park and joining the Missouri River
river through Yellowstone National Park, US

Examples

The Yellowstone River is famous for its scenic beauty and excellent fishing opportunities.
After leaving the national park, the Yellowstone River continues northward through Montana.
Many visitors enjoy rafting on the Yellowstone River during the summer.
