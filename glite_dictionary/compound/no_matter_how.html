no matter how

1. SCONJ

/ noʊ ˈmætər haʊ / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈmætə haʊ / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is true or will happen regardless of the method, degree, or circumstances involved
regardless of the degree or way

Examples

No matter how late it gets, she never seems to get tired.
No matter how hard he trained, he couldn't win the race.
You have to keep trying no matter how many times you fail.

Synset Examples

I could never forget you, no matter how hard I try.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regardless of - without being affected by; despite
