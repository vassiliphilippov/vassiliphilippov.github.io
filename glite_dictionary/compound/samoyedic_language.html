Samoyedic language

1. NOUN

/ ˌsæməˈjɛdɪk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌsamɔɪˈɛdɪk ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
Any of the Uralic languages spoken by the Samoyed peoples in northwestern Siberia.
Uralic language group of northwestern Siberia

Examples

The Samoyedic languages are a branch of the Uralic language family.
She is studying a dialect of a Samoyedic language spoken in Siberia.
Only a few thousand people speak any Samoyedic language today.

Etymology

From Russian 'Samoyed' (the people) + 'ic' (forming adjectives), referring to their languages.
