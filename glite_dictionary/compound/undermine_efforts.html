to undermine efforts

1. VERB

/ ˌʌn.dɚˈmaɪn ˈɛf.ɚts / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dəˈmaɪn ˈɛf.əts / (BrE)
to weaken or reduce the effectiveness of attempts or actions aimed at achieving something
to weaken attempts to achieve something

Examples

Negative attitudes can undermine efforts to improve workplace morale.
Spreading false information will only undermine efforts to build trust among the group.
The new regulations unintentionally undermine efforts to attract more investors.

Synset Examples

They tried to undermine the team's efforts with gossip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sabotage - deliberately destroy or damage something to prevent success
