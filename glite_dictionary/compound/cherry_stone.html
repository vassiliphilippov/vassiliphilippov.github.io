cherry stone

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛri stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri stəʊn / (BrE)
the hard stone-like seed found inside a cherry; the pit of a cherry
hard seed inside a cherry

Examples

Be careful not to swallow the cherry stone when eating fresh cherries.
He planted a cherry stone in the garden, hoping it would grow into a tree.
The jam was made with whole cherries, but the cherry stone had been removed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pit - the stone or hard seed inside certain fruits
