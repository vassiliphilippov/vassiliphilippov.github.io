time of arrival

1. NOUN

/ taɪm əv əˈraɪvəl / (AmE), / taɪm əv əˈraɪvəl / (BrE)
the scheduled or expected time when a vehicle or person is due to arrive at a destination
scheduled or expected arrival time

Examples

Please check the time of arrival for your train before heading to the station.
The airline updated us with the new time of arrival after the delay.
You should inform your host about your expected time of arrival so they can prepare.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arrival time - the time when someone or something arrives
  • Antonym: departure time - the time when someone or something leaves a place
