atmospheric state

1. NOUN

/ ˌætməsˈfɪrɪk steɪt / (AmE), / ˌatməsˈfɛrɪk steɪt / (BrE)
the condition of the atmosphere at a specific place and time, especially as described by its weather or climate
the condition of the atmosphere at a given time/place

Examples

The atmospheric state this morning was unusually calm, with barely a breath of wind.
Meteorologists use instruments to measure the atmospheric state before making forecasts.
A sudden shift in the atmospheric state caused the temperature to drop rapidly.

Synset Examples

the atmosphere was thick with fog

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weather - the state of the atmosphere at a particular place and time
  • Synonym: climate - the general or average atmospheric conditions of a region over a long period

Etymology

From 'atmosphere' (Greek atmos 'vapor' + sphaira 'sphere') and 'state' (Latin status 'condition').
