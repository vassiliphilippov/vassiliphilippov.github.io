cesspit of trash

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛsˌpɪt əv træʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɛs.pɪt əv træʃ / (BrE)
a place that is extremely dirty, unpleasant, or filled with waste and rubbish
filthy or disgusting place

Examples

The alley behind the restaurant became a cesspit of trash after weeks of neglect.
Critics called the neglected playground a cesspit of trash unfit for children.
They cleared out the garage, which had turned into a cesspit of trash over the years.

Synset Examples

The abandoned lot turned into a cesspit of trash.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dump - a place for discarded waste or refuse
  • Synonym: cesspool - a pit for receiving sewage or waste
