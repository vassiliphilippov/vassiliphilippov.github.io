peace of mind

1. NOUN

/ pis əv maɪnd / (AmE), / piːs əv maɪnd / (BrE)
A feeling of being calm and free from worry or anxiety.
state of calm without worry

Examples

After installing the security system, she finally felt a sense of peace of mind in her home.
Travel insurance gives many people peace of mind during their trips.
Meditation can help you achieve peace of mind in stressful times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tranquillity - a state of calm and peacefulness
  • Antonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease
