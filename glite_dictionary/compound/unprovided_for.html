unprovided for

1. ADJ

/ ʌnprəˈvaɪdəd fɔr / (AmE), / ʌnprəˈvaɪdɪd fɔː / (BrE)
Not supplied with what is necessary; not prepared or equipped for a situation or need.
not prepared or supplied

Examples

After losing his job, he found himself completely unprovided for in the city.
The sudden storm caught the hikers unprovided for the harsh weather.
Many elderly people are left unprovided for after retirement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unprepared - not ready or equipped
  • Antonym: prepared - ready; equipped with what is needed

/ ˌʌn.prəˈvaɪ.dɪd fɔr / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prəˈvaɪ.dɪd fɔː / (BrE)
without financial support, resources, or means for basic needs or maintenance
lacking financial support or means

Examples

Many retired individuals fear becoming unprovided for in old age.
He worried that his death would leave his family unprovided for.
