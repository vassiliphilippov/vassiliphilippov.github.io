taps aff announcement

1. NOUN

/ tæps æf əˈnaʊnsmənt / (AmE), / taps af əˈnaʊnsmənt / (BrE)
an announcement made to signal that alcohol will no longer be served, especially in a bar or pub
announcement to stop serving alcohol
british

Examples

After the taps aff announcement, customers finished their drinks and began to leave the bar.
The bartender made the taps aff announcement at midnight, ending the lively party.
Regulars groaned when the familiar taps aff announcement echoed through the crowded pub.

Synset Examples

The pub was quiet after the taps aff announcement.

Etymology

From 'taps aff' (taps off), Scots/Irish English for 'stop serving beer', with 'announcement'.
