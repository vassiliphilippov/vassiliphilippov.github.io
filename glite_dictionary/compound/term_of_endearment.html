term of endearment

1. NOUN

/ tɜrm əv ɪnˈdɪrmənt / (AmE), / tɜːm əv ɪnˈdɪəmənt / (BrE)
a word or phrase used to express affection toward someone
affectionate word or phrase

Examples

He called his daughter 'sweetheart', a common term of endearment in his family.
Many couples use special terms of endearment for each other in private.
In her letter, she included a gentle term of endearment to comfort her friend.

Synset Examples

She called her friend Pally as a term of endearment.
Habeebatee is a term of endearment in their relationship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pet name - a name expressing fondness or familiarity, often used for a loved one
