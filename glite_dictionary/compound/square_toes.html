square toes

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr toʊz / (AmE), / skwɛə təʊz / (BrE)
a person who is very formal and conservative in manner or views; someone with old-fashioned ideas
a formal, old-fashioned person

Examples

He was considered a square toes by his more progressive colleagues.
Despite working in a modern office, her square toes attitude made her seem out of touch.
They laughed at his square toes views on dating and relationships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conservative - a person who is averse to change and holds traditional values
  • Synonym: stick-in-the-mud - a person who is old-fashioned and resistant to change

Etymology

From 'square' (rigid, proper) and 'toes,' alluding to the angular, unfashionable footwear of earlier periods.
