school of medicine

1. NOUN

/ skuːl əv ˈmɛdəsən / (AmE), / skuːl əv ˈmɛdɪsɪn / (BrE)
a graduate institution providing advanced education and training in medicine, leading to a medical degree
graduate institution for medical education

Examples

She was accepted into the school of medicine after completing her bachelor's degree.
Applicants to the school of medicine must take the MCAT exam.
The university's school of medicine is known for its research in cardiology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medical school - a graduate school for training physicians
