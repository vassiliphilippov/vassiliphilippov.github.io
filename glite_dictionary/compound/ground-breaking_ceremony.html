ground-breaking ceremony

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡraʊndˌbreɪkɪŋ səˈrɛmoʊni / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊndˌbreɪkɪŋ səˈrɛməni / (BrE)
a ceremony held to mark the official start of construction on a building or other project
ceremony marking start of construction

Examples

Many dignitaries attended the ground-breaking ceremony for the new hospital.
The mayor gave a speech at the ground-breaking ceremony yesterday.
Photographers captured the moment the first shovel hit the earth during the ground-breaking ceremony.

Synset Examples

The ground-breaking ceremony attracted many local leaders.
