ten dollar bill

1. NOUN

/ tɛn ˈdɑlər bɪl / (AmE), / tɛn ˈdɒlə bɪl / (BrE)
A United States paper currency note with a value of ten dollars.
US currency note worth ten dollars
american

Examples

She handed the cashier a ten dollar bill to pay for the groceries.
I found a ten dollar bill in the pocket of my old jacket.
He exchanged a ten dollar bill for smaller denominations at the bank.
