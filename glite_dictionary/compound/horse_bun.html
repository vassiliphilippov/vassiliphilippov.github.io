horse bun

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs bʌn / (AmE), / hɔːs bʌn / (BrE)
A type of savory pastry, typically filled with spiced or seasoned meat or vegetables.
savory pastry, often filled

Examples

She tried a horse bun from the new bakery in town.
The horse buns are best served warm, fresh from the oven.
We ordered several varieties, but the spicy horse bun was my favorite.

Synset Examples
