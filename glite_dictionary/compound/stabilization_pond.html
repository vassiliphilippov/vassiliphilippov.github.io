stabilization pond

1. NOUN

/ ˌsteɪ.bɪ.ləˈzeɪ.ʃən pɑnd / (AmE), / ˌstæb.ɪ.lɪˈzeɪ.ʃən pɒnd / (BrE)
a large, shallow pond used to treat wastewater by allowing sunlight, bacteria, and algae to break down organic matter
pond for treating wastewater with sunlight and microorganisms

Examples

The new stabilization pond reduced the community’s sewage treatment costs significantly.
Engineers recommended building a stabilization pond to improve the quality of discharged water.
Routine maintenance is essential to keep a stabilization pond working efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lagoon - a pond or artificial basin for treating wastewater
