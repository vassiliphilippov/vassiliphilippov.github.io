air host

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr hoʊst / (AmE), / ˈeə həʊst / (BrE)
a male flight attendant on an airplane, responsible for the care and comfort of passengers during a flight
male flight attendant on an airplane

Examples

The air host welcomed passengers as they boarded the plane.
As an air host, he ensured everyone followed safety procedures during the flight.
During turbulence, the air host reassured anxious passengers with a calm voice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flight attendant - person responsible for passengers' comfort and safety on a plane
  • Antonym: air hostess - female flight attendant on an airplane
