packing material

1. NOUN

/ ˈpækɪŋ məˌtɪriəl / (AmE), / ˈpækɪŋ məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
material used to protect items during shipment, storage, or transportation
material for protecting shipped items

Examples

We used bubble wrap as packing material to keep the dishes from breaking.
Most packing material can be recycled to reduce waste.
The store sells different types of packing material, including foam peanuts and air pillows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: packaging - the wrapping or container in which something is packed
  • Synonym: padding - soft material used to protect or support
