Sayanci language

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈjɑːntʃi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / saɪˈjantʃi ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in northern Nigeria, especially in Bauchi State, Tafawa Balewa Local Government Area.
a Chadic language of northern Nigeria

Examples

The Sayanci language is part of the West Chadic group.
Efforts are being made to preserve the Sayanci language among younger generations.
Linguists have studied the grammar of the Sayanci language extensively.
