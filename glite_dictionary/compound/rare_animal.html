rare animal

1. NOUN

/ rɛr ˈænɪməl / (AmE), / reə ˈænɪməl / (BrE)
an animal of a species that has a very low population and is at risk of extinction or is seldom seen
animal species with low population

Examples

The zoo is home to several rare animals that are not found anywhere else in the country.
Conservationists work tirelessly to protect rare animals from illegal hunting and habitat loss.
It’s thrilling to spot a rare animal in the wild, knowing how few remain.

Synset Examples

The conservation program aims to protect rare animals from extinction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endangered species - species at serious risk of extinction
