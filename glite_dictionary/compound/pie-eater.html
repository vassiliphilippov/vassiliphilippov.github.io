pie-eater

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪ ˌitɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ ˌiːtə / (BrE)
A humorous nickname for a person who enjoys eating pie.
person who loves eating pie (humorous)

Examples

Everyone at the party called Jake a pie-eater after he had three helpings of dessert.
When the bakery announced a pie-eating contest, every pie-eater in town showed up.
As a proud pie-eater, Marnie never missed an opportunity to try new recipes.

Synset Examples

Being a pie-eater, she baked every flavor imaginable.
He’s known as the pie-eater after winning the dessert contest.
