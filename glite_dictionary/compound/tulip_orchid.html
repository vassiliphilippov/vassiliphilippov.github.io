tulip orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːlɪp ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈtjuːlɪp ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A Mexican epiphytic orchid with grey-green leaves and partially opened yellow flowers, sometimes classified in the genus Cattleya.
a Mexican orchid with yellow flowers

Examples

The tulip orchid is prized by collectors for its striking yellow blossoms.
We saw a rare tulip orchid growing high in the branches of a Mexican oak tree.
Many horticulturists debate whether the tulip orchid belongs in its own genus or in Cattleya.
