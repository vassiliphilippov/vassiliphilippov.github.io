Davis' Birthday

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪvɪs ˈbɝθ.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪvɪs ˈbɜːθ.deɪ / (BrE)
a holiday commemorating the birth of Jefferson Davis, observed on June 3rd in parts of the southern United States
U.S. holiday for Jefferson Davis's birth

Examples

Schools in Mississippi used to close in honor of Davis' Birthday.
Some southern states continue to recognize Davis' Birthday as a holiday.
The controversy over observing Davis' Birthday has grown in recent years.
