risk of infection

1. NOUN

/ ɹɪsk əv ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / ɹɪsk əv ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
the likelihood or probability of becoming infected by a disease or pathogen, especially after exposure
likelihood of contracting an infection
medical

Examples

Wearing gloves helps reduce the risk of infection while treating patients.
After surgery, the risk of infection is highest in the first week.
Doctors advised him to avoid crowded places to minimize his risk of infection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: risk of disease - probability of contracting a disease
  • Synonym: exposure - contact with a disease-causing agent
