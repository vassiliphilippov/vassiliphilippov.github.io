worm salamander

1. NOUN

/ wɜrm səˈlændər / (AmE), / wɜːm səˈlændə / (BrE)
a small, slender salamander found on the Pacific coast of the United States, known for its worm-like appearance
slender Pacific coast salamander

Examples

The worm salamander hides under logs in moist forests along the coast.
Biologists study the worm salamander because of its unique adaptation to underground life.
We spotted a worm salamander during our hike in northern California.
