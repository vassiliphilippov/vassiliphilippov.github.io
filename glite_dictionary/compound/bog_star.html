bog star

1. NOUN

/ bɑɡ stɑr / (AmE), / bɒɡ stɑː / (BrE)
a perennial plant with oval leaves in a basal rosette and white starlike flowers marked with green lines, typically found in wet habitats
a wetland plant with star-shaped white flowers

Examples

The bog star is often found growing near ponds and marshy areas.
Botanists admire the delicate beauty of the bog star during its blooming season.
She photographed a cluster of bog stars sparkling in the wet peatland.
