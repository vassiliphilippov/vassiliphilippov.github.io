Northern Spy

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðərn spaɪ / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən spaɪ / (BrE)
a large late-ripening apple variety with yellow and red striped skin, known for its crisp flesh and tart flavor
variety of large, striped apple

Examples

The orchard specializes in Northern Spy apples, which are perfect for baking pies.
I bit into a crisp Northern Spy and enjoyed its tart, juicy flavor.
This season, the market has a good selection of Northern Spy apples for sale.
