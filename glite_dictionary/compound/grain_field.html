grain field

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪn ˌfiːld / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪn ˌfiːld / (BrE)
a field in which grain crops such as wheat, barley, or oats are grown
field where grain is cultivated

Examples

Golden grain fields stretched as far as the eye could see across the plain.
The farmer was busy harvesting his grain field before the rain started.
Children ran laughing along the edge of the grain field on a summer evening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wheat field - field where wheat is grown
  • Synonym: cornfield - field where maize or corn is grown
