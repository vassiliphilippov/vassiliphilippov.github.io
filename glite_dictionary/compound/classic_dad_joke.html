classic dad joke

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæsɪk dæd d͡ʒoʊk / (AmE), / ˈklæsɪk dad d͡ʒəʊk / (BrE)
a simple, pun-filled joke regarded as typical of those made by fathers, often considered humorously predictable or groan-worthy
a simple, pun-based father's joke

Examples

He couldn't resist telling a classic dad joke at the family barbecue.
Everyone groaned at her classic dad joke about the calendar having too many dates.
The book is filled with classic dad jokes that are sure to make you smile.

Synset Examples

He told a classic dad joke at dinner last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dad joke - a simple, pun-based joke often attributed to fathers
