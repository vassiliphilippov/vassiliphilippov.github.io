shrimp sauce

1. NOUN

/ ʃrɪmp sɔs / (AmE), / ʃrɪmp sɔːs / (BrE)
a white sauce made with whipping cream and shrimp butter, typically served with seafood dishes
creamy sauce with shrimp butter

Examples

The chef poured a generous amount of shrimp sauce over the poached fish.
This recipe calls for homemade shrimp sauce to enhance the flavor of seafood pasta.
She could taste the rich whipping cream and shrimp butter in the shrimp sauce.
