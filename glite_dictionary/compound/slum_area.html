slum area

1. NOUN

/ slʌm ˈɛriə / (AmE), / slʌm ˈeəriə / (BrE)
An urban district characterized by poverty, overcrowding, and poor living conditions.
urban district with poverty and poor conditions

Examples

Many families were forced to live in the slum area after losing their homes.
The government has announced plans to renovate the city's largest slum area.
Volunteers provide health care services to children living in the slum area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slum - a very poor and crowded area of a city
