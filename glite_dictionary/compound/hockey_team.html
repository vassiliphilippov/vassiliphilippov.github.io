hockey team

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑki tim / (AmE), / ˈhɒki tiːm / (BrE)
a group of players who form a team to compete in the sport of ice hockey
team playing ice hockey

Examples

Our local hockey team won the championship for the second year in a row.
She dreams of playing professionally for a national hockey team someday.
The coach praised the hockey team for its teamwork and dedication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice hockey - a team sport played on ice with sticks and a puck
