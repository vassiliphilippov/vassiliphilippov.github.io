just now

1. ADV

/ dʒʌst naʊ / (AmE), / dʒʌst naʊ / (BrE)
a very short time ago; only a moment ago
a moment ago

Examples

She left just now, so you might catch her outside.
I was thinking about you just now when you called.
The bus passed by just now, so we'll have to wait for the next one.
