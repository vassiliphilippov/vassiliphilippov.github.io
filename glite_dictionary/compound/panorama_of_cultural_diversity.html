panorama of cultural diversity

1. NOUN

/ pænəˈrɑmə əv ˈkʌltʃərəl daɪˈvɜrsəti / (AmE), / pænəˈrɑːmə əv ˈkʌltʃərəl daɪˈvɜːsəti / (BrE)
a comprehensive or wide-ranging view that showcases the variety of cultures within a particular context or area
wide view of cultural variety

Examples

The exhibition offered a fascinating panorama of cultural diversity from across the globe.
Her writing presents a vivid panorama of cultural diversity in the city’s neighborhoods.
The school curriculum now includes a panorama of cultural diversity to promote understanding among students.

Synset Examples

The documentary provided a panorama of cultural diversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cultural diversity - the existence of a variety of cultural or ethnic groups within a society
  • Degree Lower: panorama - an unbroken view of an entire surrounding area
