Panama redwood tree

1. NOUN

/ ˌpænəˌmɑː ˈrɛdˌwʊd ˌtriː / (AmE), / ˌpænəmə ˈrɛdwʊd ˌtriː / (BrE)
a large shrub native to Colombia, with large pinnate leaves and racemes of fragrant yellow flowers
Colombian shrub with yellow flowers

Examples

The Panama redwood tree can be found growing wild in certain regions of Colombia.
Botanists admire the Panama redwood tree for its vibrant yellow flowers and large leaves.
We planted a Panama redwood tree in the botanical garden to enhance its collection of native species.
