bar-room plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑr ruːm plænt / (AmE), / ˈbɑː ruːm plɑːnt / (BrE)
An evergreen perennial with large, attractive basal leaves, often grown indoors as a foliage houseplant.
houseplant with large foliage leaves

Examples

The bar-room plant in the corner thrives even in low light conditions.
She chose a bar-room plant to add a touch of green to her apartment.
Many people find the bar-room plant easy to care for and quite decorative.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aspidistra - a genus of plants commonly known as the bar-room plant
