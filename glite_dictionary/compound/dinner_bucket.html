dinner bucket

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnər ˌbʌkət / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə ˌbʌkɪt / (BrE)
a pail used by a worker to carry their lunch or dinner, especially to a job site
pail for carrying lunch or dinner

Examples

The miner picked up his dinner bucket before heading to work.
In those days, everyone brought a dinner bucket filled with sandwiches and coffee.
My grandfather used to hang his dinner bucket on a hook by the door every evening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunch pail - a container for carrying lunch, similar to dinner bucket
