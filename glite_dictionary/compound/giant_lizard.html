giant lizard

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt ˈlɪzərd / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
a very large species of lizard, specifically the world's largest, found on certain Indonesian islands; notably the Komodo dragon
very large lizard, esp. Komodo dragon

Examples

The giant lizard lurked in the underbrush, watching for its next meal.
Tourists travel to Indonesia hoping to catch a glimpse of the legendary giant lizard.
Scientists study the giant lizard to understand how such large reptiles survive in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Komodo dragon - the largest living lizard, native to Indonesia
