vibratory plate compactor

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪbrəˌtɔri pleɪt kəmˈpæktɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɪbrətəri pleɪt kəmˈpæktə / (BrE)
an engine-powered machine with a vibrating flat plate, used for compacting soil, sand, gravel, and similar materials in construction work
machine for compacting surfaces in construction

Examples

The workers used a vibratory plate compactor to ensure a firm foundation for the new driveway.
It's essential to operate a vibratory plate compactor correctly to avoid uneven surfaces.
Rental shops often provide a vibratory plate compactor for small paving projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plate compactor - machine used to compact soil or asphalt in construction
  • Degree Higher: road roller - heavy vehicle with a large drum used for compacting surfaces
