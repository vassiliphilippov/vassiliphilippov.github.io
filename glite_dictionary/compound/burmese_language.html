Burmese language

1. NOUN

/ bərˈmiz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / bɜːˈmiːz ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the official language spoken in Myanmar (Burma)
official language of Myanmar (Burma)

Examples

She is fluent in Burmese language and often acts as a translator for her family.
The Burmese language uses a unique script that differs from the Roman alphabet.
Learning the Burmese language can help travelers communicate more easily in Myanmar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Myanmar - a country in Southeast Asia, also known as Burma
  • Synonym: Burmese - relating to Burma (Myanmar) or its people or language

Etymology

'Burmese' from Burma (the former name of Myanmar), plus 'language'
