true religion

1. NOUN

/ tru rɪˈlɪdʒən / (AmE), / truː rɪˈlɪdʒən / (BrE)
a faith regarded as the ultimate or correct one, often in contrast to other religions
belief system considered ultimately correct

Examples

Some people believe their faith is the true religion and try to persuade others to convert.
Debates about what constitutes the true religion have divided communities for centuries.
He searched for the true religion that would give him meaning and guidance.

Synset Examples

They advocate that their beliefs are a true religion of peace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orthodoxy - authorized or generally accepted theory or doctrine, especially in religion
