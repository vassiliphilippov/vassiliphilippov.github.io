run-of-the-mine

1. ADJ

/ ˌrʌn əv ðə ˈmaɪn / (AmE), / ˌrʌn əv ðə ˈmaɪn / (BrE)
Ordinary; not special or remarkable in any way.
ordinary or unremarkable

Examples

Most candidates reviewed were run-of-the-mine applicants, with nothing unusual in their backgrounds.
The house was just another run-of-the-mine suburban home, indistinguishable from its neighbors.
Their performance was fairly run-of-the-mine, not particularly memorable but not terrible either.

Synset Examples

run-of-the-mill boxing
your run-of-the-mine college graduate
as unexceptional an incident as can be found in a lawyer's career

Related Terms

  • Synonym: run-of-the-mill - not special or outstanding; ordinary

Etymology

Originally referring to coal as it comes directly from a mine, unsorted and ungraded.
