rock bit

1. NOUN

/ rɑk bɪt / (AmE), / rɒk bɪt / (BrE)
a drill bit with hardened rotating rollers, designed for drilling through rock
drill bit with rotating rollers for rock

Examples

The engineers chose a rock bit to tackle the hard granite layer.
Regular maintenance of the rock bit is crucial for efficient drilling operations.
He replaced the worn-out rock bit before starting to drill the new well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drill bit - cutting tool for drilling holes
