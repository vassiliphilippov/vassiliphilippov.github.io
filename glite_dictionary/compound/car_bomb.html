car bomb

1. NOUN

/ kɑr bɑm / (AmE), / kɑː bɒm / (BrE)
a bomb hidden in or attached to a car, designed to explode by ignition, remote control, or timer
bomb concealed in a car

Examples

The explosion was caused by a hidden car bomb parked outside the building.
Authorities discovered a car bomb before it could be detonated near the airport.
Many cities have increased security to prevent car bombs during major events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bomb - an explosive weapon that detonates under certain conditions
