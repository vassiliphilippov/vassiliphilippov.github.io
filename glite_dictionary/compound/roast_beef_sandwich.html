roast beef sandwich

1. NOUN

/ roʊst bif ˈsænwɪtʃ / (AmE), / rəʊst biːf ˈsænwɪtʃ / (BrE)
A sandwich filled with slices of roast beef, typically served cold or hot.
sandwich with roast beef filling

Would you like your roast beef sandwich with horseradish or mayonnaise?
