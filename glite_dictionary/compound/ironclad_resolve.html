ironclad resolve

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪərnˌklæd rɪˈzɑlv / (AmE), / ˈaɪənˌklæd rɪˈzɒlv / (BrE)
a firm and unwavering determination that cannot be easily changed or weakened
unbreakable determination

Examples

Despite the challenges, her ironclad resolve never wavered throughout the ordeal.
The team's ironclad resolve helped them overcome every obstacle in their path.
He approached every task with ironclad resolve, refusing to quit or make excuses.

Synset Examples

His ironclad resolve kept him focused on his goals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determination - the quality of being determined; firmness of purpose

Etymology

The adjective 'ironclad' (literally 'armored with iron') is used figuratively here to mean strong or unbreakable.
