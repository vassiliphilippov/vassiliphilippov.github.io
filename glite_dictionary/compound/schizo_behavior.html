schizo behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪtsoʊ bɪˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / ˈskɪtsəʊ bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
Erratic or unpredictable behavior; behavior that appears inconsistent or unstable. Considered offensive due to its use of a mental health term.
erratic or unpredictable behavior (offensive)
informal offensive

Examples

His schizo behavior made it hard to predict his actions.
People grew frustrated with her schizo behavior at work, never knowing what to expect.
Some described the team's schizo behavior in the playoffs as a sign of deeper issues.

Synset Examples

His schizo behavior made it hard to predict his actions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: erratic behavior - unpredictable or inconsistent actions
  • Antonym: stable behavior - consistent and steady actions

Etymology

Formed from 'schizo', abbreviated from 'schizophrenic', plus 'behavior'.
