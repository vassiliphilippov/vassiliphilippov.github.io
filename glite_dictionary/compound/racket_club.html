racket club

1. NOUN

/ ˈræk.ɪt ˌklʌb / (AmE), / ˈræk.ɪt ˌklʌb / (BrE)
a club where members play racket sports such as tennis, squash, or badminton
club for racket sports

Examples

She joined the local racket club to improve her tennis skills.
The racket club offers both squash and badminton courts for its members.
Many tournaments are hosted at the downtown racket club each year.
