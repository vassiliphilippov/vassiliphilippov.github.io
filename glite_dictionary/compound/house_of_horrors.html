house of horrors

1. NOUN

/ haʊs əv ˈhɔɹɚz / (AmE), / haʊs əv ˈhɒɹəz / (BrE)
a place or building marked by terrifying or gruesome experiences, often associated with fear, suffering, or horror
place of terrifying or gruesome events

Examples

The old asylum had become a house of horrors, with a dark history visitors whispered about.
Investigators discovered the kidnappers' hideout was a real-life house of horrors.
Many horror films feature a house of horrors as their central setting.

Synset Examples

The movie depicted a house of horrors filled with ghosts.
Their haunted tour led them to a house of horrors.
The abandoned mansion became a house of horrors for thrill-seekers.
