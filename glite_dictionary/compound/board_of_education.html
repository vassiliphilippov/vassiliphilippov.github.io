board of education

1. NOUN

/ bɔrd əv ɛdʒəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / bɔːd əv ˌedʒʊˈkeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
A group of people officially responsible for the management and supervision of local public schools.
official group managing local schools

Examples

The board of education approved new funding for the district's science programs.
She was elected to the board of education last year.
The board of education meets monthly to discuss school policy changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: school board - a board in charge of local public schools
