Swiss stone pine

1. NOUN

/ swɪs stoʊn paɪn / (AmE), / swɪs stəʊn paɪn / (BrE)
a large pine tree native to central Europe, with five-needled clusters and edible seeds called cembra nuts
European pine tree; source of cembra nuts

Examples

The Swiss stone pine is valued for its beautiful wood and edible seeds.
During our hike, we admired the tall Swiss stone pine trees dotting the alpine slopes.
Cooks use nuts from the Swiss stone pine to add flavor to traditional European dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pine - a type of evergreen coniferous tree
