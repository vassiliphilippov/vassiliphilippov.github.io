xanthodont species

1. NOUN

/ zænˈθoʊdɑnt ˈspiːʃiːz / (AmE), / zænˈθɒdənt ˈspiːʃiːz / (BrE)
A species characterized by having yellow teeth.
species with yellow teeth

Examples

The fossil record shows several xanthodont species in the region.
Researchers identified the rodent as one of the rare xanthodont species.
Unlike their relatives, xanthodont species have distinctive yellow-stained teeth.

Synset Examples

The xanthodont species are known for their bright yellow teeth.

Etymology

From Greek xanthos (yellow) + odous, odont- (tooth).
