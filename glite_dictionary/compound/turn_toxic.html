to turn toxic

1. VERB

/ tɝn ˈtɑksɪk / (AmE), / tɜːn ˈtɒksɪk / (BrE)
(of a situation, environment, or relationship) to become extremely unpleasant, harmful, or negative.
become harmful or very unpleasant

Examples

After months of stress, the team's atmosphere turned toxic and collaboration became impossible.
The online forum turned toxic when the discussion grew heated and personal attacks began.
Their friendship turned toxic after repeated betrayals and constant arguments.

Synset Examples

The workplace turned toxic after the new management arrived.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toxic - poisonous; very harmful or unpleasant
