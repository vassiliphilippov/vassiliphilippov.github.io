Washington Monument

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɑʃɪŋtən ˈmɑnjəmənt / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃɪŋtən ˈmɒnjʊmənt / (BrE)
A tall stone obelisk in Washington, D.C., built to honor George Washington, the first president of the United States.
obelisk in Washington, D.C. honoring George Washington

Examples

Many tourists visit the Washington Monument every year when they come to the nation's capital.
He proposed to his partner at the top of the Washington Monument.
The Washington Monument is visible from many parts of Washington, D.C.
