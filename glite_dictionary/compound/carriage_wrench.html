carriage wrench

1. NOUN

/ ˈkærɪdʒ rɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˈkærɪdʒ rɛntʃ / (BrE)
A wrench specifically designed for use with carriage bolts.
wrench used with carriage bolts

Examples

The mechanic used a carriage wrench to tighten the bolts on the wagon's frame.
Before you begin assembly, make sure you have a carriage wrench handy for the carriage bolts.
Old toolboxes often included a carriage wrench alongside other specialized wrenches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spanner - a tool for gripping and turning nuts or bolts
  • Degree Lower: carriage bolt - a type of bolt with a round head and a square neck
