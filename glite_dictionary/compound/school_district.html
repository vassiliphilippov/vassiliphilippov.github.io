school district

1. NOUN

/ skuːl ˈdɪstrɪkt / (AmE), / skuːl ˈdɪstrɪkt / (BrE)
an area with defined boundaries whose public schools are administered by a common local authority
administrative area for public schools

Examples

The children in our neighborhood all attend schools in the same school district.
The school district announced a new policy for bus transportation this year.
She works as a superintendent for the local school district.
