visiting fireman

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪzɪtɪŋ ˈfaɪərmən / (AmE), / ˈvɪzɪtɪŋ ˈfaɪəmən / (BrE)
an important or distinguished visitor, especially one on a business or official visit
important visitor, often on business

Examples

The company is hosting a visiting fireman from their headquarters this week.
As a visiting fireman, he toured several branch offices during his short stay.
Whenever a visiting fireman arrives, everyone rushes to make a good impression.

Etymology

From US slang, probably alluding to a fire department official touring other cities for professional purposes.
