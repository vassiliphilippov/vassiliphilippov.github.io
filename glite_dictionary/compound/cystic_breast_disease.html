cystic breast disease

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪstɪk brɛst dɪˌziz / (AmE), / ˈsɪstɪk brɛst dɪˌziːz / (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by the presence of one or more cysts in the breast tissue.
condition with breast cysts
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with cystic breast disease after finding several cysts during the examination.
Women with cystic breast disease may experience breast pain or tenderness.
Regular monitoring is often recommended for those with cystic breast disease to track any changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fibrocystic breast disease - a benign condition causing lumpy, painful breasts, often with cysts
