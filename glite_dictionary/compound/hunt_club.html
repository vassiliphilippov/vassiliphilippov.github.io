hunt club

1. NOUN

/ hʌnt klʌb / (AmE), / hʌnt klʌb / (BrE)
An organization or association of people who gather to hunt for sport.
association of sport hunters

Examples

He joined a hunt club to pursue his interest in game bird hunting.
The annual dinner at the hunt club attracted dozens of local hunters.
Many hunt clubs organize conservation activities alongside their sporting events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hunting lodge - a building used by hunters for accommodation while hunting
