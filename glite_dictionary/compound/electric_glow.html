electric glow

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ɡloʊ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ɡləʊ / (BrE)
a visible light emitted by an electrical discharge that ionizes the surrounding atmosphere
visible light from an electrical discharge

Examples

The faint electric glow surrounded the high-voltage wires on the damp night.
Scientists studied the electric glow produced by the ionization of the gas.
A visible electric glow can sometimes be seen at the tip of radio antennas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corona discharge - ionized electrical discharge around a conductor
