joy incarnate

1. NOUN

/ dʒɔɪ ɪnˈkɑrnət / (AmE), / dʒɔɪ ɪnˈkɑːnət / (BrE)
A person who perfectly embodies or personifies joy.
someone who perfectly embodies joy

Examples

She was joy incarnate as she danced through the room, lighting up everyone's spirits.
His laughter made him seem like joy incarnate during the festivities.
Whenever he arrives, it feels like joy incarnate has entered the building.

Synset Examples

He seemed to be joy incarnate at the celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personification - the representation of an abstract quality in human form

Etymology

From 'joy' (pleasure or happiness) and 'incarnate' (from Latin 'incarnatus', made flesh).
