.38-caliber

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɜrtiˈeɪt ˈkælɪbɚ / (AmE), / ˌθɜːtiˈeɪt ˈkælɪbə / (BrE)
of a gun or its ammunition having a bore diameter of 0.38 inches (9.65 mm)
of a firearm with a 0.38-inch bore

Examples

The detective carried a .38-caliber revolver in his holster.
A .38-caliber bullet was recovered from the scene of the crime.
He bought a box of .38-caliber ammunition for target practice.

Synset Examples

a .38 caliber shell

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: .22-caliber - of a gun or ammunition with a 0.22-inch bore
  • Degree Higher: .45-caliber - of a gun or ammunition with a 0.45-inch bore

Etymology

From the numeric measurement (0.38 inch) of the bore diameter.
