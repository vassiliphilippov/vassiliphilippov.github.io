trash panda

1. NOUN

/ træʃ ˈpændə / (AmE), / træʃ ˈpændə / (BrE)
A raccoon, referred to humorously due to its habit of rummaging through garbage.
humorous slang for raccoon
slang

Examples

Last night, a trash panda knocked over our recycling bin in search of food.
Some city dwellers have started to call raccoons trash pandas because of their bandit-like appearance.
If you leave pet food outside, you might attract a curious trash panda.

Synset Examples

The raccoon rummaging through the bins is such a trash panda.
I saw a trash panda steal my sandwich from the picnic.
That adorable trash panda just loves to dig through our garbage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raccoon - a small North American mammal with a black mask-like band across the eyes
