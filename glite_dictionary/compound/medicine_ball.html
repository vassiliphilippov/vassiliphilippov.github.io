medicine ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdəsɪn bɔl / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪsɪn bɔːl / (BrE)
a heavy, usually leather-covered ball used for exercise and physical training to improve strength and coordination
heavy ball used for physical training

Examples

The trainer instructed us to throw the medicine ball back and forth to improve our coordination.
She incorporated a medicine ball into her core workout for added resistance.
You can use a medicine ball to make squats more challenging during your fitness routine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exercise ball - large, inflatable ball used for exercise

Etymology

From 'medicine' (referring to health) and 'ball', first attested in the late 19th century in American English.
