trouble-free

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrʌbəl fri / (AmE), / ˈtrʌbəl ˌfriː / (BrE)
Not likely to experience problems or difficulties; without trouble.
without problems or difficulties

Examples

The new system is designed to provide a trouble-free experience for all users.
We enjoyed a trouble-free journey to the countryside last weekend.
Her time at the company was largely trouble-free, with no major issues arising.

Synset Examples

NASA reported a trouble-free launch

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smooth - without problems or difficulties
  • Antonym: difficult - needing much effort or causing problems
