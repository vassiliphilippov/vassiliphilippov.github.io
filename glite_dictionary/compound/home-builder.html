home-builder

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊm ˌbɪldər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊm ˌbɪldə / (BrE)
a person or company that builds houses, especially as a commercial business
person or company that builds houses

Examples

The home-builder presented the newly finished houses to the excited families.
As a home-builder, she prides herself on quality and customer satisfaction.
The local home-builders association hosted a tour of modern model homes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: builder - a person who builds, especially as a profession
  • Synonym: developer - a person or company that develops real estate
