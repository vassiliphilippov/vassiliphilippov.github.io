Sultan of Swat

1. PROPN

/ ˈsʌltən əv swɑt / (AmE), / ˈsʌltən əv swɒt / (BrE)
A nickname for Babe Ruth, the famous American baseball player renowned for his home runs (1895–1948).
nickname of baseball player Babe Ruth

Examples

Babe Ruth was often called the Sultan of Swat for his incredible power at the plate.
Baseball history books frequently refer to the legendary Sultan of Swat.
The Sultan of Swat broke countless records during his illustrious career.

Related Terms
