elastic band

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlæstɪk bænd / (AmE), / ɪˈlæstɪk bænd / (BrE)
a thin, narrow loop of stretchable rubber used to hold objects together, such as papers or small items
narrow stretchable loop for holding things together

Examples

She used an elastic band to keep the stack of letters together.
The bundle of pencils was secured with a red elastic band.
After opening the package, he snapped the elastic band around his wrist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rubber band - a loop of rubber used to hold objects together; chiefly American English equivalent
