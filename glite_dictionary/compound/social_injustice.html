social injustice

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊ.ʃəl ɪnˈdʒʌs.tɪs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃəl ɪnˈdʒʌs.tɪs / (BrE)
Unfair treatment or inequality within a society, typically resulting from discrimination or prejudice against certain groups.
unfair treatment in society due to discrimination

Examples

Many activists dedicate their lives to fighting social injustice.
The film shed light on historical social injustice experienced by marginalized communities.
Addressing social injustice requires changes in both laws and attitudes.

Synset Examples

The documentary highlighted the injustice faced by minorities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discrimination - unjust or prejudicial treatment of different groups, especially on racial, sexual, or age grounds
  • Synonym: inequality - lack of equality, especially in status, rights, or opportunities
  • Antonym: justice - fair treatment and behavior
