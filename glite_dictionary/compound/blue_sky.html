blue sky

1. NOUN

/ bluː skaɪ / (AmE), / bluː skaɪ / (BrE)
the sky as it appears blue during daylight hours, typically when clear of clouds
the sky as seen in daylight

Examples

The children spent the afternoon lying under the blue sky and watching the clouds drift by.
He painted the landscape with a bright blue sky stretching across the canvas.
After days of rain, the blue sky finally reappeared, lifting everyone's spirits.

Synset Examples

he shot an arrow into the blue

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sky - the region of the atmosphere and outer space seen from the earth
