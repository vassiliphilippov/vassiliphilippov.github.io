tummy crunch

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌmi krʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈtʌmi krʌntʃ / (BrE)
an exercise involving repeated contraction of the abdominal muscles by lifting the shoulders towards the pelvis, aimed at strengthening the core
abdominal strengthening exercise

Examples

Doing tummy crunches daily can help develop stronger core muscles.
My fitness instructor demonstrated the correct way to perform a tummy crunch.
She prefers tummy crunches over sit-ups because they're easier on her back.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sit-up - an abdominal exercise involving raising the torso to a sitting position from lying flat
