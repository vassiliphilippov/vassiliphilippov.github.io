raisin bran

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪzən bræn / (AmE), / ˈreɪzɪn bræn / (BrE)
A breakfast cereal consisting of bran flakes mixed with raisins.
breakfast cereal with bran flakes and raisins

Examples

Every morning, I have a bowl of raisin bran with cold milk.
She bought a large box of raisin bran from the supermarket yesterday.
Some people add extra raisins to their raisin bran for a sweeter taste.
