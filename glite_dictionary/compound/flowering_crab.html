flowering crab

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ kræb / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ər.ɪŋ kræb / (BrE)
A small deciduous tree or shrub of the southeastern United States, cultivated as an ornamental for its showy rose-colored blossoms.
ornamental tree with rose-colored flowers

Examples

The flowering crab in our front yard is covered with pink blooms every spring.
Gardeners often plant a flowering crab for its attractive blossoms and compact size.
The delicate scent from the flowering crab filled the park as the trees began to bloom.

2. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊɚɪŋ kræb / (AmE), / ˈflaʊərɪŋ krab / (BrE)
A variety of crabapple tree cultivated for its large double pink blossoms, often grown as an ornamental plant.
ornamental crabapple tree with pink blossoms

Examples

The flowering crab in the front yard is covered in beautiful pink blossoms every spring.
Gardeners often choose a flowering crab to add color and charm to their landscape.
After the long winter, the flowering crab provides a welcome burst of color.
