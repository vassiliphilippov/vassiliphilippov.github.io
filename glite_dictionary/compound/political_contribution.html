political contribution

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪdɪkəl ˌkɑntrəˈbjuʃən / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˌkɒntrɪˈbjuːʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a sum of money or other asset given to support a politician, political campaign, or political party, often to influence policy or election outcomes
donation to a politician, campaign, or party

Examples

The senator disclosed every political contribution he received during his campaign.
Strict laws limit the amount of political contributions individuals can make.
She refused to accept any political contribution from corporations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: donation - something that is given, especially to a charity or cause
