to relieve muscle tension

1. VERB

/ rɪˈliv ˈmʌsəl ˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈliːv ˈmʌsl̩ ˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
to reduce the feeling of tightness or stiffness in the muscles
reduce tightness in muscles

Examples

A massage can relieve muscle tension after a long workout.
Gentle stretching is often recommended to relieve muscle tension.
He soaked in a hot tub to relieve muscle tension in his back.

Synset Examples

A warm bath can help relieve muscle tension.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relax muscles - make the muscles less tight or stiff
