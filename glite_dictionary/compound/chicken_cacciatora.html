chicken cacciatora

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkən ˌkɑː(t)ʃəˈtɔːrə / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn ˌkætʃɪˈtɔːrə / (BrE)
a chicken casserole prepared with tomatoes, mushrooms, and herbs in the Italian style
Italian-style chicken casserole with tomatoes and herbs

Examples

For dinner, we served chicken cacciatora with a side of crusty bread.
The restaurant's chicken cacciatora features tender meat and a rich tomato sauce.
She learned to make chicken cacciatora from an old family recipe passed down through generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken chasseur - similar French-style chicken stew with tomatoes, mushrooms, and herbs

Etymology

From Italian 'cacciatore' meaning 'hunter', as in 'alla cacciatora' ('hunter-style').
