cows' milk

1. NOUN

/ kaʊz mɪlk / (AmE), / kaʊz mɪlk / (BrE)
Milk obtained from dairy cows, as opposed to milk from other animals or plant-based alternatives.
milk from dairy cows

Examples

Many people are allergic to cows' milk and need to find alternative sources of calcium.
The recipe calls for one cup of cows' milk instead of plant-based milk.
She buys organic cows' milk from a local farm every week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milk - a white liquid produced by the mammary glands of mammals, used as food by humans
