sexual relationship

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl rɪˈleɪʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃʊəl rɪˈleɪʃ(ə)nʃɪp / (BrE)
a relationship between two people that involves sexual intimacy or sexual activity with each other
relationship involving sexual intimacy

Examples

After a year of friendship, they began a sexual relationship that changed their dynamic.
The company has strict rules regarding sexual relationships between employees and supervisors.
Their marriage evolved into a partnership without a sexual relationship but full of mutual respect.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: platonic relationship - a relationship that does not involve sexual intimacy
