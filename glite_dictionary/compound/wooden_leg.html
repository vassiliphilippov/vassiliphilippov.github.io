wooden leg

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdən lɛg / (AmE), / ˈwʊd(ə)n lɛg / (BrE)
an artificial leg, especially one made of wood, used to replace a missing leg
an artificial leg, usually made of wood

Examples

After the accident, he learned to walk with a wooden leg.
The old pirate was famous for his black hat and wooden leg.
In the museum, there was a display of a Civil War-era wooden leg.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prosthetic leg - an artificial replacement for a leg
