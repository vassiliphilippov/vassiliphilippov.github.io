unlisted stock

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈlɪstɪd stɑk / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪstɪd stɒk / (BrE)
Shares of a company that are not listed or traded on an organized stock exchange.
stock not traded on an exchange

Examples

Investors should be cautious when buying unlisted stock because of the higher risks involved.
The company's unlisted stock is available only through private transactions or over-the-counter markets.
He decided to diversify his portfolio by including some unlisted stock from emerging startups.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: listed stock - stock that is listed and traded on an organized exchange
