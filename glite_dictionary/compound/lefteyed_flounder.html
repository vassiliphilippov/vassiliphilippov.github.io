lefteyed flounder

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛftˌaɪd ˈflaʊndər / (AmE), / ˈlɛftˌaɪd ˈflaʊndə / (BrE)
A flatfish having both eyes on the left side of its head.
type of flatfish with eyes on left side

Examples

The lefteyed flounder is adapted to living on the ocean floor with both eyes on one side.
Scientists studied the camouflage abilities of the lefteyed flounder in its natural habitat.
Fishermen often catch lefteyed flounders along sandy coasts in temperate regions.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: righteyed flounder - flatfish with both eyes on the right side of the head
