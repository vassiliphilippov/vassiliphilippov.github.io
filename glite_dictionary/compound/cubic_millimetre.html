cubic millimetre

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjuːbɪk ˈmɪlɪˌmitər / (AmE), / ˈkjuːbɪk ˈmɪlɪˌmiːtə / (BrE)
a unit of volume equal to the volume of a cube with sides that each measure one millimeter; equal to one millionth of a liter
a metric unit of volume (mm³)

Examples

A single drop of water is roughly fifty cubic millimetres in volume.
Scientists measured the tumour and found it was just three cubic millimetres in size.
The required dosage is less than one cubic millimetre per injection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millilitre - a metric unit of volume equal to one-thousandth of a liter
  • Degree Higher: litre - a metric unit of capacity, equal to 1,000 cubic centimeters
