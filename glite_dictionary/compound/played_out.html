played out

1. ADJ

/ pleɪd aʊt / (AmE), / pleɪd aʊt / (BrE)
Extremely tired, drained of energy, or no longer effective; completely exhausted or worn out.
completely exhausted; worn out

Examples

After hiking all day, I was completely played out by the evening.
Her voice sounded played out after the long meeting and hours of talking.
By the time he finished his exams, he felt absolutely played out.
