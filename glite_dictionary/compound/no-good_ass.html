no-good ass

1. NOUN

/ ˌnoʊ ˈɡʊd ˌæs / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈɡʊd ˌas / (BrE)
an insulting term for a person considered worthless, contemptible, or despicable
worthless or despicable person (insult, vulgar)
informal vulgar

Examples

Don’t listen to that no-good ass—he’s always trying to stir up trouble.
After stealing from his friends, no one trusted the no-good ass again.
She called him a no-good ass for lying to everyone.

Synset Examples

Stop being a no-good ass and apologize already.
He acted like a no-good ass during the meeting.
Don’t be a no-good ass, help your friend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person
  • Synonym: scoundrel - a dishonest or unscrupulous person
