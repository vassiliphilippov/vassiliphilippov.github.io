house servant

1. NOUN

/ haʊs ˈsɝːvənt / (AmE), / haʊs ˈsɜːvənt / (BrE)
a person employed to perform domestic tasks and duties within a household
servant working in a household

Examples

The wealthy family employed several house servants to maintain their mansion.
During the colonial era, most house servants lived on the estate where they worked.
She spoke kindly to the house servant who brought her tea each morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: domestic servant - a servant who works in someone's home
  • Degree Lower: maid - a female domestic servant
  • Degree Higher: butler - the chief male servant, especially in a large household
