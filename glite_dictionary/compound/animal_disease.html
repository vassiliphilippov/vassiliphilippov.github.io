animal disease

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɪməl dɪˈziz / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
a disease that typically affects non-human animals and not humans
disease affecting non-human animals

Examples

Foot-and-mouth disease is a highly contagious animal disease that affects cattle and pigs.
Veterinarians study animal diseases to prevent outbreaks in livestock populations.
Rabies is an animal disease that can, in rare cases, be transmitted to humans.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: zoonosis - a disease that can be transmitted from animals to humans
