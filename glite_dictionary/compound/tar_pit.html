tar pit

1. NOUN

/ tɑr pɪt / (AmE), / tɑː pɪt / (BrE)
a natural deposit where thick, sticky asphalt (bitumen) accumulates at the earth’s surface, sometimes trapping and preserving the remains of animals
natural deposit of asphalt trapping animals

Examples

Many fossils have been discovered in the La Brea tar pits in Los Angeles.
Prehistoric animals were often trapped in natural tar pits and preserved for thousands of years.
Scientists study tar pits to learn about extinct species and ancient ecosystems.
