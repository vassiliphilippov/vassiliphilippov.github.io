electronic bicycle

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˈbaɪsɪkəl / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˈbaɪsɪkl̩ / (BrE)
a bicycle equipped with an electric motor that can assist or replace pedaling by the rider
bicycle with electric motor assistance

Examples

She decided to commute to work on her new electronic bicycle to avoid traffic and reduce emissions.
The city has added charging stations tailored for electronic bicycles along popular cycling routes.
With its built-in motor, an electronic bicycle makes climbing hills much easier for riders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: e-bike - a common abbreviation for electronic bicycle
  • Degree Lower: bicycle - a vehicle with two wheels powered by pedaling
