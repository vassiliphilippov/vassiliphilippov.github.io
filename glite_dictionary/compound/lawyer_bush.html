lawyer bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔjər bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈlɔːjə bʊʃ / (BrE)
a stout-stemmed trailing shrub native to New Zealand that grows over and through other vegetation
New Zealand scrambling shrub

Examples

The lawyer bush thrives in dense forests across New Zealand's North Island.
Hikers were warned about the thorny branches of the lawyer bush snagging their clothing.
The lawyer bush often forms tangled masses that are hard to walk through.
