spring pocket

1. NOUN

/ sprɪŋ ˈpɑkɪt / (AmE), / sprɪŋ ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
a pocket fitted with a spring or similar mechanism to hold items securely
pocket with securing mechanism

Examples

He kept his valuables in the spring pocket so they wouldn't fall out.
The spring pocket in her purse snapped shut automatically after use.
This jacket features a hidden spring pocket for extra security.

Synset Examples

He found a surprise in his spring pocket while cleaning.
