Aztec lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈæz.tɛk ˌlɪl.i / (AmE), / ˈaz.tɛk ˌlɪl.i / (BrE)
A Mexican bulbous plant, cultivated for its large, bright red solitary flower.
Mexican plant with red flowers

Examples

The gardener planted several Aztec lilies to brighten the flowerbed with their vibrant color.
Each Aztec lily produces a stunning solitary bloom in early summer.
She bought an Aztec lily bulb at the botanical garden's spring sale.
