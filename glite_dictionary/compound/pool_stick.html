pool stick

1. NOUN

/ ˈpuːl stɪk / (AmE), / ˈpuːl stɪk / (BrE)
a tapering rod used to strike the cue ball in pool or billiards
rod for striking cue ball in pool/billiards

Examples

He leaned over the table and aimed his pool stick carefully before making the shot.
She bought a new pool stick for the local tournament next week.
Players must always chalk the tip of their pool stick for better control.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cue - a rod used to strike the cue ball in billiards and snooker
