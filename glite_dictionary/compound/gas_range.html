gas range

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ɡæs reɪndʒ / (BrE)
A cooking stove with gas burners and an oven, designed to use gas as fuel.
cooking stove powered by gas

Examples

We decided to replace our old stove with a new gas range for better heat control.
The chef prefers cooking on a gas range because it responds quickly to temperature changes.
During the blackout, we could still cook meals thanks to our gas range.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stove - device used for cooking or heating
  • Antonym: electric range - a cooking range powered by electricity
