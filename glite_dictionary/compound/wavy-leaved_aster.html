wavy-leaved aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪvi livd ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈweɪvi liːvd ˈastə / (BrE)
a species of North American aster (Symphyotrichum undulatum) characterized by its wavy-edged leaves and pale blue flowers
a North American aster with wavy leaves

Examples

The wavy-leaved aster blooms in late summer, adding delicate color to woodland edges.
Gardeners appreciate the wavy-leaved aster for its resilience and attractive foliage.
We found a cluster of wavy-leaved asters growing along the hiking trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a plant of the genus Aster, with star-shaped flower heads
