parlor game

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrlɚ ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈpɑːlə ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a game, often involving wordplay or guessing, played indoors for amusement in a social setting
indoor social game for amusement

Examples

Charades is a classic example of a parlor game enjoyed by families and friends.
During the storm, everyone gathered for a lively parlor game to pass the time.
They invented a new parlor game that quickly became the highlight of their parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party game - a game played at social gatherings to amuse guests

Etymology

From 'parlor', meaning a sitting room, and 'game'. Refers to games played in domestic reception rooms.
