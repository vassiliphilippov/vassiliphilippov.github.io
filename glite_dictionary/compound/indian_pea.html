Indian pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən pi / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən piː / (BrE)
a European annual plant (Lathyrus sativus), used as forage and for food in some regions, particularly in India
a leguminous plant grown for food and forage

Examples

The farmer decided to grow Indian pea as a nutritious forage crop this year.
In some drought-prone regions, Indian pea is a valuable source of protein.
Exporters ship Indian pea seeds from India to various international markets.
