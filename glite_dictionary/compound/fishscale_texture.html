fishscale texture

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃˌskeɪl ˈtɛkstʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌskeɪl ˈtɛkstʃə / (BrE)
A surface pattern or texture that resembles the overlapping scales of a fish, typically used in textiles, architecture, or design.
pattern resembling overlapping fish scales

Examples

The artist used a metallic paint to highlight the fishscale texture on the statue.
The tiles in the bathroom had a fishscale texture that shimmered in the light.
Many luxury cars feature interior panels with a distinctive fishscale texture for added elegance.

Synset Examples
