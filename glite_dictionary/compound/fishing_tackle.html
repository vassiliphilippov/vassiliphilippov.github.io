fishing tackle

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃɪŋ ˌtækəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪŋ ˌtækəl / (BrE)
the equipment, such as lines, hooks, and rods, used for catching fish
equipment used for fishing

Examples

He packed his fishing tackle before heading out to the lake.
There is a shop in town that sells all kinds of fishing tackle.
His grandfather passed down a box full of old fishing tackle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fishing gear - equipment used for catching fish
