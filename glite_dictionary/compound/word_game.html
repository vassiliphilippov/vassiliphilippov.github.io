word game

1. NOUN

/ wɝːd ɡeɪm / (AmE), / wɜːd ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a game in which players form, alter, or discover words according to specific rules
game involving creating or discovering words

Examples

She enjoys playing word games like Scrabble with her family on the weekends.
Many word games are designed to improve vocabulary and spelling skills.
Crossword puzzles and anagrams are popular types of word games.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puzzle - game or activity designed to test ingenuity or knowledge
