Master of the Universe

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæstɚ əv ðə ˈjunɪˌvɝs / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstə əv ðə ˈjuːnɪvɜːs / (BrE)
a person who has or believes they have ultimate power or control over people or events
person with supreme power or control

Examples

After closing the biggest deal of the year, he strutted around the office as if he were the Master of the Universe.
She dreamed of becoming a Master of the Universe in the world of finance.
To his friends, winning the championship made him feel like the Master of the Universe.

Synset Examples

In her dreams, she was the Master of the Universe.
The kids pretended he was the Master of the Universe.
He felt like the Master of the Universe after winning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tycoon - a wealthy, powerful businessperson
  • Synonym: overlord - a person who has great power or authority over others
  • Antonym: underling - a person of lower rank or status

Etymology

Originally popularized by Tom Wolfe's 1987 novel 'The Bonfire of the Vanities'.
