phone cord

1. NOUN

/ foʊn kɔrd / (AmE), / fəʊn kɔːd / (BrE)
the coiled wire that connects a telephone handset to the base unit
cord connecting telephone handset to base

Examples

She untangled the phone cord before answering the call from her desk.
The phone cord was stretched across the kitchen counter, almost tripping me.
He replaced the old, frayed phone cord with a new one to improve the sound quality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telephone cord - cord connecting the telephone handset to the base
