parish-pump debates

1. NOUN

/ ˈpærɪʃ pʌmp dɪˈbeɪts / (AmE), / ˈpærɪʃ pʌmp dɪˈbeɪts / (BrE)
discussions focused on local, trivial, or parochial matters, often of little wider importance
local or trivial discussions
british

Examples

The local council meetings often descended into parish-pump debates over minor maintenance issues.
Critics accused the committee of engaging in parish-pump debates instead of addressing broader national policies.
He wanted to avoid the parish-pump debates that distracted from more significant challenges.

Synset Examples

The parish-pump debates were lively and engaging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parochialism - a focus on narrow local concerns

Etymology

From the image of the parish pump as a center of local gossip in rural British communities.
