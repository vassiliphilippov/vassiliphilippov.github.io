medical record

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈrɛkərd / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
an official written record of a patient's medical history, treatments, and care
official patient health history record

Examples

The doctor reviewed her medical record before starting the examination.
Hospitals are required by law to keep medical records confidential.
After moving to a new city, he requested a copy of his medical record.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: health record - written record of a person's health history
