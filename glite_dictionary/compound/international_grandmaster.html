International Grandmaster

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntərˈnæʃənl ˈɡrændˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl ˈɡrændˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a chess player who has been awarded the highest title by an international chess organization, such as FIDE
top title for a chess player

Examples

She became an International Grandmaster after her impressive victory at the world championship.
Only a few players worldwide have ever achieved the International Grandmaster title.
He dreamed of one day being recognized as an International Grandmaster in chess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandmaster - a chess player of the highest rank
