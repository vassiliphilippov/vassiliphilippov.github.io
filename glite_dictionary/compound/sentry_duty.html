sentry duty

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛntri ˌduːti / (AmE), / ˈsɛntri ˌdjuːti / (BrE)
the duty of standing guard as a sentry, especially in a military context
the role of serving as a guard

Examples

While on sentry duty, she closely watched the entrance for any unusual activity.
New recruits are often assigned sentry duty during their first few weeks of service.
He fell asleep during sentry duty, which resulted in disciplinary action.

Synset Examples

he was on guard that night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guard duty - the duty of protecting a place or people as a guard
