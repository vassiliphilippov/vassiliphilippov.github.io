laurel family

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔr.əl ˈfæm.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈlɔː.rəl ˈfæm.ɪ.li / (BrE)
A family of flowering plants (Lauraceae) that includes the laurel, avocado, and cinnamon trees.
family of plants including laurels and avocados

Examples

Many aromatic trees, such as cinnamon and camphor, belong to the laurel family.
The laurel family is known for its broad, evergreen leaves and economic value.
Avocados are members of the laurel family, along with bay laurel and sassafras.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lauraceae - the scientific name for the laurel family
