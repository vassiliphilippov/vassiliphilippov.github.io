to launch an investigation

1. VERB

/ lɔntʃ ən ɪnˌvɛstəˈɡeɪʃən / (AmE), / lɔːntʃ ən ɪnˌvɛstɪˈɡeɪʃən / (BrE)
to officially begin a formal inquiry or examination into a matter, especially by an authority or organization
begin a formal inquiry

Examples

The company decided to launch an investigation after receiving several customer complaints.
Authorities plan to launch an investigation into the sudden increase in pollution levels.
After the data breach, the government quickly launched an investigation to determine the cause.

Synset Examples

The FBI launched an investigation into the local corruption.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to investigate - to carry out a systematic or formal inquiry
