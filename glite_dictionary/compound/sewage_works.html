sewage works

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuɪdʒ wɜrks / (AmE), / ˈsjuːɪdʒ wɜːks / (BrE)
a place or system where sewage is treated and processed before being released or disposed of
facility for treating sewage

Examples

A large sewage works was built to handle the waste from the growing city.
The sewage works processes thousands of gallons of wastewater every day.
She lives near the old sewage works, which has been converted into a nature reserve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sewage treatment plant - facility for treating sewage
