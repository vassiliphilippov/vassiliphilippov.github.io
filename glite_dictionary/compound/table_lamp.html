table lamp

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəl læmp / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəl læmp / (BrE)
A lamp designed to stand on a table, typically used for reading or as a decorative light source.
lamp designed to stand on a table

Examples

She turned on the table lamp to read her book before bed.
The new table lamp adds a warm glow to our living room.
After the power returned, he switched off the table lamp and went to sleep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desk lamp - a lamp designed for use on a desk
