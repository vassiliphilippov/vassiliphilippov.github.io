genus Richea

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːkiə / (AmE), / ˈriːkiə / (BrE)
A genus of evergreen trees or shrubs native to the mountains of Australia and Tasmania.
genus of evergreen Australian/Tasmanian plants

Examples

The mountain slopes in Tasmania are home to several species within the genus Richea.
Botanists recently discovered a new shrub belonging to the genus Richea.
The distinctive flowers of the genus Richea attract many native pollinators.
