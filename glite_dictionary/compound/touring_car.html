touring car

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʊrɪŋ kɑr / (AmE), / ˈtʊərɪŋ kɑː / (BrE)
a large open automobile, typically seating four or more people, with a folding top
large open car with folding top

Examples

In the early 1900s, many families traveled across the country in a touring car.
He restored a vintage touring car from the 1920s and shows it at local car exhibitions.
The museum has a beautifully maintained touring car with a leather folding top on display.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: convertible - an automobile with a roof that can be folded back or removed
