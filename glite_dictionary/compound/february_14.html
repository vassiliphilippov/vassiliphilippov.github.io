February 14

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɛb.juːˈɛri ˈfɔːrˌtiːn / (AmE), / ˌfɛb.juːˈæɹ.i ˈfɔː.tiːn / (BrE)
the day each year known as Valentine's Day, traditionally celebrated as a time for exchanging tokens of affection and love
Valentine's Day; day for giving tokens of affection

Examples

Every year on February 14, couples around the world celebrate their love with cards and gifts.
She received a bouquet of roses on February 14, making her Valentine's Day extra special.
Some people feel pressure to be romantic on February 14, while others prefer to keep it simple.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Valentine's Day - February 14, day for expressing love
