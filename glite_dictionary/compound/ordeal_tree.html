ordeal tree

1. NOUN

/ ɔrˈdil tri / (AmE), / ɔːˈdiːl triː / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub or tree native to South Africa, known for its use in traditional trials by ordeal
South African evergreen shrub or tree

Examples

The villagers once used the ordeal tree in traditional rituals to determine guilt or innocence.
Researchers are studying the medicinal properties of the ordeal tree found in South Africa.
The toxic seeds of the ordeal tree have a significant place in local folklore.
