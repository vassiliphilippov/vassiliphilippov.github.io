card table

1. NOUN

/ kɑrd ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / kɑːd ˈteɪbəl / (BrE)
A small, portable table with folding legs, typically used for playing cards and easily folded for storage.
folding table for playing cards

Examples

She brought out the card table for their weekly bridge game.
After dinner, we set up the card table in the living room for poker night.
The card table folds up easily and fits in the closet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: table - a piece of furniture with a flat top and one or more legs
