ski conditions

1. NOUN

/ ski kənˈdɪʃ.ənz / (AmE), / ski kənˈdɪʃ.ənz / (BrE)
the amount and quality of snow and weather as they affect skiing
state of snow for skiing

Examples

The ski conditions were perfect after the fresh snowfall last night.
Before heading to the resort, she always checks the latest ski conditions online.
Due to warm weather, the ski conditions have become slushy and less ideal.
