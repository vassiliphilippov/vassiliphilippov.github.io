technoluddite views

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛk.noʊˈlʌd.aɪt vjuːz / (AmE), / ˌtɛk.nəʊˈlʌd.aɪt vjuːz / (BrE)
attitudes or opinions that oppose or resist new technology or digital innovations
opposition to technology and digital advances

Examples

Her technoluddite views often spark debates with friends.
The company's modernization efforts were met with technoluddite views from some long-term employees.
Despite the rise of smartphones, he continues to defend his technoluddite views at every opportunity.

Synset Examples

Her technoluddite views often spark debates with friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Luddite - a person opposed to technological change

Etymology

Blend of 'technology' and 'Luddite', referencing the English Luddites who opposed industrial machinery.
