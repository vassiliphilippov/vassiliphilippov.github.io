mistletoe rubber plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪsəltoʊ ˈrʌbər plænt / (AmE), / ˈmɪsəltəʊ ˈrʌbə plɑːnt / (BrE)
A shrub or small tree often grown as a houseplant, known for its foliage that resembles mistletoe.
houseplant with mistletoe-like leaves

Examples

The mistletoe rubber plant adds a touch of green to any living room.
She watered her mistletoe rubber plant every week to keep it healthy.
Many gardeners appreciate the unique foliage of the mistletoe rubber plant.
