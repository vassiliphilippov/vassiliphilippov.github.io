bitter betch

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪtər bɛtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɪtə bɛtʃ / (BrE)
a European perennial vetch (plant in the pea family) with toxic properties
European toxic vetch plant

Examples

The meadow was dotted with bitter betch among the wildflowers, so we kept our distance.
Farmers often remove bitter betch to protect their livestock from poisoning.
Botanists studied the spread of bitter betch in European grasslands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vetch - a climbing plant of the pea family
