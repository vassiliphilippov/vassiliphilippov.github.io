axle grease

1. NOUN

/ ˈæksəl ɡris / (AmE), / ˈæksəl ɡriːs / (BrE)
a thick, heavy grease used to lubricate axles and other moving mechanical parts
grease for lubricating axles

Examples

The mechanic applied axle grease to ensure the wheel turned smoothly.
Farmers often use axle grease to keep their machinery running efficiently.
Always clean off old axle grease before applying a new layer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grease - a thick, oily substance used for lubrication
