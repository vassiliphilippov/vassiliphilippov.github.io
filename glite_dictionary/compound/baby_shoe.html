baby shoe

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.bi ˌʃu / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi ˌʃuː / (BrE)
a small shoe designed to be worn by an infant, typically soft and flexible to accommodate a baby's developing feet
shoe designed for infants

Examples

She bought a pair of cute baby shoes for her newborn niece.
The tiny baby shoe was carefully preserved as a keepsake.
It's important to choose a comfortable baby shoe that allows feet to grow naturally.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bootie - a soft shoe for infants, often made of fabric or soft leather
