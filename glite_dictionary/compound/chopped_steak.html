chopped steak

1. NOUN

/ tʃɑpt steɪk / (AmE), / tʃɒpt steɪk / (BrE)
a patty made from ground beef that is cooked and typically served as a main dish
ground beef patty dish

Examples

The diner served a classic chopped steak with mashed potatoes and gravy.
She ordered a chopped steak instead of a hamburger for lunch.
The chopped steak was topped with sautéed onions and a rich brown sauce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hamburger steak - a patty of ground beef, typically cooked and served as a meal
  • Degree Lower: meatloaf - a dish of ground meat that is shaped and baked
