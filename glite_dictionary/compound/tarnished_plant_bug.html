tarnished plant bug

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑrnɪʃt plænt bʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈtɑːnɪʃt plɑːnt bʌɡ / (BrE)
a widespread North American bug (Lygus lineolaris) that feeds on and damages a variety of plants and fruits, causing crop losses
common North American crop pest bug

Examples

Farmers are concerned about the damage the tarnished plant bug can cause to their cotton fields.
The tarnished plant bug feeds on more than 300 species of plants, including strawberries and alfalfa.
Spraying crops in early summer helps manage tarnished plant bug populations effectively.
