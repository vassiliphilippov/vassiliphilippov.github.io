dictionary definition

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪkʃəˌnɛri ˌdɛfəˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈdɪkʃənəri ˌdɛfɪˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
a statement in a dictionary that explains the standard meaning or usage of a word, phrase, or symbol
definition found in a dictionary

Examples

The dictionary definition of 'freedom' can differ depending on the source.
She looked up the dictionary definition to ensure she was using the word correctly.
Many debates start by agreeing on a dictionary definition of key terms.
