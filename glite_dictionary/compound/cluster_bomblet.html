cluster bomblet

1. NOUN

/ ˈklʌstɚ ˈbɑm.lət / (AmE), / ˈklʌstə ˈbɒm.lət / (BrE)
one of the smaller explosive devices released from a cluster bomb, designed to disperse and detonate over a wide area
small bomb released from a cluster bomb

Examples

The clearing crew found an unexploded cluster bomblet in the field after the conflict.
Many civilians have been injured by cluster bomblets left behind after airstrikes.
Treaties have been signed to ban the use of cluster bomblets due to their danger to non-combatants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bomblet - a small bomb, especially one of many forming part of a cluster bomb
  • Degree Higher: cluster bomb - a bomb that releases many smaller bombs over a wide area

Etymology

From 'cluster bomb' and 'bomblet' (diminutive of 'bomb').
