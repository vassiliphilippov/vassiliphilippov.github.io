chicken breast

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪk.ən brɛst / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.ɪn brɛst / (BrE)
an abnormal protrusion of the breastbone (sternum), especially as caused by rickets; also called 'pigeon breast' in medical contexts
abnormal protrusion of the breastbone
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed the child with chicken breast caused by severe vitamin D deficiency.
A visible chicken breast may require corrective surgery if it interferes with breathing.
Children with rickets can sometimes develop a pronounced chicken breast deformity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pigeon breast - another name for the same abnormal protrusion of the breastbone
