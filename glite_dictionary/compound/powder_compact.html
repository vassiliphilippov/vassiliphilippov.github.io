powder compact

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊdɚ kəmˈpækt / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə kəmˈpækt / (BrE)
a small, flat container holding face powder and often a mirror, carried in a purse for applying makeup
small cosmetics case with powder and mirror

Examples

She pulled her powder compact out of her purse to touch up her makeup.
The elegant powder compact was decorated with tiny flowers.
My grandmother still carries a vintage powder compact in her handbag.
