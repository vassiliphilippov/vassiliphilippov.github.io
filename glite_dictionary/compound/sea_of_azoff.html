Sea of Azoff

1. PROPN

/ si əv əˈzɒf / (AmE), / siː əv əˈzɒf / (BrE)
a shallow sea in Eastern Europe, separated from the Black Sea by the Kerch Strait, lying between Russia and Ukraine.
a sea in Eastern Europe, near Russia and Ukraine

Examples

The Sea of Azoff is known for its shallow waters and unique ecosystem.
Many ships pass through the Kerch Strait to enter the Sea of Azoff.
Fishing is an important industry around the Sea of Azoff.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Black Sea - large inland sea connected to the Sea of Azoff
