smoking mixture

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmoʊkɪŋ ˌmɪks.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkɪŋ ˌmɪks.tʃə / (BrE)
a blend of tobaccos prepared for smoking, especially in a pipe
blend of tobaccos for smoking

Examples

He carefully filled his pipe with his favorite smoking mixture after dinner.
This particular smoking mixture contains Virginia and Latakia tobaccos for a rich flavor.
The store offers a wide selection of imported smoking mixtures for pipe enthusiasts.
