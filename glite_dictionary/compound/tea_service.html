tea service

1. NOUN

/ ti ˈsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / tiː ˈsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
A set of matching china or silverware used for serving tea, typically including a teapot, cups, saucers, sugar bowl, and other related items.
set of china or silverware for serving tea

Examples

The silver tea service was polished until it gleamed for the afternoon gathering.
She inherited her grandmother's antique tea service and treasures it dearly.
They arranged the porcelain tea service on the tray before guests arrived.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tea set - a set of dishes for serving tea
