food company

1. NOUN

/ fuːd ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / fuːd ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
A business that processes, manufactures, or sells food products.
business that makes or sells food

Examples

Several large food companies announced new efforts to reduce plastic packaging in their products.
The government regulates food companies to ensure public health and safety standards are met.
She accepted a marketing position at a major international food company after graduation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: food manufacturer - company that makes food products
  • Degree Lower: restaurant - business that prepares and serves food to customers
