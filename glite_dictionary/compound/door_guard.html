door guard

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔr ˌɡɑrd / (AmE), / ˈdɔː ˌɡɑːd / (BrE)
a person whose job is to guard the entrance to a building or room
person guarding a doorway

Examples

A door guard checked everyone's bags before letting them into the concert.
The hotel employed a door guard to ensure only guests could enter after midnight.
She worked as a door guard at the museum during special exhibitions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doorman - a person employed to attend the entrance of a building
