weather bureau

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛðɚ bjuˌroʊ / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə bjʊəˌrəʊ / (BrE)
an official organization responsible for collecting and predicting weather information
agency for weather observation and forecasts

Examples

The weather bureau issued a severe thunderstorm warning for the entire region.
According to the weather bureau, temperatures will drop significantly by Friday.
She called the weather bureau to get the latest updates before planning her trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meteorological office - another term for weather bureau, especially in the UK
