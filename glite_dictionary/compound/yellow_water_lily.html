yellow water lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈwɔtər ˈlɪli / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈwɔːtə ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A species of water lily that has yellow flowers, typically found in ponds and slow-moving waters.
a water lily with yellow flowers

Examples

The pond was dotted with beautiful yellow water lilies in full bloom.
A single yellow water lily stood out against the green leaves on the lake.
We took photos of the yellow water lilies growing near the edge of the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water lily - an aquatic plant with large floating leaves and flowers
