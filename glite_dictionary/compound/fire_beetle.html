fire beetle

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚ ˌbiːtəl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a tropical American click beetle known for its bright, luminous spots
tropical click beetle with luminous spots

Examples

The fire beetle emits a soft glow to attract mates during the night.
Children were fascinated by the tiny lights produced by the fire beetles in the forest.
Biologists studied the luminous organs of the fire beetle for potential scientific applications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: click beetle - a beetle of the family Elateridae that can snap its body and jump
