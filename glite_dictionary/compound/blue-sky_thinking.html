blue-sky thinking

1. NOUN

/ bluː skaɪ ˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / bluː skʌɪ ˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
creative or imaginative thinking that is not limited by current constraints or practical considerations
unconstrained creative thinking

Examples

The company encourages blue-sky thinking during their innovation workshops.
Without blue-sky thinking, we might never have discovered such a novel solution.
She is known for her blue-sky thinking when approaching tough challenges.

Synset Examples

The team encouraged blue-sky thinking during brainstorming sessions.
We need blue-sky thinking to solve this complex problem.
Her blue-sky thinking led to innovative project ideas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brainstorming - group discussion to produce ideas; generating creative ideas

Etymology

From 'blue-sky' meaning open or unrestricted, suggesting limitless possibilities.
