airship ride

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌʃɪp raɪd / (AmE), / ˈeəʃɪp raɪd / (BrE)
a journey or excursion taken in an airship, typically for leisure or sightseeing
a journey in an airship

Examples

She surprised him with an airship ride over the city for his birthday.
The company offers an airship ride package for tourists seeking adventure.
During the festival, many visitors enjoyed an airship ride to see the colorful landscape.

Synset Examples

We took an airship ride for a unique view.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: balloon ride - a journey in a hot air balloon
