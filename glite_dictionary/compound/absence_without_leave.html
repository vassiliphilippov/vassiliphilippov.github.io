absence without leave

1. NOUN

/ ˈæbsəns wɪðˌaʊt ˈliv / (AmE), / ˈæbsəns wɪðˌaʊt ˈliːv / (BrE)
the act of being away from one's post, especially in the armed forces, without official permission; unauthorized absence from duty
unapproved absence from duty
legal

Examples

The soldier was charged with absence without leave after failing to report for duty.
Repeated absence without leave can result in disciplinary action within the army.
Her record was marred by a single incident of absence without leave during her service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: AWOL - absent without official leave; military slang for unauthorized absence
