island of Jersey

1. PROPN

/ ˈaɪ.lənd əv ˈdʒɜr.zi / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.lənd əv ˈdʒɜː.zi / (BrE)
an island in the English Channel, the largest of the Channel Islands, a British Crown dependency near the coast of France
the largest Channel Island; British dependency

Examples

The island of Jersey attracts thousands of tourists each summer with its beautiful beaches and mild climate.
My grandparents retired to the island of Jersey after many years living in London.
Many species of rare birds can be found on the island of Jersey due to its unique environment.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Guernsey - the second largest of the Channel Islands

Etymology

Named after Old Norse *Geirr's ey* (Geirr's island); Jersey is of Norse origin.
