ground pink

1. NOUN

/ ɡraʊnd pɪŋk / (AmE), / ɡraʊnd pɪŋk / (BrE)
A low, wiry-stemmed branching herb native to southern California, known for its fringed pink flowers.
herb with pink fringed flowers

Examples

We spotted a cluster of ground pink blooming near the rocky trail.
Botanists studied the habitat of the rare ground pink in southern California.
The delicate flowers of the ground pink attract a variety of pollinators each spring.
