skibidi dance

1. NOUN

/ skɪˈbiːdi dæns / (AmE), / skɪˈbiːdi dɑːns / (BrE)
a viral dance trend originating from a music video by the band Little Big, popularized on social media and known for its distinctive moves
viral internet dance trend

Examples

Everyone at the party tried to do the skibidi dance together.
The skibidi dance challenge became a hit on TikTok and YouTube.
He learned the skibidi dance just by watching a few videos online.

Synset Examples

The kids were obsessed with the skibidi dance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: viral dance - a dance trend that spreads widely and quickly online

Etymology

From the title of the 2018 song 'Skibidi' by Russian band Little Big, which popularized the dance.
