side pocket

1. NOUN

/ saɪd ˈpɑːkɪt / (AmE), / saɪd ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
a pocket situated on the side of a billiard or pool table into which balls can be potted
pocket on side of billiard table

Examples

He aimed carefully and sank the ball into the side pocket.
The side pocket is usually trickier to hit than the corner pocket.
She practiced lining up shots to the side pocket every evening.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: corner pocket - a pocket located at the corner of a billiard table
