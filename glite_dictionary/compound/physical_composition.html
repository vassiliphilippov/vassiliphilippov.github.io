physical composition

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪzɪkəl ˌkɑmpəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl ˌkɒmpəˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
the structure or arrangement of parts that make up someone or something; the physical makeup of an entity.
the physical makeup or structure of something

Examples

The material's physical composition determines its strength and flexibility.
Genetic makeup contributes to a person's physical composition and appearance.
Scientists studied the rock's physical composition to learn more about its origin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: structure - the arrangement of and relations between the parts of something complex
