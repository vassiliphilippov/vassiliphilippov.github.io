bleu cheese dressing

1. NOUN

/ bluː tʃiːz ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / bluː tʃiːz ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
A creamy salad dressing made with crumbled blue cheese, often blended with mayonnaise, sour cream, and seasonings.
creamy dressing containing blue cheese

Examples

He topped his salad with a generous dollop of bleu cheese dressing.
The tangy flavor of bleu cheese dressing complements spicy chicken wings perfectly.
If you prefer a bolder taste, try adding bleu cheese dressing to your burger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ranch dressing - a creamy American salad dressing, often with herbs
  • Antonym: vinaigrette - a salad dressing of oil and vinegar

Etymology

From French 'bleu' (blue), describing the mold veins in blue cheese.
