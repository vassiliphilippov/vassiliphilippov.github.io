standing room

1. NOUN

/ ˈstændɪŋ ˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈstændɪŋ ˌruːm / (BrE)
space available for people to stand, especially in a public place such as a theater, bus, or stadium when all the seats are occupied.
space for people to stand (no seating)

Examples

There was only standing room left by the time we arrived at the concert.
The train was so crowded that passengers had to make do with standing room only.
When the play became popular, the theater was packed, and standing room tickets sold out quickly.

Synset Examples

there was standing room for thousands more people

Related Terms

  • Antonym: seat - a place to sit, such as a chair or bench
