place name

1. NOUN

/ pleɪs neɪm / (AmE), / pleɪs neɪm / (BrE)
The name used to identify a specific geographical location, such as a town, region, or feature.
name identifying a geographical location

Examples

The place name 'Springfield' is found in many states across the U.S.
She specialized in the study of ancient place names and their meanings.
The river changed course, but its place name remained the same for centuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toponym - a name derived from a place or referring to a place
