Mighty Mouse

1. PROPN

/ ˈmaɪti maʊs / (AmE), / ˈmaɪti maʊs / (BrE)
a fictional cartoon mouse character with extraordinary strength and bravery, known for fighting evil and saving others
a strong, heroic cartoon mouse character

Examples

When danger strikes, Mighty Mouse always comes to the rescue.
As a child, she loved watching Mighty Mouse defeat the villains every week.
The superhero's costume reminded him of Mighty Mouse.
