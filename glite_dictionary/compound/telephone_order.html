telephone order

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌfoʊn ˈɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈtelɪfəʊn ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
an order for goods or services that is placed or received by telephone
order made by telephone

Examples

You can place a telephone order for the book and have it delivered to your home.
All telephone orders must be confirmed with a credit card number.
The company saw a rise in telephone orders during the holiday season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mail order - an order for goods sent by mail
