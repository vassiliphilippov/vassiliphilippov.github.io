oatmeal cookie

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊt.miːl ˈkʊk.i / (AmE), / ˈəʊt.miːl ˈkʊk.i / (BrE)
a sweet baked cookie made with rolled oats, often containing raisins or nuts
a cookie made with oats

Examples

My grandmother bakes the best oatmeal cookies with lots of raisins and cinnamon.
I grabbed an oatmeal cookie and a glass of milk for my afternoon snack.
The bakery offers several varieties, but the chewy oatmeal cookie is my favorite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cookie - a sweet baked food, typically round and flat
  • Synonym: biscuit - a small baked, typically sweet cake (British English: cookie)
