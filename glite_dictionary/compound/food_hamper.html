food hamper

1. NOUN

/ fuːd ˈhæmpɚ / (AmE), / fuːd ˈhæmpə / (BrE)
a large basket or box for packing and transporting food, often used for picnics or as a gift
large basket or box for carrying food

Examples

They received a beautiful food hamper filled with cheese, wine, and chocolates.
We brought a food hamper to the park for our family picnic.
A food hamper makes a thoughtful gift for the holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hamper - a large basket with a lid used for carrying items
