major hold-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪdʒɚ ˈhoʊldˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdʒə ˈhəʊldʌp / (BrE)
a significant delay or obstruction that causes progress to be stopped or slowed considerably
significant delay or obstruction

Examples

The construction work caused a major hold-up during the morning commute.
There was a major hold-up at the airport due to weather conditions.
We apologize for the major hold-up in processing your application.

Synset Examples

The traffic caused a major hold-up on the highway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hold-up - a delay or obstruction
  • Degree Lower: minor delay - a slight or less significant delay
