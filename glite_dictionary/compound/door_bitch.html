door bitch

1. NOUN

/ dɔɚ bɪtʃ / (AmE), / dɔː bɪtʃ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person managing entry at a party, nightclub, or event, typically greeting and screening guests
person screening entry at events (slang)
informal slang vulgar

Examples

The club hired a door bitch to ensure only invited guests could enter.
She was known as the toughest door bitch in the city's nightlife scene.
As the door bitch, he greeted everyone with a sharp wit and a quick smile.

Synset Examples
