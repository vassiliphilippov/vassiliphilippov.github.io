home buyer

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm ˈbaɪər / (AmE), / həʊm ˈbaɪə / (BrE)
a person who is in the process of buying or has bought a house or apartment to live in
person buying a house or apartment

Examples

The government introduced new incentives to help first-time home buyers.
With housing prices rising, many home buyers are struggling to find affordable properties.
As a home buyer, you should always get the house inspected before making an offer.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: seller - a person who sells something, especially property
  • Degree Higher: homeowner - a person who owns the house in which they live
