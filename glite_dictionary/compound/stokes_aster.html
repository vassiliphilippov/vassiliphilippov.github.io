stokes' aster

1. NOUN

/ stoʊks ˈæstər / (AmE), / stəʊks ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
An erect perennial plant of the southeastern United States, known for its large blue flower heads.
flowering plant with large blue heads

Examples

The garden was brightened by a cluster of stokes' aster in full bloom.
Horticulturists recommend stokes' aster for its resilience and vibrant blue flowers.
She planted several stokes' asters along the path to attract butterflies.
