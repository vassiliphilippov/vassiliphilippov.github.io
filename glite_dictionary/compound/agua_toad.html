agua toad

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːɡwə təʊd / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡwə təʊd / (BrE)
a very large toad species native to Central America, notable for controlling insect pests
large Central American toad species

Examples

The agua toad is known for its impressive size and voracious appetite for insects.
Farmers appreciate the agua toad because it helps control crop pests naturally.
Researchers in Central America have studied the agua toad to understand its ecological impact.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: giant toad - another common name for the large toad species
  • Synonym: cane toad - common large toad species known for pest control, also invasive
