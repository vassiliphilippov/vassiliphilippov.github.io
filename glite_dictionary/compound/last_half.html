last half

1. NOUN

/ læst hæf / (AmE), / lɑːst hɑːf / (BrE)
the second of two halves in a game, match, or event, especially in sports or competitions
the second half of a game or event

Examples

The team played much better in the last half and managed to win the match.
Rain delayed the start of the last half but the players soon returned to the field.
The coach made strategic substitutions for the last half of the basketball game.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: first half - the first of two halves in a game or event
