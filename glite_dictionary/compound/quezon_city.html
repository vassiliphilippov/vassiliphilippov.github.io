Quezon City

1. PROPN

/ ˈkeɪ.zɒn ˈsɪt.i / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.zɒn ˈsɪt.i / (BrE)
A city in the northern part of Metro Manila, Philippines, located on Luzon Island, adjoining Manila.
city in Metro Manila, Philippines

Examples

Quezon City is known for its vibrant food scene and cultural landmarks.
My cousin moved to Quezon City last year to attend university.
Traffic in Quezon City can get very heavy during rush hour.

Etymology

Named after Manuel L. Quezon, the second President of the Philippines.
