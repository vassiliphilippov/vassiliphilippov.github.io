kiki session

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːki ˈsɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈkiːki ˈsɛʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
An informal gathering of friends for fun, gossip, and socializing, typically involving laughter and sharing stories.
an informal social gathering
informal slang

Examples

We're having a little kiki session at my place this Friday night—come join us!
After the long week, they needed a kiki session to catch up and unwind.
Their kiki session lasted until midnight, filled with laughter and stories.

Synset Examples

She invited her friends over for a kiki session.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party - a social gathering for celebration or recreation
  • Synonym: gathering - an assembly or meeting, especially for a social purpose

Etymology

From 'kiki,' U.S. gay slang for a lively social gathering or party, possibly from French 'quiquer' (to laugh).
