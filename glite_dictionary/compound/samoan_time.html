Samoan time

1. NOUN

/ səˈmoʊ.ən taɪm / (AmE), / səˈməʊ.ən taɪm / (BrE)
a relaxed or flexible approach to timekeeping, often characterized by a lack of punctuality
relaxed approach to punctuality

Examples

People here seem to operate on Samoan time, so meetings often start late.
She apologized for being late, blaming it on Samoan time.
When you travel to the islands, you quickly learn what Samoan time really means.

Synset Examples

On Samoan time, punctuality is more like a suggestion.
She joked about Samoan time while waiting for her friend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: island time - an easy-going or flexible attitude toward schedules, common in many island cultures
  • Antonym: punctuality - the quality of being on time

Etymology

From Samoan, referencing cultural attitudes to time in Samoa.
