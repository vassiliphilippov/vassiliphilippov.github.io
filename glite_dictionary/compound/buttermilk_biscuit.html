buttermilk biscuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ɚˌmɪlk ˈbɪs.kɪt / (AmE), / ˈbʌt.ə.mɪlk ˈbɪs.kɪt / (BrE)
A tender, flaky quick bread made with buttermilk and leavened partly with baking soda, popular in American cuisine.
a light, soft biscuit made with buttermilk
american

Examples

Every Sunday morning, my grandmother bakes fresh buttermilk biscuits for the whole family.
Nothing pairs better with fried chicken than a warm buttermilk biscuit and honey.
He learned to make buttermilk biscuits from a Southern recipe passed down through generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biscuit - a small baked bread; may differ in US and UK usage
