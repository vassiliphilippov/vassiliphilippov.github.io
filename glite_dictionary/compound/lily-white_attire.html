lily-white attire

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪli ˌwaɪt əˈtaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪli ˌwaɪt əˈtaɪə / (BrE)
Clothing that is pure white or immaculately clean, often suggesting an appearance of innocence or purity.
very clean or pure white clothing

Examples

She wore lily-white attire to symbolize a fresh start at the ceremony.
Children in lily-white attire paraded down the street during the festival.
His lily-white attire was immaculate even after hours at the party.

Synset Examples

He always dressed in lily-white attire for the charity gala.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotless - completely clean; without any stain

Etymology

From 'lily-white,' meaning as white as a lily (a flower associated with purity), and 'attire' meaning clothing.
