boxed in

1. ADJ

/ bɑkst ɪn / (AmE), / bɒkst ɪn / (BrE)
enclosed, trapped, or restricted as if inside a box or confined space
enclosed or trapped as if in a box

Examples

After the landslide, the hikers felt boxed in by the steep cliffs on all sides.
She hated her tiny studio apartment because it made her feel boxed in.
The new regulations left the company boxed in with few options for growth.
