Miss Priss

1. NOUN

/ mɪs prɪs / (AmE), / mɪs prɪs / (BrE)
a person, especially a woman or girl, who is excessively concerned with being proper, polite, or prim
overly proper or prim person

Examples

Don’t be such a Miss Priss about getting your shoes dirty.
Her classmates teased her, calling her Miss Priss because she followed every rule.
You don’t have to act like a Miss Priss just because we’re at a fancy restaurant.

Synset Examples

Don't be such a Miss Priss about the dress code!
He called her a Miss Priss for always being overly polite.
Everyone thinks she’s a Miss Priss with her high standards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prissy - excessively proper or prim
  • Synonym: prim - very formal and proper
  • Antonym: easygoing - relaxed and unconcerned
