one-track mind

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌn ˌtræk ˈmaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈwʌn ˌtræk ˈmaɪnd / (BrE)
a tendency to focus exclusively on a single idea, subject, or goal, often ignoring other matters
single-minded focus on one idea or goal

Examples

Sometimes his one-track mind prevents him from seeing the bigger picture.
She pursued her career with a one-track mind, ignoring distractions along the way.
It's hard for him to multitask because he tends to have a one-track mind.

Synset Examples

His one-track mind makes it hard to change his plans.
He has a one-track mind when it comes to his goals.
With a one-track mind, she often ignores other perspectives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: single-minded - having or showing a focused determination on a single goal
  • Antonym: open-minded - willing to consider different ideas or opinions
