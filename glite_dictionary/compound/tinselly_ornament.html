tinselly ornament

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪn.səli ˈɔr.nə.mənt / (AmE), / ˈtɪn.səli ˈɔː.nə.mənt / (BrE)
A shiny, often metallic-looking decoration typically used for festive occasions.
shiny decorative item

Examples

The mantel was decorated with every kind of tinselly ornament imaginable.
Each child received a sparkling tinselly ornament to hang during the holiday celebration.
