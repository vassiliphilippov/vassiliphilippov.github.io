emo vibe

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːmoʊ vaɪb / (AmE), / ˈiːməʊ vaɪb / (BrE)
A style, atmosphere, or feeling characterized by strong emotional expression, typically associated with the emo subculture.
emotional, expressive atmosphere or style
informal slang

Examples

The party had an unmistakable emo vibe with dark decor and heartfelt music.
Everyone at the show picked up on the emo vibe coming from the band's performance.
Her Instagram feed always gives off an emo vibe with its moody photos and emotional captions.

Synset Examples

The film had an emo vibe that resonated with teens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emo - A style of rock music and related subculture emphasizing emotion and expression

Etymology

"Emo" from "emotional hardcore," a punk rock music genre of the 1980s; "vibe" is short for "vibration," colloquially referring to aura or mood.
