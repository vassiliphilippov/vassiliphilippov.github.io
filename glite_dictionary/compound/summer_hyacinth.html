summer hyacinth

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmɚ haɪˈæsɪnθ / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə haɪˈæsɪnθ / (BrE)
a southern African herb with white, bell-shaped flowers, cultivated for ornamentation
southern African herb with white flowers

Examples

The summer hyacinth blooms in midsummer and covers the garden in delicate white flowers.
Gardeners often choose the summer hyacinth for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
She planted a row of summer hyacinths along the fence to add bright color during the warmer months.
