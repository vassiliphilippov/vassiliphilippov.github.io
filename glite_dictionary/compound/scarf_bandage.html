scarf bandage

1. NOUN

/ skɑrf ˈbændɪdʒ / (AmE), / skɑːf ˈbændɪdʒ / (BrE)
A wide triangular piece of cloth used as a bandage to support an injured forearm, typically hanging from around the neck.
triangular forearm-supporting bandage
medical

Examples

The nurse used a scarf bandage to support his broken arm.
A scarf bandage can be quickly made from a large handkerchief or triangular cloth.
After her fall, she wore a scarf bandage until her arm healed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sling - a piece of cloth used for supporting an injured arm
