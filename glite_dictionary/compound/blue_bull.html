blue bull

1. NOUN

/ blu bʊl / (AmE), / bluː bʊl / (BrE)
a large antelope native to India, the male of which has a blue-grey coat with white markings, while the female is brownish and hornless; also called the nilgai.
large Indian antelope (nilgai)

Examples

The blue bull is commonly found in the grasslands of northern India.
Male blue bulls have striking blue-grey coats that help them blend into their surroundings.
We spotted a herd of blue bull grazing near the edge of the forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nilgai - another name for the blue bull, a large Indian antelope
