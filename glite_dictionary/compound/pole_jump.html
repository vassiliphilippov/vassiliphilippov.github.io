pole jump

1. NOUN

/ poʊl dʒʌmp / (AmE), / pəʊl dʒʌmp / (BrE)
a competition in which athletes jump over a high bar using a long pole for assistance
athletic event using a pole to jump over a bar

Examples

She trained for months to compete in the pole jump at the national championships.
His record-breaking pole jump stunned the crowd at the stadium.
Many schools now offer lessons in the pole jump as part of their athletics programs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pole vault - an athletic event where a person uses a pole to jump over a high bar
