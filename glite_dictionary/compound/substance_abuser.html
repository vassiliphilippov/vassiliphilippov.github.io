substance abuser

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌbstəns əˌbjusər / (AmE), / ˈsʌbstəns əˌbjuːzə / (BrE)
a person who habitually uses drugs or other substances in a harmful or illegal way
person who misuses drugs or substances

Examples

The clinic offers specialized programs for substance abusers seeking recovery.
Many substance abusers struggle with both physical and mental health issues.
Support groups can help a substance abuser rebuild their life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: addict - a person who is addicted to a substance or activity
