Green Mountains

1. PROPN

/ ɡrin ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a range of the Appalachian Mountains running from south to north through the state of Vermont, USA.
mountain range in Vermont, USA

Examples

We spent our summer hiking in the Green Mountains and exploring the beautiful scenery of Vermont.
The Green Mountains are especially stunning when the fall foliage is at its peak.
She moved to Vermont to be closer to the Green Mountains and enjoy outdoor adventures.
