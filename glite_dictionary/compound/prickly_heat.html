prickly heat

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪkli hiːt / (AmE), / ˈprɪkli hiːt / (BrE)
a skin rash marked by small, itchy red spots, caused by blocked sweat ducts during hot and humid weather
skin rash from blocked sweat ducts
medical

Examples

She developed prickly heat after spending hours outside in the humid summer weather.
Wearing loose cotton clothing can help prevent prickly heat in hot climates.
The child was uncomfortable because of an itchy prickly heat rash on his back.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heat rash - skin rash caused by exposure to hot conditions
