cup of chay

1. NOUN

/ kʌp əv tʃaɪ / (AmE), / kʌp əv tʃaɪ / (BrE)
A cup of tea, typically prepared with spices, especially in South Asian tradition.
a spiced cup of tea (often South Asian)

Examples

On winter afternoons, I love having a cup of chay to keep warm.
She served her guests a cup of chay with homemade biscuits.
After dinner, they relaxed with a cup of chay and good conversation.

Synset Examples

A warm cup of chay is perfect on a cold day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chai - spiced tea beverage, especially in India
  • Synonym: tea - hot beverage made by infusing dried tea leaves in water

Etymology

From Hindi/Urdu 'chai', meaning 'tea', itself from Chinese 'chá'.
