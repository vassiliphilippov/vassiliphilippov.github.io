math whisperer

1. NOUN

/ mæθ ˈwɪspɚɚ / (AmE), / mæθ ˈwɪspərə / (BrE)
a person who is exceptionally skilled at teaching mathematics, especially in a way that makes complex ideas easy to understand
person who excels at teaching math

Examples

Students always look to their teacher, the math whisperer, for guidance before big exams.
Her reputation as a math whisperer spread quickly through the school district.
Thanks to the math whisperer, even the most challenging algebra problems became manageable.

Synset Examples

The teacher is a math whisperer, making complex concepts simple.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whisperer - a person who has a special talent for calming or communicating with animals or people

Etymology

Modelled on 'dog whisperer', using 'whisperer' to mean a person who can communicate with or understand something exceptionally well.
