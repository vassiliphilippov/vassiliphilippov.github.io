bush pea

1. NOUN

/ bʊʃ pi / (AmE), / bʊʃ piː / (BrE)
Any of several plants of the genus Thermopsis, characterized by trifoliate leaves and yellow or purple racemose flowers.
genus of plants with trifoliate leaves and showy flowers

Examples

The bush pea is often grown in gardens for its attractive yellow flowers.
Botanists studied several species of bush pea native to North America.
You can recognize a bush pea by its trifoliate leaves and brightly colored racemes.
