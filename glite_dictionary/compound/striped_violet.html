striped violet

1. NOUN

/ straɪpt ˈvaɪəlɪt / (AmE), / straɪpt ˈvaɪələt / (BrE)
a leafy-stemmed violet of eastern North America with large white or creamy flowers faintly marked with purple
North American violet with purple-marked white flowers

Examples

The striped violet blooms early in spring and brightens shaded woodland areas.
Botanists are fascinated by the distinct purple markings of the striped violet.
Hikers often stop to admire the delicate flowers of the striped violet along forest trails.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: violet - a small plant with purple, blue, or white flowers
