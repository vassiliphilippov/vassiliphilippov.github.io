false baby's breath

1. NOUN

/ fɔls ˈbeɪbiz brɛθ / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈbeɪbiz brɛθ / (BrE)
A Eurasian herb with abundant clusters of small white flowers; naturalized in North America.
Eurasian herb with small white flowers

Examples

The garden was full of false baby's breath, brightening the landscape with its delicate white flowers.
Florists often use false baby's breath as filler in bouquets for its airy appearance.
Although false baby's breath is native to Eurasia, it can now be found growing wild in North America.
