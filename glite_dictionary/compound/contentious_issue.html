contentious issue

1. NOUN

/ kənˈtɛnʃəs ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / kənˈtɛnʃəs ˈɪsjuː / (BrE)
a topic or subject that causes disagreement or heated debate
topic that causes disagreement

Examples

The new tax law has become a contentious issue among lawmakers this year.
Climate change policy is often a contentious issue in international negotiations.
The allocation of resources proved to be a contentious issue during the committee meeting.

Synset Examples

The debate on abortion remains a contentious issue.
Welfarism is a contentious topic in modern political debates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: controversial issue - a subject that gives rise to public disagreement
