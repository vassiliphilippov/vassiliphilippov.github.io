Muslim community

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌzlɪm kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / ˈmʊzlɪm kəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
A group of people who follow Islam, typically living together or sharing social connections and cultural practices.
group of people practicing Islam

Examples

The local Muslim community organized a charity drive during Ramadan.
She has worked closely with the Muslim community to promote intercultural understanding.
Members of the Muslim community celebrated Eid together at the mosque.

Synset Examples

The Muslime community gathered for their annual festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Muslim - a follower of Islam
  • Synonym: Islamic community - a community made up of followers of Islam
