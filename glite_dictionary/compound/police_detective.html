police detective

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis dɪˈtɛktɪv / (AmE), / pəˈliːs dɪˈtɛktɪv / (BrE)
a police officer whose job is to investigate crimes and gather evidence
a police officer who investigates crimes

Examples

The police detective interviewed witnesses to piece together what happened.
It took a skilled police detective to solve the complex case.
After years of experience, she was promoted to police detective.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detective - a person who investigates crimes and gathers evidence
