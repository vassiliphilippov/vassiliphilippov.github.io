get-well card

1. NOUN

/ ɡɛt ˈwɛl kɑrd / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈwɛl kɑːd / (BrE)
a greeting card sent to someone who is ill, expressing wishes for a speedy recovery
card wishing someone a quick recovery

Examples

She sent a get-well card to her friend recovering from surgery.
There were dozens of get-well cards displayed on the hospital windowsill.
He smiled when he opened the get-well card from his coworkers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greeting card - a decorative card sent to convey good wishes
