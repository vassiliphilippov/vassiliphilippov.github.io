rose leek

1. NOUN

/ roʊz lik / (AmE), / rəʊz liːk / (BrE)
A North American bulbous plant (Allium cernuum), in the onion family, with nodding clusters of pinkish flowers.
North American bulbous plant (Allium cernuum)

Examples

The rose leek is easily identified by its drooping clusters of pink flowers.
Gardeners often plant the rose leek for its attractive appearance and mild onion scent.
A variety of pollinators are attracted to the blossoms of the rose leek.
