edible asparagus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛdəbəl əˈspærəɡəs / (AmE), / ˈɛdɪbəl əˈspærəɡəs / (BrE)
A plant whose tender young shoots are cooked and eaten as a vegetable.
asparagus plant eaten as a vegetable

Examples

Many chefs prefer to use edible asparagus in spring salads for its fresh flavor.
We planted several rows of edible asparagus in our garden last year.
The market sells both wild and edible asparagus during the harvest season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asparagus - a perennial plant whose young shoots are eaten as a vegetable
