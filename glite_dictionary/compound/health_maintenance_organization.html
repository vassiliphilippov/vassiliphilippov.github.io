health maintenance organization

1. NOUN

/ hɛlθ ˈmeɪntənəns ˌɔrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / hɛlθ ˈmeɪntənəns ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an organization providing group health insurance that offers members access to a network of doctors, hospitals, and clinics for a fixed annual fee
type of group health insurance plan

Examples

Our company provides employees with coverage through a health maintenance organization to manage health costs.
She chose a health maintenance organization because it offers comprehensive care under one plan.
Many Americans receive their healthcare through a health maintenance organization, known as an HMO.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: HMO - abbreviation for health maintenance organization
  • Degree Lower: preferred provider organization - a type of health insurance plan with a network of preferred providers

Etymology

Formed from the words 'health', 'maintenance', and 'organization'; often abbreviated as HMO, widely adopted in the United States in the 1970s.
