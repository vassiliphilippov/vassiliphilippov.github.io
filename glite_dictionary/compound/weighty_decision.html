weighty decision

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪti dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈweɪti dɪˈsɪʒən / (BrE)
a choice or judgment that has serious importance or consequence
significant or serious choice

Examples

After much deliberation, the board reached a weighty decision about merging the two companies.
Choosing a major in college can feel like a weighty decision for many students.
The president's weighty decision regarding foreign policy altered the course of history.

Synset Examples

She made a weighty decision that would affect many lives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: important decision - a decision of great significance
  • Antonym: trivial decision - a decision of little significance
