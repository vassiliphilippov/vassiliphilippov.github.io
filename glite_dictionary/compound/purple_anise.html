purple anise

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl ˈænɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈænɪs / (BrE)
a small evergreen shrub with purple flowers, native to wet areas of the southeastern United States
shrubby tree with purple flowers (US Southeast)

Examples

The purple anise thrives in the moist woods of Florida and Georgia.
Gardeners value the purple anise for its vibrant flowers and aromatic leaves.
We spotted a purple anise growing along the edge of the swamp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Illicium floridanum - the botanical name for purple anise
