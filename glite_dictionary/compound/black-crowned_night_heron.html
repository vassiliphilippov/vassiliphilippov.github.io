black-crowned night heron

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈkraʊnd ˈnaɪt ˌhɛr.ən / (AmE), / blæk ˈkraʊnd ˈnaɪt ˌhɛr.ən / (BrE)
a medium-sized heron (Nycticorax nycticorax) found across the world, with a distinctive black cap on its head
a heron species with a black crown

Examples

We spotted a black-crowned night heron wading in the marsh at sunrise.
The black-crowned night heron is known for its striking red eyes and black head plumage.
Several black-crowned night herons roosted quietly in the branches above the pond.
