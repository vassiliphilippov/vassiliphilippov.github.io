California sea lion

1. NOUN

/ kæləˌfɔrniə si ˈlaɪ.ən / (AmE), / ˌkælɪˈfɔːniə si ˈlaɪ.ən / (BrE)
a species of sea lion (Zalophus californianus) found along the western coast of North America, often trained for performances
species of sea lion from western North America

Examples

The California sea lion is a common sight on the docks of San Francisco.
Trainers use whistles to teach California sea lions tricks for aquarium shows.
A California sea lion can be recognized by its external ear flaps and playful behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea lion - large marine mammal related to seals, with external ears and strong flippers
