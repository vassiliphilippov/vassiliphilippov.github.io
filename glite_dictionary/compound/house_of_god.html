house of God

1. NOUN

/ haʊs əv ɡɑd / (AmE), / haʊs əv ɡɒd / (BrE)
a building dedicated to religious worship; a place where people gather for prayer
a place of worship; religious building

Examples

Many people find peace and solace in the house of God during difficult times.
The community gathered in the house of God to celebrate the festival.
Regardless of faith, all are welcome in the house of God.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: church - a building used for Christian worship
  • Synonym: mosque - a building for Muslim worship
  • Synonym: temple - a building used for religious worship
