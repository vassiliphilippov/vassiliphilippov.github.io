nail-biter

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪl ˌbaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈneɪl ˌbaɪtə / (BrE)
An event or situation that causes intense suspense or anxiety, making people very nervous about the outcome.
tense, suspenseful situation

Examples

The championship game was a real nail-biter that kept fans on the edge of their seats.
Her job interview turned into a nail-biter when she was kept waiting for the results all day.
Every election night seems to be a nail-biter with results coming in at the last minute.

Synset Examples

His performance was a nail biter until the very end.
The suspenseful movie left everyone as a nail biter.
The final seconds of the match were a total nail biter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cliffhanger - a story or event with a suspenseful ending

Etymology

From the literal phrase of biting one's nails, a common nervous habit, used metaphorically for suspenseful situations.
