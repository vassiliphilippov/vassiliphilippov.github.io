feminoid existence

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛmɪnɔɪd ɪɡˈzɪstəns / (AmE), / ˈfɛmɪnɔɪd ɪɡˈzɪstəns / (BrE)
a state of life or being defined by characteristics associated with femininity or feminine traits
life or being characterized by feminine traits

Examples

The novel delves into what it means to live a feminoid existence in a patriarchal society.
Her sculptures symbolize the struggles and strengths inherent in feminoid existence.
Philosophers debate whether a truly feminoid existence can exist independently from social constructs.

Synset Examples

The artist's work explores the concept of a feminoid existence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feminine - having qualities or characteristics traditionally associated with women
  • Antonym: masculine existence - a state of life or being defined by characteristics associated with masculinity

Etymology

From 'feminoid' (feminine-like, from Latin 'femina' meaning woman) + 'existence'.
