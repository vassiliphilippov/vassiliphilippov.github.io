computer geek

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtɚ ɡik / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ɡiːk / (BrE)
a person who is very enthusiastic and knowledgeable about computers and technology
person enthusiastic about computers
informal

Examples

As a computer geek, she built her own PC from scratch.
The movie features a lovable computer geek who saves the day with his technical skills.
Many computer geeks spend their weekends experimenting with new programming languages.

Synset Examples

He proudly identifies as a computer geek.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: computer nerd - person devoted to computers, often socially awkward
  • Synonym: technophile - person who loves or enthusiastically embraces technology

Etymology

From 'computer' and 'geek', where 'geek' originally referred to an eccentric or intelligent person, now often in technology contexts.
