body positivity

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdi ˌpɑzəˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˌpɒzɪˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
the acceptance and appreciation of all body types, regardless of shape, size, or appearance
acceptance of all body types

Examples

Social media campaigns often encourage body positivity to challenge unrealistic beauty standards.
Her journey toward body positivity inspired many of her followers to love themselves as they are.
Promoting body positivity in schools helps students build confidence and self-esteem.

Synset Examples

She posted an unairbrushed photo to promote body positivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: body acceptance - embracing one's own body as it is
