teddy bear

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛdi ˌbɛr / (AmE), / ˈtɛdi ˌbeə / (BrE)
A soft toy in the shape of a bear, usually made of plush fabric and stuffed with soft materials, often given to children.
soft toy bear for children

Examples

As a child, Emily slept every night with her teddy bear by her side.
The little boy clung tightly to his favorite teddy bear during the storm.
Grandma gave me a handmade teddy bear for my birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stuffed animal - a child's soft toy shaped like an animal and stuffed with soft material

Etymology

Named after U.S. President Theodore 'Teddy' Roosevelt, following a 1902 incident when he refused to shoot a bear cub.
