star sign

1. NOUN

/ stɑr saɪn / (AmE), / stɑː saɪn / (BrE)
one of the twelve divisions of the zodiac, used in astrology to indicate which sign the sun was in at a person's birth
astrological zodiac sign

Examples

She asked me what my star sign was during the interview.
Many people check their horoscopes based on their star sign every day.
He doesn't believe in astrology and thinks star signs are meaningless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zodiac sign - an astrological sign corresponding to part of the zodiac
