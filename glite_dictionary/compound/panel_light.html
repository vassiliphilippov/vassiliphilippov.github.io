panel light

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænəl laɪt / (AmE), / ˈpænəl laɪt / (BrE)
a light or lamp that illuminates an instrument panel, typically in a vehicle or aircraft
light illuminating an instrument panel

Examples

The panel light dimmed, making the speedometer hard to read at night.
He replaced the burnt-out panel light in his car last weekend.
Aircraft pilots rely on the panel light for instrument visibility during night flights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dashboard light - a light illuminating the dashboard of a vehicle
