blight on the neighborhood

1. NOUN

/ blaɪt ɑn ðə ˈneɪbɚˌhʊd / (AmE), / blaɪt ɒn ðə ˈneɪbəhʊd / (BrE)
something that spoils the appearance or quality of a neighborhood, often by being neglected, unsightly, or damaging.
an eyesore or damaging presence in a neighborhood

Examples

The burned-out factory soon became a blight on the neighborhood that everyone wanted removed.
Many residents complained that the empty lot was a blight on the neighborhood.
A poorly maintained property can quickly turn into a blight on the neighborhood if left unchecked.

Synset Examples

The abandoned house became a blight on the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eyesore - something unpleasant to look at, especially a building or object
