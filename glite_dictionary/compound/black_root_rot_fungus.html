black root rot fungus

1. NOUN

/ blæk ruːt rɑt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / blæk ruːt rɒt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a fungus that causes black root rot disease, particularly in apple trees
fungus causing black root rot in apples

Examples

The orchard was devastated by black root rot fungus that attacked the apple trees' roots.
Proper soil drainage can help prevent black root rot fungus from spreading among plants.
Researchers are developing new methods to detect black root rot fungus in agricultural soils.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black root rot - a plant disease affecting roots, causing them to turn black and rot
