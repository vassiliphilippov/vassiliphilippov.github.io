stuffing box

1. NOUN

/ ˈstʌfɪŋ ˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈstʌfɪŋ ˌbɒks / (BrE)
a small chamber around a moving shaft or piston, filled with packing material that is compressed to form a seal and prevent leakage
chamber for sealing a moving shaft or piston

Examples

The engineer inspected the stuffing box to ensure there were no leaks around the shaft.
Regular maintenance of the stuffing box can prolong the life of the equipment.
The stuffing box was packed tightly to prevent water from seeping into the pump.
