birth control

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːθ kənˌtroʊl / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθ kənˌtrəʊl / (BrE)
the practice of preventing pregnancy by controlling the number or spacing of children through various methods, such as contraception
prevention of pregnancy

Examples

She decided to use birth control to plan her family.
Many young people learn about birth control options in health education classes.
The doctor discussed different types of birth control with her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contraception - prevention of pregnancy by artificial or natural means
