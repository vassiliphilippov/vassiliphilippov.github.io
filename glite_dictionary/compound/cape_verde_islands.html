Cape Verde Islands

1. PROPN

/ keɪp vɜrd ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / keɪp vɜːd ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
a group of islands in the Atlantic Ocean located off the western coast of Africa, near Senegal; now known as Cabo Verde
archipelago off West Africa (Cabo Verde)

Examples

We spent our vacation exploring the beautiful beaches of the Cape Verde Islands.
The Cape Verde Islands were an important stop for ships crossing the Atlantic in the past.
Many migratory birds stop at the Cape Verde Islands during their seasonal journeys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cabo Verde - official name of the Cape Verde Islands

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Cabo Verde,' meaning 'Green Cape.'
