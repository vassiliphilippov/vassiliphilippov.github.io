genus Scomber

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈskɒm.bər / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈskɒm.bə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Scombridae, comprising certain species of mackerel.
type genus of the mackerel family

Examples

The genus Scomber includes several species important to commercial fisheries.
Scientists study the genus Scomber to better understand mackerel distribution.
Fish in the genus Scomber are found in temperate and tropical seas.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Scombridae - the mackerel family of fish

Etymology

From Latin 'genus' (kind) and 'Scomber', the Latin name for mackerel.
