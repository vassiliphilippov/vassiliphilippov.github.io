Greater Israel

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪtər ɪzˈrɛl / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtə ɪzˈrɛl / (BrE)
a political concept advocating for territorial claims extending beyond the internationally recognized borders of the State of Israel, often referring to acquiring lands described in historic or biblical texts
political-territorial concept about Israel's borders

Examples

Debates over Greater Israel have shaped regional politics for decades.
The idea of Greater Israel remains controversial among historians and politicians alike.
Some groups support the vision of Greater Israel as part of their national ideology.
