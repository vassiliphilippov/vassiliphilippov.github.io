carrot soup

1. NOUN

/ ˈkærət suːp / (AmE), / ˈkærət suːp / (BrE)
A soup made primarily from carrots and often including other ingredients such as stock, seasonings, and sometimes cream.
soup made mainly from carrots

Examples

She served a warm bowl of carrot soup on a cold winter evening.
The restaurant is famous for its creamy carrot soup starter.
Making homemade carrot soup is both healthy and delicious.

Synset Examples

In 1908, diarrhea killed many babies in Germany. Professor Moro, at that time the head of a children hospital in Heidelberg, found out by experiment that a simple carrot soup decreased the death rate of babies suffering from diarrhea by nearly 50%.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetable soup - soup made with various vegetables
