banana bread

1. NOUN

/ bəˈnæn.ə brɛd / (AmE), / bəˈnæn.ə brɛd / (BrE)
A moist, sweet loaf bread made with mashed bananas, often served sliced and sometimes with nuts or spices added.
sweet, moist bread made with bananas

Examples

She baked a fresh loaf of banana bread for the breakfast meeting.
The aroma of banana bread filled the kitchen as it came out of the oven.
I love spreading butter on warm banana bread for a quick snack.
