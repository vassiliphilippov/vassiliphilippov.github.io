Palauan time

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlaʊən taɪm / (AmE), / pəˈlaʊ.ən taɪm / (BrE)
A relaxed or flexible approach to time and scheduling, typical of Palauan or Pacific Island culture.
relaxed approach to time/scheduling

Examples

When you're in Palau, it's normal to operate on Palauan time, so don't worry if things start late.
The dinner didn't begin at the scheduled hour; they were clearly following Palauan time.
She reminded the visitors to embrace Palauan time and relax about the schedule.

Synset Examples

Embrace Palauan time; it’s all about relaxation and enjoyment.
We are running on Palauan time; the meeting will start late.
On Palauan time, schedules are flexible and laid-back.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: island time - relaxed approach to time typical in island cultures

Etymology

From Palau, an island country in the western Pacific Ocean, referring to local attitudes toward time.
