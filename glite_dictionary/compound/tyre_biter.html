tyre biter

1. NOUN

/ taɪr ˈbaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈtaɪə ˌbaɪtə / (BrE)
A person or animal that damages tyres, especially by biting; can also refer informally to a troublesome person.
person or animal that damages tyres
informal british

Examples

We had to replace the tyres after a tyre biter attacked them during the night.
There's a real tyre biter in the neighbourhood, always puncturing everyone's bicycle tyres.
My dog is such a tyre biter—he can't resist chewing on car wheels.

Synset Examples

The tyre biter chewed through the rubber quickly.
The tyre biter caused a lot of damage to the garden.
We need to keep that tyre biter away from the car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troublemaker - a person who causes problems or difficulties
  • Synonym: nuisance - someone or something causing inconvenience or annoyance
