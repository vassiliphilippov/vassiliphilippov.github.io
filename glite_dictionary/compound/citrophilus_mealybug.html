citrophilus mealybug

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪtroʊˈfɪləs ˈmiːliˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˌsɪtrəˈfɪləs ˈmiːliˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
a species of mealybug (Pseudococcus calceolariae) that is a destructive pest, especially of citrus plants
destructive citrus plant pest (mealybug)

Examples

The citrophilus mealybug can cause severe damage to orange groves if left untreated.
Growers monitor their citrus trees closely for signs of citrophilus mealybug infestation.
Researchers found that the citrophilus mealybug multiplied rapidly in warm, humid conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mealybug - a small, soft-bodied insect that sucks plant sap
