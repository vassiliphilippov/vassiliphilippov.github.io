across the country

1. ADV

/ əˈkrɔs ðə ˈkʌntri / (AmE), / əˈkrɒs ðə ˈkʌntri / (BrE)
in or to every part of a nation; throughout the whole country
throughout the entire nation

Examples

The music festival was celebrated across the country with events in every major city.
New laws were implemented across the country to improve public safety.
After the announcement, people across the country shared their reactions on social media.

Synset Examples

nationally advertised
it was broadcast nationwide

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nationwide - covering or extending throughout an entire nation
  • Antonym: locally - within a specific area rather than across a whole country

2. ADP

/ əˈkrɔs ðə ˈkʌntri / (AmE), / əˈkrɒs ðə ˈkʌntri / (BrE)
from one side of a country to the other, usually referring to travel or movement over a long distance within a country
from one side of a nation to the other

Examples

The band embarked on a tour across the country to promote their new album.
Wildfires have spread across the country during the unusually hot summer.
He moved across the country for a new job opportunity.

Synset Examples

The trucker drove across the country in just three days.
