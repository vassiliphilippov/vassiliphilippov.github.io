canned goods

1. NOUN

/ kænd ɡʊdz / (AmE), / kænd ɡʊdz / (BrE)
Foods that have been preserved and sealed in cans to extend their shelf life.
foods preserved in cans

Examples

The pantry was stocked with canned goods for the winter months.
Donations of canned goods are welcome at the food drive.
She opened a few canned goods to prepare a quick meal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tinned food - foods preserved and sold in tins (British equivalent)
