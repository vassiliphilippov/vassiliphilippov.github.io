crow-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈkroʊ saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊ saɪzd / (BrE)
having approximately the same size as a crow; about as big as a typical crow
about as big as a crow

Examples

The mysterious bird was crow-sized and had glossy black feathers.
We saw a crow-sized drone hovering above the field during testing.
Their new sculpture features several crow-sized owls perched on a metal branch.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sparrow-sized - about as big as a sparrow
  • Degree Higher: eagle-sized - about as big as an eagle
