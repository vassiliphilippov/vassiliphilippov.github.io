chick sexer

1. NOUN

/ tʃɪk ˈsɛksər / (AmE), / tʃɪk ˈsɛksə / (BrE)
A person trained to determine the sex of chicks and other hatchlings, typically at commercial hatcheries.
person who identifies sex of chicks

Examples

The chick sexer carefully examined each hatchling to determine whether it was male or female.
Becoming a skilled chick sexer requires intensive training and a sharp eye for detail.
Many hatcheries employ a chick sexer to sort large numbers of chicks efficiently.

Synset Examples

Chick sexing is the method of distinguishing the sex of chicken and other hatchlings, usually by a trained person called a chick sexer or chicken sexer.

Related Terms
