eurotrash music

1. NOUN

/ ˈjʊroʊˌtræʃ ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / ˈjʊərəʊˌtræʃ ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
(derogatory) European pop music regarded as cheap, tasteless, or of low quality.
derogatory term for cheap European pop music
informal slang offensive

Examples

Some critics dismissed the album as just another example of eurotrash music.
The club played nothing but eurotrash music all night, which annoyed the regulars.
He claims he can't stand eurotrash music but secretly loves a few tracks.

Synset Examples

They criticized the festival for promoting eurotrash music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eurotrash - (offensive slang) A derogatory term for ostentatious Europeans regarded as shallow or tasteless

Etymology

Formed from 'eurotrash', which combines 'Euro-' (European) with 'trash' (something worthless).
