international organisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntɚˈnæʃənl ˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃn / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənl ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃn / (BrE)
an organization composed of members from several countries, usually formed to pursue a common purpose of international interest
organization involving multiple countries

Examples

The World Health Organization is a well-known international organisation focused on public health.
Joining an international organisation can help countries address issues that cross national borders.
Many students aspire to work for an international organisation like the United Nations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: international organization - US English spelling: an organization involving many different countries
