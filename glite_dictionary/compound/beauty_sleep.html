beauty sleep

1. NOUN

/ ˈbjuːti sliːp / (AmE), / ˈbjuːti sliːp / (BrE)
Sleep that is considered especially beneficial to one's appearance and overall well-being, often referring to sleep taken before midnight.
sleep beneficial for appearance, esp. before midnight

Examples

She makes sure to get her beauty sleep every night to feel refreshed in the morning.
I've heard that missing out on beauty sleep can make you look tired the next day.
He joked that he needed extra beauty sleep before the big presentation.
