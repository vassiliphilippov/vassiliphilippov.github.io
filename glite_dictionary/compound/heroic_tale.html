heroic tale

1. NOUN

/ hɪˈroʊɪk teɪl / (AmE), / hɪˈrəʊɪk teɪl / (BrE)
a story that tells of a hero's adventures, often involving brave deeds and challenges
story about a hero's adventures

Examples

The ancient bard recited a heroic tale of knights and dragons.
Children love to read a heroic tale before bedtime, dreaming of brave adventurers.
The movie was based on a heroic tale passed down through generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: epic - a long poem or story about heroic deeds
  • Synonym: legend - a traditional story sometimes regarded as historical but unauthenticated
