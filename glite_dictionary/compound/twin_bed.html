twin bed

1. NOUN

/ twɪn bɛd / (AmE), / twɪn bɛd / (BrE)
one of a pair of single beds, each just wide enough for one person, often used in pairs in a shared room
narrow bed for one person, often in pairs

Examples

The hotel room featured two twin beds separated by a nightstand.
She prefers sleeping in a twin bed because it fits her small room perfectly.
We ordered matching sheets for the kids' twin beds in their new bedroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: single bed - a bed suitable for one person
  • Antonym: double bed - a bed for two people
