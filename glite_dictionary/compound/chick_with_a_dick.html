chick with a dick

1. NOUN

/ tʃɪk wɪð ə dɪk / (AmE), / tʃɪk wɪð ə dɪk / (BrE)
A slang and often offensive term for a transgender woman or gender non-conforming individual, typically referring to someone presenting as female but having male genitalia.
