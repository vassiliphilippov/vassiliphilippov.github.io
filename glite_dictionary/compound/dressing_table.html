dressing table

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɛsɪŋ ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsɪŋ ˌteɪbəl / (BrE)
A low table with a mirror or mirrors, typically used for dressing or applying makeup.
table with a mirror for dressing or makeup

Examples

She kept her jewelry neatly arranged on the dressing table in her bedroom.
He sat at the dressing table, carefully combing his hair before the interview.
The antique dressing table added a touch of elegance to the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vanity - a piece of bedroom furniture with a mirror and drawers
