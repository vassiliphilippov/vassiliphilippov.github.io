photo mural

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊtoʊ ˈmjʊrəl / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊ ˈmjʊərəl / (BrE)
A decorative wallpaper featuring a large photographic image, typically applied to cover one wall and used for visual impact.
decorative wallpaper with a large photographic image

Examples

The designer suggested a photo mural of a forest to bring the room to life.
They installed a custom photo mural on the living room wall to create a stunning focal point.
A cityscape photo mural can make even a small office feel more dynamic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wallpaper - Decorative paper for covering walls
