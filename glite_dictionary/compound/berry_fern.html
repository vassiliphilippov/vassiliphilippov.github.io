berry fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛri fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈbɛri fɜːn / (BrE)
A North American fern, often bearing bulbils on its leaflets.
type of North American fern

Examples

The berry fern is known for producing tiny bulbils along its fronds.
Gardeners often propagate berry ferns using the bulbils found on the leaves.
While hiking, we spotted a patch of berry ferns near the stream.
