Doctor of Dental Surgery

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑktɚ əv ˈdɛntəl ˈsɜrdʒɚi / (AmE), / ˈdɒktə əv ˈdɛntəl ˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
A degree awarded to dentists upon completion of dental school, qualifying them to practice dental surgery.
degree in dental surgery

Examples

After receiving his Doctor of Dental Surgery, he opened his own dental clinic.
Applicants must hold a Doctor of Dental Surgery degree to qualify for the specialist program.
She proudly displayed her Doctor of Dental Surgery diploma on her office wall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: DDS - abbreviation for Doctor of Dental Surgery
  • Synonym: Doctor of Dental Medicine - alternative dental degree (DMD) equivalent to Doctor of Dental Surgery
