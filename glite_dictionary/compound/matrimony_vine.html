matrimony vine

1. NOUN

/ ˈmætrəˌmoʊni vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈmætrɪməni vaɪn / (BrE)
any of several shrubs or climbing plants of the genus Lycium, known for their showy flowers and bright berries, commonly found in Eurasia.
a shrub or vine of the genus Lycium

Examples

Many herbal teas include dried matrimony vine berries for their health benefits.
The matrimony vine is also called goji in some regions due to its edible fruit.
Gardeners plant matrimony vine both for its ornamental flowers and its traditional uses.
