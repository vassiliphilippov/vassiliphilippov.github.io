clapper valve

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæpər vælv / (AmE), / ˈklapə vælv / (BrE)
a simple mechanical valve with a hinged flap that allows fluid to flow in only one direction
simple one-way mechanical valve

Examples

Water can only pass through the clapper valve in one direction to prevent backflow.
The mechanic replaced the damaged clapper valve in the engine system.
A clapper valve is commonly used in pumps and fire sprinklers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: check valve - a valve that only allows flow in one direction
