film making

1. NOUN

/ fɪlm ˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / fɪlm ˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of producing movies, from initial idea through to editing and distribution
the production of movies

Examples

She has always dreamed of working in film making since childhood.
Advances in technology have greatly influenced modern film making techniques.
The university offers several courses in film making for aspiring directors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filmmaking - alternative spelling referring to the same process
  • Synonym: cinematography - the art of making motion pictures, especially as regards camera work and lighting
