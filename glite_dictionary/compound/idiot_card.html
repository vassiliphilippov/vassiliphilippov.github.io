idiot card

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪdiət kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈɪdiət kɑːd / (BrE)
a humorous term used to refer to a mistake or lapse in judgment, often implying a momentary lack of intelligence
humorous term for a mistake or lapse in judgment
informal

Examples

After forgetting his wallet at home, Tom joked that he had pulled out his idiot card again.
Every time I mess up at work, I feel like I'm flashing my idiot card.
She laughed and waved her idiot card after spilling coffee on her report.

Synset Examples

He pulled out his idiot card after forgetting the meeting.
