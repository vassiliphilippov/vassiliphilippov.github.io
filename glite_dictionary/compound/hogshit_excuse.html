hogshit excuse

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑɡˌʃɪt ɪkˈskjus / (AmE), / ˈhɒɡˌʃɪt ɪkˈskjuːs / (BrE)
A very poor or blatantly false excuse; an excuse that is obviously untrue or unacceptable.
an obviously poor excuse
vulgar informal

Examples

Don’t give me that hogshit excuse for missing the meeting again.
Her hogshit excuse didn’t fool anyone at the office.
He tried to justify his mistake, but it was just a hogshit excuse.

Synset Examples

Stop giving me that hogshit excuse for being late.
