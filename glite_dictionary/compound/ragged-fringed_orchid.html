ragged-fringed orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈræɡɪd ˈfrɪndʒd ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈræɡɪd ˈfrɪndʒd ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
a fringed orchid native to the eastern United States, characterized by greenish flowers with deeply lacerated lips
North American orchid with lacerated lips

Examples

The ragged-fringed orchid can be found in moist meadows across much of the eastern United States.
Botanists admire the ragged-fringed orchid for its delicately lacerated lip and unique coloration.
We spotted a rare ragged-fringed orchid blooming along the forest edge last summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Platanthera lacera - scientific name for the ragged-fringed orchid
