garden pink

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdən pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn pɪŋk / (BrE)
A cultivated plant of the genus Dianthus, grown for its fragrant and colorful flowers.
fragrant Dianthus flower

Examples

The border was lined with garden pinks, filling the air with a sweet fragrance.
She planted several varieties of garden pink in her flowerbed last spring.
Many gardeners prize garden pinks for their long-lasting blooms and vibrant colors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dianthus - a genus of flowering plants that includes pinks and carnations
