blotted out

1. ADJ

/ ˈblɑtɪd aʊt / (AmE), / ˈblɒtɪd aʊt / (BrE)
completely erased, destroyed, or made to disappear; reduced to nothingness.
completely erased or destroyed

Examples

The evidence of their existence was blotted out by time and decay.
A thick mist blotted out the sun, leaving the landscape in darkness.
All memories of the incident were blotted out from her mind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: erased - completely removed or wiped out
  • Synonym: eliminated - completely removed or destroyed
  • Antonym: preserved - kept safe from harm or destruction
