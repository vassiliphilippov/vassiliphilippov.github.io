flashing light

1. NOUN

/ ˈflæʃɪŋ laɪt / (AmE), / ˈflæʃɪŋ laɪt / (BrE)
a light that blinks or shines intermittently, often used as a warning or signal
a light that blinks intermittently

Examples

The ambulance approached with its flashing light and siren blaring.
He installed a flashing light outside his home for emergencies.
A flashing light at the railroad crossing warns drivers to stop.

Synset Examples

He found the flashing lights quite obtrusive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blinking light - a light that flashes on and off repeatedly
  • Antonym: steady light - a light that remains constantly on without blinking
