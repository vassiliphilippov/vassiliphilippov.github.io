coffee fungus

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔfi ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a fungus that causes disease in coffee plants and some other tropical crops
fungus infecting coffee plants

Examples

The plantation suffered severe losses due to an outbreak of coffee fungus.
Scientists are developing new methods to control coffee fungus in tropical regions.
The spread of coffee fungus has endangered both crops and local economies in several countries.
