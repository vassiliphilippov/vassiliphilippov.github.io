calm personality

1. NOUN

/ kɑm pɚˈsænələti / (AmE), / kɑːm pəˈsɒn.əl.ə.ti / (BrE)
a character trait marked by tranquility and an absence of agitation or excitement
tranquil, peaceful character

Examples

She is well known for her calm personality and ability to handle stress.
His calm personality made him a natural leader during the emergency.
People with a calm personality often excel in stressful professions.

Synset Examples

He has a calm personality that soothes others.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: nervous personality - a character trait marked by anxiety or agitation
  • Synonym: calm - free from agitation or excitement
