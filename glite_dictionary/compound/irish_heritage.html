Irish heritage

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.rɪʃ ˈhɛr.ə.tɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.rɪʃ ˈhɛr.ɪ.tɪdʒ / (BrE)
the cultural, historical, or ancestral background originating from Ireland
ancestry or culture from Ireland

Examples

Her Irish heritage influences many of her family traditions and celebrations.
He learned to cook traditional dishes as a way to reconnect with his Irish heritage.
Schools sometimes host cultural days where students can share their Irish heritage with others.

Synset Examples

She has an Irish heritage that she proudly shares.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heritage - something inherited from previous generations
