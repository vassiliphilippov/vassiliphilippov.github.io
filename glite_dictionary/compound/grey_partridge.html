grey partridge

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪ ˈpɑrtɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈpɑːtɪdʒ / (BrE)
a common European game bird with grey and brown plumage, also known as the English partridge (Perdix perdix)
European game bird, Perdix perdix

Examples

The grey partridge is often seen in open farmland across Europe.
Farmers have noticed a decline in the local grey partridge population in recent years.
We spotted a group of grey partridges feeding in the fields early this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partridge - a medium-sized game bird with a plump body
