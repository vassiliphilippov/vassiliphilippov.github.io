cart-track plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrt trækk plænt / (AmE), / ˈkɑːt træk plɑːnt / (BrE)
A common European perennial plant (Sonchus arvensis), widespread as a weed in many countries.
a common perennial weed (Sonchus arvensis)

Examples

The fields were overrun with cart-track plant after the summer rains.
Gardeners often struggle to remove the persistent cart-track plant from their plots.
The cart-track plant is known for its rapid spread along rural pathways.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sow thistle - another common name for Sonchus arvensis, the cart-track plant
