mourning cloak

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrnɪŋ kloʊk / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ kləʊk / (BrE)
a large butterfly of temperate regions, having dark purple wings with distinct yellow borders, scientifically known as Nymphalis antiopa.
a butterfly with dark wings and yellow borders

Examples

We spotted a mourning cloak resting on the tree trunk near the forest edge.
The mourning cloak is often one of the first butterflies seen in early spring.
Many nature enthusiasts recognize the mourning cloak by its dark velvet wings and pale yellow border.
