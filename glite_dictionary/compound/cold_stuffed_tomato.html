cold stuffed tomato

1. NOUN

/ koʊld stʌft təˈmeɪtoʊ / (AmE), / kəʊld stʌft təˈmɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
A tomato hollowed out and filled with a salad mixture, served cold.
tomato filled with salad and served cold

Examples

For lunch, I made a cold stuffed tomato with tuna and fresh herbs.
The restaurant's cold stuffed tomato is a popular appetizer in the summer months.
She served a cold stuffed tomato at the picnic, filled with egg salad.
