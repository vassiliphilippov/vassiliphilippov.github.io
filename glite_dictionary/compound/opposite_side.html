opposite side

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːpəzɪt saɪd / (AmE), / ˈɒpəzɪt saɪd / (BrE)
the position or area directly across from another position or area
position directly across from another

Examples

They waited for each other on opposite sides of the river.
Please sit on the opposite side of the table so I can see you clearly.
Each team started on opposite sides of the field at the beginning of the game.

Synset Examples

The two leaders stood on opposite sides of the barricades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: side - one of the surfaces or directions of something
  • Antonym: same side - the position on the same part as another
