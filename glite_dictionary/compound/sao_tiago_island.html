Sao Tiago Island

1. PROPN

/ saʊ tiˈɑːɡoʊ ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / saʊ tiˈɑːɡəʊ ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
the largest of the Cape Verde Islands in the Atlantic Ocean, known for its diverse landscapes and historic significance
largest island of Cape Verde

Examples

We spent a week exploring Sao Tiago Island, taking in its colonial architecture and volcanic landscapes.
Many immigrants from Sao Tiago Island have made significant contributions to communities abroad.
The capital city of Cape Verde, Praia, is located on Sao Tiago Island.

Etymology

Portuguese, meaning 'Saint James'.
