political arena

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪdɪkəl əˈrinə / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl əˈriːnə / (BrE)
the sphere or field of active political activity, debate, or competition
sphere of active political activity

Examples

She has spent her entire career in the political arena, fighting for social reform.
Newcomers to the political arena often struggle to make their voices heard.
The scandal shook the entire political arena and led to major changes in leadership.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: politics - activities associated with governance or government affairs
  • Synonym: public life - the aspects of life related to the community or society, especially in politics

Etymology

From 'political' and metaphorical use of 'arena' (Latin harena: sand, place of contest), meaning field of conflict or activity.
