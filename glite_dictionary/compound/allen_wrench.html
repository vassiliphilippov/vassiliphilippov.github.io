Allen wrench

1. NOUN

/ ˈælən rɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˈælən rɛntʃ / (BrE)
a tool with a hexagonal cross-section used to turn Allen screws or bolts with hexagonal sockets
hexagonal tool for Allen screws
american

Examples

I couldn't assemble the bookshelf without an Allen wrench to tighten the bolts.
The set comes with an Allen wrench for easy installation of the handlebar.
He looked for the right size Allen wrench in his toolbox.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hex key - another term for a hexagonal wrench used to drive screws with hexagonal sockets
  • Antonym: screwdriver - tool used for turning screws

Etymology

From the Allen Manufacturing Company, which popularized the tool.
