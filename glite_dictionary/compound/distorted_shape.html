distorted shape

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈstɔːrtɪd ʃeɪp / (AmE), / dɪˈstɔːtɪd ʃeɪp / (BrE)
a shape that has been changed so that it is no longer in its normal or original form, typically as a result of force or pressure.
shape altered from its original form

Examples

The heat caused the plastic to melt into a distorted shape.
Looking into the curved mirror, her face appeared as a distorted shape.
After the accident, the sculpture remained only as a distorted shape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distortion - the act or process of twisting or altering something out of its true, natural, or original state
