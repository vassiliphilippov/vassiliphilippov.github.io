level best

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛvəl ˈbɛst / (AmE), / ˈlɛvəl ˈbɛst / (BrE)
the greatest effort possible; one's utmost effort
utmost effort

Examples

She did her level best to finish the project on time, despite the challenges.
The team tried their level best to win the match, but fell just short.
Even when he was tired, he always gave his level best at work.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utmost - the greatest or most extreme extent or amount
  • Antonym: minimum - the least or smallest amount possible; opposite of maximum effort
