ordnance store

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdnəns stɔr / (AmE), / ˈɔːdnəns stɔː / (BrE)
a place where military weapons, ammunition, and equipment are stored and supplied
military storage facility for weapons and supplies

Examples

The stolen rifles were later discovered hidden in the ordnance store at the army base.
After the inspection, the officers ensured all ammunition was accounted for in the ordnance store.
A fire in the ordnance store could have catastrophic consequences for the entire facility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armory - a place where weapons and military equipment are kept

Etymology

From ordnance (military weapons and ammunition) + store (a place for keeping things).
