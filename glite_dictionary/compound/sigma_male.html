sigma male

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪɡmə meɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡmə meɪl / (BrE)
a man who is independent, self-reliant, and tends to operate outside traditional social hierarchies or expectations
a self-reliant, non-conformist man
informal

Examples

Many consider the sigma male a mystery because he does not seek validation from others.
Unlike the alpha, the sigma male prefers to chart his own path.
In popular culture, the sigma male has become a symbol of independence.

Synset Examples

His independence is what defines him as a sigma male.
The sigma male thrives outside traditional social hierarchies.
He prefers solitude, embodying the sigma male archetype.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: alpha male - a dominant man, seen as a leader
  • Synonym: lone wolf - a person who prefers to act alone

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'alpha male', using 'sigma' from the Greek alphabet to suggest an outsider status.
