tool of trade

1. NOUN

/ tuːl əv treɪd / (AmE), / tuːl əv treɪd / (BrE)
the equipment or skills essential for carrying out one's profession or occupation
essential equipment or skills for a profession

Examples

A stethoscope is a vital tool of trade for any doctor.
For a chef, sharp knives are the basic tools of trade.
Communication is an important tool of trade for a successful manager.

Synset Examples

Writers must develop their tools of trade over time.
Every artist needs the right tools of trade to create.
Mechanics rely on their tools of trade for efficient work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equipment - the necessary items for a particular purpose
