Bade language

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːdeɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːdeɪ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken mainly in northern Nigeria, especially in Yobe, Jigawa, and Bauchi States
a Chadic language of northern Nigeria

Examples

Linguists are working to document the Bade language before it becomes endangered.
She grew up speaking the Bade language with her family in Yobe State.
The conference featured a workshop on the preservation of the Bade language.
