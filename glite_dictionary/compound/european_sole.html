European sole

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən soʊl / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən səʊl / (BrE)
a species of flatfish (Solea solea) found in European waters, highly valued as a food fish
edible European flatfish (Solea solea)

Examples

The restaurant’s specialty was grilled European sole served with a lemon butter sauce.
Fishermen in the North Sea have noticed a decline in European sole populations.
She bought fresh European sole at the market for tonight’s dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sole - a type of flatfish, particularly Solea solea or related species
