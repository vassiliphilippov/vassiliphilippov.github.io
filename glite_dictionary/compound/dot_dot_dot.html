dot dot dot

1. NOUN

/ dɑt dɑt dɑt / (AmE), / dɒt dɒt dɒt / (BrE)
a spoken or written phrase used to indicate an ellipsis (...) representing omitted text, hesitation, or an unfinished thought
the spoken phrase for an ellipsis
informal

Examples

Whenever he told a story, he always added a dot dot dot for suspense.
Please don't end your sentences with a dot dot dot unless something is left unsaid.
In the script, she's supposed to pause and say dot dot dot before continuing her line.

Synset Examples

He left the message unfinished: "We need to talk... dot dot dot."

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ellipsis - a set of dots (...) indicating omission
