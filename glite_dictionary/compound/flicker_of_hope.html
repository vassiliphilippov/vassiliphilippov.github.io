flicker of hope

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɪkɚ əv hoʊp / (AmE), / ˈflɪkə əv həʊp / (BrE)
a brief or slight indication that hope still exists
a slight sign of hope

Examples

As the rescue team arrived, a flicker of hope appeared among the survivors.
Despite the setbacks, she still felt a flicker of hope for the future.
His words gave me a flicker of hope when I needed encouragement the most.

Synset Examples

I saw a flicker of hope in her eyes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glimmer of hope - a faint or slight possibility that something good will happen
