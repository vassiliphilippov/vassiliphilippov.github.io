sudden cannonade

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌd(ə)n ˌkænəˈneɪd / (AmE), / ˈsʌd(ə)n ˌkænəˈneɪd / (BrE)
A sudden and loud noise resembling the firing of cannons.
a loud noise like cannon fire

Examples

A sudden cannonade of thunder shook the windows during the storm.
She was awakened by the sudden cannonade of fireworks outside her window.
The speech was interrupted by a sudden cannonade of applause from the audience.

Synset Examples

The sudden cannonade startled everyone at the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: explosion - A violent and loud burst, often with destructive force
  • Synonym: bang - A sudden loud noise; a sharp blow or sound
  • Antonym: silence - Absence of sound or noise
