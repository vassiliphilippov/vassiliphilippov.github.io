Pyramids of Egypt

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɪrəmɪdz əv ˈiːdʒɪpt / (AmE), / ˈpɪərəmɪdz əv ˈiːdʒɪpt / (BrE)
the monumental ancient stone structures with square bases and triangular sides built as royal tombs in ancient Egypt, most famously at Giza
ancient Egyptian royal tombs

Examples

The Pyramids of Egypt attract millions of tourists every year, fascinated by their size and history.
Archaeologists have studied the Pyramids of Egypt to learn more about ancient burial practices.
She marveled at the engineering behind the Pyramids of Egypt during her trip to Giza.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pyramid - a massive monument with a square base and four triangular sides
