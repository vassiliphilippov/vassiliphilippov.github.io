crème brûlée

1. NOUN

/ kɹɛm bruˈleɪ / (AmE), / ˌkrɛm bruːˈleɪ / (BrE)
A dessert of rich custard topped with a hard layer of caramelized sugar.
custard dessert with caramelized sugar topping

Examples

For dessert, I ordered the crème brûlée and enjoyed cracking the caramelized sugar on top.
The restaurant's signature crème brûlée is served with fresh berries on the side.
Have you ever tried making crème brûlée at home with a kitchen torch?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: custard - a dish made of milk, eggs, and sugar cooked into a thick, creamy consistency

Etymology

From French crème brûlée, meaning 'burnt cream'.
