blue meanie

1. NOUN

/ bluː ˈmiːni / (AmE), / bluː ˈmiːni / (BrE)
An informal term for a person who is grumpy, unpleasant, or brings negativity to a situation.
grumpy or negative person (informal)
informal slang

Examples

Don’t be a blue meanie; just relax and enjoy the evening with us.
Whenever someone suggests new ideas, the office blue meanie finds a reason to complain.
He was labeled a blue meanie after criticizing everyone’s excitement about the holiday plans.

Synset Examples

The blue meanie tried to spoil our fun at the party.
Her blue meanie attitude was a buzzkill for everyone.
Don't be a blue meanie; join us for some laughs!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: killjoy - a person who spoils the enjoyment of others
  • Synonym: party pooper - a person who dampens enthusiasm or fun

Etymology

Originally from the antagonists called Blue Meanies in the Beatles' 1968 animated film 'Yellow Submarine.'
