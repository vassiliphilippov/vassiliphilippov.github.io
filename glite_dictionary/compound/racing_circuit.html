racing circuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪsɪŋ ˈsɝːkɪt / (AmE), / ˈreɪsɪŋ ˈsɜːkɪt / (BrE)
a specially designed track or course where automobile races are held, usually featuring multiple laps and turns
race track for automobile racing

Examples

The Formula 1 Grand Prix will be held at the newly built racing circuit next month.
Many fans traveled across the country to watch the race at the famous racing circuit.
He dreams of someday competing on an international racing circuit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racetrack - a track or course for racing, especially for horses or cars
