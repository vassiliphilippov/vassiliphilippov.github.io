Puerto Rico Trench

1. NOUN

/ ˌpwɛrtoʊ ˈriːkoʊ trɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˌpwɛːtəʊ ˈriːkəʊ trɛntʃ / (BrE)
a deep depression in the floor of the Atlantic Ocean located to the north of Puerto Rico and Haiti, constituting the deepest part of the Atlantic.
oceanic trench north of Puerto Rico

Examples

The Puerto Rico Trench is the deepest part of the Atlantic Ocean.
Earthquakes sometimes occur near the Puerto Rico Trench due to tectonic activity.
Scientists study the Puerto Rico Trench to better understand geological processes beneath the sea.
