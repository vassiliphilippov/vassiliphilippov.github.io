Indian lotus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən ˈloʊtəs / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈləʊtəs / (BrE)
a species of aquatic plant native to eastern Asia, widely cultivated for its large pink or white flowers
aquatic plant with large pink or white flowers

Examples

The pond behind the temple was covered with beautiful Indian lotus in full bloom.
She painted a mural of the Indian lotus, symbolizing purity and enlightenment.
The Indian lotus is often used in religious ceremonies throughout Asia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lotus - any of several water lilies; especially those of the genus Nelumbo
