honours list

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑnərz lɪst / (AmE), / ˈɒnəz lɪst / (BrE)
an official list issued by examiners that ranks students according to the class of honours they achieved in their degree examinations
list ranking students by degree honours
british

Examples

Only the top students made it onto the honours list this year.
After graduation, the honours list was posted outside the faculty office.
Being on the honours list is a significant achievement for any university student.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honours degree - an academic degree with a classification of higher achievement
