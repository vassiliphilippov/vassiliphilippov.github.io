data converter

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪtə kənˌvɝtər / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtə kənˌvɜːtə / (BrE)
a device or program that changes information from one format, code, or system to another, especially in computing and electronics
device or program that changes information format

Examples

The laboratory uses a data converter to process signals from analog to digital form.
Our new smartphone features an advanced data converter that improves audio quality.
They needed a specialized data converter to interface with the legacy system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: converter - a device that changes something into a different form
