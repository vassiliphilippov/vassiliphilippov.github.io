Balkan country

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːlkən ˈkʌntri / (AmE), / ˈbælkən ˈkʌntri / (BrE)
a country located on the Balkan Peninsula in southeastern Europe
country on Balkan Peninsula

Examples

Greece is often considered a Balkan country due to its location and history.
Tourism has increased in every Balkan country over the last decade.
The cuisine of each Balkan country reflects a blend of Eastern and Western influences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Balkan state - a country located on the Balkan Peninsula

Etymology

Balkan from Turkish 'Balkan' (a mountain), referring to the mountain range; 'country' from Old French 'contrée'.
