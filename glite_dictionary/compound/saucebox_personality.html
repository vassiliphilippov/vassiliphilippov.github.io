saucebox personality

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔsˌbɑks pɚˌsɒnˈæləti / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsˌbɒks pəˌsɒnˈæləti / (BrE)
A lively and talkative personality, often characterized by cheeky or spirited conversation.
lively, talkative personality
informal

Examples

Her saucebox personality made everyone feel welcome at the party.
People were drawn to his saucebox personality and infectious laughter.
Even in serious meetings, her saucebox personality lightened the mood and encouraged conversation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: reserved - shy and quiet in temperament

Etymology

Formed from 'saucebox' (an old-fashioned term for an audacious or cheeky person) and 'personality'.
