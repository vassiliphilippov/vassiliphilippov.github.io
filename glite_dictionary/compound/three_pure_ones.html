Three Pure Ones

1. PROPN

/ θri pjur wʌnz / (AmE), / θriː pjʊə wʌnz / (BrE)
the three supreme deities in Taoism who are regarded as the highest gods in the Taoist pantheon
the three chief deities of Taoism

Examples

The Three Pure Ones are central figures in traditional Taoist temples and rituals.
Many Taoist practitioners honor the Three Pure Ones during major religious festivals.
Legends describe the Three Pure Ones as manifestations of the purest forms of the Tao.
