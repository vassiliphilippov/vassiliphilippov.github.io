powder-post damage

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊdɚ poʊst ˈdæmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə pəʊst ˈdamɪdʒ / (BrE)
Damage to wood caused by infestation of powder-post beetles, resulting in fine, powdery dust and weakened structure.
wood damage from powder-post beetles

Examples

The old barn had extensive powder-post damage in its rafters, making repairs necessary.
Home inspectors found powder-post damage beneath the flooring of the historic house.
Preventing powder-post damage requires treating wood and keeping moisture levels low.

Synset Examples

They replaced the beams due to powder-post damage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powder-post beetle - wood-boring beetle whose larvae produce powdery dust in infested wood
