hiking gear

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪkɪŋ ɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈhaɪkɪŋ ɡɪə / (BrE)
the equipment and clothing needed for hiking
equipment for hiking

Examples

She packed all her hiking gear before leaving for the mountains.
Make sure your hiking gear includes sturdy boots and a waterproof jacket.
He bought new hiking gear for his trip along the Pacific Crest Trail.

Synset Examples

The duffel was heavy with all his hiking gear.
