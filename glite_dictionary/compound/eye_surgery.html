eye surgery

1. NOUN

/ aɪ ˈsɜrdʒəri / (AmE), / aɪ ˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
a surgical procedure performed on the eye or its surrounding tissues to treat vision problems or eye disorders
surgical procedure involving the eye
medical

Examples

He underwent eye surgery to correct his vision last year.
Advances in eye surgery have made treatments safer and more effective.
After her eye surgery, she was able to see clearly without glasses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ocular surgery - surgery of or relating to the eye; synonym for eye surgery
  • Degree Lower: laser eye surgery - type of eye surgery using lasers to correct vision
