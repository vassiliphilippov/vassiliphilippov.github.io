critter sitter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪt̬ɚ ˈsɪt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪtə ˈsɪtə / (BrE)
a person who takes care of pets while their owners are away from home
someone who looks after pets for others
informal

Examples

We hired a critter sitter to look after our dogs while we went on vacation.
As a critter sitter, she checks in on cats, dogs, and even parrots every day.
Finding a reliable critter sitter is crucial if you travel often for work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petsitter - a person hired to care for pets while the owner is away

Etymology

Formed from 'critter' (informal for animal) and 'sitter' (caretaker), following the pattern of 'babysitter'.
