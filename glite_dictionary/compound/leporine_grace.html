leporine grace

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛpəˌraɪn ɡreɪs / (AmE), / ˈlɛpəriːn ɡreɪs / (BrE)
elegance or grace that is characteristic of rabbits, especially in movement or appearance
rabbit-like elegance or grace

Examples

The dancer's movements had a leporine grace that captivated the audience.
With leporine grace, the gymnast vaulted effortlessly over the apparatus.
The model walked down the runway with the leporine grace of a woodland creature.

Synset Examples

He admired the leporine grace of the rabbit as it hopped away.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grace - elegance or beauty of movement

Etymology

From 'leporine', from Latin leporinus (of a hare or rabbit), and 'grace'.
