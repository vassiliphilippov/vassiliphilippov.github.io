crumb cake

1. NOUN

/ krʌm keɪk / (AmE), / krʌm keɪk / (BrE)
A cake topped with a crumbly mixture of sugar, butter, and flour, often served as a coffee cake.
cake with a crumb topping

Examples

She brought a homemade crumb cake to the office party.
I love having a slice of crumb cake with my morning coffee.
The bakery's crumb cake was topped with a thick layer of sweet, buttery crumbs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffee cake - a cake, often served with coffee, usually with a streusel or crumb topping
