living death

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvɪŋ dɛθ / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ dɛθ / (BrE)
a state of existence that is unfulfilling, unbearable, or marked by persistent suffering, likened to being dead while still alive
state of unbearable or unfulfilling existence

Examples

For years, the prisoner endured a living death in solitary confinement.
Chronic illness had turned her once joyful life into a living death.
He feared a coma would leave him trapped in a living death for decades.

Synset Examples

He described his job as a living death.
Being in that toxic relationship was a living death.
The constant pain felt like a living death.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hell on earth - a situation or place of extreme suffering or torment
  • Synonym: living hell - a life or situation filled with continuous suffering
