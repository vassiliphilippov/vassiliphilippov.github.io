pair of underwear

1. NOUN

/ pɛr əv ˈʌndɚˌwɛr / (AmE), / peə əv ˈʌndəwɛə / (BrE)
A single item of undergarment worn under outer clothing, typically referring to underpants.
one undergarment for lower body

Examples

I need to buy a new pair of underwear before my trip.
He realized he had put on his pair of underwear inside out.
There was only one clean pair of underwear left in the drawer.

Synset Examples

He forgot to pack an extra pair of underwear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underwear - clothes worn next to the skin under other clothes
