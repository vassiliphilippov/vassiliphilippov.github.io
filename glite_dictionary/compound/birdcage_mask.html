birdcage mask

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːdˌkeɪdʒ mæsk / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdˌkeɪdʒ mɑːsk / (BrE)
A protective mask made with a strong wire face, worn by baseball catchers to shield the face from injury.
baseball catcher's wire face mask

Examples

The player adjusted his birdcage mask before taking his place behind home plate.
A new birdcage mask can provide better visibility for the catcher.
He replaced his old gear with a modern birdcage mask for extra protection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catcher's mask - face protector worn by a baseball catcher
