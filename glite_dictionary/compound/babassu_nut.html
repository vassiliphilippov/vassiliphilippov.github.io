babassu nut

1. NOUN

/ bəˈbɑːsuː nʌt / (AmE), / bəˈbæsuː nʌt / (BrE)
the hard-shelled nut of the babassu palm, valued for its oil and edible kernel
nut of the babassu palm

Examples

The babassu nut is widely harvested in Brazil for its valuable oil.
She uses babassu nut oil as a moisturizer for her skin and hair.
After collecting the babassu nuts, villagers extract both the oil and the edible kernel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: babassu palm - a South American palm tree whose nut offers valuable oil

Etymology

From Portuguese 'babassu', from Tupi 'babaçu', meaning the palm species in Brazil.
