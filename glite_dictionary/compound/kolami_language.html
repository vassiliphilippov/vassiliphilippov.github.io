Kolami language

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈlɑːmi ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / kəʊˈlɑːmi ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken by the Kolam people in central India
a Dravidian language of central India

Examples

The Kolami language is classified under the Dravidian family of languages.
Few people outside central India speak or understand Kolami language.
Researchers are working to preserve the Kolami language and document its grammar.

Etymology

From Kolam (the ethnic group) + language.
