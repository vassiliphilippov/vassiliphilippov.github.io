winter-blooming

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪntɚ ˌbluːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪntə ˌbluːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant) flowering during the winter season.
flowering in winter (of plants)

Examples

The winter-blooming camellias bring color to the garden when most plants are dormant.
Many gardeners appreciate winter-blooming shrubs for their ability to brighten up bleak landscapes.
The winter-blooming jasmine is prized for its yellow flowers that appear despite the cold.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: spring-blooming - (of a plant) flowering during the spring season
  • Antonym: summer-blooming - (of a plant) flowering during the summer season
  • Antonym: autumn-blooming - (of a plant) flowering during the autumn/fall season
