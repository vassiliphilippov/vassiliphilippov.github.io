decaffeinated coffee

1. NOUN

/ diˈkæfəˌneɪtɪd ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / diˈkæfɪneɪtɪd ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
Coffee from which most or all of the caffeine has been removed.
coffee with caffeine removed

Examples

She always orders decaffeinated coffee after dinner so she can sleep better at night.
Many restaurants now offer both regular and decaffeinated coffee to their customers.
He accidentally bought decaffeinated coffee instead of the strong brew he likes.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: regular coffee - coffee containing its natural amount of caffeine
  • Synonym: decaf - abbreviation for decaffeinated coffee

Etymology

From 'decaffeinate' (remove caffeine from) + 'coffee'.
