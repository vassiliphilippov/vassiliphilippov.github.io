ice bucket

1. NOUN

/ aɪs ˈbʌkət / (AmE), / aɪs ˈbʌkɪt / (BrE)
a container designed for holding ice, often used to chill drinks such as wine or champagne
a container for holding ice to chill drinks

Examples

The waiter brought a chilled bottle of wine in an ice bucket to our table.
Please fill the ice bucket before the guests arrive.
After the party, the ice bucket was left full of melting water.

Synset Examples

An ice bucket kept the champagne perfectly chilled.
She grabbed an ice bucket to cool down the wine.
He filled the ice bucket with drinks for the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bucket - an open container with a handle, used for carrying liquids or other substances
