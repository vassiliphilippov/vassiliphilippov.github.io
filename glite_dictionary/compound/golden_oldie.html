golden oldie

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈoʊldi / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈəʊldi / (BrE)
a popular song from the past, especially one that remains well-liked or is played frequently years after its release
a popular song from the past
informal

Examples

Every week the radio show plays a golden oldie requested by a listener.
That song is a real golden oldie—I remember dancing to it in high school.
Sometimes a golden oldie becomes popular again with a new generation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classic - a popular and lasting song or recording
