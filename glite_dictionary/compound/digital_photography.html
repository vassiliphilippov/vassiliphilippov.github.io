digital photography

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪdʒɪt̬əl fəˈtɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl fəˈtɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
The method of capturing, storing, and reproducing photographs using digital technology instead of traditional film.
photography using digital technology

Examples

With digital photography, it is easy to store thousands of pictures on a single device.
Advances in digital photography have revolutionized the way we share images online.
He prefers digital photography because it allows instant review and editing of photos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photography - the art or practice of producing images by the action of light
