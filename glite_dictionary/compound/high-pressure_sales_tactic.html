high-pressure sales tactic

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈprɛʃər seɪlz ˈtæktɪk / (AmE), / haɪ ˈprɛʃə seɪlz ˈtæktɪk / (BrE)
a forceful or aggressive method used to persuade customers to buy products or services
aggressive selling method

Examples

After experiencing a high-pressure sales tactic, she decided not to make the purchase.
Some companies rely on high-pressure sales tactics to close deals quickly.
He was uncomfortable with the high-pressure sales tactic the car dealer used.

Synset Examples

The high-pressure sales tactics turned me off.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: soft sell - a gentle, subtle, or non-aggressive sales approach
  • Synonym: hard sell - a direct and forceful sales approach
