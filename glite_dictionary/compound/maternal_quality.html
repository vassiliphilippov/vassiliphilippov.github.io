maternal quality

1. NOUN

/ məˈtɜrnəl ˈkwɑləti / (AmE), / məˈtɜːnəl ˈkwɒləti / (BrE)
the quality of showing warmth, tenderness, and affection characteristic of a mother
mother-like warmth or affection

Examples

Her maternal quality made her the perfect mentor for new employees.
People admired the maternal quality with which she cared for the neighborhood children.
He appreciated the maternal quality of his grandmother's gentle advice.

Synset Examples

the girl's motherliness made her invaluable in caring for the children

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motherliness - the quality of being like a mother, especially in warmth and caring
