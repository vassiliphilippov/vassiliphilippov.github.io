labor pain

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪ.bɚ peɪn / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bə peɪn / (BrE)
the pain and discomfort experienced during contractions of the uterus in childbirth
pain during childbirth contractions
medical

Examples

She gripped her husband's hand tightly as the labor pain intensified.
Many women describe labor pain as a unique and powerful sensation.
The nurse taught breathing techniques to help manage severe labor pain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: childbirth - the process of giving birth to a baby
