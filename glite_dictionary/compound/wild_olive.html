wild olive

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ˈɑːlɪv / (AmE), / waɪld ˈɒlɪv / (BrE)
An erect shrub or climbing plant found in India and China, bearing red fruit resembling olives.
shrub or climber with red olivelike fruit

Examples

The botanist studied the wild olive growing along the forest edge in eastern India.
Local villagers collect the fruit of the wild olive for use in traditional remedies.
The wild olive thrives in a variety of soils, making it common in several Asian regions.
