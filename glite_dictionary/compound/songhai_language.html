Songhai language

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔːŋɡaɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɒŋɡaɪ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Nilo-Saharan language spoken primarily by the Songhai people of Mali and Niger
language of the Songhai people of West Africa

Examples

The Songhai language is widely spoken along the Niger River in Mali and Niger.
She is fluent in both French and the Songhai language.
Linguists have studied the Songhai language to understand its Nilo-Saharan roots.

Etymology

Named after the Songhai people of West Africa.
