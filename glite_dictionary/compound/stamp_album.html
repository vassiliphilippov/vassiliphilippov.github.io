stamp album

1. NOUN

/ stæmp ˈæl.bəm / (AmE), / stæmp ˈæl.bəm / (BrE)
A book with pages for organizing and displaying a collection of postage stamps.
book for collecting postage stamps

Examples

He proudly showed me his stamp album filled with rare stamps from around the world.
As a child, she spent hours organizing her collection in a stamp album.
A good stamp album helps preserve stamps and keep them in excellent condition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: album - A book for storing collections such as photographs or stamps
