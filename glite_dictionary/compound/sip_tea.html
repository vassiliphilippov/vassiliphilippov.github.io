to sip tea

1. VERB

/ sɪp ti / (AmE), / sɪp tiː / (BrE)
to drink tea slowly, especially to enjoy its flavor and the experience
drink tea slowly and enjoyably

Examples

Every afternoon, she likes to sip tea and watch the sunset from her porch.
He would often sip tea quietly while reading the morning newspaper.
Tourists gathered in the garden to sip tea and admire the blooming flowers.

Synset Examples

She enjoys reading while sipping tea at the teahouse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drink tea - to consume tea by drinking
