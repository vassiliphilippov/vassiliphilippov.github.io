State of Bahrain

1. PROPN

/ steɪt əv bəˈreɪn / (AmE), / steɪt əv bəˈreɪn / (BrE)
an island country in the Persian Gulf, off the coast of Saudi Arabia, known for its oil reserves and progressive economic development
island country in the Persian Gulf

Examples

The capital of the State of Bahrain is Manama, which is known for its modern skyline.
The State of Bahrain experienced rapid economic growth thanks to its oil industry.
Many international sporting events are hosted annually in the State of Bahrain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bahrain - alternative name for State of Bahrain

Etymology

From Arabic البحرين (al-Baḥrayn), meaning 'the two seas'.
