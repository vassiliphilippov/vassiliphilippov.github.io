woman's doctor

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊmənz ˈdɑktɚ / (AmE), / ˈwʊmənz ˈdɒktə / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in gynecology; a gynecologist
doctor specializing in gynecology

Examples

She scheduled an annual check-up with her woman's doctor next month.
Many women feel more comfortable discussing certain issues with a trusted woman's doctor.
The woman's doctor explained the options for birth control clearly and compassionately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gynecologist - a doctor specializing in women's reproductive health
