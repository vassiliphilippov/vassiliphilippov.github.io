speech communication

1. NOUN

/ spiːt͡ʃ kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / spiːtʃ kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of conveying information or ideas through spoken language
conveying information by speaking

Examples

Speech communication plays a crucial role in effective leadership and management.
Courses in speech communication help students improve their public speaking skills.
Technological advances have transformed the nature of speech communication in global business.

Synset Examples

his speech was garbled
he uttered harsh language
he recorded the spoken language of the streets

Related Terms

  • Synonym: verbal communication - the transmission of information using words, spoken or written
  • Antonym: written communication - the exchange of information using written language
