Short's aster

1. NOUN

/ ʃɔrts ˈæstɚ / (AmE), / ʃɔːts ˈæstə / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to the southeastern United States, usually with blue flowers.
perennial flower of southeastern US

Examples

The woods behind her house were full of Short's aster blooming in late summer.
Gardeners in the region often plant Short's aster for its vibrant blue flowers.
I spotted a beautiful Short's aster along the hiking trail yesterday.
