chain of events

1. NOUN

/ tʃeɪn əv ɪˈvɛnts / (AmE), / tʃeɪn əv ɪˈvɛnts / (BrE)
a series of related or connected events, each one leading to the next
series of connected events

Examples

The accident set off a chain of events that changed her life forever.
A minor error triggered a chain of events resulting in a massive system failure.
The detective tried to reconstruct the chain of events leading up to the crime.

Synset Examples

The chain of events led us to unexpected conclusions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: series of events - a sequence of events occurring one after another
  • Synonym: sequence - an ordered set of related things or events
