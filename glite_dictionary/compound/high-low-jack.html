high-low-jack

1. NOUN

/ haɪ loʊ dʒæk / (AmE), / haɪ ləʊ dʒæk / (BrE)
A card game in which players win points for taking the highest card, the lowest card, the jack, or the most cards during play.
card game with points for high, low, jack, and game

Examples

We played high-low-jack all evening at the cabin.
I had never learned the rules of high-low-jack before last weekend.
Many consider high-low-jack to be an exciting game for all ages.
