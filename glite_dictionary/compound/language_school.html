language school

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ skuːl / (BrE)
an institution where foreign languages are taught, especially to non-native speakers
school for learning foreign languages

Examples

After graduating, she enrolled in a language school in Spain to improve her Spanish.
Many students attend a language school during the summer holidays.
The city offers several opportunities to study at a language school for international learners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: language institute - institute for teaching languages
