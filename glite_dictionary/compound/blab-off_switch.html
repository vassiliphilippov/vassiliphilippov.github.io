blab-off switch

1. NOUN

/ blæb ɑf swɪtʃ / (AmE), / blæb ɒf swɪtʃ / (BrE)
a hypothetical device or means to stop someone from talking excessively
hypothetical way to stop excessive talking

Examples

He joked that his teacher needed a blab-off switch during long meetings.
Sometimes I wish I had a blab-off switch to press at noisy dinner parties.
Parents everywhere have dreamed of installing a blab-off switch in their chatty children.

Synset Examples

If only she had a blab-off switch for gossip!
A blab-off switch would help him focus better.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mute button - a button that turns off sound on an electronic device
  • Degree Lower: chatterbox - a person who talks a lot, especially a child
