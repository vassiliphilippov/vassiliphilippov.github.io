to find a cure

1. VERB

/ faɪnd ə kjʊr / (AmE), / faɪnd ə kjʊə / (BrE)
to discover an effective treatment or remedy for a disease or medical condition
discover a treatment for a disease

Examples

Scientists are working tirelessly to find a cure for cancer.
Many people donate money to help researchers find a cure for rare diseases.
If we can find a cure soon, it will save countless lives.

Synset Examples

The doctor promised to find a cure for his illness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cure - a treatment that eliminates a disease
