douchey joke

1. NOUN

/ ˈduːʃi dʒoʊk / (AmE), / ˈduːʃi dʒəʊk / (BrE)
An offensive or obnoxious joke, often considered tasteless or unpleasant by others.
offensive or obnoxious joke
informal slang

Examples

His attempt at humor quickly turned into a douchey joke that offended everyone at the table.
Please don't tell another douchey joke—it's making people uncomfortable.
The comedian was criticized for making a douchey joke during the live show.

Synset Examples

She hates when he makes those douchey jokes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tasteless joke - a joke considered lacking in good taste or appropriateness

Etymology

From the slang adjective 'douchey' (based on 'douche', a term co-opted for describing an unpleasant person) + 'joke'.
