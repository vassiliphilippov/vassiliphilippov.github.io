addressing machine

1. NOUN

/ əˈdrɛsɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / əˈdrɛsɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine that automatically prints addresses on letters or envelopes for mailing purposes
machine that prints addresses for mailing

Examples

The company used an addressing machine to prepare thousands of envelopes for the marketing campaign.
Advances in technology have made the traditional addressing machine largely obsolete in modern offices.
Before the days of digital printing, mailing departments relied on the addressing machine for bulk correspondence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: addressograph - a brand or type of addressing machine used for mass mailing
