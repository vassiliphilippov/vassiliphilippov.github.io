can-do attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæn ˌdu ˈæt̬ɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈkan ˌduː ˈatɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
a positive and confident approach to tasks and challenges, showing a willingness to deal with problems or new situations enthusiastically
positive and confident approach; proactive mindset

Examples

Her can-do attitude impressed everyone on the team.
Even in tough situations, he maintains a can-do attitude that motivates others.
A can-do attitude is essential when working in a fast-paced environment.

Synset Examples

He approached the challenge with a can-do attitude.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimism - hopefulness and confidence about the future
  • Antonym: pessimism - a tendency to see the worst aspect of things
