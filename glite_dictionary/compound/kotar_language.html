Kotar language

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊtɑr ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtɑː ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken by the Kota people of southern India
language spoken by the Kota people

Examples

The Kotar language is primarily spoken in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, India.
Linguists consider the Kotar language to be a member of the Dravidian language family.
Efforts are underway to document and preserve the Kotar language for future generations.

Etymology

Kotar refers to the Kota people, an indigenous group in southern India. Language from Latin lingua.
