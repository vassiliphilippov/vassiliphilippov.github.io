car loan

1. NOUN

/ kɑr loʊn / (AmE), / kɑː ləʊn / (BrE)
a loan given to an individual for the specific purpose of purchasing a car
a loan to buy a car

Examples

He applied for a car loan to help cover the cost of his new vehicle.
Many banks offer special interest rates for those seeking a car loan.
Without a car loan, she would not have been able to afford her first car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auto loan - a loan to buy a car or other vehicle
  • Antonym: mortgage - a loan to buy a house or property
