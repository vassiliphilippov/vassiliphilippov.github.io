New World least weasel

1. NOUN

/ nu ˈwɝːld list ˈwiːzəl / (AmE), / njuː ˈwɜːld liːst ˈwiːzl̩ / (BrE)
a small, slender mammal (Mustela nivalis rixosa) native to Canada and the northeastern United States, known for its white winter coat and agile hunting.
small weasel native to North America

Examples

The New World least weasel is the smallest carnivore found in North America.
During winter, the New World least weasel develops a pure white coat for camouflage.
Farmers appreciate the New World least weasel for controlling rodent populations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: least weasel - the smallest species of weasel, found in Eurasia and North America
