tortoiseshell cat

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔrt̬əsˌʃɛl kæt / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtəsʃɛl kat / (BrE)
A domestic cat with a coat marked in a patchwork of black, cream, and yellowish colors, resembling the pattern of tortoiseshell.
cat with mottled black and yellowish markings

Examples

My grandmother has a beautiful tortoiseshell cat that loves to nap in the sun.
The animal shelter featured several tortoiseshell cats looking for homes.
People often remark on the striking markings of a tortoiseshell cat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calico cat - a cat with a tri-colored coat, usually including white, black, and orange patches

Etymology

From 'tortoiseshell', describing the mottled appearance, and 'cat'. 'Tortoiseshell' refers to the shell of certain turtles, valued for its mottled pattern.
