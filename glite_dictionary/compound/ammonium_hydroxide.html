ammonium hydroxide

1. NOUN

/ əˈmoʊniəm haɪˈdrɑksaɪd / (AmE), / əˈməʊniəm haɪˈdrɒksaɪd / (BrE)
a solution of ammonia in water, often used as a cleaning agent or in chemical processes
solution of ammonia in water
medical

Examples

The laboratory prepared a dilute solution of ammonium hydroxide for the experiment.
Cleaning products containing ammonium hydroxide should be used in well-ventilated areas.
Ammonium hydroxide is commonly used to neutralize acidic waste in water treatment plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ammonia - a colorless gas with a pungent smell, used in cleaning products
  • Synonym: ammonium chloride - a white crystalline salt used in dry cells and medicine

Etymology

From 'ammonium', from ammonia, and 'hydroxide', denoting its composition as ammonia in water.
