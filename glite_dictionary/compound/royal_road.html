royal road

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɔɪ.əl roʊd / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪ.əl rəʊd / (BrE)
an easy or assured path to success or a desired goal
an easy path to success or achievement

Examples

Many students look for a royal road to academic success, but consistent effort is usually required.
Unfortunately, there is no royal road to mastering a new language.
Business leaders often wish for a royal road to innovation, but breakthroughs take time and risk.

Synset Examples

the royal road to success

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shortcut - an easier or quicker way to do something
  • Antonym: detour - a longer or indirect route to a destination

Etymology

Metaphorical use of 'royal road' (originally a literal term for ancient Persian highways), referring to a direct or privileged route.
