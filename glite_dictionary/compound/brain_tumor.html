brain tumor

1. NOUN

/ breɪn ˈtuːmɚ / (AmE), / breɪn ˈtjuːmə / (BrE)
An abnormal growth of cells in the brain, which can be benign or malignant.
abnormal growth in the brain
medical

Examples

The MRI scan revealed a brain tumor that required immediate surgery.
She was diagnosed with a benign brain tumor last year.
Doctors are researching new treatments for patients with brain tumors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tumor - an abnormal mass of tissue
