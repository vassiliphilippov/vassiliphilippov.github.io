to wash a car

1. VERB

/ wɑʃ ə kɑr / (AmE), / wɒʃ ə kɑː / (BrE)
to clean the outside surface of an automobile, usually using water and soap
clean the outside of a car

Examples

Every Saturday, I wash a car for some extra money.
She prefers to wash a car herself rather than go to the car wash.
Before the road trip, they all helped to wash a car until it shone.

Synset Examples

He decided to wash his car on Sunday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clean - to make something free from dirt or impurities
