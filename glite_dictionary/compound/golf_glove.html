golf glove

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf ɡlʌv / (AmE), / ɡɒlf ɡlʌv / (BrE)
A glove worn by golfers to improve grip on the handle of a golf club.
glove for golfers to improve grip

Examples

He wore his golf glove to get a better grip on the club during his swing.
Her golf glove was worn out after many rounds on the course.
After losing his golf glove, he struggled to hold the club securely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golf - a sport where players hit a ball into a series of holes using clubs
  • Synonym: glove - a covering for the hand with separate sections for fingers and thumb
