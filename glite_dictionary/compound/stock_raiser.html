stock raiser

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑk ˌreɪzər / (AmE), / ˈstɒk ˌreɪzə / (BrE)
a person who breeds or raises livestock, especially as an occupation
person who breeds or raises livestock

Examples

The stock raiser invested in better fencing to protect his animals from predators.
As a successful stock raiser, she specialized in breeding prizewinning cattle.
Many rural communities depend on the skills of a knowledgeable stock raiser for their livelihoods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rancher - a person who owns or manages a ranch, especially one raising livestock
  • Synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
  • Degree Lower: dairy farmer - a farmer who raises cattle for milk production
