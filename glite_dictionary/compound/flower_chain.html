flower chain

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚ tʃeɪn / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a chain created by stringing flowers together, typically for decoration or as a garland
chain made by stringing flowers together

Examples

Children sat in the meadow, weaving a flower chain to wear on their heads.
The flower chain draped across the bride's shoulders added a lovely touch to her dress.
At summer camp, making a flower chain was a cherished afternoon activity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garland - a decorative wreath or string of flowers and leaves
