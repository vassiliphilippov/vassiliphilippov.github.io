the interweb

1. NOUN

/ ði ˈɪntərˌwɛb / (AmE), / ði ˈɪntəwɛb / (BrE)
a humorous or informal term for the internet
humorous term for the internet
informal

Examples

People often joke about getting lost on the interweb when they browse random sites for hours.
She bought that vintage dress she loved after finding it on the interweb.
Rumors and memes tend to spread quickly across the interweb these days.

Synset Examples

She found the recipe on the interweb after hours of searching.
The interweb is full of fascinating information.
He searched for answers on the interweb late at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: internet - the global system of interconnected computer networks
  • Synonym: web - the World Wide Web, an information system on the internet

Etymology

Blend of 'internet' and 'web', used in jest to refer to the internet.
