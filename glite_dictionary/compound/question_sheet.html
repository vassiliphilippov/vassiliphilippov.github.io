question sheet

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɛs.tʃən ʃiːt / (AmE), / ˈkwɛs.tʃən ʃiːt / (BrE)
a printed or written paper containing questions, especially as part of a test or examination
paper with examination questions

Examples

Please write your name at the top of the question sheet before you start the exam.
The teacher handed out the question sheet to each student as the test began.
After finishing, she checked her answers against those on the question sheet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: test paper - paper or booklet containing questions for an exam
