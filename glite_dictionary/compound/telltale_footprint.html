telltale footprint

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlˌteɪl ˈfʊtˌprɪnt / (AmE), / ˈtɛl.teɪl ˈfʊt.prɪnt / (BrE)
a footprint that reveals evidence of someone's presence or actions
footprint indicating someone's presence

Examples

The detectives noticed a telltale footprint near the broken window, which confirmed their suspicions.
A single telltale footprint in the mud was all they needed to prove someone had been there.
After the snowstorm, a telltale footprint appeared on the otherwise untouched path.

Synset Examples

The telltale footprints led the detectives to the suspect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: footprint - an impression of a foot on a surface
