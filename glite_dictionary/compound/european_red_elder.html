European red elder

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən rɛd ˈɛldɚ / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən rɛd ˈɛldə / (BrE)
A Eurasian shrub (Sambucus racemosa) with clusters of small red berries, commonly found in woodlands and gardens.
Eurasian shrub with red berries

Examples

The European red elder thrives in cool, moist woodland areas across its native range.
Gardeners often plant European red elder for its ornamental red berries and attractive flowers.
Birds are attracted to the bright fruit of the European red elder during late summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red elder - a shrub with red berries
  • Synonym: elderberry - the fruit or shrub of the elder plant
