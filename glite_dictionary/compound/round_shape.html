round shape

1. NOUN

/ raʊnd ʃeɪp / (AmE), / raʊnd ʃeɪp / (BrE)
a shape that is curved and has no sharp angles
shape with no sharp angles, curved

Examples

The table had a round shape that fit perfectly in the small kitchen.
Children often find it easier to draw a round shape than a square one.
The moon appeared in the sky as a beautiful round shape last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circle - a round plane figure whose boundary consists of points equidistant from the center
  • Antonym: square - a shape with four equal straight sides and four right angles
