fashion business

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃən ˌbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˈfæʃ(ə)n ˌbɪznɪs / (BrE)
the industry involved in designing, making, marketing, and selling fashionable clothing and accessories
industry of fashionable clothing and accessories

Examples

She started her own fashion business after graduating from design school.
The fashion business is known for being fast-paced and competitive.
Many young entrepreneurs dream of making it big in the fashion business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashion industry - the industry that designs, manufactures, and sells clothing and accessories
