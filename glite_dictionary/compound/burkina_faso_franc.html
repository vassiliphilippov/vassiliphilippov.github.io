Burkina Faso franc

1. NOUN

/ bɜrˌkiːnə ˈfɑːsoʊ fræŋk / (AmE), / bɜːˌkiːnə ˈfɑːsəʊ fræŋk / (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in Burkina Faso before the adoption of the West African CFA franc
former currency unit of Burkina Faso

Examples

During the early 1980s, the Burkina Faso franc was used in everyday transactions before the region adopted the CFA franc.
She kept a few old Burkina Faso francs as souvenirs from her travels in West Africa.
The value of the Burkina Faso franc fluctuated significantly before its replacement by the CFA franc.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: West African CFA franc - the currency currently used in Burkina Faso and several other West African countries
