piece of writing

1. NOUN

/ pis əv ˈraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / piːs əv ˈraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a section of text or a work created by an author, considered especially in terms of its style, form, or content
a work or section of written text

Examples

Her latest piece of writing captivated readers with its vivid imagery and emotional depth.
He submitted a short piece of writing for the school magazine's competition.
Each applicant must provide a piece of writing as part of the entrance requirements.

Synset Examples

the writing in her novels is excellent
that editorial was a fine piece of writing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: work - an artistic creation such as a book or painting
  • Synonym: composition - a short piece of writing or music
