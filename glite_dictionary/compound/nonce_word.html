nonce word

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑns wɜrd / (AmE), / ˈnɒns wɜːd / (BrE)
A word created for a single occasion or a particular context, often not intended to be used again.
word coined for a particular occasion

Examples

The poet invented a nonce word to fit the unique rhythm of her poem.
In children's literature, authors often use a nonce word to amuse or surprise young readers.
That unusual term is just a nonce word created for this experiment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neologism - a newly coined word or expression

Etymology

From the phrase 'for the nonce' (meaning 'for the occasion') + 'word'.
