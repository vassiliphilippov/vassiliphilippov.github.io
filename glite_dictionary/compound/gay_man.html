gay man

1. NOUN

/ ɡeɪ mæn / (AmE), / ɡeɪ mæn / (BrE)
a man who is sexually or romantically attracted to other men
a homosexual man

Examples

He came out as a gay man in his early twenties and received support from his friends.
Many films now feature a gay man as a main character, reflecting social changes.
As a gay man, he found it important to connect with others in the LGBTQ+ community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homosexual - a person who is sexually attracted to people of their own sex
  • Antonym: straight man - a man who is sexually attracted to women
