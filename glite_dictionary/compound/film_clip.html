film clip

1. NOUN

/ fɪlm klɪp / (AmE), / fɪlm klɪp / (BrE)
a short segment or excerpt from a motion picture, often used in broadcasts or presentations
short segment of motion picture film

Examples

The documentary featured an old film clip of the city in the 1920s.
She showed a film clip during her presentation to illustrate her point.
Every news broadcast included a short film clip from the latest movie release.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clip - short excerpt from a film or video
