secret society

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːkrət səˈsaɪəti / (AmE), / ˈsiːkrɪt səˈsaɪəti / (BrE)
an organization whose membership, activities, or rituals are concealed from nonmembers
organization with concealed membership or activities

Examples

He was rumored to be a member of a powerful secret society.
Some secret societies have existed for hundreds of years, influencing political events behind the scenes.
The initiation ceremony of the secret society was shrouded in mystery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fraternity - a social organization, often with secret rituals, especially at colleges
  • Degree Lower: club - an association of people with a common purpose or interest
