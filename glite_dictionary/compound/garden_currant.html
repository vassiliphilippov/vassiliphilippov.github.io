garden currant

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdən ˈkɜrənt / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˈkʌrənt / (BrE)
a cultivated European shrub bearing small edible red berries, used especially in cooking and jams
a shrub with small edible red berries

Examples

She made jam from the garden currant bushes in her backyard.
A tart dessert can be enhanced by a handful of fresh garden currants.
The garden currant is a popular fruit in European gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: redcurrant - a small, red, edible berry of a related plant
