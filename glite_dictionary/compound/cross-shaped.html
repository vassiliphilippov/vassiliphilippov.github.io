cross-shaped

1. ADJ

/ krɔs ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / krɒs ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Shaped in the form of a cross.
having the shape of a cross

Examples

The church has a cross-shaped floor plan that is common in Gothic architecture.
He wore a cross-shaped pendant on a silver chain around his neck.
The garden featured a large, cross-shaped flowerbed in the center.
