travel tale

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəl teɪl / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl teɪl / (BrE)
a story recounting travel experiences or adventures
story about travel experiences

Examples

She entertained the dinner guests with a travel tale from her recent trip to Peru.
Each travel tale in his book describes a new country he visited.
Listening to a travel tale can inspire others to explore the world.

Synset Examples

Aunt Thomasina always shares her travel tales with us.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: travel story - a story about a journey or travel experience
