stank-ass cheese

1. NOUN

/ stæŋk æs tʃiz / (AmE), / stæŋk ɑːs tʃiːz / (BrE)
(slang) Cheese that has a strong, unpleasant, or spoiled smell.
very bad-smelling cheese
slang informal

Examples

No one wanted to eat the stank-ass cheese after opening the fridge.
She accidentally bought some stank-ass cheese that stunk up the whole kitchen.
If you don't refrigerate it, you'll end up with some stank-ass cheese in a few days.

Synset Examples

That stank-ass cheese was definitely past its expiration date.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smelly cheese - cheese with a strong or bad odor
  • Antonym: fresh cheese - cheese that is not spoiled or odorous
