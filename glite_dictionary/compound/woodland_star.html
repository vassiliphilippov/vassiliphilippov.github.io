woodland star

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdlənd stɑr / (AmE), / ˈwʊdlənd stɑː / (BrE)
a California perennial herb grown for its racemose clusters of white flowers with widely spreading petals, sometimes classified in the genus Tellima
a perennial herb with white star-shaped flowers

Examples

The woodland star thrives in shaded garden beds with moist soil.
She admired the delicate petals of the woodland star blooming in the wildflower patch.
Gardeners often plant woodland stars to add a touch of elegance to spring gardens.
