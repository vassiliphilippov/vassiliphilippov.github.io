fuckuva lot

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌkəvə lɑt / (AmE), / ˈfʌkəvə lɒt / (BrE)
an extremely large amount; a very considerable quantity of something, used informally and with vulgar emphasis
an extremely large amount (vulgar, informal)
informal vulgar

Examples

It took a fuckuva lot of effort to finish the project on time.
We've spent a fuckuva lot more money than we planned this month.
There's a fuckuva lot of people at the concert tonight.

Synset Examples

That's a fuckuva lot of work for one day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hell of a lot - a very large amount (informal)
  • Synonym: ton - a large amount (informal sense)
  • Antonym: little - small in amount, opposite of very large amount
