cola extract

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊ.lə ɪkˌstrækt / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.lə ɪkˌstrakt / (BrE)
A flavoring substance obtained from the kola nut, used especially in making soft drinks.
flavoring from the kola nut

Examples

The manufacturer uses only natural cola extract in their beverages for a richer taste.
Traditional recipes for cola drinks called for real cola extract as a main ingredient.
Some people are allergic to cola extract found in certain sodas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cola - a carbonated soft drink flavored with an extract from the kola nut or with artificial flavorings

Etymology

From 'cola', referring to the kola nut, and 'extract', meaning a substance drawn out from something.
