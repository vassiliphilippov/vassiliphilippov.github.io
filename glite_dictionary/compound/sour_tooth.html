sour tooth

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaʊɚ tuθ / (AmE), / ˈsaʊə tuːθ / (BrE)
a liking or craving for sour-tasting foods or flavors
preference for sour foods

Examples

When it comes to snacks, his sour tooth means he always picks salt and vinegar chips.
She satisfied her sour tooth with a handful of tart cherries after lunch.
If you have a sour tooth, you'll love this tangy lemonade.

Synset Examples

She has a sour tooth for anything citrus-flavored.
His sour tooth makes him love pickles and lemons.
Whenever I bake, I always satisfy my sour tooth.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sweet tooth - a liking or craving for sweet-tasting foods
