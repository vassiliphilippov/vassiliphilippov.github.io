candied citrus peel

1. NOUN

/ ˈkændid ˈsɪtrəs pil / (AmE), / ˈkændid ˈsɪtrəs piːl / (BrE)
strips of citrus fruit peel preserved by cooking in a sugar syrup until translucent and sweet
peel of citrus fruit cooked in sugar syrup

Examples

She added finely chopped candied citrus peel to the fruitcake batter for extra flavor.
The dessert was garnished with thin strips of candied citrus peel and fresh mint.
Making your own candied citrus peel at home gives a fresher, more vibrant taste than store-bought versions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: candied peel - fruit peel preserved by boiling in sugar
