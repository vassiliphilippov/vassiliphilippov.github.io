legal expert

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːɡəl ˈɛkspɝːt / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl ˈɛkspɜːt / (BrE)
a person who is highly knowledgeable about the law, especially civil law or international law
person highly knowledgeable about the law

Examples

We hired a legal expert to advise us on international trade agreements.
The testimony of a legal expert helped clarify the details of the lawsuit.
As a legal expert, she often appears on television to explain complex cases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jurist - an expert in law, especially a judge or legal scholar
  • Synonym: lawyer - a person who practices or studies law; an attorney or a counselor
