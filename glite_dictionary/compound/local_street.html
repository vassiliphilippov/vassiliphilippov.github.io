local street

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.kəl strit / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl striːt / (BrE)
a street designed mainly for providing direct access to properties along its route, not for through traffic
a street primarily for property access

Examples

Our house is on a quiet local street with little traffic.
The city council plans to repair several local streets damaged by winter weather.
Local residents often use the local street to walk their dogs and greet their neighbors.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: arterial road - a main road for through traffic
