lather, rinse, repeat

1. X

/ ˈlæðər rɪns rɪˈpiːt / (AmE), / ˈlɑːðə rɪns rɪˈpiːt / (BrE)
a set of instructions for washing hair with shampoo: apply (lather) the shampoo, rinse it out, and repeat the process as needed; also used figuratively for describing a routine that is repeated, often mechanically or endlessly.
repeated cycle for washing hair

Examples

Read the instructions: lather, rinse, repeat, and your hair will feel cleaner than ever.
She joked that her daily routine felt just like lather, rinse, repeat—always the same steps, nothing new.
After shampooing, remember to lather, rinse, repeat for best results, especially if your hair is oily.

Synset Examples

I always follow the lather, rinse, repeat method for clean hair.
To ensure my hair is fresh, I lather, rinse, repeat every time.
After applying shampoo, lather, rinse, repeat as needed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wash and repeat - an alternative phrase for repeating a cleaning process
