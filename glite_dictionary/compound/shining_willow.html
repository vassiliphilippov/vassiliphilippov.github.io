shining willow

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃaɪnɪŋ ˈwɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪnɪŋ ˈwɪləʊ / (BrE)
A common North American shrub or small tree with shiny, lance-shaped leaves, belonging to the willow family.
a North American willow with shiny leaves

Examples

The leaves of the shining willow reflect the sunlight beautifully along the riverbank.
Wildlife often use the shining willow for shelter and food in wetland areas.
We planted a shining willow to help stabilize the soil near the pond.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: willow - a tree or shrub with slender leaves and flexible branches
