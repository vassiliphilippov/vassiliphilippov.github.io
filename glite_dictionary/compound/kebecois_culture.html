Kebecois culture

1. NOUN

/ keɪˈbɛk.wɑː ˈkʌl.tʃər / (AmE), / keɪˈbɛk.wɑː ˈkʌl.tʃə / (BrE)
the distinctive cultural practices, traditions, and identity of the Quebec region in Canada, especially reflecting its French-speaking heritage
culture of the Quebec region in Canada

Examples

Many festivals and traditions in Montreal are rooted in Kebecois culture.
Her research focuses on the impact of migration on Kebecois culture.
The cuisine of Kebecois culture features dishes like tourtière and poutine.

Synset Examples

The Kebecois culture is rich and diverse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Québécois - relating to Quebec or its French-speaking inhabitants

Etymology

From French 'Québécois' meaning 'of Quebec' + English 'culture'.
