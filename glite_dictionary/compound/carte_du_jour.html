carte du jour

1. NOUN

/ kɑrt du ˈʒʊr / (AmE), / kɑːt dy ˈʒʊə / (BrE)
a list of dishes available at a restaurant, especially the menu of the day
menu or list of available dishes

Examples

The waiter handed us the carte du jour when we sat down.
Today's carte du jour features several seafood options and a vegetarian dish.
She always enjoys choosing from the carte du jour when she dines at French restaurants.

Synset Examples

the menu was in French

Related Terms

  • Synonym: menu - a list of dishes served at a meal or available at a restaurant

Etymology

From French 'carte du jour' meaning 'card of the day' or 'menu of the day'.
