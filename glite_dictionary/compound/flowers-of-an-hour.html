flowers-of-an-hour

1. NOUN

/ ˌflaʊərz əv ən ˈaʊər / (AmE), / ˌflaʊəz əv ən ˈaʊə / (BrE)
an annual herbaceous plant with short-lived yellow, purple-centered flowers, originally from the Old World tropics and now naturalized as a weed in North America
ephemeral-flowered annual weed

Examples

The flowers-of-an-hour bloomed briefly in the garden before withering by midday.
Many consider flowers-of-an-hour to be a weed, but some appreciate its delicate blossoms.
She recognized the yellow, purple-eyed flowers-of-an-hour growing wild near the path.
