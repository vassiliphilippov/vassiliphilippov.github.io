beam balance

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːm ˌbæləns / (AmE), / ˈbiːm ˌbæləns / (BrE)
a balance with a horizontal lever and two pans, used for measuring the weight of objects by comparing them with standard weights
a type of weighing scale with two pans

Examples

The scientist used a beam balance to determine the mass of the sample accurately.
In the classroom, students practiced weighing objects with the traditional beam balance.
Older pharmacies often relied on a beam balance to prepare precise medication dosages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: balance - an instrument for weighing objects
  • Synonym: scale - a device for measuring weight or mass
