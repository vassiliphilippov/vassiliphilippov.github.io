fumitory family

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuːməˌtɔri ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈfjuːmətəri ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of mainly herbaceous plants, including fumitories and bleeding hearts, native to the Northern Hemisphere and southern Africa
family of herbaceous plants (botany)

Examples

Many species in the fumitory family are known for their delicate, divided leaves.
The fumitory family includes plants like bleeding heart and Dutchman's breeches.
Botanists debated whether the fumitory family should remain distinct from the poppy family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Fumariaceae - the scientific name for the fumitory family
