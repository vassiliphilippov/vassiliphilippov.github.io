National Baseball Hall of Fame

1. PROPN

/ ˈnæʃənl ˈbeɪsbɔl hɔl əv feɪm / (AmE), / ˈnæʃənəl ˈbeɪsbɔːl hɔːl əv feɪm / (BrE)
a museum and Hall of Fame in Cooperstown, New York, dedicated to honoring the greatest baseball players and contributors to the sport
museum honoring famous baseball players

Examples

Every summer, thousands of fans visit the National Baseball Hall of Fame to see memorabilia from the sport’s legends.
He was overjoyed when he learned of his induction into the National Baseball Hall of Fame.
The National Baseball Hall of Fame preserves the history and achievements of the game’s greatest players.
