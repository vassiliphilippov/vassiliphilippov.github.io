mucho experience

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːtʃoʊ ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / ˈmuːtʃəʊ ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
informal: a lot of experience; extensive experience (often used humorously or for emphasis)
a lot of experience (informal)
informal

Examples

She has mucho experience in digital marketing and leads our training sessions.
If you want the job done right, you need someone with mucho experience.
With mucho experience under his belt, he tackled the project confidently.

Synset Examples

She has mucho experience in digital marketing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: a lot of experience - having much experience

Etymology

From Spanish 'mucho' (much, a lot) used in English slang plus 'experience'.
