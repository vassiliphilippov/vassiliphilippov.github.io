Open University

1. PROPN

/ ˈoʊpən juːnɪˌvɝːsəti / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən juːnɪˌvɜːsɪti / (BrE)
a British university offering flexible distance learning to students without formal entry requirements, primarily through correspondence, broadcasting, or online methods.
distance-learning British university
british

Examples

She earned her degree from the Open University after studying part-time for six years.
The Open University allows students to access course materials online from anywhere in the world.
Many adults choose the Open University to balance their studies with work and family life.
