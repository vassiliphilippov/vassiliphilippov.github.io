student union

1. NOUN

/ ˈstuːdənt ˈjuːniən / (AmE), / ˈstjuːd(ə)nt ˈjuːniən / (BrE)
a building on a college or university campus where students gather for social, recreational, and organizational activities
building for students' social and organizational activities

Examples

The university's student union hosts concerts and movie nights every weekend.
Students gathered in the student union to discuss their upcoming charity event.
She spent most of her afternoons studying in the student union café.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: student center - building on a college campus used for student activities
