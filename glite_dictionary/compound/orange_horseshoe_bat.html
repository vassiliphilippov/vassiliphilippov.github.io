orange horseshoe bat

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːrəndʒ ˈhɔːrsˌʃuː bæt / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ˈhɔːsʃuː bæt / (BrE)
a common species of bat found in northwestern Australia, characterized by its orange or yellow fur
Australian bat species with orange or yellow fur

Examples

The orange horseshoe bat is native to the forests of northwestern Australia.
Researchers observed a colony of orange horseshoe bats roosting in a remote cave.
The bright fur of the orange horseshoe bat makes it easy to distinguish from other bat species.
