bitterwood tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪtərˌwʊd tri / (AmE), / ˈbɪtəwʊd triː / (BrE)
any of various trees or shrubs of the family Simaroubaceae with wood and bark that have a bitter taste
tree with bitter wood and bark

Examples

The bitterwood tree is often used in traditional medicine for its unique properties.
Many bitterwood trees are found in tropical regions and are valued for their medicinal bark.
The extract from the bitterwood tree is commonly used as a natural remedy for digestive problems.
