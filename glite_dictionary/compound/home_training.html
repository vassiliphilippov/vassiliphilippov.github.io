home training

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / həʊm ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
Exercise routines or educational activities that are conducted at home, rather than at a gym or institution.
exercise or education at home

Examples

During the lockdown, many people switched to home training to maintain their fitness routines.
Her home training schedule includes yoga, stretching, and strength exercises.
Parents have taken an active role in their children's home training during school closures.

Synset Examples

She prefers home training to stay fit.
Home training has become popular during the pandemic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: home workout - exercise routine performed at home
  • Synonym: training - the process of learning skills or fitness
