Miss Right

1. NOUN

/ mɪs ˈraɪt / (AmE), / mɪs ˈraɪt / (BrE)
a woman regarded as the ideal or perfect partner, especially in a romantic relationship
ideal or perfect female partner

Examples

After years of searching, David finally met his Miss Right at a friend's wedding.
Some people spend their whole lives waiting for Miss Right to come along.
Jess joked that she was not interested in settling down until she found her Miss Right.

Synset Examples

He's convinced that Miss Right is out there waiting for him.
He believed she was his Miss Right from the start.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Mr Right - the ideal or perfect male partner for someone
