eel-shaped

1. ADJ

/ il ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / iːl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having a long, slender, and cylindrical form resembling that of an eel.
long and slender like an eel

Examples

The mysterious fish had an eel-shaped body that slithered through the water.
Some submarines are designed with an eel-shaped hull to move efficiently underwater.
He described the sea creature they saw as eel-shaped and several meters long.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: serpentine - having a shape or movement like a snake
