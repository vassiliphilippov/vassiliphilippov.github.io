performing arts

1. NOUN

/ pərˈfɔrmɪŋ ɑrts / (AmE), / pəˈfɔːmɪŋ ɑːts / (BrE)
forms of creative activity, such as drama, music, and dance, that are performed in front of an audience
art forms like drama, music, dance performed for an audience

Examples

She has always dreamed of working in the performing arts and appearing on stage.
Many universities offer degrees in various performing arts such as dance or theater.
The festival celebrates the rich tradition of performing arts in the region.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: visual arts - art forms primarily visual in nature, such as painting and sculpture
