new leaf

1. NOUN

/ nuː liːf / (AmE), / njuː liːf / (BrE)
a young leaf that is just beginning to grow on a plant or tree
a young, freshly grown leaf

Examples

In spring, the maple produces a new leaf almost every day.
You can tell the plant is healthy by the vibrant green of its new leaf.
Carefully handling the new leaf, she marveled at its delicate texture.

Synset Examples

Each nubbin on the tree indicates where new leaves will sprout.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: young leaf - a recently emerged leaf
