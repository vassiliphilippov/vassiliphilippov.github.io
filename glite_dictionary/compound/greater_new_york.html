Greater New York

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡreɪtər nu ˈjɔrk / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtə njuː ˈjɔːk / (BrE)
the metropolitan area comprising New York City and its surrounding suburbs and regions
the New York City metropolitan area

Examples

The population of Greater New York exceeds 20 million residents.
Many people commute daily from New Jersey and Connecticut into Greater New York for work.
The cultural diversity of Greater New York attracts visitors from around the world.
