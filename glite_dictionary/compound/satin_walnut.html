satin walnut

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæt̬.ɪn ˈwɔl.nət / (AmE), / ˈsæt.ɪn ˈwɔːl.nʌt / (BrE)
a reddish-brown wood from the heartwood of the sweet gum tree, used especially in making furniture
reddish-brown wood from sweet gum tree

Examples

The craftsman chose satin walnut for its rich color and durability when building the cabinet.
A table made from satin walnut adds elegance to any dining room.
She admired the unique grain patterns in the satin walnut flooring.
