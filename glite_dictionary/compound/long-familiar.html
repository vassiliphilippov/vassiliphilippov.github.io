long-familiar

1. ADJ

/ lɔŋ fəˈmɪljər / (AmE), / lɒŋ fəˈmɪliə / (BrE)
Known or experienced for a long time; closely or intimately familiar over an extended period.
known for a long time

Examples

She greeted him with a smile as a long-familiar friend walked through the door.
The house had the long-familiar scent of fresh bread baking in the oven.
A long-familiar melody drifted from the old radio, bringing back childhood memories.

Synset Examples

a long-familiar face
a well-known voice reached her ears

Related Terms

  • Synonym: familiar - well known from long or close association
