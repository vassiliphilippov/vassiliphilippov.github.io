sexual arousal

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl əˈraʊzl̩ / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃu.əl əˈraʊ.zəl / (BrE)
a state of excitement or physiological response that occurs as a result of sexual stimulation or desire
state of sexual excitement or stimulation

Examples

Physical touch can sometimes lead to sexual arousal in both partners.
Certain images or fantasies may trigger sexual arousal in some individuals.
Measures were taken to study the physiological signs of sexual arousal in the experiment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: libido - sexual desire or drive
  • Synonym: sexual excitement - the state of being sexually excited
