plough horse

1. NOUN

/ plaʊ hɔrs / (AmE), / plaʊ hɔːs / (BrE)
A horse that is used for pulling a plough in farming.
a horse used for pulling a plough

Examples

The farmer relied on his sturdy plough horse to prepare the fields each spring.
Before the arrival of tractors, a strong plough horse was essential for rural life.
The old photograph showed a pair of plough horses working side by side.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: draft horse - a large, strong horse bred for heavy work
