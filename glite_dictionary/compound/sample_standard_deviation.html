sample standard deviation

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæmpəl ˌstændərd ˌdiːviˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɑːmpəl ˌstændəd ˌdiːvɪˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the standard deviation calculated from data in a sample, used as an estimate of the population standard deviation in statistics.
standard deviation calculated from a sample

Examples

To calculate the sample standard deviation, follow the formula carefully for all data points.
The sample standard deviation helps estimate how spread out the test scores are.
When comparing groups, a lower sample standard deviation suggests more consistent results.

Synset Examples

The sample standard deviation distribution is a slightly complicated, though well-studied and well-understood, function.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: standard deviation - a measure of the amount of variation or dispersion in a set of values
