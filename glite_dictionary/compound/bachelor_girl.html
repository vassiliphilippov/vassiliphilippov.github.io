bachelor girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈbætʃələr ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈbætʃələ ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a young unmarried woman who lives alone, especially independently of her family
young unmarried woman living alone
archaic

Examples

In the 1920s, being a bachelor girl was seen as a symbol of independence and modernity.
The apartment complex catered to young professionals and several bachelor girls lived there.
She was proud to call herself a bachelor girl, enjoying her career and freedom in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bachelor - an unmarried man, especially one who has never married

Etymology

From 'bachelor' (an unmarried man) + 'girl', influenced by the male term to describe a similar female situation.
