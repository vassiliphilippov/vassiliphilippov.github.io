wire-haired terrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪɚˌhɛrd ˈtɛriɚ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəˌhɛəd ˈtɛriə / (BrE)
a type of terrier dog characterized by a harsh, wiry coat of hair
terrier dog with wiry hair

Examples

The wire-haired terrier has a distinctive rough coat that requires regular grooming.
She adopted a playful wire-haired terrier from the local animal shelter.
Many owners choose a wire-haired terrier for its energetic personality and sturdy build.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrier - small active dog originally bred for hunting
