gay rights

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪ raɪts / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪ raɪts / (BrE)
rights that advocate for the equal treatment and protection of people who identify as gay, lesbian, bisexual, or otherwise LGBTQ+
equal rights for LGBTQ+ people

Examples

Many organizations are dedicated to advancing gay rights around the world.
The debate over gay rights legislation has led to significant political change.
Protests in support of gay rights are held annually in numerous cities.

Synset Examples

She supports gay rights through her art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: LGBT rights - rights of lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender people
