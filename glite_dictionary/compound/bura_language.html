Bura language

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊrɑː ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbʊərə ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in parts of northeastern Nigeria, particularly in Borno and Adamawa states
a Chadic language of northeastern Nigeria

Examples

The Bura language is spoken by communities in northeastern Nigeria.
She studied the structure of the Bura language for her thesis.
Efforts are being made to preserve the Bura language among younger generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chadic language - a branch of the Afro-Asiatic language family
