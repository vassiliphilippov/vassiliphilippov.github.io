dry kiln

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪ ˌkɪln / (AmE), / ˈdraɪ ˌkɪln / (BrE)
A kiln used for drying and seasoning lumber to reduce its moisture content.
kiln for drying lumber

Examples

The lumber was placed in the dry kiln to remove excess moisture.
Modern furniture factories often use a dry kiln for efficient wood processing.
After weeks in the dry kiln, the timber was ready for construction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kiln - a furnace or oven for firing, burning, or drying
