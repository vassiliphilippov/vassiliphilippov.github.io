ice-hockey rink

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪs ˌhɑki rɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈaɪs ˌhɒki rɪŋk / (BrE)
A specially prepared ice rink where ice hockey is played, marked with lines and goals according to the rules of the game.
ice rink for playing ice hockey

Examples

The local team practices at the new ice-hockey rink every week.
Fans crowded around the ice-hockey rink to watch the championship game.
She learned to skate at an ice-hockey rink near her house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice rink - an area of ice for skating
