refrigeration dryer

1. NOUN

/ rɪˌfrɪdʒəˈreɪʃən ˈdraɪər / (AmE), / rɪˌfrɪdʒəˈreɪʃən ˈdraɪə / (BrE)
A machine that removes moisture from compressed air by cooling it to condense and separate water vapor.
device removing moisture from compressed air

Examples

The factory upgraded its system by installing a refrigeration dryer to protect the equipment from moisture damage.
Routine maintenance of the refrigeration dryer ensures optimal performance and extends its lifespan.
A refrigeration dryer is essential in industries where dry compressed air is required for sensitive processes.

Synset Examples

Refrigeration dryers employ two heat exchangers, one for air-to-air and one for air-to-refrigeration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air dryer - a machine that removes moisture from compressed air
