day of the week

1. NOUN

/ deɪ əv ðə wik / (AmE), / deɪ əv ðə wiːk / (BrE)
any one of the seven named days that together make up a week: Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, or Sunday.
one of the seven days in a week

Examples

Please write down your favorite day of the week on this form.
Saturday is usually considered the best day of the week for relaxation.
Every day of the week has its own routine at our school.
