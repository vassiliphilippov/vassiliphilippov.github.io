well-kept

1. ADJ

/ wɛl kɛpt / (AmE), / wɛl kɛpt / (BrE)
(of a place, object, or appearance) maintained in good condition; neat and tidy.
maintained in good condition

Examples

The well-kept garden was a pleasure to walk through on a summer morning.
She rented a well-kept apartment in the city center.
His suit looked well-kept despite his long working hours.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: neglected - not cared for or maintained; in poor condition
  • Synonym: tidy - neat and in order
