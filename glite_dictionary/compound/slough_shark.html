slough shark

1. NOUN

/ sluː ʃɑrk / (AmE), / sluː ʃɑːk / (BrE)
A dangerous or predatory person, especially one who preys on the vulnerable.
dangerous or predatory person
informal

Examples

The old dock was haunted by a known slough shark who took advantage of newcomers.
Rumors spread quickly when another slough shark tried to con tourists out of their money.
She managed to avoid the slough shark thanks to her friend's warning.
