second lieutenant

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛk.ənd luːˈtɛn.ənt / (AmE), / ˌsɛk.ənd lɛfˈtɛn.ənt / (BrE)
a commissioned officer in the Army, Air Force, or Marine Corps holding the lowest rank
lowest commissioned officer rank in armed forces

Examples

After completing his training, he was commissioned as a second lieutenant in the Air Force.
The young second lieutenant led his platoon through the challenging exercise.
She received her promotion to second lieutenant after graduation from the military academy.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: lieutenant - a rank of commissioned officer in the armed forces
  • Degree Higher: first lieutenant - the next rank above second lieutenant in the armed forces
