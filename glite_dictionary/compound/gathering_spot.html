gathering spot

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæðərɪŋ spɑt / (AmE), / ˈɡæð(ə)rɪŋ spɒt / (BrE)
a place where people regularly meet to socialize, talk, or spend time together
place where people meet

Examples

The new café quickly became a popular gathering spot for college students.
Every weekend, the city square serves as a gathering spot for musicians and artists.
After work, employees often head to the lobby, their favorite gathering spot to unwind and chat.

Synset Examples

The thrain was a popular gathering spot for locals.
The park's fountain was a popular gathering spot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meeting place - a place where people meet; synonym for gathering spot
