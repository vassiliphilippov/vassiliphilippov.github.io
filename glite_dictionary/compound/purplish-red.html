purplish-red

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝː.plɪʃ rɛd / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.plɪʃ rɛd / (BrE)
Of a red color that is tinged or shaded with purple.
red tinged with purple

Examples

The flowers were a striking purplish-red that stood out against the green foliage.
She wore a purplish-red scarf that complemented her dress perfectly.
The artist mixed paints to create the exact purplish-red hue needed for the sunset sky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crimson - a rich deep red color inclining to purple
  • Degree Lower: red - the color at the end of the visible spectrum next to orange and opposite violet
  • Degree Higher: purple - a color intermediate between red and blue

2. NOUN

/ ˈpɝː.plɪʃ ˈrɛd / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.plɪʃ ˈred / (BrE)
A red color with a tinge or hint of purple.
red with a hint of purple

Examples

The sunset painted the sky a brilliant purplish-red that lingered on the horizon.
She chose a purplish-red fabric for her evening gown to stand out at the event.
The artist mixed different paints to achieve the perfect purplish-red for the flower petals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crimson - a deep red color with a touch of purple
  • Degree Higher: violet - a bluish-purple color
  • Degree Lower: red - the color at the end of the spectrum next to orange
