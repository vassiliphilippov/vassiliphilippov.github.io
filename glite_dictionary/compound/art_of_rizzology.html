art of rizzology

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt əv ɹɪˈzɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ɑːt əv rɪˈzɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the skill or practice of displaying charm and charisma, especially in the context of dating or romantic pursuits
display of charm in dating
informal slang

Examples

She watched his smooth approach and realized he had truly mastered the art of rizzology.
Podcast hosts often debate the secrets behind the art of rizzology and how it influences dating success.
With a few confident words and a smile, he demonstrated the art of rizzology in action.

Synset Examples

He claims to have mastered the art of rizzology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charisma - compelling attractiveness or charm
  • Synonym: rizz - slang for romantic charisma or charm

Etymology

Blend of 'rizz' (slang for charisma, possibly from 'charisma') + -ology (study of); patterned after 'art of [subject]'.
