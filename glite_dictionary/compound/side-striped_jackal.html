side-striped jackal

1. NOUN

/ saɪd ˈstraɪpt ˈdʒækəl / (AmE), / saɪd ˈstraɪpt ˈdʒækəl / (BrE)
a medium-sized jackal native to southern and central Africa, characterized by a light-colored stripe along each side of its body
African jackal with side stripe

Examples

Researchers observed a side-striped jackal hunting at dusk near the riverbank.
The side-striped jackal can be distinguished from other species by the white stripe on its side.
Farmers sometimes spot side-striped jackals scavenging close to villages in Mozambique.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jackal - a wild dog-like animal of Africa and Asia
