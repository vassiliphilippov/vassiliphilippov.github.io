tie tack

1. NOUN

/ taɪ tæk / (AmE), / taɪ tæk / (BrE)
A small decorative pin used to fasten a necktie to a shirt and keep it in place.
decorative pin for fastening a tie

Examples

He wore a gold tie tack to hold his tie securely during the meeting.
The silver tie tack complemented his elegant suit.
After losing his tie tack, he had trouble keeping his tie straight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tie pin - a pin for fastening a tie, similar to a tie tack
