First of May

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɝst əv ˈmeɪ / (AmE), / ˌfɜːst əv ˈmeɪ / (BrE)
A holiday celebrated on the 1st of May in many countries, marking the arrival of summer or, especially in Russia and some other countries, observed as Labour Day.
May 1st holiday; May Day

Examples

In many countries, people dance around the maypole on the First of May.
Workers in Russia often march in parades to celebrate the First of May.
The tradition of celebrating the First of May dates back centuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: May Day - May 1st; an international holiday celebrating labor or spring
