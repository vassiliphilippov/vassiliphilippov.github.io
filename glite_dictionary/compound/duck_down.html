duck down

1. NOUN

/ dʌk daʊn / (AmE), / dʌk daʊn / (BrE)
the soft, fine feathers from a duck, used especially as a filling for bedding and clothing
soft feathers from a duck, used as filling

Examples

The winter jacket is insulated with duck down to keep you warm in cold weather.
She prefers sleeping under a duck down comforter because it's light and cozy.
High-quality pillows filled with duck down can be very expensive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: down - soft fine feathers from birds, used as a soft filling
