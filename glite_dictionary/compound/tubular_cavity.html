tubular cavity

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuː.bjələr ˈkæv.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈtjuː.bjʊ.lə ˈkæv.ə.ti / (BrE)
a hollow space or cavity in the shape of a tube, often found in biological or anatomical structures
tube-shaped hollow space

Examples

The digestive tract is essentially a tubular cavity running through the body.
Scientists examined the tubular cavity in the root to study fluid transport.
A blockage in a tubular cavity can disrupt normal bodily functions.
