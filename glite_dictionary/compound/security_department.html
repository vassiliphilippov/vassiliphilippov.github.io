security department

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈkjʊrəti dɪˌpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərəti dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
a department within an organization responsible for protecting property and ensuring the safety of workers and assets
department in charge of organization’s security

Examples

The security department implemented new protocols after the recent break-in at the facility.
All visitors must first check in with the security department before entering the premises.
She applied for a job in the security department because of her background in law enforcement.

Synset Examples

the head of security was a former policeman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: security office - office responsible for managing security in an organization
