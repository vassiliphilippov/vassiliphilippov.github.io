dynamite performance

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪnəˌmaɪt pərˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnəˌmaɪt pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
An outstanding or exceptional performance, especially one that impresses an audience greatly.
outstanding or exceptional performance

Examples

The lead actor gave a dynamite performance that captivated the entire audience.
Critics praised the orchestra for their dynamite performance at last night’s concert.
Her dynamite performance in the finals secured her a spot in the national competition.

Synset Examples

Her performance was dynamite, earning her a standing ovation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: show-stopping - performance that attracts a lot of attention and admiration
  • Antonym: mediocre performance - an average or unremarkable performance

Etymology

From 'dynamite' (something powerful or impressive) used figuratively, plus 'performance'.
