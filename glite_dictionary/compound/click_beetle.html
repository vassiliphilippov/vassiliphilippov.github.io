click beetle

1. NOUN

/ klɪk ˈbiːtəl / (AmE), / klɪk ˈbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a beetle known for its ability to flip itself into the air and right itself with a clicking sound when overturned
beetle that flips upright with a click

Examples

A click beetle can right itself by snapping its body and producing a sharp click.
We watched a click beetle flip into the air and land on its feet.
The sudden movement of the click beetle startled the children in the garden.
