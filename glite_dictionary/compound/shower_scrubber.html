shower scrubber

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃaʊɚ ˌskrʌbɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊə ˌskrʌbə / (BrE)
A washing tool, typically made of nylon mesh or similar material, used to wash and exfoliate the body in the shower.
nylon mesh body-washing tool

Examples

I bought a new shower scrubber to help exfoliate my skin.
She hung her shower scrubber on a hook to let it dry after bathing.
Using a shower scrubber can make your soap lather more effectively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loofah - a natural or synthetic sponge used for scrubbing the body
  • Synonym: washcloth - a small cloth for washing the body or face
