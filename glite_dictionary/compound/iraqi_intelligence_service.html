Iraqi Intelligence Service

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈrɑki ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns ˈsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / ɪˈrɑːki ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
the principal intelligence agency of Iraq, known for its involvement in national security and covert operations, especially during the regime of Saddam Hussein
Iraq's principal intelligence agency

Examples

The Iraqi Intelligence Service was widely feared during the 1980s and 1990s for its covert operations.
Many officials of the Iraqi Intelligence Service faced prosecution after the fall of Saddam Hussein's regime.
According to reports, the Iraqi Intelligence Service played a key role in monitoring dissidents.
