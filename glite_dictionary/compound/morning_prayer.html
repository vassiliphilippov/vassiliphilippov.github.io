morning prayer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˈprɛr / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˈpreə / (BrE)
the first canonical hour of prayer, traditionally observed at daybreak in Christian liturgy
first canonical prayer at daybreak

Examples

The monks gathered in the chapel for morning prayer before sunrise.
She attended morning prayer every day as part of her spiritual routine.
At dawn, the community joined together for morning prayer to begin the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: matins - the first canonical hour, a morning church service; synonym for morning prayer in some traditions
