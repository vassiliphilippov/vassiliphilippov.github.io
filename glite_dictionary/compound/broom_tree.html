broom tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈbruːm ˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈbruːm ˌtriː / (BrE)
A prickly yellow-flowered shrub found in the moors of New England and Europe, especially species of the genus Cytisus or Genista.
prickly yellow-flowered shrub of moors

Examples

The broom tree flourishes in dry, rocky soils across the European countryside.
In the spring, the broom tree is covered with bright yellow flowers.
She sheltered beneath a broom tree during her hike on the moor.
