Chinese chestnut

1. NOUN

/ tʃaɪˈniːz ˈtʃɛs.nʌt / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈniːz ˈtʃɛs.nʌt / (BrE)
a species of chestnut tree native to China and Korea, producing small, sweet edible nuts
species of chestnut tree from China and Korea

Examples

The Chinese chestnut is valued for its resistance to blight.
She planted a Chinese chestnut in her backyard for its delicious nuts.
Many orchards are now growing Chinese chestnut trees as an alternative to American chestnut.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chestnut - a deciduous tree with edible nuts; genus Castanea
