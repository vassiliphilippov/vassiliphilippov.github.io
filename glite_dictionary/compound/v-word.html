v-word

1. NOUN

/ ˈviˌwɝd / (AmE), / ˈviːˌwɜːd / (BrE)
a euphemism for a word starting with 'v' that is considered sensitive or taboo, often referring to 'violence' or 'vulgar'
euphemism for a sensitive 'v' word

Examples

The teacher avoided using the v-word during the classroom discussion.
Some parents forbid their children from saying the v-word at home.
He couldn't believe she had said the v-word on live television.

Synset Examples

She was shocked when he casually used the v-word.
He hesitated to use the v-word in front of her.
The v-word is often avoided in casual conversations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: F-word - a euphemism for the taboo word 'fuck'
  • Synonym: N-word - a euphemism for a highly offensive racial slur

Etymology

Formed by analogy with expressions like 'F-word' (for 'fuck'), indicating taboo words by their initial letter plus 'word'.
