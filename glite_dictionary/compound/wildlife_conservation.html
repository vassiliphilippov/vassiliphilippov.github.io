wildlife conservation

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪldˌlaɪf ˌkɑnsərˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldlʌɪf ˌkɒnsəˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the protection and management of wild animal and plant species and their habitats
protection of wild animals and plants

Examples

Many organizations are dedicated to wildlife conservation around the world.
The government increased funding for wildlife conservation projects in endangered areas.
Education plays a vital role in promoting wildlife conservation and public awareness.

Synset Examples

In class, we will discuss Uganda’s wildlife conservation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conservation - the protection of natural resources to prevent depletion or destruction
