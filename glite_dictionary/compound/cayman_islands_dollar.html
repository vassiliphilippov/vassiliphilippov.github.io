Cayman Islands dollar

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪmən ˈaɪləndz ˈdɑlər / (AmE), / ˈkeɪmən ˈaɪləndz ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
the official currency and basic monetary unit of the Cayman Islands
currency of the Cayman Islands

Examples

The price was listed as twenty Cayman Islands dollars for the tour.
She exchanged her US dollars for Cayman Islands dollars at the airport.
All major businesses in Grand Cayman accept payment in Cayman Islands dollars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dollar - basic monetary unit used in several countries, including the US and Australia
