infant death

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnfənt dɛθ / (AmE), / ˈɪnfənt dɛθ / (BrE)
the sudden and unexpected death of an apparently healthy infant, usually during sleep
sudden unexpected death of a healthy infant
medical

Examples

A thorough investigation was launched after the case of infant death in the community.
Doctors are researching possible causes of infant death related to sleep.
Support groups help families cope with the tragedy of infant death.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sudden infant death syndrome - the sudden and unexplained death of an apparently healthy baby
