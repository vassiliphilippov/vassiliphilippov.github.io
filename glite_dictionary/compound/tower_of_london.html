Tower of London

1. PROPN

/ ˈtaʊ.ɚ əv ˈlʌn.dən / (AmE), / ˈtaʊ.ə əv ˈlʌn.dən / (BrE)
A historic fortress on the River Thames in London, formerly used as a royal palace and prison, now a museum housing the crown jewels.
historic fortress and museum in London

Examples

The Tower of London attracts millions of tourists each year to its historic grounds.
The crown jewels are securely kept in the Tower of London.
Anne Boleyn was famously imprisoned and executed at the Tower of London.
