egg-and-tongue

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛɡ ən ˈtʌŋ / (AmE), / ˌɛɡ ən ˈtʌŋ / (BrE)
a decorative molding consisting of a series of egg-shaped figures alternating with another shape, typically a dart or tongue
decorative molding with egg-shaped and tongue-like patterns

Examples

The ceiling was bordered by a beautiful egg-and-tongue molding that added a touch of elegance to the room.
Restorers carefully replicated the original egg-and-tongue design to maintain historical accuracy.
Many classical buildings feature egg-and-tongue motifs on cornices and columns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: egg-and-dart - decorative molding with egg-shaped and dart-like patterns
