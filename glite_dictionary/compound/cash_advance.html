cash advance

1. NOUN

/ kæʃ ədˈvæns / (AmE), / kæʃ ədˈvɑːns / (BrE)
an amount of money paid or borrowed before it is earned, often as a loan or prepayment
money paid or borrowed in advance

Examples

He requested a cash advance from his employer to cover moving expenses.
Some credit cards allow you to get a cash advance at an ATM, though fees may apply.
The company offered a cash advance to contractors before the project started.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advance payment - payment made ahead of being due or earned
