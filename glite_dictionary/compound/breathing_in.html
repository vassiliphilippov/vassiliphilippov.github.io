breathing in

1. NOUN

/ ˈbriðɪŋ ɪn / (AmE), / ˈbriːðɪŋ ɪn / (BrE)
the act of drawing air or other gases into the lungs; inhalation.
the act of inhaling

Examples

Her allergy symptoms improved after practicing deep breathing in through her nose.
The doctor instructed him to focus on slow, controlled breathing in during the exercise.
Yoga emphasizes the importance of mindful breathing in to relax the body and mind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inhalation - the action of breathing in
  • Antonym: breathing out - the act of exhaling or expelling air from the lungs
