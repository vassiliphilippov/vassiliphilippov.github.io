welter of emotions

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛltər əv ɪˈmoʊʃənz / (AmE), / ˈwɛltə əv ɪˈməʊʃənz / (BrE)
a confused or disorderly mass of feelings or emotions
a confused mass of emotions

Examples

After hearing the verdict, he was overwhelmed by a welter of emotions that left him speechless.
Graduation day brought a welter of emotions, from joy to sorrow to nervous anticipation.
Caught in a welter of emotions, she wasn't sure whether to laugh or cry.

Synset Examples

She felt a welter of emotions after the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welter - a confused mass or jumble
