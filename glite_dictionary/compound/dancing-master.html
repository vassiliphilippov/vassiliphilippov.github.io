dancing-master

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæn.sɪŋ ˌmæs.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈdɑːn.sɪŋ ˌmɑː.stə / (BrE)
a professional teacher of dancing, especially in the context of formal or social dances
teacher of formal dancing

Examples

The young ladies attended lessons with a dancing-master every week.
In 18th-century England, a dancing-master was often employed by wealthy families.
He trained under a famous dancing-master before starting his own dance school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dance instructor - a person who teaches dancing
