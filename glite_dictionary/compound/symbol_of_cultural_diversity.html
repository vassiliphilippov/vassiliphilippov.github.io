symbol of cultural diversity

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪm.bəl əv ˈkʌl.tʃɚ.əl daɪˈvɝː.sə.ti / (AmE), / ˈsɪm.bəl əv ˈkʌl.tʃər.əl daɪˈvɜː.sɪ.ti / (BrE)
an object, person, or place that represents or stands for the presence of multiple distinct cultures
representation of varied cultures

Examples

The annual festival has become a symbol of cultural diversity within the city.
Her restaurant serves as a symbol of cultural diversity, bringing together flavors from around the world.
The mosaic in the town square is often hailed as a symbol of cultural diversity by locals.

Synset Examples

Paristan became a symbol of cultural diversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multiculturalism - the presence and support of multiple different cultures within a society
