little emperor

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬l ˈɛmpərər / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ˈɛmpərə / (BrE)
a child who is excessively pampered or spoiled, often acting in a demanding or overbearing way
overly pampered, spoiled child

Examples

The youngest child became a little emperor after his parents indulged his every whim.
In some families, the little emperor syndrome leads to children having little regard for rules.
Teachers sometimes struggle with students who act like a little emperor in the classroom.

Synset Examples

Her son acts like a little emperor at home.
The little emperor always gets his way in the family.
They call him the little emperor for his overbearing attitude.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spoiled brat - a child who behaves badly because they are always given what they want
