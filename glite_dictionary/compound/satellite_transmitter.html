satellite transmitter

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæt̬.əl.aɪt trænzˈmɪt̬.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈsæt.əl.aɪt trænzˈmɪt.ə / (BrE)
a transmitter used on a communications satellite to send signals to Earth or other satellites
transmitter on a satellite

Examples

The weather data was relayed to Earth by the satellite transmitter.
Engineers repaired the malfunctioning satellite transmitter to restore communications.
Each satellite transmitter operates on a specific frequency band to avoid interference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transmitter - an electronic device that sends out radio signals
