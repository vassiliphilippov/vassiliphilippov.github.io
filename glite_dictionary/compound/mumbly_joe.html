mumbly Joe

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌmbli dʒoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmʌmbli ˈdʒəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who speaks unclearly or indistinctly
person who speaks unclearly
informal

Examples

During the conference call, he turned into mumbly Joe and nobody could understand his points.
Don't be a mumbly Joe—try to project your voice in the meeting.
Whenever she gets nervous, she becomes a real mumbly Joe in front of the class.

Synset Examples

He always sounds like mumbly Joe during presentations.
Stop being a mumbly Joe and speak clearly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mumble - to speak in a low, indistinct way
