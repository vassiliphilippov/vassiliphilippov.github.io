lesser kudu

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛsər ˈkuːduː / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˈkuːduː / (BrE)
a small species of African antelope with distinctive white stripes, found in dry woodlands of East Africa
a small East African antelope

Examples

We spotted a lesser kudu grazing quietly under the acacia trees.
The lesser kudu is known for its impressive spiral horns and striking coat patterns.
Unlike its larger relative, the lesser kudu is more elusive and harder to observe in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: greater kudu - a larger species of kudu, an African antelope
