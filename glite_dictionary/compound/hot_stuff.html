hot stuff

1. NOUN

/ hɑt stʌf / (AmE), / hɒt stʌf / (BrE)
something or someone that is very popular, fashionable, or highly admired; regarded as impressive or exciting.
something very popular or impressive
informal

Examples

After her latest album, the singer is considered hot stuff in the music industry.
The new smartphone model is hot stuff among tech enthusiasts this year.
Vintage clothing is hot stuff these days thanks to social media trends.

  • Antonym: out of favor - not popular or fashionable anymore
