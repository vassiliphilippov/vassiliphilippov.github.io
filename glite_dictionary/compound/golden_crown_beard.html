golden crown beard

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən kraʊn bɪrd / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən kraʊn bɪəd / (BrE)
a coarse, greyish-green annual plant with yellow flowers, native to the southwestern United States and Mexico.
yellow-flowered annual herb of southwestern US and Mexico

Examples

The golden crown beard produces bright yellow blooms throughout the summer.
We spotted a field full of golden crown beard near the desert trail.
Gardeners in the Southwest often remove golden crown beard because it spreads quickly.
