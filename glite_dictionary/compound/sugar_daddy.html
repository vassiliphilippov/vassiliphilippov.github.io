sugar daddy

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃuːɡər ˌdædi / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡə ˌdædi / (BrE)
an older, wealthy man who gives money or gifts to a younger person, typically in exchange for companionship or romantic intimacy
wealthy man giving gifts for companionship
informal

Examples

Rumors spread that she was dating a sugar daddy who bought her a new car.
He enjoys the perks of being a sugar daddy, but admits it's not always easy.
Some websites specifically connect sugar daddies with younger partners seeking financial support.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sugar mama - a wealthy older woman who gives gifts to a younger companion

Etymology

Formed by compounding 'sugar' (slang for money or treats) and 'daddy' (term for an older man in a relationship).
