mountain ebony

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntn̩ ˈɛbəni / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntən ˈɛbəni / (BrE)
a small tree native to South and East Asia, with orchid-like pink or white flowers and hard, dark wood
tree with orchid-like flowers native to Asia

Examples

The mountain ebony is prized for its beautiful, orchid-like blooms in spring.
Carpenters often use the hard wood of the mountain ebony for making furniture.
During my trip to India, I saw several mountain ebony trees lining the roadside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bauhinia variegata - scientific name for mountain ebony tree
