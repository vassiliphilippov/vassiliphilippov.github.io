medical aid

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl eɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl eɪd / (BrE)
assistance in the form of professional treatment for illness or injury
professional treatment for illness or injury

Examples

Many refugees rely on international organizations to receive medical aid after fleeing conflict zones.
He sought immediate medical aid for his injured arm following the accident.
The remote village had no access to medical aid during the storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medical care - attention or treatment given by medical professionals
