cordless tool

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrdləs tuːl / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdləs tuːl / (BrE)
A tool powered by a battery, not requiring an electrical cord for operation.
tool powered by battery (not corded)

Examples

He used a cordless tool to assemble the furniture quickly.
The new cordless tool line comes with rechargeable batteries and lightweight designs.
When working outdoors, having a cordless tool makes the job much more convenient.

Synset Examples

I prefer chordless tools for easier handling.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: corded tool - a tool that operates while connected to mains electricity by a cord
