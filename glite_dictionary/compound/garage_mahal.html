Garage Mahal

1. NOUN

/ ɡəˈrɑːʒ məˈhɑl / (AmE), / ɡəˈrɑːʒ məˈhɑːl / (BrE)
an informal term for a garage that is notably large, luxurious, or extravagantly designed, often more elaborate than a typical home garage
extravagantly luxurious garage
informal american

Examples

After the renovations, their Garage Mahal outshone the rest of the house.
He built a Garage Mahal to showcase his collection of vintage motorcycles.
People were amazed by the size and features of their new Garage Mahal.

Synset Examples

The car collection turned the garage into a Garage Mahal.
His new garage is like a Garage Mahal.
They converted the old garage into a real Garage Mahal.

Etymology

Formed by combining 'garage' and 'Taj Mahal', suggesting palatial extravagance.
