team colors

1. NOUN

/ tim ˈkʌlɚz / (AmE), / tiːm ˈkʌləz / (BrE)
the specific set of colors officially designated to represent a particular sports team, often used on uniforms, merchandise, and decorations
official colors representing a sports team

Examples

The stadium was filled with fans proudly wearing their team's team colors.
She decorated her car with flags and banners in the team colors.
During the parade, every building on the street was lit up in team colors.

Synset Examples

The rabid fan painted his entire house in team colors.
