Transportation Security Administration

1. PROPN

/ ˌtrænspɔrˈteɪʃən səˈkjʊrəti ˌædmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtrænspɔːˈteɪʃən sɪˈkjʊərəti ˌædmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
a United States government agency established in 2001 responsible for safeguarding transportation systems, especially air travel security
U.S. agency for transportation security
american

Examples

The Transportation Security Administration introduced stricter screening procedures after 2001.
