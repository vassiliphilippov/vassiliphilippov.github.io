property woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːpɚti ˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈprɒpəti ˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female member of a stage crew who is responsible for managing and preparing props used in a theatrical production
female stage crew member in charge of props

Examples

The property woman quickly arranged the set before the actors arrived on stage.
She started her theater career as a property woman for a local playhouse.
The property woman made sure every prop was in perfect condition before the performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: property man - male member of stage crew in charge of properties
