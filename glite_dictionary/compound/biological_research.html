biological research

1. NOUN

/ baɪəˈlɑdʒɪkəl rɪˈsɜrtʃ / (AmE), / baɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl rɪˈsɜːtʃ / (BrE)
scientific study and investigation in the field of biology, often conducted by biologists to expand knowledge about living organisms
scientific study in the field of biology

Examples

Recent advances in biological research have led to groundbreaking medical treatments.
Many universities invest heavily in biological research to address environmental issues.
The company funds biological research on infectious diseases and vaccine development.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biological science - the science that studies living organisms
  • Synonym: scientific research - the systematic investigation to establish facts
