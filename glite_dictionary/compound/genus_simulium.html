genus Simulium

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs sɪˈmjuːliəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs sɪˈmjuːliəm / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Simuliidae, consisting of blackflies.
type genus of blackflies

Examples

The larvae of genus Simulium are often found in fast-flowing streams.
Scientists study genus Simulium to better understand the spread of certain diseases.
Many species within the genus Simulium are notorious for their biting behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Simuliidae - the family of small biting flies known as blackflies
  • Synonym: blackfly - common name for insects of the family Simuliidae

Etymology

From Latin 'genus' (kind, type) and 'Simulium', a taxonomic name for blackflies.
