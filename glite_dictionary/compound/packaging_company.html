packaging company

1. NOUN

/ ˈpækɪdʒɪŋ ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈpæk.ɪ.dʒɪŋ ˌkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)
a business that packages goods for sale, shipment, or storage on behalf of producers or retailers
business that packages goods

Examples

The packaging company ensured that all items were securely boxed for international shipping.
After switching to a new packaging company, our products arrived in much better condition.
The packaging company offers eco-friendly options for clients concerned about the environment.
