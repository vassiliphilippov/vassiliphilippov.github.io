mathematical approach

1. NOUN

/ məˌθæməˈtɪkəl əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / məˌθætəˈmætɪkəl əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a method or way of analyzing or solving problems using mathematics
way of analyzing using mathematics

Examples

Using a mathematical approach, the engineer quickly found a solution to the complex design problem.
The mathematical approach is often favored in physics for its precision and clarity.
Researchers adopted a mathematical approach to predict the future spread of the disease.

Synset Examples

The mathematical approach solved the problem quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: analytical approach - a method using analysis and logical reasoning
