social dancer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ˈdænsər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˈdɑːnsə / (BrE)
a person who participates in social dance events or gatherings, such as balls or dance parties, often for enjoyment rather than competition
person who attends social dance events

Examples

At the wedding reception, every social dancer was eager to take the floor.
He considers himself a social dancer and attends weekly salsa nights with friends.
Unlike competitive dancers, a social dancer focuses on enjoyment and interaction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dancer - person who dances, especially professionally or habitually
