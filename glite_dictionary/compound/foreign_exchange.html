foreign exchange

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrən ɪksˌtʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈfɒrən ɪksˌtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
the system or process by which one currency is exchanged for another, typically enabling international transactions or trade.
system for exchanging currencies

Examples

The company uses foreign exchange markets to hedge against currency risk.
Travelers often visit banks to obtain foreign exchange before going abroad.
Fluctuations in foreign exchange rates can impact international business profits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: currency exchange - the act or process of exchanging one currency for another
