work clothing

1. NOUN

/ wɝk ˈkloʊðɪŋ / (AmE), / wɜːk ˈkləʊðɪŋ / (BrE)
Clothing designed or worn for performing manual labor, typically durable and practical.
clothes for manual labor

Examples

She changed into her work clothing before heading out to the construction site.
The company provides all employees with protective work clothing for their safety.
His old jeans and shirts are kept as work clothing for chores around the farm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workwear - specialized clothes for work, especially manual or industrial jobs
