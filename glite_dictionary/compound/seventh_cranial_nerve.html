seventh cranial nerve

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛvənθ ˈkreɪniəl nɜrv / (AmE), / ˈsɛvənθ ˈkreɪniəl nɜːv / (BrE)
the cranial nerve that controls the muscles of the face and conveys taste sensations from the front part of the tongue
cranial nerve controlling facial muscles
medical

Examples

Damage to the seventh cranial nerve can cause facial paralysis on one side.
The doctor explained that Bell's palsy affects the seventh cranial nerve.
During surgery, care must be taken not to injure the seventh cranial nerve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: facial nerve - the seventh cranial nerve, innervating facial muscles and some taste functions
