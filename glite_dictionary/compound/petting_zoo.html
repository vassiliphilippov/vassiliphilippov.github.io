petting zoo

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛtɪŋ zu / (AmE), / ˈpɛtɪŋ zuː / (BrE)
a place where children and adults can touch and feed gentle animals, typically as an attraction or educational experience
zoo for petting and feeding animals

Examples

The children spent the afternoon at the petting zoo, feeding goats and sheep.
Our local fair features a petting zoo where you can meet friendly rabbits and llamas.
Visiting the petting zoo was the highlight of her birthday party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zoo - an establishment for keeping and displaying animals to the public
