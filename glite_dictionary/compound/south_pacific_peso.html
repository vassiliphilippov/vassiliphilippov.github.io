South Pacific peso

1. NOUN

/ saʊθ pəˈsɪfɪk ˈpeɪsoʊ / (AmE), / saʊθ pəˈsɪfɪk ˈpeɪsəʊ / (BrE)
the unit of currency formerly used in some parts of the South Pacific region
former currency in the South Pacific

Examples

Travelers were advised to exchange their South Pacific pesos for local currency before leaving the country.
She kept a few old South Pacific pesos as souvenirs from her trip.
Prices in the grocery store were all listed in South Pacific pesos.

Synset Examples

The local market accepts South Pacific pesos only.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peso - the basic monetary unit in several Latin American and Philippine countries
