healthcare delivery

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθˌkɛr dɪˈlɪvəri / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθˌkɛə dɪˈlɪvəri / (BrE)

provision of health care services
medical

Examples

There are major differences in healthcare delivery systems between countries.

Access to rural healthcare delivery remains a challenge for many communities.

Related Terms
