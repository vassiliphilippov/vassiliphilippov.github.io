mock turtle

1. NOUN

/ mɑk ˈtɝtəl / (AmE), / mɒk ˈtɜːtl̩ / (BrE)
a dish resembling turtle soup, made without actual turtle meat
imitation turtle soup dish

Examples

The chef prepared a classic mock turtle as the first course of the banquet.
Many Victorian cookbooks included recipes for mock turtle to substitute for real turtle meat.
She had never tasted mock turtle before, but found the flavor surprisingly rich.

Synset Examples

The recipe for mock turtle is quite elaborate.
He served mock turtle soup at the dinner party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turtle soup - a soup made using turtle meat
