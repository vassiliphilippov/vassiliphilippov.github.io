firing range

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɹɪŋ ˌɹeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪərɪŋ ˌreɪndʒ / (BrE)
an area or facility designated for shooting practice, especially with firearms, at targets
area for shooting practice with firearms

Examples

She visited the firing range to practice her marksmanship skills before the competition.
Safety guidelines are strictly enforced at the firing range to prevent any accidents.
The police recruits spent hours at the firing range learning to handle their weapons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shooting range - a place designated for shooting firearms or archery
