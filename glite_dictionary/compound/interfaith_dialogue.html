interfaith dialogue

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntərˌfeɪθ ˈdaɪəˌlɔɡ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˌfeɪθ ˈdaɪəˌlɒɡ / (BrE)
communication and discussion between members of different religious faiths, often to increase understanding and cooperation
communication between different religions

Examples

The university organized an interfaith dialogue to foster respect among students from diverse backgrounds.
Leaders from various religions participated in an interfaith dialogue addressing common social concerns.
Through regular interfaith dialogue, misunderstandings between the two communities were significantly reduced.

Synset Examples

The Qadiani community hosts events to promote interfaith dialogue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecumenism - principle or aim of promoting unity among the world's Christian churches
