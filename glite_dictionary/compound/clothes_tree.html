clothes tree

1. NOUN

/ kloʊðz tri / (AmE), / kləʊðz triː / (BrE)
a freestanding upright pole with pegs or hooks for hanging clothes, hats, or umbrellas
freestanding pole with hooks for clothes

Examples

She placed her damp coat on the clothes tree by the door.
The hallway was cluttered with hats and scarves hanging from the old clothes tree.
We bought a new clothes tree for the guest room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coat rack - a stand or rack for hanging coats
