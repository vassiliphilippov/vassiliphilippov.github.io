water meter

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌmiːtə / (BrE)
a device that measures the amount of water passing through a particular pipe or outlet
device for measuring water flow

Examples

The city installed a new water meter to monitor usage and detect leaks.
You should check the water meter if you notice an unexpected increase in your bill.
Utility workers read the water meter every month to record your consumption.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meter - device for measuring quantity
