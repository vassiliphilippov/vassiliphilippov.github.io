the great beyond

1. NOUN

/ ðə ɡreɪt biˈjɑnd / (AmE), / ðə ɡreɪt bɪˈjɒnd / (BrE)
the afterlife or an existence beyond death; an unknown realm people imagine after life ends
afterlife or unknown existence after death

Examples

Many cultures have legends and beliefs about the great beyond and what awaits us after death.
Philosophers and poets have long speculated about the mysteries of the great beyond.
After losing her grandmother, she found comfort in thinking of her at peace in the great beyond.

Synset Examples

She believes in life in the great beyond.
He often wonders about the great beyond after death.
The great beyond is a mystery for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: afterlife - existence after physical death
  • Synonym: hereafter - life after death; what comes after this life
