world of dreams

1. NOUN

/ wɝːld əv driːmz / (AmE), / wɜːld əv driːmz / (BrE)
a state or realm of fantasy or imagination, not connected to reality
a realm of fantasy or imagination

Examples

She often escapes into a world of dreams when life gets tough.
Writers sometimes draw inspiration from their own world of dreams.
Children can spend hours playing in their own world of dreams.

Synset Examples

She lives in a world of dreams and imagination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fantasy - the faculty or activity of imagining impossible or improbable things
  • Antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
