to enhance safety

1. VERB

/ ɛnˈhæns ˈseɪfti / (AmE), / ɪnˈhɑːns ˈseɪfti / (BrE)
to improve protection or reduce risk of harm
improve protection or reduce danger

Examples

The company installed new cameras to enhance safety in the parking lot.
Speed bumps were added to enhance safety for pedestrians near the school.
Regular maintenance checks help enhance safety in the workplace.

Synset Examples

They installed a guardrail to enhance safety along the highway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: improve safety - make a place or activity safer
  • Synonym: reduce risk - make something less dangerous or risky
