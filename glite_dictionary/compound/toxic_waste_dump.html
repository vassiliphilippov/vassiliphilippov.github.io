toxic waste dump

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑksɪk weɪst dʌmp / (AmE), / ˈtɒksɪk weɪst dʌmp / (BrE)
a place where toxic waste is disposed of, often illegally or unsafely
site for toxic waste disposal

Examples

The government closed the old toxic waste dump due to health concerns in the nearby community.
Residents protested when they discovered a new toxic waste dump was being built near their homes.
Investigators found evidence of illegal chemicals at an abandoned toxic waste dump.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: landfill - a site for the disposal of waste by burial
  • Antonym: recycling center - a facility for processing recyclable waste
