facial pain

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪʃəl peɪn / (AmE), / ˈfeɪʃəl peɪn / (BrE)
pain experienced in the face, often due to nerve disorders, injury, or infection
pain felt in the face
medical

Examples

She visited the doctor because of persistent facial pain near her jaw.
Trigeminal neuralgia is a condition that often causes severe facial pain.
The dentist asked if the patient had experienced any facial pain recently.

Synset Examples

He suffers from neuralgia that causes sharp facial pain.
