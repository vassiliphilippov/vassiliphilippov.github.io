wethead look

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛtˌhɛd lʊk / (AmE), / ˈwɛthɛd lʊk / (BrE)
A hairstyle that appears wet and messy, often as if just emerged from water.
wet and messy hairstyle

Examples

After the rain, his wethead look made everyone laugh at the office.
The stylist recommended some gel to achieve that natural wethead look.
She sported a deliberate wethead look at the music festival.

Synset Examples

He laughed at his wethead look after jumping into the pool.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedhead - hair that is tousled and messy from sleep
