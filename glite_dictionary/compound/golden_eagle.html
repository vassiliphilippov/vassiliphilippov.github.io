golden eagle

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈiːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈiːɡl / (BrE)
a large bird of prey with a golden-brown head and neck, found in mountains of the Northern Hemisphere
large bird of prey with golden-brown head

Examples

The golden eagle is known for its impressive wingspan and hunting skills.
We spotted a golden eagle soaring above the mountain peaks.
Conservation efforts have helped the golden eagle population recover in some areas.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eagle - large bird of prey of the family Accipitridae
