Santa Catalina Island

1. PROPN

/ ˌsæntə kəˈtælɪnə ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / ˌsæntə kəˈtælɪnə ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
an island in the Pacific Ocean off the southwestern coast of California, known for its resorts and natural scenery
island off California, popular resort

Examples

We spent our vacation exploring Santa Catalina Island and relaxing on its beaches.
Many tourists take a ferry from Los Angeles to visit Santa Catalina Island each year.
The clear waters around Santa Catalina Island are perfect for snorkeling and kayaking.

Etymology

Spanish, named after Saint Catherine (Catalina in Spanish).
