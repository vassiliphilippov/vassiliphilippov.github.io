on-the-job

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə dʒɑb / (AmE), / ɒn ðə dʒɒb / (BrE)
relating to or occurring during someone's employment or work activity, especially as part of the normal process at a workplace
relating to or during employment

Examples

The company offers on-the-job training for all new employees.
He gained valuable experience through on-the-job learning rather than classroom instruction.
Many skills are best acquired on-the-job, not from textbooks.
