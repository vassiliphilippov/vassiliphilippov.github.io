war baby

1. NOUN

/ wɔr ˈbeɪ.bi / (AmE), / wɔː ˈbeɪ.bi / (BrE)
A child conceived or born during a war, often referring to those born as a result of wartime relationships or circumstances.
child born during a war

Examples

After the conflict ended, many war babies were raised in orphanages across Europe.
She discovered that her grandfather was a war baby who grew up not knowing his father.
The documentary tells the story of war babies and their search for identity.
