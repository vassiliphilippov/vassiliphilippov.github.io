television show

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌvɪʒən ʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən ʃəʊ / (BrE)
A program that is broadcast on television, typically as part of a series or schedule.
program broadcast on television

Examples

The new television show became an instant hit with audiences around the world.
Every Monday night, my family gathers to watch our favorite television show.
Streaming services now produce more original television shows than traditional broadcasters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: TV program - a synonym for a program broadcast on television
  • Antonym: radio show - a program broadcast on radio instead of television
