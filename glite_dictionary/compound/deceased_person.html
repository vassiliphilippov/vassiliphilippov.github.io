deceased person

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈsist ˈpɜrsən / (AmE), / dɪˈsiːst ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who has died; someone no longer living.
a person who has died

Examples

The will was read in front of the family of the deceased person.
Identification of the deceased person was confirmed by dental records.
During the memorial service, many people spoke in honor of the deceased person.

Synset Examples

I wonder what the dead person would have done

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dead person - a person who is no longer alive

Etymology

From Latin 'decedere' (to die) via Old French 'deces'.
