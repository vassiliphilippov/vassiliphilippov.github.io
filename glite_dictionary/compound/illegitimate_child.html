illegitimate child

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛdʒɪˈtɪmət tʃaɪld / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛdʒɪˈtɪmət tʃaɪld / (BrE)
A child born to parents who are not legally married to each other.
child born out of wedlock

Examples

For centuries, an illegitimate child often faced social and legal disadvantages.
The inheritance laws were unclear for an illegitimate child in the 18th century.
He learned later in life that he was the illegitimate child of a nobleman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bastard - an offensive or archaic synonym for an illegitimate child
