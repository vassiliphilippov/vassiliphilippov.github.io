product launch

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑdʌkt lɔntʃ / (AmE), / ˈprɒdʌkt lɔːntʃ / (BrE)
the act or event of introducing a new product to the market, often with planned promotional activities
introduction of a new product to market

Examples

The company organized a spectacular product launch for their innovative smartphone.
A successful product launch can significantly boost a brand's reputation and sales.
We are working overtime to prepare for next month's product launch.

Synset Examples
