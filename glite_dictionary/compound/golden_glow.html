golden glow

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ɡloʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ɡləʊ / (BrE)
A tall, branching herbaceous plant with large, double yellow flower heads, commonly grown as an ornamental garden flower.
tall ornamental plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The golden glow in her garden reached over six feet tall by midsummer.
A cluster of golden glow brightened the edge of the fence with vibrant yellow blooms.
He planted golden glow beside the porch to attract butterflies and bees.
