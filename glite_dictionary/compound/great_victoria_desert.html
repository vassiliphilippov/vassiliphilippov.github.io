Great Victoria Desert

1. PROPN

/ ɡreɪt vɪkˈtɔriə ˈdɛzɚt / (AmE), / ɡreɪt vɪkˈtɔːriə ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
a large desert region in south central Australia, north of the Nullarbor Plain
large desert in south central Australia

Examples

The Great Victoria Desert covers an extensive area of Western and South Australia.
Scientists study the unique flora and fauna found in the Great Victoria Desert.
Travelers must prepare carefully before crossing the harsh environment of the Great Victoria Desert.

Etymology

Named after Queen Victoria of the United Kingdom.
