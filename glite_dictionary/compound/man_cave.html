man cave

1. NOUN

/ mæn keɪv / (AmE), / man keɪv / (BrE)
a room or space in a home set aside for a man to relax and enjoy his hobbies, typically decorated and equipped according to his personal tastes
a male's private relaxation space at home

Examples

He proudly showed off his newly decorated man cave to his friends.
The basement became his man cave, complete with a bar and pool table.
On Sundays, he escapes to his man cave to watch football and relax.

Synset Examples

They converted the garage into a cozy man cave.
His man cave is equipped with a huge TV and snacks.
He spent the weekend in his man cave watching sports.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: den - a comfortable room for relaxation, often used by one person
  • Antonym: she shed - a private retreat space for women, comparable to a man cave
