scaffy jacket

1. NOUN

/ ˈskæfi ˈdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈskæfi ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
A jacket that is shabby, untidy, or unkempt in appearance.
shabby or untidy jacket
informal

Examples

He tossed his scaffy jacket onto the back of the chair.

The student showed up to class in a scaffy jacket and worn-out jeans.
