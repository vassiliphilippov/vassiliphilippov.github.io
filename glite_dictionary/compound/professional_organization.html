professional organization

1. NOUN

/ prəˈfɛʃənəl ˌɔrɡəˌnaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃənəl ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an organization comprised of members of a particular profession, formed to support their interests, set standards, and promote professional development
association of members in a profession

Examples

Joining a professional organization can help you network and stay updated on industry trends.
Many professional organizations offer certifications to help members advance their careers.
The annual conference is organized by the national professional organization for engineers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trade association - an association formed for people in a particular trade or industry
