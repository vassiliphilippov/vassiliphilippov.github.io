free throw lane

1. NOUN

/ friː θroʊ leɪn / (AmE), / friː θrəʊ leɪn / (BrE)
the marked rectangular area on a basketball court extending from the end line to a point 15 feet in front of the backboard, within which specific rules apply during a free throw
rectangular area on basketball court for free throws

Examples

No defender is allowed in the free throw lane during the foul shot.
The coach instructed the players to stay outside the free throw lane until the ball was released.
He stepped into the free throw lane too early and was called for a violation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: key - the rectangular area around the basket in basketball
  • Synonym: paint - the area near the basket in basketball, typically the free throw lane, often called 'the paint' because it is painted
