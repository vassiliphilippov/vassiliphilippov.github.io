Romansh language

1. NOUN

/ roʊˈmænʃ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / rəʊˈmanʃ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Rhaeto-Romance language spoken in southeastern Switzerland, serving as one of the official languages of Switzerland
a language spoken in southeastern Switzerland

Examples

The Swiss government publishes documents in Romansh language alongside German, French, and Italian.
She grew up speaking Romansh language at home in the canton of Grisons.
Efforts are being made to preserve the Romansh language among younger generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rhaeto-Romance - a branch of Romance languages in the Alpine region

Etymology

From Romansh 'rumantsch', meaning 'Romanic, Romance', reflecting its Latin origins.
