school paper

1. NOUN

/ skuːl ˈpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / skuːl ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
a newspaper produced and published by students in a school, often reporting on school events and student interests
student-produced school newspaper

Examples

She wrote an article about the chess club for the school paper this semester.
The school paper is distributed to all students every Friday morning.
As editor of the school paper, he encouraged student reporters to cover diverse topics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: school newspaper - a newspaper published by students in a school
