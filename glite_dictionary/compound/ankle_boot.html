ankle boot

1. NOUN

/ ˈæŋ.kəl buːt / (AmE), / ˈæŋ.kl̩ buːt / (BrE)
A boot that reaches to or just above the ankle.
a boot covering the ankle

Examples

She wore a stylish pair of ankle boots with her dress yesterday.
These ankle boots are perfect for autumn weather.
He prefers ankle boots over taller boots for everyday wear.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: boot - a sturdy item of footwear covering the foot and ankle and sometimes the lower leg
  • Degree Higher: knee-high boot - a boot that reaches up to the knee
