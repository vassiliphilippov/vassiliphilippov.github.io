Miami Beach

1. PROPN

/ maɪˈæmi ˈbiːtʃ / (AmE), / maɪˈæmi ˈbiːtʃ / (BrE)
a city in southeastern Florida, located on an island between Biscayne Bay and the Atlantic Ocean, noted for its beaches and resort hotels
fashionable city in southeastern Florida

Examples

We spent our vacation at Miami Beach, enjoying the vibrant nightlife and sandy shores.
Miami Beach is famous for its Art Deco Historic District and beautiful ocean views.
Real estate prices in Miami Beach have soared due to its popularity among tourists and residents alike.
