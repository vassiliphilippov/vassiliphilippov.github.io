petroleum refinery

1. NOUN

/ pəˈtroʊliəm rɪˈfaɪnəri / (AmE), / pəˈtrəʊliəm rɪˈfaɪnəri / (BrE)
an industrial facility where crude oil is processed and converted into useful products such as gasoline, diesel, and other fuels
facility for processing crude oil

Examples

The new petroleum refinery will create hundreds of jobs in the region.
After the explosion at the petroleum refinery, several neighborhoods were evacuated.
Crude oil is transported by pipeline to the petroleum refinery for processing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refinery - industrial plant for purifying a crude substance
