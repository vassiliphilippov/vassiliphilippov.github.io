silverbell tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚˌbɛl tri / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəbɛl triː / (BrE)
A medium-sized deciduous tree native to the southeastern United States, known for its bell-shaped white flowers.
tree with bell-shaped flowers, native to southeastern US

Examples

The silverbell tree blooms in early spring, adding a delicate beauty to southern forests.
Gardeners admire the silverbell tree for its graceful, bell-shaped flowers.
A single silverbell tree can transform a backyard with its elegant white blossoms.
