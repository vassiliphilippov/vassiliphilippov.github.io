heavy coat

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛvi koʊt / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi kəʊt / (BrE)
a warm, thick coat worn to provide protection against cold weather
warm, thick coat for cold weather

Examples

She put on her heavy coat to go outside in the snow.
A heavy coat is essential for surviving harsh winters in the mountains.
He forgot his heavy coat and shivered all the way home.

Synset Examples

She wore a heavy coat during the winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overcoat - a long, warm coat worn over other clothes in cold weather
  • Degree Lower: jacket - a short coat, usually lighter and less warm than a heavy coat
