to greet someone warmly

1. VERB

/ ɡrit ˈsʌmwʌn ˈwɔrmli / (AmE), / ɡriːt ˈsʌmwʌn ˈwɔːmli / (BrE)
to welcome someone in a friendly and enthusiastic manner
welcome someone in a friendly way

Examples

Whenever I visit my grandparents, they always greet me warmly at the door.
He made sure to greet every guest warmly as they arrived at the party.
The teacher greeted her students warmly on the first day of school.

Synset Examples

The storekeeper greeted every customer warmly.
The old fellow at the café always greets me warmly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welcome - to greet someone in a friendly way
  • Antonym: greet someone coldly - to welcome someone in an unfriendly or distant manner
