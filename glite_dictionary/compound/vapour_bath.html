vapour bath

1. NOUN

/ ˈveɪpɚ bæθ / (AmE), / ˈveɪpə bɑːθ / (BrE)
A bath in which the body is exposed to steam, typically to induce sweating for cleansing or therapeutic effects.
bath in steam for cleansing or therapy

Examples

After her workout, she relaxed in a vapour bath to soothe her muscles.
Many spas offer a vapour bath as part of their wellness treatments.
He found the vapour bath rejuvenating, followed by a quick plunge in the cold pool.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steam bath - A bath in which the body is exposed to steam

Etymology

From Latin 'vapor' (steam) and 'bath'.
