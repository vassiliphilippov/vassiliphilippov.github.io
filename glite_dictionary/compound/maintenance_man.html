maintenance man

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪn.tənəns mæn / (AmE), / ˈmeɪn.tənəns mæn / (BrE)
a man employed to repair and maintain equipment, buildings, or machinery
male worker who does repairs and maintenance

Examples

After the storm, the maintenance man fixed the broken windows in the office.
If you need anything repaired, call the maintenance man at reception.
The apartment complex employs a maintenance man to handle daily repairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maintenance worker - a person employed to repair and maintain equipment or buildings
  • Antonym: maintenance woman - a female maintenance worker
