cooking utensil

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊkɪŋ juːˈtɛnsl̩ / (AmE), / ˈkʊkɪŋ juːˈtɛnsl̩ / (BrE)
a tool used in the kitchen for preparing, cooking, or serving food, typically made from heat-resistant materials
tool used in preparing or cooking food

Examples

She bought a new set of cooking utensils including spatulas and ladles.
The cooking utensil was made of stainless steel to withstand high heat.
