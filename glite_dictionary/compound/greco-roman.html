Greco-Roman

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡriːkoʊ ˈroʊmən / (AmE), / ˌɡrɛkəʊ ˈrəʊmən / (BrE)
Relating to both ancient Greece and Rome, especially their art, literature, or culture.
Ancient Greek and Roman cultural

Examples

The museum displays an impressive collection of Greco-Roman sculptures.
Many Western architectural styles are inspired by Greco-Roman principles.
She wrote her thesis on Greco-Roman mythology and its influence on literature.

Synset Examples

classical mythology
the classical world

Etymology

From Greek 'Graikos' (Greek) and Latin 'Romanus' (Roman).
