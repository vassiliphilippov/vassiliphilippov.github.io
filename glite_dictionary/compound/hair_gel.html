hair gel

1. NOUN

/ hɛr dʒɛl / (AmE), / heə dʒɛl / (BrE)
a jellylike substance applied to the hair to help with styling and hold
jellylike substance for styling hair

Examples

He applied some hair gel to keep his hairstyle in place all day.
Many people use hair gel to create spiky or slicked-back looks.
She forgot her hair gel at home and struggled with frizzy hair during the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair mousse - foam product for styling hair
  • Synonym: hairspray - liquid spray for holding hair in place
