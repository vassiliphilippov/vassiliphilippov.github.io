sweet orange tree

1. NOUN

/ swit ˈɔrɪndʒ ˌtri / (AmE), / swiːt ˈɒrɪndʒ ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tree (Citrus × sinensis), probably native to southern China, widely cultivated for its edible sweet oranges and juice.
tree cultivated for sweet oranges

Examples

The sweet orange tree thrives in warm, subtropical climates with plenty of sunlight.
Orchards full of sweet orange trees line the countryside in that region.
Farmers prune each sweet orange tree to ensure a healthy crop of fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orange tree - tree producing oranges
