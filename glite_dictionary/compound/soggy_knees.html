soggy knees

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑɡi niz / (AmE), / ˈsɒɡi niːz / (BrE)
Knees that are wet and uncomfortable, typically from rain, mud, or wet clothing.
wet and uncomfortable knees

Examples

After sitting on the wet bench, I stood up with soggy knees and a frown.
The soccer players came off the muddy field, all complaining of their soggy knees.
A rainstorm caught us by surprise, leaving me with soggy knees for the rest of the hike.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soggy - wet and soft, usually unpleasantly so
