attack aircraft

1. NOUN

/ əˈtæk ˈɛr.kræft / (AmE), / əˈtæk ˈeə.krɑːft / (BrE)
a fast military airplane designed specifically to attack enemy targets, often at low altitude
military airplane for attacking targets

Examples

The military deployed several attack aircraft to neutralize enemy positions near the border.
Modern attack aircraft are equipped with advanced targeting systems for precision strikes.
During the operation, an attack aircraft provided close air support to the ground troops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fighter - a military aircraft designed primarily for air-to-air combat against other aircraft
  • Degree Higher: bomber - a military aircraft designed to drop bombs on ground targets
