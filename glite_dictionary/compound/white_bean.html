white bean

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈbin / (AmE), / waɪt ˈbiːn / (BrE)
A type of edible bean with white seeds, often dried, commonly used in cooking.
a type of dried white bean

Examples

The soup was made with white bean and fresh vegetables.
You can substitute white bean for chickpeas in this recipe.
Dried white bean is a staple in many Mediterranean dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: navy bean - a small, white, dried bean, commonly used in baked beans
  • Antonym: kidney bean - a type of bean with a red color and kidney shape
