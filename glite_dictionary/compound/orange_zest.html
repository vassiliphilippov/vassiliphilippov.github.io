orange zest

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ zɛst / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ zest / (BrE)
finely grated or thinly shaved outer skin of an orange, used especially as flavoring in cooking and baking
finely grated orange peel for flavoring

Examples

You only need a teaspoon of orange zest to brighten the flavor of this cake.
The recipe calls for freshly grated orange zest and juice.
She sprinkled some orange zest over the salad for a burst of citrus aroma.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemon zest - finely grated outer skin of a lemon used for flavoring
