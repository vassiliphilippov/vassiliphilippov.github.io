open discussion

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊpən dɪˈskʌʃən / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən dɪˈskʌʃən / (BrE)
a conversation or debate in which people express their ideas and opinions honestly and without restraint
frank and honest conversation

Examples

The school encouraged open discussion about difficult topics among students.
Having an open discussion with your team can help resolve misunderstandings quickly.
We hope this forum will be a place for open discussion and the sharing of new ideas.

Synset Examples

Transgenderism is a complex topic that requires open discussion.
His moralism makes it hard to have open discussions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frank discussion - an open and honest conversation
