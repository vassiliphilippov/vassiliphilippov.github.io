straight shooter

1. NOUN

/ streɪt ˈʃuːtɚ / (AmE), / streɪt ˈʃuːtə / (BrE)
a person who is honest and direct in speech or behavior; someone who tells the truth without evasion or deceit
an honest, direct person

Examples

People trust him because he's a straight shooter who always tells it like it is.
As a straight shooter, she won the respect of her colleagues.
You can count on Jane—she's a straight shooter and never hides the truth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honest person - a person who is truthful and does not lie
  • Antonym: liar - a person who does not tell the truth
