chainlink fence

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃeɪnˌlɪŋk ˌfɛns / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪnlɪŋk ˌfɛns / (BrE)
A fence made of interwoven steel wires forming a diamond-shaped mesh pattern, used for enclosing areas such as yards or sports fields.
fence made of interwoven steel wires

Examples

The school playground is surrounded by a chainlink fence for safety.
He peeked through the chainlink fence to watch the baseball game.
A new chainlink fence was installed to keep the dogs from escaping.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: wire fence - a fence made of strands of wire; more general than chainlink fence
