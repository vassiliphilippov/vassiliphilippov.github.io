wan expression

1. NOUN

/ wɑn ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / wɒn ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
a look on a person's face that appears pale, weak, or sickly, often suggesting illness or exhaustion
pale or sickly facial look

Examples

She had a wan expression after being ill for several days.
His wan expression worried his friends, who urged him to see a doctor.
The stress of work gave him a constantly wan expression throughout the week.

Synset Examples

His wan expression showed he hadn't slept well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pallor - an unhealthy pale appearance, especially of the face

Etymology

From 'wan' (Old English *wann*, meaning pale or faded) + 'expression'.
