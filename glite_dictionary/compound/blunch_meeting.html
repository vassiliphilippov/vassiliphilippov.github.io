blunch meeting

1. NOUN

/ blʌntʃ ˈmiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / blʌntʃ ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a meeting that combines the time or meal of brunch and lunch, often involving eating and discussion
meeting held over brunch-lunch
informal

Examples

We're organizing a blunch meeting this Saturday to brainstorm ideas for the project.
The manager scheduled the blunch meeting so everyone could join after a late morning.
Our monthly blunch meeting usually features light food and productive discussions.

Synset Examples

We had a blunch meeting to discuss weekend plans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brunch - a meal eaten between breakfast and lunch
  • Synonym: lunch meeting - a meeting held over lunch

Etymology

Blend of 'brunch' (breakfast + lunch) and 'lunch', with 'meeting'.
