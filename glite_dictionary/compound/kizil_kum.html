Kizil Kum

1. PROPN

/ kɪˌzɪl ˈkuːm / (AmE), / kɪˌzɪl ˈkuːm / (BrE)
a large desert in Uzbekistan and Kazakhstan, located southeast of the Aral Sea
desert in Uzbekistan and Kazakhstan

Examples

The Kizil Kum is one of the largest deserts in Central Asia.
Scientists have studied ancient fossils found in the sands of the Kizil Kum.
Nomadic herders traverse the vast expanses of the Kizil Kum with their livestock.

Etymology

From Turkic languages, meaning 'red sand'.
