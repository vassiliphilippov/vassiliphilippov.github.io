juvenile detention

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒuːvənl̩ dɪˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˌdʒuːvɪnaɪl dɪˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
a secure facility where young offenders are held in custody, typically as a form of correction or punishment
youth correctional facility

Examples

After the trial, the judge ordered him to serve time in juvenile detention.
She spent several months in juvenile detention for vandalism.
Many advocates are working to reform the juvenile detention system.

Synset Examples

He was sent to juvie for petty theft.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: juvenile detention center - a correctional center specifically for young offenders
  • Synonym: juvenile hall - a U.S. term for a facility where young offenders are held
  • Degree Higher: prison - a place where people are legally held as punishment for crimes
