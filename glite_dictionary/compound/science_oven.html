science oven

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪəns ˌʌvən / (AmE), / ˈsaɪəns ˌʌvən / (BrE)
an oven specially designed for conducting scientific experiments, typically used in laboratories to heat or process materials under controlled conditions
laboratory oven for experiments

Examples

The new science oven allows researchers to heat samples precisely and evenly.
She placed the petri dishes inside the science oven to dry them before analysis.
Many chemistry labs require a science oven for controlled heating processes.

Synset Examples

The new science oven heats materials evenly for experiments.
