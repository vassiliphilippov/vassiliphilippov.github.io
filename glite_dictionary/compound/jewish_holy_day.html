Jewish holy day

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːɪʃ ˈhoʊli deɪ / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːɪʃ ˈhəʊli deɪ / (BrE)
a religious holiday observed in the Jewish tradition, such as Passover, Yom Kippur, or Hanukkah
religious holiday in Judaism

Examples

Yom Kippur is considered the most solemn Jewish holy day of the year.
Many businesses close early in honor of a Jewish holy day.
Families often gather to celebrate a Jewish holy day with traditional meals and rituals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jewish holiday - a holiday celebrated by Jews; synonym of Jewish holy day
