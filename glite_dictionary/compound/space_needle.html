Space Needle

1. PROPN

/ ˈspeɪs ˌniːdəl / (AmE), / ˈspeɪs ˌniːdəl / (BrE)
A distinctive observation tower 605 feet (184 meters) tall, located in Seattle, Washington, and serving as a major tourist attraction.
tall observation tower in Seattle

Examples

The Space Needle offers breathtaking views of Seattle and its surroundings.
Many tourists visit the Space Needle during their trips to the city.
The Space Needle is illuminated with colorful lights at night for special occasions.
