intense competition

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtɛns kəmˌpɛtɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛns kəmˈpɛtɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a situation in which rivalry between individuals or groups is very strong, difficult, and demanding
very strong rivalry or contest

Examples

This year's job market has seen particularly intense competition among candidates.
The team thrived under intense competition and ultimately won the championship.
Colleges are experiencing intense competition for the best students and faculty.

Synset Examples

The competition was intense this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fierce competition - competition that is very aggressive or strong
  • Synonym: stiff competition - competition that is hard and unyielding
  • Degree Lower: mild competition - competition that is not very strong or demanding
