dotted gayfeather

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑt̬ɪd ˈɡeɪˌfɛðər / (AmE), / ˈdɒtɪd ˈɡeɪˌfɛðə / (BrE)
A North American herbaceous plant with many stems topped by dense spikes of rose-lavender flowers, native to central United States and Canada through Texas and northern Mexico.
North American plant with wands of rose-lavender flowers

Examples

The prairie was filled with blooming dotted gayfeather in late summer.
Gardeners often plant dotted gayfeather to attract butterflies and bees.
I recognized the dotted gayfeather by its slender, crowded spikes of lavender flowers.
