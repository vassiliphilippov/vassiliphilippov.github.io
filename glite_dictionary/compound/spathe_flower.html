spathe flower

1. NOUN

/ speɪθ ˈflaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / speɪθ ˈflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Spathiphyllum, often cultivated for its decorative white or green spathe and fragrant flowers
ornamental plant with a white or green spathe

Examples

The spathe flower is admired for its elegant white blooms and air-purifying qualities.
She placed a pot of spathe flowers in the living room to brighten the space.
Many gardeners recommend the spathe flower for beginners because it is easy to care for.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peace lily - another common name for Spathiphyllum, a popular houseplant

Etymology

From 'spathe', a botanical term for a leaf-like bract, and 'flower'.
