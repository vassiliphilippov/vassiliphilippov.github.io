Jesus phone

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːzəs foʊn / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːzəs fəʊn / (BrE)
(slang) A smartphone, especially an iPhone, regarded as having special status, spiritual significance, or being highly desirable.
slang for a highly revered smartphone, esp. iPhone
slang

Examples

He waited in line all night to buy the latest Jesus phone.
The new Jesus phone sold out within hours of its release.
Many reviewers called the original iPhone the first true Jesus phone of its generation.

Synset Examples

She called her mom on her Jesus phone for advice.
The Jesus phone app helps him stay spiritually connected.
He believes the Jesus phone connects him to higher guidance.

Etymology

Formed from 'Jesus' (used humorously or reverently) and 'phone' (short for telephone), popularized as a nickname for the original iPhone (2007).
