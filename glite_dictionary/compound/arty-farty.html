arty-farty

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑːrti ˈfɑːrti / (AmE), / ˌɑːti ˈfɑːti / (BrE)
Pretentiously artistic or overly concerned with artistic style or quality, often to an exaggerated or insincere degree.
pretentiously artistic
informal

Examples

His explanation of the painting was too arty-farty for my taste.
The café had an arty-farty decor with abstract paintings on every wall.
Some critics dismissed the novel as being overly arty-farty and lacking substance.

Synset Examples

His presentation was filled with arty-farty jargon.
She dismissed the movie as just another arty-farty film.
I found the exhibition to be overly arty-farty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pretentious - attempting to impress by affecting greater importance or merit than is actually possessed
  • Synonym: artsy - showing an excessive interest in or preoccupation with the arts
