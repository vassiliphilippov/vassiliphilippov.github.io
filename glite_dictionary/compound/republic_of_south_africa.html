Republic of South Africa

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv saʊθ ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv saʊθ ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (BrE)
the official name of the country of South Africa, located at the southern tip of the African continent
country at the southern tip of Africa

Examples

She was born and raised in the Republic of South Africa before moving abroad.
The Republic of South Africa is known for its diverse cultures and landscapes.
After independence, the Republic of South Africa adopted its current constitution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: South Africa - the common name for the Republic of South Africa
