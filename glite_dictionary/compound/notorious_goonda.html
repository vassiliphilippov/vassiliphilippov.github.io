notorious goonda

1. NOUN

/ noʊˈtɔriəs ˈɡuːndə / (AmE), / nəˈtɔːrɪəs ˈɡuːndə / (BrE)
An infamous criminal or thug, especially as referred to in South Asian contexts.
infamous criminal or thug (Indian English)
british

Examples

The police were determined to arrest the notorious goonda who had been terrorizing the locality.
Stories about the notorious goonda spread fear among the villagers for years.
Despite his reputation as a notorious goonda, he evaded authorities for months.

Synset Examples

The police were trying to catch the notorious goonda.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goonda - A violent criminal or thug, especially in South Asia
  • Synonym: gangster - A member of a criminal gang

Etymology

From Hindi गुंडा (guṇḍā, 'thug; criminal').
