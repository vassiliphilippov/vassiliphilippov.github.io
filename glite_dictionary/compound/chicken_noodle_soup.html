chicken noodle soup

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkən ˈnuːdəl suːp / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn ˈnuːdəl suːp / (BrE)
A soup made with pieces of chicken, noodles, and usually vegetables, typically served in a chicken broth.
soup with chicken and noodles

Examples

On cold days, my mother always made chicken noodle soup to warm us up.
Many people believe that chicken noodle soup helps when you have a cold.
The cafeteria served hot bowls of chicken noodle soup for lunch.

Synset Examples

I made chicken noodle soup for the sick friend.
The kids devoured the chicken noodle soup at lunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken soup - soup made primarily with chicken
