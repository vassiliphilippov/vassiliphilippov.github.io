anxiety attack

1. NOUN

/ ænˈzaɪ.əti əˈtæk / (AmE), / æŋˈzaɪ.əti əˈtæk / (BrE)
a sudden episode of intense anxiety, often accompanied by feelings of fear or panic and physical symptoms such as rapid heartbeat
sudden episode of intense anxiety

Examples

She experienced an anxiety attack just before her big presentation at work.
The sudden noise triggered an anxiety attack that left him feeling breathless and shaky.
After several sleepless nights, he began having frequent anxiety attacks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panic attack - a sudden episode of intense fear or discomfort
  • Antonym: calm - a state of being free from anxiety or strong emotion
