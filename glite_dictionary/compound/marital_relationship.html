marital relationship

1. NOUN

/ ˈmærɪtəl rɪˈleɪʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈmærɪtəl rɪˈleɪʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
the relationship between a married couple, specifically between a husband and wife
relationship between married spouses

Examples

Trust and communication are crucial in any marital relationship.
After years of counseling, their marital relationship improved significantly.
Financial problems placed a strain on their marital relationship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marriage - the legally or formally recognized union of two people as partners in a personal relationship
  • Antonym: divorce - the legal dissolution of a marriage

Etymology

From Latin 'maritalis' (of marriage) and English 'relationship'.
