undescended testicle

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.dɪˈsɛndɪd ˈtɛstɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈsɛndɪd ˈtɛstɪkəl / (BrE)
a testicle that has not moved down into the scrotum as expected during fetal development
testicle that remains outside the scrotum
medical

Examples

The surgeon explained that an undescended testicle could lead to fertility problems later in life.
Early diagnosis of an undescended testicle increases the chances of successful treatment.
Boys born prematurely are more likely to have an undescended testicle.

Synset Examples

undescended testicles have an increased risk for cancer
