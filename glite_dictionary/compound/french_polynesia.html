French Polynesia

1. PROPN

/ frɛntʃ ˌpɑlɪˈniʒə / (AmE), / frɛntʃ ˌpɒlɪˈniːziə / (BrE)
an overseas territory of France made up of several islands in the South Pacific Ocean, including Tahiti and Bora Bora
French overseas territory in the South Pacific

Examples

I have always wanted to visit French Polynesia for its beautiful beaches and clear blue waters.
Tahiti is the largest island in French Polynesia.
Many tourists travel to French Polynesia to experience its unique culture and scenery.
