milk bar

1. NOUN

/ mɪlk bɑr / (AmE), / mɪlk bɑː / (BrE)
a snack bar or small shop that sells milk drinks, ice cream, and light refreshments
snack bar selling milk drinks and snacks

Examples

We stopped at the milk bar for milkshakes and sandwiches after school.
The old milk bar on the corner has been serving locals for decades.
During summer, the milk bar is always busy with kids buying ice cream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diner - a small, inexpensive restaurant
  • Synonym: café - small restaurant serving drinks and light meals
