Trumpster fire

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmpstər ˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpstə ˌfaɪə / (BrE)
A situation marked by chaos, scandal, or disaster, especially associated with Donald Trump or his administration.
chaotic or scandalous situation (esp. politics)
slang american

Examples

The press conference devolved into a Trumpster fire as tempers flared.
Social media erupted over the latest Trumpster fire in Washington.
Some voters saw the administration as one long Trumpster fire of controversy.

Synset Examples

Critics described the administration as a Trumpster fire.
The debate quickly became a Trumpster fire, full of insults.
The event turned into a Trumpster fire of chaos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dumpster fire - something totally chaotic or disastrous

Etymology

Modeled after 'dumpster fire' (meaning disastrous situation), blended with 'Trump'.
