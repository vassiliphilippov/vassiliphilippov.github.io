swamp lily

1. NOUN

/ swɑmp ˈlɪli / (AmE), / swɒmp ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant that grows in wet areas and has slender racemes of small white flowers.
North American wetland plant with white flowers

Examples

We found a beautiful swamp lily blooming near the edge of the pond.
The garden featured several native plants, including the swamp lily.
After the heavy rains, clusters of swamp lilies appeared throughout the marsh.
