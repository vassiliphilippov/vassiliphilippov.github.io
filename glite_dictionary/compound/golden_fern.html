golden fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən fɝn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən fɜːn / (BrE)
a tropical American fern with fronds that have light golden undersides
tropical fern with golden undersides

Examples

The golden fern adds a striking touch to tropical gardens with its unique coloration.
Botanists discovered a rare variety of golden fern in the rainforest.
Some gardeners prize the golden fern for its decorative foliage.

2. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən fɝn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən fɜːn / (BrE)
a tropical swamp fern with large fronds, especially in tropical America, notable for golden yellow sporangia on the undersides of its leaves
tropical fern with golden sporangia

Examples

In the wet marshland, a cluster of golden ferns caught the sunlight on their shimmering fronds.
The golden fern thrives in tropical swamps, where its underside glows with yellow sporangia.
Botanists studied the reproductive cycle of the golden fern during their expedition to South America.
