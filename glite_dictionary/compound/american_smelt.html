American smelt

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən smɛlt / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən smɛlt / (BrE)
a small silvery fish (Osmerus mordax) found in rivers and coastal waters of eastern North America and Alaska, valued as a food fish
a small North American food fish

Examples

The American smelt migrates upstream to spawn in the spring.
Fishermen in Maine often catch American smelt during the annual run.
Grilled American smelt is a popular dish in some coastal communities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smelt - a small silvery fish, especially of the family Osmeridae
