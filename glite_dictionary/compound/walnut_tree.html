walnut tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔlnət ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈwɔːlnʌt ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tree of the genus Juglans, grown for its edible nuts and valuable wood.
tree producing walnuts

Examples

The old walnut tree in their backyard provided delicious nuts every autumn.
They built their picnic table from walnut tree wood because it is strong and attractive.
A pair of squirrels raced up the walnut tree searching for food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: walnut - the edible nut of the walnut tree
