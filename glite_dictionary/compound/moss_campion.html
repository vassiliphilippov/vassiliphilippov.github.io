moss campion

1. NOUN

/ mɑs ˈkæmpjən / (AmE), / mɒs ˈkæmpɪən / (BrE)
a small, tufted or mat-forming perennial plant (Silene acaulis) found in Arctic and alpine regions of Europe and North America, with pink or purple flowers
Arctic/alpine perennial plant, Silene acaulis

Examples

The delicate flowers of the moss campion brightened the rocky tundra.
Hikers spotted patches of moss campion blooming across the alpine slopes.
Moss campion survives harsh Arctic conditions by growing low to the ground.
