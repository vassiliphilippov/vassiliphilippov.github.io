rose-apple tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊz ˌæpəl tri / (AmE), / ˈrəʊz ˌæpəl triː / (BrE)
A tropical tree of the East Indies, cultivated for its edible, often aromatic fruit.
tropical tree with edible fruit

Examples

The rose-apple tree produces fruit that is sweet and fragrant, popular in many tropical countries.
A rose-apple tree can be found growing in her grandfather's backyard in Indonesia.
During the harvest season, villagers gather under the rose-apple tree to collect its ripe fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Syzygium jambos - Scientific name for the rose-apple tree
