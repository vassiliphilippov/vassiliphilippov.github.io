war porn

1. NOUN

/ wɔr pɔrn / (AmE), / wɔː pɔːn / (BrE)
media that sensationalizes war or violence, often depicting it in a graphic or exploitative manner for entertainment or shock value, rather than to inform or educate
sensationalized media about war or violence

Examples

Many critics argue that the new action movie crosses the line into war porn with its gratuitous battle scenes.
The documentary was accused of being war porn for focusing more on violence than context or analysis.
Some viewers feel uncomfortable with news outlets that seem to promote war porn rather than deliver objective reporting.

Synset Examples

Some say that war porn desensitizes viewers to real suffering.
The documentary felt like war porn, showcasing violence excessively.
Critics labeled the film as war porn for its graphic scenes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: propaganda - biased or misleading information used to promote a political cause or point of view

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'porn', as in pornography, to indicate exploitative or sensational depictions of violence.
