United Self-Defense Group of Colombia

1. PROPN

/ juˈnaɪtɪd ˈsɛlf dɪˈfɛns ɡruːp əv kəˈlʌmbiə / (AmE), / juˈnaɪtɪd ˈsɛlf dɪˈfɛns ɡruːp əv kəˈlʌmbiə / (BrE)
A paramilitary and former terrorist organization in Colombia formed in 1997 as an umbrella group for various regional paramilitary forces, financed largely by narcotics trafficking and aimed at protecting its members' economic interests.
Colombian paramilitary organization (1997–)

Examples

The United Self-Defense Group of Colombia was heavily involved in the Colombian armed conflict during the late 1990s and early 2000s.
International agencies monitored the activities of the United Self-Defense Group of Colombia due to its links to narcotics trafficking.
The Colombian government began demobilizing the United Self-Defense Group of Colombia in the mid-2000s.
