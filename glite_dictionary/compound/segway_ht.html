Segway HT

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛɡ.weɪ ˌeɪtʃˈti / (AmE), / ˈsɛɡ.weɪ ˌeɪtʃ ˈtiː / (BrE)
A trademarked, self-balancing two-wheeled personal transportation device designed for use in pedestrian areas.
self-balancing two-wheeled scooter

Examples

Tourists often rent a Segway HT to explore the city center quickly and easily.
The security guard patrolled the mall corridors on his Segway HT.
Learning to balance on a Segway HT is surprisingly intuitive for most people.

Etymology

From Segway (brand name) and HT (an abbreviation of ‘Human Transporter’).
