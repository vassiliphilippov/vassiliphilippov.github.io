dolphin striker

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑːlfɪn ˌstraɪkər / (AmE), / ˈdɒlfɪn ˌstraɪkə / (BrE)
A short spar fixed below the bowsprit of a sailboat to support the martingale and maintain the tension of the rigging.
spar under a sailboat's bowsprit

Examples

The crew inspected the dolphin striker before setting sail to ensure the rigging was secure.
During the restoration, they replaced the damaged dolphin striker with a sturdier one.
A broken dolphin striker can compromise the stability of the bowsprit and the whole rigging system.
