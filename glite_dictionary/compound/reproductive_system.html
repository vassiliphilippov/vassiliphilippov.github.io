reproductive system

1. NOUN

/ ˌriː.prəˈdʌk.tɪv ˌsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˌriː.prəˈdʌk.tɪv ˌsɪs.təm / (BrE)
the organs and tissues involved in the production of offspring, including the creation, maturation, and union of gametes
organs and tissues for reproduction

Examples

The human reproductive system includes the ovaries, testes, and related structures.
Certain diseases can affect the reproductive system, leading to fertility problems.
Biology students are required to learn about the reproductive system of various animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genital system - the system of organs involved in reproduction
