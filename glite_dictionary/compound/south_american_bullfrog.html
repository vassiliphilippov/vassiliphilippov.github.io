South American bullfrog

1. NOUN

/ saʊθ əˈmɛrɪkən ˈbʊlˌfrɔg / (AmE), / saʊθ əˈmɛrɪkən ˈbʊlˌfrɒg / (BrE)
a large, toothed frog found in South and Central America, similar in appearance to the bullfrog
a large frog species from South and Central America

Examples

The South American bullfrog can grow to an impressive size, much larger than most local frogs.
Researchers studied the diet of the South American bullfrog in the Amazon rainforest.
Unlike its North American cousin, the South American bullfrog has noticeable teeth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullfrog - a large North American frog known for its deep call
