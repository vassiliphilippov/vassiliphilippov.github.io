mental masturbation

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.təl ˌmæstəˈbeɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.təl ˌmæstəˈbeɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
prolonged or self-indulgent intellectual activity that has no practical outcome or purpose
pointless intellectual activity
informal

Examples

Some academic discussions can descend into little more than mental masturbation.
The meeting turned into mental masturbation without producing any actionable ideas.
He accused the panel of engaging in mental masturbation instead of addressing real issues.

Synset Examples

Endless debates without action are just mental masturbation.
They engaged in mental masturbation instead of making real plans.
He felt his brainstorming session was just mental masturbation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: navel-gazing - self-indulgent or excessive contemplation with little practical value

Etymology

Formed by analogy to 'masturbation', implying self-gratification through thought rather than action.
