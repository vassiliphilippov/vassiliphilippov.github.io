unique rhymery

1. NOUN

/ juˈnik ˈraɪməri / (AmE), / juˈniːk ˈraɪməri / (BrE)
A distinctive style of creating rhymes or poetic expression, especially one that sets the speaker or writer apart.
distinctive style of rhyming in poetry

The poet's unique rhymery captivated audiences at every performance.

Her unique rhymery brought a fresh voice to the traditional ballad form.
