news media

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuz ˌmiːdiə / (AmE), / ˈnjuːz ˌmiːdiə / (BrE)
newspapers, magazines, and other publications or organizations that report news collectively
organizations that report news

Examples

The news media played a crucial role in covering the election results.
Many people distrust the news media due to concerns about bias.
The president addressed the news media during a press conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: press - the newspapers and news magazines collectively
