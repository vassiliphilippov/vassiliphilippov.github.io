constant jackanapery

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑnstənt ˌdʒækəˌneɪpəri / (AmE), / ˈkɒnstənt ˌdʒækəˈneɪpəri / (BrE)
Behavior that is persistently foolish or silly.
persistent silly or foolish behavior

Examples

Her manager grew tired of his constant jackanapery during meetings.
The teacher warned the class that constant jackanapery would not be tolerated.
Despite his intelligence, his constant jackanapery often distracted his coworkers.

Synset Examples

She couldn't stand his constant jackanapery at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jackanapery - foolish or impudent behavior
  • Synonym: tomfoolery - foolish or silly behavior

Etymology

Formed from "constant" and "jackanapery", the latter derived from "jackanapes" (a cheeky or impertinent person).
