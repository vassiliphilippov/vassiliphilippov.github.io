opaque explanation

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈpeɪk ˌɛkspləˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / əʊˈpeɪk ˌɛkspləˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
an explanation that is difficult to understand or unclear
unclear or hard-to-understand explanation

Examples

The professor's opaque explanation left many students feeling confused about the topic.
She grew frustrated with his opaque explanation of the technical details.
An opaque explanation can make simple things seem much more complicated than they are.

Synset Examples

His explanation was so opaque that I couldn't understand it.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clear explanation - an explanation that is easy to understand
