ad nauseam

1. ADV

/ æd ˈnɔziˌæm / (AmE), / ad ˈnɔːzɪam / (BrE)
to a sickening or excessive degree; repeated so often as to become annoying or tiresome.
repeated to a sickening extent

Examples

The debate continued ad nauseam until everyone grew tired of the arguments.
He repeated his instructions ad nauseam, hoping everyone would remember them.
The marketing campaign played the same jingle ad nauseam on every channel.

Synset Examples

he played the song ad nauseam

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endlessly - in a way that seems to have no end
  • Synonym: ceaselessly - without stopping or pausing; unendingly

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'to nausea'.
