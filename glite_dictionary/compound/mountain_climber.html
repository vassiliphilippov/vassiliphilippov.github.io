mountain climber

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntən ˌklaɪmər / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˌklaɪmə / (BrE)
a person who climbs mountains, especially as a sport
person who climbs mountains

Examples

The mountain climber scaled the peak despite the harsh weather conditions.
As an experienced mountain climber, she knew how to handle difficult terrain.
Many mountain climbers dream of conquering Mount Everest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alpinist - a person who climbs high mountains, especially in the Alps
  • Synonym: climber - a person who climbs, especially mountains or cliffs
