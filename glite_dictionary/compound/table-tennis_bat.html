table-tennis bat

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbl̩ ˌtɛnɪs bæt / (AmE), / ˈteɪbl ˌtɛnɪs bæt / (BrE)
A small flat paddle with a short handle, used to hit the ball in table tennis.
paddle used in table tennis

Examples

She gripped the table-tennis bat tightly, ready to return the serve.
His table-tennis bat had a special rubber surface for extra spin.
After losing his table-tennis bat, he had to borrow one for the match.

Related Terms
