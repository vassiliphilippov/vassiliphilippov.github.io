strip poker

1. NOUN

/ strɪp ˈpoʊkər / (AmE), / strɪp ˈpəʊkə / (BrE)
A form of poker in which players remove an article of clothing to pay for losses.
poker game where losers remove clothing

Examples

We decided to play strip poker at the party, which made things a lot more interesting.
He lost three rounds of strip poker and had to take off his shirt and socks.
Some people find strip poker embarrassing, but others see it as harmless fun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poker - a card game in which players bet and try to get combinations of cards
