funeral chapel

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuːnərəl ˈtʃæpəl / (AmE), / ˈfjuːnərəl ˈtʃæpəl / (BrE)
a building or room where the friends and family of a deceased person can visit to pay their last respects before the funeral
room or building for viewing before a funeral

Examples

The family gathered at the funeral chapel to say their final goodbyes.
A memorial service will be held in the funeral chapel on Friday afternoon.
Many people visited the funeral chapel to pay their respects before the burial.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chapel - a small building or room used for Christian worship
