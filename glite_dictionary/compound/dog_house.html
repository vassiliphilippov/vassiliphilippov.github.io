dog house

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔɡ ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡ ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a small shelter built outdoors for a dog to sleep or stay in
outdoor shelter for a dog

Examples

We built a sturdy dog house in the backyard for our golden retriever.
During the storm, the frightened puppy hid in its dog house.
The old dog house needed repairs after years of exposure to the weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kennel - a shelter or house for a dog or pack of dogs
