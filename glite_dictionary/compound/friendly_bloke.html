friendly bloke

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛndli bloʊk / (AmE), / ˈfrɛndli bləʊk / (BrE)
a man who is kind and pleasant
kind and pleasant man (informal)
informal british

Examples

Everyone said John was a friendly bloke who always greeted neighbors with a smile.
At the pub, I met a friendly bloke who offered useful advice about the city.
She described her boss as a friendly bloke who treated everyone fairly.

Synset Examples

He's a friendly bloke who loves to help.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nice guy - a man who is pleasant and agreeable

Etymology

'Bloke' is British slang for 'man,' probably from 19th-century slang.
