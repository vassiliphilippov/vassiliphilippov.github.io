hauntingly beautiful

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑntɪŋli bjuˈtɪfəl / (AmE), / ˈhɔːntɪŋli ˈbjuːtɪfl̩ / (BrE)
strikingly beautiful in a way that evokes strong or lingering emotions, often with an element of mystery or sadness
evoking strong, lingering beauty and emotion

Examples

The abandoned house had a hauntingly beautiful charm that lingered in her mind.
A hauntingly beautiful melody played softly throughout the film, moving the audience deeply.
The morning mist gave the landscape a hauntingly beautiful appearance that was hard to forget.

Synset Examples

He described the leprous landscape as hauntingly beautiful.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: beautiful - pleasing the senses or mind aesthetically
  • Degree Higher: breathtaking - very impressive or astonishing in beauty
