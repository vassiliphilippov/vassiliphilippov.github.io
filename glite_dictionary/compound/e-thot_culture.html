e-thot culture

1. NOUN

/ ˈi ˌθɑt ˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈi ˌθɒt ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
a social trend or group of online influencers, typically women, who gain attention or followers by sharing provocative or suggestive content
online influencer trend focused on provocative content
informal slang

Examples

Discussions about e-thot culture often appear on social media platforms and forums.
Some view e-thot culture as empowering, while others criticize its impact on online interactions.
The rise of e-thot culture has changed the landscape of online fame and influencer marketing.

Synset Examples

The e-thot culture often sparks debates online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: influencer culture - culture surrounding social media influencers
  • Degree Lower: sex-positive - having or promoting an open attitude towards sex

Etymology

Combination of internet prefix 'e-' (electronic) and 'thot' (slang for 'that ho over there'), meaning a woman considered sexually provocative online.
