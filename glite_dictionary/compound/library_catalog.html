library catalog

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪˌbrɛri ˈkætəlˌɔg / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.brəri ˈkæt.əl.ɒg / (BrE)
a systematic list or database of the materials held in a library, such as books, periodicals, and other resources
systematic listing of library holdings

Examples

The new library catalog allows users to search for books online from home.
He spent hours browsing the library catalog to find sources for his research paper.
When the school received new books, they were promptly added to the library catalog.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catalogue - a complete list, often systematically arranged, of items such as books or goods
  • Synonym: bibliography - a list of books or resources on a particular subject
