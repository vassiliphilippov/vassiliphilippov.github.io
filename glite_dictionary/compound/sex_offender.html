sex offender

1. NOUN

/ sɛks əˈfɛndər / (AmE), / sɛks əˈfɛndə / (BrE)
a person who has been convicted of a criminal offense involving sexual activity, such as sexual assault or abuse
person convicted of a sex crime

Examples

The police released a list of registered sex offenders living in the area.
He was labeled a sex offender following his conviction for sexual assault.
Many communities have laws about where a sex offender can live after serving prison time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: criminal - person who has committed a crime
  • Degree Lower: sex crime - a criminal offense involving sexual activity
