agricultural science

1. NOUN

/ ˌæɡ.rɪˈkʌl.tʃɚl ˈsaɪ.əns / (AmE), / ˌæɡ.rɪˈkʌl.tʃər.əl ˈsaɪ.əns / (BrE)
the scientific study of farming, including the cultivation of crops and the raising of livestock
study of farming and crop/livestock production

Examples

She plans to pursue a career in agricultural science after graduation.
The university offers a variety of courses in agricultural science.
Advances in agricultural science have greatly improved crop yields around the world.

Synset Examples

Her degree is in agricultural science.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agriculture - the practice of farming, including cultivation of the soil and rearing of animals
