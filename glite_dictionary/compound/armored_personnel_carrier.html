armored personnel carrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmɚd pɚˈsɑnəl ˈkæriɚ / (AmE), / ˈɑːməd pəˈsɒnəl ˈkæriə / (BrE)
a military vehicle with armor protection, used for transporting infantry and typically equipped with caterpillar tracks
military vehicle for transporting troops

Examples

The army deployed an armored personnel carrier to safely transport soldiers through the conflict zone.
An armored personnel carrier rumbled down the street, its caterpillar tracks crushing debris beneath it.
During the evacuation, civilians were escorted by an armored personnel carrier for protection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: APC - abbreviation for armored personnel carrier
  • Degree Higher: tank - heavily armored combat vehicle with weapons
  • Synonym: half-track - armored vehicle with both wheels and tracks
