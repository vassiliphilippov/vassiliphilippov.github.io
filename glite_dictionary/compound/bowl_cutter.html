bowl cutter

1. NOUN

/ boʊl ˈkʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / bəʊl ˈkʌtə / (BrE)
A machine used in food processing to chop, mix, or emulsify meat and other ingredients in a rotating bowl.
meat chopping machine for food processing

Examples

The meat was finely minced using a bowl cutter at the processing plant.
Many sausage recipes require ingredients to be mixed in a bowl cutter for proper texture.
A new bowl cutter can improve efficiency in industrial food production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: food processor - an electric kitchen appliance used for chopping, mixing, or blending foods
  • Synonym: meat grinder - a machine for mincing or grinding meat
