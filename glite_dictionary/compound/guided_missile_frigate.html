guided missile frigate

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡaɪdɪd ˈmɪsəl ˈfrɪɡət / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪdɪd ˈmɪsaɪl ˈfrɪɡət / (BrE)
A warship equipped with guided missiles, designed primarily for escorting and protecting other ships.
warship equipped with guided missiles

Examples

The navy commissioned a new guided missile frigate to enhance its maritime defense capabilities.
A guided missile frigate is often equipped with advanced radar and weapons systems.
The guided missile frigate escorted the aircraft carrier during the mission.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frigate - a fast, medium-sized warship
