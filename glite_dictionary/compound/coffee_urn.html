coffee urn

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑfi ɜrn / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ɜːn / (BrE)
A large metal container used for brewing and keeping coffee hot, especially for serving several people.
large container for making and serving coffee

Examples

The hotel lobby had a large coffee urn available for guests in the morning.
She filled her cup from the coffee urn at the church brunch.
They rented several coffee urns to serve drinks at the conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urn - a large decorative container, especially for holding liquids
