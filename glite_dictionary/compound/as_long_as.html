as long as

1. SCONJ

/ əz lɔŋ æz / (AmE), / əz lɒŋ æz / (BrE)
for any duration that you prefer or specify; during all the time that
for any length of time

Examples

You can borrow the book as long as you return it by Friday.
Stay here as long as you like; there's no rush.
He promised to help as long as we needed him.

Synset Examples

You can stay here as long as you want.
