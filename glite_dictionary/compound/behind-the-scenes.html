behind-the-scenes

1. ADJ

/ bɪˌhaɪnd ðə ˈsinz / (AmE), / bɪˌhaɪnd ðə ˈsiːnz / (BrE)
Done or taking place secretly or confidentially, rather than being made public.
secret or confidential, not public

Examples

Much of the company's success is due to behind-the-scenes negotiations that the public never sees.
The film offers a rare behind-the-scenes look at how a Broadway show is produced.
Despite her modest appearance, she wielded considerable behind-the-scenes influence in politics.

Synset Examples

a sub-rosa report
has their under-the-table backing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sub-rosa - in secret; not publicly known
  • Synonym: under-the-table - secretly, especially in an illicit way
