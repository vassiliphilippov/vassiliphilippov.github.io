council board

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊn.səl ˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.səl ˌbɔːd / (BrE)
the table around which members of a council or committee sit during their meetings
table used in council or committee meetings

Examples

The committee gathered around the council board to discuss the new policy.
Important decisions were often made at the old oak council board in city hall.
He placed the documents carefully on the council board before beginning his presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boardroom - a room where a board of directors meets
