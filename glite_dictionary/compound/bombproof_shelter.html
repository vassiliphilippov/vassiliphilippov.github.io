bombproof shelter

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑmˌpruf ˈʃɛltɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɒmpruːf ˈʃɛltə / (BrE)
a shelter built to protect people or things from bomb explosions by being strong enough to withstand the blast
shelter resistant to explosions

Examples

The town hall was converted into a bombproof shelter during the war.
They stored emergency supplies in the new bombproof shelter under the school.
Several families gathered in the bombproof shelter when the air raid siren sounded.

Synset Examples

They built a bombproof shelter for emergencies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bunker - a reinforced underground shelter, often used during wars
