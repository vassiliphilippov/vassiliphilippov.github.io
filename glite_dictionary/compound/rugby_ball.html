rugby ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌɡbi bɔl / (AmE), / ˈrʌɡbi bɔːl / (BrE)
An oval, inflated ball used for playing rugby.
oval ball for rugby

Examples

The children played with a rugby ball in the park all afternoon.
He bought a new rugby ball before joining his college team.
The shape of a rugby ball makes it bounce unpredictably on the field.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: football - ball used in American football or association football (soccer)
