Baffin Island

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæfɪn ˌaɪlənd / (AmE), / ˈbæfɪn ˌaɪlənd / (BrE)
the largest island in Arctic Canada, located between Greenland and Hudson Bay
large island in Arctic Canada

Examples

The Inuit have lived on Baffin Island for thousands of years.
Scientists are studying climate change effects in the region around Baffin Island.
We took a spectacular flight over Baffin Island and saw its icy fjords.

Etymology

Named after English explorer William Baffin (1584–1622).
