minor expense

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪnər ɪkˈspɛns / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə ɪkˈspɛns / (BrE)
A small expense, often not specifically budgeted or detailed.
small, often unplanned expense

Examples

He recorded every minor expense from his business trip for reimbursement.
Unexpected minor expenses can add up over the course of a project.
The company allows employees to claim minor expenses like parking fees and refreshments.

Synset Examples

he requested reimbursement of $7 for incidental expenses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incidental expense - a minor expense not specifically accounted for
