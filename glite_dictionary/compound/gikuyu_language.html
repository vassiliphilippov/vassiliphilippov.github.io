Gikuyu language

1. NOUN

/ giˈkuːju ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / gɪˈkuːjuː ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Bantu language spoken primarily by the Kikuyu people in central Kenya.
Bantu language of central Kenya

Examples

He is fluent in both English and Gikuyu language.
Many traditional stories have been preserved in the Gikuyu language.
The Gikuyu language is taught in some Kenyan schools to preserve cultural heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kikuyu - another name for the Gikuyu language and the ethnic group

Etymology

From Kikuyu 'Gĩkũyũ', the self-designation of the speakers; 'language' from Latin 'lingua'.
