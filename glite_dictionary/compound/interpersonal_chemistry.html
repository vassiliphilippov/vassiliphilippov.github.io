interpersonal chemistry

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.tɚˈpɝ.sə.nəl ˈkɛm.ɪ.stri / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈpɜː.sən.əl ˈkem.ɪ.stri / (BrE)
the dynamic or connection between two people that affects how well they get along or relate to each other
connection or dynamic between two people

Examples

There was instant interpersonal chemistry between them, making their collaboration effortless.
Despite their differences, strong interpersonal chemistry kept their friendship intact for years.
Sometimes interpersonal chemistry is more important than skills when building a team.

Synset Examples

their chemistry was wrong from the beginning — they hated each other
a mysterious alchemy brought them together

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chemistry - relationship between people; mutual attraction
