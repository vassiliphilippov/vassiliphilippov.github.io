psychometric test

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaɪkoʊˈmɛtrɪk tɛst / (AmE), / ˌsʌɪkəʊˈmɛtrɪk tɛst / (BrE)
a standardized assessment designed to measure mental abilities, personality traits, or other psychological attributes
standardized assessment of mental abilities

Examples

She took a psychometric test as part of the job application process.
The results of the psychometric test indicated high levels of emotional intelligence.
Many companies use psychometric tests to help select suitable candidates.

Synset Examples

the test was standardized on a large sample of students

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aptitude test - a test measuring ability in a particular skill or field
  • Synonym: intelligence test - an assessment designed to measure intellectual ability

Etymology

From Greek *psyche* (mind, soul) and *metron* (measure), combined with 'test'.
