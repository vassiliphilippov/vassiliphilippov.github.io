bullet hole

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlət hoʊl / (AmE), / ˈbʊlət həʊl / (BrE)
A hole made by a bullet passing through an object or surface.
hole made by a bullet

Examples

There was a bullet hole in the windowpane after the shooting.
The police examined the bullet hole in the wall for evidence.
He pointed to the bullet hole in his jacket as proof of the attack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gunshot wound - an injury caused by a bullet or projectile
