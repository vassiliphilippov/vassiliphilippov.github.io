genus Orites

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs oʊˈraɪtiːz / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ɒˈriːtiːz / (BrE)
A small genus of shrubs or trees native to Australia, belonging to the family Proteaceae.
Australian genus of shrubs or trees

Examples

The genus Orites is well known among botanists for its unique floral structures.

Etymology

From New Latin, where 'Orites' is derived from Greek 'orites', meaning 'mountain dweller'.
