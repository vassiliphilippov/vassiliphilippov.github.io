virility drug

1. NOUN

/ vəˈrɪləti drʌɡ / (AmE), / vəˈrɪləti drʌɡ / (BrE)
A drug used to treat impotence caused by erectile dysfunction.
medicine for erectile dysfunction
medical

Examples

Many men seek a virility drug as a solution to erectile dysfunction.
The new virility drug showed promising results in clinical trials.
Some doctors warn that taking a virility drug without consultation can be risky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: erectile dysfunction - inability to achieve or maintain an erection
  • Degree Lower: aphrodisiac - substance that increases sexual desire

Etymology

From 'virility' (masculine strength, from Latin 'virilitas') + 'drug'.
