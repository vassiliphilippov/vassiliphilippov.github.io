escalation of tensions

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛskəˈleɪʃən əv ˈtɛnʃənz / (AmE), / ˌɛskəˈleɪʃən əv ˈtɛnʃənz / (BrE)
a situation in which conflict, hostility, or dispute becomes more intense or serious
increase in conflict or hostility

Examples

The escalation of tensions between the two countries prompted international concern.
There was a noticeable escalation of tensions in the community after the controversial decision.
Leaders called for dialogue to prevent further escalation of tensions in the region.

Synset Examples

The escalatio of tensions was evident in the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: de-escalation - the process of reducing the intensity of conflict or tension
  • Synonym: tension - a feeling or situation of stress between people or groups
