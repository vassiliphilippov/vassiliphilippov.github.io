hiya doin'

1. X

/ ˈhaɪjə ˈduɪn / (AmE), / ˈhaɪjə ˈduːɪn / (BrE)
An informal greeting meaning 'how are you?'
informal greeting; how are you?
informal

Examples

He walked in and said, 'hiya doin'?' to everyone in the room.
Whenever I pass my neighbor, she always greets me with a friendly 'hiya doin'?'.
In the café, he leaned over and asked, 'hiya doin'?' before taking his seat.

Synset Examples

She greeted me with a cheerful, 'Hiya doin'?'
I waved back and said, 'Hiya doin'?' with a smile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: how are you doing - standard form of the greeting
