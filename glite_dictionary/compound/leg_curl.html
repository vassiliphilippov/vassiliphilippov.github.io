leg curl

1. NOUN

/ lɛɡ kɜrl / (AmE), / lɛɡ kɜːl / (BrE)
an exercise in which you bend your knee to move your lower leg toward your buttocks against resistance, intended to strengthen the hamstring muscles
exercise for hamstring muscles

Examples

I added leg curls to my workout routine to strengthen my hamstrings.
She increased the weight on her leg curl machine to build more muscle.
Proper form is important when performing leg curls to avoid injury.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hamstring curl - an alternative term for the same exercise, targeting the hamstring muscles
