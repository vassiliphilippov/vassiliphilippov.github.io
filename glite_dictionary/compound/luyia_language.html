Luyia language

1. NOUN

/ luˈjiːə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / luˈjiːə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken in western Kenya, also known as Luhya
Bantu language of western Kenya

Examples

Scholars have published several grammatical studies on the Luyia language.
Many children in western Kenya grow up speaking both Swahili and the Luyia language.
The Luyia language has several different dialects spoken across the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Luhya - another name for the Luyia language or the Luhya people
