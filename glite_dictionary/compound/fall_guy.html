fall guy

1. NOUN

/ fɔl ɡaɪ / (AmE), / fɔːl ɡaɪ / (BrE)
a person who is blamed for something, especially so that others can avoid blame or punishment
person blamed to protect others

Examples

After the scandal broke, the company urgently needed a fall guy to divert attention from its executives.
He refused to be the fall guy for problems he didn't cause.
In the political crisis, she ended up as the fall guy despite having no real responsibility.

Synset Examples

They needed a fall guy to take the blame.
She played the fall guy in the office prank.
He became the fall guy for the failed project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scapegoat - a person who is blamed for the wrongdoings of others
