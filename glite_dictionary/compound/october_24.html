October 24

1. PROPN

/ ˌɑkˈtoʊbɚ ˈtwɛnti ˈfɔr / (AmE), / ɒkˈtəʊbə ˈtwɛnti ˈfɔː / (BrE)
The day each year that marks United Nations Day, celebrating the founding of the United Nations in 1945.
United Nations Day; UN founding anniversary

Examples

Every year, countries around the world celebrate October 24 as United Nations Day.
The United Nations was officially established on October 24, 1945.
Schools often hold special events on October 24 to promote awareness of the UN's work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: United Nations Day - international holiday celebrating the founding of the UN
