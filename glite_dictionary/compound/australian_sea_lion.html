Australian sea lion

1. NOUN

/ əˈstreɪliən siː ˈlaɪən / (AmE), / ɒˈstreɪliən siː ˈlaɪən / (BrE)
a species of sea lion native to the southern and western coasts of Australia
species of sea lion native to Australia

Examples

The Australian sea lion is listed as a vulnerable species due to its declining population.
Researchers are studying the diet of the Australian sea lion to understand its feeding habits.
During our trip to Kangaroo Island, we saw a colony of Australian sea lions basking on the beach.
