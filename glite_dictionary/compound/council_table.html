council table

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊnsəl ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈkaʊnsəl ˌteɪbəl / (BrE)
a large table around which members of a council or conference sit during a meeting
table used by a council or group in meetings

Examples

The committee gathered around the council table to discuss the new policy.
He placed the documents neatly in front of him on the council table.
The mayor presided over the meeting from the head of the council table.
