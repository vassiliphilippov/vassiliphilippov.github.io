muscular structure

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌskjələr ˈstrʌktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌskjʊlə ˈstrʌktʃə / (BrE)
the system or arrangement of muscles in an organism's body
the organism's muscle system

Examples

Regular exercise can help to strengthen your muscular structure and improve posture.
The cheetah's unique muscular structure allows it to reach incredible speeds when hunting.
Understanding the muscular structure of different species is important for veterinary medicine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muscular system - the system of muscles in the body
