epic journey

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛpɪk ˈdʒɝːni / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪk ˈdʒɜːni / (BrE)
a long and impressive trip, often involving adventure, significance, or challenges
significant or adventurous travel experience

Examples

Their road trip turned into an epic journey across the country.
Her solo trek through the Himalayas was an epic journey of self-discovery.
The novel follows the protagonist’s epic journey through danger and triumph.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: odyssey - a long and eventful journey or experience
