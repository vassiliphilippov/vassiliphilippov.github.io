name and address

1. NOUN

/ neɪm ənd əˈdrɛs / (AmE), / neɪm ənd əˈdrɛs / (BrE)
the identifying information of a person or organization, including their name and the location to which mail or packages should be delivered
recipient's identifying and delivery information

Examples

Please write your name and address clearly on the envelope before sending it.
The form asks for your name and address so they can contact you if needed.
They store the name and address of every customer in their database.
