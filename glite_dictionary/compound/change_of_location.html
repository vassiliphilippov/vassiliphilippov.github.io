change of location

1. NOUN

/ tʃeɪndʒ əv loʊˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ əv ləʊˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of moving something from one place to another
movement to a different place

Examples

The change of location for the meeting surprised everyone at the office.
After the change of location, customers found it easier to visit the new store.
A sudden change of location can disrupt transportation schedules.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relocation - the action of moving to a new place and establishing a new residence or site
  • Antonym: immobility - the state of not moving or being unmovable
