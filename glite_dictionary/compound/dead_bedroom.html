dead bedroom

1. NOUN

/ dɛd ˈbɛdˌrum / (AmE), / dɛd ˈbɛdˌruːm / (BrE)
a situation in a romantic relationship where physical or sexual intimacy has significantly diminished or disappeared
relationship lacking sexual intimacy

Examples

Many couples find themselves in a dead bedroom after years of routine and stress.
They attended counseling to address the issues causing their dead bedroom.
Online forums offer support for people experiencing a dead bedroom in their relationship.

Synset Examples

Communication issues led to a dead bedroom situation.
They sought help to revive their dead bedroom.
Their relationship felt like a dead bedroom lately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexless marriage - a marriage in which sexual activity is infrequent or absent
