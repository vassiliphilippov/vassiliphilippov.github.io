car tire

1. NOUN

/ kɑr taɪr / (AmE), / kɑː ˈtaɪə / (BrE)
a round rubber covering, typically inflated, that fits around the rim of a car wheel and provides traction and cushioning
rubber covering on a car wheel

Examples

The mechanic replaced the worn car tire with a brand new one.
She checked the pressure in each car tire before starting her road trip.
A sharp object on the street punctured his car tire and caused it to deflate quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tire - a rubber covering placed around a wheel to form a flexible contact with the road
  • Degree Lower: wheel - a circular object that rotates on an axle and forms part of a machine or vehicle
