lookout woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʊk.aʊt ˈwʊ.mən / (AmE), / ˈlʊk.aʊt ˈwʊ.mən / (BrE)
A woman whose job or role is to keep watch for a particular event or to warn others of approaching danger, especially in criminal contexts.
female person who keeps watch

Examples

The lookout woman gave the signal as soon as the police arrived.
They hired a lookout woman to keep watch at the entrance of the warehouse.
During the robbery, the lookout woman stood by the door with a walkie-talkie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lookout man - male person employed to keep watch for an anticipated event
  • Synonym: lookout - person who keeps watch for some anticipated event
