to please someone's palate

1. VERB

/ pliz ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈpælət / (AmE), / pliːz ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈpælət / (BrE)
to satisfy or appeal to someone's sense of taste; to make food or drink enjoyable for someone
satisfy someone's taste for food or drink

Examples

The chef worked hard to please someone's palate with a blend of spices in every dish.
A well-balanced wine can easily please someone's palate during a meal.
The dessert was sweet enough to please someone's palate without being overpowering.

Synset Examples

As the mealer, she knew how to please everyone's palate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delight - to give someone great pleasure or satisfaction
