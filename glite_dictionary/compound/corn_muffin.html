corn muffin

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn ˈmʌf.ɪn / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈmʌf.ɪn / (BrE)
A small, individual muffin made from cornmeal, similar to cornbread but in muffin form.
muffin made from cornmeal
american

Examples

She served warm corn muffins with butter for breakfast.
The restaurant offers a delicious corn muffin as a side with every meal.
He prefers a corn muffin to a regular wheat muffin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cornbread - bread made from cornmeal
  • Degree Lower: bran muffin - muffin made with bran as the primary ingredient
