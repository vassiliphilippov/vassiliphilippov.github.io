military march

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri mɑrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri mɑːtʃ / (BrE)
a piece of brisk, rhythmic music designed for soldiers to march to in military parades or ceremonies
marching music for military parades

Examples

The band played a rousing military march as the soldiers paraded through the city square.
A classic military march can inspire discipline and unity among troops.
He composed a military march that became the regiment's official anthem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: march - a piece of music with a strong regular rhythm, originally for marching
