sound alphabet

1. NOUN

/ saʊnd ˈæl.fə.bɛt / (AmE), / saʊnd ˈæl.fə.bɛt / (BrE)
an alphabet consisting of characters designed to represent the distinct sounds of speech
alphabet representing speech sounds

Examples

Linguists often use a sound alphabet to transcribe and study unfamiliar languages.
The International Phonetic Alphabet is the most widely known sound alphabet in the world.
Learning a sound alphabet can help actors master different accents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phonetic alphabet - alphabet designed to represent the sounds of speech
