yellow henbane

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈhɛnˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈhɛnbeɪn / (BrE)
a plant found on seashores from Virginia to South America, with greenish-yellow flowers and orange or yellow berries
coastal plant with yellowish flowers and berries

Examples

The dunes were dotted with yellow henbane thriving in the salty air.
She warned the children not to pick the yellow henbane berries along the shore.
Researchers studied the distribution of yellow henbane along the Atlantic coastline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: henbane - a poisonous plant of the nightshade family with sticky leaves and an unpleasant smell

Etymology

From 'yellow' (referring to its flower color) and 'henbane', an Old English term for several poisonous plants in the genus Hyoscyamus.
