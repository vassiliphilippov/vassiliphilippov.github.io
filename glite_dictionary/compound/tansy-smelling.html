tansy-smelling

1. ADJ

/ ˈtænzi ˌsmɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtanzi ˌsmɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
Having the characteristic scent of tansy, a bitter-tasting aromatic plant.
having the scent of tansy

Examples

The herbal tea had a distinct tansy-smelling aroma that reminded her of wild meadows.
A tansy-smelling breeze drifted through the old stone cottage in spring.
He described the bouquet as slightly bitter and tansy-smelling in his gardening journal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tansy - a bitter-tasting aromatic plant with yellow flowers
