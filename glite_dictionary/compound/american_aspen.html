American aspen

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛɹ.ɪ.kən ˈæs.pən / (AmE), / əˈmɛɹ.ɪ.kən ˈas.pən / (BrE)
A slender aspen tree native to North America, known for its pale bark and fluttering leaves.
slender North American aspen tree

Examples

The American aspen is easily identified by its smooth, white bark.
In autumn, the leaves of the American aspen turn a brilliant yellow.
Wildlife often rely on groves of American aspen for shelter and food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aspen - a type of tree with rounded, fluttering leaves
