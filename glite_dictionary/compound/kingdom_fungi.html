kingdom Fungi

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm ˈfʌnˌdʒaɪ / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm ˈfʌndʒi / (BrE)
the taxonomic kingdom that includes fungi such as yeasts, molds, smuts, mushrooms, and toadstools, distinct from plants and animals
taxonomic kingdom of fungi

Examples

The kingdom Fungi includes organisms that decompose organic material in their environment.
Scientists have discovered new species within the kingdom Fungi that can survive extreme conditions.
Not all mushrooms belong to the same group within the kingdom Fungi.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fungus - a member of the kingdom Fungi

Etymology

From Latin 'fungus' (mushroom, fungus), with 'kingdom' denoting a taxonomic rank.
