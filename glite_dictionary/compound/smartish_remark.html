smartish remark

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmɑːrtɪʃ rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈsmɑːtɪʃ rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
a clever or witty comment, often made in a slightly cheeky or playful way
clever or witty comment

Examples

She couldn't resist making a smartish remark during the discussion.
He was known for his quick smartish remarks that made everyone laugh.
One smartish remark at the wrong time can get you into trouble.

Synset Examples

His smartish remarks impressed everyone at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quip - a witty remark
  • Synonym: retort - a sharp or witty reply
