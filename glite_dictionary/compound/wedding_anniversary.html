wedding anniversary

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ ænɪˌvɝːsɛri / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ ənɪˌvɜːsəri / (BrE)
the anniversary of the date on which a couple was married, or the celebration of that date
anniversary of marriage

Examples

We celebrated our tenth wedding anniversary by taking a trip to Paris.
They received many gifts on their silver wedding anniversary.
Her parents throw a party every year for their wedding anniversary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anniversary - the date on which an event occurred in a previous year
