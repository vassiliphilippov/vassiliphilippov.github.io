bill poster

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪl ˌpoʊstər / (AmE), / ˈbɪl ˌpəʊstə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to paste up bills or posters on walls or billboards
person who puts up posters or bills

Examples

The bill poster worked through the night, covering the city with advertisements for the new play.
Every morning, a bill poster made his way along the high street, putting up colorful notices.
Council regulations now restrict where a bill poster can legally work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poster - a person who puts up posters
