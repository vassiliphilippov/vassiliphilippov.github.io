mixed marriage

1. NOUN

/ mɪkst ˈmærɪdʒ / (AmE), / mɪkst ˈmærɪdʒ / (BrE)
A marriage between two people who come from different racial, religious, or cultural backgrounds.
marriage between people of different backgrounds

Examples

Some communities still hold conservative views on mixed marriage.
Growing up in a mixed marriage family gave her a broader cultural perspective.
The documentary explores the challenges faced by couples in a mixed marriage.

Synset Examples

the families of both partners in a mixed marriage often disapprove
