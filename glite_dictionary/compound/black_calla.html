black calla

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈkælə / (AmE), / blak ˈkalə / (BrE)
An ornamental plant native to the Middle East, cultivated for its dark purple spathe.
ornamental plant with dark purple spathe

Examples

The black calla adds a dramatic touch to any garden with its dark purple blooms.
She received a bouquet featuring a rare black calla for her birthday.
Unlike traditional lilies, the black calla stands out for its striking color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calla lily - ornamental plant with a spathe, often white
