assistant foreman

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɪstənt ˈfɔrˌmæn / (AmE), / əˈsɪstənt ˈfɔːmən / (BrE)
a person who supports the foreman by supervising other workers in a work team or gang
supervisor supporting a foreman

Examples

The assistant foreman was responsible for assigning tasks to the crew each morning.
When the foreman was away, the assistant foreman ensured the work continued smoothly.
As assistant foreman, she reported both to the employees and to management.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: foreman - the person in charge of a group of workers
