Jedi Master

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛdaɪ ˈmæstər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛdaɪ ˈmɑːstə / (BrE)
a highly skilled and wise leader in the Jedi order, a fictional group from the Star Wars universe; sometimes used figuratively for someone extremely skilled and knowledgeable
senior member of the Jedi order in Star Wars

Examples

After years of training, he was finally named a Jedi Master in the council.
She became the team's Jedi Master for troubleshooting complex issues.
His calm wisdom at work has earned him the nickname Jedi Master among his colleagues.

Synset Examples

She felt like a Jedi Master after solving the problem.
He trained for years to become a Jedi Master.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: master - an expert or highly skilled person

Etymology

From 'Jedi,' coined by George Lucas for Star Wars, and 'master,' meaning expert or teacher.
