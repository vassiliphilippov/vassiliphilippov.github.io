living thing

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌθɪŋ / (BrE)
a creature or organism that is alive, such as an animal, plant, or bacterium
an organism or creature that is alive

Examples

Every living thing on Earth needs water to survive.
Biologists study how a living thing grows, reproduces, and responds to its environment.
She believes that every living thing deserves respect and compassion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organism - an individual living entity, such as an animal, plant, or microbe
