soft white light

1. NOUN

/ sɑft waɪt laɪt / (AmE), / sɒft waɪt laɪt / (BrE)
light that is both white in color and gentle or diffuse, without being harsh or glaring
gentle, non-harsh white illumination

Examples

A soft white light glowed from the bedside lamp, creating a calm and relaxing atmosphere.
The hallway was illuminated by soft white light, making it easy on the eyes in the early morning.
She preferred soft white light bulbs when reading at night to avoid eye strain.

Synset Examples

The room was filled with soft white light from the lamp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white light - light that contains all visible wavelengths, appearing colorless
  • Synonym: soft light - light that is diffused and gentle, not harsh
  • Antonym: harsh light - light that is intense, strong, or glaring
