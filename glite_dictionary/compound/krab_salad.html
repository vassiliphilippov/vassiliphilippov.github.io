krab salad

1. NOUN

/ kræb ˈsæləd / (AmE), / kræb ˈsaləd / (BrE)
a salad made with imitation crab meat, often combined with mayonnaise and vegetables
salad with imitation crab meat

Examples

I often order a krab salad for lunch when I want something light and tasty.
The grocery store deli serves a fresh krab salad every day.
She topped her crackers with a spoonful of krab salad as an appetizer.

Synset Examples

The menu features a delicious krab salad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab salad - salad made with real crab meat

Etymology

From 'krab' (a brand/generic spelling for imitation crab) plus 'salad'.
