puppy love

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌp.i lʌv / (AmE), / ˈpʌp.i lʌv / (BrE)
A temporary and intense romantic attraction, often felt by adolescents and not regarded as serious or lasting.
adolescent temporary romantic infatuation

Examples

Many teenagers experience puppy love during their first year of high school.
They dismissed their daughter's feelings as just a case of puppy love.
Looking back, he realized his first relationship was simply puppy love and nothing more.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crush - a brief but intense infatuation with someone

Etymology

The term compares the inexperience and playfulness of young love to the behavior of young puppies.
