speech therapist

1. NOUN

/ spitʃ ˈθɛrəpɪst / (AmE), / spiːtʃ ˈθɛrəpɪst / (BrE)
A specialist who diagnoses and treats speech and language disorders.
specialist in speech/language therapy

Examples

Her child made remarkable progress after working with a speech therapist for several months.
The speech therapist designed exercises to help the patient articulate words more clearly.
A speech therapist can assist children with developmental speech delays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speech-language pathologist - specialist in diagnosing and treating speech and language disorders
