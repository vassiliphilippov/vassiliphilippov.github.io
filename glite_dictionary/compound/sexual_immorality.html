sexual immorality

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl ɪˌmɔːˈræləti / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl ɪˌmɒˈralɪti / (BrE)
Sexual behavior that is considered morally wrong or unacceptable according to societal or religious standards.
sexual behavior deemed morally wrong

Examples

Many religious texts warn against sexual immorality and promote values of discipline and restraint.
The community was deeply concerned by the rise in sexual immorality among young people.
He was criticized for promoting sexual immorality through his writings and public remarks.

Synset Examples

sexual immorality is the major reason for last year's record number of abortions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promiscuity - having many sexual partners; lack of sexual restraint
  • Antonym: chastity - sexual purity; not engaging in unlawful sexual activity
