sexual assault

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl əˈsɔlt / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl əˈsɔːlt / (BrE)
Unwanted sexual contact or activity, especially one that involves the use or threat of force, and is considered a criminal offense.
unwanted, forced sexual act; criminal offense
legal

Examples

The organization provides support to survivors of sexual assault.
New laws have been introduced to increase the penalties for sexual assault.
She spoke publicly about her experience as a victim of sexual assault to raise awareness.

Synset Examples

most states have replaced the common law definition of rape with statutes defining sexual assault

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rape - sexual intercourse carried out forcibly against a person's will; criminal offense
