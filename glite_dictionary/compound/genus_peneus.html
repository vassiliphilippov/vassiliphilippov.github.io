genus Peneus

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs pəˈniː.əs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs pɛˈniː.əs / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Peneidae, comprising certain prawns and shrimps.
type genus of prawn family Peneidae

Examples

The genus Peneus includes several commercially important species of prawns.
Marine biologists are studying the genus Peneus to understand shrimp biodiversity.
Several prawns in the genus Peneus are harvested for human consumption.

Etymology

From Latin 'genus' (kind, type) and 'Peneus', named after the Greek river god Peneus.
