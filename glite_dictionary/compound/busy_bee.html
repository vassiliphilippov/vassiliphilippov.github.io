busy bee

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪzi bi / (AmE), / ˈbɪzi biː / (BrE)
a person who is always busy, active, and industrious
an active and hardworking person

Examples

Ever since she started her new job, she's been a real busy bee around the office.
My grandmother is a busy bee, always finding something to do even on weekends.
The volunteers were busy bees as they prepared meals for the charity event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workaholic - a person who compulsively works long and hard hours
  • Antonym: idler - a person who is habitually lazy or does not work
