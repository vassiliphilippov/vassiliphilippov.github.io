lemon-wood tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛmən ˌwʊd tri / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən ˌwʊd triː / (BrE)
a South African evergreen tree with hard, tough wood, often used in carpentry and furniture-making
South African evergreen tree with tough wood

Examples

The furniture was crafted from the tough wood of the lemon-wood tree.
A lemon-wood tree can provide dense shade in gardens due to its evergreen leaves.
Carpenters in South Africa highly value the timber from the lemon-wood tree.
