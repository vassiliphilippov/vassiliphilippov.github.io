hidden truth

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪdən truθ / (AmE), / ˈhɪdən truːθ / (BrE)
a fact or reality that has been concealed or not made known to others
a concealed fact or reality

Examples

The documentary uncovered a hidden truth about the famous artist's mysterious disappearance.
Sometimes people go to great lengths to keep a hidden truth from coming to light.
Her memoir revealed the hidden truths behind her family's public image.

Synset Examples

Gloriagate revealed many hidden truths about the administration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secret - something that is kept or meant to be kept unknown or unseen by others
  • Antonym: lie - a false statement made with deliberate intent to deceive
