ballsy move

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔlzi muːv / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlzi muːv / (BrE)
an action or decision that is considered very bold or audacious, often involving risk or courage
bold or audacious decision
informal

Examples

Resigning without another job lined up was a truly ballsy move.
It was a ballsy move to challenge the CEO so openly in the meeting.
Her ballsy move paid off when the risky investment doubled in value.

Synset Examples

It was a ballsy move to quit her job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bold move - an act or decision showing courage or confidence
  • Antonym: timid move - an act or decision lacking courage or confidence
