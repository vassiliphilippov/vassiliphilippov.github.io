Little Wabash

1. PROPN

/ ˌlɪt.əl wəˈbæʃ / (AmE), / ˌlɪt.əl wəˈbæʃ / (BrE)
a river in eastern Illinois, United States, that flows southeastward to join the Wabash River.
river in eastern Illinois, USA
