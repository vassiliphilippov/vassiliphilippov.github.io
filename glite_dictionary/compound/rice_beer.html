rice beer

1. NOUN

/ raɪs bɪr / (AmE), / raɪs bɪə / (BrE)
An alcoholic beverage made from fermented rice, commonly associated with Japanese sake, and sometimes served hot.
alcoholic drink made from fermented rice

Examples

He ordered a cup of rice beer with his sushi at the Japanese restaurant.
Traditional ceremonies in some Asian countries include the sharing of homemade rice beer.
The aroma of freshly brewed rice beer filled the festival hall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sake - Japanese rice wine; an alcoholic drink made from fermented rice
