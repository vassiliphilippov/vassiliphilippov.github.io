taco fest

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑːkoʊ fɛst / (AmE), / ˈtakəʊ fɛst / (BrE)
A festival or public event featuring tacos and other taco-related foods, typically including food vendors, tastings, and entertainment.
festival celebrating tacos and related food

Examples

We spent the afternoon sampling different salsas at the taco fest downtown.
The annual taco fest draws thousands of visitors to the city each summer.
Tickets for the taco fest sold out within hours of going on sale.

Synset Examples

We spent the day enjoying the taco fest activities.
The taco fest attracted food lovers from miles away.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: food festival - an event that showcases food, often organized around a theme or cuisine
