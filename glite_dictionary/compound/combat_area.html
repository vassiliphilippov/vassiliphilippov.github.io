combat area

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmˌbæt ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˈkɒmbæt ˈeəriə / (BrE)
an area where armed forces engage in military operations against an enemy
area of military operations or fighting

Examples

During the conflict, the journalists were not allowed to enter the combat area for their own safety.
The medic was quickly transported from the combat area after sustaining injuries.
Tensions increased as more troops were deployed to the combat area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: battlefield - an area where a battle is fought
