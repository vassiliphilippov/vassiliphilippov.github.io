real shitland

1. NOUN

/ ˈɹiːəl ˈʃɪtlænd / (AmE), / ˈɹɪəl ˈʃɪtlənd / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) An extremely undesirable or unpleasant place.
very undesirable place (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar

Examples

After moving there, he realized he was living in a real shitland with almost no basic services.
Many people consider the abandoned factory district to be a real shitland at night.
She referred to her old neighborhood as a real shitland because of its crime and decay.

That town is a real shitland with no opportunities.
