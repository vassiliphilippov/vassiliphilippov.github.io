bad person

1. NOUN

/ bæd ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / bæd ˈpɜːsn / (BrE)
a person who intentionally causes harm to others or engages in morally wrong behavior
person who harms others

Examples

Some believe that a bad person can change and become better over time.
The movie's main character is a bad person who never regrets his actions.
Don't let a bad person influence your decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: villain - a wicked or evil person
  • Synonym: evil-doer - a person who does evil things
  • Antonym: good person - a person who behaves in a morally good way
