chamois cloth

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃæmi klɔθ / (AmE), / ˈʃæmɪ klɒθ / (BrE)
a soft, absorbent leather or synthetic cloth used especially for cleaning and polishing surfaces such as windows or cars
soft, absorbent cloth for cleaning

Examples

After washing the car, he used a chamois cloth to dry it and leave a streak-free finish.
The windows sparkled after she wiped them down with a clean chamois cloth.
A good chamois cloth is essential for detailing your vehicle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shammy - soft leather cloth used for cleaning

Etymology

From French chamois, originally referring to the animal; 'cloth' added for clarity.
