environmental sustainability

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˌvaɪɹənˈmɛntəl səˌsteɪnəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ɪnˌvʌɪɹənˈmɛntəl səˌsteɪnəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the practice of using resources in a way that maintains ecological balance and avoids depletion or long-term harm to the environment
maintenance of ecological balance

Examples

Many companies are investing in environmental sustainability to reduce their ecological footprint.
Government policies now increasingly emphasize environmental sustainability as a key priority.
Educating children about environmental sustainability helps prepare them to protect the planet.

Synset Examples

The organization espouses environmental sustainability.
His politique focuses on environmental sustainability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sustainability - the ability to be maintained at a certain rate or level, esp. without harming the environment
  • Antonym: environmental degradation - the deterioration of the environment through depletion of resources and pollution
