hyped for

1. ADJ

/ haɪpt fɔr / (AmE), / haɪpt fə / (BrE)
very excited or enthusiastic about something that is going to happen
excited about something upcoming
informal

Examples

I'm really hyped for the concert next weekend.
Everyone in the office was hyped for the big presentation.
She got hyped for her birthday party days in advance.

Synset Examples

The kids were hyped for the school field trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excited - feeling eagerness or enthusiasm
  • Synonym: thrilled - extremely pleased or excited
  • Antonym: disinterested - not interested or enthusiastic

Etymology

From "hype" (heightened publicity or excitement) + "for" indicating the cause of the excitement.
