child abuse

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪld əˌbjus / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪld əˌbjuːs / (BrE)
The physical, emotional, or sexual mistreatment of children, often by a parent or caregiver.
mistreatment of children

Examples

The government launched a campaign to raise awareness about child abuse and its warning signs.
She works for an organization that supports victims of child abuse.
Laws against child abuse have been strengthened in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: child neglect - failure to provide for a child's basic needs
  • Antonym: child protection - measures taken to prevent or respond to child abuse
