sewing kit

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊɪŋ kɪt / (AmE), / ˈsəʊɪŋ kɪt / (BrE)
a small container or case holding needles, threads, and other items needed for sewing
container with sewing tools and materials

Examples

She always carries a sewing kit in her purse in case of emergencies.
The hotel room provided a complimentary sewing kit for guests.
My grandmother gave me her old sewing kit, filled with colorful threads and vintage needles.
