double-double

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʌbəl ˈdʌbəl / (AmE), / ˌdʌbəl ˈdʌbəl / (BrE)
The achievement of winning two sports tournaments in each of two consecutive years.
winning two tournaments in two consecutive years

Examples

Only two teams in league history have accomplished the double-double by winning both the cup and the championship two years in a row.
After her second consecutive year winning both singles and doubles titles, she celebrated her double-double.
Achieving a double-double is rare, making it a highly regarded accomplishment among athletes.
