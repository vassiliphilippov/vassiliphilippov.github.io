human beings

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuː.mən ˈbiː.ɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈhjuː.mən ˈbiː.ɪŋz / (BrE)
all people; all living members of the human species considered collectively
all people; members of the human species

Examples

All human beings deserve respect and equality, regardless of their background or beliefs.
Some scientists are researching how human beings adapt to life in extreme environments.
The rights of human beings must be protected by international law.

Etymology
