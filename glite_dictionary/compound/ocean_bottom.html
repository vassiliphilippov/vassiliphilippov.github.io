ocean bottom

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊʃən ˌbɑtəm / (AmE), / ˈəʊʃən ˌbɒtəm / (BrE)
the floor or bed at the deepest part of an ocean or sea
the floor of the ocean

Examples

Scientists have discovered new species living on the ocean bottom.
The submarine descended slowly to the ocean bottom.
Thick layers of sediment accumulate year after year on the ocean bottom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea floor - the bottom surface of a sea or ocean
  • Synonym: sea bed - the ground at the bottom of the sea
