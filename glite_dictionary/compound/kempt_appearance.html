kempt appearance

1. NOUN

/ kɛmpt əˈpɪrəns / (AmE), / kɛmpt əˈpɪə.rəns / (BrE)
a neatly combed or well-groomed look, especially in relation to a person's hair or overall presentation
neatly groomed look

Examples

His kempt appearance made a great first impression at the job interview.
Even after a long flight, her kempt appearance impressed her colleagues.
A kempt appearance is valued in many professional settings.

Synset Examples

His kempt appearance made a great first impression.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unkempt - having an untidy or disheveled appearance
  • Synonym: neat - tidy and arranged in an orderly way

Etymology

From Middle English 'kempt', the past participle of 'kemben' (to comb), combined with 'appearance'.
