studlike appearance

1. NOUN

/ ˈstʌdˌlaɪk əˈpɪrəns / (AmE), / ˈstʌdˌlaɪk əˈpɪərəns / (BrE)
an attractive or impressive appearance, especially one reminiscent of a 'stud'—a sexually appealing or confident man
an attractive and impressive look

Examples

His studlike appearance made him the center of attention at every event.
The actor maintained his studlike appearance with regular workouts and a confident attitude.
Many people admired the model's studlike appearance and charismatic smile.

Synset Examples

His studlike appearance turns heads everywhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stud - an attractive or sexually desirable man
  • Antonym: plain appearance - an ordinary or unremarkable look

Etymology

From 'stud' (slang, an attractive man) and 'appearance' (look, aspect).
