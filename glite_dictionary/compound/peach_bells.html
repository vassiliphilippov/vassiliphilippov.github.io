peach bells

1. NOUN

/ pitʃ bɛlz / (AmE), / piːtʃ bɛlz / (BrE)
A perennial European bellflower (Campanula persicifolia) with racemose clusters of white or blue flowers.
European perennial bellflower plant

Examples

The peach bells in her garden bloomed with delicate blue petals this spring.
Hikers admired the clusters of peach bells growing along the woodland path.
Gardeners often choose peach bells for their elegant, racemose flowers and hardy nature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bellflower - a plant with bell-shaped flowers, especially of the genus Campanula
