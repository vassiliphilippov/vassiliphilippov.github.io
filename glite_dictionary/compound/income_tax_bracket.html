income tax bracket

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnˌkʌm ˈtæks ˈbræk.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌkʌm ˈtæks ˈbræk.ɪt / (BrE)
a category that defines the rate of income tax a person pays based on their annual earnings
tax category by income level

Examples

If your earnings increase, you may move into a higher income tax bracket.
The government is considering changes to the income tax bracket thresholds this year.
She hired an accountant to ensure she understood which income tax bracket she falls into.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tax bracket - category of income taxed at a particular rate
