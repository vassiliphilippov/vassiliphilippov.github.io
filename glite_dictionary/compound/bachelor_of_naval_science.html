Bachelor of Naval Science

1. NOUN

/ ˈbætʃələr əv ˈneɪvəl ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈbætʃələ əv ˈneɪvəl ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
A bachelor's degree awarded for undergraduate study in naval science.
undergraduate degree in naval science

Examples

After four years at the academy, he graduated with a Bachelor of Naval Science.
Obtaining a Bachelor of Naval Science can lead to a career as a naval officer.
She majored in engineering and earned a Bachelor of Naval Science simultaneously.
