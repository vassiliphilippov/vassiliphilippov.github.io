Moquelumnan language

1. NOUN

/ moʊˌkɛləmˈnæn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / məʊˌkɛləmˈnæn ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Penutian language or group of languages historically spoken by the Miwok people of California.
language of the Miwok people

Examples

The Moquelumnan language is an important part of the Miwok people's cultural heritage.
Several dialects of the Moquelumnan language were spoken across central California.
Linguists have worked to document the Moquelumnan language before it became extinct.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Miwok - a member of a Native American people of California

Etymology

From Moquelumnes, an early ethnonym used in California, combined with 'language'.
