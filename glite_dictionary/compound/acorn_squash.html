acorn squash

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪˌkɔrn skwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈeɪkɔːn skwɒʃ / (BrE)
A type of winter squash with a hard, ridged rind and yellow flesh, shaped like an acorn.
type of winter squash

Examples

She roasted an acorn squash with herbs and olive oil for dinner.
You can use acorn squash as a substitute for pumpkin in soups.
The farmers' market had piles of fresh acorn squash in autumn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squash - a family of vegetables with fleshy fruit, often eaten as food

a ribbed winter squash with sweet flesh

Examples

You can find acorn squash in most grocery stores during the autumn months.
