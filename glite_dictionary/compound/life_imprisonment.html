life imprisonment

1. NOUN

/ laɪf ɪmˈprɪznmənt / (AmE), / laɪf ɪmˈprɪznmənt / (BrE)
the punishment of being kept in prison for the rest of a person's life
prison sentence lasting for life
legal

Examples

The judge sentenced him to life imprisonment for his crimes.
She campaigned against life imprisonment for juvenile offenders.
After the trial, the defendant received life imprisonment without the possibility of parole.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life sentence - a sentence of imprisonment for the remainder of one's life
