steel production

1. NOUN

/ stiːl prəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / stiːl prəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the process of making steel from pig iron or other iron-containing materials, usually in large industrial facilities
process of making steel from pig iron

Examples

The country's economy relies heavily on steel production for exports.
Recent advances in steel production have improved efficiency and reduced environmental impact.
After the new plant opened, steel production in the region increased significantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steelmaking - the manufacture of steel from pig iron or scrap
