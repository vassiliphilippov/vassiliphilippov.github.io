historical root

1. NOUN

/ hɪˈstɔrɪkəl ruːt / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪkəl ruːt / (BrE)
the origin or basis of something in history; the point or source from which something developed over time
origin or basis in history

Examples

Scholars traced the historical root of the ritual to ancient agricultural practices.
Many modern traditions have a historical root in religious ceremonies from centuries ago.
Understanding the historical root of the conflict helps explain its current complexity.

Synset Examples

The concept of dhimmitude has historical roots in the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: origin - the point or place where something begins or is created
  • Synonym: foundation - the basis or groundwork of something
