soft pretzel

1. NOUN

/ sɔft ˈprɛtsəl / (AmE), / sɒft ˈprɛtsl / (BrE)
a type of pretzel made from soft, doughy bread, typically baked and served warm, often with coarse salt on top
a warm, soft-bread pretzel

Examples

We bought a delicious soft pretzel from the street vendor in New York.
She sprinkled extra salt on her soft pretzel before eating it.
At the fair, the aroma of freshly baked soft pretzels filled the air.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pretzel - a crisp, baked bread snack shaped into a knot or stick
