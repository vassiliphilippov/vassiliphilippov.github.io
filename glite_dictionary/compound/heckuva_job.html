heckuva job

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛkəvə dʒɑb / (AmE), / ˈhɛkəvə dʒɒb / (BrE)
An excellent or impressive job; outstanding work.
outstanding job; impressive work
informal

Examples

You did a heckuva job cleaning that garage; it looks amazing now.
The team put in a heckuva job to finish the project ahead of schedule.
He gave a heckuva job presenting his research to the audience.

Synset Examples

She did a heckuva job organizing the event.
She did a helluva job on the presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helluva job - an impressive job; outstanding work (slang)

Etymology

Informal contraction of 'heck of a job,' where 'heck' is a mild euphemism for 'hell.'
