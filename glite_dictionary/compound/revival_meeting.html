revival meeting

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈvaɪ.vəl ˈmit̬.ɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈvaɪ.vəl ˈmiː.tɪŋ / (BrE)
a religious service held to reinvigorate faith and encourage religious conversion or renewed commitment, often characterized by enthusiastic preaching and singing
a religious service to renew faith

Examples

Hundreds gathered at the revival meeting to listen to the passionate sermon.
The community organized a revival meeting to reinvigorate spiritual interest among its members.
Old hymns echoed through the tent during the lively revival meeting on Sunday night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revival - the act of becoming active or popular again
