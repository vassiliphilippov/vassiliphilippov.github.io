historical gem

1. NOUN

/ hɪˈstɔrɪkəl dʒɛm / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪkəl dʒɛm / (BrE)
a place of special historical importance, beauty, or value
notable historical site or treasure

Examples

The city is full of historical gems waiting to be discovered by visitors.
Experts consider the restored castle a true historical gem of the region.
Hidden in the countryside, the old monastery is a historical gem often overlooked by tourists.

Synset Examples

The ancient fort of Kangla is a historical gem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: landmark - an important or historic building or place
