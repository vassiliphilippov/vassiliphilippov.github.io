wumao troll

1. NOUN

/ wuːˈmaʊ troʊl / (AmE), / wuːˈmaʊ trəʊl / (BrE)
an internet user, often from China, who posts comments online supporting the Chinese government, usually as part of a coordinated effort
pro-government Chinese internet troll

Examples

Many social media platforms struggle to deal with wumao trolls spreading propaganda.
Some discussions became heated when wumao trolls flooded the comment section.
Journalists often receive messages from wumao trolls whenever they post about China.

Synset Examples

He dismissed the criticism as coming from wumao trolls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troll - someone who posts inflammatory or provocative messages online

Etymology

From the Chinese 'wǔ máo' (五毛, meaning 'fifty cents'), referring to claims that such commenters are paid fifty cents per post.
