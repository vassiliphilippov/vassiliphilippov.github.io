incendiary device

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈsɛndiˌɛri dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛndɪəri dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
a weapon or bomb designed to start fires through the use of chemical substances
explosive designed to start fires

Examples

The police safely disposed of an incendiary device placed near the gas station.
During the war, soldiers encountered several incendiary devices used to destroy supplies.
An incendiary device can cause massive damage if deployed in a crowded area.

Synset Examples

The incendiary device was found in an abandoned building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firebomb - a bomb designed to cause fires; synonym of incendiary device

Etymology

From Latin 'incendium' (conflagration) + English 'device'.
