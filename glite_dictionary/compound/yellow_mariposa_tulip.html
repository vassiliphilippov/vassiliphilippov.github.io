yellow mariposa tulip

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ məˌripoʊsə ˈtuːlɪp / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ məˌrɪpəʊsə ˈtjuːlɪp / (BrE)
a wildflower native to the California coastal ranges, having clusters of large, deep yellow, bell-shaped flowers on slender stems
California wildflower with yellow bell-shaped flowers

Examples

The yellow mariposa tulip blooms in the spring along the California hillsides.
We spotted several yellow mariposa tulips during our hike along the coastal range.
The yellow mariposa tulip is valued for its striking bell-shaped blossoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Calochortus luteus - scientific name for the yellow mariposa tulip
  • Synonym: mariposa lily - a group of North American wildflowers with showy flowers
