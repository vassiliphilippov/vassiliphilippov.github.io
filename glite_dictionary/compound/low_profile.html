low profile

1. NOUN

/ loʊ ˈproʊfaɪl / (AmE), / ləʊ ˈprəʊfaɪl / (BrE)
a deliberate state or manner of avoiding attention or publicity; keeping oneself or one's activities from being noticed
deliberate avoidance of public attention

Examples

During the investigation, the company chose to maintain a low profile to avoid media scrutiny.
After the scandal, she kept a low profile for several months.
It's wise to adopt a low profile when moving to a new neighborhood.

Synset Examples

he was never one to keep a low profile

Related Terms

  • Antonym: high profile - a state of attracting much attention or publicity
