police boat

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis boʊt / (AmE), / pəˈliːs bəʊt / (BrE)
a boat operated by the police, especially for patrolling harbors or coastal areas and enforcing maritime law
a police-operated patrol boat

Examples

The harbor was closely monitored by a police boat patrolling the area day and night.
A police boat responded quickly to the distress call from the capsized vessel.
During the festival, a police boat ensured maritime safety and enforced all regulations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patrol boat - a small naval vessel for patrol duties
  • Degree Lower: fireboat - a boat equipped with firefighting apparatus
