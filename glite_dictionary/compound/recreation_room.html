recreation room

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃən ruːm / (AmE), / ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃən ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a house or building equipped for relaxation and informal entertainment, often containing games or leisure activities
room for relaxation and informal entertainment

Examples

They turned the basement into a recreation room with a pool table and dartboard.
Every Friday night, friends gather in the recreation room to play board games.
The apartment complex includes a spacious recreation room available for residents' use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: game room - room equipped for playing games as a form of recreation
  • Degree Lower: living room - a common room in a house used for relaxing and social activities
