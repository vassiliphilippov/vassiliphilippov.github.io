Forbidden City

1. PROPN

/ fərˈbɪdən ˈsɪti / (AmE), / fəˈbɪdn ˈsɪti / (BrE)
the former imperial palace in Beijing, China, which was closed to most people for centuries and is now a museum
former imperial palace in Beijing, China

Examples

The Forbidden City attracts millions of tourists each year who marvel at its architecture and history.
During the Ming and Qing dynasties, only the royal family could enter the Forbidden City.
The vast complex of the Forbidden City contains nearly a thousand buildings spread over 180 acres.

Examples

The Forbidden City was once off-limits to ordinary citizens for centuries.
