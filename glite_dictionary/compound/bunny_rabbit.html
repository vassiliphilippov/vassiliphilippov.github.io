bunny rabbit

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌni ˌræbɪt / (AmE), / ˈbʌni ˌræbɪt / (BrE)
an informal and affectionate term for a rabbit, especially a young one
informal term for a young rabbit
informal

Examples

The children were excited to see a bunny rabbit hopping across the field.
She has a soft toy that looks just like a bunny rabbit.
During spring, we often find a bunny rabbit in our garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rabbit - a small mammal with long ears, typically found in burrows

Etymology

From 'bunny', a diminutive of 'bun', and 'rabbit'; commonly used in children's English.
