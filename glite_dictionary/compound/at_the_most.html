at the most

1. ADV

/ ət ðə moʊst / (AmE), / ət ðə məʊst / (BrE)
used to say that a number or amount is the highest possible and not more than this
not more than; maximum limit

Examples

We waited fifteen minutes at the most before our table was ready.
You should invite twenty people at the most to keep the event manageable.
This repair should cost fifty dollars at the most, if nothing else goes wrong.

Synset Examples

spend at most $20 on the lunch

Related Terms

  • Antonym: at the least - used to say a number or amount is the lowest possible
