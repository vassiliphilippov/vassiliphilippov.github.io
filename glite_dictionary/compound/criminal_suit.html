criminal suit

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪmɪnəl suːt / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmɪnəl suːt / (BrE)
a legal case brought against an individual or entity for violating criminal law
legal case for a criminal offense
legal

Examples

The company is facing a criminal suit for violating environmental regulations.
After the police investigation, a criminal suit was filed against the suspect.
Unlike a civil case, a criminal suit can result in imprisonment if the accused is found guilty.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: civil suit - a lawsuit for a non-criminal matter, such as disputes between individuals
