ultraviolet source

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌltrəˈvaɪələt sɔrs / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈvaɪələt sɔːs / (BrE)
a device or object that emits ultraviolet radiation as a form of illumination
device emitting ultraviolet radiation

Examples

A mercury vapor lamp is a common type of ultraviolet source used in laboratories.
Some reptiles require an ultraviolet source in their enclosures for proper health.
The research team calibrated the ultraviolet source before beginning the experiment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ultraviolet lamp - a lamp designed to emit ultraviolet light
