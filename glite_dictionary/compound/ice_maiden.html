ice maiden

1. NOUN

/ aɪsˈmeɪdən / (AmE), / aɪsˈmeɪdn̩ / (BrE)
a woman who is emotionally cold, distant, or unresponsive
emotionally cold or reserved woman

Examples

She was often called an ice maiden by her colleagues because she never showed any emotion.
Despite her reputation as an ice maiden, she deeply cared for her friends.
The media portrayed the tennis champion as an ice maiden on and off the court.

Synset Examples

The ice maiden never showed her emotions in public.
She was known as the ice maiden for her reserved nature.
Even the ice maiden smiled when she received flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice queen - a woman who is cold and unemotional
