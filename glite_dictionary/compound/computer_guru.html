computer guru

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjutər ˈɡʊru / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈɡʊruː / (BrE)
an expert or highly knowledgeable person in computers and computing
expert in computers

Examples

If you have trouble with your laptop, just ask the computer guru in the IT department.
Everyone relies on James, the local computer guru, for tech support.
She became known as the office computer guru after fixing the network issue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expert - a person with comprehensive knowledge or skill in a particular area
  • Antonym: novice - a person new to and inexperienced in a job or situation

Etymology

From 'computer' and Sanskrit 'guru' (teacher or expert), entered English as a term for an expert or mentor.
