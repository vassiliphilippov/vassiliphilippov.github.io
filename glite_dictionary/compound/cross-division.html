cross-division

1. NOUN

/ ˌkrɔs dɪˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / ˌkrɒs dɪˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
classification according to more than one attribute or criterion at the same time
classification by multiple attributes

Examples

The researchers used cross-division to analyze patterns in the data set.
A cross-division of the products was performed by both category and price range.
By applying cross-division, they compared the results based on gender and age groups.

Synset Examples

the cross-classification of cases was done by age and sex

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cross-classification - classification using multiple attributes simultaneously
