sweet moment

1. NOUN

/ swit ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / swiːt ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
a brief period filled with affection, tenderness, or pleasure, often cherished or memorable
brief cherished or tender experience

Examples

They shared a sweet moment while watching the sunset together on the beach.
The photograph captured a sweet moment between the mother and her newborn baby.
She will always treasure that sweet moment when her childhood friend surprised her.

Synset Examples

We shared a sweet moment during our last dance at the wedding.
The couple shared a sweet moment, with him calling her wifie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tender moment - a brief period characterized by feelings of gentleness or affection
