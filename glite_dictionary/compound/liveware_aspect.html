liveware aspect

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪvˌwɛr əˈspɛkt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪvˌwɛə əˈspɛkt / (BrE)
the human component or factor within a technology system, as distinct from hardware and software
human component in technology systems

Examples

Our project is designed to improve the liveware aspect of digital workflows in the company.
When discussing system failures, experts often focus on the liveware aspect—the ways people interact with the technology.
Training programs are essential for addressing the liveware aspect of cybersecurity.

Synset Examples

Our team focuses on the liveware aspect of technology.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: software - programs and other operating information used by a computer
  • Antonym: hardware - the physical parts of a computer or device

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'hardware' and 'software,' combining 'live' (referring to people) and 'ware.'
