to rush to the crapper

1. VERB

/ rʌʃ tə ðə ˈkræpər / (AmE), / rʌʃ tʊ ðə ˈkræpə / (BrE)
(slang) To go quickly to the bathroom, usually because of an urgent need to relieve oneself.
hastily go to the bathroom (slang)
slang

Examples

After eating that spicy food, he had to rush to the crapper in the middle of dinner.
She barely made it through the meeting without needing to rush to the crapper.
He woke up feeling sick and immediately rushed to the crapper.

Synset Examples

He rushed to the crapper after eating too much.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: run to the bathroom - to go quickly to the bathroom, often in haste
  • Synonym: use the toilet - to go to the bathroom for relief

Etymology

'Crapper' is a slang term for toilet, deriving from the name of Thomas Crapper, an English plumber associated with early flush toilets.
