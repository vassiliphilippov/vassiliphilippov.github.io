atomic number 91

1. NOUN

/ əˌtɑmɪk ˈnʌmbɚ naɪn.ti wʌn / (AmE), / əˌtɒmɪk ˈnʌmbə naɪn.ti wʌn / (BrE)
the chemical element protactinium, a short-lived radioactive metallic element formed from uranium and disintegrating into actinium and then into lead
the element protactinium (Pa), atomic number 91
