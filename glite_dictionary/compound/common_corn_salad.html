common corn salad

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən kɔrn səˈlæd / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən kɔːn ˈsæləd / (BrE)
A small leafy plant (Valerianella locusta) cultivated for use as a salad green and sometimes grown as a pot herb; also found as a weed.
a salad green; Valerianella locusta

Examples

The chef added fresh common corn salad to the spring greens mix.
You can grow common corn salad in your garden for an early harvest of tender leaves.
Common corn salad is sometimes found growing wild as a weed in fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lamb's lettuce - another common name for Valerianella locusta
