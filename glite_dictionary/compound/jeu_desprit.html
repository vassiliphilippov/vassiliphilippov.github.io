jeu d'esprit

1. NOUN

/ ʒu deɪˈspri / (AmE), / ʒø dɛˈspri / (BrE)
a witty or clever remark or piece of writing, often displaying light-hearted intelligence or playfulness
witty remark or clever writing

Examples

Her new column is a delightful jeu d'esprit filled with puns and clever observations.
The author's jeu d'esprit charmed critics and readers alike.
He penned a jeu d'esprit in response to the formal invitation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: witticism - a witty remark
  • Synonym: quip - a clever or witty remark

Etymology

From French, literally 'play of the mind/spirit'.
