grubby t-shirt

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrʌbi ˈti ˌʃɜrt / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌbi ˈti ː ʃɜːt / (BrE)
a t-shirt that is dirty or looks unwashed and neglected
dirty, unkempt t-shirt

Examples

He felt self-conscious about wearing his grubby t-shirt to the interview.
She threw her grubby t-shirt into the laundry basket as soon as she got home.
A pile of grubby t-shirts was left on the floor after the soccer match.

Synset Examples

He wore a grubby t-shirt to the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dirty shirt - a shirt that is unclean
