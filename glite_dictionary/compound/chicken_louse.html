chicken louse

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkən laʊs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn laʊs / (BrE)
A louse that is parasitic on chickens and other poultry.
louse parasitic on poultry

Examples

The farmer discovered a chicken louse infestation in his flock.
Regular cleaning of the coop can help prevent chicken louse problems.
The veterinarian recommended a treatment specifically for chicken louse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: louse - a small, wingless parasitic insect
