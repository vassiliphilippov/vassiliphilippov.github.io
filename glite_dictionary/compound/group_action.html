group action

1. NOUN

/ ɡruːp ˈækʃən / (AmE), / ɡruːp ˈækʃən / (BrE)
an action performed collectively by a group of people
action taken by a group

Examples

The protest was a coordinated group action demanding change from the government.
A successful group action requires planning and cooperation among all members.
The company faced a group action from employees over unfair working conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collective action - shared or coordinated action taken by a group
