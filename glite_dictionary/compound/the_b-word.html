the b-word

1. NOUN

/ ði ˈbiː wɝːd / (AmE), / ði ˈbiː wɜːd / (BrE)
a euphemism used to refer to the word 'bitch', which is considered vulgar and offensive
euphemism for 'bitch'
informal offensive vulgar
