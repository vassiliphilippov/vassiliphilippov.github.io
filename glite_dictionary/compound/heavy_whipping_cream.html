heavy whipping cream

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛvi ˈwɪpɪŋ kriːm / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi ˈwɪpɪŋ kriːm / (BrE)
cream with a high fat content, typically 48% or more, used for whipping and in cooking.
cream with high fat content (48%+) for whipping

Examples

You need heavy whipping cream to make a rich, fluffy whipped topping for desserts.
The recipe calls for one cup of heavy whipping cream to ensure a creamy sauce.
If you can't find heavy whipping cream, substitute with double cream for similar results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double cream - rich cream with a high fat content, similar to heavy whipping cream
  • Degree Lower: light cream - cream with a lower fat content than heavy whipping cream
