rose-tinged

1. ADJ

/ ˈroʊz tɪndʒd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊz tɪndʒd / (BrE)
having or showing a slight tint or coloration of rose or pink
slightly tinted with rose color

Examples

The sky was rose-tinged at dawn, casting a warm glow over the city.
She wore a dress in a beautiful rose-tinged shade that complemented her complexion.
The old photographs had a rose-tinged hue, giving them a nostalgic feel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pink - a pale red color
