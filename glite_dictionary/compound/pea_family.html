pea family

1. NOUN

/ ˈpi ˌfæməli / (AmE), / ˈpiː ˌfæməli / (BrE)
a large family of plants, including trees, shrubs, vines, and herbs, that bear bean pods, known scientifically as Fabaceae or Leguminosae
family of bean pod-bearing plants

Examples

The pea family includes many important crops such as beans, lentils, and peas.
Acacia trees are members of the pea family and are known for their feathery leaves.
The flowers of the pea family often have a distinctive butterfly-like shape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legume - a plant or fruit of the pea family
