bean eater

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːn ˌiːtər / (AmE), / ˈbiːn ˌiːtə / (BrE)
a person who eats a vegetarian or particularly frugal diet
person following a vegetarian or frugal diet

Examples

My uncle has been a bean eater ever since he gave up meat for health reasons.
As a self-proclaimed bean eater, she enjoys finding new vegetarian recipes to try.
They laughed and called him a bean eater because of his simple, plant-based meals.

Synset Examples

He joked that he was a bean-eater at heart.
The bean-eater in the group suggested a vegetarian dish.
