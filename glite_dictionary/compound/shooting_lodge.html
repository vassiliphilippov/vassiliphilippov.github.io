shooting lodge

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃuːtɪŋ lɑːdʒ / (AmE), / ˈʃuːtɪŋ lɒdʒ / (BrE)
A small country house used by hunters as accommodation during the shooting season.
country house for hunters

Examples

The guests stayed at a luxurious shooting lodge in the Scottish Highlands.
He inherited an old shooting lodge that his family had owned for generations.
During the autumn, the shooting lodge was always full of visitors eager for the season's hunt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hunting lodge - country house used by people on hunting trips
