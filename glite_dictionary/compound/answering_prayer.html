answering prayer

1. NOUN

/ ˈænsɚɪŋ ˈprɛr / (AmE), / ˈɑːnsərɪŋ ˈprɛə / (BrE)
bringing about the fulfillment of a hope or request, especially one made earnestly or in prayer
fulfillment of a hope or prayer

Examples

The rain finally came, answering prayer and bringing relief to the dry fields.
The medicine's effectiveness was described as answering prayer for desperate patients.
Her safe return from the accident seemed like an answering prayer to her worried family.

Synset Examples

The rain finally came, answering farmers' prayers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miracle - an extraordinary event attributed to divine intervention
