bench warmer

1. NOUN

/ bɛntʃ ˈwɔrmər / (AmE), / bɛntʃ ˈwɔːmə / (BrE)
(informal, sports) A substitute player who rarely or never takes part in a game.
rarely used substitute player (sports)
informal

Examples

After joining the team, he spent most of the season as a bench warmer.
Despite practicing hard every week, she remained a bench warmer for most games.
The coach finally gave the longtime bench warmer a chance to play in the last match.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: substitute - a player who replaces another during a game
  • Antonym: starter - a player who begins the game on the field or court
