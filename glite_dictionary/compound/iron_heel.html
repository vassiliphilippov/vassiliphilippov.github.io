iron heel

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪɚn hiːl / (AmE), / ˈaɪən hiːl / (BrE)
An instrument of torture designed to heat or crush the foot and leg, often made of iron.
instrument of torture for foot and leg

Examples

The prisoner was subjected to the agonies of the iron heel during the interrogation.
Historical accounts describe the iron heel as a device used to inflict brutal punishment on captives.
In the exhibit, a replica of an iron heel reveals the cruel ingenuity of medieval torturers.
