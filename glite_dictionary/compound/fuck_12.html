fuck 12

1. X

/ fʌk twɛlv / (AmE), / fʌk twɛlv / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) An expression used to show strong dislike or hostility toward the police or law enforcement authorities.
slang, vulgar expression against police
slang vulgar

Examples

During the rally, people started chanting fuck 12 in unison.
He wore a shirt with fuck 12 printed boldly across the front.
Social media posts with fuck 12 trended during the protest.

Synset Examples

It's a common phrase in the community to say 'fuck 12'.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuck the police - slang phrase expressing hostility toward police
  • Synonym: ACAB - acronym meaning 'All Cops Are Bastards', used in anti-police contexts

Etymology

'12' is slang for the police, possibly originating from the police radio code '10-12' (visitors present) or '12' as a code for law enforcement officers. 'Fuck' is used in the vulgar imperative sense.
