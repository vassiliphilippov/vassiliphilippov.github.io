skittle alley

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪt̬l ˌæli / (AmE), / ˈskɪtl ˌæli / (BrE)
a long, narrow lane along which a ball is rolled toward skittles (pins) in the game of skittles
lane for playing skittles

Examples

The old pub still has a skittle alley at the back, where locals play every weekend.
He set up the pins at the end of the skittle alley before taking his turn.
Many traditional English inns used to feature a wooden skittle alley as a popular attraction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowling alley - a lane or building for bowling

Etymology

From 'skittle' (quoit or pin in a game) + 'alley' (narrow passage or lane).
