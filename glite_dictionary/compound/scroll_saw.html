scroll saw

1. NOUN

/ skroʊl sɔ / (AmE), / skrəʊl sɔː / (BrE)
a power saw with a fine, narrow blade used for cutting intricate curves in wood, metal, or other materials
power saw for intricate curved cutting

Examples

The craftsman used a scroll saw to cut delicate patterns for the wooden ornament.
A scroll saw is ideal for making intricate curves that other saws can't handle.
She purchased a new scroll saw for her woodworking projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jigsaw - a power tool with a reciprocating blade used for cutting curves
