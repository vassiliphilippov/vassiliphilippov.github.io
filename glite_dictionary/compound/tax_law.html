tax law

1. NOUN

/ tæks lɔ / (AmE), / taks lɔː / (BrE)
the body of laws and regulations governing the assessment and collection of taxes
laws governing taxation
legal

Examples

She is an expert in tax law and advises corporate clients on compliance issues.
Recent changes in tax law have affected small business owners.
Many lawyers choose to specialize in tax law due to its complexity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxation - the system or process of collecting taxes by the government
  • Degree Lower: tax avoidance - the legal use of laws to minimize tax liability
  • Antonym: criminal law - the body of laws dealing with crimes and their punishment
