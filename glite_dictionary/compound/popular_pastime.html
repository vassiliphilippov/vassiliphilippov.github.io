popular pastime

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑpjələr ˈpæstaɪm / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊlə ˈpɑːstaɪm / (BrE)
an activity or hobby that is widely enjoyed by many people
widely enjoyed hobby or activity

Examples

Reading novels is still a popular pastime despite the rise of digital entertainment.
Gardening has become a popular pastime in recent years, especially among city dwellers.
In many countries, soccer is not just a sport but a popular pastime for all ages.

Synset Examples

Playing Warcrack has become a popular pastime among teens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hobby - an activity done regularly in one's leisure time for pleasure
  • Synonym: pastime - an activity that someone does regularly for enjoyment rather than work; a hobby
