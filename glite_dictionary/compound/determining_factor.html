determining factor

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈtɜrmɪnɪŋ ˈfæktər / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪnɪŋ ˈfæktə / (BrE)
a fact or element that decisively influences the outcome of a situation
decisive influencing element

Examples

The price of the house was the determining factor in their decision to buy it.
Experience is often a determining factor when selecting candidates for the job.
Weather conditions can be a determining factor in whether or not a flight is delayed.

Synset Examples

education is an important determinant of one's outlook on life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deciding factor - a factor that causes a certain result to occur

2. NOUN

/ dɪˈtɝːmɪnɪŋ ˈfæktɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪnɪŋ ˈfæktə / (BrE)
a fact, condition, or argument that decisively influences the outcome of something
decisive influence or cause

Examples

Price was the determining factor in their decision to buy the house.
The weather can often be a determining factor in outdoor sports events.
Her skills and experience were the determining factor in her successful job application.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decisive factor - a factor that makes the final difference to an outcome
  • Synonym: main reason - principal reason for something happening
