military science

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
the study of the principles, methods, and conduct of warfare and military operations
study of warfare principles and methods

Examples

She decided to study military science at the academy to understand the strategies of warfare.
Advances in military science have dramatically changed the nature of conflict in the modern world.
Many leaders rely on the advice of experts trained in military science to guide their defense policies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warfare - engagement in or the activities involved in war or conflict
