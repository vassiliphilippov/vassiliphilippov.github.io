food manufacturer

1. NOUN

/ fuːd ˌmæn.jəˈfæk.tʃɚ / (AmE), / fuːd ˌmæn.jʊˈfæk.tʃər / (BrE)
a person or company that produces food products on a large scale, typically for commercial distribution
producer of food products

Examples

The food manufacturer is launching a new line of organic snacks this year.
Strict health regulations must be followed by every food manufacturer.
After years of working as a chef, she became a successful food manufacturer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: producer - a person or company that makes goods for sale
