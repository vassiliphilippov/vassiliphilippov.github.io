a lot about

1. X

/ ə ˈlɑt əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ə ˈlɒt əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
a large amount of knowledge or information concerning a particular subject
much knowledge about something

Examples

She seems to know a lot about classical music.
He talked a lot about his travels during the meeting.
You need to learn a lot about the topic before taking the exam.

Synset Examples

That bloke knows a lot about cars.
She knows a lot about the history of art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: know a lot about - to have much knowledge of a subject
  • Degree Lower: a little about - a small amount of knowledge of a subject
