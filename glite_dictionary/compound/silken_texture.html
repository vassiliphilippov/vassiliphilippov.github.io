silken texture

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlkən ˈtɛkstʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlkən ˈtɛkstʃə / (BrE)
A surface quality that is extremely smooth and soft, resembling silk.
smooth, soft surface quality

Examples

The silken texture of her scarf made it a pleasure to wear.
Chefs strive to achieve a silken texture when preparing creamy soups.
He ran his fingers over the silken texture of the polished wood.

Synset Examples

She admired the silken texture of the chocolate mousse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silky - smooth and soft like silk
