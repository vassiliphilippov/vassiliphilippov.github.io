person of color

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːsən əv ˈkʌlɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsən əv ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
a person who is not considered white or of European descent, especially in contexts discussing social or racial identity
non-white person; social identity term

Examples

The organization is dedicated to supporting every person of color on campus.
As a person of color, he often faced discrimination in his industry.
Many scholarships are designed to help a person of color achieve higher education.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: person - an individual human being
  • Antonym: white person - a person of European descent, traditionally considered white
