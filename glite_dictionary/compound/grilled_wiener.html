grilled wiener

1. NOUN

/ ɡrɪld ˈwiːnɚ / (AmE), / ɡrɪld ˈviːnə / (BrE)
A wiener sausage that has been cooked on a grill, often served in a bun as a hot dog.
sausage cooked on a grill
american

Examples

She put a grilled wiener in a bun and added mustard for lunch.
At the cookout, everyone enjoyed a grilled wiener fresh from the barbecue.
Would you like your grilled wiener with onions and relish?

Synset Examples

He grilled a wiener for the barbecue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot dog - a cooked sausage served in a split roll, often grilled or boiled

Etymology

From 'grilled' (cooked by direct heat) and 'wiener', derived from German 'Wiener' (Viennese sausage).
