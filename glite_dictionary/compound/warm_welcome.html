warm welcome

1. NOUN

/ wɔrm ˈwɛlkəm / (AmE), / wɔːm ˈwɛlkəm / (BrE)
a friendly and enthusiastic reception or greeting
friendly reception or greeting

Examples

The guests received a warm welcome when they arrived at the village.
Her warm welcome put everyone at ease right away.
After his long journey, he was grateful for the warm welcome from old friends.

Synset Examples

She knocked on the door, hoping for a warm welcome.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cold welcome - an unfriendly or unenthusiastic reception
  • Synonym: cordial reception - a warm and heartfelt greeting
