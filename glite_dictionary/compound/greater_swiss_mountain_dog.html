Greater Swiss Mountain dog

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪtər swɪs ˈmaʊntən dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtə swɪs ˈmaʊntɪn dɒɡ / (BrE)
a large breed of working dog from Switzerland, known for its strength and tricolor coat, and the largest of the four Swiss mountain dog breeds
large Swiss working dog breed

Examples

The Greater Swiss Mountain dog excels at pulling carts and guarding livestock in the Alps.
Because of its gentle temperament, the Greater Swiss Mountain dog is a popular family companion.
Despite its size, the Greater Swiss Mountain dog is known for being affectionate and loyal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bernese Mountain dog - a Swiss breed of large, long-haired working dog
  • Degree Lower: Appenzeller - a medium-sized Swiss breed of mountain dog
  • Degree Lower: Entlebucher Mountain dog - the smallest of the four Swiss mountain dog breeds

Etymology

Named after Switzerland and its Alpine regions; 'Greater' distinguishes it from related Swiss breeds.
