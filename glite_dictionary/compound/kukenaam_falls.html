Kukenaam Falls

1. PROPN

/ ˌkuːkəˈnɑm fɔlz / (AmE), / ˌkuːkəˈnɑːm fɔːlz / (BrE)
a prominent waterfall located on the Kukenaam River in Venezuela, known for its great height and natural beauty
a major waterfall in Venezuela
