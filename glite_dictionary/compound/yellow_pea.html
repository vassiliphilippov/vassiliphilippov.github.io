yellow pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ piː / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ piː / (BrE)
A bushy herb native to the western United States, having yellow, pea-like flowers.
western U.S. bushy herb with yellow pea-like flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with the bright blossoms of the yellow pea in early summer.
Botanists studied the yellow pea for its drought resistance.
A yellow pea bush grew wild along the hiking trail.
