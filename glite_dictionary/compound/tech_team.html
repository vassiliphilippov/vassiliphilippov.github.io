tech team

1. NOUN

/ tɛk tim / (AmE), / tɛk tiːm / (BrE)
a group of people responsible for handling technology-related tasks within an organization
group handling technology tasks

Examples

The tech team celebrated the successful launch of the new software platform.
Whenever we have computer issues, the tech team responds quickly to resolve them.
A strong tech team can give a company a significant competitive advantage.

Synset Examples

The tech team celebrated the launch of the new masterbit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: IT department - a division responsible for information technology systems
