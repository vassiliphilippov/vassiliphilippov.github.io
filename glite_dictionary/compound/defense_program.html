defense program

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛns ˌproʊɡræm / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns ˌprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
an official plan or system organized to protect a country from its enemies, especially through military means
plan or system for national defense

Examples

The government allocated more funds to its defense program in response to rising security threats.
Critics argue that the new defense program is too costly and lacks transparency.
During the briefing, officials outlined the main goals of the nation's defense program.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military program - a program organized for military objectives
  • Antonym: civilian program - a program not related to military defense
