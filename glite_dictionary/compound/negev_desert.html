Negev Desert

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɛɡɛv ˈdɛzɚt / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡɛv ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
a large desert region in southern Israel, known for its arid climate and distinctive landscapes
desert region in southern Israel

Examples

The Negev Desert covers more than half of Israel's land area.
Many unique plant species have adapted to the harsh conditions of the Negev Desert.
Tourists come to hike and explore the ancient sites scattered throughout the Negev Desert.

Etymology

From Hebrew *Negev*, meaning 'south' or 'dry.'
