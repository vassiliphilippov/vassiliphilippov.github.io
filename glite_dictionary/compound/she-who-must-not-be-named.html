She-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named

1. NOUN

/ ʃi hu mʌst nɑt bi neɪmd / (AmE), / ʃi hu mʌst nɒt bi neɪmd / (BrE)
a humorous or playful reference to a woman whose name is deliberately avoided, usually because it is considered taboo or sensitive
person (woman) whose name is deliberately avoided
informal

Examples

Whenever Mark mentioned his ex-boss, he used She-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named instead of her actual name.
At family gatherings, my aunt is only ever referred to as She-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named.
To avoid drama in the group chat, everyone called her She-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named.

Synset Examples

He jokingly referred to her as She-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named.
In our circle, we avoid discussing She-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named.
We don't speak of her; it's She-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: He-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named - a person (typically a man) whose name is deliberately avoided

Etymology

Popularized by the 'Harry Potter' series as a variant of 'He-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named', referring to Voldemort.
