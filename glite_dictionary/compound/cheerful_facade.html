cheerful facade

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪɚfəl fəˈsɑd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪəfʊl fəˈsɑːd / (BrE)
an outwardly happy appearance that conceals true feelings or emotions
an outwardly happy appearance

Examples

Despite his cheerful facade, he was feeling very anxious about the exam.
Anna maintained a cheerful facade at work even though she was going through a difficult time.
It's not always easy to see past someone's cheerful facade to what they're really feeling.

Synset Examples

Behind her cheerful facade, she was struggling inside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mask - a way of hiding one's true feelings
  • Synonym: front - a false or misleading outward appearance
