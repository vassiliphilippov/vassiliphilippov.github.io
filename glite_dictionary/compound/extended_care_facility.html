extended care facility

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈstɛndɪd kɛr fəˈsɪlɪti / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛndɪd keə fəˈsɪlɪti / (BrE)
a medical institution that provides long-term care for people recovering from illness or needing ongoing medical support
medical institution for prolonged care
medical

Examples

After her surgery, Mary stayed at an extended care facility while she recovered.
The hospital transferred John to an extended care facility for several weeks of rehabilitation.
Many elderly patients benefit from the specialized services provided by an extended care facility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nursing home - residential facility for elderly or disabled people who need constant care
