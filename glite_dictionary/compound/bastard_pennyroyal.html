bastard pennyroyal

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæstərd ˈpɛniˌrɔɪəl / (AmE), / ˈbɑːstəd ˈpɛniˌrɔɪəl / (BrE)
an aromatic plant of the eastern United States, resembling true pennyroyal but belonging to a different genus
aromatic plant resembling pennyroyal

Examples

Wild bastard pennyroyal grows abundantly in the woodlands of the eastern United States.
The leaves of bastard pennyroyal have a distinct, minty aroma used in herbal remedies.
Botanists are studying the ecological benefits of bastard pennyroyal in native plant gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pennyroyal - an aromatic plant of the mint family, Mentha pulegium or Hedeoma pulegioides
