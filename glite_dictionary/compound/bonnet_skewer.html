bonnet skewer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑː.nət ˈskjuː.ər / (AmE), / ˈbɒn.ɪt ˈskjuː.ə / (BrE)
a thin, pointed rod used for securing decorations, especially on bonnets, or for holding food items together during cooking
thin rod for fastening decorations or food

Examples

She carefully pinned the flowers to her hat with a bonnet skewer.
The bonnet skewer kept the slices of meat neatly stacked for roasting.
A decorative bonnet skewer added a touch of elegance to the presentation.

Synset Examples

He secured the decorations with a bonnet skewer.
The chef used a bonnet skewer to hold the dish together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skewer - a long, thin pin used to hold meat together while cooking
