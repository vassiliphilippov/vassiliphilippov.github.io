sausage hound

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɒsɪdʒ haʊnd / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsɪdʒ haʊnd / (BrE)
An informal term for a dachshund, a dog breed with a long body and short legs.
informal term for a dachshund dog
informal

Examples

Their new pet is a mischievous sausage hound who loves to dig in the garden.
At the park, several people stopped to pet the adorable sausage hound in her bright red sweater.
He couldn't help laughing when his sausage hound chased after the bigger dogs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dachshund - a breed of dog with a long body and short legs
