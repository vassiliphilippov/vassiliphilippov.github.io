e-mail address

1. NOUN

/ ˈiː ˌmeɪl əˈdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈiː ˌmeɪl əˈdrɛs / (BrE)
a unique identifier consisting of a username and domain used to send and receive electronic mail messages
identifier for sending/receiving email

Examples

Please enter your e-mail address to receive the newsletter.
He changed his e-mail address after getting too much spam at the old one.
Make sure you type your e-mail address correctly to avoid errors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: email - system for sending electronic messages

Etymology

Formed from 'e-mail' (electronic mail) and 'address'.
