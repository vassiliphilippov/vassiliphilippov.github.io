desk dictionary

1. NOUN

/ dɛsk ˈdɪkʃənɛri / (AmE), / dɛsk ˈdɪkʃənəri / (BrE)
a concise dictionary of a size suitable for use at a desk, typically designed to be easily handled and referred to.
compact, handy-sized dictionary

Examples

She reached for her desk dictionary to check the spelling of the word.
A desk dictionary is handy when you need a quick definition while writing.
His battered old desk dictionary sat beside his computer for easy reference.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: unabridged dictionary - a dictionary containing all the words of a language, with full details
  • Degree Lower: pocket dictionary - a very small dictionary designed to fit in a pocket
