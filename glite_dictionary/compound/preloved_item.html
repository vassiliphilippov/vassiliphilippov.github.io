preloved item

1. NOUN

/ priˈlʌvd ˈaɪtəm / (AmE), / priˈlʌvd ˈaɪtəm / (BrE)
an object that has been previously owned and cared for, typically sold secondhand
previously owned and cared-for object

Examples

You can often find a unique preloved item in secondhand shops or online marketplaces.
Buying a preloved item is an eco-friendly choice that supports sustainability.
The preloved item arrived in excellent condition, just as described by the seller.

Synset Examples

She bought a preloved book for her collection.
The preloved dress fit her perfectly.
They sell preloved items at the thrift store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: used item - an object that has been previously owned or utilized
  • Antonym: new item - an object that has not been previously owned or used

Etymology

Formed from 'pre-' (before) + 'loved' (cherished), referring to something cherished in its previous ownership.
