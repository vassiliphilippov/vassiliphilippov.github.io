image scanner

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪmɪdʒ ˌskænər / (AmE), / ˈɪmɪdʒ ˌskanə / (BrE)
an electronic device that converts a physical image into a digital form for input to a computer
device converting images to digital form

Examples

The office uses a high-speed image scanner to digitize documents quickly.
She placed the photograph on the image scanner to create a digital copy.
Many libraries have an image scanner available for public use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scanner - a device that examines or reads data or images for input into a computer
