at church

1. ADP

/ ət tʃɝtʃ / (AmE), / ət tʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
in or at a church, especially when attending a religious service or activity
in a place of Christian worship

Examples

My parents spend Sunday mornings at church with the rest of the family.
She volunteers at church every week, helping with the children's group.
We met at church last Christmas during the holiday choir rehearsal.

Synset Examples

My grandmother is at church every time the doors are open.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in church - inside a church building, especially during a service
