local department

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.kəl dɪˈpɑrt.mənt / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl dɪˈpɑːt.mənt / (BrE)
a permanent division within a local government responsible for specific functions or services
division within local government

Examples

The city's local department of public works is responsible for road maintenance and waste management.
Every local department must submit its annual budget to the city council for review.
She works for the local department of parks and recreation, organizing community events.
