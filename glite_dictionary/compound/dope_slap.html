dope slap

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊp ˌslæp / (AmE), / ˈdəʊp ˌslap / (BrE)
An act of slapping oneself or someone else on the head as an expression of frustration, realization, or to prompt alertness.
slap on the head for frustration or realization
informal

Examples

Feeling foolish after his mistake, he gave himself a quick dope slap.
A sudden dope slap of inspiration hit him during the meeting.
She laughed and gave her brother a playful dope slap for forgetting his keys again.

Synset Examples

He gave himself a dope slap for forgetting the meeting.

The coach's dope slap motivated him to improve his performance.
