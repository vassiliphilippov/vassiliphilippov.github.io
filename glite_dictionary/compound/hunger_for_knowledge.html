hunger for knowledge

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌŋɡɚ fər ˈnɑlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhʌŋɡə fə ˈnɒlɪdʒ / (BrE)
a strong and persistent desire or craving to learn and acquire new information or understanding
strong desire to learn

Examples

Her hunger for knowledge led her to read hundreds of books in a single year.
Teachers love to see students with a genuine hunger for knowledge in their classrooms.
His hunger for knowledge was evident in the diverse subjects he chose to explore.

Synset Examples

His hunger for knowledge drove him to study all night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thirst for knowledge - a strong desire to learn
