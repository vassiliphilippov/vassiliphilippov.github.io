feather boa

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛðər ˈboʊə / (AmE), / ˈfɛðə ˈbəʊə / (BrE)
A long, fluffy scarf made of feathers, often worn as a glamorous fashion accessory.
long, fluffy feather scarf

Examples

She draped the feather boa around her shoulders for the costume party.
A bright pink feather boa added the perfect touch to her 1920s outfit.
He bought a feather boa as a playful gift for his friend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boa - a long, thin scarf of feathers or fur
