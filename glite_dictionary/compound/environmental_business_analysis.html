environmental business analysis

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəl ˈbɪznəs əˌnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntl ˈbɪznəs əˌnæləsɪs / (BrE)
the systematic process of identifying environmental factors affecting a business, assessing their impact, and developing strategies to address them
systematic analysis of business environmental factors

Examples

The company conducted an environmental business analysis to identify potential risks related to climate policies.
A thorough environmental business analysis can reveal both threats and opportunities in the external market.
Before expanding overseas, management emphasized the importance of an environmental business analysis to guide their decision-making.

Synset Examples

There are also many other business environment reforms that have taken place over the past two years, having their genesis in the IDB's business environment analysis of nearly a decade ago, but these will require another article to document.
