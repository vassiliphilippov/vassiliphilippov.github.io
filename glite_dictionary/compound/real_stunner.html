real stunner

1. NOUN

/ ˌriːəl ˈstʌnər / (AmE), / ˌrɪəl ˈstʌnə / (BrE)
A person or thing that is exceptionally attractive or impressive.
someone or something very attractive
informal

Examples

Everyone stopped to look when she walked in—she was a real stunner.
That new sports car is a real stunner on the road.
He was speechless when he saw the painting; it's a real stunner.

Synset Examples

That dress was a real stunner at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stunner - a person or thing of striking beauty or excellence
