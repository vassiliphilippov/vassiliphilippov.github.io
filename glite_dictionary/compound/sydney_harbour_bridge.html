Sydney Harbour Bridge

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪdni ˈhɑrbər brɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɪdni ˈhɑːbə brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a large steel arch bridge in Sydney, Australia, spanning Sydney Harbour and linking the central business district with the North Shore
famous steel bridge in Sydney, Australia

Examples

Many tourists take photos of the Sydney Harbour Bridge from the Opera House steps.
The Sydney Harbour Bridge is often called the 'Coathanger' because of its distinctive arch shape.
Every New Year's Eve, fireworks light up the Sydney Harbour Bridge in a spectacular display.
