hop hornbeam

1. NOUN

/ hɑp ˈhɔrnˌbim / (AmE), / hɒp ˈhɔːnˌbiːm / (BrE)
a tree of the genus Ostrya, resembling hornbeams and bearing fruiting clusters similar to hops
tree resembling a hornbeam with hop-like fruit

Examples

In autumn, the hop hornbeam produces fruit clusters that resemble hops used in brewing.
The forest is home to several hop hornbeams, easily recognized by their flaky bark.
A hop hornbeam can be a valuable addition to a wildlife-friendly garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hornbeam - a deciduous tree of the genus Carpinus, known for its hard wood

Etymology

Named for its hop-like fruit clusters and resemblance to hornbeam trees.
