mainland China

1. PROPN

/ ˈmeɪnˌlænd ˈtʃaɪ.nə / (AmE), / ˈmeɪnlənd ˈtʃaɪ.nə / (BrE)
The part of China that is under the direct administration of the People's Republic of China, excluding Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.
the territory of China excluding Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan

Examples

My family immigrated to the United States from mainland China in the 1980s.
Many international companies have offices both in Hong Kong and mainland China.
Travelers often need special permits to go from Hong Kong to mainland China.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: China - the country in East Asia, officially the People's Republic of China
