porkpie hat

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔrkˌpaɪ ˌhæt / (AmE), / ˈpɔːkˌpaɪ ˌhæt / (BrE)
a man's hat with a low, flat crown and a short, turned-up brim
hat with low, flat crown and short brim

Examples

He tipped his porkpie hat as he entered the room, catching everyone's attention.
The jazz musician was easily recognized by his signature porkpie hat and sunglasses.
Vintage shops often carry porkpie hats popularized in the mid-20th century.

Etymology

Named for its resemblance to a traditional British pork pie.
