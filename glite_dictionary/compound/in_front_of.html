in front of

1. ADP

/ ɪn ˈfrʌnt ʌv / (AmE), / ɪn ˈfrʌnt ɒv / (BrE)
in a position ahead of or facing something or someone; in the presence of
ahead of or facing something/someone

Examples

He parked his car in front of the house so it would be easy to unload groceries.
She gave her acceptance speech in front of a large audience.
Please do not use your phone in front of the children.
