hulk of a man

1. NOUN

/ hʌlk əv ə mæn / (AmE), / hʌlk ɒv ə mæn / (BrE)
a man who is very large and physically strong, often impressively so
very large, strong man

Examples

The football team hired a hulk of a man to boost their defense.
Everyone stared as the hulk of a man entered the tiny café, ducking under the low doorway.
Despite his reputation as a hulk of a man, he was gentle with the children.

Synset Examples

The hulk of a man easily moved the furniture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: giant - a person of great size and strength

Etymology

From 'hulk', originally meaning a large, heavy ship or object, figuratively referring to a person's imposing size.
