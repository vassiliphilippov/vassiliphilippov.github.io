genus Pandion

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs pænˈdaɪ.ən / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs pænˈdaɪ.ən / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Pandionidae, comprising the ospreys.
the genus of ospreys in Pandionidae

Examples

Scientists classify the osprey under the genus Pandion.
The genus Pandion is unique among raptors for having only one living species.
Debates continue about whether there are subspecies within the genus Pandion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: osprey - a large fish-eating bird of prey (Pandion haliaetus)

Etymology

From New Latin 'Pandion', after the mythological Greek king Pandion.
