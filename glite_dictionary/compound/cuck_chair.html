cuck chair

1. NOUN

/ kʌk tʃɛr / (AmE), / kʌk tʃɛː / (BrE)
(informal, derogatory) A chair symbolizing submissiveness or humiliation, especially used as an internet meme or idiom for self-reflection, realization, or acknowledging one's mistakes or failures.
derogatory term for a chair symbolizing submissiveness
informal slang offensive

Examples

After losing the argument, he joked about spending time in the cuck chair.
Social media often uses the cuck chair to mock moments of public embarrassment or regret.
The meme of the cuck chair spread quickly among online communities.

Synset Examples

The concept of a cuck chair has become a meme lately.
Sitting in the cuck chair made him realize his mistakes.

Etymology

From 'cuck', a derogatory slang shortening of 'cuckold', plus 'chair'.
