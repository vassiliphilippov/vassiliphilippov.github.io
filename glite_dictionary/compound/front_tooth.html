front tooth

1. NOUN

/ frʌnt tuθ / (AmE), / frʌnt tuːθ / (BrE)
A tooth located at the front of the mouth, typically used for cutting food; usually refers to an incisor or canine.
tooth at the front of the mouth

Examples

The dentist said my front tooth needed a small filling.
She chipped her front tooth while playing basketball.
His front tooth has a noticeable gap.

Synset Examples

his malocclusion was caused by malposed anteriors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incisor - a tooth adapted for cutting, at the front of the mouth
