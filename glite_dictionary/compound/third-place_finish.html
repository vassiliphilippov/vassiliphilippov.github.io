third-place finish

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɝd pleɪs ˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈθɜːd pleɪs ˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
the achievement of coming in third position in a competition, race, or event
coming in third in a competition

Examples

The runner was thrilled with her third-place finish in the national marathon.
After a close contest, the team secured a third-place finish in the tournament.
His third-place finish was celebrated by the whole school.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: second-place finish - coming in second in a competition
  • Degree Higher: first-place finish - coming in first in a competition
