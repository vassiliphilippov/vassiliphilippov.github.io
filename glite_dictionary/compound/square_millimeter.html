square millimeter

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr ˈmɪlɪˌmitər / (AmE), / skwɛː ˈmɪlɪˌmiːtə / (BrE)
a unit of area equal to a square with sides of one millimeter in length
unit of area (one millimeter square)

Examples

The chip's active region measures just 10 square millimeters in total area.
Please specify the required thickness in micrometers and the area in square millimeters.
A tiny crack, less than one square millimeter, compromised the device.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: square centimeter - unit of area equal to a square with sides of one centimeter
  • Degree Lower: square micrometer - unit of area equal to a square with sides of one micrometer

Etymology

Formed from 'square' + 'millimeter', with 'millimeter' from French 'millimètre', ultimately from Latin 'mille' (thousand) + 'metrum' (measure).
