air gun

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈɛə ˌɡʌn / (BrE)
A gun that fires projectiles using compressed air or gas instead of gunpowder.
gun that uses compressed air

Examples

He used an air gun to shoot targets in his backyard.
The museum displayed an antique air gun from the 19th century.
All competitors must use a regulation air gun in the tournament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: BB gun - a type of air gun that shoots small metal balls called BBs
