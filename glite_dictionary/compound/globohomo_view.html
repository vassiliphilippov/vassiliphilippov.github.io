globohomo view

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡloʊboʊˈhoʊmoʊ vjuː / (AmE), / ˌɡləʊbəʊˈhəʊməʊ vjuː / (BrE)
A controversial term, often used disparagingly, to describe the belief in or promotion of global cultural homogeneity at the expense of local traditions.
belief in global cultural homogeneity
informal slang offensive

Examples

Many activists reject the globohomo view as an attack on cultural diversity.
The author's essay criticizes what he calls the globohomo view dominating international institutions.
Debates about the globohomo view have become common in some online forums.

Synset Examples

He believes that globohomo views threaten local traditions.

Etymology

Blend of 'global', 'homogenization', and 'homosexual', originally internet slang; now used in various political contexts.
