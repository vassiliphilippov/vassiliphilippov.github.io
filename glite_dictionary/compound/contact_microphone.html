contact microphone

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑn.tækt ˈmaɪ.krəˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tækt ˈmaɪ.krəˌfəʊn / (BrE)
A microphone designed to be in direct contact with a sound source or a resonating surface, used to pick up vibrations rather than airborne sound.
microphone for direct contact with surfaces

Examples

The engineer attached a contact microphone to the guitar to capture the resonance directly from the wood.
Using a contact microphone, you can record subtle vibrations that ordinary microphones might miss.
She experimented with a contact microphone to amplify the sound of a ticking watch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microphone - an instrument for converting sound waves into electrical signals
