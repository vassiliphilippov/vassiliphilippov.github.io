oral smear

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔr.əl smɪr / (AmE), / ˈɔː.rəl smɪə / (BrE)
a laboratory test in which a sample of material obtained from the mouth is spread on a slide for microscopic examination
sample from mouth for testing
medical

Examples

The doctor ordered an oral smear to check for signs of infection.
An oral smear can help diagnose certain fungal or bacterial diseases of the mouth.
Laboratory technicians prepared the oral smear for microscopic analysis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: throat swab - sample collected from the throat for medical testing
