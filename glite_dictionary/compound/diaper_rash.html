diaper rash

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪpər ræʃ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪ.pə ræʃ / (BrE)
A skin irritation or inflammation, typically affecting a baby’s thighs and buttocks, caused by prolonged exposure to a wet or soiled diaper.
skin irritation in infants from diapers

Examples

The pediatrician recommended using a soothing cream to treat the baby's diaper rash.
Frequent diaper changes can help prevent diaper rash in infants.
After a week of home remedies, her son's diaper rash finally improved.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dermatitis - inflammation of the skin
