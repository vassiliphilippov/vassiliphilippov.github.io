Imperial Manila

1. PROPN

/ ɪmˈpɪriəl məˈnɪlə / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪəriəl məˈnɪlə / (BrE)
The historical area of Manila, Philippines, particularly during the period of Spanish colonial rule, known for its significant government, cultural, and religious buildings.
historic district in Manila, Philippines

Examples

Many important events in Philippine history took place in Imperial Manila.
During colonial times, Imperial Manila was the center of Spanish authority in the islands.
Tourists often explore the churches and forts of Imperial Manila.

Synset Examples

I visited Imperial Manila during my trip to the Philippines.
