petit dejeuner

1. NOUN

/ pəˌtiː deɪˈʒəːnər / (AmE), / pəˌtiː deɪˈʒɜːnə / (BrE)
A light breakfast, usually consisting of a roll and coffee or tea, typical of French cuisine.
light French-style breakfast

Examples

The hotel serves a traditional petit dejeuner with fresh croissants and coffee every morning.
She enjoyed her petit dejeuner at the café while reading the newspaper.
A typical French petit dejeuner might include a baguette, butter, and jam with tea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: continental breakfast - a light breakfast typically featuring bread, pastries, and beverages

Etymology

From French 'petit déjeuner' meaning 'little breakfast'.
