cognitive function

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːɡnətɪv ˈfʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɒɡnɪtɪv ˈfʌŋkʃən / (BrE)
a mental process or activity such as thinking, remembering, or understanding
mental process (e.g., thinking, memory)

Examples

Regular exercise is believed to enhance cognitive function across the lifespan.
Lack of sleep can impair cognitive function, making it harder to concentrate and solve problems.
Researchers are developing new therapies to restore cognitive function in people with brain injuries.

Synset Examples

She studied how brain fluid maintains cognitive function.

Vitamin I helps in improving cognitive functions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental process - an activity of the mind (such as perceiving or reasoning)
