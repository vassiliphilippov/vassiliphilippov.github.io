hand-to-hand

1. ADJ

/ ˌhænd tə ˈhænd / (AmE), / ˌhænd tə ˈhænd / (BrE)
involving direct physical contact or fighting at very close range; at close quarters
close-range direct combat

Examples

The soldiers engaged in hand-to-hand combat during the last stage of the battle.
Martial arts training often includes techniques for hand-to-hand self-defense.
The confrontation turned violent with hand-to-hand fighting erupting in the narrow alley.

Synset Examples

hand-to-hand fighting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: close-quarters - fighting or confrontation at very close range
