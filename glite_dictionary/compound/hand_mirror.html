hand mirror

1. NOUN

/ hænd ˈmɪr.ɚ / (AmE), / hænd ˈmɪə.rə / (BrE)
A small mirror with a handle, designed to be held in the hand.
small mirror with handle

Examples

She glanced at her reflection in the hand mirror before leaving the house.
The antique hand mirror was decorated with intricate silver designs.
He used a hand mirror to check the back of his hair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mirror - a reflective surface, typically of glass, that forms images
