magnetic recorder

1. NOUN

/ mægˈnɛtɪk rɪˈkɔrdər / (AmE), / mæɡˈnɛtɪk rɪˈkɔːdə / (BrE)
a device used for recording sound, images, or data on magnetic media such as tape or disks
device for recording on magnetic media

Examples

The journalist used a magnetic recorder to capture the entire interview for later transcription.
Many documentaries from the 1970s were preserved on magnetic recorders before digital technology became widespread.
A faulty magnetic recorder can cause audio dropouts or loss of important data.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tape recorder - a device for recording and playing back sound on magnetic tape
  • Degree Higher: digital recorder - a device for recording sound or data in digital format
