Mycelia Sterilia

1. NOUN

/ maɪˌsiːliə stɜːˈrɪliə / (AmE), / mɪˌsiːlɪə stɛˈrɪlɪə / (BrE)
An order of imperfect fungi characterized by the absence of a known spore-producing stage.
order of fungi without known spore stage

Examples

Researchers found several species belonging to Mycelia Sterilia in the forest soil.
The fungi categorized as Mycelia Sterilia do not reproduce by known spore formation.
Identification of Mycelia Sterilia relies heavily on their mycelial characteristics.

Etymology

From New Latin, meaning 'sterile mycelia', referring to the lack of spore production.
