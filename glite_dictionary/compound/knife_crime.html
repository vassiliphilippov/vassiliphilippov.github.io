knife crime

1. NOUN

/ naɪf kraɪm / (AmE), / naɪf kraɪm / (BrE)
violent crime in which a knife is used as a weapon, especially referring to patterns or increases in such offenses
crime involving the use of knives

Examples

The government is taking urgent action to address the growing problem of knife crime in urban areas.
After several high-profile incidents, community leaders launched campaigns to reduce knife crime.
The police released statistics showing a significant drop in knife crime over the past year.

Synset Examples

The city is facing a rise in knifecrime this year.
Local authorities are cracking down on knifecrime in the area.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gun crime - crime involving the use of firearms
