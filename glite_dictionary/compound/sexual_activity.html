sexual activity

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl ækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl ækˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)

actions involving sexual intercourse or arousal

Examples

Some diseases can be transmitted through unprotected sexual activity.
