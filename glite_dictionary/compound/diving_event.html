diving event

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪvɪŋ ɪˌvɛnt / (AmE), / ˈdaɪvɪŋ ɪˌvɛnt / (BrE)
An athletic competition in which participants perform dives into water, often judged for technique and difficulty.
athletic diving competition

Examples

The men's diving event drew a large crowd at the Olympic stadium.
She trained for years to qualify for the national diving event.
Each diving event is judged based on the difficulty and execution of the dives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diving - the sport or activity of jumping into water headfirst
