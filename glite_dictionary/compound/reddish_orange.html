reddish orange

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈɔːrɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
A shade of orange that is closer to red than to yellow.
a color between red and orange

Examples

The sunset cast a reddish orange glow across the sky.
She chose a reddish orange paint for the living room walls.
A reddish orange flower stood out among the greenery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vermilion - a brilliant red or scarlet color with a slight orange tint
  • Degree Lower: orange - the color between red and yellow in the spectrum
  • Degree Higher: red - the color at the end of the visible spectrum next to orange
