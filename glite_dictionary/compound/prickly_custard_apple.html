prickly custard apple

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪkli ˈkʌstəɹd ˈæpəl / (AmE), / ˈprɪkli ˈkʌstəd ˈæpəl / (BrE)
a small tropical American tree (Annona muricata) bearing large, spiny fruit with juicy, slightly acidic pulp, also known as soursop
tropical tree bearing spiny, acidic fruit

Examples

The prickly custard apple thrives in warm, humid climates throughout Central and South America.
She made a refreshing juice from the ripe prickly custard apple she bought at the market.
The spiky skin of the prickly custard apple protects its soft, tangy flesh inside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soursop - the fruit of Annona muricata; another name for prickly custard apple
