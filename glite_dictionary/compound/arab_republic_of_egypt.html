Arab Republic of Egypt

1. PROPN

/ əˌræb rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈiːdʒɪpt / (AmE), / əˌræb rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈiːdʒɪpt / (BrE)
the official name of Egypt, a country in northeastern Africa, formerly known as the United Arab Republic until 1971; noted for its ancient civilization.
official name of Egypt (country in Africa)

Examples

The Arab Republic of Egypt is rich in historical sites like the Pyramids of Giza and the Valley of the Kings.
After 1971, the country officially became known as the Arab Republic of Egypt.
Many international agreements are signed with the formal name, Arab Republic of Egypt.

  • Synonym: Egypt - a country in northeastern Africa
