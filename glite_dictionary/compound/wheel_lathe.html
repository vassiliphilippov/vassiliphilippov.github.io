wheel lathe

1. NOUN

/ wil leɪθ / (AmE), / wiːl leɪθ / (BrE)
a lathe designed for turning and shaping the wheels of railway cars and locomotives
lathe for railway wheels

Examples

The old railway workshop still uses a wheel lathe to restore locomotive wheels.
Specialists operated the wheel lathe to ensure the wheels were perfectly balanced.
A wheel lathe is essential equipment for servicing train wheels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lathe - a machine for shaping metal, wood, or other material
