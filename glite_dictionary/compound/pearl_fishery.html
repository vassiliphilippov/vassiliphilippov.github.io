pearl fishery

1. NOUN

/ pɜrl ˈfɪʃəri / (AmE), / pɜːl ˈfɪʃəri / (BrE)
a place or business where pearl oysters are collected or harvested to obtain pearls
place where pearl oysters are harvested

Examples

The region was once famous for its pearl fishery, which attracted traders from distant lands.
Strict regulations govern every pearl fishery to protect the precious oyster beds.
The decline of the pearl fishery affected the local economy for many years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fishery - a place where fish are caught for commercial purposes
