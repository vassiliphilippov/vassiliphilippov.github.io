mental balance

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛntəl ˈbæl.əns / (AmE), / ˈmɛntəl ˈbæl.əns / (BrE)
A healthy state of mind characterized by emotional stability and good judgment.
stable, healthy state of mind

Examples

After months of stress, meditation helped her regain her mental balance.
Traveling alone for too long can sometimes affect your mental balance.
It is important to maintain mental balance during challenging times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sanity - the ability to think and behave in a normal and rational manner; sound mental health
  • Antonym: insanity - a state of being seriously mentally ill; madness
