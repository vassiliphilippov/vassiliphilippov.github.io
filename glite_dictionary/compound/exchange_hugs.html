to exchange hugs

1. VERB

/ ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ hʌɡz / (AmE), / ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ hʌɡz / (BrE)
(of two or more people) to hug each other, especially as a greeting or at parting.
embrace one another, usually in greeting or farewell

Examples

When the old friends met at the airport, they exchanged hugs and caught up on their lives.
After the ceremony, the team members exchanged hugs to celebrate their victory.
We exchanged hugs and said goodbye before boarding our separate trains.

Synset Examples

They exchanged hugs before parting with a playful 'gradoo'.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embrace - to put your arms around someone, especially as a sign of affection
