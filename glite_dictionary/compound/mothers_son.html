mother's son

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌðɚz sʌn / (AmE), / ˈmʌðəz sʌn / (BrE)
Every man or male person; used to refer to all men collectively.
every man or male person

Examples

Every mother's son in the village volunteered to help rebuild the community center.
She said that not a mother's son among them dared to speak up.
The coach made sure every mother's son ran five laps before practice ended.

Synset Examples

every mother's son who could walk was there

Related Terms

  • Synonym: father's son - A set phrase similar in form, referring to a male child
