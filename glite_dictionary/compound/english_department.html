English department

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ dɪˌpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
An academic department in a college or university that is responsible for teaching English language and literature, including English and American works.
university unit for English studies

Examples

She decided to major in English and spent most of her time in the English department.
The English department is organizing a poetry reading next Friday evening.
Several new professors joined the English department this semester to expand the range of courses offered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: literature department - the department responsible for teaching literature broadly
