faith cure

1. NOUN

/ feɪθ kjʊr / (AmE), / feɪθ kjʊə / (BrE)
a method of healing illnesses or injuries through prayer and belief in divine intervention, rather than medical treatment
healing by prayer and faith in God

Examples

The community turned to a faith cure after conventional medicine failed to help.
She believes that a faith cure can heal ailments that medicine cannot reach.
Some people attend special meetings hoping for a faith cure through collective prayer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faith healing - treatment of illness by spiritual or religious means, especially prayer
