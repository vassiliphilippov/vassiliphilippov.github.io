rainbow seaperch

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪnboʊ ˈsiːˌpɜrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈreɪnbəʊ ˈsiːˌpɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a species of marine fish with vibrant coloration, found along the Pacific coast of North America
Pacific coast marine fish

Examples

We spotted a rainbow seaperch near the kelp forest while snorkeling.
The rainbow seaperch is known for its striking blue and orange stripes.
Fishermen along the coast often catch rainbow seaperch during the spring.
