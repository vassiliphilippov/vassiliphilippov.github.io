social occasion

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊ.ʃəl əˈkeɪ.ʒən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃəl əˈkeɪ.ʒən / (BrE)
a formal or important event where people gather socially, such as a ceremony, celebration, or official gathering
formal social event or ceremony

Examples

She wore her finest dress for the social occasion at the embassy.
Weddings and graduations are special kinds of social occasions that families cherish.
The fundraiser was the largest social occasion of the year in the small town.

Synset Examples

the party was quite an affair
an occasion arranged to honor the president
a seemingly endless round of social functions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: event - an occurrence, especially one of importance
  • Synonym: gathering - an assembly or meeting of people
  • Antonym: private gathering - a non-public or intimate meeting
