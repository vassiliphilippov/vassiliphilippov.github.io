file cabinet

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪl ˌkæb.ə.nət / (AmE), / ˈfaɪl ˌkæb.ɪ.nət / (BrE)
a piece of office furniture with drawers or compartments for storing and organizing documents and papers
office furniture for storing documents

Examples

She opened the file cabinet to find last year's tax documents.
The office purchased a new file cabinet to store important client records.
Ben organizes his contracts alphabetically in a metal file cabinet next to his desk.
