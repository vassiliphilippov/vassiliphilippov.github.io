hot stuffed tomato

1. NOUN

/ hɑt stʌft təˈmeɪdoʊ / (AmE), / hɒt stʌft təˈmɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
A tomato that has been hollowed out, filled with a savory mixture, and briefly baked.
baked stuffed tomato dish

Examples

I ordered a hot stuffed tomato filled with cheese and herbs as my appetizer.
The recipe calls for baking each hot stuffed tomato for twenty minutes until the tops are golden brown.
She served a vegetarian hot stuffed tomato at the dinner party, and it was a huge hit.
