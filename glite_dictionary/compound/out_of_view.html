out of view

1. ADV

/ aʊt əv vjuː / (AmE), / aʊt əv vjuː / (BrE)
no longer visible; not within sight
no longer visible

Examples

The fox ran into the bushes and was soon out of view.
As the train sped away, it quickly disappeared out of view.
She waited until her friends were out of view before she started crying.

Synset Examples

the ship disappeared behind the horizon and passed out of sight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of sight - no longer visible
