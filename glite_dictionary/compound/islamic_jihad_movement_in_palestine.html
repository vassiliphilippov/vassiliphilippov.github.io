Islamic Jihad Movement in Palestine

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈslɑmɪk dʒɪˈhɑd ˈmuːvmənt ɪn pælˈɛstɪn / (AmE), / ɪˈslæmɪk dʒɪˈhɑːd ˈmuːvmənt ɪn pælˈɛstiːn / (BrE)
a militant Palestinian organization founded in 1979, advocating the creation of an Islamic state in Palestine and the destruction of Israel; smaller and more militant than Hamas
