little dudelet

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪtəl ˈduːdlət / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ˈdjuːdlət / (BrE)
an affectionate or playful term for a small or young boy
playful term for a young boy
informal

Examples

His little dudelet is already into video games.
She picked up her little dudelet and gave him a big hug.
The little dudelet ran across the playground, laughing loudly.

Synset Examples

Etymology

Formed from 'little' and diminutive 'dudelet' (from 'dude' + '-let', meaning small).
