financial analysis

1. NOUN

/ fəˈnænʃəl əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / faɪˈnænʃəl əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
The evaluation of a company's financial statements and performance to assess its efficiency, profitability, and financial stability.
evaluation of a company's finances

Examples

The accountant completed a thorough financial analysis before making recommendations to the board.
Investors often rely on financial analysis to determine the health and prospects of a company.
Her expertise in financial analysis made her an asset to the consulting firm.

Synset Examples

It was clear to the investment banker after reviewing the company's financial analysis that the annual loan renewal would be denied.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: financial statement - formal record of financial activities of a business
