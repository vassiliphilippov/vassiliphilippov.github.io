tiger snake

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪɡər sneɪk / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡə sneɪk / (BrE)
a highly venomous snake with brown and yellow banding, native to Australia and Tasmania
venomous Australian snake

Examples

The tiger snake is known for its potent venom and distinctive stripes.
During our hike in Tasmania, we spotted a tiger snake basking on a rock.
Farmers in southern Australia are cautious because tiger snakes can be found near water sources.
