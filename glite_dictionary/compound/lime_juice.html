lime juice

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪm ˌdʒus / (AmE), / ˈlaɪm ˌdʒuːs / (BrE)
the juice obtained from limes, often used as a beverage ingredient or flavoring
juice from limes

Examples

Squeeze fresh lime juice over the salad for a zesty flavor.
He ordered a glass of lime juice to cool off on the hot day.
Adding lime juice to the marinade helped tenderize the meat.

Related Terms
