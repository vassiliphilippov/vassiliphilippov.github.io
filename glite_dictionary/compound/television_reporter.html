television reporter

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛləˌvɪʒən rɪˈpɔrtər / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˌvɪʒən rɪˈpɔːtə / (BrE)
A journalist who reports news or events on television.
journalist reporting on TV

Examples

The television reporter stood outside city hall, ready to deliver the breaking news live.
As a television reporter, she often travels to cover major international events.
The mayor answered questions from a television reporter during the press conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newsreader - person who reads news on television or radio
  • Synonym: anchor - main presenter of a television news program
  • Synonym: correspondent - journalist reporting from a particular place or on a subject
