Indian grackle

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən ˈɡrækəl / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈɡrækəl / (BrE)
a glossy black Asiatic starling (Gracula religiosa), often trained to mimic human speech
a black Asiatic starling known for mimicking speech

Examples

The Indian grackle is famous for its ability to mimic a wide range of sounds, including human voices.
As a pet, the Indian grackle can learn to repeat simple words and phrases.
Bird watchers often travel to observe the striking plumage of the Indian grackle in its native habitat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hill mynah - another name for the Indian grackle, a starling known for speech mimicry
