Balochi language

1. NOUN

/ bəˈloʊtʃi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / bəˈləʊtʃi ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an Iranian language spoken mainly in Pakistan, Iran, and Afghanistan, with communities in parts of the Persian Gulf and Russia.
an Iranian language of South and Central Asia

Examples

Many people in western Pakistan speak Balochi language as their mother tongue.
The poet writes in both Persian and Balochi language, reflecting his heritage.
Linguists study the Balochi language to understand its relation to other Iranian languages.

Etymology

From Baloch, the ethnic group name, combined with 'language'.
