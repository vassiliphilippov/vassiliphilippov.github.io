tennis shot

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnɪs ʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs ʃɒt / (BrE)
an act of striking a tennis ball with a racket during play
act of hitting a tennis ball

Examples

She practiced her tennis shot for hours to improve her backhand.
His powerful tennis shot won the match at the final point.
The coach complimented her on the accuracy of her tennis shot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stroke - a single movement of hitting a ball in sports, especially tennis or golf
