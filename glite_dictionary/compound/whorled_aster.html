whorled aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜrld ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈwɜːld ˈæstə / (BrE)
a North American perennial plant with apparently whorled leaves and showy white, purple-tinged flowers
North American perennial plant with showy flowers

Examples

The whorled aster adds delicate color to woodland gardens in late summer.
We spotted several whorled asters blooming along the forest trail.
Botanists study the unique leaf arrangement of the whorled aster for identification.
