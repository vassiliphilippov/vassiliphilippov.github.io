axe handle

1. NOUN

/ æks ˈhændl̩ / (AmE), / æks ˈhændl̩ / (BrE)
the wooden or metal handle attached to the head of an axe, used for gripping and swinging the tool
handle of an axe

Examples

The old axe handle broke when he tried to chop the log.
She sanded down the axe handle to make it smooth and comfortable to hold.
Replacing the axe handle saved him from buying a whole new tool.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shaft - the long handle of a tool or weapon
