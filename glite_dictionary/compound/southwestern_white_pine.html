southwestern white pine

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaʊθˈwɛstərn waɪt paɪn / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈwɛstən waɪt paɪn / (BrE)
a medium-sized pine tree native to northwestern Mexico, with dark brown, furrowed bark when mature
pine tree native to northwest Mexico

Examples

The southwestern white pine is prized for its attractive wood and ornamental value.
Researchers are studying how drought affects the southwestern white pine in its native habitat.
I spotted a tall southwestern white pine while hiking in the mountains of northern Mexico.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white pine - a type of pine tree with pale wood and soft needles
