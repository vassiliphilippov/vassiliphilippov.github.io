orange hawkweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ ˈhɔkˌwid / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ˈhɔːkwiːd / (BrE)
A European hawkweed with bright orange-red flower heads, often considered a troublesome weed, especially as naturalized in northeastern North America.
a European plant with orange-red flowers

Examples

The hillside was covered in a vibrant mix of grasses and orange hawkweed.
Gardeners often struggle to control the spread of orange hawkweed in their lawns.
I recognized the wildflower by its bright color as orange hawkweed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hawkweed - any plant of the genus Hieracium
