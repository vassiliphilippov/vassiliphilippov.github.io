stadium jumping

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪdiəm ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsteɪdiəm ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport of riding horses in competitions over set courses designed to demonstrate skill in jumping over obstacles
equestrian sport involving horse jumping over obstacles

Examples

She excelled in stadium jumping and won several medals at national competitions.
The riders prepared their horses for the stadium jumping event at the equestrian show.
Proper training and technique are crucial for success in stadium jumping.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: show jumping - equestrian sport of jumping horses over obstacles in competition
