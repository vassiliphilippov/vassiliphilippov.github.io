thank offering

1. NOUN

/ θæŋk ˈɑːfɚɪŋ / (AmE), / θæŋk ˈɒfərɪŋ / (BrE)
An offering made to express gratitude, especially in religious ceremonies as a sign of thanks for blessings received.
offering given to express thanks

Examples

After the harvest, the villagers brought a thank offering to the temple.
He made a thank offering to show his gratitude for his recovery.
Traditionally, a thank offering was given after surviving a dangerous voyage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: offering - something offered, especially as a religious sacrifice or token of devotion
