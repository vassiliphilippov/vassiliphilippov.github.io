nut-leaved screw tree

1. NOUN

/ nʌt liːvd skruː triː / (AmE), / nʌt liːvd skruː triː / (BrE)
An East Indian shrub, often cultivated for its hairy leaves and orange-red flowers.
East Indian shrub with hairy leaves and orange-red flowers

Examples

The nut-leaved screw tree is popular in gardens because of its bright, attractive flowers.
Researchers collected samples from a nut-leaved screw tree growing near the village.
Her garden features a rare nut-leaved screw tree with unusually dense, hairy leaves.
