sheep dip

1. NOUN

/ ʃip dɪp / (AmE), / ʃiːp dɪp / (BrE)
a liquid pesticide mixture in which sheep are immersed to eliminate parasites such as lice and ticks
liquid pesticide bath for sheep

Examples

The farmer prepared the sheep dip before bringing the flock into the pen.
Regular use of sheep dip helps prevent infestations of lice and ticks on the animals.
Always wear protective gloves when handling sheep dip chemicals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pesticide - a substance used for destroying pests
  • Synonym: insecticide - a substance used for killing insects
