intelligence activity

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns ækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns ækˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
the operations involved in gathering, analyzing, and using information about an enemy or potential threats, often for military or security purposes
operation for gathering enemy information

Examples

The agency specializes in intelligence activity aimed at preventing cyber attacks.
During wartime, intelligence activity becomes crucial to national security.
He was recruited because of his expertise in intelligence activity overseas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: espionage - the practice of spying or using spies
  • Synonym: reconnaissance - military observation to gather information
