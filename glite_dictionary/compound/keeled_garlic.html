keeled garlic

1. NOUN

/ ˈkild ˈɡɑrlɪk / (AmE), / ˈkiːld ˈɡɑːlɪk / (BrE)
A Eurasian bulbous plant (Allium carinatum) with slender leaves and umbels of purple flowers, related to garlic.
Eurasian bulbous plant related to garlic

Examples

The garden was filled with keeled garlic, its purple blooms swaying gently in the breeze.
Botanists often study keeled garlic for its unique flowering structure and aroma.
She planted keeled garlic alongside other Allium species to attract bees.
