container vessel

1. NOUN

/ kənˈteɪnɚ ˈvɛsəl / (AmE), / kənˈteɪnə ˈvɛsəl / (BrE)
a large ship specifically designed to transport containerized cargoes
ship for transporting containerized cargo

Examples

The company recently invested in a new container vessel to expand its shipping fleet.
A container vessel can carry thousands of containers across international waters efficiently.
Delays at the port kept the container vessel anchored for several days.
