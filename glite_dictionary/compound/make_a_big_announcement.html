to make a big announcement

1. VERB

/ meɪk ə bɪɡ əˈnaʊnsmənt / (AmE), / meɪk ə bɪɡ əˈnaʊnsmənt / (BrE)
to declare or reveal important news publicly, often with formal or special emphasis
declare important news publicly

Examples

The company will make a big announcement tomorrow regarding the new product launch.
She was nervous because she had to make a big announcement at the family reunion.
Whenever politicians make a big announcement, people pay close attention to what they say.

Synset Examples

He’s about to make a big announcement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to announce - to make a public declaration about something
