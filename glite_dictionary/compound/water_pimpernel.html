water pimpernel

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˈpɪmpərnɛl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈpɪmpənɛl / (BrE)
a small aquatic plant of the genus Samolus, typically bearing white flowers and found in wet habitats
white-flowered aquatic plant (Samolus)

Examples

The delicate water pimpernel thrives along the edge of the stream.
Botanists discovered a patch of water pimpernel near the flooded meadow.
Her field guide helped her identify the small white flowers as water pimpernel.
