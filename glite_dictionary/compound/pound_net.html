pound net

1. NOUN

/ paʊnd nɛt / (AmE), / paʊnd nɛt / (BrE)
a large fishing trap consisting of nets arranged to guide fish into an enclosure, from which they cannot escape
type of fishing trap using nets

Examples

The fishermen checked their pound net every morning to collect their catch.
A pound net can be highly effective for catching migrating fish near the river mouth.
Environmental regulations restrict the placement of pound nets in certain bays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fish trap - a general device used to catch fish
