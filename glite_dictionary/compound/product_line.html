product line

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑː.dʌkt laɪn / (AmE), / ˈprɒd.ʌkt laɪn / (BrE)
a group of related products or merchandise offered by the same company under a single brand or category
range of related products from one company

Examples

The company expanded its product line to include organic foods.
They are famous for their premium skincare product line.
Our new product line targets young professionals seeking innovative kitchen gadgets.

Synset Examples

a nice line of shoes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: product range - the set of products made or sold by a company
