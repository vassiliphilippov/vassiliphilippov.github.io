Saran Wrap

1. NOUN

/ səˈræn ræp / (AmE), / səˈræn ræp / (BrE)
A thin, transparent plastic film used for wrapping food to keep it fresh.
thin plastic food wrap
american

Examples

Please cover the leftovers with Saran Wrap before putting them in the fridge.
She used Saran Wrap to keep the sandwiches fresh for lunch.
The recipe suggests sealing the bowl with Saran Wrap during the dough's first rise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plastic wrap - thin plastic film for sealing food

Etymology

Saran is a trademark, originally developed by Dow Chemical for polyvinylidene chloride film.
