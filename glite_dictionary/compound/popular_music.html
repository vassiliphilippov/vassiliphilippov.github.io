popular music

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑpjələr ˈmjuzɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊlə ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
Music that is liked by many people and often associated with contemporary trends, typically having a broad, mainstream appeal.
music with wide, mainstream appeal

Examples

The rise of popular music in the 20th century changed the way people experience art and entertainment.
She studies the impact of popular music on modern culture in her sociology class.
Many radio stations play only popular music from the past decade to attract younger listeners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pop music - a genre of popular music with a strong rhythmic element and simple melodies
  • Antonym: classical music - art music rooted in the traditions of Western culture
