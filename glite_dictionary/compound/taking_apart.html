taking apart

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪkɪŋ əˈpɑɹt / (AmE), / ˈteɪkɪŋ əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
the act of dismembering or cutting something, especially a body, into pieces; the removal of limbs.
the act of cutting something into pieces

Examples

The coroner described the gruesome taking apart of the body during the investigation.
Some criminals were notorious for their brutal taking apart of victims in historical accounts.
The story graphically detailed the taking apart of the statue piece by piece.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dismemberment - the act of cutting off the limbs of a person or animal
