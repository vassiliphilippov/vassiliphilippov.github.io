sweet cup

1. NOUN

/ swit kʌp / (AmE), / swiːt kʌp / (BrE)
the edible yellow fruit of the Jamaica honeysuckle (Passiflora laurifolia), native to tropical America.
edible yellow fruit of Jamaica honeysuckle

Examples

The sweet cup is prized in the Caribbean for its juicy, aromatic pulp.
Vendors at the market were selling freshly picked sweet cups alongside other tropical fruits.
She enjoyed tasting the sweet cup for the first time during her trip to Jamaica.
