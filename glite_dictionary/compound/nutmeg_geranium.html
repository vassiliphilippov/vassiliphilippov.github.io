nutmeg geranium

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ dʒəˈriːniəm / (BrE)
A type of geranium with round, fragrant leaves and small white flowers, often grown for its aromatic foliage.
geranium with fragrant round leaves and white flowers

Examples

The nutmeg geranium thrives in pots on a sunny windowsill.
She crushed a leaf of the nutmeg geranium to enjoy its spicy fragrance.
Gardeners often grow nutmeg geranium for both its beauty and its scent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geranium - a plant with lobed leaves and usually pink, white, or purple flowers
