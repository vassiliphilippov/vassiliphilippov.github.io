scatter pin

1. NOUN

/ ˈskæt̬ɚ pɪn / (AmE), / ˈskætə pɪn / (BrE)
a small decorative pin, often worn in groups of two or more, typically as an accessory on clothing
decorative pin worn in groups

Examples

She wore three delicate scatter pins on the collar of her blouse.
Vintage scatter pins are popular among jewelry collectors.
The jeweler designed a set of floral scatter pins for the spring collection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brooch - a decorative jewelry item designed to be attached to garments
