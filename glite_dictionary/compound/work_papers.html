work papers

1. NOUN

/ wɝk ˈpeɪ.pɚz / (AmE), / wɜːk ˈpeɪ.pəz / (BrE)
legal documents providing evidence or authorization required for employment, especially regarding foreign nationals or minors in certain countries.
documents required for employment
legal

Examples

You must bring your work papers to your first day on the job.
The company assists immigrants with obtaining the necessary work papers.
Without valid work papers, he was not allowed to begin employment.
