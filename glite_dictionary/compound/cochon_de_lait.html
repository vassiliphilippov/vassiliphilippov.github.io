cochon de lait

1. NOUN

/ koʊˌʃɒn də ˈleɪ / (AmE), / kəʊˌʃɒ̃ də ˈleɪ / (BrE)
A whole young piglet, especially one prepared for roasting.
young pig suitable for roasting

Examples

The chef prepared a traditional Cuban cochon de lait for the holiday feast.
For special occasions, they often roast a cochon de lait over an open fire.
The restaurant's signature dish is a slow-roasted cochon de lait served with seasonal vegetables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suckling pig - a young pig still feeding on its mother's milk, typically roasted whole

Etymology

From French 'cochon de lait' meaning 'milk pig' (suckling pig).
