baking chocolate

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪkɪŋ ˌtʃɑklət / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkɪŋ ˌtʃɒklət / (BrE)
pure unsweetened chocolate used in baking, icings, sauces, and candies
pure unsweetened chocolate for baking

Examples

She melted baking chocolate to make a rich, velvety cake frosting.
Always use baking chocolate rather than milk chocolate in this recipe for the right texture.
The bittersweet flavor of baking chocolate makes it perfect for decadent brownies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cooking chocolate - chocolate used in cooking, often interchangeable with baking chocolate
  • Degree Higher: bittersweet chocolate - dark chocolate with some sugar added, less sweet than semisweet but sweeter than baking chocolate
