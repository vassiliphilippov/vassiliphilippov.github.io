toy dog

1. NOUN

/ tɔɪ dɔɡ / (AmE), / tɔɪ dɒɡ / (BrE)
a very small dog belonging to a breed developed to be kept as a pet rather than for work or hunting
very small dog kept as a pet

Examples

Many people enjoy the companionship of a toy dog because of its small size and playful nature.
The family decided to adopt a toy dog instead of a larger breed due to their apartment's limited space.
At the show, several breeds competed in the toy dog category.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lapdog - a small pet dog that is often held in the lap
