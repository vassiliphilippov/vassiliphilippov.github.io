Wheatley elm

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːtli ɛlm / (AmE), / ˈwiːtli ɛlm / (BrE)
A cultivated variety of the English elm with upright branches and broader leaves, often used as an ornamental tree.
variety of English elm tree

Examples

The Wheatley elm is prized for its graceful, upright form in park landscapes.
Unlike other varieties, the Wheatley elm features broader leaves and more vertical branches.
Gardeners often choose the Wheatley elm for its resilience and decorative appeal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: English elm - a species of elm tree native to Europe, Ulmus procera
