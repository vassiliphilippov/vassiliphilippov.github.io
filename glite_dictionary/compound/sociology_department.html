sociology department

1. NOUN

/ soʊˈsiː.əˌlɑdʒi dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / səʊˈsɪ.əˌlɒdʒi dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
The division within a university or college responsible for teaching and research in sociology.
university division for sociology

Examples

She applied to join the sociology department at her local university.
The sociology department organized a seminar on urban communities this semester.
Research funding was increased for the sociology department after its recent achievements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: department - a division of a larger organization such as a university or business
