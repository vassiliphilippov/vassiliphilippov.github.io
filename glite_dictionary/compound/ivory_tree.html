ivory tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.vɚi triː / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.vəri triː / (BrE)
a tropical Asian tree with hard white wood and bark formerly used as a remedy for dysentery and diarrhea
tropical Asian tree with hard white wood

Examples

The bark of the ivory tree was once prized for its medicinal properties.
Carpenters value the wood from the ivory tree for its unusual hardness.
Researchers studied the ivory tree to rediscover its traditional uses in treating illness.
