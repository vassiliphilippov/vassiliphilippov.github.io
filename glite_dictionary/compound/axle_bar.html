axle bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈæksəl bɑɹ / (AmE), / ˈæksəl bɑː / (BrE)
An iron or metal bar serving as an axletree, typically forming the main shaft supporting the wheels of a vehicle or mechanism.
iron bar acting as an axletree

Examples

The old wagon's axle bar needed to be replaced due to excessive rust.
Engineers inspected the axle bar for stress fractures after the accident.
He forged his own axle bar for the custom-built carriage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: axle - the central shaft for a rotating wheel or gear
  • Synonym: axletree - a bar connecting the opposite wheels of a carriage or vehicle
