final boss

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪ.nəl bɑs / (AmE), / ˈfaɪ.nəl bɒs / (BrE)
the last and most challenging opponent or enemy that a player must defeat in a video game to complete the main objective or storyline
the last and hardest opponent in a video game

Examples

After ten hours of gameplay, we finally defeated the final boss and completed the story.
She watched a walkthrough to learn the best strategy for the final boss.
Many players find the final boss much harder than anything else in the game.

Synset Examples

Defeating the final boss was incredibly satisfying.
After hours of gameplay, I finally faced the final boss.
She strategized for days to beat the final boss.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: boss - a powerful enemy in a video game, often more difficult than regular enemies
