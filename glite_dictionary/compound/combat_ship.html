combat ship

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmˌbæt ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈkɒmbæt ʃɪp / (BrE)
a government ship designed and equipped for waging war
military ship used in warfare

Examples

The navy commissioned a new combat ship to enhance its maritime defense capabilities.
During the conflict, every combat ship in the fleet was deployed to the front lines.
The crew of the combat ship trained extensively to prepare for naval battles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warship - a ship built and primarily intended for combat
