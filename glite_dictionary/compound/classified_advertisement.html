classified advertisement

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæsɪˌfaɪd ˌædvərˈtaɪzmənt / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsɪfaɪd ˌædvəˈtaɪzmənt / (BrE)
A small advertisement placed in a newspaper or magazine, grouped with others of the same category, usually offering goods, jobs, or services.
small newspaper or magazine ad

Examples

He placed a classified advertisement in the local paper to sell his car.
Many job seekers browse the classified advertisements for employment opportunities.
The lost dog was reunited with its owner thanks to a classified advertisement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classified ad - short form of 'classified advertisement'
