bee orchid

1. NOUN

/ bi ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / biː ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
a European orchid (Ophrys apifera) whose flowers resemble bumble bees in shape and color
European orchid with bee-like flowers

Examples

The bee orchid is easily identified by its flower that mimics a bumble bee.
Conservationists are working to protect habitats where the bee orchid still thrives in the wild.
We spotted a single bee orchid blooming in the meadow during our nature walk.
