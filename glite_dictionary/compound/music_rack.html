music rack

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuː.zɪk ræk / (AmE), / ˈmjuː.zɪk ræk / (BrE)
a stand or support designed to hold sheets of printed music for musicians while playing an instrument
stand for holding sheet music

Examples

The pianist carefully arranged her sheets on the music rack before starting her performance.
Please make sure the music rack is secure so that the pages don't fall off.
He bought a new music rack to use during his violin practice sessions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: music stand - a support for holding sheet music, often adjustable in height and angle
