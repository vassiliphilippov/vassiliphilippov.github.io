gap between rich and poor

1. NOUN

/ ɡæp bətwin rɪtʃ ənd pʊr / (AmE), / ɡap bɪtwiːn rɪtʃ ənd pɔː / (BrE)
the economic and social disparity in wealth or income between the wealthy and the poor in a society
economic disparity between rich and poor

Examples

The gap between rich and poor has become a major political issue worldwide.
Many policies aim to narrow the gap between rich and poor in society.
Experts warn that the gap between rich and poor threatens social stability.

Synset Examples

The gap between rich and poor is widening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wealth gap - difference in wealth between groups or individuals
  • Synonym: income inequality - unequal distribution of income within a population
