power pack

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊ.ɚ ˌpæk / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə ˌpak / (BrE)
a device that converts or supplies electrical power at a required voltage for particular equipment
device supplying required electrical voltage

Examples

My laptop came with a universal power pack that works with any voltage.
He replaced the power pack in his camera to ensure it worked throughout the trip.
This medical device requires a special power pack to function properly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power supply - a source of electrical energy for a device
