heart pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrt ˌpi / (AmE), / ˈhɑːt ˌpiː / (BrE)
A woody perennial climbing plant, native to tropical regions of India, Africa, and America, with large, ornamental, balloon-like seed pods.
woody tropical climbing plant with balloon-like seed pods

Examples

The garden featured a trellis covered in heart pea vines, their balloon-shaped pods gently swaying in the breeze.
Botanists are studying the unique seed dispersal mechanisms of the heart pea.
In tropical climates, the heart pea is grown both for its ornamental value and its interesting seed pods.
