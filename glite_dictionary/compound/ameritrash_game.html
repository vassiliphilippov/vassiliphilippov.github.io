Ameritrash game

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪˌtræʃ ɡeɪm / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪˌtræʃ ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A board game characterized by strong themes, elaborate components, and elements of luck, often associated with American board game design.
thematic, luck-driven board game style
informal

Examples

Many players enjoy the immersive stories that an Ameritrash game can offer.
The club's latest event featured several classic Ameritrash games with elaborate miniatures.
Though some criticize them as flashy, an Ameritrash game usually provides a memorable experience.

Synset Examples

They love Ameritrash games for their flashy components.
I prefer strategy games over Ameritrash titles.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Eurogame - strategy-focused board games with less luck and simpler themes, typical of European design
  • Synonym: thematic game - board game focusing on narrative or theme

Etymology

Blend of 'America' and 'trash', coined in the board gaming community to describe American-style games emphasizing theme and spectacle.
