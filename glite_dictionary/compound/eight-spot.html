eight-spot

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪt.spɑt / (AmE), / ˈeɪt.spɒt / (BrE)
a playing card with eight pips or symbols on its face
playing card with eight pips

Examples

She drew the eight-spot of hearts and placed it on the pile.
In this game, the eight-spot is considered a lucky card.
He had two eight-spot cards in his hand at the end of the round.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eight - the number 8
  • Degree Higher: nine-spot - a playing card with nine pips on its face
  • Degree Lower: seven-spot - a playing card with seven pips on its face
