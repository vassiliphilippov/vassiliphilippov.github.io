stunning images

1. NOUN

/ ˈstʌnɪŋ ˈɪmɪdʒɪz / (AmE), / ˈstʌnɪŋ ˈɪmɪdʒɪz / (BrE)
Photographs or pictures that are visually striking or remarkably beautiful.
visually impressive photographs

Examples

The wildlife photographer shared some stunning images of tigers in their natural habitat.
Scientists released stunning images of distant galaxies taken by the new telescope.
Her portfolio is filled with stunning images that capture the beauty of the city at night.

Synset Examples

The Nikon choir captured stunning images at the event.
The orbiter captured stunning images of Mars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beautiful pictures - Images that are attractive or pleasing to look at
  • Antonym: unremarkable images - Images that are not notable or striking
