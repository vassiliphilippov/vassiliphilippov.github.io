food colour

1. NOUN

/ fuːd ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / fuːd ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
a substance added to food or drink to give or restore colour, typically one approved for consumption
substance added to give colour to food

Examples

Add a few drops of food colour to the icing for a vibrant look.
Some natural food colour is made from beetroot extract.
She avoids artificial food colour due to allergies.

Synset Examples

food color made from vegetable dyes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: food coloring - substance used to add colour to food (American spelling)
