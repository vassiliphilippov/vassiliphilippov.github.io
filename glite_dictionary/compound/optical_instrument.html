optical instrument

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑptɪkəl ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / ˈɒptɪkəl ˈɪnstrəmənt / (BrE)
a device that uses lenses, mirrors, or other optical components to assist or improve vision or observation
device for aiding vision or observation

Examples

The scientist examined the bacteria under an optical instrument for greater detail.
A telescope is an optical instrument that allows us to observe distant stars.
Proper maintenance is essential for any delicate optical instrument used in research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microscope - an instrument for viewing very small objects
  • Synonym: telescope - an instrument for viewing distant objects
  • Synonym: camera - a device for recording images using optical principles
