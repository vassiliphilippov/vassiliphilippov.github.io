weak part

1. NOUN

/ wiːk pɑrt / (AmE), / wiːk pɑːt / (BrE)
a place or aspect of someone or something that is especially vulnerable or susceptible to harm or failure
especially vulnerable place or aspect

Examples

Every organization has a weak part that could be exploited by competitors.
The engineer identified the bridge's weak part and recommended immediate repairs.
Critics often target the weak part of an argument during a debate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weak point - an aspect that is especially susceptible to failure or attack
  • Antonym: strength - a quality or attribute of being strong or effective
