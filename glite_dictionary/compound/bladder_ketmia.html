bladder ketmia

1. NOUN

/ ˈblædɚ ˈkɛtmiə / (AmE), / ˈblædə ˈkɛtmiə / (BrE)
An annual weedy herb with short-lived yellow flowers with purple centers, native to the Old World tropics and now naturalized as a weed in North America.
a weedy herb with yellow, purple-centered flowers

Examples

The garden was quickly overrun by bladder ketmia, which thrives in warm climates.
Many fields in the region are dotted with the distinctive yellow flowers of bladder ketmia.
Researchers observed that bladder ketmia adapts easily to disturbed soils.
