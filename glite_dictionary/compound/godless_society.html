godless society

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑd.ləs səˈsaɪ.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈɡɒd.ləs səˈsaɪ.ə.ti / (BrE)
a society in which religious belief or belief in God is absent or rejected
society lacking religious belief

Examples

The author describes a godless society where moral values are constantly questioned.
Some argue that a godless society lacks a sense of shared purpose and meaning.
Debates about secularism often focus on the consequences of living in a godless society.

Synset Examples

They lived in a godless society, devoid of hope.
The book explores themes of godlessness in modern society.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atheist society - a society made up of people who do not believe in God
  • Antonym: religious society - a society organized around religious beliefs
