price tag

1. NOUN

/ praɪs tæɡ / (AmE), / praɪs tæɡ / (BrE)
a label attached to an item that shows its price
label displaying the price of an item

Examples

She checked the price tag before deciding to buy the jacket.
The price tag on the vase shocked everyone at the auction.
Please remove the price tag from the gift before wrapping it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: label - a piece of paper, cloth, or similar material attached to something to identify or describe it
