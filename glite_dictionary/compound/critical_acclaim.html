critical acclaim

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪdɪkəl əˈkleɪm / (AmE), / ˈkrɪtɪkəl əˈkleɪm / (BrE)
public praise and admiration from critics, especially in the arts or entertainment
praise from critics

Examples

The novel received critical acclaim upon its release and was shortlisted for several literary awards.
Her performance drew critical acclaim from reviewers across the country.
Despite mixed public reactions, the play achieved critical acclaim for its innovative staging.

Synset Examples

The film managed to garner critical acclaim.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: praise - expression of approval or admiration
  • Antonym: disapproval - expression of unfavorable opinion
