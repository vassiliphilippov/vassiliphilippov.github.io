Dark Brandon

1. PROPN

/ dɑrk ˈbrændən / (AmE), / dɑːk ˈbrændən / (BrE)
a satirical nickname for U.S. President Joe Biden, portraying him as powerful, forceful, or unexpectedly assertive, especially in online memes
satirical nickname for a strong, assertive Biden
informal slang american

Examples

Many social media users posted images of Dark Brandon after the announcement.
The concept of Dark Brandon turned into a viral meme across the internet.
Commentators debated whether the rise of Dark Brandon changed public perceptions of Biden.

Synset Examples

They joked about Dark Brandon taking charge of the situation.
The meme of Dark Brandon spread quickly online.
Dark Brandon is often used to portray Biden's fiercer side.

Etymology

Originated as an internet meme in the early 2020s, combining the nickname "Brandon" (from the "Let's Go Brandon" meme) with "dark" to imply a powerful, forceful persona.
