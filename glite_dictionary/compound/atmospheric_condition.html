atmospheric condition

1. NOUN

/ ˌætməsˈfɛrɪk kənˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌatməsˈfɛrɪk kəndɪʃən / (BrE)
the state of the atmosphere at a particular place and time, especially regarding temperature, wind, clouds, and precipitation
state of the atmosphere (weather)

Examples

Unfavorable atmospheric conditions forced the airline to delay all morning flights.
High humidity is just one aspect of the atmospheric condition we have to monitor.
The team checked the atmospheric condition before launching the weather balloon.

Synset Examples

they were hoping for good weather
every day we have weather conditions and yesterday was no exception
the conditions were too rainy for playing in the snow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weather - the day-to-day state of the atmosphere, especially in relation to temperature, wind, and precipitation
