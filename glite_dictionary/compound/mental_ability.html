mental ability

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.tl̩ əˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.tl̩ əˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the capacity to learn, understand, or retain knowledge, and to perceive the significance of information or behavior
capacity to learn or understand

Examples

His mental ability was tested through a series of cognitive exercises.
After the accident, doctors evaluated her mental ability to make her own decisions.
Success in the profession depends more on mental ability than physical strength.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intelligence - the ability to acquire and apply knowledge and skills
