rabbit-eared bandicoot

1. NOUN

/ ˈræbɪt ɪrd ˈbændɪˌkut / (AmE), / ˈræbɪt ɪəd ˈbændɪˌkuːt / (BrE)
a small Australian marsupial with long, leathery ears resembling those of a rabbit
small Australian marsupial with rabbit-like ears

Examples

The rabbit-eared bandicoot is rarely seen during the day, as it is nocturnal.
Farmers are working to protect the habitat of the endangered rabbit-eared bandicoot.
With its large ears and soft fur, the rabbit-eared bandicoot is often mistaken for a small rabbit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bilby - long-eared Australian marsupial, also called the rabbit-eared bandicoot
