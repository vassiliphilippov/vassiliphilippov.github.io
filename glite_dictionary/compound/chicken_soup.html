chicken soup

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪk.ən suːp / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.ɪn suːp / (BrE)
a soup made from chicken broth, usually containing pieces of chicken and vegetables
soup made from chicken broth

Examples

My grandmother always makes chicken soup when someone in the family is sick.
On a cold day, nothing beats a hot bowl of chicken soup.
The restaurant is famous for its homemade chicken soup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broth - liquid in which meat, fish, or vegetables have been cooked

2. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkən suːp / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn suːp / (BrE)
A hot soup made with chicken as the primary ingredient, often served as a comfort food and traditionally believed to help treat colds or soothe discomfort.
hot soup made with chicken

Examples

After a long walk in the rain, she made a pot of chicken soup to warm up.
Many people turn to chicken soup when they're feeling under the weather.
Homemade chicken soup is often passed down through generations as a family remedy.

Synset Examples

On cold days, nothing beats a warm bowl of chicken soup.
She believes chicken soup cures a cold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broth - a thin soup made by boiling meat or vegetables
  • Antonym: vegetable soup - soup made primarily with vegetables
