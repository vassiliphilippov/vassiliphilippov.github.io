winceworthy moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪnsˌwɝːði ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈwɪnsˌwɜːði ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
a situation or event that causes feelings of discomfort or embarrassment in the observer
moment causing discomfort or cringe

Examples

The comedian's off-color joke was definitely a winceworthy moment for the entire audience.
She accidentally called her teacher 'mom'—a classic winceworthy moment in any classroom.
The interview had more than one winceworthy moment that made viewers squirm.

Synset Examples

The movie was full of winceworthy moments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cringe - to react with embarrassment or discomfort
  • Synonym: awkward moment - a situation that causes embarrassment or unease

Etymology

Formed from 'wince' (to react with embarrassment or pain) + '-worthy' (deserving), combined with 'moment'.
