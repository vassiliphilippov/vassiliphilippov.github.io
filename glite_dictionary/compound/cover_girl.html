cover girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌvɚ ɡɝːl / (AmE), / ˈkʌvə ɡɜːl / (BrE)
A very attractive female model whose photograph appears on the front cover of a magazine.
female model featured on magazine cover

Examples

The magazine's new cover girl was an instant sensation.
She always dreamed of becoming a famous cover girl.
The cover girl smiled confidently at the launch event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: model - a person employed to pose for artists or photographers
