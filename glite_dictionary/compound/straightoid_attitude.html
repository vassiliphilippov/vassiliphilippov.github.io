straightoid attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈstreɪtoʊɪd ˈætɪˌtjud / (AmE), / ˈstreɪtɔɪd ˈætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
a mindset or outlook that favors or upholds heterosexual norms and expectations, often to the exclusion or marginalization of other sexual orientations
mindset favoring heterosexual norms

Examples

Many schools still promote a straightoid attitude through their sex education programs.
She felt uncomfortable because the team's straightoid attitude dismissed any non-heterosexual identities.
The movie received criticism for its straightoid attitude toward relationships.

Synset Examples

The straightoid attitude in the office can be quite stifling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heteronormativity - belief or assumption that heterosexuality is the default or normal sexual orientation

Etymology

Modeled on 'heteronormative', combining 'straight' (slang for heterosexual) with the suffix '-oid' (resembling or like).
