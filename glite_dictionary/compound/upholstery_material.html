upholstery material

1. NOUN

/ ʌpˈhoʊlstəri məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / ʌpˈhɒlstəri məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
fabric or other material used to cover and pad furniture, especially chairs and sofas.
fabric used for upholstering furniture

Examples

The company only uses high-quality upholstery material for its custom sofas.
When choosing an upholstery material, consider both durability and texture for the best results.
Fading and stains can be prevented by selecting the right upholstery material for your living room furniture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: upholstery - the soft, padded textile covering of furniture
