slime tutorial

1. NOUN

/ slaɪm təˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / slaɪm tjʊəˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
an online instructional guide, especially a video, that shows how to make slime as a craft or toy
instructional video or guide for making slime

Examples

My little sister loves watching slime tutorials to learn new recipes and techniques.
The most popular slime tutorial on the channel has over two million views.
I followed a slime tutorial online and made my own glitter slime at home.

Synset Examples

She found a great slime tutorial on YouTube for beginners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tutorial - an instructional guide or lesson
