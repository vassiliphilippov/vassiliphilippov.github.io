purrfect gift

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrfɪkt ɡɪft / (AmE), / ˈpɜːfɪkt ɡɪft / (BrE)
a gift that is ideal or perfect, often said playfully, especially as a pun involving cats
ideal or perfect gift (playful, often cat-related)
informal

Examples

I think this cat mug makes a purrfect gift for any feline lover.
The holiday sale helped me pick out the purrfect gift for my sister.
A handmade collar would be the purrfect gift for her new kitten.

Synset Examples

She found the purrfect gift for her friend's birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfect gift - a gift that is exactly right for someone or a situation

Etymology

Playful alteration of 'perfect' using 'purr', imitating the sound a cat makes.
