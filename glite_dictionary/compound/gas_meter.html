gas meter

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈmiːtɚ / (AmE), / ɡæs ˈmiːtə / (BrE)
A device that measures the quantity of gas flowing through a pipe, typically used for billing purposes in homes and businesses.
device measuring gas flow in pipes

Examples

The technician came to check the gas meter outside our apartment.
Make sure your gas meter is easily accessible for readings and maintenance.
They installed a new gas meter after renovating the heating system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electricity meter - a device measuring electricity consumption
  • Synonym: water meter - a device measuring water usage
