chicken sexer

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkən ˈsɛksər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn ˈsɛksə / (BrE)
a person trained to determine the sex of chicks and other hatchlings, usually in the poultry industry
person who determines chick gender

Examples

After years of practice, the chicken sexer could quickly identify the gender of hundreds of chicks a day.
Becoming a skilled chicken sexer requires intensive training and a keen eye for detail.
The hatchery hired an experienced chicken sexer to ensure the efficiency of its breeding program.

Synset Examples

Chick sexing is the method of distinguishing the sex of chicken and other hatchlings, usually by a trained person called a chick sexer or chicken sexer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chick sexer - person who determines the sex of baby chickens
