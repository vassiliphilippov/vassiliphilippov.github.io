misguided chauvinist

1. NOUN

/ mɪsˈɡaɪdɪd ˈʃoʊvɪnɪst / (AmE), / mɪsˈɡaɪdɪd ˈʃəʊvɪnɪst / (BrE)
a person who holds a prejudiced or exaggerated belief in the dominance or superiority of one gender, especially when such beliefs are based on errors or misunderstandings
person with mistaken sexist beliefs

Examples

Many critics called the character a misguided chauvinist for his outdated views on women's roles.
He was labeled a misguided chauvinist after making several controversial remarks about women in the workplace.
Despite his good intentions, his actions revealed him to be a misguided chauvinist.

Synset Examples

The film portrayed the protagonist as a misguided chauvinist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chauvinist - a person displaying aggressive or exaggerated patriotism or a belief in the superiority of their own group
  • Antonym: feminist - a person who supports equality of the sexes
