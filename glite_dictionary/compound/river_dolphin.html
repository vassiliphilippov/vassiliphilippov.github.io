river dolphin

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪvɚ ˌdɑlfɪn / (AmE), / ˈrɪvə ˌdɒlfɪn / (BrE)
A long-snouted dolphin that lives primarily in freshwater rivers of South America and southern Asia.
freshwater dolphin of rivers

Examples

The Amazon river dolphin is famous for its pink color and playful nature.
Scientists are concerned about the declining population of the river dolphin in Asia.
During the boat tour, we spotted a rare river dolphin swimming near the surface.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dolphin - a highly intelligent aquatic mammal related to whales
