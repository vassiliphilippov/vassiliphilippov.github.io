jelly fungus

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛli ˌfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛli ˌfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
any fungus of the orders Tremellales or Auriculariales with a jellylike fruiting body when fresh
fungus with jellylike fruiting body

Examples

We found several jelly fungi growing on the decaying log after the rain.
The chef used pieces of jelly fungus to garnish the salad in a unique way.
Some species of jelly fungus are edible and prized in Asian cuisine.
