total shitburger

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.təl ˈʃɪtˌbɝː.ɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl ˈʃɪtˌbɜː.ɡə / (BrE)
An extremely poor, unacceptable, or ridiculous excuse or situation.
completely unacceptable excuse or mess
informal vulgar

Examples

The boss wasn't fooled by his total shitburger for missing the meeting.
Their apology for the error sounded like a total shitburger to everyone.
I can't believe you thought that total shitburger would work as an excuse.

Synset Examples

His excuse for being late was a total shitburger.

Related Terms
