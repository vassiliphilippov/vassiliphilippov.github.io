Chinese scholar tree

1. NOUN

/ ˌtʃaɪˈniz ˈskɑlɚ tri / (AmE), / ˌtʃaɪˈniːz ˈskɒlə triː / (BrE)
a deciduous tree native to China and Japan, with compound dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant creamy-white flowers
a deciduous tree from China and Japan

Examples

The Chinese scholar tree is often planted along city streets for its beautiful flowers and shade.
In summer, the Chinese scholar tree produces clusters of creamy-white blossoms that attract many bees.
Gardeners prize the Chinese scholar tree for its resilience and ornamental value.
