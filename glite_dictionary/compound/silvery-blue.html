silvery-blue

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlvəri bluː / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəri bluː / (BrE)
having a light, shiny blue color resembling silver
light shiny blue color

Examples

The artist chose a silvery-blue paint for the sky in her landscape.
She wore a delicate scarf in a silvery-blue shade that shimmered in the light.
The surface of the lake appeared silvery-blue at dawn, reflecting the early morning sun.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: azure - bright blue color, often associated with the sky
  • Degree Lower: silver - a precious shiny grayish-white metal; resembling a shiny gray color
  • Degree Higher: navy blue - a very dark shade of blue
