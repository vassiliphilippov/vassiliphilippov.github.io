bovine pattern

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊvaɪn ˈpætərn / (AmE), / ˈbəʊvaɪn ˈpætn̩ / (BrE)
a design that resembles the markings or appearance of cattle, especially cowhide
design resembling cattle markings

Examples

She chose a bovine pattern wallpaper to give her kitchen a rustic feel.
The bovine pattern on the handbag made it a standout accessory.
Artists often use a bovine pattern when designing farm-themed décor items.

Synset Examples

She wore a dress with a unique bovine pattern.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cow print - a pattern resembling the irregular black and white patches of a cow's hide
