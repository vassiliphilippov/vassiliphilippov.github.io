telephone call

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌfoʊn kɔl / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn kɔːl / (BrE)
an act of communicating with someone by speaking to them over the telephone
act of communication by phone

Examples

I received an important telephone call from my doctor this morning.
She made a telephone call to check on her parents after the storm.
During the meeting, his telephone call interrupted the discussion.

Synset Examples

she reported several anonymous calls
he placed a phone call to London
he heard the phone ringing but didn't want to take the call

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phone call - an act of communication by speaking on the telephone

2. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlɪˌfoʊn kɔl / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn kɔːl / (BrE)
an instance of speaking with someone by telephone
conversation by telephone

Examples

She made a telephone call to her parents as soon as she arrived.
I'll return your telephone call after my meeting is finished.
During the interview, he received an unexpected telephone call offering him the job.

Synset Examples

He received a telephone call from an old friend.
