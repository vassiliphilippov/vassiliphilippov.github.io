office building

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːfɪs ˌbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɒfɪs ˌbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
A building that contains offices where business or professional work is carried out.
building with multiple offices

Examples

The company is moving to a new office building downtown next month.
Several high-rise office buildings line the city's financial district.
Our firm rents the top floor of an office building overlooking the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: office block - a building containing offices (mainly British)
