palm tree

1. NOUN

/ pɑm tri / (AmE), / pɑːm triː / (BrE)
a tree with a tall, unbranched trunk topped with large, divided leaves, belonging to the palm family (Palmae or Arecaceae), often found in tropical and subtropical regions
a tropical tree with a tall trunk and large leaves

Examples

A row of palm trees lines the beach, providing shade for sunbathers.
During their vacation, they relaxed under a tall palm tree by the sea.
Some palm trees can live for over a century in tropical climates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: date palm - a species of palm tree known for producing edible dates
  • Synonym: coconut palm - a species of palm tree that produces coconuts
