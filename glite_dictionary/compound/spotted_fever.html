spotted fever

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑːtɪd ˈfiːvər / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd ˈfiːvə / (BrE)
Any of several serious infectious diseases marked by fever and skin rashes with spots.
febrile disease with skin spots
medical

Examples

Rocky Mountain spotted fever is transmitted by ticks and can be life-threatening without prompt treatment.
Doctors suspected spotted fever when the patient developed a high fever and a distinctive spotted rash.
Vaccination can prevent some forms of spotted fever in endemic regions.
