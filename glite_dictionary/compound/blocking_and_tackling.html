blocking and tackling

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɑːkɪŋ ənd ˈtækəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblɒkɪŋ ən ˈtækəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the basic skills or fundamental tasks that are crucial for success, especially in American football or in achieving goals in other areas
basic skills; fundamental tasks
american

Examples

Mastering the blocking and tackling of project management is key to success.
The coach emphasized blocking and tackling throughout every practice session.
In our new business plan, we'll start with the blocking and tackling before adding advanced features.

Synset Examples

We focused on blocking and tackling to improve our team.
The project requires some serious blocking and tackling.
In football, blocking and tackling are essential skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basics - the essential facts or principles of a subject or skill
  • Synonym: fundamentals - the most important or foundational elements

Etymology

Originally from American football, referring to the essential actions of blocking opponents and tackling ball carriers.
