Boy Scout

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔɪ ˌskaʊt / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪ ˌskaʊt / (BrE)
A boy who is a member of the Boy Scouts, an organization that promotes outdoor skills, citizenship, and community service.
member of the Boy Scouts organization

Examples

As a Boy Scout, he learned valuable survival and leadership skills.
Every Boy Scout in the troop volunteered at the community cleanup event.
Joining the Boy Scout organization encouraged him to earn badges and try new activities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Girl Scout - a girl who is a member of the Girl Scouts
