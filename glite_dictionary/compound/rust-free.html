rust-free

1. ADJ

/ rʌst fri / (AmE), / rʌst friː / (BrE)
Not affected by or containing any rust; free from rust.
not containing any rust

Examples

The old car is surprisingly rust-free despite its age.
Make sure you use rust-free screws for bathroom fixtures.
He was proud that his bicycle remained rust-free after a rainy winter.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rusty - covered with rust or affected by rust
