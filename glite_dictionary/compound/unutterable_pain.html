unutterable pain

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈʌt̬ɚəbəl peɪn / (AmE), / ʌnˈʌtərəbl̩ peɪn / (BrE)
pain so intense or overwhelming that it cannot be described in words
pain that cannot be put into words

Examples

She experienced an unutterable pain after hearing the tragic news.
His silence spoke of the unutterable pain he felt inside.
In her diary, she tried to capture the unutterable pain that words could not express.

Synset Examples

Her grief was an unutterable pain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indescribable pain - pain that cannot be described
  • Antonym: expressible pain - pain that can be described or communicated in words
