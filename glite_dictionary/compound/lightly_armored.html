lightly armored

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪtli ˈɑrmɚd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtli ˈɑːməd / (BrE)
equipped with armor that provides protection against light weapons but not heavier firepower.
protected with light armor

Examples

The scout vehicle was lightly armored to maximize speed and maneuverability.
These lightly armored trucks are vulnerable to heavier weapons on the battlefield.
Although lightly armored, the vessel could withstand attacks from small arms fire.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heavily armored - equipped with thick armor for maximum protection
