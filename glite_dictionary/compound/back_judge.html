back judge

1. NOUN

/ bæk ˈdʒʌdʒ / (AmE), / bæk ˈdʒʌdʒ / (BrE)
an official in American football who stands deep in the defensive backfield to oversee the play and enforce rules
football official deep in defense

Examples

The back judge signaled that the pass was incomplete.
As a back judge, he is responsible for monitoring players in the defensive secondary.
The referee and back judge conferred before making the final call.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: referee - the main official who oversees a sports game
