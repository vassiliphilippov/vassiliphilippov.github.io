temporary worker

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛmpəˌrɛri ˈwɝːkər / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərəri ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person employed for a limited period, often to fill a short-term need or vacancy
employee hired for a limited time

Examples

The company hired several temporary workers to help during the busy holiday season.
As a temporary worker, she had fewer benefits than full-time employees.
Many students take jobs as temporary workers during their summer break.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: temp - shortened form meaning temporary worker
