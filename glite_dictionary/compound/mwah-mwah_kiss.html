mwah-mwah kiss

1. NOUN

/ mwɑː mwɑː kɪs / (AmE), / mwɑː mwɑː kɪs / (BrE)
an exaggerated kissing sound or gesture made to express affection, often without actual contact, as by blowing a kiss
an exaggerated kissing sound or gesture
informal

Examples

She ended the call with a cheerful mwah-mwah kiss to her friend.
After his speech, he blew a mwah-mwah kiss to the audience.
Instead of a real kiss, he gave her a playful mwah-mwah kiss across the room.

Synset Examples
