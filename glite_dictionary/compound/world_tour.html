world tour

1. NOUN

/ wɝld tʊr / (AmE), / wɜːld tʊə / (BrE)
a journey or series of performances, events, or visits that takes place in several countries around the world
a tour through multiple countries

Examples

The band announced their world tour dates for next year.
She documented her world tour experiences in a travel blog.
Their world tour included stops in Asia, Europe, and South America.

Synset Examples

The pope visited our city during his world tour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tour - a journey for pleasure in which several different places are visited
