studio apartment

1. NOUN

/ ˈstuːdioʊ əˈpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈstjuːdiəʊ əˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
a small apartment consisting of one main room that serves as the living room, bedroom, and kitchen, with a separate bathroom
a one-room apartment with a bathroom

Examples

She decided to rent a studio apartment in the city to save money on rent.
His studio apartment may be small, but it's cozy and well-designed.
The real estate agent showed us a bright studio apartment near the university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: efficiency apartment - a small apartment with a combined living and sleeping area
