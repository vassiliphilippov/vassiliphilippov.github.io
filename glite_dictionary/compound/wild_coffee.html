wild coffee

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / waɪld ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
a coarse, weedy perennial herb native to America, with large usually perfoliate leaves and purple or dull red flowers
a weedy American perennial herb

Examples

Wild coffee can be found growing in the understory of many southeastern forests.
The leaves of wild coffee are broad and often appear to wrap around the stem.
Gardeners sometimes cultivate wild coffee for its attractive flowers and tolerance of shade.
