foxtrot oscar

1. X

/ ˈfɑks.trɑt ˈɑs.kɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɒks.trɒt ˈɒs.kə / (BrE)
slang term, especially in military contexts, used as a euphemistic way to tell someone to go away or leave forcefully (from the phonetic alphabet for 'F.O.', meaning 'f*** off')
military euphemism for 'f*** off'
slang

Examples

When he realized he was being lied to, he told them to foxtrot oscar.
The sergeant barked 'Just foxtrot oscar!' when the recruits got in his way.
If you're not going to help, you can simply foxtrot oscar!

Synset Examples

When he heard the news, he shouted foxtrot oscar in frustration.
She told him to foxtrot oscar after his rude comment.
He was so angry that he just yelled foxtrot oscar and left.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fuck off - to go away; to leave someone alone (vulgar slang)

Etymology

From the NATO phonetic alphabet code words for the letters 'F' and 'O', standing for 'f*** off'.
