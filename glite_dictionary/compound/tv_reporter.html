TV reporter

1. NOUN

/ tiː viː rɪˈpɔrtər / (AmE), / tiː viː rɪˈpɔːtə / (BrE)
a journalist who reports news stories on television
journalist reporting news on television

Examples

The TV reporter interviewed several witnesses live at the scene.
As a TV reporter, she often travels to cover breaking news events.
The local TV reporter received an award for his investigative journalism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reporter - a person who reports news, especially as a profession
