hod carrier

1. NOUN

/ hɑd ˈkæriər / (AmE), / hɒd ˈkæriə / (BrE)
a laborer whose job is to carry bricks, mortar, or other supplies to bricklayers or masons at a construction site
laborer who carries supplies to bricklayers

Examples

The hod carrier delivered bricks up the scaffolding all morning.
Before becoming a bricklayer, he started as a hod carrier on building sites.
During the renovation, the hod carrier made countless trips with heavy loads of mortar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laborer - a person doing unskilled manual work

Etymology

From 'hod' (a V-shaped trough used for carrying materials) and 'carrier'.
