Slovene language

1. NOUN

/ sloʊˈviːn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / sləʊˈviːn ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the South Slavic language spoken primarily by the people of Slovenia
language of Slovenia

Examples

Many residents of Ljubljana speak the Slovene language as their mother tongue.
The official documents are translated into the Slovene language for government use.
She is studying the Slovene language as part of her Slavic linguistics course.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Slovenian - another term for Slovene language

Etymology

Slovene, from Sloveni, the people of Slovenia, and language.
