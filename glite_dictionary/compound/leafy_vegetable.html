leafy vegetable

1. NOUN

/ ˈlifi ˌvɛdʒtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈliːfi ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ / (BrE)
any of various plants with edible leaves and stems, eaten as vegetables
vegetable with edible leaves and stems

Examples

Eating a variety of leafy vegetables can provide important vitamins and minerals.
Spinach, kale, and lettuce are popular types of leafy vegetables.
You should wash leafy vegetables thoroughly to remove any dirt or insects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: green vegetable - vegetable with edible green parts, especially leaves
