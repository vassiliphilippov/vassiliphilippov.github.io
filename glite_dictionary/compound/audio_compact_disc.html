audio compact disc

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːdioʊ ˈkɑːmpækt dɪsk / (AmE), / ˈɔːdiəʊ ˈkɒmpækt dɪsk / (BrE)
A compact disc designed for storing and playing back sound recordings such as music and voice.
CD for audio recordings

Examples

I bought the album as an audio compact disc instead of downloading it.
He prefers listening to music on an audio compact disc for better sound quality.
Many cars still come equipped with audio compact disc players.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compact disc - a small, flat, round plastic disc that stores data, particularly audio

Etymology

Formed from 'audio' (from Latin 'audire', to hear) and 'compact disc'.
