express mail

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈsprɛs meɪl / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛs meɪl / (BrE)
a postal service for sending letters and packages quickly using a fast and efficient delivery system
fast postal delivery service

Examples

We sent the contract via express mail to ensure it arrived the next morning.
She paid extra for express mail so her birthday gift would not be delayed.
The company uses express mail for all urgent international shipments.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: priority mail - postal service for faster-than-standard delivery, but not as rapid as express mail
  • Degree Lower: regular mail - standard postal delivery service without expedited handling
