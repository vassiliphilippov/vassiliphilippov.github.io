Maltese terrier

1. NOUN

/ mɔlˈtiz ˈtɛriər / (AmE), / mɔːlˈtiːz ˈtɛrɪə / (BrE)
a breed of small toy dogs known for their long, silky white coat
breed of small white toy dog

Examples

The Maltese terrier is prized for its elegant appearance and affectionate nature.
Her family adopted a playful Maltese terrier from the animal shelter.
Many owners enjoy grooming their Maltese terrier to keep its white coat looking pristine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Maltese - breed of small, long-haired toy dog

Etymology

From Maltese (relating to Malta) + terrier (from Old French *chien terrier*, 'earth dog').
