ratbite fever bacterium

1. NOUN

/ ˈræt.baɪt ˈfiː.vɚ bəˈtɪr.i.əm / (AmE), / ˈræt.baɪt ˈfiː.və bəˈtɪə.rɪ.əm / (BrE)
a bacterium that causes ratbite fever, an infectious disease transmitted by the bite of a rat
bacterium causing ratbite fever
medical

Examples

The patient developed a fever after exposure to a ratbite fever bacterium.
Treatment for infection with a ratbite fever bacterium usually involves antibiotics.
Scientists identified the ratbite fever bacterium in the laboratory samples.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Streptobacillus moniliformis - one species known to cause ratbite fever
  • Synonym: ratbite fever - the infectious disease caused by these bacteria
