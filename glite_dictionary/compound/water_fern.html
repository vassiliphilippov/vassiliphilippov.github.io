water fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑt̬ɚ fɝn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə fɜːn / (BrE)
A type of fern that grows in water or very wet areas, often found in ponds, marshes, or aquariums.
a fern that grows in water

Examples

The aquarium was decorated with lush water fern to create a natural habitat for the fish.
Some species of water fern can help purify water by absorbing excess nutrients.
We spotted a rare water fern growing along the edge of the pond during our hike.
