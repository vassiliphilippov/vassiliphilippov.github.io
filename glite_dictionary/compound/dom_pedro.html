Dom Pedro

1. NOUN

/ dɑm ˈpeɪdroʊ / (AmE), / dɒm ˈpeɪdrəʊ / (BrE)
A South African dessert cocktail made by mixing ice cream with whisky, often served as an after-dinner drink.
South African ice cream and whisky dessert cocktail

Examples

After dinner, we enjoyed a creamy Dom Pedro as a special treat.
The South African restaurant is famous for its signature Dom Pedro cocktails.
He blended whisky with ice cream to make a homemade Dom Pedro for his guests.
