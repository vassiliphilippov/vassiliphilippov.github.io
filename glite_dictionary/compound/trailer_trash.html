trailer trash

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪlɚ træʃ / (AmE), / ˈtreɪlə træʃ / (BrE)
(offensive, slang) An insulting term for poor people who live in trailer parks, especially in the United States.
offensive insult for poor trailer park residents
offensive slang american

Examples

Some people cruelly refer to their neighbors as trailer trash, which is both unfair and hurtful.
The article criticized the use of the label trailer trash to describe poor families in rural areas.
He was tired of being called trailer trash simply because of where he grew up.

Synset Examples

They lived in a trailer park, often labeled as trailer trash.
The movie depicted the struggles of a family considered trailer trash.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white trash - (offensive, slang) derogatory term for poor white people, especially in rural US
