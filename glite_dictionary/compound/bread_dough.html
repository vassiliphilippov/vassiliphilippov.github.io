bread dough

1. NOUN

/ brɛd doʊ / (AmE), / brɛd dəʊ / (BrE)
a mixture of flour, water, and other ingredients that is kneaded and prepared for baking bread
dough for making bread

Examples

She set the bread dough aside to rise in a warm spot.
The aroma of fresh bread dough baking filled the kitchen.
Kneading bread dough can be quite therapeutic for some people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dough - a thick mixture of flour and liquid, used for baking
