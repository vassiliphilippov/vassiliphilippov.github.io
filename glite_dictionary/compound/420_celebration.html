4/20 celebration

1. NOUN

/ fɔːr twɛnti sɛlɪˈbreɪʃən / (AmE), / fɔː ˈtwɛnti sɛlɪˈbreɪʃən / (BrE)
an event or gathering held on April 20 (4/20) to celebrate cannabis culture, often involving the consumption of marijuana
event celebrating cannabis culture on April 20

Examples

Thousands gathered at the park for the annual 4/20 celebration to advocate for cannabis legalization.
She wore a T-shirt advertising the local 4/20 celebration downtown.
Music and laughter filled the air during yesterday's 4/20 celebration at the university campus.

Synset Examples

Her brother is a pothead who never misses a 4/20 celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 420 - number associated with cannabis culture
  • Synonym: cannabis culture - social practices and norms around cannabis use

Etymology

From the slang term "420" (pronounced "four-twenty"), associated with cannabis use, and "celebration."
