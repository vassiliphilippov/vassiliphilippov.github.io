Parji language

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑr.dʒi ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.dʒi ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken primarily by the Dhurwa tribe in south central India, particularly in the districts of Koraput and Bastar in Chhattisgarh state
a Dravidian language of south central India

Examples

The Parji language is recognized for its distinct structure among Dravidian languages.
Many elders in the tribe still teach their children to speak Parji language at home.
Researchers have documented folk tales and songs in the Parji language.
