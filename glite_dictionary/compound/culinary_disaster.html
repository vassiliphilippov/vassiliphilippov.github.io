culinary disaster

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɪˌnɛri dɪˈzæstər / (AmE), / ˈkʌlɪn(ə)ri dɪˈzɑːstə / (BrE)
an occurrence of severe failure or mistake in cooking, resulting in inedible or unappetizing food
serious cooking failure or mishap

Examples

The burnt soufflé was a complete culinary disaster that everyone tried to forget.
After several culinary disasters, she finally learned how to make bread properly.
His attempt at homemade sushi turned into a culinary disaster when the rice wouldn't stick.

Synset Examples

We avoided discussing the culinary disaster, nicknamed Velveeta Voldemort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cooking mishap - a mistake or accident while preparing food
  • Antonym: success - the achievement of the desired cooking result
