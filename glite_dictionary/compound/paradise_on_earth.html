paradise on earth

1. NOUN

/ ˈpærəˌdaɪs ɑn ɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈpærədaɪs ɒn ɜːθ / (BrE)
a place or situation of perfect beauty, joy, and happiness in the world
a place or situation of earthly perfection

Examples

Many consider the mountain village to be a paradise on earth due to its breathtaking views.
She described her childhood in the countryside as a paradise on earth.
Tourists flock to the island, hoping to experience what they call a paradise on earth.

Synset Examples

The tropical island felt like a paradise on earth.
The artist captured the scene of a paradise on earth beautifully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paradise - a place or state of great happiness and beauty
  • Synonym: heaven on earth - a state or place of ideal bliss in the world
