Golden Delicious

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡoʊldən dɪˈlɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌɡəʊldən dɪˈlɪʃəs / (BrE)
a sweet variety of eating apple with yellow skin, known for its crisp texture and mild flavor
a yellow-skinned sweet apple variety

Examples

She packed a crisp Golden Delicious in her lunchbox for a healthy snack.
The orchard specializes in growing Golden Delicious apples for local markets.
A bowl of Golden Delicious sat on the kitchen table, adding a splash of color to the room.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Granny Smith - a tart green apple variety
