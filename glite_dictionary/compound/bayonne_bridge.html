Bayonne Bridge

1. PROPN

/ beɪˈoʊn ˈbrɪdʒ / (AmE), / beɪˈəʊn ˈbrɪdʒ / (BrE)
A steel arch bridge connecting the city of Bayonne, New Jersey, to Staten Island, New York, spanning the Kill Van Kull waterway.
steel arch bridge connecting NJ and Staten Island

Examples

The Bayonne Bridge was closed for maintenance over the weekend.
Many commuters use the Bayonne Bridge to travel between New Jersey and Staten Island.
Construction crews worked to raise the roadway of the Bayonne Bridge for larger ships to pass underneath.
