nerve compression

1. NOUN

/ nɝv kəmˈprɛʃən / (AmE), / nɜːv kəmˈprɛʃən / (BrE)
harmful pressure on a nerve, often causing nerve damage, pain, or muscle weakness
harmful pressure on a nerve
medical

Examples

The patient experienced numbness in his hand due to nerve compression in his wrist.
Physical therapy can help relieve symptoms of nerve compression in the neck.
Prolonged awkward posture may lead to nerve compression and chronic pain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compression neuropathy - damage to a nerve caused by sustained pressure
