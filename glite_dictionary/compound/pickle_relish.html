pickle relish

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪkəl ˈrɛlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɪkəl ˈrɛlɪʃ / (BrE)
a condiment made from chopped pickles, often sweetened and used as a topping or flavoring
chopped pickle condiment

Examples

Would you like some pickle relish on your hot dog?
She prepared a homemade pickle relish for the picnic.
The burger was topped with onions, cheese, and pickle relish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relish - a condiment eaten with plain food to add flavor
