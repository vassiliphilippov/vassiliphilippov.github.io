teething ring

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiðɪŋ rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtiːðɪŋ rɪŋ / (BrE)
a small ring, usually made of rubber or plastic, given to teething babies to chew on and soothe sore gums
ring for babies to chew when teething

Examples

The baby calmed down as soon as she started chewing on her teething ring.
Most parents keep a teething ring in the freezer to help soothe irritated gums.
He dropped his teething ring on the floor and reached out for it again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teether - an object for a baby to chew when teething
