Gulf of Riga

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡʌlf əv ˈriːɡə / (AmE), / ˌɡʌlf əv ˈriːɡə / (BrE)
an inlet of the Baltic Sea located between Latvia and Estonia
Baltic Sea inlet between Latvia and Estonia

Examples

Many migratory birds stop at the Gulf of Riga during their seasonal journeys.
Commercial fishing is an important industry in the Gulf of Riga.
The Gulf of Riga is partially frozen over in winter due to its northern location.
