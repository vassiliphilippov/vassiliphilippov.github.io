two-note call

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuː noʊt kɔl / (AmE), / ˈtuː nəʊt kɔːl / (BrE)
a birdcall that consists of exactly two distinct notes or tones sounded in succession
birdcall with two notes

Examples

The cuckoo is famous for its distinctive two-note call heard throughout spring.
We identified the bird by listening for the two-note call it repeated from the treetops.
Certain species use a two-note call during their mating season to attract partners.

Synset Examples

the two-note call of the cuckoo
