chipping sparrow

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪpɪŋ ˌspɛroʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪpɪŋ ˌspærəʊ / (BrE)
A small North American finch with a distinctive rufous crown, common in gardens and urban areas.
small North American finch

Examples

A chipping sparrow landed on the garden fence, its rufous cap gleaming in the sunlight.
During spring, the chipping sparrow is often heard singing its distinctive trill.
We spotted several chipping sparrows searching for seeds in the city park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sparrow - a small bird from the family Passeridae or Emberizidae
