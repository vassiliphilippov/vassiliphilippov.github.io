Oahu Island

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈɑːhuː ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / əʊˈɑːhuː ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
the third largest and most populous island of Hawaii, located between Molokai and Kauai; the site of Honolulu, the state capital
the chief island of Hawaii

Examples

Many tourists visit Oahu Island every year to enjoy its beautiful beaches and vibrant city life.
Honolulu, the capital of Hawaii, is located on Oahu Island.
Pearl Harbor, found on Oahu Island, is a site of major historical significance.

Etymology

From the Hawaiian name Oʻahu.
