plastic blow moulding machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæstɪk bloʊ ˈmoʊldɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈplæstɪk bləʊ ˈməʊldɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A machine used to manufacture hollow plastic parts by shaping molten plastic in a mould using compressed air.
machine for shaping hollow plastic parts

Examples

The factory installed a new plastic blow moulding machine to increase production efficiency.
Training was provided to operators on how to safely use the plastic blow moulding machine.
Advancements in technology have improved the energy efficiency of the plastic blow moulding machine.

Synset Examples

The injection blow molding machine is based on an extruder barrel and screw assembly which melts the polymer.
