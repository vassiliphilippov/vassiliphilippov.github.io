on occasion

1. ADV

/ ɑn əˈkeɪʒən / (AmE), / ɒn əˈkeɪʒən / (BrE)
sometimes; from time to time, but not often or regularly.
sometimes; from time to time

Examples

I go to the theater on occasion, when there's a show that interests me.
She likes to travel on occasion, but prefers staying at home most of the time.
We meet on occasion to catch up and share stories about our lives.

Synset Examples

he was arrogant and occasionally callous
open areas are only occasionally interrupted by clumps of trees
they visit New York on occasion
now and again she would take her favorite book from the shelf and read to us
as we drove along, the beautiful scenery now and then attracted his attention

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occasionally - at infrequent or irregular intervals; sometimes
  • Antonym: frequently - happening often or regularly
