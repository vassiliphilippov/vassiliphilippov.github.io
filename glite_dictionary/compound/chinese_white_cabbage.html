Chinese white cabbage

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪniz waɪt ˈkæbɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪniːz waɪt ˈkæbɪdʒ / (BrE)
A type of Asian plant grown for its cluster of edible white stalks with dark green leaves, commonly used in Asian cuisine.
Asian vegetable with white stalks and green leaves

Examples

Stir-frying Chinese white cabbage with garlic makes for a quick and healthy side dish.
The market had several varieties of Chinese white cabbage available for sale this morning.
You can substitute spinach with Chinese white cabbage in many Asian soup recipes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bok choy - a common synonym for Chinese white cabbage
