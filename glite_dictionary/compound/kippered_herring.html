kippered herring

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪpɚd ˈhɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɪpəd ˈhɛrɪŋ / (BrE)
A herring that has been split, salted, and smoked, especially as a traditional British food.
salted and smoked herring
british

Examples

For breakfast, she grilled a kippered herring and served it with toast.
Smoky and salty, kippered herring is a popular choice in traditional British cuisine.
The market stalls offered fresh, kippered herring alongside other smoked fish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kipper - a herring that has been split, salted, and smoked

Etymology

From 'kipper', from Middle English kypre, of uncertain origin, meaning a cured herring.
