rose garden

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊz ˌɡɑrdən / (AmE), / ˈrəʊz ˌɡɑːdn̩ / (BrE)

a garden for growing roses

Examples

The city park features a beautiful rose garden that attracts visitors every spring.
She spent her morning planting new varieties in her backyard rose garden.
Guided tours of the historic rose garden are available during the summer months.
