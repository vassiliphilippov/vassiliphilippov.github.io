to make science accessible

1. VERB

/ meɪk ˈsaɪəns ækˈsɛsəbəl / (AmE), / meɪk ˈsaɪəns əkˈsɛsəbəl / (BrE)
to present scientific concepts in a way that can be easily understood by people without specialized knowledge
explain science clearly to non-experts

Examples

Her lectures are designed to make science accessible to high school students.
Film documentaries have helped make science accessible to a global audience.
He believes it is crucial to make science accessible for informed public debates.

Synset Examples

Sagan's work made science accessible to everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: popularize science - to make science popular and easily understood
