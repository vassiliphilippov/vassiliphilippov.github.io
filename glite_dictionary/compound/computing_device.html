computing device

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjutɪŋ dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtɪŋ dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
a machine that performs calculations or processes data automatically, such as a computer or calculator
machine that processes data automatically

Examples

Every modern business relies on at least one computing device to manage its data.
Children today are introduced to a computing device at a very young age.
The smartphone is a compact computing device with many advanced features.
