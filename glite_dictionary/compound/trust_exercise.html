trust exercise

1. NOUN

/ trʌst ˈɛksərˌsaɪz / (AmE), / trʌst ˈɛksəsaɪz / (BrE)
an activity intended to build trust among members of a group, often used in team-building or therapy
activity for developing group trust

Examples

The manager organized a trust exercise to help the team communicate better.
Falling backward into a colleague's arms is a classic trust exercise.
During the trust exercise, participants learned to rely on each other.

Synset Examples

He wore a blindfold during the trust exercise.
