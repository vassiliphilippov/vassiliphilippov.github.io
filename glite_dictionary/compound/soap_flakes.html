soap flakes

1. NOUN

/ soʊp fleɪks / (AmE), / səʊp fleɪks / (BrE)
small, thin pieces of soap used for washing, which dissolve easily in water to produce suds
thin shavings of soap for washing

Examples

She added a handful of soap flakes to the bucket of warm water.
Many people prefer using soap flakes for washing delicate fabrics by hand.
The soap flakes dissolved quickly, making lots of bubbles in the sink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soap powder - finely ground soap used for laundry or cleaning
