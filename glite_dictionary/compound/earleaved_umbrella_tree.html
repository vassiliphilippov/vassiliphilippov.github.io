earleaved umbrella tree

1. NOUN

/ ɪrˌliːvd ʌmˈbrɛlə triː / (AmE), / ɪəˌliːvd ʌmˈbrɛlə triː / (BrE)
a small, upright deciduous tree with large leaves arranged in coiled formations at the tips of its branches
small deciduous tree with large, coiled-tip leaves

Examples

The earleaved umbrella tree is prized for its unique foliage and graceful appearance in garden landscapes.
Botanists studied the growth patterns of the earleaved umbrella tree in its native habitat.
A mature earleaved umbrella tree can create cool, shaded areas in a backyard.
