street appeal

1. NOUN

/ stɹit əˈpiːl / (AmE), / stɹiːt əˈpiːl / (BrE)
The attractive appearance of a property or place when seen from the street, especially as judged by passers-by or potential buyers.
attractiveness of a property from the street

Examples

The house has been repainted to maximize its street appeal.
Real estate agents often stress the importance of street appeal when selling a home.
Adding flowers and lights increased the café’s street appeal overnight.

Synset Examples

The house has great street appeal with its vibrant colors.
They renovated the front yard to enhance its street appeal.
Her new café really boosts the street appeal of the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curb appeal - the attractiveness of a property as seen from the street, often focused on potential buyers
