supple skin

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌp.əl skɪn / (AmE), / ˈsʌp.əl skɪn / (BrE)
skin that is soft, flexible, and elastic, often considered healthy and youthful
soft, flexible, healthy skin

Examples

Many skincare products promise to give you supple skin if used regularly.
The baby's supple skin was admired by everyone who saw her.
Drinking plenty of water can help you maintain supple skin throughout the year.

Synset Examples

Her supple skin glowed under the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soft skin - skin that is smooth and not rough or hard
  • Antonym: rough skin - skin that is dry, coarse, or not smooth
