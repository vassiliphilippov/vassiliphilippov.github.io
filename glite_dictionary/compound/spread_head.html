spread head

1. NOUN

/ sprɛd hɛd / (AmE), / sprɛd hed / (BrE)
the heading or title that spans across two facing pages of a book or other publication
heading across two facing pages

Examples

The book's main section begins with a spread head introducing the chapter themes.
Designers chose a bold font for the spread head to capture readers' attention.
Each new topic is marked by a colorful spread head that spans both pages.
