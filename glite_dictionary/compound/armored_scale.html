armored scale

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrmərd skeɪl / (AmE), / ˈɑːməd skeɪl / (BrE)
a small insect of the family Diaspididae, especially the female, with a tough waxy covering that protects its body
insect with a hard waxy covering

Examples

The citrus orchard was infested with armored scale, damaging many of the fruit trees.
An armored scale often attaches itself to leaves and stems, making detection difficult.
Gardeners use natural predators to help control armored scale populations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scale insect - small insect with protective scale-like covering
