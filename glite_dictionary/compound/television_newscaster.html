television newscaster

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛləˌvɪʒən ˈnuːzˌkæstər / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˌvɪʒən ˈnjuːzkɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who presents and reports news stories on television broadcasts
news presenter on television

Examples

The television newscaster delivered the breaking news calmly and professionally.
Many viewers trust their favorite television newscaster to keep them informed about current events.
She aspired to become a television newscaster after studying journalism in college.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newsreader - person who reads news stories on television or radio
  • Synonym: anchor - main news presenter on television
