wrestling ring

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛs.lɪŋ rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛs.lɪŋ rɪŋ / (BrE)
a square, roped-off platform where wrestling matches are held
roped platform for wrestling matches

Examples

The crowd cheered as the fighters entered the wrestling ring for the championship match.
After years of training, she finally stepped into the wrestling ring for her debut bout.
The referee inspected the wrestling ring before the wrestlers began their match.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boxing ring - a square, roped-off platform where boxing bouts are held
