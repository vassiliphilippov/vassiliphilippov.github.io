a thousand one excuse

1. NOUN

/ ə ˈθaʊzənd ˈwən ɪkˈskjus / (AmE), / ə ˈθaʊzənd ˈwʌn ɪkˈskjuːs / (BrE)
a large and varied number of excuses or reasons given, often to avoid blame or responsibility
many different excuses given

Examples

He always seems to have a thousand one excuses for not finishing his work on time.
She came up with a thousand one excuses to explain why she missed the meeting.
Whenever he's late, he offers a thousand one excuses instead of apologizing.

Synset Examples

There were a thousand one excuses for being late.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: a thousand and one excuses - a very large number of excuses
