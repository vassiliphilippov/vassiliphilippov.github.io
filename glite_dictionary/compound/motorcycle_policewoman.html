motorcycle policewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊt̬ɚˌsaɪkəl pəˈlisˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəˌsaɪkəl pəˈliːsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female police officer who rides a motorcycle to patrol or enforce traffic laws
female police officer on a motorcycle

Examples

The motorcycle policewoman pulled over speeding vehicles on the highway.
A motorcycle policewoman escorted the presidential motorcade through the city.
We saw a motorcycle policewoman directing traffic after the parade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motorcycle policeman - a male police officer who patrols on a motorcycle
