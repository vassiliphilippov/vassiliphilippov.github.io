silver gray

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ ˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
a light shade of grey resembling the color of silver
a light, silvery shade of grey

Examples

Her new car was painted a sleek silver gray that sparkled in the sunlight.
He admired the silver gray of her hair, which shimmered softly under the lights.
The bridesmaids wore dresses in silver gray, complementing the elegant theme of the wedding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gray - a color between black and white
  • Synonym: silver - a shiny grayish-white color like the metal
