home-fried potatoes

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊm fraɪd pəˈteɪtoʊz / (AmE), / ˈhəʊm fraɪd pəˈteɪtəʊz / (BrE)
Sliced pieces of potato fried in a pan until brown and crisp, usually served as a side dish.
pan-fried sliced potatoes dish

Examples

She ordered home-fried potatoes with her eggs for breakfast.
The restaurant is famous for its crispy home-fried potatoes.
My grandmother taught me how to make perfect home-fried potatoes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hash browns - grated or chopped potatoes pan-fried until brown
