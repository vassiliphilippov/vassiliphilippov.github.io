office furniture

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑfɪs ˈfɜrnɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈɒfɪs ˈfɜːnɪtʃə / (BrE)
Furniture that is designed and intended for use in an office, such as desks, chairs, and filing cabinets.
furniture for use in offices

Examples

The company purchased new office furniture for the renovated headquarters.
She specializes in ergonomic office furniture that promotes good posture.
All the office furniture was delivered and assembled over the weekend.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: furniture - large movable equipment, such as tables and chairs, used to make a house, office, or other space suitable for living or working
