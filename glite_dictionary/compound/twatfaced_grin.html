twatfaced grin

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɑtˌfeɪst ɡrɪn / (AmE), / ˈtwætˌfeɪst ɡrɪn / (BrE)
An unpleasant or irritating smile, often used to describe someone regarded as obnoxious or contemptible.
an unpleasant, irritating smile
vulgar slang british

Examples

He walked into the meeting with a twatfaced grin that made everyone roll their eyes.
Her twatfaced grin was the last thing I wanted to see after such a long day.
Stop giving me that twatfaced grin—it's really starting to annoy me.

Synset Examples

He has a twatfaced grin that annoys everyone.

Etymology

From 'twat' (vulgar British slang meaning an obnoxious person) and 'faced', combined with 'grin'.
