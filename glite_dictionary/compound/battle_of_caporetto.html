battle of Caporetto

1. NOUN

/ ˈbætəl əv kəpəˈrɛtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbætəl əv kapəˈrɛtəʊ / (BrE)
a major World War I battle in 1917, in which Italian forces were defeated by Austrian and German armies
World War I battle, 1917, Italians defeated

Examples

The disastrous retreat during the battle of Caporetto marked a turning point for the Italian Army in World War I.
Historians often cite the battle of Caporetto as one of the greatest military disasters for Italy.
After the battle of Caporetto, significant changes were made to the Italian military leadership.

Etymology

Named after the Italian town of Caporetto (now Kobarid, Slovenia), site of the battle.
