chemical process

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈprɑː.sɛs / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈprəʊ.ses / (BrE)
a process involving changes in the atomic or molecular composition and structure of substances
process involving chemical changes

Examples

Fermentation is a chemical process used in the production of alcohol and bread.
Rusting is a slow chemical process that affects iron when exposed to moisture.
The breakdown of food in the stomach involves a complex chemical process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chemical reaction - a process that involves rearrangement of the molecular or ionic structure of a substance
