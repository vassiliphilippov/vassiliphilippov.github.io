upside down

1. ADV

/ ˌʌpˌsaɪd ˈdaʊn / (AmE), / ˌʌpsʌɪd ˈdaʊn / (BrE)
in a position such that the top is where the bottom normally is; in an inverted or overturned manner.
in an inverted or overturned manner

Examples

After the storm, many of the boats were left upside down on the shore.
She realized the painting had been hanging upside down for weeks.
Turn the jar upside down to get the last bit of sauce out.

Synset Examples

the box was lying on the floor upside down

Related Terms

  • Antonym: right side up - in a normal, correct upright position
