official emissary

1. NOUN

/ əˈfɪʃəl ˈɛmɪˌsɛri / (AmE), / əˈfɪʃəl ˈɛmɪsəri / (BrE)
an appointed representative sent by a government or organization, especially as a member of a diplomatic mission or legation
appointed diplomatic representative

Examples

The president dispatched an official emissary to negotiate a peace agreement.
As an official emissary, she maintained diplomacy between the two countries.
The official emissary attended several high-level meetings on behalf of the government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: envoy - a person sent as a representative, especially on a diplomatic mission
  • Synonym: delegate - a person sent to represent others, especially at a conference
  • Degree Higher: ambassador - the highest-ranking diplomatic representative sent by one government to another
