Yale University

1. PROPN

/ jeɪl juːnɪˈvɝːsəti / (AmE), / jeɪl juːnɪˈvɜːsəti / (BrE)
An Ivy League research university located in New Haven, Connecticut, known for its academic excellence and historical significance.
prestigious university in Connecticut, USA

Examples

She was accepted into Yale University to study law next fall.
Many famous leaders and authors have graduated from Yale University.
The architecture of Yale University attracts visitors from all over the world.

Etymology

Named after Elihu Yale, a benefactor of the university in the 18th century.
