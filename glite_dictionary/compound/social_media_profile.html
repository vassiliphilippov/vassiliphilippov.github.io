social media profile

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ˈmiːdiə ˈproʊfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˈmiːdiə ˈprəʊfaɪl / (BrE)
an online account or page that displays a person's personal information, posts, and other shared content on a social media platform
user's personal page on social media

Examples

I updated my social media profile to include my new job title.
Her social media profile contains photos from her recent travels.
Employers often look at an applicant's social media profile before making a hiring decision.

Synset Examples

His social media profile showcases his photography talent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: user profile - personal data and settings associated with a user's account
