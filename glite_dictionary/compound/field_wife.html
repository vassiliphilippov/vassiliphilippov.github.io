field wife

1. NOUN

/ fiːld waɪf / (AmE), / fiːld waɪf / (BrE)
a woman who manages multiple responsibilities in rural settings, especially supporting farm activities or daily operations
woman supporting rural or farm life

Examples

As the field wife, she organized planting, oversaw livestock, and handled household duties.
In her community, being a field wife was a respected and demanding role.
The field wife balanced her children's schooling with the needs of the crops.

Synset Examples

Being a field wife means juggling many roles at once.
She proudly referred to herself as the field wife of the farm.
The field wife managed daily operations while her husband traveled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farm wife - woman managing a farm household
