Fiji Islands

1. PROPN

/ ˈfiːdʒi ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / ˈfiːdʒi ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
a group of over 800 islands in the southwestern Pacific Ocean, with about 100 inhabited, forming the country of Fiji
group of islands in the Pacific forming Fiji

Examples

She spent her honeymoon exploring the beautiful Fiji Islands and their vibrant coral reefs.
The Fiji Islands are renowned for their white-sand beaches and clear blue waters.
The tour will take us to several remote islands within the Fiji Islands archipelago.

Etymology

The name 'Fiji' comes from the native name 'Viti'; 'Islands' is from Old English 'īegland'.
