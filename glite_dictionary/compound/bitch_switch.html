bitch switch

1. NOUN

/ bɪtʃ swɪtʃ / (AmE), / bɪtʃ swɪtʃ / (BrE)
a sudden change in someone's behavior to a harsh or aggressive attitude, often triggered by an event or insult
sudden shift to harsh attitude
informal slang offensive

Examples

When her colleagues criticized her ideas, her bitch switch flipped immediately.
If you mess with him, you'll see his bitch switch in action.
Karen's friends always joke about her quick bitch switch when things don't go her way.

Synset Examples

When her friends ditched her, the bitch switch was on.
