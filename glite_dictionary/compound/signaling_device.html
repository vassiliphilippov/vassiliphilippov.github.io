signaling device

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪɡnəlɪŋ dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnəlɪŋ dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
a device designed to send signals, often to convey information or attract attention
device used to send signals

Examples

The train operator used the signaling device to alert maintenance crews of a problem on the tracks.
Sailors once relied on a flare as a simple signaling device during emergencies at sea.
Each classroom is equipped with a signaling device in case of fire or other emergencies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alarm - a device that warns or alerts by a sound or signal
