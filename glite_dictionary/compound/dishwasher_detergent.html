dishwasher detergent

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪʃˌwɑʃɚ dɪˈtɝdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈdɪʃˌwɒʃə dɪˈtɜːdʒənt / (BrE)
a cleaning product specially formulated to be used in dishwashers, producing little or no suds
detergent for use in dishwashers

Examples

We had to buy more dishwasher detergent before running the next load of dishes.
Make sure to use dishwasher detergent instead of regular soap in your machine.
The store offers several brands of eco-friendly dishwasher detergent.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dish soap - soap or detergent used for hand-washing dishes
