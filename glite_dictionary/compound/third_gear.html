third gear

1. NOUN

/ θɝd ɡɪr / (AmE), / θɜːd ɡɪə / (BrE)
the third forward gear in a vehicle's transmission, used to provide a higher speed than first or second gear while offering more torque than higher gears
the third forward gear in a vehicle

Examples

After turning onto the highway, she shifted into third gear for better acceleration.
You shouldn’t try to start the car in third gear because it might stall.
He noticed a strange noise whenever he changed into third gear while driving.

Synset Examples

you shouldn't try to start in third gear

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: second gear - the gear that provides a ratio lower than third gear in a vehicle's transmission
  • Degree Higher: fourth gear - the gear that provides a higher ratio than third gear in a vehicle's transmission
