art object

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt ˈɑbdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ɑːt ˈɒbdʒɪkt / (BrE)
an object, such as a painting or sculpture, considered to have artistic value
an object of artistic value

Examples

The museum recently acquired a rare art object from the 17th century.
Collectors compete fiercely at auctions for valuable art objects.
Each art object in the gallery has been carefully curated for its cultural significance.

Synset Examples

this store sells only objets d'art
it is not known who created this piece

Related Terms
