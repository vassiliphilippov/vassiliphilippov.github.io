business activity

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs ækˌtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ækˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
an action or set of actions undertaken as part of running a commercial enterprise, such as buying, selling, or providing services
action within a commercial enterprise

Examples

The company is expanding its business activity into new international markets.
Government regulations can have a significant impact on business activity during a recession.
Accounting auditors review all business activities to ensure compliance with the law.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commercial activity - activity relating to commerce or trade
  • Degree Higher: economic activity - any activity that involves the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services
