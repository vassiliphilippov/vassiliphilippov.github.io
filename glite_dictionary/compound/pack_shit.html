to pack shit

1. VERB

/ pæk ʃɪt / (AmE), / pæk ʃɪt / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) To prepare and organize one's belongings or personal items, typically before traveling or moving.
organize belongings, usually for travel (vulgar)
informal vulgar

I always forget to pack shit like phone chargers and snacks when I travel.
Make sure you pack shit tonight so we can leave early in the morning.
He rushed to pack shit before the taxi arrived at his apartment.
