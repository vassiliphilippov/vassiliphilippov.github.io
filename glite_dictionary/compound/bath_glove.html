bath glove

1. NOUN

/ bæθ ɡlʌv / (AmE), / bɑːθ ɡlʌv / (BrE)
A mitt made of coarse material, used for washing or scrubbing the body during bathing.
mitt used for washing the body

Examples

She used a bath glove to scrub her arms and legs thoroughly.
A bath glove provides a gentle exfoliation as you wash in the shower.
After buying a new bath glove, he noticed his skin felt much smoother.
