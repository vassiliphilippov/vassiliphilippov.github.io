Genesis creation narrative

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛnɪsɪs kriˈeɪʃən ˈnærətɪv / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnɪsɪs kriˈeɪʃən ˈnærətɪv / (BrE)
the biblical account of God's creation of the universe as described in the Book of Genesis
the biblical story of the creation of the universe

Examples

The Genesis creation narrative is foundational to Judeo-Christian understandings of the world's origin.
Scholars often debate the interpretation of the Genesis creation narrative.
Many artists have depicted scenes from the Genesis creation narrative in their works.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creation story - a story describing the origins of the world and humanity

Etymology

Genesis from Greek *génesis* (origin, creation), referring to the first book of the Bible.
