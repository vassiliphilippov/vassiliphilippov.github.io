Tunguz language

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʊŋɡuːz ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtʊŋɡuːz ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Tungusic language spoken by the Evenki people of eastern Siberia
language of the Evenki people in Siberia

Examples

Only a small number of people still speak the Tunguz language today.
Linguists are working to document the grammar of the Tunguz language.
The Tunguz language belongs to the Tungusic language family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Evenki language - the language spoken by the Evenki people in Siberia

Etymology

From Russian Тунгусский язык (Tungusskiy yazyk), referring to the Evenki language.
