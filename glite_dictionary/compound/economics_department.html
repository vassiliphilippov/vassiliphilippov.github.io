economics department

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈkɑː.nə.mɪks dɪˈpɑrt.mənt / (AmE), / iːˈkɒn.əmɪks dɪˈpɑːt.mənt / (BrE)
the academic division of a college or university responsible for teaching and research in economics
university division for economics studies

Examples

She decided to major in economics after talking with the head of the economics department.
The economics department is organizing a guest lecture on global financial markets next week.
Several students presented their research at the annual economics department conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: department - an academic division in a college or university
