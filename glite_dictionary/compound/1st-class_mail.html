1st-class mail

1. NOUN

/ fɜrst klæs meɪl / (AmE), / fɜːst klɑːs meɪl / (BrE)
a category of mail for letters, postcards, and sealed packages given priority handling and delivery
priority mail service for letters and packages

Examples

I sent the documents by 1st-class mail to ensure they arrived quickly.
Packages shipped as 1st-class mail are usually delivered within a few days.
She prefers to use 1st-class mail for important correspondence.
