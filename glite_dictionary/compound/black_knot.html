black knot

1. NOUN

/ blæk nɑt / (AmE), / blæk nɒt / (BrE)
a fungal disease affecting plum and cherry trees, characterized by black, knot-like swellings on branches and twigs
fungal disease of plum and cherry trees

Examples

The plum orchard was severely affected by black knot last spring.
Gardeners should prune infected branches to manage black knot on cherry trees.
Black knot can seriously reduce fruit yield if left untreated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plant disease - any abnormal condition that impairs the normal function of a plant
