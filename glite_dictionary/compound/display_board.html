display board

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈspleɪ bɔrd / (AmE), / dɪˈspleɪ bɔːd / (BrE)
a board or panel on which information, notices, or items can be displayed for public view
board for public display of information

Examples

The exhibition featured artwork on every display board in the gallery.
A new display board was installed to show arrival and departure times at the station.
Teachers often use a display board to present students' projects in the classroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noticeboard - a board for displaying notices, especially in public areas
  • Synonym: bulletin board - a board for the posting of notices, especially one in a public place
