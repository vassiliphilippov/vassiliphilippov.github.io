cylinder separator

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlɪndər səˈpɛrətər / (AmE), / ˈsɪlɪndə səˈpɛrətə / (BrE)
a machine used to separate grain based on the length of the kernels, typically employing rotary cylinders.
machine for sorting grain by kernel length

Examples

The cylinder separator effectively sorted the wheat by kernel length during processing.
Farmers invested in a new cylinder separator to improve the quality of their grain output.
During harvest season, the cylinder separator ran continuously to handle the large volume of grain.
