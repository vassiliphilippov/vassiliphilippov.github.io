lunch period

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌntʃ ˌpɪriəd / (AmE), / ˈlʌntʃ ˌpɪəriəd / (BrE)
the regular or designated time during the day set aside for eating lunch, especially in schools or workplaces.
designated time for eating lunch

Examples

Students are not allowed to leave the school grounds during lunch period.
The cafeteria is busiest during the lunch period.
She scheduled the meeting so it wouldn't interfere with anyone's lunch period.

Synset Examples

he observed a regular lunchtime

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunchtime - the time at which lunch is usually eaten
