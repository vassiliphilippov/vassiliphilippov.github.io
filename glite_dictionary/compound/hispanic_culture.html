Hispanic culture

1. NOUN

/ hɪˈspænɪk ˈkʌltʃɚ / (AmE), / hɪˈspænɪk ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
The customs, traditions, social practices, and values characteristic of Spanish-speaking communities, especially those of Latin America and Spain.
customs and traditions of Spanish-speaking peoples

Examples

Many schools host events that highlight Hispanic culture during National Hispanic Heritage Month.
Her studies focus on how food traditions are preserved within Hispanic culture.
The museum's new exhibition explores the influence of Hispanic culture on modern art.

Synset Examples

The festival celebrates Hispanic culture and traditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Latino culture - customs and traditions of Latin American peoples
