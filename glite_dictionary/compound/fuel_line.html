fuel line

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuːəl laɪn / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəl laɪn / (BrE)
a tube or pipe that carries fuel from the fuel tank to an engine
pipe carrying fuel to an engine

Examples

The mechanic replaced the fuel line after discovering a leak.
A clogged fuel line can cause the engine to stall unexpectedly.
Be sure to check the fuel line for any cracks during routine maintenance.

Synset Examples

the car wouldn't start because dirt clogged the gas line

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gas line - a pipe that carries gas (especially fuel gas)
