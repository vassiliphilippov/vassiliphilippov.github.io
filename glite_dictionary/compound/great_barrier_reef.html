Great Barrier Reef

1. PROPN

/ ɡreɪt ˈbæriər riːf / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈbæriə riːf / (BrE)
the world's largest coral reef system, located in the Coral Sea off the northeastern coast of Australia
largest coral reef off Australia

Examples

Scientists study marine life extensively at the Great Barrier Reef each year.
The Great Barrier Reef attracts millions of tourists who come to snorkel and dive in its vibrant waters.
Climate change has significantly affected the health of the Great Barrier Reef.
