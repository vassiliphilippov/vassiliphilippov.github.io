Scotland Yard

1. PROPN

/ ˈskɑt.lənd ˌjɑrd / (AmE), / ˈskɒt.lənd ˌjɑːd / (BrE)
the headquarters of the Metropolitan Police Service of London, often used to refer to its detective department
London police headquarters/detective branch
british

Examples

Detectives from Scotland Yard were called in to investigate the high-profile case.
The press conference was held at Scotland Yard to announce updates on the investigation.
Many famous British crime stories feature detectives from Scotland Yard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Metropolitan Police - the police force for Greater London, excluding the City of London

Etymology

Named after Great Scotland Yard, the street in Westminster where the original police headquarters was located.
