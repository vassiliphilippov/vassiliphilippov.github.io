camel racing

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæməl ˌreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkaməl ˌreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport of racing camels, usually over a set distance, popular especially in some Middle Eastern and North African countries.
the sport of racing camels

Examples

Camel racing is a popular sport in the United Arab Emirates and other Gulf countries.
The annual camel racing festival attracts participants from all over the region.
In some areas, children used to be jockeys in camel racing, but now robots are more common.
