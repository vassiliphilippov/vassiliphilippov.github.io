staging area

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪdʒɪŋ ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈsteɪdʒɪŋ ˌeəriə / (BrE)
an area where troops and equipment are assembled and prepared before a military or other large-scale operation
area for assembly before an operation

Examples

The soldiers waited in the staging area until the commander gave the order to advance.
All supplies must be checked and organized at the staging area before deployment.
The airport hangar served as a temporary staging area for the disaster relief team.
