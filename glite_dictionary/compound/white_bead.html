white bead

1. NOUN

/ waɪt biːd / (AmE), / waɪt biːd / (BrE)
A North American herb (Actaea pachypoda) with white, poisonous berries that resemble beads.
North American herb with white poisonous berries

Examples

The berries of the white bead plant are highly poisonous and should not be eaten.
Walking through the forest, she pointed out a cluster of white bead growing alongside the path.
Because of its distinctive fruit, white bead is also known as doll's eyes.
