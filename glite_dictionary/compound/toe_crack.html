toe crack

1. NOUN

/ toʊ kræk / (AmE), / təʊ kræk / (BrE)
a crack or split occurring on the front part of a horse's hoof, especially in the hindfoot
crack in the forepart of a horse's hoof

Examples

The farrier treated the horse's toe crack to prevent further splitting.
A deep toe crack can cause lameness if left unattended.
Prevention of a toe crack involves proper hoof maintenance and regular trimming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quarter crack - crack on the side (quarter) of a horse's hoof
