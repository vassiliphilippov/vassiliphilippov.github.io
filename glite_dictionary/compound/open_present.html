to open present

1. VERB

/ ˈoʊpən ˈprɛzənt / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən ˈprɛzənt / (BrE)
to unwrap a gift in order to see what is inside
unwrap a gift

Examples

On Christmas morning, the children rushed to open presents under the tree.
She couldn't wait to open her present to see what was inside.
At the party, everyone gathered around to watch him open his present.

Synset Examples

The kids were eager to open their presents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to unwrap - to remove the covering from something, especially a gift
