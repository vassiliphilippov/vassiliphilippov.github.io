rough green snake

1. NOUN

/ rʌf ɡrin sneɪk / (AmE), / rʌf ɡriːn sneɪk / (BrE)
a slender, bright green, non-venomous snake (Opheodrys aestivus) native to the southern and eastern United States, known for its rough-textured scales
a green non-venomous snake of the US

Examples

We spotted a rough green snake slithering through the tall grass near the pond.
The rough green snake relies on its bright color to camouflage among the leaves.
Many people keep the rough green snake as a harmless pet due to its calm temperament.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smooth green snake - a similar snake species with smooth scales
