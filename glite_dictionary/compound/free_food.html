free food

1. NOUN

/ friː fuːd / (AmE), / friː fuːd / (BrE)
food that is given or provided without needing to be paid for
food provided at no cost

Examples

The college event attracted many students with the promise of free food.
We went to the open house mainly for the free food and drinks.
Every Friday, the cafe offers free food samples to passersby.

Synset Examples

The festival provided each guest a token for free food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: complimentary - provided free of charge, especially as a courtesy or favor
  • Antonym: paid food - food which must be purchased
