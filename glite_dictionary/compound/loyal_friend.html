loyal friend

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔɪ.əl frɛnd / (AmE), / ˈlɔɪ.əl frɛnd / (BrE)
a person who is consistently supportive and faithful as a companion
faithful and supportive companion

Examples

She proved to be a loyal friend during the most difficult times of my life.
Everyone needs at least one loyal friend they can trust completely.
Even after many years apart, he remained a loyal friend to Sarah.

Synset Examples

He is a loyal friend who always supports me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friend - a person whom one knows and with whom one has a bond of mutual affection
