quick trip

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk trɪp / (AmE), / kwɪk trɪp / (BrE)
a journey to a place and back that is done in a short time
brief or fast journey

Examples

She made a quick trip to the grocery store before dinner.
We're planning a quick trip to Paris for the weekend.
I have time for a quick trip to the post office before the meeting.

Synset Examples

He made a quick trip there and back in an hour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: short trip - a journey with a short duration
  • Antonym: long trip - a journey that takes a long time
