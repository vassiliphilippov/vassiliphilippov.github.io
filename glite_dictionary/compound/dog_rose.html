dog rose

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ ˈroʊz / (AmE), / dɒɡ ˈrəʊz / (BrE)
A wild rose with prickly stems and pink or white scentless flowers, native to Europe.
wild European rose species

Examples

We saw a beautiful dog rose blooming along the country lane.
The hedgerow was full of wild dog roses in summer.
She picked a single dog rose to press in her notebook.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rose - a woody perennial flowering plant of the genus Rosa
