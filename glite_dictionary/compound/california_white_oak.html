California white oak

1. NOUN

/ ˌkælɪˈfɔrnjə ˈwaɪt oʊk / (AmE), / ˌkælɪˈfɔːnɪə ˈwaɪt əʊk / (BrE)
a tall, graceful deciduous oak tree native to California, with leathery leaves and slender pointed acorns
oak tree species native to California

Examples

The California white oak can live for several centuries in its native environment.
Wildlife depends on the shade provided by the California white oak during hot summers.
Botanists often study the leaf structure of the California white oak to distinguish it from other oaks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: valley oak - oak tree native to California, often synonymous with California white oak
