winter cress

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪn.tɚ krɛs / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə krɛs / (BrE)
a yellow-flowered plant of the cress family (genus Barbarea), cultivated for use in winter salads
a plant used in winter salads

Examples

Winter cress is a hardy vegetable that can be harvested throughout the colder months.
A fresh salad made with winter cress tastes especially good in early spring.
Gardeners often plant winter cress for its nutritious leaves during the off-season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cress - a small edible plant with peppery leaves

2. NOUN

/ ˈwɪntər krɛs / (AmE), / ˈwɪntə krɛs / (BrE)
A yellow-flowered Eurasian plant of the genus Barbarea, often cultivated for use in winter salads.
yellow-flowered salad plant of Barbarea genus

Examples

In early spring, winter cress provides a welcome splash of yellow flowers in the garden.
Some foragers seek out winter cress for its tender leaves, which add a peppery flavor to salads.
The fields along the river are filled with blooming winter cress each April.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellow rocket - another name for winter cress, especially Barbarea vulgaris
  • Degree Higher: cress - a general term for various plants with edible leaves, often used in salads
