plan of attack

1. NOUN

/ plæn əv əˈtæk / (AmE), / plæn əv əˈtæk / (BrE)
a strategy or set of actions designed to deal with a particular problem or achieve a goal
strategy for dealing with a problem

Examples

The team met to develop a plan of attack before starting the project.
Her plan of attack for the exam included making detailed notes and regular revision.
We need a new plan of attack if we want to solve this problem before the deadline.

Synset Examples

his approach to every problem is to draw up a list of pros and cons
an attack on inflation
his plan of attack was misguided

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strategy - a plan of action designed to achieve a long-term or overall aim
  • Synonym: approach - a way of dealing with something; a method
