hotel detective

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈtɛl dɪˈtɛktɪv / (AmE), / həʊˈtɛl dɪˈtɛktɪv / (BrE)
a private detective employed by a hotel to investigate theft, maintain security, and handle disturbances on the premises
detective employed by a hotel

Examples

The hotel detective quickly discovered who had been stealing from the guests' rooms.
Whenever there was trouble in the lobby, the hotel detective was the first on the scene.
Some luxury hotels employ their own hotel detective to ensure the safety of their clients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detective - a person, especially a police officer, whose occupation is to investigate and solve crimes
