real solution

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːəl səˈluːʃən / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl səˈluːʃən / (BrE)
an effective or genuine answer or resolution to a problem or difficulty
effective answer or resolution

Examples

After months of searching, they came up with a real solution to reduce energy costs.
We need a real solution, not just more temporary fixes and excuses.
The new technology offers a real solution to water scarcity in rural areas.

Synset Examples

She finally found a real solution to the problem.
