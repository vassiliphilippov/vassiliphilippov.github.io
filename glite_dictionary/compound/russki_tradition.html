Russki tradition

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌski trəˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈrʌski trəˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
the traditional customs and practices characteristic of Russian culture
customs of Russian culture

Examples

Many Russki traditions are celebrated with music, dance, and hearty meals.
He studied the influence of Russki tradition on contemporary folk art.
Her wedding incorporated several Russki traditions handed down through generations.

Synset Examples

She enjoyed learning about Russki traditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Russian tradition - traditional customs of Russian culture

Etymology

'Russki' from Russian 'русский' (russkiy), meaning 'Russian,' combined with 'tradition' from Latin 'traditio' (a handing over).
