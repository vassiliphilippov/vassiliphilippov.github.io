City of Light

1. PROPN

/ ˌsɪti əv ˈlaɪt / (AmE), / ˌsɪti əv ˈlaɪt / (BrE)
a popular nickname for Paris, the capital of France, known for its contributions to art, culture, and enlightenment
nickname for Paris, France

Examples

Many artists and writers flocked to the City of Light to find inspiration in its vibrant culture.
She had always dreamed of seeing the Eiffel Tower in the City of Light.
The City of Light captivated travelers with its beauty and charm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Paris - the capital and largest city of France, known as 'the City of Light'

Etymology

Nicknamed in the 19th century for Paris's early adoption of street lighting and its reputation as a center of enlightenment.
