Mickey Mouse ring

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪki ˌmaʊs rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɪki ˌmaʊs rɪŋ / (BrE)
A ring decorated with an image or likeness of the cartoon character Mickey Mouse.
ring featuring Mickey Mouse

Examples

She wore her favorite Mickey Mouse ring to the party.
He gave his niece a sparkling Mickey Mouse ring for her birthday.
Collectors often look for vintage Mickey Mouse rings online.

Synset Examples

He bought her a Mickey Mouse ring as a fun gift.

Etymology

From the name of the cartoon character Mickey Mouse, created by Walt Disney.
