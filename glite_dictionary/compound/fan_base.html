fan base

1. NOUN

/ fæn beɪs / (AmE), / fæn beɪs / (BrE)
A group of enthusiastic supporters of a person, team, product, or entertainment franchise.
group of enthusiastic supporters

Examples

The band's fan base grew rapidly after their first hit single was released.
With a strong fan base, the sports team sold out every game this season.
She credits her loyal fan base for supporting her throughout her long acting career.

Synset Examples

EverCrack has a loyal fan base despite its flaws.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: following - group of supporters or admirers
  • Degree Lower: audience - people assembled to watch or listen to something
