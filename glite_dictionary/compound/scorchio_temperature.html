scorchio temperature

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɔrtʃioʊ ˈtɛmp(ə)rətʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈskɔːtʃiəʊ ˈtɛmp(ə)rətʃə / (BrE)
informal expression describing extremely hot weather or conditions.
extremely hot weather (informal)
informal british

Examples

It's another scorchio temperature today, so remember to stay hydrated.
The city experienced several days of scorchio temperatures last summer.
We cancelled the football match because of the scorchio temperature in the afternoon.

Synset Examples

The weather forecast called for scorchio temperatures all week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heatwave - period of abnormally hot weather

Etymology

From 'scorchio,' an informal term popularized in British media, based on 'scorch' + mock Italian-style ending -io.
