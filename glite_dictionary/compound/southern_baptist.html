Southern Baptist

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌðɚn ˈbæptɪst / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən ˈbæptɪst / (BrE)
a member of the Southern Baptist Convention, a major Protestant Christian denomination in the United States
member of Southern Baptist Convention

Examples

She grew up as a Southern Baptist in Texas.
Many Southern Baptists attend church services every Sunday.
The church is affiliated with the Southern Baptist Convention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Baptist - a member of a Protestant Christian denomination that baptizes believers by immersion
