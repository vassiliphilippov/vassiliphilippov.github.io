moving expense

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvɪŋ ɪkˌspɛns / (AmE), / ˈmuːvɪŋ ɪkˌspɛns / (BrE)
the cost incurred when relocating from one residence to another, typically covering transportation, packing, and related services
cost of relocating residence

Examples

The company agreed to reimburse his moving expenses when he transferred to the new office.
She kept all the receipts related to her moving expenses for tax purposes.
Unexpected moving expenses made the relocation more costly than they had planned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relocation cost - the total cost associated with moving to another place, including moving expenses and other fees
