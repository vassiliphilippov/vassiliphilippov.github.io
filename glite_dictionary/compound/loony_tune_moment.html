loony tune moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːni tuːn ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈluːni tjuːn ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
an incident or episode characterized by absurd or silly behavior
an instance of absurd or silly behavior
informal

Examples

It was such a loony tune moment when he danced on the table at the party.
Everyone burst out laughing at her loony tune moment during the meeting.
The movie is full of loony tune moments that defy all logic.

Synset Examples

She described his behavior as a loony tune moment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy moment - a moment marked by wild or irrational behavior
  • Antonym: sensible moment - a moment marked by sound judgment or reason

Etymology

From 'Looney Tunes', the title of a famous animated cartoon series, used here figuratively for silliness or absurdity.
