bowling ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊlɪŋ bɔl / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlɪŋ bɔːl / (BrE)
a large, heavy ball with finger holes, used to knock down pins in the sport of bowling
large ball used in bowling

Examples

He picked up the bowling ball and aimed for the center pin.
Each bowling ball has three holes for your fingers.
She chose a bright blue bowling ball for her game.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: bowling pin - the pin knocked down by the bowling ball
  • Synonym: bowling - the sport in which a bowling ball is used
