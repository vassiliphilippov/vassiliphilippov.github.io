mammoth skeleton

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæməθ ˈskɛlɪtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈmæməθ ˈskɛlɪtən / (BrE)
the fossilized or preserved remains of a mammoth, especially its bones arranged in the form they appeared during life
fossilized remains of a mammoth

Examples

The museum recently acquired a complete mammoth skeleton from Siberia.
Scientists carefully excavated the mammoth skeleton from the icy permafrost.
She gazed in awe at the enormous mammoth skeleton towering above the visitors.

Synset Examples

They found a mammoth skeleton preserved in the ice.
