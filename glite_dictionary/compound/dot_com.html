dot com

1. NOUN

/ dɑt kɑm / (AmE), / dɒt kɒm / (BrE)
A company that conducts its business primarily on the internet, especially one with a website whose address ends in '.com'.
internet-based company

Examples

Many dot com companies emerged during the late 1990s technology boom.
She works for a successful dot com that specializes in online marketing.
Several dot coms collapsed when the internet bubble burst.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: internet company - A business operating primarily on the internet

Etymology

From the '.com' domain used for commercial websites; 'dot com' was popularized during the 1990s internet boom.
