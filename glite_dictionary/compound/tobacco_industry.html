tobacco industry

1. NOUN

/ təˈbækoʊ ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / təˈbækəʊ ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector of business that manufactures and sells products made from tobacco, such as cigarettes and cigars
business sector making and selling tobacco products

Examples

The tobacco industry has faced numerous lawsuits over the health effects of smoking.
Many governments have imposed strict regulations on the tobacco industry to reduce smoking rates.
She conducted research into the marketing practices of the tobacco industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cigarette industry - business focused on manufacturing and selling cigarettes
  • Synonym: alcohol industry - sector producing and distributing alcoholic beverages
