video display

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪdioʊ dɪˈspleɪ / (AmE), / ˈvɪdiəʊ dɪˈspleɪ / (BrE)
an electronic device that shows information visually, such as a screen or monitor
device for visual display of information

Examples

The laboratory uses a video display to monitor the experiment in real time.
He adjusted the settings on the video display for better picture quality.
The technician replaced the damaged video display with a newer model.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monitor - a screen that displays images and text from a computer or other device
  • Synonym: screen - a flat surface on which images or data are displayed electronically
