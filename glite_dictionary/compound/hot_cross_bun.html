hot cross bun

1. NOUN

/ hɑt krɑs bʌn / (AmE), / hɒt krɒs bʌn / (BrE)
a small, sweet, spiced bread roll containing dried fruit, marked with a cross on top and often eaten at Easter
spiced sweet bun with a cross, eaten at Easter

Examples

Every Easter, my grandmother bakes hot cross buns for the whole family to enjoy.
The sweet smell of hot cross buns filled the bakery on Good Friday morning.
She bought a dozen hot cross buns for the church bake sale.

Etymology

Named for the cross design on the bun, traditionally eaten hot on Good Friday.
