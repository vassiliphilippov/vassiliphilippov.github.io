mountain clubmoss

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntən ˈklʌbˌmɑs / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˈklʌbmɒs / (BrE)
A small evergreen plant found in northern Europe and America, resembling a miniature fir tree; a species of clubmoss.
a small evergreen clubmoss plant

Examples

The mountain clubmoss thrives in cold, rocky alpine regions where few other plants survive.
Hikers in Scandinavia often spot the distinctive mountain clubmoss on forest floors.
Botanists studied the mountain clubmoss for its unique adaptation to harsh northern climates.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: clubmoss - a primitive, spore-bearing plant resembling a moss or small conifer
