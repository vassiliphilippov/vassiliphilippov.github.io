straight flute

1. NOUN

/ streɪt fluːt / (AmE), / streɪt fluːt / (BrE)
a type of rock drill or drill bit with grooves (flutes) that run straight along its length, rather than spiral or helical.
drill bit with straight grooves

Examples

The engineer recommended using a straight flute for drilling into concrete walls.
This straight flute is designed for high-precision rock sampling.
Unlike spiral designs, a straight flute offers faster chip removal in certain materials.
