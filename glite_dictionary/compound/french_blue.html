French blue

1. NOUN

/ frɛntʃ blu / (AmE), / frɛntʃ blu / (BrE)
a brilliant ultramarine pigment prepared artificially, used especially in painting and dyeing
artificial bright ultramarine pigment

Examples

She chose French blue for the walls to achieve a vibrant, artistic effect in the living room.
The artist mixed French blue with other pigments to create subtle shades for her landscape painting.
Textiles dyed with French blue have a rich, eye-catching hue popular in fashion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ultramarine - a deep blue pigment originally made from lapis lazuli
