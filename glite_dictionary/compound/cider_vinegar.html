cider vinegar

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪdər ˌvɪnɪɡər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdə ˌvɪnɪɡə / (BrE)
Vinegar made from fermented apple cider, commonly used in cooking and as a condiment.
vinegar made from apple cider

Examples

She drizzled a little cider vinegar over the salad for extra flavor.
Many natural remedies recommend using cider vinegar for its health benefits.
I substituted cider vinegar for white vinegar in the recipe and it worked perfectly.
