barn millet

1. NOUN

/ bɑrn ˈmɪlɪt / (AmE), / bɑːn ˈmɪlɪt / (BrE)
a coarse annual grass, often considered a weed, sometimes used for hay or grazing
a coarse grass used for hay or grazing

Examples

The cattle grazed freely on the field of barn millet during the summer months.
Some farmers harvest barn millet as a low-cost source of hay.
Because barn millet grows quickly, it can become a troublesome weed in cultivated fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millet - a type of small-seeded grain plant
