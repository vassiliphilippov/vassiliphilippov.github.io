peccant practice

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛkənt ˈpræktɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɛkənt ˈpræktɪs / (BrE)
an action or behavior that is immoral, unethical, or wrong, especially within an organization or institution
immoral or wrong behavior

The board investigated the peccant practice that led to the financial scandal.
