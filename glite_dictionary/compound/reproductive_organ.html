reproductive organ

1. NOUN

/ ɹiˌproʊˈdʌktɪv ˈɔɹɡən / (AmE), / ɹiˌprəʊˈdʌktɪv ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
An organ in animals or plants that is involved in sexual reproduction, such as testes, ovaries, stamens, or pistils.
organ involved in sexual reproduction
medical

Examples

The flowers' reproductive organs include both stamens and pistils.
Damage to a reproductive organ can affect fertility.
In many animals, the reproductive organs develop during puberty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gonad - an organ that produces gametes; a testis or ovary
  • Synonym: sex organ - an organ involved in sexual reproduction
