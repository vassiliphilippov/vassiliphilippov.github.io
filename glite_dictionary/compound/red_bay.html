red bay

1. NOUN

/ rɛd beɪ / (AmE), / rɛd beɪ / (BrE)
a small tree native to the southern United States with dark red heartwood, known for its aromatic leaves
tree with dark red heartwood, southern US

Examples

The leaves of the red bay are often used in cooking for their flavor.
Hurricanes have threatened the red bay populations along the Gulf Coast.
The red bay is valued for its beautiful, reddish heartwood.
