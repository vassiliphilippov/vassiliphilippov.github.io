West Sussex

1. PROPN

/ wɛst ˈsʌsɪks / (AmE), / wɛst ˈsʌsɪks / (BrE)
a county in southern England, bordering East Sussex, Hampshire, and Surrey, with the English Channel to the south
county in southern England
british
