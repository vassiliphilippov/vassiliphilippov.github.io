drone pipe

1. NOUN

/ ˈdroʊn ˌpaɪp / (AmE), / ˈdrəʊn ˌpaɪp / (BrE)
a pipe of the bagpipe that produces a single continuous tone, usually serving as a constant background note
bagpipe pipe producing continuous tone

Examples

The drone pipe of the bagpipe provides a rich, continuous background sound to the melody.
She spent hours tuning her drone pipe before the performance.
Traditional Scottish music is known for the distinctive hum of the drone pipe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drone - a sustained monotone musical note or tone
