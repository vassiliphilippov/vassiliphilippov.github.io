button snakeroot

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌtən ˈsneɪkˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈbʌtən ˈsneɪkˌruːt / (BrE)
a coarse, prickly perennial plant (eryngo) with aromatic roots, native to the southeastern United States, often mistaken for rattlesnake master
a prickly perennial plant of the southeastern US

Examples

The roots of the button snakeroot have been used in traditional remedies for generations.
We found a dense patch of button snakeroot while hiking in the pine barrens.
Many gardeners appreciate the unique look of button snakeroot in native plant beds.
