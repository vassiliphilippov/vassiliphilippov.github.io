teriyaki chicken

1. NOUN

/ tɛriˈjɑki ˈtʃɪkən / (AmE), / tɛrɪˈjɑːki ˈtʃɪkɪn / (BrE)
A Japanese dish consisting of chicken glazed with a sweet soy-based sauce, often grilled or broiled.
chicken cooked in teriyaki sauce

Examples

I ordered the teriyaki chicken for lunch at the new Japanese restaurant.
She loves making homemade teriyaki chicken with steamed rice and vegetables.
The cafeteria offered teriyaki chicken as today's special.

Synset Examples

Have you tried the Japanese pizza with teriyaki chicken?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teriyaki - Japanese cooking method of grilling or broiling with soy sauce glaze

Etymology

Japanese, from 'teriyaki': 'teri' (shine) + 'yaki' (grilled or broiled).
