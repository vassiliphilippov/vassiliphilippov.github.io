cultural attache

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌltʃɚəl æˌtæʃˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərəl ˌætæˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
a diplomat attached to an embassy who is responsible for promoting and managing cultural relations between their country and the host country
diplomat in charge of cultural affairs

Examples

The French cultural attache organized an exhibition of French art at the local museum.
As a cultural attache, her job included attending concerts, festivals, and other cultural events.
He was appointed cultural attache to foster greater understanding between the two countries.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: attache - a person on the staff of an ambassador, usually with a specialized area

Etymology

‘Attache’ from French, meaning 'attached', referring to someone assigned to an embassy.
