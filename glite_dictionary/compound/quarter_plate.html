quarter plate

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɔrtər pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə pleɪt / (BrE)
a photographic plate measuring 3.25 inches by 4.25 inches, used historically in photography
photographic plate size: 3.25 x 4.25 inches

Examples

The old camera still had a quarter plate inside from its last use.
She collected vintage quarter plates for her photography exhibit on early techniques.
Photographers in the 19th century often preferred the quarter plate format for its convenient size.
