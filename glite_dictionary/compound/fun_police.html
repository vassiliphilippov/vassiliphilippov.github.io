fun police

1. NOUN

/ fʌn pəˈlis / (AmE), / fʌn pəˈliːs / (BrE)
a person who prevents others from enjoying themselves, often by enforcing strict rules or acting disapprovingly
person who spoils others' fun
informal

Examples

Don't be such a fun police and let us dance a little longer tonight.
Whenever we start telling jokes, Sarah turns into the fun police.
The fun police showed up and ended the party early.

Synset Examples

Don't be such a fun police; let us enjoy!
He plays the fun police whenever we plan outings.
The fun police ruined the atmosphere with strict rules.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: killjoy - a person who spoils the enjoyment of others
  • Synonym: wet blanket - a person who discourages others from having fun
