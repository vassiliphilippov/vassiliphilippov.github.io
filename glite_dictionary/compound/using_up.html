using up

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuzɪŋ ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈjuːzɪŋ ˌʌp / (BrE)
the act of spending, consuming, or exhausting something completely
the act of consuming or exhausting something

Examples

The rapid using up of natural resources is a major global concern.
His using up of all the printer paper annoyed everyone in the office.
There was a gradual using up of supplies during the long winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consumption - the act of consuming something
