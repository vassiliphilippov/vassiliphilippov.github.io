soft shield fern

1. NOUN

/ sɔft ʃild fɜrn / (AmE), / sɒft ʃiːld fɜːn / (BrE)
A European species of shield fern (Polystichum setiferum) cultivated in many varieties for ornamental purposes.
European ornamental shield fern species

Examples

Many gardeners prefer the soft shield fern for its delicate fronds and shade tolerance.
The soft shield fern thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade.
Several varieties of soft shield fern are popular in shaded garden borders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shield fern - any fern of the genus Polystichum distinguished by their shield-shaped sori
