situation analysis

1. NOUN

/ sɪtʃuˈeɪʃən əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / sɪtjʊˈeɪʃən əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
the process of systematically collecting and evaluating information about an organization's internal and external environment to identify factors that may affect its performance and strategy
systematic evaluation of an organization's environment

Examples

Before launching the new product, the marketing team conducted a thorough situation analysis to understand current market trends.
A situation analysis can reveal both opportunities and threats that might otherwise be overlooked by decision-makers.
Successful strategic planning often begins with a detailed situation analysis of the internal and external business environment.

Synset Examples

The situation analysis looks at both the macro-environmental factors that affect many firms within the environment and the micro-environmental factors that specifically affect the firm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: SWOT analysis - an analysis of strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats
