postal service

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstl ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstl ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
the official system for sending and delivering letters, parcels, and other mail
official mail delivery system

Examples

The postal service has introduced new measures to speed up mail delivery.
Many people rely on the postal service to receive important documents and packages.
After the storm, the postal service was delayed for several days in the area.

Synset Examples

the mail handles billions of items every day
he works for the United States mail service
in England they call mail ‘the post’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mail service - the system for handling mail
  • Synonym: post office - a public office or building for mail collection and delivery

Etymology

From 'postal' (from post, mail delivery) + 'service' (system that provides a public utility).
