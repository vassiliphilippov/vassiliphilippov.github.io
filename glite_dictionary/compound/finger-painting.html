finger-painting

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪŋɡɚ ˌpeɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə ˌpeɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
A method of painting in which the fingers are used instead of brushes to spread the paint.
painting technique using fingers

Examples

In kindergarten, children often enjoy finger-painting because it allows them to be creative and messy.
The museum displayed a section dedicated to modern finger-painting techniques.
She tried finger-painting for the first time and found it surprisingly relaxing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to finger-paint - to paint using one's fingers rather than brushes
  • Degree Higher: painting - the activity or art of making pictures using paint
