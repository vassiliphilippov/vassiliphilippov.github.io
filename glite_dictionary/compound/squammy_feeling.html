squammy feeling

1. NOUN

/ ˈskwæmi ˈfiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskwæmi ˈfiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
an unpleasant or uneasy sensation, typically one that is hard to define or describe
indefinable uneasy sensation

Examples

She had a squammy feeling as she entered the old, deserted house.
There was a sudden squammy feeling in the room after John’s awkward comment.
He couldn’t explain the squammy feeling that crept over him before the job interview.

Synset Examples

The squammy feeling after that meal made me regret it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: queasy feeling - a feeling of nausea or discomfort in the stomach
  • Antonym: comfortable feeling - a sensation of comfort or ease
