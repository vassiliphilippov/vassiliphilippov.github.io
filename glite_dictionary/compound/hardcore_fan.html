hardcore fan

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrdˌkɔr fæn / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌkɔː fæn / (BrE)
a person who is extremely devoted or enthusiastic about a particular person, group, activity, or interest
extremely devoted admirer or enthusiast

Examples

As a hardcore fan of the series, she owns every piece of merchandise ever released.
Only a hardcore fan would wait in line overnight for concert tickets.
He’s been a hardcore fan of the football team since he was a child.

Synset Examples

He’s a hardcore fan of the band since the 80s.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fan - a person with a strong interest or admiration for something
  • Degree Higher: superfan - a person with an even higher level of devotion than a typical fan
