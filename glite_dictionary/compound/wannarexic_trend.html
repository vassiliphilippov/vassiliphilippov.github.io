wannarexic trend

1. NOUN

/ ˌwɑn.əˈrɛks.ɪk trɛnd / (AmE), / ˌwɒn.əˈrɛk.sɪk trɛnd / (BrE)
A trend, especially among young people, of expressing a desire to have anorexia nervosa or to appear anorexic, often without having a clinical diagnosis.
trend of aspiring to be anorexic
informal medical

Examples

The wannarexic trend on social media has alarmed many mental health professionals.
Some young people feel pressured to join the wannarexic trend to fit in with certain peer groups.
Experts are seeking ways to counteract the influence of the wannarexic trend on vulnerable teenagers.

Synset Examples

The wannarexic trend is concerning among young girls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anorexia nervosa - an eating disorder characterized by extremely low body weight and fear of gaining weight

Etymology

Blend of 'wannabe' and 'anorexic'; 'wannarexic' emerged in the early 2000s to describe those who imitate anorexic behavior without a diagnosis.
