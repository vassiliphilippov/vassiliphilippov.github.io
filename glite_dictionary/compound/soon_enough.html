soon enough

1. ADV

/ suːn ɪˈnʌf / (AmE), / suːn ɪˈnʌf / (BrE)
At a sufficiently early time; without being too late.
without being too late

Examples

If you leave now, you'll arrive soon enough for the meeting.
Don't worry, you'll understand the concept soon enough.
The train will come soon enough, so there's no need to rush.

Synset Examples

we made it to the party in time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in time - early enough; without being late
  • Antonym: too late - not early enough; after the acceptable time
