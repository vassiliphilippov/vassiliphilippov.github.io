sports announcer

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrts əˌnaʊnsər / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts əˌnaʊnsə / (BrE)
a person who reads sports news or provides live commentary on sporting events, especially on radio or television
person who presents or comments on sports broadcasts

Examples

The sports announcer delivered a play-by-play account of the thrilling soccer match.
After years as a sports announcer, he became a familiar voice for baseball fans across the country.
She dreamed of becoming a sports announcer and covering the Olympics on television.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commentator - a person who provides live commentary during events, especially sports
  • Degree Lower: newsreader - a person who reads news reports on radio or television
