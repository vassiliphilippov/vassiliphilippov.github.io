mop up

1. NOUN

/ mɑp ʌp / (AmE), / mɒp ʌp / (BrE)
a final action taken to complete or finish off a process, task, or operation, especially after the main part has been done
final action to complete a process

Examples

After the main negotiations ended, a mop up session was held to finalize the details.
The military sent in troops for the mop up once the main battle was over.
Cleanup crews were called for a mop up after the festival had finished.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conclusion - the end or finish of an event or process
  • Synonym: wrap-up - a summary or final stage of something
