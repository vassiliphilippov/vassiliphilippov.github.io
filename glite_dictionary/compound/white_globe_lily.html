white globe lily

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ɡloʊb ˈlɪli / (AmE), / waɪt ɡləʊb ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A type of globe lily found in southern California, with open branched clusters of egg-shaped white flowers.
globe lily with egg-shaped white flowers

Examples

In the spring, the hills were covered with blooming white globe lilies.
Botanists identified the rare white globe lily growing in the canyon.
The delicate petals of the white globe lily shimmered in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: globe lily - a plant of the genus Calochortus with globe-shaped flowers
