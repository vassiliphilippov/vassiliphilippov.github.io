a nibble of

1. NOUN

/ ə ˈnɪbəl ʌv / (AmE), / ə ˈnɪbəl ɒv / (BrE)
a small bite or portion of something, especially food
a small bite of something

Examples

She took a nibble of the apple and smiled.
Would you like a nibble of this cheese before dinner?
The mouse sneaked up and had a nibble of the cracker.

Synset Examples

She had a nibble of the chocolate cake.
The rabbit enjoyed a nibble of fresh lettuce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nibble - a small bite of food
