dogbane family

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔɡˌbeɪn ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡbeɪn ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of chiefly tropical trees, shrubs, or herbs, often with milky sap and showy flowers, many of which are sources of medicinal drugs (family Apocynaceae)
a family of tropical plants with milky sap

Examples

Several species in the dogbane family produce compounds used in traditional medicine.
The dogbane family includes colorful flowering plants often found in tropical climates.
Some members of the dogbane family are grown as ornamentals due to their attractive flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Apocynaceae - the scientific name for the dogbane family

Etymology

Named for the dogbane plant (genus Apocynum) originally included in the family; from Greek 'apokynon' meaning 'dog killer'.
