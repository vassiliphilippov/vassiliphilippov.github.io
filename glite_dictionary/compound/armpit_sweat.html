armpit sweat

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrmˌpɪt swɛt / (AmE), / ˈɑːmpɪt swet / (BrE)
sweat produced in the area under the arms, typically in the armpits
sweat under the arms

Examples

David could feel armpit sweat gathering on his shirt during the interview.
The hot weather caused noticeable armpit sweat marks on her blouse.
He used deodorant to reduce armpit sweat before going to the gym.

Synset Examples

The heat made his armpit sweat excessively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perspiration - the process of sweating, or the moisture produced by it
  • Synonym: underarm sweat - sweat found in the underarm or armpit area
