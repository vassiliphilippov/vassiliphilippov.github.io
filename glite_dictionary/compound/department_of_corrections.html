Department of Corrections

1. NOUN

/ dəˈpɑːrtmənt əv kəˈrɛkʃənz / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtmənt əv kəˈrɛkʃənz / (BrE)
the part of a government responsible for overseeing prisons and the management, rehabilitation, and supervision of convicted offenders
government agency for prisons and offender management

Examples

After his release, he continued to work with the Department of Corrections to help former inmates transition back into society.
The governor announced new reforms within the Department of Corrections aimed at reducing recidivism rates.
She applied for a position as a counselor with the state Department of Corrections last summer.

Synset Examples

for a career in corrections turn to the web site of the New Jersey Department of Corrections

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prison - a building where people are kept as punishment for a crime
  • Synonym: penitentiary - a prison for people convicted of serious crimes
