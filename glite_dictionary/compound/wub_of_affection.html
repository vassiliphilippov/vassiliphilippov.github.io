wub of affection

1. NOUN

/ wʌb əv əˈfɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / wʌb əv əˈfɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
an informal or playful expression of love or fondness toward someone or something
informal expression of affection
informal

Examples

He greeted his cat with a warm wub of affection each morning.
She gave her boyfriend a big hug and a wub of affection before he left.
The child smiled, happy to receive a wub of affection from his grandmother.

Synset Examples

She gave her dog a wub of affection.

Etymology

Playful alteration of 'love', mimicking baby talk.
