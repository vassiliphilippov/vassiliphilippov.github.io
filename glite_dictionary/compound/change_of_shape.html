change of shape

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv ʃeɪp / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv ʃeɪp / (BrE)
the action or process by which the shape of something is altered
alteration of form

Examples

Heating the metal caused a change of shape as it became more malleable.
The artist's sculpture demonstrated a remarkable change of shape from one angle to another.
Long-term pressure on the sofa cushions led to a gradual change of shape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deformation - the process of changing shape, especially in physics or engineering
