house nigger

1. NOUN

/ haʊs ˈnɪɡər / (AmE), / haʊs ˈnɪɡə / (BrE)
A highly offensive and derogatory term, historically used to refer to a Black slave who worked or lived in the slave owner's house, or more generally to a Black person seen as serving the interests of white people over those of other Black people; now considered extremely offensive and inappropriate in any context except discussions about the history of racism and language.
offensive term for Black person serving whites
offensive vulgar

Examples

The documentary discussed how the term house nigger was used to divide enslaved people.
He condemned the use of house nigger as both racist and deeply hurtful.
Historians warn against using house nigger outside educational or historical contexts due to its offensive nature.

Synset Examples

The term 'house nigger' reflects a painful history of slavery.
