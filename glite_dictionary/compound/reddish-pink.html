reddish-pink

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛdɪʃ pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈrɛdɪʃ pɪŋk / (BrE)
Of a pink color that has a noticeable tint of red.
pink with red tint

Examples

She chose a reddish-pink lipstick that complemented her outfit perfectly.
The roses in the garden were a beautiful reddish-pink shade, blending easily with the other flowers.
A reddish-pink glow lit up the clouds at sunrise.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: pink - a pale red color
  • Degree Higher: red - the color at the end of the visible spectrum next to orange
