breadcrumb topping

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛdˌkrʌm ˈtɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrɛdkrʌm ˈtɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
A layer of breadcrumbs, sometimes mixed with butter or seasoning, used as a topping on dishes to add texture and flavor.
breadcrumb layer used as a topping

Examples

She sprinkled a breadcrumb topping over the macaroni and cheese before baking it in the oven.
The breadcrumb topping added a satisfying crunch to the creamy casserole.
For a golden crust, brush the breadcrumb topping with a little melted butter before cooking.

Synset Examples

The recipe included a breadcrumb topping for added crunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crumble topping - a topping made from flour, butter, and sugar, used for desserts
