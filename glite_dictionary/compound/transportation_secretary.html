Transportation Secretary

1. NOUN

/ ˌtrænspərˈteɪʃən səˈkrɛtɛri / (AmE), / ˌtrænspɔːˈteɪʃən sɪˈkrɛtəri / (BrE)
the head of the United States Department of Transportation, responsible for federal transportation policy and administration
US government cabinet official for transportation
american

Examples

The Transportation Secretary announced new regulations to improve airline safety.
As Transportation Secretary, she prioritized investment in high-speed rail.
The president nominated an experienced administrator as the next Transportation Secretary.
