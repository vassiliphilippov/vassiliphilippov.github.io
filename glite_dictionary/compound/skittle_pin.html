skittle pin

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪtəl pɪn / (AmE), / ˈskɪt.əl pɪn / (BrE)
A wooden bowling pin used in the games of skittles or ninepins, especially in British English.
bowling pin for skittles or ninepins
british

Examples

He knocked down every skittle pin on his first throw.
The children set up the skittle pins at the end of the alley before starting the game.
Each skittle pin was painted in bright colors for the fair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowling pin - a pin used in the game of bowling (tenpins)

Etymology

From 'skittle' (the game) and 'pin'; 'skittle' from Middle English and Old Norse 'skytla' (to shoot, throw).
