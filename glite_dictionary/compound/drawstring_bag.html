drawstring bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɔˌstrɪŋ bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːˌstrɪŋ bæɡ / (BrE)
A bag that is closed at the top by pulling a drawstring.
bag closed with a drawstring at the top

Examples

She carried her gym clothes in a drawstring bag for convenience.
The souvenir shop sells colorful drawstring bags made of cotton.
He tightened the drawstring bag to keep the contents from spilling out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backpack - a bag carried on the back, often with shoulder straps
