classic case of wisdumb

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæsɪk keɪs əv ˈwɪzdʌm / (AmE), / ˈklasɪk keɪs əv ˈwɪzdəm / (BrE)
A typical example of foolishness, especially when someone acts as if being wise but is actually being foolish.
typical example of foolishness
informal

Examples

Deciding to repair the electrical wiring yourself was a classic case of wisdumb that could have ended badly.
Her attempt to multitask while driving and texting was a classic case of wisdumb.
Ignoring all warnings and going hiking in a storm turned out to be a classic case of wisdumb.

Synset Examples

Ignoring advice is a classic case of wisdumb.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolishness - lack of good sense or judgement
  • Antonym: wisdom - the quality of being wise

Etymology

Blend of 'wisdom' and 'dumb' to create a humorous term.
