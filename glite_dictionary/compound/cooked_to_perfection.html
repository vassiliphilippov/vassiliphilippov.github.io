cooked to perfection

1. ADJ

/ kʊkt tə pərˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / kʊkt tə pəˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
(of food) having been cooked exactly right, to the highest standard possible.
cooked exactly right

Examples

The steak was cooked to perfection, tender and juicy with every bite.
Every dish was cooked to perfection by the award-winning chef.
This salmon is cooked to perfection, with a delicate, flaky texture.

Synset Examples

The Roastbeef was cooked to perfection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfectly cooked - cooked in a perfect manner; just right
