four hundred

1. NUM

/ fɔr ˈhʌndrəd / (AmE), / fɔː ˈhʌndrəd / (BrE)
The number 400; the cardinal number equal to one hundred more than three hundred.
number: 400

Examples

The museum welcomed over four hundred visitors on its opening day.
There are four hundred seats available for the concert.
My grandmother is proud of her four hundred knitted scarves.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: three hundred - the number 300
  • Degree Higher: five hundred - the number 500

Etymology

From 'four' and 'hundred', both of Old English origin.
