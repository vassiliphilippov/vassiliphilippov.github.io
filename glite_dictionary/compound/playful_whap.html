playful whap

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪfəl wæp / (AmE), / ˈpleɪfl̩ wæp / (BrE)
a light, quick hit or tap done in a playful manner
a light, playful hit or tap

Examples

The kitten gave my hand a playful whap before running away.
She laughed when her friend greeted her with a playful whap on the arm.
You could hear the playful whap of pillows during the sleepover.

Synset Examples

The cat gave the toy a playful whap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to whap - a sharp or sudden blow or slap
  • Synonym: tap - a light touch or strike
