blackboard eraser

1. NOUN

/ ˈblækˌbɔrd ɪˈreɪsər / (AmE), / ˈblækˌbɔːd ɪˈreɪsə / (BrE)
a soft implement used for removing chalk marks from a blackboard.
device for cleaning chalk from blackboard

Examples

The teacher picked up the blackboard eraser and wiped away the math problem.
After class, I found a blackboard eraser covered in chalk on the windowsill.
Please hand me the blackboard eraser so I can clean the board before the next lesson.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chalkboard eraser - an eraser for removing chalk from a chalkboard
  • Antonym: whiteboard eraser - an eraser for removing marker from a whiteboard
