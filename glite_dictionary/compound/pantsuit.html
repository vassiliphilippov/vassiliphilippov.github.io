pantsuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæntˌsuːt / (AmE), / ˈpæntsuːt / (BrE)
A set of women's clothes consisting of a pair of trousers and a matching jacket, worn together.
women's matched trouser suit

Examples

She looked professional and confident in her navy pantsuit at the interview.
The designer showcased a collection featuring a bold red pantsuit on the runway.
Many women choose to wear a pantsuit instead of a dress to formal events.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: skirt suit - a set of women's clothes consisting of a skirt and matching jacket
