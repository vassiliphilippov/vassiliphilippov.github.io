diesel oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈdiːzəl ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈdiːzəl ɔɪl / (BrE)
a heavy mineral oil used as fuel in diesel engines
fuel oil for diesel engines

Examples

The generator runs on diesel oil during power outages.
The ship was refueled with several tons of diesel oil.
They tested the engine's performance using low-sulfur diesel oil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diesel fuel - liquid fuel used in diesel engines

Etymology

From the name of Rudolf Diesel, inventor of the diesel engine, and 'oil'.
