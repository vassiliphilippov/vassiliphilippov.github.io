horse trade

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs treɪd / (AmE), / hɔːs treɪd / (BrE)
the exchange or swapping of horses, typically involving intense bargaining or negotiation
swapping horses with bargaining

Examples

Back in the old days, a horse trade would often last for hours as each side tried to get the better deal.
The horse trade ended with both men satisfied with their bargains.
He became known in the county for his skillful horse trade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bargain - to negotiate the terms of a sale or exchange
