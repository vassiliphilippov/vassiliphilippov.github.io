military force

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri fɔrs / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪˌtəri fɔːs / (BrE)
a group of soldiers or armed personnel organized as part of a country's military service
group of soldiers or military unit

Examples

The government deployed a military force to restore order in the region.
A small military force was stationed at the border to deter any threats.
During the parade, the military force marched proudly through the city streets.

Synset Examples

he sent Caesar a force of six thousand men

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armed force - organized group with weapons used for defense or attack
  • Antonym: civilian - a person not in the armed services
