grape-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪp saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪp sʌɪzd / (BrE)
Having approximately the same size as a grape.
about the size of a grape

Examples

The doctor found a grape-sized lump on her neck during the examination.
Tiny, grape-sized tomatoes are popular in fresh salads.
He collected grape-sized stones from the riverbed for his aquarium.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pea-sized - having the approximate size of a pea
