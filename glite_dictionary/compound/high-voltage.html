high-voltage

1. ADJ

/ haɪ ˈvoʊltɪdʒ / (AmE), / haɪ ˈvəʊltɪdʒ / (BrE)
Operating on or powered by a high voltage of electricity.
operating with high electrical voltage

Examples

The laboratory installed a high-voltage transformer for their experiments.
Workers must wear protective gear when repairing high-voltage power lines.
This area is restricted due to the presence of high-voltage equipment.

Synset Examples

a high-voltage generator

Related Terms

  • Antonym: low-voltage - operating at or powered by a low electrical voltage
