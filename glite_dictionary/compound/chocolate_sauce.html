chocolate sauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑklət sɔs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət sɔːs / (BrE)
A sweet sauce made from unsweetened chocolate or cocoa, sugar, and water, often used as a dessert topping.
sweet sauce made from chocolate or cocoa

Examples

She poured chocolate sauce over her ice cream for an extra treat.
The chef drizzled chocolate sauce on the plate to decorate the dessert.
We always keep a bottle of chocolate sauce in the fridge for sundaes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot fudge - rich, thick chocolate sauce served warm, typically over ice cream
