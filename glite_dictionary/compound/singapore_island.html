Singapore Island

1. PROPN

/ ˌsɪŋəˈpɔr ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋəˈpɔː ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
the main island of Singapore, located to the south of the Malay Peninsula
main island of Singapore

Examples

The city-state of Singapore is situated entirely on Singapore Island.
Many tourists visit Singapore Island to experience its unique mix of cultures.
Singapore Island is connected to Malaysia by a series of bridges.

Etymology

Name derived from Singapore, from Sanskrit 'Siṃhapura' (lion city), combined with 'island' from Old English 'īegland'.
