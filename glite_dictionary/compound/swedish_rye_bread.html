Swedish rye bread

1. NOUN

/ ˈswiːdɪʃ raɪ brɛd / (AmE), / ˈswiːdɪʃ raɪ brɛd / (BrE)
a moist, aromatic bread made with rye flour, molasses, and orange rind, typically raised with yeast and associated with Swedish cuisine
a sweet, spiced rye bread from Sweden

Examples

She served slices of Swedish rye bread with butter and jam for breakfast.
Baking Swedish rye bread fills the kitchen with the scent of spices and citrus.
The bakery's Swedish rye bread is known for its moist texture and rich flavor.

Etymology

Named after Sweden, where this style of rye bread originated.
