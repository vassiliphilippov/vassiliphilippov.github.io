sweet orange

1. NOUN

/ swit ˈɔrɪndʒ / (AmE), / swiːt ˈɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
a type of orange with sweet, juicy pulp, commonly eaten fresh or used for juice
a variety of orange with sweet edible pulp

Examples

The juice from a sweet orange is refreshing and perfect for breakfast.
She bought a sweet orange at the market for a healthy snack.
Unlike the bitter kind, a sweet orange is delicious to eat raw.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bitter orange - a variety of orange with a sour, bitter taste

2. NOUN

/ swit ˈɔrɪndʒ / (AmE), / swiːt ˈɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
A round, sweet, orange-colored fruit (Citrus × sinensis), widely cultivated for eating fresh and for juice.
a type of orange grown for eating and juice

Examples

The sweet orange is the most popular fruit for making orange juice worldwide.
She bought a basket of fresh sweet oranges from the market.
Farmers in Florida grow sweet orange for both eating and processing into juice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orange - a round, orange-colored citrus fruit
