moth plant

1. NOUN

/ mɑθ plænt / (AmE), / mɒθ plɑːnt / (BrE)
Any of several orchids of the genus Phalaenopsis, with broad, often drooping, glossy dark green leaves, sometimes with purple or mottled markings.
a type of orchid of the genus Phalaenopsis

Examples

The moth plant thrives in warm, humid environments and is popular among indoor gardeners.
She bought a beautiful white moth plant at the botanical garden's orchid show.
The broad leaves of the moth plant often have distinctive silvery patterns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Phalaenopsis - a genus of orchids commonly called moth orchids
