sausage pizza

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔːsɪdʒ ˈpiːtsə / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsɪdʒ ˈpiːtsə / (BrE)
A pizza topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and sausage pieces.
pizza with sausage topping

Examples

We ordered a large sausage pizza for the party.
Do you prefer sausage pizza or plain cheese?
The restaurant's special tonight is a thick-crust sausage pizza.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pepperoni pizza - pizza with pepperoni sausage slices as a topping
