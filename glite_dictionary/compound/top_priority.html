top priority

1. NOUN

/ tɑp praɪˈɔrəti / (AmE), / tɒp praɪˈɒrɪti / (BrE)
the task, goal, or matter that is considered most important and given the highest level of attention
most important concern or task

Examples

Improving customer service is our company's top priority this year.
When resources are limited, safety must remain the top priority.
Making sure the children are safe was her top priority during the trip.

Synset Examples

Pedestrian safety is a top priority for the city council.
Finding a necessary house was their top priority.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: highest priority - the task or matter considered highest in importance
  • Antonym: low priority - a task or matter considered less important or urgent
