common tarweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈtɑrˌwid / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈtɑːˌwiːd / (BrE)
A California annual plant with yellow flower rays marked by red-brown spots near the base.
a California annual plant with spotted yellow flowers

Examples

The fields were covered in common tarweed during the height of summer.
Botanists noted the distinctive spotted petals of the common tarweed during their survey.
The common tarweed is often one of the last wildflowers to bloom in California's dry season.
