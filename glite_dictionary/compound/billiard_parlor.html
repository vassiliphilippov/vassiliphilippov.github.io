billiard parlor

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪljərd ˈpɑrlər / (AmE), / ˈbɪljəd ˈpɑːlə / (BrE)
A room or establishment where people play billiards.
room or establishment for playing billiards

Examples

The old town still has a billiard parlor where locals gather every Friday night.
He spent hours practicing his shots at the billiard parlor downtown.
They renovated the billiard parlor to include a new snack bar and digital scoring tables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pool hall - Establishment with pool or billiards tables for public use
