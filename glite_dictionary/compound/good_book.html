Good Book

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd bʊk / (AmE), / ɡʊd bʊk / (BrE)
a respectful or informal term for the Bible, the sacred text of Christianity
the Bible; Christian sacred text

Examples

Every Sunday, she would read a passage from the Good Book to her children.
He has always tried to live by the teachings found in the Good Book.
Many people turn to the Good Book for comfort during difficult times.

Synset Examples

he went to carry the Word to the heathen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bible - the sacred scriptures of Christianity

Etymology

Refers to the Christian Bible, called 'good' for its authoritative spiritual guidance.
