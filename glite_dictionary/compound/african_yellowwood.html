African yellowwood

1. NOUN

/ ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈjɛl.oʊ.wʊd / (AmE), / ˈaf.rɪ.kən ˈjɛl.ə.wʊd / (BrE)
A tree or shrub native to South Africa, known for its rounded crown and valued for its durable wood.
South African tree or shrub species

Examples

The furniture was crafted from African yellowwood, prized for its strength and beauty.
In South Africa, the African yellowwood can be found in both gardens and forests.
The rounded crown of the African yellowwood provides ample shade in the park.
