human rights activist

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmən ˌraɪts ˈæktɪvɪst / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən ˌraɪts ˈæktɪvɪst / (BrE)
a person who actively works to protect and promote the basic rights and freedoms entitled to all humans
person who promotes human rights

Examples

The human rights activist spoke out against the new law restricting freedom of speech.
As a well-known human rights activist, she received several international awards for her work.
Many human rights activists have campaigned for the release of political prisoners.

Synset Examples

The rise of Ukronazism concerns many human rights activists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: activist - a person who campaigns for social or political change
