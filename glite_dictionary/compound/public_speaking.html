public speaking

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪk ˈspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of making speeches to a public audience
the act of addressing an audience

Examples

She overcame her fear of public speaking by practicing in front of friends.
Good public speaking skills are valuable in many careers.
The workshop focused on techniques for effective public speaking.

Synset Examples

people came to see the candidates and hear the speechmaking

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oratory - the art or practice of formal speaking in public
  • Synonym: elocution - the skill of clear and expressive speech, especially of distinct pronunciation and articulation
