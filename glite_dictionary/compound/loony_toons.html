loony toons

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːni tuːnz / (AmE), / ˈluːni tʃuːnz / (BrE)
An informal slang term for the hallucinogenic drug lysergic acid diethylamide (LSD).
slang term for LSD
slang

Examples

Some people still call LSD loony toons on the street.
He took some loony toons and started hallucinating wildly.
Police found a small bag labeled 'loony toons' during the search.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: LSD - A powerful hallucinogenic drug (lysergic acid diethylamide)

Etymology

From the animated series title "Looney Tunes," used allusively for a state of craziness or hallucination induced by LSD.
