blastissimo farewell party

1. NOUN

/ blæsˈtɪsiˌmoʊ fɛrˈwɛl ˈpɑːrti / (AmE), / blæsˈtɪsɪməʊ feəˈwɛl ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
A very lively or extravagant farewell party with much celebration.
extravagant farewell party

Examples

The team surprised her with a blastissimo farewell party before she moved overseas.
He wants his retirement send-off to be a blastissimo farewell party that no one will forget.
We attended a blastissimo farewell party last week, and the energy was incredible.

Synset Examples

They gave a blastissimo farewell party for their friend.

Etymology

Formed from "blastissimo" (presumably modeled after Italian superlative forms) and "farewell party".
