military headquarters

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri hɛdˌkwɔrtərz / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəˌri hɛdˌkwɔːtəz / (BrE)
the main location or building from which military commanders oversee and direct operations and administration
central command location for military operations

Examples

The commander called a meeting at the military headquarters to discuss the battle plan.
During the crisis, journalists gathered outside the military headquarters for official updates.
The city's old fortress now serves as the military headquarters for the regional defense force.

Synset Examples

the general's headquarters were a couple of large tents

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headquarters - a place from which operations are controlled
