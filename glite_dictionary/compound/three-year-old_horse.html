three-year-old horse

1. NOUN

/ ˌθriː jɪr ˈoʊld hɔrs / (AmE), / ˌθriː jɪə ˈəʊld hɔːs / (BrE)
a horse that is three years old, especially one raced in events restricted to that age group
a three-year-old racehorse

Examples

This year's Derby features a promising three-year-old horse with an impressive record.
The trainer specializes in preparing three-year-old horses for top-level races.
Winning as a three-year-old horse is often a sign of future success on the track.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colt - a young male horse, especially under four years old
  • Synonym: filly - a young female horse, especially under four years old
