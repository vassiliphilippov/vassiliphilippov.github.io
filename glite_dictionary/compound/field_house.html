field house

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːld ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A building or facility equipped for indoor sports, often used for athletic training, games, and related activities.
building for indoor sports and training

Examples

The university's new field house includes basketball courts and a running track.
Athletes gathered in the field house before heading out to the stadium.
Construction on the state-of-the-art field house was completed last fall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gymnasium - a room or building equipped for indoor sports and physical exercise
