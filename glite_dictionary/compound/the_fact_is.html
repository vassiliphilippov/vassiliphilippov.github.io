the fact is

1. X

/ ðə fækt ɪz / (AmE), / ðə fækt ɪz / (BrE)
used to introduce a statement that expresses the true situation or reality, often to emphasize a point or correct a misunderstanding
used to state the reality of a situation

Examples

The fact is, we cannot afford to buy a new car right now.
The fact is, nobody knows what will happen next.
The fact is, she already finished the assignment last week.

Synset Examples

The fact is, he has already made his decision.
The fact is, we need more time to complete this.
The fact is, she never liked the movie.
