yeast cake

1. NOUN

/ jist keɪk / (AmE), / jiːst keɪk / (BrE)
a small, solid mass of compressed moist yeast used in baking to make bread and other doughs rise
compressed mass of moist yeast for baking

Examples

The recipe calls for one yeast cake to be dissolved in warm water.
She bought a fresh yeast cake from the bakery to make her bread rise.
Store the yeast cake in the refrigerator to keep it active longer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yeast - a microscopic fungus used to ferment and leaven dough
