deep-set brow

1. NOUN

/ ˌdiːpˈsɛt ˈbraʊ / (AmE), / ˌdiːpˈsɛt ˈbraʊ / (BrE)
a brow or eyebrows that are set back and appear prominent or recessed, often giving a distinct or intense expression
eyebrows set back in the face

Examples

The actor's deep-set brow gave his face a brooding expression.
She inherited her father's deep-set brow and sharp features.
The sculpture was characterized by a strong jawline and a deep-set brow.

Synset Examples

The actor's deep-set brows added to his intense persona.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prominent brow - a brow that stands out or is easily noticeable
