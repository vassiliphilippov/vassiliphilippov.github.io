quick read

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk ɹid / (AmE), / kwɪk ɹiːd / (BrE)
a book or text that can be read easily and quickly, typically because it is short or straightforward
an easy and fast-to-read book or article

Examples

That novel was a quick read and kept me entertained during my entire flight.
If you're looking for a quick read, this magazine article covers the basics.
She prefers a quick read when relaxing on a Sunday afternoon.

Synset Examples

This book is a quick read, perfect for the weekend.
The article was a quick read, only taking ten minutes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: page-turner - a book that is very engaging, making you want to keep reading

2. NOUN

/ kwɪk rid / (AmE), / kwɪk riːd / (BrE)
a text that is brief or easy to read, often finished in a short period
brief or easy-to-read text

Examples

That novel is perfect if you're looking for a quick read on a rainy afternoon.
I picked up a magazine for a quick read while waiting at the doctor's office.
Short stories are great when you want a quick read but don't have much time.

Synset Examples

I enjoy a quick read before going to bed.

Related Terms
