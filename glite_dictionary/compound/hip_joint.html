hip joint

1. NOUN

/ hɪp dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / hɪp dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
the ball-and-socket joint where the head of the femur fits into the acetabulum of the pelvis
joint connecting thigh and pelvis
medical

Examples

After his injury, the pain in his hip joint made it difficult to walk.
Doctors recommended surgery to replace her damaged hip joint.
The hip joint allows for a wide range of movement in the leg.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoulder joint - ball-and-socket joint connecting the upper arm and shoulder blade
