khaki short

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæki ʃɔrt / (AmE), / ˈkɑːki ʃɔːt / (BrE)
A pair of shorts made from khaki-colored fabric, typically light brown and often used for casual or outdoor wear.
shorts made from khaki fabric

Examples

I packed my favorite khaki shorts for the beach vacation.
He wore khaki shorts and a polo shirt to the picnic.
The uniform required plain khaki shorts and a white T-shirt.

Synset Examples

He wore khaki shorts to the summer barbecue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: khaki trousers - trousers made from khaki fabric
  • Synonym: cargo shorts - shorts with multiple pockets, often made from khaki fabric

Etymology

From Hindi 'khākī' meaning 'dust-colored' and English 'shorts.'
