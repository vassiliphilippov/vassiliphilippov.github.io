raised fist

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪzd fɪst / (AmE), / ˈreɪzd fɪst / (BrE)
a hand gesture in which the fist is held up, symbolizing defiance or solidarity, especially in protests or demonstrations
hand gesture meaning defiance or solidarity

Examples

She stood on the stage, her raised fist a sign of unity with the crowd.
The photograph captured a sea of raised fists during the protest.
He finished the race and saluted the cheering fans with a triumphant raised fist.

Synset Examples

He celebrated his victory with a raised fist in the air.
The protestors marched with their raised fists in solidarity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fist bump - a gesture in which two people lightly bump their fists together
  • Antonym: handshake - a greeting or parting gesture in which two people grasp and shake each other's hands
