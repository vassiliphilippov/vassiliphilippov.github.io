half page

1. NOUN

/ hæf peɪdʒ / (AmE), / hɑːf peɪdʒ / (BrE)
Either the top or bottom half of a page, typically used in reference to space for text or advertisements.
top or bottom half of a page

Examples

The magazine placed an advertisement on the half page at the bottom.
Her essay continued onto the half page left at the end of the chapter.
He carefully illustrated only the half page to make space for his notes.
