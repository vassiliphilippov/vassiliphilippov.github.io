domestic ass

1. NOUN

/ dəˈmɛstɪk æs / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk as / (BrE)
A domesticated mammal descended from the African wild ass, used as a beast of burden; also called a donkey.
a domesticated donkey; beast of burden

Examples

The domestic ass has served humanity as a beast of burden for thousands of years.
Farmers often rely on the domestic ass for carrying loads over rough terrain.
Unlike horses, the domestic ass is known for its patience and endurance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: donkey - a domesticated hoofed mammal used as a beast of burden
  • Degree Higher: mule - an animal that is the offspring of a male donkey and a female horse

Etymology

From Latin 'asinus' (ass), with 'domestic' meaning tamed for home or farm use.
