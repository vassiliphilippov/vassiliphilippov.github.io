on to something

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑn tə ˈsʌmθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɒn tə ˈsʌmθɪŋ / (BrE)
having discovered or being close to discovering something important, or having realized the truth about a situation
aware of or close to discovering something significant

Examples

I think I'm on to something with this new theory.
The detective realized he was on to something when he found the missing clue.
Keep going—you’re definitely on to something with this design.

Synset Examples

I think I'm on to something big here.
If you're on to the truth, don’t back down.

Related Terms
