permastubble style

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːməˌstʌbəl staɪl / (AmE), / ˈpɜːməˌstʌbəl staɪl / (BrE)
A consistently maintained stubbly beard look, kept at a short, even length as a deliberate style choice.
deliberately maintained stubbly beard look

Examples

He spent ten minutes each morning trimming his permastubble style to perfection.
The actor is famous for his trademark permastubble style on the red carpet.
Many men find the permastubble style both easy to maintain and fashionable.

Synset Examples

Her boyfriend always maintained his permastubble style.

Etymology

From 'perma-' (a combining form meaning permanent) and 'stubble' (short beard growth), styled after similar fashion terms.
