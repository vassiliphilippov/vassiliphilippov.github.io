false goatsbeard

1. NOUN

/ fɔls ˈɡoʊtsˌbɪrd / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈɡəʊtsˌbɜːd / (BrE)
A North American plant (Astilbe biternata) with panicles of creamy white flowers, resembling true goatsbeard.
North American plant with white panicles

Examples

We planted false goatsbeard along the shaded side of the garden for its delicate flowers.
The woodland trail was lined with clusters of blooming false goatsbeard.
Gardeners appreciate false goatsbeard for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded locations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goatsbeard - a plant with feathery flower heads, of the genus Aruncus or Tragopogon
