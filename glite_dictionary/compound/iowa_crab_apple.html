Iowa crab apple

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.oʊ.ə kræb ˈæp.əl / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.əʊ.ə kræb ˈæp.əl / (BrE)
a species of wild crab apple tree native to the western United States, known for its fragrant pink flowers
wild crab apple tree of the western US

Examples

The Iowa crab apple thrives in the woodlands of the western United States.
In spring, the Iowa crab apple produces beautiful, fragrant pink blossoms.
Gardeners value the Iowa crab apple for its hardiness and ornamental flowers.
