dogwood family

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔɡˌwʊd ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡˌwʊd ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants (Cornaceae), including trees and shrubs such as dogwoods, belonging to the order Cornales.
family of flowering plants (Cornaceae)

Examples

Many species in the dogwood family are cultivated for their attractive flowers and foliage.
The dogwood family includes both deciduous and evergreen trees and shrubs.
Botanists classify Cornus florida, the flowering dogwood, in the dogwood family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cornaceae - scientific name for the dogwood family
  • Synonym: dogwood - tree or shrub of the dogwood family
