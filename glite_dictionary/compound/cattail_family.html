cattail family

1. NOUN

/ ˈkætˌteɪl ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈkætˌteɪl ˈfamɪli / (BrE)
A family of perennial marsh plants, typically with creeping rootstocks and long, linear leaves, including species such as cattails.
family of marsh plants including cattails

Examples

Many birds nest in wetlands dominated by plants of the cattail family.
The cattail family includes species known for their cigar-shaped brown flower spikes.
Marsh restoration projects often feature members of the cattail family to improve habitat quality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Typha - Genus of marsh plants commonly known as cattails
