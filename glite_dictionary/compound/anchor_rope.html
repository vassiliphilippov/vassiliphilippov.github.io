anchor rope

1. NOUN

/ ˈæŋkɚ roʊp / (AmE), / ˈæŋkə rəʊp / (BrE)
a chain or rope used to attach an anchor to a vessel
chain or rope attaching anchor to vessel

Examples

The anchor rope was coiled neatly on the deck after we dropped anchor.
Years of use had frayed the anchor rope, so it needed to be replaced.
She watched carefully as the crew pulled the anchor rope to bring the anchor aboard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anchor chain - a chain used to attach an anchor to a vessel
