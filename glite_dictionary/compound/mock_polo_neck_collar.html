mock polo neck collar

1. NOUN

/ mɑk ˈpoʊloʊ ˈnɛk ˈkɑlər / (AmE), / mɒk ˈpəʊləʊ ˈnɛk ˈkɒlə / (BrE)
A collar that looks like a polo neck, with a soft fold and standing up around the neck, but both ends are sewn to the neckline.
collar resembling a polo neck but sewn at both ends

Examples

The mock polo neck collar is often used in winter clothing for added warmth without the bulk.
She preferred a mock polo neck collar to a full turtleneck for a less restrictive feel.
A mock polo neck collar gives the top a stylish, streamlined look.

Synset Examples

The mock polo neck clings to the neck smoothly, is easy to manufacture, and works well with a zip closure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polo neck - a high, close-fitting, turned-over collar
