to forget someone's birthday

1. VERB

/ fərˈɡɛt ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈbɝθ.deɪ / (AmE), / fəˈɡɛt ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈbɜːθ.deɪ / (BrE)
to fail to remember the date of someone’s birthday
fail to remember a birthday

Examples

I completely forgot someone's birthday and had to apologize the next day.
He was upset when his friends forgot his birthday this year.
You should mark the date on your calendar so you don't forget someone's birthday.

Synset Examples

I felt 🤖 when I realized I forgot her birthday.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: remember someone's birthday - recall the date of someone's birthday
