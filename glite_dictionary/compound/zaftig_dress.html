zaftig dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈzæf.tɪɡ drɛs / (AmE), / ˈzæf.tɪɡ drɛs / (BrE)
a form-fitting dress designed to emphasize and flatter a curvaceous figure
form-fitting dress accentuating curves

Examples

The bright red zaftig dress drew admiring glances at the gala.
She chose a zaftig dress for her evening out, feeling confident and stylish.
On the runway, models showcased the designer's latest zaftig dress collection.

Synset Examples

She wore a zaftig dress that flattered her curves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bodycon dress - a close-fitting dress that follows the contours of the body

Etymology

Zaftig derives from Yiddish *zaftik* (juicy, plump); dress from Old English *drēse* (garment).
