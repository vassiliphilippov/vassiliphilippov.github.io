bomb blast

1. NOUN

/ bɑm blæst / (AmE), / bɒm blɑːst / (BrE)
an explosion caused by a bomb
explosion caused by a bomb

Examples

The building was severely damaged in the bomb blast last night.
Many people were injured by the bomb blast at the market.
After the bomb blast, emergency services arrived quickly at the scene.
