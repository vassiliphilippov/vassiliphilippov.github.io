outrageous comment

1. NOUN

/ aʊtˈreɪdʒəs ˈkɑːmɛnt / (AmE), / aʊtˈreɪdʒəs ˈkɒmɛnt / (BrE)
a statement that is shocking, extreme, or likely to cause strong reactions due to its boldness or impropriety
shocking or extreme statement

Examples

She was fired after making an outrageous comment during the meeting.
His outrageous comments on social media sparked public outrage.
People often react strongly to any outrageous comment made by a public figure.

Synset Examples

That motherferyer is known for his outrageous comments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remark - something that is said or written; a comment
  • Synonym: comment - an expression of opinion or reaction
  • Degree Lower: mild comment - a statement that is gentle or moderate in tone
