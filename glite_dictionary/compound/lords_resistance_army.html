Lord's Resistance Army

1. PROPN

/ lɔrdz rɪˈzɪstəns ˈɑːrmi / (AmE), / lɔːdz rɪˈzɪstəns ˈɑːmi / (BrE)
a Ugandan rebel group founded in the late 1980s, known for violent acts including terrorizing civilians, abducting children, and committing human rights abuses
Ugandan rebel group noted for violence and abductions

Examples

The Lord's Resistance Army has been responsible for countless atrocities in northern Uganda.
Many families live in fear of attacks by the Lord's Resistance Army in remote villages.
Efforts to bring the leaders of the Lord's Resistance Army to justice have continued for years.
