rodent dropping

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊdənt ˈdrɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdənt ˈdrɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
the excrement or feces produced by rodents such as mice or rats
excrement of rodents

Examples

The inspector found several rodent droppings behind the refrigerator.
If you see rodent droppings in your kitchen, it may indicate a mouse problem.
Cleaning up rodent droppings requires wearing gloves and a mask for safety.

Synset Examples

The attic was filled with rodent droppings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dropping - excrement, especially of animals or birds
