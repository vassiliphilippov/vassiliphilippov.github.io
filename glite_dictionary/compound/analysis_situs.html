analysis situs

1. NOUN

/ əˈnæləsɪs ˈsaɪtəs / (AmE), / əˈnæləsɪs ˈsɪtʊs / (BrE)
the branch of mathematics concerned with those properties of objects that are preserved under continuous transformations; now more commonly known as topology
branch of mathematics; topology

Examples

The development of analysis situs in the 19th century greatly expanded our understanding of geometric structures.
Many early papers on topology use the term analysis situs to describe the study of continuity and transformation.
Although analysis situs is an older term, its influence in mathematics continues through the modern field of topology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: topology - the branch of mathematics dealing with the properties of space that are preserved under continuous transformations

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'analysis of position'; historical term for topology.
