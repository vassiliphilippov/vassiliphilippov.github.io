retirement savings plan

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈtaɪərmənt ˈseɪvɪŋz plæn / (AmE), / rɪˈtaɪəmənt ˈseɪvɪŋz plan / (BrE)
a financial arrangement in which money is regularly set aside for use after retirement
plan for saving money for retirement

Examples

She enrolled in a retirement savings plan to ensure financial security after she stops working.
Many employers offer a retirement savings plan as part of their benefits package.
It's important to start contributing to a retirement savings plan early in your career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pension plan - a plan for providing income after retirement
