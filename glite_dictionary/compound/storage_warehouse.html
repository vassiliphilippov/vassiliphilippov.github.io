storage warehouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔrɪdʒ ˈwɛrˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈstɔːrɪdʒ ˈwɛəhaʊs / (BrE)
a large building where goods or merchandise are kept until they are needed or distributed
building for storing goods or merchandise

Examples

The company rents a storage warehouse near the port to hold their imported products.
Old furniture was packed away in the storage warehouse for safekeeping.
He manages a storage warehouse specializing in electronics and home appliances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warehouse - a large building for storing goods
