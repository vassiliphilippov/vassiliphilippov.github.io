snuffbox fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnʌfˌbɑks fɝn / (AmE), / ˈsnʌfˌbɒks fɜːn / (BrE)
a fern native to northeastern North America, known for its delicate, lacy fronds
a North American fern species

Examples

The woods were carpeted with snuffbox fern in the springtime.
Botanists admire the fine texture of the snuffbox fern's leaves.
We found several clusters of snuffbox ferns along the shaded stream.
