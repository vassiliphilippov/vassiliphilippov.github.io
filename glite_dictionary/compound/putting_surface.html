putting surface

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌtɪŋ ˌsɝfɪs / (AmE), / ˈpʌtɪŋ ˌsɜːfɪs / (BrE)
the area of closely mown grass on a golf course surrounding the hole, where putting takes place
area of grass around the hole on a golf course

Examples

The golfer carefully studied the putting surface before taking his shot.
Heavy rain made the putting surface slower than usual.
Maintaining a smooth putting surface is essential for good play on the course.

Synset Examples

the ball rolled across the green and into the bunker

Related Terms
