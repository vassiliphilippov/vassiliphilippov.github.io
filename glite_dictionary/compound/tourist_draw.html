tourist draw

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʊrɪst drɔ / (AmE), / ˈtʊərɪst drɔː / (BrE)
a place, event, or attraction that brings in many visitors or tourists
attraction for tourists

Examples

The Eiffel Tower is a famous tourist draw in Paris.
After the renovation, the museum became a major tourist draw for the city.
The new art festival has turned into a popular tourist draw during the summer months.

Synset Examples

Theme parking has become a major tourist draw.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tourist attraction - a place that tourists visit for pleasure and interest
