knuckle sandwich

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌkəl ˈsænwɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈnʌkəl ˈsænwɪtʃ / (BrE)
a punch in the mouth, especially said as a threat or in jest
punch in the face (usually joking)
informal

Examples

If you keep interrupting, you might get a knuckle sandwich.
He jokingly offered his friend a knuckle sandwich after losing the game.
She threatened a knuckle sandwich to anyone who spoiled the movie.

Synset Examples

If you keep talking back, you'll get a knuckle sandwich!
He threatened to give him a knuckle sandwich for the insult.
He jokingly offered me a knuckle sandwich after I teased him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punch - a blow with the fist
