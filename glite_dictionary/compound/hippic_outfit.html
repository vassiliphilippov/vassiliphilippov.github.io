hippic outfit

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪpɪk ˈaʊtfɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɪpɪk ˈaʊtfɪt / (BrE)
Clothing and attire specifically designed for horse riding or equestrian events.
equestrian riding attire

Examples

She bought a new hippic outfit for the annual horse show.
Wearing a hippic outfit, he impressed the judges with his style and skill.
The shop offers a range of hippic outfits for both beginners and professional riders.

Synset Examples

She wore a hippic outfit to the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equestrian outfit - attire for horse riding

Etymology

From 'hippic' (relating to horses or horse riding, from Greek 'hippos' meaning 'horse') and 'outfit'.
