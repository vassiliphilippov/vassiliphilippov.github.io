folding chair

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊldɪŋ tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈfəʊldɪŋ tʃeə / (BrE)
A chair designed to be folded flat so that it can be stored or carried easily.
chair that folds flat for storage

Examples

We keep a folding chair in the trunk for picnics and outdoor events.
He unfolded the folding chair and sat down by the campfire.
After the meeting, everyone helped stack the folding chairs against the wall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deck chair - a folding chair, typically with a fabric or plastic seat, used outdoors
