slow-burning passion

1. NOUN

/ sloʊ ˈbɜrnɪŋ ˈpæʃən / (AmE), / sləʊ ˈbɜːnɪŋ ˈpæʃən / (BrE)
a gradually intensifying strong feeling of enthusiasm or love for something or someone
gradually intensifying enthusiasm

Examples

Her slow-burning passion for classical music inspired her to attend every local concert.
The novel explores the slow-burning passion between the two main characters over many years.
A slow-burning passion for teaching eventually drew him back to the classroom.

Synset Examples

His slow-burning passion for art finally led him to paint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passion - a strong and barely controllable emotion
