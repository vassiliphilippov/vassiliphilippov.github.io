negro vine

1. NOUN

/ ˈniːɡroʊ vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈniːɡrəʊ vaɪn / (BrE)
a twining plant with hairy leaves and dark purplish-brown flowers, known scientifically as a species of vine
a twining vine with dark brownish flowers

Examples

The negro vine can quickly cover trellises in a shady garden spot.
Botanists admire the unique foliage and flowers of the negro vine.
She grew a negro vine along the fence to add some color to her backyard.
