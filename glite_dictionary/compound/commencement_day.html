commencement day

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɛnsmənt deɪ / (AmE), / kəˈmɛnsmənt deɪ / (BrE)
the day on which a university or college awards degrees to its graduating students in a formal ceremony
day university degrees are awarded

Examples

We took dozens of photos on commencement day to remember the occasion.
Her parents traveled across the country to attend her commencement day ceremony.
The university president delivered a memorable speech at commencement day this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graduation day - the day on which students graduate from an educational institution
