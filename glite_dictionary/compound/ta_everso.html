ta everso

1. X

/ tɑː ˈɛvərsoʊ / (AmE), / tɑː ˈɛvəsəʊ / (BrE)
(informal, British) Thank you very much; an expression of sincere gratitude.
informal British thanking phrase
informal british

Examples

Ta everso for inviting me to your party—I'm really looking forward to it!
She smiled and said, 'ta everso,' when the waiter brought her tea.
If you could send that document over today, ta everso!

Synset Examples

I appreciate your help, ta everso!
Ta everso for the lovely gift!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ta - informal British word for 'thank you'

Etymology

'Ta' is a British informal term for 'thank you', possibly from Scandinavian or baby-talk; 'everso' means 'very' or 'so much'.
