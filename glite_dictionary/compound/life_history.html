life history

1. NOUN

/ laɪf ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / laɪf ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
an account of the series of events and experiences that make up a person's or organism's life
account of a person's or organism's life events

Examples

The researcher compiled the life history of each participant to understand their background.
Reading her life history gave me insight into the challenges she faced growing up.
Every life history reveals a unique set of experiences and personal growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biography - an account of someone's life written by someone else
  • Degree Higher: autobiography - an account of a person's life written by that person
