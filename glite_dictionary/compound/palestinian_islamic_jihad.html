Palestinian Islamic Jihad

1. NOUN

/ pæləsˈtɪniən ɪzˈlɑmɪk dʒɪˈhɑd / (AmE), / pæləsˈtɪniːən ɪzˈlɑːmɪk dʒɪˈhɑːd / (BrE)
a militant Palestinian organization founded in 1979, committed to creating an Islamic state in Palestine and the destruction of Israel
militant Palestinian organization (founded 1979)

Examples

The government accused Palestinian Islamic Jihad of orchestrating the recent attack along the border.
Palestinian Islamic Jihad is often mentioned in reports about the Israeli-Palestinian conflict.
Talks between Palestinian Islamic Jihad and other factions have failed to reach a ceasefire agreement.
