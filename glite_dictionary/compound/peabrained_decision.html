peabrained decision

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiˌbreɪnd dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈpiːˌbreɪnd dɪˈsɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
an extremely foolish or unintelligent choice or judgment
a foolish or stupid choice
informal

Examples

Calling off practice the night before the match was a peabrained decision by the coach.
His peabrained decision to skip studying left him unprepared for the test.
Investing all the money in a scam was a truly peabrained decision.

Synset Examples

He made a peabrained decision that cost us the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolish decision - an unwise or silly choice
  • Antonym: wise decision - a sensible or prudent choice

Etymology

From 'pea-brained' (having a small brain, hence foolish) + 'decision'.
