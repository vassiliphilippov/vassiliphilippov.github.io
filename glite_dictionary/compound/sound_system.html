sound system

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaʊnd ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈsaʊnd ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a set of electronic equipment used for recording, amplifying, or reproducing sound
audio recording or playback equipment

Examples

The new theater installed an advanced sound system that makes every seat feel close to the stage.
She connected her phone to the sound system to play music at the party.
The sound system in the car provides clear and powerful audio for the driver.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stereo - a system for playing audio in two channels for a spatial effect
  • Synonym: public address system - an electronic setup for amplifying speech for an audience
