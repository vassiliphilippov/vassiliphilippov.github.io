horned chameleon

1. NOUN

/ hɔrnd kəˈmiːliən / (AmE), / hɔːnd kəˈmiːliən / (BrE)
a species of chameleon distinguished by prominent horn-like structures on its head, native to parts of Africa
African chameleon with horn-like head structures

Examples

The horned chameleon can change its color to match its surroundings for camouflage.
Many reptile enthusiasts keep the horned chameleon as an exotic pet.
Researchers observed a horned chameleon using its horns in a territorial display.
