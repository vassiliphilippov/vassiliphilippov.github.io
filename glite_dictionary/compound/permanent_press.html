permanent press

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɚmənənt prɛs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːmənənt prɛs / (BrE)
A type of fabric that has been chemically treated to resist wrinkles and maintain its shape after washing.
wrinkle-resistant treated fabric

Examples

This shirt is made from permanent press fabric, so I never have to iron it.
Remember to use the low heat setting when drying permanent press clothes.
Many business clothes are labeled as permanent press for easy care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrinkle-resistant - fabric designed to resist creasing and wrinkling
  • Synonym: wash and wear - fabric requiring minimal or no ironing after washing
