dinner service

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnər ˌsɜːrvɪs / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə ˌsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a set of matching crockery, cutlery, and sometimes glassware used for serving dinner
set of tableware for dinner

Examples

We only use the special dinner service when we have guests over for a formal meal.
Her grandmother gave her a beautiful porcelain dinner service as a wedding present.
The dinner service was arranged neatly on the table in preparation for the holiday feast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dinner set - a set of dishes and serving pieces for dinner
