earthly concern

1. NOUN

/ ˈɜrθli kənˈsɜrn / (AmE), / ˈɜːθli kənˈsɜːn / (BrE)
a matter or worry relating to worldly life, as opposed to spiritual or afterlife matters
concern related to worldly life

Examples

She tried to set aside every earthly concern and focus on her meditation.
The philosopher dismissed earthly concerns in favor of contemplating eternal truths.
Even in her final days, he cared little for earthly concern, seeking only spiritual peace.

Synset Examples

they consider the church to be independent of the world

Related Terms

  • Synonym: material concern - a matter relating to material or physical things
  • Antonym: spiritual concern - a matter related to the spirit or religious matters
