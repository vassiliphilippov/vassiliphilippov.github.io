Mexico City

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɛk.sɪˈkoʊ ˈsɪ.ti / (AmE), / ˌmɛk.sɪˈkəʊ ˈsɪ.ti / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Mexico, serving as the country's main political, cultural, commercial, and industrial center.
capital of Mexico; major city

Examples

Many tourists visit Mexico City each year to experience its rich history and vibrant culture.
The air pollution in Mexico City has been a major concern for local authorities.
She moved to Mexico City after receiving a job offer from a leading international company.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Ciudad de México', meaning 'City of Mexico'.
