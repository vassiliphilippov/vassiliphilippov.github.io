female chest

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːˌmeɪl tʃɛst / (AmE), / ˈfiːˌmeɪl tʃɛst / (BrE)
the chest of a woman, especially as distinct from a man's chest in anatomical or biological terms
chest of a woman

Examples

The doctor explained the medical differences between the female chest and the male chest.
Some clothing brands design bras specifically to support the female chest during exercise.
The anatomy textbook included detailed diagrams of the female chest for students to study.
