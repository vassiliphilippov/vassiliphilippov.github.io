autumn sneezeweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔtəm ˈsniːzˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəm ˈsniːzˌwiːd / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant with bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.
North American plant with yellow late summer flowers

Examples

The field was dotted with autumn sneezeweed in full bloom late August.
Gardeners appreciate autumn sneezeweed for its vivid yellow flowers and long blooming season.
Many pollinators are attracted to autumn sneezeweed when other flowers have faded.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sneezeweed - A common name for several plants in the genus Helenium, known for causing sneezing
