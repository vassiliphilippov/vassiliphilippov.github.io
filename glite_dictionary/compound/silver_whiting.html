silver whiting

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ ˈwaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˈwaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a dull silvery fish of the whiting family found along the southern Atlantic and Gulf Coasts of the United States
a South Atlantic/Gulf Coast whiting fish

Examples

The silver whiting is commonly sought after by anglers along the Gulf Coast.
Local markets often sell freshly caught silver whiting during the summer months.
Biologists conducted a survey of silver whiting populations in the southeastern United States.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whiting - a type of fish in the cod family
