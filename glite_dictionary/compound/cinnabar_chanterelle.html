cinnabar chanterelle

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪn.əˌbɑr ˈʃæn.təˌrɛl / (AmE), / ˌsɪn.əˌbɑː ˈʃæn.təˌrɛl / (BrE)
a species of mushroom distinguished by its pink to vermillion colored fruiting body
brightly colored mushroom species

Examples

The cinnabar chanterelle is easily identified by its striking reddish-orange color in the forest undergrowth.
We found several cinnabar chanterelles during our mushroom hunt last weekend.
Unlike other mushrooms, the cinnabar chanterelle has a peppery flavor and vibrant hues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chanterelle - a type of edible mushroom with a trumpet-shaped cap, often yellow or orange

Etymology

From Latin 'cinnabaris' (red mineral pigment) and French 'chanterelle' (a type of mushroom).
