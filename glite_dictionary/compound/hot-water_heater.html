hot-water heater

1. NOUN

/ hɑt ˈwɔtɚ ˌhitɚ / (AmE), / hɒt ˈwɔːtə ˌhiːtə / (BrE)
A device that heats and stores water for use in a building.
device for heating and storing water

Examples

We need to replace our old hot-water heater before winter arrives.
The plumber installed a new hot-water heater in the basement last week.
A malfunctioning hot-water heater can cause both inconvenience and damage to your home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water heater - device that heats water
  • Synonym: boiler - device for heating water or generating steam
