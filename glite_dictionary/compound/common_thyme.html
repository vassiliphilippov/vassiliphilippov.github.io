common thyme

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən θaɪm / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən taɪm / (BrE)
a small aromatic Mediterranean plant (Thymus vulgaris) with gray-green leaves, used as a culinary herb and formerly in medicine
aromatic herb used in cooking

Examples

She adds fresh common thyme to her roasted chicken for extra flavor.
Growing common thyme in your garden is easy and rewarding.
A sprig of common thyme can turn a simple soup into a gourmet dish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thyme - an aromatic herb of the genus Thymus, used for flavoring food
