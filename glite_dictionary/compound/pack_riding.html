pack riding

1. NOUN

/ pæk ˈraɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / pæk ˈraɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of riding in a group, especially as part of a pack in activities such as cycling or horseback riding
riding in a group (pack), especially cycling or horses

Examples

The cyclists practiced pack riding to improve their teamwork during the race.
Safety rules for pack riding are essential for group cyclists and horse riders.
She found pack riding much more challenging than riding solo due to the close quarters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: group riding - riding together as a group
