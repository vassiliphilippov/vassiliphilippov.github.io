health profession

1. NOUN

/ hɛlθ prəˈfɛʃən / (AmE), / hɛlθ prəˈfɛʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
all the occupations whose practitioners help maintain or restore people's health, especially through clinical care or preventive measures
occupation in healthcare field

Examples

Nursing is considered a vital health profession in many countries.
Many students aspire to join a health profession such as medicine or dentistry.
Regulations for each health profession ensure high standards of patient care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: healthcare - the organized provision of medical care to individuals or a community
  • Synonym: medical profession - the profession of those who practice medicine, especially doctors
