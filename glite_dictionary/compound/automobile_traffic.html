automobile traffic

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑtəmoʊˌbil ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəməˌbiːl ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
the flow or movement of cars along roads or streets
cars moving on roads

Examples

Heavy automobile traffic filled the downtown streets during rush hour.
Due to construction, automobile traffic was diverted onto side roads.
Noise from automobile traffic can be heard throughout the city at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traffic - vehicles moving along a road
