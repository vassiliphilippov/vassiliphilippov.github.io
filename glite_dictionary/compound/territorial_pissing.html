territorial pissing

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛrɪˈtɔriəl ˈpɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌtɛrɪˈtɔːriəl ˈpɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
an act of aggressively asserting dominance or claiming boundaries, often in a competitive or confrontational manner
aggressive assertion of dominance
informal vulgar

There was a lot of territorial pissing among the new managers trying to prove themselves.
Sometimes office meetings feel like nothing more than territorial pissing.
The two artists engaged in public territorial pissing to establish their reputations.
