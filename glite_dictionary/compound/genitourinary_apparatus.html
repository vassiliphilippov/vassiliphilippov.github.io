genitourinary apparatus

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒɛn.ɪ.toʊˈjʊr.ɪˌnɛr.i əˈpær.ə.təs / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛn.ɪ.tjʊəˈrɪn.ər.i əˈpar.ə.təs / (BrE)
the system of organs in the body involved with reproduction and with the production and excretion of urine
organs for reproduction and urination
medical

Examples

The genitourinary apparatus includes both the reproductive organs and those involved in the formation of urine.
Certain diseases can affect the genitourinary apparatus, leading to complications in both reproduction and urination.
Doctors performed tests to assess the health of her genitourinary apparatus after her surgery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urinary system - organs involved in the production and excretion of urine
  • Synonym: reproductive system - organs involved in reproduction

Etymology

From Latin genus (birth, race) + urina (urine) + apparatus (equipment, apparatus).
