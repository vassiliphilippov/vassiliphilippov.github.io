shoofly pie

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃuˌflaɪ paɪ / (AmE), / ˈʃuːˌflʌɪ paɪ / (BrE)
An open pie filled with a mixture of molasses and sweet crumb topping, traditionally made in Pennsylvania Dutch cuisine.
molasses-based dessert pie
american

Examples

My grandmother baked a delicious shoofly pie for the family gathering.
The bakery is famous for its traditional shoofly pie recipe.
Have you ever tried shoofly pie with a scoop of vanilla ice cream?

Etymology

From 'shoo' + 'fly', supposedly named because the sweet pie attracted flies that had to be shooed away.
