health facility

1. NOUN

/ hɛlθ fəˈsɪl.ɪ.ti / (AmE), / hɛlθ fəˈsɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
A building or institution where medical care and services are provided to people.
place for medical care

Examples

The government opened a new health facility to serve the growing community.
Many rural areas lack access to a proper health facility.
The health facility was equipped with modern technology and specialized staff.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hospital - an institution providing medical and surgical treatment
  • Synonym: clinic - a place where people receive medical treatment or advice
