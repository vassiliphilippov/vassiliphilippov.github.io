canarybird vine

1. NOUN

/ kəˈnɛr.i.bɝd vaɪn / (AmE), / kəˈneə.ri.bɜːd vaɪn / (BrE)
A climbing plant with bright yellow flowers, resembling the color of canaries, often grown for ornamental purposes.
climbing plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The canarybird vine covered the trellis with a bright cascade of yellow flowers.
Gardeners often choose the canarybird vine to add color and height to their fences.
The vibrant blooms of the canarybird vine attract bees and butterflies throughout the summer.
