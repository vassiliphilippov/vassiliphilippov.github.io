light dessert

1. NOUN

/ laɪt dɪˈzɜrt / (AmE), / laɪt dɪˈzɜːt / (BrE)
a dessert that is not heavy or rich, often easy to digest or with fewer calories
a dessert that is not heavy or rich

Examples

After a heavy meal, I usually prefer a light dessert like fruit salad or sorbet.
The restaurant offers several light dessert options for guests watching their calorie intake.
A bowl of berries with yogurt is the perfect light dessert for summer evenings.

Synset Examples

After that stodge, I need a light dessert.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rich dessert - a dessert that is heavy or contains a lot of fat, sugar, or cream
