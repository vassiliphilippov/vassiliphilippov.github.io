federal official

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛdərəl əˈfɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˈfɛdərəl əˈfɪʃəl / (BrE)
a law-enforcement officer employed by the federal government, especially in the United States
law-enforcement officer working for the federal government

Examples

The federal official led the investigation into the high-profile cybercrime case.
During the press conference, a federal official answered questions from reporters about the new policy.
Several federal officials were present at the emergency coordination meeting.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: state official - a government officer working at the state level
