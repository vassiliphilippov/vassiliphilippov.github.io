to call 911

1. VERB

/ kɔl naɪn wʌn wʌn / (AmE), / kɔːl naɪn wʌn wʌn / (BrE)
to telephone the emergency services for immediate help, especially in the United States or Canada
contact emergency services (US/Canada)
american

Examples

If you witness a serious accident, you should call 911 immediately.
She taught her children how to call 911 in case of an emergency.
During the fire, several neighbors rushed to call 911 for help.

Synset Examples

In an emergency, you should always call 911.
Call 911 if you see any suspicious activity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: call emergency services - to telephone emergency responders (general phrase)
