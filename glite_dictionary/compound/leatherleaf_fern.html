leatherleaf fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛðɚˌlif fɝn / (AmE), / ˈlɛðəˌliːf fɜːn / (BrE)
a widely distributed fern of the tropical Southern Hemisphere with leathery, deeply divided fronds
fern with leathery divided fronds

Examples

The florist used leatherleaf fern as greenery in the bouquet arrangements.
In the wild, leatherleaf fern thrives in shaded, humid environments throughout the Southern Hemisphere.
Many gardeners appreciate leatherleaf fern for its hardy, attractive fronds.
