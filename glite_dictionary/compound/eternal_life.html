eternal life

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈtɝːnəl laɪf / (AmE), / ɪˈtɜːnəl laɪf / (BrE)
life that has no beginning or end; especially, existence continuing forever after death or outside of time, as in religious or philosophical belief.
life without beginning or end

Examples

Many religions promise eternal life to those who follow their teachings.
Philosophers have long debated whether eternal life is desirable or even possible.
She found comfort in the belief of eternal life after the loss of her loved one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immortality - the state of never dying or decaying
