slipper orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈslɪpər ˌɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈslɪpə ˌɔːkɪd / (BrE)
a wild orchid with an inflated pouch-shaped lip, especially found in America, difficult or impossible to cultivate in gardens
wild orchid with a pouch-shaped lip

Examples

The slipper orchid is prized by botanists for its distinctive pouch-shaped flower.
Many species of slipper orchids are protected due to their rarity in the wild.
Gardeners find the slipper orchid challenging to cultivate successfully outside its natural habitat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lady's slipper - another common name for slipper orchid
