Congo River

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑːŋɡoʊ ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡəʊ ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A major river in central Africa, one of the world's longest, flowing through the Democratic Republic of the Congo to the Atlantic Ocean.
major river in central Africa

Examples

The Congo River is the second longest river in Africa after the Nile.
Many communities depend on the Congo River for transportation and fishing.
Explorers once struggled to map the upper reaches of the Congo River.

Etymology

Named after the Kingdom of Kongo, an African kingdom along its lower course.
