stock market index

1. NOUN

/ stɑk ˈmɑrkɪt ˈɪndɛks / (AmE), / stɒk ˈmɑːkɪt ˈɪndɛks / (BrE)
an indicator that measures and reports the changes in prices of selected groups of stocks, representing a portion of the stock market
indicator of stock market performance

Examples

The stock market index reached an all-time high amid positive economic news.
Many investors track the stock market index to gauge overall market performance.
A sudden drop in a major stock market index can influence markets worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: index - a numerical measure of changes in a representative group of individual data points
