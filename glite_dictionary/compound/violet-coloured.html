violet-coloured

1. ADJ

/ ˈvaɪələt ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈvaɪələt ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
Having the color of violet; colored like the violet flower.
having the color of violet

Examples

She wore a beautiful violet-coloured scarf around her neck.
The artist used a violet-coloured paint for the evening sky in the landscape.
Tiny violet-coloured flowers covered the hillside in spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purple - a color intermediate between red and blue
  • Degree Lower: blue - the color of the clear sky
  • Degree Higher: indigo - a deep and purplish blue color
