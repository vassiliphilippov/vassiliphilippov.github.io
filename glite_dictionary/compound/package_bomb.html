package bomb

1. NOUN

/ ˈpækɪdʒ bɑm / (AmE), / ˈpækɪdʒ bɒm / (BrE)
an explosive device concealed in a package or envelope, designed to detonate when opened
bomb hidden inside a package

Examples

Police discovered a package bomb on the steps of the embassy before it could be opened.
Authorities warned the public after a package bomb injured two people in a residential neighborhood.
The package bomb was disguised to look like a normal delivery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: letter bomb - a bomb concealed in a letter or envelope
