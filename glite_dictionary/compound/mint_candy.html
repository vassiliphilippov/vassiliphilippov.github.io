mint candy

1. NOUN

/ mɪnt ˈkændi / (AmE), / mɪnt ˈkandi / (BrE)
a sweet or confectionery flavored with mint oil, usually hard or chewy in texture
candy flavored with mint oil

Examples

She always keeps a mint candy in her purse for a refreshing treat after lunch.
He offered me a mint candy while we waited for the bus on a hot afternoon.
The restaurant gives out complimentary mint candies with the bill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peppermint - a strong mint flavor used in candy
  • Synonym: candy - a sweet food made with sugar
