Photostat machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊtəˌstæt məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊstæt məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a duplicating machine used to make quick positive or negative copies directly onto prepared paper
early duplicating machine

Examples

The library still has a working Photostat machine in its archives.
Before digital copiers, offices relied on a Photostat machine for duplicating important documents.
A technician explained how to operate the vintage Photostat machine on display.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photocopier - a machine for making paper copies of documents and visual images quickly and cheaply

Etymology

Photostat is a trademark formed from 'photo-' (relating to light or photography) and 'stat' (from 'static', indicating an instantaneous copy).
