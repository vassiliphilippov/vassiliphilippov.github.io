to give it a try

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ɪt ə traɪ / (AmE), / ɡɪv ɪt ə traɪ / (BrE)
to attempt to do something, especially for the first time or to see if it is possible or enjoyable
attempt something; try

Examples

I've never played tennis before, but I'll give it a try.
If you're not sure you can finish the puzzle, just give it a try anyway.
She was nervous about public speaking, but decided to give it a try at the event.

Synset Examples

let's give it a whirl!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: try - make an attempt or effort to do something
  • Synonym: give it a go - informal compound meaning to attempt or try something
