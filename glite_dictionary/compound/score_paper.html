score paper

1. NOUN

/ skɔr ˈpeɪ.pɚ / (AmE), / skɔː ˈpeɪ.pə / (BrE)
Paper printed with lines for writing musical notation.
paper for writing music notation

Examples

She jotted down her new melody on a sheet of score paper before she forgot it.
The music teacher distributed score paper to the students for their composition assignment.
You can buy score paper at most stores that sell musical supplies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manuscript paper - blank sheets with staves for musical notation
  • Synonym: sheet music - printed music notation
