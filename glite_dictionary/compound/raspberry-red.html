raspberry-red

1. ADJ

/ ˈræzˌbɛri rɛd / (AmE), / ˈrɑːzbəri rɛd / (BrE)
A deep, vivid red color resembling that of fresh raspberries.
deep, vivid red color

Examples

She wore a raspberry-red dress that stood out at the party.
The raspberry-red lipstick complemented her complexion beautifully.
We painted the kitchen walls a cheerful raspberry-red to brighten the space.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crimson - a rich deep red color inclining to purple
  • Degree Lower: scarlet - a brilliant red color with a slightly orange tinge
