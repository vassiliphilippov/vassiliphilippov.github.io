Walt Disney World

1. PROPN

/ wɔlt ˈdɪzni wɜrld / (AmE), / wɔːlt ˈdɪzni wɜːld / (BrE)
A large amusement park and resort complex opened in 1971 near Orlando, Florida, known for its themed attractions and entertainment.
large Florida amusement park and resort

Examples

My family saved for years to visit Walt Disney World during the summer holidays.
Walt Disney World is famous for its Magic Kingdom and Epcot theme parks.
Many people dream of spending their honeymoon at Walt Disney World in Florida.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Disneyland - an amusement park built by Walt Disney in California

Etymology

Named after Walt Disney (1901–1966), American film producer and founder of The Walt Disney Company.
