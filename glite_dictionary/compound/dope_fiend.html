dope fiend

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊp fiːnd / (AmE), / ˈdəʊp fiːnd / (BrE)
an informal and often derogatory term for a person addicted to drugs, especially narcotics
person addicted to drugs
informal slang offensive

Examples

The main character was a dope fiend looking for a way out of addiction.
He was dismissed as a dope fiend by those who didn't understand his struggle.
Many neighborhoods have been affected by the presence of dope fiends seeking their next fix.

Synset Examples

She spoke about the dope fiend's impact on the community.
He used to be a dope fiend before turning his life around.
The movie portrayed him as a dope fiend struggling for recovery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: junkie - slang term for a drug addict
  • Synonym: addict - person who is physically or psychologically dependent on a substance

Etymology

From 'dope' (slang for drugs, especially narcotics) + 'fiend' (evil spirit or addict).
