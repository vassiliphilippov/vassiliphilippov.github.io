to boost production

1. VERB

/ bust prəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / buːst prəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
to increase the amount or rate of output or manufacture in a business or industry
increase output or manufacture

Examples

The company plans to boost production by upgrading its assembly lines next year.
Government incentives helped farmers boost production during the season of high demand.
Investing in renewable energy will boost production while reducing environmental impact.

Synset Examples

We invested in new machinery to boost production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: increase production - to make more products or output
  • Antonym: reduce production - to decrease the amount of products made
