hearty meal

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrti ˈmiːl / (AmE), / ˈhɑːti ˈmiːl / (BrE)
A large and satisfying meal, especially one that is nourishing and filling.
large, satisfying meal

Examples

After hiking all day, we sat down to a hearty meal that restored our energy.
The inn served a hearty meal of roast meat and fresh bread to its guests.
A hearty meal is just what you need on a cold winter evening.

Synset Examples

They welcomed us with a hearty meal after our long journey.
They enjoyed a hearty meal together by the fire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feast - a large meal, typically a celebratory one
  • Antonym: snack - a small amount of food eaten between meals
