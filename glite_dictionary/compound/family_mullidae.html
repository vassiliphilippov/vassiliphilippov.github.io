family Mullidae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli mjuːˈlɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli mʌlˈlɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of marine fishes also known as goatfishes or red mullets, typically having barbels on the chin and found in warm seas.
a family of marine fish (goatfishes)

Examples

The family Mullidae includes several species of brightly colored fish found in coral reefs.
Biologists have studied the unique feeding habits of the family Mullidae for decades.
Goatfishes belonging to the family Mullidae are distinguished by their chin barbels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goatfish - a marine fish in the family Mullidae
  • Synonym: red mullet - a common name for species in the family Mullidae

Etymology

From Latin 'mullus' (mullet), with scientific suffixes denoting family; used in zoological taxonomy.
