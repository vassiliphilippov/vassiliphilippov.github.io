fuckwitted response

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌkˌwɪtɪd rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / ˈfʌkˌwɪtɪd rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
a reply or answer that is extremely foolish or stupid, often said with strong disapproval or contempt
extremely foolish reply
vulgar informal

Examples

Her fuckwitted response left the entire team speechless in the meeting.
Please don't give me another fuckwitted response when I ask a serious question.
After hearing his fuckwitted response, it was clear he hadn't read the instructions.

Synset Examples

She gave a fuckwitted response that surprised everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolish answer - an answer that lacks sense or intelligence
  • Antonym: clever response - an intelligent or witty reply

Etymology

From 'fuckwitted' (vulgar slang for 'very stupid'), modeled after similar compounds like 'half-witted'.
