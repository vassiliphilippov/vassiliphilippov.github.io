Human Genome Project

1. PROPN

/ ˈhjuːmən dʒiːˌnoʊm ˈprɑːdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən ˈdʒiːnəʊm ˈprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
an international scientific research project aimed at mapping and understanding all the genes of the human genome
global study mapping human genes

Examples

The Human Genome Project has revolutionized our understanding of genetics and medicine.
She wrote her thesis on the ethical implications arising from the Human Genome Project.
The Human Genome Project was completed in 2003, providing a map of the human DNA sequence.
