Bombay ceiba

1. NOUN

/ bɑmˈbeɪ ˈseɪbə / (AmE), / bɒmˈbeɪ ˈsiːbə / (BrE)
A large tropical tree with deeply ridged trunk, producing seed pods covered with silky floss; source of kapok fiber.
large tropical tree; source of kapok fiber

Examples

The Bombay ceiba is often cultivated for its soft, silky kapok fiber used in pillows and mattresses.
Towering above the village, the massive Bombay ceiba serves as a shelter for birds and small animals.
Many artisans use the wood from the Bombay ceiba to carve traditional masks and sculptures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kapok tree - tropical tree producing kapok fiber
  • Synonym: ceiba - tropical tree of the genus Ceiba
