musical accompaniment

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪkəl əˈkʌmpənimənt / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl əˈkʌmpənimənt / (BrE)
A musical part, either vocal or instrumental, that supports or provides background for other musical parts.
supporting musical part

Examples

The singer performed with a gentle musical accompaniment from a classical guitar.
Her solo was enhanced by the subtle musical accompaniment of the orchestra.
Jazz improvisation often features a strong musical accompaniment that supports the lead melody.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accompaniment - music that is played to support or complement another part
