facial expression

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪʃl ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈfeɪʃəl ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
a look on a person's face that shows their feelings, thoughts, or mood
look on a person's face showing feeling or mood

Examples

Her facial expression changed from happy to worried in an instant.
His calm facial expression gave no hint of his excitement.
The actor is known for his ability to convey emotion through subtle facial expressions.

Synset Examples

a sad expression
a look of triumph
an angry face

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expression - a look on someone's face that shows emotions or thoughts
  • Synonym: grimace - a twisted expression on a person's face, typically expressing disgust or pain

2. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪʃəl ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈfeɪʃəl ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
a movement or arrangement of the facial muscles that conveys a person's feelings, mood, or reaction
movement of facial muscles showing feelings

Examples

Her facial expression showed exactly how surprised she felt.
He tried to hide his disappointment, but his facial expression gave him away.
The comedian changed his facial expression remarkably for each character.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expression - the act of conveying thoughts or feelings
