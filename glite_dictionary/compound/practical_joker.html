practical joker

1. NOUN

/ ˈpræktɪkəl ˈdʒoʊkər / (AmE), / ˈpræktɪkəl ˈdʒəʊkə / (BrE)
a person who plays tricks or pranks on others for amusement, often in practical ways
person who plays pranks

Examples

He is known as the office practical joker who always has a new trick to play.
The practical joker filled my desk drawer with confetti as a harmless prank.
Although he thought his prank was funny, not everyone appreciated the practical joker at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prankster - a person who plays pranks
