basil mint

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪzəl mɪnt / (AmE), / ˈbæzəl mɪnt / (BrE)
a perennial herb native to the eastern United States, with aromatic narrow leaves and small greenish flowers
aromatic perennial herb of eastern US

Examples

The garden was filled with the fresh scent of basil mint after the rain.
She rubbed the leaves of basil mint between her fingers to release their aroma.
Native to the eastern United States, basil mint is often used in herbal remedies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mint - aromatic herb of the genus Mentha
