Republic of Malawi

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk ʌv məˈlɑwi / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv məˈlɑːwi / (BrE)
a landlocked country in southeastern Africa, formerly known as Nyasaland, which gained independence from the United Kingdom in 1964
country in southeastern Africa

Examples

The Republic of Malawi is known for its beautiful lake and diverse wildlife.
In 1964, the Republic of Malawi became independent from British colonial rule.
She traveled across southern Africa, visiting the Republic of Malawi during her journey.
