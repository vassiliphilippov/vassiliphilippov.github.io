dunny can

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌni kæn / (AmE), / ˈdʌni kæn / (BrE)
a portable toilet or lavatory, especially one using a removable container for waste
portable toilet with removable container
british

Examples

We had to carry the dunny can a long way to the collection point.
The farm used a dunny can system before indoor plumbing was installed.
Please make sure the dunny can is emptied before the next visitors arrive.

Synset Examples

We need to take the dunny can to the dump today.
The smell from the dunny can was unbearable.
He forgot to empty the dunny can before the weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outhouse - a small building containing a simple toilet
  • Synonym: chemical toilet - a portable toilet using chemicals to neutralize waste

Etymology

Australian English; 'dunny' from British dialect, perhaps from Scots 'dung' (manure), with 'can' referring to the removable container.
