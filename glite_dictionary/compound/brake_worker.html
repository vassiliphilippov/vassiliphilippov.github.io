brake worker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪk ˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈbreɪk ˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a railroad employee responsible for operating and maintaining a train's brakes
railroad worker in charge of train brakes

Examples

The brake worker carefully inspected the train's braking system before departure.
During the journey, the brake worker communicated with the engineer to ensure smooth stops.
In the past, every freight train needed a brake worker onboard for safe operation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brakeman - a railroad worker responsible for a train's brakes
