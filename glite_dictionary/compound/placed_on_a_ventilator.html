to placed on a ventilator

1. VERB

/ pleɪst ɑn ə ˈvɛntəˌleɪtɚ / (AmE), / pleɪst ɒn ə ˈvɛntɪleɪtə / (BrE)
connected to a machine that helps a person breathe, especially in a hospital setting
connected to a breathing machine
medical

Examples

The patient was placed on a ventilator after experiencing severe respiratory distress.
Following the accident, she needed to be placed on a ventilator to assist her breathing.
Doctors decided he should be placed on a ventilator until his condition stabilized.

Synset Examples

The patient was placed on a ventilator for support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mechanically ventilated - receiving artificial breathing support via mechanical ventilator
