camphor spirit

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæm.fɚ ˈspɪr.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈkæm.fə ˈspɪr.ɪt / (BrE)
A solution of alcohol and camphor used as a steam inhalant for respiratory relief or applied topically to the skin.
alcohol-camphor solution for medical use
medical

Examples

She rubbed camphor spirit on her chest to relieve her cough.
The doctor recommended using camphor spirit as a steam inhalant for congestion.
A bottle of camphor spirit was kept in the medicine cabinet for colds and aches.
