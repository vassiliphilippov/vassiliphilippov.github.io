baby carriage

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.bi ˌkær.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi ˌkær.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
a small vehicle on four wheels in which a baby or young child is pushed while lying down or sitting
small vehicle for pushing a baby

Examples

She gently rocked the baby carriage to soothe her newborn to sleep.
They took a walk in the park, pushing their daughter in a baby carriage.
The old baby carriage was found in the attic, dusty but still sturdy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pram - a British term for a baby carriage
  • Synonym: stroller - a seat on wheels for pushing a baby or small child
