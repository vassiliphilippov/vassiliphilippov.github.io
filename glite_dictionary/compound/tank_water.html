tank water

1. NOUN

/ tæŋk ˈwɔtɚ / (AmE), / tæŋk ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
water contained in or designated for an aquarium or similar tank, often for keeping fish or aquatic animals
water in an aquarium or tank

Examples

Over time, the tank water can accumulate harmful chemicals and needs to be changed regularly.
The temperature of the tank water should be checked to ensure a healthy environment for the fish.
Proper filtration helps keep the tank water clear and safe for aquatic plants and animals.

Synset Examples

Treatment for lens louse involves changing the tank water.
