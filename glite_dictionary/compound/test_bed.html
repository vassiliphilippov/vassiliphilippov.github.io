test bed

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛst bɛd / (AmE), / ˈtɛst bɛd / (BrE)
A place or setup equipped with instruments for testing engines, machinery, computer programs, or other systems under working conditions.
facility for testing equipment under real conditions

Examples

The new jet engine was installed on a test bed for performance evaluation.
Software teams use a test bed to simulate real user environments.
Before mass production, the robot was run on a test bed to detect any faults.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: testing ground - a place or situation for trying out new methods or ideas

Etymology

Formed from 'test' and 'bed', with 'bed' meaning a base or platform for supporting something.
