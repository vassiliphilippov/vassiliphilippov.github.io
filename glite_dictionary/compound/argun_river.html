Argun River

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑːrɡən ˈrɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡʊn ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in eastern Asia that originates in China, forms part of the border between China and Russia, and flows into the Amur River
river on China-Russia border

Examples

The Argun River serves as a natural boundary between China and Russia for much of its length.
Scientists have studied pollution levels in the Argun River due to its international importance.
Flooding along the Argun River often affects villages on both sides of the border.
