Bavarian cream

1. NOUN

/ bəˈvɛriən ˈkriːm / (AmE), / bəˈvɛəriən ˈkriːm / (BrE)
a rich, creamy dessert made from custard set with gelatin, often flavored and chilled in a mold
rich custard dessert set with gelatin

Examples

The restaurant offers a delicious Bavarian cream for dessert.
She made a chocolate Bavarian cream to impress her dinner guests.
After dinner, they served slices of strawberry Bavarian cream topped with fresh fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: custard - a dessert made with eggs, milk, and sugar, thickened by heat

Etymology

From French 'crème bavaroise' (Bavarian cream), named after Bavaria, Germany.
