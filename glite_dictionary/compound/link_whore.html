link whore

1. NOUN

/ lɪŋk hɔɹ / (AmE), / lɪŋk hɔː / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) A person who aggressively seeks backlinks for increased web traffic or self-promotion, often by spamming links online.
someone who aggressively seeks web links for self-promotion
slang vulgar

Examples

Some users view him as a link whore because he posts the same article everywhere.
The forum moderators banned all link whores to reduce spam and improve discussion quality.
She admitted being a link whore during the early days of her blogging career.

Synset Examples

The blogger became a link whore chasing traffic.
He was labeled a link whore for spamming forums.
Avoid being a link whore; focus on quality content.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spammer - someone who sends unsolicited messages or posts links for promotion

Etymology

Formed from 'link' (web address) and 'whore' (used here for someone who excessively or indiscriminately pursues something).
