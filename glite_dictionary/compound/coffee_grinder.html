coffee grinder

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑfi ˌɡraɪndər / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ˌɡraɪndə / (BrE)
a machine or device for grinding roasted coffee beans into powder or granules
a device for grinding coffee beans

Examples

He filled the coffee grinder with fresh beans before breakfast.
The old-fashioned coffee grinder on the shelf once belonged to my grandmother.
With a new coffee grinder, you can enjoy freshly ground coffee every morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grinder - a machine or device for grinding something
  • Degree Higher: coffee maker - a machine used to brew coffee
