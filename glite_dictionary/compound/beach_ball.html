beach ball

1. NOUN

/ biːtʃ bɑl / (AmE), / biːtʃ bɔːl / (BrE)
a large, lightweight inflatable ball used for play at the beach or in swimming pools
large inflatable ball for beach play

Examples

The children tossed the beach ball back and forth across the sand.
A colorful beach ball floated in the pool all afternoon.
She bought a new beach ball for their summer vacation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ball - a round object used in games and sports
