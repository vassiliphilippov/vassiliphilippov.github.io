dorm room

1. NOUN

/ dɔrm ruːm / (AmE), / dɔːm ruːm / (BrE)
a bedroom in a dormitory, typically containing several beds and shared by students
shared bedroom in a dormitory

Examples

After moving into her college dorm room, she quickly decorated the walls with photographs.
They stayed up late talking in their dorm room during freshman year.
He found it difficult to concentrate in a noisy dorm room shared with three others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dormitory - a building providing sleeping quarters for students

Etymology

Shortened from 'dormitory room', where 'dormitory' comes from Latin 'dormitorium' (sleeping place).
