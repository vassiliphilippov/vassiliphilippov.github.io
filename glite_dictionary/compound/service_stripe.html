service stripe

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝː.vɪs straɪp / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.vɪs straɪp / (BrE)
a narrow strip of cloth or embroidery worn on a uniform to indicate years of service, typically in the military or in public service organizations
insignia indicating years of service

Examples

After twenty years in the army, he proudly displayed five service stripes on his sleeve.
Each service stripe represents four years of honorable military duty.
The veteran explained the meaning of the service stripe sewn onto his uniform.
