bandy leg

1. NOUN

/ ˈbændi lɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈbændi lɛɡ / (BrE)
A leg that is bowed outward at the knee or below the knee, resulting in a noticeable outward curve.
leg curved outward at or below the knee

Examples

The child was born with a bandy leg that required corrective surgery.
His bandy legs were noticeable as he walked across the field.
Stories often described the old man with his distinctive bandy leg.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowleg - A leg that curves outward at the knee; synonym for bandy leg

Etymology

From 'bandy', meaning curved or bowed, likely of unknown origin.
