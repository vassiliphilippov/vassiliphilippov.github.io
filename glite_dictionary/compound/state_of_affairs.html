state of affairs

1. NOUN

/ steɪt əv əˈfɛrz / (AmE), / steɪt əv əˈfeəz / (BrE)
the general situation or combination of circumstances affecting a particular time or event
current situation or set of circumstances

Examples

The state of affairs in the company has improved dramatically in recent months.
Given the current state of affairs, we must reconsider our strategy for next year.
She expressed concern about the political state of affairs in her country.

Synset Examples

the present international situation is dangerous
wondered how such a state of affairs had come about
eternal truths will be neither true nor eternal unless they have fresh meaning for every new social situation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: situation - a set of circumstances in which one finds oneself; a state of affairs
  • Synonym: condition - the particular state that something or someone is in

2. NOUN

/ steɪt əv əˈfɛrz / (AmE), / steɪt əv əˈfeəz / (BrE)
the situation or set of circumstances at a particular time, especially with reference to something important or problematic
current situation or set of circumstances

Examples

The economic state of affairs in the country has caused concern among many citizens.
We need to adapt quickly to the changing state of affairs in the industry.
Given the current state of affairs, our priorities may need to shift.

Synset Examples
