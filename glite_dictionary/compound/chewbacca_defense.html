Chewbacca defense

1. NOUN

/ tʃuˈbɑkə dɪˈfɛns / (AmE), / tʃuːˈbækə dɪˈfɛns / (BrE)
a legal defense that aims to confuse or distract from the main issue by presenting absurd, irrelevant, or nonsensical arguments
absurd, distracting legal defense strategy
informal legal american

Examples

The lawyer's Chewbacca defense left the jury more confused than convinced.
Critics claim the company is using a Chewbacca defense to distract from the real issues.
Trying a Chewbacca defense in court rarely leads to success.

Synset Examples

Etymology

Popularized by the television show South Park, referencing the Star Wars character Chewbacca.
