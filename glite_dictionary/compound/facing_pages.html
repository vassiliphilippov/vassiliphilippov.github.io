facing pages

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪsɪŋ ˈpeɪdʒɪz / (AmE), / ˈfeɪsɪŋ ˈpeɪdʒɪz / (BrE)
two pages in a book, magazine, or other publication that are opposite each other when the book is open
two opposite pages in a book or publication

Examples

The illustration appears across the facing pages of the book, making a striking impression.
She wrote notes on both facing pages of her journal for easier comparison.
Advertisements often take up facing pages in glossy magazines for greater visual impact.
