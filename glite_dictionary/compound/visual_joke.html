visual joke

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪʒuəl dʒoʊk / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒuəl dʒəʊk / (BrE)
A joke whose humor is conveyed primarily through visual elements rather than words or dialogue.
joke conveyed by visual means

Examples

The classic silent films are full of clever visual jokes that still make audiences laugh today.
The cartoon uses bright colors and visual jokes to entertain viewers of all ages.
He relied on visual jokes rather than dialogue to create a comedic atmosphere in his films.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sight gag - a joke or humorous element in film or comedy achieved by visual action
