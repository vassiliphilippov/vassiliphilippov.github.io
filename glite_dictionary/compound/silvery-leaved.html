silvery-leaved

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlvəri liːvd / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəri liːvd / (BrE)
(of a plant) having leaves that appear silvery in color.
having silvery-colored leaves

Examples

The silvery-leaved sage stood out against the green shrubs in the garden.
Gardeners prize the silvery-leaved varieties of eucalyptus for their ornamental value.
She admired the silvery-leaved olive tree shimmering in the afternoon sun.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: silver - having the color or appearance of silver
