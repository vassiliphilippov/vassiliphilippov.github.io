Tar Heel State

1. PROPN

/ tɑr hil steɪt / (AmE), / tɑː hiːl steɪt / (BrE)
A nickname for the state of North Carolina in the southeastern United States.
nickname for North Carolina, U.S. state

Examples

She grew up in the Tar Heel State before moving to New York for college.
The Tar Heel State is famous for its beautiful coastline and mountain ranges.
Many people refer to North Carolina as the Tar Heel State in conversation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: North Carolina - A southeastern U.S. state, one of the original 13 colonies

Etymology

Refers to North Carolina's historical production of tar, pitch, and turpentine from the state's pine forests.
