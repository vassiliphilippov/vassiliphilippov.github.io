high-energy physics

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈɛnɚdʒi ˈfɪzɪks / (AmE), / haɪ ˈɛnədʒi ˈfɪzɪks / (BrE)
the branch of physics that deals with the study of subatomic particles and their interactions, typically at very high energies
study of subatomic particles and their interactions

Examples

Many groundbreaking discoveries in high-energy physics have deepened our understanding of the universe.
Researchers in high-energy physics often work with particle accelerators.
He is pursuing a PhD in high-energy physics at a prestigious university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: particle physics - the branch of physics dealing with subatomic particles
