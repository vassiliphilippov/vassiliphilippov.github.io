batting helmet

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæɾɪŋ ˌhɛlmɪt / (AmE), / ˈbætɪŋ ˌhɛlmɪt / (BrE)
a protective helmet worn by a batter in baseball to safeguard the head from pitched balls
protective helmet worn by a baseball batter

Examples

The coach reminded each player to wear a batting helmet before stepping up to the plate.
He bought a new batting helmet for the upcoming baseball season.
Her batting helmet fit snugly, providing comfort and protection throughout the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helmet - a hard or padded protective head covering
