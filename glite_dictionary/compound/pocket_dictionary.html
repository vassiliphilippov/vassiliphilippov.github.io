pocket dictionary

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkɪt ˈdɪkʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt ˈdɪkʃən(ə)ri / (BrE)
a small dictionary designed to be carried easily, typically in one's pocket
small, portable dictionary

Examples

She always keeps a pocket dictionary in her bag when traveling abroad.
A pocket dictionary is handy for quick reference during language classes.
He bought a French-English pocket dictionary to help him communicate on his trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dictionary - a reference book listing words and their meanings, pronunciations, etc.
