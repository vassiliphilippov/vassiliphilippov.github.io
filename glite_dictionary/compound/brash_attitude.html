brash attitude

1. NOUN

/ bræʃ ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / bræʃ ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
a manner of behaving that is aggressively bold or self-assertive, often in a way considered rude or tactless
aggressively bold behavior

Examples

Her brash attitude often makes her the center of attention at meetings.
People were put off by his brash attitude during the interview.
Despite her brash attitude, she was well-liked for her honesty.

Synset Examples

She has a brash attitude that draws attention.
His brash comments often offend others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bold - willing to take risks; confident and courageous
  • Antonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence; easily frightened
