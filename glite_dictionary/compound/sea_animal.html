sea animal

1. NOUN

/ si ˈænɪml / (AmE), / si ˈænɪml / (BrE)
an animal that lives in the sea, such as a fish, mollusc, or marine mammal
an animal living in the sea

Examples

Many children are fascinated by the colorful sea animals they see at the aquarium.
Pollution threatens the survival of several species of sea animal every year.
The documentary features rare footage of deep-sea sea animals in their natural habitat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marine animal - an animal that lives in the sea or ocean
