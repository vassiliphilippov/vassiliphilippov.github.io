Belarusian language

1. NOUN

/ bəˌlɛrusiən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / bɛˌlæːrʊsiən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the East Slavic language spoken primarily in Belarus
East Slavic language of Belarus

Examples

Many people in Belarus speak both Russian and Belarusian language in their daily lives.
She is studying the Belarusian language at university to better understand Belarusian culture.
The official documents were translated from English into the Belarusian language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Russian language - the East Slavic language spoken in Russia
  • Synonym: Ukrainian language - the East Slavic language spoken in Ukraine

Etymology

From Belarus + English 'language'; Belarusian is from Belarus (the country) and the suffix -ian.
