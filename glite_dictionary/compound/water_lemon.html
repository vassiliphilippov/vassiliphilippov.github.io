water lemon

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ ˌlɛmən / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌlɛmən / (BrE)
the edible yellow fruit of the Jamaica honeysuckle (Passiflora laurifolia), known for its juicy pulp and sweet flavor
edible yellow fruit of Jamaica honeysuckle

Examples

The tropical market had some fresh water lemons on display next to the passion fruit.
She tasted the sweet pulp of a ripe water lemon for the first time while visiting Jamaica.
Botanists studied the flowering habits of the water lemon in its native habitat.
