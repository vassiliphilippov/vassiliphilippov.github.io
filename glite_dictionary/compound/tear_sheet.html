tear sheet

1. NOUN

/ tɛr ʃit / (AmE), / tɛə ʃiːt / (BrE)
a page in a magazine, newspaper, or similar publication designed to be easily removed, often for reference or evidence of publication.
easily removable page from a publication

Examples

The advertiser requested a tear sheet as proof their ad was published in the magazine.
I clipped the recipe from a tear sheet in the newspaper last weekend.
Designers often collect tear sheets for inspiration from fashion magazines.
