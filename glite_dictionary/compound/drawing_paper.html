drawing paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɔɪɪŋ ˌpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːɪŋ ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
Paper specially made with a surface suitable for drawing, drafting, or sketching.
paper for drawing or drafting

Examples

She bought a pad of drawing paper for her art class.
The architect asked for high-quality drawing paper to prepare the blueprints.
Different textures of drawing paper can affect the outcome of a pencil sketch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sketchbook - a book with blank pages for drawing or sketching
