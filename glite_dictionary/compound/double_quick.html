double quick

1. ADV

/ ˌdʌbl ˈkwɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʌbl ˈkwɪk / (BrE)
at a very fast speed; very quickly
very quickly; at a very fast speed

Examples

We need to finish this task double quick if we want to meet the deadline.
The children tidied up double quick when they heard their parents coming.
They marched double quick to reach the next checkpoint before sunset.

Synset Examples

now let's play the piece again double-quick

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quickly - at a fast speed; rapidly
  • Antonym: slowly - at a slow speed; not quickly

Etymology

From the military term 'double-quick', meaning to move at twice the regulation marching pace.
