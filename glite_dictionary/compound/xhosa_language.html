Xhosa language

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊsə læŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkɒsə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Bantu language spoken primarily in South Africa, closely related to Zulu and noted as the country's second most common home language.
Bantu language spoken in South Africa

Examples

She is fluent in both Xhosa language and English.
The Xhosa language uses distinctive click sounds that are not found in most other languages.
Learning the Xhosa language helped him connect with local communities in South Africa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Zulu language - A Bantu language closely related to Xhosa and widely spoken in South Africa

Etymology

From Xhosa, the name of the ethnic group and their language; 'language' from Latin 'lingua'.
