air station

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌsteɪʃən / (BrE)
a military base where aircraft are stationed, maintained, and operated
base for military aircraft

Examples

The new coastal air station will improve rapid response to emergencies at sea.
He was assigned to the air station in northern Alaska for his two-year tour of duty.
After the ceremony, several jets took off from the air station to mark the occasion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air base - a military base for aircraft operations; often inland
