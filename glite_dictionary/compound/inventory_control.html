inventory control

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnvənˌtɔri kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / ˈɪnvəntrɪ kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
The management of supplies and stock to ensure adequate availability without excessive surplus.
management of inventory levels

Examples

The company improved its inventory control to reduce storage costs and avoid product shortages.
Effective inventory control helps businesses maintain the right balance between supply and demand.
Advances in technology have automated many inventory control processes in warehouses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stock control - management of the supply and flow of stock in a business
