celery seed

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛləri siːd / (AmE), / ˈsɛləri siːd / (BrE)
the small seed of the celery plant, used as a seasoning in cooking
seed used as seasoning

Examples

She sprinkled celery seed into the potato salad for extra flavor.
Some recipes call for celery seed instead of chopped celery stalks.
The dressing is made more aromatic by adding a pinch of celery seed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celery - a vegetable plant with crisp, pale green stalks
  • Synonym: caraway seed - aromatic seed used as seasoning similar to celery seed
