grape smoothie

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪp smuːði / (AmE), / ɡreɪp smuːði / (BrE)
A blended drink made with grapes, often mixed with yogurt, juice, or ice.
a smoothie made with grapes

Examples

After exercising, she made herself a refreshing grape smoothie with a handful of ice and yogurt.
You can boost your vitamin intake with a homemade grape smoothie every morning.
The café's most popular drink in summer is their icy grape smoothie.

Synset Examples

I love a grape smoothie on hot summer days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoothie - a thick, cold beverage made from pureed raw fruit and/or vegetables
