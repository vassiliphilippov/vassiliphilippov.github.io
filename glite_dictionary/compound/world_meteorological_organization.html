World Meteorological Organization

1. PROPN

/ wɝːld miˌtiːriəˈlɑːdʒɪkl ˌɔːrgənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / wɜːld miˌtiəriəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a specialized agency of the United Nations responsible for international cooperation and coordination of meteorological, climatological, hydrological, and related geophysical activities
UN agency for weather and climate coordination

Examples

The World Meteorological Organization coordinates global weather monitoring systems to improve forecasts.
According to the World Meteorological Organization, average global temperatures have reached record highs this year.
The World Meteorological Organization hosts annual conferences on climate change and disaster preparedness.
