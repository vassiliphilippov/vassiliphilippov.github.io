Catalan language

1. NOUN

/ kæˈtælən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / kəˈtælən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Romance language spoken mainly in Catalonia, eastern Spain, and also in parts of France, Andorra, and Italy
a Romance language of Catalonia and nearby regions

Examples

She is fluent in both Spanish and Catalan language.
The Catalan language has official status in Andorra.
Children in Barcelona learn the Catalan language at school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Spanish language - the Romance language spoken in Spain and many countries in Latin America
  • Synonym: Occitan language - a Romance language spoken in southern France and nearby regions

Etymology

From Catalan 'català', referring to Catalonia, a region in northeastern Spain.
