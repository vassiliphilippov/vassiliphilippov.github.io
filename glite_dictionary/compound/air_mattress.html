air mattress

1. NOUN

/ ɛr ˈmætrɪs / (AmE), / ɛə ˈmætrəs / (BrE)
A mattress that can be stored flat and inflated with air for sleeping or lying on.
inflatable mattress for sleeping or lying on

Examples

We decided to bring an air mattress for extra guests who might stay overnight.
The air mattress deflated during the night, leaving me almost on the floor.
He prefers sleeping on an air mattress when camping instead of a sleeping bag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inflatable mattress - an alternative term for an air mattress
