totally loco

1. ADJ

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬ə.li ˈloʊ.koʊ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təli ˈləʊ.kəʊ / (BrE)
Extremely crazy or wild; behaving in an uncontrolled or eccentric way.
extremely crazy or wild
informal slang

Examples

After hearing his plan, I thought he was totally loco to even try it.
The crowd went totally loco when the band started playing their hit song.
She decorated her apartment in a totally loco style, with bright colors everywhere.

Synset Examples

That party last night was totally loco!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged; extremely enthusiastic or wild
  • Synonym: insane - seriously mentally ill; extremely foolish

Etymology

"Loco" from Spanish 'loco' meaning 'crazy'; "totally" added for emphasis in informal English.
