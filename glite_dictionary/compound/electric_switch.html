electric switch

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk swɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk swɪtʃ / (BrE)
a device for making, breaking, or changing connections in an electrical circuit
device that controls an electrical circuit

Examples

She flipped the electric switch to turn on the light in the hall.
Make sure the electric switch is off before replacing the bulb.
The old electric switch was faulty and needed to be replaced.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: switch - device for making or breaking an electrical circuit
