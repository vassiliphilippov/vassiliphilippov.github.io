data entry

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪtə ˈɛntri / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtə ˈɛntri / (BrE)
the act or process of entering information into a computer system, typically using a keyboard or data input device
inputting information into a computer

Examples

She was hired for her fast and accurate data entry skills.
The company is outsourcing data entry tasks to improve efficiency.
Manual data entry can lead to errors if not carefully checked.

Synset Examples

The automated process means there’s no human involved in data entry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: data input - the process of sending data into a computer system
