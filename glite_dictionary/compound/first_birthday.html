first birthday

1. NOUN

/ fɝst ˈbɝθ.deɪ / (AmE), / fəːst ˈbɜːθ.deɪ / (BrE)
the celebration marking a child's first year of life
celebration of a child's first year

Examples

We invited our friends and family to celebrate our daughter's first birthday.
Her parents threw a big party for her first birthday complete with balloons and cake.
Photographs from a child's first birthday are treasured for years to come.

Synset Examples

They created a storybook for their daughter’s first birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birthday - the anniversary of the day on which a person was born
