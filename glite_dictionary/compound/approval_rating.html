approval rating

1. NOUN

/ əˈpruː.vəl ˈreɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈpruː.vəl ˈreɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
The percentage of people who express support or approval for a person, organization, or idea, especially in public opinion polls.
percentage indicating public support

Examples

The president's approval rating soared after the successful trade agreement was signed.
A low approval rating may prompt companies to rethink their leadership strategies.
Public trust often ebbs and flows with a politician's approval rating.

Synset Examples

His approval rating dipped to 41% after the scandal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: popularity - state of being liked or admired by many people
