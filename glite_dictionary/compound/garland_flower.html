garland flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrlənd ˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːlənd ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
a low evergreen shrub (Daphne cneorum) widely grown for its dense clusters of fragrant pink to deep rose flowers
a shrub with clusters of fragrant pink flowers

Examples

The garland flower blooms in early spring, filling the garden with a sweet fragrance.
We planted a garland flower hedge along the walkway for its lovely pink blossoms.
Many gardeners prize the garland flower for its evergreen leaves and vibrant clusters.
