Calamity Jane

1. PROPN

/ kəˈlæməti dʒeɪn / (AmE), / kəˈlæm.ɪ.ti dʒeɪn / (BrE)
(1852–1903) United States frontierswoman and folk hero of the American Wild West, known for her marksmanship and adventurous life.
noted frontierswoman and Wild West figure

Examples

Many books have been written about Calamity Jane and her exploits in the Wild West.
The legend of Calamity Jane continues to fascinate historians and Western enthusiasts alike.
Some historians question whether all the stories about Calamity Jane are entirely true.

Etymology

Nickname; 'Calamity' for her reputed knack for finding trouble, 'Jane' from her given name Martha Jane Cannary.
