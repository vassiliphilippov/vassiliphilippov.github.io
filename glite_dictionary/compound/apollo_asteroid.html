Apollo asteroid

1. NOUN

/ əˈpɑloʊ ˈæstərˌɔɪd / (AmE), / əˈpɒləʊ ˈæstərɔɪd / (BrE)
an asteroid whose orbit crosses Earth's orbit, belonging to a group named after the asteroid Apollo
type of Earth-crossing asteroid

Examples

The discovery of another Apollo asteroid raised new concerns about planetary defense.
Many Apollo asteroids pass close to Earth but pose no immediate threat.
Scientists are monitoring the orbit of the Apollo asteroid to assess any potential impact risk.

Etymology

Named after the asteroid Apollo, which was the first of its type discovered in 1932.
