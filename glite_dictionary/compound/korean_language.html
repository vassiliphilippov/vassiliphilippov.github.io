Korean language

1. NOUN

/ kəˈriːən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / kəˈriːən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the official language of Korea, spoken by Koreans in South and North Korea and by Korean communities worldwide
language spoken in Korea

Examples

She spent three years learning Korean language at university.
The Korean language has a unique writing system called Hangul.
Many words in the Korean language have Chinese origins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Korean - a person from Korea or relating to Korea or its people or language

Etymology

From Korean 'Han-guk-eo' (한국어), referring to the language of Korea.
