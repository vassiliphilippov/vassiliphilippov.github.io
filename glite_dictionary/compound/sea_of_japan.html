Sea of Japan

1. PROPN

/ si əv dʒəˈpæn / (AmE), / si əv dʒəˈpæn / (BrE)
a sea in the northwest Pacific Ocean, bordered by Japan, Korea, North Korea, and Russia
sea between Japan and the Korean Peninsula

Examples

The Sea of Japan is known for its abundant marine life and important shipping lanes.
Disputes over fishing rights sometimes occur in the Sea of Japan.
Warm currents in the Sea of Japan influence the climates of nearby coastal regions.
