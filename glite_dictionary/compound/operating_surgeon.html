operating surgeon

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːpəˌreɪtɪŋ ˈsɝːdʒən / (AmE), / ˈɒpəˌreɪtɪŋ ˈsɜːdʒən / (BrE)
a physician who performs surgical operations
a doctor who carries out surgery
medical

Examples

The operating surgeon carefully explained the procedure to the patient before the operation.
During the emergency, the operating surgeon made quick decisions that saved the patient's life.
The success of the transplant depended greatly on the skill of the operating surgeon.
