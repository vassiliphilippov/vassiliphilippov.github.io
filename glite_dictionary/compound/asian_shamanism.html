Asian shamanism

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪʒən ˈʃæmənɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈeɪʃ(ə)n ˈʃæmənɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
An animistic religion in northern Asia where communication with the spirit world is carried out by shamans acting as mediators.
animistic religion of northern Asia

Examples

Many indigenous communities still practice Asian shamanism as part of their cultural heritage.
Scholars have studied the rituals and symbols of Asian shamanism to understand its influence on local traditions.
The role of the shaman in Asian shamanism involves healing, divination, and communicating with spirits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shamanism - religious practice involving shamans as intermediaries with the spirit world
