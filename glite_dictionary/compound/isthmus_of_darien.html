Isthmus of Darien

1. PROPN

/ ˈɪsθməs əv dɛəriən / (AmE), / ˈɪsθməs əv dɛərɪən / (BrE)
a historical name for the isthmus joining Central America and South America, now generally known as the Isthmus of Panama.
historical name for Panama isthmus

Examples

In the 16th century, explorers struggled to cross the Isthmus of Darien due to its challenging terrain.
Balboa's expedition across the Isthmus of Darien marked a pivotal moment in the Age of Discovery.
The Isthmus of Darien was the proposed site for an early canal connecting the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans.

Synset Examples

Balboa crossed the Isthmus of Darien

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Isthmus of Panama - the isthmus joining Central and South America, current name

Etymology

Darien is believed to come from a local Indigenous name for a region in eastern Panama.
