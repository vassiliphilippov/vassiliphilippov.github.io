in person

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ɪn ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
physically present in a particular place, rather than communicating by phone, online, or via another person
physically present; not represented by another

Examples

Please make sure to submit the application in person at our office.
He wanted to apologize in person, rather than over the phone.
The celebrity appeared in person to greet her fans.

Synset Examples

I went there personally
he appeared in person

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personally - by oneself, in person
