lucky scratcher

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌki ˈskrætʃər / (AmE), / ˈlʌki ˈskratʃə / (BrE)
a person who wins frequently in lotteries or lottery-style games, especially with scratch tickets
person who often wins in lottery games

Examples

He considers himself a lucky scratcher, having hit the jackpot three times in the past year.
As a lucky scratcher, she's the envy of everyone at the local convenience store.
My neighbor claims to be a lucky scratcher, but I'm still waiting for my first big win.

Synset Examples

I'm a lucky scratcher, winning twice last month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: winner - a person who wins something
  • Antonym: loser - a person who does not win
