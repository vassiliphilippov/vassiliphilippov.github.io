bladder disorder

1. NOUN

/ ˈblædɚ dɪˌsɔrdɚ / (AmE), / ˈblædə dɪˌsɔːdə / (BrE)
a medical condition affecting the urinary bladder
medical condition affecting the bladder
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with a bladder disorder after she complained of frequent urination.
Certain medications can help manage symptoms of a bladder disorder.
He missed work for a week due to complications from his bladder disorder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urinary tract infection - an infection in any part of the urinary system, often affecting the bladder
  • Degree Higher: kidney disease - a disorder affecting the kidneys, often related to urinary tract
