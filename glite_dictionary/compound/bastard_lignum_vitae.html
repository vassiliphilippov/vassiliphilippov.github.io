bastard lignum vitae

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæstərd ˈlɪɡnəm ˈvaɪti, vaɪˌti / (AmE), / ˈbɑːstəd ˈlɪɡnəm ˈvaɪti, vaɪˌti / (BrE)
a small evergreen tree native to the southern United States and West Indies, valued as a source of lignum vitae wood
small evergreen tree used for lignum vitae wood

Examples

The bastard lignum vitae is prized for its extremely dense and durable wood.
Early settlers in the Caribbean used bastard lignum vitae for crafting tool handles and bearings.
It can be difficult to distinguish true lignum vitae from bastard lignum vitae by appearance alone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lignum vitae - type of very hard wood from certain tropical trees
