Indian rice

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən raɪs / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən raɪs / (BrE)
an edible grain from aquatic grasses native to North America, commonly known as wild rice
edible North American grass grain (wild rice)

Examples

The chef prepared a delicious dish using Indian rice instead of regular white rice.
Native Americans have harvested Indian rice from lakes and rivers for centuries.
We served roasted vegetables with a side of seasoned Indian rice at the dinner party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wild rice - edible grain from North American aquatic grasses
