business life

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznɪs laɪf / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs laɪf / (BrE)
the part of someone's life that is spent working in business or professional activities; a professional career in commerce or industry
professional or commercial career

Examples

After spending thirty years in business life, he decided to retire early.
She made many valuable connections throughout her business life.
The skills you develop in your business life can be useful in other areas as well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: career - an occupation undertaken for a significant period of a person's life and with opportunities for progress
  • Synonym: professional life - the activities and experience of working in a profession
