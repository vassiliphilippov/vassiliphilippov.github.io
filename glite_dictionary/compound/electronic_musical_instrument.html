electronic musical instrument

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˌmjuːzɪkəl ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˌmjuːzɪkəl ˈɪnstrəmənt / (BrE)
A musical instrument that produces sounds using electronic circuitry rather than acoustic means.
music device producing sound electronically

Examples

The band's keyboardist used an electronic musical instrument to create futuristic sounds.
Many modern composers experiment with the capabilities of the electronic musical instrument.
Owning an electronic musical instrument allows you to produce a variety of tones not possible with traditional instruments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: synthesizer - an electronic instrument producing a wide variety of sounds
  • Antonym: acoustic instrument - musical instrument that produces sound naturally without electronics
