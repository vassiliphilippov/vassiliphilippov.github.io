gravy holder

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪvi ˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪvi ˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a dish, often boat-shaped, used for serving gravy or sauce at the table
dish for serving gravy or sauce

Examples

She passed the gravy holder to her husband during Sunday dinner.
The elegant silver gravy holder was placed at the center of the table.
Please fill the gravy holder before everyone sits down.
