Warji language

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑːrdʒi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈwɑːdʒi ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken mainly in northern Nigeria, particularly in Bauchi and Jigawa states
Chadic language of northern Nigeria

Examples

The Warji language is spoken by thousands of people in northern Nigeria.
Linguists are working to document the grammar and vocabulary of the Warji language.
She is fluent in both Hausa and the Warji language.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Chadic language - a branch of the Afro-Asiatic language family
