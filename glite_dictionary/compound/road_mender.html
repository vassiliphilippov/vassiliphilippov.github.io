road mender

1. NOUN

/ roʊd ˈmɛndər / (AmE), / rəʊd ˈmɛndə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to repair and maintain roads
person who repairs roads

Examples

The road mender spent all morning filling potholes after the storm.
Local authorities hired several road menders to keep the highways in good condition.
Wearing a reflective vest, the road mender directed traffic around the repair site.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: road worker - person employed to construct or repair roads
