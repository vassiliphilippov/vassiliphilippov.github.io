final reckoning

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪ.nəl ˈrɛk.ən.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪ.nəl ˈrɛk.ən.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the time when a person must face the ultimate consequences of their actions or be judged, often at the end of a process or life
moment of ultimate judgment or consequences

Examples

After years of questionable decisions, the company finally reached its final reckoning when the audit was completed.
The villain tried to escape, but his final reckoning was inevitable.
She dreaded the final reckoning when her actions would be revealed to everyone.

Synset Examples

He faced his final reckoning after years of deceit.
The final reckoning came when the truth was revealed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: day of reckoning - the time when the consequences of one's actions are faced
  • Synonym: judgment day - the day at the end of the world when all people will be judged
