defense contractor

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛns kənˌtræktər / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns kənˌtræktə / (BrE)
a company or individual engaged in the development and manufacture of military systems, equipment, or services for a government
company or individual supplying military systems/services

Examples

The government awarded a multi-million dollar contract to a leading defense contractor.
As a defense contractor, the company specializes in advanced radar systems for the military.
Several defense contractors attended the conference on cybersecurity and national defense.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military contractor - company that provides services or supplies to the military
