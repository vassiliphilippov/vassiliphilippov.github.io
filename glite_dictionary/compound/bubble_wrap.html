bubble wrap

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌb.əl ræp / (AmE), / ˈbʌb.əl ræp / (BrE)
a pliable transparent plastic material used for packing fragile items, consisting of a sheet with regularly spaced, air-filled bubbles
plastic packing material with air bubbles

Examples

She wrapped the glass vase in bubble wrap before placing it in the box.
Kids love popping the bubbles on bubble wrap after packages arrive.
We need more bubble wrap to protect the electronics during shipping.

Synset Examples

The fragile items were packed safely in bubble-wrap.
