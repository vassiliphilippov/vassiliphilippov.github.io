credit analyst

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛdɪt ˈænəˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪt ˈanəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who examines the financial details of individuals or businesses to assess their ability to repay borrowed money
person who evaluates financial trustworthiness

Examples

After reviewing the applicant's finances, the credit analyst recommended approval of the loan.
She worked as a credit analyst at a major bank for five years.
The company hired a new credit analyst to improve their risk assessment procedures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: analyst - a person who analyzes information
