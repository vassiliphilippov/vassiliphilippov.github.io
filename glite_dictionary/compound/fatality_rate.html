fatality rate

1. NOUN

/ fəˈtæləti reɪt / (AmE), / fəˈtæləti reɪt / (BrE)
the proportion of deaths occurring in a specific population, typically expressed per 1,000 individuals per year
proportion of deaths in a population

Examples

The fatality rate of the disease dropped significantly after the introduction of the vaccine.
Authorities tracked the fatality rate in the region to monitor the impact of the disaster.
A low fatality rate is often an indicator of effective healthcare systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mortality rate - the rate of death in a population, often used synonymously
