psychology department

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈkɑlədʒi dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / saɪˈkɒlədʒi dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
an academic department at a college or university responsible for teaching and research in psychology
university department for psychology

Examples

She decided to major in psychology and soon became involved in research within the psychology department.
The psychology department is known for its innovative studies on human behavior.
There was an open house at the psychology department for prospective students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: department - a division of a large organization such as a university
