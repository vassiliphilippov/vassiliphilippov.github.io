dykey attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪki æˌtɪtjud / (AmE), / ˈdaɪki ˈætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
behavior, manner, or style considered to be masculine, especially as associated with lesbian women; usually regarded as slang and may be offensive.
masculine behavior, often used of lesbian women
slang offensive

Examples

Her dykey attitude often surprised her conservative friends.
Some people admired her dykey attitude and the confidence it expressed.
The film challenged stereotypes by portraying a main character with a dykey attitude unapologetically.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butch - having appearance or behavior typically considered masculine, especially in lesbian culture
  • Antonym: feminine attitude - behavior or style associated with traditional female or feminine traits

Etymology

From 'dykey,' a slang term derived from 'dyke,' itself a slang expression for a lesbian woman, often with a butch or masculine appearance.
