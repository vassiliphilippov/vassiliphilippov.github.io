higher rank

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪər ræŋk / (AmE), / ˈhaɪə ræŋk / (BrE)
a position or status that is superior to others, especially due to longer service or greater authority
superior position or status

Examples

After the promotion, she held a higher rank within the company.
Officers of higher rank have greater responsibilities in the military hierarchy.
He was respected by his peers because of his higher rank and experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seniority - the quality of being more senior or having greater length of service
  • Antonym: lower rank - a position or status that is inferior to others
