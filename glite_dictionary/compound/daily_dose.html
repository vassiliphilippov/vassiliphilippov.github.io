daily dose

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪli doʊs / (AmE), / ˈdeɪli dəʊs / (BrE)
the amount of a medicine or substance that is taken or should be taken each day
amount taken each day

Examples

Many people get their daily dose of news from online sources.
The doctor increased his daily dose to help manage his symptoms.
She never forgets to take her daily dose of vitamin C.

Synset Examples

I need to take my daily dose of vitamins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dose - a measured quantity of a medicine or drug
