English runner bean

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈrʌnər biːn / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈrʌnə biːn / (BrE)
A type of runner bean with long pods, typically sliced for cooking, commonly eaten in Britain.
long edible bean pod, British favorite
british

Examples

English runner bean is delicious when lightly steamed and served with a little butter.
She prepared a salad with chopped tomatoes and fresh English runner beans.
During the summer, my grandmother always grows English runner beans in her garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: runner bean - a climbing plant with long edible pods, also known as Phaseolus coccineus
