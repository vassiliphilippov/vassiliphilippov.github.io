West Briton

1. NOUN

/ wɛst ˈbrɪtən / (AmE), / wɛst ˈbrɪtən / (BrE)
a weekly newspaper published in Cornwall, England, or a person from the west of Britain
Cornish newspaper or person from west Britain

Examples

The West Briton regularly reports on local news and community events in Cornwall.
As a West Briton, she was deeply passionate about the culture of southwestern England.
Many residents read the West Briton to stay informed about what’s happening in their region.
