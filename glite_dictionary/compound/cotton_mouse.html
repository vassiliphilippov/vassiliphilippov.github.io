cotton mouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːtn̩ maʊs / (AmE), / ˈkɒtn maʊs / (BrE)
a large, dark-colored species of mouse native to the southeastern United States, often found in wooded areas
large dark mouse of southeastern U.S.

Examples

The cotton mouse inhabits forests and swamps throughout the southeastern states.
Researchers captured several cotton mice for their ecological study.
Unlike the house mouse, the cotton mouse prefers outdoor habitats with dense cover.
