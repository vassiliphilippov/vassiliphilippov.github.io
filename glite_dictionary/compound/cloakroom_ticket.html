cloakroom ticket

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊkˌruːm ˈtɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈkləʊkˌruːm ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
a ticket given to someone depositing an item in a cloakroom, used to reclaim the item later
ticket for retrieving items from a cloakroom

Examples

Please hold on to your cloakroom ticket to collect your coat after the show.
She misplaced her cloakroom ticket and had to wait while staff searched for her bag.
You need your cloakroom ticket if you want to get your umbrella back.
