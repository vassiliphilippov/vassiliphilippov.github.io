Grand Lama

1. NOUN

/ ɡrænd ˈlɑːmə / (AmE), / ɡrænd ˈlɑːmə / (BrE)
the chief lama and formerly the ruler of Tibet, often known as the Dalai Lama
chief lama of Tibet

Examples

The Grand Lama played a crucial role in both the spiritual and political life of Tibet.
Many pilgrims traveled great distances to seek the blessing of the Grand Lama.
Historically, the Grand Lama was revered as a living embodiment of Buddhist wisdom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dalai Lama - the spiritual leader of Tibetan Buddhism and former temporal ruler of Tibet
