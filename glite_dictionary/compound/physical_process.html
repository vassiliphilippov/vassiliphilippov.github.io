physical process

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈprɑːsɛs / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
a natural phenomenon involving gradual change or movement in physical matter or energy, often observable in nature or experiments
natural phenomenon involving physical change

Examples

Photosynthesis is a physical process essential for plant growth.
The melting of ice into water is a common physical process that we observe in daily life.
Gravity-driven erosion is a physical process that shapes landscapes over time.
