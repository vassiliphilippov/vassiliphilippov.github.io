west coast hemlock

1. NOUN

/ wɛst koʊst ˈhɛm.lɑk / (AmE), / wɛst kəʊst ˈhɛm.lɒk / (BrE)
A tall evergreen tree native to western North America, valued for its timber.
evergreen timber tree of western North America

Examples

Lumber from the west coast hemlock is prized for its strength and versatility.
The dense forests of British Columbia are home to many west coast hemlock trees.
Carpenters use west coast hemlock for both construction and fine woodworking projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hemlock - a type of tree or poisonous plant
