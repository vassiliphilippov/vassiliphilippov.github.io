to sound fishy

1. VERB

/ saʊnd ˈfɪʃi / (AmE), / saʊnd ˈfɪʃi / (BrE)
to seem suspicious or untrustworthy
seem suspicious or dubious

Examples

That story about losing his wallet really sounds fishy to me.
If a deal sounds fishy, you should probably avoid it.
Her excuse sounded fishy, so the manager decided to look into it further.

Synset Examples

Her explanation sounded a bit fishy to me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seem suspicious - to appear untrustworthy or questionable
