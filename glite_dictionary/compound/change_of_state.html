change of state

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv steɪt / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv steɪt / (BrE)
the process in which something is transformed into something different in essential characteristics
the process of becoming something different

Examples

The change of state from solid to liquid is called melting.
A sudden change of state in the project’s approach led to better results.
The chemical reaction caused a change of state in the substance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transformation - the process of a thorough or dramatic change in form or appearance
