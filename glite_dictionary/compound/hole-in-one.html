hole-in-one

1. NOUN

/ ˌhoʊl ɪn ˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˌhəʊl ɪn ˈwʌn / (BrE)
A shot in golf that lands the ball in the hole with a single stroke from the tee.
golf shot completed in a single stroke

Examples

He was ecstatic when he scored a hole-in-one on the seventh hole.
Getting a hole-in-one is a rare achievement for any golfer.
The club celebrated her hole-in-one with a small ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ace - a shot in golf where the ball is holed in one stroke
