plant material

1. NOUN

/ plænt məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / plɑːnt məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
substance or matter derived from plants, such as leaves, stems, roots, or fibers
matter derived from plants

Examples

Compost is made by decomposing plant material, such as leaves and grass clippings.
Many animals use plant material to build their nests and shelters.
Scientists analyzed the plant material for traces of pesticides after the harvest.
