lamp house

1. NOUN

/ læmp haʊs / (AmE), / læmp haʊs / (BrE)
a compartment or enclosure that holds a lamp, especially in devices such as movie projectors or searchlights
enclosure for holding a lamp

Examples

The technician carefully opened the lamp house to replace the old bulb in the projector.
Dust can accumulate in the lamp house, so regular maintenance is necessary for optimal performance.
Early film projectors had a separate lamp house to protect operators from the intense heat.
