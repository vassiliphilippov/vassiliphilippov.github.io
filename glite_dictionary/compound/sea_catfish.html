sea catfish

1. NOUN

/ si ˈkætˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / siː ˈkatˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
any of various marine catfish, many of which are mouthbrooders and are not commonly used for food.
a type of marine catfish

Examples

The mangroves are home to a variety of species, including the sea catfish.
Fishermen often catch sea catfish accidentally in their nets.
Unlike their freshwater relatives, most sea catfish are not considered desirable for eating.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catfish - a type of freshwater or marine fish with whisker-like barbels
