fits and starts

1. NOUN

/ fɪts ənd stɑrts / (AmE), / fɪts ənd stɑːts / (BrE)
short periods of activity followed by periods of inactivity or pause; irregular bursts of action
irregular bursts of activity

Examples

The project moved forward in fits and starts rather than following a steady pace.
Her recovery continued in fits and starts, making it difficult to predict her progress.
They cleaned the house in fits and starts, never finishing a room all at once.

Synset Examples
