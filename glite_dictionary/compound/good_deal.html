good deal

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd diːl / (AmE), / ɡʊd diːl / (BrE)
a large quantity or great amount of something, often used with 'of'
large quantity; great amount

Examples

There's a good deal of work left to finish before the deadline.
He has shown a good deal of improvement this year.
She spent a good deal of time preparing for the interview.
