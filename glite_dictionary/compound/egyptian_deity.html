Egyptian deity

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈdʒɪpʃən ˈdiːəti / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒɪpʃən ˈdeɪɪti / (BrE)
a god or goddess worshipped by the ancient Egyptians
a god of ancient Egypt

Examples

The pharaoh claimed to be the living embodiment of an Egyptian deity.
Horus is a well-known Egyptian deity associated with the sky and kingship.
Artifacts depicting an Egyptian deity were uncovered in the tomb.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deity - a god or goddess
  • Synonym: Egyptian god - a male deity worshipped by the ancient Egyptians
  • Antonym: Greek deity - a deity worshipped in ancient Greece
