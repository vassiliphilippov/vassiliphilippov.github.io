fat content

1. NOUN

/ fæt ˈkɑn.tɛnt / (AmE), / fæt ˈkɒn.tɛnt / (BrE)
the amount of fat present in a particular food or substance, usually expressed as a percentage or quantity
amount of fat in food

Examples

Some yoghurts have a lower fat content than others.
Manufacturers are required to display the fat content on food packaging.
She checks the fat content of every product before buying it.

Synset Examples

She’s concerned about the fat content in her diet.
