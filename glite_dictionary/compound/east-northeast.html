east-northeast

1. NOUN

/ ˌist ˌnɔrθˈist / (AmE), / ˌiːst ˌnɔːθˈiːst / (BrE)
The compass point halfway between east and northeast, corresponding to 67.5° on a compass.
compass point between east and northeast

Examples

They sailed toward the east-northeast to avoid the storm.
The wind is coming from the east-northeast this morning.
The trail runs east-northeast across the hills.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: northeast - the compass point between north and east
  • Degree Higher: east - the direction of the compass to the right of north and to the left of south

Etymology

Formed from 'east' and 'northeast,' indicating its position between these two directions.
