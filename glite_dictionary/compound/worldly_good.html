worldly good

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːldli ɡʊd / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldli ɡʊd / (BrE)
a material possession, as opposed to something spiritual or intangible
material possession

Examples

Many people pursue worldly goods but neglect their inner happiness.
She gave away all her worldly goods and devoted herself to helping others.
The will stated how his worldly goods should be distributed among his children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: possession - something that is owned or possessed
  • Antonym: spiritual good - something associated with spiritual or religious benefit, as opposed to material benefit
