nuclear club

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuː.kli.ɚ klʌb / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.klɪ.ə klʌb / (BrE)
The group of nations that possess nuclear weapons.
countries possessing nuclear weapons

Examples

The five permanent members of the UN Security Council are all part of the nuclear club.
India and Pakistan joined the nuclear club in the late twentieth century.
Access to advanced technology is often restricted to members of the nuclear club.
