crossback design

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɔsˌbæk dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsbæk dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
a style in which straps or bands cross each other at the back, commonly found in clothing such as dresses, tops, or swimsuits
a design with straps crossing at the back

Examples

The new sports bra uses a crossback design for added support during workouts.
Many evening gowns incorporate a crossback design to create a striking visual effect.
She chose a swimsuit with a crossback design because it provided a comfortable and secure fit.

Synset Examples

The dress featured an elegant crossback design that caught everyone's attention.
