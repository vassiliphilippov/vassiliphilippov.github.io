stamp collection

1. NOUN

/ stæmp kəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / stæmp kəˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
A collection of postage stamps, often assembled and studied as a hobby.
a collection of postage stamps

Examples

My grandfather's stamp collection includes rare stamps from over fifty countries.
She spent years building her impressive stamp collection before donating it to the museum.
Children and adults alike enjoy adding new finds to their stamp collection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: philately - the collection and study of stamps and postal history

/ stæmp kəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / stæmp kəˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)

group of collected postage stamps

Examples

Many children start a stamp collection to learn about geography and history.
