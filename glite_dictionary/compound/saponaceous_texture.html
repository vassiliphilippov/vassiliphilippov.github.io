saponaceous texture

1. NOUN

/ ˌsæpəˈneɪʃəs ˈtɛkstʃɚ / (AmE), / ˌsæpəˈneɪʃəs ˈtɛkstʃə / (BrE)
A texture that is smooth, slippery, or feels like soap.
soap-like feel or texture

Examples

The lotion had a saponaceous texture that made her skin feel silky and clean.
He described the clay sample’s saponaceous texture in his geology report.
The chef avoided ingredients with a saponaceous texture when making the sauce.

Synset Examples

She loved the saponaceous texture of the soap.

Etymology

From Latin 'sapo' (soap) + '-aceous' (having the nature of).
