Liberal Party

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪbərəl ˌpɑːrti / (AmE), / ˈlɪbərəl ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
a political party found in Australia, Canada, and other countries, and formerly in Great Britain, typically supporting liberal principles such as individual rights and free markets
political party supporting liberal policies

Examples

The Liberal Party has held power in Australia for several years at different times.
Many Canadians support the Liberal Party because of its progressive policies.
The Liberal Party in Great Britain was replaced by the Liberal Democrats.
