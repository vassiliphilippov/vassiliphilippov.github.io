stupid o'clock

1. NOUN

/ ˈstuːpɪd əˈklɑk / (AmE), / ˈstjuːpɪd əˈklɒk / (BrE)
an unreasonably early or inconvenient time, especially when one is forced to be awake or active
very early or inconvenient time
informal

Examples

I had to catch a flight at stupid o'clock this morning and barely got any sleep.
She called me at stupid o'clock just to chat about her new job.
We arrived at the airport at stupid o'clock because of the early departure time.

Synset Examples

I hate waking up at stupid o'clock on weekends.
