hotel desk clerk

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈtɛl dɛsk klɜrk / (AmE), / həʊˈtɛl dɛsk klɑːk / (BrE)
a person whose job is to assist guests at the front desk of a hotel, handling check-ins, reservations, and other guest services
person assisting guests at hotel front desk

Examples

The hotel desk clerk greeted us with a smile when we arrived late at night.
If you need extra towels, just ask the hotel desk clerk at the front desk.
As a hotel desk clerk, she managed reservations and helped guests with their requests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: receptionist - person employed to greet visitors and answer inquiries at a business
