prime location

1. NOUN

/ praɪm loʊˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / praɪm ləʊˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a place considered highly desirable or advantageous, especially for business or living purposes
highly desirable place

Examples

The new restaurant opened in a prime location near the city center.
She was lucky to find an apartment in such a prime location at an affordable price.
They paid extra to secure a prime location for their booth at the trade show.

Synset Examples

I decided to put my store in a prime location.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desirable location - a place seen as attractive or advantageous
