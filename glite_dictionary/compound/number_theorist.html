number theorist

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌmbɚ ˌθiərɪst / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbə ˌθɪə.rɪst / (BrE)
a mathematician who specializes in the study of number theory, the branch of mathematics concerned with properties of integers.
mathematician specializing in number theory

Examples

As a number theorist, she spent years studying the properties of prime numbers.
The conference invited a renowned number theorist to discuss his latest findings.
Many number theorists have contributed to solving Fermat's Last Theorem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mathematician - an expert or student in mathematics
  • Synonym: number theory - the branch of mathematics dealing with integers
