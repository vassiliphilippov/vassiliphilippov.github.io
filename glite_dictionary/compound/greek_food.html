Greek food

1. NOUN

/ ɡrik fud / (AmE), / ɡriːk fuːd / (BrE)
the traditional cuisine, dishes, and cooking styles originating from Greece
traditional cuisine of Greece

Examples

Whenever we go out, Sarah always wants to try Greek food because she loves the flavors.
He researched how to cook authentic Greek food before hosting his dinner party.
The festival featured music, dancing, and a variety of delicious Greek food for everyone to enjoy.

Synset Examples

She loves Greek food, especially moussaka and tzatziki.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Greek cuisine - the cooking traditions and typical foods of Greece
