crucial ingredient

1. NOUN

/ ˈkruːʃəl ɪnˈɡriːdiənt / (AmE), / ˈkruːʃəl ɪnˈɡriːdiənt / (BrE)
An essential component or element needed for something to succeed or function properly.
essential component or element

Examples

Patience is a crucial ingredient in successful teaching.
Trust is the crucial ingredient in any strong relationship.
Innovation has become a crucial ingredient for modern businesses to thrive.

Synset Examples

Salt is a crucial ingredient in this recipe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: essential ingredient - a component that is absolutely necessary
  • Antonym: optional ingredient - a component that is not required
