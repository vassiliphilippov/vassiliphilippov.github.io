booger brain

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːɡər breɪn / (AmE), / ˈbʊɡə breɪn / (BrE)
A foolish or silly person; used as an insult, often playfully or humorously.
silly or foolish person (insult, humorous)
informal slang american

Examples

Stop being such a booger brain and pay attention to what I'm saying.
She laughed and called her brother a booger brain when he tripped over his own feet.
I can't believe I forgot my keys again—what a booger brain moment!

Synset Examples

Don't be a booger brain; let's work together.
He called his friend a booger brain for forgetting the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blockhead - a very stupid person
  • Synonym: dummy - a person who is not intelligent
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence

Etymology

From 'booger' (slang for nasal mucus, also used as a mild insult) and 'brain,' used humorously to suggest someone has a brain made of boogers.
