breezy attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrizi ˌætɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈbriːzi ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
a relaxed, cheerful, and unconcerned manner or demeanor
relaxed and carefree manner

Examples

Even under pressure, she maintained a breezy attitude that put everyone at ease.
His breezy attitude often made him popular at social gatherings.
Managers appreciated her breezy attitude in the face of tight deadlines.

Synset Examples

He had a breezy attitude about the whole situation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carefree attitude - having no worries or anxieties
  • Antonym: serious attitude - a manner that is earnest or not light-hearted
