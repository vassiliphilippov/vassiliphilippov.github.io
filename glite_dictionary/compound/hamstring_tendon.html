hamstring tendon

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæmˌstrɪŋ ˈtɛn.dən / (AmE), / ˈham.strɪŋ ˈtɛn.dən / (BrE)
one of the strong tendons at the back of the knee, connecting the hamstring muscles to the bones of the leg
tendon at the back of the knee
medical

Examples

He tore his hamstring tendon while sprinting during the match.
Physical therapists focus on strengthening the hamstring tendon after knee injuries.
An MRI revealed inflammation in her hamstring tendon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tendon - a flexible but inelastic cord of strong fibrous collagen tissue connecting a muscle to a bone
