halal certified

1. ADJ

/ həˈlɑl ˈsɝː.tɪ.faɪd / (AmE), / həˈlɑːl ˈsɜː.tɪ.faɪd / (BrE)
officially approved as conforming to Islamic dietary laws, especially in reference to food and drink
approved as meeting Islamic dietary laws

Examples

The company only serves halal certified meat to its customers.
Many airlines now offer halal certified meals on international flights.
All products labeled as halal certified have been inspected by a qualified authority.

Synset Examples

He ensures all his meals are halal certified.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kosher certified - approved as meeting Jewish dietary laws

Etymology

From Arabic 'halal' (permissible) and English 'certified' (officially recognized).
