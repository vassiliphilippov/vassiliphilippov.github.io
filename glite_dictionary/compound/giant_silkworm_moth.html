giant silkworm moth

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt ˈsɪlkˌwɝm mɔθ / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈsɪlkˌwɜːm mɒθ / (BrE)
Any large moth of the family Saturniidae, known for their impressive size and silk-producing larvae.
large moth of family Saturniidae

Examples

The giant silkworm moth is prized for its beautiful, intricate wing patterns.
Several species of giant silkworm moth inhabit the forests of South America and Asia.
Entomologists study the life cycles of the giant silkworm moth to better understand silk production.
