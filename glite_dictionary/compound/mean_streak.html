mean streak

1. NOUN

/ min strik / (AmE), / miːn striːk / (BrE)
a noticeable tendency in someone's character to be unkind, cruel, or harsh
tendency to be unkind or harsh

Examples

Everyone liked Tom, but they warned me about his mean streak when he lost his temper.
Even the friendliest person can show a mean streak under extreme stress.
Her mean streak comes out when she's feeling threatened.

Synset Examples

He has a mean streak that surfaces when he's frustrated.
His competitive nature reveals a mean streak during games.
Despite her kindness, a mean streak occasionally shows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cruel streak - a tendency to be cruel
  • Antonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, or considerate; the opposite of cruelty
