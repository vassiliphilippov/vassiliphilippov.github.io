pair of tweezers

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛr əv ˈtwizərz / (AmE), / ˈpeə əv ˈtwiːzəz / (BrE)
a small hand tool with two narrow arms joined at one end, used for picking up small objects by pinching them together
hand tool for grasping small objects

Examples

She used a pair of tweezers to remove the splinter from his finger.
Please hand me a pair of tweezers so I can pick up the tiny screw.
A pair of tweezers is essential for any first aid kit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tweezers - small hand tool for picking up small objects

2. NOUN

/ ˈpɛr əv twiːzərz / (AmE), / ˈpeə əv ˈtwiːzəz / (BrE)

tool for picking up small objects

Examples

A pair of tweezers is essential in any first aid kit.
He carefully picked up the tiny screw using a pair of tweezers.
