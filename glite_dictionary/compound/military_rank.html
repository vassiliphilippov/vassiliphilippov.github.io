military rank

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪləˌtɛri ræŋk / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ræŋk / (BrE)
a position or level of authority in a military organization, determined by official system of hierarchical grades
position in a military hierarchy

Examples

He achieved the military rank of colonel before retiring.
Advancement to a higher military rank requires both experience and leadership skills.
Her father held a senior military rank in the navy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rank - position or grade within an organization or society
