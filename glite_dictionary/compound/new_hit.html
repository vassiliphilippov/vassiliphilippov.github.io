new hit

1. NOUN

/ nuː hɪt / (AmE), / njuː hɪt / (BrE)
a song or other work that has recently become very popular or successful
recently popular song or work

Examples

The band's new hit quickly climbed to the top of the charts.
Fans can't stop streaming her new hit single on every platform.
Everyone at the party was dancing to that new hit you played.

Synset Examples

The rapper performed his new hit at the concert last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hit - a successful or popular song
