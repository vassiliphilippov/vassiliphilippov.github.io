pill pusher

1. NOUN

/ pɪl ˈpʊʃɚ / (AmE), / pɪl ˈpʊʃə / (BrE)
an informal or sometimes derogatory term for a healthcare professional, especially a pharmacist or doctor, who dispenses or prescribes medications
informal term for a drug-dispensing professional
informal slang

Examples

He didn't want to be seen as just a pill pusher who hands out prescriptions without caring about his patients.
Some people refer to their pharmacists as pill pushers, but that's not really fair.
As a doctor, she found the label pill pusher insulting to her years of medical training.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pharmacist - a person qualified to prepare and dispense medicinal drugs
  • Synonym: doctor - a qualified practitioner of medicine

2. NOUN

/ ˈpɪl ˌpʊʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɪl ˌpʊʃə / (BrE)
a person who promotes or sells drugs, especially unethically or illegally
person who unethically sells drugs
informal offensive

Examples

The doctor warned his patients to stay away from any pill pusher promising miracle cures.
Authorities arrested the suspected pill pusher for distributing prescription medications without a license.
She was accused of being a pill pusher after several patients became addicted to painkillers.

Synset Examples
