spindleberry tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪndlˌbɛri tri / (AmE), / ˈspɪndlˌbɛri triː / (BrE)
A shrubby tree or woody vine of the genus Euonymus, noted for its showy, usually reddish berries.
shrub/tree with showy reddish berries (Euonymus genus)

Examples

The garden was planted with a spindleberry tree whose red berries brightened the winter.
Birds are attracted to the bright fruit of the spindleberry tree every autumn.
A mature spindleberry tree can add both color and texture to a landscape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spindle tree - a shrub or small tree of the genus Euonymus
