wall rue spleenwort

1. NOUN

/ wɔl ruː ˈspliːnwɜrt / (AmE), / wɔːl ruː ˈspliːnwɜːt / (BrE)
a small, delicate spleenwort fern that grows on steep slopes, walls, or cliffs in Eurasia and North America
a spleenwort fern on walls or cliffs

Examples

The old stone bridge was covered in wall rue spleenwort and other mosses.
Hikers spotted rare wall rue spleenwort growing on the cliff face.
The wall rue spleenwort prefers shady, rocky habitats in temperate regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spleenwort - a type of fern belonging to the genus Asplenium
