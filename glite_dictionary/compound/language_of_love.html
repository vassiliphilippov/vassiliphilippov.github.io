language of love

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ əv lʌv / (AmE), / ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ əv lʌv / (BrE)
words, gestures, or actions used to express romantic feelings or affection
romantic forms of expression

Examples

They wrote poems in the language of love to capture their deepest emotions.
Music is often called the language of love because it conveys feelings words cannot express.
The bouquet of roses was his way of speaking the language of love.

Synset Examples

The language of love is universal and timeless.
