forest fire fighter

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrɪst ˈfaɪɚ ˈfaɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɒrɪst ˈfaɪə ˈfaɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to control and extinguish fires in forests and to protect forested areas from fire damage
person who fights fires in forests

Examples

The forest fire fighter bravely battled the blaze to prevent it from spreading to nearby towns.
As a forest fire fighter, her work often involved days in remote wilderness areas controlling wildfires.
The team of forest fire fighters received special training to handle emergencies in rugged terrain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firefighter - a person whose job is to put out fires
