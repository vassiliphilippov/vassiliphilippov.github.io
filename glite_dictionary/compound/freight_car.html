freight car

1. NOUN

/ ˈfreɪt kɑr / (AmE), / ˈfreɪt kɑː / (BrE)
a railroad vehicle designed for carrying goods rather than passengers
rail car for carrying goods

Examples

The freight car was loaded with crates of produce bound for the city.
A long train of freight cars rumbled slowly through the countryside at dawn.
Workers carefully inspected each freight car before departure to ensure safety regulations were met.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boxcar - an enclosed railway freight car
  • Antonym: passenger car - railroad car designed for carrying passengers
