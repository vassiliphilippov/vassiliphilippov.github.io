purple fringeless orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl ˈfrɪndʒləs ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈfrɪndʒlɪs ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A North American orchid species closely related to the purple fringed orchids, with rosy-purple or violet flowers and finely toothed leaf divisions.
North American orchid with violet flowers

Examples

We spotted a rare purple fringeless orchid while hiking in the wet meadows of Vermont.
The vibrant flowers of the purple fringeless orchid attract many pollinators each summer.
Botanists are working to conserve the endangered purple fringeless orchid in its native habitats.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: purple fringed orchid - a related orchid species with fringed petals
