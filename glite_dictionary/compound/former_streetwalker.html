former streetwalker

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrmər ˈstritˌwɔkər / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmə ˈstriːtwɔːkə / (BrE)
A person who previously worked as a streetwalker (prostitute).
person who was a streetwalker

Examples

She now works as a counselor, helping other former streetwalkers rebuild their lives.
The novel tells the story of a former streetwalker struggling to find acceptance in society.
The charity provides support to former streetwalkers who want to start new careers.

Synset Examples

The documentary highlighted the struggles of a former streetwalker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: streetwalker - a prostitute who solicits customers on the street
