genus Majorana

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˌmædʒəˈreɪnə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌmadʒəˈrɑːnə / (BrE)
a small genus of herbs, usually included within the genus Origanum, that includes sweet marjoram.
botanical genus including marjoram

Examples

The genus Majorana is often considered synonymous with Origanum by some botanists.
Her research focused on the medicinal properties of plants in the genus Majorana.
Sweet marjoram is classified within the genus Majorana in some botanical texts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Origanum - a plant genus that includes oregano and marjoram

Etymology

From Latin 'Majorana', the genus name, possibly ultimately from Arabic 'mardakūsh' (marjoram).
