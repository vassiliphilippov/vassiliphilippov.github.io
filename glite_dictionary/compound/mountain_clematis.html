mountain clematis

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntən ˈklɛm.ə.tɪs / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntən ˈklɛm.ətɪs / (BrE)
A climbing plant native to northeastern North America, noted for its waxy purplish-blue flowers.
North American climbing plant with purplish-blue flowers

Examples

The mountain clematis blooms beautifully along the forest edges every spring.
Gardeners often plant mountain clematis for its striking purplish-blue flowers and vigorous growth.
We spotted a wild mountain clematis climbing over the fence near the hiking trail.
