United States dollar

1. NOUN

/ ˌjuːnɪtɪd ˈsteɪts ˈdɑːlər / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪtɪd ˈsteɪts ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
the official unit of currency in the United States, equal to 100 cents
US currency unit

Examples

The price of the book is fifteen United States dollars.
He exchanged euros for United States dollars before his trip to New York.
Many international transactions are conducted in United States dollars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dollar - the basic unit of money in several countries, including the US
