gig-mill

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪɡ mɪl / (AmE), / ɡɪɡ mɪl / (BrE)
a textile mill that uses rotary wire cylinders for raising the nap on fabrics
textile mill for napping fabrics

Examples

The old town's economy once revolved around the large gig-mill by the river.
After touring the gig-mill, we learned how fabrics were given a softer texture.
Technological advances eventually made the gig-mill obsolete in modern textile production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: napping mill - a mill where fabrics are napped to raise the surface fibers
