dancing dogcow

1. NOUN

/ ˈdænsɪŋ ˈdɔgˌkaʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnsɪŋ ˈdɒgˌkaʊ / (BrE)
A fictional creature that combines features of a dog and a cow, often depicted humorously, especially in technology culture.
fictional dog-cow creature, humorous

Examples

The old Mac software featured a small dancing dogcow in the print preview window.
At the tech conference, someone wore a costume of the dancing dogcow.
That meme reminded me of the dancing dogcow from classic computer icons.

Synset Examples

I saw a funny video featuring a dancing dogcow.

Etymology

Coined by Apple developers in the 1980s, combining 'dog' and 'cow', with 'dogcow' first appearing in Macintosh software.
