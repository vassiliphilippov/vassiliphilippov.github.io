heroic poem

1. NOUN

/ hɪˈroʊɪk ˈpoʊəm / (AmE), / hɪˈrəʊɪk ˈpəʊəm / (BrE)
a long narrative poem that tells the deeds of a legendary or historical hero
long narrative poem about a hero's deeds

Examples

Homer's 'Iliad' is a classic example of a heroic poem from ancient Greece.
The heroic poem celebrated the bravery of its central character during wartime.
Students studied the structure and themes of the traditional heroic poem in literature class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: epic - a long poem about heroic deeds
  • Antonym: ode - a lyric poem with elaborate structure
