vegan cuisine

1. NOUN

/ ˈviːɡən kwɪˈzin / (AmE), / ˈviːɡən kwɪˈziːn / (BrE)
food that is prepared and cooked without any animal products, including meat, dairy, eggs, or honey.
animal-product-free style of food

Examples

The restaurant is famous for its innovative vegan cuisine, which attracts both vegans and non-vegans alike.
She learned to prepare vegan cuisine while living abroad and now teaches classes in her hometown.
More people are choosing vegan cuisine for environmental and health reasons.

Synset Examples

They opened a new joint specializing in vegan cuisine.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: vegetarian cuisine - food prepared without meat or fish, but may include dairy and eggs

Etymology

‘Vegan’ from English ‘vegan’ (coined in 1944 from 'vegetarian'), combined with French ‘cuisine’ (kitchen, style of cooking).
