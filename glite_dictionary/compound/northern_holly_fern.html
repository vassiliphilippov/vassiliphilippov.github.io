northern holly fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðɚn ˈhɑli fɝn / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈhɒli fɜːn / (BrE)
an evergreen fern native to Europe, commonly grown as an ornamental plant
evergreen European ornamental fern

Examples

The northern holly fern is favored by gardeners for its glossy, holly-like leaves.
You can find the northern holly fern thriving in shaded woodland gardens across Europe.
Many people grow the northern holly fern indoors as a decorative plant.
