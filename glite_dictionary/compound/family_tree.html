family tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli tri / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli triː / (BrE)
a diagram showing the relationships and lines of descent among members of a family over several generations
diagram of family relationships

Examples

He created a family tree tracing his ancestors back to the 18th century.
The family tree revealed surprising connections between the two families.
She filled in the names on her family tree for her history project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genealogy - the study or investigation of ancestry and family history

2. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈfæməli ˌtriː / (BrE)
a diagram that shows the relationships between different members of a family over several generations
diagram of family relationships

Examples

The children made a family tree for their school project.
After researching her ancestry, Lisa updated her family tree to include her great-great-grandparents.
A family tree can help you understand your heritage and history.

Synset Examples

Tracing our family tree revealed a grandcestor from the 18th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genealogy - the study of family histories and lineages
