offensive slur

1. NOUN

/ əˈfɛnsɪv slɝː / (AmE), / əˈfɛnsɪv slɜː / (BrE)
a word or phrase used to insult or demean a particular group, especially on the basis of race, ethnicity, or other identity
derogatory insult targeting a group
offensive

Examples

Using an offensive slur can cause serious harm and be deeply hurtful to others.
The company has a strict policy against any use of an offensive slur in the workplace.
She was shocked when she overheard someone use an offensive slur during the meeting.

Synset Examples

The term 'wop' is considered an offensive slur.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insult - an offensive remark or action
  • Synonym: pejorative - a word or expression that expresses contempt or disapproval
