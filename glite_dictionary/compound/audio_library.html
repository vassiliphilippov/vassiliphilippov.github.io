audio library

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔdiˌoʊ ˈlaɪˌbrɛri / (AmE), / ˈɔːdiəʊ ˈlaɪbrəri / (BrE)
a collection of sound recordings stored for reference, playback, or distribution
collection of sound recordings

Examples

The university's audio library contains thousands of rare music recordings.
She built an extensive audio library of natural soundscapes for her research.
The company offers an online audio library for podcast creators.

Synset Examples

This is my own audio library.
