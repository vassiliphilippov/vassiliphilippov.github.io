high-stakes affair

1. NOUN

/ haɪ steɪks əˈfɛr / (AmE), / haɪ steɪks əˈfeə / (BrE)
a situation or event involving considerable risk, consequence, or importance for those involved
important or risky situation or event

Examples

The merger became a high-stakes affair as millions of dollars were at risk.
His testimony turned the trial into a high-stakes affair watched by the entire nation.
Launching the new product was a high-stakes affair for the struggling company.

Synset Examples

The negotiation was a high-stakes affair for both sides.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high-stakes - involving a high level of risk or importance
