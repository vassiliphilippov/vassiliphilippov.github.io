fringed gentian

1. NOUN

/ frɪndʒd ˈdʒɛnʃən / (AmE), / frɪndʒd ˈdʒɛnʃən / (BrE)
a North American wildflower of the genus Gentianopsis, with blue, lacy-margined petals
blue wildflower with fringed petals

Examples

The fringed gentian blooms in late summer along meadows and stream banks.
Poets often praise the delicate blue petals of the fringed gentian.
We found a cluster of fringed gentians growing wild in the field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gentian - any plant of the genus Gentiana, noted for bright blue flowers
