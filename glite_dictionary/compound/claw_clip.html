claw clip

1. NOUN

/ klɔː klɪp / (AmE), / klɔː klɪp / (BrE)
a hair accessory featuring two interlocking rows of teeth connected by a spring, used to hold hair in place.
hair accessory with interlocking teeth

Examples

She secured her hair with a claw clip before starting her workout.
I bought a new claw clip in a bright pink color.
He noticed her claw clip matched the pattern on her dress.
