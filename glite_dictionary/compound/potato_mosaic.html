potato mosaic

1. NOUN

/ pəˈteɪɾoʊ məˈseɪɪk / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ məˈseɪɪk / (BrE)
a viral disease affecting potato plants, characterized by mottled or mosaic-like discolouration of the leaves
viral disease in potato plants

Examples

The spread of potato mosaic can significantly reduce crop yields if not managed properly.
Farmers inspected their fields for signs of potato mosaic after noticing discoloured patches on the leaves.
Researchers are developing resistant varieties to combat potato mosaic and protect harvests.
