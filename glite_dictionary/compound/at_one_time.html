at one time

1. ADV

/ æt wʌn taɪm / (AmE), / ət wʌn taɪm / (BrE)
simultaneously; at the same moment or period.
simultaneously, at the same moment

Examples

All the lights in the building turned off at one time, leaving everyone in darkness.
He tried to answer all the questions at one time, but quickly became overwhelmed.
It’s difficult to focus on several tasks at one time.

Synset Examples

he took three cookies at a time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at the same time - simultaneously; during the same period
