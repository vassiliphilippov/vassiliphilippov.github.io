party boss

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːrti bɔs / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti bɒs / (BrE)
a powerful leader in a political party who controls votes and dictates appointments, often wielding significant influence behind the scenes
leader who controls a political party

Examples

The city's party boss secretly chose which candidates would run for office.
During the 20th century, several party bosses shaped local governments through patronage.
Accusations of corruption often surrounded the actions of the influential party boss.

Synset Examples

party bosses have a reputation for corruption

Related Terms

  • Synonym: political boss - a leader who controls a political party or organization
