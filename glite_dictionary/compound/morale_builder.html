morale builder

1. NOUN

/ məˈræl ˌbɪldər / (AmE), / məˈrɑːl ˌbɪldə / (BrE)
something that helps to improve or boost morale, especially in a group or workplace setting.
something that improves morale

Examples

The company retreat turned out to be a real morale builder for the employees.
Positive feedback from customers can act as an effective morale builder for any team.
Fresh coffee in the break room every morning has become a small but powerful morale builder for staff.

Synset Examples

the sight of flowers every morning was my morale builder

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incentive - something that encourages a person to do something or to work harder
  • Antonym: demotivator - something that decreases morale or motivation

2. NOUN

/ məˈræl ˌbɪldər / (AmE), / məˈrɑːl ˌbɪldə / (BrE)
a person or thing that helps increase confidence, enthusiasm, and a positive attitude within a group or team
person or thing that boosts morale

Examples

The coach acted as a real morale builder during the tough season.
Weekly recognition of achievements serves as a morale builder in the workplace.
Sometimes, a simple thank-you can be a powerful morale builder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motivator - a person or thing that motivates others
  • Antonym: demoralizer - a person or thing that causes loss of confidence or hope
