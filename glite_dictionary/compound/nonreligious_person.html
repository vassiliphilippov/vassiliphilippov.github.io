nonreligious person

1. NOUN

/ nɑn.rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈpɝː.sən / (AmE), / nɒn.rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈpɜː.sən / (BrE)
a person who does not follow or practice any religion or show religious devotion
person with no religious beliefs or practices

Examples

Many nonreligious person prefer to celebrate holidays in a secular way.
She identifies as a nonreligious person and does not attend any religious services.
As a nonreligious person, he finds meaning in philosophy and science.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atheist - a person who believes that God does not exist
  • Synonym: agnostic - a person who claims neither faith nor disbelief in God
