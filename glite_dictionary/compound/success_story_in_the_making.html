success story in the making

1. NOUN

/ səkˈsɛs ˌstɔri ɪn ðə ˈmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / səkˈsɛs ˌstɔːri ɪn ðə ˈmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
a developing person, project, or situation that is likely to become successful
something likely becoming a success

Examples

Many believe this small tech startup is a success story in the making.
Her steady progress shows she is clearly a success story in the making.
This environmental initiative could be a success story in the making if it continues to gain support.

Synset Examples

This project is a success story in the making.
