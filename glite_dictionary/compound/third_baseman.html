third baseman

1. NOUN

/ θɜrd ˈbeɪsmən / (AmE), / θɜːd ˈbeɪsmən / (BrE)
a player whose position is at third base in baseball, responsible for fielding near third base
baseball player at third base

Examples

The team's third baseman made several impressive plays during the game.
She trained all summer to become the starting third baseman for her college team.
The coach praised the third baseman for his quick reflexes and strong arm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infielder - a baseball player who plays near the infield
  • Degree Lower: first baseman - baseball player at first base
  • Degree Higher: shortstop - baseball player between second and third base
