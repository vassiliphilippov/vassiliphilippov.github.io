metal wood

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛt̬l wʊd / (AmE), / ˈmɛtl wʊd / (BrE)
A golf club with a head made of metal instead of the traditional wooden head, designed for long-distance shots.
golf club with metal head

Examples

Many professional golfers prefer using a metal wood for their tee shots on long holes.
She replaced her old driver with a new titanium metal wood that improved her distance.
The store carries the latest metal woods from all the top golf brands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wood - a type of golf club traditionally made with a wooden head
  • Antonym: iron - a type of golf club with a flat metal head, distinct from a wood
