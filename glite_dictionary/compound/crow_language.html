Crow language

1. NOUN

/ kɹoʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / kɹəʊ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Siouan language spoken by the Crow people, primarily in Montana, USA
the language of the Crow people

Examples

Few young people today are fluent in the Crow language.
Linguists have documented the grammar and vocabulary of the Crow language.
A revitalization program has been launched to teach children the Crow language in schools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Siouan - family of Native American languages including Crow

Etymology

Named after the Crow people, whose name in their own language is Apsáalooke; 'Crow' is an English translation.
