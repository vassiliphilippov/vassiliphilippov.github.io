built-soap powder

1. NOUN

/ bɪlt soʊp ˈpaʊdɚ / (AmE), / bɪlt səʊp ˈpaʊdə / (BrE)
Powdered soap to which alkaline builders have been added to improve cleaning effectiveness.
powdered soap with added builders

Examples

Modern laundry products often contain built-soap powder to enhance stain removal.
The janitorial staff prefers built-soap powder for cleaning heavily soiled floors.
Environmental regulations have influenced the formulation of built-soap powder in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detergent - cleaning agent for removing dirt and stains
  • Degree Lower: soap powder - soap in powdered form
