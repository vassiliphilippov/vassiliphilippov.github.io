Hindu Kush

1. PROPN

/ ˌhɪnduː ˈkʊʃ / (AmE), / ˌhɪnduː ˈkʊʃ / (BrE)
a mountain range in Central and South Asia, extending west of the Himalayas, mainly in Afghanistan and Pakistan
mountain range west of the Himalayas

Examples

The Hindu Kush has historically served as a natural barrier between Central Asia and the Indian subcontinent.
Many rivers originate in the Hindu Kush before flowing into the plains below.
Trekking in the Hindu Kush offers spectacular views and challenging terrain.

Etymology

From Persian 'Hindū Kūh', meaning 'Hindu mountains'.
