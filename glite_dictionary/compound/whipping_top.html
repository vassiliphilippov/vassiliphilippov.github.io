whipping top

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪpɪŋ tɑp / (AmE), / ˈwɪpɪŋ tɒp / (BrE)
A child's toy, shaped like a top, that is spun by striking it with a whip or cord.
toy top spun by whipping

Examples

He spent the afternoon spinning his whipping top in the playground.
The old-fashioned whipping top requires skill to keep it moving with the whip.
Children competed to see whose whipping top would spin the longest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top - a spinning toy that rotates on an axis
