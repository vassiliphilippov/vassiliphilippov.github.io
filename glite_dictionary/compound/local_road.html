local road

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.kəl roʊd / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl rəʊd / (BrE)
a street or road intended mainly for access to properties adjacent to it rather than for through traffic
road mainly for local access

Examples

There is little traffic on our local road, making it safe for children to play outside.
The city plans to repair several local roads damaged by the winter storms.
Delivery trucks often use local roads to reach individual homes and businesses.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: arterial road - a major road for through traffic
  • Synonym: residential street - a street in a neighborhood with houses and little through traffic
