sphincter ani

1. NOUN

/ ˈsfɪŋktər ˈænaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsfɪŋktə ˈani / (BrE)
the ring-shaped muscle surrounding and controlling the opening of the anus
muscle controlling the anus
medical

Examples

The sphincter ani plays a crucial role in controlling bowel movements.
Damage to the sphincter ani can result in problems with continence.
The doctor explained how the sphincter ani functions during defecation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anal sphincter - the muscle surrounding the anus, controlling its opening and closing

Etymology

From Latin 'sphincter ani', where 'ani' is the genitive of 'anus', meaning 'of the anus'.
