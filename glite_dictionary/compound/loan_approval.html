loan approval

1. NOUN

/ loʊn əˈpruvəl / (AmE), / ləʊn əˈpruːvəl / (BrE)
formal permission granted by a lender, especially a bank, to borrow a specific amount of money
official permission to receive a loan

Examples

The bank's loan approval process can take several weeks to complete.
After receiving loan approval, she was able to purchase her first home.
Many students apply for financial aid, but not all receive loan approval.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loan authorization - official permission to borrow money

2. NOUN

/ ˈloʊn əˌpruvəl / (AmE), / ˈləʊn əˌpruːvəl / (BrE)
the official process by which a lender decides to grant a loan to an applicant
decision to grant a loan

Examples

The bank’s loan approval process took several weeks to complete.
After receiving loan approval, she started searching for her new house.
Delays in loan approval can affect a company’s ability to invest.

Synset Examples

The bank required a hypothec for the loan approval.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loan application - the process or form used to apply for a loan
  • Antonym: loan denial - the refusal to grant a requested loan
