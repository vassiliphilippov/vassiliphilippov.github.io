laughing tackle

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæfɪŋ ˌtækəl / (AmE), / ˈlɑːfɪŋ ˌtækəl / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly British) a person's distinctive laugh, or their humorous way of expressing themselves, especially one that others find enjoyable or infectious.
distinctive or infectious laugh (informal, British)
