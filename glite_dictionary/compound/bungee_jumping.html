bungee jumping

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌndʒi ˌdʒʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʌndʒi ˌdʒʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of jumping from a high place while attached to an elastic cord, which prevents you from hitting the ground
jumping from a height with an elastic cord

Examples

He decided to try bungee jumping on his trip to New Zealand.
Many people find bungee jumping thrilling but terrifying at the same time.
Safety is the top priority when organizing bungee jumping events.

Synset Examples

You want to try bungee jumping? Go on then!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skydiving - the sport of jumping from an aircraft and free-falling before opening a parachute
  • Synonym: paragliding - the sport of gliding through the air with a fabric wing

Etymology

From 'bungee' (elasticky cord of uncertain origin) + 'jumping' (from jump).
