integrated circuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.təˌɡreɪ.tɪd ˈsɜːr.kɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tɪˌɡreɪ.tɪd ˈsɜː.kɪt / (BrE)
a small electronic device consisting of a set of interconnected electronic components formed on a single semiconductor chip, performing the functions of a whole circuit
microelectronic chip with integrated components

Examples

The development of the integrated circuit revolutionized modern electronics in the 20th century.
Most smartphones contain dozens of different integrated circuits to control their various functions.
The students studied how an integrated circuit could replace several discrete electronic components.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microchip - a small semiconductor used to relay information in electronic devices
