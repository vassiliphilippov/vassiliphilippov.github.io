yellow-spotted

1. ADJ

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˌspɑt̬ɪd / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˌspɒtɪd / (BrE)
Marked with or having yellow spots on its surface.
having yellow spots

Examples

The yellow-spotted frog is known for its distinctive markings.
We discovered a yellow-spotted beetle in the garden.
Her favorite fish was the small, yellow-spotted variety that swam near the rocks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotted - having spots or marks
