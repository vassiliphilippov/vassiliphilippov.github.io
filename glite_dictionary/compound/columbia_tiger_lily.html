Columbia tiger lily

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlʌmbiə ˈtaɪɡər ˈlɪli / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbiə ˈtaɪɡə ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A species of lily native to western North America, with striking orange-red flowers marked with purple spots.
species of lily with spotted orange-red flowers

Examples

The Columbia tiger lily is often found growing wild in the mountains of the Pacific Northwest.
Gardeners prize the Columbia tiger lily for its vibrant and unique flowers.
During our hike, we spotted several Columbia tiger lilies blooming along the trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lily - flowering plant of the genus Lilium
