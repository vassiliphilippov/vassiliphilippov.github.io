old-maid's bonnet

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊld ˈmeɪdz ˌbɑnɪt / (AmE), / ˌəʊld ˈmeɪdz ˌbɒnɪt / (BrE)
A stout perennial plant native to eastern and central North America, with palmate leaves and striking racemes of blue flowers.
North American perennial with blue flowers

Examples

We spotted an old-maid's bonnet growing by the woodland trail.
The vibrant blue flowers of the old-maid's bonnet attract many native pollinators.
Gardeners value the old-maid's bonnet for its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
