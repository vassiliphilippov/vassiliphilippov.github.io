table napkin

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəl ˈnæpkɪn / (AmE), / ˈteɪbl ˈnap.kɪn / (BrE)
A small piece of cloth or paper used at a meal to wipe the mouth and fingers and to protect clothing.
piece of cloth or paper for use at meals

Examples

She placed a table napkin on her lap before starting to eat.
At formal dinners, cloth table napkins are often neatly folded.
He dabbed his mouth with the table napkin after sipping the soup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: napkin - a piece of cloth or paper used at meals
