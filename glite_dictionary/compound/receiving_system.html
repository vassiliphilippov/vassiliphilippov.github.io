receiving system

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsivɪŋ ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / rɪˈsiːvɪŋ ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a set of equipment designed to receive radio or television signals
equipment for receiving radio or TV signals

Examples

The engineer tested the receiving system to ensure it was picking up all available channels.
Storms can sometimes interfere with a satellite receiving system and disrupt the signal.
Modern homes often have a digital receiving system installed for better television reception.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: receiver - device that receives radio or TV signals
