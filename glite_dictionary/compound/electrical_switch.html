electrical switch

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl swɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl swɪtʃ / (BrE)
a device for making, breaking, or changing the connections in an electrical circuit
device for controlling an electrical circuit

Examples

Please turn off the lights by flipping the electrical switch near the door.
The electrical switch was faulty, causing the power to go out unexpectedly.
Modern homes often include a smart electrical switch that can be controlled remotely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: switch - a device for making or breaking a connection in an electric circuit
