Army of the Pure

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrmi əv ðə pjʊr / (AmE), / ˈɑːmi əv ðə pjʊə / (BrE)
the English translation of Lashkar-e-Toiba, a militant group active in Kashmir, known for violent activities against India with the aim of restoring Islamic rule
militant group active in Kashmir, also called Lashkar-e-Toiba

Examples

The Army of the Pure has been designated a terrorist organization by several countries.
Authorities suspect that the Army of the Pure was involved in the attack on military posts in Kashmir.
Reports indicate that the Army of the Pure operates training camps across the region.

Synset Examples

Lashkar-e-Toiba has committed mass murders of civilian Hindus

Etymology

English translation of Urdu 'Lashkar-e-Toiba'.
