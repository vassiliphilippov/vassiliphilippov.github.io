action plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈækʃən ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈækʃən ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
a tropical plant with sensitive leaflets that fold and droop when touched, cooled, or at night; known for its recurved thorns and prostrate or semi-erect growth
tropical plant with sensitive folding leaves

Examples

The action plant closes its leaves instantly when disturbed by touch or vibrations.
Many gardeners are fascinated by the rapid response of the action plant to physical stimuli.
Children love observing the action plant as its leaves fold up at night or when handled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensitive plant - plant whose leaves fold when touched
