marriage bed

1. NOUN

/ ˈmærɪdʒ bɛd / (AmE), / ˈmærɪdʒ bɛd / (BrE)
the bed shared by a married couple, especially newlyweds
bed shared by a married couple

Examples

The antique marriage bed had been in the family for generations.
On their wedding night, they retired early to their marriage bed.
The couple decorated their new home, giving special attention to their marriage bed.
