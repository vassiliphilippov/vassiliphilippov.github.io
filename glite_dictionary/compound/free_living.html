free living

1. NOUN

/ fri ˈlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / friː ˈlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
A lifestyle characterized by easy indulgence of appetites and lack of restraint, especially in pleasures and luxuries.
indulgent lifestyle; lack of restraint

Examples

The novel depicts the free living of the wealthy elite during the Roaring Twenties.
Her free living eventually caught up with her health and finances.
Critics accused the artist of promoting a free living attitude among young people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hedonism - the pursuit of pleasure; sensual self-indulgence
