with us

1. ADV

/ wɪð ʌs / (AmE), / wɪð ʌs / (BrE)
present together with our group, or still alive or present in a situation involving us.
present together with us; still alive/involved

Examples

Despite all the changes, she is still with us after all these years.
We are grateful to have you with us for tonight's ceremony.
He is no longer with us, but his legacy lives on.

Synset Examples

It's comforting to know that he's still with us today.
