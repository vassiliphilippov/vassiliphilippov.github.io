giant petrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt ˈpɛtrəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈpɛtrəl / (BrE)
a large brownish seabird of the petrel family, found mainly in Antarctic seas
large Antarctic seabird

Examples

During the expedition, we spotted a giant petrel soaring above the icy waters.
The giant petrel feeds on carrion as well as fish and squid.
Researchers observed several giant petrels nesting along the rocky coast.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: petrel - a small to medium-sized seabird in the family Procellariidae
