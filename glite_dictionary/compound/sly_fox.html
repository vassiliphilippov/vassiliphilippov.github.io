sly fox

1. NOUN

/ slaɪ fɑks / (AmE), / slaɪ fɒks / (BrE)
A person who is clever and cunning, often using deceit to achieve their goals.
cunning or clever person

Examples

Everyone knows he's a sly fox who always gets the best deals.
Don't be fooled by her charm; she's a sly fox when it comes to business.
The company's sly fox found a clever way around the new regulations.

Synset Examples

He’s a sly fox when it comes to negotiations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fox - a person who is clever and cunning
  • Synonym: snake - a person who is treacherous or deceitful
