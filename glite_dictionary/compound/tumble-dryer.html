tumble-dryer

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌmbəlˌdraɪər / (AmE), / ˈtʌmbəlˌdraɪə / (BrE)
A machine that dries wet clothes by spinning them in a drum with heated air.
machine for drying clothes with heated air

Examples

After washing her shirts, she put them in the tumble-dryer to dry quickly.
Our old tumble-dryer stopped working, so we had to hang our clothes outside.
Using a tumble-dryer in winter is much more convenient than air-drying laundry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clothes dryer - a machine for drying wet clothes by heated air
