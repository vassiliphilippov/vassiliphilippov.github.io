facial recognition

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪʃəl ˌrɛkəɡˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfeɪʃəl ˌrɛkəɡˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of identifying or verifying a person by analyzing and comparing facial features with stored images
biometric identification by analyzing facial features

Examples

Many smartphones now unlock using facial recognition technology.
Airports are increasingly using facial recognition to speed up security checks.
Concerns over privacy have been raised about the widespread use of facial recognition in public spaces.

Synset Examples

they used face recognition to spot known terrorists

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biometric identification - identification using unique biological characteristics
