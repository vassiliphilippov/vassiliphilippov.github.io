lighting industry

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪtɪŋ ˌɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtɪŋ ˌɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector concerned with the manufacture, sale, and installation of lighting products and systems
industry for lighting products and services

Examples

The lighting industry has evolved rapidly with the introduction of LED technology.
She found a rewarding career in the lighting industry as a product designer.
Many companies in the lighting industry are focusing on energy-efficient solutions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lighting - providing or using light, especially artificially
