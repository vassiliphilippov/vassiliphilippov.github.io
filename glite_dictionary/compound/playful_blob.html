playful blob

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪfəl blɑb / (AmE), / ˈpleɪfl̩ blɒb / (BrE)
an abstract, rounded shape or form, often used in art or design, that appears fun or whimsical
fun, abstract shape; art/design term

Examples

The artist filled the canvas with a large playful blob of blue that drew everyone’s attention.
In the logo design, a playful blob brings a sense of creativity and warmth to the brand.
Children loved the mural with its every playful blob and splash of color lighting up the school hallway.

Synset Examples

He created a playful blob using bright colors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blob - a shapeless mass or drop
