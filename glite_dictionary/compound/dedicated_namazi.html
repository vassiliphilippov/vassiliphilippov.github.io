dedicated namazi

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛdəˌkeɪtɪd nəˈmɑːzi / (AmE), / ˈdɛdɪkeɪtɪd nəˈmɑːzi / (BrE)
A devout Muslim who prays regularly, especially performing the five daily prayers.
devout Muslim who prays regularly

Examples

He is known as a dedicated namazi in the community and leads by example.
As a dedicated namazi, she never misses her daily prayers, no matter how busy her schedule is.
Being a dedicated namazi brings her a sense of peace and discipline.

Synset Examples

He is a dedicated namazi, praying five times daily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devotee - a person who is very interested in and enthusiastic about someone or something
  • Synonym: worshipper - a person who shows reverence and adoration for a deity

Etymology

Namazi from Urdu/Farsi نمازى‎ (namāzī), meaning 'one who prays' (namaz = prayer).
