well timed

1. ADJ

/ wɛl taɪmd / (AmE), / wɛl tʌɪmd / (BrE)
happening or done at the most suitable or appropriate moment; timely
happening at the right time

Examples

Her arrival at the meeting was well timed, just as the discussion began.
His well timed comment lightened the mood in the room.
The rescue team's well timed intervention saved several lives.

Synset Examples

a timely warning
with timely treatment the patient has a good chance of recovery
a seasonable time for discussion
the book's publication was well timed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: timely - happening at the right or opportune time
  • Antonym: untimely - happening at an unsuitable or premature time
