order Mycrosporidia

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər ˌmaɪkroʊspəˈrɪdiə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌmaɪkrəʊspəˈrɪdɪə / (BrE)
A taxonomic order of microscopic parasitic organisms within the subclass Cnidosporidia.
taxonomic order of microscopic parasites
medical

Examples

The order Mycrosporidia consists of microscopic parasites that infect a variety of hosts.
Scientists studied the life cycle of organisms within the order Mycrosporidia.
New research has identified several species belonging to the order Mycrosporidia in marine environments.

Etymology

From Greek 'mykēs' (fungus) and 'spora' (seed), indicating spore-forming organisms.
