soybean meal

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔɪˌbin miːl / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪˌbiːn miːl / (BrE)
a coarse powder made by grinding soybeans, commonly used as animal feed or as a food ingredient
coarse powder made from soybeans

Examples

Many farmers use soybean meal as a major protein source in livestock feed.
The bakery adds soybean meal to its bread for enhanced nutrition.
Export of soybean meal has increased due to rising global demand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soybean - an edible seed used as a source of food and oil
