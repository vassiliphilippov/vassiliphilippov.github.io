air hostess

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌhoʊstəs / (AmE), / ˈɛː ˌhəʊstɪs / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to look after passengers on an aircraft
female flight attendant

Examples

The air hostess helped passengers stow their luggage before take-off.
As a child, she dreamed of becoming an air hostess and traveling the world.
The friendly air hostess served drinks and snacks during the flight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flight attendant - a person employed to serve passengers on an aircraft
  • Synonym: air stewardess - a female steward on an airplane
  • Antonym: air steward - a male steward on an airplane

Etymology

Formed from 'air' and 'hostess', the feminine form of 'host', from Old French 'hostesse'.
