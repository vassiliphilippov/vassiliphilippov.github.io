printing company

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪntɪŋ ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈprɪntɪŋ ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
A company that provides commercial printing services, such as producing books, magazines, and promotional materials.
company offering commercial printing services

Examples

She contacted a local printing company to produce brochures for the new business.
The printing company delivered the finished booklets ahead of schedule.
Many businesses rely on a printing company for marketing materials and stationery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: printer - a person or company engaged in printing
