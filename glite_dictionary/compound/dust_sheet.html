dust sheet

1. NOUN

/ dʌst ʃit / (AmE), / dʌst ʃiːt / (BrE)
a large piece of cloth used to cover furniture in order to protect it from dust when not in use
cloth to cover furniture from dust

Examples

Before painting the living room, we covered the sofa with a dust sheet to keep it clean.
The old armchair sat under a dust sheet in the attic for years.
She carefully folded each dust sheet after spring cleaning was finished.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drop cloth - a cloth used to protect surfaces from dust or paint
