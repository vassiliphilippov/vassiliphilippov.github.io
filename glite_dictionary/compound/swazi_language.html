Swazi language

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɑzi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈswɑːzi ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Bantu language closely related to Zulu, spoken primarily in Eswatini and South Africa.
Bantu language of Eswatini and South Africa

Examples

The young girl is fluent in both English and Swazi language.
Many people in Eswatini use the Swazi language in everyday conversation.
Linguists study the similarities between Zulu and the Swazi language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Zulu - A Bantu language closely related to Swazi, spoken in South Africa

Etymology

Named after the Swazi people of Eswatini.
