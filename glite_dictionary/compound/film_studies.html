film studies

1. NOUN

/ fɪlm ˈstʌdiz / (AmE), / fɪlm ˈstʌdiz / (BrE)
the academic study of films, including their history, theory, and criticism
academic discipline studying films

Examples

She decided to major in film studies at university.
The professor specializes in European cinema within the field of film studies.
Recent advancements in film studies have expanded the discipline to include digital media.

Synset Examples

Since the launch of the hugely influential Cinegraph project in the early 1980s, both German film and German film studies have changed practically beyond all recognition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cinema studies - the academic discipline focused on the analysis of cinema
