Trinidad and Tobago dollar

1. NOUN

/ ˌtrɪnɪˌdæd ən təˈbeɪɡoʊ ˈdɑlər / (AmE), / ˌtrɪnɪˌdad ən təˈbeɪɡəʊ ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
the official currency and basic unit of money in Trinidad and Tobago
currency of Trinidad and Tobago

Examples

I exchanged my US dollars for Trinidad and Tobago dollars at the airport.
The price of groceries is listed in Trinidad and Tobago dollars throughout the country.
Tourists are advised to carry some Trinidad and Tobago dollars when visiting local markets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dollar - basic monetary unit in several countries, including the US, Canada, Australia, etc.
