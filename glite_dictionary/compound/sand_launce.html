sand launce

1. NOUN

/ sænd lɔns / (AmE), / sænd lɔːns / (BrE)
A very small, slender silvery fish that often forms schools and burrows into sandy beaches.
small silvery burrowing fish

Examples

Thousands of sand launces gathered in the shallow waters near the shore.
Seabirds often feed on sand launce during their migration.
At low tide, you can sometimes spot a sand launce quickly burrowing into the sand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sand eel - another name for the sand launce
