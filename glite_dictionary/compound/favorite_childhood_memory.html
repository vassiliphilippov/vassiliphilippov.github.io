favorite childhood memory

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪvərɪt ˈtʃaɪld.hʊd ˈmɛməri / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərɪt ˈtʃaɪld.hʊd ˈmɛməri / (BrE)
a memory from one's childhood that is especially cherished or fondly remembered
a most cherished childhood recollection

Examples

My favorite childhood memory is baking cookies with my grandmother every Christmas.
He shared his favorite childhood memory during the group discussion at camp.
Whenever I feel sad, I think about my favorite childhood memory of playing in the garden.

Synset Examples

Flying a kite is her favorite childhood memory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recollection - the action of remembering something; memory
  • Antonym: to forget - to be unable to remember
