anarkid lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.ər.kɪd ˈlaɪf.staɪl / (AmE), / ˈæn.ə.kɪd ˈlaɪf.staɪl / (BrE)
A way of living that rejects or challenges traditional societal structures and norms, often associated with anarchist attitudes or youth subcultures.
lifestyle rejecting societal structures

Examples

After high school, he chose an anarkid lifestyle and refused to follow in his parents' footsteps.
Artists in the punk scene often adopted an anarkid lifestyle, rejecting authority and materialism.
Her novel explores the consequences of leading an anarkid lifestyle in a conformist society.

Synset Examples

He embraced an anarkid lifestyle, challenging societal norms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: counterculture - A way of life or set of attitudes opposed to or at variance with the prevailing social norm
  • Antonym: mainstream lifestyle - A lifestyle conforming to established societal norms and values

Etymology

Blend of 'anarchist' and 'kid,' referring to rebellious youth culture; 'lifestyle' from life + style.
