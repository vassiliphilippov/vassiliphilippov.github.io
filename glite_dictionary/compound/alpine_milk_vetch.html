alpine milk vetch

1. NOUN

/ ˈælpaɪn mɪlk vɛtʃ / (AmE), / ˈalpʌɪn mɪlk vɛtʃ / (BrE)
A perennial plant of mountainous regions in Eurasia and North America, belonging to the vetch genus.
a perennial mountain plant (vetch genus)

Examples

Botanists have discovered a new population of alpine milk vetch on the mountain slopes.
The delicate purple flowers of alpine milk vetch attract many pollinators in the summer.
Hikers often encounter alpine milk vetch near melting snow patches at high elevations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milk vetch - any plant of the genus Astragalus, to which alpine milk vetch belongs
