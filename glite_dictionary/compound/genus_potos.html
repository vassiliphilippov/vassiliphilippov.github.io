genus Potos

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈpoʊ.toʊs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈpəʊ.tɒs / (BrE)
A genus of mammals in the family Procyonidae, including the kinkajou.
a genus of mammals (kinkajou)

Examples

The genus Potos is best known for the kinkajou, a small rainforest mammal.
Scientists recently published a comprehensive study on the evolutionary history of genus Potos.
Only one living species, the kinkajou, is classified within genus Potos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kinkajou - a mammal species in genus Potos

Etymology

From Latin 'genus' (kind, type) and 'Potos', the genus name.
