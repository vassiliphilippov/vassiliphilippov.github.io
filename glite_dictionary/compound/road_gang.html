road gang

1. NOUN

/ roʊd gæŋ / (AmE), / rəʊd ɡæŋ / (BrE)
a group of workers employed to build, repair, or maintain roads, especially as part of a crew
group of road workers

Examples

The road gang worked tirelessly through the night to repair the damaged highway.
A road gang was assigned to lay new asphalt along Main Street this summer.
During the Great Depression, many unemployed men joined a road gang to earn a living.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: work crew - a group of people working together on a task, especially manual labor
