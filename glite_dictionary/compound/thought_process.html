thought process

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɔt ˌproʊ.ses / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt ˌprəʊ.ses / (BrE)
the series of mental steps and reasoning involved in considering or solving something
series of mental steps; reasoning

Examples

Her thought process was clear and logical during the meeting.
Meditation can help slow down your thought process and reduce stress.
I tried to understand his thought process before responding to his question.

Synset Examples

thinking always made him frown
she paused for thought

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reasoning - the action of thinking about something in a logical way
  • Synonym: train of thought - a series of connected ideas or thoughts
