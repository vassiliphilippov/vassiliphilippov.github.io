ratchet screwdriver

1. NOUN

/ ˈrætʃət ˌskruːˌdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˈrætʃɪt ˌskruːˌdraɪvə / (BrE)
a screwdriver fitted with a ratchet mechanism that allows the blade to turn in one direction only and often a spiral handle, enabling rotation of the blade with downward pressure
screwdriver with a ratcheting mechanism

Examples

He used a ratchet screwdriver to assemble the furniture more quickly.
The mechanic prefers a ratchet screwdriver because it makes repetitive screwing much easier.
A ratchet screwdriver is especially useful when working in tight spaces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screwdriver - a hand tool for turning screws
