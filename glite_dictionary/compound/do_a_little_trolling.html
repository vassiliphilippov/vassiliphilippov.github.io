to do a little trolling

1. VERB

/ duː ə ˈlɪtəl ˈtroʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / duː ə ˈlɪtl ˈtrəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
to deliberately provoke or annoy people on the internet for amusement, usually in a lighthearted or mischievous way
provoke people online for amusement
informal slang

Examples

Occasionally, he likes to do a little trolling in comment sections just to see people's reactions.
She admitted she would do a little trolling when she was bored at work.
They sometimes do a little trolling on social media for entertainment.

Synset Examples

He loves to do a little trolling in online forums.
Sometimes, I do a little trolling just for fun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troll - to make deliberately offensive or provocative online posts
