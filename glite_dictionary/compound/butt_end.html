butt end

1. NOUN

/ bʌt ɛnd / (AmE), / bʌt end / (BrE)
the thicker or broader end of something, especially the handle of a tool or weapon
the thick end of something, esp. a handle

Examples

He gripped the butt end of the axe tightly as he prepared to swing.
The butt end of the bat was worn from years of use.
She used the butt end of the broom to knock the object off the shelf.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tip - the pointed or thin end of something
