smoke-cured

1. ADJ

/ smoʊk kjʊrd / (AmE), / sməʊk kjʊəd / (BrE)
(of meat or fish) preserved and flavored by drying and curing in wood smoke.
preserved by curing in wood smoke

Examples

The restaurant is famous for its smoke-cured bacon, which has a rich, distinctive flavor.
Traditional recipes often call for smoke-cured ham during the holidays.
She prefers smoke-cured salmon on bagels for breakfast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoked - (of food) preserved or flavored by exposure to smoke
