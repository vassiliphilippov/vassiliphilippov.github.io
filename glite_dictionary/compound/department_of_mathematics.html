department of mathematics

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑrtmənt əv ˌmæθəˈmætɪks / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtmənt əv ˌmæθəˈmatɪks / (BrE)
the academic unit of a college or university responsible for teaching and research in mathematics
university academic mathematics unit

Examples

She decided to join the department of mathematics at the university for her graduate studies.
The department of mathematics organizes weekly seminars on algebra and geometry.
Several professors in the department of mathematics have received national awards for their research.
