full stomach

1. NOUN

/ fʊl ˈstʌmək / (AmE), / fʊl ˈstʌmək / (BrE)
a feeling of having eaten enough food; the state of being satiated after a meal
having eaten enough; satiated after a meal

Examples

Exercising on a full stomach can sometimes cause discomfort.
After the big dinner, I lay down with a full stomach and watched TV.
Students might find it hard to focus in class if they have a full stomach.

Synset Examples

She felt sleepy on a full stomach after lunch.
It's hard to concentrate on a full stomach.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hungry - feeling the need to eat food
  • Synonym: satiated - having had enough to eat; fully satisfied
