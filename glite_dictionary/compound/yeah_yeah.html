yeah, yeah

1. INTJ

/ jɛə jɛə / (AmE), / jɛə jɛə / (BrE)
an informal expression used to show indifference, impatience, or lack of interest in what someone is saying
expression of indifference or impatience
informal

Examples

He kept insisting he would fix it, and I just said, yeah, yeah, to move on.
Whenever my brother starts telling the same story again, I just nod and say yeah, yeah.
"Yeah, yeah, I'll do it later," she replied without looking up from her phone.

Synset Examples

He kept talking about his plans, and I just replied, yeah, yeah.
She kept repeating her excuses, and I just said, yeah, yeah.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whatever - expression signifying indifference
  • Synonym: I don't care - expression for indifference or lack of concern
  • Synonym: oh, really - expression sometimes showing disinterest or doubt
