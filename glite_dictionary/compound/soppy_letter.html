soppy letter

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑpi ˈlɛtər / (AmE), / ˈsɒpi ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
A letter that is overly sentimental or emotional, often expressing excessive affection or sentiment.
overly sentimental letter

Examples

He blushed as he read the soppy letter from his girlfriend.
She couldn't help but giggle at the soppy letter tucked inside the envelope.
After a long day, receiving a soppy letter from home lifted his spirits.

Synset Examples

She wrote a soppy letter that made him smile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: love letter - a letter expressing feelings of love
