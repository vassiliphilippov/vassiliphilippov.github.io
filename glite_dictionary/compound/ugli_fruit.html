ugli fruit

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌɡli frut / (AmE), / ˈʌɡli fruːt / (BrE)
A large, sweet, juicy citrus fruit that is a hybrid between a tangerine and a grapefruit, with thick, wrinkled skin.
hybrid citrus fruit (tangerine × grapefruit)

Examples

The ugli fruit is prized for its sweet, tangy flavor and juicy segments.
She bought an ugli fruit at the market to make a refreshing citrus salad.
Despite its odd appearance, the ugli fruit is delicious and easy to peel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tangelo - a hybrid citrus fruit of tangerine and pomelo or grapefruit

Etymology

From 'ugly', referring to its wrinkled appearance, and 'fruit'. 'Ugli' is a trademarked name from Jamaica, where the fruit originated.

2. NOUN

/ ˈʌɡli frut / (AmE), / ˈʌɡli fruːt / (BrE)
a hybrid citrus fruit between a grapefruit and a mandarin, known for its rough, wrinkled skin and cultivated especially in Florida
hybrid citrus fruit

Examples

The ugli fruit is surprisingly sweet despite its unattractive, wrinkled skin.
She bought an ugli fruit at the farmers’ market and enjoyed its unique citrus flavor.
Ugli fruit can be eaten fresh or used in salads and desserts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tangelo - a hybrid of tangerine and pomelo or grapefruit
  • Degree Higher: grapefruit - a large, round citrus fruit with a bitter, acidic taste
  • Degree Lower: mandarin - a small, sweet citrus fruit

Etymology

Blend of 'ugly' (referring to its appearance) and 'fruit'. Coined as a trademark in Jamaica.
