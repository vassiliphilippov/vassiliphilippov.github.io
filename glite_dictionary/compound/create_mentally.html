to create mentally

1. VERB

/ kriˈeɪt ˈmɛntəli / (AmE), / kriːˈeɪt ˈmɛntəli / (BrE)
to bring something into existence in the mind or imagination rather than through physical means
form or imagine in the mind

Examples

Artists often create mentally before putting their ideas on canvas.
She was able to create mentally an entire world for her story.
Some people create mentally detailed solutions before acting on them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to imagine - form a mental image or concept of something
  • Synonym: to conceive - form or devise (a plan or idea) in the mind
