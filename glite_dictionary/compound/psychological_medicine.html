psychological medicine

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaɪkəˈlɑdʒɪkəl ˈmɛdəsən / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˈmɛdɪsɪn / (BrE)
the branch of medicine that deals with the diagnosis and treatment of mental disorders
medical field for mental disorders
medical

Examples

After years of studying psychological medicine, she opened her own mental health clinic.
Research in psychological medicine has led to improved treatments for depression and anxiety.
He plans to pursue a career in psychological medicine to help those with mental health conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychiatry - the medical specialty devoted to the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of mental disorders
  • Degree Lower: psychotherapy - treatment of mental disorder by psychological rather than medical means

Etymology

From 'psychology' (the scientific study of mind and behavior) and 'medicine' (the science of diagnosing and treating disease).
