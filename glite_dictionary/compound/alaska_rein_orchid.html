Alaska rein orchid

1. NOUN

/ əˈlæskə reɪn ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / əˈlɑːskə reɪn ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A North American orchid (Platanthera unalascensis) similar to the coastal rein orchid, but with smaller flowers, found from Alaska to Baja California and east to the Dakotas and Colorado.
a North American orchid species

Examples

We stumbled upon a beautiful Alaska rein orchid while hiking in the mountains of Colorado.
The Alaska rein orchid is prized by botanists for its delicate, smaller flowers.
Habitat loss has made the Alaska rein orchid less common in some parts of its range.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: coastal rein orchid - a related orchid species with larger flowers found near the coast
