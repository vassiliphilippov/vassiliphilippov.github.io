bright red

1. ADJ

/ braɪt rɛd / (AmE), / braɪt rɛd / (BrE)
A vivid, intense shade of red.
vivid shade of red

Examples

The walls of the restaurant were painted a bright red to create a lively atmosphere.
He bought a bright red bicycle that stands out in the crowd.
The artist mixed several pigments to achieve the perfect bright red for her painting.

Synset Examples

She wore a bright red dress to the gala.
