receiving end

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsiːvɪŋ ɛnd / (AmE), / rɪˈsiːvɪŋ end / (BrE)
the person or group who experiences the effect, especially a negative one, of an action by others
person experiencing the effect of an action

Examples

After the mistake, the manager was on the receiving end of several complaints.
The comedian found herself on the receiving end of harsh criticism after her remarks.
As a customer service agent, he is often on the receiving end of people's frustration.

Synset Examples

She was on the receiving end of his harsh criticism.
Being on the receiving end of that prank was unexpected.
He found himself on the receiving end of a surprise party.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: giving end - the person or group performing an action, especially a negative one, towards others
