Greater London

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡreɪtər ˈlʌndən / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtə ˈlʌndən / (BrE)
the administrative region comprising London and its surrounding boroughs, forming the metropolitan area of the capital of England
metropolitan region including London and its suburbs

Examples

The population of Greater London continues to grow each year.
Many commuters travel from towns outside Greater London into the city for work.
Property prices in Greater London are among the highest in the UK.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: London - the capital city in southeastern England
