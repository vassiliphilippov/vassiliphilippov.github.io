favorable position

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪvərəbəl pəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərəbl pəˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
A situation or state that gives someone or something a competitive advantage over others.
a situation of competitive advantage

Examples

Her impressive qualifications gave her a favorable position in the job market.
The team secured a favorable position early in the tournament.
By investing cautiously, they found themselves in a favorable position when the economy recovered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advantage - a condition or circumstance that puts one in a superior or favorable position
  • Antonym: disadvantage - an unfavorable circumstance or condition that reduces the chances of success
