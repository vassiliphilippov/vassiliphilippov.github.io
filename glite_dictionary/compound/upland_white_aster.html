upland white aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌpˌlænd waɪt ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈʌplənd waɪt ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
A tufted, rigid perennial plant native to North America, with loose clusters of white flowers.
a North American perennial flowering plant

Examples

The meadows were dotted with blooming upland white aster during late summer.
She planted upland white aster in her native wildflower garden for its delicate white blooms.
Botanists noted a significant increase in upland white aster populations in the region this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a plant of the genus Aster, with star-shaped flowers
