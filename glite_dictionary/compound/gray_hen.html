gray hen

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪ hɛn / (AmE), / ɡreɪ hɛn / (BrE)
the female of the black grouse, a bird found in Europe and Asia
female black grouse

Examples

The gray hen is often more camouflaged than the brightly colored male grouse.
During the breeding season, the gray hen searches for a safe nesting site.
A flock of gray hens was spotted feeding at the forest edge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black grouse - a large bird native to Eurasia, known for the male's black plumage
