urban dweller

1. NOUN

/ ˈɝːbən ˈdwɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˈɜːbən ˈdwɛlə / (BrE)
a person who lives in a city, as distinct from someone in a rural area
person living in a city

Examples

The urban dweller often has access to public transportation and cultural amenities.
As an urban dweller, he finds it easy to enjoy different cuisines from around the world.
Being an urban dweller means learning how to adapt to noise and crowded streets.

Synset Examples

The urban dweller faced challenges in finding affordable housing.
As an urban dweller, she enjoys the city's conveniences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: city-dweller - a person living in a city; synonym for 'urban dweller'
  • Antonym: rural dweller - a person living in the countryside; opposite of 'urban dweller'
