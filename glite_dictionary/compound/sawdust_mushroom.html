sawdust mushroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔːˌdʌst ˈmʌʃˌrʊm / (AmE), / ˈsɔːdʌst ˈmʌʃruːm / (BrE)
an edible mushroom with black, coarsely wrinkled caps, found growing in piles of hardwood sawdust
edible mushroom found in sawdust piles

Examples

We found a cluster of sawdust mushrooms growing behind the old lumber mill.
Some chefs prize sawdust mushrooms for their unique texture and flavor in gourmet dishes.
Remember to properly identify a sawdust mushroom before eating it, as with all wild fungi.
