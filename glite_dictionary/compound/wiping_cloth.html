wiping cloth

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪpɪŋ klɔθ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪpɪŋ klɒθ / (BrE)
a piece of cloth used for cleaning grease, oil, or dirt, especially in industrial settings
cloth for cleaning in industrial contexts

Examples

The mechanic grabbed a wiping cloth to clean the grease off his hands.
Every workstation in the factory is supplied with a fresh wiping cloth each morning.
Please use a wiping cloth to remove any oil spills from the machinery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rag - a piece of old cloth used for cleaning; synonym for wiping cloth
