service door

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːvɪs dɔːr / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs dɔː / (BrE)
a door intended for use by staff, for deliveries, or for removal of refuse, rather than for main entry
door for staff, deliveries, or refuse

Examples

The delivery truck parked near the service door at the back of the restaurant.
Only employees are allowed to use the service door for entry during business hours.
Trash is collected each morning from the bins beside the service door.
