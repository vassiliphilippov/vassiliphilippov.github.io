risky venture

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪski ˈvɛntʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɪski ˈventʃə / (BrE)
An undertaking or business activity that involves a high level of risk and uncertainty.
an undertaking involving significant risk

Examples

Starting a new business with little experience is often seen as a risky venture.
The explorers embarked on a risky venture into the uncharted jungle.
Investing all your savings in one startup can be a risky venture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adventure - an unusual and exciting or daring experience
  • Antonym: safe bet - something that is very likely to succeed
