family history

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
information about diseases or medical conditions present in a person's family, especially as relevant to the person's own health risks.
medical background of a person's family
medical

Examples

The doctor asked about my family history of heart disease during the checkup.
You should tell your physician if you have a family history of diabetes.
A detailed family history can help assess a patient's risk for certain cancers.
