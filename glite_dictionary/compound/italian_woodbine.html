Italian woodbine

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈtæliən ˈwʊdˌbaɪn / (AmE), / ɪˈtæliən ˈwʊdˌbaɪn / (BrE)
A deciduous climbing shrub with fragrant yellow-white flowers, commonly known as a type of honeysuckle.
deciduous climbing shrub; fragrant yellow-white flowers

Examples

The Italian woodbine flourished along the old stone wall, filling the air with its sweet scent.
Gardeners often train Italian woodbine to climb trellises for its attractive flowers and foliage.
Bees are drawn to the nectar of the Italian woodbine during the early summer months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honeysuckle - a climbing shrub with tubular flowers, often fragrant
