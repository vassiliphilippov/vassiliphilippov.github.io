Air Force Space Command

1. PROPN

/ ɛr fɔrs speɪs kəˈmænd / (AmE), / eə fɔːs speɪs kəˈmɑːnd / (BrE)
a former command of the United States Air Force responsible for space and intercontinental ballistic missile operations
US Air Force space operations command

Examples

The Air Force Space Command played a crucial role in overseeing military satellite launches.
After restructuring, responsibilities of the Air Force Space Command were absorbed by the U.S. Space Force.
Many advanced technologies were developed under the supervision of the Air Force Space Command.
