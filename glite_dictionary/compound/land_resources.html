land resources

1. NOUN

/ lænd rɪˈsɔr.sɪz / (AmE), / lænd rɪˈsɔː.sɪz / (BrE)
natural resources consisting of arable or usable land, especially as needed for agriculture or development
arable or usable land as a resource

Examples

The government is investing in sustainable use of land resources to ensure future food security.
Rapid urbanization can put pressure on a country's land resources and limit space for farming.
Efficient management of land resources is critical for preserving the environment and boosting agricultural production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: natural resources - materials or substances such as minerals, forests, water, and fertile land that occur in nature and can be used for economic gain
