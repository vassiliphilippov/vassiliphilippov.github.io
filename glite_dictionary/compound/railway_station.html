railway station

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪl.weɪ ˈsteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈreɪl.weɪ ˈsteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A place where trains stop to pick up or drop off passengers or goods.
place where trains stop for passengers or goods

Examples

We waited for our friends at the railway station before boarding the train.
The old railway station is now a museum showcasing local history.
Commuters rush through the railway station every morning to catch their trains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: train station - place where trains stop for passengers or goods (especially in American English)
