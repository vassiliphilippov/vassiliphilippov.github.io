cherry crab

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛri kræb / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri krab / (BrE)
An Asian species of wild crab apple, cultivated for its small, acidic, usually red fruit, often used for making preserves.
Asian wild crab apple; red fruit for preserves

Examples

The cherry crab tree in her backyard produced a bumper crop this year.
Jars of homemade jelly lined the shelves, each labeled with the words cherry crab.
Some gardeners plant cherry crab as an ornamental for its vibrant spring blossoms and red fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab apple - Tree producing small, tart apples; related fruit
