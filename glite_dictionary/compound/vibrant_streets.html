vibrant streets

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪ.brənt strits / (AmE), / ˈvaɪ.brənt striːts / (BrE)
streets that are full of life, color, and activity
lively, colorful streets

Examples

The vibrant streets of Barcelona are filled with music, art, and people from all walks of life.
Tourists love wandering through the vibrant streets of New Orleans during Mardi Gras.
She took endless photos of the vibrant streets decorated for the festival.

Synset Examples

We spent our vacation exploring the vibrant streets of Manila.
I spent my summer vacation exploring the vibrant streets of Joburg.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bustling streets - streets busy with activity and people
  • Antonym: quiet streets - streets that are calm and have little activity
