dead on

1. ADV

/ dɛd ɑn / (AmE), / dɛd ɒn / (BrE)
exactly correct or accurate; precisely right.
exactly correct; precisely right

Examples

His answer to the math problem was dead on and impressed the teacher.
The critics say her portrayal of the character is dead on.
You were dead on about the time it would take to finish the project.

Synset Examples

Her prediction was dead on; the stock market crashed.
The weather forecast was dead on for the weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exact - completely correct or accurate
  • Synonym: precise - marked by exactness; accurate
  • Antonym: off the mark - not accurate or correct
