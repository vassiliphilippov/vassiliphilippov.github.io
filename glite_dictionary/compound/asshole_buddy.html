asshole buddy

1. NOUN

/ ˈæsˌhoʊl ˈbʌdi / (AmE), / ˈasˌhəʊl ˈbʌdi / (BrE)
A friend who has a rude or obnoxious personality but with whom one maintains a friendly relationship.
rudely behaving friend
informal vulgar

Examples

Even though he's sometimes rude, he's still my asshole buddy and we always look out for each other.
At the party, my asshole buddy made everyone laugh with his sarcastic jokes.
Sometimes you need an asshole buddy to tell you the harsh truth you refuse to hear.

Synset Examples

He’s my asshole buddy, but we have fun together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - an obnoxious or mean person
  • Synonym: friend - a person whom one knows and with whom one has a bond of mutual affection
