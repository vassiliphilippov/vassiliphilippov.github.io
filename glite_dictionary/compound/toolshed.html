toolshed

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːlʃɛd / (AmE), / ˈtuːlʃɛd / (BrE)
A small building or structure used for storing tools, usually found in a garden or backyard.
shed for storing tools

Examples

I keep my gardening supplies in the toolshed behind the house.
After the storm, we found the old toolshed had blown over.
He built a new toolshed to organize his woodworking equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shed - a simple roofed structure used for storage

Etymology

From 'tool' + 'shed', both of Old English origin.
