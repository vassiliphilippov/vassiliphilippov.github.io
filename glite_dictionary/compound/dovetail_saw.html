dovetail saw

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊvˌteɪl sɔ / (AmE), / ˈdəʊv.teɪl sɔː / (BrE)
A small backsaw with a fine blade, used for cutting dovetail joints in woodworking.
a small saw for cutting dovetail joints

Examples

He used a dovetail saw to create precise joints in the oak cabinet.
A dovetail saw is essential for detailed woodworking such as making drawers.
The fine teeth of a dovetail saw allow for clean, accurate cuts in hard woods.

Synset Examples

Although most dovetail saw teeth are set for cross-cutting, a rip saw tooth pattern is more efficient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backsaw - a saw with a stiffening rib on the edge opposite the cutting edge
