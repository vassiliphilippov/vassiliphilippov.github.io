leaving do

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːvɪŋ duː / (AmE), / ˈliːvɪŋ duː / (BrE)
a party or social gathering held to mark someone's departure from a job, group, or place
farewell party for someone's departure
british

Examples

We all chipped in to buy a cake for her leaving do.
Tom's leaving do will be held at the local pub after work next Friday.
The team presented him with a signed card at his leaving do.

Synset Examples

His leaving do was a heartfelt farewell from the team.
Everyone attended the leaving do to wish him luck.
We organized a leaving do for her last Friday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farewell party - a party given to say goodbye to someone who is leaving
