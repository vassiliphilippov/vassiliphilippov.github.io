like a zillionaire

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə zɪl.jəˌnɛr / (AmE), / laɪk ə zɪl.jəˌnɛə / (BrE)
in a very extravagant or lavish manner, as if one had an unlimited amount of money
in a very extravagant or lavish way
informal

Examples

After winning the lottery, he started spending like a zillionaire on expensive cars and holidays.
During their trip, the couple dined like a zillionaire every night at top restaurants.
She redecorated her house like a zillionaire, sparing no expense on furnishings.

Synset Examples

She lives like a zillionaire with her lavish lifestyle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extravagantly - in a lavish or excessive manner
