northern beech fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðərn ˈbiːtʃ fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈbiːtʃ fɜːn / (BrE)
a beech fern native to North America and Eurasia, typically found in moist woodlands
a woodland fern of North America and Eurasia

Examples

The northern beech fern thrives in the shaded areas of deciduous forests.
We spotted a cluster of northern beech fern along the damp woodland trail.
The delicate fronds of the northern beech fern made it easy to identify among other plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beech fern - a type of fern with delicate, beech-like leaves
