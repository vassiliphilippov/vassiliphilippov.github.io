funeral parlor

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjunərəl ˈpɑrlər / (AmE), / ˈfjʊnərəl ˈpɑːlə / (BrE)
a place where dead bodies are kept and prepared for burial or cremation, and where people can visit to pay their respects to the deceased
place for preparing and viewing the deceased

Examples

The family gathered at the funeral parlor to remember their loved one before the ceremony.
Visitors paid their final respects at the local funeral parlor on Friday evening.
She arranged the flowers in the funeral parlor before the viewing began.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mortuary - a place where dead bodies are kept before burial or cremation
  • Synonym: funeral home - an establishment where the dead are prepared for burial or cremation and last respects are paid
