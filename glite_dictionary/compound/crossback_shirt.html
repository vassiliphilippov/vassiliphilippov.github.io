crossback shirt

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɔsˌbæk ʃɜrt / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsbæk ʃɜːt / (BrE)
a shirt featuring straps that cross over at the back, often leaving parts of the back exposed
shirt with crossed back straps

Examples

She paired her crossback shirt with high-waisted jeans for a casual look.
The store just received a new shipment of crossback shirts in various colors.
His crossback shirt was perfect for the warm summer evening.

Synset Examples

He wore a stylish crossback shirt to the party.
