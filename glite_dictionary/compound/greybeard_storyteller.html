greybeard storyteller

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪˌbɪrd ˈstɔr.iˌtɛl.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪˌbɪəd ˈstɔː.riˌtɛl.ə / (BrE)
an elderly man, often wise or experienced, who tells stories, typically evoking a sense of tradition or folklore
elderly male storyteller

Examples

The greybeard storyteller sat by the fire, enchanting everyone with ancient legends.
Children gathered in awe as the greybeard storyteller began his tale.
At every festival, the village welcomed the greybeard storyteller to share his wisdom.

Synset Examples

The greybeard storyteller captivated the children with his tales.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storyteller - a person who tells stories

Etymology

From 'greybeard' meaning an old man with a grey beard, and 'storyteller.'
