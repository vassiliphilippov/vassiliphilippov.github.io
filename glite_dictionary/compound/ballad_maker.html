ballad maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæləd ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbæləd ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who composes the words or music of ballads or popular songs
composer of ballads or popular songs

Examples

The famous ballad maker crafted emotional lyrics that resonated with listeners across generations.
As a young ballad maker, she spent countless nights perfecting her melodies and verses.
Many of the traditional songs we know today were shaped by anonymous ballad makers centuries ago.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: songwriter - a person who writes songs or the music for them
