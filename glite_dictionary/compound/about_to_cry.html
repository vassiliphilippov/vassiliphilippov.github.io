about to cry

1. ADJ

/ əˈbaʊt tʊ ˈkraɪ / (AmE), / əˈbaʊt tʊ ˈkraɪ / (BrE)
very close to beginning to cry; on the verge of tears
on the verge of tears

Examples

She looked about to cry when she heard the sad news.
His voice trembled as if he was about to cry during the speech.
The child stood in the doorway, clearly about to cry after losing her toy.

Synset Examples

She looked like she was about to cry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tearful - showing signs of crying or being on the verge of tears
