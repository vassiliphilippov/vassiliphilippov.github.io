air lock

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr lɑk / (AmE), / ˈeə lɒk / (BrE)
a chamber used to allow safe passage between areas at different air pressures, preventing pressure loss or contamination
chamber for passage between pressure zones

Examples

Before entering the main spacecraft, he waited for the air lock to pressurize.
The submarine is equipped with an air lock to allow safe entry and exit underwater.
The laboratory features a small air lock to prevent contamination from outside air.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decompression chamber - a chamber used to adjust pressure for divers or astronauts
