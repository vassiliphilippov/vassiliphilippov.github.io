wave shape

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪv ʃeɪp / (AmE), / ˈweɪv ʃeɪp / (BrE)
the shape of a wave as represented by a graph showing how its value changes over time
shape of a wave shown as a graph

Examples

The wave shape of the signal was analyzed to identify electrical interference.
Different musical instruments produce distinct wave shapes that give each their unique sound.
By adjusting the oscillator, you can change the wave shape from a sine wave to a square wave.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waveform - a graphical representation of the shape of a wave
