mouse hare

1. NOUN

/ maʊs hɛr / (AmE), / maʊs heə / (BrE)
A small, short-eared burrowing mammal of rocky uplands in Asia and western North America; also known as a pika.
small burrowing mammal, also called pika

Examples

The mouse hare is well adapted to the cold climate of the mountains.
Scientists studied the behavior of the mouse hare in its natural habitat.
Unlike rabbits, the mouse hare has shorter ears and lives among rocks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pika - small burrowing mammal of rocky uplands, alternative name for mouse hare
