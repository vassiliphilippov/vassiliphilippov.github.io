second period

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛk.ənd ˈpɪr.i.əd / (AmE), / ˈsek.ənd ˈpɪə.ri.əd / (BrE)
the second division or segment of play in a game, especially in sports where play is divided into periods
second segment of a game or match

Examples

The team scored twice in the second period to take the lead.
After a rough second period, the coach made several line changes.
The pace of the game changed completely during the second period.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: first period - the initial division of play in a game
  • Degree Higher: third period - the third division of play in a game
