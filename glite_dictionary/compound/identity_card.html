identity card

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈdɛntəti kɑrd / (AmE), / aɪˈdɛntɪti kɑːd / (BrE)
an official card that proves who a person is, typically issued by a government or authority
official card proving identity

Examples

You must present your identity card when applying for a passport.
Her employer asked for a valid identity card before processing her application.
All students are required to carry their identity card at all times on campus.

Synset Examples

he had to show his card to get in

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ID card - abbreviation of identity card
