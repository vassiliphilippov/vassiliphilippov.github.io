Port of Spain

1. PROPN

/ ˌpɔrt əv ˈspeɪn / (AmE), / ˌpɔːt əv ˈspeɪn / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Trinidad and Tobago, located on the west coast of the island of Trinidad
capital city of Trinidad and Tobago

Examples

Many international companies have their offices in Port of Spain.
The Carnival in Port of Spain is famous for its vibrant festivities.
She traveled from London to Port of Spain for a business conference.

Etymology

From English 'port' and 'Spain', referring to the city’s historical naming by Spanish colonists.
