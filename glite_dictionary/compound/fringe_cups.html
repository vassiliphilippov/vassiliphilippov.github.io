fringe cups

1. NOUN

/ frɪndʒ kʌps / (AmE), / frɪndʒ kʌps / (BrE)
a perennial plant of western North America with clumps of mostly basal leaves and racemes of cream or pale pink fringed flowers
a North American plant with fringed flowers

Examples

The woodland trail was lined with fringe cups blooming in the spring sunlight.
Botanists studied the fringe cups for their unique fringed petals and distribution range.
She identified the delicate fringe cups by their pale pink flowers clustered in racemes.
