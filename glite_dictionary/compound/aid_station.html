aid station

1. NOUN

/ eɪd ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / eɪd ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
a place near a battle or emergency area where first aid is given to the wounded
a first aid site near combat or emergencies

Examples

The wounded soldiers were rushed to the aid station for immediate medical attention.
During the marathon, volunteers set up an aid station halfway through the course.
Medics established an aid station close to the front lines to treat casualties quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first aid post - a place where first aid is administered
