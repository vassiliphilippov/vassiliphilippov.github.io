resort hotel

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈzɔrt hoʊˈtɛl / (AmE), / rɪˈzɔːt həʊˈtɛl / (BrE)
a hotel located in a resort area that offers a wide range of amenities and recreational facilities for guests
hotel in a resort area with many amenities

Examples

The family spent their holiday at a luxurious resort hotel by the beach.
Many resort hotels offer all-inclusive packages that cover meals, activities, and entertainment.
After a long drive, we finally arrived at the resort hotel nestled in the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hotel - an establishment providing accommodation, meals, and other services for travelers
  • Synonym: resort - a place frequented for vacations or recreation
