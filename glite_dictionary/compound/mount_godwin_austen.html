Mount Godwin Austen

1. PROPN

/ maʊnt ˌɡɑdwɪn ˈɔstɪn / (AmE), / maʊnt ˌɡɒdwɪn ˈɔːstɪn / (BrE)
the second highest mountain in the world, located in the Karakoram Range on the border of Pakistan and China, also known as K2.
second highest mountain (K2)

Examples

Mountaineers from around the world dream of scaling Mount Godwin Austen despite its notorious dangers.
The breathtaking views from the slopes of Mount Godwin Austen are revered among alpinists.
Due to severe weather conditions, several expeditions to Mount Godwin Austen have been postponed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: K2 - another name for Mount Godwin Austen, the world's second highest peak

Etymology

Named after English surveyor Henry Haversham Godwin-Austen.
