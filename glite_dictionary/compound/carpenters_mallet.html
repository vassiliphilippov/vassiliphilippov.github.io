carpenter's mallet

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːr.pən.tɚz ˈmæl.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pən.təz ˈmæl.ɪt / (BrE)
a short-handled wooden mallet used by carpenters for striking chisels or wedges
wooden mallet used by carpenters

Examples

He used a carpenter's mallet to tap the chisel gently into the wood.
The old carpenter's mallet had a worn wooden head but was still very effective.
Always choose a carpenter's mallet with a comfortable grip for long projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mallet - a hammer with a large, usually wooden head
