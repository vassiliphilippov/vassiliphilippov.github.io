razor-billed auk

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪzər bɪld ɔk / (AmE), / ˈreɪzə bɪld ɔːk / (BrE)
a black-and-white seabird of the northern Atlantic with a compressed, sharp-edged bill
northern Atlantic seabird with a sharp bill

Examples

The razor-billed auk can often be seen nesting on rocky cliffs along the North Atlantic coast.
A group of razor-billed auks dived into the sea in search of fish.
The razor-billed auk is easily recognized by its compressed, sharp-edged bill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: razorbill - another term for the razor-billed auk (Alca torda)
