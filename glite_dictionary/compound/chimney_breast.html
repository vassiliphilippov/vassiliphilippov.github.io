chimney breast

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪmni brɛst / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪmni brɛst / (BrE)
The part of a wall that protrudes into a room to house and enclose the lower part of a chimney.
part of wall enclosing a chimney

Examples

The old house had a large chimney breast in the living room, adding to its character.
They removed the chimney breast to create more floor space during renovations.
A picture hung above the chimney breast, drawing attention to the fireplace.
