right-on response

1. NOUN

/ ˌraɪt ˈɑn rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / ˌraɪt ˈɒn rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
a reply or answer that is exactly appropriate or correct for the situation
exactly appropriate reply

Examples

His right-on response impressed everyone at the meeting.
She gave a right-on response when asked about the project's timeline.
I wasn't expecting such a right-on response to my tricky question.

Synset Examples

He gave a right-on response to the criticism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: correct answer - an answer that is accurate or appropriate
