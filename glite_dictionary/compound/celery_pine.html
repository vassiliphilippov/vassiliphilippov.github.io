celery pine

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛləri paɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɛləri paɪn / (BrE)
An Australasian evergreen coniferous tree with foliage resembling celery, comprising phyllodes borne in the axils of scalelike leaves.
Australasian evergreen coniferous tree

Examples

The celery pine thrives in the mild coastal climates of eastern Australia.
Some gardeners appreciate the unique foliage of the celery pine in landscaped parks.
The timber from the celery pine has various uses, including furniture making.
