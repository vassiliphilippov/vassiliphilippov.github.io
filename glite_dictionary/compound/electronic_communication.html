electronic communication

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the transfer of information or messages using electronic means such as computers, email, or the Internet
transfer of messages via electronic means

Examples

Many businesses now rely on electronic communication to coordinate with remote teams.
The rise of electronic communication has transformed the way people stay in touch.
Students submitted their assignments through electronic communication platforms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: email - messages sent electronically over a computer network
  • Synonym: telecommunication - communication over distance by wire, radio, etc.
