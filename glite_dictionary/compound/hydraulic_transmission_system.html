hydraulic transmission system

1. NOUN

/ haɪˈdrɑː.lɪk trænzˈmɪʃ.ən ˈsɪs.təm / (AmE), / haɪˈdrɒ.lɪk trænzˈmɪʃ.ən ˈsɪs.təm / (BrE)
a system that transmits power using fluid under pressure, commonly used in machines and vehicles
power transfer system using fluid pressure

Examples

The new model of the excavator features an advanced hydraulic transmission system for smoother operation.
If the hydraulic transmission system fails, the vehicle may lose its ability to shift gears properly.
Technicians are trained to maintain the hydraulic transmission system in modern industrial machinery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hydraulic transmission - power transmission using pressurized fluid
  • Antonym: mechanical transmission - power transmission using gears and shafts

Etymology

From 'hydraulic' (Greek hydor, water + aulos, pipe) and 'transmission system'.
