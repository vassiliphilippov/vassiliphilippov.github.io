salad greens

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæləd ɡrinz / (AmE), / ˈsæləd ɡriːnz / (BrE)
leafy vegetables that are suitable for eating raw in salads, such as lettuce, spinach, or arugula
leafy vegetables for salads

Examples

We bought fresh salad greens from the farmers' market yesterday.
She tossed the salad greens with a light vinaigrette before serving.
Adding a mix of salad greens to your plate makes any meal healthier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lettuce - a common salad green with crisp leaves
  • Synonym: spinach - a leafy green vegetable often used in salads
