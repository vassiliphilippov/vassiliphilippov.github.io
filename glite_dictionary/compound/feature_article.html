feature article

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːtʃɚ ˈɑrtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈfiːtʃə ˈɑːtɪkl / (BrE)
A special or prominent article in a newspaper or magazine that covers a particular subject in depth.
special article in publication

Examples

The magazine published a fascinating feature article about renewable energy innovations.
She was honored when her feature article appeared on the front page of the Sunday edition.
Editors often assign a feature article to staff writers with expertise in the subject.

Synset Examples

they ran a feature on retirement planning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feature - a special newspaper or magazine article
