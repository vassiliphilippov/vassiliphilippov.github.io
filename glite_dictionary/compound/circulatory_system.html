circulatory system

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɝː.kjəˈleɪ.tɔːr.i ˈsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˌsɜː.kjəˈleɪ.tər.i ˈsɪs.təm / (BrE)
the system of organs and tissues, including the heart and blood vessels, responsible for circulating blood and lymph throughout the body
system that circulates blood and lymph
medical

Examples

The circulatory system transports oxygen and nutrients to the body's tissues.
Doctors can detect certain diseases by examining how well the circulatory system is functioning.
Exercise helps keep your circulatory system healthy and efficient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardiovascular system - the system comprising the heart and blood vessels
  • Synonym: lymphatic system - the system that carries lymph through the body

Etymology

From Latin 'circulare' (to move in a circle) and 'systema' (system).
