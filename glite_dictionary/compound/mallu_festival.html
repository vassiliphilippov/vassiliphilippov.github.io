Mallu festival

1. NOUN

/ ˈmælu ˌfɛstəvəl / (AmE), / ˈmæluː ˌfɛstəvəl / (BrE)
a cultural festival held to celebrate the traditions and heritage of the Malayalam-speaking community
festival celebrating Malayalam heritage
informal

Examples

Every year, the city organizes a Mallu festival featuring dance, music, and traditional food.
At the Mallu festival, visitors enjoyed authentic Kerala cuisine and colorful performances.
The Mallu festival attracted people of all ages eager to connect with their heritage.

Synset Examples

She attended a Mallu festival celebrating their heritage.

Etymology

Mallu is an informal shortening of 'Malayali,' referring to people from Kerala, India.
