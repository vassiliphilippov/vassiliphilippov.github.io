New York City

1. PROPN

/ nu ˈjɔrk ˈsɪti / (AmE), / njuː ˈjɔːk ˈsɪti / (BrE)
The largest city in New York State and in the United States, located in southeastern New York at the mouth of the Hudson River, renowned as a major financial, cultural, and population center.
largest city in the United States

Examples

She moved to New York City in search of better job opportunities.
Many tourists visit New York City to see landmarks like the Statue of Liberty and Central Park.
Despite its size, New York City is known for neighborhoods with a distinct local character.

Etymology

From the name of the English Duke of York and the earlier settlement of New Amsterdam.
