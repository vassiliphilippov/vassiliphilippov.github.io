genus Sciadopitys

1. NOUN

/ ˌsi.ədoʊˈpaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌsi.adəʊˈpaɪtɪs / (BrE)
the only genus of the family Sciadopityaceae, consisting of the Japanese umbrella pines
genus of Japanese umbrella pines

Examples

The genus Sciadopitys includes only one living species, native to Japan.
Botanists find the genus Sciadopitys fascinating due to its unique evolutionary lineage.
Many gardens feature trees from the genus Sciadopitys for their ornamental value.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sciadopitys verticillata - species of Japanese umbrella pine, sole surviving species in the genus

Etymology

From Greek 'skias' (umbrella) and 'pitys' (pine).
