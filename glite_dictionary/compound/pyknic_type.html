pyknic type

1. NOUN

/ pɪkˈnɪk taɪp / (AmE), / pɪkˈnɪk taɪp / (BrE)
A body type characterized by a round, fat, and heavy build, especially as classified in early physical anthropology and psychology.
round, fat, and heavy body type

Examples

The psychologist classified him as having a pyknic type physique according to Kretschmer's typology.
Some scientists believed that personality traits were linked to the pyknic type body structure.
Her grandfather, with his pyknic type, always stood out in family photos.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: asthenic type - a slender and lightly built body type
  • Degree Higher: athletic type - a muscular and well-built body type

Etymology

From Greek pyknos (thick, dense) + English type.
