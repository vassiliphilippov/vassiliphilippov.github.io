yellow bunting

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈbʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈbʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
A European species of bunting (Emberiza citrinella), the male of which has bright yellow plumage.
European bunting with bright yellow male

Examples

The yellow bunting is easily identified by its vivid yellow plumage during the breeding season.
We spotted a yellow bunting singing on a hedgerow while hiking in the countryside.
Conservationists are concerned about the declining numbers of yellow buntings in some regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellowhammer - Another common name for the yellow bunting (Emberiza citrinella)
  • Degree Lower: bunting - Small songbird of the Emberizidae family
