go-devil

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊ ˌdɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ ˌdɛvəl / (BrE)
A person who is eager for adventure or excitement, often acting in a reckless or daring way.
recklessly adventurous person
informal

Examples

Everyone considered her a true go-devil after she volunteered for the risky expedition.
His go-devil attitude sometimes got him into trouble, but he never backed down from a challenge.
The team relied on their go-devil to push boundaries and try new things.

Synset Examples

She’s a go-devil, always ready for an adventure.
He was such a go-devil, always seeking thrills.
That go-devil jumped off the cliff without hesitation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daredevil - a person who is daring or takes risks
