butterfly flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ərˌflaɪ ˈflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌflaɪ ˈflaʊə / (BrE)
any plant of the genus Schizanthus, having finely divided leaves and brightly colored, variegated flowers
ornamental plant with showy variegated flowers

Examples

She planted several butterfly flowers in her garden to add a splash of color.
The butterfly flower is admired for its delicate and vibrant petals.
Gardeners value the butterfly flower for its ease of care and attractive appearance.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ɚˌflaɪ ˈflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌflaɪ ˈflaʊə / (BrE)
A shrub or small tree native to Dutch Guiana, known for clusters of pink flowers streaked with purple.
tropical shrub with pink-purple flowers

Examples

The butterfly flower displays vibrant pink blossoms streaked with purple in the spring.
Gardeners often choose the butterfly flower for its striking and colorful floral clusters.
In Dutch Guiana, the butterfly flower is admired for both its shade and its ornamental beauty.
