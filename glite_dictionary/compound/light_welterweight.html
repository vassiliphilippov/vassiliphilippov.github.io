light welterweight

1. NOUN

/ laɪt ˈwɛltərˌweɪt / (AmE), / laɪt ˈwɛltəˌweɪt / (BrE)
A weight division in boxing and other combat sports, for fighters heavier than lightweight but lighter than welterweight.
boxing/martial arts weight division

Examples

He won the light welterweight title after a close twelve-round fight.
This tournament features several rising stars in the light welterweight division.
As a light welterweight, she trains to maintain the strict weight limit for her class.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: lightweight - a lighter boxing weight category than light welterweight
  • Degree Higher: welterweight - the next heavier weight class above light welterweight

Etymology

Formed from 'light' and 'welterweight', indicating a class between lightweight and welterweight.
