first appearance

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɝːst əˈpɪrəns / (AmE), / ˌfɜːst əˈpɪərəns / (BrE)
the occasion or act of something or someone being seen or starting for the first time in a particular place or role
the initial showing or beginning of something

Examples

The young actor made his first appearance on stage to thunderous applause.
Many fans were eager to see the team's first appearance in the championship games.
Her first appearance as conductor was praised by critics and audiences alike.

Synset Examples

they looked forward to the debut of their new product line

Related Terms

  • Synonym: debut - a person's first appearance or performance
  • Synonym: premiere - the first public performance or showing of a play, film, etc.
