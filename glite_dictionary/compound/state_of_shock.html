state of shock

1. NOUN

/ steɪt əv ʃɑk / (AmE), / steɪt əv ʃɒk / (BrE)
a condition of extreme surprise, distress, or emotional numbness caused by an unexpected event
extreme surprise or emotional distress

Examples

After hearing about the accident, he remained in a state of shock for several hours.
The whole community was in a state of shock following the announcement of the closure.
She struggled to speak, clearly still in a state of shock after the incident.

Synset Examples

She is in a state of shock from the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shock - a sudden and disturbing emotional or physical response
