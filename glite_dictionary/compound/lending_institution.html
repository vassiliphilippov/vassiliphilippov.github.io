lending institution

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛndɪŋ ˌɪnstəˈtuːʃən / (AmE), / ˈlɛndɪŋ ˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
A financial organization whose primary business is making loans to individuals or companies.
financial organization that makes loans

Examples

The company obtained a loan from a reputable lending institution to expand its operations.
Many people approach a lending institution when they need funds for buying a house or car.
Each lending institution has its own criteria for approving loans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bank - financial institution that accepts deposits and makes loans
