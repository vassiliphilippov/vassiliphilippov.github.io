genus Arbutus

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ɑrˈbjuːtəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ɑːˈbjuːtəs / (BrE)
A group of evergreen shrubs and trees native to southern Europe and western North America, including the strawberry tree and madrona.
a group of evergreen shrubs and trees (strawberry tree, madrona)

Examples

The genus Arbutus includes both the strawberry tree and several species native to North America.
Botanists often study the genus Arbutus for its attractive flowers and fruit.
Several members of the genus Arbutus are valued in landscaping for their colorful bark and evergreen leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Arbutus - A genus or specific species of evergreen tree or shrub known as strawberry tree or madrona

Etymology

From Latin 'arbutus', the name for the strawberry tree.
