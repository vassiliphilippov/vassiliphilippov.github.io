vape juice

1. NOUN

/ veɪp dʒus / (AmE), / veɪp dʒuːs / (BrE)
liquid containing nicotine and flavorings used in electronic cigarettes; also known as e-liquid or vape liquid.
liquid for electronic cigarettes

Examples

He bought a new bottle of vape juice with a watermelon flavor.
Many users prefer vape juice with lower nicotine content when trying to quit smoking.
The store carries a variety of vape juices including mint, coffee, and fruit blends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: e-liquid - liquid solution used in e-cigarettes
  • Synonym: e-juice - another term for liquid used in vaping

Etymology

Blend of 'vape' (short for vaporizer) and 'juice' (slang for liquid).
