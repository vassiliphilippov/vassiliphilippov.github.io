hardstemmed bulrush

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrd.stɛmd ˈbʊl.rʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːd.stɛmd ˈbʊl.rʌʃ / (BrE)
A widely distributed North American sedge with rigid, olive green stems.
a North American sedge with rigid stems

Examples

The wetlands were covered with hardstemmed bulrush, providing habitat for many bird species.
Conservationists work to protect areas where hardstemmed bulrush naturally grows.
The rigid stems of hardstemmed bulrush make it easily distinguishable from other marsh plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulrush - a tall rush or sedge growing in wetlands
