space helmet

1. NOUN

/ speɪs ˈhɛlmɪt / (AmE), / speɪs ˈhɛlmɪt / (BrE)
a protective helmet worn by astronauts to provide life-support and safety in outer space
helmet for astronauts in space

Examples

Every astronaut must wear a space helmet during a spacewalk.
The space helmet protects against micrometeoroids and extreme temperatures.
She saw her reflection in the visor of her bulky space helmet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spacesuit - a suit worn by astronauts in space
