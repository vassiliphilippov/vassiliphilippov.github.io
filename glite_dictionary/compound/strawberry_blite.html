strawberry blite

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔːˌbɛri blaɪt / (AmE), / ˈstrɒˌbɛri blaɪt / (BrE)
A European annual plant with clusters of greenish flowers that produce red, pulpy, berrylike fruit; naturalized in North America.
a plant with red, berrylike fruit

Examples

The gardener pointed out the strawberry blite, noting its bright red berries among the green foliage.
In some regions, strawberry blite is foraged for its edible leaves and fruit.
After learning to identify strawberry blite, she added it to her list of wild edibles.

Etymology

From 'strawberry' for its fruit's resemblance, plus 'blite', from Greek bliton (a plant name).
