peach bell

1. NOUN

/ pitʃ bɛl / (AmE), / piːtʃ bɛl / (BrE)
A perennial European bellflower (Campanula persicifolia) with racemes of white or blue bell-shaped flowers.
perennial European bellflower plant

Examples

The peach bell added a delicate touch of blue to the garden border.
She planted several peach bells among the daisies for a pop of color.
Each peach bell blooms in early summer, attracting bees and butterflies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bellflower - any plant of the genus Campanula, with bell-shaped flowers
