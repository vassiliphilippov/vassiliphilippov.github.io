stable companion

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪbəl kəmˈpænjən / (AmE), / ˈsteɪbəl kəmˈpanjən / (BrE)
a horse that is kept in the same stable as another, often referring to horses owned by the same person
a horse kept with another in the same stable

Examples

The champion was always accompanied by his stable companion during training sessions.
Their stable companions often became their closest friends as they traveled from race to race.
After the race, she led her stable companion back to the barn for a well-deserved rest.
