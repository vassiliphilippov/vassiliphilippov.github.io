Republic of Belarus

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv bəˈlærəs / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv bɛˈlarʊs / (BrE)
the official name of the country Belarus, a landlocked republic in eastern Europe, formerly part of the Soviet Union
official name of the country Belarus

Examples

The United Nations recognized the Republic of Belarus as a sovereign nation in 1991.
Many foreign diplomats are stationed in the capital city of the Republic of Belarus.
After the dissolution of the Soviet Union, the Republic of Belarus declared its independence.
