scouring pad

1. NOUN

/ ˈskaʊərɪŋ pæd / (AmE), / ˈskaʊərɪŋ pad / (BrE)
a small, abrasive cleaning pad, typically made of synthetic fibers or steel wool, used for scrubbing pots, pans, and other kitchenware
abrasive cleaning pad for pots and pans

Examples

She used a scouring pad to remove the burnt residue from the pan.
A fresh scouring pad works wonders on stubborn kitchen stains.
Always rinse the scouring pad thoroughly after cleaning to keep it sanitary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sponge - a soft, absorbent cleaning tool often used alongside a scouring pad
