New York Shitty

1. NOUN

/ nu jɔrk ˈʃɪti / (AmE), / njuː ˈjɔːk ˈʃɪti / (BrE)
A humorous or derogatory nickname for New York City, used especially when referring to its messiness or undesirable aspects.
humorous/vulgar name for New York City
slang vulgar

Examples

Some comedians have described the subway during rush hour as New York Shitty at its finest.
After the garbage strike, residents jokingly started calling their neighborhood New York Shitty.
The blog post criticized the city's condition, referring to it as New York Shitty throughout the article.

Synset Examples

After the storm, the streets looked like New York Shitty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New York City - largest city in the United States

Etymology

Pun on 'New York City' with 'shitty' substituted for humorous or vulgar effect.
