polo mallet

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊloʊ ˈmælɪt / (AmE), / ˈpəʊləʊ ˈmælɪt / (BrE)
a long-handled mallet used to strike the ball in the game of polo
mallet used in polo

Examples

The player gripped the polo mallet tightly as he prepared to hit the ball.
His polo mallet broke during the final match, forcing him to switch equipment.
Learning how to swing a polo mallet correctly takes a lot of practice.
