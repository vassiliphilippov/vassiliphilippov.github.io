sweep-second

1. NOUN

/ swip ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / swiːp ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
A second hand on a clock or watch that moves smoothly and is mounted at the same center as the hour and minute hands.
second hand mounted with hour and minute hands

Examples

His new watch features a large sweep-second for easy reading.
The sweep-second glides smoothly around the dial, unlike traditional ticking hands.
She preferred clocks with a sweep-second because she found them more accurate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: second hand - the hand on a clock or watch that indicates seconds

Etymology

From 'sweep' (move smoothly) + 'second' (referring to the second hand).
