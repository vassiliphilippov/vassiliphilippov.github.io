knife thrower

1. NOUN

/ naɪf ˈθroʊɚ / (AmE), / naɪf ˈθrəʊə / (BrE)
a performer who throws knives as part of an act or entertainment, often aiming at targets or around a person
performer who throws knives

Examples

The circus featured a daring knife thrower whose precision amazed the audience.
She watched a skilled knife thrower hurl blades at a spinning target with perfect aim.
Being a professional knife thrower requires years of practice and a steady hand.

Synset Examples

He dreamed of becoming a knife thrower in a big show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knife throwing - the act of throwing knives as a skill or performance
  • Degree Lower: juggler - a performer who entertains by keeping several objects in motion in the air
