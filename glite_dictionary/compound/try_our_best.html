to try our best

1. VERB

/ traɪ aʊɚ bɛst / (AmE), / traɪ aʊə bɛst / (BrE)
to make the greatest effort possible to achieve something
make the greatest effort possible

Examples

No matter how difficult the exam is, we always try our best.
Even though we lost the game, the coach said we tried our best.
Let's try our best to finish the project before the deadline.

Synset Examples

Miss Ann always encouraged us to try our best.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to do one's best - to try as hard as possible
  • Synonym: make an effort - to attempt to do something
