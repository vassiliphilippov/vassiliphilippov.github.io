Ewenki language

1. NOUN

/ ɛˈwɛŋki ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɛˈwɛŋki ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Tungusic language spoken by the Evenki people in eastern Siberia
language of the Evenki people of Siberia

Examples

The Ewenki language is still spoken by communities in eastern Siberia.
She is conducting research on the grammar of the Ewenki language.
Efforts are being made to preserve and revitalize the Ewenki language among younger generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Evenki - a member of the indigenous people native to Siberia

Etymology

From Ewenki (autonym of the Evenki people), adapted into English.
