beyond control

1. ADV

/ biˈjɑnd kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / bɪˈjɒnd kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
out of control; unable to be managed or governed
out of control; unmanageable

Examples

After the storm intensified, the situation quickly got beyond control.
Rumors spread so fast online that they soon became beyond control.
The fire was beyond control by the time the firefighters arrived.

Synset Examples

the riots got out of hand

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of control - not under control; unmanageable

2. ADJ

/ biˈjɑnd kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / bɪˈjɒnd kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
not able to be influenced, managed, or changed by anyone involved
not able to be influenced or changed

Examples

The shipping delay was caused by circumstances beyond control.
Many natural disasters are simply beyond control and must be endured.
His anger sometimes seems to be beyond control, worrying those around him.

Synset Examples
