PO Box No

1. NOUN

/ piː oʊ bɑks ˈnʌm.bər / (AmE), / piː əʊ bɒks ˈnʌm.bər / (BrE)
the identifying number assigned to a post office box where mail can be collected at a post office
number assigned to a post office box

Examples

Please send your completed application to PO Box No 12345, New York, NY 10001.
All correspondence should be addressed to PO Box No 678, London SW1A 1AA.
They rented a PO Box No to securely receive important mail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: post office box - a numbered box kept at a post office for a person's or organization's mail

Etymology

Abbreviation PO from 'Post Office', used in mailing addresses.
