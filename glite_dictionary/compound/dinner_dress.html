dinner dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnər dɹɛs / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə dɹɛs / (BrE)
a formal gown designed to be worn at evening dinners or events
formal gown for evening occasions

Examples

She wore an elegant dinner dress to the charity gala last night.
In the 1920s, a dinner dress was considered essential for fashionable women attending evening events.
The boutique specializes in custom-made dinner dresses for special occasions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evening gown - a long formal dress for evening occasions
  • Degree Lower: cocktail dress - a short elegant dress for semi-formal occasions
