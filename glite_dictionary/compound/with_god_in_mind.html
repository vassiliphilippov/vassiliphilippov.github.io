with God in mind

1. ADP

/ wɪð ɡɑd ɪn maɪnd / (AmE), / wɪð ɡɒd ɪn maɪnd / (BrE)
while considering religious beliefs, morality, or what God would want
considering religious beliefs or morals

Examples

She chose her words carefully, always speaking with God in mind.
The organization's policies are developed with God in mind to align with its core values.
Many people make major life decisions with God in mind.

Synset Examples
