Malheur wire lettuce

1. NOUN

/ mælˈhɜr ˈwaɪr ˈlɛtɪs / (AmE), / mælˈhɜː ˈwaɪə ˈlɛtɪs / (BrE)
a rare flowering plant native to Oregon, resembling mustard and classified as a threatened species
rare Oregon plant resembling mustard

Examples

The Malheur wire lettuce is listed as a threatened species due to habitat loss.
During our hike in Oregon, we were excited to spot the rare Malheur wire lettuce.
Efforts are underway to protect the Malheur wire lettuce from extinction.

Etymology

Named after Malheur County, Oregon, where the plant is found.
