skin flick

1. NOUN

/ skɪn flɪk / (AmE), / skɪn flɪk / (BrE)
A film that contains explicit sexual content; a pornographic movie.
pornographic movie
slang

Examples

He was caught watching a skin flick late at night when his parents came home unexpectedly.
The store carries a variety of foreign films, documentaries, and the occasional skin flick.
She's never felt comfortable with the idea of starring in a skin flick.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: porn film - a movie that features explicit sexual activity
  • Synonym: blue movie - an old-fashioned term for a pornographic film

Etymology

From the slang use of 'skin' (naked body) and 'flick' (movie), dating from mid-20th century US English.
