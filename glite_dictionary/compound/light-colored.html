light-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪtˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having a pale or not intense color; not dark in shade
pale or not dark in color

Examples

She chose a light-colored dress for the summer party.
The room looks brighter with light-colored walls and curtains.
He spilled flour, leaving a light-colored powder on the counter.

Synset Examples

light blue
light colors such as pastels
a light-colored powder

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dark-colored - having a deeply saturated or not pale color
