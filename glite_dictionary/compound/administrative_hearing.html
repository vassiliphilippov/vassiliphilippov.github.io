administrative hearing

1. NOUN

/ ədˌmɪnəˈstreɪtɪv ˈhɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪtɪv ˈhɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
a hearing held outside the traditional court system, typically before an official or tribunal authorized to resolve disputes involving government agencies or regulations
legal proceeding before an administrative body
legal

Examples

The company was required to attend an administrative hearing regarding alleged violations of safety regulations.
During the administrative hearing, both parties presented their evidence to the hearing examiner.
If you wish to contest the penalty, you may request an administrative hearing within 30 days.
