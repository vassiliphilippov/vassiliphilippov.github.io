frogbit family

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɔɡˌbɪt ˈfæmli / (AmE), / ˈfrɒɡˌbɪt ˈfæmli / (BrE)
A small family of nearly stemless freshwater aquatic plants, widely distributed in temperate and tropical regions.
family of aquatic plants

Examples

The frogbit family includes several species that float on the surface of ponds and lakes.
Many botanists study the unique adaptations of plants in the frogbit family.
Members of the frogbit family are often used in aquariums due to their attractive floating leaves.
