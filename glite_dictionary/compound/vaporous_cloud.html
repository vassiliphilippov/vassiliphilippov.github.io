vaporous cloud

1. NOUN

/ ˈveɪpərəs klaʊd / (AmE), / ˈveɪpərəs klaʊd / (BrE)
A cloud that appears misty, thin, or ethereal, often giving a dreamlike or insubstantial impression.
misty, ethereal cloud

Examples

The sunrise cast golden light through the vaporous cloud above the valley.
A vaporous cloud drifted across the moon, shrouding it in a mysterious haze.
She watched as a vaporous cloud floated over the still lake, transforming the scene into a fairytale.

Synset Examples

The vaporous clouds created a dreamy landscape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mist - cloud of tiny water droplets in the air, reducing visibility
  • Degree Higher: fog - a thick cloud of water droplets near the earth's surface
