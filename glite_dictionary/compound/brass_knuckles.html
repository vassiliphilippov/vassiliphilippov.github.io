brass knuckles

1. NOUN

/ bræs ˈnʌkəlz / (AmE), / brɑːs ˈnʌkəlz / (BrE)
A metal weapon consisting of a band worn over the knuckles to increase the force of a punch.
metal weapon worn over knuckles

Examples

The police discovered a pair of brass knuckles hidden inside his coat pocket.
Carrying brass knuckles is illegal in many states due to their dangerous nature.
He wore brass knuckles for protection, though he never had to use them.

Etymology

brass (the metal) + knuckles, referring to a weapon made to fit over the knuckles.
