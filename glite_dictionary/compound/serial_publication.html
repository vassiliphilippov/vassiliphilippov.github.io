serial publication

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪriəl ˌpʌblɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəl ˌpʌblɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
A periodical that is issued at scheduled intervals, such as a magazine or journal.
periodical issued at regular intervals

Examples

The library subscribes to several serial publications in the fields of science and technology.
Many serial publications require yearly subscriptions from their readers.
The archivist catalogued each serial publication by title and publication date.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: periodical - a magazine or journal published at regular intervals
