Pastor sturnus

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæs.tɔr ˈstɜːr.nəs / (AmE), / ˈpɑːstɔː ˈstɜː.nəs / (BrE)
A glossy black bird with a pink back and abdomen, native primarily to Asia.
Asian glossy black bird with pink back and abdomen

Examples

Ornithologists observed a flock of Pastor sturnus migrating across northern India.
The distinctive coloration of Pastor sturnus makes it easy to identify in the field.
During breeding season, the plumage of Pastor sturnus becomes particularly vibrant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rosy starling - Common name for Pastor sturnus
  • Degree Higher: starling - A type of passerine bird in the family Sturnidae

Etymology

From Latin: 'Pastor' (shepherd, also a bird genus name), 'sturnus' (starling).
