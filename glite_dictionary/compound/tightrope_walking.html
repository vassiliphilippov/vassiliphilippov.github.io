tightrope walking

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪtˌroʊp ˌwɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtrəʊp ˌwɔːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of walking along a tightrope or slack rope, especially as a performance or skill
walking on a tightrope as a skill or performance

Examples

The circus featured an impressive tightrope walking act that left the audience awestruck.
He has been practicing tightrope walking for years to master his balance and concentration.
Learning tightrope walking requires patience, focus, and a great deal of coordination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tightrope - a tightly stretched rope or wire for walking or performing on
