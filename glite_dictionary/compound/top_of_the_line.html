top of the line

1. ADJ

/ tɑp əv ðə laɪn / (AmE), / tɒp əv ðə laɪn / (BrE)
of the highest quality or among the best available
highest quality; best available

Examples

She insisted on buying a top of the line smartphone for her photography hobby.
Their new kitchen appliances are all top of the line and feature the latest technology.
If you want reliability and performance, choose the top of the line model.

Synset Examples

This camera is the top of the line model available.
Their top of the line products come with a warranty.
He bought a top of the line laptop for gaming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high-end - of superior quality, price, or sophistication
  • Antonym: low-end - of lower quality or at the lower price range
