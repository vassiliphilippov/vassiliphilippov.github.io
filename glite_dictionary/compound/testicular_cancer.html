testicular cancer

1. NOUN

/ tɛˈstɪkjələr ˈkænsər / (AmE), / tɛˈstɪkjʊlə ˈkɑːnsə / (BrE)
A malignant tumor that develops in the testicles, usually in adult men.
malignant tumor of the testicles
medical

Examples

Early detection of testicular cancer greatly increases the chances of successful treatment.
Doctors recommend regular self-exams to help identify testicular cancer in its early stages.
His diagnosis of testicular cancer came as a shock, but treatment began immediately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cancer - a disease caused by uncontrolled division of abnormal cells
