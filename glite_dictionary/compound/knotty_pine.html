knotty pine

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑti paɪn / (AmE), / ˈnɒti paɪn / (BrE)
Pine wood that contains many visible knots, commonly used for paneling and furniture to give a rustic appearance.
pine wood with many visible knots

Examples

The walls of the cabin were lined with knotty pine to create a cozy atmosphere.
He built the bookshelves from knotty pine for a classic, rustic look.
Many vintage homes feature knotty pine paneling in their basements or dens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pine - wood from a pine tree
