tough game

1. NOUN

/ tʌf ɡeɪm / (AmE), / tʌf ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a match, competition, or contest that is especially challenging or difficult to win
challenging or difficult match

Examples

It was a tough game, but the team never gave up until the final whistle.
Yesterday's tough game left the players exhausted but proud of their effort.
Despite facing injuries, she managed to score the winning goal in a tough game.

Synset Examples

His bloody back was a reminder of the tough game.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: easy game - a match or contest that is not challenging
