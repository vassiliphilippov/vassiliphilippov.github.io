barber's sign

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːrbərz saɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbəz saɪn / (BrE)
a sign, often displaying red and white stripes, indicating that a shop is a barber shop
sign indicating a barber shop

Examples

The barber's sign swung in the breeze outside the shop.
He painted a new barber's sign to attract more customers.
The traditional barber's sign featured red and white stripes.
