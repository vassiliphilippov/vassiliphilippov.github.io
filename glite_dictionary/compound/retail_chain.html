retail chain

1. NOUN

/ ˈriː.teɪl tʃeɪn / (AmE), / ˈriː.teɪl tʃeɪn / (BrE)
A group of retail stores owned, operated, or franchised by the same company and selling similar merchandise.
group of retail stores under one company

Examples

The retail chain plans to open twenty new locations across the country this year.
Many people prefer shopping at a local store rather than a large retail chain.
The retail chain offers discounts to loyal customers every month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chain store - a single store that is part of a retail chain
