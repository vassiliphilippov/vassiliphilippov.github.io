home fries

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm fraɪz / (AmE), / həʊm fraɪz / (BrE)
Sliced or diced pieces of potato fried in a pan until brown and crisp, often served as a breakfast side dish.
fried potatoes, often served for breakfast

Examples

We ordered a side of home fries with our eggs at the diner.
The home fries were perfectly crisp and golden this morning.
She likes to add onions and peppers to her home fries for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hash browns - shredded potatoes fried until brown and crisp
