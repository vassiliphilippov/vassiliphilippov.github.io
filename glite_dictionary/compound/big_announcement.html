big announcement

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ əˈnaʊnsmənt / (AmE), / bɪɡ əˈnaʊnsmənt / (BrE)
an important or significant declaration, often made publicly
important public declaration

Examples

Everyone gathered in the auditorium for the big announcement about the new company policy.
After weeks of speculation, the mayor finally made a big announcement regarding the city's future.
Sarah couldn’t hide her excitement when she shared her big announcement with the whole family.

Synset Examples

He made a big announcement at the conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: announcement - a public or formal statement about a fact or intention
