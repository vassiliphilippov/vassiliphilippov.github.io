back-cloth star

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæk klɔθ stɑːr / (AmE), / ˈbæk klɒθ stɑː / (BrE)
A person who is famous within a limited circle or a performer who plays a supporting role in theater.
person with niche fame or minor role in theater

Examples

After years of hard work, she was content with being a back-cloth star in the regional theater company.
Even as a back-cloth star, he earned the respect of his colleagues and local audiences.
Many talented performers are proud to be known as a back-cloth star within their own niche community.

Synset Examples

They were thrilled to see their friend become a back-cloth star.
He dreamed of being a back-cloth star in his hometown.
She often performed as a back-cloth star in local theater.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supporting actor - an actor in a secondary role
  • Degree Higher: star - a main performer in a show or field
