gut-wrenching decision

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌt ˌrɛntʃɪŋ dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈɡʌt ˌrɛn.tʃɪŋ dɪˈsɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
a choice that is extremely difficult or emotionally distressing to make
very emotionally difficult choice

Examples

Making a gut-wrenching decision about her father's care left her feeling anxious for days.
He faced a gut-wrenching decision when offered a job overseas that meant leaving his family behind.
After hours of debate, the committee finally made the gut-wrenching decision to close the school.

Synset Examples

Her gut-wrenching decision changed everything.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agonizing decision - a decision causing great mental pain or distress
  • Antonym: easy decision - a decision that is not difficult or distressing

Etymology

From 'gut-wrenching', meaning causing emotional anguish, + 'decision'.
