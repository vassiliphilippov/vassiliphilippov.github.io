hand-held vacuum cleaner

1. NOUN

/ ˈhænd ˌhɛld ˈvæk.jum ˌkliː.nɚ / (AmE), / ˈhænd ˌhɛld ˈvæk.juːm ˌkliː.nə / (BrE)
A small, portable vacuum cleaner, powered by batteries or mains electricity, designed for cleaning small areas or minor spills.
small portable vacuum cleaner for small spills

Examples

She used the hand-held vacuum cleaner to clean up the crumbs from the couch.
For quick cleanups in the car, a hand-held vacuum cleaner can be very convenient.
After the kids spilled cereal on the floor, he grabbed the hand-held vacuum cleaner to tidy up.

Synset Examples

Some battery-powered handheld vacuums are wet/dry rated; the appliance must be partially disassembled and cleaned after picking up wet materials, to avoid developing unpleasant odors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vacuum cleaner - an electrical appliance for cleaning carpets and floors
