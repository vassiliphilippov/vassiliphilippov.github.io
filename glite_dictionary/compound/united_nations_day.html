United Nations Day

1. PROPN

/ juˈnaɪt̬ɪd ˈneɪʃənz deɪ / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪtɪd ˈneɪʃənz deɪ / (BrE)
An international observance held on October 24th each year to commemorate the founding of the United Nations in 1945.
international observance marking the UN's founding

Examples

Every year, schools around the world celebrate United Nations Day with cultural performances and discussions about global cooperation.
The secretary-general gave a speech to mark United Nations Day and emphasized the importance of international solidarity.
Many people use United Nations Day as an opportunity to learn more about the UN's work on peace and development.
