staff-tree family

1. NOUN

/ stæf tri ˈfæm.ə.li / (AmE), / stɑːf tri ˈfæm.ɪ.li / (BrE)
a family of trees, shrubs, and woody vines, typically bearing bright-colored fruits; scientifically known as Celastraceae.
family of plants with bright-colored fruits

Examples

Many species in the staff-tree family are grown for their ornamental fruit.
The staff-tree family includes both tropical and temperate plants.
Bright red berries are a characteristic feature of the staff-tree family.
