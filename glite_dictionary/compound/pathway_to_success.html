pathway to success

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæθˌweɪ tu səˈsɛs / (AmE), / ˈpɑːθˌweɪ tʊ səkˈsɛs / (BrE)
a means or method of achieving success, especially in a career or endeavor
route or method to achieve success

Examples

Many young professionals view internships as a pathway to success in their chosen fields.
Education is often described as the most reliable pathway to success for disadvantaged communities.
She believes hard work and determination provide the true pathway to success.

Synset Examples

She found a new pathway to success by networking with industry leaders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: road to success - a course of action leading to success
  • Synonym: pathway - a route or way of achieving something
