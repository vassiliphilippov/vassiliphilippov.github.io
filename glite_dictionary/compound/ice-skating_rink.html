ice-skating rink

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪsˌskeɪtɪŋ rɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈaɪsˌskeɪtɪŋ rɪŋk / (BrE)
A specially prepared area of ice, typically indoors, where people can skate or play ice hockey.
arena with an ice floor for skating or hockey

Examples

The city built a new ice-skating rink in the downtown park.
Many families enjoy spending weekends at the local ice-skating rink.
After school, the children went to the ice-skating rink to practice their routines.

Synset Examples

the crowd applauded when she skated out onto the ice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skating rink - a rink for skating, usually on ice
