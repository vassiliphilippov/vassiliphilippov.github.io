Barbie shipper

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑrbi ˈʃɪpər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbi ˈʃɪpə / (BrE)
a fan who supports or promotes a romantic pairing involving the character Barbie
fan of Barbie romantic pairings

Examples

As a Barbie shipper, she writes fanfiction about Barbie and Ken.
The panel was filled with excited Barbie shippers discussing their favorite couples.
Many Barbie shippers create artwork imagining new love interests for Barbie.

Synset Examples

He identifies as a Barbie shipper and enjoys discussing theories.
The Barbie shipper community is vibrant on social media.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shipper - a fan who supports a romantic pairing between characters

Etymology

Blend of 'Barbie' (the doll's name) and 'shipper' (from 'relationship').
