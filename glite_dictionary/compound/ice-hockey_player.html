ice-hockey player

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪs ˌhɑki ˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈaɪs ˌhɒki ˌpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays the sport of ice hockey, typically as a member of a team
athlete who plays ice hockey

Examples

As a talented ice-hockey player, she quickly rose to the top of her league.
The young ice-hockey player practiced every morning before school to improve his skills.
Many children dream of becoming a professional ice-hockey player when they grow up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hockey player - an athlete who plays hockey (any form)
