like crazy

1. ADV

/ laɪk ˈkreɪzi / (AmE), / laɪk ˈkreɪzi / (BrE)
(informal) With great speed, effort, or intensity; used to emphasize the degree to which something is done.
with great intensity or effort (informal)
informal

Examples

She was running like crazy to catch the last bus.
The team celebrated like crazy after their unexpected victory.
He worked like crazy to finish the project on time.
