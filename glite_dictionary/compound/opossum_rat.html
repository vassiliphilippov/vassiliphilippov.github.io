opossum rat

1. NOUN

/ əˈpɑsəm ˌræt / (AmE), / əˈpɒsəm ˌræt / (BrE)
A small, terrestrial marsupial of southern South America that resembles a shrew.
small South American marsupial resembling a shrew

Examples

The opossum rat is found primarily in the grasslands of southern South America.
Unlike true rats, the opossum rat is a marsupial and carries its young in a pouch.
Scientists are studying how the opossum rat adapts to its environment and diet.
