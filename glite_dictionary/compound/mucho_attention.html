mucho attention

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːtʃoʊ əˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˈmuːtʃəʊ əˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
A large amount of attention, especially given to someone or something; much attention.
a lot of attention (informal)
informal slang

Examples

The media gave the event mucho attention after the shocking announcement.
She always gets mucho attention when she walks into a room.
His new hairstyle has been getting mucho attention at school.

Synset Examples

He paid mucho attention to his fitness regimen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attention - noticing, thinking about, or dealing with something or someone
  • Synonym: focus - concentration of attention or effort

Etymology

Borrowed from Spanish 'mucho' (much, a lot), blended with English 'attention.'
