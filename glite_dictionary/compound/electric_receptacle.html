electric receptacle

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛk.trɪk rɪˈsɛp.tə.kəl / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛk.trɪk rɪˈsɛp.tə.kəl / (BrE)
a socket in a wiring system that provides a connection point for electrical devices to receive current
socket for connecting electrical devices

Examples

The lamp won't turn on unless it's plugged into an electric receptacle.
Every room in the house has at least one electric receptacle for appliances.
Make sure the electric receptacle is properly grounded before using it with power tools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power outlet - a device providing a place for an electric plug to connect to a power supply
  • Antonym: plug - a device for making a connection to an electric receptacle
