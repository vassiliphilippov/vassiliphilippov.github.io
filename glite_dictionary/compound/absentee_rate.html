absentee rate

1. NOUN

/ ˌæb.sənˈtiː ˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˌæb.sənˈtiː ˌreɪt / (BrE)
the percentage of employees who are absent from work during a given period
percentage of workers absent from work

Examples

The company's absentee rate increased significantly during the flu outbreak.
A low absentee rate is crucial for maintaining productivity in the factory.
Human resources tracks the absentee rate to identify trends in employee attendance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absenteeism - the practice or problem of employees not attending work
