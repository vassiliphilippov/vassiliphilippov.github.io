oral presentation

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrəl ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈɔːrəl ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a spoken address or report delivered to an audience, especially in an academic or professional context
spoken address or report to an audience

Examples

She gave an excellent oral presentation on climate change at the conference.
Every student is required to make an oral presentation as part of the final assessment.
The team prepared slides to support their oral presentation to the board.
