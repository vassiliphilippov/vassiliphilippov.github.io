nickel nurser

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪkəl ˈnɜrsər / (AmE), / ˈnɪkəl ˈnɜːsə / (BrE)
a person who grows young plants, especially of uncommon or rare species
person who grows rare young plants

Examples

As a nickel nurser, she always made sure the rare seedlings were well protected.
Becoming a nickel nurser requires patience and a love of unique plants.
Every nickel nurser knows the importance of proper care in the early stages of growth.

Synset Examples

As a nickel nurser, she always cared for the smallest plants.
The nickel nurser tended to every seedling in her garden.
His role as a nickel nurser made him an expert in breeding rare plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nurseryman - a person whose occupation is the cultivation of plants in a nursery
