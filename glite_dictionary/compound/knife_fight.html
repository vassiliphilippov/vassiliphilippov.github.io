knife fight

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪf ˌfaɪt / (AmE), / ˈnaɪf ˌfaɪt / (BrE)
A violent physical fight between people using knives as weapons.
fight involving knives

Examples

The two gang members were arrested after a knife fight broke out downtown.
He suffered injuries to his arm during a knife fight outside the bar.
News reports described a dangerous knife fight that left several people hospitalized.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gunfight - a fight involving guns as weapons
  • Degree Lower: fistfight - a fight using only fists
