tone system

1. NOUN

/ toʊn ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / təʊn ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
The set of pitch patterns used to distinguish meaning in a language or dialect, especially in tone languages.
system of pitch distinctions in a language

Examples

Mandarin has a complex tone system with four distinct pitch patterns.
Linguists study the tone system to understand how meaning changes in different dialects.
Some African languages have a simple tone system with only two tones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tone language - a language in which pitch is used to distinguish word meaning
