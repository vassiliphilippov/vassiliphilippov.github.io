annual clambake

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.ju.əl ˈklæm.beɪk / (AmE), / ˈæn.ju.əl ˈklæm.beɪk / (BrE)
a yearly event where seafood, especially clams, is cooked and served, often as a social gathering
yearly seafood cooking event

Examples

Every summer, the local community hosts an annual clambake by the shore.
Tickets for the annual clambake sold out in just one day this year.
The annual clambake has become a beloved tradition for families in the area.

Synset Examples

The annual clambake attracted thousands of seafood lovers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clambake - a gathering at which clams and other seafood are cooked and eaten outdoors
