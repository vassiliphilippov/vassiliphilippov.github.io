poker player

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊkər ˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈpəʊkə ˌpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays the card game poker, especially professionally or regularly
someone who plays poker

Examples

She became a professional poker player after winning several tournaments.
The poker player skillfully bluffed his way to victory at the final table.
Many aspiring poker players study game theory to improve their chances of winning.

Synset Examples

He’s an excellent poker player at the local casino.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gambler - a person who bets money in games of chance
