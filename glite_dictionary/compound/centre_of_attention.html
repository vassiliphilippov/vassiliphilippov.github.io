centre of attention

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛntər əv əˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɛntə ɒv əˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
the person or thing that everyone is most interested in or focused on in a particular situation
person or thing most focused on

Examples

The birthday girl was clearly the centre of attention at her party.
He enjoys being the centre of attention wherever he goes.
When the dog started dancing, it quickly became the centre of attention at the event.

Synset Examples

his stories made him the center of the party

Related Terms

  • Synonym: center of attention - US spelling of centre of attention; most focused on by others
