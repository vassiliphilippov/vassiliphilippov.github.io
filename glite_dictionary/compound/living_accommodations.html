living accommodations

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvɪŋ əˌkɑməˈdeɪʃənz / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ əˌkɒməˈdeɪʃənz / (BrE)
places, such as rooms or buildings, where people live or are housed collectively
places where people are housed

Examples

After searching for weeks, they finally found living accommodations close to campus.
The company provides living accommodations for employees who relocate for work.
Many college students struggle to afford suitable living accommodations in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: housing - buildings or places for people to live in
