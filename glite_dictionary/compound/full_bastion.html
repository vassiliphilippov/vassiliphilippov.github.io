full bastion

1. NOUN

/ fʊl ˈbæstʃən / (AmE), / fʊl ˈbæstɪən / (BrE)
A bastion that is completely filled so the ground inside is level with the rampart, with no open space at the center.
bastion filled level with rampart

Examples

The fortress was well defended by a full bastion at each corner, making direct assault difficult.
Engineers decided to construct a full bastion to maximize usable space behind the rampart.
Unlike a hollow type, a full bastion offers greater structural stability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bastion - a projecting part of a fortification
