knight of the pestle

1. NOUN

/ naɪt əv ðə ˈpɛstl̩ / (AmE), / naɪt əv ðə ˈpɛstl / (BrE)
A highly skilled cook or chef, especially one known for expertise in grinding spices and preparing fine dishes.
expert chef skilled in grinding spices

Examples

In the busy restaurant, the knight of the pestle ground fresh spices for every dish.
She prided herself on being called the knight of the pestle in her family.
The guests praised the knight of the pestle for the complex flavors in their meal.

Synset Examples

In the kitchen, she’s the knight of the pestle, grinding spices.
He earned the title knight of the pestle for his culinary skills.
The knight of the pestle prepared the finest sauces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chef - a professional cook, typically the chief cook in a restaurant or hotel
  • Synonym: cook - a person who prepares food
