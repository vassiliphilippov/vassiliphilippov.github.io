sympathetic ear

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk ɪr / (AmE), / ˌsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk ɪə / (BrE)
a willingness to listen to someone with compassion and understanding, especially when they have a problem or are in distress
readiness to listen and understand

Examples

Whenever you need to talk, I'm here to offer a sympathetic ear.
After her loss, she found great comfort in a sympathetic ear.
He was grateful to his colleague for lending a sympathetic ear during a difficult time.

Synset Examples

She always lends a sympathetic ear to her friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: listening ear - someone who listens patiently and supportively
