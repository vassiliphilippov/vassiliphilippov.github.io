Texas purple spike

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛk.sɪs ˈpɜːr.pəl spaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɛk.səs ˈpɜː.pəl spaɪk / (BrE)
a species of orchid with slender, nearly leafless reddish-brown stems and loose racemes of reddish-brown flowers, found in open brushy woods of southeastern Arizona and central Texas
a reddish-brown orchid native to Arizona and Texas

Examples

The Texas purple spike is an elusive orchid found in the brushy woods of southeastern Arizona and central Texas.
We spotted several Texas purple spikes blooming along the edge of the trail during our hike.
Botanists study the habitat preferences of the Texas purple spike to aid conservation efforts.
