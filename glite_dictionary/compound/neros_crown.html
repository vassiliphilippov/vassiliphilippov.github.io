Nero's crown

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪəroʊz kraʊn / (AmE), / ˈnɪərəʊz kraʊn / (BrE)
A tropical shrub with glossy leaves and fragrant, nocturnal flowers with wavy or crimped corollas, native to northern India and Thailand.
tropical shrub with fragrant nocturnal flowers

Examples

The garden featured a rare Nero's crown in full bloom near the entrance.
Botanists are interested in the fragrant flowers of Nero's crown for their nocturnal blooming habit.
She planted a Nero's crown alongside other tropical species to enhance the nighttime aroma of her backyard.
