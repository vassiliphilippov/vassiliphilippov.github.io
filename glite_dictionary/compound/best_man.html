best man

1. NOUN

/ bɛst mæn / (AmE), / bɛst mæn / (BrE)
the chief male attendant of the bridegroom at a wedding, typically responsible for helping the groom and performing certain ceremonial duties
chief male attendant at a wedding

Examples

My brother asked me to be his best man at the wedding.
As the best man, he gave a heartfelt speech during the reception.
It is traditional for the best man to hold the wedding rings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groomsman - a male friend or relative assisting a groom at a wedding
  • Antonym: maid of honor - the chief female attendant of the bride at a wedding
