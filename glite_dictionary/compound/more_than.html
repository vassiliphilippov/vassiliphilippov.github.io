more than

1. ADJ

/ mɔr ðæn / (AmE), / mɔː ðæn / (BrE)
Used to indicate a quantity, size, degree, or amount greater than a specified number or value.
greater than a specified amount

Examples

The company employs more than one thousand people across the globe.
It rained more than usual this winter in the valley.
You need more than just talent to succeed in this industry.

Synset Examples

more land
more support
more rain fell
more than a gallon

Related Terms

  • Antonym: less than - indicating a quantity, size, or amount smaller than a specified amount
