launching site

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔn.tʃɪŋ saɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɔːntʃɪŋ saɪt / (BrE)
A place equipped with facilities for launching rockets, missiles, or other spacecraft.
site where rockets or missiles are launched

Examples

The new launching site was built near the coast to facilitate rocket takeoffs.
Security is very strict at the launching site during preparation for a satellite launch.
Engineers are conducting tests at the launching site ahead of tomorrow's scheduled launch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: launch pad - the platform from which a rocket or missile is launched
