hamburger roll

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæmˌbɚɡɚ roʊl / (AmE), / ˈhambəˌɡə rəʊl / (BrE)
a round, soft bread roll designed to hold a hamburger patty and its toppings
round bun for hamburgers

Examples

She placed the grilled patty on a toasted hamburger roll before adding the lettuce and tomato.
We ran out of hamburger rolls and had to use sliced bread for our burgers.
The bakery sells fresh hamburger rolls every Saturday morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hamburger bun - a round bun for holding a hamburger
