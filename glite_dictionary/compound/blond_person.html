blond person

1. NOUN

/ blɑnd ˈpɜrsən / (AmE), / blɒnd ˈpɜːs(ə)n / (BrE)
a person with fair skin and light-colored hair, especially yellow or pale gold
a fair-skinned person with light-colored hair

Examples

The study focused on how a blond person experiences exposure to sunlight.
As a blond person, she burns easily during the summer.
A blond person may have blue or green eyes.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: brunet - a person with dark brown or black hair
