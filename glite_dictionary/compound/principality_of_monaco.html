Principality of Monaco

1. PROPN

/ prɪnˈsɪp(ə)lɪti əv məˈnɑːkoʊ / (AmE), / prɪnˈsɪp(ə)lɪti əv məˈnɒkəʊ / (BrE)
a small independent country and constitutional monarchy located on the French Riviera, known for its wealth, casinos, and status as a tax haven
small country on the French Riviera

Examples

The Principality of Monaco is famous for its annual Formula One Grand Prix.
She traveled through the Principality of Monaco during her summer trip to Europe.
The Principality of Monaco has one of the highest population densities in the world.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'principatus' (principality) and the name 'Monaco', derived from the ancient Greek 'Monoikos'.
