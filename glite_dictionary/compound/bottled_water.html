bottled water

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑt̬.əld ˈwɔt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɒt.əld ˈwɔː.tə / (BrE)
water that is sold in bottles, often sourced from a spring or purified for drinking
water sold in bottles for drinking

Examples

During the hike, she made sure to carry a bottle of bottled water for hydration.
Many people prefer bottled water when traveling to places with questionable tap water quality.
The restaurant offered both tap and bottled water to its customers.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tap water - water supplied through pipes to homes and businesses
  • Synonym: mineral water - naturally occurring water containing minerals, often bottled
