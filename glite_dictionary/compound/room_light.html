room light

1. NOUN

/ ɹum laɪt / (AmE), / ɹuːm laɪt / (BrE)
a light fixture or lamp that provides general illumination for a room
light fixture illuminating a room

Examples

She turned on the room light as soon as she entered the apartment.
The room light flickered during the thunderstorm, making everyone nervous.
Please switch off the room light before you leave the house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ceiling light - a light fixture mounted on the ceiling
  • Synonym: lamp - a device for giving light
