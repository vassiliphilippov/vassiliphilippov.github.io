waste-yard

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪstˌjɑrd / (AmE), / ˈweɪstˌjɑːd / (BrE)
An area of land designated for depositing or storing waste materials.
area for dumping waste materials

Examples

The city council decided to build a new waste-yard on the outskirts of town.
Hazardous materials must not be disposed of in an ordinary waste-yard.
Tourists were advised to avoid the waste-yard due to strong odors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dump - a site for depositing garbage or refuse
