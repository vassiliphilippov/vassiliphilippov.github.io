missionary position

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪʃəˌnɛri pəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɪʃənəri pəˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
a sexual position in which the man lies on top of the woman, facing her, during intercourse
man-on-top sexual position

Examples

Some couples prefer the missionary position because it allows for intimacy and eye contact.
The article discussed various advantages and disadvantages of the missionary position in sexual health.
Despite its reputation for simplicity, the missionary position is still popular among many people.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: woman-on-top position - sexual position with the woman on top during intercourse

Etymology

Said to derive from Christian missionaries' belief that this was the 'proper' sexual position, though this origin is likely apocryphal.
