coffee berry

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑfi ˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ˌbɛri / (BrE)
the fruit of the coffee tree containing the seed from which coffee is made
fruit of the coffee tree

Examples

Each coffee berry contains two seeds that become coffee beans after processing.
The coffee berry turns a deep red when it is ripe and ready for harvest.
Farmers carefully pick each coffee berry by hand to ensure quality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffee bean - seed of the coffee berry, used to make coffee
