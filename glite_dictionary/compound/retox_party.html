retox party

1. NOUN

/ ˈriː.tɑks ˌpɑːr.ti / (AmE), / ˈriː.tɒks ˌpɑː.ti / (BrE)
a social gathering where people indulge in food, drinks, or activities after completing a period of detoxification, often as a celebration
party after a detox period
informal

Examples

After a month of healthy eating, they held a retox party to celebrate.
Many people organize a retox party right after a strict detox regimen.
She invited her friends to a retox party filled with pizza and desserts.

Synset Examples

She enjoyed a retox party with friends after her cleanse.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: detox - a period of abstaining from unhealthy substances to cleanse the body

Etymology

From 'retox', a blend of 're-' and 'toxin', modeled after 'detox', and 'party'.
