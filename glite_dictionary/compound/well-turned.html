well-turned

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛl ˌtɝnd / (AmE), / ˈwɛl ˌtɜːnd / (BrE)
Having a gracefully shaped or pleasing form, especially in reference to parts of the body or expressions.
gracefully or attractively shaped
