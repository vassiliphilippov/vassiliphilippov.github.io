parcel bomb

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːr.səl bɑːm / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.səl bɒm / (BrE)
An explosive device concealed within a package or envelope, designed to detonate when opened.
explosive device hidden in a package

Examples

The police safely defused the parcel bomb before it could cause any harm.
She received a suspicious package, which was later confirmed to be a parcel bomb.
Security measures were increased after a parcel bomb was found at the office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: letter bomb - an explosive device sent in an envelope or package by mail
