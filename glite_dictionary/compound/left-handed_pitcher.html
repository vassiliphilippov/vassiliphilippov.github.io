left-handed pitcher

1. NOUN

/ lɛft ˈhændɪd ˈpɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / lɛft ˈhændɪd ˈpɪtʃə / (BrE)
A baseball pitcher who throws the ball with the left hand.
baseball pitcher who throws left-handed

Examples

The team signed a new left-handed pitcher to strengthen their rotation.
As a left-handed pitcher, he has an advantage against certain batters.
Many scouts are searching for young left-handed pitchers with strong fastballs.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: right-handed pitcher - a baseball pitcher who throws the ball with the right hand
