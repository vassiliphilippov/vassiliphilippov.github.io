clip artist

1. NOUN

/ klɪp ˈɑrtɪst / (AmE), / klɪp ˈɑːtɪst / (BrE)
a person who swindles or defrauds others, especially by overcharging or cheating
a swindler or cheat
informal

Examples

The unsuspecting tourist fell victim to a clip artist at the crowded market.
Police arrested the notorious clip artist after several complaints from local businesses.
She warned her friends to be careful of any clip artist looking for easy targets in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swindler - a person who cheats others out of money or assets
  • Synonym: con artist - a person who tricks others into giving up money or property

Etymology

From the verb 'clip' meaning to cheat or swindle, combined with 'artist' to denote expertise.
