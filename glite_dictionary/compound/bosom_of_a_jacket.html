bosom of a jacket

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊzəm əv ə ˈdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈbʊzəm əv ə ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
the inner part or pocket of a jacket, typically used to store or conceal small items
inner pocket or part of a jacket

Examples

He tucked the letter safely into the bosom of his jacket before leaving the house.
She found an old photograph hidden in the bosom of her jacket.
The spy slipped the microfilm into the bosom of his jacket without anyone noticing.

Synset Examples

He found a bird in the bosom of his jacket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inside pocket - a pocket sewn on the inside of a jacket or coat

Etymology

From 'bosom' meaning chest or breast, figuratively used for the inside part close to the chest.
