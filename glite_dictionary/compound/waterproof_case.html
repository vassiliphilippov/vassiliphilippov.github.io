waterproof case

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtərˌpruf keɪs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌpruːf keɪs / (BrE)
a protective container designed to prevent water from entering and damaging its contents
protective container resisting water

Examples

She bought a waterproof case to protect her camera during their kayaking trip.
The waterproof case kept his phone completely dry when it accidentally fell in the pool.
Make sure your documents are in a waterproof case before you go camping in the rain.

Synset Examples

I need a waterproof case for my phone at the beach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: case - a container or covering for protecting something
