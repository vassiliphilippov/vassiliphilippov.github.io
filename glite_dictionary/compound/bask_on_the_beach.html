to bask on the beach

1. VERB

/ bæsk ɑn ðə bitʃ / (AmE), / bɑːsk ɒn ðə biːtʃ / (BrE)
to lie or relax on a beach while enjoying the warmth of the sun
relax in the sun on a beach

Examples

We spent the afternoon basking on the beach with cold drinks and good books.
Every summer, tourists flock to Florida to bask on the beach.
She loves to bask on the beach for hours, listening to the sound of the waves.

Synset Examples

They decided to bask on the beach during vacation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to bask - to lie in and enjoy warmth and light, typically from the sun
