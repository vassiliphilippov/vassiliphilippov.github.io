pride of Barbados

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪd əv bɑrˈbeɪdəs / (AmE), / ˈpraɪd əv bɑːˈbeɪdɒs / (BrE)
a tropical shrub or small tree with showy yellow to orange-red flowers, Caesalpinia pulcherrima, widely grown as an ornamental plant
tropical flowering shrub, ornamental plant

Examples

The pride of Barbados brightened the garden with its vibrant red and yellow flowers.
Locals often plant pride of Barbados along roadsides for its ornamental appeal.
The delicate foliage of the pride of Barbados makes it a favorite among landscape designers.

Etymology

Named after the Caribbean island of Barbados, where the plant is notably cultivated.
