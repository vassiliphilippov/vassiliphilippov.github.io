Nappy Valley

1. PROPN

/ ˈnæpi ˈvæli / (AmE), / ˈnæpi ˈvæli / (BrE)
an urban or suburban area noted for being popular with young families and offering family-friendly amenities
family-friendly urban or suburban area
british

Examples

Many parents move to Nappy Valley for its excellent schools and community spirit.
Housing prices in Nappy Valley have risen due to its growing popularity with families.
The local council in Nappy Valley organizes regular family-friendly events throughout the year.

Etymology

Modelled after 'Napa Valley' (California), with 'nappy' (British English for diaper) referencing young children.
