divine revelation

1. NOUN

/ dəˈvaɪn ˌrɛvəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪn ˌrɛvəˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the communication of knowledge or truth to humans by a deity or supernatural power
knowledge revealed by a deity

Examples

The prophet claimed to have received a divine revelation while meditating in the desert.
Throughout history, many religions have been founded on principles of divine revelation.
Some theologians debate the interpretation of divine revelation in sacred texts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revelation - the act of revealing or disclosing

Etymology

From Latin 'divinus' (of a god) and 'revelatio' (disclosure).
