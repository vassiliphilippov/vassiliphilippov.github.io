peace symbol

1. NOUN

/ pis ˈsɪmbəl / (AmE), / piːs ˈsɪmbəl / (BrE)
A sign consisting of three lines within a circle, used to represent peace.
icon representing peace

Examples

The protesters carried banners with the peace symbol on them.
She wore a necklace featuring the iconic peace symbol.
The artist drew a large peace symbol on the mural to promote unity.

Synset Examples

In the 1950s the ‘peace sign’ as it is known today, was designed as the logo for the British Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament and adopted by anti-war and counterculture activists in the United States and elsewhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peace sign - another term for the peace symbol (three lines within a circle)
