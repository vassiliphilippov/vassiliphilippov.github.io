pumpkin-headed mask

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌmpkɪn ˌhɛdɪd mæsk / (AmE), / ˈpʌmpkɪn ˌhɛdɪd mɑːsk / (BrE)
a mask shaped or designed to resemble a pumpkin, especially worn during Halloween festivities
mask resembling a pumpkin for Halloween

Examples

He wore a pumpkin-headed mask to the Halloween party and scared his friends.
The store was selling a variety of pumpkin-headed masks for the upcoming festival.
Children love putting on a pumpkin-headed mask for trick-or-treating.

Synset Examples

He wore a pumpkin-headed mask to the Halloween dance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jack-o'-lantern - a lantern made from a hollowed-out pumpkin, often carved with a face for Halloween
