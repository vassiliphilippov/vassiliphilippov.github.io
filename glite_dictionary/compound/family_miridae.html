family Miridae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli maɪˈrɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli mɪˈrɪdɪi / (BrE)
A large family of insects known as leaf bugs, many of which are plant feeders; includes numerous species in the order Hemiptera.
a family of leaf bugs (Hemiptera)

Examples

The family Miridae includes over 10,000 species of plant-feeding insects.
Many members of the family Miridae are important agricultural pests due to their feeding habits.
Scientists recently discovered a new species in the family Miridae in tropical rainforest regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plant bug - a common name for members of the Miridae family

Etymology

From scientific Latin 'Miridae', based on the type genus 'Miris'.
