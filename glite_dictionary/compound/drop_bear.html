drop bear

1. NOUN

/ drɑp bɛr / (AmE), / drɒp beə / (BrE)
A mythical Australian creature said to drop from trees onto unsuspecting people, typically used to scare or amuse tourists.
mythical Australian tree-dwelling creature

Examples

Some Australians love to tell visitors wild stories about the dangerous drop bear.
The legend of the drop bear is a popular joke among locals.
I was warned to wear a hat in the bush, just in case a drop bear was nearby.

Synset Examples

Tourists laugh about the drop bear stories in Australia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bunyip - mythical Australian water-dwelling creature
