Guarani language

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡwɑːrəˈniː ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌɡwɑːrəˈniː ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the indigenous language spoken by the Guarani people of Paraguay, Bolivia, and neighboring regions
language of the Guarani people

Examples

Many Paraguayans speak both Spanish and Guarani language fluently.
The Guarani language is recognized as an official language of Paraguay.
Researchers are working to document and preserve the Guarani language for future generations.

Etymology

From Spanish 'guaraní', from the name of the Guarani people.
