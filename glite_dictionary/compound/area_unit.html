area unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛriə ˈjunɪt / (AmE), / ˈeəriə ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a standard unit used to measure the size of an area, such as square meters or acres
unit for measuring area

Examples

The hectare is a common area unit used in land measurement.
He converted the field’s size into a different area unit for the report.
Architects often work with the square meter as their standard area unit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: square meter - the SI base unit of area
  • Synonym: acre - a unit for measuring land area, commonly used in the UK and US
