Skinemax movie

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪnɪˌmæks ˈmuːvi / (AmE), / ˈskɪnɪˌmæks ˈmuːvi / (BrE)
An adult film, especially of a softcore nature, typically broadcast late at night on cable television channels such as Cinemax.
softcore adult film shown on cable TV
informal american

Examples

He stayed up late, flipping through channels, and ended up watching a Skinemax movie.
She laughed about how predictable the plots in every Skinemax movie tend to be.
Back in the 1990s, Skinemax movies were a running joke about cable television.

Synset Examples

He joked about how Skinemax movies are always predictable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: softcore - a film or media with sexual content not as explicit as hardcore pornography
  • Degree Higher: porn film - a film that depicts explicit sexual content
  • Synonym: Cinemax - the American cable television network that inspired the term

Etymology

Blend of 'skin' (slang for nudity or sex scenes) and 'Cinemax', an American cable television channel known for airing such movies late at night.
