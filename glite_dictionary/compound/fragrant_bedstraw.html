fragrant bedstraw

1. NOUN

/ ˈfreɪgrənt ˈbɛdˌstrɔ / (AmE), / ˈfreɪɡrənt ˈbɛdstrɔː / (BrE)
a perennial plant of the Old World, known for its pleasant fragrance, small white flowers, and narrow leaves; used in flavoring and sachets, and often cultivated as a ground cover
a fragrant perennial ground cover plant

Examples

The shady path was bordered with fragrant bedstraw, filling the air with its sweet scent.
Dried fragrant bedstraw can be used to perfume linens and clothes.
Gardeners often plant fragrant bedstraw as an attractive ground cover beneath trees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweet woodruff - a synonym for fragrant bedstraw, commonly used in gardening and herbalism
