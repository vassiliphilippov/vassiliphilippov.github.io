laugh line

1. NOUN

/ læf laɪn / (AmE), / lɑːf laɪn / (BrE)
a wrinkle that appears at the outer corner of the eyes, typically caused by frequent smiling or laughing
wrinkle near outer eye caused by smiling

Examples

As she smiled, the laugh lines became more pronounced around her eyes.
He considered his laugh lines a sign of a happy life.
Creams that claim to reduce the appearance of laugh lines are popular in the beauty industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crow's foot - a wrinkle or fine line at the outer corner of the eye

2. NOUN

/ ˈlæf ˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɑːf ˌlaɪn / (BrE)
the part of a joke or humorous story intended to provoke laughter, especially the punchline or main humorous point
main humorous point of a joke or story

Examples

The comedian paused for effect before delivering the laugh line that brought down the house.
She spent hours writing the perfect laugh line for her comedy routine.
Everyone waited expectantly, knowing the laugh line was about to come.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punchline - the final phrase or sentence of a joke or story, delivering the intended humor
