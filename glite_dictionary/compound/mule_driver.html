mule driver

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːl ˌdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˈmjuːl ˌdraɪvə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to drive or control mules, especially for transporting goods or in agriculture
person who drives mules

Examples

The mule driver led the pack animals through the narrow mountain pass.
In the early 20th century, a mule driver was an essential part of the mining operation.
The mule driver shouted instructions as the animals pulled the heavy cart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teamster - a person who drives a team of draft animals, especially horses or mules
