any time now

1. ADV

/ ˈɛni taɪm naʊ / (AmE), / ˈɛni taɪm naʊ / (BrE)
used to say that something is expected to happen very soon
expected very soon

Examples

The rain is about to start any time now.
She said she would call any time now, so I'm keeping my phone handy.
The package is arriving any time now, according to the tracking update.

Synset Examples

The pizza will be here any time now.
The train should arrive any time now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soon - in or after a short time
