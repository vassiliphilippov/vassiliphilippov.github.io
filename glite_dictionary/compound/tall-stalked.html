tall-stalked

1. ADJ

/ tɔl ˈstɔkt / (AmE), / tɔːl ˈstɔːkt / (BrE)
(of a plant) having a relatively long stalk.
having a long stalk (in plants)

Examples

The tall-stalked sunflowers swayed gently in the afternoon breeze.
Many gardeners prefer tall-stalked varieties for their striking appearance in flowerbeds.
This species is known for its tall-stalked blooms, which can reach over a meter in height.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: short-stalked - having a relatively short stalk (in plants)
