strike leader

1. NOUN

/ ˈstraɪk ˌliːdər / (AmE), / ˈstraɪk ˌliːdə / (BrE)
a person who leads and organizes a workers' strike
person who leads a workers' strike

Examples

The strike leader addressed the crowd to outline their demands.
Union members rallied behind their strike leader during the negotiations.
After the protest, the company agreed to meet with the strike leader to discuss terms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strike - a period when workers stop working to protest
  • Antonym: strikebreaker - a person who works or is employed in place of others who are on strike
