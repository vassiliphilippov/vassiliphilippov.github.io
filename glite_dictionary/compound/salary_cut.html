salary cut

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæləri kʌt / (AmE), / ˈsaləri kʌt / (BrE)
a reduction in the amount of pay or wages received by an employee
a reduction in pay

Examples

After the company merger, many employees faced a salary cut to help reduce expenses.
The new policy would result in a salary cut for all managerial staff.
She accepted the job despite the significant salary cut compared to her previous position.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pay cut - a reduction in the amount of pay or wages
