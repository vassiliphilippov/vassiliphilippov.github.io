genus Capra

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈkæp.rə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈkæp.rə / (BrE)
a genus of mammals in the family Bovidae that includes true goats, such as the domestic goat and wild goats
the genus of goats

Examples

The genus Capra includes both wild and domestic species of goats.
Researchers studied the adaptations present within the genus Capra to understand their survival in harsh environments.
The Alpine ibex is a member of the genus Capra, native to the European Alps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goat - a domesticated ruminant mammal kept for milk, meat, and wool
  • Antonym: sheep - a domesticated ruminant animal related to goats

Etymology

From Latin 'Capra' meaning goat, used in scientific taxonomy.
