wagon tire

1. NOUN

/ ˈwæɡən ˌtaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈwæɡən ˌtaɪə / (BrE)
a metal hoop fitted around a wagon wheel to form its tread and protect the wheel
metal hoop on a wagon wheel

Examples

The blacksmith heated the wagon tire before placing it onto the wooden wheel.
During the restoration, we had to replace every wagon tire on the old carriage.
Early pioneers relied on the strength of a solid wagon tire to survive rough terrain.
