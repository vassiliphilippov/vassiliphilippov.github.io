travel mug

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəl mʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl mʌɡ / (BrE)
A portable, insulated cup designed for carrying beverages while traveling.
insulated portable cup for drinks

Examples

She poured her tea into a travel mug before leaving for work.
His travel mug kept his coffee warm during the long commute.
I forgot my travel mug at home and had to buy a disposable cup.

Synset Examples

She always carries a travel mug for her coffee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermos - a container that keeps liquids hot or cold for long periods
