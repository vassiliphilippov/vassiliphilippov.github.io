unmannerly remark

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈmænɚli ɹiˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ʌnˈmænəli rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
a comment that is rude or impolite, often lacking respect or consideration for others
rude or impolite comment

Examples

The manager scolded him for making an unmannerly remark in front of the client.
Her unmannerly remark offended everyone at the dinner table.
He regretted his unmannerly remark immediately after he saw the hurt look on her face.

Synset Examples

She was criticized for her unmannerly remarks during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rude comment - a comment lacking in manners or respect
  • Antonym: polite remark - a comment that is respectful and shows good manners
