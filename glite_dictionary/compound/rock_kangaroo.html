rock kangaroo

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈkæŋ.ɡəˌru / (AmE), / rɒk ˈkæŋ.ɡə.ruː / (BrE)
a slender, long-legged wallaby native to Australia that typically lives in caves and rocky areas
Australian wallaby living in rocky areas

Examples

We spotted a rock kangaroo cleverly blending into the boulders near the gorge.
The rock kangaroo is well-adapted to leaping between steep rocks and cliffs.
Researchers are studying threats to the endangered habitats of rock kangaroos in Western Australia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wallaby - a small or medium-sized kangaroo native to Australia and neighboring islands
