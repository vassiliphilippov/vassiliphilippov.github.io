puffer jacket

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌfər ˌdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈpʌfə ˌdʒækɪt / (BrE)
A warm jacket filled with down or synthetic insulation, featuring quilted sections that give it a puffy appearance.
quilted insulated jacket

Examples

When temperatures dropped below freezing, she reached for her favorite puffer jacket.
His red puffer jacket made him easy to spot on the slopes.
The store displayed a range of puffer jackets in bold winter colors.

Synset Examples

She wore a pink puffer jacket on the chilly day.
He loves his puffer jacket during winter hikes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: down jacket - a jacket insulated with the soft feathers of birds, especially ducks or geese
