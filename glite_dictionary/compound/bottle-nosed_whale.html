bottle-nosed whale

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑtəlˌnoʊzd weɪl / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəlˌnəʊzd weɪl / (BrE)
A beaked whale found in the northern Atlantic, characterized by a bulbous forehead.
Atlantic beaked whale with bulbous forehead

Examples

A large bottle-nosed whale was spotted off the coast of Scotland last week.
Marine biologists study the unique echolocation abilities of the bottle-nosed whale.
The bottle-nosed whale is known for its distinctive bulbous forehead and deep-diving skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beaked whale - a whale of the family Ziphiidae, characterized by a beaklike snout
