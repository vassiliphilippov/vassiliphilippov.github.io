yellow mountain saxifrage

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˌmaʊntən ˈsæksɪfrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˌmaʊntən ˈsæksɪfrɪdʒ / (BrE)
A tufted evergreen perennial plant with hairy-edged leaves and yellow corymbed flowers, often with orange spots, found in mountains.
Alpine perennial plant with yellow flowers

Examples

We identified the yellow mountain saxifrage growing along the rocky slope of the northern trail.
The delicate blossoms of the yellow mountain saxifrage brighten the alpine meadow each spring.
She included yellow mountain saxifrage in her sketchbook of rare mountain flora.

Etymology

From Latin saxifraga (rock-breaker), referring to the plant's growth among rocks, with 'yellow' and 'mountain' describing its flowers and typical habitat.
