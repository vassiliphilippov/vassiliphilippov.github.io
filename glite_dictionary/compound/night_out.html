night out

1. NOUN

/ naɪt aʊt / (AmE), / naɪt aʊt / (BrE)
an evening spent outside the home socializing or entertaining, especially at bars, restaurants, or events
evening spent socializing or entertaining

Examples

We're planning a night out to celebrate her birthday.
After a stressful week, he needed a fun night out with friends.
A spontaneous night out often leads to the best memories.

Synset Examples

She enjoyed a night out with friends at the bar.
The Essex man often enjoys a night out with friends.
We had an amazing night out at the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: night on the town - an evening spent out enjoying entertainment or social activities
