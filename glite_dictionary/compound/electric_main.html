electric main

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk meɪn / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk meɪn / (BrE)
a principal pipe or line that distributes electricity from a power supply to consumers or regions
main distribution line for electricity

Examples

The city upgraded its electric main to ensure a more reliable power supply.
A fault in the electric main caused a significant blackout across several neighborhoods.
Maintenance workers inspected the electric main after residents reported frequent power outages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power line - a cable carrying electrical power
  • Synonym: water main - a principal pipe in a water supply system
