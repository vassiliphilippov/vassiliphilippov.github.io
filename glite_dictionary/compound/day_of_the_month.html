day of the month

1. NOUN

/ deɪ əv ðə mʌnθ / (AmE), / deɪ ɒv ðə mʌnθ / (BrE)
the particular numbered day within a given month as specified on a calendar
a specific calendar day within a month

Examples

Please write your signature and the day of the month at the bottom of the page.
Rent is due on the same day of the month every month.
She asked him to remind her the day of the month their anniversary falls on.
