goofproof recipe

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːfˌpruːf ˈrɛsəpi / (AmE), / ˈɡuːfˌpruːf ˈrɛsəpi / (BrE)
A recipe designed to be very easy to follow, making it almost impossible to make mistakes.
very easy, mistake-proof recipe

Examples

She chose a goofproof recipe for her first attempt at baking bread.
This cookbook is full of goofproof recipes perfect for beginners.
Even my little brother can follow a goofproof recipe without any help.

Synset Examples

With this goofproof recipe, anyone can bake a cake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolproof - impossible to get wrong or make errors with

Etymology

Formed from 'goofproof' (meaning protected against simple mistakes) and 'recipe'.
