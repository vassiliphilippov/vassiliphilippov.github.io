Memorial Day

1. PROPN

/ məˈmɔɹi.əl deɪ / (AmE), / məˈmɔː.ri.əl deɪ / (BrE)
A legal holiday in the United States observed on the last Monday in May, commemorating members of the United States armed forces who died in war.
U.S. holiday honoring fallen soldiers
american

Examples

On Memorial Day, many people visit cemeteries to honor fallen soldiers.
The parade held on Memorial Day attracts thousands of spectators each year.
She placed flowers on her grandfather's grave for Memorial Day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Veterans Day - U.S. holiday honoring all military veterans; observed on November 11
