rough-grained fabric

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌf ɡreɪnd ˈfæbrɪk / (AmE), / ˈrʌf ɡreɪnd ˈfæbrɪk / (BrE)
Fabric characterized by a coarse or uneven texture, often feeling slightly rough to the touch.
fabric with a coarse texture

Examples

The coat was made from a rough-grained fabric that provided extra warmth during winter.
Many upholstery materials use rough-grained fabric to create a rustic look.
She disliked wearing rough-grained fabric because it irritated her skin.

Synset Examples

She preferred rough-grained fabrics for a more natural feel.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smooth-grained fabric - fabric with a fine, even texture
