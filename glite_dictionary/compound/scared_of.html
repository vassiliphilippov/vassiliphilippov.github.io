scared of

1. ADJ

/ skɛrd ʌv / (AmE), / skɛəd ɒv / (BrE)
frightened or afraid of someone or something
frightened or afraid of

Examples

Many children are scared of the dark and need a night-light to sleep.
She admitted she was scared of flying and avoided airplanes whenever possible.
The puppy was scared of loud noises and hid under the bed during thunderstorms.

Synset Examples

The cat is hiding in a corner, scared of thunder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: afraid of - feeling fear or apprehension about someone or something
