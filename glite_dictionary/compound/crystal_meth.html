crystal meth

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪstl mɛθ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstl mɛθ / (BrE)
a powerful, illegal stimulant drug in the form of clear crystals, chemically known as methamphetamine
illegal stimulant drug (methamphetamine)

Examples

Police seized a large quantity of crystal meth during the drug bust.
Many communities have struggled with the rise of crystal meth addiction.
He was arrested for the possession of crystal meth with intent to distribute.

Synset Examples

He struggled with addiction to crystal meth for years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: methamphetamine - a synthetic stimulant drug
  • Synonym: amphetamine - a stimulant drug related to methamphetamine

Etymology

Shortened from 'crystal methamphetamine', referring to its crystalline form.
