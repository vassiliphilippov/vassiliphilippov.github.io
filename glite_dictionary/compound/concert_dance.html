concert dance

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːn.sɚt dæns / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.sət dɑːns / (BrE)
A style of dance performed on stage as a theatrical art form, usually choreographed and presented to an audience, often as ballet or modern dance.
stage dance performance as art

Examples

She trained for years to perform concert dance at prestigious theaters around the world.
Many choreographers push the boundaries of concert dance by blending different styles and techniques.
Attending a concert dance performance can be a deeply moving artistic experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ballet - a classical style of concert dance with formalized steps and gestures
