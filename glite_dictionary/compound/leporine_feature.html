leporine feature

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛpəˌraɪn ˈfitʃər / (AmE), / ˈlɛpəriːn ˈfiːtʃə / (BrE)
a characteristic or trait resembling that of a rabbit, especially in appearance or expression
rabbit-like characteristic

Examples

The artist skillfully captured the leporine feature of the animal's ears in her drawing.
His gentle face had a distinctly leporine feature that made him look soft and approachable.
In fairy tales, characters with a leporine feature are often depicted as timid but clever.

Synset Examples

The leporine features of the sculpture captured my attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rabbit-like - resembling a rabbit
  • Antonym: vulpine feature - a feature resembling a fox

Etymology

From Latin 'leporinus' (of a hare/rabbit) + English 'feature'.
