fruity cocktail

1. NOUN

/ ˈfruːti ˈkɑkˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈfruːti ˈkɒkˌteɪl / (BrE)
A sweet, fruit-flavored alcoholic beverage, often made with fruit juices or liqueurs.
sweet fruit-flavored alcoholic drink

Examples

I ordered a fruity cocktail with pineapple and coconut at the bar.
Her favorite drink at summer parties is a fruity cocktail served over ice.
Many tropical resorts are known for their signature fruity cocktail recipes.

Synset Examples

She prefers fruity cocktails over traditional ones.
Everyone loved the fruity locktail he made.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cocktail - an alcoholic mixed drink
