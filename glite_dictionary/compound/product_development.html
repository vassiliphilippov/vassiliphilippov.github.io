product development

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑː.dʌkt dɪˌvɛl.əp.mənt / (AmE), / ˈprɒd.ʌkt dɪˌvɛl.əp.mənt / (BrE)
the process of creating new products or improving existing ones, especially in business or manufacturing
process of creating or improving products

Examples

The company invested heavily in product development to stay ahead of its competitors.
Effective product development requires understanding customer needs and emerging market trends.
She works in product development, designing the next generation of smartphones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: innovation - the introduction of new ideas, methods, or products
  • Synonym: research and development - work directed toward the innovation and improvement of products and processes
