pot farm

1. NOUN

/ pɑt fɑrm / (AmE), / pɒt fɑːm / (BrE)
A plot of land where marijuana is grown and harvested, often secretly.
marijuana cultivation site

Examples

Authorities discovered a hidden pot farm deep in the mountains last year.
The documentary exposed the risks faced by workers on illegal pot farms.
Many national parks are struggling to control the spread of clandestine pot farms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marijuana farm - A farm for growing marijuana plants

Etymology

From 'pot', a slang term for marijuana, and 'farm', meaning a place for cultivation.
