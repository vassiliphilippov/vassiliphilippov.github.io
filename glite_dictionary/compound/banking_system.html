banking system

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæŋkɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈbæŋkɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
all the banks in a country or region, considered collectively as an organized system for managing money and financial transactions
all banks in a country or region

Examples

After the crisis, the government took steps to stabilize the banking system.
International regulations are designed to ensure the banking system remains secure and transparent.
She studied the impact of digital technology on the banking system.
