crazy house

1. NOUN

/ ˈkreɪzi haʊs / (AmE), / ˈkreɪzi haʊs / (BrE)
A slang and often derogatory term for a psychiatric hospital or asylum.
slang for psychiatric hospital
slang offensive

Examples

The old crazy house on the edge of town has been abandoned for years.
He joked about being sent to the crazy house after finals week.
Many people now recognize that using terms like crazy house to describe psychiatric hospitals is inappropriate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asylum - a hospital for people with mental illnesses
  • Synonym: mental hospital - hospital for the treatment of mental illness
