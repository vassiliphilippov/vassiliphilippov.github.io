wedding guest

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ ɡɛst / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ ɡɛst / (BrE)
a person who has been invited to attend a wedding ceremony and/or its related celebrations
person invited to a wedding

Examples

The wedding guest wore a beautiful blue dress to the ceremony.
As a wedding guest, you are expected to RSVP ahead of time.
Each wedding guest received a small favor as a thank you for attending.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guest - a person invited to visit or stay at someone's home or attend an event
