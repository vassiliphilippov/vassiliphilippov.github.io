for the weekend

1. ADP

/ fər ðə ˈwiːkˌend / (AmE), / fə ðə ˈwiːkˌend / (BrE)
used to refer to something happening or lasting during the weekend period, usually from Friday evening to Sunday night.
during the weekend period

Examples

We're visiting my parents for the weekend to celebrate my dad's birthday.
They rented a cabin for the weekend to escape the city.
Are you staying in town for the weekend or going somewhere?

Synset Examples

We plan to go to Prestwich for the weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: over the weekend - during the course of the weekend
