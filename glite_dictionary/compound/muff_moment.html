muff moment

1. NOUN

/ mʌf ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / mʌf ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
An instance in which someone makes a clumsy mistake or blunder.
a moment of making a blunder
informal

Examples

He had a muff moment during his presentation when he forgot his lines.
Dropping the cake right before serving was definitely my biggest muff moment this year.
We all have an occasional muff moment, so don't worry about it.

Synset Examples

I had a muff moment when I dropped my phone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blunder - a stupid or careless mistake
  • Synonym: gaffe - an unintentional act or remark causing embarrassment

Etymology

From 'muff', meaning to bungle or make a mess of something, origin unclear but in use since the 19th century.
