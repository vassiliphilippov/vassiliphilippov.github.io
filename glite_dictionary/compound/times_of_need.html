times of need

1. NOUN

/ taɪmz əv nid / (AmE), / taɪmz əv niːd / (BrE)
periods or situations in which assistance or support is required
periods when help is needed

Examples

Friends became especially important during times of need.
Charities provide essential support to families in times of need.
In times of need, communities often come together to help one another.

Synset Examples

In times of need, she turns to the good doctor.
