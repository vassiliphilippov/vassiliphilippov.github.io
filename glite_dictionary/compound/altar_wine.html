altar wine

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔltɚ waɪn / (AmE), / ˈɔːltə waɪn / (BrE)
Wine that is used during a Christian communion service or Eucharist.
wine used in communion services

Examples

The church keeps its supply of altar wine in a special cabinet.
Only a small amount of altar wine is needed for the ceremony.
He was asked to prepare the altar wine for Sunday mass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communion wine - wine used in the Christian sacrament of communion
