bunko game

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌŋkoʊ ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋkəʊ ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A swindle involving cheating at gambling or persuading someone to buy worthless property.
a swindling scheme or fraud

Examples

The police warned citizens about the bunko game targeting the elderly in the neighborhood.
He lost thousands of dollars in a bunko game that promised huge returns.
Authorities launched a crackdown on bunko games operating in the downtown area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: con game - an act of cheating or tricking someone to get money illegally
  • Synonym: swindle - to cheat someone out of money or property

Etymology

From American English 'bunco' (fraud, swindle), possibly from Spanish 'banco' (bank, bench).
