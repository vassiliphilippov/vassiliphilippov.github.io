reed instrument

1. NOUN

/ rid ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / riːd ˈɪnstrʊmənt / (BrE)
a musical instrument that produces sound by means of a vibrating reed, such as a clarinet or saxophone
musical instrument with vibrating reed

Examples

The clarinet is a popular reed instrument in both jazz and classical music.
Many children learn to play a reed instrument in their school band.
The oboe, another reed instrument, has a distinct and penetrating sound.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: woodwind - a family of musical instruments including reed and non-reed instruments
