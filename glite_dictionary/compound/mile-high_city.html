Mile-High City

1. PROPN

/ maɪl haɪ ˈsɪti / (AmE), / maɪl haɪ ˈsɪti / (BrE)
a nickname for Denver, the state capital and largest city of Colorado, named for its elevation of approximately one mile above sea level
nickname for Denver, Colorado

Examples

Many people move to the Mile-High City for its vibrant culture and outdoor lifestyle.
The Mile-High City boasts numerous parks and stunning views of the Rocky Mountains.
She has always dreamed of living in the Mile-High City because of its mild climate and booming tech industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Denver - the capital and largest city of Colorado

Etymology

Refers to Denver's elevation of approximately one mile (5,280 feet) above sea level.
