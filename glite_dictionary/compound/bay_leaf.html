bay leaf

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ ˌliːf / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ ˌliːf / (BrE)
the dried aromatic leaf of the bay laurel, used to flavor soups, stews, and other dishes
dried aromatic leaf for cooking

Examples

Remove the bay leaf from the soup before serving.
She added a bay leaf to the stew for extra flavor.
The recipe calls for two dried bay leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laurel - an evergreen shrub or tree with aromatic leaves used in cooking
