parking area

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːrkɪŋ ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkɪŋ ˌeəriə / (BrE)
An area or lot designated for parking vehicles, especially cars.
area designated for parking vehicles

Examples

I found a free parking area near the entrance to the museum.
The hotel offers a large parking area for its guests.
Please do not leave your vehicle outside the designated parking area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parking lot - an area where vehicles can be parked, especially outdoors
  • Synonym: car park - an area for parking cars, primarily used in British English
