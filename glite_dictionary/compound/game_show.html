game show

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪm ˌʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪm ˌʃəʊ / (BrE)
a television or radio program in which people compete in games or quizzes for prizes
television or radio competition program

Examples

She was a contestant on a popular game show and won a new car.
Many families enjoy watching a game show together in the evening.
The game show featured a lightning round where players could double their earnings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quiz show - a television or radio program where people answer questions for prizes
