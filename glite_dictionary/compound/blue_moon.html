blue moon

1. NOUN

/ bluː muːn / (AmE), / bluː muːn / (BrE)
a very rare event or occurrence
very rare event or occurrence

Examples

I only eat chocolate cake on a blue moon—it's a special treat for rare occasions.
Major scientific breakthroughs like this happen once in a blue moon.
A promotion in this company is as rare as a blue moon.

Synset Examples

We'll only meet again when pigs fly or a blue moon occurs.
He pointed to the glowing blue-moon in the sky.
It feels like a blue moon since we last saw each other.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: once in a blue moon - very rarely; not often

Etymology

From the phrase 'once in a blue moon', referring to the rare occurrence of a second full moon in a calendar month.
