automated teller

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔːtəˌmeɪtɪd ˈtɛlər / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəməˌteɪtɪd ˈtelə / (BrE)
a machine, typically located outside banks, that dispenses cash and performs basic banking transactions when used with a personal coded card
cash-dispensing banking machine

Examples

The automated teller outside the supermarket was out of service last night.
You can withdraw cash from any automated teller if you have a debit card.
Many people prefer using an automated teller for quick banking transactions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ATM - automated teller machine, the common abbreviation

Etymology

From 'automated' (made to operate by automation) and 'teller' (a person who receives or pays out money at a bank).
