attention deficit disorder

1. NOUN

/ əˈtɛnʃən ˈdɛfəsət dɪsˈɔrdər / (AmE), / əˈtɛnʃən ˈdɛfəsɪt dɪsˈɔːdə / (BrE)
A neurodevelopmental disorder characterized by persistent patterns of inattention and, in some cases, hyperactivity and impulsivity that interfere with functioning or development.
neurodevelopmental disorder causing inattention
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed him with attention deficit disorder after months of observing his behavior.
Children with attention deficit disorder may struggle to focus on schoolwork or follow instructions.
Effective treatments are available for people living with attention deficit disorder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attention deficit hyperactivity disorder - Neurodevelopmental disorder with both inattention and hyperactivity-impulsivity
