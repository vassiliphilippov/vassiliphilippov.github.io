rosy boa

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊzi ˈboʊ.ə / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzi ˈbəʊ.ə / (BrE)
a species of boa snake (Lichanura trivirgata) found in rocky deserts of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico, known for its distinctive pinkish or rosy coloring
species of desert boa snake

Examples

The rosy boa is one of the few boa species native to the United States.
Because of its docile nature, the rosy boa is a popular choice among reptile enthusiasts.
We spotted a rosy boa slithering among the rocks during our desert hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boa - a large constrictor snake

Etymology

From 'rosy' (pinkish color) and 'boa' (a type of snake, from Latin 'boa').
