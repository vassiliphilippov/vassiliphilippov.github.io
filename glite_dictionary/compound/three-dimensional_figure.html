three-dimensional figure

1. NOUN

/ θri dɪˈmɛnʃənəl ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / θriː dɪˈmenʃənəl ˈfɪɡə / (BrE)
A shape or object that has height, width, and depth, and can be described using three dimensions in geometry.
a geometric shape with three dimensions

Examples

A cube is a classic example of a three-dimensional figure in geometry.
Students learned how to calculate the volume of different three-dimensional figures such as spheres and cylinders.
Unlike a square, which is flat, a three-dimensional figure has depth in addition to height and width.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solid - a three-dimensional geometric figure; a solid object in geometry
