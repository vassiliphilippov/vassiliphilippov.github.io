blazing fire

1. NOUN

/ ˈbleɪzɪŋ ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈbleɪzɪŋ ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
a fire burning with great intensity or brightness
very intense, bright fire

Examples

The blazing fire lit up the entire night sky.
She watched in horror as the blazing fire consumed their barn.
Villagers gathered around the blazing fire to keep warm during the cold winter night.

Synset Examples

Firefighters worked to extinguish the blazing fire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inferno - a large and dangerous fire
  • Synonym: raging fire - a very fierce and uncontrollable fire
