spiny-backed

1. ADJ

/ ˈspaɪni ˌbækt / (AmE), / ˈspaɪni ˌbækt / (BrE)
having the back covered with spines or sharp projections
having a back covered with spines

Examples

The spiny-backed orb-weaver is a small but distinctive spider found in gardens.
We observed a spiny-backed lizard basking in the sun on the rock.
The scientist wore gloves while handling the spiny-backed fish to avoid injury.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiny - covered with or having spines
