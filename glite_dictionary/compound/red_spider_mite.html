red spider mite

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈspaɪdər maɪt / (AmE), / rɛd ˈspaɪdə maɪt / (BrE)
a small, web-spinning mite that is a serious pest of orchards and other plants, known for damaging leaves by feeding on their sap
small web-spinning plant pest mite

Examples

The gardener discovered a severe infestation of red spider mites on her tomato plants.
Orchard owners take preventive measures against red spider mites every spring.
Leaves infested by the red spider mite often turn yellow and fall off prematurely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mite - tiny arachnid in the subclass Acari, often a plant pest
