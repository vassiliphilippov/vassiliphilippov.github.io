motor lodge

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊtər lɑːdʒ / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə lɒdʒ / (BrE)
a hotel designed for motorists, typically providing direct access from rooms to the parking area
hotel for motorists with parking access

Examples

We decided to stay at a nearby motor lodge because it was affordable and convenient.
The motor lodge had parking spaces right in front of each room for easy access.
After a long day of driving, they were relieved to find a clean motor lodge on the highway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motel - a roadside hotel designed for motorists, with parking outside rooms

Etymology

Formed from 'motor' (referring to motor vehicles) and 'lodge' (a type of accommodation).
