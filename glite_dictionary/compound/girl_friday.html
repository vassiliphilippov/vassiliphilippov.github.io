girl Friday

1. NOUN

/ ɡɜrl ˈfraɪ.deɪ / (AmE), / ɡɜːl ˈfraɪ.deɪ / (BrE)
a female assistant who helps with a wide variety of tasks, especially in an office or business setting
female assistant with varied duties

Examples

After the company expanded, they hired a girl Friday to help with scheduling and communications.
As the manager's girl Friday, she handled everything from phone calls to organizing files.
He said his girl Friday was indispensable for keeping the office running smoothly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: man Friday - a male assistant with varied duties

Etymology

From the character 'Man Friday' in Daniel Defoe's novel 'Robinson Crusoe' (1719), later adapted to refer to a female assistant.
