Mr. Moto

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɪstər ˈmoʊtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌmɪstə ˈməʊtəʊ / (BrE)
A fictional Japanese detective created by the American author John Marquand, featured in novels and films.
fictional Japanese detective character

Examples

The film adaptation brought Mr. Moto's adventures to a much wider audience.
Many readers admire Mr. Moto for his intelligence and unpredictable nature.
