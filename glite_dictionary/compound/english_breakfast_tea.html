English breakfast tea

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈbrɛkfəst ti / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈbrɛkfəst tiː / (BrE)
a strong blend of black teas, typically consumed with milk and sugar, commonly served at breakfast in Britain
a strong black tea blend for breakfast
british
