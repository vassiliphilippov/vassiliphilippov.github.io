Joint Direct Attack Munition

1. NOUN

/ dʒɔɪnt dəˈrɛkt əˈtæk məˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / dʒɔɪnt dɪˈrɛkt əˈtæk mjʊˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
a U.S. military guidance kit attached to a gravity bomb to convert it into a precision-guided smart bomb
U.S. bomb guidance kit

Examples

The Joint Direct Attack Munition allows conventional bombs to hit targets with pinpoint accuracy.
After being fitted with a Joint Direct Attack Munition kit, the aircraft's payload became far more versatile.
The military plans to upgrade its arsenal with more Joint Direct Attack Munitions for precision strikes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smart bomb - bomb equipped with guidance systems for precision targeting
  • Antonym: dumb bomb - unguided gravity bomb; the opposite of smart bomb

Etymology

Joint (military usage for combined service); direct attack (immediate strike on target); munition (from Latin 'munitionem', fortification, defense).
