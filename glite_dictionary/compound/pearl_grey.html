pearl grey

1. ADJ

/ pɜrl ɡreɪ / (AmE), / pɜːl ɡreɪ / (BrE)
a pale grey color with a soft, lustrous or pearly appearance
a pale, lustrous grey color

Examples

The walls were painted a soft pearl grey that brightened the room.
She wore a pearl grey dress to the wedding, which looked elegant under the lights.
His new car is a sleek pearl grey, making it stand out from standard silver.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gray - of the color between black and white
  • Degree Higher: silver - metallic, shiny gray color
