ethically challenged

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛθɪkli tʃæˈlɛndʒd / (AmE), / ˈɛθɪkəli tʃəˈlɛndʒd / (BrE)
lacking strong moral principles or prone to questionable ethical behavior.
lacking strong moral principles

Examples

The committee refused to hire anyone they considered ethically challenged.
Many believe that the new policy favors the ethically challenged over honest business owners.
Concerns were raised when the project was handed to an ethically challenged contractor.

Synset Examples

He faced backlash for his ethically challenged decisions.

Related Terms
