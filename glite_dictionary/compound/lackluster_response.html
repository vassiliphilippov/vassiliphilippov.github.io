lackluster response

1. NOUN

/ ˈlækˌlʌstər rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / ˈlakˌlʌstə rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
a reaction or reply that is dull, uninspiring, or shows little enthusiasm
uninspiring or dull reaction

Examples

The proposal was met with a lackluster response from investors.
Despite the hype, the film's premiere earned a lackluster response from critics.
Her announcement drew only a lackluster response from the crowd.

Synset Examples

They received a lackluster response from the audience.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: enthusiastic response - a reaction that is very positive and energetic
