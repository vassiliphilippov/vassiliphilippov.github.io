striptease artist

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɪp.tiz ˌɑr.tɪst / (AmE), / ˈstrɪp.tiːz ˌɑː.tɪst / (BrE)
a performer who entertains an audience by slowly undressing, often to music, in an erotic manner
erotic performer who undresses to music

Examples

The striptease artist captivated the audience with her graceful performance.
He began his career as a striptease artist in downtown clubs.
The club advertised a famous striptease artist for its Saturday night show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stripper - a person who removes their clothes as entertainment
