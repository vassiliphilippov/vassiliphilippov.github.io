bottom of the inning

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːɾəm əv ði ˈɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəm əv ði ˈɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the second half of an inning in baseball, when the home team is at bat
second half of a baseball inning
american

Examples

The home run in the bottom of the inning secured the team's victory.
They rallied in the bottom of the inning to tie the game.
The pitcher struggled during the bottom of the inning, allowing three runs.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: top of the inning - the first half of an inning in baseball, when the visiting team is at bat
