tansy-leaved rocket

1. NOUN

/ ˈtænzi liːvd ˈrɑːkɪt / (AmE), / ˈtænzi liːvd ˈrɒkɪt / (BrE)
a perennial herb with stellate hairs and small yellow flowers, native to the mountains of southern Europe; sometimes classified in the genus Sisymbrium
a mountain herb with yellow flowers, southern Europe

Examples

The tansy-leaved rocket grows abundantly in rocky alpine meadows.
Botanists sometimes debate whether the tansy-leaved rocket belongs to the genus Sisymbrium.
The hairy leaves of tansy-leaved rocket help it conserve moisture in its mountainous habitat.
