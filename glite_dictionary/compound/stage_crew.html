stage crew

1. NOUN

/ steɪdʒ kru / (AmE), / steɪdʒ kruː / (BrE)
the group of workers responsible for moving scenery and handling props in a theatrical production
theatre workers who move scenery and props

Examples

The stage crew worked quietly behind the scenes during the play.
Without a dedicated stage crew, the production would not have run so smoothly.
She joined the stage crew in college and learned how to operate the lighting equipment.
