Army of the Righteous

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrmi əv ðə ˈraɪtʃəs / (AmE), / ˈɑːmi əv ðə ˈraɪtʃəs / (BrE)
A militant Islamic terrorist group, primarily active in Kashmir, that fights against India with the stated aim of restoring Islamic rule over the region.
Islamic militant terrorist group in Kashmir

Examples

The Army of the Righteous has been implicated in several deadly attacks in Kashmir.
Many countries have designated the Army of the Righteous as a terrorist organization.
Authorities are increasing security due to threats allegedly made by the Army of the Righteous.

Synset Examples

Lashkar-e-Toiba has committed mass murders of civilian Hindus
