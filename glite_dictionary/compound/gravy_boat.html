gravy boat

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪvi boʊt / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪvi bəʊt / (BrE)
A long, boat-shaped serving dish with a spout, used for pouring gravy or sauce.
dish for serving gravy or sauce

Examples

She poured the homemade gravy into the gravy boat before setting it on the table.
We passed the gravy boat around so everyone could add sauce to their potatoes.
That antique gravy boat has been in our family for generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sauceboat - a small dish with a spout for serving sauces
