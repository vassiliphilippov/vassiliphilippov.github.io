sapodilla plum

1. NOUN

/ ˌsæpəˈdɪlə plʌm / (AmE), / ˌsæpəˈdɪlə plʌm / (BrE)
A tropical fruit with rough brownish skin and very sweet brown flesh, also known simply as sapodilla.
tropical fruit with sweet brown flesh

Examples

The sapodilla plum is prized in many tropical countries for its sweet, malty flavor.
She tasted the unfamiliar sapodilla plum and was surprised by its caramel-like sweetness.
Farmers harvest sapodilla plums when the fruit’s skin turns slightly soft and brown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sapodilla - the same fruit; alternate, more common name

Etymology

From Spanish 'zapote' (sapote, a type of fruit) and possibly from the original Taíno word 'zapodilla'.
