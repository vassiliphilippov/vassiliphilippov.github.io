data processor

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ.tə ˌproʊ.ses.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ.tə ˌprəʊ.ses.ə / (BrE)
A machine or device that performs calculations or manipulates data automatically, often as part of a computer system.
machine for automatic data calculation

Examples

Our company recently upgraded its main data processor to improve performance and reliability.
The data processor automatically handles millions of transactions every second.
Early computers served primarily as massive data processors for government and scientific research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: computer - an electronic device for storing and processing data
