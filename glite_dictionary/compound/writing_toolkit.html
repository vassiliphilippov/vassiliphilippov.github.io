writing toolkit

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtɪŋ ˈtʊlˌkɪt / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋ ˈtʊl.kɪt / (BrE)
a set of aids and materials designed to support writing, such as guides, templates, or reference tools
collection of writing resources and tools

Examples

Every author has a unique writing toolkit to help inspire creativity and overcome writer's block.
The new online course provides students with a comprehensive writing toolkit for academic essays.
She added grammar guides and style manuals to her writing toolkit for quick reference.

Synset Examples

His writing toolkit includes various resources and tools.
