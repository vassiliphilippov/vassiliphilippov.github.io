band saw

1. NOUN

/ bænd sɔ / (AmE), / bænd sɔː / (BrE)
a power saw with a long, sharp, continuous band of toothed metal stretched between two wheels, used for cutting materials
power saw with a continuous toothed band

Examples

The carpenter used a band saw to cut intricate curves in the wood.
Safety goggles should always be worn when operating a band saw.
A band saw is ideal for resawing thick boards into thinner slabs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circular saw - a power saw with a circular blade
