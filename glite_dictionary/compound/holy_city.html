Holy City

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊli ˈsɪti / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli ˈsɪti / (BrE)
a poetic or religious term for Heaven, especially in Christian contexts
poetic/religious term for Heaven

Examples

In many hymns, Heaven is described as the Holy City where true peace reigns.
Pilgrims spoke longingly of reaching the Holy City at the end of their earthly journey.
The novel uses the Holy City as a metaphor for ultimate salvation.

Synset Examples

the Celestial City was Christian's goal in Bunyan's ‘Pilgrim's Progress’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Heaven - the place believed to be the home of God and angels, and of the good after death
  • Synonym: Celestial City - literary term for Heaven, from 'Pilgrim's Progress'
