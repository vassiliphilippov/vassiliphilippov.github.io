Vepse language

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛps ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈvɛps ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Finnic language spoken by the Veps people of northwestern Russia
Finnic language of the Veps

Examples

The Vepse language is considered endangered, with only a few thousand speakers remaining.
She is conducting research on the grammar of the Vepse language.
Efforts to revitalize the Vepse language include publishing textbooks and literature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Finnish language - the language of Finland, closely related to Vepse
  • Synonym: Karelian language - another Finnic language related to Vepse

Etymology

From the name of the Veps people who speak the language.
