patriotic correctness

1. NOUN

/ ˌpeɪtriˈɑːtɪk kəˈrɛktnəs / (AmE), / ˌpætriˈɒtɪk kəˈrɛktnəs / (BrE)
Conformity to or strict adherence to accepted patriotic values and norms, especially as demanded or expected in public or political contexts.
strict adherence to patriotic values

Examples

The government was accused of promoting patriotic correctness in schools.
Media outlets sometimes struggle with the expectations of patriotic correctness during national events.
Critics argue that patriotic correctness can suppress open debate about controversial issues.

Synset Examples

The speech was criticized for its lack of patriotic correctness.
His views were shaped by a sense of patriotic correctness.
Many debate the limits of patriotic correctness in media.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: political correctness - the practice of avoiding language or behavior that could offend particular groups

Etymology

Modelled on the pattern of 'political correctness,' combining 'patriotic' and 'correctness.'
