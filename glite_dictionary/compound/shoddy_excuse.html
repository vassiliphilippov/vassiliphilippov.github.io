shoddy excuse

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑdi ɪkˈskjus / (AmE), / ˈʃɒdi ɪkˈskjuːs / (BrE)
an insincere, careless, or unconvincing justification for something
insincere or weak justification

Examples

He offered a shoddy excuse for missing the meeting, blaming it on his alarm clock.
They could all see through her shoddy excuse for not finishing the report on time.
His shoddy excuse failed to persuade the teacher, resulting in extra homework.

Synset Examples

His shoddy excuses didn’t convince anyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lame excuse - an unconvincing or inadequate justification
