colour TV

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɚ ˌtiːˈviː / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ˌtiːˈviː / (BrE)
A television set that displays images in color rather than in black and white.
television set displaying color images
british

Examples

When they bought their first colour TV, the whole family gathered to watch the news.
The living room was transformed after he upgraded to a modern colour TV.
I still remember watching cartoons on my grandparents’ old colour TV as a child.

Related Terms
