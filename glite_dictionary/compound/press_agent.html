press agent

1. NOUN

/ prɛs ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / prɛs ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person employed to generate publicity for a company, organization, or public figure
person who arranges publicity

Examples

The press agent sent out dozens of press releases to announce the new product launch.
As a press agent, she was responsible for managing the singer’s media appearances.
When the company faced a scandal, their press agent worked overtime to handle the publicity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: publicist - person responsible for promoting public figures or organizations
