water park

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑːtɚ ˌpɑːrk / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌpɑːk / (BrE)
An amusement park featuring water-based attractions such as swimming pools, water slides, and splash pads.
amusement park with water attractions

Examples

The kids begged to spend the entire day at the water park.
After a long week, we relaxed by floating along the lazy river at the water park.
Many families plan vacations around a visit to a popular water park.

Synset Examples

The kids loved the sewer slide at the water park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amusement park - a venue with various rides and entertainment
