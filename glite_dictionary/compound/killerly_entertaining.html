killerly entertaining

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɪlərli ɛn.t̬ɚˈteɪ.nɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɪləli ɪn.təˈteɪ.nɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely entertaining; very enjoyable or absorbing, often in a thrilling or impressive way.
extremely entertaining
informal slang

Examples

The new video game was killerly entertaining and kept players hooked for hours.
Her stand-up routine proved to be killerly entertaining at the comedy club last night.
Everyone agreed that the festival was killerly entertaining from start to finish.

Synset Examples

The movie was killerly entertaining from start to finish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: highly entertaining - very entertaining
  • Antonym: dull - not entertaining or interesting

Etymology

From slang 'killer' (excellent, outstanding) plus adverbial suffix '-ly' and 'entertaining'.
