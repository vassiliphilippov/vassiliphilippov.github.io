wedding decor

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ dɪˈkɔr / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ dɪˈkɔː / (BrE)
the decorations and ornamental arrangements used for a wedding ceremony and reception
decorations for a wedding ceremony

Examples

The wedding decor featured elegant white flowers and golden candlelight.
She hired a professional to handle the wedding decor for her outdoor reception.
Their wedding decor matched the vintage theme perfectly, with lace tablecloths and antique candelabras.

Synset Examples

The wedding decor was simple yet tasteful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wedding decorations - decorative items used for weddings
