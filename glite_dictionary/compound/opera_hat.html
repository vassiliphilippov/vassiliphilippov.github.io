opera hat

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːpərə hæt / (AmE), / ˈɒpərə hæt / (BrE)
a formal men's hat with a tall, collapsible crown, often covered with silk or beaver fur, traditionally worn to the opera or other formal events
collapsible formal men's hat

Examples

He donned his opera hat and tails for the gala evening at the theatre.
In Victorian times, a gentleman's opera hat was an essential accessory for formal occasions.
The magician pulled a rabbit from his collapsible opera hat to the delight of the audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top hat - a tall, flat-crowned, broad-brimmed formal hat for men
