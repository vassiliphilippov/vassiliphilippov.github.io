shoulder blade

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊldər ˌbleɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə ˌbleɪd / (BrE)
either of the two flat, triangular bones located on each side of the upper back, forming part of the shoulder
flat bone of the upper back/shoulder

Examples

She felt a sharp pain near her shoulder blade after lifting the heavy box.
The masseuse worked to relieve tension around my shoulder blade.
An X-ray revealed a minor fracture in his right shoulder blade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scapula - the technical anatomical term for shoulder blade
