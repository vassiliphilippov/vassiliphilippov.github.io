communication skill

1. NOUN

/ kəˌmjunəˈkeɪʃən skɪl / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən skɪl / (BrE)
the ability to effectively convey or exchange information, ideas, or feelings with others through speaking, writing, or other means
the ability to share information effectively

Examples

Strong communication skills are essential for effective leadership in any organization.
She improved her communication skills by participating in debate club and public speaking workshops.
Employers increasingly look for candidates with excellent communication skills during the hiring process.

Synset Examples

Communication skills play a role in effective teamwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interpersonal skill - ability to interact well with others
  • Synonym: soft skill - personal attribute enabling effective work with people
  • Antonym: technical skill - proficiency in specialized practical tasks or technologies
