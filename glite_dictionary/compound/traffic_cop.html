traffic cop

1. NOUN

/ ˈtræfɪk kɑp / (AmE), / ˈtræfɪk kɒp / (BrE)
a police officer whose job is to direct and control the flow of vehicles on roads
police officer who directs traffic

Examples

The traffic cop stood at the busy intersection, directing cars and pedestrians with clear hand signals.
When the lights went out, a traffic cop was dispatched to manage the rush hour chaos.
A traffic cop stopped us for blocking the crosswalk during the parade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traffic officer - police officer specialized in managing road traffic
  • Degree Higher: police officer - member of the police force
