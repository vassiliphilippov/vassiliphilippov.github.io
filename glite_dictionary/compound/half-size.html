half-size

1. ADJ

/ ˈhæfˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈhɑːfˌsaɪz / (BrE)
Measuring half the usual or standard size.
measuring half the usual size

Examples

The model ship is a half-size replica of the original vessel.
I bought a half-size bed for the guest room to save space.
She wore a half-size ring to fit her smaller finger.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: full-size - of the normal or standard size
