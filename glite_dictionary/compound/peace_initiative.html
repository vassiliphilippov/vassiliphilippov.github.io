peace initiative

1. NOUN

/ pis ɪˈnɪʃətɪv / (AmE), / piːs ɪˈnɪʃətɪv / (BrE)
an action or proposal intended to start negotiations aimed at achieving peace, especially between countries or groups in conflict
action to start peace negotiations

Examples

The president announced a new peace initiative to bring an end to the conflict.
After months of violence, the leaders finally agreed to meet and discuss a peace initiative.
The United Nations supported the regional peace initiative, hoping it would lead to a formal treaty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peace proposal - a suggestion or plan put forward to achieve peace
  • Degree Higher: peace process - the series of steps taken to establish peace, often after an initiative
