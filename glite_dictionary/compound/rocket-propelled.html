rocket-propelled

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɑkɪt prəˌpɛld / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪt prəˌpɛld / (BrE)
Propelled or driven by a rocket engine.
driven by a rocket engine

Examples

The military used a rocket-propelled grenade to destroy the tank.
The new vehicle is rocket-propelled, allowing it to reach incredible speeds.
She described the missile's rocket-propelled launch as both loud and powerful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rocket-powered - powered by a rocket engine
