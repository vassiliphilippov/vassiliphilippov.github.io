law practice

1. NOUN

/ lɑː ˈpræktɪs / (AmE), / lɔː ˈpræktɪs / (BrE)
the profession or business of providing legal services, especially as conducted by a lawyer or law firm
the professional practice of law

Examples

He joined a prestigious law practice after graduating from law school.
Running a successful law practice requires both legal knowledge and business skills.
She opened her own law practice in the downtown area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legal practice - the work or business of a lawyer; another term for law practice
