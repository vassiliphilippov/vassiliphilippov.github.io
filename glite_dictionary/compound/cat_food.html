cat food

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæt fuːd / (AmE), / ˈkat fuːd / (BrE)
food that is specially prepared and formulated to be eaten by cats, typically as a pet product.
food prepared for cats

Examples

She bought a new brand of cat food to see if her kitten would like it better.
Most cat food contains the nutrients essential for a healthy feline diet.
The pantry is stocked with cans of cat food for their three pets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog food - food prepared for dogs
