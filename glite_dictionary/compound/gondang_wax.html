gondang wax

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑn.dæŋ wæks / (AmE), / ˈɡɒn.dæŋ wæks / (BrE)
a hard, cream-colored wax obtained from a Javanese fig tree, used in various applications
waxy substance from a Javanese fig tree

Examples

Artisans sometimes use gondang wax because of its cream color and hardness.
The export of gondang wax from Indonesia has increased in recent years.
Researchers are studying the properties of gondang wax for use in cosmetics and polishes.

Etymology

From Indonesian/Malay 'gondang', referring to the fig tree producing the wax.
