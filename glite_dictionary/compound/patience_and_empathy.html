patience and empathy

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪʃəns ənd ɛmˈpæθi / (AmE), / ˈpeɪʃəns ənd ɪmˈpɛθi / (BrE)
the ability to remain calm and understanding towards others, especially in challenging situations
calmness and understanding towards others

Examples

Working with children requires a great deal of patience and empathy.
Successful leaders often demonstrate patience and empathy when facing difficult situations.
She handled the complaint with patience and empathy, making the customer feel valued.

Synset Examples

Understanding autism requires patience and empathy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patience - the capacity to accept or tolerate delay or trouble without getting angry
  • Synonym: empathy - the ability to understand and share the feelings of another
