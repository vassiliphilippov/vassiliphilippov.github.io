Christmas star

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪs·məs stɑr / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs·məs stɑː / (BrE)
A tropical American plant with showy, usually scarlet, petal-like leaves and small yellow flowers, commonly used as a decoration at Christmas; also called poinsettia.
poinsettia plant, often used at Christmas

Examples

Every year, my grandmother decorates her house with a beautiful Christmas star in the living room.
The vivid red leaves of the Christmas star brighten up homes during the holiday season.
Before the party, we placed a large Christmas star near the entrance to welcome our guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poinsettia - a tropical American plant known for its red and green foliage, often used as a Christmas decoration
