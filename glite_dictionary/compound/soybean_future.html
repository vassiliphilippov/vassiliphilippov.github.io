soybean future

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔɪˌbin ˈfjuːtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪˌbiːn ˈfjuːtʃə / (BrE)
a contract for buying or selling soybeans at a set price for delivery at a specified date in the future
futures contract for soybeans

Examples

The price of soybean futures rose sharply due to concerns about drought in major growing regions.
Traders monitor soybean futures to anticipate changes in the agricultural market.
He invested heavily in soybean futures, expecting demand to increase over the year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: futures contract - a legal agreement to buy or sell a commodity at a predetermined price at a specified time in the future
