Bernie bro

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːni broʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːni brəʊ / (BrE)
A (typically male) supporter of Bernie Sanders, especially one who is outspoken or active online.
male supporter of Bernie Sanders
informal american

Examples

Many online debates became heated when a Bernie bro joined the discussion.
She was accused of being a Bernie bro despite her obvious qualifications.
During the primaries, the term Bernie bro often appeared in political commentary.

Synset Examples

He was labeled a Bernie bro for his political views.
The Bernie bro defended his candidate passionately online.
A group of Bernie bros gathered for the rally.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sandernista - Supporter of Bernie Sanders (less gendered)

Etymology

Blend of Bernie (from Bernie Sanders, U.S. politician) and bro (slang for 'brother', meaning male friend or companion).
