tummy time

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌmi taɪm / (AmE), / ˈtʌmi taɪm / (BrE)
A period during which an infant is placed on their stomach while awake to help develop neck, shoulder, and arm strength as well as motor skills.
period for infants to lie on their stomach

Examples

Tummy time helps babies build the muscles they need for crawling.
Our pediatrician recommended doing tummy time several times a day.
Some babies dislike tummy time at first, but they usually get used to it.

Synset Examples

We made tummy time fun with toys and music.
Tummy time is essential for developing neck strength.
The baby enjoys tummy time on the colorful mat.
