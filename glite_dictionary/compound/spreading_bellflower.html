spreading bellflower

1. NOUN

/ ˈsprɛdɪŋ ˈbɛlˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈsprɛdɪŋ ˈbɛlˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A European perennial plant with clumping, spreading stems and blue or white bell-shaped flowers.
European perennial with spreading stems and bell-shaped flowers

Examples

The spreading bellflower adds a splash of color to shaded garden beds.
Clusters of spreading bellflowers can be found in woodland clearings across Europe.
Gardeners often choose the spreading bellflower for its attractive blue blossoms and hardy growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bellflower - any plant of the genus Campanula with bell-shaped flowers
