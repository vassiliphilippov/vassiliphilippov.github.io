vixen-like charm

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪksən laɪk tʃɑrm / (AmE), / ˈvɪksən laɪk tʃɑːm / (BrE)
an alluring or seductive appeal, likened to that of a vixen; an attractive, confident, and sometimes slightly mischievous charm
alluring or seductive appeal

Examples

She possessed a vixen-like charm that captivated everyone at the party.
His novel describes the vixen-like charm of the mysterious stranger.
The actress was famous for her vixen-like charm on and off the screen.

Synset Examples

He always admired her vixen-like charm and confidence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seductiveness - the quality of being sexually attractive or alluring
  • Antonym: innocence - the quality of being innocent or lacking seductiveness

Etymology

From 'vixen' (a female fox, or a spirited or quarrelsome woman) + 'like' + 'charm.'
