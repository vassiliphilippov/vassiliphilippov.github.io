half-pint glass

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæf paɪnt ɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf paɪnt ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a drinking glass designed to hold half a pint of liquid, typically used for serving beverages such as beer or cider
glass holding half a pint

Examples

She poured water into a half-pint glass before handing it to her guest.
At the pub, I ordered a half-pint glass of bitter instead of a full pint.
The recipe recommends serving the dessert in a half-pint glass for the perfect portion size.

Synset Examples

She filled her half-pint glass with lemonade.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: pint glass - a glass holding a full pint
