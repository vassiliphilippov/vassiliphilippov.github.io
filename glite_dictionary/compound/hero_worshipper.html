hero worshipper

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪroʊ ˌwɜrʃɪpər / (AmE), / ˈhɪərəʊ ˌwɜːʃɪpə / (BrE)
a person who admires or idolizes another person, often excessively, regarding them as a hero
person who idolizes someone as a hero

Examples

As a child, he was a hero worshipper who decorated his room with posters of famous athletes.
Many fans become hero worshippers and follow their idols’ every move.
She criticized him for being a hero worshipper rather than forming his own opinions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admirer - someone who holds another in high regard or esteem
  • Antonym: skeptic - a person inclined to question or doubt accepted opinions
