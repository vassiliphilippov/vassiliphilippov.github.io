false bugbane

1. NOUN

/ fɔls ˈbʌɡˌbeɪn / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈbʌɡˌbeɪn / (BrE)
A tall perennial plant native to the eastern United States, with large basal leaves and white flowers that bloom in summer.
a tall U.S. perennial with white flowers

Examples

We found false bugbane growing along the shaded woodland trail.
The large leaves of false bugbane distinguish it from other native perennials.
Gardeners value false bugbane for its elegant white summer blossoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bugbane - a plant of the genus Cimicifuga, known for tall spikes of flowers
