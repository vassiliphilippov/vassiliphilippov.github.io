color bearer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɚ ˌbɛrɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ˌbeərə / (BrE)
A soldier who carries the flag or standard of a military unit in parades or in battle.
soldier carrying a unit's flag

Examples

The color bearer marched proudly at the head of the regiment during the ceremony.
During the heat of battle, the color bearer became a symbol of courage for the soldiers.
Losing their color bearer was a significant blow to the morale of the entire unit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: standard-bearer - person who carries a standard or flag
