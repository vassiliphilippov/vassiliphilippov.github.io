nonfat dry milk

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɑːnˌfæt ˌdraɪ ˈmɪlk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌfæt ˌdraɪ ˈmɪlk / (BrE)
powdered milk made by removing all fat and water from skimmed milk
powdered milk without fat

Examples

The recipe calls for two tablespoons of nonfat dry milk mixed with water.
Nonfat dry milk can be stored for long periods without refrigeration.
She added nonfat dry milk to her coffee as a low-fat alternative.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skimmed milk - milk with most or all of the cream removed
  • Antonym: whole milk - milk containing all its natural fat
