trained nurse

1. NOUN

/ treɪnd nɜrs / (AmE), / treɪnd nɜːs / (BrE)
a nurse who has completed formal education and clinical training at a recognized nursing school
nurse with formal training

Examples

After her studies, Maria became a trained nurse and began working in the city hospital.
The clinic employs only trained nurses to ensure high-quality patient care.
As a trained nurse, he is qualified to give expert medical assistance in emergencies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: registered nurse - a nurse who has passed a licensing exam and is registered with a licensing body
