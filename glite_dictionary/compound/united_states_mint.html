United States Mint

1. PROPN

/ ˌjuːnɪtɪd ˈsteɪts mɪnt / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪtɪd ˈsteɪts mɪnt / (BrE)
the federal agency responsible for manufacturing and distributing coins in the United States and processing gold and silver bullion
official US coin producing agency

Examples

Tours of the United States Mint in Philadelphia allow visitors to see how coins are produced.
The United States Mint released a new commemorative quarter this year.
Collectors often purchase proof sets directly from the United States Mint.
