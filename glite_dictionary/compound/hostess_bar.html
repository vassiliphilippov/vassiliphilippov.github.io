hostess bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊstɪs bɑr / (AmE), / ˈhəʊstɪs bɑː / (BrE)
A bar where female hostesses provide entertainment and company to customers, often engaging in conversation and serving drinks.
bar with female hostesses entertaining guests

Examples

The atmosphere in the hostess bar was both lively and elegant.
After work, some colleagues went to a hostess bar for drinks and conversation.
Many tourists are curious about the hostess bar culture in Japan.
