peach orchard

1. NOUN

/ pitʃ ˈɔrtʃərd / (AmE), / piːtʃ ˈɔːtʃəd / (BrE)
an area of land where peach trees are grown, typically for their fruit
grove of peach trees

Examples

The peach orchard produced a record harvest this year thanks to the warm spring.
We took a picnic lunch to the peach orchard and enjoyed the beautiful scenery.
Workers were busy pruning trees in the peach orchard during the early morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchard - an area where fruit trees are grown
