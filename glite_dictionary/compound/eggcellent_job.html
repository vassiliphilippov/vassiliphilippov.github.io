eggcellent job

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛɡsələnt dʒɑb / (AmE), / ˈɛɡsələnt dʒɒb / (BrE)
A humorous way of saying 'excellent job,' using a pun on the word 'egg.'
pun for 'excellent job'
informal

Examples

She did an eggcellent job with her presentation today.
Your report was an eggcellent job—I loved the attention to detail!
The chef received compliments for doing an eggcellent job on the breakfast menu.
