ginger snap

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪndʒər snæp / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒə snap / (BrE)
A small, crisp, round cookie flavored with ginger and often other spices.
crisp ginger-flavored cookie

Examples

I love to eat a ginger snap with my afternoon tea.
She baked fresh ginger snaps for the holiday party.
The spicy aroma of ginger snap cookies filled the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gingerbread - a sweet, spiced cake or cookie flavored with ginger
  • Synonym: biscuit - a small baked good (British term for cookie)
