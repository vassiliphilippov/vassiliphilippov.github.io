cycloptic creature

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈklɑptɪk ˈkriːtʃər / (AmE), / saɪˈklɒptɪk ˈkriːtʃə / (BrE)
A creature or being having only one eye, especially resembling a Cyclops from mythology.
one-eyed creature

Examples

The ancient legend spoke of a cycloptic creature guarding the hidden treasure.
She designed a costume inspired by a cycloptic creature for the fantasy convention.
A cycloptic creature was depicted in the mural beneath the crumbling archway.

Synset Examples

He drew a cycloptic creature in his sketchbook.
The cycloptic monster roamed the island, terrifying locals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cyclops - a member of a race of one-eyed giants in Greek mythology

Etymology

From 'Cyclops,' a race of one-eyed giants in Greek mythology, with 'cycloptic' formed as an adjective, plus 'creature.'
