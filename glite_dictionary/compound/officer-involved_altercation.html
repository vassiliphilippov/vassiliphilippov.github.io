officer-involved altercation

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔfəsər ɪnˈvɑlvd ˌɔltərˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈɒfɪsə ɪnˈvɒlvd ˌɔːltəˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a conflict or physical confrontation in which one or more law enforcement officers are directly involved
conflict involving police officers

Examples

The report detailed the officer-involved altercation that occurred downtown last night.
After the officer-involved altercation, the police department released a public statement.
Witnesses were interviewed following an officer-involved altercation outside the shopping center.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: altercation - an angry argument or dispute
