running postman

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌnɪŋ ˈpoʊstmæn / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ ˈpəʊstmən / (BrE)
a hairy, trailing or prostrate vine native to western Australia, known for its bright scarlet-pink flowers
Australian vine with scarlet-pink flowers

Examples

The running postman adds a splash of red to the native Australian garden.
Botanists are studying the growth patterns of the running postman in sandy soils.
We noticed a beautiful running postman vine trailing along the bush track.
