sports desk

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrts ˌdɛsk / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts ˌdɛsk / (BrE)
the department of a newspaper or media organization responsible for editing and supervising sports news coverage
newspaper department for sports news

Examples

She started her journalism career at the sports desk of a local newspaper.
The sports desk will be covering the championship game live tonight.
He was promoted to editor on the sports desk after years as a junior reporter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news desk - the department responsible for news coverage in a newspaper
