piece of shit

1. NOUN

/ pis əv ʃɪt / (AmE), / piːs əv ʃɪt / (BrE)
a thing or person regarded as extremely bad, worthless, or contemptible, often used as an insult
something extremely bad or worthless (vulgar slang)
slang vulgar

Examples

I can't believe I wasted my money on this piece of shit car.
After the argument, he felt like a total piece of shit.
That movie was a complete piece of shit; I walked out halfway through.

Synset Examples

She felt like a piece of shit after lying.
He called that last game a piece of shit.
This product is a piece of shit, I regret buying it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: junk - something of poor quality or little value
  • Antonym: gem - something of high value or quality (opposite)
