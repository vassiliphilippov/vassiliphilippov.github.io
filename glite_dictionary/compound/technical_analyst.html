technical analyst

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛk.nɪ.kəl ˈæn.ə.lɪst / (AmE), / ˈtɛk.nɪ.kəl ˈan.ə.lɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies patterns in stock prices and trading volumes to forecast future market movements, typically using charts and graphs rather than company fundamentals
stock market forecaster using charts

Examples

After years as a technical analyst, she developed expertise in identifying bullish patterns.
Many investors rely on the advice of a technical analyst before making trading decisions.
The technical analyst suggested that the recent trend indicated a potential market reversal.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fundamental analyst - a stock market analyst who evaluates companies based on financial statements and fundamentals
