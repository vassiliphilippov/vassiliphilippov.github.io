table knife

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəl naɪf / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəl naɪf / (BrE)
A knife with a blunt edge, used for eating food at a dining table.
knife used for eating at a table

Examples

He placed a table knife beside each plate before dinner was served.
Using a table knife, she easily spread butter on her bread.
Please make sure every guest has a table knife and fork at their setting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dinner knife - a type of table knife used during main courses
