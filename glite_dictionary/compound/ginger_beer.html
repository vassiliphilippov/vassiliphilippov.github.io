ginger beer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪndʒɚ bɪr / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒə bɪə / (BrE)
A carbonated drink with a slightly alcoholic content, flavored with fermented ginger.
slightly alcoholic carbonated ginger drink

Examples

He poured himself a glass of ginger beer to enjoy with lunch.
The recipe calls for two bottles of ginger beer to add a fiery kick.
She prefers ginger beer over ginger ale because of its stronger flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ginger ale - non-alcoholic carbonated drink flavored with ginger
