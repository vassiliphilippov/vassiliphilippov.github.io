spiritual rebirth

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrɪtʃuəl rɪˈbɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtjʊəl rɪˈbɜːθ / (BrE)
a profound personal transformation or awakening that leads to a new way of life, often through spiritual enlightenment
spiritual awakening leading to a new life

Examples

After his spiritual rebirth, he dedicated his life to helping others.
Many religions describe spiritual rebirth as a process of leaving the past behind and embracing a new hope.
She spoke about her spiritual rebirth during her time of personal crisis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conversion - the adoption of new religious beliefs or a dramatic change in life philosophy
