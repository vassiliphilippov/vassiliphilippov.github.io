to understand the assignment

1. VERB

/ ˌʌndərˈstænd ði əˈsaɪnmənt / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈstænd ði əˈsaɪnmənt / (BrE)
to fully grasp what is required or expected in a particular task or situation
comprehend what a task requires

Examples

It was clear from her thorough report that she truly understood the assignment.
Before starting the project, make sure you understand the assignment completely.
He failed the course because he never really understood the assignment.

Synset Examples

If you understand the assignment, the task will be easy.
He definitely did not understand the assignment given.
She always makes sure to understand the assignment before starting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to comprehend - to grasp mentally; understand
  • Antonym: to misunderstand - to fail to understand correctly
