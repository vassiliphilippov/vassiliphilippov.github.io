cabin liner

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæbɪn ˈlaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈkæbɪn ˈlaɪnə / (BrE)
a passenger ship equipped with cabins for travelers
passenger ship with cabins

Examples

The cabin liner was luxurious, with private rooms for every passenger.
During the early twentieth century, many immigrants crossed the Atlantic on a cabin liner.
We booked a week-long cruise on a classic cabin liner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liner - a large passenger ship
