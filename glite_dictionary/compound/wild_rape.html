wild rape

1. NOUN

/ waɪld reɪp / (AmE), / waɪld reɪp / (BrE)
A Eurasian plant in the cabbage family with yellow, mauve, or white flowers and podlike fruits, often considered a weed.
a Eurasian flowering plant weed

Examples

The field was overrun with wild rape that bloomed in yellow clusters.
Farmers often remove wild rape to protect their crops.
In spring, patches of wild rape can be seen by the roadside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rape - an oilseed plant also called rapeseed

Etymology

From Old English 'rapa' (turnip) via Latin 'rapum' (turnip); 'wild' indicates it is not cultivated.
