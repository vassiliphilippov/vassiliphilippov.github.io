muffin case

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌfɪn keɪs / (AmE), / ˈmʌfɪn keɪs / (BrE)
a round, usually scalloped paper, foil, or silicone cup used to hold and shape a muffin during baking
cup-shaped holder for baking muffins

Examples

She poured the batter carefully into each muffin case before baking.
The recipe recommends using silicone muffin cases for easy removal of the cakes.
After baking, the muffin cases peeled away cleanly from the blueberry muffins.

Synset Examples

A variety of sizes for muffin cases are available.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cupcake liner - a paper or foil case used to line a cupcake tin for baking
