common fig

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmən fɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən fɪɡ / (BrE)
A Mediterranean tree (Ficus carica) widely cultivated for its sweet, edible fruit, known as the fig.
type of fig tree with edible fruit

Examples

The common fig thrives in warm climates and produces sweet fruit in late summer.
They planted a common fig in the garden, hoping to enjoy fresh figs every year.
Dried common fig is a popular ingredient in Mediterranean desserts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fig - the edible fruit of the fig tree
