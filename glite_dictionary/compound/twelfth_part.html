twelfth part

1. NOUN

/ twɛlfθ pɑrt / (AmE), / twɛlfθ pɑːt / (BrE)
One of twelve equal portions into which something is or can be divided.
one of twelve equal parts

Examples

Each member received a twelfth part of the inheritance.
A twelfth part of the cake was left after the party.
If you divide an hour into twelve, each twelfth part is five minutes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twelfth - ordinal number corresponding to the number twelve in a sequence
  • Degree Higher: half - one of two equal parts
  • Synonym: one-twelfth - fraction representing one part out of twelve
