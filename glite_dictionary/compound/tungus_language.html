Tungus language

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuŋɡəs ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtʊŋɡʊs ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
The Tungusic language spoken by the Evenki people in eastern Siberia.
language of the Evenki in Siberia

Examples

The Tungus language is traditionally spoken by communities across eastern Siberia.
There are efforts to preserve the Tungus language among younger generations of the Evenki people.
Linguists have studied the Tungus language to understand the history of the Tungusic language family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Evenki - the people who speak the Tungus language

Etymology

From Russian Тунгус (Tungus), referring to the Evenki people.
