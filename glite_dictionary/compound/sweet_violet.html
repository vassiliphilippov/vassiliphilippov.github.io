sweet violet

1. NOUN

/ swit ˈvaɪ.ələt / (AmE), / swiːt ˈvaɪ.əlɪt / (BrE)
A small European plant (Viola odorata) with fragrant purple or white flowers, widely cultivated and naturalized in many regions.
European violet with fragrant purple or white flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with the delicate scent of sweet violet in early spring.
She picked a bouquet of sweet violets for the kitchen table.
Sweet violet has long been valued for its fragrant blossoms.
