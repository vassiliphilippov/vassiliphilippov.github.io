fortune telling

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔr.tʃən ˌtɛl.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.tʃuːn ˌtɛl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
The practice of predicting a person's future or gaining insight into future events by supernatural or mystical methods.
predicting the future by supernatural means

Examples

Many people seek fortune telling to learn about their love life or financial prospects.
Skeptics argue that fortune telling relies on psychological tricks rather than true supernatural abilities.
In some cultures, fortune telling is an important part of celebrations and rituals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: divination - the practice of seeking knowledge of the future by supernatural means
