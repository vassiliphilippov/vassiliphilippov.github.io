bed jacket

1. NOUN

/ bɛd ˈdʒækɪt / (AmE), / bɛd ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
A lightweight, short jacket worn over bedclothes, typically while sitting up in bed for warmth or modesty.
lightweight jacket worn over bedclothes

Examples

She wore a bed jacket while reading in her hospital room.
In the chilly mornings, my grandmother always put on her bed jacket before breakfast in bed.
The catalog offered a range of bed jackets made from soft fleece.
