Iowa crab

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.oʊ.ə kræb / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.əʊ.ə kræb / (BrE)
a wild crab apple tree native to the western United States, with fragrant pink flowers
western US wild crab apple tree

Examples

The Iowa crab produces small, tart fruit that wildlife enjoy.
In spring, the Iowa crab tree is covered in fragrant pink blossoms.
Gardeners often plant Iowa crab trees for their hardiness and ornamental value.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab apple - a small, sour wild apple or the tree that bears it
