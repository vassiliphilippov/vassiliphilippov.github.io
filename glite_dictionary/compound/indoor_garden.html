indoor garden

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnˌdɔr ˈɡɑrdən / (AmE), / ˈɪndɔː ˈɡɑːdn̩ / (BrE)
a garden created or arranged within a building, often for decorative or recreational purposes
garden within a building

Examples

She created a beautiful indoor garden filled with tropical plants in her living room.
The office's indoor garden provides a relaxing space for employees to unwind.
Maintaining an indoor garden helps improve air quality and adds a touch of nature to your home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greenhouse - a glass building for growing plants
