aviation medicine

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪviˈeɪʃən ˈmɛdəsən / (AmE), / ˌeɪvɪˈeɪʃən ˈmɛdəsɪn / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the health, safety, and performance of people involved in air and space flight
medical field addressing flight-related health
medical

Examples

After receiving his degree, Dr. Carter specialized in aviation medicine and worked with commercial pilots.
Aviation medicine plays a vital role in preparing astronauts for space missions.
Many symptoms unique to high-altitude flying are studied within the field of aviation medicine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aerospace medicine - the branch of medicine associated with flight, especially space flight
