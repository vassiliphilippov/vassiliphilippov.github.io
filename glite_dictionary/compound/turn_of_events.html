turn of events

1. NOUN

/ tɝn əv ɪˈvɛnts / (AmE), / tɜːn əv ɪˈvents / (BrE)
an unexpected change or development in a situation
unexpected change in a situation

Examples

The trial took a surprising turn of events when a new witness came forward.
Nobody could have predicted the shocking turn of events that changed everything overnight.
With this unexpected turn of events, the company's future is suddenly uncertain.

Synset Examples

events suddenly took an awkward turn

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twist - an unexpected change in a story or situation
  • Synonym: turning point - a time at which a decisive change occurs in a situation
