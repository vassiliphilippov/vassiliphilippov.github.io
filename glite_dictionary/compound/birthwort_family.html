birthwort family

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɜrθˌwɜrt ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθwɜːt ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants, including birthworts and wild gingers, known scientifically as Aristolochiaceae
family of flowering plants (Aristolochiaceae)

Examples

The birthwort family includes both exotic vines and common wild ginger.
Many species in the birthwort family have distinctive pipe-shaped flowers.
Botanists classified the new plant in the birthwort family after analyzing its floral structure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aristolochiaceae - the scientific name for the birthwort family of plants
  • Synonym: wild ginger - a plant genus (Asarum) included in the birthwort family
