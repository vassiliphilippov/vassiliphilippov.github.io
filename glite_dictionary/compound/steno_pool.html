steno pool

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɛnoʊ puːl / (AmE), / ˈstɛnəʊ puːl / (BrE)
a group of stenographers in an organization who work together, especially to provide typing and transcription services for different departments
group of workplace stenographers

Examples

The steno pool was responsible for preparing all legal documents on short notice.
She was promoted to supervisor after two years in the steno pool.
With the rise of modern technology, the traditional steno pool has become less common.

Synset Examples

The steno pool was busy transcribing the meeting notes.
She worked in the steno pool for several years.
The steno pool provided critical support during the trial.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: typing pool - a group of typists providing secretarial services in an office

Etymology

Shortening of 'stenographic pool', from 'stenographer' (a person specializing in transcription) and 'pool' (a collective group).
