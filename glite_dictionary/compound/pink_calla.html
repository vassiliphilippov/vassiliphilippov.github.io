pink calla

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋk ˈkælə / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈkalə / (BrE)
A type of calla lily with a rose-colored or pink spathe, grown as an ornamental plant.
calla lily with a pink spathe

Examples

She planted a pink calla in the corner of her garden for a splash of color.
The florist arranged white roses and a single pink calla in the bouquet.
In spring, the pink calla blooms beautifully beside the pond.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calla lily - a flowering plant with a large spathe, often grown ornamentally
