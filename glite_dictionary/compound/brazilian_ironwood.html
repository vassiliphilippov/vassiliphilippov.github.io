Brazilian ironwood

1. NOUN

/ brəˈzɪliən ˈaɪərnˌwʊd / (AmE), / brəˈzɪlɪən ˈaɪənˌwʊd / (BrE)
a thornless tree native to Brazil, known for its exceptionally heavy and dense wood
thornless Brazilian tree with heavy wood

Examples

The furniture was crafted from Brazilian ironwood, giving it unmatched durability.
Many guitar manufacturers prize Brazilian ironwood for making fingerboards.
The density of Brazilian ironwood makes it ideal for heavy-duty construction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ironwood - a term for various trees with very hard wood
