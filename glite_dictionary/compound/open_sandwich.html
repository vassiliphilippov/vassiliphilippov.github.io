open sandwich

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊpən ˈsænwɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən ˈsænwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A slice of bread with a filling or toppings, served without a second slice covering it.
slice of bread with toppings, no covering slice

Examples

She ordered an open sandwich with smoked salmon and dill for lunch.
In Scandinavia, the traditional open sandwich is often served with herring and onions.
The café’s menu features a delicious avocado and egg open sandwich on rye bread.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sandwich - food consisting of two pieces of bread with filling in between
