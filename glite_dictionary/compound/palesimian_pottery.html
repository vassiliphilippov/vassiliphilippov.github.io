Palesimian pottery

1. NOUN

/ ˌpeɪlˈsɪmiən ˈpɑt̬əri / (AmE), / ˌpeɪlˈsɪmiən ˈpɒtəri / (BrE)
pottery made in or characteristic of the Palesimian culture, distinguished by its unique styles and techniques
pottery from Palesimian culture

Examples

The museum has an entire exhibit dedicated to Palesimian pottery from the Bronze Age.
She specializes in the restoration of Palesimian pottery discovered in ancient settlements.
Researchers identified the shard as a piece of Palesimian pottery due to its distinctive geometric patterns.
