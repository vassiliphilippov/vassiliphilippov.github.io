potato fungus

1. NOUN

/ pəˈteɪtoʊ ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a fungus that infects potatoes, causing black scurfy spots on the tubers
fungus causing disease in potatoes

Examples

The farmer lost half his crop to potato fungus last year.
Experts suggest rotating crops to help prevent potato fungus from spreading.
After noticing the black spots, she realized her potatoes were infected with potato fungus.
