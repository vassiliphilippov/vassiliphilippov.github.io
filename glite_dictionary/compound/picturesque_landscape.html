picturesque landscape

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɪktʃərˈɛsk ˈlændskeɪp / (AmE), / ˌpɪktʃərəˈɛsk ˈlændskeɪp / (BrE)
a view or area of natural scenery that is visually charming or striking in appearance, like a picture
scenery that is visually charming

Examples

We spent our vacation hiking through the picturesque landscape of the region.
The artist was drawn to the picturesque landscape that surrounded the old village.
A picturesque landscape greeted us as we reached the top of the hill.

Synset Examples

In her painting, she depicted the picturesque landscapes of Germanland.
The restaurant offers a picturesque view of the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scenery - the natural features of a landscape considered in terms of their appearance
  • Synonym: landscape - an area of land and the features that are visible from one place
