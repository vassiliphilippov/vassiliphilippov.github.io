Indian rhinoceros

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən raɪˈnɑːsərəs / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən raɪˈnɒsərəs / (BrE)
a large rhinoceros of northern India and Nepal, distinguished by its single horn and thick, folded skin
one-horned rhinoceros of India and Nepal

Examples

The Indian rhinoceros is known for its distinctive single horn and armor-like skin folds.
Wildlife reserves in Nepal protect the endangered Indian rhinoceros from poachers.
We spotted an Indian rhinoceros grazing by the river during our safari tour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rhinoceros - a large, thick-skinned herbivorous mammal with one or two horns on the snout
  • Degree Lower: Javan rhinoceros - a rhinoceros species native to Java, also with a single horn
