favorable reception

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪvərəbəl rɪˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərəbl rɪˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)
the act of being accepted or welcomed in a positive or satisfactory manner
being accepted or welcomed positively

Examples

The new policy received a favorable reception from employees across all departments.
Her proposal met with a surprisingly favorable reception at the conference.
The book’s favorable reception by critics boosted its early sales.

Synset Examples

he bought it on approval

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acceptance - the action of consenting to receive or approve
