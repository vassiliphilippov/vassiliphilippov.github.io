BW defence

1. NOUN

/ biː ˈdʌfens / (AmE), / biː ˈdiːfɛns / (BrE)
Measures and strategies designed to protect against biological warfare.
defense against biological warfare

Examples

The military has increased funding for BW defence research in response to new threats.
Effective BW defence requires rapid detection and containment of biological agents.
International agreements aim to strengthen BW defence through cooperation and information sharing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biological warfare - the use of biological toxins or pathogens in war

Etymology

BW is an abbreviation of 'biological warfare'.
