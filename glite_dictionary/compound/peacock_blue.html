peacock blue

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiˌkɑk blu / (AmE), / ˈpiːkɒk bluː / (BrE)
a brilliant shade of blue with a noticeable greenish tint, resembling the color of a peacock's feathers
blue-green shade, like peacock feathers

Examples

The artist chose peacock blue for the walls to create a dramatic effect in the room.
She wore a stunning dress in peacock blue that drew everyone's attention.
The vase was decorated with intricate patterns in shades of gold and peacock blue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teal - a dark greenish-blue color
  • Synonym: turquoise - a greenish-blue color

Etymology

Refers to the distinctive blue-green color found in a peacock's plumage.
