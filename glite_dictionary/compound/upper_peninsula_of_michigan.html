Upper Peninsula of Michigan

1. PROPN

/ ˈʌpər pəˈnɪnsələ əv mɪˈɡænɪn / (AmE), / ˈʌpə pəˈnɪnsjʊlə əv mɪˈɡænɪn / (BrE)
the peninsula between Lake Superior and Lake Michigan forming the northwestern part of Michigan, known for its forests, lakes, and rural character
peninsula in northwestern Michigan

Examples

Many people travel to the Upper Peninsula of Michigan in the autumn to see the spectacular fall colors.
Wildlife such as moose and wolves can be found in the forests of the Upper Peninsula of Michigan.
The Upper Peninsula of Michigan is famous for its outdoor recreation and scenic beauty.
