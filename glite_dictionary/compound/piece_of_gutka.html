piece of gutka

1. NOUN

/ piːs əv ˈɡʊtkə / (AmE), / piːs ɒv ˈɡʊtkə / (BrE)
a small amount or serving of gutka, a chewable tobacco preparation commonly used in South Asia
serving of chewable gutka tobacco

Examples

She offered him a piece of gutka during the long train journey.
It is common for shopkeepers in some regions to sell a piece of gutka alongside other snacks.
The doctor advised him to stop chewing even a single piece of gutka for his health.

Synset Examples

She offered him a piece of gutka as a snack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gutka - a chewable mixture of tobacco, areca nut, and other ingredients popular in South Asia

Etymology

Gutka from Hindi-Urdu 'guṭkā', a type of chewable tobacco mix.
