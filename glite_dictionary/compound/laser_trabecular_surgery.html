laser trabecular surgery

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪzɚ trəˈbɛkjələr ˈsɝdʒəri / (AmE), / ˈleɪzə trəˈbɛkjʊlə ˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
a surgical procedure for the eye in which a laser makes multiple tiny burns to improve drainage of aqueous humor, used to treat glaucoma
eye surgery to improve fluid drainage (glaucoma)
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended laser trabecular surgery to help reduce the pressure in my eye.
After undergoing laser trabecular surgery, many glaucoma patients experience improved fluid drainage.
New advancements in laser trabecular surgery have made the procedure safer and more effective.
