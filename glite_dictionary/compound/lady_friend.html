lady friend

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪdi ˌfrɛnd / (AmE), / ˈleɪdi ˌfrɛnd / (BrE)
a woman or young woman with whom a man is romantically involved; a romantic female companion
a man's romantic female companion

Examples

He brought his lady friend to the company holiday party and introduced her to everyone.
His lady friend is an artist who loves to travel and paint landscapes.
After dinner, he was seen walking through the park with his lady friend.

Synset Examples

his girlfriend kicked him out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: girlfriend - a female partner in a romantic relationship
