pearl onion

1. NOUN

/ pɝl ˈʌnjən / (AmE), / pɜːl ˈʌnjən / (BrE)
a small, round onion with a milder and sweeter flavor than regular onions, often used whole in cooking
small, sweet-flavored onion

Examples

She added a handful of pearl onions to the stew for extra sweetness.
Pearl onion pickles are a popular addition to holiday platters.
The chef sautéed pearl onions in butter and served them alongside the roast.

Synset Examples

Pearl onions are a staple to the cuisine of Northern Europe, especially Sweden.
