written assignment

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪt̬.ən əˈsaɪn.mənt / (AmE), / ˈrɪt.ən əˈsaɪn.mənt / (BrE)
a task or piece of work that involves writing something, often given as coursework or for evaluation
task involving writing, often for school

Examples

For tomorrow's class, we have a written assignment on climate change.
She spent the weekend working diligently on her written assignment for English literature.
The teacher collected all the written assignments at the end of the lesson.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: essay - a short piece of writing on a particular subject
  • Synonym: homework - tasks assigned to students to be completed outside class
