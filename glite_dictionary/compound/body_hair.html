body hair

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdi hɛr / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi heə / (BrE)
short hair that naturally grows on a person's body, excluding the scalp, eyebrows, and eyelashes
hair growing on a person's body

Examples

Some people have more body hair than others due to genetic factors.
He decided to remove his body hair for the swimming competition.
Hormonal changes during puberty often increase the amount of body hair one has.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: facial hair - hair that grows on the face, such as beards and mustaches
  • Degree Lower: pubic hair - hair that grows in the pubic region
  • Degree Higher: scalp hair - hair that grows on the top of the head
