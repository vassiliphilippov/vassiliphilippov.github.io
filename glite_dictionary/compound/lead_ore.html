lead ore

1. NOUN

/ lɛd ɔr / (AmE), / lɛd ɔː / (BrE)
a naturally occurring mineral from which lead can be extracted
mineral containing lead

Examples

The mine produces large quantities of lead ore for industrial use.
Early miners extracted lead ore from deep underground tunnels.
Galena is one of the most common forms of lead ore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: galena - the most common mineral form of lead ore (lead sulfide)
