blank space

1. NOUN

/ blæŋk speɪs / (AmE), / blæŋk speɪs / (BrE)
an empty area, especially one intended for writing or filling in information
empty area for writing or adding

Examples

Please sign your name in the blank space at the bottom of the form.
The application has several blank spaces for additional comments.
She left a blank space in her essay to add more information later.

Synset Examples

write your name in the space provided

Related Terms

  • Synonym: space - an area that is empty or available
