four-spot

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔr ˌspɑt / (AmE), / ˈfɔː ˌspɒt / (BrE)
a playing card, domino, or die with four pips showing on its upward face
card, domino, or die showing four

Examples

He drew the four-spot of diamonds from the deck.
It's your turn—roll the dice and hope for a four-spot.
If you play the four-spot, you'll be able to beat his pair of threes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: four - the number 4 or a playing card with four spots
  • Degree Lower: ace - a playing card with a single spot, number one
  • Degree Higher: five-spot - a playing card, domino, or die showing five pips
