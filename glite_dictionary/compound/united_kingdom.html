United Kingdom

1. PROPN

/ jəˈnaɪtɪd ˈkɪŋdəm / (AmE), / jʊˌnaɪtɪd ˈkɪŋdəm / (BrE)
a country in northwestern Europe comprising England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland; officially known as the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland
country: England, Scotland, Wales, N. Ireland

Examples

The United Kingdom is known for its rich history and diverse culture.
She moved from Canada to the United Kingdom to study art at university.
Brexit marked the United Kingdom's decision to leave the European Union.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Great Britain - the island containing England, Scotland, and Wales

Etymology

From the 1707 unification of the kingdoms of England and Scotland into a single sovereign state.
