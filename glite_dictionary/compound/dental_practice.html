dental practice

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛntəl ˌpræktɪs / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl ˌpræktɪs / (BrE)
A business or clinic where dentistry is practiced and dental care is provided.
a clinic or business for dental care

Examples

She opened a new dental practice in the city center last year.
After graduating, he joined an established dental practice with several experienced dentists.
The dental practice offers a range of services, from routine cleanings to complex surgeries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dentistry - the professional care of teeth, including diagnosis and treatment
