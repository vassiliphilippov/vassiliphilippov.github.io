hoofed mammal

1. NOUN

/ hʊft ˈmæməl / (AmE), / hʊft ˈmaməl / (BrE)
a mammal that has hooves, including various animals that may not be closely related but share this feature
mammal with hooves

Examples

Many large herbivores, such as deer and horses, are considered hoofed mammals.
The rhinoceros is an unusual hoofed mammal with a thick, protective hide.
Scientists study hoofed mammals to learn more about the evolution of different types of feet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ungulate - a hoofed mammal
