cottage tulip

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑt̬ɪdʒ ˈtuːlɪp / (AmE), / ˈkɒtɪdʒ ˈtjuːlɪp / (BrE)
A variety of tulip with long stems and egg-shaped, variously colored flowers that typically bloom in May.
a long-stemmed tulip blooming in May

Examples

The vibrant colors of the cottage tulip made it the highlight of the spring garden.
She planted a row of cottage tulips along the fence to enjoy their blooms in May.
Gardeners prize the cottage tulip for its long stems and classic, egg-shaped blossoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tulip - a bulbous spring-flowering plant of the lily family
