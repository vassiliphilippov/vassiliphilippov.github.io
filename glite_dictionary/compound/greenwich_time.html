Greenwich Time

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrɛn.ɪtʃ ˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈɡrɛnɪtʃ ˌtaɪm / (BrE)
the mean solar time at the Prime Meridian (0° longitude) passing through Greenwich, England, used as the standard of time throughout the world
mean solar time at Greenwich, England

Examples

All international flights are scheduled according to Greenwich Time to avoid confusion.
Meteorologists use Greenwich Time when comparing data from different countries.
The contract specified deadlines by Greenwich Time to ensure clarity for all parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Greenwich Mean Time - the mean solar time at the Royal Observatory in Greenwich, London, used as a global time standard

Etymology

Named after Greenwich, a district in London through which the Prime Meridian passes.
