rat cheese

1. NOUN

/ ræt tʃiz / (AmE), / ræt tʃiːz / (BrE)
An informal term for American cheddar cheese, especially in southern or rural U.S. usage.
informal term for American cheddar cheese
informal american

Examples

He put a thick slice of rat cheese on his sandwich for extra flavor.
My grandmother always kept a block of rat cheese in the fridge.
The store was out of rat cheese, so I bought sharp cheddar instead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheddar cheese - a hard, smooth-textured cheese, originally made in Cheddar, England
  • Degree Lower: American cheese - processed cheese popular in the U.S.
