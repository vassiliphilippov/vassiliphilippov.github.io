cultural heritage

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌltʃərəl ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərəl ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (BrE)
the traditions, customs, objects, and practices of a group or society passed down through generations, forming their shared legacy
traditions and legacy of a community

UNESCO works to safeguard sites of outstanding cultural heritage globally.
