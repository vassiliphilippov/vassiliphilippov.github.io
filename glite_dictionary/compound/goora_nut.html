goora nut

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːrə nʌt / (AmE), / ˈɡʊərə nʌt / (BrE)
A tropical African tree that produces large brown nuts containing caffeine, used as a source of cola extract.
tree yielding nuts for cola extract

Examples

The goora nut is prized for its stimulating properties due to its caffeine content.
Traditional African communities often chew the goora nut for its energizing effects.
Manufacturers use extracts from the goora nut in the production of some soft drinks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kola nut - nut from the African cola tree, used as a flavoring and stimulant
