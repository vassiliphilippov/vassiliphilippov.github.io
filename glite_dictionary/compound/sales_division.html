sales division

1. NOUN

/ seɪlz dɪˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / seɪlz dɪˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
the part of a company responsible for selling its products or services
department responsible for sales

Examples

The sales division exceeded its quarterly targets thanks to a new marketing strategy.
After the merger, the sales division was reorganized to improve efficiency.
She worked in the sales division before moving into a management role.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sales department - the department in a business responsible for sales activities
