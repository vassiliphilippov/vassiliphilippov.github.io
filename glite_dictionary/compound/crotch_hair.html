crotch hair

1. NOUN

/ krɑːtʃ hɛr / (AmE), / krɒtʃ heə / (BrE)
hair that grows in the pubic area at the base of the torso
hair in the pubic area

Examples

The doctor explained that crotch hair is a normal part of puberty.
Some people prefer to shave their crotch hair for personal comfort.
Different cultures have varying attitudes towards crotch hair and body grooming.
