workbench

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːkˌbɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌbɛntʃ / (BrE)
A strong, sturdy table at which manual work such as carpentry or mechanical tasks is carried out.
sturdy table for manual work

Examples

He stored his tools neatly on the workbench in the garage.
The old workbench was covered with sawdust after hours of carpentry.
Every mechanic needs a reliable workbench for repairs and projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worktable - a table for doing manual work or crafts
  • Degree Lower: counter - a long flat surface in shops or kitchens where work or business is done
