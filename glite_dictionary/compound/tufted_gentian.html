tufted gentian

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌftɪd ˈdʒɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˈtʌftɪd ˈdʒɛnʃən / (BrE)
a small blue-flowered fringed gentian native to the Sierra Nevada mountains of North America
a blue-flowered gentian of Sierra Nevada

Examples

The tufted gentian is prized by wildflower enthusiasts for its vibrant blue petals.
During our hike in the Sierra Nevada, we spotted several blooming tufted gentians along the trail.
Botanists study the tufted gentian to learn more about the region's unique alpine flora.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gentian - a plant of the genus Gentiana, noted for its blue flowers
