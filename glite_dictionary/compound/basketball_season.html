basketball season

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskətˌbɔl ˈsiːzən / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtbɔːl ˈsiːzən / (BrE)
the period of the year when organized basketball games are played, often referring to the official schedule for teams or leagues
period when basketball is played

Examples

The college basketball season usually begins in November and ends with a national tournament in March.
Many fans eagerly await the start of the NBA basketball season every year.
She trains intensively during basketball season to stay at the top of her game.
