toilet room

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔɪlət ˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlɪt ˌruːm / (BrE)
A room containing a toilet, often referred to as a bathroom or restroom.
room with a toilet

Examples

She asked the receptionist where the nearest toilet room was located.
The house has a separate toilet room adjacent to the main bathroom.
New building regulations require at least one accessible toilet room on each floor.

Synset Examples

Please use the toilet room quietly during the meeting.
The toilet room was surprisingly clean and well-stocked.
He stepped into the toilet room to freshen up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bathroom - a room containing a toilet and typically a bath or shower
  • Synonym: restroom - a room with a toilet, used in public places
