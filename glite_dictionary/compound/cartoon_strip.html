cartoon strip

1. NOUN

/ kɑrˈtun strɪp / (AmE), / kɑːˈtuːn strɪp / (BrE)
a sequence of drawings, typically with captions, that together tell a story or joke, found especially in newspapers or comic books
sequence of comic drawings

Examples

Every Sunday, I look forward to reading my favorite cartoon strip in the newspaper.
The artist created a cartoon strip starring a mischievous cat and a clueless dog.
She collected the entire year's cartoon strips into a colorful book.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comic strip - a sequence of drawings telling a story, often with dialogue, in newspapers or magazines
