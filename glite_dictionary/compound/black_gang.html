black gang

1. NOUN

/ blæk gæŋ / (AmE), / blæk ɡæŋ / (BrE)
A group, often associated with criminal activity or violence, composed primarily of Black members.
criminal gang with primarily Black members

Examples

The police launched an investigation into the activities of the black gang in the city.
Many community leaders are working to prevent young people from joining a black gang.
The trial attracted attention because several members of a notorious black gang were involved.

Synset Examples

The black gang was notorious in the neighborhood.
They feared that the black gang would retaliate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gang - an organized group often involved in criminal activity
