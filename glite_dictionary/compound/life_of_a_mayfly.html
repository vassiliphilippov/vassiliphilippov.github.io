life of a mayfly

1. NOUN

/ laɪf əv ə ˈmeɪflaɪ / (AmE), / laɪf əv ə ˈmeɪflaɪ / (BrE)
a very short or fleeting existence; something that lasts only a brief time
very brief existence

Examples

Their happiness had the life of a mayfly, vanishing almost as soon as it appeared.
The artist's fame proved to be the life of a mayfly, quickly forgotten by the public.
She worried their new friendship would have the life of a mayfly and fade away by summer.

Synset Examples

He compared their brief romance to the life of a mayfly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fleeting - lasting for a very short time
  • Synonym: ephemeral - lasting for a very short time
