metric hundredweight

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛtrɪk ˈhʌndrədˌweɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrɪk ˈhʌndrɪdˌweɪt / (BrE)
a unit of weight equal to 100 kilograms in the metric system
unit of weight; 100 kilograms

Examples

The shipment was listed as five metric hundredweights of wheat.
A metric hundredweight is commonly used in agricultural markets.
Each bale of cotton weighed about one metric hundredweight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hundredweight - a unit of weight equal to either 112 or 100 pounds, depending on the system
