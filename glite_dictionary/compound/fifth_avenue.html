Fifth Avenue

1. PROPN

/ fɪfθ ˈævəˌnu / (AmE), / fɪfθ ˈævənjuː / (BrE)
an avenue in Manhattan, New York City, known for its upscale shops and landmarks; it divides the east and west sides of Manhattan.
famous street in Manhattan, NYC

Examples

Many tourists visit Fifth Avenue to see its luxury boutiques and flagship stores.
The parade marched proudly up Fifth Avenue during the city's annual celebration.
Her office building is just a block away from Fifth Avenue in Midtown Manhattan.
