burial ground

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛriəl ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈbɛriəl ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
A piece of land used for burying the dead; a cemetery or graveyard.
land for burying the dead

Examples

The ancient burial ground was discovered during the construction of the new building.
Many legends surround the old burial ground at the edge of the village.
The community worked together to clean up the neglected burial ground after the storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cemetery - an area of ground used for burying the dead
  • Synonym: graveyard - a burial ground, typically churchyard for the burial of the dead
