island of solitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.lənd əv səˈluːd / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.lənd əv səˈljuːd / (BrE)
a place or situation where one is isolated for quiet reflection or to be alone
a place for isolated reflection

Examples

After a stressful day, she retreats to her island of solitude to meditate.
His study became an island of solitude where he could write without interruption.
Many people dream of having an island of solitude away from the noise of daily life.

Synset Examples

He often retreats to his island of solitude to think.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sanctuary - a place of refuge or safety
  • Synonym: retreat - a quiet or secluded place in which one can rest and relax
