Republic of Maldives

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv mælˈdiːvz / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈmɔːldiːvz / (BrE)
an island country in the Indian Ocean, southwest of India and Sri Lanka, known for its atolls and tropical beaches; gained independence from the United Kingdom in 1965
island country in the Indian Ocean

Examples

The Republic of Maldives attracts tourists from around the world for its pristine beaches and coral reefs.
After gaining independence in 1965, the Republic of Maldives joined the United Nations.
The government of the Republic of Maldives is taking action to address the challenges of climate change.
