mountain rice

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntən raɪs / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn raɪs / (BrE)
a valuable forage grass found in dry upland areas and plains of western North America to northern Mexico
forage grass of western North America

Examples

Cattle graze on mountain rice in the arid plains during the summer months.
Researchers studied the growth patterns of mountain rice in its natural habitat.
The drought-resistant qualities of mountain rice make it valuable for ranchers in dry regions.
