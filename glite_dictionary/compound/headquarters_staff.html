headquarters staff

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdˌkwɔrtərz stæf / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌkwɔːtəz stɑːf / (BrE)
military personnel who work at a headquarters, responsible for planning, administration, and coordination.
military personnel working at headquarters

Examples

The headquarters staff coordinated the movements of all units during the operation.
As a member of the headquarters staff, she was responsible for strategic planning.
The general addressed the headquarters staff at the morning briefing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: staff - a group of people employed by an organization
