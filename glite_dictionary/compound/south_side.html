south side

1. NOUN

/ saʊθ saɪd / (AmE), / saʊθ saɪd / (BrE)
the part or side of something that faces or is towards the south
the side facing south

Examples

They planted roses along the south side of the house to catch the sunlight.
The south side of the mountain is covered in dense forest.
During winter, the south side receives more warmth from the sun.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: north side - the side that is on the north
  • Antonym: east side - the side that is on the east
  • Antonym: west side - the side that is on the west
