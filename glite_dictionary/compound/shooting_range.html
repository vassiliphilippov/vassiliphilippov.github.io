shooting range

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃuːtɪŋ reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈʃuːtɪŋ reɪndʒ / (BrE)
An area or facility where people can practice shooting firearms at targets.
place for practicing firearm shooting

Examples

The police officers practiced their marksmanship at the shooting range every week.
After buying his rifle, he went to the shooting range to improve his aim.
Many shooting ranges require visitors to wear protective ear and eye gear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firing range - area or facility for shooting practice
