yellow bristlegrass

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈbrɪsəlˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈbrɪsəlɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a common weedy grass with bristly seed heads, found in many temperate regions
a weedy grass with bristly heads

Examples

The field was overrun with yellow bristlegrass by late summer.
Farmers often struggle to control the spread of yellow bristlegrass in their crops.
You can identify yellow bristlegrass by its distinctive bristly seed heads.
