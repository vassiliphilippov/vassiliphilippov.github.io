town clerk

1. NOUN

/ taʊn klɝk / (AmE), / taʊn klɑːk / (BrE)
an official responsible for keeping the records and documents of a town or municipality
official who keeps town records

Examples

The town clerk is responsible for maintaining all the official municipal documents.
During the meeting, the town clerk read the minutes from the previous session.
She was appointed town clerk after five years working in the council office.
