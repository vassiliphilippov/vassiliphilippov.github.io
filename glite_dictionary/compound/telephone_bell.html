telephone bell

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlɪˌfoʊn bɛl / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn bɛl / (BrE)
an electric bell that sounds to alert someone to an incoming telephone call
bell that rings for a telephone call

Examples

The old rotary phone in the hallway still has a loud telephone bell that startles everyone when it rings.
He replaced the broken telephone bell so that he wouldn't miss important calls.
Some vintage telephones are prized for their distinctive telephone bell sound.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doorbell - an electric bell installed at an outer door, rung by visitors
