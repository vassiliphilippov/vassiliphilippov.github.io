oblique bandage

1. NOUN

/ əˈbliːk ˈbændɪdʒ / (AmE), / əˈbliːk ˈbændɪdʒ / (BrE)
a bandage applied so that each turn is made at an angle, spiraling up or down a limb
bandage wrapped in a slanting spiral
medical

Examples

The nurse carefully applied an oblique bandage to the patient's swollen ankle.
An oblique bandage provides effective support for limbs with uneven surfaces.
Learning the oblique bandage technique is an essential skill for first aid responders.
