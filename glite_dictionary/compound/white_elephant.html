white elephant

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈɛl.ə.fənt / (AmE), / waɪt ˈel.ɪ.fənt / (BrE)
An albino Indian elephant, rare and considered sacred or venerated in some parts of East Asia.
rare sacred albino elephant

Examples

In ancient Siam, a white elephant was a highly prized and sacred animal.
Legend tells of a king who would gift a white elephant to courtiers he wished to ruin.
The temple is famous for housing a revered white elephant said to bring good fortune.
