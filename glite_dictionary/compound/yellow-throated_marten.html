yellow-throated marten

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˌθroʊtəd ˈmɑrtən / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˌθrəʊtɪd ˈmɑːtən / (BrE)
a large species of marten with distinctive yellow throat and black markings, found in southern China and Burma
a large marten with a yellow throat, from Asia

Examples

The yellow-throated marten is known for its striking yellow and black fur pattern.
Researchers spotted a yellow-throated marten hunting in the forests of southern China.
Unlike other martens, the yellow-throated marten is active during the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marten - a weasel-like carnivorous mammal of the genus Martes
