jerkwater idea

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɝkˌwɑtɚ aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːkˌwɔːtə aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
An idea regarded as trivial, insignificant, or nonsensical.
trivial or nonsensical idea
informal

Examples

Stop coming to the meeting with jerkwater ideas that waste everyone's time.
The committee dismissed the proposal as just another jerkwater idea.
She was tired of hearing jerkwater ideas that had no practical value.

Synset Examples

Stop filling my head with jerkwater ideas.
His excuses were nothing but jerkwater nonsense.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - ideas or speech that are foolish or meaningless
  • Synonym: trivial - of little value or importance

Etymology

From American slang 'jerkwater', originally referring to small, insignificant towns where trains stopped merely to take on water, later used to describe things regarded as minor or unimportant.
