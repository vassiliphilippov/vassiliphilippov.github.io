Joe Public

1. NOUN

/ dʒoʊ ˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / dʒəʊ ˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
an average or typical member of the public; an ordinary person representing the general population
the average or typical member of the public

Examples

Many government policies have little impact on Joe Public but benefit large corporations.
Companies need to make their products appealing to Joe Public if they want mass-market success.
The story was written from the perspective of Joe Public, highlighting common concerns.

Synset Examples

Joe Public often feels ignored in politics.
The politician promised to listen to Joe Public's needs.
Joe Public is concerned about rising taxes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: John Q. Public - another term for an ordinary or average person

Etymology

Combination of the given name 'Joe,' used generically for an average man, and 'Public,' meaning the people in general.
