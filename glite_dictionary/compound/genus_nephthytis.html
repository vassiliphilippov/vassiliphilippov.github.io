genus Nephthytis

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs nɛpˈθaɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs nɛpˈθɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
A small genus of creeping or twining tropical herbs native to western Africa, belonging to the family Araceae.
genus of tropical African herbs (botany)

Examples

The genus Nephthytis includes several popular ornamental houseplants known for their attractive foliage.
Researchers are studying the chemical properties of plants in the genus Nephthytis for potential medicinal uses.
A specimen from the genus Nephthytis was discovered in the rainforests of western Africa.

Etymology

From Latin 'genus' (kind, type) and 'Nephthytis', the genus name assigned in modern botanical Latin.
