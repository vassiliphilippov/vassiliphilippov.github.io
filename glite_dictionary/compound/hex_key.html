hex key

1. NOUN

/ hɛks ki / (AmE), / hɛks kiː / (BrE)
A small, L-shaped wrench with a hexagonal cross-section, used to turn Allen screws or bolts with hexagonal recesses.
L-shaped wrench for hex screws

Examples

I need a hex key to assemble this furniture.
Can you pass me the 5mm hex key from the toolbox?
Most bicycle repairs require a set of hex keys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Allen key - A tool for driving bolts and screws with hexagonal sockets
