moss agate

1. NOUN

/ mɑs ˈæɡət / (AmE), / mɒs ˈæɡət / (BrE)
a semi-precious gemstone variety of agate characterized by brown, black, or green moss-like inclusions or markings
agate with moss-like inclusions

Examples

She wore a pendant featuring a beautiful moss agate set in silver.
Collectors prize moss agate for its unique, natural patterns that resemble tiny trees or ferns.
The jeweler recommended moss agate as an affordable alternative to more expensive gemstones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agate - a semi-precious gemstone consisting of a banded form of chalcedony
