'Arryish behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈæri ɪʃ bɪˈheɪvjər / (AmE), / ˈæri ɪʃ bɪˈheɪvɪə / (BrE)
behavior regarded as typical of the character 'Arry; often seen as endearingly boisterous, unsophisticated, or slightly vulgar.
boisterous, unsophisticated behavior like 'Arry
british

Examples

Her 'Arryish behavior made her the life of the party, though some guests frowned upon it.
Some find 'Arryish behavior charming, while others consider it rather tiresome.
Despite his wealth, he still displays 'Arryish behavior at public events.

Synset Examples

'Arryish behavior can sometimes be endearing or annoying.

Etymology

From 'Arry, a comic representation of a working-class Englishman, plus the suffix -ish.
