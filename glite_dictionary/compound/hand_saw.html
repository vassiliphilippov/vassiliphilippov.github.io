hand saw

1. NOUN

/ hænd sɔ / (AmE), / hænd sɔː / (BrE)
A saw operated with one hand, used for cutting wood by hand.
a manual saw for cutting wood

Examples

He used a hand saw to cut the wooden plank in half.
The carpenter prefers a hand saw for detailed work instead of a power tool.
After sharpening his hand saw, he finished building the new shelf.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saw - a tool with a toothed blade used for cutting wood or other materials
