art collection

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt kəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / ɑːt kəˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
a group of works of art that are owned or displayed together, usually by a person, institution, or gallery
group of art works owned or displayed together

Examples

The museum acquired a new art collection featuring works from modern European painters.
His private art collection includes several rare sculptures and paintings.
She decided to donate her entire art collection to the local gallery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collection - a group of objects or works gathered together
