willing and able

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪlɪŋ ənd ˈeɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈwɪlɪŋ ənd ˈeɪbəl / (BrE)
ready and having the necessary ability to do something; not reluctant
ready and capable of acting

Examples

Our team is willing and able to assist you at any time.
The country proved it was willing and able to respond to the crisis.
Finding someone who is both willing and able can be a challenge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: willing - ready to do something if needed
  • Antonym: reluctant - unwilling or disinclined to do something
