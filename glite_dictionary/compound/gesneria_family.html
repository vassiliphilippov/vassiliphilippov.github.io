gesneria family

1. NOUN

/ ɡɛzˈnɪriə ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ɡɛzˈnɪəriə ˈfæməli / (BrE)
A large family of tropical herbs, shrubs, or lianas, officially known as the Gesneriaceae, found chiefly in tropical regions.
family of tropical flowering plants (Gesneriaceae)

Examples

The gesneria family includes many striking flowering plants prized by indoor gardeners.
Saintpaulia, known as African violet, belongs to the gesneria family.
Researchers study the diversity of the gesneria family to understand tropical plant evolution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gesneriaceae - the botanical name for the gesneria family
  • Synonym: African violet - a common houseplant, member of the gesneria family

Etymology

Named after Swiss naturalist Conrad Gesner (1516–1565), combined with 'family' for biological classification.
