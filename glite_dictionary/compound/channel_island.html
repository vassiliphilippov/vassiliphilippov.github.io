Channel Island

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃæn.əl ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / ˈtʃæn.əl ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
any of a group of British islands in the English Channel, located off the northern coast of France, including Jersey, Guernsey, Sark, and Alderney.
British islands in the English Channel

Examples

We spent our holiday exploring the beautiful landscape of a Channel Island.
Guernsey is one of the most popular Channel Island destinations for tourists.
He moved to a Channel Island seeking a quieter life away from the city.
