genus Bletilla

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs bləˈtɪl.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs blɪˈtɪl.ə / (BrE)
A small genus of hardy terrestrial orchids native mainly to East Asia, similar to the genus Bletia.
a genus of Asian terrestrial orchids

Examples

The genus Bletilla is well known for its attractive purple and white flowers.
Gardeners often cultivate species from the genus Bletilla for their resilience and beauty.
Members of the genus Bletilla are commonly found in eastern Asia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bletia - genus of orchids similar to Bletilla

Etymology

From Neo-Latin genus name 'Bletilla', diminutive of 'Bletia', another orchid genus.
