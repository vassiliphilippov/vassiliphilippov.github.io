time note

1. NOUN

/ taɪm noʊt / (AmE), / taɪm nəʊt / (BrE)
a promissory note that specifies the time or times at which repayment is to be made
promissory note with scheduled repayment times
legal

Examples

The bank issued a time note that required full repayment within six months.
As collateral, she provided a signed time note to guarantee payment by the agreed date.
Unlike a demand note, a time note clearly states when the debt must be settled.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: demand note - a promissory note payable on demand
