no room for

1. X

/ noʊ ruːm fər / (AmE), / nəʊ ruːm fə / (BrE)
used to indicate that there is not enough space or opportunity for something or someone
not enough space or chance for

Examples

There's no room for extra luggage in the car.
The company has no room for mistakes in this project.
After the renovations, there's no room for a dining table in the kitchen.

Synset Examples

My parents claimed there was no room for a pony.
