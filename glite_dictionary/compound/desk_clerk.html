desk clerk

1. NOUN

/ dɛsk klɝk / (AmE), / dɛsk klɑːk / (BrE)
a person who works at the reception desk in a hotel, responsible for receiving guests, handling reservations, and providing information.
hotel receptionist or front desk worker

Examples

I asked the desk clerk for directions to the nearest restaurant.
The desk clerk checked us in quickly and provided our room keys.
If you lose your key, just let the desk clerk know at the front desk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: receptionist - person who greets visitors or guests, especially in an office or hotel
