even a little

1. ADV

/ ˈivən ə ˈlɪt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈiːvən ə ˈlɪtl̩ / (BrE)
to any extent at all; in the slightest degree.
to any extent at all

Examples

He didn't seem even a little concerned by the news.
Are you even a little interested in coming with us to the concert?
