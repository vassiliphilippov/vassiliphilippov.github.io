antique velocipede

1. NOUN

/ ænˈtik vəˈlɑsəˌpid / (AmE), / ænˈtiːk vɪˈlɒsɪpiːd / (BrE)
a human-powered vehicle from the past, typically referring to early bicycles or similar conveyances from the 19th century
historical human-powered vehicle

Examples

At the auction, an antique velocipede attracted attention from collectors of rare bicycles.
The museum displayed an antique velocipede alongside other innovations in transportation history.
He rode his restored antique velocipede during the Victorian parade.

Synset Examples

She restored an antique velocipede for the museum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: velocipede - an early type of bicycle or human-powered land vehicle
