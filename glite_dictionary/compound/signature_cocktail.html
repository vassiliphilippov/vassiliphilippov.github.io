signature cocktail

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪɡ.nə.tʃɚ ˈkɑk.teɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.nə.tʃə ˈkɒk.teɪl / (BrE)
A distinctive drink created by and unique to a particular bar or restaurant, often highlighting the establishment's style or specialty.
unique drink of a bar or restaurant

Examples

The new restaurant is famous for its signature cocktail made with exotic herbs and spices.
Each guest was greeted with a refreshing signature cocktail at the wedding reception.
She asked the bartender to recommend their signature cocktail before ordering.

Synset Examples

He ordered a signature cocktail at the roof bar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: house cocktail - a cocktail featured or invented by the establishment
