Treasury Department

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɛʒəri dɪˌpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒəri dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the department of the U.S. federal government responsible for managing national finances, collecting revenue, and producing currency
U.S. government department for finances and revenue
american

Examples

The Treasury Department announced new regulations to improve financial transparency.
After the financial crisis, the Treasury Department played a major role in economic recovery.
She was appointed to a senior position in the Treasury Department last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Department of the Treasury - alternative full name for the U.S. Treasury Department
  • Synonym: Internal Revenue Service - U.S. government agency under the Treasury Department responsible for tax collection
