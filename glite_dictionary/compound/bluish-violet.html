bluish-violet

1. ADJ

/ ˈbluːɪʃ ˈvaɪələt / (AmE), / ˈbluːɪʃ ˈvaɪələt / (BrE)
having a violet color tinged with blue; intermediate in color between blue and violet
of a violet color with a bluish tint

Examples

The walls were painted a soft bluish-violet that changed color with the sunlight.
She chose a dress in a delicate bluish-violet shade for the wedding.
The flower had subtle bluish-violet petals that stood out in the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bluish - slightly blue in color
  • Synonym: violet - a bluish-purple color
