tax system

1. NOUN

/ tæk ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / tæks ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
The set of laws and procedures by which a government assesses and collects taxes.
system for assessing and collecting taxes

Examples

The country's tax system is considered one of the most complicated in the world.
She believes the tax system should be reformed to close loopholes for large corporations.
Debates about the fairness of the tax system are common during election years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxation - the act or process of imposing taxes
  • Synonym: tax law - the body of law dealing with taxes
