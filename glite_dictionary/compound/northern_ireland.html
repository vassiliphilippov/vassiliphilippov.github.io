Northern Ireland

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɔrðərn ˈaɪərlənd / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈaɪələnd / (BrE)
a country that is part of the United Kingdom, occupying the northeastern part of the island of Ireland
country in the United Kingdom, on island of Ireland

Examples

Hundreds of tourists visit Northern Ireland each year to explore its scenic coastline.
The peace process in Northern Ireland has been studied as a model for conflict resolution.
Belfast, the capital of Northern Ireland, is known for its vibrant cultural scene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ireland - the island west of Great Britain containing the Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland
  • Antonym: Republic of Ireland - the sovereign nation occupying most of the island of Ireland
