Say Hey Kid

1. PROPN

/ seɪ heɪ kɪd / (AmE), / seɪ heɪ kɪd / (BrE)
a nickname for Willie Mays, a celebrated American baseball player known for his outstanding career and accomplishments (born 1931)
nickname for baseball player Willie Mays
american

Examples

Many fans consider the Say Hey Kid to be one of the greatest baseball players of all time.
The Say Hey Kid made countless memorable catches during his career in center field.
In 2004, the Say Hey Kid was honored with the Presidential Medal of Freedom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Willie Mays - American baseball player, Hall of Famer, born 1931

Etymology

Nickname attributed to the habit of Willie Mays greeting others with "Say hey!".
