peaked cap

1. NOUN

/ ˈpikt ˌkæp / (AmE), / ˈpiːkt ˌkæp / (BrE)
A cap with a flat circular top and a projecting visor, often worn as part of military or police uniforms.
cap with flat top and visor

Examples

The officer adjusted his peaked cap before stepping out onto the parade ground.
Members of the marching band wore matching uniforms and a traditional peaked cap.
He recognized the police inspector by the shiny badge on his peaked cap.
