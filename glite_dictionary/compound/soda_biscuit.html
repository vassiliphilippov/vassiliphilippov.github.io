soda biscuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊdə ˈbɪskɪt / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdə ˈbɪskɪt / (BrE)
A light, tender biscuit made with baking soda and buttermilk as leavening agents.
biscuit made with soda and buttermilk

Examples

She served fresh soda biscuits with homemade strawberry jam for breakfast.
Many traditional Southern recipes call for soda biscuits as a side to fried chicken.
The soda biscuit was warm, flaky, and perfect for sopping up gravy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baking powder biscuit - biscuit leavened with baking powder
