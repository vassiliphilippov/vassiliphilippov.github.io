commissioned naval officer

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɪʃənd ˈneɪvəl ˈɑfəsər / (AmE), / kəˈmɪʃənd ˈneɪvəl ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
an officer in the navy who has received a commission from the government, holding legal authority and rank
naval officer with commissioned rank

Examples

After years of training, he achieved the rank of commissioned naval officer.
A commissioned naval officer is entrusted with significant responsibilities aboard ship.
Many aspire to become a commissioned naval officer for the leadership opportunities it provides.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naval officer - an officer serving in a navy
  • Antonym: non-commissioned officer - military officer who has not received a commission
