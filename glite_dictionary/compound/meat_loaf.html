meat loaf

1. NOUN

/ mit loʊf / (AmE), / miːt ləʊf / (BrE)
A dish consisting of ground meat mixed with other ingredients, shaped into a loaf and baked.
baked dish of ground meat

Examples

My grandmother's meat loaf is a family favorite at every holiday dinner.
He ordered a slice of meat loaf with mashed potatoes and green beans.
Many people add breadcrumbs and vegetables to their meat loaf recipe for extra flavor.
