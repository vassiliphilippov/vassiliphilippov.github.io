pig in a blanket

1. NOUN

/ pɪɡ ɪn ə ˈblæŋkɪt / (AmE), / pɪɡ ɪn ə ˈblaŋkɪt / (BrE)
A sausage or hot dog wrapped in pastry or dough and baked or cooked, typically served as a snack or appetizer.
sausage wrapped in pastry or dough

Examples

We offered fresh pigs in blankets as appetizers at the holiday gathering.
The children excitedly ate every pig in a blanket on the platter.
She learned how to make pigs in blankets from her grandmother's recipe.

Synset Examples

We served pig in a blanket at the party.
