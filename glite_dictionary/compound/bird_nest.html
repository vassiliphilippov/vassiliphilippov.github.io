bird nest

1. NOUN

/ bɝd nɛst / (AmE), / bɜːd nɛst / (BrE)
A nest built by a bird for laying its eggs and raising its young.
nest built by a bird for eggs and young

Examples

A mother robin sat quietly in her bird nest, keeping her eggs warm.
Children marveled at the intricate bird nest they found in the old oak tree.
It was amazing to watch the sparrows build a bird nest on the balcony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nest - a structure made or chosen by a bird for laying eggs and sheltering its young
