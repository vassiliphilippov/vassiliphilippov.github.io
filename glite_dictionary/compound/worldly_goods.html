worldly goods

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːldli ɡʊdz / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldli ɡʊdz / (BrE)
all the possessions and property owned by a person, especially material and tangible items
material possessions owned by a person

Examples

After years of travel, he returned home with all his worldly goods in a single bag.
When she moved to the new country, she sold most of her worldly goods.
They donated their worldly goods to charity before joining the monastery.

Synset Examples

he left all his worldly possessions to his daughter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worldly possessions - material possessions and property owned by someone
