Irish Republican Army

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪrɪʃ rɪˈpʌblɪkən ˈɑːrmi / (AmE), / ˈaɪrɪʃ rɪˈpʌblɪkən ˈɑːmi / (BrE)
a paramilitary organization of Irish nationalists that campaigned, often through violence, for the end of British rule in Northern Ireland and the reunification of Ireland
Irish nationalist paramilitary group

Examples

The Irish Republican Army played a major role in the political history of Northern Ireland.
After the bombing, authorities suspected the Irish Republican Army was responsible.
Talks between the British government and the Irish Republican Army aimed to bring about peace.
