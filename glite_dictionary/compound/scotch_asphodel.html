Scotch asphodel

1. NOUN

/ skɑtʃ ˈæsfəˌdɛl / (AmE), / skɒtʃ ˈæsfədɛl / (BrE)
a plant of mountainous regions of Europe, having spikes of white flowers and resembling an asphodel
a European mountain plant with white flower spikes

Examples

The fields were dotted with blooming Scotch asphodel in early summer.
Botanists studied the ecology of Scotch asphodel growing in the highlands.
Hikers admired the delicate white spikes of the Scotch asphodel along the trail.
