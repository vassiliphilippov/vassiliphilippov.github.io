baseball bat

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl bæt / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl bæt / (BrE)
A smooth, usually wooden or metal club used by a batter to hit the ball in the game of baseball.
club used to hit in baseball

Examples

He swung the baseball bat with all his strength and hit a home run.
The little league coach bought new baseball bats for the team.
She gifted her brother an autographed baseball bat for his birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bat - a club used to hit a ball in sports
