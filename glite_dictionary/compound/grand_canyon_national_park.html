Grand Canyon National Park

1. PROPN

/ ɡrænd ˈkænjən ˌnæʃənl ˈpɑrk / (AmE), / ɡrænd ˈkænjən ˌnæʃənəl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in northern Arizona, USA, that encompasses the Grand Canyon and preserves its unique geological formations and natural history
national park in Arizona, USA

Examples

Every year, millions of visitors travel to Grand Canyon National Park to witness its breathtaking views.
The Colorado River flows through Grand Canyon National Park, creating some of the park's most famous landscapes.
Ranger-led tours in Grand Canyon National Park help visitors learn about its geology and wildlife.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Grand Canyon - a steep-sided canyon carved by the Colorado River in Arizona
