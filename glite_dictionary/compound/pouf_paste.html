pouf paste

1. NOUN

/ puf peɪst / (AmE), / puːf peɪst / (BrE)
a light batter used to make hollow pastry cases for various fillings
light batter for pastry cases

Examples

The chef filled the crisp pouf paste shells with a creamy chicken mixture.
You need to pipe the pouf paste onto a baking sheet before cooking.
Traditional recipes for éclairs often call for pouf paste as the base.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: choux pastry - light pastry dough used for éclairs and profiteroles
