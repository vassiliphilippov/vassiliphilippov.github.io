man-about-town

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæn əˈbaʊt ˌtaʊn / (AmE), / ˌmæn əˈbaʊt ˌtaʊn / (BrE)
a man who is socially active and frequently goes out to restaurants, theaters, parties, and other fashionable places, often in the pursuit of pleasure
socially active man often seen at fashionable places

Examples

He was known as a man-about-town who never missed an important social event.
After years as a man-about-town, he decided to settle down and start a family.
The magazine featured an article about the city's most eligible man-about-town.
