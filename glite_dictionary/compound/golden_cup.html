golden cup

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən kʌp / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən kʌp / (BrE)
a plant native to the Mexican highlands, known for its glossy clear yellow flowers and blue-grey, finely divided leaves
plant with yellow flowers and divided blue-grey leaves

Examples

The golden cup thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.
Gardeners often choose the golden cup for its striking yellow blooms and attractive foliage.
In spring, the hillside was painted with patches of golden cup among the wildflowers.
