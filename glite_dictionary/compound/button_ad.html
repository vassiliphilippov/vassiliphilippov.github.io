button ad

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌtən ˌæd / (AmE), / ˈbʌt.ən ˌæd / (BrE)
a small, usually rectangular or square online advertisement typically placed at the bottom of a webpage
small online advertisement on a webpage

Examples

Many companies use a button ad to promote their products on popular blogs.
A button ad at the bottom of the page caught my attention while I was scrolling.
The designer updated the website layout to better display each button ad.

Synset Examples

They are mostly ideal for co-branding exercises, where one website associates itself intrinsically with another via the use of a button ad instead of normal hyperlink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banner ad - a wide, horizontal online advertisement, often at the top of a webpage
