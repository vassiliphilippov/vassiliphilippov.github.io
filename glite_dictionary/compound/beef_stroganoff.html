beef Stroganoff

1. NOUN

/ bif ˈstrɑɡənˌɔf / (AmE), / biːf ˈstrɒɡənɒf / (BrE)
A dish made of sautéed strips of beef and mushrooms in a sour cream sauce, typically served with noodles.
Russian dish of beef in sour cream sauce

Examples

For dinner, she prepared a delicious beef Stroganoff with homemade noodles.
The restaurant's specialty is beef Stroganoff served with a side of fresh vegetables.
I've never tried to cook beef Stroganoff before, but the recipe looks simple.

Etymology

From Russian 'Stroganov', the name of a Russian family after which the dish is named.
