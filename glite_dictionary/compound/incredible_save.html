incredible save

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈkrɛdəbəl seɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈkrɛdɪbəl seɪv / (BrE)
an outstanding act of blocking or preventing a goal, especially in sports
exceptional goal-blocking action in sports

Examples

The goalkeeper's incredible save stunned the crowd in the final minute.
She pulled off an incredible save to keep the team in the match.
Fans are still talking about that incredible save during last night's game.

Synset Examples

The goalie made an incredible save in the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: save - an act of stopping the ball from entering the goal (sports)
