culture vulture

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌltʃɚ ˌvʌltʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃə ˌvʌltʃə / (BrE)
a person with a strong and enthusiastic interest in cultural activities, especially the arts
person keenly interested in the arts
informal

Examples

Her friends describe her as a real culture vulture because she never misses a new play or exhibition.
As a culture vulture, he spends his weekends at museums and galleries.
Despite being a culture vulture, he also enjoys watching sports on occasion.

Synset Examples

He’s such a culture vulture, always seeking new experiences.
As a culture vulture, she attends every exhibition.
They call her a culture vulture for her love of the arts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: art lover - person who loves or appreciates art
