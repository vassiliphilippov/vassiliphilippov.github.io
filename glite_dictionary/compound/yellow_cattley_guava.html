yellow cattley guava

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈkætli ˈɡwɑːvə / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈkætlɪ ˈɡwɑːvə / (BrE)
a small tropical shrub or tree (Psidium cattleyanum) that bears small, yellowish edible fruit
tropical shrub with yellowish fruit

Examples

The yellow cattley guava produces sweet fruit that is highly valued in tropical regions.
Farmers often grow yellow cattley guava for its aromatic, edible berries.
Birds are attracted to the ripe fruit of the yellow cattley guava.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guava - a tropical fruit or the tree that bears it (genus Psidium)
