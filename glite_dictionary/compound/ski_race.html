ski race

1. NOUN

/ ski ˌreɪs / (AmE), / ski ˌreɪs / (BrE)
a competition in which people wearing skis race against each other, usually over a set course
race competition on skis

Examples

The annual ski race attracts thousands of spectators to the mountain resort.
He trained for months to compete in his first ski race.
Weather conditions forced the organizers to cancel the ski race this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skiing - the sport or activity of traveling over snow on skis
  • Synonym: race - a competition of speed
