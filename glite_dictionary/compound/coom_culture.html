coom culture

1. NOUN

/ kum ˈkʌltʃɚ / (AmE), / kuːm ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
A subculture, especially online, that centers around sexual themes, memes, and jokes often related to excessive sexual desire or behavior.
internet subculture focused on sexual topics
slang

Examples

Many forums have been criticized for fostering coom culture and explicit discussions.
Popular memes about coom culture are often shared on social media.
Some users reject coom culture, calling it unhealthy or obsessive.

Synset Examples

The meme about coom culture went viral overnight.
He joked about coom-related topics at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incel - member of an online subculture of involuntarily celibate men
  • Synonym: meme culture - internet culture oriented around memes

Etymology

From 'coom', a slang spelling of 'cum' (slang for semen), popularized in Internet memes.
