witches' brew

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪtʃɪz bruː / (AmE), / ˈwɪtʃɪz bruː / (BrE)
a dangerous or frightening mixture of different elements, often with harmful or unpredictable effects
dangerous mixture; harmful combination

Examples

The city was gripped by a witches' brew of political unrest and economic instability.
His addiction was fueled by a witches' brew of drugs and alcohol.
The report warned of a witches' brew of threats facing the region.

Synset Examples

a witches' brew of gangsters and terrorists
mixing dope and alcohol creates a witches' brew

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mixture - a combination of different substances or elements
  • Synonym: cocktail - an often figurative blend of different components, especially harmful or potent ones

Etymology

From the image of witches in folklore brewing magical or poisonous potions.
