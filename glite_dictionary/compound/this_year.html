this year

1. NOUN

/ ðɪs jɪr / (AmE), / ðɪs jɪə / (BrE)
the current calendar year, referring to the period from January 1 to December 31 of the present year
the current calendar year

Examples

This year has been full of unexpected challenges for everyone.
I'm hoping to travel more later this year when things improve.
Our company's profits have increased significantly this year.

Synset Examples

I can't believe how this year has been.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: last year - the previous calendar year
  • Antonym: next year - the calendar year after the current one
