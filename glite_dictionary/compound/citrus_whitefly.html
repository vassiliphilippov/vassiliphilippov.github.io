citrus whitefly

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪtrəs ˈwaɪtˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪtrəs ˈwaɪtˌflʌɪ / (BrE)
a small whitefly (Dialeurodes citri) that is a common pest of citrus trees, feeding on their leaves and weakening the plant
pest insect of citrus trees

Examples

The citrus whitefly can cause significant damage to orange groves if not controlled.
Farmers often use natural predators to keep the citrus whitefly population in check.
Infestation by the citrus whitefly leads to the yellowing and dropping of leaves on citrus plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whitefly - a small flying insect that is a common plant pest
