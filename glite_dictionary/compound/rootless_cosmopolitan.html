rootless cosmopolitan

1. NOUN

/ ˈrutləs ˌkɑzməˈpɒlɪtən / (AmE), / ˈruːtləs ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtən / (BrE)
a person who feels no permanent attachment to any one country or culture, often embracing multiple identities or places as home
person with no fixed cultural or national ties

Examples

After years of moving from city to city, he identified as a rootless cosmopolitan.
The novel tells the story of a rootless cosmopolitan struggling to find a sense of belonging.
Some critics accused the artist of being a rootless cosmopolitan disconnected from local traditions.

Synset Examples

She embraced her identity as a rootless cosmopolitan traveler.
As a rootless cosmopolitan, she felt at home everywhere.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: cosmopolitan - a person who is at home in any country; someone free from local prejudices

Etymology

Formed from 'rootless' (lacking roots or permanent attachments) and 'cosmopolitan' (from Greek 'kosmopolitēs', citizen of the world).
