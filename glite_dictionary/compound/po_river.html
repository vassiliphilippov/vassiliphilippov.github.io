Po River

1. PROPN

/ poʊ ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / pəʊ ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
the longest river in Italy, flowing eastward across northern Italy into the Adriatic Sea
major river in northern Italy

Examples

The fertile valley created by the Po River is crucial for Italian agriculture.
Several important cities, including Turin, are located along the Po River.
During our trip to Italy, we crossed the Po River by train.
