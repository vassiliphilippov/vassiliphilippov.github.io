forward-moving

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrwərd ˈmuːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːwəd ˈmuːvɪŋ / (BrE)
moving in a forward direction or advancing ahead
moving forward; advancing

Examples

The forward-moving train slowly accelerated out of the station.
We support forward-moving policies that encourage innovation and progress.
Her forward-moving attitude helped the team achieve its goals despite the challenges.
