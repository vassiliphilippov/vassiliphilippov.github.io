newspaper advertisement

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər ˌædvərˈtaɪzmənt / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə ˌædvəˈtaɪzmənt / (BrE)
an advertisement that is printed and published in a newspaper
advertisement published in a newspaper

Examples

He placed a newspaper advertisement to sell his old car.
The company launched a large newspaper advertisement to promote its newest product.
Many people still look for jobs through the newspaper advertisements in the Sunday edition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classified ad - small advertisement in a newspaper, often organized by category
  • Synonym: advertisement - notice or announcement promoting a product, service, or event
