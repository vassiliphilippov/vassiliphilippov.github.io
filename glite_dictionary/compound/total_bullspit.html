total bullspit

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.təl ˈbʊl.spɪt / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.tl̩ ˈbʊl.spɪt / (BrE)
Nonsense or a statement that is deliberately false; a euphemistic form of 'bullshit.'
nonsense; a euphemistic falsehood
informal slang

Examples

Everything he said during the meeting was total bullspit, and nobody believed a word.
You expect me to fall for that total bullspit? Try again.
Calling his elaborate excuses total bullspit was putting it mildly.

Synset Examples

His excuse for being late was total bullspit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullshit - nonsense or lies; crude slang term
  • Antonym: truth - the quality or state of being true

Etymology

Formed as a euphemistic alteration of 'bullshit', substituting 'spit' for the vulgar term.
