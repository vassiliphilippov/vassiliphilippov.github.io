in the name of

1. ADP

/ ɪn ðə neɪm ʌv / (AmE), / ɪn ðə neɪm ɒv / (BrE)
with the authority or sanction of (someone or something), especially as invoked in official commands or proclamations.
by authority of someone or something

Examples

He ordered the crowd to disperse in the name of the city council.
The soldiers acted in the name of their country.
The officer shouted, "Stop, in the name of the law!"

Synset Examples

halt in the name of the law
