gate rape

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪt reɪp / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪt reɪp / (BrE)
A term referring to sexual assault or invasive searching occurring at the entrance of an event, especially when involving inappropriate physical contact during security checks.
sexual assault at event entry
offensive

Examples

Several attendees reported incidents of gate rape after the new security procedures were enforced.
The documentary highlighted the trauma caused by gate rape at large-scale concerts.
Activists demanded better training for staff to prevent gate rape during event screenings.

Synset Examples

The term 'gate rape' describes a serious violation at events.
The festival implemented stricter measures to prevent gate rape.
They campaigned against gate rape to raise awareness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexual assault - unwanted sexual contact or behavior

Etymology

Formed from 'gate' (entrance) and 'rape' (sexual assault), referencing reported incidents at event security checkpoints.
