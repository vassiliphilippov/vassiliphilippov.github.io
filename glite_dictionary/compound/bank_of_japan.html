Bank of Japan

1. PROPN

/ ˌbæŋk əv dʒəˈpæn / (AmE), / ˌbæŋk əv dʒəˈpan / (BrE)
the central bank of Japan, responsible for issuing currency and implementing monetary policy
Japan's central bank

Examples

The Bank of Japan announced new monetary policy measures to stimulate the economy.
Currency exchange rates were affected by the latest decision of the Bank of Japan.
Many economists closely follow announcements from the Bank of Japan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: central bank - a national bank that provides financial and banking services for its country's government and commercial banking system
