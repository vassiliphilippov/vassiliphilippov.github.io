dilleniid dicot family

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈlɛniɪd ˈdaɪkɒt ˈfæmɪli / (AmE), / dɪˈleniɪd ˈdʌɪkɒt ˈfamɪli / (BrE)
a family of advanced dicotyledonous trees, shrubs, or herbs found within the order Dilleniidae.
a family of advanced dicotyledonous plants

Examples

Many species in the dilleniid dicot family exhibit unique floral structures.
Botanists have reclassified some members formerly placed in the dilleniid dicot family.
Research on the dilleniid dicot family has improved our understanding of plant evolution.
