pit nutter

1. NOUN

/ pɪt ˈnʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / pɪt ˈnʌtə / (BrE)
An extremely enthusiastic fan of motorsport, especially one who is obsessive or fanatical about car racing.
extremely enthusiastic motorsport fan
informal

Examples

Jake is such a pit nutter; he knows every driver's pit stop time by heart.
The pit nutter in the stands waved a huge flag for his favorite racing team.
Only a real pit nutter would camp out all night before a grand prix.

Synset Examples

Everyone knows he's a pit nutter when it comes to racing.
You can always find a pit nutter at the racetrack.
The pit nutter was obsessed with his modified car.
