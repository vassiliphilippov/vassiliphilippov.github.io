gallimaufry of flavors

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡælɪˈmɔːfri əv ˈfleɪvɚz / (AmE), / ˌɡælɪˈmɔːfri əv ˈfleɪvəz / (BrE)
a diverse mixture or jumble of different tastes or culinary styles
mixture of different flavors

Examples

The chef's new tasting menu is a gallimaufry of flavors from around the world.
At the street fair, we sampled a gallimaufry of flavors from dozens of different food stalls.
Her homemade soup turned into a gallimaufry of flavors, blending spices and ingredients from several recipes.

Synset Examples

The buffet was a gallimaufry of flavors and cuisines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medley - a varied mixture of people or things
  • Synonym: jumble - an untidy or confused mixture

Etymology

From French 'galimafrée' (stew), evolved to mean any jumble or medley.
