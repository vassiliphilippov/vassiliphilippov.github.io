love and loss

1. NOUN

/ lʌv ənd lɔs / (AmE), / lʌv ənd lɒs / (BrE)
the combined experiences or themes of deep affection and the grief that follows its absence or end, often discussed in literature, art, and personal relationships
affection and subsequent grief as a theme

Examples

Many poems explore the universal experience of love and loss.
The movie beautifully captures the pain and hope that come with love and loss.
His music often reflects themes of love and loss throughout his career.

Synset Examples

The themes of love and loss resonate throughout the novel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grief - intense sorrow, especially caused by someone's death
  • Synonym: heartbreak - overwhelming distress, especially from losing love
