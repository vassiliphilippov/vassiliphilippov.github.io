in the interest of

1. ADP

/ ɪn ðə ˈɪntrɪst əv / (AmE), / ɪn ði ˈɪntrəst ɒv / (BrE)
for the benefit or sake of (something or someone)
for the benefit of; for the sake of

Examples

All decisions were made in the interest of public safety.
She skipped her vacation in the interest of finishing the project on time.
The company reduced costs in the interest of remaining competitive.

Synset Examples

The lawyer acted in the interest of justice for her client.
They reopened the case in the interest of justice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for the sake of - for the purpose or benefit of
  • Synonym: on behalf of - representing or for the benefit of someone
