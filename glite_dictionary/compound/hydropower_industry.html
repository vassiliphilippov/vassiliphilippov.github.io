hydropower industry

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪdroʊˌpaʊ.ɚ ˈɪn.də.stri / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.drəʊˌpaʊ.ə ˈɪn.də.stri / (BrE)
the sector comprising all industries involved in generating electricity and energy from falling or running water
sector producing power from water

Examples

The hydropower industry is a key player in the transition to renewable energy sources.
Many countries are investing more in the hydropower industry to reduce carbon emissions.
Careers in the hydropower industry can range from engineering to environmental management.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hydroelectric power - electricity produced from the energy of moving water

Etymology

Compound of 'hydropower' (from 'hydro-' meaning 'water' + 'power') and 'industry'.
