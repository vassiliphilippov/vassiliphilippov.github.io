telephone line

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlɪˌfoʊn laɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn laɪn / (BrE)
a wire or cable that carries telephone signals between two points
wire or cable for phone signals

Examples

During the storm, the telephone line was knocked down by a falling tree branch.
She waited patiently for the technician to repair the telephone line so she could make a call.
Many rural areas still rely on a single telephone line for all communications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telephone wire - a wire used to transmit telephone signals

2. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌfoʊn laɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn laɪn / (BrE)
a physical wire or circuit that provides a connection for transmitting telephone calls
a wire for telephone connection

Examples

We had to wait several days for the telephone line to be installed in our new house.
The telephone line went dead during the thunderstorm, cutting off all calls.
Many businesses still depend on a dedicated telephone line for customer support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phone line - a line or circuit providing telephone connectivity
