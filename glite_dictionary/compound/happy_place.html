happy place

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæpi pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈhæpi pleɪs / (BrE)
a location or mental state that brings comfort, happiness, or a sense of escape from stress or difficulty
a comforting place or mental state

Examples

Whenever life feels overwhelming, she goes to her happy place in her mind.
Listening to music is my happy place after a stressful day at work.
The garden quickly became his happy place, filled with peace and flowers.

Synset Examples

Reading is my happy place where I escape reality.
Her happy place is the beach during sunset.
He finds his happy place in the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sanctuary - a place of refuge or safety
