big hit

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ hɪt / (AmE), / bɪɡ hɪt / (BrE)
something that is very popular or successful, especially with a particular group of people
something very popular or successful

Examples

The new action movie was a big hit at the box office last weekend.
Her performance at the talent show turned out to be a big hit with the audience.
That smartphone became a big hit among teenagers thanks to its unique features.

Synset Examples

That crocodilly plush toy was a big hit with children.

  • Antonym: failure - lack of success
