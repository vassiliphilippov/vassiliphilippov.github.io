starfish flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑrˌfɪʃ ˈflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌfɪʃ ˈflaʊə / (BrE)
A succulent plant of the genus Stapelia, notable for its leafless toothed stems and large, often star-shaped flowers that emit a strong, unpleasant odor.
tropical succulent plant with star-shaped, foul-smelling flowers

Examples

The starfish flower attracted flies with its foul-smelling, star-shaped bloom.
Gardeners are often surprised by the size and odor of the starfish flower when it blooms indoors.
Because of its striking appearance, the starfish flower is a popular plant among succulent collectors.
