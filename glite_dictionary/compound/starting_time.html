starting time

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑrtɪŋ taɪm / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtɪŋ taɪm / (BrE)
the scheduled time at which something is planned to begin
scheduled beginning time

Examples

Please arrive at least 15 minutes before the starting time of the meeting.
The concert’s starting time was delayed due to technical issues.
Check your ticket for the exact starting time of the movie.

Synset Examples

they got an early start

Related Terms

  • Synonym: start time - the time at which something begins
  • Antonym: finishing time - the time at which something ends or is scheduled to end
