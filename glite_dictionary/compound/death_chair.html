death chair

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛθ tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈdɛθ tʃɛː / (BrE)
a chair used as an instrument of execution by electrocution, resembling an ordinary seat for one person
chair for electrocution execution

Examples

Many condemned prisoners feared the death chair more than any other form of execution.
The old prison still houses an original death chair from the early 20th century.
He was convicted and ultimately faced the death chair for his crimes.

Synset Examples

the murderer was sentenced to die in the chair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electric chair - a chair used to execute a condemned person by electrocution
