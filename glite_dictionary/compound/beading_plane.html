beading plane

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːdɪŋ pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈbiːdɪŋ pleɪn / (BrE)
a hand tool with a concave blade used to cut decorative mouldings with bead shapes into wood surfaces
woodworking tool for making bead mouldings

Examples

The cabinetmaker used a beading plane to add intricate trim to the wooden panels.
Before the arrival of electric routers, the beading plane was essential for creating decorative edges.
She restored an antique beading plane and now uses it in traditional woodworking projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moulding plane - a plane used for forming mouldings in wood
