jamjar

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒæmˌdʒɑr / (AmE), / ˈdʒamˌdʒɑː / (BrE)
A glass jar used for storing jams, jellies, or other preserves.
jar for jams or preserves

Examples

She saved every jamjar to reuse for homemade pickles.
After breakfast, only a spoonful of honey remained in the jamjar.
The shelf was lined with a variety of jamjars filled with colorful preserves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jar - a wide-mouthed container, usually made of glass or pottery
