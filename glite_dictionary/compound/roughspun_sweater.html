roughspun sweater

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌfˌspʌn ˈswɛtɚ / (AmE), / ˈrʌfˌspʌn ˈswɛtə / (BrE)
a sweater knitted from coarse, unfinished wool, giving it a rough texture
a sweater made from coarse, unfinished wool

Examples

He pulled on his roughspun sweater before heading out into the chilly morning.
The coarse fibers of her roughspun sweater scratched her skin but kept her warm.
A traditional roughspun sweater adds rustic charm to any winter outfit.

Synset Examples

He wore a roughspun sweater for extra warmth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweater - a warm knitted piece of clothing worn on the upper body
