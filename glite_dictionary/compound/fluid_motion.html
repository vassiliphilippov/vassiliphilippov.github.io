fluid motion

1. NOUN

/ ˈfluːɪd ˈmoʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈfluːɪd ˈməʊʃən / (BrE)
smooth and graceful movement, appearing continuous and without abrupt changes
smooth, graceful movement

Examples

The dancer captivated the audience with her fluid motion across the stage.
The robot's arm moved with unexpected fluid motion, mimicking the human form.
Through years of practice, his handwriting developed a fluid motion that made it uniquely beautiful.

Synset Examples

The artist created a fluid motion in her painting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graceful movement - movement that is attractive and elegant
  • Antonym: jerky motion - movement that is abrupt and lacking smoothness
