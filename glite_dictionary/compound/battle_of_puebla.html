Battle of Puebla

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬l əv ˈpwɛblə / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˈpwɛblə / (BrE)
A battle fought on May 5, 1862, in which Mexican forces defeated the French army near the city of Puebla.
1862 battle—Mexican victory over French forces

Examples

The Battle of Puebla is celebrated annually in Mexico as Cinco de Mayo.
Historians regard the Battle of Puebla as a turning point in Mexican history.
Despite being outnumbered, Mexican troops won the Battle of Puebla against the French.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Puebla,' the name of the city where the battle occurred; 'battle' from Old French 'bataille.'
