word on the wire

1. NOUN

/ wɝd ɑn ðə waɪɚ / (AmE), / wɜːd ɒn ðə waɪə / (BrE)
informal news or information that is circulating through communication channels, especially unofficially or by rumor.
informal circulating news or information
informal

Examples

There’s word on the wire that the company is hiring again.
She got the latest word on the wire about the upcoming event.
I heard through word on the wire that their new product launches next month.

Synset Examples

The news came through word on the wire about the layoffs.
There’s word on the wire about a possible merger.
I received word on the wire that the concert is sold out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rumor - a currently circulating story or report of uncertain or doubtful truth
  • Synonym: gossip - casual or unconstrained conversation or reports about other people, typically involving details that are not confirmed as true
