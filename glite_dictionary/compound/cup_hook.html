cup hook

1. NOUN

/ kʌp hʊk / (AmE), / kʌp hʊk / (BrE)
a small, curved metal hook that is screwed into a surface, typically the underside of a shelf, and used for hanging cups or mugs by their handles
small hook for hanging cups or mugs

Examples

She installed a cup hook under the shelf to hang her favorite tea mug.
You can find a cup hook in most hardware stores for kitchen organization.
The cup hook was sturdy enough to hold even the heaviest mugs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hook - a curved piece of metal for hanging things
