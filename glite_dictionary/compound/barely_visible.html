barely visible

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛrli ˈvɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈbeəli ˈvɪzɪbəl / (BrE)
difficult or nearly impossible to see; only just perceptible to sight
hard to see or just perceptible

Examples

The text on the faded sign was barely visible even in broad daylight.
Through the thick fog, the mountain peak was barely visible to the hikers below.
Her handwriting was so light that it was barely visible on the page.

Synset Examples

On smoggy days, the skyline is barely visible.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: invisible - impossible to be seen
  • Degree Higher: visible - able to be seen
