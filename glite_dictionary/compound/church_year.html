church year

1. NOUN

/ tʃɝːtʃ jɪr / (AmE), / tʃɜːtʃ jɪə / (BrE)
the annual cycle of seasons and feast days observed in the Christian church calendar, including holidays such as Christmas and Easter
Christian ecclesiastical calendar cycle

Examples

The church year begins with Advent and ends with the feast of Christ the King.
Many Christian traditions observe special events throughout the church year.
During the church year, believers commemorate events from the life of Jesus and other saints.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liturgical year - the cycle of liturgical seasons in Christian churches
