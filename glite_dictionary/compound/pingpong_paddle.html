pingpong paddle

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪŋ.pɑŋ ˌpæd.əl / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋ.pɒŋ ˌpæd.əl / (BrE)
A small, flat bat with a short handle, used to strike the ball in table tennis.
bat used in table tennis

Examples

She gripped her pingpong paddle tightly, preparing for the next serve.
The rubber surface of his pingpong paddle was worn from years of use.
Each player must bring their own pingpong paddle to the tournament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: table tennis bat - the equivalent term mainly used in British English
