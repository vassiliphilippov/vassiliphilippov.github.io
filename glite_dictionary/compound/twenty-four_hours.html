twenty-four hours

1. NOUN

/ ˌtwɛnti ˈfɔr ˈaʊɚz / (AmE), / ˌtwɛnti ˈfɔː ˈaʊəz / (BrE)
the period of time it takes the Earth to make a complete rotation; a full day and night cycle
a full day (24 hours)

Examples

He promised to finish the work within twenty-four hours.
A day consists of twenty-four hours, divided into day and night.
Emergency services are available twenty-four hours a day.
