goose liver

1. NOUN

/ ɡus ˈlɪvɚ / (AmE), / ɡuːs ˈlɪvə / (BrE)
the liver of a goose, often used as food, especially in certain dishes and delicacies
liver of a goose, used as food

Examples

The chef prepared a delicious appetizer using goose liver and herbs.
She sampled goose liver pâté during her trip to France.
The restaurant is famous for its exquisite dishes made with goose liver.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foie gras - the fattened liver of a goose or duck, used as a luxury food product
