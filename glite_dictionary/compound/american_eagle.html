American eagle

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən ˈiɡəl / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
a large North American eagle with a white head and dark wings and body, also known as the bald eagle
North American eagle with white head

Examples

The American eagle is a symbol of freedom in the United States.
From his perch, the American eagle surveyed the river below for fish.
Many coins feature the image of an American eagle spreading its wings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bald eagle - the common name for the American eagle, Haliaeetus leucocephalus
