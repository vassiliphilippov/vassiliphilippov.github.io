street talk

1. NOUN

/ strit tɔk / (AmE), / striːt tɔːk / (BrE)
informal language or conversation typical of urban environments, often including slang and local expressions.
informal urban language or slang
informal

Examples

Many of the musicians in the city include street talk in their lyrics to connect with young audiences.
He picked up the local street talk quickly after moving to the neighborhood.
Teachers sometimes struggle to understand their students’ street talk outside the classroom.

Synset Examples
