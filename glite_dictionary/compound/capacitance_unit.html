capacitance unit

1. NOUN

/ kəˈpæsɪtəns ˈjunɪt / (AmE), / kəˈpæsɪtəns ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a unit used to measure the capacitance of an electrical component or circuit
unit of electrical capacitance

Examples

The capacitance unit used internationally is the farad.
Different capacitance units can be used depending on the scale of measurement required.
To calculate this value, make sure all numbers are in the same capacitance unit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farad - the SI unit of capacitance
