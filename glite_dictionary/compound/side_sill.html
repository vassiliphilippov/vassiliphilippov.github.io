side sill

1. NOUN

/ saɪd sɪl / (AmE), / saɪd sɪl / (BrE)
one of the longitudinal beams along each side of a railway vehicle, supporting the bodywork and often forming part of the floor structure.
longitudinal support beam of a railway vehicle

Examples

The engineer inspected the side sill for signs of rust or structural damage.
During restoration, they replaced the old side sills to ensure the safety of the carriage.
A crack was discovered near the side sill, prompting immediate maintenance work.
