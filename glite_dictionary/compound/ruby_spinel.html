ruby spinel

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːbi spɪˈnɛl / (AmE), / ˈruːbi spaɪˈnɛl / (BrE)
a variety of spinel, typically dark red, used as a gemstone and often resembling a ruby
a dark red spinel gemstone

Examples

The jeweler showed me a beautiful ruby spinel set in a silver ring.
Unlike a real ruby, a ruby spinel can be more affordable and just as attractive.
Many famous crown jewels have been found to contain ruby spinel instead of genuine rubies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spinel - a hard glassy mineral used as a gemstone
