garden forget-me-not

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdn̩ fərˈɡɛt mi nɑt / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn fəˈɡɛt mi nɒt / (BrE)
a small biennial or perennial plant of Europe, northern Africa, and western Asia, with blue, purple, or white flowers, commonly grown in gardens
a flowering garden plant with blue or purple blooms

Examples

Garden forget-me-not blooms early in spring with masses of tiny blue flowers.
Many gardeners choose to plant garden forget-me-not for its delicate colors and easy care.
The garden forget-me-not is often used to brighten shady corners in flower beds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forget-me-not - a small plant with blue flowers, genus Myosotis

Etymology

From 'garden' (cultivated plot) and 'forget-me-not', a common name for plants in the genus Myosotis.
