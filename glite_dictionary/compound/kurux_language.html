Kurux language

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊrʊks ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkʊrʊks ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken mainly in eastern India, especially by the Kurukh people.
Dravidian language of eastern India

Examples

The Kurux language is used for daily communication among the Kurukh people in Jharkhand.
Linguists have studied the Kurux language to understand its Dravidian roots.
Efforts are being made to preserve the Kurux language through education and literature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kurukh - another name for the Kurux language or its speakers
