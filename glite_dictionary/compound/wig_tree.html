wig tree

1. NOUN

/ wɪɡ triː / (AmE), / wɪɡ triː / (BrE)
An Old World shrub (Cotinus coggygria) with large plumes of yellowish, feathery flowers that resemble puffs of smoke.
Old World shrub with puffy yellow flowers

Examples

The wig tree in her garden produced beautiful feathery plumes every summer.
We planted a wig tree at the edge of the lawn for its striking appearance.
In autumn, the leaves of the wig tree turn vivid shades of red and orange.
