genus Lasthenia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs læsˈθiː.ni.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs lasˈθiː.nɪ.ə / (BrE)
A small genus of annual herbs native to the Pacific coast of North and South America, commonly known as goldfields.
genus of Pacific coastal herbs

Examples

The bright yellow flowers of genus Lasthenia often carpet California fields in spring.
Researchers studied the habitat preferences of genus Lasthenia in different coastal regions.
Several species within genus Lasthenia are popular among wildflower enthusiasts.

Etymology

From the genus name Lasthenia, named after a female disciple of Plato in Greek mythology.
