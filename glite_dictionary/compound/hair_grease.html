hair grease

1. NOUN

/ hɛr ɡris / (AmE), / heə ɡriːs / (BrE)
a thick, oily substance applied to the hair to add shine and moisture, or to style it
a thick hair styling product

Examples

He uses hair grease every morning to keep his hair smooth and shiny.
Some believe that hair grease can help with dry scalp and hair breakage.
The barber recommended a light hair grease to style my curly hair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pomade - a scented ointment applied to the hair for grooming
  • Synonym: hair oil - a liquid product used to add moisture and shine to hair
