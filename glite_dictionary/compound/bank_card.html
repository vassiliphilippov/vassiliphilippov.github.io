bank card

1. NOUN

/ bæŋk kɑrd / (AmE), / bæŋk kɑːd / (BrE)
a plastic card issued by a bank, used to make electronic payments or withdraw cash from ATMs
plastic payment card from a bank

Examples

You need your bank card to withdraw cash from the ATM.
She used her bank card to pay for groceries at the store.
If you lose your bank card, contact your bank immediately to block it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: debit card - a card allowing the holder to transfer money electronically from their bank account
  • Degree Higher: credit card - a card allowing the holder to purchase goods or services on credit
