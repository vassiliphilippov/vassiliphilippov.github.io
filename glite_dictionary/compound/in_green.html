in green

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ɡrin / (AmE), / ɪn ɡriːn / (BrE)
(of a financial account or investment) showing a profit or a positive balance.
showing financial profit or gain

Examples

After a difficult year, her business is finally in green again.
Investors like seeing their portfolios in green at the end of the quarter.
He checks his stocks daily, hoping they will stay in green.

Synset Examples

He felt lucky, as his investment was finally in green.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in the red - showing a financial loss or negative balance
