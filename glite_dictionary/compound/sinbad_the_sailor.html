Sinbad the Sailor

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪnˌbæd ðə səˈleɪlər / (AmE), / ˈsɪnbæd ðə səˈleɪlə / (BrE)
a legendary sailor and hero in the tales of the Arabian Nights, known for recounting his extraordinary sea adventures
legendary sailor from Arabian Nights

Examples

The tales of Sinbad the Sailor have inspired generations of adventurers and storytellers.
As a child, I was fascinated by the magical voyages of Sinbad the Sailor in the Arabian Nights.
The movie adaptation brought the exploits of Sinbad the Sailor to life with vibrant animation and music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sinbad - shortened name for Sinbad the Sailor, legendary Arabian Nights character

Etymology

From Arabic Sindbād, the name of the character in Middle Eastern folklore.
