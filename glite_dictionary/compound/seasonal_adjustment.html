seasonal adjustment

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːzənəl əˈdʒʌstmənt / (AmE), / ˈsiːzənəl əˈdʒʌstmənt / (BrE)
a statistical modification applied to data to account for predictable seasonal fluctuations within a year
statistical data correction for seasonal patterns

Examples

The economists made a seasonal adjustment to the unemployment data before presenting the report.
Retail sales figures often undergo seasonal adjustment to reflect true trends beyond holiday surges.
After applying a seasonal adjustment, the housing market appeared steadier throughout the year.

Synset Examples

seasonal adjustments for housing starts must be made in mid-winter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seasonal variation - fluctuation in data that occurs at regular intervals due to seasonal factors
