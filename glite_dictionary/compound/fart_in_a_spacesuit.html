fart in a spacesuit

1. NOUN

/ fɑrt ɪn ə ˈspeɪsuːt / (AmE), / fɑːt ɪn ə ˈspeɪsjuːt / (BrE)
a humorous reference to the unpleasant and potentially problematic effects of flatulence within a confined, sealed environment such as a spacesuit
flatulence in a confined space suit
informal

Examples

If you fart in a spacesuit, there's nowhere for the smell to escape.
Astronauts have to think about what happens if they fart in a spacesuit.
He used the phrase 'fart in a spacesuit' to describe an uncomfortable, self-made problem.

Synset Examples

Imagine the consequences if you fart in a spacesuit!
He joked that farting in a spacesuit could be dangerous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fart - to expel intestinal gas through the anus
