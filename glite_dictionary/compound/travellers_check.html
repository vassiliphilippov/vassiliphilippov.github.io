traveller's check

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævələrz tʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈtrævələz tʃɛk / (BrE)
A cheque issued by a bank or financial company, intended for use while travelling and paid to the bearer on presentation and signature.
pre-paid cheque for travellers

Examples

When traveling abroad, she carried a few traveller's checks for emergencies.
Many hotels still accept traveller's checks in addition to credit cards.
He replaced his lost wallet with cash and a new traveller's check at the bank.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bank draft - a cheque issued by a bank, withdrawn from its own funds
  • Antonym: personal check - a cheque drawn by an individual from their own account

Etymology

From traveler/traveller + check, originally used for safer transactions during travel.
