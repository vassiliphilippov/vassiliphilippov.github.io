fringed loosestrife

1. NOUN

/ frɪndʒd ˈluːsstraɪf / (AmE), / frɪndʒd ˈluːsstraɪf / (BrE)
a North American perennial herb (Lysimachia ciliata) of the primrose family, with yellow flowers and fringed leaves
North American herb with yellow flowers

Examples

We spotted a fringed loosestrife blooming by the edge of the lake.
The garden features native plants such as fringed loosestrife and wild columbine.
Many bees were gathering nectar from the fringed loosestrife in June.
