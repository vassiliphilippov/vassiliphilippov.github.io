fishing industry

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃɪŋ ˌɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪŋ ˌɪndəstri / (BrE)
the business sector involved in catching, processing, and selling fish and other seafood
sector involved in catching and selling fish

Examples

The collapse of the fishing industry devastated the region's economy.
Sustainable practices are essential for the future of the fishing industry.
Many coastal towns depend heavily on the fishing industry for employment.

Synset Examples

Invasive carp are disrupting local fishing industries.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: agriculture - the practice or science of farming, including cultivation of the soil and rearing of animals
  • Synonym: fishery - an area where fish are caught for commercial purposes or the activity of catching fish
