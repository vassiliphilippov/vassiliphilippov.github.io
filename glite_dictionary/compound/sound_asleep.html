sound asleep

1. ADJ

/ saʊnd əˈsliːp / (AmE), / saʊnd əˈsliːp / (BrE)
sleeping deeply and peacefully, not easily awakened
sleeping deeply

Examples

The baby was sound asleep in her crib despite the noise.
I checked on him at midnight and found him sound asleep on the couch.
After the long hike, she was sound asleep within minutes of lying down.

Synset Examples

lying fast asleep on the sofa
it would be cruel to wake him: he's sound asleep

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fast asleep - sleeping deeply; in a state of deep sleep
