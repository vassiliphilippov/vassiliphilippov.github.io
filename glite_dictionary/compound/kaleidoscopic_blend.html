kaleidoscopic blend

1. NOUN

/ kəˌlaɪdəˈskɑpɪk blɛnd / (AmE), / kəˌlaɪdəˈskɒpɪk blend / (BrE)
a mixture that is made up of many different, often colorful, parts or elements
mixture of many colorful elements

Examples

The market offered a kaleidoscopic blend of sights, sounds, and scents from around the world.
His paintings are known for their kaleidoscopic blend of abstract shapes and vivid colors.
The festival featured a kaleidoscopic blend of music, dance, and culinary traditions.

Synset Examples

Her dress was a kaleidoscopic blend of vibrant hues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medley - a varied mixture of people or things
  • Synonym: mixture - a combination of different things

Etymology

From 'kaleidoscopic' (relating to a kaleidoscope, from Greek 'kalos' (beautiful), 'eidos' (form), and 'skopein' (to look)) and 'blend' (from Old English 'blandan', to mix).
