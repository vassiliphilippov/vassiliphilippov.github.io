orange-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ saɪzd / (BrE)
having the approximate size of an orange.
about the size of an orange

Examples

The doctor found an orange-sized lump on her shoulder.
He carried an orange-sized stone out of the garden.
The hailstones were orange-sized after the severe storm.
