draft copy

1. NOUN

/ dræft ˈkɑpi / (AmE), / drɑːft ˈkɒpi / (BrE)
a preliminary version of a written work, subject to revision before the final version is produced
preliminary version of a written work

Examples

She made several changes to the draft copy before sending it to her supervisor.
Please review the draft copy and let me know if you have any comments.
Each draft copy of the report was carefully checked for errors.

Synset Examples

a preliminary draft
the final draft of the constitution

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: final draft - the last version of a document before publication or submission
