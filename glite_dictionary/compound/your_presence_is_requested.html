your presence is requested

1. X

/ jɔr ˈprɛzəns ɪz rɪˈkwɛstəd / (AmE), / jɔː ˈprɛzəns ɪz rɪˈkwɛstɪd / (BrE)
used as a formal way to invite someone to attend an event or gathering.
formal invitation to attend

Examples

Your presence is requested at the annual board meeting this Friday evening.
The invitation stated that your presence is requested at the wedding ceremony.
At the start of the gala, an usher approached and said, 'Your presence is requested in the main hall.'

Synset Examples

Milord, your presence is requested at the banquet.
