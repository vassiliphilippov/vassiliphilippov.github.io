knockout drops

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑkaʊt drɑps / (AmE), / ˈnɒkaʊt drɒps / (BrE)
A mixture, especially of chloral hydrate and alcohol, given secretly to make someone unconscious.
drug mixture to make unconscious

Examples

The criminal slipped knockout drops into his victim’s drink to render him unconscious.
She was wary of strangers after reading stories about knockout drops being used in bars.
Tests revealed that the wine had been laced with knockout drops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mickey finn - a drink laced with a drug to make a person unconscious
