funereal atmosphere

1. NOUN

/ fəˈnɪriəl ˈætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / fjʊˈnɪəriəl ˈatməsfɪə / (BrE)
a somber or mournful mood or environment, similar to that of a funeral
gloomy or mournful mood or setting

Examples

There was a funereal atmosphere in the office after the layoffs were announced.
The courtroom maintained a funereal atmosphere during the sentencing hearing.
A funereal atmosphere hung over the city in the aftermath of the tragedy.

Synset Examples

The atmosphere was funereal after the bad news.
The decorations gave the event a funereal vibe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: somber - dark or dull in color or tone; gloomy
  • Antonym: cheerful atmosphere - a bright or happy mood or setting
