golf shot

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf ʃɑt / (AmE), / ɡɒlf ʃɒt / (BrE)
an act of swinging a golf club to hit the ball in the game of golf
the act of hitting a golf ball

Examples

He took a powerful golf shot that sent the ball straight down the fairway.
Her first golf shot landed just a few feet from the hole.
A successful golf shot requires concentration and good technique.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stroke - a single hit with a club, especially in golf
