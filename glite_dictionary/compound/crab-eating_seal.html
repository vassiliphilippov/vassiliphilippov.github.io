crab-eating seal

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræbˌiːtɪŋ siːl / (AmE), / ˈkræbˌiːtɪŋ siːl / (BrE)
a silvery grey seal native to Antarctica that feeds mainly on crustaceans
Antarctic seal that eats crustaceans

Examples

The crab-eating seal is well adapted to the icy waters of Antarctica.
Unlike its name suggests, the crab-eating seal primarily consumes small crustaceans, not crabs.
Researchers tracked several crab-eating seals to study their migration patterns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seal - a marine mammal with flippers
