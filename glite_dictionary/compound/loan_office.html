loan office

1. NOUN

/ loʊn ˈɑfɪs / (AmE), / ləʊn ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
A shop or business where money is lent to people who leave personal property as security for the loan.
shop where loans are made with property as security

Examples

He took his watch to a loan office when he needed quick cash.
The loan office was filled with bicycles, jewelry, and electronics left as collateral.
After losing his job, he visited the loan office to borrow money against his old guitar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pawnbroker - a person or business that lends money at interest on personal property left as security

2. NOUN

/ loʊn ˈɑfɪs / (AmE), / ləʊn ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
an office where loans are arranged, managed, and repaid
place where loans are handled

Examples

The loan office is open during business hours for customers who need assistance with their accounts.
She visited the loan office to discuss repayment options for her student loan.
Every branch has a dedicated loan office to help people apply for mortgages and other types of credit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bank - an institution for receiving, lending, and safeguarding money
  • Synonym: credit union - a cooperative financial institution that provides banking services to its members
