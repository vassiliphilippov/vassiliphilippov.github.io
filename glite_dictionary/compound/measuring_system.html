measuring system

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛʒərɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒ(ə)rɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
An instrument or set of instruments designed to determine the extent, amount, quantity, or degree of something.
instrument for determining quantities

Examples

The laboratory was equipped with a sophisticated measuring system for monitoring air quality.
Engineers installed a new measuring system to track temperature changes inside the facility.
Before starting the experiment, she calibrated the measuring system to ensure accurate results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: measuring instrument - device used for determining amounts or dimensions
