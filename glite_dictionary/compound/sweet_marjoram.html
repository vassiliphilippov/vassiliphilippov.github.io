sweet marjoram

1. NOUN

/ swit ˈmɑrdʒərəm / (AmE), / swiːt ˈmɑːr.dʒər.əm / (BrE)
an aromatic herb (Origanum majorana) native to the Mediterranean region, valued for its fragrant leaves used in cooking and herbal remedies
Mediterranean aromatic herb used in cooking

Examples

She sprinkled a pinch of sweet marjoram onto the roasted vegetables for extra flavor.
In traditional medicine, sweet marjoram is sometimes used to soothe digestive discomfort.
The scent of sweet marjoram filled the kitchen as she prepared the sauce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oregano - an aromatic herb closely related to sweet marjoram, also used in cooking
