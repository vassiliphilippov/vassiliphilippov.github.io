type slug

1. NOUN

/ taɪp slʌɡ / (AmE), / taɪp slʌɡ / (BrE)
A strip of type metal used for spacing in typesetting.
metal spacer in typesetting

Examples

The printer inserted a type slug between the words to adjust the spacing.
Original typesetting often required placing a type slug in empty areas.
A misplaced type slug caused uneven spacing in the old newspaper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slug - piece of type metal used for spacing in printing
