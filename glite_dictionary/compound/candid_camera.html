candid camera

1. NOUN

/ ˈkændɪd ˈkæmərə / (AmE), / ˈkændɪd ˈkamərə / (BrE)
A small, unobtrusive camera with a fast lens designed to take photographs without attracting attention.
miniature, fast-lens camera for unobtrusive photography

Examples

The detective used a candid camera to gather evidence without being noticed.
Photographers often rely on a candid camera to capture natural expressions.
His candid camera shots revealed everyday life in the city.
