derogatory use

1. NOUN

/ dəˈrɑɡətɔri juːs / (AmE), / dɪˈrɒɡətri juːs / (BrE)
the use of a word or expression in an offensive or disrespectful manner towards a person or group
offensive or disrespectful usage

Examples

The committee criticized the derogatory use of certain words in official documents.
She was offended by the derogatory use of her cultural background in that joke.
Teachers should be aware of the derogatory use of language among students.

Synset Examples

The community condemned the derogatory use of 'teenigger' in media.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pejorative - expressing contempt or disapproval
