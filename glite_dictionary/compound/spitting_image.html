spitting image

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪtɪŋ ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈspɪtɪŋ ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
A person or thing that looks exactly like someone or something else.
exact likeness; duplicate

Examples

She's the spitting image of her mother at that age.
When I met his brother, I realized they were the spitting image of each other.
People always say I'm the spitting image of my grandfather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double - a person who looks exactly like another
  • Synonym: lookalike - someone who closely resembles another person

Etymology

Probably an alteration of 'spit and image,' where 'spit' means 'exact likeness' in dialect.
