aromatic aster

1. NOUN

/ əˈɹoʊˌmætɪk ˈæstər / (AmE), / əˈɹəʊˌmætɪk ˈastə / (BrE)
a perennial plant with fragrant leaves and purple or lavender flowers, native to central North America, scientifically known as Symphyotrichum oblongifolium.
a North American fragrant-flowered aster

Examples

The aromatic aster adds a lovely scent and color to autumn gardens.
Many gardeners appreciate the resilience of the aromatic aster in dry conditions.
Butterflies are often attracted to the blooms of the aromatic aster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - plant of the genus Aster, with star-shaped flowers
