blue jasmine

1. NOUN

/ blu ˈdʒæzmɪn / (AmE), / bluː ˈdʒæzmɪn / (BrE)
a climbing plant of the southern United States with bluish-purple flowers, commonly cultivated for ornament
climbing plant with bluish-purple flowers

Examples

The garden trellis was covered with blue jasmine, its flowers adding a splash of color.
She planted blue jasmine along the fence for its lovely scent and attractive blooms.
Every spring, the blue jasmine in their yard blossomed with vibrant bluish-purple flowers.
