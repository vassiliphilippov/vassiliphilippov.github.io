acerb comment

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɝb ˈkɑːmɛnt / (AmE), / əˈsɜːb ˈkɒmɛnt / (BrE)
a remark or criticism that is harsh, bitter, or sharply critical
harsh or bitter remark

Examples

Her acerb comment left the entire team feeling demoralized.
A single acerb comment can ruin an otherwise positive discussion.
He was known for his acerb comments during meetings, which often stung.

Synset Examples

His acerb comments often hurt people's feelings.
She delivered an acerb critique of the presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caustic remark - a remark that is severely critical and biting
  • Synonym: sarcastic comment - a comment expressing mockery or irony, often to hurt or criticize
  • Antonym: kind comment - a friendly or considerate remark

Etymology

From Latin 'acerbus' (sour, harsh, bitter); 'comment' from Latin 'commentum' (invention, interpretation).
