carpenter's plane

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːrpəntərz pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpəntəz pleɪn / (BrE)
a hand tool with an adjustable blade, used by carpenters for smoothing or shaping wood surfaces
carpenter's tool for smoothing wood

Examples

He carefully ran the carpenter's plane along the edge of the table to smooth it.
The old carpenter's plane had a wooden handle and a steel blade.
After sharpening the blade, she used her carpenter's plane to finish the wooden door.

Synset Examples

the cabinetmaker used a plane for the finish work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plane - a hand tool for smoothing or shaping wood
