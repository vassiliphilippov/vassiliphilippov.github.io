deer trail

1. NOUN

/ dɪr treɪl / (AmE), / dɪə treɪl / (BrE)
a narrow path in the woods or fields formed by the repeated passage of deer
narrow path formed by deer

Examples

We followed the deer trail through the dense forest until we reached a clearing.
The fresh tracks on the deer trail indicated that several animals had recently passed by.
Hunters often use a deer trail to observe wildlife in its natural habitat.
