light diet

1. NOUN

/ laɪt ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / laɪt ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet consisting of easily digestible foods, typically prescribed for bedridden or recovering people and avoiding fried or highly seasoned items
easily digestible foods for recovery
medical

Examples

After her surgery, the doctor recommended a light diet to help her recovery.
A light diet usually excludes fried or spicy foods to avoid upsetting the stomach.
He was put on a light diet while he was in the hospital.
