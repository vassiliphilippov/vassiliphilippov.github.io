right-angled triangle

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪt ˌæŋgəld ˈtraɪˌæŋgəl / (AmE), / ˈraɪt ˌæŋɡld ˈtraɪˌæŋɡl / (BrE)
A triangle that has one angle equal to 90 degrees.
triangle with a right angle

Examples

A right-angled triangle has one angle measuring exactly 90 degrees.
You can use the Pythagorean theorem to solve problems involving a right-angled triangle.
The floor plan featured a right-angled triangle to maximize space usage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: isosceles triangle - triangle with two equal sides
  • Synonym: equilateral triangle - triangle with all sides equal
