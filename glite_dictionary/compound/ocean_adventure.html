ocean adventure

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊʃən ədˈvɛntʃər / (AmE), / ˈəʊʃən ədˈvɛntʃə / (BrE)
an exciting or unusual experience that takes place at sea or involves the ocean
exciting experience at sea

Examples

As a teenager, he dreamed of going on an ocean adventure and discovering hidden islands.
Her memoir describes a thrilling ocean adventure during a month-long sailing trip.
Children listened eagerly to the captain’s tale of his ocean adventure in a distant land.

Synset Examples

The fishwife shared tales of her ocean adventures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adventure - an exciting or unusual experience
