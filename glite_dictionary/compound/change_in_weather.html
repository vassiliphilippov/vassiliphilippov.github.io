change in weather

1. NOUN

/ tʃeɪndʒ ɪn ˈwɛðɚ / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ ɪn ˈwɛðə / (BrE)
a shift or alteration in atmospheric conditions, especially relating to temperature, precipitation, or wind
a shift in atmospheric conditions

Examples

A sudden change in weather forced the hikers to find shelter.
Farmers closely monitor any change in weather during the planting season.
The forecast predicted a change in weather by the end of the week.

Synset Examples

The barometer indicated a change in weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weather change - a variation in weather conditions
