the art of witchery

1. NOUN

/ ði ɑrt əv ˈwɪtʃəri / (AmE), / ði ˈɑːt əv ˈwɪtʃəri / (BrE)
the practice or skill of performing magic, especially as associated with witches
practice or skill of performing magic

Examples

The annual festival honors the art of witchery with music, costumes, and traditional rituals.
She devoted her life to mastering the art of witchery, studying ancient spells and potions.
Many legends tell of wise women who practiced the art of witchery to heal the sick and protect their villages.

Synset Examples

The festival celebrated the art of witchery and magic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: witchcraft - the practice of magic, especially using spells and rituals
  • Synonym: sorcery - the use of supernatural powers to influence events
