woodworking plane

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdˌwɝkɪŋ pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌwɜːkɪŋ pleɪn / (BrE)
a carpenter's hand tool with a sharp, adjustable blade for smoothing or shaping wood surfaces
carpenter's tool for smoothing wood

Examples

The craftsman adjusted his woodworking plane to achieve a perfectly smooth finish on the tabletop.
She inherited her grandfather's antique woodworking plane and still uses it in her workshop.
Before assembling the cabinet, he carefully passed a woodworking plane over each board to remove any roughness.

Synset Examples

the cabinetmaker used a plane for the finish work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plane - a tool used for shaping wood by shaving thin layers
