word hole

1. NOUN

/ wɝːd hoʊl / (AmE), / wɜːd həʊl / (BrE)
A confusing or complicated discussion lacking focus or clarity.
confusing or unfocused discussion
informal

Examples

He realized halfway through the meeting that they had fallen into a word hole with no clear outcome.
Her presentation quickly became a word hole as she lost her train of thought.
After an hour in that word hole, nobody remembered the original question.

Synset Examples

He got lost in a word hole during the discussion.
The meeting turned into a word hole with no clear direction.
She fell into a word hole while trying to explain her idea.
