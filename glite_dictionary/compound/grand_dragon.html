grand dragon

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡrænd ˈdræɡən / (AmE), / ˌɡrænd ˈdræɡən / (BrE)
a senior official in the Ku Klux Klan, historically in charge of a state or region within the organization.
senior Ku Klux Klan official

Examples

The notorious grand dragon was responsible for organizing Klan activities throughout the state.
During the trial, the former grand dragon testified about the group's hierarchy.
Historians have documented the influence held by a grand dragon in the early 20th-century Klan.
