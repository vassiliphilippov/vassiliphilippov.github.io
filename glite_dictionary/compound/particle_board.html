particle board

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrtɪkəl bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtɪkl bɔːd / (BrE)
A type of building material made from wood chips or shavings bonded together with resin and pressed into rigid sheets, commonly used in furniture and construction.
composite wood material

Examples

Many inexpensive cabinets are made from particle board rather than solid wood.
The desk fell apart after the particle board swelled from water damage.
He repaired the shelves using thick sheets of particle board cut to size.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chipboard - a type of board made from wood chips and resin
