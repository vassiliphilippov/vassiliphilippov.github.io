lieutenant JG

1. NOUN

/ luːˈtɛnənt ˌdʒeɪ ˈdʒiː / (AmE), / lɛfˈtɛnənt ˌdʒeɪ ˈdʒiː / (BrE)
a commissioned officer rank in the United States Navy or Coast Guard, below lieutenant and above ensign
US Navy/Coast Guard officer rank below lieutenant
american

Examples

After completing his training, he was promoted to lieutenant JG in the Navy.

Many officers serve as lieutenant JG before advancing further in their naval careers.
