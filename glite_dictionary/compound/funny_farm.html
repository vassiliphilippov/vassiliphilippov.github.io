funny farm

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌni fɑrm / (AmE), / ˈfʌni fɑːm / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) An old and informal term for a psychiatric hospital or mental institution.
slang for psychiatric hospital
slang offensive

Examples

After his breakdown, they sent him to the funny farm to recover.
She joked that if her job got any crazier, she'd end up in a funny farm.
Using terms like funny farm is now considered insensitive by mental health advocates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insane asylum - a hospital for people with mental illnesses
  • Synonym: psychiatric hospital - a hospital specializing in the treatment of mental disorders
