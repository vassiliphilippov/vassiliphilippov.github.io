first step

1. NOUN

/ fɝst stɛp / (AmE), / fɜːst stɛp / (BrE)
the initial action or stage in a process or series of events
initial action in a process

Examples

Taking the first step is often the hardest part of any new project.
Learning to ask questions is an important first step in problem-solving.
Her first step toward recovery was admitting she needed help.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beginning - the point in time or space at which something starts
  • Antonym: last step - the final action or stage in a process
