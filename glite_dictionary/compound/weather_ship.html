weather ship

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛðɚ ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə ʃɪp / (BrE)
An oceangoing vessel equipped to make meteorological observations.
ship for meteorological observations at sea

Examples

The weather ship transmitted data about the approaching storm to shore stations.
In the mid-20th century, a weather ship played a vital role in predicting Atlantic weather patterns.
After months at sea, the crew of the weather ship finally returned home.
