lemon curd

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛmən kɝd / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən kɜːd / (BrE)
A smooth, thick spread made from lemons, butter, eggs, and sugar, often used as a filling or topping for desserts.
sweet spread made from lemons

Examples

She spread lemon curd on her toast for breakfast.
The cake was filled with a tangy lemon curd and topped with whipped cream.
Homemade lemon curd tastes much better than store-bought versions.
