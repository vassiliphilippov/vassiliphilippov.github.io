library card

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪˌbrɛri kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.brəri kɑːd / (BrE)
an official card issued by a library that allows the holder to borrow books and access other services
card granting library borrowing privileges

Examples

You need a library card to check out books from the public library.
After moving to the city, she quickly applied for a library card.
He presented his library card to access the online resources.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reader's card - card allowing use of a library's resources
