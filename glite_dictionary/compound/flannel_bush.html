flannel bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈflænəl bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈflæn.əl bʊʃ / (BrE)
a handsome evergreen shrub of California and northern Mexico, with soft, downy lobed leaves and showy yellow flowers
evergreen shrub with yellow flowers

Examples

The garden's new flannel bush attracted attention with its bright yellow blossoms.
A mature flannel bush can grow quite large, making it an impressive feature in landscaping.
Its leaves feel soft to the touch, which is why the flannel bush got its name.

Etymology

Refers to the soft, flannel-like texture of the plant's leaves.
