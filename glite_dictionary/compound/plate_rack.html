plate rack

1. NOUN

/ pleɪt ræk / (AmE), / pleɪt ræk / (BrE)
a rack used for holding plates upright to dry after they have been washed
rack for drying plates

Examples

She placed the clean dishes in the plate rack to dry by the sink.
The kitchen had a wooden plate rack above the counter for easy access.
After washing the plates, he stacked them neatly on the plate rack.
