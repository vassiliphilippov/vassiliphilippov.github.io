doughnut machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊ.nət məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈdəʊ.nət məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used for frying large quantities of doughnuts at once, typically in commercial settings
machine for frying many doughnuts at once

Examples

The new bakery installed a doughnut machine to increase their daily production.
He watched the doughnut machine as it automatically fried and glazed dozens of treats.
Most large donut shops rely on a commercial doughnut machine for efficiency.
