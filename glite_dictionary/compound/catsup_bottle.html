catsup bottle

1. NOUN

/ ˈkætsʌp ˈbɑːtl̩ / (AmE), / ˈkætsʌp ˈbɒtl̩ / (BrE)
A bottle designed for holding and dispensing catsup.
bottle for holding catsup

Examples

She reached for the catsup bottle to add some flavor to her fries.
The restaurant keeps a catsup bottle at every table for customers.
After dropping the catsup bottle, there was a red mess on the kitchen floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ketchup bottle - a bottle for holding ketchup
