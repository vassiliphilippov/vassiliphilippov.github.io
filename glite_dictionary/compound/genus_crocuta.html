genus Crocuta

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs kroʊˈkjuːtə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs krəʊˈkjuːtə / (BrE)
A genus of the hyena family (Hyaenidae), which includes the spotted hyena.
genus of hyenas (includes the spotted hyena)

Examples

The genus Crocuta is best known for its only living member, the spotted hyena.
Fossil records suggest that the genus Crocuta had a much wider distribution in the past.
Scientists classified the animal as belonging to the genus Crocuta after a detailed analysis of its skull.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotted hyena - A species of hyena (Crocuta crocuta)

Etymology

From Latin 'Crocuta', traditional genus name in zoological taxonomy.
