ice hockey

1. NOUN

/ aɪs ˈhɑki / (AmE), / aɪs ˈhɒki / (BrE)
A team sport played on ice, where players use sticks to direct a puck into the opposing team's goal.
team sport played on ice with a puck

Examples

The local team practices ice hockey at the rink three times a week.
He has been watching professional ice hockey since he was a child.
Canada is well known for its passion for ice hockey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hockey - a team sport played on a field or ice where players use sticks to hit a puck or ball
