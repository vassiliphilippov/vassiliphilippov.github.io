multivitamin pill

1. NOUN

/ ˌmʌltiˈvaɪtəmən pɪl / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈvʌɪtəmɪn pɪl / (BrE)
a pill or tablet that contains a combination of different vitamins, often taken as a dietary supplement
pill containing several vitamins

Examples

She takes a multivitamin pill every morning to ensure she gets all the essential nutrients.
Doctors often recommend a multivitamin pill for people with dietary restrictions.
I forgot to buy my daily multivitamin pill when I was at the pharmacy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multivitamin - a preparation containing several vitamins

Etymology

From 'multi-' (many) + 'vitamin', indicating a product with several vitamins.
