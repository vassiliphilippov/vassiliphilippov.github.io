securities market

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈkjʊrɪtiz ˈmɑrkɪt / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərɪtiz ˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
An organized marketplace where stocks, bonds, and other securities are bought and sold by professional brokers.
market for trading securities

Examples

Investors closely watch the securities market for changes in stock prices and trends.
New regulations have significantly affected the operation of the securities market this year.
The company was listed on the national securities market to raise capital for expansion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stock exchange - a marketplace for buying and selling stocks and other securities
