hillbilly hot tub

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪlˌbɪli hɑt tʌb / (AmE), / ˈhɪlˌbɪli hɒt tʌb / (BrE)
a makeshift or rustic outdoor tub, typically improvised for casual soaking, often using unconventional materials or methods
improvised outdoor soaking tub
informal

Examples

After spending hours building a hillbilly hot tub, they finally enjoyed a warm soak under the stars.
The hillbilly hot tub was nothing more than a cattle trough heated by a campfire.
Camping trips are always better when someone rigs up a hillbilly hot tub by the lake.

Synset Examples

The neighbors set up a hillbilly hot tub in their backyard.
After a long day, nothing beats a hillbilly hot tub soak.
