crab-eating dog

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræb ˌitɪŋ dɔg / (AmE), / ˈkræb ˌiːtɪŋ dɒg / (BrE)
a wild dog of northern South America that primarily feeds on crabs and other aquatic animals
wild dog of northern South America

Examples

The crab-eating dog hunts along riverbanks in search of its favorite prey.
Scientists are studying the diet of the crab-eating dog in the Amazon rainforest.
Unlike domestic dogs, the crab-eating dog is well adapted to wetland environments.
