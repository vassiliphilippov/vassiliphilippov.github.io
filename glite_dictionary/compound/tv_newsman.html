TV newsman

1. NOUN

/ tiː viː ˈnuːzˌmæn / (AmE), / tiː viː ˈnjuːzˌmæn / (BrE)
A male journalist who reports news stories on television.
male television news reporter

Examples

The TV newsman delivered the evening headlines with composure and authority.
As a renowned TV newsman, he traveled to conflict zones around the world.
After years as a TV newsman, he became a respected political analyst.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news anchor - person who presents news on television
  • Antonym: newswoman - female journalist who reports news

Etymology

Formed from 'TV' (abbreviation of 'television') + 'newsman'.
