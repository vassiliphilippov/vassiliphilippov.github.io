lingering smell

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪŋɡərɪŋ smɛl / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋɡərɪŋ smɛl / (BrE)
a smell that remains for a long time and is slow to disappear
persistent or lasting odor

Examples

There was a lingering smell of smoke in the room long after the fire was out.
The lingering smell of garlic made the kitchen uninviting.
Despite repeated cleaning, a lingering smell remained in the basement after the flood.

Synset Examples

The pet stain on the carpet left a lingering smell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stench - a strong and very unpleasant smell
  • Antonym: aroma - a pleasant or distinctive smell, often from food or drink
