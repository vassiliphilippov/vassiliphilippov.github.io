pygmy mouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪɡmi maʊs / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡmi maʊs / (BrE)
a very small, dark greyish brown mouse resembling a house mouse, found in Texas and Mexico
tiny mouse species of Texas and Mexico

Examples

The pygmy mouse is known for its tiny size and agile movements.
Scientists discovered a new population of pygmy mice in northern Mexico.
Unlike the house mouse, the pygmy mouse tends to avoid human dwellings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: house mouse - a common small grey rodent found in houses (Mus musculus)
