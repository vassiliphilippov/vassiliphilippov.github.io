peaches-and-cream

1. ADJ

/ ˈpiːtʃɪz ənd ˈkriːm / (AmE), / ˈpiːtʃɪz ənd ˈkriːm / (BrE)
(of complexion or color) having a soft, smooth, and attractive appearance with the tones of peaches and cream; fair and rosy.
soft, fair color or complexion

Examples

She has a peaches-and-cream complexion that many people admire.
The decorator suggested a peaches-and-cream palette for the nursery walls.
Her peaches-and-cream skin glowed in the morning sunlight.
