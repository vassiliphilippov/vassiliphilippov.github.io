Spanish Civil War

1. PROPN

/ ˈspænɪʃ sɪˈvɪl wɔr / (AmE), / ˈspænɪʃ sɪˈvɪl wɔː / (BrE)
a civil war in Spain from 1936 to 1939 in which General Franco overthrew the republican government, becoming a significant conflict between fascist and socialist forces
civil war in Spain (1936–1939)

Examples

Many intellectuals from around the world volunteered to fight in the Spanish Civil War.
The Spanish Civil War was a turning point in twentieth-century European history.
