blinking chickweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɪŋkɪŋ ˈtʃɪkˌwid / (AmE), / ˈblɪŋkɪŋ ˈtʃɪkwiːd / (BrE)
a small plant of northern regions, also known as Indian lettuce, with delicate white flowers and edible leaves
a small northern plant, Indian lettuce

Examples

The trail was lined with patches of blinking chickweed beside the mossy rocks.
Foragers often collect blinking chickweed to add a fresh, slightly tangy note to salads.
We studied the spread of blinking chickweed in the subarctic meadows during our botany field trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Indian lettuce - another common name for blinking chickweed
  • Degree Lower: chickweed - related plants of the genus Stellaria
