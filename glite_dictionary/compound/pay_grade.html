pay grade

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪ ˌɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ ˌɡreɪd / (BrE)
a level or classification that determines the salary or compensation for a position or employee
level of salary or compensation

Examples

After switching departments, her pay grade increased along with her responsibilities.
The company uses a transparent pay grade system for all employees.
He was not eligible for that benefit because it was limited to a higher pay grade.

Synset Examples

She was surprised by the pay grade assigned to her role.
The job description clearly outlines the expected pay grade.
His promotion raised his pay grade significantly.
