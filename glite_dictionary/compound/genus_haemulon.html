genus Haemulon

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs hæˈmjuː.lɒn / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs hɪˈmjuː.lɒn / (BrE)
A genus of fish in the family Haemulidae, which includes various species commonly known as grunts.
a genus of grunt fishes

Examples

The genus Haemulon includes several species of fish widely known as grunts.
Marine biologists often study the genus Haemulon to understand reef ecosystems.
One identifying feature of the genus Haemulon is its distinctive mouth shape.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Haemulidae - the family of fishes known as grunts

Etymology

From Latin 'haemulon', derived from Ancient Greek 'haimuleos' (a kind of fish).
