War of 1812

1. PROPN

/ wɔr əv eɪtˈin ˈtwɛlv / (AmE), / wɔːr əv eɪtˈiːn ˈtwɛlv / (BrE)
A war fought between the United States and Great Britain from 1812 to 1814, mainly over British restrictions on American trade and impressment of American sailors.
war between the US and Britain (1812–1814)

Examples

The War of 1812 was a turning point in early American history.
Many Canadians view the War of 1812 as a defining moment of national identity.
Several battles during the War of 1812 were fought near the Great Lakes.
