solar battery

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊlər ˈbætəri / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə ˈbætəri / (BrE)
An electrical device consisting of a large array of connected solar cells that converts sunlight into electrical energy.
array of connected solar cells

Examples

The new house is powered by a solar battery installed on the roof.
Scientists are developing a more efficient solar battery to store renewable energy.
We decided to invest in a solar battery to reduce our reliance on fossil fuels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solar cell - a device that converts sunlight into electricity
