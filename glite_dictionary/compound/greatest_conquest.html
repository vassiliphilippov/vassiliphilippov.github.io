greatest conquest

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪtəst ˈkɑŋkwɛst / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtɪst ˈkɒŋkwɛst / (BrE)
the most significant or important achievement attained by a person
most significant achievement

Examples

Winning the Nobel Prize was regarded as her greatest conquest in the field of science.
He considered overcoming his childhood fears to be his greatest conquest.
For the artist, the completion of her mural was her greatest conquest.

Synset Examples

His greatest conquest was winning the championship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: achievement - something accomplished successfully
