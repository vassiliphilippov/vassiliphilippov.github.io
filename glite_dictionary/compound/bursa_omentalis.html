bursa omentalis

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɜrsə oʊˌmɛnˈteɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˌbɜːsə əʊˌmɛnˈteɪlɪs / (BrE)
a part of the peritoneal cavity located behind the stomach, separated from the main cavity by the stomach and associated structures
space behind the stomach in the abdomen
medical

Examples

During the surgery, the doctor accessed the bursa omentalis to reach the pancreatic area.
An abscess in the bursa omentalis can complicate diagnosis due to its deep location.
The anatomy class studied the relationship between the bursa omentalis and surrounding abdominal organs.

Etymology

From Latin 'bursa' (pouch) and 'omentum' (a fold in the abdomen), with the suffix -alis meaning 'pertaining to'.
