common plantain

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmən ˈplæntən / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈplæntɪn / (BrE)
A widespread European perennial herb (Plantago major) that grows as a weed in lawns and fields worldwide.
a widespread perennial weed (Plantago major)

Examples

The garden was overrun by common plantain despite our efforts to remove it.
Children often pick the broad leaves of the common plantain while playing on the lawn.
Many lawn care guides list common plantain as a persistent and troublesome weed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Plantago major - botanical name of the common plantain
