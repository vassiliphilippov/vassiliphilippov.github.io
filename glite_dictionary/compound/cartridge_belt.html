cartridge belt

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrtɹɪdʒ bɛlt / (AmE), / ˈkɑːtɹɪdʒ bɛlt / (BrE)
a wide belt with loops or pockets designed for carrying ammunition cartridges
belt for carrying ammunition

Examples

The hunter wore a cartridge belt filled with extra rounds during the expedition.
Soldiers strapped on their cartridge belts before heading out on patrol.
He kept his cartridge belt ready and fully loaded for target practice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bandolier - a shoulder belt with loops or pockets for cartridges
