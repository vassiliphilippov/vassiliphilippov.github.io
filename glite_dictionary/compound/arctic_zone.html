Arctic Zone

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrk.tɪk zoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɑːk.tɪk zəʊn / (BrE)
the region around the North Pole, including all areas north of the Arctic Circle
region north of the Arctic Circle

Examples

Only a few hardy species can survive the extreme cold of the Arctic Zone.
Climate change is causing dramatic changes in the Arctic Zone.
Researchers travel to the Arctic Zone to study polar bears and sea ice.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Antarctic Zone - region around the South Pole, south of the Antarctic Circle
