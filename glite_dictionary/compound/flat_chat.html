flat chat

1. NOUN

/ flæt tʃæt / (AmE), / flæt tʃæt / (BrE)
a conversation that lacks energy, interest, or originality, often becoming dull or uninspiring
dull or uninspiring conversation

Examples

The team leader noticed our flat chat and tried to energize the discussion.
After a long week, our dinner conversation ended up being a rather flat chat.
Her interview was just a flat chat with no memorable moments.

Synset Examples

His flat chat made the presentation dull.
The meeting was flat chat with no new ideas.
We had a flat chat over coffee this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banal conversation - an exchange that is unoriginal or lacking in interest
  • Antonym: lively chat - an animated or energetic conversation
