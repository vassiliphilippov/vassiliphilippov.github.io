unique dialect

1. NOUN

/ juˈnik daɪˌlɛkt / (AmE), / juːˈniːk ˈdaɪ.ə.lɛkt / (BrE)
a distinctive form of a language spoken in a particular region, different from all others
distinctive regional form of a language

Examples

The mountain village is known for preserving its unique dialect despite outside influences.
Researchers have studied the unique dialect spoken only on that island.
She takes pride in her family's unique dialect and teaches it to her children.

Synset Examples

Each region has its unique dialect and phrases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dialect - a particular form of a language specific to a region or group
