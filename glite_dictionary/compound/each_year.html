each year

1. ADV

/ itʃ jɪr / (AmE), / iːtʃ jɪə / (BrE)
every year; happening or measured once every year, especially with reference to an amount paid or received
every year; annually

Examples

The membership fee is due each year on January 1st.
Employees receive a performance review each year to assess their progress.
The museum attracts millions of visitors each year.

Synset Examples

he earned $100,000 per annum
we issue six volumes per annum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annually - happening once every year
