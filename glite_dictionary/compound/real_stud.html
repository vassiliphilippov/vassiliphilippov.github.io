real stud

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːəl stʌd / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl stʌd / (BrE)
an attractive, confident man who is admired for his looks or self-assuredness, especially by others
attractive, confident man
informal slang

Examples

Everyone at the party thought Jake was a real stud in his new jacket.
Ever since he started working out, Mike has become a real stud at school.
With his charming smile and confidence, Josh was considered a real stud by his friends.

Synset Examples

That new guy is a real stud in the gym.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stud - an attractive and confident man
