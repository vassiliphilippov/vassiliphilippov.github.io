up to now

1. ADV

/ ʌp tu naʊ / (AmE), / ʌp tu naʊ / (BrE)
Until the present moment; until this point in time.
until the present moment

Examples

Up to now, the investigation has produced few results.
He had never seen snow up to now.
Up to now, I haven't had any problems with the new software.

Synset Examples

So far he hasn't called
the sun isn't up yet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: so far - until now
  • Synonym: as yet - up until the present (mainly in negative or interrogative)

2. ADV

/ ʌp tu naʊ / (AmE), / ʌp tə naʊ / (BrE)
until the present time; before now.
until the present time

Examples

Up to now, everything has gone according to plan and there have been no problems.
No solution has been discovered up to now, but research is still ongoing.
She had never traveled abroad up to now, so this trip is very exciting for her.
