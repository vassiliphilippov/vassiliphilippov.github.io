pocket book

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkɪt ˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt ˌbʊk / (BrE)
A small, portable paperback book designed to fit in a pocket.
small portable paperback book

Examples

She slipped a pocket book into her purse for the commute.
I prefer to carry a pocket book rather than a bulky hardcover.
The store offers a wide selection of classic novels in pocket book form.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paperback - book bound in flexible paper covers
