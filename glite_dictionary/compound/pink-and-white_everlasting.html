pink-and-white everlasting

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋk ənd waɪt ˈɛvərˌlæstɪŋ / (AmE), / pɪŋk ənd waɪt ˈɛvəlɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
a flower native to southwestern Australia, noted for its bright pink daisy-like papery flowers, often grown for drying
Australian flower with papery pink blooms

Examples

The pink-and-white everlasting is a favorite among gardeners for its striking appearance and long-lasting blooms.
They decorated the hall with dried pink-and-white everlastings for a touch of natural beauty.
During our trip to Australia, we saw fields of pink-and-white everlasting flowers in full bloom.
