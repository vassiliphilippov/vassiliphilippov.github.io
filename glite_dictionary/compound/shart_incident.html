Shart incident

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑrt ˈɪn.sɪ.dənt / (AmE), / ʃɑːt ˈɪn.sɪ.dənt / (BrE)
a humorous, informal reference to an embarrassing accident where a person accidentally passes stool while attempting to pass gas
embarrassing accidental bowel incident
informal slang

Examples

He couldn't stop laughing after recalling the Shart incident at the party.
The infamous Shart incident quickly became a running joke among their friends.
She warned him not to eat too many beans to avoid another Shart incident.

Synset Examples

She jokingly blamed the burrito for the Shart incident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to shart - to accidentally defecate slightly when one intended only to pass gas

Etymology

Blend of 'shit' and 'fart', used humorously; 'incident' denotes the occurrence.
