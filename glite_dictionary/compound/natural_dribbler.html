natural dribbler

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃrəl ˈdrɪbələr / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl ˈdrɪblə / (BrE)
a player who is exceptionally skilled at dribbling and does so with apparent ease and instinct
player skilled at effortless dribbling

Examples

The coach praised him as a natural dribbler after his remarkable solo goal.
Even as a child, she was seen as a natural dribbler on the basketball court.
Opponents find it hard to stop a true natural dribbler in full flow.

Synset Examples

He's a natural dribbler, weaving through defenders with ease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dribbler - a person who dribbles, especially in sports such as soccer or basketball
