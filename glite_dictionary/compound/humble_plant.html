humble plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌm.bəl ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈhʌm.bəl ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
A tropical plant (Mimosa pudica) with sensitive grey-green leaves that fold and droop when touched or cooled, and stems with recurved thorns.
sensitive tropical plant (Mimosa pudica)

Examples

The humble plant immediately folded its leaves when the child touched it.
Gardeners enjoy showing visitors how quickly the humble plant reacts to gentle contact.
In some regions, the humble plant is grown for its intriguing movements and thorny stems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensitive plant - another term for the Mimosa pudica, a plant whose leaves fold when touched
