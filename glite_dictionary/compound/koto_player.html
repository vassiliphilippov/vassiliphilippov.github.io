koto player

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊtoʊ ˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtəʊ ˌpleɪə / (BrE)
A musician who plays the koto, a traditional Japanese stringed instrument.
musician who plays the koto

Examples

The concert featured a renowned koto player from Tokyo.
She became a skilled koto player after years of dedicated practice.
A koto player often performs in traditional Japanese ceremonies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: koto - a traditional Japanese stringed musical instrument

Etymology

Koto from Japanese 琴 (koto), a traditional instrument; player from English.
