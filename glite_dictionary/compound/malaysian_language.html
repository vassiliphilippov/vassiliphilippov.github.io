Malaysian language

1. NOUN

/ məˈleɪʒən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / məˈleɪziən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Malay language as spoken in Malaysia and sometimes in neighboring countries, distinguished from other varieties of Malay
Malay as spoken in Malaysia

Examples

The official documents in Malaysia are written in Malaysian language.
She decided to learn the Malaysian language before moving to Kuala Lumpur for work.
The film was dubbed into Malaysian language for local audiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Malay - the Austronesian language of the Malay people
