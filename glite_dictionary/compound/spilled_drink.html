spilled drink

1. NOUN

/ spɪld drɪŋk / (AmE), / spɪld drɪŋk / (BrE)
a quantity of liquid that has accidentally been poured out from its container, often causing a mess
liquid accidentally poured out

Examples

He slipped on the kitchen floor because of a spilled drink.
The waitress quickly cleaned up the spilled drink before anyone noticed.
A spilled drink ruined the book I was reading.

Synset Examples

His shirt was wet from the spilled drink.
