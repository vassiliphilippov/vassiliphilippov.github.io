archeological site

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑrkiəˈlɑdʒɪkəl saɪt / (AmE), / ˌɑːkiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl saɪt / (BrE)
a place where remains of past human activity are explored and studied by archaeologists
place of archaeological excavation

Examples

The team discovered ancient artifacts at the archeological site near the river.
During our tour, we visited an archeological site dating back to the Roman Empire.
New technology allows experts to better preserve delicate items found at the archeological site.

Synset Examples

they set up camp next to the dig

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excavation - the act of digging out and removing earth, often for archaeological purposes
  • Synonym: dig - an informal term for an archaeological excavation site
