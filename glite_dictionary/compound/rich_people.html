rich people

1. NOUN

/ rɪtʃ ˈpiːpəl / (AmE), / rɪtʃ ˈpiːpl̩ / (BrE)
People who possess great wealth or material possessions, considered as a group.
people with great wealth

Examples

Many rich people donate large sums of money to charity each year.
The city is known for its luxury neighborhoods populated mainly by rich people.
Some believe that rich people should pay higher taxes to support public services.

Synset Examples

only the very rich benefit from this legislation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wealthy - having a great deal of money, resources, or assets; rich
  • Antonym: poor people - people who have little or no money, possessions, or resources
