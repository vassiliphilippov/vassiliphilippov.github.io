illegitimate enterprise

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɪdʒɪˈtɪmət ˈɛntərˌpraɪz / (AmE), / ɪˌlɪdʒɪˈtɪmɪt ˈɛntəprʌɪz / (BrE)
A business operation that is illegal and conducted for profit, such as extortion, fraud, drug dealing, or prostitution.
illegal business operation for profit

Examples

The authorities shut down the illegitimate enterprise that was trafficking counterfeit goods.
He made his fortune managing an illegitimate enterprise involved in money laundering.
Investigators spent months gathering evidence against the illegitimate enterprise operating throughout the city.
