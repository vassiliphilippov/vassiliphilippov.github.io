dental procedure

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛntəl proʊˌsiːdʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl prəˈsiːdʒə / (BrE)
a medical procedure performed by a dentist on a patient's teeth, gums, or mouth
medical work by a dentist
medical

Examples

My dentist explained that the dental procedure would only take an hour.
She felt anxious before her first dental procedure, but everything went smoothly.
Routine checkups often reveal problems that require a dental procedure to fix.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oral surgery - surgical treatment within the mouth
  • Synonym: dental treatment - treatment provided by a dentist
