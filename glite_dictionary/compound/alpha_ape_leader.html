alpha ape leader

1. NOUN

/ ˈælfə eɪp ˈliːdər / (AmE), / ˈælfə eɪp ˈliːdə / (BrE)
the dominant individual in a group of apes, typically possessing the highest social rank and authority
dominant leader in an ape group

Examples

The group followed their alpha ape leader during the migration through the forest.
After a fierce challenge, a new alpha ape leader emerged in the troop.
Researchers observed the alpha ape leader asserting control over food distribution.

Synset Examples

The documentary featured the rise of the alpha ape leader.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dominant male - male animal with highest social rank in a group
  • Antonym: subordinate - individual with lower rank in social hierarchy

Etymology

From 'alpha' (first letter of the Greek alphabet, denoting dominance in animal social hierarchies) + 'ape' + 'leader'.
