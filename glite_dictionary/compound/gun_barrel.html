gun barrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌn ˌbærəl / (AmE), / ˈɡʌn ˌbærəl / (BrE)
the long metal tube of a gun through which the bullet or shot travels when the gun is fired
the tube of a gun from which a bullet is fired

Examples

He stared nervously down the gun barrel as the officer aimed at the target.
The hunter cleaned the gun barrel after returning from the forest.
There's a dent in the gun barrel, which could affect its accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barrel - the tube-shaped part of a gun
