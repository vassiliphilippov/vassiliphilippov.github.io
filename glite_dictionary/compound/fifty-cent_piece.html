fifty-cent piece

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪfti sɛnt pis / (AmE), / ˈfɪfti sɛnt piːs / (BrE)
a United States coin with a value of fifty cents, equal to half of a dollar
U.S. coin worth fifty cents
american

Examples

He showed me a rare fifty-cent piece from the 1960s.
The cashier gave me a fifty-cent piece as change.
Collectors often look for fifty-cent pieces in mint condition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: half dollar - a United States coin worth fifty cents
