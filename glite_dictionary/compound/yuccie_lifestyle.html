yuccie lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ ˈjʌki ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈjʌki ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
A way of living associated with young urban creatives, characterized by a focus on creativity, entrepreneurship, and a trendy, urban mindset.
lifestyle of young urban creatives

Examples

After moving to the city, she quickly adopted the yuccie lifestyle and started her own art gallery.
The rise of tech startups has fueled the popularity of the yuccie lifestyle among millennials.
He documents his yuccie lifestyle on social media, sharing photos of trendy cafes and art exhibits.

Synset Examples

She embraced her yuccie lifestyle with pride.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hipster lifestyle - a lifestyle associated with hipsters, emphasizing non-mainstream culture

Etymology

From 'yuccie', a blend of 'young urban', on the model of 'yuppie' (young urban professional) and 'creative'.
