social organization

1. NOUN

/ ˌsoʊʃəl ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsəʊʃl ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃn / (BrE)
the structure and system of relationships and roles that organize people within a society or group
structure of social relationships

Examples

The social organization of this tribe emphasizes shared responsibilities among all members.
Changes in social organization can affect family life and education systems.
Researchers compared the social organization of traditional and modern societies.

Synset Examples

the social organizations of England and America are very different
sociologists have studied the changing structure of the family

Related Terms

  • Synonym: society - the aggregate of people living together in a more or less ordered community
