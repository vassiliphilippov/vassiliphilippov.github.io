stuffed cabbage

1. NOUN

/ stʌft ˈkæbɪdʒ / (AmE), / stʌft ˈkæbɪdʒ / (BrE)
A dish made by scooping out a parboiled head of cabbage, stuffing it with chopped meat or other fillings, and baking it, typically served with tomato or cheese sauce.
dish of baked, stuffed cabbage with sauce

Examples

She prepared her grandmother’s famous stuffed cabbage for the family dinner.
The restaurant is known for its delicious stuffed cabbage served with a tangy tomato sauce.
Many Eastern European countries have their own version of stuffed cabbage as a traditional dish.
