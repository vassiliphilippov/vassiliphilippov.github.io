life of a jet-setter

1. NOUN

/ laɪf əv ə ˈdʒɛtˌsɛtər / (AmE), / laɪf əv ə ˈdʒɛtˌsɛtə / (BrE)
a lifestyle characterized by frequent travel for leisure, often to fashionable or exotic places
lifestyle of frequent, fashionable travel

Examples

After winning the lottery, she embraced the life of a jet-setter and visited dozens of countries.
Photographers often capture the glamorous life of a jet-setter in fashion magazines.
He left his corporate job to pursue the life of a jet-setter, hopping between Paris and New York.

Synset Examples

He's living the life of a jet-setter with his frequent flyer miles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jet-setter - someone who travels frequently for pleasure, often internationally

Etymology

From 'jet-setter' (a person who frequently travels by jet for pleasure); 'jet set' originated in the 1950s.
