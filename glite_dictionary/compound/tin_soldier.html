tin soldier

1. NOUN

/ tɪn ˈsoʊldʒər / (AmE), / tɪn ˈsəʊldʒə / (BrE)
a small toy soldier made of tin, often used as a child's plaything or collectible
small toy soldier made of tin

Examples

As a child, he loved lining up his tin soldiers on the windowsill.
The antique shop had a rare set of 19th-century tin soldiers in a glass case.
She inherited her grandfather's collection of tin soldiers, each with intricate hand-painted uniforms.

Synset Examples

The display featured a row of tin soldiers, each painted uniquely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toy soldier - a small toy figure of a soldier, usually made of plastic or metal
