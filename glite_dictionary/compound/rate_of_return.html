rate of return

1. NOUN

/ ˌreɪt əv rɪˈtɜrn / (AmE), / ˌreɪt əv rɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
the profit or loss on an investment over a period, expressed as a percentage of the original cost
investment profit as a percentage

Examples

The company's annual rate of return impressed its investors.
If you want to beat inflation, your rate of return must be higher than the inflation rate.
She calculated the rate of return before deciding to invest in the new fund.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: return on investment - profit received from an investment in relation to the amount invested
