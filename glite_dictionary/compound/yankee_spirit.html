Yankee spirit

1. NOUN

/ ˈjæŋki ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˈjaŋki ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
A resilient and determined attitude, often associated with ingenuity and perseverance, especially regarded as a characteristic of Americans from the northern United States.
resilient and determined attitude; American cultural trait

Examples

Her Yankee spirit helped her overcome every obstacle during the difficult project.
The team showed their Yankee spirit by never giving up, even when they were losing.
Many admire his Yankee spirit and can-do attitude in the face of adversity.

Synset Examples

She has a Yankee spirit, always ready for a challenge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grit - courage and resolve; strength of character
  • Synonym: determination - the quality of being resolute; firmness of purpose
  • Synonym: resilience - the capacity to recover quickly from difficulties; toughness

Etymology

Yankee, originally a nickname for Americans (especially Northerners), combined with 'spirit' (attitude, resolve).
