consequences of colonialism

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsɛkwənsɪz əv koʊˈloʊniˌɪzəm / (AmE), / kənˈsɛkwənsɪz əv kəˈləʊniəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the effects or outcomes, typically negative, that result from the political, economic, and social control of a territory by a foreign power
effects resulting from colonial rule

Examples

Many societies are still grappling with the consequences of colonialism decades after achieving independence.
The consequences of colonialism can be seen in the economic disparity between former colonies and colonizing powers.
Scholars debate whether the consequences of colonialism were entirely negative or if there were some positive developments as well.

Synset Examples

The museum exhibit focused on the consequences of colonialism.
History classes often discuss the impacts of colonialism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colonialism - policy of acquiring and maintaining colonies
