sacred mushroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪkrɪd ˈmʌʃruːm / (AmE), / ˈseɪkrɪd ˈmʌʃrəm / (BrE)
a type of hallucinogenic mushroom containing psilocybin, traditionally used in religious or spiritual rituals
a hallucinogenic mushroom used in rituals

Examples

Many indigenous cultures have used the sacred mushroom in spiritual ceremonies for centuries.
Curious about its effects, the researchers studied the sacred mushroom and its active compound, psilocybin.
He traveled to Mexico to experience a traditional sacred mushroom ritual firsthand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magic mushroom - a mushroom containing psilocybin or similar hallucinogens
