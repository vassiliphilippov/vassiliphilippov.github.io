left fielder

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛft ˌfiːldər / (AmE), / ˈlɛft ˌfiːldə / (BrE)
the player who occupies the left field position in baseball or softball
player covering left field (baseball/softball)

Examples

The left fielder made a spectacular catch to end the inning.
As a left fielder, she needs both speed and a strong throwing arm.
The coach moved him from second base to left fielder for today's game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: center fielder - player covering center field in baseball/softball
  • Synonym: right fielder - player covering right field in baseball/softball
