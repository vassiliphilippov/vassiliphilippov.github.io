absurdist humor

1. NOUN

/ əbˈsɝːdɪst ˈhjuːmɚ / (AmE), / əbˈsɜːdɪst ˈhjuːmə / (BrE)
Humor that is based on deliberately violating logical reasoning, resulting in situations or behaviors that are blatantly illogical or nonsensical.
humor characterized by illogical or nonsensical situations

Examples

Many modern internet memes rely on absurdist humor to surprise and amuse their audience.
The play is filled with absurdist humor that challenges traditional narrative logic.
Fans of absurdist humor often enjoy sketches where bizarre things happen without explanation.

Synset Examples

Surreal humour is also found frequently in avant-garde theatre such as Waiting for Godot and Rosencrantz & Guildenstern Are Dead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surreal humor - humor based on bizarre or dreamlike situations

Etymology

From 'absurdist', referring to the philosophy or style of the absurd, and 'humor'.
