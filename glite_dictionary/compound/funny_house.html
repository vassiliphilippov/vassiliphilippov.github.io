funny house

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌni haʊs / (AmE), / ˈfʌni haʊs / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) A pejorative term for a mental institution or insane asylum.
offensive term for mental institution
slang offensive

Examples

He joked about being sent to the funny house, but his friends found it insensitive.
Decades ago, society would often lock people away in the funny house instead of offering real help.
It's a cruel thing to call a psychiatric hospital a funny house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insane asylum - a hospital for people with mental illnesses
  • Synonym: psychiatric hospital - hospital specializing in mental illness treatment
