perfectly cromulent

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝː.fɪkt.li ˈkrɑː.mjə.lənt / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.fɪkt.li ˈkrɒ.mjʊ.lənt / (BrE)
Completely adequate or acceptable, especially in a context where adequacy is all that is required.
entirely acceptable; adequate
informal american

Examples

Your answer to the quiz was perfectly cromulent given the information available.
It's a perfectly cromulent solution, even if it's not the most creative one.
The manager assured us that the report was perfectly cromulent for submission.

Synset Examples

That explanation is perfectly cromulent for our needs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acceptable - Satisfactory or adequate
  • Synonym: adequate - Satisfactory or acceptable in quality or quantity

Etymology

Coined in 1996 on the American TV show 'The Simpsons' as a fictitious word meaning acceptable or legitimate.
