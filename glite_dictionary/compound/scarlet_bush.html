scarlet bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑrlət bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈskɑːlɪt bʊʃ / (BrE)
a shrub with showy orange to scarlet or crimson flowers, native to Florida, the West Indies, Mexico, and Brazil
a shrub with striking scarlet flowers

Examples

The scarlet bush added a splash of color to the garden with its vivid flowers.
Gardeners often choose the scarlet bush for its ability to attract hummingbirds.
We planted a scarlet bush near the patio to enjoy its blooms throughout the summer.
