what's the story

1. X

/ wʌts ðə ˈstɔri / (AmE), / wɒts ðə ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
(informal) Used to ask for news, information, or details about a situation or someone's current state.
informal inquiry about news or details
informal

Examples

When she walked in looking worried, I asked, what's the story?
Hey, what's the story with the new project at work?
He called me late at night just to say, 'what's the story?'

Synset Examples

I leaned in and asked, what's the story, bud?
What's the story, bud? You seem down.
She smiled and said, what's the story of your weekend?
He grinned and asked, what's the story, bud?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: what's up - informal greeting or inquiry about someone's situation
