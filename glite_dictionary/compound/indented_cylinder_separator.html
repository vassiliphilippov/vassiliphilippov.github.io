indented cylinder separator

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdɛntɪd ˈsɪlɪndər səˈpɛrətər / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛntɪd ˈsɪlɪndə səˈpɛrətə / (BrE)
a machine used to separate grains or seeds based on kernel length using a revolving indented cylinder
machine for separating grains by kernel length

Examples

The indented cylinder separator efficiently removes broken grains from the bulk harvest.
Modern mills often use an indented cylinder separator to ensure the uniformity of kernel size.
After cleaning, the wheat passed through an indented cylinder separator for further grading.
