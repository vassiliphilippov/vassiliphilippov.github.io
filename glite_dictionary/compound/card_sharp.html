card sharp

1. NOUN

/ kɑrd ʃɑrp / (AmE), / kɑːd ʃɑːp / (BrE)
a person, usually a professional, who cheats at card games to win money
person who cheats at card games

Examples

It turned out that the wealthy gentleman was a notorious card sharp in casinos across Europe.
A card sharp can manipulate the deck so subtly that few ever suspect cheating.
Detectives arrested a suspected card sharp during the high-stakes poker tournament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: card shark - person who cheats or is very skilled at card games
