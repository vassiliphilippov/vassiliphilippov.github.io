phone monkey

1. NOUN

/ foʊn ˈmʌŋki / (AmE), / fəʊn ˈmʌŋki / (BrE)
(slang) A person whose job is to answer phone calls, especially in a customer service or call center setting.
person who answers calls, esp. in customer service
slang informal

Examples

She got her first job as a phone monkey in a bustling call center downtown.
Tired of his phone monkey duties, Sam decided to look for a new career.
Despite being called a phone monkey, she handled complex customer issues with professionalism.

Synset Examples

The phone monkey often answers calls without a break.
As a phone monkey, he handles customer complaints daily.
She joked about being a phone monkey at her job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: receptionist - a person employed to answer the telephone and deal with clients in an office
