publicity woman

1. NOUN

/ pəˈblɪsəti ˌwʊmən / (AmE), / pʌˈblɪsəti ˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to arrange publicity for an organization or public figure
woman who arranges publicity

Examples

As a publicity woman, she coordinated media interviews and press releases for the company.
The film star's publicity woman ensured the event received national attention.
Many firms now employ a publicity woman to manage their public image.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: publicist - a person whose job is to arrange publicity
