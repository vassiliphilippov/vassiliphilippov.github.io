hoop snake

1. NOUN

/ huːp sneɪk / (AmE), / huːp sneɪk / (BrE)
a mythical North American snake said to grasp its tail in its mouth and roll along like a hoop
mythical snake believed to roll like a hoop

Examples

The legend of the hoop snake has been passed down for generations in rural America.
Some early settlers believed they saw a hoop snake chasing its own tail down a hill.
Despite tales of the hoop snake, scientists have found no evidence that such a creature exists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mythical creature - an imaginary or legendary animal not proven to exist
