proof of identity

1. NOUN

/ pruf əv aɪˈdɛntəti / (AmE), / pruːf əv aɪˈdɛntɪti / (BrE)
documentation or evidence that confirms a person's identity, typically required for official or legal purposes.
evidence confirming a person's identity

Examples

The bank requires proof of identity before opening a new account.
You will need to show proof of identity when picking up your package.
He forgot to bring proof of identity to the exam center and was not allowed in.

Synset Examples

He brought proof of his identity to the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: identification - official document or card used to prove who someone is
