window-washing

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪndoʊ ˌwɑːʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ ˌwɒʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of washing windows to clean them
the act of cleaning windows

Examples

The company specializes in window-washing for high-rise buildings.
Regular window-washing keeps the office looking bright and welcoming.
He started a small window-washing business in his neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: window cleaning - the act or business of cleaning windows
