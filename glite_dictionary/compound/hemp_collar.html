hemp collar

1. NOUN

/ hɛmp ˈkɑlər / (AmE), / hɛmp ˈkɒlə / (BrE)
A collar made from hemp fiber, often used for pets or as an eco-friendly accessory.
collar made from hemp fiber

Examples

Our dog wears a hemp collar because it's gentle on her skin.
She bought a stylish hemp collar from a local artisan for her cat.
Many eco-conscious pet owners prefer a hemp collar over synthetic materials.

Synset Examples

He wore a hempen collar to the eco-friendly event.
