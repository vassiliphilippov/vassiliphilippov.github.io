professional basketball

1. NOUN

/ prəˈfɛʃənəl ˈbæskətˌbɔl / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃ(ə)nəɫ ˈbɑːskɪtˌbɔːl / (BrE)
Basketball played as a paid occupation rather than as an amateur sport.
basketball as a paid occupation

Examples

After college, she pursued a career in professional basketball and joined the WNBA.
Tickets for professional basketball games are often difficult to get because of their popularity.
He admired the discipline and dedication required in professional basketball compared to the amateur leagues.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: amateur basketball - basketball played without pay
