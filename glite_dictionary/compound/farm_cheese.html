farm cheese

1. NOUN

/ fɑrm tʃiːz / (AmE), / fɑːm tʃiːz / (BrE)
A mild white cheese made from the curds of soured skim milk, often produced on small farms.
mild white cheese from skim milk curds

Examples

She served homemade farm cheese with fresh bread for breakfast.
The recipe calls for a cup of farm cheese to add a creamy texture.
At the market, they sell a variety of local farm cheese made from pasteurized milk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cottage cheese - fresh, soft white cheese with a mild flavor and lumpy texture
