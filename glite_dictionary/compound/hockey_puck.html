hockey puck

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑki ˌpʌk / (AmE), / ˈhɒki ˌpʌk / (BrE)
A flat, round disk made of vulcanized rubber, used instead of a ball in ice hockey.
disk used in ice hockey

Examples

The referee dropped the hockey puck at the start of the game.
He bought a souvenir hockey puck after watching his first match.
A hockey puck can travel at incredible speeds when shot by professional players.
