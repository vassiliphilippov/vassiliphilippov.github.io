ladies' tresses

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪdiz ˌtrɛsɪz / (AmE), / ˈleɪdiz ˌtrɛsɪz / (BrE)
an orchid of the genus Spiranthes, with slender, often twisted spikes of small white flowers
a type of orchid with twisting white flower spikes

Examples

While hiking in the meadow, we spotted a cluster of ladies' tresses blooming among the tall grasses.
The delicate spiral of the ladies' tresses makes it a favorite among wildflower enthusiasts.
He photographed several species of ladies' tresses during his botanical survey.

Etymology

Named for the resemblance of the flower spikes to braided women's hair (tresses).
