curly pondweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɜrli ˈpɑndˌwid / (AmE), / ˈkɜːli ˈpɒndwiːd / (BrE)
a European aquatic plant with wavy-edged leaves, naturalized in the eastern United States and California
aquatic plant with wavy leaves

Examples

We found curly pondweed growing abundantly along the shallow edges of the lake.
The aquarium was cleaned, and the curly pondweed thrived in the fresh water.
Scientists studied the spread of curly pondweed in various American rivers.
