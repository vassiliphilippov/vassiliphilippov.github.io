equipment casualty

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈkwɪpmənt ˈkæʒuəlti / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪpmənt ˈkaʒjʊəlti / (BrE)
the loss or destruction of military equipment, especially as a result of damage or enemy action
loss of military equipment (military term)

Examples

The battalion reported an equipment casualty after one of their tanks was destroyed by enemy fire.
Maintaining spare parts can reduce the risk of serious equipment casualty during extended missions.
After the helicopter crash, the team had to deal with both a personnel and an equipment casualty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: casualty - a person killed or injured; or equipment lost in war or accident
