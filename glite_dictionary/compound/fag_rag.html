fag rag

1. NOUN

/ fæɡ ræɡ / (AmE), / fæɡ ræɡ / (BrE)
A derogatory slang term for a magazine or publication focused on LGBTQ+ culture or topics.
slang for LGBTQ+ magazine (offensive)
offensive slang

Examples

She was insulted when someone called her favorite magazine a fag rag.
The journalist refused to write for what he described as a fag rag.
Some activists reclaimed the term fag rag as a badge of pride.
