potato wart fungus

1. NOUN

/ pəˈteɪtoʊ wɔrt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ wɔːt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a fungus that causes potato wart disease, a serious disease affecting potato tubers
fungus causing wart disease in potatoes

Examples

The potato wart fungus can devastate potato crops if not controlled.
Strict quarantine is necessary to prevent the spread of potato wart fungus.
Researchers are developing resistant potato varieties to combat potato wart fungus.
