toy chest

1. NOUN

/ tɔɪ tʃɛst / (AmE), / tɔɪ tʃɛst / (BrE)
a large box or chest used for storing children's toys
large box for storing toys

Examples

The brightly painted toy chest in the corner was overflowing with stuffed animals and games.
After cleaning up, Emma neatly placed all her blocks back into the toy chest.
We bought a wooden toy chest for the playroom to keep everything organized.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toy box - a box for storing children's toys
