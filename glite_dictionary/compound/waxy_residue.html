waxy residue

1. NOUN

/ ˈwæksi rɪˈzɪdu / (AmE), / ˈwæksi rɪˈzɪdjuː / (BrE)
a sticky or greasy film or coating, similar in texture to wax, that remains on a surface
sticky film or coating like wax

Examples

The wood table was covered in a waxy residue after the candles melted.
After polishing her car, she found a waxy residue left by the product.
The fruit's skin was slippery with a waxy residue from the natural coating.

Synset Examples

She noticed a waxy residue on the leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: film - a thin layer covering a surface
  • Synonym: deposit - a layer of substance left behind on a surface
