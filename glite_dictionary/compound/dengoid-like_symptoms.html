Dengoid-like symptoms

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛŋˌɡɔɪd laɪk ˈsɪmptəmz / (AmE), / ˈdeŋɡɔɪd laɪk ˈsɪmptəmz / (BrE)
Symptoms that resemble those of Dengue fever, such as high fever, rash, and muscle pain.
symptoms resembling Dengue fever
medical

Examples

The patient was admitted with Dengoid-like symptoms after returning from a tropical region.
Physicians should be vigilant when diagnosing Dengoid-like symptoms during an outbreak of mosquito-borne illnesses.
Her sudden fever and rash were recognized as Dengoid-like symptoms by the attending doctor.

Synset Examples

Her Dengoid-like symptoms required immediate medical attention.

Etymology

From 'Dengue' (a mosquito-borne viral disease) plus '-oid' meaning 'resembling' and 'symptoms'.
