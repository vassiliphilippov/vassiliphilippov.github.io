Balkan Mountains

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæl.kən ˈmaʊn.tənz / (AmE), / ˈbæl.kən ˈmaʊn.tɪnz / (BrE)
a major mountain range in Bulgaria and the Balkan Peninsula, extending from eastern Serbia to the Black Sea
mountain range in Bulgaria and the Balkans

Examples

The Balkan Mountains divide northern and southern Bulgaria.
Many rivers originate in the Balkan Mountains before flowing to the Danube.
The Balkan Mountains are known for their scenic beauty and biodiversity.

Etymology

Named after the Turkish word 'Balkan', meaning 'mountain'.
