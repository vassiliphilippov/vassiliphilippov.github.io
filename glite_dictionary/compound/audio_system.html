audio system

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːdioʊ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈɔːdiəʊ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A set of electronic devices designed for recording, reproducing, or amplifying sound.
electronic sound equipment set

Examples

She installed a new audio system in her living room to enjoy high-quality music.
The audio system in this car delivers a rich and immersive sound experience.
They spent the afternoon setting up the audio system for the school concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sound system - a set of equipment for amplifying and reproducing sound, especially in public spaces or venues
