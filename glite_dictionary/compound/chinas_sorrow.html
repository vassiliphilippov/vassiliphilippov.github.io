China's Sorrow

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪnəz ˈsɑːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪnəz ˈsɒrəʊ / (BrE)
A nickname for the Yellow River in China, referring to its frequent and devastating floods.
nickname for the Yellow River

Examples

For centuries, the Yellow River has been tragically known as China's Sorrow due to its catastrophic floods.
Many communities were displaced during one of China's Sorrow's worst flood events in the 20th century.
Historians still study the social impact of China's Sorrow on ancient Chinese civilizations.
