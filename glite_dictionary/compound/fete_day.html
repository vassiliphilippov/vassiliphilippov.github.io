fete day

1. NOUN

/ feɪt deɪ / (AmE), / feɪt deɪ / (BrE)
A day set aside for celebration or feasting, typically marking a special occasion or festival.
day for celebration or feast

Examples

The village holds a fete day every summer, complete with games, music, and a communal feast.
In many cultures, a fete day is marked by traditional dances and special foods.
The children looked forward to fete day all year, eager for the festivities and treats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: festival - an organized series of events, typically for cultural or religious celebration
  • Synonym: holiday - a day of festivity or recreation when no work is done

Etymology

From French 'fête' (festival, feast) and English 'day'.
