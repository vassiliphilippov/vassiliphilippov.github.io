harsh reality

1. NOUN

/ hɑrʃ riˈæləti / (AmE), / hɑːʃ rɪˈælɪti / (BrE)
an unpleasant or difficult fact or situation that must be accepted
unpleasant fact or situation

Examples

After losing his job, he was forced to face the harsh reality of unemployment.
The movie portrays the harsh reality of life in the slums.
It took time for her to accept the harsh reality that dreams don't always come true.

Synset Examples

The princeling was often shielded from the harsh realities of life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hard truth - an unpleasant or difficult fact
