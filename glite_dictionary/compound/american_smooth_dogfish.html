American smooth dogfish

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən smuːð ˈdɔːɡˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən smuːð ˈdɒɡˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a species of small shark found along the Atlantic coast of the Americas, known for its smooth skin and slender body
a small shark species of the Atlantic coast

Examples

Fishermen occasionally catch the American smooth dogfish off the coast of New Jersey.
The American smooth dogfish is recognized by its smooth skin and lack of prominent dorsal spines.
Conservationists are studying population changes in the American smooth dogfish along the eastern United States.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dogfish - small shark of various species, especially from the family Squalidae
