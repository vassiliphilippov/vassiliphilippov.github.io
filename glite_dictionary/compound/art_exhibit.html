art exhibit

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt ɪɡˈzɪbɪt / (AmE), / ɑːt ɪɡˈzɪbɪt / (BrE)
A public display or presentation of artworks, often in a gallery or museum.
public display of artworks

Examples

The local museum opened a new art exhibit featuring contemporary painters.
Many visitors attended the annual art exhibit to admire works by emerging artists.
She submitted her sculpture to the school's art exhibit and won first prize.

Synset Examples

The transphilic art exhibit showcased powerful stories of transition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: art exhibition - a public showing of art works; often in a gallery
