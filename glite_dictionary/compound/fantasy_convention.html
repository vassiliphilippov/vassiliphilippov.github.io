fantasy convention

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæn.tə.si kənˈvɛn.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈfan.tə.si kənˈvɛn.ʃən / (BrE)
an organized event where fans of fantasy genres gather to participate in activities, meet creators, and celebrate fantasy works
event for fantasy genre fans

Examples

This year's fantasy convention is expected to attract thousands of fans from around the world.
She wore an elaborate dragon costume to the fantasy convention in Chicago.
Panels at the fantasy convention included discussions with popular authors and illustrators.

Synset Examples

He dressed as an orc for the fantasy convention.
Dressed as a hobbit, he joined the fantasy convention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: science fiction convention - an event for fans of science fiction genres
