city council

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪti ˈkaʊn.səl / (AmE), / ˈsɪti ˈkaʊn.səl / (BrE)
the group of elected officials who govern a city by passing laws and making financial decisions
elected governing body of a city

Examples

The city council voted unanimously to approve the new park project.
After a heated debate, the city council decided to increase funding for local schools.
Residents can voice their concerns at the weekly city council meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: municipal council - elected local government body, especially in cities or towns
  • Synonym: city government - the administrative body that governs a city
