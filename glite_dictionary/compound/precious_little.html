precious little

1. ADJ

/ ˈprɛʃəs ˈlɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃəs ˈlɪtəl / (BrE)
very little; hardly any or almost nothing
very little or almost nothing

Examples

They made precious little progress despite working all week.
She has precious little patience left after the delays.
After all his efforts, he gained precious little from the experience.

Synset Examples

They achieved precious little progress in the meeting.
He had precious little time to finish his project.
She learned precious little from that experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: very little - a very small quantity or amount
