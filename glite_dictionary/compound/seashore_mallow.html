seashore mallow

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiˌʃɔr ˈmæloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsiːʃɔː ˈmæləʊ / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Kosteletzya, commonly found in coastal habitats, cultivated for its hibiscus-like flowers.
coastal plant with hibiscus-like flowers

Examples

The bright pink flowers of the seashore mallow attract many pollinators during the summer.
Gardeners often plant seashore mallow near ponds because it thrives in moist soils.
We spotted a wild seashore mallow blooming along the salt marsh trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kosteletzya - genus of coastal flowering plants
