Battle of Gettysburg

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬l̩ əv ˈɡɛtizbɝɡ / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˈɡɛtɪzbɜːɡ / (BrE)
the decisive battle fought in 1863 during the American Civil War, resulting in a major Union victory over Confederate forces led by General Robert E. Lee
decisive 1863 American Civil War battle

Examples

The Battle of Gettysburg is often regarded as the turning point of the American Civil War.
Many tourists visit the site of the Battle of Gettysburg to learn more about American history.
President Lincoln delivered the Gettysburg Address shortly after the Battle of Gettysburg.
