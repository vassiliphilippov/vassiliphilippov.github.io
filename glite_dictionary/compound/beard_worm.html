beard worm

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪrd wɜrm / (AmE), / ˈbɪəd wɜːm / (BrE)
a slender, tube-dwelling marine animal with tentacles, living on the deep ocean floor
deep-sea tube-dwelling marine animal

Examples

Scientists discovered a new species of beard worm near deep-sea hydrothermal vents.
The beard worm absorbs nutrients directly from seawater around its tube.
Beard worms are fascinating creatures often studied in marine biology.
