downy haw

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaʊni hɔ / (AmE), / ˈdaʊni hɔː / (BrE)
An American species of hawthorn (Crataegus mollis) with red fruit, and stems and leaves densely covered with short, woolly hairs.
a red-fruited, hairy-stemmed American hawthorn

Examples

The forest was dotted with downy haws in full fruit, their red berries gleaming among the pale leaves.
Gardeners often plant downy haw for its attractive flowers and downy stems.
I came across a dense thicket of downy haws while hiking in Illinois.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hawthorn - a type of shrub or small tree with thorny branches and red fruits
