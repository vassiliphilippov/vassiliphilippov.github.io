small-grained

1. ADJ

/ smɔl ˈɡreɪnd / (AmE), / smɔːl ˈɡreɪnd / (BrE)
Consisting of very fine or small particles or grains.
made of fine or small particles

Examples

The scientist analyzed the small-grained sediment under a microscope.
Her pottery is made using a small-grained clay that gives a smooth texture.
This bread has a small-grained structure, making it soft and delicate.

Synset Examples

powdered cellulose
powdery snow
pulverized sugar is prepared from granulated sugar by grinding

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fine-grained - consisting of very small grains or particles
  • Antonym: coarse-grained - consisting of relatively large grains or particles
