one hell of a

1. X

/ wʌn hɛl əv ə / (AmE), / wʌn hɛl əv ə / (BrE)
an extremely impressive or intense example of something
extremely impressive or intense
informal

Examples

That was one hell of a concert; I've never seen anything like it.
She made one hell of a speech that moved the entire audience.
It's one hell of a climb to the top of the mountain, but the view is worth it.

Synset Examples

That was one hell of a party last night.
It was one hell of a game, full of surprises.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one heck of a - an informal and less strong variant meaning 'an impressive or extreme example of something'
