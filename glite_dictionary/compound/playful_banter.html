playful banter

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪfəl ˈbæn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪfəl ˈbæn.tə / (BrE)
humorous and teasing conversation conducted in a light-hearted spirit
light-hearted humorous conversation

Examples

Their playful banter kept everyone at the dinner table entertained all evening.
The two colleagues exchanged playful banter before the meeting began.
She loved the playful banter that always made their time together so much fun.
