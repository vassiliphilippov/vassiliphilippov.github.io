true potential

1. NOUN

/ tru pəˈtɛnʃəl / (AmE), / truː pəˈtɛnʃəl / (BrE)
the full capability or ability that someone or something can achieve or realize
full capability or ability

Examples

Only after years of training did she realize her true potential.
The new technology helps athletes unlock their true potential.
He feels that the company has not reached its true potential yet.

Synset Examples

In the story, the fleshling discovers its true potential.
