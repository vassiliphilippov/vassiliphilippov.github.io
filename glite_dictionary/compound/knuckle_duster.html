knuckle duster

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌkəl ˌdʌstər / (AmE), / ˈnʌkəl ˌdʌstə / (BrE)
A small metal weapon designed to fit over the knuckles to increase the force of a punch, often used as a weapon.
metal weapon worn over knuckles

Examples

The police found a knuckle duster hidden in his jacket pocket.
Carrying a knuckle duster is illegal in many countries due to its potential for harm.
He slipped the knuckle duster onto his hand before entering the ring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brass knuckles - a similar metal weapon used over the knuckles
