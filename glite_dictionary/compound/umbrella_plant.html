umbrella plant

1. NOUN

/ əmˈbrɛlə plænt / (AmE), / ʌmˈbrɛlə plɑːnt / (BrE)
a perennial herbaceous plant with large, umbrella-like leaves and clusters of white to pink flowers, commonly found along stream banks in California's Sierra Nevada.
a plant with umbrella-shaped leaves

Examples

The umbrella plant thrives best in moist, shaded areas near streams.
Gardeners admire the umbrella plant for its striking, broad leaves and colorful blooms.
We spotted an umbrella plant growing along the riverbank during our hike.
