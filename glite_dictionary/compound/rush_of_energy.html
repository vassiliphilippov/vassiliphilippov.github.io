rush of energy

1. NOUN

/ rʌʃ əv ˈɛnɚdʒi / (AmE), / rʌʃ əv ˈɛnədʒi / (BrE)
a sudden, strong feeling of energy or excitement that comes unexpectedly
sudden burst of energy or excitement

Examples

After drinking a cup of coffee, she experienced a sudden rush of energy that helped her finish her work.
He felt a rush of energy as he crossed the finish line.
The exciting news gave everyone in the room a rush of energy and optimism.

Synset Examples

She felt a rush of energy and just wanted to bounce around.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burst of energy - a sudden, intense wave of energy
  • Synonym: surge - a sudden and powerful forward or upward movement
