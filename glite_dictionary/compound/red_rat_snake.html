red rat snake

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ræt sneɪk / (AmE), / rɛd ræt sneɪk / (BrE)
a large, harmless, reddish-colored snake native to the southeastern United States, often found on farms and in wooded areas
large harmless reddish snake of the southeastern US

Examples

The red rat snake is often seen slithering through cornfields in the South.
Farmers appreciate the red rat snake because it helps control rodent populations.
During our nature walk, we spotted a beautiful red rat snake basking in the sun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corn snake - a species of rat snake commonly called 'red rat snake'
