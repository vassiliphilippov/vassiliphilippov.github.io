overly fussy

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊvɚli ˈfʌsi / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəli ˈfʌsi / (BrE)
Too particular or difficult to please; excessively concerned with small details or perfection.
excessively particular or hard to please

Examples

The chef refused to accommodate customers who were overly fussy about their meals.
If you are overly fussy when packing, it can take hours to finish your suitcase.
Her overly fussy attitude made it hard for her to enjoy group activities.

Synset Examples

His attitude was often seen as overly fussy.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fussy - particular or hard to please
