signet ring

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪɡ.nət rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.nɪt rɪŋ / (BrE)
a ring set with a seal, often engraved with a family crest or initials, used to mark or authenticate documents
ring bearing an engraved seal

Examples

He inherited his grandfather's signet ring, which had the family crest engraved on it.
In ancient times, a signet ring was used to authenticate important documents by stamping wax seals.
She wore a gold signet ring on her pinkie finger at every formal event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seal ring - a ring bearing a seal, especially for making impressions in wax

Etymology

From Old French 'signet' (small seal), diminutive of 'signe' (sign).
