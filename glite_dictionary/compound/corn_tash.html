corn tash

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn tæʃ / (AmE), / kɔːn tæʃ / (BrE)
A type of cornbread dish wrapped in cabbage leaves and baked in hot ashes, traditionally prepared in the southern United States.
cornbread wrapped in cabbage leaves and baked in ashes
american

Examples

My grandmother used to make corn tash over the open fire during family gatherings.
The recipe for corn tash has been passed down through generations in our region.
Locals enjoyed corn tash at the annual heritage festival last weekend.
