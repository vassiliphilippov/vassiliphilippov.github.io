butacaine sulfate

1. NOUN

/ ˌbjuː.təˌkeɪn ˈsʌlˌfeɪt / (AmE), / ˌbjuː.təˌkeɪn ˈsʌlˌfeɪt / (BrE)
a white crystalline compound used as a local anesthetic, especially on mucous membranes
local anesthetic chemical compound
medical

Examples

The dentist applied butacaine sulfate to numb the patient's gums before the procedure.
