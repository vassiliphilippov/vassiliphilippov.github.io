blue bugle

1. NOUN

/ blu ˈbʌɡəl / (AmE), / bluː ˈbjuːɡl̩ / (BrE)
a perennial plant of southern Europe with upright stems and bright blue flowers, scientifically known as Ajuga genevensis.
a European plant with blue flowers

Examples

The garden's border was lined with striking blue bugle in full bloom.
She planted blue bugle to attract bees and butterflies to her backyard.
Botanists studied the spread of blue bugle in Mediterranean habitats.
