pea plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiː ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈpiː ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
a leguminous plant (Pisum sativum) with small white flowers and long green pods containing edible green seeds
leguminous plant with edible green seeds

Examples

The children planted a pea plant in their backyard garden.
A single pea plant can produce dozens of pods each season.
Scientists often use the pea plant to study genetics because of its distinct traits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pea - the small round green seed eaten as a vegetable
