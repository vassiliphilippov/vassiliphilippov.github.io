family Sparidae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli spəˈraɪdi / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli spəˈraɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of marine fish commonly known as porgies or scups, found in temperate and tropical waters.
a family of marine fishes (porgies, scups)

Examples

The family Sparidae contains many species prized for their flavor.
Most members of the family Sparidae are found in shallow temperate waters.
She studied the anatomy of several fish in the family Sparidae.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: porgy - a common name for fishes in the Sparidae family

Etymology

From New Latin 'Sparidae', from 'Sparus', a type genus within the family.
