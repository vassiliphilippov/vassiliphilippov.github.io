order Anthocerotales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːrdɚ ænˌθoʊsəˈroʊteɪlz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ænˌθəʊsəˈrəʊteɪlz / (BrE)
A taxonomic order of hornworts, non-vascular plants with a thalloid gametophyte, sometimes classified under the class Hepaticopsida.
an order of hornworts (plants)
medical

Examples

The order Anthocerotales comprises a group of non-vascular plants commonly known as hornworts.
Researchers discovered several new species in the order Anthocerotales during the rainforest expedition.
In older classification systems, the order Anthocerotales was sometimes included in the class Hepaticopsida.

Etymology

From Greek anthos (flower) + keras (horn) + Latin -ales (order suffix).
