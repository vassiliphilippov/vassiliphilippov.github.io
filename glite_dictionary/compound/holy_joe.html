Holy Joe

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊli ˈdʒoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli ˈdʒəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a chaplain in one of the military services, especially used in a mildly humorous or disparaging way
military chaplain (informal)
informal

Examples

When the troops needed guidance, the Holy Joe always made time for them.
The battalion’s Holy Joe led Sunday services in the field.
Despite being called Holy Joe, the chaplain was respected by all ranks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chaplain - a member of the clergy attached to a branch of the military or other institution

Etymology

Holy (from Old English hālig) + Joe (common given name), used to refer humorously to a chaplain.
