turkey stuffing

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝːki ˈstʌfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɜːki ˈstʌfɪŋ / (BrE)
A seasoned mixture of bread, herbs, and other ingredients used to fill or accompany roast turkey.
seasoned mixture for filling a turkey

Examples

My grandmother always makes her famous turkey stuffing for Thanksgiving dinner.
The secret to moist turkey stuffing is using plenty of broth and fresh herbs.
We cooked the turkey stuffing separately to make sure it was safe to eat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stuffing - seasoned mixture used to fill poultry or other foods
