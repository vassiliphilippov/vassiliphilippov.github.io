covered couch

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌvɚd kaʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈkʌvəd kaʊtʃ / (BrE)
a portable seat or litter with a canopy or covering for privacy, typically carried by people or animals
portable litter with cover for privacy

Examples

The queen traveled through the streets in a covered couch, shielded from public view.
The ancient nobles preferred a covered couch for their journeys to ensure privacy and comfort.
Servants lifted the covered couch onto their shoulders and began the ceremonial procession.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: palanquin - a covered litter for one passenger, carried on poles
