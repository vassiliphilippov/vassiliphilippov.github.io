killerly impressive

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɪlərli ɪmˈprɛsɪv / (AmE), / ˈkɪləli ɪmˈprɛsɪv / (BrE)
Extremely impressive or outstanding; exceptionally remarkable.
extremely impressive; outstanding
informal slang

Examples

Her presentation was killerly impressive and wowed the entire audience.
The graphics in the new video game are killerly impressive for any platform.
That skateboard trick was killerly impressive, even the judges stood up to applaud.

Synset Examples

Her performance was killerly impressive and left us speechless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impressive - producing a strong effect; admirable
  • Synonym: spectacular - visually striking; very impressive

Etymology

Formed from 'killer' (slang for excellent) and the adverbial suffix '-ly', plus 'impressive'.
