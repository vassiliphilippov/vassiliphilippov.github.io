football season

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊt.bɔl ˌsiː.zən / (AmE), / ˈfʊt.bɔːl ˌsiː.zən / (BrE)
the period of the year during which football games are officially scheduled and played
time when football is played

Examples

Every autumn, excitement builds as football season approaches.
During football season, the stadium is packed every weekend.
She works as an usher at the stadium only during football season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: season - a period of the year characterized by a particular activity or event
