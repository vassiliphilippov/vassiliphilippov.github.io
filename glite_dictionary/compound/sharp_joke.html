sharp joke

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑːrp dʒoʊk / (AmE), / ʃɑːp dʒəʊk / (BrE)
a joke that is particularly witty, clever, or cutting
witty or clever joke

Examples

His sharp joke had everyone at the table laughing uncontrollably.
She is known for making a sharp joke at just the right moment.
Sometimes a sharp joke can lighten even the tensest atmosphere.

Synset Examples

That joke was sharp; if it had teeth, it'd bite one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: witty remark - a clever and amusing comment
