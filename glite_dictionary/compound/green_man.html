green man

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin mæn / (AmE), / ɡriːn mæn / (BrE)
a decorative figure or motif, typically of a face surrounded by or made from leaves, symbolizing nature and rebirth and often found in art and architecture
decorative motif of foliage-faced figure

Examples

The stone arch was adorned with a beautifully carved green man surrounded by ivy.
Many medieval churches in England feature the mysterious image of the green man on their walls.
The artist explained that the green man represents the spirit of nature in her mural.

Synset Examples

She painted a cute little green man on her canvas.
