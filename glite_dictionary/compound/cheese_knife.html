cheese knife

1. NOUN

/ tʃiz naɪf / (AmE), / tʃiːz naɪf / (BrE)
a knife designed specifically for cutting cheese, often featuring a narrow blade to reduce sticking
knife designed for cutting cheese

Examples

The chef selected a cheese knife to serve the brie at the party.
A good cheese knife prevents soft cheese from sticking to the blade.
She bought a new set, including a cheese knife with a forked tip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knife - a tool or implement with a blade used for cutting
