buoyant laughter

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔɪənt ˈlæftɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪənt ˈlɑːftə / (BrE)
laughter that is cheerful, lively, and uplifting
cheerful, uplifting laughter

Examples

The sound of her buoyant laughter made everyone in the café smile.
His buoyant laughter was contagious, lifting the spirits of the entire group.
After hearing the good news, buoyant laughter echoed through the house.

Synset Examples

The child's buoyant laughter filled the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hearty laughter - laughter that is loud and sincere
