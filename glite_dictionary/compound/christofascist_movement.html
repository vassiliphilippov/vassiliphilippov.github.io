Christofascist movement

1. NOUN

/ ˌkrɪstəʊˈfæʃɪst ˈmuˌvmənt / (AmE), / ˌkrɪstəʊˈfæʃɪst ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
a political movement that combines elements of Christianity with authoritarian or fascist ideologies
political movement combining Christianity and fascism

Examples

The journalist investigated the influence of the Christofascist movement on local politics.
Scholars argue that the Christofascist movement poses a threat to democratic values.
Protesters gathered to oppose policies inspired by the Christofascist movement.

Synset Examples

Her article discussed the rise of Christofascist movements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theocracy - a system of government in which priests rule in the name of God
  • Synonym: fascism - an authoritarian and nationalistic right-wing system of government
  • Antonym: secularism - the principle of separation of the state from religious institutions

Etymology

Blend of 'Christian' and 'fascist', originally used in political criticism in the late 20th century.
