air letter

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌlɛt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌletə / (BrE)
a lightweight letter sheet that is specifically designed to be sent by air mail, often folding into its own envelope
lightweight letter sent by air mail

Examples

She sent her family an air letter every month while she was living abroad.
Please use an air letter if you want your message to arrive quickly by international mail.
He carefully folded the air letter before addressing it to his cousin in Australia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aerogramme - a lightweight sheet for writing a letter to be sent by airmail, folding into its own envelope
