Ad-Dahna Desert

1. PROPN

/ æd ˈdɑːnə ˈdɛzərt / (AmE), / ad ˈdɑːnə ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
A desert region located in southern Saudi Arabia, known for its long arc of red sand dunes.
desert in southern Saudi Arabia

Examples

The Ad-Dahna Desert features striking red sand dunes that stretch for hundreds of kilometers.
Explorers often travel through the Ad-Dahna Desert when crossing central Saudi Arabia.
The unique landscape of the Ad-Dahna Desert supports only sparse vegetation due to its arid climate.

Etymology

From Arabic 'Ad-Dahna' meaning 'the red (desert)', referring to the color of its sand.
