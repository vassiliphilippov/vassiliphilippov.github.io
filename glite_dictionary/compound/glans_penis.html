glans penis

1. NOUN

/ ɡlænz ˈpiːnɪs / (AmE), / ɡlan(z) ˈpiːnɪs / (BrE)
the rounded, sensitive tip of the penis, composed of erectile tissue
the head of the penis
medical

Examples

The glans penis is highly sensitive and an important part of male sexual anatomy.
Proper hygiene of the glans penis is important to prevent infections.
During circumcision, the foreskin covering the glans penis is surgically removed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glans clitoridis - the rounded, sensitive tip of the clitoris

Etymology

From Latin glans (acorn) + penis (penis).
