humming top

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌmɪŋ tɑp / (AmE), / ˈhʌmɪŋ tɒp / (BrE)
a spinning toy that produces a humming sound as it spins, usually operated by twisting or pushing a plunger
spinning toy that hums

Examples

The children played with the humming top on the kitchen floor, delighted by its whirring sound.
She gave her nephew a brightly painted humming top for his birthday.
As the humming top spun faster, the soft drone grew louder and more musical.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top - a child's spinning toy
