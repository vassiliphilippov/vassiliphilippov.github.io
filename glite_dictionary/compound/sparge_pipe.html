sparge pipe

1. NOUN

/ spɑrdʒ paɪp / (AmE), / spɑːdʒ paɪp / (BrE)
a horizontal pipe with small holes along its length, used to spray water or other liquids evenly
pipe with holes for spraying water

Examples

The sparge pipe distributes water evenly across the filter bed during backwashing.
Engineers inspected the sparge pipe for any signs of clogging or corrosion.
A new sparge pipe was installed to improve the efficiency of the cooling system.

Etymology

From 'sparge' (to sprinkle or scatter), from Latin 'spargere' (to sprinkle), plus 'pipe'.
