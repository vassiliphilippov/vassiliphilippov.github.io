Achras zapota

1. NOUN

/ ˈækrəs zəˈpoʊtə / (AmE), / ˈækrəs zəˈpəʊtə / (BrE)
A large evergreen tropical American tree, also known as sapodilla, that produces chicle gum and edible fruit.
tropical tree yielding chicle and sapodilla fruit

Examples

The latex from Achras zapota is a traditional source of natural chewing gum.
Botanists studied the growth patterns of Achras zapota in various climates.
The fruit of Achras zapota is sweet and popular in many tropical countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sapodilla - Common name for Achras zapota, a tropical tree with edible fruit

Etymology

From Latinized scientific name; 'Achras' from Greek, 'zapota' from indigenous American origins.
