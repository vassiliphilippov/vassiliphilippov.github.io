one-eyed glasses

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌn.aɪd ˈɡlæs.ɪz / (AmE), / ˈwʌn.aɪd ˈɡlɑː.sɪz / (BrE)
humorous term for a pair of glasses with only one lens.
glasses with only one lens (humorous)
informal

Examples

After breaking his spectacles, he wore his one-eyed glasses and made everyone laugh.
She brought a pair of one-eyed glasses as a prank for the costume party.
He joked about taking notes with his one-eyed glasses on, winking at the audience.

Synset Examples

He joked about being a cyclops with his one-eyed glasses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monocle - a single eye glass for one eye, often held in place by eye muscles
