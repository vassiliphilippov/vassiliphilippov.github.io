fragrant cliff fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈfreɪɡrənt klɪf fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈfreɪɡrənt klɪf fɜːn / (BrE)
a fern native to northern Eurasia and North America, distinguished by its fragrant fronds
a fern with fragrant fronds

Examples

The fragrant cliff fern thrived in the cool, shaded areas of the northern forest.
Her garden boasts a variety of ferns, including the rare fragrant cliff fern.
Botanists were excited to discover a patch of fragrant cliff ferns along the rocky slope.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fern - a non-flowering vascular plant with feathery leaves
