competitor analysis

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpɛtətər əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / kəmˈpɛtɪtə əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
the process of evaluating the strengths and weaknesses of existing and potential business competitors, especially to inform strategy in marketing and management
assessment of business competitors

Examples

The company conducted a thorough competitor analysis before launching its new product.
Effective competitor analysis can help businesses identify opportunities and threats in their industry.
Her job involves performing competitor analysis to support the marketing team's strategic decisions.

Synset Examples

If you are looking at one specific communications area, such as a website, conduct a competitor analysis of that in a bit more detail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: market analysis - an assessment of a specific market within an industry
