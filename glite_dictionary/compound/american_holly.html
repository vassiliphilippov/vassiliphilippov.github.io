American holly

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛɹɪkən ˈhɑli / (AmE), / əˈmɛɹɪkən ˈhɒli / (BrE)
an evergreen tree of eastern North America (Ilex opaca), with prickly leaves and red berries, often used for decoration at Christmas
evergreen tree with prickly leaves and red berries

Examples

The American holly is popular for its glossy leaves and bright red berries during the winter.
Gardeners often plant American holly as an ornamental tree in their yards.
Birds are attracted to the berries of the American holly in late autumn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holly - any tree or shrub of the genus Ilex, especially those with prickly leaves and red berries
