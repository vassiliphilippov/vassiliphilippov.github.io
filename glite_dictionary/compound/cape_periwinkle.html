cape periwinkle

1. NOUN

/ ˌkeɪp ˈpɛriˌwɪŋkəl / (AmE), / ˌkeɪp ˈpɛrɪˌwɪŋkl̩ / (BrE)
a woody plant of the Old World, often cultivated for its large pinkish to red flowers; also known as Madagascar periwinkle
woody plant with pinkish-red flowers

Examples

The cape periwinkle is a popular choice for gardens due to its vivid blooms.
Researchers study the cape periwinkle for its medicinal properties.
She planted several cape periwinkles along the walkway for a splash of color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Madagascar periwinkle - an alternative name for the same flowering plant species
  • Degree Lower: periwinkle - a group of flowering plants in the genera Vinca and Catharanthus
