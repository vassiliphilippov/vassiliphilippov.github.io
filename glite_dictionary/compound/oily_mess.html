oily mess

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔɪli mɛs / (AmE), / ˈɔɪli mɛs / (BrE)
a dirty or greasy substance, especially one that is messy or difficult to clean up
greasy, dirty substance

Examples

The mechanic wiped the oily mess off his hands after fixing the engine.
She accidentally spilled salad dressing, leaving an oily mess on the kitchen counter.
After the boat leaked, there was an oily mess floating on the surface of the water.

Synset Examples

The car's engine was making an oily mess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grease - a thick oily substance used as a lubricant or for cooking
