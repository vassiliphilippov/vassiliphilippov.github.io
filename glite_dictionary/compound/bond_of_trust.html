bond of trust

1. NOUN

/ bɑnd əv trʌst / (AmE), / bɒnd əv trʌst / (BrE)
a strong and enduring relationship based on mutual confidence and reliance between people
relationship based on mutual confidence

Examples

The two colleagues developed a bond of trust that made teamwork easy.
A lasting bond of trust between teacher and student can transform learning experiences.
After years of honesty, a bond of trust was formed between the families.

Synset Examples

Their friendship was built on an unshakable bond of trust.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trust - firm belief in the reliability, truth, or ability of someone or something
  • Synonym: bond - a strong connection or tie between people
