chameleon personality

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmiliən pərˌsænələti / (AmE), / kəˈmiːliən pəˈsɒnələti / (BrE)
a person whose character or behavior changes to adapt to different situations or people
someone who adapts to fit in

Examples

She has a chameleon personality that allows her to thrive in any environment.
His chameleon personality makes him blend seamlessly into every new group he joins.
With a chameleon personality, he always knows how to act around different people.

Synset Examples

His chameleon personality makes him popular everywhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: social chameleon - a person who changes behavior to suit different social situations

Etymology

From 'chameleon', a lizard known for changing its color to blend with surroundings, used metaphorically for adaptability.
