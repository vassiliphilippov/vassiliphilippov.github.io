red mullet

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈmʌlɪt / (AmE), / rɛd ˈmʌlɪt / (BrE)
a brightly colored tropical fish with chin barbels, often reddish in color, found in warm seas
a tropical fish with chin barbels

Examples

Local markets often sell red mullet caught fresh from the Mediterranean Sea.
The chef prepared a delicious dish using grilled red mullet and aromatic herbs.
Divers observed a school of red mullet darting among the coral reefs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mullet - a fish of the family Mullidae, related and similar in appearance
