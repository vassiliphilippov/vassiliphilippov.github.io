hand-held microcomputer

1. NOUN

/ ˈhænd ˌhɛld ˌmaɪkroʊkəmˈpjuːtər / (AmE), / ˈhænd ˌhɛld ˌmʌɪkrəʊkəmˈpjuːtə / (BrE)
A small, portable, battery-powered computer that is compact enough to be carried in a hand or pocket.
small portable computer

Examples

The engineer used a hand-held microcomputer to collect data in the field.
Early hand-held microcomputers paved the way for modern smartphones and tablets.
With a hand-held microcomputer, users could access information anywhere and anytime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: palmtop - a very small portable computer that fits in your palm

Etymology

From 'hand-held' (able to be held in the hand) and 'microcomputer' (small computer).
