manufacturing business

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæn.jəˈfæk.tʃɚ.ɪŋ ˈbɪz.nɪs / (AmE), / ˌmæn.jʊˈfæk.tʃər.ɪŋ ˈbɪz.nɪs / (BrE)
a company or enterprise engaged in the production of goods using labor, machines, tools, and chemical or biological processing or formulation
company that manufactures goods

Examples

After years of planning, she finally launched her own manufacturing business producing eco-friendly packaging.
The city’s economy depends heavily on small manufacturing businesses that employ thousands of workers.
Government policies can greatly affect the success of a manufacturing business in a competitive market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manufacturer - a person or company that manufactures goods
  • Antonym: service business - a business that provides services rather than goods
