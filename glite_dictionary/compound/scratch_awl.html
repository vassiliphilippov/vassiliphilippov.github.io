scratch awl

1. NOUN

/ ˈskrætʃ ɔl / (AmE), / ˈskrætʃ ɔːl / (BrE)
a sharp-pointed tool used to mark wood or metal surfaces before cutting
tool for marking wood or metal before cutting

Examples

The carpenter used a scratch awl to mark the line for the saw cut.
Before drilling, she made a small dent with a scratch awl to keep the bit from slipping.
A scratch awl is an essential tool for precise woodworking projects.
