personal magnetism

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrsənl mægˈnɛtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl mæɡˈnɛtɪzəm / (BrE)
An attractive or compelling quality in a person that enables them to influence or charm others.
influential personal charm or appeal

Examples

The candidate's personal magnetism drew supporters from across the country.
Despite lacking experience, her personal magnetism quickly impressed everyone at the meeting.
Many leaders rely on personal magnetism to motivate their teams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charisma - compelling attractiveness or charm that can inspire devotion in others
  • Synonym: charm - the power or quality of delighting, attracting, or fascinating others
