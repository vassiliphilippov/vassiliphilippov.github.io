cracker joke

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrækər ˌdʒoʊk / (AmE), / ˈkrækə ˌdʒəʊk / (BrE)
a very funny or clever joke that causes a lot of laughter
a particularly funny or clever joke
british informal

Examples

The comedian is known for telling cracker jokes that have the entire audience in stitches.
She always comes up with a cracker joke at the perfect moment to lighten the mood.
His latest cracker joke went viral on social media for its clever punchline.

Synset Examples

The comedian is known for his cracker jokes that always make people laugh.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: joke - something said or done to provoke laughter
  • Synonym: wisecrack - a clever or sarcastic remark or joke

Etymology

From British slang 'cracker' meaning 'something excellent', plus 'joke'.
