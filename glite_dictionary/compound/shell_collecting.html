shell collecting

1. NOUN

/ ʃɛl kəˈlɛktɪŋ / (AmE), / ʃɛl kəˈlɛktɪŋ / (BrE)
the hobby or practice of collecting and studying mollusc shells
hobby of collecting mollusc shells

Examples

As a child, Emily developed a passion for shell collecting during her summer holidays by the sea.
Shell collecting has become a popular pastime among nature enthusiasts around the world.
His extensive knowledge of marine life comes from years of dedicated shell collecting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conchology - the scientific study of mollusc shells
