milking table

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlkɪŋ ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkɪŋ ˌteɪbəl / (BrE)
a table designed or used for milking cows, typically assisting with the milking process on a farm
table used for milking cows

Examples

The farmer demonstrated how to use a milking table during the morning chores.
She cleaned the milking table thoroughly before bringing in the cows.
Modern dairies sometimes replace the traditional milking table with automated equipment.

Synset Examples

They set up a milking table for the farm tour.
At the festival, children learned about cows at the milking table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milking stool - a simple stool used while milking cows
