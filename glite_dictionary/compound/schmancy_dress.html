schmancy dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃmæn.si drɛs / (AmE), / ˈʃmæn.si drɛs / (BrE)
An informal term for a fancy or stylish dress, often used humorously or playfully.
fancy or stylish dress (informal)
informal

Examples

She wore a schmancy dress to the gala and caught everyone's attention.
I don't have a schmancy dress for the party, but I'll make do with what I have.
They showed up in a schmancy dress, making the event feel even more special.

Synset Examples

She wore a schmancy dress to the gala, turning heads everywhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fancy dress - dress that is elegant or elaborate

Etymology

From 'schmancy', a playful alteration of 'fancy' influenced by Yiddish reduplication patterns (as in 'fancy-schmancy').
