poverty porn

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑvɚti pɔrn / (AmE), / ˈpɒvəti pɔːn / (BrE)
media, images, or narratives that depict poverty in a sensationalized or exploitative way, often to provoke sympathy or shock.
sensationalized exploitation of poverty in media

Examples

The documentary was accused of indulging in poverty porn instead of telling real stories.
Many critics argue that some charity advertisements resemble poverty porn.
She urged the media to avoid poverty porn when covering disadvantaged communities.

Synset Examples

The documentary was criticized for its poverty porn approach.
He felt the article fell into the trap of poverty porn.
They avoided poverty porn in their fundraising campaign.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: misery porn - media that exploits or sensationalizes suffering for emotional impact

Etymology

Formed by combining 'poverty' and 'porn', drawing on 'porn' as a suffix for material that stimulates a strong emotional reaction.
