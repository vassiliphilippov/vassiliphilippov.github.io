real queenie

1. NOUN

/ ˈɹiəl ˈkwini / (AmE), / ˈɹiːəl ˈkwiːni / (BrE)
someone who behaves in an exaggeratedly flamboyant or dramatic manner, often attracting attention; sometimes used in reference to effeminate behavior.
flamboyant or dramatic person

Examples

At every meeting, she acts like a real queenie, demanding all the attention.
He turned on his real queenie persona for the talent show, stealing the spotlight.
Whenever things don't go her way, she becomes a real queenie and makes a scene.

Synset Examples

She always acted like a real queenie at parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drama queen - someone who habitually overreacts or behaves melodramatically
