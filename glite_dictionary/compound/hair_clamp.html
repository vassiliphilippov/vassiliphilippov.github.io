hair clamp

1. NOUN

/ hɛr klæmp / (AmE), / heə klamp / (BrE)
a type of hair clip featuring interlocking teeth and a spring mechanism, used to hold hair securely in place
spring-loaded hair clip with interlocking teeth

Examples

She used a hair clamp to pin her hair up for work.
I prefer a hair clamp to an elastic band because it doesn’t pull my hair.
My sister carries a hair clamp in her purse for quick touch-ups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair clip - a device used to hold hair in place
  • Synonym: barrette - a bar-shaped clip for holding hair in place
