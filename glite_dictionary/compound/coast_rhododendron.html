coast rhododendron

1. NOUN

/ koʊst ɹoʊdəˈdɛndrən / (AmE), / kəʊst rəʊdəˈdɛndrən / (BrE)
a medium-sized rhododendron native to the Pacific coast of North America, with large rosy flowers spotted with brown
rhododendron shrub of Pacific North America

Examples

The coast rhododendron blooms beautifully each spring in the forests of Washington state.
Gardeners prize the coast rhododendron for its striking rosy flowers and hardy nature.
We spotted several coast rhododendrons along the hiking trail near the Pacific coastline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rhododendron macrophyllum - botanical name for coast rhododendron
