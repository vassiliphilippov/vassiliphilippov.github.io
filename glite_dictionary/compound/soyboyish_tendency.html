soyboyish tendency

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔɪˌbɔɪɪʃ tənˈdɛnsi / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪˌbɔɪɪʃ tɛnˈdənsi / (BrE)
behavior or traits that are considered unmasculine or weak, often used in a derogatory or mocking way in online contexts
perceived weak or unmasculine behavior (derogatory, slang)
slang offensive

Examples

His soyboyish tendency was apparent when he backed down from every argument.
Some online forums mock anyone showing a soyboyish tendency or sensitive emotions.
Critics accused the celebrity of having a soyboyish tendency after his emotional response during the interview.

Synset Examples

His soyboyish tendencies made him the target of jokes.
The character's soyboyish traits were played for laughs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: effeminacy - quality of having traits more associated with women than men
  • Antonym: manliness - the quality of being typically masculine

Etymology

From slang 'soyboy' (mocking term for unmasculine men) + '-ish' (having characteristics of) + 'tendency'. 'Soyboy' likely refers to the stereotype linking soy consumption to lower masculinity.
