pistol shrimp

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪstəl ˌʃrɪmp / (AmE), / ˈpɪstəl ˌʃrɪmp / (BrE)
a small marine shrimp with one oversized claw that snaps shut with a loud sound, used to stun prey and communicate
shrimp with snapping claw

Examples

The pistol shrimp can create a sonic blast strong enough to stun small fish.
Marine biologists study the unique snapping mechanism of the pistol shrimp.
The aquarium featured a display of colorful pistol shrimp in their natural habitat.
