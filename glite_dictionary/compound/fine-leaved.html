fine-leaved

1. ADJ

/ faɪn livd / (AmE), / faɪn liːvd / (BrE)
(of a plant) having slender, delicate, or narrow leaves.
having slender or delicate leaves

Examples

The fine-leaved fescue is popular in low-maintenance lawns.
Many fine-leaved herbs have a delicate appearance in the garden.
Some fine-leaved plants thrive in sandy, well-drained soils.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: broad-leaved - having broad or wide leaves
