crittercam technology

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪtərˌkæm tɛkˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˈkrɪtəˌkæm tɛkˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
a type of camera system attached to animals to record their behavior and environment in the wild
animal-mounted camera technology

Examples

Researchers deployed crittercam technology to monitor the hunting habits of seals in their natural habitat.
The use of crittercam technology has revolutionized the way scientists study marine animals without disturbing them.
With advances in crittercam technology, high-definition footage of elusive wildlife behavior is now possible.

Synset Examples

Scientists use crittercam technology to study animal migration.

Etymology

From 'critter' (informal for animal) + 'cam' (short for camera) + 'technology'.
