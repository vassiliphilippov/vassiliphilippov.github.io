armour plate

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmɚ pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈɑːmə pleɪt / (BrE)
A specially hardened metal plate used to protect vehicles or structures from enemy fire or explosions.
hardened protective metal plate

Examples

The tank was equipped with thick armour plate to withstand direct hits.
Workers installed armour plate on the ship's hull for added protection.
Decades of exposure had rusted the old fortress's armour plate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armor - protective covering, often metal, used to prevent damage
