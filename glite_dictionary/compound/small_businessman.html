small businessman

1. NOUN

/ smɔl ˈbɪznɪsˌmæn / (AmE), / smɔːl ˈbɪznɪsˌman / (BrE)
a person who owns or manages a business with fewer than 100 employees
owner of a small business

Examples

The small businessman struggled to keep his store open during the economic downturn.
Many small businessmen rely on local suppliers to keep their operations running smoothly.
As a small businessman, he values personal relationships with his employees and customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entrepreneur - a person who starts or organizes a business, taking risks for profit
