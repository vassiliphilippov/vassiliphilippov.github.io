bomber crew

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːmər kruː / (AmE), / ˈbɒmə kruː / (BrE)
the group of people who operate a bomber aircraft
the crew of a bomber aircraft

Examples

The bomber crew completed their mission despite heavy enemy fire.
Every member of the bomber crew had a specific role during the flight.
During World War II, a bomber crew often faced dangerous conditions in the skies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crew - a group of people who work on and operate a vehicle, especially an aircraft, ship, or train
