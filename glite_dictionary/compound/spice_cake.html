spice cake

1. NOUN

/ spaɪs keɪk / (AmE), / spaɪs keɪk / (BrE)
A cake flavored with a mixture of spices, such as cinnamon, nutmeg, or cloves.
cake flavored with spices

Examples

My grandmother always bakes a delicious spice cake for the holidays.
The aroma of spice cake filled the kitchen as it baked in the oven.
Would you like a slice of spice cake with your tea?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gingerbread - a cake or cookie flavored primarily with ginger and spices
  • Synonym: fruitcake - a cake containing dried fruit and nuts
