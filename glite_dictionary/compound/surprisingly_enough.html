surprisingly enough

1. ADV

/ sərˈpraɪzɪŋli ɪˈnʌf / (AmE), / səˈpraɪzɪŋli ɪˈnʌf / (BrE)
Used to indicate that something is unexpected or more surprising than anticipated.
unexpectedly; more than one might think

Examples

Surprisingly enough, the restaurant was still open after midnight.
Surprisingly enough, she finished the marathon despite her injury.
Surprisingly enough, he agreed to help even though he was very busy.

Synset Examples

The phone box still worked, surprisingly enough.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unexpectedly - in a way that is not expected
