drinking chocolate

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ ˌtʃɑk(ə)lət / (AmE), / ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ ˌtʃɒklət / (BrE)
A sweet, hot beverage made from cocoa powder, milk, and sugar.
hot cocoa drink

Examples

I like to have a cup of drinking chocolate before bed on cold nights.
She ordered drinking chocolate at the café instead of her usual coffee.
Rich and creamy drinking chocolate is a popular treat at winter festivals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot chocolate - a hot drink made with chocolate or cocoa and milk or water
