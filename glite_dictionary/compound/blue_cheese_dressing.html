blue cheese dressing

1. NOUN

/ blu tʃiz ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / bluː tʃiːz ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
a creamy salad dressing made with crumbled blue cheese, mayonnaise, and other ingredients
creamy salad dressing with blue cheese

Examples

He poured blue cheese dressing over his salad before digging in.
Wings are often served with celery sticks and a side of blue cheese dressing.
Some people prefer blue cheese dressing as a dip for vegetables.
