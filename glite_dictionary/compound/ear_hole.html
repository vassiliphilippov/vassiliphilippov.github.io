ear hole

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪr hoʊl / (AmE), / ˈɪə həʊl / (BrE)
an opening in a helmet or similar object that allows sound to reach the ear
opening in helmet for the ear

Examples

Make sure the ear hole of your helmet lines up with your ear before fastening the straps.
Modern headphones are designed with a small ear hole for better sound transmission.
Some medieval armor included an ear hole to help soldiers hear commands during battle.
