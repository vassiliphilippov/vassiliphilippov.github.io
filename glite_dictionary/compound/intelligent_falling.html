intelligent falling

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtɛlədʒənt ˈfɔlɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənt ˈfɒlɪŋ / (BrE)
a satirical theory proposing that the phenomenon of gravity is caused by the intervention of a higher intelligence rather than natural physical laws; used to parody non-scientific explanations for scientific phenomena
parody theory attributing gravity to intelligent agency
informal

Examples

The scientific community dismissed intelligent falling as a joke rather than a credible theory.
He used the idea of intelligent falling to highlight misunderstandings about physics.
Many online articles reference intelligent falling to satirize debates over science and faith.
