Northern Baptist

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðərn ˈbæptɪst / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈbæptɪst / (BrE)
a member of a formerly northern U.S. branch of the Baptist Church, now known as the American Baptist Churches USA
member of a U.S. Baptist denomination

Examples

She grew up as a Northern Baptist and still attends church every Sunday.
The Northern Baptists supported various social reform movements in the early twentieth century.
My grandfather was a devoted Northern Baptist all his life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: American Baptist - a member of the American Baptist Churches USA
