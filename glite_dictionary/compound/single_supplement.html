single supplement

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋɡəl ˌsʌpləˌmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl ˌsʌplɪmənt / (BrE)
A charge added to the price of a trip or accommodation for a person traveling alone, to cover the cost of single occupancy.
extra fee for solo travelers

Examples

There is a single supplement of $200 for those wishing to have their own room.
Many travel companies charge a single supplement for solo travelers.
I was disappointed to learn the cruise required a hefty single supplement.
