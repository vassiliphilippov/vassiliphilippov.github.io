effulgent smile

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈfʌldʒənt smaɪl / (AmE), / ɪˈfʌldʒənt smaɪl / (BrE)
A smile that is radiant or shining with joy or warmth.
radiant or shining smile

Examples

Her effulgent smile brightened everyone's day at the office.
He greeted his friends with an effulgent smile that was impossible to resist.
The bride's effulgent smile reflected her happiness on her wedding day.

Synset Examples

Her effulgent smile lit up the entire gathering.
Her refulgent smile lit up the entire room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiant smile - A smile that glows with happiness or energy

Etymology

From Latin 'effulgere' (to shine forth); 'smile' from Old English 'smīelan'.
