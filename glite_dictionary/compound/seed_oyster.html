seed oyster

1. NOUN

/ siːd ˈɔɪstər / (AmE), / siːd ˈɔɪstə / (BrE)
a young oyster, especially one of a size suitable for transplantation to an oyster bed for further growth
young oyster for transplantation

Examples

The hatchery supplies seed oysters to local shellfish farmers every spring.
Growing seed oysters in protected beds helps boost wild oyster populations.
After a few months, the seed oysters are transplanted to open water to mature.
