policeman bird

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlismən bɝd / (AmE), / pəˈlismən bɜːd / (BrE)
a large, mostly white Australian stork, also known as the black-necked stork, found in wetlands and floodplains.
Australian stork; black-necked stork

Examples

The policeman bird waded gracefully through the shallow wetlands, its striking colors catching the sunlight.
Many tourists hope to spot a policeman bird in the northern regions of Australia.
The distinctive call of the policeman bird can often be heard near riverbanks during the rainy season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black-necked stork - a large wading bird with a black neck found in Asia and Australia
