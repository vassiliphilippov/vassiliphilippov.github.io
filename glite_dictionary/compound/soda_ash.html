soda ash

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊdə ˌæʃ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdə ˌæʃ / (BrE)
sodium carbonate, a white powdery substance used chiefly in making soap, glass, and paper.
sodium carbonate used in industry

Examples

The factory uses soda ash to manufacture glass bottles.
She ordered several bags of soda ash for her soap-making business.
Industrial processes often require large quantities of soda ash.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sodium carbonate - the chemical name for soda ash
