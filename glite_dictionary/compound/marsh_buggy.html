marsh buggy

1. NOUN

/ mɑrʃ ˈbʌɡi / (AmE), / mɑːʃ ˈbʌɡi / (BrE)
an amphibious vehicle with a raised body and large wheels, designed for travel over wet, marshy ground
amphibious vehicle for marshy terrain

Examples

The engineers used a marsh buggy to access the flooded swamp for repairs.
A marsh buggy can easily traverse areas where standard vehicles would get stuck.
They hired a marsh buggy to transport equipment through the wetlands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amphibious vehicle - a vehicle that can operate both on land and in water
