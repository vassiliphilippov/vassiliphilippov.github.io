on the move

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə muv / (AmE), / ɒn ðə muːv / (BrE)
actively engaged in traveling, changing locations, or involved in a series of activities or changes
actively changing location or activity

Examples

During the campaign season, the politician was constantly on the move across the country.
The wildlife documentary followed elephants as they were on the move through the savannah.
With a new job offer, she feels her life is once again on the move.

Synset Examples

The team is on the move to meet the deadline.
She’s always on the move, never slowing down.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on the go - being very busy or active
