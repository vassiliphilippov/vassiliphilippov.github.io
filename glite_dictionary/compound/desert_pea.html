desert pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛzɚt pi / (AmE), / ˈdezət piː / (BrE)
a sprawling shrubby perennial plant native to arid regions of Australia, known for its striking scarlet flowers with a black spot
Australian plant with scarlet-black flowers

Examples

The Australian outback is home to the beautiful desert pea, with its dramatic red and black blooms.
Gardeners admire the desert pea for its ability to thrive in harsh, dry conditions.
We spotted a cluster of desert peas growing along the sandy roadside.
