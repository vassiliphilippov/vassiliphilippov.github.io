simulated military operation

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪmjəˌleɪtɪd ˌmɪlɪˈtɛri ˌɑpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɪmjʊˌleɪtɪd ˌmɪlɪˈtɛri ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
an activity in which armed forces practice tactics and maneuvers under conditions designed to imitate real combat, without actual hostilities
military training exercise mimicking real combat

Examples

The army regularly conducts simulated military operations to prepare for potential conflicts.
During the simulated military operation, soldiers practiced urban warfare tactics in a controlled environment.
The general observed the progress of the simulated military operation from the command center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: war game - a military exercise simulating warfare conditions
