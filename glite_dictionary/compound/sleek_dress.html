sleek dress

1. NOUN

/ sliːk drɛs / (AmE), / sliːk drɛs / (BrE)
a dress that is smooth-fitting, elegant, and stylish in appearance
smooth-fitting, stylish dress

Examples

She turned heads when she arrived in a sleek dress at the awards ceremony.
The boutique specializes in custom-made sleek dresses for special occasions.
With its modern cut and glossy fabric, the sleek dress was perfect for the cocktail party.

Synset Examples

She wore a sleek dress to the gala.
