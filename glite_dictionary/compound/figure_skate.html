figure skate

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪɡjɚ skeɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡjə skeɪt / (BrE)
an ice skate designed for figure skating, featuring a slightly curved blade and a row of jagged points at the front for performing jumps and spins
ice skate for figure skating

Examples

She laced up her figure skate before stepping onto the rink for practice.
The front of a figure skate blade has toe picks to help skaters execute jumps.
He accidentally brought a hockey skate instead of his figure skate to the lesson.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice skate - a boot with a blade for skating on ice
