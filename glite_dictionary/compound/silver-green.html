silver-green

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlvər ˌɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˌɡriːn / (BrE)
having a light, shiny green color with a silvery sheen
light green with a silvery shine

Examples

The leaves glimmered in the morning sun with a silver-green hue.
She chose a dress of delicate silver-green silk for the evening party.
A silver-green moss covered the stones by the riverbank.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: olive-green - a yellowish-green color resembling that of an olive
  • Degree Higher: emerald-green - a bright, rich green color like that of an emerald
