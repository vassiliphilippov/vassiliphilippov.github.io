Pacific bottlenose dolphin

1. NOUN

/ pəˈsɪfɪk ˈbɑt̬l̩ˌnoʊs ˈdɑlfɪn / (AmE), / pəˈsɪfɪk ˈbɒtl̩ˌnəʊs ˈdɒlfɪn / (BrE)
a type of bottlenose dolphin (Tursiops truncatus gillii) found in the Pacific Ocean
bottlenose dolphin of the Pacific Ocean

Examples

Many tourists hope to spot a Pacific bottlenose dolphin during their boat trips off the California coast.
Researchers are studying the migration patterns of the Pacific bottlenose dolphin to better understand their feeding habits.
A pod of Pacific bottlenose dolphins was seen playing near the shore early this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bottlenose dolphin - a species of dolphin with a rounded forehead and short beak
