wap routine

1. NOUN

/ wɑp ruˈtin / (AmE), / wɒp ruˈtiːn / (BrE)
a choreographed dance style, often involving twerking and dramatic moves, popularized by the song 'WAP' by Cardi B and Megan Thee Stallion
specific dance style from 'WAP' song
informal

Examples

The dancers received a standing ovation after flawlessly executing the wap routine.
She learned every move of the wap routine from online tutorials.
Some schools banned the wap routine due to its provocative nature.

Synset Examples

Everyone was surprised when she performed the wap routine.

Etymology

From 'WAP', the title of a 2020 song by Cardi B and Megan Thee Stallion, combined with 'routine' meaning a sequence of dance steps.
