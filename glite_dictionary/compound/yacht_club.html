yacht club

1. NOUN

/ jɑt klʌb / (AmE), / jɒt klʌb / (BrE)
an organization or social club for people interested in yachting and boating, often providing facilities and organizing events
club for yachting and boating enthusiasts

Examples

She became a member of the local yacht club to learn more about sailing.
The annual regatta is organized by the city's prestigious yacht club.
Members of the yacht club often gather for social events and boat races.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boating club - a club that promotes and supports boating in general
