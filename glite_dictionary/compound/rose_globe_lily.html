rose globe lily

1. NOUN

/ roʊz ɡloʊb ˈlɪli / (AmE), / rəʊz ɡləʊb ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A globe lily with deep rose-pink or purple egg-shaped flowers on bending stems, native to the western slopes of the Sierra Nevada in California's San Joaquin Valley.
a globe lily with rose-pink flowers, native to California

Examples

The rose globe lily blooms in early spring across the foothills of the Sierra Nevada.
Botanists carefully studied the habitat of the beautiful rose globe lily in the San Joaquin Valley.
Hikers admired the striking purple petals of the rose globe lily growing along the trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: globe lily - a type of lily with globe-shaped flowers
