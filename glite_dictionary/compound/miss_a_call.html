to miss a call

1. VERB

/ mɪs ə kɑl / (AmE), / mɪs ə kɔːl / (BrE)
to fail to answer a telephone call before it stops ringing
fail to answer a phone call

Examples

I didn't hear my phone ring, so I missed a call from my boss this morning.
If you miss a call, check your voicemail for any important messages.
She was busy in a meeting and missed a call from her doctor.

Synset Examples

He called me yesterday, but I missed his call.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignore a call - to deliberately decide not to answer a phone call
