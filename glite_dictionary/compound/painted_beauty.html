painted beauty

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪntɪd ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪd ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
an American butterfly with dark brown wings marked with white and golden orange spots
American butterfly with spotted brown wings

Examples

The painted beauty alighted gracefully on the blossom, its wings flashing in the sunlight.
Many gardeners enjoy attracting painted beauties by planting native wildflowers.
We spotted a painted beauty fluttering along the edge of the forest.
