steak and kidney pie

1. NOUN

/ steɪk ənd ˈkɪdni paɪ / (AmE), / steɪk ənd ˈkɪdni paɪ / (BrE)
A pastry pie filled with beef steak, kidneys, and usually onions, often cooked in gravy or stock.
pastry pie with beef, kidneys, and gravy
british

Examples

A traditional British meal, steak and kidney pie is often served with mashed potatoes and peas.
He learned to make steak and kidney pie from his grandmother's old recipe.
We ordered a piping hot steak and kidney pie at the country pub.
