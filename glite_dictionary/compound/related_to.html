related to

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈleɪtɪd tu / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪtɪd tʊ / (BrE)
connected with or associated with something in some way, either logically, causally, or by shared characteristics.
connected with; associated with

Examples

Many of her symptoms are related to stress at work.
The museum houses artifacts related to the region's history.
Questions related to the project should be directed to your supervisor.

Synset Examples

painting and the related arts
school-related activities
related to micelle formation is the … ability of detergent actives to congregate at oil-water interfaces

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unrelated to - not connected or associated with; opposite of 'related to'
  • Synonym: associated with - having a connection or relation to something
