public holiday

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪk ˈhɑlɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈhɒlɪˌdeɪ / (BrE)
A day that is recognized by law as a holiday, when work and official business are generally suspended.
officially recognized holiday

Examples

All government offices will be closed on the public holiday next week.
Christmas is considered a public holiday in many countries around the world.
She planned her trip to avoid the crowds during the busy public holiday period.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bank holiday - an official public holiday in the UK
