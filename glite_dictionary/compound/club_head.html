club head

1. NOUN

/ ˈklʌb ˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈklʌb ˌhɛd / (BrE)
the part of a golf club that strikes the ball
head of a golf club

Examples

The new driver has a larger club head for greater distance and accuracy.
Over time, dirt and grass can accumulate on your club head, affecting your shot.
He accidentally struck the ground with his club head during the swing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clubface - the striking surface on the head of a golf club
