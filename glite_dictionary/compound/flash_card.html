flash card

1. NOUN

/ flæʃ kɑrd / (AmE), / flæʃ kɑːd / (BrE)
a small card with words, numbers, or pictures used as a teaching aid, shown quickly to help learning or memorization
card used for teaching or memorization

Examples

The teacher held up a flash card with the letter 'A' for the children to see.
Medical students often use flash cards to memorize vocabulary and facts.
Each flash card in the set features a different animal picture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cue card - a card with prompts for a speaker
