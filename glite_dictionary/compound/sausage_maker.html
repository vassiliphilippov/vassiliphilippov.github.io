sausage maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔsɪdʒ ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsɪdʒ ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a device, often a small hand press, used for stuffing sausage meat into a casing
device for stuffing sausage meat

Examples

My grandmother still uses a vintage sausage maker to prepare homemade sausages.
We bought an electric sausage maker for the kitchen to speed up the process.
The old-fashioned sausage maker takes some effort to operate, but produces delicious results.
