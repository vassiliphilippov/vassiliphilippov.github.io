Anglo-Saxon deity

1. NOUN

/ ˌæŋɡloʊ ˈsæksən ˈdiːəti / (AmE), / ˌæŋɡləʊ ˈsæksən ˈdiːɪti / (BrE)
a god or goddess worshipped by the Anglo-Saxons in their religious beliefs and mythology
a god worshipped by the Anglo-Saxons

Examples

Many ancient rituals were performed in honor of an Anglo-Saxon deity believed to control the harvest.
The mythology course covered the characteristics of each Anglo-Saxon deity in detail.
Few written records survive describing how the Anglo-Saxon deity Woden was worshipped.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deity - a god or goddess in a religion
