military policeman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈpoʊlɪsˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmɪl.ɪ.tər.i ˈpəʊ.lɪs.mæn / (BrE)
a male member of the military police responsible for enforcing discipline among soldiers and guarding prisoners
male member of military police

Examples

The military policeman checked every vehicle at the base entrance.
As a military policeman, he was responsible for enforcing discipline among troops.
A military policeman escorted the prisoners to the detention facility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military police - the police organization of a military force
  • Degree Lower: policeman - a male police officer
