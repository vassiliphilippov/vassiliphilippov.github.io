phone zombie

1. NOUN

/ foʊn ˈzɑmbi / (AmE), / fəʊn ˈzɒmbi / (BrE)
a person who is so absorbed in their phone that they are oblivious to their surroundings
person oblivious to surroundings due to phone use
informal

Examples

More and more commuters are turning into phone zombies on the morning train.
Try not to be a phone zombie when you're walking across the street.
During meetings, he acts like a total phone zombie and misses important points.

Synset Examples

She walked into traffic, a complete phone zombie.
Don't be a phone zombie during dinner!
He’s become a phone zombie, ignoring everything around him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zombie - a person regarded as lifeless or with no awareness

Etymology

Formed from 'phone' (short for 'telephone') and 'zombie' (figuratively, a person without awareness), reflecting modern slang adoption.
