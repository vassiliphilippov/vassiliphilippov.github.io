buckwheat cake

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌkˌwiːt keɪk / (AmE), / ˈbʌkwiːt keɪk / (BrE)
A type of pancake made with buckwheat flour, commonly served for breakfast.
pancake made with buckwheat flour

Examples

My grandmother always served freshly made buckwheat cakes with maple syrup for Sunday breakfast.
The restaurant is famous for its fluffy buckwheat cakes served with whipped butter.
He prefers buckwheat cake to regular pancakes because of its rich, nutty flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pancake - a thin, flat cake made from batter and cooked on a griddle or frying pan
