love wand

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌv ˌwɑnd / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ˌwɒnd / (BrE)
a metaphorical tool or quality that spreads love, happiness, or charm; something that attracts or enchants others
metaphorical object that spreads love or charm

Examples

With her love wand, she spread joy wherever she went.
He seemed to wave a love wand over the crowd, leaving everyone smiling.
She wished she had a love wand to make the party more cheerful.

Synset Examples

With her love wand, she spread joy wherever she went.
She waved her love wand and made everyone smile.
His charm was like a love wand that enchanted everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charm - the power of pleasing or attracting others
  • Synonym: magic wand - a literal or figurative rod used to perform magic or cause change
