Nentsy language

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛntsi ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈnɛntsi ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Uralic language spoken by the Nentsy people of northern Siberia
Uralic language of the Nentsy people

Examples

Linguists have been studying the Nentsy language to better understand Uralic language development.
There are educational programs aiming to revive the Nentsy language among younger generations.
She wrote her thesis on the grammar and phonology of the Nentsy language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nenets - the Nentsy people; indigenous group of northern Siberia

Etymology

Nentsy, the self-designation of the indigenous Samoyed people of Siberia, plus 'language'.
