fascist propaganda

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃɪst prəˌɡæn də / (AmE), / ˈfæʃɪst prəˈɡændə / (BrE)
information or material disseminated to promote fascist ideology or further the goals of a fascist movement
information promoting fascism

Examples

The regime relied on fascist propaganda to manipulate public opinion.
Many schools banned fascist propaganda materials from being distributed to students.
She studied the techniques used in fascist propaganda during the 1930s.

Synset Examples

She criticized the fascist propaganda circulating online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: propaganda - information, especially of a biased or misleading nature, used to promote a political cause
