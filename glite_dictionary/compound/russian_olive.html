Russian olive

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌʃən ˈɑːlɪv / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃ(ə)n ˈɒlɪv / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe and western Asia, with gray leaves and small yellow fruits covered in silvery scales; sometimes spiny.
deciduous shrub or tree with gray leaves and yellow fruits

Examples

A row of Russian olive trees lined the back of the property, offering a silvery-green border.
The Russian olive is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in poor soil.
In some regions, the Russian olive has become invasive, crowding out native species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oleaster - a shrub or small tree of the genus Elaeagnus, commonly called oleaster or Russian olive
