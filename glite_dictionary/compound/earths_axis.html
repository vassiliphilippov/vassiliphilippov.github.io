Earth's axis

1. NOUN

/ ɝːθs ˈæksɪs / (AmE), / ɜːθs ˈæksɪs / (BrE)
an imaginary line about which the Earth rotates, passing through the North and South Poles
the imaginary rotation line of the Earth

Examples

The tilt of the Earth's axis causes the changing seasons throughout the year.
Scientists study the movement of the Earth's axis to understand climate changes over centuries.
If the Earth's axis were not tilted, we would not experience significant seasonal variations.

Synset Examples

The Earth's axis affects the seasons significantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: axis - an imaginary line about which a body rotates
