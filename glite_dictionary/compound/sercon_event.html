sercon event

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɚ ˌkɑn ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / ˈsəː ˌkɒn ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
A convention or conference that is serious in tone, often focusing on academic or literary discussions rather than social or entertainment activities.
serious convention or conference
slang

Examples

The sercon event featured lectures by notable science fiction authors and scholars.
Unlike previous gatherings, this year's sercon event emphasized critical analysis over casual discussion.
Attendees appreciated the focused atmosphere of the sercon event where ideas took center stage.

Synset Examples

The sercon event attracted experts from various fields.

Etymology

Blend of 'serious' and 'convention'; fandom slang dating from mid-20th century.
