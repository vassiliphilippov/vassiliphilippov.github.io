to change someone's life

1. VERB

/ tʃeɪndʒ sʌmwʌnz laɪf / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ sʌmwʌnz laɪf / (BrE)
to have a profound and lasting effect on someone's circumstances, attitudes, or future
profoundly affect someone's future or circumstances

Examples

Winning the scholarship would change her life and open up many new opportunities.
The inspiring teacher truly changed his life for the better.
That trip abroad changed my life in ways I never expected.

Synset Examples

Finding buried treasure would change their lives forever.
Changing your attitude can change your life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to transform - to change completely in form or appearance
  • Antonym: ruin someone's life - to cause someone's life to become much worse
