hard fern

1. NOUN

/ hɑrd fɝn / (AmE), / hɑːd fɜːn / (BrE)
a fern of the genus Blechnum, especially Blechnum spicant, having tough, leathery fronds and commonly found in woodland habitats
a fern of the genus Blechnum

Examples

The hard fern thrives in damp woodlands and shaded areas.
Botanists can identify hard fern by its stiff, dark green leaves.
Gardeners value hard fern as an ornamental plant for shady gardens.
