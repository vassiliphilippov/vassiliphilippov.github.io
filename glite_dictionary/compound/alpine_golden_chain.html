Alpine golden chain

1. NOUN

/ ˈælpaɪn ˈɡoʊldən tʃeɪn / (AmE), / ˈælpaɪn ˈɡəʊldən tʃeɪn / (BrE)
an ornamental shrub or small tree (Laburnum alpinum) with hanging clusters of yellow flowers, commonly grown in gardens
ornamental shrub with yellow flowers (Laburnum)

Examples

The Alpine golden chain produces magnificent yellow flowers every spring.
Gardeners admire the Alpine golden chain for its vibrant, pendulous blossoms.
We planted an Alpine golden chain near the entrance for its attractive foliage and blooms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laburnum - a genus of small trees with hanging yellow flowers
