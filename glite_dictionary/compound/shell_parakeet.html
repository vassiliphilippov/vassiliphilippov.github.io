shell parakeet

1. NOUN

/ ʃɛl ˈpɛr.əˌkit / (AmE), / ʃɛl ˈpar.əˌkiːt / (BrE)
a small Australian parakeet, typically light green with black and yellow markings in the wild, but bred in many colors as a pet
an Australian parakeet; common pet bird

Examples

Many families keep a shell parakeet as a cheerful and colorful pet.
In the wild, the shell parakeet is usually bright green with black and yellow spots.
She breeds shell parakeets in a variety of beautiful colors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: budgerigar - a small Australian parakeet, also known as the common pet parakeet
