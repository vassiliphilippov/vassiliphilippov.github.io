seasoned quarterback

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːzənd ˈkwɔːrtərˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈsiːzənd ˈkwɔːtəbæk / (BrE)
an experienced and skilled football player who plays the quarterback position
experienced football quarterback

Examples

The team relied on their seasoned quarterback to stay calm under pressure.
A seasoned quarterback can read defenses quickly and make smart decisions.
Despite his injuries, the seasoned quarterback led his team to victory.

Synset Examples
