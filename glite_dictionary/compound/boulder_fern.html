boulder fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊldər fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈbəʊldə fɜːn / (BrE)
a species of fern native to eastern North America, characterized by pale green fronds and a scent similar to hay
North American fern with pale fronds and hay-like aroma

Examples

We came across a patch of boulder fern while hiking in the Appalachian Mountains.
The pale green fronds of the boulder fern stood out against the forest floor.
Botanists appreciate the distinctive hay-like aroma of the boulder fern during field research.
