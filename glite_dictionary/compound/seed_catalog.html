seed catalog

1. NOUN

/ sid ˈkæt̬.əˌlɑɡ / (AmE), / siːd ˈkæt.əlɒɡ / (BrE)
a booklet or list that advertises seeds for sale, usually including descriptions and prices
booklet listing seeds for sale

Examples

I ordered several tomato varieties from the seed catalog this spring.
The seed catalog offers detailed descriptions and planting tips for each type of flower.
She spent hours browsing through the colorful seed catalog before making her selection.
