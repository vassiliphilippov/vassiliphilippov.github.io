placement center

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪsmənt ˌsɛntɚ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪsmənt ˌsɛntə / (BrE)
an office or agency that helps people find suitable employment by matching applicants with available job opportunities
office that finds employment for applicants

Examples

The university's placement center assisted hundreds of graduates in finding their first jobs.
After losing his job, he visited the local placement center for career guidance and support.
Many companies partner with the placement center to recruit talented candidates for open positions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: employment agency - a business that finds jobs for people seeking employment
