takeover attempt

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪkˌoʊvɚ əˈtɛmpt / (AmE), / ˈteɪkˌəʊvə əˈtɛmpt / (BrE)
an effort made to gain control of a company, especially by buying enough shares to do so
an effort to gain control of a company

Examples

The company's board rejected the takeover attempt by a larger rival.
Investors reacted nervously to news of a hostile takeover attempt.
After several unsuccessful takeover attempts, the firm remained independent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: takeover - the act of gaining control of a company by buying its shares
