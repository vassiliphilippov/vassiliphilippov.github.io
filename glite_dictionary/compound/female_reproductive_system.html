female reproductive system

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːmeɪl ɹɪˌproʊˈdʌktɪv ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈfiːmeɪl rɪˌprəʊˈdʌktɪv ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
the system of organs and structures in female animals or humans responsible for producing eggs and supporting reproduction, including the ovaries, fallopian tubes, uterus, and vagina.
organs involved in female reproduction
medical

Examples

The female reproductive system includes the uterus, ovaries, and other related organs.
Hormonal changes can greatly affect the female reproductive system.
Doctors study the female reproductive system to better understand fertility and pregnancy.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: male reproductive system - the system of organs in males for reproducing offspring
