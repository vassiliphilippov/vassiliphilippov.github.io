food web

1. NOUN

/ fuːd wɛb / (AmE), / fuːd wɛb / (BrE)
A complex system of interconnected food chains in a community of organisms, showing how different species are related through what they eat.
interconnected system of food chains

Examples

Scientists study the food web to understand how energy flows through an ecosystem.
If one species disappears from a food web, it can affect many others in the community.
Pollution can disrupt the natural balance of a food web, harming multiple organisms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: food chain - a linear sequence of organisms in which each is eaten by the next member
