cardiovascular system

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːrdioʊˈvæskjələr ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌkɑːdiəʊˈvæskjʊlə ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
the system of organs and tissues, including the heart and blood vessels, responsible for circulating blood and lymph throughout the body
system of heart and blood vessels
medical

Examples

Regular exercise helps strengthen the cardiovascular system and improve overall health.
Doctors study the cardiovascular system to better understand heart disease.
Smoking can have a negative impact on the cardiovascular system, leading to serious health issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circulatory system - system responsible for circulation of blood and lymph

Etymology

From Latin 'cardia' (heart) + 'vasculum' (small vessel) + 'systema' (system).
