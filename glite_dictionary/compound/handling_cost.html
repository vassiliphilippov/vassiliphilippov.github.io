handling cost

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændlɪŋ kɑst / (AmE), / ˈhændlɪŋ kɒst / (BrE)
the amount charged or spent for the process of preparing, packaging, and delivering goods, especially when fulfilling an order
charge for preparing and shipping goods

Examples

The company adds a handling cost to each online order to cover packaging and labor fees.
Our invoice shows a separate line for handling cost aside from the shipping charges.
Many customers are unaware that the handling cost includes more than just postage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shipping cost - the expense of transporting goods
  • Degree Lower: packaging cost - the cost associated with packaging goods for sale or delivery
