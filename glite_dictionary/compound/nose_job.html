nose job

1. NOUN

/ noʊz dʒɑb / (AmE), / nəʊz dʒɒb / (BrE)
An operation to change the shape or appearance of the nose for cosmetic reasons.
cosmetic surgery on the nose

Examples

She decided to get a nose job before her graduation ceremony.
His nose job made a noticeable difference in his appearance.
There is still some swelling after her recent nose job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rhinoplasty - plastic surgery to change the nose
