gasoline pump

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæsəliːn pʌmp / (AmE), / ˈɡæsəliːn pʌmp / (BrE)
A machine at a service station used for dispensing gasoline into vehicles.
machine for dispensing gasoline

Examples

She pulled up to the gasoline pump to refill her car's tank.
The gasoline pump was out of order, so they had to find another station.
Always turn off your engine before using the gasoline pump.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petrol pump - machine for dispensing petrol into vehicles (British English)
