measuring device

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛʒɚɪŋ dɪˌvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒərɪŋ dɪˌvaɪs / (BrE)
An instrument used to determine the size, amount, or degree of something.
instrument for measuring quantities

Examples

The laboratory was equipped with every kind of measuring device needed for the experiment.
Before starting the project, make sure you have a reliable measuring device to record the temperature.
A ruler is the simplest form of a measuring device used in schools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gauge - an instrument for measuring or testing
  • Synonym: meter - a device for measuring and displaying a quantity
