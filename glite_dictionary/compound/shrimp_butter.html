shrimp butter

1. NOUN

/ ʃrɪmp ˈbʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ʃrɪmp ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
Butter blended with chopped shrimp or flavored with an extract made from shrimp shells, used as a spread or in cooking.
butter blended or flavored with shrimp

Examples

He spread some shrimp butter on warm toast as an appetizer.
The chef prepared a delicate sauce using homemade shrimp butter.
You can elevate your seafood dishes by adding a dollop of shrimp butter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anchovy butter - butter blended with anchovies or anchovy essence
