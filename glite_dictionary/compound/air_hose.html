air hose

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr hoʊz / (AmE), / ˈeə həʊz / (BrE)
A flexible tube used to convey air under pressure, typically for inflating tires or operating pneumatic tools.
flexible tube for pressurized air

Examples

He connected the air hose to the compressor before inflating the car tire.
The mechanic replaced the damaged air hose on the pneumatic drill.
Always check the air hose for leaks before using it in the workshop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hose - a flexible tube conveying fluids or air
