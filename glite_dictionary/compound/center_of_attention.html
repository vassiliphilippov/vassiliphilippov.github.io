center of attention

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛntɚ əv əˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɛntə əv əˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
a person or thing that is receiving the most interest and attention from others in a particular situation
person or thing receiving most attention

Examples

Ever since she arrived at the event, Maria was the center of attention among all her friends.
The magician's amazing tricks quickly made him the center of attention at the party.
Wearing the brightest costume, he became the center of attention on stage.

Synset Examples

his stories made him the center of the party

Related Terms

  • Synonym: focus of attention - person or thing that is the main focus; synonym for 'center of attention'
