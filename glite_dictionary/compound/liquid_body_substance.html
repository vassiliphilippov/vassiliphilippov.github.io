liquid body substance

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪkwɪd ˈbɑdi ˈsʌbstəns / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd ˈbɒdi ˈsʌbstəns / (BrE)
any of the various fluids found in a living body, such as blood, lymph, or saliva.
fluid found in the body
medical

Examples

During the medical examination, the doctor collected samples of several liquid body substances for testing.
Blood is one of the most important liquid body substances circulating in our bodies.
The presence of infection can often be detected through changes in liquid body substances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: body fluid - any liquid found within the human body
