paramilitary organisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌpærəˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈmɪlɪtəri ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a group of civilians organized in military style, often operating in place of or in support of a regular army
civilian group organized like military

Examples

The government accused the rebel paramilitary organisation of orchestrating the attack.
Members of the paramilitary organisation received training similar to that of professional soldiers.
Several countries have relied on paramilitary organisations to support their armed forces during conflicts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: militia - a military force raised from the civilian population

Etymology

From 'para-' (beside) + 'military', meaning 'beside the military', and 'organisation', from French 'organisation'.
