white picket fence

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈpɪkət fɛns / (AmE), / waɪt ˈpɪkɪt fɛns / (BrE)
A symbol of the idealized suburban lifestyle, representing comfort, security, and traditional family values.
symbolic of an ideal suburban life

Examples

The couple saved money for years to buy a house with a white picket fence.
The novel explores what lies beneath the surface of the white picket fence lifestyle.
Many people equate happiness with a steady job and a white picket fence.

Synset Examples
