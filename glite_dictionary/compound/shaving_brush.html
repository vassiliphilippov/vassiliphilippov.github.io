shaving brush

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃeɪvɪŋ brʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪvɪŋ brʌʃ / (BrE)
A brush with a handle, used to apply shaving cream or soap to the face before shaving.
brush for applying shaving cream

Examples

He swirled his shaving brush in the soap before lathering his face.
A good shaving brush helps create a rich foam for a smoother shave.
The set included a razor, a shaving brush, and a bowl.
