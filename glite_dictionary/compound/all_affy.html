all affy

1. ADJ

/ ɔl ˈæfi / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈæfi / (BrE)
Very happy or pleased; in a state of great happiness.
very happy or pleased

Examples

When the results came in, she was all affy and couldn't stop smiling.
The kids seemed all affy after opening their presents on Christmas morning.
He felt all affy about his promotion at work.

Synset Examples

They were all affy when they heard the good news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delighted - feeling or showing great pleasure
  • Antonym: miserable - feeling very unhappy
