corn stalk

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn stɑk / (AmE), / kɔːn stɔːk / (BrE)
the stem or main support of a corn plant
stem of a corn plant

Examples

After the harvest, the corn stalk is often left standing in the field.
The children played hide-and-seek among the tall corn stalks in late summer.
Farmers sometimes use dried corn stalks for animal bedding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stalk - the stem or main axis of a plant
