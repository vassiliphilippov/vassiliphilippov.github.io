pink-tipped

1. ADJ

/ pɪŋk tɪpt / (AmE), / pɪŋk tɪpt / (BrE)
Having the end or tip colored pink, especially used to describe leaves, petals, or other parts of plants.
having a pink-colored tip

Examples

The pink-tipped leaves of the plant added a delicate touch to the garden.
She admired the pink-tipped petals that stood out among the white flowers.
The bush produces pink-tipped buds in early spring, making it especially attractive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pink-edged - having a pink edge or border
  • Degree Lower: yellow-tipped - having a yellow tip
