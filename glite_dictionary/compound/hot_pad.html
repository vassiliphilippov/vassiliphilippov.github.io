hot pad

1. NOUN

/ hɑt pæd / (AmE), / hɒt pad / (BrE)
a flexible pad containing electric heating elements, used to apply heat for warmth or pain relief
electric heating pad for warmth or pain relief

Examples

She placed the hot pad on her sore back to relieve the pain.
The doctor recommended using a hot pad to help with muscle relaxation.
During winter, a hot pad can be comforting for cold hands and feet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heating pad - an electric pad for applying heat to the body

2. NOUN

/ hɑt pæd / (AmE), / hɒt pad / (BrE)
A small pad placed under a hot dish to protect a surface such as a table or countertop from heat damage.
protective pad for hot dishes

Examples

Always place a hot pad under the casserole dish to prevent damaging the table.
She grabbed a hot pad before moving the steaming pot from the stove.
We keep a drawer full of hot pads for family dinners and gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trivet - a stand or support placed under hot dishes to protect surfaces
  • Synonym: pot holder - a thick cloth pad used for handling hot cookware
