good look

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd lʊk / (AmE), / ɡʊd lʊk / (BrE)
an appearance, behavior, or action that is seen as attractive or favorable, often in social or style contexts
attractive or favorable appearance
informal

Examples

Wearing that jacket with those shoes is definitely a good look.
Letting your team take the credit was a good look for you.
Speaking kindly to the staff is always a good look.

Synset Examples

Stop being so loserly; it’s not a good look.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bad look - an unattractive or unfavorable appearance, behavior, or action
