the Alps

1. PROPN

/ ði ˈælps / (AmE), / ði ˈælps / (BrE)
a vast mountain range in south-central Europe, known for its scenic landscapes and popularity as a destination for winter sports and tourism
major European mountain range

Examples

Many tourists visit the Alps each year to enjoy skiing and hiking.
The breathtaking views of the Alps attract photographers from around the world.
She spent her holiday trekking through the Alps with her friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the Himalayas - a major mountain range in Asia

Etymology

From Latin Alpes, referring to the mountain range.
