athletic facility

1. NOUN

/ əˈθlɛtɪk fəˈsɪlɪti / (AmE), / əˈθlɛtɪk fəˈsɪlɪti / (BrE)
a building or area equipped for sports and physical exercise, such as a gymnasium, stadium, or sports complex
a place for sports or exercise

Examples

The university built a new athletic facility to support its sports teams and students.
The city council approved funding for renovations to the old athletic facility.
Students use the school's athletic facility for basketball, volleyball, and physical education classes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stadium - a large facility for outdoor sporting events
  • Synonym: gymnasium - a building equipped for indoor sports and exercise
