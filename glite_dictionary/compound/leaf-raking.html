leaf-raking

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːf ˌreɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈliːf ˌreɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of gathering or collecting fallen leaves, typically with a rake.
gathering fallen leaves

Examples

The children helped with the leaf-raking after school.
Autumn means a lot of leaf-raking in our backyard.
He injured his back while leaf-raking last weekend.

Synset Examples

We spent the afternoon leaf-raking in the yard.
After the storm, leaf-raking became our weekend chore.
