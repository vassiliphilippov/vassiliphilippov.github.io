line of thought

1. NOUN

/ laɪn əv θɔt / (AmE), / laɪn əv θɔːt / (BrE)
A particular way of thinking or reasoning, characteristic of an individual or group.
a way of thinking or reasoning

Examples

She followed his line of thought carefully during the discussion.
This theory represents a different line of thought on the subject.
He lost his line of thought when the phone rang.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: train of thought - a sequence of connected ideas or thoughts
