southwest by west

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaʊθˌwɛst baɪ ˈwɛst / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˌwɛst baɪ ˈwɛst / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point west of southwest, corresponding to 236°15′ on a compass.
compass point slightly west of southwest

Examples

The ship set its course to southwest by west, avoiding the cliffs.
Hikers were told to travel southwest by west until they reached the old oak tree.
On the map, their destination lay almost exactly southwest by west from the village.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: southwest - the compass point halfway between south and west
  • Degree Higher: west-southwest - the compass point one point further west than southwest by west
