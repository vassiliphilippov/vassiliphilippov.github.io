filter-tipped cigarette

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪltɚ tɪpt sɪgəˌrɛt / (AmE), / ˈfɪltə tɪpt sɪɡərɛt / (BrE)
A cigarette fitted with a filter tip designed to reduce the inhalation of tar and other substances.
cigarette with a filter tip

Examples

Since switching to filter-tipped cigarettes, he says he coughs much less in the morning.
The store only sells filter-tipped cigarettes, not the unfiltered kind.
Many smokers believe that filter-tipped cigarettes are less harmful than regular ones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filter cigarette - a cigarette with a filter tip
  • Antonym: plain cigarette - a cigarette without a filter tip
