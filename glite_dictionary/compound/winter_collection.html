winter collection

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪn.tɚ kəˈlɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə kəˈlɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
a range of clothing and accessories designed and released for sale during the winter season, typically by a fashion brand or designer
fashion line for winter season

Examples

The designer showcased her new winter collection at Paris Fashion Week to much acclaim.
Retail stores often introduce heavy coats and knits in their winter collection.
Have you seen this year's winter collection from your favorite brand?

Synset Examples
