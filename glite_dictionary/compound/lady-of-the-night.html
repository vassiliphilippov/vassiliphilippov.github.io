lady-of-the-night

1. NOUN

/ ˌleɪ.di əv ðə ˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ˌleɪ.di əv ðə ˈnaɪt / (BrE)
a West Indian shrub (Brunfelsia americana) with fragrant, showy yellowish-white flowers, often blooming at night
West Indian shrub with fragrant flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with the sweet scent of lady-of-the-night in the evening air.
He planted a lady-of-the-night beside the porch to enjoy its nocturnal blooms.
At dusk, the lady-of-the-night opened its pale flowers, attracting moths and other pollinators.
