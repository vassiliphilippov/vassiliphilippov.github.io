purple-tinged

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːpəl tɪndʒd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl tɪndʒd / (BrE)
Having a slight or noticeable tint or hue of purple added to the main color.
tinged with purple

Examples

The sky took on a purple-tinged glow as the sun set behind the mountains.
She admired the purple-tinged petals of the rare flower in the garden.
His bruised arm showed a purple-tinged mark after the accident.
