pendant earring

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛn.dənt ˈɪr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.dənt ˈɪə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
An earring that features a hanging or dangling ornament attached to it.
earring with a dangling ornament

Examples

She wore sparkling pendant earrings to complement her elegant evening dress.
The jeweler displayed a collection of gold pendant earrings in the shop window.
Long pendant earrings can add drama to a simple outfit.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stud earring - a small earring that sits directly on the earlobe without dangling
