electrical outlet

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl ˈaʊtlɛt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl ˈaʊtlət / (BrE)
A socket in a wall or other surface into which an electrical plug can be inserted to connect to the power supply.
socket for electrical plugs

Examples

Be sure to plug your phone charger into a electrical outlet that is not overloaded.
There is an electrical outlet behind the couch if you need to use your laptop.
Modern kitchens often have a electrical outlet near every countertop for appliances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power outlet - another term for electrical outlet
  • Synonym: electrical socket - another term for electrical outlet
  • Antonym: plug - device inserted into an electrical outlet to make a connection
