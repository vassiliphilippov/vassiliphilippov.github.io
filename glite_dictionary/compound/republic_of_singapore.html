Republic of Singapore

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌsɪŋəˈpɔr / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌsɪŋəˈpɔː / (BrE)
the official name of Singapore, a sovereign country in Southeast Asia consisting of Singapore Island and some smaller islets, independent since 1965
sovereign country in Southeast Asia

Examples

The Republic of Singapore is known for its modern economy and strict laws.
She was born in the Republic of Singapore and moved abroad as a child.
Diplomatic relations between the Republic of Singapore and its neighbors have been largely cooperative.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Singapore - a city-state in Southeast Asia; the island as a country
