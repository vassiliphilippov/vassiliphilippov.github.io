Marathi language

1. NOUN

/ məˈrɑːti ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / məˈrɑːti ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an Indic language spoken primarily in the Indian state of Maharashtra and written in the Devanagari script
state language of Maharashtra, India

Examples

The Marathi language is spoken by over 80 million people across India.
She learned to read and write in the Marathi language at school.
Many classic poems are written in the Marathi language and are studied by students in Maharashtra.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hindi language - an Indo-Aryan language also written in Devanagari script
  • Synonym: Gujarati language - another major western Indian language

Etymology

From Marathi 'Marāṭhī', from Maharashtrian Prakrit.
