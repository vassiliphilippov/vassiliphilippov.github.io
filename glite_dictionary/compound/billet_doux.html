billet doux

1. NOUN

/ biˌleɪ ˈdu, ˌbɪlɪt ˈdu / (AmE), / ˌbɪleɪ ˈduː, ˌbiːlɪ ˈduː / (BrE)
a personal letter written to a loved one expressing affection or romantic feelings
love letter; affectionate note

Examples

She smiled as she read the billet doux that had been left on her pillow.
In the old drawer, I discovered a faded billet doux addressed to my grandmother.
He wrote her a heartfelt billet doux before leaving for his trip abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: love letter - a written message expressing romantic love

Etymology

From French 'billet doux', literally 'sweet note'.
