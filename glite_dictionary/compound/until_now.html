until now

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈtɪl naʊ / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɪl naʊ / (BrE)
used to indicate that a situation has continued up to the present time, often in negative statements; so far or up to this point.
up to the present time

Examples

Until now, we have not received any updates about the project.
No one knew about the discovery until now.
She hadn't realized the mistake until now.

Synset Examples

So far he hasn't called
the sun isn't up yet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: so far - up to the present moment or point
  • Synonym: yet - up to now or until now (in negative or interrogative contexts)
