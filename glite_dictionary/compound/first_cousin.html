first cousin

1. NOUN

/ fɝst ˈkʌzən / (AmE), / fɜːst ˈkʌzən / (BrE)
the child of your aunt or uncle; a cousin with whom you share a set of grandparents.
child of your aunt or uncle

Examples

My first cousin lives in Canada, and we visit each other every summer.
She is close to her first cousin because they grew up together.
At the family reunion, I met my first cousin for the first time.
