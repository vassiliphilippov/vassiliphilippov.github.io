self-defense skill

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf dɪˈfɛns skɪl / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf dɪˈfɛns skɪl / (BrE)
the ability to protect oneself from physical harm or attack, often by using martial arts or defensive techniques
ability to protect oneself from harm

Examples

After taking several classes, her self-defense skill improved noticeably.
Learning a self-defense skill can boost your confidence and personal safety.
Many schools now offer self-defense skill workshops for students.

Synset Examples

He practiced jujitsu to improve his self-defense skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: martial art - various systems of training for combat and self-defense
  • Synonym: self-protection - the act of keeping oneself safe from harm or danger
