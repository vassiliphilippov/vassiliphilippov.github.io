moo goo gai pan

1. NOUN

/ mu ɡu ɡaɪ pæn / (AmE), / muː ɡuː ɡaɪ pæn / (BrE)
A Cantonese dish consisting of chicken and sautéed vegetables, often in a light sauce.
Cantonese chicken and vegetable dish

Examples

Have you ever tried moo goo gai pan at the new Chinese restaurant downtown?
My favorite takeout order is moo goo gai pan because it's light and flavorful.
She cooked moo goo gai pan for dinner using fresh mushrooms and chicken breast.

Etymology

Cantonese origins: from Cantonese characters for 'mushroom, chicken slices.'
