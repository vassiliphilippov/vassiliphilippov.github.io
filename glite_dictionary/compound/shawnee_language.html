Shawnee language

1. NOUN

/ ʃɔːˈniː ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ʃɔːˈniː ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Algonquian language traditionally spoken by the Shawnee people of North America
language of the Shawnee people

Examples

There are several revitalization efforts to preserve the Shawnee language among tribal members.
She is fluent in both English and the Shawnee language, which she teaches at local schools.
The dictionary includes translations from English into the Shawnee language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Algonquian language - a family of Native American languages that includes Shawnee
  • Antonym: Cherokee language - another Native American language distinct from Shawnee

Etymology

Shawnee, from the people’s self-designation, combined with 'language', from Latin 'lingua' (tongue, language).
