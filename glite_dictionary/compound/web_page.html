web page

1. NOUN

/ wɛb peɪdʒ / (AmE), / wɛb peɪdʒ / (BrE)
a document available on the World Wide Web, usually consisting of text, images, and links, viewable in a web browser
internet document viewable in a browser

Examples

Please visit our web page for more information about our services.
She spent the afternoon designing a web page for her photography portfolio.
Every web page should be easy to navigate and quick to load.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: website - a collection of related web pages under a single domain

Etymology

Formed from 'web' (short for World Wide Web) and 'page' (in the sense of a document or sheet).
