secretarial school

1. NOUN

/ ˌsiːkrəˈtɛriəl skuːl / (AmE), / ˌsiːkrəˈtɜːriəl skuːl / (BrE)
a school where students are taught secretarial skills such as typing, shorthand, and filing
school teaching secretarial skills

Examples

After graduating from secretarial school, she quickly found a job as an administrative assistant.
Many companies used to require job applicants to have a secretarial school diploma.
He learned typing and office management during his year at a secretarial school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: business school - a school teaching business-related subjects
