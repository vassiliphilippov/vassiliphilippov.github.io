travel plan

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəl plæn / (AmE), / ˈtræv.əl plæn / (BrE)
a detailed proposal or schedule outlining a route or itinerary for a journey
proposed route or itinerary for a journey

Examples

Before leaving for Europe, I made a detailed travel plan to visit five countries.
The company requires all employees to submit their travel plan for approval prior to business trips.
Unexpected weather forced them to change their travel plan at the last minute.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: itinerary - a planned route or journey
