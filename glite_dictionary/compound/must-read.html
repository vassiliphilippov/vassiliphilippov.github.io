must-read

1. NOUN

/ mʌst ˈrid / (AmE), / mʌst ˈriːd / (BrE)
a book, article, or publication considered essential or highly recommended to read
highly recommended reading

Examples

Her latest novel is a must-read for anyone interested in historical fiction.
This article is a must-read for all new employees joining the company.
Critics agree that the book is a must-read classic in modern literature.

Synset Examples

This book is, for my money, a must-read.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: page-turner - a book that is very exciting and engaging, making you want to keep reading
