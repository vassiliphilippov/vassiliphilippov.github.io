ebony spleenwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛbəni ˈspliːnwɝt / (AmE), / ˈɛbəni ˈspliːnwɜːt / (BrE)
A common North American fern with shiny black stalks and delicate green leaves, often found in rocky woodlands.
North American fern with black stalks

Examples

The ebony spleenwort grows well in shaded, rocky gardens with good drainage.
She recognized the delicate fronds and polished black stems as those of ebony spleenwort.
Nature enthusiasts often search for ebony spleenwort in forested limestone outcrops.
