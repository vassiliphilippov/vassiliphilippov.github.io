girl math

1. NOUN

/ ɡɝl mæθ / (AmE), / ɡɜːl mæθ / (BrE)
a humorous way of explaining or justifying personal spending habits, often by using logic that does not make conventional financial sense
humorous spending justification
informal slang

Examples

After a big shopping trip, she used girl math to tell herself she had actually saved money.
According to girl math, buying something with cash means it was basically free.
He laughed when his friend explained airline points using girl math to justify another vacation.

Synset Examples

Using girl math, she calculated how much she saved.
Girl math says that if it's on sale, it's practically free.
She used girl math to justify buying those shoes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boy math - a humorous counterpart phrase referring to rationalizations typically attributed to men
