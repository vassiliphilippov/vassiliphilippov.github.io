healthy mind and body

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθi ˌmaɪnd ənd ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθi ˌmaɪnd ənd ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
a state in which both mental and physical health are good and well-balanced
state of mental and physical well-being

Examples

Maintaining a healthy mind and body requires a balanced diet and regular exercise.
Meditation can help you achieve a healthy mind and body by reducing stress.
Schools should promote the importance of a healthy mind and body among students.

Synset Examples

Regular exercise contributes to a healthy mind and body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: well-being - a state of being comfortable, healthy, or happy
