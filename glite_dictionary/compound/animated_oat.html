animated oat

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɪˌmeɪtɪd oʊt / (AmE), / ˈænɪˌmeɪtɪd əʊt / (BrE)
a Mediterranean species of wild oat, considered to be an ancestor of the modern cultivated oat
wild Mediterranean oat species

Examples

The fields were full of animated oat, swaying in the Mediterranean breeze.
Researchers identified animated oat as a key ancestor of cultivated oats.
You can often spot animated oat growing wild along the hillsides of southern Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wild oat - any wild-growing species of oat grass
