symbol of peace

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪmbəl əv pis / (AmE), / ˈsɪmbəl əv piːs / (BrE)
an object, image, or figure recognized as representing the idea of peace
emblem representing peace

Examples

The olive branch has long been used as a symbol of peace in many cultures.
After the war, the monument became a symbol of peace for the entire nation.
The white dove is universally recognized as a symbol of peace.

Synset Examples

Hiroshima remains a symbol of peace and resilience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emblem - a thing serving as a symbolic representation of a quality or concept
  • Synonym: dove - a bird commonly used as a symbol of peace
