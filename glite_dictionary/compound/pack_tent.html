pack tent

1. NOUN

/ pæk tɛnt / (AmE), / pæk tɛnt / (BrE)
a lightweight tent designed to be easily carried in a backpack, often used for camping or hiking
a portable, lightweight tent for hiking or camping

Examples

We brought a pack tent and some supplies for our hiking trip.
The pack tent was easy to carry and quick to set up at the campsite.
She prefers using a pack tent when backpacking through the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backpacking tent - a very lightweight tent intended for backpackers
