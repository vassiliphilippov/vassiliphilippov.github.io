sunray lamp

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌreɪ læmp / (AmE), / ˈsʌnreɪ læmp / (BrE)
a mercury-vapor lamp that emits ultraviolet light, used in medical or cosmetic treatments to imitate sunlight
ultraviolet lamp for medical or cosmetic uses
medical

Examples

The dermatologist recommended short sessions under a sunray lamp to treat the skin condition.
They purchased a sunray lamp for the clinic to assist with certain therapeutic procedures.
Excessive exposure to a sunray lamp can be harmful without proper supervision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ultraviolet lamp - a lamp that emits ultraviolet rays for medical or cosmetic purposes
