direct marketing

1. NOUN

/ dəˈrɛkt ˈmɑrkətɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛkt ˈmɑːkɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a method of marketing in which promotional messages or offers are sent directly to individual potential customers, rather than through mass media
marketing aimed directly at individuals

Examples

Many companies use direct marketing techniques like email campaigns and targeted mailings to reach new customers.
The success of direct marketing depends on understanding the preferences of individual consumers.
He works in direct marketing, designing personalized offers for potential clients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telemarketing - the marketing of goods or services by telephone
  • Antonym: mass marketing - marketing aimed at a large, undifferentiated group of people
