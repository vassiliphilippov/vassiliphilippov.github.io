street broom

1. NOUN

/ strit ˈbruːm / (AmE), / striːt ˈbruːm / (BrE)
a large broom designed for sweeping streets or other outdoor public areas
large broom for street cleaning

Examples

The city worker used a street broom to clear the sidewalks after the parade.
Every morning, she sweeps the market area with a sturdy street broom.
Old-fashioned street brooms with wooden handles are still used in some towns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broom - a brush with a long handle for sweeping
