at rest

1. ADJ

/ æt rɛst / (AmE), / æt rɛst / (BrE)
in a state of quiet, stillness, or sleep; not active or moving
in a state of stillness or sleep

Examples

The child was finally at rest after a long day at the park.
She looks so peaceful at rest, you can hardly tell she was ever stressed.
Machines that are at rest consume less energy than when they are running.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asleep - in the state of sleep
  • Antonym: awake - not sleeping; conscious and alert

2. ADJ

/ ət rɛst / (AmE), / ət rɛst / (BrE)
in a state of quiet, peace, or calm; not moving or disturbed
in a state of calm or stillness

Examples

After finishing all his work, he finally felt at rest.
The house seemed at rest once everyone had gone to bed.
She could not be at rest until she heard from her family.

Synset Examples

His mind was at rest after the long meditation.
The statue is finally at rest in the garden.
The storm has passed, and the sea is at rest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at ease - free from worry or discomfort
  • Synonym: at peace - in a state of tranquility and lack of conflict
  • Antonym: agitated - feeling or appearing troubled or nervous
