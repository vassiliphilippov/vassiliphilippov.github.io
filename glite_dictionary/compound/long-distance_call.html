long-distance call

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋ ˈdɪstəns kɔl / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈdɪstəns kɔːl / (BrE)
a telephone call made to a location outside the caller's local area, usually incurring extra charges
telephone call outside local area

Examples

My aunt used to save money by making long-distance calls only on weekends.
He received a surprise long-distance call from his friend in another state.
Back when I was a child, a long-distance call was considered something special.

Synset Examples

I talked to her by long distance

Related Terms

  • Antonym: local call - a telephone call made within the caller's local area, typically at a lower cost
