increased visibility

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈkrist vɪzəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ɪnˈkriːst vɪzɪˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the state of being more publicly recognized or noticed; greater public awareness or prominence
greater public awareness or prominence

Examples

The new marketing campaign has resulted in increased visibility for the brand.
Advocacy groups have worked hard to achieve increased visibility for marginalized communities.
Sponsoring the local event gave the company increased visibility in the region.

Synset Examples

The rise of transmania has led to increased visibility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exposure - the state of having attention focused on one
  • Antonym: obscurity - the state of being unknown or not prominent
