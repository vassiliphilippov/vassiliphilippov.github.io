electric catfish

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛktrɪk ˈkætˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrɪk ˈkatˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a freshwater catfish of the Nile and tropical central Africa, notable for its ability to produce electric shocks
African catfish able to produce electricity

Examples

The electric catfish can deliver a shock strong enough to stun its prey.
Researchers studied the electric catfish to understand its unique biological adaptations.
Local fishermen are cautious when handling a electric catfish because of its electric charge.
