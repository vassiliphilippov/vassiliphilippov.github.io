cold cuts

1. NOUN

/ koʊld kʌts / (AmE), / kəʊld kʌts / (BrE)
Sliced cooked or cured meats, typically served cold and often assorted, used in sandwiches or as part of a platter.
sliced assorted cold meats

Examples

We ordered a platter of cold cuts and cheese for the party.
She made sandwiches with turkey and other cold cuts for lunch.
The deli counter offers a wide selection of cold cuts for customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deli meats - processed meats sold at a delicatessen, similar to cold cuts
  • Synonym: charcuterie - prepared meat products, especially pork, served cold
