Ceylon bowstring hemp

1. NOUN

/ səˈlɑn ˈboʊˌstrɪŋ hɛmp / (AmE), / sɪˈlɒn ˈbəʊˌstrɪŋ hɛmp / (BrE)
a tropical plant with thick, fibrous leaves banded in light and dark green, commonly grown as an ornamental and source of fiber
fibrous-leaved tropical ornamental plant

Examples

The Ceylon bowstring hemp thrives in well-drained soil and makes an excellent houseplant.
Fiber from the Ceylon bowstring hemp was once used to make strong ropes and bowstrings.
We placed a pot of Ceylon bowstring hemp in the living room for a touch of greenery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sansevieria - genus of African and Asian tropical plants known for fibrous leaves

Etymology

Ceylon (former name for Sri Lanka) + bowstring hemp (named for its use in making bowstrings).
