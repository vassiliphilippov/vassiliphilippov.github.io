sweet cherry liqueur

1. NOUN

/ swit ˈtʃɛri lɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / swiːt ˈtʃɛri lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
A sweet alcoholic beverage flavored with cherries, typically used in cocktails and desserts.
sweet cherry-flavored liqueur

Examples

The bartender added a splash of sweet cherry liqueur to enhance the flavor of the cocktail.
She drizzled sweet cherry liqueur over the dessert for a fruity twist.
A bottle of sweet cherry liqueur makes a perfect gift for those who enjoy creative drinks.
