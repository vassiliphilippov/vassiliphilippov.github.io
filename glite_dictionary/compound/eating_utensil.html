eating utensil

1. NOUN

/ ˈitɪŋ juˈtɛnsəl / (AmE), / ˈiːtɪŋ juːˈtɛnsl / (BrE)
An object, such as a fork, knife, or spoon, used for cutting and eating food.
object used for eating food

Examples

Please place each eating utensil neatly beside the plate.
In many cultures, chopsticks are the primary eating utensil.
After the party, we collected all of the eating utensils for washing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tableware - utensils and dishes used at the table for eating and serving food
