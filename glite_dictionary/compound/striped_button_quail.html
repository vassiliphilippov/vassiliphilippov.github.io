striped button quail

1. NOUN

/ straɪpt ˈbʌtn ˈkweɪl / (AmE), / straɪpt ˈbʌtn ˈkweɪl / (BrE)
a small ground-dwelling bird species with striped plumage, belonging to the button quail group
a bird species with striped markings

Examples

The striped button quail is often found in open grasslands and farmlands.
Birdwatchers spotted a female striped button quail near the village yesterday.
The distinguishing feature of the striped button quail is the series of stripes along its back.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: button quail - a small bird of the family Turnicidae, resembling true quails
