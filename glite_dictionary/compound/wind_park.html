wind park

1. NOUN

/ wɪnd pɑrk / (AmE), / wɪnd pɑːk / (BrE)
A site where a group of wind turbines is used to generate electricity.
group of wind turbines for electricity

Examples

The new wind park is expected to power over 50,000 homes in the region.
Investors are showing interest in building a large offshore wind park along the coast.
Tourists were impressed by the sight of dozens of turbines spinning at the wind park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wind farm - an area of land with a group of wind turbines for producing electricity
