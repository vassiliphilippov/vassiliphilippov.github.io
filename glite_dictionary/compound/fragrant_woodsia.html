fragrant woodsia

1. NOUN

/ ˈfreɪɡrənt ˈwʊd.si.ə / (AmE), / ˈfreɪɡrənt ˈwʊd.sɪ.ə / (BrE)
a small rock-dwelling fern found in northern temperate regions, characterized by rusty-brown stalks and lance-shaped, divided fronds
a small northern rock fern
