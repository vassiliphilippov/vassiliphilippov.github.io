ankle bracelet

1. NOUN

/ ˈæŋkəl ˌbreɪslət / (AmE), / ˈaŋkəl ˌbreɪslɪt / (BrE)
A piece of jewellery worn around the ankle, usually as an ornament.
ornamental jewellery worn around the ankle

Examples

She wore a delicate silver ankle bracelet that sparkled in the sunlight.
His gift to her was an engraved ankle bracelet for their anniversary.
The dancer's ankle bracelet jingled softly with every graceful step.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anklet - ornamental band or chain worn around the ankle
