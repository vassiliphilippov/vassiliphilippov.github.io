air filter

1. NOUN

/ ɛr ˈfɪltər / (AmE), / eə ˈfɪltə / (BrE)
a device that removes dust and other particles from air passing through it, typically used in vehicles, buildings, and machinery
device that removes particles from air

Examples

The air filter in your car should be replaced every 12,000 miles.
Make sure the air filter in your home is clean for better air quality.
He bought a new air filter to reduce dust and allergens in his apartment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filter - device for removing impurities from liquids or gases
