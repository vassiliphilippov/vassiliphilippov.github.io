purple saxifrage

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝː.pəl ˈsæk.səˌfrædʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.pəl ˈsæks.ɪ.frɪdʒ / (BrE)
a low, cushion-forming plant with bright reddish-purple flowers, found in rocky areas of Europe, Asia, and western North America
mountain plant with reddish-purple flowers

Examples

The rocky slopes were dotted with blooming purple saxifrage in early spring.
Hikers admired the resilience of purple saxifrage thriving in the harsh alpine climate.
Clusters of purple saxifrage added a splash of color to the tundra landscape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saxifrage - a plant of the genus Saxifraga, often found in rocky places
