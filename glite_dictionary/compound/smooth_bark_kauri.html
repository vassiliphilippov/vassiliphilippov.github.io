smooth bark kauri

1. NOUN

/ smuð bɑrk ˈkaʊri / (AmE), / smuːð bɑːk ˈkaʊri / (BrE)
An Australian timber tree resembling the kauri, but with wood that is lighter in weight and softer.
Australian timber tree, lighter wood than kauri

Examples

The smooth bark kauri is prized for woodworking because of its light and soft wood.
Unlike the New Zealand kauri, the smooth bark kauri has a distinctive pale trunk and softer wood.
Many Australian forests are home to the smooth bark kauri, an important species for local timber industries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kauri - A large coniferous tree native to New Zealand and Australia, known for its timber.
