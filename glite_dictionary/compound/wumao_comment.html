wumao comment

1. NOUN

/ wuːˈmaʊ ˈkɑmɛnt / (AmE), / wuːˈmaʊ ˈkɒmɛnt / (BrE)
An online comment made by a person or group, often paid or organized, that supports the Chinese government, typically as part of a coordinated effort to influence public opinion.
online comment supporting the Chinese government

Examples

The forum was filled with wumao comments defending the government's policy.
Many users accused the account of posting wumao comments to manipulate public opinion online.
It's often hard to distinguish genuine opinions from wumao comments in controversial threads.

Synset Examples

The online debate was flooded with wumao comments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: internet troll - person who posts inflammatory or off-topic messages online

Etymology

From Chinese 'wǔmào' (五毛), meaning 'fifty cents', referring to the alleged payment per post for pro-government comments.
