pillow block

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪloʊ ˌblɑk / (AmE), / ˈpɪləʊ ˌblɒk / (BrE)
A metal block, typically of cast iron or steel, used to support a rotating shaft or journal, usually containing a bearing.
support block with a bearing for a rotating shaft

Examples

The pillow block holds the shaft securely in place while allowing it to rotate smoothly.
After replacing the old pillow block, the machine ran much more quietly.
A faulty pillow block bearing can cause significant vibration in industrial equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bearing - machine element that constrains relative motion to only the desired motion and reduces friction between moving parts
