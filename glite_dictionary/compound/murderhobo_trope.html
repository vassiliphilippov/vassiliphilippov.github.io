murderhobo trope

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɝdɚˌhoʊboʊ tɹoʊp / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdəˌhəʊbəʊ trəʊp / (BrE)
(in roleplaying games) a stereotype or recurring theme in which characters aimlessly travel and kill indiscriminately without engaging with story or setting.
gaming stereotype of indiscriminate killing characters

Examples

The campaign quickly devolved into the murderhobo trope, with players attacking everyone they met.
Many game masters dislike the murderhobo trope because it undermines story-driven adventures.
The podcast parodied the murderhobo trope by having its heroes avoid combat entirely.

Synset Examples

They laughed at the murderhobo trope in their favorite RPG.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: murderhobo - a player character who wanders and kills indiscriminately in games

Etymology

Blend of 'murder' and 'hobo', indicating a wanderer who kills; 'trope' from Greek 'tropos' (turn, manner).
