courtship ritual

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrtʃɪp ˈrɪtʃuəl / (AmE), / ˈkɔːtʃɪp ˈrɪtjuəl / (BrE)
a set of traditional or customary behaviors performed by individuals seeking romantic partners, especially prior to forming a long-term relationship
customary behaviors for seeking romantic partners

Examples

In many animals, the courtship ritual involves colorful displays and elaborate dances.
Anthropologists study how the courtship ritual varies between different human societies.
Their first date followed the courtship ritual typical of early twentieth-century England.

Synset Examples

He enjoyed the traditional courtship rituals in his culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: courtship - the act or period of seeking the affections of someone, often with the intent to marry
