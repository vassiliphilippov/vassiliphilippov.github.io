school text

1. NOUN

/ skuːl tɛkst / (AmE), / skuːl tɛkst / (BrE)
a book prepared for use as a standard study material in schools or colleges
book used in schools or colleges

Examples

The teacher asked each student to bring their school text to class every day.
She found her old mathematics school text while cleaning the attic.
Many schools now offer digital versions of the school text to reduce costs.

Synset Examples

his economics textbook is in its tenth edition
the professor wrote the text that he assigned students to buy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: textbook - a book used as a standard work for a particular subject of study
