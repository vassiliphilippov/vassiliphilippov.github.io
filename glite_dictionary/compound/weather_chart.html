weather chart

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛðər tʃɑrt / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə tʃɑːt / (BrE)
a map showing the main meteorological conditions, such as pressure, temperature, and wind, in a specific area at a particular time
meteorological map showing weather conditions

Examples

The meteorologist studied the weather chart before giving the forecast on television.
Pilots often consult a weather chart to plan safer flight routes.
The weather chart showed a large cold front moving across the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: synoptic chart - a similar map summarizing meteorological data
