information bulletin

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.fərˈmeɪ.ʃən ˈbʊl.ə.tən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.fəˈmeɪ.ʃən ˈbʊl.ə.tɪn / (BrE)
a brief publication or announcement providing the latest news, updates, or information about a particular subject or organization
short publication providing latest updates

Examples

The school sends out an information bulletin every week to keep parents updated.
Please refer to the latest information bulletin for details about the upcoming changes.
An information bulletin about public health guidelines was distributed to all residents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulletin - a short official statement or broadcast summary of news
