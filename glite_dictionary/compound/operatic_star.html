operatic star

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːpəˈrætɪk stɑr / (AmE), / ˌɒpəˈrætɪk stɑː / (BrE)
a singer who performs principal roles in operas and is noted for outstanding talent and fame
famous leading opera singer

Examples

She dreamed of becoming an operatic star and singing on the world's greatest stages.
Last night, the operatic star received a standing ovation for her powerful performance as Tosca.
Many aspiring singers look up to the great operatic stars of the past for inspiration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diva - a distinguished female opera singer, often a prima donna
