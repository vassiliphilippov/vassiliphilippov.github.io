keyboard tray

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːbɔrd treɪ / (AmE), / ˈkiːbɔːd treɪ / (BrE)
a sliding or retractable platform under a desk designed to hold a computer keyboard
sliding desk platform for keyboard

Examples

Many office workers use a keyboard tray to improve their typing posture and reduce strain.
The computer desk I bought has a convenient keyboard tray that slides in and out easily.
She adjusted her keyboard tray to a comfortable height before starting work.

Synset Examples

The most common form of the computer desk is a variant of the ergonomic desk, which has an adjustable keyboard tray and sufficient desktop space for handwriting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tray - a flat, shallow container used for carrying or holding items
