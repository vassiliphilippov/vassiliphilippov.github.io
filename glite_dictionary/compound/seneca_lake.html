Seneca Lake

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɛnɪkə leɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɛnɪkə leɪk / (BrE)
the largest of the Finger Lakes, a glacial lake located in central New York State
largest Finger Lake in New York

Examples

The vineyards along Seneca Lake are famous for producing excellent Riesling wines.
We spent our summer vacation boating and swimming in Seneca Lake.
The depth of Seneca Lake makes it a popular spot for scuba diving.

Etymology

Named after the Seneca people, a Native American tribe of the Iroquois Confederacy native to the region.
