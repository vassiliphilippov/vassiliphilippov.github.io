sweatpants

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɛtˌpænts / (AmE), / ˈswɛtˌpænts / (BrE)
Loose, casual trousers with elasticized cuffs and waistband, typically made from soft fabric, worn for exercise or comfort.
casual loose trousers for exercise or comfort

Examples

After work, he likes to relax at home in his comfortable sweatpants.
She slipped on a pair of sweatpants before heading out for a morning jog.
Many college students wear sweatpants to class for a more laid-back look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: track pants - lightweight athletic pants, often with stripes

Etymology

From 'sweat' (in reference to athletic activity or perspiration) + 'pants'. Modeled after garments worn for sports.
