Munjuk language

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌn.dʒʊk ˌlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmʌn.dʒʊk ˌlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken south of Lake Chad, especially in the Far North Region of Cameroon.
a Chadic language of Cameroon

Examples

The Munjuk language is primarily spoken in villages south of Lake Chad.
Linguists are working to document the grammar of the Munjuk language.
Few educational materials are currently available in the Munjuk language for local schools.
