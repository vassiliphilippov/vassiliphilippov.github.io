medical procedure

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl proʊˈsiːdʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈmedɪkəl prəˈsiːdʒə / (BrE)
an action or series of actions carried out by medical or dental professionals to diagnose, treat, or prevent a health problem
action by a healthcare professional

Examples

The doctor explained that the medical procedure would help relieve my symptoms.
Advancements in technology have made many medical procedures safer and more effective.
Before the medical procedure, patients are usually required to fast for several hours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: operation - a surgical procedure to repair or remove part of the body
  • Synonym: surgery - the treatment of injuries or disorders by operative methods
  • Synonym: treatment - medical care given for an illness or injury
