royal pain

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɔɪ.əl peɪn / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪ.əl peɪn / (BrE)
a person or thing that is extremely annoying or troublesome
person or thing that is very annoying
informal

Examples

My little brother can be a royal pain when he wants attention.
Filling out all that paperwork was a royal pain last week.
This software update is a royal pain because it keeps crashing my computer.

Synset Examples

He can be a royal pain when he doesn't get his way.
Her constant questions were a royal pain during the meeting.
The project turned out to be a royal pain for everyone involved.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nuisance - a person or thing causing inconvenience or annoyance
  • Synonym: bother - a source of annoyance or trouble
  • Antonym: pleasure - a feeling of happy satisfaction and enjoyment
