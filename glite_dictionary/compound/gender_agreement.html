gender agreement

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛndɚ əˈɡriːmənt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛndə əˈɡriːmənt / (BrE)
the correspondence in grammatical gender between related words within a phrase or sentence
grammatical matching of gender between words

Examples

In Spanish, adjectives show gender agreement with the nouns they modify.
Mistakes in gender agreement are common for learners of French.
The test assessed the students' understanding of gender agreement in Italian.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agreement - the correspondence of grammatical features between words

Etymology

From Latin 'genus' (kind, gender) and Middle English 'agrement' (agreement).
