philanthropic gift

1. NOUN

/ fɪˌlænθrəˈpɪk ɡɪft / (AmE), / fɪˌlanθrəˈpɪk ɡɪft / (BrE)
a donation made voluntarily to promote human welfare, often for charitable or humanitarian purposes
donation for promoting human welfare

Examples

The museum expansion was made possible by a generous philanthropic gift from a local family.
Universities often rely on philanthropic gifts to fund scholarships and research.
Her philanthropic gift to the hospital helped build a new children's wing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: donation - something that is given to a person or organization, usually for charity
  • Synonym: charitable contribution - a gift or donation made for charitable purposes

Etymology

From 'philanthropic', from Greek *philanthrōpos* (loving mankind), and 'gift' (something given).
