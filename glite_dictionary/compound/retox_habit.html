retox habit

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːˌtɑks ˈhæbɪt / (AmE), / ˈriːtɒks ˈhæbɪt / (BrE)
a pattern of indulging in unhealthy behaviors following a period of abstinence or detoxification
indulgence after detoxing

Examples

His retox habit made it difficult to maintain a healthy lifestyle long-term.
Many people struggle with a retox habit after finishing a strict cleansing diet.
She's trying to break her retox habit and find a better balance.

Synset Examples

His retox habits often conflicted with his fitness goals.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: detox - to abstain from unhealthy substances to remove toxins

Etymology

Formed from 'retox' (reverse of 'detox') + 'habit'. 'Retox' is a blend of 're-' (again) and 'toxin'.
