woodland oxeye

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊd.lənd ˈɑk.saɪ / (AmE), / ˈwʊd.lənd ˈɒk.saɪ / (BrE)
A hairy Eurasian perennial plant with deep yellow daisy-like flowers, typically found in open woodland and on rocky slopes.
perennial yellow-flowered plant

Examples

Clusters of woodland oxeye brighten the forest clearings each summer.
She sketched a delicate woodland oxeye in her botanical journal.
The woodland oxeye thrives in partial shade on rocky hillsides.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oxeye daisy - a related plant with similar daisy-like flowers
