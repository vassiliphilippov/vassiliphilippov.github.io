booby house

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːbi ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈbuːbi ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a place designed for silly or lighthearted entertainment, especially appealing to children
place for silly entertainment

Examples

Every summer, the children begged to visit the booby house at the fair.
The new booby house downtown is filled with prank mirrors and wacky games.
Laughter echoed from the booby house as kids tried the funny obstacle course.

Synset Examples

We went to the booby house for some silly fun.
The kids thought the booby house was the funniest place ever.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: funhouse - a building with amusing or confusing attractions
  • Synonym: carnival - a traveling amusement show with rides and games
