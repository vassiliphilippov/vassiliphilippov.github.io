rock cress

1. NOUN

/ rɑk krɛs / (AmE), / rɒk krɛs / (BrE)
any of various small flowering plants of the genus Arabis that typically grow among rocks or in rocky areas
a small plant of the genus Arabis

Examples

The gardener planted rock cress along the stone pathway to add some spring color.
Rock cress thrives in well-drained soil and is perfect for rock gardens.
Some species of rock cress have delicate white or pink flowers in early spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Arabis - the plant genus including rock cress species
  • Degree Higher: cress - a type of plant with small, pungent leaves
