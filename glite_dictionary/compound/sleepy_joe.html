Sleepy Joe

1. NOUN

/ ˈsliːpi dʒoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsliːpi dʒəʊ / (BrE)
A nickname for a person who frequently falls asleep or appears drowsy, often used colloquially and sometimes pejoratively.
nickname for someone frequently sleepy
informal

Examples

Everyone in the office started calling him Sleepy Joe after he nodded off during meetings.
After staying up all night, I was the Sleepy Joe of our breakfast group.
The teacher jokingly named the student Sleepy Joe for falling asleep in class again.

Synset Examples

They affectionately called him Sleepy Joe after he dozed off.
