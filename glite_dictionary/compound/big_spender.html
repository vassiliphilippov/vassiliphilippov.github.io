big spender

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ˈspɛndɚ / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈspɛndə / (BrE)
a person who spends a lot of money, often in a showy or extravagant way, especially on entertainment or luxury items
person who spends money extravagantly

Examples

She's a big spender who doesn't hesitate to treat her friends to expensive dinners.
During the auction, the big spender made headlines by purchasing four paintings in ten minutes.
Though his salary was modest, he liked being seen as a big spender at parties.

Synset Examples

the last of the big spenders

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spendthrift - person who spends money in an extravagant, irresponsible way

/ bɪɡ ˈspɛndɚ / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈspendə / (BrE)
a person who spends large amounts of money, often freely or extravagantly
person who spends a lot of money

Examples

The restaurant attracts big spenders looking for a luxurious dining experience.
Being a big spender can impress some people, but it may also lead to financial trouble.
