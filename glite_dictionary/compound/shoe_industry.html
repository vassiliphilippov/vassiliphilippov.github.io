shoe industry

1. NOUN

/ ʃu ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / ʃuː ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
the business sector involved in the manufacturing and sale of shoes
sector for making and selling shoes

Examples

The shoe industry has faced significant changes as consumer preferences have evolved.
She works as a designer in the shoe industry, creating new styles for international markets.
After years of decline, the local shoe industry is now experiencing a revival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: footwear industry - sector for manufacturing and selling all types of footwear
