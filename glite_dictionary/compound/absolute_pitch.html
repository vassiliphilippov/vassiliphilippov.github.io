absolute pitch

1. NOUN

/ ˌæb.səˌluːt ˈpɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˌabsəˈluːt pɪtʃ / (BrE)
the rare ability to identify or sing a musical note without a reference tone
musical ability to identify pitches by ear

Examples

Only a small number of musicians have absolute pitch and can name notes on hearing them.
Her absolute pitch made it easy for her to tune all the instruments by ear.
Researchers are still studying how absolute pitch develops in early childhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfect pitch - the ability to identify or reproduce a musical note without a reference
