birth certificate

1. NOUN

/ bɝːθ sərˈtɪfɪkət / (AmE), / bɜːθ səˈtɪfɪkət / (BrE)
an official document stating the details of a person's birth, such as name, date, and place of birth
official record of a person's birth

Examples

You will need your birth certificate to apply for a passport.
The hospital provided a copy of the baby's birth certificate.
She discovered an error on her birth certificate when registering for school.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: death certificate - an official document stating the details of a person's death
