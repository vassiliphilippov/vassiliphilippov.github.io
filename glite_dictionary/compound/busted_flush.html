busted flush

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌstɪd flʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʌstɪd flʌʃ / (BrE)
In poker, a hand that is one card short of a flush and therefore has little or no value.
a losing poker hand with no value

Examples

His excitement disappeared as he revealed a busted flush at the showdown.
She bet heavily, not realizing her cards added up to just a busted flush.
Even an experienced player can end up holding a busted flush during a tense game.

Synset Examples

His poker hand was a busted flush, costing him the game.
After betting big, he realized he had a busted flush.
In poker, a busted flush means no winning hand.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: flush - a poker hand where all cards are of the same suit
