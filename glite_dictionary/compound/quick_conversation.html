quick conversation

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk ˌkɑnvərˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / kwɪk ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
a brief and informal exchange of words or ideas
brief, informal chat or discussion

Examples

We had a quick conversation in the hallway before the meeting began.
Sometimes a quick conversation is all it takes to clear up a misunderstanding.
I called her for a quick conversation about the upcoming project.

Synset Examples

Gtalk was popular for quick conversations among friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chat - an informal conversation
  • Degree Higher: discussion - the process of talking about something extensively
