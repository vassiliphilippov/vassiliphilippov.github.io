parking brake

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrkɪŋ breɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkɪŋ breɪk / (BrE)
a brake in a vehicle, operated by hand or foot, that keeps it stationary when parked
a hand-operated brake to keep a parked vehicle stationary

Examples

Always engage the parking brake before leaving your car on a steep hill.
The mechanic said my parking brake needed adjustment after my last service.
She accidentally forgot to release the parking brake before driving off, causing a loud screeching noise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handbrake - a brake operated by hand, especially for keeping a parked vehicle stationary
