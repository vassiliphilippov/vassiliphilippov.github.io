locker room

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑːkər ruːm / (AmE), / ˈlɒkə ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a sports facility, gym, or workplace where people can change clothes and store personal belongings in lockers
room for changing clothes and storing belongings

Examples

After practice, everyone went to the locker room to shower and change.
She forgot her phone in the locker room after her workout.
The locker room was crowded before the big game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: changing room - a room for changing clothes, especially at a sports facility
