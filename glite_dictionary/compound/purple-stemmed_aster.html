purple-stemmed aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrpl ˌstɛmd ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˌstɛmd ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
A North American perennial wildflower with purple stems and clusters of violet or blue flowers, classified as Symphyotrichum puniceum.
a North American wildflower with purple stems

Examples

The purple-stemmed aster blooms late in the summer along the edges of wetlands.
Gardeners admire the purple-stemmed aster for its vibrant color and hardiness.
We found a patch of purple-stemmed asters while hiking through the meadow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a type of flowering plant in the daisy family
