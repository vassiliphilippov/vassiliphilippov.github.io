bacon sanger

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪkən ˈsæŋər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkən ˈsaŋə / (BrE)
An informal term, especially in Australia, for a bacon sandwich.
Australian slang for bacon sandwich
informal slang

Examples

After a long night, all I wanted was a hot bacon sanger for breakfast.
He stopped by the café and ordered a bacon sanger to go.
Nothing beats a freshly made bacon sanger with extra crispy bacon.

Synset Examples

A classic bacon sanger makes for a perfect breakfast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bacon sandwich - a sandwich filled with slices of bacon

Etymology

Australian slang, 'sanger' being a colloquial form of 'sandwich'.
