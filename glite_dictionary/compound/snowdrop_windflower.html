snowdrop windflower

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊˌdrɑp ˈwɪndˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌdrɒp ˈwɪndˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A Eurasian herb with solitary, nodding, fragrant white flowers, belonging to the buttercup family.
Eurasian herb with white, nodding flowers

Examples

The snowdrop windflower blooms early in spring, brightening woodland areas with its delicate white petals.
Gardeners often plant the snowdrop windflower to add fragrance and beauty to shaded gardens.
We spotted several snowdrop windflowers nodding gently in the morning breeze.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: windflower - another name for members of the Anemone genus, flowering plants in the buttercup family
  • Synonym: snowdrop - a small European plant with white, drooping, bell-shaped flowers
