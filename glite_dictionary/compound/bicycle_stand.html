bicycle stand

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪsɪkəl stænd / (AmE), / ˈbaɪsɪkl̩ stænd / (BrE)
a metal or wooden rack for holding and securing parked bicycles
rack for parking bicycles

Examples

She locked her bike to the bicycle stand outside the café.
The school installed a new bicycle stand for students who ride to class.
All the bicycle stands near the train station were occupied by noon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bike rack - a rack for holding and locking bicycles
