deer hunter

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪr ˌhʌntər / (AmE), / ˈdɪə ˌhʌntə / (BrE)
a person who hunts deer for sport or food
person who hunts deer

Examples

Every autumn, the deer hunter would prepare his gear for the upcoming season.
The deer hunters quietly made their way through the forest at dawn.
He became an experienced deer hunter after spending years tracking wildlife in the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hunter - a person who hunts wild animals
