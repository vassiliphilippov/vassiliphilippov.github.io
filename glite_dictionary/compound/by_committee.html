by committee

1. X

/ baɪ kəˈmɪɾi / (AmE), / baɪ kəˈmɪti / (BrE)
decided or developed collectively by a group, rather than by an individual
collectively decided or created by a group

Examples

The policy was written by committee, resulting in several compromises.
Their logo design, created by committee, lacks a clear vision.
This proposal was assembled by committee, which explains its cautious tone.

Synset Examples

The decision was made by committee.
They drafted the plan by committee.
The project was developed by committee.
