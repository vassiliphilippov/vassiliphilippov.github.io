Jamaica bayberry

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈmeɪ.kə ˈbeɪˌbɛri / (AmE), / dʒəˈmeɪ.kə ˈbeɪˌbəri / (BrE)
a West Indian tree (Pimenta racemosa) whose leaves are used to make bay rum
West Indian tree; source of bay rum

Examples

The leaves of the Jamaica bayberry are distilled to produce the famous bay rum.
Many traditional Caribbean remedies use extracts from the Jamaica bayberry tree.
The spicy scent of Jamaica bayberry filled the local apothecary shop.
