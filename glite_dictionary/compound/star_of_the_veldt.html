star of the veldt

1. NOUN

/ stɑr əv ðə vɛlt / (AmE), / stɑː əv ðə vɛlt / (BrE)
any of several South African plants cultivated for their bright yellow, daisylike flowers and aromatic, leafy mounds; commonly grown as ornamentals.
South African ornamental plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The star of the veldt brightened the garden with its clusters of golden flowers.
Gardeners often choose star of the veldt for its vibrant color and fragrant foliage.
We planted several stars of the veldt along the walkway to add a touch of South African charm.

Etymology

From 'star' (referring to the shape of the flower) and Afrikaans 'veldt' (open grassland).
