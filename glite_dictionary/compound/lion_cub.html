lion cub

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪ.ən kʌb / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.ən kʌb / (BrE)
A young lion; a lion in its early life before reaching maturity.
a young lion

Examples

The zookeeper carefully bottle-fed the orphaned lion cub every morning.
Photographers waited for hours to catch a glimpse of a playful lion cub in the wild.
The lion cub stayed close to its mother as it explored its surroundings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cub - a young member of certain animals, including lions, bears, and tigers
