mine pig

1. NOUN

/ maɪn pɪɡ / (AmE), / maɪn pɪɡ / (BrE)
Pig iron produced entirely from ore, without using scrap iron.
pig iron made from ore only

Examples

The foundry ordered a shipment of mine pig for the next production phase.
Due to its purity, mine pig is preferred in certain metallurgical processes.
They analyzed samples of mine pig to ensure it contained no recycled material.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pig iron - raw iron from a blast furnace
