to cancel trip

1. VERB

/ ˈkæn.səl ˈtrɪp / (AmE), / ˈkæn.səl ˈtrɪp / (BrE)
to decide not to go on a planned journey or travel, usually due to unforeseen circumstances.
to call off planned travel

Examples

Due to the snowstorm, we had to cancel trip at the last minute.
She was disappointed that she needed to cancel trip because of a work emergency.
They decided it was safer to cancel trip when they heard about the travel warnings.

Synset Examples

She had to cancel her trip due to ill health.
They decided to cancel the picnic due to the storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to call off - to cancel something that was planned
  • Antonym: postpone trip - to delay a trip to a later time
