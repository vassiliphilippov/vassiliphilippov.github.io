two-thirds

1. NOUN

/ tu ˈθɝdz / (AmE), / tuː ˈθɜːdz / (BrE)
An amount equal to two out of three equal parts of a whole.
two of three equal parts

Examples

Two-thirds of the students agreed with the proposal.
The recipe calls for two-thirds of a cup of sugar.
Only two-thirds of the tickets were sold before the event.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: third - one out of three equal parts
