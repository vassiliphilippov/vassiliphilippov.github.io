Copacabana Beach

1. PROPN

/ koʊpəkəˈbɑːnə biːtʃ / (AmE), / kɒpəkəˈbænə biːtʃ / (BrE)
a famous sandy beach and tourist destination in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, known for its vibrant atmosphere and oceanfront promenade
renowned beach in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Examples

Many tourists flock to Copacabana Beach every year to enjoy the lively festivals and sunbathe on the sand.
She took stunning photos of the sunset at Copacabana Beach during her trip to Brazil.
The annual New Year's Eve celebration at Copacabana Beach attracts millions of visitors from around the world.

Synset Examples

We spent our vacation relaxing at Copacabana beach.

Etymology

From Copacabana, the neighborhood in Rio de Janeiro, named after a Bolivian town meaning 'View of the Lake' in Aymara.
