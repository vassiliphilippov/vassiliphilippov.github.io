to make the best

1. VERB

/ meɪk ðə bɛst / (AmE), / meɪk ðə bɛst / (BrE)
to produce or create something of the highest possible quality
produce the highest quality

Examples

Every year, they make the best apple pies in the county for the harvest festival.
He was determined to make the best presentation possible for the interview panel.
She knows how to make the best use of fresh seasonal ingredients in her cooking.

Synset Examples

They always make the best desserts at this café.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to create - to bring something into existence
  • Antonym: to make do - manage with what is available
