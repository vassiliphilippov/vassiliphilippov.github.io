evening primrose

1. NOUN

/ ˈiv.nɪŋ ˈprɪmˌroʊz / (AmE), / ˈiːv.nɪŋ ˈprɪm.rəʊz / (BrE)
A flowering plant of the Onagraceae family, typically having yellow flowers that open in the evening.
a flowering plant of the Onagraceae family

Examples

Many people cultivate the evening primrose for its bright yellow flowers.
Oil extracted from the evening primrose is used in various herbal supplements.
The evening primrose blooms at dusk, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths.
