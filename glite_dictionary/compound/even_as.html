even as

1. SCONJ

/ ˈivən æz / (AmE), / ˈiːvən æz / (BrE)
at the very moment that; while something else is happening at the same time
at the same time as; while

Examples

Even as she spoke, the lights suddenly went out, plunging the room into darkness.
The team was celebrating victory even as the final whistle blew.
Even as the new law was announced, protests began to form in the city streets.

Synset Examples

even as he lay dying they argued over his estate
the building collapsed just as he arrived

Related Terms

  • Synonym: just as - at exactly the moment that; simultaneously with
