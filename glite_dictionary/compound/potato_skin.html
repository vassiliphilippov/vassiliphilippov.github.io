potato skin

1. NOUN

/ pəˈteɪɾoʊ skɪn / (AmE), / pəˈtəʊtəʊ skɪn / (BrE)
a crisp fried or baked appetizer made from the skin of a potato, often topped with cheese, bacon, or other fillings
crisp fried or baked potato peel appetizer

Examples

We ordered a plate of potato skins topped with cheddar and bacon as an appetizer.
The restaurant's signature dish is crispy potato skins served with sour cream.
At the party, everyone enjoyed the homemade potato skins with chives and melted cheese.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: potato chip - a thin slice of potato that is deep fried or baked until crisp
