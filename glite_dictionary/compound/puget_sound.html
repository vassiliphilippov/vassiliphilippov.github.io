Puget Sound

1. PROPN

/ ˈpjuːdʒɪt saʊnd / (AmE), / ˈpjuːdʒɪt saʊnd / (BrE)
a large inlet of the North Pacific Ocean located in northwestern Washington State, known for its complex system of interconnected marine waterways and islands
inlet of the North Pacific in Washington State

Examples

Orcas can often be spotted in Puget Sound during the summer months.
The city of Seattle is located on the eastern shore of Puget Sound.
Environmental efforts are underway to protect the ecosystem of Puget Sound.

Etymology

Named after Peter Puget, a lieutenant in the British Royal Navy who explored the area in 1792.
