to cultivate a love for

1. VERB

/ ˈkʌltɪˌveɪt ə lʌv fər / (AmE), / ˈkʌltɪveɪt ə lʌv fə / (BrE)
to foster or encourage a deep affection or strong liking for something
foster or encourage deep affection

Examples

Teachers often try to cultivate a love for science in their students.
Traveling to new countries can cultivate a love for different cultures.
Her parents wanted to cultivate a love for music from an early age.

Synset Examples

He wants to cultivate a love for reading in his children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nurture - to care for and encourage growth or development
