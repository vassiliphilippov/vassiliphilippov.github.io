sand trap

1. NOUN

/ sænd træp / (AmE), / sænd træp / (BrE)
a shallow pit filled with sand, serving as a hazard on a golf course
golf course hazard filled with sand

Examples

His ball landed in the sand trap near the green, making his next shot difficult.
Golfers try to avoid the sand trap when aiming for the hole.
She used her wedge to chip the ball out of the sand trap and onto the fairway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bunker - a hazard on a golf course filled with sand
