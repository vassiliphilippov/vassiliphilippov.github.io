to emboss initials

1. VERB

/ ɪmˈbɑs ɪˈnɪʃəlz / (AmE), / ɪmˈbɒs ɪˈnɪʃəlz / (BrE)
to impress initials so that they are raised above the surface, usually for decoration or personalization
to impress initials in relief

Examples

The company can emboss initials on the front cover of your notebook.
He decided to emboss initials on his leather wallet as a special touch.
Many gift shops offer to emboss initials on purchased items for personalization.

Synset Examples

He loves to emboss his initials on all his belongings.
The artist used a tool to emboss intricate designs on the leather.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to engrave - to carve or cut a design or letters into a hard surface
