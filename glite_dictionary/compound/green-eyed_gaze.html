green-eyed gaze

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡrin ˈaɪd ˌɡeɪz / (AmE), / ˌɡriːn ˈaɪd ˌɡeɪz / (BrE)
A look or expression that shows jealousy or intense emotion.
look showing jealousy or intensity

Examples

Her green-eyed gaze fixed on me as I spoke to her boyfriend at the party.
He noticed the green-eyed gaze from across the table and quickly changed the subject.
With a green-eyed gaze, she made her displeasure known without uttering a word.

Synset Examples

Her green-eyed gaze seemed to pierce through my thoughts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jealous look - a look revealing jealousy

Etymology

From the figurative use of 'green-eyed' for jealousy, as popularized by Shakespeare, combined with 'gaze'.
