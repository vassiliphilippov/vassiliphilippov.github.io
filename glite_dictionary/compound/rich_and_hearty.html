rich and hearty

1. ADJ

/ rɪtʃ ənd hɑrti / (AmE), / rɪtʃ ənd hɑːti / (BrE)
(of food or drink) full of flavor, substantial, and very satisfying.
full-flavored and satisfying (food)

Examples

The soup was rich and hearty, perfect for a cold winter evening.
She served a rich and hearty casserole that everyone enjoyed.
A rich and hearty breakfast kept him full until afternoon.

Synset Examples

The sinew in the stew made it rich and hearty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rich - full of flavor, heavy or substantial (of food)
  • Synonym: hearty - (of food) wholesome and filling
