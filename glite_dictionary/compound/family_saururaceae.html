family Saururaceae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli sɔːˌrjʊəˈreɪsiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli sɔːˌrjʊərəˈsiː / (BrE)
A family of perennial aromatic herbs including the genera Saururus, Anemopsis, and Houttuynia.
a family of aromatic perennial herbs

Examples

The family Saururaceae includes several aromatic herbs found in wetland habitats.
Botanists have studied the family Saururaceae to understand its evolutionary relationships with other plant groups.
Members of the family Saururaceae are known for their distinctive leaf shapes and floral structures.

Etymology

From scientific Latin Saururaceae, from Saururus (type genus) + -aceae (suffix for plant family names).
