Oxford University

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑːksfərd juːnɪˌvɝːsəti / (AmE), / ˈɒksfəd juːnɪˌvɜːsəti / (BrE)
A prestigious university located in Oxford, England, known as one of the oldest and most distinguished universities in the world.
university in Oxford, England

Examples

She dreamed of studying at Oxford University since she was a child.
Many world leaders have graduated from Oxford University.
The libraries at Oxford University are renowned for their extensive collections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: University of Cambridge - prestigious university in Cambridge, England

Etymology

Named after the city of Oxford; 'University' from Latin 'universitas' (whole, total; a society, corporation).
