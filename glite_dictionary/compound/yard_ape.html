yard ape

1. NOUN

/ jɑrd eɪp / (AmE), / jɑːd eɪp / (BrE)
an informal and sometimes playful term for a lively, mischievous child
mischievous, playful child
informal slang

Examples

My nephew is a real yard ape—he never seems to run out of energy.
During the summer, the park is full of yard apes playing tag and yelling.
She's always been a bit of a yard ape, climbing everything in sight.

Synset Examples

He’s such a yard ape, always climbing trees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rascal - a mischievous or cheeky person, usually a child
  • Synonym: scamp - a person, especially a child, who is mischievous in a likable way
  • Antonym: angel - a well-behaved child
