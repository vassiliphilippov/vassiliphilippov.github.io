bank check

1. NOUN

/ bæŋk tʃɛk / (AmE), / baŋk tʃɛk / (BrE)
A written order instructing a bank to pay a specified sum of money from the drawer's account to the person or organization named on the order.
written order for bank payment

Examples

He wrote a bank check to cover the repairs on his car.
Payment can be made by bank check or electronic transfer.
The store does not accept personal bank checks without proper identification.

Synset Examples

he paid all his bills by check

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheque - A written order directing a bank to pay money, chiefly British spelling
  • Synonym: money order - A written certificate for payment similar to a check, but issued by a bank or post office
  • Antonym: cash - Money in the form of bills or coins
