stroke of business

1. NOUN

/ ˈstroʊk əv ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / strəʊk əv ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
a notably successful or fortunate business action or decision
successful business decision

Examples

The merger was a stroke of business that revitalized both companies.
Her acquisition of that startup proved to be a real stroke of business in hindsight.
The CEO's decision to enter the international market was a stroke of business that paid off.

Synset Examples

The merger was a stroke of business that revitalized both companies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masterstroke - a particularly clever or skillful action
  • Antonym: misstep - an unwise or unfortunate step or decision
