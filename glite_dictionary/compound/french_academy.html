French Academy

1. PROPN

/ frɛntʃ əˈkædəmi / (AmE), / frɛntʃ əˈkædəmi / (BrE)
an official body of distinguished French writers, scholars, and thinkers that oversees matters pertaining to the French language
official French language body

Examples

The French Academy has played a major role in defining standards for the French language.
Victor Hugo was elected as a member of the French Academy in the 19th century.
The French Academy regularly publishes updated editions of the official French dictionary.

Synset Examples

the French Academy sets standards for the use of the French language

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Académie française - the French name for the French Academy, France's official authority on the French language
