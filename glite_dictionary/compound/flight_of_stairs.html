flight of stairs

1. NOUN

/ flaɪt əv stɛrz / (AmE), / flaɪt əv stɛəz / (BrE)
a set of stairs between one floor or landing and the next in a building
set of stairs between floors or landings

Examples

She climbed the flight of stairs to reach the second floor.
There was a beautiful painting hanging at the top of the flight of stairs.
He paused halfway up the flight of stairs to catch his breath.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: staircase - a set of stairs and its surrounding structure
  • Synonym: landing - a level area at the top of a staircase
