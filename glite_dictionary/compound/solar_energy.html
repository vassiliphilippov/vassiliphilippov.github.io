solar energy

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊlər ˈɛnərdʒi / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə ˈɛnədʒi / (BrE)
energy from the sun that is converted into heat or electricity for use
energy from the sun used as heat or electricity

Examples

Many homes now use solar energy to power their lights and appliances.
Researchers are developing new ways to store solar energy for use at night.
Solar energy is a clean alternative to fossil fuels for generating electricity.

Synset Examples

the amount of energy falling on the earth is given by the solar constant, but very little use has been made of solar energy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solar power - power generated from sunlight, especially using panels
  • Degree Higher: renewable energy - energy from naturally replenished sources
