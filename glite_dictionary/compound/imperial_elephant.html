imperial elephant

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈpɪriəl ˈɛlɪfənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪəriəl ˈɛlɪfənt / (BrE)
an extinct species of mammoth, the largest known, that lived in North America during the Pleistocene epoch
extinct large mammoth species of North America

Examples

The imperial elephant once roamed across vast regions of North America during the Ice Age.
Scientists have studied the fossils of the imperial elephant to understand its massive size.
Remains of the imperial elephant have been found alongside those of other prehistoric animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mammoth - any of various large extinct elephants of the genus Mammuthus
