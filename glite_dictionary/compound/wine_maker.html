wine maker

1. NOUN

/ waɪn ˈmeɪkər / (AmE), / waɪn ˈmeɪkə / (BrE)
an establishment where wine is made
place where wine is made

Examples

The wine maker produces thousands of bottles each year for local and international markets.
She visited the oldest wine maker in the region to learn about traditional production methods.
Many tourists enjoy tours and tastings at the wine maker during the harvest season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: winery - a building or property where wine is produced
