table mustard

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəl ˈmʌstərd / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəl ˈmʌstəd / (BrE)
A pungent powder or paste made from ground mustard seeds, typically used as a condiment at the table.
pungent condiment from mustard seeds

Examples

He added a spoonful of table mustard to his sandwich for extra flavor.
Table mustard is commonly found alongside ketchup and salt on restaurant tables.
The recipe calls for a teaspoon of table mustard to enhance the dressing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mustard - a yellow or brown condiment made from the seeds of certain plants
