adjustable wrench

1. NOUN

/ əˈdʒʌstəbl rɛntʃ / (AmE), / əˈdʒʌstəbəl rɛntʃ / (BrE)
a wrench with a movable jaw that allows it to fit different sizes of nuts and bolts
wrench with a movable jaw

Examples

He grabbed an adjustable wrench to tighten the loose pipe under the sink.
An adjustable wrench is essential for any basic tool kit.
The plumber used his adjustable wrench to fit the new valve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crescent wrench - an American term for an adjustable wrench
