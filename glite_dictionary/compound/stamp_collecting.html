stamp collecting

1. NOUN

/ stæmp kəˈlɛktɪŋ / (AmE), / stæmp kəˈlɛktɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

After inheriting his grandfather's albums, he developed a deep interest in stamp collecting.
The club meets every month to share their passion for stamp collecting and exchange rare finds.
