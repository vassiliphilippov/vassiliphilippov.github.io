starvin' Marvin

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑrvɪn ˈmɑrvɪn / (AmE), / ˈstɑːvɪn ˈmɑːvɪn / (BrE)
A playful or humorous name for someone who is very hungry.
playful term for a very hungry person
informal american

Examples

After a day without lunch, I was practically starvin' Marvin by dinnertime.
Whenever her brothers get home from football practice, they turn into starvin' Marvin at the kitchen table.
Don’t skip breakfast or you’ll be starvin' Marvin by mid-morning!

Synset Examples

The kids called me starvin' Marvin when I missed breakfast.
She looked like starvin' Marvin after the long hike.
He joked that he felt like starvin' Marvin after skipping lunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: starving - extremely hungry

Etymology

Formed from 'starving' and the common name 'Marvin', popularized in the 1990s by American TV programs and advertising.
