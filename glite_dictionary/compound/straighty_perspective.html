straighty perspective

1. NOUN

/ ˈstreɪti pərˈspɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˈstreɪti pəˈspɛktɪv / (BrE)
a viewpoint or attitude limited to heterosexual norms, often overlooking LGBTQ+ perspectives
viewpoint limited to heterosexual norms
informal

Examples

Her straighty perspective makes it difficult for her to understand LGBTQ+ experiences.
The panel discussion challenged the straighty perspective on family and relationships.
Critics argued that the film was too focused on a straighty perspective and lacked representation.

Synset Examples

His straighty perspective limits his understanding of diverse viewpoints.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heteronormativity - the attitude that heterosexuality is the only normal sexual orientation
  • Antonym: inclusive perspective - a viewpoint that includes diverse sexual orientations
