tiger beetle

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪɡər ˌbiːtəl / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡə ˌbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a fast-moving, usually brightly colored beetle that hunts and feeds on other insects
predatory brightly colored beetle

Examples

A tiger beetle darted across the sandy trail, chasing after its prey.
Scientists study the tiger beetle for its exceptional speed and predatory skills.
Many tiger beetles have iridescent green or blue colors that shimmer in the sunlight.
