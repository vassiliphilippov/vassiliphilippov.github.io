genus Galleria

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs gəˈlɪəriə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ɡəˈlɪəriə / (BrE)
A genus of moths in the family Pyralidae, which includes the greater wax moth or honeycomb moth (Galleria mellonella).
genus of moths including the greater wax moth

Examples

The genus Galleria includes the well-known greater wax moth, a pest of beehives.
Researchers studied the behavior of moths in the genus Galleria for insights into their impact on apiaries.
Larvae from the genus Galleria are often used in scientific experiments due to their susceptibility to infections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Galleria mellonella - the greater wax moth, principal species of the genus Galleria

Etymology

From Latin 'genus' (kind, type) and 'Galleria', the genus name.
