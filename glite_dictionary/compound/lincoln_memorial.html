Lincoln Memorial

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪŋkən məˈmɔriəl / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋkən mɪˈmɔːriəl / (BrE)
a large memorial building in Washington, D.C., commemorating President Abraham Lincoln and featuring a marble statue of him
monument honoring Abraham Lincoln in Washington, D.C.

Examples

Tourists from all over the world visit the Lincoln Memorial each year.
The Lincoln Memorial is one of the most famous landmarks in Washington, D.C.
A large crowd gathered at the Lincoln Memorial for the historic speech.

Etymology

Named after Abraham Lincoln (1809–1865), 16th president of the United States.
