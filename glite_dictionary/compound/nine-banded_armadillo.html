nine-banded armadillo

1. NOUN

/ ˌnaɪn ˈbændəd ˌɑːrməˈdɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˌnaɪn ˈbændɪd ˌɑːmədɪləʊ / (BrE)
a species of armadillo (Dasypus novemcinctus) having nine hinged bands of bony plates, native to regions from Texas to Paraguay
species of armadillo with nine bands

Examples

The nine-banded armadillo is often seen crossing roads in southern United States at night.
Researchers studied the habits of the nine-banded armadillo to understand its spread into new territories.
The distinctive armor of the nine-banded armadillo helps protect it from predators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armadillo - a burrowing mammal with a leathery armored shell
