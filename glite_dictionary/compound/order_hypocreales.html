order Hypocreales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər ˌhaɪpoʊsiˈreɪliz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌhaɪpəʊsiˈreɪliːz / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of fungi that includes the family Hypocreaceae and related groups
a taxonomic order of fungi

Examples

The order Hypocreales includes several species important in agriculture and medicine.
Researchers have discovered new genera within the order Hypocreales in tropical forests.
Members of the order Hypocreales are known for their brightly colored fruiting bodies.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin 'Hypocreales', based on 'Hypocreaceae', the type family, and the standard taxonomic suffix '-ales'.
