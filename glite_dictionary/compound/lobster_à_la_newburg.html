lobster à la Newburg

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑbstɚ ɑ lɑ ˈnuːbɚɡ / (AmE), / ˈlɒbstə ɑː lɑː ˈnjuːbɜːɡ / (BrE)
A dish consisting of lobster cooked in Newburg sauce and served on buttered toast or rice.
lobster in creamy Newburg sauce

Examples

For a special occasion, he prepared lobster à la Newburg for his guests.
The restaurant's most famous dish is its classic lobster à la Newburg.
She learned to cook lobster à la Newburg from a vintage cookbook.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lobster thermidor - lobster dish with creamy egg-based sauce, often baked

Etymology

Named after Newberg (later Newburg) sauce, believed to be created in the late 19th century at Delmonico's Restaurant, New York.
