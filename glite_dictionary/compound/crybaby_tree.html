crybaby tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi tri / (AmE), / ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi triː / (BrE)
a small spiny tree native to South America, with dark crimson and scarlet flowers borne singly or in clusters
spiny South American tree with scarlet flowers

Examples

The crybaby tree dazzles with clusters of bright scarlet flowers each spring.
Gardeners value the crybaby tree for its unusual blooms and attractive spiny branches.
The crybaby tree is native to tropical regions of South America and thrives in warm climates.
