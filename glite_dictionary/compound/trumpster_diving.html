Trumpster diving

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmp.stɚ ˌdaɪ.vɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmp.stə ˌdaɪ.vɪŋ / (BrE)
Searching for sensational or controversial material online, often involving trolling or aggressive debate, usually with a political motive.
controversy-seeking online behavior, often political
informal slang

Examples

Accusations of Trumpster diving filled the comment section whenever the topic turned controversial.
Her repeated Trumpster diving only escalated the heated online debate.
Some pundits avoid Trumpster diving to focus on substantive discussion.

Synset Examples

Critics said she was just Trumpster diving for attention.
They dismissed his arguments as mere Trumpster diving.
He was accused of Trumpster diving in the online forums.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: dumpster diving - searching through refuse for useful or interesting items

Etymology

Blend of 'Trumpster' (a colloquial term for supporters of Donald Trump) and 'dumpster diving' (searching through refuse for items of value).
