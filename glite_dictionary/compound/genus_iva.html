genus Iva

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈaɪ.və / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈaɪ.və / (BrE)
A small genus of American herbs or shrubs, sometimes classified in the family Ambrosiaceae.
genus of American herbs or shrubs

Examples

The genus Iva includes species commonly found in North American wetlands.
Botanists have debated whether to place the genus Iva in the family Asteraceae or Ambrosiaceae.
Plants in the genus Iva are known for their tolerance to saline soils.

Etymology

From Latin 'Iva', the scientific name of the genus, used in botanical nomenclature.
