multicultural workforce

1. NOUN

/ ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəl ˈwɜrkˌfɔrs / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəl ˈwɜːkfɔːs / (BrE)
A group of employees from different cultural backgrounds within an organization.
workforce from diverse cultures

Examples

The company values its multicultural workforce as a source of innovation and global perspective.
Managing a multicultural workforce requires sensitivity to different traditions and communication styles.
Their multicultural workforce allowed them to expand successfully into international markets.

Synset Examples

Their office has a multicultural workforce from around the globe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diverse workforce - workforce composed of people of different backgrounds
