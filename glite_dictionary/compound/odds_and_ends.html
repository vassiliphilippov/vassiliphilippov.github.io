odds and ends

1. NOUN

/ ɑdz ənd ɛndz / (AmE), / ɒdz ənd endz / (BrE)
various small, miscellaneous items or remnants, especially those that are left over or of little value
miscellaneous small items

Examples

She keeps a box full of odds and ends in the attic.
The drawer was filled with odds and ends like old keys and scraps of paper.
They gathered all the odds and ends left after the event for recycling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bits and pieces - small, assorted things

Etymology

First recorded in the late 1700s, from the notion of odd pieces and leftover ends.
