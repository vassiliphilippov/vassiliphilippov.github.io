custard apple tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌstərd ˈæpəl triː / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəd ˈæpl triː / (BrE)
A tropical American tree that bears fruit with a soft, edible pulp, commonly known as the custard apple.
tropical tree bearing custard apple fruit

Examples

The custard apple tree thrives in warm, humid climates with plenty of rainfall.
Farmers in South America often cultivate the custard apple tree for its delicious fruit.
We planted a custard apple tree in our backyard to enjoy fresh fruit every season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: custard apple - the fruit of the custard apple tree
