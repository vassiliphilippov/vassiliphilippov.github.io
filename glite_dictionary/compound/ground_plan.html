ground plan

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡraʊnd ˌplæn / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊnd ˌplæn / (BrE)
a scale drawing showing the arrangement of rooms and spaces on the ground floor of a building
floor plan for ground level

Examples

The architect presented a detailed ground plan of the new library for approval.
Before construction, we carefully reviewed the ground plan to understand the building’s layout.
The ground plan included space for both offices and a communal lounge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: floor plan - a scale drawing showing the layout of rooms in a building
