private school

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪvət skuːl / (AmE), / ˈpraɪvət skuːl / (BrE)
A school that is owned and run independently of government funding, usually supported by fees and private donations.
independently funded school

Examples

Their children have attended a prestigious private school since kindergarten.
Many private schools offer scholarships to students from diverse backgrounds.
The high tuition fees make private schools inaccessible for some families.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: public school - a school supported by public funds
