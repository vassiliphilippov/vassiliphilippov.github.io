car part

1. NOUN

/ kɑr pɑrt / (AmE), / kɑː pɑːt / (BrE)
a component or piece of a car, such as the engine, brakes, or windshield
component of a car

Examples

He spent the afternoon replacing the car part that was causing the noise.
Many online retailers specialize in selling rare car parts for older vehicles.
She ordered a new car part to fix her broken headlight.
