broken arch

1. NOUN

/ ˈbroʊkən ɑrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkən ɑːtʃ / (BrE)
An arch with a gap at its apex, often filled with decorative elements.
arch with a gap at the apex

Examples

The entryway was topped by a broken arch adorned with elaborate carvings.
A broken arch can add visual interest to a classical architectural design.
They restored the old church, carefully repairing the ornate broken arch above the altar.
