elephant legs

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛləfənt lɛɡz / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪfənt lɛɡz / (BrE)
Legs that appear large or oversized, especially as a result of wearing loose or baggy clothing.
legs that look large or oversized (esp. with baggy clothes)

Examples

She felt self-conscious that her sweatpants gave her elephant legs.
Baggy jeans can sometimes make your legs look like elephant legs.
His costume’s pants were so loose, they resembled elephant legs.

Synset Examples

She laughed, saying her new jeans gave her elephant legs.
The oversized trousers made his legs appear like elephant legs.
His pants were so baggy, they looked like elephant legs.
