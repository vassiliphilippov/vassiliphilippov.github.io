Japanese Red Army

1. PROPN

/ dʒəˈpæniz rɛd ˈɑːrmi / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæn.iːz red ˈɑː.mi / (BrE)
a revolutionary militant group founded in Japan in 1970, known for armed attacks and acts of terrorism internationally, aiming to overthrow the Japanese government and promote world revolution
Japanese leftist militant group (1970s–2000s)

Examples

The Japanese Red Army carried out several notorious attacks in the 1970s.
Authorities linked the Japanese Red Army to international terrorist networks.
The 1972 Lod Airport massacre was attributed to the Japanese Red Army.
