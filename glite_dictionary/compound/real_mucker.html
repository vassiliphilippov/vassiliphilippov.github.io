real mucker

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːəl ˈmʌk.ər / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl ˈmʌk.ə / (BrE)
a person who is especially helpful or cooperative, particularly as part of a team
helpful or cooperative person
informal

Examples

Everyone said that Tom was a real mucker during the company's busiest season.
It helps to have a real mucker on your side when taking on difficult projects.
Sarah acted like a real mucker, always supporting her colleagues in every situation.

Synset Examples

He's a real mucker when it comes to teamwork.
