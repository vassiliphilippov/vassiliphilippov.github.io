test ban

1. NOUN

/ tɛst bæn / (AmE), / tɛst ban / (BrE)
an official agreement between nations to prohibit the testing of nuclear weapons
prohibition on nuclear weapons testing

Examples

The two countries signed a test ban to reduce the threat of nuclear war.
Many activists have called for a global test ban on nuclear weapons.
The test ban treaty marked a turning point in international arms control.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: nuclear test - the detonation of a nuclear weapon for testing purposes
  • Degree Higher: comprehensive test ban treaty - an international agreement banning all nuclear explosions for testing
