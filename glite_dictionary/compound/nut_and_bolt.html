nut and bolt

1. NOUN

/ nʌt ənd boʊlt / (AmE), / nʌt ənd bəʊlt / (BrE)
a fastener consisting of a bolt and a matching nut, used together to join materials securely by means of a screw thread
type of fastener using a screw and nut

Examples

The mechanic secured the engine part with a nut and bolt to ensure it stayed in place.
She found a rusty nut and bolt on the workshop floor while cleaning.
The assembly instructions require every piece to be joined with a nut and bolt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fastener - a device that joins two or more objects together
