European plan

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən plæn / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən plæn / (BrE)
a hotel rate that includes only the cost of the room, with no meals provided
hotel rate with room only, no meals

Examples

Many travelers prefer the European plan because it gives them the freedom to explore local restaurants.
The hotel's European plan does not include breakfast or any other meals.
You will be charged extra for meals since you booked the European plan.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: American plan - hotel rate including room and all meals
  • Synonym: continental plan - hotel plan including breakfast with room
