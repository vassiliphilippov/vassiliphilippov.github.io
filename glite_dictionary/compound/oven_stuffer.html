Oven Stuffer

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌvən ˌstʌfər / (AmE), / ˈʌvən ˌstʌfə / (BrE)
A brand name for a large roasting chicken, especially as sold in supermarkets.
brand of roasting chicken
american

Examples

She bought an Oven Stuffer for Sunday dinner and roasted it with herbs and vegetables.
The recipe calls for a whole Oven Stuffer, but you can use any large roasting chicken.
Supermarkets often advertise Oven Stuffers at a discount during the holiday season.
