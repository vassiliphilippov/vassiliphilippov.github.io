Department of Commerce

1. PROPN

/ dɪˈpɑːrtmənt əv ˈkɑːmɝːs / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtmənt əv ˈkɒmɜːs / (BrE)
the United States federal department responsible for promoting and regulating domestic and international trade, and overseeing agencies such as the Census Bureau and Patent Office
U.S. federal department for trade and commerce
american

Examples

The Department of Commerce announced new regulations affecting international trade.
Every ten years, the Department of Commerce conducts the national census in the United States.
She received a job offer from the Department of Commerce to work on economic research projects.
