true kemosabi

1. NOUN

/ truː ˌkiːmoʊˈsɑːbi / (AmE), / truː ˌkɛməˈsɑːbi / (BrE)
a person who is a loyal and trusted friend or partner
loyal and trusted friend or partner
informal

Examples

Everyone needs a true kemosabi to rely on during tough times.
She stayed by his side in every crisis, proving herself a true kemosabi.
After years of working together, he called her his true kemosabi.

Synset Examples

He treated his partner like a true kemosabi.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sidekick - a close companion or assistant, especially one who assists a more important person
  • Synonym: partner - a person who shares or is associated with another in some action or endeavor; associate

Etymology

Kemosabi derives from the catchphrase in the 20th-century American TV series The Lone Ranger, where it meant 'trusted friend'.
