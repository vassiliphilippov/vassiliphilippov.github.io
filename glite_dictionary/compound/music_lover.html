music lover

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuː.zɪk ˌlʌ.vɚ / (AmE), / ˈmjuː.zɪk ˌlʌ.və / (BrE)
a person who enjoys listening to music, often attending concerts or musical events
a person who enjoys music

Examples

As a true music lover, she never missed a chance to attend local concerts.
The festival attracted thousands of music lovers from all around the country.
Being a music lover, he has an impressive collection of vinyl records.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: melomaniac - a person with a great passion for music
