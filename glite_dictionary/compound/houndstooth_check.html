houndstooth check

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊndstuθ tʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈhaʊndstuːθ tʃɛk / (BrE)
A textile pattern featuring small, broken or jagged checks typically in two contrasting colors.
textile pattern of broken checks

Examples

She wore a tailored coat with a classic houndstooth check pattern.
The designer chose houndstooth check fabric for the suit to create a striking visual effect.
You can recognize houndstooth check by its distinctive, jagged black-and-white pattern.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: houndstooth - a textile pattern of broken checks
  • Synonym: plaid - a checkered or tartan twilled cloth

Etymology

From 'hound's tooth', referring to the shape of the checks resembling a canine tooth, and 'check', meaning a pattern.
