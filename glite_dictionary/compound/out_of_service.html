out of service

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / aʊt əv ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
not working or not available for use, especially temporarily
not working; unavailable for use

Examples

The elevator is currently out of service and will be repaired tomorrow.
Several buses were out of service due to routine maintenance.
The ATM was out of service when I needed to withdraw cash.

Synset Examples

The lavatory was out of service during the flight.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in service - in working condition or available for use
  • Synonym: out of order - not functioning correctly; broken
