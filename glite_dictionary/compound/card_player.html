card player

1. NOUN

/ kɑrd ˈpleɪər / (AmE), / kɑːd ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays card games, either recreationally or professionally
person who plays card games

Examples

She is an experienced card player who often participates in poker tournaments.
As a casual card player, he enjoys spending weekends playing bridge with friends.
The skilled card player quickly spotted his opponent's bluff.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gambler - a person who bets money on games of chance
