seventy-something

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛv.ən.tiˌsʌm.θɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɛv.ən.tiˌsʌm.θɪŋ / (BrE)
a person who is aged between 70 and 79
person in their seventies

Examples

My grandfather, a seventy-something, still enjoys skiing every winter.
The club is open to everyone, from teenagers to seventy-somethings.
As a seventy-something, she volunteers at the library every week.

Synset Examples

At seventy-something, he still runs marathons.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: octogenarian - person aged between 80 and 89
  • Degree Lower: sixty-something - person aged between 60 and 69
