riproarious tale

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɪpˈrɔriəs teɪl / (AmE), / ˌrɪpˈrɔːriəs teɪl / (BrE)
a story or account that is extremely funny or uproarious, often provoking loud laughter or amusement
an extremely funny or uproarious story

Examples

The comedian told a riproarious tale that had everyone in stitches for minutes afterward.
Grandpa always shares a riproarious tale at family gatherings, making us laugh until we cry.
Her new book is filled with one riproarious tale after another, keeping readers thoroughly entertained.

Synset Examples

His riproarious tales kept the audience entertained.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uproarious tale - a story that is very funny, causing loud laughter

Etymology

From 'riproarious', formed in imitation of 'uproarious' (itself from 'uproar'), with influence from 'rip-roaring' (lively, boisterous).
