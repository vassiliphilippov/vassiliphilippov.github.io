air-filled

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛr fɪld / (AmE), / ˈeə fɪld / (BrE)
containing or filled with air; inflated with air.
containing or full of air

Examples

He slept on an air-filled mattress during the camping trip.
The doctor examined the patient's lungs for air-filled cavities.
The festival featured hundreds of colorful, air-filled balloons floating in the sky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inflated - expanded or filled with air or gas
