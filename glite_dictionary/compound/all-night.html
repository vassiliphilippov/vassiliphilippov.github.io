all-night

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔlˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɔːlˌnaɪt / (BrE)
lasting, open, or operating through the whole night; continuing for the entire night.
lasting or operating all night

Examples

We stopped at an all-night diner after the concert.
The city is famous for its all-night bus service.
He studied for his exams during an all-night session at the library.

Synset Examples

a nightlong vigil
an all-night drugstore
an overnight trip

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overnight - lasting or occurring through the night
