suborder Gorgoniacea

1. NOUN

/ ˌsʌbˈɔrdər ˌɡɔrɡəˈniːəsiə / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈɔːdə ˌɡɔːɡəˈniːəsɪə / (BrE)
A former taxonomic suborder of soft corals including the red corals and sea fans.
a former suborder of soft corals

Examples

Scientists discovered a new species of suborder Gorgoniacea off the coast of Indonesia.
The distinctive shapes of suborder Gorgoniacea can be seen in many tropical reefs.
Many jewelry items are made from the skeletons of suborder Gorgoniacea, especially red coral.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea fan - a type of soft coral with a fanlike appearance
  • Synonym: red coral - a type of red-colored soft coral

Etymology

From New Latin, based on genus Gorgonia and Greek mythology (Gorgons).
