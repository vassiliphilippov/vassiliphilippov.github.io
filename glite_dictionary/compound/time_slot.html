time slot

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪm ˌslɑt / (AmE), / ˈtaɪm ˌslɒt / (BrE)
a specific period of time that is scheduled or allocated for a particular event, activity, or purpose
scheduled period of time

Examples

The meeting is scheduled for the 10 a.m. time slot on Monday.
She managed to book a time slot at the gym in the evening.
That television program was moved to a later time slot this year.

Synset Examples

the TV program has a new time slot
an aircraft landing slot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slot - an allocated period of time in a schedule
  • Degree Lower: interval - a period between two events
