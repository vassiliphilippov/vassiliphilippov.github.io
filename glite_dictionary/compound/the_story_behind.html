the story behind

1. NOUN

/ ðə ˈstɔri bɪˈhaɪnd / (AmE), / ðə ˈstɔːri bɪˈhaɪnd / (BrE)
the background information or explanation for how or why something exists, happened, or was created
background information or explanation

Examples

I love hearing the story behind family heirlooms; they always have such rich histories.
Do you know the story behind this old abandoned building in town?
She shared the story behind her success during the interview.

Synset Examples

What's the story behind that painting?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: background - the circumstances or facts that form the setting for an event
  • Synonym: origin - the beginning or source of something
