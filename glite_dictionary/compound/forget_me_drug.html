forget me drug

1. NOUN

/ fərˈɡɛt mi ˌdrʌɡ / (AmE), / fəˈɡɛt mi ˌdrʌɡ / (BrE)
A colloquial and often pejorative term for flunitrazepam, a powerful sedative sometimes used illicitly in drug-facilitated crimes.
slang term for flunitrazepam (Rohypnol)
slang

Police warned about the rising use of the forget me drug in nightclubs.

The report detailed the effects and dangers of the forget me drug on unsuspecting individuals.
