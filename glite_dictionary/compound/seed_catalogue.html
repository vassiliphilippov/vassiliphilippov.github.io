seed catalogue

1. NOUN

/ sid ˈkætəlˌɔg / (AmE), / siːd ˈkætəlɒg / (BrE)
a printed or online publication listing seeds for sale, often with descriptions and prices
list of seeds and prices for sale

Examples

I requested a seed catalogue from the nursery so I could plan my spring garden.
The seed catalogue lists dozens of tomato varieties, each with a detailed description.
Gardeners eagerly await the arrival of the new seed catalogue every winter.
