sewing basket

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊɪŋ ˌbæskət / (AmE), / ˈsəʊɪŋ ˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
a basket used for holding sewing materials and supplies
basket for sewing materials

Examples

She kept her old sewing basket on the shelf next to the armchair.
He searched through the sewing basket for a matching button.
The colorful threads were neatly arranged inside the sewing basket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workbasket - a basket for holding materials for needlework or other craftwork
