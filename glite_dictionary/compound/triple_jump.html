triple jump

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɪpəl dʒʌmp / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpəl dʒʌmp / (BrE)
an athletic event in which competitors must perform a hop, a step, and a jump in sequence without stopping
athletic event: hop, step, and jump in sequence

Examples

She set a new national record in the triple jump at the championships.
The triple jump requires athletes to master technique as well as speed and strength.
He has trained for years to compete in the triple jump at the Olympics.
