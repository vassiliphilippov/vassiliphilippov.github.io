fresh popcorn

1. NOUN

/ frɛʃ ˈpɑːpˌkɔrn / (AmE), / frɛʃ ˈpɒpkɔːn / (BrE)
Popcorn that has been recently prepared and is still warm and crisp.
recently made popcorn

Examples

The smell of fresh popcorn greeted everyone as they entered the movie theater.
She bought a bag of fresh popcorn from the street vendor during the festival.
Nothing beats the taste of fresh popcorn right out of the popper.

Synset Examples

The smell of fresh popcorn filled the air at the fair.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stale popcorn - popcorn that is no longer fresh or crisp
