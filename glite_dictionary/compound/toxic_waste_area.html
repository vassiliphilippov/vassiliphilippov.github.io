toxic waste area

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑksɪk ˈweɪst ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˈtɒksɪk ˈweɪst ˈeəriə / (BrE)
an area or site where toxic wastes have been dumped and require cleanup, often designated by environmental authorities
site where toxic wastes have been dumped

Examples

The government is planning to clean up the toxic waste area near the river.
Living near a toxic waste area can pose serious health risks to the community.
After years of neglect, the toxic waste area was finally listed for environmental remediation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toxic waste dump - a place where toxic waste is stored or disposed of
  • Degree Lower: landfill - a site for waste disposal by burial in the ground
