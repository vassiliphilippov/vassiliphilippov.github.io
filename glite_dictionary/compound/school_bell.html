school bell

1. NOUN

/ skul bɛl / (AmE), / skuːl bɛl / (BrE)
a bell rung in a school to signal the beginning or end of classes or breaks
bell used to signal class times at school

Examples

The school bell rang loudly, signaling the end of the first period.
As soon as the school bell sounded, students rushed out to the playground.
The teacher paused mid-sentence when the school bell interrupted the lesson.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bell - a hollow device made of metal that rings when struck
