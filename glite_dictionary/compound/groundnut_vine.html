groundnut vine

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡraʊndˌnʌt vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊndˌnʌt vaɪn / (BrE)
a North American climbing plant (Apios americana) with fragrant blossoms and edible tubers, used as food by Native Americans
North American vine with edible tubers

Examples

The edible tubers of the groundnut vine were an important food source for some tribes.
Clusters of purple flowers appear on the groundnut vine in late summer.
She grew a groundnut vine in her garden to try its delicious tubers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groundnut - edible tuber of the North American plant Apios americana or a related species
