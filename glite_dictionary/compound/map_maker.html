map maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæp ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈmæp ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to create or draw maps
person who creates maps

Examples

The map maker spent months surveying the area before beginning his project.
As a skilled map maker, she contributed to several important atlases.
Early explorers often relied on a map maker to chart their journeys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cartographer - a person who draws or produces maps
