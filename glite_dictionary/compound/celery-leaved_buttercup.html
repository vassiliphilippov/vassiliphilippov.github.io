celery-leaved buttercup

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛləri liːvd ˈbʌtərkʌp / (AmE), / ˈsɛləri liːvd ˈbʌtəkʌp / (BrE)
an annual herb (Ranunculus sceleratus) with celery-like leaves, typically found in marshy or wet areas
annual herb with celery-like leaves

Examples

The celery-leaved buttercup thrives in wet meadows and marshy ditches.
Gardeners often avoid planting celery-leaved buttercup due to its toxic properties.
We identified the celery-leaved buttercup by its bright yellow flowers and deeply divided leaves.
