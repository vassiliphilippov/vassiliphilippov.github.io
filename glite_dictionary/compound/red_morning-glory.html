red morning-glory

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˌɡlɔri / (AmE), / rɛd ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˌɡlɔːri / (BrE)
An annual climbing herb with scarlet flowers, native to the eastern United States.
annual herb with scarlet flowers

Examples

The red morning-glory added a vibrant splash of color to the garden fence.
Gardeners in the eastern United States often cultivate red morning-glory for its striking scarlet blooms.
She planted several red morning-glory seeds along the trellis in early spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: morning-glory - a common name for various climbing plants with trumpet-shaped flowers
