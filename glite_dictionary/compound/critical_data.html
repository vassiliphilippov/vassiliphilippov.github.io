critical data

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪtɪkəl ˈdeɪtə / (AmE), / ˈkrɪtɪkəl ˈdeɪtə / (BrE)
information that is essential for a particular analysis, decision, or operation
essential information for analysis or decisions

Examples

The researchers relied on critical data from multiple sources to support their conclusions.
Losing critical data could severely impact the company's ability to function.
She ensured that all critical data was backed up before upgrading the system.

Synset Examples

The satellite provided critical data for the weather forecast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: essential data - information absolutely necessary for a process or decision
