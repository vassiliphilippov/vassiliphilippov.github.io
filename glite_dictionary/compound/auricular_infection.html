auricular infection

1. NOUN

/ ɔːˈrɪkjələr ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / ɔːˈrɪkjʊlə ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
an infection of the ear, especially of the outer ear
infection of the ear
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with an auricular infection after examining her ear pain.
Antibiotics are often prescribed to treat an auricular infection, especially when it is caused by bacteria.
Swimming in dirty water can increase the risk of developing an auricular infection.

Synset Examples

He had an auricular infection that affected his hearing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ear infection - an infection in any part of the ear

Etymology

From Latin 'auricula' (ear) and Latin 'infectio' (infection).
