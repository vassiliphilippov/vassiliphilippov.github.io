British Cabinet

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪtɪʃ ˈkæbɪnət / (AmE), / ˈbrɪtɪʃ ˈkæbɪnɪt / (BrE)
the group of senior ministers responsible for making major decisions in the government of the United Kingdom
senior ministers of the UK government
british

Examples

The Prime Minister announced a reshuffle of the British Cabinet this morning.
Membership in the British Cabinet is considered one of the highest honors in UK politics.
The British Cabinet met urgently to discuss the unfolding international crisis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cabinet - a body of advisers to a head of state, especially in government
