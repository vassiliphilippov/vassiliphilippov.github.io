total scag

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊtəl skæɡ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl skæɡ / (BrE)
something that is broken, worthless, or of very poor quality
something broken or worthless
informal slang

Examples

My old phone is a total scag now that the screen is cracked.
After the accident, his bike looked like a total scag.
She threw away the lamp because it was a total scag and couldn't be fixed.

Synset Examples

He called the broken chair a total scag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: junk - something that is considered to be of little value or use
  • Synonym: trash - something of little worth; waste material or objects
