Atlantean civilization

1. NOUN

/ ətˈlæntiən ˌsɪvələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ætˈlæntiən ˌsɪvɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the legendary ancient society said to have existed on the mythical island of Atlantis, often described as advanced and powerful
mythical society of Atlantis

Examples

Many historians have speculated about the existence of the Atlantean civilization.
The Atlantean civilization is often depicted as highly advanced for its time.
Legends claim the Atlantean civilization was destroyed by a great catastrophe.

Synset Examples

Many myths revolve around the legendary Atlantean civilization.
The story of Atlantis features the Atlantean people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Atlantis - the mythical island described by Plato

Etymology

From Atlantis, the mythical island described by Plato, + civilization.
