mid-twenties

1. NOUN

/ mɪd ˈtwɛn.tiz / (AmE), / mɪd ˈtwɛn.tiz / (BrE)
the period of a person's life when they are aged around 24 to 26 years old
ages about 24–26 years

Examples

She started her own business in her mid-twenties and quickly found success.
Many people in their mid-twenties face important career or relationship decisions.
He traveled through Europe during his mid-twenties before settling down.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: early twenties - the period of life aged around 20–23 years old
  • Degree Higher: late twenties - the period of life aged around 27–29 years old
