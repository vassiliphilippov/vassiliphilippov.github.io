cornflower blue

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrnˌflaʊɚ blu / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌflaʊə bluː / (BrE)
a shade of medium-to-light blue, similar to the color of a cornflower, containing little green
a shade of medium-to-light blue

Examples

She chose a dress in cornflower blue for the spring wedding.
The living room walls were painted cornflower blue, giving the space a calm, airy feel.
His eyes were a striking shade of cornflower blue that caught everyone's attention.

Synset Examples

Her suit ties in bows of self-fabric on the shoulder, and a circular skirt buttons on with straw flowers of cornflower blue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: azure - bright blue in color, like a cloudless sky
  • Degree Higher: royal blue - a deep, vivid blue color
  • Degree Lower: sky blue - a pale shade of blue

Etymology

Named after the bluish flower of the cornflower plant (Centaurea cyanus).
