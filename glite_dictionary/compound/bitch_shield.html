bitch shield

1. NOUN

/ bɪtʃ ʃiːld / (AmE), / bɪtʃ ʃiːld / (BrE)
(slang) A defensive attitude, behavior, or manner—often sarcastic or aloof—adopted to protect oneself from criticism, rejection, or unwanted attention.
slang: defensive attitude to ward off criticism
