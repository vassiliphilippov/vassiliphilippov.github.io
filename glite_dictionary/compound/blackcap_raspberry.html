blackcap raspberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈblækˌkæp ˈræzˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈblækˌkap ˈræzˌbəri / (BrE)
A species of raspberry native to eastern North America that bears black, thimble-shaped fruit.
raspberry with black fruit, native to eastern North America

Examples

Wild blackcap raspberry bushes grow along the edge of the forest near the lake.
She made homemade jam from freshly picked blackcap raspberries.
The blackcap raspberry is valued for its distinctive flavor and color in desserts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raspberry - a common red or black edible berry produced by several species of bramble
