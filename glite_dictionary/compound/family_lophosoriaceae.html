family Lophosoriaceae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli lɒfəˌsɔriˈeɪsiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli lɒfəˌsɔːriˈeɪsiː / (BrE)
a very small family of tree ferns found in certain tropical regions
a family of tree ferns

Examples

The family Lophosoriaceae is characterized by a single genus of tree ferns.
Researchers studied the distribution of the family Lophosoriaceae in South American rainforests.
Members of the family Lophosoriaceae are known for their distinctive fronds.

Etymology

From New Latin Lophosoriaceae, based on genus Lophosoria + family suffix -aceae.
