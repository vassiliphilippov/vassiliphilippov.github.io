musk cat

1. NOUN

/ mʌsk kæt / (AmE), / mʌsk kæt / (BrE)
a small, elusive mammal known for its musky scent, often found in marshy or forested areas
a small mammal with a musky scent

Examples

The musk cat releases a strong odor to mark its territory.
Many researchers have struggled to photograph the elusive musk cat in the wild.
Local folklore often describes the musk cat as a mysterious, ghost-like creature.

Synset Examples

The musk cat is elusive and prefers marshy areas.
In the wild, the musk cat is known for its unique odor.
