spring break

1. NOUN

/ sprɪŋ breɪk / (AmE), / sprɪŋ breɪk / (BrE)
a period of vacation, typically one week or more, during the spring term at a school or university
school or university vacation in spring

Examples

Many college students travel to Florida for spring break every year.
During spring break, the campus is almost deserted as most students go home.
Families often take vacations together when their children have spring break from school.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: winter break - a school or university vacation during winter
  • Antonym: summer break - a school or university vacation during summer
  • Synonym: holiday - a period when schools are closed
