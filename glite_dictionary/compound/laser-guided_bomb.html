laser-guided bomb

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪzɚˌɡaɪdɪd bɑm / (AmE), / ˈleɪzəˌɡaɪdɪd bɒm / (BrE)
a bomb equipped with a guidance system that homes in on laser light reflected from its target, allowing for precise strikes
a bomb guided by reflected laser light

Examples

The military deployed several laser-guided bombs to minimize collateral damage in the operation.
Cloud cover can interfere with the accuracy of laser-guided bombs by blocking the reflected signal.
Development of laser-guided bombs has revolutionized air-to-ground precision attacks.

Synset Examples

laser-guided bombs cannot be used in cloudy weather

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smart bomb - a bomb that can guide itself to a target
  • Antonym: dumb bomb - an unguided bomb dropped from an aircraft
