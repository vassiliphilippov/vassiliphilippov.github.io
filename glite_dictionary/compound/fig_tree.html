fig tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪɡ tri / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡ triː / (BrE)
A tree of the genus Ficus, especially one that bears edible figs, with a closed pear-shaped receptacle that ripens to become fleshy and edible.
tree producing edible figs (genus Ficus)

Examples

There is a large fig tree growing in the corner of our garden.
The ancient fig tree is considered sacred in many cultures.
We enjoyed fresh fruit picked right from the fig tree outside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fig - the sweet edible fruit of the fig tree
