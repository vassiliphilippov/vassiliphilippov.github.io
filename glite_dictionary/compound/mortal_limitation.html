mortal limitation

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrtl lɪˌmɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɔːtl lɪˌmɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the natural boundaries or weaknesses inherent to humans because of their mortality
human weakness or finite constraint

Examples

The hero struggled bravely against his mortal limitations in pursuit of the impossible dream.
Despite our mortal limitations, we continue to seek knowledge and meaning in life.
She was painfully aware of her mortal limitations as she watched others surpass her in strength and endurance.

Synset Examples

We all face our mortal limitations eventually.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: human frailty - the inherent weakness or vulnerability of human beings
