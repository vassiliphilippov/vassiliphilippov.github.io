musk-scented

1. ADJ

/ ˈmʌsk ˌsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈmʌsk ˌsɛntɪd / (BrE)
Having the smell or fragrance of musk.
having the odor of musk

Examples

The perfume had a musk-scented undertone that lingered throughout the day.
A gentle breeze carried the musk-scented aroma from the nearby garden.
He bought a candle with a musk-scented wax that filled the room with warmth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musky - having the scent of musk
