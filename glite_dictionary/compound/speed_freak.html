speed freak

1. NOUN

/ spiːd friːk / (AmE), / spiːd friːk / (BrE)
a person who is addicted to or habitually uses stimulant drugs, especially amphetamines
person addicted to stimulant drugs
slang

Examples

The police suspected he was a speed freak because of his erratic behavior.
She struggled for years to overcome her reputation as a speed freak.
His friends didn't know he was a speed freak until he admitted to using drugs daily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drug addict - a person addicted to drugs
  • Synonym: amphetamine user - someone who regularly uses amphetamines
