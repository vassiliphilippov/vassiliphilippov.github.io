unquestioning support

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
complete and loyal support given without doubt or hesitation
complete support without doubt

Examples

She was grateful for the unquestioning support of her family during a difficult time.
His unquestioning support for the team never wavered, even after several defeats.
Leaders rely on unquestioning support from their followers to maintain authority.

Synset Examples

The unquestioning support from her friends helped her a lot.
He followed the rules with unquestioning obedience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loyal support - support that is consistently faithful and devoted
