Battle of the Spanish Armada

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬l əv ðə ˌspænɪʃ ɑːrˈmɑːdə / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ðə ˌspænɪʃ ɑːˈmɑːdə / (BrE)
the naval battle in 1588 in which a small English fleet defeated the large Spanish Armada sent by Philip II to invade England
1588 naval battle: England vs Spanish Armada

Examples

Many historians consider the Battle of the Spanish Armada a turning point in European history.
The English navy's victory in the Battle of the Spanish Armada boosted national confidence.
Books about the Battle of the Spanish Armada often highlight unexpected weather as a factor in Spain's defeat.
