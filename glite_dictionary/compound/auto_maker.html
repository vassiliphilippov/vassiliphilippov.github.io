auto maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔˌtoʊ ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊ ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a company that manufactures automobiles
company that makes automobiles

Examples

Several leading auto makers announced new electric vehicle models this year.
The global auto maker is expanding its production facilities in Asia.
As an auto maker, the company faces increasing pressure to reduce carbon emissions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automobile manufacturer - a company producing automobiles

Etymology

Formed from 'auto' (short for 'automobile') and 'maker'.
