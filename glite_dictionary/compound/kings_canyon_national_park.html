Kings Canyon National Park

1. PROPN

/ kɪŋz ˈkænjən ˌnæʃənəl ˈpɑrk / (AmE), / kɪŋz ˈkænjən ˌnæʃənəl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in east-central California, known for its giant sequoia trees, deep canyons, alpine lakes, and glaciers
national park in California, USA

Examples

We spent a week hiking through Kings Canyon National Park and marveling at the enormous sequoias.
Many visitors come to Kings Canyon National Park to enjoy its stunning alpine lakes and scenic beauty.
The rangers at Kings Canyon National Park offer informative tours about the park's unique geology and wildlife.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sequoia National Park - a national park adjacent to Kings Canyon, famous for its giant sequoias
