bright yellow

1. ADJ

/ braɪt ˈjɛloʊ / (AmE), / braɪt ˈjɛləʊ / (BrE)
a vivid and intense shade of yellow, often used to describe objects or colors that are strikingly yellow and highly visible
vivid, intense yellow color

Examples

She decorated the nursery with bright yellow curtains to create a cheerful atmosphere.
A bright yellow umbrella stood out among all the dull, gray coats on the street.
The artist chose bright yellow for the sunflowers to make the painting more lively.

Synset Examples

She wore a bright yellow dress to the party.
She decided to paint the living room a bright yellow.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: yellow - the color between green and orange in the spectrum
  • Degree Higher: golden yellow - a rich, warm yellow shade
