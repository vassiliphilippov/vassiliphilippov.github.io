rummy game

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌmi ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈrʌmi ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a card game in which the aim is to form sets or runs by matching cards of the same rank or sequence and suit
card game of matching sets or runs

Examples

Every Sunday evening, the family enjoys a friendly rummy game around the kitchen table.
We played a rummy game late into the night, competing to see who could form sets the fastest.
She taught her grandchildren the basics of a rummy game during their summer visit.

Synset Examples

We played a rummy game late into the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rummy - a family of card games focused on matching cards

Etymology

From 'rummy', possibly from 'rum' (odd, queer), denoting a peculiar or unusual game.
