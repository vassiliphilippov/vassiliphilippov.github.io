Australian English orthography

1. NOUN

/ əˈstreɪliən ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ɔrˈθɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / ɒˈstreɪliən ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ɔːˈθɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the system of spelling and writing conventions used in Australian English, often differing in detail from British and American orthography
spelling conventions of Australian English

Examples

Students are taught Australian English orthography in schools across the country.
The organisation prefers to use Australian English orthography in its official publications.
There are notable differences between American and Australian English orthography in spelling certain words.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: British English orthography - the spelling system of English as used in Britain
  • Antonym: American English orthography - the spelling system of English as used in the United States
