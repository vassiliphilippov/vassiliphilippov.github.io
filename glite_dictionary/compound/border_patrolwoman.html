border patrolwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔrdər pəˈtroʊlˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdə pəˈtrəʊlˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to patrol and protect the borders of a country, especially to prevent illegal entry or smuggling
female officer who patrols borders

Examples

The border patrolwoman checked our passports at the crossing.
As a border patrolwoman, she spends long hours monitoring remote areas for illegal activity.
The new recruit aspired to become a border patrolwoman after finishing her training.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: border patrolman - a man who patrols the borders of a country
  • Synonym: border patrol - the group or force responsible for patrolling a country's borders
