miracle-worship

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪrəkəl ˌwɜrʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈmɪrəkəl ˌwɜːʃɪp / (BrE)
the practice or act of worshiping miracles or giving them undue reverence, especially in a religious context
worship or reverence of miracles

Examples

In some traditions, miracle-worship is criticized as undermining the core teachings of faith.
The rise of miracle-worship led to heated debates among scholars and theologians.
He wrote extensively about the dangers of miracle-worship within organized religion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idol worship - the worship of idols or physical objects as gods
  • Degree Lower: miracle - an extraordinary and welcome event believed to be caused by divine intervention
