rock beauty

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / rɒk ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
a gold and black butterflyfish (Holacanthus tricolor) found in the western Atlantic Ocean from the West Indies to Brazil.
a gold and black butterflyfish species

Examples

The rock beauty is a common sight in coral reefs throughout the Caribbean.
We spotted a vibrant rock beauty swimming near the shipwreck.
Marine biologists are studying the diet of the rock beauty in coastal waters.
