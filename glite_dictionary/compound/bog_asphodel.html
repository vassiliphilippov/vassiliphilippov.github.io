bog asphodel

1. NOUN

/ bɑɡ ˈæs.foʊ.dəl / (AmE), / bɒɡ ˈæs.fə.dəl / (BrE)
A herbaceous plant found in boggy areas of northern temperate regions, with yellow flowers and grasslike leaves.
herbaceous bog plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The bog asphodel brightened the wet moorland with its vivid yellow flowers.
In midsummer, clusters of bog asphodel can be found in peat-rich soils.
Botanists monitored the growth of bog asphodel to track changes in the bog’s ecosystem.

Etymology

From 'bog' (wet, spongy ground) and 'asphodel', from Greek 'asphodelos,' a type of lily.
