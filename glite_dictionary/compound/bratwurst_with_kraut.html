bratwurst with kraut

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɑːt.wɜrst wɪð kraʊt / (AmE), / ˈbrat.wɜːst wɪð kraʊt / (BrE)
A dish consisting of bratwurst sausage served with pickled cabbage (sauerkraut), commonly found in German cuisine.
bratwurst sausage with pickled cabbage

Examples

She ordered bratwurst with kraut at the German-themed festival.
At Oktoberfest, many people enjoy drinking beer and eating bratwurst with kraut.
The food truck serves a traditional bratwurst with kraut topped with mustard.

Synset Examples

The German restaurant served delicious bratwursts with kraut.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sauerkraut - pickled cabbage, often used as a condiment or side dish

Etymology

From German 'Bratwurst' (sausage) and 'Kraut' (cabbage, commonly sauerkraut).
