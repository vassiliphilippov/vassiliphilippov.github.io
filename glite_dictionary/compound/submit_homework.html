to submit homework

1. VERB

/ səbˈmɪt ˈhoʊmˌwɜrk / (AmE), / səbˈmɪt ˈhəʊmˌwɜːk / (BrE)
to hand in or present assignments for evaluation or grading, typically to a teacher or instructor
to turn in assignments for grading

Examples

All students must submit homework before the deadline to receive credit.
She always makes sure to submit homework through the online portal every week.
If you fail to submit homework on time, your grade may be affected.

Synset Examples

You're supposed to submit your homework online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hand in homework - to give assignments to a teacher
  • Synonym: turn in homework - to submit assignments for evaluation
