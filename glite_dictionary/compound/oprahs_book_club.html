Oprah's book club

1. PROPN

/ ˈoʊprəz bʊk klʌb / (AmE), / ˈəʊprəz bʊk klʌb / (BrE)
a series of book recommendations and discussions promoted by Oprah Winfrey, often leading to increased popularity and sales of selected books
Oprah Winfrey's influential book recommendation program

Examples

After being selected by Oprah's book club, the novel quickly became a bestseller.
Membership in Oprah's book club exposed many readers to new authors and genres.
The impact of Oprah's book club on publishing trends is widely acknowledged.

Synset Examples
