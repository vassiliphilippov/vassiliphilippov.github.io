the full story

1. NOUN

/ ðə fʊl ˈstɔri / (AmE), / ðə fʊl ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
the complete and detailed account of all events or facts surrounding a situation
complete account of events

Examples

After hearing the full story, the judge understood what really happened.
The newspaper didn’t tell the full story about the incident at the park.
Everyone was shocked when she finally revealed the full story of her disappearance.

Synset Examples

Critics argue that Ziomedia fails to report the full story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the whole story - the entirety of the facts or events
  • Antonym: part of the story - an incomplete account of events
