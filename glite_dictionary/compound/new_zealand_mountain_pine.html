New Zealand mountain pine

1. NOUN

/ nuː ˈziːlənd ˈmaʊntən paɪn / (AmE), / njuː ˈziːlənd ˈmaʊntɪn paɪn / (BrE)
a shrub native to New Zealand, known for its needle-like leaves and bushy appearance
a New Zealand shrub with needle-like leaves

Examples

The New Zealand mountain pine thrives in rocky and alpine areas across the country.
Gardeners often plant New Zealand mountain pine for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
Scientists are studying the New Zealand mountain pine to understand its role in native ecosystems.
