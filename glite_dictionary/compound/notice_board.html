notice board

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊ.tɪs ˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ.tɪs ˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a board used for posting public announcements, messages, or information, usually attached to a wall
board for displaying announcements

Examples

The meeting agenda was pinned to the notice board in the hallway.
Please check the notice board for important updates about upcoming events.
She stopped at the notice board to read the new announcements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulletin board - a board for displaying notices and announcements
  • Degree Lower: whiteboard - a glossy white surface for non-permanent markings
