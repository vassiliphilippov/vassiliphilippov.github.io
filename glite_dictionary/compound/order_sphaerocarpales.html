order Sphaerocarpales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər sfɪəˌroʊˌkɑːrˈpeɪliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə sfɪərəʊˌkɑːpˈɑːliːz / (BrE)
a small order of liverworts, sometimes classified within the order Jungermanniales
order of liverwort plants

Examples

The order Sphaerocarpales includes species with unique reproductive features among liverworts.
Taxonomists once debated whether the order Sphaerocarpales should be part of Jungermanniales.
Recent studies continue to clarify the classification of plants in the order Sphaerocarpales.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: order Jungermanniales - an order of liverworts, sometimes including Sphaerocarpales

Etymology

From New Latin 'Sphaerocarpales', based on 'Sphaerocarpus', the type genus, with '-ales' denoting an order.
