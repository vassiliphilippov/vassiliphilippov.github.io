U-Haul lesbian

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuː hɔl ˈlɛz.bi.ən / (AmE), / ˈjuː hɔːl ˈlɛz.bi.ən / (BrE)
(slang, informal) A lesbian woman who quickly moves in with a romantic partner, especially soon after beginning a relationship.
lesbian who moves in quickly with partner
informal slang

Examples

After just two dates, she joked that she was becoming a U-Haul lesbian.
Many queer women laugh about the stereotype of the U-Haul lesbian in dating culture.
They moved in together after only a week, fitting the classic U-Haul lesbian narrative.

Synset Examples

She joked about being a U-Haul lesbian after their first date.
The term U-Haul lesbian describes couples moving in quickly.
After a whirlwind romance, they became the classic U-Haul lesbians.

Etymology

From the moving truck rental company 'U-Haul' and 'lesbian', referring humorously to the stereotype of lesbians moving in together rapidly.
