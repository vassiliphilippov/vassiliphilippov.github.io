unused to

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈjuzd tu / (AmE), / ʌnˈjuːzd tuː / (BrE)
not familiar with or not accustomed to something or a situation
not accustomed to something

Examples

She felt unused to city life after years in the countryside.
I'm still unused to this much responsibility at work.
The cat seemed unused to being handled by strangers.

Synset Examples

I'm still unused to the cold weather here.
He’s unused to speaking in front of large crowds.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: accustomed to - familiar with or used to something
