stud farm

1. NOUN

/ stʌd fɑrm / (AmE), / stʌd fɑːm / (BrE)
A farm where horses are bred, especially for racing or other purposes.
farm for breeding horses

Examples

The wealthy family owned a stud farm renowned for its champion racehorses.
Many tourists visited the stud farm to see the foals during spring.
She hopes to manage a stud farm after completing her equine studies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breeding farm - a farm for breeding animals
