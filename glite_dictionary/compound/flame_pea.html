flame pea

1. NOUN

/ fleɪm pi / (AmE), / fleɪm piː / (BrE)
an Australian shrub or twining vine with yellow to orange-red flowers, belonging to the genus Chorizema or related genera
Australian shrub/vine with orange-red flowers

Examples

The flame pea is prized in gardens for its vibrant orange-red flowers.
Many species of flame pea are native to Western Australia.
She planted a flame pea near the fence to add color in spring.
