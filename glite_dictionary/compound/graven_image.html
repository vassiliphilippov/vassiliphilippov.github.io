graven image

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪ.vən ˈɪ.mɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪ.vən ˈɪ.mɪdʒ / (BrE)
an image or physical representation, especially a statue or idol, that is worshipped, often forbidden in religious contexts
idol or physical object of worship

Examples

The ancient people constructed a graven image and offered sacrifices before it.
He accused the community of turning wealth into a graven image in their lives.
The law strictly prohibited making or bowing down to any graven image.

Synset Examples

thou shalt not make unto thee any graven image
money was his god

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idol - an image or representation of a god used as an object of worship

Etymology

From graven (past participle of 'grave' meaning to carve) + image.
