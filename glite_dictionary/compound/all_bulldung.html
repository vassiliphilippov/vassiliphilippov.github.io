all bulldung

1. NOUN

/ ɔl ˈbʊlˌdʌŋ / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈbʊlˌdʌŋ / (BrE)
(informal) Complete nonsense or meaningless talk; something not to be believed.
nonsense; meaningless talk
informal

Examples

Everything he said at the meeting was just all bulldung.
She refused to believe the rumors, calling them all bulldung.
That explanation sounds like all bulldung to me.

Synset Examples

Don't listen to his excuses; it's all bulldung.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - speech or writing that has no meaning or makes no sense
  • Synonym: bullshit - (informal, vulgar) nonsense; lies or deceptive talk

Etymology

Blend of 'bull' (as in 'bullshit') and 'dung' (animal excrement), meaning nonsense.
