third class

1. NOUN

/ θɝd klæs / (AmE), / θɜːd klɑːs / (BrE)
the cheapest category of accommodations on a ship or train, typically offering basic services and limited comfort
cheapest ship or train accommodations

Examples

Many immigrants traveled in third class when coming to America by ship.
She could only afford a third class ticket for the cross-country train journey.
The conditions in third class were crowded but affordable for families.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: first class - the highest-quality accommodations on a ship or train
  • Degree Higher: second class - an intermediate category of accommodations on a ship or train

2. NOUN

/ ˌθɝd ˈklæs / (AmE), / ˌθɜːd ˈklɑːs / (BrE)
printed matter sent at a reduced postal rate, traditionally designated as a category of mail for items like advertising and circulars
type of mail at reduced postal rate

Examples

Many businesses used third class mail to send out advertising flyers to customers.
Sending the newsletter as third class significantly reduced shipping costs for the publisher.
The post office no longer offers third class rates, as services have been renamed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: first class - highest category of mail service, prioritizing speed and reliability
  • Degree Higher: second class - category of mail service, often for periodicals and newspapers
