to consolidate offices

1. VERB

/ kənˈsɑː.lə.deɪt ˈɔː.fɪs.ɪz / (AmE), / kənˈsɒ.lɪ.deɪt ˈɒf.ɪs.ɪz / (BrE)
to combine multiple separate offices into a single office or location, often for efficiency or cost-saving reasons
merge multiple offices into one location

Examples

After the recent acquisition, the company chose to consolidate offices to reduce overhead costs.
The university announced its plan to consolidate offices from several buildings into the new administration center.
By consolidating offices, the firm improved communication and streamlined workflow among its teams.

Synset Examples

They decided to consolidate their offices under one roof.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to merge - to combine two or more things, especially companies or organizations, into one
