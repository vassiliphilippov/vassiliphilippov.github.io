garbage hauler

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ ˌhɔlər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ ˌhɔːlə / (BrE)
a person or company whose job is to collect and remove refuse or waste
person or company that collects refuse

Examples

The garbage hauler comes to our neighborhood every Monday morning.
After contracting a private garbage hauler, the city improved its waste management service.
Local garbage haulers are on strike, leading to piles of trash on the streets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garbage collector - person who collects refuse or waste
  • Synonym: refuse collector - person whose job is collecting refuse
