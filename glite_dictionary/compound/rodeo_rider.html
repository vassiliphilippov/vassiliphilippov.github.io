rodeo rider

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊ.di.oʊ ˈraɪ.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.di.əʊ ˈraɪ.də / (BrE)
a person who competes in or performs horse riding, roping, and similar stunts at a rodeo
person who competes in rodeo events

Examples

The rodeo rider thrilled the crowd with his impressive bull-riding skills.
As a seasoned rodeo rider, she traveled across the country for competitions.
The documentary focused on the daily life of a young rodeo rider in Texas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cowboy - a person who herds and tends cattle, often involved in rodeos

Etymology

From Spanish *rodeo* (public cattle round-up; from *rodear*, to surround) and English *rider*.
