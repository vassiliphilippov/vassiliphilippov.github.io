onion yellow-dwarf virus

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌnjən ˈjɛloʊ ˈdɑːrf ˈvaɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈʌnjən ˈjɛləʊ ˈdɑːf ˈvaɪrəs / (BrE)
a virus that causes stunted growth and yellowing of the leaves in onion plants
a virus affecting onion plants

Examples

The onion yellow-dwarf virus can seriously reduce crop yields if not controlled.
Farmers look for early signs of onion yellow-dwarf virus in their fields each season.
Resistant onion varieties have been developed to combat the spread of onion yellow-dwarf virus.
