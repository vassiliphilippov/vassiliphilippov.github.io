Book of Wisdom

1. PROPN

/ ˈbʊk əv ˈwɪzdəm / (AmE), / ˈbʊk əv ˈwɪzdəm / (BrE)
an Apocryphal book of the Bible, traditionally ascribed to Solomon, consisting mainly of meditations on wisdom and believed to have been written in the first century BC
an Apocryphal biblical book meditating on wisdom

Examples

The Book of Wisdom is included in the Catholic and Eastern Orthodox biblical canons.
Scholars often analyze the literary style of the Book of Wisdom.
In his sermon, the priest quoted from the Book of Wisdom to illustrate his point.
