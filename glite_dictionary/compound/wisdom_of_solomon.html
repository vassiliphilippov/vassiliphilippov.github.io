Wisdom of Solomon

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪzdəm əv ˈsɑləmən / (AmE), / ˈwɪzdəm əv ˈsɒləmən / (BrE)
an Apocryphal book consisting mainly of meditations on wisdom, traditionally attributed to Solomon but likely written in the first century BC
Apocryphal book of wisdom in the Bible

Examples

The Wisdom of Solomon is included among the books of the Old Testament Apocrypha.
Many scholars believe the Wisdom of Solomon was not actually written by King Solomon.
Her essay referenced several passages from the Wisdom of Solomon to support its theme.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Book of Wisdom - another name for the Wisdom of Solomon
