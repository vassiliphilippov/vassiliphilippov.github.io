plastic film

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæstɪk fɪlm / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstɪk fɪlm / (BrE)
a thin, flexible sheet of plastic, often transparent, used to wrap or cover objects for protection or preservation
thin sheet of plastic for wrapping or covering

Examples

Please cover the leftover food with plastic film to keep it fresh.
The workers wrapped the furniture in plastic film before moving it to prevent scratches.
Greenhouses often use plastic film as a low-cost alternative to glass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cling film - thin plastic wrap for sealing food items
  • Synonym: plastic wrap - transparent plastic material for wrapping food
