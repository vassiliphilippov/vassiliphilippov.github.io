art of persuasion

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt əv pɚˈsweɪʒən / (AmE), / ɑːt əv pəˈsweɪʒən / (BrE)
the skill or ability to convince people to believe or do something
skill of convincing others

Examples

Mastering the art of persuasion can help you succeed in many areas of life.
She credits her success in negotiations to her art of persuasion.
Politicians often rely on the art of persuasion to gain support.

Synset Examples

The art of persuasion is crucial in sales.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: persuasion - the act or process of persuading someone
