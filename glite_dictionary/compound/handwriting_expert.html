handwriting expert

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændˌraɪtɪŋ ˈɛkspɚt / (AmE), / ˈhændˌraɪtɪŋ ˈɛkspɜːt / (BrE)
a specialist who analyses handwriting to infer character traits or authenticate documents
specialist in analyzing handwriting

Examples

The court hired a handwriting expert to verify the authenticity of the signature.
As a handwriting expert, she can detect subtle differences in penmanship.
The police consulted a handwriting expert to analyze the ransom note.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graphologist - a person who studies handwriting to analyze character
