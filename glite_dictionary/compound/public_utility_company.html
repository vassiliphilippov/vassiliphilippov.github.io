public utility company

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪk juːˈtɪlɪti ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk juːˈtɪlɪti ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a company that provides essential public services such as water, electricity, or gas, and is regulated by the government
company providing regulated public services

Examples

The public utility company announced a rate increase for electricity starting next month.
After the storm, the public utility company worked quickly to restore water to the affected neighborhoods.
Many residents called the public utility company to report outages during the severe weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utility company - a company that supplies essential services such as water or electricity
