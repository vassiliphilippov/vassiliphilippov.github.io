player hater

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪ.ɚ ˌheɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪ.ə ˌheɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person who expresses jealousy or hostility toward someone else's romantic or social success, especially in dating
jealous critic of others' success, especially in dating
slang informal

Examples

Some people will always be a player hater when they see someone happy in a relationship.
Ignore the player haters and focus on your own happiness.
He was labeled a player hater after spreading rumors about his friend's new girlfriend.

Synset Examples

Her friends call her a player hater for always criticizing couples.
He’s such a player hater; he can't stand anyone else dating.
I ignore the player haters and focus on my goals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hater - a person who dislikes a particular person or thing intensely

Etymology

Formed from slang sense of 'player' (someone successful or active in dating) plus 'hater' (one who dislikes or criticizes).
