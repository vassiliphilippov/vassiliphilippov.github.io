boob light

1. NOUN

/ buːb laɪt / (AmE), / buːb laɪt / (BrE)
an informal term for a domed ceiling light fixture that is typically inexpensive and considered unattractive, resembling a woman's breast in shape
basic domed ceiling light fixture
informal

Examples

The rental apartment had a typical boob light in every room.
She quickly swapped out the old boob light for something more modern.
Many people dislike the look of a boob light but keep them for their affordability.

Synset Examples

They replaced the old fixture with a stylish boob light.
She prefers elegant chandeliers over common boob lights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ceiling light - a light fixture mounted on the ceiling

Etymology

From 'boob' (slang for breast) and 'light', referring to its resemblance to a woman's breast.
