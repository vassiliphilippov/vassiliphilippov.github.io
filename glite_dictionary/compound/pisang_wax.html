pisang wax

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːsɑŋ wæks / (AmE), / ˈpiːsæŋ wæks / (BrE)
a natural wax obtained from the leaves of the plantain tree, used in various industrial applications
wax from plantain leaves

Examples

The laboratory used pisang wax as a coating in several experiments.
Producers extract pisang wax from plantain leaves for commercial use.
Pisang wax is valued for its glossy finish and water-resistant properties.

Etymology

From Malay 'pisang' meaning 'banana' or 'plantain'.
