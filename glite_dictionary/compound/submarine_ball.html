submarine ball

1. NOUN

/ ˌsʌbməˈrin bɔl / (AmE), / ˌsʌbməˈriːn bɔːl / (BrE)
In baseball, a pitch delivered using a sidearm or underhand motion rather than the conventional overhead throw.
sidearm baseball pitch

Examples

The pitcher surprised the batter with a submarine ball that curved sharply across the plate.
Throwing a submarine ball can be challenging for hitters used to traditional pitching styles.
He developed a reputation for his accurate and deceptive submarine ball delivery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sidearm pitch - a pitch delivered with the arm swung sideways
