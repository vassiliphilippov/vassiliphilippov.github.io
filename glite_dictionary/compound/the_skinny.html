the skinny

1. NOUN

/ ðə ˈskɪni / (AmE), / ðə ˈskɪni / (BrE)
slang term for the essential or most useful information about something; inside information.
inside information; the essential facts
slang

Examples

I want the skinny on what really happened at the meeting.
She gave me the skinny about their secret project before anyone else knew.
If you need the skinny on the latest trends, talk to Mia.

Synset Examples
