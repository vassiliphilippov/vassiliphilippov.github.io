spack attack

1. NOUN

/ spæk əˈtæk / (AmE), / spæk əˈtæk / (BrE)
a sudden outburst of frustration, anger, or energy, especially in response to a stressful situation
sudden burst of frustration or anger
informal

His spack attack lasted only a few minutes, but everyone cleared the room.
Every time the project hits a setback, her spack attack pushes the team to keep going.
