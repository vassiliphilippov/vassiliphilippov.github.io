leaf beetle

1. NOUN

/ lif ˈbit̬əl / (AmE), / liːf ˈbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a brightly colored beetle that feeds on plant leaves; its larvae may infest roots and stems
type of beetle feeding on plant leaves

Examples

The garden was damaged by a leaf beetle infestation this summer.
Farmers often check their crops for signs of leaf beetles eating the foliage.
Scientists are studying the leaf beetle to understand its impact on agriculture.
