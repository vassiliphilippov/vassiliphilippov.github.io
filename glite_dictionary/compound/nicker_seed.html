nicker seed

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪkər siːd / (AmE), / ˈnɪkə siːd / (BrE)
the hard, shiny, grey seed of the bonduc tree, often used for making jewelry and decorative items
seed of the bonduc tree, used in jewelry

Examples

She wore a necklace made of polished nicker seeds collected from the beach.
The children spent the afternoon searching for nicker seeds in the sand dunes.
Many artisans use nicker seed to create unique pieces of jewelry.
