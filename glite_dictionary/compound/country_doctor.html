country doctor

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌntri ˈdɑktər / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri ˈdɒktə / (BrE)
a doctor who works and provides medical care in rural areas, often distant from modern hospitals
doctor in rural areas

Examples

The country doctor would often travel miles on horseback to visit his patients.
Growing up, our family relied on a country doctor who knew everyone in the community.
Many country doctors still make house calls when patients can't get to the clinic.

Synset Examples

do country doctors still make house calls?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physician - a person qualified to practice medicine, especially not in surgery
  • Antonym: urban doctor - a doctor who works in a city or urban area
