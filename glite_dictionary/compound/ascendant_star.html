ascendant star

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɛndənt stɑr / (AmE), / əˈsɛndənt stɑː / (BrE)
a person or thing that is rising to prominence or gaining influence in a particular field
person rising to prominence

Examples

The film industry is always looking for the next ascendant star to headline major projects.
As an ascendant star in politics, her speeches attract national attention.
Many see him as an ascendant star destined to lead the company.

Synset Examples

The ascendant star is captivating audiences everywhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rising star - someone who is quickly becoming successful
  • Degree Higher: superstar - a very famous and successful person, especially in entertainment or sports
