global climate change

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡloʊbəl ˈklaɪmət tʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊbəl ˈklaɪmət tʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
A significant and lasting alteration in the Earth's overall climate patterns, especially those resulting from human activity.
worldwide changes in climate patterns

Examples

Many scientists agree that global climate change is already affecting weather patterns worldwide.
Governments are taking action to slow the progress of global climate change.
The effects of global climate change may be felt most strongly in coastal communities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: climate change - a long-term change in Earth's climate
  • Synonym: global warming - the long-term rise in the average temperature of the Earth’s climate system
