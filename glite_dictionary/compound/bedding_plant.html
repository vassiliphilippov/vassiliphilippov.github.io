bedding plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdɪŋ plænt / (AmE), / ˈbɛdɪŋ plɑːnt / (BrE)
an ornamental plant grown specifically for planting in flowerbeds, typically for seasonal display in gardens or public spaces
ornamental plant for flowerbeds

Examples

The gardener chose vibrant bedding plants to create a colorful border along the pathway.
Petunias and marigolds are popular choices as bedding plants for summer displays.
In spring, the parks department replaces old flowers with fresh bedding plants to brighten the city gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annual - a plant that completes its life cycle in one year, often used as a bedding plant
