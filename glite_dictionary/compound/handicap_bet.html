handicap bet

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændiˌkæp bɛt / (AmE), / ˈhændɪˌkap bɛt / (BrE)
a wager in which a weaker competitor is given a virtual advantage or head start by the bookmaker to make the contest more even
wager with virtual head start for underdog

Examples

He decided to place a handicap bet on the underdog, hoping the head start would make a difference.
With a handicap bet, you can back the weaker team even if they don't win outright.
The odds for a handicap bet are often more appealing to experienced gamblers.

Synset Examples

You can place a 0-2 handicap bet on Brazil vs. Cyprus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spread bet - a wager based on the margin of victory, similar in intention to a handicap bet
