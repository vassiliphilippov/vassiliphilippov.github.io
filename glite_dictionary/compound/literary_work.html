literary work

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬əˌrɛri wɝːk / (AmE), / ˈlɪtərəri wɜːk / (BrE)
A piece of writing that is imaginative or creative, such as a novel, poem, or play.
piece of creative writing

Examples

The novel is considered an important literary work of the twentieth century.
Her collection of poems has been praised as a remarkable literary work.
Students were asked to analyze a famous literary work for their final essay.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: novel - a long written story about imaginary people and events
  • Synonym: poem - a piece of writing in verse form, usually expressing emotions or ideas
