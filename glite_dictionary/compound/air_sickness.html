air sickness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌsɪk.nəs / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌsɪk.nəs / (BrE)
a feeling of nausea and discomfort caused by motion during air travel, especially as a result of turbulence
nausea during air travel
medical

Examples

Many passengers experienced air sickness during the turbulent flight.
She always carries medication to prevent air sickness when flying long distances.
The airline distributed bags in case anyone suffered from air sickness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motion sickness - nausea caused by movement during travel, such as by ship, car, or plane
