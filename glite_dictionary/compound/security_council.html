Security Council

1. PROPN

/ sɪˈkjʊr.ɪ.ti ˈkaʊn.səl / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊə.rɪ.ti ˈkaʊn.səl / (BrE)

UN's main peacekeeping council

Examples

The Security Council convened an emergency meeting to address the escalating conflict.
China is one of the five permanent members of the Security Council.
The United Nations Security Council passed a resolution calling for a ceasefire.
