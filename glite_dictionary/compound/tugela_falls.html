Tugela Falls

1. PROPN

/ tuˈɡeɪlə fɔlz / (AmE), / tjuːˈɡeɪlə fɔːlz / (BrE)
a major multi-tiered waterfall located in the Drakensberg Mountains of South Africa, known as one of the highest waterfalls in the world
major waterfall in South Africa

Examples

Tourists from around the world visit Tugela Falls to witness its breathtaking cascades.
The source of Tugela Falls is high in the Drakensberg Mountains in South Africa.
At 948 meters, Tugela Falls is considered one of the tallest waterfalls on Earth.

Etymology

Named after the Tugela River, itself from Zulu 'Thukela' meaning 'the sudden one' or 'startling'.
