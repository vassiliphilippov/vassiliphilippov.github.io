social affair

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl əˈfɛr / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl əˈfeə / (BrE)
a formal or official social gathering, party, or ceremony attended by people
formal social gathering or ceremony

Examples

The annual charity ball is a notable social affair in the city.
She was invited to a social affair honoring the new ambassador.
Every year, their wedding anniversary is celebrated with a lavish social affair.

Synset Examples

the party was quite an affair
an occasion arranged to honor the president
a seemingly endless round of social functions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: social event - an organized gathering for social purposes
  • Synonym: party - a social gathering for celebration or recreation
  • Synonym: ceremony - a formal religious or public occasion
