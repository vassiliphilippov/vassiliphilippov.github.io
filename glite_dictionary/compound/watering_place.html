watering place

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ərɪŋ pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtərɪŋ pleɪs / (BrE)
a resort or location, often near a spring or at the seaside, where people go for health and relaxation
health resort near a spring or seaside
archaic

Examples

Many Victorian-era travelers flocked to the watering place during the summer months for its therapeutic waters.
She spent a month at a coastal watering place to recover her health.
The novel describes the social scene at a fashionable watering place by the sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spa - health resort with mineral springs
