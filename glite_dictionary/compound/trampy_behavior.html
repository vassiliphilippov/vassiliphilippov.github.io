trampy behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈtræmpi bɪˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / ˈtræmpi bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
inappropriate or promiscuous conduct, especially of a sexual nature; behavior considered improper or lacking in decency
inappropriate or promiscuous conduct
informal

Examples

Her friends were concerned about her trampy behavior at parties.
Rumors about his trampy behavior quickly spread around the office.
The principal warned students that trampy behavior would not be tolerated at school events.

Synset Examples

His trampy behavior at the club was embarrassing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promiscuous behavior - having or involving many transient sexual relationships
  • Antonym: modest behavior - showing decency and propriety
