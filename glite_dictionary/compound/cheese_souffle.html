cheese souffle

1. NOUN

/ tʃiz suːˈfleɪ / (AmE), / tʃiːz suːˈfleɪ / (BrE)
A light, puffy baked dish made with cheese, eggs (with the whites beaten separately), and a white sauce.
light, puffy baked cheese dish

Examples

She ordered a cheese souffle as her main course at the French restaurant.
The cheese souffle puffed up beautifully in the oven but soon collapsed.
Would you like to try making a cheese souffle for the dinner party tonight?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: souffle - a light, fluffy baked dish made with egg yolks and beaten egg whites, sweet or savory

Etymology

Soufflé from French souffler (to blow, puff).
