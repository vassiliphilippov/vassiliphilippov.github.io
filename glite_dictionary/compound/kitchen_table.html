kitchen table

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtʃən ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃɪn ˈteɪbəl / (BrE)
a table that is located in or used for dining in the kitchen
table in a kitchen

Examples

They gathered around the kitchen table to discuss their plans for the weekend.
A vase of fresh flowers sat in the center of the kitchen table.
The children did their homework at the old wooden kitchen table every afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dining table - a table at which meals are eaten
