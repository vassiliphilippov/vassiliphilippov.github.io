shower stall

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃaʊɚ stɔl / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊə stɔːl / (BrE)
a small enclosed area in a bathroom, equipped with a shower for washing yourself
enclosed area with a shower

Examples

The new apartment has a modern shower stall in the master bathroom.
She slipped on the wet floor while stepping out of the shower stall.
Cleaning the shower stall is part of my weekly chores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shower cubicle - enclosure for showering, similar meaning
