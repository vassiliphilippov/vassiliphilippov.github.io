heart specialist

1. NOUN

/ hɑrt ˈspɛʃəlɪst / (AmE), / hɑːt ˈspɛʃəlɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in diagnosing and treating diseases of the heart
doctor specializing in heart diseases

Examples

My father was referred to a heart specialist after his ECG showed irregularities.
As a heart specialist, she has performed hundreds of cardiac procedures each year.
If you have persistent chest pain, it's best to see a heart specialist without delay.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardiologist - a doctor who specializes in diseases of the heart
