electric drill

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk drɪl / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk drɪl / (BrE)
a hand-held power tool with a rotating drill bit, powered by an electric motor, used for drilling holes
power tool with rotating bit, electrically powered

Examples

He used an electric drill to make a hole in the wall.
The electric drill comes with several different drill bits for various materials.
After charging the battery, the electric drill was ready for the next project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power drill - a general term for drills powered by electricity, battery, or other sources
