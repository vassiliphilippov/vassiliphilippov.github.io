total spazmo

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊtl ˈspæzmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtl ˈspæzməʊ / (BrE)
A slang term for a person who is extremely energetic, excitable, or clumsy, often to a noticeable or amusing degree.
very excitable or clumsy person (slang)
slang informal

Examples

Whenever there's a party, Jake turns into a total spazmo and dances wildly.
Don't be such a total spazmo—you knocked over the lamp again!
She was acting like a total spazmo after drinking all that soda.

Synset Examples

He acts like a total spazmo when he's excited.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to spaz - a clumsy or foolish person (slang)

Etymology

Formed from 'total' (complete) and 'spazmo,' an alteration of 'spaz' (slang for a clumsy person).
