armored dinosaur

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmɚd ˈdaɪnəˌsɔr / (AmE), / ˈɑːməd ˈdʌɪnəsɔː / (BrE)
a dinosaur characterized by the presence of bony armor plates or structures covering its body, such as the ankylosaur or stegosaur
dinosaur with bony armor

Examples

The skeleton of an armored dinosaur was discovered in the Alberta Badlands.
Scientists believe the bony plates on an armored dinosaur helped protect it from predators.
The museum's new exhibit features a life-sized model of an armored dinosaur.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ankylosaur - a type of heavily armored herbivorous dinosaur
  • Synonym: stegosaur - a dinosaur with distinctive back plates, an example of armored dinosaurs
