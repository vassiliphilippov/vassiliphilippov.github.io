simple cami

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪmpəl ˈkæmi / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpəl ˈkæmi / (BrE)
a basic, unadorned camisole top, typically sleeveless and lightweight
basic camisole top
informal

Examples

She wore a simple cami under her blazer for a polished yet relaxed look.
A white simple cami is a versatile piece for any summer wardrobe.
Layering a simple cami beneath a sweater adds comfort and style without bulk.

Synset Examples

A simple cami can elevate any casual outfit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camisole - a woman's loose-fitting undergarment for the upper body

Etymology

Informal shortening of 'camisole', from French 'camisole'.
