love of someone's life

1. NOUN

/ lʌv əv ˈsʌmwʌnz laɪf / (AmE), / lʌv əv ˈsʌmwʌnz laɪf / (BrE)
the person someone loves most deeply and considers the most important romantic partner in their life
most significant romantic partner

Examples

He always said that Maria was the love of his life, even after decades of marriage.
When she met Daniel, she knew she had found the love of her life.
After losing the love of his life, he struggled to move on.

Synset Examples

She believes he's the love of her life.
