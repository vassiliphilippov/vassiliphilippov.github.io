farm animal

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑrm ˌænɪməl / (AmE), / ˈfɑːm ˌænɪməl / (BrE)
an animal that is kept on a farm for use or profit, such as livestock or poultry
animal kept on a farm for use or profit

Examples

The children learned how to care for a farm animal during their visit to the countryside.
Raising a farm animal requires knowledge of feeding and health care.
She has always dreamed of adopting a farm animal from a local rescue organization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: livestock - animals, such as cattle or sheep, kept for farming purposes
