cargo hatch

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrɡoʊ hætʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːɡəʊ hætʃ / (BrE)
a hatch that provides access to the cargo compartment of a vessel or aircraft
hatch to access cargo compartment

Examples

The crew opened the cargo hatch to load the supplies onto the ship.
Access to the luggage was only possible through the rear cargo hatch.
A sudden gust of wind slammed the cargo hatch shut during unloading.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hatch - an opening in a ship's deck or aircraft for access or loading
