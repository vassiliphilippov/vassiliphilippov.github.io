purplish blue

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝː.plɪʃ bluː / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.plɪʃ bluː / (BrE)
a shade of blue that has a tinge of purple
a blue shade with a hint of purple

Examples

The artist chose a purplish blue to paint the evening sky in her landscape.
His shirt was a striking purplish blue that complemented his dark jeans.
The flower petals displayed a delicate purplish blue hue in the morning light.
