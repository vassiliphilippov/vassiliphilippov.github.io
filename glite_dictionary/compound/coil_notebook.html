coil notebook

1. NOUN

/ kɔɪl ˈnoʊtˌbʊk / (AmE), / kɔɪl ˈnəʊtˌbʊk / (BrE)
A notebook bound with a spiral or coil of wire or plastic, allowing pages to turn easily and lie flat.
notebook with spiral coil binding

Examples

She took a coil notebook from her bag to jot down some ideas during the meeting.
The students were required to use a coil notebook for all their science notes.
I prefer a coil notebook because the pages can be easily turned and folded back.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiral notebook - notebook with a metal or plastic spiral binding
