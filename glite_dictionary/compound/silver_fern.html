silver fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ fɝn / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə fɜːn / (BrE)
a tropical American fern with fronds that have white undersides
fern with white undersides of fronds

Examples

The silver fern is easily identified by the pale color beneath its fronds.
Many gardeners admire the silver fern for its distinctive appearance.
In its native habitat, the silver fern thrives in humid, shaded areas.

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə fɜːn / (BrE)
a southern African fern with fronds that have white undersides, known for its silvery appearance
African fern with silvery undersides

Examples

The silver fern grows abundantly in the forests of southern Africa.
Botanists admire the silver fern for the striking color on the underside of its leaves.
A cluster of silver ferns added an exotic touch to the conservatory.
