college level

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑlɪdʒ ˌlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒlɪdʒ ˌlɛvəl / (BrE)
the standard of knowledge or skill expected of a student at a college or university
standard of college education

Examples

The course is taught at the college level, so it requires advanced reading skills.
She is taking mathematics at a college level to prepare for university.
Many employers seek candidates who have college-level writing and analytical abilities.
