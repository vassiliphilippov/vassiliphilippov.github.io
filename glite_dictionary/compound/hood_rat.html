hood rat

1. NOUN

/ hʊd ræt / (AmE), / hʊd ræt / (BrE)
(slang, often derogatory) A person, usually from a poor urban environment, who is perceived as engaging in or associated with street culture or delinquent behavior.
slang; person from poor urban background
slang offensive

Examples

The movie portrayed her as a tough hood rat.
The rapper spoke proudly of his past as a hood rat.
Some people unfairly judge anyone from the neighborhood as a hood rat.

Synset Examples

The movie portrayed her as a tough hood rat.
They knew he was a hood rat by his attitude.
He grew up as a hood rat in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: streetwise - knowledgeable about the ways of urban life; clever in dealing with difficult situations in a city

Etymology

From 'hood', slang for 'neighborhood' (urban area), combined with 'rat', indicating a person who frequents such areas.
