angel baby

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪndʒəl ˈbeɪbi / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəl ˈbeɪbi / (BrE)
A very cute or innocent-looking child or baby, often used as a term of endearment.
a very cute or innocent baby or child

Examples

Everyone said her newborn nephew was an angel baby because of his sweet smile.
She dressed her angel baby in a soft white blanket for the photoshoot.
Whenever he napped peacefully, his mother would whisper, 'My little angel baby.'

Synset Examples

Every time he smiled, he looked like an angel baby.
The parents called their daughter their angel baby.
She cuddled her newborn, her little angel baby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cherub - an innocent-looking, chubby child, often depicted as a small angel
