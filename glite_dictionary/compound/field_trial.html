field trial

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːld ˌtraɪəl / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌtraɪəl / (BrE)
a competitive test of hunting dogs in natural conditions to evaluate their skill in pointing, retrieving, or following game
competitive test for hunting dogs

Examples

She entered her dog in a field trial to test its natural hunting instincts.
The judges at the field trial observed each dog's ability to locate and retrieve game.
Winning a field trial is a prestigious achievement for any hunting dog owner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trial - a test, usually in a formal or competitive context
