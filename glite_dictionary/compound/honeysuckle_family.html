honeysuckle family

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈhʌnɪˌsʌkəl ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of shrubs, small trees, and woody vines that includes the honeysuckle and related plants (family Caprifoliaceae)
family of shrubs and vines (Caprifoliaceae)

Examples

The honeysuckle family contains many ornamental garden plants.
Several species in the honeysuckle family are grown for their fragrant flowers.
Botanists studied the diversity within the honeysuckle family across different continents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Caprifoliaceae - the scientific name for the honeysuckle family
