yellow woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
An offensive and derogatory term for an Asian woman, based on racist stereotypes.
offensive term for an Asian woman
offensive slang
