coffee-and-chat

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːfi ənd tʃæt / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ənd tʃat / (BrE)
an informal meeting where people gather to talk and drink coffee together
informal meeting for conversation over coffee
informal

Examples

Our community center hosts a coffee-and-chat every Wednesday morning for newcomers.
She invited her friends over for a relaxed coffee-and-chat after work.
A coffee-and-chat is a great way to catch up with old colleagues.

Synset Examples

They organized a coffee-and-chat session every Friday.
