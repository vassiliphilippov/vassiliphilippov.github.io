strong-smelling

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrɔŋˌsmɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstrɒŋˌsmɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
Having a powerful or noticeable odor; emitting a strong smell.
having a powerful or noticeable odor

Examples

The strong-smelling cheese filled the room with its scent.
She avoided using strong-smelling cleaning products because of her allergies.
A strong-smelling perfume can sometimes be overwhelming in a small space.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pungent - having a strong, sharp taste or smell
  • Antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant, sweet smell
