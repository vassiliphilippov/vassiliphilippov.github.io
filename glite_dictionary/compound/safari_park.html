safari park

1. NOUN

/ səˈfɑri pɑrk / (AmE), / səˈfɑːri pɑːk / (BrE)
an area of parkland where wild animals are kept and can be observed by visitors driving through in vehicles
park with free-roaming wild animals for visitors to drive through

Examples

We took the children to a safari park where they saw lions and giraffes roaming freely.
Visitors are required to stay inside their vehicles while driving through the safari park.
The new safari park specializes in conservation and breeding of endangered species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zoo - an establishment where animals are kept for exhibition

Etymology

From Swahili 'safari' (journey) and English 'park'.
