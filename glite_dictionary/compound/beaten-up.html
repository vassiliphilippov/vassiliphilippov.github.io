beaten-up

1. ADJ

/ ˈbitən ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈbiːtən ˌʌp / (BrE)
(of an object) damaged or worn as a result of repeated blows or hard use.
damaged or worn from use

Examples

He drove a beaten-up pickup truck that looked like it might break down any minute.
The chair was old and beaten-up, but it was surprisingly comfortable.
They found a beaten-up suitcase in the attic filled with old photographs.

Synset Examples

a battered old car
the beaten-up old Ford

Related Terms

  • Synonym: battered - damaged by repeated blows or hard usage
