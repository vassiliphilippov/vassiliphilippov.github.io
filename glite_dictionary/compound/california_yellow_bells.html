California yellow bells

1. NOUN

/ kəˌlɪfɔrnjə ˈjɛloʊ bɛlz / (AmE), / kəˌlɪfəˈnɪə ˈjɛləʊ bɛlz / (BrE)
A sticky herb of arid or desert regions in the southwestern United States, with hanging yellow flowers.
desert herb with yellow flowers

Examples

We spotted several California yellow bells growing along the edge of the desert trail.
The pendulous yellow flowers of California yellow bells make them stand out in arid landscapes.
Botanists study how California yellow bells adapt to the dry climate of the American Southwest.
