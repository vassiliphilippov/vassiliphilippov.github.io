up the stairs

1. ADV

/ ʌp ðə stɛrz / (AmE), / ʌp ðə steəz / (BrE)
on or to a higher floor of a building, especially by using the stairs
on a higher floor; to a higher level via stairs

Examples

She heard a strange noise up the stairs and decided to investigate.
You can find the manager’s office up the stairs on the left.
Whenever I feel tired, walking up the stairs is a real challenge.

Synset Examples

they lived upstairs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: upstairs - on or to an upper floor
  • Antonym: down the stairs - on or to a lower floor, via stairs
