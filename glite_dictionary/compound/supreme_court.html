Supreme Court

1. PROPN

/ səˈprim kɔrt / (AmE), / suːˈpriːm kɔːt / (BrE)
the highest federal court in the United States, which has final authority over all other courts in the nation
highest court in the United States
legal american

Examples

The Supreme Court will hear arguments in the landmark case tomorrow.
Decisions made by the Supreme Court are binding on all other American courts.
She hopes to one day argue a case before the Supreme Court.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: court - a tribunal presided over by a judge or judges
  • Degree Lower: appeals court - a court that hears appeals from lower court decisions
