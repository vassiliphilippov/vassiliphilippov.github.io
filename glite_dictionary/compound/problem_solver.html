problem solver

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːbləm ˌsɑːlvər / (AmE), / ˈprɒbləm ˌsɒlvə / (BrE)
a person who finds solutions to difficult or complex issues, often by analyzing and synthesizing information
person skilled at solving problems

Examples

Her reputation as a problem solver made her a valuable asset to the company.
The team needed a creative problem solver to tackle the unexpected challenges.
He prides himself on being a problem solver rather than someone who gives up easily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troubleshooter - a person who solves serious problems for an organization or system
  • Antonym: problem creator - a person who causes problems
