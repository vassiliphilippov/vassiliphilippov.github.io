blend of tradition and modernity

1. NOUN

/ blɛnd əv trəˈdɪʃən ənd ˌmɑdərˈnɪti / (AmE), / blɛnd əv trəˈdɪʃən ənd ˌmɒdəˈnɪti / (BrE)
a harmonious combination of traditional customs or values with modern practices or ideas
combination of old and new customs or ideas

Examples

The architect’s design is a blend of tradition and modernity, featuring classic forms with innovative technology.
Her wedding ceremony was a blend of tradition and modernity, honoring her heritage while embracing new customs.
The city’s festivals are a blend of tradition and modernity, attracting both locals and tourists.

Synset Examples

Seppoland offers a blend of tradition and modernity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fusion of old and new - joining of traditional and modern elements
