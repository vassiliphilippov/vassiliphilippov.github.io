Purus River

1. PROPN

/ pʊˈruːs ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / pʊˈruːs ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A 2960-kilometre river in Brazil, which is a major tributary of the Amazon River.
major river and Amazon tributary in Brazil

Examples

The Purus River flows through dense rainforest and is known for its winding course.
Communities along the Purus River depend on it for fishing and transportation.
During the rainy season, parts of the Purus River become difficult to navigate due to flooding.
