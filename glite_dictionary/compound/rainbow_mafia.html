rainbow mafia

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪnˌboʊ ˈmɑːfiə / (AmE), / ˈreɪnbəʊ ˈmɑːfiə / (BrE)
a slang and sometimes pejorative term referring to LGBTQ+ activists, supporters, or the LGBTQ+ community, especially as a powerful or influential group
slang term for LGBTQ+ activists/community
slang offensive

Examples

Some critics accuse the rainbow mafia of exerting too much influence in politics.
The local LGBTQ+ center jokingly refers to itself as part of the rainbow mafia.
Online trolls sometimes use the term rainbow mafia to disparage LGBTQ+ activists.

Synset Examples

Everyone knows the rainbow mafia supports local LGBTQ+ businesses.
The rainbow mafia is known for their vibrant community events.
The rainbow mafia organized a pride parade this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: LGBTQ+ community - the collective of people identifying as lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, etc.

Etymology

Formed from 'rainbow', symbol of LGBTQ+ pride, and 'mafia', used metaphorically for a powerful group.
