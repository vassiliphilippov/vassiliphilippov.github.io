spring squill

1. NOUN

/ sprɪŋ skwɪl / (AmE), / sprɪŋ skwɪl / (BrE)
a European flowering plant (Scilla verna) with small blue or purple flowers, commonly found in coastal areas
European plant with blue or purple flowers

Examples

The cliffs were covered with blooming spring squill in early May.
Botanists documented the presence of spring squill in the coastal grasslands.
She picked a bouquet of spring squill during her walk along the shore.
