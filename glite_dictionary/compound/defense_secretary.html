Defense Secretary

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛns ˈsɛkrəˌtɛri / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns ˈsɛkrətri / (BrE)
the head of the Department of Defense, a senior government official responsible for national defense policy and military affairs in the United States.
head of the US Department of Defense
american

Examples

The president appointed a new Defense Secretary after the previous one resigned.
The Defense Secretary announced changes to military strategy during the press conference.
Historically, the Defense Secretary plays a crucial role in coordinating national security policies.

Synset Examples

the position of Defense Secretary was created in 1947

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Secretary of Defense - official US title for head of the Department of Defense
