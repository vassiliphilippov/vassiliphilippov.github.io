green goddess

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin ˈɡɑdəs / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈɡɒdəs / (BrE)
(slang) Marijuana; cannabis, especially when referring to its green color.
slang term for marijuana
slang

Examples

Some people refer to marijuana as the green goddess because of its color and potency.
He whispered that he was looking for the green goddess at the festival.
The scent of the green goddess filled the entire room during the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marijuana - a psychoactive drug from the Cannabis plant
