pale yellow

1. NOUN

/ peɪl ˈjɛloʊ / (AmE), / peɪl ˈjɛləʊ / (BrE)
a light shade of yellow, often appearing dull or diluted by mixing with white
a light, dull yellow color

Examples

The walls of the kitchen were painted a soothing pale yellow.
She chose a dress in pale yellow for the summer party.
The morning light gave the room a soft pale yellow glow.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: yellow - a bright primary color between green and orange in the spectrum
