stuffing nut

1. NOUN

/ ˈstʌf.ɪŋ nʌt / (AmE), / ˈstʌf.ɪŋ nʌt / (BrE)
a nut used to compress packing in a stuffing box to prevent leakage around a moving shaft
nut used to tighten a stuffing box

Examples

The engineer tightened the stuffing nut to stop the leak around the pump shaft.
Every month, the stuffing nut should be checked for proper tension to maintain a good seal.
A worn stuffing nut can allow oil to escape from the machinery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stuffing box - a device that prevents leakage along a rotating or reciprocating shaft
