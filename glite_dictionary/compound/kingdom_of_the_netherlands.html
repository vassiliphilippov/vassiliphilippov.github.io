Kingdom of the Netherlands

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm əv ðə ˈnɛdərləndz / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm əv ðə ˈnɛdələndz / (BrE)
the sovereign state comprising the Netherlands, Aruba, Curaçao, and Sint Maarten as constituent countries
sovereign state with four constituent countries

Examples

The Kingdom of the Netherlands includes several territories in the Caribbean, not just the European country.
Aruba is one of the four constituent countries within the Kingdom of the Netherlands.
Since 2010, Curaçao has held the status of a country within the Kingdom of the Netherlands.
