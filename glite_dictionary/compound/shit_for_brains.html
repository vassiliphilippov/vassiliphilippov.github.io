shit for brains

1. NOUN

/ ʃɪt fər breɪnz / (AmE), / ʃɪt fə breɪnz / (BrE)
a very foolish or incompetent person (used as a strong vulgar insult)
an extremely foolish or incompetent person (vulgar insult)
slang vulgar

Examples

He called me a shit for brains after I forgot the keys again.
Only a shit for brains would try to fix electrical wiring without turning off the power.
Stop acting like a shit for brains and read the instructions first.
