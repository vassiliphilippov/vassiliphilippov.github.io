black ace

1. NOUN

/ blæk eɪs / (AmE), / blæk eɪs / (BrE)
a playing card with a single black ace symbol (spades or clubs); figuratively, a key person or decisive factor, often associated with clever maneuvers
an ace of spades or clubs; key, decisive person or move
