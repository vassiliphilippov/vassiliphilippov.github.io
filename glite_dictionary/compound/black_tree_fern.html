black tree fern

1. NOUN

/ blæk tri fɝn / (AmE), / blæk tri fɜːn / (BrE)
a large and ornamental tree fern native to New Zealand and Australia, noted for its crown of pinnate fronds with whitish undersides.
a tree fern from New Zealand and Australia

Examples

The black tree fern adds a lush, tropical feel to gardens in temperate climates.
Many forests in New Zealand are dominated by the majestic black tree fern.
Strolling through the park, I admired the elegant fronds of the black tree fern arching gracefully overhead.
