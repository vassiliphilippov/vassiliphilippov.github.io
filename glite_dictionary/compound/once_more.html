once more

1. ADV

/ wʌns mɔr / (AmE), / wʌns mɔː / (BrE)
again; one more time or on another occasion.
again; one more time

Examples

Could you repeat the question once more so I can be sure I understand it?
After failing the first time, she decided to try once more.
He looked at the document once more before signing it.

Synset Examples

she tried again
they rehearsed the scene again

Related Terms

  • Synonym: again - another time; once more
