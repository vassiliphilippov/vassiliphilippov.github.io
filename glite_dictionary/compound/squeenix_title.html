Squeenix title

1. NOUN

/ ˈskwiː.nɪks ˈtaɪ.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˈskwiː.nɪks ˈtaɪ.tl̩ / (BrE)
a video game, especially a role-playing game, published by Square Enix
Square Enix-published video game
informal

Examples

Many fans were excited when the latest Squeenix title was announced at the convention.
I'll always remember my first Squeenix title; it was an epic adventure.
Her collection includes every Squeenix title released in the past decade.

Synset Examples

Have you played the latest Squeenix title?
I'm a huge fan of Squeenix's RPGs.

Etymology

Blend of 'Square Enix' (the Japanese game publisher) and 'title', meaning a released work.
