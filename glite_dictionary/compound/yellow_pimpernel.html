yellow pimpernel

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛl.oʊ ˈpɪm.pər.nəl / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊ ˈpɪm.pə.nəl / (BrE)
a trailing European evergreen plant with yellow flowers, commonly found in damp woodlands
European evergreen plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The yellow pimpernel thrives in the shaded, damp woodlands of Europe.
In spring, clusters of small yellow pimpernel flowers brighten the forest floor.
She collected a specimen of yellow pimpernel during her botany field trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pimpernel - any of several small plants of the genus Lysimachia or Anagallis
