violet family

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪələt ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈvaɪələt ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants known as Violaceae, which includes the genus Viola and related genera.
a family of flowering plants (Violaceae)

Examples

Many species in the violet family are valued for their attractive flowers.
The violet family includes both wild and cultivated types of violets.
Botanists classify pansies as members of the violet family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Violaceae - the botanical family to which violets belong
  • Antonym: rose family - a different family of flowering plants (Rosaceae)
