micrometer caliper

1. NOUN

/ maɪˈkrɑːˌmiːtər ˈkælɪpər / (AmE), / maɪˈkrɒmɪtə ˈkælɪpə / (BrE)
A precision caliper used for measuring very small distances or thicknesses, typically to a high degree of accuracy.
precision instrument for measuring small distances

Examples

The engineer used a micrometer caliper to check the thickness of the metal sheet.
She carefully adjusted the micrometer caliper before recording her measurements.
Accurate readings from a micrometer caliper are essential in precision engineering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vernier caliper - a caliper with a vernier scale for precise measurements
  • Degree Lower: caliper - an instrument for measuring distances or thicknesses

Etymology

From 'micrometer,' from Greek 'micros' (small) and 'metron' (measure), and 'caliper,' from French 'calibre'.
