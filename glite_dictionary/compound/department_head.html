department head

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑrtmənt hɛd / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtmənt hɛd / (BrE)
the person in charge of a department within an organization, institution, or company
chief of a department

Examples

After fifteen years with the company, she was promoted to department head.
The department head is responsible for setting annual budgets and managing staff.
I scheduled a meeting with the department head to discuss curriculum changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head of department - the leader of a department, especially in academic contexts
