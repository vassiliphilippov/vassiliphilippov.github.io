ivy family

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.vi ˈfæm.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.vi ˈfæm.ɪ.li / (BrE)
a family of mostly tropical trees, shrubs, and lianas, including the genera Panax (ginseng) and Hedera (ivies)
family of plants including ivies and ginseng

Examples

The ivy family includes plants such as English ivy and ginseng.
Many members of the ivy family are valued for their ornamental foliage.
Researchers have discovered new medicinal compounds in the ivy family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Araliaceae - the scientific name for the ivy family of plants
  • Synonym: hedge family - another term sometimes used for the Araliaceae family
