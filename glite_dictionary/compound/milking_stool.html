milking stool

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlkɪŋ stuːl / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkɪŋ stʊːl / (BrE)
a low, three-legged stool with a rounded seat, used for sitting while milking a cow
three-legged stool for milking cows

Examples

The old farmer sat on his milking stool every morning to milk the cows by hand.
She bought an antique milking stool at the country fair for her rustic kitchen.
Because the barn floor was uneven, the three-legged milking stool never wobbled.
