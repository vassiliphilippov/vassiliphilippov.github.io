fire truck

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚ ˌtrʌk / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌtrʌk / (BrE)
a large vehicle equipped for fighting fires and carrying firefighters and equipment to the site of a fire
vehicle for firefighting and firefighters

Examples

The fire truck raced down the street with its siren blaring.
Children waved excitedly as the bright red fire truck passed by.
During the parade, a vintage fire truck was on display for the public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fire engine - a vehicle equipped for putting out fires
