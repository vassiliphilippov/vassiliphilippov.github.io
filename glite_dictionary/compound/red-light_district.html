red-light district

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛd ˌlaɪt ˈdɪstrɪkt / (AmE), / ˈrɛd ˌlaɪt ˈdɪstrɪkt / (BrE)
an area of a city where there are many brothels and establishments offering sexual services
area with many brothels

Examples

Amsterdam's red-light district is famous among tourists for its vibrant nightlife and legal adult establishments.
The city council debated introducing stricter regulations in the red-light district to protect vulnerable workers.
Many guidebooks warn travelers to exercise caution when visiting the local red-light district after dark.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brothel - a house where people pay to have sex with prostitutes

Etymology

From the practice of using red lights to indicate brothels, combined with 'district' for area.
