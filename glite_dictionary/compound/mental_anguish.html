mental anguish

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛntəl ˈæŋɡwɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɛntl ˈæŋɡwɪʃ / (BrE)
Deep and lasting pain or suffering experienced in the mind, often caused by distress, anxiety, or trauma.
severe emotional suffering

Examples

After losing her job, she suffered mental anguish for several months.
The accident caused him years of mental anguish and sleepless nights.
Victims of fraud often experience intense mental anguish in addition to financial loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distress - extreme anxiety, sorrow, or pain
  • Synonym: torment - severe physical or mental suffering
  • Antonym: happiness - state of being happy; joy
