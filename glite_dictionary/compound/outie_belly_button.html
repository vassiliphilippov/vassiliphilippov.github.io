outie belly button

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊti ˈbɛli ˌbʌtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈaʊti ˈbɛli ˌbʌtn̩ / (BrE)
a belly button that sticks out rather than being recessed
belly button that protrudes outward

Examples

Some people have an outie belly button while others have an innie.
Her outie belly button became noticeable during pregnancy.
Children are often curious about whether they have an outie belly button or an innie.

Synset Examples

His outie belly button always drew comments at the beach.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: innie belly button - a belly button that is recessed or goes inward

Etymology

From 'outie' (outward-protruding) + 'belly button'. 'Outie' as opposite of 'innie'.
