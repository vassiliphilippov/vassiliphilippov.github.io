research director

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsɜrtʃ dəˈrɛktər / (AmE), / rɪˈsɜːtʃ dɪˈrɛktə / (BrE)
the person responsible for overseeing and managing research activities in a research institution or organization
supervisor in a research center

Examples

As research director, she coordinated several large-scale clinical trials.
The research director presented the annual report to the board of trustees.
Each department's research director is responsible for securing funding and setting priorities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: research supervisor - person overseeing research activities
