passenger car

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæsəndʒɚ kɑɹ / (AmE), / ˈpæsəndʒə kɑː / (BrE)
a railway vehicle designed to carry passengers
railway vehicle for passengers

Examples

The passenger car was filled with commuters heading for the city.
She reserved a seat in the first passenger car behind the locomotive.
During the journey, snacks were served to travelers in the passenger car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coach - a railway carriage for carrying passengers
  • Antonym: freight car - a railway wagon for carrying goods, not people
