cleanup event

1. NOUN

/ ˈkliːnʌp ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / ˈkliːnʌp ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
an organized activity where people gather to clean up a particular area, such as a park, street, or beach
organized activity for cleaning an area

Examples

Our neighborhood holds a cleanup event every spring to keep the park beautiful.
Volunteers teamed up for a cleanup event along the riverbank last weekend.
The school hosted a cleanup event to teach students about environmental responsibility.

Synset Examples

The climateer organized a local cleanup event.
