black cherry tree

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈtʃɛɹi tri / (AmE), / blæk ˈtʃɛɹi triː / (BrE)
A large North American tree (Prunus serotina) that bears round, black, sour edible fruit.
tree with edible black fruit

Examples

The black cherry tree blooms in spring, producing clusters of small white flowers.
Birds and other wildlife are attracted to the fruit of the black cherry tree.
The wood from a black cherry tree is prized for making furniture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cherry tree - tree that produces cherries
