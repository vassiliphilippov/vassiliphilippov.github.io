cozy home

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊzi hoʊm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊzi həʊm / (BrE)
A living space that is warm, comfortable, and inviting.
warm and comfortable living space

Examples

After a long day at work, she loved returning to her cozy home to relax.
They decorated their apartment to make it feel more like a cozy home.
A fireplace can help transform a simple house into a truly cozy home.

Synset Examples

She turned her house into a cozy home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comfortable home - a home that provides comfort and ease
  • Antonym: cold home - a home lacking warmth or comfort
