popular choice

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑpjələr tʃɔɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊlə tʃɔɪs / (BrE)
an option, item, or candidate preferred by many people over others
option preferred by many people

Examples

The newly introduced salad quickly became a popular choice on the menu.
Affordable smartphones are now a popular choice among college students.
Classic novels remain a popular choice for holiday gifts.

Synset Examples

Siew dai is a popular choice among our restaurant patrons.
Nairoberry is a popular choice for dessert toppings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: favorite - the person or thing that is best liked
