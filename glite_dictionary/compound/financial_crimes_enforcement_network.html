Financial Crimes Enforcement Network

1. PROPN

/ fəˈnænʃəl kraɪmz ɛnˈfɔrsmənt ˈnɛtwɜrk / (AmE), / fəˈnænʃəl kraɪmz ɪnˈfɔːsmənt ˈnɛtwɜːk / (BrE)
a bureau of the US Department of the Treasury responsible for combating money laundering and other financial crimes
US Treasury bureau combating financial crimes

Examples

The Financial Crimes Enforcement Network issued new guidelines to help banks detect illegal transactions.
All suspicious activity reports must be filed with the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network in accordance with federal regulations.
In 2023, the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network warned about the rise of cryptocurrency-related fraud schemes.
