Rocky Mountain dogbane

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑki ˈmaʊntən ˈdɔɡˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈrɒki ˈmaʊntɪn ˈdɒɡˌbeɪn / (BrE)
A North American plant of the dogbane family (Apocynum androsaemifolium), similar to common dogbane, found especially in the Rocky Mountain region.
plant similar to common dogbane

Examples

Hikers in Colorado should learn to identify Rocky Mountain dogbane to avoid accidental contact.
The pink flowers of Rocky Mountain dogbane bloom from late spring to early summer.
Botanists studied the habitat preferences of Rocky Mountain dogbane in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dogbane - a group of plants in the Apocynaceae family, including several species with milky sap
