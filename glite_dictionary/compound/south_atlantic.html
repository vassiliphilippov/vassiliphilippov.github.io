South Atlantic

1. NOUN

/ saʊθ ətˈlæntɪk / (AmE), / saʊθ ətˈlæntɪk / (BrE)
the part of the Atlantic Ocean that lies south of the equator
part of the Atlantic Ocean south of the equator

Examples

Many marine species in the South Atlantic are unique to its waters.
The ship's route took it across the South Atlantic from Brazil to South Africa.
Storms in the South Atlantic can be particularly violent during winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Atlantic Ocean - the second largest ocean in the world
