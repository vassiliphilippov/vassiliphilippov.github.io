deeply affected

1. ADJ

/ ˈdipli əˈfɛktɪd / (AmE), / ˈdiːpli əˈfɛktɪd / (BrE)
strongly influenced emotionally or touched by something, especially in a way that causes a significant emotional response
emotionally influenced or touched strongly

Examples

Many viewers were deeply affected by the documentary's tragic ending.
He was deeply affected by the loss of his childhood home.
The speech left everyone in the audience deeply affected.

Synset Examples

She was deeply affected by the violence she witnessed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moved - emotionally touched or stirred
