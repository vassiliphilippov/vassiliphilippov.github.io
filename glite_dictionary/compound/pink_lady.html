pink lady

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋk ˈleɪdi / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈleɪdi / (BrE)
A cocktail made with gin and brandy, lemon juice, grenadine, and egg white, shaken with ice.
cocktail with gin, brandy, grenadine, and egg white

Examples

She ordered a pink lady at the bar to start the evening.
The recipe for a classic pink lady includes gin and a splash of grenadine.
He surprised his guests with homemade pink ladies during cocktail hour.
