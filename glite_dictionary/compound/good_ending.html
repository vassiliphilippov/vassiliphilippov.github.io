good ending

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
a positive or satisfying conclusion, especially to a story, event, or experience
positive or satisfying conclusion

Examples

The novel surprised everyone by having a good ending after so much suspense.
She believes that every challenge in life can lead to a good ending.
Despite the setbacks, the play delivers a good ending that leaves the audience smiling.

Synset Examples

I love stories that have a good ending.
The movie had a surprisingly good ending.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: happy ending - a conclusion to a story in which everything turns out well
  • Antonym: bad ending - a negative or unsatisfying conclusion to a story
