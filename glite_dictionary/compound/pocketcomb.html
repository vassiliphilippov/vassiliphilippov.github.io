pocketcomb

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkətˌkoʊm / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪtˌkəʊm / (BrE)
A small comb designed to be easily carried in a pocket for grooming hair on the go.
small comb for pocket use

Examples

He reached into his jacket and pulled out a pocketcomb to fix his hair before the meeting.
Every morning, Sarah tucks her pocketcomb into her handbag for quick touch-ups during the day.
After getting caught in the wind, he was glad to have a pocketcomb handy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comb - a toothed device for arranging or grooming hair
