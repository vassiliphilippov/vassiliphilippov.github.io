whip snake

1. NOUN

/ wɪp sneɪk / (AmE), / wɪp sneɪk / (BrE)
any of several small, fast-moving snakes with long slender bodies and tails resembling a whip, found in various parts of the world
a slender, fast-moving snake with a long tail

Examples

The whip snake darted quickly through the dry grass, almost invisible except for its movement.
Several species of whip snake can be found in the rocky hills of southern Europe.
With its slender build and swift movements, the whip snake is well adapted to catching agile prey.
