moving company

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvɪŋ ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈmuːvɪŋ ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a business that specializes in transporting people's or businesses' possessions from one location to another
business specializing in moving possessions

Examples

We hired a moving company to help us relocate to our new house.
The moving company handled all the packing and transportation efficiently.
It's a good idea to get quotes from several moving companies before making a decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: removal company - British term for a company that moves possessions
