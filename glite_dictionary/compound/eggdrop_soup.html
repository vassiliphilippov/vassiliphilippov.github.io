eggdrop soup

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛɡˌdrɑp suːp / (AmE), / ˈɛɡˌdrɒp suːp / (BrE)
A Chinese soup consisting of beaten eggs poured into hot broth, creating fine strands or flakes.
Chinese soup with beaten eggs in broth

Examples

She ordered a steaming bowl of eggdrop soup to start her meal.
At the restaurant, the eggdrop soup was both comforting and flavorful.
He watched as the chef prepared fresh eggdrop soup by pouring eggs into the simmering broth.
