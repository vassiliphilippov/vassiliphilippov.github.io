Ninth of Av

1. NOUN

/ naɪnθ əv ɑːv / (AmE), / naɪnθ əv ɑːv / (BrE)
a major Jewish fast day commemorating the destruction of the temples in Jerusalem
Jewish fast day marking destruction of temples

Examples

Many synagogues hold special services on the Ninth of Av to remember the tragedies of Jewish history.
On the Ninth of Av, observant Jews refrain from eating and drinking for 25 hours.
The significance of the Ninth of Av is taught in religious schools around the world.

Etymology

From Hebrew 'Tisha B'Av', literally 'ninth of Av', Av being the name of a month in the Hebrew calendar.
