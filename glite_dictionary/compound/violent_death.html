violent death

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪələnt dɛθ / (AmE), / ˈvaɪələnt dɛθ / (BrE)
death caused by the application of physical force or violence, rather than from natural causes or illness
death caused by violence

Examples

The detective concluded that the victim had met a violent death as a result of the struggle.
Many soldiers faced violent deaths on the battlefield during the war.
The documentary explores the impact of violent death on families and communities.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: natural death - death from natural causes or illness
