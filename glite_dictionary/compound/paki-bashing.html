Paki-bashing

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæki ˌbæʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpæki ˌbaʃɪŋ / (BrE)
Violence or discrimination directed against Pakistanis, typically motivated by racism.
anti-Pakistani violence or discrimination
offensive

Examples

The government pledged to tackle Paki-bashing after several shocking incidents.
There has been a disturbing increase in Paki-bashing reported in some areas.
Community leaders are working together to end Paki-bashing and promote tolerance.

Synset Examples

They organized a rally to protest against Paki-bashing.
Paki-bashing has no place in our community.
The media condemned the rise of Paki-bashing incidents.

Etymology

From 'Paki', a derogatory contraction of 'Pakistani', and 'bashing' (violent assault or strong criticism).
