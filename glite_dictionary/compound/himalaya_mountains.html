Himalaya Mountains

1. PROPN

/ hɪˈmɑːl.jə ˈmaʊn.tənz / (AmE), / hɪˈmæl.i.ə ˈmaʊn.tɪnz / (BrE)
a vast mountain range in Asia, extending along the border between India and Tibet, and containing the world's highest peaks, including Mount Everest.
major Asian mountain range

Examples

The Himalaya Mountains are home to many rare species of plants and animals.
She has always dreamed of trekking in the Himalaya Mountains.
Many rivers in South Asia originate from the Himalaya Mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mount Everest - the highest mountain in the world, located in the Himalayas

Etymology

From Sanskrit *himālayaḥ* meaning 'abode of snow' (*hima* 'snow' + *ālaya* 'dwelling').
