clothes moth

1. NOUN

/ kloʊðz mɔθ / (AmE), / kləʊðz mɒθ / (BrE)
A small moth whose larvae feed on fabrics such as wool, often causing damage to clothing and textiles.
moth that damages fabrics

Examples

The clothes moth caused small holes in my favorite sweater.
Keep your closet clean to prevent a clothes moth infestation.
She discovered that clothes moths had ruined her wool coat over the winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moth - an insect related to butterflies, typically nocturnal
