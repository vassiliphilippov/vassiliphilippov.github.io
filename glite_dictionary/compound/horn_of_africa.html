Horn of Africa

1. NOUN

/ hɔrn əv əˈfrɪkə / (AmE), / hɔːn əv əˈfrɪkə / (BrE)
a region in northeastern Africa, including Somalia, Djibouti, Eritrea, and eastern Ethiopia, forming the continent's easternmost part
region in northeastern Africa

Examples

The Horn of Africa is often affected by severe drought and food shortages.
Many unique cultures and languages are found in the Horn of Africa.
Piracy along the coasts of the Horn of Africa became a major international concern.
