Susan B Anthony dollar

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːzən ˈbiː ˈænθəni ˈdɑlər / (AmE), / ˈsuːzən ˈbiː ˈænθəni ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
a United States dollar coin issued from 1979 to 1981 and again in 1999, featuring the image of Susan B. Anthony
U.S. dollar coin depicting Susan B. Anthony
american

Examples

He found a rare Susan B Anthony dollar in his collection of old coins.
The cashier gave me a Susan B Anthony dollar as change.
Many people mistook the Susan B Anthony dollar for a quarter due to its size.

Etymology

Named after Susan B. Anthony, an American social reformer and women's rights activist.
