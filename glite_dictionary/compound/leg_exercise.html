leg exercise

1. NOUN

/ lɛɡ ˈɛksɚˌsaɪz / (AmE), / lɛɡ ˈɛksəsaɪz / (BrE)
An exercise intended to strengthen or tone the muscles of the legs.
exercise for strengthening the legs

Examples

The personal trainer recommended adding more leg exercise to my workout routine.
Squats and lunges are common forms of leg exercise for building strength and endurance.
Injured athletes may start with gentle leg exercise during their rehabilitation process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squat - a strength exercise in which you lower your hips from a standing position and then stand back up
  • Antonym: upper body exercise - exercise designed to strengthen the muscles of the upper body
