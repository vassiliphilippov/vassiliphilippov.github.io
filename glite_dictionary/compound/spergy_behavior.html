spergy behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɜrɡi bəˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / ˈspɜːɡi bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
Socially awkward or eccentric behavior, often in a way reminiscent of stereotypes associated with Asperger's syndrome; considered offensive.
offensive slang for awkward behavior
slang offensive

Examples

Some people found his spergy behavior difficult to relate to in group settings.
Her spergy behavior made social gatherings uncomfortable at times.
The movie depicted the character with exaggerated spergy behavior, which critics found insensitive.

Synset Examples

Her spergy behavior sometimes annoyed her friends.

Etymology

From 'spergy,' a slang shortening of 'Asperger's syndrome,' and 'behavior.'
