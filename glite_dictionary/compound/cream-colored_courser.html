cream-colored courser

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrim ˌkʌlɚd ˈkɔrsɚ / (AmE), / ˈkriːm ˌkʌləd ˈkɔːsə / (BrE)
a pale, sandy-colored bird of desert and semidesert regions of the Old World, noted for its swift running
pale desert bird of Old World

Examples

The cream-colored courser is well adapted to arid environments, often seen running swiftly across sandy plains.
Birdwatchers traveled for miles to catch a glimpse of the rare cream-colored courser during its migration.
A cream-colored courser was spotted near the oasis, delighting wildlife enthusiasts with its distinctive pale plumage.
