sad ass

1. NOUN

/ sæd ˌæs / (AmE), / sæd ˌɑːs / (BrE)
A disparaging or self-deprecating term for someone who is feeling down, dejected, or overly negative.
disparaging term for a dejected person
informal slang

Examples

Stop being such a sad ass and try to enjoy yourself.
She felt like a real sad ass after getting rejected.
His friends called him a sad ass for moping around all day.

Synset Examples

Feeling like a sad ass, she skipped the party.
Don't be a sad ass, let's go have fun!
He called himself a sad ass after missing the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: downer - A person who is unfailingly negative or depressing
