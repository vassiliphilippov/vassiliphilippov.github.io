mentally ill

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛn.t̬ə.li ˈɪl / (AmE), / ˈmen.təl.i ˈɪl / (BrE)
Suffering from a recognized mental illness, especially one that severely disrupts normal thinking, feeling, or behavior.
suffering from mental illness

Examples

The hospital offers specialized care for mentally ill patients.
Many people with depression are not considered mentally ill in the legal sense.
She has dedicated her career to supporting mentally ill individuals.

Synset Examples

of unsound mind

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insane - suffering from severe mental illness; not sane
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind; not suffering from mental illness
