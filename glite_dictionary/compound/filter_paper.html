filter paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪltɚ ˌpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɪltə ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a type of porous, unsized paper used in laboratories for filtering liquids or powders from mixtures
porous paper used in filtering

Examples

The laboratory technician placed the filter paper into the funnel before pouring the solution.
You should always use clean filter paper to avoid contaminating your experiment.
Wet filter paper can help support seedlings during the first stages of growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filter - a device or material for removing impurities or particles
