fishscale tile

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃˌskeɪl taɪl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌskeɪl taɪl / (BrE)
A tile designed with a curved pattern resembling overlapping fish scales, often used in decorative architecture or interior design.
tile with overlapping curved pattern like fish scales

Examples

The bathroom walls were covered in glossy fishscale tiles that sparkled in the light.
They chose green fishscale tiles to add a vintage touch to the kitchen backsplash.
Artisans carefully aligned each fishscale tile to maintain the flowing pattern across the entryway.

Synset Examples

The new flooring resembles beautiful fishscale tiles.
