safe-deposit

1. NOUN

/ ˌseɪf dɪˈpɑzɪt / (AmE), / ˌseɪf dɪˈpɒzɪt / (BrE)
a secure metal box, typically in a bank, used for storing valuable items or documents
secure bank storage box for valuables

Examples

She kept her grandmother's jewelry in a safe-deposit at the bank for added protection.
The bank manager escorted me to the vault to access my safe-deposit.
Important documents should be stored in a safe-deposit to prevent loss or theft.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safe-deposit box - a box in a bank's vault for storing valuables
