potato race

1. NOUN

/ pəˈteɪdoʊ reɪs / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ reɪs / (BrE)
a novelty race in which participants carry or move potatoes from one place to another, typically one at a time
novelty race with carrying potatoes

Examples

Every summer, the children look forward to the potato race at the school fair.
She won the potato race by balancing each potato perfectly on her spoon.
At the family picnic, the potato race caused plenty of laughter and friendly competition.
