logistic assistance

1. NOUN

/ loʊˈdʒɪstɪk əˈsɪstəns / (AmE), / ləˈdʒɪstɪk əˈsɪstəns / (BrE)
support provided to coordinate the supply, movement, and maintenance of resources, especially within or between military commands or organizations.
support with logistics, esp. military

Examples

The army relied on logistic assistance from its allies to maintain its supply lines.
Effective logistic assistance ensured that all units received essential equipment and food during the operation.
International logistic assistance played a crucial role in the rapid deployment of humanitarian aid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: logistical support - provision of supplies and services, especially in military contexts
  • Synonym: operational support - support for the execution of tasks or missions, especially in military or organizational contexts

Etymology

logistic from French 'logistique', related to supplying troops; assistance from Latin 'assistere' (to help).
