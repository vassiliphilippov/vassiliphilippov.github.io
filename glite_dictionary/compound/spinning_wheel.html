spinning wheel

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪnɪŋ wiːl / (AmE), / ˈspɪnɪŋ wiːl / (BrE)
a small domestic machine for spinning thread or yarn from fibers, typically operated by hand or foot
domestic machine for spinning thread or yarn

Examples

The antique spinning wheel in the museum was beautifully preserved.
She learned how to use a spinning wheel to turn wool into yarn.
A spinning wheel can be powered by either hand or foot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spindle - a rod used for spinning fibers manually
