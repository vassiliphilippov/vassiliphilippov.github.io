light source

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪt ˌsɔrs / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt ˌsɔːs / (BrE)
a device or object that emits light, especially as a means of illumination
something that produces light

Examples

The only light source in the room was a single candle on the table.
Scientists use a laser as a light source in certain experiments.
Make sure your desk has a good light source to avoid eye strain while reading.

Synset Examples

he stopped the car and turned off the lights

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lamp - a device for giving light, especially one that has an electric bulb
  • Synonym: bulb - the glass part of an electric lamp that gives light when it is switched on
