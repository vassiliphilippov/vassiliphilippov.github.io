sports fan

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrts ˌfæn / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts ˌfan / (BrE)
A person who is very enthusiastic about and devoted to one or more sports or sports teams.
enthusiastic supporter of sports

Examples

He has been a lifelong sports fan, supporting his local football team through every season.
As a dedicated sports fan, she watches every basketball game, no matter the time.
The bar was packed with sports fans celebrating the championship win.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fan - a person with a strong interest or admiration for something
