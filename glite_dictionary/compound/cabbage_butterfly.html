cabbage butterfly

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæbɪdʒ ˌbʌtərˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈkabɪdʒ ˌbʌtəflaɪ / (BrE)
A common white butterfly, especially of the genus Pieris, whose larvae feed on cabbage and related plants.
white butterfly, larvae feed on cabbage

Examples

I found a cabbage butterfly resting on the leaves in my garden.
The larvae of the cabbage butterfly can cause significant damage to vegetable crops.
Farmers use netting to protect cabbages from the cabbage butterfly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cabbage white - another term for cabbage butterfly
