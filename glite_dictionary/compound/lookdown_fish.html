lookdown fish

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʊkˌdaʊn fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈlʊkˌdaʊn fɪʃ / (BrE)
a marine fish with a flattened, silvery body and eyes placed high on a steep, truncated forehead, resembling a moonfish
a type of flat silvery marine fish

Examples

The lookdown fish has a distinctive silvery sheen that shimmers in the water.
Aquariums often display lookdown fish because of their unique body shape and reflective scales.
Divers spotted a group of lookdown fish near the coral reef, gliding effortlessly in the current.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moonfish - a flat, silvery fish of the genus Selene, related species
