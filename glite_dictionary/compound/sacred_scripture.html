sacred scripture

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪkrɪd ˈskrɪptʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈseɪkrɪd ˈskrɪptʃə / (BrE)
a text or writing considered holy and authoritative by a religious group
holy text of a religion

Examples

Many people read their sacred scripture daily for spiritual guidance.
The Quran is considered a sacred scripture in Islam.
Scholars compared passages from different sacred scriptures to better understand various faiths.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holy scripture - text regarded as holy by a religion
  • Synonym: scripture - the sacred writings of a religion
