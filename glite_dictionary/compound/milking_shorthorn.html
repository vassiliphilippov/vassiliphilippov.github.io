milking shorthorn

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlkɪŋ ˈʃɔrthɔrn / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkɪŋ ˈʃɔːthɔːn / (BrE)
a breed of dairy cattle evolved from shorthorn beef cattle, known for its milking ability as well as beef quality
breed of dairy cattle from shorthorn stock

Examples

The milking shorthorn is valued for both its dairy and beef qualities on many farms.
Our neighbors recently invested in a herd of milking shorthorns to improve their milk production.
You can recognize a milking shorthorn by its distinctive red and white coat pattern.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shorthorn - a breed of cattle originally bred for both meat and milk

Etymology

From 'milking', referring to dairy production, and 'shorthorn', the breed from which it was developed.
