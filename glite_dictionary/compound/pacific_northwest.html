Pacific Northwest

1. PROPN

/ pəˈsɪfɪk nɔrθˈwɛst / (AmE), / pəˈsɪfɪk nɔːθˈwɛst / (BrE)
a region in the northwestern United States, typically including Washington and Oregon, and sometimes extending into southwestern British Columbia, Canada
region in the northwestern United States

Examples

Rainy winters are a well-known characteristic of the Pacific Northwest climate.
Many tech companies have headquarters based in the Pacific Northwest.
The coastline of the Pacific Northwest is famous for its natural beauty.
