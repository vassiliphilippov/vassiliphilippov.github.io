ring-necked pheasant

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪŋ nɛkt ˈfɛzənt / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋ nɛkt ˈfɛznt / (BrE)
a common pheasant with strikingly bright plumage and a distinctive white ring around its neck
a pheasant with a white neck ring

Examples

We spotted a magnificent ring-necked pheasant strutting across the field at sunrise.
The ring-necked pheasant is prized by hunters for its colorful feathers and swift flight.
A flock of ring-necked pheasants startled us as they took off from the bushes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pheasant - a large, long-tailed game bird
