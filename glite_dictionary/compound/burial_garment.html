burial garment

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛriəl ˈɡɑrmənt / (AmE), / ˈbʊəriəl ˈɡɑːmənt / (BrE)
a cloth or garment used to cover or dress a corpse for burial
cloth for dressing a corpse for burial

Examples

The monk was dressed in a simple burial garment before being laid to rest.
Ancient cultures often placed the deceased in an elaborate burial garment to honor them.
The family carefully chose a traditional burial garment for their loved one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shroud - a cloth used to wrap a body for burial
