self-improvement

1. NOUN

/ sɛlf ɪmˈpruvmənt / (AmE), / sɛlf ɪmˈpruːvmənt / (BrE)
The process or activity of making yourself better in knowledge, character, skills, or habits.
the process of making yourself better

Examples

He dedicated an hour every day to self-improvement through reading and exercise.
Many people attend workshops and seminars for self-improvement purposes.
Her commitment to self-improvement inspired those around her to work harder on themselves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-development - the process of improving yourself through activities, learning, etc.
  • Antonym: self-neglect - failure to care for or improve oneself

Etymology

Formed from 'self-' (referring to oneself) + 'improvement' (from 'improve').
