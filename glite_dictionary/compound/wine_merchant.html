wine merchant

1. NOUN

/ waɪn ˈmɜrtʃənt / (AmE), / waɪn ˈmɜːtʃənt / (BrE)
a person or business that buys and sells wine commercially, often specializing in quality or rare wines
person or business selling wine

Examples

The wine merchant recommended an excellent Bordeaux for our dinner party.
She inherited the family business and became a successful wine merchant in the city.
Many upscale restaurants work closely with a trusted wine merchant to curate their wine list.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vintner - a person who makes or sells wine
