printing process

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪntɪŋ ˌprɑːsɛs / (AmE), / ˈprɪntɪŋ ˌprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
a method of reproducing text or images by applying ink to paper, typically for publication
method of reproducing images or text with ink

Examples

The newspaper uses a traditional printing process to produce thousands of copies every morning.
Advancements in the printing process have made color books much more affordable.
Understanding the printing process is essential for anyone working in publishing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: printing - the act or process of producing printed material
