municipal government

1. NOUN

/ mjuːˈnɪsəpəl ˈɡʌvərnmənt / (AmE), / mjuːˈnɪsɪpəl ˈɡʌvəmənt / (BrE)
the government responsible for the administration of a city or town
local city or town government

Examples

The municipal government recently announced plans to improve public transportation in the city.
Many citizens attended the meeting to voice their concerns to the municipal government.
Tax revenues help fund the services provided by the municipal government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: local government - the administration of a particular town, area, or district
