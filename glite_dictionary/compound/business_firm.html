business firm

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs fɝːm / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs fɜːm / (BrE)
a company or organization engaged in commercial, industrial, or professional activities, typically consisting of its owners and employees
company or organization engaged in commerce

Examples

After graduating, she joined a well-known business firm in the city.
The business firm expanded rapidly, opening offices in three countries.
Many startups hope to become a successful business firm with global reach.

Synset Examples

he worked for a brokerage house

Related Terms

  • Synonym: company - a business organization that makes, buys, or sells goods or provides services
  • Synonym: corporation - a large company or group of companies authorized to act as a single entity
  • Synonym: partnership - a business organization in which two or more individuals manage and operate the business
