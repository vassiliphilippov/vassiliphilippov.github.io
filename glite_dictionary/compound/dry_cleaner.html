dry cleaner

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪ ˌkliːnər / (AmE), / ˈdraɪ ˌkliːnə / (BrE)
a person or business that cleans clothes and fabrics using chemical solvents rather than water
operator of a dry-cleaning business

Examples

I need to take my suit to the dry cleaner before the wedding.
She works at a dry cleaner in the city center.
The dry cleaner removed the stains from my jacket perfectly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: launderer - a person or business that washes clothes, usually with water
