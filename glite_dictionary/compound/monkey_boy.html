monkey boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌŋki bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki bɔɪ / (BrE)
An informal term for a boy who is playful, energetic, or mischievous, often used affectionately.
playful or mischievous boy (informal)
informal

Examples

Ever since he learned to walk, Jack has been a real monkey boy, getting into everything.
Don't be such a monkey boy—get down from that counter!
His parents called him their little monkey boy because he was always so active and playful.

Synset Examples

He's such a monkey boy, always climbing trees.
My little brother is a total monkey boy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rascal - a mischievous or cheeky person, especially a child
  • Synonym: imp - a mischievous child
