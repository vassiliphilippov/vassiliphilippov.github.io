piston chamber

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪstən ˈtʃeɪmbər / (AmE), / ˈpɪstən ˈtʃeɪmbə / (BrE)
A compartment or chamber in an engine or pump within which a piston moves to compress or transfer a fluid.
engine compartment for a moving piston

Examples

The mechanic inspected the piston chamber for signs of wear or scoring.
A clean piston chamber is essential for efficient engine operation.
Oil leaks into the piston chamber can cause engine performance issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cylinder - cylindrical chamber in which a piston moves
