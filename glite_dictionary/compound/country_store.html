country store

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌntri stɔːr / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri stɔː / (BrE)
a retail store located in a rural area, typically stocking a wide variety of goods needed by the local community
rural general retail store

Examples

On weekends, the locals gather at the country store to catch up on news and buy supplies.
The country store carries everything from hardware to homemade jams.
That old country store has been the heart of the community for generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: general store - a store selling a wide variety of goods, especially in a small town or rural area
