hemp agrimony

1. NOUN

/ hɛmp əˈɡrɪməni / (AmE), / hɛmp əˈɡrɪməni / (BrE)
a coarse European herb (Eupatorium cannabinum) with divided leaves and clusters of small reddish-purple flower heads, commonly found in damp areas
European herb with purple flower heads

Examples

The marsh was filled with hemp agrimony, attracting butterflies and bees during the summer months.
Gardeners often cultivate hemp agrimony for its resilience and attractive clusters of purple flowers.
You can recognize hemp agrimony by its divided leaves and reddish-purple flower heads.
