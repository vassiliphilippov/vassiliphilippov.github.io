hockey coach

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːki ˌkoʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɒki ˌkəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a person responsible for training and directing a team of hockey players
person who coaches a hockey team

Examples

The hockey coach designed a new play to help the team win.
As a hockey coach, she motivates her players to perform their best.
The hockey coach gave feedback after every practice session.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coach - person who trains athletes or a team
