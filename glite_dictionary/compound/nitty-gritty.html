nitty-gritty

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɪtiˈɡrɪti / (AmE), / ˌnɪtiˈɡrɪti / (BrE)
the most important or essential aspects of a subject, situation, or problem
essential or most important part

Examples

Before we make a decision, let's get down to the nitty-gritty of the proposal.
The documentary really explores the nitty-gritty of daily life in the city.
She prefers to focus on the nitty-gritty rather than the big-picture ideas.

Synset Examples

the gist of the prosecutor's argument
the heart and soul of the Republican Party
the nub of the story

Related Terms

  • Synonym: essence - the intrinsic nature or indispensable quality of something
  • Synonym: core - the central or most important part of something
  • Antonym: triviality - something of little value or importance

Etymology

Origin unknown; first recorded in mid 20th century American English.
