haunting realism

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔntɪŋ ˌriəˈlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈhɔːntɪŋ ˌrɪəˈlɪzəm / (BrE)
an evocative and memorable depiction that leaves a lasting impression due to its vivid and lifelike quality
evocative, memorable lifelike depiction

Examples

The director achieved haunting realism in the war scenes, making viewers feel as though they were present.
Her novel is celebrated for its haunting realism, which lingers with readers long after they finish the book.
The painting's haunting realism captured the grief and suffering of its subject.

Synset Examples

The film depicted the crucifixion with haunting realism.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: realism - the quality of representing a person, thing, or situation accurately or in a way that is true to life
