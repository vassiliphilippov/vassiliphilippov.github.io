raw throat

1. NOUN

/ rɔ ðroʊt / (AmE), / rɔː ˈθrəʊt / (BrE)
a sore or irritated feeling in the throat, often accompanied by inflammation or pain, especially when swallowing
sore or irritated throat

Examples

After yelling at the concert all night, I woke up with a raw throat.
The cold air made my raw throat feel even worse this morning.
If you have a raw throat, warm tea with honey might help soothe it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sore throat - painful or uncomfortable sensation in the throat, often due to infection
