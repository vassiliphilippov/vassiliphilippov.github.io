insurance firm

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈʃʊr.əns fɝːm / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃʊə.rəns fɜːm / (BrE)
a business organization that provides insurance policies and risk coverage for clients in exchange for premiums
company selling insurance

Examples

She decided to work for a leading insurance firm after graduating from university.
The insurance firm denied his claim due to insufficient evidence.
Many insurance firms are now offering discounts for customers with safe driving records.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insurance company - a company that sells insurance policies
