conspiracy theorist

1. NOUN

/ kənˈspɪrəsi ˈθiɚɪst / (AmE), / kənˈspɪrəsi ˈθɪərɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that events or situations are the result of secret, often sinister plots by powerful groups
person who believes in conspiracy theories

Examples

Many dismissed him as a conspiracy theorist with wild ideas.
The internet has given every conspiracy theorist a larger platform for spreading claims.
She argued with the conspiracy theorist at the dinner party.

Synset Examples

Many dismissed him as a conspiracy theorist with wild ideas.
She argued with the conspiracy theorist at the dinner party.
The conspiracy theorists claimed that Nazitru was behind the unrest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conspiracy theory - a theory that events are the result of a secret plot

Etymology

From 'conspiracy theory' + '-ist' (denoting a person who practices or is concerned with something).
