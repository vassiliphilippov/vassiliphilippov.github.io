ivory tickler

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪvəri ˈtɪk.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈaɪvəri ˈtɪk.lə / (BrE)
(slang) A piano, especially with reference to its ivory keys or as played by a pianist.
slang term for piano
slang

Examples

He was known as an exceptional ivory tickler at every jazz bar in town.
She spent hours at the ivory tickler, practicing her favorite classical pieces.
The old ivory tickler in the corner added a unique charm to the room.

Synset Examples

She took lessons to master the ivory tickler.
He played the ivory tickler beautifully at the concert.
The ivory tickler's melody enchanted everyone in the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piano - a large keyboard musical instrument

Etymology

From 'ivory' (referring to piano keys, traditionally made from ivory) and 'tickler' (one who playfully touches or plays).
