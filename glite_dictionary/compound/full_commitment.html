full commitment

1. NOUN

/ fʊl kəˈmɪtmənt / (AmE), / fʊl kəˈmɪtmənt / (BrE)
the state or quality of giving complete dedication or responsibility to a task or cause
total dedication or responsibility

Examples

The leader's full commitment to the cause inspired everyone involved.
Completing this challenge will require your full commitment and attention to detail.
She questioned whether her team members had given their full commitment to the project.

Synset Examples

The project requires nothing less than full commitment.
He is fully committed to making this work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commitment - the state or quality of being dedicated to a cause or activity
