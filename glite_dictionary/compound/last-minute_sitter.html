last-minute sitter

1. NOUN

/ læst ˈmɪnət ˈsɪtər / (AmE), / lɑːst ˈmɪnɪt ˈsɪtə / (BrE)
A babysitter who is arranged or booked shortly before they are needed, often in urgent or unexpected situations.
babysitter hired at short notice

Examples

After her meeting ran late, she had to find a last-minute sitter online.
It's not always easy to trust a last-minute sitter with your kids.
They were grateful when their neighbor agreed to be a last-minute sitter.

Synset Examples

She used a babysitter app to book a last-minute sitter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: babysitter - a person engaged to care for children temporarily
