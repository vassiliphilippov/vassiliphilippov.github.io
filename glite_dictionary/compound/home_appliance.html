home appliance

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm əˈplaɪəns / (AmE), / həʊm əˈplaɪəns / (BrE)
A device or machine designed to perform a specific household task, such as cooking, cleaning, or food storage.
device for performing household tasks

Examples

We recently bought a new home appliance to make our everyday chores easier.
Energy efficiency is an important factor when choosing a home appliance.
The kitchen was filled with every modern home appliance you could imagine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appliance - a device or piece of equipment designed to perform a specific task
