great team

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪt tim / (AmE), / ɡreɪt tiːm / (BrE)
a group of people who work together very effectively to achieve success
highly effective group working together

Examples

The two scientists formed a great team and won numerous awards for their research.
With her leadership and his creativity, they make a great team on the project.
Every successful company relies on having a great team behind its achievements.

Synset Examples

Their contrasting personalities make them a great team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: team - a group of people working together
