star player

1. NOUN

/ stɑr ˈpleɪər / (AmE), / stɑː ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
the most outstanding or important player in a team or group
most outstanding player

Examples

She was the star player of the volleyball team, leading them to victory in the finals.
After his injury, the team's star player had to watch the match from the sidelines.
The coach praised their star player for his dedication and leadership on the field.

Synset Examples

In the game, Number Eleven was the star player.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: MVP - player considered most valuable to a team
