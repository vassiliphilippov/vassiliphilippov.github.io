plot of land

1. NOUN

/ plɑt əv lænd / (AmE), / plɒt əv lænd / (BrE)
a small area of ground used for a particular purpose, often cultivated or covered with specific vegetation
small area of ground, often for plants

Examples

They bought a plot of land on the edge of town and planted a vegetable garden.
The farmer divides his field into several plots of land for different crops.
She tends a small plot of land behind her house, growing herbs and flowers.

Synset Examples

a bean plot
a cabbage patch
a briar patch

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patch - a small piece of ground, especially one planted with something
