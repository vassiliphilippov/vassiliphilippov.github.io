desire to know

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈzaɪɚ tə noʊ / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪə tə nəʊ / (BrE)
a strong feeling of curiosity that motivates investigation or the pursuit of knowledge
curiosity-driven motivation to learn

Examples

Her desire to know led her to study dozens of ancient languages.
Scientists are often driven by a profound desire to know how the universe works.
His desire to know more about biology prompted him to read scientific journals in his spare time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curiosity - a strong desire to learn or know something
