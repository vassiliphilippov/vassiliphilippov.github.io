Gulf of Suez

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡʌlf əv suˈɛz / (AmE), / ˌɡʌlf əv sjuːˈɛz / (BrE)
a northwestern extension of the Red Sea, connected to the Mediterranean Sea by the Suez Canal.
northwestern arm of the Red Sea

Examples

The Gulf of Suez is an important route for international shipping.
Several oil fields are located along the Gulf of Suez.
The Gulf of Suez separates the Sinai Peninsula from mainland Egypt.
