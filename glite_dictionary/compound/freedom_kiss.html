freedom kiss

1. NOUN

/ ˈfriːdəm kɪs / (AmE), / ˈfriːdəm kɪs / (BrE)
a kiss that symbolizes affection, liberation, or celebration, often exchanged during significant moments
kiss symbolizing freedom or affection

Examples

As the clock struck midnight, the couple shared a freedom kiss to mark the new year.
She greeted her partner with a joyous freedom kiss after months apart.
During the celebration, everyone exchanged a heartfelt freedom kiss to express their happiness.
