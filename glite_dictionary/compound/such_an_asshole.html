such an asshole

1. NOUN

/ sʌtʃ ən ˈæshoʊl / (AmE), / sʌtʃ ən ˈæshəʊl / (BrE)
a very unpleasant or rude person, used as a strong insult
a very rude or unpleasant person (strong insult)
informal vulgar offensive

Examples

I can't believe he lied to everyone—he's such an asshole.
Don't be such an asshole when people make honest mistakes.
After yelling at the waiter, everyone thought she was such an asshole.

Synset Examples

He's such an asshole for treating her like that.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person
  • Synonym: bastard - an unpleasant or difficult person (insult)
  • Antonym: nice guy - a pleasant, kind person
