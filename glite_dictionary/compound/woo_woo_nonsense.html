woo woo nonsense

1. NOUN

/ ˈwuː ˌwuː ˈnɑːnsəns / (AmE), / ˈwuː ˌwuː ˈnɒnsəns / (BrE)
Ideas, claims, or beliefs that are unscientific, irrational, or not supported by evidence, often relating to pseudoscience or the paranormal.
unscientific or irrational ideas
informal

Examples

The new book was criticized for its woo woo nonsense about miracle cures.
She rolled her eyes at the woo woo nonsense being discussed at the dinner party.
This forum has a strict policy against posting woo woo nonsense.

Synset Examples

The seminar was filled with woo woo nonsense.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pseudoscience - a system of theories not based on scientific method

Etymology

Formed from 'woo woo', an informal term for mystical or pseudoscientific ideas, and 'nonsense'.
