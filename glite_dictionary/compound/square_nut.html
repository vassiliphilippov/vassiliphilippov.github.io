square nut

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr nʌt / (AmE), / skweə nʌt / (BrE)
a nut with four flat sides and a square shape, used with a bolt for fastening
nut with a square shape

Examples

The carpenter tightened the square nut to secure the metal plate.
Vintage machinery often uses a square nut instead of a hex nut.
He replaced the missing bolt and square nut during the repair job.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: hex nut - a nut with six flat sides
