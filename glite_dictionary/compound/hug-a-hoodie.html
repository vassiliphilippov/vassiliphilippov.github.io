hug-a-hoodie

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌɡ ə ˈhʊdi / (AmE), / ˈhʌɡ ə ˈhʊdi / (BrE)
A thick and comfortable hoodie designed to provide extra warmth and coziness, often oversized and used for lounging or staying warm.
oversized, cozy hoodie for warmth

Examples

She wrapped herself in a hug-a-hoodie and read by the fireplace.
On lazy weekends, he likes to lounge around in his hug-a-hoodie.
The hug-a-hoodie kept me warm during the camping trip.

Synset Examples
