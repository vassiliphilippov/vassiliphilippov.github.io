bear's-paw fern

1. NOUN

/ bɛrz pɔ fɝn / (AmE), / bɛəz pɔː fɜːn / (BrE)
an epiphytic fern with large fronds, native to Taiwan and the Philippines
epiphytic fern from East Asia

Examples

The bear's-paw fern thrives in humid, shady environments attached to tree trunks.
Many gardeners grow the bear's-paw fern for its attractive, broad fronds.
We spotted a rare bear's-paw fern while hiking in the forests of Taiwan.
