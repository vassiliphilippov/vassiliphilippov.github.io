main argument

1. NOUN

/ meɪn ˈɑːrɡjəmənt / (AmE), / meɪn ˈɑːɡjʊmənt / (BrE)
the most important point or assertion made in a discussion, essay, or debate
primary point in discussion or essay

Examples

Her main argument against the proposal was its high cost.
The professor asked us to identify the main argument of the article.
You should clearly state your main argument in the introduction.

Synset Examples

The main argument in his essay was compelling.
The praevia was crucial for understanding the main argument.
You should underline your main arguments for clarity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: argument - a reason or set of reasons given to persuade others
