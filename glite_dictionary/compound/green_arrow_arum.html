green arrow arum

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin ˈæroʊ ˈɛrəm / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈærəʊ ˈærəm / (BrE)
A perennial herb native to the eastern United States, characterized by arrowhead-shaped leaves, a long pointed spathe, and green berries.
perennial U.S. herb with arrow-shaped leaves

Examples

The green arrow arum is often found in wetlands throughout the eastern United States.
Gardeners appreciate the unique shape of the green arrow arum's leaves.
The berries of the green arrow arum turn a striking green as they mature.
