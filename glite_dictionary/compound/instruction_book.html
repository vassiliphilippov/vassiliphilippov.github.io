instruction book

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈstrʌkʃən bʊk / (AmE), / ɪnˈstrʌkʃən bʊk / (BrE)
a book containing directions or information on how to use or operate a particular device or piece of equipment
book of directions for using equipment

Examples

I found the instruction book inside the box with my new camera.
Please refer to the instruction book before assembling the furniture.
The instruction book explains how to troubleshoot common problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manual - a handbook or book of instructions especially for operating equipment
  • Synonym: user guide - a book providing instructions for using a product
