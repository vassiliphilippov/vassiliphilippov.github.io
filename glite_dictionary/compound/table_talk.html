table talk

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪ.bəl tɑk / (AmE), / ˈteɪ.bəl tɔːk / (BrE)
conversation that takes place during a meal, especially informal or social discussion at the dining table
conversation during a meal

Examples

Family table talk often covers everything from daily events to important news.
The book provides a glimpse into the table talk of famous philosophers.
Good table talk can make a simple meal memorable for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dinner conversation - conversation held at the dinner table

2. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəl tɑk / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəl tɔːk / (BrE)
informal conversation or discussion that occurs at a meal, especially one that is lively or engaging
conversation during a meal

Examples

The chef joined our table talk, making the meal even more enjoyable.
Their table talk ranged from stories about travel to debates on current events.
Good table talk can turn an ordinary dinner into a memorable experience.

Synset Examples

The restaurant's ambiance encouraged cheerful table talk.
We enjoyed lively table talk during dinner.
Their table talk often veered into politics and philosophy.

Etymology

From 'table' (furniture for meals) + 'talk' (conversation); used in English since the 16th century.
