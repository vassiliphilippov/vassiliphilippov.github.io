helmet hair

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlmət hɛr / (AmE), / ˈhɛlmɪt heə / (BrE)
Hair that becomes flattened, messy, or misshapen as a result of wearing a helmet.
flattened or messy hair from wearing a helmet

Examples

I always get helmet hair after riding my motorbike to work.
The new bicycle helmet promised comfort and no more helmet hair.
She brushed out her helmet hair as soon as she took off her helmet.

Synset Examples

After biking, she always has helmet hair.
He styled his hair to avoid helmet hair.
She complained about her helmet hair after the ride.
