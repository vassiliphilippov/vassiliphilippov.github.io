hamstring injury

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæmˌstrɪŋ ˈɪndʒəri / (AmE), / ˈhæm.strɪŋ ˈɪn.dʒəri / (BrE)
An injury to one or more of the muscles at the back of the thigh, commonly occurring in athletes or during physical activity.
injury to thigh muscles (hamstrings)

Examples

After his hamstring injury, the runner had to withdraw from the competition.
Physiotherapists recommend specific exercises to help recover from a hamstring injury.
Preventing a hamstring injury involves proper warm-up and stretching before intense activity.

Synset Examples

She always stretches to prevent a hamstring injury.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muscle strain - an injury involving the stretching or tearing of a muscle
