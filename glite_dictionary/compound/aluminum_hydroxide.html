aluminum hydroxide

1. NOUN

/ əˌluːmɪnəm haɪˈdrɑksaɪd / (AmE), / ˌaljʊˈmɪnɪəm haɪˈdrɒksaɪd / (BrE)
a white crystalline compound that occurs naturally as the mineral gibbsite and is used in medicine and industry
white crystalline compound; mineral gibbsite

Examples

The doctor prescribed aluminum hydroxide to help neutralize excess stomach acid.
Aluminum hydroxide is found in nature as the mineral gibbsite.
The laboratory synthesized pure aluminum hydroxide for use in industrial processes.
