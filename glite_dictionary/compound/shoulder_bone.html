shoulder bone

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊldɚ boʊn / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə bəʊn / (BrE)
either of the two flat, triangular bones located on each side of the upper back, forming the back part of the shoulder (the scapula) in humans.
the bone forming the back of the shoulder

Examples

He fractured his shoulder bone in a cycling accident.
Your shoulder bone helps connect your arm to your torso.
After the fall, there was swelling near her shoulder bone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scapula - technical term for the shoulder bone
