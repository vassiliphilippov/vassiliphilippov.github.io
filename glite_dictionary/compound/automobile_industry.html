automobile industry

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑː.t̬ə.moʊˌbiːl ˈɪn.də.stri / (AmE), / ˌɔː.tə.məˌbiːl ˈɪn.də.stri / (BrE)
all the companies and organizations involved in the manufacture of automobiles, considered as a collective sector of the economy
sector involved in manufacturing automobiles

Examples

The automobile industry has faced rapid changes due to advancements in electric vehicles.
Many workers lost their jobs when the automobile industry experienced a downturn.
Japan is known for its highly efficient automobile industry and innovative car designs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automotive industry - sector of industry involved in manufacturing motor vehicles
