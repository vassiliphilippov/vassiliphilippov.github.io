fireman's ax

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚmənz ˌæks / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəmənz ˌæks / (BrE)
A specialized ax with a long handle, a blade on one side, and a pointed tip on the other, used by firefighters for breaking through barriers.
specialized ax used by firefighters

Examples

The firefighter used a fireman's ax to break down the door during the rescue.
A fireman's ax is designed to penetrate walls and roofs in emergency situations.
She inspected the sharp edge of the fireman's ax before climbing onto the fire truck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: axe - a tool with a heavy bladed head used for chopping
