element 113

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛləmənt wʌn ˈwʌn ˈθri / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪmənt wʌn ˈwʌn ˈθri / (BrE)
a radioactive, synthetic transuranic element with the atomic number 113, also known as nihonium.
radioactive transuranic element (nihonium)

Examples

Scientists succeeded in creating element 113 for the first time in a laboratory in Japan.
The discovery of element 113 was a major breakthrough in nuclear chemistry.
Unlike naturally occurring elements, element 113 must be synthesized due to its instability.
