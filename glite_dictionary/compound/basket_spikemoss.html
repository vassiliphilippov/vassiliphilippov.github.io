basket spikemoss

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskɪt ˈspaɪkˌmɒs / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪt ˈspaɪkmɒs / (BrE)
A species of spikemoss (Selaginella apoda) that forms dense mats, native to eastern North America.
species of spikemoss forming dense mats

Examples

The forest floor was covered in a thick layer of basket spikemoss.
Researchers studied the growth habits of basket spikemoss in wetlands across the region.
Gardeners often use basket spikemoss as a ground cover in shaded areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spikemoss - any of various small, moss-like plants of the genus Selaginella
