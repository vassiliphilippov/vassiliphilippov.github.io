tack hammer

1. NOUN

/ tæk ˈhæmər / (AmE), / tæk ˈhamə / (BrE)
a small, light hammer designed for driving tacks into wood or other surfaces
small light hammer for driving tacks

Examples

He used a tack hammer to attach the fabric to the chair frame.
A tack hammer is ideal for precision work that requires small nails or tacks.
The upholsterer reached for his tack hammer and started working on the new sofa.

Related Terms
