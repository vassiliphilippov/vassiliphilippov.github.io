church service

1. NOUN

/ tʃɝtʃ ˈsɝvɪs / (AmE), / tʃɜːtʃ ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a religious ceremony or set of formal worship activities held in a church
formal religious worship in a church

Examples

We attend the church service every Sunday morning.
The choir sang beautifully during the church service.
The church service will begin at ten o'clock.

Synset Examples

don't be late for church

Related Terms

  • Synonym: service - a formal act of public worship
  • Synonym: mass - the main religious service in the Roman Catholic Church
