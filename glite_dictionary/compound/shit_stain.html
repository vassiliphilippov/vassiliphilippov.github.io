shit stain

1. NOUN

/ ʃɪt steɪn / (AmE), / ʃɪt steɪn / (BrE)
A lasting mark of disgrace or a significant blemish in one's reputation or history, used as a vulgar metaphor.
vulgar metaphor for disgrace or blemish
vulgar

Examples

The corruption scandal left a shit stain on the politician's otherwise clean record.
Even years later, that mistake was still a shit stain on his legacy.
She feared that one reckless comment would become a shit stain she could never erase.

Synset Examples

That scandal left a shit stain on her career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stigma - a mark of disgrace associated with a circumstance, quality, or person
  • Synonym: taint - a trace of a bad or undesirable quality or substance
