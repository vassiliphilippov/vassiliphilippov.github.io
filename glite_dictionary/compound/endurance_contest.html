endurance contest

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdʊr.əns ˈkɑn.tɛst / (AmE), / ɪnˈdjʊə.rəns ˈkɒn.tɛst / (BrE)
a competition or undertaking that tests how long participants can withstand difficult or strenuous conditions
long, challenging competition or undertaking

Examples

Competing in the triathlon was the toughest endurance contest she had ever attempted.
The reality show turned into an endurance contest as the challenges grew more exhausting each day.
Rowing across the Atlantic was an extreme endurance contest that pushed every participant to their limits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marathon - a long-distance running race, often seen as an endurance contest
