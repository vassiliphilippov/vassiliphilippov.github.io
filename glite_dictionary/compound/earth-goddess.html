earth-goddess

1. NOUN

/ ˈɝːθ ˌɡɑːd.əs / (AmE), / ˈɜːθ ˌɡɒd.ɪs / (BrE)
A goddess believed to preside over fertility, vegetation, and the earth in various mythologies and religions.
goddess of fertility and the earth

Examples

In many ancient cultures, the earth-goddess was revered as the source of life and abundance.
The festival was held in honor of the earth-goddess to ensure a bountiful harvest.
Mythologies around the world feature different versions of an earth-goddess, each with unique attributes and stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mother goddess - a goddess who represents motherhood, fertility, or the earth
