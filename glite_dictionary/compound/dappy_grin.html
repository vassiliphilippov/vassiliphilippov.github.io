dappy grin

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæpi ɡrɪn / (AmE), / ˈdæpi ɡrɪn / (BrE)
a silly or goofy smile, often appearing absent-minded or foolish
silly or goofy smile
informal

Examples

He couldn't stop showing a dappy grin when he won the contest.
She gave me a dappy grin that made everyone in the room laugh.
After the prank, he walked around with a dappy grin on his face.

Synset Examples

He had a dappy grin after hearing the good news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grin - a broad smile
