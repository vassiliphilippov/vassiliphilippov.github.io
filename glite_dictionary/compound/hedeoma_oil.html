hedeoma oil

1. NOUN

/ hɪˈdiːəmə ɔɪl / (AmE), / hɛˈdiːəmə ɔɪl / (BrE)
an aromatic essential oil obtained from the American pennyroyal plant, used in flavorings and herbal remedies
aromatic oil from American pennyroyal
medical

Examples

The herbalist recommended using a small amount of hedeoma oil for its calming effects.
Researchers studied the chemical properties of hedeoma oil extracted from wild plants.
Some traditional remedies include hedeoma oil as an ingredient for treating minor ailments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pennyroyal oil - aromatic oil from European or American pennyroyal plants

Etymology

From the genus name Hedeoma, derived from Greek 'hēdeos' (sweet) and 'osmē' (smell).
