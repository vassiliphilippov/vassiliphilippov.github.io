rainbow runner

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪnboʊ ˈrʌnər / (AmE), / ˈreɪnbəʊ ˈrʌnə / (BrE)
a large, streamlined, cigar-shaped tropical fish (Elagatis bipinnulata) with a blue-green back and yellow stripes, valued as a game fish.
a tropical game fish with yellow stripes

Examples

The rainbow runner is prized by anglers for its speed and fighting ability.
While snorkeling, we saw a school of rainbow runners gliding through the clear water.
Because of its vibrant colors, the rainbow runner stands out among other game fish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jack - a type of marine fish in the family Carangidae
