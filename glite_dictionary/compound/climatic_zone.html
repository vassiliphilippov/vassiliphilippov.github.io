climatic zone

1. NOUN

/ klaɪˈmætɪk zoʊn / (AmE), / klaɪˈmætɪk zəʊn / (BrE)
a region of the Earth divided according to prevailing climate and latitude, such as tropical, temperate, or polar zones
geographical area defined by climate

Examples

The Sahara Desert lies within a very arid climatic zone.
Many types of crops can only be grown in the right climatic zone.
The country spans more than one climatic zone, leading to diverse weather conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: climate zone - a region defined by its climate characteristics
