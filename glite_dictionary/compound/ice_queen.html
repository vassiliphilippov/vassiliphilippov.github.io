ice queen

1. NOUN

/ aɪs kwiːn / (AmE), / aɪs kwiːn / (BrE)
a woman who is emotionally cold, distant, or unfeeling in her interactions with others
emotionally cold or aloof woman

Examples

Many colleagues thought of her as an ice queen because she rarely smiled or engaged in small talk.
After the breakup, he accused her of being an ice queen who never showed her feelings.
She was unfairly labeled an ice queen even though she cared deeply about her friends.

Synset Examples

Her reputation as the ice queen made her feared.
The ice queen ruled her kingdom with a cold grip.
He called her the ice queen after she rejected him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cold fish - a person who is emotionally distant or unresponsive
  • Antonym: warm-hearted - kind and friendly; showing affection or compassion
