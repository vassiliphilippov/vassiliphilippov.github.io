Congress of Racial Equality

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑŋɡrəs əv ˈreɪʃəl iˈkwɑləti / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡrɪs əv ˈreɪʃəl ɪˈkwɒləti / (BrE)
a US civil rights organization founded in 1942 to promote racial equality and end segregation and discrimination
US civil rights organization (founded 1942)

Examples
