Brooklyn Bridge

1. PROPN

/ ˈbrʊklɪn brɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbrʊklɪn brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a suspension bridge spanning the East River in New York City, connecting Manhattan and Brooklyn; opened in 1883
famous bridge in New York City

Examples
