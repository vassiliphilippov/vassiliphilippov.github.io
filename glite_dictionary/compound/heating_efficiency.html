heating efficiency

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiː.tɪŋ ɪˈfɪʃ.ən.si / (AmE), / ˈhiː.tɪŋ ɪˈfɪʃ.ən.si / (BrE)
The effectiveness of a heating system in converting energy into usable heat.
effectiveness of a heating system

Examples

Improving your home's heating efficiency can help lower your energy bills significantly.
The heating efficiency of this furnace is much higher than older models.
Many people upgrade insulation to boost heating efficiency during winter.

Synset Examples

They installed a new boiler to improve heating efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: efficiency - ability to accomplish a task with minimal waste of energy
