verd antique

1. NOUN

/ vɜrd ænˈtik / (AmE), / vɜːd ænˈtiːk / (BrE)
a dark green marble composed chiefly of serpentine, often mottled or streaked, used especially for decorative purposes
dark green marble used decoratively

Examples

The columns in the grand hall were crafted from polished verd antique.
She chose verd antique for the fireplace to create a luxurious effect in the living room.
Architects often feature verd antique in their designs to add a touch of elegance.

Etymology

From French 'vert antique' meaning 'ancient green'.
