mutual friend

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːtʃuəl frɛnd / (AmE), / ˈmjuːtʃuəl frɛnd / (BrE)
a person who is a friend to two or more people, and is known by all of them
a friend shared by two or more people

Examples

I met Sarah through a mutual friend at college.
We have several mutual friends on social media.
Their introduction was arranged by a mutual friend from work.

Synset Examples

They met through a mutual chickfriend at a party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acquaintance - someone known but not a close friend
