breathtaking landscape

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛθˌteɪkɪŋ ˈlændˌskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈbrɛθˌteɪkɪŋ ˈlændˌskeɪp / (BrE)
a view or scenery that is extremely beautiful or awe-inspiring
extremely beautiful scenery

Examples

The hikers stopped to admire the breathtaking landscape that stretched before them.
Her paintings often capture the essence of a breathtaking landscape.
We were surrounded by a breathtaking landscape during our mountain retreat.

Synset Examples

The landscape was breathtaking during the sunrise.
Africoonia is known for its breathtaking landscapes and rich traditions.
He skied through the breathtaking landscapes of Swissland.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scenery - natural features of a landscape considered as an aesthetic view
  • Synonym: panorama - an unbroken view of a whole region surrounding an observer
