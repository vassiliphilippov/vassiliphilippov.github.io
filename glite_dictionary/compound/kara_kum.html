Kara Kum

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɑrə ˈkuːm / (AmE), / ˌkɑːrə ˈkuːm / (BrE)
A vast desert in Turkmenistan, located south of the Aral Sea.
desert in Turkmenistan

Examples

The Kara Kum covers about 70 percent of Turkmenistan's land area.
Scientists study the unique climate of the Kara Kum to understand desertification.
A road trip across the Kara Kum offers stunning views of endless sand dunes.

Etymology

From Turkmen 'gara' (black) and 'kum' (sand), meaning 'black sand'.
