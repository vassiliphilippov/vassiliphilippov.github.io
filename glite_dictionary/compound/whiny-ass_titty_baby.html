whiny-ass titty baby

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪni ˌæs ˈtɪti ˌbeɪbi / (AmE), / ˈwaɪni ˌæs ˈtɪti ˌbeɪbi / (BrE)
A highly offensive slang term for someone who is excessively sensitive, immature, or complains in a childish way.
offensive slang for an overly sensitive, childish complainer
offensive vulgar slang
