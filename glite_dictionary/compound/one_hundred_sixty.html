one hundred sixty

1. NUM

/ wʌn ˌhəndɹəd ˈsɪksti / (AmE), / wʌn ˌhʌndrəd ˈsɪksti / (BrE)
The number that is ten more than one hundred fifty; 160.
the number 160

Examples

There are exactly one hundred sixty seats in this auditorium.
The project required one hundred sixty volunteers to complete on time.
After one hundred sixty miles, they finally reached their destination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hundred and sixty - British variant for 160
