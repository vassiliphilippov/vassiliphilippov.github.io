espresso shop

1. NOUN

/ ɛˈsprɛsoʊ ʃɑp / (AmE), / ɛˈsprɛsəʊ ʃɒp / (BrE)
A café or small restaurant specializing in serving espresso and other coffee drinks.
café specializing in espresso drinks

Examples

The new espresso shop on Main Street offers a cozy atmosphere and locally roasted coffee.
She spends her mornings working at her favorite espresso shop around the corner.
They opened an espresso shop to share their love of Italian coffee with their community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cafe - small restaurant selling light meals and drinks

Etymology

From Italian 'espresso' (pressed-out coffee) + English 'shop'.
