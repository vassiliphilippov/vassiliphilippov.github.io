mountain avens

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntn ˈævənz / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntən ˈævənz / (BrE)
a creeping evergreen shrub with large white flowers, found in northern Eurasia and North America
creeping evergreen shrub with white flowers

Examples

In Alaska, the mountain avens thrives in harsh, rocky soils above the tree line.
The delicate flowers of the mountain avens are a common sight during the brief Arctic summer.
Hikers often stop to admire the mountain avens blooming along alpine trails.
