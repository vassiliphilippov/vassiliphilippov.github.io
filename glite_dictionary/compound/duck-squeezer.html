duck-squeezer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌk ˌskwizər / (AmE), / ˈdʌk ˌskwiːzə / (BrE)
a toy in the shape of a duck that makes a quacking sound when squeezed
toy duck that quacks when squeezed

Examples

My baby giggles every time she squeezes her duck-squeezer in the bath.
We gave a bright yellow duck-squeezer as a birthday present to our nephew.
The sound from the duck-squeezer always makes the children laugh during playtime.

Synset Examples

The kids loved playing with the silly duck-squeezer.
