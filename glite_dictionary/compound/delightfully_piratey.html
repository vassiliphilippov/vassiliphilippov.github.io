delightfully piratey

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈlaɪtfəli ˈpaɪrəti / (AmE), / dɪˈlaɪtfəli ˈpaɪrəti / (BrE)
charmingly or pleasingly reminiscent of a pirate or the style of pirates.
pleasingly pirate-like
informal

Examples

She wore a delightfully piratey hat with a large feather sticking out the side.
The decorations gave the whole room a delightfully piratey atmosphere for the children's birthday party.
He greeted the guests in a delightfully piratey manner, shouting 'Ahoy!' as they entered.

Synset Examples

His costume was delightfully piratey for the Halloween party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piratey - resembling or characteristic of a pirate
