meadow-beauty family

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdoʊ ˈbjuːti ˈfæmɪli / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəʊ ˈbjuːti ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants (Melastomataceae) consisting of trees, shrubs, and herbs, many cultivated for their decorative flowers

Examples

The meadow-beauty family includes many attractive shrubs and herbs grown in gardens.
