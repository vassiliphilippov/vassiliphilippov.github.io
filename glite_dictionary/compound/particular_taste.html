particular taste

1. NOUN

/ pərˈtɪkjələr teɪst / (AmE), / pəˈtɪkjʊlə teɪst / (BrE)
a person's specific preferences or likes, especially regarding food, art, or other choices
someone's specific preferences or likes

Examples

She has a particular taste in music and won't listen to just anything.
His particular taste for spicy food makes dining out a challenge.
The designer created the room to suit the client's particular taste.

Synset Examples

He always chooses restaurants for one's particular tastes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preference - a greater liking for one alternative over others
  • Degree Lower: taste - the sense of what is beautiful, attractive, or agreeable
