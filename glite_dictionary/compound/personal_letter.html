personal letter

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːsənl ˈlɛtər / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
a letter written to someone for private or personal matters, rather than official or business reasons
letter on personal matters

Examples

He received a personal letter from his childhood friend after many years apart.
Please keep this personal letter confidential and do not share its contents.
Writing a personal letter can make your message feel warmer and more sincere.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: business letter - a formal letter used for business purposes
