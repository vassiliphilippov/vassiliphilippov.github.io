season ticket

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːzən ˈtɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈsiːzən ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
A ticket that allows attendance at multiple events, performances, or journeys in a series, usually at a reduced price.
multi-event or multi-trip ticket at reduced rate

Examples

She bought a season ticket for the football matches this year.
A season ticket offers significant savings compared to buying single entry tickets.
With his train season ticket, he commutes to work much more economically.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: single ticket - ticket valid for one event or trip
  • Synonym: subscription - periodic payment for regular admission or service
