pet stain

1. NOUN

/ pɛt steɪn / (AmE), / pɛt steɪn / (BrE)
a mark or discoloration caused by an animal, often with an unpleasant odor, especially from urine or feces
a stain caused by a pet, often smelly

Examples

The new carpet cleaner is designed to remove even the toughest pet stain.
She was frustrated to find a large pet stain in the middle of the living room.
If you have a puppy, be prepared to deal with the occasional pet stain.

Synset Examples

He noticed a pet stain on the couch after the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stain - a mark that is difficult to remove
