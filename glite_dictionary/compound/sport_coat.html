sport coat

1. NOUN

/ spɔrt koʊt / (AmE), / spɔːt kəʊt / (BrE)
A man's lightweight, single-breasted jacket, typically not part of a suit, often featuring patterned or colored fabric.
lightweight, single-breasted men's jacket

Examples

He wore a navy sport coat and khaki trousers to the dinner party.
My grandfather gave me his old tweed sport coat from college.
A striped sport coat is part of the school's official uniform.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blazer - a type of jacket similar to a sport coat, often with a more formal or solid-colored appearance
