sun heater

1. NOUN

/ sʌn ˈhiːtər / (AmE), / sʌn ˈhiːtə / (BrE)
A portable, rotating electric heater that uses halogen elements to provide heat, often emitting light resembling sunlight.
portable halogen electric heater

Examples

We kept warm through winter with a sun heater placed in the living room.
The sun heater rotates, distributing heat evenly across the space.
Because the sun heater uses halogen elements, it heats up very quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: space heater - a device for heating an enclosed room
  • Synonym: fan heater - a heater that uses a fan to distribute hot air
