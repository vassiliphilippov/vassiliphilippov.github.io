United Self-Defense Forces of Colombia

1. PROPN

/ juːˈnaɪtɪd ˌsɛlf dɪˈfɛns ˈfɔrsɪz əv kəˈlʌmbiə / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪtɪd ˌsɛlf dɪˈfɛns ˈfɔːsɪz əv kəˈlʌmbiə / (BrE)
a right-wing paramilitary organization in Colombia, formed in 1997 as an alliance of local and regional groups, involved in drug trafficking and violent activities
Colombian paramilitary organization (AUC)

Examples

The United Self-Defense Forces of Colombia were implicated in numerous human rights abuses during the late 1990s.
After years of violent conflict, the Colombian government eventually dismantled the United Self-Defense Forces of Colombia.
Journalists reporting on the drug trade often reference the United Self-Defense Forces of Colombia as a major player.
