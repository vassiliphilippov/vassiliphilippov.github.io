training ship

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪnɪŋ ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnɪŋ ʃɪp / (BrE)
A ship used for instructing students in the skills and practices of seamanship.
ship used to train sailors

Examples

The navy assigned him to a training ship for his first six months at sea.
Several cadets boarded the training ship for their introductory voyage.
The harbor was bustling as the old training ship returned from its latest expedition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: school ship - a ship serving as a school for training sailors
