bumper jack

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌmpɚ ˌdʒæk / (AmE), / ˈbʌmpə ˌdʒæk / (BrE)
a mechanical device for lifting a motor vehicle by its bumper, typically to change a tire or perform repairs
car jack for lifting by the bumper

Examples

He used a bumper jack to lift the car and change the flat tire.
The old bumper jack was kept in the trunk for emergencies.
Modern cars rarely come with a traditional bumper jack these days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car jack - a device for lifting a vehicle, generally by any lifting point
