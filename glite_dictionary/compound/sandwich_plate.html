sandwich plate

1. NOUN

/ ˈsændwɪtʃ pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈsændwɪtʃ pleɪt / (BrE)
A serving consisting of a sandwich or sandwiches, usually presented with garnishes such as salad or pickles.
serving of sandwich(es) with garnishes

Examples

He ordered the sandwich plate with ham and cheese for lunch.
The cafe serves a delicious sandwich plate that includes fresh salad and crisps.
Would you like a sandwich plate or just a plain sandwich?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: platter - a large serving dish or its contents, often assorted foods served together
