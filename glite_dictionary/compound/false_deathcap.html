false deathcap

1. NOUN

/ fɔls ˈdɛθˌkæp / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈdɛθˌkæp / (BrE)
a species of mushroom (Amanita citrina) resembling the deadly death cap but generally not poisonous
a mushroom resembling the death cap

Examples

The false deathcap can be mistaken for the highly poisonous death cap due to its similar appearance.
Foragers are advised to learn the differences between the false deathcap and its deadly cousin.
Despite its name, the false deathcap is usually not harmful if consumed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: death cap - a deadly poisonous mushroom (Amanita phalloides)
