irritable bowel syndrome

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪrɪtəbl ˈbaʊəl sɪnˌdroʊm / (AmE), / ˈɪrɪtəbl ˈbaʊəl ˌsɪndrəʊm / (BrE)
A common disorder involving recurrent abdominal pain and changes in bowel habits, such as diarrhea or constipation, often associated with emotional stress.
digestive disorder causing pain and bowel changes
medical

Examples

Many people with irritable bowel syndrome find that stress can trigger symptoms.
A high-fiber diet may help some individuals manage irritable bowel syndrome.
He was diagnosed with irritable bowel syndrome after months of unexplained abdominal pain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: IBS - abbreviation for irritable bowel syndrome
