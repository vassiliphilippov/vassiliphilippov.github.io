lazy daisy stitch

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪzi ˈdeɪzi stɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈleɪzi ˈdeɪzi stɪtʃ / (BrE)
An embroidery stitch in which long chain stitches are arranged in a petal-like flower pattern.
embroidery stitch forming flower shapes

Examples

The flowers on the cushion were embroidered using the lazy daisy stitch technique.
She taught her daughter how to make petals with a lazy daisy stitch.
A simple lazy daisy stitch can add a cheerful touch to any fabric.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chain stitch - an embroidery stitch in which loops are formed in a chain pattern
