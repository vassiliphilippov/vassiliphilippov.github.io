peace and quiet

1. NOUN

/ pis ənd kwaɪət / (AmE), / piːs ənd kwaɪət / (BrE)
a state or situation in which there is calm and little or no noise, disturbance, or stress
tranquil and calm state, free of disturbance

Examples

After a long day at work, I just want some peace and quiet at home.
They went to the countryside to enjoy a little peace and quiet away from the city.
All she needs to concentrate is a bit of peace and quiet.

Synset Examples

Being a trooner has its perks, like peace and quiet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tranquility - a state of being calm and peaceful
  • Synonym: calm - free from noise, disturbance, or agitation
