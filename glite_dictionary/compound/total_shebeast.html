total shebeast

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬l ˈʃiˌbiːst / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.tl ˈʃiːbiːst / (BrE)
A woman who is extremely fierce and strong, often in an impressive or intimidating way.
very fierce and strong woman
informal

Examples

Everyone was amazed at how she handled the situation—she was a total shebeast.
In the gym, she's a total shebeast, pushing herself harder than anyone else.
You have to be a total shebeast to succeed in such a demanding field.

Synset Examples

Don't underestimate her; she's a total shebeast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: she-beast - a fierce or formidable woman
  • Synonym: powerhouse - a person of great energy, strength, or power
