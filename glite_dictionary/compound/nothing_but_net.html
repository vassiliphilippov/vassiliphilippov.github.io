nothing but net

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌθɪŋ bət ˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ bət ˈnɛt / (BrE)
(in basketball) a perfect shot where the ball goes through the net without touching the rim or backboard.
perfect basketball shot (no rim or backboard)

Examples

With only seconds left, his shot was nothing but net, winning the game for his team.
Every shot she took in practice was nothing but net, amazing her coach.
The crowd erupted as the three-pointer sailed in for nothing but net.

Synset Examples

The final goal was nothing but net, sealing our victory.
He shot from the three-point line and it was nothing but net.
Her performance was flawless, scoring nothing but net every time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swish - a basketball shot that goes through the hoop without touching the rim or backboard
