shady dealing

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃeɪdi ˈdiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪdi ˈdiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
Dishonest or illegal activities, especially in business or financial contexts.
dishonest or illegal activity

Examples

The investigation revealed several shady dealings within the organization.
Journalists uncovered shady dealing between politicians and business leaders.
He denied any shady dealings despite the growing evidence.

Synset Examples

They exposed the scumlord for his shady dealings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fraud - wrongful or criminal deception intended to result in financial or personal gain
  • Synonym: scam - a dishonest scheme or trick
  • Antonym: honest dealing - fair, open, and lawful activity or behavior
