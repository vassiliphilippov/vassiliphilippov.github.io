project head

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːdʒɛkt hɛd / (AmE), / ˈprɒdʒɛkt hɛd / (BrE)
the person who leads or is in charge of a particular project
leader of a project

Examples

After the restructuring, she was appointed as the project head for the new initiative.
The project head must coordinate between different teams to ensure deadlines are met.
As project head, he is responsible for setting goals and managing resources.

Synset Examples

She’s the project head, overseeing everything.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: project manager - person responsible for planning and execution of a project
