Christmas present

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪsməs ˌprɛzənt / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsməs ˌprɛzənt / (BrE)
a gift given to someone at Christmas time
gift given at Christmas

Examples

I wrapped each Christmas present with shiny paper and colorful ribbons.
She was thrilled to find a bicycle waiting for her as her main Christmas present.
Children eagerly opened their Christmas presents on Christmas morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gift - something that is given willingly to someone without payment; a present
  • Synonym: Christmas gift - another term for 'Christmas present'
