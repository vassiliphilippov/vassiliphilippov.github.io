plastics industry

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæstɪks ˌɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstɪks ˌɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector of manufacturing engaged in producing plastic materials and items
sector producing plastic products

Examples

She has worked in the plastics industry for over a decade, specializing in packaging solutions.
Due to environmental concerns, the plastics industry is exploring more sustainable materials.
The growth of the plastics industry has contributed to significant advancements in consumer products.

Related Terms
