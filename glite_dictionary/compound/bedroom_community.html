bedroom community

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdˌruːm kəˈmjuːnəti / (AmE), / ˈbedruːm kəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
A residential area where most residents commute to work in a nearby city or urban center, rather than working locally.
residential area for commuters

Examples

Many people move to a bedroom community to escape the bustle of the city but still work downtown.
After work, she returned to her quiet bedroom community thirty miles outside the city center.
Rapid growth has turned the area into a thriving bedroom community for nearby city workers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commuter town - A town whose residents typically travel elsewhere for work

Etymology

Formed by analogy to 'bedroom suburb', referring to a place chiefly used for sleeping between workdays.
