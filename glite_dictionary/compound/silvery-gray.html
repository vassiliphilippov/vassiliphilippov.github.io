silvery-gray

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlvəri ɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəri ɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a gray color that resembles silver; having a shiny or metallic appearance similar to silver.
gray resembling silver

Examples

The old wolf had a thick silvery-gray coat that shone in the dawn light.
Her silvery-gray hair gave her an air of elegance and wisdom.
Clouds drifted across a silvery-gray sky before the rain began.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: silver - a precious shiny gray-white metal
  • Degree Lower: gray - a color between black and white
