vegetable oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒtəbəl ɔɪl / (BrE)
A liquid edible fat obtained from plants, commonly used for cooking and food preparation.
edible plant-derived oil

Examples

She always uses vegetable oil instead of butter when baking cookies.
The restaurant fries its food in pure vegetable oil to appeal to vegetarian customers.
A bottle of vegetable oil sat on the kitchen counter next to the stove.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: olive oil - a type of vegetable oil made from olives
  • Degree Lower: canola oil - a type of vegetable oil made from rapeseed
  • Antonym: animal fat - edible fat derived from animals, as opposed to plants
