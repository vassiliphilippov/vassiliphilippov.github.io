raspberry bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈræzˌbɛri bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɑːzbəri bʊʃ / (BrE)
A woody shrub that produces raspberries, usually with prickly stems and red, black, or yellow edible berries.
woody shrub that produces raspberries

Examples

We planted a raspberry bush in the garden last spring, and it is already producing fruit.
The raspberry bush at the back of the yard attracts birds and bees when it is in bloom.
Pruning the raspberry bush in the autumn helps encourage healthy growth the following year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raspberry - the edible fruit produced by the raspberry bush
  • Degree Higher: blackberry bush - a shrub producing blackberries
