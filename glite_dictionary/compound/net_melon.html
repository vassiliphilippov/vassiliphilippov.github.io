net melon

1. NOUN

/ nɛt ˈmɛlən / (AmE), / nɛt ˈmɛlən / (BrE)
A variety of muskmelon with a thin, net-like rind and sweet green flesh.
muskmelon with thin netted rind and sweet flesh

Examples

The farmer picked a ripe net melon from the vine for the market.
I prefer the flavor of net melon over other types of muskmelons.
We served chilled slices of net melon at the picnic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cantaloupe - a type of muskmelon with orange flesh and rough skin

Etymology

From the net-like pattern on the rind of the fruit.

2. NOUN

/ nɛt ˈmɛlən / (AmE), / nɛt ˈmɛlən / (BrE)
a variety of muskmelon with a rind covered in netlike markings and typically green flesh
a melon with netlike rind and green flesh

Examples

She sliced the net melon and offered the sweet, green flesh to her guests.
The market vendor arranged the net melons beside the watermelons and cantaloupes.
A net melon is often enjoyed as a refreshing summer dessert in many Asian countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cantaloupe - a type of melon with orange flesh and netted rind
