devil's urn

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛvəlz ɝn / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəlz ɜːn / (BrE)
a dark, cup-shaped fungus of the family Sarcosomataceae, with a brown to black exterior and a black interior
a dark, cup-shaped fungus

Examples

We came across a rare devil's urn while hiking in the woods last spring.
The devil's urn gets its name from its dark, deep-cup appearance.
Mycologists are excited to monitor the spread of devil's urn in the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fungus - a member of a group of spore-producing organisms
