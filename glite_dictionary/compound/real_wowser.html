real wowser

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːəl ˈwaʊzər / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl ˈwaʊzə / (BrE)
Something or someone that is exceptionally impressive, remarkable, or astonishing.
something very impressive or astonishing
informal american

Examples

That final scene was a real wowser and left the whole audience speechless.
Her performance in the competition was a real wowser—absolutely unforgettable.
This new smartphone is a real wowser with all its cutting-edge features.

Synset Examples

That new roller coaster is a real wowser!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: showstopper - something that is exceptionally impressive or attention-grabbing

Etymology

From informal American English; 'wowser' comes from 'wow', expressing astonishment.
