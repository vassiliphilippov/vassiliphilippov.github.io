Gouda cheese

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡaʊdə tʃiz / (AmE), / ˈɡaʊdə tʃiːz / (BrE)
a mild, cream-colored Dutch cheese, usually shaped into balls or wheels
mild Dutch cheese shaped in balls or wheels

Examples

She served slices of Gouda cheese with crackers at the party.
The creamy texture of Gouda cheese makes it perfect for sandwiches.
I bought a wheel of aged Gouda cheese from the market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Edam cheese - mild Dutch cheese with a red rind

Etymology

Named after the city of Gouda in the Netherlands, where the cheese originated.
