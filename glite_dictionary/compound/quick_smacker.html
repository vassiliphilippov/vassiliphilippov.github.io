quick smacker

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk ˈsmækər / (AmE), / kwɪk ˈsmækə / (BrE)
a brief kiss or a quick, light slap, usually delivered suddenly
brief kiss or quick slap

Examples

After the assembly, she gave her friend a quick smacker before saying goodbye.
He leaned over and landed a quick smacker on his daughter's forehead.
Their playful argument ended with a quick smacker to his arm.

Synset Examples

He gave her a quick smacker on the cheek.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peck - a light, quick kiss
  • Synonym: slap - a sharp blow with the open hand
