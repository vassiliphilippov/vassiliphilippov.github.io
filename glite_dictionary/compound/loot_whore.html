loot whore

1. NOUN

/ lut wɔɚ / (AmE), / luːt wɔː / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) A player in video games who is obsessed with collecting in-game loot or valuable items, often at the expense of teamwork or gameplay enjoyment.
slang term for a loot-obsessed gamer
slang offensive

Examples

In every raid, there's always one loot whore who tries to grab all the best gear.
Some guilds ban loot whores to maintain fair play among members.
Calling someone a loot whore can spark heated debates during loot distribution.

Synset Examples

As a loot whore, she spends hours grinding for gear.
Being a loot whore can ruin the fun of the game.
He’s become a loot whore, only caring about rare items.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loot goblin - (slang) a player overly focused on collecting loot in games
  • Antonym: team player - a person who cooperates well with others in a game
