plain wanderer

1. NOUN

/ pleɪn ˈwɑndɚɚ / (AmE), / pleɪn ˈwɒndərə / (BrE)
a small Australian bird (Pedionomus torquatus) related to the button quail, usually found in open plains
Australian plain-dwelling bird

Examples

The plain wanderer is rarely seen because it prefers remote grasslands in Australia.
Birdwatchers traveled great distances hoping to spot a plain wanderer during migration season.
Conservation groups are working to protect habitats crucial to the survival of the plain wanderer.
