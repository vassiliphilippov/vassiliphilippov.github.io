Pee Dee River

1. PROPN

/ pi di ˈrɪvɚ / (AmE), / piː diː ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river flowing through central North Carolina and northeastern South Carolina, emptying into the Atlantic Ocean
river in North and South Carolina, USA

Examples

The Pee Dee River is known for its rich biodiversity and scenic beauty.
Several towns along the Pee Dee River rely on it for water and recreation.
Heavy rainfall last year caused the Pee Dee River to flood its banks.

Etymology

Named after the Pee Dee Native American people of the region.
