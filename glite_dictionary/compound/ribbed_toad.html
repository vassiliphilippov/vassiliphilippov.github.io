ribbed toad

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪbd toʊd / (AmE), / ˈrɪbd təʊd / (BrE)
a frog found in western North America, notable for having a taillike copulatory organ
North American frog with a taillike organ

Examples

The ribbed toad is easily recognized by its unusual taillike organ during mating season.
Biologists studied several ribbed toads in their natural habitat in western North America.
Unlike most frogs, the ribbed toad has a prominent structure used for reproduction.
