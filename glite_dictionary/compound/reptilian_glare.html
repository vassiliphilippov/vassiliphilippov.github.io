reptilian glare

1. NOUN

/ rɛpˈtɪliən ɡlɛr / (AmE), / rɛpˈtɪliən ɡlɛə / (BrE)
A cold, unfeeling stare, likened to that of a reptile.
cold, unfeeling stare

Examples

Her reptilian glare made it clear that she had no sympathy for their plight.
He silenced the room with a reptilian glare that left everyone feeling uneasy.
The villain regarded his opponent with a reptilian glare, cold and devoid of emotion.

Synset Examples

She had a reptilian glare that intimidated everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stare - a fixed or intent look
  • Synonym: glare - an intense, piercing look
