display panel

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈspleɪ ˈpænəl / (AmE), / dɪˈspleɪ ˈpanəl / (BrE)
a vertical surface or screen used for presenting information publicly or visually
vertical surface for showing information

Examples

The museum installed a new display panel explaining the history of the exhibit.
Each display panel in the conference hall showed different event schedules.
The display panel was damaged by the storm and had to be replaced.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: display board - a board for posting notices or displaying information
