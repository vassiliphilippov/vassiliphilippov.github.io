family Lygaeidae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli laɪˈdʒiːɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli laɪˈdʒiːɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of true bugs known as lygaeid bugs, typically seed-feeding insects found worldwide.
family of seed bugs (Lygaeidae)

Examples

Scientists recently discovered a new species within the family Lygaeidae in South America.
Members of the family Lygaeidae are commonly known as seed bugs because they feed on seeds.
The family Lygaeidae includes over a thousand described species worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lygaeid bug - Any insect belonging to the family Lygaeidae

Etymology

From scientific Latin: family name formed from genus Lygaeus.
