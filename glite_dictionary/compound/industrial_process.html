industrial process

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdʌstriəl ˈprɑsɛs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəl ˈprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
a systematic series of mechanical or chemical operations used in industry to manufacture or produce something
method of manufacturing or production in industry

Examples

The company invested in a new industrial process to increase efficiency and reduce waste.
Automation has revolutionized the industrial process for many manufacturers in recent years.
Stringent safety protocols must be followed during every stage of the industrial process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manufacturing process - series of steps involved in making a product
