job candidate

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑb ˈkændəˌdeɪt / (AmE), / dʒɒb ˈkændɪdeɪt / (BrE)
a person who applies for or is being considered for a particular job
person applying for a job

Examples

Each job candidate must submit a résumé and cover letter before the interview.
The company interviewed several job candidates for the marketing position.
As a job candidate, she prepared thoroughly for every stage of the hiring process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: applicant - a person who formally applies for something, typically a job
