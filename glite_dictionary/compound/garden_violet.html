garden violet

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdn ˈvaɪələt / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˈvaɪəlɪt / (BrE)
A European violet plant, typically having purple to white flowers, that is widely naturalized in gardens.
European violet with purple or white flowers

Examples

The garden violet adds vibrant color to early spring borders.
Many gardeners favor the garden violet for its delicate, fragrant blooms.
She picked a bouquet of garden violets from the edge of the lawn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: violet - a small plant with purple or white flowers
