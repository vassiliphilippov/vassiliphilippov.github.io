picnic ground

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪk.nɪk ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈpɪk.nɪk ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
An area of land designated for people to have picnics, often equipped with tables or other facilities.
area for picnicking

Examples

Let's meet at the picnic ground near the lake for lunch this Saturday.
The city has recently renovated the old picnic ground with new benches and grills.
Families gathered at the picnic ground to celebrate the summer festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: picnic area - an area for picnics, often with tables or benches
