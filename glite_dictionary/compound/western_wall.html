Western Wall

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɛstərn wɔl / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən wɔːl / (BrE)
The last remaining wall of the Second Temple in Jerusalem, considered a sacred place of prayer and lamentation in Judaism.
sacred Jewish site in Jerusalem

Examples

Thousands of people gather at the Western Wall to pray every year.
During their visit to Jerusalem, they left a note in the cracks of the Western Wall.
The Western Wall is a site of deep emotional and historical significance for many Jews.
