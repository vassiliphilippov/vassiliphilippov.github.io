apple mint

1. NOUN

/ ˈæp.əl mɪnt / (AmE), / ˈæp.əl mɪnt / (BrE)
A species of mint with apple-scented leaves, native to southern and western Europe and naturalized in parts of the United States, often used in cooking and herbal teas.
a mint plant with apple-scented leaves

Examples

She grows apple mint in her garden for use in salads and herbal teas.
The refreshing scent of apple mint filled the kitchen as he chopped the leaves.
Apple mint can add a unique flavor to sauces and desserts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mint - an aromatic plant of the genus Mentha
