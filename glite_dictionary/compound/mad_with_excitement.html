mad with excitement

1. ADJ

/ mæd wɪð ɪkˈsaɪtmənt / (AmE), / mad wɪð ɪkˈsaɪtmənt / (BrE)
extremely enthusiastic or thrilled, often to the point of losing self-control
extremely excited or thrilled

Examples

The children were mad with excitement when they saw the roller coaster for the first time.
She became mad with excitement as the concert started and her favorite band appeared on stage.
Fans rushed the field, mad with excitement after the team scored the winning goal.

Synset Examples

They were mad with excitement after winning the championship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecstatic - feeling or expressing overwhelming happiness or joy
  • Antonym: calm - not showing or feeling excitement, anger, or nervousness
