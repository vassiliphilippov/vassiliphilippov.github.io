really sucky

1. ADJ

/ ˈɹɪli ˈsʌki / (AmE), / ˈɹiːli ˈsʌki / (BrE)
Very bad, unpleasant, or disappointing; of notably poor quality or unsatisfactory.
very bad or disappointing
informal

Examples

The weather today is really sucky—it's cold and pouring rain.
Her interview went really sucky, and she didn't get the job.
This hotel room is really sucky, with broken lights and no hot water.

Synset Examples

The food at that restaurant was really sucky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrible - very bad, of poor quality
  • Synonym: awful - extremely bad or unpleasant
