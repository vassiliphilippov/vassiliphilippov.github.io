for sale

1. ADJ

/ fɔɹ seɪl / (AmE), / fɔː seɪl / (BrE)
available to be bought by someone; offered for purchase
available to buy

Examples

The apartment next door is for sale, and several people have already viewed it.
All items marked with a red sticker are for sale at half price.
She placed a sign in the yard saying the car was for sale because she was moving abroad.

Synset Examples

purchasable goods
many houses in the area are for sale

Related Terms

  • Antonym: for rent - available for someone to rent
