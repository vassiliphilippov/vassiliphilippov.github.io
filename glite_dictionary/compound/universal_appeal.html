universal appeal

1. NOUN

/ juˌnɪvɚsəl əˈpil / (AmE), / juːˌnɪvəsəl əˈpiːl / (BrE)
the quality of being attractive or interesting to people of all types and backgrounds
broad attractiveness to many people

Examples

The film’s combination of humor and adventure gave it a universal appeal.
Songs with universal appeal often become international hits.
The designer hoped his new product would have universal appeal across cultures.

Synset Examples

The movie’s plot had a universal appeal to audiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wide appeal - attractiveness to a large number of people
