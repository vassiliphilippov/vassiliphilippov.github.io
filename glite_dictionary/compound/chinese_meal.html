Chinese meal

1. NOUN

/ tʃaɪˈniz miːl / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈniːz miːl / (BrE)
a meal consisting of dishes characteristic of Chinese cuisine
a meal with Chinese cuisine

Examples

We decided to order a Chinese meal for dinner last night.
A traditional Chinese meal often includes rice, several side dishes, and soup.
They celebrated his promotion with a special Chinese meal at their favorite restaurant.

Synset Examples

They enjoyed a Chinese meal together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chinese food - food from Chinese cuisine
