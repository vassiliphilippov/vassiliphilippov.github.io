pillar box

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪlər ˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈpɪlə ˌbɒks / (BrE)
A tall, red, cylindrical public letter box, especially as found on streets in Britain and some other countries.
a red, cylindrical British street letter box
british

Examples

You can post your letter in the pillar box on the corner.
The distinctive red pillar box is a familiar sight across British towns.
He stopped beside the pillar box to mail a postcard to his friend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mailbox - a box for receiving or sending mail
