viral infection

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪrəl ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈvaɪərəl ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
An illness or disease caused by the invasion of the body by a virus.
illness caused by a virus
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her illness as a viral infection rather than a bacterial one.
Rest and fluids are usually recommended to help your body fight a viral infection.
Common cold and flu are both examples of a viral infection.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bacterial infection - infection caused by bacteria rather than a virus
