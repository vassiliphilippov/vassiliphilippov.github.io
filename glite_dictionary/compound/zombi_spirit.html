zombi spirit

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɑm.bi ˈspɪr.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈzɒm.bi ˈspɪr.ɪt / (BrE)
In voodooism, a spirit or supernatural force believed to reanimate a dead body.
voodoo spirit that reanimates bodies

Examples

According to Haitian folklore, a zombi spirit can possess the body of a deceased person.
Some rituals are believed to invoke a zombi spirit to reanimate the dead.
The legend of the zombi spirit has inspired many stories and films about the undead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zombie - a dead body reanimated by supernatural means or a person in such a state; voodoo folklore

Etymology

From West African Kongo *nzambi* (god, spirit), entering English via Haitian Creole and French.
