Christmas bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪs·məs bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.məs bʊʃ / (BrE)
An Australian tree or shrub with red flowers, often used as a decoration during Christmas.
Australian shrub/tree used in Christmas decoration

Examples

The Christmas bush in their garden looked especially beautiful during the holiday season.
Australians often decorate their homes with Christmas bush instead of traditional fir trees.
She picked some bright red flowers from the Christmas bush to use in a festive arrangement.
