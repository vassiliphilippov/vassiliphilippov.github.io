vermillion flycatcher

1. NOUN

/ vərˈmɪljən flaɪˌkætʃər / (AmE), / vəˈmɪljən flaɪˌkatʃə / (BrE)
a tropical American bird of the flycatcher family, with the adult male showing bright scarlet and black plumage, found as far north as southern Texas and Arizona
tropical American bird with bright red male plumage

Examples

Birdwatchers were thrilled to spot a vermillion flycatcher perched near the riverbank.
The male vermillion flycatcher dazzles observers with its bright red and black coloring.
In southern Texas, the vermillion flycatcher is considered a special find among migrants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flycatcher - a bird of a family that catches insects in flight

Etymology

From 'vermillion', meaning bright red, referring to the male bird's coloration, and 'flycatcher', the bird family name.
