personal best

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːsənl ˈbɛst / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl ˈbɛst / (BrE)
the best performance an individual has ever achieved in a particular activity or sport
one's best individual achievement

Examples

After months of training, she finally set a new personal best in the 100-meter sprint.
He was thrilled to finish the exam in record time and achieve his personal best score.
Despite difficult conditions, the swimmer broke his personal best for the distance.

Synset Examples

I’m going to tri for a personal best in the race.
He wanted to beat his personal best in the marathon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: record - the best performance officially recorded
  • Antonym: worst - the poorest performance by an individual
