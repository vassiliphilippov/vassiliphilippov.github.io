geothermal industry

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒiːoʊˈθɜːrməl ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəʊˈθɜːməl ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
all industries involved in the production, development, and sale of geothermal energy.
sector producing geothermal energy

Examples

Government policies have played a crucial role in the growth of the geothermal industry worldwide.
She found a job as an engineer in the geothermal industry after graduating from university.
Research and innovation have made the geothermal industry more competitive with other energy sectors.

Synset Examples

Broader issues concern the extent to which the legal framework for encouragement of renewable energy assists in encouraging geothermal industry innovation and development.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: renewable energy industry - sector involved in producing energy from renewable sources
