line of work

1. NOUN

/ laɪn əv wɜrk / (AmE), / laɪn əv wɜːk / (BrE)
a particular type of job, profession, or occupation that a person does to earn a living
type of job or profession

Examples

She has been in this line of work for over twenty years and still loves her job.
In my line of work, attention to detail is absolutely essential.
People often ask how I got into this particular line of work.

Synset Examples

he's not in my line of business

Related Terms

  • Synonym: profession - an occupation requiring specialized education and training
  • Synonym: occupation - a person's usual or principal work or business
  • Antonym: hobby - an activity done regularly for pleasure, not as a main job
