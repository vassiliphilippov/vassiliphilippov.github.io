in view

1. ADJ

/ ɪn vju / (AmE), / ɪn vjuː / (BrE)
at or within a distance where something can be seen; visible or within sight
visible; within sight

Examples

The mountain peak was finally in view as we reached the summit.
Please keep the children in view while they play outside.
She kept her bicycle in view so it wouldn't be stolen.

Synset Examples

not a policeman in sight
kept the monkey in view

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in sight - visible, within the field of vision
  • Antonym: out of view - not visible, hidden from sight
