Revolutionary Proletarian Army

1. PROPN

/ rɪˌvɑːluˈʃəːneri proʊˌliːˈtɛriən ˈɑːrmi / (AmE), / rɪˌvɒljʊˈʃənəri prəʊˌliːˈtɛəriən ˈɑːmi / (BrE)
a guerrilla group and former urban hit squad associated with the Communist Party in the Philippines, formed in the 1980s
guerrilla group in the Philippines

Examples

The Revolutionary Proletarian Army was involved in several armed conflicts during the late 20th century in the Philippines.
After splitting from the main communist party, the Revolutionary Proletarian Army pursued its own agenda.
The government initiated peace talks with the Revolutionary Proletarian Army to resolve ongoing hostilities.
