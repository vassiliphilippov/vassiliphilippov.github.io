canary grass

1. NOUN

/ kəˈnɛri ɡræs / (AmE), / kəˈneəri ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A grass native to the Canary Islands, with seeds commonly used as food for caged birds.
grass with seeds used for bird feed

Examples

Farmers often grow canary grass to harvest its seeds for use in birdseed mixtures.
The pet shop stocks several varieties of canary grass for caged birds.
Researchers are studying how canary grass can be used as a sustainable crop.
