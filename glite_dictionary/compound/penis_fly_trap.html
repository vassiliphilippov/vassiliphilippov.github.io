penis fly trap

1. NOUN

/ ˈpinɪs flaɪ træp / (AmE), / ˈpiːnɪs flaɪ træp / (BrE)
A humorous or slang term for a revealing or suggestive costume or outfit, especially one designed or styled to attract attention to the wearer's crotch area.
slang term for a suggestive costume style
slang informal
