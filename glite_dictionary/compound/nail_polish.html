nail polish

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪl ˌpɑlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈneɪl ˌpɒlɪʃ / (BrE)
a colored or clear cosmetic lacquer applied to fingernails or toenails to add color or shine
cosmetic lacquer for fingernails or toenails

Examples

She applied a fresh coat of nail polish before the party.
His collection of nail polish includes every color imaginable.
The nail polish dried within minutes, leaving a glossy finish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: varnish - resinous liquid that dries to a hard, shiny coating
