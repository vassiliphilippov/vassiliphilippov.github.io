controversial figure

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːntrəˈvɜːrʃəl ˈfɪgjər / (AmE), / ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃl̩ ˈfɪɡə / (BrE)
A person who provokes strong public disagreement or debate.
person causing public disagreement

Examples

He became a controversial figure after publishing his outspoken essays.
Although admired by some, the politician remained a controversial figure throughout his career.
The director's bold decisions turned him into a controversial figure in the film industry.

Synset Examples

Billary has become a controversial figure lately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: controversial - giving rise or likely to give rise to controversy or public disagreement
