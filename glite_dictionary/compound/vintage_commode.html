vintage commode

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪntɪdʒ kəˈmoʊd / (AmE), / ˈvɪntɪdʒ kəˈməʊd / (BrE)
an antique or old-fashioned toilet, especially one found in historical buildings
antique or old-style toilet

Examples

The old museum still showcases a vintage commode in the restored bathroom.
She purchased a vintage commode at an antique auction for her period home.
Many visitors were fascinated by the ornate design of the vintage commode on display.

Synset Examples

The old house had a vintage commode that looked charming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commode - a piece of furniture serving as a toilet or for storage

Etymology

From French 'commode' (convenient, toilet), with 'vintage' denoting age or period style.
