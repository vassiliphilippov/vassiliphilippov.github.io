go board

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ bɔːd / (BrE)
a flat square board marked with a grid on which the game of Go is played
board used for playing Go

Examples

He bought a traditional wooden go board for his collection.
The players gathered around the go board to start their match.
A 19x19 go board is standard for tournament play.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Go - an abstract strategy board game for two players
