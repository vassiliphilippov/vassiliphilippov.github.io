large indefinite quantity

1. NOUN

/ lɑrdʒ ˌɪndəˈfɪnət ˈkwɑntəti / (AmE), / lɑːdʒ ˌɪndɪˈfɪnɪt ˈkwɒntɪti / (BrE)
an amount or number that is not specified but greater than average in size or magnitude
a vague but substantial amount

Examples

The chef needed a large indefinite quantity of flour for his special recipe.
We received a large indefinite quantity of complaints about the broken website.
There is a large indefinite quantity of data to analyze before we can draw any conclusions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quantity - an amount or number of something
  • Antonym: small indefinite quantity - an indefinite quantity that is below average in size or magnitude
