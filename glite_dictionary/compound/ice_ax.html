ice ax

1. NOUN

/ aɪs æks / (AmE), / aɪs æks / (BrE)
a mountaineering tool with a sharp pick and adze, used for cutting footholds in ice and aiding climbers' safety
tool for cutting footholds in ice

Examples

The climber used his ice ax to carve steps into the frozen slope.
Always carry an ice ax when venturing onto glaciers for safety.
She learned how to properly grip the ice ax during her mountaineering course.
