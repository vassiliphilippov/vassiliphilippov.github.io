piano accordion

1. NOUN

/ piˈænoʊ əˌkɔrdiən / (AmE), / pɪˈænəʊ əˈkɔːdiən / (BrE)
a type of accordion fitted with a piano-style keyboard for the right hand and buttons for the left hand
accordion with piano keyboard

Examples

She played the piano accordion at the family reunion, filling the room with lively music.
The music store had a wide selection of piano accordions in various sizes and colors.
Learning the piano accordion requires mastering both the keyboard and the bellows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accordion - a musical instrument played by expanding and contracting a bellows while pressing keys or buttons
