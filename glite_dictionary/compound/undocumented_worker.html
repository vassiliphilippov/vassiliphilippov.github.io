undocumented worker

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌnˈdɑːkjəˌmɛntɪd ˈwɜrkɚ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈdɒkjʊˌmɛntɪd ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who works in a country without legal immigration status or work authorization
person working without legal immigration status

Examples

Many industries rely on undocumented workers for labor, despite the legal and ethical issues involved.
The government introduced new policies affecting the rights of the undocumented worker.
As an undocumented worker, she faced constant fear of deportation.

Synset Examples

His story highlighted the struggles of being an undocumented worker.
The restaurant hired an undocumented worker by mistake.
They discussed the challenges faced by undocumented workers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illegal immigrant - person living in a country without legal permission
