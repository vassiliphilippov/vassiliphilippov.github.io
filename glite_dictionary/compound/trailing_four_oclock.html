trailing four o'clock

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪlɪŋ fɔr əˈklɑk / (AmE), / ˈtreɪlɪŋ fɔːr əˈklɒk / (BrE)
A trailing plant found in the southwestern United States and Mexico, known for its clusters of three brilliant deep pink flowers close to the ground.
a trailing plant with deep pink clustered flowers

Examples

The trailing four o'clock blossoms add vibrant color to desert gardens.
We spotted a trailing four o'clock growing among the rocks on our hike.
Botanists study the unique flowering patterns of the trailing four o'clock.
