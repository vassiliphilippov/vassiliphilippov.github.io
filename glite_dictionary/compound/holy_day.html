holy day

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊli deɪ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli deɪ / (BrE)
A day set aside for religious observance or celebration.
day for religious observance

Examples

Many people attend church on a holy day to honor their faith.
The festival is considered a holy day in several religions.
Some businesses close during significant holy days out of respect for tradition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holiday - a day of festivity or recreation when no work is done
