riding crop

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪdɪŋ krɑp / (AmE), / ˈraɪdɪŋ krɒp / (BrE)
a short whip with a thin flexible end, used in horse riding
short whip used in horse riding

Examples

The jockey struck the horse lightly with the riding crop as they neared the finish line.
She forgot to bring her riding crop to the stable and had to borrow one.
The old riding crop hung on the wall as a reminder of her equestrian days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whip - a long, thin object for striking or controlling animals
