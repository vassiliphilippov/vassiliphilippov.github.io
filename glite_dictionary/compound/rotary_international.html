Rotary International

1. PROPN

/ ˈroʊˌtɛri ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəl / (AmE), / ˈrəʊtəri ˌɪntəˈnæʃ(ə)nəl / (BrE)
an international service organization comprised of local clubs of business and professional leaders who promote humanitarian work and world peace
global service club organization

Examples

My grandfather was an active member of Rotary International for over 30 years.
Rotary International organizes annual fundraisers to support clean water projects worldwide.
The city honored Rotary International for its ongoing commitment to community service.

Etymology

Founded in 1905 in Chicago, named from the early practice of rotating meeting locations among members' places of business.
