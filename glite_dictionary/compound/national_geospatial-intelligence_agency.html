National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency

1. PROPN

/ ˌnæʃənəl dʒioʊˌspæʃl ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns ˈeɪndʒənsi / (AmE), / ˌnæʃənəl dʒiːəʊˌspæʃl ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns ˈeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
a U.S. government agency responsible for providing geographic intelligence in support of national security and defense operations
U.S. agency for geographic intelligence

Examples

After the natural disaster, the National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency provided vital maps to emergency responders.
The National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency uses satellite imagery to support military operations worldwide.
Many analysts at the National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency specialize in interpreting aerial photographs for security purposes.
