golden buttons

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈbʌtn̩z / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈbʌtn̩z / (BrE)
a perennial aromatic herb native to Eurasia, with buttonlike yellow flower heads and bitter pinnate leaves, sometimes used medicinally
a Eurasian perennial herb with yellow buttonlike flowers

Examples

The golden buttons blossomed early in the meadow, spreading a warm yellow across the field.
Her grandmother used dried golden buttons to make herbal remedies for headaches.
Clusters of golden buttons lined the village path, their scent filling the morning air.
