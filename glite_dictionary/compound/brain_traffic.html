brain traffic

1. NOUN

/ breɪn ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / breɪn ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
a state of having excessive mental activity or cluttered thoughts, often causing distraction or difficulty concentrating
mental clutter or excessive thoughts
informal

Examples

After a long day at work, my brain traffic makes it hard to relax.
She used breathing techniques to reduce her brain traffic and regain her focus.
Too much brain traffic during the meeting left him feeling exhausted.

Synset Examples

Meditation helps reduce brain traffic and improve focus.
There's too much brain traffic to think clearly.
He felt overwhelmed by the brain traffic during exams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental clutter - disorganized or excessive thoughts interfering with focus
  • Synonym: mind chatter - persistent inner dialogue or unquiet thoughts
  • Antonym: mental clarity - a state of clear, focused thinking
