twinge of sadness

1. NOUN

/ twɪndʒ əv ˈsædnəs / (AmE), / twɪndʒ əv ˈsædnəs / (BrE)
a brief, sharp feeling of sadness that occurs suddenly or unexpectedly
brief sharp feeling of sadness

Examples

She felt a twinge of sadness when she saw her childhood home for the last time.
A twinge of sadness passed over his face as he heard the news.
Even in moments of joy, a twinge of sadness can suddenly appear.

Synset Examples

A twinge of sadness hit me as I read the letter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pang of sadness - a sudden sharp feeling of sadness
