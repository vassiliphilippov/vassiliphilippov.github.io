Green River

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡrin ˈrɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡriːn ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in the western United States that rises in Wyoming and flows south through Utah, joining the Colorado River
river in the western United States

Examples

The Green River winds through beautiful canyons in Utah and Wyoming.
We camped near the banks of the Green River during our road trip across the west.
The Green River eventually merges with the Colorado River, adding to its flow.
