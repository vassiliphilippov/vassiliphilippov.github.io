grey sole

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪ soʊl / (AmE), / ɡreɪ səʊl / (BrE)
The greyish-white edible flesh of a flatfish, often used as food.
edible flesh of a flatfish

Examples

The restaurant featured lightly grilled grey sole with lemon butter sauce.
She bought a pound of fresh grey sole for dinner.
Many seafood lovers appreciate the delicate flavor of grey sole.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemon sole - edible flesh of another type of flatfish
  • Synonym: flounder - flesh or fish of another kind of flatfish
