health care provider

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθ ˌkɛr prəˈvaɪdər / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθ ˌkeə prəˈvaɪdə / (BrE)
A person or organization that provides medical, preventive, diagnostic, or therapeutic services to individuals.
person or organization giving medical care
medical

Examples

If you have any symptoms, consult your health care provider as soon as possible.
The law requires every health care provider to protect patient privacy.
Most health care providers recommend regular check-ups for children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: health professional - a person qualified to practice medicine, nursing, or allied health professions
