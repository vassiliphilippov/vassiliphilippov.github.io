biblical theme

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪblɪkəl θiːm / (AmE), / ˈbɪblɪkəl θiːm / (BrE)
A theme, idea, or subject in literature, art, or culture that relates to the Bible or its teachings.
theme relating to the Bible

Examples

Many Renaissance paintings feature a biblical theme inspired by stories from the Old Testament.
The novel explores forgiveness as a central biblical theme in its narrative.
Her sermon focused on the biblical theme of redemption and hope.

Synset Examples

The movie had a biblical theme that resonated deeply.
