curtained off

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɝːtənd ˈɔf / (AmE), / ˈkɜːtənd ˈɒf / (BrE)
separated or isolated from an area by a curtain, typically to provide privacy
separated by a curtain for privacy

Examples

The beds in the emergency room were all curtained off to give patients some privacy.
A section of the large hall was curtained off for a private meeting.
The artists' workspace was curtained off from the rest of the gallery.

Synset Examples

The doctor curtained off the waiting area for patients' privacy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screened off - separated or divided from an area using a screen for privacy or protection
