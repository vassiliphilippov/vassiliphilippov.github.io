Gaelic-speaking

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡeɪlɪk ˌspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡaːlɪk ˌspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
able to communicate in Gaelic; speaking the Gaelic language.
speaking the Gaelic language

Examples

Many people on the Isle of Skye are Gaelic-speaking and continue traditional customs.
The Gaelic-speaking community gathered for the annual cultural festival.
She enrolled her child in a Gaelic-speaking primary school in the Highlands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gaelic - the Celtic language spoken in parts of Scotland and Ireland
