hairpin bend

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrˌpɪn bend / (AmE), / ˈheə.pɪn bend / (BrE)
a sharp U-shaped bend in a road, often found on steep inclines or mountain routes
a sharp U-shaped bend in a road

Examples

We had to slow down significantly to navigate the hairpin bend on the mountain road.
Several accidents have occurred at that hairpin bend during foggy weather.
He carefully steered the car through each hairpin bend during the rally.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: switchback - a road with many sharp turns, especially in mountains
  • Degree Lower: curve - a general bend or turn in a road or path
