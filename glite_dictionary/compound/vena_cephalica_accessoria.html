vena cephalica accessoria

1. NOUN

/ ˈveɪ.nə səˈfæl.ɪ.kə əkˈsɛs.əˌrɪ.ə / (AmE), / ˈviː.nə sɪˈfæl.ɪ.kə əkˈsɛs.əˌrɪ.ə / (BrE)
a vein that runs along the radial edge of the forearm and joins the cephalic vein near the elbow
a vein of the forearm
medical

Examples

During the surgical procedure, the vena cephalica accessoria was clearly visible near the patient's elbow.
Doctors sometimes use the vena cephalica accessoria for intravenous access if other veins are not suitable.
Anatomy students must be able to identify the vena cephalica accessoria during dissection of the forearm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cephalic vein - a large superficial vein running along the lateral side of the arm

Etymology

From Latin: 'vena' (vein), 'cephalica' (of the head), 'accessoria' (accessory).
