wild spurge

1. NOUN

/ waɪld spɜrdʒ / (AmE), / waɪld spɜːdʒ / (BrE)
a perennial spurge of the United States with showy white petal-like bracts
a perennial US spurge with white bracts

Examples

The fields were dotted with patches of wild spurge blooming in early summer.
Gardeners should be cautious when handling wild spurge, as its sap can irritate the skin.
We identified the plant by its distinctive showy white bracts, typical of wild spurge.
