business deal

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs diːl / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs diːl / (BrE)
an agreement or arrangement in which business is transacted, typically involving buying or selling goods or services
agreement for a business transaction

Examples

The company closed a major business deal with an international partner last month.
Negotiating a business deal requires keen attention to detail and effective communication.
He was praised for his ability to finalize a business deal quickly and efficiently.

Synset Examples

it was a package deal
I had no further trade with him
he's a master of the business deal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transaction - an instance of buying or selling something; a business exchange
  • Synonym: agreement - a negotiated arrangement between parties
