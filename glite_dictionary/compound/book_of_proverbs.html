Book of Proverbs

1. PROPN

/ bʊk əv ˈprɑːvɚbz / (AmE), / bʊk əv ˈprɒvɜːbz / (BrE)
a book of the Old Testament in the Bible, composed of wise sayings and teachings, traditionally attributed to Solomon and other Israeli sages
biblical book of wise sayings

Examples

Many people turn to the Book of Proverbs for guidance on leading a moral life.
She quoted a verse from the Book of Proverbs during her speech.
The wisdom found in the Book of Proverbs has inspired generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Proverbs - another name for the Book of Proverbs in the Bible
