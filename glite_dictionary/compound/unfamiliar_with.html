unfamiliar with

1. ADJ

/ ʌnfəˈmɪljɚ wɪθ / (AmE), / ʌnfəˈmɪliə wɪð / (BrE)
having little or no knowledge or experience of something
lacking knowledge of

Examples

She felt unfamiliar with the new software, so she asked for help.
Many tourists are unfamiliar with local customs when they first arrive in a foreign country.
As a new employee, he was unfamiliar with the company's procedures.

Synset Examples

unacquainted with city ways

Related Terms

  • Antonym: acquainted with - having knowledge or experience of
