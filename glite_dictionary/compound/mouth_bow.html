mouth bow

1. NOUN

/ maʊθ boʊ / (AmE), / maʊθ bəʊ / (BrE)
a simple musical instrument consisting of a flexible bow with a string, played by holding it between the teeth and changing the mouth shape to alter pitch and tone while striking or plucking the string
musical instrument held in mouth and played by plucking

Examples

The musician demonstrated how to play the mouth bow, producing haunting melodies with subtle changes in mouth shape.
In some cultures, the mouth bow is used to accompany traditional songs and stories.
She made rhythmic sounds with her mouth bow by plucking the string and adjusting her mouth cavity.
