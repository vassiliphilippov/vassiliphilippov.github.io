tropical pitcher plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɑpɪkəl ˈpɪtʃər plænt / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪkəl ˈpɪtʃə plɑːnt / (BrE)
a tropical carnivorous plant of the genus Nepenthes, bearing distinctive pitcher-shaped leaves that trap and digest insects
a tropical carnivorous plant (Nepenthes)

Examples

The tropical pitcher plant uses its modified leaves to trap and digest insects in rainforests.
Botanists often study tropical pitcher plants to understand carnivorous plant evolution.
A tropical pitcher plant can be a fascinating addition to a greenhouse collection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nepenthes - genus of tropical pitcher plants
  • Synonym: pitcher plant - any plant with leaves modified to form a pitcher-like trap

Etymology

From 'pitcher plant' (due to the shape of its leaves); genus Nepenthes from Greek nēpenthes, 'banishing sorrow'.
