pregame festivities

1. NOUN

/ ˌpriːˌɡeɪm fɛˈstɪvɪtiz / (AmE), / ˌpriːˌɡeɪm fɛˈstɪvɪtiz / (BrE)
celebrations or social events held before a sports game or match, often involving food, drinks, and entertainment
celebrations before a sports game

Examples

The pregame festivities included a barbecue, live music, and games for children in the parking lot.
Fans arrived early to take part in the pregame festivities before kickoff.
She enjoys the pregame festivities as much as watching the actual match.

Synset Examples

We always have snacks during the pregame festivities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pre-game - occurring before a game or event
