fantard enthusiasm

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæn.tɑrd ɪnˈθu.ziˌæzəm / (AmE), / ˈfan.tɑːd ɪnˈθjuː.zi.æzəm / (BrE)
Excessive and sometimes obsessive enthusiasm for a particular fandom, often displayed in an overzealous or irrational way. The term is considered derogatory and may be offensive.
excessive, often obsessive fandom enthusiasm
slang offensive

Examples

His fantard enthusiasm sometimes made other fans uncomfortable at the conventions.
You could see her fantard enthusiasm in every online debate about the show.
Many online communities warn against fantard enthusiasm turning discussions toxic.

Synset Examples

Her fantard enthusiasm was evident during the trivia contest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fanaticism - extreme enthusiasm or zeal, especially in religion or politics
  • Degree Lower: enthusiasm - intense and eager enjoyment or interest

Etymology

Blend of 'fan' (enthusiast) and 'retard' (offensive term; used here pejoratively), plus 'enthusiasm'.
