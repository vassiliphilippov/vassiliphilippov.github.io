Canandaigua Lake

1. PROPN

/ kəˌnæn.dəˈɡweɪ leɪk / (AmE), / kəˌnæn.dəˈɡweɪ leɪk / (BrE)
A glacial lake in central New York State, one of the Finger Lakes.
glacial lake in central New York, USA

Examples

Tourists often visit Canandaigua Lake for boating and fishing during the summer months.
The vineyards surrounding Canandaigua Lake are known for producing excellent wines.
During our trip to upstate New York, we took a scenic drive alongside Canandaigua Lake.

Etymology

From the Seneca word *Ga-nun-da-gwa* meaning 'the chosen spot.'
