mask of pregnancy

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæsk əv prəˈɡnænsi / (AmE), / ˈmɑːsk əv prəˈɡnænsi / (BrE)
a tan or brownish discoloration of the face occurring in women, especially during pregnancy or when using oral contraceptives
facial discoloration during pregnancy
medical

Examples

Some women develop a noticeable mask of pregnancy on their cheeks and forehead during the second trimester.
The mask of pregnancy typically fades a few months after childbirth.
Doctors advise using sunscreen to prevent the mask of pregnancy from becoming more pronounced.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: melasma - dark skin discoloration, especially on the face, common in pregnancy
