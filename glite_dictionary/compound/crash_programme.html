crash programme

1. NOUN

/ kræʃ ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / kræʃ ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
a rapid and intensive course of action, training, or research, especially undertaken to meet an urgent need or emergency
intensive emergency action or training
british

Examples

The government launched a crash programme to improve healthcare infrastructure after the outbreak.
She enrolled in a crash programme to learn computer skills before starting her new job.
A crash programme was implemented to increase energy production during the crisis.

Synset Examples

he took a crash course in Italian on his way to Italy
his first job was a crash course in survival and in learning how to get along with people
a crash programme is needed to create new jobs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crash course - an intensive, short course of instruction on a subject
