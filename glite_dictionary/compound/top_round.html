top round

1. NOUN

/ tɑp ˈraʊnd / (AmE), / tɒp ˈraʊnd / (BrE)
a lean cut of beef from the upper part of the round, typically used for roasting or slicing for deli meat
a lean beef cut from the upper round

Examples

The chef recommended roasting the top round for a tender and flavorful dinner.
I bought a top round to make homemade roast beef sandwiches this weekend.
When sliced thin, top round works well in stir-fry recipes for a lean protein option.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bottom round - a cut of beef from the lower part of the round
