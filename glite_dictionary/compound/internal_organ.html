internal organ

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtɜrnəl ˈɔrɡən / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːnəl ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
a major organ situated within the body, such as the heart, liver, or kidneys
organ located inside the body

Examples

The heart is an internal organ vital for pumping blood throughout the body.
A severe injury can damage an internal organ and lead to life-threatening complications.
Doctors used ultrasound to examine each internal organ after the accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organ - a part of the body with a specific function
