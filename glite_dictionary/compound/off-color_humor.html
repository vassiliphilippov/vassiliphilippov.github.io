off-color humor

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔf ˈkʌlɚ ˈhjuːmɚ / (AmE), / ˌɒf ˈkʌlə ˈhjuːmə / (BrE)
Humor that is considered inappropriate, risqué, or vulgar, often involving subjects seen as offensive or in poor taste.
inappropriate or vulgar humor

Examples

Many late-night talk shows rely on off-color humor to get big laughs from their audience.
Some people enjoy off-color humor, while others find it distasteful or offensive.
The movie was criticized for its reliance on off-color humor throughout the script.

Synset Examples

The comedian's off-color humor was not well received.
His off-color jokes didn't sit well with the audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crude humor - humor that is vulgar or indecent
  • Antonym: clean humor - humor that avoids vulgar or offensive topics

Etymology

From the phrase 'off-color' (meaning improper or indecent), originally from color terminology in printing and textiles.
