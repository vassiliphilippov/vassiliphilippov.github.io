newspaper ad

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər æd / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə æd / (BrE)
an advertisement printed and published in a newspaper
advertisement in a newspaper

Examples

She found her first job through a newspaper ad in the local press.
The company placed a large newspaper ad to announce its new product line.
Many people still read the newspaper ad section when looking for an apartment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classified ad - advertisement listed with others in a newspaper, usually in a small format
  • Synonym: display ad - advertisement in a newspaper featuring graphics or large text, visually distinct from classified ads
