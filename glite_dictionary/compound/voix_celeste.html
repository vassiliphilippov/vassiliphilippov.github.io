voix celeste

1. NOUN

/ vwa sɛˈlɛst / (AmE), / vwa sɛˈlɛst / (BrE)
an organ stop that produces a gentle, undulating tremolo effect, often used to create an ethereal sound
organ stop with gentle tremolo effect

Examples

The organist selected the voix celeste to add a shimmering quality to the hymn.
During the recital, the voix celeste stop was used to evoke a sense of tranquility.
Many romantic organs feature a voix celeste for expressive musical passages.

Etymology

French, meaning 'heavenly voice'.
