tech guru

1. NOUN

/ tɛk ˈɡʊru / (AmE), / tɛk ˈɡʊːruː / (BrE)
a person who is highly knowledgeable and skilled in technology, especially computing and digital innovation
expert in technology or computing

Examples

Everyone turns to the tech guru when they have computer problems.
As a tech guru, she is always up to date on the latest gadgets.
The company hired a tech guru to improve their cybersecurity.

Synset Examples

He’s a tech guru who can solve any problem instantly.
She consulted a tech guru for her startup's app.
The tech guru offered insights on digital marketing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expert - someone very skilled or knowledgeable in a particular field
  • Antonym: newbie - a person who is new to technology or a particular field

Etymology

Formed from the English abbreviation 'tech' (technology) and Hindi 'guru' (teacher or expert).
