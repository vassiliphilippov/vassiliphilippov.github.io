key role

1. NOUN

/ ki roʊl / (AmE), / kiː rəʊl / (BrE)
an important or essential function or part in a process, event, or organization
an essential or crucial function

Examples

She played a key role in organizing the event, ensuring everything ran smoothly.
The immune system has a key role in defending the body against disease.
Innovation will take a key role in the company's future growth.

Synset Examples

Testosterone plays a key role in muscle development.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crucial role - an extremely important or decisive part or function
  • Antonym: minor role - an unimportant or less significant function or part
