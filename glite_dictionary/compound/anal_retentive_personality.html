anal retentive personality

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪ.nəl rɪˈtɛn.tɪv pɚˌsɑːnˈæ.lə.ti / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.nəl rɪˈtɛn.tɪv pəˌsɒnˈæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
(psychoanalysis) a personality type marked by excessive orderliness, meticulousness, and reserve, thought to develop from fixation during the anal stage of childhood.
personality type marked by excessive orderliness
medical

Examples

Her anal retentive personality makes her insist that everything is organized perfectly at all times.
People with an anal retentive personality are often described as overly cautious and perfectionistic.
His anal retentive personality sometimes leads to conflict with more relaxed colleagues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anal-retentive - excessively neat or fussy (in personality)

Etymology

From psychoanalytic theory; 'anal retentive' refers to Freud's anal stage of psychosexual development.
