southern spatterdock

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌðərn ˈspætərˌdɑk / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən ˈspætədɒk / (BrE)
A species of aquatic plant found in flowing waters of the southeastern United States, known to sometimes form dense, obstructive mats in streams.
aquatic plant of southeastern U.S. streams

Examples

The dense mats of southern spatterdock made canoeing down the river much more difficult.
Researchers are studying the impact of southern spatterdock on stream ecosystems in Georgia.
We spotted several turtles resting on the huge leaves of the southern spatterdock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellow pond-lily - another common name for Nuphar species, similar aquatic plant
