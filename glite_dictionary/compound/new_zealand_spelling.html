New Zealand spelling

1. NOUN

/ nuː ˈziːlən(d) ˈspɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / njuː ˈziːlənd ˈspɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
the standard form of English spelling used in New Zealand, characterized by certain conventions also found in British and Commonwealth English
standard English spelling in New Zealand

Examples

She prefers to use New Zealand spelling in all her written assignments.
The word 'organise' is a common example of New Zealand spelling.
Publishers may require New Zealand spelling instead of American or British variants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: British spelling - the standard form of English spelling used in Britain
  • Antonym: American spelling - the standard form of English spelling used in the United States
