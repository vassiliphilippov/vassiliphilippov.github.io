bad boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæd bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈbæd bɔɪ / (BrE)
a man who is rebellious or unconventional, often considered exciting or problematic
rebellious or unconventional man
informal

Examples

He had the reputation of being a bad boy but was actually very kind.
Many successful musicians have cultivated a bad boy image to attract fans.
She couldn't resist the charm of the rugged bad boy from her neighborhood.

Synset Examples

She liked the thrill of dating a bad boy.
He always played the bad boy in movies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rebel - someone who resists authority or control
  • Antonym: goody-goody - a person who behaves well and always follows rules
