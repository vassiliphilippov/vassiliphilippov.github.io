pork pie

1. NOUN

/ pɔrk paɪ / (AmE), / pɔːk paɪ / (BrE)
A small pie filled with minced seasoned pork, typically eaten cold.
small pie filled with minced seasoned pork

Examples

I bought a traditional pork pie from the bakery for our picnic.
At lunchtime, she enjoyed a pork pie with a side of salad.
The pork pie is a staple of British delicatessens.
