fat farm

1. NOUN

/ fæt fɑrm / (AmE), / fæt fɑːm / (BrE)
an informal term for a health spa or residential facility where people go to lose weight, often through diet and exercise programs
weight-loss health spa (informal)
informal

Examples

After months at the fat farm, he returned home noticeably slimmer and healthier.
Some celebrities check into a fat farm before movie roles that require them to lose weight.
The company paid for their executives to spend a week at a fat farm as part of a wellness initiative.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: health spa - a place offering health and wellness treatments
