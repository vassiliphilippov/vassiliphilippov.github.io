genteel business

1. NOUN

/ dʒənˈtiːl ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / dʒɛnˈtiːl ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
a way of conducting business characterized by politeness, refinement, and an emphasis on courtesy and social grace
polite and refined business conduct

Examples

The family prided themselves on running a genteel business that valued discretion and tradition.
His genteel business manners were appreciated by clients from high society.
Maintaining a genteel business sometimes meant turning down offers that did not align with their values.

Synset Examples

They operated a genteel business focused on personal service.
Her genteel business approach attracted a wealthy clientele.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polite - showing good manners and respect toward others
  • Synonym: refined - elegant and cultured in appearance or manners

Etymology

From 'genteel', rooted in Old French 'gentil' (high-born, noble), and 'business'.
