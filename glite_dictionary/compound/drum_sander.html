drum sander

1. NOUN

/ drʌm ˈsændər / (AmE), / drʌm ˈsandə / (BrE)
a power tool with a rotating drum covered with sandpaper, used for smoothing or sanding wood surfaces
woodworking power tool for sanding

Examples

He used a drum sander to refinish the hardwood floor in the living room.
The drum sander quickly removed the old paint from the tabletop.
After renting a drum sander, she was able to smooth out all the rough spots in the boards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: belt sander - a sander using a loop of sandpaper mounted on a belt mechanism
