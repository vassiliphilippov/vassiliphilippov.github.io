Donbas conflict

1. NOUN

/ dɑnˈbɑs ˈkɑn.flɪkt / (AmE), / ˈdɒn.bæs ˈkɒn.flɪkt / (BrE)
the ongoing armed conflict in the Donbas region of eastern Ukraine, beginning in 2014, involving Ukrainian government forces and separatists supported by Russia
ongoing conflict in the Donbas region of Ukraine

Examples

The Donbas conflict has drawn international attention.
Several ceasefire attempts have failed to bring an end to the Donbas conflict.
Journalists continue to report from the frontlines of the Donbas conflict.

Synset Examples

The Donbas conflict has drawn international attention.

Etymology

From Donbas, a region in eastern Ukraine, a contraction of Donets Basin, and conflict, meaning war or clash.
