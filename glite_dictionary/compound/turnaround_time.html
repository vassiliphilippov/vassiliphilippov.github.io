turnaround time

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɜrnəˌraʊnd taɪm / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnəraʊnd taɪm / (BrE)
the time required to prepare a ship, vessel, or operation for its next journey, task, or stage, especially after arrival or completion of a previous one
time needed to prepare for next trip/task

Examples

The port improved its efficiency, reducing the turnaround time for cargo ships by several hours.
Quick maintenance and refueling minimized the aircraft's turnaround time between flights.
In the shipping industry, a shorter turnaround time means more profitable use of each vessel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: processing time - duration taken to complete a process
  • Synonym: lead time - total time needed to complete a process from initiation to completion
