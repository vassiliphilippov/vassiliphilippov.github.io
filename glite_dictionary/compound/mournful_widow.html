mournful widow

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrnfəl ˈwɪdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnfl ˈwɪdəʊ / (BrE)
an Old World annual plant with fragrant purple to deep crimson flower heads, naturalized in the United States
a plant with purple or crimson flowers

Examples

The mournful widow brightened the garden with its deep crimson blossoms.
Gardeners enjoy the fragrance of the mournful widow in late spring.
The mournful widow has become naturalized in parts of the northeastern United States.
