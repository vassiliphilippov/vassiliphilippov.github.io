community of scholars

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmjunəti əv ˈskɑlɚz / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnəti əv ˈskɒləz / (BrE)
the collective group of individuals who have attained advanced academic degrees and contribute to academic pursuits
group of people with advanced degrees

Examples

The community of scholars plays a crucial role in advancing scientific knowledge.
Universities around the world contribute to the global community of scholars.
As a new professor, she was eager to engage with the community of scholars in her field.
