local government

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊkəl ˈɡʌvərnmənt / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəl ˈɡʌvənmənt / (BrE)
The official organization or system that governs a specific town, city, county, or district, rather than the whole country.
government of a local area

Examples

The city council is a part of local government responsible for public services.
Funding for the park comes from the local government, not the federal authorities.
Many people attended the meeting to voice concerns to their local government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: government - the system or group of people governing an organized community
