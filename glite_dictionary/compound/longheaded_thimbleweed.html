longheaded thimbleweed

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋˌhɛdɪd ˈθɪmbəlˌwid / (AmE), / lɒŋˌhɛdɪd ˈθɪmbəlwiːd / (BrE)
A species of thimbleweed native to northern North America, recognized by its elongated seed head.
a North American thimbleweed species

Examples

The longheaded thimbleweed is often found growing wild in northern forests.
Botanists conducted a study on the pollinators attracted to longheaded thimbleweed.
Gardeners appreciate the distinctive seed heads of the longheaded thimbleweed in the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thimbleweed - a plant of the genus Anemone with thimble-shaped fruit
