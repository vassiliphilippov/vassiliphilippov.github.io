desk sergeant

1. NOUN

/ dɛsk ˈsɝːdʒənt / (AmE), / dɛsk ˈsɑːdʒənt / (BrE)
a police sergeant responsible for managing the front desk and overseeing routine operations in a police station
police sergeant at the station's front desk

Examples

The desk sergeant took my complaint and directed me to the correct officer.
When the phone rang, the desk sergeant answered promptly and professionally.
She became the first female desk sergeant at the local police station.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sergeant - a non-commissioned officer in the police or military
