catchy slogan

1. NOUN

/ ˈkætʃi ˈsloʊɡən / (AmE), / ˈkætʃi ˈsləʊɡən / (BrE)
a phrase that is easy to remember and attracts attention, often used for advertising or promotion
memorable and appealing phrase (esp. for advertising)

Examples

The company hired a marketing team to come up with a catchy slogan for their new campaign.
Everyone remembered the catchy slogan long after the commercial had ended.
A catchy slogan can make a product much more recognizable to consumers.

Synset Examples

They created a catchy slogan for the new product.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catchphrase - a well-known sentence or phrase, especially one associated with a particular famous person
