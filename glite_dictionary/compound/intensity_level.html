intensity level

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtɛnsəti ˌlɛvəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛnsɪti ˌlɛvəl / (BrE)
the measurable amount of energy transmitted by acoustic, electromagnetic, or other types of radiation; often expressed in decibels
measure of energy transmitted (e.g. sound, radiation)

Examples

The intensity level of the alarm was too high for comfort.
Scientists monitored the intensity level of radiation in the area.
To improve clarity, he raised the intensity level of the microphone.

Synset Examples

he adjusted the intensity of the sound
they measured the station's signal strength

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intensity - the strength or force of something such as light, sound, or feeling
