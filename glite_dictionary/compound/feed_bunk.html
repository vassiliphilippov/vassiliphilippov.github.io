feed bunk

1. NOUN

/ fid bʌŋk / (AmE), / fiːd bʌŋk / (BrE)
a long trough, often raised, used for delivering food to cattle or other livestock
trough for feeding cattle

Examples

The farmer filled the feed bunk with fresh silage for the cattle every morning.
Rainwater collected in the feed bunk, so it had to be cleaned before feeding time.
New regulations require that each feed bunk is positioned to reduce food waste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trough - a long, narrow open container for animals to eat or drink from
