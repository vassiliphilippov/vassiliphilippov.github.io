retail marketing

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːˌteɪl ˈmɑrkɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈriːteɪl ˈmɑːkɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the business activity of promoting and selling goods directly to consumers
selling goods to consumers

Examples

She studied the latest trends in retail marketing before launching her new store.
Digital technology has transformed the landscape of retail marketing over the last decade.
Many companies invest heavily in retail marketing to increase customer loyalty.

Synset Examples

The unorthodox entrepreneur challenged the norms of retail marketing.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wholesale marketing - the activity of selling goods in large quantities to be retailed by others
