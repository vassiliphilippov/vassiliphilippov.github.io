genus Phthirius

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈθaɪ.ri.əs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈθɪɹ.i.əs / (BrE)
A genus of lice that includes the crab louse, an insect parasitic on humans.
genus of parasitic crab lice
medical

Examples

The genus Phthirius includes species known for infesting human body hair.
Infestation by insects of the genus Phthirius often requires medical treatment.
Researchers studied the life cycle of the genus Phthirius to develop better prevention methods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab louse - a parasitic louse (Phthirius pubis) that infests humans

Etymology

From New Latin 'Phthirius', influenced by Greek 'phthir' (louse).
