basal body temperature method

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsəl ˈbɑːdi ˈtɛmpɚˌtʃʊr ˈmɛθəd / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsəl ˈbɒdi ˈtɛmpərətʃə ˈmeθəd / (BrE)
a natural family planning technique in which a woman's fertile period is determined by tracking the rise in basal body temperature that usually occurs at ovulation
fertility tracking by measuring basal body temperature
medical

Examples

She used the basal body temperature method to track her ovulation and plan her pregnancy.
The basal body temperature method requires taking your temperature every morning before getting out of bed.
Many women combine the basal body temperature method with other natural family planning techniques for greater accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: natural family planning - methods of birth control based on observation of natural fertility signals
