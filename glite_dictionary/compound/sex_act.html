sex act

1. NOUN

/ sɛks ækt / (AmE), / sɛks ækt / (BrE)
an act involving sexual activity between individuals, typically referring to sexual intercourse
an act of sexual intercourse or related sexual activity

Examples

The couple was arrested for performing a sex act in a public place.
Discussing what constitutes a sex act can be important in sexual education classes.
He was accused of engaging in a sex act without consent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexual intercourse - sexual union, specifically vaginal penetration
  • Antonym: abstinence - the practice of refraining from sex acts
