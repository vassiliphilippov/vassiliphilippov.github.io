beach pancake

1. NOUN

/ bitʃ ˈpænkeɪk / (AmE), / biːtʃ ˈpænkeɪk / (BrE)
A North American coastal plant with hemispherical heads of dark red flowers, found in dunes from California to Mexico.
coastal plant with wine-red flower heads

Examples

The beach pancake thrives in sandy soils along the Pacific coastline.
Clusters of beach pancake bloom every spring, covering the dunes in red and green.
Botanists study the beach pancake for its adaptation to harsh, salty environments.
