barbarian hero

1. NOUN

/ bɑːrˈbɛriən ˈhɪroʊ / (AmE), / bɑːˈbɛərɪən ˈhɪərəʊ / (BrE)
a rough and powerful warrior, often from a primitive or uncivilized background, featured as the main character in stories or myths
main warrior from uncivilized backgrounds in stories

Examples

The novel follows a barbarian hero as he battles powerful sorcerers and monsters.
Many stories set in ancient times feature a barbarian hero who fights for justice.
In the comic book, the barbarian hero wanders a brutal landscape in search of adventure.

Synset Examples

In the movie, the barbarian hero saves the kingdom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hero - a person admired for courage or noble qualities
  • Synonym: barbarian - a person from an uncivilized or primitive culture, or seen as uncultured
