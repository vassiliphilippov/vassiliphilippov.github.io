final speech

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪnəl spiːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnəl spiːtʃ / (BrE)
the last address or presentation given by a person, especially before leaving a position or ending a significant event
last address or presentation

Examples

The CEO delivered his final speech to the employees before stepping down.
Many students were moved by the professor’s heartfelt final speech at graduation.
In his final speech, the ambassador thanked everyone for their support over the years.

Synset Examples

He gave his final speech before retiring next week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farewell speech - a speech made when someone leaves a job or place
