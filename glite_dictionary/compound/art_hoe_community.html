art hoe community

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt hoʊ kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / ɑːt həʊ kəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
A group or community, often primarily young people online, who value art, creative self-expression, and aesthetic appreciation, typically associated with a distinctive, eclectic personal style and social media presence.
online community focused on art and self-expression
informal slang

Examples

The art hoe community often shares artwork and inspiration on social media platforms.
She found a sense of belonging within the art hoe community because of her love for painting and vintage style.
The art hoe community celebrates individuality through creative expression and appreciation for visual arts.

Synset Examples

The art hoe community is vibrant and diverse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: art hoe - a person who values art, creativity, and personal aesthetic (informal, slang)
  • Synonym: art community - a group of people involved in and valuing the arts

Etymology

From 'art hoe', a slang term originating in online communities combining 'art' and 'hoe' (slang for a person enthusiastic about something), popularized on social media in the 2010s.
