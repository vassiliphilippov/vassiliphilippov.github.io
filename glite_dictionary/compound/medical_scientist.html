medical scientist

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who conducts research into disease processes, diagnosis, or treatment of medical conditions.
scientist researching diseases

Examples

She works as a medical scientist at a leading research institute specializing in infectious diseases.
The conference brought together medical scientists from around the world to discuss new treatments.
Becoming a medical scientist requires years of study and a passion for medical research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scientist - a person who is studying or has expert knowledge of one or more of the natural or physical sciences
  • Degree Lower: doctor - a qualified practitioner of medicine; a physician
