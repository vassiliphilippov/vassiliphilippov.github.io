Mexican poppy

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛksɪkən ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / ˈmɛksɪkən ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
an annual herb with prickly stems and large yellow flowers, native to the southern United States, West Indies, and Mexico
annual herb with yellow flowers and prickly stems

Examples

We spotted a Mexican poppy blooming beside the desert trail.
The fields were filled with the bright yellow flowers of the Mexican poppy.
Gardeners often admire the resilience of the Mexican poppy in dry climates.
