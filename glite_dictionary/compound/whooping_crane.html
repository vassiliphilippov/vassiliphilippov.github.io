whooping crane

1. NOUN

/ ˈhuːpɪŋ kreɪn / (AmE), / ˈwuːpɪŋ kreɪn / (BrE)
a rare North American crane with black-and-white plumage and a loud, trumpeting call
rare North American crane species

Examples

The whooping crane is one of the most endangered birds in North America.
Conservationists have worked tirelessly to increase the whooping crane population.
We spotted a pair of whooping cranes near the wetland preserve.
