sun-ray lamp

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌn ˌreɪ læmp / (AmE), / ˈsʌn ˌreɪ læmp / (BrE)
a type of mercury-vapor lamp used to provide artificial ultraviolet rays for medical or cosmetic treatments
mercury-vapor lamp for medical or cosmetic ultraviolet treatment
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended using a sun-ray lamp to treat her skin condition.
Salons often use sun-ray lamps during cosmetic procedures for their ultraviolet effects.
Safety precautions are important when operating a sun-ray lamp due to the risk of UV exposure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: UV lamp - a lamp that emits ultraviolet light, used for various purposes
