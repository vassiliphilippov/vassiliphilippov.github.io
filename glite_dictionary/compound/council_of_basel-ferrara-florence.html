Council of Basel-Ferrara-Florence

1. PROPN

/ ˈkaʊnsəl əv ˈbæzəl fəˈrɑrə flɔːˈrɛns / (AmE), / ˈkaʊnsəl əv ˈbæzəl fəˈrɑːrə flɒˈrɛns / (BrE)
an ecumenical council of the Roman Catholic Church held from 1431 to 1449, focusing on eliminating heresies and enacting internal reforms
15th-century Catholic Church council for reform and heresies

Examples

The Council of Basel-Ferrara-Florence played a key role in attempting to unify Western and Eastern Christian churches.
Scholars continue to debate the political impact of the Council of Basel-Ferrara-Florence on late medieval Europe.
Reforms discussed at the Council of Basel-Ferrara-Florence addressed major issues within the Catholic Church.
