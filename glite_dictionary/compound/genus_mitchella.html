genus Mitchella

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs mɪˈtʃɛl.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs mɪˈtʃɛl.ə / (BrE)
A genus of creeping evergreen herbs native to North America, belonging to the Rubiaceae family.
genus of creeping evergreen North American herbs

Examples

Botanists often study the genus Mitchella to understand its adaptation to forest habitats.
The genus Mitchella includes the well-known partridgeberry plant.
You can find members of the genus Mitchella spreading over the forest floor in shaded areas.

Etymology

Named after John Mitchell (1724–1768), English-American physician and botanist.
