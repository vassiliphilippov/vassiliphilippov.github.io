shortbread cookie

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɔrt.brɛd ˈkʊk.i / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt.brɛd ˈkʊk.i / (BrE)
A rich, thick biscuit made with a large amount of butter, sugar, and flour, typically crisp and crumbly.
rich, buttery biscuit/cookie
american

Examples

Would you like a shortbread cookie with your tea?
She baked a batch of homemade shortbread cookies for the holidays.
The shortbread cookie melted in my mouth, rich and buttery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shortbread - A traditional Scottish biscuit made with butter, sugar, and flour
  • Synonym: biscuit - Hard, sweet baked good (British usage: 'biscuit')

Etymology

Adaptation of 'shortbread' (from Scottish baked good) to American English 'cookie'.
