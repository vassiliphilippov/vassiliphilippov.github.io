woodworking bench

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdˌwɜrkɪŋ bɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌwɜːkɪŋ bɛntʃ / (BrE)
A sturdy table designed to hold workpieces securely while they are being shaped, cut, or assembled by woodworkers.
bench for holding workpieces in woodworking

Examples

He built his own woodworking bench to fit perfectly in his garage workshop.
The sturdy woodworking bench features multiple vises for holding lumber in place.
Every serious woodworker needs a reliable woodworking bench for precise and safe craftsmanship.

Synset Examples

There are many styles of woodworking benches, each reflecting the type of work to be done or the craftsman's way of working.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workbench - a bench at which practical work is done, especially in a workshop
