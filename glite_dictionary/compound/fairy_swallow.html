fairy swallow

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛri ˈswɑloʊ / (AmE), / ˈfeəri ˈswɒləʊ / (BrE)
a fancy breed of domestic pigeon known for its blue-and-white plumage and heavily feathered feet
breed of domestic pigeon

Examples

The fairy swallow is prized by pigeon enthusiasts for its striking appearance and graceful flight.
Our aviary recently acquired a pair of blue-and-white fairy swallows for breeding.
She photographed a fairy swallow displaying its elaborate feathered feet at the bird show.
