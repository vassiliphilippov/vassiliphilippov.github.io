medicine cabinet

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdəsɪn ˈkæbənət / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪsɪn ˈkabɪnət / (BrE)
a small cabinet, typically in a bathroom, used for storing medicines and toiletries
bathroom cabinet for medicines and toiletries

Examples

She opened the medicine cabinet to find some painkillers.
We keep all our first-aid supplies in the medicine cabinet above the sink.
The medicine cabinet was stocked with bandages and antiseptic cream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cabinet - a cupboard with shelves or drawers for storing or displaying items
