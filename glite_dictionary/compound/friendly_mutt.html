friendly mutt

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛn(d)li mʌt / (AmE), / ˈfrɛn(d)li mʌt / (BrE)
A dog, typically of mixed breed, that is known for being friendly and approachable.
a friendly, mixed-breed dog

Examples

The kids played all afternoon with the friendly mutt that lived next door.
She found a friendly mutt wandering near the park and decided to take him home.
At the animal shelter, a friendly mutt greeted every visitor with a wagging tail.

Synset Examples

He adopted a friendly mutt from the shelter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mutt - a dog, especially one of mixed breed
