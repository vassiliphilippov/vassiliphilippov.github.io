flood of emotion

1. NOUN

/ flʌd əv ɪˈmoʊʃən / (AmE), / flʌd əv ɪˈməʊʃən / (BrE)
a sudden and overwhelming outpouring of strong feelings
overwhelming feelings

Examples

She was caught off guard by a sudden flood of emotion as she heard the news.
During the speech, a flood of emotion swept over the audience.
He struggled to hold back a flood of emotion when he saw his childhood home again.

Synset Examples

Tears streamed down her face in floods of emotion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rush of emotion - a sudden strong surge of feeling
