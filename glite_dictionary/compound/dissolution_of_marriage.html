dissolution of marriage

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪsəˈluːʃən əv ˈmɛrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌdɪsəˈluːʃən əv ˈmarɪdʒ / (BrE)
the legal termination or annulment of a marriage, ending the marital relationship
legal ending of a marriage
legal

Examples

The dissolution of marriage must be approved by the court before it is final.
They filed for the dissolution of marriage after several years of separation.
Counseling is sometimes recommended before couples proceed with the dissolution of marriage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: divorce - the legal dissolution of a marriage by a court or other competent body
  • Synonym: annulment - a formal declaration that a marriage is null and void
