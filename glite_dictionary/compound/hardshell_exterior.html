hardshell exterior

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrdˌʃɛl ɪkˈstɪriɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdʃɛl ɪkˈstɪəriə / (BrE)
A tough outer layer that provides protection for an object or organism.
protective hard outer layer

Examples

The hardshell exterior of the suitcase withstood rough baggage handling during the flight.
Turtles are known for their natural hardshell exterior that protects them from predators.
Many laptops feature a hardshell exterior to safeguard their sensitive electronics against drops and impacts.

Synset Examples

The hardshell exterior of the phone case keeps it safe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shell - the hard outer covering of something, especially an animal or object
  • Synonym: coating - a covering that is applied to the surface of something
