home computer

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm kəmˈpjuːtər / (AmE), / həʊm kəmˈpjuːtə / (BrE)
A personal computer designed or intended for use in the home, typically for tasks such as word processing, games, and internet access.
personal computer for home use

Examples

Many families bought their first home computer in the 1980s.
With a home computer, you can easily manage household finances or play video games.
The rise of the internet greatly increased the usefulness of the home computer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personal computer - a general-purpose computer designed for individual use
