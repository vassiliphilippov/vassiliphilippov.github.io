touch of love

1. NOUN

/ tʌtʃ əv lʌv / (AmE), / tʌtʃ əv lʌv / (BrE)
a small amount of care or affection given to something, often making it special or improved
a small amount of care or affection

Examples

The old house was restored with a touch of love and care.
Her handmade cards always have a touch of love that sets them apart.
A simple meal can become extraordinary with a touch of love in the preparation.
