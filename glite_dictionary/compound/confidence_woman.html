confidence woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːn.fɪ.dəns ˌwʊ.mən / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.fɪ.dəns ˌwʊ.mən / (BrE)
a woman who deceives others by gaining their trust in order to swindle or defraud them
female swindler using trust to defraud

Examples

The confidence woman tricked several people out of their money by posing as a charity worker.
Police arrested the confidence woman after she attempted to swindle an elderly couple.
The movie portrayed a clever confidence woman who targeted wealthy businessmen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confidence man - a man who swindles others by gaining their trust

Etymology

Modelled on 'confidence man,' from 'confidence' (trust) + 'woman.'
