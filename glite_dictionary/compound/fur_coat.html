fur coat

1. NOUN

/ fɝ ˈkoʊt / (AmE), / fɜː ˈkəʊt / (BrE)
a coat made from the fur of animals, worn as an article of clothing
a coat made from animal fur

Examples

She wore her elegant fur coat to the opera on a chilly evening.
Activists protested outside the store that sold expensive fur coats.
His grandmother's vintage fur coat was passed down through the family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overcoat - a long, warm coat worn over other clothing
