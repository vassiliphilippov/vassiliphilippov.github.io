satellite receiver

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæt̬.əl.aɪt rɪˈsiː.vɚ / (AmE), / ˈsæt.əl.aɪt rɪˈsiː.və / (BrE)
a device on a communications satellite that receives signals transmitted from the ground or from other satellites
receiver on a communications satellite

Examples

The satellite receiver was damaged during the meteor shower and lost signal completely.
Engineers regularly perform maintenance on the satellite receiver to ensure uninterrupted communication.
A malfunctioning satellite receiver can disrupt television broadcasts worldwide.
