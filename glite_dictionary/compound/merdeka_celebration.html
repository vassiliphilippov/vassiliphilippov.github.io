Merdeka celebration

1. NOUN

/ mərˈdeɪkə ˌsɛləˈbreɪʃ(ə)n / (AmE), / məˈdeɪkə ˌsɛlɪˈbreɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a celebration marking a country's independence or freedom, especially in Malaysia and Indonesia
independence celebration (Malaysia/Indonesia)

Examples

Every year, people gather in Kuala Lumpur for the grand Merdeka celebration.
The Merdeka celebration included parades, fireworks, and cultural performances across major cities.
Schools organized special assemblies to honor the nation’s freedom during the Merdeka celebration.

Synset Examples

Merdeka celebrations filled the streets with joy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Independence Day - a national holiday commemorating a country’s independence

Etymology

Malay and Indonesian merdeka, meaning 'independent' or 'free', from Sanskrit maharddhika.
