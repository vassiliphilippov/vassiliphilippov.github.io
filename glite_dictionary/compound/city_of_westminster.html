City of Westminster

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪti əv ˈwɛstmɪnstɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɪti əv ˈwɛsmɪnstə / (BrE)
an inner borough of Greater London along the River Thames, containing notable sites such as Buckingham Palace, the Houses of Parliament, and Westminster Abbey
borough in central London, UK

Examples

The City of Westminster is home to some of the most famous landmarks in London.
Many tourists visit the City of Westminster to see Buckingham Palace and Westminster Abbey.
The City of Westminster council oversees a large area of central London.
