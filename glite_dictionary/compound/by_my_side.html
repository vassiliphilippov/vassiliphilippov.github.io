by my side

1. ADP

/ baɪ maɪ saɪd / (AmE), / baɪ maɪ saɪd / (BrE)
close to or alongside someone, typically to provide support or companionship
close to someone for support

Examples

My best friend was by my side through all the challenges I faced.
She promised to stay by my side no matter what happened.
Whenever I felt nervous, my dog was always by my side.

Synset Examples

Buster is my loyal dog, always by my side.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beside - at the side of; next to
