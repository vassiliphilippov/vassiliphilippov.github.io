etymological dictionary

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛtəˌmɑːˈlɑdʒɪkəl ˈdɪkʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌɛtɪməˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˈdɪkʃənəri / (BrE)
a dictionary that provides the historical origins and development of words
dictionary of word histories

Examples

The university library has an extensive etymological dictionary collection for linguistics students.
I used an etymological dictionary to find out where the word 'robot' originated.
Every serious language scholar owns at least one etymological dictionary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dictionary - a reference book containing words and their meanings

Etymology

etymological from Greek 'etumologikos' (relating to the true sense of a word); dictionary from Latin 'dictionarium'.
