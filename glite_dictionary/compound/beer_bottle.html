beer bottle

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪr ˌbɑːt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈbɪə ˌbɒt.l̩ / (BrE)
a glass or plastic bottle specifically designed for holding beer
bottle designed to hold beer

Examples

He opened a cold beer bottle after a long day at work.
The broken beer bottle was lying on the sidewalk.
She collected empty beer bottles for recycling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bottle - a container, typically made of glass or plastic, with a narrow neck, used for storing drinks or other liquids
