mellow glow

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛloʊ ɡloʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɛləʊ ɡləʊ / (BrE)
a soft, warm, and gentle light that creates a pleasant or relaxed atmosphere
soft, gentle, warm light

Examples

The fireplace filled the room with a mellow glow as we relaxed after dinner.
Candles added a mellow glow to the quiet restaurant, creating a romantic mood.
Her lamp gave off a mellow glow that was perfect for reading in bed.

Synset Examples

The sunset cast a mellow glow over the horizon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gleam - a faint or brief light, especially one reflected from something
  • Degree Higher: radiance - bright or glowing light
  • Degree Lower: glimmer - a dim or wavering light
