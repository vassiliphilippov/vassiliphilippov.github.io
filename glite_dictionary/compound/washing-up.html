washing-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑʃɪŋ ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃɪŋ ˌʌp / (BrE)
the act of washing dishes, cutlery, and other utensils after a meal
washing dishes after a meal
british

Examples
