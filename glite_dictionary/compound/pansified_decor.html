pansified decor

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæn.səˌfaɪd dɪˈkɔr / (AmE), / ˈpæn.sɪˌfaɪd dɪˈkɔː / (BrE)
decor that is excessively soft, delicate, or stereotypically feminine in style, often used disparagingly
overly soft or feminine decor
offensive

Examples

The pansified decor made the café feel welcoming and cozy, if a bit over the top for some patrons.
He complained that the living room's pansified decor was too frilly and pastel for his taste.
Some critics argued that the hotel's pansified decor alienated guests who preferred a more neutral style.

Synset Examples

The pansified decor made the café feel welcoming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: effeminate - having qualities regarded as characteristic of women
  • Antonym: masculine decor - decor that is stereotypically associated with male characteristics

Etymology

From 'pansy' (derogatory slang term for an effeminate man) + '-fied' (made or turned into), and 'decor.'
