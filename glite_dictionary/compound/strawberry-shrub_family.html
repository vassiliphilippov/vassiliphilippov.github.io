strawberry-shrub family

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔːˌbɛri ʃrʌb ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈstrɒˌbəri ʃrʌb ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of shrubs or small trees with aromatic bark, native to eastern United States and eastern Asia, including the strawberry shrub (Calycanthus)
plant family with aromatic bark, native to eastern US/Asia

Examples

The strawberry-shrub family is known for its sweetly scented bark and appealing flowers.
Several species in the strawberry-shrub family are valued in gardens for their fragrant blooms.
Botanists have discovered rare members of the strawberry-shrub family in remote forests of eastern Asia.
