pas seul

1. NOUN

/ pɑː sɜl / (AmE), / pɑː sɜːl / (BrE)
(in ballet) a solo dance or dance figure, performed by one person.
solo ballet dance

Examples

Her pas seul received a standing ovation from the audience.
The choreographer added a dramatic pas seul to showcase the lead dancer's skills.
Each dancer prepared a unique pas seul for the competition.

Etymology

From French 'pas seul' meaning 'single step'.
