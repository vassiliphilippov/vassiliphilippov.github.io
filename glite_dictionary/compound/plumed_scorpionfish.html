plumed scorpionfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈplumd ˈskɔrpiənˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpluːmd ˈskɔːpiənˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a scorpionfish species characterized by its feathery fins and camouflage, found in reef habitats
species of scorpionfish with feathery fins

Examples

The plumed scorpionfish is known for its excellent camouflage on the ocean floor.
Divers spotted a colorful plumed scorpionfish resting among the coral.
Despite its beauty, the plumed scorpionfish should be observed from a safe distance due to its venomous spines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scorpionfish - any marine fish of the family Scorpaenidae, known for venomous spines
