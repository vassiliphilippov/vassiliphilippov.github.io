to up and decide

1. VERB

/ ʌp ənd dɪˈsaɪd / (AmE), / ʌp ənd dɪˈsaɪd / (BrE)
to suddenly or unexpectedly make a decision or take action, often without prior warning or planning
suddenly make a decision
informal

Examples

She up and decided to quit her job and travel the world.
Without warning, he up and decided to move to another city.
They up and decided to throw a party at the last minute.

Synset Examples

They up and decided to go on a spontaneous road trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to decide - to make a decision
