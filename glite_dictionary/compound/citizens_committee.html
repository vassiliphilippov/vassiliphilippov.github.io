citizens committee

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪtɪzənz kəˌmɪti / (AmE), / ˈsɪtɪzənz kəˌmɪti / (BrE)
a group of private individuals who form an organization, usually to address or promote a specific issue or cause in the community
group formed by citizens for civic action

Examples

A citizens committee was formed to improve public safety in the neighborhood.
The mayor met with the citizens committee to discuss the proposed park renovations.
A local citizens committee successfully lobbied for changes in school policy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: committee - a group of people appointed for a specific function
