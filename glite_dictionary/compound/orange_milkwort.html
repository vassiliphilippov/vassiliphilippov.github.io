orange milkwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ ˈmɪlkˌwɔrt / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ˈmɪlkˌwɔːt / (BrE)
a bog plant of the pine barrens in the southeastern United States, with spikes of irregular yellow-orange flowers
bog plant with yellow-orange flowers

Examples

The orange milkwort thrives in the acidic bogs of the southeastern United States.
We spotted an orange milkwort blooming beside the trail during our hike.
Botanists often study the unique flower structure of the orange milkwort.
