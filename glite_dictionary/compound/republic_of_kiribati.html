Republic of Kiribati

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌkɪrɪˈbæs / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌkɪrɪˈbɑːti / (BrE)
A sovereign island country in the west central Pacific Ocean, just south of the equator.
Pacific island country south of the equator

Examples

The Republic of Kiribati is particularly vulnerable to rising sea levels caused by climate change.
She conducted anthropological research in the Republic of Kiribati during the summer.
The flag of the Republic of Kiribati features a frigatebird flying over the ocean.

Etymology

Name 'Kiribati' is the local pronunciation of 'Gilberts', after the main island group (the Gilbert Islands).
