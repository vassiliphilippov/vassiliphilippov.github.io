tail lamp

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪl læmp / (AmE), / ˈteɪl læmp / (BrE)
a red light mounted at the rear of a motor vehicle, used to indicate its presence to vehicles behind
rear light on a vehicle

Examples

The car's tail lamp was cracked after the minor collision.
It's important to check that your tail lamps are working before driving at night.
He replaced the broken tail lamp with a new one from the auto parts store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taillight - a red light at the rear of a vehicle
