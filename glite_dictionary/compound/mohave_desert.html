Mohave Desert

1. PROPN

/ moʊˈhɑːvi ˈdɛzərt / (AmE), / məˈhɑːvi ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
a large desert region located in southern California and western Arizona, known for its arid climate and distinctive landscapes
desert in southern California and western Arizona

Examples

The Mohave Desert experiences extremely high temperatures during the summer months.
Many unique species of plants and animals are found only in the Mohave Desert.
Tourists visit the Mohave Desert to see its iconic landscapes and Joshua trees.

Etymology

Named after the Mohave (Mojave) people, indigenous to the area.
