civic spirit

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪvɪk ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪvɪk ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
a sense of pride and responsibility towards one's city or community, often leading to active involvement in its welfare or improvement
pride and responsibility for one's city

Examples

The mayor praised the civic spirit of residents who volunteered to clean up the park.
A strong civic spirit can lead to a safer and more vibrant community.
Her civic spirit inspired others to participate in local improvement projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: civic pride - a feeling of pride in the city where one lives
