mother-naked

1. ADJ

/ ˈmʌðɚ ˌneɪkɪd / (AmE), / ˈmʌðə ˌneɪkɪd / (BrE)
Completely naked; as unclothed as at birth.
completely naked

Examples

He stood mother-naked in front of the mirror, unashamed and fearless.
The child ran mother-naked through the field, laughing under the warm sun.
When the water spilled, she found herself mother-naked and searching for a towel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stark naked - completely without clothes
