Guided Bomb Unit-28

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡaɪdɪd bɑm ˈjuːnɪt ˈtwɛnti eɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪdɪd bɒm ˈjuːnɪt ˈtwenti eɪt / (BrE)
a 5,000-pound laser-guided bomb designed to penetrate to a programmed depth before detonating, used for targeting hardened underground facilities
a laser-guided, deep-penetration bomb

Examples

The air force deployed a Guided Bomb Unit-28 to destroy the heavily fortified facility.
Military engineers relied on the precision of the Guided Bomb Unit-28 during the operation.
The Guided Bomb Unit-28 is capable of penetrating deep underground bunkers before detonating.
