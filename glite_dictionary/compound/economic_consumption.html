economic consumption

1. NOUN

/ ˌiː.kəˈnɑː.mɪk kənˈsəm.pʃən / (AmE), / ˌiː.kəˈnɒm.ɪk kənˈsʌmp.ʃən / (BrE)
the use of goods and services to satisfy needs or in the process of producing other goods and services, especially in economics
use of goods and services in economics

Examples

Economic consumption plays a vital role in determining a country’s standard of living.
When analyzing national statistics, economic consumption must be considered alongside production.
Rising incomes often lead to higher economic consumption of goods such as electricity and vehicles.

Synset Examples

the consumption of energy has increased steadily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consumption - the act of consuming; in economics, using goods or services
  • Antonym: production - the process of creating goods and services
