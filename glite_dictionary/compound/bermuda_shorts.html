Bermuda shorts

1. NOUN

/ bərˈmjuːdə ʃɔrts / (AmE), / bəˈmjuːdə ʃɔːts / (BrE)
Short trousers that reach to or just above the knee, typically worn in warm weather.
knee-length summer shorts

Examples

He packed his favorite Bermuda shorts for the beach holiday.
Many offices in Bermuda allow men to wear Bermuda shorts as part of business attire.
The store carries a wide selection of colorful Bermuda shorts for summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shorts - short pants, typically ending above the knee

Etymology

Named after Bermuda, where such shorts are part of semi-formal attire.
