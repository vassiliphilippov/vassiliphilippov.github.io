mental quickness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.tl ˈkwɪk.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmen.təl ˈkwɪk.nəs / (BrE)
the ability to think and respond rapidly and accurately, especially when solving problems or answering questions
ability to think and respond rapidly

Examples

Her mental quickness made her the star of the debate team.
Interviewers were impressed by his mental quickness and ability to solve problems on the spot.
Games like chess can help improve your mental quickness over time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quick-wittedness - the quality of being able to think and respond swiftly
  • Antonym: slowness of mind - the quality of being slow to think or respond
