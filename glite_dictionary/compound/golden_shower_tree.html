golden shower tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊl.dən ˈʃaʊ.ɚ triː / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊl.dən ˈʃaʊ.ə triː / (BrE)
a tropical deciduous or semi-evergreen tree (Cassia fistula) with fragrant yellow flowers in long drooping clusters and pods with medicinal pulp, native to Asia, Central and South America, and Australia
tropical tree with yellow flowers (Cassia fistula)

Examples

The golden shower tree is valued for its beautiful yellow blossoms during the summer months.
Many streets in Southeast Asia are lined with golden shower trees that create a vibrant display.
The medicinal properties of the golden shower tree have been used in traditional remedies for centuries.
