health professional

1. NOUN

/ hɛlθ prəˈfɛʃənl̩ / (AmE), / hɛlθ prəˈfɛʃənəl / (BrE)
a person qualified to practice in a health care profession, such as medicine, nursing, or allied health fields
person qualified in a health care profession

Examples

You should consult a health professional if you have ongoing symptoms.
Many health professionals attended the medical conference this year.
A health professional can advise you on the best way to manage your condition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medical practitioner - a person licensed to practice medicine; a doctor
