rock hyrax

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈhaɪˌræks / (AmE), / rɒk ˈhaɪ.raks / (BrE)
A small, herbivorous mammal native to Africa and the Middle East, living in rocky terrain; also called a rock rabbit or dassie.
small mammal of rocky areas (Africa, Middle East)

Examples

The rock hyrax is well-adapted to life among steep, rocky outcrops.
We spotted a group of rock hyraxes sunbathing on the boulders.
Despite its small size, the rock hyrax is closely related to elephants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hyrax - small, herbivorous mammal found in Africa and the Middle East
