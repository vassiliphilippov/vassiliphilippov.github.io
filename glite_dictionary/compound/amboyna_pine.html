amboyna pine

1. NOUN

/ æmˈbɔɪnə paɪn / (AmE), / æmˈbɔɪnə paɪn / (BrE)
a tropical tree native to the Moluccas and Philippines, known as a source of dammar resin
tropical tree; source of dammar resin

Examples

The amboyna pine is valued for the resin it produces.
Furniture makers sometimes use wood from the amboyna pine for decorative veneers.
The forests of the Philippines include species such as the amboyna pine.

Etymology

From Amboyna (now Ambon), an island in Indonesia, where the tree is found.
