labor agreement

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪ.bɚ əˈɡriː.mənt / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bə əˈɡriː.mənt / (BrE)
a formal contract between workers and management that sets out wages, benefits, and working conditions
contract on wages and work conditions

Examples

The union and the company finally reached a labor agreement after months of negotiations.
Our wages and health benefits are protected under the current labor agreement.
A new labor agreement will improve working conditions for all employees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collective bargaining agreement - formal contract between employer and group of employees
