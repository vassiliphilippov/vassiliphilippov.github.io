musk rose

1. NOUN

/ mʌsk roʊz / (AmE), / mʌsk rəʊz / (BrE)
a species of rose native to the Mediterranean, with curved or climbing branches and loose clusters of musky-scented flowers
a Mediterranean rose with musky fragrance

Examples

The musk rose filled the garden with its distinctive, sweet aroma each summer evening.
Unlike traditional roses, the musk rose often grows as a climbing shrub.
She planted a musk rose beside the old trellis to add a subtle fragrance to her backyard.
