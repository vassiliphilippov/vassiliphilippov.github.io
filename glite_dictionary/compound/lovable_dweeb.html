lovable dweeb

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌvəbəl dwiːb / (AmE), / ˈlʌvəbəl dwiːb / (BrE)
A socially awkward but endearing or charming person.
endearing, socially awkward person
informal

Examples

Jake is such a lovable dweeb that everyone forgives his mistakes.
The sitcom's main character is a lovable dweeb with a big heart.
Despite his clumsy ways, he won everyone over as the office's resident lovable dweeb.

Synset Examples

The movie portrayed the main character as a lovable dweeb.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dweeb - a person regarded as socially inept or boring
