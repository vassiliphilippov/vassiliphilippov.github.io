hydrangea family

1. NOUN

/ haɪˈdreɪndʒə ˈfæməli / (AmE), / haɪˈdrændʒɪə ˈfæməli / (BrE)
A family of flowering plants (Hydrangeaceae), including the hydrangea and related species, sometimes classified within Saxifragaceae.
family of flowering plants including hydrangeas

Examples

The hydrangea family includes many popular ornamental plants known for their large, showy blooms.
Scientists debate whether to classify certain shrubs within the hydrangea family or the saxifrage family.
Many species in the hydrangea family are native to both Asia and the Americas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hydrangeaceae - the botanical family of hydrangeas
