after-shave lotion

1. NOUN

/ ˈæf.tɚ ʃeɪv ˈloʊ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈɑːf.tə ʃeɪv ˈləʊ.ʃən / (BrE)
a scented lotion applied to a man's face after shaving to soothe and refresh the skin
fragrant lotion used after shaving

Examples

He splashed some after-shave lotion on his face to feel refreshed.
The scent of his after-shave lotion lingered long after he had left the room.
Many men choose an after-shave lotion with soothing ingredients for sensitive skin.
