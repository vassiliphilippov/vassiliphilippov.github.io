muscle building

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsəl ˌbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmʌsl̩ ˌbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of increasing muscle mass and strength through specific exercises and training
exercise to increase muscle size and strength

Examples

He started muscle building after recovering from his injury.
Proper nutrition is essential for effective muscle building.
Many gym-goers have a dedicated muscle building routine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bodybuilding - the activity of developing muscles through weightlifting, often as a sport
