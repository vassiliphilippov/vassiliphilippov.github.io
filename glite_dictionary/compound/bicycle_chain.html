bicycle chain

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪsɪkəl tʃeɪn / (AmE), / ˈbʌɪsɪkl tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a chain that transfers power from the pedals to the rear wheel of a bicycle
chain transmitting pedal power on a bicycle

Examples

After a long ride, I noticed my bicycle chain was covered in dirt and needed cleaning.
She replaced the broken bicycle chain before setting off on her trip.
Keeping your bicycle chain lubricated helps prevent rust and ensures a smooth ride.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chain - a series of linked metal rings, used for fastening or pulling
