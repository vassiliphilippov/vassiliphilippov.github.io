stress test

1. NOUN

/ strɛs tɛst / (AmE), / strɛs tɛst / (BrE)
a test designed to determine how well a system, device, or individual functions under pressure or adverse conditions
test of performance under stress

Examples

The engineers conducted a stress test on the bridge to ensure its safety under heavy loads.
Before approving the software for release, the team ran a rigorous stress test.
Doctors scheduled a cardiac stress test to evaluate her heart's function during exercise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trial - a test to determine the performance or quality of something
  • Synonym: assessment - the act of evaluating or appraising a person or system
