police work

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis wɝk / (AmE), / pəˈliːs wɜːk / (BrE)
the activities involved in investigating crimes and enforcing the law, especially those carried out by the police
the investigation and enforcement activities done by police

Examples

She always admired those who dedicated their lives to police work.
Modern police work relies heavily on forensic science and technology.
After years of police work, he decided to become a private investigator.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detective work - investigative activities to solve crimes
