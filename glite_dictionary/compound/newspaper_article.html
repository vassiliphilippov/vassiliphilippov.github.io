newspaper article

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər ˈɑːrtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə ˈɑːtɪkl̩ / (BrE)
an article reporting news, published in a newspaper
news report published in a newspaper

Examples

She read an interesting newspaper article about advances in medical technology.
The newspaper article provided a detailed account of the recent election results.
He clipped the newspaper article to include in his research project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news story - a report of recent events or information
  • Degree Higher: editorial - a newspaper article expressing opinions of the editors
