curry sauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɝːi sɔs / (AmE), / ˈkʌri sɔːs / (BrE)
a savory sauce flavored with curry powder, often used as an accompaniment to meat, rice, or vegetables; sometimes made with coconut milk for a creamier texture
savory sauce flavored with curry powder

Examples

She poured the curry sauce over the steamed rice and vegetables.
The restaurant's signature curry sauce is made with coconut milk and a blend of spices.
You can buy ready-made curry sauce from most supermarkets these days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gravy - a sauce often served with meat or potatoes

Etymology

From Tamil 'kari' (sauce, relish for rice) and English 'sauce'.
