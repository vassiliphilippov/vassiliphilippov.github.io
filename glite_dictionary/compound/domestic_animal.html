domestic animal

1. NOUN

/ dəˈmɛstɪk ˈænɪməl / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk ˈænɪməl / (BrE)
An animal that has been tamed and bred to live in close association with humans, often as a pet or for agricultural purposes.
tame animal kept by humans

Examples

The cow is a classic example of a domestic animal that provides milk for humans.
Unlike a wild fox, a dog is considered a domestic animal and adapts well to a home environment.
Veterinarians often specialize in the care of domestic animals such as cats and dogs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pet - an animal kept for companionship
  • Antonym: wild animal - an animal living in its natural, untamed state
