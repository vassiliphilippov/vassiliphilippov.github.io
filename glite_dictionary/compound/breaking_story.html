breaking story

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪkɪŋ ˈstɔri / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkɪŋ ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
a news report about an event that is currently happening or has just happened
urgent news report; current event coverage

Examples

The reporter rushed to the scene to cover the breaking story for the evening news.
Every network interrupted its programming to report the breaking story of the earthquake.
Social media often spreads a breaking story much faster than traditional news outlets.

Synset Examples

They created a newsfroup to share breaking stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breaking news - news about an event as it happens; urgent coverage
