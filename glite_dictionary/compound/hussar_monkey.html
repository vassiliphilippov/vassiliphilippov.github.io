hussar monkey

1. NOUN

/ həˈsɑr ˈmʌŋki / (AmE), / hʊˈzɑː ˈmʌŋki / (BrE)
a reddish long-tailed monkey native to West Africa, known for its agile movements and distinctive coloration
reddish long-tailed monkey of West Africa

Examples

The hussar monkey is easily identified by its reddish fur and long tail.
Researchers observed a group of hussar monkeys foraging along the savanna edge.
Unlike other primates in the region, the hussar monkey prefers open grassland habitats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patas monkey - a similar monkey species of West Africa

Etymology

From 'hussar', possibly referring to the monkey's lively appearance, and 'monkey'.
