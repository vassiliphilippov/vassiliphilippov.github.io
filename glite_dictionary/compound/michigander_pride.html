Michigander pride

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪʃɪˈɡændər praɪd / (AmE), / ˌmɪʃɪˈɡandə prʌɪd / (BrE)
a strong sense of pride and identification with the state of Michigan and its people
pride in being from Michigan

Examples

The Michigander pride was evident at the festival, where everyone dressed in state colors.
Waving their blue flags, fans showed off their Michigander pride during the big game.
Her speech was filled with references to Michigander pride and the state’s enduring spirit.

Synset Examples

The Michigander pride was evident at the festival.

Etymology

From "Michigander" (a person from Michigan) + "pride".
