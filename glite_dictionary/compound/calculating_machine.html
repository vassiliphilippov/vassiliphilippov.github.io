calculating machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈkælkjəˌleɪtɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈkælkjʊˌleɪtɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine, either mechanical or electronic, for performing mathematical calculations, especially those preceding modern electronic calculators
device for mathematical calculations

Examples

Before the invention of the electronic calculator, a calculating machine was essential in business offices.
The museum has several early mechanical calculating machines on display.
His grandfather once worked as an operator of a calculating machine in a bank.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calculator - an electronic device for performing mathematical calculations
  • Degree Lower: abacus - a manual counting frame used for calculations
