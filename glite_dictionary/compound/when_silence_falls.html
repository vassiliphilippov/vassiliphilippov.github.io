when silence falls

1. X

/ wɛn ˈsaɪləns fɔlz / (AmE), / wɛn ˈsaɪləns fɔːlz / (BrE)
at the moment when quietness begins, especially at the start of night
when quietness begins; onset of silence

Examples

Many poets find inspiration when silence falls and the world becomes still.
He only writes music when silence falls over the city each evening.
The streets feel mysterious when silence falls after midnight.

Synset Examples

At night, when silence falls, the ghost walks through the halls.
