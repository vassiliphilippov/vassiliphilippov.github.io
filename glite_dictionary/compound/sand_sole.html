sand sole

1. NOUN

/ sænd soʊl / (AmE), / sænd səʊl / (BrE)
a common flatfish of the Pacific coast of North America known for its sandy coloration
flatfish of the Pacific coast of North America

Examples

We spotted a sand sole while snorkeling off the coast of California.
The sand sole uses its sandy coloring to camouflage itself on the ocean floor.
Fishermen often catch sand sole along the Pacific shoreline in spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sole - a marine flatfish of the family Soleidae
