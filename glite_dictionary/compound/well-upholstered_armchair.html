well-upholstered armchair

1. NOUN

/ ˌwɛl əpˈhoʊl.stɚd ˈɑrmˌtʃɛr / (AmE), / ˌwɛl ʌpˈhəʊl.stəd ˈɑːmˌtʃeə / (BrE)
an armchair with high-quality padding and fabric covering, offering extra comfort
armchair with quality padding and covering

Examples

She settled into the well-upholstered armchair and sighed with relief after a long day.
The hotel lobby was decorated with several well-upholstered armchairs to make guests feel at home.
His favorite spot to read was the well-upholstered armchair beside the fireplace.

Synset Examples

He purchased a well-upholstered armchair for comfort.
