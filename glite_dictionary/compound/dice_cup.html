dice cup

1. NOUN

/ daɪs kʌp / (AmE), / daɪs kʌp / (BrE)
a small cup, open at one end, used for shaking and throwing dice by hand in games
container for shaking and throwing dice

Examples

She shook the dice inside the dice cup before rolling them onto the table.
A leather dice cup is often included with traditional backgammon sets.
He forgot to bring his lucky dice cup to the game night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dice - small throwable objects with marked sides used in games
  • Synonym: cup - a small, round container, typically with a handle
