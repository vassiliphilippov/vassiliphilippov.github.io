energy vampire

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛnɚdʒi ˈvæmpaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛnədʒi ˈvæmpaɪə / (BrE)
a person who habitually drains other people's emotional energy or positivity, often through negative or demanding behavior
person who drains others' positivity or energy
informal

Examples

After spending an hour with the energy vampire at work, I felt completely exhausted.
You should set boundaries with energy vampires to protect your own well-being.
Many people don't realize they are behaving like an energy vampire until someone points it out.

Synset Examples

She felt drained after meeting with an energy vampire.
He realized his friend was an energy vampire.
Avoiding energy vampires can improve your mood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toxic person - a person whose behavior negatively affects others

Etymology

Formed by combining 'energy' with 'vampire,' referencing the mythical creature that drains life, used metaphorically.
