white arsenic

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈɑrsənɪk / (AmE), / waɪt ˈɑːsənɪk / (BrE)
a poisonous white powder, arsenic trioxide, used in glass manufacturing and as a pesticide and weed killer
poisonous white powdered arsenic trioxide

Examples

The laboratory handled white arsenic with extreme caution, given its toxic nature.
Old rat poisons often contained white arsenic as their main active ingredient.
White arsenic plays a role in the production of certain types of glass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arsenic trioxide - a poisonous white, crystalline solid; chemical compound of arsenic and oxygen
