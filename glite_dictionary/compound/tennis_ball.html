tennis ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnɪs bɔl / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs bɔːl / (BrE)
A small, typically yellow or white ball covered with felt, used in the game of tennis.
ball used in tennis

Examples

He tossed a tennis ball over the net during practice.
The dog loves to chase a tennis ball around the yard.
Make sure your tennis balls are not too old before the match.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ball - a spherical object used in games and sports
