adult intelligence

1. NOUN

/ əˈdʌlt ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns / (AmE), / əˈdʌlt ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns / (BrE)
the average intelligence quotient or cognitive ability level of adults in a particular population or group
average IQ of adults in a population

Examples

Research suggests that the adult intelligence in this region has remained stable over several decades.
Educational reforms may have a long-term impact on adult intelligence across generations.
Scientists collected data on adult intelligence to assess national cognitive health.
