scouting organization

1. NOUN

/ ˈskaʊtɪŋ ˌɔːrɡənəˌzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈskaʊtɪŋ ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an organization that promotes and administers the activities of the Scout Movement, such as Boy Scouts or Girl Guides
group supporting the Scout Movement

Examples

The scouting organization welcomed hundreds of new members at the annual jamboree.
She volunteered for a local scouting organization dedicated to youth leadership.
Several countries have more than one scouting organization due to historical reasons.

Synset Examples

Countries such as the United States have maintained separate Scouting organizations for boys and girls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Scout Movement - the worldwide movement that aims to support young people in their physical, mental, and spiritual development
