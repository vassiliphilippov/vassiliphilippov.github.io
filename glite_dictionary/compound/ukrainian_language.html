Ukrainian language

1. NOUN

/ juːˈkreɪniən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / juːˈkreɪniən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the East Slavic language spoken mainly in Ukraine and by Ukrainian communities worldwide
East Slavic language of Ukraine

Examples

She is fluent in both Russian and Ukrainian language.
The Ukrainian language uses a Cyrillic script similar to Russian but has distinct letters and sounds.
Learning the Ukrainian language helped him connect with his heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Russian language - the East Slavic language spoken in Russia and neighboring countries
  • Synonym: Belarusian language - the East Slavic language spoken in Belarus

Etymology

From Ukraine (country) + English 'language'. Related to Slavic roots.
