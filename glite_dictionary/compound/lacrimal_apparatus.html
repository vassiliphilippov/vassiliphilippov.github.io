lacrimal apparatus

1. NOUN

/ ˈlækrɪməl əˈpærətəs / (AmE), / ˈlækrɪməl əˈparətəs / (BrE)
the parts of the eye that produce, convey, and drain tears, including the lacrimal gland and associated ducts
tear-producing and draining system in the eye
medical

Examples

The lacrimal apparatus helps to keep the surface of the eye moist and free from dust.
Damage to the lacrimal apparatus can result in excessive tearing or dry eyes.
Her doctor explained how the lacrimal apparatus works to maintain eye health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tear duct - a tube draining tears to the nasal cavity
  • Synonym: lacrimal gland - gland that produces tears

Etymology

From Latin 'lacrima' (tear) and 'apparatus' (equipment, apparatus).
