waste basket

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪst ˌbæskɪt / (AmE), / ˈweɪst ˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
a container, typically with an open top, used for collecting wastepaper and other small refuse
container for wastepaper or small rubbish

Examples

She tossed the crumpled note into the waste basket beside her desk.
Please empty the waste basket before it overflows.
The office supplies included pens, folders, and a new waste basket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trash can - a container for household or office rubbish, usually with a lid
  • Synonym: wastepaper basket - a basket for holding wastepaper
