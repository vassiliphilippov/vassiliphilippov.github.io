manager haircut

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænɪdʒɚ ˈhɛrˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒə ˈheəkʌt / (BrE)
(informal) A stereotypical short, often angular women's haircut, popularly associated with demanding or authoritative customers, especially those who insist on speaking to the manager.
stereotypical demanding customer's haircut
informal slang american

Examples

People often joke that the manager haircut is a sign someone will complain about service.
She changed her style, saying she didn't want the infamous manager haircut anymore.
Memes online have made the manager haircut a symbol of entitled behavior.

Synset Examples

I want to speak to the manager haircut about my order.
