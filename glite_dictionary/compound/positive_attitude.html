positive attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːzətɪv ˈæt̬ɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈpɒzətɪv ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
an optimistic and constructive way of thinking or approaching situations
optimistic and constructive mindset

Examples

Her positive attitude helped the team overcome many challenges during the project.
It's important to maintain a positive attitude even when things don't go as planned.
Teachers often encourage students to approach problems with a positive attitude for better results.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: negative attitude - a pessimistic or unconstructive way of thinking
