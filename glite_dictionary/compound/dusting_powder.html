dusting powder

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌstɪŋ ˌpaʊdər / (AmE), / ˈdʌstɪŋ ˌpaʊdə / (BrE)
a fine, usually scented powder applied to the skin, typically after bathing, to absorb moisture and prevent irritation
powder applied to skin after bathing

Examples

She used a light dusting powder to keep her skin fresh after her shower.
The baby’s delicate skin was soothed with dusting powder after each bath.
Many people prefer perfumed dusting powder for its pleasant scent.
