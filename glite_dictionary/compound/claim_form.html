claim form

1. NOUN

/ kleɪm fɔrm / (AmE), / kleɪm fɔːm / (BrE)
an official document used to request payment or reimbursement, especially from an insurance company or organization
official document for filing a claim

Examples

Please complete the claim form and attach all relevant receipts.
The company rejected my claim form due to missing information.
You can download a claim form from the insurance provider's website.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: application form - a document used to apply for something (e.g., a job, loan, etc.)
