personal memory

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːsənl ˈmɛməri / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl ˈmɛməri / (BrE)
an individual's memory of specific events or experiences from their own life
memory of one's own life events

Examples

As I looked at old photographs, many personal memories came flooding back to me.
Writing a diary can help preserve personal memories for years to come.
Traumatic events often remain vivid in a person's personal memory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autobiographical memory - a person's memory of events and experiences from their own life
