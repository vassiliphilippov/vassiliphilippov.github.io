government department

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌvərnmənt dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvənmənt dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
a division of a government with specific responsibilities and functions, often administering public services or policies
division of a government with specific functions

Examples

The education government department announced new funding for schools last week.
Each government department is responsible for a particular area of public policy.
Many citizens find it difficult to navigate the different services offered by each government department.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ministry - a government department headed by a minister
  • Synonym: agency - an organization within government responsible for a specific function
