chamber of commerce

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃeɪmbɚ əv ˈkɑːmɚs / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪmbə əv ˈkɒməs / (BrE)
an organization of businesspeople formed to protect and promote the interests of the business community in a particular area
business association promoting local interests

Examples

The local chamber of commerce organized a networking event for small business owners.
Our company joined the chamber of commerce to connect with other local enterprises.
The chamber of commerce advocates for policies that benefit regional businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trade association - an organization founded and funded by businesses that operate in a specific industry
