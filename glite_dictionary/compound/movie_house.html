movie house

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuː.vi ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈmuː.vi ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a cinema or theater where films are shown to the public
a theater for showing films

Examples

We decided to go to the movie house to watch the latest blockbuster.
The small town's only movie house was packed on Saturday night.
She bought tickets to the historic movie house downtown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cinema - a theatre where films are shown for public entertainment
  • Synonym: movie theater - a theater where films are shown
