body of work

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdi əv wɜrk / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi əv wɜːk / (BrE)
the complete collection of works produced by a writer, artist, or other creator
complete works of an artist or writer

Examples

The exhibition featured the body of work she created over three decades.
Critics often compare the body of work of different filmmakers to assess their legacy.
His body of work continues to inspire musicians around the world.

Synset Examples

he studied the entire Wagnerian oeuvre
Picasso's work can be divided into periods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oeuvre - the works of a painter, composer, or author regarded collectively
