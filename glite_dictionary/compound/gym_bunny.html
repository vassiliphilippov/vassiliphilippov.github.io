gym bunny

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪm ˈbʌni / (AmE), / dʒɪm ˈbʌni / (BrE)
a person who frequently goes to the gym and is highly dedicated to fitness and exercise routines
person very dedicated to gym and fitness
slang

Examples

She's a true gym bunny and rarely misses a workout session.
People sometimes tease him for being a gym bunny, but he enjoys his routine.
After joining the local fitness center, Sarah quickly became a gym bunny.

Synset Examples

She’s a gym bunny, always posting workout selfies.
He jokingly calls her a gym bunny for her dedication.
Being a gym bunny keeps her motivated and fit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fitness enthusiast - a person keenly interested in physical fitness

Etymology

From 'gym' (short for gymnasium) and 'bunny' (slang for an enthusiast or someone devoted to something).
