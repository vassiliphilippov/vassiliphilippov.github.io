spiny-stemmed

1. ADJ

/ ˈspaɪni stɛmd / (AmE), / ˈspaɪni stɛmd / (BrE)
(of a plant) having a stem that is covered with spines or thorns.
having a stem with spines or thorns

Examples

The spiny-stemmed rosebush made pruning a careful task.
Several spiny-stemmed plants can be found in arid environments, protecting themselves from herbivores.
A cactus is a classic example of a spiny-stemmed plant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thorny-stemmed - having a stem with thorns
  • Synonym: spiny - covered with spines or thorns
