felt tip

1. NOUN

/ fɛlt tɪp / (AmE), / fɛlt tɪp / (BrE)
a pen with a writing tip made of felt or similar fibrous material, often used for drawing or coloring
pen with a felt writing tip

Examples

She used a felt tip to make colorful posters for the event.
The label was written in blue felt tip and easy to read.
Children often prefer drawing with felt tip pens because of their bright colors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marker - a pen with a broad, felt tip, used for writing or drawing
  • Antonym: ballpoint pen - a pen with a small metal ball at the tip for dispensing ink
