drip pot

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɪp ˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈdrɪp ˌpɒt / (BrE)
A coffeepot designed for making drip coffee by allowing water to pass slowly through ground coffee.
coffeepot for drip coffee

Examples

She prefers to brew her morning coffee with a drip pot for a richer flavor.
The kitchen counter had a well-used drip pot next to the grinder and beans.
He carefully poured hot water over the grounds in his drip pot to make a fresh cup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffeemaker - an appliance for making coffee
