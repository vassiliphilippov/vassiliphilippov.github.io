heart-leaf

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrt ˌlif / (AmE), / ˈhɑːt ˌliːf / (BrE)
A wild ginger plant with persistent, heart-shaped, pungent leaves, native to regions from West Virginia to Alabama.
wild ginger with heart-shaped leaves

Examples

The dense cluster of heart-leaf lined the shady path through the woods.
Gardeners admire the unique shape and aroma of the heart-leaf in their shaded plots.
Botanists discovered a rare heart-leaf specimen growing along the creek in Alabama.
