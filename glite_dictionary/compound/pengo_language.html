Pengo language

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛŋɡoʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɛŋɡəʊ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Dravidian language spoken in south central India, especially in Odisha.
Dravidian language of Odisha, India

Examples

Linguists have studied the Pengo language to understand its relationship to other Dravidian languages.
Many younger members of the community are no longer fluent in Pengo language.
Efforts are underway to preserve traditional folk stories in the Pengo language.
