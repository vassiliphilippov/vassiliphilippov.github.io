evergreen hit

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛvərˌɡrin hɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛvəˌɡriːn hɪt / (BrE)
a song or piece of music that retains its popularity and appeal over a long period of time
a song always popular over time

Examples

Mariah Carey's Christmas song has become an evergreen hit played every December.
The band's only evergreen hit still brings back memories of the 1980s.
Despite being released decades ago, the song remains an evergreen hit worldwide.

Synset Examples

The song remains an evergreen hit every holiday season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classic - a popular song from the past that is still appreciated
  • Antonym: one-hit wonder - an artist or song that was only briefly popular

Etymology

From 'evergreen' (always fresh or popular) + 'hit' (successful song)
