defence policy

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛns ˈpɑləsi / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns ˈpɒləsi / (BrE)
a plan or set of principles adopted by a government to manage the defense and security of a country against external threats
plan for national defense

Examples

The government is preparing a new defence policy to respond to changing global threats.
Changes in technology often require updates to a country's defence policy.
Her expertise in international relations helps shape national defence policy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: security policy - a plan covering measures to protect from threats and ensure safety
