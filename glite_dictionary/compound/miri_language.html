Miri language

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪri ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɪəri ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a little-known Kamarupan language spoken in parts of northeastern India
a Kamarupan language of northeastern India

Examples

Researchers have been working to document the Miri language before it is lost.
Very few children now speak Miri language in their daily lives.
Linguists consider the Miri language a valuable resource for understanding the region's culture.
