mind's eye

1. NOUN

/ maɪndz aɪ / (AmE), / maɪndz aɪ / (BrE)
the ability to imagine or visualize something as if seeing it in one's mind
imaginative visualization in the mind

Examples

She could see every detail of the house in her mind's eye.
With his mind's eye, he pictured how the garden would look in spring.
The author used her mind's eye to create a vivid world for her story.

Synset Examples

She envisioned the artwork in her mind's eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imagination - the faculty of forming new ideas or images not present to the senses
  • Synonym: to visualize - to form a mental image; to see in one's mind
