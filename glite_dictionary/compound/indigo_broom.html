indigo broom

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndɪɡoʊ bruːm / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪɡəʊ bruːm / (BrE)
a much-branched, upright herb with bright yellow flowers, found from Massachusetts to Florida in the United States.
upright herb with yellow flowers (US)

Examples

The fields were dotted with clusters of indigo broom in full bloom.
Gardeners in the Southeast often plant indigo broom for its vibrant yellow flowers.
We studied the distribution of indigo broom along the Atlantic coast during our research.
