breakfast area

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛkfəst ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˈbrɛkfəst ˈeəriə / (BrE)
a small space, usually near a kitchen, designated for eating light meals such as breakfast
space for eating breakfast near kitchen

Examples

They designed a cozy breakfast area next to the kitchen with large windows.
The hotel offers complimentary coffee and pastries in the breakfast area every morning.
We enjoyed reading the newspaper together in the sunny breakfast area.

Synset Examples

the breakfast nook had a built in table and seats

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breakfast nook - a small alcove or space for eating breakfast, often with built-in seating
