wood aster

1. NOUN

/ wʊd ˈæstər / (AmE), / wʊd ˈæstə / (BrE)
any of several species of aster native to eastern North America, typically found growing in woodlands
a woodland aster of North America

Examples

We spotted a beautiful wood aster blooming near the old oak tree.
The forest floor was dotted with clusters of wood aster in late summer.
She collects wildflowers, and her favorite is the delicate wood aster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a plant of the genus Aster with star-shaped flowers
