big and bad

1. ADJ

/ bɪɡ ənd bæd / (AmE), / bɪɡ ənd bad / (BrE)
extremely powerful, impressive, or intimidating; having great strength or impact often in a way that inspires awe or fear
extremely powerful or impressive

Examples

The team hired a big and bad linebacker to strengthen their defense.
Everyone was afraid of the big and bad dog guarding the gate.
They built a big and bad new engine for the racing car.

Synset Examples

The movie featured a big and bad villain.
He thinks his big and bad reputation helps him.
The big and bad storm left a trail of destruction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imposing - having an impressive or commanding presence
  • Synonym: formidable - inspiring fear or respect through being large, powerful, or capable
  • Antonym: weak - lacking power or strength
