Dari language

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑːri ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdɑːri ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
An Iranian language spoken mainly in Afghanistan, serving as one of the country's official languages.
official Iranian language of Afghanistan

Examples

He is fluent in both Pashto and Dari language.
The constitution of Afghanistan recognizes Dari language as an official language.
Many Afghan refugees attending the school speak Dari language at home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persian - the Iranian language of Iran and western Afghanistan, closely related to Dari

Etymology

From Persian 'Dari', meaning 'court language', referring to the language used at the royal court.
