retirement check

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈtaɪərmənt tʃɛk / (AmE), / rɪˈtʌɪəmənt tʃɛk / (BrE)
a regular payment received by a person who has retired from employment, typically issued monthly as a pension or benefit
monthly payment to a retired person

Examples

After working for forty years, my grandmother receives her retirement check every month.
He used part of his retirement check to travel around the country.
Many seniors depend on their retirement check for daily living expenses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pension - regular payment made during retirement from an investment fund or government
  • Antonym: salary - regular payment from employment
