to take a picture

1. VERB

/ teɪk ə ˈpɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / teɪk ə ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
to make a photograph of someone or something
to photograph

Examples

Could you take a picture of us in front of the statue?
She loves to take pictures when she travels to new places.
Yesterday, I took a picture of the sunset from my balcony.

Synset Examples

We took pictures of the snow on the rooftop today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photograph - to take a photograph of; to capture an image with a camera
