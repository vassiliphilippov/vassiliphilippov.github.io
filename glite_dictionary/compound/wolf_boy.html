wolf boy

1. NOUN

/ wʊlf bɔɪ / (AmE), / wʊlf bɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy who is believed to have been raised by wolves, often characterized by feral behavior
boy raised by wolves

Examples

The story of the wolf boy fascinated researchers and the public alike.
Legends from India often mention a mysterious wolf boy living in the jungle.
The villagers were afraid when they first encountered the wolf boy near the forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feral child - a child who has lived isolated from human contact from a very young age
