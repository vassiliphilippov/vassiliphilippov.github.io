dress shirt

1. NOUN

/ drɛs ʃɝt / (AmE), / drɛs ʃɜːt / (BrE)
a man's formal shirt with a stiff or starched front, typically white, worn with evening wear such as a tuxedo
formal shirt for evening wear

Examples

He wore a crisp dress shirt and a black bow tie to the gala.
A proper dress shirt is required when wearing a tuxedo.
His dress shirt had stylish pleats down the front.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tuxedo shirt - formal shirt worn with a tuxedo
