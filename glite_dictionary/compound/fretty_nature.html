fretty nature

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛti ˈneɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈfrɛti ˈneɪ.tʃə / (BrE)
A nervous or anxious temperament or personality.
nervous or anxious temperament

Examples

Her fretty nature often caused her to overthink even the smallest details.
People with a fretty nature may find it difficult to relax in unfamiliar situations.
Despite his fretty nature, he was able to stay focused during the stressful meeting.

Synset Examples

Her fretty nature made her a great planner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anxious - experiencing worry, unease, or nervousness
  • Antonym: calm nature - a relaxed or untroubled temperament

Etymology

From 'fretty', meaning inclined to fret or worry, plus 'nature'.
