flow rate

1. NOUN

/ ˈfloʊ ˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈfləʊ ˌreɪt / (BrE)
The volume of fluid passing through a point in a system per unit of time.
volume of fluid per unit time

Examples

The engineer measured the flow rate of the river during the rainy season.
If the flow rate increases, more coolant will move through the system in less time.
Doctors monitored the flow rate of the intravenous drip to ensure proper hydration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volume - the amount of space that a substance or object occupies
  • Degree Higher: velocity - the speed of something in a given direction
