endgame phase

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛn(d)ˌɡeɪm feɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛn(d)ˌɡeɪm feɪz / (BrE)
the final stage of a process, activity, or negotiation when the outcome is decided or imminent
final stage of a process

Examples

As the negotiations entered the endgame phase, both sides were eager to reach an agreement.
The project is now in its endgame phase, with final testing underway.
In the endgame phase of the merger, legal teams ironed out the remaining details.

Synset Examples

Negotiations have reached the endgame phase.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endgame - final stage of a chess game or process
  • Antonym: beginning phase - initial stage of a process

Etymology

From 'endgame', originally a chess term for the final stage of a game, plus 'phase'.
