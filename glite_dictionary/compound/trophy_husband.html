trophy husband

1. NOUN

/ ˈtroʊfi ˌhʌzbənd / (AmE), / ˈtrəʊfi ˌhʌzbənd / (BrE)
an attractive younger husband, often of a wealthy older woman, valued primarily for his appearance or status rather than for personal qualities
attractive younger husband of a wealthy older woman

Examples

Some people assumed he was just a trophy husband, but he was actually a successful entrepreneur.
She enjoys the attention that comes with having a trophy husband much younger than herself.
In the socialite’s circle, having a trophy husband became a new trend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trophy wife - an attractive young wife of an affluent older man, considered a status symbol

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'trophy wife', using 'trophy' in the sense of a prize or status symbol.
