painted-leaf begonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪntɪd liːf bɪˈɡoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪd liːf bɪˈɡəʊniə / (BrE)
a type of ornamental begonia with distinctive, colorful, patterned leaves, often grown as a houseplant
decorative begonia with patterned leaves

Examples

The painted-leaf begonia added a splash of color to her living room with its vibrant foliage.
Gardeners prize the painted-leaf begonia for its unique leaf patterns and ease of care indoors.
He chose a painted-leaf begonia as a centerpiece, impressed by its shimmering silver and red hues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: begonia - a genus of flowering plants including many ornamental varieties
