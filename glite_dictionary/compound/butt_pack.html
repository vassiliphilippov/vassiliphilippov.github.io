butt pack

1. NOUN

/ bʌt pæk / (AmE), / bʌt pæk / (BrE)
A type of waist pack worn with the pouch positioned at the back, often used for carrying personal items during activities like hiking or in the military.
waist pack worn at the back

Examples

He adjusted his butt pack before heading out on the trail.
The soldier carried extra ammunition in his butt pack.
For longer day trips, I prefer using a butt pack to keep my hands free.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fanny pack - a waist pouch typically worn at the front
