bouncing Bet

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaʊnsɪŋ ˌbɛt / (AmE), / ˈbaʊnsɪŋ ˌbɛt / (BrE)
A perennial plant of European origin, Saponaria officinalis, with pink or white flowers and leaves that yield a soap-like substance when bruised.
a European plant with soap-like leaves and pink or white flowers

Examples

The old farmhouse garden was filled with blooming bouncing Bet in late summer.
She used the leaves of bouncing Bet as a natural soap for washing clothes.
Wild bouncing Bet grows along the riverbanks, adding a splash of pink to the landscape.

Etymology

Nickname, probably from 'Betty', used as a familiar name; the 'bouncing' describes the robustness of the plant.
