fig wax

1. NOUN

/ fɪɡ wæks / (AmE), / fɪɡ waks / (BrE)
a hard, cream-colored wax obtained from the leaves of certain Javanese fig trees, used in polishes and other products
wax from Javanese fig trees

Examples

The polish contained fig wax to provide a glossy finish.
Some cosmetic products use fig wax as a natural ingredient.
Harvesting fig wax from Javanese trees requires careful processing.
