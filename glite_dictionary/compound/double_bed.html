double bed

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌbəl bɛd / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl bɛd / (BrE)
a bed large enough for two people to sleep in
bed for two people

Examples

We decided to buy a double bed for the guest room to make visitors comfortable.
Many hotel rooms offer either two twin beds or one double bed for couples.
They slept soundly together in the spacious double bed after a long journey.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: twin bed - a single bed for one person; often one of a pair
  • Degree Higher: queen bed - a bed larger than a double bed, for two people
  • Degree Higher: king bed - a bed larger than a queen bed, usually the largest standard size
