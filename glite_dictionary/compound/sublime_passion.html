sublime passion

1. NOUN

/ səˈblaɪm ˈpæʃən / (AmE), / səˈblaɪm ˈpæʃən / (BrE)
a feeling of intense and elevated enthusiasm or admiration, often inspiring awe or reverence
intense elevated enthusiasm

Examples

The artist's work reveals a sublime passion for color and form.
Her speech was infused with sublime passion for social justice.
They watched in awe as the conductor led the orchestra with sublime passion.

Synset Examples

He spoke with a sublime passion for his art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passion - strong and barely controllable emotion
  • Synonym: enthusiasm - intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval
