purple virgin's bower

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl ˈvɝːdʒɪnz ˈbaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈvɜːdʒɪnz ˈbaʊ.ə / (BrE)
A climbing plant native to northeastern North America, noted for its waxy purplish-blue flowers.
climbing plant with purplish-blue flowers

Examples

The purple virgin's bower brightened the garden with its striking blue flowers.
Hikers spotted the delicate blooms of purple virgin's bower along the woodland trail.
She planted purple virgin's bower to climb the trellis beside the porch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clematis - a genus of climbing plants with showy flowers
