industrial enterprise

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdʌstriəl ˌɛntɚˌpraɪz / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəl ˌɛntəˌprʌɪz / (BrE)
the large-scale development, organization, or undertaking of industry, especially in manufacturing or production
large-scale industrial activity or business

Examples

The country's rapid economic growth was fueled by its expanding industrial enterprise.
Government incentives encouraged new industrial enterprise in the northern region.
Global competition has made industrial enterprise more innovative and efficient over the past decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: industry - economic activity concerned with processing raw materials and manufacturing goods
