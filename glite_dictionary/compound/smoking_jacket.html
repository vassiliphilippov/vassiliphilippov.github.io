smoking jacket

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmoʊkɪŋ ˌdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkɪŋ ˌdʒækɪt / (BrE)
a man's comfortable, often velvet, jacket with a tie belt, traditionally worn at home while smoking or relaxing
man's soft home jacket with tie belt

Examples

He changed into his smoking jacket before joining his guests in the lounge.
The vintage photograph showed a gentleman in a velvet smoking jacket reading by the fire.
Wearing his favorite smoking jacket, he relaxed with a book and a glass of brandy.

Etymology

From 'smoking' + 'jacket', originally designed to protect clothes from tobacco smoke in the 19th century.
