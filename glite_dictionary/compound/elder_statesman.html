elder statesman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛldɚ ˈsteɪtsmən / (AmE), / ˈɛldə ˈsteɪtsmən / (BrE)
an experienced and respected senior political leader whose advice is often sought by those currently in power
respected senior political adviser

Examples

After retiring from office, he became an elder statesman admired by politicians across party lines.
The president often consulted the elder statesman during times of national crisis.
International leaders respected him as an elder statesman known for his wisdom and diplomacy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: statesman - a skilled, experienced, and respected political leader

2. NOUN

/ ˈɛldər ˈsteɪtsmən / (AmE), / ˈɛldə ˈsteɪtsmən / (BrE)
a respected senior man, especially one who formerly had an important position, whose advice is valued
respected senior man, former leader

Examples

The former president became an elder statesman, offering guidance to younger politicians.
Many in the industry view him as the elder statesman of broadcasting.
As an elder statesman, his opinions still carry considerable weight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wise man - a man considered to be knowledgeable or experienced; a sage
