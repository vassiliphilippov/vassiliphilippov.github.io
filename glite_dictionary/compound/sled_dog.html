sled dog

1. NOUN

/ slɛd dɔɡ / (AmE), / slɛd dɒɡ / (BrE)
a dog trained to pull a sled, typically as part of a team, especially in snowy or Arctic regions
dog trained to pull a sled

Examples

The team of sled dogs raced across the frozen tundra, pulling the musher behind them.
Alaskan Huskies are often bred and trained as sled dogs for harsh winter conditions.
During the expedition, their sled dog proved invaluable for transporting heavy equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: husky - a breed of sled-pulling dog known for endurance and thick fur
  • Synonym: sled - a vehicle for traveling over snow, often pulled by animals
