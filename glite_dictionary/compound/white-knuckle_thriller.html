white-knuckle thriller

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈnʌkəl ˈθrɪlər / (AmE), / waɪt ˈnʌkəl ˈθrɪlə / (BrE)
An especially exciting or suspenseful film that causes feelings of anxiety or tension in the audience.
very exciting, suspenseful film

Examples

That latest action movie is a true white-knuckle thriller with nonstop suspense.
If you enjoy edge-of-your-seat cinema, you won't want to miss this white-knuckle thriller.
Critics are calling it the most intense white-knuckle thriller of the year.

Synset Examples

The movie was a white-knuckle thriller from start to finish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thriller - an exciting or suspenseful film, book, etc.

Etymology

From the image of gripping something so tightly in fear or excitement that one's knuckles turn white.
