wrestling mat

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛs.lɪŋ mæt / (AmE), / ˈrɛs.lɪŋ mæt / (BrE)
a thick, padded mat on which wrestling matches or practice sessions are conducted
mat used for wrestling matches

Examples

The referee checked the wrestling mat before the tournament began to ensure it was clean and safe.
Wrestlers practiced their moves on the wrestling mat after school every day.
It is important for a wrestling mat to have enough padding to prevent injuries during matches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mat - a piece of material used as a protective covering on a surface
