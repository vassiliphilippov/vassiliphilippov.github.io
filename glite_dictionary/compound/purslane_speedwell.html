purslane speedwell

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrslən ˈspiːdˌwɛl / (AmE), / ˈpɜːslən ˈspiːdˌwɛl / (BrE)
A North American annual plant with small white flowers, often found as a weed and naturalized in South America and Europe.
North American annual plant; weed

Examples

Purslane speedwell often appears in gardens and lawns, quickly establishing itself as a common weed.
Botanists note that purslane speedwell has adapted well to various climates outside its native range.
We spent the afternoon pulling purslane speedwell from the flower beds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speedwell - any plant of the genus Veronica, often with small blue or white flowers
