moonbatty antics

1. NOUN

/ ˈmunˌbæti ˈæntɪks / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌbæti ˈæntɪks / (BrE)
crazy or eccentric behavior; actions that are odd, bizarre, or unreasonable, often in a way that attracts attention
crazy or eccentric behavior
slang

Examples

His moonbatty antics on stage always left the audience both confused and entertained.
Despite criticism, the artist's moonbatty antics helped him build a cult following.
The tabloids thrived on the politician's latest moonbatty antics during the campaign.

Synset Examples

His moonbatty antics often made headlines in the tabloids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - unconventional and slightly strange
  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged, extremely foolish
  • Antonym: sane - mentally healthy, reasonable

Etymology

Formed from 'moonbat', a slang term for an eccentric or crazy person, and 'antics'.
