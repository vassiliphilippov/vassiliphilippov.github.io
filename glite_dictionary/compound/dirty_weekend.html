dirty weekend

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɝːti wiːkˌɛnd / (AmE), / ˈdɜːti wiːkˌɛnd / (BrE)
a weekend spent away from home, usually by a couple, for romantic or sexual activities, often in secret or with an illicit element
weekend away for romantic or sexual activities

Examples

They planned a dirty weekend in Paris to escape their busy lives.
Rumors of his latest dirty weekend caused quite a stir at the office.
She joked about needing a dirty weekend with her boyfriend after months apart.

Synset Examples

Their dirty weekend turned into a fun adventure.
She looked forward to a relaxing dirty weekend with her partner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tryst - a secret meeting between lovers
  • Synonym: romantic getaway - a vacation or trip intended for romance
