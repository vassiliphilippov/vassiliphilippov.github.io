materials handling

1. NOUN

/ məˈtɪriəlz ˈhændlɪŋ / (AmE), / məˈtɪəriəlz ˈhændlɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of moving, loading, unloading, and storing goods within a factory or warehouse, especially using mechanical equipment
movement and storage of goods in industry

Examples

Proper materials handling is essential for maintaining workplace safety and efficiency.
The company invested in new equipment to improve their materials handling processes.
Automation has transformed the field of materials handling in modern factories.
