modern design

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑdɚn dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɒdn dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
a style of design characterized by clean lines, minimal ornamentation, and contemporary materials; typically associated with the period from the early to mid-20th century
contemporary, minimalist style of design

Examples

The new office building features a modern design with large glass windows and open spaces.
Many homeowners prefer modern design for its simplicity and functionality.
His furniture pieces reflect the principles of modern design through their sleek forms and lack of ornament.

Synset Examples

The architect plans to give dome a modern design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contemporary design - current design style reflecting present trends
