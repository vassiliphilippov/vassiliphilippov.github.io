sharp suit

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑrp sut / (AmE), / ʃɑːp sjuːt / (BrE)
A suit that is stylish and attractively well-fitted.
stylish and well-fitted suit

Examples

He impressed the interviewers by walking in wearing a sharp suit and polished shoes.
She bought her brother a sharp suit for his graduation ceremony.
Nothing makes a better first impression at a gala than a sharp suit.

Synset Examples

He wore a sharp suit to the important business meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stylish suit - a suit that is fashionable and attractive
  • Synonym: smart suit - a neat, well-dressed suit (especially in British English)
