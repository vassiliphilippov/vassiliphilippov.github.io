non compos mentis

1. ADJ

/ non ˌkoʊmpoʊs ˈmɛntɪs / (AmE), / nɒn ˌkɒmpɒs ˈmɛntɪs / (BrE)
Not of sound mind; legally not competent to manage one's own affairs or to stand trial.
not of sound mind; legally incompetent
legal

Examples

The defendant was declared non compos mentis and could not stand trial.
His actions suggested he was non compos mentis at the time of the incident.
The will was contested on the grounds that the testator was non compos mentis when it was written.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insane - mentally ill; not sane
  • Antonym: compos mentis - of sound mind; legally competent

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'not of sound mind'.
