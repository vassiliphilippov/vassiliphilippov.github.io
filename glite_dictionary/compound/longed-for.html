longed-for

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɔŋd fɔr / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋd fə / (BrE)
Greatly desired or eagerly wished for over a period of time.
greatly desired; eagerly awaited

Examples

After years of hard work, she finally achieved her longed-for promotion.
Their reunion after so many years was a longed-for event for the whole family.
He looked at the diploma, his longed-for reward for all those late nights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desired - wanted strongly; wished for
  • Synonym: coveted - strongly desired, especially something belonging to another
  • Antonym: unwanted - not desired or wished for

Etymology

Formed from the past participle of 'long' (to yearn) and the preposition 'for.'
