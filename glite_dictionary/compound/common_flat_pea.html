common flat pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈflæt pi / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈflæt piː / (BrE)
A low, spreading evergreen shrub of southern Australia, with triangular or heart-shaped leaves, orange-yellow flowers, and flat, winged pods.
Australian evergreen shrub with orange-yellow flowers

Examples

The common flat pea is often found growing along the sandy coastal areas of southern Australia.
Botanists admire the bright orange-yellow flowers of the common flat pea during the spring.
Gardeners appreciate the common flat pea for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
