hay ride

1. NOUN

/ ˈheɪ ˌraɪd / (AmE), / ˈheɪ ˌraɪd / (BrE)
a recreational ride in a wagon or cart filled with hay, typically offered as entertainment at fairs or on farms
wagon ride on hay for fun

Examples

Every autumn, our family goes on a hay ride through the apple orchard.
The children laughed and sang during the hay ride at the festival.
They organized a hay ride for the guests as part of the country wedding celebration.

Synset Examples

The kids were thrilled by the hay ride at the fair.
We enjoyed a fun hay ride through the pumpkin patch.

Etymology

Formed from 'hay' (cut dried grass) and 'ride' (journey as a passenger), referencing the vehicle's hay-filled bed.
