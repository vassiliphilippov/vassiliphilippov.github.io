solution to

1. NOUN

/ səˈluʃən tu / (AmE), / səˈluːʃən tuː / (BrE)
an answer or means of solving a problem or difficulty
answer to a problem

Examples

The solution to the puzzle took her several hours to figure out.
After hours of discussion, they finally found a solution to their disagreement.
There is no easy solution to climate change, but every effort helps.

Synset Examples

Dere's always a solution to every problem.
The solution to the problem is quite simple.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: answer to - something spoken, written, or done in return to a question or problem
