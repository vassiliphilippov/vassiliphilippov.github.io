balance sheet

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæl.əns ˌʃit / (AmE), / ˈbæl.əns ˌʃiːt / (BrE)
a statement showing the financial position of a company or organization at a particular date, detailing its assets, liabilities, and equity
financial statement listing assets and liabilities

Examples

The company's balance sheet revealed a significant increase in assets over the past year.
Before investing, she carefully reviewed the balance sheet to assess the firm's stability.
Auditors found discrepancies when analyzing the latest balance sheet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: income statement - financial statement showing income and expenses over a period
  • Degree Lower: liabilities - the amounts owed by a company; debts listed on the balance sheet
  • Synonym: statement of financial position - another term for balance sheet
