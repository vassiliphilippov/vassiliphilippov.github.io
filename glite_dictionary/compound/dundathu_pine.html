dundathu pine

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌn.də.θu ˌpaɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʌn.də.θjuː ˌpaɪn / (BrE)
An Australian timber tree resembling the kauri but with lighter and softer wood.
Australian timber tree with light, soft wood

Examples

The furniture was crafted from dundathu pine, making it both attractive and lightweight.
Many Australian homes use dundathu pine because of its ease of working and soft texture.
Unlike kauri, dundathu pine is preferred for items where lighter wood is desired.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: kauri - Large New Zealand coniferous tree (Agathis australis) known for its valuable timber
