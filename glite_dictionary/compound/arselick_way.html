arselick way

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrs.lɪk weɪ / (AmE), / ˈɑːs.lɪk weɪ / (BrE)
An excessively flattering or sycophantic manner, often insincere, especially to gain favour.
excessively flattering manner
informal slang british

Examples

No one respects him because of his arselick ways with the boss.
She recognized his arselick way of dealing with management to get ahead.
In meetings, he always adopts an arselick way to impress superiors.

Synset Examples

I can't stand his arselick ways in the office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sycophantic - behaving in an overly flattering way to gain advantage
  • Synonym: obsequious - excessively eager to please or obey

Etymology

From British slang 'arselick' (to flatter), combining 'arse' (buttocks) and 'lick'; 'way' meaning manner.
