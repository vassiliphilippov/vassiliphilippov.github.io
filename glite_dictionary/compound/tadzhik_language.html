Tadzhik language

1. NOUN

/ tɑːdˈʒiːk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / tædˈʒiːk ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Iranian language spoken by the Tajik people, closely related to Farsi and used mainly in Tajikistan and parts of Iran
Iranian language of the Tajik people

Examples

She studied the Tadzhik language while living in Dushanbe.
The Tadzhik language uses the Cyrillic script in Tajikistan, unlike Persian in Iran.
Many words in the Tadzhik language are borrowed from Russian due to the country's history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tajik - the Iranian language of the Tajik people
  • Degree Higher: Farsi - the Persian language spoken in Iran

Etymology

From Russian 'Tadzhik', from Persian 'Tājīk', combined with 'language'.
