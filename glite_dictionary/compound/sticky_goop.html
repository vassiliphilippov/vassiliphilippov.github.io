sticky goop

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪki ɡuːp / (AmE), / ˈstɪki ɡuːp / (BrE)
a thick, adhesive substance that is often messy and sticks to surfaces
thick, sticky substance

Examples

There was sticky goop all over the kitchen floor after the experiment.
The children got sticky goop on their fingers from the arts and crafts project.
She tried to wash the sticky goop out of her hair but it took several rinses.

Synset Examples

I can't believe how sticky this goop is on my hands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goo - a soft, sticky substance
