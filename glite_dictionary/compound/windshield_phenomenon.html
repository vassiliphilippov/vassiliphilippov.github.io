windshield phenomenon

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪndʃiːld fəˈnɑːmɪnɑːn / (AmE), / ˈwɪndʃiːld fɪˈnɒmɪnən / (BrE)
an optical effect on a car windshield, such as glare or distortion, that reduces visibility for the driver
optical effect reducing windshield visibility

Examples

The windshield phenomenon caused a blinding glare during her morning commute.
She struggled with the windshield phenomenon while driving at night in heavy rain.
During the storm, the windshield phenomenon made it nearly impossible to see the road.
