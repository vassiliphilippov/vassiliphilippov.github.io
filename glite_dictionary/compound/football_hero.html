football hero

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊt.bɔl ˌhɪə.roʊ / (AmE), / ˈfʊt.bɔːl ˌhɪə.rəʊ / (BrE)
a football player who is greatly admired for outstanding skill or achievement
famous and admired football player

Examples

After scoring the winning touchdown, he became a football hero in his hometown.
Many young athletes dream of one day being called a football hero.
The crowd cheered as the retired football hero walked onto the field for a final farewell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: football star - a renowned and talented football player
  • Degree Higher: football legend - a football player with legendary status due to their achievements
