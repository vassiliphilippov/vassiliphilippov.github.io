brick-molding machine

1. NOUN

/ brɪk ˈmoʊldɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / brɪk ˈməʊldɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
an industrial machine designed to shape and manufacture bricks from raw materials.
machine for making bricks

Examples

The factory invested in a new brick-molding machine to increase production capacity.
Regular maintenance of the brick-molding machine ensures efficient and high-quality output.
Operators must be trained before using the advanced brick-molding machine on the assembly line.
