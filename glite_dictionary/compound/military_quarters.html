military quarters

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪləˌtɛri ˈkwɔrtərz / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˈkwɔːtəz / (BrE)
the accommodations or living spaces provided for personnel at a military post
living accommodations for military personnel

Examples

The soldiers returned to their military quarters after a long day of training.
Each base had separate military quarters for officers and enlisted personnel.
Visitors were not allowed inside the restricted area of the military quarters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barracks - buildings where military personnel live
