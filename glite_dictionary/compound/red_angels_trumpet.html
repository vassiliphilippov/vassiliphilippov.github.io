red angel's trumpet

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈeɪndʒəlz ˈtrʌmpɪt / (AmE), / rɛd ˈeɪndʒəlz ˈtrʌmpɪt / (BrE)
A South American shrub with large orange-red, trumpet-shaped flowers.
a shrub with large orange-red flowers

Examples

The red angel's trumpet is prized in gardens for its striking flowers.
Gardeners should be cautious, as the red angel's trumpet contains toxic compounds.
A mature red angel's trumpet can grow several meters high and bloom throughout the summer.
