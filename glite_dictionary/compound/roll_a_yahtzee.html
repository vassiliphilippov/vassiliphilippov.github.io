to roll a Yahtzee

1. VERB

/ roʊl ə ˈjɑːtsi / (AmE), / rəʊl ə ˈjɑːtsi / (BrE)
to achieve the highest possible score on a single roll in the game of Yahtzee by throwing five dice of the same number
achieve top roll in Yahtzee game

Examples

She was thrilled when she rolled a Yahtzee on her first turn.
If you roll a Yahtzee, you get fifty points in the standard rules.
I've never rolled a Yahtzee despite playing dozens of games.

Synset Examples

She couldn't believe her luck when she rolled a Yahtzee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: score a Yahtzee - obtain a Yahtzee during play

Etymology

From the proprietary name Yahtzee, a dice game invented in the 20th century.
