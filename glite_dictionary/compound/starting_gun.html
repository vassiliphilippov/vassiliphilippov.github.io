starting gun

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑrtɪŋ ɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtɪŋ ɡʌn / (BrE)
a gun fired to signal the beginning of a race or other timed event
gun for signaling the start of a race

Examples

The runners took their positions, waiting for the starting gun to fire.
After the starting gun sounded, the athletes dashed off the blocks.
Spectators held their breath until the starting gun marked the official beginning of the marathon.

Synset Examples

The starting gun fired, signaling the beginning of the race.
The coach fired the starting gun, and the race began.
Everyone was tense as they awaited the starting gun.
They are ready to fire the starting gun on the project.
When the starting gun went off, they sprinted ahead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: starter pistol - a pistol used to signal the start of a race; synonym of 'starting gun'
