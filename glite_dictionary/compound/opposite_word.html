opposite word

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːpəzɪt wɜːrd / (AmE), / ˈɒpəzɪt wɜːd / (BrE)
a word that has a meaning directly contrary to that of another word; an antonym
a word with the opposite meaning (antonym)

Examples

Can you think of an opposite word for 'happy'?
In English, 'black' is often considered the opposite word to 'white'.
Teachers sometimes ask students to find the opposite word for each vocabulary item.

Synset Examples

to him the antonym of ‘gay’ was ‘depressed’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antonym - a word with the opposite meaning to another
  • Antonym: synonym - a word with a similar meaning to another
