points from Gryffindor

1. NOUN

/ pɔɪnts frəm ɡrɪfəndɔr / (AmE), / pɔɪnts frəm ɡrɪfəndɔː / (BrE)
points subtracted from a team's total, especially in the context of Hogwarts in the Harry Potter series, often as a form of punishment or penalty
penalty points (Harry Potter context)

Examples

The class groaned when their behavior cost them five points from Gryffindor.
Breaking the rules will result in losing points from Gryffindor.
She worked hard all week to avoid losing any points from Gryffindor.

Synset Examples

She was upset after losing ten points from Gryffindor.
He lost ten points from Gryffindor for being late.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: house points - points awarded or deducted to Hogwarts houses in Harry Potter
  • Synonym: to deduct - to subtract or take away an amount or part from a total

Etymology

Refers to the house points system in J.K. Rowling's Harry Potter books, where Gryffindor is one of the four houses.
