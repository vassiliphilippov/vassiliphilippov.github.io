calabash pipe

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæl.əˌbæʃ paɪp / (AmE), / ˈkæl.əˌbæʃ paɪp / (BrE)
A type of smoking pipe with a curved stem and a large bowl made from a calabash gourd.
smoking pipe with a calabash gourd bowl

Examples

He collected rare calabash pipes from antique shops all over the world.
The detective in the novel was often pictured with a calabash pipe in hand.
Smoking a calabash pipe gives the tobacco a unique, mellow flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pipe - a tube with a bowl for smoking tobacco

Etymology

From 'calabash' (the gourd used, from Spanish 'calabaza') + 'pipe'.
