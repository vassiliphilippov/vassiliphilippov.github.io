future history

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuːtʃər ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / ˈfjuː.tʃər ˈhɪs.tər.i / (BrE)
an account or speculative analysis of imagined future events, often based on current trends or projections
speculative account of future events

Examples

Many science fiction writers construct a future history to provide context for their stories.
The idea of creating a future history helps researchers explore the consequences of new technologies.
His novel is set within a meticulously crafted future history that spans several centuries.

Synset Examples

Studying future history helps us understand potential outcomes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alternate history - a genre of fiction narrating events as if history had unfolded differently
