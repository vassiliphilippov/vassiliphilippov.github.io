carrot stick

1. NOUN

/ ˈkærət stɪk / (AmE), / ˈkærət stɪk / (BrE)
a stick-shaped piece of raw carrot, typically eaten as a snack or part of a salad
piece of raw carrot cut into a stick shape

Examples

She packed some carrot sticks in her lunchbox for a healthy snack.
The children dipped their carrot sticks in hummus at the party.
I prefer eating carrot sticks instead of potato chips when watching TV.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celery stick - a stick-shaped piece of celery eaten raw
  • Synonym: carrot - an orange root vegetable
