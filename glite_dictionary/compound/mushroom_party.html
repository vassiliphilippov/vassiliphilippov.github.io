mushroom party

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌʃruːm ˈpɑːrti / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃrʊm ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
a themed social gathering where mushrooms are the focus, often involving mushroom-based food or activities
themed event focused on mushrooms

Examples

Every year, their garden hosts a mushroom party featuring unique dishes made from wild mushrooms.
We decorated the room with mushroom-themed decorations for the mushroom party.
After the mushroom party, everyone exchanged recipes they had discovered.

Synset Examples

The annual mushroom party attracted food lovers from everywhere.
We had a fun mushroom party with friends last weekend.
