orbiculate leaf

1. NOUN

/ ɔːrˈbɪkjələt liːf / (AmE), / ɔːˈbɪkjʊlət liːf / (BrE)
A leaf that is circular or nearly circular in shape.
circular or nearly circular leaf

Examples

The water lily has an orbiculate leaf that floats on the surface of the pond.
Some plant species are easily recognized by their large, flat, orbiculate leaves.
In her botany textbook, the diagram showed a classic example of an orbiculate leaf.

Etymology

From Latin 'orbiculatus' (rounded), diminutive of 'orbis' (circle or disc).
