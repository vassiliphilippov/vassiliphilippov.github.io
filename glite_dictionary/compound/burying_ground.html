burying ground

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛr.i.ɪŋ ˌɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈbɛr.i.ɪŋ ˌɡraʊnd / (BrE)
an area of land used for burying the dead; a cemetery or graveyard
land for burials; cemetery

Examples

The old church burying ground was covered in wildflowers and moss.
They discovered several ancient tombstones in the village burying ground.
The local historical society preserves the town's oldest burying ground.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cemetery - a place where dead people are buried
  • Synonym: graveyard - an area of land, often beside a church, where people are buried
