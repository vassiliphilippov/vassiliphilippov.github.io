Humber Bridge

1. PROPN

/ ˈhʌmbər brɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhʌmbə brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a large suspension bridge across the Humber estuary at Hull, England, notable for its length and engineering significance
suspension bridge in Hull, England

Examples

The Humber Bridge was once the longest single-span suspension bridge in the world.
Thousands of vehicles cross the Humber Bridge every day, connecting communities on both sides of the estuary.
From the top of the Humber Bridge, you can see for miles across the surrounding countryside.
