Gypsy language

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪpsi ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪpsi ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an alternative name, now considered dated and potentially offensive, for the Romani language spoken by the Romani people
dated name for the Romani language

Examples

She has spent years studying the Gypsy language and its dialects across Europe.
The book provides an overview of the Gypsy language and its rich oral tradition.
It's important to refer to the Gypsy language as 'Romani' to avoid outdated terminology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Romani - the Indo-Aryan language of the Roma people
