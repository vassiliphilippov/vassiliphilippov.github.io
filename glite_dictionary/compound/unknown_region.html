unknown region

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈnoʊn ˈriːdʒən / (AmE), / ʌnˈnəʊn ˈriːdʒən / (BrE)
an area that has not been discovered, explored, or is unfamiliar
area not discovered or explored

Examples

The explorers set out into the unknown region beyond the mountains, hoping to map new territory.
Space scientists often refer to the depths of the ocean as an unknown region still full of mysteries.
For centuries, the Arctic remained an unknown region to most of the world.

Synset Examples

they came like angels out of the unknown

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terra incognita - a region that has not been mapped or documented
  • Antonym: known region - an area that has been identified or explored
