milk punch

1. NOUN

/ mɪlk pʌntʃ / (AmE), / mɪlk pʌntʃ / (BrE)
an alcoholic beverage made with spirits, milk, sugar, and spices, served cold
alcoholic drink with milk, sugar, spices

Examples

He served a delicious milk punch at the holiday party, garnished with nutmeg.
The recipe for milk punch calls for rum, brandy, milk, and a variety of spices.
After dinner, guests enjoyed a glass of chilled milk punch in the garden.
