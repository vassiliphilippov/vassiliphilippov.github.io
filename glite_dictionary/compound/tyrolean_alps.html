Tyrolean Alps

1. PROPN

/ taɪˈroʊliən ælps / (AmE), / tɪˈrəʊliən ælps / (BrE)
a mountain range in the Tyrol region of Austria and Italy, known for its scenic beauty and popularity as a tourist destination
mountain range in the Tyrol region (Austria/Italy)

Examples

Skiing in the Tyrolean Alps attracts visitors from all over the world.
The quaint villages nestled in the Tyrolean Alps offer a unique cultural experience.
They spent their honeymoon hiking through the breathtaking Tyrolean Alps.

Etymology

From Tyrol, the region in the Alps, and Alps, from Latin Alpes.
