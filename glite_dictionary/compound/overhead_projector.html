overhead projector

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊvərˌhɛd prəˈdʒɛktər / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˌhɛd prəˈdʒɛktə / (BrE)
a device that projects images of transparencies onto a screen, typically used in presentations or classrooms
device for projecting images from transparencies

Examples

The teacher used the overhead projector to display diagrams during the lesson.
Many conference rooms are still equipped with an overhead projector and a stack of transparencies.
She wrote her notes on a transparency to be shown with the overhead projector.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: projector - a device that projects images or movies onto a surface
