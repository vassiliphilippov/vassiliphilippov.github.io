rat terrier

1. NOUN

/ ræt ˈtɛriɚ / (AmE), / ræt ˈtɛriə / (BrE)
a small breed of terrier originally bred to hunt and catch rats
breed of small hunting terrier

Examples

The rat terrier is known for its energetic personality and intelligence.
Farmers used rat terriers to control rodent populations in barns and fields.
As a puppy, her rat terrier learned to fetch balls and chase squirrels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrier - a breed of dog often used for hunting small animals
