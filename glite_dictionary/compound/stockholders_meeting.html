stockholders meeting

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑːkˌhoʊldɚz ˈmiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɒkˌhəʊldəːz ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a regular or special meeting where the management of a company reports to its stockholders and company-related matters are discussed or voted on
meeting of company stockholders

Examples

The company will announce its new strategy at the upcoming stockholders meeting.
Every year, all investors are invited to attend the annual stockholders meeting.
Questions about executive compensation were raised during the stockholders meeting last spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annual general meeting - yearly meeting of a company's shareholders
  • Degree Lower: board meeting - meeting of a company's board of directors
