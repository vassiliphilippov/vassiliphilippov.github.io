jive turkey

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪv ˌtɝːki / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪv ˌtɜːki / (BrE)
a person who is insincere, dishonest, or pretentious, often trying to impress others with meaningless talk or falsehoods
insincere or pretentious person (slang)
slang american

Examples

Don't listen to that jive turkey; he never tells the truth.
Back in the day, calling someone a jive turkey was a serious insult.
Nobody trusted the jive turkey after he was caught lying again.

Synset Examples

He's such a jive turkey, always making excuses.
Don't be a jive turkey; tell the truth.
She called him a jive turkey for lying again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phony - someone who is fake or insincere
  • Antonym: straight shooter - someone who is honest and direct

Etymology

American slang; 'jive' (deceptive talk) plus 'turkey' (a foolish person), popularized in African-American communities in the 1970s.
