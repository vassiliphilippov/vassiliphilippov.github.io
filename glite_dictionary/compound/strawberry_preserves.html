strawberry preserves

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔːˌbɛri prɪˈzɜrvz / (AmE), / ˈstrɒbəri prɪˈzɜːvz / (BrE)
a sweet spread made from whole strawberries and sugar, often cooked until thick and used on bread or desserts
sweet spread made from strawberries

Examples

She spread strawberry preserves on her toast for breakfast.
The bakery sells homemade strawberry preserves in glass jars.
I used strawberry preserves as a filling for the cake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jam - a sweet spread made from crushed fruit and sugar
  • Synonym: marmalade - a preserve made from citrus fruit, especially oranges
