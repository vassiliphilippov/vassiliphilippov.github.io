text tennis

1. NOUN

/ tɛkst ˈtɛnɪs / (AmE), / tɛkst ˈtɛnɪs / (BrE)
a rapid or extended exchange of text messages between two people, similar to a back-and-forth rally in tennis
back-and-forth texting exchange
informal

Examples

She and her brother were locked in text tennis for hours, joking about their day.
Our text tennis conversation lasted the entire train ride home.
After a misunderstanding, they cleared things up through some intense text tennis.

Synset Examples

I love engaging in a bit of text tennis with friends.
We played text tennis until late at night.

Etymology

Modelled after the game of tennis, with 'text' referring to text messages and 'tennis' alluding to the exchange of shots.
