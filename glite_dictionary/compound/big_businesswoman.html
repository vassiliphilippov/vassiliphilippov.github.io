big businesswoman

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ˈbɪznɪsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈbɪznəsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A very wealthy or powerful woman who is active in business.
wealthy or powerful businesswoman

Examples

She became a big businesswoman after launching several successful tech startups.
The awards ceremony honored a big businesswoman in the construction industry for her innovative leadership.
Many young women aspire to be a big businesswoman one day.
