nativity story

1. NOUN

/ nəˈtɪvəti ˈstɔri / (AmE), / nəˈtɪvɪti ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
the story of the birth of Jesus Christ, especially as recounted in the Christian Gospels
the story of Jesus's birth in the Bible

Examples

Every Christmas, children in the church put on a play retelling the nativity story.
Many artists have depicted scenes from the nativity story over the centuries.
The teacher read the nativity story to the class before the holiday break.

Synset Examples

The nativity story is celebrated every Christmas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nativity - the birth of Jesus Christ
  • Synonym: birth of Christ - the event of Jesus Christ's birth

Etymology

From Middle English 'nativite' (birth), from Latin 'nativitas'; 'story' from Old French 'estorie', from Latin 'historia'.
