nohowish attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊˌhaʊɪʃ ˈæt̬ɪˌtjud / (AmE), / ˈnəʊˌhaʊɪʃ ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
An unhelpful or negatively disposed demeanor or manner; a tendency to be discouraging or obstructive.
negative or unhelpful demeanor
informal

Examples

Her nohowish attitude made teamwork extremely difficult for everyone involved.
The manager addressed his nohowish attitude during the performance review, urging him to be more constructive.
You won't get far in life if you maintain a nohowish attitude towards every challenge.

Synset Examples

He has a nohowish attitude that annoys everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: negative attitude - a pessimistic or unhelpful manner
