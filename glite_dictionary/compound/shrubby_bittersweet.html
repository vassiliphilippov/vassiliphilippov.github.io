shrubby bittersweet

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃrʌbi ˈbɪtərˌswit / (AmE), / ˈʃrʌbi ˈbɪtəˌswiːt / (BrE)
A twining shrub of North America with yellow capsules that contain scarlet seeds, known for its decorative use.
North American twining shrub with yellow capsules

Examples

The berries of the shrubby bittersweet add a splash of color to autumn gardens.
Gardeners value shrubby bittersweet for its attractive, twining vines and distinctive seeds.
I planted a shrubby bittersweet along the fence to create a natural screen.
