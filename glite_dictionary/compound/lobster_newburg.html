lobster Newburg

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑbstər ˈnuːˌbɜːrɡ / (AmE), / ˈlɒbstə ˈnjuːˌbɜːɡ / (BrE)
A dish of lobster served in a rich, creamy Newburg sauce, typically on buttered toast or rice.
lobster dish in creamy Newburg sauce

Examples

He ordered the lobster Newburg as his main course at the elegant restaurant.
The chef prepared a delicious lobster Newburg and served it over steaming rice.
For the special occasion, she made homemade lobster Newburg following a traditional recipe.

Etymology

From the name 'Newburg,' possibly after Ben Wenberg, a 19th-century sea captain, with the name later altered.
