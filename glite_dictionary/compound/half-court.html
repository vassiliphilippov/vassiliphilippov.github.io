half-court

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæf kɔrt / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf kɔːt / (BrE)
the middle area of a basketball court, especially the area around the center line
middle area of a basketball court

Examples

She dribbled past her opponent at half-court before passing the ball.
The team set up their defense just beyond half-court.
He launched the ball from half-court and scored as the buzzer sounded.

Synset Examples

The athlete made an impressive shot from half-court.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: center line - the line dividing a basketball court into two equal halves
