Mad Anthony Wayne

1. PROPN

/ mæd ˈænθəni weɪn / (AmE), / mæd ˈænθəni weɪn / (BrE)
(1745–1796) An American general known for his bold and daring tactics during the American Revolution.
American Revolutionary War general

Examples

Mad Anthony Wayne led his troops with relentless courage at the Battle of Stony Point.
Many historians credit Mad Anthony Wayne with revitalizing American morale during the war.
Despite the odds, Mad Anthony Wayne never hesitated to lead from the front.
