corporate ladder

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔːrpərət ˈlædər / (AmE), / ˈkɔːpərət ˈlædə / (BrE)
the hierarchy of positions or levels of authority within a company, often viewed as a series of steps leading to higher status or responsibility
hierarchy of positions in a company

Examples

She was determined to climb the corporate ladder despite the challenges she faced.
Many employees set their sights on reaching the top of the corporate ladder.
After years of hard work, he finally landed a position high up on the corporate ladder.

Synset Examples

He quickly moved up the corporate ladder.
He stepped down from the corporate ladder to pursue his passion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: career path - the general course or progression of a person's working life
  • Antonym: glass ceiling - an unofficial barrier to advancement in a profession, especially affecting women and minorities
  • Synonym: promotion - the act of raising someone to a higher rank or position
