genus Mesembryanthemum

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛz.əmˌbraɪ.ænˈθiː.məm / (AmE), / ˌmiːz.ɛmˌbraɪ.ænˈθiː.məm / (BrE)
a genus of South African annual or biennial plants with flowers that open only in bright sunlight
genus of South African sun-loving plants

Examples

The genus Mesembryanthemum includes many species known for their dazzling flowers.
Botanists study the genus Mesembryanthemum to understand plant adaptation to arid environments.
Gardeners appreciate plants from the genus Mesembryanthemum for their ability to thrive in full sun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice plant - common name for species in the genus Mesembryanthemum

Etymology

From Greek 'mesembria' (midday) + 'anthemon' (flower), referring to the flowers opening at midday.
