dark glasses

1. NOUN

/ dɑrk ˈɡlæsɪz / (AmE), / dɑːk ˈɡlɑːsɪz / (BrE)
Spectacles with darkened or polarized lenses worn to protect the eyes from bright light or sunlight.
sunglasses; spectacles with dark lenses

Examples

She always wears dark glasses when she goes outside, even on cloudy days.
The movie star arrived at the airport in dark glasses to avoid being recognized.
He put on his dark glasses before driving into the bright morning sun.

Synset Examples

he was wearing a pair of mirrored shades

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunglasses - glasses with tinted lenses to protect against sunlight
