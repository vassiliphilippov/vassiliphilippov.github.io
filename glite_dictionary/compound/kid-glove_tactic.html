kid-glove tactic

1. NOUN

/ kɪd ɡlʌv ˈtæk.tɪk / (AmE), / kɪd ɡlʌv ˈtæk.tɪk / (BrE)
a method of handling a sensitive issue with great care and delicacy
delicate or careful approach to a sensitive issue

Examples

The CEO used a kid-glove tactic when discussing layoffs with her staff.
In negotiations with the protesters, the mayor favored a kid-glove tactic to avoid escalation.
The therapist recommended a kid-glove tactic for raising the issue with the child.

Synset Examples

They approached the sensitive topic with kid-glove tactics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gentle approach - an approach that is gentle and cautious
  • Antonym: heavy-handed tactic - a harsh or forceful method

Etymology

From 'kid glove,' referring to gloves made from kid (young goat) leather, symbolizing gentleness and care, combined with 'tactic'.
