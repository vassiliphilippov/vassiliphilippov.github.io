adding machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈædɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈadɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a mechanical or electronic calculator that performs basic arithmetic operations, especially addition and subtraction
simple calculator for arithmetic

Examples

Before computers, accountants used an adding machine for all financial calculations.
He watched as the numbers rolled up on the adding machine after each sale.
The museum displays an old adding machine next to early computers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calculator - an electronic or mechanical device for making mathematical calculations
