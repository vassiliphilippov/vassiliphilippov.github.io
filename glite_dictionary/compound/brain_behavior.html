brain behavior

1. NOUN

/ breɪn bəˈheɪvjər / (AmE), / breɪn bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
the relationship between brain activity and observable behavior, often studied in neuroscience or psychology
relationship between brain activity and behavior

Examples

The researcher examined brain behavior to find links between cognitive processes and neural activity.
Advances in technology have made it easier to study brain behavior in living subjects.
Understanding brain behavior is essential for treating neurological disorders.

Synset Examples

He pursued a degree in neuropsych to understand brain behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neuropsychology - the study of the relationship between brain function and behavior
