to unravel the mystery

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈrævəl ðə ˈmɪstəri / (AmE), / ʌnˈravəl ðə ˈmɪstəri / (BrE)
to solve or make clear something that is complex or difficult to understand, especially a situation or problem that was previously puzzling.
solve or explain a mystery

Examples

Detectives worked tirelessly to unravel the mystery surrounding the missing painting.
It took years of research to finally unravel the mystery of the strange illness.
New evidence helped the journalist unravel the mystery behind the company's sudden collapse.

Synset Examples

The mystery began to unravel after the detective arrived.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to solve - to find an answer to a problem or puzzle
  • Synonym: to clear up - to make a situation or mystery easier to understand
