cave woman

1. NOUN

/ keɪv ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / keɪv ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman from prehistoric times who is imagined as living in caves, often depicted as using primitive tools and wearing simple clothing.
prehistoric woman living in a cave

Examples

Many stories and movies portray the cave woman as a brave hunter and gatherer.
A cave woman would have relied on simple tools made from stone and bone.
The archaeologist showed us a reconstruction of what a prehistoric cave woman might have looked like.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cave man - A prehistoric man who lived in caves
