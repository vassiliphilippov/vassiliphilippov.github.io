pediculosis pubis

1. NOUN

/ ˌpidɪkjəˈloʊsɪs ˈpjuːbɪs / (AmE), / ˌpiːdɪkjʊˈləʊsɪs ˈpjuːbɪs / (BrE)
A medical condition involving infestation of the pubic hair by crab lice (Phthirus pubis).
infestation of pubic hair by crab lice
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with pediculosis pubis after discovering crab lice during the examination.
People with pediculosis pubis often experience intense itching in the affected area.
Proper treatment is essential to eliminate pediculosis pubis and prevent its spread to others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab louse - a louse (Phthirus pubis) that infests human pubic hair

Etymology

From Latin 'pediculus' (louse) and 'pubis' (the hair covering the pubic region).
