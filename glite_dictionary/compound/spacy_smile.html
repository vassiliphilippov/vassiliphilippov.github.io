spacy smile

1. NOUN

/ ˈspeɪsi smaɪl / (AmE), / ˈspeɪsi smaɪl / (BrE)
a vacant or absent-minded facial expression, often indicating daydreaming or distraction
vacant or absent-minded facial expression

Examples

She had a spacy smile on her face throughout the meeting, barely paying attention.
His spacy smile made it clear he was lost in thought.
I noticed the spacy smile and realized she was daydreaming again.

Synset Examples

He gave a spacy smile as he daydreamed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blank look - expression showing no emotion or thought
  • Synonym: vacant expression - face appearing empty of thought or emotion
