Jane Hancock

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒeɪn ˈhæŋ.kɑːŋk / (AmE), / ˌdʒeɪn ˈhæŋ.kɒŋk / (BrE)
(informal) A person's signature, especially used in a playful or humorous way.
(slang) a person's signature
informal slang american

Examples

She placed her Jane Hancock at the bottom of the page to make it official.
Just give me your Jane Hancock right here, and we're good to go.
He had a unique way of signing his Jane Hancock on every document.

Synset Examples

Her Jane Hancock was needed to finalize the agreement.
He scribbled his Jane Hancock with a flourish.
Make sure to add your Jane Hancock on the contract.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: John Hancock - (U.S. slang) a person's signature
  • Synonym: signature - a person's name written by themselves

Etymology

Modelled on 'John Hancock', the name of a prominent US statesman whose large signature appears on the US Declaration of Independence.
