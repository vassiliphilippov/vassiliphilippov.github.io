toilet tissue

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔɪlət ˈtɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlət ˈtɪsjʉː / (BrE)
soft, thin absorbent paper used for cleaning oneself after using the toilet
soft paper used in toilets

Examples

She realized they were out of toilet tissue while she was in the bathroom.
The hotel provides complimentary toilet tissue in every room.
He accidentally bought scented toilet tissue instead of the regular kind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toilet paper - soft absorbent paper for use in toilets; synonym for 'toilet tissue'
