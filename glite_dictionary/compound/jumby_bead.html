jumby bead

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌmbi ˌbiːd / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmbi ˌbiːd / (BrE)
A West Indian tree similar to Ormosia monosperma but larger, with smaller leaflets and seeds.
West Indian tree with small seeds

Examples

The bright red jumby bead seeds are often used in jewelry across the Caribbean.
Botanists identified the large tree as a jumby bead growing near the river.
Children in the village collected jumby bead seeds to make colorful necklaces.
