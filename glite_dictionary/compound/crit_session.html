crit session

1. NOUN

/ krɪt ˈsɛʃən / (AmE), / krɪt ˈsɛʃ.ən / (BrE)
a meeting where work is discussed and evaluated to provide critique and feedback, typically in creative or academic fields
meeting for critique and feedback

Examples

The designers gathered for a crit session.
During the crit session, students received constructive feedback on their projects.
She prepared her latest artwork for tomorrow's crit session.

Synset Examples

The designers gathered for a crit session.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: critique - detailed analysis and assessment, especially of creative work

Etymology

Shortening of 'critique session', with 'crit' as a clipped form of 'critique'.
