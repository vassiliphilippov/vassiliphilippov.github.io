Entsy language

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛntsi ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɛntsi ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Uralic language spoken by the Enets people of Siberia.
Uralic language of the Enets in Siberia

Examples

The Entsy language is considered severely endangered due to its declining number of speakers.
Linguists are studying the grammar and vocabulary of the Entsy language.
He recorded folk tales in the Entsy language for an ethnographic project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nenets language - Uralic language closely related to Entsy, spoken by the Nenets people
  • Degree Higher: Uralic languages - the family of languages including Entsy, Nenets, and others

Etymology

From 'Entsy' (Enets), the people speaking the language, and 'language'.
