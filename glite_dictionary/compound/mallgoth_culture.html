mallgoth culture

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæl.ɡɑθ ˈkʌl.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈmæl.ɡɒθ ˈkʌl.tʃə / (BrE)
A subculture characterized by a gothic-inspired fashion style and social activities centered around shopping malls, especially popular in the late 1990s and early 2000s.
gothic-influenced subculture in malls

Examples

The influence of mallgoth culture can still be seen in some fashion trends today.
Many teenagers found a sense of belonging through mallgoth culture in the 1990s.
She decorated her room to reflect her love for mallgoth culture.

Synset Examples

Mallgoth culture thrived in the local shopping center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goth culture - subculture linked with gothic music, fashion, and aesthetics

Etymology

Blend of 'mall' (shopping center) and 'goth' (gothic subculture).
