brick-making machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪk ˌmeɪkɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈbrɪk ˌmeɪkɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine designed for manufacturing bricks from clay or other materials, typically used in industrial production.
industrial machine for making bricks

Examples

The factory installed a new brick-making machine to increase production efficiency.
A modern brick-making machine can produce thousands of bricks per hour.
Maintenance of the brick-making machine is essential to ensure consistent quality.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: brick - a rectangular block made of clay for building
  • Synonym: kiln - a furnace or oven for firing bricks or ceramics
