People's Republic of China

1. PROPN

/ ˌpipəlz rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈtʃaɪnə / (AmE), / ˌpiːplz rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈtʃaɪnə / (BrE)
the official name of the country commonly known as China, a communist nation in eastern Asia; the most populous country in the world
official name of China, Asian country

Examples

The People's Republic of China has experienced rapid economic growth over the past four decades.
China officially refers to itself as the People's Republic of China in diplomatic documents.
After 1949, the People's Republic of China was established under communist rule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: China - a country in East Asia, commonly referred to as 'China'
