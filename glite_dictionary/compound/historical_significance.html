historical significance

1. NOUN

/ hɪˈstɔrɪkəl sɪgˈnɪfɪkəns / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪkəl sɪgˈnɪfɪkəns / (BrE)
the importance or impact that something has had in history, especially in shaping events, people, or ideas
importance in history

Examples

The museum highlights the historical significance of the ancient artifacts on display.
Many buildings in the old town have great historical significance due to their connection with key events.
The documentary explored the historical significance of the civil rights movement.

Synset Examples

She visited Selma to understand its historical significance.
Bukit Aman is famous for its historical significance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: historical importance - the quality of being important in history
