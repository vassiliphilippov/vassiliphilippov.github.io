dining-room suite

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪnɪŋ ruːm swit / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnɪŋ ruːm swiːt / (BrE)
a matched set of furniture designed for use in a dining room, typically including a table and chairs, and sometimes cabinets or sideboards
set of furniture for a dining room

Examples

They bought a beautiful dining-room suite for the new house.
The antique dining-room suite included a large oak table and eight chairs.
Her grandmother's dining-room suite was passed down through generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suite - a set of matching furniture pieces for a room
