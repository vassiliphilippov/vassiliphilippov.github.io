phone jack

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊn ˌdʒæk / (AmE), / ˈfəʊn ˌdʒæk / (BrE)
a socket or outlet into which a telephone plug is inserted to connect a telephone to a network or circuit
socket for connecting a telephone

Examples

Make sure your telephone is securely connected to the phone jack before making a call.
He needed to replace the old phone jack in the living room to fix the connection.
Some modern homes no longer have a traditional phone jack installed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: phone plug - a plug that fits into a phone jack
