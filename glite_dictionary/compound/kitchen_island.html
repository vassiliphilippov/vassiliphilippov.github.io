kitchen island

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtʃɪn ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃɪn ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
A freestanding counter in a kitchen that is accessible from all sides, often used for food preparation or dining.
freestanding kitchen counter

Examples

We renovated our kitchen and added a kitchen island for extra workspace.
The chef used the kitchen island to prepare all the ingredients before cooking.
Many modern homes feature a kitchen island with built-in storage and seating.
