call mark

1. NOUN

/ kɔl mɑrk / (AmE), / kɔːl mɑːk / (BrE)
a symbol or combination of characters written on a book to indicate its location on the shelves, especially in a library
library identifier on books

Examples

Please check the call mark before reshelving the book.
Every book in the reference section has a unique call mark.
She wrote the call mark on a slip to help locate the book quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: call number - an identification number assigned to a library item for its shelf location
