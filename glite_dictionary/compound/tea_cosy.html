tea cosy

1. NOUN

/ ti ˈkoʊzi / (AmE), / tiː ˈkəʊzi / (BrE)
a padded or knitted covering placed over a teapot to keep the tea warm
padded or knitted teapot cover

Examples

She placed a bright red tea cosy over the pot to keep the tea warm.
The hand-knitted tea cosy added a charming touch to their afternoon tea.
After pouring, he quickly replaced the tea cosy to maintain the temperature.

Etymology

From 'tea' (the beverage) and 'cosy' (from English dialect 'cosy', meaning snug or warm).
