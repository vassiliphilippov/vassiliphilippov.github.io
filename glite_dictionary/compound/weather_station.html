weather station

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛðɚ ˌsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə ˌsteɪʃən / (BrE)
a place equipped with instruments and staff to observe, measure, and record meteorological conditions
facility for recording weather data

Examples

The national weather station network provides real-time updates on storms and rainfall.
Every morning, the technicians at the weather station check and calibrate the measuring instruments.
Data collected by the mountaintop weather station helps scientists track climate change.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meteorological station - station for recording meteorological data
