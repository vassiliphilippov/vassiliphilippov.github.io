bucket of bolts

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌkɪt əv boʊlts / (AmE), / ˈbʌkɪt əv bəʊlts / (BrE)
an old or badly functioning vehicle or device, especially one that is noisy or falling apart
old, poor vehicle or device
informal

Examples

After years of faithful service, his truck was little more than a bucket of bolts.
She laughed when he pulled up in that noisy bucket of bolts.
No mechanic wants to spend all day fixing a bucket of bolts like that.

Synset Examples

He joked that his bike was a real bucket of bolts.
This computer is a bucket of bolts that's falling apart.
My old car is nothing but a bucket of bolts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jalopy - an old, dilapidated car
  • Synonym: clunker - an old or badly functioning vehicle
