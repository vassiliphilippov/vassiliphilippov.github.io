body type

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdi taɪp / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi taɪp / (BrE)
a category or classification of a person's physique, especially in terms of build, shape, or structure
classification of physique or build

Examples

Each athlete has a different body type that affects their performance and training needs.
The fashion industry often favors certain body types over others.
Knowing your body type can help you choose clothes that fit you best.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physique - the form, size, and development of a person's body
  • Synonym: build - the physical structure of a person's body
