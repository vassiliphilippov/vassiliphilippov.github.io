ice chest

1. NOUN

/ aɪs tʃɛst / (AmE), / aɪs tʃɛst / (BrE)
A portable container insulated to keep food and drinks cool, typically by using ice or ice packs.
portable insulated container for keeping items cool

Examples

We packed sandwiches and drinks in the ice chest for our picnic.
Please grab a soda from the ice chest by the tent.
She filled the ice chest with ice to keep the water bottles cold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cooler - a portable insulated container for keeping food and drinks cold
