French person

1. NOUN

/ frɛntʃ ˈpɜrsən / (AmE), / frentʃ ˈpɜːsn / (BrE)
a person who is a citizen or national of France
a person of French nationality

Examples

Marie is a French person who moved to London for work.
As a French person, Pierre celebrates Bastille Day every year.
The restaurant owner is a French person passionate about sharing traditional cuisine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Frenchman - a man of French nationality
  • Synonym: Frenchwoman - a woman of French nationality
