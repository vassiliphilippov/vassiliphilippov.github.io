halo spot

1. NOUN

/ ˈheɪloʊ spɑt / (AmE), / ˈheɪləʊ spɒt / (BrE)
A bacterial disease of bean plants characterized by pale spots with light-colored halos on the leaves.
bacterial disease of bean plants

Examples

Halo spot is a serious issue for farmers growing beans in humid regions.
You can identify halo spot by the distinctive pale circles on affected leaves.
The agricultural extension office has published a guide on managing halo spot in crops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blight - a plant disease, especially one caused by fungi or bacteria
