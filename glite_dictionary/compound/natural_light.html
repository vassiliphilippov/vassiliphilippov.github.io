natural light

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃ.ɚl laɪt / (AmE), / ˈnætʃ.ərəl laɪt / (BrE)
light that comes from the sun or other natural sources, not from artificial lamps or bulbs
light from the sun or natural sources

Examples

The paintings look especially vibrant when displayed in natural light.
Large windows were installed to maximize the amount of natural light in the room.
Photographs taken in natural light often have softer, more flattering tones.

Synset Examples

This design looks better in natural light.
She opened the curtain to let in natural light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daylight - the natural light of the day coming from the sun
