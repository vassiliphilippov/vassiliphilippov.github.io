finger drill

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪŋɡɚ drɪl / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə drɪl / (BrE)
a small hand-operated drill used for delicate or precision tasks, such as making tiny holes in materials
small hand drill for precision work

Examples

A finger drill is ideal for work where precision and control are essential.
