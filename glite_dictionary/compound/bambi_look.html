Bambi look

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæmbi lʊk / (AmE), / ˈbæmbi lʊk / (BrE)
an expression or appearance characterized by innocence and naivety, typically with wide, childlike eyes
an innocent or naive facial expression

Examples

With her Bambi look, she convinced everyone she was telling the truth.
He gave the teacher a Bambi look when asked about the missing homework.
The actress is famous for her Bambi look and gentle manner onscreen.

Synset Examples

She has a Bambi look with those big, innocent eyes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: innocent look - an expression showing innocence

Etymology

From Bambi, the innocent deer character in the 1942 Disney film, and the English word 'look'.
