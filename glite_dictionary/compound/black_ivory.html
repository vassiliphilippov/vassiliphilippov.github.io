black ivory

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈaɪvəri / (AmE), / blak ˈaɪvəri / (BrE)
a rare and expensive coffee made from beans eaten and excreted by elephants, noted for its unique flavor
rare, luxury coffee processed by elephants

Examples

On his trip to Thailand, he sampled the renowned black ivory coffee.
Some connoisseurs seek out black ivory for its smooth taste and exclusivity.
The hotel offered a complimentary cup of black ivory to its VIP guests.

Synset Examples

He treated himself to a cup of black ivory.
Black ivory coffee is known for its unique flavor.
