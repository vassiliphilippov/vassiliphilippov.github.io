two-way

1. ADJ

/ ˈtuˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈtuː.weɪ / (BrE)
operating, moving, or allowing movement in both directions; involving or permitting activity or communication in two opposite directions.
allowing movement or operation in both directions

Examples

The city council decided to convert the street from two-way to one-way traffic to ease congestion.
We need a two-way radio to communicate with the rescue team.
You must be careful when driving on two-way roads in unfamiliar countries.

Synset Examples

a two-way valve
two-way traffic
two-way streets

Related Terms

  • Antonym: one-way - operating or permitting operation in only one direction
