transfer of training

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrænsfɚ əv ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrænsfə əv ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the application of skills or knowledge learned in one context to a different but similar situation
applying learned skills to new contexts

Examples

Teachers aim to improve the transfer of training so that students use classroom skills in everyday life.
A key challenge in employee development is ensuring the transfer of training to the work environment.
The study measured how effective the transfer of training was from the simulation to real-world tasks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: generalization - process of extending knowledge or skills to new situations
