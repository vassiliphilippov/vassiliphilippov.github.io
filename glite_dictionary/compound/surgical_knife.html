surgical knife

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝdʒɪkəl naɪf / (AmE), / ˈsɜːdʒɪkəl naɪf / (BrE)
a very sharp knife designed for use during surgical procedures
sharp knife used in surgery

Examples

The surgeon picked up a surgical knife to make the first incision.
Proper sterilization of each surgical knife is critical to prevent infection.
In the operating room, various surgical knives are used for different types of tissue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scalpel - a small, extremely sharp knife used in surgery
