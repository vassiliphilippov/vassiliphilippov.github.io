fir clubmoss

1. NOUN

/ fɝ ˈklʌbˌmɑs / (AmE), / fə ˈklʌbˌmɒs / (BrE)
A species of clubmoss found in northern Europe and America, resembling a miniature fir tree.
a clubmoss resembling a miniature fir

Examples

The forest floor was dotted with fir clubmoss, giving the area a soft, green carpet.
Botanists studied the fir clubmoss to understand its adaptation to cold climates.
Children gathered fir clubmoss to use in their miniature holiday decorations.
