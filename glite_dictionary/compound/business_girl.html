business girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a woman who is actively involved in business activities, especially in a professional environment
woman engaged in business

Examples

After college, she quickly became a successful business girl in the finance sector.
The magazine featured several prominent business girls who are leading startups.
Everyone admired her ambition and work ethic as a business girl in a competitive industry.

Synset Examples

She’s a real business girl, always closing deals.
The conference was full of ambitious business girls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: businesswoman - a woman who works in business, especially at a high level
