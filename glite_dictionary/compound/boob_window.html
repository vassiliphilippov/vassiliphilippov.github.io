boob window

1. NOUN

/ bub ˈwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / buːb ˈwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
a cut-out section in clothing, especially tops or dresses, that exposes cleavage
cut-out in clothing showing cleavage

Examples

Her dress had a prominent boob window that drew everyone's attention.
The designer was criticized for using a boob window in the runway collection.
Some people find the boob window trend bold and empowering, while others see it as too revealing.

Synset Examples

She wore a top with a boob window to the party.
The outfit featured a controversial boob window for the fashion show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleavage - the hollow between a woman's breasts as exposed by a low-cut garment
