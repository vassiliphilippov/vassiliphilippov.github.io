hetero sapiens

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɛt̬əroʊ ˈseɪpiənz / (AmE), / ˌhɛtərəʊ ˈseɪpiənz / (BrE)
a semi-humorous or academic term for modern humans who are heterosexual, emphasizing straight individuals as a social group
term for heterosexual humans

Examples

Some sociologists use the term hetero sapiens to discuss issues of sexuality and culture.
The documentary explored how hetero sapiens interact with LGBTQ+ communities.
In certain articles, hetero sapiens is used humorously to highlight the majority culture's perspective.

Synset Examples

The study focused on the behaviors of hetero sapiens in modern society.
Many hetero sapiens struggle to understand LGBTQ+ experiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Homo sapiens - the scientific name for modern humans
  • Synonym: heterosexual - sexually attracted to people of the opposite sex

Etymology

Modeled after Latin 'Homo sapiens', combining 'hetero-' (different) and 'sapiens' (wise, human species).
