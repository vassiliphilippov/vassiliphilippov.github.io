marathon of fun

1. NOUN

/ ˈmærəˌθɑn əv fʌn / (AmE), / ˈmærəθ(ə)n əv fʌn / (BrE)
a long-lasting event or activity filled with enjoyment or entertainment
an extended period of enjoyment

Examples

The festival was a marathon of fun that lasted all weekend.
Our vacation turned into a marathon of fun with nonstop activities and laughter.
Children's camp is designed to be a marathon of fun from start to finish.

Synset Examples

The charity event turned into a marathon of fun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party - a social gathering for entertainment

Etymology

from 'marathon', originally a long-distance race, used metaphorically, and 'fun' (enjoyment)
