county line

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊnti laɪn / (AmE), / ˈkaʊnti laɪn / (BrE)
the boundary separating two counties
boundary between two counties

Examples

The old oak tree stands exactly on the county line between Harrison and Clark counties.
We crossed the county line just before sunset, entering a new jurisdiction.
The road follows the county line for several miles before turning north.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: border - the edge or boundary of something
  • Synonym: county - a territorial division within a country
