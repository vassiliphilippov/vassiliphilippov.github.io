rabbit-o mode

1. NOUN

/ ˈræbɪt oʊ ˈmoʊd / (AmE), / ˈræbɪt əʊ ˈməʊd / (BrE)
A state of becoming overly alert or anxious, especially in response to a stressful situation.
state of extreme alertness or anxiety
informal

Examples

Whenever there’s a sudden loud noise, she goes into rabbit-o mode.
The team went into rabbit-o mode just before the big presentation.
He tends to switch to rabbit-o mode when he feels overwhelmed at work.

Synset Examples

He shifted into rabbit-o mode when the meeting started.
