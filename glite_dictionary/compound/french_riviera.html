French Riviera

1. PROPN

/ frɛntʃ rɪˈvjɛrə / (AmE), / frɛntʃ rɪˈvɪəriə / (BrE)
the coastal region of southeastern France on the Mediterranean, renowned for its beaches, resorts, and mild climate.
Mediterranean coast of southeastern France

Examples

Many celebrities have luxury homes on the French Riviera.
The French Riviera is famous for its sunny weather and glamorous film festivals.
She spent her honeymoon exploring the picturesque towns of the French Riviera.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Riviera - the coastal region along the Mediterranean, especially in southeastern France and northwestern Italy
