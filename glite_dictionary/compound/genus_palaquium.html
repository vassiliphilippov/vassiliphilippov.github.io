genus Palaquium

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs pəˈlɑːkwiəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs pəˈlakwiəm / (BrE)
a large genus of Malaysian trees with milky sap and leathery leaves, including species such as the gutta-percha tree
botanical genus of Malaysian trees

Examples

The genus Palaquium includes numerous trees valued for their latex.
Botanists discovered a new species within the genus Palaquium in the rainforests of Borneo.
The gutta-percha tree is a well-known member of the genus Palaquium.

Etymology

Genus named from Malay and Latinized scientific botanical nomenclature.
