stylus printer

1. NOUN

/ ˈstaɪləs ˈprɪntər / (AmE), / ˈstaɪləs ˈprɪntə / (BrE)
an impact printer that creates characters and images by striking a pattern of dots onto paper using wires or styli
printer using wires or styli to form dots

Examples

The office used a stylus printer for generating large batches of invoices.
A stylus printer works by forming characters out of tiny dots on the page.
Before laser printers became common, stylus printers were popular in banks and businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dot matrix printer - impact printer that creates images from dots
