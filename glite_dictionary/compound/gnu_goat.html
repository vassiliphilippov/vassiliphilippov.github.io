gnu goat

1. NOUN

/ nu ˈɡoʊt / (AmE), / njuː ˈɡəʊt / (BrE)
a large, heavily built goat-antelope found in the eastern Himalayas
large Himalayan goat-antelope

Examples

The gnu goat is well adapted to the rocky slopes of the eastern Himalayas.
Researchers observed several gnu goats grazing at high altitudes during their expedition.
A mature gnu goat can weigh over 200 kilograms.
