finnan haddie

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪnən ˈhædi / (AmE), / ˈfɪnən ˈhædi / (BrE)
a haddock that has been smoked, traditionally prepared by baking or sometimes broiling with butter
smoked haddock dish

Examples

For breakfast, he enjoyed a traditional Scottish dish of finnan haddie served with toast.
The restaurant’s signature finnan haddie was delicately flavored with butter and herbs.
She learned to make finnan haddie from her grandmother, who grew up near the Scottish coast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoked haddock - haddock preserved by smoking

Etymology

From 'Finnan', a region in Scotland, and 'haddie', a diminutive of 'haddock'.
