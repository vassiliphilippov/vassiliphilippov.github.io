Pesh Merga

1. NOUN

/ pɛʃ ˈmɛrɡə / (AmE), / pɛʃ ˈmɛːɡə / (BrE)
A member of the Kurdish guerrilla or military forces in Iraqi Kurdistan, known for their role in regional security and conflicts.
Kurdish guerrilla force in Iraq

Examples

The Pesh Merga played a crucial role in the fight against ISIS in northern Iraq.
Many Pesh Merga soldiers have been involved in defending Kurdish towns from attacks.
The bravery of the Pesh Merga has been widely recognized by international observers.

Etymology

From Kurdish pêşmerga, literally meaning 'one who faces death'.
