customer agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌstəmɚ ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəmə ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a foreign buyer who purchases goods directly for resale in another market
foreign purchaser who resells goods

Examples

The company appointed a customer agent in Asia to expand its international sales.
A customer agent typically purchases goods in bulk for overseas distribution.
Several exporters rely on a customer agent to facilitate sales in foreign markets.
