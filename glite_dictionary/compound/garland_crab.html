garland crab

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑr.lənd kræb / (AmE), / ˈɡɑː.lənd kræb / (BrE)
a medium-sized tree of the eastern United States, noted for its pink blossoms and small yellow fruit
tree with pink blossoms and yellow fruit

Examples

The garland crab is celebrated for its profusion of delicate pink blossoms each spring.
Birds are attracted to the yellow fruit produced by the garland crab.
We planted a garland crab in the front yard for its ornamental value.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crabapple - any tree of the genus Malus with small, tart fruit and blossoms
