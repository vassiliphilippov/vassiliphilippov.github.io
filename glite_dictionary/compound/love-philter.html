love-philter

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌv ˌfɪltər / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ˌfɪltə / (BrE)
A drink supposed to have magical properties, especially one that causes the person who drinks it to fall in love with someone.
magic potion causing love

Examples

The sorceress handed him a love-philter to win the queen's heart.
Legends often feature a love-philter as a key element in tales of enchantment.
She secretly slipped a love-philter into his wine, hoping he would fall for her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: love potion - a drink with supposed magic power to cause love

Etymology

From 'love' + 'philter', with 'philter' from Greek *philtron* (love charm).
