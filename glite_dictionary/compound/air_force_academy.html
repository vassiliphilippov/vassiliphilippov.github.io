air force academy

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌfɔrs əˈkædəmi / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌfɔːs əˈkædəmi / (BrE)
An educational institution for training future officers in an air force.
academy for training air force officers

Examples

After graduating from the air force academy, he was commissioned as a second lieutenant.
Admission to the air force academy is highly competitive and based on academic and physical performance.
The air force academy offers specialized programs in leadership and aviation science.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military academy - an academy for training military officers
