business coaching

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs ˈkoʊtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˈkəʊtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
professional guidance and support provided to individuals or groups to enhance their effectiveness and performance in a business environment
professional development guidance for business

Examples

She decided to invest in business coaching to develop her leadership skills.
Many successful entrepreneurs credit business coaching with helping them navigate difficult challenges.
The company offers business coaching services to employees at all levels.

Synset Examples

Often these people have very little available time and have to fit their business coaching sessions in between important meetings and other work commitments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: executive coaching - coaching aimed at senior managers or executives
