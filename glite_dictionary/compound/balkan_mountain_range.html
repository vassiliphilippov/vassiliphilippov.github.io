Balkan Mountain Range

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæl.kən ˈmaʊn.tən reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈbæl.kən ˈmaʊn.tɪn reɪndʒ / (BrE)
a major mountain range in Bulgaria and the Balkan Peninsula, extending from the eastern border of Serbia to the Black Sea
mountain range in Bulgaria and the Balkan Peninsula

Examples

The Balkan Mountain Range forms a natural barrier between Northern and Southern Bulgaria.
During their trip, they hiked across the scenic trails of the Balkan Mountain Range.
The Balkan Mountain Range has played a significant role in the region's history and climate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Balkan Mountains - another name for the Balkan Mountain Range

Etymology

From the Turkish 'balkan', meaning 'mountain', adopted into English via geographic naming.
