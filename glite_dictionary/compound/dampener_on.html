dampener on

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæmpənər ɑn / (AmE), / ˈdæmpənə ɒn / (BrE)
an event or thing that spoils or reduces enjoyment or excitement of something
something that lessens enjoyment

Examples

The heavy traffic put a dampener on our plans to leave early.
Her criticism cast a dampener on the otherwise cheerful meeting.
Losing the first game was a dampener on the team's spirits.

Synset Examples

The rain was a real dampener on our picnic plans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spoiler - something that spoils enjoyment or outcome
  • Antonym: boost - something that increases excitement or enjoyment
