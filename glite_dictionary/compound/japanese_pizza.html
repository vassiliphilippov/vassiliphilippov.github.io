Japanese pizza

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈpænɪz ˈpiːtsə / (AmE), / dʒəˈpænɪz ˈpiːtsə / (BrE)
A style of pizza prepared with toppings and flavor influences from Japanese cuisine, such as seafood, mayonnaise, or teriyaki sauce.
pizza with Japanese-inspired toppings

Examples

For dinner, we tried a Japanese pizza with eel and seaweed as toppings.
The restaurant is famous for its Japanese pizza, which features unusual combinations like wasabi and sweet corn.
I was surprised by how much I enjoyed the spicy tuna Japanese pizza at the new fusion place.

Synset Examples

We ordered Japanese pizza topped with squid and mayonnaise.
