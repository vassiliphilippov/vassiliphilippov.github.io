genus Spizella

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs spɪˈzɛlə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs spɪˈzɛlə / (BrE)
A genus of small American sparrows including the chipping sparrow, field sparrow, and tree sparrow.
genus of small American sparrows

Examples

The genus Spizella includes the well-known chipping sparrow found across North America.
Ornithologists often study the genus Spizella to understand the evolution of American sparrows.
Several species within the genus Spizella are noted for their distinctive melodic songs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sparrow - a small, plump, brown and grey bird

Etymology

From New Latin 'Spizella', diminutive of Greek 'spiza' meaning 'finch'.
