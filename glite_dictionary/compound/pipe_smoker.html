pipe smoker

1. NOUN

/ paɪp ˈsmoʊkər / (AmE), / paɪp ˈsməʊkə / (BrE)
a person who regularly smokes tobacco using a pipe
person who smokes tobacco in a pipe

Examples

My grandfather was a dedicated pipe smoker who enjoyed relaxing with his favorite tobacco blend every evening.
At the club, a group of pipe smokers gathered to discuss their latest finds and rare pipes.
Shops specializing in tobacco products often cater specifically to pipe smokers with unique blends and accessories.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: cigarette smoker - a person who smokes cigarettes
  • Degree Higher: chain smoker - a person who smokes one cigarette after another
