cell theory

1. NOUN

/ sɛl ˈθiəri / (AmE), / sɛl ˈθɪəri / (BrE)
the scientific theory that cells are the fundamental structural and functional units of all living organisms
theory that cells are basic units of life

Examples

Many students first learn about cell theory in high school biology.
The development of cell theory was a major milestone in scientific history.
Modern research in genetics builds upon the principles of cell theory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cell - the smallest structural and functional unit of an organism
