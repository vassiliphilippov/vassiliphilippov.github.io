badminton racket

1. NOUN

/ ˈbædˌmɪntən ˈrækɪt / (AmE), / ˈbædmɪntən ˈrækɪt / (BrE)
a light, long-handled racket with a small head used to strike the shuttlecock in badminton
racket used in badminton

Examples

She bought a new badminton racket before joining the local club.
The lightweight frame of this badminton racket makes it easy to swing quickly.
He accidentally broke his badminton racket during an intense match.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racket - a sports implement with a handle and round or oval frame, used to hit a ball or shuttlecock
