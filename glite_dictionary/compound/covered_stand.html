covered stand

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌvɚd stænd / (AmE), / ˈkʌvəd stænd / (BrE)
a tiered section of seating at a sports venue, with a roof providing shelter for spectators
sheltered seating area at sports venues

Examples

We had tickets for the covered stand, so we stayed dry despite the rain.
The covered stand offers the best view of the pitch and shelter from the sun.
Fans rushed to the covered stand when the clouds turned dark.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandstand - the principal covered seating area at a sports ground or racecourse
