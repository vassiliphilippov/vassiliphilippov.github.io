No Fun City

1. NOUN

/ noʊ fʌn ˈsɪti / (AmE), / nəʊ fʌn ˈsɪti / (BrE)
an informal term for a place or situation perceived as boring, restrictive, or lacking entertainment
dull or restrictive place; lacking fun
informal

Examples

After all the bars closed early, the town earned the nickname No Fun City.
Some people call it No Fun City because of the strict regulations and lack of nightlife.
Once the festival was canceled, the city turned into No Fun City for the weekend.

Synset Examples

With all the restrictions, this place became No Fun City.
After the new laws, it felt like No Fun City.
They called it No Fun City after the curfew was imposed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boring - not interesting; dull
  • Antonym: exciting - causing great enthusiasm and eagerness
