cigar smoker

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈɡɑr ˌsmoʊkər / (AmE), / sɪˈɡɑː ˌsməʊkə / (BrE)
a person who smokes cigars regularly or occasionally
person who smokes cigars

Examples

He became a dedicated cigar smoker after retiring from his job.
The club has a lounge reserved for cigar smokers.
As a lifelong cigar smoker, he appreciated the subtle flavors and aromas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoker - a person who smokes tobacco products
