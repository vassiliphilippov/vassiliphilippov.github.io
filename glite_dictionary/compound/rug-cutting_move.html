rug-cutting move

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌɡ ˌkʌtɪŋ muːv / (AmE), / ˈrʌɡ ˌkʌtɪŋ mʊːv / (BrE)
a lively or impressive dance move, especially one performed energetically or skillfully
lively or impressive dance move
informal american

Examples

She practiced a new rug-cutting move before the school dance.
That rug-cutting move brought everyone to their feet in applause.
Jake surprised the crowd with an unexpected rug-cutting move during the competition.

Synset Examples

Her rug-cutting moves impressed everyone at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dance move - a movement or gesture performed while dancing

Etymology

From the slang idiom 'cut a rug', meaning 'to dance', originating in American English in the 20th century.
