bursting explosive

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɜrstɪŋ ɪkˈsploʊsɪv / (AmE), / ˈbɜːstɪŋ ɪkˈspləʊsɪv / (BrE)
a high explosive designed to destroy or damage a target, used especially in military shells or bombs
high explosive used to damage a target

Examples

The shell was filled with a powerful bursting explosive to maximize damage upon impact.
Modern artillery rounds often use a bursting explosive to penetrate enemy armor.
Most warheads contain some form of bursting explosive to achieve their intended effect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high explosive - an explosive material that detonates with very high velocity
