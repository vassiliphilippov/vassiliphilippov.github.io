panel truck

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænəl ˌtrʌk / (AmE), / ˈpænəl ˌtrʌk / (BrE)
a type of van with sides enclosed by panels, used for delivering goods or services to customers
enclosed delivery van

Examples

The company uses a panel truck to deliver fresh bread to local stores each morning.
A vintage panel truck was restored and featured in the classic car show.
He loaded the tools into the back of his panel truck before heading to the job site.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: van - a covered vehicle for transporting goods or people
  • Degree Lower: pickup truck - a small truck with an open back
