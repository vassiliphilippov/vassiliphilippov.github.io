numeration system

1. NOUN

/ ˌnuːməˈreɪʃən ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌnjuːməˈreɪʃən ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a system or notation used for representing numbers
system for representing numbers

Examples

The decimal numeration system is commonly used in everyday life.
Ancient civilizations developed their own numeration systems to record quantities.
The binary numeration system is fundamental in computing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: number system - a system of writing numbers; another term for numeration system

Etymology

From Latin numeratio (a numbering), from numerare (to count) + systema (system).
