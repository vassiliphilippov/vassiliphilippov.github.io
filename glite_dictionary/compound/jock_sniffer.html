jock sniffer

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑk ˈsnɪfər / (AmE), / dʒɒk ˈsnɪfə / (BrE)
(slang, derogatory) A person who excessively admires, flatters, or seeks the attention of athletes, especially in an obsequious or sycophantic way.
person who fawns over athletes (slang)
slang informal american

Examples

Nobody wanted to be called a jock sniffer in high school because it meant you had no independence.
He's such a jock sniffer that he memorizes every stat about the team and never misses a game.
The coach grew tired of jock sniffers pestering him for autographs after practice.

Synset Examples

The jock sniffer was always following the star athlete around.
He was labeled a jock sniffer for his constant praise.
Being a jock sniffer doesn't impress anyone but other fans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groupie - a fan who follows musicians or celebrities obsessively
  • Synonym: sycophant - a person who acts obsequiously toward someone important, in order to gain advantage

Etymology

From 'jock' (slang for athlete) + 'sniffer' (one who sniffs; figuratively, an overly eager admirer); originated in North American campus slang.
