bar bit

1. NOUN

/ bɑr bɪt / (AmE), / bɑː bɪt / (BrE)
A type of horse bit consisting of a solid bar of metal, used in the mouthpiece of a bridle to control the horse.
solid metal horse bit

Examples

The trainer decided to use a bar bit because the horse responded better to its steady pressure.
Unlike jointed bits, the bar bit provides uniform contact across the horse's mouth.
Some riders prefer a bar bit for horses with sensitive tongues or mouths.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bit - a metal mouthpiece for controlling a horse
