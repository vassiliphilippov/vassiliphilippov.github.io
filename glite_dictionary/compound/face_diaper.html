face diaper

1. NOUN

/ feɪs ˈdaɪpɚ / (AmE), / feɪs ˈdaɪpə / (BrE)
(informal, sometimes derogatory) A mask or covering worn over the mouth and nose, especially a surgical or cloth mask used for health protection.
mask covering mouth and nose
informal slang american

Examples

Many people refused to wear a face diaper even in crowded public spaces.
She joked that her glasses fog up every time she puts on a face diaper.
Online forums were full of debates about the need for face diapers during the pandemic.

Synset Examples

The face diaper covered his mouth and nose snugly.
Parents are advised to change the face diaper regularly.
The baby wore a face diaper while playing outside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: face mask - a protective covering worn on the face

Etymology

Modelled on 'diaper' (a cloth covering) + 'face'; popularized as a slang or mocking term for protective face masks during the COVID-19 pandemic.
