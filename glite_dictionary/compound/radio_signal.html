radio signal

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪdioʊ ˈsɪɡnəl / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊ ˈsɪɡnəl / (BrE)
A radio wave that is used for transmitting or receiving information, such as sound or data.
electromagnetic wave for communication

Examples

The technician analyzed the radio signal to determine if there was any interference.
Spacecraft use a radio signal to communicate with mission control on Earth.
She lost the radio signal as her car entered the remote mountain area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radio wave - an electromagnetic wave used for wireless broadcasting or communication
