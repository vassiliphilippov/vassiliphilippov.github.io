gray partridge

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪ ˈpɑrtrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈpɑːtrɪdʒ / (BrE)
a small, plump game bird native to Europe and Asia, with grey feathers and a distinctive orange face and throat, also known as the English partridge
a species of European game bird

Examples

The gray partridge is often spotted in open farmland across Europe.
Hunting the gray partridge has long been a tradition in certain rural communities.
The gray partridge populations have declined due to changes in agricultural practices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partridge - a medium-sized game bird with a plump body, found in Europe and Asia
