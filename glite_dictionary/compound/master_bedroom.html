master bedroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæstɚ ˌbɛdruːm / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstə ˌbɛdruːm / (BrE)
the largest or main bedroom in a house, typically intended for use by the head of the household
main or largest bedroom in a house

Examples

The master bedroom has an en-suite bathroom and a walk-in closet.
They decided to repaint the master bedroom a calming blue color.
From the balcony of the master bedroom, there is a stunning view of the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedroom - a room for sleeping in
