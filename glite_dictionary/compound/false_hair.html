false hair

1. NOUN

/ fɔls hɛr / (AmE), / fɔːls heə / (BrE)
Hair, either human or artificial, that is worn to cover the scalp or add volume for disguise or decoration.
artificial or added hair for disguise or adornment

Examples

She wore false hair to cover her thinning scalp at the party.
Many actors rely on false hair to transform their appearance for different roles.
The display included ornate combs and pieces of false hair from the eighteenth century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wig - an artificial covering of hair for the head
  • Synonym: hairpiece - a partial wig of real or synthetic hair
