Worst Korea

1. PROPN

/ wɜrst kəˈriə / (AmE), / wɜːst kəˈriːə / (BrE)
A pejorative term used to refer contemptuously to North Korea, contrasting it with South Korea ('Best Korea').
derogatory term for North Korea
offensive slang

Examples

Some internet forums use Worst Korea to mockingly refer to North Korea.
The sanctions imposed on Worst Korea have remained in place for years.
Calling it Worst Korea shows a dismissive attitude towards that nation.

Synset Examples

Many documentaries highlight the struggles in Worst Korea.
The news from Worst Korea is always alarming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: North Korea - Asian country, officially the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
  • Antonym: Best Korea - Pejorative or humorous term for South Korea

Etymology

Modeled as a jocular contrast to 'Best Korea', emerging from internet slang culture in the early 21st century.
