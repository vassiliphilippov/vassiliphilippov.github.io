tranime style

1. NOUN

/ trænˌiːm staɪl / (AmE), / trænˌiːm staɪl / (BrE)
an art style that combines elements of traditional Japanese anime and Western animation or illustration
blend of anime and Western art styles

Examples

His latest comic is illustrated using a distinctive tranime style.
Many young artists experiment with the tranime style to create unique character designs.
Fans appreciate how the tranime style blends expressive Western features with anime aesthetics.

Synset Examples

He draws characters inspired by tranime styles.

Etymology

Blend of 'traditional' and 'anime', referencing the combination of anime and Western styles.
