insect bite

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnˌsɛkt baɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪnsɛkt bʌɪt / (BrE)
a wound, often painful or itchy, caused by an insect stinging or puncturing the skin
wound from insect stinger or mouthparts

Examples

After spending the night outdoors, she woke up covered in insect bites.
This cream helps reduce the itching from a recent insect bite.
He had a severe allergic reaction to a single insect bite while hiking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sting - a wound made by an insect, animal, or plant's stinger
  • Degree Higher: itch - an uncomfortable skin sensation causing a desire to scratch; common result of insect bites
  • Degree Higher: scratch - to rub the skin, often due to irritation from insect bites
