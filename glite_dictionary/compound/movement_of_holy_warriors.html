Movement of Holy Warriors

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvmənt əv ˈhoʊli ˈwɔːriərz / (AmE), / ˈmuːvmənt əv ˈhəʊli ˈwɔːriəz / (BrE)
an Islamic militant group founded in Pakistan, originally fighting the Soviet Union in Afghanistan in the 1980s, now operating mainly in Kashmir as a terrorist organization seeking Kashmir's accession to Pakistan
Islamic militant group in Pakistan and Kashmir

Examples

The government declared the Movement of Holy Warriors to be a banned terrorist organization.
Reports indicate that the Movement of Holy Warriors was involved in recent attacks in Kashmir.
Many international agencies keep a close watch on the activities of the Movement of Holy Warriors.
