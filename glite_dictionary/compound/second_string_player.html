second string player

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd strɪŋ ˈpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈsekənd strɪŋ ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
A sports player who is not part of the starting lineup and serves as a backup for the main players.
sports backup player, not a starter

Examples

After months of hard work, he was promoted from second string player to the starting lineup.
Even as a second string player, she contributed greatly to the team's success.
The coach relies on every second string player during a long season.

Synset Examples

He was a second string player, but still gave his best.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: starter - a player who is part of the starting lineup
  • Antonym: first-string player - a player who is part of the main team lineup
  • Synonym: substitute - a player who replaces another in a sports match

Etymology

From the phrase 'second string', originally referring to the reserve string on a bow, extended to mean a backup in any context.
