line of verse

1. NOUN

/ laɪn əv vɜrs / (AmE), / laɪn əv vɜːs / (BrE)
a single line of words forming part of a poem
single line in a poem

Examples

She recited a beautiful line of verse from her favorite poem.
Each line of verse in the sonnet follows a strict metrical pattern.
He wrote a single line of verse to capture his feelings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: verse - writing arranged with a metrical rhythm, typically having rhyme
