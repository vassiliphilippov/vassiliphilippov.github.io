plum sauce

1. NOUN

/ plʌm sɔs / (AmE), / plʌm sɔːs / (BrE)
A sweet and tangy sauce made from plums, sugar, vinegar, and spices, commonly served with Chinese dishes.
Chinese sweet sauce made from plums

Examples

We always order spring rolls with plum sauce at our favorite Chinese restaurant.
The plum sauce added a sweet and tart flavor to the crispy duck.
She bought a jar of plum sauce to use as a dip for fried wontons.
