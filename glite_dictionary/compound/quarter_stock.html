quarter stock

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɔrtɚ stɑk / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə stɒk / (BrE)
A stock with a par value of $25 per share.
stock with $25 par value

Examples

The company issued a new series of quarter stock to attract smaller investors.
Investors were interested in the dividends paid by the quarter stock shares.
Many older corporations used to offer quarter stock as part of their capital structure.
