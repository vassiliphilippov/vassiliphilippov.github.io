even keel

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːvən kiːl / (AmE), / ˈiːvən kiːl / (BrE)
a calm, stable, or balanced condition or situation, especially after being unsettled
a stable, balanced condition

Examples

After the crisis, the company was finally back on an even keel.
It's important to keep your finances on an even keel during uncertain times.
Meditation helps her return to an even keel after a stressful day.

Synset Examples

After some time, her emotions returned to an even keel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stability - the state of being stable or unchanging
  • Antonym: instability - the state of being unstable

Etymology

Originally a nautical term referring to a ship being balanced along its keel.
