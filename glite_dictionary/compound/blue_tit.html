blue tit

1. NOUN

/ blu tɪt / (AmE), / bluː tɪt / (BrE)
a small European songbird with bright blue wings, tail, and crown, belonging to the tit family
a small European blue-crowned songbird

Examples

A blue tit was pecking at the bird feeder in the garden.
The vibrant colors of the blue tit make it easy to spot among the branches.
Many British gardens welcome the cheerful song of the blue tit each spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: great tit - a larger tit species with black and yellow coloring
