stud muffin

1. NOUN

/ stʌd ˈmʌf.ɪn / (AmE), / stʌd ˈmʌf.ɪn / (BrE)
An attractive and charming man; a man who is considered physically appealing and has an aura of confidence or sex appeal, often admired by others.
attractive, charming man
informal slang

Examples

All the women at the party were whispering about the new stud muffin in the office.
He walked into the room with the confidence of a true stud muffin.
After he starred in the movie, he became the ultimate Hollywood stud muffin.

Synset Examples

She called him a stud muffin for his charm.
She found him looking exceptionally stud-muffinly tonight.
Every girl wants to date that stud muffin.
He’s known as the stud muffin of our group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartthrob - a man who is very attractive and popular with women
  • Synonym: beefcake - a man with a muscular and attractive physique

Etymology

From "stud" (an attractive man) and "muffin" (used as a term of endearment or affection).
