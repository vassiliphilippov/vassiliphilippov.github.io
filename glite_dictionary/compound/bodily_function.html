bodily function

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdli ˈfʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / ˈbɒdɪli ˈfʌŋkʃən / (BrE)
a natural process that occurs in the body, such as breathing, digestion, or excretion
natural process in the body

Examples

Regular exercise can help improve nearly every bodily function.
The doctor explained how sleep is an important bodily function for good health.
Illness can sometimes interfere with basic bodily functions such as eating or breathing.

Synset Examples

respiratory activity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physiological process - a process that occurs in a living organism to maintain life
