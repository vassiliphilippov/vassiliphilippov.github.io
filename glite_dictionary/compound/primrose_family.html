primrose family

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪmˌroʊz ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈprɪmrəʊz ˈfæməli / (BrE)
A family of flowering plants (Primulaceae) with regular flowers, widely distributed in the Northern Hemisphere.
family of flowering plants (Primulaceae)

Examples

The primrose family includes many ornamental plants such as primroses and cyclamens.
Several alpine species belong to the primrose family, thriving in cool, moist environments.
She specializes in researching members of the primrose family found in European woodlands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Primulaceae - the botanical family known as the primrose family
