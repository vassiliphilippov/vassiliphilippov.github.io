gift wrapper

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪft ˈræpər / (AmE), / ɡɪft ˈræpə / (BrE)
decorative paper or material used to cover a present before it is given
decorative covering for a present

Examples

He carefully selected a gift wrapper with colorful balloons for the birthday present.
The shiny gift wrapper made the package look extra special under the tree.
After unwrapping her present, she folded the gift wrapper to reuse it later.

Synset Examples

The gift came in a beautiful wrapper that impressed everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrapping paper - paper used to wrap gifts
