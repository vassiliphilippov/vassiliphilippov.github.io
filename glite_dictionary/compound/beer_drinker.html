beer drinker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪr ˌdrɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈbɪə ˌdrɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person who frequently drinks and enjoys beer or ale, often as their preferred beverage
person who prefers beer or ale

Examples

As a proud beer drinker, he loved sampling new craft brews on weekends.
The pub was filled with beer drinkers enjoying their favorite lagers and ales.
Although she once preferred cocktails, she is now a dedicated beer drinker.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: teetotaler - a person who does not drink alcohol
