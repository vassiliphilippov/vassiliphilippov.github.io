legal age

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːɡəl eɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl eɪdʒ / (BrE)
The age at which a person is considered by law to be an adult, competent to manage their own affairs.
the age of legal adulthood

Examples

You must be at least eighteen to reach the legal age for voting in many countries.
She waited impatiently until she reached legal age to move out of her parents' house.
Drinking alcohol is prohibited for anyone who has not yet attained the legal age in this state.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: age of majority - the age at which a person is legally considered an adult
  • Antonym: minor - a person under the legal age
