twinkly-eyed smile

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɪŋkli aɪd smaɪl / (AmE), / ˈtwɪŋkli aɪd smaɪl / (BrE)
a joyful expression characterized by sparkling, lively eyes and a cheerful smile
a sparkling, joyful smile with lively eyes

Examples

Her twinkly-eyed smile made everyone in the office feel more at ease.
With a twinkly-eyed smile, the old shopkeeper handed the boy a piece of candy.
He greeted us at the door with a twinkly-eyed smile that instantly lifted our spirits.

Synset Examples

She had a twinkly-eyed smile that lit up the room.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: smile - an expression formed by curving the lips, usually to show pleasure
