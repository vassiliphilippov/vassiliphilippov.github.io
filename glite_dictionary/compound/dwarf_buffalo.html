dwarf buffalo

1. NOUN

/ ˈdwɔrf ˈbʌfəloʊ / (AmE), / ˈdwɔːf ˈbʌfələʊ / (BrE)
a small buffalo native to Sulawesi (Celebes), Indonesia, with straight, short horns, also known as the anoa
small buffalo from Sulawesi (anoa)

Examples

The dwarf buffalo is a rare species found only on Sulawesi Island.
Unlike larger relatives, the dwarf buffalo has delicate, straight horns and a smaller frame.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the endangered dwarf buffalo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anoa - small, straight-horned wild buffalo of Sulawesi
