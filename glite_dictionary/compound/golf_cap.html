golf cap

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf kæp / (AmE), / ɡɒlf kap / (BrE)
A cap with a projecting bill, typically worn when playing golf.
cap with a bill for golf

Examples

He wore his favorite golf cap to shield his eyes from the sun on the course.
The shop sells a variety of golf caps in different colors and styles.
After the tournament, he proudly displayed the signed golf cap in his office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baseball cap - a cap with a rounded crown and a stiff bill, commonly worn in baseball
