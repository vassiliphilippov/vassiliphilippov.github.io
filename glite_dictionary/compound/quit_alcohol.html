to quit alcohol

1. VERB

/ kwɪt ˈælkəˌhɔl / (AmE), / kwɪt ˈælkəhɒl / (BrE)
to stop drinking alcoholic beverages, either temporarily or permanently
to stop drinking alcoholic drinks

Examples

After his doctor's warning, he decided to quit alcohol completely.
Many people choose to quit alcohol to improve their overall well-being.
She found it challenging to quit alcohol, but her family supported her throughout the process.

Synset Examples

He decided to quit alcohol for a healthier lifestyle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: give up drinking - stop drinking alcohol
  • Synonym: to abstain - choose not to do or have something, especially alcohol
