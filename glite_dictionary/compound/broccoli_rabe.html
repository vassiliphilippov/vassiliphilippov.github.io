broccoli rabe

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɑːkəli ˈrɑːb / (AmE), / ˈbrɒkəli ˈrɑːb / (BrE)
A green vegetable with slightly bitter dark green leaves and clusters of small flower buds, related to the turnip and often used in Italian cooking.
bitter green vegetable, turnip family

Examples

She sautéed broccoli rabe with garlic and olive oil for a side dish.
The recipe calls for fresh broccoli rabe to add a slightly bitter flavor.
I bought a bunch of broccoli rabe at the farmer's market to try in pasta.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rapini - another name for broccoli rabe

Etymology

From Italian 'rapa' (turnip), influenced by 'broccoli'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbrɑkəli rɑb / (AmE), / ˈbrɒkəli rɑːb / (BrE)
a vegetable with bitter, pungent, edible leafy shoots and small buds, used in cooking
bitter leafy green vegetable

Examples

The chef sautéed broccoli rabe with garlic and olive oil for a side dish.
You can substitute spinach for broccoli rabe if you prefer a milder flavor.
She added chopped broccoli rabe to the pasta for extra nutrition and taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rapini - another name for broccoli rabe

Etymology

From Italian 'broccoletti di rapa' or 'rapini', referring to turnip greens.
