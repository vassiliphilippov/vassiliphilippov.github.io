delivery van

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈlɪvəri væn / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvəri van / (BrE)
a van designed for transporting goods or providing services to customers
van for delivering goods or services

Examples

The bakery uses a delivery van to bring fresh bread to local stores each morning.
He loaded the packages into the delivery van before starting his route around the city.
A new delivery van arrived at the warehouse, ready to transport electronics to customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: van - a medium-sized road vehicle, typically box-shaped, for transporting goods or people
