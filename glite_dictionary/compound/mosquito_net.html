mosquito net

1. NOUN

/ məˈskitoʊ nɛt / (AmE), / məˈskiːtəʊ nɛt / (BrE)
A fine mesh net, usually placed around beds or sleeping areas, used to protect people from mosquito bites.
protective mesh against mosquitos

Examples

We hung a mosquito net over the bed to keep insects away while we slept.
Travelers are advised to use a mosquito net in areas where malaria is common.
He repaired a small tear in the mosquito net before bedtime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bed net - a net suspended over a bed to protect against insects

Etymology

From Spanish *mosquito* (little fly) + English *net*.
