strawberry geranium

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔːˌbɛri dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / ˈstrɒːbəri dʒəˈreɪniəm / (BrE)
an eastern Asiatic plant (Saxifraga stolonifera) with creeping stolons and racemes of small red-and-white flowers, often grown as a houseplant or ground cover
a creeping Asiatic plant with red-and-white flowers

Examples

The strawberry geranium is popular for its delicate foliage and trailing habit.
Gardeners often use strawberry geranium as a ground cover in shaded areas.
She placed a potted strawberry geranium on her windowsill to brighten up the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Saxifraga - a genus of plants also called saxifrages
