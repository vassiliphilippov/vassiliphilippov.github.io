six-shooter

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪkˌʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈsɪksˌʃuːtə / (BrE)
a revolver, especially one with a cylinder that holds six cartridges
revolver with six chambers

Examples

The sheriff drew his six-shooter as the outlaws approached the saloon.
He inherited a vintage six-shooter from his grandfather, a relic of the Old West.
The cowboy spun his six-shooter around his finger before holstering it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revolver - a handgun with a revolving cylinder

Etymology

From 'six' (number of chambers) + 'shooter' (one that shoots).
