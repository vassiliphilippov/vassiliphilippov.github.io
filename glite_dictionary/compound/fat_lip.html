fat lip

1. NOUN

/ fæt lɪp / (AmE), / fæt lɪp / (BrE)
a swollen lip, typically as a result of a blow or injury
swollen lip from injury

Examples

He walked into the room sporting a fat lip after his skateboarding accident.
She tried to hide her fat lip with makeup, but it was still visible.
After the argument turned physical, he ended up with a fat lip and a bruised ego.

Synset Examples

The boxer ended the match with a fat lip.
He got a fat lip during the rough game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swollen lip - lip that has increased in size due to injury or reaction
