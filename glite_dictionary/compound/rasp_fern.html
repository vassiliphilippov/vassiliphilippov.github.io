rasp fern

1. NOUN

/ ræsp fɜrn / (AmE), / rɑːsp fɜːn / (BrE)
Any fern of the genus Doodia, characterized by pinnate fronds with sharply toothed leaflets.
a fern of the genus Doodia with toothed fronds

Examples

The damp forest floor was dotted with rasp ferns alongside mosses and small shrubs.
Botanists identified the rare species as a rasp fern native to Australia.
The sharply toothed leaves of the rasp fern distinguish it from other ferns in the region.
