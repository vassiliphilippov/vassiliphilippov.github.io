Hub of the Universe

1. NOUN

/ hʌb əv ði juˈnɪvɚs / (AmE), / hʌb əv ði juˈnɪvəs / (BrE)
A nickname for Boston, referring to its status as a major cultural, financial, and historical center.
nickname for Boston, Massachusetts

Examples

Many people travel to the Hub of the Universe to experience its rich history and culture.
After graduating, he moved to the Hub of the Universe for better job opportunities.
The Hub of the Universe offers world-class museums and universities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Boston - the capital and largest city of Massachusetts

Etymology

Originates from Oliver Wendell Holmes in the 19th century, who described Boston as the 'Hub of the Solar System,' later popularized as 'Hub of the Universe'.
