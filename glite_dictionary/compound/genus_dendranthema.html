genus Dendranthema

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛn.drænˌθi.mə / (AmE), / ˈdɛn.drænˌθiː.mə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae, often included in the genus Chrysanthemum, comprising species that are important as florist's chrysanthemums.
a genus of flowering plants, including florist's chrysanthemums

Examples

The genus Dendranthema includes many varieties prized by florists for their colorful blooms.
Recent taxonomic changes placed several cultivated chrysanthemums under the genus Dendranthema.
Botanists debate whether certain species belong to the genus Dendranthema or should remain in Chrysanthemum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genus Chrysanthemum - a closely related or combined genus including traditional chrysanthemums
  • Synonym: chrysanthemum - a popular flowering plant cultivated for bouquets and gardens

Etymology

From Greek dendron (tree) + anthemon (flower), referring to the appearance of the plants.
