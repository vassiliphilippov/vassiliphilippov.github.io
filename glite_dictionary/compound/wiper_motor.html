wiper motor

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪpɚ ˌmoʊtɚ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪpə ˌməʊtə / (BrE)
an electric motor that drives the windshield wipers of a vehicle
electric motor for windshield wipers

Examples

The wiper motor stopped working during the storm, making it hard to see through the windshield.
If your wiper motor is making a strange noise, it might be time for a replacement.
Replacing a faulty wiper motor can usually be done with some basic tools and a little patience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: windshield wiper - a device used to clear rain or debris from a vehicle's windshield
