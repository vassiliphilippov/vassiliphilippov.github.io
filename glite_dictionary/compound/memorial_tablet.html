memorial tablet

1. NOUN

/ məˈmɔriəl ˈtæblət / (AmE), / mɪˈmɔːriəl ˈtæblət / (BrE)
a flat plaque, often made of brass or stone, inscribed to commemorate a person or event
commemorative plaque, often brass

Examples

The church displayed a memorial tablet in honor of those who served in the war.
A bronze memorial tablet was affixed to the wall below the stained glass window.
During the ceremony, the mayor unveiled a new memorial tablet bearing victims' names.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plaque - a flat plate or tablet, often inscribed, used as a memorial or marker
  • Synonym: monument - a structure erected to commemorate persons or events
