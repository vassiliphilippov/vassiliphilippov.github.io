Master of Education

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæstər əv ˌɛdʒuˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɑːstə əv ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
A postgraduate academic degree awarded for studies in the field of education.
a master's degree in education

Examples

She earned her Master of Education degree before starting her teaching career.
Applicants with a Master of Education are preferred for administrative positions.
He completed his Master of Education at a top-ranked university.

Related Terms
