ale drinker

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪl ˌdrɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈeɪl ˌdrɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person who habitually drinks or prefers ale or beer
person who prefers ale or beer

Examples

As a proud ale drinker, he enjoys trying new craft brews at local pubs.
The club was full of ale drinkers celebrating the brewery’s anniversary.
Being an ale drinker, she prefers traditional English pubs over trendy cocktail bars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beer drinker - a person who drinks or prefers beer
