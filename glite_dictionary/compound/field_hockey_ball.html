field hockey ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːld ˌhɑːki bɔl / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌhɒki bɔːl / (BrE)
A hard, small ball used for playing the sport of field hockey.
ball used in field hockey

Examples

She bought a new field hockey ball before the tournament started.
The coach collected all the field hockey balls after practice.
During the match, the striker hit the field hockey ball straight into the goal.
