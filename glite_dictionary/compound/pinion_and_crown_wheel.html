pinion and crown wheel

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪnjən ənd kraʊn wiːl / (AmE), / ˈpɪnjən ənd kraʊn wiːl / (BrE)
a system of gears that mesh at an angle, typically used to transfer rotational motion at right angles; especially in the differential of a vehicle
angled gears, esp. in vehicle differentials

Examples

The pinion and crown wheel are essential components in a car's differential system.
Proper lubrication of the pinion and crown wheel extends the life of the gears.
He inspected the pinion and crown wheel for wear after hearing a grinding noise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bevel gear - gear cut at an angle, used to transfer motion between intersecting axes
