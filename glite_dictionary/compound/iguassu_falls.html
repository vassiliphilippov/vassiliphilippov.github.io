Iguassu Falls

1. PROPN

/ ɪˌɡwɑːsu ˈfɔlz / (AmE), / ɪˌɡwæsjuː ˈfɔːlz / (BrE)
a large waterfall system located on the border between Argentina and Brazil, known for its impressive size and scenic beauty
large waterfall on Argentina-Brazil border

Examples

Many tourists visit Iguassu Falls each year to witness its breathtaking beauty.
The roar of Iguassu Falls can be heard from miles away during the rainy season.
Our guided tour included a boat trip to the base of Iguassu Falls for a closer look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Niagara Falls - large waterfalls on the border of the United States and Canada

Etymology

From the Guarani word 'Yguasu' meaning 'big water'.
