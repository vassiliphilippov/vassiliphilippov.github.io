laboratory coat

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæbrəˌtɔri koʊt / (AmE), / ləˈbɒrətəri kəʊt / (BrE)
a light, protective garment worn over regular clothing to shield it from substances while working in a laboratory
protective coat for laboratory use

Examples

She put on her laboratory coat before starting the chemistry experiment.
The laboratory coat is required attire when handling chemicals in the lab.
His white laboratory coat was stained from months of scientific work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lab coat - shortened form of laboratory coat
