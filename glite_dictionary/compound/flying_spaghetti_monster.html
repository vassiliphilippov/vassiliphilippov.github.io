Flying Spaghetti Monster

1. PROPN

/ ˈflaɪɪŋ spəˈɡɛti ˈmɑnstər / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ spəˈɡɛti ˈmɒnstə / (BrE)
a satirical deity created to mock certain arguments for the existence of God, representing the central figure of the Church of the Flying Spaghetti Monster, often used as a parody of religion.
satirical deity in a parody religion

Examples

Many people reference the Flying Spaghetti Monster in debates about science and religion.
Her T-shirt with the Flying Spaghetti Monster logo sparked conversations at the campus event.
Some atheists use the Flying Spaghetti Monster as a symbol of secularism.

Synset Examples

He jokingly referred to his lunch as the Flying Spaghetti Monster.
She wore a shirt featuring the Flying Spaghetti Monster at the protest.
The debate about the Flying Spaghetti Monster continues in the classroom.

Etymology

Coined in 2005 by Bobby Henderson as a satirical entity in an open letter about teaching intelligent design.
