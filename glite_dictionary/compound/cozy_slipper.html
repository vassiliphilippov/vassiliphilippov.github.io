cozy slipper

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊzi ˈslɪpər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊzi ˈslɪpə / (BrE)
A warm and comfortable indoor shoe, typically made of soft materials and worn for relaxation at home.
warm, comfortable indoor footwear

Examples

After a long day, she slipped her feet into her cozy slippers and sank into the couch.
He received a pair of cozy slippers as a birthday gift from his grandmother.
During the cold evenings, nothing is more comforting than wearing your favorite cozy slippers.

Synset Examples

She bought a new pair of cozy slippers for winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slipper - a light, comfortable shoe for indoor wear
  • Synonym: house shoe - another term for indoor footwear
