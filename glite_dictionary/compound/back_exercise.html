back exercise

1. NOUN

/ bæk ˈɛksərˌsaɪz / (AmE), / bæk ˈɛksəsaɪz / (BrE)
a physical exercise intended to strengthen or improve the muscles of the back
strengthening exercise for the back

Examples

The physiotherapist recommended a daily back exercise routine to help relieve her pain.
He demonstrated a simple back exercise to strengthen the lower muscles.
Many people perform at least one back exercise during their gym sessions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stretching - exercises to improve flexibility of muscles
  • Synonym: core exercise - exercise targeting the muscles of the trunk
