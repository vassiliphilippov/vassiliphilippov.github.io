cargo deck

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrɡoʊ dɛk / (AmE), / ˈkɑːɡəʊ dɛk / (BrE)
the area or floor in a ship or aircraft designated for storing cargo
area for storing cargo on ship or aircraft

Examples

Heavy machinery was carefully loaded onto the cargo deck before departure.
The cargo deck of the airplane was filled with medical supplies for the relief effort.
Workers inspected the cargo deck to ensure all containers were properly secured.
