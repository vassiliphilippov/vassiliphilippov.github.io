Walbiri language

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑːlˌbiːri ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːlˌbɪəri ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an Australian Aboriginal language spoken in the Northern Territory of Australia
Aboriginal language of Northern Territory, Australia

Examples

Linguists have extensively studied the Walbiri language for its unique structure and vocabulary.
Efforts are being made to preserve and teach the Walbiri language to younger generations.
