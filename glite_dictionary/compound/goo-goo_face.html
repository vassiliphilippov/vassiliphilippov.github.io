goo-goo face

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuː ˌɡuː feɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡuː ˌɡuː feɪs / (BrE)
a silly or exaggeratedly affectionate facial expression, especially one made to amuse a baby or show affection
silly or affectionate facial expression
informal

Examples

She made a goo-goo face at the baby to make him laugh.
He couldn't resist pulling a goo-goo face when he saw the puppy.
Whenever she talks to her cat, she puts on that ridiculous goo-goo face.

Synset Examples

He couldn't help but make goo-goo faces at the kitten.

Etymology

Reduplication of 'goo', imitative of baby talk, plus 'face'.
