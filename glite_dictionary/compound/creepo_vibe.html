creepo vibe

1. NOUN

/ ˈkripoʊ vaɪb / (AmE), / ˈkriːpəʊ vaɪb / (BrE)
an unsettling or disturbing atmosphere or feeling, often caused by someone or something that seems creepy or suspicious
an unsettling or creepy atmosphere
informal slang

Examples

She said the abandoned house had a serious creepo vibe after dark.
I left the bar early because it just had a weird creepo vibe that night.
That message gave me a creepo vibe, so I decided not to respond.

Synset Examples

That guy gives off a real creepo vibe at parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creepy - having a disturbing or frightening feeling
  • Synonym: unsettling - causing anxiety or uneasiness

Etymology

Formed from 'creepo' (informal for 'creep' or a creepy person) and 'vibe' (atmosphere or feeling).
