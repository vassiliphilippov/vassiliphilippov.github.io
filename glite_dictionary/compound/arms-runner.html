arms-runner

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmz ˌrʌnər / (AmE), / ˈɑːmz ˌrʌnə / (BrE)
a person who illegally transports or smuggles firearms, typically across borders
person who smuggles guns

Examples

The police arrested the notorious arms-runner at the border with a truck full of illegal weapons.
Stories about the daring exploits of an arms-runner have inspired many movies and books.
Authorities are cracking down on arms-runners operating in the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arms smuggler - someone who illegally imports or exports weapons

Etymology

From 'arms' (weapons) and 'runner' (someone who transports goods illicitly).
