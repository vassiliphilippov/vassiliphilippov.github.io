loose end

1. NOUN

/ lus ɛnd / (AmE), / luːs end / (BrE)
an unfinished detail, task, or part of a project that remains unresolved
unfinished detail or task

Examples

Before we launch the project, let's make sure there isn't a single loose end left.
She spent the afternoon dealing with every loose end before her vacation started.
There always seems to be one loose end that needs tying up after a big move.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unfinished business - matters that have not yet been completed
  • Antonym: to wrap up - to finish something completely
