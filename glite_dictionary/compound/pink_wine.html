pink wine

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋk waɪn / (AmE), / pɪŋk waɪn / (BrE)
A type of wine with a pale pink color, made from red grapes whose skins are removed early during fermentation.
wine with a pale pink color

Examples

She prefers pink wine with seafood dishes in the summer.
A glass of chilled pink wine sounded perfect for the warm evening.
Many vineyards now offer a selection of pink wine alongside reds and whites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rosé - wine made from red grapes with limited skin contact, resulting in a pink color
