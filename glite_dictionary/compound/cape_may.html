Cape May

1. PROPN

/ keɪp ˈmeɪ / (AmE), / keɪp ˈmeɪ / (BrE)
a cape at the southern tip of New Jersey, extending into the Atlantic Ocean
cape in southern New Jersey

Examples

Many tourists visit Cape May each summer for its beautiful beaches and historic charm.
The ferry from Delaware docks near Cape May in New Jersey.
Birdwatchers come to Cape May to observe the spectacular migration each autumn.

Etymology

Named after Dutch explorer Cornelis Jacobsen Mey.
