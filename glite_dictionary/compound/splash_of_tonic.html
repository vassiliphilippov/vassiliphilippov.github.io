splash of tonic

1. NOUN

/ splæʃ əv ˈtɑː.nɪk / (AmE), / splæʃ əv ˈtɒn.ɪk / (BrE)
a small quantity of tonic water added to a drink, typically to give it flavor or dilution
small amount of tonic water added to a drink

Examples

He ordered a gin with a splash of tonic before dinner.
Add a splash of tonic to lighten the taste of the cocktail.
The bartender finished the drink with just a splash of tonic.

Synset Examples

A splash of tonic makes the drink refreshing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dash of bitters - a small quantity of bitters added to a drink
