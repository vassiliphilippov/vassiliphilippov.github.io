plate-and-frame filter press

1. NOUN

/ pleɪt ənd freɪm ˈfɪltər prɛs / (AmE), / pleɪt ənd freɪm ˈfɪltə prɛs / (BrE)
a type of filter press consisting of a series of alternating plates and frames, used for separating solids from liquids in industrial processes
industrial filter device with plates and frames

Examples

The factory upgraded its system by installing a modern plate-and-frame filter press to improve filtration efficiency.
In lab-scale experiments, a plate-and-frame filter press separates solid residues from the liquid stream precisely.
Routine maintenance is crucial for any plate-and-frame filter press to prevent clogging and ensure optimal operation.

Synset Examples

Plate filter press is extensively used in sugaring operations such as the production of maple syrup in Canada, since it offers very high efficiency and reliability.
