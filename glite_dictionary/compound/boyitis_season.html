boyitis season

1. NOUN

/ bɔɪˈaɪtɪs ˈsiːzən / (AmE), / bɔɪˈaɪtɪs ˈsiːzən / (BrE)
A humorous term for a period when someone is noticeably distracted by boys, often to the detriment of other activities.
humorous term for being distracted by boys
informal slang

Every spring, it feels like boyitis season in our class, and no one pays attention to the lectures.
