Gobi Desert

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡoʊ.bi ˈdɛz.ɚt / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ.bi ˈdɛz.ət / (BrE)
a large desert region in northern China and southern Mongolia, known for its harsh climate and sparse vegetation
desert in northern China and Mongolia

Examples

The Gobi Desert is famous for its unique landscapes and paleontological discoveries.
A strong wind swept across the vast Gobi Desert, carrying clouds of sand for miles.
Wild Bactrian camels are among the few animals adapted to survive in the Gobi Desert.

Etymology

From Mongolian gobi (desert, semidesert).
