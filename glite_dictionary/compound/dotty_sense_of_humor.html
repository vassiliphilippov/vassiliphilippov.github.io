dotty sense of humor

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑti sɛns əv ˈhjumɚ / (AmE), / ˈdɒti sɛns əv ˈhjuːmə / (BrE)
A quirky or eccentric sense of humor, often marked by unconventional or whimsical jokes.
eccentric or quirky humor

Examples

She has a dotty sense of humor that always makes everyone laugh.
His dotty sense of humor often surprises new acquaintances at parties.
People with a dotty sense of humor tend to see the world in delightfully unexpected ways.

Synset Examples

She has a dotty sense of humor that always makes me laugh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quirky sense of humor - an unusual or unconventional sense of humor
  • Antonym: dry sense of humor - a subtle, understated sense of humor

Etymology

From 'dotty' (eccentric, odd), itself from 'dot', meaning a minor mental lapse, British slang in the early 20th century.
