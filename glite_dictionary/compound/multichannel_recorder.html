multichannel recorder

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌltiˌtʃænəl rɪˈkɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈmʌltɪˌtʃænəl rɪˈkɔːdə / (BrE)
A recording device capable of capturing and storing two or more separate channels of audio or signals at the same time.
device for recording multiple channels simultaneously

Examples

The studio upgraded to a new multichannel recorder for higher-quality audio production.
A multichannel recorder allows sound engineers to capture each instrument separately during a live performance.
We analyzed the electrical signals using a multichannel recorder to track brain activity in different regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recorder - a device for recording sound, images, or data
