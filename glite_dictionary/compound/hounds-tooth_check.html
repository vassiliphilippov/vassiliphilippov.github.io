hound's-tooth check

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊndz tuːθ tʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈhaʊndz tuːθ tʃɛk / (BrE)
a textile pattern of small broken or jagged checks, typically in black and white
jagged checked fabric pattern

Examples

She wore a stylish blazer featuring a classic hound's-tooth check.
The designer's new collection highlighted hound's-tooth check in vibrant colors.
Traditionally, hound's-tooth check is woven in black and white, but modern versions use many color combinations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: houndstooth - textile pattern of broken checks

Etymology

Named for its resemblance to a dog's (hound's) tooth; 'check' refers to the checked pattern.
