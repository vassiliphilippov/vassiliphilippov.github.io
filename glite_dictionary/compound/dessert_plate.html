dessert plate

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈzɝt pleɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːt pleɪt / (BrE)
a small plate designed for serving dessert
small plate for dessert

Examples

She placed a slice of cake onto the dessert plate before passing it around the table.
Each guest was given a fresh dessert plate for the chocolate mousse course.
The waiter cleared the empty dessert plates as the coffee was served.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plate - flat dish from which food is eaten or served
