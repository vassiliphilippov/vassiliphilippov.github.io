consulting firm

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsʌltɪŋ fɝːm / (AmE), / kənˈsʌltɪŋ fɜːm / (BrE)
a company of experts that provides professional advice to organizations for a fee
company providing professional advice

Examples

After hiring a consulting firm, the company improved its management structure significantly.
Many businesses rely on a consulting firm for strategic planning and market research.
The consulting firm was praised for its innovative solutions to complex problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consultancy - the professional practice of giving expert advice
