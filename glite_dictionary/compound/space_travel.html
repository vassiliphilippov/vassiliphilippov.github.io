space travel

1. NOUN

/ speɪs ˈtrævəl / (AmE), / speɪs ˈtrævəl / (BrE)
the act or activity of traveling beyond the Earth's atmosphere, usually by spacecraft
voyage beyond Earth's atmosphere

Examples

Advances in technology have made space travel more accessible than ever before.
The movie explores the challenges of space travel to distant planets.
Many children dream of becoming astronauts and experiencing space travel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spaceflight - the flight of a spacecraft in outer space
