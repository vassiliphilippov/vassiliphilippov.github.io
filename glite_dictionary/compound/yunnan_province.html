Yunnan province

1. PROPN

/ juˈnɑn ˈprɑvɪns / (AmE), / juːˈnæn ˈprɒvɪns / (BrE)
a province in southern China, known for its diverse culture, rich biodiversity, and scenic landscapes, with Kunming as its capital
province in southern China

Examples

Many tourists visit Yunnan province to explore its unique ethnic cultures and beautiful scenery.
The tea produced in Yunnan province is famous around the world.
