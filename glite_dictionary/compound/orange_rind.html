orange rind

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːrɪndʒ raɪnd / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ raɪnd / (BrE)
the outer skin or peel of an orange, often used as flavoring or garnish in cooking
outer skin or peel of an orange

Examples

The recipe calls for a teaspoon of grated orange rind to add extra flavor.
She decorated the cake with strips of candied orange rind.
You can use dried orange rind to make a fragrant potpourri.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orange peel - the skin of an orange, especially when used in cooking
