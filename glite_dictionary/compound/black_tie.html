black tie

1. NOUN

/ blæk taɪ / (AmE), / blæk taɪ / (BrE)
A semiformal evening dress code for men, typically involving a black dinner jacket and a black bow tie.
semiformal evening dress code for men

Examples

The charity gala required guests to wear black tie attire.
He rented a tuxedo for the black tie event last night.
Invitations marked 'black tie' indicate a semiformal dress code.

/ blæk taɪ / (AmE), / blæk taɪ / (BrE)

formal dress code; black bow tie with dinner jacket

Examples
