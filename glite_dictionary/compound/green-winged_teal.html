green-winged teal

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡrin ˈwɪŋd ˈtil / (AmE), / ˌɡriːn ˈwɪŋd ˈtiːl / (BrE)
a small species of duck, found in Eurasia and North America, notable for the iridescent green patch on its wings
a small duck with a green wing patch

Examples

We spotted a flock of green-winged teal feeding near the riverbank at dusk.
The green-winged teal is easily identified by the bright green patch on its wings.
During migration season, many birdwatchers hope to glimpse a green-winged teal in the wetlands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teal - a small freshwater duck
