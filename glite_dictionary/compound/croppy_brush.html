croppy brush

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɑpi brʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈkrɒpi brʌʃ / (BrE)
A brush with short bristles, used for detail work in painting or similar activities.
brush with short bristles for detail work

Examples

The artist used a croppy brush to paint the intricate patterns on the vase.
A croppy brush is perfect for adding highlights and small accents to your work.
She always keeps a croppy brush in her toolkit for precise touch-ups.

Synset Examples

The artist used a croppy brush for fine details.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detail brush - a brush used for small or intricate painting work
