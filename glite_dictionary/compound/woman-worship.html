woman-worship

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊmən ˌwɜrʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈwʊmən ˌwɜːʃɪp / (BrE)
the act or practice of showing reverence or adoration specifically towards women
reverence or adoration of women

Examples

His poem reflected a deep sense of woman-worship that resonated with many readers.
Some critics accused the movement of promoting woman-worship rather than genuine equality.
Her book explored the origins and social implications of woman-worship in different cultures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idolatry - the worship of idols or excessive devotion to something
