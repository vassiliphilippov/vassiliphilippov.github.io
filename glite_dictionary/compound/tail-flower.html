tail-flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪlˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈteɪlˌflaʊə / (BrE)
any of several tropical American plants, especially Anthurium species, cultivated for their ornamental foliage and distinctive flower spikes
tropical ornamental plant (Anthurium)

Examples

The tail-flower is prized in tropical gardens for its vivid red spathes.
She arranged a bouquet featuring a striking tail-flower at its center.
Many hotels in the region decorate their lobbies with tail-flowers for their exotic appearance.
