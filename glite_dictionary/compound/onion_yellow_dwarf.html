onion yellow dwarf

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌnjən ˈjɛloʊ ˈdɜrf / (AmE), / ˈʌnjən ˈjɛləʊ ˈdɜːf / (BrE)
A viral disease of onion plants characterized by yellowing and stunting of the leaves.
a viral disease affecting onions

Examples

Farmers found that onion yellow dwarf was reducing their crop yields significantly this season.
Certain methods can help protect onions from contracting onion yellow dwarf.
Research is ongoing to develop onion varieties resistant to onion yellow dwarf.
