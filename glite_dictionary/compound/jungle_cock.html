jungle cock

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌŋɡəl kɑk / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋɡl kɒk / (BrE)
the male jungle fowl, a wild bird species closely related to the domestic chicken
male jungle fowl

Examples

The feathers of the jungle cock are prized by traditional fly fishers for making lures.
A brightly colored jungle cock strutted through the undergrowth, searching for food.
Wild populations of jungle cock are found mainly in the forests of South and Southeast Asia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jungle fowl - a wild bird species native to Asia, ancestor of the domestic chicken
