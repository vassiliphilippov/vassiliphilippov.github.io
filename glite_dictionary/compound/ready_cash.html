ready cash

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdi kæʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdi kæʃ / (BrE)
money in the form of cash that is immediately available for use
money immediately available in cash form

Examples

He didn't have enough ready cash to pay for the repairs immediately.
Small businesses often struggle with a lack of ready cash for unexpected expenses.
She likes to keep some ready cash at home in case of emergencies.

Synset Examples

his wife was always a good source of ready cash
he paid cold cash for the TV set

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hard cash - money in coins or notes, not checks or credit
  • Synonym: cold cash - money that is immediately available in coins or notes
