pièce de résistance

1. NOUN

/ pjɛs də rɪˈzistɑ̃s / (AmE), / pjɛs də reˈziːstɑ̃s / (BrE)
the most important or remarkable dish in a meal, usually presented as the main attraction
the main or featured dish of a meal

Examples

The chef prepared a pièce de résistance of roast duck with cherry sauce for the dinner guests.
Everyone complimented the pièce de résistance at the banquet, which was an impressive seafood paella.
For the holiday meal, the pièce de résistance was a perfectly glazed ham in the center of the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: main course - the main dish of a meal

Etymology

French, literally ‘piece of resistance’ (the highlight or main part of something).
