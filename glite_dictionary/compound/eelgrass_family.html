eelgrass family

1. NOUN

/ ˈilˌɡræs ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈiːlˌɡrɑːs ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of aquatic flowering plants, especially seagrasses, in some classifications essentially equivalent to Potamogetonaceae
a family of aquatic flowering plants

Examples

Scientists conducted a survey of the eelgrass family in the coastal wetlands.
Members of the eelgrass family provide important habitats for marine animals.
Some taxonomists include pondweeds in the eelgrass family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Potamogetonaceae - the botanical family of pondweeds and related aquatic plants
