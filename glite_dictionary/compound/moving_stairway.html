moving stairway

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvɪŋ ˈstɛrˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈmuːvɪŋ ˈsteəweɪ / (BrE)
a set of stairs in which the steps move continuously on a circulating belt, used for carrying people between floors of a building
escalator; stairs with moving steps

Examples

The moving stairway carried shoppers effortlessly to the next level of the mall.
Please stand on the right side of the moving stairway to allow others to pass.
When the moving stairway stopped unexpectedly, passengers had to walk the rest of the way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: escalator - a moving staircase for carrying people between floors
