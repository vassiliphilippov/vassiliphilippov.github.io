smoked mackerel

1. NOUN

/ smoʊkt ˈmæk.ə.rəl / (AmE), / sməʊkt ˈmæk.ər.əl / (BrE)
Mackerel that has been preserved and flavored by smoking.
mackerel preserved by smoking

Examples

We served smoked mackerel on toast as an appetizer.
The rich flavor of smoked mackerel pairs well with a crisp white wine.
He bought fresh smoked mackerel from the market for dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoked salmon - salmon that has been cured and flavored by smoking
