powder keg

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊdɚ kɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə kɛɡ / (BrE)
a situation or place marked by the possibility of sudden violence or trouble; a potentially explosive state
tense or explosive situation

Examples

With growing political unrest, the country was seen as a powder keg ready to explode.
The city became a powder keg after weeks of demonstrations and mounting tension.
Their heated argument turned the atmosphere at work into a powder keg.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tinderbox - a situation that is highly volatile or likely to erupt in conflict

Etymology

Originally referred to a barrel of gunpowder, later used figuratively for dangerous situations.

2. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊdər kɛg / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə kɛg / (BrE)
A strong container, typically made of metal, used to store gunpowder or blasting powder.
container for gunpowder or blasting powder

Examples

The old fort still had a powder keg stored in its cellar.
Accidentally dropping a lantern near the powder keg could have caused a huge explosion.
Each miner carried a small powder keg to the blasting site.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keg - a small barrel or container, especially for liquids

3. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊdər kɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə kɛɡ / (BrE)
a situation or place that is dangerously volatile and likely to erupt into conflict or chaos
volatile situation likely to erupt

Examples

The unresolved conflict made the city a true powder keg over the summer.
Years of economic hardship have turned the country into a powder keg.
If no compromise is reached soon, the boardroom could become a powder keg of anger and frustration.

Synset Examples

Ignoring the issues will only lead to a powder keg.
The region is a powder keg ready to explode.
The political tensions created a powder keg situation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flashpoint - a critical moment or place where violence or conflict might suddenly break out
  • Antonym: calm - free from disturbance or violent activity

Etymology

Originally referred to a barrel of gunpowder, now used metaphorically for tense situations.
