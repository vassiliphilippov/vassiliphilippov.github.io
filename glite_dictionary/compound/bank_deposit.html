bank deposit

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæŋk dɪˌpɑzɪt / (AmE), / ˈbaŋk dɪˌpɒzɪt / (BrE)
money placed in a bank or similar financial institution for safekeeping or future withdrawal
money kept in a bank

Examples

She made a bank deposit every month to save for her vacation.
The bank deposit was large enough to cover the down payment on the car.
It's important to keep your bank deposit slip as proof of your transaction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deposit - a sum of money placed in a bank or similar institution
