steel wool

1. NOUN

/ stil wʊl / (AmE), / stiːl wʊl / (BrE)
a mass of fine, flexible steel filaments twisted or matted together, used mainly as an abrasive for cleaning, polishing, or smoothing surfaces
fine steel filaments used as an abrasive

Examples

I used steel wool to scrub the rust off the metal pipes.
The craftsman polished the wooden surface with fine steel wool to make it smooth.
Remember to wear gloves when handling steel wool to avoid cuts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abrasive - a substance used for grinding, polishing, or cleaning a hard surface
