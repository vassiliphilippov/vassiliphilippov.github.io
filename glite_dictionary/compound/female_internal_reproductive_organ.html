female internal reproductive organ

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːmeɪl ɪnˈtɜrnəl rɪˌproʊˈdʌktɪv ˈɔːrɡən / (AmE), / ˈfiːmeɪl ɪnˈtɜːnəl rɪˌprəʊˈdʌktɪv ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
an internal organ in the female body involved in reproduction, such as the uterus, ovaries, or fallopian tubes
internal female reproductive organ
medical

Examples

The ovaries are an example of a female internal reproductive organ.
Damage to a female internal reproductive organ can affect fertility.
Surgery may be required if a female internal reproductive organ is affected by disease.
