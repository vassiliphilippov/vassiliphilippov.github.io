US Government Printing Office

1. PROPN

/ ˌjuː ˈɛs ˈɡʌvərnmənt ˈprɪntɪŋ ˈɑːfɪs / (AmE), / ˌjuː ˈɛs ˈɡʌvənmənt ˈprɪntɪŋ ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
a former agency of the legislative branch of the United States government responsible for printing and binding official government publications for Congress and federal departments
United States federal printing agency
american

Examples

The US Government Printing Office published hundreds of official reports each year.
After 2014, the US Government Printing Office was renamed to the Government Publishing Office.
Many legal documents are produced by the US Government Printing Office for public access.
