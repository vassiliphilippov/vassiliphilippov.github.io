to need some poon

1. VERB

/ nid səm pun / (AmE), / niːd səm puːn / (BrE)
(slang) To desire or crave sexual intercourse, typically used by men referring to their own need for sex.
slang for wanting sex
slang vulgar

Examples

After being single for months, he admitted he really needed some poon.
Whenever his friends went out, they'd joke about who needed some poon the most.
He laughed and said he needed some poon after that stressful project.

Synset Examples

He joked about needing some poon after a long week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horny - sexually aroused or feeling desire for sex
  • Antonym: celibate - abstaining from sexual activity

Etymology

From slang 'poon', meaning 'sex' or 'female genitalia', itself probably from 'poon tang', African American Vernacular English.
