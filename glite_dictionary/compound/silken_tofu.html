silken tofu

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlkən ˈtoʊfu / (AmE), / ˈsɪlkən ˈtəʊfuː / (BrE)
A type of tofu with a soft, smooth texture, often used in soups, desserts, and delicate dishes.
very soft, smooth tofu

Examples

She added silken tofu to the smoothie for a creamy texture.
Many vegan desserts use silken tofu as an egg substitute.
The chef pureed silken tofu to make a light, airy mousse.

Synset Examples

The silken tofu blended perfectly in the soup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tofu - curd made from mashed soybeans, used chiefly in Asian and vegetarian cooking
