mail train

1. NOUN

/ meɪl treɪn / (AmE), / meɪl treɪn / (BrE)
a train that is used to transport mail
train used to carry mail

Examples

The mail train arrived at the station before dawn, carrying letters from across the country.
In the 19th century, the mail train was an essential part of the postal system.
Security was tight on the mail train because it often transported valuable packages.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: passenger train - a train that carries passengers
  • Synonym: freight train - a train that carries goods and cargo
