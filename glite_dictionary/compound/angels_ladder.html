angel's ladder

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪn.dʒəlz ˈlæd.ər / (AmE), / ˈeɪn.dʒəlz ˈlad.ə / (BrE)
a metaphorical pathway to success or achievement; a means or process by which goals are reached
metaphorical path to achievement

Examples

She viewed education as her angel's ladder to a better future.
Hard work became the angel's ladder that led him to his dreams.
They called perseverance their angel's ladder out of adversity.

Synset Examples

They saw life as an angel's ladder to reach dreams.
She climbed the angel's ladder to success.
His hard work was his angel's ladder to happiness.
