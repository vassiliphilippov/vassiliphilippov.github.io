to gain attention

1. VERB

/ ɡeɪn əˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / ɡeɪn əˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
to attract interest or be noticed by others
attract interest or notice

Examples

The new product quickly gained attention in the market due to its innovative design.
Her unusual outfit helped her gain attention at the crowded event.
The campaign video is starting to gain attention on social media platforms.

Synset Examples

The debate on coal burning continues to gain attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attract attention - to cause people to notice something or someone
