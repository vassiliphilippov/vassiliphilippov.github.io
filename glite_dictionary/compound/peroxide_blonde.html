peroxide blonde

1. NOUN

/ pəˈrɑːksaɪd blɑːnd / (AmE), / pəˈrɒksaɪd blɒnd / (BrE)
A person, especially a woman, whose hair has been artificially bleached to a very light blonde using peroxide.
person with peroxide-bleached blonde hair

Examples

She decided to become a peroxide blonde for the summer, completely changing her look.
The actress was famous for her striking appearance as a peroxide blonde.
In the 1950s, being a peroxide blonde was considered glamorous and daring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blonde - person with light-colored hair, usually yellowish

Etymology

From peroxide (short for hydrogen peroxide, used as a bleaching agent) + blonde.
