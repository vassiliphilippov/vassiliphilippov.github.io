Thunder Bay

1. PROPN

/ ˈθʌndɚ ˌbeɪ / (AmE), / ˈθʌndə ˌbeɪ / (BrE)
A port city in northwestern Ontario, Canada, situated on the shore of Lake Superior.
city in Ontario, Canada, on Lake Superior

Examples

The city of Thunder Bay is known for its beautiful views of Lake Superior.
My cousin moved to Thunder Bay to attend university there.
We stopped in Thunder Bay while driving across Canada.

Etymology

Named after the nearby Thunder Bay on Lake Superior.
