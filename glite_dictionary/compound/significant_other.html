significant other

1. NOUN

/ sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt ˈʌðɚ / (AmE), / sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt ˈʌðə / (BrE)
a person with whom one has a long-term romantic or sexual relationship, especially one with whom one lives, but who is not necessarily a spouse.
long-term romantic partner

Examples

She brought her significant other to the company party, and everyone was eager to meet him.
Do you plan to travel with your significant other this summer?
When filling out the form, you could list your significant other as your emergency contact.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partner - a person with whom one is in a close personal relationship

Etymology

Originally used in psychology in the mid-20th century to indicate a person of importance in someone's social environment.
