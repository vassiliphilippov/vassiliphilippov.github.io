double pneumonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌbəl pəˈmoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈdʌbl pəˈməʊniə / (BrE)
A type of pneumonia that affects both lungs, usually causing severe symptoms.
pneumonia in both lungs
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with double pneumonia after she complained of difficulty breathing.
His lengthy hospital stay was due to a severe case of double pneumonia.
Because double pneumonia affects both lungs, it typically requires more intensive treatment than single-lung pneumonia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pneumonia - inflammation of one or both lungs usually caused by infection
