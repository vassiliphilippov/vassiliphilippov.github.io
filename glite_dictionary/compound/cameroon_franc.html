Cameroon franc

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɛruːn fræŋk / (AmE), / kəˈmɛəruːn fræŋk / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Cameroon before the adoption of the Central African CFA franc
former currency of Cameroon

Examples

The old Cameroon franc was replaced by the Central African CFA franc in 1972.
Many collectors seek coins and banknotes from the time when the Cameroon franc was in use.
He inherited a box full of Cameroon francs from his grandfather.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Central African CFA franc - current currency used in Cameroon and other Central African countries
