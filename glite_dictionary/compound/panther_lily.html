panther lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænθər ˈlɪli / (AmE), / ˈpanθə ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A lily native to the western United States, characterized by orange-red to crimson flowers with maroon spots.
a lily with spotted orange-red to crimson flowers

Examples

The panther lily blooms each summer in the mountain meadows of California.
Their garden was filled with vibrant panther lily blossoms and other wildflowers.
We stopped to admire a beautiful panther lily growing near the hiking trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leopard lily - another common name for the same or similar lily species
