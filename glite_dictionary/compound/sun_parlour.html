sun parlour

1. NOUN

/ sʌn ˈpɑrlɚ / (AmE), / sʌn ˈpɑːlə / (BrE)
a room largely enclosed with glass to allow maximum exposure to sunlight, typically used for relaxation or enjoying the view
glass-enclosed room for sunlight

Examples

We spent the afternoon reading in the sun parlour, enjoying the warmth and natural light.
Their house features a beautiful sun parlour overlooking the garden.
She grows her orchids in the sun parlour, where they get plenty of sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunroom - a room with large windows or glass walls for sunlight exposure
