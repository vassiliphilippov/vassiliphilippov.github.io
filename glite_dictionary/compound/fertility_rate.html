fertility rate

1. NOUN

/ fɚˈtɪl.ə.t̬i reɪt / (AmE), / fəˈtɪl.ɪ.ti reɪt / (BrE)
the number of live births in a population per 1,000 people in a given year
number of births per 1,000 people

Examples

The country's fertility rate has declined steadily over the past decade.
Experts predict a decrease in the fertility rate as education levels rise.
A high fertility rate can contribute to rapid population growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birth rate - the number of births per 1,000 people per year
  • Antonym: mortality rate - the number of deaths in a population per 1,000 people per year
