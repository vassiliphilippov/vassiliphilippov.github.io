Japan cedar

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈpæn ˈsiːdər / (AmE), / dʒəˈpan ˈsiːdə / (BrE)
a tall evergreen tree native to Japan and China, valued for its soft, durable wood
evergreen tree of Japan and China

Examples

The forest was filled with ancient Japan cedar trees towering above the walking path.
Lightweight furniture is often made from softwood like Japan cedar.
The temple was constructed using durable Japan cedar timber.
