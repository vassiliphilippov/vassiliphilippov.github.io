seat of ease

1. NOUN

/ sit əv iz / (AmE), / siːt əv iːz / (BrE)
a comfortable place to sit, often providing relaxation or relief
comfortable place to sit

Examples

After a tiring afternoon, he collapsed gratefully into his seat of ease by the fireplace.
The library's window nook became her favorite seat of ease for quiet afternoons.
Each guest quickly found a seat of ease while waiting for the meeting to begin.
