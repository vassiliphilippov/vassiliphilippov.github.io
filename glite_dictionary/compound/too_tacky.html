too tacky

1. ADJ

/ tuː ˈtæki / (AmE), / tuː ˈtæki / (BrE)
excessively flashy, gaudy, or cheap in appearance to the point of being inappropriate or unpleasant
excessively gaudy or cheap-looking

Examples

The decorations at the party were too tacky for my taste.
She decided not to wear the necklace because it looked too tacky with her dress.
His jokes were too tacky to be funny at a family gathering.

Synset Examples

That shirt is too tacky for a formal event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tacky - gaudy or cheap in appearance or quality
