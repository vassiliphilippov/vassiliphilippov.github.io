international candle

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəl ˈkændl̩ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃnəl ˈkændl / (BrE)

former unit of luminous intensity

Examples

Laboratories once measured light sources using the international candle as their reference.
Modern lighting calculations no longer use the international candle due to updated SI standards.
