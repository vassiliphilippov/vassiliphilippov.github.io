Kissimmee River

1. PROPN

/ kɪˈsɪmi ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / kɪˈsɪmi ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in central Florida that flows southward into Lake Okeechobee
river in central Florida, USA

Examples

The Kissimmee River restoration project has improved the region's ecosystem.
We spent the afternoon boating along the Kissimmee River in Florida.
Historic flooding occurred near the Kissimmee River after heavy summer rains.

Etymology

Named after the Kissimmee area of Florida; 'Kissimmee' is thought to derive from a Native American (possibly Calusa or Creek) word.
