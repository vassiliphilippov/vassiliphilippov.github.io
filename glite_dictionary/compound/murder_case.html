murder case

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɝːdər keɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdə keɪs / (BrE)
a legal case involving the investigation and prosecution of a homicide
legal case involving homicide

Examples

The detective was assigned to a notorious murder case that captured national attention.
The jury deliberated for hours before reaching a verdict in the murder case.
After months of investigation, the police finally made an arrest in the unresolved murder case.

Synset Examples

The detective was solving a mysterious murder case.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homicide - the act of killing a person
