Duolingo competition

1. NOUN

/ ˌduːoʊˈlɪŋgoʊ ˌkɑːmpəˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdjuːəˈlɪŋgəʊ ˌkɒmpəˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
a contest in which participants compete using the Duolingo language-learning platform, usually to achieve the highest score or complete the most lessons within a set time
contest on the Duolingo app

Examples

My friends and I started a Duolingo competition to see who could learn the most Spanish in one week.
The school organized a Duolingo competition to motivate students to practice their language skills daily.
After joining the Duolingo competition, she spent every evening trying to maintain her lead.

Synset Examples

They challenged each other to a Duolingo competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contest - an event in which people compete for supremacy in a sport or other activity

Etymology

Duolingo is the name of the language learning platform, founded in 2011; 'competition' from Latin 'competitio' (rivalry).
