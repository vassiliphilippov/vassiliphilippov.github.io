pineapple family

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪˌnæpəl ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈpaɪˌnæpəl ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
A family of tropical American plants (Bromeliaceae) that includes the pineapple and related species, many of economic importance.
plant family including the pineapple

Examples

The pineapple family includes many beautiful ornamental plants besides the well-known fruit.
Scientists discovered a new species in the pineapple family growing in the rainforest.
Many houseplants, such as bromeliads, belong to the pineapple family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bromeliaceae - the scientific name for the pineapple family
