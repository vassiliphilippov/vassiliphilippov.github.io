corn pudding

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn ˈpʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈpʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
a baked dish made with corn, cream, and eggs, often served as a side dish in American cuisine
baked corn, cream, and egg dish
american
