chick car

1. NOUN

/ tʃɪk kɑɹ / (AmE), / tʃɪk kɑː / (BrE)
A small or cute car that is often stereotypically associated with women.
small or cute car associated with women
slang

Examples

She bought a bright yellow chick car that turns heads wherever she goes.
Some people dismiss the Mini Cooper as a chick car, but others love its sporty look.
He joked that his new convertible looked like a chick car because of its size and color.

Synset Examples

Her chick car is bright pink and very cute.
She loves her chick car with floral designs.

Etymology

From slang "chick" (young woman) + "car".
