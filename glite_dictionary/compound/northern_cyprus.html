Northern Cyprus

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɔrðərn ˈsaɪprəs / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈsaɪprəs / (BrE)
the self-declared state in the northern part of the island of Cyprus, recognized only by Turkey
self-declared state in northern Cyprus

Examples

The government of Northern Cyprus is recognized only by Turkey.
Many tourists visit Northern Cyprus for its beautiful beaches and historical sites.
Talks between Greek Cypriots and Northern Cyprus have often aimed at reunification of the island.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cyprus - an island country in the eastern Mediterranean
