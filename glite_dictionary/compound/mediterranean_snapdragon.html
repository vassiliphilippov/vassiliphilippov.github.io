Mediterranean snapdragon

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən ˈsnæpˌdræɡən / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən ˈsnæpˌdræɡən / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region, widely cultivated for its purple or pink flowers.
Mediterranean plant with purple or pink flowers

Examples

The Mediterranean snapdragon thrives in sunny gardens with well-drained soil.
Many gardeners choose the Mediterranean snapdragon for its showy purple and pink blossoms.
We spotted several Mediterranean snapdragons growing along the rocky coast during our hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snapdragon - plant with dragon-shaped flowers, genus Antirrhinum
