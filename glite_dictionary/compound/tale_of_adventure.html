tale of adventure

1. NOUN

/ teɪl əv ədˈvɛntʃɚ / (AmE), / teɪl əv ədˈvɛntʃə / (BrE)
a story involving exciting and often dangerous experiences, typically featuring daring deeds or exploration
story of exciting or dangerous experiences

Examples

As a child, I loved reading tales of adventure set on the high seas.
The old storyteller captivated us with tales of adventure and discovery.
Many classic novels are essentially tales of adventure that inspire young readers.

Synset Examples

Children gathered to hear tales of Kamikwasi's adventures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adventure story - a story involving risk, excitement, and unusual experiences
