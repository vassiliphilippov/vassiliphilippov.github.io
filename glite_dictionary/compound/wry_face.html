wry face

1. NOUN

/ raɪ feɪs / (AmE), / raɪ feɪs / (BrE)
a facial expression showing disdain, annoyance, or amusement, typically involving a twisted or contorted look
a disdainful or twisted facial expression

Examples

She gave him a wry face when he told yet another bad joke.
He made a wry face at the bitter taste of the medicine.
The boy pulled a wry face when asked to do his chores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grimace - a twisted expression indicating disgust, pain, or wry amusement
