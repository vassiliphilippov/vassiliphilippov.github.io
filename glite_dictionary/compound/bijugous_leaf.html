bijugous leaf

1. NOUN

/ baɪˈdʒuːɡəs liːf / (AmE), / baɪˈdʒuːɡəs liːf / (BrE)
A pinnate leaf with two pairs of leaflets.
pinnate leaf with two pairs of leaflets
medical

Examples

The bijugous leaf of this species makes it easy to identify among other plants.
You can recognize a bijugous leaf by its two distinct pairs of leaflets.
Many botanists use the presence of a bijugous leaf as a key characteristic in plant classification.

Etymology

From Latin 'bi-' meaning 'two' and 'jugum' meaning 'yoke' or 'pair'.
