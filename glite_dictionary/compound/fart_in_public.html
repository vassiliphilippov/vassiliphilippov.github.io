to fart in public

1. VERB

/ fɑrt ɪn ˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / fɑːt ɪn ˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
to release gas from the bowels openly in the presence of others, especially in social settings
pass gas openly in social settings
informal vulgar

Examples

It can be very embarrassing to fart in public, especially in a crowded elevator.
Some people find it funny to fart in public, but others see it as rude.
He tried his best not to fart in public during the important meeting.
