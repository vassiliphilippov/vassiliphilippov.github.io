water sapphire

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ər ˈsæfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈsæfaɪə / (BrE)

deep blue gemstone variety of cordierite

Examples

The necklace featured a single water sapphire set in silver.
Collectors prize water sapphires for their unique blue-violet color.
He gave her a ring adorned with a rare water sapphire.
