perfect cannonball

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜr.fɪkt ˈkæn.ənˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.fɪkt ˈkæn.ənˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a dive into water performed by curling the body tightly into a ball, executed in an ideal or exemplary manner
an ideal cannonball dive into water

Examples

His perfect cannonball made a huge splash and impressed everyone at the pool.
She practiced every afternoon until she could do a perfect cannonball.
The judges gave him high marks for his perfect cannonball during the competition.

Synset Examples

He executed a perfect cannonball into the pool.
