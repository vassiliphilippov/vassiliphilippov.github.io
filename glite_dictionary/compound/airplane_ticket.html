airplane ticket

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌpleɪn ˈtɪkət / (AmE), / ˈeəˌpleɪn ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
a ticket entitling the holder to travel on a specific flight or flights by airplane
ticket for travel by airplane

Examples

She bought her airplane ticket online two weeks before her trip.
Please make sure you have your airplane ticket and passport ready at the airport.
The price of an airplane ticket can vary depending on the season and destination.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: ticket - a piece of paper or electronic record that gives the holder a right to enter a place, travel, etc.
  • Synonym: boarding pass - a document provided by an airline giving a passenger permission to enter the restricted area of an airport and board the airplane
