trash bin

1. NOUN

/ træʃ bɪn / (AmE), / træʃ bɪn / (BrE)
A container for holding rubbish until it is collected and disposed of.
container for holding rubbish

Examples

Please take the full bag out of the trash bin before it starts to smell.
He accidentally threw away his keys in the trash bin.
City workers emptied every trash bin on the street this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dustbin - British term for a bin that holds rubbish
  • Synonym: garbage can - North American term for a bin that holds rubbish
