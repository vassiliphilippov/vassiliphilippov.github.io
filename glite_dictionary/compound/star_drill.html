star drill

1. NOUN

/ stɑr drɪl / (AmE), / stɑː drɪl / (BrE)
A steel tool with a star-shaped point, used for drilling holes in stone or masonry by hammering and rotating it.
tool for drilling holes in stone or masonry

Examples

The mason selected a star drill to create a hole in the granite slab.
You should wear safety goggles when using a star drill on masonry.
After several blows with the hammer, the star drill finally punctured the stone wall.
