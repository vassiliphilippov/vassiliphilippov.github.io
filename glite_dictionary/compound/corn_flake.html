corn flake

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn fleɪk / (AmE), / kɔːn fleɪk / (BrE)
A thin, crisp, toasted flake made from corn, typically eaten as a breakfast cereal, often with milk.
crisp flake breakfast cereal made from corn

Examples

I poured a bowl of corn flakes and added some fresh strawberries for breakfast.
After running out of bread, she ate corn flakes as a snack in the afternoon.
The kids like their corn flakes with a little honey on top.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cereal - any grain used for food, especially breakfast dishes
