hoary marmot

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔri ˈmɑrmət / (AmE), / ˈhɔːri ˈmɑːmət / (BrE)
a large North American marmot found in mountainous regions, known for its grizzled gray fur
large North American mountain marmot

Examples

We spotted a hoary marmot sunning itself on the rocks during our hike.
The alpine meadows are home to the hoary marmot, which is famous for its loud whistling calls.
Unlike other marmots, the hoary marmot has a distinctive silvery-grey coat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marmot - a type of large ground squirrel found in mountainous areas
