assholey attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈæsˌhoʊli ˈæ.tɪ.tjud / (AmE), / ˈæshˌhəʊli ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
An offensive, rude, or unpleasant manner or behavior typically characterized by insensitivity or arrogance.
rude or offensive behavior
informal vulgar

Examples

The manager's assholey attitude made the entire team dread meetings with him.
If you drop the assholey attitude, people might actually start listening to you.
Her assholey attitude completely killed the positive mood in the room.

Synset Examples

She found his assholey attitude hard to tolerate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad attitude - a negative or uncooperative manner

Etymology

Formed from 'asshole' (vulgar slang for an unpleasant person) plus '-y' and 'attitude'.
