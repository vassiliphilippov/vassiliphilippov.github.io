side road

1. NOUN

/ saɪd roʊd / (AmE), / saɪd rəʊd / (BrE)
a minor road that branches off from a main road
a minor road off a main road

Examples

We decided to take a side road to avoid the traffic on the highway.
Many side roads in the area are unpaved and less maintained.
The accident happened at the junction of the main street and a narrow side road.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: main road - a principal or major road, especially in a city or town
