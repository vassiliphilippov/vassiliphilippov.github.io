South Bend

1. PROPN

/ saʊθ bɛnd / (AmE), / saʊθ bɛnd / (BrE)
A city in northern Indiana, United States, known as the home of the University of Notre Dame.
city in northern Indiana, USA

Examples

The University of Notre Dame is located in South Bend, attracting visitors from all over the world.
