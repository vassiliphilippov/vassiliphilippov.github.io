pruritus ani

1. NOUN

/ pruːˌraɪtəs ˈeɪnaɪ / (AmE), / prʊəˌraɪtəs ˈaniː / (BrE)
A persistent itching of the skin around the anus, often caused by irritation or infection.
itching around the anus
medical

Examples

The patient complained of persistent pruritus ani that disrupted his sleep.
Hygiene and avoiding irritants are important in managing pruritus ani.
Certain infections and allergies can be underlying causes of pruritus ani.

Etymology

From Latin 'pruritus' (itching) and 'ani' (of the anus).
