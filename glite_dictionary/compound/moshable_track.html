moshable track

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑʃəbəl træk / (AmE), / ˈmɒʃəbəl træk / (BrE)
A song or piece of music that is suitable for energetic dancing (especially moshing) at a concert.
song suitable for moshing
informal slang

Examples

That new band always opens their set with a moshable track to excite the crowd.
If you want the audience to get moving, play a seriously moshable track next.
The playlist was full of moshable tracks that kept everyone energized all night.

Synset Examples

Everyone loves a moshable track that gets the energy up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mosh pit - an area where people dance energetically to rock music
  • Synonym: dance track - a song suitable for dancing

Etymology

From 'mosh' (to dance energetically in a style associated with punk and heavy metal concerts) plus '-able', forming 'moshable', and 'track' (song or piece of music).
