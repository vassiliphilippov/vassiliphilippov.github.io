nancy way

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæn.si weɪ / (AmE), / ˈnæn.si weɪ / (BrE)
an effeminate or overly sensitive manner or way of behaving, often used disparagingly about men
effeminate or overly sensitive behavior
informal offensive

Examples

His classmates mocked his nancy way when he refused to play football.
She told him to drop his nancy ways and toughen up.
Critics accused him of having a nancy way of speaking and dressing.

Synset Examples

He was teased for his nancy ways, but he didn’t mind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: effeminate - having characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly
  • Antonym: masculine - having qualities or appearance traditionally associated with men

Etymology

From 'Nancy' as a slang term for an effeminate man; 'way' meaning manner.
