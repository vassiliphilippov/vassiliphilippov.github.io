nothing else

1. X

/ ˈnʌθɪŋ ɛls / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ els / (BrE)
used to emphasize that at least one thing is true, even if nothing more is; at the very least
used to mean 'at the very least'

Examples

Nothing else matters as long as everyone is safe.
If nothing else, you'll gain some valuable experience from this project.
Nothing else can be done until we hear back from the manager.

Synset Examples

If nothing else, it was a valuable lesson.
If nothing else, at least we had fun.
If nothing else, we can always try again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at least - at the lowest or minimum
