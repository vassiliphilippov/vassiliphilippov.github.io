Florida gallinule

1. NOUN

/ flɔːˈrɪdə ˈɡælɪnjuːl / (AmE), / flɒˈrɪdə ˈɡælɪnjuːl / (BrE)
A dark bluish-grey gallinule native to North America, also known as the common gallinule or moorhen.
North American bluish-grey gallinule bird

Examples

We spotted a Florida gallinule gliding through the marsh at dawn.
The lake is home to herons, egrets, and the distinctive Florida gallinule.
A pair of Florida gallinules was nesting near the water's edge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: common gallinule - alternate name for the Florida gallinule
  • Synonym: moorhen - alternate name for the Florida gallinule
