white-flowered

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪt ˌflaʊərd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪt ˌflaʊəd / (BrE)
(of a plant) having white flowers.
having white flowers (of a plant)

Examples

The white-flowered jasmine filled the garden with its sweet scent.
Gardeners often prefer white-flowered varieties for moonlit landscapes.
We planted a white-flowered rose bush near the entrance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: red-flowered - (of plants) having red flowers
  • Antonym: yellow-flowered - (of plants) having yellow flowers
