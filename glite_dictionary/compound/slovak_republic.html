Slovak Republic

1. PROPN

/ sloʊˈvæk rɪˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / sləʊˈvæk rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
the official name of Slovakia, a landlocked country in central Europe, which became independent from the Czech Republic in 1993
central European country (official name of Slovakia)

Examples

The Slovak Republic joined the European Union in 2004.
After 1993, the Slovak Republic emerged as a sovereign state distinct from its neighbor.
Many tourists visit the Slovak Republic for its castles and mountain scenery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Slovakia - common name for the Slovak Republic, a country in central Europe
  • Antonym: Czech Republic - a neighboring country from which the Slovak Republic separated in 1993
