horse trader

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs ˈtreɪdər / (AmE), / hɔːs ˈtreɪdə / (BrE)
a person who is skilled or tough in bargaining, often using shrewd or hard-nosed tactics in negotiations
a tough or shrewd negotiator

Examples

He became known as a horse trader in business, always driving a hard bargain.
You need to be a bit of a horse trader to get the best deal in these negotiations.
As a seasoned horse trader, she never settled for the first offer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: negotiator - person who negotiates agreements
  • Synonym: bargainer - person who bargains

Etymology

Originally referred to people who bought and sold horses, a trade known for tough bargaining.
