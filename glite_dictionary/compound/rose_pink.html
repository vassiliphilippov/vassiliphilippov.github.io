rose pink

1. NOUN

/ roʊz pɪŋk / (AmE), / rəʊz pɪŋk / (BrE)
A pink-flowered marsh plant of the eastern United States, resembling a true centaury.
pink-flowered marsh plant

Examples

The wetlands were dotted with clusters of rose pink blooming in the summer sunlight.
Botanists discovered a rare species of rose pink near the edge of the marsh.
Gardeners value rose pink for its vibrant flowers and its ability to attract pollinators.
