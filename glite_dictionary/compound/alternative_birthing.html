alternative birthing

1. NOUN

/ ɔlˈtɝnətɪv ˈbɝθɪŋ / (AmE), / ɔːlˈtɜːnətɪv ˈbɜːθɪŋ / (BrE)
A method of childbirth that avoids intrusive medical interventions and high-tech equipment, favoring more natural and home-like settings.
childbirth with minimal medical intervention

Examples

More parents are choosing alternative birthing methods to create a more relaxed and natural experience.
The hospital offers classes on alternative birthing for expectant families who prefer less medical intervention.
She decided on alternative birthing after researching the benefits of home and water births.
