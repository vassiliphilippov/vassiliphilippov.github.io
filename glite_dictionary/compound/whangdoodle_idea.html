whangdoodle idea

1. NOUN

/ ˈwæŋˌduːdəl aɪˈdiːə / (AmE), / ˈwæŋˌduːdəl aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
A fanciful, silly, or absurd idea, often regarded as imaginative but unrealistic.
silly or absurd idea
informal

Whenever he starts talking about his whangdoodle ideas, the whole room bursts into laughter.

Related Terms

Etymology

Whangdoodle: whimsical formation, 19th-century American English, meaning a fanciful creature or object.
