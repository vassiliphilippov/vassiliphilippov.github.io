progressive rock

1. NOUN

/ prəˈɡrɛsɪv rɑk / (AmE), / prəˈɡrɛsɪv rɒk / (BrE)
a style of rock music characterized by complex compositions, experimentation, and influences from other music genres, popularized in the 1970s
complex, experimental rock music genre

Examples

The band is known for its unique blend of progressive rock and classical music elements.
Many fans consider the 1970s to be the golden age of progressive rock.
He prefers progressive rock to mainstream pop because of its experimental nature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: art rock - a subgenre of rock music with experimental and artistic elements
