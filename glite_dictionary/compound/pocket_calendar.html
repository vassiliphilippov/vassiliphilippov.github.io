pocket calendar

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkɪt ˈkæləndɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt ˈkæləndə / (BrE)
A small calendar designed to fit in a pocket or wallet, often as a card, booklet, or notebook.
small, portable calendar

Examples

She always keeps a pocket calendar in her purse to track appointments.
The company gave everyone a branded pocket calendar at the New Year.
He found a forgotten pocket calendar from 1995 in the drawer.

Synset Examples

My grandfather still uses pocket calendars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desk calendar - a calendar designed to be kept on a desk
