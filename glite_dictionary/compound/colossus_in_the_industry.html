colossus in the industry

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlɑːsəs ɪn ði ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / kəˈlɒsəs ɪn ði ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
a company, organization, or person that is a dominant force or major player within a particular industry or field
dominant company or figure in an industry

Examples

After acquiring several rivals, the corporation became a true colossus in the industry.
With its innovative technology, the startup hopes to compete with the colossus in the industry.
Despite being a colossus in the industry, the company continues to invest heavily in research and development.

Synset Examples

The company's growth made it a colossus in the industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: giant - a person or thing of great size or importance
  • Synonym: powerhouse - a person or organization with great energy, strength, or influence
  • Antonym: minor player - an entity with little influence in its field

Etymology

Figurative use of 'colossus', from Greek kolossos (giant statue), to mean something or someone of enormous size or importance.
