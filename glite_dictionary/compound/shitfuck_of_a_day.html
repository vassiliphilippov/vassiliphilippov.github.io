shitfuck of a day

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtfʌk əv ə deɪ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtfʌk əv ə deɪ / (BrE)
A very bad or extremely frustrating day, expressed with strong vulgarity.
an extremely bad or frustrating day (vulgar)
vulgar offensive informal

Examples

I had a shitfuck of a day and just want to go home and relax.
It was another shitfuck of a day thanks to endless meetings and traffic jams.
After dealing with rude customers, Sarah called it a shitfuck of a day.

Synset Examples

That was a shitfuck of a day at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrible day - a very bad or unpleasant day
