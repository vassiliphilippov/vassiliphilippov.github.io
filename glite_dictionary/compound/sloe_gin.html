sloe gin

1. NOUN

/ sloʊ dʒɪn / (AmE), / sləʊ dʒɪn / (BrE)
A sweet, reddish liqueur made by infusing gin with sloes, the fruit of the blackthorn.
gin liqueur flavored with sloes

Examples

She offered her guests a glass of sloe gin before dinner.
You can make sloe gin at home by steeping sloes in a bottle of gin for several months.
The bar specializes in cocktails featuring sloe gin as a main ingredient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gin - a clear alcoholic spirit distilled from grain or malt and flavored with juniper berries
