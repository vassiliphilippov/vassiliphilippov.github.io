hand truck

1. NOUN

/ hænd ˈtrʌk / (AmE), / hænd ˈtrʌk / (BrE)
a small cart with two wheels and handles, used for moving heavy objects by tilting and pushing it on its wheels
two-wheeled cart for moving heavy items

Examples

The movers loaded the boxes onto a hand truck to carry them out to the van.
She used a hand truck to transport several heavy crates across the warehouse floor.
A hand truck can make moving large appliances much easier and safer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dolly - a platform on wheels used for moving heavy objects
