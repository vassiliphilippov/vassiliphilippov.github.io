humorous context

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmərəs ˈkɑnˌtɛkst / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmərəs ˈkɒn.tɛkst / (BrE)
a situation, setting, or manner in which something is intended to be funny or amusing
situation meant to be funny

Examples

Please remember that some jokes are only appropriate in a humorous context.
The word was used in a humorous context and was not meant to offend anyone.
It's important to recognize when a humorous context may not be understood by everyone.

Synset Examples

The term 'Jewbag' is often used in a humorous context among friends.
The term clithead is often used in humorous contexts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: serious context - a situation or setting intended to be taken seriously, not for humor
