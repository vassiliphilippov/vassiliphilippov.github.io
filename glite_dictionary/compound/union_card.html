union card

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuːniən kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈjuːniən kɑːd / (BrE)
an official card that shows proof of membership in a labor union
card proving labor union membership

Examples

He had to show his union card before entering the meeting.
Only members with a valid union card are allowed to vote.
She applied for a job that required a union card as proof of membership.
