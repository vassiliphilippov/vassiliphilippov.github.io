hyp moment

1. NOUN

/ haɪp ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / haɪp ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
A particularly exciting or hyped moment, often characterized by intense anticipation or enthusiasm.
an exciting or hyped moment
informal slang

Examples

The crowd went wild during the hyp moment of the show when the headliner appeared.
We all agreed that scoring the winning goal was the hyp moment of the game.
Everyone pulled out their phones to capture the hyp moment as the fireworks lit up the sky.

Synset Examples

They shared a hyp moment before the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hyped - Excited or full of anticipation
  • Synonym: exciting moment - A moment filled with excitement

Etymology

Formed from 'hyp', a clipped form of 'hyped' (excited, stimulated), and 'moment'.
