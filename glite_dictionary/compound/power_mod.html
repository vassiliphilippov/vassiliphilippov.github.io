power mod

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊɚ mɑd / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə mɒd / (BrE)
a modification made to enhance the performance or output, especially of vehicles or gaming devices
modification to improve performance

Examples

She installed a power mod on her car to increase its horsepower.
After adding a power mod, his gaming console ran much faster.
Many enthusiasts seek out the latest power mods for their devices.

Synset Examples

She loves to customize her bike with a power mod.
He installed a power mod to enhance his gaming console.
The power mod gave the car an impressive boost.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mod - a modification, especially to a device or vehicle
