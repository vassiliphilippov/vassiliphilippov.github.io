muffin man

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌf.ɪn mæn / (AmE), / ˈmʌf.ɪn mæn / (BrE)
a man who formerly sold muffins door to door, especially in Britain
historical muffin seller (male)
archaic british

Examples

In Victorian London, the muffin man would ring his bell to attract customers.
Many children know the song about the muffin man who lives on Drury Lane.
Before bakeries became common, a muffin man brought fresh muffins to people's doors.
