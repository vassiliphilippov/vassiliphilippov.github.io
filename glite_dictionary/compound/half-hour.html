half-hour

1. NOUN

/ hæf ˈaʊɚ / (AmE), / hɑːf ˈaʊə / (BrE)
A period of thirty minutes; half of an hour.
thirty-minute period

Examples

We waited for a half-hour before the bus finally arrived.
She takes a half-hour walk every morning to stay healthy.
The meeting was scheduled to last only a half-hour, but it ran much longer.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: hour - a period of time equal to sixty minutes
  • Degree Lower: minute - a period of time equal to sixty seconds
