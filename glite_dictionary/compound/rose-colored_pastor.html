rose-colored pastor

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊzˌkʌlɚd ˈpæstər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzˌkʌləd ˈpɑːstə / (BrE)
a glossy black bird with a pink back and abdomen, chiefly found in Asia; also called the rosy starling
an Asian bird with pink back and glossy black parts

Examples

Each spring, flocks of rose-colored pastor migrate across the steppes of Central Asia.
A rose-colored pastor perched on the fence, showing off its striking plumage.
The naturalist documented the sighting of a rose-colored pastor near the wetland sanctuary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rosy starling - glossy black bird with pink coloring, species Pastor roseus
