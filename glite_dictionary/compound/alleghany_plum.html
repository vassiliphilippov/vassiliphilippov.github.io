Alleghany plum

1. NOUN

/ ˌæl.əˈɡeɪ.ni plʌm / (AmE), / ˌæl.ɪˈɡeɪ.ni plʌm / (BrE)
a wild plum tree native to the northeastern United States, producing dark purple fruits with yellow flesh
wild plum tree of northeastern US

Examples

The Alleghany plum is prized by foragers for its sweet, yellow flesh.
Conservationists are working to protect the Alleghany plum from habitat loss.
I tasted an Alleghany plum while hiking in Pennsylvania last fall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plum - a sweet, fleshy fruit with a stone, or the tree that bears it
