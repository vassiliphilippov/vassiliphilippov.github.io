free burning

1. ADJ

/ fri ˈbɜrnɪŋ / (AmE), / fri ˈbɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an electric arc or similar process) burning continuously without interruption, typically in an open or unrestricted manner.
burning continuously (esp. of electric arcs)

Examples

The technician studied the properties of a free burning arc in the laboratory.
Free burning conditions are essential for certain welding operations.
Researchers measured the heat transfer in free burning electrical discharges.

Synset Examples

heat transfer to the anode in free burning arcs
