elegant Habenaria

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛl.ɪ.ɡənt həˈbæn.ə.ri.ə / (AmE), / ˈɛl.ɪ.ɡənt həˈbæn.ə.ri.ə / (BrE)
A slender species of inland rein orchid, distinguished by its pale greenish-yellow flowers, similar to the coastal rein orchid.
a slender inland rein orchid with pale greenish-yellow flowers

Examples

The elegant Habenaria blooms in late spring across the grasslands of inland Australia.
Unlike its coastal cousin, the elegant Habenaria has petals with a distinct pale yellow-green hue.
Botanists were excited to find a rare elegant Habenaria growing beside the riverbank.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: coastal rein orchid - a related species of rein orchid found in coastal regions

Etymology

Habenaria is a genus name from Latin, meaning 'having a rein or strap,' referring to the lip shape of the flower.
