cactus wren

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæktəs rɛn / (AmE), / ˈkæktəs rɛn / (BrE)
a large, harsh-voiced American wren found in arid regions of the southwestern United States and Mexico
large wren of the US southwest and Mexico

Examples

A cactus wren built its nest in the tall saguaro just outside our window.
The piercing call of the cactus wren echoed across the dry desert landscape.
We spotted several cactus wrens darting among the prickly pear cacti.
