little theater

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬l ˈθiətər / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ˈθɪətə / (BrE)
A small theater that is primarily used for experimental drama or productions by collegiate or community groups.
small theater for experimental or community drama

Examples

After moving to the city, Heather joined the little theater to pursue her passion for acting.
The little theater downtown often features innovative new plays by local writers.
Many aspiring actors get their start performing in college or community little theaters.
