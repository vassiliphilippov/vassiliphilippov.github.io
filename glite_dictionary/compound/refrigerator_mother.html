refrigerator mother

1. NOUN

/ ɹɪˈfɹɪdʒəˌreɪtɚ ˈmʌðɚ / (AmE), / ɹɪˈfrɪdʒəˌreɪtə ˈmʌðə / (BrE)
a historical term for a mother whose cold or emotionally distant behavior was wrongly believed to cause autism in her child
(historical) theory about distant mothers causing autism

Examples

The outdated 'refrigerator mother' theory caused a great deal of pain for many families.
She was wrongly labeled a refrigerator mother by some psychologists in the 1960s.
Modern research has thoroughly debunked the concept of the refrigerator mother.

Synset Examples

They used to blame the refrigerator mother for autism.
The term refrigerator mother is now considered outdated.
She fought against the refrigerator mother theory for years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autism - a developmental disorder characterized by difficulties with social interaction and communication
  • Synonym: cold - emotionally distant or unresponsive (personality trait)

Etymology

Formed from 'refrigerator' (suggesting coldness) and 'mother'; first used in mid-20th century psychological literature.
