grey mullet

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪ ˈmʌlɪt / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈmʌlɪt / (BrE)
a type of freshwater or coastal fish with a spindle-shaped body, found worldwide and often used as food
a worldwide food fish species

Examples

Fishermen caught several grey mullet near the riverbank early this morning.
The chef prepared the grey mullet with a lemon and herb dressing.
In many countries, grey mullet is considered a popular ingredient in traditional seafood dishes.
