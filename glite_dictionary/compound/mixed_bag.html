mixed bag

1. NOUN

/ mɪkst bæg / (AmE), / mɪkst bæɡ / (BrE)
a collection or group consisting of different kinds or a variety of things, often with differing qualities or values
assortment of varied things or types

Examples

The exhibition was a mixed bag of traditional and modern artwork.
Feedback on the new policy has been a mixed bag, with both praise and criticism.
The reviews for the film are something of a mixed bag—some loved it, others were disappointed.

Synset Examples

a great assortment of cars was on display
he had a variety of disorders
a veritable smorgasbord of religions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assortment - a collection of different things or people
  • Synonym: variety - a number of different types

2. NOUN

/ mɪkst bæg / (AmE), / mɪkst bæɡ / (BrE)
a group or collection of people, things, or results that are varied in quality, character, or type
a variety of mixed elements or qualities

Examples

The reviews for the new restaurant were a mixed bag, some praising the food and others criticizing the service.
Her performance throughout the season was a mixed bag, with some standout moments and a few disappointing lapses.
Our holiday experience turned out to be a mixed bag—incredible scenery but unpredictable weather.

Synset Examples
