gymnastic horse

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪmˈnæstɪk hɔrs / (AmE), / dʒɪmˈnastɪk hɔːs / (BrE)
a padded apparatus supported by legs, used in gymnastics for vaulting or performing exercises
padded apparatus for gymnastics exercises

Examples

The gymnast approached the gymnastic horse with confidence before executing a flawless vault.
During practice, he spent an hour perfecting his routine on the gymnastic horse.
Every competition features skilled athletes performing difficult moves on the gymnastic horse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pommel horse - a gymnastic horse with handles, used for routines involving circular movements
