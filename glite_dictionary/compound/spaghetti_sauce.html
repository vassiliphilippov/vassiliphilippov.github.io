spaghetti sauce

1. NOUN

/ spəˈɡɛti sɔs / (AmE), / spəˈɡɛti sɔːs / (BrE)
a sauce, often tomato-based, served with spaghetti or other pasta dishes
sauce for spaghetti or pasta

Examples

She simmered the spaghetti sauce for hours to bring out the flavors.
I always keep a jar of spaghetti sauce in my pantry for quick meals.
The recipe calls for homemade spaghetti sauce with fresh basil and garlic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marinara sauce - a popular tomato-based Italian pasta sauce
  • Antonym: alfredo sauce - a creamy white pasta sauce
