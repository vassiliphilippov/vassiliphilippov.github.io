opposite number

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑpəzɪt ˈnʌmbər / (AmE), / ˈɒpəzɪt ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
a person who holds the same position or does the same job as someone in another organization or group
person with corresponding role elsewhere

Examples

During the conference, the finance director met her opposite number from the French partner company.
The British ambassador discussed the issue with his American opposite number.
As chief engineer, she often collaborates with her opposite number in the Japanese branch.

2. NOUN

/ ˈɑːpəzɪt ˈnʌmbər / (AmE), / ˈɒpəzɪt ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
a person who holds a position equivalent to that of someone else in another organization or group
corresponding role in another organization

Examples

The marketing manager often consults with her opposite number at the partner company.
During the international conference, each delegate was introduced to their opposite number.
He arranged a meeting with his opposite number from the competitor's headquarters.

Synset Examples

Her opposite number at the rival firm is quite competitive.
He met with his opposite number to discuss the merger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: counterpart - a person or thing that corresponds to or closely resembles another
