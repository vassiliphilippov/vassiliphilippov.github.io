finickety about

1. ADJ

/ fɪˈnɪkɪti əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / fɪˈnɪkɪti əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
excessively particular or fussy regarding specific things or tasks
excessively fussy or particular
british

Examples

My grandmother is finickety about the way her table is set for dinner.
He's always finickety about the brands of clothes he wears.
She became finickety about where she shops after her last experience.

Synset Examples

The cat is finickety about what food she eats.
He's very finickety about how his coffee is made.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finicky - excessively particular or difficult to please

Etymology

Variant of 'finicky', possibly from English dialect 'finick' (affected, dainty).
