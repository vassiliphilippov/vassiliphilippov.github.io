firing off

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪərɪŋ ˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈfaɪərɪŋ ˌɒf / (BrE)
the act of discharging a gun or other firearm
act of discharging a gun

Examples

The sudden firing off of a rifle startled everyone in the camp.
They practiced the firing off of weapons at the shooting range every week.
After the firing off of the starting pistol, the runners surged forward.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discharge - the act of firing a gun
