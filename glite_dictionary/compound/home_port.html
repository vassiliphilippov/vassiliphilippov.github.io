home port

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm pɔrt / (AmE), / həʊm pɔːt / (BrE)
the port from which a ship is registered or originates; a ship's principal base or port of registry
ship's base or port of registry

Examples

After a long journey at sea, the vessel finally returned to its home port.
Each ship must display the name of its home port on the stern.
The fleet's home port was busy with preparations for the next voyage.
