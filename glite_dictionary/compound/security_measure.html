security measure

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈkjʊr.ə.t̬i ˈmɛʒ.ɚ / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊə.rɪ.ti ˈmɛʒ.ə / (BrE)
a device or method designed to detect unauthorized entry and protect property or people, often by setting off an alarm when a break-in is attempted
device for detecting unauthorized entry

Examples

Installing a security measure such as a motion detector can help protect your home from intruders.
The museum invested in a new security measure to ensure the safety of its priceless artwork.
After the break-in, they added several security measures, including window sensors and alarm systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alarm system - system that alerts to unauthorized entry
