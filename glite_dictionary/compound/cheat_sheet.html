cheat sheet

1. NOUN

/ tʃit ʃit / (AmE), / tʃiːt ʃiːt / (BrE)
a summary of important information, often used for quick reference or as an aid in exams
concise reference notes

Examples

She wrote formulas on a cheat sheet to help her during the math test.
The programming cheat sheet listed all the useful keyboard shortcuts.
Before the exam, Mark quickly glanced at his cheat sheet one more time.

Synset Examples

He brought a cheat sheet to the test for quick reference.
The teacher warned against using a cheat sheet during exams.
I created a cheat sheet to help me study for finals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crib sheet - a sheet containing notes or answers for use in an exam, often illicitly
