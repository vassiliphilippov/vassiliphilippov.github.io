little bit

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪtəl bɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɪtəl bɪt / (BrE)
a small amount; a modest or tiny quantity of something
small amount

Examples

Could you move the chair just a little bit to the left?
She felt a little bit nervous before the interview.
Add a little bit of sugar to sweeten the coffee.

Synset Examples

Every little bit of small change adds up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bit - a small piece or portion
