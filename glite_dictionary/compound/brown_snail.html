brown snail

1. NOUN

/ braʊn sneɪl / (AmE), / braʊn sneɪl / (BrE)
a garden pest snail with a brown shell marked by pale zigzag lines, found in many regions worldwide
a globally distributed garden pest snail

Examples

The brown snail is often blamed for damaging young plants in vegetable gardens.
Despite its small size, a single brown snail can consume a surprising amount of foliage overnight.
Gardeners use various methods to control the spread of the brown snail population.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garden snail - a common land snail found in gardens
