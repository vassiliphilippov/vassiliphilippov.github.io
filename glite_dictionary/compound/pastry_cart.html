pastry cart

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪstri kɑrt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪstri kɑːt / (BrE)
a serving cart used to display pastry desserts to patrons in a restaurant or café
cart for serving pastry desserts

Examples

The waiter wheeled the pastry cart over, tempting us with cakes and tarts.
After dinner, we chose a slice of lemon pie from the pastry cart.
Their restaurant is famous for a lavish pastry cart filled with desserts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dessert cart - a cart for displaying and serving desserts in a restaurant
