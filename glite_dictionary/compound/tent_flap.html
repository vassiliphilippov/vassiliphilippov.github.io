tent flap

1. NOUN

/ tɛnt flæp / (AmE), / tɛnt flæp / (BrE)
A piece of material, usually canvas, that can be opened or closed to allow entrance to a tent.
canvas entrance piece of a tent

Examples

The wind blew the tent flap open in the middle of the night.
He peeked out through the tent flap to see if the rain had stopped.
She secured the tent flap with a hook before going to sleep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flap - a piece of material attached on one side and free on the other, used for covering or closing
