all day

1. ADV

/ ɔl deɪ / (AmE), / ɔːl deɪ / (BrE)
for the entire duration of the day; continuously or throughout the whole day.
for the whole day

Examples

I've been waiting for the delivery all day and it's still not here.
He could talk about sports all day without getting bored.
We spent all day at the beach enjoying the sunshine.

Synset Examples

Let’s finish this; we haven't got all day.
Hurry up, we haven't got all day!
Come on, we haven't got all day to waste.
They played video games all day long.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all morning - for the entire duration of the morning
  • Synonym: all week - for the entire duration of the week
