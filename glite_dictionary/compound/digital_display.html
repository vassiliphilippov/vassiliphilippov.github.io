digital display

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪdʒɪtəl dɪˈspleɪ / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl dɪˈspleɪ / (BrE)
a device that shows information using characters such as numbers or letters, typically on an electronic screen
device showing electronic characters or numbers

Examples

The clock features a bright digital display that's easy to read, even in the dark.
Many car dashboards now use a digital display instead of traditional gauges.
She glanced at the digital display on her watch to check the time.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: analog display - a display providing information using physical pointers or dials
