Uighur language

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːɡər ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈwiːɡə ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken mainly by the Uighur people in Xinjiang, northwestern China
Turkic language of the Uighur people

Examples

Many people in Xinjiang speak Uighur language as their mother tongue.
He is fluent in both Mandarin and Uighur language.
The Uighur language uses an Arabic-based script for its written form.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Turkish - the Turkic language spoken in Turkey and Cyprus
  • Synonym: Uyghur - alternative spelling of Uighur

Etymology

From Uighur, the self-designation of the people, via Turkic languages.
