classic car

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæsɪk kɑr / (AmE), / ˈklæsɪk kɑː / (BrE)
an older automobile that is highly valued because of its age, style, and quality, typically regarded as collectible
collectible vintage automobile

Examples

He restored his grandfather's classic car to pristine condition over several years.
The museum showcased several classic cars from the 1960s and 1970s.
Owning a classic car is a dream for many automobile enthusiasts.

Synset Examples

He said, 'I’d buy the classic car for $500, Alex.'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vintage car - an old car, especially one from the early 20th century, considered collectible
