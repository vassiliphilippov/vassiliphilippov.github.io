nuclear engineering

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuː.kli.ɚ ˌɛn.dʒəˈnɪr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.klɪ.ə ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
the branch of engineering that deals with the design, construction, and operation of nuclear reactors and related systems.
branch of engineering for nuclear reactors

Examples

She completed her degree in nuclear engineering before joining a leading energy company.
Advances in nuclear engineering have made reactors safer and more efficient.
He chose to specialize in nuclear engineering to contribute to clean energy solutions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nuclear physics - the branch of physics concerned with nuclear energy and the properties of the atomic nucleus
