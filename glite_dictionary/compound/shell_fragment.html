shell fragment

1. NOUN

/ ʃɛl ˈfræɡmənt / (AmE), / ʃɛl ˈfraɡmənt / (BrE)
a small piece or part of a broken shell, especially from a mollusk or egg
piece of a broken shell

Examples

Carefully, she picked up a colorful shell fragment from the sandy beach.
The shell fragment was sharp, so he handled it with care.
Researchers examined each shell fragment to identify the type of mollusk.

Synset Examples

They collected shell fragments for their art project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragment - a small part broken off or detached
