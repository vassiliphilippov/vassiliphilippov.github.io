car dealer

1. NOUN

/ kɑr ˈdiːlər / (AmE), / kɑː ˈdiːlə / (BrE)
a person or company that buys and sells cars, especially as a business
person or business selling cars

Examples

The car dealer offered me a great discount on a new sedan.
We visited several car dealers before deciding which model to buy.
After five years as a car dealer, she opened her own showroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dealership - a business that sells cars, usually affiliated with manufacturers
