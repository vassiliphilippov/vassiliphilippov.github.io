Lake Tsana

1. PROPN

/ leɪk ˈtsɑːnə / (AmE), / leɪk ˈtsɑːnə / (BrE)
a large lake in northern Ethiopia, the largest in the country and the source of the Blue Nile
lake in northern Ethiopia; source of the Blue Nile

Examples

Many tourists visit Lake Tsana to admire its stunning scenery and rich birdlife.
The Blue Nile flows out of Lake Tsana and travels through Sudan to reach Egypt.
Scientists study Lake Tsana to understand its unique ecosystem and significance to the region.
