red shrubby penstemon

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈʃrʌbi pɛnˈstiːmən / (AmE), / rɛd ˈʃrʌbi pɛnˈstiːmən / (BrE)
a low, branching shrub with dark green leaves and bunches of brick-red flowers at the ends of its branches, found in the coastal ranges and foothills of northern California
Californian shrub with red flowers

Examples

The red shrubby penstemon adds vibrant color to native California gardens.
We spotted a cluster of red shrubby penstemon growing along the hiking trail.
Botanists conducted a survey of red shrubby penstemon populations in the foothills.
