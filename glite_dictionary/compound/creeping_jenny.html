creeping Jenny

1. NOUN

/ ˈkripɪŋ ˈdʒɛni / (AmE), / ˈkriːpɪŋ ˈdʒɛni / (BrE)
a trailing perennial plant (Lysimachia nummularia) with rounded leaves and yellow flowers, often grown as ground cover.
trailing perennial ground cover plant

Examples

Creeping Jenny grows quickly in shady, moist areas of the garden.
Many gardeners use creeping Jenny as ground cover because of its attractive yellow blooms.
The creeping Jenny provided a lush, green carpet around the pond.
