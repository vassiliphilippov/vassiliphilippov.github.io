land mine of controversy

1. NOUN

/ ˈlænd ˌmaɪn əv kənˈtrɝː.vɚ.si / (AmE), / ˈlænd ˌmaɪn əv kənˈtrɒ.və.si / (BrE)
a situation or topic that is likely to cause a great deal of trouble or disagreement
potentially very controversial situation

Examples

His remarks about the policy became a land mine of controversy in the media.
The company’s decision touched off a land mine of controversy among its investors.
She avoided the topic completely, fearing it was a land mine of controversy.

Synset Examples

He stepped into a land mine of controversy with that comment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot button issue - a subject that causes strong opinions and reactions
  • Synonym: minefield - a subject or situation with many potential dangers or difficulties
