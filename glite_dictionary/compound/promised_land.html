Promised Land

1. PROPN

/ ˈprɑmɪst ˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈprɒmɪst ˌlænd / (BrE)
(in the Bible) the land promised by God to Abraham and his descendants; traditionally identified as the region of ancient Palestine on the eastern coast of the Mediterranean Sea.
the land promised to Abraham's descendants in the Bible

Examples

Many religious pilgrims visit the Promised Land every year to connect with their faith.
For centuries, the Promised Land has been a site of historical and spiritual significance.
Stories of the Israelites journeying to the Promised Land are central to the Bible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Canaan - the ancient name for the region identified as the Promised Land

Etymology

Refers to the land promised by God to Abraham in the Hebrew Bible.

2. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːmɪst lænd / (AmE), / ˈprɒmɪst lænd / (BrE)
A place or state regarded as the ultimate goal or reward, especially the spiritual destination sought by Christians or a hoped-for ideal situation.
the ultimate spiritual goal or ideal place

Examples

For many, forgiveness and redemption are the path to the Promised Land.
The preacher spoke about striving for the Promised Land in our daily lives.
She regarded inner peace as her own personal Promised Land.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heaven - the ultimate destination of the blessed in Christian belief

Etymology

From the Biblical account, referring to the land promised by God to Abraham and his descendants in the Hebrew Scriptures.

3. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːmɪst ˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈprɒmɪst ˌlænd / (BrE)
a place or situation where one achieves happiness, fulfillment, or success, especially after a long period of effort or hardship
place of happiness or fulfillment

Examples

After years of study and perseverance, she finally arrived at her Promised Land of professional success.
Many immigrants viewed America as the Promised Land offering hope and opportunity.
For some, retirement is the Promised Land after a lifetime of hard work.

Synset Examples

He finally reached his own Promised Land after years of hard work.
In her dreams, she found the Promised Land of happiness.
They journeyed for years in search of the Promised Land.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paradise - a place or state of perfect happiness
  • Synonym: utopia - an imagined place of ideal perfection

Etymology

From the biblical concept of the land promised by God to Abraham and his descendants in the Hebrew Bible.
