ne plus ultra

1. NOUN

/ neɪ plʊˈsʌltrə / (AmE), / neɪ pluːs ˈʌltrə / (BrE)
the highest or ultimate point of excellence; the perfect or most extreme example of something
the ultimate or highest point; perfection

Examples

The chef's new dessert is considered the ne plus ultra of fine dining in the city.
This painting represents the ne plus ultra of Renaissance art.
For many, the vintage sports car was the ne plus ultra of automotive engineering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pinnacle - the highest point of achievement or development
  • Synonym: zenith - the time at which something is most powerful or successful
  • Antonym: nadir - the lowest point in a situation

Etymology

From Latin 'ne plus ultra' meaning 'not more beyond', allegedly inscribed as a warning at the Pillars of Hercules.
