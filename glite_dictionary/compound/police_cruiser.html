police cruiser

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis ˈkruːzɚ / (AmE), / pəˈliːs ˈkruːzə / (BrE)
a car used by police officers for patrolling, typically equipped with communication devices and emergency equipment
police patrol car equipped for duty

Examples

The police cruiser sped down Main Street with its siren blaring.
Two officers exited the police cruiser to question the witnesses at the scene.
She saw a police cruiser parked outside the grocery store during her lunch break.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patrol car - a car used by police for patrolling

Etymology

From 'police' referring to law enforcement and 'cruiser', meaning a vehicle that moves about patrolling an area.
