bathroom singer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæθˌruːm ˈsɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθruːm ˈsɪŋə / (BrE)
a person who enjoys singing informally and privately, typically while in the bathroom
person who sings privately, especially in the bathroom
informal

Examples

Though she calls herself a bathroom singer, her voice is surprisingly good.
My father is a secret bathroom singer, always humming tunes in the shower.
As a bathroom singer, he dreams of performing on stage one day.

Synset Examples

Everyone knows she’s a bathroom singer, but she’s amazing.
He’s a bathroom singer, belting out tunes in the shower.
My brother is a bathroom singer who dreams of stardom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amateur singer - a person who sings for pleasure, not professionally
  • Antonym: professional singer - a person who sings as an occupation
