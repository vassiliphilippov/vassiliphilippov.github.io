Northern Alliance

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɔrðərn əˈlaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən əˈlaɪəns / (BrE)
a multiethnic military and political coalition in northern Afghanistan united in opposition to the Taliban, known for advocating a moderate form of Islam
Afghan anti-Taliban coalition

Examples

The Northern Alliance played a crucial role in the fight against the Taliban in Afghanistan.
Many international agencies coordinated with the Northern Alliance after the U.S. invasion in 2001.
The Northern Alliance was composed of several ethnic groups united by a common enemy.
