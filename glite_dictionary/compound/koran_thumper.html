Koran thumper

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɑn ˈθʌmpɚ / (AmE), / kəˈrɑːn ˈθʌmpə / (BrE)
A person who aggressively promotes or enforces Islamic beliefs, often in a fervent or preachy manner.
aggressive promoter of Islamic beliefs
offensive

Examples

The outspoken Koran thumper often got into heated debates with his classmates.
Some accuse him of being a Koran thumper, but he sees himself as a passionate believer.
The community grew wary of Koran thumpers disrupting public events with their preaching.

Synset Examples

She was called a Koran thumper for her beliefs.
A Koran thumper, he preached in the public square.
They criticized him as a Koran thumper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bible thumper - a person who aggressively promotes Christian beliefs

Etymology

Patterned after 'Bible thumper', substituting 'Koran' (the Islamic holy book) for 'Bible'.
