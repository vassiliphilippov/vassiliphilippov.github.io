fancy-dress ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈfænsi drɛs bɔl / (AmE), / ˈfænsi drɛs bɔːl / (BrE)
A formal dance or party where guests wear elaborate costumes and masks, often themed.
costume dance party event

Examples

The town's annual fancy-dress ball attracts hundreds dressed as historical figures and fairy-tale characters.
She spent weeks designing her costume for the upcoming fancy-dress ball.
At the fancy-dress ball, guests wore extravagant masks and danced late into the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masquerade ball - a formal dance where guests wear masks and costumes
