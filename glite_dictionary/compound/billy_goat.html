billy goat

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪli ɡoʊt / (AmE), / ˈbɪli ɡəʊt / (BrE)
an adult male goat
male goat

Examples

The farmer kept a billy goat to breed with the does.
A billy goat usually has a distinctive beard and large horns.
Children laughed as the billy goat jumped over the fence.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: nanny goat - female goat
