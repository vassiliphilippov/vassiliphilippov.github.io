knock rummy

1. NOUN

/ nɑk ˈrʌmi / (AmE), / nɒk ˈrʌmi / (BrE)
a version of the card game rummy in which a player may end the round if the value of their unmatched cards is less than ten points
a version of rummy with low-point knocking rule

Examples

We played knock rummy all evening during our family vacation.
In knock rummy, going out requires your deadwood to total less than ten points.
She prefers knock rummy to standard rummy because of the added challenge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gin rummy - a popular form of rummy played with two players
