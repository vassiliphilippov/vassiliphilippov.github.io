observation station

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑb.zɚˈveɪ.ʃən ˈsteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɒb.zəˈveɪ.ʃən ˈsteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a place equipped and arranged specifically for observing or monitoring particular phenomena, such as weather, wildlife, or astronomical events
facility for making observations

Examples

The weather data was collected daily at the observation station outside the city.
Scientists built a new observation station to monitor migrating birds along the coast.
The astronomers spent nights working at the mountain observation station to study the stars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: observatory - a building or place equipped for observing astronomical, meteorological, or other natural phenomena
