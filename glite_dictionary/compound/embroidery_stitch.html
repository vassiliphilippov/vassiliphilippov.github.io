embroidery stitch

1. NOUN

/ ɛmˈbroʊdəri stɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɪmˈbrəʊdəri stɪtʃ / (BrE)
A single stitch made with thread and a needle as part of a decorative design in embroidery.
a decorative stitch used in embroidery

Examples

This pattern uses a different embroidery stitch for each flower in the design.
Learning a new embroidery stitch can really enhance your sewing projects.
The teacher demonstrated how to do a basic embroidery stitch on the fabric.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stitch - a loop of thread or yarn resulting from a single pass or movement of the needle in sewing, knitting, or crocheting
