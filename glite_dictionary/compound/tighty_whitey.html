tighty whitey

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪti ˈwaɪti / (AmE), / ˈtaɪti ˈwaɪti / (BrE)
A pair of tight, white briefs worn by men, typically considered old-fashioned or unfashionable.
tight white men's briefs
informal slang

Examples

He laughed as he found his old tighty whiteys in the drawer.
Some men prefer boxers over tighty whiteys for comfort.
The commercial featured a superhero costume that looked like oversized tighty whiteys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: briefs - short, close-fitting underpants

Etymology

Formed from 'tight' and 'whitey', referring to the fit and color.
