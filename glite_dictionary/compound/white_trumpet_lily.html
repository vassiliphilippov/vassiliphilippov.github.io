white trumpet lily

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈtrʌmpɪt ˈlɪli / (AmE), / waɪt ˈtrʌmpɪt ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A tall lily with large, white, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring.
tall lily with white trumpet-shaped flowers

Examples

We planted a white trumpet lily in the garden last spring, and it bloomed beautifully.
The florist recommended the white trumpet lily for its elegant appearance and lovely fragrance.
A single white trumpet lily stood out among the other flowers in the bouquet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Easter lily - a type of lily with large white trumpet-shaped flowers, often used at Easter
