United States Government Printing Office

1. PROPN

a former agency of the U.S. legislative branch that provided printing and binding services for Congress and federal government departments
former U.S. federal printing agency

Examples

For over a century, the United States Government Printing Office supplied official documents to government agencies.
The United States Government Printing Office was reorganized into the Government Publishing Office in 2014.
