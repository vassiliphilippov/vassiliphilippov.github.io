business community

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs kəˌmjunɪti / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs kəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
the people involved in managing and operating businesses, considered as a group
people managing businesses collectively

Examples

The business community gathered to discuss the impact of new tax regulations.
Support from the business community has been crucial to the success of the city's revitalization project.
Tensions sometimes arise between the government and the business community over new economic policies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entrepreneurs - people who start and run businesses
  • Synonym: business leaders - people in top management positions in companies
