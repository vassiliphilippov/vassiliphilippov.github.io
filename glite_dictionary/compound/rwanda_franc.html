Rwanda franc

1. NOUN

/ ruˈɑːndə fræŋk / (AmE), / ruˈɑːndə fræŋk / (BrE)
the official basic unit of currency in Rwanda
basic monetary unit of Rwanda

Examples

You can exchange your dollars for Rwanda franc at the airport upon arrival.
The price of the meal was 2,500 Rwanda francs at the local restaurant.
The value of the Rwanda franc has remained stable over the past year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: franc - a former or current unit of currency in several countries
