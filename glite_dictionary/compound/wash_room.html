wash room

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑʃ ˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃ ˌruːm / (BrE)
A public room equipped with toilets and facilities for washing hands.
public toilet or restroom

Examples

The wash room is located at the end of the hall, near the main entrance.
After the long journey, she was relieved to find a clean wash room at the station.
The theater's wash rooms are regularly cleaned throughout the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restroom - A public toilet in a building
  • Synonym: lavatory - A room with a toilet and washing facilities
  • Synonym: toilet - A fixture used for defecation and urination, or the room containing it
