blood moon

1. NOUN

/ blʌd mun / (AmE), / blʌd muːn / (BrE)
A total lunar eclipse in which the moon appears reddish in color due to the Earth's atmosphere filtering sunlight.
reddish moon during a lunar eclipse

Examples

The blood moon last night was a breathtaking sight.
Many people stayed up late to photograph the rare blood moon.
Legends about the blood moon have existed in cultures around the world.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunar eclipse - the obscuring of the moon as it passes into the Earth's shadow
