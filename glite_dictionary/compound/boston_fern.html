Boston fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑstən fɝn / (AmE), / ˈbɒstən fɜːn / (BrE)
A species of sword fern with long arching or drooping fronds, commonly grown as a houseplant.
popular indoor sword fern plant

Examples

The Boston fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often placed in hanging baskets.
After buying a Boston fern for her apartment, she noticed the air felt fresher.
Many people choose the Boston fern as a decorative plant for both homes and offices.
