potent potable

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊtənt poʊˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈpəʊtənt pəʊˈteɪtəbl̩ / (BrE)
A strong alcoholic drink.
strong alcoholic drink

Examples

At the bar, he ordered a potent potable to unwind after a long week.
The menu featured several potent potables crafted by local distilleries.
She warned that even one potent potable could make you feel tipsy.

Synset Examples

She always opts for a potent potable at happy hour.
This craft beer is a potent potable with a unique flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spirit - a strong distilled alcoholic drink
  • Synonym: liquor - alcoholic drink, especially strong spirits

Etymology

From Latin 'potent' (powerful) and 'potabilis' (drinkable); 'potable' entered English via Old French.
