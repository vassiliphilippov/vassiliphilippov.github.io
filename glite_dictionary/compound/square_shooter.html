square shooter

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr ˈʃuːtər / (AmE), / skwɛə ˈʃuːtə / (BrE)
a person who is straightforward, honest, and trustworthy in their dealings
an honest and straightforward person
informal american

Examples

Everyone in the office trusts Tom because he's a square shooter who tells it like it is.
If you want honest feedback, talk to Janet—she's a real square shooter.
People know that as a square shooter, he never cuts corners or hides the truth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: straight shooter - a person who is honest and direct

Etymology

From the American English slang term "square" (honest) and "shooter" (one who acts or speaks), dating from early 20th century.
