lace making

1. NOUN

/ leɪs ˈmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / leɪs ˈmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the craft or art of creating lace by hand using threads and various techniques
the craft of making lace by hand

Examples

She learned lace making from her grandmother, who passed down traditional techniques and patterns.
Many museums offer demonstrations of lace making as part of their heritage craft exhibits.
The delicate scarf was the result of hours spent mastering the art of lace making.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tatting - a technique for handcrafting lace
  • Synonym: knitting - the craft of making fabric from yarn by interlocking loops
  • Synonym: crochet - a craft of creating fabric by interlocking loops of yarn with a hooked needle
