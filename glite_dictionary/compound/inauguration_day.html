Inauguration Day

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌnɔɡjəˈreɪʃən deɪ / (AmE), / ɪˌnɔːɡjʊˈreɪʃən deɪ / (BrE)
the day on which the President of the United States is formally sworn into office
U.S. presidential swearing-in day
american

Examples

Crowds gathered at the Capitol on Inauguration Day to witness the historic ceremony.
A new president officially takes office on Inauguration Day every four years.
Security was heightened throughout Washington, D.C. on Inauguration Day due to expected large crowds.
