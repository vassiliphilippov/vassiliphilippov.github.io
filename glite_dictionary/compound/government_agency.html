government agency

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌvərnmənt ˈeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvəmənt ˈeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
an organization that is part of the government and is responsible for a particular function or service
official part of government administration

Examples

The Environmental Protection Agency is a well-known government agency in the United States.
Every government agency must adhere to strict regulations and transparency requirements.
She applied for a job at a federal government agency after graduating from university.

  • Synonym: agency - an organization that provides a particular service
