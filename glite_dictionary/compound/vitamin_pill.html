vitamin pill

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪ.t̬ə.mɪn pɪl / (AmE), / ˈvɪ.tə.mɪn pɪl / (BrE)
A pill containing one or more vitamins, taken as a dietary supplement to support health or prevent deficiencies.
pill containing vitamins for supplementation

Examples

She takes a vitamin pill every morning to make sure she gets enough nutrients.
If you have a balanced diet, you may not need to take a vitamin pill.
My doctor recommended a daily vitamin pill during the winter months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supplement - a substance taken to add nutritional value to the diet
