Grand Canyon

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡrænd ˈkæn.jən / (AmE), / ˌɡrænd ˈkæn.jən / (BrE)
an immense and steep-sided gorge carved by the Colorado River in northern Arizona, renowned for its size and striking landscape
enormous gorge in Arizona, USA

Examples

We visited the Grand Canyon last summer and were amazed by its breathtaking views.
The Grand Canyon attracts millions of tourists from around the world each year.
Scientists study the Grand Canyon to learn about Earth's geological history.
