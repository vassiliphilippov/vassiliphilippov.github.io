ginger-scented

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɪndʒɚ ˌsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒə ˌsɛntɪd / (BrE)
having a smell or aroma of ginger.
having the smell of ginger

Examples

The candle filled the room with a ginger-scented warmth that reminded me of winter holidays.
She gave me a ginger-scented lotion as a gift for my birthday.
The ginger-scented tea was both soothing and invigorating after a long day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gingery - having a flavor or smell of ginger
