toilet powder

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔɪlət ˌpaʊdər / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlət ˌpaʊdə / (BrE)
a fine powder used on the body, especially after bathing, to absorb moisture and add fragrance
fine powder applied to the body after bathing

Examples

After her bath, she dusted her skin with a light layer of toilet powder.
The delicate scent of lavender lingered, thanks to the toilet powder she used each morning.
He bought a new container of toilet powder for his travels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talcum powder - a fine powder made from talc, used on the body

Etymology

From 'toilet' (meaning personal grooming) and 'powder' (a fine dry substance).
