bank robber

1. NOUN

/ bæŋk ˈrɑːbɚ / (AmE), / bæŋk ˈrɒbə / (BrE)
a person who steals money from a bank, especially by using violence or threats
person who robs a bank

Examples

The bank robber fled the scene before the police arrived.
Authorities are searching for the bank robber who escaped with a large sum of money.
The daring bank robber was finally captured after a week-long manhunt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: robber - a person who commits robbery
