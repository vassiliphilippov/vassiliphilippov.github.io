television star

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌvɪʒən stɑr / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən stɑː / (BrE)
A famous actor, actress, or personality known for appearing regularly on television shows.
a famous person in television shows

Examples

She became a television star after landing the lead role in a popular drama series.
Many children dream of becoming a television star when they grow up.
The television star attended the awards ceremony dressed in an elegant gown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celebrity - a famous person, especially in entertainment
  • Degree Higher: film star - an actor or actress who is famous for appearing in films
  • Synonym: actor - a person who performs in plays, movies, or television shows
