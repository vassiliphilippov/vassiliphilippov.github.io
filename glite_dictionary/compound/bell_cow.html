bell cow

1. NOUN

/ bɛl kaʊ / (AmE), / bɛl kaʊ / (BrE)
the person or thing that serves as a leader or the most prominent example in a group or activity
leading or most important example

Examples

The new software release is the bell cow for all our future projects.
Her outstanding performance made her the bell cow of the sales team.
This innovative design became the bell cow of the industry.

Synset Examples

Her innovative ideas became the bell cow for the project.
That product launch is our bell cow for the year.
The new marketing strategy is our bell cow this quarter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pace-setter - a person or thing that sets the pace in a group
  • Synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group or organization
  • Antonym: follower - a person who follows the lead or example of another

Etymology

From the practice of placing a bell around the neck of the leading cow in a herd.
