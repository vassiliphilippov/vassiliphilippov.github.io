grainy club mushroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪni klʌb ˈmʌʃˌrum / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪni klʌb ˈmʌʃˌrʊm / (BrE)
Any of various mushrooms of the class Ascomycetes, characterized by a grainy texture and a club-like shape.
a mushroom of the class Ascomycetes with grainy texture

Examples

The forest floor was dotted with grainy club mushrooms after the rain.
Mycologists found that grainy club mushrooms thrive in moist, shaded soils.
Some chefs experiment with grainy club mushroom varieties in gourmet dishes.
