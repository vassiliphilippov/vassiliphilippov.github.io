all in

1. ADJ

/ ɔl ɪn / (AmE), / ɔːl ɪn / (BrE)
Extremely tired; completely exhausted.
very tired; exhausted
informal

Examples

By the end of the long hike, I was all in and needed to rest.
After running the marathon, she looked completely all in.
He's all in after working two shifts in a row.

Synset Examples

was all in at the end of the day
so beat I could flop down and go to sleep anywhere
bushed after all that exercise
I'm dead after that long trip

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausted - very tired; drained of energy
  • Synonym: bushed - very tired (informal)
  • Antonym: refreshed - feeling rested and energetic again
