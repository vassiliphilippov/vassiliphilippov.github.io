US Mint

1. PROPN

/ ju ɛs mɪnt / (AmE), / juː ɛs mɪnt / (BrE)
the official government institution responsible for manufacturing and distributing United States coins and processing precious metals
official institution making US coins
american

Examples

Tourists can visit the US Mint in Philadelphia to learn about the coinmaking process.
The US Mint releases commemorative coins every year to celebrate important events.
Gold bullion is also processed by the US Mint in addition to coins for circulation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Royal Mint - official body producing coins for the United Kingdom
