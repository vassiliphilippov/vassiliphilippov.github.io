black spot

1. NOUN

/ blæk spɑt / (AmE), / blæk spɒt / (BrE)
any of several fungal diseases of plants that cause small black spots to appear on the leaves, stems, or fruit.
plant disease causing black spots

Examples

The roses in my garden developed black spot after several days of rain.
Black spot can cause leaves to yellow and drop prematurely.
Gardeners often treat black spot with fungicides to protect their plants.
