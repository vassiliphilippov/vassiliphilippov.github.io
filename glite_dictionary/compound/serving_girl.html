serving girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝvɪŋ ˌɡɝl / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪŋ ˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl or young woman employed as a servant, especially in a household
girl employed as a servant

Examples

The wealthy family employed a young serving girl to help with the household chores.
As a serving girl in the manor, she rose before dawn every day to begin her duties.
In many Victorian novels, the character of the serving girl represents the working class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: servant - a person who is employed to perform domestic or other duties
