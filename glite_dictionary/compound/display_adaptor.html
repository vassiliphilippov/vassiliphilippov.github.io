display adaptor

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈspleɪ əˌdæp.tɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈspleɪ əˌdæp.tə / (BrE)
an electronic device in a computer that converts digital information into signals for video output to a display screen
device converting computer data to video for display

Examples

The computer's display adaptor was upgraded to improve graphics performance.
If your monitor isn't working, the display adaptor may be faulty.
Modern laptops often have an integrated display adaptor on the motherboard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graphics card - circuit board that generates display output on a monitor
  • Synonym: video adapter - device converting digital signals for display
