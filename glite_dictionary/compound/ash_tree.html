ash tree

1. NOUN

/ æʃ tri / (AmE), / æʃ triː / (BrE)
A deciduous tree of the genus Fraxinus, often with pinnate leaves, grown for timber or as an ornamental.
deciduous tree of the genus Fraxinus

Examples

The ash tree in our garden turns golden yellow in autumn.
Many musical instruments are made from ash tree wood.
After the storm, several branches from the old ash tree had fallen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Fraxinus - the botanical genus that includes ash trees
