fresh coat of wax

1. NOUN

/ frɛʃ koʊt əv wæks / (AmE), / frɛʃ kəʊt əv wæks / (BrE)
a newly applied layer of wax, typically used to make a surface shine or protect it
newly applied layer of wax

Examples

The car gleamed after receiving a fresh coat of wax.
Give your wooden floors a fresh coat of wax to restore their shine.
He applied a fresh coat of wax to his surfboard before heading to the beach.
