flying kite

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaɪɪŋ kaɪt / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ kaɪt / (BrE)
the activity of making a kite fly in the air, typically as a form of outdoor recreation
activity of making a kite fly

Examples

We spent the afternoon flying kites at the beach.
Every spring, children can be seen flying kites in the park.
She enjoys flying kites with her family on windy weekends.

Synset Examples

It was a windy day, perfect for flying kites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kite flying - the activity or sport of flying kites
