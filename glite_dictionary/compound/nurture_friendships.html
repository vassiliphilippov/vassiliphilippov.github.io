to nurture friendships

1. VERB

/ ˈnɝːtʃɚ ˈfrɛndʃɪps / (AmE), / ˈnɜːtʃə ˈfrɛndʃɪps / (BrE)
to actively develop and maintain close relationships with friends
develop and maintain friendships

Examples

It's important to nurture friendships to maintain a happy and supportive social life.
She made a conscious effort to nurture friendships by staying in touch and meeting regularly.
If you want lasting connections, you should nurture friendships instead of taking them for granted.

Synset Examples

It's important to nurture friendships for support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cultivate friendships - to try to develop friendships
  • Antonym: neglect friendships - to fail to care for friendships
