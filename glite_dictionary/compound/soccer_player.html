soccer player

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑkər ˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈsɒkə ˌpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays soccer, either professionally or as an amateur
person who plays soccer

Examples

The young soccer player scored three goals in yesterday's match.
After years of training, she became a professional soccer player in the national league.
Many children dream of becoming a famous soccer player when they grow up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: footballer - a person who plays football (soccer), especially professionally
