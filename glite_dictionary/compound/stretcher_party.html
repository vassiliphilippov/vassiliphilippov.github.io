stretcher party

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɛtʃər ˌpɑrti / (AmE), / ˈstrɛtʃə ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
a group of people equipped with stretchers, responsible for carrying injured or sick people, especially in emergencies or during wartime.
group to carry injured people with stretchers

Examples

During the emergency drill, the stretcher party quickly transported the injured to safety.
A stretcher party was dispatched to assist those wounded on the battlefield.
The hospital organized a stretcher party to move patients during the fire alarm.
