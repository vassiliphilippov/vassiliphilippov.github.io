cherry bomb

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛri bɑm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri bɒm / (BrE)
A small, round, red firecracker known for its high explosive power.
a powerful, round red firecracker

Examples

Kids were warned not to play with cherry bombs because they can cause serious injuries.
He set off a cherry bomb during the celebration, and it made a deafening bang.
After lighting the cherry bomb, they quickly ran away to watch it explode from a safe distance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firecracker - a small explosive device designed to make a loud noise
