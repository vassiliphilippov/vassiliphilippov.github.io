perishingly tired

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛrɪʃɪŋli ˈtaɪərd / (AmE), / ˈpɛrɪʃɪŋli ˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
Extremely tired; exhausted to a high degree.
extremely tired

Examples

After pulling an all-nighter for her exams, she was perishingly tired the next morning.
He felt perishingly tired after running the marathon in the heat.
By the end of their long travels, everyone was perishingly tired and ready to go to bed.

Synset Examples

She was perishingly tired after the long hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausted - completely used up of energy; very tired
  • Degree Lower: tired - in need of rest or sleep
