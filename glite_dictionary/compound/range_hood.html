range hood

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪndʒ hʊd / (AmE), / ˈreɪndʒ hʊd / (BrE)
a device fitted above a kitchen stove to remove smoke, steam, and cooking odors by exhausting air outdoors or filtering it
exhaust device above a kitchen stove

Examples

We installed a new range hood to keep the kitchen free of smoke and smells.
The range hood needs to be cleaned regularly to maintain proper ventilation.
Her apartment was equipped with a modern range hood and stainless steel appliances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extractor fan - fan for removing air or odors from a room
  • Synonym: kitchen hood - another term for range hood, used interchangeably
