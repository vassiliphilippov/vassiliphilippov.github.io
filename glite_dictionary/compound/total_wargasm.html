total wargasm

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬əl ˈwɔːrˌɡæzəm / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.tl ˈwɔːˌɡæzəm / (BrE)
A state of overwhelming excitement or exhilaration experienced in war-themed situations, especially intense action scenes or games.
overwhelming excitement in war-themed situations
slang

Examples

Fans described the climax of the game as a total wargasm for strategy lovers.
The last ten minutes of the film are a total wargasm of explosions and combat.
For military history buffs, the new exhibition delivers a total wargasm from start to finish.

Synset Examples

The movie's action sequence was a total wargasm for enthusiasts.

Etymology

Blend of 'war' and 'orgasm', formed to suggest an intense, euphoric reaction to war-related action.
