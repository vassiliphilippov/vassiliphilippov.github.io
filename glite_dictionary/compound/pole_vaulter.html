pole vaulter

1. NOUN

/ poʊl ˈvɔltər / (AmE), / pəʊl ˈvɔːltə / (BrE)
an athlete who competes in the sport of pole vault by jumping over a high bar using a long flexible pole
athlete who performs the pole vault

Examples

The pole vaulter cleared the bar at a record height during the championship.
As a dedicated pole vaulter, she trains for hours every day to perfect her technique.
The crowd cheered as the youngest pole vaulter made a successful jump.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pole vault - an athletic event in which an athlete jumps over a bar using a pole
