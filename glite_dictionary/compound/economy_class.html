economy class

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈkɑːnəmi klæs / (AmE), / ɪˈkɒnəmi klɑːs / (BrE)
the least expensive category of seating or accommodation on an airplane, train, or ship, offering basic amenities and services
cheapest travel class on planes, trains, or ships

Examples

We chose to fly in economy class to save money on our trip.
The meal options in economy class were limited compared to other sections.
He always books economy class tickets for business travel.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: first class - the most expensive and luxurious class of accommodation on transportation
  • Degree Higher: business class - an intermediate class of accommodation, more expensive than economy but less than first class
