giant panda

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt ˈpændə / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈpandə / (BrE)
a large, herbivorous mammal native to China, with distinctive black and white markings, chiefly eating bamboo
large black-and-white bamboo-eating mammal

Examples

The giant panda is a symbol of wildlife conservation worldwide.
We saw a giant panda eating bamboo at the zoo.
Efforts to protect the giant panda have helped increase their population in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panda - another term for the giant panda or, less commonly, the red panda
