circle of academia

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜːrkəl əv ˌækəˈdiːmiə / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkəl əv ˌækəˈdiːmiə / (BrE)
a group or community of people involved in academic fields or scholarly activities
group within academic fields

Examples

Her discoveries caused excitement in the circle of academia and beyond.
Within the circle of academia, his theories were considered groundbreaking.
Many debates in the circle of academia never reach the wider public.

Synset Examples

In circles of academia, her research is highly respected.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: academic community - the community of scholars and researchers in academic institutions
