holiday meal

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːlɪˌdeɪ miːl / (AmE), / ˈhɒlɪˌdeɪ miːl / (BrE)
a meal specially prepared and eaten as part of the celebration of a holiday or festive occasion
special meal for a holiday or celebration

Examples

We always invite extended family over for a big holiday meal at Christmas.
Her favorite holiday meal is Thanksgiving dinner with turkey and all the sides.
Many people save traditional recipes to make only for their annual holiday meal.

Synset Examples

Gammon is a popular choice for holiday meals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feast - a large meal, often for a celebration
