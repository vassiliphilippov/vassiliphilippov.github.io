baseball swing

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl swɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl swɪŋ / (BrE)
a batter's attempt to hit a pitched ball in baseball, typically involving a coordinated motion of the arms and body
batter's attempt to hit a pitched ball (baseball)

Examples

He practiced his baseball swing for hours in the backyard.
A coach can help you refine your baseball swing to improve your batting average.
Her powerful baseball swing sent the ball flying over the fence.

Synset Examples

he took a vicious cut at the ball

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swing - the act of moving something in a curved path
