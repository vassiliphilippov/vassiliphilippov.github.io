gathered skirt

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæðərd skɝːt / (AmE), / ˈɡæðəd skɜːt / (BrE)
a skirt made by drawing the fabric together at the waist to create fullness
skirt with fabric gathered at the waist

Examples

She wore a floral gathered skirt that flared gracefully as she walked.
The designer chose a gathered skirt for the summer collection to add volume and movement.
A gathered skirt can be paired with a simple blouse for a classic look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pleated skirt - a skirt with fabric folded into pleats
