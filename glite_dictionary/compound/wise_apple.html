wise apple

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪz ˌæpəl / (AmE), / ˈwaɪz ˌæpəl / (BrE)
a person who is sarcastically clever or cheeky, or a clever or sarcastic remark
sarcastically clever person or remark
informal american

Examples

The meeting was interrupted by another one of his wise apple comments.
Stop being a wise apple and get back to work.
She couldn't resist making a wise apple remark during the presentation.

Synset Examples

He always has a wise apple remark during meetings.
Don’t be such a wise apple; just follow the instructions.
She rolled her eyes at his wise apple comments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wise guy - a person who is annoyingly clever or makes sarcastic remarks

Etymology

Modeled on the construction of 'wise guy' and 'bad apple'; likely originating as colloquial American English.
