hearty breakfast

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrti ˈbrɛkfəst / (AmE), / ˈhɑːti ˈbrɛkfəst / (BrE)
a substantial and filling morning meal, typically containing a variety of nutritious and plentiful foods
substantial and filling morning meal

Examples

After a long walk, we enjoyed a hearty breakfast at the local café.
She prefers a hearty breakfast of eggs, bacon, and toast to start her day.
A hearty breakfast gives you the energy needed for a busy morning.

Synset Examples

We shared a hearty breakfast before starting our adventure.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: light breakfast - a small or modest morning meal
