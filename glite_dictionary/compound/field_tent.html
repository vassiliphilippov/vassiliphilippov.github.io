field tent

1. NOUN

/ fiːld tɛnt / (AmE), / fiːld tent / (BrE)
a large canvas tent designed for use outdoors, especially by military personnel or campers in the field
large outdoor canvas tent, often military

Examples

The soldiers slept in a field tent during their week-long training exercise.
We set up our field tent near the riverbank for the night.
The expedition team brought a sturdy field tent to withstand harsh weather conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tent - a portable shelter made of cloth and supported by poles
