peace advocacy

1. NOUN

/ pis ˈædvəkəsi / (AmE), / piːs ˈædvəkəsi / (BrE)
the active support or promotion of peaceful international relations and the prevention of conflict between nations
promotion of peaceful international relations

Examples

The organization is known for its commitment to peace advocacy around the world.
Many international conferences are held to promote peace advocacy and prevent armed conflict.
She dedicated her career to peace advocacy, working with governments to resolve disputes diplomatically.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pacifism - belief in avoiding war or violence
