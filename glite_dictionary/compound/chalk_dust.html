chalk dust

1. NOUN

/ tʃɔk dʌst / (AmE), / tʃɔːk dʌst / (BrE)
The fine powder produced as a result of writing or drawing with chalk, especially on a chalkboard.
fine powder from using chalk

Examples

The chalk dust settled on the desks after the lesson was over.
Prolonged exposure to chalk dust can cause irritation for some teachers.
She wiped the chalk dust from her hands after writing on the board.

Synset Examples

chalk dust covered the teacher's hands

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dust - fine, dry particles of matter
