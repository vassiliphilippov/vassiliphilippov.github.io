power company

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊ.ɚ ˌkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə ˌkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)
A company that generates and supplies electricity to the public.
company supplying electricity

Examples

The power company restored electricity to the neighborhood after the storm.
She called the power company to report an outage in her area.
Many power companies are investing in renewable energy sources.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electric utility - a company that supplies electricity as a service
