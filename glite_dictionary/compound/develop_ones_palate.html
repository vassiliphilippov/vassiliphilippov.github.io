to develop one's palate

1. VERB

/ dɪˈvɛləp wʌnz ˈpælət / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləp wʌnz ˈpælət / (BrE)
to acquire a refined or sophisticated taste for different kinds of food and drink through experience or deliberate effort
become more discerning in taste for food and drink

Examples

As she tried more exotic cuisines, she began to develop her palate for spices and new flavors.
Wine tasting classes helped him develop his palate and appreciate subtle differences between vintages.
Traveling abroad is an excellent way to develop your palate and discover new culinary experiences.

Synset Examples

Her palate has developed significantly over the years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cultivate a taste - to try to develop a liking for something, especially food or drink
