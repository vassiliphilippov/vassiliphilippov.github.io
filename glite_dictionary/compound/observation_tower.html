observation tower

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑb.zɚˈveɪ.ʃən ˌtaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˌɒb.zəˈveɪ.ʃən ˌtaʊ.ə / (BrE)
a tall structure built to provide a wide view of the surrounding area, as for sightseeing or surveillance
a structure for viewing the surroundings

Examples

We climbed the observation tower to get a breathtaking view of the national park.
The old observation tower was once used to spot approaching ships along the coast.
From the top of the observation tower, you can see the city skyline and beyond.
