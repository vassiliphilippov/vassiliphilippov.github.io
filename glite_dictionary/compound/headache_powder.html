headache powder

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdˌeɪk ˈpaʊdər / (AmE), / ˈhɛdeɪk ˈpaʊdə / (BrE)
a powdered medicine, usually containing aspirin or similar painkillers, taken to relieve headaches
powdered aspirin for headaches

Examples

She mixed the headache powder with water and drank it to ease her pain.
After a long day, he took a headache powder instead of his usual tablet.
The pharmacist recommended a fast-acting headache powder for her migraine symptoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aspirin - a common pain-relieving drug
