massage parlor

1. NOUN

/ məˈsɑʒ ˈpɑrlɚ / (AmE), / məˈsɑːʒ ˈpɑːlə / (BrE)
a business where people can receive therapeutic massage services
business offering therapeutic massage

Examples

After a long week at work, I treated myself to a relaxing session at the local massage parlor.
The city council implemented new regulations on massage parlors to ensure public safety and proper licensing.
Many people visit a massage parlor to help relieve stress or muscle pain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spa - place offering health and wellness treatments, sometimes including massage
  • Synonym: massage therapist - a person who gives massages professionally

2. NOUN

/ məˈsɑʒ ˌpɑrlər / (AmE), / məˈsɑːʒ ˌpɑːlə / (BrE)
A business that offers massage services, often used to refer to a place where sexual services are offered illegally under the pretense of therapeutic massage.
business offering massages, often euphemism for brothel

Examples

That building was shut down after the police raided the massage parlor for illegal activity.
Some massage parlors operate legally, while others are fronts for prostitution.
The newspaper exposed a network of massage parlors involved in organized crime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brothel - a house where people pay to have sex with prostitutes
  • Antonym: spa - a place offering therapeutic massage and treatments, legit business

Synset POS: n
