Florida key

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɔːrɪdə ki / (AmE), / ˈflɒrɪdə kiː / (BrE)
a small, low island of coral or limestone off the southern coast of Florida, forming part of the Florida Keys archipelago
small low coral/limestone island near Florida

Examples

The boat anchored near a Florida key, where the water was crystal clear and teeming with fish.
Each Florida key has its own unique ecosystem and charm.
They spent their vacation exploring a remote Florida key by kayak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: islet - a very small island
