nervous tissue

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɜrvəs ˈtɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈnɜːvəs ˈtɪʃuː / (BrE)
Tissue made up of neurons and supporting cells that forms the nervous system, transmitting signals throughout the body.
tissue made of neurons; part of nervous system
medical

Examples

The brain is primarily made up of nervous tissue that enables complex thought and coordination.
Injuries to nervous tissue can result in impaired movement or loss of sensation.
Scientists study nervous tissue to better understand how signals travel through the body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: epithelial tissue - tissue forming protective layers, like skin or linings
  • Synonym: muscle tissue - tissue responsible for movement in the body
  • Synonym: connective tissue - tissue providing support and structure in the body
