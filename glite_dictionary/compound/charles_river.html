Charles River

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃɑrlz ˈrɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːlz ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A river in eastern Massachusetts, USA, flowing into Boston Harbor and forming the boundary between Cambridge and Boston.
river in eastern Massachusetts, USA

Examples

The university campus overlooks the Charles River and provides stunning views of the city skyline.
Rowers gathered early in the morning to practice on the calm waters of the Charles River.
During the annual Head of the Charles Regatta, thousands of spectators line the Charles River banks.
