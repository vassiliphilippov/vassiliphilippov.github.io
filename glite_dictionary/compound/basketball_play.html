basketball play

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskətˌbɔl pleɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtˌbɔːl pleɪ / (BrE)
A planned action or strategy executed by a basketball team during a game to achieve a specific goal, such as scoring or defending.
planned move or tactic in basketball

Examples

The coach designed a new basketball play to break the opponent's zone defense.
They practiced the basketball play over and over until everyone knew their role perfectly.
In the final seconds, a clever basketball play gave the team a last-minute victory.
