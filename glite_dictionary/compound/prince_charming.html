Prince Charming

1. NOUN

/ ˌprɪns ˈtʃɑrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌprɪns ˈtʃɑːmɪŋ / (BrE)
a man who is regarded as an ideal romantic partner, especially one who seems perfect and fulfills all the dreams of the person who loves him, often used with reference to fairy tales.
ideal romantic partner (fairy tales)

Examples

For years, she dreamed of meeting her Prince Charming and living happily ever after.
He tried to play the Prince Charming, always arriving with flowers and sweet words.
To her disappointment, her so-called Prince Charming turned out to be unreliable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knight in shining armor - a brave or heroic man who rescues or helps someone, especially in a romantic way

Etymology

From fairy tales, where a noble prince rescues and wins the heroine.
