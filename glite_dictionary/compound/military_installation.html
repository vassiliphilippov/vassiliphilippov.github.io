military installation

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪləˌtɛri ˌɪnstəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˌɪnstəˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a facility or base that houses and supports military forces or operations
facility serving military forces

Examples

The new military installation was built to improve national security along the border.
Civilians are not permitted to enter the military installation without proper authorization.
Several international treaties restrict the placement of certain weapons in any military installation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military base - a location where military operations are conducted and where troops are housed
