fence energizer

1. NOUN

/ fɛns ˈɛnɚˌdʒaɪzɚ / (AmE), / fɛns ˈɛnədʒaɪzə / (BrE)
A device that supplies electrical power to an electric fence, making it capable of delivering a shock.
device powering an electric fence

Examples

The fence energizer needs to be checked regularly to ensure the fence remains effective.
Without a functioning fence energizer, the electric fence won't deter animals.
They installed a new solar-powered fence energizer to protect the livestock.
