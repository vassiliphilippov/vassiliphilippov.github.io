experimental dish

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˌspɛr.əˈmɛn.t̬əl dɪʃ / (AmE), / ɪkˌspɛr.ɪˈmen.təl dɪʃ / (BrE)
a dish prepared in an innovative or unusual way, often as a test or for creative exploration
unusual or innovative food preparation

Examples

The chef presented an experimental dish featuring unexpected flavor combinations.
She loves trying out a new experimental dish whenever she has guests over.
Our dinner party was a success thanks to everyone bringing one experimental dish to share.

Synset Examples

At our guinea pig night, everyone brought experimental dishes.
