carving knife

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːrvɪŋ naɪf / (AmE), / ˈkɑːvɪŋ naɪf / (BrE)
a large, sharp knife used for slicing cooked meat
knife for carving cooked meat

Examples

Be sure to use the carving knife to slice the turkey at dinner.
He sharpened the carving knife before preparing the roast beef.
The wooden handle on my old carving knife makes it comfortable to use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bread knife - a knife with a serrated edge for slicing bread
  • Synonym: butcher knife - a large knife used by butchers for cutting meat
