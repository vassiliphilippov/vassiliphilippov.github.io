TV show

1. NOUN

/ tiː ˈviː ʃoʊ / (AmE), / tiː ˈviː ʃəʊ / (BrE)
a program or series of programs broadcast on television for entertainment, information, or education
television program or series

Examples

We watched our favorite TV show together every Friday night.
She was invited to appear on a popular TV show as a guest.
The new TV show quickly became a hit among teenagers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: television program - a program that is broadcast by television

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'television show'.
