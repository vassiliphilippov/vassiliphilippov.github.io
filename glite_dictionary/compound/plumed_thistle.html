plumed thistle

1. NOUN

/ plumd ˈθɪsəl / (AmE), / pluːmd ˈθɪsl / (BrE)
A thistle plant, usually biennial or perennial, with showy purple, yellow, or occasionally white flower heads.
species of thistle with colorful flower heads

Examples

The plumed thistle is easily recognized by its spiny leaves and brilliant purple flowers.
Gardeners often appreciate the beauty of the plumed thistle despite its prickly reputation.
Some species of plumed thistle can grow very tall and attract many pollinators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thistle - a plant with prickly leaves and stems, often with a purple flower head
