pocket comb

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkət koʊm / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt kəʊm / (BrE)
a small, portable comb designed to fit conveniently in a pocket for grooming hair on the go
small portable comb for pockets

Examples

He pulled a pocket comb from his jacket to tidy his hair before entering the meeting.
A pocket comb is handy when you need a quick touch-up while traveling.
She always carries a pocket comb in her purse for emergencies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comb - a toothed implement for arranging or cleaning hair
