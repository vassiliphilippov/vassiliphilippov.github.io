personal record

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːsənl ˈrɛkərd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl ˈrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
the best result or highest achievement an individual has ever attained, especially in a particular sport or activity
best achievement by an individual

Examples

She set a new personal record in the 100-meter sprint at yesterday's meet.
Every time I beat my old personal record, I feel a great sense of accomplishment.
He trains hard every week, hoping to achieve a personal record in the upcoming marathon.

Synset Examples

His personal record for deadlift is 400 pounds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personal best - an individual's best achievement in a specific activity
