nothing to do with

1. ADP

/ ˈnʌθɪŋ tə du wɪð / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ tə duː wɪð / (BrE)
having no involvement, connection, or association with someone or something
no involvement or connection

Examples

She made it clear that she had nothing to do with the missing files.
This issue has nothing to do with our original agreement.
I want nothing to do with office politics.

Synset Examples

I have nothing to do with that project anymore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unrelated - not connected or associated
  • Antonym: connected with - having an association or link
