cozy vibe

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊzi vaɪb / (AmE), / ˈkəʊzi vaɪb / (BrE)
a warm, comfortable, and welcoming atmosphere or feeling in a place
a warm and welcoming atmosphere
informal

Examples

The new café has a cozy vibe that makes everyone feel at home.
We added candles and soft music to create a cozy vibe in the living room.
Guests complimented the cozy vibe of the cabin during their weekend stay.

Synset Examples

I love the cozy vibe at Bird and Baby café.
