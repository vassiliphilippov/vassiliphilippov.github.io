search engine

1. NOUN

/ sɝtʃ ˈɛndʒən / (AmE), / sɜːtʃ ˈendʒɪn / (BrE)
A computer program that locates and retrieves information from a database or the internet, especially websites or documents.
internet program for finding information

Examples

Google is the most popular search engine used to find information on the internet.
She used a search engine to locate articles for her research project.
Many websites rely on search engines to attract new visitors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: web browser - software used to access and view websites
  • Degree Lower: database - organized collection of data
