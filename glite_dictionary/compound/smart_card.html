smart card

1. NOUN

/ smɑrt kɑrd / (AmE), / smɑːt kɑːd / (BrE)
a small plastic card with an embedded microprocessor chip, used to store and process data securely, commonly for financial transactions or identification
plastic card with embedded microchip for secure data and transactions

Examples

You can use your smart card to access the building after hours.
The new transit system accepts payments with any smart card.
Many people now store their identification and banking information on a single smart card.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: credit card - plastic payment card issued by a bank, allowing purchases on credit
  • Synonym: debit card - plastic payment card drawing funds directly from a bank account
