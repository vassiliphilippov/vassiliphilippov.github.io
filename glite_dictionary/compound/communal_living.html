communal living

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmjunəl ˈlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnəl ˈlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice or condition of people living together and sharing responsibilities and resources as a community
shared living in a community

Examples

After college, she joined a group committed to communal living in the countryside.
Many older adults appreciate the support and companionship of communal living.
The documentary explored the challenges and rewards of communal living in urban environments.

Synset Examples

The longhouse was built for communal living.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cohousing - a style of housing with private homes and shared spaces
  • Synonym: collective living - living arrangement where members share resources and responsibilities
  • Antonym: individual living - living independently, without sharing household responsibilities with a group
