in-tray

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnˌtreɪ / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌtreɪ / (BrE)
a container, typically kept on a desk, for holding incoming documents or papers to be dealt with
desk tray for incoming papers

Examples

She placed the new invoices in the in-tray for her manager to review.
My in-tray is overflowing with paperwork after the holiday.
Please leave all correspondence in my in-tray and I'll address it this afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: out-tray - tray for outgoing documents or papers
