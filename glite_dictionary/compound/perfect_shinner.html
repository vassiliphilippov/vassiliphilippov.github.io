perfect shinner

1. NOUN

/ pərˈfɛkt ˈʃɪnər / (AmE), / pəˈfɛkt ˈʃɪnə / (BrE)
A skateboarding trick in which the board strikes or lands on the rider's shin, performed with remarkable accuracy or impact.
impressive skateboarding trick hitting the shin

Examples

Despite the pain, he laughed after landing a perfect shinner during his run.
After practicing for hours, she finally nailed a perfect shinner that impressed everyone.
The video showed a skater attempting a trick but ending up with a perfect shinner instead.

Synset Examples

The skater landed a perfect shinner, earning cheers from the crowd.
