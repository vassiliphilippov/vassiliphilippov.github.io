step-by-step

1. ADJ

/ ˌstɛp baɪ ˈstɛp / (AmE), / ˌstɛp baɪ ˈstɛp / (BrE)
happening or done in a series of distinct stages, with each part completed before moving to the next
progressing in distinct stages

Examples

She followed the step-by-step instructions to assemble the furniture.
The guide provides a step-by-step approach to solving the problem.
Learning to cook can be easier with step-by-step recipes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gradual - taking place or progressing slowly or by degrees
  • Antonym: sudden - happening or coming quickly or unexpectedly
