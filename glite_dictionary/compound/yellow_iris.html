yellow iris

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈaɪrɪs / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈaɪrɪs / (BrE)
A common yellow-flowered iris (Iris pseudacorus) native to Europe and North Africa, widely naturalized and often cultivated.
yellow-flowered iris species

Examples

The pond was surrounded by blooming yellow iris in early summer.
Gardeners often plant yellow iris for its showy flowers and hardy nature.
In some regions, yellow iris has become an invasive species near wetlands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iris - a plant genus with sword-shaped leaves and showy flowers
