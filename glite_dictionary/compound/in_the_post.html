in the post

1. ADP

/ ɪn ðə poʊst / (AmE), / ɪn ðə pəʊst / (BrE)
by mail; sent or received through the postal system, especially in British English.
by mail (British English)
british

Examples

My new passport arrived in the post this morning.
She was delighted to find a birthday card in the post from her grandmother.
Please send your completed form in the post to our office address.

Synset Examples

I received a surprise gift in the post yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the mail - sent or received by mail (American English)
