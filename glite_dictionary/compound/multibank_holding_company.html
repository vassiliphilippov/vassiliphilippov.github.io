multibank holding company

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌltiˌbæŋk ˈhoʊldɪŋ ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈmʌltibæŋk ˈhəʊldɪŋ ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a company that owns and controls more than one bank, typically through shareholding arrangements
company owning multiple banks

Examples

The regulations strictly govern how a multibank holding company can acquire smaller financial institutions.
He worked in the legal department of a major multibank holding company headquartered in Chicago.
A multibank holding company may diversify its assets to reduce financial risk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bank holding company - a company that controls one or more banks
