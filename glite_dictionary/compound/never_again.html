never again

1. ADV

/ ˌnɛvɚ əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / ˌnɛvə əˈɡɛn / (BrE)
At no time in the future; not ever after now.
not at any future time

Examples

After that terrible experience, he vowed never again to trust a stranger.
Never again will I eat so much cake in one sitting!
She promised herself she would never again be late for work.

Synset Examples

Quoth the raven, nevermore!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nevermore - at no time in the future; poetic/literary synonym
