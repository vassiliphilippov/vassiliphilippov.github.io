everlasting flower

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛvɚˈlæstɪŋ ˈflaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˌɛvəˈlɑːstɪŋ ˈflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
a plant whose flowers retain their shape and color after drying, used in floral arrangements and decoration
flower that retains form and color when dried

Examples

Dried everlasting flowers added a rustic touch to the living room decor.
Gardeners often plant everlasting flowers for use in winter bouquets.
An arrangement of everlasting flowers can brighten any space long after summer ends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immortelle - a plant with flowers that can be dried without fading
  • Synonym: Helichrysum - genus of plants commonly called everlasting
