mouse nest

1. NOUN

/ maʊs nɛst / (AmE), / maʊs nɛst / (BrE)
a nest built by mice for bearing and raising their young
nest made by mice for young

Examples

The children discovered a mouse nest hidden behind the old boxes in the garage.
Farmers often find a mouse nest in piles of hay during harvest.
A mouse nest is typically made of shredded paper, fabric, or soft materials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nest - structure built by animals for laying eggs or raising young
