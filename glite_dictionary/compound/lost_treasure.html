lost treasure

1. NOUN

/ lɔst ˈtrɛʒər / (AmE), / lɒst ˈtrɛʒə / (BrE)
valuable items such as money, jewels, or artifacts that have been lost, hidden, or are yet to be discovered
valuable items that are missing or hidden

Examples

The children spent the afternoon searching for lost treasure in the old mansion.
Many explorers have devoted their lives to finding lost treasure buried at sea.
The pirate map promised clues to a lost treasure hidden for centuries.

Synset Examples

The legend of the lost treasure intrigued everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treasure - valuable items or wealth, especially when hidden or lost
