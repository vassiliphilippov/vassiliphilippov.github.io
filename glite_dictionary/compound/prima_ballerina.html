prima ballerina

1. NOUN

/ ˈpriːmə bəˈlɪrɪnə / (AmE), / ˈpriːmə bæləˈriːnə / (BrE)
the principal female dancer in a ballet company, typically recognized for her outstanding talent and role leadership
leading female ballet dancer

Examples

After years of dedication, she was finally named the prima ballerina of the national ballet company.
The audience was mesmerized by the performance of the prima ballerina in Swan Lake.
A prima ballerina must possess exceptional technique and stage presence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ballerina - a female ballet dancer

Etymology

From Italian 'prima' (first) + 'ballerina' (female dancer).
