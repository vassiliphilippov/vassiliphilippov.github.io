alcoholic beverage

1. NOUN

/ ˌæl.kəˈhɑ.lɪk ˈbɛv.ɚ.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌæl.kəˈhɒ.lɪk ˈbɛv.ər.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
A drink that contains alcohol, such as beer, wine, or spirits.
a drink containing alcohol

Examples

Consumption of alcoholic beverages is prohibited for those under the legal drinking age.
He ordered an alcoholic beverage to go with his dinner.
Beer, wine, and whiskey are all types of alcoholic beverages.

Synset Examples

alcohol (or drink) ruined him

Related Terms

  • Antonym: soft drink - a non-alcoholic beverage such as soda
  • Synonym: liquor - strong alcoholic drink; distilled spirit
  • Synonym: spirits - strong distilled alcoholic drink
