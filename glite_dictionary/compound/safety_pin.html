safety pin

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪfti pɪn / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti pɪn / (BrE)
A pin with a clasp and cover that shields the point, used to fasten pieces of fabric or clothing safely.
a pin with a clasped guard for safety

Examples

She quickly fastened her scarf with a safety pin before heading out.
The nurse used a safety pin to attach the name tag to his shirt.
I always keep a safety pin in my bag in case of wardrobe malfunctions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pin - a small, slender object for fastening or attaching things
