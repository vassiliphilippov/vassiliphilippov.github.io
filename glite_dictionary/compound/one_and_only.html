one and only

1. ADJ

/ ˌwʌn ənd ˈoʊnli / (AmE), / ˌwʌn ənd ˈəʊnli / (BrE)
used to emphasize that someone or something is unique; without equal or peerless.
unique; without equal

Examples

She is the one and only person who truly understands me.
Everyone cheered when the one and only champion entered the arena.
For his fans, there's no singer like the one and only Elvis Presley.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unique - being the only one of its kind; unlike anything else
  • Synonym: peerless - having no equal; matchless
  • Antonym: ordinary - with no special or distinctive features; normal
  • Antonym: unremarkable - not particularly interesting or surprising; average
