spooky vibe

1. NOUN

/ ˈspuːki vaɪb / (AmE), / ˈspuːki vaɪb / (BrE)
An atmosphere or feeling that is creepy, eerie, or gives a sense of supernatural unease.
creepy or eerie atmosphere

Examples

The abandoned house at the end of the street really has a spooky vibe.
Those old portraits in the hallway contribute to the spooky vibe of the mansion.
As soon as the fog rolled in, the forest took on a spooky vibe.

Synset Examples

The haunted fish tank seemed to have a spooky vibe.
The skull on the shelf gave the room a spooky vibe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creepy - Causing an unpleasant feeling of fear or unease
  • Synonym: eerie - Strangely frightening or mysterious
