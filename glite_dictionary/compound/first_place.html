first place

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɝst pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst pleɪs / (BrE)
the highest position or rank in a competition, event, or ranking
highest position in a competition

Examples

She worked hard all year and finally took first place in the championship.
The winner will receive a trophy for first place.
Our team was thrilled to finish in first place after such a tough season.

Synset Examples

Our science project won first place at the fair.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: second place - the position immediately below first in a ranking or competition
  • Degree Lower: last place - the lowest position in a competition or ranking
