brittle star

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪt̬l̩ stɑr / (AmE), / ˈbrɪtl stɑː / (BrE)
a marine animal resembling a starfish, with long, slender, fragile arms radiating from a small central disc
marine animal with slender, fragile arms

Examples

The brittle star hides beneath rocks during the day to avoid predators.
When threatened, a brittle star can shed one of its arms to escape.
Marine biologists studied the movement patterns of brittle stars on the ocean floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: starfish - a marine animal with radiating arms, also called sea star
