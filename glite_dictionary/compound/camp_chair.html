camp chair

1. NOUN

/ kæmp tʃɛr / (AmE), / kamp tʃɛə / (BrE)
a lightweight folding chair, often used outdoors or when camping
lightweight folding chair for camping

Examples

She unfolded her camp chair and sat by the fire.
Each family member brought a camp chair to the lakeside picnic.
After hiking all day, we relaxed in our camp chairs under the stars.
