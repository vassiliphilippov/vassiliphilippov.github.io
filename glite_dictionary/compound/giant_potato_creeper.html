giant potato creeper

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt pəˈteɪtoʊ ˈkriːpər / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt pəˈteɪtəʊ ˈkriːpə / (BrE)
a vine native to Costa Rica, sparsely armed with hooklike spines and bearing large lilac-blue flowers
vine with hooklike spines and lilac-blue flowers

Examples

The giant potato creeper thrives in the shaded forests of Costa Rica.
Botanists were amazed by the lilac-blue flowers of the giant potato creeper discovered during their expedition.
The hooklike spines of the giant potato creeper make it easy to recognize among other vines.
