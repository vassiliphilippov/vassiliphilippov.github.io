context of use

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑn.tɛkst əv jus / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tɛkst əv juːs / (BrE)
the situation or discourse that surrounds a word, phrase, or utterance and helps determine its meaning or interpretation
surrounding situation affecting meaning

Examples

The meaning of a word often depends on its context of use in a sentence.
In legal documents, the context of use can significantly alter the interpretation of a term.
Teachers should explain vocabulary within the context of use rather than in isolation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: context - the circumstances that form the setting for an event or statement
