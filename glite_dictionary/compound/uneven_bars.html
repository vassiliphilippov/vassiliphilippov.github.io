uneven bars

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈivən bɑrz / (AmE), / ʌnˈiːvən bɑːz / (BrE)
a pair of parallel bars set at different heights, used in women's gymnastics for exercises and routines
gymnastics apparatus with bars at different heights

Examples

The gymnast performed her routine on the uneven bars with remarkable grace and precision.
Watching the uneven bars event is always a highlight of the Olympics for me.
She dismounted from the uneven bars with a perfectly executed double backflip.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: parallel bars - a pair of parallel bars at the same height, used in men's gymnastics
