Modern Greek language

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɑːdɚn ˈɡrik ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌmɒdən ˈɡriːk ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the form of the Greek language spoken and written since the late medieval period, especially as used today in Greece and Cyprus
contemporary form of the Greek language

Examples

Many students choose to study Modern Greek language at university.
The novel was translated from Modern Greek language into English.
He is fluent in both Modern Greek language and English.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Ancient Greek language - the Greek language as spoken and written in ancient times
  • Degree Higher: Greek language - a general term for all varieties of Greek
