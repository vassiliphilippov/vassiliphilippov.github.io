sweet tooth

1. NOUN

/ swit tuθ / (AmE), / swiːt tuːθ / (BrE)
a strong liking or craving for sweet foods
strong liking for sweet foods

Examples

She definitely has a sweet tooth, judging by her love of chocolate cake.
I try to avoid dessert, but my sweet tooth always gets the better of me.
He satisfies his sweet tooth with a piece of fruit after dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweet - having the taste characteristic of sugar or honey; not salty or bitter
  • Synonym: craving - a powerful desire for something
