Tamil Tigers

1. PROPN

/ ˈtæməl ˈtaɪɡərz / (AmE), / ˈtæmɪl ˈtaɪɡəz / (BrE)
a militant organization in Sri Lanka, officially known as the Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam (LTTE), which sought an independent Tamil state through armed struggle from 1976 to 2009, and was known for its use of guerilla warfare and terrorist tactics.
Sri Lankan militant separatist group (LTTE)

Examples

The Tamil Tigers were involved in a decades-long civil conflict in Sri Lanka.
Several countries have designated the Tamil Tigers as a terrorist organization.
The Tamil Tigers were known for their sophisticated guerilla tactics and suicide bombings.

Synset Examples

the Tamil Tigers perfected suicide bombing as a weapon of war

Related Terms

  • Synonym: LTTE - Acronym for Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam, the official name of the Tamil Tigers

Etymology

Refers to the Tamil ethnic group and the tiger, symbolizing strength and national identity; official name: Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam.
