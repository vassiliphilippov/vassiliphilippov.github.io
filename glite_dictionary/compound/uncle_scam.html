Uncle Scam

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌŋkəl skæm / (AmE), / ˈʌŋkl skam / (BrE)
a person who deceives others, especially by fraudulent schemes; a con artist
deceptive or fraudulent person
informal slang

Examples

After losing money in the fake investment, she realized she'd trusted Uncle Scam instead of a legitimate adviser.
Watch out for emails from Uncle Scam promising to double your money overnight.
The news warned citizens about new schemes run by so-called Uncle Scam.

Synset Examples

He warned me about Uncle Scam and his fake lottery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scammer - a person who cheats or defrauds others
  • Synonym: con artist - a person who deceives others by gaining their trust

Etymology

Formed as a pun on "Uncle Sam" (the personification of the U.S. government) and "scam" (a fraudulent scheme).
