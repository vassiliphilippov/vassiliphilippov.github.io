bed covering

1. NOUN

/ bɛd ˈkʌvərɪŋ / (AmE), / bɛd ˈkʌvərɪŋ / (BrE)
A decorative or protective cover, such as a blanket, quilt, or bedspread, used on a bed.
decorative/protective cover for a bed

Examples

She bought a new bed covering to match the color of her curtains.
The hotel provides an elegant bed covering in every room for guests' comfort.
After washing the sheets, she carefully arranged the bed covering back in place.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedspread - a decorative cloth covering for a bed
  • Synonym: blanket - a large piece of thick cloth used for warmth as a bed covering
  • Synonym: duvet - a soft quilt filled with down, feathers, or synthetic fiber, used as a bed covering
