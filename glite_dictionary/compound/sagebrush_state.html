Sagebrush State

1. PROPN

/ ˈseɪdʒˌbrʌʃ steɪt / (AmE), / ˈseɪdʒˌbrʌʃ steɪt / (BrE)
A nickname for the U.S. state of Nevada, referring to the abundance of sagebrush in the region.
nickname for Nevada, U.S. state

Examples

Nevada is often called the Sagebrush State because of its native plant life.
Tourists visiting the Sagebrush State can explore vast deserts and scenic mountains.
The history of the Sagebrush State is closely tied to mining and ranching.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nevada - A state in the western United States; also called the Sagebrush State

Etymology

Refers to the prevalence of sagebrush plants in Nevada.
