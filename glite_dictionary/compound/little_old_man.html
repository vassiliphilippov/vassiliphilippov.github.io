little old man

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬l oʊld mæn / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl əʊld mæn / (BrE)
an elderly man, often referred to with affection or emphasizing small size and age
elderly man (affectionate or descriptive)

Examples

The kind little old man gave candies to the neighborhood children every Halloween.
We saw a little old man feeding pigeons in the park every morning.
Everyone in the town knew the little old man who wore a red hat and walked with a cane.

Synset Examples

He helped the little old man cross the street safely.
