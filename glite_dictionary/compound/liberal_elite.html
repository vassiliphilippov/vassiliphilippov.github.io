liberal elite

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪbɚəl ɪˈlit / (AmE), / ˈlɪb(ə)rəl ɪˈliːt / (BrE)
a group of influential people who hold liberal political views, often perceived as being socially and economically privileged
influential group with liberal views

Examples

Many critics accuse the liberal elite of being out of touch with ordinary voters.
The debate was dominated by members of the liberal elite from top universities.
Some newspapers claim the liberal elite shapes public opinion on key social issues.

Synset Examples

The liberal elite often influence public policy decisions.
The conference was attended by the liberal elite of the city.
Critics argue that the liberal elite are disconnected from everyday issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: establishment - the influential and dominant group in society
  • Antonym: conservative elite - a group of influential people with conservative views
