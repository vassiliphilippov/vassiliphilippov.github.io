alphabet mafia

1. NOUN

/ ˈæl.fə.bɛt ˈmɑ.fi.ə / (AmE), / ˈæl.fə.bɛt ˈmɑː.fi.ə / (BrE)
(slang, informal) A humorous or sometimes critical term referring to a group of influential LGBTQ+ individuals or activists believed to exert influence in media, politics, or corporations.
powerful LGBTQ+ group (slang, informal)
slang informal

Examples

Some critics claim the alphabet mafia is pushing for too many changes in corporate policies.
The comedian made jokes about the influence of the alphabet mafia in Hollywood.
She accused the alphabet mafia of censoring opposing viewpoints in the media.

Synset Examples

He joked that the alphabet mafia runs the tech industry.
The documentary exposed the power of the alphabet mafia in politics.
The alphabet mafia influences many decisions in corporate America.

Etymology

Based on 'alphabet' (referring to LGBTQ+ acronyms) and 'mafia' (suggesting secret or influential groups).
