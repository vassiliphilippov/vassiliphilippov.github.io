household arts

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊshoʊld ɑrts / (AmE), / ˈhaʊshəʊld ɑːts / (BrE)
the practical skills and techniques involved in managing a home, such as cooking, sewing, and housekeeping
practical skills for managing a home

Examples

She took classes in household arts to improve her cooking and sewing skills.
The school's curriculum included household arts as a way to prepare students for everyday life.
Interest in household arts has increased with the popularity of home renovation and cooking shows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: home economics - the study or practice of managing a household and its finances
