steel mill

1. NOUN

/ stiːl mɪl / (AmE), / stiːl mɪl / (BrE)
a factory where steel is manufactured from raw materials
factory where steel is made

Examples

The old steel mill was closed down after decades of production.
Many workers in the town are employed at the local steel mill.
A new steel mill was constructed to boost the region's economy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ironworks - a factory where iron or steel is processed
