Supreme Allied Commander Europe

1. PROPN

/ səˈprim ˈælaɪd kəˈmændər juˈroʊp / (AmE), / suːˈpriːm ˈælaɪd kəˈmændə juːˈrəʊp / (BrE)
the senior military commander in Europe for NATO, responsible for overseeing all Allied Command Operations forces in the region
NATO's highest military commander in Europe

Examples

The Supreme Allied Commander Europe holds ultimate authority over NATO's military operations on the continent.
General Dwight D. Eisenhower was the first Supreme Allied Commander Europe during the early days of NATO.
Appointments to Supreme Allied Commander Europe are made by consensus among NATO member countries.
