Brunswick stew

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrʌnzwɪk stu / (AmE), / ˈbrʌnzwɪk stjuː / (BrE)
a spicy Southern American stew made with chicken or small game, corn, tomatoes, lima beans, okra, onions, and potatoes
spicy Southern American stew with meat and vegetables

Examples

We served Brunswick stew at the family picnic, and everyone loved its hearty flavor.
After a long day outdoors, nothing beats a bowl of Brunswick stew to warm you up.
On our trip through Georgia, we made sure to try authentic Brunswick stew at a local diner.

Etymology

Named after Brunswick County, Virginia, or Brunswick, Georgia, in the United States, or possibly after Brunswick in Germany.
