tennis pro

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnɪs ˌproʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs ˌprəʊ / (BrE)
a professional tennis player or a person who teaches tennis for a living
professional tennis player or instructor

Examples

After years of training, she finally became a tennis pro at a prestigious club.
The tennis pro gave lessons to beginners every weekend.
He dreams of competing as a tennis pro on the international circuit one day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: professional - a person who earns a living in a sport rather than as an amateur
