soyjak image

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔɪˌdʒæk ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪˌdʒæk ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
an internet meme image featuring a cartoonish male figure with exaggerated expressions, used to convey emotions or mock reactions online
internet meme image for emotions or mockery
informal slang

Examples

Many users posted a soyjak image in response to the surprising news.
The heated discussion thread was quickly filled with various soyjak images.
He created a customized soyjak image to mock the comment.

Synset Examples

People used the soyjak image to express their frustrations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wojak - meme character often used in similar internet memes

Etymology

Blend of 'soy' (from soyboy, derogatory internet slang) and 'wojak' (a popular internet cartoon figure).
