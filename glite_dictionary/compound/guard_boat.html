guard boat

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑrd boʊt / (AmE), / ɡɑːd bəʊt / (BrE)
a boat assigned to patrol and protect a harbor or fleet, especially around warships
patrol boat for harbor or fleet protection

Examples

A guard boat circled the harbor entrance, watching for any suspicious activity.
During the naval exercise, several guard boats were stationed around the fleet.
The port authority deployed a guard boat to ensure the area remained secure overnight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patrol boat - a small naval vessel used for patrolling coastal areas or harbors
