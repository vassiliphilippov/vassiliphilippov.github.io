bilingual dictionary

1. NOUN

/ baɪˈlɪŋɡwəl ˈdɪkʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / baɪˈlɪŋɡwəl ˈdɪkʃənəri / (BrE)
A dictionary that provides translations of words between two different languages.
dictionary giving translations between two languages

Examples

Many students use a bilingual dictionary to help them learn new vocabulary.
She bought a French-English bilingual dictionary before moving to Paris.
Modern bilingual dictionaries often include grammar tips and usage notes.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: monolingual dictionary - a dictionary providing definitions in one language only

Etymology

From Latin 'bi-' (two) + 'lingua' (tongue, language) + 'dictionary'.
