steady-going

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɛdiˌɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɛdiˌɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
calm, reliable, and consistent in actions or behavior; not easily disturbed or excitable
calm and reliable in behavior

Examples

Despite stressful situations, she always remained steady-going and composed.
A steady-going employee is highly valued by any organization for their dependability.
Their father was a steady-going sort who rarely lost his temper.

Synset Examples

dependable in one's habits
a steady-going family man

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steady - firm, regular, or constant in character or behavior
