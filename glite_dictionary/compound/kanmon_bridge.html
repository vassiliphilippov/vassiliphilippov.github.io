Kanmon Bridge

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑːn.moʊn brɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkan.mɒn brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a suspension bridge connecting the Japanese islands of Kyushu and Honshu across the Kanmon Straits
suspension bridge in Japan between Kyushu and Honshu

Examples

The Kanmon Bridge offers breathtaking views of the Kanmon Straits between Kyushu and Honshu.
During my trip to Japan, I drove across the Kanmon Bridge to explore both islands.
The construction of the Kanmon Bridge greatly improved transportation links between southern and central Japan.

Etymology

From Japanese 'Kanmon' (the Kanmon Straits), combining 'Kan' (from Shimonoseki) and 'Mon' (from Moji).
