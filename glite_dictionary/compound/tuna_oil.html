tuna oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːnə ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈtjuːnə ɔɪl / (BrE)
an oil obtained from the tissues of tuna fish, often used as a nutritional supplement or ingredient in food products
oil extracted from tuna fish

Examples

Some dietary supplements contain tuna oil because it is rich in omega-3 fatty acids.
The company uses tuna oil as a flavor enhancer in its canned seafood products.
Researchers studied the health benefits of tuna oil extracted from Pacific tuna.
