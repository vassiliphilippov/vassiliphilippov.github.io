Dari Persian

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑːri pərˈʒən / (AmE), / ˈdɑːri pəˈʃɜːn / (BrE)
the variety of the Persian language spoken primarily in Afghanistan
Afghan variety of Persian language

Examples

Many government documents in Afghanistan are written in Dari Persian.
She learned to speak Dari Persian after moving to Kabul for work.
As one of the two official languages of Afghanistan, Dari Persian holds significant cultural importance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persian - the Iranian language spoken in Iran, also known as Farsi

Etymology

Dari: from Persian 'Darī', a name for Persian prose, combined with 'Persian' for the language family.

2. NOUN

/ ˈdɑːri ˈpɜːrʒən / (AmE), / ˈdɑːri ˈpɜːʃən / (BrE)
the variety of the Persian language spoken mainly in Afghanistan
Iranian language variety of Afghanistan

Examples

Dari Persian is one of the two official languages of Afghanistan.
Many government documents in Afghanistan are written in Dari Persian.
She learned to speak Dari Persian fluently after living in Kabul for several years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persian - the Iranian language of Iran, Tajikistan, and Afghanistan
  • Synonym: Farsi - the Persian language as spoken in Iran

Etymology

From Persian 'dari' (court, royal) and 'Persian' (related to Persia).
