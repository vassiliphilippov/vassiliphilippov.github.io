corrugated cardboard

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɔrəˌɡeɪtɪd ˈkɑrdˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˌkɒrəˌɡeɪtɪd ˈkɑːdˌbɔːd / (BrE)
stiff, strong cardboard made of a fluted corrugated sheet and one or two flat linerboards, often used for packing boxes
strong cardboard with a corrugated layer

Examples

We used corrugated cardboard to make the moving boxes because it's durable.
The packaging industry relies heavily on corrugated cardboard for shipping products safely.
Artists often use recycled corrugated cardboard as a base for their sculptures.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: cardboard - stiff, paper-based material used for boxes
