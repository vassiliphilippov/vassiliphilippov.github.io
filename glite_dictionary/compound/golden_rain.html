golden rain

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊl.dən reɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊl.dən reɪn / (BrE)
an ornamental shrub or small tree of the genus Laburnum, grown for its pendulous clusters of yellow flowers, often used in gardens and for Easter decorations
an ornamental shrub or tree (Laburnum) with yellow flowers

Examples

The spectacular yellow blossoms of golden rain brighten many spring gardens.
We planted a golden rain tree near the front gate last year.
During Easter, people often decorate with branches from golden rain shrubs.
