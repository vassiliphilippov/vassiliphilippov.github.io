fruit compote

1. NOUN

/ fruːt kəmˈpoʊt / (AmE), / fruːt kɒmˈpəʊt / (BrE)
A dessert made by stewing or baking pieces of fruit in sugar syrup, sometimes with added spices or liqueur.
dessert of stewed or baked fruit

Examples

For dessert, she served a delicious fruit compote prepared with pears and apricots.
The recipe called for a warm fruit compote to top the vanilla ice cream.
A bowl of fruit compote makes a refreshing breakfast or snack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stewed fruit - fruit cooked by boiling in water or syrup

Etymology

From French 'compote' (mixture made of stewed fruit), from Latin 'compositus' (put together).
