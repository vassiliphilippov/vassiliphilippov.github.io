jail delivery

1. NOUN

/ dʒeɪl dɪˈlɪvəri / (AmE), / dʒeɪl dɪˈlɪvəri / (BrE)
the act of using force to free prisoners from jail; a forcible release of inmates from incarceration.
forcible release of prisoners from jail
legal

Examples

The outlaws staged a jail delivery in the middle of the night, helping their leader escape.
After the jail delivery, the authorities launched a massive manhunt for the fugitives.
The judge described the jail delivery as a serious threat to the rule of law.
