meth habit

1. NOUN

/ mɛθ ˈhæbɪt / (AmE), / mɛθ ˈhæbɪt / (BrE)
An addiction to methamphetamine; a strong and harmful dependence on the drug meth.
addiction to methamphetamine

Examples

After years struggling with his meth habit, he finally sought professional help.
Her meth habit cost her both her job and her family.
The support group helps people break free from a meth habit.

Synset Examples

She lost everything to her meth habit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: addiction - the fact or condition of being addicted to a substance or activity
