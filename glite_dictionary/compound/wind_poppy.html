wind poppy

1. NOUN

/ wɪnd ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / wɪnd ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
a wildflower native to California, known for its slender stems and bright red, cup-shaped flowers
Californian wildflower with red flowers

Examples

The hills were dotted with vibrant wind poppies after the spring rains.
Hikers stopped to photograph a cluster of wind poppy blossoms along the trail.
Because the wind poppy is so delicate, it sways easily in the slightest breeze.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poppy - a plant with showy, colorful flowers, often with medicinal or narcotic properties
