Excited States of America

1. PROPN

/ ɪkˈsaɪt̬ɪd steɪts əv əˈmɛrɪkə / (AmE), / ɪkˈsaɪtɪd steɪts əv əˈmɛrɪkə / (BrE)
A humorous or sarcastic term for the United States, often used to comment on its energetic or exaggerated culture or behavior.
humorous term for the United States

Examples

Many comedians refer to the country as the Excited States of America when discussing its media scandals.
Some journalists jokingly call it the Excited States of America to highlight political hysteria.
Her blog post humorously critiqued the culture of the Excited States of America.

Synset Examples

The festival celebrated the culture of the Excited States of America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: United States of America - the official name of the country commonly known as the United States

Etymology

Play on 'United States of America', replacing 'United' with 'Excited' for humorous effect.
