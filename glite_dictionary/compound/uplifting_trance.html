uplifting trance

1. NOUN

/ ʌpˈlɪftɪŋ træns / (AmE), / ʌpˈlɪftɪŋ træns / (BrE)
a subgenre of trance music characterized by emotional melodies, energetic rhythms, and a euphoric or 'uplifting' feeling
a subgenre of euphoric trance music

Examples

Many club DJs prefer uplifting trance sets for peak hours to energize the crowd.
Her playlist includes classic uplifting trance tracks that are popular at music festivals.
Fans of uplifting trance appreciate its soaring melodies and positive vibes.

Synset Examples

Instead of the darker tone of Goa, uplifting trance uses similar chord progressions as progressive trance, but tracks' chord progressions usually rest on a major chord, and the balance between major and minor chords in a progression will determine how ‘happy’ or ‘sad’ the progression sounds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trance - a genre of electronic dance music
