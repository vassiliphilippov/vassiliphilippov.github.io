equipment failure

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈkwɪpmənt ˌfeɪljɚ / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪpmənt ˌfeɪl.jə / (BrE)
a situation where a machine or device stops working properly or ceases normal operation.
cessation of proper function in equipment

Examples

The factory halted production due to an unexpected equipment failure on the assembly line.
Routine maintenance can greatly reduce the risk of equipment failure in critical systems.
The plane was delayed after the pilot reported a minor equipment failure during preflight checks.

Synset Examples

there was a power breakdown

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breakdown - a mechanical or electrical failure
