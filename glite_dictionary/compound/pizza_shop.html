pizza shop

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːtsə ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈpiːtsə ʃɒp / (BrE)
a shop or restaurant where pizzas are made and sold to customers
shop selling pizzas

Examples

We decided to meet at the new pizza shop on Main Street after work.
The pizza shop was crowded on Friday night as families came for dinner.
He got a part-time job at a local pizza shop during the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pizzeria - a restaurant or shop specializing in pizzas
