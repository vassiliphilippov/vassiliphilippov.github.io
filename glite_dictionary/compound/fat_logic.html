fat logic

1. NOUN

/ fæt ˈlɑːdʒɪk / (AmE), / fæt ˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
unreasonable or flawed reasoning used to justify improper actions or decisions, especially when these justifications are self-serving
unjustified self-serving reasoning
informal

Examples

Instead of admitting her mistake, she relied on fat logic to defend her actions.
The article exposes the fat logic people use to avoid responsibility for their choices.
He tried to convince us with fat logic, but nobody accepted his explanation.

Synset Examples

They dismissed his claims as mere fat logic.
Using fat logic, she justified her excessive spending.
His reasoning was full of fat logic that didn't add up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rationalization - a defense mechanism in which controversial behaviors or feelings are justified and explained in a seemingly rational or logical manner to avoid the true explanation
  • Synonym: excuse - a reason or explanation given to justify a fault or offense
