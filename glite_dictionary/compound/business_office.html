business office

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs ˈɔfɪs / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
A place where administrative, clerical, or professional work is carried out as part of a business.
administrative workplace in a business

Examples

She works in the main business office downtown.
The company expanded and opened a new business office on the west side of the city.
Documents should be submitted to the business office for processing.

Synset Examples

he rented an office in the new building

Related Terms

  • Synonym: office - a room or building where people work at desks, usually as part of a business or other organization
