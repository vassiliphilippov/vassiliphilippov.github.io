historical record

1. NOUN

/ hɪˈstɔr.ɪ.kəl ˈrɛk.ərd / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒr.ɪ.kəl ˈrɛk.ɔːd / (BrE)
A written account or document providing authentic information about past events, regarded as reliable evidence of history.
authentic documentation of past events

Examples

The treaty is mentioned in every major historical record from the period.
Without a historical record, it is difficult to verify the authenticity of this legend.
Researchers rely on the historical record to reconstruct key events of the past.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archive - a collection of historical documents or records
  • Synonym: chronicle - a factual written account of historical events in order of time
  • Antonym: myth - a traditional story not based on fact
