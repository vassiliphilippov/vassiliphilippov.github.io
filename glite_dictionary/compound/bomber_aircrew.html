bomber aircrew

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːmər ˈɛrˌkruː / (AmE), / ˈbɒmə ˈɛəˌkruː / (BrE)
the members of staff responsible for operating and managing a bomber aircraft during flight
crew operating a bomber aircraft

Examples

During World War II, the bomber aircrew faced significant risks on each mission.
Training for a bomber aircrew requires teamwork and precision in high-pressure situations.
He joined the bomber aircrew as a navigator, responsible for mapping out the route.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bomber crew - personnel who operate and serve on a bomber aircraft
