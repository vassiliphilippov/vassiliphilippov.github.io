genus Coelogyne

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːlədʒɪˌniː / (AmE), / ˈsiːlədʒɪˌniː / (BrE)
A large and diverse genus of tropical Asiatic epiphytic orchids, known for their decorative flowers.
genus of tropical Asiatic orchids

Examples

Many collectors prize genus Coelogyne for its unusual and fragrant blooms.
Several species in the genus Coelogyne are grown as ornamental plants in greenhouses.
The tropical forests of Southeast Asia are home to the genus Coelogyne.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchid - a diverse and widespread family of flowering plants

Etymology

From Greek 'koilos' (hollow) and 'gyne' (female), referring to the hollow stigma of the flower.
