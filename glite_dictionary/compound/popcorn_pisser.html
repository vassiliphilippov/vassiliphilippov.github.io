popcorn pisser

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑp.kɔrn ˈpɪs.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɒp.kɔːn ˈpɪs.ə / (BrE)
(slang) A person who is easily frightened, overly anxious, or reacts with extreme amusement or excitement, especially during movies or events.
person who overreacts to films or events
slang vulgar

Examples

Whenever there's a tense moment in a horror film, he's the biggest popcorn pisser in the room.
She laughed so hard during the comedy that everyone called her a popcorn pisser.
If you scream at every jump scare, you'll earn the title of popcorn pisser among your friends.
