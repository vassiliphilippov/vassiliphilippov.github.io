life advice

1. NOUN

/ laɪf ədˈvaɪs / (AmE), / laɪf ədˈvaɪs / (BrE)
guidance or recommendations offered to help someone with decisions and challenges in their personal life.
guidance on personal matters

Examples

My grandmother always gave the best life advice when I was struggling with big decisions.
He started a blog to share his life advice with young professionals.
People often turn to friends for honest life advice during difficult times.

Synset Examples

Everyone respects the Ah Mm for her life advice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guidance - advice or information aimed at resolving a problem or difficulty
  • Synonym: counsel - advice given formally, especially on personal matters
