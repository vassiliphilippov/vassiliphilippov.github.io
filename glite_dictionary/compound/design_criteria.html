design criteria

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈzaɪn kraɪˈtɪəriə / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪn kraɪˈtɪəriə / (BrE)
the standards or requirements that a designer must satisfy when creating a system, product, or structure
requirements for design

Examples

The architect outlined the design criteria for the new library to ensure it was both functional and sustainable.
Our team discussed the design criteria before starting work on the prototype.
Meeting the safety design criteria was essential for the bridge's construction approval.

Synset Examples

the job specifications summarized the design criteria

Related Terms

  • Synonym: specifications - a detailed description of the criteria and requirements for a design or product
