sponge mushroom

1. NOUN

/ spʌndʒ ˈmʌʃrʊm / (AmE), / spʌndʒ ˈmʌʃrəm / (BrE)
an edible morel with a rounded or elongated head marked by an irregular pattern of pits and ridges
edible morel mushroom with a pitted head

Examples

We found a sponge mushroom in the woods during our hike.
Many chefs prize the sponge mushroom for its rich, earthy flavor in gourmet dishes.
Always be sure you've correctly identified a sponge mushroom before eating it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: morel - an edible wild mushroom with a honeycombed cap
