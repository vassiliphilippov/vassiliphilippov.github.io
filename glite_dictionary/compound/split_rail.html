split rail

1. NOUN

/ splɪt reɪl / (AmE), / splɪt reɪl / (BrE)
a rail made by splitting a log lengthwise, typically used in building fences
rail made by splitting a log for fences

Examples

The old farm was surrounded by a split rail fence that had weathered many seasons.
He used sturdy oak logs to make each split rail by hand.
They restored the historic boundary using traditional split rail fencing techniques.
