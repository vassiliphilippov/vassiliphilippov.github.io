cant dog

1. NOUN

/ kænt dɔg / (AmE), / kænt dɒg / (BrE)
A sturdy lever with a pointed end, used for moving or handling logs.
tool for moving logs

Examples

The worker used a cant dog to roll the heavy log into place.
Loggers often carry a cant dog when working in the forest.
A cant dog makes moving timber much safer and easier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peavey - a lever with a pointed end and hook for handling logs
  • Synonym: cant hook - a logging tool like a lever with a blunt end and hook
