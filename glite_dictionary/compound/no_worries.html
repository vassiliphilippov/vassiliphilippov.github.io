no worries

1. X

/ noʊ ˈwɝːriz / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈwʌriz / (BrE)
used to reassure someone that there is no problem or cause for concern; it's okay
expression meaning "it's not a problem"
informal

Examples

If you can't make it to the meeting, no worries—I'll fill you in later.
Thanks for your help! — No worries, happy to assist.
She apologized for the delay, but I told her no worries.

Synset Examples

If you're late, no worries, I'll wait.
You spilled coffee? No worries, it happens.
I forgot your birthday? No worries, we can celebrate now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no problem - expression meaning 'it's not a problem'
  • Synonym: don't worry - expression telling someone to relax or not be concerned
