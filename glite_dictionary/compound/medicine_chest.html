medicine chest

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪsən tʃɛst / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪsɪn tʃɛst / (BrE)
a small cabinet, often kept in a bathroom, used for storing medicines and toiletries
cabinet for medicines and toiletries

Examples

She opened the medicine chest to find some antiseptic cream for her cut.
Please keep all prescription drugs in the medicine chest away from children.
There were several bottles of cough syrup stored neatly in the medicine chest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medicine cabinet - a cabinet for storing medicines, often in a bathroom
