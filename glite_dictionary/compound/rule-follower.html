rule-follower

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːl ˌfɑl.oʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈruːl ˌfɒl.ə / (BrE)
a person who strictly obeys or adheres to established rules or regulations
person who strictly follows rules

Examples

As a rule-follower, she always made sure to submit her assignments ahead of the deadline.
He is known as a rule-follower and rarely challenges authority.
Even in informal situations, being a rule-follower makes her double-check what the protocol is.

Synset Examples

She’s such a rule-follower that she even checks her email at midnight.
Being a rule-follower helped him earn a promotion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stickler - a person who insists on strict adherence to rules or details
  • Antonym: rule-breaker - a person who disregards or violates rules
