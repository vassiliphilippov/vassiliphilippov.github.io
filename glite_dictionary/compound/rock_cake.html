rock cake

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑk keɪk / (AmE), / ˈrɒk keɪk / (BrE)
A small, rough-textured cake, usually made with flour, sugar, dried fruit, and baking powder, with a hard surface said to resemble a rock.
small, hard-surfaced cake with dried fruit

Examples

She baked a batch of rock cakes for the school bake sale.
The rock cake was crunchy on the outside but soft in the middle.
My grandmother taught me her special recipe for rock cakes.
