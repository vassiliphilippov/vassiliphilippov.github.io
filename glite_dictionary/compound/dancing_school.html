dancing school

1. NOUN

/ ˈdænsɪŋ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnsɪŋ skuːl / (BrE)
a school where students are taught to dance, often in various styles
school where dance is taught

Examples

She enrolled her daughter in a dancing school to learn ballet and jazz.
Many children attend dancing school after their regular classes.
The dancing school offers lessons for both beginners and advanced students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dance studio - a place where dance is practiced and taught

/ ˈdænsɪŋ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnsɪŋ skuːl / (BrE)

Examples

The local dancing school offers classes in ballet, jazz, and hip-hop for all ages.
