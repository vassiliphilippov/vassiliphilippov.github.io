pen nib

1. NOUN

/ pɛn nɪb / (AmE), / pɛn nɪb / (BrE)
the pointed metal part at the tip of a pen that draws ink onto the writing surface
tip of a pen that touches paper

Examples

He carefully replaced the pen nib when it became worn out.
A broken pen nib can make writing difficult and messy.
Calligraphers often choose different pen nibs to create a variety of line styles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nib - the pointed end part of a pen, especially a fountain pen
