congratulations letter

1. NOUN

/ kənˌɡrætʃuˈleɪʃənz ˈlɛtər / (AmE), / kənˌɡratjʊˈleɪʃənz ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
an official letter recognizing and honoring someone's achievements or success
formal letter recognizing achievement

Examples

The dean sent her a congratulations letter for graduating with honors.
After winning the award, he received a formal congratulations letter from the company’s CEO.
A congratulations letter was included with the certificate to acknowledge his achievement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: letter of commendation - an official letter praising someone's actions or achievements
