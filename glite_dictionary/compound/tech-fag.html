tech-fag

1. NOUN

/ tɛk fæɡ / (AmE), / tɛk faɡ / (BrE)
(Offensive slang) A derogatory term for an enthusiast or devotee of technology, especially someone perceived as overly interested in technical matters.
offensive slang: tech enthusiast (derogatory)
offensive vulgar slang

Examples

Some forums are hostile toward newcomers, especially if they're labeled a tech-fag.
He was mocked as a tech-fag for his obsession with the latest gadgets.
The new employee was dismissed as just another tech-fag by his colleagues.

Synset Examples

The new design feels very much like a tech-fag.
