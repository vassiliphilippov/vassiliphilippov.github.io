multiplier onion

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌltɪˌplaɪər ˈʌnjən / (AmE), / ˈmʌltɪˌplaɪə ˈʌnjən / (BrE)
a type of onion plant that produces small, clustered, mild-flavored bulbs used as a seasoning
onion with clustered, mild bulbs

Examples

She prefers to plant multiplier onion in her garden for their reliable yields.
The chef finely chopped some multiplier onion to add a mild flavor to the salad.
You can store multiplier onion bulbs through the winter for use in stews and soups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shallot - a type of onion with clustered bulbs and mild flavor
