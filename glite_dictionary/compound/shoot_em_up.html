shoot 'em up

1. NOUN

/ ʃuːt ɛm ʌp / (AmE), / ʃuːt ɛm ʌp / (BrE)
a genre of action film or video game involving a lot of shooting, typically featuring continual combat against enemies
action genre focused on shooting

Examples

He spent the evening playing his favorite shoot 'em up on the old arcade machine.
The movie is a nonstop shoot 'em up packed with explosions and gunfights.
Many classic video games from the 1980s are considered shoot 'em ups.

Synset Examples

They spent the evening playing a shoot 'em up arcade game.
The new movie is a classic shoot 'em up with great effects.
He enjoys playing shoot 'em up games on his console.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shooter - video game genre where shooting is central gameplay

Etymology

Colloquial shortening of 'shoot them up', popularized in the mid-20th century by reference to Western films and arcade games.
