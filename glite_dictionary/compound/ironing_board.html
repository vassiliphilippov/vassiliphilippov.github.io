ironing board

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪərnɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈaɪənɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
A long, narrow, padded board on collapsible supports, used as a surface for ironing clothes.
padded board for ironing clothes

Examples

She set up the ironing board in the living room to press her dress.
After folding the ironing board, he stored it in the closet.
The hotel room included an ironing board and an iron for guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ironing table - a table used for ironing clothes
