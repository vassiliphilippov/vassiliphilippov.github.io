genus Chaetodon

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs kiːˈtəʊdɒn / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs kiːˈtəʊdɒn / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Chaetodontidae, comprising numerous species of marine fishes known as butterflyfishes.
type genus of butterflyfishes

Examples

The genus Chaetodon includes many of the colorful butterflyfishes found in coral reefs worldwide.
Marine biologists often study the genus Chaetodon to understand patterns of reef biodiversity.
Several new species have been identified within the genus Chaetodon in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Chaetodontidae - family of marine fishes known as butterflyfishes

Etymology

From Greek 'chaite' (hair/bristle) and 'odōn' (tooth), referencing the bristle-like teeth of these fishes.
