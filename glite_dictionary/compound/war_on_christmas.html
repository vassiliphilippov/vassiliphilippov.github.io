war on Christmas

1. NOUN

/ wɔr ɑn ˈkrɪsməs / (AmE), / wɔː(r) ɒn ˈkrɪsməs / (BrE)
an alleged campaign against the traditional celebration or public expression of Christmas, often debated in relation to commercialization and secularization
debate over the celebration/meaning of Christmas

Examples

Some believe there's a war on Christmas happening in society today.
Debates about the war on Christmas often emerge in the media each holiday season.
The mayor denied participating in any so-called war on Christmas after complaints from residents.

Synset Examples

Some believe there's a war on Christmas in today's society.
Debates about the war on Christmas spark strong opinions.
The local news reported on the ongoing war on Christmas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: culture war - a conflict between groups with different ideals, beliefs, and philosophies
