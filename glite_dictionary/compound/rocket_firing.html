rocket firing

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑːkɪt ˌfaɪɹɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪt ˌfaɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the launching of a rocket or missile under its own power
the act of launching a rocket or missile

Examples

The first rocket firing of the mission was a complete success.
Engineers prepared for the scheduled rocket firing at dawn.
During the test, the sudden rocket firing startled the observers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: launch - to send a rocket or missile into the air or space
