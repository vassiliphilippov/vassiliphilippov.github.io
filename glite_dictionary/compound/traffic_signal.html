traffic signal

1. NOUN

/ ˈtræfɪk ˌsɪɡnəl / (AmE), / ˈtræfɪk ˌsɪɡnəl / (BrE)
A visual device that controls the flow of vehicles and pedestrians at road intersections by displaying colored lights.
device controlling road traffic with lights

Examples

We had to stop at the traffic signal because it turned red.
The traffic signal at the intersection was not working this morning.
Pedestrians can cross safely when the traffic signal indicates it is their turn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traffic light - a colored light used to control traffic at intersections
