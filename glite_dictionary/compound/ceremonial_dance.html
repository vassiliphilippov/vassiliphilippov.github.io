ceremonial dance

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛrəˈmoʊniəl dæns / (AmE), / ˌsɛrəˈməʊniəl dɑːns / (BrE)
a dance performed as part of a religious or ritual ceremony
ritual or religious dance

Examples

The tribe performed a sacred ceremonial dance to honor their ancestors.
Visitors were mesmerized by the colorful ceremonial dance during the festival.
Each region has its own unique form of ceremonial dance passed down through generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ritual dance - a dance performed as part of a traditional or religious ceremony
