baseball club

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl klʌb / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl klʌb / (BrE)
a team of professional baseball players who play and travel together as an organized unit, especially in official competitions
team of professional baseball players

Examples

The city is proud to support its local baseball club throughout the season.
He signed his first professional contract with a famous baseball club in New York.
The baseball club traveled across the country for their annual tournament.

Synset Examples

each club played six home games with teams in its own division
