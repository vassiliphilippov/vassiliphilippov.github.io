naut mi

1. NOUN

/ nɔt maɪ / (AmE), / nɔːt maɪ / (BrE)
a unit of length used in navigation, equal to exactly 1,852 meters or about 1.1508 statute miles
unit of length (1,852 meters) for navigation

Examples

The ship reported its position as five naut mi from the harbor entrance.
Pilots rely on naut mi when calculating flight distances over water.
The map's scale is marked in both kilometers and naut mi for international use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nautical mile - unit of length used in navigation, equal to 1,852 meters
  • Antonym: statute mile - standard mile used on land, equal to 1,609.34 meters

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'nautical mile'.
