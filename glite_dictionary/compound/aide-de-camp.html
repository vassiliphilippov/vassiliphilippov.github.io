aide-de-camp

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪd də ˈkæmp / (AmE), / ˌeɪd də ˈkɒ̃ / (BrE)
a military officer who acts as a personal assistant or secretary to a senior officer
military assistant to a senior officer

Examples

The general trusted his aide-de-camp with all confidential correspondence.
During the ceremony, the aide-de-camp stood at attention beside the admiral.
She was nominated to serve as aide-de-camp to the commanding officer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assistant - a person who helps someone else

Etymology

From French, literally 'helper in the field'.
