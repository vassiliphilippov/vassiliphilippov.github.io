battle-scarred hero

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæɾl̩ skɑrd ˈhiːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈbætl skɑːd ˈhɪərəʊ / (BrE)
A hero who has been affected, marked, or shaped by previous battles or hardships.
hero marked by battles or hardships

Examples

The novel's protagonist is a battle-scarred hero who struggles to adjust to civilian life.
As a battle-scarred hero, she inspired those around her with courage and resilience.
The battle-scarred hero stood silently, his eyes reflecting the pain of past conflicts.

Synset Examples

He was a battle-scarred hero from the last war.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: veteran - someone with long experience or service, especially in battle
