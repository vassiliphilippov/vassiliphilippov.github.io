employment agent

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈplɔɪmənt ˌeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔɪmənt ˌeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to run an employment agency and help people find jobs
person running a job placement agency

Examples

The employment agent matched her with the perfect company for her skills.
Many people turn to an employment agent when they are struggling to find work on their own.
After meeting with the employment agent, he received several job interviews within a week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: employment agency - a business that helps people find jobs
