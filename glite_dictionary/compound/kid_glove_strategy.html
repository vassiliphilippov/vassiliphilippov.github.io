kid glove strategy

1. NOUN

/ kɪd ɡlʌv ˈstrætədʒi / (AmE), / kɪd ɡlʌv ˈstratədʒi / (BrE)
a careful and gentle approach, especially in dealing with a sensitive situation or person
careful and gentle approach

Examples

She recommended a kid glove strategy when talking to upset clients.
The school adopted a kid glove strategy in addressing the student's concerns.
In negotiations, a kid glove strategy sometimes achieves better results than open confrontation.

Synset Examples

He approached the negotiation with a kid glove strategy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: softly-softly approach - a cautious, tactful method of dealing with something
  • Antonym: aggressive approach - a forceful or assertive method

Etymology

From the idiom 'handle with kid gloves,' where 'kid' refers to soft leather made from young goatskin.
