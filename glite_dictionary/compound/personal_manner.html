personal manner

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːsənl ˈmænər / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəl ˈmænə / (BrE)
the characteristic way in which a person acts or behaves
a person's characteristic way of behaving

Examples

Her personal manner was always calm and reassuring, even in stressful situations.
The interviewers were impressed by his polite personal manner.
Everyone has a unique personal manner that sets them apart from others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demeanor - outward behavior or bearing
  • Synonym: mannerism - a distinctive or habitual style of expression or behavior
