plastic crack

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæstɪk kræk / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstɪk kræk / (BrE)
(slang) An addictive passion for collecting plastic toys or other related collectibles, especially figurines and models.
addiction to plastic collectibles/toys (slang)
slang

Examples

Many collectors joke that buying action figures is their plastic crack.
For fans of model kits, discovering a new brand can be like finding a new source of plastic crack.
The convention was filled with vendors selling all kinds of plastic crack to eager hobbyists.

Synset Examples

His obsession with toys turned into a plastic crack habit.
Once I started collecting, I fell into the plastic crack.
Many collectors can't resist the allure of plastic crack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: addiction - a strong and harmful need to regularly have something

Etymology

From the words 'plastic' (material of toys) and 'crack' (slang for a highly addictive drug), by analogy.
