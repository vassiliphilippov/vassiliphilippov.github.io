icebreaker game

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪsˌbreɪkər ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈaɪsˌbreɪkə ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a game or activity designed to help people get to know each other and encourage conversation in a group
activity to promote group interaction

Examples

The workshop began with an icebreaker game to make everyone feel comfortable.
Teachers often use an icebreaker game on the first day of class.
Before the main event, we played a quick icebreaker game to help everyone relax.

Synset Examples

An icebreaker game helped the team connect quickly.
The icebreaker question sparked a lively discussion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: icebreaker - an activity or remark for relieving tension or starting a conversation

Etymology

From 'icebreaker' (a thing that relieves shyness or tension in a group) + 'game'.
