clever-clever response

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɛvər ˈklɛvər rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / ˈklɛvə ˈklɛvə rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
a reply that is ostentatiously or self-consciously clever or witty, often implying insincerity or showiness
an ostentatiously clever or witty reply
british

Examples

He always has a clever-clever response to criticism, but rarely addresses the real issue.
Her clever-clever response made everyone laugh, though it came off as a bit smug.
When asked about his lateness, his clever-clever response did little to impress the manager.

Synset Examples

She always had a clever-clever response for every question.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retort - a quick, witty, or sharp reply
  • Synonym: quip - a witty remark or comment
  • Synonym: comeback - a quick reply, often witty

Etymology

Formed from 'clever' duplicated for emphasis; common in British English to imply pretentiousness or excessive cleverness.
