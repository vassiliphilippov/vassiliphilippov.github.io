tippy chair

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪpi tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈtɪpi tʃeə / (BrE)
a chair that is unstable or easily tips over due to an unsteady base or design
unstable or easily tipping chair

Examples

He avoided sitting on the tippy chair after almost falling last time.
The old wooden stool had become a tippy chair over the years.
Parents worried that the tippy chair in the kitchen could be dangerous for children.

Synset Examples

The tippy chair made me nervous during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unstable chair - a chair that is not steady or secure
