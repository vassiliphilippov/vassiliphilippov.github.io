to turn on the heat

1. VERB

/ tɝn ɑn ðə hit / (AmE), / tɜːn ɒn ðə hiːt / (BrE)
to activate the heating system in a building or vehicle
activate a heating system

Examples

As soon as the temperature dropped, she turned on the heat to keep the house warm.
He forgot to turn on the heat before the guests arrived, so the room was chilly.
We usually turn on the heat in early November when the nights get cold.

Synset Examples

She turned on the heat as winter approached.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: turn off the heat - to deactivate the heating system
