ivy geranium

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪvi dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / ˈaɪvi dʒəˈreɪniəm / (BrE)
A trailing plant native to South America, widely cultivated for its rounded leaves and pink or rosy flowers, often grown in hanging baskets or window boxes.
a trailing flowering plant with rosy blooms

Examples

The ivy geranium is popular for adding color to balcony railings.
She planted an ivy geranium in her window box, where it quickly started to trail over the edge.
Gardeners appreciate the ivy geranium for its long-lasting, bright flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geranium - a genus of flowering plants often called cranesbills
