Chinese deity

1. NOUN

/ tʃaɪˈniz ˈdiːɪti / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈniːz ˈdiːɪti / (BrE)
a god or goddess worshipped in ancient or traditional Chinese religion and mythology
god or goddess in Chinese religion

Examples

The dragon is often considered a Chinese deity symbolizing power and good fortune.
Many festivals are held to honor various Chinese deities throughout the year.
Offerings were made to the Chinese deity known as the Kitchen God for blessings and prosperity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deity - a god or goddess; a divine being
