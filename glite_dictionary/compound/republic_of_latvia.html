Republic of Latvia

1. PROPN

/ rəˈpʌblɪk əv ˈlætviə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈlætviə / (BrE)
A country in northeastern Europe, situated on the eastern coast of the Baltic Sea.
country in northeastern Europe

Examples

The capital city of the Republic of Latvia is Riga.
