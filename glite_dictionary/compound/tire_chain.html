tire chain

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪɚ tʃeɪn / (AmE), / ˈtaɪə tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a chain fitted around a vehicle tire to improve traction on ice or snow
chain fitted around a tire for traction

Examples

During the snowstorm, we installed tire chains to navigate the icy roads safely.
The rental company provides tire chains for drivers heading into the mountains in winter.
He struggled to fit the tire chains on the truck before the weather worsened.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snow chain - chain fitted to tires to provide traction in snow
