prune whip

1. NOUN

/ ˈprun wɪp / (AmE), / ˈpruːn wɪp / (BrE)
A dessert made by combining prune puree with whipped cream, often sweetened and sometimes set with egg whites.
dessert of prune puree and whipped cream

Examples

My grandmother used to make prune whip for special family gatherings.
The recipe calls for egg whites to give the prune whip its airy texture.
Many vintage cookbooks include instructions for a classic prune whip dessert.
