incidental expense

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəl ɪkˈspɛns / (AmE), / ˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəl ɪkˈspɛns / (BrE)
a minor or unexpected cost that is not specifically planned or included in a budget
unplanned or minor expenditure

Examples

The company will reimburse incidental expenses such as parking and internet fees.
During the trip, I paid for several incidental expenses out of pocket.
It's good practice to set aside money for incidental expenses when planning a budget.

Synset Examples

he requested reimbursement of $7 for incidental expenses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miscellaneous expense - an expense with no clear category that is not assigned elsewhere
