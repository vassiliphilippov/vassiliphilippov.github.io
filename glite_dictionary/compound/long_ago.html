long ago

1. ADV

/ lɔŋ əˈgoʊ / (AmE), / lɒŋ əˈgəʊ / (BrE)
at a time far in the past; many years ago
in the distant past

Examples

We first met long ago during our university days.
The tradition started long ago and is still celebrated today.
He forgot about the incident long ago and moved on with his life.

Synset Examples

  • Antonym: recently - not long ago; a short time ago
