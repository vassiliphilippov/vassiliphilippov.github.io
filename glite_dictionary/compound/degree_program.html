degree program

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈɡri ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / dɪˈɡri ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
A course of study at a college or university that leads to an academic degree.
university/college course leading to a degree

Examples

She enrolled in an engineering degree program at the university last fall.
Many students choose a degree program that matches their career interests.
The college offers a flexible online degree program in business administration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curriculum - The subjects comprising a course of study in a school or college
