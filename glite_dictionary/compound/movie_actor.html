movie actor

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvi ˌæktər / (AmE), / ˈmuːvi ˌæktə / (BrE)
an actor whose profession is to perform roles in films
actor who appears in films

Examples

The movie actor accepted the award with an emotional speech.
Many fans gathered outside the studio to catch a glimpse of their favorite movie actor.
She dreamed of becoming a successful movie actor since she was a child.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: actor - a person who performs in plays, movies, or television shows
