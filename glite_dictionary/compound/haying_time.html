haying time

1. NOUN

/ ˈheɪɪŋ taɪm / (AmE), / ˈheɪɪŋ taɪm / (BrE)
the period during which grass is cut, dried, and stored for use as fodder
season for cutting and storing hay

Examples

Farmers work long hours during haying time to ensure the grass is properly dried.
The sweet scent of freshly cut grass filled the air throughout haying time.
When haying time arrived, everyone in the community pitched in to help.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: haymaking - the process of cutting and drying grass to make hay
