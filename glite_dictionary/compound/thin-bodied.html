thin-bodied

1. ADJ

/ θɪn ˈbɑdiːd / (AmE), / θɪn ˈbɒdid / (BrE)
having a slim or slender body; slim in form or structure
having a slim body

Examples

The thin-bodied dragonfly hovered gracefully above the pond.
Many fish in this river are thin-bodied, helping them move quickly through the water.
She preferred thin-bodied vases for the delicate flowers she arranged.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slender - gracefully thin in form
  • Synonym: skinny - very thin, sometimes unhealthily so
  • Antonym: thick-bodied - having a broad or thick body
