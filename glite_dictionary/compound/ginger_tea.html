ginger tea

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪndʒər ti / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒə tiː / (BrE)
a hot beverage made by infusing ginger root in water, sometimes with added sweeteners or other flavors
tea made from ginger root

Examples

Many people drink ginger tea to help with digestion after meals.
On cold days, a cup of ginger tea can be especially comforting.
You can make ginger tea by steeping fresh ginger slices in boiling water.

Synset Examples

She brewed ginger tea to soothe her throat.
