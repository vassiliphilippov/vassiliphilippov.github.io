red-breasted snipe

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛdˈbrɛstɪd ˈsnaɪp / (AmE), / ˌrɛdˈbrɛstɪd ˈsnaɪp / (BrE)
a dowitcher bird with a reddish breast, especially seen during the breeding season
a wading bird with a red breast

Examples

The red-breasted snipe frequents coastal mudflats during migration season.
Birdwatchers gathered early in the morning to spot a red-breasted snipe among the flock.
During breeding season, the red-breasted snipe displays its distinctive reddish plumage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dowitcher - a type of long-billed wading bird related to sandpipers
