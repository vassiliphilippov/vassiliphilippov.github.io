desperate criminal

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛs.pɚ.ɪt ˈkrɪ.mə.nəl / (AmE), / ˈdɛs.pər.ət ˈkrɪm.ɪ.nəl / (BrE)
a bold and dangerous outlaw, especially one active on the American frontier
bold outlaw, esp. on American frontier

Examples

The sheriff organized a posse to track down the desperate criminal who had escaped from jail.
Legends of the Wild West often feature a desperate criminal robbing stagecoaches and banks.
The authorities warned citizens to stay alert while the desperate criminal was still at large.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outlaw - a person who has broken the law and is sought by the authorities
