literary critic

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬əˌrɛri ˈkrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɪtərəri ˈkrɪtɪk / (BrE)
a person who evaluates, analyzes, and interprets works of literature as a profession or academic discipline
person who critiques literature

Examples

The well-known literary critic praised the novel's innovative narrative style in her latest review.
Many aspiring writers seek feedback from a literary critic to improve their craft.
As a literary critic, he often appears on radio programs to discuss contemporary fiction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: critic - a person who expresses opinions on the merits of works such as books, films, or art
