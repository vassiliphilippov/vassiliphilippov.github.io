fiery display

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪəri dɪˈspleɪ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəri dɪsˈpleɪ / (BrE)
a vivid and intense exhibition, often featuring bright colors or dramatic effects suggestive of fire
vivid, intense exhibition

Examples

The parade ended with a fiery display of fireworks that lit up the night sky.
Her speech was a fiery display of passion and conviction, captivating the entire audience.
The artist’s new painting is a fiery display of color and emotion that stands out in the gallery.

Synset Examples

The sunset was a fiery display of reds and oranges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spectacle - a visually impressive performance or display
  • Antonym: dull display - an unremarkable or lackluster exhibition
