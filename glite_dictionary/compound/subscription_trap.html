subscription trap

1. NOUN

/ səbˈskrɪp.ʃən træp / (AmE), / səbˈskrɪp.ʃən træp / (BrE)
a commercial practice or situation in which a consumer is unknowingly locked into recurring payments, often due to misleading terms or unclear cancellation procedures
deceptive ongoing payment scheme

Examples

Many people inadvertently sign up for a subscription trap when they accept a free trial online.
New regulations aim to protect consumers from falling prey to a subscription trap.
She discovered she was caught in a subscription trap after months of unnoticed charges.

Synset Examples

Many consumers fall victim to a subscription trap every year.
They realized too late they were in a subscription trap.
Be careful not to fall into a subscription trap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scam - a dishonest scheme or fraud
  • Degree Lower: subscription - an arrangement to receive something regularly in exchange for payment
  • Degree Higher: fraud - criminal deception intended to result in financial or personal gain
