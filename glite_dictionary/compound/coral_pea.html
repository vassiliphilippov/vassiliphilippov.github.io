coral pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrəl pi / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəl piː / (BrE)
any of various Australian climbing plants of the genus Kennedia, noted for their scarlet flowers.
Australian climbing plant with scarlet flowers

Examples

The coral pea is admired for its vivid scarlet blooms in many Australian gardens.
Several species of coral pea can be seen climbing over fences in local parks.
Botanists studied the habitat requirements of the coral pea to aid in conservation efforts.
