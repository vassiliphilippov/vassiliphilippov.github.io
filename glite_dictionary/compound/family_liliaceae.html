family Liliaceae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli lɪliˈeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli lɪlɪˈeɪsɪˌiː / (BrE)
a large family of flowering plants commonly known as the lily family, including the genus Lilium and related species, sometimes reclassified in separate families
the lily family of flowering plants

Examples

The family Liliaceae includes many well-known garden plants such as lilies and tulips.
Recent studies have led botanists to reclassify some genera once considered part of the family Liliaceae.
Members of the family Liliaceae are found in diverse habitats around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lily - a plant of the genus Lilium or a related plant in Liliaceae

Etymology

From Latin 'lilium' (lily) + '-aceae', a standard suffix for plant family names.
