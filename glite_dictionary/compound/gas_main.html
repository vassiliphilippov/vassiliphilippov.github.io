gas main

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs meɪn / (AmE), / ɡas meɪn / (BrE)
A large pipe that distributes gas in a town, city, or building complex.
pipe distributing gas in an area

Examples

A burst gas main forced the evacuation of nearby buildings last night.
The company is planning to replace the old gas main along Main Street next year.
Engineers inspected the gas main to check for any leaks after the earthquake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water main - a large pipe distributing water in a region
