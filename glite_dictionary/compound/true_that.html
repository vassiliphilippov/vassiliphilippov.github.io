true that

1. INTJ

/ tru ðæt / (AmE), / truː ðæt / (BrE)
(informal) Used to express agreement or to affirm that a statement is correct.
informal expression of agreement
informal

Examples

“It's harder than it looks.” “True that.”
He finished the assignment early—true that, he is always ahead.
Whenever she makes a good point, her friends say, 'true that!'

Synset Examples

When she stated the facts, everyone replied, true that.
He nodded in agreement, saying true that.
I agree with you, true that!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: that's true - Another way to affirm a statement or agree
