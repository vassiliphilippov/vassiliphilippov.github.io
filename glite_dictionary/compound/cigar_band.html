cigar band

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈɡɑr bænd / (AmE), / sɪˈɡɑː bænd / (BrE)
a narrow strip of paper wrapped around a cigar, often displaying the brand or logo
decorative paper band on a cigar

Examples

He admired the elegant cigar band before lighting his cigar.
The cigar band was embossed with gold letters and intricate designs.
Collectors often save cigar bands from rare or vintage cigars.
