pea jacket

1. NOUN

/ pi ˈdʒæ.kɪt / (AmE), / piː ˈdʒæk.ɪt / (BrE)
A heavy, double-breasted woolen jacket worn especially by sailors, typically navy in color and featuring large buttons.
a heavy double-breasted sailor's jacket

Examples

The chilly wind did nothing to penetrate his pea jacket as he stood on deck.
She found an old navy pea jacket at the vintage clothing store.
A classic pea jacket never goes out of style during winter months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pea coat - A heavy woolen overcoat of similar style to a pea jacket

Etymology

From Dutch 'pijjekker', with 'pij' referring to a coarse woolen cloth.
