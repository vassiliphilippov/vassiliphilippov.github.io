espresso machine

1. NOUN

/ əˈsprɛsoʊ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ɛˈsprɛsəʊ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used to brew espresso coffee by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans under high pressure
machine for brewing espresso coffee

Examples

She bought a new espresso machine to make her morning coffee at home.
Baristas often spend years mastering how to use an espresso machine properly.
The café upgraded to a commercial espresso machine to serve more customers quickly.

Synset Examples

Nothing beats my little brown fucking machine for espresso.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffee maker - an electric machine for making coffee

Etymology

Espresso from Italian 'caffè espresso' (pressed-out coffee); 'machine' from Latin 'machina'.
