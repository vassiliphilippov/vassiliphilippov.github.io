playful meow

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪfəl miˈaʊ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪfl̩ mɪˈaʊ / (BrE)
a lighthearted or teasing sound made by a cat
a cat's lighthearted or teasing sound

Examples

The kitten greeted her with a playful meow as soon as she walked in.
His cat let out a playful meow while chasing the string toy across the floor.
With a playful meow, the cat teased its owner into playing another game.

Synset Examples

She gave a playful meow to get his attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meow - a cat's characteristic vocal sound
