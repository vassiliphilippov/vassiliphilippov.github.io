medical building

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
A building used for practicing medicine or providing healthcare services, such as clinics or doctors' offices.
building for medical practice

Examples

The new medical building houses several specialist clinics and a pharmacy.
Doctors are moving into the recently renovated medical building downtown.
You must check in at the front desk when you arrive at the medical building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clinic - a facility for outpatient medical treatment
  • Synonym: hospital - an institution providing medical and surgical treatment and nursing care
