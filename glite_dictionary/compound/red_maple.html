red maple

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈmeɪpəl / (AmE), / rɛd ˈmeɪpəl / (BrE)
a deciduous tree of eastern and central North America with five-lobed leaves that turn scarlet and yellow in autumn
a North American maple tree with red autumn leaves

Examples

The leaves of the red maple turn a brilliant scarlet in the fall.
A red maple grew in the center of her yard, providing shade every summer.
We planted a red maple sapling to celebrate Arbor Day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maple - any tree or shrub of the genus Acer, known for lobed leaves and winged fruits
