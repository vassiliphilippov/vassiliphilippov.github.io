Daba language

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑːbə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdɑːbə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken chiefly south of Lake Chad in Cameroon, especially in the North and Far North Regions
a Chadic language spoken in Cameroon

Examples

Linguists have documented the grammar of the Daba language spoken in northern Cameroon.
She learned the basics of the Daba language to communicate with local communities during her research.
The Daba language is one of many Chadic languages spoken around Lake Chad.
