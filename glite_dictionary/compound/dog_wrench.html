dog wrench

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ rɛntʃ / (AmE), / dɒɡ rɛntʃ / (BrE)
a wrench with a handle shaped like a crank, used for turning pipes or cylindrical objects
crank-handled wrench

Examples

The plumber selected a dog wrench to tighten the old pipe fitting.
A dog wrench is ideal for jobs that require extra leverage and control.
He kept a dog wrench in his toolbox for tricky repair situations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crank wrench - a wrench with a handle shaped like a crank
