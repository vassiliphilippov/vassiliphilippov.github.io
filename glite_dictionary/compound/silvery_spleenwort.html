silvery spleenwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvəri ˈspliːnwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəri ˈspliːnwɜːt / (BrE)
A fern characterized by long, silvery outgrowths that enclose its developing spores.
fern with elongate silvery outgrowths

Examples

The silvery spleenwort thrives in rocky crevices with ample shade.
Botanists admire the unique silvery outgrowths of the silvery spleenwort.
I spotted a patch of silvery spleenwort along the woodland trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spleenwort - type of small fern in the genus Asplenium

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvəri ˈspliːnwɝːt / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəri ˈspliːnwɜːt / (BrE)
A North American fern with narrow fronds and yellowish leafstalks, known for a silvery sheen on the underside of its leaves.
North American fern with silvery fronds

Examples

The silvery spleenwort thrives in rocky woodlands and shaded ledges.
Botanists identified the fern as a silvery spleenwort due to its distinct fronds.
While hiking, I spotted a cluster of silvery spleenwort along the stream.
