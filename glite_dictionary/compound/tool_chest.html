tool chest

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːl tʃɛst / (AmE), / ˈtuːl tʃɛst / (BrE)
a box or cabinet used for storing and organizing hand tools
storage box for tools

Examples

He keeps all his screwdrivers and wrenches neatly organized in his tool chest.
After fixing the leak, she returned the hammer to the tool chest in the garage.
The old tool chest was overflowing with nails and other hardware.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toolbox - a container for holding tools
