fortune hunter

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔɹ.tʃən ˌhʌn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.tʃuːn ˌhʌn.tə / (BrE)
a person who seeks to acquire wealth, especially by marrying someone rich
person seeking riches through marriage

Examples

Rumors spread that he was merely a fortune hunter interested in her inheritance.
The tabloids accused her fiancé of being a fortune hunter after learning of her family's wealth.
In old novels, the fortune hunter would often target wealthy widows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gold digger - a person who forms relationships to gain money
