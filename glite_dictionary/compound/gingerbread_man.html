gingerbread man

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪndʒərˌbrɛd mæn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒəbɹɛd mæn / (BrE)
A biscuit or cookie made of gingerbread dough, typically shaped like a stylized human figure and often decorated with icing or sweets.
gingerbread biscuit shaped like a person

Examples

She baked a batch of gingerbread men for the holiday party.
The gingerbread man was decorated with colorful icing and candy buttons.
Children love to decorate gingerbread men during Christmas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gingerbread - a sweet, spicy cake or biscuit flavored with ginger and often other spices

Etymology

From 'gingerbread', derived from Old French 'gingembras', and 'man', meaning adult male person; traditionally made as festive treats.
