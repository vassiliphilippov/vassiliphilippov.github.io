sun parlor

1. NOUN

/ sʌn ˈpɑrlɚ / (AmE), / sʌn ˈpɑːlə / (BrE)
A room, typically enclosed largely with glass, designed to allow occupants to enjoy sunlight while being sheltered indoors.
room enclosed with glass to enjoy sunlight

Examples

We spent the afternoon reading in the sun parlor and enjoying the warmth.
The house features a spacious sun parlor with views of the garden.
During winter, the sun parlor is the brightest and coziest spot in the house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunroom - a room with large windows designed to admit sunlight
