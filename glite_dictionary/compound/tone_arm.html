tone arm

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊn ɑrm / (AmE), / ˈtəʊn ɑːm / (BrE)
a light, balanced arm on a record player or turntable that holds the cartridge and allows it to move across the record
arm that holds cartridge on a turntable

Examples

The tone arm must be carefully balanced to avoid damaging the record.
He replaced the old tone arm with a high-end model for better sound quality.
Dust can collect on the tone arm, so it's important to clean it regularly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turntable - rotating platform of a record player
  • Synonym: cartridge - component on a record player that holds the stylus
