cushion flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊʃən ˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʊʃ(ə)n ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
a tall straggling shrub native to Western Australia, with large round crimson-yellow flowers
Australian shrub with round crimson-yellow flowers

Examples

The cushion flower brightens the dry landscapes of Western Australia every spring.
Gardeners appreciate the unusual color combination of the cushion flower.
A cluster of cushion flowers stood out amid the native shrubs on the hillside.
