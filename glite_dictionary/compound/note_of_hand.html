note of hand

1. NOUN

/ noʊt əv hænd / (AmE), / nəʊt əv hand / (BrE)
a written promise to pay a specified sum of money on demand or at a specified time
written promise to pay a sum of money
legal

Examples

He secured a loan by signing a note of hand promising repayment within six months.
The bank required a note of hand before releasing the funds.
She was asked to provide a note of hand as a guarantee for her business transaction.

Synset Examples

I had to co-sign his note at the bank

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promissory note - a written, signed promise to pay a specific sum of money to a specified person or bearer
