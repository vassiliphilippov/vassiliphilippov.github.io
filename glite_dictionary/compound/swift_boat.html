swift boat

1. NOUN

/ swɪft boʊt / (AmE), / swɪft bəʊt / (BrE)
A fast boat used for transportation, often for rapid movement across water.
fast boat for transport

Examples

They hired a swift boat to explore the coastline at sunrise.
The rescue team arrived quickly in a swift boat after receiving the distress signal.
Tourists lined up eagerly to board the swift boat for an island adventure.

Synset Examples

They took a swift boat to the island for the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speedboat - a small, fast boat powered by an engine
