Continuity Army Council

1. PROPN

/ kənˈtɪnjuəti ˈɑːrmi ˈkaʊnsl̩ / (AmE), / kənˈtɪnjuːəti ˈɑːmi ˈkaʊnsəl / (BrE)
a clandestine Irish republican paramilitary organization formed in 1994, regarded as the armed wing of Sinn Féin and designated as a terrorist group by several governments
Irish republican paramilitary group (1994–)

Examples

The government increased security measures following threats from the Continuity Army Council.
The Continuity Army Council emerged as a splinter group rejecting the peace process in Northern Ireland.
Intelligence reports linked several attacks to the Continuity Army Council during the late 1990s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Irish Republican Army - Irish paramilitary organization seeking Irish unification
