rockrose family

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑkˌroʊz ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈrɒkrəʊz ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of shrubs or woody herbs found in temperate regions, particularly the Mediterranean, known as Cistaceae.
family of shrubs or herbs (Cistaceae)

Examples

The rockrose family includes many plants that thrive in Mediterranean climates.
Botanists are particularly interested in the rockrose family because of its diversity.
Many aromatic shrubs in gardens belong to the rockrose family.
