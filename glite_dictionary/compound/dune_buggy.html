dune buggy

1. NOUN

/ duːn ˈbʌɡi / (AmE), / djuːn ˈbʌɡi / (BrE)
A light, open recreational vehicle with large tires, designed for use on sand dunes or beaches.
recreational vehicle for sand dunes

Examples

We rented a dune buggy to drive across the sandy hills.
The kids were thrilled to ride in a dune buggy along the beach.
He spent the afternoon repairing his old dune buggy before their trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beach buggy - a similar vehicle designed for use on beaches

Etymology

From 'dune' (a mound of sand) and 'buggy' (a light vehicle).
