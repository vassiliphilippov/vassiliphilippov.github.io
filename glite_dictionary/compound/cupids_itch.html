Cupid's itch

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjuːpɪdz ɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈkjuːpɪdz ɪtʃ / (BrE)
a euphemistic term for a sexually transmitted infection; a communicable disease transmitted by sexual contact
sexually transmitted infection (euphemism)

Examples

In 19th-century literature, Cupid's itch was a common euphemism for syphilis.
He feared he had caught Cupid's itch after his reckless weekend.
During the lecture on old medical slang, the professor mentioned 'the French pox' and Cupid's itch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexually transmitted infection - an infection passed from one person to another through sexual contact
  • Synonym: venereal disease - an old term for a disease contracted through sexual intercourse

Etymology

From 'Cupid', the Roman god of love, and 'itch', referring to irritation; historically used as a euphemism for venereal disease.
