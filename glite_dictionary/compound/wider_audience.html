wider audience

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪdər ˈɔdiəns / (AmE), / ˈwaɪdə ˈɔːdiəns / (BrE)
a larger group of people, especially more people than before, who see, hear, or are affected by something
a larger group of people reached

Examples

The documentary was broadcast on national television to reach a wider audience.
By translating the novel into several languages, the author hopes to attract a wider audience.
The museum redesigned its exhibits to appeal to a wider audience beyond just art enthusiasts.

Synset Examples

We need to transport our message to a wider audience.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: narrow audience - a smaller, more limited group of people reached
