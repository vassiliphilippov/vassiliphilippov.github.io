Belgian hare

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛl.dʒən hɛr / (AmE), / ˈbɛl.dʒən heə / (BrE)
a red breed of domestic rabbit, developed to resemble a hare in appearance, originally bred in Belgium
breed of domestic rabbit

Examples

The Belgian hare is prized for its elegant appearance and lively nature.
Breeders exhibited several Belgian hares at the national rabbit show this year.
She keeps both Dutch rabbits and a Belgian hare in her garden enclosure.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: rabbit - small mammal known for long ears and hopping movement
  • Degree Higher: hare - fast-running, long-eared mammal related to rabbits
