junior high

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒuːniɚ ˈhaɪ / (AmE), / ˌdʒuːniə ˈhaɪ / (BrE)
a school for students in the early years of secondary education, usually including the 7th and 8th grades
school for 7th and 8th grade students
american

Examples

After graduating from junior high, she looked forward to starting high school in the fall.
He made several friends when he transferred to a new junior high last year.
The school district is considering moving the ninth grade out of junior high and into the high school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: middle school - school for children between elementary and high school, often grades 6-8
