shooting box

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃuːtɪŋ bɑks / (AmE), / ˈʃuːtɪŋ bɒks / (BrE)
a small country house used by hunters during the shooting season
small country house for hunters

Examples

The guests stayed in the old shooting box nestled at the edge of the woods.
He inherited a shooting box in Scotland from his grandfather.
During autumn, the family would retreat to their shooting box for hunting and relaxation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hunting lodge - a house where hunters stay while hunting
