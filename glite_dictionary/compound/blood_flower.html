blood flower

1. NOUN

/ blʌd ˈflaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / blʌd ˈflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
a tropical plant (Asclepias curassavica) with orange-red flowers, commonly found as a weed throughout the tropics
a tropical plant with orange-red flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with blood flowers blooming vividly among the greenery.
We saw a blood flower growing wild by the side of the road during our trip to Brazil.
The striking color of the blood flower attracts many butterflies and insects.
