to whipstitch the hem

1. VERB

/ ˈwɪp.stɪtʃ ðə hɛm / (AmE), / ˈwɪp.stɪtʃ ðə hɛm / (BrE)
to sew the edge of a hem using a whipstitch, a simple over-and-over sewing technique to finish or secure fabric edges
sew a hem using a whipstitch

Examples

After you fold the fabric, simply whipstitch the hem to keep it secure.
She always prefers to whipstitch the hem for a clean finish on her garments.
He showed me how to neatly whipstitch the hem of my jeans by hand.

Synset Examples

He taught me how to whipstitch the hem neatly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whipstitch - a simple overcast stitch used in sewing
