shale oil

1. NOUN

/ ʃeɪl ɔɪl / (AmE), / ʃeɪl ɔɪl / (BrE)
Oil obtained by heating oil shale, used especially as a substitute for petroleum.
oil extracted from oil shale

Examples

The company invested heavily in extracting shale oil from underground deposits.
Environmentalists are raising concerns about the impact of shale oil production on the local ecosystem.
Due to rising prices, the country turned to shale oil as an alternative energy source.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petroleum - a naturally occurring, flammable liquid used as fuel
