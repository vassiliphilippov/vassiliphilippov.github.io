numbers pool

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌmbɚz puːl / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbəz puːl / (BrE)
An illegal lottery in which people bet on numbers, typically drawn daily, also known as a numbers game.
illegal lottery game

Examples

The police raided the building where the numbers pool was being operated.
Many people relied on winnings from the numbers pool to supplement their income during tough times.
He was arrested for his involvement in the illegal numbers pool last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: numbers game - another term for an illegal daily lottery
