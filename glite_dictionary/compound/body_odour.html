body odour

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːdi ˌoʊdɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˌəʊdə / (BrE)
an unpleasant smell from a person's body, typically caused by bacteria on the skin breaking down sweat
unpleasant smell from a person's body

Examples

He became self-conscious about his body odour after working out at the gym.
Wearing breathable fabrics can help reduce body odour during hot weather.
Some people use deodorant daily to manage their body odour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: halitosis - bad breath; an unpleasant smell from the mouth
  • Antonym: fragrance - a pleasant, sweet smell
