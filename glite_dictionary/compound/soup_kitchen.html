soup kitchen

1. NOUN

/ suːp ˈkɪtʃɪn / (AmE), / suːp ˈkɪtʃɪn / (BrE)
a place where free meals are provided to people in need, especially the homeless or impoverished
place giving free meals to those in need

Examples

During the recession, many families relied on the soup kitchen for hot meals.
Volunteers at the soup kitchen serve lunch to people experiencing homelessness every day.
After losing his job, he went to a soup kitchen in the city for help.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: food bank - a place where stockpiled food is distributed to those in need

Etymology

From 'soup' + 'kitchen'; term became common during the late 19th and early 20th centuries to describe charitable food services.
