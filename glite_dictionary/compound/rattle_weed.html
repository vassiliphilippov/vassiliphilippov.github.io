rattle weed

1. NOUN

/ ˈrætl̩ wid / (AmE), / ˈræt.əl wiːd / (BrE)
a much-branched erect herb with bright yellow flowers, native to eastern North America, especially from Massachusetts to Florida
plant with yellow flowers, eastern US

Examples

The field was dotted with clusters of rattle weed in full bloom.
Many gardeners admire the bright yellow flowers of rattle weed in late spring.
We found some rattle weed growing along the hiking trail near the forest edge.
