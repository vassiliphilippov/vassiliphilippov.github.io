horned screamer

1. NOUN

/ hɔrn(d) ˈskrimɚ / (AmE), / hɔːnd ˈskriːmə / (BrE)
a large South American bird (Anhima cornuta) with a horn-like projection on its forehead, known for its loud calls
large South American bird with a hornlike projection

Examples

The horned screamer is rarely seen outside wetland habitats in South America.
A distinctive feature of the horned screamer is the slender, horn-like projection on its head.
Researchers studied the behavior of horned screamers during the breeding season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screamer - any of several large, South American birds known for loud calls
