family Cicindelidae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli sɪˌsɪndəˈliːdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli sɪˌsɪndɛˈliːdiː / (BrE)
A family of beetles commonly known as tiger beetles, noted for their predatory habits and fast running speeds.
a family of tiger beetles
medical

Examples

The family Cicindelidae includes some of the fastest running insects in the world.
Entomologists often study the family Cicindelidae to learn more about predatory beetles.
Many species in the family Cicindelidae are characterized by their bright colors and metallic sheen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiger beetle - any beetle of the family Cicindelidae

Etymology

From New Latin Cicindelidae, based on Cicindela, the type genus.
