risk of metastasis

1. NOUN

/ rɪsk əv məˈtæstəsɪs / (AmE), / rɪsk əv mɛˈtastəsɪs / (BrE)
the likelihood or probability of cancer cells spreading from their original site to other parts of the body
probability of cancer spreading
medical

Examples

Smoking increases the risk of metastasis in patients with certain types of cancer.
The treatment plan was designed to lower the risk of metastasis after surgery.
Genetic factors can influence the risk of metastasis in breast cancer cases.

Synset Examples

The doctor explained the risk of metastasis in cancer patients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metastasis - the spread of cancer cells to new areas of the body
  • Synonym: risk - possibility of harm or loss

Etymology

From Greek 'metastasis' (removal, migration), used in medicine for the spread of cancer.
