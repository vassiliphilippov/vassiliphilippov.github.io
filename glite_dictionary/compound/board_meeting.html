board meeting

1. NOUN

/ bɔrd ˈmitɪŋ / (AmE), / bɔːd ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a formal meeting of the board of directors of a company or organization to discuss and decide administrative or strategic matters
meeting of a company's board of directors

Examples

The company will hold a board meeting next week to discuss the merger proposal.
During yesterday's board meeting, several key decisions were made regarding future investments.
She has been asked to present the annual financial report at the next board meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meeting - an assembly of people for a particular purpose
