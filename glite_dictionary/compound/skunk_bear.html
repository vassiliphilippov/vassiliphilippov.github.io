skunk bear

1. NOUN

/ skʌŋk bɛr / (AmE), / skʌŋk beə / (BrE)
a stocky, shaggy-coated carnivorous mammal of North America, also known as a wolverine
another name for wolverine (animal)

Examples

The elusive skunk bear is rarely seen in the wild due to its secretive nature.
Many trappers once feared the ferocity of the skunk bear in remote northern forests.
The skunk bear is known for its strength, persistence, and powerful jaws.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wolverine - a stocky, shaggy-coated carnivorous mammal of northern regions
