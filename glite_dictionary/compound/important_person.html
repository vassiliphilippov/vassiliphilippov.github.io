important person

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈpɔrtənt ˈpɝsən / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɔːtənt ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person whose actions or opinions have a significant influence on the course of events or on other people
person of significant influence or status

Examples

Many people attended the reception to meet the important person behind the new charity.
She always acts as if she's the only important person in the company.
Journalists crowded around the important person for a statement after the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: VIP - a very important person
