classic Samoan time

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæsɪk səˈmoʊən taɪm / (AmE), / ˈklæsɪk səˈməʊən taɪm / (BrE)
a humorous expression referring to the relaxed approach to punctuality associated with Samoan culture
humorous term for relaxed punctuality
informal

Examples

Our friends told us to meet at noon, but on classic Samoan time, everyone showed up an hour later.
You know he's running on classic Samoan time when he arrives after the meeting has ended.
We were worried we were late, but thankfully everyone else was on classic Samoan time too.
