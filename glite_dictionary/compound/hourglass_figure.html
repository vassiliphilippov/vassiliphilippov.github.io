hourglass figure

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊɚˌɡlæs ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / ˈaʊəˌɡlɑːs ˈfɪɡə / (BrE)
a body shape characterized by a narrow waist with proportionally wider hips and bust, resembling an hourglass
body shape with narrow waist and wide hips and bust

Examples

Many models in the 1950s were praised for their hourglass figure.
She exercises daily to maintain her hourglass figure.
The designer's dresses flatter an hourglass figure perfectly.

Synset Examples

She wore a corset to achieve the classic hourglass figure.

Etymology

From 'hourglass' (referring to the shape) + 'figure'.
