half gainer

1. NOUN

/ hæf ˈɡeɪnɚ / (AmE), / hɑːf ˈɡeɪnə / (BrE)
A type of dive in which the diver throws the feet forward and up to perform a half backward somersault, entering the water facing the diving board.
dive with a half backward somersault

Examples

She impressed the judges with a perfectly executed half gainer from the 10-meter platform.
Learning the half gainer requires mastering proper technique and timing to ensure a safe entry into the water.
The diver attempted a half gainer but lost balance halfway through the rotation.
