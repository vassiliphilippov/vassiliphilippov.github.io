Bean Town

1. PROPN

/ ˈbiːn ˌtaʊn / (AmE), / ˈbiːn ˌtaʊn / (BrE)
A nickname for Boston, the capital and largest city of Massachusetts.
nickname for Boston

Examples

Many tourists visit Bean Town each year to experience its rich history and culture.
She moved to Bean Town for college and quickly fell in love with the city.
The sports teams in Bean Town have some of the most passionate fans in America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Boston - the capital and largest city of Massachusetts

Etymology

Refers to Boston's historical association with baked beans, a local specialty in colonial times.
