sweet spot

1. NOUN

/ swit spɑt / (AmE), / swiːt spɒt / (BrE)
the optimal or most effective point, range, or position in a particular situation or activity
optimal point or position

Examples

He finally found the sweet spot on his tennis racket for a perfect serve.
The negotiations reached a sweet spot where both sides were satisfied.
For flavor, there's a sweet spot between too little and too much seasoning.

Synset Examples

He found the sweet spot between work and play.
The temperature is at the sweet spot for baking bread.
This café has the sweet spot for the best coffee in town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimal - best or most effective possible
  • Synonym: ideal - most suitable or perfect condition
