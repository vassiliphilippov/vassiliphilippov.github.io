Komi language

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊmi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊmi ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Finno-Ugric language spoken by the Komi people of northeastern European Russia
Finno-Ugric language of the Komi people

Examples

The Komi language is spoken by about a quarter of a million people in Russia.
She studied the Komi language as part of her research into Uralic languages.
Many traditional songs are preserved in the Komi language.

Etymology

From Russian Коми (Komi) + English 'language'.
