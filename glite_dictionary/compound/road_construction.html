road construction

1. NOUN

/ roʊd kənˈstrʌkʃən / (AmE), / rəʊd kənˈstrʌkʃən / (BrE)
the process or activity of building or assembling roads, including their design, preparation, and paving
the building of roads

Examples

The city has allocated millions of dollars for road construction this year.
Due to ongoing road construction, drivers should expect delays on the main highway.
Modern equipment has greatly improved the efficiency of road construction projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roadbuilding - the activity or process of constructing roads
  • Degree Lower: road maintenance - the process of keeping roads in good condition after construction
