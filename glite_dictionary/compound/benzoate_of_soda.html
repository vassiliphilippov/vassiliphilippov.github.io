benzoate of soda

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛnzoʊˌeɪt əv ˈsoʊdə / (AmE), / ˈbɛn.zəʊ.eɪt əv ˈsəʊ.də / (BrE)
a white crystalline salt, sodium benzoate, used as a food preservative and antiseptic
sodium salt used as a preservative

Examples

Some soft drinks contain benzoate of soda as a preservative to extend shelf life.
The ingredient label listed benzoate of soda among other additives.
Pharmaceutical preparations may use benzoate of soda for its antiseptic properties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sodium benzoate - the sodium salt of benzoic acid, used as a preservative

Etymology

From 'benzoate', a salt of benzoic acid, and 'soda', referring to sodium.
