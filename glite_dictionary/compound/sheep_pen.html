sheep pen

1. NOUN

/ ʃip pɛn / (AmE), / ʃiːp pen / (BrE)
an enclosure or structure for confining sheep
enclosure for sheep

Examples

The farmer built a new sheep pen to keep his flock safe at night.
After shearing, the sheep were guided back into the sheep pen.
A sturdy sheep pen is essential for protecting lambs from predators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pen - an enclosure for confining animals
