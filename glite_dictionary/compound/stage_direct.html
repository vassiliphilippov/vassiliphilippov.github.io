to stage direct

1. VERB

/ steɪdʒ dɪˈrɛkt / (AmE), / steɪdʒ dɪˈrɛkt / (BrE)
to direct (a play or performance) for the theater stage, guiding actors and overseeing artistic aspects of a stage production
direct a theatrical stage production

Examples

She was chosen to stage direct the school's Shakespeare production this spring.
After years of acting, he decided to stage direct several musicals for the local theater.
They asked her to stage direct because of her unique artistic vision and strong leadership skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: direct - to supervise the performance or production of a play, film, etc.
