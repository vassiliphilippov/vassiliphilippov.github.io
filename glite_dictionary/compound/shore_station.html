shore station

1. NOUN

/ ʃɔr ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ʃɔː ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A military installation on land responsible for supporting and servicing naval forces.
naval support facility on land

Examples

The fleet returned to the shore station for repairs and resupply.
He served at a shore station during his time in the navy.
Supplies were shipped regularly from the shore station to the vessels at sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naval base - a military base for a navy
