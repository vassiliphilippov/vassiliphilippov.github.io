lady's smock

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪdiz smɑk / (AmE), / ˈleɪdɪz smɒk / (BrE)
a wildflower of the cabbage family, with pale lilac or white flowers, found in Europe and North America; also known as cuckooflower.
a wildflower with pale lilac or white flowers

Examples

Every spring, the meadows are dotted with delicate lady's smock blooms.
She picked a bouquet of lady's smock for the kitchen table.
Lady's smock thrives in damp, grassy fields across Europe and North America.
