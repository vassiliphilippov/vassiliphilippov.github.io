vertical surface

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɝːtɪkəl ˈsɝːfəs / (AmE), / ˈvɜːtɪkəl ˈsɜːfɪs / (BrE)
A surface that stands upright or is perpendicular to the ground.
upright or perpendicular surface

Examples

The painting was mounted on a vertical surface to prevent dust from settling on it.
Climbers must have special equipment to ascend a sheer vertical surface.
Instructions stated that the sensor should be placed on a vertical surface for accurate results.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: horizontal surface - a surface that is flat and parallel to the ground
