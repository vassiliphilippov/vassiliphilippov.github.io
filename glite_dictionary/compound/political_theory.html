political theory

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪdɪkəl ˈθiəri / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈθɪəri / (BrE)
A set of ideas or principles about politics, government, or the organization of society, typically guiding or characterizing the policy or attitudes of a group or nation.
ideas about politics and government

Examples

Her research focuses on the development of political theory in modern democracies.
The book provides an introduction to key concepts in political theory.
Different schools of political theory have influenced government policies throughout history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ideology - a system of ideas and ideals, especially in politics
