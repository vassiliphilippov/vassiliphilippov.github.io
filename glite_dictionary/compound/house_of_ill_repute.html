house of ill repute

1. NOUN

/ haʊs əv ɪl rɪˈpjut / (AmE), / haʊs əv ɪl rɪˈpjuːt / (BrE)
a building where people engage in prostitution; a brothel
brothel; building for prostitution

Examples

In the novel, the detective follows a lead to a house of ill repute on the edge of town.
Rumors spread that the abandoned building used to be a house of ill repute during the 1920s.
The politician lost support after being seen near a famous house of ill repute.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bordello - a brothel or house of prostitution
