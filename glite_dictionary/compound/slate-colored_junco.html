slate-colored junco

1. NOUN

/ ˈsleɪt ˌkʌlɚd ˈdʒʌŋkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsleɪt ˌkʌləd ˈdʒʌŋkəʊ / (BrE)
a common North American songbird with mostly grey plumage and dark brown eyes, belonging to the genus Junco
North American grey-plumaged songbird

Examples

We spotted a slate-colored junco hopping along the snowy path this morning.
A slate-colored junco is easy to recognize by its grey feathers and white underparts.
During the winter, slate-colored juncos often gather in flocks near backyard feeders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: junco - a small North American songbird of the genus Junco
