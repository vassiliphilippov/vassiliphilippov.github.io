gray-white

1. ADJ

/ ɡreɪ waɪt / (AmE), / ɡreɪ waɪt / (BrE)
Of a white color with a slight tinge of grey.
white tinged with grey

Examples

The old photograph had a distinct gray-white hue that gave it a vintage look.
She chose a gray-white paint for the living room walls to keep the space light and neutral.
The doctor noted a gray-white appearance in the patient's complexion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: off-white - a color that is white with a hint of another color, especially grey or beige
  • Degree Lower: pure white - a color of white with no tint or shade
  • Degree Higher: gray - a neutral color between black and white
