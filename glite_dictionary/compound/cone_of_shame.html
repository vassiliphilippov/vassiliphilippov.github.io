cone of shame

1. NOUN

/ koʊn əv ʃeɪm / (AmE), / kəʊn əv ʃeɪm / (BrE)
an informal term for a plastic protective collar worn by pets to prevent them from licking or biting wounds
pet's protective collar (informal)
informal

Examples

After his surgery, the vet put a cone of shame on the dog to stop him from scratching.
My cat glared at me, clearly displeased with her cone of shame.
We've decorated the cone of shame to make it a little more cheerful for our puppy.

Synset Examples

The puppy wore a cone of shame after his surgery.
My cat looked so sad in that cone of shame.
He laughed at his dog prancing around in the cone of shame.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Elizabethan collar - a protective medical collar for animals
