ex-gambler

1. NOUN

/ ɛks ˈɡæmblɚ / (AmE), / ɛks ˈɡæmblə / (BrE)
A person who previously engaged in gambling but no longer does so.
person who no longer gambles

Examples

After years of struggling with addiction, he is now an ex-gambler who helps others recover.
The support group was filled with ex-gamblers sharing their stories and successes.
As an ex-gambler, she warns others about the dangers of betting.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gambler - person who wagers money on games of chance
