June 14

1. NOUN

/ dʒuːn ˈfɔːrˌtin / (AmE), / dʒuːn ˈfɔːˌtiːn / (BrE)
In the United States, the date marking Flag Day, which commemorates the adoption of the national flag in 1777.
U.S. Flag Day (June 14)
american

Examples

On June 14, Americans celebrate Flag Day in honor of the nation's flag.
Schools across the U.S. often hold special ceremonies on June 14.
Every year, the town organizes a parade to mark June 14.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Flag Day - U.S. holiday commemorating the adoption of the national flag
