top secret recipe

1. NOUN

/ tɑp ˈsiː.krət ˈrɛs.ə.pi / (AmE), / tɒp ˈsiː.krɪt ˈrɛs.ɪ.pi / (BrE)
A cooking formula or method that is kept highly confidential and undisclosed to maintain exclusivity or uniqueness.
a highly confidential cooking formula

Examples

The restaurant has built its reputation on a top secret recipe for barbecue sauce.
She guarded her grandmother's top secret recipe and refused to reveal the ingredients to anyone.
The cereal's unique taste comes from a top secret recipe known only to a handful of employees.

Synset Examples

He shared his top secret recipe with no one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secret recipe - a recipe that is kept confidential to maintain exclusivity
