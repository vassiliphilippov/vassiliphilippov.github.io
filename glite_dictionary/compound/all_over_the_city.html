all over the city

1. ADV

/ ɔl ˈoʊvər ðə ˈsɪti / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈəʊvə ðə ˈsɪti / (BrE)
in or from many different parts of the city; throughout the entire city.
throughout the entire city

Examples

Volunteers traveled all over the city to help with the cleanup effort.
The posters were put up all over the city to promote the event.
You can find this type of restaurant all over the city these days.

Synset Examples

The Islamic festival attracted visitors from all over the city.
The YAMO festival attracted crowds from all over the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: across the city - from one side of the city to the other
  • Degree Higher: all over the country - throughout the entire country
