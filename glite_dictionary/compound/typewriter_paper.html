typewriter paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪpˌraɪtər ˈpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpˌraɪtə ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
writing paper specifically made to be used in a typewriter, typically thinner and more durable than regular paper
paper for use in a typewriter

Examples

The secretary loaded a fresh sheet of typewriter paper before starting the letter.
Vintage typewriter paper is sometimes sought after by collectors and typewriter enthusiasts.
He stacked piles of typewriter paper next to the old machine in the study.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carbon paper - thin paper coated with carbon used for making copies in a typewriter
