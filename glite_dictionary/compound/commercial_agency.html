commercial agency

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɝː.ʃəl ˈeɪ.dʒən.si / (AmE), / kəˈmɜː.ʃəl ˈeɪ.dʒən.si / (BrE)
an organization that provides businesses with information such as credit ratings about other firms
organization giving business credit/risk information

Examples

The company hired a commercial agency to assess their potential partners’ financial reliability.
Dun & Bradstreet is a well-known commercial agency that helps businesses evaluate risk.
Many exporters rely on a commercial agency for up-to-date information on foreign buyers.

Synset Examples

Dun & Bradstreet is the largest mercantile agency in the United States
