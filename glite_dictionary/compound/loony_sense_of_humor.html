loony sense of humor

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːni sɛns əv ˈhjuːmɚ / (AmE), / ˈluːni sɛns əv ˈhjuːmə / (BrE)
a style of humor characterized by eccentricity, absurdity, or craziness
eccentric or crazy style of humor

Examples

My uncle's loony sense of humor always lightens up our family gatherings.
Her loony sense of humor makes even the dullest meetings entertaining.
You need a loony sense of humor to appreciate that show.

Synset Examples

He has a loony sense of humor that I love.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zany sense of humor - a sense of humor that is remarkably odd or clownish
  • Antonym: deadpan sense of humor - deliberately impassive or expressionless style of humor
