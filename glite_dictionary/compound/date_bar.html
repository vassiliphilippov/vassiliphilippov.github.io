date bar

1. NOUN

/ deɪt bɑr / (AmE), / deɪt bɑː / (BrE)
A sweet baked bar or slice made with chopped dates as the main filling, typically layered with a crumbly crust.
sweet baked bar with date filling

Examples

She packed a homemade date bar in her lunch for a healthy snack.
Would you like to try a date bar with your afternoon tea?
The bakery's most popular treat is its chewy, delicious date bar.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: fruit bar - a confection or snack bar containing fruit
  • Synonym: granola bar - a bar-shaped snack made with oats and other ingredients
