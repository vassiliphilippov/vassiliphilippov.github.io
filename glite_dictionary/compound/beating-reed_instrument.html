beating-reed instrument

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːtɪŋ riːd ˌɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / ˈbiːtɪŋ riːd ˌɪnstrʊmənt / (BrE)
A musical instrument that produces sound by means of a vibrating reed.
musical instrument with a vibrating reed

Examples

The oboe is a popular example of a beating-reed instrument in Western classical music.
Many traditional folk bands feature at least one beating-reed instrument.
To demonstrate the science of sound, the teacher used a beating-reed instrument in her physics lesson.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reed instrument - musical instrument with a reed that vibrates to produce sound
