coral honeysuckle

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrəl ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəl ˈhʌniˌsʌkl̩ / (BrE)
an evergreen North American climbing vine with coral-red or orange tubular flowers, widely grown as an ornamental plant
North American vine with red or orange flowers

Examples

The coral honeysuckle climbed elegantly over the garden trellis, attracting hummingbirds with its bright flowers.
We planted coral honeysuckle along the fence to add color throughout the summer months.
Many gardeners choose coral honeysuckle for its hardy nature and stunning red blooms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honeysuckle - a genus of climbing or arching shrubs with sweetly scented flowers
