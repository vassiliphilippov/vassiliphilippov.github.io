fire-on-the-mountain

1. NOUN

/ ˌfaɪɚ ɑn ðə ˈmaʊn.tɪn / (AmE), / ˌfaɪə ɒn ðə ˈmaʊn.tɪn / (BrE)
A poinsettia native to the United States and eastern Mexico, often confused with Euphorbia heterophylla.
a North American poinsettia plant

Examples

The gardens were ablaze with fire-on-the-mountain blooming in bright red clusters.
She planted fire-on-the-mountain alongside her other native wildflowers to add some color.
Botanists often mistake fire-on-the-mountain for closely related species due to its similar appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poinsettia - a species of flowering plant with bright red and green foliage, widely used as a decorative plant
  • Degree Lower: Euphorbia heterophylla - a related species often confused with fire-on-the-mountain
