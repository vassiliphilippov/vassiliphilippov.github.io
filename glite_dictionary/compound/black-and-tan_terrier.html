black-and-tan terrier

1. NOUN

/ ˌblæk ən ˈtæn ˈtɛriɚ / (AmE), / ˌblæk ən ˈtæn ˈtɛriə / (BrE)
a short-haired breed of rat terrier with a black-and-tan coat, originally developed in Manchester, England
breed of terrier with a black-and-tan coat

Examples

The black-and-tan terrier was known for its agility in hunting rats.
In Victorian England, the black-and-tan terrier was a popular companion dog.
She read an old book describing the history of the black-and-tan terrier.
