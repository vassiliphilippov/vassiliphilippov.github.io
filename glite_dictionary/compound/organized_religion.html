organized religion

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɡəˌnaɪzd rɪˈlɪdʒən / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡənaɪzd rɪˈlɪdʒən / (BrE)
A system of faith and worship that is formally structured with established rituals, authorities, and doctrines.
structured system of religious practice

Examples

Many people seek spiritual fulfillment outside of organized religion.
Debates over the role of organized religion in society often spark passionate discussion.
She left organized religion behind and began her own spiritual journey.

Synset Examples

he was raised in the Baptist religion
a member of his own faith contradicted him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: religion - the belief in and worship of a superhuman controlling power, especially a personal God or gods
