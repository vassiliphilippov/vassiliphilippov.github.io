Lower Saxony

1. PROPN

/ ˌloʊər ˈsæksəni / (AmE), / ˌləʊə ˈsæksəni / (BrE)
a state in northwestern Germany, known for its large area and significant economic and cultural influence
German state in the northwest

Examples

We spent our summer vacation traveling through Lower Saxony and visiting historic towns.
The university in Lower Saxony attracts students from all over Europe.
Many innovations in renewable energy have originated in Lower Saxony.

Etymology

From English 'lower' and 'Saxony.' 'Saxony' derives from Late Latin 'Saxonia,' land of the Saxons, a Germanic people.
