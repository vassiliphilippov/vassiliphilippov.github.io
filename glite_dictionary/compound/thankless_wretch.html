thankless wretch

1. NOUN

/ ˈθæŋkləs ˈrɛtʃ / (AmE), / ˈθæŋkləs ˈrɛtʃ / (BrE)
a person who shows no gratitude; an ungrateful person
an ungrateful person

Examples

After all her kindness, he proved to be a thankless wretch who never appreciated her efforts.
The novel describes the main character as a thankless wretch rejected by his family.
Only a thankless wretch would ignore such generosity from friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ingrate - an ungrateful person
  • Antonym: grateful person - someone who shows gratitude
