negro peach

1. NOUN

/ ˈniːɡroʊ piːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈniːɡrəʊ piːtʃ / (BrE)
a stout, spreading or semi-climbing tropical African shrub with round, brownish-red, warty fruit
African shrub with reddish warty fruit

Examples

The negro peach is cultivated in several parts of tropical Africa for its ornamental value.
Locals often use the fruit of the negro peach in traditional remedies.
Researchers studied the chemical properties of extracts from the negro peach shrub.
