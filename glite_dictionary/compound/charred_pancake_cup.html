charred pancake cup

1. NOUN

/ tʃɑrd ˈpæn.keɪk kʌp / (AmE), / tʃɑːd ˈpæn.keɪk kʌp / (BrE)
A rare species of fungus (Sarcosoma globosum) in the family Sarcosomataceae, distinguished by its black, cup-shaped fruiting body that resembles a charred pancake.
a rare cup-shaped fungus (Sarcosoma globosum)

Examples

The charred pancake cup can be found in damp coniferous forests during early spring.
Researchers were excited to discover several charred pancake cups growing near the old stream bed.
Due to its dark color and gelatinous texture, the charred pancake cup is easily distinguished from other fungi.
