oil colour

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl ˈkʌlɚ / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
A type of paint made with pigment suspended in oil, used by artists for painting.
pigment-based paint in oil for artists

Examples

She prefers to work with oil colour rather than watercolour.
The artist mixed the oil colour on his palette before starting the portrait.
A tube of blue oil colour rolled off the table and onto the floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oil paint - pigment mixed with oil, used for painting
