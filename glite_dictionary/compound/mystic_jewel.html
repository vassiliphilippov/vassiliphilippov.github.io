Mystic Jewel

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɪstɪk ˈdʒuːəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪstɪk ˈdʒuːəl / (BrE)
The second member of the Taoist Trinity, representing one aspect of the supreme Tao in Taoist cosmology.
second member of the Taoist Trinity

Examples

Devotees offered incense before the altar of the Mystic Jewel during the festival.
The Mystic Jewel holds an essential place in the teachings of Taoist priests.
Ancient texts describe the role of the Mystic Jewel within the Taoist pantheon.

Related Terms
