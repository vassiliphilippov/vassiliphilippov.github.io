platinum blond

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæt.nəm blɑnd / (AmE), / ˈplæt.nəm blɒnd / (BrE)
a person whose hair is a very pale silvery blond, often achieved through artificial coloring
a person with very pale blond hair

Examples

She became famous as a platinum blond in the film industry.
Many celebrities have opted to go platinum blond for a dramatic change in appearance.
He decided to bleach his hair and become a platinum blond for the summer.
