Formosan language

1. NOUN

/ fɔrˈmoʊsən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / fɔːˈməʊsən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
any of the Austronesian languages spoken by the indigenous peoples of Taiwan (formerly Formosa)
Austronesian language of Taiwan

Examples

The extinction of many Formosan languages threatens the cultural heritage of Taiwan’s indigenous peoples.
She is conducting research on a rare Formosan language spoken in the mountains.
Efforts are being made to preserve and revitalize several Formosan languages among younger generations.

Etymology

Formosa is the former name for Taiwan, from Portuguese 'Ilha Formosa' meaning 'beautiful island'.
