moving ridge

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvɪŋ rɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmuːvɪŋ rɪdʒ / (BrE)
A ridge or swell that travels across the surface of a liquid, especially a large body of water; a wave.
travelling ridge on water; wave

Examples

A moving ridge approached the shore, growing taller as it neared the rocks.
The boat rode smoothly over each moving ridge on the lake.
The sunrise reflected beautifully on the moving ridges created by the wind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wave - a ridge of water moving across a surface
