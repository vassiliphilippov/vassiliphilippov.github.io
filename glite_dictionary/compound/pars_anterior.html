pars anterior

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɑrz ænˈtɪriər / (AmE), / ˌpɑːz ænˈtɪəriə / (BrE)
the front portion of the anterior pituitary gland, responsible for producing and releasing several hormones
front part of the anterior pituitary gland
medical

Examples

The pars anterior is essential for the production of growth hormone.
Damage to the pars anterior can affect metabolism and development.
Researchers studied the role of the pars anterior in hormone secretion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anterior pituitary - the front lobe of the pituitary gland

Etymology

From Latin 'pars' (part) and 'anterior' (before, front).
