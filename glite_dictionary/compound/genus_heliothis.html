genus Heliothis

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒiːnəs ˌhɛliˈoʊθɪs / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːnəs ˌhɛliˈɒθɪs / (BrE)
A genus of moths in the family Noctuidae, which includes several agricultural pest species.
genus of noctuid moths
medical

Examples

Several species in the genus Heliothis are significant agricultural pests.
The genus Heliothis is notable for its cosmopolitan distribution.
Researchers are studying the life cycle of the genus Heliothis to improve pest control methods.

Etymology

From Greek hēlios (sun) and -this, used in scientific naming.
