dog violet

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ ˈvaɪəlɪt / (AmE), / dɒɡ ˈvaɪəlɪt / (BrE)
A wild violet of the Old World with leafy stems and blue or purplish flowers, especially common in woods and meadows.
a species of wild violet with blue flowers

Examples

We found a patch of dog violet blooming along the woodland path.
The dog violet is easily distinguished by its blue petals and lack of scent.
Unlike sweet violets, dog violet does not have a strong fragrance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: violet - a plant of the genus Viola with purple, blue, or white flowers
