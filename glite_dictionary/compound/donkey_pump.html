donkey pump

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑŋki pʌmp / (AmE), / ˈdɒŋki pʌmp / (BrE)
a supplementary or auxiliary pump used when additional pumping capacity is needed, especially as a backup
supplementary or auxiliary pump

Examples

The firemen set up a donkey pump as an emergency backup during the operation.
When the main system failed, the crew activated the donkey pump to maintain water flow.
Our vessel is equipped with a donkey pump to handle overflow situations at sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auxiliary pump - a pump used as a backup or for additional pumping capacity

Etymology

Likely derived from 'donkey engine', an auxiliary engine used for secondary tasks aboard ships.
