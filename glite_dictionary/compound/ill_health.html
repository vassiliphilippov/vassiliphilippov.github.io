ill health

1. NOUN

/ ɪl hɛlθ / (AmE), / ɪl hɛlθ / (BrE)
a state of poor physical condition in which a person is unable to function normally or comfortably
poor physical condition, sickness

Examples

Her frequent absences from work were due to ill health.
Years of poverty had left him in a state of ill health.
She retired early because of persistent ill health.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: good health - a state of being free from illness or injury
  • Synonym: poor health - a state of having frequent or chronic health problems
  • Synonym: disease - a disorder of structure or function in a living organism

2. NOUN

/ ɪl hɛlθ / (AmE), / ɪl hɛlθ / (BrE)
a state of being unhealthy or having poor physical condition or sickness
poor physical condition; sickness

Examples

After months of ill health, she finally began to recover her strength.
The company's CEO resigned due to ill health last year.
Persistent ill health forced him to retire earlier than planned.

Synset Examples

His ill health kept him from attending the event.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: good health - the state of being healthy; absence of illness
  • Synonym: poor health - the state of having health that is not good; similar meaning
