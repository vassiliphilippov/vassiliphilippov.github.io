badminton racquet

1. NOUN

/ ˈbædmɪntən ˈrækɪt / (AmE), / ˈbædmɪntən ˈrækɪt / (BrE)
a light, long-handled racket with a small head, used to hit the shuttlecock in badminton
racket used in the sport of badminton

Examples

She bought a new badminton racquet before joining the club tournament.
His badminton racquet broke during the intense final match.
Proper care of your badminton racquet can extend its lifespan considerably.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racket - a sports implement used to hit a ball or shuttlecock
