crumb grinder

1. NOUN

/ krʌm ˈɡraɪndɚ / (AmE), / krʌm ˈɡraɪndə / (BrE)
a person who grinds bread into crumbs, or a device used to make breadcrumbs
a person or device that makes breadcrumbs
