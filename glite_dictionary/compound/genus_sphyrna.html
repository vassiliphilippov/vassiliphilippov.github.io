genus Sphyrna

1. NOUN

/ ˈʤiː.nəs ˈsfɪr.nə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈsfɪr.nə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Sphyrnidae, comprising the hammerhead sharks.
type genus of hammerhead sharks

Examples

Scientists have classified the genus Sphyrna as the group containing all hammerhead sharks.
The genus Sphyrna is notable for its species' unusual head shape.
Within the family Sphyrnidae, the genus Sphyrna includes several distinct species of hammerhead sharks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hammerhead shark - a shark of the genus Sphyrna known for its distinctive hammer-shaped head

Etymology

From Greek sphȳrna meaning 'hammer', referring to the hammer-shaped heads of these sharks.
