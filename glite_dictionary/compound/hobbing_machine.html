hobbing machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑbɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈhɒbɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine tool designed for cutting gears, splines, and sprockets using a rotating cutting tool called a hob
gear-cutting machine tool

Examples

The company invested in a state-of-the-art hobbing machine to increase their production of precision gears.
Each hobbing machine requires regular maintenance to ensure the highest quality of finished parts.
Our factory uses a hobbing machine for both gear cutting and creating custom sprockets.

Synset Examples

Modern hobbing machines, also known as hobbers, are fully automated machines that come in many sizes, because they need to be able to produce anything from tiny instrument gears up to 10 ft (3.0 m) diameter marine gears.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milling machine - machine tool used to machine solid materials
  • Synonym: gear shaper - machine tool for cutting gears using a different method

Etymology

From 'hob' (the cutting tool) + 'machine', reflecting its use of a hob for cutting.
