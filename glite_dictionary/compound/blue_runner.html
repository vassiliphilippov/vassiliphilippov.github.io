blue runner

1. NOUN

/ bluː ˈrʌnər / (AmE), / bluː ˈrʌnə / (BrE)
a species of fish of the jack family found in the western Atlantic Ocean from Cape Cod to Brazil, known for its bluish body
a western Atlantic fish of the jack family

Examples

Fishermen often catch blue runner while trolling near the coast of Florida.
The blue runner is prized for its speed and agility in the water.
We spotted a school of blue runner as we sailed past the coral reef.
