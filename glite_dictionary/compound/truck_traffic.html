truck traffic

1. NOUN

/ trʌk ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / trʌk ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
the movement or volume of trucks traveling on a road or in an area
the movement of trucks on roads

Examples

The construction zone is causing a lot of truck traffic on Main Street.
Residents have complained about the noise from increased truck traffic near the warehouse.
During harvest season, truck traffic in rural areas often doubles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traffic - the movement of vehicles along roads
