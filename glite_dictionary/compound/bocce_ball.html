bocce ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑː.tʃi bɑːl / (AmE), / ˈbɒ.tʃi bɔːl / (BrE)
A heavy ball used in the Italian game of bocce, which players try to throw or roll closest to a smaller target ball.
ball used in the game of bocce

Examples

She tossed her bocce ball towards the pallino with remarkable accuracy.
Each player selects a different colored bocce ball before the game begins.
The bocce ball rolled just past the target, missing the point by inches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowls - British lawn game played with similar heavy balls

Etymology

From Italian 'bocce' meaning 'bowls'.
