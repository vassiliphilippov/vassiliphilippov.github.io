Equador laurel

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛkwəˈdɔr ˈlɔrəl / (AmE), / ˌɛkwəˈdɔː ˈlɔːrəl / (BrE)
A large tropical American tree of the genus Cordia, known for its abundant creamy white flowers and valuable wood.
tropical American tree with white flowers and valuable wood

Examples

The furniture was made from the durable wood of the Equador laurel.
In tropical gardens, the Equador laurel is prized for its creamy white blossoms.
