free-and-easy

1. ADJ

/ ˌfri ən ˈizi / (AmE), / ˌfriː ən ˈiːzi / (BrE)
relaxed and informal; natural and unconcerned by strict rules or conventions.
relaxed and informal

Examples

The manager has a free-and-easy approach to supervising the team, making everyone feel comfortable.
Her free-and-easy manner made her popular at parties and social events.
The conversation soon became free-and-easy, with jokes and laughter filling the room.

Synset Examples

using their Christian names in a casual way

Related Terms

  • Synonym: casual - relaxed and unconcerned
  • Antonym: formal - following rules or convention, not relaxed
