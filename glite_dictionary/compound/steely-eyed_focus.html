steely-eyed focus

1. NOUN

/ ˈstiːli aɪd ˈfoʊkəs / (AmE), / ˈstiːli aɪd ˈfəʊkəs / (BrE)
an intense, unwavering concentration or attention, showing determination and resolve
intense, unwavering concentration

Examples

With steely-eyed focus, she worked through the noise and completed her project on time.
The athlete maintained his steely-eyed focus throughout the competition, never letting distractions get to him.
His steely-eyed focus inspired the whole team to raise their level of performance.

Synset Examples

He approached the challenge with a steely-eyed focus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concentration - close mental attention
  • Synonym: determination - firmness of purpose
