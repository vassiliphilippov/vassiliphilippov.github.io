East Indian rosebay

1. NOUN

/ iːst ˈɪndiən ˈroʊzˌbeɪ / (AmE), / iːst ˈɪndiən ˈrəʊzˌbeɪ / (BrE)
a tropical shrub with glossy leaves and fragrant nocturnal flowers, native from northern India to Thailand
tropical shrub with fragrant nocturnal flowers

Examples

The garden featured a beautiful East Indian rosebay in full bloom, its fragrance scenting the evening air.
Botanists studied the pollination of the East Indian rosebay as part of their tropical flora research.
The East Indian rosebay is prized for its glossy foliage and wavy flowers.
