one after the other

1. ADV

/ wʌn ˈæftɚ ðə ˈʌðɚ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈɑːftə ðə ˈʌðə / (BrE)
(of events or actions) occurring in immediate succession, without significant delay between them.
in quick succession, one following another

Examples

The runners finished the race one after the other, each just seconds apart.
Books began to fall one after the other from the crowded shelf.
He answered the interview questions one after the other without hesitation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one after another - occurring in succession, one following another
