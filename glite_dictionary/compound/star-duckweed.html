star-duckweed

1. NOUN

/ stɑr ˈdʌkˌwid / (AmE), / stɑː ˈdʌkˌwiːd / (BrE)
A small floating aquatic plant of the duckweed family, found in temperate regions, except North America.
a small aquatic plant in the duckweed family

Examples

The pond was covered with patches of star-duckweed, giving the water a green appearance.
Researchers studied the growth rate of star-duckweed under different light conditions.
Unlike other species, star-duckweed is rarely found in North America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: duckweed - a tiny floating freshwater plant
