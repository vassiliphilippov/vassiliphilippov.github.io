snap bean

1. NOUN

/ snæp biːn / (AmE), / snæp biːn / (BrE)
a tender green bean that breaks easily into pieces, usually eaten as a vegetable when young and without strings
a tender green bean, stringless and easily broken

Examples

She served steamed snap beans with butter as a side dish.
The farmer's market had baskets of fresh snap beans this morning.
My grandmother likes to blanch snap beans before adding them to salads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: green bean - an edible bean pod, often eaten when still green
  • Synonym: string bean - a variety of green bean with a fibrous string along the pod
