fuckwitted remark

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌkˌwɪt̬ɪd rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈfʌkˌwɪtɪd rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
A comment or statement that is extremely foolish or stupid, expressed in a crude or insulting manner.
extremely stupid or foolish comment (vulgar)
vulgar

Examples

His fuckwitted remark made the entire meeting grind to a halt.
I couldn't believe she would make such a fuckwitted remark during the interview.
Let's try to avoid any more fuckwitted remarks in this discussion.

Synset Examples

His fuckwitted remarks made everyone uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolish remark - a comment that lacks sense or good judgment
  • Synonym: stupid comment - an unintelligent or senseless statement
