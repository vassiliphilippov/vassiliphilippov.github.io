Empire State Building

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛmˌpaɪɚ steɪt ˈbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛmpaɪə steɪt ˈbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
a famous skyscraper in New York City, completed in 1931, and once the tallest building in the world
iconic New York skyscraper

Examples

The view from the top of the Empire State Building is breathtaking, especially at sunset.
Millions of tourists visit the Empire State Building every year to see its observation deck.
He photographed the Empire State Building illuminated against the night sky.

Etymology

Named after New York's nickname, 'The Empire State'.
