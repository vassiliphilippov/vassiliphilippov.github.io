coming of age

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌmɪŋ əv eɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkʌmɪŋ əv eɪdʒ / (BrE)
the process or moment of reaching maturity or adulthood, especially the transition from childhood to adult responsibilities or status
transition to adulthood; reaching maturity

Examples

The film is a poignant coming of age story that resonates with viewers of all ages.
Her eighteenth birthday marked her official coming of age in her community.
Many cultures have rituals to celebrate a young person's coming of age.

Synset Examples
