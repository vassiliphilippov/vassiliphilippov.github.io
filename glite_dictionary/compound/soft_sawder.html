soft sawder

1. NOUN

/ sɔft ˈsɑdər / (AmE), / sɒft ˈsɔːdə / (BrE)
a type of soft solder used for joining metals, typically with a low melting point
soft solder for joining metals

Examples

The plumber applied soft sawder to seal the copper pipes together.
Always use soft sawder for delicate electronic components to avoid heat damage.
He prefers soft sawder because it melts easily and forms reliable joints.

Synset Examples

He used soft sawder to join the metal pieces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soft solder - a low-melting-point alloy used for joining metals

Etymology

Variant of 'soft solder', with 'sawder' as an altered pronunciation or dialectal form of 'solder'.
