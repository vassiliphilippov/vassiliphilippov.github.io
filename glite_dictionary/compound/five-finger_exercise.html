five-finger exercise

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪv ˌfɪŋɡər ˌɛksərˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈfaɪv ˌfɪŋɡə ˌɛksəsaɪz / (BrE)
a simple musical or physical exercise designed to develop finger dexterity and coordination, typically involving the use of all five fingers
basic exercise for finger dexterity

Examples

To warm up, the pianist started with a few five-finger exercises.
Expect your teacher to assign five-finger exercises to help improve hand coordination.
Regularly practicing five-finger exercises can greatly benefit beginner musicians.

Synset Examples
