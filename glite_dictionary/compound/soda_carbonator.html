soda carbonator

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊ-də kɑr.bəˌneɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.də ˌkɑː.bəˈneɪ.tə / (BrE)
a device designed to add carbon dioxide to water or beverages, creating carbonated drinks.
device for carbonating beverages

Examples

We bought a soda carbonator so we could make sparkling water at home.
The new soda carbonator is small enough to fit comfortably on a kitchen counter.
After dinner, she used the soda carbonator to make fresh soda for her guests.
