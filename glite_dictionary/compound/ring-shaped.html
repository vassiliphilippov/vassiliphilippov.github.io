ring-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪŋ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋ ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the form or appearance of a ring; shaped like a circle or loop
having the shape of a ring

Examples

The archaeologist discovered a ring-shaped artifact buried beneath the ancient ruins.
They arranged the chairs in a ring-shaped pattern around the bonfire.
Some nebulae in space appear distinctly ring-shaped when viewed through a telescope.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circular - having the form of a circle
  • Degree Lower: oval - having the shape of an egg or ellipse
