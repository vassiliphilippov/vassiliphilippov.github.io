mythic journey

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪθɪk ˈdʒɝːni / (AmE), / ˈmɪθɪk ˈdʒɜːni / (BrE)
an expedition or quest of great significance, often resembling those found in myths or legends, typically involving challenges and transformation
legendary or transformative quest

Examples

The novel takes its protagonist on a mythic journey through forgotten lands and personal turmoil.
The film echoes the structure of a mythic journey, complete with trials and a transformative return.
Many view the path of self-discovery as a mythic journey filled with symbolic obstacles.

Synset Examples

Her journey felt mythic, filled with challenges and triumphs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hero's journey - archetypal story pattern involving a hero on an adventure

Etymology

From 'mythic', relating to myth, and 'journey', from Old French 'jornee' (day, a day's travel).
