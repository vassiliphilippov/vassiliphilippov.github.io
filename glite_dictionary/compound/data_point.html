data point

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ.t̬ə pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ.tə pɔɪnt / (BrE)
an individual item of factual information, often obtained through measurement or observation, used in analysis or research
individual item of factual information

Examples

The graph shows one data point for each year the survey was conducted.
We collected over a thousand data points during the experiment.
That outlier data point significantly affected the average temperature reading.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: datum - a single piece of information; singular form of data
