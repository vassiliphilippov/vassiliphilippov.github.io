healthy expression of sexuality

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθi ɪkˈsprɛʃən əv sɛkʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθi ɪkˈsprɛʃən əv sɛkʃʊˈalɪti / (BrE)
sexual activity or attitudes regarded as natural, positive, and beneficial to one's emotional or physical well-being
positive or beneficial sexual activity

Examples

Therapists often encourage a healthy expression of sexuality as part of overall well-being.
Comprehensive sex education promotes a healthy expression of sexuality among young adults.
Understanding consent is crucial for a healthy expression of sexuality in relationships.

Synset Examples

Many people find solo sex a healthy expression of sexuality.
