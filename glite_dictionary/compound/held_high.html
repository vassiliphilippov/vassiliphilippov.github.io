held high

1. ADJ

/ hɛld haɪ / (AmE), / hɛld haɪ / (BrE)
raised up in a proud or significant manner; elevated for emphasis or display
raised proudly or noticeably

Examples

She walked onto the stage with her head held high, exuding confidence.
The athlete entered the stadium with the national flag held high above his head.
After receiving the award, Mary stood with her trophy held high for everyone to see.

Synset Examples

The queen held her sceptre high during the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raise - to lift or move to a higher position
