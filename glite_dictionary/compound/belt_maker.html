belt maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛlt ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbɛlt ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person or company that makes belts, especially for clothing
person or company that makes belts

Examples

The skilled belt maker used fine leather to craft beautiful accessories.
She apprenticed with a renowned belt maker to learn traditional techniques.
Many designer brands have their own in-house belt maker for luxury products.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: belt - a band worn around the waist
  • Synonym: manufacturer - a person or company that makes goods for sale
