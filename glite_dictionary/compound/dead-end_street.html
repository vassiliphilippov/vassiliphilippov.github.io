dead-end street

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛdˌɛnd ˌstrit / (AmE), / ˈdɛdˌɛnd ˌstriːt / (BrE)
a street that is closed at one end and does not allow traffic to pass through
street closed at one end

Examples

The house is located on a quiet dead-end street away from the main road.
The children enjoy playing safely on the dead-end street near their home.
Our office moved to a building at the end of a dead-end street, which has less traffic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cul-de-sac - a street closed at one end, especially in residential areas
