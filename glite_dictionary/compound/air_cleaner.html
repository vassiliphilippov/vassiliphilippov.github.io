air cleaner

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌklinər / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌkliːnə / (BrE)
A device with a filter that removes dust and other particles from air passing through it.
device that filters dust from air

Examples

The air cleaner in my car needs to be replaced every year to ensure optimal performance.
He bought a portable air cleaner to reduce allergens in his apartment.
The technician suggested installing an air cleaner to improve indoor air quality at the office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air purifier - a device that removes contaminants from the air in a room
