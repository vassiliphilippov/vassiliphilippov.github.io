upscale cafe

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌpˌskeɪl kəˈfeɪ / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌskeɪl kaˈfeɪ / (BrE)
a coffee shop that is distinguished by its luxurious atmosphere, high-quality offerings, or exclusivity
luxurious or high-quality coffee shop

Examples

She enjoyed the plush seating at her favorite upscale cafe downtown.
The new upscale cafe offers artisanal pastries and single-origin coffees.
Business meetings are often held in an upscale cafe rather than a regular coffee shop.

Synset Examples

She felt bougie sipping coffee at the upscale cafe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luxury restaurant - a restaurant offering luxurious food and atmosphere
  • Degree Lower: coffeehouse - a place where coffee and light refreshments are served
