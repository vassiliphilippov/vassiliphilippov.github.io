supertastic time

1. NOUN

/ ˌsuː.pɚˈtæs.tɪk taɪm / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈtæs.tɪk taɪm / (BrE)
an exceptionally enjoyable or wonderful experience
exceptionally enjoyable experience
informal

Examples

We had a supertastic time at the amusement park.
Everyone agreed it was a supertastic time we would never forget.
The party last night was truly a supertastic time for all the guests.

Synset Examples

We had a supertastic time at the amusement park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: great time - a very enjoyable experience
  • Antonym: awful time - a very unpleasant experience

Etymology

Blend of 'super' and 'fantastic,' combined with 'time' as in 'a good time.'
