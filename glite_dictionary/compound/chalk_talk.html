chalk talk

1. NOUN

/ tʃɔk tɔk / (AmE), / tʃɔːk tɔːk / (BrE)
a talk or lecture that uses a blackboard and chalk for illustration or explanation
lecture using chalk and blackboard

Examples

The professor gave a chalk talk to explain the new theory.
During the chalk talk, she illustrated each step of the process on the board.
At the workshop, the instructor preferred a chalk talk over a slide presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lecture - an educational talk to an audience
