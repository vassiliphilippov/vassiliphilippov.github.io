reference manual

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛf.ɚəns ˈmæn.ju.əl / (AmE), / ˈrɛf.ərəns ˈman.ju.əl / (BrE)
A book or document that provides concise, organized information for easy consultation, often about a specific subject or system.
summary information manual for consultation

Examples

I keep a reference manual on my desk for quick access to technical information.
Before installing the software, she consulted the reference manual to check the system requirements.
The reference manual includes detailed tables and diagrams for easy lookup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handbook - a book giving information such as facts on a particular subject
  • Synonym: manual - a handbook or guide with instructions
