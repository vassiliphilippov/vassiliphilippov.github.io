growing need

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡroʊɪŋ nid / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊɪŋ niːd / (BrE)
an increasing requirement or demand for something
increasing requirement or demand

Examples

With the launch of the new project, there is a growing need for qualified engineers.
Urban areas are struggling to meet the growing need for affordable housing.
The school expanded its facilities to address the growing need for classroom space.
