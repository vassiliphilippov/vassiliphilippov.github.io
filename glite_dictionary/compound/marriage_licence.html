marriage licence

1. NOUN

/ ˈmærɪdʒ ˌlaɪsəns / (AmE), / ˈmærɪdʒ ˌlaɪsəns / (BrE)
an official document issued by a government authority that permits two people to marry
official permit to get married

Examples

You must obtain a marriage licence before you can have a wedding ceremony.
They applied for their marriage licence at the city hall last week.
Without a valid marriage licence, the marriage is not legally recognized.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marriage certificate - an official record that two people have married
