tropical prawn

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɑpɪkəl prɔn / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪkəl prɔːn / (BrE)
An edible prawn that inhabits tropical and warm waters, often used in cooking and seafood dishes.
edible prawn from tropical waters

Examples

The chef used fresh tropical prawn in her signature curry dish.
Fishermen in Southeast Asia often harvest tropical prawn for export and local markets.
A salad of mango and tropical prawn is popular during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prawn - a small edible shellfish with a long body and legs, similar to a shrimp
