auto part

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːtoʊ pɑːrt / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊ pɑːt / (BrE)
a component or piece of equipment that forms part of an automobile
component of an automobile

Examples

Many mechanics keep spare auto parts in their shop for quick repairs.
The store specializes in vintage auto parts for classic cars.
Replacing an auto part can sometimes be cheaper than buying a new vehicle.

Synset Examples

his business is auto parts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car part - component of a car
