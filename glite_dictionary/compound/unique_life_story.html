unique life story

1. NOUN

/ juˈnik laɪf ˈstɔri / (AmE), / juːˈniːk laɪf ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
the personal experiences and history that make an individual distinct from others
an individual's personal life history

Examples

She shared her unique life story during the interview, inspiring everyone in the room.
Everyone carries a unique life story shaped by choices and circumstances.
His unique life story was featured in a documentary about overcoming adversity.

Synset Examples

Every person has a unique life story to tell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biography - an account of someone's life written by someone else
