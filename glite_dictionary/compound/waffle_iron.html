waffle iron

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑːfəl ˌaɪərn / (AmE), / ˈwɒf.əl ˌaɪən / (BrE)
a kitchen appliance with two hinged, patterned metal plates used to bake waffles
kitchen appliance for making waffles

Examples

She plugged in her waffle iron to prepare breakfast for the family.
The old waffle iron left a perfect pattern on every waffle.
After cleaning the waffle iron, he stored it in the pantry.
