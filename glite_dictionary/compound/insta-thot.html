Insta-thot

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.stə ˌθɑt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.stə ˌθɒt / (BrE)
(slang, derogatory) A person, typically a woman, perceived as presenting themselves in a sexually provocative manner on Instagram in order to gain attention or followers.
derogatory term for a provocative Instagram user
slang offensive

Examples

Many celebrities have criticized the media's obsession with labeling women as Insta-thots.
He unfollowed several accounts he considered to be run by Insta-thots.
The rise of the Insta-thot stereotype reflects broader debates about online self-expression and sexism.

Synset Examples

The debate over what defines an Insta-thot continues.
His comments about her being an Insta-thot were inappropriate.
She was often labeled an Insta-thot for her posts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thot - slang term for a promiscuous woman

Etymology

Blend of 'Instagram' and 'thot' (slang for 'that ho over there').
