homing device

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊmɪŋ dɪˌvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmɪŋ dɪˌvaɪs / (BrE)
a mechanism that enables a missile or vehicle to detect and move toward a target automatically
mechanism guiding missiles to targets

Examples

The missile was equipped with a sophisticated homing device that ensured pinpoint accuracy.
Security forces traced the stolen car using a hidden homing device installed inside the dashboard.
Scientists tested the new homing device to improve the guidance of underwater drones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guidance system - a system controlling the path of a missile or vehicle
