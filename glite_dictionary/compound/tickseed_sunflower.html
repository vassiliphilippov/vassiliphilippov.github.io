tickseed sunflower

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪk.siːd ˈsʌnˌflaʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈtɪk.siːd ˈsʌn.flɑːw.ə / (BrE)
A North American plant of the daisy family, with large yellow flowers, belonging to the genus Bidens, commonly found in wetlands and meadows.
North American plant with large yellow flowers

Examples

The wetland was full of blooming tickseed sunflowers in late summer.
Gardeners often plant tickseed sunflower for its bright yellow blossoms and wildlife value.
A field of tickseed sunflowers attracted dozens of butterflies and bees.
