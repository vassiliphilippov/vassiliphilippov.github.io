feeding bottle

1. NOUN

/ ˈfidɪŋ ˌbɑtl̩ / (AmE), / ˈfiːdɪŋ ˌbɒtl̩ / (BrE)
A bottle fitted with a rubber or silicone teat, used for feeding milk or formula to infants and very young children.
bottle for feeding babies

Examples

She sterilized the feeding bottle before giving it to her newborn.
Many parents prefer to use a feeding bottle for formula feeding at night.
The nurse demonstrated how to hold the feeding bottle properly during feeding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baby bottle - another term for feeding bottle
  • Antonym: breastfeeding - feeding a baby with milk from the breast
