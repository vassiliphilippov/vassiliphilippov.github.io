milk delivery person

1. NOUN

/ mɪlk dɪˈlɪvəri ˌpɜrsən / (AmE), / mɪlk dɪˈlɪvəri ˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to deliver milk to homes or businesses
person who delivers milk

Examples

The milk delivery person brings fresh bottles to our doorstep every morning.
She waved to the milk delivery person as he made his rounds through the neighborhood.
Due to advances in refrigeration, the job of milk delivery person has become less common.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milkman - a man who delivers milk to homes or businesses
