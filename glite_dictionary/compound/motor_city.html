Motor City

1. PROPN

/ ˈmoʊtər ˌsɪti / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə ˌsɪti / (BrE)
A nickname for Detroit, Michigan, especially in reference to its role as the center of the United States automobile industry.
nickname for Detroit

Examples

Many musicians from Motor City helped shape the sound of Motown Records.
He moved to Motor City to work for one of the big automobile companies.
The sports fans in Motor City are some of the most passionate in the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Detroit - The largest city in Michigan, also known as Motor City.

Etymology

From 'motor' (relating to automobiles) and 'city,' referring to Detroit's prominence in car manufacturing.
