strong candidate

1. NOUN

/ strɔŋ ˈkændɪˌdeɪt / (AmE), / strɒŋ ˈkændɪˌdeɪt / (BrE)
an applicant who is highly qualified and likely to be successful in achieving a position, award, or opportunity
highly qualified applicant

Examples

Among hundreds of applicants, she was clearly a strong candidate for the fellowship.
His impressive resume made him a strong candidate for the management position.
The committee is searching for a strong candidate to lead the research team.

Synset Examples

He emerged as a strong candidate for the scholarship.
His experience made him a strong candidate for the job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: qualified candidate - an applicant who meets necessary requirements
  • Antonym: weak candidate - an applicant who is less likely to succeed or lacks key qualifications
