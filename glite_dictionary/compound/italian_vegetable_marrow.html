Italian vegetable marrow

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈtæliən ˈvɛdʒtəbəl ˈmæroʊ / (AmE), / ɪˈtæliən ˈvɛdʒtəbəl ˈmærəʊ / (BrE)
a type of squash plant with dark green fruit mottled with light green or yellow, commonly used in Mediterranean cooking
a squash with mottled green skin

Examples

The recipe calls for one Italian vegetable marrow, diced and sautéed with garlic.
Farmers at the market were selling freshly harvested Italian vegetable marrows this morning.
She stuffed the Italian vegetable marrow with rice, herbs, and tomatoes for dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zucchini - a slender green summer squash; also called courgette
