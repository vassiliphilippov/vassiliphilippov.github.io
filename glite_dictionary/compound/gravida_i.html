gravida I

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrævɪdə wʌn / (AmE), / ˈɡravɪdə wʌn / (BrE)
(medical) A woman who is pregnant for the first time.
woman pregnant for the first time
medical

Examples

The patient was identified as a gravida I during her prenatal examination.
As a gravida I, she was eager to learn more about childbirth and parenting.
The study focused on outcomes for gravida I women in their third trimester.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: primigravida - a woman pregnant for the first time

Etymology

From Latin 'gravida' (pregnant woman) plus Roman numeral 'I' (one), indicating the first pregnancy.
