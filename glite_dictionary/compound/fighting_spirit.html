fighting spirit

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪtɪŋ ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪtɪŋ ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
the quality of showing determination and courage to overcome difficulties or opposition
determination and courage to overcome difficulties

Examples

Despite losing the first set, her fighting spirit inspired the whole team to keep pushing forward.
The soldiers' fighting spirit did not waver, even in the face of overwhelming odds.
He credited his recovery to the fighting spirit instilled in him by his parents.

Synset Examples

Her fighting spirit helped her overcome all challenges.
The team's fighting spirit led them to victory.
He admired her fighting spirit in tough times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determination - the act of deciding firmly on a course of action
  • Synonym: resilience - the capacity to recover quickly from difficulties
  • Antonym: giving up - ceasing to make an effort; surrendering
