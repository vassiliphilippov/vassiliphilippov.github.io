tea party

1. NOUN

/ ˈti ˌpɑrti / (AmE), / ˈtiː ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
a social gathering where tea and light refreshments are served, often in the afternoon
gathering where tea is served

Examples

She invited her friends over for a delightful tea party in the garden.
The children dressed up in fancy clothes for their afternoon tea party.
He attended a traditional English tea party during his visit to London.

2. NOUN

/ ti ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / ti ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
A social gathering where tea, light refreshments, and conversation are enjoyed, often in the afternoon.
afternoon social event with tea

Examples

She invited her neighbors to a tea party in her garden.
Children dressed up for the afternoon tea party at school.
The royal family hosted a grand tea party at the palace.

Synset Examples
