Republic of Chad

1. PROPN

/ rəˈpʌblɪk əv tʃæd / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv tʃad / (BrE)
A landlocked country in north-central Africa, formerly under French control until independence in 1960.
country in north-central Africa

Examples

The Republic of Chad gained its independence from France in 1960.
Lake Chad lies at the western edge of the Republic of Chad.
The Republic of Chad faces challenges related to its desert climate and landlocked geography.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chad - country in north-central Africa
