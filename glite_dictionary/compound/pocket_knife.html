pocket knife

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkɪt naɪf / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt naɪf / (BrE)
A small knife with a blade that folds into the handle, designed to be carried in a pocket.
folding knife for carrying in a pocket

Examples

He always kept a pocket knife in his backpack for emergencies.
During the camping trip, she used her pocket knife to cut some rope.
My grandfather gave me his old pocket knife as a keepsake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: penknife - A small folding knife, similar to a pocket knife, originally used to sharpen quill pens.
