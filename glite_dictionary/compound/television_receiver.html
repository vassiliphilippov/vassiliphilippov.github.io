television receiver

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛləˈvɪʒən rɪˈsivər / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈvɪʒən rɪˈsiːvə / (BrE)
an electronic device that receives television signals and displays them on a screen
device that receives and displays TV signals

Examples

The old television receiver in the guest room still works, but the picture quality isn't great.
She donated her spare television receiver to a local community center.
With technological advances, modern television receivers offer higher resolution and smart features.

Synset Examples

the British call a tv set a ‘telly’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: television - system for transmitting moving images and sound
  • Synonym: tv set - an electronic apparatus for viewing television broadcasts
