Universal Time

1. NOUN

/ ˌjuːnɪˈvɝːsəl taɪm / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsəl taɪm / (BrE)
the mean solar time at the Prime Meridian (0° longitude) in Greenwich, England, used as a worldwide standard.
mean solar time at the Greenwich meridian

Examples

The observation was scheduled to begin at 02:00 Universal Time.
Scientists use Universal Time to record astronomical events so they are consistent worldwide.
When converting local time to Universal Time, you must account for your time zone difference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Greenwich Mean Time - mean solar time at Greenwich; a standard for civil timekeeping

Etymology

From Latin 'universalis' (of or belonging to all); refers to time 'universal' for all locations, originally based on Greenwich.
