rubbish bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌbɪʃ bæg / (AmE), / ˈrʌbɪʃ bæɡ / (BrE)
A strong plastic bag used for collecting and disposing of household or other waste.
plastic bag for waste disposal
british

Examples

Could you please take out the rubbish bag and put a new one in the bin?
The cleaner replaced every rubbish bag in the office at the end of the day.
He accidentally tore the rubbish bag while carrying it to the curb.

Synset Examples

I need a new rubbish bag for the kitchen bin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garbage bag - a bag used for holding waste or refuse (mainly US)
