to bite one's nails

1. VERB

/ baɪt wʌnz neɪlz / (AmE), / baɪt wʌnz neɪlz / (BrE)
to chew on one's fingernails, often as a result of anxiety or nervousness
chew fingernails due to anxiety

Examples

He started to bite his nails during the tense movie scene.
Whenever she's anxious about a test, she can't help but bite her nails.
Doctors warn that it's not healthy to bite your nails.

Synset Examples

He tends to bite his nails when he's nervous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nail-bite - to chew your nails
