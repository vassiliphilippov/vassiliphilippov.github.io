successful entrepreneur

1. NOUN

/ səkˈsɛsfəl ɑntrəprəˈnɜr / (AmE), / səkˈsɛsfʊl ɒntrəprəˈnɜː / (BrE)
a person who has started and managed a business venture that has achieved significant success
an entrepreneur with notable business success

Examples

Many people look up to a successful entrepreneur as a role model for business achievement.
He became a successful entrepreneur after launching his third startup.
Tips from a successful entrepreneur can be invaluable for new business owners.

Synset Examples

She aspires to be a successful entrepreneur one day.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: entrepreneur - a person who starts and operates a business
