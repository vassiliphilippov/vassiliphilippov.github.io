midterm exam

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪdˌtɜrm ɪɡˈzæm / (AmE), / ˈmɪdtɜːm ɪɡˈzæm / (BrE)
an examination given in the middle of an academic term to assess students' progress
exam given halfway through a term

Examples

Many students feel nervous before the midterm exam because it covers so much material.
Your grade will be based on the midterm exam, final exam, and class participation.
I forgot my calculator on the day of the midterm exam and had to do all the math by hand.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: final exam - an examination given at the end of an academic term
