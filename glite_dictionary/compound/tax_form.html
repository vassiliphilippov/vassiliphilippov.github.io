tax form

1. NOUN

/ tæks fɔrm / (AmE), / tæks fɔːm / (BrE)
an official document used to report income, expenses, and other relevant information for tax purposes
official tax reporting document

Examples

Before April 15th, every citizen must file a tax form with the government.
She downloaded the tax form online to prepare for the new fiscal year.
Make sure you complete each section of the tax form accurately to avoid delays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tax return - an official form submitted to report income and determine tax owed
