pica em

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪkə ɛm / (AmE), / ˈpiːkə ɛm / (BrE)
A linear unit of measurement equal to 1/6 inch, used in printing and typesetting.
printing unit of 1/6 inch

Examples

The margin should be at least two pica ems wide for aesthetic balance.
In traditional typography, a pica em is commonly used to measure line length.
He set the column width to twelve pica ems to fit the layout guidelines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pica - a unit of measurement in printing equal to 12 points (about 1/6 inch)
