six-lined racerunner

1. NOUN

/ sɪks laɪnd ˈreɪsərˌrʌnər / (AmE), / sɪks laɪnd ˈreɪsərˌrʌnə / (BrE)
a very swift lizard native to eastern and central United States, characterized by six light stripes running the length of its body
species of fast North American lizard

Examples

The six-lined racerunner darted across the sunlit path before disappearing into the underbrush.
Many reptile enthusiasts keep the six-lined racerunner for its speed and striking appearance.
While hiking, they spotted a six-lined racerunner basking on a warm rock near the trail.
