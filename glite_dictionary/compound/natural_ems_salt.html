natural Ems salt

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃɚəl ɛmz sɔlt / (AmE), / ˈnætʃrəl ɛmz sɒlt / (BrE)
A salt obtained from the mineral waters of Bad Ems and used as a medicine, especially for treating diseases of the upper respiratory tract.
medicinal salt from Bad Ems

Examples

Doctors sometimes prescribe natural Ems salt to relieve symptoms of throat infections.
You can purchase natural Ems salt at some pharmacies and health shops.
The curative properties of natural Ems salt have been recognized since the 19th century.

Synset Examples

Gargling with Emser salt water is a quick way to heal an infected throat.

Etymology

Named after Bad Ems, a spa town in Germany known for its mineral springs.
