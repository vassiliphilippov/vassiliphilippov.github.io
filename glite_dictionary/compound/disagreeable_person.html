disagreeable person

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪsəˈɡriəbəl ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˌdɪsəˈɡriːəbl̩ ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is unpleasant or difficult to get along with
unpleasant or difficult person

Examples

Nobody wanted to work with the disagreeable person who always found something to complain about.
Despite his skills, his reputation as a disagreeable person made it hard for him to make friends at the office.
She was called a disagreeable person because she frequently argued and never compromised.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unpleasant person - a person who is not pleasant
  • Antonym: agreeable person - a person who is pleasant or easy to get along with
