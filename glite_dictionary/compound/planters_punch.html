planter's punch

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæn.tɚz pʌnʧ / (AmE), / ˈplɑːn.təz pʌnʧ / (BrE)
a cocktail made with rum, lime or lemon juice, sugar, and sometimes bitters
rum-based cocktail with citrus juice

Examples

She ordered a planter's punch at the beach bar to enjoy the tropical evening.
The recipe for planter's punch calls for fresh lime juice and dark rum.
At the party, the bartender mixed a delicious planter's punch for everyone.

Etymology

Originates from the tradition of planters (plantation owners) in the Caribbean; first recorded in the late 19th century.
