science teacher

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪəns ˌtitʃər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪəns ˌtiːtʃə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to teach science, especially in a school
person who teaches science

Examples

Her science teacher inspired her to become a biologist.
The science teacher organized a field trip to the astronomy center.
As a science teacher, he encouraged students to ask questions and explore experiments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school
  • Degree Lower: science - the study of the structure and behaviour of the natural world
