storage-battery grid

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔrɪdʒ ˈbætəri ɡrɪd / (AmE), / ˈstɔːrɪdʒ ˈbætəri ɡrɪd / (BrE)
a perforated or corrugated metal plate used in a storage battery to conduct electricity and support the active material
metal plate in storage battery

Examples

The storage-battery grid must be made from materials that resist corrosion during battery operation.
When the storage-battery grid deteriorates, the battery's efficiency drops significantly.
Engineers improved the design of the storage-battery grid to extend the unit's lifespan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electrode - a conductor through which electricity enters or leaves an object, substance, or region
