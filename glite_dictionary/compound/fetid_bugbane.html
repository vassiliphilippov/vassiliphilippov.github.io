fetid bugbane

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛtɪd ˈbʌɡˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈfiːtɪd ˈbʌɡˌbeɪn / (BrE)
A species of bugbane native to Siberia and eastern Asia, known for its ill-smelling green-white flowers.
a bugbane plant with foul-smelling flowers

Examples

The strong odor of fetid bugbane makes it easy to identify in the wild.
Gardeners often avoid planting fetid bugbane because of its disagreeable smell.
Researchers study the medicinal properties of fetid bugbane despite its unpleasant fragrance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bugbane - a group of plants in the genus Cimicifuga, known for repelling insects
