parlor grand

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrlɚ ˌɡrænd / (AmE), / ˈpɑːlə ˌɡrænd / (BrE)
a small grand piano, typically smaller than a concert grand, designed for use in homes or small venues
a small grand piano

Examples

The parlor grand added elegance and rich sound to the living room.
She practiced every day on her family's cherished parlor grand.
A restored parlor grand can be a beautiful centerpiece in any home.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: grand piano - a large, wing-shaped piano with horizontal strings and action
