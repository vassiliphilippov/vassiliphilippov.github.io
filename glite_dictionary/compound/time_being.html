time being

1. NOUN

/ taɪm ˈbiːɪŋ / (AmE), / taɪm ˈbiːɪŋ / (BrE)
the present moment or a temporary period that is current now; used especially in the phrase 'for the time being.'
the present moment; a temporary period

Examples

You can stay in the guest room for the time being until you find an apartment.
For the time being, we will continue to work from home.
The repairs are only temporary and will have to do for the time being.

Synset Examples

for the nonce

Related Terms

  • Synonym: present - the current moment or period of time
  • Antonym: future - the time yet to come
  • Antonym: past - the time before the present
