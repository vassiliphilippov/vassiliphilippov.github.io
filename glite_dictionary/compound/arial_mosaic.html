arial mosaic

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛriəl moʊˈzæɪk / (AmE), / ˈɛːrɪəl məʊˈzeɪɪk / (BrE)
a composite picture created by arranging multiple aerial photographs to form a continuous image of a larger area
composite image made from aerial photos

Examples

The engineer studied the arial mosaic to plan the construction of the new highway.
We used an arial mosaic to analyze changes in land use over the last decade.
Creating an arial mosaic requires careful alignment of overlapping photographs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photomosaic - an image made from assembling many smaller photographs
