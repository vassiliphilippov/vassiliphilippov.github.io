clover fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊvər fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈkləʊvə fɜːn / (BrE)
Any of several aquatic ferns of the genus Marsilea, characterized by leaves divided into four clover-like leaflets.
aquatic fern of genus Marsilea

Examples

The pond was fringed with clover ferns that thrived in the shallow water.
Botanists often study the resilience of clover fern in wetland habitats.
We planted clover fern around the edges of our water garden for its unique appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Marsilea - Genus of aquatic ferns with four leaflets
