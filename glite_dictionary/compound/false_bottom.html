false bottom

1. NOUN

/ fɔls ˈbɑtəm / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈbɒtəm / (BrE)
a partition inside something such as a box, trunk, or ship, positioned above the actual bottom to conceal items or separate spaces
hidden partition in a container

Examples

The thief hid the stolen jewels under the false bottom of his suitcase.
Inspectors discovered a false bottom in the cargo hold of the ship.
She lifted the false bottom of the old trunk and revealed a hidden compartment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compartment - a separate section within a container
