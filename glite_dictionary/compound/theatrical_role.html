theatrical role

1. NOUN

/ θiˈætrɪkəl roʊl / (AmE), / θɪˈætrɪkəl rəʊl / (BrE)
the character or part played by an actor in a play or other stage performance
character played by an actor

Examples

After months of rehearsals, he finally landed his first theatrical role in a Broadway production.
Each theatrical role she takes on showcases her impressive range as an actress.
He spent hours studying the script to prepare for his theatrical role as Hamlet.

Synset Examples

she played the part of Desdemona

Related Terms

  • Synonym: role - the function or character played by a performer
  • Synonym: character - a person in a play or story
