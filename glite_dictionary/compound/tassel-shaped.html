tassel-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈtæsəl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈtæsəl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape or appearance of a tassel, typically long and hanging with threads or strands at one end.
shaped like a tassel

Examples

The plant produced tassel-shaped flowers that hung gracefully from its branches.
Her dress was adorned with tassel-shaped embellishments along the hem.
The chandelier's tassel-shaped crystals sparkled brilliantly in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tassel - A tuft of loose threads or cords bound at one end and hanging free at the other, used as an ornament
