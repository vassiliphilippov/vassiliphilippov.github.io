round angle

1. NOUN

/ raʊnd ˈæŋɡəl / (AmE), / raʊnd ˈaŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
An angle of 360 degrees, forming a complete circle.
angle of 360 degrees

Examples

A round angle measures exactly 360 degrees, completing a full rotation.
The hands of a clock sweep out a round angle in twelve hours.
In geometry, a round angle is the sum of four right angles.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: right angle - an angle of 90 degrees
  • Degree Lower: straight angle - an angle of 180 degrees
