Republic of Haiti

1. PROPN

/ həˈiti / (AmE), / haɪˈiːti / (BrE)
A country in the Caribbean, occupying the western part of the island of Hispaniola; became independent from France in 1804.
Caribbean country on Hispaniola

Examples

The Republic of Haiti declared its independence from France in 1804.
Many humanitarian organizations operate in the Republic of Haiti to provide aid after natural disasters.
The Republic of Haiti shares the island of Hispaniola with the Dominican Republic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Haiti - the country in the Caribbean on the island of Hispaniola

Etymology

The name 'Haiti' comes from the indigenous Taíno word 'Ayiti,' meaning 'land of high mountains.'
