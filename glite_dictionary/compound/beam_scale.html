beam scale

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːm skeɪl / (AmE), / ˈbiːm skeɪl / (BrE)
a portable balance device consisting of a pivoted bar with arms of unequal length, used for measuring weight
type of balance scale with a pivoted bar

Examples

The pharmacist used a beam scale to weigh the ingredients for the prescription.
Many antique shops display a classic beam scale as a symbol of traditional commerce.
A beam scale must be carefully calibrated before each use to ensure accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: balance - a weighing device with a horizontal bar and equal arms
  • Antonym: spring scale - a weighing device using a spring to measure weight
