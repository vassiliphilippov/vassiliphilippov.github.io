Azerbaijani language

1. NOUN

/ ˌæzərbaɪˈdʒɑni ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌazəbʌɪˈdʒɑːni ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken primarily in Azerbaijan and northwestern Iran.
Turkic language of Azerbaijan

Examples

He is fluent in both Russian and Azerbaijani language.
Many academic works have been translated into the Azerbaijani language.
The Azerbaijani language uses a modified Latin alphabet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Azeri - alternative name for Azerbaijani language
  • Synonym: Turkish - major Turkic language spoken in Turkey

Etymology

From Azerbaijan (name of the country) + English 'language'.
