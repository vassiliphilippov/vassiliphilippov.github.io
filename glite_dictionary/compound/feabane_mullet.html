feabane mullet

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːˌbeɪn ˈmʌlɪt / (AmE), / ˈfiːˌbeɪn ˈmʌlɪt / (BrE)
A hairy perennial Eurasian herb with yellow, daisy-like flowers, believed to repel or destroy fleas.
plant reputed to repel fleas

Examples

The dried leaves of feabane mullet were once used to protect homes from fleas.
Gardeners appreciate feabane mullet not only for its yellow flowers but also for its pest-repellent qualities.
She planted feabane mullet along the fence hoping it would deter insects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fleabane - plant believed to repel fleas
