Bay of Biscay

1. PROPN

/ ˌbeɪ əv ˈbɪskeɪ / (AmE), / ˌbeɪ əv ˈbɪskeɪ / (BrE)
an arm of the Atlantic Ocean off the west coast of France and the north coast of Spain
bay off France and Spain

Examples

Many ships have encountered rough weather in the Bay of Biscay.
The coastline along the Bay of Biscay is known for its dramatic cliffs and beaches.
Migratory whales are sometimes spotted in the Bay of Biscay during their seasonal journeys.
