serving dish

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːvɪŋ dɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪŋ dɪʃ / (BrE)
A dish used for presenting and serving food at the table.
dish used for serving food

Examples

We placed the vegetables in a large serving dish for everyone to share.
She handed me a beautiful ceramic serving dish filled with pasta.
After the meal, the empty serving dish was quickly cleared from the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: platter - a large flat serving dish
