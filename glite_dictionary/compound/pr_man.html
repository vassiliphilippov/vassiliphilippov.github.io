PR man

1. NOUN

/ ˌpiː ˈɑːr mæn / (AmE), / ˌpiː ˈɑː mæn / (BrE)
a man whose job is to manage publicity and public relations for an organization or individual
male public relations worker

Examples

The company's PR man worked tirelessly to improve its public image after the scandal.
As a PR man, he was skilled at turning bad news into a positive story.
Many politicians rely on a trusted PR man to handle media questions and events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: publicist - a person who manages publicity for others
  • Antonym: PR woman - a female equivalent of PR man; woman in public relations

Etymology

Abbreviation PR from 'public relations' + 'man'.
