twilight hour

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwaɪˌlaɪt aʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈtwaɪlaɪt aʊə / (BrE)
the period of time between sunset and full darkness, when the sky is partly lit
time between sunset and night

Examples

Every evening, the park looked magical during the twilight hour.
Birds often return to their nests at the twilight hour.
She enjoyed taking long walks at the twilight hour when the world felt calm.

Synset Examples

She loved reading during the twilight hours.
The twilight sky was painted with shades of orange.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dusk - the darker stage of twilight just before night
  • Degree Lower: sunset - the time when the sun disappears below the horizon
  • Degree Higher: nightfall - the time when night begins, following the twilight
