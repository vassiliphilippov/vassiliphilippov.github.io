insignia of rank

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈsɪɡniə əv ræŋk / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɪɡniə əv ræŋk / (BrE)
a badge or symbol worn on a military uniform to indicate the wearer's rank
badge showing military rank

Examples

The general's uniform was decorated with several insignia of rank on each shoulder.
Each insignia of rank clearly distinguished officers from enlisted personnel.
During the ceremony, he was formally presented with the insignia of rank for his promotion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: badge - an emblem or token worn to show membership, rank, or achievement
  • Synonym: epaulette - a type of ornamental shoulder piece on military uniforms indicating rank
