gym rat

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪm ræt / (AmE), / dʒɪm ræt / (BrE)
a person who spends a great deal of time exercising or training in a gymnasium or health club, often to the exclusion of other activities
person who spends much time at the gym
slang

Examples

After work, he turns into a total gym rat, spending hours lifting weights and running on the treadmill.
Ever since she joined the new health club, Jane has become a real gym rat.
Some people relax by watching TV, but Mark prefers being a gym rat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fitness enthusiast - a person strongly devoted to physical fitness activities
  • Antonym: couch potato - a person who spends a lot of time sitting and watching television, and not exercising

/ dʒɪm ræt / (AmE), / dʒɪm ræt / (BrE)

person who spends much time at the gym
informal

Examples

She proudly calls herself a gym rat because she goes to the gym every day.
Even on holidays, the gym rat in him couldn’t resist a quick workout.
