sex bomb

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛks bɑm / (AmE), / ˈsɛks bɒm / (BrE)
a young woman who is considered extremely sexually attractive
very sexually attractive woman
informal

Examples

The actress became a sex bomb after her breakout role in the movie.
Tabloid magazines often label rising stars as the latest sex bomb in Hollywood.
Some people find the term sex bomb outdated or objectifying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bombshell - a very attractive woman
