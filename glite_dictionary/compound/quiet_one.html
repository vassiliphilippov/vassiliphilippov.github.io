quiet one

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwaɪət wʌn / (AmE), / ˈkwaɪət wʌn / (BrE)
the least talkative or most reserved person in a group
least talkative or most reserved person

Examples

Although he was the quiet one, he always had the best ideas during meetings.
In every group of friends, there is usually a quiet one who listens more than they speak.
As the quiet one in her class, she often went unnoticed by teachers.

Synset Examples

Nork was always the quiet one in the group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: introvert - a shy, reticent person
