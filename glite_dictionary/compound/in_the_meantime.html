in the meantime

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðə ˈmiːnˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈmiːnˌtaɪm / (BrE)
during the period between two events or while waiting for something to happen
during the period between events

Examples

We're fixing the car, but in the meantime you can use mine.
The repairs will take a week. In the meantime, try to use public transport.
Several candidates have been interviewed; in the meantime, the company will continue reviewing applications.

Synset Examples

meanwhile I will not think about the problem
meantime he was attentive to his other interests
in the meantime the police were notified

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meanwhile - at the same time; during this period
