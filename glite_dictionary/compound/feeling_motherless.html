feeling motherless

1. ADJ

/ ˈfiːlɪŋ ˈmʌðərlɪs / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪŋ ˈmʌðələs / (BrE)
experiencing deep emotional loss or absence due to the loss of one's mother
experiencing loss of one's mother

Examples

After her mother died, she spent months feeling motherless and alone.
The novel's protagonist is feeling motherless as she tries to rebuild her life.
Even surrounded by friends, he was quietly feeling motherless during the holidays.

Synset Examples

She was left feeling motherless after the loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orphaned - having lost both parents, especially as a child
