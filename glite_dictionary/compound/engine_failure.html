engine failure

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛn.dʒɪn ˈfeɪ.ljɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛn.dʒɪn ˈfeɪ.ljə / (BrE)
a situation in which an engine stops working or does not run properly
engine stops working

Examples

The flight was forced to make an emergency landing due to engine failure.
Regular maintenance reduces the risk of engine failure in cars and trucks.
His boat drifted for hours after an unexpected engine failure at sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breakdown - a mechanical failure, especially of a machine or vehicle
  • Synonym: malfunction - failure to function properly
