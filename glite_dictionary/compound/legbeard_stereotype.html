legbeard stereotype

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛɡˌbɪrd ˈstɛriˌoʊtaɪp / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡˌbɪəd ˈstɛriəˌtaɪp / (BrE)
An internet stereotype of a socially awkward woman, often associated with gamer or online communities and characterized by socially inept behavior.
internet stereotype of a socially awkward woman
informal slang

Examples

Many online forums have discussed the legbeard stereotype and its impact on women's participation in gaming.
The documentary highlights how the legbeard stereotype perpetuates negative assumptions about female gamers.
Critics argue that the legbeard stereotype fails to represent the diversity within the gaming community.

Synset Examples

The legbeard stereotype is often unfair in gaming circles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neckbeard stereotype - internet stereotype of a socially awkward man

Etymology

Formed from 'legbeard' (modeled after 'neckbeard', a term for socially awkward men) and 'stereotype'.
