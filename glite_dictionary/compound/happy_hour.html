happy hour

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæpi aʊər / (AmE), / ˈhæpi aʊə / (BrE)
A period of time, typically in the late afternoon or early evening, when a bar or restaurant offers alcoholic drinks at reduced prices.
time with reduced drink prices in bars

Examples

Let's meet at the bar during happy hour for some discounted drinks.
The restaurant's happy hour specials attract a large crowd every Friday.
She always orders a cocktail during happy hour to take advantage of the lower prices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cocktail hour - time when cocktails are served, often before dinner

2. NOUN

/ ˈhæpi ˌaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈhæpi ˌaʊə / (BrE)
a period of time, usually in the late afternoon or early evening at a bar or restaurant, when drinks and sometimes food are sold at reduced prices
period of discounted drinks and food

Examples

We often go to the pub for happy hour on Fridays after work.
The restaurant offers half-price appetizers during happy hour.
She invited her colleagues to join her at happy hour to celebrate her promotion.

Synset Examples
