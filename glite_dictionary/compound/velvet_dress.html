velvet dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛlvɪt drɛs / (AmE), / ˈvɛlvɪt drɛs / (BrE)
A dress made from velvet fabric, typically worn for formal or special occasions.
dress made of velvet fabric

Examples

She wore a stunning velvet dress to the gala and captured everyone's attention.
The shop window displayed an elegant red velvet dress perfect for the holidays.
After searching for hours, she finally found the perfect velvet dress for the winter ball.
