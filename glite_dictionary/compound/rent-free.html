rent-free

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛntˈfriː / (AmE), / ˌrɛntˈfriː / (BrE)
Provided without requiring the payment of rent; complimentary.
provided without payment of rent

Examples

The caretaker was offered a rent-free cottage on the estate.
With her new job, she received a rent-free apartment in the city.
Employees who live on-site are sometimes provided rent-free housing as part of their benefits.

Synset Examples

with the job came a rent-free apartment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: free - costing nothing, without charge
