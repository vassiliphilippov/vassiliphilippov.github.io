creative design

1. NOUN

/ kriˈeɪt̬ɪv dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / kriˈeɪtɪv dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
the process of developing original or imaginative ideas and applying them to the design of objects, products, or concepts
innovative or imaginative design process

Examples

The company is known for its creative design in household appliances, making everyday items both beautiful and practical.
She studied creative design at university, where she learned how to develop innovative solutions to real-world problems.
Many startups rely on creative design to stand out in a crowded market.

Synset Examples

She won the contest with her creative plumpkin design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: innovation - the process of creating new ideas or methods
  • Antonym: conventional design - design using traditional or established methods, not innovative
