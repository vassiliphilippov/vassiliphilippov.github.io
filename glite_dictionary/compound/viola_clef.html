viola clef

1. NOUN

/ viˈoʊlə klɛf / (AmE), / vɪˈəʊlə klɛf / (BrE)
the clef that places middle C on the third line of the staff, used especially for viola music
musical clef placing middle C on third staff line

Examples

The viola clef is essential for reading music written specifically for the viola.
Many students find it challenging to switch from treble clef to the viola clef.
The sheet music for this piece was printed in viola clef, not treble clef.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alto clef - the clef that places middle C on the third line of the staff
