quirk moulding

1. NOUN

/ kwɝk ˈmoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / kwɜːk ˈməʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
A type of moulding with a small groove separating the convex part from the adjoining surface.
moulding with a small groove

Examples

The ornate doorway featured a quirk moulding to add character to the trim.
Our carpenter recommended using a quirk moulding for the base of the column.
The frame's delicate quirk moulding created a subtle play of light and shadow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moulding - a strip of material used for decoration or finishing
