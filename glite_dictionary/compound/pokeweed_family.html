pokeweed family

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊkˌwiːd ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈpəʊkˌwiːd ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of chiefly tropical herbaceous plants, including some shrubs and trees, with racemose flowers; the Phytolaccaceae family
family of tropical herbaceous plants (Phytolaccaceae)

Examples

The pokeweed family includes a variety of tropical plants found around the world.
Some members of the pokeweed family are cultivated for their striking berries and foliage.
Scientists studied genetic differences among species within the pokeweed family.
