charcoal-grey

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɑːrˌkoʊl ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːkɔːl ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
of a very dark shade of grey, resembling the color of charcoal
very dark grey color

Examples

She wore a charcoal-grey suit to the interview, making a strong impression.
The walls of the living room were painted a subtle charcoal-grey to create a modern look.
He prefers charcoal-grey trousers because they go well with almost any shirt.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gray - color between black and white
  • Degree Higher: black - the darkest color, absence of light
