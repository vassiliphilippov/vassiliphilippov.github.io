sword stick

1. NOUN

/ sɔrd stɪk / (AmE), / sɔːd stɪk / (BrE)
A walking stick or cane that conceals a hidden sword or dagger inside it.
cane concealing a sword or dagger

Examples

The collector proudly displayed his antique sword stick at the exhibition.
He carried a sword stick for protection while traveling through dangerous areas.
Victorian gentlemen sometimes used a sword stick as both a walking aid and a concealed weapon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sword cane - a cane concealing a sword or dagger
