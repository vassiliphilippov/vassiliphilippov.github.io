Virginia crownbeard

1. NOUN

/ vərˈdʒɪniə ˈkraʊnˌbɜrd / (AmE), / vəˈdʒɪniə ˈkraʊnˌbɜːd / (BrE)
A tall perennial herb native to the eastern United States, bearing clusters of white flowers.
a tall herb with white flowers, eastern US

Examples

The Virginia crownbeard can often be found growing along roadsides and in open fields.
Botanists identified several specimens of Virginia crownbeard during their survey of the wetland.
The clusters of white flowers make the Virginia crownbeard easy to recognize in late summer.
