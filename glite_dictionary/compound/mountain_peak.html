mountain peak

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntən piːk / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn piːk / (BrE)
the pointed top or highest part of a mountain
the summit of a mountain

Examples

We could see the snow-covered mountain peak shining in the morning sun.
Many climbers dream of reaching the mountain peak after hours of hiking.
The mountain peak was visible for miles around, standing tall above the clouds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summit - the highest point of a mountain or hill
  • Synonym: mountaintop - the very top of a mountain
