rain barrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪn ˌbærəl / (AmE), / ˈreɪn ˌbærəl / (BrE)
a large container used to collect and store rainwater, typically from rooftops, for later use
barrel for collecting rainwater

Examples

The rain barrel behind the garage is full after last night’s storm.
Many gardeners use a rain barrel to conserve water for their plants.
She installed a rain barrel to collect runoff from the roof.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cistern - a tank for storing water, especially one supplying taps or as part of a flushing toilet
