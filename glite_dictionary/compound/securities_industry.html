securities industry

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈkjʊrətiz ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərɪtiz ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
The sector comprising all markets and businesses involved in the issuance, trading, and regulation of financial securities such as stocks and bonds.
sector for trading financial securities

Examples

Working in the securities industry requires knowledge of financial markets and regulations.
The securities industry plays a vital role in raising capital for businesses worldwide.
New laws have been introduced to increase transparency in the securities industry.

Synset Examples

the market always frustrates the small investor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stock market - the market where stocks are bought and sold
  • Synonym: bond market - the market for trading bonds
