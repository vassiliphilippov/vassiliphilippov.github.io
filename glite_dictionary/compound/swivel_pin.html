swivel pin

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪvəl pɪn / (AmE), / ˈswɪvəl pɪn / (BrE)
a bolt or pin that acts as a pivot point in a steering joint, especially in motor vehicles
pivot bolt in vehicle steering joint

Examples

The mechanic inspected the swivel pin for signs of excessive wear.
A damaged swivel pin can lead to serious steering problems in your car.
Greasing the swivel pin helps maintain smooth steering operation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kingpin - the main pivot in the steering mechanism of a vehicle
