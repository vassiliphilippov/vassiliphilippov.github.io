Belgian waffle

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛl.dʒən ˈwɑː.fəl / (AmE), / ˈbɛl.dʒən ˈwɒf.əl / (BrE)
A thick, sweet waffle with deep pockets, often served with ice cream, fruit sauce, or whipped cream.
a thick, sweet waffle with deep pockets

Examples

For brunch, I ordered a Belgian waffle topped with fresh strawberries and whipped cream.
Kids love eating Belgian waffles with a scoop of vanilla ice cream on top.
The café is famous for its homemade Belgian waffles and rich chocolate sauce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waffle - a crisp, leavened batter cake baked in a waffle iron

Etymology

Named after Belgium, where this style of waffle originated.
