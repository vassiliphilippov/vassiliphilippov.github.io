schmancy vibe

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃmænsi vaɪb / (AmE), / ˈʃmænsi vaɪb / (BrE)
A stylish or fancy atmosphere, often used humorously or informally.
stylish or fancy atmosphere
informal

Examples

The new cocktail bar has a real schmancy vibe with its velvet couches and low lighting.
Despite the schmancy vibe, the staff were friendly and welcoming.
That rooftop lounge tries to create a schmancy vibe with its modern design and curated playlist.

Synset Examples

The restaurant had a schmancy vibe with its elegant decor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fancy atmosphere - an elegant or stylish environment
  • Antonym: casual vibe - a relaxed or informal atmosphere

Etymology

From playful reduplication of "fancy" with "schm-" prefix (imitative of Yiddish reduplication), combined with "vibe" (short for "vibration", sense of atmosphere).
