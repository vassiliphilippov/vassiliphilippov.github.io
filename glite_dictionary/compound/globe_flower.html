globe flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡloʊb ˌflaʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊb ˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
any of several plants of the genus Trollius, which have round, yellow flowers
plant with globular yellow flowers (Trollius genus)

Examples

The garden was full of globe flowers blooming in the summer sunlight.
She planted a row of globe flowers along the pond's edge for a splash of yellow.
The vivid yellow globe flower is a favorite among gardeners in cool climates.
