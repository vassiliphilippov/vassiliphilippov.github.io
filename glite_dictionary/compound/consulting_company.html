consulting company

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsʌl.tɪŋ ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / kənˈsʌl.tɪŋ ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)
a company that provides expert professional advice to other organizations for a fee
company giving expert business advice

Examples

She hired a consulting company to help modernize her organization's IT systems.
The consulting company offered strategic advice on how to improve efficiency.
Many firms turn to a consulting company during periods of rapid growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consultancy - the practice of giving expert advice within a particular field
