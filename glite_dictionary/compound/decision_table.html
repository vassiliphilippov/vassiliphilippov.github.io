decision table

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈsɪʒ.ən ˈteɪ.bəl / (AmE), / dɪˈsɪʒ.ən ˈteɪ.bəl / (BrE)
a table that lists all possible conditions and the actions to take for each combination, used to aid decision-making processes
table showing conditions and actions for decisions

Examples

Before programming the application, the team created a decision table to map out all possible user actions.
A well-designed decision table can simplify complex business rules and make them easier to understand.
Using a decision table, analysts can systematically consider every possible combination of conditions.
