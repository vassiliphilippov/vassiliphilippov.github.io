retired person

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈtaɪɚd ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / rɪˈtaɪəd ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who has stopped working because they have reached the age when work is usually stopped or because of health or other reasons
someone who has stopped working

Examples

After forty years in education, my father became a retired person and started traveling the world.
As a retired person, she enjoys gardening and volunteering in her community.
The city offers special discounts to every retired person living within the municipality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pensioner - a person who receives a pension, especially from the government
  • Synonym: senior citizen - an older person, usually over a certain age such as 60 or 65
