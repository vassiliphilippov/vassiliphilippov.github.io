Taos art colony

1. NOUN

/ taʊs ɑrt ˈkɑləni / (AmE), / taʊs ɑːt ˈkɒləni / (BrE)
a community of artists established in Taos, northern New Mexico, known for its influence on American art in the early 20th century
artists' colony in Taos, New Mexico

Examples

The Taos art colony attracted painters and writers inspired by the landscape and local culture.
She studied the works produced during the heyday of the Taos art colony.
Many artists from the Taos art colony became prominent figures in American art history.
