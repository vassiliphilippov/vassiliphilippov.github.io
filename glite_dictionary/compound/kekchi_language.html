Kekchi language

1. NOUN

/ kɛkˈtʃi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / kɛkˈtʃi ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Mayan language spoken by the Kekchi people, primarily in Guatemala and Belize.
Mayan language of the Kekchi people

Examples

The Kekchi language is still widely spoken in many villages of Guatemala.
She learned to speak the Kekchi language during her fieldwork with the community.
There are several radio programs broadcast in the Kekchi language to serve local listeners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mayan language - any of the languages belonging to the Mayan language family

Etymology

From the name of the Kekchi (also spelled Q'eqchi'), an indigenous Maya people.
