Sloppy Joe

1. NOUN

/ ˈslɑpi ˌdʒoʊ / (AmE), / ˈslɒpi ˌdʒəʊ / (BrE)
a sandwich made with ground beef cooked in a spicy tomato-based sauce and served on a hamburger bun
sandwich with seasoned ground beef sauce
american

Examples

My mom made Sloppy Joes for dinner last night, and the kitchen smelled amazing.
At the cafeteria, I ordered a Sloppy Joe with a side of fries.
Kids often love the messy, tangy flavor of a classic Sloppy Joe.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: burger - a sandwich consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat served in a bun

Etymology

Possibly from the informal use of 'sloppy' (messy) and the personal name 'Joe', first recorded in the United States in the 1940s.
