regular payment

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛɡjələr ˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊlə ˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
a payment that is made at fixed intervals, such as weekly, monthly, or annually
a recurring scheduled payment

Examples

Many people set up a regular payment for their utility bills to avoid missing deadlines.
The gym membership is charged as a regular payment each month from my account.
He arranged a regular payment to his landlord to cover the rent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: installment - a sum of money paid as part of a series of payments
  • Antonym: lump sum - a single payment of a large amount of money at once
