Camberwell beauty

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæmbərˌwɛl ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / ˈkæmbəwɛl ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
A large butterfly found in temperate regions, recognized by its dark purple wings bordered with yellow.
a butterfly with dark purple, yellow-bordered wings

Examples

We spotted a rare Camberwell beauty while walking through the woods.
The Camberwell beauty is admired for its striking coloration and elegant flight.
Conservationists are concerned about the declining numbers of the Camberwell beauty in Britain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mourning cloak - North American name for the Camberwell beauty butterfly
