garden pepper cress

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdn ˈpɛpər krɛs / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˈpɛpə kɹɛs / (BrE)
an annual herb with pungent, peppery leaves, used as a salad green and garnish
annual herb used in salads and garnishes

Examples

She served the dish with a sprinkle of fresh garden pepper cress on top for added flavor.
We grow garden pepper cress in our backyard to use in salads throughout the summer.
The sharp taste of garden pepper cress complements both sandwiches and soups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peppergrass - another name for several garden cresses with peppery flavor
  • Degree Higher: cress - a type of fast-growing edible herb
