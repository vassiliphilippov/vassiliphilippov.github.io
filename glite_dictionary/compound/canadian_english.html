Canadian English

1. NOUN

/ kəˈneɪdiən ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ / (AmE), / kəˈneɪdiən ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ / (BrE)
the variety of the English language spoken and written in Canada, with distinctive features in pronunciation, spelling, and vocabulary
the variety of English used in Canada

Examples

Many words in Canadian English are spelled the same way as in British English.
He teaches a course on the history of Canadian English.
There are subtle pronunciation differences that distinguish Canadian English from other varieties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: British English - the variety of English used in the United Kingdom
  • Synonym: American English - the variety of English used in the United States
