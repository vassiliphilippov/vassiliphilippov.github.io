sugar refinery

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃʊɡɚ rɪˈfaɪnəri / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡə rɪˈfaɪnəri / (BrE)
a factory where raw sugar is processed and purified into refined sugar
factory for refining sugar

Examples

The new sugar refinery in town provides jobs for hundreds of local workers.
After harvest, the cane is transported directly to the sugar refinery for processing.
Environmental regulations have been tightened for the old sugar refinery near the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refinery - an industrial plant for purifying a crude substance
