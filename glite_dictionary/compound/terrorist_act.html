terrorist act

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛrərɪst ækt / (AmE), / ˈtɛrərɪst ækt / (BrE)
a deliberate use or threat of violence against civilians to achieve political, religious, or ideological goals, typically by instilling fear or coercion
violent action for political or ideological aims

Examples

The bombing of the embassy was widely condemned as a terrorist act by the international community.
The government increased security measures following a spate of terrorist acts across the country.
He was accused of planning a terrorist act intended to cause widespread panic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrorism - the unlawful use of violence and intimidation for political aims
