being a nympho

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːɪŋ ə ˈnɪmfoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbiːɪŋ ə ˈnɪmfəʊ / (BrE)
exhibiting excessive sexual desire, often in a way perceived as abnormal or inappropriate (slang, vulgar)
exhibiting excessive sexual desire (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar

Examples

People sometimes judge her for being a nympho, but she is just open about her desires.
He confessed to his therapist that he worried about being a nympho.
Tabloids often love to speculate about celebrities supposedly being a nympho.

Synset Examples

Her friends teased her for being a nympho at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nympho - a person with excessive sexual desire (slang, vulgar)
  • Synonym: nymphomaniac - a woman with excessive sexual desire

Etymology

From 'nympho', a slang abbreviation of 'nymphomaniac', itself from Greek nymphē (bride, young woman) + mania (madness).
