literary study

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪtərəˌri ˈstʌdi / (AmE), / ˈlɪtərəri ˈstʌdi / (BrE)
the academic analysis and interpretation of literature, especially as a field of humanistic inquiry
study and analysis of literature

Examples

He devoted his career to the literary study of 19th-century poetry.
The university offers a comprehensive program in literary study and theory.
Advances in literary study often reflect broader cultural and social changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: literary criticism - the analysis and judgment of the merits and faults of literature
  • Degree Lower: literature - written works, especially those considered of superior or lasting artistic merit
