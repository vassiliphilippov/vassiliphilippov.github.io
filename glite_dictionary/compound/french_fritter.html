French fritter

1. NOUN

/ frɛntʃ ˈfrɪtər / (AmE), / frɛntʃ ˈfrɪtə / (BrE)
A deep-fried, yeast-leavened pastry, similar to a doughnut, often dusted with confectioners' sugar.
deep-fried yeast pastry; like a doughnut

Examples

I ordered a French fritter with my coffee at the bakery this morning.
The French fritter was crisp on the outside and light on the inside.
She dusted the warm French fritters with powdered sugar before serving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doughnut - a small fried cake of sweetened dough, often ring-shaped
