soft and ploppy

1. ADJ

/ sɑft ənd ˈplɑpi / (AmE), / sɒft ənd ˈplɒpi / (BrE)
having a gentle, yielding, and somewhat wet or squishy texture
gentle, wet, squishy texture

Examples

After the heavy rain, the grass felt soft and ploppy beneath our feet.
The clay was soft and ploppy, perfect for shaping into figures.
Her cake had a soft and ploppy center that everyone enjoyed.

Synset Examples

After the rain, the ground was soft and ploppy underfoot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soft - having a smooth or yielding surface; not hard or firm
