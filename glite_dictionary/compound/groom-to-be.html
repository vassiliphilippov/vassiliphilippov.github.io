groom-to-be

1. NOUN

/ ɡrum tə ˈbi / (AmE), / ɡruːm tə ˈbiː / (BrE)
a man who is engaged to be married
man engaged to be married

Examples

He introduced his friend, the groom-to-be, to everyone at the engagement party.
The groom-to-be looked nervous but happy as he tried on his wedding suit.
Friends organized a bachelor party for the groom-to-be before his big day.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bride-to-be - a woman who is engaged to be married
