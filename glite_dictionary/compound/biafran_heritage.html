Biafran heritage

1. NOUN

/ biˈæfrən ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (AmE), / baɪˈɑːfrən ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (BrE)
the cultural background, traditions, and identity associated with Biafra or Biafran people
cultural background linked to Biafra

Examples

She often celebrates her Biafran heritage through music and storytelling.
Discussions about identity in Nigeria frequently mention the importance of Biafran heritage.
The museum's new exhibit explores the Biafran heritage and its influence on art.

Synset Examples

His Biafran heritage is a significant part of his identity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Biafran - relating to Biafra or its people
  • Synonym: heritage - cultural traditions or inheritance passed down

Etymology

Biafran, from Biafra (a secessionist state in southeastern Nigeria, 1967–70), combined with heritage (from Old French 'eritage', from Latin 'hereditatem', meaning inheritance).
