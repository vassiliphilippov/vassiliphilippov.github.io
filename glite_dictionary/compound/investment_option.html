investment option

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈvɛstmənt ˈɑpʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɛstmənt ˈɒpʃən / (BrE)
a choice or alternative available for investing money, such as stocks, bonds, or real estate
choice available for investing money

Examples

Before choosing an investment option, it's important to assess your risk tolerance and financial goals.
Real estate is often considered a safe investment option for long-term growth.
The company offers a range of investment options, including stocks, bonds, and mutual funds.

Synset Examples

The banker advised us on our investment options.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: investment - the act of putting money into something to make a profit
  • Synonym: option - a choice between two or more possibilities
