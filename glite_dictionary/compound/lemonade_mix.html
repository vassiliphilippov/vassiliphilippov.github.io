lemonade mix

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɛm.əˈneɪd mɪks / (AmE), / ˌlɛm.əˈneɪd mɪks / (BrE)
a commercially prepared powder or concentrate used to make lemonade when mixed with water
commercial lemonade powder or concentrate

Examples

She added water and ice to the lemonade mix to prepare a refreshing drink for the party.
The pantry was stocked with several boxes of lemonade mix for the summer.
Many people prefer homemade lemonade, but some find lemonade mix more convenient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemonade - a drink made from lemon juice, water, and sugar
