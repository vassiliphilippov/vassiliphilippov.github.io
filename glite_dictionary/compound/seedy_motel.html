seedy motel

1. NOUN

/ ˈsidi moʊˈtɛl / (AmE), / ˈsiːdi məʊˈtɛl / (BrE)
a hotel or inn that is shabby, disreputable, or in poor condition, often associated with unsavory activities or clientele
shabby or disreputable hotel

Examples

After losing all his money, he ended up staying in a seedy motel on the outskirts of town.
The detective followed the suspect into a seedy motel, hoping to catch him in the act.
They tried to avoid the seedy motel, but it was the only place with vacancies late at night.

Synset Examples

The movie was set in a seedy motel near the highway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: run-down hotel - a hotel that is in poor condition due to neglect
