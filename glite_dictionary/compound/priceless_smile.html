priceless smile

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪsləs smaɪl / (AmE), / ˈpraɪsləs smaɪl / (BrE)
a smile expressing immense joy or delight, regarded as invaluable or of great worth
a smile of great value or joy

Examples

She gave me a priceless smile when I handed her the gift.
His priceless smile lit up the entire room after hearing the good news.
After months apart, seeing her priceless smile again brought me to tears.

Synset Examples

Her smile was priceless during the surprise party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiant smile - a very bright and happy smile
  • Antonym: frown - a facial expression showing displeasure or sadness
