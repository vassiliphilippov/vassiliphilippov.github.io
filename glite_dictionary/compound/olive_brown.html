olive brown

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːlɪv braʊn / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv braʊn / (BrE)
a shade of brown that has a slight greenish tinge, resembling the color of olives
a brown shade with a green tinge

Examples

The walls of the living room were painted a soft olive brown that made the space feel cozy.
She wore a jacket in olive brown, which complemented her green scarf perfectly.
The bird's feathers blended in with the olive brown leaves on the forest floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: olive - a yellowish-green color
