iced tea

1. NOUN

/ aɪst ti / (AmE), / aɪst tiː / (BrE)
a drink made from brewed tea that is cooled and served over ice, often sweetened or flavored with lemon
tea served cold over ice

Examples

On hot days, I prefer to drink iced tea on the porch.
She added a slice of lemon to her iced tea for extra flavor.
The café offers both sweetened and unsweetened iced tea on the menu.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tea - a hot beverage made by infusing dried tea leaves in boiling water

2. NOUN

/ aɪst ti / (AmE), / aɪst tiː / (BrE)
a cold beverage made by brewing tea and serving it with ice, sometimes sweetened or flavored
cold tea served with ice

Examples

On a summer afternoon, she enjoyed a tall glass of iced tea with lemon.
Would you like some iced tea or lemonade with your meal?
He ordered an iced tea to cool off after his run.
