stick figure

1. NOUN

/ stɪk ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / stɪk ˈfɪɡjə / (BrE)
a simple drawing of a person or animal in which the head is represented by a circle and the body, arms, and legs by straight lines
very simple drawing using lines and circles

Examples

As a child, she loved to draw stick figures in her notebooks.
The instructions included stick figures to demonstrate the proper way to assemble the tent.
His art style relies heavily on stick figures for humor and simplicity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outline - a line marking the outer edges of a shape

2. NOUN

/ stɪk ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / stɪk ˈfɪɡjə / (BrE)
a simple drawing of a person or animal, typically composed of lines for limbs and a circle for the head
simple drawing of a person or animal with lines

Examples

The directions included a stick figure showing how to lift the box safely.
As a child, she loved drawing stick figures on scraps of paper.
He drew a stick figure in the sand to mark the spot.

Synset Examples

He drew a stick figure to represent himself in the comic.

Related Terms
