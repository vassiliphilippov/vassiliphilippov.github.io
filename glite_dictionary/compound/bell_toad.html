bell toad

1. NOUN

/ bɛl toʊd / (AmE), / bɛl təʊd / (BrE)
A western North American frog distinguished by its taillike copulatory organ, commonly called the bell toad.
western North American frog species

Examples

Scientists studied the mating calls of the bell toad in its natural habitat.
The unique taillike organ of the bell toad sets it apart from other frogs.
Children enjoyed spotting a bell toad during their field trip in California.
