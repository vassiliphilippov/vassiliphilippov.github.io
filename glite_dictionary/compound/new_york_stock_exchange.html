New York Stock Exchange

1. PROPN

/ nuː ˈjɔrk stɑk ɪksˌtʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / njuː ˈjɔːk stɒk ɪksˌtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
the principal stock exchange in the United States, located at 11 Wall Street, New York City, where shares and other securities are bought and sold.
major US stock exchange in New York City

Examples

The New York Stock Exchange closed higher after the announcement of new economic policies.
Many of the world's largest companies are listed on the New York Stock Exchange.
She started her career as a trader at the New York Stock Exchange.
