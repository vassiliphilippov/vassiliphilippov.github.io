gentle giant

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛn.t̬əl ˈdʒaɪ.ənt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛn.tl̩ ˈdʒaɪ.ənt / (BrE)
a very large person who has a gentle and kind nature
kind, gentle large person

Examples

Despite his intimidating size, Mark was known as the gentle giant of the neighborhood.
The basketball team's gentle giant was admired for his sportsmanship and humility.
Children felt safe around the gentle giant, who always had a smile and a kind word.
