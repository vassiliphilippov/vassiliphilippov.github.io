spade bit

1. NOUN

/ speɪd bɪt / (AmE), / speɪd bɪt / (BrE)
a flat, thin drill bit with a center point and cutting edges on either side, used for boring holes in wood
flat wood-boring drill bit

Examples

He used a spade bit to drill a large hole through the wooden beam.
You will need a spade bit to make clean, wide holes for the wiring.
The carpenter reached for a spade bit when working on the new shelves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auger bit - a drill bit with a screw tip and spiral body for boring holes, especially in wood
