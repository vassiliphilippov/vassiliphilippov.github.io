rain cloud

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪn klaʊd / (AmE), / ˈreɪn klaʊd / (BrE)
a dark grey cloud that brings or indicates rain
cloud that brings rain

Examples

The sky darkened as a rain cloud moved overhead, promising a sudden downpour.
Children watched a rain cloud drift across the sun as they played in the yard.
Farmers welcomed the arrival of a large rain cloud after weeks of dry weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cloud - a visible mass of condensed water vapor in the sky
