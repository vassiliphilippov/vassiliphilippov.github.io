Algerian centime

1. NOUN

/ ælˈdʒɪriən sɛnˈtim / (AmE), / ælˈdʒɪəriən sɛnˈtiːm / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Algeria, equal to one hundredth of a dinar
Algerian currency unit (1/100 dinar)

Examples

Tourists received change in Algerian centimes after buying souvenirs at the market.
The price tag indicated both dinars and Algerian centimes for customer convenience.
Collecting old Algerian centimes has become a popular hobby among numismatists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: centime - a monetary subunit equal to one hundredth of the basic unit in various countries
  • Degree Higher: Algerian dinar - the basic monetary unit of Algeria

Etymology

From French 'centime' (one hundredth), used in francophone currency contexts.
