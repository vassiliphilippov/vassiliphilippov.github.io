dumb cluck

1. NOUN

/ dʌm klʌk / (AmE), / dʌm klʌk / (BrE)
An informal term for a person who is foolish or incompetent.
foolish or incompetent person (informal)
informal slang

Examples

Don't be such a dumb cluck and pay attention to what's being said.
He got lost in his own neighborhood, feeling like a real dumb cluck.
She called herself a dumb cluck after locking her keys in the car again.

Synset Examples

Don't be such a dumb cluck and pay attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
  • Synonym: idiot - a very foolish or stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - someone with exceptional intellectual or creative ability
