prayer beads

1. NOUN

/ ˈpreɪ.ɚ ˌbiːdz / (AmE), / ˈpreɪ.ə ˌbiːdz / (BrE)
a string of beads used to count prayers, commonly in religious practices such as Catholicism.
string of beads for counting prayers

Examples

She carried her prayer beads everywhere she went, using them to keep track of her daily devotions.
The monk silently ran his fingers along the prayer beads as he meditated in the temple.
Many faiths use prayer beads as an aid to focus and rhythm in prayer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rosary - a string of prayer beads used in Catholic devotion
