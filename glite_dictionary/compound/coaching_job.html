coaching job

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊtʃɪŋ dʒɑb / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtʃɪŋ dʒɒb / (BrE)
a position or employment as a professional coach, especially in sports or similar fields
employment as a professional coach

Examples

She applied for the coaching job at the local football club last summer.
After years as an assistant, he finally landed his first head coaching job.
Many former athletes go on to find a coaching job after retiring from competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coach - a person who trains and directs athletes or a sports team
