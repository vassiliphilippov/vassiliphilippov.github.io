celery salt

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛləri sɑlt / (AmE), / ˈsɛləri sɒlt / (BrE)
a seasoning made from ground celery seeds mixed with salt, used to flavor food
seasoning of celery seeds and salt

Examples

Sprinkle a little celery salt on your salad for extra flavor.
The recipe calls for one teaspoon of celery salt and some black pepper.
She prefers celery salt on her fries instead of regular salt.
