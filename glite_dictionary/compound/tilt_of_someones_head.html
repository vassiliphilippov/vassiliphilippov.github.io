tilt of someone's head

1. NOUN

/ tɪlt əv ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / tɪlt əv ˈsʌmwʌnz hɛd / (BrE)
a movement of the head to one side, typically indicating curiosity, confusion, or interest
head movement showing curiosity or confusion

Examples

With a puzzled tilt of her head, she tried to understand the question.
The dog's curious tilt of its head made everyone laugh.
He responded to the odd sound with a quick tilt of his head.

Synset Examples

She gave a slight tilt of her head in confusion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nod of someone's head - an up-and-down head movement showing agreement or affirmation
  • Antonym: shake of someone's head - side-to-side head movement, typically indicating disagreement
