nursing aide

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɝsɪŋ eɪd / (AmE), / ˈnɜːsɪŋ eɪd / (BrE)
a person who assists nurses by performing routine tasks in patient care that require little formal training
assistant in patient care with minimal training

Examples

The nursing aide helped the patient get out of bed and walk to the bathroom.
As a nursing aide, she made sure all the patients were comfortable throughout her shift.
Hospitals often rely on nursing aides to assist with basic patient care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nursing assistant - a person who helps nurses with patient care; synonym of nursing aide
