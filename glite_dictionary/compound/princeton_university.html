Princeton University

1. PROPN

/ ˈprɪns.tən juˌnɪ.vɝˈsɪ.ti / (AmE), / ˈprɪns.tən juːˈnɪ.vəˌsɪ.ti / (BrE)
A prestigious university located in Princeton, New Jersey, known for its academic excellence and research.
university in Princeton, New Jersey

Examples

He received his doctorate from Princeton University in 2005.
Many influential scientists have taught at Princeton University over the years.
The beautiful campus of Princeton University attracts visitors from around the world.

Etymology

Named after the town of Princeton, New Jersey, where the university was relocated in 1756.
