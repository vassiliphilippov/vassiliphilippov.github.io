tent peg

1. NOUN

/ tɛnt pɛɡ / (AmE), / tɛnt pɛɡ / (BrE)
a pointed peg pushed into the ground to secure the ropes or base of a tent
peg used to secure a tent

Examples

He hammered the tent peg into the ground to stop the tent from blowing away.
One of the tent pegs was missing, so the side of the tent kept collapsing.
Always check that each tent peg is firmly in place before leaving your campsite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stake - a strong wooden or metal post driven into the ground
