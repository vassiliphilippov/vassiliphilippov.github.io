human nature

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmən ˈneɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
the psychological and behavioral qualities and tendencies that are considered characteristic of all people
innate qualities and tendencies of humans

Examples

The novel explores different aspects of human nature in a time of crisis.
She had a deep understanding of human nature and could easily read people's intentions.
Many philosophers have debated whether human nature is fundamentally good or evil.

Synset Examples

a great observer of human nature

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humanity - the quality of being human; human beings collectively
