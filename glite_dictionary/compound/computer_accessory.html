computer accessory

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtɚ əkˈsɛsəri / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə əkˈsɛsəri / (BrE)
a device or item that can be added to a computer to enhance its capabilities or convenience
device added to a computer

Examples

Many people buy a computer accessory like a wireless mouse or external hard drive.
A good computer accessory can make your work more efficient and comfortable.
He spent extra money on every computer accessory he could find for his desktop.
