rules against fraternization

1. NOUN

/ ruːlz əˈɡɛnst ˌfrætərnəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ruːlz əˈɡɛn(st) ˌfrætə(n)ɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
policies or regulations that prohibit personal or romantic relationships between certain individuals, especially within a workplace or organization
workplace policy banning personal relationships

Examples

Because of the company's rules against fraternization, supervisors cannot date their subordinates.
Many organizations implement rules against fraternization to prevent conflicts of interest.
He was reprimanded for violating rules against fraternization with members of his team.

Synset Examples

The company has strict rules against fraternization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fraternization - the act of associating or forming a friendship, especially when improper or prohibited
