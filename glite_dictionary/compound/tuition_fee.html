tuition fee

1. NOUN

/ tuˈɪʃ.ən fiː / (AmE), / tjuˈɪʃ.ən fiː / (BrE)
a sum of money charged for teaching or instruction, especially at a college or university
money paid for education or instruction

Examples

The university raised its tuition fee for international students this year.
Before enrolling, she compared the tuition fee at several colleges.
Some scholarships will cover the entire tuition fee for qualified applicants.

Synset Examples

tuition and room and board were more than $25,000

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tuition - money paid for instruction, especially at a college or university
