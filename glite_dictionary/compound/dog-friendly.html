dog-friendly

1. ADJ

/ dɔgˈfrɛndli / (AmE), / dɒgˈfrɛndli / (BrE)
Welcoming or suitable for dogs; allowing or accommodating dogs.
welcoming or suitable for dogs

Examples

Many hotels now advertise themselves as dog-friendly to attract travelers with pets.
The new park in town is completely dog-friendly and even has water stations for dogs.
We were relieved to find a dog-friendly restaurant where our puppy could sit with us outside.

Synset Examples

They brought their Westie to the dog-friendly café.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pet-friendly - welcoming or suitable for pets in general
