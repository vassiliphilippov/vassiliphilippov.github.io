broadcast journalist

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɔdˌkæst ˈdʒɜrnəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdkɑːst ˈdʒɜːnəlɪst / (BrE)
A journalist who prepares and presents news or other information for radio or television.
journalist working in radio or television

Examples

After years as a newspaper reporter, she became a well-known broadcast journalist on national television.
The broadcast journalist reported live from the scene of the natural disaster.
Many aspiring reporters dream of becoming a broadcast journalist for a major news network.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: journalist - a person who writes for newspapers, magazines, or news websites or prepares news to be broadcast
