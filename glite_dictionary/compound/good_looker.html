good looker

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈlʊkər / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈlʊkə / (BrE)
A person or thing that is visually attractive, especially in terms of physical appearance.
an attractive person or thing
informal

Examples

She’s a good looker, always turning heads whenever she enters a room.
That sports car is a real good looker and draws plenty of attention on the streets.
Everyone agreed that the new neighbor was quite the good looker.

Synset Examples

She's a good looker, always turning heads.
That car is a good looker, isn't it?
He brought home a real good looker from the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty - a person or thing that is very attractive
  • Antonym: plain - a person who is not considered attractive
