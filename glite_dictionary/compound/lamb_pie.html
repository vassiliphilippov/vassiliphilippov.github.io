lamb pie

1. NOUN

/ læm paɪ / (AmE), / læm paɪ / (BrE)
A pie filled with lamb meat, typically baked with a savory filling encased in pastry.
pie filled with lamb meat

Examples

She ordered the lamb pie for dinner because it was the chef's specialty.
At the bakery, the lamb pie quickly sold out during lunch hours.
I baked a lamb pie with rosemary and vegetables for our family meal.

Synset Examples

We enjoyed a delicious lamb pie at the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shepherd's pie - dish made with minced lamb and mashed potato topping
