root beer

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːt ˌbɪr / (AmE), / ˈruːt ˌbɪə / (BrE)
A sweet, carbonated non-alcoholic drink flavored with extracts of roots and herbs, especially sassafras.
sweet carbonated drink flavored with roots and herbs

Examples

Would you like a glass of root beer with your meal?
She prefers root beer over traditional sodas because of its unique flavor.
Some homemade root beer recipes use natural botanical extracts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sarsaparilla - a similar carbonated drink made with sarsaparilla root
  • Degree Higher: ginger beer - a sweet, carbonated, non-alcoholic drink flavored with ginger

Etymology

Named for being originally made from the roots of plants, notably sassafras, plus 'beer' for its carbonated character.
