in a heartfelt way

1. ADV

/ ɪn ə ˈhɑrtˌfɛlt weɪ / (AmE), / ɪn ə ˈhɑːtfɛlt weɪ / (BrE)
with deep sincerity and genuine emotion; in a way that shows true feelings
with sincere and genuine emotion

Examples

She thanked her mentor in a heartfelt way for all the guidance and support.
He apologized in a heartfelt way, hoping his friends would understand his sincerity.
The author wrote in a heartfelt way about the challenges she had faced.

Synset Examples

I would dearly love to know

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wholeheartedly - with complete sincerity and commitment
