financial guru

1. NOUN

/ faɪˈnænʃəl ˈɡuːru / (AmE), / faɪˈnænʃl ˈɡuːruː / (BrE)
a person who is regarded as an expert in financial matters and gives advice or guidance on financial decisions
expert in financial matters

Examples

We hired a financial guru to help us manage our company's investments.
As a respected financial guru, she frequently appears on business news programs.
Many people follow the advice of a financial guru when planning for retirement.

Synset Examples

Find a financial guru to help with your investments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: financial expert - a person who has specialized knowledge of finance
  • Antonym: novice - a person new to or inexperienced in a subject or situation

Etymology

'Guru' from Hindi 'guru' (teacher, spiritual guide); 'financial' from finance + -al suffix.
