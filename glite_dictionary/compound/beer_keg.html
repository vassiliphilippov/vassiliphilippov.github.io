beer keg

1. NOUN

/ bɪr kɛɡ / (AmE), / bɪə kɛɡ / (BrE)
a large metal or wooden barrel used to store, transport, or serve beer, typically under pressure
barrel for storing or serving beer

Examples

The pub replaced the empty beer keg before the evening rush began.
We rented a beer keg for the party to serve all the guests.
A fresh beer keg was tapped at the start of Oktoberfest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keg - a small barrel, usually for storing liquids
