idiot box

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪdiət bɑks / (AmE), / ˈɪdiət bɒks / (BrE)
an informal or humorous term for a television set
informal term for television
informal slang

Examples

He sits in front of the idiot box for hours every evening.
Some people think watching the idiot box is a waste of time.
We unplugged the idiot box during our family vacation.

Synset Examples

the British call a tv set a ‘telly’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telly - informal British term for television
  • Synonym: television - a device for receiving and displaying television signals
