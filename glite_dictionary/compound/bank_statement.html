bank statement

1. NOUN

/ bæŋk ˈsteɪtmənt / (AmE), / bæŋk ˈsteɪtmənt / (BrE)
an official report issued periodically by a bank to a customer, listing all transactions and the current balance for an account
periodic report of account activity from a bank

Examples

I received my bank statement in the mail yesterday and checked all the transactions.
You can view your monthly bank statement online through the bank's website.
It's important to reconcile your bank statement with your personal records each month.

Synset Examples

I wish my bank statement arrived earlier in the month

Related Terms

  • Synonym: account statement - a statement summarizing activity on a financial account
