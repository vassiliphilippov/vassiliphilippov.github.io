committee member

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɪɾi ˈmɛmbər / (AmE), / kəˈmɪti ˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
a person who belongs to a committee, typically participating in its meetings and decision-making
member of a committee

Examples

She served as a committee member responsible for overseeing the project's progress.
Each committee member was given a copy of the meeting agenda prior to the discussion.
As a new committee member, he quickly became involved in organizing events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: committee - a group of people appointed for a specific function
  • Synonym: member - an individual belonging to a group or organization
