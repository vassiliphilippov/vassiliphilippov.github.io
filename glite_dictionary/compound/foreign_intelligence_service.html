Foreign Intelligence Service

1. PROPN

/ ˈfɔrən ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns ˈsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / ˈfɒrən ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
the Russian agency responsible for intelligence-gathering, foreign operations, and analysis outside of Russia
Russian external intelligence agency

Examples

The Foreign Intelligence Service plays a key role in Russia's external security strategy.
Investigations revealed that the Foreign Intelligence Service had been monitoring diplomatic communications closely.
The Foreign Intelligence Service has collaborated internationally to address threats from terrorism and organized crime.
