buried treasure

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛr.id ˈtrɛʒ.ər / (AmE), / ˈbɛr.id ˈtrɛʒ.ə / (BrE)
valuable items, such as gold or jewels, hidden underground or in a secret place
hidden valuable items, often underground

Examples

As a child, he dreamed of discovering buried treasure on a deserted island.
Pirate stories often mention maps that lead to buried treasure.
The old castle was rumored to hide buried treasure beneath its foundations.

Synset Examples

They searched for buried treasure on the old estate.
The legend spoke of buried treasure hidden in the hills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treasure - valuable things such as gold, jewels, or money
