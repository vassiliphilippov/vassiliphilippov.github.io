mule skinner

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːl ˌskɪnər / (AmE), / ˈmjuːl ˌskɪnə / (BrE)
a person who drives and takes care of mules, especially as a driver of mule teams
a person who drives mules

Examples

The experienced mule skinner guided the team safely along the mountain trails.
Many mule skinners were crucial to transporting goods during the gold rush.
As a mule skinner, he spent long days on the dusty roads with his animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muleteer - a person who drives a mule or mules
