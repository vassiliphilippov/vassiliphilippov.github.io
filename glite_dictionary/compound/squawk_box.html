squawk box

1. NOUN

/ skwɑk bɑks / (AmE), / skwɔːk bɒks / (BrE)
A loudspeaker, especially one used in an intercom or public address system.
loudspeaker on an intercom or PA system
informal

Examples

The security guard listened to the instructions coming through the squawk box at the front desk.
She buzzed reception using the squawk box installed by the office door.
Please announce your arrival through the squawk box before entering the building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intercom - A communication system within a building or group of buildings
  • Synonym: PA system - A system for amplifying sound to the public, especially announcements
