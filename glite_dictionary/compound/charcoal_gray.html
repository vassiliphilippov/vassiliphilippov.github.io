charcoal gray

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑrˌkoʊl ɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːkəʊl ɡreɪ / (BrE)
a very dark shade of grey, resembling the color of charcoal
very dark grey color

Examples

She chose a charcoal gray suit for the interview.
The walls were painted in charcoal gray to create a modern look.
His new car comes in charcoal gray, which gives it a sleek appearance.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: grey - the color between black and white
  • Degree Higher: black - the darkest color, absence of light
