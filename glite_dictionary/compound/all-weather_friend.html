all-weather friend

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔlˈwɛðɚ ˈfrɛnd / (AmE), / ˌɔːlˈwɛðə ˈfrɛnd / (BrE)
a reliable and loyal friend who supports you in all circumstances, whether good or bad
friend who is always dependable

Examples

Everyone needs at least one all-weather friend to lean on during hard times.
No matter what happens, my all-weather friend always stands by my side.
She has shown again and again that she is an all-weather friend.

Synset Examples

You need an all-weather friend to navigate life's ups and downs.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fair-weather friend - a person who is only a friend when things are going well
