genus Perca

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˈpɜːrkə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈpɜːkə / (BrE)
A genus of freshwater fish in the family Percidae, including the common perch.
a genus of freshwater perch fish

Examples

The yellow perch belongs to the genus Perca and is popular among anglers.
Scientists studied several species within the genus Perca to understand their distribution.
Many North American lakes are inhabited by fish from the genus Perca.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perch - a common freshwater fish in the genus Perca

Etymology

From Latin 'Perca', meaning 'perch', used in scientific taxonomy.
