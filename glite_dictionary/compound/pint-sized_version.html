pint-sized version

1. NOUN

/ paɪnt saɪzd ˈvɝʒən / (AmE), / paɪnt saɪzd ˈvɜːʃən / (BrE)
A smaller or miniature version of something; a copy or model that is much smaller than the original.
miniature version of something

Examples

She bought a pint-sized version of her favorite teddy bear for her little sister.
The museum store sells a pint-sized version of the famous sculpture as a souvenir.
He proudly built a pint-sized version of his dream house out of blocks.

Synset Examples

She has a pint-sized version of that doll.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miniature - A very small copy or model of something

Etymology

From 'pint-sized' (from pint, a small liquid measure) + 'version'.
