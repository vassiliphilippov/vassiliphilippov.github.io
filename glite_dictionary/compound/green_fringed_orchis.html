green fringed orchis

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin ˌfrɪndʒd ˈɔrkɪs / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˌfrɪnʤd ˈɔːkɪs / (BrE)
a North American orchid with greenish, deeply fringed flowers, native to the eastern United States
a North American orchid with greenish, fringed flowers

Examples

The green fringed orchis blooms in moist meadows during early summer.
Botanists tracked the shrinking habitats of the green fringed orchis in several states.
We spotted a rare green fringed orchis while hiking through the wetlands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fringed orchid - any orchid with irregularly fringed lips on the flower
