business success

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznɪs səkˈsɛs / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs səkˈsɛs / (BrE)
the achievement of goals or a high level of accomplishment in a business context
achievement or prosperity in business

Examples

Her innovative strategies played a major role in the company's business success.
Mentorship and leadership are key drivers of business success in competitive markets.
They celebrated their first major business success with a company-wide event.

Synset Examples

Networking is crucial for women's business success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: success - the achievement of an aim or purpose
  • Antonym: business failure - a situation in which a business does not achieve its objectives or becomes insolvent
