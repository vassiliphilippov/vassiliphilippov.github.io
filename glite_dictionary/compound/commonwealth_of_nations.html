Commonwealth of Nations

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɑː.mənˌwɛlθ əv ˈneɪ.ʃənz / (AmE), / ˌkɒm.ənˌwɛlθ əv ˈneɪ.ʃənz / (BrE)
an international association consisting of the United Kingdom and many former British colonies, now independent states, cooperating on common interests
international association of the UK and former British colonies

Examples

Fifty-four countries currently belong to the Commonwealth of Nations.
The Commonwealth of Nations fosters cooperation and shared values among its members.
India joined the Commonwealth of Nations after gaining independence from Britain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: British Commonwealth - the previous name for the Commonwealth of Nations

Etymology

From 'commonwealth' (political community) and 'nations,' previously known as the 'British Commonwealth.'
