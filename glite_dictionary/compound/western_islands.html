Western Islands

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɛstɚn ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
a group of more than 500 islands off the western coast of Scotland, also known as the Outer Hebrides
islands off western Scotland (Outer Hebrides)

Examples

We spent our summer vacation exploring the rugged beauty of the Western Islands.
Many migratory birds can be spotted in the Western Islands during the spring and autumn months.
The Gaelic culture is still vibrant on the Western Islands of Scotland.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Outer Hebrides - another name for the Western Islands of Scotland
