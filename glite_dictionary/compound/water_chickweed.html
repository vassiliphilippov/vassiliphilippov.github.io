water chickweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔːtər ˈtʃɪkˌwid / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈtʃɪkˌwiːd / (BrE)
a small flowering plant of northern regions, also known as Indian lettuce; typically found in moist environments
a northern flowering plant, Indian lettuce

Examples

Water chickweed thrives in moist, shady areas near streams and ponds.
The leaves of water chickweed are sometimes eaten as a salad green in northern regions.
Hikers in Alaska may spot water chickweed growing along the forest edge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Indian lettuce - another common name for water chickweed
  • Degree Higher: chickweed - a related group of small flowering plants in the Caryophyllaceae family
