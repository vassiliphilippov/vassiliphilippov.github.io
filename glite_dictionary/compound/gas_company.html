gas company

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / ɡas ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)
A company or public utility that supplies gas for heating, cooking, or energy to homes and businesses.
company that supplies gas

Examples

The gas company sent an engineer to check for leaks in the kitchen.
After moving to the new house, she had to set up an account with the gas company.
Recent price changes from the gas company have affected many local businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utility company - a company that provides essential public services such as water, electricity, or gas
