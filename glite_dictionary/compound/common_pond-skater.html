common pond-skater

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈpɑnd ˌskeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈpɒnd ˌskeɪtə / (BrE)
a widely distributed species of water strider, an insect that skates on the surface of ponds and slow-moving water
a common species of water strider insect

Examples

The common pond-skater can often be seen gliding across still water in the summer months.
Children enjoy watching common pond-skaters dart around on the surface of a garden pond.
A common pond-skater uses the surface tension of water to support its weight as it moves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water strider - an insect that moves quickly across the surface of water
