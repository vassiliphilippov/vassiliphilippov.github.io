yapping dog

1. NOUN

/ ˈjæpɪŋ dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈjæpɪŋ dɒɡ / (BrE)
a dog that barks sharply and noisily, often in a persistent or irritating manner
noisy, sharp-barking dog

Examples

The yapping dog next door kept me awake all night.
Whenever someone passed by the gate, their yapping dog would start barking immediately.
She found the constant noise from the yapping dog to be incredibly annoying.

Synset Examples

He called the yapping dog a cur.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barking dog - a dog that barks
  • Antonym: quiet dog - a dog that is not noisy or does not bark
