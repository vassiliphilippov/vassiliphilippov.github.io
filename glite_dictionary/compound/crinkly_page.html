crinkly page

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪŋkli peɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪŋkli peɪdʒ / (BrE)
a page in a book or document that is wrinkled, creased, or has a textured surface, often from age or handling
a wrinkled or textured page

Examples

She gently turned the crinkly page of the old letter, careful not to tear it.
The crinkly page made a soft rustling sound as I flipped through the ancient book.
Humidity over the years had left every crinkly page warped and delicate.

Synset Examples

The old book had crinkly pages that whispered stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrinkled page - a page with creases or folds
  • Antonym: smooth page - a page without wrinkles or texture
