neckbeardy look

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛkˌbɪrdi lʊk / (AmE), / ˈnɛkbɪədi lʊk / (BrE)
A style of appearance typically associated with 'neckbeards', often characterized by unkempt grooming, casual or nerdy attire, and a lack of fashion awareness.
nerdy, unkempt style associated with neckbeards
informal slang

Examples

He tried to update his style, but the neckbeardy look still lingered in his wardrobe choices.
Some tech conferences seem full of attendees with a neckbeardy look.
Her friends joked that his gaming obsession was matched by his neckbeardy look.

Synset Examples

The neckbeardy look is quite popular in some circles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neckbeard - A man, typically regarded as socially awkward, who wears an unkempt beard on his neck

Etymology

Formed from 'neckbeard', an internet slang reference to unkempt facial hair, and 'look' (appearance).
