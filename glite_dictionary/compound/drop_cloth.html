drop cloth

1. NOUN

/ drɑp klɔθ / (AmE), / drɒp klɒθ / (BrE)
a large curtain that can be lowered and raised onto a stage from above, often used as background scenery in a theater
stage curtain used as scenery

Examples

The stage manager gave the signal to lower the drop cloth for the next scene.
A beautifully painted drop cloth created the illusion of a city skyline on stage.
The crew replaced the old drop cloth with a new one before the opening night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backdrop - a painted cloth hung at the back of a stage as part of the scenery
Source: WordNet

2. NOUN

/ drɑp klɔθ / (AmE), / drɒp klɒθ / (BrE)
A large piece of cloth used to protect floors or furniture from paint or dust during decorating or construction work.
protective cloth for painting or decorating

Examples

Make sure to lay a drop cloth over the carpet before you start painting the walls.
She covered the sofa with a drop cloth to protect it from dust during the renovation.
After finishing the work, he carefully folded the drop cloth and stored it away.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dust sheet - a cloth used to cover furniture or floors to protect from dust or paint
