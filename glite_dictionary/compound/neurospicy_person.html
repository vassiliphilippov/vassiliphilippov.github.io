neurospicy person

1. NOUN

/ ˌnjʊroʊˈspaɪsi ˈpɜrsən / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈspaɪsi ˈpɜːs(ə)n / (BrE)
A person who has a neurodivergent condition, such as autism or ADHD, and whose thinking or behavior is considered unconventional or unique.
neurodivergent person; unconventional thinker
informal

Examples

Many workplaces are beginning to recognize the value that a neurospicy person brings to their teams.
As a self-described neurospicy person, she offers unique insights during group discussions.
Teachers are adapting their methods to better support each neurospicy person in the classroom.

Synset Examples

I love how neurospicy people see the world differently.
His neurospicy personality makes him a creative thinker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neurodivergent - having a brain that functions in atypical ways

Etymology

Formed playfully from "neuro-" (relating to the nervous system) and "spicy" to evoke the idea of nontraditional or 'flavorful' neurological traits.
