string of beads

1. NOUN

/ strɪŋ əv bidz / (AmE), / strɪŋ əv biːdz / (BrE)
a number of beads threaded together on a string, typically used as jewelry or decoration
beads threaded on a string

Examples

She wore a beautiful string of beads around her neck for the party.
The children made a colorful string of beads during arts and crafts.
He carefully repaired the broken string of beads his grandmother gave him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: necklace - a piece of jewelry worn around the neck
  • Synonym: bracelet - a piece of jewelry worn around the wrist
