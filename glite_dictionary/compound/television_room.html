television room

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌvɪʒən ruːm / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a house or building specifically designated for watching television
room for watching television

Examples

The family gathered in the television room to watch their favorite show.
We converted the basement into a television room for movie nights.
Please keep the noise down in the television room while others are watching.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: living room - a room in a home for general everyday use
