computer industry

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtɚ ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
the collective manufacturers and businesses involved in producing, developing, and selling computers and related technology
sector producing computers and technology

Examples

The computer industry has seen tremendous growth over the last few decades.
Many jobs in the computer industry require advanced technical skills and knowledge.
Emerging startups are helping to reshape the computer industry with innovative products and services.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: technology sector - businesses involved broadly in technological products and services
