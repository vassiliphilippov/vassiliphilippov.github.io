full-dress uniform

1. NOUN

/ fʊl drɛs juˈnɪfɔrm / (AmE), / fʊl drɛs juˈnɪːfɔːm / (BrE)
a formal naval or military uniform prescribed by regulations for use on ceremonial occasions
formal military ceremonial uniform

Examples

The officers were required to wear their full-dress uniform for the official ceremony.
He looked impressive in his navy full-dress uniform adorned with medals.
Full military honors meant that every soldier appeared in full-dress uniform at the state funeral.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uniform - distinctive clothing worn by members of the same organization
