genus Calystegia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌkælɪsˈtiːdʒiə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌkælɪsˈtiːdʒɪə / (BrE)
A genus of climbing or scrambling herbaceous plants commonly known as bindweed, belonging to the family Convolvulaceae.
genus of climbing herbaceous plants (bindweed)

Examples

The genus Calystegia includes several species of bindweed found in temperate regions worldwide.
Gardeners often struggle to control plants from the genus Calystegia because of their rapid growth.
Members of the genus Calystegia are easily recognized by their funnel-shaped flowers and twining stems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bindweed - a common name for plants in the genus Calystegia

Etymology

From Greek 'kalystegē' (covering), referring to the plant's habit of covering other vegetation.
