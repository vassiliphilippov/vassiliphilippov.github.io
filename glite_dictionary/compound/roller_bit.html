roller bit

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊlər bɪt / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə bɪt / (BrE)
a drill bit with hardened rotating rollers, used especially for drilling through rock
drill bit with rotating rollers

Examples

The oil company upgraded its equipment to include a roller bit for deeper drilling.
A roller bit can efficiently crush and cut through the hardest layers of rock.
Engineers selected a Tricone roller bit to speed up the well-drilling process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drill bit - a cutting tool used to create cylindrical holes
