eastern United States

1. PROPN

/ ˈistərn juˌnaɪt̬ɪd steɪts / (AmE), / ˈiːstən juːˌnaɪtɪd steɪts / (BrE)
the region of the United States situated to the north of the Ohio River and to the east of the Mississippi River
region east of the Mississippi River in the US

Examples

Many of the oldest cities in the eastern United States were founded during the colonial era.
Heavy snowstorms are not uncommon in the eastern United States during winter.
The Appalachian Mountains stretch through much of the eastern United States.
