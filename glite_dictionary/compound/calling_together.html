calling together

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔlɪŋ təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / ˈkɔːlɪŋ təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
the act of assembling or gathering people for a meeting or purpose.
the act of assembling people

Examples

The calling together of the council was announced last week.
His calling together of the team ensured everyone understood the new strategy.
The mayor initiated a calling together of local businesses to discuss revitalization plans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: convocation - a large formal assembly of people
