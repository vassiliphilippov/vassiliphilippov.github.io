bear-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛr saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈbeə saɪzd / (BrE)
As large as a bear; very large or bulky.
as large as a bear

Examples

The explorers were startled by a bear-sized shadow moving through the trees at dusk.
He ordered a bear-sized portion of food that could have fed three people.
They adopted a bear-sized dog who quickly became the talk of the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bear-like - resembling a bear
  • Synonym: huge - very large in size, amount, or degree
