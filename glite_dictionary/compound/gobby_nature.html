gobby nature

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑbi ˈneɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈɡɒbi ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
a talkative, outspoken, and often loud or brash personality
talkative or outspoken personality
informal british

Examples

His gobby nature often got him in trouble at school.
People either love or hate her for her gobby nature.
With his gobby nature, he quickly became the center of attention at parties.

Synset Examples

His gobby nature often got him in trouble.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outspoken - willing to speak one's mind freely
  • Antonym: reticent - not revealing one's thoughts or feelings readily

Etymology

From British slang 'gobby' meaning 'loud-mouthed' or 'talkative'.
