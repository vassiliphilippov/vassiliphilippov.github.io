decorative finial

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛkəˌreɪtɪv fəˈnaɪəl / (AmE), / ˈdɛkərətɪv fɪˈnaɪəl / (BrE)
an ornament, often sculpted or carved, placed at the top or end of an object such as a roof, post, or piece of furniture for decorative effect
ornamental feature at a top or end

Examples

She admired the decorative finial on the top of the antique bedpost.
The wrought-iron gate featured a unique decorative finial shaped like a fleur-de-lis.
Restorers carefully replaced the missing decorative finial at the building's peak.

Synset Examples

She chose a decorative finial to complete the fence.
The ornate finial gleamed in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finial - a decorative ornament at the top or end of an object

Etymology

From 'decorative' (from Latin 'decorare', to adorn) and 'finial' (from Latin 'finis', end).
