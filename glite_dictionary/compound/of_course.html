of course

1. ADV

/ əv ˈkɔrs / (AmE), / əv ˈkɔːs / (BrE)
used to show that something is obvious or expected, or to give permission or agreement.
used to indicate obviousness or agreement

Examples

Of course, I'll help you with your homework if you need it.
He forgot his umbrella at home, so of course he got wet in the rain.
Of course, everyone applauded when the winner was announced.

Synset Examples

naturally, the lawyer sent us a huge bill

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naturally - used to mean as expected or obviously
