mouse-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaʊs saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈmaʊs saɪzd / (BrE)
having approximately the same size as a mouse
about the size of a mouse

Examples

The research team discovered a mouse-sized mammal living in the dense rainforest.
She bought a mouse-sized plush toy for her little brother.
The robot is so compact it's almost mouse-sized and can fit in the palm of your hand.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: small - of a size that is less than normal or usual
