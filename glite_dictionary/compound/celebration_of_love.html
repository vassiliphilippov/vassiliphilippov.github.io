celebration of love

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛləˈbreɪʃən əv lʌv / (AmE), / ˌsɛlɪˈbreɪʃən əv lʌv / (BrE)
an event or ceremony that honors love and romantic relationships, often marking significant occasions such as weddings or anniversaries
event honoring love and relationships

Examples

Their anniversary party was a joyful celebration of love shared with friends and family.
The film depicts a grand celebration of love uniting two different cultures.
They organized a celebration of love to mark their engagement.

Synset Examples

The wedding was a beautiful celebration of love.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wedding - a marriage ceremony, especially considered as a celebration
  • Synonym: anniversary - the date on which an event took place in a previous year, especially a wedding
