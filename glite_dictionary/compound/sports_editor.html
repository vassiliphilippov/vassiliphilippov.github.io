sports editor

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrts ˌɛd.ɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts ˌed.ɪ.tə / (BrE)
the editor of a newspaper or magazine who is responsible for sports news and coverage
editor in charge of sports news

Examples

The sports editor assigned a team to cover the national football championship.
As sports editor, she oversaw all coverage of local sporting events.
He was promoted to sports editor after years as a junior reporter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: editor - person who works as an editor or oversees written content
