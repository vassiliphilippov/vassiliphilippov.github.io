Commerce Department

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑːmɚs dɪˌpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɜːs dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the United States federal department responsible for promoting and regulating domestic and international trade, and overseeing agencies such as the Census Bureau and Patent Office
U.S. department for trade and economic administration
american

Examples

The Commerce Department released new data on international trade this morning.
A spokesperson from the Commerce Department announced the launch of a new export initiative.
The Commerce Department oversees the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office.
