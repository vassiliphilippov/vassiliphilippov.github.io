sweet almond oil

1. NOUN

/ swit ˈælmənd ɔɪl / (AmE), / swiːt ˈɑːlmənd ɔɪl / (BrE)
a pale yellow oil obtained from sweet or bitter almonds, used in cooking, cosmetics, and medicine
oil from sweet or bitter almonds

Examples

Massage therapists often use sweet almond oil because it is gentle on sensitive skin.
You can substitute sweet almond oil for other oils in some baking recipes.
She applied sweet almond oil to her hair to make it shine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: almond oil - a general term for oil from almonds
