Texas toad

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛksəs toʊd / (AmE), / ˈtɛksəs təʊd / (BrE)
a nocturnal burrowing toad found in mesquite woodlands and prairies of the southwestern United States
a burrowing toad of the southwestern US

Examples

The Texas toad is well adapted to life in arid environments, often burrowing underground to avoid the heat.
We spotted a Texas toad near the mesquite trees just after sunset.
During the rainy season, Texas toads emerge in large numbers to breed in shallow puddles.
