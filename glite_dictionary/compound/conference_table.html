conference table

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːn.fɚ.əns ˌteɪ.bəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.fər.əns ˌteɪ.bəl / (BrE)
a large table around which people sit for meetings or discussions, especially in an office or boardroom
table used for meetings

Examples

The executives gathered around the conference table to discuss the merger.
A long, polished conference table dominated the boardroom.
She placed her reports neatly on the conference table before the meeting began.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meeting table - table used for holding meetings
