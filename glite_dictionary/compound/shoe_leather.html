shoe leather

1. NOUN

/ ʃuː ˈlɛðər / (AmE), / ʃuː ˈlɛðə / (BrE)
leather that is used to make shoes.
leather used for making shoes

Examples

The craftsman inspected the shoe leather for any imperfections before starting work.
Quality shoe leather is essential for durable and comfortable footwear.
Rising prices of shoe leather have affected many local shoemakers.
