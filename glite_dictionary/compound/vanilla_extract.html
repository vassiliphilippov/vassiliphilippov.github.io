vanilla extract

1. NOUN

/ vəˈnɪlə ɪkˌstrækt / (AmE), / vəˈnɪlə ɪkˌstrakt / (BrE)
a flavoring made by soaking vanilla beans in alcohol and used to add vanilla flavor to food and drinks
flavoring from vanilla beans in alcohol

Examples

Add a teaspoon of vanilla extract to the batter for extra flavor.
She prefers using pure vanilla extract instead of imitation vanilla in her recipes.
The recipe called for just a drop of vanilla extract to enhance the dessert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vanilla essence - an artificial substitute for vanilla extract
