on the first try

1. ADV

/ ɑn ðə fɝst traɪ / (AmE), / ɒn ðə fɜːst traɪ / (BrE)
at the first attempt; succeeding without needing to repeat the effort
at the first attempt

Examples

He managed to solve the puzzle on the first try, impressing everyone around him.
Some people can bake perfect bread on the first try, but it usually takes practice.
She hit the target on the first try in her archery lesson.

Synset Examples

Glory be, she passed her driving test on the first try!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at the first attempt - succeeding on a first effort
