ultrafunky art

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌltrəˌfʌŋki ɑrt / (AmE), / ˈʌltrəˌfʌŋki ɑːt / (BrE)
Art that is extremely modern, unconventional, and stylish, often breaking traditional rules or expectations.
very modern and unconventional art
informal

Examples

The gallery's new exhibit focuses entirely on ultrafunky art from the past decade.
She decorated her apartment walls with prints of ultrafunky art by emerging street artists.
His taste leans toward ultrafunky art that breaks away from established conventions.

Synset Examples

He loves ultrafunky art that challenges traditional styles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avant-garde - art that is innovative or ahead of its time
  • Antonym: conventional art - art that follows traditional styles and rules
