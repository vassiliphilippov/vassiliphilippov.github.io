police commissioner

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis kəˌmɪʃənər / (AmE), / pəˈliːs kəˈmɪʃənə / (BrE)
the head official appointed to oversee the duties and discipline of a police force
head official in charge of a police force

Examples

The police commissioner announced a new initiative to improve community relations.
After a long career, she was appointed police commissioner in the city.
The mayor met with the police commissioner to discuss recent crime statistics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police chief - the highest-ranking officer in a police department
