shitbucket day

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtbʌkɪt deɪ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtbʌkɪt deɪ / (BrE)
A day marked by a series of unpleasant or unfortunate events; a very bad day.
a very bad or unlucky day
slang vulgar

Examples

Yesterday was such a shitbucket day; my car broke down and I lost my wallet.
It feels like every Monday is a shitbucket day for me lately.
After the third disaster in a row, Mark declared it officially a shitbucket day.

Synset Examples

Everything went wrong; it felt like a shitbucket day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad day - a day during which many things go wrong or are unpleasant
