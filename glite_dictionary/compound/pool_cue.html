pool cue

1. NOUN

/ pul kju / (AmE), / puːl kjuː / (BrE)
a long, tapering stick used to strike the cue ball in the game of pool or billiards
rod used to strike ball in pool or billiards

Examples

He chalked his pool cue carefully before taking the shot.
The felt on my pool cue is starting to wear out.
She bought a new pool cue for the upcoming tournament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cue stick - stick used to strike cue ball in billiards
