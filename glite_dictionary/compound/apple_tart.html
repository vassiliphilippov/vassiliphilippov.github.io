apple tart

1. NOUN

/ ˈæpəl tɑrt / (AmE), / ˈæpəl tɑːt / (BrE)
A small open pastry case filled with sliced apples and sugar, typically baked until golden.
small open pie with sliced apples

Examples

She ordered a slice of apple tart with her coffee.
The bakery specializes in traditional French apple tarts.
He baked an apple tart for dessert last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apple pie - a pastry with a top and bottom crust, filled with apples

Would you like some cream with your slice of apple tart?
