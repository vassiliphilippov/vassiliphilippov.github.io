full-size

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʊl saɪz / (AmE), / ˈfʊl saɪz / (BrE)
being as large as the original or a standard version; not reduced in size.
same size as original or standard

Examples

The artist created a full-size replica of the ancient statue for the museum.
We bought a full-size SUV for our road trip across the country.
He set up a full-size chessboard in the backyard for the tournament.

Synset Examples

a life-size sculpture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life-size - of the same size as a real person or thing
