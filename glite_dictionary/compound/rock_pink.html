rock pink

1. NOUN

/ rɑk pɪŋk / (AmE), / rɒk pɪŋk / (BrE)
A pink-flowered perennial plant that grows in rocky regions of the western United States.
pink-flowered plant of western US

Examples

In the canyon, clusters of rock pink added a splash of color to the landscape.
The delicate blooms of the rock pink thrive in the arid, rocky soils of Utah.
Botanists studied the adaptability of rock pink to harsh mountain environments.
