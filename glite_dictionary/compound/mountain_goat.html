mountain goat

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊn.tən ˌɡoʊt / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tən ˌɡəʊt / (BrE)
a large, sure-footed, white mammal with curved horns, native to the mountainous regions of northwestern North America
sure-footed mammal of North America

Examples

The mountain goat climbed easily up the steep rocky slope.
Plenty of mountain goats can be seen grazing in the national park.
Her balance was so steady, she was compared to a mountain goat by her friends.
