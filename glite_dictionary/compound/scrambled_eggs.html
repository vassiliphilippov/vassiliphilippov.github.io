scrambled eggs

1. NOUN

/ ˈskræmbəld ɛɡz / (AmE), / ˈskræmbld ɛɡz / (BrE)
Eggs beaten and cooked while stirring, resulting in a soft, fluffy consistency.
eggs cooked while stirring

Examples

She ordered scrambled eggs and toast for breakfast.
The chef taught us how to make creamy scrambled eggs.
I like to add cheese and herbs to my scrambled eggs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: omelet - eggs beaten and cooked without stirring, often with fillings
