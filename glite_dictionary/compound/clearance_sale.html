clearance sale

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɪrəns seɪl / (AmE), / ˈklɪə.rəns seɪl / (BrE)
an event at which goods are sold at reduced prices to clear out stock, typically at the end of a season or before closing a business
sale to clear out stock

Examples

The store held a massive clearance sale to make room for new products.
During the clearance sale, many items were available at half price or less.
After the clearance sale, most of the shelves were nearly empty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sale - an event at which goods are sold at reduced prices
