moment of silence

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊmənt əv ˈsaɪləns / (AmE), / ˈməʊmənt əv ˈsaɪləns / (BrE)
a short period when people remain quiet, especially to honor or remember someone or something solemnly
brief period of silence for respect or remembrance

Examples

Before the ceremony began, the crowd observed a moment of silence for those who had lost their lives.
The principal announced a moment of silence over the intercom to remember the victims.
We stood together in a moment of silence as a sign of respect.

Synset Examples

We observed a moment of silence with flags at half-mast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silence - absence of sound or speech
