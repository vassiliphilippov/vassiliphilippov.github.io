gift wrap

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪft ræp / (AmE), / ɡɪft ræp / (BrE)
attractive paper used for wrapping gifts, usually decorated for special occasions
decorative paper for wrapping presents

Examples

She picked out the most colorful gift wrap for her friend's birthday present.
The store offers free gift wrap for any purchase during the holidays.
After carefully choosing the gift wrap, he made sure the ribbon matched the paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrapping paper - paper used for wrapping gifts
