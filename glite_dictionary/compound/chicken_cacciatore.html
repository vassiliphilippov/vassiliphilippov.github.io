chicken cacciatore

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkən kɑˌtʃɑːtəˈɔːri / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn kaˌtʃætɪˈɔːri / (BrE)
an Italian-style chicken casserole cooked with tomatoes, mushrooms, and herbs
Italian-style chicken casserole with tomatoes and herbs

Examples

I decided to make chicken cacciatore for our Italian-themed dinner tonight.
The restaurant serves a delicious chicken cacciatore with fresh basil and mushrooms.
Have you ever tried cooking chicken cacciatore from scratch?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hunter's chicken - a baked chicken dish with similar ingredients, often used in British English

Etymology

From Italian 'cacciatore' meaning 'hunter'; the dish's name translates as 'hunter's chicken'.
