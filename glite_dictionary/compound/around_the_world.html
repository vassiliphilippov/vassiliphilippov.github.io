around the world

1. ADV

/ əˈraʊnd ðə wɝːld / (AmE), / əˈraʊnd ðə wɜːld / (BrE)
in many different countries; across the globe
in many countries; globally

Examples

The charity's work extends around the world, helping people in dozens of countries.
Her music is known around the world and has won several international awards.
Many products made in this factory are shipped around the world.

Synset Examples

His stamp collection includes rare pieces from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worldwide - extending or happening throughout the world
  • Antonym: locally - in or relating to a particular area, city, or town; not worldwide
