German goiter

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɜrmən ˈɡɔɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmən ˈɡɔɪtə / (BrE)
a type of thyroid enlargement, often associated with iodine deficiency or endemic goiter
type of thyroid enlargement
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with German goiter after presenting symptoms of neck swelling and discomfort.
Some regions historically had higher rates of German goiter due to a lack of dietary iodine.
Physicians recommend iodine supplements to prevent German goiter in susceptible populations.

Synset Examples

Treatment for German goiter requires specialized care.

Related Terms
