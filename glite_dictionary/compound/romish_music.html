Romish music

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊ.mɪʃ ˈmju.zɪk / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.mɪʃ ˈmjuː.zɪk / (BrE)
Music that is related to or characteristic of Romani culture.
music of Romani culture

Examples

The lively rhythms of Romish music echoed through the square during the celebration.
Her violin performance was heavily inspired by traditional Romish music.
Many fusion bands incorporate elements of Romish music into their repertoire.

Synset Examples

The festival featured Romish music and dance performances.

Related Terms
