conversation piece

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːn.vɚˈseɪ.ʃən pis / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.vəˈseɪ.ʃən piːs / (BrE)
an object or work of art that attracts attention and serves as a topic of discussion
object that sparks conversation

Examples

The unusual sculpture in the hallway was a real conversation piece.
Her antique clock always serves as a conversation piece when guests visit.
He bought a vintage jukebox as a conversation piece for the living room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talking point - a subject that encourages discussion
