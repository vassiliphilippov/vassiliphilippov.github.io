quarter tone

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɔrtər toʊn / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə təʊn / (BrE)
A musical interval that is half the size of a semitone, used especially in some modern and non-Western music.
musical interval half a semitone

Examples

In some contemporary music, composers experiment with intervals smaller than a semitone, such as the quarter tone.
The violinist skillfully played a phrase using quarter tones to create an exotic sound.
Middle Eastern music often includes the quarter tone as part of its unique scale system.
