genus Psenes

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˈsiːniːz / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈsiːniːz / (BrE)
A genus of fish in the family Stromateidae, commonly known as driftfishes.
a genus of driftfishes

Examples

The genus Psenes includes several species of driftfishes found in tropical oceans.
Researchers observed members of the genus Psenes along the Atlantic coast.
Fish from the genus Psenes are known for drifting with currents during their early life stages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: driftfish - a general name for fish in the family Stromateidae

Etymology

From New Latin 'Psenes', the scientific name assigned to this fish genus.
