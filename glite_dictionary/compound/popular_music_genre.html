popular music genre

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːpjələr ˈmjuːzɪk ˈʒɑːnrə / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊlə ˈmjuːzɪk ˈʒɒnrə / (BrE)
a style or category of music that enjoys widespread appeal, often for a limited period of time
widely popular type of music

Examples

Hip hop quickly became a popular music genre in the 1980s.
Many teenagers gravitate toward whatever popular music genre is trending each year.
Disco was a popular music genre that dominated dance clubs in the 1970s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pop music - modern music with wide appeal
  • Degree Higher: music genre - category or style of music
