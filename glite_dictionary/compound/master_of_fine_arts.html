Master of Fine Arts

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæs.tɚ əv faɪn ˈɑrts / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.stər əv faɪn ˈɑːts / (BrE)
a postgraduate degree awarded for completing a program of study in the fine arts, such as visual arts, creative writing, or performing arts
postgraduate degree in fine arts

Examples

She earned her Master of Fine Arts in creative writing from a prestigious university.
After completing his Master of Fine Arts, he began exhibiting his sculptures internationally.
The Master of Fine Arts program requires each student to present a final thesis exhibition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: MFA - abbreviation for Master of Fine Arts
