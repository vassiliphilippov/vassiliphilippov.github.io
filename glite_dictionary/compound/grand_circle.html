grand circle

1. NOUN

/ ɡrænd ˈsɜːrkəl / (AmE), / ɡrɑːnd ˈsɜːkəl / (BrE)
a gymnastic exercise performed on the horizontal bar, involving swinging completely around it with the body fully extended
horizontal bar gymnastic move

Examples

The gymnast executed a flawless grand circle on the horizontal bar during the competition.
Learning the grand circle requires strength, flexibility, and precise timing.
He injured his wrist while practicing grand circles in the gym.
