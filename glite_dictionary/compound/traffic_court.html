traffic court

1. NOUN

/ ˈtræfɪk kɔrt / (AmE), / ˈtræfɪk kɔːt / (BrE)
a court of law that deals specifically with cases involving traffic violations and offenses
court handling traffic offenses

Examples

The police officer told him he would have to appear in traffic court next week.
She contested her speeding ticket in traffic court and was found not guilty.
Most cases handled by traffic court involve minor infractions like parking violations or speeding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: court - a tribunal established to administer justice
