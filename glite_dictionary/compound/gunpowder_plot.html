Gunpowder Plot

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡʌnˌpaʊdər plɑt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌpaʊdə plɒt / (BrE)
a failed conspiracy in 1605 in England to assassinate King James I and blow up the Houses of Parliament, led by Guy Fawkes and other Catholics in protest against Catholic persecution
a 1605 Catholic plot to blow up Parliament

Examples

Each year, Britons commemorate the Gunpowder Plot with fireworks and bonfires on November 5th.
The Gunpowder Plot was discovered before the conspirators could carry out their plan to destroy Parliament.
Guy Fawkes became the most famous figure associated with the Gunpowder Plot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Guy Fawkes - the most famous member of the Gunpowder Plot
