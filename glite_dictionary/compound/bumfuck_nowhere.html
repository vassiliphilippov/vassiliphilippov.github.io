bumfuck nowhere

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌmfʌk ˌnoʊwɛr / (AmE), / ˈbʌmfʌk ˌnəʊweə / (BrE)
(vulgar slang) A very remote, desolate, or insignificant place, often with nothing of interest and far from civilization.
extremely remote and insignificant place (vulgar slang)
slang vulgar

Examples

After hours of driving, they realized they were lost in bumfuck nowhere.
The town was so small it felt like bumfuck nowhere to anyone who visited.
Nobody visits during winter because it's basically bumfuck nowhere out here.

Synset Examples

They ended up in bumfuck nowhere during their road trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the middle of nowhere - a place that is remote and isolated
  • Synonym: boondocks - rough or remote area; backwoods
  • Synonym: backwater - a place or situation in which nothing exciting happens

Etymology

Obscene formation; from 'bumfuck' (obscure rural area; originally obscene insult) + 'nowhere.'
