order Arales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔr.dər əˈreɪliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔː.də əˈreɪliːz / (BrE)
A former order of monocotyledonous flowering plants, including the families Araceae and Lemnaceae.
plant order (Araceae, Lemnaceae families)

Examples

The order Arales includes many species often found in tropical wetlands.
Changes in plant taxonomy have led to the reclassification of the order Arales.
Members of the order Arales are known for their unique inflorescence structures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Araceae - a family of flowering plants in the order Arales
  • Synonym: Lemnaceae - a family of aquatic plants in the order Arales

Etymology

From New Latin 'Arales', based on the family name 'Araceae'.
