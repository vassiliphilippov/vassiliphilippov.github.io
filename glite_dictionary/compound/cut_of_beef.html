cut of beef

1. NOUN

/ kʌt əv bif / (AmE), / kʌt əv biːf / (BrE)
A piece or portion of meat obtained from a specific part of a cow, used as food.
portion of meat from a cow

Examples

The restaurant offers an impressive selection of premium cuts of beef on its menu.
Choosing the right cut of beef can make a big difference in the flavor and tenderness of your meal.
Each cut of beef is best suited to particular cooking methods, such as grilling, roasting, or stewing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cut of pork - portion of meat obtained from a pig
