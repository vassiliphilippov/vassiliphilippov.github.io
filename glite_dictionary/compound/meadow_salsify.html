meadow salsify

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdoʊ ˈsælsɪfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəʊ ˈsælsɪfaɪ / (BrE)
a weedy European annual plant with yellow flowers, naturalized in the United States
European plant with yellow flowers

Examples

Botanists have observed the spread of meadow salsify along many North American roadsides.
The yellow flowers of meadow salsify are easily mistaken for those of dandelions.
Gardeners sometimes struggle to remove meadow salsify from their lawns due to its persistent roots.
