square mile

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr maɪl / (AmE), / skwɛː maɪl / (BrE)
A unit for measuring area equal to the area of a square with sides of one mile, or 640 acres.
unit of area measurement (640 acres)

Examples

The city covers nearly fifty square miles of land.
The national park expands over five hundred square miles of wilderness.
Their farm occupies just one square mile in the valley.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: acre - A unit of area measurement equal to 4,840 square yards or 4,047 square meters
  • Degree Higher: square kilometer - A metric unit of area equal to a square with sides of one kilometer
