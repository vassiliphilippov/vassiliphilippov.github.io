abdominal pain

1. NOUN

/ æbˈdɑm.ɪ.nəl peɪn / (AmE), / æbˈdɒm.ɪ.nəl peɪn / (BrE)
pain occurring in the area between the chest and pelvis, often referred to as the stomach region
pain in the stomach or belly area
medical

Examples

The doctor asked if she was experiencing any abdominal pain during her illness.
Sudden abdominal pain can sometimes signal a serious medical condition.
He went to the emergency room because his abdominal pain was getting worse.

Synset Examples

A stomach ulcer can cause severe abdominal pain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stomachache - pain in the stomach area

Etymology

From Latin 'abdomen' (belly) + English 'pain'.
