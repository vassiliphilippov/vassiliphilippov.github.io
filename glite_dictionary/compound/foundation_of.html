foundation of

1. NOUN

/ faʊnˈdeɪʃən ʌv / (AmE), / faʊnˈdeɪʃən ɒv / (BrE)
the underlying basis or principle on which something is built, developed, or established
basis or groundwork for something

Examples

Honesty is the foundation of a healthy workplace culture.
The scientific method is the foundation of modern research.
Respect is the foundation of any lasting friendship.

Synset Examples

Trust is the foundation of any strong relationship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basis of - the underlying support or foundation for an idea or process
