artificial flower

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəl ˈflaʊər / (AmE), / ˌɑːtɪˈfɪʃl ˈflaʊə / (BrE)
A flower made from materials such as plastic, silk, or paper, designed to imitate a real blossom.
imitation flower made from non-natural materials

Examples

She decorated the table with beautiful artificial flowers that looked almost real.
Unlike fresh bouquets, artificial flowers can last for years without wilting.
Many brides choose artificial flowers for their wedding arrangements to keep as mementos.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: real flower - a natural flower that grows from a plant
