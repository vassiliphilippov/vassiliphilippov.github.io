vegetable matter

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛdʒ.tə.bəl ˈmæt.ər / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒ.tɪ.bəl ˈmæt.ə / (BrE)
Material that is produced by plants or consists of plant substances.
matter originating from plants

Examples

Compost piles are often rich in vegetable matter that breaks down into fertile soil.
Scientists analyzed the vegetable matter found in the ancient burial site.
After the meal, only scraps of vegetable matter remained on the cutting board.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organic matter - matter derived from living organisms
