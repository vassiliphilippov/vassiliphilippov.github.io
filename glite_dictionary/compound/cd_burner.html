CD burner

1. NOUN

/ siː diː ˈbɝːnər / (AmE), / siː diː ˈbɜːnə / (BrE)
A device used for recording data onto compact discs (CDs).
device for recording onto CDs

Examples

I bought a new CD burner to back up my music collection.
The computer's CD burner allows you to create your own audio discs.
Many laptops no longer come with a built-in CD burner these days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: CD writer - device for recording data onto CDs
  • Antonym: CD player - device for playing audio CDs
