protofascist sentiment

1. NOUN

/ ˌproʊtoʊˈfæʃɪst ˈsɛn.tə.mənt / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəʊˈfæʃɪst ˈsɛn.tɪ.mənt / (BrE)
An early indication or manifestation of fascist ideology or beliefs.
early sign of fascist beliefs

Examples

The leader's speech was criticized for containing protofascist sentiment that alarmed many observers.
Historians study periods where protofascist sentiment paved the way for more extreme movements.
There is concern that protofascist sentiment can flourish in times of economic hardship.

Synset Examples

The group was accused of harboring protofascist sentiments.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: fascist sentiment - support or approval of fascist ideology

Etymology

From proto- (Greek prōtos, meaning 'first') + fascist (from Italian 'fascista'), referring to early forms resembling fascism.
