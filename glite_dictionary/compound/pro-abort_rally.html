pro-abort rally

1. NOUN

/ proʊ əˈbɔrt ˈræli / (AmE), / prəʊ əˈbɔːt ˈræli / (BrE)
A public demonstration in support of abortion rights.
demonstration for abortion rights

Examples

Hundreds of people attended the pro-abort rally downtown last Saturday.
The pro-abort rally drew national attention due to the high-profile speakers present.
Police ensured that the pro-abort rally remained peaceful despite some counter-protesters nearby.

Synset Examples

She joined a pro-abort rally to support women's rights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pro-choice rally - a demonstration supporting the legal right to abortion
