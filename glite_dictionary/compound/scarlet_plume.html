scarlet plume

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑr.lət pluːm / (AmE), / ˈskɑː.lɪt pluːm / (BrE)
A Mexican shrub (Euphorbia fulgens) often cultivated for its bright scarlet-bracted flowers.
Mexican shrub with scarlet-bracted flowers

Examples

The scarlet plume adds a vibrant splash of color to gardens during the winter months.
Gardeners often choose the scarlet plume for its striking flowers and easy maintenance.
During the botanical exhibition, the scarlet plume attracted many visitors with its vivid display.
