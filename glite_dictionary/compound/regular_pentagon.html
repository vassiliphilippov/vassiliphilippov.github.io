regular pentagon

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛɡjələr ˈpɛntəɡɑn / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊlə ˈpɛntəɡən / (BrE)
A pentagon in which all angles are equal and all sides are of equal length.
a pentagon with equal sides and angles

Examples

To construct a regular pentagon, each side must be of equal length and each interior angle 108 degrees.
The soccer ball's pattern includes several black regular pentagons among its shapes.
A regular pentagon can be inscribed perfectly in a circle.

Synset Examples

A regular pentagon has five lines of reflectional symmetry, and rotational symmetry of order 5 (through 72°, 144°, 216° and 288°).

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pentagon - a polygon with five sides and five angles

Etymology

From Latin 'regula' (rule) + Greek 'pentagōnos' (five-angled).
