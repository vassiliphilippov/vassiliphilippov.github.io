purple loco

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜr.pəl ˈloʊ.koʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.pəl ˈləʊ.kəʊ / (BrE)
A tufted locoweed native to the southwestern United States, which produces purple, pink, or white flowers.
a plant; tufted locoweed with purple or white flowers

Examples

We spotted a cluster of purple loco blooming along the desert trail.
Ranchers are concerned because livestock sometimes graze on purple loco, which can be toxic.
The wildflowers in this region include the colorful purple loco and many other native species.
