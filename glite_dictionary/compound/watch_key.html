watch key

1. NOUN

/ wɑtʃ kiː / (AmE), / wɒtʃ kiː / (BrE)
a small key with a square hole, used for winding some mechanical watches
key used to wind old watches

Examples

The antique pocket watch came with its original watch key for winding.
He carefully turned the watch key to wind the delicate mechanism inside.
Because the watch key was lost, the old watch could no longer be wound.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: winder - a device for winding something, especially a watch or clock
