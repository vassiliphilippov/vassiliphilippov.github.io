Taurus the Bull

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɔrəs ðə bʊl / (AmE), / ˈtɔːrəs ðə bʊl / (BrE)
the second sign of the zodiac, symbolized by a bull, with the sun in this sign from about April 20 to May 20
second sign of the zodiac

Examples

Many people born under Taurus the Bull are said to be practical and reliable.
The sun enters Taurus the Bull around April 20 each year.
In astrology, Taurus the Bull is associated with patience and determination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Taurus - the second sign of the zodiac; astrological sign represented by a bull
  • Degree Lower: Aries - the first sign of the zodiac
  • Degree Higher: Gemini - the third sign of the zodiac

Etymology

From Latin 'Taurus' meaning 'bull', used as the astrological sign and constellation.
