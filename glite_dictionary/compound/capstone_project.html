capstone project

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæpˌstoʊn ˈprɑːˌdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˈkæp.stəʊn ˈprɒdʒ.ekt / (BrE)
a final project that demonstrates a student's knowledge and skills, typically marking the completion of a course or academic program
final academic project

Examples

Completing her capstone project helped her apply everything she learned in her engineering degree.
All seniors must present their capstone project before graduating from the program.
His capstone project focused on designing a sustainable energy system.

Synset Examples

The capstone project was a highlight of their final year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thesis - long essay or dissertation for a degree
  • Synonym: final project - project completed at the end of a course or program

Etymology

From 'capstone' (the top stone of a structure, signifying completion) and 'project'.
