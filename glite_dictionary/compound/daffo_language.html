Daffo language

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæfoʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdafəʊ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in northern Nigeria, especially in Plateau State and surrounding areas
Chadic language of northern Nigeria

Examples

The Daffo language is an important part of the cultural identity in Bokkos.
Linguists have studied the grammar of the Daffo language to better understand Chadic languages.
Many people in Plateau State still speak the Daffo language at home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chadic language - branch of the Afro-Asiatic language family
