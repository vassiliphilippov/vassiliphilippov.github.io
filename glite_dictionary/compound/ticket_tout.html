ticket tout

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪkɪt taʊt / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt taʊt / (BrE)
a person who buys tickets for an event and resells them at a higher price
person who resells event tickets for profit
british

Examples

The ticket tout was selling concert passes for three times their face value outside the stadium.
Police arrested a ticket tout for reselling football tickets illegally near the entrance.
It's often risky to buy tickets from a ticket tout rather than an official vendor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scalper - someone who buys and resells tickets at a higher price (mainly US term)

Etymology

From British English 'tout' (to solicit importunately or sell aggressively), possibly from Middle English 'tuten' (to look out).
