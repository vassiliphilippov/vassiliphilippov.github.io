St. Christopher-Nevis

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈkrɪstəfər ˈniːvɪs / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈkrɪstəfə ˈniːvɪs / (BrE)
A country in the Caribbean, consisting of the islands of Saint Christopher (St. Kitts) and Nevis; achieved independence from the United Kingdom in 1983.
Caribbean country; also known as Saint Kitts and Nevis

Examples

I traveled to St. Christopher-Nevis last summer and visited both islands.
The official language of St. Christopher-Nevis is English.
Before 1983, St. Christopher-Nevis was a British colony.

Etymology

Named after Saint Christopher (St. Kitts) and the island Nevis; 'Christopher' from Late Latin 'Christophorus', 'Nevis' possibly from Spanish 'Nuestra Señora de las Nieves' (Our Lady of the Snows).
