refuse collector

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛfjuːs kəˈlɛktər / (AmE), / ˈrɛfjuːs kəˈlɛktə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to collect and dispose of household or commercial waste
person collecting waste or garbage

Examples

The refuse collector emptied the bins early in the morning.
She thanked the refuse collector for always keeping the street clean.
A team of refuse collectors work together to cover the entire neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garbage collector - person who collects garbage or refuse
  • Synonym: dustman - British term for a person who collects refuse
