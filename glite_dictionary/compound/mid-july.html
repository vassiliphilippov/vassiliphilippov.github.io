mid-July

1. NOUN

/ mɪd dʒəˈlaɪ / (AmE), / mɪd dʒʊˈlaɪ / (BrE)
The middle part of the month of July.
the middle part of July

Examples

The roses in our garden usually bloom in mid-July each year.
We plan to take our summer vacation in mid-July when the weather is warmest.
Tickets for the concert will go on sale in mid-July, so be sure to check the website then.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: early July - the first part of July
  • Degree Higher: late July - the last part of July
