city financier

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪti fɪˌnæn.si.ər / (AmE), / ˈsɪti fɪˈnæn.si.ə / (BrE)
a financier who works in one of the banks in the City of London
banker in London's financial district
british

Examples

Many aspiring investment bankers dream of becoming a city financier after graduating from top universities.
The economic downturn led to the redundancy of several prominent city financiers.
As a successful city financier, she manages complex mergers and acquisitions for multinational corporations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: financier - a person skilled in large-scale financial operations

Etymology

From 'City' meaning the City of London, historically London's financial district, and 'financier' from French 'financier' (financier, financer).
