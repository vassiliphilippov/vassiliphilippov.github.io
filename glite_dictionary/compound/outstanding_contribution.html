outstanding contribution

1. NOUN

/ aʊtˈstændɪŋ kənˌtrɪbjuˈʃən / (AmE), / aʊtˈstændɪŋ kənˌtrɪbjuːˈʃən / (BrE)
a notable or exceptional work or achievement, especially one that is recognized or honored
notable or exceptional work or achievement

Examples

She received an award for her outstanding contribution to environmental research.
His outstanding contribution to the project was praised by the entire team.
The committee recognized her outstanding contribution to the arts community.

Synset Examples

He earned a medal for his outstanding contributions to the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: achievement - a thing done successfully, typically by effort, courage, or skill
  • Synonym: contribution - something given or done to help achieve or provide something
