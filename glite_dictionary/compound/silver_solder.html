silver solder

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvər ˈsɑːldər / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˈsɒldə / (BrE)
a type of solder that contains silver, used for joining metal parts, especially in electronics, plumbing, and jewelry-making
a solder alloy containing silver

Examples

The jeweler used silver solder to join the two pieces of the broken bracelet.
When repairing pipes, plumbers often choose silver solder for its strength and durability.
High-quality electronics can benefit from using silver solder because of its excellent conductivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solder - a fusible metal alloy used to join metal parts together
