gym suit

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪm suːt / (AmE), / dʒɪm sjuːt / (BrE)
a set of clothing designed to be worn while participating in gymnastic or physical exercise, typically consisting of a shirt and shorts or similar garments
clothing for gymnastic exercise

Examples

All students are required to bring a gym suit for physical education class.
She forgot her gym suit at home and couldn't participate in the activities.
After class, he changed out of his gym suit and went to lunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tracksuit - a loose, warm suit consisting of a jacket and trousers, worn for sports or exercise
