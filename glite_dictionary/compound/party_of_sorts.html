party of sorts

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrti əv sɔrts / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti əv sɔːts / (BrE)
a somewhat informal gathering that is not a typical or traditional party
an informal or makeshift gathering

Examples

After the ceremony, we had a small party of sorts in the conference room.
It was more of a party of sorts than a real celebration, but everyone enjoyed it nonetheless.
She invited a few friends over for a party of sorts to mark her new job.

Synset Examples

He threw a party of sorts to celebrate his promotion.
