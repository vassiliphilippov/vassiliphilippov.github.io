boxing equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑksɪŋ ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˈbɒksɪŋ ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
The gear and apparatus used for training or participating in the sport of boxing, such as gloves, punching bags, and protective gear.
gear used in the sport of boxing

Examples

He bought all the boxing equipment he needed for his first class.
Proper boxing equipment can help prevent injuries during practice sessions.
Stores specializing in sports often carry a wide range of boxing equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boxing gloves - padded gloves worn in boxing matches and training
