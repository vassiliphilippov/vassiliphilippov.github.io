genus Lonas

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˈloʊnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈləʊnəs / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the daisy family (Asteraceae), containing a single species commonly known as yellow ageratum.
genus of flowering plants (yellow ageratum)

Examples

The genus Lonas is noted for its attractive yellow blooms, which resemble those of the ageratum.
Researchers identified the plant as belonging to the genus Lonas, characterized by its distinct foliage.
Gardeners value species in the genus Lonas both for their ornamental qualities and hardiness.
