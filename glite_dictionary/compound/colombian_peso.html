Colombian peso

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈlʌmbiən ˈpeɪsoʊ / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbiən ˈpeɪsəʊ / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Colombia, equal to 100 centavos.
currency unit of Colombia

Examples

The price of coffee in Bogotá was listed as 5,000 Colombian pesos.
She exchanged her dollars for Colombian pesos at the airport.
A single Colombian peso is subdivided into 100 centavos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peso - basic monetary unit in various Latin American countries
  • Degree Lower: Colombian centavo - a subunit of the Colombian peso, worth one hundredth of a peso

Etymology

From Spanish 'peso', meaning 'weight' or 'coin'; adopted as the currency name in many Latin American countries.
