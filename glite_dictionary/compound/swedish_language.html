Swedish language

1. NOUN

/ ˈswiːdɪʃ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈswiːdɪʃ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the North Germanic language that is the official language of Sweden and one of the two official languages of Finland
Scandinavian language of Sweden and Finland

Examples

She learned to speak Swedish language during her year abroad in Stockholm.
Many novels are originally written in the Swedish language before being translated into English.
The Swedish language shares similarities with Norwegian and Danish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Swedish - the language spoken in Sweden and parts of Finland

Etymology

From Middle English 'Sweedish', from 'Swede' + 'ish', referring to Sweden.
