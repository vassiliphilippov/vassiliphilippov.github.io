ceremonial occasion

1. NOUN

/ sɛr.əˈmoʊ.ni.əl əˈkeɪ.ʒən / (AmE), / sɛ.rɪˈməʊ.ni.əl əˈkeɪ.ʒən / (BrE)
a formal event carried out with established procedures or rituals, often to mark a significant event or celebration
formal event or ceremony

Examples

The ambassador attended the ceremonial occasion marking the opening of the new embassy.
Graduation is a ceremonial occasion that many students look forward to each year.
A national holiday often includes a ceremonial occasion to honor important historical events.

Synset Examples
