brown soft scale

1. NOUN

/ braʊn sɑft skeɪl / (AmE), / braʊn sɒft skeɪl / (BrE)
a species of soft, brown-colored insect pest (Coccus hesperidum) that infests citrus trees and other plants, feeding on sap and causing damage to the host
insect pest of citrus trees

Examples

The brown soft scale can cause extensive damage to citrus orchards by weakening the trees.
Gardeners often treat infested plants to control the spread of brown soft scale.
Infestation by brown soft scale is indicated by the presence of sticky honeydew on leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scale insect - a small insect of the superfamily Coccoidea, some species of which are plant pests
