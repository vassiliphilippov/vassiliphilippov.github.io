Ibizan Podenco

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈbiːzən poʊˈdɛŋkoʊ / (AmE), / ɪˈbiːzən pəˈdɛŋkəʊ / (BrE)
a slender, agile, medium-sized breed of hound originating from the Balearic Islands, known for its keen sense of smell and sight, and its distinctive appearance; also called the Ibizan Hound
breed of hound from the Balearic Islands

Examples

The Ibizan Podenco is known for its strong hunting instincts and elegant appearance.
Many people admire the unique ears of the Ibizan Podenco at dog shows.
She adopted an Ibizan Podenco from an animal rescue organization in Spain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ibizan Hound - alternative name for the Ibizan Podenco
  • Degree Higher: Pharaoh Hound - a similar breed believed to have ancient origins

Etymology

From Spanish 'Podenco Ibicenco', meaning 'Ibizan hound', after Ibiza, one of the Balearic Islands.
