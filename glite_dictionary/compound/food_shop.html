food shop

1. NOUN

/ fuːd ʃɑp / (AmE), / fuːd ʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop where food products, especially ready-to-eat items, are sold.
shop selling food

Examples

She stopped at the food shop on her way home to buy some sandwiches.
There is a new food shop in the town center offering organic meals.
Most students rely on the campus food shop for a quick lunch between classes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grocery store - a shop selling food and other household goods
  • Degree Higher: supermarket - a large self-service store selling food and household items
