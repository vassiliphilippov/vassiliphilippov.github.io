first light

1. NOUN

/ fɝst laɪt / (AmE), / fɜːst laɪt / (BrE)
the time when light first appears in the morning; dawn.
the first light of day; dawn

Examples

We woke up at first light to start our journey while it was still cool.
At first light, the fishermen launched their boats into the calm sea.
He promised to return by first light the next morning.

Synset Examples

we got up before dawn
they talked until morning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dawn - the first appearance of light in the morning
  • Degree Higher: sunrise - the time when the sun first appears above the horizon
  • Degree Lower: twilight - the time just before sunrise or just after sunset
