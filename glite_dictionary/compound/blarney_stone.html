Blarney Stone

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɑːrni stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈblɑːni stəʊn / (BrE)
a stone set in Blarney Castle, Ireland, which legend says bestows the gift of eloquent speech on those who kiss it
stone in Ireland said to give eloquence to those who kiss it

Examples

Tourists from all over the world come to kiss the Blarney Stone for good luck.
Legend has it that kissing the Blarney Stone will give you the gift of eloquence.
During our trip to Ireland, we made sure to visit the famous Blarney Stone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blarney - flattering or persuasive talk, often insincere

Etymology

Named after Blarney Castle near Cork, Ireland.
