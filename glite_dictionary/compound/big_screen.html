big screen

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ skrin / (AmE), / bɪɡ skriːn / (BrE)
the cinema or movies, especially as an industry or medium for watching films
the cinema; movies as a medium

Examples

She hopes her new novel will be adapted for the big screen one day.
Many stars from television eventually make it to the big screen.
Watching action movies on the big screen is a completely different experience from watching at home.

Synset Examples

He dreams of starring in a big screen blockbuster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silver screen - the cinema, especially as a medium or industry
