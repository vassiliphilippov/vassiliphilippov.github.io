to find a solution

1. VERB

/ faɪnd ə səˈluʃən / (AmE), / faɪnd ə səˈluːʃən / (BrE)
to discover or determine an answer or method for resolving a problem or difficulty
discover an answer to a problem

Examples

After hours of discussion, the team finally managed to find a solution to the issue.
If we all work together, I'm sure we can find a solution to this problem.
She was determined to find a solution before the situation got worse.

Synset Examples

We need to find a solution someway before the deadline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to solve - to find an answer to a problem
