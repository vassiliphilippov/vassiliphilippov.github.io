clay sculpture

1. NOUN

/ kleɪ ˈskʌlptʃər / (AmE), / kleɪ ˈskʌlptʃə / (BrE)
a sculpture made by shaping and molding clay
sculpture made of molded clay

Examples

The artist spent weeks creating a clay sculpture of a lion for the exhibit.
We admired the intricate details of her clay sculpture on display at the gallery.
Children enjoyed making a small clay sculpture during their art class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sculpture - a three-dimensional work of art
