good evening

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈivnɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈiːvnɪŋ / (BrE)
Used as a polite greeting or farewell in the evening.
evening greeting or farewell

Examples

She smiled and said, 'good evening,' as she entered the room.
The host welcomed his guests with a cheerful 'good evening.','As he left the office, he nodded and wished his colleagues 'good evening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good morning - polite greeting or farewell in the morning
  • Synonym: good night - farewell used when leaving or going to bed at night
