hop field

1. NOUN

/ hɑp fiːld / (AmE), / hɒp fiːld / (BrE)
a field or garden where hops are cultivated, especially for use in brewing beer
field where hops are grown

Examples

The brewery sources its hops from a local hop field just outside the city.
Every spring, workers prepare the hop field for planting new shoots.
Rows of climbing vines filled the entire hop field by midsummer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hop garden - an enclosed area where hops are grown
