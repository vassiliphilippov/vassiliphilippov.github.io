head for business

1. NOUN

/ hɛd fər ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / hɛd fə ˈbɪznəs / (BrE)
a natural ability or talent for understanding and succeeding in business matters
ability or talent for business

Examples

She has always had a head for business and quickly climbed the corporate ladder.
If you have a head for business, entrepreneurship might be a good path for you.
Despite his lack of formal education, his head for business made him a successful shop owner.

Synset Examples

He has a head for business and is quite successful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head for figures - a natural ability or talent for working with numbers
  • Synonym: head for heights - an ability to be comfortable at great heights
