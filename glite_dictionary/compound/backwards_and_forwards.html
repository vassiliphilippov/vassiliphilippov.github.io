backwards and forwards

1. ADV

/ ˈbækwɚdz ənd ˈfɔrwɚdz / (AmE), / ˈbækwədz ənd ˈfɔːwədz / (BrE)
in both directions; to and fro, often referring to motion, debate, or repeated action
in both directions; to and fro

Examples

They argued backwards and forwards over every detail of the contract.
He walked backwards and forwards outside the office, waiting for news.
