ad agency

1. NOUN

/ æd ˈeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / æd ˈeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
a company that creates advertisements and plans advertising strategies for clients
company creating advertisements for clients

Examples

She works as a copywriter at a leading ad agency in New York.
The company hired an ad agency to launch their new campaign.
After winning several awards, the ad agency gained many new clients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertising agency - a company specializing in creating, planning, and handling advertising

Etymology

Shortened from 'advertising agency', 'ad' from 'advertisement' + 'agency'.
