green lead ore

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin lɛd ɔr / (AmE), / ɡriːn lɛd ɔː / (BrE)
A mineral composed of lead chloride and phosphate, used as a minor source of lead.
a mineral; minor lead ore

Examples

The mine produced small amounts of green lead ore alongside other minerals.
Scientists identified green lead ore as a combination of lead chloride and phosphate.
Collectors value rare specimens of green lead ore for their unique green color.
