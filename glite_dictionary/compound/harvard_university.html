Harvard University

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɑrvərd juˌnɪvərˈsɪti / (AmE), / ˈhɑːvəd juːˌnɪvɜːˈsɪti / (BrE)
a prestigious university located in Cambridge, Massachusetts, known for its academic excellence and history
prestigious university in Massachusetts

Examples

She graduated from Harvard University with a degree in economics.
Many renowned scholars have taught at Harvard University over the years.
Getting accepted into Harvard University is highly competitive.

Etymology

Named after John Harvard, an early benefactor (17th century).
