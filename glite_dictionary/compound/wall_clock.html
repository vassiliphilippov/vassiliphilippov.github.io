wall clock

1. NOUN

/ wɔl klɑk / (AmE), / wɔːl klɒk / (BrE)
A clock that is mounted on a wall, typically used for displaying the time in homes, offices, or public spaces.
clock mounted on a wall

Examples

The antique wall clock in the hallway chimes every hour.
She hung a new wall clock in the kitchen to keep track of time while cooking.
Most classrooms have a large wall clock above the whiteboard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clock - instrument for measuring and displaying time
