hoary pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔri pi / (AmE), / ˈhɔːri piː / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Tephrosia, having pinnate leaves, white or purplish flowers, and flat hairy pods
a plant of the genus Tephrosia

Examples

The fields were dotted with blooming hoary pea, its pale flowers swaying in the breeze.
Botanists studied the hairy pods of the hoary pea during their field research.
Gardeners sometimes cultivate hoary pea for its attractive foliage and flowers.
