welfare rat stereotype

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛlˌfɛɹ ˈɹæt ˈstɛɹiəˌtaɪp / (AmE), / ˈwɛl.feə ˈræt ˈstɛɹ.i.ə.taɪp / (BrE)
An offensive stereotype depicting welfare recipients as lazy or deceitful, typically used to criticize social welfare programs or those who rely on government assistance.
offensive stereotype of welfare recipients
offensive

Examples

The media often perpetuates the welfare rat stereotype, leading to increased stigma for those on assistance.
That film challenged the welfare rat stereotype by showing the real struggles of low-income families.
Many advocacy groups work to counter the harmful welfare rat stereotype in public discussions.

Synset Examples

The welfare rat stereotype harms those who genuinely need help.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welfare queen - derogatory stereotype of women exploiting welfare
