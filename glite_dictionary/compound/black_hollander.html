black Hollander

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈhɑːlændər / (AmE), / blæk ˈhɒləndə / (BrE)
an Old World willow (Salix viminalis) with light green leaves, cultivated especially for basketry
a willow cultivated for basket making

Examples

The baskets were expertly woven from the supple branches of the black Hollander.
Gardeners often cultivate the black Hollander for its usefulness in traditional crafts.
A row of black Hollanders lined the edge of the field, ready for harvesting.
