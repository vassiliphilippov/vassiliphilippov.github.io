lawn mower

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔn ˌmoʊər / (AmE), / ˈlɔːn ˌməʊə / (BrE)
a machine used for cutting the grass on a lawn
machine for cutting grass on lawns

Examples

She pushed the lawn mower across the yard on Saturday morning.
The lawn mower makes keeping the garden tidy much easier.
After a rainy week, the lawn mower struggled with the tall grass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mower - a machine or person that cuts grass or crops
