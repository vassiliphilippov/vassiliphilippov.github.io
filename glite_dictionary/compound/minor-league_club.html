minor-league club

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪnɚ ˌlig klʌb / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə ˌliːg klʌb / (BrE)
a sports team that competes in a minor league, below the highest or major leagues in skill or status
sports team in a minor league

Examples

He spent two seasons with a minor-league club before joining the majors.
The minor-league club offered young players a chance to develop their skills.
Attendance at the minor-league club's games increased after their winning streak.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: major-league club - sports team in the top league
