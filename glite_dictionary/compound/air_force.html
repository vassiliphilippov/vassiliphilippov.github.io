air force

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌfɔrs / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌfɔːs / (BrE)
the branch of a nation's armed forces that conducts military operations in the air
military branch for aerial warfare

Examples

The air force launched several jets to patrol the region.
She served as a pilot in the air force for over a decade.
Many countries invest heavily in modernizing their air force technology.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: army - the branch of a nation's armed forces that conducts military operations on land
  • Antonym: navy - the branch of a nation's armed forces that conducts military operations at sea
  • Synonym: air corps - an earlier or smaller version of an air force
