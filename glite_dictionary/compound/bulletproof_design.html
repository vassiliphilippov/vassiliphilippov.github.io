bulletproof design

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊl.ɪtˌpruf dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˈbʊl.ɪtˌpruːf dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
a design made to withstand bullets or attacks, providing enhanced protection or security
design resistant to bullets or attacks

Examples

The bank's new vault features a bulletproof design to prevent theft.
Engineers developed a bulletproof design for use in military vehicles.
Thanks to its bulletproof design, the briefcase can withstand hostile environments.

Synset Examples

The new car features a bulletproof design for safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulletproof - made to resist bullets
