chocolate-brown

1. ADJ

/ ˈt͡ʃɑklət ˈbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət ˈbraʊn / (BrE)
having a deep brown color similar to that of chocolate
deep brown color; like chocolate

Examples

She wore a chocolate-brown sweater that brought out the color of her eyes.
They chose chocolate-brown paint for the living room walls to create a warm atmosphere.
The horse's coat was a rich chocolate-brown that gleamed in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: brown - a color produced by mixing red, yellow, and blue, as of wood or earth
