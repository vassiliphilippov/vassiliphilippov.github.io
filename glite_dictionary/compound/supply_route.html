supply route

1. NOUN

/ səˈplaɪ ruːt / (AmE), / səˈplaɪ ruːt / (BrE)
a route used for transporting supplies, especially to support military or logistical operations
route for transporting supplies

Examples

The troops depended on a secure supply route to receive food and ammunition.
After the bridge was destroyed, the army had to establish a new supply route.
Efforts to cut off the enemy's supply route were crucial to the campaign's success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supply line - a line along which supplies are transported
