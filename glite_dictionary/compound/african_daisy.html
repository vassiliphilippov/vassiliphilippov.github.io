African daisy

1. NOUN

/ ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈdeɪ.zi / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈdeɪ.zi / (BrE)
any of several plants of the genus Arctotis, native to southern Africa, with brightly colored, daisylike flowers
plant with daisylike flowers, genus Arctotis

Examples

The vibrant African daisy thrives in hot, sunny gardens and brings color all summer long.
Gardeners often choose African daisy for its drought tolerance and striking appearance.
She planted orange and yellow African daisies along the walkway.
