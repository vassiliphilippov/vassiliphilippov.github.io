white fairy lantern

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈfɛri ˈlæntɚn / (AmE), / waɪt ˈfeəri ˈlæntən / (BrE)
A globe lily found in southern California, having open branched clusters of egg-shaped white flowers.
southern California globe lily with white flowers

Examples

In late spring, the white fairy lantern blooms dot the hillsides of southern California.
Botanists studied the unique reproductive process of the white fairy lantern to better understand its adaptation to the environment.
Gardeners prize the delicate beauty of the white fairy lantern for native plant landscapes.
