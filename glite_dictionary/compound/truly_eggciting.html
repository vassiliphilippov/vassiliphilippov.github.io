truly eggciting

1. ADJ

/ ˈtruːli ɛgˈsaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtruːli ɛgˈsaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely exciting; a playful or punning way of saying 'truly exciting,' often used for humor or in contexts involving eggs.
playful way to say 'truly exciting'
informal

Examples

The announcement of the new brunch menu is truly eggciting for food lovers.
Her truly eggciting costume won first prize at the Easter parade.
This children’s book is truly eggciting from start to finish.

Synset Examples

The news about the festival is truly eggciting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exciting - causing great enthusiasm and eagerness

Etymology

Blend of 'truly exciting' and 'egg,' used as a pun for humorous effect.
