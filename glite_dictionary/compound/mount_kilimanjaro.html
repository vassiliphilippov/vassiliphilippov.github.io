Mount Kilimanjaro

1. PROPN

/ maʊnt ˌkɪlɪmənˈdʒɑːroʊ / (AmE), / maʊnt ˌkɪlɪmənˈdʒɑːrəʊ / (BrE)
the highest mountain in Africa, located in northeastern Tanzania, rising to 19,340 feet (5,895 meters) above sea level
highest mountain in Africa, Tanzania

Examples

Climbers from around the world dream of summiting Mount Kilimanjaro in Tanzania.
The snow-capped peak of Mount Kilimanjaro can be seen from miles away on a clear day.
Scientists study the unique ecosystem found only on Mount Kilimanjaro's slopes.

Etymology

From Swahili Kilimanjaro, possibly from the Chagga language; the origins are uncertain.
