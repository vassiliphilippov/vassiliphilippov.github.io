honey berry

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌni ˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈhʌni ˌbɛri / (BrE)
a tropical American tree that bears a small edible fruit with green leathery skin and sweet, juicy, translucent pulp
a tropical American fruit tree; its sweet edible fruit

Examples

The honey berry thrives in warm, humid climates and produces fruit in late summer.
She made a delicious dessert using fresh honey berries from the market.
Gardeners appreciate the honey berry tree for both its attractive foliage and its edible fruit.
