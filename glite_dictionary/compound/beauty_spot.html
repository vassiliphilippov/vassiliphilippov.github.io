beauty spot

1. NOUN

/ ˈbjuːti spɑt / (AmE), / ˈbjuːti spɒt / (BrE)
A small artificial spot, often made of black material, worn on the face, especially by women, as a fashion accessory or for adornment.
decorative facial spot (fashion)

Examples

In the 18th century, it was fashionable for women to wear a beauty spot on their cheek.
She placed a tiny black beauty spot just above her lip for the party.
The actress wore a beauty spot to replicate the style of historical portraits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mouche - a small artificial mole used as a beauty aid
  • Antonym: birthmark - a natural mark on the skin present from birth
