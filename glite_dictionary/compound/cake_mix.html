cake mix

1. NOUN

/ keɪk mɪks / (AmE), / keɪk mɪks / (BrE)
a packaged mixture of pre-measured ingredients used to quickly make a cake, typically requiring only the addition of liquid and eggs
pre-packaged mixture for baking a cake

Examples

I bought a chocolate cake mix to save time for dessert.
This cake mix only needs eggs and water added before baking.
She prefers to bake from scratch rather than using a cake mix.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baking mix - a packaged mixture used for making baked goods, not just cakes
