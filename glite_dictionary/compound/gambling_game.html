gambling game

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæmblɪŋ ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈɡamblɪŋ ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a game played for money or other stakes, in which participants risk a wager with the chance of winning or losing based on luck, skill, or both
game played for money or stakes

Examples

Poker is one of the most popular gambling games in the world.
He lost a lot of money in an illegal gambling game last weekend.
Many people visit casinos to try their luck at various gambling games.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: casino game - a game of chance played in casinos, often involving gambling
