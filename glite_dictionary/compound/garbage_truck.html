garbage truck

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ ˌtrʌk / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ ˌtrʌk / (BrE)
A large vehicle designed to collect and transport household waste to a disposal site.
vehicle for collecting waste

Examples

The garbage truck comes early every Monday to collect the bins on our street.
A broken garbage truck blocked traffic for over an hour this morning.
Children often wave to the workers on the garbage truck as it passes by.

Synset Examples

in Britain a garbage truck is called a ‘dustcart’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dustcart - British term for a garbage truck
