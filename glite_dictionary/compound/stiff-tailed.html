stiff-tailed

1. ADJ

/ stɪf teɪld / (AmE), / stɪf teɪld / (BrE)
(of an animal or bird) having a tail that is rigid or inflexible.
having a stiff tail

Examples

The stiff-tailed duck is easily recognized by its upright tail feathers.
A stiff-tailed lizard tends to move awkwardly compared to more flexible species.
Several species of stiff-tailed birds inhabit this wetland.
