grape louse

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪp laʊs / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪp laʊs / (BrE)
a small insect pest, especially phylloxera, that infests and damages grape plants
insect pest of grape plants

Examples

The vineyard suffered serious losses when the grape louse infested the roots.
Scientists are researching ways to protect vines from the damaging grape louse.
Farmers inspect their grapes each season to check for signs of grape louse activity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phylloxera - an insect pest that attacks grapevines
