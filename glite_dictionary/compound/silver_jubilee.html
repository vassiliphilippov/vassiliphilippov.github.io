silver jubilee

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪl.vɚ dʒuːˈbɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈsɪl.və dʒuːˈbɪl.i / (BrE)
The celebration of a 25th anniversary, especially of a significant event, such as a reign, marriage, or founding.
25th anniversary celebration

Examples

The college is planning a grand event for its silver jubilee next summer.
Their company celebrated its silver jubilee with a series of community projects.
The silver jubilee of the queen was marked by festivities across the country.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: golden jubilee - celebration of a 50th anniversary
  • Degree Higher: ruby jubilee - celebration of a 40th anniversary
  • Degree Higher: pearl jubilee - celebration of a 30th anniversary

Etymology

From 'silver', traditionally representing 25 years, and 'jubilee', from Latin *jubilaeus* (year of jubilee).
