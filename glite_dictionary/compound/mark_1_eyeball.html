mark 1 eyeball

1. NOUN

/ mɑrk wʌn ˈaɪˌbɔl / (AmE), / mɑːk wʌn ˈaɪbɔːl / (BrE)
(informal) One's own eye or direct sight, especially as a means of observation without technical aids.
direct observation with the naked eye
informal slang

Examples

You need to inspect the wiring with your mark 1 eyeball before relying on the instruments.
We couldn't find the target on radar, so we had to use the mark 1 eyeball.
Sometimes, the mark 1 eyeball catches details that technology misses.

Synset Examples

He stared at the screen with mark 1 eyeball, fully focused.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eyeball - the eye considered as an organ of vision

Etymology

Originally military slang; 'Mark 1' signifies a basic, original model, here humorously applied to the human eye.
