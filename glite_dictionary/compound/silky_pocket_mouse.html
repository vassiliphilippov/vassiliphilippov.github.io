silky pocket mouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlki ˈpɑːkɪt maʊs / (AmE), / ˈsɪlki ˈpɒkɪt maʊs / (BrE)
a small, pale yellowish, soft-furred rodent native to the southwestern United States and Mexico
small soft-furred rodent of Southwest US and Mexico

Examples

The silky pocket mouse is known for its extremely soft fur and nocturnal habits.
Researchers observed a group of silky pocket mice burrowing in the desert sands.
Unlike other rodents, the silky pocket mouse can survive long periods without drinking water.
