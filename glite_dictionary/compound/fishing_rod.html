fishing rod

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃɪŋ rɑd / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪŋ rɒd / (BrE)
a long, thin stick or pole used in fishing to cast and control a fishing line
rod or pole used for fishing

Examples

He carefully assembled his fishing rod before heading out to the lake.
A lightweight fishing rod is ideal for catching small trout in the river.
Children often learn to cast with a simple fishing rod and reel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fishing pole - another term for fishing rod
