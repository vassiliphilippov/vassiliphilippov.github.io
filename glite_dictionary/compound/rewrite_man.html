rewrite man

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːˌraɪt ˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈriːˌraɪt ˌman / (BrE)
a man whose job is to rework and prepare written material into suitable form for publication, especially in newspapers or magazines
male journalist who rewrites articles

Examples

After the reporter called in his notes, the rewrite man crafted the story for the morning edition.
The rewrite man at the paper was known for quickly turning rough drafts into polished articles.
Many classic newsrooms relied on a skilled rewrite man to shape breaking stories under deadline pressure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rewrite woman - a woman who puts text into appropriate form for publication
  • Synonym: editor - a person who prepares written material for publication
