colorful story

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlərfəl ˈstɔri / (AmE), / ˈkʌləfəl ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
a narrative that is vivid, lively, and interesting, often including striking or imaginative details
vivid, lively narrative

Examples

He told a colorful story about his travels in South America.
Her memoir is full of colorful stories from her days as a reporter.
That was certainly a colorful story, but do you think it's entirely true?

Synset Examples

His stories were always colorful, captivating everyone around.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anecdote - a short, interesting or amusing story about a real incident or person
