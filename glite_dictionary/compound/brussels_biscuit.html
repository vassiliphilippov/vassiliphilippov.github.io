Brussels biscuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrʌsəlz ˈbɪskɪt / (AmE), / ˈbrʌsəlz ˈbɪskɪt / (BrE)
A slice of sweet, leavened bread that is baked again until it is brown, hard, and crisp.
twice-baked sweet bread slice

Examples

She served tea with a plate of Brussels biscuits and jam.
The bakery specializes in freshly made Brussels biscuits every morning.
A Brussels biscuit is perfect for dipping into hot chocolate.
