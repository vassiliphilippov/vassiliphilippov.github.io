personnel department

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɝːsəˈnɛl dɪˌpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsəˈnel dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the department in an organization responsible for hiring, training, and managing employees, including setting policies for staff management
department in charge of hiring and staff management

Examples

All job applications must be submitted to the personnel department by Friday.
The personnel department handles employee benefits and payroll.
She started working in the personnel department soon after graduating from university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: human resources department - department responsible for staff management and personnel administration
