shepherd's purse

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɛpərdz ˌpɜrs / (AmE), / ˈʃɛpədz ˌpɜːs / (BrE)
a small annual plant (Capsella bursa-pastoris) with white flowers and heart-shaped seed pods, native to Europe and widely naturalized as a weed
a small white-flowered weed with heart-shaped seed pods

Examples

The shepherd's purse grows quickly in gardens and disturbed soil across many regions of the world.
She recognized the heart-shaped pods of the shepherd's purse along the path.
Farmers often consider shepherd's purse to be a troublesome weed in their fields.

Etymology

Named for the shape of its seed pods, which resemble a shepherd’s small leather bag or purse.
