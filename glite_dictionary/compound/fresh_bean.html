fresh bean

1. NOUN

/ frɛʃ bin / (AmE), / frɛʃ biːn / (BrE)
A bean that is eaten before it is fully ripe and dried, typically tender and moist.
bean eaten before fully ripe or dried

Examples

I prefer using fresh beans in salads rather than the dried variety.
The recipe calls for one pound of fresh beans and some tomatoes.
During summer, local markets offer a wide selection of fresh beans for cooking.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dried bean - bean that is fully matured and dried before use
