bedroom nation

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdˌruːm ˈneɪʃ(ə)n / (AmE), / ˈbɛdruːm ˈneɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
an inviting, intimate, or cozy space that creates a feeling of personal connection or relaxation, often used to describe a home environment enhanced for comfort
cozy, intimate or relaxing space
