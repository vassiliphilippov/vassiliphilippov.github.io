water bottle

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚ ˌbɑt̬əl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌbɒtəl / (BrE)
a container, typically with a narrow neck, used for carrying or storing water for drinking
a bottle for holding drinking water

Examples

She always carries a water bottle to stay hydrated on long walks.
Please refill your water bottle before class begins.
He forgot his water bottle in the gym yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bottle - a container with a narrow neck used for liquids
