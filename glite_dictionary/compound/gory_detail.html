gory detail

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɔri dɪˈteɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːri dɪˈteɪl / (BrE)
A graphic or explicit detail, especially one involving violence, bloodshed, or disturbing events.
explicit detail of violence or horror

Examples

The witness recounted every gory detail of the accident to the police.
He spared us none of the gory details in his horror story.
Please don't go into the gory detail—just tell me if everyone's okay.

Synset Examples

The gorry details of the incident shocked the community.
She described the gorry scene with vivid imagery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graphic detail - explicit or vividly descriptive detail, especially about unpleasant things
  • Antonym: sanitized detail - detail presented in a way that omits disturbing aspects

Etymology

From 'gory' meaning covered in blood, from gore (old English 'gor' for blood, dirt); 'detail' from French 'détail'.
