Mister Right

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪstər ˈraɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɪstə ˈraɪt / (BrE)
A man who is considered the ideal or perfect romantic partner.
ideal male romantic partner

Examples

After several bad relationships, she believes she has finally met her Mister Right.
Some people spend years looking for their Mister Right without realizing he might not exist.
Her friends keep setting her up on dates, hoping one will introduce her to Mister Right.

Synset Examples

She finally found her Mister Right after years of searching.
I thought he was Mister Right, but he proved me wrong.
Don't settle for less; wait for your Mister Right.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Miss Right - The ideal female romantic partner
  • Antonym: Mr. Wrong - A man who is unsuitable as a romantic partner
