goat rue

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊt ruː / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊt ruː / (BrE)
A tall, bushy European perennial plant (Galega officinalis) with pinnate leaves and slender spikes of blue flowers, sometimes used in traditional medicine.
a tall European medicinal plant

Examples

The herbalist recommended using goat rue for its purported medicinal benefits.
Fields of goat rue bloom with blue flowers in the late summer across southern Europe.
Botanists studied goat rue to learn more about its effects on livestock health.
