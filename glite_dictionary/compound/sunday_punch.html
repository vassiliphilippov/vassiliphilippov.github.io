Sunday punch

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌn.deɪ pʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.deɪ pʌntʃ / (BrE)
a powerful punch, especially one delivered with the intention of knocking out an opponent in boxing
powerful knockout punch in boxing

Examples

The boxer's Sunday punch ended the match in just the second round.
He trained for months, hoping to develop a Sunday punch that would make him a contender.
Fans waited for the champion to deliver his famous Sunday punch and claim victory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knockout blow - a blow that renders an opponent unable to continue

Etymology

Alludes to the idea of a punch strong enough to put someone out for a day of rest, like Sunday.
