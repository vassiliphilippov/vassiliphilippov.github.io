he-man

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈhiːˌmæn / (BrE)
A man who is notably strong, virile, or displays overtly masculine traits.
a strongly masculine man

Examples

Many action movies feature a he-man hero who saves the day with his strength.
He likes to project the image of a he-man, but he's actually quite sensitive.
In the gym, he has the look of a classic he-man with his muscular build.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: macho - A man exhibiting aggressively masculine traits
  • Antonym: wimp - A man lacking strength or masculinity

Etymology

Formed from 'he-', denoting maleness, and 'man', in early 20th century American usage.
