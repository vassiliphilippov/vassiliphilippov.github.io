group meeting

1. NOUN

/ ɡruːp ˈmiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡruːp ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a meeting that is formally arranged for a number of people to come together and discuss something
a formally arranged gathering of people

Examples

The monthly group meeting is an opportunity for everyone to share updates and ideas.
We scheduled a group meeting to discuss the progress of the project.
Attendance at the group meeting is mandatory for all team members.

Synset Examples

next year the meeting will be in Chicago
the meeting elected a chairperson

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meeting - a gathering of people, especially for a formal purpose
