spray extraction machine

1. NOUN

/ spreɪ ɪkˈstrækʃən məˈʃin / (AmE), / spreɪ ɪkˈstrækʃən məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A machine used to clean textile surfaces such as carpets, upholstery, and mattresses by spraying a cleaning solution and then extracting it along with dirt.
machine for cleaning textile surfaces

Examples

The spray extraction machine made it easy to deep clean the carpets in the hotel lobby.
After renting a spray extraction machine, she noticed a significant improvement in her sofa's appearance.
Technicians recommend using a spray extraction machine for removing stubborn stains from fabric upholstery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpet cleaner - a machine used to clean carpets
