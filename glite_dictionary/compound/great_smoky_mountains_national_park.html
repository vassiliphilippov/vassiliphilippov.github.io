Great Smoky Mountains National Park

1. PROPN

/ ɡreɪt ˈsmoʊki ˈmaʊntənz ˈnæʃənl pɑrk / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈsməʊki ˈmaʊntənz ˈnæʃnəl pɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in the southeastern United States, spanning Tennessee and North Carolina, known for its scenic beauty, diverse wildlife, and the highest mountain in the eastern U.S.
national park in Tennessee and North Carolina

Examples

We spent our summer vacation hiking and camping in the Great Smoky Mountains National Park.
Bear sightings are common in the Great Smoky Mountains National Park, especially along popular trails.
The Great Smoky Mountains National Park attracts millions of visitors every year due to its remarkable biodiversity.
