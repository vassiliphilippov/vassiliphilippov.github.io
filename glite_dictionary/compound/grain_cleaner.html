grain cleaner

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪn ˈkliːnər / (AmE), / ɡreɪn ˈkliːnə / (BrE)
a machine used to remove impurities and improve the quality of grain before storage or sale
machine for cleaning grain

Examples

The farmer purchased a new grain cleaner to prepare his wheat for storage.
After harvest, the grain cleaner efficiently removed dust and debris from the corn.
Modern grain cleaners have improved the quality of cereals sold to the market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thresher - a machine for separating grain from plants
  • Synonym: combine harvester - a machine that harvests and cleans grain crops
