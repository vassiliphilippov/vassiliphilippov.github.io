quick-fire response

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɪkˌfaɪɚ rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / ˈkwɪkˌfaɪə rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
a reply or answer given very quickly and without hesitation, often in a spontaneous or rapid sequence
rapid and spontaneous reply

Examples

The interview impressed the panel with her quick-fire responses to challenging questions.
During the quiz, his quick-fire response gave his team a valuable lead.
Her quick-fire responses kept the audience entertained throughout the event.

Synset Examples

His quick-fire responses made the debate lively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immediate response - an answer given straight away, without delay
