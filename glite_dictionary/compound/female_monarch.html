female monarch

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːmeɪl ˈmɑːnɑːrk / (AmE), / ˈfiːmeɪl ˈmɒnək / (BrE)
A woman who reigns as the sovereign ruler of a state or country.
woman sovereign ruler

Examples

Queen Elizabeth II was the longest-reigning female monarch in British history.
Many countries have been ruled by a female monarch at some point in their history.
The succession law was changed to allow a female monarch to ascend the throne.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: queen - a female ruler of a country, especially one who inherits the position by right of birth
  • Antonym: king - a male sovereign ruler of a state or country
