garnet-coloured

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɑrnət ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːnɪt ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
Having the deep red color of the mineral garnet.
deep red color like garnet

Examples

The garnet-coloured gown shimmered under the lights at the gala.
He poured the garnet-coloured wine into a crystal glass.
Autumn leaves turned a brilliant garnet-coloured as the weather grew colder.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: red - color at the end of the visible spectrum, resembling blood
  • Degree Higher: crimson - a rich deep red color inclining to purple
