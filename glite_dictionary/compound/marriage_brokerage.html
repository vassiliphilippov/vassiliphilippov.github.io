marriage brokerage

1. NOUN

/ ˈmærɪdʒ ˈbroʊkərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmærɪdʒ ˈbrəʊkərɪdʒ / (BrE)
a business or service that arranges marriage contracts or matches between prospective spouses, typically for a fee
business arranging marriage contracts

Examples

The marriage brokerage guaranteed satisfaction or your money back.
In some cultures, a marriage brokerage is a common way to find a spouse.
She decided to contact a marriage brokerage after several unsuccessful relationships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: matchmaking - the activity of arranging or facilitating marriages or romantic partnerships
