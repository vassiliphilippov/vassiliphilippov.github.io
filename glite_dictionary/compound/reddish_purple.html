reddish purple

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈpɝpəl / (AmE), / ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈpɜːpəl / (BrE)
a shade of purple that is tinged with red; purplish-red in color
a purple shade tinged with red

Examples

The sky took on a reddish purple hue at sunset, stunning everyone on the beach.
She chose a reddish purple dress for the party, which complimented her complexion beautifully.
Artists often mix red and blue to create various reddish purple tones for their paintings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magenta - a purplish-red color
  • Degree Lower: purple - a color between red and blue
