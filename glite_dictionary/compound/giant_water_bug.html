giant water bug

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt ˈwɔt̬ɚ ˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈwɔːtə ˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
a large predatory aquatic insect with piercing and sucking mouthparts, known for feeding on small fish and invertebrates
large predatory aquatic insect

Examples

A giant water bug surfaced quietly, sending ripples across the pond.
Many young fish fall prey to the powerful bite of the giant water bug.
The children discovered a giant water bug clinging to the side of a rock in the stream.
