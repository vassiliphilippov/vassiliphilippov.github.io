spring cress

1. NOUN

/ sprɪŋ krɛs / (AmE), / sprɪŋ krɛs / (BrE)
a small white-flowered plant of the cress family, commonly found in wet places in eastern North America
a small white-flowered wetland plant

Examples

We found patches of spring cress growing near the edge of the stream.
The wet meadow was dotted with delicate, white-flowered spring cress.
Botanists are studying how spring cress thrives in marshy habitats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cress - a type of small plant with edible leaves, often used in salads
