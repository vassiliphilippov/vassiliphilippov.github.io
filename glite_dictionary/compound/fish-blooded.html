fish-blooded

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪʃ ˌblʌdɪd / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃ ˌblʌdɪd / (BrE)
Having a strong affinity for the ocean or aquatic life.
strong affinity for ocean or aquatic life

Examples

Despite growing up inland, he has always felt fish-blooded and dreams of life by the sea.
Her fish-blooded enthusiasm leads her to volunteer at the local aquarium every weekend.
They called him fish-blooded because he never seemed happier than when he was diving in the ocean.

Synset Examples

Being fish-blooded, she spends every summer by the ocean.
His fish-blooded nature drives him to explore underwater caves.
