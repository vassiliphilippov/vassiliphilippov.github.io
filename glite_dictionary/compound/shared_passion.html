shared passion

1. NOUN

/ ʃɛrd ˈpæʃən / (AmE), / ʃeəd ˈpæʃən / (BrE)
an interest or enthusiasm that is held in common by two or more people
an interest held by more than one person

Examples

Their shared passion for jazz music brought them together as close friends.
A shared passion for environmental conservation united the group.
During the interview, she spoke about her shared passion with her colleagues for innovation.

Synset Examples

He found a community in nerdo that shared his passions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: common interest - an interest shared by two or more people
