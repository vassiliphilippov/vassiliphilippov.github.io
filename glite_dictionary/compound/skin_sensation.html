skin sensation

1. NOUN

/ skɪn sɛnˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / skɪn sɛnˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
a physical feeling or perception experienced at the surface of the skin
perception or feeling at the skin's surface

Examples

He described an unusual skin sensation after touching the plant.
Some medical conditions can cause loss of skin sensations in certain areas of the body.
A tingling skin sensation spread across her hands as she entered the cold room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tactile sensation - a sensation related to the sense of touch
