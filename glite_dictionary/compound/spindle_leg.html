spindle leg

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪndl lɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈspɪndl lɛɡ / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) a thin and long leg, resembling the shape of a spindle.
a thin, long leg

Examples

The model walked down the runway, her spindle legs accentuated by the dress.
The colt had awkward spindle legs that seemed too long for its body.
With her spindle legs and delicate frame, people often mistook her for a ballet dancer.

Synset Examples

She admired the dancer's spindlelegs as they moved elegantly.

Etymology

From 'spindle', a rod used for spinning, noted for its long and slender shape, and 'leg'.
