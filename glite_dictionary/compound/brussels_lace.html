Brussels lace

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrʌsəlz leɪs / (AmE), / ˈbrʌsəlz leɪs / (BrE)
a fine handmade lace from Brussels, often featuring a raised or appliqué design
fine handmade lace from Brussels

Examples

She wore an antique veil made of Brussels lace on her wedding day.
Collectors often admire the intricate patterns found in Brussels lace.
The delicate Brussels lace trim enhanced the elegance of the gown.

Etymology

Named after Brussels, the capital of Belgium, where the lace originated.
