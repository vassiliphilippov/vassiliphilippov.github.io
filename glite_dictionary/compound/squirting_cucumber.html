squirting cucumber

1. NOUN

/ ˈskwɝːtɪŋ kjuːˈkʌmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈskwɜːtɪŋ kjuːˈkʌmbə / (BrE)
a Mediterranean vine (Ecballium elaterium) with oblong fruit that ejects its seeds and juice forcefully when ripe and touched
Mediterranean vine with explosive fruit

Examples

The squirting cucumber amazed the children when its fruit exploded at their touch.
Botanists studied the unique seed dispersal mechanism of the squirting cucumber.
In Mediterranean gardens, the squirting cucumber is sometimes grown for its unusual fruit.
