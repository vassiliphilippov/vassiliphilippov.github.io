horror show

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrɚ ʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhɒrə ʃəʊ / (BrE)
An event or situation characterized by chaos, disaster, or extreme unpleasantness.
disastrous or chaotic situation
informal

Examples

After the new software update, my laptop turned into a real horror show.
The team’s performance last night was nothing short of a horror show.
Her attempt at cooking Thanksgiving dinner ended in a horror show.

Synset Examples

His presentation was a complete horror show.
The traffic jam was a real horror show this morning.
The meeting turned into a horror show after the argument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disaster - a sudden event causing great damage or suffering
  • Synonym: mess - a situation that is confused and full of problems
