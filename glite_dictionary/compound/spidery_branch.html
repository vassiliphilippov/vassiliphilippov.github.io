spidery branch

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪdəri bræntʃ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdəri brɑːntʃ / (BrE)
A thin and delicate branch that resembles the legs of a spider.
thin, delicate branch like a spider's leg

Examples

The sunlight filtered gently through the spidery branches overhead.
Crows perched on the spidery branch of a dead tree near the creek.
A single cobweb clung to a spidery branch swaying in the wind.

Synset Examples

He admired the spidery branches of the tree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twig - a small, thin branch of a tree or bush
