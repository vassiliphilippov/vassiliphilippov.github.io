hidden spring pocket

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪdən sprɪŋ ˈpɑkɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɪdən sprɪŋ ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
a concealed pocket fitted with a spring mechanism, often designed for secure and discreet storage in clothing or accessories
concealed pocket with a spring mechanism

Examples

The designer added a hidden spring pocket to the jacket for easy access without sacrificing style.
