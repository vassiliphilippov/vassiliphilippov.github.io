shitcom

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtkɒm / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtkɒm / (BrE)
A sitcom that is very poor in quality or unfunny, usually used disparagingly.
a very bad or unfunny sitcom
slang vulgar

Examples

That new series turned out to be a shitcom with terrible jokes and flat characters.
People online keep calling the show a shitcom because of its weak writing.
After watching one episode, I realized it was just another generic shitcom.

Synset Examples

That sitcom was a real shitcom, not funny at all.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sitcom - a situation comedy; a comedy television series

Etymology

Blend of 'shit' (slang for something of poor quality) and 'sitcom' (situation comedy).
