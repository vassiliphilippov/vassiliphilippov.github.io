magic asterisk

1. NOUN

/ ˈmædʒɪk ˈæstərɪsk / (AmE), / ˈmadʒɪk ˈastərɪsk / (BrE)
an asterisk (*) used as a wildcard symbol in coding or computer operations to represent multiple possible values or entries
wildcard symbol in coding

Examples

In our search function, the magic asterisk lets users find all files with any name.
The programmer used a magic asterisk to match every record in the database.
Applying the magic asterisk in the command filtered the results efficiently.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asterisk - the symbol *
