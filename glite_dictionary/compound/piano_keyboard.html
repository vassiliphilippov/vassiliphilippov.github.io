piano keyboard

1. NOUN

/ piˈænoʊ ˈkiːbɔrd / (AmE), / piˈænəʊ ˈkiːbɔːd / (BrE)
the set of black and white keys on a piano that are pressed to produce musical notes
set of keys on a piano

Examples

She placed her hands gently on the piano keyboard and began to play a soft melody.
The piano keyboard needs cleaning after last night's performance.
Children often find the layout of a piano keyboard intriguing when they first start learning music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keyboard - set of keys on a musical instrument or computer
