Brown Swiss

1. NOUN

/ braʊn swɪs / (AmE), / braʊn swɪs / (BrE)
a large, hardy breed of brown dairy cattle originally from Switzerland, known for high milk production and adaptability
breed of Swiss dairy cattle

Examples

The Brown Swiss is valued for both its milk and its gentle temperament.
Many dairy farmers choose Brown Swiss for their resilience in different climates.
At the fair, the prize-winning cow was a purebred Brown Swiss.
