sports arena

1. NOUN

/ spɔrts əˈrinə / (AmE), / spɔːts əˈriːnə / (BrE)
a large indoor building designed for sporting events, often with seating for spectators
indoor building for sporting events

Examples

The city built a new sports arena to host basketball and hockey games.
Fans filled the sports arena to watch the championship match.
He works as an event coordinator at a popular sports arena downtown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stadium - a large structure for outdoor sports events
