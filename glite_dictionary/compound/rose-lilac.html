rose-lilac

1. ADJ

/ roʊz ˈlaɪlæk / (AmE), / rəʊz ˈlaɪlək / (BrE)
Of a pale purple (lilac) color tinged with a hint of rose or pink.
pale purple with a pinkish tinge

Examples

She chose a rose-lilac dress for the spring wedding.
The flowers were a delicate rose-lilac shade, blending perfectly with the decor.
A rose-lilac glow spread across the sky at sunset.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: lilac - pale purple color
  • Degree Higher: mauve - pale purple color with a bluish or pinkish tinge
