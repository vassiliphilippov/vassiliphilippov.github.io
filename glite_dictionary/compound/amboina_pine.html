Amboina pine

1. NOUN

/ æmˈboʊnə paɪn / (AmE), / æmˈbəʊnə paɪn / (BrE)
a tropical tree native to the Moluccas and Philippines, known as a source of dammar resin
tropical tree in the Philippines/Moluccas; dammar resin source

Examples

The Amboina pine is often tapped for its valuable dammar resin.
Local craftsmen use timber from the Amboina pine for making furniture.
The forest reserve is home to a number of Amboina pine trees.

Etymology

Amboina (now Ambon), an island in Indonesia, combined with 'pine' for its resin resemblance.
