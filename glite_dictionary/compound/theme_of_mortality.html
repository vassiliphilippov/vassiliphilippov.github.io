theme of mortality

1. NOUN

/ θim əv mɔrˈtæləti / (AmE), / θiːm əv mɔːˈtælɪti / (BrE)
the subject or idea of death and human existence, especially as explored in literature, art, or philosophy
subject of death and human existence

Examples

The novel grapples with the theme of mortality through its portrayal of aging and loss.
Many artists have addressed the theme of mortality in their most famous works.
In philosophy classes, students often debate the theme of mortality and its meaning for human life.

Synset Examples

The documentary explored the theme of mortality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mortality - the state of being subject to death
