evergreen plant

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛvərˌɡrin ˈplænt / (AmE), / ˌɛvəˌɡriːn ˈplɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant whose leaves remain green and persist throughout the year, rather than falling off seasonally.
plant with foliage year-round

Examples

The evergreen plant in the garden provided color even during the winter months.
She prefers to decorate with evergreen plants because they look vibrant all year round.
Many evergreen plants are valued for landscaping due to their persistent foliage.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: deciduous plant - a plant that sheds its leaves annually

Etymology

From 'evergreen', meaning always green, and 'plant'; 'evergreen' from Old English 'æfre' (ever) + 'grene' (green).
