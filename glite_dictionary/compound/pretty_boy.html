pretty boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪti bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈprɪti bɔɪ / (BrE)
an attractive young man, often with delicate or youthful features
attractive young man

Examples

He was always considered the pretty boy in high school, drawing attention wherever he went.
The new actor is more than just a pretty boy; he’s talented too.
Some people criticize him for being a pretty boy, but he works hard on his craft.

Synset Examples

He’s always been the pretty boy of the group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartthrob - a man who is very attractive and popular with women
  • Antonym: hunk - a sexually attractive man with a well-built physique
