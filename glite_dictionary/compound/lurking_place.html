lurking place

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɝːkɪŋ pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈlɜːkɪŋ pleɪs / (BrE)
A place that is suitable for lying in wait or hiding, especially for a secret or sinister purpose.
place used for hiding or waiting covertly

Examples

The suspect chose an abandoned warehouse as his lurking place before committing the robbery.
Dense bushes near the park became a favorite lurking place for stray animals.
Detectives examined every possible lurking place around the crime scene for clues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hideout - a place used as a hiding place, especially by criminals
  • Degree Lower: refuge - a place providing shelter or safety
