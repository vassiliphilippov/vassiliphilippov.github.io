dining room suite

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪnɪŋ ruːm swiːt / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnɪŋ ruːm swiːt / (BrE)
a matching set of furniture designed for use in a dining room, typically including a table and chairs, and sometimes other pieces like a sideboard or cabinet
set of dining room furniture

Examples

We decided to replace our old chairs with a new dining room suite for the holidays.
The antique dining room suite included a mahogany table and six matching chairs.
Her parents gave her a beautiful dining room suite as a wedding gift.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suite - a set of matching furniture
