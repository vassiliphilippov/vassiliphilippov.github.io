European country

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən ˈkʌntri / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈkʌntri / (BrE)
a country located within the continent of Europe
country in Europe

Examples

France is a well-known European country famous for its culture and cuisine.
Tourists often visit at least one European country during their vacation.
Each European country has its own unique traditions and history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: European nation - another term for a country in Europe
