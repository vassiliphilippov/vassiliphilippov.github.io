leading man

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːdɪŋ mæn / (AmE), / ˈliːdɪŋ mæn / (BrE)
an actor who plays the principal male role in a play, film, or television show
principal male actor in a production

Examples

He was cast as the leading man in the new romantic comedy.
For years, the actor was best known as a Broadway leading man.
The director was searching for a charismatic leading man for his latest movie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leading lady - an actress who plays the principal female role
