whaling gun

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪlɪŋ ɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈweɪlɪŋ ɡʌn / (BrE)
a gun or device designed to fire a harpoon or projectile at a whale, typically used in whaling
gun for shooting harpoons at whales

Examples

The crew loaded the whaling gun as they prepared to approach the giant whale.
Early whalers relied on the whaling gun to make their catches more efficient and less dangerous.
After years of disuse, the rusty whaling gun was displayed in the maritime museum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harpoon gun - a gun used for shooting harpoons, especially at whales or large sea creatures
