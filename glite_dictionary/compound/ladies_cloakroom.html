ladies' cloakroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪdiz ˈkloʊkˌrum / (AmE), / ˈleɪdiz ˈkləʊkruːm / (BrE)
a room in a public place where women can change clothes, freshen up, or leave their coats and bags
women's changing or coat room

Examples

She asked for directions to the ladies' cloakroom at the theater.
Please collect your belongings from the ladies' cloakroom before you leave.
The ladies' cloakroom was newly renovated and much more spacious.

Synset Examples

She left her coat in the ladies' cloakroom.
I waited in the ladies' cloakroom for my friend.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: men's cloakroom - a room for men to change clothes or store coats in a public place
