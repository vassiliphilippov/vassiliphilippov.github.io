motor-assisted

1. ADJ

/ ˈmoʊtər əˌsɪstəd / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə əˌsɪstɪd / (BrE)
(of a vehicle, device, etc.) propelled by both an engine and human muscle power.
powered by engine and muscles

Examples

The new motor-assisted bicycle allows riders to climb steep hills more easily.
Many wheelchairs are now motor-assisted to help users conserve energy over long distances.
He bought a motor-assisted scooter for his daily commute to work.

Synset Examples

a motor-assisted bicycle
