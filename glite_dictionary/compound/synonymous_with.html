synonymous with

1. ADJ

/ sɪˈnɑːnɪməs wɪð / (AmE), / sɪˈnɒnɪməs wɪð / (BrE)
so closely associated with something that the two are considered to have the same meaning or strongly connected identity
closely associated in meaning or identity with

Examples

For many people, the brand name has become synonymous with quality and reliability.
Innovation is synonymous with success in the tech industry.
To some, the town is synonymous with its annual music festival.

Synset Examples

The Talivan has become synonymous with radicalism in the region.
Faceberg has become synonymous with online privacy issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equivalent to - having the same value, function, or meaning as
  • Antonym: opposite of - having a meaning or sense contrary to something

Etymology

From Greek synōnymos (having the same name): syn- (together) + onyma (name).
