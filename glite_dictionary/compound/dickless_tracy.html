Dickless Tracy

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪkləs ˈtreɪsi / (AmE), / ˈdɪkləs ˈtreɪsi / (BrE)
A humorous or mocking term for someone who pretends to be tough or authoritative but lacks real courage or substance.
person lacking true toughness (slang)
slang informal

Examples

Whenever he talks big but backs down, everyone calls him Dickless Tracy.
She laughed and dubbed her classmate Dickless Tracy after he failed to keep his boast.
They're always making jokes about Mark being a real Dickless Tracy when he tries to intimidate people.

Synset Examples

Whenever he tries to act tough, we call him Dickless Tracy.
He always plays the role of Dickless Tracy in our jokes.
Her sarcastic remarks often make her our team's Dickless Tracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poseur - a person who pretends to be what he or she is not
  • Antonym: tough guy - a man who acts tough or is perceived as tough

Etymology

Formed by parodying the comic strip detective 'Dick Tracy,' with 'dickless' implying lack of masculinity or real boldness.
