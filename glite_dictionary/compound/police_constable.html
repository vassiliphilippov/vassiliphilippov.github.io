police constable

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis ˈkɑn.stə.bəl / (AmE), / pəˈliːs ˈkɒn.stə.bəl / (BrE)
a police officer of the lowest rank in the police force, especially in the United Kingdom and Commonwealth countries
lowest rank police officer
british

Examples

The police constable directed traffic at the busy intersection after the accident.
After joining the force, she started her career as a police constable in London.
A police constable must be honest and brave to serve the community effectively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: constable - an officer of the peace, usually the lowest rank in the police
