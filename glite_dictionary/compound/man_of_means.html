man of means

1. NOUN

/ mæn əv minz / (AmE), / mæn əv miːnz / (BrE)
a man who is wealthy; a man who has a lot of money, resources, or property
wealthy man

Examples

After his business took off, he became a man of means and lived a life of luxury.
She married a man of means and moved into a large estate in the countryside.
The museum relies on the generosity of any man of means who wishes to support the arts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millionaire - a person whose assets are worth at least a million units of currency
  • Antonym: pauper - a very poor person
