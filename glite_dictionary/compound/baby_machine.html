baby machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪbi məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbi məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a person, usually a woman, who has many children in a short period, often referred to humorously or informally
humorous term for prolific parent
informal

Examples

After her sixth child, her friends started calling her a baby machine as a joke.
Tabloids labeled the celebrity couple as baby machines after their fourth child in five years.
She laughed off being called a baby machine by her colleagues at work.

Synset Examples

The media dubbed her a baby machine for her rapid succession of births.
