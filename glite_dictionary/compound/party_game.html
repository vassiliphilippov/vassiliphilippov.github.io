party game

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrti ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a game played to entertain guests at a social gathering
game to amuse party guests

Examples

Charades is a classic party game that gets everyone laughing.
At her birthday, they played several party games to break the ice.
The host introduced a new party game that everyone enjoyed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parlor game - game played indoors for amusement
