crack kills

1. NOUN

/ kræk kɪlz / (AmE), / kræk kɪlz / (BrE)
a slogan or warning used to highlight the dangerous and potentially fatal effects of crack cocaine
warning slogan about crack cocaine

Examples

The school displayed posters saying 'crack kills' in the main hallway to warn students about drug use.
She wore a T-shirt with 'crack kills' printed on it as part of an anti-drug campaign.
Public service announcements repeating 'crack kills' appeared frequently on television in the 1990s.

Synset Examples

The documentary emphasizes that crack kills lives.
The campaign’s slogan is 'crack kills' to raise awareness.
Remember, crack kills; stay away from drugs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: just say no - anti-drug slogan used in campaigns against drug use
