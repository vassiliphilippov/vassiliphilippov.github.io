Austral Islands

1. PROPN

/ ˈɔstrəl ˈaɪ.ləndz / (AmE), / ˈɒstrəl ˈaɪ.ləndz / (BrE)
a group of small islands in the southern part of French Polynesia, in the South Pacific Ocean
group of islands in French Polynesia

Examples

The Austral Islands are known for their beautiful coral reefs and traditional culture.
Tourists rarely visit the Austral Islands compared to other French Polynesian destinations.
Many unique bird species can be found on the Austral Islands.

Etymology

From Latin 'australis' meaning 'southern', referring to their location; 'islands' from Old English 'īegland'.
