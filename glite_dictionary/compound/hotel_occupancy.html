hotel occupancy

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈtɛl ɑːˌkjuːpənsi / (AmE), / həʊˈtɛl ˈɒkjʊpənsi / (BrE)
the proportion of rooms occupied at a hotel during a given period, usually expressed as a percentage
percentage of occupied hotel rooms

Examples

The city's hotel occupancy reached 95% during the festival weekend.
Low hotel occupancy in winter months often leads to discounted rates.
The manager tracks hotel occupancy daily to adjust staffing levels accordingly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occupancy rate - the proportion of occupied units to the total units available
  • Antonym: hotel vacancy - the availability of unoccupied rooms in a hotel
