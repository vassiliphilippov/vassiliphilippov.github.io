Belgian beef stew

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛl.dʒən bif stu / (AmE), / ˈbɛl.dʒən biːf stjuː / (BrE)
A traditional Belgian dish of beef stewed in beer, typically seasoned with garlic and served with boiled potatoes.
beef stew cooked in Belgian style with beer

Examples

My grandmother made her famous Belgian beef stew every winter, filling the house with the aroma of beer and garlic.
We ordered Belgian beef stew at the local bistro, and it was served with delicious boiled potatoes.
The rich flavors of Belgian beef stew come from slow-cooking the meat in dark beer.
