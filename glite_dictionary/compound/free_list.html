free list

1. NOUN

/ fri lɪst / (AmE), / friː lɪst / (BrE)
a list of goods or commodities that are exempt from import tariffs
list of tariff-exempt goods

Examples

Under the new trade agreement, several products have been added to the free list.
Rice and wheat are on the free list, making them cheaper for consumers.
The government reviews the free list annually to adjust for changing economic needs.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tariff - a tax or duty to be paid on a particular class of imports or exports
