mustache cup

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌs.tæʃ ˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.tɑːʃ ˌkʌp / (BrE)
a drinking cup with a bar or guard inside the rim designed to keep a man's mustache out of the drink
cup with guard for mustache

Examples

He proudly displayed his antique mustache cup on the living room shelf.
The mustache cup was popular among gentlemen in Victorian England.
I bought a porcelain mustache cup at the flea market for my collection.

Etymology

19th-century invention reflecting the fashion of men wearing mustaches; from 'mustache' and 'cup'.
