golden-eyed fly

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən aɪd flaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən aɪd flaɪ / (BrE)
A common name for a green lacewing, an insect known for its delicate wings and distinctive golden eyes.
a green lacewing insect

Examples

The golden-eyed fly is often used in gardens as a natural pest control agent.
A golden-eyed fly hovered over the rose petals, searching for aphids.
Many gardeners attract golden-eyed flies to help reduce harmful insect populations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: green lacewing - insect known for its green body and lace-like wings
