head of hair

1. NOUN

/ hɛd əv hɛr / (AmE), / hɛd əv heə / (BrE)
a person's entire growth of hair covering the scalp
hair covering the scalp

Examples

She has a beautiful head of hair that everyone admires.
After chemotherapy, he was relieved to see his head of hair growing back.
The model's thick head of hair caught the attention of the photographers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair - threadlike strands growing from the skin, especially on the head
