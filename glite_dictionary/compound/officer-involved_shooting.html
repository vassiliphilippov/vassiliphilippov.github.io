officer-involved shooting

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑfəsɚ ɪnˌvɑlvd ˈʃutɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɒfɪsə ɪnˌvɒlvd ˈʃuːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a shooting incident in which a police officer is directly involved, typically as the shooter or the target
shooting involving a police officer

Examples

The police department released a statement following yesterday's officer-involved shooting.
Investigators are looking into the circumstances of the officer-involved shooting that occurred in the city center.
There was public outcry after the video of an officer-involved shooting was released.

Synset Examples

Details about the officer-involved shooting are still emerging.
The incident was labeled as an officer-involved shooting.
An officer-involved shooting occurred last night downtown.
The community is shaken by the officer-involved shooting.
