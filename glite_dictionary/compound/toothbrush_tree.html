toothbrush tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːθ.brʌʃ triː / (AmE), / ˈtuːθ.brʌʃ triː / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub or small tree of the genus Salvadora, whose fibrous twigs are used in some regions as a natural toothbrush
tree whose twigs are used as toothbrushes

Examples

In rural villages, people often use twigs from the toothbrush tree to clean their teeth.
The toothbrush tree is valued for its natural antibacterial properties in many traditional communities.
Camels also feed on the leaves and shoots of the toothbrush tree in arid regions.
