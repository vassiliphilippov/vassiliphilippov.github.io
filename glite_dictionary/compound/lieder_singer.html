lieder singer

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːdər ˌsɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈliːdə ˌsɪŋə / (BrE)
a singer, typically a soloist, who specializes in performing lieder (German art songs).
a singer of German art songs (lieder)

Examples

The acclaimed lieder singer captivated the audience with her expressive performance of Schubert's songs.
As a lieder singer, he is renowned for his sensitivity to both poetry and melody.
Many lieder singers collaborate closely with pianists to bring depth to their recitals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: song - a musical composition intended to be sung

Etymology

From German 'Lied' (song), with 'lieder' as the plural, combined with 'singer'.
