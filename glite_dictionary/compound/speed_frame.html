speed frame

1. NOUN

/ spiːd freɪm / (AmE), / spiːd freɪm / (BrE)
a textile machine that draws out sliver and imparts a slight twist to produce roving, preparing fibers for spinning.
textile machine producing roving

Examples

The textile mill installed a new speed frame to improve the quality of their roving.
Workers carefully monitor the speed frame to ensure consistent fiber thickness.
Modern speed frames are equipped with automation for greater efficiency in yarn production.

Synset Examples

Simplex machines give the attenuated sliver a small amount of twist to ensure the required strength of the roving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simplex machine - a type of speed frame used in spinning preparation
  • Synonym: roving frame - another term for a speed frame in textiles
