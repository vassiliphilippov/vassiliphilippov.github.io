loony left

1. NOUN

/ ˈluni lɛft / (AmE), / ˈluːni lɛft / (BrE)
A derogatory term for individuals or groups who hold extremely left-wing political views, often implying that their positions are irrational or unreasonable.
derogatory term for extreme left-wing politics
offensive

Examples

The new candidate was quickly labeled part of the loony left by her opponents.
Some newspapers blame the loony left for unpopular education policies.
He warned that supporting the loony left could alienate moderate voters.

Synset Examples

The article discussed the influence of the loony left on politics.
People often mock the loony left for their extreme views.
He criticized the loony left for their unrealistic policies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: far left - the extreme left wing of a political group
  • Synonym: radical left - left-wing political movements advocating major change
  • Degree Lower: mainstream left - the more moderate left-wing political stance
