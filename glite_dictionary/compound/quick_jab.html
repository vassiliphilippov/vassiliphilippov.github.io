quick jab

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk dʒæb / (AmE), / kwɪk dʒæb / (BrE)
a fast, sharp punch delivered quickly, especially in boxing
a fast punch in boxing

Examples

The boxer stunned his opponent with a perfectly timed quick jab to the chin.
She practiced her quick jab every morning to improve her speed and accuracy.
A quick jab can disrupt your opponent’s rhythm in a boxing match.

Synset Examples

The pugil was known for his quick jabs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jab - a quick, straight punch in boxing
