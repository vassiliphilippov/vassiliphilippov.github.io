harsh-voiced

1. ADJ

/ hɑrʃ vɔɪst / (AmE), / hɑːʃ vɔɪst / (BrE)
having a voice or sound that is unpleasantly rough, grating, or jarring
having a rough, grating voice or sound

Examples

The singer's harsh-voiced style gave the song a raw intensity that captivated the audience.
A harsh-voiced crow cawed loudly from the branch above.
She was startled by the harsh-voiced command echoing through the hallway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raspy - hoarse or harsh-sounding
  • Synonym: hoarse - sounding rough and harsh, typically as the result of a sore throat or shouting
