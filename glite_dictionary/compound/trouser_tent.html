trouser tent

/ ˈtraʊzɚ tɛnt / (AmE), / ˈtraʊzə tɛnt / (BrE)
slang. A noticeable bulge in the trousers, typically caused by male sexual arousal, or any prominent bulge in the pants.
bulge in trousers (slang, often for arousal)
