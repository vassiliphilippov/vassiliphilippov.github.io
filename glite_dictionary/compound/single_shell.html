single shell

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋɡəl ʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡəl ʃɛl / (BrE)
a narrow rowing boat designed for a single oarsman
a rowing boat for one person

Examples

She trained every morning in her single shell on the river.
The regatta featured a race for men's single shells.
Maintaining balance in a single shell requires considerable skill and core strength.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scull - a type of light, narrow boat used in rowing
