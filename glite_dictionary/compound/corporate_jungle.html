corporate jungle

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔːr.pɚ.rət ˈdʒʌŋ.ɡəl / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.pər.ət ˈdʒʌŋ.ɡəl / (BrE)
A highly competitive and potentially ruthless environment in business or corporate life.
competitive business environment

Examples

Climbing the ladder in the corporate jungle requires both skill and resilience.
She found herself lost in the corporate jungle during her first year at the company.
Surviving the corporate jungle often means dealing with office politics.

Synset Examples

Navigating the corporate jungle can be quite challenging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rat race - a continuous, competitive struggle in modern working life
