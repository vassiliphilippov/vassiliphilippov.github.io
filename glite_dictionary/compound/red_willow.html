red willow

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛd ˈwɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈrɛd ˈwɪləʊ / (BrE)
a type of Eurasian willow with reddish or purple twigs and bark rich in tannin
Eurasian willow with red or purple twigs

Examples

The basket was carefully woven from the flexible branches of red willow.
In early spring, the bright stems of red willow add color to the landscape.
Botanists often study the bark of red willow for its unique tannin content.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: osier - a willow tree with flexible branches used in basketry
