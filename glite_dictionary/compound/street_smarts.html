street smarts

1. NOUN

/ stɹit smɑɹts / (AmE), / stɹiːt smɑːts / (BrE)
the practical knowledge and experience needed to handle difficult or dangerous situations in urban environments
practical skill for surviving in tough urban settings
informal

Examples

You need street smarts to avoid trouble in that neighborhood.
Her street smarts helped her navigate the big city's challenges.
He's not academically gifted but has plenty of street smarts to get by.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: savvy - shrewdness and practical knowledge; the ability to make good judgments
  • Antonym: book smarts - academic knowledge rather than practical experience
