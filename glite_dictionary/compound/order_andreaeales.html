order Andreaeales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər ændriˈiːəˌliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə əndriˈiːəˌliːz / (BrE)
A taxonomic order of mosses consisting of a single genus, Andreaea.
order of mosses (Andreaea)

Examples

The order Andreaeales is notable for its unique adaptation to rocky habitats in the mountains.
Researchers studying moss diversity often reference the order Andreaeales in their classifications.
Unlike many other mosses, members of the order Andreaeales have distinctive dark-colored capsules.

Etymology

From New Latin 'Andreaeales', based on 'Andreaea', the genus name, honoring J.G.R. Andreae.
