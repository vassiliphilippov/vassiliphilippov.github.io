Gulf of Antalya

1. PROPN

/ ɡʌlf əv ænˈtɑːliə / (AmE), / ɡʌlf əv ænˈtælɪə / (BrE)
a gulf of the Mediterranean Sea located in southwestern Turkey, known for its scenic coastline and tourism.
gulf in southwestern Turkey

Examples

The Gulf of Antalya is famous for its beautiful beaches and clear turquoise waters.
Many cruise ships make stops along the Gulf of Antalya during the summer months.
The city of Antalya overlooks the scenic Gulf of Antalya, attracting thousands of tourists each year.
