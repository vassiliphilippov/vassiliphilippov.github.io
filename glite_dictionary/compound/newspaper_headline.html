newspaper headline

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər ˈhɛdlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə ˈhɛdlaɪn / (BrE)
the title or heading of a newspaper article, usually in large print at the top
title of a newspaper article

Examples

The front page newspaper headline screamed about the election results.
She read the shocking newspaper headline while waiting for her coffee.
Editors work hard to craft every newspaper headline to grab readers' attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headline - heading at the top of an article or page
