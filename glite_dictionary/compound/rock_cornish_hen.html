Rock Cornish hen

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈkɔrnɪʃ hɛn / (AmE), / rɒk ˈkɔːnɪʃ hɛn / (BrE)
the flesh of a small, young chicken breed, especially raised for roasting
meat of a small young chicken bred for roasting

Examples

The restaurant's specialty is roasted Rock Cornish hen with wild rice.
She prepared a delicious Rock Cornish hen for the holiday dinner.
At the market, I found frozen Rock Cornish hens perfect for individual servings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cornish hen - a small breed of chicken popular for roasting

Etymology

Named after the crossbreed between the Cornish and White Plymouth Rock chicken breeds, developed in the U.S. in the mid-20th century.
