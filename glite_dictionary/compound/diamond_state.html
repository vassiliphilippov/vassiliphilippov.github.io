Diamond State

1. PROPN

/ ˈdaɪəmənd steɪt / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəmənd steɪt / (BrE)
A nickname for the US state of Delaware, one of the original 13 colonies, with capital Dover.
nickname for the state of Delaware

Examples

Many people visit the Diamond State each year to enjoy its beautiful beaches.
The Diamond State was one of the original thirteen American colonies.
Dover is the capital of the Diamond State.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Delaware - a state in the eastern United States, also known as the Diamond State

Etymology

Nickname referencing Delaware's small size and value, likened to a diamond.
