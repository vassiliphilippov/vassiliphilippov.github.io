suck-ass waste of time

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌkˌæs weɪst əv taɪm / (AmE), / ˈsʌkˌɑːs weɪst əv taɪm / (BrE)
A very vulgar, slang term for something that is completely unworthy of time or attention; extremely pointless or disappointing.
something utterly pointless or worthless (very vulgar, slang)
slang vulgar informal american

Examples

The meeting turned out to be a suck-ass waste of time and accomplished nothing.
Honestly, that game was just a suck-ass waste of time—I regret even trying it.
Don't bother with the new show; it's a complete suck-ass waste of time.

Synset Examples

That movie was a total suck-ass waste of time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waste of time - something not worth spending time on
