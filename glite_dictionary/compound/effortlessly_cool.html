effortlessly cool

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛfərtləsli kuːl / (AmE), / ˈɛfətləsli kuːl / (BrE)
appearing stylish and attractive without seeming to try or make an effort
stylish without any apparent effort
informal

Examples

She walked into the room looking effortlessly cool in jeans and a leather jacket.
His effortlessly cool manner made everyone want to talk to him at the party.
The band's lead singer is famous for being effortlessly cool both on and off stage.

Synset Examples

His style is always cazh and effortlessly cool.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chic - elegantly and stylishly fashionable
  • Synonym: trendy - very fashionable or up to date
