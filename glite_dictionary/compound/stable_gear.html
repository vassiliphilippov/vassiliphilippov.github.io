stable gear

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪbəl ɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈsteɪbəl ɡɪə / (BrE)
the equipment and gear used for horses, especially in a stable, such as saddles, bridles, and harnesses
equipment for horses in a stable

Examples

The groom spent the morning cleaning the stable gear before the horses went out for their ride.
Make sure all the stable gear is in good condition before the show begins.
He invested in new stable gear to ensure the horses were comfortable and safe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tack - equipment used for horses, especially riding and harnessing gear
