vintage feel

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪn.tɪdʒ fil / (AmE), / ˈvɪn.tɪdʒ fiːl / (BrE)
An aesthetic quality that is reminiscent of a past era, often suggesting nostalgia or classic style.
nostalgic or classic aesthetic

Examples

This living room has a vintage feel thanks to its mid-century furniture and muted color palette.
The restaurant's decor gives off a vintage feel that reminds customers of the 1920s.
She chose a wedding dress with a vintage feel, complete with lace and pearl buttons.

Synset Examples

I love the vintage feel of this deckle-edged paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retro - style imitating or evoking a style of a past era
  • Antonym: modern look - contemporary or up-to-date aesthetic
