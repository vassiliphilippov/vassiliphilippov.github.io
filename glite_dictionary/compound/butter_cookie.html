butter cookie

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ɚ ˌkʊki / (AmE), / ˈbʌtə ˌkʊki / (BrE)
A crisp, rich cookie made with a high proportion of butter.
crisp cookie made with butter

Examples

I love having a butter cookie with my tea in the afternoon.
She baked a batch of homemade butter cookies for the holidays.
The bakery's butter cookie recipe has been passed down for generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shortbread - a type of rich, buttery Scottish biscuit
