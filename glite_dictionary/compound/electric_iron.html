electric iron

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈaɪərn / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈaɪən / (BrE)
a device with a flat metal base that is heated by electricity and used to press clothes and remove wrinkles
electrically heated clothes-pressing device

Examples

She bought a new electric iron to replace her old, rusty one.
Be careful not to leave the electric iron plugged in when you're finished.
The electric iron heats up quickly and makes ironing shirts much easier.

Synset Examples

The early electric irons had no easy way to control their temperature, and the first thermostatically controlled electric iron appeared in the 1920s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iron - a device with a flat base heated to press clothes
