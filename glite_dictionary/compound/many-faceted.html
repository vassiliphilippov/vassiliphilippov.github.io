many-faceted

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛni ˈfæsɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɛni ˈfæsɪtɪd / (BrE)
Having many different aspects, sides, or features.
having many aspects or sides

Examples

The debate on climate change is a many-faceted topic involving science, politics, and economics.
She led a many-faceted career, working as both an artist and a scientist.
This many-faceted gemstone sparkled brilliantly under the museum lights.

Synset Examples

The issue is many-faceted and requires careful analysis.
Her personality is many-faceted and intriguing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multifaceted - having many facets or aspects
