engine cooling system

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛn.dʒɪn ˈkuː.lɪŋ ˌsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˈɛn.dʒɪn ˈkuː.lɪŋ ˌsɪs.təm / (BrE)
the system in a motor vehicle that removes excess heat from the engine to prevent overheating
system that cools a vehicle's engine

Examples

A faulty engine cooling system can cause the car to overheat quickly.
He checked the engine cooling system before their long road trip across the desert.
Modern vehicles use advanced technology in their engine cooling system to improve fuel efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiator - main heat exchanger in an engine cooling system
