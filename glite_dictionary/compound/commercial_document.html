commercial document

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɝːʃəl ˈdɑːkjəmənt / (AmE), / kəˈmɜːʃəl ˈdɒkjʊmənt / (BrE)
a document that relates to commercial transactions or business activities, such as invoices, bills of lading, or contracts
document related to commerce or business

Examples

Before shipping the goods, the company prepared all the necessary commercial documents.
The customs officer inspected the commercial documents to verify the contents of the shipment.
Accurate commercial documents are essential for international trade compliance.
