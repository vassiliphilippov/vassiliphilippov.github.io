three-cornered

1. ADJ

/ ˌθriˈkɔrnɚd / (AmE), / ˌθriːˈkɔːnəd / (BrE)
involving three parties, groups, or sides; consisting of or relating to three entities
involving three parties or sides

Examples

The election turned into a three-cornered contest when the independent candidate joined the race.
Negotiations became more complicated after a three-cornered agreement was proposed.
There was a three-cornered debate among the students, each representing a different viewpoint.

Synset Examples
