Beverly Hills

1. PROPN

/ ˌbɛvərli ˈhɪlz / (AmE), / ˌbɛvəli ˈhɪlz / (BrE)
a city in southwestern California, surrounded by Los Angeles, known for its wealthy residents and strong connections to the entertainment industry
city in California, home to many celebrities
american

Examples

Many famous movie stars live in Beverly Hills.
The luxurious shops on Rodeo Drive attract tourists to Beverly Hills every year.
He grew up in Beverly Hills, surrounded by actors and musicians.
