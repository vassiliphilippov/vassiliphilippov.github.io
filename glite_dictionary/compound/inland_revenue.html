Inland Revenue

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪn.lænd ˈrɛv.əˌnu / (AmE), / ˌɪn.lənd ˈrɛv.ən.juː / (BrE)
The former British government department responsible for administering and collecting major direct taxes, including income tax and corporation tax.
former UK tax-collecting agency
british

Examples

The Inland Revenue sent me a letter about my tax return.
She had to contact the Inland Revenue to clarify a discrepancy in her records.
The merger of Inland Revenue and Customs and Excise created a single revenue agency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: HM Revenue and Customs - The British government department that replaced Inland Revenue in 2005, responsible for tax and customs
