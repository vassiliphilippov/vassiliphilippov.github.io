business suit

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs suːt / (AmE), / ˈbɪznəs sjuːt / (BrE)
A suit consisting of a matching jacket and trousers (sometimes with a waistcoat), worn in professional or formal business settings.
formal suit worn in business settings

Examples

He wore a sharp business suit to his important interview.
Most employees are required to dress in a business suit at the office.
Her navy blue business suit gave her a professional appearance on her first day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suit - a set of outer clothes made of the same fabric and designed to be worn together
  • Degree Higher: tuxedo - a formal or semi-formal evening suit
