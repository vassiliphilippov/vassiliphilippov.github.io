service contract

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːvɪs ˌkɑːntrækt / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs ˌkɒntrækt / (BrE)
a formal agreement in which one party agrees to provide maintenance or repair services for another over a specified period.
contract for maintenance services

Examples

We decided to purchase a service contract for our new washing machine to cover any repairs.
The service contract guarantees yearly maintenance visits from certified technicians.
Before signing the service contract, make sure you understand what services are included.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warranty - guarantee provided for repair or replacement of a product
