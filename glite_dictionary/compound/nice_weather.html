nice weather

1. NOUN

/ naɪs ˈwɛðɚ / (AmE), / naɪs ˈwɛðə / (BrE)
weather that is pleasant, especially warm, sunny, and comfortable
pleasant or agreeable weather

Examples

We decided to take a walk because of the nice weather this afternoon.
It's always easier to stay positive when there's nice weather outside.
During the nice weather in spring, the park is full of people.

Synset Examples

Let's eat outside to enjoy the nice weather.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bad weather - unpleasant or severe weather conditions
