chocolate topper

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑklət ˈtɑpər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət ˈtɒpə / (BrE)
a decorative layer or piece of chocolate placed on top of a cake or dessert
decorative chocolate layer on cake

Examples

The baker placed a chocolate topper on the birthday cake to make it extra special.
Each pastry was finished with a delicate chocolate topper shaped like a flower.
You can buy pre-made chocolate toppers to decorate your own desserts at home.

Synset Examples

The cake had a beautiful chocolate topper on it.
