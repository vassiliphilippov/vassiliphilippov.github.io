tree-hugging activist

1. NOUN

/ ˈtri ˌhʌɡɪŋ ˈæk.tɪ.vɪst / (AmE), / ˈtriː ˌhʌɡɪŋ ˈæk.tɪ.vɪst / (BrE)
an environmentalist who is especially passionate about protecting trees, often seen as actively campaigning against deforestation
passionate environmentalist focused on trees

Examples

The tree-hugging activist organized a rally to oppose the cutting down of the old forest.
Some people dismiss her as a tree-hugging activist, but she has made a real difference in environmental policy.
After hearing the plans for a new highway, the tree-hugging activist started a petition to protect the woodland area.

Synset Examples

Tree-hugging activists protested against deforestation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: environmentalist - a person who is concerned with or advocates for the protection of the environment
