religious person

1. NOUN

/ rəˈlɪdʒəs ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who shows strong devotion to a religion or deity
devout follower of a religion

Examples

My grandmother is a deeply religious person who attends church every Sunday.
He became a religious person after finding faith later in life.
The school welcomes students from all backgrounds, whether they are a religious person or not.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: believer - a person who has religious faith
  • Antonym: atheist - a person who does not believe in a deity or religion
