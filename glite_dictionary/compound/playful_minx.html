playful minx

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪfl ˈmɪŋks / (AmE), / ˈpleɪfl ˈmɪŋks / (BrE)
a woman who is flirtatious or mischievous in a lively or teasing way
flirtatious or mischievous woman

Examples

Everyone at the party knew her as a playful minx, always the center of attention.
He couldn't help but smile at the playful minx who kept winking at him from across the room.
With a sly grin, the playful minx slipped the note into his pocket when he wasn't looking.

Synset Examples

She was a playful minx, always teasing her friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coquette - a woman who flirts lightheartedly with men
  • Antonym: prude - a person who is excessively proper or modest

Etymology

From 'minx' (a pert, cunning woman), of uncertain origin; 'playful' adds the sense of lively teasing.
