hydroplane racing

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪdroʊˌpleɪn ˈreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrəʊˌpleɪn ˈreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
A sport in which high-speed motorboats, known as hydroplanes, compete against each other on water.
racing with high-speed motorboats

Examples

Hydroplane racing attracts thousands of spectators to lakes and rivers each summer.
He has competed in national hydroplane racing events since he was a teenager.
The team won the championship after a thrilling season of hydroplane racing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powerboat racing - racing with high-speed powered boats

Etymology

From 'hydroplane', meaning a fast motorboat that skims over the water, and 'racing'.
