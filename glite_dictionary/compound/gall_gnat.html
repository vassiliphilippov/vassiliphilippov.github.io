gall gnat

1. NOUN

/ ɡɔl næt / (AmE), / ɡɔːl nat / (BrE)
A small, delicate, mosquito-like fly that induces the formation of galls on plants.
mosquito-like fly causing plant galls

Examples

The leaves were covered with strange bumps caused by the gall gnat.
Gardeners often seek ways to protect their roses from gall gnats.
Careful inspection revealed that a gall gnat infestation was harming the plant.
