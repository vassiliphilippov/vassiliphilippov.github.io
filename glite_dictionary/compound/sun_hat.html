sun hat

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌn ˌhæt / (AmE), / ˈsʌn ˌhæt / (BrE)
A hat with a wide brim designed to shield the face from direct sunlight.
wide-brimmed hat for sun protection

Examples

She wore a bright sun hat to keep her face cool at the beach.
Children should always wear a sun hat when playing outside in the summer.
His wide-brimmed sun hat protected him while he worked in the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: straw hat - hat made of straw often used for sun protection
  • Degree Lower: baseball cap - casual cap with a peak worn for sun shading
