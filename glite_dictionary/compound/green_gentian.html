green gentian

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin ˈdʒɛnʃən / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈdʒɛnʃən / (BrE)
a tall herbaceous plant of the northwestern United States with clusters of white flowers tinged with green, sometimes placed in the genus Swertia
tall western US herb with greenish white flowers

Examples

We spotted a rare green gentian blooming along the mountain trail.
The botanist collected samples of green gentian for her research.
In summer, the meadows are dotted with green gentian and wildflowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mountain gentian - another common name for species in the same plant group
