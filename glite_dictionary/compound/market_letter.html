market letter

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrkət ˌlɛtər / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪt ˌlɛtə / (BrE)
a newsletter providing analysis and recommendations on the stock market, typically written by a financial analyst and sold to subscribers
stock market analyst's newsletter

Examples

Many investors rely on a market letter to keep informed about the latest stock trends and recommendations.
She subscribed to a reputable market letter that offers detailed analyses of emerging market opportunities.
The analyst's market letter predicted major changes in the technology sector this quarter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: investment newsletter - a publication providing investment advice
