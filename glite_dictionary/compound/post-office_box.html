Post-Office box

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊst ˌɑːfɪs bɑks / (AmE), / ˈpəʊst ˌɒfɪs bɒks / (BrE)
a numbered compartment in a post office assigned to an individual or organization for receiving mail, to be collected on the premises
numbered mail compartment at a post office

Examples

My company receives all its correspondence through a Post-Office box downtown.
If you want your package sent securely, use a Post-Office box address.
After moving frequently, he found a Post-Office box more reliable than a home mailbox.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: PO box - abbreviated form for 'Post-Office box'
