emperor butterfly

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛmpərɚ ˈbʌtɚˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛmpərə ˈbʌtəflaɪ / (BrE)
a large, brightly colored butterfly, especially one belonging to the family Nymphalidae, found in Africa and parts of Europe and Asia
a large, colorful butterfly species

Examples

The emperor butterfly is known for its vibrant blue and green wings.
We watched an emperor butterfly land gracefully on a garden flower.
Some species of emperor butterfly are considered threatened due to habitat loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butterfly - an insect with large, often brightly colored wings
