black eye

1. NOUN

/ blæk aɪ / (AmE), / blæk aɪ / (BrE)
A dark, swollen bruise around the eye, caused by a blow or injury.
swollen bruise around the eye

Examples

He came to school with a noticeable black eye after the basketball game.
If you don't wear protective gear, you might end up with a black eye.
Her brother accidentally gave her a black eye while they were playing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bruise - an injury causing skin discoloration but not breaking the skin

2. NOUN

/ blæk aɪ / (AmE), / blæk aɪ / (BrE)
an event or circumstance that causes embarrassment, damage to one's reputation, or a setback
embarrassing or damaging setback

Examples

The scandal has given the company a serious black eye in the media.
Losing such an important client was a black eye for the sales team.
The athlete's suspension was a black eye for the entire sport.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: setback - an unanticipated or disappointing event that hinders progress
  • Synonym: blow - a sudden shock or disappointment
  • Antonym: boost - something that improves or uplifts a situation
