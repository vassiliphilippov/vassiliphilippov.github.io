Sango language

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːŋˌɡoʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsaŋɡəʊ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a trade language widely spoken in the Central African Republic and parts of neighboring countries, especially Chad, serving as a lingua franca in the region.
trade language of Central Africa

Examples

The Sango language is used by millions as a common means of communication in the Central African Republic.
He learned the Sango language to work more effectively with local communities in Chad.
As a trade language, Sango language plays a vital role in uniting people of different ethnic groups.

Etymology

From the name Sango, the ethnic group after which the language is named.
