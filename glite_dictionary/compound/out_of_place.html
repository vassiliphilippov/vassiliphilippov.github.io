out of place

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv pleɪs / (AmE), / aʊt əv pleɪs / (BrE)
not in the correct or appropriate position or context; inappropriate or unsuitable for the situation
inappropriate or not fitting in a situation

Examples

Her casual clothes seemed out of place at the formal dinner.
He felt out of place among the group of experts.
The modern painting looked out of place in the antique gallery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inappropriate - not suitable or proper in the circumstances
  • Synonym: out of order - not in the correct sequence or arrangement; not working properly
  • Antonym: appropriate - suitable or proper in the circumstances
