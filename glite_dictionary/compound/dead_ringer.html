dead ringer

1. NOUN

/ dɛd ˈrɪŋər / (AmE), / dɛd ˈrɪŋə / (BrE)
a person or thing that looks exactly like someone or something else; an exact likeness or double
exact likeness or lookalike

Examples

She’s a dead ringer for her favorite actress, and people often confuse them.
That statue is a dead ringer for the one in the museum downtown.
The man who walked into the shop was a dead ringer for her late husband.

Synset Examples

She is a dead ringer for her mother in her youth.
The new product is a dead ringer for the old model.
That actor is a dead ringer for the original star.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double - a person who looks exactly like another; lookalike
  • Synonym: lookalike - a person or thing that closely resembles another

Etymology

19th-century American English; possibly from horse racing slang for a substituted, identical-looking horse (ringer) with 'dead' meaning 'exact.'
