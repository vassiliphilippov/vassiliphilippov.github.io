to bolster someone's reputation

1. VERB

/ ˈboʊlstɚ sʌmˌwʌn(z) ˌrɛpjəˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlstə ˈsʌmwʌnz ˌrɛpjʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
to improve or enhance the opinion that others have of someone
improve someone's standing in others' eyes

Examples

The CEO hoped the new partnership would bolster her reputation among investors.
He tried to bolster his reputation by donating to several local charities.
Winning the award significantly bolstered the scientist's reputation in the academic community.

Synset Examples

They sought to bolster their reputation through community service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enhance someone's reputation - to make someone's reputation better
