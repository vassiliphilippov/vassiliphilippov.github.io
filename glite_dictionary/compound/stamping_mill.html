stamping mill

1. NOUN

/ ˈstæmpɪŋ ˌmɪl / (AmE), / ˈstæmpɪŋ ˌmɪl / (BrE)
a mill where ore is crushed using heavy stamps in order to extract valuable metals.
machine for crushing ore with stamps

Examples

The old gold mine still has a stamping mill on the property for visitors to see.
Technicians operated the stamping mill around the clock to process the newly arrived ore.
Advances in technology have rendered the traditional stamping mill largely obsolete in modern mining.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stamp mill - a machine that crushes ore by pounding with stamps
