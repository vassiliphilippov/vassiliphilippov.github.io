planetary gear

1. NOUN

/ ˈplænəˌtɛri ɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈplænɪtəri ɡɪə / (BrE)
an outer gear that revolves around a central sun gear in an epicyclic gear system
outer gear in an epicyclic gear system

Examples

The planetary gear helps distribute torque evenly in automatic transmissions.
When the planetary gear meshes with the sun gear, it enables smooth rotation and speed variation.
Engineers chose a planetary gear arrangement for its compact size and high efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sun gear - the central gear in an epicyclic gear system

Etymology

From the resemblance of the gear's motion to the orbits of planets around the sun.
