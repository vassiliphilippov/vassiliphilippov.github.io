label of

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪbəl əv / (AmE), / ˈleɪbəl ɒv / (BrE)
a term or category used to classify or describe someone, especially based on their perceived characteristics or affiliations
categorizing term for a person

Examples

She rejected the label of victim and insisted on telling her own story.
The label of genius can create a lot of pressure for young athletes.
He disliked the label of troublemaker that teachers had given him.

Synset Examples

He dismissed the label of commie as a mere political joke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: label - a word or phrase used to describe or identify something or someone
