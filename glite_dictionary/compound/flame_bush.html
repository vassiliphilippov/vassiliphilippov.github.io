flame bush

1. NOUN

/ fleɪm bʊʃ / (AmE), / fleɪm bʊʃ / (BrE)
An Australian shrub with simple obovate leaves and brilliant scarlet flowers.
Australian shrub with scarlet flowers

Examples

The flame bush in their garden attracted many native birds with its vivid red flowers.
Botanists are studying the flame bush for its potential as an ornamental plant.
During spring, the hills were dotted with the bright blossoms of the flame bush.
