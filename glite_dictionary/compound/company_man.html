company man

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌmpəni mæn / (AmE), / ˈkʌmpəni mæn / (BrE)
A male employee whose primary loyalty is to his employer, often at the expense of fellow workers.
loyal male employee (to employer over coworkers)

Examples

As a company man, he rarely supported the union’s proposals.
She accused him of being a company man who never stood up for his colleagues.
The company man received a promotion for putting the firm's interests first.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yes-man - a person who always agrees with their superior
