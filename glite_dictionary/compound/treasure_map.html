treasure map

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɛʒər mæp / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒə mæp / (BrE)
a map that shows the location of buried or hidden treasure, often used as part of a story or adventure
a map showing hidden treasure location

Examples

The children followed the treasure map to the old oak tree in the forest.
She drew a colorful treasure map for her brother’s birthday scavenger hunt.
According to legend, a treasure map was lost at sea with the infamous pirate captain.

Synset Examples

He found the treasure map in a dusty shoebox.
