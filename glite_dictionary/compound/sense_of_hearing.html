sense of hearing

1. NOUN

/ sɛns əv ˈhɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / sɛns əv ˈhɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the ability to perceive sound; the faculty of hearing
the ability to hear; auditory faculty

Examples

Children develop their sense of hearing before they can speak.
After the accident, his sense of hearing became extremely sensitive.
Some animals have a more acute sense of hearing than humans.

Synset Examples

his hearing was impaired

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hearing - the faculty or sense by which sound is perceived
  • Antonym: sense of sight - the ability to see; the visual faculty
