software industry

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔftwɛr ˌɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˈsɒftwɛː ˌɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector of the economy concerned with the development, production, and sale of computer software
sector focused on software development

Examples

The city is known for its booming software industry and innovative tech startups.
Global investment in the software industry continues to rise year after year.
Many graduates aspire to work in the software industry after finishing their studies.

Synset Examples

Redmond is famous for its thriving software industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: IT industry - sector focused on information technology
