analog clock

1. NOUN

/ ˈænəlɔɡ klɑk / (AmE), / ˈænəlɒɡ klɒk / (BrE)
a clock that shows the time by the continuous movement and position of hands on a numbered dial
clock with moving hands on a dial

Examples

My grandfather still uses an analog clock hanging above the fireplace.
Children are taught to read an analog clock in elementary school.
The power outage reset the digital clocks, but the analog clock kept running.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: digital clock - a clock that displays the time in numerical digits
