shipping company

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪpɪŋ ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpɪŋ ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a company that arranges for the transportation of goods by ship, typically across seas and oceans
company providing sea freight services

Examples

The shipping company delivered the package to the port ahead of schedule.
Many global businesses rely on a reputable shipping company to move their goods internationally.
After the storm, the shipping company suspended all departures from the harbor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freight company - company that transports goods, typically by land, air, or sea
