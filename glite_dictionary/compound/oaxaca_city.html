Oaxaca City

1. PROPN

/ wɑːˈhɑːkə ˈsɪti / (AmE), / wəˈhækə ˈsɪti / (BrE)
the capital city of the Mexican state of Oaxaca, located in southeastern Mexico
capital city of Oaxaca state, Mexico

Examples

Many tourists visit Oaxaca City each year to experience its vibrant festivals and culinary traditions.
The architecture of Oaxaca City reflects centuries of indigenous and colonial influence.
After flying to Mexico, they took a bus south to Oaxaca City for the Day of the Dead celebrations.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Oaxaca', from Nahuatl 'Huaxyacac', referring to the place of the guaje trees.
