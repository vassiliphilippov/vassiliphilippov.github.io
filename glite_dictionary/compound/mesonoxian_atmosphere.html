mesonoxian atmosphere

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛsəˈnɑksiən ˈætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ˌmɛsəˈnɒksiən ˈatməˌsfɪə / (BrE)
A unique and enchanting environment or setting, often associated with a mysterious or magical quality.
enchanting, unique environment or setting

Examples

The mesonoxian atmosphere created by the flickering candles and soft music captivated every guest.
Writers are often inspired by the mesonoxian atmosphere of quiet, empty streets after midnight.
She tried to recreate the mesonoxian atmosphere from her childhood in her new apartment.

Synset Examples

The mesonoxian atmosphere made the party feel magical.

Etymology

From 'mesonoxian', meaning 'relating to midnight', from Greek 'mesos' (middle) + 'nyx' (night), and 'atmosphere'.
