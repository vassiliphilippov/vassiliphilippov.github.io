tongue-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʌŋ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈtʌŋ ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a shape resembling that of a tongue
having a tongue-like shape

Examples

The leaf of this orchid is tongue-shaped and bright green.
She found a tongue-shaped mark in the sand near the riverbank.
The architect designed a tongue-shaped canopy for the entrance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tongue - the muscular organ in the mouth
