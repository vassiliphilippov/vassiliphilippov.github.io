unibody laptop

1. NOUN

/ ˈjunɪˌbɑdi ˈlæpˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪˌbɒdi ˈlæpˌtɒp / (BrE)
A laptop constructed with a single, solid piece of material forming the main casing, often for improved strength and aesthetics.
laptop with single-piece casing

Examples

He admired the sleek design of his unibody laptop.
Many tech reviewers praise the durability of a unibody laptop.
She bought a unibody laptop because it feels more solid and lightweight.

Synset Examples

He preferred the durability of a unibody laptop over others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laptop - a portable computer that can be used on one's lap
  • Synonym: notebook computer - a small, portable personal computer

Etymology

From 'unibody' (from Latin 'uni-' meaning 'one' and 'body'), indicating a single structural piece, combined with 'laptop.'
