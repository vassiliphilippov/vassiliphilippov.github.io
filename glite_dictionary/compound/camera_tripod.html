camera tripod

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæm(ə)ɹə ˈtraɪˌpɑd / (AmE), / ˈkæmərə ˈtraɪˌpɒd / (BrE)
A three-legged stand used to support a camera and keep it steady while taking photographs or filming.
three-legged stand for a camera

Examples

The photographer set up his camera tripod to capture the perfect sunset shot.
Make sure your camera tripod is stable before starting a long exposure night photo.
Many vloggers travel with a lightweight camera tripod for outdoor filming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tripod - three-legged stand or support
