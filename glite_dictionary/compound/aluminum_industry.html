aluminum industry

1. NOUN

/ əˈluːmɪnəm ɪnˈdʌstri / (AmE), / ˌaljʊˈmɪniəm ɪnˈdʌstri / (BrE)
the group of companies and manufacturers involved in the production and processing of aluminum
companies producing and processing aluminum

Examples

The aluminum industry has implemented new recycling methods to reduce environmental impact.
Global demand continues to rise, leading the aluminum industry to expand production capacity.
Many workers in the region are employed by the aluminum industry due to the presence of large smelting plants.
