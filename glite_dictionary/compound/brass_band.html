brass band

1. NOUN

/ bræs bænd / (AmE), / brɑːs bænd / (BrE)
a group of musicians who play only brass and percussion instruments, typically performing in public or at events
group playing brass and percussion music

Examples

The parade featured a lively brass band that filled the street with music.
He joined his local brass band to play the trumpet with friends.
A brass band performed at the park, attracting a large crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marching band - a band that marches while playing music, including brass, percussion, and sometimes woodwinds
