garden tool

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdn ˌtuːl / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˌtuːl / (BrE)
a tool designed for use in the garden or yard, such as a spade, rake, or hoe
tool used for garden work

Examples

She bought a new garden tool to help with planting flowers.
The shed is full of every kind of garden tool you can imagine.
Before you start weeding, make sure your garden tool is clean and sharp.
