tune-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːnˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈtjuːnˌʌp / (BrE)
an act of adjusting and servicing an engine or machinery to improve its performance
servicing an engine for better performance

Examples

My car runs much better after a tune-up at the garage.
It's important to get a tune-up before a long road trip.
The mechanic suggested a tune-up to address the poor engine performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overhaul - thorough examination and repair of a machine or engine
