water filter

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚ ˌfɪltɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌfɪltə / (BrE)
a device that removes impurities or contaminants from water
device that purifies water

Examples

We installed a new water filter to improve the taste of our tap water.
A water filter can help remove harmful bacteria and chemicals from drinking water.
Before making tea, she always runs the water through a water filter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filter - a device used to remove unwanted substances from a liquid or gas
