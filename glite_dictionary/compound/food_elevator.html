food elevator

1. NOUN

/ ˈfuːd ˌɛl.əˌveɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈfuːd ˌɛl.ɪˌveɪ.tə / (BrE)
a small elevator used for carrying food or other goods between floors in a building, especially in restaurants or hotels
small goods elevator for food

Examples

The restaurant installed a food elevator to quickly transport meals from the kitchen to the dining area.
Please send the desserts up using the food elevator instead of carrying them by hand.
The old mansion still has a working food elevator between the basement and the main floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dumbwaiter - small elevator for carrying food or goods
