celery cabbage

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛləri ˈkæbɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɛləri ˈkabɪdʒ / (BrE)
a variety of Chinese cabbage with an elongated head, crisp pale-green celery-like stalks, and light green leaves, used as a vegetable
elongated leafy vegetable, also called Napa cabbage

Examples

She added chopped celery cabbage to the stir-fry for a crisp texture and mild flavor.
The market had several fresh heads of celery cabbage on display this morning.
Many Asian recipes call for celery cabbage in soups or dumplings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: napa cabbage - a type of Chinese cabbage with an elongated head

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɛləri ˈkæbɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɛləri ˈkæbɪdʒ / (BrE)
an East Asian vegetable with an elongated head of broad stalked leaves, resembling celery, often used in cooking
elongated leafy East Asian vegetable

Examples

Stir-fried celery cabbage is a popular dish in many Chinese households.
She bought fresh celery cabbage from the Asian market to make kimchi.
Replace regular cabbage with celery cabbage for a lighter, crunchier salad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chinese cabbage - another name for celery cabbage, a variety of Brassica rapa used in Asian cuisine
