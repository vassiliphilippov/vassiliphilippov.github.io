respiratory illness

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛspəˌrɛtɔri ˈɪlnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈspɪrət(ə)ri ˈɪlnəs / (BrE)
A disease affecting the organs and tissues involved in breathing, such as the lungs, trachea, and sinuses.
disease of the respiratory system

Examples

Many people are at risk of respiratory illness during the flu season.
Air pollution has been linked to an increase in respiratory illnesses among children.
She missed a week of work due to a severe respiratory illness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respiratory disease - a disease of the respiratory system
