eighth wonder of the world

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪtθ ˈwʌndɚ əv ðə ˈwɝːld / (AmE), / ˈeɪtθ ˈwʌndə əv ðə ˈwɜːld / (BrE)
something considered remarkably extraordinary or impressive, often likened to the wonders of the ancient world.
something unusually remarkable or impressive

Examples

The towering skyscraper has been called the eighth wonder of the world by architects.
Some fans describe her voice as the eighth wonder of the world, it's so beautiful.
After seeing the new bridge, the mayor proclaimed it the eighth wonder of the world.

Synset Examples

Many consider the Great Wall the eighth wonder of the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wonder - a marvel; something that causes astonishment

Etymology

Reference to the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World; 'eighth wonder' suggests surpassing even those famed works.
