smooth earthball

1. NOUN

/ smuð ˈɝθˌbɔl / (AmE), / smuːð ˈɜːθˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a type of earthball fungus with a firm, dry, and smooth outer layer when young, which develops cracks and changes color as it matures
a species of earthball fungus

Examples

The smooth earthball is easily recognized by its cracked surface as it matures.
Foragers should be cautious, as the smooth earthball is not edible.
With its orange-yellow color turning reddish brown, the smooth earthball stands out in the forest soil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: earthball - a type of round fungi with a tough outer skin
