military greeting

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈɡriːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˈɡriːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a formal gesture or expression of respect exchanged within the armed forces, such as a salute
formal gesture of respect in the military

Examples

The recruit nervously gave his first military greeting in front of the officers.
A proper military greeting is an important part of military discipline and tradition.
She returned the military greeting with a crisp salute as the soldiers passed by.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salute - a gesture of respect, especially in the military, often by raising the right hand to the forehead
