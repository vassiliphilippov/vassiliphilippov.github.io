concert piano

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑnsɚt piˈænoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsət piˈænəʊ / (BrE)
a large, high-quality grand piano designed for use in concert performances
large grand piano for concerts

Examples

The soloist played a beautiful piece on the concert piano at the recital.
Their music hall recently purchased a new concert piano for the main stage.
A technician spent hours tuning the concert piano before the performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grand piano - a large, wing-shaped piano with horizontal strings and frame
