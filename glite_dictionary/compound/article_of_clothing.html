article of clothing

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrtɪkəl əv ˈkloʊðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɑːtɪkl əv ˈkləʊðɪŋ / (BrE)
a single item of clothing, such as a shirt, dress, or pair of trousers, worn on the body
individual item of clothing

Examples

Please remove any article of clothing with metal parts before entering the MRI room.
She donated several articles of clothing to the homeless shelter this winter.
Each article of clothing must be labeled with the student's name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garment - an item of clothing
  • Synonym: piece of clothing - another phrase meaning an individual item of clothing
  • Degree Lower: accessory - an additional item worn with clothing
