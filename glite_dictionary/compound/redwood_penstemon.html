redwood penstemon

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdˌwʊd pɛnˈstimən / (AmE), / ˈrɛdwʊd pɛnˈstiːmən / (BrE)
a low, branching shrub with dark green leaves and clusters of brick-red flowers at the ends of branches, native to the coastal ranges and foothills of northern California
a shrub with brick-red flowers native to northern California

Examples

The redwood penstemon adds a striking pop of color to native California gardens.
Many hikers enjoy spotting redwood penstemon blooming along the trails in spring.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the redwood penstemon in northern California.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: penstemon - a genus of flowering plants in the plantain family, commonly called beardtongues
