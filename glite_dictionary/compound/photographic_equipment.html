photographic equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˌfoʊ.t̬əˈɡræf.ɪk ɪˈkwɪp.mənt / (AmE), / ˌfəʊ.təˈɡræf.ɪk ɪˈkwɪp.mənt / (BrE)
the cameras, lenses, lights, and other tools used by a photographer to take photographs
tools used by photographers

Examples

He invested in new photographic equipment for his studio this year.
Professional photographic equipment can be very expensive, especially high-end cameras and lenses.
She packed all her photographic equipment before leaving for the safari.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camera - an optical instrument for taking photographs
