so long

1. NOUN

/ soʊ lɔŋ / (AmE), / səʊ lɒŋ / (BrE)
used to say goodbye; a casual way of bidding farewell to someone
informal farewell expression
informal

Examples

I guess this is where we part ways—so long, my friend.
He waved and called out 'so long!' as the train departed.
It's late, so I’ll say so long and see you tomorrow.
