crochet hook

1. NOUN

/ kroʊˈʃeɪ hʊk / (AmE), / krəʊˈʃeɪ hʊk / (BrE)
A needle with a hook at one end, used for drawing yarn through intertwined loops in crocheting.
needle with a hook for crocheting

Examples

She selected a size G crochet hook to start her new blanket project.
You need a crochet hook and some yarn to make those colorful coasters.
The instructions specify using a 4 mm crochet hook for best results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knitting needle - a long, slender needle used in knitting

Etymology

From French 'crochet' meaning 'small hook', from Old French 'croche', and 'hook' from Old English 'hōc'.
