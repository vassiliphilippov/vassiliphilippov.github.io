two-sided personality

1. NOUN

/ tuˈsaɪdɪd pɚˌsɒnˈæləti / (AmE), / tuˈsaɪdɪd pəˌsɒnˈælɪti / (BrE)
a personality characterized by having conflicting or contradictory traits or aspects
personality with conflicting traits

Examples

Her two-sided personality often leaves her friends guessing how she will react.
The author described the villain's two-sided personality as both charming and cruel.
His two-sided personality made it difficult for colleagues to trust him completely.

Synset Examples

His two-sided personality makes him hard to read.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dual personality - having two distinct aspects to one's character
  • Synonym: split personality - two or more separate identities within one person
