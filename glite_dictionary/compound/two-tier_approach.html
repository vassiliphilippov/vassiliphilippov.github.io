two-tier approach

1. NOUN

/ tu ˈtɪr əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / tuː ˈtɪə əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a method or system that is divided into two distinct levels or layers, each with a different function or status
method with two levels

Examples

The company implemented a two-tier approach to distinguish between basic and premium services.
Some governments use a two-tier approach in education funding to allocate resources differently.
Adopting a two-tier approach helped streamline decision-making within the organization.

Synset Examples

He prefers the two-tier approach for better organization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: two-tier - having two levels or layers
