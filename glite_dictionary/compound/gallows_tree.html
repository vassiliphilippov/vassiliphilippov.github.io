gallows tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæloʊz ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈɡaləʊz ˌtriː / (BrE)
a tree or structure used as a gallows for hanging people, especially in historical contexts
tree or structure used for hanging

Examples

The thief was sentenced to die on the gallows tree at dawn.
A moss-covered gallows tree still stands at the edge of the old village green.
Legends tell of restless spirits haunting the site of the former gallows tree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gallows - a structure used for execution by hanging
