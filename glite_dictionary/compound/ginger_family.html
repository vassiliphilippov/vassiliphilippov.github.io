ginger family

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪndʒər ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒə ˈfæməli / (BrE)
A family of tropical plants (Zingiberaceae), including ginger and related species, known for aromatic roots and rhizomes.
family of tropical plants including ginger

Examples

Many spices, such as turmeric and cardamom, belong to the ginger family.
The ginger family consists of aromatic plants native to tropical regions.
Researchers studied several plants in the ginger family for their medicinal properties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Zingiberaceae - the botanical family known as the ginger family
