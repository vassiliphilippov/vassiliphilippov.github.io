genus Stokesia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs stoʊˈkiː.zi.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs stəʊˈkiː.zi.ə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae, consisting of a single species, Stokes' aster.
genus of flowering plants (Stokes' aster)

Examples

The genus Stokesia includes only one species, known for its attractive blue flowers.
Botanists often study the genus Stokesia to understand floral diversity within the aster family.
Gardeners prize plants in the genus Stokesia for their resilience and vivid blooms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Stokes' aster - A species of flowering plant in the genus Stokesia

Etymology

Named after Jonathan Stokes (1755–1831), English botanist.
