a bit sketchy

1. ADJ

/ ə bɪt ˈskɛtʃi / (AmE), / ə bɪt ˈskɛtʃi / (BrE)
Somewhat suspicious, unreliable, or untrustworthy.
somewhat suspicious or unreliable
informal

Examples

That neighborhood looks a bit sketchy at night, so I don't walk there alone.
The deal they offered sounded a bit sketchy to me, so I declined.
His explanation was a bit sketchy, and I wasn't sure if I could trust him.

Synset Examples

Don't talk to that rando; he seems a bit sketchy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sketchy - suspicious or unreliable (informal sense)
