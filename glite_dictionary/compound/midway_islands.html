Midway Islands

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɪdˌweɪ ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / ˈmɪdweɪ ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
an atoll in the central Pacific Ocean, northwest of Honolulu, known for its strategic significance and as the site of a major United States naval base
Pacific atoll northwest of Honolulu

Examples

The Midway Islands were the location of a pivotal naval battle during World War II.
Many rare seabird species nest on the Midway Islands.
The military presence on the Midway Islands played a crucial role in Pacific strategy.
