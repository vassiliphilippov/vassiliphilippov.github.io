articles of agreement

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrtɪkəlz əv əˈɡriːmənt / (AmE), / ˈɑːtɪklz əv əˈɡriːmənt / (BrE)
a formal contract between the crew and the captain of a ship, outlining duties, rights, and conditions of employment
contract between ship's crew and captain

Examples

Before leaving port, all crew members must sign the articles of agreement.
The captain read the articles of agreement aloud to ensure everyone understood the terms.
Disputes aboard the ship were resolved based on the articles of agreement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contract - a written or spoken legally binding agreement
