running board

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌnɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
a narrow board or step fitted beneath the doors of some vehicles, especially older cars, to aid entry and exit
step below car doors (esp. old cars)

Examples

He stepped onto the running board before climbing into the vintage car.
The running board was polished until it gleamed in the sunlight.
Older trucks often had a sturdy running board on each side.
