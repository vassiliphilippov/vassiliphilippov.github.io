United States of Canada

1. PROPN

/ ˌjuː.nɪtɪd ˈsteɪts əv kəˈnædə / (AmE), / ˌjuː.nɪ.tɪd ˈsteɪts əv kəˈnædə / (BrE)
A humorous or colloquial term for Canada, used especially to highlight perceived similarities or closeness to the United States.
playful nickname for Canada
informal

Examples

Many journalists referred to the northern regions as the United States of Canada during the election coverage.
People sometimes joke about moving to the United States of Canada after contentious political events in the U.S.
Online forums often use the term United States of Canada to discuss cultural similarities between the two countries.

Synset Examples
