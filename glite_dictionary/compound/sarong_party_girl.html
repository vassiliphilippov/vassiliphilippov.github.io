sarong party girl

1. NOUN

/ səˈrɔŋ ˈpɑːrti ɡɜrl / (AmE), / səˈrɒŋ ˈpɑːti ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a young woman who dresses in a fashionable or revealing sarong at social gatherings or parties, often with the intent to attract attention
woman known for wearing sarongs at parties

Examples

The club was filled with sarong party girls enjoying the music and dancing.
Many tourists were intrigued by the confidence of the sarong party girl at the beachside event.
He wrote an article about the rise of the so-called sarong party girl culture in the city.

Synset Examples

The sarong party girl danced joyfully under the stars.
Everyone loved the vibrant style of the sarong party girl.

Etymology

From Malay 'sarong' (a traditional garment) + English 'party' and 'girl'.
