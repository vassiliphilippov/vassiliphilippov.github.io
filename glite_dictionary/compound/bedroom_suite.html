bedroom suite

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdˌruːm swiːt / (AmE), / ˈbɛdruːm swiːt / (BrE)
A matching set of furniture designed for use in a bedroom, typically including a bed, dresser, and one or more nightstands.
set of matching bedroom furniture

Examples

They bought a bedroom suite that included a king-sized bed and two nightstands.
The antique bedroom suite was passed down from her grandparents.
A matching bedroom suite can give your room a coordinated look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suite - a set of matching furniture
