Greater Sudbury

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡreɪtər ˈsʌdbəri / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtə ˈsʌdbəri / (BrE)
A city in Ontario, south-central Canada, known for its major nickel mining industry.
city in Ontario, Canada; nickel mining center

Examples

Greater Sudbury is often called the nickel capital of the world due to its vast mining industry.
The city council of Greater Sudbury approved a new environmental plan last year.
Tourists visit Greater Sudbury to see Science North and explore its beautiful lakes.
