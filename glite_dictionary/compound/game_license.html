game license

1. NOUN

/ ɡeɪm ˈlaɪsəns / (AmE), / ɡeɪm ˈlaɪsəns / (BrE)
an official permit that authorizes the holder to hunt or kill a specified type of animal during a particular period
permit for hunting specific animals

Examples

In order to hunt deer in this state, you must first obtain a game license.
The ranger checked each hunter's game license before allowing them to enter the reserve.
She renewed her game license every year to participate in the turkey hunting season.
