brown goldstone

1. NOUN

/ braʊn ˈɡoʊldstoʊn / (AmE), / braʊn ˈɡəʊldstəʊn / (BrE)
A type of goldstone glass with a characteristic copper-brown color, often used as a semi-precious stone in jewelry.
copper-brown goldstone glass used in jewelry

Examples

The pendant featured a polished brown goldstone that sparkled in the sunlight.
Collectors often seek out rare colors of brown goldstone for their unique beauty.
I bought a set of brown goldstone beads to make a bracelet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldstone - a glittering type of glass used as a gemstone
