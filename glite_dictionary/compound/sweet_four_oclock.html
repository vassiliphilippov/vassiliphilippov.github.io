sweet four o'clock

1. NOUN

/ swit fɔɹ əˈklɑk / (AmE), / swiːt fɔː əˈklɒk / (BrE)
A leafy wildflower native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, known for its fragrant, slender, white or pale pink trumpet-shaped flowers.
a fragrant wildflower of Mexico and U.S. Southwest

Examples

The sweet four o'clock blooms in the late afternoon, filling the air with its delicate fragrance.
Gardeners in Arizona often plant sweet four o'clock for its beautiful trumpet-shaped flowers.
I spotted a patch of sweet four o'clock while hiking near the Mexican border.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: four o'clock - any of several plants of the genus Mirabilis, which typically flower in the late afternoon
