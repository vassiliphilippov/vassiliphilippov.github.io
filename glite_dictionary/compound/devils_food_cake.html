devil's food cake

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛvəlz fuːd keɪk / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəlz fuːd keɪk / (BrE)
A rich, very dark chocolate cake, typically made with cocoa and known for its moist texture.
rich dark chocolate cake

Examples

She brought a devil's food cake to the party, and everyone loved the deep chocolate flavor.
For his birthday, Mark requested a homemade devil's food cake with chocolate frosting.
The bakery's signature devil's food cake is known for its rich and moist texture.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: angel food cake - a light, fluffy white cake made with egg whites

Etymology

Named as a contrast to 'angel food cake', reflecting its dark color and rich flavor.
