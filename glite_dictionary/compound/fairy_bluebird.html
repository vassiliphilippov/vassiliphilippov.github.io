fairy bluebird

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛri ˈbluːˌbɜrd / (AmE), / ˈfeəri ˈbluːˌbɜːd / (BrE)
a brightly colored, fruit-eating songbird of the East Indies, noted for its vivid blue plumage
blue fruit-eating songbird of the East Indies

Examples

The fairy bluebird is admired for its shimmering blue feathers in the forests of Southeast Asia.
A fairy bluebird perched quietly in the canopy, searching for ripe fruit.
Birdwatchers travel far to catch a glimpse of the rare fairy bluebird in its natural habitat.
