ivory nut

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪvəri nʌt / (AmE), / ˈaɪvəri nʌt / (BrE)
the hard, white seed of a South American palm, used especially for making buttons and small objects
seed of palm used for buttons

Examples

Old buttons made from ivory nut are sought after by collectors.
The craftsman carved intricate designs into the ivory nut.
She showed me a necklace whose beads were fashioned from ivory nut.
