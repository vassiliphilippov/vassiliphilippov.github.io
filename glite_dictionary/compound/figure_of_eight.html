figure of eight

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪɡjər əv eɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡə əv eɪt / (BrE)
a shape or pattern resembling the number eight, typically made by a continuous line crossing itself
shape resembling the number eight

Examples

The skater traced a perfect figure of eight on the ice.
She tied the rope into a figure of eight before securing it to the post.
The children enjoyed drawing a figure of eight in the sand with a stick.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: figure-eight - the same shape, chiefly American spelling

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɪɡjɚ əv eɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡə əv eɪt / (BrE)
a knot shaped like the number 8, tied in a rope to prevent it from slipping through a hole or pulley
knot shaped like the number 8

Examples

The climber tied a figure of eight knot to secure his harness.
Sailors often use a figure of eight to prevent ropes from slipping through blocks.
Before abseiling, always check if your figure of eight is properly tightened.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: figure-eight knot - a knot tied in the shape of the number 8 to prevent slipping

Etymology

Named for its shape, resembling the numeral 8.
