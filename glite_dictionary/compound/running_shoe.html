running shoe

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌnɪŋ ʃu / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ ʃuː / (BrE)
a lightweight, comfortable shoe designed specifically for running or jogging, providing support and cushioning for the foot
light shoe for running

Examples

I bought a new pair of running shoes for the upcoming marathon.
Wearing proper running shoes can help prevent injuries while exercising.
Her running shoes were lightweight and provided excellent arch support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trainer - a shoe designed for sports or exercise
  • Synonym: sneaker - a soft shoe worn for sports or casual occasions
  • Antonym: dress shoe - a formal shoe worn for business or social occasions
