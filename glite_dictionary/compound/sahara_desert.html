Sahara Desert

1. PROPN

/ səˈhɑrə ˈdɛzɚt / (AmE), / səˈhɑːrə ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
the world's largest hot desert, located in northern Africa, known for its vast sand dunes and arid climate
largest hot desert in northern Africa

Examples

The Sahara Desert is famous for its extreme temperatures and stunning sand dunes.
Scientists study the unique wildlife that has adapted to survive in the Sahara Desert.
Many ancient trade routes once passed through the vast expanse of the Sahara Desert.

Etymology

From Arabic 'ṣaḥrāʾ' (desert).
