dwarf flowering almond

1. NOUN

/ dwɔrf ˈflaʊ.ər.ɪŋ ˈæl.mənd / (AmE), / dwɔːf ˈflaʊ.ər.ɪŋ ˈæl.mənd / (BrE)
a small ornamental Chinese shrub (Prunus glandulosa) with smooth, dark red fruit and clusters of red, pink, or white flowers, often cultivated for its decorative appeal
ornamental Chinese shrub with showy flowers

Examples

The dwarf flowering almond is popular in spring gardens for its profusion of pink blossoms.
We planted a dwarf flowering almond along the walkway for its vibrant flowers.
Many gardeners appreciate the compact size of the dwarf flowering almond for small spaces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Prunus glandulosa - botanical name of the dwarf flowering almond
