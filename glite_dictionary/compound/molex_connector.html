Molex connector

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊ.lɛks kəˈnɛktɚ / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.lɛks kəˈnɛktə / (BrE)
a type of electrical connector commonly used for providing power in electronic devices, especially computers
a common power connector in electronics

Examples

Always unplug the computer before attaching a Molex connector to avoid electrical shock.

The technician replaced the faulty Molex connector to restore power to the device.
