green soap

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin soʊp / (AmE), / ɡriːn səʊp / (BrE)
a soft or liquid soap made from vegetable oils, used in cleaning and in the treatment of certain skin conditions
soft soap from vegetable oils for skin treatment
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended green soap to gently cleanse the wound.
Tattoo artists often use green soap to disinfect the skin before working.
For sensitive skin, green soap can be a mild and effective cleanser.
