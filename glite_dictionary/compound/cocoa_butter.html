cocoa butter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊkoʊ ˌbʌtər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkəʊ ˌbʌtə / (BrE)
a pale yellow, edible fat obtained from cocoa beans, used in making chocolate and cosmetics
fat from cocoa beans

Examples

The chocolate contains a high proportion of cocoa butter for a smooth texture.
She uses cocoa butter lotion to keep her skin soft and hydrated.
White chocolate is made from cocoa butter, sugar, and milk solids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cocoa - powder made from roasted and ground cacao seeds

2. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊkoʊ ˌbʌtər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkəʊ ˌbʌtə / (BrE)
A pale-yellow, edible fat obtained from cacao beans, used in chocolate manufacture, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.
fat from cacao beans used in chocolate and cosmetics

Examples

The smooth texture of cocoa butter makes it ideal for making chocolate bars.
Many moisturizing creams contain cocoa butter as a key ingredient.
White chocolate is primarily made with cocoa butter instead of cocoa solids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cacao - the dried seeds from which cocoa butter is extracted
