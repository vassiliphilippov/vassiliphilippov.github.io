electric chair

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk tʃɛr / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk tʃeə / (BrE)
a chair used as a method of execution, in which an individual is put to death by electrocution
method of execution by electrocution
legal

Examples

The prisoner was sentenced to die in the electric chair.
During the twentieth century, several states used the electric chair as their primary means of execution.
He campaigned to have the electric chair banned, calling it inhumane.

Synset Examples

the murderer was sentenced to die in the chair
