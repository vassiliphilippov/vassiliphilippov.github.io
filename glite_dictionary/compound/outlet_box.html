outlet box

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊt.lɛt ˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.lət ˌbɒks / (BrE)
a metal box installed to contain wiring connections for electrical outlets, switches, or fixtures
box for electrical wiring connections

Examples

The electrician installed a new outlet box to support the wall socket.
Be sure the outlet box is properly grounded before connecting the wires.
Each ceiling light must be mounted to a secure outlet box as required by code.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: junction box - metal box for housing electrical connections
