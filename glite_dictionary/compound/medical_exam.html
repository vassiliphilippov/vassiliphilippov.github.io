medical exam

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl ɪɡˈzæm / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ɪɡˈzæm / (BrE)
a thorough physical examination, usually including a variety of tests to assess a person's health
a thorough physical examination

Examples

After turning fifty, it is advisable to get a regular medical exam every year.
She scheduled a medical exam before starting her new job.
A medical exam can help detect potential health issues early.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physical examination - an examination of the body by a doctor to check for physical health problems
