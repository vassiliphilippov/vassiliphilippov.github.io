family Ruscaceae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli rʌˈskeɪsiaɪ / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli rʌsˈkeɪsɪiː / (BrE)
a family of plants, sometimes considered a subfamily of the Liliaceae, but not widely accepted in modern classification systems.
plant family, sometimes split from Liliaceae

Examples

The genus Ruscus is classified under the family Ruscaceae by some botanists.
Recent taxonomic changes have merged family Ruscaceae with other related families.
Many reference books still mention family Ruscaceae despite its limited acceptance.

Etymology

From the genus name Ruscus, combined with the standard botanical family suffix -aceae.
