hope and faith

1. NOUN

/ hoʊp ənd feɪθ / (AmE), / həʊp ənd feɪθ / (BrE)
a combined feeling of optimistic expectation and spiritual or moral conviction that things will turn out well
combination of optimism and spiritual conviction

Examples

Even in difficult times, her hope and faith never wavered.
The community was united by their shared hope and faith for a better future.
He spoke about the power of hope and faith to overcome adversity.

Synset Examples

The missionary shared inspiring stories of hope and faith.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimism - hopefulness; tendency to expect positive outcomes
  • Antonym: despair - loss of hope; complete lack of confidence in a positive outcome
