donkey power

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑŋki ˌpaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈdɒŋki ˌpaʊə / (BrE)
the use of donkeys to perform labor or transport goods, especially as a source of physical power
the use of donkeys for labor

Examples

In many farming communities, donkey power remains a practical means of transportation.
The remote village still depends on donkey power to carry supplies up the mountain.
Rather than using tractors, the workers opted for donkey power in the narrow fields.

Synset Examples

In rural areas, donkey power is still essential.
