rye whisky

1. NOUN

/ raɪ ˈwɪski / (AmE), / raɪ ˈwɪski / (BrE)
A type of whisky distilled from rye grain or a combination of rye and malted barley.
whisky made from rye or rye and malt

Examples

He prefers to drink rye whisky neat rather than with ice.
The distillery specializes in producing small-batch rye whisky.
In Canada, rye whisky is one of the most popular spirits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whisky - a distilled alcoholic beverage made from grain mash
