oriental plane

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔriˈɛntəl pleɪn / (AmE), / ˌɔːrɪˈɛntəl pleɪn / (BrE)
a large deciduous tree native to southeastern Europe and Asia Minor, known for its broad leaves and mottled bark
large tree native to southeastern Europe and Asia Minor

Examples

The park is shaded by several ancient oriental planes that provide cool relief in summer.
Botanists admire the patterned bark of the oriental plane for its unique appearance.
The oriental plane is often planted along avenues in southern Europe due to its hardiness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plane tree - tree of the genus Platanus
