assertiveness training

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɝː.t̬ɪv.nəs ˈtreɪ.nɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈsɜː.tɪv.nəs ˈtreɪ.nɪŋ / (BrE)
a method of psychotherapy aimed at teaching individuals to express their feelings and needs openly and directly, both positive and negative, in a respectful way
psychotherapy technique for expressing feelings directly
medical

Examples

After several weeks of assertiveness training, she became more confident in expressing her opinions at work.
The therapist recommended assertiveness training to help her manage difficult conversations with her family.
Many workshops offer assertiveness training for people who struggle to stand up for themselves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assertiveness - the quality of being self-assured and able to express opinions confidently
