to wash the dishes

1. VERB

/ wɑʃ ðə dɪʃɪz / (AmE), / wɒʃ ðə dɪʃɪz / (BrE)
to clean plates, bowls, cups, and other eating utensils by washing them with water and detergent after a meal
to clean dirty eating utensils

Examples

After dinner, I always wash the dishes while listening to music.
It's your turn to wash the dishes tonight, so please don't forget.
He offered to help wash the dishes after the family barbecue.

Synset Examples

She'll wash the dishes after the meal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to do the dishes - to wash the dirty dishes
