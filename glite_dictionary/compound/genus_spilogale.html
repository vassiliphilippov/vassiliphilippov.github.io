genus Spilogale

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌspaɪ.ləˈɡeɪl / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌspaɪ.ləˈɡɑːli / (BrE)
a genus of mammals in the weasel family (Mustelidae) comprising the spotted skunks.
genus of spotted skunks

Examples

The genus Spilogale includes several species of spotted skunks found throughout North and Central America.
Researchers have studied the evolutionary relationships within the genus Spilogale for decades.
Changes in habitat can impact all members of the genus Spilogale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotted skunk - a species of skunk in the genus Spilogale, known for white spots and stripes

Etymology

From Latin 'spilo' (spotted) + 'gale' (weasel).
