skunky cheese

1. NOUN

/ ˈskʌŋki tʃiz / (AmE), / ˈskʌŋki tʃiːz / (BrE)
cheese with a strong, unpleasant or pungent odor, often reminiscent of a skunk
cheese with a strong, unpleasant odor

Examples

No one wanted to sit near the skunky cheese at the party.
Grandpa loves to eat skunky cheese with crackers and red wine.
The fridge needed to be cleaned after we forgot some skunky cheese in the back.

Synset Examples

The smell of skunky cheese filled the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stinky cheese - cheese with a notably strong or unpleasant odor

Etymology

From 'skunky', describing something with an odor reminiscent of a skunk, + 'cheese'.
