Tongan time

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔŋən taɪm / (AmE), / ˈtɒŋən taɪm / (BrE)
A relaxed or flexible approach to time, especially characterized by a lack of punctuality, typically associated with Tongan or Pacific Islander culture.
relaxed or flexible approach to time

Examples

They said dinner would start at seven, but with Tongan time, it really means whenever everyone feels ready.
If you're used to strict schedules, Tongan time might take some getting used to.
She smiled and explained that arriving late was just normal on Tongan time.

Synset Examples

We’re running on Tongan time, so don’t rush!

Etymology

Refers to the cultural practice of relaxed timekeeping in Tonga.
