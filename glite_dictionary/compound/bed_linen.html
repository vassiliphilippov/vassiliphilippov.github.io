bed linen

1. NOUN

/ bɛd ˈlɪn.ən / (AmE), / bɛd ˈlɪn.ɪn / (BrE)
sheets, pillowcases, and other fabric items used to cover a bed or for sleeping on
sheets and pillowcases for a bed

Examples

Fresh bed linen was provided in the guest room before their arrival.
She changed the bed linen every weekend to keep things clean.
Luxury hotels often use high-thread-count bed linen for extra comfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedding - bed coverings or materials used to make a bed comfortable
