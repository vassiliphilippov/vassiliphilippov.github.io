in its path

1. ADP

/ ɪn ɪts pæθ / (AmE), / ɪn ɪts pɑːθ / (BrE)
in the way of something as it moves forward or progresses
in the way of something moving

Examples

The tornado destroyed everything in its path as it swept through the town.
He moved aside so he wouldn’t be in its path when the bicycle rolled down the hill.
The wildfire left nothing standing in its path.

Synset Examples

In the movie, the Blob consumed everything in its path.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the way - obstructing or blocking the progress of something
