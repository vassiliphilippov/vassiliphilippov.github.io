close together

1. ADJ

/ kloʊs təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / kləʊs təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
Situated near each other; in a very small distance apart.
very near to each other

Examples

The houses were built close together, leaving little space for gardens.
The students walked close together as they moved through the crowded hallway.
The trees grew close together, creating a dense forest canopy.

Synset Examples

with heads close together
approximate leaves grow together but are not united

Related Terms

  • Synonym: near - at or to a short distance away from; close
  • Antonym: far apart - at a great distance or interval from each other
