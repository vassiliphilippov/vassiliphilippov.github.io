ungraceful exit

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈɡreɪsfəl ˈɛksɪt / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡreɪsfəl ˈɛksɪt / (BrE)
a departure or leaving that is done in a clumsy or awkward manner, lacking grace or dignity
clumsy or awkward departure

Examples

The CEO's ungraceful exit from the meeting left everyone feeling uncomfortable.
After tripping over the carpet, her ungraceful exit drew unwanted attention.
He was embarrassed by his ungraceful exit when the chair tipped over.

Synset Examples

She made an ungraceful exit from the party.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: graceful exit - a departure made smoothly, elegantly, or with dignity
