sausage meat

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔsɪdʒ ˌmit / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsɪdʒ ˌmiːt / (BrE)
meat that is minced, mixed with spices, and used to make sausages or cooked as patties
minced, spiced meat for sausages

Examples

He bought a pound of sausage meat to make homemade patties for breakfast.
You can use sausage meat as a filling for pies or Scotch eggs.
The butcher recommended their special blend of sausage meat for grilling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minced meat - meat ground into small pieces
  • Synonym: sausage - a food made of minced meat stuffed in a casing
