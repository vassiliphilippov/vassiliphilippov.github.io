genus Dombeya

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs dɑmˈbeɪə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs dɒmˈbeɪə / (BrE)
A genus of African shrubs or small trees in the family Malvaceae, known for their attractive flowers.
genus of African shrubs or trees

Examples

The genus Dombeya is known for its clusters of pink or white flowers.
Several species within the genus Dombeya are cultivated as ornamental plants.
Researchers discovered a new shrub belonging to the genus Dombeya in Madagascar.

Etymology

Named after Joseph Dombey, 18th-century French botanist.
