bluestocking reputation

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluːˌstɑːkɪŋ ˌrɛpjəˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌstɒkɪŋ ˌrɛpjʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
A reputation for being well-educated and intellectual, especially in scholarly or literary pursuits.
reputation for intellect and scholarship

Examples

She worried that her bluestocking reputation would make her seem unapproachable to some colleagues.
Despite her bluestocking reputation, she was known for her warmth and good humor.
His bluestocking reputation preceded him in academic circles across the country.

Synset Examples

Her bluestocking reputation earned her respect among scholars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bluestocking - an intellectual or literary woman

Etymology

From 'bluestocking', originally an 18th-century term for a woman with literary or intellectual interests, combined with 'reputation'.
