sweatsuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɛtˌsuːt / (AmE), / ˈswɛtˌsuːt / (BrE)
A matching set of comfortable, casual clothing consisting of sweatpants and a sweatshirt, typically worn for exercise or leisure.
matching set of sweatpants and sweatshirt

Examples

He put on his favorite sweatsuit before heading to the gym.
After a long day at work, she changed into a comfortable sweatsuit.
Many people wore a sweatsuit during the early morning run.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tracksuit - a suit of clothing designed for athletics, typically jacket and pants
