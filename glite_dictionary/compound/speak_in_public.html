to speak in public

1. VERB

/ spiːk ɪn ˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / spiːk ɪn ˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
to give a speech or talk before an audience outside of private or informal settings
to address an audience in public

Examples

She felt confident enough to speak in public after taking a communication course.
Many people have a fear of speaking in public that they want to overcome.
He was invited to speak in public at the graduation ceremony.

Synset Examples

I get nervous every time I speak in public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public speaking - the act or art of making speeches to an audience
