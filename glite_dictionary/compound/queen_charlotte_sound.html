Queen Charlotte Sound

1. PROPN

/ kwiːn ˈʃɑrlət saʊnd / (AmE), / kwiːn ˈʃɑːlət saʊnd / (BrE)
an inlet of the Pacific Ocean off the coast of British Columbia, Canada
inlet off British Columbia, Canada

Examples

The coastline along Queen Charlotte Sound is known for its dramatic beauty and diverse wildlife.
Several ferries traverse Queen Charlotte Sound as they connect Vancouver Island to the mainland.
Storms can be fierce in Queen Charlotte Sound, making navigation challenging for ships.

Etymology

Named after Charlotte of Mecklenburg-Strelitz, wife of King George III.
