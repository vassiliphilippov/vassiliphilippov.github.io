North Star State

1. PROPN

/ nɔrθ stɑːr steɪt / (AmE), / nɔːθ stɑː steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the U.S. state of Minnesota, located in the Midwestern region
nickname for the state of Minnesota

Examples

Minnesota is often called the North Star State because of its location in the northern U.S.
The North Star State is known for its thousands of lakes and cold winters.
She moved from the North Star State to pursue a job in New York.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Minnesota - a state in the north-central United States

Etymology

Refers to the motto of Minnesota, 'L'Étoile du Nord' (French for 'The Star of the North').
