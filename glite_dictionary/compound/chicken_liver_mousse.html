chicken liver mousse

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkən ˈlɪvər mus / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn ˈlɪvə muːs / (BrE)
A smooth, creamy spread made from chicken liver, typically served as a starter or appetizer.
creamy chicken liver spread

Examples

She ordered chicken liver mousse as a starter at the French restaurant.
The chef garnished the chicken liver mousse with herbs and served it on toasted bread.
Guests praised the rich, silky texture of the chicken liver mousse at the party.

Synset Examples

They served chicken liver mousse as an appetizer.
He loves the rich taste of chicken liver pâté.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pâté - a paste made of finely chopped or pureed seasoned meat, often liver
