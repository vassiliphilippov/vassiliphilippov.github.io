golden ear

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ɪər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ɪə / (BrE)
an exceptional ability to perceive differences in sound quality or recognize musical tones with great accuracy
exceptional ability in discerning music or sound quality

Examples

The audio engineer's golden ear allowed her to notice subtle distortions in the recording.
Musicians with a golden ear can easily distinguish between similar chords.
Record producers often rely on someone with a golden ear to assure sound quality.

Synset Examples

He has a golden ear for music, always picking up melodies.
Her golden ear helped her identify rare instruments.
Producers value his golden ear in music production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfect pitch - the ability to identify or reproduce musical notes without a reference tone
