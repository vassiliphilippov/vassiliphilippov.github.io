Ugandan cuisine

1. NOUN

/ juˈɡændən kwɪˈzin / (AmE), / juˈɡændən kwɪˈziːn / (BrE)
the traditional dishes and cooking styles of Uganda, reflecting local ingredients and customs
traditional food of Uganda

Examples

Ugandan cuisine features dishes such as matoke, posho, and luwombo, which are staples throughout the country.
Tourists are often surprised by the delicious flavors found in Ugandan cuisine.
She wrote a blog post highlighting the unique ingredients used in Ugandan cuisine.

Synset Examples

The Ugandan cuisine is rich and diverse, full of flavors.
