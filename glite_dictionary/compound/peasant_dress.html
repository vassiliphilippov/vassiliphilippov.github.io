peasant dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛzənt drɛs / (AmE), / ˈpɛzənt drɛs / (BrE)
A women's dress in which the bodice resembles a peasant blouse, typically featuring loose sleeves, gathered fabric, and often embroidery or smocking.
dress with a peasant-style top

Examples

She wore a flowing peasant dress to the summer festival, complete with embroidered sleeves.
The boutique displayed several colorful peasant dresses for the upcoming spring collection.
A white peasant dress paired perfectly with her straw hat and sandals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peasant blouse - a loose-fitting blouse often with gathered neck and sleeves
