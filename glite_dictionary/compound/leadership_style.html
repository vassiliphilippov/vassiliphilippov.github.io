leadership style

1. NOUN

/ ˈliː.dɚ.ʃɪp staɪl / (AmE), / ˈliː.də.ʃɪp staɪl / (BrE)
the manner or approach a person uses to lead or direct others
manner of leading others

Examples

The company's new CEO introduced a leadership style that encouraged open communication and collaboration.
Her democratic leadership style made everyone on the team feel heard and respected.
Different situations may require leaders to adopt a different leadership style to be effective.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: management style - the way in which a manager directs and organizes people or resources
