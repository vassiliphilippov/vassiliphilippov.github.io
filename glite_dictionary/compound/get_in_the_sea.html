to get in the sea

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ɪn ðə si / (AmE), / ɡɛt ɪn ðə si / (BrE)
to enter the sea, especially for swimming or playing in the water
enter the sea (for swimming)

Examples

After applying sunscreen, she was ready to get in the sea and swim.
Some children were nervous to get in the sea because of the waves.
It's refreshing to get in the sea on a hot summer day.

Synset Examples

She decided to get in the sea despite the chilly weather.
They were eager to get in the sea after a long hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go swimming - to go into the water to swim
