package tour

1. NOUN

/ ˈpækɪdʒ tʊr / (AmE), / ˈpækɪdʒ tʊə / (BrE)
a holiday or vacation arranged by a travel agent in which transport, accommodation, and sometimes meals and sightseeing are all included in one price
holiday with transport and accommodation included

Examples

We booked a package tour to Italy that included flights, hotels, and meals.
Many travelers prefer a package tour because it's more convenient and often cheaper.
The package tour offered guided excursions to several famous landmarks.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: self-guided tour - a tour taken independently rather than as part of an organized group
