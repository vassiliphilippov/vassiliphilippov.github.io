film star

1. NOUN

/ fɪlm stɑr / (AmE), / fɪlm stɑː / (BrE)
A famous actor or actress who plays leading roles in movies.
famous movie actor or actress

Examples

The young actor dreamed of becoming a film star after his first appearance on screen.
Many film stars attended the international movie premiere last night.
She is considered a film star because of her numerous lead roles in Hollywood blockbusters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: movie star - a very famous actor or actress in films
