service book

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːvɪs bʊk / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs bʊk / (BrE)
a book that contains the texts and forms for religious ceremonies and services, particularly in Christian churches
book of religious service texts

Examples

The priest read from the service book during the ceremony.
She inherited an old family service book that had been used for generations.
Several different service books are required for special holy days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: missal - a book containing the texts used in the Catholic Mass throughout the year
  • Synonym: prayer book - a book containing prayers and sometimes other texts for Christian worship
