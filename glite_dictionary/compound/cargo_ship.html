cargo ship

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrɡoʊ ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈkɑːɡəʊ ʃɪp / (BrE)
a large ship designed to carry goods and materials from one port to another
a ship for transporting goods

Examples

The cargo ship docked early this morning, bringing hundreds of containers from overseas.
He worked for years as an engineer on a cargo ship traveling between Europe and Asia.
During the storm, the cargo ship lost several containers to the sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freighter - a ship or aircraft designed to carry freight
