safety-related

1. ADJ

/ ˈseɪfti rɪˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti rɪˌleɪtɪd / (BrE)
connected with or concerned with ensuring safety; intended to reduce risk or prevent accidents
connected with ensuring safety

Examples

The company invested heavily in safety-related equipment to protect its workers.
All safety-related concerns must be addressed before starting the project.
She specializes in safety-related regulations for chemical plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safe - protected from danger or risk
  • Antonym: dangerous - likely to cause harm or injury
