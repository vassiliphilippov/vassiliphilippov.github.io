furry tongue

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɜri tʌŋ / (AmE), / ˈfʌri tʌŋ / (BrE)
a harmless condition in which the papillae of the tongue become overgrown, often appearing dark and hairy, and sometimes caused by antibiotics
benign overgrowth of tongue papillae
medical

Examples

After taking antibiotics for several weeks, she noticed a furry tongue developing.
Doctors reassured him that a furry tongue is usually harmless and temporary.
Poor oral hygiene can also contribute to the appearance of a furry tongue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black hairy tongue - benign condition causing a dark, hair-like appearance on the tongue
