drooping brome

1. NOUN

/ ˈdruːpɪŋ ˈbroʊm / (AmE), / ˈdruːpɪŋ ˈbrəʊm / (BrE)
an annual or winter annual grass of the Mediterranean, with softly hairy leaves, belonging to the genus Bromus
a Mediterranean annual grass, Bromus species

Examples

The field was covered in drooping brome, its hairy leaves swaying gently in the breeze.
Ecologists identified drooping brome among various grasses growing in the Mediterranean region.
Farmers try to control drooping brome because it can compete with cereal crops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brome - any grass of the genus Bromus
