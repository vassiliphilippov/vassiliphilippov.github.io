solar telescope

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊlər ˈtɛləˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə ˈtelɪˌskəʊp / (BrE)
a telescope specially designed for making observations of the sun
telescope for observing the sun

Examples

The observatory installed a new solar telescope to study sunspots and solar flares.
Special filters are required to safely use a solar telescope when viewing the sun.
His research focuses on the data collected with a solar telescope during solar eclipses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telescope - an optical instrument for making distant objects appear closer and larger
