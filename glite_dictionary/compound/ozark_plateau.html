Ozark Plateau

1. PROPN

/ ˈoʊzɑrk pləˈtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈəʊzɑːk plæˈtəʊ / (BrE)
a highland region of low mountains and hills in northwestern Arkansas, southeastern Missouri, and northeastern Oklahoma
upland region in central southern United States

Examples

The Ozark Plateau is known for its scenic beauty and outdoor recreational activities.
Many rivers have their source in the Ozark Plateau region.
The Ozark Plateau has a unique ecosystem compared to the surrounding plains.
