Looney Tunes

1. PROPN

/ ˈluːni tuːnz / (AmE), / ˈluːni tjuːnz / (BrE)
a series of animated short films produced by Warner Bros. featuring iconic cartoon characters such as Bugs Bunny, Daffy Duck, and Porky Pig
Warner Bros. animated cartoon series

Examples

Many of my favorite childhood memories involve watching Looney Tunes on Saturday mornings.
The humor in Looney Tunes appeals to both children and adults alike.
She decorated her notebook with stickers of Looney Tunes characters.

Synset Examples

He always quotes Looney Tunes characters in conversation.
The animation style of Looney-Tunes is iconic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Merrie Melodies - another Warner Bros. animated cartoon series, companion to Looney Tunes

Etymology

Created as a play on 'Silly Symphonies,' a similar Disney cartoon series; 'looney' meaning crazy, 'tunes' meaning songs.
