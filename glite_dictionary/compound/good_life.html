good life

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd laɪf / (AmE), / ɡʊd laɪf / (BrE)
a life that is comfortable, happy, and successful, often with material well-being and personal fulfillment
life of comfort, happiness, and success

Examples

Many people dream of retiring early and enjoying the good life.
She worked hard to provide her children with the good life she never had.
After years of saving, they finally felt they were living the good life.

Synset Examples

They live a good life by the beach.
He believes hard work leads to a good life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luxury - great comfort, especially as provided by expensive and beautiful things
  • Antonym: hardship - severe suffering or deprivation
