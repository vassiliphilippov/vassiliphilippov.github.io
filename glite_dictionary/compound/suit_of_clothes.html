suit of clothes

1. NOUN

/ suːt əv kloʊðz / (AmE), / suːt əv kləʊðz / (BrE)
a set of outer garments, typically including a matching jacket and trousers or skirt, made from the same fabric and color
matching set of outer garments

Examples

He wore his finest suit of clothes to the job interview.
She bought a new suit of clothes for the occasion.
Every gentleman should own at least one suit of clothes for formal events.

Synset Examples

they buried him in his best suit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suit - a matching jacket and trousers or skirt; a set of outer clothes made of the same fabric
