Egyptian cotton

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈdʒɪpʃən ˈkɑtən / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒɪpʃən ˈkɒtən / (BrE)
a high-quality, long-staple cotton grown in Egypt, known for its softness, strength, and durability
type of fine, long-staple cotton from Egypt

Examples

The hotel prided itself on sheets made from Egyptian cotton for extra comfort.
She purchased towels crafted from Egyptian cotton because of their luxurious feel.
Many consider Egyptian cotton to be the benchmark for premium bedding.
