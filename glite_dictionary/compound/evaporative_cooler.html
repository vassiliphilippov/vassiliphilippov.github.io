evaporative cooler

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌvæpəˌreɪtɪv ˈkuːlɚ / (AmE), / ɪˌvapəˈreɪtɪv ˈkuːlə / (BrE)
a device that cools air through the evaporation of water, commonly used as an energy-efficient alternative to conventional air conditioning in dry climates
air cooler using water evaporation

Examples

Many homes in arid regions use an evaporative cooler instead of a traditional air conditioner.
The evaporative cooler provided relief during the hot summer days.
Maintenance of your evaporative cooler is essential for it to operate efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swamp cooler - another term for an evaporative cooler, especially in North America
  • Synonym: air conditioner - a system or machine that controls the humidity, ventilation, and temperature in a building or vehicle
