white-tailed sea eagle

1. NOUN

/ waɪt teɪld si ˈiːɡəl / (AmE), / waɪt teɪld siː ˈiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
a large greyish-brown eagle with a short wedge-shaped white tail, found in Europe and Greenland
large eagle with white tail

Examples

We observed a white-tailed sea eagle soaring above the cliffs near the fjord.
Conservation efforts have helped the white-tailed sea eagle return to parts of Scotland.
The white-tailed sea eagle is known for its impressive wingspan and powerful build.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea eagle - large eagle species that live near coasts or large bodies of water
