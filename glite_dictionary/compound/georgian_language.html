Georgian language

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɔrdʒən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒɪən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the official language of Georgia, a southern Caucasian language spoken by about 3 million people, known for its unique script and long literary tradition
the official language of Georgia

Examples

She is fluent in both Russian and Georgian language.
The Georgian language has its own unique alphabet and script.
Many ancient manuscripts are written in the Georgian language.

Etymology

From 'Georgia', the name of the country, ultimately from Persian 'Gurj', and 'language'.
