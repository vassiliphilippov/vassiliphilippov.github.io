walk of life

1. NOUN

/ wɑk əv laɪf / (AmE), / wɔːk əv laɪf / (BrE)
a person's occupation, social background, or position within society
occupation or social position

Examples

People from every walk of life attended the event to show their support.
You will meet individuals from all walks of life during your travels.
Success comes to those from any walk of life, not just the privileged few.

Synset Examples

it happens in all walks of life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: profession - a paid occupation, especially one that involves prolonged training
  • Synonym: background - the circumstances or past experience of a person
