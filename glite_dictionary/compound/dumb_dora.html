dumb Dora

1. NOUN

/ dʌm ˈdɔɹə / (AmE), / dʌm ˈdɔːrə / (BrE)
An unintelligent or naive woman, especially one who is easily fooled or acts silly; a foolish person.
foolish or naive person (dated slang)
slang american archaic

Examples

She called me a dumb Dora for missing the deadline.
Don't be such a dumb Dora—think before you speak!
In old movies, the comic relief was often played by a dumb Dora character.

Synset Examples

She called me a dumb Dora for missing the deadline.
He often jokes that I'm a dumb Dora when I forget things.
Don't be a dumb Dora; check your work twice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
  • Synonym: simpleton - someone who is easily tricked or lacks intelligence
  • Antonym: smart aleck - a person who is irritatingly clever or smug

Etymology

American slang, popularized by 1920s vaudeville and comic strips; 'Dumb Dora' was the title of a well-known comic strip (1924–1936).
