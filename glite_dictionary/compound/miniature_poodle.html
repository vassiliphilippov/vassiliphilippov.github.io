miniature poodle

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪniəˌtʃʊr ˈpuːdəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪətʃə ˈpuːdl̩ / (BrE)
a breed of small poodle dog, typically characterized by a curly coat and an alert, intelligent nature
a small breed of poodle dog

Examples

The miniature poodle is known for its intelligence and friendly temperament.
Sarah adopted a black miniature poodle from the animal shelter last week.
At the dog show, a white miniature poodle won first prize in obedience.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: toy poodle - a very small breed of poodle
  • Degree Higher: standard poodle - the largest breed of poodle
