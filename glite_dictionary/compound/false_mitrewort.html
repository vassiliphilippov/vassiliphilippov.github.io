false mitrewort

1. NOUN

/ fɔls ˈmɪtroʊˌwɜrt / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈmɪtrəʊˌwɜːt / (BrE)
A plant with tiny white flowers hanging in loose clusters on leafy stems, found in moist woods from Alaska to central California and east to Montana.
plant with small white flowers in loose clusters

Examples

We found false mitrewort growing along the shaded trail in the forest.
The delicate flowers of the false mitrewort brighten up the damp woodland floor each spring.
Botanists are studying the habitat range of false mitrewort in North America.
