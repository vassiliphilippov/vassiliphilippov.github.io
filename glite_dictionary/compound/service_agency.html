service agency

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝvɪs ˌeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs ˌeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
a business that provides its specialized services or facilities to clients for a fee, often achieving cost efficiency through scale
business providing services for a fee

Examples

The company decided to outsource payroll tasks to a service agency to reduce costs.
Many small firms use a service agency for their IT needs rather than hiring in-house staff.
After comparing several options, they chose a service agency that specialized in event planning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agency - a business or organization providing a particular service
