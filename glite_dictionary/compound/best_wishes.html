best wishes

1. NOUN

/ bɛst ˈwɪʃɪz / (AmE), / bɛst ˈwɪʃɪz / (BrE)
An expression conveying hopes for someone's future happiness, welfare, or success.
expression of goodwill or good luck

Examples

She sent her best wishes to all her colleagues before leaving the company.
Please give my best wishes to your parents when you see them.
We extend our best wishes for your continued happiness and success.

Synset Examples

We sent our best wishes for a speedy recovery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: congratulations - expression of praise or good wishes on an achievement
  • Antonym: condolences - expression of sympathy, especially on someone's death
