terry blanket

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛri ˈblæŋkət / (AmE), / ˈtɛri ˈblaŋkɪt / (BrE)
A blanket made from terry cloth, featuring a soft, absorbent texture similar to that of a bath towel.
blanket made of terry cloth

Examples

On winter evenings, I like to curl up with a terry blanket and a good book.
She brought a colorful terry blanket for the picnic in the park.
The hotel room was stocked with a thick terry blanket for extra comfort.

Synset Examples

He bought a terry blanket for the chilly nights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terry cloth - a cotton fabric with loop pile, used especially for towels
  • Synonym: blanket - a large piece of wool or similar material used as a covering for warmth

Etymology

From 'terry', a type of woven cotton fabric with loops, from French 'tire', and 'blanket'.
