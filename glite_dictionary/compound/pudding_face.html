pudding face

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊdɪŋ feɪs / (AmE), / ˈpʊdɪŋ feɪs / (BrE)
a large, round, and fat human face that resembles the shape or softness of a pudding
a large, fat, rounded face

Examples

His pudding face was always red and cheerful, reflecting his good humor.
She remembered her uncle's pudding face and how it seemed to light up the room.
The portrait captured the man's pudding face with surprising kindness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moon face - a face that is round and full in appearance
