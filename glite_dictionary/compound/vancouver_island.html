Vancouver Island

1. PROPN

/ vænˈkuːvər ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / vænˈkuːvə ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
a large island off the southwestern coast of British Columbia, Canada, and the largest island on the west coast of North America
island off the west coast of Canada

Examples

Many tourists visit Vancouver Island each year to enjoy its natural beauty and mild climate.
The ferry from the mainland to Vancouver Island offers stunning views of the coastline.
Victoria, the capital of British Columbia, is located on Vancouver Island.

Etymology

Named after British explorer George Vancouver (1757–1798).
