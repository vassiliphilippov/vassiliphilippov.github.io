commercial loan

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɜrʃəl loʊn / (AmE), / kəˈmɜːʃəl ləʊn / (BrE)
a loan provided by a bank or financial institution to a business for commercial purposes, such as funding operations or expansion
loan for business purposes

Examples

The company secured a commercial loan to finance the construction of its new factory.
Many small businesses rely on a commercial loan to purchase equipment or expand their operations.
After reviewing the financial statements, the bank approved a commercial loan for the growing startup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: business loan - a loan given to a business, often as a synonym for commercial loan
