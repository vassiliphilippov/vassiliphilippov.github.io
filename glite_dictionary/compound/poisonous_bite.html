poisonous bite

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔɪzənəs baɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪzənəs baɪt / (BrE)
a bite that delivers toxins which can harm or kill
bite delivering harmful toxins

Examples

The coral snake's poisonous bite can be fatal to small animals.
Hikers are warned about the risk of poisonous bite from certain spiders in the area.
The researcher studied the effects of a poisonous bite on different types of tissue.

Synset Examples

The snake is known for its poisonous bite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: venomous bite - a bite that injects venom (harmful toxins) into another organism
  • Antonym: non-poisonous bite - a bite that does not deliver harmful toxins
