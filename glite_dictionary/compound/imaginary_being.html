imaginary being

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmædʒɪˌnɛri ˈbiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈmadʒɪnəri ˈbiːɪŋ / (BrE)
a creature or entity that exists only in the imagination, such as those found in myths, legends, or fiction
creature existing only in the imagination

Examples

Many cultures have stories about an imaginary being that protects the innocent.
The dragon is an imaginary being found in legends across the world.
As a child, she often talked to an imaginary being only she could see.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mythical creature - a creature from myths or folklore, not real
