straight face

1. NOUN

/ streɪt feɪs / (AmE), / streɪt feɪs / (BrE)
a facial expression showing neither amusement nor emotion, especially when trying not to laugh
serious facial expression (shows no amusement)

Examples

She managed to tell the outrageous story with a straight face.
It's hard to keep a straight face when someone makes a silly mistake in front of you.
He delivered the joke with a perfectly straight face, which made it even funnier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poker face - an expressionless face that gives no indication of thoughts or feelings
