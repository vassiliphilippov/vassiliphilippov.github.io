air sandwich

1. NOUN

/ ɛr ˈsændwɪtʃ / (AmE), / eə ˈsænwɪtʃ / (BrE)
A sandwich made with little or no filling, often used humorously to refer to just bread or a sandwich with almost nothing inside.
sandwich with little or no filling
informal

Examples

I was so broke that all I could afford for lunch was an air sandwich.
She laughed as she served her friend an air sandwich on an empty plate.
After checking the fridge, he realized dinner would be an air sandwich again.

Synset Examples

An air sandwich is just bread and no filling.
He made an air sandwich for lunch with just lettuce.
She joked about her diet consisting of air sandwiches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sandwich - two or more slices of bread with filling in between
