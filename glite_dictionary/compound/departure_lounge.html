departure lounge

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑrtʃɚ laʊndʒ / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtʃə laʊndʒ / (BrE)
a seating area in an airport where passengers wait before boarding their flights
airport waiting area for departing passengers

Examples

We spent nearly two hours in the departure lounge before our flight was called.
The departure lounge was crowded with travelers waiting to board their planes.
After checking in, they made their way to the departure lounge to relax before departure.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: arrival lounge - area where arriving passengers wait at an airport
