muscle Mary

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsəl ˈmɛri / (AmE), / ˈmʌsl ˈmeəri / (BrE)
a flamboyant gay man who is especially muscular, or a bodybuilder who prominently displays large muscles
muscular flamboyant gay man or bodybuilder
slang

Examples

The club was full of muscle Marys flexing and showing off their physiques.
He fondly refers to his gym partner as a muscle Mary because of his dedication to bodybuilding.
Some people find the confidence of a muscle Mary both inspiring and intimidating.

Synset Examples

He joked that his friend is a total muscle Mary.
She admires the muscle Marys who dominate the bodybuilding scene.

Etymology

From 'muscle' (referring to physique) and 'Mary,' a stereotypical camp term used in British slang for gay men.
