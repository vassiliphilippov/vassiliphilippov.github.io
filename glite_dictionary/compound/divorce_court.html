divorce court

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈvɔrs kɔrt / (AmE), / dɪˈvɔːs kɔːt / (BrE)
A court with the authority to dissolve marriages and rule on issues related to divorce.
court handling divorce cases
legal

Examples

The couple appeared before the divorce court to finalize their separation.
She works as a clerk in the divorce court downtown.
Many difficult decisions are made in divorce court regarding child custody and property.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: family court - court dealing with family-related cases
