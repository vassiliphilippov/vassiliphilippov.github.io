mental hospital

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛntəl ˌhɑːspɪtl̩ / (AmE), / ˈmɛntəl ˌhɒspɪtl̩ / (BrE)
a hospital specializing in the treatment of people with mental illnesses or psychiatric disorders
hospital for mental illness treatment

Examples

After his breakdown, he was admitted to a mental hospital for several weeks.
The city is planning to renovate the old mental hospital into apartments.
She worked as a nurse at a mental hospital early in her career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychiatric hospital - a hospital specializing in mental health care
  • Antonym: general hospital - a hospital for the treatment of all kinds of illnesses
