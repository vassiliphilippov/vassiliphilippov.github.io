California whipsnake

1. NOUN

/ kəˌlɪfɔrniə ˈwɪpsneɪk / (AmE), / kəˌlɪfɔːniə ˈwɪpsneɪk / (BrE)
a slender, fast-moving snake (Masticophis lateralis) native to scrublands and rocky hillsides of California and surrounding areas
a snake species native to California

Examples

The California whipsnake is known for its speed and agility among the rocks and shrubs.
Biologists are monitoring the habitat of the California whipsnake to ensure its continued survival.
We spotted a young California whipsnake basking in the morning sun during our hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whipsnake - any slender, fast-moving snake of the genus Masticophis or related groups
