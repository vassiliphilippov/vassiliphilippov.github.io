table service

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪ.bəl ˌsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / ˈteɪ.bəl ˌsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
a complete set of dishes, utensils, and other articles used at the dining table
complete set of tableware for dining

Examples

They received a beautiful table service as a wedding gift.
The hotel offers silver table service in the dining room every evening.
Our grandmother’s china table service is used only on special occasions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tableware - articles such as dishes, cutlery, and glasses used for serving and eating food at a table
