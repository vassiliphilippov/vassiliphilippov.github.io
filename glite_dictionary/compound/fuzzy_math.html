fuzzy math

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌzi mæθ / (AmE), / ˈfʌzi mɑːθ / (BrE)
mathematical reasoning or calculations that are vague, imprecise, or misleading
imprecise or misleading mathematics
informal

Examples

The politician used fuzzy math to make the budget seem balanced.
Critics accused the company of relying on fuzzy math in its financial statements.
Whenever the results don't add up, suspect fuzzy math might be at play.

Synset Examples

His explanation was just fuzzy math, nothing concrete.
The report's conclusions were based on fuzzy math.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad math - incorrect or erroneous mathematical reasoning
