Yoruba language

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɔːrəˌbuː læŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈjɒrʊbɑː ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language of the Yoruba people, belonging to the Kwa branch of the Niger-Congo family, spoken chiefly in southwestern Nigeria and neighboring areas
the language of the Yoruba people of Nigeria

Examples

She is fluent in Yoruba language as well as English.
Courses in the Yoruba language are now offered at several universities.
The film was dubbed into Yoruba language for local audiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yoruba - a member of an African ethnic group from southwestern Nigeria

Etymology

From Yoruba 'Yorùbá'.
