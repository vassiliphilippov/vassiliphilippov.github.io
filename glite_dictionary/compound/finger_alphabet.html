finger alphabet

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪŋɡər ˈæl.fə.bɛt / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə ˈæl.fə.bɛt / (BrE)
a system in which the letters of the alphabet are represented by specific finger positions, used especially by and with people who are deaf
hand signs representing letters

Examples

She learned the finger alphabet to communicate with her deaf friends.
The teacher demonstrated the finger alphabet in front of the class.
Many people use the finger alphabet as an introduction to sign language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sign language - a language which uses manual communication and body language to convey meaning, especially among the deaf
