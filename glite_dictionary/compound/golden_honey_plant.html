golden honey plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈhʌni plænt / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈhʌni plɑːnt / (BrE)
A perennial herb native to the eastern United States, known for its showy yellow flowers.
perennial herb with yellow flowers

Examples

The golden honey plant adds vibrant color to gardens in the eastern United States.
Botanists admire the golden honey plant for its bright yellow blossoms and hardiness.
She chose a golden honey plant to attract bees and butterflies to her yard.
