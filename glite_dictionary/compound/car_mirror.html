car mirror

1. NOUN

/ kɑr ˈmɪrɚ / (AmE), / kɑː ˈmɪrə / (BrE)
a mirror in a car that allows the driver to see areas behind or to the side of the vehicle
mirror in a car for driver visibility

Examples

He checked the car mirror before changing lanes.
Make sure your car mirror is clean for a clear view while driving.
The accident broke the left car mirror, so it needs to be replaced.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rearview mirror - a mirror in a car for the driver to see what's behind
  • Synonym: side mirror - an exterior mirror on the side of a car for the driver to see what's behind or beside
