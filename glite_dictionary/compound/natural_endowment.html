natural endowment

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃərəl ɪnˈdaʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈnatʃrəl ɪnˈdaʊm(ə)nt / (BrE)
an innate ability, talent, or quality that a person or thing possesses from birth
inborn ability or quality

Examples

She has a natural endowment for music that impressed her teachers from an early age.
Athletic prowess is often seen as a natural endowment rather than something entirely learned.
His natural endowment of intelligence made complex problems seem easy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talent - a natural aptitude or skill
  • Synonym: gift - a natural ability or aptitude
