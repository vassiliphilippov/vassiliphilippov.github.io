canyonside

1. NOUN

/ ˈkænjən.saɪd / (AmE), / ˈkænjən.saɪd / (BrE)
the steeply sloping side of a canyon
side slope of a canyon

Examples

The hikers carefully followed the narrow trail along the canyonside.
From the base, the canyonside appeared almost vertical and covered in rugged vegetation.
A herd of mountain goats climbed the steep canyonside in search of food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cliff - a steep rock face, especially at the edge of the sea
