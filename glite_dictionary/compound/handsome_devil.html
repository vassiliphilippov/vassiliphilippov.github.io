handsome devil

1. NOUN

/ ˈhænsəm ˈdɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈhansəm ˈdɛvəl / (BrE)
an informal term for a man who is especially attractive or charming, often with a playful or teasing sense
charming or attractive man (informal)
informal

Examples

Everyone at the office adores that handsome devil from accounting.
You can't trust a handsome devil like Ben with your secrets!
When he smiled, he looked like a real handsome devil.

Synset Examples

He's quite the handsome devil, isn't he?
That handsome devil won everyone's heart at the party.
She called him a handsome devil as he walked by.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartthrob - a man, typically a celebrity, who is very attractive
  • Synonym: lady-killer - a man who is very successful at attracting women
