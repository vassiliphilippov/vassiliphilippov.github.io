mod con

1. NOUN

/ mɑd kɑn / (AmE), / mɒd kɒn / (BrE)
A feature or appliance that provides comfort or convenience in a modern home, such as central heating or running water.
modern household convenience
british

Examples

The apartment comes with all the latest mod cons, including a dishwasher and central heating.
While the cottage looks traditional from the outside, it's equipped with every mod con inside.
They listed the house for sale, emphasizing its spacious rooms and abundance of mod cons.

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'modern convenience.'
