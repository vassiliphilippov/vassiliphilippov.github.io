chemistry department

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛmɪstri dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪstri dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the division of a school, college, or university that focuses on teaching and research in chemistry.
academic unit for chemistry teaching and research

Examples

The university's chemistry department recently received funding for a new laboratory.
She decided to major in biochemistry after visiting the chemistry department open house.
The chemistry department is known for its groundbreaking research on renewable energy materials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: department - division of an organization such as a university
