good afternoon

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˌæftɚˈnun / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˌɑːftəˈnuːn / (BrE)
A polite expression used to greet someone or wish them well during the afternoon.
polite daytime greeting or farewell

Examples

She smiled and said, 'good afternoon,' as she entered the meeting room.
'Good afternoon,' the receptionist greeted the visitors politely.
He waved and wished his neighbor a 'good afternoon' as he walked past.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good morning - a greeting or farewell used in the morning
  • Synonym: good evening - a greeting or farewell used in the evening
  • Synonym: good night - a farewell used at night or before going to bed
