length of service

1. NOUN

/ lɛŋkθ əv ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / lɛŋθ əv ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
the period of time that someone has worked for a particular organization or in a particular position
duration of employment or service

Examples

The company rewarded him for his length of service with a special bonus.
Employees with a longer length of service may be eligible for additional vacation days.
Her impressive length of service makes her one of the most experienced members of the team.

Synset Examples

her longevity as a star
had unusual longevity in the company

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tenure - the period during which someone holds a job or position
  • Synonym: seniority - length of time someone has held a position or worked for an organization
