naked ape

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪkɪd eɪp / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪd eɪp / (BrE)
a term for humans, especially in reference to their evolutionary origins or primal behavior, highlighting their animal nature
term for humans from an evolutionary perspective

Examples

The scientist described humanity as the naked ape to emphasize our evolutionary background.
Many philosophers have considered what sets the naked ape apart from other animals.
The book argues that the naked ape is driven by many of the same instincts as its ancestors.

Synset Examples

The documentary explored the concept of the naked ape.
His behavior was so primal, like a naked ape.
She joked that we all are just naked apes at heart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: human - a member of the species Homo sapiens

Etymology

Popularised by the title of Desmond Morris's 1967 book "The Naked Ape"; "ape" from Old English "apa".
