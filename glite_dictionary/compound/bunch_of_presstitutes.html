bunch of presstitutes

1. NOUN

/ bʌntʃ əv ˈprɛstɪˌtuts / (AmE), / bʌntʃ əv ˈprɛstɪˌtjuːts / (BrE)
a derogatory term for a group of journalists or media who are perceived as biased or corrupt and act in service of particular interests rather than objective reporting
derogatory term for biased media
offensive slang

Examples

Many people online dismissed the article, calling its authors a bunch of presstitutes.
He accused the broadcasting company of being just a bunch of presstitutes who cannot be trusted.
Referring to the reporters as a bunch of presstitutes sparked outrage in the press corps.

Synset Examples

He criticized the media as a bunch of presstitutes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: presstitute - a derogatory term for a journalist who is biased or corrupt

Etymology

Blend of 'press' (news media) and 'prostitute', suggesting journalists who compromise principles for profit or influence.
