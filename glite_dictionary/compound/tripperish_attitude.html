tripperish attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɪpərɪʃ ˈætɪˌtjud / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpərɪʃ ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
a light-hearted or carefree way of behaving or looking at things
light-hearted or carefree demeanor

Examples

Her tripperish attitude made everyone feel relaxed during the stressful meeting.
With a tripperish attitude, he brushed off the minor setbacks and kept smiling.
Their tripperish attitude was infectious, turning work into a pleasant experience.

Synset Examples

Her tripperish attitude made everyone feel relaxed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carefree - having no worries or responsibilities
  • Antonym: serious attitude - a demeanor showing earnestness or gravity
