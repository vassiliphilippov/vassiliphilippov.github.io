napkin ring

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæp.kɪn rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnæp.kɪn rɪŋ / (BrE)
a small circular band used to hold and identify an individual's napkin at a table setting
circular band for holding a napkin

Examples

Each guest had a napkin ring engraved with their initials at the dinner table.
She collected antique napkin rings from various countries.
The silver napkin ring added a touch of elegance to the place setting.
