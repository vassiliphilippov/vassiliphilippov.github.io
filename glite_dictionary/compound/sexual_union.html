sexual union

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl ˈjunjən / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl ˈjuːniən / (BrE)
the act of a male and female coming together for the purpose of reproduction
copulation or mating for reproduction

Examples

The documentary showed the sexual union of rare birds during the breeding season.
Biologists observed the sexual union as part of their study on animal behavior.
In many species, the sexual union is carefully timed to maximize reproductive success.

Synset Examples

the casual couplings of adolescents
the mating of some species occurs only in the spring

Related Terms

  • Synonym: copulation - the act of sexual procreation between a man and a woman; the union of the male and the female
  • Synonym: mating - the process by which animals come together for reproduction
