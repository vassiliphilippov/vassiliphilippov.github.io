virulent strain

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪrjələnt streɪn / (AmE), / ˈvɪrjʊlənt streɪn / (BrE)
a type or variant of a microorganism, especially a virus or bacterium, that is highly infectious, destructive, or severe in its effects
highly infectious or harmful microorganism type

Examples

Scientists warned about the emergence of a virulent strain that could lead to a new outbreak.
The hospital struggled to control the virulent strain and prevent it from spreading to other patients.
A virulent strain of influenza caused severe symptoms in many communities this year.

Synset Examples

The virulent strain of the virus spread rapidly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strain - a genetic variant or subtype of a microorganism
