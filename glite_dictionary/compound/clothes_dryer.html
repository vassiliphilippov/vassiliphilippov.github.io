clothes dryer

1. NOUN

/ kloʊðz ˈdraɪ.ər / (AmE), / kləʊðz ˈdraɪ.ə / (BrE)
A machine designed to remove moisture from washed clothes by circulating heated air.
appliance for drying clothes

Examples

After doing laundry, I always put my wet shirts in the clothes dryer.
The clothes dryer broke down, so we had to hang our clothes outside.
Buying a new clothes dryer made laundry day much more convenient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dryer - a machine or device for drying something
  • Antonym: washing machine - a machine for washing clothes
