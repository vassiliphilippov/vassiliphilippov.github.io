doubledome architecture

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌbəl doʊm ˌɑrkɪˈtɛktʃər / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl dəʊm ˌɑːkɪˈtɛktʃə / (BrE)

architecture featuring two domes
