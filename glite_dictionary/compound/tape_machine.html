tape machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪp məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈteɪp məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine for recording or playing sound using magnetic tape as the storage medium
device for recording/playback with magnetic tape

Examples

The old studio still has a working tape machine used for analog recordings.
He transferred the original recordings from the tape machine to his computer for editing.
Many classic albums were produced with the help of a tape machine before digital equipment became standard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tape recorder - a device for recording sound on magnetic tape
  • Degree Lower: cassette player - a device for playing audio cassettes
