clingy behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɪŋi bəˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / ˈklɪŋi bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
the tendency to be excessively dependent on or emotionally attached to someone, often seeking constant attention or reassurance
excessive emotional dependence on someone

Examples

Her clingy behavior made her partner feel overwhelmed and smothered at times.
He realized his clingy behavior was pushing his friends away instead of bringing them closer.
Therapists often help clients recognize the roots of their clingy behavior in relationships.

Synset Examples

Her clingy behavior annoyed her friends at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clingy - having a strong need for affection or close association
  • Antonym: independent - self-reliant and not emotionally dependent on others
