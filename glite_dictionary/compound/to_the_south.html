to the south

1. ADV

/ tə ðə saʊθ / (AmE), / tə ðə saʊθ / (BrE)
In the direction of the south; toward the south.
toward the south

Examples

After crossing the river, the hikers continued to the south until they reached the campsite.
Cold winds often blow to the south during the winter months.
Several species of birds migrate to the south in search of warmer climates.

Synset Examples

we moved south

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to the north - toward the north
  • Synonym: southward - toward the south
