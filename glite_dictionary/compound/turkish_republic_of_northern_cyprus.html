Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɜrkɪʃ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈnɔrðərn ˈsaɪprəs / (AmE), / ˈtɜːkɪʃ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈnɔːðən ˈsaɪprəs / (BrE)
A self-declared state on the northern part of the island of Cyprus, recognized only by Turkey.
self-declared state in Northern Cyprus

Examples

The Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus declared independence in 1983 but is recognized only by Turkey.
Talks concerning reunification often include representatives from the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus.
The political status of the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus remains a subject of international dispute.
