Sandwich Islands

1. PROPN

/ ˈsændwɪtʃ ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / ˈsænwɪtʃ ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
the former name for the Hawaiian Islands, a group of volcanic and coral islands in the central Pacific Ocean
former name for the Hawaiian Islands

Examples

Captain Cook was the first European to visit the Sandwich Islands in 1778.
The Sandwich Islands are now known as Hawaii.
Many ships stopped at the Sandwich Islands for supplies during long Pacific voyages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hawaiian Islands - current name: a group of islands in the central Pacific Ocean

Etymology

Named by Captain James Cook in 1778 in honor of the Earl of Sandwich.
