nitrous oxide injection system

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪtrəs ɑkˌsaɪd ɪnˈdʒɛkʃən ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtrəs ɒkˌsaɪd ɪnˈdʒɛkʃən ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A mechanical system used to inject nitrous oxide into an engine to increase its power output.
mechanical system injecting nitrous oxide for engine power

Examples

The car was modified with a nitrous oxide injection system to gain a competitive edge in the race.
Some street racers use a nitrous oxide injection system for short bursts of extra acceleration.
Installing a nitrous oxide injection system requires careful tuning to prevent engine damage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: NOS - abbreviation for nitrous oxide system
