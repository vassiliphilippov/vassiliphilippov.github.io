language teaching

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ ˈtiːtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ ˈtiːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice or profession of teaching people to speak and understand a foreign language
the act or profession of teaching a foreign language

Examples

She has devoted her career to language teaching in various countries.
Innovative methods in language teaching can help students learn faster.
Many universities offer degrees specifically focused on language teaching.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: language learning - the process of acquiring proficiency in a foreign language
  • Synonym: language education - the field concerned with teaching and learning languages
