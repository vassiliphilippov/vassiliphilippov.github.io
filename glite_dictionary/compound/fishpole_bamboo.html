fishpole bamboo

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃpoʊl bæmˈbu / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃpəʊl bamˈbuː / (BrE)
a small species of bamboo from southeastern China, known for its slender culms that are very flexible when young
a slender, flexible bamboo species from China

Examples

The garden featured a grove of fishpole bamboo swaying in the wind.
Young fishpole bamboo stalks are so flexible that they almost bend to the ground.
The craftsman used fishpole bamboo for making traditional fishing rods.
