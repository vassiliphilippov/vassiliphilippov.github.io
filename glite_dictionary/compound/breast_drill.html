breast drill

1. NOUN

/ brɛst drɪl / (AmE), / brɛst drɪl / (BrE)
a portable hand-operated drill with a plate that is pressed against the chest to apply force while drilling
hand drill with chest plate

Examples

The carpenter used a breast drill to make clean holes in the wooden beams.
Before electric drills, the breast drill was a common tool on construction sites.
He braced the breast drill against his chest to drive the bit through the metal plate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hand drill - a drill operated manually by hand
