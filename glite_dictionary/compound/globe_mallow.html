globe mallow

1. NOUN

/ ɡloʊb ˈmæloʊ / (AmE), / ɡləʊb ˈmæləʊ / (BrE)
A coarse herb or subshrub of arid regions in North and South America, with pink or scarlet flowers and round fruits, belonging to the genus Sphaeralcea.
arid-region herb with pink or scarlet flowers

Examples

The garden featured several globe mallow plants, their bright flowers attracting butterflies.
Drought-resistant and hardy, globe mallow thrives in dry, sandy soils across the Southwest.
Scientists studied the medicinal properties of globe mallow used in traditional remedies.
