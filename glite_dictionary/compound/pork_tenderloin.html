pork tenderloin

1. NOUN

/ pɔrk ˈtɛn.dɚˌlɔɪn / (AmE), / pɔːk ˈten.də.lɔɪn / (BrE)
a long, narrow, and tender cut of pork taken from the muscle along the central spine of a pig, prized for its leanness and tenderness
tender cut of pork from the loin

Examples

She marinated the pork tenderloin overnight to enhance its flavor.
The chef carefully sliced the roasted pork tenderloin for the guests.
You can grill, roast, or pan-sear pork tenderloin for a delicious dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tenderloin - the tenderest part of a loin of meat, especially beef or pork
