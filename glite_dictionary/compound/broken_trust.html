broken trust

1. NOUN

/ ˈbroʊkən trʌst / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkən trʌst / (BrE)
the loss of confidence or faith in someone due to their actions or behavior
loss of confidence in someone

Examples

After years of loyalty, the accusation left him with a sense of broken trust.
Broken trust is hard to rebuild once it has been damaged in a relationship.
The contract dispute resulted in broken trust between the company and its clients.

Synset Examples

Her trust was broken after what he did.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: betrayal - the act of being disloyal or breaking someone's trust
