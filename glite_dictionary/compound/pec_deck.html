pec deck

1. NOUN

/ pɛk dɛk / (AmE), / pɛk dɛk / (BrE)
an exercise machine with adjustable arms used to strengthen and develop the chest muscles, especially the pectorals
exercise machine for chest muscles

Examples

He spent ten minutes on the pec deck before moving to free weights.
The personal trainer demonstrated how to position your arms correctly on the pec deck.
After injuring his shoulder, he found the pec deck easier than doing push-ups.

Synset Examples

The gym was packed, so I waited for the pec deck.
Using the pec deck really targets the chest effectively.
He prefers using the pec deck for his chest workouts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butterfly machine - another term for a chest exercise machine with swinging arms

Etymology

Short for 'pectoral deck', referencing the pectoral (chest) muscles and deck (platform or unit).
