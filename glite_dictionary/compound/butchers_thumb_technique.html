butcher's thumb technique

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊtʃɚz θʌm tɛkˈnik / (AmE), / ˈbʊtʃəz θʌm tɛkˈniːk / (BrE)
A particular skill used by butchers, often involving the thumb, to perform precise or efficient cuts.
specialized butchery skill or method

Examples

She practiced the butcher's thumb technique to improve her speed and precision when preparing meat.
The instructor demonstrated the butcher's thumb technique to the students during the workshop.
Mastering the butcher's thumb technique can make a significant difference in the quality of the cuts.
