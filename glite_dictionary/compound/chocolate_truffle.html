chocolate truffle

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑːklət ˈtrʌfəl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət ˈtrʌf(ə)l / (BrE)
a small, rich chocolate confection made typically with a creamy filling and coated in chocolate, sometimes dusted with cocoa powder or other toppings
rich, creamy chocolate confection

Examples

She bought a box of assorted chocolate truffles as a gift for her friend.
The rich, creamy center makes a chocolate truffle an indulgent treat.
He savored every bite of the homemade chocolate truffle at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: truffle - an edible subterranean fungus highly prized as a delicacy

Etymology

Named for its resemblance to the truffle fungus; 'truffle' ultimately from French 'truffe'.
