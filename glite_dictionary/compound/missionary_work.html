missionary work

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪʃənˌɛri wɜrk / (AmE), / ˈmɪʃənəri wɜːk / (BrE)
the organized activities carried out by religious missionaries, usually aimed at spreading their faith and providing aid to communities
organized activities of religious missionaries

Examples

She dedicated her life to missionary work in remote villages across Africa.
Many organizations fund missionary work focused on education and healthcare.
His parents met while both were engaged in missionary work in South America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evangelism - the spreading of the Christian gospel by public preaching or personal witness
