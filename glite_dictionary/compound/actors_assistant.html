actor's assistant

1. NOUN

/ ˈæktɚz əˈsɪstənt / (AmE), / ˈæktəz əˈsɪstənt / (BrE)
a person who assists an actor, especially by helping with costumes and personal needs during a production
person assisting an actor, usually with costumes

Examples

The actor's assistant made sure every costume change went smoothly during rehearsals.
After working as an actor's assistant for several years, she learned the behind-the-scenes workings of the theater.
The lead performer trusted his actor's assistant to keep his wardrobe and props ready before each act.
