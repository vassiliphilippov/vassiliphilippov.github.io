hospital care

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːspɪtl ˌkɛr / (AmE), / ˈhɒspɪtl ˌkeə / (BrE)
medical care and treatment provided to a patient during a stay in a hospital
medical treatment in a hospital

Examples

After his surgery, he needed several days of hospital care before he could return home.
The cost of hospital care has risen significantly over the past decade.
She was admitted for emergency hospital care following the accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inpatient care - medical treatment provided to patients admitted to a hospital
  • Antonym: outpatient care - medical treatment provided to patients not staying overnight in a hospital
