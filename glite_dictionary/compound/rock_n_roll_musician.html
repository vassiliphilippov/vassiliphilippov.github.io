rock 'n' roll musician

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ən roʊl mjuˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / rɒk ən rəʊl mjuˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who performs, composes, or is closely identified with rock and roll music.
performer or composer of rock 'n' roll music

Examples

As a rock 'n' roll musician, she spent years touring with legendary bands.
The documentary features interviews with an influential rock 'n' roll musician from the 1960s.
He always dreamed of becoming a rock 'n' roll musician after hearing Elvis Presley as a child.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rock musician - performer or composer of rock music

Etymology

From 'rock and roll', a genre term from 1950s US music, and 'musician.'
