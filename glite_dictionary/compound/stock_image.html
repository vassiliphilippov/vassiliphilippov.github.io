stock image

1. NOUN

/ stɑk ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / stɒk ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
a professionally produced photograph that is available for licensing and reuse, typically for commercial or editorial purposes
licensable photograph for specific use

Examples

The magazine used a stock image to illustrate their article about travel.
She searched online for a stock image to use in the presentation.
Many websites rely on stock images to enhance their visual appeal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stock photo - a professionally shot photograph available for licensing and reuse

Etymology

Formed from 'stock' (in the sense of 'supply for use when needed') and 'image'.
