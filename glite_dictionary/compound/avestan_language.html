Avestan language

1. NOUN

/ əˈvɛstən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / əˈvɛstən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an ancient Iranian language that was used to compose the sacred texts of Zoroastrianism
ancient Iranian language of Zoroastrian scriptures

Examples

The sacred texts of Zoroastrianism were originally written in Avestan language.
Scholars study the Avestan language to better understand ancient Iranian religions.
Linguists have found that the Avestan language shares similarities with other Old Iranian dialects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Avesta - the primary collection of Zoroastrian sacred texts

Etymology

Named after the Avesta, the primary collection of Zoroastrian religious texts.
