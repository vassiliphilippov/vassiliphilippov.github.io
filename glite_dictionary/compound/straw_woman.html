straw woman

1. NOUN

/ strɔ ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / strɔː ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman whose name or identity is used to conceal the true identity of someone involved in a questionable or illegal activity.
woman used to conceal true identity

Examples

The company used a straw woman to purchase land on behalf of the real owners.
In the scandal, a straw woman was listed as the director to hide the politician's involvement.
Investigators discovered that the account belonged to a straw woman who had no real authority.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: straw man - A person used to conceal someone's identity or for deceptive legal purposes

Etymology

Based on the earlier term 'straw man', from the use of a dummy made of straw representing a false or deceptive front.
