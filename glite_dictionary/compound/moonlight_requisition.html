moonlight requisition

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːnˌlaɪt ˌrɛkwɪˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌlaɪt ˌrɛkwɪˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
an unofficial or unauthorized request or operation, usually conducted secretly at night
secret or unauthorized nighttime operation

Examples

Under the cover of darkness, the squad organized a moonlight requisition to retrieve crucial equipment.
Their success depended on a well-planned moonlight requisition that went unnoticed by rivals.
The term moonlight requisition is often used to describe secretive missions during wartime.

Synset Examples

The team completed a moonlight requisition without anyone noticing.
He recalled the moonlight requisition that secured their victory.
The army carried out a moonlight requisition for supplies.

Etymology

From 'moonlight' meaning by night + 'requisition' (act of requesting or demanding something).
