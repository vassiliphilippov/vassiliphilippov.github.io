neem cake

1. NOUN

/ nim keɪk / (AmE), / niːm keɪk / (BrE)
the solid residue left after oil is extracted from neem seeds, often used as fertilizer or pesticide
residue from pressed neem seeds, used as fertilizer

Examples

Farmers often use neem cake as a natural fertilizer and soil conditioner.
After extracting oil from the seeds, the remaining neem cake contains valuable nutrients for plants.
Organic gardening stores sometimes sell neem cake for pest control and soil enrichment.
