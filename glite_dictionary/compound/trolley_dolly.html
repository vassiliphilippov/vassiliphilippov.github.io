trolley dolly

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɑli ˈdɑli / (AmE), / ˈtrɒli ˈdɒli / (BrE)
an informal term for a flight attendant, especially one who serves food and drinks on an aircraft
informal: flight attendant
informal slang british

Examples

She worked as a trolley dolly for five years before changing careers.
The trolley dolly smiled as she pushed the drinks cart down the aisle.
He made a joke about being mistaken for a trolley dolly during the flight.

Synset Examples

As a trolley dolly, she travels the world for work.
The trolley dolly offered us refreshments on the flight.
He jokingly referred to her as the trolley dolly of the group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flight attendant - a person whose job is to look after passengers on an aircraft

Etymology

Formed from 'trolley' (a cart used to serve food and drinks) and 'dolly' (slang for a young woman).
