White Sea

1. PROPN

/ waɪt si / (AmE), / waɪt siː / (BrE)
a large inlet of the Barents Sea, located in northwestern European Russia.
a sea in northwestern Russia

Examples

The White Sea is known for its icy waters and long winters.
Several Russian ports are located along the White Sea coastline.
He studied marine biology in a research station on the White Sea.
