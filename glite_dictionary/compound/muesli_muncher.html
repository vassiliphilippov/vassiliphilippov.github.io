muesli muncher

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzli ˈmʌntʃər / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzli ˈmʌntʃə / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who frequently eats or enjoys muesli, especially as part of a healthy diet
person who enjoys eating muesli
informal
