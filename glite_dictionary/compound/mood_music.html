mood music

1. NOUN

/ muːd ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / muːd ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
music that is intended to create or enhance a particular atmosphere or feeling
music creating a specific atmosphere

Examples

The restaurant played mood music to make the evening feel special.
A little mood music always helps me relax after work.
Film producers carefully select mood music to match the tone of each scene.

Synset Examples

We played some mood music to set the dinner ambiance.
She loves to listen to mood music while studying.
