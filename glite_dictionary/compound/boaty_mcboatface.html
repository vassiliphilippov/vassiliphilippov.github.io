Boaty McBoatface

1. PROPN

/ ˈboʊti məkˈboʊtˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ˈbəʊti məkˈbəʊtˌfeɪs / (BrE)
a humorous name for a boat or ship, popularized as a public suggestion for naming a British research vessel
humorous boat name

Examples

The ship was officially named Boaty McBoatface after a public vote.
Naming their canoe Boaty McBoatface always gets a laugh from friends.
Many other vehicles have been jokingly called Boaty McBoatface in tribute to the original.

Synset Examples

The ship was humorously named Boaty McBoatface.
Everyone laughed at the name Boaty McBoatface.

Etymology

Formed in 2016 as a suggested name for a research vessel, combining 'boat' with a playful, redundant construction popularized on the internet.
