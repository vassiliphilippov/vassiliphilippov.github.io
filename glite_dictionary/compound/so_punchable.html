so punchable

1. ADJ

/ soʊ ˈpʌntʃəbəl / (AmE), / səʊ ˈpʌntʃəbl̩ / (BrE)
(informal) Extremely irritating or annoying to the extent of provoking a desire to hit the person.
extremely annoying or deserving to be hit
informal

Examples

The villain in the series is so punchable that viewers can't help but dislike him.
His smug expression made him so punchable during the interview.
She finds that arrogant colleague so punchable whenever he brags in meetings.

Synset Examples

That character in the movie was so punchable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punchable - annoying enough to deserve being punched
