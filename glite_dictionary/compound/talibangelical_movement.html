Talibangelical movement

1. NOUN

/ tæˌlɪbˌænˈdʒɛlɪkəl ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / tæˌlɪbˌænˈdʒɛlɪkəl ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
A movement or ideology that combines strict, fundamentalist evangelical Christian beliefs with attitudes, policies, or behaviors reminiscent of those associated with the Taliban.
mix of Taliban-like and evangelical ideologies
informal slang

Examples

The Talibangelical movement has sparked controversy among various Christian communities.
Critics argue that the Talibangelical movement threatens religious freedom and democratic values.
Some politicians have been accused of supporting Talibangelical movement ideals in their policy proposals.

Synset Examples

The Talibangelical movement has gained a following online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Taliban - an Islamic fundamentalist group
  • Synonym: evangelical - relating to evangelical Christianity

Etymology

Blend of 'Taliban' (an Islamic fundamentalist group) and 'evangelical' (relating to evangelical Christianity).
