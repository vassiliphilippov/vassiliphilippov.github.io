genus Thespesia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs θɛˈspiːziə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs θɛˈspiːziə / (BrE)
A small genus of tropical trees in the mallow family, including the portia tree, native to warm regions.
genus of tropical trees (mallows)

Examples

The genus Thespesia includes species that are commonly found in tropical coastal regions.
Botanists classified the portia tree under the genus Thespesia.
Several members of the genus Thespesia are valued for their ornamental flowers and durable wood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: portia tree - a tropical tree of the genus Thespesia

Etymology

From New Latin, named after Thespesia, derived from Greek "thespesios" (divine, wondrous).
