three-day event

1. NOUN

/ ˌθri deɪ ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / ˌθriː deɪ ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
an equestrian competition consisting of dressage, cross-country, and stadium jumping held over three days
equestrian competition held over three days

Examples

She competed in her first three-day event last summer and placed third overall.
The three-day event tests both the horse's endurance and the rider's skill in multiple disciplines.
Many spectators attended the three-day event to watch the exciting cross-country phase.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eventing - the sport comprising dressage, cross-country, and show jumping
