Force 17

1. PROPN

/ fɔrs ˌsɛvənˈtin / (AmE), / fɔːs ˌsɛv(ə)nˈtiːn / (BrE)
a Palestinian paramilitary unit established in 1972 to serve as a personal security force for Yasser Arafat and other PLO leaders, later known for carrying out attacks and maintaining covert operations in Europe and the Middle East
Palestinian paramilitary unit and security force

Examples

Force 17 was established in 1972 to protect Yasser Arafat and other senior PLO officials.
Investigators linked several attacks in Europe to operatives from Force 17.
International agencies monitored Force 17 due to its involvement in covert operations.
