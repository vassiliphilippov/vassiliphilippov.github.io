road show

1. NOUN

/ roʊd ʃoʊ / (AmE), / rəʊd ʃəʊ / (BrE)
a theatrical performance, exhibition, or event that travels to various locations for public viewing.
touring show or performance

Examples

The theater troupe launched their new road show across several major cities.
Fans lined up early to see the road show when it arrived in town.
After a successful opening, the musical went on a year-long road show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: touring company - a company of actors or performers that travels to different locations to perform
