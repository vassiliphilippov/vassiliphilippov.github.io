Gulf of Venice

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡʌlf əv vəˈnɛs / (AmE), / ˈɡʌlf əv vɪˈniːs / (BrE)
an arm of the northern Adriatic Sea, lying between northeastern Italy, Slovenia, and Croatia
northern arm of the Adriatic Sea

Examples

Many tourists enjoy sailing in the Gulf of Venice during the summer months.
The fishing industry plays a vital role along the coast of the Gulf of Venice.
