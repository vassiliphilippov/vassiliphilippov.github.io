South Ossetia

1. PROPN

/ saʊθ oʊˈsiːtiə / (AmE), / saʊθ əʊˈsiːtiə / (BrE)
a region in the South Caucasus that declared independence from Georgia but is recognized by most countries as part of Georgia; considered a disputed territory
disputed territory in the South Caucasus

Examples

Conflict erupted in South Ossetia after it declared independence from Georgia.
International recognition of South Ossetia remains a contentious issue.
Russian troops have maintained a presence in South Ossetia since the 2008 war.
