star-of-Bethlehem

1. NOUN

/ stɑr əv ˈbɛθlɪˌhɛm / (AmE), / stɑːr əv ˈbɛθlɪˌhəm / (BrE)
a perennial plant of the genus Ornithogalum, native to the Mediterranean region, with star-shaped white flowers
Mediterranean plant with star-shaped flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with star-of-Bethlehem blooming in the spring sunlight.
We identified the white, star-shaped flowers as star-of-Bethlehem during our hike.
Be careful when planting star-of-Bethlehem, as it can spread quickly in some gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ornithogalum - genus of star-shaped flowering plants

Etymology

Named after the biblical Star of Bethlehem, for the flower's star-like shape.
