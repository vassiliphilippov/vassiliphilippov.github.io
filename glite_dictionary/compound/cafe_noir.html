cafe noir

1. NOUN

/ kæˈfeɪ nɔr / (AmE), / kæˈfeɪ nɔː / (BrE)
A small cup of strong black coffee served without milk or cream.
strong black coffee without milk

Examples

He ordered a cafe noir after his meal at the restaurant.
I like my cafe noir strong and without sugar.
In Paris, enjoying a morning cafe noir is a daily ritual.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black coffee - coffee served without milk or cream

Etymology

From French 'café noir' meaning 'black coffee'.
