human race

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmən reɪs / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən reɪs / (BrE)
all the people living on the earth, considered as a single group
all humans collectively

Examples

The survival of the human race depends on our ability to adapt.
Climate change is a threat to the entire human race.
Throughout history, the human race has faced many challenges.

Synset Examples

all the world loves a lover
she always used ‘humankind’ because ‘mankind’ seemed to slight the women

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humankind - all human beings considered collectively
  • Synonym: mankind - the human race, men and women collectively
