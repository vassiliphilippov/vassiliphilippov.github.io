spear-point

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪr pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈspɪə pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the sharpened tip or head of a spear, designed for piercing or thrusting
the pointed tip or head of a spear

Examples

The ancient warrior carefully attached the spear-point to the shaft before battle.
Archaeologists discovered a bronze spear-point buried near the settlement.
The spear-point gleamed in the sunlight as he prepared to throw it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spearhead - the pointed head or tip of a spear
