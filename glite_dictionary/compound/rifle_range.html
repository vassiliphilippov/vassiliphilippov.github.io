rifle range

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪfəl reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈraɪfəl reɪndʒ / (BrE)
the maximum distance over which a rifle bullet can be accurately or effectively fired
distance a rifle bullet can travel

Examples

The deer was just beyond rifle range, so the hunter waited.
Make sure the target is within rifle range before taking a shot.
Accurately hitting a mark at the far edge of rifle range requires skill.

Synset Examples

the target was out of rifle range

2. NOUN

/ ˈraɪfəl reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈraɪfəl reɪndʒ / (BrE)
An area or facility designed for people to practice shooting rifles at targets.
area for target practice with rifles

Examples

The army recruits spent hours at the rifle range perfecting their marksmanship.
Sarah joined a club that meets weekly at the local rifle range.
Due to the renovation, the rifle range will be closed until next month.

Synset Examples

during the war they turned the bowling alleys into rifle ranges

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shooting range - a general facility where firearms are practiced
