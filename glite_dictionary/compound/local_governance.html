local governance

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊkəl ˈɡʌvərnəns / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəl ˈɡʌvənəns / (BrE)
the administration and management of local government within a specific area or community
administration of local government

Examples

Effective local governance is essential for community development.
Training programs are being implemented to strengthen local governance in rural regions.
Issues with local governance often impact the delivery of public services.

Synset Examples

The devolution process aimed to improve local governance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: local government - the administration of a particular town, county, or district by its own officials
