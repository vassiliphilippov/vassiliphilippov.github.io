chicken broth

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪk.ɪn brɔθ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.ɪn brɒθ / (BrE)
a liquid stock made by simmering chicken, often used as a base for soups and sauces
liquid stock made with chicken

Examples

She poured some chicken broth into the pot to start the soup.
Many recipes call for chicken broth instead of water to add more flavor.
After feeling sick, he sipped warm chicken broth to soothe his throat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken stock - liquid made by simmering chicken bones and vegetables
  • Antonym: vegetable broth - stock prepared by simmering vegetables
  • Antonym: beef broth - stock made by simmering beef
