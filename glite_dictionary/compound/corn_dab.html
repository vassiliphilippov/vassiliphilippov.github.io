corn dab

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn dæb / (AmE), / kɔːn dæb / (BrE)
a small, oval-shaped cake of cornbread, typically baked or fried, especially in the southern United States
small southern cornbread cake
american

Examples

She served hot corn dabs with butter at breakfast.
A plate of corn dabs sat on the kitchen table, fresh from the skillet.
His grandmother taught him how to make perfect corn dabs every Sunday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cornbread - a type of bread made from cornmeal, popular in the US South
