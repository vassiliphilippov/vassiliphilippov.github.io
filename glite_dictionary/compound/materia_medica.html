materia medica

1. NOUN

/ məˌtɪriə ˈmɛdɪkə / (AmE), / məˌtɪəriə ˈmɛdɪkə / (BrE)
the branch of medical science concerned with the study, preparation, properties, and uses of drugs.
study of drugs in medicine
medical

Examples

She devoted her career to the materia medica and contributed important research on herbal remedies.
A thorough knowledge of materia medica is essential for any practicing pharmacist.
The textbook covers both the history and current developments in materia medica around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pharmacology - the branch of medicine concerned with the uses, effects, and modes of action of drugs

Etymology

From Latin, literally 'medical materials'.
