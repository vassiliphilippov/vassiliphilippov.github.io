dog sleigh

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ sleɪ / (AmE), / dɒɡ sleɪ / (BrE)
a sled designed to be pulled by dogs, typically used for travel over snow or ice, especially in Arctic and sub-Arctic regions
sled pulled by dogs

Examples

They loaded their supplies onto the dog sleigh before starting the journey across the frozen tundra.
The explorers relied on a dog sleigh to transport food and equipment.
Watching the dog sleigh race was the highlight of the winter festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog sled - a sled pulled by dogs
