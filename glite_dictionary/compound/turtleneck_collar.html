turtleneck collar

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝt̬.əl.nɛk ˈkɑː.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.tl̩.nɛk ˈkɒl.ə / (BrE)
A high, close-fitting, turned-over collar that covers most of the neck, usually found on sweaters and shirts.
high turnover collar on garments

Examples

She wore a blue sweater with a turtleneck collar to keep warm in the winter.
The designer added a turtleneck collar to the dress for a touch of elegance.
I prefer shirts with a turtleneck collar during cold weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polo neck - a high, close-fitting, turnover collar in British English

Etymology

The term combines 'turtleneck', referring to the neck resembling that of a turtle withdrawing into its shell, and 'collar'.
