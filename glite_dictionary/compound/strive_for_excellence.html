to strive for excellence

1. VERB

/ straɪv fər ˈɛksələns / (AmE), / straɪv fə ˈɛksələns / (BrE)
to make great efforts to achieve the highest possible standards or quality
make strong efforts for high standards

Examples

She always encourages her students to strive for excellence in all their work.
The company's mission is to strive for excellence in customer service every day.
Despite setbacks, the athletes continued to strive for excellence throughout the season.

Synset Examples

Day after day, they continued to strive for excellence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pursue perfection - to attempt to achieve perfection
