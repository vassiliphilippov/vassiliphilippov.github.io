blackness of despair

1. NOUN

/ ˈblæknəs əv dɪˈspɛr / (AmE), / ˈblaknəs əv dɪˈspɛə / (BrE)
a feeling of overwhelming hopelessness, as if engulfed in darkness
intense feeling of hopelessness

Examples

As the news sank in, a blackness of despair settled over her.
He wrote poetry about the blackness of despair he felt after losing his job.
Many people experience the blackness of despair during difficult times in their lives.

Synset Examples

He felt the blackness of despair creeping in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: despair - the complete loss or absence of hope
