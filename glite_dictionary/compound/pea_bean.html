pea bean

1. NOUN

/ piː bin / (AmE), / piː biːn / (BrE)
a type of white-seeded bean, often dried and used as food
white-seeded edible bean, usually dried

Examples

The recipe calls for a cup of dried pea beans for the stew.
Farmers in the area primarily cultivate pea beans for their local markets.
After soaking overnight, the pea beans cooked quickly and added a creamy texture to the soup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: haricot bean - a different variety of edible bean
