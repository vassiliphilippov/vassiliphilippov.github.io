United States Secret Service

1. PROPN

/ ˌjuːnɪtɪd steɪts ˌsiːkrət ˈsɜːrvɪs / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪtɪd steɪts ˌsiːkrɪt ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a federal agency chiefly responsible for protecting the U.S. president, vice president, their families, visiting dignitaries, and combating counterfeiting and financial fraud.
U.S. federal agency for protection and anti-counterfeiting
american

Examples

The United States Secret Service investigated the threats made against the president.
She was assigned to the United States Secret Service after years with the FBI.
The United States Secret Service also handles cases of counterfeiting and financial fraud.
