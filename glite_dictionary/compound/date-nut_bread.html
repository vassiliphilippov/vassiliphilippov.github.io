date-nut bread

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪt nʌt brɛd / (AmE), / ˈdeɪt nʌt brɛd / (BrE)
A sweet, moist loaf bread made with chopped dates and nuts, often served as a snack or dessert.
sweet bread with dates and nuts

Examples

My grandmother always made her famous date-nut bread for family gatherings.
You can serve date-nut bread with butter or cream cheese for a delicious snack.
I baked a loaf of date-nut bread for the holiday brunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banana bread - sweet bread made with mashed bananas
