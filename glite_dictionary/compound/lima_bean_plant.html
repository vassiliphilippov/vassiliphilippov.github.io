lima bean plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪmə ˌbin ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈliːmə ˌbiːn ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant, either bushy or tall-growing, that produces large, flat, edible seeds known as lima beans.
plant producing lima beans

Examples

The lima bean plant requires a warm climate to thrive throughout the growing season.
We planted several lima bean plants in the back garden for their nutritious seeds.
After a few weeks, the lima bean plant began to flower and produce pods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lima bean - large, flat, edible seed of the lima bean plant

Etymology

From the name of Lima, the capital city of Peru, where the bean was first widely cultivated.
