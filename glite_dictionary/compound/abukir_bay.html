Abukir Bay

1. PROPN

/ əˌbuːˈkɪr ˌbeɪ / (AmE), / əˌbjuːˈkɪə ˌbeɪ / (BrE)
a bay on the Mediterranean Sea in northern Egypt, noted for its historical naval battles
bay in northern Egypt, Mediterranean Sea

Examples

The British fleet defeated the French at Abukir Bay in 1798.
Several ships were wrecked during the storm off Abukir Bay.
Historians often discuss the strategic importance of Abukir Bay in naval warfare.
