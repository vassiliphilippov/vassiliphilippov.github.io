steel company

1. NOUN

/ stil ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / stiːl ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a company that manufactures and sells steel products
company manufacturing and selling steel

Examples

The merger created the largest steel company in the world.
Many jobs were lost when the local steel company shut down its plant.
The steel company invested millions in new technology to improve production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steelworks - a factory where steel is made
