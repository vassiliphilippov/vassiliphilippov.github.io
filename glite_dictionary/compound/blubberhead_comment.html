blubberhead comment

1. NOUN

/ ˈblʌbɚˌhɛd ˈkɑːmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈblʌbəˌhɛd ˈkɒmɛnt / (BrE)
a remark or statement that is silly or nonsensical
silly or nonsensical remark
informal

Everyone rolled their eyes at his latest blubberhead comment during the meeting.

Those blubberhead comments made the teacher question his seriousness in class.
