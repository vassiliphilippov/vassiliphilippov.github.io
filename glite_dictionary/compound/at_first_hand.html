at first hand

1. ADV

/ ət ˈfɝst hænd / (AmE), / ət ˈfɜːst hænd / (BrE)
directly from the original source; without intermediaries or secondhand reporting.
directly from the original source

Examples

I wanted to see the results at first hand and judge for myself.
She prefers to get information at first hand rather than rely on rumors.
He experienced the devastation of the storm at first hand during his visit.

Synset Examples

I heard this story firsthand

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firsthand - obtained or experienced directly from the original source
  • Antonym: secondhand - obtained from someone other than the original source
