test range

1. NOUN

/ tɛst reɪndʒ / (AmE), / tɛst reɪndʒ / (BrE)
an area or facility designated for conducting tests, usually of equipment, weapons, or vehicles
area for conducting equipment or weapons tests

Examples

The new missile was launched from the test range in the desert.
Engineers visited the test range to evaluate the vehicle's off-road capabilities.
Safety protocols must be strictly followed at the test range to prevent accidents.
