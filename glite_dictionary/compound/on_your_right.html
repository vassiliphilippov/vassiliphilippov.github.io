on your right

1. ADP

/ ɑn jɔr raɪt / (AmE), / ɒn jɔː raɪt / (BrE)
located to the right side of you when you are facing forward
to the right side of you

Examples

When you enter the building, the reception desk is on your right.
The museum will be easily visible on your right after you cross the bridge.
Take the next exit and you’ll see the gas station on your right.

Synset Examples

After the left turn, the cafe is on your right.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: on your left - located to the left side when facing forward
