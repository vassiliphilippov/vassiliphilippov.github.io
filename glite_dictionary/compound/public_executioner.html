public executioner

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪk ˌɛksɪˈkjuːʃənər / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˌɛksɪˈkjuːʃənə / (BrE)
an official responsible for carrying out capital punishment, usually by executing those sentenced to death by a court.
official who carries out executions

Examples

The notorious public executioner was feared by many throughout the city.
As public executioner, his duty was to carry out sentences of death.
During the Middle Ages, the role of public executioner was both necessary and despised.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hangman - an executioner who hangs condemned people
  • Synonym: executioner - a person who carries out a death sentence
