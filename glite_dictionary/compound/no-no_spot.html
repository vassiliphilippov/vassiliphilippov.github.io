no-no spot

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊ noʊ spɑt / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ nəʊ spɒt / (BrE)
a place where an activity or presence is forbidden or strongly discouraged
forbidden or disallowed place
informal

Examples

Parking in the no-no spot will earn you a hefty fine.
Children are taught to avoid the no-no spot in the schoolyard.
The sign clearly marks this as a no-no spot for dogs.

Synset Examples

This area is a no-no spot for parking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forbidden zone - an area where entry or activity is not allowed
