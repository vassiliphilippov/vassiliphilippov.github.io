bristly oxtongue

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪstli ˈɑksˌtʌŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrɪstli ˈɒksˌtʌŋ / (BrE)
a European weed with spiny, tongue-shaped leaves and yellow flowers, now naturalized in the United States
a weed with spiny leaves and yellow flowers

Examples

The field was overrun with bristly oxtongue after just one season.
Gardeners often struggle to remove bristly oxtongue from their lawns due to its tough, spiny leaves.
Bristly oxtongue produces bright yellow flowers that attract bees in late spring.
