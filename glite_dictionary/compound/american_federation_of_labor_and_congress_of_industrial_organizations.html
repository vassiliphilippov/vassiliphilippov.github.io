American Federation of Labor and Congress of Industrial Organizations

1. PROPN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən ˌfɛdəˈreɪʃən əv ˈleɪbər ənd ˈkɑŋɡrəs əv ˌɪndəˈstrɪəl ˌɔrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃənz / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˌfɛdəˈreɪʃən əv ˈleɪbə ənd ˈkɒŋɡrəs əv ˌɪndəˈstrɪəl ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃənz / (BrE)
the largest federation of labor unions in North America, formed in 1955 by the merger of the American Federation of Labor and the Congress of Industrial Organizations
largest North American labor union federation

Examples

The American Federation of Labor and Congress of Industrial Organizations played a key role in shaping labor laws in the United States.
She attended a conference hosted by the American Federation of Labor and Congress of Industrial Organizations last summer.
Many unions affiliated with the American Federation of Labor and Congress of Industrial Organizations advocate for better workers' rights.
