thought transference

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɔt trænsˌfɛr.əns / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt trænsˌfɛr.əns / (BrE)
the supposed communication of ideas or feelings directly from one person's mind to another's without using the known senses; telepathy
the supposed direct transfer of thoughts; telepathy

Examples

Many science fiction stories feature thought transference between characters from different species.
She claimed to have experienced thought transference during the experiment, sensing her friend's emotions without words.
Researchers have tried to test the validity of thought transference under controlled conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telepathy - the supposed communication of thoughts or ideas by means other than the known senses
