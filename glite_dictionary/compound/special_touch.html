special touch

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛʃəl tʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəl tʌtʃ / (BrE)
a unique or distinctive quality or detail that makes something stand out or gives it added appeal
unique or distinctive quality

Examples

The artist always adds a special touch to her work that makes it instantly recognizable.
The chef's special touch transformed a simple dish into a gourmet meal.
A handwritten note gave the gift a special touch that made it more meaningful.

Synset Examples
