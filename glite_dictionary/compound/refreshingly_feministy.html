refreshingly feministy

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈfrɛʃɪŋli ˈfɛmɪnɪsti / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɛʃɪŋli ˈfɛmɪnɪsti / (BrE)
Displaying a modern and positive feminist perspective in a way that is newly appealing or unexpected.
having a notably modern feminist perspective
informal

Examples

Her essay offered a refreshingly feministy take on workplace equality.
The film's dialogue was refreshingly feministy without feeling forced or didactic.
Critics praised the novel for its refreshingly feministy portrayal of its main character.

Synset Examples

Her approach to activism is refreshingly feministy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feminist - advocating for women's rights and equality

Etymology

Blend of 'refreshingly' and 'feministy', the latter formed from 'feminist' + '-y' to denote a noticeable feminist quality.
