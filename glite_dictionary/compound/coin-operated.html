coin-operated

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɔɪn ˌɑpəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɔɪn ˌɒpəˌreɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a machine or device) requiring the insertion of a coin or coins to operate.
requires coins to operate (machines/devices)

Examples

The laundromat only has coin-operated washing machines.
We made a call from a coin-operated telephone booth on the corner.
Many old arcades featured rows of coin-operated video games.

Synset Examples

a coin-operated telephone
