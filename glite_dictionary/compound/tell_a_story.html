to tell a story

1. VERB

/ tɛl ə ˈstɔri / (AmE), / tɛl ə ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
to narrate an account or tale, often describing events or experiences, real or imagined
narrate an account or tale

Examples

Every evening, my grandfather would tell a story by the fireplace.
Can you tell a story about your childhood adventures for the class?
She tried to tell a story that would make everyone laugh.

Synset Examples

She can always tell a story that captivates everyone.
She loves to tell-a-story about her travels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to narrate - to give a spoken or written account of something
  • Synonym: to relate - to tell or recount something, especially a story
