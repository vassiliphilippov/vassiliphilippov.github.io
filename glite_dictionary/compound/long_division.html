long division

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋ dɪˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / lɒŋ dɪˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
a method of dividing one number by another in which each step of the division is written out in detail
detailed method of division

Examples

When learning mathematics, students often practice long division to improve their understanding of division.
The teacher showed the class how to use long division to solve more complex problems.
Many people use calculators today, but knowing long division can still be useful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: division - the process or result of dividing numbers
