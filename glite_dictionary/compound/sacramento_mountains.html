Sacramento Mountains

1. PROPN

/ ˌsækrəˈmɛntoʊ ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / ˌsækrəˈmɛntəʊ ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a mountain range in southern New Mexico, United States, located east of the Rio Grande
mountain range in southern New Mexico

Examples

The Sacramento Mountains are a popular destination for hiking and camping enthusiasts in New Mexico.
Snow occasionally covers the Sacramento Mountains in winter, attracting visitors to nearby ski resorts.
The lush forests of the Sacramento Mountains support a rich diversity of wildlife and plants.

Etymology

Named after the Spanish 'sacramento' meaning 'sacrament'.
