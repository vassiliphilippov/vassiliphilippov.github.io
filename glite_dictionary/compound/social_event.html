social event

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
an occasion where people gather together for social interaction, such as a party, reception, or meeting
gathering for social interaction

Examples

The annual company picnic is a popular social event for employees and their families.
Attending a social event can be a great way to meet new people and expand your network.
The charity ball was the biggest social event of the season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gathering - a coming together of people
  • Synonym: party - a social gathering for celebration or entertainment
  • Synonym: meeting - an assembly of people for a particular purpose
