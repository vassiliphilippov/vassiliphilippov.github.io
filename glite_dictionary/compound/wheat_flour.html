wheat flour

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːt flaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈwiːt flaʊə / (BrE)
flour made by grinding wheat, commonly used in baking and cooking.
flour made from wheat

Examples

For this recipe, you will need two cups of wheat flour and one cup of sugar.
Many types of bread are made with wheat flour as the main ingredient.
She prefers to use wheat flour instead of rice flour in her pancakes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flour - a powder obtained by grinding grain, used for making bread, cakes, etc.
