in so far

1. ADV

/ ɪn soʊ ˈfɑr / (AmE), / ɪn səʊ ˈfɑː / (BrE)
to the degree or extent that; only to this extent or in this respect
to the extent that

Examples

In so far as I understand the problem, it seems very complex.
The law applies in so far as it is consistent with the constitution.
We will support you in so far as we are able.

Synset Examples

insofar as it can be ascertained, the horse lung is comparable to that of man
so far as it is reasonably practical he should practice restraint

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insofar as - to the extent that; inasmuch as
