bog rhubarb

1. NOUN

/ bɑɡ ˈruː.bɑrb / (AmE), / bɒɡ ˈruː.bɑːb / (BrE)
a small Eurasian plant with broad leaves and lilac-pink, rayless flowers, typically found in moist habitats
Eurasian plant with broad leaves and lilac-pink flowers

Examples

In the wetlands, clusters of bog rhubarb add a splash of color to the landscape.
She identified the broad leaves as belonging to bog rhubarb during her nature walk.
The botanist collected samples of bog rhubarb for her research on moist habitat plants.
