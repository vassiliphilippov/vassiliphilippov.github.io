bride-gift

1. NOUN

/ ˈbraɪd ˌɡɪft / (AmE), / ˈbraɪd ˌɡɪft / (BrE)
a present given to the bride, especially on the occasion of her marriage
present given to a bride at her wedding

Examples

At the ceremony, she received a beautiful necklace as a bride-gift from her husband's family.
The tradition of giving a bride-gift dates back centuries in some cultures.
Each guest was invited to present a small bride-gift to honor the couple.
