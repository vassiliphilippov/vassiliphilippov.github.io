kiss and cry

1. NOUN

/ kɪs ənd kraɪ / (AmE), / kɪs ənd kraɪ / (BrE)
a designated area at a competition, especially in figure skating, where athletes and coaches wait for scores and often display emotional reactions
emotional waiting area at competitions

Examples

After finishing her routine, she waited nervously in the kiss and cry for her score.
The television cameras captured the skaters' reactions in the kiss and cry area.
Coaches often comfort their athletes in the kiss and cry after a disappointing performance.

Synset Examples

It was an emotional moment in the kiss and cry section.
After the performance, they went to the kiss and cry area.
She celebrated her score in the kiss and cry zone.

Etymology

Originated in figure skating, referring to the area where skaters await their scores, named for the emotional moments that often occur there.
