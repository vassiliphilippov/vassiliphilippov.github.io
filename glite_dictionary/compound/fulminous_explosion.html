fulminous explosion

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊlmɪnəs ɪkˈsploʊʒən / (AmE), / ˈfʊlmɪnəs ɪkˈspləʊʒən / (BrE)
a sudden and violent explosion, especially one that occurs with great force and speed
sudden, violent explosion

Examples

The fulminous explosion shattered every window in the building within seconds.
A fulminous explosion rocked the laboratory, prompting an immediate evacuation.
Investigators determined that a gas leak had caused the fulminous explosion in the warehouse.

Synset Examples

The fulminous explosion startled everyone nearby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: explosion - a violent bursting or blowing apart

Etymology

From Latin 'fulminare' (to strike with lightning), via 'fulminous' meaning 'sudden and explosive'.
