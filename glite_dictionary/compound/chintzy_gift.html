chintzy gift

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪntsi ɡɪft / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪntsi ɡɪft / (BrE)
A gift that is cheap or poorly made, often considered insubstantial or of low quality.
cheap or poorly made gift

Examples

He was disappointed to receive a chintzy gift for his birthday instead of something meaningful.
The company handed out chintzy gifts at the event that fell apart within days.
No one wanted to admit they had brought the chintzy gift to the secret Santa exchange.

Synset Examples

I returned the chintzy gift because it broke easily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheap gift - gift that is inexpensive or of low quality
  • Antonym: generous gift - gift that is of good quality or value

Etymology

From 'chintzy', meaning cheap or gaudy, derived from 'chintz', a type of printed calico fabric from Hindi 'chīnt'.
