rainbow lorikeet

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪnˌboʊ ˈlɔrɪˌkit / (AmE), / ˈreɪnbəʊ ˈlɒrɪˌkiːt / (BrE)
a brightly colored species of parrot native to Australia, known for its vibrant plumage and playful behavior
colorful Australian parrot species

Examples

I was amazed when a rainbow lorikeet landed on my shoulder during the nature walk.
