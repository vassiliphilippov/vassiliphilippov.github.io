tagging program

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæɡɪŋ ˌproʊɡræm / (AmE), / ˈtæɡɪŋ ˌprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
a computer program that assigns grammatical labels to the elements of text, such as parts of speech or syntactic categories.
software for labeling grammatical elements in text

Examples

The tagging program quickly identified all the nouns and verbs in the sentence.
Researchers evaluated the accuracy of the new tagging program on diverse text samples.
We installed a tagging program to help automate our linguistic data analysis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tagger - a device or program that assigns labels, especially in linguistics or computing
