tip-up

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɪp ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈtɪp ˌʌp / (BrE)
(of an object or part) designed to tip up or fold out of the way when not in use.
designed to tip or fold upward

Examples

The theater installed tip-up seats in every row to make cleaning easier.
Please fold down your tip-up tray before takeoff.
The old bus still had the original tip-up benches near the door.

Synset Examples

the little tip-up seat of the taxi

Related Terms

  • Synonym: folding - (of an object) able to be folded up for storage or use
