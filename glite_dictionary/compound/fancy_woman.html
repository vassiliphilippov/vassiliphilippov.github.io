fancy woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈfænsi ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈfænsi ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is regularly or continually in a sexual relationship with a man who is married to someone else; a mistress
a man's long-term mistress
archaic

Examples

The rumor was that he kept a fancy woman in the city for many years.
She feared her husband was seeing a fancy woman behind her back.
In Victorian novels, the existence of a fancy woman often leads to scandal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mistress - a woman, other than a man's wife, with whom he is having a sexual relationship

2. NOUN

/ ˈfænsi ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈfænsi ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who has sexual relationships with a man, typically one who provides her with financial support; historically used to refer to a prostitute or a kept mistress
a woman supported by a lover, mistress or prostitute (archaic)
archaic

Examples

In Victorian novels, a fancy woman often lived in secret apartments provided by wealthy lovers.
He was rumored to keep a fancy woman in the city, away from his family.
The scandal broke out when a politician was discovered visiting his fancy woman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mistress - a woman having an extramarital sexual relationship, especially with a married man
  • Synonym: prostitute - a person who engages in sexual activity for payment
