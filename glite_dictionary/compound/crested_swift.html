crested swift

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛstɪd swɪft / (AmE), / ˈkrɛstɪd swɪft / (BrE)
a bird of Southeast Asia and the East Indies, related to swifts, distinguished by its upright crest and tree-nesting habits
Asian tree-nesting swift with crest

Examples

The crested swift is known for its distinctive head feathers and agile flight.
Researchers studied the nesting behavior of the crested swift in the dense forests of Borneo.
Unlike most swifts, the crested swift builds its nest high in tree branches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swift - a fast-flying insectivorous bird of the family Apodidae
