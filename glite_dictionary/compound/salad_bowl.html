salad bowl

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæləd ˌboʊl / (AmE), / ˈsæləd ˌbəʊl / (BrE)
a large bowl used for mixing and serving salads
large bowl for salads

Examples

She tossed the vegetables together in a salad bowl before serving dinner.
For the party, we bought a new wooden salad bowl to match our dishes.
The restaurant served the Caesar salad in a giant salad bowl.

/ ˈsæləd boʊl / (AmE), / ˈsæləd bəʊl / (BrE)

bowl for serving salad

Examples

She placed her Caesar salad in a salad bowl before adding the dressing.
The restaurant uses elegant wooden salad bowls for their starters.
Each guest received a fresh green salad in a small salad bowl.
