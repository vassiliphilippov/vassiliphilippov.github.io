vivid wordscape

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪvɪd ˈwɝːdˌskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈvɪvɪd ˈwɜːdˌskeɪp / (BrE)
a lively and colorful depiction created with words, especially in writing or speech, that evokes a strong sensory or imaginative response
lively, colorful depiction in words

Examples

Her novel opens with a vivid wordscape of the bustling city streets.
The poet's use of imagery creates a vivid wordscape that draws readers into the scene.
Through his memoir, he crafts a vivid wordscape of his childhood experiences.

Synset Examples

She painted a vivid wordscape with her storytelling.
