hill myna

1. NOUN

/ hɪl ˈmaɪ.nə / (AmE), / hɪl ˈmaɪ.nə / (BrE)
a glossy black bird native to Asia, belonging to the starling family, often kept as a pet and noted for its ability to mimic human speech
Asiatic starling known for speech mimicry

Examples

The hill myna is prized for its remarkable ability to imitate a variety of sounds, including human speech.
Many bird enthusiasts keep a hill myna as a pet because of its talking skills.
Wild hill mynas are commonly seen in the forests of India and Southeast Asia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: myna - a bird of the starling family found in Asia
