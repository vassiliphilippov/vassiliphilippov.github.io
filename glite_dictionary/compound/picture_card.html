picture card

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪktʃɚ kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə kɑːd / (BrE)
a playing card that bears the picture of a face, such as a jack, queen, or king in a standard deck
playing card with a face, e.g., king, queen, jack

Examples

He drew a picture card from the deck and placed it on the table.
If you get a picture card, you win double points in this game.
Only the picture cards are removed before shuffling for this round.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: face card - a playing card with a face (king, queen, jack)
