mini-Trump

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪni ˈtrʌmp / (AmE), / ˈmɪni ˈtrʌmp / (BrE)
A person who resembles Donald Trump in behavior or style, especially in a less significant or smaller-scale context.
person resembling Donald Trump

Examples

Many saw the new mayor as a mini-Trump due to his brash style and rhetoric.
Critics accused her of acting like a mini-Trump during the heated debate.
The journalist referred to him as a mini-Trump after he announced his controversial policies.

Synset Examples

Everyone joked he was the mini-Trump of our group.
His tactics made him the mini-Trump of the office.

Etymology

Formed from 'mini-', meaning small, and 'Trump', referring to Donald Trump, the American businessman and former president.
