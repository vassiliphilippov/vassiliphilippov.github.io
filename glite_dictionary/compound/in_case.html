in case

1. SCONJ

/ ɪn keɪs / (AmE), / ɪn keɪs / (BrE)
as a precaution; if something happens or becomes necessary
as a precaution, if needed

Examples

You should take an umbrella in case it rains later.
I left the key under the mat in case you arrive before me.
Bring some extra cash in case the card machine isn't working.

Synset Examples

in case of trouble call 911
