chocolate syrup

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑk.lət ˈsɪr.əp / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒk.lət ˈsɪr.əp / (BrE)
A sweet, thick sauce made from unsweetened chocolate or cocoa, sugar, and water, used as a topping or flavoring for desserts and drinks.
sweet chocolate sauce for desserts

Examples

She poured chocolate syrup over her ice cream for an extra treat.
You can mix chocolate syrup into milk to make a quick chocolate drink.
The recipe calls for a generous drizzle of chocolate syrup on top.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chocolate sauce - a sauce made from chocolate, often used on desserts
