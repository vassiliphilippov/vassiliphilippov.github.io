puff adder

1. NOUN

/ pʌf ˈædɚ / (AmE), / pʌf ˈadə / (BrE)
a large African viper that inflates its body when threatened, known for its potent venom
a large venomous African viper

Examples

The puff adder is responsible for many snakebite incidents in Africa.
When threatened, a puff adder will inflate its body and hiss loudly.
We saw a puff adder basking in the sun during our safari tour.

2. NOUN

/ pʌf ˈædɚ / (AmE), / pʌf ˈædə / (BrE)
a harmless North American snake (Heterodon platirhinos) with an upturned nose, known for spreading its head and neck or playing dead when threatened
harmless North American snake with upturned nose

Examples

The puff adder will often inflate its body and hiss when it feels threatened.
Despite its alarming display, the puff adder is not venomous and rarely bites humans.
Many people mistake the puff adder for a dangerous snake because of its defensive tactics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hog-nosed snake - another common name for the same species

Etymology

Named for its behavior of puffing up its body when threatened.
