baseball team

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl tiːm / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl tiːm / (BrE)
A group of players who form an organized team to play the sport of baseball.
group organized to play baseball

Examples

The local baseball team won the championship last season.
She has been the coach of the high school baseball team for five years.
Our company is sponsoring a youth baseball team this summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: team - a group of players forming one side in a competitive game or sport
