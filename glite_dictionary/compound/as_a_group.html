as a group

1. ADV

/ əz ə ɡruːp / (AmE), / əz ə ɡruːp / (BrE)
all together; collectively as a single unit or entity.
together; collectively

Examples

The committee decided as a group to postpone the event until next month.
When asked for their opinion, the children spoke as a group rather than individually.
The neighbors acted as a group to organize the annual street party.

Synset Examples

the students turned out en masse

Related Terms

  • Antonym: individually - separately, one by one
  • Synonym: collectively - as a whole or in a group
