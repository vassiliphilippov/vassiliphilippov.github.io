preppy dress code

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛpi drɛs koʊd / (AmE), / ˈprɛpi drɛs kəʊd / (BrE)
a style of formal attire characterized by neat, classic, and collegiate-inspired clothing, typically associated with preppy youth culture
classic, collegiate style of formal attire

Examples

The school's strict preppy dress code requires students to wear blazers and ties every day.
Many fashion magazines highlighted the resurgence of the preppy dress code in college campuses.
Her preppy dress code made her stand out at the casual party.

Synset Examples

The school is known for its preppy dress code.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preppy - relating to a subculture associated with North American preparatory schools

Etymology

From 'preppy' (relating to preparatory schools) + 'dress code'. 'Preppy' derives from 'preparatory school'.
