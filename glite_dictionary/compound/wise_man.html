wise man

1. NOUN

/ waɪz mæn / (AmE), / waɪz mæn / (BrE)
a man who is respected for his wisdom and trusted as a guide or advisor
a man known for wisdom and guidance

Examples

The village often turned to the wise man for advice in difficult times.
Legends spoke of a wise man who lived atop the mountain, offering counsel to those who sought it.
Many rulers relied on a wise man at court to help them make important decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sage - a profoundly wise person, especially one who is featured in ancient history or legend
  • Synonym: counselor - a person trained to give guidance on personal or psychological problems
  • Antonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
