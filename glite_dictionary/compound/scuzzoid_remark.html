scuzzoid remark

1. NOUN

/ ˈskʌzˌɔɪd rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈskʌzˌɔɪd rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
A derogatory or contemptuous comment, often offensive or crude in tone.
insulting or contemptuous comment
informal slang

Examples

Her friend whispered a scuzzoid remark about the host, making her cringe.
He was known for dropping a scuzzoid remark whenever the conversation got dull.
The teacher quickly shut down the student's scuzzoid remark before it escalated.

Synset Examples

The scuzzoid remarks made her uncomfortable at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insult - an offensive remark or action
  • Antonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration

Etymology

Formed from 'scuzz' (slang for something dirty or despicable) + '-oid' (resembling) + 'remark.'
