scumsucking behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈskʌmˌsʌkɪŋ bəˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / ˈskʌmˌsʌkɪŋ bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
Immoral, despicable, or contemptible conduct regarded as extremely offensive or unprincipled.
despicably immoral conduct
slang american

Examples

His scumsucking behavior has alienated even his closest friends.
The manager was fired for his scumsucking behavior towards employees and customers alike.
Many were appalled by the politician's scumsucking behavior during the campaign.

Synset Examples

His scumsucking behavior is disappointing to everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deplorable behavior - behavior that deserves strong condemnation
  • Antonym: admirable behavior - behavior deserving respect or approval

Etymology

From 'scum' (refuse, filth) and 'sucking' (acting as a parasite) + 'behavior'. The term is American slang, combining vivid insult with the noun 'behavior'.
