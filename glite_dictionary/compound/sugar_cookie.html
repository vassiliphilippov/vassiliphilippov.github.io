sugar cookie

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌɡɚ ˌkʊki / (AmE), / ˈsʌɡə ˌkʊki / (BrE)
a flat, sweet cookie made from a simple dough and typically sprinkled with granulated sugar before baking
flat cookie sprinkled with sugar

Examples

She decorated each sugar cookie with colored icing and sparkling sprinkles.
Would you like a sugar cookie with your cup of tea?
The bakery sells the best holiday sugar cookies in town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shortbread - a simple crisp cookie with a high butter content
  • Degree Higher: gingerbread - a sweet baked good flavored with ginger and spices
