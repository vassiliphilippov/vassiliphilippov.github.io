amorphous shape

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɔr.fəs ʃeɪp / (AmE), / əˈmɔː.fəs ʃeɪp / (BrE)
A shape that lacks a clear structure, form, or definition.
shape lacking clear form or structure

Examples

The artist created a sculpture with an amorphous shape that defied easy classification.
Clouds often take on an amorphous shape, constantly shifting in the sky.
The stain on the wall had an amorphous shape that sparked my imagination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: formless - without a definite shape or structure
  • Synonym: shapeless - lacking a definite shape

Etymology

From Greek 'amorphos' (without form); 'shape' from Old English 'sceap'.
