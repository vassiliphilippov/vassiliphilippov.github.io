wedding reception

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ rɪˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ rɪˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)
a social gathering held after a wedding ceremony to celebrate the marriage, usually involving a meal, speeches, and dancing
party after a wedding ceremony

Examples

They invited over a hundred people to their wedding reception at a nearby hotel.
The wedding reception featured a live band, exquisite food, and heartfelt toasts.
After the ceremony, all the guests gathered for the wedding reception in the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reception - a formal party or celebration, especially after a ceremony
