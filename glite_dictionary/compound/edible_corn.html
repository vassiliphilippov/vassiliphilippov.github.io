edible corn

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛdəbəl kɔrn / (AmE), / ˈɛdɪbəl kɔːn / (BrE)
Corn that is suitable for human consumption, especially varieties or parts that are prepared and served as food.
corn that can be eaten by people

Examples

Some varieties of edible corn are especially sweet and tender when cooked fresh.
The farmer sold bushels of edible corn at the local market every summer.
Botanists study both edible corn and types used for animal feed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: field corn - corn grown primarily for livestock feed and industrial products, not for direct human consumption
  • Synonym: sweet corn - a variety of corn with high sugar content, commonly eaten as a vegetable
