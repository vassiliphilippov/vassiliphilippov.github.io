piece of paper

1. NOUN

/ pis əv ˈpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / piːs əv ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
a single sheet of paper, typically used for writing or printing
a single sheet used for writing or printing

Examples

She wrote her phone number on a piece of paper and handed it to me.
Please turn in your assignment on a clean piece of paper.
He folded the piece of paper into the shape of a crane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sheet of paper - a flat, thin piece of paper
