as far as one knows

1. ADV

/ əz fɑr æz wʌn noʊz / (AmE), / əz fɑːr æz wʌn nəʊz / (BrE)
used to indicate that what is being said is based on the information available to the speaker, to the best of their knowledge.
to the best of one's knowledge

Examples

As far as one knows, the bridge is still safe to cross despite the storm damage.
As far as one knows, the documents were all filed correctly last week.
No one has raised any objections, as far as one knows.

Synset Examples

As far as one knows, no one has arrived yet.
As far as one knows, the meeting is still on schedule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to the best of one's knowledge - according to all the information one has
