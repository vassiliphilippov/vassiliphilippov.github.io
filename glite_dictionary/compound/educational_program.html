educational program

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛdʒəˈkeɪʃənəl ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃənəl ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
an organized course or series of courses designed to provide education or training in a specific subject or skill
organized course or curriculum for education

Examples

The university launched a new educational program to help adults improve their computer skills.
Many children benefit from participating in an early childhood educational program.
She enrolled in an online educational program to study environmental science.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curriculum - the subjects comprising a course of study in a school or college
  • Synonym: course - a series of lectures or lessons in a particular subject
