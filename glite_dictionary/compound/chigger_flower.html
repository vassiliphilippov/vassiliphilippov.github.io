chigger flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪɡɚ ˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪɡə ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
An erect perennial plant native to eastern and southern United States, known for its showy orange flowers.
US perennial plant with orange flowers

Examples

We spotted a bright chigger flower blooming near the edge of the forest.
The chigger flower adds a splash of orange to southern gardens in the summer.
Botanists recorded several chigger flowers during their field study in Georgia.
