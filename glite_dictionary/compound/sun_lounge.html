sun lounge

1. NOUN

/ sʌn laʊndʒ / (AmE), / sʌn laʊndʒ / (BrE)
a room with large windows or mostly glass walls, designed to let in sunlight and provide a space to relax indoors
room with glass walls for sun exposure

Examples

We spent the afternoon reading in the sun lounge overlooking the garden.
The hotel offers a spacious sun lounge with panoramic views of the beach.
She filled the sun lounge with tropical plants to create a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conservatory - a glass-enclosed room for growing plants or relaxing
  • Synonym: sunroom - a room with glass walls for enjoying the sun
