hyped about

1. ADJ

/ haɪpt əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / haɪpt əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
(informal) Excited or enthusiastic about something.
excited or enthusiastic about
informal

Examples

I'm really hyped about the new video game coming out this Friday.
Everyone was hyped about the team's chance to win the championship this year.
After hearing the news, she got hyped about starting her new job.

Synset Examples

She felt hyped about the concert next week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excited about - feeling eagerly interested or enthusiastic
  • Synonym: enthusiastic about - showing intense and eager enjoyment or interest

Etymology

From 'hype' (informal excitement or promotion), used as a verb plus 'about'.
