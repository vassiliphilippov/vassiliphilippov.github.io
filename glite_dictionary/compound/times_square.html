Times Square

1. PROPN

/ taɪmz ˈskwɛr / (AmE), / taɪmz ˈskweə / (BrE)
a famous area in central Manhattan, New York City, known for its bright lights, theaters, and as the site of major events such as the New Year's Eve celebration
iconic area in central Manhattan, New York City

Examples

We watched the ball drop in Times Square on New Year's Eve.
Tourists flock to Times Square to see the giant electronic billboards and Broadway theaters.
Traffic in Times Square can be overwhelming, especially during the holiday season.
