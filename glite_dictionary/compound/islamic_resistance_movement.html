Islamic Resistance Movement

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈslɑːmɪk rɪˈzɪstəns məˈvmənt / (AmE), / ɪˈslæmɪk rɪˈzɪstəns məˈvmənt / (BrE)
a Palestinian Islamic fundamentalist organization seeking to establish an Islamic state in place of Israel, also known as Hamas.
Palestinian Islamic political group (Hamas)

Examples

The Islamic Resistance Movement emerged in Gaza during the late 1980s.
The objectives of the Islamic Resistance Movement include the establishment of an Islamic Palestinian state.
Many governments have designated the Islamic Resistance Movement as a terrorist organization.
