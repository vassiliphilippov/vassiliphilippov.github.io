bird's-eye bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝdz ˌaɪ bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdz ˌaɪ bʊʃ / (BrE)
a shrub with narrow, glossy evergreen leaves and yellow flowers with leathery, petal-like sepals
shrubbing plant with yellow flowers and glossy leaves

Examples

The bird's-eye bush in the garden is in full bloom during the summer months.
Gardeners appreciate the bird's-eye bush for its glossy leaves and attractive yellow flowers.
She planted a bird's-eye bush near the patio to add color year-round.
