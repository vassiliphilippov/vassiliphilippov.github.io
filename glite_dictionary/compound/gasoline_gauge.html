gasoline gauge

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæsəliːn ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɡasəliːn ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
a device in a vehicle that shows how much gasoline remains in the fuel tank
device showing fuel level in vehicle

Examples

The gasoline gauge was nearly on empty, so we stopped at the next gas station.
She glanced at the gasoline gauge to make sure she had enough fuel for the trip.
If your gasoline gauge is broken, you might run out of gas unexpectedly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuel gauge - instrument in a vehicle measuring remaining fuel
