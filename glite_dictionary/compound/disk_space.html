disk space

1. NOUN

/ dɪsk speɪs / (AmE), / dɪsk speɪs / (BrE)
the amount of storage capacity available on a magnetic disk or similar storage device, typically used to store data in computers
available storage on a disk (computing)

Examples

My computer ran out of disk space and I couldn't save any more photos.
Before installing the software, make sure you have enough disk space available.
The technician recommended clearing old files to free up disk space.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storage space - space available for storing data on a device
  • Synonym: memory - the part of a computer where data is stored temporarily or permanently
