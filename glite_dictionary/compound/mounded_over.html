mounded over

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaʊndɪd ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈmaʊndɪd ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
covered by or having a mound formed over it.
covered by a mound

Examples

After the burial, the grave was mounded over with fresh earth.
The archaeologists discovered several artifacts mounded over by centuries of dirt.
A soft hill mounded over the hidden ruins, concealing them from view.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: covered - having something placed over or on top
