nursery rhyme

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɝːsəri raɪm / (AmE), / ˈnɜːsəri raɪm / (BrE)
a short, traditional poem or song for children, typically in rhymed verse
traditional children's poem or song

Examples

Every night, her mother reads a nursery rhyme to help her fall asleep.
Children love to sing classic nursery rhymes together at school.
He memorized his favorite nursery rhyme before performing it at the school assembly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lullaby - a soothing song to send a child to sleep
