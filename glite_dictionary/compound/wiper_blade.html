wiper blade

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪpɚ bleɪd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪpə bleɪd / (BrE)
a long, narrow rubber blade attached to a vehicle's wiper arm, used to clean the windshield by wiping away water, dirt, or debris
windshield-cleaning blade on vehicles

Examples

I had to replace the wiper blade on my car because it was leaving streaks on the windshield.
During the heavy rain, the wiper blade worked hard to keep my view clear.
If your wiper blade is squeaking or skipping, it's time for a new one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: windshield wiper - a device for keeping the windshield clear by wiping it
