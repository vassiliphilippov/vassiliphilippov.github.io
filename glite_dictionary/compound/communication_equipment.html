communication equipment

1. NOUN

/ kəˌmjunəˈkeɪʃən ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
the physical devices and facilities used to transmit, receive, and process information, such as telephones, radios, and computers
devices used to transmit and receive information

Examples

The company invested heavily in new communication equipment to upgrade its network.
Modern hospitals rely on advanced communication equipment to coordinate emergency responses.
Firefighters use specialized communication equipment to stay in contact during rescues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telecommunication - communication over a distance using electronic means
  • Synonym: information technology - the use of systems for storing and sending information
