Evenki language

1. NOUN

/ iːˈvɛnki ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / iːˈvɛnki ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Tungusic language spoken by the Evenki people of eastern Siberia.
Tungusic language of the Evenki people

Examples

Linguists are documenting the Evenki language to preserve its unique grammar and vocabulary.
She is fluent in Russian and the Evenki language, which she learned from her grandparents.
Efforts are underway to introduce the Evenki language in local schools to keep the tradition alive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tungusic language - a family of languages spoken in eastern Siberia and northeastern China

Etymology

Named after the Evenki people, with roots in Siberian indigenous culture.
