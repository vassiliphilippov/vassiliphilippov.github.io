Palace of Westminster

1. PROPN

/ ˈpælɪs əv ˈwɛstmɪnstər / (AmE), / ˈpælɪs əv ˈwɛsmɪnstə / (BrE)
the building in London where the British Parliament (the House of Commons and the House of Lords) meets
building housing the British Parliament
british

Examples

Tourists from around the world come to see the Palace of Westminster in London.
Debates and decisions affecting the whole United Kingdom take place in the Palace of Westminster.
The Palace of Westminster is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its striking architecture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Houses of Parliament - another term for the Palace of Westminster or the British Parliament

Etymology

Named for its location in Westminster, London, originally a royal palace.
