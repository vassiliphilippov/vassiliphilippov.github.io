square yard

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr jɑrd / (AmE), / skwɛə jɑːd / (BrE)
a unit of area equal to the area of a square with sides one yard in length, used chiefly in Britain and North America
unit of area (one yard by one yard)

Examples

The carpet covers exactly 100 square yards of floor space.
She bought enough grass seed to plant two hundred square yards of lawn.
The advertisement lists the house as having 1,500 square yards of garden.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: square metre - unit of area equal to a square with sides one metre
  • Degree Lower: square foot - unit of area equal to a square with sides one foot
  • Degree Higher: acre - unit of area larger than a square yard, commonly used to measure land
