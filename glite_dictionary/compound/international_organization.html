international organization

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəl ˌɔrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an organization composed of members from several countries, established to promote cooperation on issues of common interest
body formed by multiple countries

Examples

The United Nations is perhaps the most prominent international organization in the world.
Joining an international organization can help countries address global challenges together.
The conference was attended by representatives from several international organizations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organization - an organized group of people with a particular purpose
