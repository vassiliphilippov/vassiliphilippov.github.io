khaki shirt

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæki ʃɝt / (AmE), / ˈkɑːki ʃɜːt / (BrE)
A shirt made from khaki-colored, often durable cotton fabric, typically associated with military, casual, or uniform wear.
shirt made of khaki fabric

Examples

The company uniform includes a khaki shirt with matching trousers.
He paired his khaki shirt with blue jeans for a laid-back look.
During the summer, a lightweight khaki shirt is comfortable for hiking trips.

Synset Examples

She wore her favorite kakie shirt to the party.
The uniform consisted of a khaki shirt and pants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: khaki - a dull brownish-yellow color or fabric
  • Synonym: shirt - a garment for the upper body

Etymology

From Urdu *khākī* (dust-colored), referring to the fabric color, and English 'shirt'.
