safety catch

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪfti kætʃ / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti katʃ / (BrE)
a device on a firearm or other weapon that prevents it from being fired accidentally
locking device on a weapon

Examples

Always make sure the safety catch is engaged before cleaning your rifle.
He forgot to release the safety catch, so the gun would not fire.
Most modern pistols are equipped with a safety catch to prevent accidents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safety - freedom from danger or risk
