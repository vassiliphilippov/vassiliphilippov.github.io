responsible for

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈspɑnsəbəl fər / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒnsəbl fə / (BrE)
having caused something to happen or having control over someone or something and being in charge of it; being the agent or cause of something.
being the agent or cause of something

Examples

The manager is responsible for assigning tasks to the team.
Who is responsible for this mess in the kitchen?
The committee was responsible for drafting the new guidelines.

Synset Examples

determined who was the responsible party
termites were responsible for the damage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in charge of - having control or responsibility for something or someone
  • Synonym: accountable for - held responsible for something, especially negative outcomes
