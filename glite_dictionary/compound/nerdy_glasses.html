nerdy glasses

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɜrdi ˈɡlæsɪz / (AmE), / ˈnɜːdi ˈɡlɑːsɪz / (BrE)
thick-rimmed or oversized glasses associated with an intellectual or geeky appearance
glasses with a geeky or intellectual style
informal

Examples

She bought a new pair of nerdy glasses to match her vintage outfits.
His nerdy glasses gave him a charmingly quirky look during the interview.
With her hair in a bun and nerdy glasses, she resembled a classic bookworm.

Synset Examples

He wore his nerdy glasses with pride at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spectacles - eyeglasses worn to correct or aid vision
  • Synonym: hipster glasses - fashionable, retro-styled thick-rimmed glasses

Etymology

From 'nerdy', informal for intellectually focused or socially awkward, plus 'glasses'.
