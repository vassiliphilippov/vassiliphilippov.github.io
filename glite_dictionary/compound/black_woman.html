Black woman

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / blæk ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who is Black, especially of African descent.
a woman of Black/African descent

Examples

Many influential leaders in history have been Black women who fought for social justice.
She is proud to be a Black woman and inspire others in her community.
The film highlights the unique experiences of Black women in modern society.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Black man - a man who is Black
  • Antonym: White woman - a woman who is White
