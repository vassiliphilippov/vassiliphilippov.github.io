narrow-leaf cattail

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛroʊ lif ˈkætˌaɪl / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ liːf ˈkætaɪl / (BrE)
A species of cattail with narrow leaves, found in America, Europe, North Africa, and Asia, often growing in marshes and wetlands.
reed-like marsh plant with narrow leaves

Examples

The wetland was full of narrow-leaf cattail swaying gently in the breeze.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the narrow-leaf cattail in this region.
She recognized the distinctive slender leaves of the narrow-leaf cattail along the pond's edge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cattail - a tall, reed-like marsh plant
