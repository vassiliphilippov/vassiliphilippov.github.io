yellow jacket

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˌdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˌdʒækɪt / (BrE)
a small, aggressive, yellow-and-black social wasp, often nesting in the ground
small yellow-and-black social wasp

Examples

We found a nest of yellow jackets in the backyard near the shed.
He was stung by a yellow jacket while mowing the lawn.
Yellow jackets can become aggressive if their nest is disturbed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wasp - a stinging winged insect related to bees and ants

2. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
A barbiturate drug, especially Nembutal, used as a sedative, hypnotic, or antispasmodic.
barbiturate drug (Nembutal) used as a sedative
medical informal

Examples

After his surgery, the doctor prescribed a yellow jacket to help him sleep.
Some people abused yellow jackets as recreational drugs in the 1960s.
The pharmacist explained the risks associated with taking yellow jackets for anxiety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nembutal - trade name for the barbiturate pentobarbital
  • Synonym: barbiturate - a class of drugs that act as central nervous system depressants
