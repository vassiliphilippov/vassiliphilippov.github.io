brickmaking machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪkˌmeɪkɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈbrɪkˌmeɪkɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
an industrial machine designed for manufacturing bricks from clay or other materials
machine for making bricks

Examples

The factory installed a new brickmaking machine to increase production efficiency.
A modern brickmaking machine can produce thousands of bricks in a single day.
He learned how to operate the brickmaking machine during his training at the plant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brick kiln - a furnace for firing bricks
