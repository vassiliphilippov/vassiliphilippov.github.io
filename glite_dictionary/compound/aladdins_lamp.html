Aladdin's lamp

1. NOUN

/ əˈlædənz læmp / (AmE), / əˈlædɪnz læmp / (BrE)
a magical lamp in the story of Aladdin, from which a genie can be summoned to grant wishes
magical lamp from Arabian Nights

Examples

Legend says that Aladdin's lamp can grant any wish to those who possess it.
In the story, Aladdin rubbed Aladdin's lamp and summoned a powerful genie.
Many people dream of finding their own Aladdin's lamp to solve all their problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genie - a supernatural being that grants wishes, originating in Arabian mythology

Etymology

From the tale of Aladdin in 'One Thousand and One Nights' (Arabian Nights), a collection of Middle Eastern folk stories.
