sugar shell

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːɡɚ ʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈsjuːɡə ʃɛl / (BrE)
A small spoon for serving sugar, often designed with a bowl shaped like a seashell.
a spoon for serving sugar

Examples

She placed the ornate sugar shell beside the bowl during afternoon tea.
The antique sugar shell was a family heirloom passed down for generations.
Please use the sugar shell to sprinkle sugar on your strawberries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teaspoon - a small spoon used for stirring tea or coffee
