paradise tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈpærəˌdaɪs triː / (AmE), / ˈpærədaɪs triː / (BrE)
a medium to large tropical American tree with odd-pinnate leaves, long panicles of small pale yellow flowers, and scarlet fruits
tropical American tree with scarlet fruits

Examples

The paradise tree is prized for its attractive scarlet fruit and lush foliage.
Botanists studied the medicinal properties of the paradise tree found in South America.
A row of paradise trees lined the edge of the tropical garden.
