hockey player

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑki ˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈhɒki ˌpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays the sport of hockey, either professionally or as an amateur
person who plays hockey

Examples

She dreamed of becoming a professional hockey player and representing her country.
The hockey player scored three goals in last night's game.
Young children watched the hockey player practicing his shots on the ice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athlete - a person who is proficient in sports and physical exercise
