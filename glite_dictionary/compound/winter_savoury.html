winter savoury

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪn.tɚ ˈsæv.ɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə ˈsæv.ər.i / (BrE)
a perennial herb (Satureja montana) with aromatic, resinous leaves used to flavor stews, stuffings, and meat dishes
herb used as a seasoning

Examples

She sprinkled chopped winter savoury over the roasted vegetables for extra flavor.
The recipe calls for a teaspoon of dried winter savoury in the meat loaf mixture.
Her garden includes several herbs, but winter savoury is her favorite for savory stews.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: summer savoury - an annual herb (Satureja hortensis) used for similar culinary purposes

Etymology

From 'savoury', the herb, with 'winter' distinguishing the perennial species (Satureja montana) from 'summer savoury' (Satureja hortensis).
