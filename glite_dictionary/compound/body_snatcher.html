body snatcher

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdi ˌsnætʃər / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˌsnætʃə / (BrE)
a person who secretly removes bodies from graves, typically to sell them for medical dissection
person who steals corpses for dissection

Examples

The notorious body snatcher was finally caught by the town authorities.
In the nineteenth century, body snatchers supplied medical schools with fresh cadavers.
Stories of body snatchers terrified local communities in Victorian England.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grave robber - someone who steals valuables from graves
