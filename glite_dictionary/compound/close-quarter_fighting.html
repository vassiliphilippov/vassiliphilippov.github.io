close-quarter fighting

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊs ˌkwɔrtɚ ˈfaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkləʊs ˌkwɔːtə ˈfaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
fighting that takes place at very short range, typically hand-to-hand combat
hand-to-hand combat at close range

Examples

The soldiers were trained extensively in close-quarter fighting before deployment.
Urban warfare often requires skills in close-quarter fighting due to the confined spaces.
He excelled in martial arts and was especially proficient in close-quarter fighting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hand-to-hand combat - fighting at close range without weapons or with melee weapons
