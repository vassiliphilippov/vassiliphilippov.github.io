meow button

1. NOUN

/ miˈaʊ ˌbʌtən / (AmE), / miˈaʊ ˌbʌtn̩ / (BrE)
a button on a device or toy that, when pressed, produces the sound of a cat meowing
button that plays a cat's meow

Examples

She laughed when her child discovered the meow button on the new stuffed animal.
The interactive book features a meow button alongside other animal sounds.
Some pet toys come with a meow button to attract your cat's attention.

Synset Examples

He pressed the meow button, and the cat perked up.
This gadget has a meow button that mimics real cat sounds.
