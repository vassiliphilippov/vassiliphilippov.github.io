housing project

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊzɪŋ ˌprɑdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˈhaʊzɪŋ ˌprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
a publicly funded and managed housing development for low-income families, typically found in urban areas
public housing development for low-income families

Examples

The city council approved the construction of a new housing project in the downtown area.
Growing up in a housing project shaped her perspective on community and opportunity.
Many housing projects were built after World War II to address the shortage of affordable homes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public housing - government-owned housing for low-income residents
