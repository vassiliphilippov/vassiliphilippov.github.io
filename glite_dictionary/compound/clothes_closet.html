clothes closet

1. NOUN

/ kloʊðz ˈklɑsət / (AmE), / kləʊðz ˈklɒzɪt / (BrE)
A closet used for storing clothes.
closet for storing clothes

Examples

I keep my winter coats in the clothes closet in the hallway.
She hung her new dresses neatly inside the clothes closet.
The guest room has a spacious clothes closet for visitors to use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wardrobe - a large piece of furniture for storing clothes
  • Synonym: cupboard - a cabinet with shelves used for storage
