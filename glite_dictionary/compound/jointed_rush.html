jointed rush

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɔɪntɪd rʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪntɪd rʌʃ / (BrE)
A species of rush native to Australia, known for its jointed stems.
Australian rush with jointed stems

Examples

The wetlands are home to the native jointed rush and various water birds.
Botanists are studying how the jointed rush adapts to changing water levels.
We spotted a clump of jointed rush growing near the creek.
