pivotman role

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪvətˌmæn roʊl / (AmE), / ˈpɪvətman rəʊl / (BrE)
an important or central position in negotiations or strategy, responsible for coordinating actions or decisions
key position in negotiations or strategy

Examples

As lead negotiator, she took on the pivotman role during delicate discussions.
His expertise made him perfect for the pivotman role in the merger talks.
Without someone in the pivotman role, the team struggled to coordinate their strategy.

Synset Examples

In negotiations, he played the pivotman role effectively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pivot - the central point, pin, or person around which things turn or depend
  • Synonym: linchpin - person or thing vital to an enterprise or organization
