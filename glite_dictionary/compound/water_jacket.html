water jacket

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚ ˌdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌdʒækɪt / (BrE)
a container or casing filled with water that surrounds a machine or part of a machine, especially the cylinder block of an engine, to keep it cool
cooling container in engines or machines

Examples

The engine overheated because the water jacket was leaking.
Regular maintenance of the water jacket ensures the engine does not overheat during use.
Most car engines use a water jacket to regulate temperature efficiently.
