woodworking vise

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdˌwɝkɪŋ vaɪs / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌwɜːkɪŋ vaɪs / (BrE)
a strong clamp with padded jaws, used in carpentry to hold pieces of wood securely without damaging them
carpenter's clamp with padded jaws

Examples

The carpenter secured the board in the woodworking vise before planing its surface.
A woodworking vise is essential for projects that require precision and stability.
He adjusted the jaws of the woodworking vise to avoid marking the finished piece.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vise - a clamping device with two jaws for holding work steady
