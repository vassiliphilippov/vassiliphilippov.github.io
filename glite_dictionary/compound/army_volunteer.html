army volunteer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmi ˌvɑlənˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˈɑːmi ˌvɒlənˈtɪə / (BrE)
a person who voluntarily enlists or offers service in the army without being conscripted
person who voluntarily joins the army

Examples

After graduating college, she decided to become an army volunteer to serve her country.
Many army volunteers help organize support for troops returning home.
He was proud to introduce himself as an army volunteer during the ceremony.

Synset Examples

She became an army volunteer to support local veterans.
He signed up as an army volunteer to help with events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volunteer - a person who offers to join or serve without pay or formal obligation
