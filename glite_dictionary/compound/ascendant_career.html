ascendant career

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɪndənt kəˈrɪr / (AmE), / əˈsɛndənt kəˈrɪə / (BrE)
A career that is rising in success, influence, or prominence.
career that is rising in success

Examples

After years of hard work and dedication, her ascendant career finally received industry recognition.
Many young professionals aspire to have an ascendant career in technology or finance.
A mentor can be invaluable during an ascendant career, offering advice as new challenges arise.

Synset Examples

Her career is ascendant after the recent promotion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: successful career - a career that has attained notable success
  • Antonym: declining career - a career that is losing success or influence
