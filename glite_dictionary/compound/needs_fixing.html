to needs fixing

1. VERB

/ nidz ˈfɪksɪŋ / (AmE), / niːdz ˈfɪksɪŋ / (BrE)
requires repair or correction; in need of fixing or improvement
in need of repair or correction

Examples

The washing machine needs fixing before we can use it again.
If your bicycle needs fixing, take it to the repair shop.
This website needs fixing because some of the links are broken.

Synset Examples

There's a leak in the roof that needs fixing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broken - not working; in need of repair
  • Antonym: functioning - working properly; not needing repair
