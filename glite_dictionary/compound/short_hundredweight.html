short hundredweight

1. NOUN

/ ʃɔrt ˈhʌndrədˌweɪt / (AmE), / ʃɔːt ˈhʌndrədˌweɪt / (BrE)
A unit of weight in the United States equal to 100 pounds (approximately 45.36 kilograms).
US unit of weight, 100 pounds
american

Examples

The shipment was measured in short hundredweights to comply with U.S. trade standards.
One short hundredweight of flour equals 100 pounds in America.
They ordered five short hundredweights of steel for the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hundredweight - a unit of weight equal to either 100 or 112 pounds, depending on country
  • Antonym: long hundredweight - British unit of weight equal to 112 pounds

Etymology

Formed from 'short' to distinguish from the British (long) hundredweight; 'hundredweight' from Middle English, referring to a standard weight of 100 units.
