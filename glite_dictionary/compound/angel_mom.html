angel mom

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪndʒəl mɑm / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəl mɒm / (BrE)
a caring and supportive mother who goes out of her way to help others
a mother who is especially caring and helpful
informal

Examples

Everyone in the community appreciates the angel mom who organizes meals for families in need.
After hearing about their struggles, the angel mom stepped in to offer her support and guidance.
She earned the title of angel mom for her tireless dedication to helping children at the shelter.
