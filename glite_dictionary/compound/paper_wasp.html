paper wasp

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpər wɒsp / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə wɒsp / (BrE)
A social wasp that builds nests from a papery material made by chewing plant fibers mixed with saliva.
a wasp that makes papery nests

Examples

The paper wasp builds its nest under the eaves of houses in many regions.
I watched a paper wasp carefully construct its nest from chewed wood pulp.
Unlike hornets, the paper wasp rarely acts aggressively unless its nest is disturbed.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: wasp - an insect of the order Hymenoptera and suborder Apocrita, known for a narrow waist and ability to sting
