parking spot

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrkɪŋ spɑt / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkɪŋ spɒt / (BrE)
a place, usually marked, that is designated for a vehicle to be parked
a designated place to park a vehicle

Examples

We drove around the block three times before a parking spot finally opened up.
She reserved a parking spot for her guest in front of the building.
He found a parking spot right next to the entrance, which made unloading the groceries easy.

Synset Examples

Finding a parking spot was surprisingly easy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parking space - a designated area for parking a vehicle
