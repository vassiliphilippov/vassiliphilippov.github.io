gun control

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌn kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / ɡʌn kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
the regulation of the sale, ownership, and use of firearms by law or policy
regulation of firearms

Examples

Debates over gun control intensify after every mass shooting in the country.
Many people have strong opinions about whether stricter gun control laws are necessary.
The politician's stance on gun control became a central issue in the campaign.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firearm regulation - laws or rules governing firearms
  • Antonym: gun rights - the idea or movement opposing gun restrictions
