game face

1. NOUN

/ ɡeɪm feɪs / (AmE), / ɡeɪm feɪs / (BrE)
a determined and focused expression or attitude, especially before or during a challenge or competition
determined, focused attitude or expression

Examples

He put on his game face before entering the interview room.
When the competition started, every player had their game face on.
During important meetings, she always wears her game face to stay professional.

Synset Examples

She showed her game face throughout the tough match.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poker face - an expressionless face, especially to hide one's feelings or intentions
