retail merchant

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːteɪl ˈmɜrtʃənt / (AmE), / ˈriːteɪl ˈmɜːtʃənt / (BrE)
a person or company that sells goods to the public in relatively small quantities for use or consumption rather than for resale
person or company selling goods to the public

Examples

The retail merchant carefully arranged the new products in the window display to attract customers.
Many small towns depend on the local retail merchant for basic groceries and supplies.
As a successful retail merchant, she built her business on excellent customer service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retailer - a person or business that sells goods to consumers
  • Antonym: wholesaler - a person or company that sells goods in large quantities at low prices to retailers
