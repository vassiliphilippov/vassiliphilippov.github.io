sea level

1. NOUN

/ ˈsi ˌlɛv.əl / (AmE), / ˈsiː ˌlɛv.əl / (BrE)
the average height of the ocean's surface, used as a standard for measuring land elevation and sea depths
average height of the ocean's surface

Examples

The city is located 500 meters above sea level.
Scientists warn that rising sea level could threaten coastal communities.
At sea level, water boils at 100 degrees Celsius.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: altitude - height above sea level
  • Synonym: elevation - the height of a place above sea level
  • Antonym: depth - distance below sea level
