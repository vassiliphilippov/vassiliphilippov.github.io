piece goods

1. NOUN

/ pis ɡʊdz / (AmE), / piːs ɡʊdz / (BrE)
fabrics or textiles sold in standard lengths, typically by the yard or meter, rather than as finished articles.
fabric sold by the yard or meter

Examples

The company specializes in importing piece goods from overseas manufacturers.
Many tailors prefer to purchase piece goods so they can cut fabric to exact measurements.
The shop offers a wide selection of piece goods for quilting and dressmaking.
