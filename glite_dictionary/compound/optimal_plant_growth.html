optimal plant growth

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑptəməl plænt ɡroʊθ / (AmE), / ˈɒptɪməl plɑːnt ɡrəʊθ / (BrE)
the best possible development of plants under ideal environmental conditions
best plant growth under ideal conditions

Examples

Proper irrigation and sunlight are essential for optimal plant growth.
Fertilizers can help achieve optimal plant growth in nutrient-poor soils.
Gardeners monitor soil conditions closely to promote optimal plant growth.

Synset Examples

Make sure the soil is pervious for optimal plant growth.

Related Terms
