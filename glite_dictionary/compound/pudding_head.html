pudding head

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊdɪŋ hɛd / (AmE), / ˈpʊdɪŋ hɛd / (BrE)
an unintelligent or foolish person
unintelligent person
informal

Examples

Stop acting like a pudding head and pay attention to the instructions.
He was called a pudding head for forgetting his own birthday.
Don't be such a pudding head—think before you speak!

Synset Examples

The economy, stupid!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blockhead - a stupid person
  • Synonym: dunce - a slow-witted or stupid person

Etymology

From 'pudding' (soft, mushy dessert) + 'head', suggesting a soft or empty head.
