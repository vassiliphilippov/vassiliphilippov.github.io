Republic of Guinea

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈɡɪni / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈɡɪni / (BrE)
a country in western Africa on the Atlantic Ocean, formerly a French colony, which gained independence in 1958
country in western Africa

Examples

The Republic of Guinea is known for its rich natural resources, particularly bauxite.
After visiting the Republic of Guinea, she wrote a book about her experiences in West Africa.
Several international organizations support development projects in the Republic of Guinea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Guinea - a country in West Africa, often referred to as 'Guinea' for short
