total cocksplat

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊɾl ˈkɑkˌsplæt / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl ˈkɒkˌsplæt / (BrE)
A complete mess or disastrous failure; something utterly ruined or spoiled, often used with crude or vulgar humor.
a complete mess or failure (vulgar)
vulgar

Examples

His attempt at baking a cake turned into a total cocksplat when it collapsed in the oven.
The event was a total cocksplat thanks to the constant technical problems and delays.
My project presentation was a total cocksplat—the slides wouldn't load and I forgot my notes.

Synset Examples

That mess on the wall is a total cocksplat.
