beg button

1. NOUN

/ bɛɡ ˈbʌtn̩ / (AmE), / bɛɡ ˈbʌtn / (BrE)
An informal, metaphorical way of describing an action or tactic used to seek help, support, or sympathy from others.
metaphorical way to seek help or sympathy
informal

Examples

Whenever he faces criticism, he is quick to hit the beg button and ask for support.
She didn't want to press the beg button, but she had no other option left.
Some employees tend to use the beg button when deadlines approach.

Synset Examples

He always uses the beg button when he's in trouble.
He pressed the beg button for help during the meeting.
