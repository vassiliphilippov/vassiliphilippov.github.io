familiar spirit

1. NOUN

/ fəˈmɪljɚ ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / fəˈmɪliə ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
A supernatural spirit, often taking animal form, believed to act as an assistant or companion to a witch or wizard.
supernatural assistant spirit, often for witches

Examples

Many legends describe a witch's familiar spirit as a black cat or a toad.
The villagers suspected she kept a familiar spirit to help with her spells.
Stories of familiar spirits appear throughout European folklore, often as protective animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: familiar - an animal-shaped spirit that aids a witch; a shortened form
