designated driver

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛzɪɡˌneɪtɪd ˈdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˈdɛzɪɡneɪtɪd ˈdraɪvə / (BrE)
a person in a social group chosen to abstain from drinking alcohol so they can drive others home safely
person chosen to stay sober and drive others

Examples

We agreed that Mark would be the designated driver for tonight’s party.
Having a designated driver is a smart way to ensure everyone gets home safely.
She volunteered to act as the designated driver for the group after the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sober driver - a driver who does not consume alcohol
