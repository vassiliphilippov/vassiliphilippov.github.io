police investigation

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis ɪnˌvɛstəˈɡeɪʃən / (AmE), / pəˈliːs ɪnˌvɛstəˈɡeɪʃən / (BrE)
an official inquiry by the police into criminal activities to discover the facts or find the perpetrator
official criminal inquiry by police

Examples

The police investigation uncovered new evidence linking the suspect to the crime scene.
After a thorough police investigation, the stolen goods were recovered and returned to their owner.
Because of the ongoing police investigation, the details of the case could not be released to the public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: criminal investigation - an inquiry into a crime, typically not limited to police work
