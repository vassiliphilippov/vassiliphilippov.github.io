narrow beech fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæroʊ bitʃ fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ biːtʃ fɜːn / (BrE)
A species of beech fern found in North America and Eurasia, known for its narrow fronds.
a beech fern species of North America and Eurasia

Examples

The narrow beech fern thrives in moist, wooded areas across much of North America.
We spotted a narrow beech fern while hiking the forest trail last weekend.
Gardens with partial shade are ideal for cultivating the narrow beech fern.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beech fern - a group of ferns in the Phegopteris genus
