to attend church

1. VERB

/ əˈtɛnd tʃɜrtʃ / (AmE), / əˈtɛnd tʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
to go to church services regularly, especially as an act of religious devotion
go to church services regularly

Examples

Maria likes to attend church with her family every Sunday morning.
Many people attend church more frequently during the holidays.
He made it a priority to attend church every week, regardless of his busy schedule.

Synset Examples

He attends church religiously every Sunday.
As a Lutheranist, she attends church every Sunday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go to church - to go to a church service
