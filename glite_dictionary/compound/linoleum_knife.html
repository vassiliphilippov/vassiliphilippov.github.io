linoleum knife

1. NOUN

/ lɪˈnoʊliəm naɪf / (AmE), / lɪˈnəʊliəm naɪf / (BrE)
A knife with a short, stiff blade and a curved point, used for cutting linoleum and similar materials.
knife for cutting linoleum

Examples

The floor installer used a linoleum knife to trim the edges of the new flooring.
Always cut away from your body when using a linoleum knife for safety.
He sharpened his linoleum knife before starting the next section of the job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utility knife - a general-purpose cutting tool with a retractable blade
