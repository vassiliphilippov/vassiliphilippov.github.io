full skirt

1. NOUN

/ fʊl skɝt / (AmE), / fʊl skɜːt / (BrE)
a long, wide skirt that is gathered at the waist and spreads out to give a voluminous appearance
a long, wide, gathered skirt

Examples

The bride chose a classic gown with a full skirt for her wedding day.
Her full skirt billowed out as she twirled on the dance floor.
Vintage dresses often feature a full skirt that creates an elegant silhouette.
