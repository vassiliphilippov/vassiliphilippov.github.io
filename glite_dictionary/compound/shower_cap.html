shower cap

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃaʊər kæp / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊə kæp / (BrE)
A waterproof, tight-fitting cap worn over the hair to keep it dry while showering or bathing.
waterproof cap for keeping hair dry in shower

Examples

She always wears a shower cap to keep her hair dry when bathing.
The hotel bathroom provided a disposable shower cap for guests.
My shower cap is decorated with colorful flowers.
