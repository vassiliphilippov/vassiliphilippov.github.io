Boston cream pie

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑstən ˈkrim ˌpaɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɒstən ˈkriːm ˌpaɪ / (BrE)
A cake consisting of sponge cake layers filled with custard or cream and topped with chocolate glaze.
a cake with custard filling and chocolate glaze

Examples

She ordered a slice of Boston cream pie for dessert after her meal.
The bakery is famous for its homemade Boston cream pie.
I've always wanted to learn how to make a Boston cream pie from scratch.

Etymology

Named after Boston, where the dessert was created in the 19th century.
