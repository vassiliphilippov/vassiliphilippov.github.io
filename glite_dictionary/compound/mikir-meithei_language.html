Mikir-Meithei language

1. NOUN

/ məˈkiːr ˈmaɪteɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / məˈkiə ˈmaɪteɪ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A group of Kamarupan languages spoken in the states of Manipur and Assam in northeastern India.
languages spoken in Manipur and Assam, India

Examples

Scholars have conducted extensive research on the Mikir-Meithei language and its dialects.
The Mikir-Meithei language is an important part of the cultural heritage of northeastern India.
Many linguists are interested in the grammar and vocabulary of the Mikir-Meithei language.
