paper plate

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpər pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə pleɪt / (BrE)
a disposable plate made from paper or cardboard, typically used for serving food at picnics or parties
disposable plate made of paper

Examples

We served the sandwiches on paper plates to make cleanup easier.
After the barbecue, we threw away all the used paper plates.
She brought a stack of paper plates for the picnic in the park.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: plastic plate - a disposable plate made of plastic
