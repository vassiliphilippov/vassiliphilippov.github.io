snow queen

1. NOUN

/ snoʊ kwiːn / (AmE), / snəʊ kwiːn / (BrE)
A woman who is emotionally cold or aloof in manner, sometimes specifically a dominant or highly skilled figure in ice skating.
an aloof or dominant woman, often in ice skating

Examples

Her icy demeanor earned her the nickname, snow queen.
After her flawless routine, the press dubbed her the snow queen of the national championships.
The main character, portrayed as a snow queen, barely showed any emotion throughout the play.

Synset Examples

The character in the play was portrayed as a snow queen.
She's known as the snow queen of the ice skating rink.
Her icy demeanor earned her the nickname, the snow queen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice queen - a woman regarded as emotionally cold
