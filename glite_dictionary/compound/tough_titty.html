tough titty

1. NOUN

/ tʌf ˈtɪti / (AmE), / tʌf ˈtɪti / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) Used to indicate indifference or a lack of sympathy towards someone's complaint or misfortune.
expression of indifference; lack of sympathy
informal vulgar

Examples

He forgot his umbrella again, but his friend just laughed and said, tough titty.
When I complained about the noisy neighbors, my roommate replied, 'tough titty.'
She wanted to change the deadline, but the boss just shrugged and muttered, tough titty.
