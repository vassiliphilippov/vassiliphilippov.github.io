quick layup

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk ˈleɪ.ʌp / (AmE), / kwɪk ˈleɪ.ʌp / (BrE)
a fast basketball shot made close to the basket, usually taken with minimal delay
fast basketball shot near the basket

Examples

She drove past her defender for a quick layup in the final seconds.
A quick layup after a steal gave their team the lead.
His specialty was the quick layup during fast breaks.

Synset Examples

He made a quick layup to secure the win.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: layup - a shot taken close to the basket in basketball
