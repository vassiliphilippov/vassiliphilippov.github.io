heart rhythm

1. NOUN

/ hɑrt ˈrɪðəm / (AmE), / hɑːt ˈrɪðəm / (BrE)
the regular pattern of beats made by the heart as it pumps blood
pattern of the heart's beating
medical

Examples

The doctor listened carefully to her heart rhythm during the examination.
An irregular heart rhythm may indicate a medical condition that needs treatment.
Regular exercise can help maintain a healthy heart rhythm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartbeat - the pulsation of the heart
