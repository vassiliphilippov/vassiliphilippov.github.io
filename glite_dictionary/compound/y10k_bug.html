Y10K bug

1. NOUN

/ waɪ tɛn keɪ bʌɡ / (AmE), / waɪ tɛn keɪ bʌɡ / (BrE)
a hypothetical computer problem that could arise from the year 10,000 causing issues with date formatting or processing
potential computer issue in year 10,000

Examples

Scientists have speculated about how to prevent the Y10K bug from disrupting future infrastructure.
The company released a satirical report on their preparedness for the Y10K bug.
Discussions about the Y10K bug often highlight how far ahead technology planning can go.

Synset Examples

We need to develop solutions for the Y10K bug.
Experts are preparing for the Y10K bug implications.
The Y10K bug could affect future technology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Y2K bug - computer problem due to the year 2000 date format

Etymology

By analogy with 'Y2K bug', from 'Y' (year), '10K' (ten thousand), and 'bug' (a fault in a computer system).
