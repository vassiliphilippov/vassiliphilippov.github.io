European dogtooth

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən ˈdɔːɡˌtuθ / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈdɒɡˌtuːθ / (BrE)
A perennial European plant of the lily family, with rose to mauve flowers, cultivated in many varieties; also known as dog's-tooth violet.
Eurasian lily with mauve flowers

Examples

The European dogtooth is prized by gardeners for its delicate mauve blooms in early spring.
Several varieties of European dogtooth are grown for ornamental purposes in shaded gardens.
When in full flower, the European dogtooth attracts bees and other pollinators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog's-tooth violet - common name for European dogtooth, a plant with mauve flowers
