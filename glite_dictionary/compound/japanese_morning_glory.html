Japanese morning glory

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈpænɪs ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˈɡlɔri / (AmE), / dʒəˈpænɪs ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˈɡlɔːri / (BrE)
an annual climbing plant native to East Asia, grown for its wide range of colorful, often frilled or double flowers
East Asian climbing plant with colorful flowers

Examples

The Japanese morning glory covered the garden fence with vibrant blue and purple blossoms.
Many gardeners appreciate the Japanese morning glory for its rapid growth and spectacular flowers.
We planted several Japanese morning glories along the trellis to brighten the patio.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: morning glory - climbing plants with trumpet-shaped flowers, genus Ipomoea
