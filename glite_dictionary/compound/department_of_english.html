department of English

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑːrtmənt əv ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtmənt əv ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ / (BrE)
the division of a school, college, or university that is responsible for instruction and research in English language and literature
academic division for English studies

Examples

She applied for a teaching position in the department of English at her local university.
The department of English is hosting a seminar on contemporary American poetry next week.
He was the head of the department of English for over a decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: English department - shorter form for department of English
