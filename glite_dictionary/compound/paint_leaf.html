paint leaf

1. NOUN

/ peɪnt liːf / (AmE), / peɪnt liːf / (BrE)
A showy poinsettia plant found from the southern United States to Peru, known for its brightly colored leaves.
a type of showy poinsettia plant

Examples

The garden was full of paint leaf plants, their vivid bracts brightening the walkway.
During the holidays, we decorate our home with paint leaf arrangements for a festive touch.
She researched how to care for a paint leaf after receiving one as a gift.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poinsettia - a plant with showy red and green foliage, native to Central America
