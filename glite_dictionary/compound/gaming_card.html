gaming card

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪmɪŋ kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmɪŋ kɑːd / (BrE)
a card from a set used for playing gambling games such as poker, blackjack, or baccarat
card used in gambling games

Examples

He shuffled the gaming cards before dealing them to the players at the table.
Casinos often replace their gaming cards regularly to prevent cheating.
Each gaming card is marked with clubs, diamonds, hearts, or spades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: playing card - a card used for playing various games
