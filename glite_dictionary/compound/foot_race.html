foot race

1. NOUN

/ fʊt reɪs / (AmE), / fʊt reɪs / (BrE)
A race in which the competitors run on foot.
race run on foot

Examples

The annual community foot race attracts runners of all ages.
He trained for months to win his first 10-kilometer foot race.
Hundreds lined the streets to watch the city’s longest foot race this year.

Synset Examples

she broke the record for the half-mile run

Related Terms

  • Synonym: race - a competition to see who is fastest
  • Degree Higher: marathon - a long-distance running race, typically 26 miles and 385 yards
