residence hall

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛzɪdəns hɔl / (AmE), / ˈrɛzɪdəns hɔːl / (BrE)
a building on a college or university campus where students live
student housing at a university or college

Examples

Freshmen are usually required to live in a residence hall during their first year.
The new residence hall on campus features modern amenities and comfortable study areas.
She made many friends while living in the residence hall at university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dormitory - a room or building providing sleeping quarters for students
