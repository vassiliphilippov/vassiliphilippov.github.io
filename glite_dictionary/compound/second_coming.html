Second Coming

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd ˈkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ˈkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
(in Christian belief) the prophesied return of Jesus Christ to earth to judge the living and the dead at the end of the world.
prophesied return of Christ for judgment

Examples

Many Christians await the Second Coming as a time when justice will be restored.
The prophecy predicts signs that will precede the Second Coming of Christ.
Some artists have depicted the Second Coming in paintings and sculptures throughout history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Last Judgment - the final judgment by God of all humankind
