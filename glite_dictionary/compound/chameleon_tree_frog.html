chameleon tree frog

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmiːliən tri frɑg / (AmE), / kəˈmiːliən tri frɒg / (BrE)
a species of tree frog known for its ability to change color, typically found in tropical forests
a color-changing tree frog species

Examples

The chameleon tree frog can quickly change its skin color to match its surroundings.
Scientists discovered a new population of chameleon tree frogs in the rainforest canopy.
The vibrant hues of the chameleon tree frog make it a favorite among amphibian enthusiasts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tree frog - a frog that lives in trees
