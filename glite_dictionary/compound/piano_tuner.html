piano tuner

1. NOUN

/ piˈænoʊ ˈtunər / (AmE), / piˈænəʊ ˈtjuːnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to adjust the tuning of a piano so that it produces the correct notes
person who tunes pianos

Examples

My piano tuner visits every six months to keep the instrument in perfect condition.
She called the piano tuner after noticing several keys sounded off.
As a piano tuner, he has worked with several famous musicians.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tuner - a person who tunes a musical instrument
