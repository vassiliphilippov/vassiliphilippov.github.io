oxblood red

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑksˌblʌd rɛd / (AmE), / ˈɒksˌblʌd rɛd / (BrE)
a dark brownish-red color, typically resembling the color of dried ox blood
a dark brownish-red color

Examples

She chose an oxblood red lipstick to match her evening dress.
The leather shoes had a rich oxblood red finish that stood out in the shop window.
Painters often prefer oxblood red as a bold accent color in interior design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burgundy - a deep red color inspired by Burgundy wine

Etymology

From oxblood (the blood of an ox) + red, referring to the color resemblance.
