Saints Peter and Paul

1. PROPN

/ ˌseɪnts ˈpiːtər ənd ˈpɔːl / (AmE), / ˌseɪnts ˈpiːtə ənd ˈpɔːl / (BrE)
A Christian feast day observed on June 29, honoring the apostles Peter and Paul.
Christian feast day for apostles Peter and Paul

Examples

Many churches celebrate the Feast of Saints Peter and Paul with special services on June 29.
In Rome, Saints Peter and Paul is a public holiday marked by religious ceremonies.
The story of Saints Peter and Paul is central to the early history of Christianity.
