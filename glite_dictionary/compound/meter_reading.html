meter reading

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːtər ˌriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmiːtə ˌriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the value or amount indicated by a measuring instrument, showing the quantity or condition being measured
numerical value shown by a measuring device

Examples

The electricity bill was calculated from the last meter reading.
She double-checked the meter reading before submitting the report.
The technician wrote down the gas meter reading during his inspection.

Synset Examples

he could not believe the meter reading
the barometer gave clear indications of an approaching storm

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reading - information indicated by a measuring instrument

2. NOUN

/ ˈmiːtər ˌriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmiːtə ˌriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of measuring and recording the value shown on a meter, such as one recording electricity, gas, or water usage
checking and recording a meter's measurement

Examples

The technician arrived to perform the meter reading at our house this morning.
All residents are required to submit a monthly meter reading to the utility company.
An accurate meter reading ensures that you are only billed for the energy you have actually used.

Synset Examples

he has a job meter reading for the gas company

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meter - an instrument for measuring and recording quantity, degree, or rate
