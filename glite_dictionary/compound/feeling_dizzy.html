feeling dizzy

1. ADJ

/ ˈfilɪŋ ˈdɪzi / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪŋ ˈdɪzi / (BrE)
experiencing a sensation in which you feel as if you or your surroundings are spinning or moving and you may be about to lose your balance.
sensation of spinning or unsteadiness

Examples

After standing up too quickly, I suddenly started feeling dizzy and had to sit down.
She told the nurse she was feeling dizzy after taking her medication.
He kept feeling dizzy throughout the day, so he decided to visit his doctor.

Synset Examples

The roller coaster left me feeling dizzy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dizzy - having or involving a sensation of spinning and loss of balance
