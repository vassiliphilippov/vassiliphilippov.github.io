golden wonder millet

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈwʌndər ˈmɪlɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈwʌndə ˈmɪlɪt / (BrE)
a type of millet with yellow grains borne in large drooping spikes
millet with yellow grains and drooping spikes

Examples

Farmers in arid regions often cultivate golden wonder millet due to its drought resistance.
The large, drooping spikes of golden wonder millet make it easy to recognize in the fields.
Nutritional analysis shows that golden wonder millet is rich in fiber and essential minerals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millet - a cereal grass grown for its small seeds
