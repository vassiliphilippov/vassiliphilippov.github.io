paradise flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈpærəˌdaɪs ˈflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈpærədaɪs ˈflaʊə / (BrE)
a tropical shrub or small tree with striking yellow to orange-red flowers, sometimes classified in the genus Poinciana
tropical shrub with showy flowers

Examples

The paradise flower brightened up the entire garden with its vivid colors.
She planted a paradise flower near the window to add a tropical touch to her home.
The guide pointed out a rare paradise flower during the nature walk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Poinciana - tropical flowering plant genus, also known as royal poinciana
