spell-checker

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛl ˌtʃɛkər / (AmE), / ˈspɛl ˌtʃɛkə / (BrE)
a computer program or tool that automatically checks written text for spelling errors
tool that checks spelling errors in text

Examples

The spell-checker highlighted several mistakes in my essay before I printed it.
Many smartphones have a built-in spell-checker to help users avoid typos.
She relied on the spell-checker to catch her typing errors, but some mistakes still slipped through.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spellcheck - to check the spelling in a document using a computer program
  • Synonym: proofreader - a person who checks text for errors before it is published
