dish rack

1. NOUN

/ dɪʃ ræk / (AmE), / dɪʃ ræk / (BrE)
a rack used for holding dishes to allow water to drain off them after washing
rack for drying dishes after washing

Examples

I put the plates in the dish rack to dry after washing them.
The dish rack was full of clean cups and bowls.
She bought a new dish rack because the old one was rusted.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: draining board - a surface for draining washed dishes
