picture book

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪktʃər bʊk / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə bʊk / (BrE)
a book, especially for children, in which the story is told chiefly through pictures with little or no text
book mainly with pictures, little text

Examples

The child sat quietly, turning the pages of her favorite picture book.
Many picture books use colorful illustrations to engage young readers.
He donated several picture books to the local library's children's section.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storybook - a book containing stories, often for children
