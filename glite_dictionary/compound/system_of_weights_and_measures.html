system of weights and measures

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪstəm əv ˈweɪts ənd ˈmɛʒərz / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəm əv ˈweɪts ənd ˈmɛʒəz / (BrE)
an organized set of units and standards used to measure length, weight, and time
set of measurement units and standards

Examples

The United States still uses a different system of weights and measures than most of the world.
Scientists prefer the metric system of weights and measures for its simplicity and consistency.
Changing to a new system of weights and measures can be a complex process for a country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metric system - a decimal-based system of weights and measures
  • Synonym: imperial system - a traditional British system of weights and measures
