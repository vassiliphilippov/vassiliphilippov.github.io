ethical question

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛθɪkəl ˈkwɛs.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈɛθɪkəl ˈkwɛs.tʃən / (BrE)
a question or issue that involves moral principles or dilemmas over what is right and wrong
issue involving moral principles or dilemmas

Examples

The new technology raises important ethical questions about privacy and consent.
Doctors often face ethical questions when making life-or-death decisions.
The documentary explored the ethical questions surrounding genetic engineering.

Synset Examples

The use of napalm raised significant ethical concerns.
Chronocide raises ethical questions about time travel.
His controversial stance on targeted killing raised many ethical questions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moral dilemma - a situation requiring a choice between equally undesirable alternatives concerning moral values
  • Synonym: ethical issue - a problem or situation that requires a choice among competing moral principles
