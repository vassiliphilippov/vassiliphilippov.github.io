to embrace identity

1. VERB

/ ɪmˈbreɪs aɪˈdɛntɪti / (AmE), / ɪmˈbreɪs aɪˈdɛntɪti / (BrE)
to accept and confidently welcome one's own personal identity, including characteristics, background, or orientation
accept and welcome one's own identity

Examples

She decided to embrace identity after years of feeling different from her peers.
Celebrities often encourage fans to embrace identity in a world that values conformity.
The campaign celebrates those who embrace identity, whatever their background may be.

Synset Examples

He proudly embraced his identity as a nerdo at the convention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accept oneself - to recognize and be at peace with who you are
  • Antonym: deny identity - to reject or fail to recognize one's own identity
