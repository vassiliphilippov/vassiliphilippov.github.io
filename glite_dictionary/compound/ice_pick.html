ice pick

1. NOUN

/ aɪs pɪk / (AmE), / aɪs pɪk / (BrE)
a tool with a steel rod and sharp point, used for breaking up blocks of ice
tool for breaking up ice

Examples

He used an ice pick to chip away at the large block of ice.
The bartender grabbed an ice pick to prepare crushed ice for the cocktails.
Always handle an ice pick carefully to avoid injury.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pickaxe - a tool with a heavy metal head, used for breaking up hard ground or rock
