wild onion

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ˈʌnjən / (AmE), / waɪld ˈʌnjən / (BrE)
any plant of the genus Allium with edible bulbs that grows in the wild, resembling a cultivated onion in taste and appearance
edible Allium plant growing wild

Examples

We picked a few wild onions in the forest for our salad.
The wild onion has a pungent smell similar to its cultivated relative.
Many chefs prize wild onions for their unique flavor in spring dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: onion - edible round bulb used as a vegetable, Allium cepa
