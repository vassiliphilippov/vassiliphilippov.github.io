flat-topped white aster

1. NOUN

/ flæt tɑpt waɪt ˈæstɚ / (AmE), / flæt tɒpt waɪt ˈæstə / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant with flat clusters of small white flowers, scientifically known as Doellingeria umbellata.
a North American white-flowered perennial plant

Examples

The flat-topped white aster blooms in late summer along the edges of wetlands.
She planted several flat-topped white asters to attract butterflies to her garden.
During the hike, we spotted a patch of flat-topped white aster in the meadow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a genus of flowering plants with star-shaped flowerheads
