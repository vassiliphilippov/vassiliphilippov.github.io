Kentucky wonder bean

1. NOUN

/ kənˈtʌki ˈwʌndər biːn / (AmE), / kənˈtʌki ˈwʌndə biːn / (BrE)
A variety of green bean with long, flat pods, commonly grown for its flavor and productivity.
variety of flat-podded green bean

Examples

She planted Kentucky wonder bean seeds in her garden last spring and harvested a huge crop.
The salad was garnished with blanched Kentucky wonder bean pieces for added crunch.
Many home gardeners prefer Kentucky wonder bean for its reliability and excellent flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: green bean - the common bean eaten as an unripe pod
