swan-neck

1. NOUN

/ swɑn ˈnɛk / (AmE), / swɒn ˈnɛk / (BrE)
Any of several orchids of the genus Cycnoches, characterized by slender, arching columns of flowers that resemble the neck of a swan.
orchid (genus Cycnoches) with arching flower columns

Examples

The greenhouse had a beautiful collection of swan-neck orchids in full bloom.
She admired the delicate curves of the swan-neck as it arched gracefully above its leaves.
Botanists often refer to the unique floral structure of the swan-neck when describing Cycnoches species.
