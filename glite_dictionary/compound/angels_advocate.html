angel's advocate

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪndʒəlz ˈædvəkət / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəlz ˈædvəkət / (BrE)
a person who defends or promotes positive, virtuous, or often unpopular causes, especially in opposition to criticism or skepticism
one who argues for virtuous or unpopular causes

Examples

She took on the role of angel's advocate during the debate, supporting the less popular proposal.
It's not easy being an angel's advocate when your views go against the mainstream.
As an angel's advocate, he spoke up for those who couldn't defend themselves.

Synset Examples

He became the angel's advocate for lost causes.
Being an angel's advocate is her life's mission.
As an angel's advocate, she fought for the voiceless.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: devil's advocate - someone who argues against a cause or for the opposing view to provoke argument or test reasoning

Etymology

Modeled on 'devil's advocate', substituting 'angel' to mean arguing for the good or virtuous side.
