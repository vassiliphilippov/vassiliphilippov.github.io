heavy security

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛvi səˈkjɚɾi / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi sɪˈkjʊərəti / (BrE)
the presence of extensive protective measures, often involving guards, equipment, or procedures to ensure safety.
extensive protective measures

Examples

There was heavy security at the airport following the recent threats.
The museum hired heavy security for the exhibition of rare jewels.
Due to the high-profile guest, the event was guarded with heavy security throughout the evening.

Synset Examples

The assassinee had heavy security during the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tight security - strict or closely controlled protection measures
  • Antonym: light security - minimal protective measures
