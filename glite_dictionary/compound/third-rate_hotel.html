third-rate hotel

1. NOUN

/ ˌθɝːd ˈreɪt hoʊˈtɛl / (AmE), / ˌθɜːd ˈreɪt həʊˈtɛl / (BrE)
A hotel of poor quality or low standard.
hotel of poor quality

Examples

Many tourists accidentally book a third-rate hotel and regret their decision upon arrival.
She complained that the third-rate hotel had dirty sheets and unfriendly staff.
After reading the reviews, we decided to avoid any third-rate hotel during our business trip.

Synset Examples

He refused to stay at a third-rate hotel on vacation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: third-rate - of inferior or poor quality; not first-rate
  • Antonym: first-rate hotel - a hotel of the highest quality or standard

Etymology

From 'third-rate' (inferior in quality), itself from naval rating of ships in Britain, combined with 'hotel'.
