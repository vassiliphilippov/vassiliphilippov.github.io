ocean pout

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊʃən paʊt / (AmE), / ˈəʊʃən paʊt / (BrE)
a species of fish (Zoarces americanus) found along the northeastern coast of North America, known for its eel-like body and pouting mouth.
a fish found off northeastern North America

Examples

The ocean pout is an important species in cold, coastal Atlantic waters.
Scientists are studying the ocean pout for its unique antifreeze proteins.
Fishermen occasionally catch ocean pouts while trawling off the coast of Maine.
