paper feed

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpər fiːd / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə fiːd / (BrE)
a device that automatically supplies sheets of paper to a printer or typewriter
device that supplies paper to a printer/typewriter

Examples

A jammed paper feed can cause delays in the office.
Make sure the paper feed is loaded correctly before you start printing.
