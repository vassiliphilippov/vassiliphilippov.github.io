hard to please

1. ADJ

/ hɑrd tə pliːz / (AmE), / hɑːd tə pliːz / (BrE)
(of a person) difficult to satisfy; fussy and demanding.
difficult to satisfy (person)

Examples

The new manager is hard to please and sets very high standards for everyone.
My grandmother is so hard to please that we never know what to cook for her.
Their clients are notoriously hard to please, so the team prepares meticulously for every meeting.

Synset Examples

his father was a hard-to-please taskmaster
was very hard to please

Related Terms

  • Antonym: easy to please - readily satisfied; not demanding
  • Synonym: fussy - hard to satisfy; overly particular
