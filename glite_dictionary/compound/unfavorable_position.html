unfavorable position

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈfeɪvərəbəl pəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪv(ə)rəbəl pəˈzɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a situation or condition in which one is at a disadvantage, especially in competition or confrontation
state of competitive disadvantage

Examples

The team found itself in an unfavorable position after conceding two early goals.
Being late for the interview put her in an unfavorable position compared to the other candidates.
Companies operating without the latest technology face an unfavorable position in today's market.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: advantage - a condition or circumstance that puts one in a favorable or superior position
  • Synonym: disadvantage - an unfavorable circumstance or condition
