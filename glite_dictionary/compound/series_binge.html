series binge

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪriːz bɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriːz bɪndʒ / (BrE)
An extended period spent watching multiple episodes of a television series in one sitting.
extended TV episode watching session
informal

Examples

We lost track of time during our series binge and stayed up past midnight.
A weekend series binge has become a routine for many people.
After a long day, she relaxed with a series binge of her favorite show.

Synset Examples

The series binge was a real time burglar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to binge-watch - to watch several episodes of a TV show in one sitting

Etymology

Formed from 'series' (television series) and 'binge' (period of excessive consumption).
