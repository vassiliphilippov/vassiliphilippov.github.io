certified public accountant

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɝː.tɪ.faɪd ˈpʌb.lɪk əˈkaʊn.t̬ənt / (AmE), / ˌsɜː.tɪ.faɪd ˈpʌb.lɪk əˈkaʊn.tənt / (BrE)
an accountant who has passed the necessary exams and fulfilled state requirements to be licensed to practice as a professional accountant in the United States.
licensed professional accountant (U.S.)
american

Examples

After passing the exams, she became a certified public accountant and opened her own practice.
Many companies require a certified public accountant to audit their financial statements annually.
He is both an attorney and a certified public accountant, which helps him advise on tax matters.

Synset Examples

in addition to accounting and auditing, CPAs also prepare tax returns for individuals and corporations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chartered accountant - a professional accountant with a similar designation in the UK and other countries
