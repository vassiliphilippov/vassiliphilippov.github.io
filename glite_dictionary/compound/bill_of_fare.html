bill of fare

1. NOUN

/ bɪl əv fɛr / (AmE), / bɪl əv feə / (BrE)
a written or printed list of the dishes served at a restaurant or meal; a menu
list of dishes at a restaurant

Examples

The waiter brought us the bill of fare as soon as we sat down.
She studied the bill of fare before deciding on the soup and salad.
Their bill of fare featured several vegetarian options and local specialties.

Synset Examples

the menu was in French

Related Terms

  • Synonym: menu - a list of food and drink items available at a restaurant
