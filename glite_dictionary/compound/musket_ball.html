musket ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌskət bɔl / (AmE), / ˈmʌskɪt bɔːl / (BrE)
a small, solid, spherical projectile fired from a musket, typically made of lead
spherical projectile for a musket

Examples

The soldier loaded a musket ball into his weapon before taking aim.
Archaeologists discovered several musket balls at the historic battlefield site.
In the eighteenth century, musket ball wounds were often serious and difficult to treat.

Synset Examples

they had to carry a ramrod as well as powder and ball

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullet - a projectile to be fired from a gun
