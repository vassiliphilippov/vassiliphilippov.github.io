genus Myadestes

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs maɪˈæd.ə.stiːz / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs maɪˈad.ɛstiːz / (BrE)
A genus of birds in the thrush family, known as solitaires, native to the Americas.
a genus of birds called solitaires

Examples

The genus Myadestes comprises several species of solitaires found throughout North and Central America.
Researchers observed unique vocalizations in a member of the genus Myadestes during their fieldwork.
Hawaiian forest birds belonging to the genus Myadestes are critically endangered due to habitat loss.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin, combining Latin 'genus' (kind) and 'Myadestes', a taxonomic name derived from Greek 'mua' (fly) and 'edestes' (eater).
