garden spade

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdn̩ speɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn speɪd / (BrE)
a spade with a flat, typically rectangular blade used by gardeners for digging and edging soil
spade used in gardening

Examples

He used a garden spade to turn over the soil in the flowerbed.
After a rainy night, the garden spade cut easily through the wet earth.
A good garden spade is essential for any serious gardener.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spade - a tool with a flat blade used for digging
