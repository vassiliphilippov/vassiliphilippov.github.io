good humor

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈhjuːmɚ / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈhjuːmə / (BrE)
A cheerful and pleasant mood or temperament; a state of being agreeable and in high spirits.
a cheerful and pleasant mood

Examples

Even after the long delay, he remained in good humor and joked with the staff.
Her good humor made everyone at the party feel welcome and relaxed.
Despite the bad weather, the children kept their good humor throughout the trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheerfulness - the quality or state of being noticeably happy and optimistic
  • Antonym: bad humor - a state or mood of irritability or grouchiness
