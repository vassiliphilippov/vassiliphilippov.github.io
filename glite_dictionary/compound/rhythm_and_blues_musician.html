rhythm and blues musician

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪðəm ənd bluːz mjuːˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈrɪðəm ən bluːz mjuːˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who performs or composes rhythm and blues music, a genre that blends elements of jazz, blues, and gospel
performer or composer of rhythm and blues music

Examples

The legendary rhythm and blues musician performed to a sold-out audience last night.
She began her career as a rhythm and blues musician in the 1980s.
Many young artists today cite classic rhythm and blues musicians as their biggest influences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument or writes music
