wingnutty remark

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪŋˌnʌti rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋˌnʌti rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
An extremely irrational, fanatic, or eccentric statement, especially in a political context.
extremely irrational or fanatic statement
informal slang

Examples

His wingnutty remark about aliens controlling the government drew laughter from the audience.
The debate took a turn when she made a wingnutty remark about the economy collapsing next year.
Journalists criticized the senator’s wingnutty remark as out of touch with reality.

Synset Examples

The politician's wingnutty remarks caused quite a stir.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wingnut - a person with extreme or odd political views

Etymology

From slang 'wingnut' (a person with extreme political opinions) combined with 'remark'.
