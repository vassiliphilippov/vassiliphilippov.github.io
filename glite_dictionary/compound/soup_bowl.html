soup bowl

1. NOUN

/ suːp boʊl / (AmE), / suːp bəʊl / (BrE)
A bowl designed for serving and eating soup.
bowl for serving soup

Examples

She placed a hot soup bowl in front of each guest before the meal began.
The kitchen cupboard was stocked with matching plates and soup bowls.
He accidentally dropped his soup bowl on the floor, and it shattered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowl - a round, deep dish used for food or liquid
