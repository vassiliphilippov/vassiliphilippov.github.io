building supply store

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪldɪŋ səˈplaɪ stɔr / (AmE), / ˈbɪldɪŋ səˈplaɪ stɔː / (BrE)
a store where materials used for constructing houses and other buildings are sold
shop selling construction materials

Examples

After deciding to remodel their kitchen, they went to the building supply store for lumber and tiles.
The new building supply store in town offers deliveries directly to construction sites.
She bought paint and plaster at the building supply store over the weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hardware store - a store selling tools and supplies for home improvement, sometimes including building materials
