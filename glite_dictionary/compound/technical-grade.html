technical-grade

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛknɪkəl ˌɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɛknɪkəl ˌɡreɪd / (BrE)
(of a chemical or material) containing small amounts of impurities; not of the highest purity, but suitable for industrial or technical use.
not highly pure, for technical use

Examples

The laboratory ordered technical-grade solvents for cleaning equipment rather than high-purity ones.
Some fertilizers are manufactured with technical-grade chemicals, which may contain minor impurities.
For most industrial applications, technical-grade sulfuric acid meets the necessary standards.

Synset Examples

technical-grade sulfuric acid

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: analytical-grade - very high purity, suitable for laboratory analysis
