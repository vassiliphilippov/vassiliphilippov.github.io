interior design

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtɪriər dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɪəriə dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
the art or practice of planning and decorating the inside spaces of buildings, such as homes or offices, to achieve a functional and aesthetically pleasing environment
art of decorating indoor spaces

Examples

She decided to study interior design because she loves creating beautiful and functional spaces.
The company hired a professional interior design firm to renovate their office.
Modern interior design emphasizes open spaces and natural light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interior decorator - a person whose job is to decorate the inside of buildings
  • Degree Higher: architecture - the art or practice of designing and constructing buildings
  • Synonym: home decor - the furnishing and decoration of the home
