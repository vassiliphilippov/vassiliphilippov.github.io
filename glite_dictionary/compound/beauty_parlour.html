beauty parlour

1. NOUN

/ ˈbjuːti ˌpɑrlər / (AmE), / ˈbjuːti ˌpɑːlə / (BrE)
a shop where hairdressers and beauticians provide cosmetic treatments and services
a place offering beauty treatments

Examples

She runs a beauty parlour in the city center that is popular with local residents.
After work, I went to the beauty parlour for a facial and manicure.
Many brides visit a beauty parlour before their wedding day for special treatments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salon - an establishment offering various beauty treatments
