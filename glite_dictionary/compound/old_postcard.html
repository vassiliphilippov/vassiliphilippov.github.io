old postcard

1. NOUN

/ oʊld ˈpoʊstkɑrd / (AmE), / əʊld ˈpəʊstkɑːd / (BrE)
a postcard from a previous era, often kept as a collectible or for its historical interest
vintage or collectible postcard

Examples

She collects old postcards from different countries as a hobby.
While cleaning the attic, we found a box full of old postcards written by our grandparents.
The old postcard featured a picture of the city as it looked a century ago.

Synset Examples

They discovered some old postcards during their renovation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vintage postcard - a postcard from an earlier period, often considered collectible
