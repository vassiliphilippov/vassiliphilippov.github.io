king orange

1. NOUN

/ kɪŋ ˈɔrəndʒ / (AmE), / kɪŋ ˈɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
a large citrus tree cultivated for its large, sweet, deep orange fruit that is easily peeled, especially grown in Florida
a citrus tree with large sweet orange fruit

Examples

The king orange trees in that orchard produce exceptionally sweet fruit.
Growers in Florida prize the king orange for its easy-to-peel skin and size.
She served fresh slices of king orange at breakfast every morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orange - a round citrus fruit with a tough bright reddish-yellow rind
