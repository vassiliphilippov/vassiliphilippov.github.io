lookit over there

1. X

/ ˈlʊkɪt oʊvɚ ðɛr / (AmE), / ˈlʊkɪt əʊvə ðeə / (BrE)
used informally to draw someone's attention to something in a particular place
informal attention-drawing phrase
informal american

Examples

Lookit over there, there's a parade coming down the street!
Hey, lookit over there—isn't that your friend from school?
Lookit over there, the sky is turning a bright shade of pink.

Synset Examples

Lookit over there, they’re giving out free samples!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: look over there - standard phrase used to direct attention to something in a certain place

Etymology

American English informal alteration of 'look at'.
