camping area

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæmpɪŋ ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈkæmpɪŋ ˌeəriə / (BrE)
an area of land designated for people to pitch tents and camp, especially as part of a recreational activity or holiday
designated site for pitching tents

Examples

We found a beautiful camping area near the lake where we set up our tent.
The park's camping area offers showers, picnic tables, and fire pits for visitors.
It's important to reserve your spot in the camping area during the busy summer months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: campsite - an area where people can camp, usually with facilities
  • Synonym: campground - a place used for camping, often with amenities
