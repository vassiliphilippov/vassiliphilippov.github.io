Lost Tribes

1. NOUN

/ lɔst traɪbz / (AmE), / lɒst traɪbz / (BrE)
the ten tribes of Israel that were exiled by the Assyrians around 720 BC and whose fate remains a historical and religious mystery
ten exiled tribes of Israel

Examples

There are many legends about the fate of the Lost Tribes of Israel.
Researchers continue to debate the whereabouts of the Lost Tribes.
Stories of the Lost Tribes have inspired explorers and writers throughout history.
