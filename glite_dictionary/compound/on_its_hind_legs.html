on its hind legs

1. ADP

/ ɑn ɪts haɪnd lɛɡz / (AmE), / ɒn ɪts haɪnd lɛɡz / (BrE)
standing upright on the back legs, typically for a better view or in order to reach something
standing upright on back legs (animal)

Examples

The meerkat stood on its hind legs to scan the horizon for predators.
When the dog begged for treats, it balanced on its hind legs and waved its paws.
The bear rose on its hind legs to appear larger and more intimidating.

Synset Examples

She laughed as the prairie dog stood on its hind legs.
