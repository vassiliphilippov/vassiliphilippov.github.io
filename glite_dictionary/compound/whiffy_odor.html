whiffy odor

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪfi ˈoʊdɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɪfi ˈəʊdə / (BrE)
a strong, unpleasant smell; a bad or offensive odor
a strong, bad smell
informal

Examples

A whiffy odor filled the locker room after the game.
She tried not to comment on the whiffy odor coming from the fridge.
Opening the old suitcase released a surprisingly whiffy odor into the room.

Synset Examples

The old socks had a rather whiffy odor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stench - a very unpleasant, strong smell
  • Antonym: fragrance - a pleasant, sweet smell
