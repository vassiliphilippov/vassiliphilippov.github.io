Commission on Human Rights

1. PROPN

/ kəˌmɪʃ.ən ɑn ˈhjuː.mən raɪts / (AmE), / kəˌmɪʃ.ən ɒn ˈhjuː.mən raɪts / (BrE)
a commission of the United Nations Economic and Social Council responsible for promoting and protecting human rights worldwide
UN body for human rights

Examples

The Commission on Human Rights played a key role in shaping international human rights standards.
Several countries presented their reports to the Commission on Human Rights during its annual meeting.
The work of the Commission on Human Rights has influenced laws around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: United Nations Human Rights Council - UN body that replaced the Commission on Human Rights in 2006
