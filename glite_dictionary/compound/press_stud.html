press stud

1. NOUN

/ prɛs stʌd / (AmE), / prɛs stʌd / (BrE)
a small fastener used on clothing, consisting of two pieces that snap together when pressed
a snap fastener for clothing

Examples

She fastened her coat quickly with the press studs along the front.
The baby's jacket uses press studs so it's easy to put on and take off.
Make sure each press stud clicks into place for a secure closure.

Synset Examples

children can manage snaps better than buttons

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snap - a fastener that closes with a snapping sound
