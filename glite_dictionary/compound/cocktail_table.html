cocktail table

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑkˌteɪl ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.teɪl ˌteɪ.bəl / (BrE)
a low table typically placed in front of a sofa, used for holding magazines, drinks, or decorative objects
low table for drinks and magazines

Examples

She placed the tray of drinks on the cocktail table in the living room.
A few magazines were neatly arranged on the glass cocktail table.
We gathered around the cocktail table for an evening chat and cocktails.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffee table - a low table typically in front of a sofa used for drinks or magazines
