gas furnace

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈfɝːnɪs / (AmE), / ɡas ˈfɜː.nɪs / (BrE)
A furnace that uses gas as a fuel to produce heat, typically for heating buildings.
furnace that burns gas as fuel

Examples

We had our old gas furnace replaced with a more efficient model last winter.
The technician inspected the gas furnace to ensure it was safe to use.
A gas furnace can provide reliable heat during the coldest months of the year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: furnace - an enclosed structure used for heating
