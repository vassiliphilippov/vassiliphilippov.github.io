automobile race

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔtəmoʊˌbil ˈreɪs / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəməˌbiːl ˈreɪs / (BrE)
a competition in which high-performance cars race against each other, usually on a closed circuit or designated course
race between high-performance cars

Examples

The annual automobile race attracted thousands of spectators from around the world.
He dreamed of driving in a professional automobile race since he was a child.
Safety regulations are strict in every automobile race to protect drivers and fans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car race - a race involving cars competing for speed or position
  • Synonym: motor race - a race involving motor vehicles, especially cars or motorcycles
  • Antonym: bicycle race - a competition involving bicycles racing against each other
