Book of Micah

1. PROPN

/ bʊk əv ˈmaɪ.kə / (AmE), / bʊk əv ˈmaɪ.kə / (BrE)
a book in the Old Testament containing the prophecies of Micah, predicting the destruction of Jerusalem
Old Testament book of prophecies by Micah

Examples

Many scholars study the Book of Micah for its social justice themes.
In church last Sunday, the pastor read from the Book of Micah.
The Book of Micah contains predictions about the fall of Jerusalem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Book of Amos - Old Testament prophetic book by Amos
  • Synonym: Book of Nahum - Old Testament prophetic book by Nahum
  • Synonym: Book of Isaiah - Old Testament prophetic book by Isaiah

Etymology

Named after Micah, a Hebrew prophet; part of the Jewish and Christian biblical canon.
