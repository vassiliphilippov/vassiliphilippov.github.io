leisure time

1. NOUN

/ ˈliʒɚ taɪm / (AmE), / ˈlɛʒə taɪm / (BrE)
time that is free from work or duties, when one can relax or enjoy activities of one's choice
free time for relaxation or enjoyment

Examples

After finishing her work, Maria spent her leisure time reading novels in the garden.
People often pursue hobbies during their leisure time.
He rarely had much leisure time because of his demanding schedule.

Synset Examples

his job left him little leisure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: free time - time when one is not working or busy
