big-eyed scad

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɪɡˌaɪd ˈskæd / (AmE), / ˌbɪɡˌaɪd ˈskad / (BrE)
A small, silvery fish of the Atlantic coastal waters, commonly used as bait by fishermen.
small Atlantic bait fish

Examples

Fishermen often catch big-eyed scad to use as live bait for larger fish.
A school of big-eyed scad was spotted near the harbor this morning.
The aquarium's new exhibit features several big-eyed scad among the coastal species.
