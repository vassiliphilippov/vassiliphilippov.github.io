rising trot

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪzɪŋ trɑt / (AmE), / ˈraɪzɪŋ trɒt / (BrE)
A way of riding at the trot in which the rider alternately rises from and sits in the saddle, usually in rhythm with the horse's movement.
equestrian riding technique at the trot

Examples

The instructor taught us how to master the rising trot for a smoother ride.
She lost her balance during the rising trot on her first lesson.
Many riders prefer the rising trot because it is easier on the horse.
