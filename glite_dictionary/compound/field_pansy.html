field pansy

1. NOUN

/ fiːld ˈpænzi / (AmE), / fiːld ˈpænzi / (BrE)
a common European wildflower (Viola arvensis) with pale creamy flowers, often tinged with violet
a wild pansy species with creamy flowers

Examples

A cluster of field pansies brightened the edge of the country lane.
The field pansy is often seen blooming in meadows during spring.
She picked a delicate field pansy and pressed it in her notebook.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wild pansy - a closely related species of pansy, often with more prominent purple and yellow markings
