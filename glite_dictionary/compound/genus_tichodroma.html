genus Tichodroma

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs tɪkəˈdroʊmə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs tɪkəˈdrəʊmə / (BrE)
A genus of small, wall-climbing birds known as wallcreepers, sometimes classified in the family Sittidae.
genus of wallcreeper birds

Examples

The only member of the genus Tichodroma, the wallcreeper, is renowned for its vivid colors and climbing ability.
Researchers studied the distribution of birds in the genus Tichodroma across mountainous regions.
The genus Tichodroma is notable for its unique adaptation to vertical rock faces.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin 'Tichodroma', based on Greek 'teikhos' (wall) and 'dromos' (runner).
