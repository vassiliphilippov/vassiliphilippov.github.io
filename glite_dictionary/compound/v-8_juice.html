V-8 juice

1. NOUN

/ vi eɪt dʒus / (AmE), / viː eɪt dʒuːs / (BrE)
A brand name for a commercially produced juice made from a blend of eight vegetables, typically tomato-based, sold in cans or bottles.
brand of mixed vegetable juice

Examples

I started my day with a glass of V-8 juice instead of orange juice.
She always keeps some V-8 juice in the fridge for a quick, healthy drink.
The recipe calls for tomato juice, but you can substitute V-8 juice for extra flavor.

Etymology

Named from 'V' for vegetables and '8' for the eight vegetable ingredients.
