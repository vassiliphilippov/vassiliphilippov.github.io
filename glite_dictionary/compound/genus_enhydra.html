genus Enhydra

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ɛnˈhaɪdrə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ɛnˈhɪdrə / (BrE)
a genus of marine mammals consisting of the sea otters
genus of sea otters

Examples

The genus Enhydra contains only one living species, the sea otter.
Scientists classify sea otters under the genus Enhydra.
Fossil evidence of the genus Enhydra has been found in both North America and Asia.

Etymology

From Greek 'enhydra' (living in water), used as a taxonomic term in scientific Latin.
