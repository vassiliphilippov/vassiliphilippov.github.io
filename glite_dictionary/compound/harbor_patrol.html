harbor patrol

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrbɚ pəˈtroʊl / (AmE), / ˈhɑːbə pəˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
a patrol of police officers or other authorities responsible for maintaining security and order in a harbor area
patrol unit policing a harbor

Examples

The harbor patrol responded quickly to reports of a suspicious boat near the docks.
Regular inspections by the harbor patrol help prevent illegal activity in the marina.
After the oil spill, the harbor patrol monitored the water closely for signs of contamination.
