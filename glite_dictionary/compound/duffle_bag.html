duffle bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌfəl bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈdʌf(ə)l bæɡ / (BrE)
A large, cylindrical bag made of heavy cloth, typically used for carrying personal belongings.
large cylindrical cloth bag for belongings

Examples

He packed all his clothes into a duffle bag before leaving for college.
The gym was full of athletes carrying duffle bags and water bottles.
She pulled a sweatshirt from her duffle bag when it got cold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kit bag - a bag used for carrying a soldier's or traveler's equipment
  • Synonym: backpack - a bag with shoulder straps carried on the back
  • Synonym: suitcase - a case with a handle for carrying clothes and personal items

Etymology

From Duffel, a town in Belgium, where the cloth was originally made.
