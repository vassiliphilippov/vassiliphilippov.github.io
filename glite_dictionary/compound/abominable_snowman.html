abominable snowman

1. NOUN

/ əˈbɑːmɪnəbəl ˈsnoʊˌmæn / (AmE), / əˈbɒmɪnəbl ˈsnəʊˌmæn / (BrE)
a large, hairy, humanlike creature said to live in the Himalayas, also known as the Yeti
mythical humanoid creature of the Himalayas

Examples

Many explorers have looked for the abominable snowman in the remote mountains of Nepal.
The legend of the abominable snowman has fascinated people for generations.
Some believe that mysterious footprints belong to the elusive abominable snowman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yeti - large hairy humanoid creature believed to inhabit the Himalayas

Etymology

Formed from abominable ('causing moral revulsion') and snowman; popularized in English accounts of Himalayan expeditions in the 20th century.
