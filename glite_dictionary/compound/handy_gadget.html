handy gadget

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændi ˈɡædʒɪt / (AmE), / ˈhændi ˈɡadʒɪt / (BrE)
a small device or tool that is useful and convenient to use
useful and convenient tool or device

Examples

She always keeps a handy gadget in her kitchen to save time with cooking.
This handy gadget folds up small enough to fit in your pocket.
The travel guide lists several handy gadgets for frequent flyers.

Synset Examples

His toolbelt was full of handy gadgets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gadget - a small device or tool, often ingenious or novel
  • Synonym: contraption - a machine or device that appears strange or unnecessarily complicated
  • Synonym: appliance - a device or machine, especially electrical, used for a particular task
