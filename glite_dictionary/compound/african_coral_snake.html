African coral snake

1. NOUN

/ ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈkɔ.rəl sneɪk / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈkɒ.rəl sneɪk / (BrE)
A small, widely distributed arboreal snake native to southern Africa, distinguished by its black and orange bands.
banded arboreal snake of southern Africa

Examples

The vibrant colors of the African coral snake help to warn predators of its toxicity.
While hiking in the savanna, we spotted an African coral snake weaving through the low branches.
Researchers have found that the habitat of the African coral snake is being affected by deforestation.
