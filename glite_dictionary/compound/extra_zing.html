extra zing

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛkstrə zɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛkstrə zɪŋ / (BrE)
a quality or ingredient that adds liveliness, excitement, or especially flavor to something
added liveliness or flavor

Examples

This spicy sauce gives the dish extra zing that everyone loves.
A squeeze of lemon can add extra zing to your water.
The new advertisement brought some extra zing to their marketing campaign.

Synset Examples

He sprinkled dillweed on the salad for an extra zing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spark - energy or liveliness that enlivens something
  • Synonym: kick - an extra quality of stimulation or impact, often in taste
