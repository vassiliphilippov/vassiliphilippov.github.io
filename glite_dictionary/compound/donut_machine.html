donut machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊ.nət məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈdəʊ.nʌt məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used for frying large quantities of doughnuts at once, typically in commercial settings
machine for frying many doughnuts

Examples

The bakery upgraded to a new donut machine to increase their daily output.
A donut machine can fry dozens of doughnuts quickly and evenly.
Many commercial kitchens rely on a donut machine for efficient production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deep fryer - an appliance for deep-frying food in hot oil
