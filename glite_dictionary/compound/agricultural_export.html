agricultural export

1. NOUN

/ ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃɚl ˈɛkˌspɔrt / (AmE), / ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəl ˈɛkˌspɔːt / (BrE)
goods produced by farming and sold to other countries
farming goods sold abroad

Examples

The country's economy is heavily dependent on agricultural exports such as wheat and corn.
A rise in agricultural exports has boosted rural incomes across the region.
The government introduced new policies to promote agricultural export growth.

Synset Examples

The region is known for its agricultural exports.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: export - goods sold to another country
  • Antonym: import - goods bought from another country
