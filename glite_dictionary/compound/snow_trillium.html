snow trillium

1. NOUN

/ snoʊ ˈtrɪliəm / (AmE), / snəʊ ˈtrɪliəm / (BrE)
a low perennial plant with white flowers, native to the southeastern United States, belonging to the genus Trillium
a white-flowered perennial plant of southeastern U.S.

Examples

The snow trillium blooms early in spring, often while snow is still on the ground.
Botanists were excited to discover a patch of snow trillium in the woodland clearing.
Gardeners admire the delicate appearance of snow trillium in shaded areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trillium - a genus of perennial flowering plants native to temperate regions of North America and Asia
