mind bleach

1. NOUN

/ maɪnd bliːtʃ / (AmE), / maɪnd bliːtʃ / (BrE)
something that is metaphorically used to erase unpleasant or disturbing memories or mental images.
metaphorical device to erase bad memories
informal

Examples

After seeing those photos, I desperately needed some mind bleach.
She joked about searching for mind bleach after the awkward conversation.
That horror movie was so creepy, I wish there was real mind bleach.

Synset Examples

She joked about using mind bleach to forget the meeting.

I need some mind bleach after that awful movie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brain bleach - another informal term for the same concept; metaphorical erasure of disturbing images
