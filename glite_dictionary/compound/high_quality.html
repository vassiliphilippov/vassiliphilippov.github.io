high quality

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈkwɑl.ə.ti / (AmE), / haɪ ˈkwɒl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the state or characteristic of being superior in standard or excellence
superiority or excellence

Examples

This product is known for its high quality and durability.
The restaurant uses only ingredients of high quality.
Consumers are willing to pay more for high quality craftsmanship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: superiority - the state of being higher in quality or status
  • Antonym: low quality - the state of being inferior in standard
