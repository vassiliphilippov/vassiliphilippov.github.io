desert four o'clock

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛzɚt ˌfɔr əˈklɑk / (AmE), / ˈdezət ˌfɔːr əˈklɒk / (BrE)
a wildflower with vibrant deep pink, tubular flowers that bloom in the evening, native to sandy and desert areas of the southwestern United States and Mexico
desert wildflower with pink evening blooms

Examples

The desert four o'clock blooms in the evening, showing off its deep pink flowers in the desert heat.
We spotted several desert four o'clock plants during our hike across the sand dunes.
Gardeners admire the desert four o'clock for its hardiness and colorful blossoms.
