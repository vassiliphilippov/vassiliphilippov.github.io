computer technology

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjutɚ tɛkˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə tɛkˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study, design, construction, and programming of computers and related systems
study and development of computers

Examples

Advancements in computer technology have transformed the way we live and work.
Many students choose to study computer technology at university due to the high demand for skills in this field.
The company invests heavily in the latest computer technology to maintain a competitive edge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: information technology - development and use of computer systems for storing, retrieving, and sending information
