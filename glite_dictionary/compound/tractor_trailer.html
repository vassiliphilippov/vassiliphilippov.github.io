tractor trailer

1. NOUN

/ ˈtræktɚ ˌtreɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈtræktə ˌtreɪlə / (BrE)
A large vehicle made up of a powerful truck (tractor) pulling a long trailer, used for transporting goods by road.
large truck with towing trailer

Examples

The highway was blocked by a tractor trailer that had overturned during the storm.
He drives a tractor trailer for a living, delivering goods across the country.
A double tractor trailer requires special training and a license to operate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: articulated lorry - a large truck with a trailer attached by a joint (UK equivalent)
