genus Blaberus

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs bləˈbɛər.əs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs bləˈbɛər.əs / (BrE)
A genus of large cockroaches native to Central and South America, known as giant cockroaches.
genus of giant cockroaches

Examples

The genus Blaberus includes some of the largest cockroach species in the world.
Entomologists often study the genus Blaberus due to its unique breeding behaviors.
A popular pet among insect enthusiasts is the Blaberus giganteus, from the genus Blaberus.

Etymology

From Latin 'genus' (kind, type) and 'Blaberus', the Latinized name for this cockroach genus.
