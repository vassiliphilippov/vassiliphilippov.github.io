snap-brim hat

1. NOUN

/ snæp brɪm hæt / (AmE), / snæp brɪm hæt / (BrE)
A hat with a brim that can be turned up or down, having a crease or 'snap' feature along the brim.
hat with a flexible up-or-down brim

Examples

He tipped his snap-brim hat as he walked down the street.
The detective wore a gray snap-brim hat in the classic film noir style.
Vintage shops often carry stylish snap-brim hats from the 1940s.
