the Pentagon

1. PROPN

/ ðə ˈpɛn.tə.ɡɑn / (AmE), / ðə ˈpɛn.tə.ɡɒn / (BrE)
the headquarters building of the United States Department of Defense, located in Arlington, Virginia, often used to refer to the Department of Defense as an organization
U.S. Department of Defense headquarters building
american

Examples

The President held a meeting at the Pentagon to discuss national security.
Officials from the Pentagon released a statement regarding the recent military deployment.
Journalists gathered outside the Pentagon following the announcement of the new defense policy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Department of Defense - the U.S. federal executive department responsible for military affairs

Etymology

Named for its five-sided (pentagonal) shape; from Greek 'pentagonon', meaning 'five-angled'.
