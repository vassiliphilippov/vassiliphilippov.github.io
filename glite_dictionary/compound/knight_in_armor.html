knight in armor

1. NOUN

/ naɪt ɪn ˈɑrmər / (AmE), / naɪt ɪn ˈɑːmə / (BrE)
A brave or gallant person who acts with courage, often used figuratively.
brave or heroic person

Examples

In her eyes, he was a true knight in armor, always ready to help anyone in need.
Whenever disaster struck, Mary hoped a knight in armor would come to her rescue.
The firefighter was hailed as a knight in armor after saving the family from the burning building.

Synset Examples

He felt like a knight in armor crossing the moat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hero - a person admired for courage or noble qualities
  • Antonym: coward - a person lacking courage
