cup final

1. NOUN

/ kʌp ˈfaɪ.nəl / (AmE), / kʌp ˈfaɪ.nəl / (BrE)
the final match in a cup competition, especially in sports, which determines the winner of the tournament
final match of a cup competition

Examples

Tens of thousands of fans attended the cup final at Wembley.
Winning the cup final was the highlight of her football career.
The television broadcast of the cup final attracted millions of viewers worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: final - the last match or round in a competition
