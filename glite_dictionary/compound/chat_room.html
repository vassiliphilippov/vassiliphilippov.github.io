chat room

1. NOUN

/ tʃæt ruːm / (AmE), / tʃæt ruːm / (BrE)
a website or online platform where multiple users can communicate in real time, often focused on a particular topic
online place for real-time group communication

Examples

Many teenagers spend hours every evening talking to friends in a chat room.
The company set up a chat room to help customers share their experiences and advice.
In the science fiction chat room, users discussed the latest developments in robotics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forum - an online message board for discussing topics
  • Synonym: message board - an online platform for leaving publicly accessible messages
