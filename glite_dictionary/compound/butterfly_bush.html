butterfly bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ərˌflaɪ bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəflaɪ bʊʃ / (BrE)
a tropical or subtropical shrub with clusters of white, violet, or yellow flowers, often attracting butterflies
flowering shrub attracting butterflies

Examples

The butterfly bush in our garden is covered with bright purple blooms every summer.
Many gardeners plant a butterfly bush to attract pollinators to their yards.
The nursery sells several varieties of butterfly bush, including those with yellow or white flowers.
