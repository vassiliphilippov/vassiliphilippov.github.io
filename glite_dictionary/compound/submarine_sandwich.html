submarine sandwich

1. NOUN

/ ˌsʌbməˈrin ˈsændwɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˌsʌbməˈriːn ˈsænwɪtʃ / (BrE)
a large sandwich consisting of a long crusty roll split lengthwise and filled with a variety of meats, cheeses, and salad vegetables; also known by names such as hoagie, hero, or grinder in different parts of the US
large sandwich on a long roll
american

Examples

She ordered an Italian submarine sandwich loaded with ham, salami, and provolone cheese.
At lunch, they shared a huge submarine sandwich and a bag of potato chips.
You can customize your submarine sandwich with extra pickles and hot peppers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoagie - large sandwich similar to a submarine sandwich
  • Synonym: hero - another US term for a submarine sandwich
  • Synonym: grinder - regional US term for a submarine sandwich

Etymology

From the resemblance of the sandwich's shape to that of a submarine vessel.
