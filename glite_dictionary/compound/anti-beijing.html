anti-Beijing

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.ti.biˈdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌænti.biˈdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
opposed to the government or policies of the People's Republic of China, particularly those of the Chinese Communist Party or Beijing authorities.
opposed to Chinese government policies

Examples

Several lawmakers voiced their anti-Beijing stance during the heated debate.
The anti-Beijing protests attracted international attention last summer.
She faced backlash from anti-Beijing activists after her comments on foreign policy.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pro-Beijing - supporting the Chinese Communist Party or Beijing's government and policies
