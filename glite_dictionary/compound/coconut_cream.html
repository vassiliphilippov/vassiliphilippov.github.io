coconut cream

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊˌkʌt ˌkriːm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌkʌt ˌkriːm / (BrE)
a thick, white liquid produced by pressing fresh coconut meat, used in cooking and beverages
thick liquid from pressed coconut meat

Examples

She added coconut cream to the curry to make it richer and creamier.
Desserts made with coconut cream have a delicious, tropical flavor.
You can find canned coconut cream at most grocery stores in the international foods aisle.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: coconut milk - a thinner liquid made from grated coconut meat and water
