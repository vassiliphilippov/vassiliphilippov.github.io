electrical phenomenon

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl fəˈnɑːməˌnɑːn / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl fəˈnɒmɪnən / (BrE)
A physical event or occurrence involving electricity or its effects.
event involving electricity

Examples

Lightning is a well-known electrical phenomenon that occurs during storms.
The aurora borealis is an electrical phenomenon resulting from solar particles entering the Earth's atmosphere.
Scientists study each electrical phenomenon to better understand the nature of electricity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electromagnetic phenomenon - A physical event involving both electricity and magnetism
