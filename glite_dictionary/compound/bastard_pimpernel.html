bastard pimpernel

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæstərd ˈpɪmpərnɛl / (AmE), / ˈbɑːstəd ˈpɪmpənel / (BrE)
a weedy plant with short, dry, chaff-like leaves, sometimes found in dry or disturbed soils
a weedy plant with chaff-like leaves

Examples

The field was full of bastard pimpernel growing alongside the wildflowers.
She identified the weed as bastard pimpernel during her botany survey.
Gardeners often remove bastard pimpernel because it quickly spreads in dry soil.
