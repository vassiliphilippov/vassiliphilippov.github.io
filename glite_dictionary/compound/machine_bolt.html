machine bolt

1. NOUN

/ məˈʃin boʊlt / (AmE), / məˈʃiːn bəʊlt / (BrE)
a bolt with a square or hexagonal head and a threaded shaft, commonly used to fasten metal parts and tightened with a wrench
a bolt with a square or hex head, used for fastening

Examples

The mechanic secured the engine block using a machine bolt and a socket wrench.
Each machine bolt in the assembly must be tightened to the manufacturer's specified torque.
She replaced the rusted machine bolt connecting the steel beams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bolt - a metal pin or bar, typically with a threaded shaft, for fastening things together
