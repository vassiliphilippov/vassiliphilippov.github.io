academic department

1. NOUN

/ əˈkædəmɪk dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / əˈkadəmɪk dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
a division within a school, college, or university that is responsible for teaching and research in a particular subject area
division overseeing a specific academic subject

Examples

The biology academic department offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate courses.
She was recently appointed chair of the English academic department at the university.
Funding for the computer science academic department has increased significantly this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faculty - teaching staff and departments of a university
  • Synonym: department - a division of an organization dealing with a specific area
