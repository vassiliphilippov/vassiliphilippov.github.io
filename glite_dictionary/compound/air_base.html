air base

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌbeɪs / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌbeɪs / (BrE)
a military facility where aircraft are housed, maintained, and operated
military facility for aircraft operations

Examples

The new squadron was stationed at the air base near the border.
During the conflict, the air base became a strategic target for attacks.
Families of military personnel often live near the air base.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airfield - a field or airport where aircraft take off and land
  • Antonym: naval base - a military base for naval forces
