southeastern United States

1. PROPN

/ ˌsoʊθˈistərn ˌjuːnəˈtaɪtɪd ˈsteɪts / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈiːstən ˌjuːnɪtɪd ˈsteɪts / (BrE)
the region in the southeastern part of the United States, typically including states like Georgia, Florida, Alabama, and the Carolinas
region in the southeast of the U.S.

Examples

Many vacationers travel to the southeastern United States for its warm climate and beautiful beaches.
The cuisine of the southeastern United States is known for its use of seafood and Southern spices.
Several major hurricanes have made landfall in the southeastern United States over the past decade.
