gear queer

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪr kwɪr / (AmE), / ɡɪə kwɪə / (BrE)
(slang) A person who is very enthusiastic about gear or equipment, especially in hobbies like cycling.
enthusiast for gear/equipment (slang)
slang

Examples

As a self-described gear queer, Danny is constantly upgrading his biking equipment.
The online forum is a popular place for gear queers to discuss the latest cycling technology.
Identifying as a gear queer means always searching for the next great gadget.

Synset Examples
