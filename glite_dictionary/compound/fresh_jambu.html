fresh jambu

1. NOUN

/ frɛʃ ˈdʒæmbu / (AmE), / frɛʃ ˈdʒambuː / (BrE)
A type of tropical fruit, also known as wax apple or water apple, commonly eaten fresh for its crisp texture and mildly sweet flavor.
a tropical fruit (wax apple)

Examples

I tasted fresh jambu for the first time yesterday, and its crispness surprised me.
The market had a basket full of fresh jambu beside the mangoes and papayas.
She served slices of fresh jambu at the party as a refreshing snack.

Synset Examples

I tasted fresh jambu for the first time yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wax apple - another name for fresh jambu, tropical fruit
  • Synonym: rose apple - related tropical fruit with similar characteristics

Etymology

From Malay 'jambu', referring to various juicy tropical fruits.
