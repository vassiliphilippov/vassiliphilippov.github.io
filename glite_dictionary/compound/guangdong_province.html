Guangdong province

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡwɑːŋˈdɔŋ ˈprɑːvɪns / (AmE), / ˌɡwæŋˈdɒŋ ˈprɒvɪns / (BrE)
a province in southern China, known for its economic significance and coastal location
province in southern China

Examples

Many international companies have set up factories in Guangdong province due to its proximity to the coast.
Cantonese cuisine, which originated in Guangdong province, is famous worldwide for its variety of flavors.
The trade fair in Guangdong province attracts visitors and businesspeople from around the globe.

Etymology

From Mandarin Chinese 广东 (Guǎngdōng), meaning 'east of the expanse'.
