local music scene

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.kəl ˈmju.zɪk siːn / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl ˈmjuː.zɪk siːn / (BrE)
the community of musicians, venues, and related activities in a particular area or city
music community in a local area

Examples

Many new bands get their start in the local music scene before gaining national attention.
The city's local music scene is known for its vibrant jazz culture.
She became heavily involved in the local music scene after moving to Austin.

Synset Examples

He’s known as a (c)rapper in the local music scene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: music scene - the community and culture surrounding music in a particular place or time
