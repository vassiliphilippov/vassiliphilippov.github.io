to convulse in pain

1. VERB

/ kənˈvʌls ɪn peɪn / (AmE), / kənˈvʌls ɪn peɪn / (BrE)
to experience a sudden, uncontrollable muscle movement as a result of intense pain
react uncontrollably due to pain

Examples

The boxer convulsed in pain after receiving a blow to the ribs.
She tried to stand, but her leg injury made her convulse in pain.
He convulsed in pain the moment the hot oil splashed onto his hand.

Synset Examples

She couldn't help but convulse in pain after twisting her ankle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: writhe in pain - to twist your body in response to severe pain
  • Synonym: grimace - to make a twisted facial expression from pain or discomfort
