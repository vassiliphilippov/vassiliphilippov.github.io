music director

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪk dɪˈrɛktər / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪk dɪˈrɛktə / (BrE)
a person who leads, conducts, or is responsible for the musical aspects of a performance, group, or organization
person who leads a musical group

Examples

The music director selected a challenging piece for the orchestra to perform at the concert.
As music director of the choir, she coordinated rehearsals and managed all musical arrangements.
After years of conducting, he was appointed music director at the city’s major symphony orchestra.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conductor - person who directs an orchestra or choir
  • Synonym: bandleader - leader of a musical band or ensemble
