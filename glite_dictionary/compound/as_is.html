as is

1. ADV

/ æz ɪz / (AmE), / æz ɪz / (BrE)
in its current condition, especially without any guarantees or alterations; commonly used to indicate that something is sold or accepted with all its faults or issues.
in its current condition (esp. for sale)

Examples

The vintage furniture is being sold as is, so please inspect it before buying.
Please note that all items are offered as is and cannot be returned.
We agreed to rent the apartment as is, understanding that there would be no renovations.

Synset Examples

The car is sold as is, no refunds.
The product comes as is, without any modifications.
They took the deal as is, without negotiations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as found - in the state in which something is found; not altered or improved
