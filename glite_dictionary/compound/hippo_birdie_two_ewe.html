Hippo birdie two ewe

1. X

/ ˌhɪpoʊ ˈbɝːdi tu ju / (AmE), / ˌhɪpəʊ ˈbɜːdi tuː juː / (BrE)
A humorous way of saying 'Happy Birthday to you,' based on a pun that mimics the phrase using animal names.
jocular birthday greeting pun
informal

Examples

As soon as she walked in, her friends shouted Hippo birdie two ewe to make her laugh.
We made a funny card that said Hippo birdie two ewe on the front.
Instead of the usual song, we sang Hippo birdie two ewe at the party.
