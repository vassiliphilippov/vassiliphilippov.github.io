spiderwort family

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪdərˌwɔrt ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdəwɜːt ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a large, widely distributed family of mostly perennial herbs or climbing plants, including spiderworts, known scientifically as Commelinaceae
family of perennial herbs or climbers (Commelinaceae)

Examples

The spiderwort family includes several ornamental plants valued for their colorful flowers.
Many members of the spiderwort family thrive in humid, tropical environments.
Scientists are studying genetic diversity within the spiderwort family to improve crop resilience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Commelinaceae - the scientific name for the spiderwort family
