citation needed

1. X

/ saɪˈteɪʃən ˈniːdɪd / (AmE), / saɪˈteɪʃən ˈniːdɪd / (BrE)
used to indicate that a statement or claim should be supported by a reliable source or reference
request for a supporting reference

Examples

The report contained several claims marked as citation needed by the editors.
Whenever a dubious statement appears on Wikipedia, users often add citation needed.
The professor wrote citation needed next to the unreferenced fact in the essay.

Synset Examples

Her claims were dismissed with a simple citation needed.
The article was flagged for a citation needed note.
He was reminded that his argument had a citation needed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fact-check - to verify the accuracy of information
  • Antonym: unsupported - not backed by evidence or references
