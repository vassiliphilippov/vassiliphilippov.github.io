paper fastener

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpɚ ˈfæstənɚ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə ˈfɑːsnə / (BrE)
a device used to hold sheets of paper together, such as a clip, pin, or clasp
device for holding papers together

Examples

He secured the documents with a paper fastener before handing them in.
Can you pass me a paper fastener to keep these pages together?
The teacher used a paper fastener to attach the permission slips to the form.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: staple - a short piece of wire bent to hold papers together
  • Synonym: paper clip - a bent wire or plastic device for holding papers together
