granola bar

1. NOUN

/ ɡrəˈnoʊlə bɑr / (AmE), / ɡrəˈnəʊlə bɑː / (BrE)
A snack bar made from oats, nuts, honey, and sometimes dried fruit, typically baked and eaten as a quick snack.
a snack bar made of granola

Examples

She packed a granola bar in her bag as a quick breakfast.
After his workout, he ate a granola bar to regain energy.
The new granola bar flavors include chocolate-chip and almond.
