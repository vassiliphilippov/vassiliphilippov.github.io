masked ball

1. NOUN

/ mæst bɔl / (AmE), / mɑːskt bɔːl / (BrE)
a formal dance or party where the guests wear masks and often elaborate costumes
a dance party with masks and costumes

Examples

The charity hosted a lavish masked ball to raise money for the hospital.
Everyone at the masked ball wore intricate costumes and mysterious masks.
She met her future husband at a masked ball in Venice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masquerade - a party or dance where people wear masks and costumes
