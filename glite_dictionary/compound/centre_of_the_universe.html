centre of the universe

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛntɚ əv ðə ˈjunɪˌvɝs / (AmE), / ˈsɛntə əv ði ˈjuːnɪˌvɜːs / (BrE)
a person or thing regarded as the most important or self-important, often used to describe someone who behaves as if everything revolves around them
someone who thinks they are most important

Examples

He acts like he is the centre of the universe and expects everyone to pay attention to him.
For teenagers, their problems often seem like the centre of the universe.
She complained so much that her friends teased her about thinking she was the centre of the universe.

Synset Examples

She always acted like she was the Centre of the Universe.
In her mind, her problems were the Centre of the Universe.
He treated his opinions as if they were the Centre of the Universe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: egocentric - self-centered or egotistical
  • Synonym: self-important - having an exaggerated sense of one's own value or importance
  • Antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low view of one's importance
