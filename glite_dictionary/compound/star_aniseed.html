star aniseed

1. NOUN

/ stɑr ˈænɪˌsid / (AmE), / stɑː ˈænɪsiːd / (BrE)
A star-shaped, aromatic spice from the fruit of a small Asian tree, used especially in Asian cooking and traditional medicine.
aromatic star-shaped spice used in cooking

Examples

We added a single star aniseed to the soup to enhance its flavor.
Traditional Chinese medicines sometimes include star aniseed as an ingredient.
The recipe calls for crushed star aniseed to provide a sweet, licorice-like aroma.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: star anise - the same star-shaped spice from Illicium verum

Etymology

From the resemblance of the fruit to a star, and 'aniseed' due to its similar flavor to anise.
