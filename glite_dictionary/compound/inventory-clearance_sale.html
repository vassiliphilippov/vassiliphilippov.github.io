inventory-clearance sale

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.vənˌtɔːr.i ˈklɪr.əns seɪl / (AmE), / ˈɪn.vən.t(ə)r.i ˈklɪə.rəns seɪl / (BrE)
a sale held to quickly sell off excess or outdated stock, often at reduced prices
sale to reduce excess stock

Examples

The store is holding an inventory-clearance sale to make room for new merchandise.
During the inventory-clearance sale, all items were marked down by up to 70 percent.
He bought several appliances at the inventory-clearance sale last weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clearance sale - a sale to clear out goods, usually at reduced prices
  • Degree Lower: sale - an event or period when goods are sold at reduced prices
