social class

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl klæs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl klɑːs / (BrE)
A group of people in society who share similar social, economic, or educational status.
group sharing similar social status

Examples

She was born into a social class that valued education above all else.
Changes in the economy often affect each social class differently.
The novel explores the relationship between different social classes in Victorian England.

Synset Examples

the working class
an emerging professional class

Related Terms

  • Synonym: class - a group of people sharing the same social or economic status
