American Revised Version

1. PROPN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən rɪˈvaɪzd vərʒən / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən rɪˈvaɪzd ˈvɜːʃən / (BrE)
a revised edition of the King James Version of the Bible, published in the United States in the late 19th century.
U.S. revised Bible version (19th century)

Examples

Many scholars refer to the American Revised Version when discussing biblical translations.
The American Revised Version aimed to update the language of the King James Bible.
She inherited an old family Bible printed in the American Revised Version.
