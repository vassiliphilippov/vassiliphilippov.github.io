sweet unicorn plant

1. NOUN

/ swit ˈjunɪˌkɔrn plænt / (AmE), / swiːt ˈjuːnɪˌkɔːn plɑːnt / (BrE)
a herbaceous plant of the genus Proboscidea, known for its distinctive horn-shaped fruit
herbaceous plant of genus Proboscidea

Examples

The sweet unicorn plant thrives in warm, sandy soils in southwestern North America.
Botanists admire the unusual curved pods of the sweet unicorn plant.
Farmers sometimes cultivate the sweet unicorn plant for its ornamental appeal.
