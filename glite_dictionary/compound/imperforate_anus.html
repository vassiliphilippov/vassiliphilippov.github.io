imperforate anus

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈpɜrfərət ˈeɪnəs / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɜːfərət ˈeɪnəs / (BrE)
a congenital medical condition in which the anal opening is partially or completely blocked or absent
congenital defect of the anal opening
medical

Examples

The newborn was diagnosed with an imperforate anus and required immediate surgical intervention.
Doctors explained that an imperforate anus is a rare birth defect affecting the digestive tract.
Treatment for an imperforate anus may involve one or more operations, depending on the severity.

Etymology

From Latin 'im-' (not) + 'perforatus' (pierced) and 'anus'.
