what's the story with

1. X

/ wʌts ðə ˈstɔri wɪð / (AmE), / wɒts ðə ˈstɔːri wɪð / (BrE)
used to ask for information or an explanation about the situation regarding someone or something
used to ask about a situation or background

Examples

What's the story with the new neighbor who just moved in?
Does anyone know what's the story with all the construction on Main Street?
He asked me what's the story with our team's recent losing streak.

Synset Examples

Can you tell me what's the story with that rumor?
