moribund industry

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɔːrɪˌbʌnd ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˌmɒrɪˌbʌnd ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
an industry that is in decline or near extinction, with little prospect of recovery
an industry in decline or near extinction

Examples

Without government support, the moribund industry will likely disappear within the next decade.
Efforts to revitalize the moribund industry have so far failed to produce positive results.
A new technology could transform a moribund industry into a thriving sector again.

Synset Examples

The moribund industry needs innovation to survive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: declining industry - industry experiencing a reduction in activity or profitability

Etymology

From Latin 'moribundus' (dying) combined with 'industry'.
