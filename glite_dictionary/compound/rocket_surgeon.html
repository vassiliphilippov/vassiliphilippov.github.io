rocket surgeon

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑkɪt ˌsɝdʒən / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪt ˌsɜːdʒən / (BrE)
A humorous term for a highly skilled professional, especially used jokingly to refer to an expert in any field.
humorous term for an expert or highly skilled professional
informal slang

Examples

You don't need to be a rocket surgeon to understand basic computer security.
My friends call me a rocket surgeon whenever I fix their gadgets, but I know it's just a joke.
Solving that puzzle made me feel like a real rocket surgeon for a moment.

Synset Examples

He claimed to be a rocket surgeon in the tech world.
Only a rocket surgeon could tackle this complex problem.
They joked that he was a rocket surgeon with his coding skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rocket scientist - a highly intelligent or skilled person (esp. in science or technology)
  • Synonym: brain surgeon - a medical specialist in surgeries of the brain; also used metaphorically for very intelligent people

Etymology

Blend of 'rocket scientist' and 'brain surgeon', two well-known terms for highly skilled professions.
