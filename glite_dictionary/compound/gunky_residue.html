gunky residue

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌŋki rɛˈzɪdju / (AmE), / ˈɡʌŋki rɪˈzɪdjuː / (BrE)
a sticky or unpleasant substance left behind after something has been removed or has come to an end
sticky or unpleasant leftover material
informal

Examples

The blender was covered in a layer of gunky residue after making the smoothie.
She used a special cleaner to remove the gunky residue from the bathtub.
Old stickers often leave gunky residue on glass surfaces.

Synset Examples

He cleaned the gunky residue off the kitchen counter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: residue - the small amount of something that remains after the main part has been removed
  • Synonym: deposit - a layer or mass of material left by a process

Etymology

From 'gunky' (from 'gunk', informal for sticky or dirty substance) + 'residue' (from Latin 'residuum', meaning something left behind).
