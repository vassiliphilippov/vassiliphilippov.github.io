football-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʊtbɔl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈfʊtbɔːl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a shape similar to that of a football, typically oval or oblong and rounded at both ends
shaped like a football

Examples

The architect designed a football-shaped dome for the new stadium.
She bought a football-shaped lamp as a gift for her brother.
The seeds were small and football-shaped, making them easy to recognize.
