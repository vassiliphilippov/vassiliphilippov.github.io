business college

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs ˌkɑlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˌkɒlɪdʒ / (BrE)
a college offering courses in clerical and administrative aspects of business and commerce
a school for business and office training

Examples

After graduating from business college, she found a job as an office manager.
Many students choose to attend a business college to gain practical skills for the workplace.
He teaches accounting at a local business college in the evenings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: business school - a school or college that teaches business and management practices
