Durango City

1. PROPN

/ duˈræŋɡoʊ ˈsɪti / (AmE), / djʊəˈræŋɡəʊ ˈsɪti / (BrE)
a city in north-central Mexico, known as a mining center and capital of the state of Durango
city in north-central Mexico

Examples

Many tourists visit Durango City to explore its historic colonial architecture.
The economy of Durango City has long relied on mining and agriculture.
Flights to Durango City are available from several major Mexican airports.

Etymology

Named after Durango Province in Spain, itself possibly from Basque *urango* (water place).
