local landmark

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊkəl ˈlændˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəl ˈlan(d)mɑːk / (BrE)
a well-known and easily recognized place or structure within a specific local area
notable place in a local area

Examples

The old bridge has become a local landmark over the years.
Everyone in town can give you directions to the local landmark near the river.
The restaurant is considered a local landmark and attracts many visitors.

Synset Examples

The Faded Giant mural is a local landmark.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: landmark - an easily recognized and important feature or structure
