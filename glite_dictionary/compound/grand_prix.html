Grand Prix

1. NOUN

/ ɡræn ˈpri / (AmE), / ɡrɑ̃ː ˈpriː / (BrE)
an important international motor race, especially one of a series forming a world championship competition
major international motor race

Examples

The Monaco Grand Prix is one of the most prestigious events in the racing calendar.
He dreams of one day winning a Formula One Grand Prix.
Tickets for the Italian Grand Prix sold out within minutes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Formula One - top international class of single-seater auto racing

Etymology

From French 'grand prix' meaning 'great prize'.
