boricua cuisine

1. NOUN

/ bɔːˈrikwə kwɪˈzin / (AmE), / bɒˈriːkwə kwɪˈziːn / (BrE)
the traditional food and culinary practices of Puerto Rico, featuring flavors and dishes typical of Puerto Rican culture
traditional Puerto Rican food

Examples

The boricua cuisine at the festival highlighted unique flavors like sofrito and plantains.
Many New York restaurants now serve authentic boricua cuisine to celebrate Puerto Rican heritage.
I learned to cook boricua cuisine from my grandmother's cherished recipes.

Synset Examples

The boricua cuisine at the party was delicious and vibrant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Puerto Rican cuisine - the cuisine of Puerto Rico

Etymology

Boricua from Spanish, derived from Borikén (the indigenous Taíno name for Puerto Rico), plus 'cuisine' from French.
