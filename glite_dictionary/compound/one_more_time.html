one more time

1. ADV

/ wʌn mɔr taɪm / (AmE), / wʌn mɔː taɪm / (BrE)
an additional occasion; once again
an additional time; again

Examples

Could you please explain the instructions one more time?
He tried calling her one more time before giving up.
They performed the song one more time for the encore.

Synset Examples

Let's try that recipe one more time for dinner.
I just need to finish this project one more time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: again - another time; once more
