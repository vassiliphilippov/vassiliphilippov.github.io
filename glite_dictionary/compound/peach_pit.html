peach pit

1. NOUN

/ pitʃ pɪt / (AmE), / piːtʃ pɪt / (BrE)
the hard stone or seed found in the center of a peach
hard stone inside a peach

Examples

Don't forget to remove the peach pit before blending the fruit into a smoothie.
The child planted a peach pit in the backyard to see if a tree would grow.
He accidentally bit down on a peach pit while eating his dessert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stone - the hard seed inside some fruits, such as peaches, plums, or cherries
