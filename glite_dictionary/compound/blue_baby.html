blue baby

1. NOUN

/ bluː ˈbeɪbi / (AmE), / bluː ˈbeɪbi / (BrE)
an infant born with a bluish skin color, typically as a result of a congenital heart defect causing insufficient oxygen in the blood
infant with bluish skin from heart defect
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that their newborn was a blue baby due to a congenital heart defect.
When she noticed the baby's lips were turning blue, she feared he might be a blue baby.
Treatments for blue babies have improved dramatically in recent decades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cyanosis - bluish discoloration of the skin from low oxygen levels
