animal stuffer

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɪml ˈstʌfər / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl ˈstʌfə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to stuff and mount the skins of animals for display
person who prepares mounted animals

Examples

The museum hired an experienced animal stuffer to prepare the new wildlife exhibit.
As an animal stuffer, he spent hours carefully reconstructing birds and mammals for educational displays.
She apprenticed with a skilled animal stuffer to learn the art of taxidermy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxidermist - person trained in preparing, stuffing, and mounting animal skins
