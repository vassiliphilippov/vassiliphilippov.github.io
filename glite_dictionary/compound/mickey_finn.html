Mickey Finn

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪki fɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɪki fɪn / (BrE)
A drink that has been secretly laced with a drug or substance intended to incapacitate or knock out the person consuming it.
drugged drink to knock out someone (slang)
slang

Examples

He claimed that someone had slipped him a Mickey Finn in the bar last night.
The detective suspected that a Mickey Finn had been used to render the victim unconscious.
Stories about criminals using a Mickey Finn to rob their targets are common in old crime novels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knockout drops - a drug added to a drink to make someone unconscious

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from the name of a Chicago bartender alleged to have used such drinks in the early 1900s.
