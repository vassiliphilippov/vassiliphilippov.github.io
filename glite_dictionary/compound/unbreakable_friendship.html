unbreakable friendship

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈbreɪkəbəl ˈfrɛndʃɪp / (AmE), / ʌnˈbreɪkəbl ˈfrɛn(d)ʃɪp / (BrE)
a strong and lasting bond between friends that cannot be easily destroyed
a strong, lasting bond between friends

Examples

After years of overcoming challenges together, their unbreakable friendship became the envy of everyone around them.
Despite living in different countries, the two maintained an unbreakable friendship through regular calls and visits.
The movie beautifully portrays the unbreakable friendship between the main characters, even in the face of adversity.

Synset Examples

Their friendship proved to be unbreakable through tough times.
