riot gun

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪət ɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈraɪət ɡʌn / (BrE)
a firearm designed to disperse crowds or rioters, typically using non-lethal ammunition such as rubber bullets or tear gas
firearm for dispersing crowds; uses non-lethal ammo

Examples

The police fired a riot gun loaded with rubber bullets to break up the protest.
He demonstrated how the riot gun could be used safely in crowd control situations.
Authorities ordered several new riot guns to equip their special response teams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shotgun - a gun that is usually smoothbore and fires shot
  • Synonym: firearm - a portable gun, such as a pistol or rifle
