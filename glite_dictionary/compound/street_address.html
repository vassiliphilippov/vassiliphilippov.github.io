street address

1. NOUN

/ strit əˈdrɛs / (AmE), / striːt əˈdrɛs / (BrE)
the specific address of a building or location, including the street name and number, where a person or organization can be found or contacted
mailing location with street name and number

Examples

Please write your street address clearly on the form for delivery purposes.
She moved to a new apartment but forgot to update her street address with the bank.
The invitation included the street address of the venue so guests could find it easily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mailing address - the address to which mail is delivered
