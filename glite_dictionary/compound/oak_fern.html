oak fern

1. NOUN

/ oʊk fɝn / (AmE), / əʊk fɜːn / (BrE)
A bright blue-green fern widely found in damp, acidic woodlands of the temperate Northern Hemisphere.
a woodland fern species

Examples

The forest floor was carpeted with delicate oak fern fronds.
After the rain, the oak fern looked especially vibrant along the woodland path.
We identified an oak fern during our nature hike in the northern woods.
