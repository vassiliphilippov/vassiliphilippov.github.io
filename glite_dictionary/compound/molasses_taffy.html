molasses taffy

1. NOUN

/ məˈlæs.ɪz ˈtæf.i / (AmE), / məˈlæs.ɪz ˈtæf.i / (BrE)
A type of chewy candy made primarily from molasses.
chewy candy made from molasses

Examples

She brought homemade molasses taffy to the family reunion.
The recipe for molasses taffy has been passed down for generations.
Children love to help stretch the sticky molasses taffy on cold winter nights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taffy - a chewy candy made by boiling sugar, molasses or honey, and butter
