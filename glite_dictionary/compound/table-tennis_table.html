table-tennis table

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəl ˌtɛnɪs ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈteɪbl̩ ˌtɛnɪs ˈteɪbl̩ / (BrE)
a rectangular table, divided by a net, on which table tennis is played
table used for playing table tennis

Examples

The club just bought a new table-tennis table for the recreation room.
She folded up the table-tennis table to make space for other activities.
International tournaments require table-tennis tables that meet strict size and surface regulations.

Related Terms
