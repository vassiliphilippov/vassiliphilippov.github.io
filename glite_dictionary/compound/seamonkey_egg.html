SEAmonkey egg

1. NOUN

/ siːˌmʌŋki ɛɡ / (AmE), / siːˌmʌŋki ɛɡ / (BrE)
the egg of a brine shrimp, especially as sold in novelty pet kits
egg of brine shrimp sold as a pet

Examples

We watched the SEAmonkey eggs hatch yesterday.
The kit includes a packet of SEAmonkey eggs and instructions for hatching them.
Children are fascinated by how quickly SEAmonkey eggs come to life in water.

Synset Examples

We watched the SEAmonkey eggs hatch yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brine shrimp - small aquatic crustacean sold as novelty pets (also known as sea monkeys)

Etymology

From 'sea monkey', a trademarked name for pet brine shrimp, with 'egg'.
