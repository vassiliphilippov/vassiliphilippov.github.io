Luxembourg franc

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌksəmˌbɜːrɡ ˈfræŋk / (AmE), / ˈlʌksəmbɜːɡ ˈfræŋk / (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in Luxembourg, replaced by the euro in 2002
former currency unit of Luxembourg

Examples

Before the euro, prices in Luxembourg were listed in Luxembourg francs and Belgian francs.
She exchanged her old Luxembourg francs for euros at the bank.
The museum displayed a collection of coins, including several Luxembourg franc notes from the 20th century.
