iron fist

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪɚn fɪst / (AmE), / ˈaɪən fɪst / (BrE)
a stern, harsh, or oppressive way of ruling or controlling others, often associated with authoritarian authority
harsh or strict control; authoritarian rule

Examples

The dictator maintained control with an iron fist.
Her father ran the household with an iron fist, allowing no disobedience.
The new manager tried to rule the team with an iron fist, but it backfired.

Synset Examples

The dictator maintained control with an iron fist.
She ruled the company with an iron fist.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: velvet glove - gentle or diplomatic approach, especially when masking force
  • Synonym: iron hand - firm or harsh control, similar to iron fist

Etymology

From the literal notion of a 'fist made of iron', used metaphorically for rigidity and forcefulness.
