mineral extraction

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪnərəl ɪkˌstrækʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɪnərəl ɪkˈstrækʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the process of removing minerals from the earth, including crushing and separating ore into valuable substances or waste by various techniques
the process of obtaining minerals from the earth

Examples

Environmental concerns have increased regulations on mineral extraction in recent years.
The company specializes in innovative techniques for mineral extraction from low-grade ores.
Traditional mineral extraction methods can cause significant damage to local ecosystems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mining - the activity or industry of extracting minerals from the earth
