hot-fudge sauce

1. NOUN

/ hɑt ˈfʌdʒ sɔs / (AmE), / hɒt ˈfʌdʒ sɔːs / (BrE)
a thick, rich chocolate sauce served hot, typically used as a topping for ice cream and other desserts
thick chocolate sauce served hot

Examples

She poured hot-fudge sauce over her ice cream sundae for a decadent treat.
The dessert menu always features brownies topped with vanilla ice cream and hot-fudge sauce.
You can make hot-fudge sauce at home with cocoa powder, sugar, and cream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chocolate sauce - a sauce made with chocolate, often used as a dessert topping
  • Antonym: caramel sauce - a sweet sauce made from caramelized sugar, often used on desserts
