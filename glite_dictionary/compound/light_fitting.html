light fitting

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪt ˌfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt ˌfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
an electrical device that holds and powers a light bulb, providing illumination and usually attached to a wall or ceiling
electrical fixture for a light bulb
british

Examples

The electrician installed a new light fitting in the hallway.
Please choose a light fitting that matches the decor of your living room.
It's important to switch off the power before changing a light fitting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: light fixture - an electrical device used to hold and power a light bulb, primarily American usage
