telephone exchange

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlɪˌfoʊn ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
a central facility where telephone calls are connected between users on a telecommunications network
center connecting telephone calls

Examples

The local telephone exchange was damaged during the storm, disrupting service for hundreds of people.
She worked as an operator at the telephone exchange in the 1950s.
Modern telecommunications have replaced the manual telephone exchange with automated switching equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: switchboard - a device for manually connecting telephone calls
  • Degree Higher: telephone network - the overall system for making telephone calls
