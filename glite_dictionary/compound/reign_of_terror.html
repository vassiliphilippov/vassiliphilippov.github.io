Reign of Terror

1. NOUN

/ ˌreɪn əv ˈtɛrər / (AmE), / ˌreɪn əv ˈtɛrə / (BrE)
the period during the French Revolution (1793–1794) marked by mass executions and political repression
period of mass executions in French Revolution

Examples

The Reign of Terror saw thousands executed by guillotine across France.
Historians debate the causes and consequences of the Reign of Terror.
The Reign of Terror left a profound impact on European history.

Synset Examples

the Reign of the Bourbons ended and the Reign of Terror began
