cigar butt

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈɡɑr bʌt / (AmE), / sɪˈɡɑː bʌt / (BrE)
the small remaining part of a cigar left after most of it has been smoked
the remaining part of a smoked cigar

Examples

He flicked the cigar butt into the ashtray and stood up to leave.
The sidewalk was littered with every sort of cigar butt and debris.
She hesitated before picking up the cigar butt that someone had left on the bench.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cigarette butt - the small part of a cigarette left after smoking
