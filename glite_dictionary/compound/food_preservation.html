food preservation

1. NOUN

/ fuːd prɛzərˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / fuːd prɛzəˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the methods or processes used to prevent food from spoiling and to keep it safe and edible for longer periods
methods to keep food from spoiling

Examples

Recent advances in food preservation have greatly reduced the risk of foodborne illness.
Canning, freezing, and drying are common techniques used in food preservation.
The company specializes in innovative food preservation technologies that extend shelf life.

Synset Examples

Irradiation is commonly used for food preservation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preservation - the act of keeping something in its original state
