dill pickle

1. NOUN

/ dɪl ˈpɪkəl / (AmE), / dɪl ˈpɪkəl / (BrE)
A cucumber that has been preserved in brine or vinegar and flavored with dill seed.
cucumber preserved with dill

Examples

She added a dill pickle to her sandwich for extra crunch and flavor.
At the deli, I ordered a corned beef on rye with a dill pickle on the side.
The tangy taste of a dill pickle pairs well with hamburgers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gherkin - small pickled cucumber; sometimes used as a synonym in British English
