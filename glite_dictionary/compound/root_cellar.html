root cellar

1. NOUN

/ rut ˈsɛlər / (AmE), / ruːt ˈsɛlə / (BrE)
a storage space, typically underground, used for storing root vegetables and other produce to keep them cool and fresh
underground storage for root vegetables

Examples

During the winter, we kept potatoes and carrots in the root cellar.
The old farmhouse still has a root cellar beneath the kitchen.
Access to the root cellar was through a trapdoor in the barn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cellar - an underground room for storing things
