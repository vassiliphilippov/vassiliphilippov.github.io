sentence stress

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛntəns strɛs / (AmE), / ˈsɛntəns strɛs / (BrE)
The natural emphasis given to certain words or syllables within a sentence when spoken, affecting the rhythm and meaning of the sentence.
emphasis pattern within a spoken sentence

Examples

English learners often struggle with sentence stress when trying to sound more natural.
The meaning of a sentence can change depending on the sentence stress used.
Teachers use drills to help students practice correct sentence stress in spoken English.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: word stress - emphasis on particular syllables in individual words
