storage solution

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔrɪdʒ səˈluʃən / (AmE), / ˈstɔːrɪdʒ səˈluːʃən / (BrE)
a product or method used for organizing, storing, or optimizing the storage of items
product or method for organizing storage

Examples

The new shelves provided a practical storage solution for her growing book collection.
Companies often invest in digital storage solutions to better manage their data.
Under-bed drawers are an excellent storage solution for small apartments.

Synset Examples

They decided to compactify their storage solutions for efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: container - an object used for holding or storing things
