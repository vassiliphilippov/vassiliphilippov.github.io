US Cabinet

1. PROPN

/ ˌjuːˈɛs ˈkæbɪnɪt / (AmE), / ˌjuːˈɛs ˈkabɪnɪt / (BrE)
the group of senior officials appointed by the President of the United States to head executive departments and advise the President on government policy
top advisers to the US President
american

Examples

The US Cabinet meets regularly to discuss major national issues.
She was nominated to serve as a member of the US Cabinet.
The President consulted the US Cabinet before making his decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: government - the governing body of a nation, state, or community
