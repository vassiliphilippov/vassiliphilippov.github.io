common sorrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈsɔrəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈsɒrəl / (BrE)
A herbaceous plant with large, sour-tasting, arrowhead-shaped leaves, often used in salads and sauces.
herb with sour-tasting leaves used in cooking

Examples

The fresh leaves of common sorrel give a tangy flavor to summer salads.
Chefs often incorporate common sorrel into creamy sauces for fish dishes.
We grew common sorrel in our garden to add zest to our homemade soups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sorrel - a plant with acidic-tasting leaves used as a herb
