facts of life

1. NOUN

/ fæks əv laɪf / (AmE), / fæks əv laɪf / (BrE)
the basic information about sex, reproduction, and human sexuality that is typically considered necessary for people to know
basic facts about sex and reproduction

Examples

When their son turned twelve, they decided it was time to explain the facts of life to him.
Many parents dread having the facts of life talk with their children.
Schools often provide lessons about the facts of life as part of sex education.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birds and the bees - a euphemism for information about sex and reproduction
