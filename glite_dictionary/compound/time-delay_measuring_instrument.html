time-delay measuring instrument

1. NOUN

/ taɪm dɪˌleɪ ˈmɛʒərɪŋ ˌɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / taɪm dɪˌleɪ ˈmɛʒərɪŋ ˌɪnstrʊmənt / (BrE)
an instrument used for measuring the time difference between two events, often with very high precision
instrument for measuring time difference between events

Examples

A time-delay measuring instrument is essential in experiments requiring precise synchronization.
The technician calibrated the time-delay measuring instrument before starting the tests.
Advanced laboratories often use a digital time-delay measuring instrument for high-speed processes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chronoscope - an instrument for measuring short time intervals with precision
