housewarming party

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌwɔr.mɪŋ ˈpɑr.ti / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌwɔː.mɪŋ ˈpɑː.ti / (BrE)
a party held to celebrate moving into a new home, often attended by friends and family
party for moving into a new home

Examples

We invited all our neighbors to our housewarming party last weekend.
She received many thoughtful gifts at her housewarming party.
They hosted a casual housewarming party after settling into their new apartment.

Synset Examples

Guests brought gifts to the housewarming celebration.
They threw a housewarming party to celebrate their new home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: housewarming - the celebration of moving into a new house

Etymology

From 'housewarming' (warming a new house with guests) + 'party'.
