power worker

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊ.ɚ ˌwɝː.kɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə ˌwɜː.kə / (BrE)
a person who works at a power station, especially involved in the operation or maintenance of generating electricity
worker at a power station

Examples

Many power workers went on strike to demand better safety measures at the plant.
The union represents thousands of power workers across the country.
During the outage, a power worker quickly restored electricity to the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plant operator - person who operates equipment at an industrial plant
