ague weed

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪɡju ˌwid / (AmE), / ˈeɪɡjuː ˌwiːd / (BrE)
A gentian plant of eastern North America, characterized by clusters of bristly blue flowers.
North American gentian with bristly blue flowers

Examples

The fields were covered in ague weed blooming under the warm autumn sun.
Botanists identified the rare ague weed by its distinctive clusters of bristly blue flowers.
Local folklore claims that ague weed was once used in traditional remedies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blue gentian - a gentian with blue flowers
