feminist dance therapy

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛmɪnɪst dæns ˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˈfɛmɪnɪst dɑːns ˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
a therapeutic practice that combines feminist principles with dance to promote empowerment and healing, especially for women
therapy combining feminism and dance

Examples

She attended feminist dance therapy sessions to connect with her body and foster self-acceptance.
The organization offers feminist dance therapy as a way to support survivors of trauma.
Many participants report feeling empowered after participating in feminist dance therapy workshops.

Synset Examples

They promote feminist dance therapy to help women reclaim their bodies.
