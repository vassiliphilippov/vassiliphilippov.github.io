filler-upper

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪlər ˌʌpər / (AmE), / ˈfɪlə ˌʌpə / (BrE)
a person or thing that fills, replenishes, or satisfies temporarily; something or someone that supplies what is lacking or needed, though not permanently
thing or person that fills temporarily
informal

Examples

The soup was a great filler-upper for a cold winter night.
At the party, she was the ultimate filler-upper, constantly refilling drinks.
This quick snack is just a filler-upper until dinner is ready.
